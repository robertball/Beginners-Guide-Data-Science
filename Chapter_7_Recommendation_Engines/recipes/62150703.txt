Title:
Chicken Ramen Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbs sesame oil
1/2 tsp turmeric
2 tsp ginger paste or fresh grated ginger root
2 tbs chili paste
1 lb skinless, boneless, chicken breast (cut into chunks)
1 (32oz carton)chicken broth
2 tsp sugar or sweetener
1/8 cup soy sauce
1-2 pkg ramen noodles (seasoning packets optional, recommend "Annie Chun's Noodle Express" variety)
1 tbs chili oil (optional-found in most Asian markets)
1 cup chopped green onions for garnish

Directions:
In a large pot, heat sesame oil over medium heat.
Cook turmeric, ginger, and chili paste until fragrant.
Add chicken and cook until no longer pink in the center, about 5-10 minutes.
Stir in broth, sugar, and soy sauce.
Bring to a boil, then introduce ramen noodles (and seasoning packets if desired) and cook for about 3 minutes.
Stir in chili oil (if desired) and green onions and remove from heat.

Submitter: 
Jellybean
Description: 
If you ever have a craving for Ramen noodles, but want something more than just packaged, instant noodles, then give this quick and easy recipe a try. I use Annie Chun's Noodle Express ramen noodles for a more healthy variation (found at Whole Foods).
