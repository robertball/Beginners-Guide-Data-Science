Title:
Pasta Pizza Venison Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces uncooked elbow macaroni
1 pound ground venison
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 (15 ounce) can pizza sauce
1 tsp garlic powder
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to package directions.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet, cook the venison, garlic powder,salt and pepper over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain if necessary.
Drain the macaroni; place half in a greased 2-qt. baking dish.
Top with half of the venison, pizza sauce, and cheese's.
Repeat layers.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 15 minutes.
Uncover; bake 10 minutes longer or until heated through and cheese is melted.
