Title:
Slow Cooker Thanksgiving Turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/523060.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (5 1/2 pound) bone-in turkey breast
3-4 tablespoons of butter + extra
1 tablespoon garlic salt
1 tablespoon paprika
1 1/2 teaspoon sage
1 1/2 teaspoon itailian seasoning
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
salt and pepper
1 cup chicken stock

Directions:
Butter slow cooker sides and bottom. Spread butter all over turkey, in the cavity and under the skin.  Season turkey all over, in the cavity and under the skin with all of the spices.  Place turkey in the slow cooker. Add the worcestershire sauce and chicken stock.  Cover slow cooker, and cook turkey 8 hours on Low.
