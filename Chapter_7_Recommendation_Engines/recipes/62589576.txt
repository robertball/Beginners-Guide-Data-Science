Title:
5 Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. olive oil
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 medium onion, diced
1/4 cup celery, diced
1/2 cup carrot, diced
3 tbsp. barley, rinsed
3 cups water
1 can each pinto beans, black beans, kidney beans, navy beans and garbanzo beans
1 cup corn
1 can diced tomatoes
1/2 tsp. dried basil
2 bay leaves
1 tsp. salt

Directions:
Heat oil in saucepan and add onion and garlic.  Cook until onion is translucent.  Add all ingredients, cover and simmer for 30 min.
