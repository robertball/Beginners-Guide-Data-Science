Title:
Banana Chocolate Chip Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup (3 med) bananas--mashed
1 1/4 cups white sugar
1/4 cup buttermilk
2/3 cup unsalted butter--softened or melted
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup bittersweet chocolate chips (60% cocoa)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Grease a 12-cup muffin pan or insert cupcake liners.
In large mixing bowl combine ingredients one at a time, in order as listed: mashed bananas, sugar, buttermilk, softened or melted butter, 2 eggs--lightly beaten, and vanilla.
In separate bowl, lightly mix flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually fold dry ingredients into wet mixture.
Fold in chocolate chips.
Spoon batter into muffin cups until 3/4 full.
Bake on middle rack for 25-30 minutes or until toothpick inserted in the center of a muffin comes out clean.
Allow to sit for 5 minutes before removing from pan. Cool on a wire rack.

Submitter: 
Cheri Nixon
Description: 
Melt-in-your-mouth goodness for those who love chocolate and bananas but don't like nuts. I love banana nut muffins but no one else in my family likes nuts, so I combined several recipes to come up with this family favorite. Always a crowd favorite, this muffin is  sure to satisfy both your banana and chocolate cravings.
