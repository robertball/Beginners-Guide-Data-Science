Title:
Big Ray's Raspberry Iced Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3738816.jpg
Ingredients:
1  cup sugar
7 cup water
3 oz fresh raspberries
3 tea bags

Directions:
Bring Sugar and 1 cup of water to a boil. Reduce heat to medium. Add in your raspberries. Crush your raspberries while in the pot, you can use the back of a large spoon for this. Turn off heat and let sit until cooled, about 25-30 minutes.
Boil 6 cups of water. Add tea bags in and let steep for about 5 minutes. Let cool.
Strain raspberries from syrup. Mix syrup and tea into pitcher and continue to let chill in the fridge.
Serve over ice with fresh raspberries.
