Title:
Cape Cod Cranberry bundt cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive oil
1/2 melted butter
3 eggs
2 cups sugar
2 cups flour
1 cup buttermilk
1 cup fresh or dried cranberries
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon ground cloves
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees F.  In large bowl beat eggs until foamy using an electric mixer. Add sugar and continue beating until mixture is light and fluffy.  Mix in olive oil and melted butter.
In small bowl combine baking soda and buttermilk and set aside.  Sift togheter flour, salt, cinnamon, and ground cloves.
Alternately beat the flour and buttermilk mixtures into the egg mixture.  Fold in the cranberries and pour into a lighty oiled and floured 9 inch tube pan or bundt cake pan. Bake for 1 hour or until tester comes out clean.  Cool for 10 mins.  Remove from pan and allow to cool compeletly.
Top with a sugar glaze (made of 1 cup powdered sugar and 1 to 2 tablespoons of water mixed and poured over cooled cake) or sprinkle with powdered sugar

Submitter: 
Robin Lee Starnes
Description: 
A sweet spice cake with a hint of tartness, great for the holidays.  Easy to make and a family favorite.
