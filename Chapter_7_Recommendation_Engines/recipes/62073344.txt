Title:
Tuna, cucumber and yellow pepper rissotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of rice
1 fish stock cube
1 tin of tuna
1 large yellow pepper - diced
1/2 a cucumber - diced
1 tin of chopped tomatoes
tobasco sauce
olive oil
mixed herbs
salt and pepper

Directions:
* Make up the fish stock ready.
* Heat a teaspoons worth of oil in a deep frying pan and fry the rice for 30 seconds or until it turns transparent.
* Add the stock to stop the rice from further frying.
* Add the tomatoes, cucumber, yellow pepper, salt, pepper, herbs and a few drops of tobasco sauce.
* Simmer for a further 45 minutes stirring occasionally.
* Once reduced and most of the liquid has evaporated add the tuna to warm.
* Serve on warmed plates/dishes.
