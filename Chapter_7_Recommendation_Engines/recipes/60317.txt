Title:
Cat's Famous Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1095047.jpg
Ingredients:
1 onion, finely chopped
1 head cabbage, finely chopped
4 carrots, grated
1 cup Greek yogurt
1 (8 ounce) can sliced pineapple, drained and chopped
salt and pepper to taste
paprika to taste
garlic powder to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix the onion, cabbage, carrots, Greek yogurt, and pineapple. Season with salt and pepper, paprika, and garlic powder. Chill 30 minutes in the refrigerator before serving.

Submitter: 
ITSIE
Description: 
This is a very light yet amazingly tasty coleslaw. Instead of usual mayo this version uses Greek yogurt and the taste doesn't suffer in the least. Very refreshing.
