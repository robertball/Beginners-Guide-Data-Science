Title:
Keto Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6134291.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup cocoa powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
2 1/4 cups hot water, divided
2 cups heavy cream
1/4 teaspoon liquid no-calorie sucralose sweetener (such as SplendaÂ®), or to taste

Directions:
Place cocoa and cinnamon into a medium pot. Add 1/4 cup hot water and whisk over medium heat until fully combined. Bring to a boil and cook, stirring continuously, for 2 minutes.
Stir heavy cream into the mixture, followed by remaining water and sweetener. Continue to cook and stir over medium heat, making sure it does not boil, until heated through, 2 to 3 minutes.
