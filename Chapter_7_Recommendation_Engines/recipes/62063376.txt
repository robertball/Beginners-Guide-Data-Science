Title:
mini bagel pizzas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 mini bagels
1/4 cup pizza sauce
1/3 cup pizza blend shredded cheese
16 hormel turkey pepperoni

Directions:
preheat oven to 425.  slice bagels in half and place on a cookie sheet covered in foil.  spoon on a thin layer of pizza sauce over each bagel half.  sprinkle with cheese and place two pepperonis on each pizza.  bake for six minutes.  remember that oven cooking times will vary.

Description: 
a homemade take on a frozen favorite.  much less fat and still yummy.
