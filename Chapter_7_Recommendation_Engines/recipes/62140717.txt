Title:
Rachel's Crockpot Seafood Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. processed cheese (eg. velveta)
1 oz. light cream cheese
1 1/2 cups sour cream
2 tsp. Old Bay Seasoning (or to taste)
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup cooked miniture shrimp
1/2 cup cooked and shredded crab meat
1/2 cup cooked and shredded lobster meat
Milk if desired for thinner consistancy
Toasted french bread cubes or fresh veggies

Directions:
In a crock pot combine cheeses sour cream and seafoods on low heat.
Cover and allow to cheese to melt and stir occasionally to smooth cheese lumps and mix in the meats.
Once the cheese is completely melted and free of lumps add old bay seasoning and worcestershire sauce.
This is a very thick dip, so you may add milk in a little at a time to a desired thinner consistancy.
Serve hot with french toasted french bread cubes or fresh vegetables.

Submitter: 
Rachel Dobbs
Description: 
One of my family and friends favorite savory cheese dips with delicate crabmeat, shrimp and lobster, crockpot style. Perfect for potlucks and holiday gatherings. I created this recipe several years ago from what ever I had on hand at the time and it has sence been requested year after year for christmas parties.
