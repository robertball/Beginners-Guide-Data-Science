Title:
S'mores Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ea	chocolate sandwich cookie
2 ea	marshmallows

Directions:
Roast marshmallows over open fire as desired.
Set cookie near fire to help soften the chocolate and then split open and place roasted marshmallows inside.
Enjoy!
