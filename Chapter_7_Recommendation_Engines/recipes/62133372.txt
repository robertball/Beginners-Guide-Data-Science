Title:
Salmon Foil Packs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups fresh or frozen vegetables to your taste. (We use broccoli, carrots, red peppers, and onions)
2 cups Kraft Roasted Red Pepper Italian Dressing with Parmesan
8 Salmon filets (fresh or frozen)
2 cans diced fire roasted tomatoes, drained
4 cups rice, slightly undercooked

Directions:
Preheat oven or grill to 425 degrees.
Make 8 foil packets and place on cookie sheet.
Mix tomatoes with the salad dressing.
Place 1/2 cup of rice in each foil packet.
Top with salmon filets.
Top with 1/2 cup of vegetables, then fold up edges.
Spread 1/4 cup of dressing with tomatoes in each packet, and then seal.
Bake or grill for 45 minutes or until salmon flakes easily with fork and vegetables are tender.
See how to fold foil packets here. https://youtu.be/Ueb-2uiDBMI
