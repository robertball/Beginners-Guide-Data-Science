Title:
Rich's Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Canned Pumpkin
1/2 Cup White Sugar
1/2 Cup Brown Sugar
1/2 Cup Butter
1 Egg
2 Cups All-Purpose Flour
2 Tea Baker Powder
1 Tea Ground Cinnamon
1/4 Tea Salt
1 Tea Baking Soda
1 Tea Milk
1 TBL Vanilla Extract
1 1/2 Cups Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
1/2 Cup Chopped Walnuts (optional)
1/4 Tea Ground Nutmeg, Clove, or Ginger (optional)

Directions:
1. Combine pumpkin, sugar, butter and egg.  In a separate bowl, stir together flour, baking powder, ground cinnamon, and salt.   Dissolve the baking soda with the milk and stir in.  Add flour mixture to pumpkin mixture and mix well.
2.  Add vanilla, chocolate chips and nuts.
3.  Drop by spoonful on greased cookie sheet and bake at 300 degrees for approximately 12 minutes or until lightly brown and firm.
