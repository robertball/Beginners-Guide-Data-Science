Title:
corn chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups water
4 cups small diced potatoes
1 cup small diced carrot
1 cup sliced celery
1/2 cup diced onion
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup flour
2 tsp salt
1 tsp. pepper
4 cups milk
2 cans creamed corn
4 cups cheddar cheese

Directions:
boil veggies in water until tender. Do not drain.
In a med saucepan, melt butter. mix in flour, salt and pepper, whisk until smooth. gradually mix in milk, stir constatly until thick.
Mix cream sauce into veggies. Stir in cheese and corn, stir until cheese is melted. Simmer for 10-15 min. Serve.
