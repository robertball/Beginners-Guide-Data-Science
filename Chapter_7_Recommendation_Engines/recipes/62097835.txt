Title:
Monkey Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mashed (real) potatoes
1 cup shredded coconut
chocolate almond bark
1/4 slab parafin wax

Directions:
Thoroughly mix mashed potaoes and coconut together. Roll into 1 inch balls and place on waxed paper.
In medium  saucepan or double boiler melt chocolate and wax over low heat, stirring occasionally. Be careful not to scorch the chocolate mixture. When chocolate mixture is completely melted. stir again to make sure you have removed air bubbles and that the wax is mixed completely.
Dip prepared potato balls into chocolate and place on waxed paper to cool.
Store in an airtight container or freeze.
Variation: Peanut Butter Balls
Just omit the potatoes and coconut and substitute 1 cup Peanut butter and 1 cup powdered sugar.  Recipe is the same after that.
