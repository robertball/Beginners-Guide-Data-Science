Title:
Chocolate Glazed Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990726.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup Bertolli® Extra Light™ Tasting Olive Oil
1 cup sugar
1/4 cup water
4 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips, divided
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 large eggs, lightly beaten
1 cup finely chopped pecans, toasted (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray 13 x 9-inch baking pan with nonstick cooking spray; set aside.
Cook Bertolli(R) Extra Light(TM) Tasting Olive Oil, sugar and water in 4-quart saucepan over medium-high heat until sugar dissolves. Stir in 2 cups chips and vanilla until smooth with wire whisk. (Batter will be gritty). Let cool 15 minutes. Stir in flour, baking soda and salt with wire whisk. Stir in eggs and pecans. Spread batter into prepared pan.
Bake 35 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Evenly sprinkle warm brownies with remaining 2 cups chips in single layer. Let stand 5 minutes to soften, then spread over brownies. Cool brownies completely on wire rack.

Submitter: 
Bertolli Olive Oil
Description: 
These classic, rich chocolate brownies made with Bertolli Extra light-tasting olive oil are topped with even more semi-sweet chocolate for an elegant dessert or family snack.
