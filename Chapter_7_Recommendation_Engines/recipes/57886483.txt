Title:
Stuffed Cabbage Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup water
1/3 cup uncooked white rice
8 cabbage leaves
1 pound lean ground beef
1/2 cup tomato juice
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon worcestshire sauce

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, bring water to a boil. Add rice and stir. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
Bring a large, wide saucepan of lightly salted water to a boil. Add cabbage leaves and cook for 2 to 4 minutes or until softened; drain.
In a medium mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, 1 cup cooked rice, onion, tomato juice, egg, salt and pepper, along with 2 tablespoons of tomato soup. Mix thoroughly.
Divide the beef mixture evenly among the cabbage leaves. Roll and secure them with toothpicks or string.
Line Bottom of Oven Casserole Dish with cabbage leaves.  Add rolls on top of leaves.  Pour sauce over rolls.  Add another layer of cabbage leaves over top.  Bake at 350 degrees for about 1 hour.
