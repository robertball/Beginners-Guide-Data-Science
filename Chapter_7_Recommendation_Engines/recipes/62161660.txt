Title:
Salt Free Seasoning Blend Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 tsp-smoked paprika
2 tsp-ground mustard
2 tsp- dehydrated onions
1 tsp-dried thyme
1 tps-ground marjoram
1 tsp-garlic powder
1 tsp-dry rosemary
1 tsp- ground white pepper
1 tsp- celerey seed
1/2 tsp-red curry powder
1/2 tsp- dill weed

Directions:
Combine all in a spice grinder. Combine until desired texture is reaches.

Submitter: 
ladybuggs5224
Description: 
This seasoning blend is packed with flavor and not salt. Great for marinades or for seasoning grilled meats.
