Title:
Summer Blues Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bags Washed baby spinach leaves
1/3 cup toasted pine nuts
6 Tbs real mayonaise
1 tbs honey
1 bud garlic
1/4 cup seasoned rice vinegar
1 1/2 cup blueberries
1 cup raspberries
2 tbs olive oil
salt and pepper

Directions:
Place spinach in bowl or platter
Sprinkle toasted pine nuts and 1 cup of blueberries and raspberries on platter or in bowl
Dressing:
Puree Together until smooth:
Mayonaise, garlic, rice vinegar, honey, 1/2 cup blueberries, olive oil
season to taste with salt and pepper. let this mellow for at least 30 minutes.
drizzle dressing on salad just before serving (using as much or as little as you prefer) . Toss and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jenny
Description: 
A lovely spinach and blueberry salad. The super creamy and sweet dressing makes this version a little more rich than other spinach salads.
