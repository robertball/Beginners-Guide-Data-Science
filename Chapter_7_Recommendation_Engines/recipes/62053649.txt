Title:
Sweet Potato Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup softened butter
2 cups sugar
4 eggs
2 2/3 cups cooked, mashed sweet potatoes
3 cups all purpose flour
1 tsp. baking soda
2 tsps. baking powder
1 tsp. freshly ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp. freshly ground nutmeg
1/4 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup flaked coconut
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Combine flour, baking powder & soda, spices, salt and set aside. Cream butter 2 minutes, gradually add sugar cream 3 minutes longer. Add eggs one at a time. Add Sweet Potatoes and vanilla, beat until mixed well. Gradually add flour mixture, coconut and nuts to batter (batter will be stiff) mix well. Put batter into a prepared 10 inch tube pan or 2-9x5 loaf pans. Bake 1 hr. 10 minutes or until it tests done.

Submitter: 
Maybelle Miller
Description: 
This bread is a welcomed treat anytime. I especially love it with a good book and a cup of my favorite tea.
