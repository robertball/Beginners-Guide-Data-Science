Title:
Really Good Vegan Caesar Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2363609.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup water
1/2 cup cashews
6 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
1/4 cup sesame seeds
1 tablespoon chopped onion, or more to taste
8 capers
1 teaspoon vegetarian Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1 clove garlic, crushed
salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Blend water, cashews, lemon juice, sesame seeds, onion, capers, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, garlic, salt, and black pepper together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Jonathan86
Description: 
I've made this recipe several times and it has always come out excellent! Requires a good amount of salt and pepper; always taste and adjust accordingly. Dressing will thicken over time.
