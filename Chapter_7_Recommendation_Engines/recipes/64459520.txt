Title:
Low-Fat Bechamel Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tsp margarine
1/4 cup flour
3 cups low-fat milk
2 whole cloves
1 small bay leaf
3 black peppercorns
1/4 tsp salt
pinch of nutmeg

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over medium low heat, melt margarine. Whisk in flour, then gradually add milk, whisking constantly, until smooth. Add spices. Reduce heat and simmer, whisking frequently, until thickened, about 10 minutes. Strain and discard the solids.
