Title:
Instant Pot Chicken Gizzards Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5182098.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 lb. chicken gizzards, washed
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 small onion, chopped
1 tbsp garlic, chopped
4 c. chicken stock/broth
dash of salt
dash of pepper
4 bay leaves

Directions:
Turn Instant Pot (IP) on Saute. Bring to temperature and add olive oil, onions and garlic. Saute for 2 minutes or until onions are starting to become transparent.
Add remaining ingredients.
Turn IP off, then press Pressure Cook. Set time for 25 minutes. Let pressure release naturally (NR).
Serve while hot.

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
Flavorful and filling; buttery texture that melts in your mouth. This quick and easy recipe can be made for both a small and large crowd. Just double in your IP! BONUS: The leftover liquid makes an excellent chicken soup stock.
