Title:
amazing crockpot cabbage rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
3/4 c cooked rice
1 egg (large works best)
1 onion, minced
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
sauce:
1/4 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1 can tomato soup
1 large can tomatoe (crushed, diced, sauce, (not stewed))
1 large cabbage head

Directions:
remove leaves (try to keep them whole) run them under hot water for a moment to soften them up a little.
prepare meat with salt, pepper and onion. in large bowl, add rice, onion, egg, salt pepper.
place aobut a tablespoon of meat mixture in toe each leaf, fold in sides and roll ends over meat. (you can use a toothpick to hold them, but i don't) Place them in crock pot, folds down, so they don't open up. Now, combine the sauce and spices together, place over the cabbage rolls. Cover and set on low for 7 to 8 hours.
enjoy.
