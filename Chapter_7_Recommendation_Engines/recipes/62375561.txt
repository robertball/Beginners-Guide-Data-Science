Title:
Vegan Faux Creamy Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c navy beans, drained and rinsed
1 TB chopped onion
2 TB tahini
1 TB white miso paste
1 TB nutritional yeast flakes
1/2 c almond milk
1 1/2 TB fresh lemon juice
dash salt
dash cayenne

Directions:
Place beans, onion, tahini, miso and nutritional yeast in a food processor and process until smooth.  Add almond milk and lemon juice, season with salt and cayenne and process until creamy.
