Title:
Rebecca's Favorite Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/874372.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups dry elbow macaroni (4 cups cooked)
1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
1/2 cup grated provolone
1/2 cup grated muenster
1/2 cup grated smoked gouda
1 tb Chives
1 1/3 cup french-fried onions (10 ozs.)
1/2 cup sour cream
4 TB butter, softened
3 eggs
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 cup heavy cream (half and half or whole milk work too)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
In a large pot - bring water to a boil to cook pasta al dente (about 10 minutes)
While pasta is cooking, prep all cheese
Drain cooked pasta when done
Put hot pasta in a large bowl and add all the cheese, chives, and 1/2 of the fried onions
In a seperate bowl, whisk the sour cream, butter, eggs, salt, pepper, and heavy cream - mix well
Add the liquid mixture to the hot pasta and cheese bowl and mix well
Spray casserole dish with nonstick cooking sprah and pour the macaronit mixture into the dish
Cook casserole at 350 for 30 minutes and then top with remainder of french fried onions
Cook casserole until onions are golden brown (about 5 mins)... done!

Description: 
We have searched long and hard for our favorite mac and cheese.... this was it for us!  Multiple cheeses make this a very flavorful mac and cheese with a fried onion crunch!
