Title:
Grilled Asian Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Marinade:
2 pounds skirt steak (flap meat)
1/2 cup apple juice
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup ketchup
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 teaspoon chinese 5 spice
1 teaspoon minced ginger
1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
Basting Sauce:
1/3 cup hoisin sauce
1 tablespoon lime juice

Directions:
Pound skirt steak to a 1/4" thickness, and place in plastic bag.
Mix marinade ingredients (apple juice through red pepper flakes) thoroughly in bowl. Pour marinade into bag with skirt steak.
Marinade meat for 24hrs, or at least 8hrs for the flavors to develop.
Mix hoisin sauce & lime juice together and baste meat with sauce during grilling. Grill 5 minutes on each side or to desired internal temperature.
Cover meat with foil after removing from the grill and let meat rest @ 10 min prior to slicing to keep juices from running.

Submitter: 
HockeyMom
Description: 
Marinated skirt steak with a fragrant brown sauce. Grilling on the barbeque gives it a nice smokey flavor. Sure to be a summertime favorite. Serve with grilled asparagus.
