Title:
Sparkling Bees Knees Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1321861.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 oz Tanqueray London Dry Gin
3/4  oz lemon juice
3/4 oz. honey
Champagne

Directions:
Combine Tanqueray London Dry Gin, lemon juice and honey in a cocktail shaker. Shake and pour into a coupe or flute glass. Top with champagne. Garnish with a lemon twist and maraschino cherry.
