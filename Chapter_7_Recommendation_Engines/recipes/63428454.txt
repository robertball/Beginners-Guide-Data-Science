Title:
Oven Baked Orange Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 chicken breasts
1/4 c butter, melted
1 6 oz can frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
1/4 c honey
3/4 c fine bread crumbs
1/2 c flour
1 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Brush chicken breast with butter.
Combine  orange juice and honey in a dish.
Mix dry ingredients in a dish.
Roll chicken first in honey mixture, then in crumb mixture.
Place in large greased baking dish and drizzle with any remaining honey mixture.
Bake at 375 degrees for 45-50 minutes, until no longer pink.
