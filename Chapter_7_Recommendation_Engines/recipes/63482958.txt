Title:
spinach balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-10 oz boxes frozen  chopped spinach, cooked and drained
2 1/2 cups herb stuffing mix
3/4 cups marg.
4 eggs
3/4 cup parmesan cheese
1 large onion, minced
1 garlic clove, minced
3/4 tsp, thyme

Directions:
mixed all ingredients together. roll into 1" balls. place on greased cookie sheet.  bake at 450 degrees for 20mins. serve warm
