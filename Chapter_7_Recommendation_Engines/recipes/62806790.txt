Title:
Garlic Teriyaki Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/659082.jpg
Ingredients:
Boil in water
1 box of whole grain linguini
8 cups of water
2 tbsp minced garlic
1 tsp crushed red pepper
.5 tbsp wok oil
.5 tbsp low sodium soy sauce
After boiling
3 tbsp Kikoman Teriyaki baste and glaze
2 tsp brown sugar
.5 tbsp wok oil

Directions:
Put water in large pot, add wok oil, celery flakes, crushed red pepper and soy sauce and bring to boil. Add noodles and return to a boil.
Drain noodles and return to pan. Add remaining wok oil, 2 tbsp brown sugar and 3 tbsp of teriyaki baste and glaze. Stir and serve.
Makes 7 half cup servings

Submitter: 
Sarah Meedel
Description: 
A great side dish full of flavor and whole grains. Goes great served under stir frys and other Asian dishes.
