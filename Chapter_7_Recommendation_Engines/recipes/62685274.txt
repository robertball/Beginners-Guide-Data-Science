Title:
Super Healthy Omelette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/603156.jpg
Ingredients:
* 3 eggs or 1 egg, 2 egg whites
* skim milk (just enough to make a good mix with egg)
* onion~desired amount
* tomato~desired amount
* mushrooms~desired amount
* spinach~desired amount
* green/red peppers~desired amount
* garlic if wanted
* low fat cheese(grated over so you use less)
* sea salt & pepper

Directions:
1. mix eggs and milk in seperate bowl
2. saute all veggies in grape seed oil~set aside
3. put a small amount of extra virgin coconut oil into pan and add egg mixture
4. sprinkle salt & pepper to taste
5. let the egg almost cook all the way through and then grate cheese over entire egg~not to much :)
6. then add sauted veggies onto one side of egg mixture
7. flip other side of egg over the stuffed side
8. let cook for a minute until golden
9. remove from heat and serve

Submitter:

Description: 
I make this for my breakie and it is so yummy, filling and healthy! It makes enough for two people and is a great start to the day!
