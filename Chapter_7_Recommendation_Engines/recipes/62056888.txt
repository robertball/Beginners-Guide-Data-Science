Title:
Candy bar chocolate cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box mix German chocolate cake
14 oz. Eagle Brand sweetened condensed milk
6 large Heath candy bars
Large container whipped cream

Directions:
Prepare cake mix and pour into 9x13 pan.  Bake as directed.  While cake is still warm, poke holes in the cake and pour sweetened condensed milk over the cake.  Crumble 3 large Heath bars and sprinkle on top of cake.  Layer whipped cream on top of cake and crumble the remaining 3 Heath bars on top.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
This is so easy and so delicious!  You can substitute other candy bars.  This cake is extremely moist and to die for!
