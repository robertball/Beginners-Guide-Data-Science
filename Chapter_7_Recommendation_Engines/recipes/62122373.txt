Title:
Easy Chocolate-Caramel Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box devil's food chocolate cake mix
1 bottle caramel sauce
1 tub cool whip
4-6 Butterfinger or Toffee candy bars

Directions:
Bake the cake as directed
While still hot, using the end of a wooden spoon, poke holes all over the top of the cake.
Fill each hole with caramel sauce.
As the sauce soaks into the cake keep filling the holes until the whole bottle is gone.
Let cake cool completely.
Top with entire tub of cool whip.
Crush candy bars in a plastic baggy and top the cool whip with the crushed candy bars.

Submitter: 
Mallery
Description: 
This cake is moist and delicious, just using a boxed cake mix and adding a little something extra!
