Title:
LowRider Crab Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup day old bread crumbs
1/2 teaspoon celery seed
1/2 teaspoon blackening seasoning
1/2 teaspoon Tony's Creole Seasoning
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 cup 3 cheese blend, Romano, Parmesan, Asiago
1/4 cup chopped roasted red pepper
1/4 cup chopped green onions
1 egg
1 tablespoon mayonnaise
8 ounce container Blackfin crab
Panko bread crumbs
Olive oil for frying

Directions:
In food processor pulse bread, celery seed, blackening, Creole Seasoning, and garlic powder.
Transfer to mixing bowl, toss in cheese, onions, and peppers, mixing well.
Drain crab and gently toss into bread mixture.
Whisk egg and mayonnaise; gently mix well with crab mixture.
Heat frying pan; add olive oil to coat the bottom.
Form crab mixture into cakes; coat with Panko; fry 3-4 minutes per side flipping over gently.
Mixture may be refrigerated overnight.
Hint; do not pulse all ingredients in the food processor or you will have a 'gummy' mess.

Submitter: 
Wilemon
Description: 
These crab cakes are full of flavor and are really good served in large roasted Portobello mushroom caps along side a juicy grilled steak, prime rib, or baked halibut.
