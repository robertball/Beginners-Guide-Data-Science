Title:
Chia veggie Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. kale, stemmed and lightly packed
1 large stalk celery, sliced
1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
1 large carrot, sliced
1 red onion, sliced
1/4 c. parsley, lightly packed
2 T. Dijon
2 T. nutritional yeast
1 1/2 tsp. chili powder, thyme, garlic powder, cumin, and smoked paprika
1/2 tsp. salt and pepper
1 can white beans, drained and rinsed
1/3 c. sunflower seeds (raw or roasted)
2 T. chia seeds
2 T. hemp seeds
1 c. garbanzo bean flour

Directions:
Put kale through salt into food processor and process till coarsely ground.  Add beans and process till mixed.
Transfer mixture to bowl and add seeds till well combined. Add flour and stir again.
Line a large baking pan with parchment paper. Drop mixture by 1/2 cups onto pan. Flatten each portion into a patty.
Refrigerate patties for at least an hour to let them firm up slightly.
Preheat oven to 375. Bake the patties for 15 minutes, till golden brown on top.
Flip patties over with a spatula and bake 10 to 15 minutes longer.
