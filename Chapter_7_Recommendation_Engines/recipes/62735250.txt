Title:
Valley of Eden Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs Yukon potatoes
3 Tbs garlic, minced
2 Tbs Brummel and Brown Butter
1 packet Hidden Valley Ranch Dip Mix
1/2 cup 2% milk

Directions:
Put potatoes in large saucepan with water and boil. Use medium-high heat for 15-20 minutes until potatoes are nice and tender, drain.
Place potatoes back in saucepan with garlic & ranch dip mix
Mix until ingredients are combined, add butter and beat. Slowly beat in milk until desired consistency.

Submitter: 
Chef Eden
Description: 
A creamy, bold take on bland mashed potatoes. This will have everyone begging for seconds and licking their plates clean!
