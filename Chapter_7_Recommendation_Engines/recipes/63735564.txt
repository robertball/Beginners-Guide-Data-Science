Title:
Mom's Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
MIX: 2 pkg Yeast
1 tbsp Sugar
1/2 c Warm Water
2 tbsp Salt
3 tbsp Sugar
4 tbsp Shortening
6-8 c Water
Flour enough to turn out and kneed.

Directions:
Let rise until double in size. Press down. Divide into four pieces. Place in greased 9x5 bread pans.
