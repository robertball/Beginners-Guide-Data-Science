Title:
Raisin Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Raisins
1 cup of oil
3 cups of water
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp of all spice
1 tsp salt
3 1/2 cup of flour
2 tsp of baking soda
1/2 tsp of baking powder

Directions:
Boil the water, sugar, raisins and spices for 3 min. Cool
Mix 3 1/2 cups of flour, 2 tsp baking soda, 1/2 tsp baking powder
Add to the raisin mixture.
Pour in a greased 9x13 pan
Bake at 375 for 45 min.
Let stand in the oven for 15 min.
