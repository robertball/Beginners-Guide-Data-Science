Title:
Lake Charles Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Lake Charles Dip
10 min | 10 min prep
SERVES 24
•	4 cups sour cream
•	4 (.6 ounce) package Good Seasonings Italian salad dressing mix
•	4 tablespoon mayonnaise
•	4 tablespoon lemon juice
•	2 avocado, diced
•	2 tomato, seeded & diced
•	4 dash Tabasco sauce or hot sauce

Directions:
Mix sour cream, Italian salad dressing mix, mayonnaise, hot sauce and lemon juice together in glass bowl. Carefully fold in tomatoes and avocado. Chill before serving.
