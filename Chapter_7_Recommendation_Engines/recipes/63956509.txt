Title:
Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces cream cheese
8 ounces crab meat
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 dash garlic powder
1 teaspoon milk
2 teaspoons mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
4 ounces cheddar cheese, shredded, divided
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Mix cream cheese, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, milk, mayonnaise, sour cream, dry mustard and half of shredded cheddar.  Gently fold in crab meat.  Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.  Sprinkle with remaining shredded cheddar, and bake for another 10 minutes.

Description: 
Rich crab dip, delicious with crackers.
