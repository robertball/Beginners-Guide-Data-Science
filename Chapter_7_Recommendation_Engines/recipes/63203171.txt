Title:
Easy Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/828612.jpg
Ingredients:
1 boneless, skinless chicken breast (seasoned with salt ad pepper)
1/2 tbsp vegetable oil for cooking the chicken
1/2 lettuce head
1/2 cucumber
1/2 red paprika
1 tomato
A handfull of roasted peanut
Salad Dressing:
1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
1 tsp dijon/yellow mustard
1 tsp dried basil
1/2 tsp splenda
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all the salad dressing ingredient in a small jar, put the lid on and shake it very well until all are emulsified. Set aside.
Cut the vegetables and put it in the refrigerator to keep it fresh.
Cook the chicken with a little oil until the chicken is slightly brown. Make sure it is cooked in the inside. Remove the chicken and cut into bite size.
Toss the vegetables with the dressing, add the roasted peanut and chicken. Serve and enjoy.
