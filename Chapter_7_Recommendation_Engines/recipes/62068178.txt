Title:
Curried Cashew, Pear and Grape Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Nuts:
3/4 cup cashews, toasted
1 tbsp melted butter
1 tsp chopped fresh rosemary
1 tsp curry powder
1 tbsp brown sugar
1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
Dressing:
3 tbsp white wine vinegar
3 tbsp dijon mustard
2 tbsp honey
1/2 cup olive oil
salt/pepper to taste
Salad:
1 bag mixed greens (12 oz)
1/2 Bosc pear, sliced thinly
1/2 cup halved red grapes
4 strips cooked chopped bacon

Directions:
Nuts:
Toast nuts in a dry skillet.
Mix other ingredients together and then add the toasted nuts while they're still warm. Stir to coat.
Dressing:
Mix first three ingredients together and then whisk the oil in slowly. Add salt and pepper to taste.
Salad:
Mix all up, add as much of the dressing as you'd like (I often serve it on the side) and toss.  Sprinkle with nuts.

Submitter:

Description: 
Whenever I make this salad for guests, I'm always asked for the recipe and my family always requests this.  We call it The Good Salad.
