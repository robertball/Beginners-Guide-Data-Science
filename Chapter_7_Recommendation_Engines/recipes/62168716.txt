Title:
Bismark Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp. butter
1/2 c. milk
1/2 c. flour
2 eggs
Confectioners sugar or maple syrup to taste

Directions:
Place butter in a cast iron or other heavy skillet and place in the oven.  Preheat oven to 475.  Carefully remove skillet from oven when butter starts to bubble and turn slightly brown.  Lightly mix flour, milk, and eggs to make a batter. It will be lumpy. Add batter to melted butter in pan and bake for 12 minutes. Remove from pan and place on plate. If preferred, sprinkle with sugar or add syrup to taste.

Submitter: 
Miss Amy
Description: 
The delicious pancakes cook in the oven and are the perfect pairing for fresh sliced peaches, nectarines or any light fruit.
