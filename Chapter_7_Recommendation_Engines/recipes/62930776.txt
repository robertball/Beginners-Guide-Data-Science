Title:
Gooey caramel cinnamon rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg yeast
2 tbl warm water
3/4 c buttermilk
1/4 c vegetable oil
2 1/2 c flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking soda
10 tbl butter, melted and divided
1 1/2 c brown sugar, divided
1 tsp cinnamon
2 tbl molasses

Directions:
1.  Mix together yeast and water in medium bowl.  Let stand 5 minutes.
2.  Whisk in oil and buttermilk.
3.  Stir in salt, baking soda, and flour.  Knead until smooth and elastic, about 10 minutes.  Let dough rest about 15 minutes.
4.  Meanwhile, melt 6 tbl butter in small bowl.  Whisk in 3/4 c brown sugar and molasses and pour mixture into greased 9X13 pan.
5.  Melt remaining butter in small bowl.  Add remaining brown sugar and cinnamon and mix well.  Set aside.
6.  Roll out dough on a well-floured surface into a rectangle about 12" long and 1/4" thick.  Spread cinnamon mixture over dough in an even layer.
7.  Roll up rectangle lengthwise into a 12"-long snake.  Using a serrated knife, slice roll in 1"-thick slices.  Arrange slices in pan cut side up.  Let rise 30 minutes or overnight in refrigerator.
8.  Preheat oven to 350Â°.  Bake 20-25 minutes or until golden brown on top.

Description: 
Sticky and sweet, these rolls are a treat to wake up to.  They can rise in the fridge overnight and be fresh-baked for breakfast.
