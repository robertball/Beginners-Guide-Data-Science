Title:
Low Fat Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-2 6 ounce cans of tuna packed water
-3 tbs low fat mayo
-2 tsp fresh lemon juice
-1/8 tsp salt

Directions:
Drain water from tuna and pour tuna into a bowl.  Mix all remaining ingredients well with a fork.  Serve chilled on sandwiches, over salad or with crackers.

Description: 
This is a tasty low fat tuna salad recipe with requires no chopping of vegetables!
