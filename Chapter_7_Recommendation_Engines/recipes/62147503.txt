Title:
Feta and Sundried Tomato Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups Water
1 1/2 cups Olive Oil and 2 tbsp olive oil for brushing crust
3 3/4 cups Bread Flour
1 1/2 tbsp Sugar
1 1/2 tsp Salt
1 tsp Basil
1 1/2 tsp Active Dry Yeast
10 oz Marinara Sauce
2 cloves Garlic, minced
6 oz Mozzerella cheese, shredded
8 oz (approx) Feta cheese, crumbled
3 cups Sundried Tomatoes, blotted of oil and sliced thinly
Pepper and Oregano, to taste

Directions:
Add water, olive oil, Bread Flour, Sugar, Salt, Basil and Active Dry Yeast to bread machine according to your machine's instructions.
Knead on dough cycle and remove promptly to shape into 2 12-inch pizza crusts.
Preheat oven to 400F
Brush crusts with oil, cover lightly with foil and allow to sit for 15 minutes.
Apply marinara sauce to the center of each crust and spread around.
Add garlic, half of feta and almost all of the sundried tomatoes to the pies.
Top with mozzerella cheese and then remaining feta and tomatoes.
Sprinkle the pizzas with pepper and oregano.
Bake in the oven for 25-30 minutes at 400F.

Submitter: 
Kristen Mango
Description: 
A tasty mediterranean-style pizza.  I used a 2lb bread machine to make the crust, but you can adjust for yours or buy premade pizza crust.
