Title:
Simple Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup     sour cream
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 tsp     vanilla

Directions:
Blend three ingredients together.  Be sure to get any lumps out of the brown sugar. Leave to set for 5 min.  Stirr again to ensure all sugar is desolved. Place in decorative bowl surounded by the fruit of your choice.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
EBSEPKE
Description: 
This is the quickest fruit dip, simple ingredients with a creamy, sweet and tangy flavor.  Great with any fruit.
