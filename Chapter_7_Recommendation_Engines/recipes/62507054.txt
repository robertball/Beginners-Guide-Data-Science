Title:
Baked Tilipia with Lemon Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the tilipia:
3 fresh tilipia filets
3 tbl. Blue agave
¼ cup lemon juice
1 tea. Sage
½ tea. Cayenne pepper
1 tea. Sea salt
1 tea cracked pepper
2 tbl. Grated parmasean
For the Lemon Cream Sauce:
½ cup butter
2 tbl flour
½ cup lemon juice
¼ cup heavy cream
Sea salt
Cracked pepper

Directions:
Baked Tilipia:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Grease a 9 x 10 baking pan. Place tilipia fillets in baking pan. Dizzle with blue agave and lemon juice. Top with sage, cayenne pepper, sea salt, cracked pepper, and parmasean. Baked in oven for 30 minute or until fish is opaque and flakes easily with  a fork.
Lemon Cream Sauce:
Melt butter in a small sauce pan over medium heat. Add flour to butter to make a thickening rue. Stir until rue is smooth and creamy. Add lemon juice and heavy cream. Stir constantly until slightly thicken. Remove from heat and season with a pinch of salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Crystal Swenson
Description: 
This lite white fish is delicious with the very tangy lemon cream sauce.
