Title:
Dorothy's  Special Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. Crisco
1 C. butter
6 cups confectioners' sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons vanilla
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream shortening until fluffy.  Add sugar, and continue creaming until well blended.
Add salt, vanilla, and   whipping cream. Blend on low speed until moistened. Add additional 2 ounces whipping cream if necessary. Beat at high speed until frosting is fluffy.
If making peanut butter frosting for a chocolate cake, Add 1/2 C. peanut butter to the Crisco and butter when you cream it.

Dorothy Clark

Ideal buttercream for frosting and borders.Modified from: Rick's Special Buttercream Frosting
