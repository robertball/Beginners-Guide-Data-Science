Title:
Tangy Tomato Tuna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
one 8 oz pkg elbow macaroni
two 7 oz cans tuna-drained
two 8 oz cans tomato sauce
1/4 cup sour cream
one onion-minced
one tsp. salt (optional)
1/2 cup bread crumbs
2 Tablespoons butter-melted
one 8 ounce container cottage cheese
one 4 oz pkg cream cheese

Directions:
Cook macaroni as described on the package directions,drain. In a 2 1/2 qt. buttered casserole dish, add the macaroni with the tuna, tomato sauce, cottage cheese, cream cheese, sour cream, onion and salt.
Mix bread crumbs and melted butter and place on top of the casserole. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes

Submitter: 
Barb Ross
Description: 
This is a family favorite. Growing up my siblings and I looked forward to eating this on Fridays during Lent!
