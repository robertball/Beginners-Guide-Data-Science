Title:
MY Baked Scallops and Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons butter, melted
1 pounds bay scallops, rinsed and drained
1/2 pound uncooked shrimp, tail off
1/2 cup Panko
Lemon juice
White wine
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Pour melted butter into a 2 quart oval casserole dish.
Saute minced garlic quickly in butter.
Add dash of lemon juice and white wine.
Combine the panko onion powder, garlic powder, paprika, parsley,and Parmesan cheese.
Dip the scallops and shrimp in butter mixture
Drag them through the panko mixture
Distribute butter, shrimp and scallops evenly inside the dish
Sprinkle the remaining panko mixture over the scallops.
Bake in pre-heated oven until scallops are firm, about 18 minutes Place under broiler for 2 minutes

Submitter: 
Robert
Description: 
Delicious baked scallops and shrimp with butter, white wine, lemon juice and garlic. Bay scallops are the little ones and they tend to be sweeter tasting than sea scallops.Modified from: Baked Scallops
