Title:
Ziti ( Spaghetti) Pasta, Broccoli and Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1/2 cup  olive oil plus 2 talb
2. 5 cloves of garlic through garlic press or 4 tea minced from jar
3. 1 large bunch fresh broccoli- wash and break apart florets...cut stem up bite size pieces( can use frozen broccoli but the flavor is not as strong)
4. 1 lb Italian sausage links - mild ( can use ground sausage)
5. 1 lb Rigatoni or Ziti Pasta ( can use other pastas)
6. 2 teas salt
7. 1 teas pepper
8. 1/3 cup grated parmigian cheese

Directions:
in large pot - place fresh cut up 3. and fill with water to cover the broccoli- bring to boil and cook broccoli till tender...use slotted spoon to remove the broccoli from the water set aside...saving the water to cook the pasta in
cook 5. as per  pkg directions ...using the broccoli water to cook the pasta... drain when done......while pasta  is cooking
Cut up sausage...cook  in large frying pan with 2 talb oil ...cook till done med heat...when cooked add rest of olive oil to fry pan salt pepper and garlic and cook till garlic done then add the cooked broccoli and stir...
in large bowl toss all with the pasta
add 8. and toss again...serve immediately

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
a family favorite recipe...so much flavor...easy to make,,,,, can leave out the sausage...secret for the flavor- cook pasta in the water you cooked the broccoli in
