Title:
Hot Tossed Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. stewed or chopped tomatoes
1.2 c. chopped onion
1 c. sliced water chestnuts
1 1/2 c. sliced carrots
3/4 c. chopped green pepper
1 c. chopped celery
1 pkg. frozen pea pods
1 pkg. frozen green beans
4 T. melted butter
3 T. tapioca
2 1/2 tsp salt and pepper (to taste)
3 T. sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Combine water chestnuts, carrots and green pepper in large casserole dish.
Add tomatoes, onion, celery, pea pods and green beans.
Mix so veggies are combined.
Stir together melted butter, tapioca, salt, pepper and sugar until dissolved.
Pour over vegetables and combine well.
Cover and bake for 1-1 1/2 hours.
