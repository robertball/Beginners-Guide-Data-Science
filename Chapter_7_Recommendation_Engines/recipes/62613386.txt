Title:
Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups, peeled and diced potatoes
4 strips bacon, diced
1 cup diced onion
1/2 cup finely diced celery
1 large or 2 small cloves of garlic
4 tablespoons flour
2 packets "G.Washington" golden broth seasonings
(note:  2 packets, NOT 2 boxes)
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
4 cups chicken broth
1 1/2 cups half and half
1/2 cup milk
Shredded cheddar cheese - opt
Freshly minced parsley - opt

Directions:
Cooked the diced potatoes in the microwave with 1/2 cup water for about 10 minutes.
Meanwhile, dice the bacon and brown.  Drain, retaining 1 T. grease.
Saute the onions, garlic and celery in the drippings.
Stir in the flour,seasoning packets and pepper.
Mix well.
Gradually add broth, stirring after each addition.
Throw in the bacon - bring to a boil, boil and stir for several minutes.
Reduce heat, add the potatoes (with the cooking water) and simmer for about an hour.
Just before serving, add the cream, milk and heat thoroughly.
Garnish with cheese and parsley, if desired.
