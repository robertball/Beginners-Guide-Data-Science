Title:
Herbed Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/875869.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T vegetable oil
4 pork loin chops, 1" thick
2 T lemon juice + 2 T water OR 1/4 c white wine
1/2 tsp thyme or rosemary
salt and pepper
2 c. seasonal vegetables (optional): onions, zucchini, broccoli, peppers, etc.

Directions:
Brown chops over medium high heat - 1 minute for each side.
Reduce heat; cook 10-12 minutes longer, turning frequently.
Remove to a plate; keep warm
Add vegetables (if using); stir and cook for about 5 minutes, until softened.
Add liquids; boil until syrupy (1-2 minutes). Add seasonings.
Put chops back in skillet; heat through.
