Title:
Jenny's Amazing Vegan/Vegetarian 'Jello' Shots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 orange-flavored gel dessert snack (such as Snack Pack Juicy Gels®)
1 1/2 fluid ounces peach schnapps

Directions:
Remove gel dessert from plastic cup and place into a microwave-safe bowl; mash with a fork. Heat in microwave on low in 10-second intervals until melted, mashing each time with fork, 1 to 2 minutes. Stir peach schnapps into melted gel. Pour into shot cup and refrigerate until set, at least 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jenny from B'lo
Description: 
A super-easy way to enjoy a party favorite!
