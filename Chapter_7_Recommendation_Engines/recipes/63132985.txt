Title:
Mandarin Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
8 Cups washed, torn spinach
1 Cup diagonally sliced celery
2 Green onions with tops, chopped
1 (11-ounce) Can mandarin oranges, drained
1 (2-ounce) Package slivered almonds
Dressing
1/2 Cup vegetable oil
2 Tablespoons vinegar
2 Tablespoons sugar
2 Drops of hot sauce
1 Teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
1 Teaspoon salt
Dash of pepper

Directions:
Salad:  Combine spinach, celery, oranges, almonds and green onion; toss gently.  Place additional mandarin oranges in desired pattern on top of spinach mixture.  Serve with the dressing.
Dressing:  Combine all ingredients in a jar.  Cover jar tightly, and shake vigorously.  Chill.  (Dressing can be served immediately after shaking.)

Submitter: 
Alley
Description: 
Sweet and sour tasting dressing drizzled over fresh spinach makes a delicious and refreshing salad.  Mix different types of lettuce with the spinach for variety.
