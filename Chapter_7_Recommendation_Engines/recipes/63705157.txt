Title:
White Bean Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth (extra may be needed)
1 (16 ounce) salsa Verde
2 (16 ounce) can diced tomatoes with chiles
1 fresh jalapeno
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon ground coriander seed
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1 can of corn or 2 fresh ears
1 pound diced, cooked chicken meat (can add ground chicken also)
2 (15 ounce) can white beans
1 pinch salt and black pepper to taste
cornstarch for thickening (1-3 Tbl)
garnish with green onions, sour cream, cheddar cheese

Directions:
Heat oil, and cook onion and garlic until soft.
Stir in broth, tomatillos, tomatoes, chilies, and spices.  Bring to a boil, then simmer for 10 minutes.
Add corn, chicken, and beans; simmer 5 minutes.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Description: 
Canned white beans and cooked chicken meat are mixed in chicken stock with tomatillos, green chilies, chopped tomatoes, and fresh corn kernels in this lightly seasoned chili.Modified from: White Bean Chicken Chili
