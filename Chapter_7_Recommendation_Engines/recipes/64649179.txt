Title:
Hash Brown Casserole with Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123168.jpg
Ingredients:
1 # sausage
2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 pint sour cream
8 oz. french onion dip
1 medium onion, chopped
1/2  green pepper, chopped
1/2  red pepper, chopped
30  oz. bag frozen hash browns

Directions:
Cook sausage until brown;  drain
Mix all ingredients together .
Put in 9x12 baking dish.
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hr.
