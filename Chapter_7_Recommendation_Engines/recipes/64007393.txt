Title:
Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1112374.jpg
Ingredients:
LEMON SUGAR TOPPING:
1/3 cup sugar (2 1/2 oz)
1 1/2 tsp lemon zest
MUFFINS:
2 cups blueberries (10oz) *OR* 1 cup blueberries and 1/4 cup blueberry jam
1 tsp sugar
1 1/8 c sugar
2 1/2 c (12 1/2oz) all purpose flour
1 tsp table salt
2 1/2 tsp baking powder
2 large eggs
4 T butter, melted and cooled
4 T vegetable oil
1 cup buttermilk
1 1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
For the topping, combine all and set aside.
Oven to 425, upper-middle position.  Spray standard size muffin tins.
Take 1 cup blueberries and 1 tsp of sugar and bring to a simmer over medium heat.  Cook, mashing the berries, until broken down and mixture has thickened slightly (around 5 minutes).  Set aside to cool.
Whisk flour, baking powder and salt together in bowl.
Whisk 1 1/8 cup sugar and eggs together in bowl until thick (45 seconds).  Whisk in butter and oil until combined.  Whisk in buttermilk and vanilla and stir until combined.
Using rubber spatula, fold egg mixture and remaining 1 cup blueberries into flour mix until just combined.
Using a 1/3 cup measure, fill muffin cups - filling should just about fill cup.
Spoon a healthy tsp of blueberry jam onto the top and gently swirl in a figure 8 pattern.  Sprinkle lemon sugar on top.
Bake for 17-19 minutes - cool in pan 5 minutes, then move to rack to cool for adt'l 5 minutes before serving.
