Title:
Cucumber Ice Cream Sorbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2369054.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large cucumbers
3 bananas
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
1/2 cup lemon juice

Directions:
Blend cucumbers, bananas, confectioners' sugar, and lemon juice in a food processor until smooth; transfer to a container and freeze, at least 3 hours.

Submitter: 
PlaygroundHunt
Description: 
Super fast, healthy ice-cream/sorbet. This cucumber-lemon ice is very refreshing.
