Title:
Fruit Salad with Orange Poppy Seed Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup orange juice
3 TBS. cider vinegar
3 TBS. French's Dijon mustard
2 TBS. honey
1 TBS. Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp. grated orange peel
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup canola or corn oil
1 TBS. poppy seeds
6 cups fruit: such as orange segments; cantaloupe, watermelon and/or honeydew melon balls; blueberries; blackberries; grapes; star fruit and/or strawberry slices; nectarine wedges
Lettuce leaves

Directions:
To prepare dressing, place juice, vinegar, mustard, honey, worcestershire, orange peel and salt in blender or food processor.  Gradually add oil in steady stream, processing until very smooth.  Stir in poppy seeds.
Arrange fruit on lettuce leaves on large platter.  Spoon dressing over fruit just before serving.
