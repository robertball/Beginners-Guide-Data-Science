Title:
Fried Crawfish Tails Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1lb peeled crawfish tails
-2-3tbsp creole seasoning
-1 cup milk
-2 cups flour
-2 eggs
-Louisiana hot sauce
-vegetable oil

Directions:
-Stir up milk, eggs, and hot sauce then throw tails in mixture. Put in refrigerator for an hour.
-Heat oil up to 360.
-Mix up flour and creole seasoning.
-Throw tails in flour mix and then put in oil.
-Fry for 1-2min.

Submitter: 
Kevin...lost
Description: 
Great way to use crawfish that is leftover after a boil. I always peel, vacuum seal, and then freeze what I have leftover. You can of course buy tails at the store if needed.
