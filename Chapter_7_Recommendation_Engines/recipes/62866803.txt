Title:
Tracy Ann's No-Cook Sweet Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/688281.jpg
Ingredients:
6-8 small (pickle) cucumbers
MARINADE:
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 cup apple cider vinegar
1/4 cup water
1 teaspoon celery seed
1 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
Slice the cucumbers with the skin. In a medium bowl, mix the sugar, vinegar, water, celery seed & garlic salt. Pour the marinade over the cucumber slices and chill in the refridgerator overnight.

Submitter: 
Tracy Ann
Description: 
I was looking for a no-cook recipe for dill pickles, but since I love sweet pickles as much, or maybe even more, and this recipe is perfect. It uses those small cucumbers with a tough skin, that don't have enough flavor for alot of salad and deli recipes. Quick, easy and delicious!
