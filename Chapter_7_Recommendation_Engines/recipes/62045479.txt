Title:
Crystal Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3 oz. package lemon gelatin
1 1/2 cup boiling water
1/2 cup pineapple juice (from can of crushed pineapple)
1/2 cup Miracle Whip
1/2 cup whipped cream, or whipped topping
1/2 cup crushed pineapple, drained
1/2 cup diced red apple, with peel
1/2 cup diced celery
1/2 cup minature marshmallows

Directions:
1.  Boil water, remove from heat, add gelatin and stir until gelatin is disolved.
2.  Add pineapple juice and stir well.
3.  Chill until slightly congealed, about 30 minutes.
4.  Fold in last 6 ingredients.
5.  Chill in a decorative mold, or a serving dish until set, at least 3 hours or over night.  Serve in dish or unmold onto plate.

Submitter: 
DONNA2002
Description: 
This is a delicious gelatin salad that is a Christmas tradition in my family.  It has a lemon base, with fruit and whipped cream mixed in.  Give it a try at your holiday table!
