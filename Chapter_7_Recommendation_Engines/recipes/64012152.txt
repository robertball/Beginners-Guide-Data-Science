Title:
Farro with Vegetables and Tahini Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
DRESSING
¼	cup tahini
2½	tablespoons soy sauce
1	tablespoon brown rice vinegar
1	tablespoon sugar
1	teaspoon toasted sesame oil
Pinch of crushed red pepper
3	tablespoons water, or more if needed
Vegetables
1	large bunch kale, stemmed, leaves coarsely chopped
4	tablespoons olive oil
10	ounces cremini or othermushrooms, sliced
½	small savoy cabbage, halved, cored, cut crosswise into thin slices
1       head broccoli cut into florets
1       red pepper, sliced
1       cup pea pods
2	cloves garlic, finely chopped
?	teaspoon crushed red pepper, or more to taste

Directions:
Cook farro according to label directions.
Meanwhile, bring a large skillet filled with ½ inch of water to a boil. Add the kale and broccoli and cook, stirring often, for 5 minutes or until tender. Drain in a colander.
Wipe out the skillet and set it over medium-high heat. Heat 2 tablespoons of the oil. Add the mushrooms, pea pods, sliced red pepper and cook, stirring often, for 5 minutes or until most of their liquid evaporates. Add 2 tablespoons more oil and when it is hot, add the cabbage, garlic, red pepper, salt, and black pepper. Cook, stirring often, for 8 to 10 minutes or until the cabbage softens and starts to brown. Taste for seasoning and add the kale and broccoli, with more salt or red pepper, if you like.
4. Divide the rice among 4 shallow bowls. Spoon the vegetables on top and drizzle with tahini dressing.

Description: 
Make the dressing a day ahead of time for the flavors to meld.
