Title:
Norma's Moms Dill pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 cups water
1c pickling salt
1c white vinegar
fresh dill
garlic cloves
Alum

Directions:
1. Boil water, salt and vinegar.
2. Put fresh dill and 1-2 cloves of garlic
3. fill jars with cucumbers.
4. add 1/4 tsp alum and more dill to jar.
5. Fill jars with hot brina and proscess in boiling water bath for 10 min.
