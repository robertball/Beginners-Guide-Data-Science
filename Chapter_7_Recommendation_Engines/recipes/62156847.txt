Title:
Jalapeno Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
7 ounces Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
7 ounce can artichoke hearts, coarsely chopped (about 2/3 cup)
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 1/2 tablespoons minced onion
1 1/2 to 2 tablespoons minced jalapeno pepper (you can use fresh or canned)
1/3 tsp dill weed
1/4 tsp salt
2 dashes garlic powder
2 dashes onion powder

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together. If serving hot (the way I like it best) place dip in small microwave proof dish and heat on medium power until warmed through.
