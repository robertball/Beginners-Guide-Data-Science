Title:
Hamburger Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 pkg Lipton onion soup mix
1 can Cream of Mushroom soup
1 1/2 cup of water
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown beef until no longer pink.
Add both soup mixes, water and pepper. Mix well.
Simmer until heated through and serve over mashed potatoes, rice or egg noodles.
