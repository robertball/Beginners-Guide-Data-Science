Title:
Garlic roasted chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Large chicken breast halves
1Tbsp lemon juice
1tsp olive oil
4 cloves garlic minced
1/2tsp seasoned salt
1/2tsp ground sage

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425. Place chicken in lightly greased roasting pan. In small bowl, combine lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, salt and sage. Brush on to chicken. Bake for approx 35-45min
