Title:
Smoky Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound dry black beans, cooked (or 4 standard cans)
1 onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
4 ounces turkey bacon, minced
4 cups chicken broth
1 Tbsp olive oil
2 tsp ground cumin
1 chipotle pepper in adobo sauce, minced

Directions:
Heat oil in large pot over medium heat.  Cook bacon, onion, and bell peppers until caramelized.  Add garlic, cumin, and chipotle and cook another minute or two.  Add beans and broth.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat, simmer 30 minutes.  Use a potato masher to mash some of the beans, to desired constistency.
