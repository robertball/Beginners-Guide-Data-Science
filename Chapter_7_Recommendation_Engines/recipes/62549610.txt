Title:
Curry Pumpkin Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T butter
4-5 garlic cloves
1/2 to 1 c applesauce
2 T or more curry
1 T or more cumin
chai spice blend (ginger, etc)
1 t or more cinnamon
32 oz canned pumpkin(Libby's)
4 c water (to desired consistency
herbs de provence
salt to taste
pepper to taste

Directions:
in large pot, soften butter over low/medium heat
add garlic
add applesauce
add seasonings
add pumpkin
add water
add salt/peppr/other seasonings to taste
add herbs de provence (crushed) to taste
simmer 20-30 min
