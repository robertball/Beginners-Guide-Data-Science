Title:
A Little Fruity Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/283922.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbls. pineapple juice
1 tsp. fresh lemon juice
2 oz. red fruit punch
2 oz. vodka
ice cubes
strawberries and black raspberries for garnish

Directions:
Fill a martini shaker with ice.
Pour in all ingredients.
Close lid and shake vigorously.
Strain into a chilled martini glass.
Place a few black raspberries on a toothpick and place it into the martini.
Make a small slit in the side of a strawberry and hang it on the edge of the glass.
Sip and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Lillian
Description: 
A very refreshing martini with lots of fruit flavor!
