Title:
Easy Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar + 1 tbsp
1 cup peanut butter
1 egg

Directions:
Cream sugar with peanut butter, add egg.  Roll into 1/2" balls, press with fork. Sprinkle lightly with sugar if desired.  Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes.  Some ovens may take a few minutes longer.

Submitter: 
KELLY BENSON
Description: 
Simple, quick 3 ingredient Peanut Butter cookies. Incredibly easy!
