Title:
Sauced Beef Tenderloins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 beef tenderloin steaks, cut 1 inch thick, about 1/4 lb each
1/2 tsp cracked pepper
1 tblsp butter
1/4 c. chopped onion
1/2 c. beef broth
1 tsp. dried marjoram, crushed

Directions:
Trim fat from steaks.  Press pepper onto both sides of each steak.  In a large heavy skillet melt butter over medium high heat.  Add steaks, reduce heat to medium.  Cook for 10 to 13 minutes for medium rater (145 deg.) to medium (160 deg) doneness, turning once.  Cover steaks; keep warm.
For sauce, stir onion into reserved drippings in skillet.  Cover steaks; keep warm.
For sauce, stir onion into reserved drippings in skillet.  Cook until onion is tender.  Remove from heat.  Carefully add beef broth, wine, and marjoram to onion in skillet, scraping up any browned bits from bottom of skillet.  REturn to heat.  Bring to boiling; reduce ehat.  Boil gently, unmcovered, about 2 minutes or until reduced to about 1/4 cup.  Serve sauce over steaks.
