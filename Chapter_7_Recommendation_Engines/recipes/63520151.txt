Title:
Cauliflower Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium head of cauliflower
3 fresh mushrooms ( about 1/3 cup when chopped)
3 fresh green onions (or equivalent of any other onion you have)
2 Tbs. butter
2 Tbs. flour
1 - 1 1/2 cups half and half (full cream)
salt, pepper and curry powder to taste
1 tsp. tumeric
5-6 large free-range eggs
Cheese if desired up to 1/2 cup any kind.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
Steam cauliflower (almost any vegetable can be used for this quiche recipe) til tender but not mushy. Let cool.
Put in food processor with mushroom and onion and blend until chunks are small but still chunky.
Separate yellow and whites of the eggs into  2 bowls. One large bowl to whip the egg whites into peaks that stand on their own.
Combine butter and flour in sauce pan with half and half over low heat until thick. Let cool slightly.
Then in another large bowl combine egg yolks, spices, the cauliflower mixture,and the white sauce and cheese if desired.
Lastly, fold in the whipped egg whites gently so as not to break them. Using a spatula or wide knife works best.
Then, pour entire mixture into a clean - non=greased glass baking dish. Sprinkly with paprika or cayenne if desired and bake for 30-35 minutes until golden brown on top and enjoy!!

Submitter: 
Christmas
Description: 
This is a great quiche recipe - high in anti-oxidants (tumeric and eggs) and protein which we need every day, especially as vegetarians. It is versatile (you can substitute other vegetables you like better or just have on hand) quick and relatively easy to make.
