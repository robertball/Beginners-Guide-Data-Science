Title:
Double Chocolate Mini Cheesecakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/396291.jpg
Ingredients:
2 - 8 ounce packages cream cheese
1 cup sugar
3 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
Box of Vanilla Wafers
2 1/2 cups Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Use a mini muffin baking pan and spray with non stick oil or line with paper liners.
Mix the cream cheese, sugar, eggs and vanilla in a bowl until smooth and creamy.
Take 2 cups of chocolate chips and put into a microwaveable bowl. Put the remaining ½ cup of chips aside. Heat chips in microwave for 2-3 minutes on medium heat. Stir every minute and heat until melted. Do not overheat.
Put a vanilla wafer in the bottom of each muffin cup.
Top each vanilla wafer with 1 teaspoon of melted chocolate. Put in 4 chocolate chips and then fill with a tablespoon of cream cheese mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 15 – 20 minutes or firm.
Cool and frost with remainder of melted chocolate
