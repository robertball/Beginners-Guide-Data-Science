Title:
Dane's Frozen Peach Margaritas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2553687.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups ice cubes, or as desired
1 fresh peach, pitted and sliced
2 1/2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers tequila
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger triple sec
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger peach schnapps
1 lime, juiced
1 tablespoon agave nectar

Directions:
Blend ice cubes, peach, tequila, triple sec, peach schnapps, lime juice, and agave nectar together in a blender until smooth, about 30 seconds.

Submitter: 
Katrina DeFazio Sinagra
Description: 
Peach season equals margarita season! Garnish with peach slice and lime slice. Enjoy.
