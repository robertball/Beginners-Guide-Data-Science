Title:
Banana-rific Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/855656.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Large Ripe Bananas
3/4 Cup White Sugar
1 Egg
1 Tsp Baking Soda
1 Tsp Baking Powder
1/2 Tsp Salt
1 1/2 - 2 Cups All Purpose Flour
1/3 Cup Butter, Melted
2/3 Cup Chocolate Chips (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F.
Combine dry ingredients, set aside.
Mash bananas. Stir in sugar and slightly beaten egg. Add melted butter. Add dry ingredients to banana mix.
Bake at 375F for apx. 18-20 minutes.
