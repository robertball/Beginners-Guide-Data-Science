Title:
Church Ladies Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter, softened
1cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp vanilla
2 eggs
2 1/2 - 3 cups flour
1 cup chocolate chips
1/2 cup nutmeats (optional)

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs and beat. Add vanilla and dry ingredients. Mix well. Add chocolate chips and nuts. Drop by teaspoon on lightly greased cookie sheet. Bake in 325 degrees oven for 12 - 13 min.

Submitter: 
Jean Vitrano
Description: 
This recipe was found in an old church cookbook that belonged to my mother. I have tried many choco chip cookie recipes, but keep coming back to this recipe. I hope you enjoy it.
