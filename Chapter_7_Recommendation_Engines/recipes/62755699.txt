Title:
White Chicken lasagna 1 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 lasagna noodles
1 lb sliced mushrooms
6 T butter
1 T olive oil
2 med cloves garlic
1/3c chopped parsley
6 T flour
2 cups milk
2 cups chicken stock
1/2 t salt
1/4 t pepper
1/4 cup half n half
3 cups cooked chicken breast, cubed
1/3lbs prosciutto/ham
1 15oz container ricotta cheese
2 cups mozerella shredded
1/2 cup parmesan, grated

Directions:
- cook noodles. drain w/ cold water. Saute mushrooms in 2T butter and 1T oil until golden, stirring frequently, bout 5 min. Reduce heat to med-low n add garlic n parsley.. saute 2 more min. Do not brown garlic!..
-sauce- in a med saucepan over med heat, melt remaining 4T butter. Add flour and cook w/o browning for 2 min. Whisking constantly:add milk and chicken stock, a little at a time. Cook until smooth and thickened, about 3 min, stiring constantly. To avoid scorching, remove sauce from heat as soon as it starts to boil. Sauce will not thicken any further once it has boiled. Season sauce with salt and pepper.
- to assemble- 10 by 12 baking pa. Pour half and half in bottom of pan, 4 noodles (or whatever fits) 1/3 mushroom, chicken, and prosciutto. Dot w/ 1/3 ricotta. Sprinkle w/ 1/3 mozarella and parmesan. Cover w/ 1/3 sauce
- Bake uncovered at 375 bout 40 min. let stand 20 min
