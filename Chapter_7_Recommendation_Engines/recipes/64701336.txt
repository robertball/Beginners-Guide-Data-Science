Title:
Authentic Mexican Picadillo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5367033.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs lean ground beef
6 tomatoes
1 tomato, chopped
1 garlic clove
1 garlic clove, minced
1/4 Onion cut into 2 pieces
1/2 Onion, chopped
1 Jalapeno, sliced
1 serrano chile
1- 7 3/4 oz. can of El Pato sauce (yellow can)
3 potatoes peeled and cubed
3 medium carrots peeled and cubed
1/2 tbsp. ground cumin
1 bay leaf
2 tbsp. chicken bouillon
3 tbsp. Vegetable oil
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
In a small pot, put the 6 tomatoes to boil. Remove from pot and add to a blender with the 1/4 onion and the garlic clove with a little bit of water, about 3/4 cup. Set aside.
In a large skillet, add the oil and let get warm. Once warm, add the onion and serrano chile. Cook for about 2 minutes until onion is transparent. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add the tomato and cook another 1 minute.
Add the ground beef and cook for about 8 minutes. Add the El Pato sauce and cook another 2 minutes.
Add the potatoes and carrots and combine well. Cook for an additional 2 minutes and then add the blended tomato sauce, ground cumin, bay leaf, jalapeno and the chicken bouillon. Mix well, reduce heat and simmer for an additional 20 minutes or until most of the liquid is absorbed.
Let sit for 5 minutes then serve.

Description: 
Picadillo is a popular dish in most South American cuisines.  It is made with lean ground beef (or can be made with ground turkey as well), tomatoes, potatoes and other ingredients. Authentic Mexican Picadillo recipe that will bring the flavors of Mexico to your plate.
