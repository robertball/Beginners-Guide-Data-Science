Title:
Pesto and Pasta with Lemon and Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987112.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 ounce) package BUITONI® Refrigerated Linguine, prepared according to package directions
8 ounces cooked medium shrimp, kept warm
1 (7 ounce) container BUITONI® Refrigerated Reduced Fat Pesto with Basil
1 1/2 teaspoons grated lemon peel
2 tablespoons thinly sliced green onion

Directions:
Combine pesto and lemon peel in medium saucepan. Cook over medium-low heat, stirring frequently, for 6 to 8 minutes or until heated through. Transfer cooked pasta to serving plates. Top with sauce and shrimp. Sprinkle with green onion.
