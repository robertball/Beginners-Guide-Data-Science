Title:
Essanaye's Chicken Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/516860.jpg
Ingredients:
1 boneless skinless chicken breast
6 large prawns (ez peel are best)
3 TBS olive oil
1/2 tsp garlic powder
3/4 cup roasted unsalted cashews
1/2 sweet onion, chopped
3-4 green onions, chopped in 1 inch lengths
1 red bell pepper
1 cup snap peas
1 cup large mushrooms, sliced
1 15 oz can mini corn
1 cup sliced carrots
1 cup broccoli
1 cup cauliflower
4-5 cloves garlic, chopped
1 TBS minced ginger
1/2 cup teriyaki sauce
1/4 cup soy sauce
3 TBS corn starch

Directions:
cube chicken and place in large ziplock bag.   Add ginger, garlic powder, soy sauce and 2 tbs corn starch and shake well to coat evenly.  Refrigerate for 30 minutes (optional).  Saute chicken in olive oil and garlic for 4 minutes on med high heat.  Add prawns, cook additional 3 minutes, stirring often.  Remove chicken/prawns from pan and keep warm.  Add vegetables, and remaining oil to pan and cook for 5  minutes, stirring often.  Add teriyaki sauce and cashews and continue cooking 2-3 minutes.  Add chicken/prawns and stir well to mix, cook another 1-2 minutes.  Sauce should thicken on it's own, but add a little more corn starch if necessary.  Serve immediately.  Adjust cooking time as necessary to ensure you don't overcook the veggies.  Serve with rice.
