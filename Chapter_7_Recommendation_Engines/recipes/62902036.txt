Title:
Mushroom Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pints mushrooms - chopped
1 sweet onion - chopped
1 can drained/rinsed black beans
1 can chili beans
1 can fire roasted tomatos
1 cup chopped peppers (red/yellow/orange)
4 tsp chopped garlic
3 T. brown sugar
1 can tomato sauce
3 tsp. chili powder
1 tsp. paprika
3 tsp. cumin
1 tsp. chey. pepper
2 tsp. soy sauce

Directions:
Lightly saute mushrooms, onion, garlic and peppers in 1T olive oil.
Add dry spices and brown sugar. Saute a minute more.
Add the rest of the ingredients, cover, simmer 1hr. or put in the crock pot for 4-5 hrs.

Submitter: 
Cathy Brown-Bergmann
Description: 
Even if you aren't vegetarian, this is a fantastic recipe !  You won't miss the meat, but could add if you must.  I like it spicy, so if you don't, half the chili powder, cumin and cey. pepper.
