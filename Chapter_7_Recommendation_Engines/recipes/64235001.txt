Title:
RICE CRISPIES / PEANUTBUTTER SQUARES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup peanut butter
½ cup corn syrup
½ cup brown sugar
1 tsp vanilla
3 cups rice crispies
1/3 cup peanut butter
3 squares semi sweet chocolate

Directions:
Put peanut , corn syrup and brown sugar in pot and melt over low heat ,stirring constantly
2.  Remove from heat and add vanilla and rice crispies
3.  Press into greased 8x8 pan and chill
4.  Top with 3 melted squares of semi sweet chocolate and 1/3 cup peanut butter
