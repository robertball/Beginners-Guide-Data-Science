Title:
Easy Pots de Creme' Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz. 70% dark chocolate, minced with a sharp knife
1 C. heavy cream
3 T. PLUS 1 tsp. white sugar
1/4 tsp. instant coffee crystals
1/2 tsp. vanilla
dash salt

Directions:
Mix the chocolate, coffee crystals, and dash of salt in a medium bowl.  Set aside.  In the top of a double boiler, Add the cream and the sugar and vanilla.  Whisk over medium heat until the mixture just starts to boil. Immediately pour the hot cream mixture over the chocolate and whisk vigorously until the chocolate is very smooth.  Ladel into 4 demitasse cups.  Chill 8 HOURS or overnight.  Serve with freshly whipped cream and garnish with cocoa powder or a fresh berry.

Submitter: 
Sally Anne
Description: 
Rich and delicious pots de creme' made without eggs.  Perfect for a romantic ocassion.
