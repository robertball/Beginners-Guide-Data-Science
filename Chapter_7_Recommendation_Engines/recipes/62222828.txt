Title:
Creamy Espresso Cool Whipped Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/316235.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) container frozen light whipped topping, thawed
1 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
1 (1 ounce) package sugar-free instant vanilla pudding mix
2 cups espresso (with about 1/4 cup creamer)
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/2 cup unsalted butter

Directions:
After brewing espresso, add creamer and blend thoroughly then set aside to cool completely.
In a medium bowl, cream butter and beat in cooled espresso, pudding mix, and vanilla. Mix  on medium speed until smooth, about 2 minutes. Gently fold in whipped topping until no streaks remain. Spread evenly over cake, cupcakes, cookies, etc.. Best if covered and refrigerated.
