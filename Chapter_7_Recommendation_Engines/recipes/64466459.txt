Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2441627.jpg
Ingredients:
- 200g of chocolate of choice (i.e. dairy milk)
- 200g of marshmallows
- Lolipop sticks/skewers
- Sprinkles (optional)

Directions:
1) Melt the chocolate in the microwave
2) Put marshmallows on the lolipop sticks/skewers and dip them into the melted chocolate and sprinkle sprinkles onto it (optional)
3) refrigerate the now chocolate marshmallows for about 10 minutes
4) ...and voila! You've made Dinomite's Fluffy Bites, which you can now enjoy and eat! :)
