Title:
BEEF AND TATER CASSEROLE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/821304.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1 chopped onion (approx a cup)
1 can (10 3/4 oz) condensed golden mushroom soup
1 can (14 1/2 oz) sliced potatoes, drained
3/4 cup shredded italian 5-cheese blend (more or less depending on your taste)
3 T italian dry breadcrumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F.
Coat 2-qt baking dish with cooking spray.
Heat large nonstick skillet over med-high heat.
Add beef and onion; cook, stirring often, until beef is browned, about 5-7 minutes. Drain, return to skillet.
Stir in soup and 1/2 cup water; cook until bubbly, about 1-2 minutes.
Transfer to baking dish; top with potatoes, cheese, and breadcrumbs.
Bake until beef mixture is bubbly and topping is golden, about 12-15 minutes.
Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
