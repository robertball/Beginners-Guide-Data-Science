Title:
Bacon-Wrapped Turkey Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991834.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups milk
1 (120 g) package Stove Top Lower Sodium Stuffing Mix for Chicken
1 3/4 pounds lean ground turkey
5 green onions, chopped
1 egg
2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
1 cup KRAFT Double Cheddar Shredded Cheese
6 slices bacon

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees F.
Add milk to stuffing mix in large bowl; stir just until moistened. Add all remaining ingredients except bacon; mix lightly.
Shape into 10x6-inch loaf in 13x9-inch baking dish. Arrange bacon slices in lengthwise fashion on top of loaf, overlapping slices as necessary to completely cover top.
Bake 55 min. to 1 hour or until done (170 degrees F). Let stand 10 min. before removing from dish and slicing to serve.

Submitter: 
KRAFT Shredded Cheese
Description: 
Bacon, Cheddar cheese, and Dijon mustard turn everyday turkey meatloaf into a weeknight star!
