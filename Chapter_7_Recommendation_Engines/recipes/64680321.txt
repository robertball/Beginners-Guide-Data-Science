Title:
Brian's Brine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart Apple Cider or Apple Juice
3/4 cup Brown Sugar
3/4 cup Kosher Salt
3 cloves Garlic, crushed
2 Bay leaves, crumbled
1 Orange, sliced
1 Lemon, sliced
1 sprig Rosemary
1 quart water
1 cup Orange Juice

Directions:
In a large pot, combine the apple cider or juice,brown sugar, salt, garlic, bay leaves, orange, lemon and rosemary.
Stir mixture just until salt and sugar are dissolved, and stir in the cold water and orange juice.
Use to brine poultry or pork.
