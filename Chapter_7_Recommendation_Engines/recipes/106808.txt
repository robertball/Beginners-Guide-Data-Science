Title:
Frozen Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985747.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups ice cubes
1/4 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa
1/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips
2 tablespoons instant nonfat dry milk powder
1 cup 1% low-fat milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender in the order listed; process mixture until smooth, stopping to scrape down sides.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Hot chocolate in the summer? You bet, when it's this cool and refreshing.
