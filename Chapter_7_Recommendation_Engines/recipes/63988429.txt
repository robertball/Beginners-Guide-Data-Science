Title:
Banana Sour Cream Bread & Brown Sugar Butter Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1106736.jpg
Ingredients:
Brown Sugar Topping:
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup flour
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1 1/2 tablespoons butter, melted
.
Batter:
3/4 cup butter
3 cups white sugar
3 eggs
6 very ripe bananas, mashed
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 teaspoons baking soda
4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
In a small bowl Mix together Topping: brown sugar, flour, oats, cinnamon, melted butter and set aside.
Grease four 7x3 inch loaf pans.
In a large bowl, cream butter and 3 cups sugar. Mix in eggs, mashed bananas, sour cream, vanilla and cinnamon. Mix in salt, baking soda and flour. Stir in nuts.  Divide into prepared pans and sprinkle topping over the top.
Then place in oven and bake for 1 hour, until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
