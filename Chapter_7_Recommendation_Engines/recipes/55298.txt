Title:
Reese's® Peanut Butter and Milk Chocolate Chip Crescents Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985767.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup REESE'S® Peanut Butter and Milk Chocolate Chips
2 tablespoons finely chopped nuts
1 (8 ounce) can refrigerated quick crescent dinner rolls
powdered sugar

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees F.
Stir together chips and nuts in small bowl. Unroll dough to form 8 triangles. Lightly sprinkle 1 heaping tablespoon chip mixture on top of each; gently press into dough. Starting at shortest side of triangle, roll dough to opposite point. Place rolls, point side down, on ungreased cookie sheet; curve into crescent shape.
Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until golden brown. Sprinkle with powdered sugar.

Submitter: 
Hershey's Kitchens
Description: 
Turn plain crescent rolls into a fabulous dessert in less than 20 minutes! Melt Reese's Peanut Butter & Milk Chocolate Chips over your favorite quick crescents and top with powdered sugar or nuts for an after-dinner treat you're bound to love!
