Title:
chicken whatchamacallit! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 small skinless chicken breats
3 tbs olive oil
1 green pepper chopped
a handful of sliced mushrooms
1 small onion chopped
6 oz egg noodles cooked
1 bag of shredded mozzerella cheese
1 bag shredded cheddar cheese
2 cans of cream of mushroom soup.
1 cup of milk

Directions:
Pre-heat oven 350 degrees
Cut up the chicken breats into bite sized pieces and then saute in the olive oil. when cooked remove and put the chicken pieces into you 13x9 or larger baking dish. saute the oinion, green pepper and mushrooms in the left over oil and you can add more olive oil if you need to. When vegis are nice and tender spread them on top of the chicken pieces in the baking dish. add both bags of the cheese and spread evenly over the chicken and vegis. drain your cooked noodles well and then place them on top of the cheese. In your frying pan that you used to saute everything add the 2 cans of cream of mushroom soup and the milk. heat through and make sure it is not lumpy but smooth and warm. Then pour the hot soup mixture over the noodles in the baking dish. Put foil over your dish and place in a pre-heated oven for 30 minutes to heat through. Enjoy!
