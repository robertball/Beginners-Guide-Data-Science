Title:
Chipotle Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/431838.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Bags Tuna
2 Roma Tomatoes - Diced
1 White onion - Diced
1 Cup Lettuce diced
1 7oz Can Diced Green Chiles
1/2 Cups Corn
1-2 Chipoltle Peppers in adobo (finely diced)
1 Cup Light Sour Cream
1/2 Cup Mayonnaise
1/2 Teaspoon Ground Pepper
1 Bag Tostadas

Directions:
Comine all ingredients into bowl and mix well.  Refridgerate 30-60 minutes.  Serve with tostadas.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This is not the normal tuna salad.  There's no noodles in this one. By adding chipotles it adds a nice smokey and spicy flavor to it.  Its best when scooped up with tostadas and eaten.
