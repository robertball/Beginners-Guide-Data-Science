Title:
Turkish/Mediterranean Quinoa with Dried Fruit, Garbanzo Beans, Olives Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 c cooked quinoa (cooked w/ garlic/olive oil)
1.5 c garbanzo beans
1 c. chopped mix of dried apricots, dates, figs
1/2-1 c olives (green or kalamata)
1 green onion
1/4 to 1/2 c chopped parsley
2 T olive oil
3 T mix of balsamic and apple cider vinegar
1 to 2 tsp Dijon mustard
agave nectar to taste
cumin
paprika
salt
pepper

Directions:
mix quinoa, chickpeas, parsley, olives, fruit, onions;
combine/whisk dressing ingredients to taste
mix all
cover, refrigerate for at least 1 hour
