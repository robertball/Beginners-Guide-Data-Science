Title:
Creamy Baked Asiago Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6873758.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1/2 cup aged asiago, grated
1/2 cup light mayonnaise
1/4 cup low fat plain Greek yogurt
1/4 cup dry white wine
1 1/2 tsps dried oregano
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
Pinch of ground nutmeg
Black pepper, to taste
Aged balsamic vinegar, to taste

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees and grease a 9x13 baking dish. Place chicken breasts in baking dish.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together asiago, mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, wine, oregano, garlic powder, onion powder, and nutmeg. Pour evenly over chicken breasts.
3. Place chicken breasts in preheated oven and bake uncovered 40-50 minutes, or until they reach an internal temperature of 165 degrees. Top with black pepper, balsamic vinegar, and additional asiago to taste just before serving.

Submitter: 
Kim
Description: 
Easy, delicious, and cheesy chicken breasts--with plenty of asiago flavor! Add a touch of black pepper, balsamic vinegar, and additional asiago cheese just before serving for the best results.
