Title:
A Different Carrot Raisin Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large carrots, shredded
1 cup raisins
1 cup walnuts
1/4 cup finely chopped celery
2 tablespoons shredded coconut
1/2 cup non-fat yogurt
2 tablespoons sour cream or more yogurt
1 tablespoon cider vinegar
1/2 teaspoon Brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine shredded carrots, raisins, walnuts, celery, and coconut. Whisk together yogurt, sour cream, vinegar, sugar, and salt. Stir dressing into carrot mixture. Chill a few hours before serving.
