Title:
Tortilla Rollups II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/487947.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) container cream cheese, softened
4 ounces sour cream
4 green onions, finely chopped
1/2 cup salsa
8 ounces shredded cheddar cheese
10 (10 inch) flour tortillas

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together the cream cheese, sour cream, green onions, salsa and cheddar cheese.
Spread the cream cheese mixture on the tortillas. Roll the tortillas and refrigerate about 4 hours before slicing into 1 inch long pieces.
