Title:
Mexican Style Stuffed Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) box jumbo pasta shells
1 pound ground beef
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
1/2 cup water
1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles
1 (12 ounce) jar medium Pace Picante Sauce
1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
1 (6 ounce) can french fried onions

Directions:
1.   Bring 4 quarts of lightly salted water to a rolling boil over high heat. Gently stir in pasta shells and return to a boil. Cook uncovered, stirring occasionally, approximately 10 minutes until pasta shells are cooked through yet still a bit firm. Drain and rinse with cold water. Arrange pasta shells on waxed paper until ready to stuff.
2.   Brown ground beef in medium saucepan with salt & pepper to taste. Stir in 1/3 can tomato sauce, the picante sauce and water. Add green chiles, 1/2 cup cheese and 1/2 can onions. Stir until well mixed.
3.   Spray a 9 x 13 baking dish with non-stick cooking spray. Add a small amount of tomato sauce to cover bottom of pan. Lightly stuff each pasta shell and arrange in dish. Add remaining tomato sauce & cheese. Cover and bake 1/2 hour at 350 degrees. Sprinkle remaining onions on top. Bake uncovered an additional 10 minutes.
