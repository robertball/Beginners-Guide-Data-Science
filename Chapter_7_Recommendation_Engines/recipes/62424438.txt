Title:
Quick Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs red or Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and diced
3 hard-cooked eggs, peeled and diced
1/2 cucumber, peeled and diced
1 tomato, diced
1 celery stalk, diced
1/4 red onion, diced
1/4 cup chopped green onion
2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
3/4 cup olive oil mayonnaise
1 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until soft, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and rinse with cold water.
Combine the potatoes, eggs, cucumber, tomato, celery, onion, green onion, relish, mayonnaise, and mustard in a large bowl; stir until evenly combined. Season with salt and pepper.
