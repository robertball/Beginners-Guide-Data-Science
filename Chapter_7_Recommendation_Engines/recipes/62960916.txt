Title:
Cream Cheese Danish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of flaky crescent rolls
2, 8 oz. packages of cream cheese
3/4 cup of granulated sugar
1 egg separated
1 teaspoon of vanilla
1 teaspoon of milk
1 teaspoon of butter
glaze:
1 cup of powdered sugar
2 tablespoons of water

Directions:
Use teaspoon of butter to grease 9x13 baking dish. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Roll first package of crescent rolls in the bottom of the baking dish. Mix softened cream cheese, 3/4 cup of sugar, teaspoon of milk, teaspoon of vanilla and egg yolk only. Mix well. Spread mixture on top layer of rolls. Place second package of crescent rolls on top of cream cheese mixture. Beat egg white and brush on top of rolls. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes.
Glaze:
Mix one cup of powdered sugar with 2 tablespoons of water. Spread mixture on top of hot danishes. Enjoy!
