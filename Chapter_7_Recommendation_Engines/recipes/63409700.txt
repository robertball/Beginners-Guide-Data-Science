Title:
Beef and Penne Pasta Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz whole wheat penne pasta
1 lb 96% extra lean ground beef
3 tablespoons or 1 package taco seasoning
1/2 sweet onion, chopped
1 cup salsa
1 cup chili sauce
1 cup spaghetti sauce
1 can Healthy Request cream of chicken soup
8 slices pepper jack cheese (or 2 cups shredded pepper jack cheese)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Bring water to a boil.  Add penne pasta and cook for about 8 minutes.
Meanwhile, sprinkle taco seasoning over ground beef.  Brown ground beef in a large, deep skillet.  Add in onion and saut√©.  Stir in salsa, chili sauce, spaghetti sauce and cream of chicken soup.  Continue stirring.
Drain pasta and pour into 9 x 13 baking dish.  Pour meat mixture over pasta.  Place slices of cheese over meat.  Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
A spicy, creamy, beef and penne pasta casserole that your family will love.  It is a great change from boring casseroles.
