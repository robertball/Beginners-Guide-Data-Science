Title:
moosegator approved  mashed taters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 - 12 large Yukon Gold Potatoes
1 and 1/2 bricks of cream cheese (room temp.)
8 ounces or a little more sour cream
2 sticks of real butter (room temp.)
1/3 -1/2 cup of half and half
1 whole medium shallot minced
1/2 cup of chopped fresh chives
1 clove of elephant gralic (must be elephant garlic)
Salt to your taste
Fresh ground pepper
Hungarian Paprika

Directions:
Cook peeled potatoes in lightly salted water until soft
Drain potatoes
beat cream cheese,sour cream and butter until well mixed
begin adding potatoes, using the half and half to make potatoes creamy.
Add a other ingredients except paprika.
Spoon the mixture into a buttered 4 quart oblong casserole
dish. Sprinkle with paprika.
Cover with plastic wrap NOT aluminium foil.
Refridgerate overnight to allow the ingredients to marry.
Remove from fridge, bring to room temperature, bake at 325 until thoroughly heated, approximately 30 minutes.
