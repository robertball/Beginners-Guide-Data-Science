Title:
Easy AND Tastey Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground Turkey
1 envelope dry Ranch salad dressing mix
1 Cup cheddar cheese

Directions:
Mix dressing mix, cheddar cheese, and Turkey until well blended. Form meat into patties and grill or bake until browned.
Add mayo, lettuce, onion. Whatever you like on a burger and enjoy!!

Submitter: 
ReneeW.
Description: 
A Turkey burger that is actually tastey and easy, couldn't be. Thanks to a good friend of mine for making these for our family one evening. I'm hooked!!
