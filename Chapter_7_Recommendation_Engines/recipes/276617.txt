Title:
Sweet and Spicy Sriracha Aioli Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
3/4 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons Thai sweet chili sauce
1 tablespoon chile-garlic sauce (such as SrirachaÂ®)
1/2 lime, juiced
1 teaspoon sesame oil
kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Place mayonnaise, sweet chili sauce, chile-garlic sauce, lime juice, and sesame oil in a small bowl; whisk until smooth. Season with kosher salt and pepper.
