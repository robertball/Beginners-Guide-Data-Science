Title:
Conch and Shrimp Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3506917.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 Vidalia onion, diced into small cubes
1 small red bell pepper, diced into small cubes
1 small green bell pepper, diced into small cubes
1 pound of conch, ground or processed in a food processor
6 small potatoes, diced into small cubes
3 carrots,  diced into small cubes
1 can tomato paste
1 large can crushed tomatoes
1 can tomato sauce
Red pepper flakes to taste
Old Bay Seasoning to taste
Salt and Pepper to taste
1/2 pound fresh shrimp, cleaned and deveined

Directions:
In a stock pot, saute the onion and bell peppers in the olive oil, until softened.
Add the conch and continue to saute for 3 minutes.
Add the potatoes, carrots, tomato products, and seasoning.
Allow to cook for 20 minutes and adjust the seasoning.
Cook for an additional 20 minutes.
Remove from heat and toss in the shrimp.
Stir - the shrimp will cook from the heat of the liquid.
Serve with crackers.

Description: 
This crowd-pleaser is a  variation of a Cuban favorite - spicy, delicious, and filling.
