Ingredients:
3/4 teaspoon dry mustard
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 teaspoon pepper
1 pound ground beef
1/2 onion, finely chopped
1 egg
1 Tbl Worcestershire sauce
3/4 cup bread crumbs
3/4 cup ketchup
1 cup milk
I-2 cloves garlic minced or pressed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In a large bowl, mix together ground beef, onion, garlic, egg, and Worcestershire sauce, milk, mustard, salt, pepper,  bread crumbs and combine well.
Place mixture into a 1-pound loaf pan. Pour ketchup over top and place in oven.
Bake uncovered for 45 to 60 minutes.
