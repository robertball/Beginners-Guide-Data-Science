Title:
Big Ray's Chicken Curry in the Slow-Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1131469.jpg
Ingredients:
10 bonless skinless chicken thighs (2-3/4 lb.)
1 jar (16 oz.) medium salsa
1 medium onion, chopped
1 can (14 oz) chick peas
3 Tbsp. curry powder
1 cup sour cream
cooked rice

Directions:
Place chicken in slow cooker. Combine next 4 ingredients; pour over chicken. Let marinate for 1 hour in slow cooker before heating.
Cook on LOW 8 to 10 hours (or on HIGH 5 hours).
Remove chicken to platter and keep warm, reserving sauce in slow cooker. Add sour cream to sauce; mix well. Serve spooned over chicken with cooked rice.
