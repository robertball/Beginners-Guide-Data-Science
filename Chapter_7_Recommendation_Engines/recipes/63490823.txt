Title:
Bread Machine Dill Wheat Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups warmed milk
1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
1 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons butter, softened
1 1/2 cups white flour
1 1/2 cups wheat flour
1 teaspoon dill
2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
3 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions:
Place ingredients except melted butter in bread machine in order recommended by manufacturer. Set machine to run on dough cycle.
Remove dough from machine onto floured surface - punch down. Separate dough into 18 pieces. Form into rounds and place on lightly oiled baking sheets.
Cover with damp cloth and let rise for 45 minutes.
Preheat oven to 350. Baste rolls with melted butter. Bake for 20 minutes.
