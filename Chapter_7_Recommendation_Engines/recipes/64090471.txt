Title:
Chicken with Orange Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast halves, skinless & boneless
2 Tbsp butter
1-1/2 Tbsp flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp dry mustard
Pinch ginger
1 cup orange juice

Directions:
In saucepan, brown chicken breasts in butter.  Set aside, reserving butter.
Add flour, salt, dry mustard and ginger to remaining butter.  Gradually add orange juice and cook, stirring until thick.
Return chicken to skillet, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes or until chicken is tender.  Serve with rice.
