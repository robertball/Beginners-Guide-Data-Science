Title:
Natalie's Saltine Cracker toffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/869004.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package saltine crackers (about 20-25 crackers)
1 sticks of butter
1 c. sugar
1/2 c. chocolate chips
1/2 c. crushed almonds

Directions:
Place crackers on a foil-lined baking sheet. Set aside.
Melt butter in a small saucepan. Add sugar to melted butter.
Pour sugar-butter mixture over crackers. Sprinkle chocolate chips and almonds over crackers.
Place in freezer for 1/2 an hour-45 minutes. Crackers are done when they are brittle enough to break apart.
