Title:
Pasta Margherita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989204.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound penne pasta, cooked and drained
1 pound mozzarella cheese, cubed or shredded
4 ounces pepperoni, sliced or cubed (optional)
1/4 cup thinly sliced loosely packed fresh basil leaves
1 (26 ounce) jar Ragu® Old World Style® Margherita Smooth Pasta Sauce, heated

Directions:
Toss all ingredients in a large serving bowl. Mangia!

Submitter: 
Ragu®
Description: 
Ready as quickly as you can boil the pasta, this tasty pasta dish gets tons of flavor from great sauce, pepperoni and fresh basil leaves.
