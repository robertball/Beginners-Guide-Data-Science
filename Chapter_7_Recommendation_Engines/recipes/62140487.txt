Title:
Spinach and Crab Lasagna - Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 12 ounce container of cottage cheese
1 - 12 ounce container of ricotta cheese
1 jar alfredo sauce (10-169 ounces)
1 bunch of spinach
1 small package of mock crab
1 container of mushrooms -15 medium sized mushrooms
1 package of lasagna noodles
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
you can substitute real crab of shrimp for mock crab.

Directions:
Put half a jar of alfredo sauce on the bottom of your crock pot.
Put a row of uncooked noodles on next.
Put a layer of fresh washed spinach... Then mushrooms... Then mock crab.
Spoon half the containers of cottage cheese and ricotta and smooth with a spatula.
Repeat noodles, spinach, mushrooms, mock crab, cottage cheese and ricotta.
Add a final layer of noodles followed by the remaining alfredo sauce.
Cook on high for 2 hours and 15 minutes.
Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and cook on high an additional 30 minutes.
It's ready to eat, but if you turn off the crock pot and allow it to cool, it will stick together better!
