Title:
Southern Italian Stuffed HOT Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Cubanella or Italian Hot  peppers, removed seeds save stem top
6 slices stale bread 1 bunch parsley, minced
2-3 cloves garlic, minced
2 cans anchovies in oil
1-2 tbsp. tomato sauce or 1 tbsp. tomato paste
1 small can pitted black olives, sliced in rings
1/2 C Parmesan or Romano cheese
1-2 sprigs fresh basil, torn
salt & fresh ground black pepper
olive oil

Directions:
Moisten the stale bread in a little water or milk and squeeze out.
Chop the anchovies reserving oil.
Put the moistened bread, minced garlic, parsley, chopped anchovies with oil, tomato sauce or paste, sliced olives and cheese in a mixing bowl and blend well. You may use a food processor for this step but omit the olives and add later.
Cut the tops of the peppers, remove seeds and white ribs but save the top with stem attached.
Fill the pepper cavity with the stuffing and put the top on.
Place in baking dish and drizzle olive oil over stuffed pepper.  Bake 350 for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Cubanella or Italian Hot peppers are stuffed with anchovies, cheese, lots of fresh parsley and baked in olive oil. Delicious for first course or appetizier.
