Title:
Mushroom/Asparagus  Ginger Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons butter, divided
1/2 pound mushrooms, quartered
1 bunch asparagus spears, cut into 3/4" pieces
7 cups (about) low-salt chicken broth
1 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
1 Tbsp fresh ginger, grated
1 large onion, chopped
1 1/4 cups arborio rice (8 to 9 ounces)
1/4 cup dry white wine
1/4 cup dry white vermouth
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese plus additional for serving

Directions:
Melt 2 tablespoons butter in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat
Add mushrooms and asparagus
Sauté mushrooms and asparagus until tender and beginning to brown, 3 to 4 minutes.
Transfer mushrooms/asparagus to medium bowl
Bring 7 cups chicken broth to simmer in medium saucepan; keep warm
Melt remaining 2 tablespoons butter with olive oil in heavy large saucepan over medium-low heat.
Add onion and ginger,sauté until tender, 4 to 5 minutes.
Add rice and increase heat to medium. Stir until edges of rice begin to look translucent, 3 to 4 minutes.
Add white wine and vermouth and stir until liquid is absorbed, about 1 minute.
Add 3/4 cup warm chicken broth; stir until almost all broth is absorbed, about 1 minute.
Continue adding broth by 3/4 cupfuls, stirring until almost all broth is absorbed before adding more, until rice is halfway cooked, about 10 minutes.
Stir in sautéed mushrooms asparagus.
Continue adding broth by 3/4 cupfuls, stirring until almost all broth is absorbed before adding more, until rice is tender but still firm to bite and risotto is creamy, about 10 minutes.
Stir in 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Transfer risotto to serving bowl. Pass additional Parmesan cheese alongside, if desired.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
Sauteed mushrooms and asparagus in a creamy risotto with a hint of ginger.
