Title:
Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups whole milk
1 lb sharp cheddar
1 tsp salt
1 tsp white pepper
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp dry mustad
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup flour
1 lb elbow macaronni, cooked very al dente
1 cup soft bread crumbs

Directions:
Zap milk in microwave for 5 minutes.
Melt butter, add flour and spices.  Whisk in flour and cook for 1 minute.  Slowly whisk in flour.  Bring to a boil, and cook for 1 full minute.
Take off heat, and add shredded cheese slowly, whisking until cheese is fully melted.  Fold in Macarroni.  Pour into well butter baking dish.  Sprinkle with bread crumbs.
Bake at 350 for 20 to 30 minutes, until heated through.
