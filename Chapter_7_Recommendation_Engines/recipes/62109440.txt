Title:
Peach and Yogurt Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 peeled and quartered peaches
8 ounces vanilla yogurt
1/2 cup milk
1 rounded tablespoon brown sugar
dash ground nutmeg
ice cubes

Directions:
Blend in blender while adding ice cubes one at a time until thick and smooth.

Submitter: 
Nurse Ellen so not a
Description: 
Fresh peaches are the taste of summer and autumn mingled together in mouthwatering juices. Sliced fresh peaches are combined with yummy vanilla yogurt for this tasty treat.
