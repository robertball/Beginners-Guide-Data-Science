Title:
Aunt Missy's Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cans Campbell's pork and beans (19.75 oz ) ( I've tried other brands it does make a difference)
1/2 Cup onions chopped finely
1/2 Cup ketchup
2 Cups firmly packed dark brown sugar (break up ALL lumps)
Oscar Mayer Center Cut Bacon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Coat a 13" x 9" pan with non stick cooking spray
In the pan, add beans, onions, ketchup and brown sugar.
Mix very well.
Cover the entire top with strips of bacon.
Put in center of oven and bake uncovered for 3 hours.
