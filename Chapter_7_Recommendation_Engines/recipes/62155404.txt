Title:
Pizzagaina Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 lb large pepperoni
1/2 lb sweet cappicola
1/2 lb hot cappicola
1 cup shredded mozzarella
1 2lb container whole milk ricotta cheese
2 eggs
1 eggs white, slightly beaten
4 9 inch pie crusts (packaged or from scratch)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F
place 1 crust in bottom of eack 9 inch pie plate
rough chop pepperoni, hot and sweet cappicola
in a medium/large bowl mix together ricotta, mozzarella, and eggs
Add in chopped meats
divide mixture evenly into 2 prepared pie plates
top each with 2nd pie crust and seal around edges
brush tops with beaten egg white
bake for 45-60 minutes...until crust is golden brown.
Let set and cool to warm and serve
refrigerate leftovers

Submitter: 
SMZCOZ
Description: 
Pizzagaina is Italian Easter Pie.  A very traditional savory dish that is a much anticipated treat for Easter.  It can be quite an undertaking to prepare but I've got a simplified recipe here that the Italian Nonnis may curse me for but tastes great!
