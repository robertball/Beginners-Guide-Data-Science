Title:
Zesty Shrimp and Avocado Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pound of cooked salad shrimp
2 Small avocados
2 Tomatos, seeded
1 Small purple onion
4 Cups cooked pasta, al dante, any type you like
Cocktail Sauce
1 Cup catchup
Prepared horseradish to taste
Dash of Wirchestershire
Dash of Lemon juice
OR you can use your favorite bottled cocktail sauce

Directions:
Cook pasta to al dante, drain in culendar and rinse with cold water to stop cooking process.
Stir Catchup, horseradish, wirchestershire and lemon juice together to make sauce and set aside.  Add more horseradish if you like a spice cocktail sauce.
Cut all veggies into large chunks, onions can be smaller if you don't like a lot of onion in each bite.  Put shrimp, veggies, and cold pasta in large bowl. Add cocktail sauce and toss gently so avocado doesn't break apart.  Chill well before serving to let flavors blend.

Submitter: 
Kim Carvalho
Description: 
A zesty shrimp, avacado and pasta salad that's wonderful on a hot day or any time.
