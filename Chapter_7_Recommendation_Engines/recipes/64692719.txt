Title:
Big Ray's Greek Orzo Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5170732.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box orzo
1 pint cherry tomatoes, quartered
4 oz spinach, chopped
1 small seedless cucumber, diced
1 small red onion, chopped
1 (8 oz) crumbled feta cheese
1 (3.5 oz) jar capers, drained
1 (24 oz) bottle bold greek salad dressing

Directions:
In a large bowl,mix all ingredients together, allow to sit refrigerated overnight.
