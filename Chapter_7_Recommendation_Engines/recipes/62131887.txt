Title:
Oatmeal and Rice Krispies Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 butter or shortening
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla
3/4 cup flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 3/4 cups oatmeal
1 cup rice krispies cereal
1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Directions:
1. Cream butter, brown sugar, white sugar, egg, and vanilla in a large bowl.
2. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt. Beat until well mixed.
3. Add the oatmeal and rice krispies and stir until evenly distributed. If wanting choclate chips, add here.
4. Drop cookie batter by spoonfuls on to an ungreased cookie sheet. Preheat oven to 325 degrees and cook for 10 minutesor until golden brown.
5. Remove from oven and let cool before serving. Great with milk!

Submitter: 
Jessica Radics
Description: 
Sweet and chewy oatmeal cookies with a great crunch!! VERY EASY!! (This recipe makes approximately 2 dozen.)
