Title:
Hamburger Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
3 cups peeled and thinly sliced potatoes
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 tsp garlic
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 cup sour cream
3/4 cup milk
salt to taste
freshly ground pepper, to taste
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef and onion, then add garlic; drain fat.
In a medium mixing bowl, combine cream of mushroom soup, sour cream, milk, salt and pepper to taste.
Mix the soup mixture with the potatoes, then combine all ingredients in in a 11x7 inch (2 quart) baking dish. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 1/2 hours, or until potatoes are tender.
