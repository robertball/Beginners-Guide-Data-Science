Title:
Grandma's Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 hard-cooked eggs
1/2 cup mayonnaise or salad dressing
1 tsp prepared mustard
1 tsp white vinegar
paprika or parsley sprigs [optional]
olive slices [optional]

Directions:
Halve hard-cooked eggs lengthwise and remove yolks.  Set whites aside.   Place yolks in a  bowl; mash with a fork.  Add mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, mix well. If desired season with salt and white pepper.  Put mix in a ziplock bag and cut off one end.  Pipe into egg white halves. If desired garnish with paprika, parsley, or olive slices.

Submitter: 
Linda Peterson
Description: 
So simple deviled eggs that my 5 year old can make them.  We have them all the time in the summer and for any occasion we can think of to make them.
