Title:
Crisp Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cucumbers
1/2 white onion (optional)
1/4 c. + 1 t. apple cider vinegar
2 T. + 1 t. sugar
4 T. + 1 t. light olive oil OR vegetable oil
1 t. celery salt
1 T. poppy seeds
1 t. pepper

Directions:
Peel and slice cucumber.  Thinly slice onion, if using.  In a bowl, whisk sugar and vinegar until sugar is dissolved.  Add olive oil, celery salt, and pepper.  Whisk to combine well.  Toss with cucumbers/onions.  Sprinkle poppy seeds over and toss to distribute.

Submitter: 
S.Michael
Description: 
This is a slightly sweet, tangy, non-mayo based salad.  It's just delicious on a hot summer day!   Do NOT use regular olive oil or it will overpower all other flavors.
