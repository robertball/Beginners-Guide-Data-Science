Title:
pumpkin pie bars Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Combine all but cake mix and butter and pour into 9 x 13 greased pan.  top with cake mix - do not mix.  Top with thinly sliced pats of butter all over top.  Bake 50-55 minutes until center set.  cool and serve chilled with whip topping or ice cream.

Directions:
