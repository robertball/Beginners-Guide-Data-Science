Title:
Chocolate Brown Sugar Bourbon Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/461453.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 inch)Deep Dish pie shell
3/4 Cp. firmly packed Dark Brown Sugar
1 cup Dark corn syrup
1/2 Cp. butter
5 eggs, beaten
1/4 Cp. + 2Tbs bourbon
1-1/4 teaspoon real vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon salt
8 ounces semisweet chocolate chips
1-1/4 cup coarsely chopped pecans roasted

Directions:
Preheat oven and cookie tray* to 325 degrees F (165 degrees F).
In a small saucepan combine sugar, corn syrup, and butter or margarine. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until butter or margarine melts and sugar dissolves. Cool slightly.
In a large bowl combine eggs, bourbon, vanilla, and salt. Mix well. Slowly pour sugar mixture into egg mixture, whisking constantly (do not turn your eggs in to a scrambled eggs, add very slowly). Stir in chocolate chips (chips do not need to melt) and pecans. Pour mixture into pie shell.
Bake in preheated oven for 50 to 55 minutes, or until clean butter knife inserted in center come out clean. *(For a good golden bottom place on a pre-heated cookie sheet.) May be served warm or chilled. A scoop of french vanilla ice cream on the side of warm pie,,,to die for. Warms in the Microwave superbly.
