Title:
My Potato Cilantro Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large potatoes
1 cup of cilantro
1 cup of green onions
1 stick of butter
1 1/2 tsps of turmeric
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Boil potatoes
2. In the meantime, chop the cilantro and green onions.
3. When the potatoes are done add the butter and the rest of the ingredients and mix through.  Try not the mix too much, this should remain in chunks.

Submitter: 
IMANKAY
Description: 
This german style potato salad is very low in fat and tastier than any other salad.  Consists of cilantro and green onions.
