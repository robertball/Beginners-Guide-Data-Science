Title:
Tofu with Black Bean Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/900536.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup chopped or ground pork
2 tsp canola oil
4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1- 14 oz. package of extra firm tofu
1 cup Chinese cooking wine
4 tsp spicy black bean garlic sauce(from Asian market)
1/2 cup canned black beans
4 green onions, chopped, w/ most of firm green stem
1 tsp sesame oil

Directions:
In a wok, stir-fry the oil, garlic and meat until the meat is lightly browned
Add the tofu, wine and black bean sauce, continue stirring for 5 minutes
Add black beans and additional sauce to taste, stir
Add  green onions and sesame oil
Keep warm until served

Fred Guevara
Description: 
An easy-to prepare lunch, or serve it with other Chinese dishes
