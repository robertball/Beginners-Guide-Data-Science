Title:
Robin's Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-900g bags macaroni
3/4 cup butter
1/3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
4 cups milk
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon black pepper
2 teaspoons parpika
4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup mozzarella
1 cup parmesan
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup dry bread crumbs
1 cup crushed saltine crackers
1/2 cup softened butter
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente.  Drain.
In a 3 quart saucepan over medium heat, melt butter and stir in flour. Cook for about 1 minute, until smooth and bubbly; stirring occasionally.
Mix in milk, cream cheese, salt, pepper, and paprika. Continue cooking until sauce is thickened.
Add 3 cups cheddar, mozzarella and parmesan cheeses.
Combine cream cheese mixture with macaroni.  When combined, stir in extra cup of cheddar cheese.  Pour into large roasting dish.
In small bowl mix together bread crumbs, butter and parsley; spread over macaroni and cheese.
Bake for 15 to 20 minutes or until golden brown and heated through.
