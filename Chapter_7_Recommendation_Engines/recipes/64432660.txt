Title:
Strawberries and cream trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can eagle brand
1/2 cup cold water
1 package (4 servings)  vanilla pudding mix
2 cups whipped cream
1package frozen pound cake
4 cups fresh strawberries
1/2 cup strawberry jam
Toasted sliced almonds

Directions:
In large mixing bowl,  combine eagle brand and water.   Add pudding mix and beat well.   Chill until set.  Fold in whipped cream.  Spoon 2 cups pudding mixture in 4qts bowl. Top with 1/2 the cake cubes, 1/2 strawberries,  1/2 jam and 1/2 the remaining pudding mixture.   Repeat the layering ending with pudding mixture.   Garnish with strawberries and almonds.   Chill 4 hours.
