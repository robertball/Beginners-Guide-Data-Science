Title:
High Hat Hamburger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. bacon, cut into small pieces
1 lb. ground beef
1 c. diced onion
1 can Cream of Chicken soup
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1 c. sour cream
1 16 oz. package egg noodles, cooked according to directions - reserved and kept hot

Directions:
In a large skillet, cook bacon, ground meat and onion until meat is browned; drain off as much fat as possible.
Add the soup, salt, pepper, garlic powder and sour cream to the mixture. Heat through very gently.
Lightly butter the prepared, hot noodles and put onto a plate. Pour the meat sauce over the noodles and serve.
