Title:
Baked Ziti With Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/885049.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ziti pasta
3 cloves garlic
10 oz box frozen chopped spinach, thawed
18 oz pasta sauce (used Don's bolognese)
14 oz can diced tomatoes
15oz ricotta cheese
8 oz mozzarella, divided
1/4 c shredded romano cheese

Directions:
preheat outdoor gas grill to 400
Boil pasta to al dente
Meanwhile, saute garlic for a couple of minutes in medium pot over medium low heat. Add spinach, sauce and tomatoes and heat through.
Drain pasta, stir in sauce mix, ricotta, and 1 cup of mozzarella.
Spread ziti in two greased 9x9 baking dishes and top with remaining moz cheese. If using non disposable bakeware, wrap dish in foil to protect it from smoke, etc.
Grill over indirect heat for about 30 minutes.
