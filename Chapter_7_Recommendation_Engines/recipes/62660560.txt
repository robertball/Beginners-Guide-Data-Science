Title:
Brie’s Gingerbread Men Dog Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/591746.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ½ cups Whole Wheat Flour
¾ cups Old Fashioned Oats
2 Large Eggs
½ teaspoon ginger
½ teaspoon Nutmeg
1 teaspoon Baking Powder
½ cup Molasses
1/3  cup vegetable oil
3 Tablespoons Water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  Lightly grease cookie sheets (I use my Pampered Chef large stoneware bar pans.
Place all the dry ingredients in to a large mixing bowl and give them a stir.
Add the eggs, Molasses and oil to the dry ingredients, mix until well blended.  Add the water if needed.
Place dough on a lightly floured surface and kneed until smooth.
Roll to ¼ inch thickness and cut with your favorite cookie cutter.
Bake in the hot oven for 30 minutes. once the initial 30 minutes is up.  Turn off the oven but leave the biscuits in the warm but off over for a further 30 minutes to crisp.
