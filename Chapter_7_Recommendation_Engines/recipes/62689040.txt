Title:
Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
60g(2oz) Butter;
45g(1½ oz) Flour;
300ml Whole Milk;
120g(4oz) full Cheese (Parmesan/Cheddar/ Gouda/Manchego/Elemental (sweeter)/any full flavour)  Grated;
Salt and Freshly Ground Black Pepper;
¾tsp Mustard or to taste;

Directions:
Melt butter in a small pan on low heat, stir in the flour and cook for a minute or so;
Blend in the milk and, stirring continuously, bring to the boil, reduce heat to low and continue stirring until thickened, no lumps;
Add cheese, pepper and mustard;
Stir continually until cheese melted;

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
A simple, basic  but tasty, full of flavour cheeses sauce, readily adaptable for any recipe or occasion
