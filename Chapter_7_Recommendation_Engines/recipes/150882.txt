Title:
Cuervo Nation Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979010.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Jose Cuervo Especial® Tequila
1/2 ounce blue curacao liqueur
1 1/2 ounces pineapple juice
1 1/2 ounces orange juice
1 wedge lime

Directions:
Fill a tumbler or rocks glass halfway with ice.
Pour Jose Cuervo Especial(R) Tequila, pineapple juice, and orange juice over the ice; top with blue curacao liqueur.
Garnish the cocktail with lime wedge.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
When creating the archetypical tequila drink, get authentic flavor with the gold standard in tequila, Jose Cuervo Especial® tequila.
