Title:
Friendship Pie Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
A dash of Honesty
A cup of Love
A pinch of Nice
A spoonful of Smiles
A spoonful or more of Happiness

Directions:
First, take a dash of honesty and mix it with a cup of love.
Add a pinch of nice to the mixture.
Lastly, fold in a spoonful of smiles.
Bake at 375 degrees for 2 hours. (optional)
Cool for 10 minutes. (optional)
Sprinkle with some happiness

Submitter: 
Bambam
Description: 
A wonderful recipe given to by my granddaughter. It is perfect anytime of the year throughout good or bad weather. All ingredients are readily available for you, if you look hard enough. It can be mixed up and served immediately. Baking is optional! Although the number of servings has to be specified between 1 and 300 for this site, the actual servings are infinite! Enjoy and pass the recipe to the ones you love.
