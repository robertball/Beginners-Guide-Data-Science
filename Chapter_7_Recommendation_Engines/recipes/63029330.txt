Title:
Turbo Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup tomatoes
1 clove garlic
1/2 cup chicken stock
1/3 cup cream (milk, nonfat yogurt)

Directions:
Add tomato, garlic, and chicken stock to the Tall Cup.
Twist on the Shaker/Steamer Top and microwave for 2-3 minutes until tomato is soft.
Open cup and add cream, and twist on Cross Blade.
Blend until you have a souplike consistency.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
This recipe is for one bowl, or 2 cups, of savory soup.
