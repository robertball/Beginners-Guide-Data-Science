Title:
Grape Jelly Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6 oz. jar reduced sugar grape jelly
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 1/2 cups chili sauce
1 tablespoon Minced onion, (optional)
2 teaspoons minced garlic, (optional)
2 lb. frozen meatballs

Directions:
Heat meatballs in oven according to package directions. In large saucepan, combine sauce ingredients.  Bring to boil over medium heat. Reduce heat.  Simmer 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Add hot meatballs to sauce.  Stir to coat.  Cook over medium heat about 5 minutes or until thoroughly heated, stirring occasionally (Or, place in slow cooker on lowest setting to serve).
