Title:
Parmesan Ranch Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7-8 chicken breasts
¾ cup parmesan cheese
¾ cup crushed Corn Flakes
2-3 Tbsp. powdered Ranch (use one large package and leave as a powder)
¼ cup melted butter
Cranberry Sauce:
2/3 cup canned whole cranberries
¼ cup brown sugar
¼ cup butter
1 Tbsp. lemon juice
½ tsp. salt

Directions:
Dip thawed chicken breasts in melted butter, and then dip in crumble.
Bake at 350 for 45 minutes
Cook sauce and stir until smooth—serve hot on chicken
