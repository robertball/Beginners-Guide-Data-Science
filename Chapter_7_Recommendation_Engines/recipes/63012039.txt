Title:
7 Layer Cookie bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1/2 cup butter
2. 1 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
3. 1 14oz can sweetened condensed milk
4. 1 6 oz pkg semi-sweet chocolate chips
5. 1 1/3 cup flaked coconut in a bag
6. 1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
oven at 350* ....in 13" x 9" pan to melt 1 in the oven....when melted add 2, evenly all over then  3, 4, 5, then 6 evenly spread out each layer...pat gently after you add 6
Bake 350* 25-30 min   cool before cutting into small squares
I double this recipe can change the flavor of chips to peanutbutter or mix choc and peanutbutter

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
a favorite since the 70's...easy to do - use 13x9 pan to melt butter in the oven- no mixing bowl needed with this recipe
