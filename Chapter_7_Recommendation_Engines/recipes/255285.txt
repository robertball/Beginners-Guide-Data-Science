Title:
Lemon Spinach Mint Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4479534.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh spinach, or more to taste
1/2 banana
1/2 avocado
1 kiwi, peeled and ends removed
1 lemon, juiced
10 mint leaves
6 raw almonds
1 tablespoon maple syrup
1 teaspoon flax seeds
1 cup cold water, or as needed to cover
3 ice cubes

Directions:
Combine spinach, banana, avocado, kiwi, lemon juice, mint, almonds, maple syrup, and flax seeds in a blender. Add enough water to almost cover ingredients; blend until smooth. Add ice and blend thoroughly.
