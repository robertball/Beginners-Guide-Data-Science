Title:
Ham Salad for Two Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/974229.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 (12 ounce) package fully-cooked ham steak, cut into strips
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped sweet onion (such as VidaliaÂ®)
1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
1/4 cup chopped sweet pickle, or more to taste
1 (8 ounce) can diced pineapple, fully drained
2 tablespoons creamy salad dressing
1 tablespoon honey mustard
1 teaspoon dried dill weed
ground black pepper to taste
1 pinch curry powder, or to taste (optional)
1 pinch garlic powder, or to taste (optional)

Directions:
Toss ham, celery, sweet onion, red bell pepper, sweet pickle, pineapple, creamy salad dressing, honey mustard, dill weed, black pepper, curry powder, and garlic powder together in a large bowl.
Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Barbara Zavadil
Description: 
A delicious blend of ham strips and fresh crunchy celery sweetened with sweet pickle.  Suitable enough for a meal in itself or with other salads. Can be served on a bed of lettuce with sliced cucumber and tomatoes.
