Title:
Bittersweet Raspberry and Parsley Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1188677.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh spinach leaves
1/2 cup ice cubes
1/2 grapefruit, peeled and sectioned
1/2 cup frozen mango chunks
1/2 cup frozen unsweetened raspberries
1/3 cup 1% milk
1/4 cup fresh parsley
2 teaspoons raw pumpkin seeds

Directions:
Blend spinach, ice, grapefruit, mango, raspberries, milk, parsley, and pumpkin seeds together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Amber
Description: 
This smoothie is great for in the morning. The mango and raspberries balance out the bitterness of the grapefruit and kick-start your taste buds. Pumpkin seeds add protein to fuel your day, while parsley is great for detoxing your liver. You can substitute kale for the spinach.
