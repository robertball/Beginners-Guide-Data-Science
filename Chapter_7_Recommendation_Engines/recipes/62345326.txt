Title:
Dixie's Hot Mexican Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 11-ounce cans Mexicorn drained
2 4-ounce cans drained green chilies
2 cups monterey jack cheese grated
3/4 cup parmesan cheese
1 cup mayonnaise

Directions:
350' oven.  Grease 9x13x2 pan.  Mix ingredients and put in pan.  Bake for 30-40 min.
