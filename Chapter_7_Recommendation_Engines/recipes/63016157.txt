Title:
Spice Tea Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886746.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup tang
1 cup instant lemon tea
3 cups sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon cloves
1 teaspoon nutmeg

Directions:
Mix all together and add 3 teaspoons per cup of hot water.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
This is a wonderful dry spice tea mix which can be packaged in jars and given as a gift.
