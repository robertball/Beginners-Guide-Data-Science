Title:
Almond Crescents Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup ground almonds
3 cups flour
4 tbsp. icing sugar
1 cup butter, softened
icing sugar to coat

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and knead until smooth.
Form into a long roll and cut into even pieces, each will be a small cookie, so portion accordingly.
Form into crescents.
Bake @ 325 until very lightly browned, about 10 - 15 minutes.
Roll in icing sugar.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A special, almond-flavored cookie my Aunt made for Christmas. A bit time-consuming, but hey, it's worth putting in the extra effort for a special occasion.
