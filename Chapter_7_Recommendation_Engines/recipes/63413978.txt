Title:
Super Easy Barbecue Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 pounds of chicken (breasts or legs)
1- 18 oz. bottle of barbecue sauce (Sweet Baby Ray's)
8 ounces of water

Directions:
pour just enough barbecue sauce in slow cooker just to very thinly cover the bottom.
evenly distribute chicken in slow cooker. (if you have multiple layers of chicken put a small amount of barbecue sauce in between the layers.
cover top of chicken with barbecue sauce.
turn slow cooker on high for 4 hours.
check doneness of chicken with a meat thermometer (minimum 165 Â°F)(may need to cook longer depending on your cooker and how much chicken you use)

Submitter: 
Lauren Barkanic
Description: 
I make this recipe whenever I am in a pinch and need something easy, and with quick prep. My family loves how tender the chicken gets and I love that my little experiment ended up being so yummy
