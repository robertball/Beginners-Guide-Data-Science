Title:
Margaret's Sour Cream Pound Cake (from Duncan Hines website) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Box Butter Recipe Cake Mix
1 Cup sour cream
1/2 Cup Sugar
2/3 Cup of wesson oil
4 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla flavoring

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Prepare a bundt pan with Bakers Joy, and set aside.
Mix first four ingredients togetherin a large bowl with a mixer at medium speed. Next add eggs, one at a time until mixed thoroughly. Stir in vanilla flavoring.
Pour batter evenly into prepared bundt pan. Bake at 350* for 45 minutes.
Cool in pan for 10 minutes and invert onto cake plate.
