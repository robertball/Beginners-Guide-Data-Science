Title:
Don't have toffee have Coffee!!!? Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 glass brewed coffee
1 T. Sugar
POUR MILK
Lots of ice
2 1/2 T. Chocolate (Hersheys) syrup

Directions:
Pour coffee in glass, add sugar and ice. Then pour milk...and add syrup! (RECOMMEND - Letting it sit for a couple of minutes, to let the ice disolve 4 a better drink!!)
