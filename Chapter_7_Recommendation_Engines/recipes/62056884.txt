Title:
White Cheese Chili con Queso Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 2lb. package of white Velveeta
1 - 16 oz. jar of salsa
1 - 4.5 oz. can of diced green chilis
1 - 8 oz. package of cream cheese
.75 lbs. of ground beef
salt, pepper and garlic to taste

Directions:
1.  Cut Velveeta into small cubes and heat in a crock pot
2.  Brown ground beef and add garlic, salt and peper to taste
3.  Once cheese is completely melted, add cream cheese and allow to melt
4.  Add ground beef
5.  For a thinner sauce, add entire jar of salsa and entire can of diced green chilis.  For a thicker sauce, strain the liquid from both the salsa and the diced green chilis before adding to the cheese.
6.  Stir until all ingredients are completely incorporated.  Allow dip to heat all the way through.
7.  Serve with warm tortillas and tortilla chips

Submitter: 
EPRUETZ
Description: 
White cheese and beef, along with salsa and green chilis make this warm dip a hit at parties!
