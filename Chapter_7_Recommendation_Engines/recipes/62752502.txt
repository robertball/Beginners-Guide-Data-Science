Title:
Cranberry Orange Bread (For Bread Machine) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•2 teaspoons yeast
•3 cups all purpose flour
•1 1/2 teaspoons salt
•3/4 cup plain yogurt
•3 teaspoons butter
•3 tablespoons honey
•1/2 cup warm water
•1 teaspoon orange oil
•1 cup dried cranberries

Directions:
Follow your bread machine's directions to add ingredients. Set bread machine on a light crust setting and press "start."
