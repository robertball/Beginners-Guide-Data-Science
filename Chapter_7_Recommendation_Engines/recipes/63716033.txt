Title:
Choco-Coco Bliss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 parts Stoli Chocolat/Coconut Vodka
1 part Cherry Pomegranate juice (or your favorite Cranberry juice mix)
3/4 part simple syrup
juice of 1/2 fresh lime

Directions:
Place ice in shaker.  Add ingredients.  Shake until the outside of the shaker is frosty and pour into martini glass.

Submitter: 
Dutchgirl
Description: 
The original recipe for this appears on the back of the Stoli bottle.  I adjusted the amounts a bit, increasing both the juice and the simple syrup by 1/4 part.  It is a lovely drink.
