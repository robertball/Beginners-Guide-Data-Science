Title:
Perfect Chewy Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup softened butter(or margarine)
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla
2 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
2 1/2 cups oatmeal
1 cup raisins, soaked and drained

Directions:
Cream butter, add sugars, egg milk and vanilla, mix well, slowly add flour, soda and cinnamon, mix well.  Stir in oatmeal and raisins by hand.  Drop by teaspoonful onto greased cokkie sheet and bake in preheaetd 400 oven for 10 minutes.  Move to cooling rack and enjoy, even when warm!
You can also add chopped walnuts, skip the raisins, or add dried cranberries for variety!
