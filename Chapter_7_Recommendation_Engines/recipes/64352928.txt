Title:
Simple Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbs butter
1 C arborio rice
4-5 cups warm water
1 tub knorr homestyle stock; chicken flavor
1tbs Parmesan cheese
2 tbs half and half (optional)
Black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium skillet melt butter over medium heat; add rice. Fry for two minutes.  Add 1 cup of water and Knorr chicken stock. Stir until liquid is absorbed.
Continue stirring and adding water until rice is absorbed. One cup at a time.p
Once rice is finished cooking stir in Parmesan cheese and half and half.
Reduce heat to simmer. Sprinkle with black pepper.
