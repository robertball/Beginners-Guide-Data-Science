Title:
Cajun Stewed Tomatos and Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups rice, cooked.
1 can (28 ounces) whole tomotoes
1 can (15 ounce) tomato sauce
1 medium Onion, chopped
1 medium green pepper, chopped
1 teaspoon garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cajun seasoning
1 pound cajun smoked sausage

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, saute onion, green pepper and garlic until tender.
Cut sausage into 1/2 inch slices. Add to onion mixture and stir for 1-2 minutes.  Add canned tomatoes and sauce. Add 2 cups water warm water and stir. Simmer on medium high heat for 30 minutes. Season with salt and creole cajun seasoning.
Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Brenda Laughlin Sumner
Description: 
Growing up in South Louisiana, this recipe was something my mother made weekly.  It's inexpensive and hearty for a cold winter day.
