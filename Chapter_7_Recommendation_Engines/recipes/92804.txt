Title:
Golden Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980267.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 Pillsbury® refrigerated pie crust, softened as directed on box
Filling:
1/3 cup packed brown sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons all-purpose flour
1 1/4 cups light corn syrup
1 1/4 teaspoons vanilla
3 eggs
1 1/2 cups chopped pecan halves
2 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees F. Place pie crust in 9-inch glass pie pan as directed on box for One-Crust Filled Pie.
In large bowl, mix brown sugar, flour, corn syrup, vanilla and eggs until well blended. Stir in pecans and butter. Pour into crust-lined pan.
Bake 40 to 50 minutes or until filling is puffed and pie is golden brown. Cool completely, about 2 hours. Store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Pillsbury®
Description: 
A classic pecan pie with lots of pecans is nestled in a brown sugar custard and baked in a flaky pastry. Mmm!
