Title:
Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tub cream cheese with onion & chives
1 can meduim shrimp, well drained
1 TBS mayonaise (do not use miracle whip)
1-2 TBS dill weed
5-6 drops Tabasco
1 tsp lemon juice
1/2 tsp garlic salt

Directions:
Mix cream cheese with mayo until smooth. Mash shrimp  or process lightly . Then add to cream cheese mix till well blended. I use food processor. Add other ingredients, adjusting to taste. Cover & refridgerate until serving. I like it to sit at least overnight.
