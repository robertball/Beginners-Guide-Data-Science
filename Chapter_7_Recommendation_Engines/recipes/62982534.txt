Title:
Macaroni and Cheese- microwave version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1 tablespoon butter or margarine
2 tablespoons all purpose flour
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1 1/2 cups milk
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
7 oz package elbow macaroni

Directions:
Place 1 tablespoon butter in a small bowl. Microwave for 45-60 seconds or until melted. Stir in breadcrumbs. Set aside.
Cook macaroni according to package directions.
Place 2 tablespoons butter in 1 1/2 quart microwave safe casserole. Microwave for 45-60 seconds or until butter melts. Stir in flour, salt and pepper.
Blend in milk and microwave at power level 7 for 6 to 10 minutes or until sauce thickens and bubbles, stirring once.
Add cheese in small batches until melted. Stir in cooked macaroni and onion powder. Top with breadcrumb mixture.
Microwave at power level 8 for 8 to 10 minutes or until sauce bubbles.
