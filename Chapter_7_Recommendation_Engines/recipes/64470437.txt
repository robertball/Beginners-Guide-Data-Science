Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2465568.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine, 1/2 cup peanut butter, 1 cup chocolate chips, 8 oz bag coloured marshmallows

Directions:
Melt margarine and peanut butter. Stir in chocolate chips until melted. Cool until you can hold hand on bottom of the pot. Add marshmallows and stir. Pack in pan lined with wax paper.
