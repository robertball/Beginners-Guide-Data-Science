Title:
Mom's Zucchini Bread- reduced carb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
.5 cup coconut flour
2.5 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
3 teaspoons ground cinnamon
3 eggs
.5 cup vegetable oil
.5 cup applesauce
.75 cup white sugar
.75 cup splenda
.25 cup milk
3 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 cups grated zucchini
.5 cup chopped almonds

Directions:
Grease and flour two 8 x 4 inch pans.  Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Sift flour, salt, baking powder, soda, and cinnamon together in a bowl.
Beat eggs, oil, vanilla, milk and sugar together in a large bowl.  Add sifted ingredients to the creamed mixture, and beat well.  Stir in zucchini and nuts until well combined.  Pour batter into prepared pans.
Bake for 40 to 60 minutes, or until tester inserted in the center comes out clean.  Cool in pan on rack for 20 minutes.  Remove bread from pan, and completely cool.

Description: 
I am trying to watch my carbs so I increase the fiber content and substitute half the sugar w/artificial sweetener. Really, really good and moist- Bread will freeze well, and keep in refrigerator for weeks.Modified from: Mom's Zucchini Bread
