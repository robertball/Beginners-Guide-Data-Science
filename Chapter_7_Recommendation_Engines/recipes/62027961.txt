Title:
Best Ever Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Butter (sweetened, unsalted)  1 Cup
Yellow Onions   3 Cups
Cream Sauce Spice*   10 Tbsp.
All Purpose Flour   3 1/2 Tbsp.
Heavy Whipping Cream  2 Quarts 2 cups
*Cream Sauce Spice
Paprika  1/3 Cup
Onion Powder  1/3 Cup
Garlic Powder   1/3 Cup
Whole Leaf Basil, Dried   1/3 Cup
Whole Thyme, Dried   3 Tbsp.
Whole Oregano, Dried   2 1/2 Tbsp.
Black Pepper, Ground   3 Tbsp.
White Pepper   2 1/2 Tbsp.
Salt   1/3 Cup

Directions:
Melt butter in large Dutch Oven size sauce pan.
Add diced onions and saute until tender.
Stir in spice mix and flour; cook one minute stirring constantly.
Slowly add the cream; stir to combine
Bring to a full boil; stirring constantly
Boil about 5 minutes while still stirring
Remove from heat
This sauce is not intended to be very thick
*For Cream Sauce Spice:
Mix all ingredients together thoroughly.
The mix makes more than what the Cream Sauce recipe calls for.  You can halve the spice recipe or just keep the extra in an air tight container stored at room temperature.

Submitter: 
myfoursonsks
Description: 
This is a cream sauce that has the perfect blend of seasonings.  It has a little zing to it but, it is not too spicy.  My kids even love it!
