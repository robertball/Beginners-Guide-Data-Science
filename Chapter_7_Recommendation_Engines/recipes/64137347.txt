Title:
Vermicelli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Vermicelli Pasta
1/4 cup Olive Oil
6 oz grated Swiss Cheese
6 oz chopped Danish Ham
1 stalk celery finely chopped
1 green pepper chopped
4 green onions chopped
1 small onion finely chopped
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sliced green olives

Directions:
Cook and drain pasta.  Toss with the olive oil, cover and let sit in refrigerator for several hours or until well chilled.  (Can sit, covered, in refrigerator overnight.)
Add remaining ingredients and toss well.  Cover and refrigerate at least an hour before serving.  Best if left in refrigerator overnight.

Submitter: 
Dorothy
Description: 
This salad can be made in an hour, but it will be well worth it if you can make it the day before and let that pasta absorb all that flavor.
