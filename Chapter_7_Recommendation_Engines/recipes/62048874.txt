Title:
No Peek Beef Tips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds beef stew meat
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can mushrooms
1 package dry onion soup
1 cup ginger ale soda

Directions:
Layer in your dish, beef stew meat, soup, mushrooms, dry onion soup and ginger ale soda.  Cover and bake at 300Â° for 3 hours.  No peeking.  Serve over rice or noodles.

Submitter: 
MATTANDANGELA
Description: 
This recipe was given to me by a co-worker and my husband absolutely loves it!  I bake it in a dutch oven and serve over egg noodles.  The meat is unbelievably tender.
