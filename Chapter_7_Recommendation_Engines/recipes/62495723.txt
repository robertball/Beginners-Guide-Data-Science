Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c. flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 c. butter
1/2 c. shortening
1 c. packed brown sugar
1/2 c. granulated sugar
2 eggs
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 (12oz) pkg (2 c.) chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 deg.
Stir together flour, soda, and salt.
In a mixer bowl beat butter and shortening on medium speed for 30 seconds.  Add sugars and beat until fluffy.  Add eggs and vanilla; beat well.   Add dry ingredients to beaten mixture, beating until well combined.  Stir in chocolate pieces.
Drop from a teaspoon 2" apart onto an un-greased cookie sheet.
Bake at 375 deg. oven for 8-10 min or until done.
Make about 72.
