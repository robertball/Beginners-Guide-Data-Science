Title:
Strawberry Basil Bourbon Smash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup fresh strawberries, cut into chunks
1 tablespoon lemon juice
5 leaves fresh basil
2 teaspoons simple syrup
3 fluid ounces bourbon
ice
3 fluid ounces sparkling water, or as needed

Directions:
Muddle strawberries, lemon juice, basil, and simple syrup together in a cocktail glass until smashed together and fragrant. Add bourbon and ice; stir to combine. Top with sparkling water.

Submitter: 
ERIKIM21
Description: 
Strawberries and fresh basil are smashed together until fragrant in this bourbon-based cocktail perfect for summer.
