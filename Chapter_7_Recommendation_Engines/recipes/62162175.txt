Title:
Italian Pizzelle Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 Cups Flour
2 tbsp. baking powder
1/2 lb. margrine or butter, melted (1Cup)
2 Cups sugar
2 tsp.vanilla
2-3 tsp. anise oil
grated rind of 2 oranges (no white)

Directions:
Beat eggs until foamy.  Add sugar and beat.  Add margarineor butter, vanilla and anise oil and beat again.
Stir together the flour and baking powder.  Add to egg mixture and knead dought until smooth.  Roll in 1 inch wide logs or "snakes".  Cut  1 inch pieces and roll in a ball.
Bake in pizzelle iron until golden. Cool completely before storing in paper bags in a cool dry place.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Pizzelles with a twist of orange makes these cookies stand out.
Get the whole family helping with shaping the dough into balls and then baking.
