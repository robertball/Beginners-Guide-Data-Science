Title:
Trishie's Pineapple & Mango Sorbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/666702.jpg
Ingredients:
7 fl oz Water
7 oz Sugar
1 Mango
1/2 Pineapple
1 Lime juiced
5 fl oz Cold Water
1 Egg White

Directions:
Put 7 oz water & 7 oz sugar into a pot and bring to boil stirring constantly and boil for 3 minutes.
Remove from heat to cool completely then refrigerate for 1 hour.
Peel and de-stone mango and cut into chunks.
Peel and core pineapple and cut into chunks.
In a blender puree the fruits with the cold sugar syrup.
Add the 5 fl oz of Cold Water
In a bowl beat egg white until stiff and stir into the puree mix making sure its fully mixed in.
Pour into Ice-Cream machine and follow instructions for "Soft" ice-cream.
When ice-cream machine is finished remove sorbet to a freezer proof container and place in the freezer for 15 minutes to firm up.
