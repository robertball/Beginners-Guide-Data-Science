Title:
Indonesian Pork Sate with Peanut Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
MARINADE:
1/4 cup sherry
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 Tbsp.honey
1/2 cup finely chopped green onions
1/4 cup grated fresh ginger
2 cloves garlic,minced
2 tsp.five spice powder
1 1/2 lbs.pork tenderloin,cut into 1"cubes
PEANUT DIPPING SAUCE:
1 Tbsp.canola oil
1 clove garlic,minced
1/2 cup crunchy peanut butter
2 Tbsp.lemon or lime juice
2 Tbsp.soy sauce
2 tsp.brown sugar
cayenne pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine marinade ingredients.Place pork in a plastic zip-lock bag.Pour marinade over pork;seal bag.
Marinate in the fridge turning occasionally for at least 4 hours,overnight is best.
Soak wooden skewers in water for at least 2 hours.
Remove pork from bag and discard marinade.
Thread onto skewers
Roast in a shallow pan at 350 for 20-25 minutes OR BBQ till done.
For sauce heat oil over medium heat.
Saute garlic for 1 minute.Remove from heat.
Stir in remaining ingedients.
Add a little water if sauce is to thick
