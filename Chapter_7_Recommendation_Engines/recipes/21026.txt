Title:
Snow Cone Topping Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 (4 ounce) packet sweetened tropical punch flavored drink mix powder
3/4 cup water

Directions:
Combine drink mix and water in squeeze bottle; seal and shake well to blend.  Squeeze over shaved ice.
