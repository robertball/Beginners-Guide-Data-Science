Title:
Bacon Chicken Ranch Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 roll refrigerated pizza crust
1 large chicken breast, cooked and shredded
1/4 small red onion, cut into rings
6 oz fresh sliced mushrooms
6 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
1/4 cup buttermilk ranch dressing
1/8 cup sour cream
1/4 cup + 2 tbs. shredded parmesan cheese
5 or 6 basil leaves, shredded
shake garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven according to pizza crust package directions
Prepare crust according to package directions by spreading across a baking sheet
In a bowl mix ranch dressing, sour cream, 2 tbs.. shredded parm, basil and garlic powder
Spread mixture over the pizza crust (if using canned crust, do NOT pre-bake)
Layer ingredients onto pizza; red onion, mushrooms, chicken and bacon
Sprinkle remaining shredded parm over the rest of the pizza
Bake according to package directions

Submitter: 
Ingalyn
Description: 
Yummy kicked up ranch replaces tomato sauce.  Add grilled chicken, onions, bacon, mushrooms and parmesan cheese and you have a new favorite pizza.
