Title:
Roasted Cabbage Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (approx 2lb) head of green or red cabbage, cut into 3/4 inch thick slices
1.5 tablespoons olive oil
2 to 3 large garlic cloves, smashed
kosher salt
freshly ground black pepper
spray olive oil OR non-stick cooking spray

Directions:
Cut core flat, Stand cabbage up on core, slice each piece about 3/4 inch thick. Oven 400 degrees for 1 hour.  Use parchment paper.  Do not turn over.  Pile garlic on top and spray with pam or drizzle olive oil.
