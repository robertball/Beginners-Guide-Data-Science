Title:
Super Easy Mac and Cheese with Ready Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5387898.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pouch BarillaÂ® Ready Pasta Cut Macaroni
1 1/2 cups heavy cream
Salt and white pepper to taste
1 1/2 cups Cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat the heavy cream until it simmers. Season with salt and pepper.
Tear Ready Pasta pouch open and add pasta to the skillet; cook for one minute or until the pasta is heated through.
Remove the skillet from the heat and add the cheese; gently stir until the cheese has melted into the cream.

Submitter: 
Barilla Canada
Description: 
Mac and cheese doesn't get easier than this recipe using Ready Pasta Cut Macaroni in combination with Cheddar cheese and heavy cream.
