Title:
Sweet Potato in Coconut Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
280g sweet potato, skinned and cubed 1cm
120g yam, skinned and cubed 1cm (if not available, sub with more sweet potato)
600ml water
5 tbs raw sugar or palm sugar
200ml coconut milk
1/2 tsp pandan (pandanus leaf) juice or essence (optional)

Directions:
Boil the water in a medium pot.
Add cubed sweet potato (and yam).
Cook on medium low heat for 12 minutes.
Add coconut milk and pandan essence.
Boil till sweet potato is soft but still retains its shape. Serves 4 in bowls, hot or chilled.

Submitter: 
argus
Description: 
It's an Asian dessert delicious served hot or cold.
