Title:
Big Daddy's Fried Shark Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 one inch cuts of shark bites
1 egg, beaten
1 cup cornmeal
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 tablespoon Big Daddy's Chia Roasted Garlic

Directions:
In a small bowl, soak shark bites in egg for 5 to 10 minutes. In a medium bowl, combine cornmeal, Big Daddy's Chia Roasted Garlic Rub, salt, and pepper.
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Dredge shark bites in the cornmeal mixture, coating evenly. Carefully place bites in hot oil; stir continuously. Reduce heat to medium when shark bites first starts to brown, and cook until golden. Drain on paper towels.

Submitter: 
BIG DADDY'S
Description: 
A simple Southern classic! Shark Bites are dredged in seasoned cornmeal, then fried until golden.Modified from: Fried Okra
