Title:
Amazing Chocolate Quinoa Cake! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked Quinoa (cooled)
1/3 cup milk
4 eggs
1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
3/4 cup melted butter
1 1/4 cups white sugar
1 cup cocoa powder
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup chocolate chips
1/2 cup pecans(optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F
Grease a rectangular cake pan.
Combine milk, eggs, vanilla, cooked quinoa, and melted butter in blender and blend until smooth.
mix together sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
Pour in bended mixture and mix well.
Add chocolate chips and pecans(optional)
Pour batter into greased cake pan and bake for 40-45 min.
Remove pan from oven and cool before eating - if you can wait that long!
Enjoy! :)
