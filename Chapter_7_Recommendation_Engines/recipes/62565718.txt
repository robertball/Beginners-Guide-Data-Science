Title:
Easy Turkey Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground turkey
1/2 cup chopped onion (can use frozed chopped onion)
1 one oz. package ranch dressing and seasoning mix
1 one oz. package taco seasoning
1 12 oz. package frozen mixed vegetables (green beans, peas, carrots, corn)
1 15 oz. can ranch style beans
2 10 oz. cans diced tomatoes and green chiles
1 15 oz. can chicken broth

Directions:
In a large soup pot saute ground turkey with onion until turkey is no longer pink.  Drain turkey and onion mixture.  Add remaining ingredients to pot with turkey and onion.  Mix well.  Bring to bowl, reduce heat and simmer on low heat for 20 minutes.  For a spicier soup add a few diced jalapenos.

Submitter: 
L2ENTTC
Description: 
Easy soup to make using ingredients that I always have on hand.  My husband loves this stuff.  He likes to put a big spoonful of left over mashed potatoes in the bowl and cover it with the soup.  I like it served with corn chips and grated cheese.
