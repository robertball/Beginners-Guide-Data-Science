Title:
Taco Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/559582.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 can refried pinto beans
1 1/2 lbs ground chuck
1 cup onions, chopped fine
1 package taco seasoning
1/2 bag corn chips
2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar or mexican mixture)
1 tomato
1 cup sour cream
1 can sliced jalapenos (if desired)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In skillet, sautee onions in vegetable oil until nearly translucent.
Add ground chuck and brown.
Add taco seasoning and 3/4 cup water. Stir until well mixed and liquid is absorbed.
Grease bottom of pie or casserole disk with butter.
Add corn chips along bottom of dish.
Spread layer of beef mixture on top of corn chips. (Add jalapenos, if desired on top.)
Sprinkle layer of cheese on top of beef mixture.
Spoon refried beans on top of cheese, then spread using a knife.
Add another layer of corn chips.
Add another layer of beef mixture, then spread last of cheese on top.
Cook in oven for 15 minutes.
After removing from oven, spoon dollops of sour cream on top, then sprinkle with tomatoes.

Submitter: 
Gina
Description: 
My mom used to make this for us kids as a treat. It's similar to Frito Pie, but without the chili taste. I kept the spice to a minimum for kids.
