Title:
Rhubarb/Cherry Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling:
3 cups chopped rhubarb
1 can (21 oz) cherry pie filling
1 cup sugar
4 TBL butter
Crust:
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Spread fruit om a 13x9x2" baking pan. Sprinkle with sugar and dot with butter. For crust, cream shortening and sugar in a mixing bowl. Add egg and beat well. Combine flour and baking powder; add alternately with milk to creamed mixture. Pour over fruit, bake at 350 degrees for 50-60 minutes. Yield about 12 servings.
