Title:
Mom's Coleslaw Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head green cabbage, thinly sliced
1 large carrot, shredded
3 scallions, sliced thin
1 tsp mustard seed
1/2 tsp celery seed
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup cider vinegar

Directions:
Mix together oil, vinegar, sugar, mustard seed, celery seed, and salt.  Mix together cabbage and carrot.  Pour vinegar mixture over cabbage mixture and toss to combine.  Serve immediately.
