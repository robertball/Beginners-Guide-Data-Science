Title:
Rebecca's Easy Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cubed hawaiian sweet bread, crusts removed and lightly toasted
1 1/2 c. skim milk
4 tbsp sugar
3 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
2 tbsp kahlua
1 tbsp baileys
1 tsp cinnamon
2 large eggs, beaten
6 hersheys kisses or other chocolate, chopped

Directions:
Combine milk thru eggs in large bowl with whisk.  Toss in bread and coat evenly.  Cover and chill for 30 minutes (May be chilled up to 4 hours)  Place in 8x8 baking dish, or 4 6oz ramekins.  If using ramekins, place them in a water bath.  Preheat oven to 325.  Top pudding with chopped chocolate and bake for 35 minutes or until set.

Submitter: 
rebeccalovestocook
Description: 
I love this recipe, it is moister and lighter than a brownie but with great chocolate taste.  If you don't use liquors, you can skip the Baileys and substitute regular coffee for the kahlua.  I like to serve it warm with a dollop of whipped cream!
