Title:
Strawberry Cheesecake Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12oz Cool Whip
18oz carton strawberry yogurt
1 box of cheesecake pudding mix
1 bag of mini marshmallows
3 bananas,sliced
1pd strawberries,nsliced

Directions:
Mix the cool whip, yogurt and cheesecake pudding well. Then add marshmallows, bananas and strawberries. Chill and serve.

Submitter: 
Valerie
Description: 
One of my favorite side fruit salads given to me by Amy Borchardt. Quick and easy!
