Title:
Greg's Famous Spaghetti & Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meatballs
12 (or 20) oz ground chuck
4 oz ground veal (if available)
4 oz ground pork
1 1/2 cup pecorino cheese
1 1/4 cup fresh breadcrumbs
3 large eggs
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup fresh parsley
1/4 cup fresh basil
1/2 medium onion
4 cloves garlic minced
Black pepper & salt to tast
Sauce
8 to 10 large cloves of garlic minced/chopped
1 large onion diced
1/4 cup olive oil
6 oz can tomato paste
(2) 28 oz cans of crushed plum tomatoes or Hunts whole tomatoes coarsely chopped in blender
2 tbs sugar- optional
1 tsb salt
1/2 tsp tellicherry pepper
1/4 tsp oregano
1/4 cup finely chopped fresh basil

Directions:
Mix all meatball ingredients.  Form into balls and drop on to cookie sheet with tinfoil coated with Pam.  Bake at 350 for ~45 minutes until lightly browned.
Saute onion until pieces are turning clean.  Add garlic and saute ~2 minutes more. Add pepper flakes and saute 1 minute. Quickly add the canned tomatoes, then the rest of the ingredients except for the basil.  Simmer for at least 30 minutes. Remove from the heat, and add the fresh basil and stirring in well ~5 minutes prior to serving. Add the meatballs, stir in and serve.
