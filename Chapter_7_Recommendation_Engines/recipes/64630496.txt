Title:
Harold's Favorite Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 green peppers - sliced or cubed
2 cucumbers - sliced or cubed
4 - 5 tomatoes - sliced or cubed
2 onions - sliced or cubed
2 carrots - sliced or cubed
Optional --Broccoli, Cauliflower, Radishes.
Marinate  -
2/3 cup sugar
1/2 cup oil
1/2 cup vinegar
Salt and Pepper to taste
Optional - Can substitute Italian Oil Dressing for marinate

Directions:
Marinate Vegetables for 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Ron Lloyd
Description: 
I got this recipe from a dear friend from Nebraska. This was her husbands favorite salad.
