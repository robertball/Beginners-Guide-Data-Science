Title:
Green Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
!/2 cup butter
! cup chopped chives or green
onions
1 cup chopped fresh parsley
4 cups vegetable broth
2 cup raw regular rice
generous dash Cayenne pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In large saucepan over medium-high heat, cook chives in hot butter until tender. Stir in remaining ingredients; heat until boiling. Pour in 3-quart casserole.
Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, until rice is tender and all broth is absorbed. Toss lightly with a fork before serving.
