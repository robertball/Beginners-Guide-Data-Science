Title:
BLT Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 slices bacon
2 pints cherry or grape tomatoes, quartered
4 oz. mozzarella, diced
3 tbsp. sherry wine vinegar
2 tbsp. olive oil
1 small clove of garlic - minced
3/4 lb. pasta of your choice
3.4 cup small basil leaves

Directions:
Cook bacon until crisp.
Drain and break into bite sized pieces.
In a large bowl, whisk together the vinegar, oil and garlic.
Add tomatoes and cheese to bowl and stir.
Cook pasta, drain, and immediately add to bowl with 3/4 of the basil.
Toss until well combined and season to taste with salt and pepper.
Top with bacon and remaining basil.
