Title:
Cheesy Tuna and Rice Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of cooked brown rice
1 cup of shredded white sharp cheddar cheese
1 6 1/2 ounce can light tuna; drained well and flaked
1/4 cup diced yellow onion
1/4 cup diced green/red bell pepper
1/4 cup of diced celery
1 teaspoon garlic salt
2 large eggs; beaten
2 tablespoons water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Lightly spray  muffin pan with cooking spray.
Saute onions, celery, and bell peppers in butter until softened, but not browned.
Whisk together the eggs and water.
In a separate bowl mix rice, cheese, tuna, sauteed vegetables, and garlic salt.
Add egg mixture to rice mixture and combine thoroughly.
Distribute mix evenly in 12 muffin cups.
Bake at 375 degrees for 10 minutes.
While muffins are baking, dress and toss the salad.
Assemble salad on plate, top with 2 muffins per plate; serve.

Submitter: 
katysue
Description: 
Cheesy Tuna and Rice Muffins (can also be made in a loaf pan).  Serve on a bed of salad greens lightly dressed to taste. Makes a nice, light, and satisfying lunch.  Serve 2 muffins per person.
