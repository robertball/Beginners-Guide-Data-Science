Title:
Tiramisu Parfaits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983869.jpg
Ingredients:
32 NILLA Wafers, divided
1/3 cup cold brewed MAXWELL HOUSE Coffee
1 (8 ounce) container PHILADELPHIA Whipped Cream Cheese
2 tablespoons powdered sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
2 cups thawed COOL WHIP Whipped Topping

Directions:
Reserve 4 wafers for garnish. Coarsely break remaining wafers; place in small bowl. Drizzle with coffee; mix lightly.
Beat cream cheese, sugar and vanilla in medium bowl with electric mixer at medium speed until creamy. Gently stir in 1-1/2 cups of the whipped topping.
Spoon 2 tablespoons of the cream cheese mixture into each of 4 (12 oz.) parfait glasses; top with 1 to 2 tablespoons soaked wafer pieces. Repeat layers. Cover with remaining cream cheese mixture.
Refrigerate at least 1 hour.  Top with remaining 1/2 cup whipped topping and 4 reserved wafers. Garnish with coffee beans and unsweetened cocoa powder, if desired.
