Title:
Kale, White Bean and Shrimp Over Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
28 thawed Shrimp
1 cup chopped kale
2 carrots peeled and cut in rounds
2 cloves garlic peeled and smashed
1 15oz can of cannelini beans
1/2 cup cherry tomatoes halved
5 baby potatoes cut in 1/2 in cubes
1 Tablespoon chopped rosemary
Fresh ground Pepper
3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
2 tablespoons lemon juice
3 tablespoons olive oil
1 cup vegetable broth
1 c rinsed quinoa
2 cups vegetable broth

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 f
Fit sheet of parchment paper in 9x13 casserole dish so it comes up above the top of the dish
Toss shrimp, kale, beans, carrots, garlic, tomatoes and potatoes in the bottom of dish. Sprinkle pepper, salt, lemon juice and olive oil and rosemary over the top and mix well.
Place a sheet of parchment over the top and crimp the edges together.
Bake for 25 minutes.
Serve over quinoa.
