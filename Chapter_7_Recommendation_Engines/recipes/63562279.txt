Title:
Peach crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Butter
Peaches
Juice
Cornstarch
Amaretto

Directions:
Slice fresh peaches and simmer in butter.
In peach juice add tsp of cornstarch . Place input on stove and stir while boiling. Add 1/4 C amaretto stir until thickened
Pour over dessert crepes
