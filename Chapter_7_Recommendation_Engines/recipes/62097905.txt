Title:
Ham, Cheese & Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of uncooked converted rice
1-1/2 to 2 cups of ham diced
1 head of broccoli - cut into small flowerets
2 cans (10-3/4 oz) Cream of Cheddar Cheese Soup
2 cans (10-3/4 oz) Chicken Consomme
1-1/2 cups of grated cheddar cheese

Directions:
Spread two cups of uncooked rice on bottom of pan.
Sprinkle ham over rice.
Sprinkle broccoli flowerets over ham
Spoon 2 cans of Cream of Cheddar Soup undiluted over ham
Pour 2 cans of Chicken Consomme over Cheddar Soup mixture.
Sprinkle Grated Cheddar Cheese over top.
Cover with foil - or lid and bake covered in 350 deg. oven for 50 minutes.
Note - you can bake in oval casserole dish or 9 x 13 pan.

Submitter: 
BETTYCOOK
Description: 
This ham, cheddar cheese, broccoli & rice casserole is so easily made.  What a great combination.  I thought of this recipe one day when I had leftover ham.  This Casserole is so cheesey and delicious. A great comfort food.  Everyone I serve this to has at least two servings.
