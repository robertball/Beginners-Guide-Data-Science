Title:
The Daytona Destroyer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3271590.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (1/4-inch thick) cucumber slices
1 ounce agave nectar
1 thinly sliced serrano chile
6 ounces tequila
4 ounces club soda
3 ounces freshly squeezed lime juice
4 cucumber slices for garnishes

Directions:
Muddle the cucumber, agave nectar, and sliced chile in a beaker. Add tequila, club soda, and lime juice along with several large ice cubes; stir vigorously.
Add crushed ice to 4 cocktail glasses.
Strain mixture into prepared glasses; garnish with a cucumber slice.

Submitter: 
FOX Broadcasting Company
Description: 
Smooth out your race day menu with this cocktail recipe that is sure to be a staple throughout the season! The combination of agave nectar, lime juice and serrano chili are tamed by cucumber as they pair up with your favorite tequila for the win.
