Title:
Salmon with White Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons olive oil
2 cans (1.5 oz. each) rinsed and drained
1 Tablespoon finely grated (from 1 lemon) plus wedges for serving
1/2 teaspoon chopped rosemary
1/2 cup chicken broth
4 skinless salmon fillets (4 to 6 oz. each)
coarse salt
ground pepper
1/2 cup fresh parsley

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat oil over medium.  Add beans, zest, and rosemary and sauté until fragrant, 1 minute.  Add broth and salmon to skillet and season with salt and pepper.  Cover and cook until salmon is opaque, about 10 minutes.  Sprinkle with parsley and serve with lemon wedges.

Submitter: 
Lorraine Friberg
Description: 
This is a fast and delicious fish dinner.  I drizzle fresh lemon juice over each entrée before serving.
