Title:
Pizza Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Hamburger
Chopped onion, as much or a little as you like
Salt & pepper to taste
1 C Ketchup
1 C Tomato soup
1 T Mustard
1 T Vinegar
1 T Brown sugar
1 t Dried Oregano
6 Hamburger buns, split
2 C shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown and crumble hamburger with onion, salt and pepper.  Combine remaining ingredients with hamburger.  Simmer for 20 minutes and let cool.  Spread hamburger mix on buns and top with cheese.
Bake at 375 until cheese melts and buns are crisp.
