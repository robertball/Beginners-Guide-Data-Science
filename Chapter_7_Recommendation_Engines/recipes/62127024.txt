Title:
breakfast Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 24 Pack of Tortillas
1 pound of Jimmy Dean grounded sausage
1/2 pound of Kraft shredded Mild cheddar cheese
1/2 dozen Eggs
1/2 stick of butter
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
Make sure Tortillas are at room temperature.
Brown sausage in skillet, strain and let cool.
Mix milk with eggs and scramble until dry and fluffy.
Set eggs aside and let cool.
Take butter and Tortillas spread on one side until all done.
Place Tortilla buttered side down in skillet.
Then put sausage, eggs and then cheese on top of Tortilla.
Next place on top another Tortilla butter side out.
Let one side get golden brown and then flip and do the same.
Remove from heat and let cool.
Then slice into fourths, serve and eat.
