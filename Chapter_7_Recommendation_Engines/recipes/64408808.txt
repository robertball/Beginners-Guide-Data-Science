Title:
Chai Limeade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2270920.jpg
Ingredients:
10 limes juiced
1 lime zested
3-4 bags chai tea brewed in 1.5 cups hot water
Sugar or sweetener to taste
Ice
Water

Directions:
Brew chai tea.
Zest 1 lime into pitcher.
Squeeze all limes into pitcher.
Fill pitcher 1/2 way with ice.
Slowly pour warm chai tea over ice in pitcher.
Stir. Add water to full line.
Sweeten to taste.
Serve over ice in coffee mugs for a feel of exotica!!

Description:
