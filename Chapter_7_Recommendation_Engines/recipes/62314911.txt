Title:
WW Morning Glory Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
3/4 cup WW flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/8 tsp ground ginger
1/3 cup drained canned crushed pineapple in juice 1/4 cup FF egg substitute
1/4 cup finely shredded carrots
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
2 Tbsp canola oil
1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Mix first seven ingredients in a bowl.
Mix next 7 ingredients in another bowl.
Add b. milk mixture to flour mixture until flour is just moistened.
Fill muffin cups evenly with batter and bake until toothpick inserted comes out clean about 22 min.  Let cool on wire pan 5 minutes.  Remove muffins from pan and let cool completely.
