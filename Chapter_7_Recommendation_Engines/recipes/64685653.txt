Title:
No Bake Lactation Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5017755.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c oats
1 c chocolate chips
1 c creamy peanut butter
3/4 c flaxseed meal
1 c honey
2 tsp vanilla
2 tbsp brewers yeast
2 tbsp brown sugar

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in bowl and mix well.
Roll mixture into small balls.
Refrigerate to set.

Delicious bites meant to boost your milk supply!
