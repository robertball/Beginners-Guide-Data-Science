Title:
Roy's Vanilla Wafer Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Box Finely Crushed Vanilla Wafers
1 Cup Butter, Melted
6 Eggs (Beaten)
1 Cup Sugar
1 Cup Raisins
1 Cup Coconut
1 Cup Chopped Pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease your favorite bundt pan. Finely crush vanilla wafers in a large bowl. Add melted butter, eggs(already lightly beaten), sugar, raisins, coconut, and pecans. Combine until everything is moist. Pour into bundt pan, place on cookie sheet, and bake in center of oven at 300-350 degrees (depending on how hot your oven burns) for 45 to 50 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Renee Ragan Bowles
Description: 
This is the first cake my dad taught me to bake and it does not include flour. It's very moist and a big hit during Thanksgiving, Christmas, and even Bake Sales. Enjoy!
