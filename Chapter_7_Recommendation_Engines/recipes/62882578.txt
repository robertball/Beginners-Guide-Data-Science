Title:
Zucchini Gingerbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/694960.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup molasses
1/4 cup vegetable oil
3/4 cup vanilla yogurt
3 eggs
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp cloves
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
2 1/2 cups shredded zucchini
3 cups flour

Directions:
Grease 2 loaf pans with non-stick spray and preheat the oven to 325 F.
Combine, sugar, oil, yogurt and eggs in a large mixing bowl.
Stir in spices, baking soda, baking powder and salt.
Gently stir in zucchini and flour and pour batter into loaf pans.
Bake for 50-60 minutes until loaf is springy to the touch on top.

A moist spicy zucchini bread perfect for the transition from summer to fall
