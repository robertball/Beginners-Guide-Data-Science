Title:
Zucchini and Carrot Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5619130.jpg
Ingredients:
1 zucchini - peeled, grated, and chopped
1 carrot - peeled, grated, and chopped
1/2 cup plain fat-free Greek yogurt
1/2 cup 1% milk
2 tablespoons rolled oats
2 tablespoons honey
1 tablespoon flax seeds

Directions:
Blend zucchini, carrot, yogurt, milk, oats, honey, and flax seeds in a blender until smooth.
