Title:
Cranberry Glog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cranberry juice
2 cups grape juice
1/4 cup sugar
2 cinnamon sticks (about 3 inches)
4 cardamom pods, (crushed)
6 whole cloves
additional sticks for each served cup (optional)

Directions:
Combine the juices together in a large saucepan. Add sugar.
Place the cardamom, cloves and cinnamon together in a spice bag. Close bag and add to juices.
Simmer, do not boil. Reduce heat and simmer gently, uncovered, for 20 minutes. Discard the spice sack. Serve in warm mugs with a cinnamon stick if desired.

Submitter: 
Bryant Evans
Description: 
A holiday hot drink that is a bit stronger than apple cider.
