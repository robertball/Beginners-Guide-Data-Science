Title:
Grilled Sesame Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp oil
3 Tbsp sesame seeds
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 Tbsp lemon juice
2 tsp sugar
1/8 tsp pepper
1-1/2 lb round steak ( or other cut)

Directions:
In a small frypan, saute sesame seeds in oil.
In baking dish mix sesame seeds, soy sauce, lemon juice, sugar and pepper.  Coat steak on both sides and marinate at least 2 hours, turning steak once.
Grill steak, brushing with marinade.  Thinly slice steak and serve with stir fried vegetables.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
Sesame seeds add a nice twist to this grilled steak recipe.
