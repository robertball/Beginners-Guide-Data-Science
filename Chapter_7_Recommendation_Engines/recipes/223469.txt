Title:
Peanut Butter Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/872477.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup rolled oats
1/4 cup blueberries
1 tablespoon milk
1 tablespoon chunky peanut butter
2 teaspoons agave nectar
1/8 teaspoon vanilla extract
salt to taste

Directions:
Stir oats, blueberries, milk, peanut butter, agave nectar, vanilla extract, and salt together in a microwave-safe bowl.
Cook in microwave on high until the oats are softened, about 90 seconds. Stir until the cooked blueberries have colored the mixture.
