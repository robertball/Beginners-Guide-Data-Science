Title:
6 Week Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart buttermilk
5 tsp. baking soda
3 cups sugar
1 cup oil
4 eggs
1/2 tsp. salt
5 cups flour
15-16 oz. Post bran flakes

Directions:
Mix buttermilk and baking soda.  Set aside until foamy, about 3-4 minutes.  In another large bowl, mix sugar, oil, eggs, and salt.  Combine the two and mix together.  Add flour and stir until well blended.  Stir in bran flakes, slightly crushed.  Allow to sit 6-24 hours in fridge.  Bake 350 for 20-25 minutes in muffin cups or greased muffin pan.

Submitter: 
Barb Andrews
Description: 
This recipe comes from my husband's grandma.  The batter lasts for 6 weeks in the fridge (after allowing 6-24 hours to set).  Muffins taste so sweet and delicious; every morning you can wake up to convenient home-baked muffins that are rich in fiber!
