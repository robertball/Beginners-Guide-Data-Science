Title:
Paprika Pork Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 onions, sliced
3 yukon gold potatoes cubed
3 carrots sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 lb boneless pork shoulder blade roast
1 cup water or beef stock
3/4 cup tomato sauce or salsa
2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
2 tbsp brown sugar
2 1/2 tbsp paprika
1 tbsp dried oregeno
1/2 tsp dry mustard and salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
3 tbsp all purpose flour

Directions:
Place onions, potatoes, carrots and garlic in slow cooker. Trim pork and cut into 1 inch cubes, place on vegetables.
Combine 1 cup water/beef stock, tomato sauce, sugar, worcestershire sauce, paprika, oregeno, mustard, salt and pepper, pour into slow cooker, stirring to combine.
Cover and cook on low until meat is fork tender, 6 to 7 hours. Skim off fat. Move meat and vegetables to one side of the slow cooker.
Whisk flour with 1/4 cup cold water, whisk into liguid in slow cooker. Stir to redistribute ingredients. Cover and cook on high until sauce is thickened, 10 to 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
DARKSACRIFICE
Description: 
A hearty pork stew with paprika, onions carrots and potatoes. Great on a crisp fall night.
