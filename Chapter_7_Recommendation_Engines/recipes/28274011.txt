Title:
Pear Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups spring mix salad greens or sweet butter lettuce
1 medium ripe pear, cubed
1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
1/4 cup dried cranberried
1/4 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
1 1/2 cups cooked chicken (optional)
Dressing:
1/4 cup olive oil
2 tablespoons orange juice
2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
1 1/2 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon grated orange peel
1/8 teaspoon salt
dash of pepper

Directions:
Arrange the greens, pear, feta cheese, cranberried and nuts.
In a small bowl, whisk the dressing ingredients.  Serve dressing on side.
