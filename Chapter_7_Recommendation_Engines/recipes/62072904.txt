Title:
Low Carb Chicken Divan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 cooked chicken breasts shredded
2 pkgs. frozen broccoli
1 C. chicken broth
1 8 oz. package cream cheese softened
1 C. mayonnaise
1 C. grated cheddar cheese
1 Cup Sour Cream
1 T. lemon juice
Parmesan cheese
2 T. Butter
salt and pepper
Paprika

Directions:
1.  Mix together chicken broth, cream cheese, sour cream, mayo, shredded cheese, lemon juice, salt and pepper  and set aside.
2. In a 9x13 dish, microwave the broccoli as directed.
3. Drain the broccoli and spread evenly on the bottom of dish. sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
4. Add the chicken evenly then pour the mixture over the top. Cover with another sprinkle of parmesan, dot with butter and sprinkle with paprika.
5. Bake at 350 for 35 minutes or until bubbly.

Submitter: 
Laura Dobbins Corbett
Description: 
A very tasty low carb version of an old time favorite.  To make it even a  quicker version you can use a store bought rotisserie chicken.
