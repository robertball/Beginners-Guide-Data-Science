Title:
Slow Cooker Picante Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/337973.jpg
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts (frozen)
2 cups picante sauce

Directions:
Add frozen chicken and sauce to slow cooker and cook on high for 4 - 6 hours.
Serve with melted cheese and some sour cream.
