Title:
Red Blush Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. rice flour
½ c. oat flour
½ c. teff flour
½ c. buckwheat flour
1 ½ tsp. baking soda
1 ½ tsp. cream of tartar
1 (8 oz) can beets
1 tsp. xanthum gum
3 Tbsp. flax seed
½ c. raisins or dates
½ c. walnuts (optional)
1 Tbsp. finely grated orange peel, optional
¼ c. agave nectar
10 squirts Stevia extract
½ c. frozen orange juice concentrate
2 tsp. vanilla
1 (8 oz) can pineapple, with juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325F.  In large mixing bowl, combine flour(s), baking soda & cream of tartar.  Set aside.
Puree drained beets in blender or food processor, approximately 2 minutes.  Transfer beets to medium bowl; mix in honey, juice concentrate, vanilla, optional orange peel, flax seed and water, pineapple, and dates or raisins.  Stir into flour mixture till just blended.
Spoon batter into two 9 x 1 ½” nonstick round baking pans (or 9 x 13” baking pan).  Bake at 325F for 35-40 minutes or till toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.  Cool on wire rack for 20 – 30 minutes and remove from cake pans.

Submitter: 
Sugar-n-spice
Description: 
This a really yummy cake, with canned beets and pineapple in it. It is wheat free, dairy free and  sugar free.
