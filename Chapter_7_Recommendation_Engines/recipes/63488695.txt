Title:
Oatmeal Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/951887.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 cup mashed ripe bananas
1-1/2 cups white whole wheat flour
1 cup quick-cooking oats
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
In a bowl, cream together butter and sugar. Add eggs & bananas; mix well.
Combine dry ingredients; stir into creamed mixture just until moistened, fold in nuts.
Fill paper-lined muffin cups two-thirds full.
Bake for 18-20 minutes or until cupcakes test done.
Cool in pan 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack.
