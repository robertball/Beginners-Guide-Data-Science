Title:
Apple Cheesecake Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust
1/2 cup softened butter
1/3 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
Filling
8oz cream cheese (softened)
1 tbsp vanilla extract
1/4 cup sugar
1 egg
Topping
4 cups peeled and sliced apples
1/3 cup sugar
2 tbsp cinnamon
1/4 cup sliced almonds - optional

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 450 degrees
To make the crust combine the sugar, butter and vanilla in a bowl. Once mixed add in flour (you may need to add more or less than the 1 cup depending on the consistency of the dough, you don't want it too sticky or too flaky)
Press dough into a 9" glass pie plate
For the filling, combine softened cream cheese, vanilla, sugar, and egg. Pour into pie crust
Toss slice apples with remaining sugar and cinnamon.
Place apples on top of filling (You can add sliced almonds on top if desired)
Bake at 450 degrees for 10 minutes, then lower the oven temperature to 350 degrees and bake for 20-25 more minutes
Let cool and serve
