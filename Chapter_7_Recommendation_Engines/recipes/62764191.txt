Title:
Brazilian Beans - Crock Pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups uncooked pinto beans
6 cups water
1 large onions, chopped
4 garlic cloves, minced
1 pkg (500mg) bacon
2 bay leaves
3 tsp salt (to taste)

Directions:
Sort through beans and disguard the shrivled or discoloured ones.
Soak beans overnight. Dump soaking water.
Cook bacon in oven - 350 degrees for 30 min. (Do 2 pkgs at a time and save extra for later use). Reserve bacon fat.
Saute onions, use some of the reserved bacon fat.
Chop bacon and crisp it up in pan.
Assemble crock pot: beans, water, salt, bay leaves, garlic, sauted onions, freshly crisped up bacon.
Set crock pot for 6 hours.

Submitter: 
Evie
Description: 
Lots of flavour and great on rice and salsa.
