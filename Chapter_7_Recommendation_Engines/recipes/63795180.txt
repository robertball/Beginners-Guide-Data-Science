Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Taco Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
1 can black beans (473 mL)
1 can kidney beans (473 mL)
1 can corn (drained) (473 mL)
1 can tomato sauce (237 mL)
2 cans diced tomatoes w/chilies (429 mL)
1 packet taco seasoning
1-2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
Cheese
Sour cream
Tortilla chips

Directions:
Mix everything together in a slow cooker except chicken. Lay chicken on top and cover.
Cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.
30 minutes before serving, remove chicken and shred. Return chicken to slow cooker and stir in.
Top with cheese, sour cream, or tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Karine Halpenny
Description: 
This is a fast, easy and tasty slow cooker recipe.
