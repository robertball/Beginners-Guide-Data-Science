Title:
Silver Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vodka
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger Scotch whiskey
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger gin
2 cups chicken broth
2 cups crushed ice
2 sprigs fresh mint, garnish

Directions:
In a mixing glass, combine vodka, Scotch, gin and chicken broth. Mix well and serve over crushed ice. Garnish with a sprig of mint.

Submitter: 
Ben Russell
Description: 
Sit back, relax and enjoy this comforting old Southern libation.
