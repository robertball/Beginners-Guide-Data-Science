Title:
The Best Steak Marinade by Deb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
5 dashes Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup steak sauce
2 teaspoons Honey mustard
2 teaspoons minced garlic
salt and pepper to taste or 1 tsp frahm seasoning

Directions:
Mix olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, steaksauce, Honey mustard, and garlic in a small bowl. Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
jakerm1985
Description: 
This savory blend of steak sauce, balsamic vinegar, and Worcestershire sauce is the perfect marinade for a thick and juicy steak.Modified from: The Best Steak Marinade
