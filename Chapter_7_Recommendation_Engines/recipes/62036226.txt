Title:
Shrimp Cucumber Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 med cucumber
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup cooked shrimp. chopped
3-4 Tbsp cream cheese (I use light)
1 Tbsp lemon juice
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1/4 tsp pepper
1 tsp. dried dill

Directions:
Peel, seed and shred cucumber into a sieve. Sprinkle with salt and let stand for 10 mins. Place in a clean tea towel and squeeze out liquid until cucumber is fairly dry. Combine cream cheese, lemon juice, dry mustard, pepper and dill until smooth. Add shrimp and cucumber. Chill at least 1 hour and up to 6 hours. Serve with crackers or sliced baquette.

Submitter: 
LESLEYCAN
Description: 
If you like shrimp, you'll love this fresh tasting, chunky spread.
