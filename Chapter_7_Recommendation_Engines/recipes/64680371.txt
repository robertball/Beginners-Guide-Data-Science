Title:
Mac & Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4877456.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbs Salted Butter for Rue, 1 Tbs for Breadcrumbs.
1.5 Tbs A/P Flour, Sifted
1 Pt Heavy Whipping Cream
3 Tbs Plain Yogurt
8 Oz Block Sharp Cheddar Cheese, Shredded
8 Oz Block Whole Milk Mozzarella, Shredded
3 Tbs High Fat Cottage Cheese
3 Tbs Soft Mascarpone Cheese
3 Tbs Ricotta Cheese
6 Oz Breadcrumbs, Day old french bread put in food processor is my choice with 1 Tbs melted butter.
1 Lb Box of Pasta, Ridges in pasta help hold sauce better

Directions:
Shred Cheeses as required.
Packaged Shredded Cheeses have Semolina sprinkled on them to prevent sticking and this can make your sauce gritty.
If making Mac & Cheese Cook now, slightly Al Dente, drain and add to 13x9 Pyrex dish.
Over Very low heat melt your butter and add your Flour, don't burn your Rue.
Slowly add 1/2 of your Cream and liquid increase temp to 125 degrees.
Save 1/2 of the Cheddar Cheese for topping if making Mac & Cheese.
Increase heat, add cheeses in small incriments shredded first then soft and Yogurt.
Use rubber spatula to scrape the pot preventing burning your sauce.
Salt and Pepper to your taste.
Pour Sauce over your Pasta and sprinkle breadcrumbs and remaining Cheddar Cheese.
Put in Broiler set to 425 to melt and brown topping to your desired doneness.
Let stand for 5 minutes before serving.
Add on items could include Diced Ham, Shredded Chicken, Ground Beef, Roasted Peppers
