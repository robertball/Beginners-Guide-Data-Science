Title:
Perfect Roast Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken
2 whole garlic
1/4 cup Olive Oil
3 T fresh orange juice
2 T honey
1/2 T rosemary leaves
1/2 T italian seasoning
salt & fresh ground pepper

Directions:
1. Rinse the chicken and loosen the skin.
2. Mix the olive oil, orange juice, honey, rosemary leaves, italian seasoning, salt and pepper to make the marinade.
3. Rub chicken with the marinade. Remember to rub the cavity of the chicken and under the skin of the chicken.
4. Bruise the garlic cloves and put inside the cavity of the chicken.
5. Marinate for a minimum of 4 hours.
6. Preheat oven at 375 degrees and bake the chicken for 2 hours. Baste chicken with it's own juices every 30 minutes. When the skin gets too dark, cover the chicken with foil. Continue to bake the chicken until the juices run clear.
