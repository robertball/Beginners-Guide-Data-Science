Title:
Slam Dunk Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987049.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup milk
1 (8 ounce) package imitation flaked crabmeat
1/4 cup sliced green onions
1/4 cup chopped sweet red pepper
1 teaspoon curry powder
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
Assorted crackers

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and milk until smooth.  Stir in the crab, onions, red pepper, curry powder and garlic salt.  Refrigerate until serving.  Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Sharon  Landeen
Description: 
The spirited crowd we invite to our home to watch college basketball is quick to dig in to this delectable dip. It's a great use of imitation crabmeat, with red pepper and green onions adding color and crunch.
