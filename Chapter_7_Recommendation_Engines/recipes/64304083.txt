Title:
Strawberry Daiquiri Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups fresh spinach
1 ½ cups water
1 grapefruit, peeled (can substitute orange)
1 ½ cup strawberries
4 medjool dates, pitted

Directions:
1.Blend leafy greens and liquid base together first. For recipes that use a whole orange, we recommend you blend the orange in this step.
2.Add remaining ingredients and blend again.
