Title:
Baked Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 cup milk
1 cup Bisquick or Jiffy brand flour mix
1 tsp. paprika
1 tsp. seasoned salt
1 tsp. black pepper
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 stick melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray or grease a glass baking dish.
Mix together all dry ingredients.
Dip chicken in milk.
Dredge in flour mixture until well coated.
Place in baking dish.
Drizzle melted butter over chicken pieces.
Bake uncovered for one hour or until cooked through.

Submitter: 
Donna Graham
Description: 
This is a very easy and fast dinner time idea. Uses ingredients in your cupboard and you can make it in the morning, then drizzle butter on top and pop it in the oven when you get home for dinner.
