Title:
Sweet Potato Pie with Caramel Nut Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Uncooked Pie Crust
2 cups Sweet Potatoes (cooked and pureed)
1 Egg
1/4 cup Milk
1 tsp Vanilla
1/4 tsp Salt
3/4 cup Brown Sugar
1/4 cup Butter (melted)
1/4 tsp Cinnamon
1/4 tsp Nutmeg
1/8 tsp Cloves
Topping:
1 cup Pecans (chopped)
1/4 cup Butter (melted)
1/4 cup Brown Sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. In a large bowl, mix all pie ingredients until smooth. Pour into unbaked pie crust and bake for 35 minutes. In a medium bowl, mix all topping ingredients. Remove pie from oven and gently spread topping mixture evenly over the top of the pie. Bake for an additional 15 minutes. Remove from oven and allow to cool completely.
