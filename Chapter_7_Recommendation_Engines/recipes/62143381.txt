Title:
Perfectly paired turkey injection marinade & rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Marinade:
1 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup lemon juice
1 stick melted real butter
1 clove of garlic minced
1 tsp minced fresh ginger
1 tsp english mustard powder
Rub:
2 sticks of softened butter
3 sticks of fresh rosemary
2-3 sprigs of fresh thyme
2 small cloves of garlic
olive oil and salt and pepper (optional)

Directions:
Combine all of the ingredients for the marinade in mixing bowl and refrigerate for 30 minutes to let flavors combine.
Mince the garlic and rough chop the rosemary and thyme. Combine garlic and herbs with softened butter.
Separate the skin from the meat of the turkey. Depositing the rub mixture in several spots all over the turkey.
Using a meat injector, inject the marinade in the meatiest parts of the turkey remembering to change the angle of the needle.
Brush turkey with olive oil and salt and pepper to taste.
Refrigerate turkey 12-24 hours and bake or fry as directed.

Submitter: 
my4teengirls
Description: 
This delicious marinade paired with the rub give your turkey just the right amount of flavor while helping to make it melt in your mouth tender and juicy. Great for deep frying or just plain old roasting.
