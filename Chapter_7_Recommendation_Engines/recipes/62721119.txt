Title:
Mexican Sloppy Joe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
¦1 lb. ground beef
¦one 10-oz. can tomatoes and green chilies
¦1 envelope taco seasoning mix
¦1/4 C. water
¦1 tomato, peeled and chopped
¦8 hamburger buns, split and toasted
¦2 C. shredded lettuce
¦1 C. shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
Directions
In a skillet, brown the meat; drain off fat.  Stir in tomatoes and green chilies, taco seasoning mix, and water.  Bring to boiling; reduce heat.  Boil gently, uncovered, until thick, about 15 minutes.  Stir in tomato; heat through.  Spoon onto toasted buns.  Sprinkle each of the burgers with lettuce and shredded cheese.
