Title:
Whole Grain Sea Salt and Garlic Croutons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990507.jpg
Ingredients:
1 loaf Roman Meal Bread, crusts trimmed, cut into 1-inch cubes
6 tablespoons olive oil
2 teaspoons garlic, chopped
1/2 teaspoon sea salt

Directions:
Place bread cubes on two foil-lined baking sheets. Let stand for 2 hours or overnight.
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F.
Combine oil and garlic. Drizzle evenly over bread cubes; stir gently to evenly coat. Sprinkle with salt.
Bake 10 to 12 minutes, stirring once, or until golden brown. Cool completely.

Submitter: 
Roman Meal
Description: 
Why buy croutons when making them at home is so simple. Roman Meal bread makes perfectly crunchy croutons filled with the goodness of whole grains.
