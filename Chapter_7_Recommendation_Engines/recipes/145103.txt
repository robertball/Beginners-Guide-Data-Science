Title:
Iceberg Slice with Summer Berries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977948.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head Tanimura & Antle® Iceberg Lettuce
1/2 cup balsamic vinaigrette
2 cups mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
1/2 cup crumbled soft goat cheese
1/2 cup sliced almonds, toasted

Directions:
Core lettuce; rinse and drain well. Cut crosswise into 4 even slices.
Drizzle lettuce slices with dressing, as desired. Top with berries, cheese and almonds.

Submitter: 
Tanimura & Antle®
Description: 
This refreshing summer salad includes lettuce, mixed berries, goat cheese, and a sprinkling of toasted almonds.
