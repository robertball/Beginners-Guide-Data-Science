Title:
Lemon Pudding cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/817380.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C. All Purpose unbleached flour
1 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
1/4 tsp. Salt
1 C. Milk
2 eggs
Juice and zest of 3 lemons
1 C. Granulated sugar
3 Tbsp. Cornstarch
3 Tbsp. All Purpose unbleached flour
1 1/2 C. Boiling water
3 egg yolks,  slightly beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit
Grease a 12 cup muffin pan and set aside
Grate lemon zest and ream lemons putting juice and zest in small bowls and set aside
In a big saucepan add sugar, flour, cornstarch, lemon zest and juice then stir until well combined
Pour water into a small saucepan and bring to a boil then add to lemon mixture and heat until mixture thickens
Take approximately 1/2 Cup of lemon mixture and add to stirred egg yolks mixing in until well combined together
Pour into large saucepan and stir until completely thickened and then set aside to cool.
In a large mixing bowl add milk and eggs then whisk together until frothy and smooth
Pour cooled lemon mixture into the large mixing bowl and whisk until no lumps remain and mixture is smooth and glossy
Add dry ingredients to creamed mixture a small amount at a time until all flour is incorporated into the batter
Pour into greased muffin cups and set in oven for 20 to 25 minutes or until lightly browned on sides of muffins
When done cakes will level out in the middle which does not affect cake texture
Wait for 10 minutes before removing from muffin cups

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A miniature cake that has a soft lemony center on the inside surrounded by a cake like exterior that is sweet and delectable for the cake lover.
