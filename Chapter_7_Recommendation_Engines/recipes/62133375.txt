Title:
Yummy Rosemary Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken thighs
3 chicken legs
1/4 fresh rosemary
1 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper

Directions:
In a bowl mix together extra virgin olive oil, rosemary, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Add cicken and allow to marinate 5 hours or overnight
Prepare charcoal grill for chicken
Place chicken on grill. Cook for about 10 minutes a side
Remove from grill when chicken is cooked all the way through
Serve up and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Elizabeth Thompson
Description: 
Chicken so good you won't be able to put it down.
