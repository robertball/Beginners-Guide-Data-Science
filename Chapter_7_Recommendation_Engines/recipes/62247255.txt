Title:
Creamy Avocado Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Chilean Hass avocado
3/4 cup fat free half and half
2 green onions, chopped
2 tablespoons fresh basil leaves, packed
1 tablespoon white wine
1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions:
Cut avocado in half, remove the pit, and peel. Scoop fruit into blender or food processor bowl. Add all remaining ingredients and blend until very smooth, about 30 seconds. Store in tightly covered container, up to 3 days.

Submitter: 
Amanda
Description: 
I adapted this recipe and changed it a little bit. I am working on a lower fat diet so I used fat-free half and half instead of buttermilk.Modified from: Creamy Avocado Dressing
