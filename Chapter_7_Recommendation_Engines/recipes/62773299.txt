Title:
Sarah's Strawberry Cheesecake Protein Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/647036.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup fat free vanilla Greek strained yogurt
1/4 cup 1% cottage cheese
3/4 cup nonfat milk
4-5 medium strawberries (frozen works well)
1/8 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 teaspoons Splenda 50/50 sugar blend
4-5 small ice cubes

Directions:
In a blender, blend all ingredients except ice until smooth.  Add ice and blend until ice is mostly smooth.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Sarah Dipity
Description: 
This nutritious smoothie has over 18 grams of protein and will fill you up.  Great for breakfast on the go!
