Title:
Basic Mashed Potatoes - Budget Friendly Version (serves 8) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/295632.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pounds baking potatoes, peeled and quartered
4 tablespoons butter
1 3/4 cups milk
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.  Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 20 minutes; drain.
In a small saucepan heat butter and milk over low heat until butter is melted.  Using a potato masher or electric beater, slowly blend milk mixture into potatoes until smooth and creamy.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Thanksgiving Budget Menu
Description: 
This recipe serves 8 and calls for a little less milk. As with the original recipe, the potatoes are blended with warmed butter and milk to create the perfect, smooth mashed potatoes everyone loves.Modified from: Basic Mashed Potatoes
