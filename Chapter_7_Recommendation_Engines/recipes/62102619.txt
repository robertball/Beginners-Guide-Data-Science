Title:
Happy Hippy anti-cancer granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C oats
1 C sweetened shredded coconut
1/4 C honey and/or maple syrup
1/4 C canola oil
3/4 C shredded almonds, walnuts, or any other nuts
1 C dried fruit (I use cranberries and blueberries)
1 tbsp. flaxseed oil
1 Tbsp. wheat germ (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Mix oats, coconut, and nuts in large bowl.
Whisk together honey and canola oil. Add to oat mixture and toss to coat.
Spread onto cookie sheet in one even layer and bake for 15 -20 mins.  Check often to avoid burning.
Let cool 10 min. then roll in dried fruit.
* variations, use raisins and sprinkle cinnamon in the dry mixture. Dried apples are also a tasty.

Description: 
Statisfying granola that includes very versitile ingredients. This recipe has been passed around to hungry college students for years.
