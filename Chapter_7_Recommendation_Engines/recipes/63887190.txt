Title:
Creamy Lemon Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp plus 1/2 cup butter, divided
1 pkg (4.3 oz) cook and serve lemon pudding mix
1/2 c cold milk
3 3/4 Confectioners' sugar
1 tsp lemon extract

Directions:
Line a 9 in square pan with foil Grease the foil with 1 1/2 tsp butter and set aside.
In large heavy saucepan, combine the pudding mix, milk and remaining butter. Cook and stir over medium heat until thickened. Remove from heat. Beat in confectioners' suugar and extract. Pour into greased pan. Refrigerate until set.
Using foil, lift fudge out of pan. discard foil, cut fudge into 1" squares. Store in refridgerator. Yield about 1 1/2 pounds.

Submitter: 
cullivoe
Description: 
Very sweet and tangy. Cut pieces can be dipped into chocolate to make lemon candy like a fondant.
