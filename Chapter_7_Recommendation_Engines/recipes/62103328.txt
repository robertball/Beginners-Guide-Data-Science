Title:
Allyson's  Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
3/4 C. milk
2/3 C. saltines, broken up/crushed
1/2 C. chopped onions
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. rubbed sage
pepper
1 1/2lb. ground beef
Sauce:
1 C. ketchup
1/2 C. brown sugar
1 tsp. worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Mix eggs, milk, saltines, onions, salt, sage, pepper and ground beef together.
Once evenly mixed, form into a loaf and place on a baking sheet or a loaf pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for approx. 1 hour.
Mix the ketchup, brown sugar and worcestershire sauce together.
Spoon over the meatloaf with 20 min. left to cook.
Allow meatloaf to set for 10 min. before cutting.

Submitter: 
Allyson
Description: 
A friend of mine gave me this recipe. It is the best meatloaf that I have EVER tasted. I love the sweet sauce that is spooned over the top. Enjoy!!!
