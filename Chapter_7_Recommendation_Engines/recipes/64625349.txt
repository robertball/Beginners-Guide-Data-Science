Title:
Baked Lobster Tails Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
lobster tails
ghee
salt
parsley

Directions:
preheat oven to 450
remove cartilage from lobster tail with scissors/shears
put lobster on foil big enough to create pouch covering all lobster
pour melted ghee over lobster tails
little salt to taste
little parsley
create pouch with foil and place on baking sheet
bake until opaque, approximately 15-18 minutes for 4oz tails
serve with more butter, lemon, parlse
