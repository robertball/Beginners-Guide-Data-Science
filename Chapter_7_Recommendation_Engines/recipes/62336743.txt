Title:
Baked Pita Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/494753.jpg
Ingredients:
2 greek style pitas
1 tbsp olive oil
sprinkle of italian seasoning
sprinkle of seasoning salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Brush pitas with olive oil.
Sprinkle each pita with desired amount of italian seasoning and seasoning salt.
Cut each pita into 8 wedges using pizza cutter.
Place wedges on lined cookie sheet.
Bake in preheated oven for 5 minutes or until lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Kelli F
Description: 
Much healthier and just as tasty as the deep-fried pita chips you get at a restaurant. These are great served with hot spinach dip.
