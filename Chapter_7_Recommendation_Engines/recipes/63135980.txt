Title:
Kielbasa with Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2c chicken broth
1/3 cup dijon
minced garlic clove
1 (14 ounce) package  all pork kielbasa sausage, sliced into 1/3 inch pieces
1 red onion, chopped
Brussels sprouts, trimmed and quartered
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Bake in oven at 375, stirring twice, until sprouts are soft and have a light caramelization.

Submitter: 
gayla
Description: 
Sliced Kielbasa sausage is cooked with Brussels sprouts and red onion to make a fast and hearty main dish.Modified from: Kielbasa with Brussels Sprouts
