Title:
Party Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 servings instant mashed potatoes, prepared
8 oz. cream cheese
1 c. sour cream
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
2 T. butter, melted
paprika and chopped chives for garnish, optional

Directions:
Combine prepared potatoes, cream cheese, sour cream, and garlic powder and mix well to combine.
Pour into a 13x9 casserole.
Smooth and brush top of potatoes with melted butter.
Sprinkle with paprika, if desired.
Bake at 350 for 30 - 40 min.
Remove from oven and garnish with chopped chives, if desired.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
Super easy dressed up instant potatoes with a beautiful crust.
