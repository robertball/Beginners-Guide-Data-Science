Title:
Amaretto Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
3TB Flour
3TB Butter
1 1/2 tsp Garlic Salt
1 1/2 TB Dijon Mustard
1 1/2 tsp ground pepper
1 6 /14 ounce can frozen Orange Juice
2 tsp Paprika
1 Cup Amaretto

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350
Combine flour, salt, pepper, paprika and garlic salt. Coat chicken with this mixture.
Heat Oil and Butter in a skillet and saute the chicken until browned.
Remove from skillet and put in casserole.
To skillet add mustard, orange juice and amaretto. Increase heat to a boil and stir constantly until thick. Pour the sauce over the Chicken and Bake Covered for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Bill Ligertwood
Description: 
A delicious chicken dish that will knock their socks off. The sweet taste of Amaretto combined with orange makes this a taste delight.
