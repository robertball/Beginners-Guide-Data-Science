Title:
Fluffy Carrot Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 c. oil
1 tsp cinnamon
1 pinch of ground cloves
1 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
3/4 c. white sugar
1 c. flour
7 ounces of creamy carrot baby food
3/4 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Grease and flour muffin tins. Preheat oven to 350.
In a large bowl, mix eggs, sugar and oil together.
Add all the dry ingredients and vanilla and mix until well blended.
Add carrot baby food last, stir until blended, do not over mix.
Fill muffin cups 3/4 full. Bake for 20 - 22 minutes until a toothpick can be inserted and come out clean. Let cool and then frost with buttercream icing.

Submitter: 
Bernadette Robinson
Description: 
These soft and fluffy carrot muffins are incredibly light and tasty. Easy to prepare and bake they're sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.
