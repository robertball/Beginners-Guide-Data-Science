Title:
GARLIC  PEPPER SHRIMP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 LBS FRESH SHRIMP, PEELED & DEVEINED
3 TBS BUTTER/MARGERINE
1 (HEAPING) TBLS MINCED GARLIC
1/2 TSP BLACK PEPPER
1/2 TSP WHITE PEPPER
1 TSP LEMON PEPPER SEASONING
1 TSP RED PEPPER FLAKES
1/4 TSP PAPRIKA
FRESH PARSLEY
SNIPPED CHIVES

Directions:
MELT BUTTER IN SKILLET AND ADD SHIRMP AND ALL SEASONINGS. SAUTE UNTIL PINK (ABOUT 5-7 MINUTES) TURN DOWN HEAT AND ADD A LITTLE FRESH PARSLEY AND SNIPPED CHIVES FOR COLOR. SERVE WITH A SIMPLE RICE DISH.
