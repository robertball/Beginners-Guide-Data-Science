Title:
Idiot's Delight Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups shredded coconut
1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
1 (8 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained
1 (11 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained
1 (10 ounce) jar maraschino cherries, drained and sliced
1 container vanilla or coconut yogurt
1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Allow friut to drain for at least 1/2 hour to drain completely.  Place in medium mixing bowl.  Add cocounut.  Stir spices into yogurt and pour into bowl of fruit.  Toss gently to mix.
