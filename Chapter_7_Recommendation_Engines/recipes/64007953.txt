Title:
Vanilla Buttercream Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks (1 cup) unsalted butter, room temp
1/2 cup vegetable shortening (like Crisco)
2 lbs sifted (1 bag) powered sugar
1 tablespoon vanilla
Scant 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
dash of salt

Directions:
1. Cream together butter and shortening.
2. A cup or so at a time, mix in the sifted sugar.  Scrape down the sides after each addition.
3. After the first addition, add the vanilla.
4. Add the cream after each next addition.
IMPORTANT:  You may not need all the cream, so add it slowly.
5. Add the salt then beat on high for 3-4 minutes.

Submitter: 
Rachel Faulkner
Description: 
This icing is delicious & great for decorating.  Tint with your favorite food coloring!
