Title:
Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups peeled and thinly slced apples
1 cup light corn syrup
1/2 cup water
2 cups brown sugar
4 cups oatmeal, uncooked
1 cup butter or margarine
1 cup flour
1 tsp. salt

Directions:
Grease a 9x13 baking dish.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Place apples in pan.
Mix together corn syrup and water; pour over apples.
Mix together brown sugar, oatmeal, butter/margarine, flour and salt until crumbly; sprinkle over apples.
Bake for 1 hour or until lightly browned and bubbly.

Submitter: 
DarcieA
Description: 
My husband gave me this recipe.  Since he has a nut allergy, the oats are an ideal substitue. It is a crowd pleaser!
