Title:
Lemon Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/755855.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
1 tablespoon powdered lemonade mix
1/4 cup graham cracker crumbs

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix the cream cheese, butter, confectioners' sugar and lemonade mix using an electric mixer. Refrigerate for 3 hours.
When the mixture is completely chilled, shape into a ball and roll in graham cracker crumbs. Serve on a platter with your favorite crackers.

Submitter: 
KATHYCHILL
Description: 
Lemonade mix flavors a sweet cheese ball to make a very refreshing snack for a spring or summer get-together. Serve with mini graham crackers.
