Title:
Apple Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Apple Dessert
Mary Munn
1 1/4 cups oatmeal
1 1/4 cups flour
3/4 t soda
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup butter (1/4 lb.)
Mix together and pat in buttered pan. (1 9"x13" or 2 8"x8" pans) Sprinkle apples (6 cans) over mixture. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
Note: I make this with any fresh fruit. (Rhubarb is especially good!)
Sprinkle following mixture over top:
1/2 cup butter
3/4 cup sugar
1 cup flour
Bake at 350 degrees - about 30 min.

Directions:
Bake at 350 degrees - about 30 min.
