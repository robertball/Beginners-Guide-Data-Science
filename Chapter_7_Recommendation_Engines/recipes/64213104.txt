Title:
"Wendy's" Apple Pecan Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
red and green apples
blue cheese
dried cranberries
toasted pecans
grilled chicken fillet
Pomegranate Vinaigrette:
1 cup pure pomegranate juice reduced to 1/3 cup
1/2 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp balsamic vinegar
1 tsp extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Combine the salad ingredients.
Toss with vinagrette

Submitter: 
TJ
Description: 
This is very close to the salad you get at Wendy's. Can be made without the chicken.
