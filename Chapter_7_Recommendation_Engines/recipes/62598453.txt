Title:
Amish Tomato Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups of tomato juice
2 tomatoes, diced
2 tablespoons of flour
1 cup of milk
salt and pepper

Directions:
bring tomato juice to a boil
add diced tomatoes and return to boil
mix flour and milk together, add to tomato juice and return to boil.
Season with salt and pepper
serve

Submitter: 
Christine Douglas- Kaiser
Description: 
a change to the ordinary.  Can be served over biscuits, pasta or meats.
