Title:
Dad's Day Strata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup broccoli florets
1 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes with basil, garlic, and oregano, drained
3 slices whole wheat bread, cubed
8 slices deli honey ham, shredded
1/4 cup pepperjack cheese, shredded
4 eggs
1/4 cup heavy cream
1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
2. Steam broccoli florets using your preferred method. (I like Zip N Steam bags in the microwave.)
3. Mix steamed broccoli, tomatoes, bread cubes, honey ham pieces and cheese in a large bowl.
4. In small bowl, beat eggs, heavy cream, garlic powder, and salt and pepper.
5. Add egg mixture to other ingredients. Mix well.
6. Pour into 9" square casserole dish.
7. Bake in preheated oven for 35 minutes. Allow to cool 3-5 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
Muffin Boots
Description: 
I wanted to make my husband a nice Father's Day brunch, but we were in dire need of a trip to the grocery store. I scrounged what we had in the kitchen, and the Dad's Day Strata was born! It turned out very yummy and is very easy.
