Title:
Banana Spinach Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1107288.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large banana (preferrably frozen overnight)
4 cups of raw spinach
1/2 cup of unsweetened almondmilk, or water
1 cup of water, or until desired consistency
sweetener, to taste (I added about 1/4 cup of sweetened almondmilk, but you can sub sugar or honey)
6 tbsp of yogurt (optional, but improves consistency)

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients in a blender until smooth throughout. Serve with ice, if desired.

Submitter: 
karkar
Description: 
Easy breakfast smoothie for a quick morning on the go, or after a large dinner the night before. Keeps me energized all morning!
