Betty's Chickpea Salad with a hint of lemon dressing Recipe

19 oz can chickpeas, drained and rinaed
3/4 c diced celery
1/4 c diced radishes
1 tbsp chopped parsley
1/2 cup grape toms cut in half
Dressing:
1/4 c mayo
2 tbsp plain yougurt or sour cream
2 tsp lemon rind
1 tsp dijon
1/2 tsp salt and pepper

Directions:
Refrigerate for at least 4 hours prior to serving.
