Title:
Sesame Mango Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3931401.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana
1 cup frozen mango
1 small cucumber
2 cups spinach
2 tablespoons cashew butter
1 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds
1 cup water

Directions:
Layer banana, mango, cucumber, spinach, cashew butter, and sesame seeds in a blender, in the order mentioned; add water. Blend until smoothie is desired consistency, adding more water if desired.

Submitter: 
Alli Shircliff
Description: 
Toasted sesame seeds add a nice toasty flavor to this mango and spinach smoothie.
