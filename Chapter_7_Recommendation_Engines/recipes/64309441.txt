Title:
Chai Butter Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1979924.jpg
Ingredients:
20 (3 inch) unbaked tart shells
1/4 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 tsp. lemon juice
1 cup golden corn syrup
1/4 tsp ground cardamom
1/8 tsp ground cinnamon
1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
1/8 tsp ground ginger
1 cup raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Cream the butter and sugar together.
Beat in the eggs, vanilla, lemon juice, corn syrup, and spices.
Put about 1 tbsp. of raisins in the bottom of each tart shell, and top with about 2 tbsp. of the filling.
Bake until pastry is golden and filling is set, about 20 minutes. Cool on racks.
