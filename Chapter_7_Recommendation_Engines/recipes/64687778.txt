Title:
Heritage Pineapple Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5065361.jpg
Ingredients:
2 packages 3 ounce lemon, or lime, or melon jello
1 cup cucumber, finely diced
1 cup crushed pinapple
1/4 cup diced pinapple
1/4 cup walnuts, minced
3 tablespoons sour cream
1 small green pepper, diced
1/4 cup mayonnaise
3 drops green food coloring (optional)

Directions:
Follow jello instruction and set it aside in a 2 quart oblong baking dish. Chill for at least 6 hours and then Cut jello into 1/2 squares.
In a bowl add green pepper, cucumber, and mix sour cream and mayonnaise togetther with food coloring.
Fold into mixture the crushed pineapple.
Fold into mixture 2/3 of jello squares.
Top with remaining jello squares, diced pineapple, and walnuts.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
This was a recipe in a family cookbook from my wife's great aunt. It was missing directions so I guessed what I thought she might have done. I didn't know if I should serve it with dinner or after as dessert.
