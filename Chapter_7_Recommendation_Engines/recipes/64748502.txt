Title:
Instant Pot® Bbq. Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - Large Chicken Breasts Frozen
1  Your Favorite Bbq. Sauce (We like Sweet Baby Ray)
1  tbsp. Chicken Seasoning
4  Cups Water
4  tsp. Salt
1  tbsp. Black Pepper (or to taste)
1  Packet of Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing Mix

Directions:
Whisk seasoning, water, salt, pepper and dressing together in Instant Pot®
Add chicken breasts to Instant Pot® mixture
Close and cook on high pressure for 3 minutes
(Takes about 7 minutes to come up to pressure)
Let pressure release naturally (about 13 to 18 minutes)
Shred chicken and add Bbq sauce.
Enjoy with baked bbq. beans or your favorite combination

Submitter: 
Marla
Description: 
So easy, and only 1 pot, no dishes!
From the freezer to the table in 30 minutes
