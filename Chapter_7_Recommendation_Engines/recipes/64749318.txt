Title:
Cauliflower Mash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6691618.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head of cauliflower
2 garlic cloves
1 oz of cream cheese
1 tbsp of butter
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut cauliflower into florets, and steam with garlic until tender (approx 10 min)
With a staff mixer or food processor, blend all ingredients until smooth
