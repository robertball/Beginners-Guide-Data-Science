Title:
Apple Glazed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. chicken tenderloins
Greek seasoning, to taste
3/4 cup apple juice
2 T. lemon juice

Directions:
Heat nonstick skillet at med-high and spray with cooking spray.  Season chicken with Greek seasoning and add to pan.  Cook chicken 4 minutes on each side, until done.  Remove from pan, keep warm.
Add apple juice and lemon juice to skillet and stir to remove browned pieces.  Continue to heat at med-high for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally until apple juice reduces and sauce thickens.  Add chicken to sauce and stir to coat evenly.

Submitter: 
paynes
Description: 
This is smothered in a delicious apple juice reduction.  It's quick, simple, and a definite keeper.  Serve the chicken and sauce over rice for an easy meal.
