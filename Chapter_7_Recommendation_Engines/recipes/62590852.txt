Title:
Lemon Cream Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup + 1 tbsp. flour - divided
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
6 chicken cutlets
1/4 cup butter
1 cup chicken broth
1 cup heavy cream - divided
3 tbsp. lemon juice
1/2 lb. mushrooms

Directions:
In a large zip loc bag, combine 1/2 cup flour, salt and pepper.
Add chicken and toss to coat.
In a large skillet, cook chicken in butter until done.
Remove from pan and keep warm.
Add broth to drippings and bring to a boil over medium heat.
Simmer, uncovered for about 10 min. or until it is reduced to about 1/3 cup.
Stir in 3/4 cup cream, lemon juice and mushrooms.
Cook over medium-low heat for 5 min.
Combine remaining flour and cream until smooth and stir into skillet.
Bring to a boil, cook and stir for 2 min. or until thickened.
Return chicken to skillet and heat through.
