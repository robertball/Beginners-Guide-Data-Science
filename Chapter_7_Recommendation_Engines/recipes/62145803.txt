Title:
Mom's Party Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag pretzels
4 cups wheat chex
8 cups rice chex
8 cups corn chex
6 cups cheerios
2 11.5oz. cans mixed nuts (optional)
4 cubes butter or margarine
1 cup oil
6 TBSP worcestershire sauce
1 TBSP garlic salt
1 TBSP seasoned salt
1 TBSP celery salt

Directions:
In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine butter, oil, worcestershire and spices. Stir until well blended.
Combine cereals, pretzels and nuts in a large roasting pan (like for turkey).  Mix well.
Pour sauce over cereal, stir until there are no noticeable dry spots in the mix.
Cover with foil and bake at 225 degrees for 1 hour.
Uncover, and continue baking for another hour, or until everything is crispy.
STIR FREQUENTLY.
Store in air-tight containers like gallon-sized baggies or tupperware.  Lasts forever!  Make for holidays, ball-games, whatever!

Submitter: 
Kris Swiggum
Description: 
Since I was a kid, this is what I remember most about the holidays: when Mom had a pan of this mix in the oven!
