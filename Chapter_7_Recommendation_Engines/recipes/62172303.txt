Title:
Easy Garlic-Lemon Scallops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
3 tablespoons minced garlic
1 pounds large sea scallops
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon Old Bay
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
3 tablespoons white wine
3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Stir in garlic, and cook for a few seconds until fragrant. Add scallops, and season w/Old Bay & cayenne and cook for several minutes on one side, then turn over, and continue cooking until firm and opaque.
Remove scallops to a platter, then whisk salt, pepper, and lemon juice & wine into butter. Finish with chopped parsley. Pour sauce over scallops to serve.

Submitter: 
Christine Foley Wanner
Description: 
Scallops sauteed in butter and garlic will melt in your mouth. Lemon juice gives it a nice kick.Modified from: Easy Garlic-Lemon Scallops
