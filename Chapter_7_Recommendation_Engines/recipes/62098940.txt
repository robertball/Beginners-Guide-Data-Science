Title:
Funky Chocolate Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box chocolate brownie mix.
2 sm. chocolate pudding boxes
1 sm vanilla pudding
1 8 oz cream cheese
milk
sm. heavy cream
vanilla
15 oreo cookies
vanilla flavoring
Chocolate bar
Cool whip
chocolate syrup

Directions:
Put cookies in freezer.
Mix brownie batter
add two tbsp vanilla to the batter.
Bake according to box directions.
Prepare chocolate pudding use 1/2 c milk
use 1/2 c. heavy cream.
Set chocolate pudding aside.
Take cookies out of freezer.
Put cookies in food processer.
Pulse until to very fine.
Soften cream cheese.
make vanilla pudding to box directions.
Using hand mixer
mix in cream cheese
add cookie crumbs.
Mix until well blended.
Put both puddings in fridge.
Let brownies cool completly.
After brownies cool.
You are ready to assemble.
Cut brownies in sm. squares.
Put enough brownie squares to cover bottom of bowl.
Put a nice layer of chocolate pudding a top brownies.
Add another layer of brownies.
Add a large layer of vanilla/cookie mixture
a top of brownies.
Add another layer of brownies.
Add last layer of chocolate pudding.
Add a of swirl chocolate syrup.
Crumble a brownie square in hand.
Pour over top.
Cover the top with Cool Whip.
Shave a small chocolate bar for added decoration.
You will have some left over.
I mix both pudding mixtures together in a bowl.
Put on a serving plate and use for a dip for the extra brownies.
This is a great dessert for birthday parties.
Enjoy
