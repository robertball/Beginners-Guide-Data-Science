Title:
Roasted Whole Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 medium carrots (1 to 1.25 in. in diameter at thickest part)
2 tsp olive oil
1/4 tsp kosher salt
1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Scrub carrots, and dry well; peel, if desired.
Arrange carrots in a single layer, well spaced on a baking sheet.
Drizzle with oil, turning to coat all over. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Tightly cover pan with foil. Bake at 400 degrees for 10 minutes. Uncover and return pan to oven; bake an additional 18 minutes or until carrots are tender, turning once.
Cals: 70; Fat 2.6g; Pro 1g; Fiber 3g; Sugars 6g; Sodium 204 mg

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
Oven roasting surrounds vegetables with dry, hot, even heat that heightens flavor, browns, and crisps exteriors.
