Title:
Homemade Fudgesicles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14 oz. can of sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Mix together 1 14 oz can of sweetened condensed milk,1/2 cup milk,1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder and 1 tsp vanilla extract until smooth.
Pour into ice pop molds and freeze for about 4 hours.
