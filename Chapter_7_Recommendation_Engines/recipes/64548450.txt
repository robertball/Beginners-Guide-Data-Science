Title:
Whole Wheat Spent Grain Bread for the Bread Maker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cup Whole Wheat Flour
1 Cup Bread Flour
11 oz. Warm Water
3   Tablespoon Honey
3    Tablespoon Oil/Butter
1-1/2 Teaspoon Salt
2     Teaspoon Yeast
1/4 Cup Spent Brewing Grain

Directions:
Add everything into your bread maker according to mfg. directions. Select wheat bread or other rustic type baking cycle.

Description: 
Whole Wheat flour and spent brewing grain add fiber to this delicious bread made in the bread maker
