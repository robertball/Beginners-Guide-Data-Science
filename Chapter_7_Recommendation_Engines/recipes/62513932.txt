Title:
White Chocolate Cranberry Blondies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter,cubed
1 1/2 cups packed light brown sugar
2 eggs
3/4 tsp vanilla
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 tespoon salt
1/8 tespoon ground cinnamon
1/2 cup dried cranberries
6 oz white baking chocoate,coarsely chopped
Frosting
1 pkg (8 oz) cream cheese,softened
1 cup confectioners sugar
1 Tablespoon grated orange peel,optional
6 oz white baking chocolate,melted
1/2 cup dried cranberries chopped

Directions:
In a microwave,melt butter,stir in brown sugar.Transfer to a large bowl,cool to room temerature.Beat in the eggs and vanilla.Combine the flour,baking powder,salt and cinnamon,gradually add to butter mixture.Stir in cranberries and chopped chocolate (batter will be thick )
Spread into a greased 13-in x 9 in  baking dish.Bake at 350 for 18-21 min or until a toothpick inserted near center comes out clean(do not overbake).Cool on wire rack
For the frosting, in a large bowl,beat the cream cheese,confectioners sugar and orange peel if desired until well blended.Graduall add half  of the melted white chocolate.beat until blended.Frost brownies, Sprinkle with chopped cranberries.Drizzle with remaining melted white chocolate.Cut into bars.Store in refrigerator

Submitter: 
Lindalou
Description: 
For a fancier presentation,cut the bars into triangle shapes and drizzle white chocolate over each one individually.It wows people every time
