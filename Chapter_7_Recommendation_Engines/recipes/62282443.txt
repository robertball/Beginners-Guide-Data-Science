Title:
Pam's Best Pasta Sauce ! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound bulk Hot Italian sausage
2 Tblsp.olive oil
1/2 cup minced bell pepper
1/2 cup minced onion
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1/2 cup red wine
1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1 lrg. or 2 sm. bay leaf
1 1/2 tsp. dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon fennel seed
1/2 tsp.crushed red pepper flakes
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
In a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat add olive oil and the sausage, onion,bell peppers and garlic cook until well browned; drain fat.Add red wine and let reduce. Stir in diced tomatoes, tomato paste. Mix in  fennel seed, oregano,bay leaf, salt, and pepper. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.Add to your favorite pasta!
