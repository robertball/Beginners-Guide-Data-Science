Title:
Indian Spiced Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/363307.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups lentils
2 (15 oz.) cans chicken broth
3 cups water
1 small yellow onion, diced
2 large stalks celery, diced
1 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon corriander
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon dried mustard
1/2 teaspoon turmeric
4 cloves garlic, chopped
1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles
1 tablespoon dried parsley
2 chicken breast halves

Directions:
Rinse the lentils under cold water until it runs clear. Dump into 4-5 quart crock pot. Add broth and water. Add onion and celery. Add all spices, the garlic, and chiles. Stir to combine.
Add chicken breasts to soup.
Cover and cook on low for about 7 hours, or on high for about 4. Before serving, remove chicken from the slow cooker, chop it up, and stir back in.
