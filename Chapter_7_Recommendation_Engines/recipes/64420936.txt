Title:
Beef tenderloin Ruth Chris style with mushroom sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce Sauce:
4 tablespoons (1/2 stick) chilled unsalted butter, divided
1/2 cup chopped portobello mushrooms
3 tablespoons Cognac or brandy
1 fresh rosemary sprig
1 teaspoon coarsely cracked black pepper
1 cup ruby or tawny Port or Marcela wine
1 Consommé soup
I cup water
Beef
2-4 tenderloin 1 ¹/4 inch thick
Melted butter
Sea salt
Pepper

Directions:
Sauce
Melt 2 tablespoons butter in large saucepan over medium-low heat. Add mushrooms ; sauté until soft, 3 minutes. Add Cognac, rosemary, and 1 teaspoon cracked pepper and cook until liquid evaporates, 1 minute. Add wine ; bring to simmer. Add all of beef Consommé and water . Boil until reduced, about 20 minutes. Strain into medium saucepan, pressing on solids to extract as much liquid as possible.
Beef
Heat barbecue at high
Melt butter and brush tenderloin on all sides.  Put salt and pepper on both sides
Cook steak approximately 8 minutes on both side for medium
