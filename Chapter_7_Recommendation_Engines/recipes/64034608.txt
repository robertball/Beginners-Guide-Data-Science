Title:
Honey Angel Food Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 C egg whites
1 3/4 tsp cream of tartar
1 C cake flour sifted
1/2 C honey
1 C sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Place 10" tube pan in oven set @ 425 degrees.
Add salt to egg whites beat until frothy. Add cream of tartar and beat until egg whites stand in peaks. Gradually add honey beating all the time. Sift flour and sugar together & add to egg whites by thirds folding in gently with whisk or spoon.  Add vanilla.
Remove heated pan from oven & pour in batter. Thump pan several times on flat surface to break air bubbles in batter. Bake 22-25 minutes @ 425.
Remove from oven and inert on the pan legs (or raise so air can flow) Cool for at least 2 hours. Run a long thin metal spatula or knife around edge of pan separate pan & Remove bottom
