Title:
Mushroom Swiss Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987064.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds lean (at least 80%) ground beef
1 (9 ounce) pouch Progresso™ Recipe Starters™ creamy portabella mushroom cooking sauce
1/4 cup Progresso® Plain Bread Crumbs
2 teaspoons Montreal steak seasoning
6 (1 ounce) slices Swiss cheese
6 hamburger buns, split
Garnishes, if desired:
12 frozen, breaded onion rings

Directions:
Heat gas or charcoal grill for direct heat. Carefully brush oil on grill rack. In large bowl, mix beef, 3/4 cup of the cooking sauce, the bread crumbs and steak seasoning. Shape mixture into 6 patties, about 1/2 inch thick. Spray both sides of patties with cooking spray.
Place patties on grill over medium heat. Cover grill; cook 10 to 12 minutes, turning once, until meat thermometer inserted in center of patties reads 160 degrees F. Spread 1 1/2 teaspoons cooking sauce on each burger, and top with cheese slices during last 1 to 3 minutes of grilling or until cheese is melted.
Meanwhile, heat onion rings as directed on package. Place patties on bottom halves of buns; top each with 2 onion rings. Top with tops of buns.
