Title:
Homemade Ketchup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup (8 oz.) tomato sauce
2 TB. tomato paste
2 TB. Worcestershire sauce
1 TB. soy sauce
1 TB. brown sugar
1 TB. white vinegar
1/2 TSP. ground black pepper

Directions:
Thoroughly whisk together tomato sauce, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, brown sugar, vinegar,  and black pepper in a medium size bowl.
Whisk until the mixture resembles ketchup
