Title:
Hamburger Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef or turkey
1 small onion, diced
1 tsp minced garlic
1 can cream of chicken or cream of mushroom soup
1 small can tomato sauce
1 can corn
1 cup baby carrots, steamed
1 small can mushrooms, diced
1 package instant mashed potatoes
1-2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
1. Brown the meat with onion &amp; garlic
2. Add meat, corn, carrots and mushrooms to a 9x13 baking dish and mix to combine.
3. Mix together soup &amp; tomato sauce and add to the other ingredients in the pan. Mix together.
4. Prepare mashed potatoes according to package directions and spread on top of meat/vegetable mixture.
5. Top with shredded cheese.
6. Bake for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Chelsea Rice
Description: 
Made this on a whim one night when I needed a quick dinner & was short on ingredients. My family of 4 devoured the entire thing in one night. We always have leftovers, but we didn't that night!
