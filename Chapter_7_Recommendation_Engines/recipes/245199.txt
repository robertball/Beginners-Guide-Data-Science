Title:
Peach Icebox Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2675646.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups fat-free Greek yogurt, plain
1 (14 ounce) can fat-free sweetened condensed milk
1 (8 ounce) container non-dairy whipped topping, softened
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon almond extract
2 (21 ounce) cans LUCKY LEAFÂ® Premium Peach Fruit Filling & Topping
1 (16 ounce) package cinnamon graham crackers

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together yogurt and sweetened condensed milk. Fold in whipped topping. Stir in cinnamon and almond extract; set aside.
Line a 9x13-inch baking dish with parchment paper. Place a layer of cinnamon graham crackers over the parchment paper. Top with 1/3 of the whipped topping mixture. Dot with peach fruit filling. Continue adding layers, ending with fruit filling.
Cover and chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Lucky Leaf Fruit Filling
Description: 
Layers of peaches and a creamy filling on a graham cracker crust make an easy and delicious chilled dessert.
