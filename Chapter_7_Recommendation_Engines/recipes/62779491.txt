Title:
Cilantro Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 packed cup coarsely chopped fresh cilantro
1 packed cup flat leaf parsley
1/4 cup marcona or blanched almonds
2 tsp white wine vinegar

Directions:
Pour oil into blender.  Add cilantro, parsley, almonds and vinegar.  Blend until smooth.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.  Scrape into a storage container and press a piece of plastic wrap to the surface to prevent pesto from turning dark.
