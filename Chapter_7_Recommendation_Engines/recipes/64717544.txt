Title:
Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ c butter							½ tsp. baking soda
½ c. firmly packed brown sugar			½ tsp. baking powder
½ c. sugar							½ tsp. salt
1 egg							1 c. quick-cooking oats
1 tsp. vanilla						¾ c. chocolate chips
1 Tbsp. milk						¼ c. raisins (optional)
1 c. all-purpose flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Cream together the butter and sugar until smooth. Add to the creamed mixture, the egg, vanilla and milk. Stir well.
In a separate bowl combine the flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt and oatmeal. Stir into the creamed mixture. If chips or raisins are desired, stir into the batter.
Drop by teaspoonfuls about 2” apart onto a well-greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350 for 10-12 minutes or until golden.
