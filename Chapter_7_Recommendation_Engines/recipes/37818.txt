Title:
Sunny Orange French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978403.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups CREAM OF WHEAT Cereal
3 eggs, slightly beaten
3/4 teaspoon grated orange peel
1 cup orange juice
3 tablespoons granulated sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
12 slices firm white bread (3/4-inch thick)
1/4 cup margarine or butter , divided
Powdered sugar (optional)

Directions:
Place cereal on waxed paper.
Whisk eggs, orange peel, orange juice, granulated sugar and vanilla in shallow dish until well blended.
Dip bread slices into orange juice mixture; coat lightly with cereal.
Melt 1 Tbsp. margarine in large skillet over medium heat.  Add bread slices in batches to skillet; cook until browned on both sides, adding more margarine as needed.  Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
