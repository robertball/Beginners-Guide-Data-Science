Title:
Toffee Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 teaspoon maple flavoring
2 cups flour
12 ounces chocolate chips

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except chocolate chips; put in large cookie sheet and bake 15 minutes at 350. Melt chocolate bits in microwave or double boiler and spread over cookies. Top with chopped nuts if desired.  Cut into bars while still warm.

Submitter: 
SFRYMAN
Description: 
The maple flavoring in these soft shortbread bars give a buttery toffee taste without a lot of work. These have been a family favorite for three generations.
