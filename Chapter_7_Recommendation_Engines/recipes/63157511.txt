Title:
Green Olive Tapenade (spicy) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/809256.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups pitted manzanilla olives or other green Spanish olives, rinsed, drained well
1/2 tablespoon of drained capers
1 large garlic clove, minced
1/2 tsp. of black pepper
1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
1/4 cup olive oil (preferably extra-virgin)
1/2 tsp. Jalapeno powder
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Combine olives, capers, cilantro, and pepper and jalapeno powder and garlic in processor and chop finely. With motor running, gradually add lemon juice and olive oil and process until very well blended. Transfer tapenade to bowl. Can be made 1 day in advance cover and refrigerate.
Serve tapenade with crackers or baguette slices.
