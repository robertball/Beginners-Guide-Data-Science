Title:
Donut Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1045219.jpg
Ingredients:
6-8 powdered or cake donuts (leftovers work great)
2 - 3.4 oz. boxes of vanilla pudding - prepared according to the box.
fresh fruit of your choice - i.e. blueberries, strawberries, raspberries, bananas  ***
8 oz. whipped topping

Directions:
Break up the donuts and place half in a small trifle bowl or line individual dishes with donuts on the bottom.  Add a layer of prepared pudding.  Top with fresh fruit of your choice. Finish with a layer of whipped topping.  Repeat layers one time.  Top with whipped topping a few pieces of fruit.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Molly
Description: 
I created this when I had donuts starting to stale and knew no one would eat them.  But, they did devour this dessert.
