Title:
Cheddar Spirals Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package spiral pasta
2 cups half-and-half cream
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed Cheddar cheese soup
1/2 cup butter, melted
4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 TBLS. dry mustard
1/2 TSP Worchestershire sauce

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions; drain.  In a 5-qt. slow cooker, combine the cream, soup and butter until smooth; stir in the cheese and pasta.  Cover and cook on low for 2-1/2 hours or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
DRRO
Description: 
'Our kids just love this and will sample a spoonful right from the slow cooker when they walk by,' says Heidi Ferkovich of Park Falls, Wisconsin.  'Sometimes I add cocktail sausages, sliced Polish sausage or cubed ham to the cheesy pasta for a hearty all-in-one dinner.'Modified from: Cheddar Spirals
