Title:
Cranberry Waldorf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups chopped cranberries, fresh, frozen, or dried
1 cup chopped red apple
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup seedless green grapes, halved
1/3 cup raisins
1/4 cup chopped walnuts
2 tablespoons white sugar
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 (8 ounce) container vanilla yogurt

Directions:
Chop apples, cranberries, and celery in food processor.
Combine cranberries, apple, celery, grapes, raisins, walnuts, sugar, cinnamon, and yogurt. Toss to coat. Cover and chill 2 hours.

Submitter: 
ANITASHAMMER
Description: 
This is a very low-fat, low-calorie salad. It is especially great for Thanksgiving and Christmas. Freeze a few bags of cranberries to use at a later date. I know this will become a tradition to anyone who tries it, it's delicious!Modified from: Cranberry Waldorf with Yogurt
