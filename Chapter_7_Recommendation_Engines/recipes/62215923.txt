Title:
Patty's Puddin' Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf Artisan raisin bread, cut into cubes
6 eggs beaten
1 can evaporated milk
1/2 can(use same can as evap. milk) low fat milk
1 C Splenda brown sugar (see footnote)
1 Tbs Cinnamon
1 1/2 tsp. Garam Masala
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 bag chocolate chips
1/2 toasted sliced almonds,(topping)
Syrup of your choice and powdered sugar for the dusting, if you like snow on it. Fun for the kids!!

Directions:
Oil a 9 x 13 pan and add the cubed bread. In a bowl, add the rest of the ingreds. Add almonds and cover with foil. Put into the fridge overnight. The next morning, heat oven to 350 degrees. Bake for 15 mins, covered, remove foil and bake an extra 25 mins.Let cool for 5 mins. Serve with your favorite syrup. Bring on the SNOW! Enjoy:)
