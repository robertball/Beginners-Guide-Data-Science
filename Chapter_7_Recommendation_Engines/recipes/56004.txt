Title:
Fudgy Chocolate Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992841.jpg
Ingredients:
1 READY CRUST® Chocolate Pie Crust
1 (12 ounce) package semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
3/4 cup sugar
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 eggs

Directions:
Melt chips in a microwave safe bowl on high for 1 minute and stir. Microwave an additional 10-20 seconds until smooth;  stir in vanilla, set aside.
Beat cream cheese and sugar in a mixing bowl until fluffy.  Add flour and eggs, beat on low, until combined.
Add melted chocolate mixture to cream cheese mixture. Mix well and spoon into crust. Bake at 350 F for approximately 40 minutes. Chill. Serve.

Submitter: 
Ready Crust® Kitchens
Description: 
This decadent dessert made with a Ready Crust® Chocolate Pie Crust and a creamy chocolate cheesecake filling will be on everyone's holiday wish list.
