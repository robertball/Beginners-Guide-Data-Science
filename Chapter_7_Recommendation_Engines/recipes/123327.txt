Title:
Garlic Angel Hair Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976132.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces uncooked angel hair pasta
2 garlic cloves, peeled and halved
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon snipped fresh or dried chives
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions, adding garlic to the water. Drain; discard garlic. Place pasta in a serving bowl; add butter. Toss gently until butter is melted. Add Parmesan cheese, chives and garlic salt if desired; toss to coat.
