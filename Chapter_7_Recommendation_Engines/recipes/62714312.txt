Title:
Sausage Swirls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound tube breakfast sausage, (Owens mild or spicy)
1 pkg. (17 1/4 oz.) Peppridge Farm Frozen Puff Pastry, thaw according to directions.
1 cup Hot & Sweet Mustard, divided

Directions:
Brown sausage and drain.  Divide sausage in half.
Unfold one sheet of pastry and spread with 1/2 cup mustard.
Spread half of the sausage over mustard.
Roll long end tightly, wrap tight in foil.  Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
Repeat with other half of mustard and sausage on another sheet of puff pastry.
Preheat oven to 450.
Cut roll into Â½ inch slices.  Place on ungreased baking sheets, bake for 15-20 minutes, until puffed and golden brown.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
Makes 2 rolls which will be sliced into pinwheels.  The Hot & Sweet Mustard really makes these appetizers tasty.
