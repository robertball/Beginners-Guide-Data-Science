Title:
Potato Cheese Soup with Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ cup margarine
¾ large onion, sliced
1 ¼ cup celery, diced
3 ½ cups raw potatoes, diced
2 cups milk
4 cups milk
1 cup chicken broth
2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 can (7.5 oz) salmon or smoked salmon
Salt & pepper to taste
Optional: 1 tsp. thyme
1 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Microwave on high (or sauté) first 3 ingredients. Then add potatoes and microwave until tender.
Puree in blender with 2 cups milk.
Add remaining ingredients and microwave or simmer on stove until hot.
