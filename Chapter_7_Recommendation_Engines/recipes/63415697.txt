Title:
Millie's Hermit Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups raisins
1 cup water
1 tsp baking soda
1 cup butter
2 cups brown sugar
3 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
3 cups all purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Boil water, add raisins and continue boiling for one minute.
Add baking soda, stir and remove from heat to cool.
Cream butter and sugar together.
Add eggs and vanilla and mix until smooth.
In a separate bowl, mix flour, baking powder and salt.
Add dry ingredients to butter mixture and combine.
Strain raisins and add to batter.
Add nuts if using.
Drop by tablespoonful onto parchment lined cookie sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Leanne Cooke
Description: 
Millie was my Grandma and these cookies will always remind me of baking with her!  They are packed with raisins and are a great snack.
