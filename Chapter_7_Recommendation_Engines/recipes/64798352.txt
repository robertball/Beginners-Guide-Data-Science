Title:
Chicken Flavored Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked jasmine rice
1/2 tbs olive oil
2 3/4 cups of water
1/2 tsp salt
2 heaping tsps of chicken flavor "Better than Boullion"
1/2 onion chopped
2 garlic cloves minced
2 tbls butter

Directions:
1.  Sprinkle rice pot with olive oil. Heat oil to on medium heat. Add rice. Saute for 1-2 min
2. Add water, salt, butter, boullion, and chopped onion. Stir to mix ingredients, heat to boiling
3.  Cover pot with foil and then cover tightly with lid.
4. Put heat to low and simmer for 25 min
