Title:
Breakfast tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 flour or corn tortillas
2 Tbps butter
1 small onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1/8 tsp chili powder
1/2 lb lean ground turkey sausage
6 eggs beaten
1/2 cup prepared salsa
1/4 cup sour cream
1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Wrap stacked tortillas in aluminum foil and heat in oven 10 minutes or until hot.
In a large frying pan over medium heat, melt butter.
Add onion, garlic, and chili powder; sauté until onion is soft.
Meanwhile, brown sausage in a separate pan, drain any grease.
Add to sautéed mixture then pour in beaten eggs and ¼ cup salsa.
Lower heat and scramble egg mixture until eggs are thickened and no visible liquid remains; remove from heat.
Remove tortillas from oven.
Using tortillas, one at a time (keep tortillas covered as you work with them), spoon scrambled egg mixture into center of each tortilla; sprinkle with 3 Tbsp. cheese.
Fold tortillas and serve with remaining salsa, sour cream and cheddar cheese
