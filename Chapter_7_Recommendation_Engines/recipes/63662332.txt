Title:
Eggs Benedict Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1017558.jpg
Ingredients:
3 hard-cooked eggs
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp. dijon mustard
2 TBS fresh squeezed lemon juice
dash of ground cayenne pepper
1 oz. Canadian bacon, diced
1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
2 english muffins, split
chives for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Dice the hard-cooked eggs and place in small bowl.  Set aside.
In medium bowl, combine mayo, dijon mustard, lemon juice and dash of cayenne pepper.  Stir until combined.  Add in reserved diced hard-cooked eggs and stir until a creamy sauce forms.  Stir in the diced Canadian bacon and cheddar cheese.
Preheat oven to broil.  Place english muffin halves on a sheet pan and top each with about a 1/4 to a 1/3 cup of the egg mixture.
Broil for 3 - 5 minutes, or until the tops start to brown. Garnish with chopped chives, if desired. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
ReneePaj
Description: 
This quick and easy muffin' is perfect for breakfast on the go.  With all the flavors of eggs Benedict, but none of the work preparing hollindaise sauce or poaching eggs. Also great for lunch served with a salad on the side!
