Title:
Pumpkin Bars with Maple Caramel Sauce & Spiced Pecans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pumpkin Bars:
1 can (15 oz) pumpkin puree
4 eggs
3/4 cup canola oil
2 tsp vanilla extract
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 cups granulated sugar
1 Tbsp pumpkin pie spice
2 tsp cinnamon
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
Maple Caramel Sauce:
2 cups whipping cream
3/4 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup pure maple syrup
1/4 tsp maple flavouring
Spiced Pecans:
1 egg white
2 cups pecans
6 Tbsp sugar
1 Tbsp cinnamon
1/8 tsp nutmeg
1/8 tsp cloves
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
For Pumpkin Bars; Beat together pumpkin, eggs, oil, and vanilla until well mixed.
Combine dry ingredients; stir into pumpkin mixture and mix well.
Pour into a greased 15"x10"x1" baking pan.
Bake @ 350 degrees F for 20-25 minutes, or until skewer comes out clean.
For Maple Caramel Sauce: Combine cream, sugar, and maple syrup in a heavy saucepan. Stir over medium heat until sugar dissolves.
Increase heat and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until caramel coloured and reduced to 1 3/4 cups (~35 minutes); whisk occasionally.
Mix in maple flavouring. Cool slightly.
To make Spiced Pecans: Beat egg white until smooth; stir in pecans.
In a small bowl, combine sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves; stir into pecan mixture.
Spread on a baking sheet and bake @ 325 degrees F for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Remove from oven and stir in salt.
To serve: Drizzle warm maple caramel sauce over a piece of pumpkin bar and top with several spiced pecans.

Description: 
This is a perfect ending to a Fall or Winter meal - A moist spiced pumpkin bar with warm maple caramel sauce and sweetly spiced pecans. Also great with a scoop of vanilla ice cream!
