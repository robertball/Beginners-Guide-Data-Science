Title:
Zucchini & Corn Fritters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1353601.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups grated zucchini
1 cup corn kernels
1 cup grated carrots
1/3 cup minced onion
1 egg
2 T plain yogurt
1/2 t salt
1/4 t pepper
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup cornmeal
2 t baking powder
1/2 cup cheese (optional)

Directions:
Mix egg, yogurt,salt,pepper,flour,cornmeal,baking powder and cheese together.
Add zucchini, carrots, corn, and onion.
In large frying pan cover bottom with oil and heat on medium heat.
Place spoonfuls of mixture into pan, gently pressing down each fritter.
Cook aprox. 3 min. per side or until each side is golden brown.

Submitter: 
Marci
Description: 
With all the zucchini I have fresh and frozen, this is my favorite way to eat it for breakfast. It's also great for the kids to eat as a snack, cold right out of the refrigerator (no need for plate or silverware)
