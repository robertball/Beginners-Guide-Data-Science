Title:
Marinated Green Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28 oz. can French cut green beans,drained
1 small to medium white onion,minced
7 oz. white vinegar
1 tsp. Olive oil or canola oil
salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:
Rinse drained green beans and drain again.
Add mined onion, salt and pepper.
In a small cup whisk oil and vinegar together and pour immediately over beans.  Stir well and set in the refrigerator for 10 - 20 minuted or until ready to eat.

Submitter: 
Kilyena
Description: 
This is a family favorite my Mother-In Law always brought to family cook-outs.  It's great in the summer but we eat it all year round. Sometimes I substitute finely sliced radishes (small bag) in place of beans. It makes a great side dish for most any meat.
