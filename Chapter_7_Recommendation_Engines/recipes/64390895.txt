Title:
Sassy Ricotta Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C minced onion
1/4 C minced garlic
3 Tbsp olive oil(divided)
2 eggs(slightly beaten)
2 lbs ground beef
1 C grated Parmesan cheese
15 oz Ricotta
1 C Panko(or Italian)bread crumbs
2 1/2 Tbsp dried parsley(1/2 C fresh)
2 1/2 Tbsp dried basil(1/2 C fresh)
1/2 tsp oregano
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
Marinara sauce(if desired)

Directions:
Preheat oven 350. Line 2 baking sheet with parchment. Either saute onions and garlic in 1 Tbsp oil about 8 minutes or put in bowl and microwave 2 minutes, bowl will be hot! Cool. In large bowl add eggs, beef, cheese, Ricotta, bread crumbs, herbs, and salt and pepper. Add onions/garlic and mix. Make 2" or smaller balls and place on pans. Brush with 2 Tbsp oil. Bake 22 minutes or until center reaches 165F. Bring sauce to boiling, add meatballs, simmer 5 minutes.
