Title:
Mango Watermelon Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1133320.jpg
Ingredients:
Half a watermelon,1 mango,1/2 cup of water,1 tablespoon of sugar,ice(optional)

Directions:
Find a clean space to work in.Peel the skin off the mango,set aside.Fill 5 cups of watermelon and place in blender.Put the peeled mango inside the blender as well(NOT THE SEED!)Add the water and the sugar.Blend until smooth and serve with or without ice.Enjoy!

Description:
