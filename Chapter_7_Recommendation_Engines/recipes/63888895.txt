Title:
Seedy Good-Health Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1079050.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 c butter, melted
1/2 c applesauce
1/2 c white sugar
1/2 c raw sugar
2 large eggs
2 tsp vanilla extract
3/4 c all-purpose flour
3/4 c whole wheat, unbleached flour
1 1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp sea salt
1/2 c sour cream
1/2 c chopped, roasted almonds
2 bananas, mashed
1/4 c teff
1/4 c poppy seeds
1/4 c chia seeds
1/4 c flax seeds
1/4 c coconut oil or coconut oil spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F
Lightly grease 2-9x5in loaf pans with coconut oil (for raw coconut oil, use paper towel to lightly coat bottom and sides of pans, for coconut oil spray, lightly spray sides and bottom of pans)
In large bowl, stir butter, applesauce and eggs together (allow butter to cool before doing so) Add vanilla, white sugar, sea salt, raw sugar, mixing well.
In a separate bowl, mix together mashed bananas, baking soda and sour cream. Stir until smooth.
Add banana mixture to sugar mixture, stirring well.
Gradually add white flour (1/4 c at a time), stirring well.
Gradually add whole wheat flour (1/4 c at a time), stirring well.
Add almonds, teff, poppy, chia and flax to mixture, stirring well to distribute evenly.
Pour 1/2 mixture into 9x5 loaf pan. Pour remaining mixture into 9x5 loaf pan.
Bake on middle rack of oven for 40-50 minutes, or until knife or toothpick, inserted into center, comes out clean.
Allow to cool for 15 minutes before removing from pans. Cool for additional 20 minutes on wire rack. Loaves should be molasses colored - firm outside, moist inside.
Slice and serve warm or cold.

This is a rich, moist and delicious dessert bread, full of healthy goodness!
