Title:
Mom's Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cloves Garlic
1 Large Onion
1 Large Granny Smith Apple
1½ Pounds Chicken (or Beef)
2 Tablespoons Curry Powder
¼  Teaspoon Black Pepper
2 Tablespoons Flour
2 Tablespoons Lemon Juice
3 Bouillon Cubes
3 Cups Water

Directions:
Cube apple, onion, garlic, and meat.
Brown meat.
Add onion, apple, garlic, pepper, curry, flour, bouillon cubes, and lemon juice.
Add water, bring to boil, constantly stirring.
Simmer for 1½ hours.
Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Liz Bensman
Description: 
This curry recipe was shared with me by my mom.  I personally think this is the best curry ever...I love it!
