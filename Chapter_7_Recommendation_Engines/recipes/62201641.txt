Title:
Chex Party Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tbl butter
1 tsp seasoned salt
4 tbl worcestershire sauce
2 cups corn chex cereal
2 cups rice chex cereal
2 cups wheat chex cereal
1 1/2 cups mixed nuts

Directions:
Melt butter in shallow pan over low heat.
Stir in seasoned salt and worcestershire sauce.
Add chex and mixed nuts
Mix until all pieces are coated.
Heat in 250 oven for 45 minutes; stirring every 15 minutes.
Spread out on absorbent paper to cool.
Makes about 7 1/2 cups.
