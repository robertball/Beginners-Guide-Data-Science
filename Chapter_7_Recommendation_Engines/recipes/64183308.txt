Title:
Meatballs and Brown Gravy over Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1508269.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package egg noodles, prepared
1 package Armour Meatballs
4 jars of Heinz Beef Gravy

Directions:
1.  Cook meatballs in oven according to package directions.
2. Combine meatballs and gravy in a pot over medium heat until gravy is hot.
3. Serve over egg noodles.

Submitter: 
Natasha Sellner
Description: 
A quick and simple recipe for those late nights when you really don't feel like cooking.  This recipe will make sure they all get full bellies, with little effort.
