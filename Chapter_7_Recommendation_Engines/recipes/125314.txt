Title:
Shevaun's Jamaican Ten Speed Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2880579.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce melon liqueur
1 fluid ounce vodka
1 fluid ounce coconut flavored rum
3 fluid ounces cranberry juice
3 fluid ounces pineapple juice

Directions:
Pour the melon liqueur, vodka, rum, cranberry juice, and pineapple juice into a cocktail shaker over ice. Cover, and shake until the outside of the shaker has frosted. Strain into chilled martini glasses to serve.

Description: 
A non-dairy version of the sweet classic. If you like sugary drinks, this one's for you!
