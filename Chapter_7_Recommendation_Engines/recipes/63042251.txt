Title:
Crab Rangoon Dip with Parmesan Wonton Dippers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
2 tablespoons milk (or more)
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
8 ounces imitation crabmeat, chopped/flaked
1/4 cup sliced green onions
1 teaspoon dried parsley
2 tsp low sodium soy sauce
1/4 cup sweet and sour sauce/duck sauce
1 (12 ounce) packages wonton wrappers
nonstick cooking spray
parmesan cheese

Directions:
Crab Rangoon Dip:
Stir together all ingredients (except wonton wrappers and parmesan cheese).
Put in mini crockpot, set on low until dip ready - 1 hour.  (Could also make in oven or microwave).
Parmesan Wonton Dippers:
Preheat oven 375 degrees.
Cut each wonton wrapper in half.
Spray with nonstick cooking spray (on pan and on top) and sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
Bake 5-7 minutes at 375 or until slightly brown.
Cool on wire rack.
When dip is warm - Serve with parmesan wonton dippers and optional extra sweet and sour or duck sauce spiced up with sweet chili sauce.
Enjoy!
