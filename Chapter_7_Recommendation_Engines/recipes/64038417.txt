Title:
Slow Cooker Brown Sugar Balsamic Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cloves of garlic - minced
1/4 cup spicy brown mustard
1 cup brown sugar
3 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
3/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup chicken broth or water
1/2 tsp. white pepper
1 1/2 to 3 lbs. pork tenderloins

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except pork and whisk together.
Place pork in slow cooker and pour mixture over it.
Cook on high for 4 hours and flip half way through if you are able to.
