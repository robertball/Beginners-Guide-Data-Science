Title:
Spaghetti Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 spaghetti squash, halved and seeded
1 pound ground beef
1/4 cup diced green bell pepper
1/4 cup diced red onion
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, drained (or 2 chopped fresh tomatoes)
1/2 pkg mushrooms
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1 pinch thyme
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 1/4 cups shredded mozarella cheese
1 C. crushed ritz crackers

Directions:
Lightly butter squash halves, cover with foil, and cook at 357 for 40 min. Meanwhile, cook meat, onion, garlic, tomatoes, pepper, garlic, mushrooms, and spices.  When squash is cooked, scrape out and mix with meat mixture. Place in casserole dish and mix with mozarella. Top with crushed crackers and bake at 375 for 40 min.
