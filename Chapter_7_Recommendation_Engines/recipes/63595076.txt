Title:
Gluten free macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz gluten free macaroni
4-8 oz cheddar cheese
4-8 oz cream cheese
4 oz sour cream
1/2 cup pasta water

Directions:
Cook macaroni until al dente as directed on the macaroni instructions. Reserve the water from the cooked pasta. Melt cheddar and cream cheeses together in microwave. Add the pasta water to the cheese. Mix until smooth consistency adding more pasta water as needed. Pour cheese sauce over macaroni. Do not stir. Let the dish sit for 5 minutes. After it has sat, stir sauce into macaroni. Add sour cream and stir.

Submitter: 
Dawn Foster
Description: 
Gluten free, but might also work well with regular pasta. Very quick and easy pasta dish.
