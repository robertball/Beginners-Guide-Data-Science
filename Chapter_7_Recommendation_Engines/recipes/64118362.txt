Title:
Mom's Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 2 lbs. ground beef
* 1 cup min. quick oats
* 2 eggs
* 1/2 cup ketchup
* 1 small onion, chopped
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
* 1/4 cup ketchup
* 1-2 cups diced green bell pepper

Directions:
1)Mix all ingredients except 1/4 cup ketchup in large bowl. Press into 9"x5"x3" loaf pan. Spread 1/4 ketchup over top. Bake in a 350 Degree F oven for 1 hour 15 minutes. Allow to sit a few minutes before slicing.
