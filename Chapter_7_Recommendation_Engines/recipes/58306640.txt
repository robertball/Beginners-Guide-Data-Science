Title:
Wedding Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pints frozen strawberries
24 oz. frozen lemonade
2 2/3 C. sugar
6 C. cold water.
48 oz. ginger ale

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except ginger ale.  Chill.  Just before serving, add the ginger ale.
