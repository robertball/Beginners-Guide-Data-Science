Title:
Caliente Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pounds lean ground beef
1 pound ground pork sausage
1 (46 fluid ounce) can tomato juice
1 (29 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chiles (ex:rotel)
1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles
2 chopped onion
8 stalks chopped celery
3 chopped bell peppers (assortment of colors green, red and purple)
1/4 cup chili powder
2 tablespoons ground chili ancho powder
2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 teaspoon seasoned salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
1 (15 ounce) can red beans, drained and rinsed
2 (15 ounce) cans chili beans

Directions:
Place ground beef and pork in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown. Drain, crumble, and set aside.
Using the same skillet add the celery, let saute for about 5 minutes then add the onion and saute another 5 minutes.  Remove from heat and set aside.
Add all ingredients to a large kettle. Bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 2 to 3 hours, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
I adapted my mothers chili recipe to add a little more heat.  I like to serve it with basmati rice or with some cheese and tortilla strips or crackers.  Enjoy!
