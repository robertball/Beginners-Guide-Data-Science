Title:
Homeade Hashbrowns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Medium potato
1 Tablespoon EVOO
Garlic Salt to Taste
Salt & Pepper to Taste
Pinch of Red Pepper Flakes
1/2 Tbsp of Butter

Directions:
Heat the oil in the pan over med-high heat.
Slice the potato very very thin into rings and stack them up. Then slice the stack into thin strips. Throw the potato strips into the pan and continue to stir for 3 minutes. Add the salt, pepper, and garlic salt. Let sit for about 3 minutes and stir again.  Add 1/2 tablespoon of butter and keep stiring until melted. Lower the heat down to Medium and let sit for about 5 minutes then flip and continue to sit for about 3 more minutes.
Once browned to your desire. Form the potatoes into 2 patties and let sit over low heat for 2 minutes.
Serve warm.

Submitter: 
AshleyTex
Description: 
This is a real hashbrown similar to what you would find in a country restaurant. Very versitile for any spices or herbs if you prefer. Serve with 2 sunny side up eggs and bacon and you have a hearty breakfast for anyone!
