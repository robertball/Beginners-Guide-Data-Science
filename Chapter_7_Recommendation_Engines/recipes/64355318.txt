Title:
Sour Cream Coffee Cake by Bagga Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 lb margarine
3/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
1/2 pint sour cream
1 tsp baking soda
1 heaping tsp baking powder
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups flour
1/2 cup blueberries
1/4 cup cranberries,chopped
cinnamon/sugar mixture equal to 1/2 cup

Directions:
Cream sugar & margarine. Add eggs one at a time.  Add vanilla. Add 1 Cup flour, baking powder, baking soda.  Mix. Add 1/2 cup sour cream and remaining flour mixture. Fold in berries. Add remaining sour cream and mix.  Spray bundt pan with Pam. Spoon in 1/3 mixture, sprinkle with cinnamon & sugar mixture.  Add 1/3 mixture and sprinkle with cinnamon & sugar mixture.  Repeat for topping. Cook 350 for 50-54 minutes/
