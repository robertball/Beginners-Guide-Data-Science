Title:
Savory Garlic Steak Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp Liquid Smoke
2 (1/2 lb ribeyes)
2 Tbsp Brown Sugar
1 tsp. onion powder
1 1/2 Tbsp. Montreal Steak Seasoning
3 Tbsp minced garlic (about 15 - 18 cloves)
Pinch Cyanne Pepper
Pinch Salt
5 Tablespoons Balsamic Vinegar
2 Tbsp Olive Oil
1/4 cup Soy Sauce
1 tsp Worcestershire

Directions:
Place steak in a shallow glass dish. Rub well with the Liquid Smoke.  Set aside.
Mix all remaining ingredients in a small bowl. Mix well.  Pour over steaks.  For best flavor, marinade for 1-2 hours in the refrigerator.
Preheat grill for medium high to high heat.  Lightly oil grill grates. Grill steaks 7 minutes per side until reaching desired doneness.  Discard any leftover marinade.
