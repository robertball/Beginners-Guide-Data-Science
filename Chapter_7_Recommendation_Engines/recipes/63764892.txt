Title:
Quinoa Squash Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1043780.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat flour, 1 1/2 cups flour, 1/2 tsp salt, 1 1/2 tsp baking powder, 1 tsp baking soda, 1 1/2 tsp cinnamon, dash of cloves/nutmeg/allspice/ginger/cayenne - whatever you like, 1/2 cup brown sugar, 1 2/3 cups cooked quinoa, 1 1/2 cups cooked or shredded squash, 2 eggs, 1 tsp vanilla, 1 1/2 cups yogurt, 1/2 cup raisins, 1/2 cup walnuts

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients together. In separate bowl, mix remaining ingredients together. Mix in dry ingredients. Bake for 30 minutes until dark golden brown in cupcake tins.

Submitter: 
moonl8
Description: 
Protein and texture from the Quinoa, Sweet pockets from the squash and raisins. Enjoy.
