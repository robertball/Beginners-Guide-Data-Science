Title:
Easy Key Lime Pie I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 egg yolks, beaten (Reviewers suggest 4)
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk (Reviewers suggest 2 cans)
1/2 cup key lime juice (Reviewers suggest 3/4)
1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Combine the egg yolks, sweetened condensed milk and lime juice. Mix well. Pour into unbaked graham cracker shell.
Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes. Allow to cool. Top with whipped topping and garnish with lime slices if desired.
