Title:
Grandma's Microwave Toffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped almods
1 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup butter
3 tblsp water
1/2 cup real chocolate chips

Directions:
1. butter an 8x8 in pan and sprinkle with nuts, set aside.
2.In heat proof bowl combine sugar, butter, and water. Cook on high power in the microwave for 5-6 minutes or until thick and creamy. Stir several times. When mixture is ready a drop in water will hard ball. Quickly pour over nuts. Sprinkle chocolate chips. Cover with wax paper and let stand a few minutes. Spread melted chocolate chips over toffee. Chill in refrig. until hard. Break into serving pieces.
