Title:
Rice Cooker Mexican Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup rice
2 1/4 cups chicken broth
1/2 of a 6oz can of tomato paste
2 tbs butter (optional)
1/2 cup diced onion
1 garlic clove minced

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in rice cooker and cook according to instructions.
