Title:
Grilled Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salmon filet
sliced lemon
stir fry or teriyaki sauce
wood plank

Directions:
Soak the wood plank for 20 minutes.
Place salmon filet on plank.
Cover with sauce.
Place sliced lemons on top.
Cook on preheated grill for 15-20 minutes on medium.
