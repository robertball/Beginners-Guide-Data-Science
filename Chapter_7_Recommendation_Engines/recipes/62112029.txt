Title:
Chinese Spareribs and Pineapple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon wine
2 tablespoon sugar
3 tablespoon vinegar
2 tablespoon light soy sauce
2 tablespoon dark soy sauce
5 tablespoon water
1 pound spareribs
1 can of sliced pineapples

Directions:
Cut ribs into small pieces.
Stir-fry ribs in a medium pot with high heat.
Pour in all other ingredients, turn to medium heat.  Let it stew for 1 hour or until most of the liquid dries up.  Don't let all the sauce dry up completely though!
Use pineapples to garnish plate and serve with spareribs.

Submitter: 
sugarnsp1ce
Description: 
This is one of my favorite classic Chinese dishes that my mom makes for Chinese New Year's. It's easy to make and is very savory with rice!
