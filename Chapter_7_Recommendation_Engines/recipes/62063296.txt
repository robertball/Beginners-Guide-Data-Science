Title:
fudgsicles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. instant pudding (any flavor)3.9 oz size,   1/2 cup sugar,   3 cups milk

Directions:
combine all in blender.  Pour into popsicle molds or dixie cups.  Freeze till firm.  once thick, add craft/popsicle sticks.  Once hardened serve by peeling off cover/paper cup.
