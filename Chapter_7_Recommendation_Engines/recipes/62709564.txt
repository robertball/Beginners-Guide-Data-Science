Title:
Quick Chicken Biscuit Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/614905.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cubed grilled chicken breast meat, shredded into bite size pieces
2 cups broccoli florets, slightly steamed and chopped fine
(10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of potato soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 cup skim milk
Salt & Pepper to taste
1 box Jiffy Buttermilk Biscuit Mix

Directions:
In a Dutch Oven combine chicken, broccoli, cream of potato soup, cream of chicken soup and milk.  Heat through stirring constantly until bubbling.
Make Jiffy Buttermilk Biscuit Mix according to directions.  Place on top of filling and bake in preheated 450 degree oven for 10-12 minutes until biscuits are golden brown.

Submitter:

Description: 
My modification of Tracy Hibb's Chicken Pot Pie Soup.  Using reduced calorie ingredients makes this soup with cans condensed cream of potato and chicken soup combined with broccoli and cubes of cooked chicken both low in both calories and fat.Modified from: Chicken Pot Pie Soup
