Title:
Quite Possibly the Best, Good for You & Tastes Good Too Fruit Smoothie! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/650131.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large Naval orange
1 large apple, chopped
1 large peach (or necterine), cut into 1x1 inch chuncks
1 large banana, broken into 2 inch pieces
1 cup diced fresh (or frozen) strawberries

Directions:
Using a citrus juicer, juice 1/2 the orange in a blender
Add apple, peach, banana, and strawberries
Juice the other half of the orange on top
Blend til smooth
Drink

Submitter: 
Candice
Description: 
A superior way to start your day (or end it) while getting the recommended number of fruits per day in one easy to prepare Smoothie. Oh! And By-The-Way... It's DELICIOUS!!!
