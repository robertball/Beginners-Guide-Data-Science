Title:
Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2341565.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds potatoes washed and sliced thinly
1 medium onion, sliced thinly (optional)
10 oz can Campbell's Cream of Broccoli Soup
3 1/2 cups cold water (to combine for 4 c liquid with soup)
3 tbsp cornstarch
black pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
Spray bottom and sides of 9" x 13" pan or casserole dish with cooking spray
using hand blender mix in the cornstarch into the soup and water and to break up the broccoli chunks
Place potatoes, onion slices and sauce in alternating arrangement in dish sprinkle pepper in between the layers
Bake in oven 50-60 minutes or until done
Let stand about 10 minutes before serving

my adaption of the classic quick recipe using cream of broccoli soup
