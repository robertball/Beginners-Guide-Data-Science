Title:
S'More Bars I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7480071.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup white sugar
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 egg
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups milk chocolate candy bar, chopped
3 cups miniature marshmallows
1/2 cup chopped peanuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).  Lightly grease 13 x 9 x 2 inch pan.
In large mixer bowl combine sugar, butter and egg until creamy (2 or 3 minutes).   Reduce mixer speed and add flour, baking powder and salt until blended.
Spread batter into baking pan.   Bake for 10 to 12 minutes, until set.
Sprinkle chopped chocolate bar  over batter and top with marshmallows and peanuts.  Continue baking 10 to 12 minutes until marshmallows are lightly browned.  Cool and cut into bars.

Submitter: 
Mellan
Description: 
These taste amazingly like the ones we use to make sitting by the fire.  You could use chocolate chips (semi-sweet or milk chocolate) instead of the chocolate bar.
