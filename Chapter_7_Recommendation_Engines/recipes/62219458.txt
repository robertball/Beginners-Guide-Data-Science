Title:
Fudge Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz unsweetened chocolate
1 cup melted-hot butter
1 tsp vanilla
4 eggs
2 cups sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease a 9x13 pan.
Mix chocolate and melted hot butter in sauce pan until well mixed and liquid.
Mix all the other ingredients into the chocolate and butter until just mixed.  DO NOT WHIP AIR INTO THE MIXTURE.
Pour into the 9x13 pan.
Bake for 30-40 minutes at 350 degrees or until the brownie puffs in the center.
Cool, chill.
