Title:
southwest tortellini salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/853843.jpg
Ingredients:
2 packages cheese-filled tortellini
2 tomatoes, diced
1 green pepper, diced
1 red pepper, diced
1/2 red onion, finely diced
1 jalapeno, finely diced
cilantro to taste
1/4 c. oil
1/4 c. lime juice
1 Tbsp. honey
2 garlic cloves, crushed (or more to taste)
2 tsp. cumin
1 Tbsp. chili powder
1/2 tsp. salt
cider vinegar to taste (1-5 Tbsp)
hot sauce (sriracha) to taste

Directions:
cook tortellini and chill. dice all vegetables and add to chilled tortellini. for dressing, combine oil, lime juice, honey, garlic, spices, and vinegar in jar with tight fitting lid and shake to combine.
pour over salad and shake to coat. let marinate prior to serving

Submitter: 
Laura 'Nymoen' Westberg
Description: 
lime juice, cumin and jalapeno give this oil and vinegar-based tortellini salad some zip as a hearty side dish
