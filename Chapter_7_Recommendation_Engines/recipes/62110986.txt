Title:
Layered Strawberry Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25oz) package butter recipe golden cake mix
2/3 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup butter, softened
3 eggs
1/2 cup strawberry preserves, divided
2 cups whipping cream
3 tablespoons powdered sugar
2 quarts strawberries, sliced

Directions:
Beat first 4 ingredients at low speed until cake mix is moistened.  Beat at medium speed for 4 minutes.  Pour batter into 2 greased and floured 9 inch round cake pans.
Bake at 350 for 18-20 min.  Cool on wire racks for 10 min.  Brush top of each layer with 2 tablespoons preserves.  Cool completely on wire racks.
Beat remaining preserves with whipping cream and powdered sugar at high speed util stiff peaks form.
Place 1 layer on serving plate.  Arrange half of strawberries over layer.  Top with half of whipped cream mixture.  Repeat with remaining layer.  Garnish with extra strawberries.
