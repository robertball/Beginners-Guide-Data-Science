Title:
Mom's Wilted Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 slices bacon
1 (6-oz) bag spinach
2 green onions, thinly sliced
1/4 C sugar
1/3 C vinegar
1/2 t seasoned salt
Dash lemon-pepper seasoning
1/2 t garlic powder
1/4 t dry mustard
2 hard-cooked eggs, peeled and sliced

Directions:
Cook bacon in skillet or on microwave-sake rack. Set aside 1/4 C drippings for the dressing. Drain bacon on paper towels. Crumble bacon; set aside
Tear spinach into bite-size pieces, removing any tough stems. Combine spinach and green onions in salad bowl.
Heat reserved bacon drippings, wntil warm; pour over salad.
In 2-cup glass measure, stir together sugar, vinegar, seasoned salt, lemon-pepper, garlic powder and mustard. Microwave on high until mixture boils, about 1 min. Stir to dissolve sugar; immed. pour over salad, mixing well until spinach is evenly coated. Garnish with bacon and eggs. Serve immed.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Similar to the wilted lettuce salad Mom used to make that Dad loved. Uses bacon drippings to wilt spinach.
