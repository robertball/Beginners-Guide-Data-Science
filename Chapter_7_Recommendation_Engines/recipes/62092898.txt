Title:
Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lb Rump Roast
1 can low sodium beef broth
1/4 cup cider vinegar
1 T. salt
4 whole garlic cloves
1/2 onion halved and sliced
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in large pan on medium heat. Brown roast on all sides.
Add roast to crock pot.
Add broth, vinegar, salt, garlic and onion, and pepper if desired.
Cover and cook on low heat 6-8 hours.

Submitter: 
SaresMama
Description: 
Tender moist beef pot roast that has amazing flavor. You'll get compliments everytime after making it!
