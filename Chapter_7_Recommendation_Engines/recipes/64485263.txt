Title:
Grilled Caprese Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2544817.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 baguette, cut lengthwise
Drizzle oil oil (~2 tablespoons)
1 garlic clove, minced
Salt and pepper to taste
1 Tomato, sliced thin
1 teaspoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon dried basil
4 oz mozzarella, sliced thin
Salt and pepper to taste
6 Fresh basil leaves
Sprig of parsley (optional)

Directions:
Preheat Grill on low heat.
Slice baguette lengthwise. Drizzle bread with olive oil, add minced garlic and season with salt & pepper to taste.
Add sliced tomatoes, season tomato again with salt, pepper, parsley and basil. Top tomatoes with sliced mozzarella.
Place bread on tin foil and grill on low heat for 5-8 minutes or until the cheese begins to melt.
Top with fresh basil and parsley.
Cut into slices and serve.
