Title:
Orange Slice cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups butter
3 cups sugar
5 eggs
3 cups flour
1 tbsp. orange extract
3/4 cup orange soda

Directions:
Cream together butter and sugar, beat until light and fluffy.Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Add flour, beat in orange extract and orange soda.
Pour batter into well greased and floured bundt pan.
Bake at 325 degrees for 1 hour 15 minutes.
