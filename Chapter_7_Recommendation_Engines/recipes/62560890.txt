Title:
Fish Tacos in lettuce "shells" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/536301.jpg
Ingredients:
4 4oz tilapia fillets
1 tsp cajun seasoning
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp garlic salt
1 tsp pepper
juice of one lemon
12 romaine lettuce leaves
1/2 avocado
1/4 onion, minced
1/2 jalepeno, seeded and minced
1 small garlic clove, minced
1 Tbsp water
1 tsp dried cilantro
1 cup sliced cherry tomatoes

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine, cajun seasoning, chili powder, paprika, garlic salt, and pepper. Sprinkle seasoning mixture and lemon juice on each side of tilapia fillets. Bake fillets on 400 degrees for 8-10 minutes, or until cooked through.
shred tilapia and divide each fillet into 3 equal portions. Place each portion into a romaine leaf.
to make avacado salsa verde, blend avacado, onion, jalapeno, garlic, and water in a blender. Stir in  cilantro.
Top fish tacos with verde, and cherry tomatoes

Description: 
Baked tilapia, flaked and placed in romaine lettuce leaves with tomatoes, cilantro and avacado salsa verde
