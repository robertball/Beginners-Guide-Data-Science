Title:
Kid Approved Bento Toolbox Lunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4537254.jpg
Ingredients:
1 whole wheat tortilla
1 apple, sliced
Peanut butter
Celery
Baby carrots
Grape tomatoes
Golden tomatoes
Ranch dressing

Directions:
Spread peanut butter onto the whole wheat tortilla and place apple slices end on end down the middle of the tortilla. Roll up the tortilla tightly and cut into serving pieces.
Cut celery into smaller strips for the little ones.
Serve celery, tomatoes and carrots with ranch dressing.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
Every little one enjoys a healthy lunch complete with toys.  This toolbox lunch comes complete with a tractor loader, dump truck and utensil tools for the lunch break. Eating healthy was never more fun.
