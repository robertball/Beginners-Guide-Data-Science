Title:
A different Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large potatoes, boiled
4 stems of green onions, chopped
1 bunch of cilantro, chopped
1 tablespoon of turmeric
1 stick of butter
salt to taste

Directions:
Boil the potatoes and peel.
Add butter to the potatoes and mix.
Add the other ingredients and mix,try to keep it chunky..
Serve.

Submitter: 
IMANKAY
Description: 
This potato salad consists of cilantro, green onions, and turmeric. This is a great variation from mayonnaise potato dishes that are so fattening.
