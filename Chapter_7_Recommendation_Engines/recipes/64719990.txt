Title:
Gluten Free Cinnamon & Pumpkin Spice Chex Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5887349.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups Gluten Free Corn Chex Cereal
1 cup Peanuts
1/4 c butter, melted
1/2 Tbsp. Cinnamon
1/2 Tbsp. Brown Sugar
1 tsp. Pumpkin spice
pinch of salt
1 cup Milk Chocolate Chips (optional)
1 cup Raisins (optional)
1/2 cup Pumpkin Seeds (optional)

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 250Â° F.
Melt the butter in a microwave, about 40 seconds. (Cover bowl with wax paper)
In a small bowl, combine the cinnamon, brown sugar, pumpkin spice and pinch of salt. Mix in butter.
In a large bowl add the Corn Chex cereal, peanuts and pumpkin seeds. Add in butter and spice mixture and blend until coated.
Spread cereal mix onto a large baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
Bake for 45 minutes, stirring every 15-20 minutes.
Remove from the oven, allow to cool, and serve. Store in airtight container.
