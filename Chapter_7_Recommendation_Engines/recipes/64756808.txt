Title:
Instant Pot Korean Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp.  Olive Oil
2 Lbs. Ground Beef (80/20 lean or leaner)
1 Tbsp. Minced Garlic
1/2 Cup Beef Broth
1/2 Cup Soy Sauce
1/2 Cup Dark Brown Sugar
1 Tbsp. Sesame Oil
1/2 Tbsp. Ginger Paste
1/2 tsp. Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
1/2 tsp. Black Pepper
1/2 Tbsp. Mirin or Rice Wine
1 Cup Rice Rinsed Well
2 Tbsp. Butter
2 Cups Water
Green Onion for garnish
Sesame Seeds for garnish

Directions:
1. Turn Instant Pot to Saute mode.
2. Add olive oil to the bottom and ground beef
3. Brown the ground beef, add the minced garlic, and stir.
4. Once browned, add all sauce ingredients and stir well.
5. Add a trivet to the pot, and a 6 Inch pot on top of the trivet.
6. Add the rinsed rice, the butter, and water
7.  Put the lid on, and lock it into place.
8. Set valve to Sealing.
9. Use the manual setting to set to 4 minutes.
10. Let naturally release for 10 minutes, then quick release.
11. Remove rice and fluff with a fork.
12. Garnish with green onion and toasted sesame seeds.
