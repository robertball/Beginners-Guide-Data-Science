Title:
Matcha Green Tea Ice Latte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/860166.jpg
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon green tea powder (matcha)
2 1/2 cups hot water
1/2 cup 2% milk
4 teaspoons honey

Directions:
Whisk the green tea powder, water, and milk together in a bowl; sweeten with the honey and continue whisking until dissolved into the tea. Chill completely in the refrigerator. Serve poured over ice.

Submitter: 
GK
Description: 
Iced latte made out of the finest Japanese green tea. Enjoy this drink as a cool and refreshing alternative during the hot summer days.
