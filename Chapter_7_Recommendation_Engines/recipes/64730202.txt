Title:
Coq Au Vin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz sliced bacon
6 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs salt and pepper to taste
2 shallots, sliced
garni  parsley (or parsley stalks, which have lots of flavour), a few sprigs of thyme and a bay leaf.
1/2 large yellow onion, diced (traditionally they use pearl onions)
10 large button mushrooms, quartered
2 tsp butter or more for thicker gravy
2 tsp flour   or more for thicker gravy
1 1/2 cups red wine
1 cup chicken broth or stock
springs thyme or parsely for after

Directions:
cook bacon from cold pan medium heat until crisp remove and brown chicken on higher heat and remove
keep 1tbsp of fat dicard the rest
cook up in pan onions  shallots stir then cook up mushrooms with a big pitch of salt stir  scrape bottom  until carmalized then add butter and flour cook a minute or two then add wine stir until back to a simmer add in herbs add bacon reduce by a third add in broth stir then add chicken when it starts to simmer bask then put in 375 oven for 1 hour or tender (bask half way)
when done remove chicken and cover and reduce sauce on high until slightly thickened and  (SKIM THE FAT) TASTE  for seasoning watch for to much salt
serve on plate add sauce on top perhaps more thyme maybe parsely on top
