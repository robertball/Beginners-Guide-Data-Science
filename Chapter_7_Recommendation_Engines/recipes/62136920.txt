Title:
Toblerone® Shortbread Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1/4 cup cornstarch
2 Toblerone® bars, broken into pieces

Directions:
In a medium sized bowl, cream butter and sugar together.
Add cinnamon and vanilla extract, mix until ingredients are well whipped.
To the wet ingredients, gradually add the flour and cornstarch and beat until mixture is light.
Measure a tablespoon of batter and form into a ball.
Push a piece of Toblerone® into the centre of the ball and pinch the opening closed.
Place on a cookie sheet and lightly flatten the cookie.
Bake cookies at 375 degrees for 12-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
erin.parker
Description: 
A great traditional shortbread cookie recipe, with a twist! The chocolate in the middle of these cookies makes them irresistible.
