Title:
Jennie's Heavenly Crock Pot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breast halves
2 T. butter
1 envelope (.7 oz) Italian dry salad dressing mix
1 can (10.34 oz) condensed golden mushroom soup
8 oz. tub cream cheese with chive and onion
1/2 c. white wine
rice

Directions:
Place chicken in a crock pot. In a medium saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in Italian salad dressing mix. Stir in mushroom soup, cream cheese, and wine until combined. Pour the soup mixture over chicken. Cover and cook on low-heat setting for 4 hours. Serve the chicken and soup mixture over hot  rice.

Submitter: 
Jennie Hood Flynn
Description: 
This is the best chicken crock pot recipe ever, and I've tried a lot of them.  I can't remember where I originally got it from, my version has been tweaked a little.  I don't like mushrooms but the mushrooms in the condensed soup are really big and easy to take out. :)
