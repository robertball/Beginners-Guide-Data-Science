Title:
Coconut Flour - Almond Butter and Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1111358.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sifted coconut flour
1 cup almond butter
3/4 cup honey
4 eggs
3/4 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 cup raisins

Directions:
1) preheat oven to 375F
2) mix together almond butter, honey, eggs and vanilla
3) sift in coconut flour while constantly stirring
4) fold in raisings
5) drop by the spoonful on a greased cookie sheet
6) bake at 375F for 14 minutes
7) take out of oven and wait for cookies to cool slightly and remove from the cookie sheet while they are still warm; can cool on a rack or just leave loose on the cookie sheet
