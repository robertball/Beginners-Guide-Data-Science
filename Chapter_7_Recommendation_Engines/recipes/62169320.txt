Title:
Glazed Lemon Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1040959.jpg
Ingredients:
3 C flour
1 1/2 C sugar
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
zest of 2 lemons (reserve 1 tbsp)
2 eggs
2/3 C vegetable oil
1 C buttermilk
2 tsp vanilla extract
Juice from 2 lemons
12 oz blueberries
1/2 tsp nutmeg
For glaze:
1/2 C butter, melted
2 C powdered sugar
2 tsp vanilla
1/4 C hot water
remaining lemon zest

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Line muffin tray with paper baking cups.
In large bowl stir together flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, 1 tbsp lemon zest, and nutmeg.
In medium bowl whisk eggs, vegetable oil, buttermilk, vanilla, and lemon juice
Stir wet ingredients into dry ingredients until just moistened.  Stir in the blueberries.
Spoon mixture into baking cups, about 3/4 of the way full.  Bake 20-25 minutes.
Remove muffins from oven and transfer to wire cooling rack.  Combine butter, powdered sugar, vanilla, hot water, and remaining lemon zest in a medium bowl.  Whisk to combine.  After muffins have cooled for 15 minutes, dip muffin tops in glaze.  Allow to firm up, approximately 10 minutes.  Dip in glaze a second time.
