Title:
Slow Cooker Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/451401.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup orange juice
12 oz. cranberries (fresh or frozen)
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 granulated sugar
1/4 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Put freshed or thawed cranberries into 2 quart slow cooker.
Add orange juice, water, brown sugar, granulated sugar and cinnamon.
Stir ingredients together.
Cover and cook on high for 3 hours.
Stir cranberries every hour.
Push down on all cranberries with a spoon when softened and stir well.
After 3 hours, take lid off slow cooker and heat cranberries on high for another 45 minutes.
Can be served immediately when hot or let cool if preferred.
