Title:
Peanutty Peanut Butter Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2206499.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all purpose flour
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1/2 cup (1 stick) butter
1/4 cup peanut butter
1 1/2 cups sugar
1/2 cup peanut butter
4 large eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1/4 cup coconut milk
1/2 cup coarsely chopped dry roasted peanuts

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.  Spray 9"x13" pan.
Place flour and powdered sugar in large bowl.  Cut in butter and peanut butter until crumbly.  Reserve 1 cup mixture, press rest into prepared pan.  Bake 10 minutes
While that is baking, mix 1/2 cup reserved mixture, sugar, 1/2 cup peanut butter, eggs, vanilla and coconut milk.
Pour peanut butter mixture over baked crust. Sprinkle rest of reserved mixture and chopped peanuts on top.  Bake another 25 minutes.
Cool completely, cut into bars.
Store covered, in refrigerator.

Submitter:

Description: 
Peanut lover delights!  Peanut butter crust, peanut butter filling and peanuts themselves make these bars a treat for any time!
