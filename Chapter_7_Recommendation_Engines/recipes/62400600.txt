Ingredients:
1 cup of mayo (I used light)
1/2 cup of sour cream (again, I used light)
1 lb of macaroni (I used Whole Grain shells)
2 cans of tuna in water (drained)
1 8 oz block of cheddar cheese (cubed)
1 cup of frozen peas
1 tsp of celery salt
1/2 tsp of onion salt

Directions:
Mix mayo, sour cream and seasonings. Add in tuna and cubed cheese. Rinse frozen peas with the cooked pasta- drain well and stir to combine. Chill well before eating. Enjoy ?

A light summer salad we ate for dinner on hot summer nights when I was growing up.
