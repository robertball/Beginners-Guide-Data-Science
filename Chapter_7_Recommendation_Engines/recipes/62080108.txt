Title:
Guera's brocollie and cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package brocollie cuts (2lb. bag)
1 jar Ragu Double Cheddar Sauce can be substitude with Velveta- diced and melted)
pinch salt

Directions:
1.)Boil brocollie till tenderwith salt.
2.)Drain and return to pan.
3.)Add Double Cheddar sauce to brocollie and bring to a boil.
4.)Once boiled remove from heat and serve.

Submitter: 
GUERAGUERRERO
Description: 
We eat this every year as a side dish on Thanksgiving Day.It is a easy recipe that everyone will love even the kids who hate vegetables. My husband hated brocolli until he ate this recipe of mine, this is the only way he will it brocolli. My daughter who is a very pickie eater even loves this one!!
