Title:
Yogurt Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Nonfat Greek Yogurt
1 low fat graham cracker
3 Tablespoons sugar, or to taste

Directions:
Crush graham cracker.  Add graham cracker and sugar to yogurt.  Mix well.  Freeze completely.  Thaw completely.  Serve.

Scott Simmons
