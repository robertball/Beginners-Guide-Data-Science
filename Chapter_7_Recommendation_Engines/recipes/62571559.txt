Title:
Chicken Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups cold cooked rice
1 cup finely chopped mushrooms
2/3 cup diced chicken breast
4 green onions finely chopped
1 egg white
2 eggs plus 1 egg yolk
Salad oil
Sesame oil
Salt
Aji
1 T. cornstarch
2 tsp. sake
1 1/2 tsp. ginger grated

Directions:
Loosen rice.
Mix in bowl: sake, egg white, ginger and cornstarch. Add chicken and coat well.
Combine eggs with salt and fry in oil and set aside.
In 3 T. of hot oil, stir fry chicken and remove.
Heat 3 T. oil and 1 tsp. sesame oil and add mushrooms, onions, sake and 1 1/2 tsp. salt.
Add chicken and egg and stir fry over medium heat.
Mix in 1/4 tsp. aji and mix thoroughly over medium heat.

Submitter: 
Julie Moon

The best fried rice I've ever had.
