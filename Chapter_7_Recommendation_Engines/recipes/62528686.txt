Title:
Cranberry Fluff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz package cream cheese
1 can cranberry sauce
1 10oz can crushed pineapple-drained-
1 12oz container whipped topping-thawed-

Directions:
Beat cream cheese with mixer.Mix till fluffy,add cranberry sauce and mix w/ mixer.Add pineapple and fold in whipped topping.Freeze or serve very cold. ENJOY!!!:)
