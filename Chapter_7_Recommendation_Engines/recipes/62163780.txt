Title:
Onion Chicken Fingers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 envelope onion soup mix
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1 pound chicken breast, cut in strips
2 eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine soup mix with bread crumbs.
Dip chicken pieces in egg.
Coat chicken with crumb mixture.
Place on baking sheet and bake til there's no pink, about 40 minutes.
Turn chicken after 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
linda
Description: 
The onion flavour adds a nice twist to this old classic.
