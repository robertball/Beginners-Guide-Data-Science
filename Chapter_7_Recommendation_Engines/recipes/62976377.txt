Title:
Peanut Butter Cup Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/737836.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 1/2 cups chocolate cookie wafer crumbs
1/3 cup butter, melted
Filling:
5 packages (8oz) cream cheese, softened
1 cup sugar
1 cup creamy peanut butter
2 tsp. vanilla extract
3 eggs, lightly beaten
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
Topping:
several chocolate peanut butter cup candies coarsely chopped
semi-sweet chocolate chips, melted (for drizzling)

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the chocolate wafer crumbs and butter.  Press onto the bottom and 1 in. up the sides of a 10-in. spring form pan.
In a large bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth.  Add peanut butter and vanilla; mix well.  Add eggs; beat on low just until combined.  Stir in chocolate chips.  Pour over crust.
Bake at 350 degrees for 65-75 minutes or until center is set.  Turn off oven and let set until cool.
For Topping, sprinkle with coarsely chopped peanut butter candies then drizzle with melted chocolate.  Let cool in refrigerator for several hours or overnight.
