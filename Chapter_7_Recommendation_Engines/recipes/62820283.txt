Title:
The World's Best Canned Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup dark brown sugar, lightly packed
1/2 cup white sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
3/4 cup half 'n' half (or milk)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 29 ounce can or jar, freestone peaches or peaches in a heavy syrup (please see footnotes for best results), lightly drained & chopped
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine flour, sugars, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon.
Add vanilla to the half 'n' half and mix with the dry ingredients.  Then, stir in melted butter until the mixture is thoroughly combined.
Spoon the mixture into a greased 9x13 inch baking pan and spread it out evenly. Spoon peaches evenly on top of the mixture and sprinkle with cinnamon.
Bake in preheated oven for 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Brent BeSaw
Description: 
The best quick & easy peach cobbler that uses canned peaches.  Great to make any time of the year.  Watch it disappear in record time when you serve it with vanilla ice cream!Modified from: The World's Best Canned Peach Cobbler
