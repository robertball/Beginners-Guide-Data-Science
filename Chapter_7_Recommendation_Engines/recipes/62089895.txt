Title:
Oranges with Chocolate Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. Sour Cream
2 T. light cream
1 oz. semi-sweet chocolate, grated
1/4 c. sugar
1/2 tsp. vanilla
6 chilled oranges, in sections

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, except oranges.
Turn into small serving bowl.
Chill for at least 30 minutes.
Serve with oranges for dipping.

Submitter: 
Rachel Hardy Cunningham
Description: 
This dip is to die for.  My ex mother-in-law used to make this for holidays.  It was always the first thing to go. You can also use strawberries or pineapple for dipping, if desired.
