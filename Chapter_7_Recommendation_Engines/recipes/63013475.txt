Title:
Pear Pizza Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pita breads
2 teaspoons Olive Oil
1/2 cup crumbled mild Gorgonzola cheese
1 medium ripe pear, thinly sliced
1/4 cup toasted almond slivers
1 tablespoon Honey
1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Place pita breads on an ungreased baking sheet. Brush with oil; sprinkle with cheese. Top with pear slices and almonds.
Bake a 400 Degrees for 12-15 minutes or until bread is crisp and cheese is melted.
Combine honey and vinegar; drizzle over pits.
Cut into wesges and serve warm.

Submitter: 
Deedles
Description: 
Sweet and savory wedges that are a huge hit every time. You won't be able to stop eating these.
