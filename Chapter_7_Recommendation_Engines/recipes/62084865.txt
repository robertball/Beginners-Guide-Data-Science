Title:
Mexican Chicken Tortilla Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless/skinless chicken breast halves, cut into 1/2" pieces
2 cans cream of chicken soup
10 oz. salsa
3 cups Monterrey Jack cheese, shredded
10 (12") flour tortillas cut into 1" pieces

Directions:
Stir fry chicken pieces in hot oil until thoroughly cooked.  In a large bowl, mix together chicken, soup, salsa and 2 1/2 cups of the cheese. Transfer to a greased 9x13 baking dish.  Bake uncovered at 350ยบ for 30-40 minutes or until hot and bubbly.  Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup cheese during last 5 minutes of baking.  Great served with additional salsa and Spanish rice.
