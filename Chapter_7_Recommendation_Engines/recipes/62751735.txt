Title:
Chewy Shreddies Cereal Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¾ cup light corn syrup
¾ cup smooth peanut butter
3 cups shreddies cereal
½ cup dried apricots
¼ cup unsweetened flaked coconut
¼ cup dried cranberries

Directions:
Spray 8-inch square pan with cooking spray; set aside. Microwave corn syrup and peanut butter in large microwavable bowl on high 1 ½ minutes or until peanut butter is almost melted; stir until well blended.
Add remaining ingredients; mix well. Press into prepared pan.
Refrigerate 1 hour or until firm.

Submitter: 
Amanda
Description: 
Great snack time option for kids or at the office or when you're on the go!
