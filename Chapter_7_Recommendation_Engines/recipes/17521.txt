Title:
Fruit Dip III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/276845.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
2 tablespoons orange juice
1/4 cup confectioners' sugar
1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme

Directions:
Cream together cream cheese, orange juice, and powdered sugar. Fold in marshmallow creme.

Submitter: 
Jodi Lynn Longenecker
Description: 
This creamy, marshmallowy, orangey dip is great for fruits!
