Title:
Scot's Pickled Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Assorted fresh veggies, cut in pieces
1 cup sugar
1 cup white vinegar
1 cup water
2  1/2 tbsp Kosher salt (or less)
Big pinch red pepper flakes
12 black peppercorns
2 cloves garlic
1/4 tsp dried dill

Directions:
Sightly grill veggies just until they have some grill marks (Note: grilling is optional).
Combine  all ingrdients except the veggies in large sauce pan.
Bring to simmer and remove from heat.
Tightly pack veggies into sterilized canning jar.
Pour brine over veggies in jar and place lid.
Refrigerate for at least 24 hours before eating.
Keep refrigerated after opening.

Submitter:

Description: 
Quick way to use up garden fresh veggies and make a healthy snack.
