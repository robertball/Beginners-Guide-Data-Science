Title:
Red Lobster Garlic Cheese Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups minus 1 tablespoon flour
1 tablespoon plus 2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
Pinch salt
3 tablespoons butter, chilled
3/4 cup buttermilk
1 1/2 ounces sharp cheddar cheese, grated
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450
Line baking sheet with parchment, spray with cooking spray
Stir together flour, baking powder, soda and salt
Add 2 tablespoons of butter; blend with pastry cutter
Add buttermilk and cheese, combine with fork
Handle dough as little as possible
Drop by spoonfuls onto baking sheet; bake 10 minutes
Melt remaining butter with garlic powder
Brush biscuits with butter mixture when they are done
Serve immediately
