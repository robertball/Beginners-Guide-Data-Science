Title:
Best Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/359590.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground beef or turkey
1/2 cup water
1 egg
3/4 cup bread crumbs
1 tbs dried or fresh basil
1 tbs dried or fresh oregano
1 tbs garlic powder
1 medium onion, finely chopped
salt and pepper to taste
1 can tomato paste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl, expect water and tomato paste.
When ingredients are barely mixed, begin adding water slowly until all water is used.
Stir all ingredients together until completely mixed.
Spread meat mixture in a lightly greased 9 by 13 loaf pan. Spread tomato paste evenly on top.
Bake 40-60 minutes until completely cooked.
