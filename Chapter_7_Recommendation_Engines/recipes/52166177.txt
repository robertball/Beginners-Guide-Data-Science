Title:
Three Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/463913.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15 oz) can each green beans, yellow wax beans and light red kidney beans
1 onion, sliced in thin rings
3/4 cup white sugar
2/3 cup apple cider or white distilled vinegar
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon celery seed or dried dill or 1/4 teaspoon both

Directions:
Rinse and drain green beans, yellow wax beans and light red kidney beans. Transfer to bowl with sliced onion rings. Stir with whisk sugar, vinegar, vegetable oil, salt, pepper, and celery seed. Pour over beans and seal in airtight container. Refrigerate at least 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Candice
Description: 
We love this salad. And, I make it alot during the summer months. There's something special about a crisp tangy bean salad with picnics, bar-b-ques, and potlucks. Quick and easy, and inexpensive too.
