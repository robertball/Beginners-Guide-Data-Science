Title:
Pulled Pork and Coleslaw Bundles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2054625.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package Pepperidge Farm® Puff Pastry Shells
2 cups prepared pulled pork, warmed
1/2 cup prepared creamy coleslaw
Chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Bake, cool and remove the 'tops' of the pastry shells according to the package directions.
Spoon 1/3 cup pork into each pastry shell. Top each with 2 tablespoons coleslaw and sprinkle with the parsley.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
These flavor-packed bundles feature Pepperidge Farm® Puff Pastry Shells baked until golden and filled with prepared pulled pork and a touch of creamy coleslaw.
