Title:
Hummamole or Guacamummus Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
2 avocados, peeled, pitted, and chopped
1 (15 oz.) can of chickpeas, drained
1/2 cup fresh chopped cilantro or 3 Tbs. dried cilantro
1/4 cup medium chopped red onion
3 cloves garlic, peeled and smashed
2 Tbsp. tahini paste (optional)
1 Tbsp. olive oil
1 lime, juiced
1 tsp. cumin
1/4 tsp. salt (more or less to taste)
1 jalapeno or Serrano pepper chopped, de-seeded, and de-veined (optional)
1 medium tomato (optional)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a food processor and blend until smooth. Or finely chop all ingredients and blend with a hand held mixer.
Add in a few extra tablespoons of water to thin out the consistency if youâ€™d like.
