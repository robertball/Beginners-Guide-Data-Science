Title:
My Crock Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large pot roast
1 can of beer
1 envelope Good Seasons Cheese & Garlic mix
1 envelope Zesty Italian dressing mix
1 bag baby carrots
6 potatoes, quartered

Directions:
Sear meat & place in crockpot w/ veggies. Mix seasonings w/ the beer & pour over all. Cook 5-8 hrs in crockpot depending on heat setting.
