Title:
Moms Famous NY style  Chocolate Chip Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust
1. 1 cup grahm cracker crumbs
2. 3 tablespoons sugar
3. 3 tablespoons melted butter
Cheesecake
4. 3- 8 ounce pkgs cream cheese softened
5. 3/4 cup sugar
6. 3 eggs
7. 1 cup chocolate chips
8. 1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Make the crust by mixing 1. 2. 3. together and pressing in the bottom of a 9" spring form pan
Mix in a large bowl 4. 5. at med speed with mixer...add 6. one at a time mixing well after each egg...blend in 7. and 8.  ...pour over the crust bake 450* for 10 min and then lower the oven to 250* for 30 min....
loosen cake from rim and cool before removing the side...chill and store in fridge
may serve with fudge sauce or squirt of choc syrup on top...may omit the chocolate chips and serve with cherries, strawberries, caramel sauce etc

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
this recipe is so easy and real NY style...thick and creamy...you may leave the Chocolate chips out and have regular cheesecake
