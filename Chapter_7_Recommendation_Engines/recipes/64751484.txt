Title:
Garlic-Roasted Broccoli with Balsamic Vinegar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp olive oil
1 garlic clove, minced
1 large bunch of broccoli
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 475. In a small bowl, combine olive oil and garlic.
Cut broccoli into florets with 2-3 inches of stem and spread on a rimmed baking sheet. Pour garlic oil over broccoli and toss to coat. Season with salt & pepper.
Roast 7-9 minutes, turning once, until broccoli is tender and charred at edges. Transfer to a serving dish and sprinkle with vinegar.
