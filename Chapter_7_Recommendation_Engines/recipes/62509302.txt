Title:
Banana- Chocolate Chip Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 very ripe bananas
1/4 C canola oil
1 egg
1 egg white
1 t vanilla extract
1/2 C brown sugar
2 C flour
1 t baking soda
1/4 t salt
1/2 C chocolate chips
1/2 C chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Coat standard 12-cup muffin tin with nonstick cooking spray or line with muffin liner cups.
In a med. bowl, mash bananas. Add oil, egg, egg white, vanilla and brown sugar; stir well.
In large bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, and salt. Pour into banana mixture; stir.
Gently mix in chocolate chips and, if desired, walnuts.
Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups, filling 2/3 full.
Bake 25 mins or until toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
Cool in cups 5 min. Remove and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
A great use for over-ripe bananas. The addition of chocolate chips makes these muffins an instant favorite.
