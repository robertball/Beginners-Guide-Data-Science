Title:
Rotel Steak Pizzaiola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7402719.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pound petite sirloin steaks (4 pieces)
1 can Rotel mild or hot
4 mini sweet peppers, sliced
1/2 cup diced mushrooms
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup fresh parsley
1 TBS olive oil
1 TBS butter
1 TBS balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup white wine
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Add olive oil to hot pan, sear steaks on both sides.
Remove steaks and set aside, leaving oil in pan.
Add butter and mushrooms, salt and pepper into skillet and saute about 4 minutes.
Add peppers to skillet and saute another 3 minutes.
Add garlic, cook 1 minute, then add wine and simmer about 3-4 minutes.
Add Rotel tomatoes, fresh parsley and balsamic vinegar and heat through.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Place steaks back into pan with vegetable mix and cook until done to your liking - 5-10 minutes.
