Title:
spinach salad dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup oil
1/2 Cup red wine vinegar
1/2 Cup sugar
1/3 Cup catsup
8 slices bacon
1 small onion chopped

Directions:
Cook bacon until crisp and drain on paper towels
Cook chopped onion in pan drippings until translucent and drain
Whisk together, oil, vinegar sugar and catsup.  Add bacon and onion.
Heat until just warmed and serve over your favorite spinach salad recipe

Submitter: 
LINDA MCLEAN
Description: 
The hard boiled eggs, the bacon ad catsup truly make this the best spinach salad dressing I've ever tasted
