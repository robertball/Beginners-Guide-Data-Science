Title:
Curried Chicken and Mushroom Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1090000.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp. salted butter
2 cups sweet onion, chopped
1 cup carrots, diced
1 cup celery, diced
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 1/2 cups mushrooms, chopped
1 1/2 cups white jasmine rice
3 tsp. mild curry powder
1/4 tsp. cayenne
64 oz Kirkland Organic Chicken Stock
3 - 12.5 oz cans Kirkland Chicken Breast, drained and rinsed (or 5 cups shredded cooked chicken)
Salt and Pepper to taste.

Directions:
Melt butter in a dutch oven over medium heat.  Add onions, carrots and celery.
Saute until tender.  Add garlic and mushrooms and stir to combine.  Cover and cook for 5 minutes.
Add rice, curry powder and cayenne, and cook for 1 minute stirring occasionally.
Add chicken stock and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 10 minutes.
Add chicken and bring back to a simmer.  Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 10-15 minutes more.
