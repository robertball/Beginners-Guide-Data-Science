Title:
Cabbage Noodle Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small cabbage head
1/2 cup sesame seeds
1 pkg (or more) Mr. Noodles beef or chicken flavor oriental noodles
1/2 cup slivered almonds
1/2 cup olive oil
1/4 cup sugar
5 tbsp. vinegar
1/2 tsp. salt
seasoning package from the Mr. Noodles soup
chopped green onion

Directions:
Finely slice the cabbage into thin pieces.
Brown together in a frying pan the sesame seeds, noodles from the Mr. Noodles package, and the slivered almonds.
Blend the olive oil, sugar, vinegar, salt, seasoning package from the soup, and the chopped green onion.
Just before serving, toss the dressing, noodle mixture and cabbage together.

Submitter: 
Lori Guilderson
Description: 
This recipe was passed on to me by a dear friend, and is a very flavorful salad.  The recipe has been requested often!
