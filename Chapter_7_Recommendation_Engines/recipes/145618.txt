Title:
Sassy Sauerkraut Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/578997.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) can sauerkraut, drained
1/2 cup chopped green bell peppers
1/2 cup chopped red bell peppers
1/2 cup chopped celery
1 small onion, chopped
3/4 cup white vinegar
1 cup SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together the sauerkraut, green peppers, red peppers, celery, onion, vinegar and SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener.  Refrigerate 8 hours or overnight before serving.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
Sauerkraut, pepped up with crunchy, colorful peppers, celery and onion, makes a scrumptious relish for hot dogs, hamburgers or grilled pork chops.
