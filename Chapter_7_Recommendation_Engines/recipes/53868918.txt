Title:
Chinese Barbecue Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup hoisin sauce
3 T black bean sauce
3/4 cup orange marmalade
1 T fresh ginger
2 cloves garlic
1 t 5 spice powder
2 T vinegar
1/4 cup soy sauce
3 T sugar
1/2 cup ketchup

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, in a blender if you want a smoother sauce.  Use as a marinade or a brush on sauce.
