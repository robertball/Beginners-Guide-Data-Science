Title:
Malabari Chicken Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons of Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2 medium onions, sliced
3 fresh green chilies, sliced
3 Cardamom
2 Bay Leaves
3 Cloves
1" Cinnamon Stick
3 large potatoes, cubed
2 TSP Ginger/Garlic Paste
2 carrot sticks, diced(optional)
1 teaspoons black pepper
1/2 teaspoons Turmeric Powder
2 teaspoons Coriander Powder
Salt to taste (I use 1/2 teaspoon)
1 1/2 cans of Coconut Milk
1/2 cup of water
4 Chicken Breasts, cubed
2 Tablespoons of plain yogurt
1 Tablespoon of Cream (optional)
1 tomato, diced (optional)

Directions:
Put the Olive Oil, Cardamoms, Cinnamon Stick, Bay leaves, Cloves, Onions and Green Chilies in a pot. Cook for 10 mins. Stir frequently.
Add the Potatoes, Carrots and Ginger/Garlic Paste. Cook for 5 mins. Stir frequently.
Add the Pepper, Salt, Turmeric and Coriander powder. Cook for another 2 mins. Stir Frequently.
Add in the water, coconut milk, yogurt, cream, tomatoes and chicken. Cook on low heat, partially covered for 20-30 mins, stir every 5 minutes. Or until chicken and/or potatoes are done.

Submitter: 
Kay
Description: 
Made with coconut milk as its broth, this stew is very different but a VERY tasty dish! 

Had a packaged Malabari Stew and wanted to make it myself. Found a recipe online and tweaked it from there :)
