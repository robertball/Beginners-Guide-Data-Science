Title:
Pineapple Ham Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large flour tortillas
2 small tubs pineapple flavored cream cheese, softened
12 large slices thinly sliced deli ham

Directions:
Spread each tortilla with about half of one tub of cream cheese.
Lay 3 slices of ham so that they cover the cream cheese.
Roll the tortilla tightly. If time allows, chill 1 hour before slicing. When ready, slice rollup into 1 inch pieces. Secure with toothpicks if desired.

Submitter: 
andrea_d
Description: 
Quick and easy, pineapple cream cheese and ham pair up for a yummy snack great for company and potlucks.
