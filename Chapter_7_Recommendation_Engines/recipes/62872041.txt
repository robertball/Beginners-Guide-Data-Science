Title:
Pineapple Infused Vodka Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 fresh pineapple, peeled and sliced
1 (750ml) bottle Stolichnaya vodka

Directions:
Stack the pineapple slices in a circle around the edges of a large glass jar, leaving the center open. Be sure to choose a jar with an air tight seal.
Fill with vodka, making sure that the pineapple is completely covered and not exposed to air.
Seal closed and store out of direct sunlight for 10 days.
After the 10 days, remove the pineapple and strain the vodka through a mesh strainer back into the jar. Seal and keep refrigerated until served.

Submitter: 
sd061507
Description: 
This infused vodka will keep for approx. 2 weeks in the fridge after it has been strained. Serve shaken in a martini glass and enjoy!
