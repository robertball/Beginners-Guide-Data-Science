Title:
Garlic-Herb Flatbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 c all-purpose flour
1/2 c cornmeal
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 c cold butter
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 tbsp rosemary
1 1/2 tbsp thyme
1/2 c cold water
2 tbsp white vinegar

Directions:
In a large bowl combine the first 5 ingrdients. Cut in butter until crumbly. Stir in herbs. Srinkle with water and vinegar. Toss with a fork until a ball forms. Wrap tightly in plastic wrap, place in freezer until firm. Cover a large cutting board with aluminum foil. Turn dough onto foil, cover with plastic wrap, and roll out very thin. Cut into rectangles and place on greased cookie sheets. Sprinkle with grated parmesean cheese. Bake at 375 degrees until golden brown and crispy.
