Title:
Tim's Chilli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground beef
1 medium onion
1 green pepper
1 large can of whole tomatoes with juice
2 cans tomato soup
1 large can chilli hot beans
1/4 jar chilli powder
2 c. macaroni noodles
2 TBSP hot sauce

Directions:
Chop onion and green pepper.
Brown beef with onion and green pepper, drain.
Add remaining ingredients.
Add 3-4 c. water.
Macaroni noodles will cook in chilli.
Bring to a boil and then simmer 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kellie M
Description: 
An original recipe with a kick to it!  My entire family loves this chilli.  It's my most requested recipe, easy to put together, and makes a wonderful family meal.  Excellent with shredded cheese, sour cream, and chopped green onion.
