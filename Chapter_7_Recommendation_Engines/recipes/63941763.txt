Title:
Apples and PB with Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 apples cored cut in half
1/2 c peanut butter
1/2 cup granola

Directions:
spread 1 tbsp of peanut butter over cut sides of each apple. Sprinkle granola over peanut butter pressing to gently adhere.
