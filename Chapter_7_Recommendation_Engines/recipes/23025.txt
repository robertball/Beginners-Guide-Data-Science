Title:
Rainbow Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5329959.jpg
Ingredients:
4 liters lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
2 pints rainbow sherbet
6 cups crushed ice

Directions:
In a large punch bowl, pour in the lemon-lime soda. Slide in the sherbet. Add crushed ice.
