Title:
Onion Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup flour
1 Tbsp. cornmeal
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
Pinch of pepper
1 Tbsp. sugar
Milk
2 1/2 cups onions, diced

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients together.
Add enough milk to make a thick paste.
Add the diced onions and stir well.
Drop by teaspoons into hot vegetable oil, 350 degrees.
(Dutch oven is great for this)
Fry until golden brown on each side.
Drain on paper towels.

Submitter: 
BARBARA1940
Description: 
A friend in my garden club shared this recipe with me, many years ago. It is really yummy! I prefer to use any of the sweet onions, not a white onion. It is very good with most beef dishes and is an easy side dish to prepare.
