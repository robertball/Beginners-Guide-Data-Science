Title:
Perfect Pumpkin Seeds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups of Pumpkin Seeds
3/4 Tbs Liquid Smoke
1/2 cup Worcestershire sauce
1 tbs Tabasco
1 Tbs bacon grease
1 1/2 Tbs Cayenne Pepper (or to taste)
1 Tbs Garlic Salt
Table salt (to taste)

Directions:
Soak pumpkin seeds in all liquid indgredients and in bacon grease overnight in container with lid.  Add cayenne pepper and garlic salt to pumpkin seeds and shake well.  Spread seeds on baking sheet and drizzle table salt.  Broil, turning seeds twice, until seeds are golden brown.

Submitter:

Description: 
Spicy, smokey & salty pumpking seeds that are just a little different from your normal recipes--but still just as easy.
