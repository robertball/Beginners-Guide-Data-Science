Title:
Rice, Broccoli 'n Cheese Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991992.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Minute® White Rice, uncooked
1 cup chicken broth
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli, thawed and drained
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/4 cup ranch dressing
2 eggs, lightly beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Prepare rice as directed on package, substituting chicken broth for the water. Place cooked rice in large mixing bowl; cool slightly. Stir in remaining ingredients until well blended.
Spoon mixture evenly into 8 greased muffin cups.
Bake 20 minutes or until lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Minute® Rice
Description: 
These individual baked servings of cheesy Minute® Rice and broccoli are made even more flavorful with ranch dressing.  Kids will love them!
