Title:
Air Fryer Shimp a la Bang Bang Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5610650.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup Sweet Chile Sauce
1 Tbsp. Sriracha
1/4 cup flour
1 cup Panko breadcrumbs
1 lb. raw shrimp, peeled and deveined (30 to 40 count work best)
loose leaf lettuce
green onions for garnish if desired

Directions:
1.  Set air fryer to 400 degrees.
2.  Mix mayonnaise, sweet chili sauce, and Sriracha in a bowl until smooth. Reserve some for dipping if desired.
3. Place flour on a plate. Place breadcrumbs on a separate plate.
4. Coat shrimp with flour, then the mayo mix, and then in the breadcrumbs. Place on a baking sheet. Repeat with remaining shrimp.
5. Place as many shrimp as you can in the basket of your air fryer without overcrowding. Cook for 12 minutes. Repeat with remaining shrimp.
6. Serve in lettuce wraps and garnish with green onions and reserved bang bang sauce.

Submitter: 
Soup Loving Nicole
Description: 
This is my take on a popular dish from a restaurant chain. My version is made in an air fryer instead of being deep-fried. The end result was perfectly crispy sweet and spicy shrimp.
