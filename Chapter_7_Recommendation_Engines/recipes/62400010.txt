Title:
Chili Chicken (Indo-Chinese) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breasts, cut into strips
1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
1 large green bell pepper, chopped
1 large onion, diced
4 green chilies, sliced in halves
2 tablespoons white vinegar
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon cayenne pepper
1 tablespoon ketchup
2 cups chicken stock
1 tablespoon olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Marinate chicken in salt & pepper, and 1 tablespoon soy sauce for 10 minutes. Brown in a pan, 10 minutes on each side until lightly brown. Set aside.
Heat oil in a wok on medium-high. Add ginger garlic paste, stir for 1 minute. Add chilies, onions, and green peppers, and saut√© for 5 minutes.
Add 1 tablespoon soy sauce, ketchup and cayenne pepper, vinegar, and 1/2 cup chicken stock. Bring mixture to a boil.
Add chicken and stir. For more gravy, add chicken stock as needed. Serve over white or fried rice.
