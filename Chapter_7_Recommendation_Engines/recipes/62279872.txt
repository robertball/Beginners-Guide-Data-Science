Title:
Chili for the Fans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs. lean ground beef, browned and drained
1 1/2 cups diced onion
3 cans black beans, rinsed and drained
3 cans kidney beans
2 cans Rotel® tomatoes with chiles(your choice of heat)
1 teaspoon salt - or to taste
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper - or to taste
1 tablespoon chili powder - again, to taste
1 64 oz. tomato juice

Directions:
Find a BIG pot.
Brown and drain the beef; add onions
Add remaining ingredients and stir well.
Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
Cover and simmer for 2 hours, stirring as needed.
Uncover and simmer as long as you wait!
Enjoy with shredded cheese, sour cream, corn chips or your favorite chili fixings.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
This simple chili recipe satisfies the biggest appetite on a cold winter football day.  Everyone wants to take the leftovers home! You can use the leftovers, if any, for chili-topped Tostitos® with cheese toasted under the broiler.
