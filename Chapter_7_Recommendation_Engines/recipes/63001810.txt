Title:
Paleo Friendly Banana Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 frozen bananas
Pure Vanilla Extract (to taste)
Heavy Cream or Coconut Milk
Optional:
Pecans, walnuts, almonds, mint leaves, cocoa powder

Directions:
Put bananas and optional ingredients in a blender on low speed
Add just enough cream or coconut milk to reach desired consistency
Add a tsp of vanilla or more if desired

Submitter: 
Michelle Cooley Davis
Description: 
No sugar, all natural, and yummy!
