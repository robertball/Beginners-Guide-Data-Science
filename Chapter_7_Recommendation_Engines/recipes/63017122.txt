Title:
Chicken Bog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 3 lb. whole chicken, quartered
1 lb turkey sausage
1 c onion, chopped
1/4 c butter
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp ground red pepper
3 bay leaves
8 c water
3 c white rice, uncooked

Directions:
Slice the sausage into 1/2 inch slices.  In a stockpot, combine the chicken, sausage, onion, butter, salt, pepper, garlic powder, red pepper, and bay leaves.  Add water, bring to a boil.  Cover and cook at a low boil for 40 minutes.
Remove the chicken from the pot and let cool slightly.  Pick the meat from the bones, discarding the bones and skin.
Add the rice to the pot and bring to a boil, stirring well.  Boil for 10 minutes, then reduce the heat, cover the pot and simmer for 10 minutes or until the rice is done.
Remove the bay leaves and return chicken to the pot.  Stir and serve.
