Title:
Arroz con pollo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large chicken cut in pieces (you can use boned and skinned chicken breasts and thighs to equal meat of one chicken)
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 can stewed tomatoes
1 medium chopped onion
1 chopped bell pepper
2 clove garlic, minced
2 whole cloves of garlic
1 bay leaf
1 tsp oregano
2 tsps. salt or to taste
1/2 tsp. pepper
1 cup uncooked rice (brown is ok)
2-3 cups chicken stock
1/2 cup sliced black olives
1/4 cup chopped pimento
1 cup peas (or corn)
1/2 cup raisins

Directions:
1. Using a dutch oven or large skillet you can put into the oven, brown the chicken in the oil.
2. Remove and set aside chicken, leaving oil in the pan.  Saute the onion, garlic, and pepper for a few minutes.
3. Add the chicken back in, add all seasonings, the rice, and enough stock to cover.
4. Bake at 350 for 1 hour 30 min.
5. Remove, stir in olives, pimento, peas and raisins. Cook for additional 15 min.
5. Fluff the rice carefully with a fork before serving, and enjoy!
