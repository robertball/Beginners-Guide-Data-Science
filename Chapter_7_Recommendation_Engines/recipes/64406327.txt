Title:
Super Fast and Easy Bresaola Roll-Ups (No Cooking Required) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
100 grams sliced bresaola (Italian cured beef)
8 ounces soft goat cheese
1 tsp fresh ground black pepper, divided
1.5 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil, divided
1 Tbsp fresh chopped chives, divided
toothpicks, if desired

Directions:
With a fork, mix the cheese, 1/2 Tbsp olive oil, 1/2 Tbsp chives and 1/2 tsp. of pepper together in a small bowl.
Add a spoonful of the cheese mixture onto the middle of each slice of bresaola.
Roll each of the bresaola slices into a tube and fix with a toothpick or shape into a dumpling form so you won't need the toothpick.
Garnish with remaining olive oil, chives and pepper.
Serve with crispy bread sticks.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
No need to slave over the stove or use precious oven space for your appetisers when preparing your next brunch or party. Sliced bresaola (Italian cured beef) is stuffed with fresh goat cheese accented with chives and fresh ground pepper. The perfect combination: no cooking, barely any mess and ready in less than 10 minutes. These can be made up to 24 hours in advance, covered and refrigerated until the moment you need them.
