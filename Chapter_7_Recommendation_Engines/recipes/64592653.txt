Title:
Slow Cooker Pork Chop Potato Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless pork chops (about 1/2" thick)
1 tbsp. oil
1 1kg (or less) pkg. frozen hash browns, thawed
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese, divided
1 can condensed cream of chicken or mushroom soup, undiluted
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 tsp. seasoning salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 2/8oz can French-fried onions, divided

Directions:
In large skillet, brown chops in oil on both sides; set aside & keep warm.
In a bowl, combine thawed hash browns, 1 cup cheese, soup, milk, sour cream, salt and pepper.
Stir in half of the onions.
Transfer to a GREASED 5qt slow cooker; top with pork chops.
Cover and cook on high x 2 1/2 - 3 hours or till meat juices run clear.
Sprinkle with remaining cheese and onions.
Cover and cook x 10 min. longer or till cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Dinner is ready in one dish. A slow cooker makes this even more convenient.
Pork chops, hash browns, cheese and onion flavors.
