Title:
Anna & Natalie's super easy toffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 sticks of butter (2 c.)
1/3 c. water
2 Tblsp. lite corn syrup (or 1 1/2 Tblsp. dark corn syrup)
2 c. sugar
1-2 c. chocolate chips
1 c. crushed almonds (opt)

Directions:
Melt butter in a medium saucepan until completely melted, but do not burn. Add water, corn syrup, and sugar. Stir. Candy is ready when candy thermomater reads 300 degrees(F) or when candy slightly hardens when a small amount is dropped into lukewarm/cold water.
Pour into foil-lined cookiesheet or baking dish. Let sit about 10-20 minutes until slightly hardened, but still warm. Thickly sprinkle chocolate chips in a single layer, and let sit 1-5 minutes until chips begin to melt, then spread with a plastic spatulaand sprinkle crushed almonds.
Place in the fridge. Keep in the frigde for 2-5 hrs. until completly hardened.
Break into pieces and serve.
Store extras at room tempature in an air- tight container.
ENJOY!
