Title:
My Mom's Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick Butter
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 eggs
2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
3-4 peeled & smashed brown bananas

Directions:
Mix butter, sugar and vanilla with mixer.  Add the smashed bananas to mixture and mix slightly.  Then add the flour and baking soda and mix with mixer.  Do not over mix or it will loose the banana flavor.  Just mix enough to combine ingredients.

Description: 
This is the recipe that has been handed down from generation to generation.
