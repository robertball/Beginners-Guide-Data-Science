Title:
Instant Mango Frozen Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1295573.jpg
Ingredients:
4 1/2 cups diced frozen mango, not thawed
1 1/2 cups nonfat plain Greek yogurt
1/3 cup confectioners' sugar or brown sugar

Directions:
Combine mango, yogurt and sugar in a food processor. Process until smooth.

Submitter: 
Breana Lai, M.P.H., R.D.
Description: 
In this quick Greek frozen yogurt recipe, frozen mango and Greek yogurt are blended in a food processor to create a luscious, healthy dessert.
