Title:
Mediteranean Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. tuna
2 tbs. roasted garlic hummus like Sabra
1/2 tbs. mayo
1 tbs. diced hot or mild banana peppers
1 tbs. diced sun-dried tomatoes packed in oil
1/2 tbs. fresh basil or lemon basil, chopped
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine tuna, hummus, mayo, banana peppers, tomatoes and basil.  Mix together.  Refrigerate immediately or serve in pitas with sliced tomato and cucumber.

Submitter: 
TARAWVU
Description: 
I came up with this one day when I wanted a different spin on traditional tuna salad.  It's so good I rarely make it the old way anymore!
