Title:
Broccoli Liguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/713894.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. Broccoli florettes steamed
1 stick butter
3-4 garlic cloves crushed
1 lb. box pasta cooked al dente and drained
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Melt butter in a large skillet or pan big enough to toss all ingredients together. Saute crushed garlic over medium heat for 1 minute. Add steamed broccoli cooked pasta and toss until coated. sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and toss again. Pour into large pasta bowl and top with more cheese, as much as you like. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Buffy Ehlers Whitney
Description: 
A most satisfying meal and you won't even miss the meat! A garlic lovers dream dish.
