Title:
Grilled Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Hellmann's Mayonnaise
3 TBL low fat or skim milk
2 TBL chopped green onions
1 TBL lemon juice
Lettuce and cut up veggies
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves (1 lb) grilled or broilled

Directions:
In small bowl combine mayo, milk, green onions and lemon juice.
Arrange lettuce and vegetables on dinner plates; top with sliced chicken and drizzle with dressing. If desired, sprinkle with freshly ground pepper.
