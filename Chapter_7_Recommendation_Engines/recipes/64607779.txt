Title:
Tomato Bacon vegetable soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 carrotts chopped (2 sticks celery optional)
2 large potatoes
2 onions chopped
1 lb bacon
5 cups chicken broth
1 Quart Jar Canned tomatoes
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Fry bacon and chop into very small pieces
Fry onions till soft and well cooked
Fry carrotts and celery (if using) till golden
Add everything to tomato and chicken broth and simmer for at least 1 hour then put in fridge overnight.
