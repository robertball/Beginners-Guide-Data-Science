Title:
Twice Baked Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 baking potatoes
green onion bunch, thinly sliced
2 Tbsp. butter or margarine
1/2 cup milk, hot
1/2 tsp. salt
dash pepper
dash nutmeg
dash paprika
half pound bacon
2 Tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese
2 Tbsp. grated sharp Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Wash potatoes, pat dry.  Prick each potato with a fork in a few places and rub with salt.  Bake at 400 degrees for about 1 hour.  Cut in half and scoop out the pulp into a bowl.
Mash potato pulp with the hot milk until fluffy.  Beat in the butter and the green onion.  Add seasonings.
Fry bacon until crisp.  Crumble and add to potato mixutre.  Spoon potato mixture into shells.  Sprinkle with both cheeses.
Place potatoes on baking sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees until potatoes are piping hot and cheese is melted, about 15 minutes.
