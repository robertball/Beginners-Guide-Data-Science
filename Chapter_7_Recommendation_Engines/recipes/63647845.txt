Title:
Super soft potato rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup warm water
3 cups flour
1/2 cup plain mashed potatoes
1/4 cup butter flavored shortening
1/4 cup sugar
2 1/4 tsp. yeast
1 tsp. salt

Directions:
1. Mix yeast and sugar into the hot water.
2. In a large bowl, mix flour and salt.
3. Cut in shortening and mashed potatoes.
4. Add the yeast mixture and knead until dough is smooth.
5. Cover with a damp cloth and let rise until double in size.
6.Pour dough out onto a floured board, turning the dough over so all sides are lightly floured.
7. Pinch off 12 equal size portions of dough and place them in a greased 9x12 pan.
8. Cover with damp towel and let the rolls rise again until doubled in size.
9. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
10. Bake the rolls for 10-11 minutes until golden on top and cooked through.

Submitter: 
iceemama
Description: 
This is a deliciously soft roll. I came up with the recipe after accidentally omitting an egg in my normal recipe. The end result was a much lighter roll which still has a great flavor.
