Title:
Crockpot Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•	2 jars Ragu pizza sauce
•	1 big can of Hunts spaghetti sauce with mushrooms
•	2lbs of ground beef or Italian sausage (I use 1lb of each)
•	1 small jar of mushrooms
•	1 bag Kluski noodles (egg noodles) COOKED
•	1 package of pepperoni
•	4 cups of mozzarella cheese
•	2 cups of cheddar cheese

Directions:
•	Brown ground beef/sausage, drain grease
•	Mix together: meat, pizza sauce, spaghetti sauce, and mushrooms
In Crockpot
•	Put a light layer of sauce in bottom of crockpot
•	Layer noodles, sauce, cheesed, and pepperoni
•	Repeat layers
•	Try to get 2 to 3 layers depending on crockpot size
•	End with pepperoni on top to make it look like pizza
•	Cook on low 5-6 hours
