Title:
Bison Balls and Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/330249.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs of ground bison meat 7% fat
1 16 oz can stewed tomatoes
1 26.5 oz can of Hunts Original Style Mushroom spaghetti sauce
13.25 oz Barilla Whole Grain Spaghetti
6 oz of sliced mushrooms
1/4 cup of fresh parsley chopped fine
1/4 cup of fresh oregano chopped fine
1/4 cup of fresh basil chopped fine
5 green onions chopped fine
1 yellow onion chopped
1 egg
1 tablespoon of red wine
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 slice of bread crumbled
1 tablespoon of salt
1 tablespoon of butter
salt and pepper to taste
Parmesan cheese to taste

Directions:
1.Combine parsley, basil, oregano and green onions, mix well in bowl. Take 1/2 cup of green mixture and sprinkle on ground meat.
2.On ground meat add Worcestershire sauce, egg, bread crumbs, 1 teaspoon of salt and mix well. making sure you have equal parts of mixture throughout the meat.
3.Shape into meatballs 1.5 inches wide (yeilds about 8 balls) and place into mid-high heated skillet. Add red wine and cover for 8 minutes and flip over. Cover again for 8 minutes or until centers are fully cooked at 160 degrees. Set aside and keep warm.
4.In another large sauce pan or wok melt butter on med-high heat. Combine mushrooms, yellow onion, remaining parsley-oregano-green onion-basil mix and teaspoon of salt. Saute until onions are translucent, about 10 minutes.
5.Add stewed tomatoes and tomato/mushroom sauce, bring to a boil, season with salt and pepper (I always find canned tomatoes to need more salt and pepper)
6.Cook spaghetti as instructed on package and drain.
7.Serve sauce over cooked noodles, top with bison meatball and Parmesan, enjoy.
