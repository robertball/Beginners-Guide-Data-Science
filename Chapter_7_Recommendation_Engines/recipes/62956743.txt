Title:
Chicken Fettuccini Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into cubes
6 tablespoons butter, divided
4 cloves garlic, minced, divided
1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
1 pound fettuccini pasta
1 onion, diced
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon salt
3/4 teaspoon ground white pepper
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup half-and-half
1 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Optional Veggie Ideas:
Fresh Tomatoes chopped
Fresh Mushrooms chopped
Fresh Green Pepper chopped

Directions:
Directions
1. In a large skillet over medium heat combine chicken, 2 tablespoons butter, 2 cloves garlic, and Italian seasoning. Cook until chicken is no longer pink inside. Remove from skillet and set aside. Salt and pepper the chicken before you cook it.
2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
3. Meanwhile, melt 4 tablespoons butter in the skillet. Saute onion, 2 cloves garlic, and mushrooms until onions are transparent. Stir in flour, salt and pepper; cook 2 minutes. Slowly add chicken stock and half-and-half, stirring until smooth and creamy. Stir in Parmesan; stir until cheese is melted. Stir in chicken mixture, tomatoes and and other veggies you might use. Serve over cooked fettuccini or stir in the pot of sauce..
