Title:
Potato Bread I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1363429.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup water
2/3 cup instant mashed potato flakes
1 egg
2 tablespoons margarine
2 tablespoons white sugar
1/4 cup dry milk powder
1 teaspoon salt
3 cups bread flour
1 1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast

Directions:
Add ingredients to bread machine in order suggested by the manufacturer.   Add enough water to make a firm dough; use less if leftover potatoes are used.
Bake at regular bread cycle.

Diane Johnson
