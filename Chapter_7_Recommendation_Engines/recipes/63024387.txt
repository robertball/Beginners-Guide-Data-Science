Title:
Jim Dunbar Famous Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 12 ounces semisweet Chocolate chips
2. 7 ounces marshmallow creme
3. 12 ounces evaporated milk
4. 1/2 cup butter
5. 4 cups sugar
6. 2 cups coarsely chopped walnuts ( optional)

Directions:
Butter 9 x 13 pan ( I use my glass one that has a lid)
combine in large bowl 1. 2. and place the  bowl in a sink filled 1/2 way with hot water ( to begin the melting process of the chips)
In a heavy 4qt saucepan combine 3. 4. 5. ...medium heat stir occasionally till comes to a boil and then continue to heat for 6 minutes stirring occasionally
pour over bowl of melting chips...with a wooden spoon beat mixture till creamy ...add 6. ( see my note below)
pour into prepared pan and chill in fridge for at least 6 hours till firm...cut into 1" pieces
keeps well in fridge for several weeks
