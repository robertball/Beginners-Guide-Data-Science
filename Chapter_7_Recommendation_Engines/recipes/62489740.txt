Title:
Hong Kong Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup  honey garlic sauce
1 cup  white wine vinegar
1/4 cup  finely chopped green onions
1 cup  garlic oil (make by slowly simmering whole peeled garlic cloves in a pot of oil until cloves are soft)

Directions:
1. Mix all ingredients together with a whisk. Stir again before adding to salad. Lasts weeks!

Submitter: 
Gillian Kehler
Description: 
This is a quick and easy four-ingredient salad dressing recipe that I thought up on the fly at work! Great for a lettuce or cabbage salad with classic Chinese vegetables, such as julienned peppers, carrots, onions, etc. Very easy to make, and soooo tasty!
