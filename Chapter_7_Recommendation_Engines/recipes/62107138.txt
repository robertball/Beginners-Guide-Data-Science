Title:
Zippy Lima Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
1 16 ounce bag frozen baby lima beans, prepared according to package directions and cooled.
1 15 ounce can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 15 ounce can garbanzo beans, rinsed and drained
1 11 ounce can Mexicorn, drained
1 red or green bell pepper, diced
1/2 cup finely minced red onion
1 fresh jalapeno pepper, finely minced
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
Salt and pepper to taste
Dressing:
1 0.7 ounce package dry Zesty Italian Salad Dressing mix, prepared according to package directions
2 Tablespoons sugar, or equivalent sugar substitute

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together all salad ingredients.
Add the sugar to the prepared salad dressing and mix well to disolve.
Pour the dressing over the salad and toss.
Season with salt and pepper to taste
Cover and allow to chill for at least 2 hours to blend flavors.
