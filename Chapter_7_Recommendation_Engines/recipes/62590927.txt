Title:
Baked Blueberry French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 slices of day old French bread - 1/2" thick
1 - 8oz. pkg. cream cheese - cubed
2/3 cup milk
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup plain yogurt
1/3 cup maple syrup
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
2 cups egg substitute (or equivalent quantity of fresh eggs)
2 cups blueberries (do not thaw if using frozen)
2 tbsp. powdered sugar

Directions:
Place 12 slices of bread in a 13x9 greased baking dish.
In blender or food processor, combine cream cheese, milk, sour cream, yogurt, syrup, vanilla, nutmeg and cinnamon.
Blend until smooth.
Add eggs and blend until smooth.
Pour half of mixture over bread and sprinkle with blueberries.
Top with remaining bread and egg mixture.
Cover and refrigerate for 8 hours or overnight.
Preheat oven to 350 and remove French toast from refrigerator 30 min. before baking.
Uncover and bake 20-30 min. or until a knife comes out clean.
Let stand 10 min. before serving and sprinkle with powdered sugar.
