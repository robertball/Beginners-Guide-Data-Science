Title:
Sandy's Creamy Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Medium potatoes,cubed into bit-sized pieces
1 Can of creamed corn
1 Can whole Kernel corn
2 Cups of chicken broth
1 Small Onion,chopped
1 small carrot, chopped
1 small celery stalk,chopped
2 Tablespoons Margarine
2 cups of Half and Half
2 Tablespoon Cornstarch
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt margarine in a sauce pan, add onions and saute until almost tender.
Place potatoes both cans of corn, chicken broth,onions,carrot,celery,salt and pepper into slow cooker.
Cook on low for 7 to 8 hours.or until vegetables are tender.
Next add the cornstarch to the half and half stir well to combine.
During the last half hour of cooking turn the slow cooker to high, add the half and half and cornstarch mixture to the crock pot, stir well.
Simmer on high stirring constantly as mixture thickens, stir until desired consistency. Chowder will also thicken a little more as it cools.
