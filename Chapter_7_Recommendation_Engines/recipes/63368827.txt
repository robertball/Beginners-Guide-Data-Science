Title:
Irma Rombauer's World Famous Potato Salad II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sliced, boiled white or red potatoes
1/2 cup vinaigrette dressing (see recipe below)
Chop or slice and add discreetly a mixture of any of the following:
Hard cooked eggs, onions, olives, pickles, celery w/leaves, cucumbers, capers
Cayenne pepper to taste
Paprika to taste
Mayonnaise
Vinaigrette:
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 Tbsp vinegar or lemon juice
1/4 tsp dry mustard
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 Tbsp vinegar or lemon juice
3 Tbsp olive oil
1 clove garlic pressed

Directions:
For  potato salad:
Combine all ingredients.  Add vinaigrette dressing.  Adjust seasonings.
Serve chilled.
For vinaigrette:
Combine 1st five ingredients in small bowl and mix well with a wire whisk.
Add 2 Tbsp olive oil and whisk well.
Add final three ingredients and whisk well
