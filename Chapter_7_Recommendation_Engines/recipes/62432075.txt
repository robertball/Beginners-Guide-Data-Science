Title:
Green Beans with Lemon and Goat Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/472599.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb fresh green beans, ends trimmed
bowl of ice water
1/2 sweet onion, chopped
1 clove gralic, minced
zest from l lemon
2oz  goat cheese
olive oil
salt and pepper

Directions:
Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add the green beans and cook for 6-8 minutes. The green beans will be a vibrant green and still have a snap when bitten into. Immediately transfer the green beans from the pot to the bowl of ice water. Stir gently until cooled then lay out on a dish towel to dry. Set aside.
Heat olive oil in a large skillet to medium heat. Add the onions to the hot oil and season with salt and pepper. Cook for 8 minutes or so until the onions are translucent. Add the garlic and lemon zest and cook for 5 minutes more. Add the green beans and cover. Raise the heat to medium-high and let cook covered for 5-6 minutes more.  Transfer to a serving platter and crumble the goat cheese over the top. Slice the zested lemon into wedges and serve on platter next to green beans.
This dish can be served warm or at room temperature.
