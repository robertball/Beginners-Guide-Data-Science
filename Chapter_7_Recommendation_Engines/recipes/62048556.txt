Title:
Coconut-Pineapple Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Betty Crocker® SuperMoist® butter recipe yellow cake mix
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 cup raisins
1 can (8 ounces) crushed pineapple, well drained
1 1/3 cups shredded coconut
1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup sliced almonds

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350ºF. Stir together dry cake mix, butter and cinnamon until crumbly. Press evenly in ungreased 13x9x2-inch rectangular pan.
2.  Sprinkle raisins and pineapple over mixture in pan. Sprinkle with coconut. Pour sweetened condensed milk over coconut; top with almonds.
3.  Bake 25 to 27 minutes or until set and almonds are light golden brown. Cool completely, about 1 hour. For 36 bars, cut into 9 rows by 4 rows. Store tightly covered.

Submitter: 
Kate Fisher
Description: 
Easy dessert made with a yellow cake mis. Tropical flavors of coconut and pineapple, easy and tasty.
