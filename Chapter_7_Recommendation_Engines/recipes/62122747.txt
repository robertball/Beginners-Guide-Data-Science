Title:
Tacos In A Bag Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 container of Chi Chi's Taco Sauce with Seasoned Ground Beef
6 individual size bags of Doritos Brand Nacho Cheese Chips
1 cup shredded taco blend cheese
Toppings such as sliced olives, sour cream and shredded lettuce

Directions:
Heat the meat according to package directions. Open the bags of chips, and add the meat mixture, cheese and toppings. Serve in the bags.

Submitter: 
Ray
Description: 
This is a quick and easy meal to make when you are in a rush, and don't really have time to cook.
