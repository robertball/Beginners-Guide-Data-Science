Title:
Amazing grilled cheese. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One slice Smoked Gouda
Two slices of Munster
Three slices of bread
Melted butter
Cookie sheet
(Optional) electric pancake griddle.

Directions:
Brush melted butter on the large face of each slice of bread crust to crust making sure to stir the melted butter each time to keep it from separating.
Place bread on hot griddle butterside down and add cheese slices immediately.
While the bread toasts in the griddle turn on your broiler in your oven.
Once bread is a beautiful golden brown throughout place the slices on your cookie sheet and put in oven under broiler until the cheese begins to bubble.
Once bubbling pull the cookie sheet and assemble your grilled cheese slicing diagonally. (Sandwiches always taste better when sliced into triangles.)
Plate your sandwich and Push slices together and pull sligtly revealing a beautiful scaffolding of molten cheese.
Serve on its own or with a small bowl of tomato soup.
