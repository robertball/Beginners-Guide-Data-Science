Title:
Aromatic Stuffed Acorn Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 acorn squash, halved
3 1/2 ounces cooked chicken breast meat, chopped
1 apple, diced
1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
2 tbsp milk
1/4 cup celery, chopped
1/4 cup onion, chopped
2 tbsps dried currants or raisins
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt
2 tbsp cinnamin
1 tsp allspice
1 tsp thyme
1 tsp poultry seasoning
1 tsp brown sugar
1 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp nutmeg

Directions:
Place squash in large pot with some water, cook until soft.
In a separate pot, mix apple, chicken, celery, onion, applesauce, milk and raisins. Add spices and cook to allow flavors to blend.
Spoon mixture into acorn squash half and bake in preheated oven (350F)for 20 minutes or until apple is soft.

Submitter: 
Felicity
Description: 
This is a wonderful dish for fall, very healthy and perfect on a cool fall day! Made with acorn squash, chicken and apples!
