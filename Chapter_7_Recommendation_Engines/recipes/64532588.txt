Title:
Baked Garlic Parmesan Potato Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3065599.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large russet potatoes, sliced into wedges
4 Tbsp. olive oil
1/2 cup Parmesan
Salt to taste
2 tsp Italian seasoning
2 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp paprika

Directions:
Place potato wedges in a large bowl. Drizzle olive oil and toss to coat. Sprinkle potato wedges with shredded cheese and toss again.
In a small bowl, mix salt, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, and paprika. Sprinkle seasoning onto potato wedges and toss to coat.
Place potato wedges on a baking sheet with skin-sides-down. Bake for 40 minutes at 400°F / 200°C until potatoes are fork-tender and golden.
