Title:
MUSSELS WITH GARLIC & LEMON Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mussels
1/cup white wine
1/2 butter
3 tbsp lemon juice
1 garlic clove

Directions:
scrub mussels under cold water
wrap looosely in aluminum foil and cover with wine
cook over high for 8 - 10 minutes
serve with dipping sauce of butter lemon and garlic
