Title:
Lemon Asparagus Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 stalks of asparagus
1 small onion (diced)
1 celery stalk (diced)
1 garlic clove (minced)
4 cups low sodium chicken stock
1 cup uncooked arborio rice
1/2 cup dry white wine
1/4 freshly grated parmesan cheese
1/8 cup fresh lemon juice
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 teaspoon lemon zest (about half a lemon)
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Trim and discard the woody thick ends from the asparagus. Wash asparagus well my soaking them in water for a few minutes. Steam asparagus by placing stalks in your steamer for  5 minutes.  You can also roast asparagus by placing stalks, 1 tablespoon of olive oil, pinch of salt and pepper on a baking sheet and baking them in the oven at 350 F (180 C) for 15 minutes.
Cut cooked asparagus into 1 inch pieces and set aside.
In a medium sized pot warm chicken stalk over medium heat.
In a medium sized pan saute onion and celery in olive oil over medium heat.
Add 1/4 tsp. each of salt and pepper.
Once onions are translucent add garlic and rice.
Continue to saute for 5 minutes.
Add wine and stir until wine has evaporated.
Add one ladle of stock and stir until evaporated.
Repeat this process until all of the stock has been absorbed.
Add asparagus and stir.
Remove from heat and add lemon juice, zest and cheese.
Stir to combine.
Serve immediately.
