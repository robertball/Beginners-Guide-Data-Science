Title:
Non-Alcoholic Mojito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
Fresh mint
1 lime
1 packet sugar substitute
Crushed ice
6 fluid ounces lime-flavored sparkling water

Directions:
Crush a sprig of mint in the bottom of a cocktail glass. Squeeze the juice of one fresh lime into the glass. Add 1 package of sugar substitute, crushed ice, and 6 ounces of lime-flavored sparkling water. Garnish with mint and serve.

Submitter: 
The South Beach Diet Online
Description: 
Sparkling limeade and crushed ice with a hint of mint. This phase 1 drink is cool and refreshing.
