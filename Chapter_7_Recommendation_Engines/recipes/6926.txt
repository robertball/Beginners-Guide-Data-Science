Title:
Bruce's Honey Sesame Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/995291.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups water
1/4 cup honey
1 tablespoon powdered buttermilk
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
3 cups bread flour
3 tablespoons wheat bran
1/2 cup sesame seeds, toasted
2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast

Directions:
Place ingredients in bread machine pan in the order suggested by the manufacturer.
Select Basic Bread cycle.

Submitter: 
Karen Hartman
Description: 
This is a deliciously chewy, sweet, nutty-tasting bread.
