Title:
Sweet 'n' Sticky ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large rack of pork back ribs
1 can pineapple chunks
2 teaspoons corn starch
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon of vinegar (more if you want it less sweet)
1-2 tablespoons maple syrup
Fresh ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut rack of ribs in half
Bring large pot of water to boil, throw in ribs and boil for about 20 minutes.
Preheat oven to 350
Line baking pan with foil (unless you like scrubbing dishes)
Drain and keep juice from pineapple chunks, set aside the chunks for later.
Mix the juice, sugar, vinegar, corn starch and syrup well, making sure corn starch and sugar dissolves.  Add pepper to your liking.
Put ribs in pan bone side up and pour sauce mixture in.
Cover pan with foil.
Cook about an hour then flip ribs and baste.
Cook for another 30 minutes, uncovered, then turn heat down to 300 and add pineapple chunks and baste some more.
Cook another 15-20 minutes to heat pineapple. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
CraZee A
Description: 
Just how we like them...tender and sweet, the sauce sticks to the ribs (and yours and the plates haha).  I invented this for my boyfriend who has the worst sweet tooth on the planet, loves his ribs and is a sweet guy!
