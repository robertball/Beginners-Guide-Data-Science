Title:
Pecan Pie Surprise Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123263.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. yellow cake mix
1/2 c. melted butter
4 eggs
1/2 c. brown sugar
1 1/2 c. dark corn syrup
1 tsp. vanilla
1 c. chopped pecans

Directions:
Reserve 2/3 c. dry cake mix for filling.
In large mixing bowl, combine remaining dry cake mix, butter, and one egg.  Mix until crumbly.
Press in greased 13x9 pan.
Bake at 350 degrees 15-20 minutes, until golden brown.
Meanwhile, prepare filling of reserved cake mix, brown sugar, corn syrup, vanilla and three eggs.  Beat with mixer.
Pour filling over partially baked crust.  Sprinkle with chopped pecans.
Return to oven and bake 30-35 minutes, till filling is set.  Cool.
Cut into bars.
