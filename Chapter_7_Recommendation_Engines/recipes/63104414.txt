Title:
Italian Tex-Mex Pork Loin And Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/787653.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
4 pork loin or boneless pork chops
1 large onion, Diced
2 cloves of minced garlic
2 teaspoons chili powder
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried Basel
1 teaspoon dried rosemary
1 teaspoon dried thyme
2 15 ounce cans of tomatoes
8 ounce can of tomato sauce
1 cup of water
1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Pound Pork Chops Or Pork Loin Thin (about 1/8 Inch)
Heat the Olive oil in a skillet over high heat, and quickly brown the pork.
Remove the pork from the fry pan and put on a plate.
Reduce skillet heat to medium, and cook the chopped onion until tender.
Add the meat back to the frying pan
Add the tomatoes, Tomato sauce, garlic, water, Worcestershire sauce, chili powder, red pepper flakes, Basel, rosemary, thyme and oregano.
Cover and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to low, and cook 25 minutes.
Just before serving salt and pepper to taste
