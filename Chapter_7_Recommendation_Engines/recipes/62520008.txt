Title:
Lindsay's Chocolate Cafe Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 C regular rolled oats
2 C all-purpose flour
1 t. baking powder
1 t baking soda
1/2 t salt
1 C butter, softened
1 C granulated sugar
1 C brown sugar, packed
2 eggs
1 t. vanilla
1 12-oz pkg. semisweet chocolate pieces (2 C)
1 1/2 C chopped walnuts or pecans
4 oz. milk chocolate bar, grated

Directions:
In med. bowl, mix oats, flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt; set aside.
In lg. bowl, beat butter with electric mixer 30 sec. Add sugars; beat until mixture is combined, scraping sides of bowl occ. Beat in eggs and vanilla until combined. Beat in as much flour as you can with mixer. Stir in any remaining flour with wooden spoon until combined. Stir in choc. pieces, nuts and grated chocolate.
Drop from large cookie scoop(3 T)4 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheets or a baking stone. Bake in 375 oven 9-10 min. or until edges are light brown. Cool on cookie sheets 1 min. Transfer to wire racks; cool.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Midwest Foods top pick-from O'Fallon, Mo cafe. The secret is rolled oats, grated milk chocolate, nuts; baking on preheated stone which makes slighly crispier edges.
