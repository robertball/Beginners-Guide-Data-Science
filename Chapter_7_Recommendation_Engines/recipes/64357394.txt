Title:
Mom's Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 eggs
1/2 cup sugar
4 cups whole milk
2 slices bread, cubed
1 tsp vanilla
Nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
In large bowl, beat eggs.  Add sugar, milk, bread and vanilla and mix well.
Pour into casserole dish and top with nutmeg.
Place bowl in pan of water.  Bake for 1-1/4 hours or until set.  (Raisins can be added)

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
A custard version of an old time favorite.
