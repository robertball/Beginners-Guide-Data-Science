Title:
Raspberry Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1248589.jpg
Ingredients:
4C fresh or frozen raspberries
1/3C sugar
1/3C + 3TBSP all-purpose flour, divided
1/2C quick-cooking oats
1/3C packed brown sugar
1/4C coconut
1/4C cold butter, cubed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
In a large bowl, gently toss raspberries with sugar and 3TBSP of flour.
Pour the berries into a greased 8-inch square baking dish.
In another large bowl, combine oats, brown sugar, coconut, remaining flour.
Add in the butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
Lightly sprinkle the crumb mixture over the berries.
Bake in preheated oven for 30-40minutes, or until golden brown.
Let cool, and serve with your favorite vanilla ice cream. ENJOY!
