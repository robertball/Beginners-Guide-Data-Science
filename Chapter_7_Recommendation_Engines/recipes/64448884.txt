Title:
Simple Sloppy Joe's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lb. ground beef
2 TBSP Dried Celery - Harmony House Foods bought online
2 TBSP Dried Red or Green Pepper - Harmony House Foods bought online
1 bottle chili sauce
1/4 cup V8 Juice
2 TBSP tomato Paste
1 teaspoon Garlic Salt
1 teaspoon Onion Powder
10 Hamburger Buns

Directions:
Brown Hamburger on the stove-top, once cooked through, drain in a colander.  Add meat back to pan and cook on low heat, add remaining ingredients minus the buns and simmer until combined and warm. Add to hamburger buns and serve!
Could be added to a slow cooker if needed.

Submitter: 
Lisa Ermgodts
Description: 
Quick and easy!  Recipe can be doubled to feed a hungry crowd and it can be transferred to a slow cooker to be kept warm.

Kids Love it!
