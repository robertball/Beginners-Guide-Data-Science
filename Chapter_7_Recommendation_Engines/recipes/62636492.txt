Title:
Mexican hot dog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/579909.jpg
Ingredients:
10-12 sausages (mild, spicy, or jalapeno with cheese)
1 sliced red bell pepper
1 sliced green bell pepper
1 large white oninon
3 tbsp cilantro
3 tbsp butter
miracle whip/mayo
ketchup
mustard
pico de gallo
guacamole
hot dog buns
jalapenos (if desired)
salt and pepper (to taste)

Directions:
Grill the sausages on an outdoor grill or george formean grill.
While the hotdogs are grilling, melt the butter in a skillet.  Once the butter is melted, sautee the onions, red bell pepper and green bell pepper.
Season the sauteed veggies with salt and pepper. Set aside.
Quickly grill/warm the hot dog buns so that they are warm.
Once the sausages are cooked, add the sausage to the hot dog bun.
Top the sausage in the following order: sauteed veggies, pico de gallo, guacamole, mayo, ketchup, mustard, cilantro and jalapenos (if desired).
