Title:
Easy Chipotle Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1005492.jpg
Ingredients:
One jar Herdez Traditional Chipotle Cooking Sauce
One chicken, cut into pieces
2 tablespoons oil for frying
One cup of water
Two limes, cilantro and sour cream, to garnish

Directions:
Heat oil in a frying pan. Cook chicken until skin is  carmelized on each side.
Add one cup of water and cook on low heat for about 30 minutes, making sure chicken is cooked thoroughly.
Add jar of sauce and simmer for seven minutes.
Place on serving platter, garnish with chopped cilantro, lime wedges and a big spoonful of sour cream.

Submitter: 
Cynthia Ross
Description: 
Chicken is a favorite, especially when it's covered with this tangy, flavorful, but not hot sauce. My neighbor called this recipe Real good... a touch-down.
