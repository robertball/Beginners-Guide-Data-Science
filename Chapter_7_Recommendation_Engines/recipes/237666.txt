Title:
Smooth Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1130653.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup nonfat dry milk powder
5 tablespoons white sugar
3 tablespoons baking cocoa
1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon, or more to taste
1 dash salt
3 cups boiling water

Directions:
Stir milk powder, sugar, cocoa, cinnamon, and salt together in a saucepan. Pour boiling water over the milk powder mixture; stir until mixture is dissolved into the liquid.
