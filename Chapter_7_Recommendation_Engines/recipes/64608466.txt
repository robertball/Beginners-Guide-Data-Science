Title:
Rhubarb Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 - 3 1/2 cups Rhubarb chopped
2 oz butter
1 vanilla bean, scraped
1 cup brown sugar (not packed)
1/4 tsp salt
3 cups half and half
7 - 10 drops cherry blossom extract

Directions:
combine rhubarb, butter, vanilla bean (and seeds) vanilla extract, sugar and salt in a medium saucepan
cover and place on medium-low heat
simmer until rhubarb is soft
break up rhubarb with a spoon if you want
in a medium bowl add milk and rhubarb mixture
if using cherry blossom extract, add it now
chill completely
Churn in ice cream maker as you normally would.
