Title:
Easy Chicken Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
2 Boneless Breast
2 Bags frozen stir-fry veggies
Stir-Fry Sauce

Directions:
Cut chicken into small peices or slices and brown.  Then add veggies and saute till tender then add sauce and cook for a few more minutes then serve.
