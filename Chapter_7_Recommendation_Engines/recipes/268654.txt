Title:
Dairy-Free Everything Bagel Seasoning Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5860536.jpg
Ingredients:
5 ounces plain coconut milk yogurt
3 tablespoons cashew butter
3 tablespoons dairy-free mayonnaise
1 tablespoon stone-ground mustard
2 tablespoons seasoned salt (such as Trader Joe'sÂ® Everything But the Bagel)
1 tablespoon dehydrated onion flakes
1 tablespoon dehydrated celery flakes
1 teaspoon snipped chives, or to taste

Directions:
Combine coconut yogurt, cashew butter, mayonnaise, and mustard in a medium bowl. Whisk until there are no lumps. Stir in seasoned salt, onion flakes, and celery flakes. Cover and refrigerate until dehydrated vegetables are softened, 20 to 30 minutes.
Garnish dip with chives before serving.

Submitter: 
Bibi
Description: 
Combine 'everything bagel'-flavored seasoned salt, coconut yogurt, cashew butter, and dried onion and celery flakes for a flavorful, dairy-free dip.
