Title:
Spicy Pepper Jack Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/497080.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag yellow corn tortilla chips
1 large boneless skinless chicken breast, cooked and shredded
1 medium bell pepper, diced
1 medium jalape√±o pepper, finely diced
2 green onions
sour cream, optional
cilantro, optional
1 cup grated pepper jack cheese
1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit. Place half bag of chips on a pizza stone or a large oven proof dish.
Spread half of the chicken, onions, and peppers over chips. Add half of each cheese, and layer more chips on top. Add the rest of the chicken, peppers and onions, Top with the rest of the cheese.
Bake in oven until cheese is melted and crisp, about 15 minutes. Top with sour cream and cilantro if desired.
