Title:
Slow Cooker - BBQ Pulled Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4.5-5lb boneless pork shoulder
1/2 large onion, thingly sliced into rings
4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
1 cup chicken stock
1 tbsp brown sugar
1- 1/2 tbsp chili powder
1 tbsp kosher salt
1 tsp ground cumin
1/8 tsp ground cinnamon
2 cups bbq sauce

Directions:
Set crockpot to low. Add onion and garlic in the bottom of the crockpot. Pour in chicken stock. Take pork and pat dry with paper towel. Combine remaining ingredients EXCEPT bbq sauce in a small bowl. Rub the pork with the spice mixture. Place pork on top of the onions. Cover and let simmer until tender and flaky.
Low: 10 hr
High: 7hr
Remove pork to a colander to drain. Shred pork. Heat bbq sauce and pour over pork. Mix well. Serve with spicy slaw on top and a bun.

Submitter: 
Jackie
Description: 
Sweet pulled pork slow cooked and mixed with bbq sauce. Serve with spicy slaw on the top and a bun.
