Title:
Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Egg Noodles
Celery
Carrots
Rosemary
Onion
Bone-In Chicken
Chicken Bouillon Cube

Directions:
Boil chicken without skin
Remove meat from bone when cool
Skim broth
Add chicken pieces, bouillon cube, rosemary
Add Celery, Carrots and Onion
Boil for about 1/2 hour
Add egg noodles and cook for another 5-10 minutes

Jenny Deveau
Description: 
Jen's homemade chicken noodle soup, good for freezing
