Title:
Tembleque de Coco Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of coconut milk
1/2 cup of cornstarch
3/4 cups of sugar
1/2 tsp of salt
1 tsp of vanilla flavoring
1 tsp of coconut flavoring
1/2 cup of shredded dry coconut
Ground cinnamon (optional)

Directions:
In a saucepan, at medium, heat the coconut milk, the sugar and the salt.
Dilute the cornstarch with some of the coconut milk and add it to the saucepan.
Stir constantly, adding the shredded coconut slowly, until it boils then lower it to a simmer until it thickens. Remove from heat as soon as it thickens.
Pour mix into a mold and let it cool to room temp.
Cover with plastic
Refrigerate for 2 hrs or overnight
Take a spatula and run it around the edges to loosen it and flip it onto a plate.
Sprinkle cinnamon over it before serving. BUEN PROVECHO!

Submitter: 
Chef Kiki
Description: 
This is a very popular Puerto Rican dessert. It has a jell-o like consistency once it cools off, which is where it gets its name from.
