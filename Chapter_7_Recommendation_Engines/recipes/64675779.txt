Title:
Quick but Authentic Thai Red Curry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb chicken breast, sliced into bite size pieces
1 cup jasmine rice
2 Tbsp peanut oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2" piece of ginger, minced
2 Tbsp Thai red chili paste
1 can of coconut milk
1 cup chicken broth
1 tsp fish sauce
1 stalk of lemongrass, bruised - you can omit if you don't have
3 kaffir lime leaves - you can omit if you don't have
juice of 1 lime
1 stalk of broccoli, cut into bite size pieces
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large saute pan, saute chicken in 1 Tbsp of peanut oil, salt and pepper, and 1 clove of minced garlic, until browned and fully cooked. Set aside.
Prepare rice per directions.
In the saute pan, add the remaining oil and gentle cook the remaining garlic and the ginger for a minute. Stir in the red chili paste.
Add the coconut milk and stir until blended. Simmer for 5 minutes.
Add the chicken broth, fish sauce, lemongrass and kaffir lime leaves, and lime juice. Simmer with lid off for 10 minutes.
Add chicken and broccoli, and simmer gently for 10 more minutes. Taste and season as necessary.
Serve over the rice!
