Title:
Italian "Meatball"Sandwich Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c chopped green onion
1/4c Italian bread crumbs
3 TB grated Parmesan Cheese
1 lb ground beef, bison or turkey
2 tsp minced garlic
1 lb load Italian bread, cut into 1" cubes
1 8oz pkg light or fat free cream cheese
1/2 c light mayo or Miracle Whip
1 tsp+ Italian Seasonings
addt'l dried basil, oregano, garlic powder to taste
1/4 tsp black pepper
2 cups shredded mozzerella cheese, divided
1 jar Barilla Italian Baking Sauce
1 cup water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cube bread and place in very large mixing bowl.
In a large non-stick dutch oven, brown ground beef, onion and garlic.  Turn heat to low.  Add Parmesan, cream cheese, mayo, Italian seasonings, addt'l spices, pepper.  Stir until cream cheese melts.  Pour in Baking Sauce, mix thoroughly.  Add 1 cup of water to baking sauce jar, shake, and pour into mixture.  Add 1 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese, stir.  Pour over bread cubes and toss to coat.
Pour sauce/bread mixture into sprayed 9x13 baking dish.  Top with remaining cheese.
Bake, uncovered, about 30 minutes until cheese browns a little and dish is bubbly.

This is a fairly quick casserole to put together because I cheat and don't actually make meatballs...but everything a good meatball would have is in this casserole.  :)
