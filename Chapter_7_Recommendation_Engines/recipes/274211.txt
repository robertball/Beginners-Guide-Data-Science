Title:
Lemonade with Whiskey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups bourbon
1 (6 ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed and undiluted
1 (6 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed and undiluted
1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
1 (2 liter) bottle lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage, chilled
10 ounces club soda, chilled

Directions:
Combine bourbon, orange juice concentrate, lemonade concentrate, and lemon juice in a large pitcher and mix well. Chill for at least 3 hours.
Add lemon-lime beverage and club soda to the pitcher and mix. Pour over crushed ice to serve.

Submitter: 
CajunGrocer.com
Description: 
This delicious adult beverage combines those perennial favorites -- lemonade and whiskey -- to kick off your summer party!
