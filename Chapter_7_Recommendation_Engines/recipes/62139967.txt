Title:
Broiled Salmon with Lemon Dill Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp mayonnaise
2 Tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp Dijon mustard
3/4 tsp dill weed
1 tsp black pepper
4 six-ounce salmon steaks

Directions:
Preheat oven to broil.
Sprinkle pepper on both sides of salmon steaks.
Combine mayonnaise, lemon juice, mustard, and dill weed until a smooth sauce is achieved.
Broil salmon 3-5 minutes on each side or until it easily flakes.
Portion steaks on plate and spoon sauce over salmon.
Garnish with a lemon wedge if desired.

Submitter: 
Rick Janssen
Description: 
This easy to make recipe is good for even non-fish eaters.
