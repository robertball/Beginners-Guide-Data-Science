Title:
Apple Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons low-calorie granulated sugar substitute
1 tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cups All-Bran® Original or All-Bran® Bran Buds®
2 cups apple juice
1 egg
1/4 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Stir together flour, sugar substitute, baking powder and salt. Set aside.
In large mixing bowl, combine Kellogg's(R) All-Bran(R) cereal and apple juice.  Let stand about 2 minutes or until cereal softens. Add egg and oil. Beat well. Add flour mixture, stirring only until combined. Portion evenly into twelve 2 1/2-inch muffin-pan cups coated with cooking spray.
Bake at 400 degrees F about 26 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Kellogg's® All-Bran®
Description: 
These apple-flavored muffins are packed with fiber, making them a healthy, anytime snack.
