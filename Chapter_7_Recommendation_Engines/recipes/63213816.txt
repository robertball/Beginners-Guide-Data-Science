Title:
Chipotle Lime Shrimp Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound devained large shrimp, approximately 20 shrimp
1/2 of 7oz can chipotle peppers in adobo (freeze extra for later use)
1 lime, juiced
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons cilantro, chopped
1-1/2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
1/8 cup olive oil
1/4 teaspoons cumin
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon salt (or more to taste)
2 limes, cut into quarters
5 wooden skewers, soaked for 1 hour

Directions:
Comine all ingredients in blender.  Blend until smooth 2-3 minutes.  Place shrimp into resealbale bag, pour marinade over shrimp and mix around until evenly distributed.  Place in fridge for 1-2 hours.
Place wooden skewers to soak in warm water.
Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat.
Remove skewers from the water, pat dry, and spray with nonstick cooking spray. Skewer the shrimp. Grill the shrimp on the grill on both sides until pink and opaque, about 5 minutes per side.
Serve with limes, rice and grilled veggies.
