Title:
Root Beer Glazed Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/614650.jpg
Ingredients:
GLAZE:
24 ounces (two cans) root beer
1 1/2 tbsp pepper jelly
1 bay leaf
1 1/2 tbsp hot sauce
6 cloves
1 stick cinnamon
1/4 c. orange juice
1 tsp lemon pepper
HAM:
1 cured, smoked ham 5+ lbs
DRY RUB:
1/2 tsp. dry mustard
3/4 c. brown sugar

Directions:
The night before combine all ingredients for glaze into saucepan.
Bring mixture to rolling boil.
Boil for 3 minutes, then lower temperature and let simmer for 30 minutes.
Strain the pan contents to get rid of solids.
Refrigerate the mixture.
The next morning preheat oven to 350.
Place ham in disposable foil roasting pan.
Spoon on prepared glaze to wet the ham.
Put rub over the ham.
Pour 1/2 c. water into bottom of pan.
Place pan in preheated oven.
Spoon glaze over ham every 15 minutes during baking.
Keep adding water to the bottom of pan as needed to make sure it never dries up.
Bake until internal temperature of ham is 160 degrees.
