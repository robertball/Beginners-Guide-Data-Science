Title:
Spicy Pineapple Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 60oz can pineapple tidbits (or cubes)
1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
1/3 cup diced green bell pepper
1/2 cup finely chopped red onion
3 T minced fresh cilantro
2 T seasoned rice wine vinegar
1/4 cup finely chopped green onions
1/8-1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes

Directions:
In a non-aluminum bowl, mix all ingredients. Add salt and pepper to taste. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Jackie Cree
Description: 
This sweet and slightly spicy salsa goes great with grilled dishes.
