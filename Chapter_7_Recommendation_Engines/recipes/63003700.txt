Title:
madeleines banana bread cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup self rising flour  1 cup milk  1 cup sugar 1/2 cup butter,melted  4 bananas ,sliced  vanilla ice cream  Topping:3/4 cup brown sugar 1/2 cup flour 1/2 cup butter,softened vanilla ice cream  1 box banana cream pudding mix 2 cups milk (for the pudding)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.Whisk together flour,sugar,milk,and butter.Pour into a lightly greased baking dish.Top with sliced bananas and sprinkle topping  Topping:mix all ingredients together well.bake for 40 min to a hour or until a dark brown top with pudding serve warm.mix pudding mix together with 2 cups of milk
