Title:
Creamy Bowties and Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1034655.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups Bow tie Pasta(tricolor)
8 Oz. low fat/no fat cream cheese
10.5 Oz. of Cream of Chicken soup
14.5 Oz. of Italian Style Dice Tomatoes(drain all liquid)
2 packages of chicken gravy
12 Oz. of water
2 chicken breast halves (skinless and boneless)(baked)
1 Teaspoon of dried chives
1/2 Teaspoon of salt
1/2 Teaspoon of pepper or to taste.
2 Teaspoons garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Wash the chicken breast halves and place in a baking dish or pan of your choice. Salt and pepper and sprinkle some garlic powder on the chicken.Sprinkle the chives over the chicken at this point. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes or until juices run clear.
While your chicken is baking, combine cream of chicken soup, gravy mixes, water, tomatoes and room temperature cream cheese. Heat this mixture on medium heat for 15 minutes or until cream cheese has melted. turn your heat off and wait for chicken to bake. Cut the baked chicken into 1" pieces and add to your liquid mixture. Heat to boiling slowly.
While your sauce is heating cook pasta about 9 minutes done. Drain pasta and add sauce. Enjoy!
