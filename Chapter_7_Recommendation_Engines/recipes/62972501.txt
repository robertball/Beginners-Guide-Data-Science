Title:
Cran-Rasp Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/735527.jpg
Ingredients:
8 Cups Water
10 Lipton Tea Bags
6 Cups Ocean Spray Cranberry-Raspberry Juice
2 Tablespoon 100% lemon juice
1 Cup Pineapple Juice
1 Cup White Sugar

Directions:
Boil 4 cups of water with the tea bags in it for 10 minutes in a medium sized pot
Lower heat to keep warm for about 10 mins.
Take out the tea bags and squeeze them dry into pot to get flavor out. Throw away bags.
Add sugar and stir until dissolved. Cut off heat
In a gallon jug add the cran-rasp juice,pineapple juice, lemon juice, and four cups of water, and stir.
Pour tea into jug and cool in fridge.
Serve over ice
