Title:
Jim's Slow Cooker BBQ Pulled Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/475212.jpg
Ingredients:
8 pound boneless pork shoulder
1/4 cup chopped garlic
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 dash soy sauce
1 tablespoon corn syrup
2 tablespoons orange juice
3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon molasses
1/4 cup red wine
2 cups Italian salad dressing such as Wishbone
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1/4 teaspoon seasoning salt such as lawrys
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 bottle BBQ sauce such as Sweet baby Rays

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix garlic, onion, soy sauce, corn syrup, orange juice, Worcestershire sauce, molasses, wine, Italian salad dressing, cider vinegar, garlic powder, salt, onion powder, Cajun seasoning, crushed red pepper, seasoning salt and brown sugar.
Place the pork shoulder in your slow cookerwith all the ingredients and cook on low for 8 hours.
After 8 hours shred the pork shoulder with a fork then add the BBQ sauce and cook for an additional 2 hours. The wait is worth it!
