Title:
Smoke Bombs (Habanero Deviled Eggs) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1600249.jpg
Ingredients:
1 dozen eggs hard boiled
1 Habanero pepper FINELY minced
Smoked paprika
Dried Pepper Flakes

Directions:
1. Dust empty egg whites with smoked paprika, salt and pepper before filling.
2. Mix your normal yolk filling, but add in finely minced habanero.
3. Place yolk filling in baggy and pipe into whites.
4. Finish with another dusting of smoked paprika and dried chilie flakes.
5. ENSURE CHILDREN DO NOT EAT WITHOUT ADULT SUPERVISION.
