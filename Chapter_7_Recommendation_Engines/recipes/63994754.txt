Title:
Oven Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup brown rice
1 cup beef broth
1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
1/2 c. chopped onion
1/2 c. chopped mushrooms
2 tbsp. butter, melted
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In a 2 quart casserole dish, mix together rice, beef broth, chicken broth, butter, garlic salt, and seasoned salt. Bake uncovered in preheated oven for 1 1/2 hrs stirring a couple of times, until liquid is absorbed and rice is tender.
