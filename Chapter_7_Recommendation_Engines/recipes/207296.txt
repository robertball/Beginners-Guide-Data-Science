Title:
Kahlua with Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988571.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces Kahlua
3 fluid ounces cold milk

Directions:
Combine in a highball glass. For a delicious variation, try with Kahlua Mocha, Kahlua Hazelnut or Kahlua French Vanilla.

Submitter: 
Kahlua
Description: 
A grownup treat, this cocktail of Kahlua and milk is perfect with cookies!
