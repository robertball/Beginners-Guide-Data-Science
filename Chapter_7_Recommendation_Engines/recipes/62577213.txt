Title:
B.C's Avocado Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Avocados, peeled and mashed
Juice of one lime
2 - 3 cups Chicken Broth
1 cup unsweeetened natural yogurt
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Place all ingredients into a blender.  Blend until thoroughly pureed.  Serve cold or at room temperature.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
This soup is the creation of BC Marks from Mazatlan Mexico.  It is simple to prepare, healthy and a joy to eat.  Serve cold or at room temperature.
