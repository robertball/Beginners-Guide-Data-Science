Title:
Chicken Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/480409.jpg
Ingredients:
3 md Chicken Breasts (cooked, cut into strips)
1 lb Angel Hair Pasta
2 heads Broccoli
1 btl KRAFT Asian Toasted Sesame Dressing
1 pkg Snow Peas (optional)

Directions:
BOIL water in large stockpot.
ADD broccoli, cook until done, remove without draining water.
ADD angel hair to the boiling water and cook until el dente, drain.
MIX together chicken, pasta, broccoli and dressing.
SERVE with extra dressing (optional) and green onions.

Submitter: 
Jody
Description: 
This is an easy chicken stir fry that contains chicken, angel hair, broccoli and a ready made dressing.
