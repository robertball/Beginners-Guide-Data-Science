Title:
Small Peanut Butter Powder Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1095242.jpg
Ingredients:
Bread: 3 Cups unbleached all purpose flour
1 1/2 Cups warm water
1 Tablespoons white sugar
1 Tablespoons  dry yeast
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
Filling:  3 Tablespoons white sugar
3 Tablespoons unbleached all purpose flour
3 Tablespoons all natural peanut butter powder
4 Tablespoons peanut oil

Directions:
Dough:Put the warm water in a small bowl, and then add dry yeast into the water.
In a large bowl mix the water mixture and the flour together, mix well and form it into a dough. Knead  the dough until smooth.
Put a little bit of oil around the dough, and cover the top of the large bowl with plastic wrap.
Let rise for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
Filling: Mix all the ingredients together, and divide 10 parts.
Divide the dough into 10 equal pieces  and knead them until smooth ball.
Roll each dough into thin flat circles, about 4 inches ,and place filling on each of them.
Pull and pinch the edges of the flat circle  together to enclose the filling in the dough.
Flatten each dough and roll the dough into a oval shape , about 6 inches.
Fold the two sides together and roll it, about 6 inches length.
Fold the 2 sides again and  roll it,  about 6 inches length ,then fold 2 sides. And flatten  slightly.
Place each dough 2 inches apart onto a nonstick cook sheet, rest for 30 minutes for second time rises.
Preheat the oven to 385 degrees F (196 degrees C ).
Bake for 12 to 15 minutes in the preheated oven, until top is light brown. Let them cool and store in plastic bread bag.
