Title:
Soy Ginger Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound skinless salmon fillets
1/3 cup brown sugar, divided
2 teaspoons lemon pepper, divided
1 teaspoon garlic powder, divided
1/3 cup low sodium soy sauce
2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
2 tsp. ginger
1/3 cup orange juice
1 tbsp. corn starch

Directions:
Rub salmon with about 1 tablespoon brown sugar. Lightly sprinkle with lemon pepper and garlic powder; rub seasoning into fish.
Into a small saucepan set over medium heat, pour soy sauce and 1 tbsp. olive oil. Stir in ginger and remaining brown sugar, lemon pepper, and garlic powder. Bring to a gentle simmer, stirring constantly until sugar has dissolved. Remove from heat, stir in orange juice.
Place fish and marinade into a resealable plastic bag, seal, and refrigerate overnight, or for at least 3 hours.
Preheat olive oil in skillet. Place fish in skillet and sear both sides to lock in juices. Reserve marinade.
Add cornstarch to marinade and heat till thick. Use sauce over salmon.

Description: 
Salmon is marinated overnight in soy sauce, brown sugar, orange juice, spices, and fresh ginger; then pan fried to perfection.Modified from: Soy Ginger Salmon
