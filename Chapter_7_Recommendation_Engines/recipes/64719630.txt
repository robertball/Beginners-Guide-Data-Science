Title:
Vegetarian Chicken Cheese Enchilada's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Can Fri-Chick, shredded
1 Can 4 ounces green chili's, diced
1/2 C Sour Cream
Monterey Jack Cheese
1 Can Red or Green Enchilada Sauce
Corn Tortillas

Directions:
Mix chicken, chilies, onion and sour cream and 1/2 the cheese.
Warm tortillas in canola oil.
Place 1/4 cup mixture down center of tortilla.  Roll and place in baking dish.
Pour enchilada sauce on top and cover with remaining cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 40 minutes.
Let sit 10 minutes prior to serving.
