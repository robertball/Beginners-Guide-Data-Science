Title:
Creamy Tortellini with Chicken and Artichoke Hearts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
1 medium onion, sliced thin
1 medium red pepper, cut in strips
1 tsp garlic powder
3 cups cooked, cubed chicken breast
1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
1 can (15 oz) quartered, marinated artichoke hearts, drained
1 can (10 3/4 ounces) condensed Cream of Mushroom soup
1/2 cup milk
3/4 cup Greek yogurt or sour cream
1/4 cup dry white wine
1 tsp. dried parsley
salt and pepper to taste
2-12 ounce packages mushroom and cheese or spinach and cheese refrigerated tortellini

Directions:
In a large skillet, saute onions and peppers in olive oil and garlic powder.
When vegetables are tender, add chicken and cook until pieces are lightly browned.
Add mushrooms and artichoke hearts, and cook just until mushrooms are slightly soft.
Reduce heat to medium-low, and stir in condensed soup, milk, yogurt, wine, parsley, and salt and pepper.
Meanwhile, cook tortellini according to package directions.
Drain well, and add to skillet.
Mix well, and simmer 5-10 minutes more, or until bubbly and heated through.

Submitter: 
Mrs. L
Description: 
Tender chicken and artichoke hearts combine with fresh tortellini in a creamy sauce for a main dish that will have your family asking for seconds!
