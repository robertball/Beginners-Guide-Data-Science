Title:
Baby Spinach Salad with Mandarin Orange and Red Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. baby spinach, about 5 or 6 cups
1 cup mandarin Oranges, drained
1/2 cup chopped red onions
1 tablespoon orange marmalade
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil
salt and pepper

Directions:
Arrange spinach leaves on a large platter or individual salad plates.  Top with mandarin sections and chopped red onion.  Whisk together marmalade and vinegar.  Stream oil into dressing while continuing to whisk.
When ready to serve, pour dressing over salad and season with salt and pepper.
