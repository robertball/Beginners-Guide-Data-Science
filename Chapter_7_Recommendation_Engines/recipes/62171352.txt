Title:
Oriental Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T. Honey
1 1/2 T. Rice wine vinegar
1/4 C. Mayonnaise
1 t. Grey Poupon dijon mustard
1/8 t. Sesame oil

Directions:
1. Mix all ingredients together in a small mixing bowl
2. Refrigerate for 2 hours to chill or overnight

Submitter: 
Whitney
Description: 
This dressing is sweet and tangy, and good on chicken salad!
