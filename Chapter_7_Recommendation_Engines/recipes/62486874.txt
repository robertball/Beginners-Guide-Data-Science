Title:
Healthy Carrot Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c whole wheat flour flour
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
3 eggs
2/3 c brown sugar
3/4 cup melted butter & applesauce (half and half or all applesauce if you prefer)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups grated carrot

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Combine flour, cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder, and salt; set aside.
3. Beat the eggs and sugar until frothy and lightened in color. Stir in the oil, vanilla extract, and grated carrot.
4.  Fold in the flour mixture.
5. Pour the batter into the prepared muffin tins.
6. Bake in preheated oven until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean, 15 to 20 minutes. Remove to cool on wire racks.
