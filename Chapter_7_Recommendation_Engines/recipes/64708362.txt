Title:
Chicken Bacon Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz bow tie pasta
2-3 chicken breasts, cut into bite size pieces
4 slices bacon
2 tsp Cajun seasoning
2 cups heavy cream
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package instructions.
Meanwhile, fry up the bacon until crisp over medium-high heat. Remove bacon to paper towel lined plate to drain, do not discard bacon grease. Fry chicken pieces in grease until cooked through.
When chicken is done and cooked through, drain grease from pan. Add the cream and Cajun seasoning to the pan with the chicken and bring to a boil, stirring often. Reduce heat and let simmer 3-4 minutes or until sauce has thickened. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until fully incorporated.
Stir in the cooked, drained pasta and stir to coat. Crumble bacon pieces over the top, stir and serve!
