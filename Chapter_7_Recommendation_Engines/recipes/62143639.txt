Title:
Shelby's Microwave Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/597454.jpg
Ingredients:
Shelby's MICROWAVE MEATLOAF
Total cooking time: 14 minutes
1 can (8 ounces) TOMATO SAUCE
1/4 cup BROWN SUGAR
1 tsp. prepared MUSTARD
2 EGGS, lightly beaten
1 medium ONION, minced
1/4 cup finely minced GREEN PEPPER
1/4 tsp. GARLIC POWDER or 1 GARLIC clove crushed
1/2 cup fine SALTINE CRACKER CRUMBS or BREAD CRUMBS
2 pounds EXTRA LEAN GROUND BEEF
1 tsp. SALT
1/4 tsp. PEPPER

Directions:
1.In small bowl, combine tomato sauce, brown sugar, and mustard.
2. Set aside.
3. In large mixing bowl, combine eggs, onion, green pepper, garlic, cracker crumbs, salt and pepper.
4. Add beef and Â½ cup of tomato sauce mixture and mix thoroughly.
5. Place meat mixture in 2 qt. MICROWAVE SAFE DISH.
6. Spread remaining tomato sauce mix over top of meat.
7. Cook uncovered, on HI (max power) for 14 minutes.
8. Drain most of the grease as possible as soon as you take it out of the oven.
9. Let stand, uncovered, 10 to 15 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Shelby Snider
Description: 
Great Meat loaf, quick, easy and  ready to eat in 45 minutes. It will be ready by the time you get the creamed patatoes ready!
