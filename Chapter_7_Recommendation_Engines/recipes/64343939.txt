Title:
Milkshake Magic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk* (see note)
1/4 teaspoon vanilla
1 teaspoon sugar
1/4 cup dry milk* (see note)
1 Tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon chocolate drink mix
Ice cubes

Directions:
You will need a blender or smoothie maker to do this.  Put all the ingredients in the blender and blend at high speed intil well mixed.  Add ice cubes until mizture becomes thick.
