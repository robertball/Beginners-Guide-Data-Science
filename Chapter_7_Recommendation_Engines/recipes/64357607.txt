Title:
Cauliflower Macaroni and Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2135686.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C. Dry macaroni
1 Medium size head of cauliflower
2 C. Cheddar cheese, grated
2 Slices of bread, toasted
1 Cooked sausage, diced
4 - 5 Tbsp. All purpose flour
4 - 5 Tbsp, Butter, melted
1 - 1 /2 C, Milk, Allow enough milk to make a nice creamy sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
Grease a 2 1/2 qt, casserole and set aside
Cook macaroni according to package directions,  Allow to cool.
On a cutting board slice cauliflower into smaller florets and rinse under cold running water in a colander
Place florets in a medium size saucepan inside of a steamer basket and steam until cauliflower is just tender. Set aside to cool
Grate cheddar cheese onto a serving plate
Reserve 1 C. of Cheddar cheese for top of casserole
In a medium saucepan melt butter over medium heat and add flour  to make a thick roux . Mix in milk slowly, stirring constantly to avoid scorching on the bottom Add grated cheese to roux mixture  When sauce has thickened to right consistency set aside.
In the casserole dish spread cauliflower florets over the entire surface of casserole dish
Spread macaroni noodles over the cauliflower making sure to cover the entire surface.
Take the cheese sauce and stir into the cauliflower macaroni mixture until everything is well coated.
Sprinkle reserved grated cheese, toasted bread crumbs and sausage meat on top layer of casserole.
Place casserole dish in preheated oven and bake for 35 minutes until cheese is nicely melted

Cheerios
Description: 
A delicious way to enjoy cauliflower with a creamy cheese sauce that really adds much moisture to this casserole,
