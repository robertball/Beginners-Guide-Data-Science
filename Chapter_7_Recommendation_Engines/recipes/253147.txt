Title:
Very Berry Chocolate Milk Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3648430.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup LACTAID® Whole Milk
1/4 cup powdered sugar
4 scoops LACTAID® Berry Chocolate Crumble Ice Cream
2 tablespoons chocolate syrup
8 fresh blackberries or raspberries

Directions:
Place Lactaid(R) whole milk and powdered sugar in a blender. Blend for 1 to 2 minutes. Add the Lactaid(R) Berry Chocolate Crumble Ice Cream. Pulse 2 or 3 times to combine.
Pour into two chilled glasses. Drizzle with chocolate syrup and garnish with fresh berries.

Submitter: 
Lactaid
Description: 
Lactaid® Berry Chocolate Crumble Ice Cream and fresh summer berries create a true summer treat.
