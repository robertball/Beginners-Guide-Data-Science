Title:
Sweet Italian Chicken Sausage and Tortellini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/823275.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package al frescoÂ® All Natural Sweet Italian Chicken Sausage
1 1/2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
5 cloves garlic
1/2 cup white wine
3 (14 ounce) cans, chicken broth
9-13 ounces refrigerated cheese tortellini
1 (14.5oz) can diced tomatoes
6 ounces baby spinach leaves
1 tablespoon butter
Freshly shaved Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat Dutch oven with 1/2 tsp of oil to coat and saute chicken sausage until browned and internal heat is 165 degrees. Remove from pan and slice into small pieces and set aside.
Meanwhile, finely mince the garlic cloves.
Heat remaining oil in pan, add garlic and saute for 30 seconds, stir in wine and broth and bring to a boil. Cook for about 2 minutes then add tortellini. Cook for another 5 minutes and then stir in spinach and diced tomatoes. Cook until the spinach wilts, 2 minutes.
Return sauteed sausage pieces to the soup, cook for an additional 5 minutes add the butter and serve when butter is melted.
Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
