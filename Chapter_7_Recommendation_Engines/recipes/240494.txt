Title:
Chipped Beef Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4503824.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 (2.5 ounce) package chipped beef, chopped
2 stalks celery, finely chopped
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 teaspoon celery salt
1 pinch paprika, or as desired

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, chipped beef, celery, onion, and celery salt together in a bowl; form into a ball. Sprinkle paprika over cheese ball.
