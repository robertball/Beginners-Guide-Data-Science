Title:
Five Spice Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/846302.jpg
Ingredients:
16 oz. Chicken Stock
1 1/2 c. shredded Napa Cabbage
1 medium Carrot peeled and diced
2 oz. Dry Udon Noodles
1/2 t. Chinese Five Spice
1/4 c. Green Peas
2 T. sliced Green Onion
1/4 t. Sriracha Hot sauce - optional
1/4 t. soy sauce - optional

Directions:
Combine chicken stock, cabbage, carrot and Udon noodles in a medium pot. Bring to simmer and cook till veggies are soft and noodles are tender. Add chinese five spice, green peas, green onion and hot sauce and/or soy sauce if using. Let sit 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
Asian inspired, this simple and easy to put together soup is made with common ingredients you find in the grocery store. The Chinese Five Spice adds a wonderful unique flavor and the Sriracha Hot sauce (if used) adds spicey warmth.  Leftover chicken can be added if you have it.
