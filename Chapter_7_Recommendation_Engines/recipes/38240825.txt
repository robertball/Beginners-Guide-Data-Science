Title:
Polenta with Bacon and Fontina Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/700932.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup finely chopped bacon
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 garlic clove, minced
5 cups low-salt chicken broth
1 cup frozen corn kernels, thawed
1 cup polenta
1 cup (packed) grated Fontina cheese
1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
2 T chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Saute chopped bacon in heavy large skillet over med-hi heat until crisp, about 8 minutes.  Using slotted spoon, transfer to bowl.  Pour off all but 2T drippings.
Add onion and garlic to drippings in skillet and saute until golden brown, about 2 minutes.
Add chicken broth, corn and bacon; bring to boil.
Gradually add polenta cornmeal, whisking constantly.  Cook until polenta is soft and thick, stirring frequently, about 20 minutes.
Add Fontina and Parmesan, stirring until melted, about 2 minutes.
Stir in chopped parsley.
Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Transfer to bowl and serve hot.

Description: 
This is my favorite polenta recipe.  It's delicious with braised short ribs and sauce as shown in the picture.  It's also just a great side by itself!
