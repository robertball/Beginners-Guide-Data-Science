Title:
Gail's Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6039991.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup white vinegar
3/4 c oil
1 tsp salt
1 tsp celery seed
1 tsp dry mustard
1 cup sugar

Directions:
Bring all the ingredients to a boil.
Let it cool down and pour it over
2 bags of slaw in a bowl that can be covered
Shake the bowl so all the liquids cover the slaw .
Leave in refrigerator for 24 hours.
