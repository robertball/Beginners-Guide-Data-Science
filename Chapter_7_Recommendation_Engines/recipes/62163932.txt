Title:
Zucchini Pizza Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 4 cups shredded zucchini (unpeeled)
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 2 eggs
* 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
* 2 cups (8 ounces) shredded part-skim
mozzarella cheese, divided
* 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded Monterey Jack cheese,
divided
* 1 pound ground beef or ground turkey
* 1/2 cup chopped onion
* 1 tsp italian seasoning
* 1 can (15 ounces) Italian tomato sauce
* 1 medium green pepper, chopped
* 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
* 1/2 cup sliced black olives

Directions:
Place zucchini in a strainer and sprinkle with salt. Let drain for 15 minutes. Squeeze out moisture.
Combine zucchini with the eggs, Parmesan and half of the mozzarella and Monterey Jack cheeses. Press into greased 13-in. x 9-in. baking dish.
Bake, uncovered, at 400Â° for 20 minutes. While that is baking, brown the meat and onion over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain. Stir in the tomato sauce; carefully spoon over zucchini mixture and top with the remaining cheeses,  green pepper, mushrooms, and black olives.
Bake 20 minutes longer or until heated through. Let sit 5 minutes before serving.
