Title:
Southwestern Pineapple and Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Can 20 oz Pineapple slices
1 Tbsp lime juice
1 Tbsp veg oil
1-1/2 tsp chili pwdr
1/2 tsp oregano
1 clove garlic, chopped
5 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
Drain pineapple; reserve 1/2 cup juice.
Combine juices, oil, spices and garlic in resealable bag.
Add chicken. Seal and marinate in refrigerator 15 min.
Grill or broil chicken and pineapple, brushing occasionally with marinade, 5 to 8 min per side until chicken is cooked and pineapple is lightly browned.
