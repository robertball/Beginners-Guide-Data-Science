Title:
Red Velvet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/286805.jpg
Ingredients:
Dry Ingredients
2 1/2 cups cake flour
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp cocoa powder
Wet Ingredients:
1 cup buttermilk
2 eggs
1 tsp vinegar (white)
1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp butter flavoring
1 oz red food coloring
Frosting:
1 8 oz cream cheese at room temp
2 sticks butter at room temp
4 cups powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup chopped nuts pecans prefered

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Grease 2 9 inch round cake pans.
Sift together all dry ingredients.
Beat wet ingredients, adding food coloring last in an electric mixer on medium speed until well incorporated. Slowly add dry ingredients to bowl, until all ingredients are combined.
Bake for 20-30 minutes.
Cool for 10 min in pan then turn onto wire rack
Frosting:
Mix together the cream cheese and the softened butter. Gradually add confectioners sugar for about 5 min or until smooth
Add vanilla extract and frost the cake.
After cake is frosted add nuts all aroud the sides of the frosted cake.
Serves 10-12

Description: 
Southern traditional Red Velvet that is not overpowered by the chocolate.  Moist and flavorful.
