Title:
Ham and Cheddar Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
3 egg whites
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground pepper
1 1/2 c (4 oz) frozen broccoli florets, thawed
6 oz deli smoked ham, cut into 1/2 inch cubes (1 1/2 c)
1/3 c drained bottle roasted red bell peppers, cut into thin strips (canned roasted green chilies can be substituted)
1 tbsp butter
1/2 c (2 oz) shredded sharp cheddar cheese

Directions:
1. Preheat broiler
2. Beat eggs, egg whites, salt and pepper in a large bowl until blended.  Stir in broccoli, ham and pepper strips.
3. Melt butter over medium heat in 10-inch ovenproof skillet with sloping side.  Pour egg mixture into skillet; cover.  Cook 5 to 6 minutes or until eggs are set around edge (center will be wet).
4. Uncover; sprinkle with cheese, transfer skillet to broiler; broil, 5 inches from heat source. 2 minutes or until eggs are set in center and cheese is melted.
5. Let stand 5 minutes; cut into wedges.
