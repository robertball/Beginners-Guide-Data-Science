Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2450871.jpg
Ingredients:
Kale (Buy every bit the store has)
More Kale!
Come on, you think that's enough?
Potatoes (Less than the Kale)
Oil for frying

Directions:
Buy all the Kale!
Take the Kale home (or wherever you plan on making this delicious dish) and chop it to bits.
Chop up the potatoes and turn them to mash.
Mix up the Kale and mashed potatoes.
Make them into balls (big or small, whatever size you want) and fry those awesome fluffy delicious bites up!
Ta da!
