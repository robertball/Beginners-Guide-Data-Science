Title:
Fabulous Rice Krispie Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 stick butter
1/2 cup creamy peanut butter or to taste
1 regular bag miniature marshmallows
1 - 7 ounce jar marshmallow crème
1 regular box rice krispies
1 regular bag chocolate chips
1 regular bag butterscotch chips

Directions:
Put butter in large micro-safe container and melt on high in microwave, then add peanut butter and melt - about 1 minute.
Add marshmallows and heat on high for about 2 minutes
Add marshmallow cream and heat on high for no more than 2 minutes
Pour over rice krispies and spread in large sprayed pan and pat down
Melt chips in microwave slowly on mid-power and pour over all.
Enjoy
