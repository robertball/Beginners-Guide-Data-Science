Title:
White Peach Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ounces white zinfandel wine (chilled)
2 ounces peach schnapps (chilled)
2 ounces 100% pineapple juice (chilled)
4 ounces carbonated lemon-lime beverage (chilled)
fresh peach slices (optional)
fresh strawberry slices (optional)
pineapple wedge for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Mix all but pineapple in a glass. Garnish with pineapple. Serve.
