Title:
Spicy Scarborough Chicken Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/485626.jpg
Ingredients:
1 TBSP chopped fresh parsley
1 teaspoon chopped fresh sage
3 sprigs thyme, stripped and chopped
1 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme
1/4 cup olive oil
3/4 cup dry white wine
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 habenero pepper, finely diced

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl and whisk to combine. Place chicken into a large zippered plastic bag. Add marinade. Zip bag, forcing out as much air as possible. Refrigerate for four hours to overnight.

Submitter: 
Pam Ziegler Lutz
Description: 
Parsley, sage, rosemary, and thyme combine with garlic and spicy habenero pepper for a chicken marinade you'll want to sing about!
