Title:
Vegetarian Mexican Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp. olive oil
1 1/2 cups chopped onion
3 cups diced zucchini
1 can (14.5 oz.) Rotel, drained
1 can (15 oz.) black beans, drained and rinsed
1 can (11 oz.) yellow corn, drained
1 can (10 oz.) mild enchilada sauce
2 1/2 tsp. ground cumin
1/2 cup chopped cilantro (optional)
5 (8 inch) whole wheat flour tortillas
1 1/4 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees.  Coat a 3 quart round baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium high heat.  Add onion and saute 5 minutes.
Add zucchini and cumin and saute 5 minutes or until zucchini is crisp-tender.
Stir in tomatoes, beans, corn, and enchilada sauce; bring to a simmer.
Remove from heat and stir in cilantro if desired.
Put 1 tortilla in baking dish; top with 1 1/2 cups zucchini mixture; sprinkle with 1/4 cup cheese.  Repeat layers 4 times.
Cover and bake 20-25 minutes until bubbly around edges and cheese is melted.
Cool for 5 minutes.  Cut into wedges and serve with sour cream.

Submitter: 
DarcieA
Description: 
This recipe uses zucchini, black beans, and corn in place of meat.  A delicious, guilt free way to enjoy Mexican food!
