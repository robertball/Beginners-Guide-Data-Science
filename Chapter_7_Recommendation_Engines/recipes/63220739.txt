Title:
World's Best Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/836401.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package dried lasagne noodles
2 tsp salt
.
MIX & SIMMER:
---------------
1 lb. ground beef, cooked
1 (2 lb) jar Ragu spaghetti sauce
1 (15 oz) can petite diced tomatoes, undrained
1 cup water
2 TBSP sugar
Dash or two garlic powder (or fresh minced garlic)
1 TBSP italian seasoning
.
CHEESE FILLING:
---------------
16 oz. small curd cottage cheese
1 cup shredded mozzarella
1/2 cup shredded parmesan
1 egg, slightly beaten
couple dashes pepper
1 TBSP dried parsley
----------------------
1/2 cup mozzarella cheese (optional)

Directions:
Bring water to boil with salt.  Add lasagne noodles slowly (a few at a time).  Cook until almost done (usually 8-9 minutes).  Drain and lay out on sheet of foil.
Spray 11x13 pan with cooking spray.  Assemble lasagne as follows:
Small amount of sauce in bottom of pan.
-Layer of pasta
-Sauce
-Cheese
Repeat...
-Layer of pasta
-Sauce
-Cheese
End with...
-Layer of pasta
-Sauce
-1/2 cup mozzarella cheese (not necessary though)
Cover and bake at 350 for 45 minutes.  Let stand for 10 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
I tried lots of lasagne recipes and none were very good.  So I came up with my own and my kids love it!  It's a little sweeter than most lasagnes and not quite so tomatoey.
