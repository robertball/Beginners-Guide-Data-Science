Title:
Middle easter pita pizza (from Chondra) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pita breads
1/2 c. hummus (roasted garlic)
1 c. crumbled feta (4 oz)
1 small red onion, sliced
2 c. shredded spinach
2 med. tomatoes, chopped (1/2 c.)
4 T. sliced ripe kalamata olives

Directions:
Heat oven to 400F.  Place pita breads in ungreased cookie sheet.  Spread hummus on each bread.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Bake 8-10 minutes or till cheese is melted.  Top each pizza with onion, spinach, tomato, and olives.
