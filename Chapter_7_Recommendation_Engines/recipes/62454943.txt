Title:
Classic Chicken Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts, chunked in 1.5 inch squares
1 bell pepper, chunked
1 onion, chunked
1 fresh (or canned) pineapple, chunked
1 dozen grape or cherry tomatoes
Optional: ham chunks
Chicken Marinade (or make your own with soy sauce, garlic, lemon juice, vinegar) (or Italian dressing)

Directions:
Place ingredients on skewers based on individual preferences. Marinate for 30-60 minutes. Grill slowly.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
There's nothing like kabobs on the grill to remind me of summer.
