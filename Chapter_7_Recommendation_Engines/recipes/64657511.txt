Title:
Dave's King Ranch Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Chicken Breasts
1/2 Yellow Onion (chopped)
1/2 Green Bell Pepper (chopped)
3 tspn Butter
1 Can Cream of Chicken
1 Can Cream of Celery
1 Can Chicken Broth
1 Can Diced Tomatoes
1/3 Can Jar of Mild Salsa (Clint's Salsa)
1 Small Can of diced mild green jalapeno chiles
12 Yellow Corn Tortillas (Optional 6 Additional tortillas for covering)
1 1/2 Cups shredded Cheddar Cheese (Freshly shredded Tillamuk)
1 Tblsp Nature's Seasoning
1 Tblsp Adobe Seasoning
1 Tblsp McCorminck Poultry Seasoning
Non-stick cooking spray
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil and shred 4 chicken breasts in salted water with McCormick poultry seasoning.
Sautee chopped onion and bellpepper with dash of salt
Add cream of chicken , cream of celery, chicken broth, tomatoes, canned diced chiles, salsa, shredded chicken, and season to taste
Spray 9"x13" cooking dish with non-stick cooking  spray. and spread out 6 corn tortillas in bottom of dish.
Spread layer of shredded chicken with sauce over tortillas and sprinkle shredded cheese over it. Coat all tortillas. Add another layer of 6 corn tortillas and repeat the process of spreading the chicken mixture and covering with shredded cheese. (Optional: Cover with an extra layer of 6 corn tortillas  and shredded cheese if you want a more firm topping to it.)
Bake at 350 degree for 60 minutes covered lightly with foil. Uncover for last 10 minutes of cooking to brown. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
