Title:
Rosemary Lemon Lamb Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup olive oil
1/2 cup lemon juice
2 TBS chopped fresh Rosemary
1 TBS Fresh Oregano
6 cloves chopped Garlic
Kosher Salt or Sea Salt to taste
Ground Pepper to taste
12 lamb chops

Directions:
Stir together olive oil, lemon juice, rosemary, oregano, and garlic in a small bowl.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Place lamb chops in a ziplock bag and pour the olive oil mixture over the chops.
Marinate in the refrigerator for 10-24 hours.
Preheat grill for high heat.
Lightly oil grill grate. Place lamb chops on grill, and discard marinade. Cook for 10 minutes, turning once, or to desired doneness.
