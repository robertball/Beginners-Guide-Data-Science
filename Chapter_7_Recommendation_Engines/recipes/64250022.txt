Title:
Overnight Apple Cinnamon French Toast - Large Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup butter, melted
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 (21 ounce) cans apple pie filling, drained with liquid reserved
14-16 slices or 40 oz Portuguese sweet bread, cubed
8 eggs
3 cups milk
2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup maple syrup

Directions:
Spray a 9x13 inch baking pan with nonstick cooking spray.
In a very large bowl, beat the eggs with the milk, melted butter, brown sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla.  Add cubed bread and stir to coat.
Pour half of the bread mixture into the prepared pan.
Layer in the apple pie filling.
Pour in the remaining bread mixture. Drizzle over the reserved apple liquid.
Cover with nonstick aluminum foil, nonstick side down.
In the morning, remove casserole from refrigerator, and preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place covered pan into the oven and bake for 60 minutes.  Uncover and drizzle on maple syrup and cook for an additional 30 minutes.
Remove from the oven and let stand for 10 minutes, then cut into squares and serve warm.
