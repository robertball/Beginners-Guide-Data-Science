Title:
Cordell's pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-1/4 cups flour
2 eggs
1 T. oil
1 t. baking soda
1 t. baking powder
Buttermilk and blueberries to taste

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients and then add oil.  Add buttermilk to thin out.
Stir in bluberries.
Cook on a medium hot griddle.
Serve with real maple syrup only

Submitter: 
Mary Johnston
Description: 
My father-in-law's famous pancake recipe.  Enjoyed by family and friends!
