Title:
Marble Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
2 cups sugar
1 teas. vanilla or almond flavoring
4 eggs
3 cups flour
3 teas. baking powder
1/2 teas. salt
1 cup milk
4 oz german chocolate

Directions:
Cream together butter and sugar, add vanilla. Blend well with a mixer. Add 4 eggs one at a time beating well in between for a total of 10 minutes beating. Add alternately the dry ingredients and the milk mixing well.  Take 1/4 of the batter and place in a separate bowl.  Add the melted chocolate to the 1/4th batter.
Place batter in a tube pan add the chocolate batter and swirl with a fork to marblize.
Bake 350 degrees for 60 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Janet Larsen
Description: 
This is a moist heavier marble cake. Great to use for lunches...travels well.  An old family favorite.  I used a new silicone tube pan, sprayed it with cooking spray and the cake comes out clean every time!
