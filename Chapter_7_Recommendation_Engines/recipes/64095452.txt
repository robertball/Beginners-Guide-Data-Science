Title:
Big Ray's Zesty Roasted Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1208638.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb.  fresh mushrooms
1/2 lb.  baby carrots
1 onion, cut into 1/2-inch-thick wedges
1 large  yellow pepper, cut into 8 strip
1 cup cherry tomatoes, cut in half
1/3 cup  Italian Dressing
1/3 cup  Grated Parmesan Cheese, divided

Directions:
Heat oven to 450Â°F.
Cover rimmed baking sheet with foil; spray with cooking spray.
Toss vegetables with dressing and 1/4 cup cheese; spread onto prepared baking sheet.
Bake 28 to 30 min. or until vegetables are tender, stirring after 15 min. Sprinkle with remaining cheese.

One of the nice things about this dish is you can change it to use whatever vegetables are in season such as zucchini or squash.
