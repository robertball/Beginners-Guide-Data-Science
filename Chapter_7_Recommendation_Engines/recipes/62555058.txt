Title:
Cherry Chocolate Shortbread Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/532504.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 tsp almond extract
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 cup corn starch
1/2 cup dried cherries, chopped
1 ounce semi-sweet chocolate, finely chopped (about 1/4 cup)
1 tbsp sugar
2 ounces bittersweet chocolate, roughly chopped
1 tsp shortening

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees.  Mix butter, sugar and almond extract thoroughly using an electric mixer.  Gradually blend in flour and corn starch.  Add cherries and chocolate.  Form into 1-inch balls and place on parchment lined baking sheet.  Place sugar onto plate and roll ball until covered.  Gently press dough down with bottom of drinking glass to flatten.  Bake for 20 - 30 minutes or until bottom begins to brown.  Cool five minutes then remove to cooling rack.  TO MAKE THE DRIZZLE: Place 2 ounces chocolate and shortening into a small resealable freezer bag.  Microwave on high 30 seconds, until chocolate is melted.  Snip off corner and drizzle over cookies.
