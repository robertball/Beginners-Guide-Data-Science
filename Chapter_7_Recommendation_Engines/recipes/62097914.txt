Title:
Spicy Cheesy Refried beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon chopped garlic
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 16oz can spicy fat free refried beans
4 oz cream cheese
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Melt butter in saucepan and saute garlic and onions, 2-3 minutes or till translucent. Add refried beans and mixthrough till hot. Remove from heat and mix in cream cheese. Spread in an ovenproof pie plate or similar sized dish. Top with cheddar cheese and bake for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Challengergurl
Description: 
Refried beans spiced up with garlic, onions and cream cheese. I serve this as a side dish when we have tacos.
