Title:
Banana-Cinnamon Spice Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980461.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large banana, sliced
1 (6 ounce) ready-to-use reduced fat graham cracker crumb crust
1 1/2 cups cold fat-free milk
2 pkg. (4 serving size)  JELL-O White Chocolate Flavor Fat Free Sugar Free Instant Reduced Calorie Pudding & Pie Filling
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 (8 ounce) tub COOL WHIP FREE Whipped Topping, thawed

Directions:
Arrange banana slices in single layer on bottom of crust.
Pour milk into large bowl. Add dry pudding mixes and cinnamon. Beat with wire whisk 2 minutes. Gently stir in whipped topping. Spoon over bananas in crust.
Refrigerate 4 hours or until set. Store leftover pie in refrigerator.

This easy-to-make pie, made with better-for-you products, not only tastes great but can also fit into your healthful eating plan.
