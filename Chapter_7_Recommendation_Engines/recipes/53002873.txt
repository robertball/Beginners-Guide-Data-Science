Title:
Sweet Red Onion Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium red sweet onions, halved and thinly sliced
3 TBS lemon juice
1 TBS extra virgin olive oil
2 tsp honey
1/4 tsp Kosher salt
1/8 tsp  cayenne pepper

Directions:
Place onions in a large bowl. In a small bowl, combine the lemon juice, olive oil, honey and cayenne pepper; stir until mixed well. Pour over onions.  Cover and refrigerate for at least 3 hours.
Drain and discard liquid from onions before serving.

Good to to use as a condiment on hamburgers and hot dogs. It adds a special zip!                                          -Modified from: Crisp Onion Relish
