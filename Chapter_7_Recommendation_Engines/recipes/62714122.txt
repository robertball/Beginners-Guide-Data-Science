Title:
Orange Cinnamon Tofu Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C orange juice
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. sugar
1/4 (1-pound) package firm tofu, sliced into 4 thin strips
4 slices whole wheat bread

Directions:
1. Combine the orange juice, cinnamon, and sugar in medium saucepan over medium heat.
2. When the mixture begins to bubble, add the strips of tofu.  Cook for 3 minutes.
3. While the tofu is heating, toast the bread.
4. Remove the tofu from the saucepan and lay a few strips on each slice of toast.  Drizzle with juice from the pan.
