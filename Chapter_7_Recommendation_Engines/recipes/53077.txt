Title:
White Russian ..Because Joe Perry Drank Them! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/937811.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes
1 fluid ounce coffee flavored liqueur
1 fluid ounce vodka
1 fluid ounce heavy cream

Directions:
Place the ice cubes in a highball glass, or one of similar size. Pour the coffee, vodka and cream over the ice. Stir and serve. No garnishes here.

Submitter: 
Patricia M
Description: 
I wanted to drink a White Russian after I read that Joe Perry from Aerosmith drank them for breakfast in his wild days! Although I'm not the coffee flavor lover, I tried them and they were good!! Easy to make..perfect for after work...relaxing...listening to Aerosmith!!
