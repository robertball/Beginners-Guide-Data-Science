Title:
Vegan Date, Walnut, Zucchini Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1118135.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cashew nut flour
1/4 cup flax seed flour
2 T Tapioca starch
2 T coconut flour
1 T cinnamon
1/2 t salt
1 t baking soda
1 dropper full stevia
2 T melted coconut oil
1 cup chopped dates
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup shredded zucchini
8 oz water
4 T chia seeds
1/3 cup apple sauce
paper muffin cups

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Soak the Chia seeds in the water until it forms a thick paste. Takes about 5 to 10 minutes
Mix dry ingredients
Heat the coconut oil to liquid (stove or microwave)
Shred the zucchini
Chop the dates (if you bought whole ones, be sure to remove the seed)
Mix wet ingredients and nuts and dates.
Mix together.
Spoon into paper muffin cups in a muffin pan.
Cook, test for done with a toothpick. Depending on how wet the batter is, it may take another 5 minutes to cook.
Cool on a rack for 10 minutes before removing from the muffin pan. Ready to eat 5 minutes after that.

Submitter: 
DoryGuy
Description: 
I worked this recipe up after we had dairy, egg, almond, and grain allergies. They are delicious! I looked at other basic muffin recipes and adjusted the ingredients to fit our dietary needs and then experimented with them until I got it just right.
