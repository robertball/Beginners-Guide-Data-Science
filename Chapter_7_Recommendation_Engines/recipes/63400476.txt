Title:
Salmon with Dill Couscous and Spicy Kale (from RR) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Two fillets of salmon (pref. no skin)
1 red onion
1 bunch (3 - 4 large handfuls) of kale, stemmed and chopped
2 tablespoons of EVOO
1 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
1 can of chicken stock
1 cup of couscous
1 teaspoon of Old Bay seasoning
salt and pepper
2 - 3 tablespoons of butter
1/4 cup of fresh dill, chopped
1/2 cucumber, diced
4 cloves of garlic, diced
3 tablespoons of red wine vinegar
1/2 tablespoon of Koop's Horseradish Mustard

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400.
Make quick marinade for fish, 2 of the garlic cloves diced, sprinkle Old Bay, 1/4 of the dill.
Whisk the red wine vinegar, some EVOO, salt and pepper, garlic powder, mustard and the juice of half a lemon and pour over fillets (don't stick in the fridge, let it sit room temperature)
Heat EVOO in large skillet with a lid, cook onions and 1/2 the garlic for 3 - 4 minutes, then add the red pepper flakes.
Add the kale, some salt and pepper, and then pour 1/2 the chicken stock to wilt and simmer on low.
Bake salmon at 400 while the kale simmers.
Heat the remaining chicken stock and 2 pats of butter until boiling, turn off the heat and add the couscous, dill and garlic, stir and close the lid of the pot.
Chop the cucumber.
Once the fish is cooked to 130 temp, remove from oven.
Layer the kale, then couscous, then cucumber, then salmon and pour the remaining marinade over all the layers.
