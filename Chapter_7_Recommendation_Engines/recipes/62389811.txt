Title:
Rouladen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb thinly sliced round steak
6 - 8 slices of uncooked bacon
dried minced onion
vegetable oil

Directions:
Cut round steak into 2 1/2" strips. If too thick may be pounded flat.
Place a slice of uncooked bacon on a strip of beef. sprinkle with minced onion.
Roll and secure with 2 toothpicks. Prepare all meat rolls.
Brown in vegetable oil. Place in oven dish with small amount of water. Cover and bake in 350Â° oven for 3 hours.

Submitter: 
Lynn Pennec
Description: 
Our simple version of a German dish handed down from my mother's family.
