Title:
Gluten Free Fluffy Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cup milk
1/4 cup white vinegar
2 cups Gluten Free all-purpose flour
1 1/4 teaspoon Xanthan Gum
1/4 cup white sugar
2 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
1/4 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Combine milk with vinegar in a medium bowl and set aside for 5 minutes to "sour".
Combine flour, xanthan gum, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large mixing bowl. Whisk egg and butter into "soured" milk. Pour the flour mixture into the wet ingredients and whisk until lumps are gone.
Heat a griddle to 300Â°. Pour 1/4 cupfuls of batter onto the griddle, and cook 3 minutes. Flip with a spatula, and cook another 3 minutes.
