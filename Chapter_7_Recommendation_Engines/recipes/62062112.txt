Title:
baked boursin and tomato dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint cherry tomatoes, quartered
4 Tablespoons chopped fresh basil, divided
2-5 1/2 ounce containers boursin herb cheese
6-8 Tablespoons milk

Directions:
Spread tomatoes over the bottom of a shallow baking dish.
Sprinkle with 2 Tablespoons basil.  Combine cheese with enough milk to form a smooth  sauce.
Pour mixture over tomatoes, covering completely.  Sprinkle with remaining chopped basil.
Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 20-25 minutes.  Serve hot with water crackers or french bread.
This can be assembled 1 day ahead and brought to room temperature before baking.

Submitter: 
cookin'mama
Description: 
This appetizer dip is quick and easy, but always a hit! I am  a little embarrassed to pass on the recipe because people are always stunned when they find out how easy it is.  For variety, add some canned artichoke hearts.
