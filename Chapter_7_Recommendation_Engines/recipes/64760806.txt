Title:
Sweet Carrot Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
2 cups vinegar
2 cups water
Sprinkle cinnamon and cloves
Whole cloves
Carrots, peeled and sliced into spears, enough to fill dutch oven

Directions:
Put carrots in dutch oven. Boil 8-9 min.
Meanwhile, boil sugar, vinegar and water together until sugar dissolved.
Put a clove or two in each jar. Pack carrots into jars.
Pour syrup over carrots.
Seal
Makes ~ 5 or 6 pints

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
One of my Grandma's recipes that was my little bro's favorite
