Title:
Pork Tenderloin Slow Cooker (Sweet) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065537.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pound Pork Tenderloin
1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
1 TBS Yellow Mustard
2 TBS maple syrup
2 TBS olive oil
2 TBS Diced dried onions
1 1/2 TSP Garlic Powder

Directions:
Mix ingredients above. Pour over Tenderloin in the crock pot and cook on low for 6 hours.
