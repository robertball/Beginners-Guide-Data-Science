Title:
Chicken Pesto Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large whole skinless, boneless chicken breast
1 T. olive oil
¼ c. pine nuts
1 clove garlic, minced
¼ tsp. salt
1 c. mayo or to taste
2 T. pesto
12 black olives, halved
1 c. Romaine, cut in thin strips

Directions:
Toss the chicken into a pot of boiling water, remove it from the heat, cover the pot and let sit about 20 minutes or until cooked.
Heat oil in small skillet.  Add pine nuts, garlic, and salt, and stir-cook until nuts just begin to brown.  Be careful you don't burn them.  Remove pan from heat.
Cut the chicken into ½ inch squares.  In a large bowl, mix the mayo with the pesto.  Stir in the chicken, olives, and nut mixture.  Just before serving, stir in the lettuce strips.
