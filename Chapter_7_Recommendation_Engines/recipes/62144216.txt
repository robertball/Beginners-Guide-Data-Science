Title:
Garlic Shrimp Scampi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg angel hair
1 lb large shrimp, pre-cooked
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup dry white wine
1/2 cup butter
2 cloves garlic
1/2 can (10.35 oz) stewed tomatoes, chopped
1/4 cup finely chopped yellow onion
1/4 cup scallions
3 medium sized white mushrooms, sliced
1/2 of a lemon, if desired
2 tablespoons parsley
salt/pepper, to taste
2 tablespoon flour, if desired for thicker broth

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package
In medium skillet or frying pan melt butter
Add onion, scallion, mushrooms and garlic, saute until clear
Add chicken broth, wine, parsley and stewed tomatoes
Bring to a boil
Stir, reduce heat, cover and simmer for 10 minutes
Meanwhile, Mix shrimp and flour in bag and mix, if desired
Add shrimp to scampi, cook on low until desired tenderness
Pour sauce and shrimp over angel hair
Squeeze lemon juice over pasta, if desired
Serve with salt and pepper, as desired

Description: 
This dish is easy to make and quick! The more garlic the better! I make this recipe at least once a week! Serve with garlic bread and a salad for a complete meal :)
