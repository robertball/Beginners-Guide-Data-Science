Title:
Crab stuffed mushrooms with a kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large packages white stuffing mushrooms, cleaned and stems removed
1 large can of lump crab or crab claws
1/2 cup jalapeno peppers, minced
1 garlic clove, minced
1 cup pepper jack cheese, grated
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp. hot sauce
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
On a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper, place mushroom caps
Mix together the rest of the ingredients and place a heaping tablespoon into each mushroom cap
Bake caps for 30 minutes.
Serve immediately

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
These moist, juicy mushrooms are stuffed with crabmeat.  The pepper jack cheese and hot sauce give them a little kick.  They have no bread crumbs and can be made ahead of time.
