Title:
BUSH'S® Chili-Cheese Game Day Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983122.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package reduced-fat cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup fresh chunky salsa (from the deli)
1 cup reduced fat shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
1 (16 ounce) can BUSH'S® Mild or Medium Chili Beans, undrained
1/3 cup sliced green onion
Baked, low carb tortilla chips

Directions:
Combine softened cream cheese with salsa and spread in bottom of 9-inch microwaveable pie plate. Sprinkle with 1/2 cup cheese. Spoon chili beans evenly over shredded cheese. Top with remaining cheese and green onions.
Cover and vent with plastic wrap and microwave for 2 minutes on medium high power, rotate dish and repeat.
Remove wrap and microwave on high for 1 minute more or until mixture is thoroughly heated and cheese is melted.
Let cool slightly and serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
BUSH'S® Beans
Description: 
A hearty dip with chili beans that is perfect for game day parties or tailgates!
