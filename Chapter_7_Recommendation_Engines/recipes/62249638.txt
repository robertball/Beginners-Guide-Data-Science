Title:
asparagus pesto lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c. flour
3 1/2 c. milk, divided
6 T. pesto
2 T. grated parmesan
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 tsp. olive oil
1 1/4 # asparagus spears, tips cut off and reserved, spears trimmed and chopped into 1/4-inch pieces
1 clove garlic, minced
16 no-cook lasagna noodles (9 oz)
2 c. shredded Fontina or mozzarella, divided

Directions:
Whisk flour and 1/2 c. milk in saucepan until smooth.  Gradually whisk in remaining milk.  Bring to a boil over medium heat, whisking constantly, and boil one minute or till thickened.  Remove from heat; stir in pesto, Parmesan, salt and pepper.  Set aside one cup of sauce for later.
In a large skillet, cook asparagus (not tips) in hot oil, stirring often, 5 minutes or till tender.  Add garlic; cook, stirring, 1 minute, and season with salt and pepper.  Remove from heat.
Preheat oven to 350F.  Coat a 13x9-inch baking dish with cooking spray.  Place layer of noodles in dish, overlapping slightly.  Layer with half of cooked asparagus and 3/4 c. Fontina and half of sauce.  Add another layer of pasta, remaining sauce, remaining asparagus, and 3/4 c. Fontina.  Top with layer of noodles, then with reserved 1 c. of white sauce.  Arrange reserved asparagus tips over top and sprinkle with remaining Fontina.
Bake uncovered 35 to 40 minutes or until golden. Let stand 10 minutes.
