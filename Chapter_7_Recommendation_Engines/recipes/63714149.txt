Title:
Raspberry Applesauce Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1031981.jpg
Ingredients:
Filling:
1 C. Fresh raspberries
1 C.  Applesauce
1/2 C. Oat bran
1/2 Granulated sugar
Topping:
1 1/2 C. Quick oats
1/2 C. Butter
1 C. Brown sugar
1/2 C. All purpose flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Grease a 13" by 9" by 2" baking pan and set aside
Mix the first four ingredients together and set aside
Combine quick oats,  butter and sugar together with a pastry blender and cut butter into fine pieces blending with flour and oats until mixture is crumbly
Spread half of the mixture in the bottom of greased pan and bake for 20 minutes until crumbs are lightly browned
Spread filling onto layer of cooled crust and put remaining crumbs on top of filing and bake for another 20 minutes until topping is browned lightly

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A sweet raspberry filling surrounded by oats makes a good snack for tea time.
