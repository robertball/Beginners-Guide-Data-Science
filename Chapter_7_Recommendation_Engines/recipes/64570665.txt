Title:
Skinny White Chocolate Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3534476.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbs Sugar Free Torani White Chocolate Syrup
1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
1 pkt Truvia (optional)
1 cup coffee

Directions:
Heat milk and sweetener (if using) to a low simmer.  Froth  hot milk (I used an immersion blender).
Place White Chocolate Syrup in mug.
Top with hot milk, holding froth back with a spoon.
Brew coffee over syrup.
Pour froth over coffee.
Note:
If using Ninja Coffee Bar, pour frothed milk over syrup, choose Cup Size, Specialty Brew.

Submitter: 
Yoly
Description: 
I made this beautifully layered coffee drink in my Ninja Coffee Bar. You can also achieve the same effect by slowly pouring the froth over the coffee. Plus it's 'Skinny'!
