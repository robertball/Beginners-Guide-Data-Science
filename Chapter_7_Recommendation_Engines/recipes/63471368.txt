Title:
Amazing No Cook Spinach Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 shallots
8 cloves garlic, or more to taste
1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1 can (8 oz)water chestnuts, drained and chopped
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
1 (8 ounce) package shredded Parmesan cheese
1 (8 ounce) package reduced-fat cream cheese, softened
1 1/2 (12 ounces total) containers reduced-fat sour cream

Directions:
Blend onion and garlic in a food processor until finely chopped. Add artichoke hearts and water chestnuts; process until chopped. Add spinach, Parmesan cheese,  process until well incorporated. Transfer mixture to a nonmetallic bowl.
Blend cream cheese and sour cream in the food processor until smooth; stir into artichoke mixture.

Submitter: 
cookbookangie
Description: 
A no-cook version of artichoke dip is quick and easy to prepare with a food processor. Combine  shallots, canned artichoke hearts, spinach, and sour cream for a creamy and flavorful dip.Modified from: Amazing No Cook Spinach Artichoke Dip
