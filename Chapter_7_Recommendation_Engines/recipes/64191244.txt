Title:
Creole Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1535799.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds tilapia / swai / cod fillets
Juice of 2 limes; divided use
Creole seasoning to taste
1 large onion sliced into strips
4 bell peppers (multiple colors) sliced into strips
4-6 diced roma tomatoes
1 jalapeno, chopped
2 cloves garlic, diced
1/3 bunch fresh cilantro
1/4 cup olive oil; divided use
1/2 cup shredded Mexican style cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees Fahrenheit.
Layer bell peppers, jalapeno pepper, tomatoes, garlic, cilantro and onion into a baking dish. Drizzle with half the olive oil. Add juice of one lime. Mix well.
Arrange fish fillets on top of the vegetable mix. Sprinkle fish with creole seasoning to taste.
Top all with the shredded cheese and remaining juice of a lime.
Bake covered for 40 minutes or until fish flakes with a fork.

Submitter: 
Cheri
Description: 
A great way to prepare any white fish fillets you may have on hand. A side of Mexican style rice is a wonderful enhancement to the meal.
