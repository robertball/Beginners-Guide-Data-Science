Title:
Twice Baked Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large russet potatoes
3 tbsp butter
2 tbsp minced green onion
salt, pepper, paprika to taste
1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
1/2 cup milk
1 egg yolk

Directions:
Bake at 400 degrees F. for an hour to cook potatoes.  Lay potato on its side and cut off top third of potato.  Carefully scoop out potato and add top third of potato to bowl.  Flake apart with a fork; add butter and other ingredients.  Put top cut-off potato piece into bottom of larger scooped out skin. Place mixture inside. Make fork marks on top and drizzle a small amount of butter. Put potatoes back into the oven for 20-30 to brown after stuffing.
