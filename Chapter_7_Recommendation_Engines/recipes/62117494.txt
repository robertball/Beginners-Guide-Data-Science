Title:
Simple Chicken Korma Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Chicken Breasts (Skinless and Boneless)
1 Small Onion
50g Margarine
1 Chicken Stock Cube (e.g. Oxo)
1 Clove of Garlic
1 Tablespoon Creames Coconut
2-4 Teaspoons Curry Powder
Half a Small Pot Natural Yoghurt

Directions:
Melt butter in a large saucepan until sizzling and add the chopped chicken.
Cook until brown and no pink is showing on the outside on medium heat.
Chop onions and garlic and add to saucepan, cook until soft, around 5 mins.
Make stoch with boiling water as directed on packaging.
Mix all other ingredients into the saucepan, EXCEPT yoghurt, and bring to the boil.
Turn down heat until simmering, place lid on top and leave for 30 mins.
Turn off the heat and stir in yoghurt.
Mix well and enjoy!
