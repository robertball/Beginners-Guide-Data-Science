Title:
Turkey Burgers with flare Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5lbs Ground turkey
3 Tbs Yoshidas marinade
1/4 cup Kansas City Styke bbq sauce
2 Tbs worcestershire sauce
2 Tbs grill seasoning
2 Tbs chopped garlic
1 Tsp salt
1/4 cup diced bell pepper
1/4 cup diced onion
1/2 cup chopped mushrooms

Directions:
In a small pan saute onions and peppers until almost soft, add mushrooms and cook 2 mins. Remove from heat and let cool. In a small bowl mix together; Yoshidas maranade,bbq sauce,worcestershire sauce,grill seasoning, chopped garlic and salt. In a large bowl place ground turkey and add mixture mix well. Add cooked onions,peppers and mushrooms mix together.
Form into patties and refrigerate 30 minutes. Cook on indoor or outdoor gril until done. Serve on a kaiser roll with all the fixings.

Submitter:

Description: 
A moist delicious way to have bugers that are low fat.These burgers have so much flavor you forget the beef!
