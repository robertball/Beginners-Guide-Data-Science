Title:
Yakisoba Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup water
1/4 cup rice wine vinegar
1/4 cup sugar
2 tablespoon lemon juice
several dashes worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon grated ginger
1 1/2 teaspoon crushed garlic
1/2 teaspoon sesame oil

Directions:
Combine ingredients in sauce pan. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 10 min or until of desired consistancy.
Add sauce to stir fried noodles and veggies, tofu optional.
