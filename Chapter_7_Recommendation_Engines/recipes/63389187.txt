Title:
Lemony Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp olive oil
3 tsp dried thyme
2 Tbsp capers, drained
6 garlic cloves
6 bone-in, skin-on chicken breasts
2 lemons

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees
Pour oil into baking dish large enough to hold chicken pieces snugly.  Move the dish around to coat the bottom.
Sprinkle bottom of dish with 1 tsp of thyme.
Evenly scatter with capers.
Slice each garlic clove in half and scatter in dish.
Place chicken, skin side down, in dish.
Slice lemons into quarters then squeeze the juice over chicken.  Tuck remaining lemon pieces around chicken.
Sprinkle chicken with 2 tsp thyme.
Roast, uncovered, 25 minutes.
Turn chicken, scatter capers and tuck garlic under chicken.
Continue to roast, uncovered, until internal temperature of 165 degrees is reached.  (about 20 minutes)
Remove to a plate.  Mash garlic into the pan juices and spoon over the chicken.

Submitter: 
Leanne Cooke
Description: 
A really easy and tasty way to roast those chicken breasts you have.
