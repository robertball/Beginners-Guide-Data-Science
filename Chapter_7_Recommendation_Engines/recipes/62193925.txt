Title:
Bacon Wrapped Water Chestnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Brown Sugar
3 Tablespoons Ketchup
1 teaspoon Mustard
2 teaspoons Soy Sauce
2 teaspoons Worchestire Sauce
2 cans Whole Water Chestnuts
1 lb of Bacon

Directions:
Combine first five ingredients in a sauce pan and simmer 5 minutes. Wrap 1/3 slice of bacon around the water chestnuts and secure with a tooth pick. Place in a shallow pan and bake at 325 degrees for 1 hour or until Bacon is cooked. Place Bacon wrapped chestnuts in a chaffing dish and pour sauce over them.

Submitter: 
Jan Sanders
Description: 
This appetizer will truley be a hit at any party. It is a must have for the holiday get togethers.
