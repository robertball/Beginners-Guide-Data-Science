Title:
Bran Muffins Monson Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water, boiling
2 cups dates, chopped
5 teaspoons baking soda
1 cup shortening
2 cups sugar
4 eggs
1 quart buttermilk
4 cups Bran BudsÂ®
2 cups Bran Flakes
5 cups flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup nuts, chopped

Directions:
In small bowl, pour boiling water over chopped dates.  When cool add soda.
In large bowl, cream together shortening, sugar, eggs.  Add buttermilk, Bran Buds, Bran Flakes, flour, salt, and nuts.
Keep in covered container in refrigerator.  When using DO NOT stir.  Spoon into greased or lined muffin tins.
Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Karen Monson
Description: 
These are the ones my mom used to make.  So moist and sweet!  Love, Love, Love!  I can't eat another bran muffin.
