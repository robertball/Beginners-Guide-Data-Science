Title:
Ultimate Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups diced potatoes
4 stalks celery, diced
2 cup chopped onion
8 cup chicken broth made rich, divided
4 cups half-and-half cream
8 tablespoons butter, melted
8 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 clove garlic chopped
1 15-1/4 oz can sweet corn
2 cup baby carrots

Directions:
In a large pot, bring potatoes, celery, onion and carrots to a boil in 4 cups of broth.  Cook until potatoes are tender, 15 minutes.
Combine 4 cups broth and half-and-half in pot.  In a bowl, combine melted butter and flour.  Stir into half-and-half  mixture over medium heat.  Stir until thickened.  Stir in reserved vegetables, remaining broth, bouillon and pepper.  Heat through and serve.
