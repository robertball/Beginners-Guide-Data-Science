Title:
Grandma Drexler's Bavarian Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*1/2 chopped onion (coarse)
1 clove garlic (minced)
*1 tablespoon butter
*1 teaspoon paprika
*1/2 teaspoon salt
*dash of basil,dill seed,ground mustard seed
*Aprox. 1 tablespoon of marjorum (not ground)
*2 1/2 cup water
1 pound pork (chopped into 1" cubes)
1 cup saurkraut
3/4 cup sourcream

Directions:
In a large pot on medium high heat
throw in the butter, onions and saute until onions are golden brown.
Throw in your pork and spices
Cook until meat is fully cooked...
Add 1/2 of your water,then all of your sourkraut
Fold in your sourcream
Bring to a boil
When everything begins boiling add the rest of your water
Again, bring to a boil for about 15 minutes
If the thickness is not to your liking you can sift in flour until the desired thickness is reached
Serve hot over dumpling or whatever you feel like eating with(dumplings recommended, ooh I should put my dumpling recipe on here...maybe)
