Title:
Pineapple Upside Down Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/560188.jpg
Ingredients:
1(18 ounce)box yellow cake mix
3 eggs
1 1/3 c. water
1 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. butter
1 (20 ounce) can sliced pineapples (need 12 pineapples, 10 come in a can but cut 2 in half)
jar of maraschino cherries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325. Prepare cake, follow instructions on cake box.  Set Aside.
Melt butter and pour into the bottom of a cake pan(oblong cake pan).
Sprinkle brown sugar over the butter the butter
Place pan in oven and heat slowly (cook until butter and sugar melt into a liquid then remove from oven)
Evenly space the pineapple slices around the bottom of pan in the heated sugar and butter.
Then place the cherries in the middle of pineapples slices.
Pour cake batter on top of pineapples and cherries.
Bake for 45-50 minutes. Make sure cake is completely done and it a pretty dark golden brown.
When the cake is down, cut around the edges so the cake want stick, place a cookie sheet( preferably one that covers the cake pan)over the cake pan and turn it upside down and carefully remove from the pan.
