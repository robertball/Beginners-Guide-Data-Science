Title:
Mint Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2c sugar
12 Tbsp lemon juice
20 fresh mint leaves
2 2/3c Water

Directions:
1- Fine mince the mint leaves, or you may pulverize them in a mortar and pestal
2- Combine the mint with the sugar and lime juice, untill you have formed an even mixture
3- combine with water (you could use club soda too, if you like)in a pitcher.
4- hold a paper towel over your glass before you pour, to strain out the leaves.

Submitter:

Description: 
Easy, and very wonderful drink. I have enormous mint plants in my yard, so i make it often.
