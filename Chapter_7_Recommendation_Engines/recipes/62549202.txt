Title:
Slow-Simmered Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds flank steak
1 onion
3-5 cloves of garlic
1 cup red wine
1/2 cup water
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp salt
1 can golden mushroom condensed soup

Directions:
Cut onion in half and use the flat edge of a knife and crush garlic cloves; peel off skin.  Place onion and whole garlic cloves into slow cooker.
Add red wine and water, place steak on top.
Pour soup over top of steak and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Cover and cook on how for 8 hours or high for 5 hours.
When steak is finished cooking remove to plate.  with a wire strainer, strain gravy into gravy boat.
Discard onion and garlic.
