Title:
Cajun Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Beef
1/2 onion, chopped
1/2 bell pepper, chopped
3 Tbsp Toni's Cajun Seasoning
2 Tbsp Garlic Salt
1 Tbsp Lemon Pepper
2 Pkgs Jiffy Cornbread
1 can cream corn
1 Jalapeno, seeds removed and chopped
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown beef with onions and green peppers.  Add cajun seasoning, garlic salt, and lemon pepper to mix.  Drain well.
Mix cornbread according to package directions.  Mix in can of cream corn and jalapenos.
Spray 9"x13" pan with non-stick spray.  Spread 1/2 of cornbread mixture.  Layer meat mixture and top with cheddar cheese.  Spread last of cornbread mixture on top.
Bake according to cornbread package directions plus an extra 5-10 minutes.
