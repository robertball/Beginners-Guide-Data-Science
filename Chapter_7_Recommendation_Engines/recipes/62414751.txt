Title:
Spicy Asian Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 crowns of broccoli, roughly chopped
1/4 a small sweet onion, finely diced
5 cloves of garlic, finely diced
1/4 cup oyster sauce
2 tbsp of Hoisin sauce
1 tbsp of sriracha sauce (chili paste)
1 tsp of freshly ground pepper
2 tbsp of vegetable oil

Directions:
Heat up a large skillet to medium heat and add the oil.
Add onions, garlic, broccoli and pepper and turn heat to medium low and cover.
Once the broccoli and onions start to soften add the sauces and mix well. Turn heat to low and let simmer for a couple minutes.

Submitter: 
Raquel Teixeira
Description: 
A tasty side Asian dish that can be whipped up last minute which is what I did when creating it.
