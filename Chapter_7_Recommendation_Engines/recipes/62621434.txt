Title:
Mom's Awesome Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 avocadoes
1/3 cup chopped grape tomatoes
1/3 cup chopped red onions
1 Tbsp. Key Lime juice
2 Tbsp. sour cream
a dash of salt
a dash of black pepper

Directions:
1. scoop out insides of avocadoes and mash with a fork in a medium sized mixing bowl
2. chop up onions and grape tomatoes and add to bowl; mix well
3. measure out sour cream and Key Lime juice and add to bowl
4. add salt and black pepper to taste
5. chill for 20 minutes before serving

Description: 
Standard guacamole with a zing of Key Lime
