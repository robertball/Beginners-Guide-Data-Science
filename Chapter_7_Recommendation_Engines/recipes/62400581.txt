Title:
Spiral Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 soft cracker bread (15 inch circle/17 oz. package)
4 oz. package herb & garlic spreadable cheese
8 oz. thinly sliced smoked turkey
1 large tomato; thinly sliced
6 oz. thinly sliced swiss cheese
1/2 cup alfalfa sprouts
about 18 large spinach leaves

Directions:
Cover bread evenly with herb & garlic cheese.
Layer turkey, tomato, swiss cheese, alfalfa sprouts and spinach on bread.
Roll up bread
Trim each end with serrated knife; then cut into 1 inch slices.

Submitter: 
GevingCook
Description: 
This sandwich makes a great appetizer for many people or a meal for about 4 people.  It's one of our favorite road trip sandwiches.  ~ Gina
