Title:
Potato Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bacon
2 peeled and diced potatoes
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon seasoning salt
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
6 eggs, beaten
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown. Remove bacon slices, reserving grease. Crumble bacon and set aside.
Add potatoes to bacon grease and season with garlic powder, seasoned salt and black pepper. Cook until potatoes are soft.
When potatoes are tender, Pour eggs over potatoes and cook until firm. Spread with cheese and cover with lid until melted.add crumbled bacon.
For variation add onions and peppers, cook them with the diced potatoes.
