Title:
Paperbag Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/316370.jpg
Ingredients:
9-inch unbaked pie shell
Filling:
5-6 apples (see note below)
juice of 1 lemon
2 tbsp.  flour
1/2  cup of sugar
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
Topping:
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup soft butter

Directions:
Heat oven to 425 degrees.
Peel and cut apples into 3/4 inch chunks or small slices
Combine apples with other filling ingredients in a bowl and toss well, then put into pie shell.
Combine topping mix until crumbly and pile on top of the apples.
Put pie into brown paper bag and staple shut.  Put on a metal cookie sheet and put into oven (rack on bottom 1/3 of oven.)  Bake for one hour.  When you remove pie from oven,  slit bag open carefully so the stem doesn't burn you.

Submitter: 
food-dancer
Description: 
A luscious apple pie with a sweet, crispy topping.  It's baked in a brown, paper bag on a cookie sheet.
