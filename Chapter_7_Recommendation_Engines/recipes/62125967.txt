Title:
Momma's Meatloaf Meal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1/2 lb. ground pork
1/2 cup plain bread crumbs
1 egg
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
1 tsp. minced onion
1 can of tomato soup; divided in half
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. parsley flakes

Directions:
Preheat oven at 350 degrees.
Mix all ingredients together very well with bare hands.
Combine well.
Shape into a loaf and place in glass baking dish.
Spread remaining half can of soup over the top.
Bake for 1.5 hours.

Submitter: 
Samantha Kristina
Description: 
My mother's recipe for meatloaf is the only one I can find that is loved across the board.
