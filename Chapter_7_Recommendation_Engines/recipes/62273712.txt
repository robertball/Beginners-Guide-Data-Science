Title:
Olive Burger Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/488724.jpg
Ingredients:
12 Tbl sliced olives(green w/pimentos)
8 Tbl mayo
*3 Tbl olive juice(see footnote)

Directions:
Stir together all ingredients in a small bowl. Adjust to taste.  Refrigerate until ready to serve on your favorite burgers. The "juice" is definitely optional, my footnote disappeared.

Submitter: 
linda2d
Description: 
When I was young my grandma made this every time we had burgers. My whole family loves it even though none of us agree on the ratio of mayo to olives. It seems to be unique to MI but we never knew that.
