Title:
Aaron's Missouri Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1Lb Lean Ground Beef
2tsp Onion Powder
1/4 cup Honey Mustard or Horseradish Mustard
1tsp Garlic Powder
2tsp Crushed Red Pepper
1/2tsp Salt "optional"
1/4 cup Brown Sugar
2Tbs Olive Oil
4 slices Swiss or American Cheese "optional"
4 Hamburger Buns

Directions:
Preheat Large skillet on medium heat with 1 Tbs olive oil
In a large bowl, combine ground beef, onion powder, honey mustard or horseradish mustard, garlic powder, crushed red pepper, salt, brown sugar until well mixed. Shape into 1/4 lb patties.
Cook patties turning about every 2 minutes until desired level of cooking is achieved, just before removing from pan top with your favorite cheese.
