Title:
Chicken Florentine Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of Progresso Fat Free Chicken Broth
1/2 an onion, chopped small
2 tbs minced garlic
1 bay leaf
2 teaspoons dried parsley
1 packet Sazon goya sin achiote
1 cup grape tomatoes, quartered
1/2 cup parboiled brown rice
1 can canneloni beans
1 can chic peas
2 cups chopped spinach
1 skinless chicken breast (whole)
1/2 teaspoon celery seed
3 peppercorns
2 tbs olive oil

Directions:
1. Heat olive oil over medium heat in a large sauce pot
2. Sear both sides of chicken breast until browned then remove it from the pot
3. Reduce heat to low and saute onions, garlic a pinch of salt, parsley, bay leaf, sazon and celery seed  until onions are soft and fragrant.
4. Add tomatoes to the pot and saute about 2 minutes
Add broth and turn heat to high.
5. Return chicken to pot and add beans, chick peas, rice and pepper corns
6. Bring to a boil and let boil, uncovered for 5 minutes then reduce to a simmer and cover 30 minutes.
7. Turn off heat and remove chicken from pot and shred before returning it to the pot.
