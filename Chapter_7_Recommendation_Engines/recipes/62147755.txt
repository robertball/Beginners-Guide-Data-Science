Title:
Broccoli Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cooking spray
1 c. onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
16 oz package of frozen broccoli
2 c. yellow or white potatoes, peeled and diced
28 oz. chicken broth
1 tsp seasoned salt (Lawryâ€™s)
1 tsp Italian seasoning (Mrs. Dash)
1 bay leaf
8 oz. Velveeta, cubed
2/3 c milk
1 T lemon juice

Directions:
Heat large pot with cooking spray.
Add onion and cook 5 minutes.
Add garlic and cook 30 seconds.
Add broccoli, potato, broth, seasoning, and bay. Bring to boil.
Cover and simmer 20 minutes until vegetables are tender.
Discard bay leaf. Put mixture into a blender and process until almost smooth (use immersion blender if you have one)
Return to pot and add cheese and milk. Cook over medium 2 minutes or until cheese melted.
Stir in lemon juice and serve.

Submitter: 
Kristin C
Description: 
Creamy and cheesy - My son always asks for seconds and thirds! I normally serve with a salad or a BLT sandwich.
