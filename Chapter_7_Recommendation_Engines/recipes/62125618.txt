Title:
Caramels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/353787.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C very fine sugar
1 (16 oz.) bottle light Karo corn syrup
1/2 C butter – no subsitutes
1 (12 oz.) can evaporated milk
1 t vanilla

Directions:
Combine sugar, syrup and butter.  Stir over medium heat until bubbly and doesn’t feel grainy.  Add milk drop by drop stirring constantly.  Once can of milk is half empty it can be poured in by a small stream.  Use candy thermometer – 2 marks over “soft ball”.  Remove from heat and add vanilla; stir.  Pour into a buttered 9x13 pan.  Cool completely.  Cut into pieces and wrap in waxed paper.
