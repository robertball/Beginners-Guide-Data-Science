Title:
Tortellini with Fresh Tomato-Basil Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg (20 oz) uncooked refrigerated cheese-filled tortellini
6-8 medium tomatoes, seeded and coarsely chopped
3 TBL snipped fresh basil leaves
1 tsp balsamic vinegar
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
2 TBL olive oil
2 large garlic cloves, pressed
1/4 cup (1 oz) grated fresh Parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Cook tortellini according to package directions in (8 qt) stockpot; drain. Meanwhile, combine tomatoes, basil, vinegar, salt and black pepper in bowl; mix gently.
2. Heat oil over medium heat in same stockpot. Add garlic; stir 1 minute or until garlic is lightly browned and remove from heat. Add tortellini and tomato mixture; toss gently. Spoon pasta into serving bowl; sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
