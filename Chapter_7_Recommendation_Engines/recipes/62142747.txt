Title:
Sweet and Sour Swedish Meatball Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can pinapple chunks, in heavy syrup, drained and syrup reserved.
1 3/4 cups water
3/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup distilled white vinegar
1/4 cup ketchup
2 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup cornstarch
Orange food coloring (optional)

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, combine 1 1/2 cups water, white and brown sugars, vinegar, pineapple syrup, ketchup, soy sauce and Worcestershire sauce. Heat to boil.  Add food coloring, if desired.
Combine 1/4 cup cornstarch and 1/4 cup water, pour slowly into sauce and allow sauce to thicken.

Submitter: 
Lindsey Harbison Neill
Description: 
The perfect sauce for any homemade or store bought Swedish Meatballs.
