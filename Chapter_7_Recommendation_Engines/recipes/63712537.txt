Title:
Almanzo's Fried Apples N Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 apples, peeled, sliced
4 T butter
1 onion, julienned
1/4 c brown sugar
Cinnamon, optional

Directions:
Sliced peeled apples fairly thick.  Melt butter in skillet.  Add apples and julienned onions, throw in brown sugar.  Cook on med high until onions are translucent.  Reduce sauce til slightly caramelized.  Let cool and serve.

Submitter: 
jillbont
Description: 
I loved Little House on the Prairie, and Almanzo's favorite dish was Fried Apples 'N Onions.  Fascinated, I made this recipe up (lol, not complicated) and have loved the combination!
