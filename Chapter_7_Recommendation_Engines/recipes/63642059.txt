Title:
Home Made Veggie Wash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tbsp baking soda
4 Tbsp lemon juice
4 cups of water
1-1/2 tbsp dish soap detergent

Directions:
Mix ingredients in a bowl.
Transfer to a spray bottle.
Spray vegetables and fruits.
Rub thoroughly and let stand for 5 minutes.
Rinse thoroughly and cook or eat treated vegetable or fruit.
