Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, diced
cumin, parsley, poultry seasoning, seasoned salt and pepper
2 tablespoons butter
1 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 onion, finely diced
carrots, finely diced
frozen peas
sliced potatos
celery, finely diced
3/4 c. low sodium chicken broth
1 (10 ounce) package refrigerated biscuit dough, torn into pieces

Directions:
Place the chicken, seasonings, butter, soup, and veges in a slow cooker, and fill with enough low sodium chicken broth to cover.
Cover and cook for 6.5 hours on High. About 3 hours before serving, place the torn biscuit dough in the slow cooker. Cook until the dough is no longer raw in the center.
