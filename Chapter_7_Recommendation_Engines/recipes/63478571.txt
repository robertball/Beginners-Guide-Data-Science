Title:
Hamburger Cassarole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 teaspoon onion powder
3/4 cup BBQ sauce
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1 can pork and beans (remove fat slab from can)
12 oz can (10 count) flaky layer biscuits (not jumbo size)
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Put ground beef in skillet, sprinkle with onion powder and cook meat over medium heat until browned.
Drain fat from skillet and return to stove.
Add BBQ sauce, brown sugar and pork and beans to the skillet and cook together for 10 minutes
While the beef mixture is simmering, preheat oven to 375 degrees and spray a 2 quart pan with cooking spray.
Put meat mixture into baking dish and place biscuits around the edge. Top with shredded cheddar.
Bake at 375 for 25-30 minutes.
