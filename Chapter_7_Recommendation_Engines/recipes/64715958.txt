Title:
Keto Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5744787.jpg
Ingredients:
2.5 cups almond meal
1 tbsp baking powder
1 cup pecans, chopped
1 cup heavy cream, room temperature
2 tbsp ghee, room temperature
3 eggs, room temperature
1 tsp vanilla bean paste

Directions:
Mix together the almond meal, baking powder, and pecans in a large bowl. Set aside.
Whisk together the heavy cream, ghee, eggs, and vanilla paste.
Heat a waffle iron to medium-high.
Mix together the dry and wet ingredients. Allow to rest about 5 minutes. During this time the batter will thicken. This is expected.
Stir the batter and make the waffles according to your machine instructions.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Waffles made with almond meal, heavy cream, and ghee will satisfy your craving for your breakfast favorite while meeting your keto diet requirements.
