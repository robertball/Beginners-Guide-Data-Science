Title:
Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound bacon
4 cups sliced yellow/zucchini squash
1/2 cup chopped onion
Dried Oregano
35(1 sleeve) buttery round crackers, crushed
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
nutmeg
1 egg, beaten
3/4 cup milk
1/4 cup butter, melted
1/2 t. garlic powder
1/2 t. garlic pepper
1 teaspoon salt
ground black pepper to taste
4 tablespoons butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Slice bacon cross ways, fry and drain on paper towel. Place squash and onions in a large bowl. Sprinkle liberally with dried oregano.
In a medium bowl, mix together cracker crumbs and cheese. Stir half of the cracker mixture into the squash and onions along with 1/8 t nutmeg. In a small bowl, mix together egg and milk, then add to squash mixture. Stir in 1/4 cup melted butter, and season with garlic powder, garlic pepper, salt and pepper. Spread into a 7x11 baking dish. Bake 15 minutes. Sprinkle with bacon and remaining cracker mixture, then dot with butter.
Continue baking 10 more minutes, or until lightly browned.

Description: 
Tender squash, gooey cheese and crunchy crackers make this a memorable side dish or a hearty main course. This is a great dish that can be made with low-fat ingredients and is still just as good!Modified from: Yellow Squash Casserole
