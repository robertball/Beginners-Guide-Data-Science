Title:
Ultimate Mint Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 stick unsalted butter
3/4 cups firmly packed light brown sugar
1/2 cup Palm sugar
2 tablespoons almond milk
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
1/4 tsp mint extract
1 large egg
2 cups Flour
1 teaspoon salt
3/4 teaspoon baking soda
1 c. Dark Chocolate chips
3/4 c. Andes Mint chips (or 1 pkg chopped)

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees F.
Combine shortening, sugars, milk and vanilla in large bowl. Beat at medium speed of electric mixer until well blended. Beat in egg and mint extract. Combine flour, salt and baking soda. Mix into butter mixture until just blended. Stir in chocolate & mint chips.
Drop by rounded measuring tablespoonfuls 3 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheet.
Bake 8 to 10 minutes for chewy cookies, or 11 to 13 minutes for crisp cookies. Cool 2 minutes on baking sheet on a cooling rack. Remove cookies to rack to cool completely.

Submitter: 
Rebekah Richards
Description: 
With this versatile recipe, you can make chocolate chip drop cookies, bar cookies, large round cookies, or chocolate drizzled cookies.Modified from: Ultimate Chocolate Chip Cookies
