Title:
Ham Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. center cut smoked ham ground
1 1/2 lb. ground pork
1 c. bread crumbs
2 eggs - well beaten
1 c. milk
1 1/2 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. vinegar
1/2 c. water
1 T. prepared mustard or 2 tsp. dry

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 300.
Mix first 5 ingredients together and form into balls.
Place these in a 9x13 in. pan
Boil sugar, vinegar and water for 5 minutes. Pour this mixture over the ham balls
Cook for 2 1/2 hours at 250-300
