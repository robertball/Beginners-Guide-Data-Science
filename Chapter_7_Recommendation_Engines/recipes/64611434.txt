Title:
Deviled Eggs with a sweet kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 hard-cooked eggs
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup sweet salad cubes
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
1 tsp yellow mustard
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 tsp white pepper
1 pinch paprika, or to taste

Directions:
Slice eggs in half lengthwise and remove yolks; set whites aside. Mash yolks with a fork in a small bowl. Stir in mayonnaise, sweet salad cubes, dry mustard, mustard, salt and white pepper; mix well. Stuff or pipe egg yolk mixture into egg whites. Sprinkle with paprika. Refrigerate for at least an hour.
