Title:
Chicken and noodles paprikash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 garlic cloves minced
2 red peppers cut into 1 inch pieces
1 green pepper cut into 1 inch pieces
1 onion cut in half then sliced
12 ounces egg noodles
salt
2 tablespoons paprika
1 tablespoon flour
1 pound boneless chicken breasts cut into pieces
1 teaspoon chicken boullion
4 ounces sour cream

Directions:
in 2 tablespoons of oil cook the peppers, onion and garlic until tender about 10-15 minutes. Remove vegetables to a bowl.Combine   the flour and paprika coat the chicken. In same skillet heat 1 tablespoon oil cook chicken 3 minutes. add the boullion, 1 cup of water, 3/4 teaspoon salt. Heat to a boil cover and simmer 8-10 minutes.
Cook noodles and drain. stir in sour cream and peppermixture into the chicken and heat through. Serve over noodles
