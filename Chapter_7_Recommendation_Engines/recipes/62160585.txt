Title:
Super Secret Burger N Steak Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Parts Brown Sugar
2 Parts Black Pepper
1 Part White Pepper
1 Part Cumin
1 Part Powdered Mustard
1 Part Thyme
1 Part Kosher Salt

Directions:
All measurements are in volumetric parts.
Mix well and store in an air tight container.
For burgers use 1 Tbs per pound meat plus 1 Tbs Worcestershire sauce.

Submitter: 
noogie01
Description: 
This is my Secret recipe for beef seasoning. I use it in burgers (1 Tbs per pound of beef with 1 Tbs Worcestershire sauce) and as a steak rub. I also use it in beef stew, on roasts, beef brisket, beef-etc.
