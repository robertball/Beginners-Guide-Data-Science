Title:
No Bake Cookies I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/271366.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups white sugar
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup butter
4 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 cup crunchy peanut butter
3 cups quick-cooking oats
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, combine sugar, milk, butter, and cocoa. Bring to a boil, and cook for 1 1/2 minutes. Remove from heat, and stir in peanut butter, oats, and vanilla. Drop by teaspoonfuls into cookie cutters onto wax paper. Let cool until hardened.

Submitter: 
Cooks4Kids
Description: 
These cookies are so quick and easy that my kids love them. I spoon them out into cookie cutters so that they are fun shapes.Modified from: No Bake Cookies I
