Title:
Crunchy Lemon-Dill Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990807.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups FRENCH'S® Original French Fried Onions
1/2 teaspoon dill weed
1/2 teaspoon lemon pepper
6 (4 ounce) fillets salmon
2 tablespoons FRENCH'S® Spicy Brown Mustard

Directions:
Mix French Fried Onions, dill and lemon pepper in plastic bag. Crush with hands or rolling pin.
Brush salmon with mustard. Coat top of salmon pieces with seasoned onion crumbs; press firmly to adhere.
Bake salmon on a foil-lined baking sheet at 350 degrees F for 20 minutes or until opaque in center.

Submitter: 
French's®
Description: 
French Fried Onions, dill, and lemon pepper give these baked salmon fillets a tasty and crunchy coating.
