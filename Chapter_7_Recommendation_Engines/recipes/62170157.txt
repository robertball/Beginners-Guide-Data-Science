Title:
Turkey Pumpkin Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Cups cooked turkey meat-chopped
1 15 oz can refried beans
1 29 oz can pumpkin puree (NOT seasoned)
8 10 inch whole wheat tortillas
1 29 oz can tomato sauce
1-2 chipotle peppers
8 oz mild cheddar cheese-shredded
2 T chicken buillion
4 T taco seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees and spray a 9x13 inch baking dish with cooking spray (Pam).
Add turkey, beans, pumpkin, and taco seasoning in a bowl and mix well.
Fill tortillas and place in baking dish.
Put tomato sauce, chipotle peppers and chicken buillion in a blender and blend until smooth.
Pour sauce over enchiladas and cover with cheese.
Bake for 35-45 minutes or until cheese is melted and the sauce is bubbly.
Serve with sour cream if desired.

Submitter:

Description: 
Use up the leftover turkey in this creamy, gooey and mostly good for you recipe.
