Title:
Left over roast beef over rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of pre-cooked roast beef, cut into cubes
1/2 cup of spanish onion chopped
1 tbsp of butter
1 cup of water
1/2 cup of soya sauce
1 cup broccoli peices
1/2 a green pepper
2 cups white rice

Directions:
Prepare the rice so it will be ready to serve.
Take you left over peice of roast beef and cut it into bit sized peices. In a medium sauce pan, add the butter and onions and saute until onions are translucent then add your beef. Add the water and soyasauce and cover and let simmer at low hear for 10 mins. Add all other vegitables and cook uncovered for 10 mins. Serve over white rice.
