Title:
Nutty Apple Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/58018.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium unpeeled tart apple, cored
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 cup crushed cornflakes

Directions:
Cut apple into 12 thin wedges. Spread peanut butter on cut sides; roll in cornflakes.

Submitter: 
Beatrice  Richard
Description: 
A crunchy coating turns apples and peanut butter into a finger-licking after-school snack, shares Beatrice Richard of Posen, Michigan. 'Even very young kinds will have a blast spreading peanut butter on the apple wedges and rolling them in cornflake crumbs,' she jots.
