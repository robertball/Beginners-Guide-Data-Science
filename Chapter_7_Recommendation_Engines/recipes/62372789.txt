Title:
Crisp Dilly Beans by whodunitrdr Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds fresh green beans
1 teaspoon dried red pepper flakes
4 cloves garlic
4 heads fresh dill
2 cups water
1/4 cup pickling salt
2 cups apple cider vinegar

Directions:
Wash and stem beans but leave whole; pack beans uniformly into 4 sterilized hot pint jars.
To each pint, add 1/4 teaspoon red pepper, 1 clove garlic and 1 head dill.
Combine water, salt and vinegar in a saucepan.
Bring to a rolling boil, pour over beans, filling to within 1/4 inch of jar top.
Wipe jar rim, adjust lids.
Process in boiling water bath 5 minutes. Start to count processing time when water in canner returns to full boil.
Remove from water and let cool.
Makes 4 pints.

Submitter:

These are one of our favorite pickled vegetables. They are great with a holiday meal or even a sandwich. Recipe from Farm Journal.
