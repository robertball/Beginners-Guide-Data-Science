Title:
Lemon Garlic Shrimp with Roasted Brussel Sprouts and Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535413.jpg
Ingredients:
Lemon Garlic Shrimp:
1 ¼ lb(s) Shrimp, peeled and deveined.
1 spray cooking spray
¼ cup fresh lemon juice
2 tbsp light butter, melted
3 cloves of garlic, minced
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
¾ tsp lemon pepper
¼ teaspoon ground red pepper
3 tbsp parsley, chopped
Brussel Sprouts:
4 cups brussels sprouts sliced in half
1 cup sliced red onion
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
salt and pepper to taste
Brown rice:
2 cups instant brown rice
4 cups water

Directions:
Lemon Garlic Shrimp:
Preheat oven 425 degrees
Arrange shrimp in a sinle layer in a 13x9 baking dish coated with cooking spray. Combine lemon juice and next 5 ingredients; pour over shrimp. Bake at 425 for 8-10 minutes or until shrimp are done. Sprinkle parsley over shrimp. Serves 4 (serving size 3 ounces shrimp with 2 tablespoons sauce)
Brussel Sprouts:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
combine brussel sprouts and onions, and toss with olive oil and salt. Roast for 20 minutes. 1 cup per serving
Rice:
Bring water to a boil in a medium pot, add rice and simmer for 5 minutes or until all liquid is absorbed.
1 cup per serving

Shrimp baked in a lemon garlic sauce, sided with lightly roasted brussel sprouts and brown rice.
