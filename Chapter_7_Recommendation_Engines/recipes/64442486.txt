Title:
Homemade Icy Paws treat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-32oz cartons plain or vanilla yogurt (greek?)
1-8oz can tuna in water
2 tsp garlic powder
24 3oz plastic cups, not paper

Directions:
open yogurt. if full to the top, scoop out 1 c (will be frozen as plain yogurt). put half can of tuna in each yogurt container, add garlic powder, stir thoroughly.
scoop mixture into cups with spoon. place on tray and freeze overnight.
