Title:
Pumpkin Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cornmeal
3 T whole wheat flour
1 T baking powder
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. allspice
1 cup soy milk
1 cup sour cream
1 egg
3/4 cup pumpkin puree
1/4 cup oil
1/4 cup agave syrup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Coat a 9x13 pan with vegetable spray.
Combine cornmeal, flour, baking powder, and spices, set aside.
Combine soy milk, sour cream, egg, pumpkin puree, oil, and agave syrup in large bowl. Add dry ingredients, mix well.
Pour into prepared pan, spread evenly.
Bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes, until top springs back when gently touched, and edges have browned.

Submitter: 
sueb
Description: 
I developed this recipe as a cross between pumpkin pie and cornbread!  This makes a moist, flavorful cornbread.  This is sweet without being too sweet!
