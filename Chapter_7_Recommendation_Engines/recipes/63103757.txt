Title:
** White Chicken Chili Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 small flour tortillas
2 c. cooked, shredded chicken (can use roasted deli chicken)
2 c. shredded Monterey Jack (or cheddar) cheese
Canned red enchilada sauce
1 1/2 Tbsp. butter
1 1/2 Tbsp. flour
1 c. chicken broth
1/2 c. sour cream
1 (4 oz) can diced green chillies (or 1/2 grn chilis & 1/2 jalapeno for an extra bite)
(chopped onions, opt.)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 9x13 pan.
Saute onions, if using (opt.).
Mix chicken, onions (opt.) and 1 cup cheese. Dip tortillas in enchilida sauce. Roll up chicken/cheese in tortillas and place in pan.
In a sauce pan, melt butter, stir in flour and cook 1 minute.  Add broth and whisk until smooth.  Heat over medium heat until thick and bubbly.
Stir in sour cream and chilies.  Do not bring to boil to prevent curdled sour cream.
Pour over enchiladas and top with remaining cheese.
Bake 20 min and then under broiler for 3 min to brown the cheese.
