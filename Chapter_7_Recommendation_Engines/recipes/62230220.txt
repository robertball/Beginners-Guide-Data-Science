Title:
Chicken Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Rolls:
6oz cream cheese (softened)
2 cups cubed chicken (boneless skinless, uncooked)
3 cups crushed Ritz crackers
2 packages crescent rolls (16 rolls)
4 tbsp soft margarine
1/2 tsp pepper
Sauce:
1 tbsp onion
1 cup milk
6 tbsp butter
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 cup chicken broth
1 cup half and hald
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 cup flour

Directions:
Rolls: combine cream cheese, butter, and pepper until smooth. Stir in chicken.
Put spoonful in each crescent roll and roll up.
Dip in melted butted and then ritz cracker crumbs.
Bake 375*F oven for 20 mins.
Sauce: saute onion in butter.
Slowly add in the flour, salt, and pepper. This is your base for the sauce, so allow it to cook well but be careful to not burn.
Gradually add milk, half & half, and chicken broth. You may or may not need to add more chicken broth to get the right texture/taste you desire.
Stir until thick and smooth. Serve over the cooked chicken rolls.
