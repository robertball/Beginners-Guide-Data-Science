Title:
Italian Dressing Mix - Carol Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon granulated garlic
1 tablespoon granulated onion
1 tablespoon white sugar
2 tablespoons dried oregano
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 tablespoon dried parsley
1/4 teaspoon celery salt
2 tablespoons salt

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix together the garlic, onion, sugar, oregano, pepper, thyme, basil, parsley, celery salt and regular salt.  Store in a tightly sealed container.
To prepare dressing, whisk together 1/4 cup red wine or balsamic vinegar, 2/3 cup olive oil, 2 tablespoons water and 2 tablespoons of the dry mix.

Submitter: 
Carol A
Description: 
This Italian dressing mix is a combination of eight pungent herbs that you can store and use to whip up fresh Italian dressing in seconds whenever the mood strikes!Modified from: Italian Dressing Mix
