Title:
Orzo with Bacon and grape tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Orzo
2 cups  Chicken stock
1 cup small chopped onion
3 cloves garlic
8 Grape tomatoes
2 tablespoons of capers
2 stripes of Bacon
1 cup of frozen corn
1 cup of frozen peas

Directions:
In a skillet cook bacon till crisp remove and set aside
drain all Bacon grease except 2 Table spoons
saute Onions for 2 minutes add Garlic saute for another minute
Add orzo and brown for 2 minutes
Add 2 cups of chicken stock and bring it to a boil stirring slowly to keep it from sticking
When it is to a full boil add tomatoes,capers, corn and peas. Cover and bring it back to a boil and then remove from heat.
In 5 minutes it will be ready to serve (Always check the orzo)
Crumble up Bacon and serve on top

Submitter: 
Darin Terwilliger
Description: 
This is a light delicate side dish of Orzo, that will make you want to keep coming back for more
