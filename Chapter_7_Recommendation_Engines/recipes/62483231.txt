Title:
Maple Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and cleaned
4 slices of uncooked bacon, sliced in 1/2 inch pieces
4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
3 tablespoons good quality maple syrup
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 F (205 C).
Place Brussels sprouts in a baking pan in a single layer.
Drizzle with maple syrup and olive oil.
Sprinkle with bacon, salt, pepper and stir to combine.
Roast in oven for 45 minutes, stirring half way through.

Submitter: 
stefychefy
Description: 
Who knew Brussels sprouts could ever taste this good!
Caramelized and crispy these sprouts gain a nutty flavor from the roasting process.
