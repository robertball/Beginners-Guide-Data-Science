Title:
D's Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/2 c. mayonnaise (not salad dressing)
1 c. baby spinach leaves, chopped
1/2 c. walnuts, chopped
1 T. fine, dry bread crumbs
2 T. grated parmesan cheese
salt and pepper, if desired

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
Place chicken breast halves in an oiled baking dish.
Mix remaining ingredients in a medium bowl and spoon some topping over each breast half.
Bake for approx. 25 to 30 min.

Submitter: 
Diana S.
Description: 
Boneless chicken breasts topped with a delicious spinach and walnut topping. This is an easy, yummy and low-carb dinner.
