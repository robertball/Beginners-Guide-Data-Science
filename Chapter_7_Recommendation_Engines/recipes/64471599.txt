Title:
PulpEpidemics Dinomite Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2473646.jpg
Ingredients:
100 grams of crushed Nice Biscuits (Graham crackers would also work)
4 tbsps of Milo or Chocolate Nesquick
3/4 can of condensed milk (375g can)
10 Marshmallows
Shredded coconut to sprinkle over

Directions:
In a large bowl mix crushed biscuits and milo until combined.
Add condensed milk and mix until completely combined.
Prepare a baking tray by lining it with baking paper
Grab a marshmallow in one hand and chocolate mixture in the other and wrap the chocolate mixture around the marshmallow. This is very stick work. Try to get a ball shape.
Place on prepared baking tray. Repeat with all the marshmallows.
Leave to set int he fridge for 3 hours.
Rawr like a dinosaur while you eat them. Makes the experience even more enjoyable.
