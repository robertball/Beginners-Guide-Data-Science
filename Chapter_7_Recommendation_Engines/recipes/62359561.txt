Title:
Summertime BBQ'd black and white beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz can  of white beans drained
1 15 oz can  of black beans drained and rinsed
1 1.3 oz packet of sloppy joe seasoning mix
1/4 cup cilantro chopped
1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:
Place beans and Sloppy Joe Seasoning in high sided pot. Mix well and cover.
Cook over medium heat until it’s at a rolling simmer.  Reduce heat to low for 5 minutes. Stir in sour cream and cilantro  and serve.
