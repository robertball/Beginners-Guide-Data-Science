Title:
Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups steamed whole grain rice
green onions
garlic
3 eggs
oil

Directions:
Brown rice, green onions, garlic in hot oil.  Move rice to one side of pan and scramble eggs, mix with rice.
