Title:
Chicken and Dumplings I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 pound) whole chicken
chicken broth
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper
ms. b's biscuit

Directions:
Fill a large pot with broth add whole chicken. Bring to a boil, and then reduce heat.  Simmer until tender, about 1 to 2 hours.
Remove chicken from broth, and set aside to cool.  Reserve broth.  When cool enough to handle, remove meat from bones; cut into bite size pieces. Discard skin and bones.
flatten biscuits, pick up dough on fork.  Hold over boiling broth, and cut off small pieces into broth until all the dough has been used.  Stir, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
Add chicken meat, and stir. Serve hot.
