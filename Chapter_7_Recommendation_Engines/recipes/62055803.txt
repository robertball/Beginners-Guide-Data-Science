Title:
Cheesy Broccoli-Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-2 tbsp. butter or margarine
-1 large onion, chopped
-16 ounces process cheese spread loaf, cut into cubes
-1 can (10 oz) condensed cream of mushroom soup
-2/3 cup milk
-1/4 tsp. pepper
-2 cups of 1/2 inch fresh broccoli flowerets
-3 cups cooked rice
-1 cup fine soft bread crumbs (about 1 1/2 slices of bread)
-2 tbsp. butter or margarine, melted

Directions:
1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 13x9 inch baking dish.
2. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a large pan over medium-high heat. Cook onion in butter, stirring occasionally, until tender; reduce heat to medium. Stir in cheese, soup, milk, and pepper. Cook, stirring frequently, until cheese is melted.
3. Stir in broccoli and rice. Spoon into baking dish. Mix bread crumbs and 2 tablespoons melted butter; sprinkle over rice mixture. Bake uncovered 30 to 35 minutes or until light brown on top and bubbly around edges.
