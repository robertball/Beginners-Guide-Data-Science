Title:
Leftover Turkey Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 C stuffing, divided
4 C turkey
3/4 C mayo, divided
1/4 C whole cranberry sauce
2 C mashed potatoes
1 1/2 C shredded mozzella cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees
Spray a 8" baking dish w/cooking spray
Spoon 2C leftover stuffing, top w/chopped turkey.
Combine cranberry sauce w/ 1/4C mayo and spread over turkey
Combine 1/2C mayo, mashed potatoes and cheese and spread over cranberry sauce
Top with rest of stuffing
Bake 40 min. Let stand 10 min. before serving

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Use up that leftover turkey, potatoes, stuffing, and cranberry sauce in this easy casserole.
