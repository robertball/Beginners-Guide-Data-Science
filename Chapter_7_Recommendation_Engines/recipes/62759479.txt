Title:
Mushroom Apple Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 Granny Smith Apple, thinly sliced
1 medium onion, chopped
1 clove of garlic, minced
2 cups fresh Crimini mushrooms, sliced
1 cup Sour Cream
1 can (10 3/4 oz) cream of mushroom soup
1 Tbsp. ground cinnamon
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1 package long grain and wild rice
Cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Cook rice according to package.
Saute onion and garlic in oil in a skillet on low heat until transparent. Add sliced apples and mushrooms and continue to saute for 5 minutes.
Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
Place chicken breasts in skillet and brown on each side.
In a separate bowl, mix sour cream, cream of mushroom soup, and cinnamon.
Put rice in the center of a roasting pan that has been sprayed with cooking spray. Top the rice with sauted Onions, apples, and mushrooms. Arrange the chicken breast around the rice mixture.
Pour sour cream mixture over the chicken and rice.
Bake in a 350 degree oven for 45 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
A delicious fall meal, savory chicken breast, mushrooms, onions, and tart apple in a spicy sauce, baked, and served over wild rice.
