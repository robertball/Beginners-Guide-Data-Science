Title:
Standard Israeli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large cucumbers
2 large beefsteak tomatos
2 large green bell peppers
1 lime
1 lemon
granulated garlic
salt, pepper, olive oil, & balsamic vinegar to taste

Directions:
1. Dice all the ingredients to equal size, small pieces, no bigger then a 1/4" cubed.  Toss together in a bowl with sprinkle of garlic and black pepper.
2. 5 Minutes before serving, add the juice of 1 lime and 1 lemon. Also add salt.  Toss together and let stand for 5 minutes.
Serve with the olive oil and balsamic on the side, let guests use as desired (tastes great without it).  Also, tastes great with mayo on it.

Submitter: 
LONBINDER
Description: 
This is the salad served at every Israeli or Persian restaurant, its easy and delicious, and may be prepared in advance.
