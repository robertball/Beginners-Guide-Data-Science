Title:
Cabbage Fat-Burning Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cloves of garlic, minced
5 carrots, chopped
3 onions, chopped
2 (16 ounce) cans whole peeled tomatoes, with liquid
1 large head cabbage, chopped
1 (1 ounce) envelope dry onion soup mix
1 (1 ounce) envelope Lipton Noodle Soup
1 (15 ounce) can cut green beans, drained
2 quarts tomato juice * OR* crushed tomatoes
2 green bell peppers, diced
10 stalks celery, chopped
1 (14 ounce) can beef broth

Directions:
Sautee garlic and onions until onion is soft.
Add Carrots and celery, stirring often to insure they don't stick to the bottom of the stockpot
Before veggies begin to burn, add  tomatoes,beef broth, soup mixes, tomato juice, and enough water to cover vegetables. Simmer until vegetables are tender. May be stored in the refrigerator for several days.

Submitter: 
Emily Pollock Doyle
