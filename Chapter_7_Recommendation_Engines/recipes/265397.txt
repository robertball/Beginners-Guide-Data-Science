Title:
Vegan Gluten-Free Breakfast Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5454512.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana
1/2 cup frozen raspberries, plus more for garnish
1 orange, juiced
1 tablespoon flax seeds, or more to taste
2 tablespoons chopped, toasted almonds

Directions:
Combine banana, raspberries, and orange juice in a blender; puree until smooth. Pour into a breakfast bowl; top with flax seeds, chopped almonds, and extra raspberries.

Submitter: 
MisterHealthy
Description: 
I have recently discovered smoothie breakfast bowls and am enjoying trying different combinations. This one is dairy free and gluten free but packed with flax seeds and almonds for energy.
