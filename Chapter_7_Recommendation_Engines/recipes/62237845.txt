Title:
Scrumptious Summery Shrimp Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium tomatoes diced -or- 1 14.5 oz. can diced tomatoes
1/4 c. diced white onion
1/2 c. loosely-packed, finely-chopped cilantro
2-3 diced and seeded serano or jalapeno peppers
3 TB ketchup
3 TB lime juice, divided
20-30 cooked shrimp, cut in small pieces
1 tsp. salt, divided
1/2 medium cucumber, peeled and diced
1/4 cup diced red onion
1 TB finely-chopped fresh oregano
1 TB EVOO
pepper, to taste

Directions:
Mix in medium bowl: tomatoes, white onion, cilantro, peppers, ketchup, 2 TB lime juice, 1/2 tsp. salt, and  shrimp.
In separate bowl, mix cucumber, EVOO, red onion, oregano, 1/2 tsp. salt, 1 TB lime juice, and pepper, to taste.
In trifle dish, or clear tall bowl, layer tomato mixture, followed by cucumber mixture, and top with broken pieces of tortilla or torn romaine lettuce.
