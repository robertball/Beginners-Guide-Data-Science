Title:
Peanut Butter Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/764199.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust
1 1/4 c chocolate cookie crumbs
1/4 c sugar
1/4 c margarine melted
FILLING
1 8 oz package of cream cheese
1 cup of peanut butter
1 cup of sugar
1 tbsp margarine
1 tsp vanallia
1 cup of heavy cream

Directions:
combine crust ingredients..put into pie pan and bake at 375 for 10 min and cool
mix cream cheese, peanut butter, sugar margarine, vanilla until smooth
Mix heavy cream seperate....until fluffy
Then mix heavy cream into the above mixture...
Pour into pie crust
Then refrigerate for at least 1 hr! And serve!!!!!!!
