Title:
Really Quick Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can tuna (preferably canned in water)
3 tbsp light mayo
3 tbsp sweet relish
6 saltine crackers (crushed)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Drain tuna and combine all ingredients.
Eat as a sandwich or on a bed of lettuce.
