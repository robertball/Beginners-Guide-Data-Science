Title:
EZ Tzatziki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One 12-oz container French Onion dip, sour-cream based
1 cucumber, peeled, seeded, diced
3 Tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons lemon juice, fresh if possible
1/2 teaspoon dill, dried
1/4 tsp. white pepper, ground
2 whole cloves garlic, pressed

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together, refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.  Taste, correct seasonings if necessary, no salt is usually needed if using the prepared dip.  Serve with pita bread pieces, or as a sauce for topping gyros or baked potatoes.

Submitter: 
SHOGIRL
Description: 
A shortcut recipe for Greek tzatziki which is easily made with ingredients on hand, and tastes pretty authentic
