Title:
Chocolate Coconut-Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 prepared unbaked pie crust
1/3 C margarine (but butter is fine)
4 oz. unsweetened chocolate
1 cup light or dark corn syrup
1 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
3 whole eggs
1/2 cup flaked coconut (sweetened or unsweetened)
1-1/2 cups pecans, whole or chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350oF, and place pie crust on a cookie sheet.
In a double-boiler, melt margarine and unsweetened chocolate, stirring until smooth.  Cool for fifteen minutes.
Stir in corn syrup, sugar, and salt.  Beat in eggs, one at a time, until thoroughly blended.  Add vanilla, and stir in coconut and pecans.
Pour mixture into pie crust, and place cookie sheet with filled pie in oven.  Bake 350oF for approximately one hour.  Cool and serve.
