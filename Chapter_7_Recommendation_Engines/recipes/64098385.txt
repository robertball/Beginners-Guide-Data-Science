Title:
Scot's Pulled Pork with Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pulled Pork Rub:
3 tbsp paprika
1 tbsp garlic powder
1 tbsp brown sugar
1 tbsp dry mustard
3 tbsp coarse sea salt
1 -5 lb port shoulder or Boston Butt
Meat Sauce:
1 1/2 c cider vinegar
1 c yellow or brown mustard
1/2 c. ketchup
1/3 c packed brown sugar
2 garlic cloves, smashed
1 tsp Kosher salt
1 tsp cayenne
1/2 tsp fresh ground pepper.
Pan drippings from roasted portk
Coleslaw:
1 head green cabbage, shredded
2 carrots, grated
1 red onion, thinly sliced
2 green onions chopped (both white and green parts)
1 fresh red chili sliced (optional)
1 1/2 c. mayonnaise
1/4 c Dijon mustard
1 tbsp cider vinegar
Juice of one lemon
Pinch Sugar
1/2 tsp celery seed
Several dashes hot sauce
Kosher Salt
Fresh ground pepper
Hamburger type buns

Directions:
Roasting Pork:
Mix dry rub and rob all over pork.  Refrigerate 1 hour to overnight.
Preheat oven to 300 degrees F.
Roast covered pork for 6 hours or until it pulls apart easily with fork and meat thermometer must read at least 170 degrees.
Remove from oven, remove from pan (reserving the drippings) and rest meat 10 minutes.
Deglaze roasting pan drippings with 3/4 c. water.  Reduce to half. Add to meat sauce and cook five minutes.
Pull pork with two forks.
Place shredded pork in large bowl.
Add 1/2 of the prepared over the pork in the bowl.   Reserve remaining sauce to pour over sandwich for personal preference.
May serve coleslaw over pork on a hamburger type bun.
