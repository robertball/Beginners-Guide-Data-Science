Title:
Strawberry Delight Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16.5 oz.) strawberry cake mix
3 large eggs
1 cup water
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 (14 oz.) can sweetened condensed milk
1 (16 oz.) container of frozen sugar sweetened strawberries, thawed but NOT drained
1 (12 oz.) tub Cool Whip

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine strawberry cake mix, eggs, water, and oil and mix according to package directions.
Place in a 9 x 13-inch pan and bake according to package directions, about 24-27 minutes.
When cake comes out of oven, poke holes all over it with the handle of a wooden spoon.
Pour sweetened condensed milk over cake and into holes.
Cool cake.
Pour thawed strawberries over cake, letting juices seep into holes.
Cover cake with Cool Whip.
Keep in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
This strawberry cake is really sweet but oh so good! Make it at least 2 days in advance.  It gets moister the longer it sits.
