Title:
Flank Steak with Chimichurri Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6994573.jpg
Ingredients:
Flank Steak:
2 lbs flank steak
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp kosher salt
1 tsp fresh cracked black pepper
Chimichurri Sauce:
1/3 bunch fresh Italian parsley
1/3 bunch fresh cilantro
1/2 red onion quartered
1/2 large jalapeno sliced and seeded
1 clove garlic smashed
2-3 tbsp lime juice from fresh limes
1 tsp kosher salt
1 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp coriander seeds or ground coriander
1/2 cup olive oil

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet over high heat.
Season steak generously with salt and pepper.
Once skillet/oil hot add steak and cook until browned well (approximately 3-4 minutes per side).
Remove steak from heat and place on cutting board. Cover with tin foil for 5 minutes to let juices rest. While steak resting make sauce.
In blender combine parsley, cilantro, red onion, jalapeno, lime juice, garlic, salt, cumin and coriander. Pulse till chopped.
Add olive oil and puree to a smooth consistency (approximately 1 minute).
To serve: slice flank steak into thin slices across the grain and drizzle with a generous helping of sauce.
*note: if want more heat replace jalapeno with 1 small habanero and cook time is for medium rare so adjust cook time of steak for your personal liking.

Submitter: 
Sahara B
Description: 
I am always looking to cook affordable steak choices. Flank steak is one of those cuts that is affordable and easy to work with. This is an easy dish that will sure to please the family. I like more heat so I use habanero pepper. That's the Texan in me.
