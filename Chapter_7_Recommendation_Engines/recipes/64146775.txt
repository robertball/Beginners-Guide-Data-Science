Title:
Vietnamest Barbecue Pork and noodle salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 tsp dark sesame oil divided
2 TBS reduced salt Soy Sauce
1 1/2 TBS Sambal Oelek (fresh ground chile paste)
1 TBS brown sugar
6 garlic cloves chopped
1 large shallot, chopped
1 12 OZ pork tenderloin
cooking spray
6 OZ uncooked thin rice noodles (rice vermicelli)
1 TBS fresh lime juice
2 cups sliced romaine lettuce
1 cup thinly sliced cucumber
1/2 cup julienne-cut carrot
2 TBS chopped dry-rosted undalted peanuts
4 lime wedges

Directions:
Combine 1 TBS sesame oil and next 5 ingredients (through shallot) in a mini food processor; process until smooth. Place pork in a large zip-top plastic bag. Set aside 1/4 cun marinade to bag, and seal. Marinate pork in refrigerator 1 hour, turning after 30 minutes
Preheat oven to 450
Remove pork from bag, discard remaining marinade. Place pork on a broiler pan coated with cooking spray. Cook 25 minutes or until a thermometer inserted in thickest portion registers 140. Let port rest 10 minutes; thinly slice.
Cook noodles according to pkg directions; drain and toss with remaining 2 tsp sesame oil and lime juice. Divide noodles evenly among 4 bowls, and top each serving with 1/2 cup lettuce, 1/4 cup cucumber, and 2 TBS carrot. Drizzle each serving with 1 TBS reserved marinade. Divide pork slices evenly among bowls; top each serving with 1 1/2 TSP chopped peanuts and 1 lime wedge.
