Title:
Italian Stewed Tomatoes & Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp olive oil
1 garlic clove, minced
2 large zucchini (about 8 inches in length), quartered lenghwise and sliced in 1/4 inch slices
1 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes
1 tsp Italian seasoning
1/2 - 3/4 tsp kosher salt

Directions:
Over medium heat, saute garlic in olive oil 1-2 minutes.  Add tomatoes and Italian seasoning.  Bring to boil, cover, reduce heat to low and simmer about 5 - 10 minutes.  Add zucchini slices, cover, and simmer 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Salt to taste.
