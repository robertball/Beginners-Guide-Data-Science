Title:
Mango Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 diced mango
1 clove chopped garlic
3 tbl fresh parsley
3 tbl fresh lime juice
1 tsp chili flakes
1/4 tsp cumin powder
pinch salt

Directions:
Puree all ingredients in a food processor.
Refrigerate 1/2 of the mixture.
Pour the remaining half over your favorite meat, fish, or vegetables.
Refrigerate for 2 hours to overnight, turning halfway through marinade time.
Discard marinade from meat. Cook meat to desired doneness.
Use the reserved 1/2 marinade as a sauce.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a great way to use up mangoes.
