Title:
Instant Pot Spaghetti Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 spaghetti squash
1 cup water

Directions:
Cut spaghetti squash in half crosswise.
Place trivet in Instant Pot.
Pour 1 cup water in liner.
Place squash halves on trivet.
Close and  lock lid, making sure steam valve is closed.
Press manual and lower time to 10 minutes.
Do QPR when 10 minutes is up.
Remove strands using a fork.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
It's so easy to cook a spaghetti squash! Just follow directions and walk off for 10 minutes.
