Title:
Tiramisu Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup heavy cream
2 teaspoons instant coffee
8 ounces mascarpone cheese
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup powdered sugar
2 tablespoons grated semi-sweet chocolate

Directions:
combine cream and coffee; stir until dissolved.  Add mascarpone, vanilla, and powdered sugar.  Mix together on low, then beat on high until peaks form (do not overmix).
Transfer to a serving bowl, and sprinkle with grated chocolate.
Serve with biscotti, or thin cookies (such as Anna's Swedish cookies).

Submitter:

Description: 
A creamy dip that gives you a tiramisu flavor without the hassle!
