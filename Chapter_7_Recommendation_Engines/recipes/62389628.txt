Title:
Crunchy Rice & Veggie Summer Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked brown rice
1/3 cup minced green onion
1/3 cup minced red bell pepper
1/3 cup coursely grated carrot
1/4 cup minced celery
1 tsp dried parsley
1 tsp dried basil
1/4 cup light mayonnaise
1 tsp low-sodium soy sauce
1 tbsp lemon juice
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients and mix well. Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
PolkaDot
Description: 
Super simple and pretty healthy, this salad is a great alternative to pasta salad at barbecues and picnics. It can be made up to a day ahead of time.
