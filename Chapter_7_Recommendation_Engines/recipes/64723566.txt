Title:
Fresh Cranberry Relish Jello Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6036648.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pounds Cranberries, fresh or frozen
1 large Apple
1 small Orange
1 C Sugar
1 C Walnuts
1 8 ounce package Raspberry Jello
2 C Boiling Water
1 C Cold Water

Directions:
Grate fruit in food processor and add sugar.
Dissolve jello in hot water, add cold water.
When jello has slightly thickened, add cranberry mixture and nuts.
Put in jello mold and refrigerate until firm (usually overnight)
