Title:
Hawaiian S'mores Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3566065.jpg
Ingredients:
2 HONEY MAID Honey Grahams, broken in half
2 ounces white chocolate bar
1 thin slice fresh pineapple, cored
4 Market Pantryâ„¢ Large Marshmallows, toasted
1 tablespoon sweetened flaked coconut

Directions:
Top each of 2 cracker halves with half the white chocolate. Heat grill. Grill pineapple slice until warm and surface is golden brown. Cut in half. Place half onto each s'more. Top with marshmallows, coconut and remaining cracker. Gently press. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Target Test Kitchen
Description: 
Grilled pineapple, white chocolate and a sprinkle of coconut make this s'more a s'more-lover's summer dream.
