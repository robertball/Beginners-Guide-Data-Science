Title:
Strawberry Almond Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1102336.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c strawberry puree
1 c sugar (or less)
3 eggs
3 c almond meal/flour
1 tsp baking powder
confectioners sugar for topping
whole strawberries for topping

Directions:
puree strawberries
mix eggs and sugar until creamy
add almond flour, baking powder, strawberries
bake 350 for 45-50 min in 9 inch round pan, buttered
cool for 10 min
remove from pan to finish cooling
top with confectioners sugar and sliced strawberries or as desired

Description: 
gluten free almond strawberry cake. very moist
