Title:
Baked Corned Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 lb Flat cut corned beef brisket
3T ketchup
2t worsteshire sauce
1 chopped onion
1t instant coffee
1 cup water

Directions:
mix ketchup and worsteshire sauce together and spread over meat. Sprinkle onions and coffee over meat. Place in a baking/roasting pan. Pour (slowly) 1/2 cup water over all, place other 1/2 on bottom of the pan. Bake covered at 300 degrees for approx 3 1/2 hours.Baste every 30 minutes.
