Title:
Green Chile and Cilantro Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979699.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups chopped fresh cilantro
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
3/4 cup pine nuts, toasted
1 (4 ounce) can ORTEGA® Whole Green Chiles
4 cloves garlic, peeled
1 tablespoon lime juice
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
PLACE cilantro, cheese, pine nuts, chiles, garlic, lime juice and salt in food processor; cover.  Process until finely grated.  Process, while slowly adding vegetable oil, for 1 minute or until almost smooth.
SERVE over pasta or as a dip for vegetables.

Submitter: 
Ortega®
Description: 
Does your pesto have a case of the blahs?  You can't go wrong with ingredients like toasted pine nuts, chopped fresh cilantro, garlic, lime juice and Ortega® Whole Green Chiles.  Presto - Green Chile and Cilantro Pesto!
