Title:
Granddaddy's  Easy Coconut Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/942807.jpg
Ingredients:
1 5ounce can of evaporated milk
1 1/3 cups of shredded flaked coconut
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 regular pie crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix all ingredients together until well blended.
Pour into pie crust. Bake for 50-55 minutes until lightly browned.
Cool and serve.

Submitter: 
Susie
Description: 
My grandfather always made this recipe at Christmas.  It is the easiest recipe ever and so delish!
