Title:
Spicy Sausage Sweet Potato Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2022191.jpg
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons olive oil
3 white sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into wedges
2 sweet onions, peeled and quartered
2 (16 ounce) packages Hillshire FarmÂ® Smoked Sausage or Polska Kielbasa, cut into bite-size pieces
1 (10 ounce) jar pickled banana peppers, drained
4 tablespoons chimichurri sauce (optional)
1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
4 tablespoons butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Lightly oil a 9x13 glass baking dish.
Arrange sweet potatoes, onions, sausage in baking dish. Pour entire jar of pickled peppers over ingredients. Sprinkle with seasoning, drizzle with chimichurri, and top with pats of butter. Cover dish tightly with foil.
Bake for 15 minutes. Remove foil and bake until vegetables begin to brown, about 10 more minutes.

Submitter: 
Angela Sackett  Superhotmama
Description: 
This sweet and spicy, comforting and easy sausage bake with sweet potatoes, peppers, and onions is grain- and gluten-free.
