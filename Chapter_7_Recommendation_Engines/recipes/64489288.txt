Title:
Dark Lager Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs Ground Beef
1lb Hot Italian Sausage
6 Large Garlic Cloves
1 Large Onion Diced
6 Jalapenos Diced
6 Jalapenos Sliced
3 Diced Cayenne Peppers
3 12oz Cans Diced Tomatoes
2 12oz Cans Dark Kidney Beans
2 12oz Cans Light Kidney Beans
1 12oz Can Sliced Black Olives
2 Tablespoons Basil
3 Tablespoons Chili Powder
12oz Dark Lager Beer

Directions:
Brown the Ground Beef
Remove the Hot Italian Sausage from their casings and Brown as well
Dice 1 Large Onion
Dice 6 Large Garlic Cloves
Dice 6 Jalapenos
Slice 6 Jalapenos
Combine the above and all other ingredients in your slow cooker and set on low for 8 hours.

Very spicy chili for those that like it hot.
