Title:
Tarragon Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, chopped
1 stick butter
1 cup rice
1 1/2 tsp dried tarragon
2 cups chicken broth

Directions:
Melt the butter in a large skillet.  Saute the onions for 5-8 minutes.  Stir in the rice and tarragon.  Cook for three minutes stirring constantly.  Meanwhile, bring stock to a boil.  Add to the rice mixture & cook on low until liquid is absorbed.

Submitter: 
Kristin Turrell
Description: 
This is the most flavorful and easy rice dish you could make.  It's great for dinner parties & goes with anything!
