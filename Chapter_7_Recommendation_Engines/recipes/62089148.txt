Title:
Christmas in The Islands Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Large Eggs
1/2 Cup Vegetable Oil
1/2 Cup Milk
2 1/2 Cups All-Purpose Flour
1 Cup Granulated Sugar
1 Tsp Baking Powder
1 Tsp Baking Soda
1 Tsp Nutmeg or Cinnamon
1/2 Tsp Salt
2 Cups Drained Chunk/Shredded Pineapple
1/2 Cup Dried Cherries
1 1/3 Cups Toasted Coconut
1 Tsp Dark Rum or Rum Flavoring

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease 2 large bread pans.
In medium bowl, mix together the eggs, oil, and milk.
In seperate large bowl, stir together flour, sugar, soda, powder, spice, and salt.
Slowly add wet ingredients and mix till just moistened.
Add pineapple, cherries, coconut, and rum.
Stir together untill all ingredients are carefully blended.
Mixture should be very chunky and moist.
Pour into two oiled bread pans.
Bake at 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes.
Let cool 10 minutes before removing.
