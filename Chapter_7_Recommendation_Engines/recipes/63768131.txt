Title:
Creamy Jalapeno Ranch Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream or greek yogurt
* 2 (1 ounce) packages ranch dressing mix
* 1/4 cup Herdez Tomatillo Verde Mexican Cooking Sauce
* 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
* 2 jalapeño peppers, seeded and chopped (or for more heat leave in the seeds)
* 1 clove garlic, minced
* 1-1/2 teaspoons lime juice

Directions:
Place the Herdez Tomatillo Verde Cooking sauce, cilantro, garlic and jalapeños in a food processor or blender. Blend until smooth and set aside.
Add the sour cream and ranch dressing mix in a medium bowl. Whisk in about 3/4 of the green sauce; taste and adjust the heat level by adding more Herdez sauce.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
Sour cream, ranch dressing mix, and a spicy tomatillo cilantro sauce makes a delicious dip.Modified from: Jalapeno Ranch Salad Dressing
