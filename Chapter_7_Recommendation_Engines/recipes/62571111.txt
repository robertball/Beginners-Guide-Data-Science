Title:
Super Simple Linguica and Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
3 cups cubed potatoes (cut in 1" chunks)
1 lb. linguica, sliced
1/2 cup onion, diced
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat.
Add potatoes and onion; cook about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add linguica and continue cooking until potatoes are tender - about 5 to 7 minutes.
Season to taste with salt & pepper.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
Linguica, a Portuguese sausage, is sliced and sauteed with chunks of potato and onion for a flavorful side dish...or main, if you like! My Mom used to make this often, either stovetop or wrapped in foil and tossed on the grill.
