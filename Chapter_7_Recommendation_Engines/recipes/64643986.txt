Title:
Goulash quick and easy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4521416.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs of lean ground turkey or beef
1/2 box elbow macaroni noodles
1 package of frozen tri color pepper and onion mixture
1 Tablespoon of minced garlic
1  large jar of spaghetti sauce (46 oz) ... I use ragu chunky garden
1 large can of diced tomatoes (28 oz) - do not drain
2 Tablespoons of dried basil
2 Tablespoons of dried oregano
salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste

Directions:
Cook the ground turkey/beef in a pan just until no longer pink
Add the pepper/onion frozen mix, canned tomatoes, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil and simmer together.
Drain the mixture and place in a large pot mixing in half of the spaghetti sauce in. Cook on low heat or in a slow cooker on High.
Cook the elbow macaroni according to the directions on the package and drain.
Add the elbow macaroni to the meat and veggie mixture and slowly add the remaining sauce.
Once everything is mixed together allow to simmer for about 10 minutes and add any salt, pepper, and garlic powder to bring it to the taste you like.
