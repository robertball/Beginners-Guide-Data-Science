Title:
Almond Rhapsody Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/437531.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup quick cooking oatmeal
1/4 cup whole almonds
1 banana, broken into pieces
1/2 cup frozen raspberries
1 cup milk
1/3 cup plain yogurt
1/2 tsp almond extract

Directions:
In a blender combine oatmeal and almonds and blend to a fine texture.
Add remaining ingredients and blend until smooth, serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Sarah-May
Description: 
Sweet and satisfying this drink makes a great snack or breakfast on the go.
