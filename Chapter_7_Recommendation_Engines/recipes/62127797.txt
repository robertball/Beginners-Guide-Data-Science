Title:
Gourmet Blue Cheese Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
2 tablespoons seasoned bread crumbs
1 tablespoon dinced onion
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese
salt and pepper to taste
dusting of flour

Directions:
1. Mix all ingredient in a medium sized bowl and form into 4 patties. Sprinkle patties lighlty with flour on each side.
2. Grill patties, turning once, until internal temperature reaches 180 degrees F.
3.  Enjoy by itself or on a nice homestyle bun with your favorite toppings.
