Title:
Mini Reubens with Honey Mustard Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990738.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package Hillshire Farm速 Polska Kielbasa
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup honey mustard
2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
1 (16 ounce) package shredded cabbage or coleslaw blend
8 ounces sliced Swiss cheese
1 (16 ounce) package cocktail rye bread

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.  Cut sausage on an angle into 1/4" slices.
Combine mayonnaise, mustard and dill in medium bowl.  Stir in cabbage, mixing lightly to combine.  Cut each slice of cheese into 4 smaller squares.
Place bread slices on 2 rimmed baking sheets.  Top each slice with about 2 tablespoons cabbage mixture, 1 slice of sausage and a small square of cheese.  Bake 18-20 minutes or until heated through and cheese is lightly browned.
