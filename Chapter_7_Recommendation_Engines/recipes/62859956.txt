Title:
Tempeh Tamale Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, chopped
1 8oz package, tempeh, crumbled
2 teaspoon minced garlic
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoon apple cider or red wine vinegar
1 teaspoon maple syrup
1 can tomato sauce
1 can pinto beans, drained and rinsed
For Crust
2/3 cup cornmeal
1/3 cup whole wheat pastry flour
2 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup non-dairy milk
1 tablespoon maple syrup
2 tablespoon unsweetened applesauce
1 cup frozen corn, defrosted and drained
1/3 cup non-dairy cheddar cheese
sliced olives and vegan sour cream for garnish

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Place a large skillet over medium high heat and spritz with cooking spray or oil.  Add onion and saute until softened and starting to brown, about 5 minutes.
Crumble tempeh into a pan with the saute onion.  (use grater)
Lightly born tempeh, cooking for additional 3 minutes
To the pan, add garlic, chili powder, 1/2t salt, vinegar, 1t maple syrup, tomato sauce and beans.
Allow bean mixture to simmer for a few minutes to meld flavors.  Transfer to a 2 quart baking dish that has been sprayed and set aside.
In a medium bowl, combine cornmeal, flour, baking powder and salt.
In your liquid measuring cup, combine milk, maple syrup and applesauce.
Add wet ingredients to the cornmeal mixture and stir until just combined.
Gently fold in the defrosted corn.
Spread corn mixture carefully and evenly over the top of the casserole dish.
Top the casserole with the shredded "cheese"
Bake for 15-20 minute, until cheese has melted and crust is set and starting to brown.
Serve with sour "cream" and olives if desired.
