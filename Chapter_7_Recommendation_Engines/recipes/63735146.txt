Title:
Karrie's Crock Pot Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion, chopped
1 tbs olive oil
2 cans 16 oz. butter beans
1 large can kidney beans
1 large Bushes Vegetarian Baked Beans
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
1 cup ketchup
2 tsp prepared mustard
4 tbs vinegar
3 tbs A1 Bold and Spicy

Directions:
Saute onion in oil until translucent. Rinse and drain butter beans and kidney beans. In small bowl mix ketchup, mustard, brown sugar, vinegar and A1. Put butter beans, kidney beans and Baked beans in sauce in crock pot. Stir in onions and ketchup mixture.  Turn crock pot on low and heat for 2-3 hours (depending on Crock pot).
