Title:
Rich Chocolate Peppermint Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/793695.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups all-purpose flour
2/3 Cup unsweetened baking cocoa
3/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 Cup butter, softened
1-1/2 Cups white sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon peppermint extract
1/2 Cup York Peppermint Candy Pieces, plus extra for decorating

Directions:
Whisk together the flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt in a small bowl.
In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add the eggs one at a time, then mix in the peppermint extract.
Add the flour mixture one cup at a time to the butter/sugar mixture.
Cover and chill dough for an hour.
Preheat oven to 350
Form dough into 1" balls. Place 2" apart on ungreased baking sheet and flatten slightly.
Bake for 8-10 or until edges are firm.

Super soft, chocolately cookies full of York Candy Pieces make for a delicious combination perfect for all mint lovers.
