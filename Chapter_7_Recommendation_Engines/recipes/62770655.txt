Title:
Stacy's Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz package of light cream cheese
1 cup reduced fat cottage cheese
1/4 cup reduced fat mayo
1 6.5 oz can of crabmeat
1 tbsp lemon juice
3 tbsp chopped green onions
1/2 tsp garlic salt
3 drops hot sauce

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, cottage cheese and mayo in a mixer and blend for about 1 minute or until well blended.
Add remaining ingredients and blend for another minute.
