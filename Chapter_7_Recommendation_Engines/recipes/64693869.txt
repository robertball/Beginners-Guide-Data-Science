Title:
Bill's Master Vegetarian Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 20 Ounce Can Vegetarian Burger
4 Eggs
1/2 Cup Quick Oats
1 Cup Plain Bread Crumbs
1 Tablespoon Garlic Powder
1 Tablespoon Onion Powder
1/4 teaspoon Sage
1/4 teaspoon Cumin
1 teaspoon Seasoning Salt
1 Tablespoon McCormick's Meatloaf Seasoning

Directions:
Combine all ingredients throughly.  Form into patties and fry until cooked through.
Serve as desired in a sandwich or otherwise.
