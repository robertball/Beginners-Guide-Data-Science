Title:
Butterkoek Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. of butter
1 cup of sugar
1 egg
1 tsp of almond extract
2 cups of flour

Directions:
Mix butter, sugar, egg and almond extract with spoon.
Then add 2 cups of flour.
Place in a 9 inch greased pie plate.
Brush with milk.
Top with slivered almonds, if so desired
Bake at 300 for 45-50 minutes

Submitter: 
Cindy Yonkman
Description: 
Delicious Dutch Treat- without the hassle of almond paste!
