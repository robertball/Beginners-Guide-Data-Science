Title:
Sour Cream Brown Sugar Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c flour
2 c brown sugar
3/4 c margarine
1 c sour cream
1 egg
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
2 T chopped pecans

Directions:
Combine flour, brown sugar and margarine with pastry blender or fork until crumbly.  Reserve 2/3 c of this mixture for topping.  Add the rest of the ingredients to the brown sugar mixture.  Mix well.  Pour batter into a greased 9 x 13 pan; sprinkle 2/3 c reserved ingredients and 2 T chopped pecans over top.  Bake at 350F for 30-35 minutes.
