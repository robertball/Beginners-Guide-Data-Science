Title:
Kay's Mini Cheesecakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6661743.jpg
Ingredients:
3  (8oz) packages cream cheese, at room temperature
1  cup sugar
5  eggs
1 1/2   teaspoon vanilla
8  oz  sour cream
1/4 cup   sugar
1/2  teaspoon vanilla
Strawberry,  cherry or other preserves
98oz)

Directions:
Prepare 6 dozen mini muffin pans with papers and a light coating of PAM.
Beat cream cheese until soft and creamy.
Gradually add 1 cup sugar,  beating until light and fluffy.
Add eggs, one at a time.  Then add vanilla.
Spoon batter into pans, filling 2/3 full.
Bake at 300 degrees for 30 minutes.
Combine sour cream and 1/4 cup sugar and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla, stirring well.
Spoon 1/4 teaspoon mixture on each cupcake.
Top with a small dollop of preserves (I've also used blueberry and lingonberry).
Refrigerate until ready to serve.
