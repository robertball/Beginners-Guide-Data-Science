Title:
Heavenly Fruit Cocktail Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1423936.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 cup sugar
2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
2 large eggs, slightly beaten
2 cups fruit cocktail  (see note)
ICING
1/2 cup evaporated milk
1 1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup butter
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup chopped nuts
1/2 cup coconut

Directions:
Cake:  Mix flour, sugar, salt and baking soda.  Add slightly beaten egg and fruit cocktail.  Stir only till mixed.  Bake in greased 9x13 pan for 30- 35  minutes at 350 degrees.  Remove from oven and place on cooling rack.  With the end of a wooden spoon poke holes into the cake (space about 1 inch apart).
Icing:  Boil butter, milk and sugar in a saucepan for 1 minute, stirring constantly. Remove from heat.   Add vanilla.
Sprinkle nuts and coconut onto the warm cake.  Pour hot icing over top.
Serve Warm or cool.
