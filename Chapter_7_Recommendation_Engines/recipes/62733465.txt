Title:
Easy Creamy Crab and Asparagus Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/625753.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Slices Bacon, diced small
1/2 Cup Onion, diced fine
1 Clove Garlic, crushed
1/2 Cup Yellow Bell Pepper, diced
3/4 Cup Asparagus, cut in 1/4" pieces
1 Cup Potato, diced small
8 Ounces Crab Meat
2 Cups Skim Milk
1 Cup Half and Half
1/2 Teaspoon Pepper
1 Teaspoon Salt

Directions:
Saute bacon and onion until onion is translucent.
Add garlic and yellow bell pepper and saute for 2 more minutes.
Add remaining ingredients and simmer until potatoes are tender (about 20-30 minutes).

Description: 
Creamy soup with crab, potatoes, and asparagus.  

I made this on whim one Sunday when crab meat was on sale at the store.  It comes together quickly and is very tasty!
