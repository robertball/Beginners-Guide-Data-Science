Title:
Perfect Cheese Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cheddar cheese, shredded
8 oz. swiss cheese, shredded
2 T flour
1/4 t black pepper
1 clove garlic, smashed
12 oz. beer
dash tobasco

Directions:
1. In plastic bag, mix 2 cheeses with flour and pepper. Toss together until cheese is coated.
2. Rub crushed garlic clove in fondue pot or other pan.
3. Pour beer into pan and heat over medium-low heat until hot.
4. Add cheese mixture a little at a time, stirring until cheese melts and ingredients blend.
5. Serve with pieces of crusty french bread to dip into fondue!

Submitter: 
LLIGETT
Description: 
Such a favorite in my house that my sister always demands it for holiday meals. Can be varied with different cheeses, although the classic cheddar-swiss blend is sure to please everyone!
