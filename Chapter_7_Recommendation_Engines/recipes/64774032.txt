Title:
Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7569751.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 tsp. salt
10 2/3 TBSP Shortening (butter flavored if possible)
7 TBSP cool water

Directions:
Mix together flour and salt.
Cut in shortening.
Mix in water
Roll it flat, wrap around rolling pin and roll into pie plate
Cut off edges and shape.

Submitter: 
Matthew Freestone
Description: 
General use flaky pie crust recipe with a hint of salty taste.
