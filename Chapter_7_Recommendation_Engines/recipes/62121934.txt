Title:
Elegant But Easy Red Velvet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 18 ounces box chocolate cake mix (white cake mix or yellow cake mix may be subbed)
1 3 1/2 ounces box instant chocolate fudge pudding
1 cup sour cream (or sub 3/4 cup milk, 3/4 teaspoon lemon juice and 1/3 cup butter or margarine, blended)
1/3 cup oil
4 large eggs, room temperature
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 (1 ounce) bottle red food coloring
cream cheese frosting
colored sugar sprinkles, optional
chopped walnuts, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
Grease 2 9 inch cake pans and set aside.
In a meduim bowl, add cake mix, pudding mix, sour cream, oil, eggs, vanilla, and food coloring.
Mix well either by hand or with mixer until well blended, and food coloring is evenly distributed throughout the batter.
Pour the batter into the two greased 9 inch round cake pans.
Time and bake cakes according to the cake mix directions.
Let cake cool comepletly.
Frost cooled cake with cream cheese frosting.
Sprinkle the top with the sugar sprinkles and/or sides with chopped walnuts, if desired.

Description: 
A fast dessert that will please most. Looks and tastes like you spent hours preparing, but it is so easy that you will have time to enjoy the occasion.
