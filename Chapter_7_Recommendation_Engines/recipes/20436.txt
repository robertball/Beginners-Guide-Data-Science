Title:
JoeDogg's Spicy Red Beer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/785178.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle beer
1 (5.5 ounce) can tomato-vegetable juice cocktail
1 dash Louisiana-style hot sauce
1 dash Worcestershire sauce
1 pinch seasoning salt

Directions:
In a frozen beer mug combine tomato-vegetable juice, hot sauce, Worcestershire sauce and seasoning salt. Pour in cold beer.

Submitter: 
JoeDogg
Description: 
This is a delicious addition to any meal or just by itself anytime day or night. Yummy and satisfying not to mention the health benefits.
