Title:
Baileys Holiday Escape Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991236.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 ounces Baileys® Caramel Irish Cream Liqueur
1/2 ounce Captain Morgan Parrot Bay® Coconut Rum
1 dash Angostura bitters
Maraschino cherry for garnish

Directions:
Pour Baileys Caramel Irish Cream Liqueur, Captain Morgan Parrot Bay Coconut Rum and Angostura Bitters into an ice-filled shaker.
Shake and roll into a rocks glass filled with ice.
Garnish with a cherry and parasol.

Submitter: 
Baileys®
Description: 
Bring the tropics to your house this winter with Baileys® Caramel Irish Cream and Captain Morgan Parrot Bay® Coconut Rum.
