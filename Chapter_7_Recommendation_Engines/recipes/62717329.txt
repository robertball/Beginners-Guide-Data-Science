Title:
Baked Buffalo Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871580.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast halves
1/4 cup hot sauce
1 tablespoon vinegar
1 tablespoon stick margarine, melted
1 teaspoon celery seeds
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1/2 cup blue cheese dressing

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
2. Spray skillet with cooking spray. Add chicken and cook 4 minutes each side until browned.
3. Place chicken in an 11x7 baking dish coated with cooking spray.
4. Combine hot sauce and next 4 ingredients. Pour over chicken and bake uncovered for 25 minutes. Serve with dressing.

Submitter: 
J Scholfield
Description: 
These buffalo chicken breasts are as good as buffalo chicken wings without all of the mess
