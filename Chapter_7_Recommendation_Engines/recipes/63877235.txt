Title:
Quick Banana Bread for Marty Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box yellow cake mix
2 eggs
2cups bananas(4 large bananas frozen whole- thawed-peeled)
2 teaspoons vanilla(i use mexican)
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
thaw and peel bananas in mixing bowl add eggs, mix well with hand mixer-add vanilla- add 1 box yellow cake mix well- fold in nuts- put into 5 small or 2 large sprayed loaf pans- bake @ 350 for 40 minutes-

Description: 
quick and easy dessert- not overly sweet- no icing needed-
