Title:
Lite Coffee Mocha Freeze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T instant coffee granules (caff or decaff)
2 T Splenda
2 T Hershey's Chocolate Syrup Lite
1 cup skim milk
14 normal, square icecubes

Directions:
Milk first, put all ingredients into a blender and blend on low/med until the ice is smooth - about 30 seconds.  Shake the blender to be sure all of the big pieces of ice are fully chopped.

Submitter: 
LilPhotog
Description: 
Low-calorie, very low fat, icy mocha coffee drink.  Delicious enough to believe it's really bad for you.  It's an incredibly simple recipe too.  Get your chocolate and coffee fix w/o the guilt!
