Title:
Wedding Gift Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/591399.jpg
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter
3 tablespoons of olive oil
3 cloves of garlic chopped
1 large onion chopped
1 pound of ground beef
1 pound of mild sausage
4 teaspoons of italian seasoning (dried)
2 teaspoons of rosemary (dried)
1 1/2 teaspoons of oregano (dried)
2 teaspoons of salt (opt)
1/2 teaspoons of pepper
29 ounce can of tomatoe puree (or smaller cans that = 29 oz)
18 ounce can of tomatoe paste (or smaller cans that = 18 oz)

Directions:
Melt the butter with the olive oil, chopped garlic and chopped onion
Add the ground beef and sausage
Brown until not pink and meat is crumbly
Add the spices
Simmer for 20 minutes
Add 29 oz can of tomatoe puree and two cans of water
Add 18 oz can of tomatoe paste and one can of water
Cook over low heat for two hours or longer-longer is better

Submitter: 
Dog Fish Head aka Fabius
Description: 
This recipe was given to me as part of a wedding gift recipe collection from a very dear friend.  It is rich, aromatic, flavorfull and makes ALOT.  So please grab the biggest pot you can find.  I mean REALLY BIG.  Extra sauce freezes well for future pasta dishes.
