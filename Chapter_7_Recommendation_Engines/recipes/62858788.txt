Title:
Quick ‘n’ Easy Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups biscuit mix (such as Bisquick)
2/3 cup milk
6 cups chicken stock
2 springs fresh thyme
1 bay leaf
½ tsp salt
½ tsp freshly ground black pepper
2 Tbsp all-purpose flour
¼ cup water
2 cups cooked chicken, shredded (rotisserie chicken)
2 cups frozen mixed vegetables (like peas, corn, carrots)
2 Tbsp flat-leaf parsley, minced

Directions:
In a small bowl, stir biscuit mix and milk until just combined. Set aside
In a medium pot, combine stock, thyme, a bay leaf, salt and pepper, and bring to a boil. In a small dish, whisk flour with water, and stir into chicken stock. Add chicken and mixed vegetables. Using tablespoon or ice cream scoop, drop dumpling batter into boiling stock. Reduce heat to a simmer, and cook uncovered for 10 minutes. Cover and cook an additional 10 minutes. Garnish each serving with fresh parsley.
