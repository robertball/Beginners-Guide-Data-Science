Title:
Valentine's Slice and Bake Cinnamon Heart Shortbread Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1101250.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 cup cornstarch
1/2 cup icing sugar
1 3/4 cups all purpose flour
1/2 cup cinnamon hearts, ground to a fine powder

Directions:
Beat butter in a large bowl with a hand mixer until fluffy.
Add vanilla, cornstarch, icing sugar and cinnamon heart powder, mix with hand mixer until combined.
Add flour 1/4 cup at a time, mixing with your hands until dough comes together.
Divide dough in half and roll each half into a 8 inch cylinder.
Wrap dough in waxed paper and freeze until firm, at least 1 hour or overnight.
Remove dough from freezer.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Slice logs into 1/4 inch slices, place on cookie sheets, bake for 10 minutes, rotating sheets half way through, do not let the cookies brown.
Cool cookies on a rack for 5 minutes, remove cookies from sheet and allow to finish cooling on rack.
Store in a Tupperware container or freeze.
