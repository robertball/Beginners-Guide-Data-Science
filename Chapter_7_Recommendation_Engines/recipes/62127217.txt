Title:
Easy Greek Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups spiral or shell pasta
1 can black olives
1 lb chicken tenders,cooked and chunked
1 medium cucumber
1 bunch cherry tomatoes on the vine
1 cup feta cheese
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 red wine vinegrette

Directions:
Cook pasta, drain, rinse with cool water.
Combine cut vegetables, pasta, feta, and chicken in a large mixing bowl. Add olive oil and red wine vinegrette. Mix well. Refridgerate one hour.
Serve salad cold.
