Title:
Dirty Red Beans & Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 pound chirizzo,removed from casing
1 yellow onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
2 beef bouillon cubes
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon thyme
1 can spicy Rotel
1 can red beans
2 teaspoon cajun spice,of your favorite brand
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 cups water
1 cup uncooked white rice

Directions:
Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and stir in the ground beef,chirizzo,onion,green peppers, salt,garlic,cajun spices and pepper. Cook and stir until the meats are crumbly, evenly browned, and no longer pink. Drain and discard any excess grease.  Stir in the water, beef bouillion cubes and rice. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the rice is tender, and the liquid has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes.
Add Rotel, red beans and thyme.

Description: 
A quick and spicey side for cookouts with onion and  bell peppers cooked with ground beef and chirrizo and rice. A flavorful twist on Dirty Rice combined with Red Beans and Rice!!Modified from: Ann's Dirty Rice
