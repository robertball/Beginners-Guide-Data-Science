Title:
Deconstructed Apple Pie Sundae Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp butter
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp salt
2 Granny Smith apples (peeled, cored, & chopped)
1/2 cup pecans
1/4 cup toffee bits (optional)
Vanilla ice cream

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine butter, sugar, cinnamon, salt and apples.  Cook over medium heat until apples begin to soften (approximately 10 minutes), stirring occasionally.  Add pecans and cook 2 minutes longer.  Remove from heat and cool slightly.
Spoon topping over vanilla ice cream servings.  Sprinkle with optional toffee bits.
