Title:
Cindy's Easy Pineapple Upside-down Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/440684.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 20oz can pineapple chunks, drained
2 cups baking mix (such as Bisquick)
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup water
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  While it's preheating, melt the 3 T. butter in 8" x 8" square pan.  Remove from oven and spread brown sugar over butter evenly.  Place pineapple chunks over the brown sugar & butter.
Mix the rest of the ingredients together with a whisk.  Pour over the pineapple and bake for 35-40 minutes.
Let rest in pan for 5 minutes, turn out onto serving platter.

Submitter: 
Cindy in Pensacola
Description: 
In a hurry for a quick dessert?  This easy pineapple upside-down cake fits the bill.
