Title:
Native Indian Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups corn kernels (homny corn for a more traditional soup)
2 cups chopped green beans
2 cups cubed peeled butternut squash
1.5 cups diced peeled potatoes
2 tbsp butter
2 tbsp all-purpose flour
5 cups water
1 can chicken boullion
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
In a large pot, bring corn, green beans, squash, potatoes, water and boullion to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer for about 10 minutes or until vegetables are soft.  Blend four with butter; stir into soup.  Increase heat to medium; cook stirring occasionally for about 5 minutes or until broth thickens.  Stir in pepper.

Submitter: 
THE HOOVE
Description: 
The Iroquois Indians cultivated and used the Three Sisters (corn, squash and beans) in their diet.  This is a recipes passed down by our relatives.
