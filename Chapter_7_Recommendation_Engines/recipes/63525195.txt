Title:
BLOW YOUR SOCKS OFF Chicken Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6 oz can Boneless chicken-drained
1/2 can crushed pineapple-drained(optional)
1 packet dry ranch
1 8 oz package cream cheese

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in bowl and form into shape of ball of loaf
Refrigerate until use or STUFF YOUR FACE right after making
