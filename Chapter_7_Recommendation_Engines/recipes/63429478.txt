Title:
Buttermilk Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
1 c buttermilk
2 tsp dry ranch dip mix
1 c panko bread crumbs
1/4 tsp salt
Pinch of pepper

Directions:
Place chicken between plastic wrap and pound to 1/2" thickness.
Whisk buttermilk and ranch mix, and dip chicken to coat.
Mix panko crumbs with salt and pepper.
Shake off excess buttermilk mixture, and dip into panko crumbs to coat.
Place in greased baking sheet and bake at 375 degrees of 30-45 minutes, until chicken tests done and crust is crisp and golden.
