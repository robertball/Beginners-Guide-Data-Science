Title:
Oatmeal Everything Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup softened margarine (2 sticks)
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 cups quick-cooking oats
2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup golden raisins
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup coconut

Directions:
Beat margarine, sugars, eggs and vanilla for 5 minutes.
Combine dry ingredients, add slowly to creamed mixture.
Stir in fruits, chips and nuts.
Drop by teaspoons onto ungreased baking sheets.
Bake at 350 degrees for 12-14 minutes. Cool on racks.
Makes 4 1/2 dozen.

Description: 
I came up with these cookies when I looked in my baking drawer and realized I did not have enough of one thing to make a whole batch of cookies. I combined several recipes and came up with these winners, they are really good and packed full of yummy treats.
