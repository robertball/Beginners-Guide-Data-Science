Title:
Kraft Fantasy Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/757986.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups white sugar
3/4 cups salted butter
2/3 cup evaporated milk
12 ounces semi-sweet chocolate chips
7 ounces marshmallow cream
1 cup pecans- chopped
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
bring sugar, butter, and milk to a rolling boil (240Â°) for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
remove from heat
stir in chocolate chips
stir in marshmallow cream
stir in vanilla
pour into a buttered 13" x 9" pan.
cool to room temperature and devour!

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
Yes, this is the original Fantasy Fudge recipe from the back of the Kraft Marshmallow Creme jar. We'll never know why they changed the recipe on us, but we've preserved the recipe for you right here.
