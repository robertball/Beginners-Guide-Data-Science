Title:
Chipotle Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Avocados
1/4 of a red onion, finely chopped
2 heaping Tablespoons of Cilantro, finely chopped
1/4 of a lime, juiced
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Combine all of the ingredients into a bowl and mash together until smooth.
You could add more lime juice if needed.

Submitter: 
Amy Lee
Description: 
The original recipe calls for 1/2 a jalapeno pepper with the seeds taken out.
