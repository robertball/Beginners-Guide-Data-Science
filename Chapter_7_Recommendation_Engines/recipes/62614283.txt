Title:
Skirlie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
50g of butter or dripping
1 onion - finely chopped
175g oatmeal
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Melt the butter in a pan and add the onion, frying gently to soften.
Stir in the oatmeal, season and cook gently for 10 minutes or so.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
Skirlie is a fabulous 0ld traditional Scottish dish. 
It's normally a side dish or a stuffing but can be used as a Main. It's great to use as chicken/turkey stuffing and is dead easy to make
