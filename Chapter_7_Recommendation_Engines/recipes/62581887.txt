Title:
Creamy Coconut Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz soft cream cheese
1 5 oz can cream of coconut
16oz tub cool whip

Directions:
Mix cream cheese & cream of coconut till well blended & fold in the cool whip. Chill several hours

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
This is a fun dip for summer pool parties! Serve w/ pineapple, apples & bananas & graham stix
