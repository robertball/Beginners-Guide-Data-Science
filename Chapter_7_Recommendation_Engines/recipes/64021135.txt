Title:
Cheesecake Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz. cream cheese, softened
3 tbsp. milk
1 tsp. vanilla
2 tbsp. powdered sugar
2 cups Cool Whip

Directions:
Beat cream cheese, sugar and milk until smooth then add vanilla and mix.
Fold in Cool Whip.
Refrigerate before serving.
