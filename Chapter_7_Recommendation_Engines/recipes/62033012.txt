Title:
Strawberry Blue Cheese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups diced strawberries
1 bag spring mix
1 8 oz container blue cheese crumbles
1/2 cup chopped toasted pecans
1/2 cup diced red onion
3 tbl raspberry vinegar
3 tbl balsalmic vinegar
3 tbl olive oil

Directions:
Dressing:
Whisk together raspberry vinegar, balsalmic vinegar, and olive oil.
Salad:
Combine spring mix, blue cheese crumbles, strawberries, toasted pecans, and red onion.  Pour dressing over all, toss, and eat immediately!

Submitter: 
CHRISPENTINA
Description: 
Unusual ingredients make this salad refreshingly different!  Even kids like it!
