Title:
Savory Onion and Pecan Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg yeast
3 cups bread flour
1 1/2 tsp sugar
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 cup and 1 TBS of warm milk
1/4 cup butter
3/4 cup pecan pieces
1/2 cup red onion, chopped fine

Directions:
Using your bread machine, place all liquid in bottom of insert. Place all dry ingredients on top. Bake on regular cycle (3 hour cycle) in Bread machine.

Submitter: 
oscar'smom
Description: 
A friend of mine gave this to me some years back. It makes a wonderfully addictive savory bread. The pecans give it a great texture along with it's slightly nutty flavor.
