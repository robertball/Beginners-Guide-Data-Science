Title:
Fresh Pepper and Onion Sausage Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 # pepper and onion sausage
3 lg. bell peppers(multi-colored), cut into 1-in. pieces
8 oz mushrooms, halved
1 lg yellow onion, cut into 1-in pieces
3 T vegetable oil
1 t oregano
Salt and pepper to taste
2 C Monterey Jack cheese, shredded

Directions:
Arrange sausage in single layer of a Dutch oven and cover with cold water.
Heat over high and bring to boil; gently boil for 8 min. With tongs, remove sausages; set aside. Drain water and wipe Dutch oven with paper towel.
Add oil to Dutch oven and set over med-high heat. When oil is hot, add peppers, mushrooms, onion, oregano, salt and pepper. Stir fry until onion is limp and just begins to brown, 5 to 10 min.
Cut sausages into 1-in thick pieces, reserving any juice. Add sausage and juice to vegetables.
Stir fry until ingred. are cooked through and flavors are blended, 5 to 10 min
Stir in cheese and serve immediately
