Title:
Creamy Tomato Garlic Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2T olive oil
1 lb precooked shrimp (tails off)
1 small onion, diced
5 cloves garlic, minced
1T fresh basil or 2tsp basil flakes
1 15oz can tomato sauce
1 15oz can Italian seasoned  diced tomatoes
1/4c cooking sherry
1/4c lemon juice
1 8oz pkg light cream cheese, cubed
1 16oz box ziti pasta
Parmesan cheese (to garnish)

Directions:
Prepare ziti pasta according to package directions.
In a large skillet, saute shrimp, onion, and garlic cloves in olive oil  over a medium heat until shrimp is hot and onions are clear-approximately two minutes. Remove shrimp and set aside.
Stir in tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, basil, lemon juice and sherry.
Simmer, covered for 5 minutes until bubbly.
Add cream cheese and stir.
Cover and simmer another 5 minutes until cream cheese is melted. Add shrimp back in and simmer another 1-2 minutes until shrimp is heated through.
Stir well.
Stir in precooked pasta.
Top with Parmesan cheese, if desired.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Kotz Walls
Description: 
This is our family's favorite pasta recipe. It is easy to make and uses items you have on hand.
