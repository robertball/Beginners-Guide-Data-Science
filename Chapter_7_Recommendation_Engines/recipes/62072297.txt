Title:
Dad's Crab Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pound Maryland Jumbo Lump crabmeat
1 1/2 tbsp mayonaise
1 tsp dijon mustard
dash of worchestershire sauce
parsley
1 packet of Old bay Crab Cake Seasoning

Directions:
Add all ingredients in bowl. Gently mix ingredients together as not to break up the lumps of crabmeat.Refrigerate for at least 2 hours so crabcakes hold together when you broil them. Broil 8-10 minutes on each side. Enjoy!!!

Submitter: 
KARYN821
Description: 
This is my dad's crab cake recipe that has earned him many compliments over the years! Best I have ever had! We are from Maryland and you can't beat our crab cakes!!
