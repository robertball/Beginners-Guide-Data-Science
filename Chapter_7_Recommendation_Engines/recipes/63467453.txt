Title:
Pico de Gallo Cornbread Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/944719.jpg
Ingredients:
Leftover Cornbread (1/2 Pan)
Left Over Pico de Gallo (1 1/2 cups)
1 can Cream of Onion Soup condensed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Crumble leftover cornbread in bowl.
Mix in condensed soup and leftover pico.
Toss lightly to coat all.
Place in shallow baking dish.
20 mins or until golden brown toasted on top.
