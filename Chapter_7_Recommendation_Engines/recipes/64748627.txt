Title:
Creamed Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons of olive oil
2 tablespoons of butter, divided
1  med. sweet onion, halved and sliced
5 to 6 medium sized crookneck squash
2 Tablespoons all purpose flour
1/2 cup milk
Kosher salt and freshly cracked black pepper, to taste

Directions:
Slice Squash into thin rounds,
Add sliced Squash in 3 QT pot, fill and cover squash with water until  Fully  covered, cook until Squash is soft,  Drain squash and water from pot:
Leaving Squash in colander
add 2 tablespoons olive oil,  and 1 tablespoon of the butter in he 3 qt pot,
In a 3 qt pan,  melt butter and olive oil  and onion, cook until transparent,
Off heat - add the flour and milk and stir until well blended.
Transfer  the cooked Squash into the 3 qt pan with the onion,
Mash the squash thoroughly  stir until well blended, stir in salt and pepper, cover and cook over medium-low heat, stirring occasionally, about 5 minutes, or until nicely thickened. Taste, adjust salt and pepper and serve.
