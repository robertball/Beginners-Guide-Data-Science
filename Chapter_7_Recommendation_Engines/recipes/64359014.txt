Title:
Basic Quesadillas Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
8 corn or flour tortillas
2 cups grated cheese
1 cup filling, room temperature (optional)**
Sour cream, for serving
Salsa for serving
Pickled jalape√±os for serving (optional)
**quesadillas are a great way to use up leftovers or used your creativity. suggestions: add a can of beans or can of chicken. other ideas include fresh spinach or corn.

Directions:
Heat a skillet over medium heat, and place 1 tortilla in pan; sprinkle with 1/4 cup grated cheese, 1/4 cup filling, if using, and another 1/4 cup grated cheese. Top with second tortilla, and cook until cheese is melting and the bottom of tortilla is golden brown. Flip, and cook until golden on both sides, the cheese is melted, and the filling hot.
Serve with sour cream, salsa, etc, if desired. Repeat with the remaining ingredients, serving each quesadilla hot from the pan. (If you are feeding a large group, keep the quesadillas warm in a 300 degrees oven on a baking sheet until ready to serve.)
