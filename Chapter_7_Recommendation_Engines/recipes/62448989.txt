Title:
Better than sex Kahlua Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups water
6 cups sugar
1 heaping cup Folgers instant coffee
1/4 cup vanilla
1/2 gallon vodka

Directions:
Put water, sugar and coffee in a large pot and bring to a boil.
Once it reaches a boil, reduce to medium heat for about one hour so it reduces (very important for the consistency).
Remove from heat, add vanilla and let it cool.
Once it has cooled to room temperature, stir in vodka, and store in your favorite container.

Submitter: 
Ally
Description: 
Here is a simple recipe to make your own kahlua at home, even the cheapest vodka will make a batch better than the real stuff!
