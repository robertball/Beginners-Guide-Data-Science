Title:
Hatch Chili Dirty Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1036831.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb mild breakfast sausage
1/2 lb bacon sliced into short strips
1 large onion
1 lb roasted mild hatch chilies
1 1/2 cup jasmine rice uncooked
Seasonings to taste
Cheese for top optional

Directions:
In large skillet brown sausage and bacon and drain.
Add in onion and saute.
Add water and bring to boil.
Season and taste.
Add rice and bring down to simmer for 15-20 mins.
Fluff and serve.
Cheese on top optional
