Title:
Fresh Fruit with BACARDI® Rum Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985393.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
8 fluid ounces BACARDI® Rum
2 tablespoons vanilla
Fresh fruit

Directions:
Mix egg, milk, BACARDI(R) Rum and vanilla in blender on a medium speed for several minutes.
Refrigerate overnight and then pour over fresh fruit.

Submitter: 
BACARDI® Mixers
Description: 
Serve your favorite fresh fruit in this creamy vanilla and rum sauce. This is perfect for entertaining, because you make the sauce the night before.
