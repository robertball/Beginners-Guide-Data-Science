Title:
Roasted Tomato Basil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs plum tomatoes sliced in half
1/4 cup Olive oil
1 T kosher salt
1 t black pepper
2 C yellow onions chopped
6 cloves garlic
2 T butter
28 oz centro plum tomatoes
4 C packed basil leaves
1 t fresh thyme
1 Q chicken broth

Directions:
preheat oven to 400.  toss olive oil, tomatoes and salt together and roast 45 minutes.  In a large stockpot on medium heat, saute onions and garlic with 2T of olive oil, butter for 10 minutes.  Add the remaining ingredients.  Add the oven roasted tomatoes including any liquid.  Bring to a boil and simmer 40 minutes.  Put in blender on coarse.
