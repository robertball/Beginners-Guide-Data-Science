Title:
One Dish Chicken and Rice Bake(boneless) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can(10 3/4 oz.)cream of mushroom
soup
1 cup water(for creamier rice,
increase water to 1 1/3 cups)
3/4 cup uncooked white rice
1/4 tsp. paprika
freshly ground black pepper
4 skinless, boneless chicken
breast halves(about 1 pound)

Directions:
In 2-quart shallow baking dish, mix soup, water, rice, paprika, and pepper. Place chicken over rice mixture. Sprinkle with additional paprika and pepper. Cover.
Bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink, and rice is done.
