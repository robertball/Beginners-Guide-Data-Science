Title:
Soy-Free Bourbon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. Chicken Breast (diced, raw)
1 tsp Garlic (Minced)
½ tsp Ginger
½ tsp Chili Powder
2 Tbsp. Honey
¼ Cup Ketchup
1 Tbsp. Balsamic Vinegar
2 Cups Apple Cider
¼ Cup Bourbon
1 Packet Concentrated Chicken Broth (I get mine at Trader Joe’s)

Directions:
In a large bowl, toss Diced Chicken with Garlic, Ginger, Chili Powder, Honey, Ketchup, and Balsamic Vinegar until well coated.
Move mixture to a pot and add Apple Cider, Bourbon, and Concentrated Chicken Broth.
Stir until all combined and covered.
Heat over medium low heat for 20-25 minutes stirring occasionally until chicken is cooked through (160*F).
Add salt to taste if desired.
Serve over rice.
Optional: Top with chopped green onion. You can also add cheddar cheese or sour cream if desired.
