Title:
I've Lost My Noodle Vegetarian Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Spaghetti Squash, skinned and sliced 1/4" thick
1 Jar of Your Favorite Spaghetti Sauce
2 Eggs
2 C. Cottage Cheese
2 C. Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
1/2 C. Parmesan Cheese
2 Tbsp Italian Seasoning
1 tsp salt

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Lightly spray 13x7 pan with olive oil cooking spray.
2. Mix eggs, cottage cheese, mozzarella cheese, spices and salt in small bowl.
3. Place spaghetti squash slices on bottom of pan.
4. Pour half a bottle of spaghetti sauce on top of squash.
5. Spread cottage cheese mixture evenly on top of sauce.
6. Cover cheese mixture with remainder of spaghetti sauce.
7. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top.
8. Cover with foil and cook at 375 degrees for 35 minutes.
9. Uncover and cook another 5 minutes until cheese topping is lightly browned.
