Title:
Cynthia's Keish Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Vegetarian Recipe
1/2 cup mushrooms
6 green onions
1 6 oz. can olives
1 medium size zucchini
1 branch of broccoli
1 4 oz. can green chilis
3 medium sized tomatoes
Grated carrots
Can use any vegetables you want and chop up in small pieces.
1 cube butter
10 large eggs
1/2 cup flour
Melt butter and add to ingredients in blender and blend.

Directions:
Put everything in a large bowl. Add 2 cups grated cheese and 1 small carton of cottage cheese.  Blend with egg mixture and pour into a greased 9x11 pan.
Bake at 425 degrees for 10 minutes and turn down to 350 degrees for another 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sylvia Wasden
Description: 
Yummy recipe for breakfast, lunch or dinner.  Can be warmed or even served cool.
