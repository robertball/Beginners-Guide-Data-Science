Title:
Tofu Pad Thai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce:
1/2 Tomato
2 TB Sugar
1/4 C Water
1-2 Garlic Cloves
Salt- To taste
Chili Powder- To taste
1 package Rice Noodles
1 Egg
1/2 package of Firm Tofu
1 TB Olive Oil
1 TB Sesame Oil
1/4 Cup Diced Onion
1/2 Cup Fresh Bean Sprouts
1/4 Cup Peanutes

Directions:
To Make the Sauce:
Finely dice the tomato, saving as much juice as you can
In a mixing bowl add the ingrediants and stir until well mixed, set aside
In Sauce Pan:
Boil 3 cups water
Add Rice Noodles and turn off heat
Let sit for about 5 minutes until tender
Drain and rinse with cold water for 30 seconds
In a large frying pan or wok:
Heat up 1 TB of Olive Oil and 1 TB of Sesame Oil
Add Onions
Add egg, scramble together
After the egg has hardened, add the diced tofu, making sure every side gets covered with the oil (for flavor)
When the tofu is brown on the outside add the peanuts and bean sprouts
Fry for 2 to 3 minutes
Add the Sauce, bringing it to a boil
Turn off the heat
Add noodles and mix
Serve Hot and Enjoy!

Description: 
This is tangy dish using rice noodles and tofu, sure to please anyone!
