Title:
susie's buttermilk pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup buttermilk
2 cup all-purpose flour
4 tablespoons white sugar
2 tablespoons vanilla
2 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 egg
4 tablespoons butter, melted
cooking spray

Directions:
Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large mixing bowl. Whisk egg and butter into buttermilk, vanilla. Pour the flour mixture into the wet ingredients and whisk until lumps are gone.
Heat a large skillet over medium heat, and coat with cooking spray. Pour 1/4 cupfuls of batter onto the skillet, and cook until bubbles appear on the surface. Flip with a spatula, and cook until browned on the other side.
