Title:
Bandstand Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBS. veggie oil
1-1/2 cup chopped onions
1-1/2 cup chopped red bell peppers
2 TBS. mild Mexican seasoning
1 clove garlic, minced
28 oz. tomato puree with tomato bits
15-1/2 oz. light red kidney beans
2 cups chopped cooked BUTTERBALL boneless doung turkey

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat until hot. Add onions and bell peppers, mexican seasoning and garlic. Cook and stir 4-5 minutes. Add tomato puree and beans; stir in turkey. Reduce heat to low. Simmer 5 minutes
