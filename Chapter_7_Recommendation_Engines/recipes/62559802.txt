Title:
Cranberry Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535602.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag of Ocean Spray (or comparable store brand) whole fresh Cranberries
1 whole Navel Orange- quartered (leave peel on)
1 C. Sugar

Directions:
Into a food processor place the contents of the whole bag of cranberries, the quartered orange (skin and all!) and the cup of sugar. Process on high for 30 seconds, then pulse to desired consistancy. Place relish into bowl, cover and chill in refrigerator. Serve cold.

Submitter: 
janaluwho
Description: 
Deee-lish dish!  So very easy to prepare and can be used as a side dish for those who are crazy about cranberries or as a tasty compliment to chicken, turkey or ham. My mother taught me this recipe and it's been a family favorite for over 50 years!
