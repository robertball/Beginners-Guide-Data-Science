Title:
Grilled Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 red peppers
Olive oil

Directions:
Rinse peppers and cut in half, remove stem and seeds and white bits.  Coat both sides with olive oil.
Place skin side down and grill 5 minutes on each side, over hot coals, turning once,   It's okay if the skin gets charred.
To peel, place in closed paper bag for 10-15 minutes and peel charred skin (Cool enough to handle).  Slice and serve.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
Easy to grill, easy to peel off skin.
