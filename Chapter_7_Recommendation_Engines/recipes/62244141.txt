Title:
Chocolate Delights Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 24 oz pkg microwavable chocolate flavored bark
1 cup creamy peanut buter
2 cups rice crispies
1 cup chopped pecans
2 cups miniature marshmellows
1 1/2 cups chocolate chips (real, not flavored choc)

Directions:
Place entire pkg of bark into a microwave safe bowl.  Microwave 90 seconds, then stir well, if not completely melted continue to microwave at 15 second intervals. Stirring well after each interval until smooth. Do not overcook.
Add 1 cup peanut butter and stirl until melted, add rice crispes and chopped pecans and stirr well, add marshmellows and chocolate chips last, stir well. Drop by spoonful onto wax paper, cool
