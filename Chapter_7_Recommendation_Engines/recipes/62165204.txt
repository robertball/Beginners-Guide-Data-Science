Title:
Ultimate No-Bake Peanut Butter and Fudge Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 premade graham cracker or chocolate crumb pie shell
1 cup of crunchy peanut butter
1 cup of Fudge frosting
1 regular size milk chocolate bar
1 regular size reeses peanut butter pieces or peanut butter cups chopped
1 cup of whipped cream

Directions:
Spread broken milk chocolate bar on bottom of pie shell
Mix 1 cup of peanut butter and 1 cup of fudge frosting and then layer on top of the chocolate in the pie shell
Spread reeses peanut butter pieces or peanut butter cups on top of pie filling
Top with 1 cup of whipped cream of choice
Grate or sprinkle choc bar or cocoa powder on whipped cream

Submitter: 
twgentry
Description: 
This is a combination of off the shelf products mixed together to make the ultimate flavor of Peanut Butter and Fudgie goodness.
