Title:
Italian pepper squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans refrigerated crescent rolls
1/4 lb salami, sliced
1/4 lb Swiss cheese, sliced
1/4 lb pepperoni, sliced
1/4 lb American chese, sliced
1/4 lb boiled ham, sliced
1/4 lb Provolone cheese, sliced
2 eggs
1/2 tsp garlic powder
12 oz jar roasted red peppers, drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Lightly butter 13&quot; x 9&quot; dish. Unroll one can of crescent rolls and press firmly into bottom of dish and 3/4&quot; up the sides. Pinch perforations to seal.
Layer meats and cheeses in order above.
In a small bowl, beat eggs and add garlic powder and pepper until well blended. Pour over meats and cheeses.
Place roasted peppers on top.
Unroll second can of rolls, firmly press perforations and lay on top of dish. Pinch edges to seal.
Cover with foil. Bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and back 15-20 minutes more, or until dough is deep golden brown.
Cool 15 min before serving. Cut into squares, serve warm.
