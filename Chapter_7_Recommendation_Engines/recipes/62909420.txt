Title:
Yellow Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoons Olive Oil
2 Shallots, minced
2 Cloves Garlic, pressed
1 tsp Tumeric
1/8 tsp Cyanne Pepper
2 1/4 Cups Brown Jasmine Rice, rinsed
3 1/2 Cups Chicken Broth
1 1/2 tsp Salt
2 T Red Pepper, diced

Directions:
Heat oil to shimmering.  Add shallot and garlic.  Cook until soft and fragrant.  Add spices and cook until they begin to toat, but not brown.  Add rice, broth, and salt.  Stir and simmer for 35 minutes.  Add red pepper and cook for an addtional 10 minutes.
