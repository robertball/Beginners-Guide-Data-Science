Title:
Spinach pinneapple salad. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag baby spinach (or arugula) leaves (4-6 oz)
1/3 cup praline pecans, finely chopped
4 oz pineable chuncks, finely chopped
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
1/4 cup low-sodium tangerine honey dressing (or poppy seed dressing)

Directions:
Place spinach in salad bowl
Chop nuts and pineapple
Add feta cheese & dressing
Toss and serve.
