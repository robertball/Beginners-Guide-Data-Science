Title:
Southwestern Rice and Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups cold cooked brown rice
1 large sweet red bell pepper, cut into narrow, 2in strips
1 large firm ripe avocado, diced
3 tablespoons EVOO
1/2 juice from lime
1 16ounce can pinto or black beans, drained and rinsed
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
2 scallions, thinly sliced
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients in a serving container and toss until thoroughly mixed.  Serve at once.

Submitter: 
Mary Rantanen
Description: 
When you have leftover brown rice, this is a super way to make use of it.  Serve with vegan quesadillas, nachos or greens
