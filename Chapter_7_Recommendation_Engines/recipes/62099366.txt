Title:
jewish cracker snacks/cracker blintzes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
40 saltine or ritz crakers
8 oz of cottage or farmer cheese
1/4 cup matzoh meal or flour
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 eggs, beaten
vegetable oil for frying

Directions:
mix cheese, sugar, salt and matzoh meal together.
place a small amount of filling on one cracker and top with another.
press down slightly
dip in egg on both sides
fry on medium heat in small amount of oil
brown on both sides
serve warm

Submitter: 
merri rosenthal
Description: 
my jewish grandma used to make homemade cheese blintzes which are crepes. i adapted this for my family as a snack. making crepes can be tedious. these are wonderful.
