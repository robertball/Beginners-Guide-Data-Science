Title:
Creamy Jalapeno Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans artichoke heart quarters
2 blocks cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 cup shredded Italian cheese
1/2 cup chopped or sliced jalapenos, mild or hot
1 tbs garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix all ingredients in a shallow baking dish. Bake for 15 minutes or until bubbly.

Submitter: 
mama2006
Description: 
A twist on artichoke dip with that spicy tang of jalapenos. Perfect for any party!
