Title:
Shepard's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. hamburger
2 c. cream of mushroom soup or 1 can
1 lg. bag mixed frozen vegetables
1 pkg. instant mashed potatoes (2 cups) or 4 potatoes
1 pkg. Cheddar cheese 4 oz
1 onion optional
parsley
garlic
frozen peas

Directions:
Prepare hamburger; drain fat. Mix soup and milk. Mix with hamburger. Prepare mix vegetables as directed. Place over hamburger mixture, then place prepared mashed potatoes, then cheese. Bake at 350-375 degrees until hot.
