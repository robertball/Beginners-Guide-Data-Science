Title:
Banana, Honey and Peanut Butter Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons of peanut butter
1 tablespoon of honey
1/4 banana finely sliced
2 slices of bread

Directions:
1. Simply spread the peanut butter and honey on each side of both slices of bread, and then stick the finely sliced banana slices on it evenly, and enjoy!
