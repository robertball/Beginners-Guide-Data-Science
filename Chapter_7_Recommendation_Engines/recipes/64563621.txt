Title:
A Perfect Pear-ing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small pear (I would do a Japanese apple pear)
5 Ritz crackers
1 oz colby & monterey jack cheese sliced
12 grape tomatoes
1/4 cup (1 oz) nuts
3 slices (1 oz) shaved honey ham

Directions:
Slice pear and cheese. Serve with crackers, nuts, tomatoes and ham.
