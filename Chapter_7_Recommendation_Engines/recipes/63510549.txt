Title:
Basic Stir-Fry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/958900.jpg
Ingredients:
Basic Stir Fry Sauce
Ingredients:
2-3 Tbs. soy sauce
1 1/2 tsp. sesame oil
1 tsp. minced garlic
1/2 tsp. minced ginger
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 Tbs. brown sugar
1 1/ 2 tsp. lemon or lime juice
1 tsp.cornstarch
Optional:
1 Tbs. rice wine or sherry

Directions:
1. Heat the sesame oil in a small saucepan.
2. Add the ginger and garlic and stir-fry 15-30 second over medium heat to bring out the flavor.
3. Add the chicken broth, soy sauce, brown sugar, and lemon juice.
4. Bring just to a boil, stirring.
5. Dissolve the cornstarch in 2-3 tsp. water and whisk into the sauce.
Simmer for 30 seconds, or until sauce thickens and reaches a full boil. Remove from the heat and set aside.
