Title:
Turkey Sausage Hashbrown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey sausage or 1 bag precooked turkey sausage crumbles
8 ounces sharp cheese, shredded
1 bag (20 ounces) refrigerated shredded hash browns
8 eggs
1/4 cup milk
2 tsp dry mustard
1/2 tsp onion powder
Salt & Pepper to taste (optional)

Directions:
If using raw ground turkey sausage, brown in pan until cooked through.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Spray a 13 x 9-inch pan with nonstick cooking spray.
Combine hash browns and shredded cheese in a large mixing bowl, mix well.  Mix in turkey sausage and set aside.  In a seperate bowl beat eggs, milk, onion powder, dry mustard, salt and pepper until smooth.  Add to hashbrown mix and stir thoroughly.
Spread casserole in baking pan, cover with aluminum foil.  Bake for 30 minutes, remove foil, continue baking 15 minutes or until egg is set.  You can allow to cool for a few minutes and dig in OR cool for 1 hour, cover and refrigerate.  Reheat individual plates on 70% for 1-2 minutes depending on portion size.

Submitter:

Description: 
An easy casserole to assemble during the weekend, cook and refrigerate for quick reheating during the week!  Can easily be altered for different breakfast meats or to add onions, peppers, veggies, various herbs.  Serve with fruit for a filling, delicious breakfast!
