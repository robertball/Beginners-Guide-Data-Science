Title:
secret carrot and raisin salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups carrots grated
3/4 cup  golden raisins
1/2 cup boiling water
1/2 cup white sugar
3/4 cup celery sliced
1/2 cup mayonnaise

Directions:
-add boiling water and raisins to sauce pan and let sit for 5 min
-add sugar to the raisins and cook at high heat till light golden in color (should be less then ten min)
-add carrots, celery, mayonnaise,and caremel raisins to a bowl and mix well
-chill  in refrigerator until cold
