Title:
Spiced Grouper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Grouper Fillets 6oz each.
2 Limes
1 TB Olive Oil
2 tsp Chillie/ Cayenne powder
1/2 tsp Turmeric
1 tsp Cumin seeds
Bunch of Coriander Leaves
Salt to taste

Directions:
Rinse the fish and keep aside. Make dressing with the juice of the limes, olive oil, salt, turmeric and chillie powder.
Marinate the fish with dressing for half an hour. Oil a heavy base pan, and place fish on high heat first, saving the dressing. After 5min put in the remaining dressing and lower the heat and sprinkle the cumin seeds. Cook for another 20 min, until fish is done. Sprinkle with chopped coriander leaves.
