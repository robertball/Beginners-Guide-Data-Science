Title:
Cashew Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts- cut in bite size pieces
1 Tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp cornstarch
3 Tbsp olive oil
1/2 cup cashew halves
1/2 lb snow peas (remove end and string)
1 tsp ginger root, minced
1/2 onion, chopped
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp vinegar
1/2 tsp cornstarch
1 Tbsp soy sauce
Dash of cayenne pepper

Directions:
1. Mix chicken pieces, 1 Tbsp soy sauce and 1 tsp cornstarch.  Set aside.
2. In a small bowl mix 1/2 tsp cornstarch, sugar, vinegar, 1 Tbsp soy sauce and cayenne pepper.  Set aside.
3. Stir fry cashews in 1 Tbsp olive oil at 240 degrees F until lightly brown. Remove.
4. Stir fry snow peas, ginger root, and onion in 1 Tbsp olive oil at 375 degrees F until cooked, but crisp.  Remove.
5. Stir fry chicken in 1 Tbsp olive oil until cooked through.  Add soy sauce mixture  and coat chicken.  Add snow pea mixture and cashews and mix well.  Serve with rice.
