Title:
Cinnamon Snails Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices soft texture whole wheat bead, or white bread
1/4 cup cream cheese
3 tbs sugar
1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Trim crusts from bread slices. Spread 1 tbsp cream cheese on each slice. Roll up bread slices with cream cheese inside. Cut into thirds. Stir together sugar and cinnamon till well mixed. Dip each roll into the melted butter then the cinnamon sugar mixture, don't dip the ends of the roll. Place seam down onto an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 350 for about 12 minutes or until crisp and light brown. Serve warm or cool.
