Title:
Mexican polvorones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 butter at room temp.
1/3 cup powdered sugar
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
3/4 cup finely chopped pecans
Powdered sugar for dusting

Directions:
Preheat 325 F
Lightly grease booking sheet
In large bowl using a mixer or spoon cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy
Add vanilla, salt, flour and nuts and mix well.
Roll dough in 3/4 inch balls between palms.
Place on sheet.
Space 1 1/2 inch apart
Bake 15-18 mins.
Cool slightly
Dust with powder sugar
