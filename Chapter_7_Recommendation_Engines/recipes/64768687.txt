Title:
Pearl Onions & Vodka Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg Pearl Onions (10 oz), peeled and halved
2 Tbsp. Olive Oil
1 16 oz Can Diced Tomatoes
Salt and Pepper to taste
1/2 Cup Vodka
1 Cup Cream

Directions:
Saute onions in olive oil over low heat.
When translucent, add tomatoes and cook to reduce liquid, about 15 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
Add vodka and stir to incorporate, then add cream. Let simmer about 5 minutes and serve.
