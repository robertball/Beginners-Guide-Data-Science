Title:
The Best Dry Roasted Chickpea Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can garbanzo beans
2 teaspoons of olive oil
1/4 teaspoon of salt or to taste
1 few grinds of pepper
or any seasoning you would like

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.  Drain and rinse chickpeas.  Pour into a baking dish and pat dry with a paper towel.  Bake for 22 minutes tossing/mixing the peas half way through.  Remove from pan and pour into a bowl mixing with the olive oil and salt and pepper (or other seasonings).  Toss back into the baking dish and cook for another 22 minutes tossing/mixing every 10 minutes.  Once the beans "rattle" they are done.

Submitter: 
thefmw
Description: 
This is a tasty, crunchy snack and you can customize your seasoning based on your preferences.  Dry roasting is the key.  Enjoy!
