Title:
Presidential Debate Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/896454.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb hamburger
1 T cooking oil (if needed)
1 c chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 16oz cans tomatoes, diced
1 c beef broth
1 4oz can diced green chiles, or diced jalapenos
3/4 T chili powder or ground red pepper
1 T brown sugar
2 t dried oregano
1 t ground cumin
1/2 t salt
2 cans Pinto beans (western style)

Directions:
DIRECTIONS:
1. Brown hamburger with onion and garlic. Drain off fat.
2. Stir in undrained tomatoes, beef broth, green chile peppers, chili powder, brown sugar, oregano, cumin & salt.
3. Add beans.
4. Bring mixture to boiling; reduce heat. Cover and simmer for 1 hour.
5. Serve with rice and cheddar cheese, or cornbread!

Submitter: 
Diana71
Description: 
A basic but tasty chili recipe that you can keep mild or season to a fire-hot! A great dinner, game day meal, OR perfect when watching political debates. :)  Add as a side - rice or cornbread, fritos or crackers, and definitely grated cheese, and you'll have a winner.  (Blue ribbon chili cook-off winner.)
