Title:
Caramel Apple Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2C butter
1 1/2C light brown sugar, packed
3/4C light corn syrup
14 oz can sweetened condensed milk
1t vanilla
1/4T cinnamon
pinch of salt
6 med. apples, sliced

Directions:
Place butter, brown sugar, corn syrup and condensed milk in saucepan and heat over med. heat, stirring to dissolve sugar, about 5 min.
Stir in vanilla, cinnamon and salt
Pour into serving dish.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Great for all types of fresh apples, and quick to make.
