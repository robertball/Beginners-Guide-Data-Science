Title:
Mexi-Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8.5 oz)  box Jiffy cornbread mix
1 egg
1/3 cup milk
1 (4.5 oz) can chopped green chiles
1 cup shredded Mexican blend cheese

Directions:
Preheat the oven according to box instructions for "Johnny Cake".
In a medium mixing bowl combine the cornbread mix, egg and milk. Then add the green chiles and shredded cheese; mix well.
Pour batter into a well greased 8 1/2x4 1/2 inch baking dish.
Bake according to box directions. Remove from oven and cut into squares and serve hot.
