Title:
Italian Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs beef roast
1 tbsp oregano
1 tbsp garlic salt
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 bottle italian salad dressing
2 cups water

Directions:
Combine ingredients and pour over roast in the crockpot.
Cook on low for 7 hours, shred and remove fat. Cook an additional hour in juice after shredding.
Serve on rolls with shredded mozzerella on top.
