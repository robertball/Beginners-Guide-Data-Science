Title:
West Coast Prawn Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Baby spinach and mixed greens
1 Avocado, sliced or chopped
Strawberries , sliced
1/2 cup Quinoa cooked and cooled
2 carrots, julienned
1 cup Candied pecans (mix with a couple of tablespoons of maple syrup and put on a baking sheet for 5 minutes at 350C)
1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
Grilled prawns (3 per person)
Citrus Honey vinagrette:
6 tbsp. grapeseed oil (i used this), safflower oil or olive oil
4 tbsp freshly squeezed orange juice
grated zest of 1 lime
2 tbsp freshly squeezed lime juice
2 tsp liquid honey
1/2 small shallot, minced
salt and freshly ground pepper

Directions:
Mix the salad ingredients, add dressing and put a couple of prawns on top of each plate and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Here's To Good Friends
Description: 
A delicious salad I had at Earl's Restaurant last summer so I tried to recreate.
