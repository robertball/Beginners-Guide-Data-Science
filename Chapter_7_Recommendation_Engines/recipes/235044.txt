Title:
Kahlua® Black Russian Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 1/2 fluid ounces Kahlua
1 fluid ounce ABSOLUT® Vodka

Directions:
Fill a rocks glass with ice. Add Kahlua and vodka and stir.
