Title:
Garlic Grated Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 zucchini squash
2 cloves garlic minced
3-4 Tablespoons olive oil
salt to taste
1/3 cup grated parmesan (optional)

Directions:
Mince garlic.
Grate zucchinis on a cheese grater.
Place grated zucchini in dish towel and squeeze out as much liquid as you can.  Discard liquid.
Saute garlic in olive oil on low heat, do not let brown. Add grated zucchini and saute with garlic for a few minutes over medium heat, add parmesan for last minute or two of cooking. Salt to taste and serve.

Submitter: 
Camille
Description: 
This is so delicious that even my little kids eat it up and yet it is still nice enough to be served as an elegant side dish.  Easy to make too!
