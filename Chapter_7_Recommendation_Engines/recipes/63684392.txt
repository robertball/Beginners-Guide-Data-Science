Title:
Strawberry Pie My favorite! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
In a saucepan pour one can sprite, add a box of strawberry jello, mix with wisk, then add 2 Tbs. cornstarch which has been mix in 1/4 c.water. Cook till it becomes clear, stirring constantly till clear. Cool, then add 2 cups sliced strawberries.Pour into a graham cracker crust pie shell. Cool in a refrigerator several hrs.Garnish with whip topping.
