Title:
Lazy smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz can triple cherry fruit cocktail
2 cups cherry pomegranate crystal light flavored water
1 cup ice

Directions:
Put ice in the blender and pulse it 4-5 times. Add fruit cocktail and water. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
iowa guy
Description: 
This recipe is quick, tasty and refreshing. Doesn't make a bad quick breakfast if you're in a hurry either.
