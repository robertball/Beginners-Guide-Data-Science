Title:
Dutch Green Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs dried green split peas
1 large ham bone
14 Cups Water
1 bunch celery chopped
1 bunch carrots chopped
3 large leeks sliced and rinsed well
2 onions sliced
1 smoked sausage
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
place all ingredients in a large heave bottomed stock pot.  Bring to a boil.  Skim the top and lower to a simmer - cook for about 2 hours remove ham bone and continue cooking stirring every so often for about another 2 hours or until peas and vegetables are soft
If you like a chunky soup remove  about 2 cups and puree and return to pot - if you like a smooth soup you can also puree it all.  then add the thinly sliced smoked sausage and any ham that remained on the bone taste and then season with salt and pepper

Submitter: 
Glenda Morrison de Kruif
Description: 
This is my mother in law Corries recipe straight from the heart of Amsterdam!
