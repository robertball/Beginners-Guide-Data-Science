Title:
Okra and Sausage Sensation Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. fresh okra (sliced into medium chunks)
1-2 lbs. smoked sausage cut into 1/4" slices (poundage depends on how meaty you want it)
1 lg. red onion coarsely chopped
2 bell pepper any color but green coarsely chopped
1 can petite diced tomatoes (well drained)
2 T minced garlic
2 T of olive oil
Optional Ingredient:
1 fresh jalapeno pepper thinly sliced

Directions:
Open can of petite diced tomatoes, pour into colander, set in sink and let drain while preparing other ingredients.
Wash okra, cut, and set aside in a bowl.
Chop/slice remaining veggies.
Slice sausage and place in skillet on medium high heat.  Cook until brown.
Transfer sausage to the bowl of okra.  (Do not turn off heat and place skillet back on burner)
Add oil to the skillet then add the minced garlic, onions,  and bell pepper.  Cook on medium heat stirring occasionally until the onion is translucent.
Add all other ingredients cover and simmer until okra just begins to soften (stir occasionally to ensure everything heats evenly).
Serve over rice and top with your favorite hot sauce.
Enjoy!
