Title:
Raspberry Yum Yum Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. flour
1/2 c. chopped nuts
1/2 c. butter
1/4 c. brown sugar
1 c. white sugar
2 egg whites
2 tsp. lemon juice
2 c. fresh or thawed frozen raspberries
8 oz. cool whip

Directions:
Mix flour, nuts, butter, and brown sugar like pie dough to thoroughly combine.
Bake at 325 on a rimmed baking sheet for 10 min, stirring often to break apart into crumbles.
Cool crumble thoroughly.
Sprinkle 1/2 of the crumble into a 9x13 casserole.
In a separate bowl, beat egg whites, sugar, lemon juice, and berries for 10 min.
Fold in cool whip.
Pour over nut mixture in casserole.
Sprinkle remaining nut mixture over top.
Freeze.
Cut into squares.
Before serving, remove from freezer and let stand for 10 min. to thaw slightly.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
This is a really unique hot weather dessert.  Fluffy frozen raspberry filling sandwiched between nutty crumb layers.  Perfect!
