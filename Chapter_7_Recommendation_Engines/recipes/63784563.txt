Title:
Vitamix: Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Roma tomato, 4.5 ounces
1 stalk celery, 2 ounces
6 baby carrots, 2 ounces
1 garlic clove, unpeeled
1/2 chicken or vegetarian flavored bouillon cube
1.5 teaspoons taco seasoning
2.5 cups water
add-in ingredients
1.5 cup frozen corn kernels, thawed
1 cup tortilla chips, lightly broken
1 cup packed fresh spinach

Directions:
Place tomato, celery, carrots, garlic, bouillon, taco seasoning, and water into Vitamix and secure lid
Select Variable 1
Turn machine on and slowly increase speed to variable 10 then to High
Blend for 7 minutes or until heavy steam escapes from the vented lid
Reduce speed to Variable 3 and remove the lid plug opening
Add corn, tortilla chips, and spinach through the lid plug opening, Replace lid plug
Blend for 10 seconds.  Serve immediately
