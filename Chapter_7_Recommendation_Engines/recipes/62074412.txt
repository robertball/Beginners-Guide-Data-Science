Title:
broccoli slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
salad: #1 bag cole slaw mix; 1# bag broccoli slaw;2 pkgs Ramen Beef Noodles;1Cup sliced almonds; 1 Cup sunflower seeds.
dressing: 1T. soy sauce, 1 Cup oil; 1/2 Cup sugar; 1/3 Cup vinegar

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together except  the Ramen noodles.  Break up noodles and brown in 1/4 C butter. Add beef packs to noodles. Add the noodles and toss just before serving

crunchy and refreshing. This salad goes fast at every gathering. This recipe is actually my friend Debbie's recipe which she got from a friend. In any event, it is surprisingly delicious and refreshing
