Title:
Farm Apple Pan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
--Crust--Egg Yolk Pastry--
5 c flour
1/2 t salt
1/2 t baking powder
4 t sugar
1 1/2 c lard or shortening
2 egg yolks
--Filling--
approx 5lbs. apples peeled and sliced
4 t lemon juice
3/4 c sugar
3/4 c brown sugar
1 t cinnamon
1/4 t salt
1/2 t nutmeg

Directions:
Combine dry ing. Cut in lard. Put egg yolks in a cup and stir, add enough water to make a scant cup. Sprinkle gradually oven dry ing. Roll half of pastry in large rectangle to line 10x15 inch pan. Place half of apples in pan. Combine remaining ing. and sprinkle half on top. Cover with remaing apples and rest of sugar mixture. Bake 400 for 50 min. When cool drizzle with 1c powdered sugar and 2c milk
