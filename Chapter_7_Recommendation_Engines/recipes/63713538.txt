Title:
Eggplant with Pesto Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cloves garlic, minced
1 large onion, large dice
1 eggplant, large dice
3 Roma tomatoes, large dice
8 oz mushrooms, sliced
Juice of 1 lemon
1 bunch of fresh oregano
Salt & Pepper to taste
Pesto sauce to taste

Directions:
Saute garlic and onion over medium low flame for 5 minutes.
Add eggplant and oregano and continue saute for 15 minutes.
Add tomatoes and mushrooms and continue saute for 5 minutes.
Add salt & pepper to taste.
Add pesto sauce to taste.
Continue cooking until eggplant is creamy.
Adjust seasonings and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
I was looking for something to do with an orphan eggplant in the fridge.  My wife said, this is yummy, so I'm hoping others like it as well.
