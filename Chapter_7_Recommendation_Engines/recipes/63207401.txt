Title:
Lavender Ginger Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/830427.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (8 ounce) package goat cheese, softened
2 tablespoons candied ginger, diced small
1/2 teaspoon dried culinary lavender flowers
2 tablespoons honey flavored Greek yogurt
1 tablespoon water

Directions:
Beat all ingredients together in a bowl until smooth. Cover and refrigerate overnight before serving.

Submitter: 
ewwiener
Description: 
Spread on toast, crackers, or pita bread.
