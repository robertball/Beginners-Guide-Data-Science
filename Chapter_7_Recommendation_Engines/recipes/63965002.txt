Title:
Dangerously Red Smoothy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup low-fat strawberry yogurt
1/2 cup cranberry juice
1 1/2 cups frozen strawberries
1 cup frozen raspberries

Directions:
Combine yogurt and cranberry juice in a blender.  Add strawberries and raspberries.  Blend until smooth.
