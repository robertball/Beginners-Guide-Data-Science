Title:
Mai Tai My Way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/910599.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 oz. light Rum
1/2 oz. Orgeat
1/2 oz. triple sec
2 oz. fresh squeezed orange juice
2 oz. pineapple juice
1/2 oz. grenadine
3/4 oz. Myers Original Dark Rum
1/2 slice of a Valencia orange
1 maraschino cherry

Directions:
Fill a glass (approx 1-1/2 cup) only half way with ice.
Pour in rum, orgeat, triple sec, fresh squeezed orange juice, pineapple juice then the grenadine.
Top with dark rum.
Garnish with a slice of orange and a maraschino cherry held together with a halved toothpick.
ENJOY!
