Title:
Grandma's Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 cup powdered sugar
2 t vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour
1 cup rolled oats
rainbow sprinkles

Directions:
1. Beat butter and sugar, add vanilla.
2. Add flour, then stir in oats.
3. Form into logs about 1-1.5 inches in diameter. Roll logs in rainbow sprinkles until coated all around. Wrap in waxed paper and chill overnight.
4. Slice cookies about 1/4 inch thick off of logs. Bake on cookie sheet at 325 degrees for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
LLIGETT
Description: 
Every summer when we went to visit grandma, my sister and I ran straight to the cookie jar looking for these favorites!
