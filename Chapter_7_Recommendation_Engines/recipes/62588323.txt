Title:
Terri's Egg Scramble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 recipe FROGHOPPER's Breakfast Sausage seasoning
1 lb ground pork or hamburger
1/2 onion, chopped
18 eggs
2 C. milk
1 1/2 tsp salt
3 C. shredded chedar cheese
3 C. seasoned croutons

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*.
Brown pork or hamburger with FROGHOPPER's Breakfast Sausage seasoning, and onion, drain grease, set aside.
Mix together eggs, milk, and salt. Set aside.
Place sausage into greased 9x13 pan. Sprkinle the cheese over the sausage, pour egg mixture over cheese, top eggs with croutons.
Bake uncovered 45-50 minutes.
This can be put together the night before.

Submitter:

Description: 
A great breakfast/brunch casserole dish that my MIL makes often, this is her recipe.
