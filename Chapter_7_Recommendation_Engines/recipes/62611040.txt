Title:
Bacon Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/566132.jpg
Ingredients:
3 slices bacon, crisply cooked and crumbled
6 large button mushrooms
1 clove garlic, minced
2 Tbsp celery, diced
1 Tbsp chives, chopped
2 Tbsp cream cheese, softened
2 Tbsp butter, divided
Salt & Fresh Ground Pepper to taste

Directions:
Clean and remove stems from mushrooms, enlarge cap cavity and then finely chop stems and mushroom pieces.
Melt 1 Tbsp butter in frying pan, saute mushroom stems/pieces for about 10 minutes on med-low, add garlic and celery and saute another 5 minutes, remove from heat and set aside to cool.
Add remaining butter to frying pan and saute mushroom caps until golden on top and bottom, remove from heat to parchment lined cookie sheet.
Mix chives with cream cheese, add to cooled mushroom mixture and mix well, season to taste with salt and pepper.
Divide mixture evenly amongst the 6 mushroom caps, patting stuffing down into cap gently.
Bake in a 375F oven for about 15-20 minutes.
Remove and cool 5 minutes before serving.
