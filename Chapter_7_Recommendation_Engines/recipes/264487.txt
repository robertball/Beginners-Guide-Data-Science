Title:
Tropical Green Power Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6824184.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups milk
1 cup chopped fresh pineapple
1 cup fresh spinach
1/2 cup plain yogurt
1/2 banana
2 tablespoons unsweetened coconut flakes
1 tablespoon ground flax seeds

Directions:
Combine milk, pineapple, spinach, yogurt, banana, coconut flakes, and ground flax seeds in a blender; blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Evan Chambers
Description: 
This yummy milkshake with tropical fruit is like a pina colada, but with lots of protein and a cup of spinach.
