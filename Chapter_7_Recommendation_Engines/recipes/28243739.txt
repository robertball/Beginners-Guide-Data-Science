Title:
Chocolate Cherry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 cups (11.5 oz.) Nestle Tollhouse Milk Chocolate Morsels, divided
1/4 cup granulated sugar
1/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup chopped candied cherries, maraschino cherries or rasins
3/4 cup milk
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 egg

Directions:
Combine flour, 1-3/4 cups morsels, granulated sugar, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt in large bowl.  Stir in cherries.
Combine milk, oil and egg in small bowl; stir into flour mixture just until moistened.  Spoon into greased or paperlined muffin cups.  Sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup morsels.
Bake in preheated 375 oven for 18-20 minutes until golden brown.  Cool 5 minutes; remove from pan.
