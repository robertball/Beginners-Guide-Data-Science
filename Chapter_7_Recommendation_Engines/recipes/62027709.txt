Title:
Quick chinese style Vermicelli (Rice noodles) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
250g vermicelli (rice noodle)
2 tablespoon oil
1 tablespoon soya sauce
1/2 tablespoon chilli sauce
salt and pepper
spring onions, chopped

Directions:
1.Bring a large pot of water to boil, add vermicelli. Cook until vermicelli is al dente; drain well.
2.  In a hot pan, add in oil and garlic.
3. Combine the vermicelli, oil with garlic, salt, pepper, soya sauce and chilli sauce in large sauce pot.
4.  Sprinkle spring onions on top.Ready to serve. Serve with ^cooked ham or chicken, optional.
