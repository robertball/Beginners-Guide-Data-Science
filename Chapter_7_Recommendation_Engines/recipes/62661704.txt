Title:
Spicy Roast Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/592322.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (5 1/2 - 6  pound) whole chicken
2 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 teaspoon salt (omit salt if brinning meat)
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
Brine chicken overnight (8 hours) in gallon water (1 cup salt and 1 cup sugar and any other spices you desire, ex. crushed pepper corns, sprinkle of oregano, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, bay leaf. Remove chicken from brine after 8 hours. Rinse chicken thoroughly inside and out under cold running water and remove any visible fat. Pat dry with paper towels.
Rub with olive oil. Mix the salt, pepper, oregano, basil, garlic powder, paprika and cayenne pepper together and sprinkle over chicken.
Place chicken in rotisserie and cook for 2 hours at 375 degrees or until internal temp reaches 180 (165 min remove and cover with foil to reach 180)
OR
Roast the chicken in the preheated oven on 450 degrees for 20 minutes. Lower the oven to 400 degrees F (205 degrees C) and continue roasting 40 minutes, to a minimum internal temperature of 180 degrees F (85 degrees C). Let cool 10 to 15 minutes and serve.

Description: 
Well-seasoned chicken blasted with high cooking heat for a speedy roast that still leaves the bird moist and flavorful.Modified from: Spicy Rapid Roast Chicken
