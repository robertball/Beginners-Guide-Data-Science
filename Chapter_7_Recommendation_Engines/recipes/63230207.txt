Title:
Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 scoops steamed, chilled white rice
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
3 teaspoons olive oil
1 cup diced cooked bacon, ham or chicken
1/8 cup soy sauce
1 (10 ounce) package frozen green peas, thawed
2 green onions, chopped
Sesame oil to taste

Directions:
Season eggs with pepper.
Heat olive oil in pan. Stir in rice; break up any clumps and toss to coat with oil.  Stir in meat, soy sauce, peas, and green onions. Stir and cook until heated through. Add beaten eggs; stir until cooked. Add SMALL amount of sesame oil, stir and serve.
