Title:
Cinnamon Nut Bundt Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Yellow cake mix, any brand is okay
1 small pkg. Instant Vanilla Pudding mix.
1/2 cup canola oil
1 tsp vanilla
5 eggs or Egg Beaters type product equal to 5 eggs
1 cup (8 oz.) fat-free sour cream
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
1 1/2 â€“ 2 cup chopped toasted nuts, (your choice, pecans, walnuts, slivered or sliced almonds, any kind you would like or a blend of nuts is okay)
1 cup sugar, brown sugar can be used, if you wish
3 Tbs. cinnamon (more if you want)
(Mix sugar and cinnamon together)
4 TBS maple syrup, divided, optional
2/3 cup coconut, divided, optional
extra cinnamon and sugar for dusting baking pan

Directions:
Heat oven to 300 degrees
Grease bundt pan then coat with cinnamon/sugar.
Mix first 6 ingredients together for 10 minutes in a mixer.
Pour half into the prepared bundt pan or Springform pan.
Sprinkle 1/2 of the sugar/cinnamon mixture and 1/2 of the nuts over the batter.
Sprinkle with 1/3 cup coconut, (half the coconut)
Drizzle 2 TBS maple syrup over sugar/nut layer.
Draw a knife through the mixture.
Pour remaining batter carefully over first layer and sprinkle with remaining sugar/cinnamon mixture and nuts.
Sprinkle with remaining coconut.
Drizzle with remaining maple syrup.
Draw knife through cake to swirl the sugar/nut filling through the cake.
Bake for 1 hour or until toothpick comes out clean.
Cool and remove from pan.
