Title:
Chicken Tamale Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked chicken, cut in 3/4 inch cubes(Great with leftover turkey too!)
1 cup canned Green Enchilada sauce
2 tablespoons salsa
1 teaspoon coriander
1 cup plain non fat yoghurt
1 8 oz. package of cornbread mix
1/3 cup water
5 green pitted olives sliced
1/3 cup grated Monteray Jack Pepper cheese

Directions:
Combine first 4 ingredients and 1/2 cup of the yoghurt in a glass or ceramic bowl.  Mix well.
Combine remaining 1/2 cup yoghurt, cornbread mix, and water in another glass or ceramic bowl.  Mix well.
Spray 9x9 glass baking dish with cooking spray.  Spread 1/2 of the cornbread mixture on bottom of pan. Top with chicken mixture, spread evenly. Sprinkle cheese and olives over chicken mixture.  Spread remaining cornbread mixture over top
Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes, until top is golden brown.

Submitter: 
laidback
Description: 
Easy and great alternative to all the work of hand wrapping chicken tamales, with no masa!
