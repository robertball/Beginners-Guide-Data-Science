Title:
No Bake Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk
2 cups sugar
1/2 cup butter
4 Tbsp. cocoa
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 tsp. vanilla
3 cups oatmeal

Directions:
Mix milk, sugar, butter and cocoa and bring to a boil. Boil for 1 minute, stirring constantly. Remove pot from heat and add peanut butter, vanilla and oatmeal. Mix and drop by spoonful onto cookie sheets or wax paper. Let cool.
