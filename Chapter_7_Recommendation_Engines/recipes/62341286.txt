Title:
Kibbles and Bits Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick oleo (margarine) or butter
1 c. peanut butter
6 oz. pkg. milk chocolate chips
8 c. Rice Chex Cereal
2 c. powdered sugar

Directions:
Melt together oleo, peanut butter and chocolate chips. Pour over Chex cereal and mix lightly. Put powdered sugar in a paper sack and add the cereal and shake. Store in air tight container.

Submitter: 
irishlass
Description: 
Great snack using Chex Mix. It has to be good if I remember it from the 1980's. The perfect crispy snack for those who have a weakness for peanut butter and chocolate. It is so easy and the kids love it!
