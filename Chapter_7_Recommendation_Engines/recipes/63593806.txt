Title:
Curried Pineapple Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-4 tbl red curry paste
2 (13.5 oz) cans coconut milk (unshaken)
2 lbs pork
2 tbl fish sauce
1/4 c sugar
1 red bell pepper, sliced
1/2 small onion, chopped
1-2 tbl cornstarch
2 cups fresh pineapple chunks

Directions:
Scrape out white paste off the top of the 2 cans of coconut milk and melt in a large pot on med-high heat until bubbling. Add 2-4 tbl curry paste (to your liking). Fry for about 1 minute.
Add the pork to the pot along with the rest of 1 can of coconut milk. Once it begins to bubble, turn down the heat to med-low and simmer for about 15 minutes.
Add onion, pepper, fish sauce, sugar and continue simmering for about 8 minutes. Add cornstarch to reserved coconut milk, mixing well, add to pot and simmer for an additional 3 minutes.
Turn heat to low and add pineapple, stirring occasionally for 5 minutes.
Serve over basmati or jasmine rice.
