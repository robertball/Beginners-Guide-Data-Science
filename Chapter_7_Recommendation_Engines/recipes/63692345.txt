Title:
Black Powder Rib Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 TBS salt
1 Tbs. Celery Salt
2 Tbs. Black Pepper
2 Tbs. Black Tea leaves ground to a powder in spice grinder
1/2 Tbs. cayenne pepper
1/2 Tbs. Lemon pepper
1/2 TBS garlic powder
1/ TBS. Dried Apple peel ground to a powder in spice grinder
1 TBS. dry mustard

Directions:
Mix together and place in closed container to keep on hand. Sprinkle and pat generously on pork ribs before placing in smoker or barbecuing

Submitter: 
Terry E.
Description: 
the Black tea adds a depth and Smokey secret undertone to your BBQ ribs.
