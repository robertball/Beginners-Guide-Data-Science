Title:
Insanely Crunchy  Easy Breakfast Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound bacon
1 cup flour

Directions:
Dip individual slices of bacon in flour, coating both sides.  Pan fry as usual till brown.  The bacon will continue to darken after it is removed.

Submitter: 
K. Lea
Description: 
This is a family favorite.  Easy to make and so crunchy.  It stays crunchy for a long time, but somehow disappears quickly.
5 servings of 3 slices in average pound.
