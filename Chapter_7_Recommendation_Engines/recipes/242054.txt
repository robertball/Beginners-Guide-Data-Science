Title:
Yogurt and Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2250020.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) container fat-free plain yogurt
1/4 cup granola
1 tablespoon light agave syrup
1 tablespoon flaxseed meal
1 pinch ground cinnamon, or to taste

Directions:
Stir yogurt, granola, agave syrup, flaxseed meal, and cinnamon together in a bowl.
