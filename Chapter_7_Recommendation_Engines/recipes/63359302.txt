Title:
Gyro Cucumber Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. plain Yogurt (not low-fat)
1 medium cucumber
1/4 tsp. white pepper or 1/2 tsp. black pepper
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. white sugar
2 tbsp. dill
2 peeled garlic cloves, finely minced
Juice of 1/2 lemon or equivalent lemon juice

Directions:
Place 1 medium cut up cucumber in a chopper (peel, remove pulp and seeds as they are very bitter), chop until finely chopped.
Spoon out on paper towels to soak up as much moisture as possible, place the chopped cucumber in a small bowl
Stir in minced garlic cloves, lemon juice, salt, sugar, pepper, and dill.
Blend in yogurt. Do not over mix.
Cover bowl, place in the refrigertor and let set undisturbed for 24 hours so the ingredients blend and the sauce thickens.

Submitter: 
judy2304
Description: 
Can be used on gyros, roast pork beef, fish and other seafood, and grilled vegetables. This sauce is excellent for making authentic gyros.
