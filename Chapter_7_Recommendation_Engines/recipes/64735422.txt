Title:
Spinach Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

5-7 pieces of bacon
3 hard boiled eggs,  cut in slices
1 can bean sprouts (optional)
Croutons
Tomatoes
1 cup salad oil
1 diced onion, chopped
1/4 cup wine vinegar
1/3 cup ketchup
1/2 cup sugar

Directions:
Mix dressing ingredients in jar and store  for several hours
