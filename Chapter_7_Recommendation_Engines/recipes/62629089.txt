Title:
Thai Pineapple Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp canola oil
1 Tbsp red curry paste
1/2 (13.5 ounce) can lite coconut milk
1/2 lb skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into thin strips
1/2 red bell pepper, julienned
1/2 green bell pepper, julienned
1/2 small onion, chopped
1 cup pineapple chunks, drained

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet over med-high heat.
Add curry paste, cook until it starts to foam. Add chicken.
When chicken is about halfway cooked add coconut milk. Cook about 5 minutes more, or until chicken is cooked through.
Add veggies, cook 3-4 minutes more being careful not to overcook.
Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Brie Kress
Description: 
My version of this recipe is a little lighter, a little milder, and omits the fish sauce, as I found it to be really overpowering.Modified from: Thai Pineapple Chicken Curry
