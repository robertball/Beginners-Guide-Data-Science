Title:
Lemon Italian Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cookies:
3 eggs, beaten
3/4 cup milk
2 1/2 tsp. lemon extract
1/2 cup butter or margarine
4 cups flour, sifted
2 tbsp. baking powder
1 cup sugar
Lemon Icing:
1 1/2 cups confectioner's sugar
3 tsp lemon extract
Enough milk to make a thick icing (start with a tbsp)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
Cream together butter and sugar; beat in eggs and milk.  Add extract.
Sift together dry ingredients and add to creamed mixture.
Roll into 1" balls and place on well-greased cookie sheets.  Bake 12-15 minutes.
When cool, dip into or drizzle with lemon icing.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
Lemon dough is rolled into balls and baked, then dipped in a powdered sugar/lemon icing.
