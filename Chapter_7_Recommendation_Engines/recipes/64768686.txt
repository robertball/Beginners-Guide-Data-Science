Title:
Corn-in Corn Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box 8.5 oz Jiffy Corn Muffin Mix
1 can 14.75 oz Green Giant Cream Style Sweet Corn
1 egg

Directions:
Mix ingredients in large bowl until consistent
Fill muffin liners to 2/3 full
Bake for 20-25 minutes or until inserted toothpick comes out clean
Optionally, sprinkle with cheddar cheese during final minute of baking and/or add chopped jalapenos to batter before baking

Submitter: 
Stephen Enochian
Description: 
Cornbread is a classic, but this twist makes it a little sweeter and puts tangible corn pieces in to enjoy!
