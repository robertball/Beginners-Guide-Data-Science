Title:
Steamed Shrimp and Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup jasmine rice, uncooked
1 pound raw medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
1/4 cup green onion, chopped
2 tablespoons cilantro, chopped
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 teaspoon fresh ginger, finely chopped
2 tablespoons sesame oil
12 ounces fresh green beans
1 whole red bell pepper, julienne
2 cups shiitake mushrooms, julienne
2 cups bean sprouts (optional)
1/2 cup oyster sauce
1/3 cup cashews (optional)

Directions:
Cook rice according to package directions.
Combine shrimp, green onion, cilantro, garlic, ginger and sesame oil in a large bowl; set aside.
Heat an inch of water in a wok or steamer pan to a rapid boil.
Layer beans in steamer basket, cover and steam about 2 minutes.  Add bell pepper and mushrooms, cover and steam another 2 minutes.
Using a second steamer basket or removing the vegetables from the first basket, arrange the shrimp mixture in the steamer basket and then palce steamed vegetables on top.  Cover an steam for 5 minutes or until shrimp is cooked through.  Carelfully layer bean sprouts on top, cover and steam another 1 minute.
Combine cooked vegetables, shrimp, oyster sauce and cashews in a serving bowl.  Toss lightly.
Serve over steamed rice.
