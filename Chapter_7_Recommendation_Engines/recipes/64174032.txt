Title:
Southern Peanut Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1478186.jpg
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1 cup Agave Nectar
1/2 cups light corn syrup
1/4 cup butter, melted
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1-1/2 cups chopped dry-roasted salted peanuts
9-inch unbaked deep dish pastry shell

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375*
Beat eggs until foamy. Add sugar, Agave Nectar, syrup, butter, salt and vanilla; continue to beat until thoroughly blended. *Stir in peanuts. Pour into unbaked pastry shell. Bake in a 375Â° F. oven for 50 to 55 minutes. Cool completely on wire rack before cutting. May be garnished with whipped cream or ice cream. Makes 8 servings.

Submitter: 
Tracy Ann
Description: 
This is a great alternative to Pecan Pie at the holidays. It's almost like a peanut-brittle taste and not as heavy as a Pecan Pie. A quick and easy pie.
