Title:
Sasser Family Oven Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
4 potatoes, chopped
2 carrots, chopped
1 onion, minced
1 (46 fluid ounce) bottle tomato-vegetable juice cocktail

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
In a large oven-proof pot, combine beef, potatoes, carrots, onion and vegetable juice.  Cover and bake in preheated oven 1 1/2 hours.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is my favorite stew in the world! I used a basic stew recipe and added some touches of my own to it.  My husband, our friend Jeff and I love it!
