Title:
Bourbon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs Boneless Chicken Thighs
1/4 cub Soy Sauce
2 tbsp Rice Vinegar
1/4 cup Bourbon
1/4 cup Brown Sugar
1 Scallion
1 tsp Powdered Ginger
2 Garlic Cloves
2 tbsp Peanut Oil

Directions:
Coarsly chop the scallion and mix all but the chicken and peanut oil together in a bowl and wisk until well blended.  Set aside.
Dice chicken thighs into bite sized pieces and saute in very hot pan or WOK with the peanut oil until lightly browned on the outside.
Add liquid mixture to the chicken and reduce heat to medium.
Cook this uncovered until liquid is reduced to a glaze on the chicken bites and chicken is fully cooked.
Serve with you choice of side dish.  Fried rice or LoMein noodles work well.
