Title:
Marinated grilled pork tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Marinade Ingredients:
2 (3/4 lb) pork tenderloins
1/4 c honey
1/4 c soy sauce
1/4 c oyster sauce
2 T brown sugar
1 T plus 1 teaspoon fresh gingeroot
1 T minced garlic
1 T catsup
1/4 t onion powder
1/4 t ground red pepper
1/4 t ground cinnamon
fresh parsley sprigs

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large ziploc bag.  Add tenderloins and coat them with marinade.  Marinate for up to 24 hours.  The longer the better!
Cook over hot coals for 20 - 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Donna Leigh
Description: 
This is a wonderful tenderloin that tastes best when marinated for 24 hours.
