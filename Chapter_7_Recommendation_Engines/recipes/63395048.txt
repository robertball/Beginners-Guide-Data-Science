Title:
cheesy potato chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion (chopped)
4 Tbsp butter
3 Tbsp flour
6 medium red potatoes
3 Cups milk
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
1 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 can Cream of Chicken soup (10.5oz)
1 Cup potato stock (reserved from potatoes)
1-2 Cups shredded chedder cheese

Directions:
Cut potatoes into 1/4 inch cubes. Boil in large pot until you can stab with a fork. While potatoes are boiling, in another large pot, melt butter and add chopped onion.  Cook over medium heat until onion becomes translucent approx 3-4 minutes.  Add flour to onion and butter. Once thickened add milk, crushed red pepper, black pepper and garlic powder. Whisk together until milk begins to thicken. Continue cooking over medium heat. Once thickened add 1 cup reserved potato stock, cream of chicken soup and chedder cheese.  Once cheese is melted thoroughly add cooked potatoes and simmer until heated through.
