Title:
Chocolate Cherry Cake II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1117880.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package chocolate cake mix
1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling
1 teaspoon almond extract
2 eggs
1 cup white sugar
5 tablespoons butter
1/3 cup milk
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix with a spoon (not an electric mixer) the cake mix, cherry pie filling, eggs, and almond extract  until moistened. Spread into 9 x 13-in greased pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 25 to 30 minutes.
To Make Frosting:    Bring sugar, butter, and milk to a boil. Boil for 1 minute.   Add chocolate chips; stir until chips are melted. Pour frosting over warm cake. Cool.

Submitter: 
Roxanne Shepphird
Description: 
This easy recipe makes a great-tasting chocolate cake with chocolate frosting using chocolate cake mix and cherry pie filling.
