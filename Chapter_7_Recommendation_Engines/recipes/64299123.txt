Title:
chocolate cherry crock pot cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1974660.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box red velvet cake mix, 2 eggs, 1/2 cup water, 1/2 cup sour cream, 1 small pkg chocolate fudge instant pudding, 1 cup semi sweet chocolate chips, 1 can cherry pie filling.

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well with mixer except chips and pie filling. Gently stir in chips and pie filling. Pour into greased crock pot and set on low. Cook until set or for about 4 hours.

Submitter: 
beth toz
Description: 
This makes a great Valentines dessert. The richest cake you will ever taste. No need for icing. Scoop and serve hot with ice cream.
