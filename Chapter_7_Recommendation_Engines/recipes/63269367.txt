Title:
Red Velvet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. shortening
2 eggs
1 t. vanilla
2 T. cocoa
2 C. flour
1 t. salt
1 c. buttermilk
1 t. vinegar
1 t. baking soda
1 1/2 c. sugar
2 oz. red food coloring

Directions:
Cream shortening, sugar, eggs, vanilla, and coloring.
Add cocoa alternately with buttermilk, then beat. Mix baking soda into the vinegar and pour into the batter.
Bake at 350 degrees until done, about 45 mins.
