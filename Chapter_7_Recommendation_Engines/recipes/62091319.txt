Title:
Sausage Balls in Apple Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. sausage
16 oz. jar of apple butter

Directions:
Form sausage into 1-inch balls and brown until cooked.  Marinate overnight in apple butter.  Simmer until apple butter is thoroughly heated before serving.
