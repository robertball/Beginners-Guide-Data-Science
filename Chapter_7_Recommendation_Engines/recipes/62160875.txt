Title:
Kentucky Hearty Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 15-16 ounces cans pork & beans
1 15-16 ounce can tomato sauce
1 15-16 ounce can diced tomatoes, drained
1/4 cup BBQ sauce
2 Tablespoons Liquid Smoke
1 Tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup maple syrup
1 heaping Tablespoon of yellow prepared mustard
1 medium onion, diced
1 medium green bell pepper, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
6 slices bacon, fried, drained, and crumbled
1/2 cup cubed ham
1 Tablespoon red pepper flakes, optional
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Pretheat oven to 350 F.
Drain pork and beans in a colander, and discard the cubed fat pork pieces.
Rinse under cold water and drain well.
In a medium bowl add all ingredients, mixing well, and adjusting seasonings to taste.
Pour into a 3 quart glass baking dish.
Bake for 30 minutes, or until thick and bubbly.
Let rest for 5 minutes before serving to allow it to thicken.

Submitter: 
JOYCE
Description: 
Pork & beans gets a great makeover in this family favorite that accompanies a great BBQ'd meal that is meaty, slightly sweet, slightly bold and simply easy to make. A great dish to take to potlucks. Bet that your pan will come back empty!
