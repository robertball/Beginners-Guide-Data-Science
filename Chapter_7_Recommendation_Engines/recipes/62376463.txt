Title:
Pixie's Tender Chicken Boobie Nuggets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken boobs
1 egg, beaten
1 cup bread crumbs
2 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt
freshly ground black pepper
some plain flour
3 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
Rinse chicken boobs, pat dry, slice into strips or nuggets and season with garlic powder, pepper and salt to taste.
Place the beaten egg in a small bowl.
Dredge the chicken boob strips lightly in flour, dip in the egg, and coat liberally with bread crumbs.
Heat the oil in a medium skillet over low to medium-high heat.
Fry the chicken boob strips 5 minutes per side, or until the breading appears well browned.
When done, remove from skillet and drain on absorbant kitchen paper.
Serve with a sauce of freshly ground black pepper, lime juice and salt.

Submitter: 
Devi Vanhon
Description: 
This is my very own creation! These nuggets remain moist and tender. You probably have all the ingredients in your kitchen to make this. Serves two.
