Title:
Cindy's Snappy Sensational Superfood Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 yellow potatoes (such as Klondike GoldustÂ®), cubed
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
2 large carrots, chopped
1 can (14 oz) tomatoes
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 onion, chopped
3/4 cup dry lentils
water, or amount to cover (or use chicken broth)
Cindy's Magical Motorvatin' Spice Mix:
1/4 teaspoon chopped fresh basil
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/8 teaspoon ground turmeric
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/8 teaspoon ground ginger
sea salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine the potatoes, spinach, carrots, onions, garlic, tomatoes, and lentils in a slow cooker. Pour enough water or broth over the mixture to cover.
In a bowl, stir the fresh basil, chili powder, cinnamon, dried basil, paprika, turmeric, cayenne pepper, ground ginger, sea salt, and black pepper; stir into the slow cooker.
Cover the cooker, and cook on High for 8 hours.

Submitter: 
Colleen Robertson Curtiss
Description: 
Root vegetables and lentils cook all day in a slow cooker with a mixture of spices to deliver a comforting, vegan-friendly soup perfect for a chilly fall dinner.Modified from: Cindy's Snappy Sensational Superfood Soup
