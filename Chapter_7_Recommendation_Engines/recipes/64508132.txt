Title:
Slow Cooker Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 pound hot Italian sausage
1/2 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1 cup bread crumbs
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
2 eggs
1/2 cup whole milk
1/2 cup chopped parsley
2 jars of your preferred sauce

Directions:
1) Combine all ingredients but sauce in a mixing bowl
2) Roll into golfball sized meatballs
3) Coat bottom of a slow cooker with sauce
4) layer meatballs and sauce
5) Cook on high for 2-2 1/2 hours, until meatballs are cooked through.

Submitter: 
Galen Dobbs
Description: 
spicy, luscious meatballs that are especially wonderful slow cooked in arrabiata sauce.
