Title:
Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup shredded cheddar cheese
½ small onion  chopped
10-12 slices of bacon, crisp and crumpled
2 heads of broccoli, cut in bite size pcs
½ cup mayonnaise
¼ cup sugar
1 tablespoon vinegar

Directions:
In a bowl add cheddar cheese, onion, bacon and broccoli
Blend together in a small bowl mayonnaise, sugar and vinegar. Mix dressing into other ingredients.Eat chilled.

Submitter: 
Ursula
Description: 
Best cold salad ever! And different from a pasta salad.
