Title:
MiMi's Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken cooked and cut into tiny, bite-sized pieces.
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of celery soup
2 cups frozen vegetables or 1 can of veg-all
1 cup flour
1/2 cup melted butter
1 cup milk

Directions:
Combine the chicken, soups, and vegetables together.  Spread in a greased 9 x 13 dish.  Whisk together the flour, butter and milk until smooth.  Pour over the chicken mixture.  Bake at 350 for 45 minutes or until the edges of the topping are golden brown.

Submitter: 
Tiffany
Description: 
This is a very easy chicken casserole passed down through my family from my surrogate grandmother, Mimi.  It has a creamy filling with chicken and vegetables and a buttery crust that is my personal favorite of the dish.  Hope you enjoy!
