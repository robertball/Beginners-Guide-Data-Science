Title:
Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups white sugar
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground cloves
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/8 teaspoon Cayenne
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 1/4 cups vegetable oil
4 eggs
3 cups grated carrots (1 Lb)
1 cup chopped walnuts
=========================================
Cream Cheese frosting!
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup butter, softened
3 cups sifted confectioners' sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease one 9 or 10 inch tube pan.
Combine the sugar, cloves,Cayenne, flour, salt, cinnamon, soda, oil. With an electric mixer beat in the eggs one at a time.
Stir in the carrots and the walnuts.
Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour 20 minutes. Note: this cake can be made in two 9 inch layer cake pans just change the baking time to 40 minutes.
To Make Frosting: In a medium bowl, cream together the cream cheese and butter until creamy. Mix in the vanilla, then gradually stir in the confectioners' sugar. Store in the refrigerator after use.
