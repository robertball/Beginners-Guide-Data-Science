Title:
Apple Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Store bought pie crust
4 honey crisp apples - about 6-8 oz. each
1/4 cup + 1 tbsp. dark brown sugar
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. salt
12 tbsp. butter - softened
1 egg
1 tsp. water
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Peel and core apples then cut in half crosswise.
Combine 1/4 cup brown sugar, cinnamon salt and nuts if using.
Combine 1 tbsp. brown sugar with butter.
Rub each apple with the sugar cinnamon mixture.
Work pie crust into 8" square pieces.
Place an apple half in the center of the dough and fill core with some of the butter/sugar mixture.
Pull dough around apple the create a purse and gently tie corners together with butcher's twine.
Place in baking pan lined with parchment.
Lightly beat egg and water then brush dough with mixture.
Bake about 25 minutes or until dough is golden brown and apples are cooked through.
Serve warm with ice cream or caramel sauce if desired.
