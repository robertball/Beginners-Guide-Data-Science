Title:
Herb infused pork chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 center cut pork chops
1tsp season salt like emerils
2tsp dried thyme
2tsp dried sage
2tsp dried basil
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/4 Rosa wine
2tsp crushed garlic
1 med onion slice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Season chops on each side with the season salt, thyme, sage, basil and garlic.  Place in baking dish.  Pour wine and chicken broth along the side of the chops, keeping as much of the seasoning as possible on top of the chops.  Slice onion and top.  Cover with foil and bake

Submitter: 
prell2k4
Description: 
These are very tasty moist pork chops.  The sauce is great over rice or potatoes.  You can thicken it if you like.
