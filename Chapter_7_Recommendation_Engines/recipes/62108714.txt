Title:
Mock Orange Julius Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c. Orange Juice
1/2 c. milk
1/3 c. Powdered Sugar
2 tsp. Meringue Powder
1/2 tsp. coconut extract (optional)
8 ice cubes

Directions:
Place ice cubes in blender.
Put remaining ingredients in blender.
Mix for approximately 30 seconds or until ice is crushed and drink is frothy.
Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
Jessica Kimble
Description: 
I've been making this recipe since I was a child, it's very cool and refreshing! The powdered sugar and meringue powder are the trick to the authentic taste in this recipe.
