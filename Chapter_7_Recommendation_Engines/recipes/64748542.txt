Title:
Cream Cheese and Jam Grilled Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices bread
Cream Cheese
Jam/jelly of you choice
Butter
Cooking spray

Directions:
Pre-heat a pan or griddle to medium high heat.
Coat with a light coat of cooking spray.
Spread a generous amount of cream cheese on the front of one side of one slice of bread
Coat one side of the other slice of bread with your choice of jam/jelly
Put the two slices of bread together with the cheese and jam facing each other
Coat the back sides of each slice of bread with butter
Lay the sandwich on the hot griddle/pan and brown one side.
Now flip the sandwich and brown the other side
remove from heat, slice in two and enjoy.

Description:
