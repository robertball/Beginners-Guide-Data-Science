Title:
Hula's Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup coconut oil or butter
1/2 tsp vanilla
2 eggs
3 really ripe bananas mashed
2 cups flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda

Directions:
Grease loaf pan.
Set oven to 325.
Mix 1/2 cup coco oil OR butter + 3/4 cup sugar + 1/2 tsp vanilla
Add 2 eggs and mix some more
Add 2 bananas and get your mix on some more
Sift dry ingredients 2 cups flour + 1tsp salt + 1 tsp baking soda & GENTLY blend
