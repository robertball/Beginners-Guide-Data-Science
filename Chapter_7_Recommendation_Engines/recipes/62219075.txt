Title:
Russian chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar apricot jam
1 bottle russian salad dressing
1 package onion soup mix
1 chicken ( cut up) or 20 chicken wings

Directions:
Place chicken in greased casserole dish. Mix ingredients in bowl and pour over chicken peices. Cover and bake for 1 to 1 1/2 hours or until tender.

Submitter: 
C.Lynn
Description: 
Tangy chicken served with mashed potatoes and favorite veggie.
