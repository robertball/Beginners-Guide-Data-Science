Title:
Sweettreat's Pork Chili Verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 to 3 lbs lean pork, 1 inch cubes
1 large onion, chopped
1 large bell pepper, chopped
4 garlic cloves, minced
2 jalapeno's, diced
8 ounces Herdez Salsa Verde
2 teaspoons oregano
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
2 teaspoons rubbed sage
1 teaspoon cumin
black pepper to taste
14.5 ounce can chicken broth
4 tablespoons corn starch
6 to 8 Burrito size tortillas
Colby Jack cheese
Sour Cream (optional)

Directions:
Cut the pork into one inch cubes. Chop onions, garlic, bell pepper and jalapeno's add to the crock pot. Add seasonings, Herdez salsa verde and chicken broth. Cook on low for 8 hours or high for 4 hours. Remove one cup of the broth and mix in 4 tablespoon cornstarch add to the crock pot and mix and cook for one hour or until the broth thickens. With a slotted spoon remove the meat trying to remove as much of the liquids as possible. In a frying pan add enough oil to cover the bottom of the pan. Fill large tortillas with the meat mixture and cheese. Place in frying pan and brown both sides of the burrito about two minutes each side. Remove and place on a plate covered with a paper towel. Place burrito on plate cover with Chili Verde sauce and cheese. Enjoy!
