Title:
Chili Cheese Waffle Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1054102.jpg
Ingredients:
22 oz. pkg Ore-Ida Waffle Fries
2 C prepared chili
1 C shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 C diced onion

Directions:
Prepare Waffle Fries according to package directions.  Warm chili.
Put waffle fries on plate.  Top with warmed chili, shredded cheddar, and onions.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
Waffle fries are delicious covered with your favorite chili!  Cheese, onions, and any other favorite toppings finish of this delicious treat.
