Title:
Ricotta Fluff Stuff Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 large container of ricotta cheese
1 box SF Jell-O instant cheesecake pudding
dollop or two of sour cream (to taste) bowl.  Warning: Walmart's ricotta is very grainy in texture and never mixes to anything smooth and creamy. Tasty, but strange. BTW - this is even good with the grainy Walmart ricotta!

Directions:
A little bit of milk to lighten it up (about a cup-add last and gradually) Mix it all up and enjoy however you want it. I love it with strawberries! It's a great fruit dip or just eating straight out of the
