Title:
Chipotle Mayo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/402212.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayo (or miraclewhip)
6 tablespoons of dijon mustard (my favourite has roasted garlic and a hint of beer!)
3 teaspoons of chipotle hot sauce
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
black pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingrediants in a small serving dish.
Spread on your favourite sandwich or use as a dip with your favourite veggies or yam fries.
Refrigerate leftovers immediately.

Submitter: 
Alexis May Tanner
Description: 
A spicy mayonaise that's great as a dipping sauce for yam fries or veggies or as a spread on your favourite sandwich. Try it with a steak wrap or breaded chicken on ciabatta!
