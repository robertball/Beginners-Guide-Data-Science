Title:
Pork and Stars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Chicken and Stars soup
1/2 cup rice
1 14 oz can chicken broth
1 pork tenderloin, sliced into medallions
salt and pepper, to taste
flour (optional)
butter

Directions:
Prepare 9x13 pan with non-stick spray.  Combine soup, rice and broth in prepared pan.  Cover with foil and place in 325F oven for approximately 15 minutes.  Meanwhile, season pork with salt and pepper.  If desired, dredge in flour.  Brown in skillet with a small amount of butter.  Gently place browned pork on top of rice.  Cover and continue baking 40 minutes until rice is tender.
Hope husband is being cooperative and willing to eat it.

Submitter:

Description: 
My mom always made this with pork loin chops.  My family prefers it with pork tenderloin.
