Title:
Big Ray's Slow Cooked Country Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1129413.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup orange juice concentrate
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup white wine
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
6 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp salt
1 tsp ground black pepper
1 tsp dried rosemary
2 medium onions, sliced
3 lbs boneless country style pork ribs

Directions:
In a medium bowl, whisk together orange juice concentrate, vegetable oil, white wine, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, salt, ground black pepper, ginger and rosemary.
Place ribs in a large, shallow dish, and pierce several times with a fork. Pour 1/2 the marinade over ribs, reserving remainder. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator 8 hours, or overnight, turning once.
Lay onion slices on bottom of slow cooker.  Remove ribs from marinade and place on top of onions.  Pour remaining marinade over ribs.  Cook on low for 8 hours.
