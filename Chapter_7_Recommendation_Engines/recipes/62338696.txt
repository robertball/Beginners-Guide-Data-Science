Title:
Aunt Beth's Pineapple Soufflé Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/389926.jpg
Ingredients:
5 slices bread, cubed
½ Cup sugar
1/3 Cup margarine or butter
4 eggs
¼ tsp cinnamon
1 large can crushed pineapple (20 oz, I think)

Directions:
Melt butter in oven in baking pan.
Mix all other ingredients and pour over butter.
Bake 30 minutes at 350.
Serves 6

Submitter: 
CasaCullen
