Title:
Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15oz can chickpeas (garbonzo beans), drained
4 T. lemon juice
4 T. Roasted Tahini (sesame seed paste)
2 T. hot water
1/2 tsp. salt
2 garlic cloves
Optional:
1/8 tsp. each of cumin, cayenne pepper, and paprika
1 T. each of olive oil and parsley

Directions:
Put first 6 ingredients in food processor or blender for about 1 1/2- 2 minutes or until smooth. You can sprinkle optional ingredients on hummus before serving. Keep refrigerated.

Submitter: 
JOLLYGREEN23
Description: 
Goes great on tortilla chips or pita.
