Title:
BBQ Pizza - Hawaiian Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1045212.jpg
Ingredients:
1 - pizza crust (homemade or your favorite store bought)
1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce (warmed)
1 1/2 cups ham (sliced or small cubes)
1 cup of pineapple, cut in small pieces (fresh or well-drained canned)
4 green onions, diced -or- 1/4 cup diced red onion
1/2 cup bacon - cooked and crumbled
1 cup Cheddar cheese (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375Â°  Prepare pizza crust and put on pizza pan.  If using homemade, prebake for 5 minutes.
Top with warmed BBQ sauce; ham, pineapple, onions and bacon pieces.  Top with cheese, if you are using it.  Bake for 12-20 minutes.  Watch your oven for exact baking time.
