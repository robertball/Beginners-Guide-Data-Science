Title:
Grilled Pork Steaks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork steaks ½ to ¾ in. thick
1 onion
1 bell pepper
1 ripe tomato
8 slices bacon
4 slices cheese (swiss, cheddar, american)

Directions:
Soak several toothpicks in water.
Slice the onion, tomato, and pepper about &#8531; in. thick.
Tenderize the pork steaks with a meat mallet. (if desired/needed)
Salt and pepper the steaks.
Arrange 2 slices of bacon in a cross pattern.  Place a pork steak and a slice each of tomato, onion, and bell pepper in the center of the bacon.  Fold the bacon strips over the top of the vegetables.  Use toothpicks to secure the bacon.  Grill the steaks on indirect or low direct heat 25-40 minutes or until the pork steaks reach 155-160 degrees.  Remove from grill and place a slice of cheese.  Serve hot.
