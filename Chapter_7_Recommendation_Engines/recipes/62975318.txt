Title:
Crockpot Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. stew beef (or ground beef)
salt and pepper, to taste
1 24 oz. can crushed tomatoes
1 14.5 oz. can diced tomatoes
1 c. beer
3 T. chili powder
1 T. brown sugar
1 T. cumin
2 t. oregano
1 t. smoked paprika
2 green peppers, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1 15 oz. can corn
1 15 oz. can black beans
2 15 oz. cans kidney beans

Directions:
Brown meat over medium heat, salt and pepper to taste.
Add all ingredients to crockpot and stir. Cook on Low for 8 hours.
Serve with sour cream and shredded cheddar cheese, if desired.
