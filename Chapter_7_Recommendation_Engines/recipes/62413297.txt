Title:
Salmon Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Can (15oz) Salmon
2 eggs
1/2 C Bread Crumbs
1/4 C melted Butter or Olive Oil
1 Tbls Parsely - Fine Chop
2 Tbls Onion - Minced
2 Stalks Celery - Minced
1/2 tsp Mrs. Dash Garlic & Herbs
Scant 1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350F. Put all ingedients in a bowl.  Mix together and form into a loaf. Place in greased cassarole. Bake 40-50 mins
