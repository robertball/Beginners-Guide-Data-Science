Title:
Trieste Tequila Cooler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986510.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce Gran Gala Triple Orange Liqueur
2 fluid ounces Corazon Blanco Tequila
1/2 fluid ounce lime juice
1 1/2 tablespoons fresh cilantro
San Pellegrino sparking mineral water
1 slice orange

Directions:
Muddle lime juice with 1.5 tbsp. fresh cilantro
Add Blanco Tequila & Gran Gala Orange Liqueur. Stir with ice and strain into an ice filled glass. Top with Pellegrino.
Garnish with orange.

Submitter: 
Gran Gala
Description: 
Fresh cilantro, lime juice, and an orange garnish make this Gran Gala and tequila cooler a perfect warm-weather cocktail.
