Title:
Coconut Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Tablespoons flour
2 1/2 Tablespoons corn starch
1 1/2 sugar
pinch of salt
2 egg yolks
2 Cup milk
2 Cups coconut
2 Tablespoons butter
1 Tablespoon vanilla

Directions:
Combine flour,cornstarch, sugar and salt mix well in large pan.  Add milk and bring to boil will thicken quickly once milk is heated.  temper eggs add in boil 1 minute.  stir in butter and vanilla add 2 cups coconut. cool completely
