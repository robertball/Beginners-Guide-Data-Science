Title:
Vegan Nacho Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Plain sugar-free non-dairy milk
1/4 cup nutritional yeast
1/4 cup Mashed sweet potato starch or cornstarch
1 tablespoon lemon juice
salt (To Taste)
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon onion powder
1/8 teaspoon ground cumin
1 10oz can diced tomatoes
chipotle Powder

Directions:
Put all ingredients (except tomatoes) into blender.
Blend until smooth
pour into saucepan, stir continuously over medium heat until boil.
reduce heat to low and keep stirring until very thick.
Stir in drained tomatoes.
If sauce is too thick, add liquid from tomato can.
