Title:
Christmas Breakfast Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground pork sausage
1/2 teaspoon salt
pepper to taste
1/2 cups milk
crescent rolls
6 eggs
8 ounces mild Cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Crumble sausage into a medium skillet. Cook over medium heat until evenly brown; drain.
Let sausage cool.
Line a greased 9x13 inch baking dish with crescent roll dough, sprinkle sausage and cheese over rolls dough.
Cover, and chill in the refrigerator for 8 hours, or overnight.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix eggs, milk, salt and pepper together.
Pour over sausage and cheese.
Bake 20 minutes.
Uncover, and reduce temperature to 325 degrees.
Bake until set.

Submitter: 
Womanmarine
Description: 
Sausage, eggs, crescent rolls, and cheese snuggle down in a casserole, chill overnight, and bake in the morning. It's a great dish for a Holiday brunch.Modified from: Christmas Breakfast Sausage Casserole
