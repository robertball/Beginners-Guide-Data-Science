Title:
Spinach Feta White Cheddar Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6215056.jpg
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 and 1/2 cups whole milk
1 and 1/4 cups sauteed and chopped fresh spinach
1 and 1/4 cups small cubed or crumbled feta
1 and 1/4 cups shredded white cheddar cheese
3 Tablespoons finely diced white or yellow onion
2 Tablespoon all purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon fresh cracked black pepper
1 pie crust ready made or make ahead

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Prepare and pie crust and place into pie pan.  In a medium bowl whisk eggs.  Whisk in milk and pepper.  Mix flour into grated cheese.   Add all ingredients into egg mixture and blend with a spoon.  Carefully pour into prepared pie crust.   Bake until set in the middle (55 - 60 minutes).
*Place pan on cookie sheet while baking to prevent overspills in oven
