Title:
Sweet & Sour Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1/2 cup white sugar
4 tsp tomato paste
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
2 tsp cornstarch
Dash powdered ginger

Directions:
Whisk all ingredients together in small saucepan until smooth.  Cook and stir constantly over a low heat about five minutes, until sauce is thick and clear.  Good for dipping.

Submitter: 
STARFLOWER
Description: 
From reading the label as to what is in restaurant sweet & sour sauces, I went home and concocted this simple sauce myself.  I think it mimicks perfectly the kind of sauce served in Chinese restaurants.
