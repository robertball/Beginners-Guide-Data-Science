Title:
Microwave Caramel Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123112.jpg
Ingredients:
2 bags popcorn puffs (5.5 oz each)
1 stick butter
1 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup light Karo syrup
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Bring to boil on low heat: butter, sugar, syrup and salt.
Cook 4 minutes stirring often.
Take off heat and add baking soda and vanilla and stir.
Put popcorn in paper bag and pour syrup over and shake bag.
Microwave 80 seconds and shake.
Microwave 60 seconds and shake.
Microwave 70 seconds and shake
Spread on cookie sheet to cool
Be careful- very hot!
