Title:
My Favorite Macaroni & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C chopped onion
1/2 C chopped green or red pepper
2 Tbsp margerine or butter
16 oz Velveeta Cheese, cubed
1/2 C milk
2 C (7oz) elbow macaroni, cooked, drained
1/2 C parmesan cheese, grated

Directions:
Saute vegetables in margerine/butter until tender.  Add 3/4 lb Velveeta and milk; stir over low heat until cheese melts.  Stir in macaroni.  Spoon into 2-quart casserole dish; sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.  Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.  Top with remaining Velveeta; continue baking until cheese begins to melt.

Submitter: 
Dianna Russell
Description: 
The peppers and onion give this easy dish added flavor.  Wonderful served with Eckerich Smoked Sausage.  Great with holiday ham as well.
