Title:
Mashed Yams Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-10 medium sweet potatoes
4 eggs
1/2 cup evaporated milk
1 cup sugar
2 sticks melted butter
2 teaspoons vanilla
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon nutmeg
Toffee:
1/3 cup flour
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 cup butter (softened)
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. lightly grease a 10 X 13 dish.
Peel sweet potatoes and cut in med-large pieces, place in a large pot of water and boil until potatoes are soft. about 20 minutes.
remove potatoes from heat, drain and mash. In a separate bowl, beat eggs, fold in evaporated milk. Add to mashed sweet potatoes..sugar, melted butter, vanilla, cinnamon, & nutmeg, mix with a mixer for 3 minutes until mixed smooth.
Add egg and milk mixture to the sweet potato mixture and mix on low just till blended through.
Pour sweet potato mixture in prepared 10 X 13 baking dish.
To make Toffee:
Mix flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, & nutmeg together. Add soft butter and mix with hands until crumbly. Add pecans and mix with hands to distribute pecans evenly through sugar butter mixture. Crumble mixture on top of Sweet potato mixture evenly.
Place in 350 degree oven uncovered and bake 35-40 minutes.
