Title:
Hoppin' John Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbs oil
1/2 cup onion, chopped
1/2 cup green pepper, chopped
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 cup uncooked rice
1/2 cupped chopped celery
1-2 cups ham chopped
1 lb can black eyed peas, rinsed and drained
2 cups water or chicken stock
crushed red pepper flakes to taste

Directions:
saute onion and rice in oil.  The last few mins add garlic and green bell pepper.  Then add ham, black-eye peas, and water.  Simmer 20 mins.
Can decrease the rice to 1/2 cup and decrease chicken stock to 1 cup.
Add one can of rinsed and drained okra, 10 oz package of spinach, thawed squeezed and dried.
