Title:
Janey's Napolitana Creamy Pesto Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3668359.jpg
Ingredients:
300 g pasta (boiled)
2 Large plum tomatoes (diced)
1 Large onion (chopped)
2 Large carrots (diced into tiny cubes)
1 Large green bell pepper (diced into tiny cubes)
6 Large mushrooms (thinly sliced)
2 Tablespoons tomato Paste
1/2 Teaspoon chili paste
50 g butter
1 Tablespoon lemon (squeezed)
50 ml cooking creme
2 Tablespoons pesto sauce
1 Pinch of salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Vegetable Mix: in a bowl add the tomatoes, bell peppers, carrots, mushrooms, tomato paste, chilli paste, and lemon. Add salt and pepper to taste. This mixture must not be thick. Mix well.
Creme Mix: in a bowl add the creme and pesto sauce. Mix well.
Sauce Mix: In a warm pot, melt the butter on low heat. Add in the onions and simmer for 1 minute. Then add the vegetable mix and simmer for 2 minutes on medium heat. Add in the creme mix and stir for 3 minutes on high heat until it boils.
Add the pasta to the sauce and mix.
Serve while hot or keep the lid on to serve later.
