Title:
Creamy Eggless Eggnog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/332730.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 gallon cold milk, divided
1 (3.4oz) pkg instant french vanilla pudding mix
1/4 cup sugar
1 tbsp vanilla extract
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp ground nutmeg

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, mix together 2 cups cold milk & pudding mix until smooth. Add in sugar, vanilla, cinnamon & nutmeg. Scraping the bowl to make sure all ingredients are added & mixed thoroughly. Stir in remaining milk slowly. Store in an air tight container. Refrigerate. Stir before serving.
NOTE: For a thicker drink increase instant pudding mix to 2 boxes.

Submitter: 
Melissa Goff
Description: 
The taste of eggnog without the eggs. With milk, instant pudding mix, sugar and spices that makes everything such a delight. This is a thick creamy drink, sure to be liked by all. My kids favorite holiday drink.
