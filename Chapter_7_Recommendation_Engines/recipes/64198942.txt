Title:
Rice Krispy Peanut Butter Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup corn syrup
1-1/4 cups peanut butter
4 cups Rice Krispies
1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Heat sugar and syrup until very hot, but do not boil.  Mix in peanut butter, stirring until smooth.  Add Rice Krispies.
Place in 9 x 13 inch baking pan.  Sprinkle with chocolate chips and place under broiler to melt, then spread for icing.
