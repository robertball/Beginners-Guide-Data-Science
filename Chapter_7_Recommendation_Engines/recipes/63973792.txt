Ingredients:
2 Tbsp gluten free white rice flour
2 Tbsp olive oil
2 cups milk
1/4 tsp nutmeg
Pinch of salt

Directions:
Heat a saucepan over medium.
Add the oil and wait until hot.
Add the flour and stir until light brown.
Add the milk.
Once the milk has heated, add the salt and nutmeg.
Continue stirring until the mixture reaches the consistency desired.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Bechamel sauce usually calls for butter, this recipe tries to cut down on the fat.  The taste is just as delicious.
