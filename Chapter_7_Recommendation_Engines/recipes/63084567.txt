Title:
Chocolate Chip Cheesecake Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup unsealed butter, softened
3/4 cup powdered sugar
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1teaspoon vanilla
1cup miniature chocolate chips
Graham crackers

Directions:
1. In a medium bowl, beat cream cheese and butter until smooth.
2.  Add sugar and vanilla, mix.
3.  Stir in miniature chocolate chips.
4. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
Spoon mixture into serving bowl and serve with graham crackers and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Angela Naumann
Description: 
This dip is addicting!  Imagine a chocolate chip cheese cake batter that is safe to eat.  Served with graham crackers this dip will quickly become a family favorite.
