Title:
Bacon and Fresh Pasta in a Jar Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F2507717.jpg
Ingredients:
3 ounces fresh pasta
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 cup chopped fresh pea vines
1 clove garlic, minced
1 slice cooked bacon, broken into pieces
1 tablespoon butter
Â½ teaspoon chile oil, or to taste

Directions:
Bring a pot of water to a boil; add fresh pasta and cook until tender, 3 to 4 minutes. Drain.
Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir pea vines and garlic until vines are wilted, 3 to 4 minutes. Add pasta, bacon, butter, and chile oil to pea vines mixture; toss using tongs until butter is melted. Transfer pasta mixture to a 1-pint mason jar.

Submitter: 
Alli Shircliff
Description: 
Fill a mason jar with pasta, bacon, and pea vines for a quick and easy on-the-go lunch that is perfect for a picnic!
