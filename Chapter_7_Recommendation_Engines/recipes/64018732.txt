Title:
Thelma's Candied Almond Poppy Seed Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz. (6 cups) baby spinach
2 c. sliced strawberries
1 c. halved red grapes
1/4 c. thinly sliced sweet onion (maui, Vidalia, or red)
7 oz. gouda cheese, cubed (about 1 c.)
Dressing:
1/4 c. vegetable oil
3 T. white wine vinegar
2 T. minced shallot
2 T. sugar
2 tsp. poppy seed
Almonds:
1/3 c. slivered or chopped almonds
3 T. sugar.

Directions:
Almonds:
In small heavy skillet, cook almonds and sugar over med. heat, stirring constantly, until sugar melts and almonds are lightly toasted (about 5 min).
Spread on parchment paper.
Break apart if needed.
Dressing:
Whisk together all dressing ingredients until well blended.
Salad:
Rinse spinach; spin or pat dry.
In large salad bowl, combine all salad ingredients.
When ready to serve, drizzle with dressing and crumble candies almonds over the top.
Toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
This is a super fancy version of the traditional poppy seed salad thanks to the candied nuts.  The gouda should be diced small enough to make easy bites.  Don't leave out the gouda, it's the best!
