Title:
Pico De Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb campari tomatoes, diced
1  red onion, diced
1 - 2 jalapenos, minced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 bunch cilantro, finely chopped
1 lime, juiced
1/2 t kosher salt
4 hass avocados, mashed as desired

Directions:
Dice tomatoes and put inside vegetable spinner.  Spin well to remove excess juice and seeds.  Combine tomatoes with diced onion, jalapeno, garlic, cilantro, lime juice and salt.  Combine mashed avocados with pico de gallo mixture.  Mix well.  Serve immediately and use within 1 day for best flavor.

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
I love Guacamole and Pico de gallo.  This recipe has a good heaping amount of Pico which I love!  I use camparii tomatoes since they have such a wonderful flavor and are readily ripe.
