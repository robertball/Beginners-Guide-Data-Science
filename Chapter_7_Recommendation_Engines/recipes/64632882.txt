Title:
Slow cooker Capron Hawaiian Style (Goat meat) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pound boneless capron (Or veal) shoulder trimmed of all fat and cut into 1 -inch cubes
1 cup water
1/4 Cup Sherry
2 tablespoon soy sauce
1 teaspoon ground ginger
2 teaspoons of brown sugar (or 1 teaspoon artificial sweetener)

Directions:
Lightly brown the capron cubes in a large skillet over medium-high heat
Combine remaining ingredients in slow cooker, stir in capron,
Cover and cook on low for 6 hours
serve over cooked rice.
Variations: Also works well for pork shoulder, veal shoulder and venison.

Submitter: 
Frugal Dusky
Description: 
This is a veal recipe that adapts itself wonderfully to goat, if you happen to be lucky enough to have some goat shoulder roast on hand!
