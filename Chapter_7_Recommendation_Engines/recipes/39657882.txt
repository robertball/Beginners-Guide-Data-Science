Title:
Cheerios Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter, softened and cut up
1 bag regular marshmallows
1/3 cup peanut butter
6 cups cheerios
1 bag mini m&m's (frozen!)

Directions:
Butter 9x13" pan, set aside.
In a large bowl (that will fit into your microwave) mix marshmallows, butter and peanut butter.  Microwave on high for 1 to 2 minutes or until marshmallows are melted.  Stir thoroughly, add cheerios and mix well.  Work fast!  Stir in mini m&m's.
Pour all into buttered pan.  Press firmly into pan, working into corners.
When cool, turn out onto cutting board and cut into bars.

Description: 
Fast and easy recipe that is loved by kids and adults.
