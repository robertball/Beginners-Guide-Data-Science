Title:
Parsnip & Potato Puree Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Russet potatoes
2 large parsnips
chicken broth

Directions:
Peel and chop potatoes and parsnips into small pieces.
In a large stock pot, cover potato and parsnip pieces with water.
Bring to boil over high heat.
Reduce heat and simmer for 20-30 minutes, until very tender.
Remove from heat and drain.
Run through a potato ricer or puree using an immersion blender, food processor, or blender, adding broth as needed until desired consistency is achieved.
