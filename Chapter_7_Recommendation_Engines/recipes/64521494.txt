Title:
Easy Homeade Caramels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup corn syrup
1/4 cup melted butter
1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
Sea Salt to taste
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar

Directions:
-Add corn syrup + Melted butter into a bowl
-Add condensed milk and salt
-Add white sugar
-Add brown sugar
-Whisk well, when done place bowl into the microwave on high for 6 mins, stirring every 2 mins
-Line a pyrex dish or pan with parchment paper & pour mixture in, let cool for 2 hours
-After cooling, place in fridge for 3 more hours until mixture is hardened
-Cut into squares with a sharp knife

Submitter: 
Lexy N
Description: 
These are so easy, and surprisingly delicious considering they are made in the microwave!
