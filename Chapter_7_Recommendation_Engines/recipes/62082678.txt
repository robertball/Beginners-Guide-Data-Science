Title:
Worlds Greatest Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 bunches broccoli
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of mushroom
6 mushrooms sliced
1 large chopped onion
1 tbsp. chopped garlic
2 cups grated cheddar cheese
3 tbsp. parmesean cheese
1 cup bread crumbs
1 tbsp. butter
salt/pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil the broccoli until almost tender.
In a pan sautee the onion, mushrooms and garlic in a bit of butter until soft.  Add the cans of soup and bring to a light boil and then add 1 1/2 cups of the cheddar cheese.  Salt and pepper to taste.
Layer the broccoli in a casserole dish and then pour the soup mixture over top.
Melt the butter in a pan and add bread crumbs, remaining cheddar cheese and parmesean cheese until the bread crumbs are lightly toasted and the cheese is not too melted.
Pour crumb mixture over broccoli mixture. Bake at 375 for 45mins.
