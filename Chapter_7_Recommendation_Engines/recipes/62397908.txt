Title:
Chicken Roll Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts, cooked and diced
1/2 cup green onion, diced
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
2 packages refrigerated crescent rolls
1 cup Italian bread crumbs
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 can cream of chicken soup
8 ounces sour cream
1 Tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
ROLL UPS: Mix together chicken, onions, cream cheese and garlic powder in a small bowl.
Unwrap crescent rolls and place 2 T. of chicken mixture in the large end and roll up.
Meanwhile, combine bread crumbs & Parmesan cheese in a shallow bowl.
Place crescent rolls in melted butter and then in the crumb mixture.
Place on an un-greased cookie sheet.
Bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
SAUCE: In a small sauce pan combine the soup, sour cream and Worcestershire sauce and add salt/pepper to taste.
Heat until hot and smooth.
Serve Hot roll ups with sauce on top.
