Title:
Zucchini Apple Spice Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3073448.jpg
Ingredients:
1 zucchini (1 cup shredded)
1 medium apple (I used gala)
1 cup almond butter
¼ cup light honey
2 eggs whites (substitute 2 tbsp flax meal + 6 tbsp water for vegan)
½ teaspoon baking powder
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
? teaspoon ground cloves
¼ teaspoon ground ginger
½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
½ teaspoon ground allspice
dash of sea salt

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees
Prepare a muffin tin with cooking spray or liners
Peel zucchini & apple then shred with a cheese grater, you can also use a food processor but will then need to squeeze the liquid out
Add all ingredients to a bowl and mix by hand
Add to muffin tin
Bake 15-20 minutes
Cool 5 minutes then eat

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Warm, winter spices come together in this flourless zucchini and apple muffin.
