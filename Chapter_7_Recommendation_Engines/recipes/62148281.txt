Title:
Whole Wheat Pancake Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Cups Whole wheat flour
3 Cups Unbleached white flour
1/2 Cup sugar
2 1/2 Tablespoons baking powder
4 Teaspoons baking soda
4 Teaspoons salt

Directions:
Mix completely, and store in an air-tight container.
To make pancakes, mix together 1 cup of the mix, 1 cup milk, 1 egg, and 2 tabelspoons oil.
Cook on a hot griddle, and serve with your choice of toppings.
Also makes good waffles!
