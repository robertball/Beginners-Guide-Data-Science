Title:
Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068421.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup white corn syrup
1 cup brown sugar, packed
1/3 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup butter, melted
3 eggs
2 cup pecans, chopped
1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch single crust pie

Directions:
Combine syrup, sugar, salt, and melted butter or margarine.
Slightly beat the eggs, and add to sugar mixture.
Beat well, and pour into uncooked pie shell.
Sprinkle pecans on top.
Bake at 350Â° for 50 to 60 minutes.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Corn syrup and brown sugar do magical things in this simple pecan pie that bakes up rich, gooey and delicious. The filling is stirred up, poured into an uncooked pie shell, sprinkled with pecans and baked.Modified from: Kentucky Pecan Pie
