Title:
Sticky Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 frozen dinner rolls
1 C brown sugar
1/2 C butter
1/2 C pecans
1/2- 3oz box butterscotch pudding
1 t cinnamon
1 t sugar

Directions:
Grease bundt pan. Place frozen rolls around the bottom. Sprinkle with the pudding, sugar and cinnamon. In a suacepan, melt the butter, brown sugar and nuts. Pour over the buns in the pan. Cover with foil and a dish towel. Let rise overnight. In the morning, bake in a preheated 375 oven for 30-40 minutes. Turn upside down immediatly and eat while warm.
