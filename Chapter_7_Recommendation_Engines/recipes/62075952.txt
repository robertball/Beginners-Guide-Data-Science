Title:
Chicken Broccoli and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts cooked and cut into bite size pieces
2-4 cups broccoli florettes steamed
1 can(10 oz) cream of chicken soup
3 cups cooked white rice
2-3 cups mild chedder cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Brown chicken in large fring pan until done. Set aside to rest. Steam broccoli until soft. When broccoli is soft, soak in an ice bath till cold. Meanwhile, cook rice acording to package directions. Cut chicken into bite size pieces and put in a large bowl. Add broccoli, rice, cream of chicken soup, and 1/2 of the cheese to chicken. Mix well. Pour into a 6x12 cassorle dish. Sprinkle the remander of the cheese on top. Bake for 1 hour or until cheese is melted and soup is bubbling on the sides. Let rest for 5 min before serving.
