Title:
Key West Chicken Bourbon Reduction Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon ground ginger
4 ounces soy sauce
1 T butter
2 tablespoons minced onion
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
3/8 cup bourbon
1 teaspoon garlic

Directions:
In a skillet, melt butter and saute onion and garlic. Add remaining ingredients and simmer until reduced to a thick syrup.
