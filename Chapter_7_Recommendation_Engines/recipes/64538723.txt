Title:
Baked potato Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 potatoes
Shortening or olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
Wash and rinse the dirt off the skin of the potatoes
Liberally cover the entire potatoes with shortening
Stab each potatoe with a knife at least three times
Place potatoes directly on rack in preheated oven and bake for 1 hour or until the internal temperature of the potatoe is 210.
