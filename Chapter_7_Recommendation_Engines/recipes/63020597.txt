Title:
White Chocolate Cherry Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/756447.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Cups White Chocolate Chips
1 Can (14 oz) Sweetened Condenced Milk
2 Tablespoons Butter
1 Cup Dried Cherries, Chopped
1/2 Tablespoon Almond Extract

Directions:
Grease 8x8 inch pan or similar size (or double recipe and use 9x13 pan).
Over low-medium heat, melt together the chocolate chips, sweetened condenced milk and butter.
Take off heat, and mix in cherries and almond extract.
Pour mixture into greased pan, and smooth out.  Let harden and cool in fridge - should be ready in 1-2 hours.

Submitter: 
Erica
Description: 
Really easy recipe.  White chocolate, Dried cherries and a few ingredients mix together to make this delicious fudge.
