Title:
Lemon Spice Wellness Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/765892.jpg
Ingredients:
1.  A large pot of water (6 - 8 cups), use spring water for extra minerals.
2.  4 Lemons, zested and juiced
3.  a large finger of ginger, peeled and coarsely chopped
4.  a whole star anise
5.  6 green cardamom pods, bruised
6.  one loose stick of true cinnamon, Canela Entera
7.  unpasteurized honey to taste (3 or more tbsp)

Directions:
Start your water to boil.
Add in all spices.
Add chopped ginger.
Zest lemons over pot. Proceed to juice them and add to juice to pot.
Once the pot has come to a boil reduce to a simmer.  The longer you let it simmer the more flavorful your tea will become.
Finish with honey.
Pour from a ladle through a fine strainer into mugs and add contents from strainer back into pot.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a classic throw-in-what-you've-got tea that will kick a cold where it hurts: In the throat.
It is made with whole ingredients and no sugar - a sure sign you're on your way to wellness.
