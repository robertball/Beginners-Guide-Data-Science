Title:
Avocado-Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 ripe avocado,  coarsely copied
1/4 cup milk
1 cup vanilla yogurt
1 tablespoons honey (optional)
1 banana, coarsely chopped
4-5 ice cubes

Directions:
Combine the avocado, banana, milk, yogurt, honey, and ice cubes in a blender; blend until smooth.
