Title:
Granny's Czechoslovakian Spice Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine
2 cups brown sugar
3 eggs, beaten until fluffy
2-1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. nutmeg
1 tsp. cloves
1 tsp. baking soda
Pinch of salt
1 cup chopped pecans
1 cup halved raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325degrees.  Cream together butter and brown sugar, add eggs.  In a separate bowl, sift together flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, salt, and baking powder.  Add flour mixture to sugar mixture about 1/3 at a time.  Stir in pecans and raisins.  Drop by teaspoonful onto a greased cookie sheet Bake for 8 to 12 minutes at 325 degrees until cookies are light brown.  (Makes about 6 dozen).

Submitter: 
Jan Hermann Holly Hamrick
Description: 
My mom was from Czechoslovakia and she made these cookies every year at Christmas for my sisters and I.  Now, my daughter and granddaughter make them with me at Christmas. I have lost my recipe about 4 times so I am posting it so I won't lose it.
