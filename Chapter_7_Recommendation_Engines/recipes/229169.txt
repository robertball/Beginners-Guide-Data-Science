Title:
Vanilla French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982151.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1/2 cup milk
2 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoon Spice Islands® Pure Vanilla Extract
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 tablespoon butter
6 slices bread

Directions:
Whisk eggs, milk, sugar, vanilla and cinnamon in a medium bowl.
Melt butter in skillet over medium-high heat. Dip bread slices in egg mixture and brown each side in skillet. Serve with maple syrup.

Submitter: 
Spice Islands®
Description: 
Vanilla extract and ground cinnamon bring ordinary French toast to a new flavor level, especially when served with real maple syrup!
