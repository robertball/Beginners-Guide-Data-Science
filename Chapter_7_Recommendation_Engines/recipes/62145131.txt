Title:
Zuericher Geschnetzeltes (Vegetarian) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6304402.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz. Vegetarian Chicken, if canned reserve liquid to add to recipe
2 Tablespoons butter or olive oil, divided
2 Shallots, minced
1 Large Leek washed and finely sliced, both white are green portions
1 Head garlic minced
2 Cups sliced mushrooms (If using canned mushrooms save and use as stock for recipe)
2 Carrots, peeled and sliced
1 Cup white wine (optional)
3 Tablespoons Chicken McKays Seasoning (or other vegetarian Chicken Base)
1 Cup Water (If not using the reserve liquid of the FriChick)
1 Cup Cream (light cream, Half & Half, or whipping cream)
1/4 Ground Turkey Tail Mushroom (Optional)
16 Ounces Sour Cream
1 Tablespoon Curry powder
1 teaspoon lemon zest
Salt, to taste
Ground pepper, to taste
2 T. chopped, fresh parsley

Directions:
Melt 1 tablespoon butter over mod heat saute onions until translucent.
Then add leeks saute for a few minutes then add garlic.
Add white wine.  Allow to mostly cook off.
Add olive oil as and if needed.
Add celery, mushrooms, and carrots.
Saute over moderate heat until carrots are no longer firm.
Add chicken McKays seasoning and water.
Add vegetarian chicken if canned with broth, if not brown veggie chicken before adding to pan.
Bring to almost a boil but avoiding a boil.
Add cream, ground turkey tail mushroom and sour cream
Add lemon zest.
Add curry powder.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Garnish with parsley.
*If broth is runny add Tablespoon of all purpose flour to thicken.
