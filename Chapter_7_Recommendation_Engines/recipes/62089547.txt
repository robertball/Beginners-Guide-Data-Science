Title:
Goat Cheese Stuffed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 fresh boneless, skinnless chicken breasts
1 cup fresh goat cheese
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/8 cup chopped fresh chives
1/2 cup dry white wine
1tbsp butter
1/8 cup + 1 tbsp olive oil
juice of one lemon
1/8 cup white sugar
salt and pepper
1 bunch of mesclun salad greens

Directions:
1) Pound chicken breasts flat between two pieces of wax paper, using a meat cleaver or heavy object. In a small bowl mix goat cheese, parsley, chives, and a little salt and pepper. Spread cheese mixture evenly over tops of chicken breasts. Roll breats and secure with toothpicks or twine.
2) Melt butter and 1 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Place rolled chicken breasts in skillet. Brown each side about 2 minutes. When all sides are browned pour white wine over breasts and cover skillet. Cook about 8 minutes, until wine has reduced and chicken is fully cooked.
Remove chicken from skillet. Continue to cook wine until it reduces into a sauce. About 5 minutes.
3) In a seperate small bowl mix remaining oil with the lemon juice, sugar, and salt and pepper. Toss over salad greens.
3) Place greens on plate, top with chicken, and top chicken with a little bit of sauce from the skillet.
Serve immediately.
(PS: Leftover chicken can be refridgerated and served, sliced in medalions, the next day ontop of a green salad or in a chef's salad.)
