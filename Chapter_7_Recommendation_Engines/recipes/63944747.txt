Title:
Big Ray's Blonde Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1093516.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup melted butter
2 cups light brown sugar, packed
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Spray 13" x 9" pan with cooking spray.
In a medium size bowl,combine butter and sugar and mix well until creamy.  Add eggs and vanilla and mix well.
Combine flour, baking powder and salt and stir into egg mixture.  Stir in chocolate chips and walnuts (batter will be stiff).
Pour batter into pan.  If desired, sprinkle more chocolate chips and walnuts on top.
Bake 35 to 40 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with moist crumbs.  Let cool and cut into squares.
