Title:
Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-lb bag frozen broccoli cuts
1 can cream of celery soup
2 T. evaporated milk
1 can sliced mushrooms, drained (optional)
8 saltine crackers, crumbled
2 hard-boiled eggs
1/2 to 1 cup grated mild cheddar cheese

Directions:
1. In a large mixing bowl, stir together the broccoli, cream of celery soup, evaporated milk, mushrooms, and crackers.
2. Chop one of the hard-boiled eggs and stir into the mixture.  Pour everything into a 2-qt. casserole dish.
3.  Sprinkle the grated cheese on top of the casserole.  Slice the other hard-boiled egg around on top of the cheese.
4.  Bake at 350 degrees for about 30 minutes, until cheese is melted and casserole is bubbly.

Submitter: 
NCwriter
Description: 
My family has made this broccoli casserole for over 40 years! Particularly good with lamb or pork roast.
