Title:
Unflaky Ambrosia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can fruit cocktail, drained
1 (11 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
1 cup miniature marshmallows
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 teaspoon coconut extract

Directions:
Mix fruit cocktail, mandarin oranges, pineapple, marshmallows, walnuts, sour cream, and coconut extract in a bowl. Cover bowl and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Stir and serve.

Submitter: 
MOMFISH
Description: 
Got someone who doesn't care for flakes of coconut in their food? This is the recipe for you!
