Title:
Hot Cocoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. cocoa
1/2 c. sugar
dash salt
1/3 c. hot water
Boil 2 minutes.
Add 1 qt milk
3/4 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Mix first 4 ingredients.
Boil 2 min.
Add milk and vanilla, and reheat.

Submitter: 
Sheryl Johnson
Description:
