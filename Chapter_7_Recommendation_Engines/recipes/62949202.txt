Title:
Onion Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons Fat(butter, fat from meat your cooking etc...)
2 cups beef broth (or enough water added to meat juices to make 2 cups)
1/4cup sugar
2med onions thinly sliced
2Tablespoons flour
1Tablespoon vinegar
1/4teaspoon kitchen bouquet
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large skillet brown sugar in the fat.
Add the sliced onions and suate stirring constantly till tender. Push the onions to one side of the skillet.
Add the flour and brown slightly.
Stir in broth, vinegar and kitchen bouquet and wisk over med heat till smooth and thickened and bubbling.
Cook 2 to 3 minutes and season to taste.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
This is the sweet and tangy onion gravy that my kids grew up loving!
