Title:
Apple Crisp IV Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/329114.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 cups rolled oats
3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 cups butter
5 Granny Smith peeled, cored and sliced apples

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine the flour, oatmeal, cinnamon, nutmeg and brown sugar. Cut butter into mixture until crumbly.
Take half of the mixture and pat it into the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Cover crumb mixture with apple slices, then sprinkle apple slices with remaining crumb mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 to 50 minutes or until apples are tender.

Submitter: 
Kathy Higgins
Description: 
Three layers make up this hearty 9x13-inch crisp. Sweet apple slices find their home between generous layers of a delicious crumbly mixture -flour, brown sugar, butter, cinnamon and oatmeal.Modified from: Apple Crisp IV
