Title:
Grandma Jean’s Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4483104.jpg
Ingredients:
½ cup margarine
1 egg
Pinch of salt
1 tsp. baking soda
1 cup sugar
1 cup mashed bananas (3 bananas)
1 ½ cup flour
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients. Cream sugar, butter and remaining wet ingredients, then add dry ingredients.
Bake at 375 for approx. 20 minutes.
