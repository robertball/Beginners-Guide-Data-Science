Title:
Party Cheese Bread I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter melted
2 T. lemon juice
2 T. Dijon mustard
1 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. onion powder
1/2 tsp. celery salt
1 round loaf 1 lb. sourdough bread
1 lb. monterrey jack cheese sliced

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the first 6 ingredients and set aside.  Cut bread diagonally into 1" slices to within a 1/2" of the bottom of loaf.  Repeat cuts in the opposite direction.  Arrange cheese slices in cuts.  Drizzle butter mixture over bread, wrap loag in foil and place on a baking sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.  Uncover and bake for 10 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.
