Title:
Pasta Shells with Chicken and Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T cooking oil
3 T butter
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 t salt
1/2 t pepper
1/2 red onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, chopped
3/4 # Brussels sprouts ( or one 10 oz. pkg. frozen), cut into quarters from top to stem end
1 C canned low-sodium chicken broth
1/8 t dried pepper flakes
1 1/2 t lemon juice
1/4 C chopped fresh parsley
1/3 C grated Parmesan cheese
8 oz med. pasta shells

Directions:
In large nonstick frying pan, heat 1 T each of oil and butter over mod. heat. Season chicken with 1/4 t. each of salt and pepper. Cook breasts until browned and just done, 4-5 min per side. Remove chicken from pan and let rest for 5 min. Cut into small pieces.
In same pan, heat the remaining 1 T oil over mod. low heat. Add red onion and cook, stirring occ., until starting to soften, about 3 min. Stir in garlic, Brussels sprouts, broth, and red-pepper flakes. Bring to a simmer and cook, covered, until the Brussels sprouts are just done, about 5 min. Add chicken, lemon juice, parsley, Parmesan, and remaining 2 T butter, 3/4 t salt, and 1/4 t. pepper. Remove from heat.
In large pot of boiling salted water, cook pasta until just done, about 10 min. Drain and toss with sauce.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Frozen Brussels sprouts are surprisingly good in this recipe, though not as fine as fresh.
