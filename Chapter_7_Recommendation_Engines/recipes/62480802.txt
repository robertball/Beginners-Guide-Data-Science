Title:
Chicken With Mushroom Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
3/4 t salt
1/4 t pepper
3 t EVOO
8 oz mushrooms, quartered
3 scallions, thinly sliced
1/2 C chicken broth
2 T reduced-fat cream cheese
1/4 t dried rosemary

Directions:
Season chicken with 1/2 t salt and the pepper.
In lg. skillet, heat 2 t oil over med. heat. Add chicken and cook until golden brown, about 3 min per side. Transfer to plate and keep warm.
Add mushrooms, scallions and remaining 1 t oil. Cook, stirring, until mushrooms have wilted, about 3 min.
Add broth, cream cheese, rosemary and remaining 1/4 t salt. Stir until cream cheese melts, about 1 min.
Return chicken to pan and bring to a simmer. Cover and cook until chicken is cooked through and still juicy, about 5 min.
