Title:
Im"meat"iate Spaghetti Sauce (Marinara) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup chicken broth
1 cup tomatoes
1/4 cup onion
2 Tbs. red wine (optional)
2 Tbs. tomato paste
2 cloves garlic
2 sprigs basil
1/4 cup raw hamburger, steak or turkey (or cooked meat)
pinch of Italian seasoning (optional)
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup heavy cream (optional)

Directions:
Add all ingredients in order, except cream, to Tall Cup and twist on Cross Blade. Pulse using quick Pulse until you've reached a good consistency.
Twist off Cross Blade and twist on Shaker/Steamer Top.
Place sauce in microwave safe bowl and cook on high for 8-10 minutes, until meat is cooked. (You may want to stop and stir, or add more liquid during cook time).
Optional-Add heavy cream to cooked sauce and Pulse one or two times.
Serve over hot pasta.
