Title:
Hamburger Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. ground beef
1 can can
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/4 cup chopped pimento
3 cups egg noodles, cooked and drained
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Cook meat and onions in pan until browned. Add the rest of the ingredients and then add noodles.
Place in casserole dish and bake at 300ยบ F for 1 hour.
