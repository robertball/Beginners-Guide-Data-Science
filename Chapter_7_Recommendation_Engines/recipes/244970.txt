Title:
Runners Trail Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup dried cherries
1 cup walnut halves
1 cup pumpkin seeds
1 cup soy nuts
1/2 cup dried goji berries
1/2 cup dried blueberries

Directions:
Mix cherries, walnuts, pumpkin seeds, soy nuts, goji berries, and blueberries together in a bowl.

Submitter: 
Cat
Description: 
Great mix for runners on the go! Healthy trail mix without all the bad stuff.
