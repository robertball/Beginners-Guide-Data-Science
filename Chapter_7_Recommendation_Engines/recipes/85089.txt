Title:
Sweet and Sassy Vanilla Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978935.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head Napa or Chinese cabbage, shredded
1 (16 ounce) package broccoli coleslaw mix
Dressing:
1/2 cup sugar
1/3 cup white wine vinegar
1/3 cup olive oil
2 teaspoons McCormick® Pure Vanilla Extract
1 teaspoon McCormick® Coarse Grind Black Pepper
1/2 teaspoon McCormick® Ground Ginger
1/4 teaspoon McCormick® Ground Red Pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Mix cabbage and broccoli slaw mix in a large bowl. Set aside.
Combine sugar and remaining ingredients; stir until well blended. Just before serving, pour over cabbage mixture; toss gently.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
This sensational slaw is a mix of vanilla, ginger, red pepper and black pepper which adds sweet-heat to the crisp, crunch you love about traditional coleslaw. It goes great with Hot Tempered Baby Back Ribs with Sweet Guava Glaze.
