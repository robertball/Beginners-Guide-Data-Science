Title:
Tea Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup luke warm water
2 tsp. sugar
2 Tbsp. yeast
1 1/2 cups scalded milk
2 1/2 cups flour
3 eggs
1/2 cup butter
1 1/2 cups floured raisins
1/2 cup sugar
1 1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Scald milk and cool to lukewarm.
Set yeast in water and sugar for 10 minutes; add to milk, then beat in flour.
Set aside in a warm place until light and full of bubbles.
Beat down, cream together and add remaining ingredients.
Mix well, then add flour to make a soft dough. Knead smooth and set aside in a warm place (let rise until double in bulk). Turn out onto board and cut in pieces the size of a tea cup.
Shape into large round buns and let stand for 20 minutes, then pat out with a rolling pin until the size of a bread and butter plate.
Place on baking sheet; let rise until doubled in bulk. Bake at 375 until golden brown.
