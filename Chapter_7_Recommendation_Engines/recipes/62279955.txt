Title:
Crisp Crusted Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 frying chicken, 3-3 1/4 lbs., cut up
1 egg
2 Tbsp. milk
1 cup instant potato flakes
1 tsp. garlic powder
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup butter or margarine
Celery leaves and thinly sliced celery for garnish

Directions:
1. Wash chicken pieces and pat dry. Set aside.
2. Beat egg and milk in a bowl.
3. In another bowl mix potato flakes, garlic powder and Parmesan cheese.
4. Roll chicken first in egg mix, then in the potato flakes mixture.
5. Melt butter in shallow baking pan. Roll coated chicken pieces in the butter and place them skin side up in the pan.
6. Bake at 400 degrees F for 45-50 min. or until juices from thighs run clear when pierced with a fork. Transfer to serving platter, garnish with celery leaves and celery slices and serve.
