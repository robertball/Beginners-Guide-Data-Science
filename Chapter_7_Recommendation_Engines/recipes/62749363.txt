Title:
steak fajitas for two Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C cooked steak, cut in strips
1/4 C corn
1/4 C black beans
1 Tbl taco seasoning
1/4 C water
2 plum tomatoes, diced
1 avacado, diced
1/2 green onion, cut in small rings
juice of 1/2 lime
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 C cheddar cheese, shredded
4 large tortilla shells

Directions:
Add first 5 ingredients to a pan on MED-HI heat. Simmer until most of the water is absorbed. Keep warm.
In a small bowl, combine the rest of the ingredients except cheese and tortillas.
Place a tea cup 3/4 filled with water in the microwave for 1 minute. Place the tortillas on a plate, cover with plastic wrap. With the tea cup in the microwave, heat the tortillas for 35 seconds.
Spoon 1/4 of the meat mixture on each tortilla shell, top with salsa mix and cheddar cheese. You can add lettuce, sour cream or any other desired topping.
