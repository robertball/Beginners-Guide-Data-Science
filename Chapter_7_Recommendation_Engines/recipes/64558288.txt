Title:
Beachside Daiquiri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 ounce orgeat, or Torani almond syrup
1 ounce fresh-squeezed lime juice
2 ounces fresh pineapple-infused gold Barbados rum

Directions:
Mix orgeat, lime juice and pineapple-infused gold rum with ice in a shaker. Strain into a coupe glass and garnish with a lime half and mint leaves.
Cook's note: Making pineapple-infused rum is easy; see my own recipe.

Submitter: 
Matt Wencl
Description: 
Refreshing tiki flavors mingle in this delicious reinterpretation of the classic daiquiri.
