Title:
Janet's Carmelized Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 extra large sweet onions (about 3 lbs)
1 10 oz can chicken or beef broth
3/4 c. butter

Directions:
Cut onions in half; cut each half into 1/2" thick slices.
Combine all ingredients into a 3 1/2 qt crock pot.  Cool, covered, at HIGH 8 hours or until golden brown and very soft.  Store onions in an airtight container; refrigerate up to 2 weeks or freeze up to 2 months.

Submitter: 
Amy Hague Miller
Description: 
Use these for onion soup or as a topping for a steak sandwich.
