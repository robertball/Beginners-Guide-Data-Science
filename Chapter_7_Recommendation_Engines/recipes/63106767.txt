Title:
Rhubarb Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 egg, beaten
2 cups flour, sifted
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp soda
1 cup sour milk or buttermilk
2 cups rhubarb, chopped
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar; add egg and beat. Sift dry ingredients and add alternately with sour milk. Gently blend in rhubarb and vanilla. Pour into 9x13" baking pan. Sprinkle top with 1/2 cup sugar and 1/2 tsp of cinnamon.
Bake for 350 degrees for 45 to 50 minutes.
