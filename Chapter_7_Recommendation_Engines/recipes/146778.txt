Title:
Sesame Strawberry Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/970225.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
2 tablespoons sesame seeds
1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
1 tablespoon poppy seeds
1 1/2 teaspoons dried minced onion
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
2 bunches fresh spinach - chopped, washed and dried
1 pint strawberries, halved

Directions:
Whisk together the SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, sesame seeds, sesame oil, poppy seeds, dried onion, paprika, oil and vinegar. Refrigerate until chilled.
In a salad bowl, combine the spinach and strawberries. Drizzle with dressing, toss lightly and serve.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
Strawberries and spinach join a pungent dressing in this unusual salad combo. Sesame oil and balsamic vinegar give this version of poppy seed dressing a bit more robust taste.
