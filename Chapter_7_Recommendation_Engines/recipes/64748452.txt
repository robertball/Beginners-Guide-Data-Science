Title:
Homemade Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6868456.jpg
Ingredients:
6 eggs
3 cups sugar
3 half pints whipping cream
pure vanilla
Milk

Directions:
Beat eggs.
Add sugar and beat.
Add whipping cream and beat well.
Pour into ice cream freezer and add milk until about 2/3 full.
Add vanilla to taste.
Freeze with ice cream salt and ice until thick.
