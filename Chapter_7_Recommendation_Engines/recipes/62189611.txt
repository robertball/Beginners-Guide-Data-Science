Title:
Spicy Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pack of Chicken Breasts(fajita flavored)
1 8oz package of Philadelphia Cream Cheese, softened
1 8oz package of Pepper Jack Cheese
1 cup Ranch dressing
1 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 package of your favorite dipping chips ( I used Blue Corn Tortilla Chips)

Directions:
I grilled the chicken breasts on the outside grill until throughly done.
Mix cream cheese and pepper jack cheese together and add a few splashes of hot sauce (whatever kind you like).
Shred chicken into bite size pieces.
Mix all ingredients together in a casserole dish.
Add your shredded Cheddar Cheese on top.
Bake at 350 in oven until Cheddar Cheese is a golden brown.
