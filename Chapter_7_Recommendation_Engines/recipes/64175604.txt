Title:
Grapefruit Chermoula Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large grapefruit
1 large shallot, finely chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
3 T lemon juice
1 t kosher salt
1/2 small preserved lemon, flesh discarded, peel finely chopped (sub. fresh lemon peel in emergency)
1/2 c finely chopped fresh cilantro
2 T olive oil
2 t finely grated peeled ginger
2 t harissa paste or sriracha
1.5 t ground cumin
1 t honey
1/2 t tomato paste
freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Combine shallot, garlic, lemon juice, and 1 t salt in a medium bowl. Let stand 10 min to mellow shallot and garlic.
Remove peel and pith from grapefruit. Working over a bowl, cut sections away from membranes. Coarsely chop sections. Squeeze additional juice from membranes to make about 2 T juice.
Mix grapefruit, lemon peel, cilantro, oil, ginger, harissa, cumin, honey, tomato paste, and shallot mixture. Salt and pepper to taste.
Keeps 5 days in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
meghanmacrae
Description: 
North African condiment to complement vegetables and fish. Very nice over avocado halves (grilled or fresh).
