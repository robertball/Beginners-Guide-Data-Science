Title:
Gourmet Tuna Noodle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-5 button mushroms diced
2 shallots finely diced
2 C fresh broccoli
1 box(16oz) whole wheat rotini pasta(organic preferred)
1 (8oz) container heavy whipping cream(organic preferred)
1/2 C sour cream (organic preferred)
3/4 lb velveeta
1/4 C frozen peas
2(6oz) cans tuna in water, drained
2 T butter
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 C shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 C bread crumbs

Directions:
heat oven to 350 degrees.
Melt butter over a medium high heat saute mushrooms and shallots until tender.
meanwhile boil pasta in lightly salted boiling water according to package directions until al dente.  in the last 4-5 minutes of cooking add broccoli florets.  drain and set aside.
when onions and mushrooms are tender add drained tuna. Add heavy cream and bring just to a simmer.  Add velveeta and lower heat.  Stir until melted.  Remove frome heat and stir in sour cream until a smooth consistency.  Add sat and pepper to taste.
Toss the pasta and broccoli with the frozen peas. Mix with the sauce.  Spray a 9x11 baking dish with non stick cooking spray.  Pour pasta mixture in dish.  sprinkle with cheddar cheese and bread crumbs.
Bake in preheated oven until cheese is melted and a nice crust is formed.

Submitter: 
Maryam
Description: 
Tuna noodle casserole gone gourmet.  Whole wheat pasta in a sauce of mushrooms, shallots, heavy cream, cheese and finished with sour cream.  This isn't your mama's casserole.
