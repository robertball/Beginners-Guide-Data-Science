Title:
Pita Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Package Active Dry Yeast
3 1/2 Cups All Purpose Flour
1/4 Cup Shortening
1 1/2 Tspn Salt
1 1/4 Cup Warm Water

Directions:
In large bowl soften yeast in water (about 5 Mins)
Add About 2 Cups Flour, Salt and Shortening.  Mix 2 mins. with a fork.
Add as much of the remaining flour as you can mix with a spoon.
Turn on to a lightly floured work surface and kneed in enough flour to make a soft, smooth, elastic ball.
Cover and let rest in warm area for about 15 Mins.
Divide Dough into 12 equal portions.
Roll each peice into a ball with floured hands.
Cover with damp cloth and let rest for 10 more mins.
Flatten balls with finger tips. Cover an let rest 10 more minutes.
On a well floured surface, lightly roll one peice of dough at a time into a circle about 6 inches in diameter, being carefull not to puncture the dough.
Place a single layer on baking sheet and bake 3 to 4 minutes, until dough puffs up, flip with spatula and bake an additional 2 minutes. Repeat with remaining dough.  Only roll enough dough at a time to bake.  To serve: Slice pita in half and fill pockets with your favorite filling, or for dipping,  cut into wedges and pan fry in a little olive oil.
