Title:
Avocado Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983238.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ripe avocados, mashed
1/2 cup Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoons garlic, minced
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 cup Roma tomatoes, chopped
1/2 cup cilantro, chopped
4 slices pepper bacon, sauteed and cooled
1/2 cup onion, chopped

Directions:
Mix together avocado, slaw dressing, salt, pepper, garlic and olive oil until well blended.  Fold tomatoes, cilantro, bacon and onion into avocado mixture.  Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Description: 
A yummy dip for tortilla chips, it's also a wonderful topping for burgers and sandwiches.
