Title:
Eggnog Punch Float Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/567706.jpg
Ingredients:
2 quarts eggnog
2 (1.5 quart) containers vanilla ice cream, softened
2 (2 liter) bottles lemon-lime soda (such as 7 UpÂ®)
1 pinch ground cinnamon

Directions:
Pour eggnog slowly into a large bowl or pitcher. Add ice cream, 1 tub at a time, stirring with a big spoon. Pour in lemon-lime soda. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
