Title:
Big Ray's Spanish Chickpea Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3940867.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb chorizo, sliced (can use ground beef)
1 medium onion, chopped
1 medium red bell pepper, chopped
1 (15 oz) can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
1/2 tsp cumin
8 oz baby kale
1 Tbsp hot sauce
1 Tbsp olive oil
2 oz goat cheese, crumbled (can use feta)
2 (8 ") pita rounds, warmed and halved

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium heat, brown chorizo stirring occasionally about 6 minutes until cooked. remove from skillet with slotted spoon and drain on paper towels, reserving 2 Tbsp of drippings in the skillet.
Add onions and pepper and cook, stirring occasionally until tender about 5 minutes.  Stir in chickpeas and cumin; cook, stirring until heated through about 3 minutes.
Add kale and chorizo; cook until kale is wilted about 2-3 minutes.  Top with hot sauce, oil and cheese.  Serve with pita halves.
