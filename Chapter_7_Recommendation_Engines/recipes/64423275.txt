Title:
Roasted Greek Potatoes with Feta Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup water
1/3 cup olive oil
3 Tbsp. lemon juice
4 garlic cloves, minced
2 tsp. dried oregano
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
3 obs. Yukon Gold potatoes (about 6 medium, each cut into eight wedges
3/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 450 degrees.  In a bowl, whisk the first seven ingredients.  Arrange potatoes evenly in a shallow roasting pan.  Pour water mixture over the top.
2.  Roast 40-50 minutes or until potatoes are golden brown and tender.  Sprinkle with cheese.
