Title:
D's Salmon & Cheese (with an attitude) Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/569199.jpg
Ingredients:
Egg Tart Pastry:
2 cups flour
2 Tbsp sugar
2/3 cup butter
1 egg
Filling:
2 stocks chopped celery
6 chopped chives
3 large cloves minced garlic
2 Tbsp butter
3 Tbsp Best Food Olive oil mayonnaise
4 oz. softened cream cheese
3 Tbsp V-8 or tomato juice
2 c. crumbled smoked salmon
3 Tbsp.finely shredded smoked "Chipotle" cheese
12 bay shrimp

Directions:
Egg Tart Pastry:
Combine flour and sugar together. Cut in butter til pea size. With a fork, stir in slightly beaten egg. Form a ball.  Roll on lightly floured surface until quite thin but easily handled. Cut with biscuit or cookie cutter.Line ungreased muffin tins and bake 30 min. at 375 degrees. Cool shells and set aside.
Filling:
Combine mayo, softened cream cheese, V-8 or tomato juice, and crumbled salmon in medium size bowl.  Add cooled sauted celery, chives, and garlic.  Mix.  Fill cooled tart shells. Top with Chipotle cheese. Add bay shrimp for garnish.
