Title:
Cheeseburger Macaroni Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 # lean ground beef
1/2 C chopped onion
1 C uncooked whole wheat elbow macaroni (or whole wheat penne or rotini pasta)
1 med. tomato, chopped
8 oz can tomato sauce
1/2 t seasoned salt, opt.
1/8 t pepper
1 C shredded reduced-fat cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray 8x8 inch pan with nonstick spray; set aside. In large skillet, cook ground beef and onion until beef is browned and onion is soft; drain
Cook macaroni according to package directions, omitting salt; drain. Spoon macaroni into prepared pan. Spread beef mixture and chopped tomato over macaroni. Pour tomato sauce over dish and sprinkle with seasoned salt and pepper.
Sprinkle with cheese and cover loosely with foil; bake 35 min or until cheese is melted and edges of casserole are bubbling.
