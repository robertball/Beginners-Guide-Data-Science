Title:
Amy's Smothered Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/4 cup oil
1 T butter
1 medium onion
1 pkg baby bella mushrooms
salt
pepper
greek seasoning
1 1/2 cup colby jack cheese

Directions:
In a large skillet add the oil, heat on med high until shimmers.  Add the chicken, season each side nicely with pepper and greek seasoning, cook on each side for 3-4 minutes until nicely golden brown and then place the breasts into a baking dish. Discard all but about 1 T oil from the pan and add the 1 T of butter.  Add the onions and mushrooms to the skillet. Salt and Pepper the veg.  Cook until tender and cover the breasts with the veggies.  Cover the veggies with the cheese.  Bake in a 350 over for 30 minutes.  Keep an eye, if cheese gets too done cover with a foil tent to keep cheese melty.
