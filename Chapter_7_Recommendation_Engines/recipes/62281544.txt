Title:
CHILI CHEESECAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 Cups crushed tortilla chips
4 Tbl melted butter
3 (8oz) pkg Reduce fat cream cheese, rm temp
3 med eggs
8 oz pepper jack cheese, grated
1/2-3/4 cup sour cream
1 cup Pace,
3/4 cup shredded cheddar, opt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325
Coat 9" springform pan with Pam
Crush chips. Transfer to bowl & toss w/butter
spread in prepared pan.
Bake at 325 for 10-12 minutes. This can be done in advance
Process cream cheese in food processer until smooth. Add eggs & pepper jack cheese & mix well. Mound in large spoonfuls over chip crust & spread over crust. Bake at 325 for 25 min.
Remove & cool 6-8 min , then spread with sour cream 7 top with drained Pace. Garnish with cheddar, if desired.
