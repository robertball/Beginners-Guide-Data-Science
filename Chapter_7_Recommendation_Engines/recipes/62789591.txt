Title:
BBQ Chutney Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 onion, sliced in wedges
3 lbs. chicken pieces (thighs, drumsticks, and breast halves), skinned
1/4 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. ground black pepper
1/2 cup mango chutney
2/3 cup BBQ sauce
1 tsp. curry powder
1/2 mango, finely chopped
2 green onions, chopped
3 cups hot cooked rice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients (but rice, fresh mango and green onion) and cook on low for 4 hours.
Toss cooked rice, mango and green onions. Serve chicken over rice mixture.
