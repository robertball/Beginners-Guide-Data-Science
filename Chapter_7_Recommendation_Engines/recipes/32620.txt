Title:
Mango Lassi II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/199894.jpg
Ingredients:
2 mangos - peeled, seeded and diced
2 cups plain yogurt
1/2 cup white sugar
1 cup ice

Directions:
In a blender, combine mangos, yogurt, sugar and ice. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
YONNA
Description: 
An Indian yogurt drink - smooth, creamy, and absolutely heavenly!
