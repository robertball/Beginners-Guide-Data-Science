Title:
Quinoa stir fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. quinoa
2 T. oil
1 tsp. minced garlic
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 c. walnut pieces
1 bag (6 oz) fresh baby spinach or other greens
1 c. grape or cherry tomatoes
1/2 c. Pamigiano-Reggiano cheese
torn basil leaves (garnish)

Directions:
Rinse quinoa; drain well.
Heat oil in medium skillet.  Add quinoa.  Toast, stirring, over medium heat until golden, about 10 minutes.  Add garlic and cook, stirring, 1 minute.  Add salt and 2 c. water.  Cover and cook over medium-low heat until water is absorbed, about 15 minutes
Spread walnuts in small skillet and stir over medium-low heat until toasted, about 5 minutes.  Set aside.
When quinoa is cooked, add spinach and tomatoes to skillet.  Stir fry until spinach is almost wilted and tomatoes are warm.  Add cheese and garnish with basil leaves.
