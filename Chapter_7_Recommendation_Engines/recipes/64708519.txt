Title:
Nutty Cream Cheese-Stuffed Celery Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5521365.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound cream cheese, at room temperature
2 tablespoons cream or milk
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 1/3 cups walnuts, chopped
1/2 teaspoon onion salt
1 bunch celery, trimmed, separated into ribs, rinsed, leafy ends kept on

Directions:
1 of 3
In medium-size bowl, with an electric mixer on medium speed, beat together the cream cheese, milk and Worcestershire sauce until smooth, about 2 minutes. Stir in the walnuts, onion salt and pepper until thoroughly blended.
2 of 3
Cut celery ribs into 3-inch pieces. Fill each with about 1 tablespoon of the filling.
3 of 3
Arrange the celery on a serving platter. Cover loosely with plastic wrap. Refrigerate until ready to serve. Makes about 3-1/2 dozen pieces.
