Title:
Amazing Peach Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2511806.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Triple Sec or orange liquor like Cointreau
Juice from 1 lemon
2 tablespoons sugar
4 peaches
1 750 bottle of Prosecco, chilled
1 cup frozen grapes
1 cup frozen strawberries

Directions:
Add the Triple Sec, lemon juice and sugar in bowl and stir to dissolve.
Peel and dice the peaches and add to bowl. Combine all the ingredients and put in refrigerator overnight.
When ready to serve add peach mixture to pitcher, top with Prosecco and serve immediately.
Add a few frozen grapes and strawberries in each glass.  They act as garnish and as ice.

Submitter: 
Sophie Pipilas
Description: 
This peach sangria is light and refreshing and so easy to make.  I've tried many and I keep coming back to this one.
