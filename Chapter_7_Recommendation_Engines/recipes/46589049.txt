Title:
JC's Mexican Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup milk
1 (16 ounce) package frozen corn
1 red bell pepper, diced
1/4 cup of finely chopped onion
2 up to 8  fresh jalapeno peppers, finely diced
1 can of chopped chilies
Add a dash or 2 to taste of Seasoned salt
dash of Paprika
Garlic to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a saucepan over medium-low heat, melt the cream cheese and butter, and mix with the milk until smooth and bubbly.
Mix the ingredients with the cream cheese mixture, coat evenly.
Pour mixture in a medium casserole dish
Sprinkle top with Paprika
Bake 35 to 45 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly and lightly brown.
