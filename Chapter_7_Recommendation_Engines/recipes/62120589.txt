Title:
Tasty Saltine Toffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
40 saltine crackers
1 c brown sugar
1 c butter (no substitutes)
1 c chocolate chips
1 c sliced almonds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
Place foil over an edged baking sheet, lay out 40 crackers flat right next to one another.
Bring brown sugar and butter to a boil, boil for 3 minutes.
Pour brown sugar/butter mixture over the top of crackers, spread evenly so all crackers are completely covered.
Bake in oven for 5 minutes.
Upon removal from oven, sprinkle chocolate chips over top, let sit for 5 minutes to melt, then easily spread melted chocolate chips over top.
Sprinkle sliced almonds over all
Place in fridge to cool, cut into bite sized pieces when cooled. Enjoy!
