Title:
Big Ray's Perfect Bloody Mary Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7307108.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp hot giardiniera mix
1 Tbsp ground jalapeno pepper
1 Tbsp ground horseradish
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
1 tsp celery seed
4 dashes sriracha sauce
1 tsp lime juice
1 tsp lemon juice
1 48 oz can vegetable juice
celery stalks, olives, dill pickles/asparagus, bacon and or shrimp for garnish

Directions:
Blend ingredients until smooth.  Keep refrigerated. Salt rim of glass and add 2 oz. vodka to 4 oz. mixture for bloody mary, garnish as you wish.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
I like to prepare a jug of this and have it on hand in the fridge, not only for drinking bloody marys but to have as a morning drink without the alcohol to start the day.  I like it on the spicy side, so feel free to cut back on the spices if you prefer it on the mild side.
