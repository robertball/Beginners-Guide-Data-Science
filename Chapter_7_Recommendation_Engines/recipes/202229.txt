Title:
Cuervo Especial Pomegranate Margarita Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989893.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Cuervo Especial Gold
3 ounces Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix
2 ounces pomegranate juice
1 piece lime wheel
1 tablespoon salt

Directions:
Combine Cuervo Especial Gold, Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix and pomegranate juice into cocktail shaker half full with ice.
Shake well and strain into a chilled martini glass (rim glass with salt optional).
Garnish with lime or lemon wheel.
