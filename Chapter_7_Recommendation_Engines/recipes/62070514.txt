Title:
Quick and easy welfare spagetti sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz. can tomato sauce
1/2 cup pico de gallo
1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
1/2 cup white onions
4 tbsp. white sugar
pepper to taste

Directions:
in a medium saucepan combine all ingreedients and bring to a boil. Then reduce heat, cover and simmer for 10 min.
