Title:
Fire Roasted Pico de Gallo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1405838.jpg
Ingredients:
Olive Oil
4 Fresh whole Jalepenos
2 Fresh whole Onions
1 Bunch Fresh Cilantro tied
2 Fresh Tomatoes
4 Tblsp Roasted Garlic (prepared)
2 Limes (juice only)
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
1.  Place whole jalepenos, whole tomatoes, whole bunch of cilantro, halved onions in bowl and liberally douse with Olive Oil.  2.  Place on outside grill and use cilantro bunch as basting utensil.  3.  Char on all sides and remove to cool.  4.  Roast off the cilantro bundle last allowing bundle to char up on outside.  5.  Once cooled, rough chop all. 6.  Add garlic, Lime juice, salt and pepper to taste.  7.  Let rest another 20-30 mins stirring gently periodically to combine olive oils, lime juice, and roasted vegetable juice that forms. Refrigerate leftovers.

Submitter: 
MitchJGray
Description: 
You won't believe the sweetness and depth of roasted flavor. Good on grilled meats, with chips, on eggs.
