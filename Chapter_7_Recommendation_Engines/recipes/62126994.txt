Title:
Taco Nacho Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground beef
1 old el paso taco seasoning mix
1 large bag tostitos corn chips
1 8 oz shredded sharp cheddar or shredded colby/monterey
1 8 oz Breakstone Sour Cream
jalapenos
green onions (diced)
1 tomato (diced)
lettuce(shredded)
1/2 tsp.Crushed red pepper flakes
1 can Refried Beans (optional)

Directions:
Spray baking pan with cooking spray. Place chips in baking pan and bake in oven at 350 degrees for 5 minutes. In medium saucepan brown ground beef, drain and bring back to saucepan. Add taco seasoning and 2/3 cup of water. Remove chips from oven and pour ground beef mixture on top, then add cheese. Add tomatoes, jalapenos and green onions.Sprinkle pepper flakes on top. Place back in oven until cheese is melted. Once out of the oven add lettuce and sour cream. Enjoy.
