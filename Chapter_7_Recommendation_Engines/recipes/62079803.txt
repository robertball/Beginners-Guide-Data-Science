Title:
Broccoli and Cheese Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Rice
2 cups Water
2 small or 1 large package frozen chopped broccoli
1/2 cup onion, chopped
1/2 cup celery, chopped (optional)
3 to 4 tablespoons margarine
1 can mushrooms
1 jar cheez whiz
1 can cream of mushroom soup

Directions:
Cook Rice and water as directed on package.  Cook frozen broccoli as directed.  Saute chopped onion and celery in margarine until tender.  Add mushrooms and cook down liquid to about half.  Add Cheez Whiz and soup.  Stir to blend until heated through.  Add drained broccoli and rice.  Mix well and pour into 13x9 baking dish.  Bake at 375 for 30 to 45 minutes or until top is bubbly.  You can also refrigerate and bake next day.
