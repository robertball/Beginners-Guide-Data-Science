Title:
Chicken and Rice Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2pcs pieces of boneless chicken (or chicken legs, thighs, etc)
1/2cup rice
1/2onion, chopped fine
1/2tsp salt
1cup water
1/4cup celery, chopped
1/2can mushroom soup
1tbsp margarine or butter

Directions:
In a baking pan, place rice and chopped onion on the bottom. Boil the water. Mix all remaining ingredients in the pan and place the chicken on top. Sprinkle with pepper. Cover with tin foil. Bake at 350C for 45min (or until water is evaporated)

Submitter: 
Jewels
Description: 
Always a favorite as a child. My mom always made this and it was devoured by all. Warms my heart everytime I make it. It's cut to 2 servings for when I moved out on my own...but can easily be doubled for larger families.
