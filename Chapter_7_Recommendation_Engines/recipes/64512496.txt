Title:
Dulce de Leche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 14oz. cans sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Place unopened cans of milk in slow cooker.
Fill cooker with warm water so that it comes above the cans by 1 1/2-2 inches
Cover cooker.   Cook on High 2 hours
Cool unopened cans
When opened, the contents should be thick and spreadable.

Submitter: 
Natasha Sellner
Description: 
Use as a filling between 2 cookies or crackers.  Can also be used as a spread on bread or toast.
