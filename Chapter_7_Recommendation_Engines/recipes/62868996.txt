Title:
Penne with Vodka Sauce & Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/688936.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box (14-1/2 oz.) Barilla Plus Multigrain Penne
1 pounds zucchini, cut into 1/2 inch quarter moons
1 cups frozen peas
8 cups baby spinach
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 small onion, peeled and finely chopped
6 ounces sliced Canadian bacon, chopped
1/3 cup vodka
1 can (15 oz.) no-salt-added diced tomatoes
1 can (8 oz.) no-salt-added tomato sauce
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1/2 cup fat-free half and half
1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add penne and cook following package directions, 11 minutes. add zucchini and peas during last 3 minutes. Stir in spinach. Drain and place back into the pot.
Meanwhile, back on the ranch, heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion and cook 5 minutes, stirring occasionally; add Canadian bacon and vodka and cook 1 minute. Stir in tomatoes, tomato sauce, salt and red pepper. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes.
Stir half-and-half and 1/2 of the Parmesan cheese into the sauce. Toss with pasta and let stand for 10 minutes. Stir and spoon into serving bowl and top with remaining Parmesan cheese.
