Title:
Italian Sausage Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/731481.jpg
Ingredients:
- Penne Pasta
- Italian Sausage (Mild or Medium)
- 2 8-oz Tomato Sauce Cans
- 1 Red Onion (Optional)
- 3 Bell Peppers (Red, Yellow & Green "For color")
- 1-2 Tbsp of Basil
- 1 Tbsp of Oregano
- Salt and Pepper (As much desired for flavor)

Directions:
1)In a skillet start cooking the Italian Sausage (Optional to cut it in 2-inch bite size pieces or have it whole) Note: "When sausage it cut in pieces it cooks quicker". Drain excess fat and Add the sauce, bring it to a Simmer.
2)While the sausage is cooking; In a Pan bring water to a boil and add Penne Pasta.
3) Cut the Bell Peppers & Onion vertically. If you wish to eat your Bell Peppers raw, if not in a skillet cook Bell Peppers & Red Onion until they are tender. (Substitute Olive Oil with Butter for more flavor)
4) When the Pasta is cooked, Mix all together with Italian Sausage, Bell Peppers & Onion. Serve and Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Mrs Sarah
Description: 
A simple and delicious Italian dinner that the whole family will enjoy!! Will basic flavors of Bell Peppers, Italian Sausage and Penne Pasta
