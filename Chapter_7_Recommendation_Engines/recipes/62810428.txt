Title:
B.C's Perfect Matzoh Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Eggs
1-1/2 Cups of Matzoh Meal
Salt
Pepper
6 Tbsps Vegetable Oil
3/4 Cup of Seltzer Water
3 qts of Chicken Broth
Parsley for Garnish

Directions:
Mix all of the ingredients.  The batter should be the consistency of pancake batter.
Refrigerate for 2 hours.
Wet hands and form matzoh batter into balls just slightly larger than golf balls.
Heat chicken broth to a slow boil.
Using a large cooking spoon, gently lower the raw matzoh balls into boiling chicken broth.
Once all of the matzoh balls have been placed into the broth and broth is boiling, reduce heat and simmer covered for 1 hour.
Serve garnished with parsley

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
These light and fluffy matzoh balls will melt in your mouth.  This recipe was given to me by my friend BC Marks in Mazatlan Mexico.
