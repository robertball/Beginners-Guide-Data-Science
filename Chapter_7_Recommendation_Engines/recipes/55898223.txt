Title:
Cowboy Bake Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
6 pieces of bacon,fried
2 (16 ounce) can kidney beans, with liquid
1 (16 ounce) can butter beans, drained
1 (16 ounce) can pork and beans
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1Tbls liquid smoke
1teaspoons ground mustard
1Tbls worchester sauce

Directions:
Brown hamburger, fry bacon.
combine all ingredients in a crokpot and cook on high for 3to 4 hours,or on low for 6 hours

Submitter: 
risa
Description: 
All our friends love this dish...it tastes so good with ground beef and a blend of different beans. Brown sugar and mustard are a nice accent to the tomato paste.--Linda Biggs, Canfield, OhioModified from: Cowboy Bake Beans
