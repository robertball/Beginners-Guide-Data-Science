Title:
Molasses-Almond Quinoa Vegan Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Quinoa
2 cups water
Bring to a boil, then simmer for 20 minutes.
3 Tablespoons Flax Seeds
1 cup warm water
1 cup Spelt Flour
1 cup  Amaranth Flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup chopped raw almonds
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup agave
1/2 cup succanat
1/4 cup molasses
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 teaspoons almond extract

Directions:
Make Quinoa then preheat oven 350 F.
In a blender add 3 Tablespoons flax seeds and 1 cup warm water.
Let sit for 10 minutes.
In a bowl mix flours, baking powder, salt, almonds and 2 cups of cooked quinoa.
Set aside.
Add vanilla extract, almond extract, agave, succanat and molasses to flax seeds mixture and blend at a high speed.
Add wet mixture from blender to the bowl of flours.
Mix thoroughly.
Use a tablespoon to spoon out onto greased baking pan or just parchment paper.
Bake for 12-15 minutes.
Let cool and store away.

Description: 
Quinoa a super grain in a cookie! Molasses and Almonds add extra nutrition to these tasty treats. Enjoy!
