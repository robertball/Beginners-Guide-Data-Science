Title:
Soda Cracker Candy II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package saltine crackers
1 1/2 cups butter (do not substitute margarine)
1 1/2 cups packed dark brown sugar
1 (12 ounce) package chocolate chips
1 cup chopped almonds, pecans, or walnuts

Directions:
* Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
* Line a 10x15 inch jelly roll pan with NON-STICK aluminum foil or regular aluminum foil generously sprayed with non-stick cooking spray.
* Place crackers in a single layer over prepared pan. Use more or fewer crackers as needed to completely cover the bottom of your pan. Fill in spaces with cracker pieces.
* In a small saucepan over low heat combine butter and brown sugar; bring to a full, rolling boil. Then set a timer to boil for 3 minutes stirring occasionally.
* Pour as evenly as possible over crackers to cover.
* Bake in preheated oven for 5 minutes. Top will bubble in oven.
* Remove from oven and turn oven off now.
* Sprinkle chocolate chips evenly over the top.
* Return to oven (turned off) for 2 minutes.
* Remove and use a spatula to spread chocolate chips evenly over the top.
* Sprinkle almonds over chocolate chips.
* Press nuts into chocolate so they stick.
* Chill in refrigerator for at least 2-3 hours, or until set.
* Break into pieces and store, sealed, in refrigerator.

Submitter:

Description: 
Saltine crackers are smothered with butter and brown sugar, then topped with chocolate chips and chopped nuts.  This is a holiday treat you can give as gifts, or keep all to yourself!Modified from: Soda Cracker Candy
