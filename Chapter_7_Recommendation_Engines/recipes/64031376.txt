Title:
wiccan Samhain soul cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter or margarine
3 tablespoons sugar
1 1/2 cups flour

Directions:
soften butter
mix the flour and sugar in a large bowl
while mixing pour in the butter
make into small round cookies.
Bake at 325 for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Ashley Johnson
Description: 
a sweet treat to honer the wiccan new year.
