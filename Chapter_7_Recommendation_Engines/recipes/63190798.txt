Title:
Pete's Creamed Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp. oil
1/2 c. chopped onions
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 T. butter or margarine
2 T. flour
1 c. milk
1/4 t. salt
1/4 t. nutmeg
1/8 t. pepper
Fresh spinach (8-10 oz. bag), chopped

Directions:
In a small pan, saute onion and garlic together until caramelized. Set aside.
In a 12" pan, melt butter, stir in flour, and add milk. Stir constantly until thick.
Add seasonings.
Add onion and garlic mixture.
Add spinach and cook  over low heat until wilted.
Serve immediately.
