Title:
Fresh Strawberry Banana Sunrise Smoothie With Truvia® Natural Sweetener Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/998452.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup strawberries
1 banana
1 cup nonfat plain yogurt
1 cup orange juice
1 cup ice
2 tablespoons Truvia® natural sweetener spoonable, plus
1 teaspoon Truvia® natural sweetener spoonable*

Directions:
Add all ingredients to blender.
Blend on high until smooth.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Truvia®
Description: 
This smoothie features the popular flavor combination of strawberry and banana in a tangy treat sweetened with Truvia® natural sweetener.
