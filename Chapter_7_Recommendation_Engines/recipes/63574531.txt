Title:
Mediterranean Greek Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cucumber, seeded and sliced
1 1/2 cups crumbled feta cheese serve on side
1 cup black olives, serve on side
3 cups diced roma tomatoes
1/2 red onion, sliced
1 large head green leaf lettuce shredded
1 head Romaine shredded

Directions:
In a large salad bowl, toss together the cucumber, lettuce, roma tomatoes, and red onion. Serve the oilves and feta on the side. Chill until serving.

Submitter: 
Anne Soie
Description: 
Hearty, robust, and delicious! All the stuff you dreamed about in a Greek salad  - tomatoes, olives, feta cheese, cucumbers, and sun-dried tomatoes - all glistening from a splash of oil.Modified from: Mediterranean Greek Salad
