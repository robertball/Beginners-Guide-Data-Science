Title:
Sizzlin Steak a la Montreal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 teaspoons McCormick® Grill Mates® Montreal Steak Seasoning
2 teaspoons McCormick® Ground Mustard
1 1/2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
2 New York strip or rib eye steaks, cut 1-inch thick

Directions:
Combine Grill Mates(R) Montreal Steak(R) Seasoning, ground mustard and balsamic vinegar to make a paste. Rub paste over both sides of steak.
Grill or broil over high heat 5 to 7 minutes per side or to desired degree of doneness.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
When you're looking to liven up a meal, try Sizzlin' Steak a la Montreal. McCormick® Montreal Steak® Seasoning, paired with balsamic vinegar and ground mustard, will jazz up any steak.
