Title:
Best Chocolates Chip Cookies! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4539660.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of butter
1 cup of packed light brown sugar
1/2 sugar
1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
2 eggs
2 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 11.5 oz bag of milk chocolate chips (or 1 1/2 cups)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees for dark or non stick  cookie sheets or 350 for others.
Combine butter, sugars, vanilla and eggs until creamed (for best results use room temperature butter and eggs).
Add dry ingredients and mix until well combined.
Fold in chocolate chips until evenly incorporated.
For best results let dough chill for 30 minutes before baking and bake for 12-14 minutes.

Submitter: 
Krystel
Description: 
After trying many different recipes, and experimenting I have found the best way to make a wonderfully delicious chocolate chip cookie that isn't too soft and cake like yet not hard and flat!
