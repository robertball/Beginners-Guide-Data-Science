Title:
Pita Pizza for One Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pita
3 tablespoons pizza sauce
3 tablespoons part-skim (low-fat) mozzarella cheese
1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese
Optional GP-friendly toppings
tolerated veggies (cooked spinach, mushrooms, etc.)
lean ground turkey, cooked and drained
skinless chicken breast, cooked and shredded

Directions:
Preheat oven or toaster oven to 400 degrees.
Spread sauce on pita and top with desired toppings.
Sprinkle on cheeses.
Broil in oven or toaster oven for 4-5 minutes until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
This recipe from the Gastroparesis-Friendly Kitchen is a great family-friendly meal idea since each family member can choose their own toppings.
