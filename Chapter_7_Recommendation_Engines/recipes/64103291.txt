Title:
Buttermilk Maple Bacon Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1238843.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
2 cups sugar
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
3 eggs
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 1/2 cups buttermilk
1 tsp maple flavoring
1 tsp vanilla
4 slices crisp bacon- crumbled into bits

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Mix together dry ingredients.
Add eggs, buttermilk, maple, and vanilla. Mix well. Stir in crumbled bacon.
Fill cupcake tins 2/3 full. Bake for 17-20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sherillynn
Description: 
These cupcakes won first at the Winneshiek County Fair in the Bacon Dessert competition. They remind me of pancakes!
