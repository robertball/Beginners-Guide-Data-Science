Title:
celery tuna casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans campbells Cream of Mushroom Soup
1/2 cup milk
1 cup celery
2 cans tuna
2 cups hot cooked medium egg noodles
2 tablespoons dry bread crumbs
1 tablespoon butter or margarine

Directions:
Mix soup, milk, celery, tuna and cooked noodles in a casserole dish. Bake at 400 degrees F. for 20 minutes or until hot. Stir.
Mix bread crumbs with butter and sprinkle on top. Bake 5 minutes.  Then enjoy!
