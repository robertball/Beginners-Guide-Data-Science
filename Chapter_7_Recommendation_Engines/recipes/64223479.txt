Title:
Stevie's Bishops Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 cups Crisco ( butter works too)
2 cups brown sugar
3/4 cup buttermilk
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. soda
2 beaten eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven 425 degrees.
Mix flour, sugar, salt and Crisco. Take out 1/2 cup of this mixture, then add baking powder, soda, cinnamon, eggs and milk to remainder.  Beat until smooth. Pour into shallow pan or bread pan. Scatter the 1/2 bread crumbs on top, sprinkle with cinnamon. Bake for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Amy Gooch
Description: 
Our family grew up on this from my older brother's recipe.  Now that he's left us, his recipe lives on. A simple, not overly rich or filling sweet bread that goes famously with breakfast or just by itself.
