Title:
Speedy Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cans (14-1/2 ounces each) petite diced tomatoes, drained
2 medium onions, chopped
1/2 cup fresh cilantro, minced
2 jalapeno peppers, seeded and minced
2 tablespoons sugar
1 teaspoon salt
Tortilla chips

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the tomatoes, onions, cilanto, jalapenos, sugar and salt.
May serve immediately  or cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
Serve with tortilla chips
