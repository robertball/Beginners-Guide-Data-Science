Title:
Creamy Chicken Gnocchi Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993910.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon extra virgin oil
2 stalks celery, diced
1 yellow onion, diced
1/2 red bell pepper, diced
1 large zucchini, diced
2 carrots, shredded
4 minced cloves of garlic
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup flour
1 cup half and half
1 pint fat-free half and half
4 cups chicken broth, estimate
2 cups rotisserie chicken meat, shredded or diced
1 16 ounce package small gnocchi (or large cut in half)
2 cups fresh spinach, torn
salt and pepper
1/2 teaspoon thyme
1/4 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg

Directions:
Saute the onion, celery, pepper, carrots and garlic in the butter and olive oil over medium heat until soft.
Add butter, melt, then add the flour and make a roux.  Cook 2 minutes.
Add 3 cups chicken broth and cook until thickened.
Add all of the half and half and thicken again.
Add the chicken, gnocchi, spinach, and seasoning.
Simmer until gnocchi are cooked.
Adjust seasoning and liquid (chicken broth)to taste.

Submitter: 
Kristin
Description: 
This is a favorite for cool weather sports nights when we all eat at different times.  After adding the chicken and gnocchi, throw it in a crock pot on the lowest setting so it is ready when you are.  Serve with pesto bread sticks.
