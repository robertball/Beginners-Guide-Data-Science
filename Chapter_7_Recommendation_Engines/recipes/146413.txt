Title:
BUSH'S® Barbeque Bean Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992933.jpg
Ingredients:
6 (6 inch) flour tortillas
Vegetable cooking spray
1 (16 ounce) can BUSH'S® Barbecue Baked Beans
1 1/2 cups shredded Monterey Jack and Cheddar cheese blend
1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
Sour cream
Thinly sliced jalapenos
Cherry tomato slices

Directions:
Preheat grill to medium.
Spray one side of each tortilla lightly with cooking spray. Place tortilla, spray side down, on board. Spoon 1/4 cup of beans on half of the tortilla. Sprinkle with 1/4 cup of cheese and 1 tablespoon cilantro. Fold remaining tortilla half over filling (half-moon shape).
Grill over medium heat until tortillas are lightly browned and crisp on bottom, 2 to 3 minutes on each side. Remove from grill and cut each quesadilla in half. Serve with sour cream, sliced cherry tomatoes and jalapeno slices.

Submitter: 
BUSH'S® Beans
Description: 
A new twist on quesadillas! The Barbecue Baked Beans give this dish a sweet flavor.
