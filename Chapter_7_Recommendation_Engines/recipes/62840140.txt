Title:
Simple Broccoli Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups water
1 package (6 oz.) chicken flavor stuffing mix
2 cups cooked and cubed chicken
1 cup broccoli florets, chopped
1 can condensed broccoli cheese soup, undiluted
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a small saucepan, bring water to a boil.  Stir in stuffing mix.  Remove from heat; cover and let stand for 5 minutes.
Layer chicken and broccoli in a greased 9"x13" baking dish.  Top with soup.  Fluff stuffing with a fork, then spoon over soup.  Sprinkle with cheese.
Bake uncovered at 350 for 30-35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kelly Michael Hammer
Description: 
Very easy chicken casserole even the kids will enjoy.
