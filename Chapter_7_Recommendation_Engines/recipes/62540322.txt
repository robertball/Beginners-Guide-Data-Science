Title:
Easy Turkey Potato Croquettes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups of mashed potatoes
1.5 cups of cooked turkey (or chicken)
2 eggs
1 tablespoon fresh or dried parsley
salt & pepper to taste (optional)
Italian bread crumbs or Panko crumbs
Vegetable oil for frying
gravy (optional)

Directions:
1. Combine first 5 ingredients. Form mixture into patties (approx. 1.2 inch thick & 2 inches wide). Dredge patties in bread crumbs or panko crumbs.
2. Heat vegetable oil and fry patties until lightly browned on each side. Serve with leftover thanksgiving gravy (optional).

Submitter: 
WorkingMom
Description: 
My dad used to make these for dinner the day after Thanksgiving. GREAT way to use up leftover turkey and mashed potatoes! Serve with leftover gravy. You can also use leftover chicken from Sunday dinners!
