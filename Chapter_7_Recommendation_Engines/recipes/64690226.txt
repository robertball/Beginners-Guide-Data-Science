Title:
Hula's Chocolate Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks butter
2/3 cup cocoa powder
3 cups powdered sugar
1/3 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Melt 2 sticks of butter
Combine 2/3 cup cocoa powder with melted butter
Add 3 cups powdered sugar + 1/3 cup milk + 1 tsp vanilla to the cocoa/butter mixture and beat well
