Title:
Lemon Supreme  Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 9 inch unbaked pie crust
Lemon Filling:
1 1/4 cup sugar (divided)
6 TBSP cornstarch
1/2 tsp. salt
1 1/4 cup water
2 TBSP butter
1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
Yellow food coloring (optional)
Cream Cheese Filling
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
3 oz. cream cheese, softened
3/4  cup powdered sugar
1 1/2 cup cool whip
1 TBSP fresh lemon juice
Whipped cream for topping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Line an unpricked pastry shell with a double thickness of foil. Bake 8 minutes, remove foil and bake 5 minutes longer. Cool on a wire rack.
Lemon filling:
In a saucepan, combine 3/4 cup of sugar, cornstarch and salt, stir in water; bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Reduce heat and add remaining sugar. Cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened and bubbling. Remove from heat; stir in butter. Gently stir in lemon juice (do not over mix). Cool to room temperature about 1 hour. Do not stir. Add food coloring now, if desired.
Cream cheese filling:
In a mixing bowl beat cream cheese and powdered sugar until smooth. Fold in whipped topping and lemon juice. Spread cream cheese mixture into pie shell; top with lemon filling and garnish with shipped cream. Chill overnight. Store in the refrigerator.
