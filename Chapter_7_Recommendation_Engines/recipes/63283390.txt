Title:
Simple Semi-Homemade Low Carb No-Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/864240.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs or more of Ground Meat (beef, turkey, or chicken)
2 approx 14oz Cans of diced tomatoes
2 approx 15oz Cans of crushed tomatoes
2 pkgs of Chili mix (any heat level)
or your own spices (Cumin, Chili Powder, and Garlic Pepper)

Directions:
In a large pot (dutch oven) on your stove top, brown your ground meat. Sometimes I add a 2 Tablespoons or so of olive oil to help.
Dump your 2 cans diced tomatoes with juice, and 2 cans crushed tomatoes on top of cooked meat. Stir.
Pour your spices on top. Stir. Put on a lid at medium heat and cook for 20-30 min.

This is good, low carb, and really tasty! Mmmm and so flexible to your taste and in it's final use. But first, it's Chili with no beans!
