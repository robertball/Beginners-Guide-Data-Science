Title:
Beefaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb extra lean ground beef
1 large onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1.5 tsp minced garlic
1.5 tsp Worcestershire sauce
Ground black pepper (to taste)
1.5 Cups elbow macaroni
2 14.5 oz cans tomato sauce
Grated Parmesan cheese (if desired)

Directions:
1) In a large saute pan, brown ground beef. Do not drain.
2) Add onions, green pepper, garlic, worcestershire sauce, and black pepper. Cook 3-5 minutes, stirring occasionaly.
3) Stir in elbow macaroni. Cook about 2 minutes.
4) Add tomato sauce. Stir mixture. Bring to boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 30 minutes, stirring 2-3 times. Serve hot, topping with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.
