Title:
Sierras Salmon Teriyaki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 TBSP soy sauce
3 TBSP Pure maple syrup
1-2 cloves crushed garlic
1 tsp crushed ginger
1 tsp sriracha
Cornstarch to thicken

Directions:
preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
in sauce pan whisk the 3tbsp soy sauce, 3 tbsp maple syrup, 1-2 cloves garlic, 1 tsp ginger, 1 tsp sriracha,  over med heat, can add cornstarch to thicken
Place salmon on sheet pan with broccoli and pour sauce over salmon to coat it. bake 10-12 min or until cooked through. garnish with green onion,
