Title:
Russian chicken with feta cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/667364.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken fillets
3 tsp Vegeta or similar seasoning
5 tbs butter
1 clove garlic, pressed
1 small onion (60g), chopped
1 tbs flour
30 ml vodka
100 ml water
1 tbs tomato concentrate
½ tsp pepper
200 ml cream
100 g feta cheese
1 tbs parsley. chopped

Directions:
Lightly rub chicken fillets with Vegeta. Heat 3 tablespoons of butter in a frying pan and fry the fillets until golden.
Remove from the pan and place in an ovenproof dish.
Add to the pan in which the fillets were fried 2 remaining tablespoons of butter, sauté garlic and onion for several minutes until the onion is tender.
Stir in flour, then add vodka and water and bring to the boil.
Stir in tomato paste, pepper, sour cream and feta cheese. Simmer, gently stirring, until the sauce becomes thick.
Pour the prepared sauce over chicken and bake in the preheated oven at 180°C for 30 minutes.
Sprinkle with parsley, serve with gnocchi or cooked rice.
