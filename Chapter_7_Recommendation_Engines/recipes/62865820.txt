Title:
Sirloin Steak Marinade by Laurie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 tablespoons cooking white wine
1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
1 tablespoon worcheshire sauce
2 teaspoons yellow mustard
1 clove minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon pepper
2 Sirloin Steaks

Directions:
1. In a small bowl, combine all ingredients but steak.  Put meat in a shallow dish.  Pour Marinade over steak.  Cover and refridgerate for 6 hours.
2.  Grill steak on medium to high heat 5 minutes each side or until desired doneness.
