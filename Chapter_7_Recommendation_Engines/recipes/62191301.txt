Title:
Delicious Fruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz fat-free peach yogurt
6 oz strawberry cheesecake yogurt
2 1/2 teaspoons white sugar
1/4 cup fresh blackberries
1 small banana, sliced
3/4 cup frozen sliced strawberries
1/4 cup apple juice
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 tablespoons milk

Directions:
Place all of the ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth, about 2 minutes.
Pour and enjoy!
