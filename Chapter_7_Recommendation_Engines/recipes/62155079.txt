Title:
Simple Saucy Steak Tips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Pounds of sirloin tips (cubed)
2 Cups of beef broth or water
1 Bottle of your favorite steak marinade
2 Heaping tablespoons of minced garlic
1 Medium onion (sliced)
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
1.) Combine all ingredients (except sliced onions) in a large sauce pan and cook over medium-high heat until the liquid thickens and the steak is done & tender (about 30-45 minutes). Add onions during the last 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Dominique Lynne
Description: 
A tasty way prepare steak tips! Given to me by a close friend, it was one of the first recipes I tried after I moved out on my own! Everyone always loves them because you can adjust the recipe to suit your own taste...add different spices or other kinds of veggies and it still comes out great! Sooo easy...you really can't go wrong!
