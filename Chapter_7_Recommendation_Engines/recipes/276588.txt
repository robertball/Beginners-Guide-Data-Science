Title:
Em's Arugula Salad with Mango Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
Dressing:
1/4 cup lemon juice
1/4 cup olive oil
2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
1 teaspoon agave nectar
1 dash garlic powder
Salad:
1 large mango, cubed
1 avocado, diced
3/4 cup roasted, salted macadamia nuts
5 cups arugula
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix lemon juice, olive oil, vinegar, mustard, agave, and garlic powder together in a bowl.
Toss mango, avocado, and macadamia nuts together in a separate bowl; pour in the dressing. Place salad on top of arugula. Toss when ready to serve and season with pepper.

Submitter: 
EmWillFish
Description: 
If you like arugula, you'll love this salad with mango! Simple, fast, and good for you. Great for a refreshing side dish or a light summer dinner.
