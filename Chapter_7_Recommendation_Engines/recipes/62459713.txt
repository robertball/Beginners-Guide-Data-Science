Title:
Seafood & Vegetable Coconut Curry Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/470944.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tbs olive oil
3 green onions, chopped
10 shrimp
6 scallops
2 cans coconut water
6 mushrooms, thinly sliced
2 baby zucchinis, thinly sliced
1/4 cup pickled jalepenos
4 tsp yellow curry powder
1 tsp vegetable "Better Than Bouillon"
3 tsp salt
Sriracha hot sauce

Directions:
Saute the chopped green onions in a pot.
Add the shrimp and scallops to the pot and cook for a few minutes.
Pour the coconut water into the pot and bring to a boil.
Add the mushrooms, zucchini, jalepenos, curry powder, bouillon, salt, and hot sauce. Let it cook for 5-10 minutes and serve.
