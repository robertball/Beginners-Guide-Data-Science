Title:
John's Tasty German Cherry Beer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5238384.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup cherry juice
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle wheat beer (Hefeweizen)

Directions:
Pour cherry juice into a 16 ounce glass; pour in beer. Drink!

Submitter: 
ERLENSEE_GERMANY
Description: 
This drink is remarkable!  I used to order it at my favorite bar in Gelnhausen, Germany!  If you don't like the taste of beer alone, you will love this drink!
