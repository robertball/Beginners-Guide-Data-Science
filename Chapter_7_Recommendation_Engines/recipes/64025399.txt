Title:
Hot Fudge Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz Nestles milk chocolate chips or semi sweet
14 oz sweetened condensed milk
14 oz light Karo syrup
1/2 lb butter

Directions:
In the top of a double boiler over simmering water, melt chocolate chips into the condensed milk and Karo syrup until smooth.
Add butter and stir until melted
Cook on low for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally
Beat with an electric mixer and serve.
