Title:
Mini pirozhki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 t yeast
1/4 c water
1 c milk
6 bT butter
2 eggs
1 T sugar
1 t salt
4-5 c flour
FILLING
2 onion
2 T oil
2 hard boiled eggs
2 T stock
2 T dill (fresh)
1 T parsley
2 t salt
2 t pepper

Directions:
Make the bread and roll into 48 balls 3 1/2" circle
Cook onion in oil and mix all.
Fill and rise for 30 min.
Bake 350* for 20 min
