Title:
Big Ray's Western Beef  Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1212352.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey or beef
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1 can (11 oz) Mexican-style corn, drained
1 can (11 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
1 cup (4 ounces) shredded cheddar cheese
3/4 cup salsa
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp chili powder
2 small boxes corn muffin mix
1 cup (4 ounces) shredded cheddar cheese, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
In a skillet, brown ground beef, add onions and garlic cooking until onion is limp; drain grease. Stir in remaining filling ingredients; set aside.
Prepare corn muffins as suggested on box, adding 1/2 cup cheddar cheese; mix well. Spread on the bottom and up the sides of a greased 2-1/2-qt. baking dish or 10-in. ovenproof skillet. Bake crust for about 10 minutes.
Pour filling into crust. Sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake, uncovered for 15-20 minutes or until cheese is melted and browned..
