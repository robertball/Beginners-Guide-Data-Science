Title:
Big Ray's Peanut Butter Cake Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1132029.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup butter, melted
1 cup peanut butter
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
4 eggs
2 tsp vanilla extract
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 package (11-1/2 ounces) semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix the butter, sugar and brown sugar well, mix in the peanut butter. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Beat in vanilla. Combine the flour, baking powder and salt; gradually add to creamed mixture. Stir in chocolate chips.
Spread into a greased 13-in. x 9-in. baking pan. Bake at 350Â° for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool on a wire rack. Cut into bars.
