Title:
Chicken and Vegetable Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (32-ounce) container fat free chicken broth
4 skinned, bone in chicken breast halves (about 2 1/4 pounds)
1 medium onion, chopped
2 celery ribs, chopped
1 (16 ounce) package frozen baby lima beans
1 (14 ounce) package frozen white corn
1 (14 1/2 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
1/3 cup ketchup
1/4 cup chopped country ham
1 tablespoon sugar
3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 to 1 teaspoon hot sauce
1 teaspoon dried marjoram

Directions:
Bring broth to a boil in a Dutch oven over medium high heat.  Add chicken, onion, and celery, and return to a boil.  Reduce heat, and simmer 30 minutes or until chicken is tender.  Remove chicken from pan, and let cool slighty.
Remove chicken from bones, discarding bones; shred chicken.  Add corn, next 9 ingredients, and chicken to Dutch oven.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, 30 minutes or until beans are tender.

Submitter:

Description: 
This one pot dish is not only easy to prepare it is healthy. Kids love it too! We always have this simple stew with hot out of the oven cornbread.  Yummy on those cool fall nights.
