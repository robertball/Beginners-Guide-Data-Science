Title:
WW Lemony Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBSP butter
1/2 tsp grated lemon rind
1 TBSP fresh lemon juice
1 tsp olive oil
4 cups broccoli florets
2 TBSP water
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp reshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Place 1st 4 ingredients in a small bowl; cover and microwave for 45 seconds.  Stir to blend.
Place broccoli in a larger bowl; add 2 TBSP water and cover with wax paper.  Microwave on high for 4 minutes or until crisp-tender.  Drain well.
Drizzle lemon mixture over broccoli, salt & pepper and toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Shannon Campbell
Description: 
Serve this broccoli dish with roasted meats or seafood.  The lemon sauce can be used to brighten the flavor of steamed asparagus, green beans, or brussel sprouts
