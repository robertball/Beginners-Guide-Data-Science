Title:
Hot German Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1004483.jpg
Ingredients:
5 pounds red potatoes, diced
1 pound bacon
2 sweet onions, diced
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup water
1 cup white vinegar
2 tablespoons sugar
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Boil the diced potatoes in salted water until done.  Meanwhile, fry bacon in large skillet, until cooked through.  Remove bacon to clean paper towels to drain.  Add diced onion to bacon drippings and saute until translucent.  Add garlic and saute for another minute or two.  Remove from heat.
In bowl, combine water, vinegar, sugar and salt.  Place potatoes in large slow cooker. Add the sauteed onions and garlic, along with the bacon drippings.  Add crumbled bacon and the vinegar mixture.  Stir to combine.  Cook on low heat 3-4 hours, stirring occasionally.

Delicious, tangy German potato salad is a great side dish for bratwurst.
