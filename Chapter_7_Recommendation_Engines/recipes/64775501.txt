Title:
Slow Cooker Large Pearl Tapioca Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup large tapioca pearls
4 cups whole milk
3/4 cup sugar
3 eggs beaten
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Place tapioca pearls in a bowl and fill with water. Stir tapioca around and drain. Refill the bowl with water and allow to sit for at least 7 hours and up to overnight. Drain the tapioca and set aside.
Place milk, sugar, eggs, vanilla and salt in a bowl or slow cooker. Stir well. Add tapioca and cook on warm for 4 to 6 hours. Cool and store in air tight container.
