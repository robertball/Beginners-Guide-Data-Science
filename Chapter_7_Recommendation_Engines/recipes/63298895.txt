Title:
Lemon Cupcakes ~ Lemon Version of Sweet Vanilla Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups plus 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 1/8 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt (heaping)
1/2 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
1 teaspoon lemon extract
1 tablespoon lemon zest
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 stick (1/2 cup) butter, room temperature
1 cup sugar
2 eggs, room temperature

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Prepare muffin pan with liners.
Sift together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
Mix milk and vanilla, lemon extract, lemon zest and lemon juice in a separate cup.
Cream butter and then add sugar a little at a time. Mix until light and fluffy.
Beat in the eggs one at a time until light and fluffy.
Add flour mixture and milk mixture alternately, starting with flour mixture and ending with flour mixture until fully--but not overly--mixed.
Fill muffin papers 1/2 to 3/4 full. Bake for 18-20 minutes until top springs back lightly when touched.

Description: 
Lemon version of Sweet Vanilla Cupcakes that are so popular here at AR Buzz (Marianne's Personal Recipe for Sweet Vanilla Cupcakes*). This lemon  version has a refreshing lemony flavor and big crumb with a texture similar to the original vanilla version.  Woderful with cream cheese frosting.
