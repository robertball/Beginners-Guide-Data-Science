Title:
Cowboy Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Head of Lettuce
3 Tomatoes
1-2 Cucumbers
Onions (optional)
Other Salad Items (optional)
Small Bottle Catalina or Sweet French Dressing
Fritos (Half a bag - or to your liking)
1 cup Shredded Cheddar
1 can Ranch Style Beans (drained)

Directions:
Make a normal garden salad using ingredients you like.  Add Catalina Dressing, fritos, cheese, and beans.  Toss and serve.

Submitter: 
Vicki Guthrie Reece
Description: 
Love this recipe for family dinners and to take for a potluck.
