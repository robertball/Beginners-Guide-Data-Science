Title:
Chocolate Dipped Orange Shortbread Rounds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/754398.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter, softened
1/2 cup superfine sugar*
2 tsp. finely grated orange peel
1/4 tsp salt
2 cups all-purpose flour
3 tbsp cornstarch
2 bars (100 g each) good quality bittersweet chocolate Lindt or Ghirardelli, chopped

Directions:
Beat together butter, sugar, orange rind and salt until fluffy; blend in flour and cornstarch until smooth.
Divide dough in half.  Form each half  into a 10" long log, a little over 1" in diameter.  Wrap in plastic and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
Preheat oven to 325 F.  Slice into 1/4" rounds and place 1" apart on parchment-lined baking sheets.  Bake 15 minutes or until firm.  Cool on baking sheets 5 minutes, then remove to wire racks to cool completely.
Melt chocolate.  Dip each cookie half way into chocolate them place on waxed paper lined sheets.  Refrigerate until firm, about 30 minutes.
