Title:
Scrambled Egg Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound bulk pork sausage
12 eggs
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped green pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 (10-oz) pkg frozen spinach

Directions:
In a skillet, cook the sausage, onion, green pepper over medium heat until no longer pink; drain. In a bowl, beat the eggs. Add spinach, salt, pepper and garlic powder. Stir in sausage and cheese.
Spoon by 1/3 cupfuls into greased muffin cups. Bake at 350 degrees F for 20-25 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.

KarenS
Description:
