Title:
Rhubarb Custard Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 to 3 cups chopped rhubarb
2 eggs
1 cup sugar (or adjust to decrease sweetness a bit)
2 heaping tbsp. flour
1 tbsp. butter, melted

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.
Bake between pie crusts @ 350 until set (approx. 35 minutes or more)

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
An old family favorite - rhubarb pie that's just the right sweetness in a custard base. You'll need a double pastry crust for this. Apples can be substituted for some of the rhubarb.
