Title:
One Minute Cucumber /Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cucumber (peeled) sliced then cut into half pieces
Optional (tomato, onion, peppers)
1-2 tablespoons of hellmann's mayonnaise
1 tsp white vinegar
salt and pepper to taste (I like alot!)

Directions:
Mix the vinegar and mayo together.  Add the cucumber and (optional veggies)
Add salt and pepper to taste
Refrigerate until cool.

Submitter: 
Toni Losada
Description: 
This is the easiest cucumber/tomato salad you'll find, just try it...it only takes a minute and I'm sure you have the ingredients.
