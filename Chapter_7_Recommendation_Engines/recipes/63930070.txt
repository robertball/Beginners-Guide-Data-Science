Title:
Easy Crockpot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts, boneless, skinless
2/3 cup dry white wine
1/2 cup instant chopped onion
1 tablespoon chicken bouillon granules
1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
1 teaspoon lemon-herb seasoning
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon dried tarragon
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1 28 oz can petite diced tomato, undrained
6 cups hot cooked rice
Parasean cheese, grated (to taste)

Directions:
Trim the fat from the chicken.
Place chicken in a crockpot. Stir in wine and next 8 ingredients (wine through tomatoes). Cover with lid and cook on high heat for 1 hour.
Reduce heat to low and cook for 4 hours.
When done remove the chicken breasts from the crockpot and place on a bed of rice on each plate. Stir remaining sauce in the crockpot and then spoon some sauce over each chicken breast.
Sprinkle grated parmasean cheese over the chicken before serving.

Description: 
This is our family's favorite chicken recipe. It is easy and yummy!
