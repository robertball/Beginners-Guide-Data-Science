Title:
Cinnamon Roll Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
3 c. all purpose flour
1/4 tsp.salt
1 c. sugar
4 tsp. baking powder
1 1/2 c. milk
2 eggs, lightly beaten
2 tsp. vanilla
1/2 c. butter, melted
Topping:
1 c. butter, softened
1 c. brown sugar
2 Tbsp. flour
1 Tbsp. cinnamon
Glaze:
2 c. powdered sugar
5 Tbsp. milk
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Mix the cake ingredients together except for the butter & mix well. Slowly stir in the melted butter and pour into a greased 9x13 cake pan.
For the topping, mix all the ingredients together until well combined. Drop evenly over the batter and swirl with a knife.
Bake at 350 for 28-32 minutes.
Allow to cool a few minutes & then mix glaze ingredients & pour evenly over top of still warm cake.
Best served slightly warm.
