Title:
Grilled  Garlic Parmesan Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh Brussels sprouts
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 onion, diced small
2 teaspoon salt (may need more)
1/4 teaspoon pepper
15 shavings of Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat grill to medium heat.
Cut stem end off of the Brussels sprouts and remove any yellow leaves. Cut Brussels sprouts in half and place into a microwave safe bowl. Microwave on high for 3 minutes.
Add the olive oil, garlic, onion, salt and pepper to the Brussels sprouts. Allow to sit for 5 minutes.
Transfer Brussels sprouts to a sheet of tin foil. Shape tin foil into a packet and seal edges.
Place tin foil packet onto grill and cook for 20-30 (depending on size) minutes until Brussels sprouts are tender shaking foil packet every 5 minutes.
Return them to the original mixing bowl and toss with any remaining garlic oil mixture. Shave Parmesan cheese over Brussels sprouts and season with additional salt and pepper if needed.
