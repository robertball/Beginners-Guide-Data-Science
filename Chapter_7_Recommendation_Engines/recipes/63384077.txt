Title:
Kale with smoked sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/906892.jpg
Ingredients:
1 kg potatoes
2 onions
1,5 kg sliced kale (or 500 gr frozen)
125 gr bacon, in cubes
1 dl milk
10 ml vinegar
3 gr nutmeg
1 smoked sausage (275 gr)

Directions:
Peal and slice the onions. Peal the potatoes, slice them in pieces and boil them for about 20 minutes, or untill they're done.
In another pan, boil the kale for about 20 minutes, or untill done. (In case of frozen kale, please follow instructions on the packaging.)
Fry the bacon in a frying pan, untill it's nice and crisp. Add the snips of onion and fry for 2-3 minutes.
Prepare the smoked sausage as instructed on the packaging.
Heat the milk untill warm but not boiling.
Drain the potatoes.
Add the warm milk to the potatoes and mash. Drain the kale and add to the mash with the onion, bacon and vinegar. Add salt and pepper to taste.
Serve with the smoked sausage!
