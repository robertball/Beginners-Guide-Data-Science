Title:
Mom's Chicken Cacciatore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour for coating
1/2 teaspoon salt, plus extra to season as you go
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper, plus extra to season as you go
4 lg. chicken breasts, halved (approx 3 lbs.)
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
2-3 green bell peppers, cut into strips
1 (28 ounce) can petite diced tomatoes
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
Few dashes of crushed red pepper
1 cup red wine
chicken stock to deglaze pan

Directions:
Combine the flour, salt and pepper in a plastic bag. Shake the chicken pieces in flour until coated. Heat the oil in a dutch oven or saute pot(one that has a cover/lid). Fry the chicken pieces until they are browned on both sides [do in 2 batches]. Remove from skillet.
Deglaze the pan with chicken stock.
Add the onion, garlic and bell pepper to the skillet and saute until the onion is slightly browned. Return the chicken to the skillet and add the tomatoes, oregano, crushed red pepper and wine. Cover and simmer for approx. 45 minutes over medium low heat.
