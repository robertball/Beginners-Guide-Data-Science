Title:
Holy Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
2 cups sugar
2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
6 heaping Tbsp. baking cocoa
2 tsp. vanilla
2 Tbsp. vinegar
10 Tbsp. shortening, melted
2 scant cups warm water
Frosting of your choice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Grease and lightly flour a 9x13 cake pan.
In a large bowl, stir all dry ingredients together.  Form 1 larger hole and 2 smaller holes in the mix.
Pour vanilla in one of the smaller holes.
Pour vinegar in the other small hole.
Pour melted shortening in larger hole.
Pour warm water over the ingredients.
Stir until mixed well, being careful not to overstir.  There may be some smaller lumps remaining.
Pour batter into cake pan.  Bake at 350 for 30 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center just comes clean.
Cool thoroughly and frost.
