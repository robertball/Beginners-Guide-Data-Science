Title:
Shrimp Curry with Coconut Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large unpeeled tart apple (Granny Smith)
3 Tablespoons vegetable oil
2 Tablespoons chopped fresh ginger
1 Medium onion, coarsely chopped
4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
3 teaspoons curry powder
1 teaspoon ground coriander
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (or to taste)
4 medium tomatoes (can use canned/drained)chopped
1 cup frozen peas
1 13-1/2 oz coconut milk unsweetened
1-1/2 pounds peeled fresh shrimp/deveined
1/2 Cup fresh cilantro

Directions:
1. Quarter, core and slice apple into 1/4 inch pieces, set aside.
2.  Heat oil in 12 inch skillet over medium heat, add ginger, onion, and garlic.  Cook, stirring until onion is tender and golden (5-8 minutes)
3.  Add curry powder, coriander, cumin, salt and cayenne and cook stirring for 1-2 minutes.
4.  Add tomatoes, apple and 1 cup water.  Bring to a boil over high heat.
5.  Reduce heat to medium and cook for 5 minutes.
6.  Add peas and coconut milk and cook an additional 5 minutes.
7.  Add shrimp and cook 4-5 minutes depending on size of shrimp.
8.  Stir in cilantro.  Thicken sauce with a cornstarch/water mixture if desired.
9.  Serve with hot cooked rice.
