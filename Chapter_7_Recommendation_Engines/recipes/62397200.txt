Title:
Great Mustard Relish! Recipe

Ingredients:
1/2 cups of pickle chips
1/2 cups of diced
1 tablespoon of mustard

Directions:
Grind the pickle chips in a food processor until they become the familiar size of relish
Empty out pickles into bowl
Rinse food processor and dry with rag
Grind the onions in the food processor
Empty into the same container as the pickles
Mix with a spoon. Do not use a electronic mixer
Put the mustard in the relish and mix more
