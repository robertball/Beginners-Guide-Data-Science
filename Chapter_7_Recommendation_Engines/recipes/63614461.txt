Title:
Overnight Apple-Cinnamon Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 baking apples like Fuji (sweet) or Granny Smith (tart)
1 1/2 cups milk (I used whole)
1 1/2 cups water
1 cup steel cut oats
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 tablespoons butter
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 tablespoon ground flax seed
Kosher or coarse salt for sprinkling when serving
Toppings: Brown sugar, cinnamon or apple pie spice, raisins, chopped pecans or walnuts, milk, etc.

Directions:
Spray a 3 1/2-quart or larger slow cooker with non-stick cooking spray. Combine all the ingredients except for the salt and additional toppings. Cover and cook on low for 6 1/2 hours. Serve with additional toppings. Serves 5-7.

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
Simple and good (needs more brown sugar and milk before serving).
