Title:
German Sauerkraut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1857167.jpg
Ingredients:
1 TBSP Bacon Drippings
1 Onion, finely chopped (1 c)
1 lg Red Apple, diced
1 tsp Caraway Seeds
2 lb Sauerkraut, drained/rinsed
2 TBSP Brown Sugar
1/2 c White Wine
1/2 C Chicken or Veggie Stock

Directions:
In dutch oven heat bacon drippings over medium heat; cook and stir onion until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
Add diced apple and caraway seeds, stir to combine and cook until apple is just tender, about 3 minutes.
Add sauerkraut to onion and stir to combine.
Stir brown sugar, wine and stock together until sugar is dissolved, then add to the sauerkraut.
Reduce heat to low, cover and cook for two hours or until cooking liquid is reduced.
