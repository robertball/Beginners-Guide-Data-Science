Title:
Zesty Key Lime Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7919074.jpg
Ingredients:
4 sticks butter (room temp)
3 cups sugar
6 large eggs plus 2 yolks
3 1/2 cups All Purpose flour
1/2 cup fresh key lime juice
1/4 cup evaporated milk
1/4 cup key lime zest
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
1. Beat butter until light in color.  Add sugar and beat for 10-15 mins more.
2. One at a time, add eggs and beat only until yellow disappears.
3. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
4. Combine zest, lime juice, evaporated milk, and vanilla.
5. Mixing by hand, gradually add flour to butter and sugar mixture alternating with liquid mixture.  Begin and end with flour and mix only enough to incorporate ingredients.
6. Pour evenly into a tube pan and tap to release air bubbles.
7.  Bake 90 minutes or until a cake tester comes out with a few moist crumbs but not moist.
8.  Cool for 15 mins in pan on a cooling rack.  Remove from pan and cool 1 hour on cooling rack.
