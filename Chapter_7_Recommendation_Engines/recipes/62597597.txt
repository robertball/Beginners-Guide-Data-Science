Title:
Dill Dip Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1t dill weed
2/3c sour cream
2/3c mayonnaise
1t seasoned salt
1t parsley flakes
1/2T minced onion

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and refrigerate until served.
