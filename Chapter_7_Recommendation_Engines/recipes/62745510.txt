Title:
Ham Cake-ettes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C of finely chopped ham
1 1/2 C of leftover cold mashed potatoes
3 green onions finely chopped
1 egg
1 TBSP Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp dill weed
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 1/2 C panko bread crumbs -divided
1/2 C of oil (or more as needed)

Directions:
Mix the ham, potatoes, onions together. Combine the egg, mustard and spices together. Add to the ham mixture, along with 1/4 - 1/2 C of the bread crumbs and fully blend so that the mixture is rather stiff. Scoop into a palm sized ball and slightly flatten. Roll into the remaining bread crumbs coating all sides. Heat the oil in a shallow frying pan til hot but not smoking. Fry 2 -3 of the cake-ettes at a time til golden brown on both sides.

Submitter: 
Avon- status quo PRO
Description: 
Easter ham turns into a perfect combo of pancakes and croquettes for a nice way to use up some of the leftovers.
