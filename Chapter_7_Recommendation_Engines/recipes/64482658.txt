Title:
Chicken With Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sliced mushrooms
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
4 tablespoons butter
6 ounces mozzarella or swiss cheese, sliced
3/4 cup chicken broth

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Saute mushrooms in 2 Tbsp. butter, then place half of the mushrooms in a 9x13 inch pan. Mix Parmesan cheese with bread crumbs. Dip chicken into beaten eggs, then roll in bread crumbs.
In skillet, melt 2 Tbsp. butter over medium heat. Brown both sides of chicken in skillet. Place chicken on top of mushrooms, arrange remaining mushrooms on chicken. Add chicken broth to pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear. When done, place cheese on top & either put under broiler or microwave until melted.
