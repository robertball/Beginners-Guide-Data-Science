Title:
MOMS SPAGHETTI SAUCE with  STEAK OR MEATBALLS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 28oz can Crushed Tomatoes
1 28oz can Tomato Sauce
1 12oz can Tomato Paste
4 TB Parsley
3 TB Oregano
1 TB Basil
1 TB Sugar
1 Cup chopped Celery
3 whole Cloves - remove when done  - I use ground cloves instead.
3 cloves Garlic
1 or 2 chopped Onions
1 chopped Green or Red Pepper
1 Cup chopped mushrooms
1/3 cup greated cheese
2 Bay Leaves - remove when done
With Steak -    use a 2-3 lbs Flat Steak
With Meatballs -
2 lbs Ground Round
1 chopped Onion
2 Cloves crushed Garlic
1 cup Italian bread crumbs
2 eggs
1/3 cup grated cheese
2 TB Oregano
3 TB Parsley
1 Tea Basil
Salt

Directions:
With Steak -
Rub with parsley and garlic
Brown Steak and remove to platter
In large pot brown - Onions, garlic, and celery.  Add tomato paste stir until well blended, add tomato sauce and crushed tomato,  Add rest of ingredients; spices, cheese, WHOLE pepper (cleaned) top cut off and cleaned.  Add Steak to pot and cook on low for 4 hours; stirring often.
For Meatballs:
Combine all ingredients together.
Make into meatballs, about 2 inches,
Fry in skillet till all sides are brown.
Drain on paper towels
Add to Sauce
Cook about 4 hours

Submitter: 
Debbie Cash
Description: 
These are the 'simple' quickly written recipes from my MIL for a  GREAT  Spaghetti with Flank Steak, or with Meatballs. She has passed away now, but shared with me just before she died.   Both are good.  From a true Italian cook
