Title:
Candy Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
2/3 cup white corn syrup
1/3 cup butter
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup powdered milk
food coloring (optional)

Directions:
Combine sugar, butter, and corn syrup in pan and bring to a boil, stirring constantly.
Turn heat to low and boil 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Remove from heat and add vanilla.
In a separate dish, combine powdered sugar, salt, and powdered milk. Add to sugar mixture in the pan, adding food coloring if desired.
Stir until cool enough to handle. Shape into creative pieces and let cool and dry.

Submitter: 
Whitney
Description: 
Make your own candy corn at home! We made this recipe in our home-economics class in junior high, it was fun candy to make at home!
