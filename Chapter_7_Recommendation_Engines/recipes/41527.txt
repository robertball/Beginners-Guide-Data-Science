Title:
Spiced Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984993.jpg
Ingredients:
1 gallon apple cider
1 cup orange juice
1/2 teaspoon McCormick® Pure Orange Extract
1/2 teaspoon McCormick® Pure Lemon Extract
4 McCormick® Cinnamon Sticks
2 teaspoons McCormick® Whole Cloves
1 teaspoon McCormick® Whole Allspice

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a very large saucepan or Dutch oven. Cover pan, heat to boil.  Reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes.
Strain into a large punch bowl.  Serve hot or cold.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
Spiced Cider is the perfect holiday beverage. Not only does this festive drink taste sensational, but the simmering cider greets your guests with a wonderful aroma.
