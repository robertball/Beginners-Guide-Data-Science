Title:
Beefy Mushroom Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/515644.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ½ lb Beef Stew Meat Cut into bite size pieces
1 10 ¾ oz Can Cream of Mushroom Soup
3 4oz Cans Sliced Mushrooms Drained
½ Cup Beef Broth
1 1oz Package Dry Onion Soup Mix

Directions:
In Slow Cooker, combine all ingredients. Mix well and cover. Cook on low heat for 6 to 8 hours.
