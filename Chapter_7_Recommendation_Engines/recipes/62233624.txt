Title:
Sugar-Free Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2.1 ounce) package sugar-free instant pudding mix
1 cup milk
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 (8 ounce) container lite frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine pudding mix and milk. Mix well and let stand until thickened.
In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Add pudding and mix well. Finally, mix whipped topping until throughly combined.

Submitter: 
Ms. Christina
Description: 
I reduced the milk to 1 cup and used the larger box of pudding (6 serving size).  I also mix the whipped topping in throughly instead of folding it in.Modified from: Sugar-Free Frosting
