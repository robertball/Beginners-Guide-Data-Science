Title:
Easy Taco Seasoning Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1023844.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons onion powder
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 tablespoon ground cumin
1 tablespoon paprika
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon ground oregano
1 tablespoon white sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Mix onion powder, chili powder, cumin, paprika, garlic powder, oregano, sugar, and salt together in a small bowl.

Submitter: 
Star Wilson
Description: 
Easy taco mix. This is equal to a 1 1\4 ounce package of commercial taco seasoning.
