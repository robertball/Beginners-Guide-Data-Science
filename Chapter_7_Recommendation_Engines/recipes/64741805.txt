Title:
Keto Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c/8oz (224 g) low moisture mozzarella cheese, shredded
3-4 T coconut flour (24g)
salt
spices of choice (garlic powder, cinnamon, etc)

Directions:
preheat oven 350
melt cheese in 30 second increments in large bowl
add coconut flour, combine well
roll out to approx 1/16 inch
cut into squares
separate on parchment lined pan so they don't stick together (they will puff a lot)
bake 10 min or until golden
for crispier, bake at 325 for longer time
