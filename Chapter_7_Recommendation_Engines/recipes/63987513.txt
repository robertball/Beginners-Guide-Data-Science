Title:
Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground Chuck
1 lb. Ground Pork
2 Eggs
3/4 c. Milk
1 c. Plain Breadcrumbs
1 T. Onion Powder
1 T. Paprika
1/2 T. Ground Sage
1/2 t. Garlic Powder
1/2 t. Pepper
1/2 t. Salt
1/4 t. Ground Red Pepper
1/8 t. Cumin

Directions:
Beat eggs, add milk, add all seasoning, and then add breadcrumbs. It should now resemble a paste, if looks too dry add more milk. Add meat, and make sure everything is well mixed. Form into a loaf, and bake at 350 degrees until internal temperature reads 160 degrees. Let sit for about 5 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
John
Description: 
Perfect for those who don't like chunks in their meatloaf.
