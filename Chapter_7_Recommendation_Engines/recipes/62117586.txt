Title:
kicken carrot soup! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 onion, chopped
1 large clove of garlic, chopped
2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 1/2 teaspoons fennel seeds
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 lb. carrots, chopped
2 cups beef broth
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Dice onion and chop garlic, saute in large pot until tender, about 5 min.  Add cumin, fennel, salt, pepper, and continue to cook another 2 min.  Add carrots, chopped or shredded.  Coat thoroughly with onion and spice mix.  Add broth and cook on high until carrots are soft, about 25 min.  Put all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.  Pour back in pot and add water to desired consistancy.
