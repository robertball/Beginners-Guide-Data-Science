Title:
Chicken in Mushroom Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/203761.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1 cup sour cream
4 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled

Directions:
Place chicken in a slow cooker. Combine soup and sour cream; pour over chicken. Cover and cook on low for 4-5 hours or until chicken is tender. Sprinkle with bacon.

Submitter: 
Kathy  Gallagher
Description: 
'My father thinks a restaurant could make a fortune with this flavorful chicken,' writes Kathy Gallagher from La Crosse, Wisconsin. Bacon and sour cream add richness to a simple sauce that really dresses up everyday chicken.
