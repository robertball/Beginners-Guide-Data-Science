Title:
Heart Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Flour
1 Cup Powdered Sugar
.25 Teaspoon Salt
1 Cup Unsalted Butter
.25 Cup Jam

Directions:
Combine first four ingredient and process. Refridgerate one hour. Roll out dough to 1/8 inch thick.  Cut dough with heart cutter.  Cut half cookies again with smaller heart cutter. Bake at 325.  13-16 minutes for large cookies.  10 minutes for smaller cookies.  Spread jam on large cookies and place hollowed ones on top.
