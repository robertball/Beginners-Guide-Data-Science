Title:
Grilled Marinated Leg of Lamb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 trimmed 5 lb boneless and butterflied leg of lamb
whole head of garlic, cloves minced
*1/2 cup dijon mustard
1/2 cup olive oil
4 tbsp chopped fresh rosemary
1/2 cup dry white wine
1/4 fresh lemon juice--and lemon zest, from 1 lemon
kosher salt and ground pepper
Italian parsley for garnish

Directions:
Cover lamb with salt and pepper, set aside. Combine all the ingredients in a food processor and puree. Cover Lamb with puree and place in bowl or in large ziploc bag and chill for at least 2 hours (can also be kept in fridge for up to 2 days, or freeze for up to six mos.)
Preheat grill on Med heat. Roll lamb up and wrap in kitchen twine. Grill for 15 min on each side for med-rare (should take 1 hour total) and internal temp is 130 degrees (med rare-- 140 medium). Let rest for 10 mins before slicing. Sprinkle with Italian parsley.
