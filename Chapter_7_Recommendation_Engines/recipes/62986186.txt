Title:
Thumb Print Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter (1 stick)
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 egg, seperated
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup finely chopped nuts
Jam or Jelly of your chioce - Lemon Curd & Seedless Raspberry

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix thoughly butter, sugar, egg yolk and vanilla. Work in flour and salt until dough holds together. Shape dough by teaspoons into 1 inch balls. Beat egg whites slightly. Dip each ball into egg white then roll in nuts. Place 1 inch apart on an ungreased baking sheet; press thumb deeply into center of each one. Bake about 10 minutes or until light brown. Immediately remove from baking sheet; cool. Fill thumb prints with Jam. Makes about 3 dozen.
