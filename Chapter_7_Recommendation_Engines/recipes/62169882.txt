Title:
Mom's Broccoli Cheese Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 stalks celery, chopped
1 Tbsp onion flakes, or onion powder
2 carrots, grated
1 stick butter
3 cans chicken broth
1 lb Velveeta cheese
3 cans cream of potato soup
1 package frozen, chopped broccoli
8 oz Sour Cream

Directions:
Saute celery, and carrots in butter on medium heat, until soft.  Add onion flakes, chicken broth, potato soup, and Velveeta and let melt together.  Add cooked package of frozen broccoli and stir in sour cream.  Heat through & serve with some french bread and roasted garlic.

Submitter: 
Brooke129
Description: 
A rich and creamy soup to warm the soul!  Perfect with some french bread and roasted garlic!
