Title:
Amy's Ratatouille Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 eggplant
2 large tomatoes
1 small onion
1 pkg baby bellas
3 small zuccini
1 tsp olive oil
1 T olive oil
1 tsp balasmic vinegar
1 tsp fresh garlic
1/4 tsp oregano
s & p to taste
dash of white pepper
1/2 cup shredded romano cheese

Directions:
Cut up veggies, add them to a large glass baking dish that you rubbed the tsp of olive oil on.  Layer the veggies, ending with the onion.  I place one tomato on the bottom, and one on the top just under the onion.  Sprinkle with all seasonings including the garlic minced.  Cover with aluminum foil bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes.  Then uncover, add 1/2 cup of romano cheese.  Bake for an addl 15 minutes uncovered.  Let cool and enjoy.
