Title:
Mexican Cinnamon-Sugar Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tbs sugar
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
6 6-inch fajita-size tortillas
1 tbs. light corn syrup
TOPPING:
2 tsp sugar
1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees. Mix cinnamon and sugar. Brush one side of each tortilla with corn syrup and sprinkle with sugar mixture.  Cut into 6 wedges and place on baking sheet.  Bake for 10 minutes, until golden and crispy.
Stir sugar into sour cream and serve with warm chips.
