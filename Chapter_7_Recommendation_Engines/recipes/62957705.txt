Title:
Home-Made French Onion Dip Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/729098.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup dried onion flakes
2 tablespoons low-sodium beef bouillon granules (or 6 bouillon cubes)
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon parsley flakes
1/8 teaspoon celery seeds
1/8 teaspoon paprika
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Stir together.
Store in an air tight container.
Equal to 1 envelope onion soup mix.

Submitter: 
Charlatte
Description: 
For those times you're cooking away and the next ingredient is french onion dip mix...then you look in the pantry and realize that you don't have any! In those times of need, you have two options: go all the way to the grocery store for one item, or make the mix from scratch. The thought of making a store bought ingredient yourself seems impossible! But fret not, with this easy and quick recipe you'll not only be able to get through in a pinch, you'll never go back to Lipton's expensive french onion dip mix in your recipes again!
