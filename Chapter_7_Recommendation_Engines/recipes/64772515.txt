Title:
Duchess Potatoes  /  pommes Duchesse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz  Baking Potatoes
2 egg yolks
1 oz Butter
1/4 tsp. Nutmeg.

Directions:
Boil, peel and put through a Potato ricer.  quickly add the scrambled yolks, melted Butter and Nutmeg.  Mix thoroughly with a whisk.  Let Cool.  Fill a piping back with a large Star Fitting and pipe Golf ball sized rosettes on to parchment paper on baking sheet.  Brush carefully with butter and Bake/Broil;  until  lightly Brown.  Do not Burn.
Keep warm for serving;  with:   Fish, Chicken.
