Title:
Crunchy Romaine Toss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads of romaine lettuce -- chopped
1 bunch broccoli - coursely chopped
1 package Ramen noodles uncooked
1 cup chopped walnuts
4 Tablespoons unsalted butter
4 green onions -chopped
Sweet and Sour Dressing:
1 cup vegetable oil
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup red wine vinegar
3 teaspoons soy sauce
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Crush the uncooked ramen noodles and combine with walnuts and butter in skillet. Brown the mixture then cool on paper towels.
Whisk and blend all the ingredients for the sweet and sour dressing in separate bowl.
Combine noodle and walnut mixture with lettuce, broccoli, and onions. Toss with the sweet and sour dressing.

Submitter: 
Susan LeBlanc
Description: 
This is a great crunchy salad that goes well with spaghetti or steak. The homemade dressing makes it even better!
