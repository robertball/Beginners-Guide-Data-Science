Title:
Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs lean hamburger
1 large onion minced
2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 cup flour
1 1/2 cup ketchup
1 tbsp. mustard
2 1/2 cups water
16 buns

Directions:
Scramble fry meat, onions, salt and pepper.
Blend in flour; add water, Worcestershire sauce, ketchup and mustard, stirring occasionally.Simmer stirring occasionally 15 - 20 minutes.
Serve on toasted buns.
