Title:
Blackened Chicken & Burger Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. paprika
1 tsp. onion powder
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. cayenne pepper
3/4 tsp. white pepper
3/4 tsp. black pepper
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. thyme
1/2 tsp. oregano
.

Directions:
Mix together blackened spice mix ingredients. Store in airtight container.
To season chicken or hamburgers, coat each piece with about 1 tablespoon oil and 1 tablespoon of spice mix.  Grill as usual.

Submitter:

Description: 
Great blackened seasoning for grilled chicken or burgers.
