Title:
White Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1099790.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 boneless chicken breasts (approx 1 1/2 lbs), cooked & chopped*
3 cups chicken broth **
1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chile peppers
1 teaspoons ground cumin
1 teaspoons dried oregano
1/8 tsp ground cloves
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
2 cans Cannellini Beans (1 drained, one not) or 1 cannelllini undrained and 1 pinto beans-drained
2+ cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese or pepper jack/cheddar combo
Rice/brown rice
Sour Cream
Cilantro

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onions and saute until tender, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and saute 1 minute. Add the chicken, chicken broth, green chile peppers, cumin, oregano, cloves, cayenne pepper, and beans. Bring to a low boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 to 30 minutes. Add 1 cup of shredded cheese. Serve over rice and top with cheese, sour cream and cilantro.
*Simmered chicken breasts in 4 cups of water, 1 tsp. of salt, 1/4 tsp. pepper, 1 tsp. chili powder, 1 tsp. cumin for 45 minutes.
** Strained broth from cooking chicken, added 2 tsp. chicken bouillon.
