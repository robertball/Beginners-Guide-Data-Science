Title:
White Chocolate Peppermint Bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. white melting chocolate or white chocolate chips
1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract
6-8 regular candy canes, crushed into small pieces
Cookie sheet lined with waxed paper

Directions:
Make sure to crush the candy canes before you start.
Melt the white chocolate in fifteen second intervals, stirring after each, until chocolate is smooth and completely melted (no lumps).
Add the extract and blend well.
Pour in the crushed candy cane, mix well then spread mixture onto wax lined cookie sheet.  Refrigerate until set, about ten minutes.  Take it out of the fridge and let it become room temperature before breaking off into pieces.

Submitter:

Description: 
Delicious, peppemint candy.  I get so many compliments when I put this candy out and everyone thinks that I worked really hard.  It is so easy, it's almost embarassing!
