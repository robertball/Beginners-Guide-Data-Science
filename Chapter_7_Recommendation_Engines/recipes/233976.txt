Title:
Orange Cinnamon Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1057030.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups orange juice
3 cups vanilla yogurt
3 large  navel oranges, peeled and cut into 1-inch chunks
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Blend orange juice, yogurt, oranges, and cinnamon together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
BetterCookingForSingleFathers
Description: 
This is a quick and easy way to add some variety to drinking your daily dose of orange juice.
