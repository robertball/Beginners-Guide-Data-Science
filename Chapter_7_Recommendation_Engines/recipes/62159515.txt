Title:
Chick Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 32 oz. can chick peas (garbanzo beans)- drained, but not rinsed
1 small red onion, diced
1 green onion (scallion), thinly sliced (green and some white)
1 small tomato, seeded and diced
2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
1 T. each chopped fresh dill, mint, basil and cilantro
juice of one lemon
2 T. white wine vinegar (or to taste)
2 T. Olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and let salad marinate for at least 1 hour.
That's it!

Submitter: 
kherder330
Description: 
This delicious salad is easy to throw together and can be served in a variety of ways.  It's great by itself, but you can serve it as a bruschetta topping or a pita filling.  You can also switch up the herbs to your liking-- there are lots of possibilities!
