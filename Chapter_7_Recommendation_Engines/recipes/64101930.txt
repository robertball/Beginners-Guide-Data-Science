Title:
Orange Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups carrots, chopped
1 cup red pepper
1 cup yellow pepper
1 lb. chicken breasts, cubed
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
8 oz orange juice (orange pineapple will do)

Directions:
Add all chopped ingredients, spices and juice to the slow cooker
Cook on low for 4 to 6 hours
Garnish with mandarine orange slices (2 cans) and green onions
Serve on rice
