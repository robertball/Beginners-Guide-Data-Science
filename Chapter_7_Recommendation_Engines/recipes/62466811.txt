Title:
Butternut Squash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons chopped onion
garlic cloves
4 tablespoons butter/margarine
6 cups peeled and cubed butternut squash
or just roast cut lengthwise skin side up
3 cups water
4 cubes  chicken bouillon
1/2 teaspoon dried marjoram
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/8 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese-
or just a dab for flavor

Directions:
Roast together: squash, onion, garlic (head in foil with evoo--salt and pepper.  Puree in batches until smooth. Return to saucepan, and heat through. Add estras: cream cheese,Do not allow to boil.
