Title:
Simple Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Baguette - toasted then sliced lengthways into 10 to 15 cm (4 to 6in) lengths
Clove Garlic - Peeled
Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Fresh Vine and/or Italian Plum Tomatoes - thinly sliced or chopped
Handful Basil Leaves - torn
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
Optional - Parmesan Cheese - grated

Directions:
Gently rub the toasted slices with garlic clove, spread on the tomatoes and scatter over the torn basil;
Season with salt and pepper to taste;
Drizzle over with olive oil;
To add or not - Sprinkle over with Parmesan Cheese

Description: 
My take on an Italian classic. Absolutely delicious. The ideal appetiser and/or starter for parties. Fit for any table and/or occasion.
