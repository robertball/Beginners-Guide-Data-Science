Title:
Sweet Crock Pot Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6.5 lb. bone-in Picnic Ham
1 (1 lb.) box light brown sugar
1 (20 oz.) can crushed pineapple
¾ c. Tazo Organic Chai (Black Tea) concentrate

Directions:
Cover bottom of crock with ¾ box of brown sugar.  Rinse ham and place in crock, flat side down.  Pour pineapple and juice over ham, and rub ham with remaining brown sugar.  Pour Chai Tea concentrate over ham.  Insert meat thermometer and cover with lid.  Cook 5 ½ hours on low heat or until thermometer reads 160°.  Baste ham once or twice with juices while cooking.

Submitter: 
smoochthechef
Description: 
This ham is so easy to put together and cook in a crock pot, and with a meat thermometer you can't go wrong!  The juices can be thickened with cornstarch at the end to make a glaze.
