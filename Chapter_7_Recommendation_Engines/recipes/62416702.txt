Title:
Crispy Chicken Cutlets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 chicken cutlets, boneless & skinless
1 1/3 c.  mayonnaise
3/4 c. corn flake crumbs
1/2 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
1/2 c. milk
1/2 c. shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 t. dried thyme leaves

Directions:
Brush cutlets with 1/3 c. mayo; add salt & pepper to crumbs.
Coat cutlets. Place in greased baking dish. Bake @ 350 for 35-40 minutes.
Sauce: stir milk into 1 c. mayo, add cheese & thyme. Cook on medium heat, stirring constantly, for 10 minutes. Serve sauce over cutlets.
