Title:
Holupki Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small head cabbage
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 small onion, diced
1 small green pepper, diced (optional)
1/2 c. uncooked rice
3 15oz. cans tomato sauce
1 c. water
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Pre cook rice, using 1/2 c. rice and 1 cup water (do not need to cook thoroughly). Chop cabbage (1 in. squares) and place in bottom of large baking dish. Brown ground beef, onion and pepper in large dutch oven. Stir in rice, tomato sauce, salt and pepper, bring to boil. Pour mixture over the top of cabbage. Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hours. Mix contents together when baking is complete.  Serve with warm fresh bread.
