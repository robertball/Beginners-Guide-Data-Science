Title:
Juicy Orange Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 trimmed chicken breasts
1 cup traditional bread crumbs
1 tablespoon garlic powder
ground sea salt
ground black pepper
1 large orange
1 medium onion
olive oil

Directions:
cut onion into thin rings
sautee onions in a bit of olive oil in a large skillet on low heat
mix bread crumbs, garlic powder, salt and pepper in a medium bowl
coat chicken with bead crumb mix
raise temperature of skillet and move onions to the sides
place chicken in hot skillet until brown and repeat on other side
lower temperature to low heat and squeeze the juice from orange
cover
cook until juices run clear
serve with the onions on top

Submitter: 
Sara Perry
Description: 
The wonderful coating of bread crumbs and orange caramelized onions are a delectable conbination! Pair with rice or just a salad and you will have a great meal.
