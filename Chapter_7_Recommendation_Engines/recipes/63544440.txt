Title:
thekruser's Rib Rub Recipe

Ingredients:
1/3 c sugar
1/4 non-iodized table salt
1/4 c brown sugar, dried
3 t chili powder
2 t Creole or Cajun seasoning
2 t ground cumin
1 t onion powder
1 t Accent (MSG)
1 t cayenne pepper
1 t black pepper
1 t granulated garlic

Directions:
Combine ingredients and enjoy!
