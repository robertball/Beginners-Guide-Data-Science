Title:
Killer Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 to 16 oz pk caramels
vanilla
Pet milk
Duncan Hines German Chocolate Cake Mix
12 oz bag milk choc chips
butter
oil
water
chopped pecans

Directions:
Caramel Sauce
melt caramels, 1/2 tsp vanilla, 2 TB water, 1/3 cup Pet milk in microwave. set aside
Mix dry Cake Mix together with
6TB melted butter
2 TB oil
1/3 c Pet milk
1 tsp vanilla
1 to 1/4 cups chopped pecans
Spread 1/2 cake mixture into a greased 9x13 pan. I use my hands to pat mixture down. Bake for 6 minutes at 350 degrees. Remove from oven and immediately sprinkle the 12 oz of choc chips over cake. Then pour caramel sauce over this. Take the remainder of the brownie batter and with hands sprinkle evenly over sauce. Bake additional 20 to 25 minutes or until done. Let cool then cut into squares.
