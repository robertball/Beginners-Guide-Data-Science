Title:
Instant Pot Stew by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3lbs stew meat
2tbsp flour
Salt, pepper, garlic powder Lowry seasoning or Ricks salt or favorite seasoning
1/2 onion
2 cloves garlic
2tbsp tomato paste
3/4c red wine
3 c beef both
3 carrots, cut in chunks
3 stalks celery, cut in chunks
3 parsnips, cut in chunks
1.5 lb potatoes, cut in chunks
1/2 tsp dried thyme, or 3 sprigs fresh
Small “handful” parsley, chopped

Directions:
Mix seasoning and flour and coat beef.
Set Instant Pot to sauté, melt butter and brown beef in small batches,  remove from pot when done.
Sauté onion about 4 minutes, add garlic and tomato paste, cook about 30 seconds until melded together.
Add wine and broth, bring to a simmer and scrape up the cooked bits on the bottom of the pan.
Add beef, vegetable and thyme. Add additional seasoning if desired. Cover, set Instant Pot on HIGH pressure for 20 minutes (tougher cuts of meat like chuck,  use 25 minutes). NPR about 15 minutes.
Remove “solids” and simmer liquid base until reduced by about half (10 minutes).
