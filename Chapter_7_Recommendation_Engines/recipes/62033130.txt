Title:
Tossed Salad  Vinagrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup whit wine vinegar
2 teaspoons sugar
1 teaspoon chopped fresh chives
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
Salad:
1 head romaine lettuce torn
3 plum tomatoes seeded and chopped
1 green bell pepper chopped
1 small red onion chopped
1/4 cup sliced black olives
1 carrot shredded

Directions:
For the vinagrette combine first 6 ingredients in jar with lid shake well. In bowl combine all salad ingredients. Pour dressing over salad and toss well.
