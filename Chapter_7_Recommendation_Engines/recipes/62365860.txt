Title:
Chompo Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/407594.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup light corn syrup
3 cups mini marshmallows
6 cups cocoa rice cereal
1 1/4 cups smooth peanut butter
2 cups semi sweet chocolate chips
1 tsp vegetable or light olive oil

Directions:
Grease a 9 x 13 baking pan. In a heavy sauce oan, combine marshmallow and corn syrup. Heat over low-medium until marshmallows are melted, mix well.
Add the cereal and mix until well combined. Press into the bottom of the baking pan. Cover with a piece of foil directly on top of the cereal mix. Using a soup can and press the cereal firmly together, to make a dense layer for the base.
Remove foil and spread peanut butter over the top of the cereal layer. Place in refrigerator.
Over a double boiler, melt the chocolate chips and oil together until completely smooth. Once the chocolate has cooled slightly, spread chocolate over the peanut butter layer. Return bars to refrigerator until the chocolate layer has hardened. Cut into 36 bars. The bars can be frozen.

Submitter: 
sugarbean
Description: 
After reading a storybook to my daughter one night, she started calling chocolate covered granola bars, Chompo Bars. I came up with recipe so we could have a homemade version of Chompo Bars. A yummy combo of rice cereal, chocolate and peanut butter.
