Title:
Traditional Fish Fillet Club Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986116.jpg
Ingredients:
Gorton's® Crunchy Golden Fish Fillets
sesame seed sandwich roll
crispy bacon
leafy lettuce - iceberg or other
sliced tomato
Ranch dressing

Directions:
Place 2 Crunchy Fillets on a sandwich roll. Add crispy bacon, leafy iceberg lettuce, sliced tomato and ranch dressing.

Submitter: 
Gorton's®
Description: 
An easy lunch time solution for you and the kids. If Gorton's Fillets are too big, use Gorton's Tenders instead.
