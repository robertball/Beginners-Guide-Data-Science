Title:
Easy Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 Chicken Breasts
1 Onion (quartered)
1 Toe Garlic
2 cans Cream of Chicken & Mushroom Soup
2 cans Green Chili's
1 can Sliced Black Olives
1/2 cup Sour Cream
2 cups Shredded Cheddar
1/4 cup Milk
10 - 20 Tortillas (size optional)

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350.
Boil chicken with onion and garlic.
Mix soup, chili's, olives, sour cream, milk and 1 cup of the cheese.  Set 1/4 - 1/3 of mixture aside for topping.
Cut up (diced) cooked chicken, and add to mixture.  Mix well.
Spoon mixture into tortillas, roll up, and place in pan.
Cover enchiladas with the 1/4 -1/3 mixture that was set aside.
Take remaining cheese (1 cup) and sprinkle over top.
Bake in 350 degree oven for 20-25 min.
Take out and enjoy
