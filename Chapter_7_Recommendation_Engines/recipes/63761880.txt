Title:
Chocolate Dipped Almond Crescents Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1	cup almond, pecan, or walnut pieces
2	cups confectioner’s sugar
1	cup unsalted butter, softened
1 ½	teaspoon vanilla extract
2	cups all purpose flour
¼	teaspoon salt
1	cup semisweet, or milk chocolate chips,
1	tablespoon solid vegetable shortening

Directions:
In a food processor finely grind nuts, and ¼ cup sugar.  In a large bowl, on low speed, beat butter until creamy.  Add ½ cup sugar; beat until fluffy.  Beat in nut mixture, vanilla, then flour, salt.  Chill 1 hour.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  With floured hands, roll level 1 ½ teaspoons of dough into 3 inch ropes; shape into crescents.  Place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets.  Bake 15 to 17 minutes, until lightly browned on bottoms and edges.  Transfer to racks.  Sift remaining 1 ¼ cups confectioners’ sugar onto plate.  Roll warm cookies in sugar.
In  a 2 cup glass measure, microwave chocolate and shortening on high 1 ½ minutes; stir to blend.  Brush sugar off one end of crescents; dip in melted chocolate.  Place on waxed paper to set.  Store in airtight container at cool room temperature up to 2 weeks.
