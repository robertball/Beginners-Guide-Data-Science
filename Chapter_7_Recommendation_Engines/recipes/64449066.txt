Title:
Carrot Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2384216.jpg
Ingredients:
2 carrots (I have used Bolthouse Farms 100% carrot juice as a substitute between 1/2 to 1 cup of carrot juice)
1 banana or an apple
1/2 cup of almond milk (unsweetened )
a pinch of nutmeg
a pinch of cinnamon
1 clove
2 dates (I have used dried dates)
2 tbsp coconut cream (optional can leave it out or use coconut milk, diary milk, or soymilk)
Sprinkle with chia seeds

Directions:
Blend almond milk, spices, dates, coconut cream and banana until smooth
Add chopped carrots and blend until smooth.
Sprinkle with chia seeds
