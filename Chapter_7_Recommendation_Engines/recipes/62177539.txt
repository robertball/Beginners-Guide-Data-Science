Title:
Chicken & Zucchini Stoup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/288572.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 1/2 lbs. Chicken Breasts, tenderized
8 oz. Chopped Baby Bella Mushrooms or Cremini's
3 Medium sized Zucchini, chopped thick & cut into small cubes
4 Medium sized Shallots, chopped
2 Cloves Garlic, chopped
4 Sprigs of Thyme, chopped
2 Tbsp Fresh Parsley
1 Pint of RIPE Grape Tomatoes
4 Cups Chicken Stock
3 Slices White/Wheat Bread
Softened Butter
Salt & Pepper
1/4 c. Freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano Cheese

Directions:
In a medium pan over medium heat, pour 1 cup of the chicken stock & let it get warm. Add the chicken breasts &  with a fork, poke several holes in the breasts on each side. Cook until slightly pink. Set aside to cool.
In a medium/large pot, heat the olive oil over medium/high heat & add the mushrooms, stirring until brown.
Add the garlic, shallots, zucchini, & thyme. Season with salt/pepper. Stir in the tomatoes & cover the pot until the tomatoes burst, about 5-7 minutes.
Squash the tomatoes with a wooden spoon & add the remaining chicken stock.
Meanwhile, coarsely chop the chicken breasts on an angle, as if the chicken were being torn apart.
Add to the stoup, season w/ salt & pepper, & cook for 5-8 minutes at a simmer.
Toast the 3 slices of bread until crispy & butter them, then finely chop.
In a small bowl, mix the bread crumbs & cheese.
Serve stoup in a bowl & top with bread crumb mixture.
