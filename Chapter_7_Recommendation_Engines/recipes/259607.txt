Title:
"The Jackson" Detox Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6820933.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups frozen strawberries, divided
1 1/2 cups soy milk
1/2 cup frozen blueberries
2 large scoops acai powder
1 tablespoon flaxseed oil
2 teaspoons stevia powder

Directions:
Place 1 cup strawberries in a blender; blend on ice crush setting until smooth. Add the remaining 1/2 cup strawberries, soy milk, and blueberries; blend until well combined. Add acai powder, flaxseed oil, and stevia with a spoon. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Rene Tutt Jackson
Description: 
Making the most of the antioxidants, this smoothie tastes great while doing great things for your body! Very filling; makes a great meal substitute. Drink immediately and enjoy the road to detoxification!
