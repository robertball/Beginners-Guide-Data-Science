Title:
Sunset Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981129.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup Pace® Thick & Chunky Salsa
Tortilla chips

Directions:
Spread the cream cheese in a 9 inch microwaveable pie plate. Sprinkle with the Cheddar cheese.
Microwave on HIGH for 2 minutes or until the Cheddar cheese is melted.  Top with the salsa.  Serve with the tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Keep these ingredients on hand so you can serve this fabulous dip any time. Each time, try a different flavored Pace® Salsa.
