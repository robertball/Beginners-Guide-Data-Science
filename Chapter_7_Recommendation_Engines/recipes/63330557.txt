Title:
MeMe's Mexican Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 (THREE) PKGS OF THE MEXIKIT CORMBREAD MIX
2 CANS OF CREAM CORN
3 EGGS
MEME’S TACO MEAT
2 TO 3 CUPS OF KRAFT CHEDDAR CHEESE (MILD OR SHARP) DIVIDED
1 MEDIUM ONION (CHOPPED)

Directions:
PRE-HEAT OVEN TO º400
NEED A 13 X9 CASSOROLE DISH OR BAKING PAN (METAL OR GLASS)
MEME’STACO MEAT: BROWN 1 ½ TO 2 LBS OF HAMBURGER MEAT, DRAIN, PUT BACK INTO PAN AND MIX MEAT 1 8OZ CAN OF TOMATOE SAUCE, 1/3 CUP OF KETCHUP, 1 PKG TACO SEASONING , COUPLE OF DASHES OF CHILI POWDER, & SIMMER ABOUT 10-15 MINUTES, SET ASIDE)
CORNBREAD BATTER: STIR TOGETHER THE CORNBREAD MIXES, EGGS &  CREAMCORN
SPREAD  ½ OF THE CORNBREAD BATTER ON BOTTOM OF PAN, THEN LAYER TACO MEAT, ONIONS AND CHEESE, THEN TOP WITH REMAINING CORNBREAD BATTER.  PLACE IN OVEN FOR ABOUT 25 TO 30 MINUTES OR UNTIL GOLDEN BROWN ON TOP, SERVE W/BEANS (I LIKE MINE W/RANCH STYLE BEANS OR POT OF PINTO BEANS)

Submitter: 
Karyn Osborne
Description: 
Love this cornbread with a pot of beans!
