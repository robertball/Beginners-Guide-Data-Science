Title:
Memories of Currywurst Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/490729.jpg
Ingredients:
4 sausages (pork is more traditional)
1 (680ml) can of tomato sauce
1 tbsp chilli sauce or powder
1/2 tsp onion flakes
1/2 tsp salt
3-4 tbsp brown or white sugar
1 tbsp curry powder

Directions:
Cook sausages as per directions on the package.
In the meantime, make a sauce by combining rest of ingredients in a saucepan and bring to a boil.
Pour sauce over sausages and sprinkle with curry powder.

Submitter: 
Shawna Hambly
Description: 
My version of Currywurst. It's sweet and spicy, and has more curry than what you'll find in most recipes. I had it in Germany this summer and it was absolutely yummy, not to mention my mom's favourite thing from Germany, other than wine ;)
