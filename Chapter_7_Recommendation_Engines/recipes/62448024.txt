Title:
Glorious Green Goddess Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/462687.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Mayonnaise
3 Tablespoons Fresh Chopped Parsley
3 Tablespoons Chopped Red Onion
2 Tablespoons Fresh Chopped Chives
1 Large Clove of Garlic, Chopped
1 Tablespoons Fresh Lemon Juice
4 to 5 Caper Rolled Anchovies (from 2 ounce can)
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Take prepared ingredients and place in food processor or blender and mix on medium for 30 seconds. Place in air tight container and chill for a minimum of 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
The Green Goddess
Description: 
The original Green Goddess Dressing recipe was created by the chef at the Palace Hotel in San Francisco for George Arliss who was opening in The Green Goddess This is my twist on the original. This recipe also makes a great dip for fresh veggies and can be used as a sandwich spread.
