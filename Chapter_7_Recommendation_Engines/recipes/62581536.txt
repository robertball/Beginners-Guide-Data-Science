Title:
Diabetic Breakfast Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Fruit
1/4 cup Bran flakes
1/4 cup Ice cubes
1 cup Skim milk
1/2 cup Plain yogurt
2 Packets of Equal
1 tsp Vanilla
1 Dash cinnamon (optional)
1 Dash nutmeg (optional)

Directions:
Prepare the night before. Combine fruit, bran & ice cubes and put in freezer. Combine the milk, yogurt, sweetener and vanillla and put in the refridgerator.
In the morning combine both the frozen items and refrigerated items in a blender or Magic Bullet. Add the cinnamon & nutmeg and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Jeniflower
Description: 
I prepare this in the evening and blend it in the morning for a quick breakfast out the door. I just put the fruit & bran in the freezer and the rest in the fridge.
