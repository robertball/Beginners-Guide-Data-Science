Title:
Baileys Chocolatini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979014.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ounces Baileys® Irish Cream
1/4 ounce Smirnoff No. 21™ Vodka
1/4 ounce Godiva® Chocolate Liqueur
3 raspberries

Directions:
Pour Baileys(R) Irish Cream , Smirnoff No. 21(TM) Vodka, and Godiva(R) Chocolate Liqueur into a shaker with ice.
Shake and strain the cocktail into  martini glass; garnish with raspberries.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Adding a touch of richness to any drink, Baileys® Irish cream liqueur mixes fine Irish whiskey, fresh dairy cream and a hint of chocolate flavors.
