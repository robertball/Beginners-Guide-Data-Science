Title:
Raspberry Margaritas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1352815.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen unsweetened raspberries
1 cup brandy-based orange liqueur (such as Grand MarnierÂ®)
3/4 cup gold tequila
1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
2 tablespoons superfine sugar
ice cubes

Directions:
Place the raspberries, brandy-based orange liqueur, tequila, lemon juice, and sugar in a blender. Blend until smooth. Serve in glasses over ice cubes.
