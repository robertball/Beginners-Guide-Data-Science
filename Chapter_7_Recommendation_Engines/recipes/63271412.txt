Title:
Bruschetta Couscous Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
ounces Israeli couscous
1/2 small red onion, finely diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
3 ribs celery, finely diced
20 ounces cocktail tomatoes, quartered
1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 cup small fresh mozzarella balls
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
Salt
Ground black pepper

Directions:
1. Cook couscous according to package directions. Allow to cool.
2. In a large bowl, combine cooled couscous with red onion, garlic, celery, tomatoes, basil, Parmesan, mozzarella, olive oil and vinegar. Stir to combine, then season with salt and black pepper to taste. (The salad can be served immediately, but the flavors improve if it is allowed to sit for 30 minutes.)
Per serving: 310 calories; 11g fat; 4g saturated fat; 20mg cholesterol; 11g protein; 40g carbohydrate; 3g fiber; 180mg sodium.
