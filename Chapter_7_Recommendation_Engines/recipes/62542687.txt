Title:
Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 roast
1 tsp. oregano
1 tsp. basil
1 tsp. celery salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
2 T. seasoned salt
1 pkg. Lipton onion soup mix
baby carrots
3-4 potatoes
sliced onion
celery leaves
2 c. water

Directions:
Mix oregano, basil, celery salt, pepper, seasoned salt and Lipton onion soup mix together. Rub roast with mixture and place in crockpot fat side up. Slice onion in large rings and place over roast. Remove celery leaves from celery bunch and place in crockpot around roast. Peel potatoes and quarter. Arrange potatoes and baby carrots around roast. Pour water over roast and vegetables and cook on high 4 hours or on low 8 hours or until roast is stringy and falls apart when fork is inserted. Remove celery leaves and onion slices. Serve warm.
