Title:
Fancy Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987793.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1/4 cup minced fresh parsley
1 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 cups Brussels sprouts, halved
1 (8 ounce) can water chestnuts, drained and diced
1 tablespoon butter or margarine

Directions:
In a saucepan over medium heat, bring water, parsley, sugar and salt to a boil. Add brussels sprouts. Cover and simmer for 6-8 minutes or until tender; drain. Add water chestnuts and butter; heat through.

Submitter: 
Dorothy  Anderson
Description: 
This is a simple and tasty way to dress up brussels sprouts for the holidays. The parsley, sugar and crisp water chestnuts make the sprouts fresh tasting and festive looking. -Dorothy Anderson, Ottawa, Kansas
