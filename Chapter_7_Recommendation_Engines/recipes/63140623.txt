Title:
Zucchini Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c grated zucchini
6 eggs
8 fresh basil leaves
2 tbsp fresh parsley
3/4 c shredded cheese of your choice
salt and pepper
3 slices bacon
1 small tomato, seeded and diced
4 scallions, chopped
1 tbsp butter

Directions:
1.  Place zucchini in a large bowl with salt and let sit for 10 min., squeeze out liquid.
2.  In a separate bowl, whisk eggs, then add parsley, basil, 1/2 of cheese, 1/4 tsp salt, 1/8 tsp pepper.
3.  Heat oven proof skillet and cook bacon until crisp, set bacon aside.  Crumble bacon and add to eggs.
4.  Remove all but 1 tbsp grease. Add zucchini and saute for 3 min. Add tomato and scallions to skillet and saute 3 min. more.Add butter and stir into vegetables
5.  Whisk eggs again and pour over vegetables.  Do not stir.
6.  Cook over med. heat 4 min.  Sprinkle with cheese and put under broiler, 8 inches from heat for 2 min.
7.  Remove from oven and check doneness with a fork.
8.  Let cook 5 min., slide onto serving plate.  cool 5 more min. cut into wedges.
