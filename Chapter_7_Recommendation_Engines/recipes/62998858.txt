Title:
Classic Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 teaspoons baking powder
1 tablespoon white sugar
2 egg yoiks
2 egg whites, beaten
1 3/4 C. milk
4 tablespoons melted butter or shortening

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.  Add egg yolks and milk.  Beat until smooth.  Add melted butter or shortening.  Fold in beaten egg whites. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
