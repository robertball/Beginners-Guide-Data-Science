Title:
Chinese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/849702.jpg
Ingredients:
Dressing:
1-2 garlic cloves, chopped
1/2 cup of olive oil
1/4 cup of soya sauce
Salad:
2 cups of cooked rice, cold
1 cup of fresh bean sprouts
1 bag of baby spinach (150g or about 6 cups)
3 celeri sticks, slices
3/4 cup dried raisins
3/4 cup of cashew nuts
1/2 cup of chopped green onions
1 chopped red pepper

Directions:
Mix the ingredients for the dressing with a fork.
Mix all the ingredients for the salad and add the dressing, mix well and refrigerate.
The dressing should be added at least a 1/2 hour before serving.
Leftovers are awesome, they shoud be kept in the fridge.

Submitter:

Description: 
This salad is fresh, simple and delicious, and a big plus,  leftovers are awesome which is rare for a salad. Adjust the ingredients to your liking: a little more of this, a little less of that to make it your own!
