Title:
Homemade Puréed Sweet Potato Baby Food Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4419083.jpg
Ingredients:
2-3 Sweet Potatoes, peeled and halved
1/2-3/4 cup reserved cooking water

Directions:
Fill small saucepan with water. Add sweet potatoes and bring to a boil.
Continue to boil for 25-30 minutes or until very fork tender.
Transfer cooked potatoes to immersion blender cup reserving the cooking water for later.
Puree until smooth. Add 1:2-3/4 cup reserved cooking water or more to achieve your desired consistency.
Note: Can store cooked purée for 3 days in refrigerator or 3 months in freezer. Breast milk or formula can be used instead of cooking water. Please consult your Pediatrican before starting your baby on solid foods.

Submitter:

Description: 
A quick and easy method for providing baby food for your little one with any added preservatives, sugar or salt.
