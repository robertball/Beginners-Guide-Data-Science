Title:
Simple Balsamic Steamed Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
salt to taste
black pepper to taste
garlic powder to taste
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup olive oil
1 pound of fresh veggies
(Recommended vegetables: asparagus, green beans, broccoli, cauliflower, mushrooms, zucchini.)

Directions:
Place all vegetables in a pot.
Pour balsamic vinegar and olive oil on top.
sprinkle salt, pepper and garlic pepper according to taste.
cover with lid
every few minutes stir vegetables.
when vegetables reach desired tenderness remove from heat and serve.

Submitter: 
Nicole Skorka
Description: 
Fast and flavorful veggies.  I use this recipe for both steaming and BBQ!
