Title:
Sausage Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of Pillsbury Hot Roll Mix
1 3/4 lb. of hot sausage
1/2 small onion
2 eggs beaten
2/3 of a cap of parmesean cheese
1 pkg. of motzerella cheese (8 oz. or 12 oz.)

Directions:
1) Mix box of Pillsbury mix as directed, let it rise for a few minutes
2) Roll dough on cookie sheet (flour counter)
3) Cook sausage, drain grease
4) Add onion, egss and cheese to sausage, let it melt in the pan
5) Spread mix evenly out onto the dough, roll up
Bake 400 for 20 minutes

Submitter: 
mollymoo
Description: 
This is a recipe my mother-in-law gave me. It is very versatile b/c you can have it as an appetizer, breakfast, lunch, or dinner dish. I hope you love this as much as my family does!
