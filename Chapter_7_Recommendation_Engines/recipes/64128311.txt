Title:
Sour Cream Cucmbers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sour cream
3 Tablespoons white vinegar
1 Tablespoon sugar
pepper to taste
4 medium cucumbers peeled and sliced
fresh dill chopped to taste
small sweet onion- thinly sliced into rings

Directions:
Whisk first four ingredients in a small bowl. Mix in with cucumbers, dill and onion. Refrigerate for 4 hours and serve.

Submitter: 
Sherillynn
Description: 
This is a great way to use fresh cucumbers in the summer. Nothing says summer salad like this one.
