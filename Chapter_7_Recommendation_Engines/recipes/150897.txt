Title:
Mango Mojito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976516.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Captain Morgan Parrot Bay™ Mango
4 ounces mango puree
1 sprig fresh mint

Directions:
Pour Captain Morgan Parrot Bay(TM) Mango and mango puree into a shaker; shake with ice and pour into tall glass and garnish with mint.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Add fruit flavor to your drinks without all the slicing by using Captain Morgan Parrot Bay™ in Coconut, Mango, Pineapple or Passion Fruit.
