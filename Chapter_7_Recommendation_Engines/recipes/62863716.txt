Title:
Garlic Schmeared Rosemary Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Roasted Garlic Puree
4 heads garlic
3 T olive oil, divided
salt and pepper
Chicken
6 chicken breasts or 1 whole chicken
2 T olive oil
1 T salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 T rosemary, finely chopped
1/3 cup roasted garlic puree

Directions:
Roasted Garlic Puree
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.  Cut off tops of the bulbs.  Drizzle with 1 T oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Wrap in tin foil and roast 1 hour.  Remove from oven and let cool.  Remove cloves and smash with lemon juice and rosemary.  Stir in remaining olive oil.
Chicken
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.  Rub chicken with oil and season with salt and pepper.  Sprinkle rosemary over chicken.  Place chicken on rosemary stems and roast for 30 minutes.  Remove from oven and schmear with roasted garlic puree.  Return to oven for 15 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
