Title:
Italian Beef on a Bun Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lg chuck roast
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1 tsp Accent
1/4 tsp basil
1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp onion salt
1/2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp seasoned salt

Directions:
Cook the roast in a crockpot until tender, reserve all liquid.  Lift out meat & shred.  To the broth add the remaining seasonings.  Add shredded meat to the broth & simmer for 20-30 minutes. Spoon onto bun.
