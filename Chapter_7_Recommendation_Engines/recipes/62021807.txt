Title:
Cornmeal Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. flour
1 c. cornmeal
1/4 c. shortening
1 tbsp. Baking powder
1 tsp. Salt
2 tbsp. Honey
3/4 c. milk

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients and cut in shortening until it is in pea-sized pieces.  Add honey and milk and stir to mix.  Drop by large spoonfuls on greased baking sheets.  Bake at 375 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
CLOUDSSUNRAIN
Description: 
Easy, tasty biscuits made with a cornmeal batter and dropped on a baking sheet.  Recipe is easily doubled!
