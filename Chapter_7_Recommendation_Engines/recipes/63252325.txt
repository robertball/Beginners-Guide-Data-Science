Title:
Chicken Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cloves chopped garlic
1/2 green pepper chopped
1 tbs olive oil
1 tsp minced onion flakes
1 tsp italian seasoning mix
3 cups low sodium college inn chicken broth
1 1/2 cups arborio rice

Directions:
saute garlic and green peppers in olive oil. Once tender add rice and saute for about 3 minutes or until it turns translucent. Add chicken stock, onion flakes and italian seasoning and stir. Cook about 20 minutes over medium heat, stirring throughout to release the starches of the rice and thicken sauce.

Submitter: 
Jena LaBree
Description: 
Creamy flavorful risotto dish goes perfectly with chicken.
