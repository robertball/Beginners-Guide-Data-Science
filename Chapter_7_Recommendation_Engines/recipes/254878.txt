Title:
Quick and Easy Edamame Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 cups hulled edamame
1 (15.5 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
3/4 cup frozen corn, thawed
1/2 cup diced red onion
1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
2 tablespoons ginger sesame dressing, or to taste
2 teaspoons salt

Directions:
Combine edamame, black beans, corn, red onion, red pepper, ginger sesame dressing, and salt in a bowl; stir until well mixed. Place in the refrigerator until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Groove1
Description: 
This salad is high in protein and healthy. A great side dish or stand-alone as a healthy lunch to bring to work. 
Sesame-ginger dressing is available at most supermarkets and Japanese restaurants.
