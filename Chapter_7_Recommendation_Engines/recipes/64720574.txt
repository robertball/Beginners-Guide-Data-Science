Title:
hotsy shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound medium sized raw shrimp peeled and deveined
1/2 cup Buttermilk
1/2 cup Cornstarch
1/2 cup Mayonnaise
1/4 cup Sriracha hot sauce
2 tablespoons Thai Sweet Chili Sauce
1 tablespoon Honey
Oil for Frying

Directions:
Place shrimp and buttermilk in a medium bowl briefly. Coat the shrimp well and drain the buttermilk, and shake off the excess.
Coat the shrimp pieces in cornstarch and set aside.
Fry in Fry Daddy for 3 minutes and drain in strainer
Whisk together mayonnaise, sriracha, thai sweet chili sauce and honey
Toss shrimp with sauce
