Title:
Cranberry Cheesecake Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRUST
1 (18.25 oz) pkg. Butter Recipe cake mix
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 egg
1/4 cup chopped pecans
FILLING
8 oz. pkg. cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup powdered sugar
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 egg
1 (16 oz. can) whole berry cranberry sauce
1/4 tsp. nutmeg

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  In large bowl, combine cake mix, butter and 1 egg at low speed until crumbly.  Add pecans & mix well.  Press evenly in bottom of ungreased 13x9 inch pan.  Bake at 350 for 5-8 minutes or until crust is set (crust will not be browned.)
In small bowl, combine cream cheese, powdered sugar, vanilla and 1 egg, and beat until smooth.  Add nutmeg to cranberry sauce & blend well.  Carefully pour cream cheese mixture over crust.  Spoon cranberry sauce in 3 rows lengthwise in pan.  Pull knife through cranberry sauce and into cream cheese mixture to form swirls.  Bake for 30-40 minutes or until cream cheese mixture is set.  Cool completely and cut into bars.

Description: 
Here's a yummy holiday bar from my Mother's recipe collection that my family & friends just love!  The delicious butter crust makes it special.  It goes very nicely on a Thanksgiving dessert buffet table (if you have room for dessert)!
