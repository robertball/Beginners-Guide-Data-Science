Title:
Quick Fudge Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1/4 cup cocoa
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup milk
2 tbsp. white cor syrup
1/8 teaspoon salt
2 cups confectioners sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Add to a medium boiler the first six ingredients, which are the sugar,cocoa, butter, milk, white corn syrup, and salt.
Bring to a full rolling boil. Boil vigorously, stirring occasionally for three minutes. Cocoa syrup must become thick and coat the spoon, so be sure mixture boils hard.
Let cool for 10 to 15 minutes.
Beat in sifted confectioners sugar and vanilla.
Let stand until icing is of sreading consistency.
Frost cake.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
Quick and easy homemade fudge icing. My mom kept this recipe after her and some classmates used it in school quite a few years ago. It's a great but simple and easy to make homemade icing.
