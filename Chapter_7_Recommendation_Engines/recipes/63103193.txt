Title:
strawberry buttercream frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/787204.jpg
Ingredients:
double batch:
2 cups of unsalted butter(4 sticks)at room temperature
2 lbs confectioner's sugar
2 TBS meringue powder
2 tsp vanilla
3/4 cup thawed frozen strawberries
milk(depends upon desired consistency)

Directions:
puree strawberries and set aside.
beat butter with stand mixture until light. scrape sides of bowl and add meringue powder and vanilla. mix. scrape sides again and SLOWLY beat in meringue powder, alternating with strawberry puree, scraping down the sides occasionally. add milk until desired consistency is reached.
