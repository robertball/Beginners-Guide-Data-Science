Title:
Spice Rubbed Roasted Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp. olive oil
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. cumin
1/2 tsp. ground coriander
1/4 tsp. paprika
1/8 tsp. cinnamon
1/8 tsp. pepper
1 (2 1/4 lbs.) skinless salmon fillet
2 cups thinly sliced onion

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Rub olive oil on fish.
Combine spices and rub evenly over salmon.
Spray baking pan with cooking spray.
Place onion in pan and place fish on top of onion.
Cook for about 20 minutes or until fish flakes easily.
