Title:
Country Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/934690.jpg
Ingredients:
3 (14 ounce) cans Swanson® Chicken Broth
1/8 teaspoon poultry seasoning
1/8 teaspoon dried thyme leaves, crushed
1 medium carrot, sliced
1 stalk celery, sliced
1 small onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup uncooked regular long-grain white rice
2 (5 ounce) cans Swanson® Premium Chunk Chicken Breast in Water, drained

Directions:
Mix broth, poultry seasoning, thyme, carrot, celery and onion in saucepan. Heat to a boil. Stir in rice. Cover.
Cook over low heat 20 minutes or until rice is done.
Add chicken and heat through.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Homemade soups like this chicken and rice recipe are easy to make with Swanson® Broth and are also heartwarming to eat.
