Title:
Peanut Butter Pineapple Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup peanut butter
12 (6 inch) flour tortillas
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, well drained
2 medium bananas, chopped
1 cup chopped kiwi fruit
1 cup chopped strawberries
1/2 cup flaked coconut

Directions:
Spread 2 teaspoons peanut butter on each tortilla.
Top each tortilla with 1 tablespoon each pineapple, bananas, kiwi and strawberries. Sprinkle with coconut. Roll up tortillas.

This odd combination is a favorite among kids!
