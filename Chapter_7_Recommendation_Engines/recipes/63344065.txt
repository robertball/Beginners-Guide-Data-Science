Title:
Remoulade Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
1/2 cup shopped sweet onions
1/2 cup chopped green onions (green and white parts)
1/4 cup chopped celery
2 Tablespoons prepared horseradish
3 tablespoons creole or whole grain mustard
3 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
3 tablespoons ketchup
3/4 cup peanut oil
3 tablespoons chopped parsley
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
Combine lemon juice, sweet onions, green onions, celery, horseradish, mustards, and ketchup in food processor.  Process on high for 30 seconds.
With machine running, slowly drizzle 3/4 cup of the peanut oil into the food processor until well incorporated and slightly thickened.
Add the parsely, 1/2 tsp of salt, and cayenne pepper.
Store in refrigerator in air-tight container.
