Title:
Spicy Chicken and vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breast
2 tbsp garlic powder
2 tbsp onion powder
1 tbsp creole seasoning
1 medium onion sliced
1 green bell pepper sliced
1 red bell pepper sliced
1 can Rotel tomatoes and green chilis
2 cloves garlic minces
1 8 oz can tomatoe sauce
3 tbsp vegetable or olive oil

Directions:
Season chicken breast with garlic powder, onion powder and creole seasoning.
Heat oil in skillet and add seasoned chicken breast.  Cook until chicken is light brown on each side.
Add garlic,onion and peppers to skillet.  Cook over medium heat until wilted.
Add Rotel tomatoes and green chilis and tomatoe sauce, reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 minutes or until chicken in no longer pink inside.
Serve over pasta or rice.
