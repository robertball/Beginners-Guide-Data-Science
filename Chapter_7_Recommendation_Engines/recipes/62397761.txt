Title:
Cranberry chocolate orange muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/429388.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup dried cranberries
1 tbsp grated orange zest
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 cup boiling water
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
1 3/4 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup semi sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F.
Place cranberries, orange zest and baking soda in a bowl and pour boiling water over them.  Let stand 5 minutes at room temp.
Mix together brown sugar, oil, egg and vanilla.  Add cranberry mixture and beat well.
In a seperate bowl stir together flour and baking powder.
Stir flour mixture into wet mixture until just combined.
Gently fold in chocolate chips.
Spoon batter evenly into a greased or paper lined muffin tin.
Bake for 20-25 minutes until muffins are golden brown and springy to the touch.
