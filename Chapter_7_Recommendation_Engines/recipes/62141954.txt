Title:
Tomato Tortellini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Beef Bouillon Cubes
8 Cups of Water
(1) 16 oz. Frozen Beef Tortellini
(1) 14.5 oz. Can of Diced Tomatoes with Basil and Oragano (Undrained)
1 1/4 cup of Tomato Juice
(2) 15 oz. Cans of Mixed Vegetables (drained)
1/2 Cup Milk
1/2 Cup Grated Parmasean Cheese
1/4 teaspoon of each or desired amount - Salt/Pepper/Garlic Powder
Shreaded Mozzarella to sprinkle on each indevidual bowl of soup

Directions:
Cook Tortellini According to Instructions on Package.
While Tottellini is cooking bring the 8 cups of water to a boil and add the Beef Bouillon Cubes.  Stir until cubes are desolved.
Lower to low-Medium heat.
Add undrained diced tomatoes, tomato juice, drained mixed vegetables and milk.
When tortellini is finished, drain and add to soup.
Add grated cheese and salt/pepper/garlic powder as desired.
Heat for additional 10 minutes
Sprinkle individual bowls of soup with desired amount of mozzarella cheese.  Hint: Too much mozzarella cheese will be really stringy.
I also add a splash of hot sauce to my individual bowl to give it a little kick.

Submitter: 
MICHMEIG
Description: 
This is a quick easy soup that taste really good.  It can also be made in the crock pot.
