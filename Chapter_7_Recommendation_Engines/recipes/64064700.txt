Title:
The Best Hot Chocolate EVER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
chocolate almond milk
1 packet powder hot chocolate mix
1 cup chocolate pudding
2-6 tablespoons heavy cream (play it by year)
2 tablespoons chocolate chips
large mug
optional topping of your choice (marshmallows, whipped cream etc.)

Directions:
using almond milk and hot cacao mix make hot chocolate
melt/ temper chocolate chips
add melted chocolate chips, pudding, and heavy cream to hot chocolate
stir
heat for 30 seconds in microwave
stir again before adding topping of your choice (whipped cream, marshmallows etc.)

Submitter: 
demdessertstho
Description: 
This hot chocolate is so rich and delicious it's worth the extra work! Perfect for winter time!
