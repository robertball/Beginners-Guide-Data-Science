Title:
Moms Chicken and Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Eggs
2 half egg shells of water
1/4 Teaspoon Salt
4 drops yellow food coloring
1.5 cups Flour
Chicken and Chicken broth

Directions:
In large bowl beat eggs slightly.
Beat in water, salt and food coloring.
Stir in enough flour to bake a soft dough.
On a floured surface, knead dough until it can be rolled out.
Roll out dough to desired thickness.
Let dry 4 to 5 hours, flipping noodles over several times while drying.
When dry and not sticky to touch, Cut dough into 4 equal portions.
Roll up like jelly roll, with sharp knife slice crosswise into noodles of desired width.
Spread cut noodles on cookie sheet and let dry overnight.
When ready to cook drop noodles into seasoned boiling chicken stock and simmer slowly for 1 hour.
When noodles are done add pieces of cooked chicken to noodles.
Enjoy
