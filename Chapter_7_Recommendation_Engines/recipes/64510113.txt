Title:
Honey-Soy Glazed Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbs honey
1 Tbs Soy sauce
1 Tbs Apple Cider Vinegar
1 Tbs brown sugar
1 tsp hot sauce
4  salmon filets

Directions:
Mix lines #1 through 5. Saute salmon and baste with sauce. After sauce is gone, let salmon continue to cook until residual sauce in pan is soaked up.
