Title:
Pumpkin Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1052001.jpg
Ingredients:
2 sticks butter
3 cups sugar
2 cups pumpkin puree
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
2 cups quick oats
4 cups all-purpose flour
2 tsp baking powder
2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp salt
2 cups chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.  In large mixing bowl cream butter and sugar together.  Add pumpkin, eggs and vanilla. In separate bowl, mix oats, flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon and salt.  Add flour mixture to mixing bowl.  Add chocolate chips.  Scoop onto baking sheet and bake for 12 minutes.

Submitter: 
anjelique
Description: 
This is a recipe I adapted from my grandmother.  It is my favorite pumpkin cookie recipe. Plus it makes enough for a crowd!
