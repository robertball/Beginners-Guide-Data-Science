Title:
Christian's Killer Brisket Paste Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cup apple juice
1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup prepared mustard
1/3 cup seasoning salt
1/4 cup paprika powder
1/4 cup chili powder
1/4 cup garlic powder
1/4 cup onion powder
1/2-1 teaspoon cayenne
2 tablespoons fresh krushed rosemary

Directions:
Mix all ingredients. Pour and rub the paste over brisket. Leave in frifge for 8-12 hours. Prepare brisket in your smoker.

Somwhere between a rub and a marinade you'll find this paste for perfect brisket.
