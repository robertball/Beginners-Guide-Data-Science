Title:
Mexican  Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Beer
1 tbs.. Lemon Juice
2 Chili Peppers (seeded and chopped)
1/2 Bell Pepper (seeded and chopped)
1 lb. Monterey Jack Cheese (shredded)
2 tbs. flour
1/2 tsp. Paprika
Pinch Cumin
Salt to taste

Directions:
Heat beer on high until it foams
Add lemon juice, chili and bell peppers
Reduce heat to medium
Mix cheese with flour, paprika and cumin
Slowly stir cheese into beer
Salt to taste
Serve with tortilla chips
