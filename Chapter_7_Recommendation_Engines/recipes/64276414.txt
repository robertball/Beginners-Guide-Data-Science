Title:
Coops Ham and Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound dry great Northern beans
1/2 pound cooked ham, diced
1 small onion, diced
2 tablespoons brown sugar
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
1 tablespoon dried parsley

Directions:
Rinse beans in a large pot; discard shriveled beans and any small stones. Add 8 cups of cold water. Let stand overnight or at least 8 hours. Drain and rinse beans.
Return beans to pot and add ham, onion, brown sugar, salt, pepper, cayenne and parsley and  water to cover. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer 1 1/2 to 2 hours (or 4 hours in a high crock pot), until beans are tender. Add more water if necessary during cooking time.
