Title:
Ham Salad Croissants Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/321916.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Croissants
Ranch Dressing
2 cups ground ham
1/3 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*.
Mix ham with the amount of ranch dressing you want.*
Scoop equally among sliced croissants.
Top with cheese.
Bake in oven until croissant is toasted and cheese is melted, about 10-15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jessica
Description: 
My mom always made these when we had leftover ham and we couldn't wait until they got out of the oven!
