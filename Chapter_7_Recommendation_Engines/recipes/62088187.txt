Title:
Banana Bread/Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese	2 ¼ c flour
1 c sugar		1 ½ t baking powder
¼ c margarine		½ t baking soda
1 c smashed banana
2 eggs

Directions:
Mix sugar and eggs until well blended. Continue to mix wet ingredients. Then blend in dry ingredients.
Bake at 350 for 1 hour and 10 minutes for a loaf or 30 minutes for 12 muffins.
