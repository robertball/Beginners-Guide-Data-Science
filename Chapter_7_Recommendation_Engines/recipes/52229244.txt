Title:
Five Ingredient Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup cold evaporated milk
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 pint heavy cream or half and half

Directions:
In a medium bowl, stir together cold milk, vanilla, condensed milk and salt.  Set aside.
In a large bowl, beat heavy cream with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form.  Fold milk mixture into whipped cream.
Pour into shallow 2 quart dish, cover and freeze for 4 hours, stirring once after 2 hours or when edges start to harden (can transfer to two one-quart plastic containers).  Serve or store in an airtight container up to 10 days.
