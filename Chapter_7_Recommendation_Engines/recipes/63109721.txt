Title:
Cheesy Ranch Tuna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. cooked short pasta
1 cup ranch vegetable dip
1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of cheddar soup
2 cans tuna, drained
1 tbsp of garlic powder
1 tsp of pepper
salt to taste although generally it tastes fine
2 cups of veggies such as peas, carrots or broccoli
1/4 cup shredded cheese for topping

Directions:
Mix all ingredients but the pasta and 1/4 cup cheese together. Add the pasta and bake covered at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Uncover and add the 1/4 cup cheese to the top and bake another 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Frugalmommy
Description: 
A creamy delight that my family loves. Works great in the crockpot on low all day.
