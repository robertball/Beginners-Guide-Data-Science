Title:
Easy Peach Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Pillsbury Pie Crust
1/4 Cup Sugar
1/4 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp. Cinnamon
1/4 tsp. Nutmeg
4 Cups Canned Sliced Peaches
1/4 Cup Peach Juice
1 TBSP Lemon Juice (optional)
2 TBSP melted butter
5 TBSP of Flour or Tapioca

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.  Unfold first pie crust and put into your pie dish.  Cut excess crust off.  Mix all the ingredients together.  Pour into pie crust.  Add the top pie crust, press bottom crust and top crust edges together, and cut four slits in the top of your pie crust.  Bake for 15 minutes and then put foil around the edges of your crust.  Bake for an additional 25 minutes or until bubbly.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a very simple peach pie using canned peach slices.
