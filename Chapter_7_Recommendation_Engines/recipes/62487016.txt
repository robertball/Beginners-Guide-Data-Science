Title:
Lumpia-Mollica Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground pork
1 medium yellow onion
1 lg stalk celery
1 lg carrot
1 tbs minced fresh garlic
1/2 tsp powdered tumeric, ginger, cumin, and corriander
1 tsp red chili flakes and garlic powder
1/4 tsp celery seeds
2 pkgs spring roll wrappers

Directions:
* Add a dash of salt and pepper to each step of cooking (veggies, and meat).  Finely chop celery, carrot, and onion. Saut√© veggies until onion is carmelized (celery and carrots still be a little crunchy)about 5-7 minutes on med low. Add garlic and ground pork. Cook  pork thoroughly.  Drain fat, add spices, stir, then let cool. When cooled add 1 tbs pork mixture per spring roll, then wrap into small rolls.
Fry in 350 degree oil for about 4-6 minutes if frozen, or until golden brown.
Makes approximately 50 rolls.  Can be frozen for up to 4 months.
