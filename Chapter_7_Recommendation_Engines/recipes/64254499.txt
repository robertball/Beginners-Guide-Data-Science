Title:
Fake Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box white cake mix (or any flavor you can compliment with pie filling)
12 oz./1 can soda(any flavor)
2 eggs
21 oz./1 can pie filling(any flavor)

Directions:
in mixing bowl- whisk together eggs and soda
add cake mix- mix well- til lumps are dissolved
fold in pie filling
spray 9 X 13 pan, spoon in batter-and bake at 325 for 40-50 minutes- rotating half way thru
*** may add glaze-a sprinkle of nuts.coconut.chips.cinnamon to taste

Submitter: 
nanny129
Description: 
Enjoy a great cake in under an hour- it will amaze family & friends...no mixer needed.
