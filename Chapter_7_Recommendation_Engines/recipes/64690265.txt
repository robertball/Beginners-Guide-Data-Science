Title:
Ranch Cheddar Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground turkey
1 (1 ounce) envelope dry ranch dressing mix
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (low fat)
1/4 cup chopped green onion (scallion)
6 whole wheat buns

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in bowl.
Form into 6 patties
Cook in skillet or on grill until cooked through, appox. 6-7 minute per side.
Serve on buns with lettuce and tomato.

Submitter: 
Alexis Redden
Description: 
These are great. Even my kids love them. Serve on a whole-wheat bun and you have a tasty , healthy meal.
