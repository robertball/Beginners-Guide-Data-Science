Title:
Easiest Peach Tart Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 peaches, Blached, peeled and sliced (abt 1 1/2 lbs.)
1/4 c blueberries or raspberries
1/2 lemon
2 Tbls sugar
Cinnamon to desired taste (optional)
1 pkg (9 in) refrigerated pie crust

Directions:
Unroll pie crust, pile fruit in center leaving two to three inches from edge. Squeeze lemon over and sprinkle sugar and cinnamon evenly across fruit. Fold crust up and over fruit. Leave the center open to allow fruit to peak through.
Bake 375 degrees for 20-25, until crust turns golden brown.  Cook on parchment paper for super easy clean up.  Serve with ice cream or whipped topping.
