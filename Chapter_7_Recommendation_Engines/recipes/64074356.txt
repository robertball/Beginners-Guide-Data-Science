Title:
Chili Paneer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1133746.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Indian Cottage Cheese/ Paneer cubed (Use extra-firm tofu for vegan version)
1 Onion- chopped
4-6 Green Chillies - Slit vertically into half.
1 inch Ginger finely chopped/grated
4 cloves of garlic finely chopped
1 bell pepper/capsicum - cubed (can use different colored peppers)
Handful of Cilantro/Coriander leaves for garnishing
2 springs of spring onion for garnishing (Optional)
4 tbsp Corn Starch (More if required)
4 tbsp Oil for frying
Gravy:
2 tbsp Soy sauce
2 tbsp Chili sauce (red)
2 tbsp Tomato ketchup/Sauce
1 tsp of Sugar
1/2- 1 tsp of Salt or as per taste

Directions:
Coat paneer/tofu pieces with corn starch. Alternatively you can make a thick batter of corn starch using water and then coat paneer pieces with it.  Deep fry in oil over medium heat. Take out on a tissue.
In the remaining oil, add ginger, garlic. Add onions, chilies and bell peppers. Fry until golden brown.
Add soy sauce, chili sauce and tomato ketchup. Add sugar, salt and remaining corn starch solution in it.
Add some water if too thick.
Add paneer pieces and Saute. Can also cook covered for a few minutes.
When the gravy is thick, turn off heat and serve. Garnish with cilantro and spring onions and serve hot.
Can be served with rice.
