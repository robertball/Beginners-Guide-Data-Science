Title:
Creamy Chocolate Tofu Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. semi-sweet dark chocolate chips
1 tsp. vanilla
2 pkgs. extra firm tofu
9 inch pie crust (precooked)
GARNISH: 1 cup fresh raspberries

Directions:
TIP: If pie crust is not pre-made, make now and allow to cool.
1. melt chocolate in microwave oven with 2 tbl. water. Thoroughly blend tofu in a blender or food processor.
2. Add melted chocolate and vanilla to the tofu and blend on high speed for 2 minutes.
3. Pour mixture into cool pie crust. Refrigerate for 1-2 hours. Garnish with raspberries and serve.
