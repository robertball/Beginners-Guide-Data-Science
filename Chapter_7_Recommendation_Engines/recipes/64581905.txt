Title:
Spiralized Apple 'Spaghetti' With Cinnamon Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3666547.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium sized firm fleshed apple (like a red delicious)
6 oz plain Greek Yogurt
1/4 tsp cinnamon
2 - 3 tsp honey, to taste
1/4 tsp vanilla
1 oz walnuts

Directions:
Using the 'C' blade, spiralize the apple and set aside
In a small bowl, combine  yogurt, cinnamon, honey and vanilla and mix well
Toss apples in yogurt sauce and garnish with walnuts
Serve

Description: 
Cool and refreshing dessert with the crunch of walnuts.
