Title:
OMG Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/510283.jpg
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken (remove the giblet package)
1 lemon (cut in half)
2 cloves garlic (whole)
1 small onion (cut in qtrs)
3 tablespoons of butter
salt
pepper
paprika
cayenne pepper

Directions:
preheat over to 350
rinse chicken.
pat dry
season with salt, pepper, cayenne pepper and paprika to taste.... inside and out.
put garlic, lemon, butter and onion inside chicken.
tie chicken legs together.
place chicken in roaster or on a cooking pan if you dont have a roaster. (cover bottom with tin foil for easy clean up)
put in oven for 1 hour 15 minutes. baste occasionally if you want to...
check that internal temp is 180.
cover with tin foil and cook an additional 30 min.
enjoy!
