Title:
Fish Stick Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991652.jpg
Ingredients:
12 Gorton's® Crunchy Golden Fish Sticks
1 large onion, sliced
1 large green pepper, sliced
1 large red pepper, sliced
1 large orange or yellow pepper, sliced
1 teaspoon minced garlic
2 tablespoons cooking oil
Fajita dry seasoning mix
Flour tortillas
Shredded cheese
Salsa
Sour cream
Guacamole

Directions:
Cook Gorton's Fish Sticks according to directions.
While the sticks are cooking, heat oil in a skillet, add garlic, onions and peppers. Stir and cook 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender. Stir in seasoning mix.
Place 1 or 2 Fish Sticks in middle of tortilla, top with vegetable mixture, cheese, and salsa, sour cream and guacamole if desired.

Submitter: 
Gorton's®
Description: 
Sizzle up dinner tonight with a Mexican flare.  Gorton's Fish Sticks give these Fajitas a new and exciting taste.  Serve them up with sizzling veggies, sour cream, salsa, and guacamole for a meal that everyone will love.
