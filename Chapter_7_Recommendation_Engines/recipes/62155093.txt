Title:
Grilled Lamb with Tomato Basil Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 1 inch thick loin lamb chops or 1/2 boneless leg of lamb butterflied and trimmed of excess fat
1 6 oz can tomato paste
1 1/2 T olive oil
2/3 cup red wine (Merlot, Zinfindal or your favorite red wine)
6 cloves garlic peeled and minced
12 fresh basil leaves sliced into ribbons and then chopped
sea salt and cracked black pepper

Directions:
Mix tomato paste, wine, olive oil, minced garlic and basil in a small bowl.  Place chops or butterflied lamb in a glass or ceramic container and paint both sides heavily with the marinade.  Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hrs.
Heat grill to 475 degrees.
Wipe some of the marinade off the lamb but leave a light coating to carmalize during grilling. Sprinkle with salt and cracked black pepper.  Grill chops on an lightly oiled rack until medium rare about 4-5 minutes each side,or grill butterflied leg of lamb 8 minutes per side. Adjust time for rare, medium or well done servings or according to the thickness of the lamb.

Submitter: 
Juanita/Jane
Description: 
Lamb prepared with this easy marinade and grilled to perfection invites your guests to enjoy a dinner that's simple to prepare but offers the aroma and taste of an evening in Tuscany. 
What could be better than that?
