Title:
Taco Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 c shopped green pepper
1/2 c chopped onion
2/3 c water
1 envelope taco seasoning
1 can (15 oz) black beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (14.5 oz) Mexican diced tomatoes
6 flour tortillas (8 inches)
1 can (16 oz) refried beans
3 c (12 oz) shredded Mexican cheese

Directions:
In a large skillet, fry beef, green, pepper, and onion over medium heat until meat is no longer pink. Drain.
Add water and taco seasoning. Bring to boil, reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, for two minutes.
Stir in black beans and tomatoes. Simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes.
Place two tortillas in greased 13x9 inch baking dish. Spread with half of refried beans and half of beef mixture. Sprinkle with 1 c cheese. Repeat layers. Top with remaining tortillas and cheese.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 30-45 minutes.
