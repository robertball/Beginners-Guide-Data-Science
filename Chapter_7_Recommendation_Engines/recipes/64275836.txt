Title:
Bacon Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound sliced bacon
1 medium sized sweet onion
8 eggs, lightly beaten
5 cups frozen shredded hash brown potatoes
2 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
2 cups small curd cottage cheese
1 1/2 cups shredded Swiss cheese
1/2 cup shredded monteray jack cheese

Directions:
Cook bacon until crispy.
Crumble bacon and set aside with all other ingredients accept onion.
Chop onion and add to frying pan with bacon grease.
Combine all ingredients from pan into large bowl with bacon.
Mix well and pour into large baking pan.
Bake at 350 degrees 35 minutes.
Remove and let cool for 5 minutes.
Serve and enjoy!
