Title:
Artichoke Nibbles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2666998.jpg
Ingredients:
2 - 6 oz jars artichoke hearts
1 sm onion finely chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
4 eggs
1/4 C dry bread crumbs
1/4 t salt
1/8 t oregano
1/8 t pepper
1/2 lb sharp cheddar cheese
2 T parsley

Directions:
Drain marinade from artichokes hearts into frying pan. Chop artichokes and set aside. To marinade add onion and garlic. In a bowl, beat the eggs with a fork and add bread crumbs, salt, oregano and pepper. Stir in cheese and parsley. Pour into greased 9x11 baking pan and bake at 325 for 30 minutes or until set when lightly touched. Let cool and cut into one inch squares.
