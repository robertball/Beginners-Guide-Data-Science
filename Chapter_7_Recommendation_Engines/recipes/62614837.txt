Title:
Mars Bar squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/568601.jpg
Ingredients:
4 regular size mars bars
1/2 c butter
1 c milk choco chips

Directions:
Melt butter and mars bars over med-low heat in large sauce pan until smooth.
Remove from heat; stir in rice crispies.
Top with choco chips put under broiler for 30 seconds.
remove from heat spread choco even over mix.
cool in fridge. cut into squares.
