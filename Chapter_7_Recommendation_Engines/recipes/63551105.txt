Title:
Belgian waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 tablespoon  baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup oil
2 cups milk
2 eggs

Directions:
1. Spay Pam on both sides of waffle maker and plug in.
2. Separate eggs. Put the whites in one bowl and yolks in another.
3. Beat the whites until they are stiff. Set aside.
4. Add the flour, baking powder, salt, oil and milk to the egg yolks.
5. Beat the mixture until smooth.
6. Carefully fold the egg whites into flour mixture.
7. Pour 3/4 cup of batter onto bottom of heated waffle maker, close top.
8. Cook until light goes out.
9. Carefully remove the waffle.
