Title:
Green Goddess Dressing I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1 1/2 tablespoons snipped fresh parsley
1/4 cup minced green onions
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/2 clove garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 pinch ground black pepper

Directions:
In a medium-size mixing bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, sour cream, parsley, green onions, lemon juice, garlic, salt and pepper. Cover, and refrigerate until chilled.

Submitter: 
The California Egg Commission
Description: 
This creamy salad dressing recipe is a great version of an American classic.  You can also use tarragon vinegar or white wine vinegar instead of the lemon juice, if desired.
