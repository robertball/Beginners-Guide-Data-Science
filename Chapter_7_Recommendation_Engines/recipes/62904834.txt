Title:
Asparagus and Goat Cheese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/704821.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. fresh asparagus, trimmed
2 T extra virgin olive oil
4 cups Bibb lettuce
1 large tomato, cut into small wedges
1/4 cup crumbled goat cheese (1 oz.)
1/4 cup chopped walnuts, toasted
1/2 cup balsamic vinaigrette

Directions:
Preheat grill to medium (350 degrees)  Place asparagus in a large shallow dish.  Season with salt and pepper to taste; drizzle with oil.
Place asparagus on grill.  Grill 4 to 6 minutes or until crisp-tender.
Place lettuce on a serving platter.  Place grilled asparagus on lettuce and top with tomatoes, cheese and walnuts.  Drizzle with vinaigrette and serve.

Submitter: 
Peggy
Description: 
A delicious and healthy salad.  Goes great with chicken or steak.
