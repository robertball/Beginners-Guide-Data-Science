Title:
Holiday Sausage Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. roll of Jimmy Dean hot sausage
10 oz. sharp cheddar cheese, grated
2 1/2 cups Bisquick

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix all ingredients together by hand, in food processor, or mixer.
Roll into small balls.
Bake 15 minutes, longer if frozen.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
I always make these sausage balls for Thanksgiving and Christmas well before the holiday and freeze them.  Then I just take them out and add 5-10 minutes to the baking time.  I like these sausage balls because they aren't as bready as other recipes.
