Title:
Beth's Blueberry Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbs. Butter, softened
3 eggs
3 1/2 cups milk
1 3/4 fresh blueberries
3/4 C. sugar
2 tsp. vanilla
2 Tbs. sugar
1/2 tsp. ground cimmamon
8 Cups sourdough bread cubes
Golden Sauce
3 Tbs. Butter
1 C. Whipping Cream
1/2 C. Sugar

Directions:
First make Golden Sauce by combining butter and sugar in a medium sauce pan. Cook over low heat until butter is melted. Stir in whipping cream. Bring to boil; reduce heat. Boil gently, uncovered for 5-10 minutes or until mixture is slightly thickened. cover w/ plastic wrap and set aside.
Preheat oven to 350. In a large mixing bowl beat butter and the 3/4 C. sugar w/ electic mixer until well combined. Add eggs and vanilla; beat 2-3 minutes or until fluffy. Slowly stir in the milk. Place bread cubes in a large bowl; pour milk mixture over bread, let stand 5 minutes. Stir in blueberries; transfer to an ungreased 3 quart baking dish.
COmbine the 2 tbs. sugar and cinnamon; sprinkle evenly over bread mixture. Drizzle 1/3 C. Golden Sauce over bread mixture. Bake about 35 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean. Serve warm w/ remaining Golden Sauce.
