Title:
Big Ray's Potato Kielbasa Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1130230.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds new red potatoes, cubed
1/4 cup water
1 1/2 pound Polish Kielbasa Sausage or Polish sausage, cut into 1/4-inch slices
1 medium onion, chopped
4 Tbl olive oil, divided
3 Tbl brown sugar
3 Tbl cider vinegar
3 Tbl Dijon mustard
1 1/2 tsp dried thyme
1/2 tsp pepper
6 cups fresh baby spinach
5 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled

Directions:
Place potatoes and water in a microwave-safe dish. Cover and microwave on high for 4 minutes or until tender; drain.
In a large skillet, saute kielbasa and onion in 1 Tbl olive oil until onion is tender. Put potatoes in a 13x9 pan coated generously with 3 Tbl olive oil, tossed them to coat, placed in broiler until crispy tossing every min or two to even the crisp.
Combine the brown sugar, vinegar, mustard, thyme and pepper; stir into skillet. Bring to a boil.  Add potatoes and reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 2-3 minutes or until heated through. Add spinach and bacon; cook and stir until spinach is wilted.

Description: 
This is perfect on those cold late fall and early winter nights. The smokey kielbasa steals the show in this hearty home-style all-in-one meal.
