Title:
Turkey soft tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
2 Tbsp taco seasoning
8 tortilla wraps
Top with:
2 Tbsp shredded lettuce
2 Tbsp shredded cheese
1 Tbsp light sour cream
1 tsp taco sauce
2 Tbsp chopped tomato

Directions:
Cook turkey until no longer pink (about 10 minutes). Add taco seasoning and water, cook another 5 minutes. Top tortilla with turkey and toppings.
