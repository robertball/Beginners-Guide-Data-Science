Title:
Slow Cooker Salsa Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds (32 ounces) chicken breasts/thighs boneless and skinless
1 cup salsa homemade or purchased
1 cup petite diced canned tomatoes (we use Rotelle habanero)
2 tablespoons Taco Seasoning (we use Penzeys Bold Taco)
1 cup onions, diced fine
1/2 cup celery diced fine
1/2 cup carrots, shredded
Optional Toppings
Sour Cream
Grated Cheese
Chopped Scallions
Cilantro

Directions:
Directions
Salsa Chicken  is easy to make; just put all the ingredients  in a slow cooker and let the machine do the work. There are infinite variations, and your family is guaranteed to like each one.
6-8 hours to prepare; 10 minutes of active cooking time. Makes 8 one-cup servings of chicken.
Place the chicken in a slow cooker. Sprinkle the taco seasoning over the meat then layer the vegetables and salsa on top. Pour a quarter cup of water over the mixture, set on low and cook for 6-8 hours. The meat is cooked when it shreds or reaches an internal temperature of 165Â°F. When ready to serve, break up the chicken using two forks.
Serve in any way you choose (See note). Top with grated cheese, sour cream, cilantro and chopped scallions.
