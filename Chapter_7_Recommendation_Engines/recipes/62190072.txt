Title:
Easier Than You Think Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
4 fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 small zuchinni,diced fine
2 tablespoons capers,drained
1 pinch dried basil
1 pinch dried oregano
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
1 (14.5 ounce)can whole Italian tomatoes
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium heat, saute onions,green bell pepper and diced zuchinni in olive oil until onions are translucent and the peppers and zuchinni are tender. Add the mushrooms,garlic, basil, oregano,salt and ground black pepper.
Add the can tomatoes with liquid and reduce heat; simmering until the tomatoes are soft and begin to fall apart(about 20-30 mins). Add the tomato sauce and stir; add tomato paste to thicken.Add meats at this time ,if desired. Simmer on very low heat for about 15 minutes. Serve over you favorite pasta.
