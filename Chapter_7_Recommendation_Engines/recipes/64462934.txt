Title:
Zesty Summer Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the salad:
1 cucumber
1 red onion
4 roma tomatoes
4 cloves garlic
1/4c sundried tomatoes
1/4c spanish olives
1/2c pepperocinis
For the dressing:
1/2c white vinegar
1T pepperocini juice
1T sun-dried tomato oil
2T extra virgin olive oil
1t sugar
1t seasoned salt

Directions:
Combine peeled, seeded, sliced cucumber, thinly sliced red onion, sliced roma tomatoes, and minced garlic in a large bowl.  Add minced garlic, chopped sundried tomatoes, sliced pepperocinis, and whole olives.  Toss to combine.
Whisk together dressing in a separate bowl.  Pour over salad.
Refrigerate salad until serving, at least 2 hours.

Submitter: 
mrs.embee
Description: 
This tangy side salad is a great way to use up those garden-fresh cucumbers and tomatoes.
