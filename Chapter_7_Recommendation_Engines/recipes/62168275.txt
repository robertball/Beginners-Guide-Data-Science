Title:
Brown Butter Butternut Squash soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lg butternut squash
3 lg red potatoes
15 baby carrots
2 med cloves garlic
1/3 c butter
1/4, 1/4, 1 1/2 c vegetable broth
1 small yellow onion
1tbsp, 1 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp dried rosemary
1/4 c water
sea salt

Directions:
boil water in large dutch oven, add carrots and sea salt to taste.
while boiling peel potatoes, squash and onion. dice onion. chop potatoes and squash into 2" pieces, add to carrots. boil on MED-HIGH until able to mush with a spoon, about 20-25 minutes.
pre heat oven to 350 degrees.
cook butter on LOW in small sauce pan until it becomes a dark chocolate brown color, about 25 minutes.
saute onions with 1tbsp oil and 1/4 c water until clear.
when oven is ready, roast garlic on light colored baking sheet about 8 minutes, until golden, turning once.
when onions, butter, and garlic are done, blend in food processor or blender until smooth. it will turn into a thin paste.
clean machine, and when the potatoes, squash, and carrots are done, place in batches (about 2) in blender with 1/4c vegetable broth per batch, pureeing until smooth- no lumps.
put vegetables, garlic paste, and rest of vegetable broth back in the pot, and simmer on LOW for about 10 minutes, stirring consistently.
serve with crusty bread and butter
