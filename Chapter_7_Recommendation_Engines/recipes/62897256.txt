Title:
Spinach and Rice Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/701268.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 jar Bertoli Alfredo Sauce
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 cups packed torn fresh spinach leaves
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
1 1/2 cups hot cooked rice

Directions:
In a small saucepan, head alfredo sauce, bring to a slow simmer and add fresh chopped spinach. Cook for 1 minute or until spinach is wilted. Stir in Parmesan cheese; cook until the cheese is melted. Add salt and black pepper to taste.  Stir in rice.

Submitter: 
Kelly
Description: 
Even folks who don't like spinach enjoy this rich, cheesy rice and spinach combination. We prefer it with pork chops, but it goes well with chicken or steak as well.Modified from: Spinach and Rice Alfredo
