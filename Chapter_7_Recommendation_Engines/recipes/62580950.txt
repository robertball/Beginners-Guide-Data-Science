Title:
Green Bean & Pea Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz. can English Peas (drained)
1 15 oz.can French cut green beans (drained)
1 (2 ounce) bottle diced pimento peppers (drained)
1 (10.75 ounce)can Cream of Mushroom soup
1 c. mayonnaise
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
Fried onions for topping.

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Mix all ingredients except cheese and onions.
Top evenly with cheese and fried onions .
Bake for 20 to 25 minutes in preheated oven, until browned and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Trisha Frary Haynes
Description: 
Great combination of vegetables. Delicious and easy, but not too heavy! Great for luncheons or holiday dinner!
