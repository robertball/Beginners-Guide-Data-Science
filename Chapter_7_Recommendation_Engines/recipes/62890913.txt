Title:
Simple and Healthy Fruit Smoothy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/698463.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 Mixed frozen berries
1 Cup plain Kefir

Directions:
Put the ingredients in the blender and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Deb C
Description: 
A thick and healthy probiotic drink that is not too sweet.  If you prefer it sweeter just add sugar to your taste preference.
