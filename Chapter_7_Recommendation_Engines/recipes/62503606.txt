Title:
Holiday "Leftovers" Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/631864.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups mashed potatoes
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese; divided use
1 2.8oz can french fried onions; divided use
1 1/2 cups diced ham, turkey or chicken.
1 10oz package frozen mixed vegetables such as peas  and carrots. Thawed.
1 can condensed cream mushroom soup (ham)/condensed cream chicken soup (turkey or chicken).
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
1/4 teaspoon granulated garlic
1/4 teaspoon fresh ground pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Stir in half the cheese and half the onions in mashed potatoes. Spread the potato mixture over the bottom and up sides of a 2 quart casserole dish to form a shell.
Combine ham/turkey or chicken, soup, vegetables, mustard, garlic and pepper. Spoon into shell. Cover loosely and bake in 375 degree oven for 35 to 45 minutes.
Sprinkle with remaining cheese and onions, bake uncovered for 5 additional minutes before serving.

Submitter:

Description: 
Leftovers couldn't taste better. This versatile casserole works well with ham, turkey, or chicken.
