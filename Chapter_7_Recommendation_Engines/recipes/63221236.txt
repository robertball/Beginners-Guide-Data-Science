Title:
Easy Chicken & Beef  Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. Italian-style fat free dressing
1/2 cup soy sauce
1 tsp. ground ginger
1 tsp. garlic powder
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl.  Pour over chicken or meat of choice to marinade.

Submitter: 
kmurph13
Description: 
The perfect blend of seasoning and spices.  Great to use one many types of chicken and could be used on steak tips too!
