Title:
Cool Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white vinegar
1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
1/2 cup water
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp dill
1 tsp salt
1/3 cup sugar
2 medium cucumbers, cleaned and sliced 1/8" thick

Directions:
Put vinegar, water, spices, and sugar in a medium bowl.
Stir until dissolved.
Add sliced cucumbers, stirring again.
Refrigerate before serving.

Submitter: 
Shantelle
Description: 
Refreshing, crisp with the right amount of tangy sweetness.
