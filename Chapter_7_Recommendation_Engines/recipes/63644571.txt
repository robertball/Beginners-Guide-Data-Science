Title:
Chocolate Sugar Cookie Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1013846.jpg
Ingredients:
4 c. powder sugar
4 Tbs. cocoa powder
6 Tbs. pasturized liquid egg whites
1/2 tsp. cream of tartar
1 tsp. chocolate flavor extract

Directions:
sift powder sugar, cocoa and cream of tartar to remove clumps into mixing bowl.
add egg whites and chocolate extract.
mix slowly till all is incorporated well. increase speed and mix approximately 5 minutes till stiff glossy peaks appear.
use immediately or store in airtight container in refrigertor.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
Chocolate flavored icing that dries hard enough to stack, but not so hard that it's not edible.
