Title:
Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts (thawed or frozen)
1 package McCormicks White Chicken Chili
1 can chicken broth
1 onion diced
1 can black beans (drained)
1 can great northern beans (drained)
1 can sweet corn
Torn cilantro leaves
1 8 oz. package of cream cheese
1 can hominy (drained)

Directions:
Place chicken breasts on bottom of Instant Pot and remaining ingredients on top.  Stir seasoning into ingredients and ensure there is some liquid under chicken; placing 8 large cubes of cream cheese on top.  Pressure cook for 20 minutes, allow steam to release for 10 minutes.  Shred chicken and stir.  Serve with cilantro, diced avocado, tortilla chips & shredded cheese
