Title:
Irish Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 medium potatoes
4 large carrots
1/2 medium onion
2 stalks celery
6 slices bacon (or more...!)
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1 10 oz can chicken broth
4 - 6 cups milk

Directions:
Peel and chop veggies. Boil potatoes and carrots in water, until tender. Drain and mash.
Meanwhile fry bacon till partially cooked.
Add onions and celery to bacon and cook until they are tender.
Add milk, broth and bacon mixture to veggies.
Heat slowly, stirring. If this can simmer awhile, it gets better and thicker.
