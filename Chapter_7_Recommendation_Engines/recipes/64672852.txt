Title:
Tater Tot Sausage Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs breakfast sausage, browned
1 32 oz bag frozen tater tots
1 1/2 c shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 c shredded mozzarella cheese
8 eggs
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp onion powder
2 c milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°.
In a greased 9x13 pan, gently toss browned sausage, tater tots, and cheeses.
In a bowl, whisk together eggs, seasonings, and milk. Pour over tater tot mixture.
Bake uncovered 50-60 minutes.
