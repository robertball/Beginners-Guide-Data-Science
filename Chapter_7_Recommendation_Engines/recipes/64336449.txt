Title:
Marie's Tropical Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Ripe Avocados
1 Lime, juiced
½ Jalapeno, seeded and minced
½ cup Pineapple, finely diced
½ cup Mango, finely diced
½ red Onion, finely diced
1 tsp Shredded Coconut
3-4 Mint Leaves, finely chopped

Directions:
Using a potato masher, mash the avocados.
Mix in lime juice, pineapple, mango, red onion, jalapeño, coconut and mint leaves.
Serve immediately or store in the refrigerator for up to three days.

Submitter: 
fordmarie
Description: 
After all, avocados are a fruit, so when you pair some awesome fruit and a gentle herb like mint, oh my goodness.. so delicious!
