Title:
Hot Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
2 garlic cloves chopped
1 small onion halved quartered and sliced
4 cups green cabbage shredded
1 medium carrot peeled cut into matchsticks
1 small red pepper cut into matchsticks
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

Directions:
In skillet or wok heat oil and crushed red pepper. Add onions and garlic cook 5 minutes on low. Stir in cabbage, carrot and red pepper increase heatnto medium saute until cabbage is wilted about 10-12 minutes. Toss with lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter:

Description: 
I use this as a vegetable side dish with Asian inspired main dishes. Also great mixed in with rice.
