Title:
Penne With Zucchini And Bacon(Penne Zucchini E Pancetta) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large zucchini
1 large onion
3/4 cup pancetta or bacon chopped
1 tbsp. olive oil
200 ml of heavy cream
1/2 of a bouillon cube
125 ml of water
250 g penne
grated parmesan cheese to serve

Directions:
Cut the zucchini into small cubes.
Chop the onion and saute in a large skillet with the bacon or pancetta  and the oil until the onion is soft and the bacon cooked.
Add the cubed zucchini and the bouillon cube
Brown slightly and then add the water
Cover the skillet and cook  until the zucchini is soft and the water absorbed
Lastly add the cream and mix well. Remove from the heat
Meanwhile  cook the pasta in plenty of salted boiling  water
As soon as the Penne is cooked drain and add to the skillet.
Mix well and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
NOTES::: When i make this i do not use heavy cream.
I use So Delicious Coconut Milk. Due to Dairy Intolerance.
