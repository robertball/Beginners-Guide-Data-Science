Title:
Thai Bananas in Coconut Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large bananas
1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Pinch of salt
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
toasted coconut, for garnish

Directions:
Peel bananas and cut into diagonal pieces, about 2‚Äù each.
In a medium saucepan over high heat, whisk together the Coconut Milk, sugar, salt and nutmeg.
Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Reduce heat and add bananas.
Simmer for two minutes, until bananas are just heated through.
Serve in bowls,  with toasted coconut sprinkled on top.
