Title:
Chai Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/122224.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces vodka
2 fluid ounces cold chai tea

Directions:
Pour the vodka and chai into a cocktail shaker over ice. Cover, and shake until the outside of the shaker has frosted. Strain into a chilled martini glass to serve.

Submitter: 
Ms. Robinson
Description: 
Shake up these aromatic cocktails for an exotic treat at an Indian-themed party, or just relax with one (or two) on a chilly evening.
