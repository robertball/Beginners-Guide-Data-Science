Title:
Barbie's Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 (7 ounce) cans white tuna, drained and flaked
1 1/3 c mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/4 c + 1 T Parmesan cheese
2/3 sweet pickle relish
1 1/4 teaspoon dried minced onion flakes
1 1/4 teaspoon curry powder
1/4 c + 1 T dried parsley
1 T + 2 t dried dill weed
5 pinches garlic powder
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
In a medium bowl, stir together the tuna, mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, and onion flakes. Season with curry powder, parsley, dill and garlic powder. Sprinkle with salt & pepper. Mix well and refrigerate overnight.
