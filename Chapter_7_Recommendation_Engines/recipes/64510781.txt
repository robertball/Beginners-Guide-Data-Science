Title:
Meatless Monday Pasta Fiesta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 package whole wheat bows
1/2tbsp coconut oil
1 cup diced red pepper
1 cup frozen corn
1 tbsp ground cumin
1 tbsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp salt and pepper
3 cups diced tomato
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
2 tbsp cider vinegar
1 tbsp minced garlic
2 green onions, chopped

Directions:
Cook bows according to package directions
Meanwhile, heat coconut oil in a large nonstick skillet. Add red pepper, corn, cumin, oregano, salt and pepper; sauté for 3 minutes. Add tomato, black beans, garlic; sauté for 8 minutes or until hot and saucy.
Spoon sauce over hot cooked bows; garnish with onion.
