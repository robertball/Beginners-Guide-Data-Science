Title:
Poppyseed Kiwi-Apple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head curly leaf lettuce, chopped
1 lg red apple
5 md kiwifruit
poppy seeds
sesame seeds
grapeseed oil
rice vinegar

Directions:
Cut the apple into slices.  Cut the slices in half.
Peel and slice the kiwi.
Combine all ingredients, seasonings to taste, in a salad bowl and toss thoroughly, making sure that all the apple pieces get coated in vinegar.
