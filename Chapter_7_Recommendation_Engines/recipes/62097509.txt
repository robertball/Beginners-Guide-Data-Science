Title:
Melissa's Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 large yukon gold potatoes peeled and cubed
1 onion chopped
2 tablespoons of sour cream
2 cups of shredded sharp cheddar cheese
3 tablespoons of butter
1 tablespoon of oil
3 cloves of garlic
1 1/2 lbs of ground beef
1/2 lb crumbled sausage
3 tablespoons of flour
1 bay leaf
1 1/2 cups of beef broth
1 tablespoon ketchup
2 (11 oz) cans of whole corn
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to boil.  Add potatoes and cook until tender.  Drain and mash.  Mix in butter, 1/2 cup of cheese, sour cream, 2 tablespoons of onion and salt and pepper to taste.  Set aside.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. While potaotes are boiling, heat oil in a large skillet.  Add onion and garlic and cook until tender, but not brown.  Add ground beef and sausage and cook until brown.  Drain excess fat.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Stir in flour and cook for 1 minute.  Add broth, bay leaf and ketchup and cook for 5 minutes. Remove bay leaf.
Spread beef mixture on the bottom of a 9x15 pan.  Follow with a layer of corn and a layer of potatoes.  Sprinkle the remaining cheese on top.
Cook for 20 minutes or until cheese is melted.  For golden brown cheese, broil for the last 2-3 minutes.

Submitter:

What's better than a hearty home cooked meal that the whole family can enjoy?  This cassarole is sure to please those who love winter comfort foods.
