Title:
Barbecue Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz thick-sliced bacon, diced
1 cup diced white onion
1/2 cup seeded and diced Anaheim chiles
3 large cloves garlic, minced
1 15oz can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
1 15oz can navy beans, drained and rinsed
1 15oz can red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 cup ketcup
1 cup barbecue sauce
1/3 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
2 tbsp spicy brown mustard
1 tsp hot sauce, such as Tobasco
salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a Dutch oven, cook bacon until crisp.  Transfer to a paper towel lined plate and discard all but 2 tablespoons of the drippings.
Add onion, chiles, and garlic to the drippings and cook over medium heat until softened, about 5 minutes.
Add beans, ketchup, BBQ sauce, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, and hot sauce.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat to medium low, and cook until thickened, approximately 25 minutes.
Season beans with salt and pepper and stir in bacon.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
A staple of barbecues everywhere, these beans are flavorful and good either hot or cold.
