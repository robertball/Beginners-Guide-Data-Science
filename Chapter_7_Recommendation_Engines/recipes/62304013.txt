Title:
Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
2 (16 oz) cans chili beans (do not drain)
1 (16 oz) can black beans (drained and rinsed)
1 (16 oz) can tomato sauce
1 (6 oz can) tomato paste
1 (15 ounce) can petite diced tomatoes
1 can diced green chilies
1 can Chicken broth
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning
3-4 whole skinless, boneless chicken breasts
Toppings:
shredded Cheddar cheese (optional)
sour cream (optional)
crushed tortilla chips (optional)

Directions:
Place all canned ingredients and seasoning in crock pot and stir to blend. Lay raw chicken breasts on top of the mixture, pressing down slightly until just covered by the other ingredients. Set slow cooker for low heat, cover, and cook for approx 5 hours. Remove chicken breasts from the pot, and allow to cool long enough to be handled, shred chicken. Stir the shredded chicken back into the soup, and continue cooking for approx 1-2 hours or till ready to serve. Serve topped with shredded Cheddar cheese, a dollop of sour cream, and crushed tortilla chips (optional), if desired.

Submitter: 
Ruth Jones
Description: 
A hearty combination of beans, tomatoes, and taco seasonings, slow cooked with shredded chicken. Deliciously finished, if you like, with Cheddar cheese, sour cream, and crushed tortilla chips.Modified from: Slow Cooker Chicken Taco Soup
