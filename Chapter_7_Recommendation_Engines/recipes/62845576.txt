Title:
Broccoli Raisin Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups fresh broccoli florets
3/4 cup golden raisins
1 small chopped red onion
1/2 cup Miracle Whip
1 Tablespoon vinegar
2 teaspoons sugar
3 cooked and crumbled bacon strips

Directions:
Toss first 3 ingredients together.
Mix Miracle Whip, vinegar and sugar together and toss with first 3 ingredients.
Top salad with the coooked and crumbled bacon strips.
