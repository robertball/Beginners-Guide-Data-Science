Title:
Circus Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. butter
22 Large marshmallows
2 1/2 C. crispy rice cereal (like Rice Krispies)
1 capful red food coloring
2 tsp. multicolored nonpareils

Directions:
Spray a 9x9 brownie pan with non-stick cooking spray.
In a large saucepan over medium low heat, melt the butter.
Add the marshmallows and stir until melted.
Stir in the food coloring
Add half the nonpareils and the cereal, stirring until cereal is coated.
Press into pan.  Top with remaining nonpareils.

Submitter: 
KathCook
Description: 
Rice Krispie treats that taste just like those pink & white circus animal cookies!  Great for kids' playdates or a circus-themed party.
