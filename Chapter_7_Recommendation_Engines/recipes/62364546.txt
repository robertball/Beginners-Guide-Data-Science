Title:
Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 lbs pound cubed chuck roast
2 (10.75 ounce) can's  condensed golden mushroom soup
1 sm chopped onion
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup beef broth
2 ounces cream cheese
2 ounces sour cream
3 gloves garlic minced
4 to 6 ounces sauteed mushrooms
1 tbs heaping corn starch

Directions:
In a slow cooker, combine the meat, soup, onion,garlic, Worcestershire sauce and broth.
Cook on Low setting for 8 hours, or on High setting for about 5 hours. 1/2 hour before serving stir in cream cheese,sour cream and mushrooms, mix corn starch with some juice from the pot and stir in.

Submitter: 
Ernie
Description: 
Stew beef, condensed golden mushroom soup chopped onion and Worcestershire sauce are combined and slow cooked. Cream cheese is stirred in just before serving.Modified from: Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff I
