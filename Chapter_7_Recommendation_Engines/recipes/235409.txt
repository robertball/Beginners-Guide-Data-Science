Title:
Hannah's Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5011264.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (8 ounce) package Neufchatel cheese
2 tablespoons dried parsley
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 tablespoon minced onion
1/4 teaspoon liquid smoke flavoring
1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup chopped hazelnuts

Directions:
Mix Cheddar cheese, Neufchatel cheese, parsley, lemon juice, onion, liquid smoke flavoring, Worcestershire sauce, and salt together in a bowl; shape into a ball.
Spread hazelnuts onto a plate. Roll cheese ball in hazelnuts to coat.

Submitter: 
hmcneil
Description: 
Great for holiday gatherings. Not like any other cheese ball. Serve with your favorite crackers.
