Title:
Perfect Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs lean ground beef
1/2 cup Italian seasoned breadcrumbs
1 cup portabello mushrooms, diced
2 eggs
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 jalapeno, diced
2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp Kosher salt
1 branch fresh rosemary, chopped
pepper, to taste
1 cup crushed tomatoes

Directions:
Combine all ingredients together except for crushed tomatoes. Once all ingredients are mixed, form into a loaf and put into 5x9 loaf pan.
Spread crushed tomatoes on top of loaf until it covers the entire area.
Bake for 45 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Cara
Description: 
I've been making meatloaf for years, but I think I've finally found the perfect combination of ingredients that keeps it juicy and delicious. Use grass-fed ground beef, if available, for best results. Especially delicious when served over a bed of fresh mashed potatoes.
