Title:
Mediterranean Chickpea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15-oz can chickpeas, rinsed and drained or 2 cups cooked chickpeas (from dried)
1/3 cup chopped or quartered Kalamata olives
1/4 cup chopped almonds
2 ounces fata cheese, crumbled
1/2 cup chopped fresh flat-leaf (Italian) parsley
2 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
2 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
1 small garlic clove, minced or pressed
Sea salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Place chickpeas, olives, almonds, feta and parsley in a medium bowl.  Toss gently to combine.  Add lemon juice, olive oil and garlic to bowl, toss again to coat.  Taste and add salt and pepper as needed.  Can be served chilled or at room temperature.

Submitter: 
Lorraine Friberg
Description: 
This salad has many foods that are central to the Mediterranean diet and can be served on its own or as a side dish.
