Title:
Rice Cooker Toddler Overnight Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 nuts
3/4 oats
1/4 museli
2 tbs each wheat germ & flax
2 milk kefir
1 water kefir
1 T coconut oil
1/3 dried fruit (rasins, curraints, dates, etc.)

Directions:
Add all ingredients into the rice cooker
Stir a little to get all of the ingredients mixed
Let soak for at least 4 hours (4+ hour timer)
Cook on porridge setting
Once cooked, add frozen fruit, oatmeal, and tofu to a bowl and mix to combine. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Mindy
Description: 
I make this for my girls every morning. It's super easy and super fast!
