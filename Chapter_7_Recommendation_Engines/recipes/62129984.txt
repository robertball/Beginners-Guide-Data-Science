Title:
Garlic Cheese Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf french bread
1/2 c butter
1 c mayonnaise
1 c shredded jack cheese
1 c shredded asiago cheese
2 cloves garlic, pureed
3/4 c chopped green onions

Directions:
Preheat over to 350 degrees.
Split french bread into half horizontally.
Mix butter, cheeses, mayo, green onion, and garlic in a bowl.
Spread the cut side of the bread with mixture.
Bake at 350 for 7 minutes, then place under broiler for 2-3 minutes longer.
Cut into desired size slices and serve.
