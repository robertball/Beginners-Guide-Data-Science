Title:
Marshmallow Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Brownies:
1 cup softened butter
2 cup sugar
1/3 cup cocoa
4 eggs
1 1/2 cups flour
2 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups chopped nuts (optional)
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 lb mini marshmallows
Frosting:
1 2/3 cups powdered sugar
1/2 cup butter
1/3 cup cocoa
1/3 cup evaporated milk

Directions:
Mix butter, cocoa, and sugar together in a large bowl.
Stir in 4 eggs.
Add flour, vanilla,  salt, chocolate chips, and if desired, add nuts.
Stir mixture for 3 minutes or until smooth.
Pour mixture into a greased 1 inch deep baking sheet. (cookie sheet)
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
While the brownies are baking, mix the powdered sugar, butter, cocoa, and evaporated milk in a small bowl to make frosting. Mix for 3 minutes to make sure frosting is well mixed and smooth.
When brownies are done baking, take them out of the oven.
Cover the brownies (while hot) with marshmallows.
Bake with marshmallows for an additional 3 minutes.
Take out of oven, wait until marshmallows are cool, then spread the frosting over marshmallows.
Let the brownies cool for atleast 15 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
