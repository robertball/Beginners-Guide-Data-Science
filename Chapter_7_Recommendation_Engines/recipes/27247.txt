Title:
Pesto Vinaigrette Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983409.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup BUITONI® Refrigerated Pesto with Basil
3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
mixed salad greens

Directions:
COMBINE pesto, vinegar and mustard in small bowl; whisk until smooth.  Serve over salad greens.

Submitter: 
Buitoni®
Description: 
Pesto makes a flavorful base for this vinaigrette made with balsamic vinegar and Dijon mustard. Toss with your favorite salad greens, and you have a perfect accompaniment for any Italian-Style meal.
