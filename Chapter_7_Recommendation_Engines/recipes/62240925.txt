Title:
Cappuccino Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3&1/2 quarts Water
2&1/3 cups Sugar
3/4 cup Instant Coffee Granules
1/2 cup Chocolate Syrup
1 gallon Vanilla Ice Cream
1 pint Coffee Ice Cream

Directions:
Bring water to boil in large pot.
Remove from heat and stir in sugar, coffee and chocolate syrup until sugar is dissolved.
Cool to room temperature.
Transfer to four half-gallon containers.
Cover and refrigerate 4 hours or overnight.
Just before serving, pour coffee mixture into a punch bowl.
Add scoops of vanilla and coffee ice cream
Stir until partially melted.
