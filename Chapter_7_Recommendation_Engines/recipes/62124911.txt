Title:
Striped Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
1/4 cup sugar
1/3 cup melted butter
1st layer:
1 8oz pkg softened cream cheese
1/4 cup sugar
2 T milk
4 oz cool whip
2nd layer:
2 pkg instant chocolate pudding (4 serving size)
3 1/2 cup cold milk

Directions:
Crust:
Mix ingredients well and press into bottom of 9 x 13 dish
1st layer:
Beat cream cheese, sugar and milk together until smooth
Fold in cool whip
Spread over crust
2nd layer:
Mix pudding mix and cold milk until smooth
Pour over cream cheese layer
Chill several hours
Cover top with cool whip before serving

Submitter: 
ChefJoe
Description: 
A cool dessert, great for summer pot lucks and get togethers.
