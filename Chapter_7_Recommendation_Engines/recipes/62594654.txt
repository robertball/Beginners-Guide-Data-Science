Title:
Chicken Salad with Jalepeno Smoked Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups shredded or diced cooked chicken
2 ounces pre-cooked jalapeno smoked sausage
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup mozzarella cheese, (optional)
1 tablespoon ranch dressing mix
1 tablespoon lemon pepper seasoning
6 to 8 cherry tomatoes, chopped into halves
1 tablespoon dried parsley flakes
1 tablespoon finely minced onion
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1 dash soy sauce
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine chicken and sausage in a large bowl, set aside. In another bowl, combined dry ingredients then whisk together with remaining ingredients; add to salad and toss to coat. Serve warm or chill until serving.

Submitter: 
Renee Parker
Description: 
This is our pick when the evening calls for something easy and a little bolder than traditional chicken salad.Modified from: Chicken Salad
