Title:
Gramps' Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRUST:
9-10 graham crackers
1/4 cup melted butter
BOTTOM LAYER:
16 oz. cream cheese (room temperature)
2 eggs
1/2 cup sugar
2 tsp. cinnamon
TOP LAYER:
1 pint sour cream
4 Tbsp. sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1 pint berries (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
CRUST:  Put the graham crackers in a storage bag, roll with a rolling pin to crush crackers into crumbs.  Combine crumbs with melted butter.  Press mixture into bottom of pan (8" or 9", square or springform).
BOTTOM LAYER:  Mix room temperature cream cheese, eggs and 1/2 cup sugar until creamy.  Spread over graham cracker crumb crust.  Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.  Sprinkle cinnamon on top.  Let it cool for 10 minutes.
TOP LAYER:  Mix sour cream, 4 Tbsp sugar and vanilla until creamy.  Spread mixture over bottom layer. Bake at 350 for 5 minutes.  Cool.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.
Serve plain or with berries if desired.

Submitter: 
scarlett
Description: 
The must have dessert for my husband's family.  We usually serve it with strawberries or raspberries.  It's great plain too!
