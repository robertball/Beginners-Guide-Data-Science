Title:
Chocolate Covered Pretzels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 20-oz bag chocolate chips
1 bag mini pretzles

Directions:
Melt chocolate in double boiler over medium heat on stovetop
Quickly dip pretzels in chocolate. Allow excess to run off
Place dipped pretzels on waxed paper lined cookie sheets
Sprinkle with chopped nuts, colored sugars or other toppings (coconut, sprinkles, etcâ€¦)
Allow to harden. You may need to refrigerate for a while
Once hardened, remove from sheets and store in a cool place, with waxed paper between layers.
