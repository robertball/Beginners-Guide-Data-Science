Title:
Spiked Hot Apple Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gal. Apple Cider
750 ml Spiced Rum
Whole Cloves
1 Orange Rind
3 Cinnamon Sticks
Raisins

Directions:
Heat Apple Cider in a large pot over medium heat.
Add a handful of Cloves and a handful of Raisins.
Cut Rind into 1 inch chunks and halve cinnamon sticks. Add to the Apple Cider.
Let sit for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add Spiced Rum and stir.
Strain into your desired punch bowl or container.
Serve hot.
