Title:
Smoked Salmon Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/566180.jpg
Ingredients:
3 oz Cream Cheese, softened
3 Tbsp Mayonnaise (Hellman's preferred)
3 Tbsp Smoked Salmon, diced
1/3 Cup Cherry Tomatoes, diced
1 Tbsp Chives, finely chopped
2 Tbsp Celery, finely chopped
Salt & Fresh Ground Pepper to taste
2 - 10" Whole Wheat Tortillas

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except tortillas and mix well.
Divide mixture evenly among the 2 tortillas.
Roll up tortillas and wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
Slice into 1/2 inch slices and serve.

Submitter: 
Gitano
Description: 
I created this for the Jan 2011 GangUp Challenge - Appetizer Edition :) Was looking for something different to do with smoked salmon (and the limited number of ingredients issued in the challenge) then after 3 days of serious thinking ~ this popped into my head!
