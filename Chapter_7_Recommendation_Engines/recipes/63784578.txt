Title:
Vitamix: Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 banana, peeled
1 medium orange, peeled with pith intact
1 inch thick slice pineapple with core, peeled
1.5 cups packed fresh spinach
1/8 teaspoon grated lime zest, or thin slice of lime with peel (~1 inch diameter)
2 tablespoons agave nectar
1 cup ice cubes

Directions:
Place all ingredients in the Vitamix in the order listed and secure lid
Select Variable 1
Turn machine on and slowly increase speed to Variable 10, then to High
Blend for 30 seconds or until desired consistency
