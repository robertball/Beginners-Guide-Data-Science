Title:
Sweet & Spicy Venison Jerky Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c worchestershire sauce
1/4 c teriyaki sauce
1/4 c soy sauce
1 tbsp. liquid smoke
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp hot pepper sauce
1 tbsp garlic salt
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp black pepper
1/2 c brown sugar
1 lb venison strips

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in sealable container. Seal and place in refirigerator, tossing occasionally for 24 hours. Place on racks of dehydrator and dry at highest setting for atleast 4 hours. You may also place it in a 160 degree oven for 6 to 8 hours. Store in airtight container, or sealable plastic bag.
