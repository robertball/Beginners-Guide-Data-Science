Title:
Peanut Brittle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c sugar
1 c light corn syrup
1/2 c hot water
1 lb raw peanuts
1 T margarine
2 T baking soda
1 t vanilla
1/2 T salt

Directions:
In a heavy 3 quart saucepan, combine sugar, corn syrup, and water.  Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until mixture reaches 240'.  Stir in raw peanuts, cook, stirring constantly,until mixture reaches 310'.  Remove from heat, quickly and baking soda, vanilla, margarine, and salt, stirring well.  Pour mixture into 2 buttered cookie sheets.  Let stand until cook.  Break apart and store in airtight containers.
