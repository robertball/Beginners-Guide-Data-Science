Title:
Parmesan Pull-Apart Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3T margarine, melted
1/2 c Parmesan cheese (plus Italian seasoning if desired)
10 oz refrigerated biscuits

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Spray loaf pan.
Quarter each biscuit.
Dip in melted margarine, then cheese.
Place pieces in loaf pan.
Bake 30-35 minutes.
