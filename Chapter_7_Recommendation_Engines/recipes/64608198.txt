Title:
Fall Party Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 quarts popped Jolly Time Pop Corn
2 cups slim pretzel sticks
2 cups cheese curls
1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt
1/4 cup butter
1 Tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
I cup peanuts

Directions:
In a shallow baking pan, mix popcorn, pretzel sticks, and cheese curls.
Melt butter and stir in  seasonings .
Pour over dry ingredients and mix well.
Bake at 250 degrees for about 45 minutes, stirring several times.
Pour mix onto cookie sheets lined with paper towels to cool.

Submitter: 
Paula S.
Description: 
Made with a popcorn base, this is a tasty and economical recipe.
