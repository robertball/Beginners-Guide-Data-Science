Title:
Awesome Korean Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds thinly sliced Scotch fillet (chuck eye steaks)
1/2 cup soy sauce
3 T white sugar
2 T brown sugar
2 1/2 tablespoons sesame seeds
2 tablespoons sesame oil
3 green onion, thinly sliced
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1 tsp. ginger, crushed
5 T Korean rice wine or Japanese sweet rice wine
*** shredded Korean Pear for tenderizing or 1/8 tsp. mashed kiwi ****

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir together the soy sauce, sugar, sesame seeds, green onions, garlic, ginger and rice wine. Add the meat, and stir to coat. Cover and refrigerate for 12 to 24 hours. (Add sesame oil just before frying meat)
Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Fry the meat for 5 to 10 minutes, or until no longer pink. Serve with rice.
