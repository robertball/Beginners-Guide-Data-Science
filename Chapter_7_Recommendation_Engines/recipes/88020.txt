Title:
Fizzy Gelatin Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/28451.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup unsweetened applesauce
1 (3 ounce) package lime flavored Jell-OÂ® mix
1 cup ginger ale

Directions:
Pour applesauce into a small saucepan, and bring to a boil. Stir in gelatin until dissolved, then set aside to cool for about 1 hour. When cooled, gradually whisk in the ginger ale. Refrigerate until set, about 4 hours.
