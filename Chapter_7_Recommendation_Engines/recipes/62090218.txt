Title:
Kiki's Baked Sweet Onion & Peppered Bacon Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. peppered bacon, chopped
2 cups Swiss cheese, finely shredded
2 cups mayonnaise
2 cups Texas Sweet Onion (or other sweet onion like Vidalia), finely chopped
4 Tablespoons. shredded fresh Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Brown bacon in skillet until crispy, set aside to drain on papertowel.
Combine Swiss cheese, onion and mayonnaise in medium bowl, stirring well. Fold in bacon.
Spoon mixture into a lightly greased 1-qt. baking dish. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
Bake at 325 degrees for 40-45 minutes. Serve warm with an assortment of crackers and veggies.

Submitter: 
KIKI810
Description: 
I love cheese, bacon and onions and have served this appetizer for years at gatherings and parties.  It never fails to get many requests for the recipe.  I hope your guests enjoy it as much as mine do.
