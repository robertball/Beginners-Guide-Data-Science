Title:
Fabulous French Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups milk, 2% milk fat or higher
1 cup sugar
2 cups cold heavy cream
1 egg
3 egg yolks
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
a pinch of salt

Directions:
1. In a saucepan, heat the milk, sugar, and salt until mixture reaches a simmer. Then remove from heat immediately.
2. Make an ice bath by placing a 2 quart bowl into a larger bowl that is partially filled with ice and water. Pour cold heavy cream into smaller bowl.
3. In a separate bowl, beat the egg and egg yolks
4. Gradually pour one-third of the warm milk mixture into the eggs, constantly whisking while pouring.
5. Pour egg mixture into pan with the rest of the milk mixture and cook on medium heat, stirring constantly.
6. When custard mixture reaches 170 to 175 degrees F and coats the back of a spoon, remove from heat.
7. Pour custard into heavy cream and stir until cooled. Add vanilla extract.
8. Place the ice cream mixture in the refrigerator and when it is cool enough for condensation not to form on the lid, cover and chill thoroughly in the refrigerator, preferably overnight.
9. Freeze according to manufacturerâ€™s instructions. Enjoy!

Description: 
The ingredients and method used in preparing this ice cream make it smooth, creamy, and delicious; a real treat for the summer or anytime. More vanilla extract, or vanilla bean can be used.
