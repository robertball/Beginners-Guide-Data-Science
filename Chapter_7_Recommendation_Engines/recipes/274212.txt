Title:
Tuscan Lemonade Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7104250.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed
6 fluid ounces lemon-flavored vodka
3 fluid ounces limoncello liqueur
2 lemons, thinly sliced
1 (32 fluid ounce) bottle lemon-flavored sparkling water
ice

Directions:
Mix lemonade concentrate, vodka, limoncello, and sliced lemons together in a big pitcher. Chill until ready to serve. Add sparkling water to pitcher and serve over ice.
