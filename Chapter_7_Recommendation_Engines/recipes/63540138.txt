Title:
Cheesy Hash Browns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/967012.jpg
Ingredients:
36oz Hash Browns
16oz Sour Cream
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
1 cup shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 medium Onion minced
1 tbsp Salt
1 tsp Pepper

Directions:
combine all ingredients in 9x13 baking dish. Bake on 350 degrees for 40 minutes with aluminum foil, then 10 minutes without foil. Should be brown and bubbly.
