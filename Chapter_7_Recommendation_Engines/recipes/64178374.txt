Title:
Vegan Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 prepared pie shell or make your own.
1 small  pumpkin
4 tablespoons chia seeds
1/4 cup almond milk
1/2 cup vegan yogurt
1/3 - 1/2 cup sugar sucanat
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons corn starch
1/2 cup ricotta cheese ( optional)
1 egg (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven 375 degrees fahrenheit.
Cut the pumpkin, remove seeds
Steam the pumpkin.
Scoop out pumpkin pulp.
Combine chia seeds and pumpkin pulp, let sit for 10 minutes or more.
Blend in a blender chia seeds, yogurt, pumpkin pulp, sucanat, ginger, nutmeg, salt, cloves, corn starch.
Place in a prepared pie shell.
Bake for 35 minutes or until done.
