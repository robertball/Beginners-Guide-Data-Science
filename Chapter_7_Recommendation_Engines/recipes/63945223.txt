Title:
Indian Style Chicken & Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-6 cloves of garlic, peeled
-2 serrano chile, stemmed
-2 inch piece of ginger, peeled and thinly sliced
-1/4 cup of shed or clarified butter
-2lb of skinless drumsticks (or mix of chicken breast pieces and drumsticks
- salt and pepper for taste
-1 large white onion
-1 tablespoon dired fenugreek leaves
-2 teaspoons ground coriander
-1 teaspoon garam masala
-1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
-3 plum tomatoes, grated
-3/4 cup of milk
-1/2 cup of heavy cream
-1/3 cup finely chopped cilantro

Directions:
Puree garlic, chili, ginger, and 3 tablespoons water until smooth. Heat ghee in a 6-qt saucepan over medium high heat. Season chicken with desired amount of salt and pepper. Add chicken to the ghee saucepan, cook, flipping once, until browned, 5-7 minutes total. Transfer chicken to plate. Add garlic puree; cook, unit golden, 2-3 minutes Add onions; cook until golden 5-7 minutes. Add fenugreek leaves, coriander garam masala, and turmeric; cook 1 minute. Add tomatoes; cook until slightly caramelized, 4-6 minutes. Add chicken, milk, and cream; bring to a  boil. Reduce heat to medium and cover; cook until chicken is tender, 15-20 minutes. Transfer chicken to a serving platter. Cook sauce until the amount of liquid is slightly reduced, 5-7 more minutes. Stir in cilantro; pour of chicken.
Serve with hot naan or Basmiti rice
