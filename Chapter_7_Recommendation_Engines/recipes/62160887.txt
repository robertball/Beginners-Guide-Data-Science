Title:
Kentucky Grilled Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium head cabbage, rinsed, cored and sliced in 6 wedges
salt and pepper to taste
3 teaspoons sugar, optional
6 Tablespoons butter or margarine
1 cup total of the following add ins: onions, crumbled cooked bacon, chopped garlic, and/or bell pepper, optional

Directions:
Place each wedge on a sheet of double lined foil.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Add 1/2 about Tablespoon sugar to each wedge if needed.
Drizzle each wedge with 1 Tablespoon melted butter.
Add additional add ins if you wish.
Wrap up the cabbage with foil.
Place on a medium heated grill.
Grill or roast for 30 minutes, or until tender, turning after 1st 15 minutes.
Note: this can also be made in the oven. Bake at 350F for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
JOYCE
Description: 
Cabbage is a nice side to almost any grilled meat, and goes well with grilled pork, and grilled chicken. Grill it as your meat is grilling. Even non-cabbage eaters will enjoy!
