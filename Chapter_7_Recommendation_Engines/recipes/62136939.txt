Title:
Sausage Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf of frozen bread dough (do not let rise, thaw in fridge wrapped in plastic wrap)
1 lb. sage sausage
1/2 c. finely chopped onion
1/3 c. dried parsley
1/3 c. kraft-style parmesan cheese
1 egg
1 c. (approx.) powdered sugar

Directions:
Cook sausage and onion together in pan.  Crumble with a spatula.  Cool and set aside.
Lightly beat egg in a bowl, add cheese and parsely. Set aside.
Roll thawed dough on a surfaced sprinkled with the powdered sugar (instead of using flour)  Roll into a rectangle shape to about 1/4" thickness.
Combine egg mixture into the sausage mixture, and place in a neat row down the middle of the rolled out dough.
Work the dough around the sausage, stromboli style, until you have one straight loaf.  Form a circle with the stuffed loaf and seal ends.Place onto a cookie sheet, and bake in a 350 oven for about 35 minutes, until dough is golden brown.
Let cool 10 minutes before slicing.

Submitter: 
Michaels
Description: 
Great for any pot-luck function or brunch, sure to be the first item gone!  Very good as a left-over reheated.
