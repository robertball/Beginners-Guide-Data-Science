Title:
Asparagus Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp butter or margarine
3 tbsp flour
1/2 tsp mustard powder
1 10oz can mushroom soup
1 small can water chestnuts, chopped
5 hard cooked eggs, sliced
2 cans cut-up asparagus
1 can onion Rings, chopped up
1 cup grated cheddar cheese

Directions:
Melt butter in pan, stir in flour.  Add mustard, soup, chestnuts. Heat.
Butter casserole, cover with half the egg slices, 1/2 the asparagus, and half the soup mixture.  Repeat layers.  Sprinkle top with onion rings, then cheese.
Bake 350f. - 30 minutes, or until bubbling.

Submitter: 
Jan Mowbray
Description: 
An easy recipe,with a nice texture, tasty, and quick to assemble. Contains hard cooked eggs, chopped onion rings and cheese.
