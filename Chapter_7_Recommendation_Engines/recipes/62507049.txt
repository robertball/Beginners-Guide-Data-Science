Title:
Veggie Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large carrot
1 small zuccini
2 large mushrooms
2 cups fresh spinach
3 green onions
½ cup ricotta cheese
½ cup shredded mozzerella
½ cup shredded cheddar
Stuffing mix
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 cup crushed ritz crackers
4 eggs
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 tablespoon sea salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In large mixing bowl grate carrot and zuccini. Finely chop mushrooms, spinach, and onion- add to bowl and mix together. Fold in ricotta, mozzerella and cheddar cheeses.  Combine stuffing mix, flour and ritz crackers and add to mixture. Add eggs and season with chili powder and sea salt.  Mixture should be the consistancy of cookie dough.  Spoon out 1 ½ inch balls on to greased cookie sheet.  Bake for aproximatly 10 minutes.  Cool on rack, then serve with a dollop of blue cheese dressing.  (Bites also freeze nicely).

Submitter: 
Crystal Swenson
Description: 
This is a savory appetizer I created that brings out the natural goodness of fresh vegetables. It is also a great lite alternative to quiche.
