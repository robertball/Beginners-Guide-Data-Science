Title:
Fruit Punch Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 (.75 fluid ounce) jigger Amaretto
1/2 (.75 fluid ounce) jigger Southern Comfort
1/2 (.75 fluid ounce) jigger Yukon Jack
2 (3 fluid ounces) jiggers Orange Juice
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger Pineapple Juice
splash of grenadine

Directions:
Pour all ingredients into a shaker, shake well, pour into glass over ice.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Kristi Goulette Lozensky
Description: 
A sweet and refreshing adult drink that tastes like a fruit punch. But watch out...after a few they sneak up on you!
