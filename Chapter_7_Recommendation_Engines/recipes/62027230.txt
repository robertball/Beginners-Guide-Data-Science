Title:
Penne with Chicken and Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T butter
2 T olive oil
4 boneless chicken breast halves, skinned & cut into 1/4" strips
2 cloves garlic, minced
salt & pepper
1 1/4 C heavy cream
1/4 c prepared pesto sauce
3T grated Parmesan
1 lb. Penne or ziti pasta, cooked

Directions:
Saute chicken & garlic in butter and oil over moderate heat until chicken is almost cooked.  Reduce heat.  Add salt, pepper, cream, pesto and Parmesan.  Cook until chicken is cooked through, about 2 minutes.  Mix in penne pasta.

Submitter: 
MARYSTEVE
Description: 
I sometimes substitute chicken broth for half the heavy cream called for in the recipe to reduce calories.  I also use additional Parmesan.
