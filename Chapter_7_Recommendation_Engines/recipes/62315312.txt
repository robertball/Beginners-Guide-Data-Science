Title:
Joe's Famous Michelada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Large chilled mug or schooner, salted rim
Ice
1/4 teas. Salt
1 Lemon
Tabasco (to taste)
1/2 teas. Soy Sauce
Clamato Juice
1 12 oz Beer of your choice

Directions:
Fill a chilled salt-rimmed mug or schooner with ice.
Add salt, juice of 1 whole lemon, a couple dashes of tabasco (to taste), and 1/2 teas soy sauce.
Fill glass about 3/4 of the way with Clamato Juice
Add your choice of beer to fill to the top. Stir & serve. You can keep adding more beer to the same glass as you drink.

Submitter: 
PoodleMom
Description: 
This is my husband's recipe for a Michelada, a Mexican beer cocktail. He sampled many of them from almost all the Mexican Restaurants here in town...and came up with his own way of making them.  Now he doesn't even bother ordering them out anymore.
