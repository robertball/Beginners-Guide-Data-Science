Title:
Warm Italiano Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/894503.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. cream cheese
1/4 cup pesto
1 plum tomato (or small grape tomatoes), chopped
1/4 cup shredded mozza or Italian cheese blend
French bread/baguette or Trisket/Ritz crackers.

Directions:
Spread base of 9 inch baking dish with cream cheese. Then add a layer of pesto. Cook, covered, at 350 for 15-20 minutes (or until bubbling).
Sprinkle pesto layer with chopped tomatoes, followed by shredded cheese.
Broil in oven until cheese has melted.
Serve with sliced French bread, baguette, or crackers

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
Delicious with French bread and pasta/lasagna.
