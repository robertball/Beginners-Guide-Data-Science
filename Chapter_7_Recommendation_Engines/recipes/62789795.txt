Title:
Almond-Apricot Crescent Ring Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crescent Ring:
1 8oz can Pillsbury crescent rolls - sheet, if available
4 T. butter, softened
4 T. brown sugar
1/2 c. chopped dried apricots
1/3 c. chopped almonds
Glaze:
1/2 c. powdered sugar
2 - 3 t. milk
1 T. butter

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees.  Spray a large cookie sheet with cooking spray.
Unroll dough on work surface, press into 12 x 8 inch rectangle.
In small bowl, mix butter, brown sugar, and apricots.  Spread to 1/2 inch of dough edges.  Sprinkle with almonds.
Starting with long side of rectangle, roll up; pinch edge to seal.  With serrated knife, cut into 12 slices.  Arrange slices on cookie sheet in a circle, overlapping slightly.
Bake 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.  Cool 5 minutes.  Remove from cookie sheet to cooling rack.
In a small bowl, mix powdered sugar, milk, and butter until smooth enough to drizzle.  Drizzle over crescent ring.
