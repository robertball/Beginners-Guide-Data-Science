Title:
Grilled New York Strip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/2 cup Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup minced garlic
1/4 cup A-1 steak seasoning
1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
4 (1/2 pound) New York strip steaks
MARINATE OVERNIGHT

Directions:
In a bowl, mix the olive oil, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, A-1steak seasoning, balsamic vinegar, basil, and Italian seasoning.
Pour into a large resealable plastic bag.
Pierce steaks on all sides with a fork, place in the bag and seal; toss to coat.
Marinate steaks a minimum of 2 hours in the refrigerator; preferably overnight.
Preheat grill for high heat.
Lightly oil the grill grate.
Discard marinade.
Place steaks on the grill, and cook 7 minutes on each side, or to desired doneness.
*90
