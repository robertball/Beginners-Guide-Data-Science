Title:
Carribbean!: Caribbean Cool Avocado Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 3 oz. package lime Jell-o
1/4 tsp. salt
1 cup boiling water
1 (8 3/4 oz.) can crushed pineapple, drained; reserve juice
2 Tbs. lime juice
1 medium avocado, peeled and diced
1- 3 oz. package cream cheese
1 carton cool-whip; 1 cup reserved
1- 9 inch graham cracker pie crust

Directions:
Dissolve gelatin and salt in boiling water.
Drain pineapple, combining the pineapple juice with the lime juice. Add cold water to juices to make 3/4 cup. Add to the gelatin.
Chill until very thick; but not fully set.
As gelatin sets, dice half of the avocado. With a fork, mash the remaining half until smooth. Blend cheese into mashed avocado until creamy. Then fold cheese mixture, diced avocado, and 1 cup cool whip into thickened gelatin. Spoon into pie crust. Chill until firm. Top with remaining cool whip; garnish with lime slices, and enjoy!

I came up with this recipe after a trip to the Jamaica.  I had a similiar dessert at our hotel restaurant and was floored at the wonderful, cool taste.  I love anything with avocados, and it you do too; you will Love this pie!!
