Title:
Healthier Marie's Easy Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/281265.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds chuck roast
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup reduced-sodium beef cooking stock
2 onions, chopped
3 carrots, chopped
2 lbs potatoes, unpeeled and cubed
1 stalk celery, chopped
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Directions:
Season the chuck roast with salt and pepper to taste. Brown on all sides in a large skillet over high heat.
Place in the slow cooker. Pour the broth into the skillet and deglaze. Simmer 2 minutes and pour broth into the slow cooker. Stir the onions into the slow cooker, cover and cook on low setting for 5 hours.
Add the carrots, potatoes and celery. Cover and cook on low setting for 3 an additional hours. (Optional: stir in some fresh chopped parsley!)
