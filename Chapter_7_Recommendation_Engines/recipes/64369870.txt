Title:
Oatmeal Berry Burst Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2173462.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking powder
1/2 tsp. Baking soda
1 1/4 C. yogurt
2 Eggs, beaten
1/2 C. Granulated sugar
1/2 C. Brown sugar
1 C. Quick oats
1 C. Frozen cranberries, thawed and sliced in half
1 C. Frozen blueberries, thawed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Grease a 12 cup muffin pan. Set aside
Blend flour, B. powder and B. soda together in a small bowl. Set aside
In a separate bowl beat eggs until light and frothy
On a cutting board chop cranberries in half. Set aside
Combine yogurt, sugar and beaten eggs together in a large bowl. Stir with a wire whisk until light and creamy
Add oats and berries to mixture. Stir all ingredients together until no lumps remain
Slowly stir in dry ingredients and mix until all the flour is incorporated into the muffin batter
Put small amounts of batter into each muffin cup filling 2/3 full and bake for 20 minutes until light brown on top
Allow to cool 5 minutes before removing from pan and place on a serving plate

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A muffin with a burst of fruit flavour from berries for a good breakfast treat or brunch
