Title:
Egg omelet burrito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbs. liquid egg white
1 large egg
1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 tbs. salsa
2 slices of deli meat
1/8 tsp. nutmeg
pinch of salt
pinch of pepper

Directions:
Lightly grease cooking pan and heat to medium-low.
Mix large egg and egg white with cheese. Add in nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
Pour the egg mixture on the pan and cook for about 3 minutes.Flip when edges look brown and crispy.
Take the egg omelet off the pan and lay it flat on a plate.
Next, place two slices of deli meat on egg. Spread the salsa over the meat.
Finally, roll the egg omelet into the shape of a burrito. Be careful to keep the salsa and deli meat from slipping out.

Submitter:

Description: 
A quick breakfast that is easy to make and extremely filing.
