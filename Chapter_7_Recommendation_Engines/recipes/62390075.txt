Title:
Fantastic Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. elbow macaroni
3 (7 ounce) can white tuna, drained and flaked
1 cup mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/3 cup Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup sweet pickle relish
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon curry powder
4 tablespoons dried parsley
4 teaspoon dried dill weed
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon salt

Directions:
Boil macaroni, drain.  Place in cold water until cool, drain.  In a large bowl, combine all ingredients.  Mix well.  Refigerate until ready to eat.

Submitter: 
Andrea Patton
Description: 
Curry and Parmesan cheese are the secret ingredients in this tuna salad recipe! I have never tasted another tuna salad like it.Modified from: Barbie's Tuna Salad
