Title:
The Breakfast Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3589675.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tbsp peanut butter
1 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
1 frozen peeled banana
1 cup coffee (cold)
3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
1 scoop chocolate whey protein powder

Directions:
1) Drop these ingredients in a blender
2) Crank it up until it's smooth
3) Pour it in a big cup
4) Go

Submitter: 
Will Lee
Description: 
I make this on weekday mornings because, like many people, I'm not out of bed until 20 minutes prior to running to my car.  It has a lot packed in there, so it should keep you going until lunch.
