Title:
Aunt Kathy's Chicken Tetrazzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C Sliced Mushrooms
8 T Butter
1/2 C Flour
2 t salt
1/2 t pepper
2 C buttered fresh bread crumbs
4 C Milk
1/2 C dry white wine
4 C julienne strips cooked chicken
1 C broken spaghetti, cooked
1/2 C grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Saute mushrooms in 2 T butter, remove from pan.
Melt remaining butter in same pan, add flour, salt, pepper, and cook, stirring constantly until boils 1 minute.
Stir in wine, chicken, cooked spaghetti and mushrooms.
Spoon into buttered 9 X 13 inch baking dish.
Sprinkle with cheese and bread crumbs.
Bake at 425 degrees for 15-20 minutes

Description: 
This recipe serves more than 8 if used as a side dish.  It was a favorite of mine and I make it for large gatherings and its always a hit.
