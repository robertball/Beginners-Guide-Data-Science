Title:
Singapore Peanut sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/748990.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
1/4 cup sweet shredded coconut,finely chopped
3 cloves garlic,minced
2 tsp.fresh ginger,peeled and minced
2 Tbsp.lime juice
2 Tbsp.soy sauce
4 tsp.sugar
2 tsp.chili powder
1/2 tsp.crushed red pepper flakes

Directions:
Combine all ingredients with 1/2 cup water and stir till well blended.Store in fridge for days.
