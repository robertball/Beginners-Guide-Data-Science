Title:
Super Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 to 1 cup Unsweetened vanilla almond milk.
Approx 1 tablespoon Honey (optional)
1 medium banana, peeled and cut into approx. 1" long pieces.
1 naval orange, peeled and segmented.
1 gala apple with peel, cored and cut into small pieces.
1/3 cup (approx) each:
Frozen Blueberries
Frozen Black Raspberries
Frozen Red Raspberries
Frozen Strawberries
Frozen Peaches

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into a blender in the order listed. Pulse several times, pausing for a few seconds between each. Then increase blending speed until a vortex forms. Regulate speed of blender to maintain the vortex for approximately 1 to 2 minutes or until desired consistency is reached. Immediately refrigerate any leftover.

Submitter: 
Kevin Barberie
Description: 
I fix this smoothie a couple times a week and have a glass with my breakfast and refrigerate the rest. Please keep in mind that this a whole fruit smoothie so there will be bits and pieces of seeds.
