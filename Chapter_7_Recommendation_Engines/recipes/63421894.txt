Title:
RAISIN PUDDING Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/924858.jpg
Ingredients:
1 CUP FLOUR
3/4 CUP WHITE SUGAR
2 TSP BAKING POWDER
PINCH OF SALT
1 CUP RAISINS
1/2 CUP MILK
1/2 CUP BROWN SUGAR
1 TBSP BUTTER
2 CUPS BOILING WATER

Directions:
SIFT TOGETHER FLOUR, SUGAR, BAKING POWDER AND SALT.
ADD IN RAISINS AND MILK; COMBINE AND PUT INTO A GREASED BACKING DISH.
MIX BROWN SUGAR, BUTTER AND BOILING WATER.
POUR OVER THE BATTER AND BAKE AT 350 DEGREES FOR 30-40 MINUTES UNTIL BROWN ON TOP.
