Title:
Shrimp Cocktail Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/785071.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg.cream cheese
2 Tbsp.sour cream
1 can small shimp
1 jar seafood sauce
[mild or spicy]
3/4cup grated marble cheese
2 chopped green onion

Directions:
Blend together the cream cheese and sour cream.Spread on bottom of pie plate.Rinse shrimp and dry well,add on top of cream cheese.Spread jar of seafood sauce on top.Sprinkle with cheese then green onions.Serve with ritz or other crackers.
