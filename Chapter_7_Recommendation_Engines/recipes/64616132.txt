Title:
Rose Mazza's poor man cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
2 cups sugar
2 cups of coffee cooled
1 box of raisins medium size
4 cup of flour
2 sticks of butter
1 teaspoon of nutmeg
1 teaspoon of cinnamon
2 t baking powder

Directions:
Mix the water, sugar, coffee and raisins and let it come to a boil, cool 5 minutes or more
Mix the dry ingredients together and add the above raisin mixture.  Bake in oblong cake pan at 350 degrees for one hour.   You can add dry fruit to make this a fruit cake.

Submitter: 
Cheryl Ann
Description: 
It is like a raisin cake.  Our family favorite.
