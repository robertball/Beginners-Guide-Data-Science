Title:
EASY BEAN DIP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 LB. Hamburger
1 LB. small block velveeta cheese
1 10.5 ounce can chuncky salsa (hot,mild)doesn't matter
1 can refried beans
1 bag 10 ounce tortilla chips

Directions:
cook hamburger in skillet (drain)add to a slow cooker
cube whole block velveeta cheese add to slow cooker
add salsa and refried beans
cover put slow cooker on low stir occasionally when cheese and everything comes together  ENJOY!!

Submitter: 
Christopher Davis
Description: 
Bean dip that is very easy for all those Sunday football parties your friends will love it.
