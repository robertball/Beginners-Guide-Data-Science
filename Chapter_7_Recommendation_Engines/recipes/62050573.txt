Title:
Rhubarb Upside-Down Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 recipe yellow cake mix, prepared
2 1/2 cups rhubarb, sliced (4 stalks)
1 1/2 cups sugar
Cinnamon
1 cup whipping cream (or 1/2 & 1/2)

Directions:
Toss rhubarb and sugar and sprinkle over cake batter in prepared 8 x 13" pan.  Sprinkle with cinnamon.  Pour unwhipped cream over all.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45-50 minutes.  Cool 15 minutes, then turn upside down.  Delicious!
