Title:
Olive Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
1/2 cup sun dried tomatoes, drained and julienned
1/4 cup black olives, roughly chopped
1/4 cup green olives, roughly chopped
1/4 cup kalamata olives, roughly chopped
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 Tbsp white wine vinegar
2 Tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
1 Tbsp fresh oregano, chopped
2 green onions, chopped

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil and white wine vinegar. Stir in fresh herbs. Let sit for 5 minutes.
In a large bowl, stir together feta, sun dried tomatoes, olives and green onions. Pour in olive oil mixture and stir to combine. Refrigerate for 15 minutes to let flavors meld and dip is ready to eat.

Submitter: 
Lori Rowley-Sipple
