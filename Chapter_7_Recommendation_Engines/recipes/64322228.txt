Title:
Easy Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups chocolate chips
1 can sweetened condensed milk (14 oz)
4 tbsp butter
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Line 8x8 pan with foil and spray with non-stick cooking spray.
Combine chocolate chips, condensed milk and butter. Microwave for 1 minute, then stir well - until all of the chocolate and butter are melted and the fudge is smooth.
Add the vanilla and salt. Stir until the fudge is smooth and well mixed.
Pour into prepared pan.
Refrigerate to set-for at least 2 hours.
Remove foil and cut into small squares. Best served at room temperature.
