Title:
Rosemary Chicken with Balsamic Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp. olive oil
4 chicken cutlets
2 tsp. fresh rosemary, chopped
1/2 tsp. fresh ground pepper
1 small red onion, thinly sliced
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 tbsp. balsamic vinegar
2 cups grape or cherry tomatoes, halved
1/4 cup chicken broth
Salt to taste
1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
2 tsp. lemon zest

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet.
Sprinkle chicken with rosemary, salt and pepper.
Place in skillet and cook until cooked through and browned on each side.
Remove chicken from skillet.
Add onion and garlic to pan and cook until onion is tender.
Add vinegar, and cook until vinegar is almost evaporated.
Stir in tomatoes and broth.
Cook, stirring often, until the tomatoes begin to soften and sauce thickens slightly - about 5 minutes.
Stir in basil and zest.
Return chicken and any juices to pan and heat through.
Serve chicken topped with tomato mixture.
