Title:
Roast Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Whole Chicken, make sure it is defrosted completely if bought frozen (defrost in a sink of cold water overnight)
Stuffing
1/2 cup butter
1 white onion, chopped
3 celery stalks, chopped
1 1/2 tsp thyme leaves
1/2 tsp sage
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Whole Chicken, make sure it is defrosted completely if bought frozen (defrost in a sink of cold water overnight)
Clean inside of the chicken
Preheat oven to 325 and cook for 1.5 to 2 hours depending on size and if you use an oven bag (recommended as it cuts the cooking time and gives more drippings for gravy)
Fry onions, celery, thyme and sage together
In a large bowl combine onion mixture and bread crumbs
Place stuffing into a cheese cloth or stuffing bag, then into the bird
Place the bird into the Oven bag, then into the oven
