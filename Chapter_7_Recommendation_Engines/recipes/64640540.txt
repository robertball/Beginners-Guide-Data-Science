Title:
Hash Brown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz. Package of Shredded Hash Brown Potatoes, thawed
1 Can of Campbell's Cheddar Cheese Soup
8 oz. Chicken Broth
1/2 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/2 cup finely Chopped Onion
1/2 cup Sour Cream
3 tablespoons Butter, melted
1/2 teaspoon Salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine all the ingredients in a large bowl and mix together thoroughly.
Place mixture in a greased 13"x 9" pan and bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.  Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
LonestarNR
Description: 
This Hash Brown Casserole is easy to make and is delicious.  This can be made as a side dish for lunch or dinner or as a morning breakfast casserole.  Either way, there won't be any left.
