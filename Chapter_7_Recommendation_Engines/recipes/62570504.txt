Title:
Chocolate Coconut macaroons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
(1)
2 cups instant oatmeal
1 cup coconut
4 table spoons cacao powder
(2)
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup milk
2 cups sugar

Directions:
Mix first three ingredients (1) in a large bowl, bring last 3 ingredients (2) to a boil.  As soon as it boils, remove from fire and add to first mixture.  Mix with spoon, drop on wax paper and cool.  This should make about 15 cookies.  You can also freeze these!  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Mama Corsilli
Description: 
No fuss, no bake coconut, oatmeal and chocolate cookies!  One of our Christmas favorites!
