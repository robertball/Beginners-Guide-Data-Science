Title:
Panko Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/404593.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
4 tablespoons good olive oil
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon dried parsley
salt and black pepper to taste
1 to 1 1/2 cups Panko Bread Crumbs

Directions:
Mix all the spices with the Olive Oil and brush on the Chicken. Then roll Chicken in the Panko crumbs, making sure to press the Panko Crumbs firmly on the chicken. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Sally Rogers
Description: 
Panko bread crumbs turn this chicken into crunchy fried chicken goodness, without all of the time and fuss. The Panko really stick on with just an oil rub, so quick and easy!
