Title:
Marge's Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices white bread, crusts removed
butter
1 1/2 c. shredded longhorn cheese
5 eggs
2 c. half and half
1 t. salt
1 t. dry mustard
1 1/2 - 2 c. ham, bacon, or sausage

Directions:
Spread bread with butter and place butter side down in 9 x 13" pan.  Top with cheese.
Combine remaining ingredients, beat well, and pour over bread.  Chill at least 8 hours (overnight is fine).  Remove from refrigerator and bake at 350 degrees for 40-50 minutes.
