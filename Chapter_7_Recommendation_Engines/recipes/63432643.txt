Title:
Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts
2-3 stalks celery, chopped
2-3 carrots, chopped
1 onion, chopped
6-8 c chicken stock
2 tsp vinegar
1 12-16 oz pkg frozen noodles
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine first 6 ingredients in stock pot. Bring to boil, cover and simmer for 40 minutes.
Remove chicken, and tear into pieces with two forks; place in fridge
Place broth in fridge and allow to cool enough to skim off  fat.
Return chicken to broth, add noodles, salt and pepper, and simmer 20-25 minutes.
