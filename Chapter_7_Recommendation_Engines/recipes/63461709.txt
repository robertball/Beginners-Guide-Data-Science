Title:
Cranberry Orange Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/942177.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (12 ounce) packages cranberries
1 orange, zest and juice
1.75 cups brown sugar
1 stick cinnamon

Directions:
Rinse cranberries, juice and zest orange, break cinnamon stick in two, and mix all ingredients in a saucepan.
Start heating over medium heat until it starts to boil, then lower heat to simmer.
Simmer until the cranberries have popped and the sauce is thick, approximately 15 minutes.
Cool and refrigerate.  Serve at room temperature, after removing the cinnamon stick.
