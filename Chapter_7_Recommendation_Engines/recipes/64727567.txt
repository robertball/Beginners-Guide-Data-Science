Title:
Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6150309.jpg
Ingredients:
2 bags of hash browns with onions & peppers
1 pint sour cream
2 cans of cream of mushroom soup
4 tbs. butter
salt & pepper
1 8oz bag shredded cheddar
2-3 c. cornflakes

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees
coat a 9 x 13 dish with cooking spray
mix all ingredients except butter and cornflakes in a large bowl
place contents into dish
mix melted butter and cornflakes
put on top of casserole
bake in oven 1 hour.
