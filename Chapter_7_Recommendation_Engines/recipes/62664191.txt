Title:
Southern Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 to 1 ½ pounds Brussels sprouts, bottoms trimmed off and cut into quarters
1 cup yellow onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/8 cup bacon bits, or 3 slices bacon chopped (if using bacon Omit olive oil)
¼ cup chicken broth
½ to 1 cup canned diced tomatoes, with juices (to taste)
Salt and pepper to taste.

Directions:
1. In large skillet over medium heat add oil bacon bits or bacon, Brussels sprouts, onion, and garlic.  Sauté uncovered 5 minutes.  Then add broth stir, cover and cook for 20 to 30minutes. Stirring occasionally Add tomatoes, salt and pepper to taste and continue cooking uncovered until tender and sweet.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
this is an easy, sweet and comforting dish using bacon, onion, garlic and canned tomatoes.
