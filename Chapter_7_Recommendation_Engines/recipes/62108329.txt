Title:
Dad's Favorite Sugar Free Baked Fruit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large	Apples,peeled, cored and cubed
3 large Pears, cored and cut into wedges
½ cup 	“Cary’s” maple sugar free syrup
1 tsp Ground cinnamon
1 tsp Ground cloves
1 tsp ground nutmeg
1 tsp ground ginger (optional)
½ cup Chopped walnuts or pecans
2 pts Light whipping cream
1/4-1/2 cup Splenda sugar substitute

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Place apples and pears together in bottom of 9” x 13” glass baking dish in a single layer.
Pour syrup evenly over top and sprinkle with spices and nuts.
Bake 20-25 minutes or until fruit is tender.
Whip cream and Splenda together until light and fluffy, cover and chill until ready to serve.
Allow to stand about 10 minutes before serving with whipped topping.

Submitter: 
Linda Glenn
Description: 
This is my Dad's favorite since he's a diabetic.  He and the rest of my family have foregone other desserts in favor of this sugar free recipe!  Try adding  fresh cut peaches or plums, chunked strawberries, or pre-sweetened whole cranberries to the mix for a more festive flair.
