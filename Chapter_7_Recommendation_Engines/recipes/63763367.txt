Title:
Almost No Fat Banana Mini Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose gluten-free flour
1/2 cup white sugar
1 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
2 eggs
3/4 teaspoon xanthum gum
1 cup banana, mashed
1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce with cinnamon
2 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Lightly spray mini muffin pan.
In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, xanthum gum, cinnamon and nutmeg. In a separate bow, stir together eggs, vanilla and applesauce.  Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients; stirring just until combined.  Use small scoop to drop batter into mini muffin pan.  Sprinkle tops with tiny bit of brown sugar.
Bake in preheated oven for 18 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into center of muffin comes out clean. Turn out onto wire rack.
