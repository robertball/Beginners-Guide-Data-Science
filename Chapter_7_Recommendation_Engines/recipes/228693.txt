Title:
Cheesy Southwest Chicken Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F1126918.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups rotini pasta
2 cups cubed deli rotisserie chicken
1 (9 ounce) pouch Progresso(<sup>&trade;</sup>) Recipe Starters(<sup>&trade;</sup>) creamy three cheese cooking sauce
1 (4.5 ounce) can Old El Paso(<sup>&reg;</sup>) diced green chiles, undrained
1 cup shredded taco-flavored cheese
Diced red bell pepper (optional)
Sliced ripe olives (optional)
Tortilla chips (optional)

Directions:
In 12-inch skillet, heat 3 cups water to boiling; add pasta. Cook 8 to 9 minutes or until pasta is tender; drain and return to skillet.
Add chicken, cooking sauce and chiles; heat to boiling, stirring occasionally. Sprinkle with cheese. Cover; let stand 3 to 5 minutes or until cheese is melted. Sprinkle with bell pepper, olives and tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Progresso
Description: 
Make this 30-minute skillet dinner featuring pasta, chicken, Progresso™ Recipe Starters™ creamy three cheese cooking sauce and Old El Paso® diced green chiles--perfect for southwestern meals.
