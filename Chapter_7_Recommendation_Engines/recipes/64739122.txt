Title:
Spicy Black Beans and Rice with Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6350449.jpg
Ingredients:
1lb Andouille sausage
1 14oz can stewed tomatoes
1 15 oz can Black Beans
2 cups White Rice
1/2 onion diced
1 rib of celery diced
3 1/2 cups chicken broth
1 Tbsp olive oil
salt and pepper to taste
cajun seasoning to taste

Directions:
1. Heat olive oil in a dutch oven, add diced onion and celery. Cook until tender.
2. Add sausage and cook until slightly browned.
3. Drain and rinse black beans, add to dutch oven along with the stewed tomatoes, rice and chicken broth. Mix thoroughly.
4. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender.
5. Add salt, pepper and cajun seasoning to taste and serve.
