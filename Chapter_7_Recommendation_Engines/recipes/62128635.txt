Title:
Portabella Bison Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground bison
1 tsp. cumin
1/4 tsp. chipotle chili powder
1/2 tsp. dried thyme
1 tsp. dried oregano
3/4 cup chopped portabella mushrooms
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
Salt and pepper
4 whole wheat buns

Directions:
Preheat grill or pan over medium high heat.
In medium bowl, combine ground bison, cumin, chipotle powder, thyme and oregano.
Add mushrooms, onion and cheddar cheese and mix until well distributed.
Season with salt and pepper.
Divide evenly into four sections and shape into patties.
Place burgers on grill and reduce heat slightly.  Grill 3 to 4 minutes, flip over and grill an additional 3 to 4 minutes.
Serve on toasted whole wheat buns, with your choice of garnishes.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
I like to add chopped mushrooms to lean meats, such as bison, when making burgers - it helps keep the patties moist and delicious.  For even more flavor, I add some onions, cheddar cheese and a touch of smoky spice to make these burgers complete.
