Title:
Everyone's Favorite Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c Brown Sugar
1 1/2 c sugar
3/4 c oil
3/4 c Crisco
1 c butter
1 tsp Vanilla
3 tsp baking soda
1 1/2 tsp salt
3 eggs
5 1/2- 6 c flour
2 bags chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix shortening, butter, and oil with sugars.
Add eggs and blend well.
Add vanilla.
Mix and add flour, baking soda, and salt a little at a time.
Blend well and add chocolate chips.
Bake at 350 for 10-12 minutes.

Submitter: 
Marcia
Description: 
This cookie has been our family favorite for many years. Use red and green M&M;'s for a quick holiday cookie!
