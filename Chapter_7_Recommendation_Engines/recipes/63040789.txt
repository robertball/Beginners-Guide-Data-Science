Title:
Quick Ravioli Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 25 oz pkg cheese ravioli, thawed
1 pound pkg frozen stir-fry vegetables, thawed and drained
1 cup shredded pepper cheese
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 26 oz jar Classico Roasted Chicken with Parmesan and Garlic Pasta Sauce
4 oz mozzarella cheese, cut into strips

Directions:
Spray 2-quart casserole with vegetable cooking spray. Layer half each ravioli, pasta sauce, vegetables and cheese; repeat layering. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until hot.
