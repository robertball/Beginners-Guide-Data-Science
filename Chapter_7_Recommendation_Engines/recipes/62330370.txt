Title:
SUGAR FREE, JELLO / CREAM CHEESE / WHIPPED CREAM Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 SMALL PACKAGE JELLO (ANY FLAVOR)
1 CUP BOILING WATER
1 CUP SUGAR OR 3/4 CUP SPLENDA
8 OZ CREAM CHEESE
1 CAN "MILNOT" BRAND MILK OR EVAPORATED MILK

Directions:
PREPARE PACKAGE JELLO WITH 1 CUP BOILING WATER.
WHIP CREAM CHEESE WITH SUGAR.
MIX CREAM CHEESE MIXTURE WITH JELLO.
WHIP CAN OF MILK.
FOLD WHIPPED MILK INTO JELLO/CREAM CHEESE MIXTURE.
FREEZE
