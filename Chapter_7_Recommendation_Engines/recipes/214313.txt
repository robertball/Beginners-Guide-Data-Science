Title:
Easy Banana Pudding Parfaits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992362.jpg
Ingredients:
12 NILLA Wafers, divided
1/4 cup thawed COOL WHIP Whipped Topping, divided
1 small banana, sliced, divided
2 JELL-O Vanilla Pudding Snacks

Directions:
Crush 10 wafers to form coarse crumbs; place 1/4 crumbs in each of 2 parfait glasses. Top each with 1 Tbsp. COOL WHIP, 2 banana slices and half of 1 pudding snack. Repeat layers of crumbs, bananas and pudding.
Refrigerate 15 min. Meanwhile, wrap reserved banana slices tightly in plastic wrap; refrigerate until ready to use.
Top parfaits with remaining COOL WHIP, wafers and banana slices just before serving.

Submitter: 
Nilla
Description: 
This layered banana parfait is ready for your guests in just 30 minutes, including chill time.
