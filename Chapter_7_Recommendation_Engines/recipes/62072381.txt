Title:
Baked Cranberry Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 cup milk
3 Tbsp brown sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
14 tsp ground nutmeg
14 tsp ground cloves
34 cup rolled oats
34 cup apple chopped
13 cup dried cranberries

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees and grease a 8X8 inch baking dish.  Bring the milk, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves to a boil.  In another bowl mix the oats, chopped apples, walnuts, and crasins together.  Spread oat mix in the baking dish and pour the milk mixture over it.  Bake, uncovered for 30-35 minutes.
