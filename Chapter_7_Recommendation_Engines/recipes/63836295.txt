Title:
pot roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
roast, about 2 lbs
1 bottle red cooking wine
oil
potatoes
onion
carrots
celery
salt
pepper
1 cup beef bullion

Directions:
Rub roast with pepper.
Sear in pan so it is brown on all sices
Put in slow cooker or roasting pan.
Add potatoes, carrots, celery, onion.
Pour red cooking wine and beef bullion over all
Cook at 250 for 4 hours or in crock pot for 6 hours on low.
