Title:
O-O-O My Goodness Mashed Potatoes! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs. potatoes, peeled and quartered
8 oz. Philadelphia Cream Cheese
8 oz. Sour Cream
1 stick butter, or margarine
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil potatoes in large pot of water 15-20 minutes, till tender
Drain water and mash potatoes
Whip potatoes with cream cheese, sour cream and butter
Salt and pepper to taste

Submitter: 
Love2Cook
Description: 
Quick and easy recipe for the creamiest and tastiest mashed potatoes you have ever tasted.
