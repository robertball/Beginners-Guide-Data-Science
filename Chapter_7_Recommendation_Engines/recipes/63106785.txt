Title:
Peasant Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast OR 2 1/4 tsp. dry active yeast.
2 cups warm water (110 degrees to 115 degrees), divided
1 Tbsp. sugar
4 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons salt
1 tablespoon butter or margarine, melted

Directions:
Dissolve yeast AND the sugar in 1 cup warm water.
In a large bowl, combine flour, salt.
Add the yeast mixture and remaining water; stir until combined.
OPTIONAL...Turn out onto a floured surface and knead for a few minutes.
Cover and let rise in a warm place until doubled, about 1 hour.
Stir/Punch dough down. Divide in half.
Knead a second time (optional).
Place each half in a 1-qt. round casserole or ovenproof bowl. OR freehand two loaves and place on a greased cookie sheet.
Brush tops with butter and cut decorative slits in each loaf if you wish.
Let rise in a warm place until doubled, about 45 minutes.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 35 minutes or until each loaf sounds hollow when tapped.
Remove from pans and top with melted butter; serve warm if desired.
