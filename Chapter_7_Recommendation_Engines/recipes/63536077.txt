Title:
Shake and Bake Chicken Coating Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C Seasoned Bread Crumbs
2 tsp Dried Parsley
2 tsp Garlic Powder
1 tsp Onion Powder
1 tsp Paprika
1/2 tsp Sugar

Directions:
Pour ingredients into a gallon-size zip bag and shake to mix well.  Add chicken that has been rubbed with canola oil, a few pieces at a time, seal and shake to coat.  Bake chicken on ungreased baking sheet at 350 degrees F for one hour.

Submitter: 
Gina Guerra
Description: 
A quick and easy shake-on mix to liven up baked chicken.
