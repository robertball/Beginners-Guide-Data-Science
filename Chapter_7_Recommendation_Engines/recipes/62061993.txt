Title:
Allie's Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thawed.
1 can of cream of chicken soup
8 slices of swiss cheese
1 small box of stuffing mix
1/2 stick butter, melted

Directions:
Place thawed chicken into a 13 x 9 inch pan.  Cover the chicken with soup and layer the cheese on top of it.  Then, sprinkle stuffing mix on top.  Melt the butter and pour over stuffing.  Bake at 375 until juices run clear, about 20-25 mins.

Submitter: 
Traveling_Is_Love
Description: 
Boneless, skinless chicken breasts are topped with cream of chicken soup, swiss, and stuffing mix, then just baked to perfection.  So easy and so fast... yet so good!  This recipe has virtually no prep time at all and will have you eating in no time!
