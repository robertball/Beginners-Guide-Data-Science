Title:
in a pinch bread sticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 - 1 loaf of bread (white or wheat work best)
butter
parmesan cheese
mozzarella cheese (optional)
oregano

Directions:
spread butter on slices of bread
cut bread slices into thirds
sprinkle parmesan cheese and oregano over bread
for extra flavor sprinkle with mozzarella cheese
bake in oven at 350 degrees for about 10 minutes or until golden.

Submitter: 
MAMA29
Description: 
No time to make dough or run to the store?  You can make breadsticks with ingredients from your cabinet!
