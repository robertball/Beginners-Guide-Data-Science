Title:
Tortellini a la Pana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. dried or fresh cheese tortellini
1 1/2 cups mushrooms
1 small can sliced black olives
1 cup sliced cooked ham
1/2 cup whipping cream
1/2 cup half 'n half
2 cloves minced garlic
1/2 onion, chopped
2 tblsp fresh chopped basil
4-6 oz. canned tomatoes
1/2 tblsp tomato paste
1 tblsp olive oil
1/4 tsp fresh ground black pepper

Directions:
Saute garlic and onion in olive oil in large saute pan under medium heat.
Add mushrooms and saute.
Add ham for 1 minute.  Stir in tomatoes and tomato paste.
Add cream and half n half, basil, olives, pepper.
Bring to boil then reduce heat and slow boil for 10 minutes or until sauce slightly thickens.
Serve over tortellini on pasta plate.
