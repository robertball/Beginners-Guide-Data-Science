Title:
ID Spark’s Pesto Perfection Chicken Ciabattas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6948283.jpg
Ingredients:
½ c. mayonnaise
1 tsp Italian Dressing Mix
¾ c. pesto
1 1/2 cups shredded chicken
¼ c  ½ c softened or melted margarine
8 ciabatta rolls
8 slices provolone cheese

Directions:
1.  Mix mayonnaise, Italian Dressing Mix and pesto in a bowl. Add chicken and stir.
2.  Brush the bottoms of rolls with margarine and set on parchment or wax paper  Turn rolls right side up and add approximately ¼ cup chicken mixture to each and top with a slice of cheese.
3.  Place 4 ciabattas, on press, (or as many as your press allows),  and brush the tops with margarine.
4.	Cook 5-8 minutes, or until tops are golden brown and cheese has melted.
5.    Enjoy!
