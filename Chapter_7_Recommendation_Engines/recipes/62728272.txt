Title:
White Sauce Made With Vegetable Broth Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp butter (coconut or peanut oil)
2 Tbsp flour
1/2 tsp salt
Dash of pepper
1/2 cup milk (soy or almond)
1/2 cup water in which the vegetables were cooked

Directions:
Melt the butter, then add the flour, salt and pepper.
Heat the milk and water of medium high heat. Pour the butter mixture into the liquids.
Cook until the mixture has thickened.
Pour this over the drained vegetable and serve it hot.
