Title:
Southwestern Salsa with Black Beans and Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 15-ounce cans black beans, rinsed and drained
2 15-ounce cans shoepeg corn, drained
1 T cumin seeds
juice of 2 limes (about 2/3 c)
1/2 c olive oil
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp salt
1 tsp crushed red pepper
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 red pepper, chopped
1 purple onion, chopped
3/4 c fresh cilantro, chopped
3/4 c fresh parsley, chopped

Directions:
Rinse and drain beans in colander. Add corn to colander to drain without rinsing.
Toast cumin seeds in skillet over medium heat until fragrant and starting to brown.
Meanwhile, combine lime juice, oil, garlic, salt, and dry peppers in large bowl. When cumin seeds are toasted and still hot, add to dressing in bowl.
Stir chopped pepper, onion, parsley, and cilantro into dressing.
Fold beans and corn into vegetables.
Refrigerate up to 3 days. Serve with tortilla chips.
