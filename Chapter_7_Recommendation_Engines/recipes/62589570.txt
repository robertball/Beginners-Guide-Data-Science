Title:
Marilyn's Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2c butter, melted and cooled
2 beaten eggs
1 1/3c brown sugar
2/3 c milk
2c flour
2t baking powder
1t cinnamon
1/2t salt
1 c blueberries

Directions:
Add all ingredients in order. Do not overmix.
Spray paper muffin cups.
Bake at 350 for 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Christine Simon Michener
Description: 
Even better with a powdered sugar glaze!
