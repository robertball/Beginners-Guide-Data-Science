Title:
Chicken Fettuccini with Lemon Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5183128.jpg
Ingredients:
16 ounce Fettuccine
9 ounce Cooked Chicken Breast, diced
1 Onion, Diced
1 Red Pepper, Diced
1/2 Yellow Onion, Diced
4 ounce mushroom, sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoon olive oil
4 ounce butter
1/2 cup white wine
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 lemon juiced
1/4 cup grated paramesan
1/2 teaspoon lemon pepper (optional)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook the pasta
Sautee garlic, onion, pepper, and mushrooms in olive oil.
In a sauce pan heat butter, wine, chicken broth, and lemon juice.
Add chicken, saute vegetables, and Parmesan cheese. Heat until hot again and then serve over pasta.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
This is a chicken and fettucine pasta dish in a lemon butter sauce with sautee garlic, onion, pepper, and mushrooms. Spoon the mixture over pasta, top with some grated paramesan, and serve with  a salad and garlic bread.
