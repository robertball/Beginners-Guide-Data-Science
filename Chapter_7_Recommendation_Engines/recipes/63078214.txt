Title:
Butter Pecan Turtle Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup butter - softened
Carmel Layer
2/3 cups butter
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1 cup whole pecan halves
1 cup choco chips

Directions:
Preheat over to 350 degrees
In medium bowl combine crust ingredients.  Mix @ medium speed, scraping sides of bowl often, 2-3 min. or until well mixed and particles are fine.
Pat firmly into ungreased 13x9 pan.  Sprinkle pecans evenly over unbaked crust.
Prepare carmel layer:
In heavy 1 qt. saucepan combine brown sugar and butter.  Cook over med. heat stirring constantly, until entire surface of mixture begins to boil.
Boil 1/2 to 1 min. stirring constantly.
Pour carmel layer evenly over pecans and crust.  Bake near center of over @ 350 degree oven for 18 - 22 minutes or until entire caramel layer is light golden brown.
Immediately sprinkle with chips.  Allow chips to melt slightly.
Swirl chips as they melt to get marble affect.
Cool completely.  But into bars.
