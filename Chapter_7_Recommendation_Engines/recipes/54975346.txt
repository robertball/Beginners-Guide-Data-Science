Title:
Rotel knockoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 qts peel/chop/seed tomatoes (about 4 lbs)
12 jalapenos/hot chili diced
1 tsp salt

Directions:
put all in 4 qt pan
simmer until reduced to 1 quart   cans at the grocers are 10oz
so freeze in 1 cup portions
Adjust hot peppers for heat
making small batches is so easy
