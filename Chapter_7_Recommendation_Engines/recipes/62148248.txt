Title:
Orange Julius Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6oz can frozen O.J.
1 cup milk
1 cup water
1/3 cup sugar
1/2 tsp. vanilla
12 ice cubes

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in blender until slushy.

Submitter: 
Christina J Dykstra Goetzke
Description: 
Delicious creamy and refreshing slushy drink your kids will love! We made these all the time after we came home from school when we were kids.
