Title:
Barbecued Beef (or Pork) Pulled Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb roast (round, shoulder, or brisket)
1 c ketchup
1 can beer
1 envelope dried onion soup mix

Directions:
Place roast in roasting pan (can also use crock pot on low)
In separate bowl, combine ketchup, beer, and soup mix and pour over roast.
Roast at 250 degrees for 4-5 hours, until meat can be shredded with a fork.
Serve on buns, with good bottled barbecue sauce on top.
