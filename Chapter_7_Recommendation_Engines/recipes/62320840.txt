Title:
Slow cooker Melt-In-Your-Mouth Short Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 serving-sized pieces beef short ribs (about 3 pounds)
2 tablespoons packed brown sugar
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon dried thyme leaves, crushed
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 can (10 1/2 ounces) Campbell'sÂ® Condensed French Onion Soup
1 bottle (12 fluid ounces) dark ale or beer
Hot mashed potatoes or egg noodles

Directions:
1. Place the beef into a 5-quart slow cooker. Add the brown sugar, garlic, thyme and flour and toss to coat.
2. Stir the soup and ale in a small bowl. Pour the soup mixture over the beef.
3. Cover and cook on LOW for 8 to 9 hours or until the beef is fork-tender. Spoon off any fat from the surface of the sauce. Serve with the mashed potatoes.
4. Time-Saving: Or you may cook this recipe on HIGH for 4 to 5 hours.
