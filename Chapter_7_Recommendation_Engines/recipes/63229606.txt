Title:
Classic Daiquiri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/840088.jpg
Ingredients:
1 oz fresh lime juice (juice from 1 lime)
1 1/2 oz light rum
1 oz triple sec (or other orange flavored liquor)
1 tsp sugar (adjust to taste)
1 cup ice cubes
lime wedge, optional
sugar, optional

Directions:
For sugared rim, place a small layer of sugar in a small plate.  Rub lime wedge around the rim of a glass.  Dip glass rim in sugar to coat.
For a frozen Daiquiri, combine all ingredients in blender and blend on highest setting for 15-20 seconds.  Pour into glass.
To serve on the rocks, combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker. Shake for 20-30 seconds. Strain into ice filled glass.

Submitter: 
Patrick Washburn
Description: 
Enjoy this classic daiquiri frozen or on the rocks.
