Title:
Lally's Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup diced onion
1 1/3 cup ketchup
2/3 cup cider vinegar
1/3 cup packed brown sugar
1 Tbsp dry mustard
1 tsp dry ginger
1/2 tsp each salt & pepper (to taste)
4 boneless chicken breasts ( or equivalent servings of thighs)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Mix all ingredients except the chicken in a saucepan.  Bring to a boil, then simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Spray a casserole dish with oil and place the chicken in. Bake covered for 20 minutes, then uncovered 10 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Linda McMurtrie
Description: 
Fast, easy and so tasty, chicken is sweet/tangy and moist.  I serve with rice, but mashed potatoes are good too.  Comfort food from my grandma.
