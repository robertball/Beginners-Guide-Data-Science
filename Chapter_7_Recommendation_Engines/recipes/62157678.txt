Title:
Easiest and Yummiest Donut Holes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tube Premade Biscuit Dough
Oil for Deep Frying
About 3/4 Cup Powdered Sugar
Cinnamon to Taste
Pinch Nutmeg
Tiny Pinch Ground Cloves

Directions:
1- Open tube of biscuits.
2- Either a.- cut each biscuit into fourths then follow step 3 or b.- cut a circle out of the center of each biscuit with plastic bottle cap (then you get donut holes and donut rings)
3-Heat enough oil to cover donut/donut holes plus a couple inches more to 350 degreesF in deep pot.
4- Slowly put 10 donuts/holes in hot oil.
5-Fry untill golden brown on one side.
5-Flip over and fry other side untill golden brown.
6-Drain on paper towlels.
7- Put powdered sugar, cinnamon,nutmeg,cloves into brown paper bag. Add donuts. Shake bag and enjoy.
These are great with coffee!
