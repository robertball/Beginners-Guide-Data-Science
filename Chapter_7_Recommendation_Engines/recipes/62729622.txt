Title:
Super Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/735769.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb.hamberger
1 large chopped onion
1 can refried beans
1-4oz.can chopped
green chilies
1 jalapeno[if desired]
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 cup taco sauce
1 cup sour cream
1/4 cup chopped green onions
1 chopped tomato

Directions:
Brown beef and onion,drain all fat.Season with salt.Spread refried beans on bottom of 9x9 pan or casserole dish.Spread so beans are slightly up the sides.Sread hamberger on top,then add chili peppers and jalapeno.Add the cheese evenly on top.Drizzle with taco sauce.Bake in oven at 325 for 45 min.or till hot.When done remove from oven and garnish with sour cream,green onions and tomato.Serve with taco chips.
