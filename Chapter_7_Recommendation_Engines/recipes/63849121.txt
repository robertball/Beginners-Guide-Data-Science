Title:
Super simple vegetable beef soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb ground beef
1 15oz can mixed vegetables
1 15oz can whole potatoes
1 15oz can green beans
1 small onion, chopped
1 8oz can tomato sauce
2 12oz cans spicy vegetable juice
2 tbs worcesterchire sauce
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper

Directions:
Brown ground beef. drain
Combine ground beef with all other ingredients in a pot and heat on stove top until boiling. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
SAMARTHUR
Description: 
A simple recipe that's ready in less than 30 minutes.  Goes great with crackers or corn bread.
