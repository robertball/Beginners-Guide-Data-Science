Title:
Strawberry Roy - A Bourbon Experience Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 jiggers Maker's Mark, Woodford Reserve, or other fine Bourbon Whiskey
1 jigger Framboise
Splash of grenadine
2 Red maraschino cherries

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients in an ice-filled shaker. Cap and shake vigorously. Serve in a martini and garnish with the cherries.

Submitter: 
The Jazz Chef
Description: 
This simple substitute of French framboise, the strawberry liqueur, brings a new dimension to the Rob Roy with a touch of strawberry flavor that blends delicously with the bourbon.
