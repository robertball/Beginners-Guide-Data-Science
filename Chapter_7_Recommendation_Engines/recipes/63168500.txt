Title:
Black Sticky Rice Pudding  (Bubur Ketan Hitam) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/813221.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups black sticky rice
7 cups water
Sugar 1 cup or to taste
1 can Chaokoh - Coconut Milk 13.5 Fl. Oz.
1/2 tsp Salt

Directions:
Wash the rice  . Cook the rice and water in pressure cooker for 35 minutes. Add sure until sweet enough to your taste.
Cook the coconut milk with the salt
