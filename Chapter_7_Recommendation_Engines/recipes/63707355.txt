Title:
Don Sutton’s Zucchini Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbls. Butter
1 med. Onion
1 lb. Ground round
½ tsp. salt
½ tsp. ground pepper
3 med. Zucchini (1 lb.)
6 slices sharp cheddar cheese or 6 oz.
1 can cream of mushroom soup
½ to 1-cup saltine cracker crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven: 350°F
In a skillet, melt butter and sauté finely chopped onion.  Add meat, salt and pepper.   Put whole zucchini in a pot of water, with 1 tsp. salt, bring to a boil, and boil for five minutes.  Remove zucchini and cut into ¼” slices.
Cook rice—
In 2 qt. Casserole, layer all of rice, and alternate layers of meat, zucchini, and cheese.  Pour soup on top.  Don’t stir!  Sprinkle top with cracker crumbs.
