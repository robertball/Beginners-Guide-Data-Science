Title:
Slow Cooker Pork Chops with a Twist Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1003227.jpg
Ingredients:
4- Center Cut pork chops
2- 10.5 oz cans Cream of Celery
1- 10.5 oz can Cream of mushroom
1- 10.5 oz can Cream of potato
4- 4 oz cans sliced mushrooms
2- cubes beef bouillon
1- T italian seasoning
1- T hot sauce such as El Yucateco

Directions:
Put everything in the slow cooker for 6 hours on high or 10 hours on low and let it go.
