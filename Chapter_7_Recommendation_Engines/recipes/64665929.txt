Title:
BEEF TENDERLOIN with Dijon mustard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Baste
1 tbsp (15 mL) Dijon mustard
1 tbsp (15 mL) soy sauce
1 tsp freshly ground pepper
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp (15 mL) olive oil

Directions:
1. Preheat grill to high.
2. Combine mustard, soy sauce, salt, pepper and oil. Brush onto beef.
3. Place beef on grill and sear 1 side for 2 minutes, roll onto second side and sear another 2 minutes, roll again and sear the remaining side. Brush with baste. Turn meat after 15 minutes. Grill meat until medium-rare, about 135ºF (60ºC) on an instant-read thermometer or to desired degree of doneness. Check onions and remove when browned around the edges and softened, about 15 minutes
4. Remove meat from grill. Let meat sit for 5 minutes.

This is a superb presentation for beef tenderloin. The outside is crusty and the inside pink and succulent
