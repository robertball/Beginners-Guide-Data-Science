Title:
Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans pork & beans
3 T ketchup
2 T onion, diced
2 1/2 t brown sugar
2 1/2 t honey
1 T yellow mustard
1/2 t Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Mix all together and bake @ 350 for 30 to 40 minutes.
