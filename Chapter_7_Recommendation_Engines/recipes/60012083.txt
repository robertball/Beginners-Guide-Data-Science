Title:
Chicken Fiesta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 (1.27 ounce) packet dry fajita seasoning, divided
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (11 ounce) can Mexican-style corn
1/2 cup salsa
1 (10 ounce) package mixed salad greens
1 onion, chopped
1 bunchchopped fresh cilantro
1 tomato, cut into wedge
8 0z. Low fat Mexican blend cheese
Baked Tostitos

Directions:
Rub chicken evenly with 1/2 the fajita seasoning. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat, and cook the chicken 8 minutes on each side, or until juices run clear; set aside.
In a large saucepan, mix beans, corn, salsa and other 1/2 of fajita seasoning. Heat over medium heat until warm.
Prepare the salad by tossing the greens, onion and tomato. Top salad with chicken, bean and corn mixture, cheese, cilantro & crushed baked tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Hana
Description: 
This is an attractive and zesty all in one dish. It's full of veggies and chicken, and loaded with flavor. A quick fix for a long day. Top with shredded cheese and tortilla chips and fresh cilantro.Modified from: Chicken Fiesta Salad
