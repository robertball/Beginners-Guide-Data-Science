Title:
Chili-Garlic Shrimp Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz soba noodles
1/4 cup canola oil
2 T lime juice
2 T reduced-sodium soy sauce
1 tsp toasted sesame oil
1 pound peeled and deveined medium shrimp
1 T Asian chili-garlic sauce
2 cups shredded purple cabbage
2 cups halved snap peas
4 thinly sliced carrots

Directions:
Cook soba noodles.
Toss with 2 T each canola oil, lime juice, and reduced-sodium soy sauce and 1 tsp toasted sesame oil.
Cook shrimp in 2 T canola oil in large skillet over medium-high heat, tossing frequently, until opaque throughout, 2-3 minutes.
Stir in chili-garlic sauce.
Serve shrimp and noodles topped with cabbage, peas, and carrots.
Serve with lime wedges and additional chili-garlic sauce.
