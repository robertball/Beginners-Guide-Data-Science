Title:
Blushing White Zinfandel Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon tarragon
1/4 teaspoon ground coriander
1/8 teaspoon white pepper
as needed xanthun gum
1 clove garlic
1 tablespoon white vinegar
1/4 cup white Zinfandel wine
3/4 cup canola oil

Directions:
Blend it up. Add the xanthun gum, then the oil.

Submitter: 
Falvor King
Description: 
Perfect for Valentine's Day salads, this sweet and tangy, pink dressing will soon become a year-round favorite with your family!
