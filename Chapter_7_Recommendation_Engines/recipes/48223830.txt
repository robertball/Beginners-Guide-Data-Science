Title:
MOHINI’S SPICY SHRIMP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. 		shrimp, peeled and deveined
2 tsp. 		Cayenne pepper, divided
1 tsp. 		Turmeric, divided
1-1/2 tsp. 	Salt or to taste
Juice of 1 lemon
1 Tbsp. 	minced peeled gingerroot
1 Tbsp. 	minced garlic
2 tsp. 		Coriander powder
2 tsp. 		Cumin powder
2 tsp. 		Mango powder (amchur) or 3 tbsp. freshly squeezed lemon juice
2 Tbsp. 	oil
1 can (7.5 oz) 	tomato sauce
1/3 cup 	cilantro, chopped

Directions:
Mix shrimp with 1/2  tsp. Cayenne pepper, 1/4  tsp. Turmeric, salt and lemon juice.  Set aside.  Meanwhile, in blender, combine ginger, garlic, coriander, cumin and mango powders, remaining cayenne pepper and 3 Tbsp. water to make a paste.  In a large wok, heat oil and sauté masala paste and remaining turmeric until masala is fragrant, 4 to 5 minutes.  Stir in tomato sauce and cook until reduced by about one third.  Increase heat to high and mix in shrimp.  Cook until shrimp turns opaque, 4 to 5 minutes.  Do not overcook.  Fold in cilantro and serve immediately.
