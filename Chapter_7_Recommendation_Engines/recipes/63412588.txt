Title:
YUMMY PEANUT BUTTER POPCORN! Quick treat! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bags of microwave light butter popcorn
1 Cup light corn syrup
1 Cup light brown Sugar
1 Cup of Chunky of reg Peanut Butter

Directions:
Pop corn in microwave, pick out the old maids or unpopped kernals. Place in large bowl. (throw away unpoppped kernals) in med saucepan boil syrup and brown sugar.  remove from heat and whisk in peanut butter.  pour over popcorn and stir to coat. Place on a baking sheet to cool.  place in air tight containers for longer lasting goodness!  This is really simple and very GOOD!  Enjoy!
