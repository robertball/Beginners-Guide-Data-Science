Title:
Lamb and Eggplant Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs Lean Lamb or Goat
4 large tomatoes
2 medium onions
6 palm sized purple eggplant (or thai eggplant)
4 tlbs ginger garlic paste
5 cardamoms split
3 whole cinnamon sticks
1 tsp whole black peppercorn
1 tsp whole cloves
1 tsp star anise (dried)
2 tsp red chili powder (or cayenne pepper)
2 tsp turmeric
2 slit green chillies
3 tlbs vegetable, canola or sunflower oil
salt to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in pan
Add whole spices (cardamon, cinamon, clove, peppercorn, star anise)
Wait for the oil to bubble and release fragrances
Add chopped onions and green chillies, saute till transparent
Add ginger garlic paste
Add meat chopped in 1 inch cubes , and stir till well mixed
Add dry spices (red chili, turmeric, salt,)
Stir well, a 1 & 1/2 cup water
Place lid (with a little space for venting) cook on medium to high for 20 mins stirring occasionally.
Serve with jasmine rice, roti or naan

Description: 
This mildly-spicy and flavourful dish, is found all the way from Morocco,Lebanon, through Afghanistan,India right to Mongolia. Best eaten with jasmine rice, roti or naan, its a very popular dish during festivals and celebrations.
