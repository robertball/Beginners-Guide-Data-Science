Title:
Grandma Dixon's Fudge Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C Butter
1 C H2O
2 Squares Unsweetened Chocolate
2 C Flour
1 tsp Baking Powder
2 C Sugar
1/2 C Sour Milk
2 Eggs
1 tsp Vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F
Cream Butter, Sugar, & Eggs
Mix Flour, Baking Powder
Melt Chocolate in 1 C H2O and add to creamed mixture and beat well.
Add Flour, Sour Milk and Vanilla.
Beat at high speed for 3 mins.
Pour batter into greased and floured 9" x 13" pan.
Bake at 350 F for 30-35 mins
