Title:
Spanish rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, chopped
1/2 green pepper
1 lb ground beef
1 C uncooked regular rice
2 8 oz cans tomato sauce
1 3/4 C hot water
1 t salt
dash pepper

Directions:
Brown meat, add green pepper, onion. Cook 5 minutes, stirring. Remove excess fat.
Add remaining ingredients, bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Lynn Pennec
Description: 
Never had this in Spain, but a good casserole from my youth.
