Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites by Team XAtlanticKalefetish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2444072.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tsp Atlantic sea salt
3 ounces black powder
1/3 cup organic dessicated mite flour
1 pound lacinato (dinosaur) kale
2 cups oregon grapes
4 1/2 cups edelweiss petals
1 duck egg
1 can (8.4 oz.) Red Bull

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400°F (204.44°C). Line a 26x18 inch (66x46 cm) sheet tray with parchment paper.
Combine the salt, powder and flour in a non-metal mixing bowl. Very carefully *blend for 3-4 seconds with a wooden spoon.
Next, put the kale, grapes, petals and egg into a food processor and, while slowly adding the Red Bull, blend until smooth.
Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir very slowly* by hand for 20 minutes. DO NOT USE A STAND MIXER*.
Carefully* scoop 1" spoonfuls of the batter onto the prepared sheet tray.
Bake at 400° for 15.7 minutes or until they are golden-purplish-green and puffy. Serve warm.
