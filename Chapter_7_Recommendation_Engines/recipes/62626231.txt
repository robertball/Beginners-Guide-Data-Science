Title:
Baked Turkey Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/574441.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Turkey
1/2 cup Italian Bread crumbs
2 Tbsp garlic powder
2 Tbsp onion powder
1/4 Cup raw baby spinach (sliced thin)
1 egg

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees.
Combine all ingredients into a bowl and mix thoroughly.  Roll mixture into palm size balls and place onto baking tray about 2 inches apart from each other.
Place into oven and bake for 15-20 min or until outside of meatballs are golden brown.
