Title:
Black Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/456496.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 large onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 pound lean ground beef
3 (15 ounce) cans black beans, undrained
2 large Cherokee Purple tomatoes, diced
3 tablespoons chili powder
1 tablespoon dried oregano
1 tablespoon dried basil leaves
1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
1 teaspooon cumin
1 tablespoon cayenne pepper
1 tablespoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1 tablespoon paprika
1 (6oz) can tomato paste
Hot & Spicy Cheese-Its
Sour Cream
Mexican shredded cheese blend

Directions:
Saut√© onion and garlic in a large skillet until onion is translucent.  Add beef and cook until browned.  Add everything, including meat, to a Crockpot and cook on low until the smell drives you crazy and you have to give in and eat it before the six hours are up.  Top with a dollop of sour cream, shredded cheese, and Cheese-Its.
