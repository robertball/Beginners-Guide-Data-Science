Title:
Kelly's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb boneless skinless chicken
2 cans diced tomatoes
2 cans light red kidney beans
3 potatoes
2 onions (small to meduim size)
3/4 corn (frozen or fresh)
1 tbs ground pepper
3 tbs chilli powder
salt to taste

Directions:
1. Cut chicken into bite size pices and cook the meat in a skillet only till the outsides are white.
2. Take chicken out and put it in large pan.
3. Cut potatoes and onions and add them in the pan with the chicken in it. Empty in tomato cans and the juice of the kidney beans into the pan. If the corn is frozen add this add it also. Keep the heat at meduim.
4. Add pepper, salt to taste and chilli powder. The quanitity of chilli powder varies on how you want it to taste. More you add more spicy it becomes.
5. When all vegetables are almost cooked add kidney beans and cook till done.
6. Serve with cheddar cheese and sour cream

Submitter: 
SHREY
Description: 
This is the best chilli recipe ever! My friend Kelly taught me how to make it hence the name. My younger brother loves this recipe so much he makes me make it every two weeks.
