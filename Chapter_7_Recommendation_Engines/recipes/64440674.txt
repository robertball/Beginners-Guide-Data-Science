Title:
Chicken with Garlic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 bone-in, skin-on chicken
Salt, freshly ground pepper, to taste
2T unsalted ghee
40 cloves of garlic, peeled
1 1/2c chicken broth
1T dried thyme
2T almond flour
2T coconut milk

Directions:
Season chicken with salt and pepper to taste. Melt ghee in a skillet over med/high heat. Add chicken, skin-side down, and sear both sides until golden brown, about 2-3 min per side, set aside. Add garlic to the skillet and cook, stirring frequently, until golden brown, about 4-5 min. Stir in chicken broth and bring to a boil. Add chicken and sprinkle with thyme. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer until chicken is cooked through, about 25-30 min, set aside chicken, reserving the juices in the skillet. In a small bowl, whisk together flour and milk. Whisk in flour mixture to the skillet until slightly thickened, about 1-2, season with salt and pepper,to taste. Serve immediately.
