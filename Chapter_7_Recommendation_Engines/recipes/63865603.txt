Title:
Cranberry Meat Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 lb. sausage (I used turkey sausage)
1 tube crackers (Ritz or saltines)
1 small onion (chopped)
Garlic and Italian seasonings to taste
Salt & Pepper
3 Eggs
1/2 Cup Barbecue sauce
sauce:1 Cup Barbecue sauce
1 Cup brown sugar
1 can sauerkraut
1 can whole cranberry sauce

Directions:
Mix together and form meatballs.  Arrange in baking dish.
Sauce
1 Cup Barbecue sauce
1 Cup brown sugar
1 can sauerkraut
1 can whole cranberry sauce
Mix together well and pour over meatballs.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 - 60 minutes.
Serve with rice

Submitter: 
Grammy M B H
Description: 
These are amazing meat balls and are a main dish entree.  No one can guess the secret ingredient-sauerkraut.  Amazing!  My friend Martha, brought it to a pot luck and it was devoured and loved by all.  I serve it with rice and green beans.
