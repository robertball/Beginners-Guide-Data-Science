Title:
Acorn Squash Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 medium acorn squash, halved and seeded
2 tablespoon butter
4 tablespoons brown sugar

Directions:
Turn acorn squash upside down onto a cookie sheet.
Bake at 350Â° until it begins to soften, approximately 30 to 45 minutes.
Place 1 tablespoon of butter and 2 tablespoons brown sugar into each half of squash, bake another 10 minutes.
