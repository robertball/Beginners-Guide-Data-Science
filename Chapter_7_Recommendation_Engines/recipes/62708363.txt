Title:
Cuban Chicken Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon chopped chipotle in adobe
4 hoagie rolls
Dijon mustard
1 slice deli ham
dill pickle slices
2 slices Swiss cheese

Directions:
Mix 1/4 cup mayonnaise and 1 tsp chopped chipotle in adobo; spread on top halves of 4 hoagie rolls.
Spread Dijon mustard on bottom halves and add sliced cooked chicken, 1 slice deli ham, dill pickle slices and 2 slices Swiss cheese on each.
Grill sandwiches on panini press or in a skillet.
