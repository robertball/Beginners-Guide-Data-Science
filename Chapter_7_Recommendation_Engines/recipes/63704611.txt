Title:
Creamy Jambalaya Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1030791.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound of wheat Farfalle pasta
1/2 lb smoke sausage, cut into bite-size pieces
1/2 cup of half and half or sour cream
1 tbsp garlic powder
1 tbsp onion powder
1 1/2 tsp Cajun seasoning
1 1/2 tsp smoke paprika
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 cup white wine
4 tbs tomato sauce
1 1/2 tsp chopped parsley
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package.
Cook sausage with onion and garlic powders in large skillet.
Add white wine to sausage and cook for 2 minutes.
Stir in Cajun seasoning, paprika, pepper, and tomato sauce.
Add yogurt and heat to almost bubbling, then reduce heat to low.
Heat at low for five minutes.
Drain pasta and add to skillet.
Toss with parsley and Parmesan.
