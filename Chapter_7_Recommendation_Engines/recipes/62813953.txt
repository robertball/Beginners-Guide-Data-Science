Title:
BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
48 oz ketchup
1 c red wine vinegar
1 c brown sugar
12 oz honey
1-2 oz tobasco sauce
16 oz tomato puree
1 T dry mustard
1 T lime juice
1 T black pepper
1 t salt
3.5 oz bottle liquid smoke
3 cloves garlic, minced

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a large pot and simmer over low heat for 2-3 hours.
Store in the refrigerator in an airtight container for up to 12 months.

Submitter: 
fullerla
Description: 
A sweet, spicy, tangy sauce made in a big batch to last the summer BBQ season.  Many thanks to the father of one of my good friends for developing this recipe which is now our family favorite!
