Title:
Healthy Quinoa Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1011760.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup green onions
1 cup diced tomatoes
1 cup diced cucumber
1 cup chopped parsley
1 cup to 1 1/2 cups cooked quinoa
1 cup canned, rinsed chick peas
2 tbsp evoo (extra virgin olive oil)
3 tbsp lemon juice
2 tsp chopped fresh mint
3 gloves minced garlic
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
Cook quinoa, and set aside to cool, or speed cooling time by refrigerating, or making the day before. (10 minutes to cook quinoa).
In a large serving bowl add your; green onions, diced tomatoes, diced cucumber, chopped parsley, cooked quinoa, and the chick peas.
In a smaller separate bowl mix together; evoo (extra virgin olive oil), lemon juice, chopped fresh mint, minced garlic, salt and pepper.

Description: 
This is an absolutely delicious, refreshing and healthy salad that I'm sure everyone will enjoy.  It could also be made with lentils, barley, rice, and served with falafel, fish, or chicken.  It's DELICIOUS, fast and easy to prepare.  Enjoy! :)
