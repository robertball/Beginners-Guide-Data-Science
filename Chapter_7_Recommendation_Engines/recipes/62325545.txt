Title:
Chicken Breast in a Dates Capers Mascarpone Sauce with Cous Cous Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/381244.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Chicken Breast
1/4 cup Mascarpone
1/4   Cucumber
1/2 Tomato diced
3 Dates dried diced
1/4 Cup Capers in Vinegar
1/4 Cup Cooking Cream
1 Tablespoon salted Butter
Pinch Salt
Pinch Black Pepper
1 Teaspoon Lemon Juice
1/2 Cup Cous Cous
1 Tablespoon unsalted Butter
1/2 Cup Water

Directions:
1. Add the salted  Butter Capers and diced Dates in a Saucier Pan and heat up slowly .
2. Add the Mascarpone and Cooking Cream and bring to boil , reduce heat and let simmer to reduce slightly. Add Salt and pepper to your preference.
3. Grill the Chicken breast and slice in 2 Parts
4. In a Saucier Pan bring the Cous Cous and Water To boil. Take of heat and let the Grains soak up the water. Add the spoon butter and pinch salt  and turn slowly .
5.Place Cous Cous on the plate decorate with entire Date
6.Place Chicken Breast on Plate and cover with Sauce
7. Sprinkle the Cucumbers dices and Tomato Dices over the Chicken Breast.
8. Sprinkle a little bit Lemon Juice over the Chicken and Sauce ( Optional Step )
Voila Enjoy !!!
