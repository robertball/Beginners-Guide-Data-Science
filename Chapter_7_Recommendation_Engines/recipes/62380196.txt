Title:
German Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1 C. milk
1 C flour
1/4 t. salt
1 cube butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450Â°
In blender or bowl, mix eggs, milk, flour, and salt
Melt butter in 9x13 pan in oven.
Remove pan from oven and pour in batter.
Return to oven and bake 18 min.
Pancake should be high and fluffy.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
A light, fluffy pancake baked in the oven. This is especially tasty with a touch of cinnimon.
