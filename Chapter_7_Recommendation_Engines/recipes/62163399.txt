Title:
Out of this World Kalbi Beef Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup water
1 Cup La Choy Soy Sauce
1 Cup Sugar
(4) 1/4" thick slices of ginger root
1/4 Cup chopped green onions
2 TBS Sesame oil
2 TBS Roasted Sesame Seeds
6-8 lbs cross cut flaken beef ribs

Directions:
Mix water, soy and sugar in bowl till sugar is diluted
add rest of ingredients and mix
Wash ribs to remove bone chips
Place ribs in marinade for no more than 4 hours
Pre-heat grill
Cook ribs until medium rare

Submitter: 
Cooking from the Hip
Description: 
Although it's not authentic, I never have complaints or left overs... This is one of my families favorites.
