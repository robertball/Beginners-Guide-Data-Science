Title:
Summertime Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982894.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 pound sushi-grade tuna, diced
1 avocado, diced
1 pint strawberries, diced
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 small lime
1 French baguette, sliced
2 tablespoons olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Toss tuna, avocado and strawberries in a large bowl with 1 Tbsp. of oil and juice of one lime.
Brush baguettes with remaining oil and broil in oven for about 3 minutes, or until lightly brown.
Spread tuna mix on baguettes and serve immediately.
