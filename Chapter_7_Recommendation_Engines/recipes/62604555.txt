Title:
Creamy Linguine With Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 ounces Linguine
12 ounces raw shrimp
1 large thinly sliced red bell pepper
1 large thinly sliced green bell pepper
1 cup peas
3 cloves minced garlic
1 1/2 cup fat free plain yogurt
1 tbs skim milk powder
1/4 cup fresh chopped parsley
1/4 cup fresh chopped cilantro
1 tbs extra virgin olive oil
1 1/2 tsp basil
1 1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground pepper

Directions:
Bring Water to a boil. Add linguine. 3 minutes before it's done, add raw shrimp, red pepper, green pepper / broccoli and peas.
Drain completely when pasta is done.
Add remaining ingredients. Cook until sauce sticks to pasta.

Submitter: 
J9
Description: 
Toss a garlicky, Middle Eastern-inspired yogurt sauce with pasta, shrimp, green pepper, peas and red bell pepper for a fresh, satisfying summer meal. Serve with: Slices of cucumber and tomato tossed with lemon juice and olive oil.
