Title:
Pulled Pork Breakfast Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3780106.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon coconut oil or unsalted butter
1 large sweet potato, diced (about 1 cup)
1 red bell pepper, seeded and diced
1 yellow onion, diced
1 clove garlic, minced
1 teaspoon sea salt
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
2 tablespoons fresh herbs

Directions:
In a large saut√© pan melt coconut oil or butter.  Once it is hot add in sweet potato and let cook for about 5 minutes.  Add in red pepper, onions and garlic.  Cook for about 5-7 minutes then season with salt and pepper.
Add in pulled pork, cover and reduce to medium.  Cook for 5-7 minutes.  Add in fresh herbs before serving.

Submitter: 
Cindy Anschutz Barbieri
Description: 
Here is another great recipe to create from my 5 ingredient pulled pork.  This goes great with poached eggs.

I loved the crispy edges that were created by using my cast iron pan!
