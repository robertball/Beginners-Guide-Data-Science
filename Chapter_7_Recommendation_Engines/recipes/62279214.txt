Title:
Fish and Spinach Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/349875.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package frozen spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
1 cup sharp Cheddar cheese
1/4 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1 egg, beaten
1 pound white fish fillets
1/2 cup french fried onions
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
In a bowl, mix the spinach, 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese,  bread crumbs, and the egg. Spread the mixture into the bottom of a small baking dish. Arrange the fillets on top of the spinach mixture, and season with salt and pepper. Top with the remaining Cheddar cheese and french fried onions
Cover, and bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until fish flakes easily with a fork.

Submitter:

Description: 
Spinach is topped with any white fish, then a layer of cheese and french fried onions is added.Modified from: Aunt Carol's Spinach and Fish Bake
