Title:
Slow Cooker Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 red potatoes, sliced
1 1/2 pounds boneless pork chops
S/P, garlic powder, onion powder
Olive Oil
1 sweet onion, sliced
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/4 cup milk
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 pckg frozen green beans

Directions:
Slice potatoes and place in bottom of slow cooker.
Genorously season chops with salt, pepper, garlic powder,and onion powder.  Brown on each side in small amount of olive oil.  Reserve pan drippings.  Place pork chops on top of potatoes in slow cooker.  Top chops with sliced sweet onions.
Pour soup into pork chop browning pan. Stir in milk and 1 tsp black pepper, scrapping up drippings. Pour over chops, potatoes and onions.
Cover, and cook on Low for 6 hours.
Add green beans and cook on low for 1 hour.
Stir, remove from heat, and serve.
*Can cook all ingredients together on high for 3 hours.

Submitter: 
Shannon
Description: 
Use your slow cooker to make these creamy and comforting pork chops. Adding green beans and potatoes to lightly seasoned pork chops in cream of mushroom soup makes this a hearty one-pot meal.Modified from: Slow Cooker Chops
