Title:
Mom's Sausage Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-pound hamburger
1-pound Bob Evans sausage.
1-large onion chopped
2-4oz cans chopped green chiles
2-crushed cloves of garlic
1-cup celery chopped
2-15.5oz cans red kidney beans
1-1 pound 12oz can diced tomatoes
1-6oz can tomato paste
2-teaspoons chili powder
2-teaspoons salt

Directions:
Brown sausage and ground beef in a large pot till nicely browned.
Put chopped onions, celery and crushed garlic in with the meat and continue to saute till vegtables are soft.
Add in the chile peppers, tomato paste, diced tomatoes and kidney beans, all undrained, and blend the mixture.
Season with salt and chili powder.
Simmer on the stovetop for 1-2 hours on very low heat careful not to burn. Stir ocasionally.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
This was a family favorite when my children were growing up! We always served it with shedded cheddar cheese and chopped onions. Oh and dont forget the fresh made corn muffins!
