Title:
Mini Chocolate Chip Breakfast Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup rolled oats
scant 1/2 cup applesauce OR mashed banana
1/4 cup roasted cashew butter OR allergy-friendly alternative
1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract
1/16 tsp salt
1-2 tbsp mini chocolate chips or chocolate chunks
2 1/2 tbsp freeze-dried raspberries (or 1 tbsp dried fruit OR more chocolate chips)
pinch Stevia OR 1 tbsp sugar (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mash applesauce or banana with nut butter, then add all other ingredients and mix until well combined.
Shape into cookies using a cookie scoop and bake on greased cookie sheet for 14-16 minutes.
Let cool 10 minutes before removing from sheet.
