Title:
Yukon Gold and Sweet Potato Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 T butter, room temperature, divided
2 1/4 ponds Yukon Gold potatoes,rinsed
1 1/2 pounds, red-skinned sweet potatoes, peeled
2 cups whole milk
1 garlic clove, pressed
1 T kosher salt
2 t fresh thyme
1 t ground black pepper
1/8 t ground nutmeg
1 c whipping cream

Directions:
Peheat oven to 400.   Coat a 13x9x2 inch glass baking dish with 2 T butter.  Thinly slice all potatoes, place in prepared dish.  Bring milk and next 5 ingredients to a boil in medium suacepan; pour over potatoes.  Dot with 2 tablespoons butter. Cover with foil.  Bake until potatoes are tender and milk is almost absorbed, about 50 minutes.
Bring cream to a boil in suacepan.  Uncover potatoes, pour cream over, and dot with 2 T  butter.  Bake uncovered until top is brown in spots, about 25 minutes.  Cool slightly.
