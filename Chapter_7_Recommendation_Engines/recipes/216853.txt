Title:
Easy Tomato-Basil Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990593.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 inch) ready-to-use baked pizza crust
1/2 cup PHILADELPHIA Italian Cheese and Herb Cooking Creme
1 tomato, thinly sliced
8 leaves fresh basil leaves, torn
1/2 cup KRAFT Shredded Mozzarella Cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 425 degrees F.
Spread pizza crust with cooking creme; top with remaining ingredients.
Place crust directly on middle oven rack.
Bake 10 to 12 min. or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
PHILADELPHIA Cooking Creme
Description: 
This pizza--made with PHILADELPHIA Italian Cheese and Herb Cooking Creme--has a delightful creamy base, topped with tomatoes, basil and mozzarella.
