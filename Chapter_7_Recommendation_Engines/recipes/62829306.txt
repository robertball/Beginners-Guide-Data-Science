Title:
Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Walnut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups rolled oats (don't use steel cut)
1 cup spelt flour
1/4 tsp sea salt
1/4 tsp baking powder
2/3 cup real maple syrup
1/2 cup melted butter (unsalted)
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 egg
1/2 cup chopped walnuts or almonds
1/2 cup good quality dark chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine oats, flour, salt and baking powder together in a bowl; set aside
In separate bowl, mix together maple syrup, butter, vanilla, and egg
Stir in nuts and chips
Drop onto cookie sheet
Bake 8-10 minutes until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Paula DiBacco
Description: 
No white sugar is used in this cookie recipe.  The ingredients used are all organic.
