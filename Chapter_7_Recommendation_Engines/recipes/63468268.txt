Title:
Slow Cooker Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
2 tablespoons instant minced onion
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
8 ounces tomato sauce
1 1/2 teaspoons Italian seasoning (Oregeno, Basil, Rosemary)
3 cups tomato juice
4 ounces spaghetti, broken into pieces

Directions:
Brown beef and place in crockpot.
Add all remaining ingredients except spaghetti and stir well.
Cover and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours.
Turn to high the last hour and stir in spaghetti.

Submitter: 
Sidnie Anderson
Description: 
You can make tomato juice with 1 part water to 1 part tomato juice or paste.
