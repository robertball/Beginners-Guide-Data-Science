Title:
Snicker Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
1 c. brown sugar
1 c. butter
1 c. peanut butter
1 (16 oz.) pkg. SNICKERS® bars, miniature size
2 tsp. vanilla extract
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. soda
3 c. all purpose flour
2 eggs, beaten

Directions:
Cream together sugar, brown sugar, butter and peanut butter. Add vanilla extract and eggs; mix. Add dry ingredients. Cut candy bars in thirds. Wrap dough around each piece forming a ball.
Bake on ungreased cookie sheet at 350°F for 12-18 minutes or until golden brown.
