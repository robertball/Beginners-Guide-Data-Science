Title:
No Bake Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. white sugar
1/3 c. cocoa
1/2 c. margarine
1/2 c. milk
1/2 t. vanilla
1/2 c. peanut butter
3 c. quick oatmeal

Directions:
Place sugar, cocoa, milk, and margarin in a pan; bring to a boil and boil HARD for 2 minutes.
Remove from heat and add vanilla and peanut butter.
Blend well, then stir in oatmeal.
Drop by spoonfuls onto waxed paper and let cool.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
Personal family favorite; I always triple the batch, otherwise they're gone before they even harden!
