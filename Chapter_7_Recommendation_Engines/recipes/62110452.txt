Title:
Baked Crouton Eggplant Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Small/Medium Eggplant (Japanese)
1 Egg
2 Pinches Salt
1/2 tsp. Ground Black Pepper
2 cup Garlic Croutons
1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
1/2 (16oz.) Package Prated Mozzarella Cheese
1-1/2 cup Pasta Sauce
1 tsp. Garlic powder

Directions:
For CROUTON CRUMBS:
Food process croutons for 30 seconds until coarsely ground
Set aside in plate
In a bowl: mix egg, salt, and pepper
Slice eggplant into 1/2" slices
Coat eggplant slices in egg mixture
Transfer to plate with crouton crumbs and coat evenly on both sides
Transfer to oven-safe pan covered with foil/wax paper
Bake in oven at 350 degrees until golden brown on both sides (around 10-15 minutes each)
Mix garlic powder with sauce
Pour half the sauce and half the parmesan into the another oven-safe dish (with high sides)
Transfer baked eggplant slices unto sauce/parmesan in oven-safe dish
Coat with layers in the following order: mozzarella cheese, rest of sauce, parmesan cheese
Bake in oven at 350 degrees for 25 minutes or until parmesan starts to get light golden brown

Description: 
This is a recipe for those who have never cooked eggplant before and want to try it. This is very simple to make and also very quick. Very fool-proof!!
