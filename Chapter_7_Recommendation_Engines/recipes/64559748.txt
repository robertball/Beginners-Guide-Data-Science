Title:
Amber's Quick Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground Beef
1/2 cup Onion, chopped
1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 can Beef Broth
1 pkg. Philadelphia Cream Cheese, cubed
Cooked wide egg noodles or rice

Directions:
Brown ground beef together with onion in a skillet.  Drain off excess fat.
Add cream of mushroom soup and beef broth, stirring over medium heat until well mixed.
Add cream cheese.  Continue cooking over low heat, stirring constantly.
Serve over cooked rice or wide egg noodles.

Submitter: 
Kevin G
Description: 
Amber's quick and easy ground beef version of a beef stroganoff one skillet dish.  Serve over rice or wide egg noodles
