Title:
Melt-in-your-mouth Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C baking mix
1 C milk
1 T sugar
2 T lemon juice
2 t. baking powder
2 eggs

Directions:
Mix ingredients until blended.
Pour onto hot greased skillet. (Usual size uses just less than 1/4 C.)
Cook until edges are dry. Turn; cook until golden.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
This recipe makes the lightest, fluffiest pancakes ever.
