Title:
Spiced Shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup butter, melted
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. ginger
1/8 tsp. ground cloves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 275.
Mix flour and spices together.
In separate bowl, cream butter and sugars.
Add dry ingredients; stir till evenly mixed.
Press into greased 9" square pan.
Bake for 40 minutes or till lightly browned.
Cut into slices while still warm.

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
I recommend serving this delicious shortbread warm with vanilla ice cream or a fruit topping.  My husband and I love it with fresh sliced mango!
