Title:
Curry Chicken with mango sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts, skinless and boneless
1/2 tsp. salt
2 TB olive oil
2 TB butter
1 medium onion, diced
2 garlic cloves, diced
1 tsp. ground ginger
1 tsp. curry powder
1/2 tsp. ground coriander
6 oz. pimentos, drained
1 large mango, cubed
1-1/4 cups chicken broth
1 TB cornstarch

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat butter and olive oil, making sure not to burn the butter
Pound chicken breasts to 1/4 inch thick and add salt to chicken
Place chicken in skillet and brown and cook each side for approximately 4 minutes until no longer pink inside or internal temperature reaches 170 degrees.
Remove and keep warm
In the same skillet, add onion and saute until soft
Add garlic, ginger, curry powder and coriander and stir fry for about 1 minute, making sure not to burn the garlic.
Add mango and stir fry for about 1 minute
Mix the cornstarch with the chicken broth until smooth
Turn the heat up and add cornstarch mixture and stir constantly to thicken sauce.
Return chicken to skillet in the sauce

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
Skinless, boneless chicken breasts are served with a slightly sweet mango sauce with a touch of curry.  This is a quick and easy recipe with alot of flavor.  I served it with brown rice.
