Title:
LOMI LOMI SALMON Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb         fresh wild caught salmon
5 large        Roma tomatoes, diced
1 small bunch  green onions, chopped
1 large        maui or other sweet onion, finely chopped
1 cup          crushed ice

Directions:
Soak salmon in water for 30 minutes. Remove skin and bones; shred with your hands ( this is lomi - massage). Combine salmon, tomatoes, ice and onions; lomi until well mixed. Chill thoroughly. Makes 6 servings.

Submitter: 
Mama Smith
Description: 
Cold salmon dish that is a part of every luau.
