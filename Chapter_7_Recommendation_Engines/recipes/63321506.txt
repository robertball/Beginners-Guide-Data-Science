Title:
5 spice chicken thighs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c. soy sauce
1/4 oil
1 t. garlic
2 t. chinese 5 spice
1 t. ginger
1 T. sherry
3 lbs. chicken

Directions:
Mix ingredients.
Put chicken in marinade for an hour and then bake at 375 for 45 minutes or cook on the grill.
