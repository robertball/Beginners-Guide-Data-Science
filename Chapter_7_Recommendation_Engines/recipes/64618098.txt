Title:
Escalloped Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter
1 can Creamed Corn
1 Can of whole kernel corn
2 eggs, beaten
1 C Sour Cream
1 pkg. Jiffy corn muffing mix

Directions:
Mix corn and creamed corn together with eggs and sour cream. Melt margarine in 8x12 baking dish. Add to corn mixture. Stir in one package Jiffy corn muffin mix. Bake 40 min at 350 or until firm to the touch.

Submitter: 
Andrea Shreve Wood
Description: 
A delicious side dish that goes right along on the holidays with Green Bean Casserole! A little southern and an old family favorite. Hope you enjoy it as much as we have throughout the years.
