Title:
Jalapeno & Cheese Bubble Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12oz. can refrigerated biscuits, such as Grands Jr.Â® Golden Layers Buttermilk biscuits.
3 Tablespoons butter, melted
1 8oz. block pepper jack cheese, shredded
1/4 cup drained pickled diced jalapenos, or more to taste
1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees; spray 9x5-inch loaf pan with cooking spray.
Cut each biscuit into 1/4's and dip 1/2 of them into the melted butter; place into prepared pan.
Top with 1/2 of the shredded cheese, 2 Tablespoons of the diced jalapenos and 1/4 teaspoon of the garlic powder; repeat layers.
Bake in preheated oven 35-40 minutes, until golden brown and done in the center.
Cool in pan 10 minutes, run a knife around the edge to loosen and remove from pan. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
Great use of a can of refrigerated biscuits! Goes great with taco salad. It's buttery, rich and delicious...Enjoy!
