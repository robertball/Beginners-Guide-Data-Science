Title:
Stuffed Manicotti with Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Whole rotisserie Chicken
1 Green Chilies
8oz Sour Cream
1 large tomato
1 large bell pepper
1/2 large onion
16oz mexican melting cheese
1 box manicotti shells

Directions:
Take off all of the white meat off of the rotisserie chicken (you can use the dark if you want) and shread into small pieces. Dice the onion, bell pepper and tomato into small chunks. Add the chicken, chilies, sour cream, tomato, bell pepper and onion in a large bowl fold together. In a large pot boil water and add the manicotti shells until tender. Take the boiled shells and stuff them with the chicken mixture. Place the stuffed shells into a glass baking dish and add the mexican melting cheese on top. Place in a preheated 375 oven for 20 minutes or until chesse is completely melted.
