Title:
Golden Sweet Cornbread - Vegan, Wheat-Free, Low-Fat & Low Glycemic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/964844.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup spelt flour
1 cup yellow cornmeal
1/4 cup agave
1/4 teaspoon vanilla
1 teaspoon salt
3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 Chia egg
1/2  cup almond milk
1/3 cup apple sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Spray or lightly grease a 9 inch round cake pan.
In a large bowl, combine flour, cornmeal, salt and baking powder.
In blender Chia egg, almond milk , vanilla, agave, and apple sauce until well combined.
Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean.
