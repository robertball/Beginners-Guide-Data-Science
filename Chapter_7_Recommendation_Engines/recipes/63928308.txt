Title:
Perfect Buttermilk Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups all-purpose flour
2 Tbsp baking powder
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1/4 cup white sugar
8 eggs
2 Tbsp vanilla extract
4 cups buttermilk
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup canola oil

Directions:
Measure butter and oil together in glass container and melt in microwave.  In medium bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, and baking soda. In large bowl, beat eggs until homogenous.  Add buttermilk, vanilla, salt, and sugar. Dump in the dry mixture and whisk until fully moistened, but still lumpy.  Pour in the oil/melted butter and stir until just combined.  Ladle into hot waffle iron and cook a few minutes, to desired doneness.

This is my version of buttermilk waffles for a crowd!
