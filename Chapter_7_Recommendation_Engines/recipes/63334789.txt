Title:
Oven Scrambled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp butter
6 eggs
1/4 teaspoons salt
6 Tbsp milk

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Pour melted butter into a small glass baking dish. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs and salt until well blended. Gradually whisk in milk. Pour egg mixture into the baking dish.
Bake uncovered for 10 minutes, then stir, and bake an additional 10 to 15 minutes, or until eggs are set. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Melissa Gardner
Description: 
These light and fluffy scrambled eggs  are a snap to put together for a big crowd. I usually make 2 pans for our Christmas Brunch, and I never have much left over!Modified from: Oven Scrambled Eggs
