Title:
Vegetable Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/532705.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pkgs crescent rolls
2 8 oz., pkgs cream cheese, softened
1/2 c. mayonnaise or miracle whip
1 pkg knorr vegetable soup mix
6 c. chopped fresh veggies
Veggie suggestions (the ones we use have a star): *Radishes, roma tomatoes, *green, red, and yellow pepper, *broccoli, *cauliflower, *carrots, black or green olives, mushrooms, green onions, *celery

Directions:
THE DAY BEFORE SERVING Spread crescent rolls together in the bottom of a 11x14" shallow baking tray. Bake at 375ยบ F for about 10 minutes until done. Allow to cool.
Mix together cream cheese, mayonnaise, and dressing packet. Spread over cooled crust.
Top with chopped veggies, pressing them into the cheese mixture to stick.
Cut into squares, cover with plastic wrap and keep refrigerated.
