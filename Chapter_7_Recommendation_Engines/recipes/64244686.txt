Title:
Instant pot pinto beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pinto beans
bay leaf
1/2 large onion
4 cloves garlic
1 pkg lipton onion soup
2 sams apple wood cured pork chops
2 tbl ham seasoning

Directions:
Put all in instant pot and cover with water 2" above.  Do not go over 1/2 the capacity.  Cook on chili/bean setting for 40 min.
Do not soak the beans.  Remove the onions.
