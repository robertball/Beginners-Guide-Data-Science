Title:
Slow Cooker Cilantro Lime Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) jar salsa
cumin
chili powder
1 lime, juiced
3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
3 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
tortillas
shredded cheese

Directions:
Place the salsa, chili powder and cumin, lime juice, and cilantro into a slow cooker, and stir to combine. Add the chicken breasts, and stir to coat with the salsa mixture. Cover the cooker, set to High, and cook until the chicken is very tender, about 4 hours. If desired, set cooker to Low and cook 6 to 8 hours. Shred chicken with 2 forks to serve.

Submitter: 
messyjessi, RD

Chicken breasts, slow-cooked in Mexican-inspired cilantro-lime salsa, makes tasty taco filling.Modified from: Slow Cooker Cilantro Lime Chicken
