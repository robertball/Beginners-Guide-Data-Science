Title:
Fields of Green Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 package refrigerated pizza crust dough
1/2 cup tomato sauce
1 cup shredded Mozzarella
1 small head lettuce, chopped
1/2 small tomato, chopped
1/2 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing
Other favorite salad toppings, optional (optional)

Directions:
Spread pizza crust on baking sheet and bake according to package directions.  Spread tomato sauce evenly; add cheese.  Bake pizza until cheese is melted.
Toss lettuce and tomato with Ranch and favorite salad toppings.  Place on top of baked pizza and serve.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
Your favorite salad fixings tossed with Ranch dressing top this pizza for a fast and easy meal.
