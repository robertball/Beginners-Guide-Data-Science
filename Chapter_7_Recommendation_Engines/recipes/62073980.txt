Title:
Spaghetti Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 oz spaghetti noodles (cooked)
1 beaten egg
1/3 c parmesan cheese
mix above 3 together, this is the "crust"  put into a pie pan
then mix
15 oz ricotta cheese
1 lb cooked ground beef
15 oz spaghetti sauce

Directions:
Pour on top of noodles, top with mozzerella cheese and bake at 350 for 20-30 minutes

Submitter: 
TINA B
Description: 
This is not your basic spaghetti!  Similar to lasagna, but a lot easier to make.  This is so delicious and easy!
