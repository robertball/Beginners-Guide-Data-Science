Title:
Spring Max Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/957667.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound box rotini pasta
1 pound lean ground beef
3 beef bouillon cubes
2 cooking onions
2-3 cloves garlic, chopped
8 oz can tomato sauce
46 oz bottle tomato-vegetable juice
2 tsp chili powder
1 tsp garlic salt
pinch of paprika
salt & pepper, to taste
garlic powder, to taste

Directions:
In a large, heavy pot brown and crumble the ground beef, add in the onions and garlic and cook until softened; drain.
Add in the tomato sauce, tomato juice, beef bouillon, and spices, bring to a boil while stirring.
Simmer to reduce for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Meanwhile, cook the pasta according to package directions.
When sauce had reduced, add in the pasta and mix well.
Serve hot.

Description: 
My grandma and mom used to make this all the time. I never cared for it as a child, but now that I'm grown I get a craving for it every once in a while. It's a simple, saucy meal that feeds a crowd.
