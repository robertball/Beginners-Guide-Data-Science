Title:
Austrian Vanille Kipferl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup white sugar
3/4 cup ground almonds
1 cup unsalted butter
pinch of salt
5 drops Almond flavor
1/4 cup vanilla sugar
1/4 cup confectioners' sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F .Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
Combine flour, 1/2 cup sugar, and ground almonds. Cut in butter with pastry blender, then quickly knead into a dough in the bowl till the dough forms a round ball . Cover with Saran wrap and refrigerate 1/2 hour.
Use half of the dough at a time, Shape dough into logs and cut off 1/2-inch pieces. Shape each piece into a crescent and place on prepared baking sheet.
Bake in preheated oven until edges are golden brown, 30 minutes. Cool 5 minute and carefully roll in vanilla sugar mixture. Plain powdered sugar is fine also
