Title:
Marinated Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887015.jpg
Ingredients:
5 medium tomatoes, sliced
1/4 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
3/4 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 tablespoon fresh parsley, minced
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a large shallow dish, layer the tomatoes and mushrooms.
In a bowl, whisk the oil, vinegar, garlic, parsley and salt.
Pour over vegetables.
Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, turning occasionally.
Serve with a slotted spoon.
