Title:
Ciroc Champagne Cosmo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980262.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce Ciroc® Vodka
1 tablespoon Grand Marnier liqueur
1 1/2 fluid ounces cranberry juice
1/2 fluid ounce fresh lime juice
1 twist orange peel
1 fluid ounce Champagne

Directions:
Shake Ciroc(R) Vodka, Grand Marnier liqueur, cranberry juice, and lime juice in a cocktail shaker filled with ice.
Strain into a chilled champagne flute.
Top with Champagne.
Garnish with orange twist.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Elegant cocktails taste as refined as they look when made with Ciroc® vodka, an uncommon vodka with subtle aromatics made from grapes.
