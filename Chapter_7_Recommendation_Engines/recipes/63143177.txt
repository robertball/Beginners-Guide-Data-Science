Title:
BBQ Salmon with Mango Peach Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds salmon fillets
1/3 cup barbecue sauce
2 whole mangos

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Brush salmon fillets with barbecue sauce and bake until fish is cooked through, but not dry (time will vary depending on the size of the fillets).
Dice mangos and serve with the salmon.
