Title:
Meat Lover's Barbecued Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977951.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 1/2-pound) beef flank steak, 1 inch thick
1 clove garlic, halved
1 large onion, cut into 1/4-inch slices
1 cup KRAFT Original Barbecue Sauce

Directions:
Rub each side of steak with halved garlic clove.
Place steak and onion on greased grill over medium-hot coals.
Grill, uncovered, 15 to 20 minutes or until steak is cooked through, brushing steak and onion frequently with barbecue sauce and turning occasionally.  Serve steak with onions.

Submitter:

Description: 
Wow your family and guests and make grilling easy with this tasty dish.
