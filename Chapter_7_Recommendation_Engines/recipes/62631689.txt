Title:
Chicken Enchiladas I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 onion, chopped
1/2 pint sour cream
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 tablespoon dried parsley
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
1 (14-16 ounce) jar salsa
small can diced green chiles
1 clove garlic, minced
8 (10 inch) flour tortillas
1 (10 ounce) can enchilada sauce
3/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Cook chicken: put water, chick broth in pan and bring to low boil.  Add chicken and cook 15 min.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Cube or shred chicken and return it to the skillet/drained pot. Add the onion, sour cream, Cheddar cheese, parsley, oregano and ground black pepper. Heat until cheese melts. Stir in salsa, chiles and garlic.
Roll even amounts of the mixture in the tortillas. Arrange in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Cover with enchilada sauce and 3/4 cup Cheddar cheese. Bake uncovered in the preheated oven 20 minutes. Cool 10 minutes before serving.
