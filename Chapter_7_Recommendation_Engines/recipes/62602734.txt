Title:
Chicken and Vegetable Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/580101.jpg
Ingredients:
Chicken/Veggies:
2 tbsp vegetable oil
1 large skinless boneless chicken breast, cut into strips
3/4 of onion, chopped
1 C thinly roll-cut carrots
2 C chopped broccoli
2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1 1/2 tsp minced chives
1/2 tsp minced basil
1/2 tsp minced parsley
Rice:
1 1/2 C uncooked white or brown rice
2 cups water
(or see directions on rice package)
Sauce:
1 tbsp soy sauce (or 2 tbsp, depending on taste)
2 tbsp corn starch
1 C chicken broth
1 tbsp minced garlic

Directions:
In large fry pan over medium heat, sautee chicken with onion in oil until browned (5 min). Add Worcestershire sauce and spices.
Bring small pot of water to a boil. Add rice and simmer until all water is absorbed.
Meanwhile, add carrots and broccoli to chicken. Cook until vegetables are tender.
Mix soy sauce, corn starch, chicken broth, and garlic in small mixing bowl with whisk. Stir into chicken and veggie mixture.
Bring to a boil, simmer 5-10 minutes until sauce is thickened. Serve over rice.
