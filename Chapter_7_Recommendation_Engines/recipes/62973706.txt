Title:
Figs & Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*1 6 oz package Near East Rice Pilaf (prepared in a separate pan by the box)
*3 tbsp butter
*1/2 c. cooked chicken (optional to omit for vegetarian, leftovers work well)
*6-10 dried figs, depending on size
*1/4 c. dried currants, craisins, or raisins.
*1 mango, peeled and diced
*1/4 - 1/2 c water
*2 tbsp half-and-half
*1/4 c cashews, chopped
*1/4 c. blue cheese, crumbled

Directions:
1) Prepare rice pilaf as directed on box.
2) In separate pan, melt butter over medium heat, and add chopped figs and dried fruit.  Toss to coat.  Heat for 2 mins.
3) Add mango and water, stir occasionally for several minutes until mango has softened and dried fruit has absorbed water.
4) Add cashews and half-and-half.  Mixture should resemble loose sauce.
5) Add crumbled blue cheese and serve over cooked pilaf.  Salt to preference
