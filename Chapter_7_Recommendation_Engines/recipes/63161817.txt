Title:
Overnight Fruit and Yogurt Steel Cut Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/811482.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 c. steel cut oats
1/4 c. milk
3 T. vanilla yogurt
2 peach slices
1/8 t. cinnamon

Directions:
place oats in a small container and pour milk over top. cover and place in refridgerator overnight. before serving stir in yogurt, top with peaches and cinnamon.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
Simply soak steel cut oats overnight in milk and mix with yogurt and fruit the next morning for a heart healthy wholesome breakfast. Soaking overnight leaves the oats chewy and delicious. Makes a great snack too.
