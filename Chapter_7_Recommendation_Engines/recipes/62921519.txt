Title:
Raw Avocado Cabbage Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups organic cabbage, finely sliced
1 organic red bell pepper, diced
1 organic yellow bell pepper, diced
2 avocados, diced
3 Tbs hemp oil (Flax oil works too)
1 small organic lemon, juiced
1 very small organic leek, diced
1/4 cup organic hemp seeds
2 Tbs fresh parsley
1/2 tsp sea salt

Directions:
1. In a large bowl, toss all ingredients together.
2. Mix well and serve.
