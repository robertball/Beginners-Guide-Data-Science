Title:
Asian-Style Lettuce wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb soy crumbles
1/2 cup chopped red bell
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 Tbl minced garlic
1 tsp fresh ginger (opt)
1/4 cup duck sauce
2 Tbl low sodium soy sauce
1 tsp sirracha
1 tsp sesame oil
romain leaves

Directions:
Use a capful or 2 of olive oil. cook onion, red bell & garlic until tender. Add crumbles & mix well. Reduce heat to low & add everything else, except lettuce.
Simmer up to 20min. Serve in lettuce leaves
