Title:
Yummy Saurkraut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 2 lb. bag of prepared Saurkraut
1 Granny Smith Apple chopped
2 t. Caraway Seeds

Directions:
Heat Saurkraut in lg. sauce pan over medium heat. Add chopped apple and Caraway Seeds and stir until well mixed. Bring to a low boil.  Serve hot.

Submitter: 
janaluwho
Description: 
When I was a kid, my parents couldn't get me to touch Saurkraut with a 10 foot pole. But now as an adult I love it!  It's a New Year's Day tradition around my house.
