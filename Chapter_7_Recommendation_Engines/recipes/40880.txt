Title:
Cinnamon Crisp Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985534.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) package Keebler® Cinnamon Crisp® Grahams
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup mixed nuts
1/2 cup raw sunflower seeds
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1/2 cup honey

Directions:
Break Cinnamon Crisp(R) into bite-size pieces. Spread Cinnamon Crisp(R) pieces, raisins, nuts, and sunflower seeds on large greased jelly roll pan, about 15 x 10 inches.
In small saucepan melt butter with honey; drizzle over graham mixture.
Bake in preheated 250 degrees F oven 45 minutes. Allow to cool in pan. Loosen mixture with metal spatula. Store in airtight container or jar.

Submitter: 
Keebler® Kitchens
Description: 
Keebler® Cinnamon Crisp® Grahams, raisins, mixed nuts, and sweet honey create a protein-packed snack perfect for every occasion.
