Title:
Big Daddy's Venison Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 onion, chopped
2 tablespoons Big Daddy's Roasted Garlic
1 pound venison, cut into 1" cubes
1 (28 ounce) can stewed tomatoes
1 (8 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
2 cups cut carrots
5 potatoes cubed
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoonsof MILA

Directions:
Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir the onion in the hot oil until the onion is translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the venison to the skillet; cook and stir until evenly browned. Place the venison in the slow cooker and cook 6 hours until tender. Stir the MILA, tomatoes, mushrooms, Big Daddy's Roasted Garlic, potatoes, carrots and salt into the mixture; cover the skillet and allow the mixture to simmer 30 to 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
BIG DADDY'S
Description: 
This simple venison stew uses stewed tomatoes and mushrooms for a quick and tasty meal.Modified from: Easy Venison Stew
