Title:
Lady Joy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982224.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 fluid ounce lemon rum
2/3 fluid ounce Blue Curacao
6 fluid ounces champagne
1 twist lemon zest, garnish

Directions:
In a champagne flute, pour the lemon rum and blue curacao. Fill glass with champagne and garnish with a twist of lemon.

Submitter: 
Kramer
Description: 
Lemon rum and blue curacao at the bottom of a flute of sparkling wine.
