Title:
Greek Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3T Olive Oil
1 onion, chopped
2-3T fresh garlic
2 pounds fresh spinach, rinsed and stemmed
2 Roma Tomatoes, chopped
2 cups chicken stock
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried parsley
salt and pepper to tast
1 cup jasmine rice
Lemon Wedges
Feta Cheese

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Saute onions in the oil until soft and translucent, add garlic and cook until fragrant. Add chicken stock, spices and tomatoes. Bring to a boil, stir in rice, reduce heat to low, and simmer uncovered until rice is nearly done.  Add spinach and recover, allowing spinach to wilt.
Gently fluff with a fork and serve with lemon wedges and feta.

Description: 
This is a Greek side dish that our family loves.  Add a little Feta cheese and fresh lemon after it's cooked and you have heaven.Modified from: Spinach and Rice (Spanakorizo)
