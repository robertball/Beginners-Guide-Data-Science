Title:
Big Ray's Chicken & Roasted Red Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1073784.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Ranch Dressing
6 large bone-less skin-less chicken breasts
8 slices Bacon
1-1/2 lb.  red potatoes (about 5), cut into 1-inch chunks
1  large onion, cut into 1/2-inch chunks
1 8 oz bag Shredded Cheddar Cheese
2 Tbsp.  chopped fresh parsley
Sour cream

Directions:
POUR dressing over chicken in shallow dish. Refrigerate 30 min. to overnight to marinate.
HEAT oven to 400ºF. Cook bacon in large skillet on medium heat until crisp. Remove bacon from skillet, reserving 1 Tbsp. drippings in skillet. Drain bacon on paper towels.
ADD potatoes and onions to reserved drippings; cook 5 min., stirring occasionally. Remove from heat. Crumble bacon. Add to potato mixture; mix lightly. Spoon into 13x9-inch baking dish. Remove chicken from marinade; discard marinade. Place chicken over potato mixture.
BAKE 55 min. to 1 hour or until potatoes are tender and chicken is done (165ºF). Top with cheese,parsley and sour cream.

All your favorites in one dish, potatoes, onions, bacon and chicken all baked together and topped with melty cheese.
