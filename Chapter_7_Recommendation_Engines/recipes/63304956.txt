Title:
Parmesan Chicken Cutlets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup parmesan cheese, grated
2 TBSP dried italian seasoned breadcrumbs
1/8 teaspoon paprika
1 tsp dried parsley
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp fresh ground pepper
4 boneless skinless chicken breast halves (about 1 lb.)

Directions:
Preheat oven to400 degrees.
In a resealable plastic bag, combine cheese, crumbs, and all seasonings; shake well.
Transfer mixture to plate; dip each chicken breast in cheese mixture, turning to coat all sides.
Arrange on a nonstick baking sheet (or use parchment).
Bake until chicken is cooked through, 20-25 minutes.
