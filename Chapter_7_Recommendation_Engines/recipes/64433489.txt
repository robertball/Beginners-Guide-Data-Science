Title:
Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2339576.jpg
Ingredients:
3 or 4 overripe bananas
1 1/4 cups sugar
1 stick butter melted and slightly cooled
2 eggs room temperature
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees. Place bananas and sugar in a bowl and mix for about 2-3 minutes. Add the butter, eggs and vanilla, whipping well and scraping  down the sides. Add flour, salt, baking soda and nuts and combine.
Pour into a lightly greased 9x5x3 pan. Bake for about 1 hour or until golden brown and cake tester comes out clean. Cool for about 10 minutes and remove from pan and cool completely.

Submitter: 
Sandy Wall- Krusinski
Description: 
This a wonderfully moist banana bread. It's a great way to use old bananas. The smell while it's baking is heavenly.
