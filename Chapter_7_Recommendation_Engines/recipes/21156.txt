Title:
People Chow Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1070101.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (17.5 ounce) package crispy rice cereal squares
1/2 cup butter
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
3/4 cup peanut butter
4 cups confectioners' sugar

Directions:
Pour cereal into a medium paper bag.  In a medium saucepan over medium heat, melt butter with chocolate chips and peanut butter, stirring until smooth.  Pour chocolate mixture into bag with cereal, seal and shake well.  Pour in confectioners' sugar and shake well again.  Store in an air tight container.

Submitter: 
Liz Deswert McCormick
Description: 
Crispy cereal squares covered in chocolate, peanut butter and confectioners' sugar -- it's people chow.
