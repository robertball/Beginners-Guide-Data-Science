Title:
Pear Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ripe pear
1/2 cup white wine
1 garlic clove
2 t dijon mustard
1/4 cup white balsamic vinegar(or unseasoned rice vinegar)
1t ground black pepper
1/4 t sea salt
1/2 cup olive oil

Directions:
add first seven ingredients to food processor until well blended drizzle in the olive oil until imulsified.

Submitter: 
Jill Tall
Description: 
This is a light and lovely salad dressing that lends itself well to a simple and elegant salad. I love to use it on a salad of; butter lettuce, Arugala, goat cheese & carmelized pecans simply delicious!
