Title:
Ginger Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. ground ginger
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. salt
dash ground cloves
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup margarine, softened
2/3 cup molasses
1 egg
3/4 boiling water
whipped topping

Directions:
Mix flour, baking soda, ginger, cinnamon, salt and cloves; set aside.
Cream sugar and margarine; add molasses and egg, mixing well.
Stir in dry ingredients alternately with water; mix well.
Pour into a greases 9" square pan.
Bake @ 350 x 35 - 40 min. until tests done.
Cool completely (although I've served it warm - yum!).
Serve with whipped topping or ice cream.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A moist, dark cake with a little kick from ginger.
