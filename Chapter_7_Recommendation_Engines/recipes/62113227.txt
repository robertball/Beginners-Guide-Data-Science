Title:
Chicken Curry Salad in a Hurry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Rotisserie Chicken, precooked
2 hard boiled eggs, chopped
1/2 cup mayo
1/2 cup chopped apple
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup chopped onions
1 tsp curry
1/2 tsp paprika
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
salt to taste

Directions:
Easy! Take the rotisserie chicken and pull off all the meat and some of that yummy skin, and chop the chicken and skin.
Next ... add all the ingredients into a bowl, and mix! Give it an hour to chill in the refrigerator before serving. It's just that simple!

Submitter: 
Buddy Greer
Description: 
Just a simple no frills chicken salad recipe I came up with .. can be made quickly and easily with most ingredients on hand. Enjoy it on bread, in a pita pocket, or over a bed of lettuce.
