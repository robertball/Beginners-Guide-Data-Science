Title:
Strawberry Muddy Buddies Valentines Treat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups Rice Chex Mix
12 oz melting white chips/ bark
2 cups strawberry cake mix
1 cup powder sugar
M&M . red hearts or other candy wish to add ( red & white candy corn)

Directions:
in lg zip lock bag combine cake mix and pow sugar shake to mix
melt chocolate in microwave stirring
in lg bow place chex and pour melted choc over them, stir to coat
add cake mix and other candies...stir to coat well
