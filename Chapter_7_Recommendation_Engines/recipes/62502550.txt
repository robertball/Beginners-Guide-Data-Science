Title:
Slow cooker beef stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. sirloin steak, cut into 1‚Äù cubes
1 tablespoon cooking oil
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 gloves garlic, minced
8 ounces sliced mushrooms
1 cup beef broth
2 tablespoons tomato paste
2 tablespoons cooking sherry
8 ounces sour cream
4 tablespoons cornstarch
1/4 cup beef broth chilled

Directions:
Crock Pot Beef Stroganoff
Serves: 4
Brown beef in skillet in hot oil; drain.
Combine beef, onion, garlic, and mushrooms in the crock pot. Combine tomato paste with beef broth, add sherry. Pour ingredients in the crock pot.  Cook on low for approximately 6 1/2 hours.
Whisk cornstarch into chilled, 1/4 cup beef broth and add sour cream. Stir to mix well. Add to crock pot, stirring again. Cover and cook for an additional 30-40 minutes. Do not allow it to boil. It will thicken as it cools.
Can be served over rice or buttered egg noodles.
