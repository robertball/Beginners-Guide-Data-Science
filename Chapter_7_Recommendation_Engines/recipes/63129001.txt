Title:
Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/799509.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pre-cooked rotisserie style chicken
1 family size bag of steamer broccoli florets, steamed for 3 minutes
1- 10 3/4 oz can of cream of mushroom soup
1-12oz can of evaporated milk
11/2 cups shredded cheddar or Colby-jack cheese
1/2 - 1 cup fried onions (I use French's)

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.
Shred chicken into a 9"x13" pan.  Add broccoli florets.
Mix together soup and condensed milk. (I pour mine into a shaker bottle and mix).
Pour soup mixture over chicken and broccoli.
Cover with shredded cheese.
Bake at 350 for 45 minutes.  Sprinkle fried onions on top and bake an additional 5 minutes or until onions are crispy.  Let stand for 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Dani Schulman
Description: 
Quick and easy chicken casserole that everyone will love!  My kids ask for this for their birthday dinners when they get to pick the menu!
