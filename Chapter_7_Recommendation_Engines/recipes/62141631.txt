Title:
Easy Lebanese Garlic Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large cloves garlic
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup mayonaise (low-fat works as well)
1/3 cup sour cream

Directions:
Squeeze garlic through a garlic press and whisk in lemon juice and salt.  Whisk in mayo and sour cream until smooth, adding more salt to suit your taste if needed.  Refrigerate until ready to use.

Submitter: 
Noor
Description: 
After a failed attempt to make it homemade, I whipped up this quick and easy clone that is great with all things grilled.
