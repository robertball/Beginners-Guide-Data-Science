Title:
Tomato and Olive Soup with Basil Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 small onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp fennel seeds
1 tsp oregano
1 6 oz can tomato paste
1 28 oz can whole tomatoes in juice
3 cups chicken broth
5 oz pitted ripe olives
1/2 cup cream
3 Tbsp fresh basil, minced

Directions:
Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat.  Add onion, garlic, fennel and oregano:  cover and simmer about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Add tomatoes and broth;  cover and simmer 15 minutes.  Stir olives into tomato mixture.  cover and continue to simmer 15 minutes.  Ladle soup into serving bowls.  Whip cream until doubled in volume.  Stir in basil.  Spoon over soup.
