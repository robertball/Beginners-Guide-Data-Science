Title:
Vegan Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5268493.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium head cabbage, finely sliced
2 large carrots, peeled and shredded
1 teaspoon salt
6 tablespoons sunflower seed oil
4 tablespoons white vinegar
1 tablespoon confectioners' sugar
1 teaspoon caraway seeds
freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine cabbage and carrots in a large bowl, season with salt, and allow to sit for 30 to 45 minutes.
Squeeze out as much liquid as possible from cabbage and carrots with your hands; drain well.
Stir together oil, vinegar, confectioners' sugar, caraway seeds, and pepper and pour over the salad. Mix well. Allow to sit to intensify the flavor, about 4 hours.

Submitter: 
westcoastgirl
Description: 
A simple vegan coleslaw without any mayo. I add caraway seeds to my coleslaw as it helps with digestion. It tastes best after it has marinated for a few hours.
