Title:
Grilled New York Strip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Two New York Strip Steaks (8-12 ounces each)
Rub:
1 Tbsp Kosher salt
2 tsp brown sugar
1/4 tsp cornstarch
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp garlic salt
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp turmeric
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp chili powder
1 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Combine ingredients for steak rub in ziploc sandwich bag, shake to mix.
Rub steaks with seasoning and let stand a room temperature for 1 hour.
Preheat gas grill on high for 10-15 min. Oil grates with paper towel soaked in canola oil. Maintain gas grill temperature at approximately 400 degrees (medium-high to high heat).
Place steaks vertically on grill and grill for 90 seconds. Turn steaks 90 degrees (horizontally) and grill for another 90 seconds. Flip and repeat.
Reduce temperature to medium and continue to grill until internal temperature reaches 145 degrees (an additional 2-3 min).
Remove steaks from grill and let rest for 5-10 minutes before serving.
