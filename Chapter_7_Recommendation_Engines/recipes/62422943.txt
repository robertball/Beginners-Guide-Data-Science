Title:
Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/446014.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
2 heaping tablespoons homemade taco seasoning mix
1 head iceberg lettuce, shredded
1 red onion, sliced
1 bunch green onions, chopped
2 large tomatoes, chopped
1 avocados - peeled, pitted, and cubed
8 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (16 ounce) package corn chips
1 (16 ounce) bottle Catalina salad dressing

Directions:
Prepare the ground beef seasoned with taco seasoning. Set aside.
In a large bowl, combine the beef mixture, lettuce, red onion, green onion, tomatoes, avocado and cheese. Mix well.
Before serving, add the corn chips and enough dressing to coat. Mix well and serve immediately.
