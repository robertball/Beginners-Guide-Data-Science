Title:
Garlic and Herb Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123157.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 # baby potatoes - cut in half
3-4 whole garlic cloves
whole sprigs thyme and rosemary
3/4 t. salt
1/2 t. pepper
2 T. olive oil

Directions:
Toss all ingredients.
Place on baking sheet.
Roast at 475 degrees for 30-35 min.
