Title:
WINNIPEG'S OWN  PASTA SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/693444.jpg
Ingredients:
4 CUPS OF UNCOOKED MACARONI OR PASTA OF YOUR CHOICE
3 CHICKEN BREAST-COOK AND CUT IN SMALL CHUNKS,CHILL
6 CUPS BROCCOLI,CUT IN BITE SIZE PIECES,STEAM,CHILL
2 CUPS CARROTS,SLICE THIN,COOK,CHILL
4 CUPS MUSHROOMS,CUT IN CHUNKS,COOK,CHILL
DRESSING:
1/2 CUP SOUR CREAM
1/2 CUP HELLMANS MAYO
2 TBSP.OIL
1 TBSP.LEMON JUICE

Directions:
Blend together sour cream,hellman's,oil and lemon juice.Chill in fridge.Cook pasta,run under cold water,drain well,add dressing, chill. cook chicken,broccoli,carrots and mushrooms.Return to fridge,chill 4 hours.Add cold ingredients to pasta and serve.
