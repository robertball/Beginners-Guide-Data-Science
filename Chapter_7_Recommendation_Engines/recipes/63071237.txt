Title:
Crab and Lobster Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887195.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup melted butter, divided
1 pound fresh mushrooms, stems removed
1 cup crushed seasoned croutons
1 cup Mozzarella cheese, shredded
1 (6 ounce) can crabmeat, drained
1 pound lobster tail, cleaned and chopped
3 tablespoons garlic, minced
1/4 cup Mozzarella cheese, shredded (optional)

Directions:
Brush a large baking sheet with about 1/4 cup of melted butter. Arrange mushroom caps in a single layer over the baking sheet.
In a medium bowl, mix together the crushed croutons, remaining 1/2 cup butter, shredded cheese, crabmeat, lobster and garlic.
Spoon into mushroom caps where the stems used to be.
Bake at 375ยบ for 10 to 12 minutes, or until lightly browned on the top.
Sprinkle with additional cheese if desired, and serve hot!
