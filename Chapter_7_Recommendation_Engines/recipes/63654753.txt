Title:
Tortellini with Ham & Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb fresh or frozen cheese tortellini
1 c frozen peas
3 tbsp unsalted butter cut into pieces
1/2 c heavy cream
Salt
Pepper
1/2 c grated Parmesan
1/4 lb chopped ham

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil
Cook tortellini until al dente (do not over cook)
Add peas to the pot for at least 30 sec of cooking time
Drain tortellini and peas and return to the pot
Stir in butter, cream, salt, pepper, parmesan, and ham.
Turn stove to medium and cook, stirring occasionally until ingredients are heated thoroughly.  (2-3 min)
Serve immediately
