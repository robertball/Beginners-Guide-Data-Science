Title:
One-Skillet Egg Breakfast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/961151.jpg
Ingredients:
6 strips of bacon
1/4 cup olive oil
6 large white potatoes, peeled and cubed
1/2 small onion, diced
4 tablespoons butter
4 large eggs
1 cup Sargento Chef Blends 4 State Cheddar Shredded Cheese

Directions:
Cook bacon in skillet until crisp, remove from pan (place on paper towel-lined plate to drain).  Crumble when cool.
Cook cubed potatoes and diced onion in olive oil until lightly browned and until a fork goes through the potatoes easily.
Add in crumbled bacon.
Add butter.
Crack eggs over potato/onion/bacon mixture and stir in eggs.  Cook until eggs are firm.
Top with shredded cheese.
Cover skillet with lid and turn heat off.  Let sit until cheese is melted.
