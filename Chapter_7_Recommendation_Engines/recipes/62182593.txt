Title:
Spicy Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can whole kernel corn, drained
1 Carton of chicken broth
3 diced (cooked) chicken breasts
1 can cici beans
1 can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers, drained
1 can cream of celery soup
1 cup cooked rice
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 tsp paprika
2 tsp cumin
1 tsp chipotle chili powder
1/2 small diced onion
1 tblsp butter
1 Avocado, diced
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt the butter in a heavy bottomed pot.  Cook onion until soft.  Add all over ingredients except avocado and bring to a boil.  Turn down heat and allow to simmer for 10-15 minutes.
Served with diced avocado
