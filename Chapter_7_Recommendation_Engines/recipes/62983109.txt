Title:
Aunt Helen Sue's Holiday Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Boiling Water
1 Box Orange Jello (Sugar Free version of everything)
1 Box Instant Vanilla Pudding
1 Container of refrigerated Cool Whip
2 Cans drained Mandarin Oranges
2 Cups Marshmellows (optional)

Directions:
Boil 2 cups water in a pan , add orange jello and vanilla pudding.  Boil till dissolved and set aside to cool.
Mix cool whip and mandarin oranges together, fold in marshmellows and jello mixture once it is cool.
Refridgerate 1 hour to set up and enjoy.
