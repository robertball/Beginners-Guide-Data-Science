Title:
Banana Bread With A twist Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup white sugar
1 teasp ground cinnamon
3/4 cup butter
3/4 cup chunky peanut butter
3 cups white sugqr
4-6 large, very ripe bananas mashed
1 (16 oz) sour cream
2 teasp vanilla extract
2 teasp ground cinnamon
1/2 teasp salt
3 teasp baking soda
4 cups all purpose flour (KING arthur is prefered)
1 cup chopped nuts (your favorite, optional).    I prefer not to add since I used chunky peanut butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  gREASE and flous three loaf pans or equivilent (I use a round casserole dish for one of the pans.  Grease pans and dust with sugar cinnamon mixture.
In large bowl, cream butter, peanut butter and 3 cups sugar.  Mix in eggs, mashed bananas, sour cream, vanilla and cinnanmon.  Mix in salt, baking soda and flour. Stir in nuts.  Divide into prepared pans.  Bake 1 1/2 hours until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
