Title:
Dumpling Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tsp Minced Ginger
1 Stalk of Scallion, thinly slices
2 Tsp Veggie Oil
1 Clove Minced Garlic
Combine:3 Tbsp Soy Sauce and 1 1/2 Tsp Spring Water
Couple of Pinches of Sugar
Few Drops of Toast Sesame Oil
Asian Chili Garlic Sauce or Tabasco Sauce (optional)

Directions:
1. Combine Ginger, Garlic, Sugar and Scallion in a microwave-safe dish and microwave for 1 minute
2. Let sit for a couple of minutes before removing from microwave
3. Take the dish out and slowly whisk in soy sauce and water mixture
4. Drizzle with a few drops of Sesame oil
5. If desired, add hot sauce and let sit for 2 minutes and serve

Submitter: 
ANGELBEBEBOO
Description: 
Yummy dipping sauce for pot stickers or steamed wontons..mmm!  And it's all done in the Microwave!!
