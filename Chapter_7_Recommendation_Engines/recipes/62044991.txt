Title:
Parsnip Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 fresh parsnips
half a stick of butter
1/2 cup flour
2 cups vegetable oil
salt
chili powder
cayenne

Directions:
Peel parsnips, slice in 1/4 in. rounds, and cook in lightly salted water 5 minutes until tender and crisp. Dip in melted butter and chill until butter hardens (about 1/2 hour). Roll parsnips in flour and fry in hot vegetable oil until golden crisp. Add salt to taste.

Submitter: 
KOOLBRICK
Description: 
A different kind of chip that's cooked in vegetable oil and very simple to make. Just add salt. If you want to give it a kick, add chili powder and cayenne.
