Title:
Fresh Mint Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3tbsp fresh Mint  finely chopped;
½ tsp Caster Sugar (but in a pinch any is good);
2tbsp Boiling Water;
2tbsp White Wine Vinegar;
Pinch of Salt.

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix the mint and sugar with the boiling water;
Stir in the wine vinegar and the salt;
Leave to cool or chill slightly in fridge;
Serve

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
A piquant delight with lamb Roast or chops. Magic in bringing out the true flavour.
