Title:
Antibacterial Bathroom Spray Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups water
2 Tbsp liquid Castile soap
1 Tbsp White thyme essential oil (can be purchased from amazon.com)
10 drops lavender essential oil

Directions:
using a funnel, pour water, soap and oils into a bottle.  Shake the bottle well.
TO USE: Spray the area and let sit for 20 minutes, then wipe down with a damp cloth.

Submitter: 
TheBritishBaker
Description: 
Use this germ killer on sinks, vanities and toilet seats.  White thyme oil although a little expensive unhibits bacterial growth.  Costs approx $11.57 for a 3 month supply.
