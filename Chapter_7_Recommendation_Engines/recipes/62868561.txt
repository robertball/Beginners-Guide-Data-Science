Title:
Black Magic Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/718327.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 c. flour
2 c. sugar
3/4 c. cocoa
2 t. baking soda
1 t. baking powder
1 t. salt
2 eggs
2 t. instant coffee in 1 c. of boiling water
1 c. buttermilk OR sour milk
1/2 c. oil
1 t. vanilla

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour 1 - 9"x13" pan.
Blend sugar, flour, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl.
Make an indentation in the dry ingredients and add aggs, coffee, buttermilk, oil and vanilla.
Mix until the clumps are gone (use a whisk if need be).
Pour batter into pan. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.
