Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Barbecue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1023589.jpg
Ingredients:
6 frozen skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 (12 ounce) bottle barbeque sauce
1/2 cup Italian salad dressing
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Place chicken in a slow cooker.
In a bowl, mix the barbecue sauce, Italian salad dressing, brown sugar, and Worcestershire sauce.
Pour over the chicken.
Cover, and cook 6 to 8 hours on low.
Shred chicken with two forks.
Serve on buns.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Use your slow cooker to prepare this great twist on basic barbecue chicken. It is easy to prepare and the sauce also makes a great topping for baked potatoes.Modified from: Zesty Slow Cooker Chicken Barbecue
