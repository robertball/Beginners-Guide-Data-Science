Title:
Cinnamon Sugarloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1160993.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking powder
1/2 tsp. Baking soda
2 Tbsp. Cinnamon
3 Eggs, beaten
1 C. Dry Post Grape Nuts cereal
3/4 C. Light cream
1 C. Granulated sugar
1 Tbsp. Maple sugar, for topping

Directions:
Grease a 9" by 5" by 3" loaf pan. Set aside
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
Blend flour, b. powder, b. soda and cinnamon together in a small bowl. Set aside
Pour Post Grape Nuts cereal into a bowl and stir in cream to moisten cereal
Beat eggs in a small bowl until light and frothy
Mix sugar, cereal and  beaten eggs together. Add dry ingredients to wet mixture and combine all ingredients well.
Pour batter into greased loaf pan and sprinkle maple sugar over the top of the loaf
Bake in preheated oven for 40 minutes until loaf tests done in the middle
Remove from the oven and allow to cool.  Loosen the sides of the loaf pan and tap gently tilting the loaf pan on its side to drop the loaf onto a rack. cool completely before slicing and serving with butter.

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very chewy loaf with the goodness of whole grain sweetened with maple sugar on top and a cinnamon taste to savour with your morning breakfast or afternoon tea.
