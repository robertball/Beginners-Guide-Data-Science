Title:
Onion Ring Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
2 T adobo sauce (from canned chipotles peppers)
1 T horseradish sauce
dash paprika
dash pepper

Directions:
Whisk all together and allow to chill at least one hour prior to serving.
May add minced chipotles peppers to adobo sauce if more heat desired.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Copy cat recipe - similar to Logans Roadside onion petal dipping sauce (but with a little more attitude).  Goes great on burgers as well.
