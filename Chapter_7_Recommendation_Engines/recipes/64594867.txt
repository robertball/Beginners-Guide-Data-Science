Title:
Chicken Wing Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken thighs
Chicken breast
Cheddar Cheese
Mozzarella Cheese
Franks Red Hot
Garlic
Blue Cheese Dressing
Liquid Smoke
Chipotle powder

Directions:
Chicken Remove skin
1 bottle of beer, 1 cup franks, 5-6 drops of liquid smoke (unless you are smoking the chicken first)
Combine in pan or pot and add 2 cups water Boil for 45 min â€“ 1 hour of med-high heat
Remove chicken, let cool then shred chicken removing all bones
Sauce:
Sautee 4 cloves garlic (pressed) then add
3 cup Franks red hot, 1tsp Chipotle powder, 5-6 drops of liquid smoke
Cook med-low for 30 min
In a crockpot add:
1 bottle blue cheese dressing
1 block of philly cream cheese
3 cups of cubed cheddar. 2 cups cubed mozzarella
Let cook on low setting for approx. an hour, add chicken
Bonus:  Bourbon Brow.n Sugar Bacon!
Lay strips of bacon on cookie sheet
Oven at 350 for about 15 min, flip bacon, About 10 min bacon should be just about cooked.
When cooked (slightly limp but cooked) spray bourbon on bacon and sprinkle brown sugar
Leave in oven for another minute or two then remove and let cool, then chop and add to dip or on the side
