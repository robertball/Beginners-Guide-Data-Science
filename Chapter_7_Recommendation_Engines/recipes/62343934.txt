Title:
Aileen's Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (46 fluid ounce) can pineapple juice
1 (46 fluid ounce) can apricot nectar (or mango/papaya mix)
1 (6 ounce) can frozen limeade concentrate
2 liters lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
(There is a tropical 7 Up)

Directions:
In a punch bowl combine pineapple juice, apricot nectar and limeade; finally, pour in the lemon-lime soda.

Submitter: 
Dorothy Clark
Description: 
Pineapple juice, apricot nectar and limeade concentrate are combined with lemon-lime soda.Modified from: Aileen's Punch
