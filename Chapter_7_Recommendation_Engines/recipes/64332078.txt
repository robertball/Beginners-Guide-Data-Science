Title:
Chia Seed Bean Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ cup filtered water
¼ cup tamari
¼ cup tomato paste
1 teaspoon liquid hickory smoke
3 Tablespoons chia seeds
4 large cloves of garlic pressed or chopped fine
½ teaspoon of red pepper flakes - optional
½ cup packed fresh parsley or cilantro or a mix of both
½ cup diced onion
2  15 ounce cans of black beans, drained and rinsed  or cooked from dried
1  15 ounce can of pinto beans, drained and rinsed  or cooked from dried
1 ¼ cup old fashioned oats

Directions:
In a large bowl add: water, tamari, tomato paste, liquid smoke, chia seeds, garlic and red pepper flakes-optional for spicy flavor
Stir together, it will thicken up after a few minutes from the chia seeds.
In a food processor add parsley and process until chopped fine
Add onions with drained and rinsed beans to the parsley and process until smooth
Add blended bean mixture to the bowl with the chia seed mixture and incorporate together.
Add oats and stir until evenly distributed
Refrigerate mixture overnight or for at least 5 hours
After burger mix is chilled preheat oven to 400 degrees
Line a baking pan with parchment paper
Form 6 burgers and place onto baking pan.
Bake for 30 minutes and allow to rest and cool for 10 minutes on the hot baking pan before assembling your burger.

Thick, hearty and flavorful vegan, oil free burgers.
