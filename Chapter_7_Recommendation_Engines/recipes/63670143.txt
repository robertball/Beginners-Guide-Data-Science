Title:
Basic Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups ice cream
1/2 cup milk
(optional)1/4 cup fruit or candy

Directions:
In a blender, combine ice cream, milk. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.
