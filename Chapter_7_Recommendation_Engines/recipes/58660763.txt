Title:
Bean Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (16 ounce) cans kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 (16 ounce) can garbanzo beans, rinsed and drained
1/2 cup minced onion
1 clove garlic, minced
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup sweet relish
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 dash white pepper
1 teaspoon dry mustard

Directions:
In a medium bowl, stir together beans, onion, garlic, mayonnaise, and relish. Season with salt, white pepper, and mustard. Mix well, and refrigerate for several hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Diane
Description: 
My dad's friend used to serve this as an appetizer in his restaurant in Ipswich, Mass.  He shared it with us many years ago and it has been a family favorite ever since. Serve with crackers or favorite crudites.Modified from: Bean Relish
