Title:
Whoopie Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/604192.jpg
Ingredients:
½ C. shortening
1 C. sugar
2 egg yolks
1 C. Milk
2 C. flour
5 Tbl. cocoa
1 tsp. baking powder
½ tsp. salt
½ tsp. vanilla
FILLING:
½ C. shortening
2 egg whites
2 C. confectioners sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
¼ tsp. salt

Directions:
Sift dry ingredients together.  Add remaining ingredients and mix until smooth and well blended.  Drop by teaspoonful on an ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake 10 to 12 minutes in 375º oven until set but not cripst.  When cool, put together sandwich style with filling.
FILLING:  Beat ingredients until smooth and fluffy.

Submitter: 
Deb C
Description: 
There is a smile behind every whoopie pie.  This is a very old recipe that makes the traditional whoopie pie.
