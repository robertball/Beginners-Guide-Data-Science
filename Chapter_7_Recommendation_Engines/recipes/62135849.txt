Title:
Mama Jensen's Frozen Key Lime Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. graham crackers, crushed
1/3 c. butter, melted
1/4 C. sugar
3 eggs, separated
dash salt
3/4 tsp. lime zest
1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
1/2 c. key lime juice

Directions:
Mix graham crumbs, butter and sugar and press into a 9in pie plate.
Beat egg whites and dash of salt until stiff, set aside.
Beat egg yolks until creamy, add can of sweetened condensed milk. Gradually add key lime juice.  Add zest; carefully so that zest does not collect on the back of your beaters.
Fold egg yolk into beaten egg whites.
Pour into crust and freeze for at least 3 hours.

Submitter: 
Carolyn Hilliard Nelson
Description: 
This recipe from my colleagues late Floridian mom folds beaten egg whites into a base with no added sugar.  Tangy and refreshing. Check your local grocer for eggs pasteurized to eliminate salmonella.
