Title:
Carrot Machine Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4994880.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/8 cup water
1 1/2 tablespoon butter soften
1 tablespoon orange extract (optional)
2 cups bread flour
1 cup all purpose flour
1 1/2 tablespoon dry milk
1 1/2 tablespoon brown sugar
1 1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon ground clove
3/4 cup Grated Carrot
2 teaspoons Active Dry Yeast

Directions:
In the bread machine case add the liquid ingredients.
In a bowl mix all the dry ingredients.
Add dry ingredients into the bread case.
Select basic bread medium crust setting.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
This was my attempt to come up with a machine bread for carrots. I've made variations of this bread where I've added raisins or substituted coconut components (coconut milk for water, Palm Coconut Sugar for Brown Sugar, and added 1/4 cup Coconut Flake).
