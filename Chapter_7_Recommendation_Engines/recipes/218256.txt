Title:
Wacky Mac® Five-Ingredient Tomato and Basil Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989989.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Wacky Mac®, cooked according to package directions
1/4 cup low-fat vinaigrette dressing or Italian dressing
1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese or gorgonzola cheese
2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved or quartered, according to size
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Place Wacky Mac in large serving bowl. Add dressing and toss gently but well. Add blue cheese, tomatoes, basil, salt and pepper. Toss again. Set aside 5 minutes for flavors to blend.

Submitter: 
Wacky Mac®
Description: 
Five easy ingredients transform a dish of Wacky Mac into a flavourful combination with blue cheese, tomatoes and basil.
