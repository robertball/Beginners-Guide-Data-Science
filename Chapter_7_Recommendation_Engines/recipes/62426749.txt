Title:
Easy Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Cream of Chicken Soup
1 can cream of mushroom or celery soup
garlic, seasonings
1 (10 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables, thawed
1 package frozen potato cubes, thawed
1 cup cubed rotisserie chicken
1/2 cup milk
1 egg
1 cup all-purpose baking mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Mix soup, vegetables and chicken in 9-inch pie plate.
Mix milk, egg and baking mix. Pour over chicken mixture. Bake 30 minutes or until golden.

Submitter: 
Cathy Heinemann Tucker
Description: 
Chicken and vegetables are paired in a creamy sauce and topped with a golden biscuit crust.Modified from: Campbell's Kitchen Easy Chicken Pot Pie
