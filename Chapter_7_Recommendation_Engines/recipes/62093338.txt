Title:
Rosemary Chicken Salad Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. chopped roasted skinless, boneless chicken breast
1/3 c. chopped green onions
1/4 c. chopped smoked almonds
1/4 c. light mayonnaise
1 tsp. chopped fresh rosemary
1 tsp. Dijon mustard
1/8 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. fesh ground black pepper
10 slices whole grain bread

Directions:
Combine the first 9 ingredents, stirring well. spread about 2/3 c. of the chicken mixture over each of the 5 bread slices, and top with the remaining bread slices. Cut the sandwiches in half. Yield: 5 servings ( serving size: 1 sandwich).

Submitter: 
DREMWEVR
Description: 
Light and refreshing, a twist on the tipical chicken salad sandwich.
