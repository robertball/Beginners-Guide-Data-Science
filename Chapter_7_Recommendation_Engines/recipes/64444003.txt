Title:
Chicken, Broccoli and Brown Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2368862.jpg
Ingredients:
7 oz Brown Rice
1 tbs Olive Oil
1 small Onion, finely chopped
8 oz sliced Mushrooms
8 oz skinless-boneless Chicken Breast
3/4 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp Black Pepper
12 oz Broccoli
1 1/2 Cup 1% Milk
3 Tbs all-purpose Flour
3 oz shredded sharp Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
1. Preheat broiler to High + cook rice according to instructions
2. While rice cooks, heat a large 12-in ovenproof skillet over medium-high heat. Add oil to pan, swirl to coat. Add Onion, Mushrooms, and Chicken; sprinkle with 1/4 tsp Salt and Pepper. Saut√© 6 minutes or until Chicken and onion are done.
3. Steam Broccoli until tender.
4. Combine Milk and Flour, stirring with a whisk or fork until smooth
5. Stir Milk mixture into Chicken mixture in skillet
6. Cook 2 minutes until bubbly and thick, stirring frequently
7. Stir in remaining 1/2 tsp salt, Rice, and Broccoli. Sprinkle with Cheese
8. Broil for 1 minute or until cheese just begins to brown
