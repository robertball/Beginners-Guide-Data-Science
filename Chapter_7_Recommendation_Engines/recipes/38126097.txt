Title:
Green Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound thin spaghetti, cooked and drained
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, grilled or pan fried with salt and pepper.
6-8 Roma tomatoes, rough chopped
1 can medium black olives, drained and rough chopped
1 package dry pesto mix

Directions:
Cube cooked chicken and place into a deep bowl.
Prepare pesto per package directions, cool slightly then pour over chicken.
Place prepared pasta in bowl.
Toss in black olives and tomatoes.
Toss all ingredients until pesto is distributed.
Dish is good room temperature or chilled in refrigerator.

Great cold pasta salad.
