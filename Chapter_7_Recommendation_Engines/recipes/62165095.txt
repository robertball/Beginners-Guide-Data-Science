Title:
Quick Rice and Sausage Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (6 large links) of garlic mozzarella chicken sausage OR any other type of gourmet sausage- casing removed and sliced into 1/4 inch coins
1 package of ready-to-heat Basmati rice (like Uncle Ben's Microwave in bag)
1/2 cup onion diced
1 clove of garlic sliced
3 TBS white wine
2 TBS olive oil
1 TBS lemon juice
1 boullion cube
3/4 cup water
1 cup canned corn
1 cup canned creamed corn
Pepper

Directions:
In a large skillet cook sausage over medium heat until golden brown.  Remove from skillet set aside.
Deglaze pan with wine; scrape pan.
Add olive oil, onion and garlic.  Saute until onion is soft.
Add lemon juice, boullion and water- cook until boullion is dissolved.
Fold in corn and creamed corn;  Season with pepper.
Break rice apart in package and then add to skillet.
Mix well.
Add sausage and cook through.
