Title:
Lorna's Scalloped Brussel Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs brussel sprouts
2 Tbsp butter
1 onion, chopped
1/2 tsp thyme
1/4 Cup flour
2 Cups milk
1 1/2 Cup Shredded gruyere cheese
3/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
bread crumbs/cornflake/butter rounds for topping

Directions:
Steam or roast brussel sprouts (I prefer to roast)\
Place in greased casserole dish
Sautee onions in butter and thyme.
Add flour then milk, cooking till thick
Stir in cheese, salt and pepper
Pour over brussel sprouts and bake @ 350 for 30 mins
Sprinkle on topping and bake another 25 mins or so

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
Delish.  You could use this base for pearled onions also
