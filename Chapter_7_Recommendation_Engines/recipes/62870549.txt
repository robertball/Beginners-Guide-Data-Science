Title:
Chicken Lawrey's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 pieces of bone in chicken
2 C White Worcestershire
2 pks Laerey's Origional Spaghetti Sauce
1 stick butter
1 C white wine

Directions:
Marinade chicken in worcestershire sauce for several hours. Drain. Roll in Lawrey's. Please in 9x12 pan that contains melted butter. Pour wine over top and bake at 350 uncovered for 45 minutes.
