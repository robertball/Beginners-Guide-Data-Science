Title:
Pierogi filling Sauerkraut: Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 ounces of sauerkraut
1 stick butter
1 medium onion, chopped
1 cups chopped mushrooms (optional)
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Grind sauerkraut in a food processor, then cook sauerkraut for 10 minutes.
Saut√© onion and mushrooms in butter. Drain the sauerkraut well, and add to the onion mixture.
Fry a few minutes. Remove mixture from the pan and spread out to cool and dry.
Cool before filling the pierogi.
