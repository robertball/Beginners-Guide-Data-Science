Title:
Tuna salad with Capers and Green Olives Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 5 ounce can of tuna drained.
6 green olives, chopped
2 tablespoons of capers
1 medium stalk of celery, diced
2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
1/4 teaspoon of garlic salt
1 lettuce leaf (optional)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl. Stir to break up and coat the tuna.
Serve on a lettuce leaf or eat right out of the bowl.
