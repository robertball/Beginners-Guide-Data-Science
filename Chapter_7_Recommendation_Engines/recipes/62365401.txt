Title:
Creamy Green Beans Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/407296.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed and snapped
2 tablespoon butter
1/2 cup heavy cream
4 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
ground nutmeg - few shakes
salt, to taste
ground black pepper, to taste
1 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Prepare and steam green beans, set aside.
Melt the butter in a large skillet over medium-low heat; stir the cream, 4 tablespoons of Parmesan cheese, and nutmeg into the melted butter. Bring the mixture to a simmer. Gently stir the beans into the mixture to coat; season with salt and pepper. Return to a simmer, transfer to a serving dish, and sprinkle with 1 tablespoons of Parmesan cheese to serve.

Submitter: 
Mary
Description: 
Fresh green beans get a simple but delicious sauce made with cream, Parmesan cheese, and a hint of nutmeg.Modified from: Creamy Green Beans Parmesan
