Title:
Bacon cheddar muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 slices bacon
2 Tbsp. reserved bacon drippings
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 Tbsp. sugar
1 Tbsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1-1/2 cups cheddar cheese, grated
1 egg, beaten
1 cup milk
3 Tbsp. butter, melted

Directions:
Cook bacon until crisp.  Reserve 2 Tbsp. drippings, then drain and crumble bacon.  In large bowl combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.  Stir in bacon and 1 cup of cheese (reserve 1/2 cup for topping).  In small bowl combine egg, milk, butter and reserved bacon drippings.  Add to dry ingredients' stir just until moistened.  Divide batter among 12 greased muffin cups.  Sprinkle remaining cheese over top of muffins.  Bake at 350 for about 20 minutes.
If using swiss cheese, increase butter by 1 Tbsp.
Variation:  substitute 2/3 cup chopped cooked ham for bacon.

Submitter: 
AUNT MAMIE
Description: 
These savory muffins are an excellent supplement to soup or salad.  Substitute swiss cheese for the cheddar for a unique taste.
