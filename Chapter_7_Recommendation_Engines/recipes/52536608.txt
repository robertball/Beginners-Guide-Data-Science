Title:
Asian Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages ramen noodles, crushed - discard seasoning packet
1 cup peanuts - chopped (You could easily use slivered almonds if preferred)
2 teaspoons sesame oil
3 TB  butter, melted
2 head napa cabbage, shredded
1 bunch green onions, chopped
DRESSING:
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup distilled white vinegar
1/2 cup white sugar
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1-2 tsp of sesame oil

Directions:
In a medium skillet over low heat brown ramen noodles and peanuts with melted butter or margarine and a 1/2 tsp of sesame oil. Once browned, take off heat and let cool.
In a small saucepan bring vegetable oil, sugar, and vinegar to boil for 1 minute. Cool. Add soy sauce.
In a large bowl , combine shredded napa cabbage and chopped green onions. Wait to add the noodle and soy sauce mixture until your nearly ready to serve as it does get soggy over time
