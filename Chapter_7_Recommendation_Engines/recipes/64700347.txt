Title:
Tostadas de Jaiba (Imitation Crab) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5340811.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 lbs jaiba (imitation crab)
1/2 onion
1 jalapeño chopped
1 bunch cilantro finely chopped
3 tomatoes chopped
1 cucumber peeled, seeded and chopped
6-8 limes (do not use lime in bottle)
1 cup Clamato picante
1 1/2 tbsp. Tapatío sauce
Salt and pepper to taste
Tostadas
Avocado (optional)

Directions:
Shred the jaiba in a medium bowl. I like to use my stand mixer to shred it, super easy and takes only a minute or so.
Combine all the ingredients except the avocado and tostadas.
Put in refrigerator for about an hour to chill.
Serve on tostadas and add avocado if desired
