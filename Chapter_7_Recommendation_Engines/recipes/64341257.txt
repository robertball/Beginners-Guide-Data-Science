Title:
Lorna's Sticky Icing (Like Boiled Icing) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 egg whites
1C brown or white sugar
2-3Tbsp corn syrup
Use a double broiler

Directions:
1. Mix ingredients together well with wisk
2. Head over water in double broiler until it's too hot to put your finger in
3. Beat on high with hand mixer until thic

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
This frosting tastes just like my gramma's boiled frosting.  It's delicous!
