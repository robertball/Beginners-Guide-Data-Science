Title:
Chae's Sweet Potato Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/704729.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Medium sweet potatoes/yams
Cleaned and sliced into fries
and rinsed in cold water.
1 Tsp. cinnamon
1/4 Tsp. cardomum
1/4 Tsp ginger
1/4 Tsp. Coriander
1/4 Tsp. Cumin
1/4 Tsp. Chili powder
2 TBSP light brown sugar
Kosher salt and Fresh Ground Black Pepper To taste
Olive Oil cooking Spray for coating fries

Directions:
Rinse french fries with cold water, let drain. Combine spices and sugar, sprinkle over fries evenly.
Place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
Spread fries on baking sheet to ensure they cook evenly.
Spray the fires with olive oil and bake at 350 degrees until tender.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste then serve.
