Title:
The Absolute Southwestern Enchilada Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 tsp. Mexican Chili Powder (or chili powder of your choice)
1 1/2 tsp. Cumin
2 tsp. Garlic Powder
1/4 tsp. Salt
1/4 tsp. White Pepper (or Black)
1 tsp. Cocoa Powder
1 tsp. Oregano (dried)
1 tsp. Sugar (gets rid of the TOMATOey taste)
2 tbsp. Flour
2 Tbsp. Butter or Margarine
1 tbsp. Chopped Jalapenoes (or green chilies)
3 cups  Water
6 oz. Can Tomato Paste

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients well.
Add 2 cups of the Water and the Tomato Paste. Stir to blend.
Add remaining water.
Put it all in the Blender and Liquify for 2 minutes. This Chops up and blends all the ingredients well, before cooking begins.
Pour into pan and add Butter.
Stir on Medium High til bubbly and thickening a bit.
Turn to Low and Simmer for 30 to 45 minutes.
