Title:
Nacho Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds baking potatoes, cubed
2Tbs. chili powder
1 tsp. garlic salt
2 Tbs. butter
1 small can chopped green chilies
8 slices cooked bacon, crumbled
1 1/2 cups shredded cheese
1/3 cup sliced green onion
sour cream
salsa

Directions:
Spray an 8-9 inch baking pan with cooking spray.  Spread potatoes in the bottom.  Sprinkle Chili powder and garlic over the potatoes and toss to coat. Layer pieces of cold butter over the top.  Layer the green chilies over that.  Microwave for 8-10 minutes stirring halfway through.
Make sure potatoes are tender, then top with cheese and green onion and bacon.  Wait for cheese to melt. Serve with sour cream and/or salso on the side.
