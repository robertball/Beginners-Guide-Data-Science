Title:
Pumpkin Sage Pasta Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/635654.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon olive oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 medium onion, finely diced
1 bay leaf
4-6 sprigs fresh sage - cut into chiffonade (2 Tbls)
1 cup dry white wine
1 cup chicken stock
1 cup canned pumpkin puree (not pie filling)
1/2 cup milk or cream
1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
Course salt and pepper to taste
Parmesan cheese for serving

Directions:
Heat 1 Tablespoon of Olive Oil in a large non stick skillet.  Add diced onion and garlic.  Saute for 3 - 5 minutes on medium to medium low heat for 3 - 5 minutes until onions are tender.
Add bay leaf, sage and wine to pan.  Reduce wine by half, about 2 minutes.
Add broth and pumpkin and stir to combine, stirring sauce until it starts to bubble.
Stir in milk or cream and continue to simmer.
Add cinnamon, nutmeg, salt and pepper, to taste.
Simmer mixture 5 - 10 minutes to thicken sauce.
Remove bay leaf and serve over pasta, spaghetti squash or ravioli.  Garnish with parmesan cheese.
Serve with a simple salad and a nice loaf of bread for soaking up the delicious sauce.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
Do you have a can or two of pumpkin hanging out in the pantry?  Put them to work in this delicious light and easy pasta sauce.  This is a great sauce to make ahead and freeze for a quick and easy light dinner.
