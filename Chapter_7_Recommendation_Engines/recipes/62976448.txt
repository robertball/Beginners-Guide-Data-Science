Title:
Chicken Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 potatoes
1/4 cup butter
2/3 cup milk
1 medium head roasted garlic
1 pound cooked, shredded chicken breast meat
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
1 onion, diced
1/2 cup red bell pepper, diced
1 cup fresh corn kernels
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Peel, chop and boil potatoes until tender. Drain, then mash potatoes. In the meantime, dollap of butter add onion and red pepper and 2 cloves garlic and saute.Add butter, milk and sauted vegetables and mix in. Set aside.
Arrange chicken evenly in the bottom of a casserole dish, then sprinkle with spices, then layer  corn and all but 1/4 cup cheese. Spread mashed potatoes on top, sealing everything in. Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 25 minutes or until brown around edges.
