Title:
Texas Brownies with Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients:
Brownies:
2 cups flour
2 cups sugar
1 stick butter (1/2 cup)
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup strong, brewed coffee
1/4 cup cocoa
1/2 cup buttermilk
2 eggs
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Frosting:
1 stick butter (1/2 cup)
2 tablespoons cocoa
1/4 cup milk
3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Pecan halves, to taste
Directions:
For brownies:
In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour & the sugar. Set aside.
In a heavy saucepan, combine the butter, shortening, coffee, & cocoa.
Stir & heat to boiling.
Pour boiling mixture over the flour & sugar in the bowl.
Add the buttermilk, eggs, baking soda, & vanilla extract.
Mix well, using a wooden spoon or high speed on an electric mixer.
Pour into a well-buttered 15x11x1" jelly roll pan.
Bake at 400F. for 20 minutes or until the brownies test done in the center.
For frosting:
While the brownies bake, prepare the frosting.
In a saucepan, combine the butter, cocoa, & milk.
Heat to a boil, stirring.
Mix in the powdered sugar & vanilla until frosting is smooth.
Pour warm frosting over brownies as soon as you take them out of the oven.
Sprinkle pecan halves over the icing before it "sets".
Cool the brownies, & cut into 48 bars.
Makes 48 brownies
Tips:
If you don't have buttermilk on hand, substitute 2 teaspoons vinegar or lemon
juice mixed into 1/2 cup of milk.
