Title:
Crockpot Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes, cubed
8 ounces cream cheese
1 pound sour cream
1 cup milk
1 stick butter
1 teaspoon onion powder
2 teaspoons salt
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Place potatoes in a large pot of lightly salted water. Bring to a boil, and cook until tender, about 15 minutes. Drain, and mash.
In a large bowl, mix mashed potatoes, cream cheese, sour cream, butter, milk, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Transfer to the crockpot. Rewarm on low, approximately 4 hours.

Submitter: 
The-Baby-Bow-Lady
Description: 
You can make these ahead several days and store in the fridge. Warm up in the crockpot. Fits exactly in my large crockpot.Modified from: Make-Ahead Mashed Potatoes
