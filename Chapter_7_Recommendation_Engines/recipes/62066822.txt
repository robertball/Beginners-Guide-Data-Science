Title:
Peanut Butter Surprise Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1&3/4cup flour
1tsp baking soda
1/2tsp salt
1/2cup butter
1/2cup Peanut butter
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1tsp vanilla
1 bag Kisses
1/4 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Cream butter and sugars together until creamy. Add egg and mix until well blended. Add peanut butter and vanilla. Mix well.
In a small bowl mix together flour, baking soda, and salt. Add gradually to butter mixture until well blended.
Wrap 1 teaspoon (or to your liking) of dough around hershey kiss to cover. Roll into a ball. Then roll into powdered sugar to cover.
Place on parchment paper or greased cookie sheet about 2 inches apart. They will spread a tad bit. Bake for 7-9 minutes or until slightly brown.

Submitter: 
Renee Trivelli Milillo
Description: 
This is another way to make those cookies with the chocolate on top. The chocolate is hidden in a lite tasting peanut butter cookie! I have been eating this since I was a kid and making them since I was a mom! The kids love them!
