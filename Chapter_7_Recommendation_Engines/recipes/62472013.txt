Title:
Gobble Gobble Chili with Chipotle Baja Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/479492.jpg
Ingredients:
Chili
1 lb ground turkey
1 med green pepper, chopped
1 med onion, chopped
3 stalks celery, chopped
2 15 oz cans spicy chili beans
2 15 oz cans diced tomatoes, zesty chili style
1 15 oz can tomato sauce
1 12 oz beer
1 Chipotle chili with Adobo (from can)Chopped
1 Tsp Cumin
1 Tsp Ground Black Pepper
Baja Sauce
1/2 Cup Plain Greek Yogurt
1/3 Cup Mayonnaise
2 Tbsp. Fresh Lime juice
2 Cloves of Garlic
1 Chipotle Chili Pepper, chopped
Garnish
1 8 oz pkg shredded no fat cheddar cheese (Optional)

Directions:
Chili:
Brown Ground Turkey, Add Pepper, Onion & Celery cook 5 to 10 minutes, add beans, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, beer, chipotle, cumin & pepper. Simmer for two hours.
Chipotle Baja Sauce:
Combine all ingredients in a food processor or blender and blend until smooth. Refrigerate until needed
Scoop Chili into bowl top with no-fat chedder cheese & drizzle with sauce.

Submitter: 
The Green Goddess
Description: 
This is a wonderful low fat zesty turkey chili finished with a  fat free cheddar cheese and greek yogurt baja sauce. Serve with your favorite corn bread recipe.
