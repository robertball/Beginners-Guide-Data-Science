Title:
Award winning Mostaccioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb mild Italian sausage
1/2 lb spicey Italian sausage
1 med. onion
2 cloves garlic, chopped
2 32 oz jars of marinara sauce
1 small jan tomoate paste
1/2 lb  provolone cheese sliced
1 lb mozzarella cheese shredded
1 lb mostaccioli noddles
Salt and pepper to taste
Olive oil

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 350
Chop onion and saute in pot with olive oil and garlic
Cut sausage in to bite size pieces and add to onions, brown through
Once meat is done add the marinara sauce and paste, stir and season to taste with salt and pepper
Boil noddles until done but still some what with a crunch (4-5) min, drain
Mix noddles and sauce together in pot
Grease large casserole dish
Layer noddle mixture, then cover with provolone cheese
Repeat until dish is full, start and end with noddles
Cover with tin foil and bake at 350 for 40 min
Remove from oven and cover with mozzarella, place back in oven uncovered until cheese is melted

Submitter: 
violet
Description: 
This is always a crowd pleaser.  My stand by to bring to a friend when a meal is needed.  We think is taste best if you prepare it and place the whole casserole in the fridge over night and then bake.
