Title:
Basic Berry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/856881.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3  - 1 c sugar, depending on sweetness of the berries used (1-1/2 c for gooseberries)
2 tbsp cornstarch (or 4 tbsp flour)
1/8 tsp salt
1 tbsp butter

Directions:
Mix sugar, cornstarch or flour, and salt.
Sprinkle  over fruit in pie shell.
Dot with butter.
Place top crust.
Bake 450 degrees (F) for 10 minutes then reduce to 350 degrees (F) for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Pie filling recipe I've used through the years for any berry pie including Gooseberries.
