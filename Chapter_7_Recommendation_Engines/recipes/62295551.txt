Title:
Buffalo Chicken and Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/361021.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 lb. chicken tenderloins cut in 1" strips
1/3 c. buffalo hot sauce
6 c. thawed hash browns
1/2 c. shredded cheddar
1 can cream of celery soup
1 c. blue cheese dressing
1 c. crushed cornflakes
2 T. margarine, melted
1-2 green onions, sliced

Directions:
Spray 13x9" baking pan with PAM.
Mix chicken strips with hot sauce and set aside.
In large bowl mix potatoes, soup, dressing and cheese.  Put into baking dish.  Top with chicken strips.
Mix cornflakes and margarine.  Sprinkle crumbs on top of chicken.  Cover with foil and bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees.  Remove foil and continue to cook 20-25 minutes until chicken tests tender and potatoes are done.  Top with sliced green onions.
