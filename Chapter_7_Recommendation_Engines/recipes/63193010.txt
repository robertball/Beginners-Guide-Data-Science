Title:
Vegan Quacomole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ripe avocados
1/2 tsp of onion powder
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 tomato, chopped in small cubes

Directions:
mush avocados with fork until creamy
add in the salt, onion and garlic powder
add tomato
if not consumed soon after preperation, add 2-3 avocado seeds to the mix to prevent browning

Submitter: 
ishallbevegan
Description: 
perfect dip with chips as an appetizer or a spread on a sandwich with tomato!
