Title:
Buttermilk Banana Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/556906.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 level cup whole wheat four
1/2 level cup of all purpose flour
3 tbsp. light brown sugar
1/2 tsp. Baking powder
1/4 tsp. Baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1 cup buttermilk
1 large egg beaten
1 1/2 tbsp. Melted unsalted butter
2 tsp. vegetable oil
1 large ripe banana sliced
Maple syrup (try with walnuts to give crunch and flavor!)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250
In a large mixing bowl combine the flours, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
Slowly pour in the buttermilk, egg and melted butter. Whisk till smooth.
In a skillet heat oil on medium heat, Pour batter in 1/4 cup portions into skillet.
Once it starts to bubble and and brown on the edges and an equal numbe of banana slices to the top of the pancake.
Flip carefully and continueto cook them till they begin to puff.
Put the pancakes on a plate or heatsafe dish and cover with tin foil and put in the oven to keep warm till you finish the batch. Then serve with maple syrup and garnish with more banana or walnuts.
