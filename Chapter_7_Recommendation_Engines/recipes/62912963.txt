Title:
Lemon Crinkle Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 cup sugar
1/2 tsp Vanilla extract
1 whole egg
1 tsp lemon zest or zest from 1 lemon
1 Tbs fresh lemon juice
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking powder
1/8 tsp baking soda
1 1/2 cup flour
Roll in:
1/2 cup powdered sugar or yellow colored sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar together until light and fluffy
Whip in vanilla, egg, lemon zest and juice
stir in all dry ingredients slowly until just combined
Roll teaspoon sized balls in sugar
Place on baking sheet covered with parchment paper and bake 9-11 minutes

Submitter: 
Shannon Lindell
Description: 
A sweet lemon taste with just the right amount of chewiness.
