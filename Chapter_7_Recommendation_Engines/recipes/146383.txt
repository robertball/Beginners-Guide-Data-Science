Title:
Easy Asian Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5924587.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup rice wine vinegar
2 tablespoons honey
3 cloves minced garlic
2 tablespoons minced fresh ginger root
2 teaspoons sesame seeds
2 teaspoons sesame oil

Directions:
Whisk together the soy sauce, vinegar, honey, garlic, ginger, sesame seeds, sesame oil in a bowl.

Submitter: 
gregoryz
Description: 
This easy dipping sauce tastes great with chicken balls, spring rolls, or dumplings.
