Title:
Low Country Fried Green Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/818264.jpg
Ingredients:
Ingredients
1 extra large egg
4 tablespoons milk
1 cup cornmeal
1 cup all-purpose flour
3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
4 green tomatoes, sliced
1 Container of Palmetto Cheese (pimento cheese)

Directions:
Directions
In a small bowl whisk together egg and milk. In another small bowl mix cornmeal and flour.
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat; use more or less oil to reach about 1/4 inch in depth.
Dredge tomato slices first in egg mixture, then in cornmeal mixture. Carefully place slices in hot oil and cook until browned on both sides.
Layer placing Palmetto cheese in between layers.  Let cheese melt and garnish to taste.

Description: 
This is a wonderful southern dish that I created straight out of the Low Country.  It is a very impressive and wonderful dish.  Fried green tomatoes layered with Palmetto Cheese.  (pimento cheese)
