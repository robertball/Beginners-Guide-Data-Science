Title:
Strawberry Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1041533.jpg
Ingredients:
2 quarts strawberries (about 3 pounds)
box vanilla wafers
1 3/4 to 2 cups sugar
3 tablespoons flour
sprinkle salt
2 eggs
2 cups milk
2 teaspoons vanilla

Directions:
Clean and slice strawberries and set aside
Combine sugar, flour and salt
Stir in eggs and milk.
Cook until thickened on stovetop stirring constantly, or in microwave 6-8 minutes stirring every 2 minutes.
Add vanilla
Arrange a layer of vanilla wafers on bottom of bowl
Cover with a layer of strawberries
Cover with a layer of the pudding mixture
Continue layers ending with a pudding layer.
