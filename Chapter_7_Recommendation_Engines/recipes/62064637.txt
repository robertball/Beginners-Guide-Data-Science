Title:
Crock Pot Apple Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cooking Apples
Sugar
Cinnamon
Allspice
Cloves

Directions:
Wash, core and slice cooking apples. Then place in a large saucepan and cook over medium heat, covered, until soft like applesauce.  Then spoon soft apples into a blender and puree.  Check the side of the blender to see how many cups you have made and then pour into crock-pot.  For every two cups of pureed apples add:
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon allspice
1/2 teaspoon cloves
Cook on low for 6-8 hours, uncovered.  Then pour into containers and let cool (with lids off)  When cool refridgerate or freeze.

Submitter: 
BRIMAT143
Description: 
This wonderful fall treat tastes great on bread, bagels and over ice cream.  My kids love it on bread with peanut butter.  It freezes well also so make up a whole crock pot full!
