Title:
Lower Fat Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package devil's food cake mix
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. nutmeg
1 15 oz. canned pumpkin puree
4 egg whites
2/3 cup water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease and flour 1 9x13 pan or bundt pan.
In large mixing bowl, combine cake mix and cinnamon and nutmeg.  Blend in pumpkin, egg whites and water.  Mix on medium speed for two minutes.  Pour into prepared pan and bake according to cake mix directions.

Submitter: 
Jennifer K
Description: 
The secret to this moist cake is canned pumpkin!  Other cake mixes work just as well.
