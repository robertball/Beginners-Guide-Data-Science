Title:
Cindy's Rum Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup white rum
1/3 cup amber rum
1/3 cup dark rum
1/3 cup coconut rum
1/3 cup peach schnapps
2 cans of frozen orange juice pulp free
1 tin of pineapple juice
1 2L bottle of Sprite or 7Up
1/4 cup grenadine
Lots of ice

Directions:
Put the alcohol in your punch bowl
Add the two cans of frozen orange juice and the water as per instructions on the package
Add one tin of pineapple juice, 2L of Sprite or 7Up and lots of ice
Add the grenadine. The punch should not look pink, just a dark orange.

Submitter: 
__C__
Description: 
This is a nice, light, fruity punch, that your guests will love!  It's a definite crowd pleaser!
