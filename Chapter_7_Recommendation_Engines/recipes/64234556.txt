Title:
Shrimp Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion, finely chopped (about 1/4 C)
2 T butter
2 cups chicken broth
1 C uncooked, parboiled rice
1/3 C dry white wine,
dash of thyme
1 can tiny shrimp, rinsed and drained or 1 small bag frozen salad shrimp
2 T butter
1/2 C grated parmesan cheese
1 T snipped parsley

Directions:
Cook and stir onion in butter in 10" skillet over medium heat until onion is tender, about 2 min.
In a separate pan, heat broth to boiling, remove from heat.
Stir rice into onion mixture; cook and stir 1 minute. Stir in wine, cook about 1 min. Stir in 1/2 of broth; stir thyme into remaining broth. Cook rice mixture uncovered until broth is almost absorbed, about 10 min. Stir in remaining broth. Cook until broth is absorbed and rice tender (about 15 min). Stir in shrimp and 2 T butter, heat through. Stir in Cheese and parsley.
