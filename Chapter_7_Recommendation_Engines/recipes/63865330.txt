Title:
Baked French Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large baking potato (300g)
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (230 degrees C).
Cut potato into wedges. Mix olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, chili powder, salt and onion powder together. Coat potatoes with oil/spice mixture and place on a baking sheet.
Bake for 30 minutes in preheated oven. Flip half way through
