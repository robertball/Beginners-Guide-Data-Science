Title:
Penzes molasses sugar cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2c shortening melted 1 tsp. vanilla
2/3c molasses             1 tsp. sea salt
1c brown sugar            1 tbsp.& 1tsp. baking soda
1c white sugar            4 c. flour
1/2 tsp. ground cloves    sugar to roll cookies in
2 tsp. cinnamon
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. sea salt
1 tbsp. plus 1 tsp. baking soda
4 c. flour
extra sugar to roll cookies in.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
1 Cool shortening, add molasses, sugar, and eggs, beat well.
2 mix in spices, salt, baking soda in to flour.  Add to wet ingredients.
3 chill 60-90 min. Roll into balls and roll in sugar.  Bake 8-10 min.
