Title:
Easy and Great Tasting Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tomatoes, diced
1 cloves garlic, minced
¼ cup fresh basil leaves, coarsely chopped
¼ tsp fresh lemon juice
2 tbs extra-virgin olive oil
salt and pepper to taste
French or Italian bread slices

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix all ingredients except bread; set aside. Toast slices of French or Italian bread.
Serve mixture, room temperature in a bowl with a spoon and lots of toasted bread slices.

Submitter: 
Angela
Description: 
This is an easy and great tasting bruschetta.  It is delicious over toasted Italian bread.
