Title:
Taco Tidbits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983548.jpg
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons butter or margarine
2 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
8 cups Corn ChexÂ® cereal
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Place butter in an 11-in. x 7-in. x 2-in. microwave-safe dish. Cover and microwave on high for 60-70 seconds or until melted. Add taco seasoning. Stir in the cereal until evenly coated. Microwave on high for 1 minute; stir. Heat 1 to 1-1/2 minutes longer; stir. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese; microwave for 1 minute. Stir; heat 1 minute longer.

Submitter: 
Sharon  Mensing
Description: 
'This four-ingredient combination is a great change of pace from typical snack mixes,' writes Sharon Mensing of Greenfield, Iowa. And it's a good thing the crispy treat is so simple to throw together. After one handful, your family will empty the bowl in no time.
