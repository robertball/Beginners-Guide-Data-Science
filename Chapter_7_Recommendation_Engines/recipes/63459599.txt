Title:
Cream Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 (8 oz.) pkgs. cream cheese
1 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
3 pkgs. Buddig Beef
2 bunches green onions (chop & use green part)
Garlic salt (or powder) to taste

Directions:
Use cream cheese at room temperature to make stirring easier.  Chop Buddig Beef into small squares.  Chop green part of onions into small pieces.  Mix together all ingredients and form into ball shape. Refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Rachel Faulkner
Description: 
We always have this cheese ball at holiday gatherings.  It is delicious, easy, and addictive!
