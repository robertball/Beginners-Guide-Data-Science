Title:
GREEN GODDESS FIESTA SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 c. torn salad greens
15 oz. can dark red kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 c. thinly sliced celery
1 avocado, seeded, peeled and cut - up
2 red onion slices, separated into rings
1 1/2 c. broken corn chips
2/3 c. Green Goddess dressing

Directions:
Mix together.  Add avocado, corn chips and dressing just before serving.

Submitter: 
Linda Girton Barton
Description: 
This is a recipe that Mom often took to church dinners or served to company.  It always got rave reviews.
Unfortunately, Green Goddess Dressing disappeared from the market.
