Title:
Church Window Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 20 oz bag of chocolate chips (24 oz bags work too)
1 stick butter
1 bag brightly colored marshmallows
1/2 C powdered sugar

Directions:
Melt butter and chocolate chips in microwave
Mix butter and chocolate
Let mixture cool
When they're no longer in danger of melting add marshmallows
Spoon mixture onto foil and form a log
Sprinkle with powdered sugar
Wrap mixture in foil (in shape of log) and refrigerate
When log becomes hard, cut into slices

Submitter: 
JOLLYGREEN23
Description: 
This recipe is super easy! Kids love this and it has been a tradition in my in-laws family during the holidays. They're beautiful and delicious!
