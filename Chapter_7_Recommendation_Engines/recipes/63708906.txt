Title:
Grilled Portobello and Goat Cheese Quesadilla Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2039902.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg portabello mushrooms, sliced
1/2 onion chopped
1 clove garlic chopped
1Tbsp oil
1/2 tsp cumin
1 tsp chili powder
Tortilla wraps
Goat Cheese (also try sharp cheddar or monteray jack with feta cheese)

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet
Add mushrooms, onions, and garlic and saute until soft
Add cumin & chili powder
Mix well.
Place 1/2 of mixture into tortilla shell (half side)
Sprinkle with goat cheese (and monterey jack if desired) and fold wrap
Cook over medium heat until crispy on outside
Serve with fresh veggies and dip or a side salad.
