Title:
Pork Roast with apples,  beer and sauerkraut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 to 4 lb. pork loin roast
1 lg. can sauerkraut
2 lg. apples
2 lg. onions
1/3 c. brown sugar
1 to 2 12oz beers (you decide)

Directions:
In large oven pan place roast.
Pour sauerkraut around roast.
Quarter apple and onion (don't need to peel apple).
Place around on top of sauerkraut.
Sprinkle with brown sugar and pour on 1 can of beer cover pan, sealing sides with foil.
Bake in 250 degree oven for 3 to 4 hours depending on size
of the roast.
Add the 2nd beer if too dry.
When ready to serve mix sauerkraut, apples and onions together
