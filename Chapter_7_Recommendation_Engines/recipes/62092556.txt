Title:
Potato Chapati Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mashed potatoes
2 cups whole wheat flour(approx)
salt to taste
1/2 tea spoon chilli powder
1/2 tea spoon turmeric powder
1 tablespoon finely cut corrinder or mixed dried herbs
3 tablespoons oil
1 tablespoon table butter

Directions:
In a large bowl take mashed potatoes. Mix in all the ingredients except wheat flour.
Mix well.
Add wheat flour a little at a time. Go on adding  wheat flour and  kneading till you obtain a stiff pliable dough.(If need be you may add a bit of water).
Apply oil to the dough, cover and keep aside for 10 minutes
Heat non-stick griddle to medium heat
Take lemon size balls of the dough & using dry flour roll them out to 1/4in thickness
Cook on both sides using up to 1 tea spoon oil to each side, till well cooked with brownish spots on both sides.
Brush with butter & serve hot.

An Indian bread which is eaten for breakfast with curds and pickle or served for special dinner with any curry
