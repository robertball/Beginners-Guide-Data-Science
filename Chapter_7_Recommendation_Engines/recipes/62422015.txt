Title:
Colorado Chokecherry Jelly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/454389.jpg
Ingredients:
24 oz. chokecherry juice
12 oz. "Old Orchard" Apple Cherry Juice (frozen juice concentrate
DO NOT ADD WATER TO THE JUICE AS THE DIRECTIONS SAY!
1 pkg. pectin
2 c. sugar
1 Tbsp lemon juice

Directions:
Put first 3 ingredients in large pan. DO NOT PUT THE SUGAR IN WITH THIS! IT WON'T GEL. Mix well.Add sugar. bring to a rolling boil and boil for 2 min.
Pour into prepared jelly jars and top with lids and wait for "PING".
Chokecherries are juiced the same as sand plumbs.

Submitter: 
Jannabofanna
Description: 
This jelly was made with Colorado chokecherries. The jelly is much darker than I remember from what grandma Miller made.
