Title:
Southwestern Breakfast Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983132.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 (1.48 pound) package JENNIE-O TURKEY STORE® Breakfast Sausage Links
5 eggs
2 tablespoons water
1/4 teaspoon chili powder
1 (7 ounce) can sweet corn niblets, drained
1/2 cup canned black beans, drained, rinsed
2 tablespoons chopped green chiles
4 MANNY'S® 8-inch Fajita Style Flour Tortillas, warmed
1 cup pepperjack cheese, shredded
2 tablespoons chopped cilantro

Directions:
In 10-inch nonstick skillet cook sausage over medium heat for 12 to 15 minutes, turning occasionally, until lightly browned. Remove sausage from pan; drain. Cool sausages slightly; slice into  inch pieces.
In small bowl whisk together eggs, water and chili powder until well beaten. Add egg mixture to skillet; cook over low heat. Once egg begins to set top with sliced sausage, corn, black beans and green chiles. Using spatula, lift edges of eggs as they cook, letting uncooked part run underneath until egg is set. Top with cheese and cilantro.
To serve spoon egg mixture into warmed tortillas. Roll up tortilla to enclose filling.

Submitter: 
JENNIE-O TURKEY STORE®
Description: 
Eggs, beans, corn and cheese are rolled inside tortillas with juicy sausage links and Southwest seasonings.
