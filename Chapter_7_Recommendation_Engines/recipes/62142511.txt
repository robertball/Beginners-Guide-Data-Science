Title:
Salmon Couscous Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
1/4 cup golden raisins, chopped
3 tablespoons chopped green olives
2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
3 tablespoons lemon juice
Salt and freshly ground black pepper
1 cup couscous
2 salmon fillets
1 cup water
1/3 cup white wine

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
In a large bowl stir together the parsley, raisins, olives, olive oil, and lemon juice.
Stir in the couscous to moisten completely and season generously with salt and pepper.
Put the salmon in a baking dish and season with salt and pepper.
Spoon the couscous mixture over the top of the fish and the remaining in the baking dish.
Add the water and wine over couscous.
Drizzle olive oil on the couscous that is on top of the salmon.
Put the dish in the oven and bake 25 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
Salmon and couscous baked together for a quick meal that needs no side dish. I am always looking for a new way to make couscous that isn't bland.
