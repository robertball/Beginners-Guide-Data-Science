Title:
Xavier Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 New York Strip steaks, at least 1" thick
8 slices swiss cheese
6 stalks fresh asparagus
2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
2 tbsp olive oil
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Sprinkle steaks with salt and pepper on all sides and place in a glass container with Worcestershire sauce. Coat all sides with sauce and place in Fridge.
Turn steaks after 15 mins and leave in fridge for an additional 15 mins.
Preheat grill with high heat.
Wash asparagus and cut about an inch off the bottoms, set aside.
Add steaks to grill and cover for 7 minutes.
Turn steaks using tongs, being careful not to pierce meat to ensure juices do not run out.
Add asparagus to grill and brush with an olive oil and salt mixture and cover for 6 minutes, turning asparagus once after 3 minutes.
Top each steak with 3 stalks of asparagus and 4 slices of swiss cheese and cover for additional 1-2 minutes, until cheese is melted.
Let steaks sit for 5-6 minutes before cutting or serving.
