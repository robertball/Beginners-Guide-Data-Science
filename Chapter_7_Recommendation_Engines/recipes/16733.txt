Title:
Bob's Blue Cheese Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1024247.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup crumbled blue cheese
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup sour cream
2 teaspoons lemon juice
1 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1 tablespoon honey
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon sea salt

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk together the blue cheese, mayonnaise, sour cream, lemon juice, hot pepper sauce, Worcestershire sauce, parsley, honey, pepper, and salt.  Chill until serving.

Submitter: 
GOOBNORTON
Description: 
Prepare a flavorful summer salad by serving this dressing over mixed mesclun greens, hearts of romaine, sliced cucumber, tomato, and red onion.
