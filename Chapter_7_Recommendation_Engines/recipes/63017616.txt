Title:
Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
7 oz marshmallow creme
8 oz cream cheese softened
1 cup cool whip
2 tbsp. orange juice
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together with a mixer-- or by hand of your strong. ;-) Then  chill
