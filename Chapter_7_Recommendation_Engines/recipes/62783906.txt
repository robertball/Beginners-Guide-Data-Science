Title:
Acorn Squash - quicker way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium acorn squash, halved and seeded
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons brown sugar
sprinkle of salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Cut it in half and seed it. Cut bottoms off a little bit if needed to level.  Microwave on high for 5 minutes. Put about a tablespoon of butter in each have and spread it around on the top edges and inside. Litely sprinkle nutmeg all over and then about twice as much cinnamon. (sprinkle of salt) Follow it up with brown sugar (1-2T each half depending on how sweet you like it) and bake at 350 for about 20 minutes
