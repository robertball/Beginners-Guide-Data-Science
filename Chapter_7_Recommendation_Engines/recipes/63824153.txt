Title:
Cheesy Potato and Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped onion
2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
3 cups peeled and cubed yukon gold potatoes
1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn
1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles
1 (2.5 ounce) package country style gravy mix
1 cup whole milk
1 cup half & half
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/8 cup real bacon bits

Directions:
In large saucepan, melt butter over medium high heat.  Add celery and onion; cook and stir until tender, about 5 minutes.
Add chicken broth; bring to a boil.  Add potatoes; cook and cover over medium-high heat for 20 to 25 minutes or until potatoes are soft, stirring occasionally.
Stir in corn and chiles; return to boiling.  Dissolve gravy mix in milk; stir into boiling mixture.  Add cheese; cook and stir over low heat until cheese is melted. Add bacon bits and stir until mixed.

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
Country-style milk gravy from a mix is added to vegetables simmered in chicken broth in this soup with Mexican-style processed cheese and green chiles.Modified from: Cheesy Potato and Corn Chowder
