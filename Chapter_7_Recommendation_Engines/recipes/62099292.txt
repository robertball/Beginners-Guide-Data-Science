Title:
Squeak's Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sleeves of Club crackers
2 sticks margarine or butter, melted
2 cans cut green beans, drained
1 8oz can Water Chestnuts, drained
2 Tbs Instant Onion
4 boiled eggs, chopped
1 small can Carnation milk
1 can Cream of Chicken soup
1 4 oz can mushrooms, drained (optional)

Directions:
Crush the Club crackers, add melted margarine and mix well.  Line a 9x13" baking dish with half the cracker mixture. Reserve the remaining cracker mixture.
In a large bowl, combine the remaining ingredients. Spoon into the baking dish over cracker mixture.
Cover green bean mixture with remaining half of cracker mixture.
Bake at 350 or until golden brown, about 30 min.

Submitter: 
Deana Slack
Description: 
This Green Bean casserole has a wonderful crunchy crust, combined with the creamy green bean mixture, it has to be tasted to really appreciate the flavor!
