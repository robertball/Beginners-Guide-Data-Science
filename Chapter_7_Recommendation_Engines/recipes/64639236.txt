Title:
Prime Rib Bone Slow Cooker Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 to 3 meaty leftover prime rib bones
2 medium onions, minced
2 large ribs celery with leaves, coarsely chopped
1 1/2 cups of  baby carrots,or regular carrots, coarsely chopped
1 1/2 cups of baby  potatoes or regular potatoes , coarsely chopped
1 overflowing cup sliced white mushrooms
1 14 ounce can of fire roasted diced tomatoes
3 cups beef broth
1 cup water
1 envelope of dried onion soup mix  (or dried onion soup mix with garlic)
1 tsp dried parsley
1 tsp ground black pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in slow cooker and stir well. Cook on  high for 5 hours.    Lift out prime rib bones and cut beef still attached to bone into small pieces off the bones and return prime rib to soup . Taste before serving and adjust seasonings.

Submitter: 
Phoebe
Description: 
When there really isn't enough prime rib left to make a substantial  meal but still meat stuck to the bones, try this!  Don't waste that expensive cut and the lovely bones add flavour.
