Title:
Spicy Pecan Whiskey Tart Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pastry
1/14 cups flour
2 tbsp. sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1/3 cup cold butter
2 tbsp cold water
1 tsp white vinegar
Filling
3 eggs
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup corn syrup 1/4 cup rye whiskey or bourbon
2 tbsp butter,melted
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp ground cloves
1/4 tsp allspice
1 1/4 cups pecan halves
2 tbsp corn syrup, warmed
Whipped cream for garnish

Directions:
To make pastry:
In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar and salt. Cut in butter with a pastry cutter until mixture resembles fine crumbs. Add water and vinegar and mix until blended but still crumbly. Squeeze together in small handfuls and pat evenly over bottom and up sides of a 9" (23cm) tart pan with a removable bottom. Press down firmly and refrigerate for 15 minutes. You can make ahead, cover and refrigerate for 24 hours.
Filling:
Preheat oven to 375F. In a bowl, whisk together eggs,sugar, corn syrup, rye, butter, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves and allspice. Stir in pecans. Scrape filling into prepared tart shell. Bake on the bottom rack of oven until filling is just firm to the touch about 45 minutes. Cover edge with foil if it starts to become too brown. Brush filling with corn yrup and let cool on a wire rack. You can bake, cool and cover and let stand at room temperature for up to 1 day . Gently remove sides, and base. Serve with a dollop of whipped cream. Serves 12.t
