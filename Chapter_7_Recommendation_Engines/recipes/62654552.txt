Title:
Rosebud's Garam Masala Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon ground cardamom
2 tablespoon ground cinnamon
2 tablespoon ground cloves
2 tablespoon whole cumin
2 tablespoon peppercorns
Blend spices together in a spice grinder or by hand using a mortar and pestle. Store in glass jar. Serve with the Basmati Rice. This is Garam Masala and I've used it for years. Not sure where I got it, but enjoy.
