Title:
Helen's Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup margarine
1 2/3 cups sugar
5 eggs
2 cups flour
½ teaspoon vanilla or other flavoring may be used…almond, rum, orange, lemon etc.

Directions:
Mix margarine and sugar together until fluffy.  Add eggs one at a time.  Fold in four with a spoon. Pour into greased and floured loaf pan. Bake ate 300 degrees for 90 minutes.

Submitter: 
SCOOBYVC
Description: 
My older neighbor use to make this when I was growing up. I have never tasted another pound cake I liked as much as this one.
