Title:
Streusel Topped Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c freshly roasted and cooled pumpkin (or 2 15 oz cans of 100% pumpkin)
2 14 oz low fat sweetened condensed milk (not evap milk)
2 eggs
2 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp ginger
1 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp salt
1 pie shell pastry
1/2 c brown sugar, firmley packed
4 tbsp flour
4 tbsp cold butter
1 c chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Combine pumpkin, sweetened condensed milk, eggs, spices and salt. Mix well, pour into pie shell. (if there is extra filling, put into small glass custard cups, or small ramekins.)
Put pie (and cups) in oven and bake for 15 mins. Reduce oven to 350 degrees.
Combine brown sugar, flour and nuts. Cut in cold butter until crumbly. Stir in chopped nuts. Sprinkle streusel mixture on the pie (and cups).
Bake pie another 40 mins (cups 15 mins) or until set.
Can be served warm, or at room temp with optional whip cream. Regfridgerate leftovers.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
A pumkin pie with the lightness and texture of soufle that melts in the mouth, with a spicey cruchy sweet topping. This has been a family expectation/favorite for many many years.
