Title:
Flourless Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Blender Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3109909.jpg
Ingredients:
1 very ripe banana
1/2 cup peanut butter (any nut butter works)
1 egg white
3 tbsp honey
1 tbsp almond extract or vanilla
1/4 tsp baking soda
dash of sea salt
1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven 400 degrees
Prepare a muffin tin with nonstick cooking spray
To your blender, add all ingredients except mini chocolate chips
Blend ingredients until smooth
Add chocolate chips & stir into the batter by hand
Fill muffin cavities 3/4 full
Bake 9 minutes until a toothpick can be removed clean
Allow to cool in the pan 5 minutes before removing
Remove from the pan then cool completely on a baking rack

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
A flourless muffin made with 8 everyday ingredients. This soon to be favorite recipe's a quick mix in the blender for the easiest baking of your life.
