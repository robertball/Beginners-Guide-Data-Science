Title:
Chocolate-Almond Tofu Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/449486.jpg
Ingredients:
4oz melted butter or margarine (preferably non-hydrogenated)
1 1/2 pkg graham crackers
1/2 cup almonds (almost any nut will do)
2 pkgs silken tofu
3 cups melted chocolate chips (semi-sweet or milk)
2 tbs vanilla extract

Directions:
1. preheat oven to 400. in food processor combine melted butter, graham crackers, and nuts. pulse to a course crumb.
2. press that into a large pie plate and bake 15 min at 400 degrees until slightly toasted cool 15 min.
3. melt chocolate chips over a double boiler
4. combine tofu, melted chocolate chips and vanilla in food processor until smooth and creamy. scraping sides several times.
5. pour into pre-baked crust
6. chill at least 4 hours.
