Title:
Pork Chop Suey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pork cut into cubes (fresh or may use leftover from roast)
1 1/2 t brown sugar
1/4 t salt
2 chicken bouillon cubes
small can mushrooms
2 1/4 C water
1 1/2 T cornstarch
1 can bean sprouts, drained
1 C chopped onion
1 t ginger
1/2 t pepper
2 C sliced celery
1 T soy sauce

Directions:
If fresh pork, brown in skillet and remove. Add onions and saute untl soft. Return pork to pan (if using leftover put in now), stir in brown sugar, ginger, salt, pepper, bouillon cubes, mushrooms and 2 C water; cover and simmer 40 min. Add celery, cover and simmer 10 min.
In a cup blend cornstarch, soy sauce and remaining water, stir into meat mixture. Cook, stirring constantly until it thickens and boils 3 min. Stir in drained sprouts; heat until bubbly. Serve with rice or oriental noodles. (May substitute chicken for pork.)
