Title:
Tomato Basil Soup with Tortellini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1777494.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
3 Tbs. olive oil
1 28 oz. can fire-roasted diced tomatoes
fresh basil, to taste, chopped
4 oz. cream cheese, cubed
2 cups milk

Directions:
Saute onion and garlic over medium heat in the olive oil. Cook until vegetables are tender.  Add tomatoes and basil.  Stir together and cook just to simmering.  Add cream cheese and milk and stir together until cream cheese melts.  Using an immersion blender, puree all ingredients to a smooth consistency.  Cook over low to medium heat for 20-30 minutes longer.  Serve topped with fresh basil, Parmesan cheese or croutons.

Submitter: 
Angie Yetterboe
Description: 
A creamy warming soup that is fast and easy to prepare.
