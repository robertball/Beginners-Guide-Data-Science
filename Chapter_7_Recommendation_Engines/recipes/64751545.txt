Title:
Easy Asian Fusion Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c mayonnaise
1/2 c chopped cilantro
1/4 c Schechwan General Tso sauce
1/ 1/2 tsp minced garlic
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/8 tsp black pepper
1/8 tsp salt
1 1/2 lbs beef patties
Soy Sauce
Chopped Lettuce
Hamburger Buns

Directions:
1.  Heat cast iron pan on medium-high heat.
2. Add mayonnaise, chopped cilantro, General Tso sauce, and garlic to a small mixing bowl.
3.  Add crushed red pepper, onion powder, black pepper, and salt to mixture and stir well.
4.  Add 4 hamburger patties to the pan add 3-4 drops of soy sauce to each burger and then add black pepper to your taste.  Flip when ready to your taste.
5. Prepare hamburger buns by adding a generous amount of mayo mixture to buns and chopped lettuce.
6. Add cooked patties to hamburger buns.

Submitter: 
Jennie Reddig
Description: 
Easy Asian Fusion Burgers are a fusion between Chinese and American flavors.  I did not add ginger because of my families taste but I think 1 tsp might be a good add-in.
Make it keto by chopping up the burger and adding it to a low-carb tortilla.
