Title:
Honey Mustard Chicken with Pretzel Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBS Red Wine Vinegar
1 TBS Yellow Mustard
1 TBS Spicy Mustard
1 TBS Grainy Mustard
1 TBS Honey
1 TBS Vegetable Oil
½ Cup Mayo
2 TBS Yellow Mustard
3 TBS Honey
½ tsp Salt
4 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
4 cups of pretzels
Cooking Spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Mix together the Red Wine Vinegar, Yellow Mustard (1 TBS), Spicy Mustard, Grainy Mustard, Honey (1 TBS), and Vegetable Oil.
Pound out chicken until about ¼ inch thick.
Coat with the mustard mixture.
Put 4 cups of pretzels in a Ziploc bag.
Crush with a rolling pin until finely ground.
Dredge chicken in pretzels until coated.
Place chicken on a foil lined baking sheet sprayed with cooking spray.
Spray tops of the chicken with additional cooking spray.
Bake at 400 degrees for 25 minutes or until golden brown and cooked through.
Turn chicken halfway through cooking.
While chicken cooks, mix together mayo, yellow mustard (2 TBS), honey (3 TBS) and salt.
Drizzle honey mustard sauce over cooked chicken and serve.

Description: 
A crunchy and salty crust to dress up chicken!
