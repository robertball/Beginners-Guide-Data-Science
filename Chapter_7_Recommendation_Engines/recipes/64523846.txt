Title:
Sauted Tilapia with Squash and Spinish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Butternut Squash, diced
Olive Oil
salt Pepper
Baby Spiniage
Capers, rinsed
3 Scallops
8 garlic cloves
2 Tilapia filets

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Peel and dice Squash into 1/2 pieces
Cut scallions into wedges
Place scallions, squash and garlic on baking sheet, add salt Pepper and drizzle with oil and toss.  Bake till tender.
Remove two cloves of garlic and one scallion from baking sheet.  Mince garlic and thinly slice scallion length wise.  Place in oil over medium heat.  Season Tilapia with salt, pepper and it Italian seasoning.  Saute Tilapia till tender and lightly browned on both sides, about 10 min.
Remove Tilapia from pan.  Add spinish and capers cook only till slightly wilted, about 1 minute.
Place Squash on plate, top with spinish and tolapia.  Enjoy.

Submitter: 
Randy Warner
Description: 
Tilapia served over roasted Butternut Squash and sauted spinish.
