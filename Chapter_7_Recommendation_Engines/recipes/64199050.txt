Title:
Kolachis Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
1 Tbsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup shortening
1/2 cup milk, scalded
1 Tbsp yeast
1 egg, slightly beaten
1/2 tsp vanilla
Pie fillling
Powdered sugar

Directions:
Mix flour, sugar and salt.  Cut in shortening to make coarse crumbs.
Scald milk and cool until warm.  Add yeast and dissolve.  Add egg and vanilla.  Add milk mixture to flour mixture and mix well.
Divide dough into 4 parts.  Roll out each part on board dusted with powdered sugar.  Cut into small squares.  Place 1 heaping teaspoon pie filling in center of square.  Fold opposite corners toward center and pinch together.  Let stand 10 minutes.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 10-12 minutes.  Roll in powdered sugar.
