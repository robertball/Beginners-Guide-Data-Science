Title:
Broccoli Cheese Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon olive oil
1/4 cup onion, chopped
4 cups chicken broth
1 cup rice
1 small head broccoli chopped
1 rounded cup sharp cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
1. Cook onion in oil until translucent, about 2 minutes, add rice and stir to coat with oil, cook until lightly toasted 2-4 minutes.
2. Add 1/2 cup broth cook stirring constantly until broth in completely absorbed.  Add the rest of the broth 1/2 cup at a time stirring constantly.
3. While risotto is cooking boil broccoli briefly 2-3 minutes (not necessary if using frozen broccoli), drain well.
4. When the last 1/2 cup of broth is mostly absorbed add broccoli.  Continue cooking until liquid is absorbed.
5. Stir in sharp cheddar.  Serve wam.

Submitter: 
Suzanne
Description: 
I wanted to make broccoli cheese casserole but I didn't want to use condensed soup or processed cheese sauce.  This is a better alternative, I was surprised by how well it came out and how good it tastes!
