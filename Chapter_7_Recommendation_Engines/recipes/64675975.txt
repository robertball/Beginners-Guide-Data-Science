Title:
Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Some butter
Olive oil
12 ounces Arborio rice
Some salt
Zest of a lemon
Hot chicken stock
Another lemon
Couple of fistfulls of Parmigiano-reggiano
Half a carton of creme fraiche

Directions:
Combine the butter and olive oil in a saucepan, melt
Add rice, some salt and the lemon zest
Saute until the rice turns golden
Add a ladle of hot stock; wait until absorbed before adding another
Continue until rice is soft
Stir in zest of another lemon, cheese and creme fraiche
Serve hot
