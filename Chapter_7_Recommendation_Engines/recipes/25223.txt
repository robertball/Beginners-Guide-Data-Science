Title:
Super-Good Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/205348.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup rolled oats
1 cup water
1 tablespoon honey
1 tablespoon wheat germ
1 teaspoon flaxseed oil
1/4 cup soy milk

Directions:
Mix the oats and water and microwave on medium power for 5 minutes.  Stir in the honey, wheat germ, and flax oil.  Top with the soy milk.

Description:
