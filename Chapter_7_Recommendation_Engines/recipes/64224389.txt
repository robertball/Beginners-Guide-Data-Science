Title:
Grandma Bettie's light fruit cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1665958.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups butter
2 cups sugar
6 eggs
1 cup raisins
1 cup blanched almonds
1 cup candied or dried pineapple
2 cups candied cherries
1 cup flour plus 2 1/2 cups flour
1tsp. Baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1tsp. vanilla extract
1tsp. Almond extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees F. Line two loaf pans with brown paper, or parchment. You may also use one large round pan 9"x4" high instead. Coat fruits and nuts with the 1 cup of flour. Cream butter, sugar and eggs. Sift the 2 1/2 cups flour with baking powder and salt. Add the flour to the butter mixture along with the vanilla and almond extracts. Fold in the fruits. Spoon into prepared pans and spread batter evenly. Bake for 1 1/2 to 2 hours depending on size of pan. Test with toothpick to make sure it is done! After cooling, wrap tightly and age in a cool place for 2 to 4 weeks.

Submitter: 
Leanne Mentz
Description: 
An easy light fruit cake that my mother made most years. Try substituting fruits that you like! To me it's the taste and smell of Christmas.
