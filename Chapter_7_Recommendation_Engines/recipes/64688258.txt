Title:
Grilled Corn-Basil Pesto Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ears fresh sweet corn
3/4 cup jarred basil pesto
1/4 cup shaved Parmesan cheese
2 tablespoons thinly sliced fresh basil
1/2 teaspoon lemon zest
Cracked black pepper

Directions:
For a gas or charcoal grill, grill the corn on the rack of a covered grill directly over medium-high heat for 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly charred, turning occasionally. Remove from grill. Let cool until easy to handle.
Cut kernels from cobs; discard cobs. In  large bowl combine the corn kernels and pesto. Stir to coat.
Transfer to a serving dish. Top with Parmesan cheese, basil, lemon zest, and cracked black pepper.
