Title:
Pinneapple Dipping Sauce for Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup packed brown sugar
1 Tbls. cornstarch
1/4 tsp. salt
1 can (8 1/4 oz) crushed pineapple in syrup, undrained
2 Tbls. lemon juice
1 Tbls. yellow mustard

Directions:
Mix brown sugar, cornstarch and salt in a 1 quart saucepan. Stir in pinneapple, lemon juice and mustard. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens and boils. Boil and stir 1 minute. If using the sauce as a glaze put it on the ham the last 20 minutes of baking.

Submitter: 
SUDEMERS
Description: 
My Mother in law gave me this recipe. Every time she make a ham she makes this sauce to dip the meat in. What is nice about this recipe you can also use it to glaze the ham.
