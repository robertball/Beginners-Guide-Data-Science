Title:
Deb's Spicy Enchilada Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil, 2 cloves of garlic-minced, 1/3 cup chopped onion, 1/2 teaspoon oregano, 1 teaspoon chipolte chili powder, 1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder, 1/2 teaspoon dried basil, 1/8 teaspoon pepper, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin, 1 teaspoon dried parsley, 4-5 roma tomatoes chopped, 2 jalepeno peppers chopped, 1 (8 ounce) can tomatoe sauce, 1/2 cup water

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.  Add the garlic and onion, and saute for 1-2 minutes.  Add all the remaining ingredients except the water.
Mix together and then stir in the water.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 minutes.
