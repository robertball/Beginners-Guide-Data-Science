Title:
Pecan Honey Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup honey
1 cup butter or margarine.
1/2 cup chopped pecans-toasted

Directions:
Slowly beat the honey into the butter or margarine until well blended. Stir in chopped pecans. Pack in gift containers and refrigerate. Bring to room temperature before serving on biscuits, rolls, or other bread.

Submitter: 
CHARITYBOO
Description: 
Yummy butter to use on Starbuck's pumpkin bread, pancakes, waffles, or toast.
