Title:
Garlic Home Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large or 8 smaller russet potatoes, quartered and sliced
6 tbs butter or margarine
1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
5 clove garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Melt the butter and olive oil in a large, non stick, coated fry pan over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and cook for about 5 mins or until tender. Add the potatoes and spread out in an even layer. Season with 1/2 the salt and pepper; stir to coat potatoes evenly than add the rest of the salt and pepper.
Cover and cook on med heat for about 20 mins. Remove conver and flip potaoes. Increase heat to medium-high and cook for another 10 minutes, stirring frequently, or until potatoes are tender.
