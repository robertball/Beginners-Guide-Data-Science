Title:
Denise's Always Requested Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb box elbow macaroni noodles
1 c. Mayonaise
1/2 chopped large purple onion
1 large firm tomato
1/3 chopped green pepper
1/3 chopped red pepper
1/3 chopped yellow pepper
1 lb block montery chddar cheese (cubed small)
Zesty Italian Salad Dressing
dash of salt and pepper

Directions:
Boil macaroni noodles as directed on box, rinse, strain, let cool.
Mix in mayonaise, all chopped peppers and onions
Add tomatoes just before serving.
Tomatoes should be de seeded and only the firm part used.
Add Zesty Italian Salad Dressing, salt and pepper to taste
Chill for one hour and serve

Submitter: 
Denise Denise
Description: 
This macaroni salad is unique and colorful!  It is great for out door cookouts and get togethers!  Even kids love it as much as the adults do!
