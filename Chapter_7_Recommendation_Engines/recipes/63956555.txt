Title:
Little Cheddar Meat Loaves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 cup shredded cheddar
1/2 cup quick cooking oats
1/2 chopped onion
1 teaspoon salt
1 pound ground beef
2/3 cup ketchup
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 1/2 teaspoon mustard

Directions:
In bowl, beat egg and milk.  Stir in cheese, oats, onion and salt.  Add beef and mix well.  Shape into 8 loaves.  Place in greased baking dish.  Combine ketchup, brown sugar and mustard.  Spoon over loaves.  Bake at 350 45 minutes, meat thermometer reads 160.

Tasty mini meat loaves topped with sweet and tangy sauce.
