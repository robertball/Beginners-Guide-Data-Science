Title:
Crispy Crunchy Chocolate Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 1/4 oz mini marshmallows
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/4 cup butter
1 1/2 cups Rice Krispies
1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/8 cup butter
1/8 cup milk
1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Grease an 8x8 baking dish and set aside.
In a microwave safe bowl combine mini marshmallows, peanut butter, 1/2 cup of chocolate chips, and 1/4 cup butter.
Cover and microwave on high for 2 to 2 1/2 minutes.
Stir until well combined, mixture will be lumpy.
Add Rice Krispies and stir until coated. Spread mixture evenly in baking dish.
In another microwave safe bowl combine 1/2 cup chocolate chips, 1/8 cup butter and 1/8 cup milk.
Cover and microwave on high 1 to 1 1/2 minutes.
Mix well.
Add powdered sugar and vanilla.
Using electric mixer, beat until icing is smooth.
Spread evenly over Rice Krispie mixture.
Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours. Cut into bars and serve.
