Title:
Po-Girl Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices of your favorite bread
1 teaspoon Mayonnaise
3 tomato slices
dash of salt
dash of pepper

Directions:
Toast 2 slices of your favorite bread -
Slather one slice with mayonnaise
Sprinkle with salt and pepper to liking
Add tomato slices to second piece of Toast
Cover with a piece of sliced cheese
Place on a plate and put in microwave 10-15 seconds
or just long enough to melt the cheese
Put your two pieces together and enjoy..

Submitter: 
Crixxie
Description: 
Perfect for a simple sandwich anytime.
Here's one for the girls that the guys take a liking to as well.
Enjoy !!
