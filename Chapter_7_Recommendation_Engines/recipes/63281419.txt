Title:
Garlic Baked Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tilapia fillets
8 tbsp melted butter/margarine
4 tsp lemon juice
8 tsp garlic powder
4 tsp parsley
8 tsp Lemon-Pepper seasoning

Directions:
Pre-heat the oven to 375 degrees F.
Place fillets in a baking dish, and pour butter and lemon juice on each side of fish. Mix herbs together, and sprinkle on each side of fillets.
Place uncovered baking dish into oven for about 15 minutes or until opaque and flaky, turning over halfway through.

A flavourful fish fillet, and easy to make!
