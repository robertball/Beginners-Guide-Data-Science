Title:
Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/796557.jpg
Ingredients:
8 Whole Eggs
4 Cups Milk
2/3 Cup Sugar
1 Teaspoon Cinnamon
1 Teaspoon Vanilla
1/2 Teaspoon Salt
1 Cup Raisins
6 Cups Bread Cubes, Dried

Directions:
Beat together until smooth eggs, milk, sugar, cinnamon, vanilla, and salt.
Fill 11"X12" pan with dried bread cubes. Sprinkle with raisins to taste.
Pour egg mixture over all.
Bake in 350Â° oven for an hour to and hour and a half. (Or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.)

Great for a weekend breakfast with a large family.
