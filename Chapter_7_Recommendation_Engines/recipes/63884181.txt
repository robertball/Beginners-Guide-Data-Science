Title:
Pizza Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1077563.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. cream cheese, softened
1/2 c pizza sauce
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1/2 c chopped pepperoni
1/3 c diced red bell pepper
5 med green onions, sliced
1/2 c shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 c shredded basil leaves
hard breadsticks or tortilla chips, if desired

Directions:
Set oven to broil. In small bowl, stir cream cheese, pizza sauce, & garlic until well mixed. Spread in thin layer on 12" ovenproof serving plate. Top with pepperoni, bell pepper & green onions. Sprinkle with cheese.
Broil with top 4" from from heat 1-2 mins or until cheese is melted. Sprinkle with basil. Serve immediately with breadsticks.
