Title:
Vegan Applesauce Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup brown sugar
1 T ground flax or chia seed mixed with
3 T warm water
1 1/4 cup applesauce
1 cup whole spelt flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
3 tablespoons applesauce
1 tablespoon soy milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
In a medium bowl, mix together the brown sugar and thickened flax mixture until smooth. Stir in applesauce. Combine the flour, baking soda, salt and pumpkin pie spice; stir into the applesauce mixture until well blended. Spread evenly into the prepared pan.
Bake for 25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are golden. Cool in the pan over a wire rack.
In a small bowl, mix together the confectioners' sugar and applesauce. Stir in vanilla and milk until smooth. Spread over cooled bars before cutting into squares.
