Title:
Roasted Carrots and Radishes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/371792.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of radishes
1 cup of baby carrots (or large carrots- peeled and cut in 1 inch chunks)
2 TBS olive oil
Garlic salt and lemon pepper to taste

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.
Wash carrots and radishes.
Trim ends off radishes and slice in half or quarter, depending on size.
Place carrots and radishes on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil.
Sprinkle with garlic salt and lemon pepper and toss to coat.
Bake for 20-25 minutes until tender.
