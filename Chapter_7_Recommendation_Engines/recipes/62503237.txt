Title:
Yukon Low-Bush Cranberry and Orange muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4483084.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large oranges
1 cup flour
1 cup graham cracker crumbs
½ cup brown sugar
2 tsp. baking powder
½ tsp. salt
1 cup cranberries
1 cup raisins
½ cup chopped pecans (preferably toasted)
1 egg
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F.
Grease muffin tins
Finely grate peel from one orange and squeeze juice from both.
Measure juice and add enough water to bring volume up to 1 cup.
Measure flour, graham cracker crumbs, brown sugar, baking powder and salt into a large mixing bowl.
Stir with a fork until well mixed and make a well in the centre of the bowl. Set bowl aside.
Cut cranberries in half, stir cranberries with raisin, pecans and orange peel in a small mixing bowl.
Add to flour mixture and mix well.
In a small bowl, lightly beat egg and stir orange juice, oil and vanilla into egg mixture.
Add to flour mixture and stir until just combined.
Immediately spoon batter into muffin cups. Bake in center of oven until a dark golden color.  About 20-25 minutes.
Remove from oven and let stand 5 minutes, then turn onto cooling rack.
