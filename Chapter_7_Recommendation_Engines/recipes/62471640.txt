Title:
Jan's "Like Magic" Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/479295.jpg
Ingredients:
1 16 ounce jar peanut butter (I had low fat on hand)
1 16 ounce container marshmallow fluff
2 extra large eggs
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 12 ounce bag of chocolate chips

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
line baking sheets with parchment paper.
Put all ingredients in to a mixing bowl and combine until well blended.
Drop by rounded tablespoon about 2 inches apart on cookie sheets.
Bake 12 minutes or until golden and "set" do not over bake.
Cool 2 minutes them remove to wire rack to cool completely.

Submitter:

Description: 
I found this recipe amongst my moms hand written collection.  No title... just the ingredients with the note that they bake up like magic.  I had to try it myself to believe it!  They are very good, chewy and best of all EASY!
