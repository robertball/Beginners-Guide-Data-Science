Title:
Mom's Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large potatoes, peeled and sliced
1 large onion, chopped
1 tbsp flour
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup butter or margarine
Milk - enough to cover potatoes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease a large casserole dish.
Place a layer of potatoes in the casserole dish.   Dot with butter and sprinkle with onions.  Repeat, making 3 layers.   Pour enough milk to barely cover potatoes.
Bake at 350 degrees until tender, about 1 hour.
