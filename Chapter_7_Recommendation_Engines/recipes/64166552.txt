Title:
Absolutely Delicious - Russian Chicken Stew (Zharkoe)! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1452303.jpg
Ingredients:
- 2 Lbs. chicken thighs (bone-in or boneless)
- 1 Large Onion, diced
- 3 med size potatoes (red or baking)
- 1 med carrot
- 3 or 4 Baby Bella mushrooms
- 2 or 3 colorful small peppers (or one large one)
- 3 Bay leafs
- 1 1/2 Cup Chicken Broth
- 1 1/2 tsp. Corn Starch
- 2 Tbsp. Dill paste
- garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, poultry seasoning
- salt/pepper
- Olive or Vegetable Oil

Directions:
Rinse and pat dry the meat. Make you own chicken rub by combining: garlic powder, onion powder, poultry seasoning, paprika, salt and pepper. Rub the chicken pieces and let them absorb the flavor.
Meanwhile,  dice and brown one large onion in a deep pan. Transfer to a baking dish.
Turn heat to med-hi, add more oil, then add the thighs. Cook for 5 - 10 min on each side until brown. Add sliced mushrooms and carrots. Continue cooking for few more minutes. Transfer to a baking dish.
Add the potatoes and peppers to the pan. Dust with spices listed above. Cook for a few minutes. It doesn't have to be completely cooked since you will continue cooking in the oven. Transfer to a baking dish.
Dissolve the corn starch in the chicken broth and add to the pan. Add the dill paste and bay leafs. Bring to boil and turn off the heat. Pour on top of the chicken and potatoes.
Bake in the Preheated 350F oven Covered with foil for 40 min. Remove the foil and continue baking for another 20 min. Enjoy!
