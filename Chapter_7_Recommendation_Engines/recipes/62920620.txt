Title:
Chicken potatoe baked with sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Thin chicken breasts
6 med size gold potatoes
4 Fresh chopped some Tomatoes or 2 large cans diced tomatoes
Kosher salt
Black Pepper
Good Olive oil
1 tablespoon minced fresh rosemary
1 large head of garlic peeled
1 tea spoon Fennel Seeds (Very Important do not omit)
1 can of tomatoe puree
1 1/2 cups chicken stock
1 cup white wine
1 cup of favorite white cheese shed.

Directions:
Pat Chicken dry with paper towel and season with salt, pepper, and rosemary.  Heat the olive oil over med heat and brown chicken until browned on both side.  Remove and place on plate for later.
In the same pan add garlic, fennel seeds, tomatoes, tomatoe puree, chicken stock, white wine, salt and pepper.  Stir and scrape the brown bits on the bottom of pan. Simmer for 30 minutes or until tomotoes are soft and cooked.  And garlic is tender.
Mean while cut potatoes in slices and place in the bottom of a cassrole dish.  Preheat oven to 300 degrees.  Potatoes are now layers in the pan, now next layer chicken over potatoes.
Carefully pour the sauce in the food processor and puree unjtil smooth. Place the sauce in the cassrole dish over potatoes and chicken.  Top with cheese and cover.  Bake for 45-55 minutes or until potatoes done. Serve with crusty french bread.
