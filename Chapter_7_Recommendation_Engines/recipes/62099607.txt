Title:
Roasted Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large heads of broccoli, cut into florets
1/2 cup olive oil
2 large cloves of garlic, minced
1-1/2 tablespoons of Grill Seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Mix together oil, garlic and grill seasoning.  In a large bowl, pour mixture over broccoli florets and stir to coat thoroughly.  Spread coated broccoli on baking sheet and bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes, or until broccoli reaches preferred tenderness.

Submitter: 
Randi
Description: 
A delicious side dish, easy enough for every day but interesting enough for special dinners.
