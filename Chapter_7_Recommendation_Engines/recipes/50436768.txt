Title:
Candied Yams Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (29 ounce) can sweet potatoes
2-3 T. butter, cut into pieces
1 T. white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 1/2 cups miniature marshmallows (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Place sweet potatoes in a medium baking dish. Sprinkle with a light dusting of cinnamon. Distribute butter pieces evenly over the sweet potatoes. Sprinkle l T. of white sugar over potatoes. Sprinkle with brown sugar. Layer with miniature marshmallows.
Bake in the preheated oven 25 minutes, or until sweet potatoes are tender and marshmallows have melted.

Submitter: 
Dorothy Clark
Description: 
This is a simple and quick way to enjoy a Thanksgiving favorite! Canned yams or sweet potatoes may be used.Modified from: Candied Yams
