Title:
Spicey Veggie Crumbles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Package or container of crumbles (about 1 and 1/3 cup to 1 and ¾ cup)
2 inches of fresh ginger, shredded
¼ tsp of fresh ground black pepper
½ sweet onion minced
1 clove garlic minced
1 to 3 handfulls of fresh baby spinach
1 tbsp of extra virgin olive oil, more if needed
2 tbsp of soy sauce
Other spices to taste

Directions:
Put oil in 8 to 10 inch skillet.  When hot add onion.  Saute until soft.
Place crumbles in a zip lock back (unless they came that way) and close bag except for 1 inch and microwave for 3-4 minutes to soften.
Add crumbles, garlic and all seasonings including ginger to skillet and continue to cook, stirring frequently.  Add more oil if necessary.
When everything is good and hot, add the spinach and continue to cook and stir until all spinach is wilted.
