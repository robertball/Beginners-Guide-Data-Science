Title:
Ham and Creamy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Small pre-cooked ham, sliced or cubed
1 large bag frozen Hash Brown type shredded potatoes
1 can reduced fat cream of Mushroom soup
1 cup skim milk
4 oz. (1/2 block) fat free cream cheese
small bundle scallions, cleaned and sliced thinly
1 can sliced mushrooms
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except ham together until well combined. Pour 1/2 cup water in bottom of crockpot and cover with a layer of potato mixture, then ham, then potato mixture, then more ham, top with remaining potato mixture. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese, if desired. Cook covered on low for 2-4 hours or until completely heated through.
