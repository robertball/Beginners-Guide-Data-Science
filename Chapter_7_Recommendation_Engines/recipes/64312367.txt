Title:
Herb Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large egg(s)
1/2 cup(s) egg substitute
2 tablespoon milk, fat-free
pepper, black ground
1 pinch thyme, dried
1 pinch basil, dried
1 pinch parsley, fresh
1 pinch chives, fresh
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Beat egg, egg substitute, milk, herbs, salt, and pepper together. Pour into a small pie plate. Cover with waxed paper. Microwave on high 2 minutes or until cooked through, stirring once during cooking. Remove from microwave and keep covered. Let stand 1 to 2 minutes. Fold in half. Serve immediately.
