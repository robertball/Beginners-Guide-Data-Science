Title:
Oil Free Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 c rolled oats
1-2 c ribbon flaked coconut
1-2 c almonds
In blender:
2 bananas
1 c dates, pitted
1/2 c water
1 tsp salt
1 T vanilla

Directions:
Put the dry ingredients in a bowl and mix. Pour over it the blended wet ingredients and mix well with your hands. Put on a baking pans. Put in oven on lowest setting and leave overnight. In morning, done with no mixing needed. Store in air tight container. 1/2 cup is a serving - use to top hot cereal or even fruit.
