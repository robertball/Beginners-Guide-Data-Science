Title:
Braised Beef with Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lb lean stewing beef
1/4 cup flour
2 Tbsp oil
1 onion, chopped
1/5 stalk celery, finely chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp parsley, finely chopped
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
Pinch of thyme
Pinch of black pepper
2 cups beef stock
1/4 lb fresh mushrooms, sliced

Directions:
Cut beef into 1/2 inch cubes and dredge with flour.  In large saucepan, brown beef cubes in oil.  Lower heat and add remaining ingredients except mushrooms.
Simmer until beef is tender, about 40 minutes.  Add mushrooms last 10 minutes of cooking time.
