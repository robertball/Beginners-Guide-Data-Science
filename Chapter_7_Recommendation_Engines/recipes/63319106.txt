Title:
Rice Cooker Rice A Roni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 rice cooker cups of rice
2 cups chicken broth
1/2 cups angel hair pasta in 1/2 inch pieces
1 tbs canola oil
1 tsp salt
2 tsp Mrs. Dash Onion and Herb seasoning

Directions:
Brown rice and pasta in rice cooker with 1 tbs canola oil.  Add remaining ingredients and cook according to instructions.
