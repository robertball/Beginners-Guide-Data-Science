Title:
Smothered Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/735355.jpg
Ingredients:
4 potatoes, washed, peeled, snd cut
1 med onion, finely diced
4 tbls oil (bacon grease is even better)
salt and pepper (to taste)

Directions:
Wash, peel and cut potatoes into about 1/4 inch circles.
dice onion fine
Heat oil in skillet.
Add in a layer of potaoes,
add a layer of onions,
lightly salt and pepper that layer,
repeat: layering unil ingredients are all seasoned and in skillet.
Cook covered, on med heat, until potatoes are tender. "potatoes on bottom will brown, to keep from burning, occationaly stir by flipping potatoes from bottom to top."

Submitter: 
Nauntie
Description: 
Golden brown soft fried seasoned potatoes, are great for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. We grew up on this dish!
