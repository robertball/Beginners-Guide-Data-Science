Title:
lime white chocolate muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1/2 cup white sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup lime juice
1/4 cup coconut rum
1/2 cup butter, melted
2 eggs
2/3 cup white chocolate chips
1/4 cup brown sugar for topping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Either oil 12-cup muffin tin, or put a paper liner in each cup.
In medium bowl, combine dry ingredients (flour, white sugar, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt.)
In a small bowl, combine lime juice, rum, melted butter, and eggs.
Slowly stir the wet mixture into the dry mixture, until moistened.
Fold in the white chocolate chips.
Pour batter into the muffin cups. Sprinkle additional brown sugar on the top of each muffin.
Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes, or until a knife inserted comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Elske G.
Description: 
Yummy cake-like muffins, with lime and white chocolate. I used freshly squeezed lime juice, but I'm sure bottled lime juice would be good too. These are closer to tart than sweet - not sour, but a nice change from very sweet desserts.
