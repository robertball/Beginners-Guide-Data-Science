Title:
Warm Zucchini Couscous Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. dry couscous
1 1/4 c. water
3 Tbsp. olive oil
1/3 c. chopped red onion
2 small zucchini, diced
8 oz. canned chickpeas, rinsed
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1/2 c. parmesan cheese
1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper
1 lemon, quartered
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a small saucepan, bring water to a boil.
Immediately drop couscous in water, cover pan and remove from heat.
Meanwhile, heat olive oil in pan, saute zucchini for 3 minutes.
Add red onion, garlic, and chickpeas to zucchini, saute until onions begin to lose color, about 5 minutes over medium heat.
Season veggies with red pepper and salt.
Add the prepared couscous to pan of veggies.
Stir in parmesan cheese.
Squeeze on lemon juice, beginning with one quarter slice, adding more to taste.
Add additional salt and pepper to taste.
Serve warm or cold.

Submitter: 
SPEARL20
Description: 
I like to eat this simple summer salad with ricotta and hummus for a quick meal.  You can dress it up or down as much as you like with more or less olive oil, cheese and lemon juice.  Delicious with grilled meats in the summer as a quick side dish.
