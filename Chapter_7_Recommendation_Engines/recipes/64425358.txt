Title:
Greek Lamb Shish Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2313573.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb lamb
Juice of 2 lemons
4 T olive oil
2 T grated onion
2 T minced green chilies
1 t coriander
1 t ginger
1 clove garlic, mashed to a pulp
2 t curry powder (can sub turmeric)
3 t salt
VEGGIES (choose whatever you like)
Mushroom caps
Cherry tomatoes
Green pepper
Onion
Melted butter

Directions:
Cut lamb into 1-1/2" pieces. Combine lemon, oil, onion, chilies, coriander, ginger, garlic, curry, and salt. Marinate 4-6 hours. Run the marinated meat onto skewers, alternating with similarly sized slices of vegetables.  Grill to likeness, basting with melted butter.

Submitter: 
Betsy B
Description: 
This was George & Franny's family secret - they didn't give me this recipe until 12/11/87. The actual recipe is from the Gourmet Cookbook, published in 1954.
