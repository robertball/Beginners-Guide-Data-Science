Title:
Victor's Pasta Fagioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans cannellini beans – undrained
2 medium onions – diced
4 garlic cloves – diced small
1 lb bacon – cook crisp and break into pieces
2 – 15 oz cans tomato sauce
1 – 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
A dash of each – pepper, parsley, basil, and garlic powder.
1 box noodles – ditalini -  Cooked al dante

Directions:
Slightly brown onions and garlic in a large pot. Add all the above ingredients except noodles. Simmer 45 minutes. Add noodles before serving
