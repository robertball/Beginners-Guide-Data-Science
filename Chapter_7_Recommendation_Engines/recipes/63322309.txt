Title:
Sea Salt & Balsamic Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads of broccoli, trimmed,separate into flowers
1/4 c extra virgin olive oil
1 tsp minced garlic
Fine Sea Salt, to taste
Fresh ground pepper, to taste
3 Tbl balsamic vinegar
,

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425. Line backing sheet w/parchment. Combine broccoli & oil & toss well. Add garlic, salt & pepper & toss again.
Arrange broccoli in single layer on baking sheet. Bake, uncovered, for 15 minutes.
Serve hot or room temp, drizzled with balsamic.
