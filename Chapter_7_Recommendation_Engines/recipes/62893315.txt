Title:
Classic Crepes Suzette With Grand Marnier Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/699563.jpg
Ingredients:
Crepe Batter
1 1/2 cups cake flour
3 eggs
Pinch salt
2 cups milk
1/2 cup butter (1 stick)
1/2 cup sugar
1 orange, grated rind of
Sauce
3 1/2 ounces butter
1 lemon, juice and grated rind of
2 oranges, juiced
2 ounces caster sugar
5 tablespoons Cointreau or Grand Marnier

Directions:
Place first six ingredients in a food processor and whiz until combined and mixture forms a smooth batter. Pour into a 4 cup measuring cup, and let sit for 30 minutes. Heat a small frying pan over medium-high heat. Carefully brush the pan with a little melted butter. Tilting the pan slightly, pour in enough batter to just cover the base. Cook for 1-2 minutes until base of the crepe is lightly browned.
Carefully flip the crepe to brown the other side. Transfer to a plate and repeat until batter is used.
To make the sauce, heat butter, over med heat, stirring to dissolve sugar. cook for 4-5 minutes until golden brown. Add orange juice and zest, and simmer for a few minutes. Pour in liqueur and brandy and stir to combine. Flamb√©, after the alcohol burns out, Dip a crepe into the sauce, then fold it into quarters and place on a serving plate.
Repeat with remaining crepes, serving 2-3 per person.
