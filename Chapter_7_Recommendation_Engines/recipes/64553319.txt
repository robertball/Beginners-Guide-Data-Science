Title:
Tuna Cassorole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of Elbow Macaroni
2 cans of tuna fish
1 can of Cream of Celery
1/2 soup can of milk
1/4 cup of breadcrumbs

Directions:
1. Cook Elbows Macaroni as on package
2. Drain Macaroni
3. Put tuna fish in casserole dish and break apart into small pieces with a fork
4. Combine tuna, elbows, soup, and milk.
5. Mix well
6. Add breadcrumbs and mix
7. Sprinkle Panco Breadcrumbs on top
8. Bake at 350 for 35-45 minutes
