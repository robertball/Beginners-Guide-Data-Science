Title:
Aunt Glady's Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1lb cans Bush's Pork & Beans
1 8 3/4 oz can Pineapple Tidbits drained
1/2 cup ketchup
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tsp dried mustard
4-6 bacon slices

Directions:
Pre-heat over to 350
Mix first 5 ingredients together & simmer for 30 minutes
Transfer to greased 11/2 quart cassaroloe, top with bacon strips
Bake in pre-heated oven for 30 minutes
