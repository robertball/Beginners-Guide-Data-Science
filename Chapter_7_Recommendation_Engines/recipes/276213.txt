Title:
Mango-Strawberry Smoothie with Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/866703.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen mango chunks
1/2 cup frozen strawberries, chopped
1/4 cup apple juice
1/2 cup ice cubes
1/4 cup vanilla nonfat yogurt

Directions:
Combine mango, strawberries, and apple juice in a blender. Place ice over the fruit and pour in yogurt. Blend on high speed until smooth. Pour into a glass.

Submitter: 
EddieDare
Description: 
A quick and simple mango strawberry smoothie. Great for an after-workout snack or just to enjoy on a hot day!
