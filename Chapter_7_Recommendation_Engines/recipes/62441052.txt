Title:
Over-night Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 cup sugar (less if desired)
¾ cup oil
2 Tbsp. molasses
2 cup milk
2 cup flour (1 cup brown flour)
1 cup bran
2 tsp. baking powder
2 tsp. baking soda
½ tsp. salt
Raisins/dates if preferred

Directions:
Mix ingredients and put in fridge overnight. Don’t stir in the morning if batter has risen, simply put in muffin tins.
Bake at 375 for 20 minutes.
