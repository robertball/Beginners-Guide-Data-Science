Title:
BAKED FISH FILLET Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb fish fillets (approx 3 pieces)
1/2 tsp salt
Pepper to taste
2 tsp melted butter
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp finely chopped onion

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Place fish in ungreased baking dish
Season with salt and pepper
Mix butter, lemon juice and onion then pour on fish
Bake at 400 until fish is opaque and flakes with fork approx 25-30 minutes

Submitter: 
JOAN2005
Description: 
We love boating here in the Hamptons in NY.  Fluke (summer flounder) fishing is one of our favorite past times.  The water is so clean you can actually watch the fish going to the hook.  With fresh fish...we enjoy very basic, tasty recipes.  This is a keeper
