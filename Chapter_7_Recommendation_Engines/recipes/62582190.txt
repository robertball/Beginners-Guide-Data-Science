Title:
Hot Spiced Cranberry Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 qts apple cider or 1 C apple cider powder with 8 C water
2 cinnamon sticks
2 (3oz) pkgs cranberry gelatin or use cranberry juice to taste (I used both, gelatin and about 32 oz cranberry juice (to liking)
Orange slices from 1 large orange

Directions:
Mix and put in crockpot or simmer on stove
