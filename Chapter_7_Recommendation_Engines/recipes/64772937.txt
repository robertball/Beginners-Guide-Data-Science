Title:
Coconut Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds chicken breasts
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 cup onion, chopped
1/2 cup peas
2 scallion, chopped
1 jalape√±o pepper, chopped
1 tablespoon ginger, chopped
3 cloves garlic, chopped
2 cans coconut milk
1 tablespoon curry powder
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
1. Salt and pepper chicken. Cook in oil. Remove chicken.
2. Cook veggies, ginger, and garlic for 5 mins.
3. Add coconut milk, curry, and salt. Simmer for a minute.
4. Add chicken, and simmer for 20 mins.
Serve with rice.
