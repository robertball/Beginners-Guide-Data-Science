Title:
Hidden Valley Ranch Layered Vegetable Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977732.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup shredded lettuce
1 cup cauliflower florets*
1 cup broccoli florets*
1 cup grated carrots*
1 cup cooked green peas*
1 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Dressing
Croutons (optional)

Directions:
Alternately layer vegetables in four bowls.  Spoon Ranch over top of salad or in between each layer.  Top with croutons, if desired.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
Layers of crunchy vegetables are topped with creamy Ranch dressing.  Create a salad your children will enjoy by substituting your child's favorite vegetables.
