Title:
Ellen's Baby Back Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/667083.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds pork baby back ribs
1 (18 ounce) bottle barbecue sauce
1/2 package pork rub mixture (homemade or store bought)

Directions:
Cover the ribs liberally with the rub of your choice, then coat the ribs with a BBQ sauce of your choice. Wrap tightly with 2-3 sheets of alunimum foil and let sit in the 'fridge overnight.
Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
Bake ribs wrapped tightly in the foil at 300 degrees F (150 degrees C) for 2 1/2 hours. Remove from foil and place under broiler for 5-10 minutes until it slightly charrs (it adds a nice "grill" taste).
