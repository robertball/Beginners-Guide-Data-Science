Title:
Spicy Salmon Salad Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices whole wheat bread
1 can salmon - do not drain
1/4 cucumber
1 T. capers reserve juice
2 T. light mayonnaise
1 T. red wine vinegar
1 t. red chili paste
1/4 t. Siracha

Directions:
Put salmon with juice in a medium bowl. Using fork, break up salmon, incorporating juice. Chop capers and cucumber, add to salmon.
Mix mayonnaise, caper juice, red wine vinegar and chili paste together. Taste. Add Sirracha a few drops at a time, to taste.
Toast bread.

Submitter: 
glypnirsgirl
Description: 
I received a case of canned salmon for Christmas. I was looking for something a little different than tuna salad and decided to use some of the canned salmon. I adjusted a favorite tuna salad recipe to make this spicy version.
