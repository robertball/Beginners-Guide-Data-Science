Title:
Old Fashioned Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups milk (1 quart)
3-4 tbsp. sugar
4 1/2 tbsp. long grain rice
1 tsp. vanilla
Cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300.
Combine all ingredients in a casserole dish.
Cook for three hours, stirring after the first hour.
