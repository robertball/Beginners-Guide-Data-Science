Title:
Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C butter (1lb.)
1 C powder sugar (1 additional C. separated for butter balls)
1 t. vanilla
4 C flour

Directions:
Cookies: Preheat oven to 350Â°
Whip butter, sugar, vanilla.
Add flour, mix well
Roll on floured surface and cut into shapes
Bake 8 mins. They will NOT be golden. Let cool 2 min. in pan.

Description: 
Light and versitile. You can get very creative with these cookies. Warning: these cookies are like potato chips- one is never enough!
