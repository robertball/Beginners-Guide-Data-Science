Title:
Amazing "secret ingredient" Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 or 19 oz unseasoned can of black beans (drained and rinsed.
4 eggs
3/4 wheylow (sugar substitute) or 1 cup Splenda
2 tsp. baking powder
2 tbsp. unsweetened applesauce (or tbsp oil)
1 tbs vanilla
1/2 tsp baking soda (optional - recommended if using Splenda)
1/4-1/3 cup low fat ricotta cheese (for a moister brownie)
chopped nuts or semi-sweet chocolate chips (optional)

Directions:
Preheat over to 350 degrees.  Mix all ingredients in a food processor or blender.  Pour into an 8x8 baking pan sprayed with cooking spray or oiled with canola or olive oil.  Bake for 30-40 minutes (until set).  Freezes well.

Submitter: 
Karen Grant
Description: 
This is a super-easy, moist, sugar-free and flourless brownie, which makes it South Beach Diet friendly.  This recipe comes from the South Beach message boards and is simply delicious.  No one who tries this can believe that the main ingredient is black beans.
