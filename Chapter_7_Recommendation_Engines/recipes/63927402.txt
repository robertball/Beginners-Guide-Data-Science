Title:
Jan's Creamy  Crab and Vodka Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1092517.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tbsp. of unsalted butter
1 small onion minced
4 cloves of garlic minced
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
1 28 ounce can of crushed tomatoes
1/2 cup vodka
1 cup whole milk
4 ounce package of Neufchatel cream cheese
1/2 cup Romano cheese
2 tsp onion powder
2 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp crushed red pepper (or to taste)
Salt and pepper to taste
1 - 16 ounce can fresh Crab claw meat

Directions:
Melt butter in large sauce pan.  Add chopped onion and cook until translucent.
Add garlic, parsley and basil and cook until garlic begins to brown slightly and herbs soften - about 2 minutes.
Add tomatoes and vodka.  Bring to a simmer, stirring constantly for an additional 10 minutes.
Add in cream and cream cheese.  Blend until smooth.  Add Romano cheese and stir well until fully incorporated.  Add onion and garlic powder, crushed red pepper, salt and pepper to taste.  Add crab being careful not to break up larger pieces and simmer on low for about 10 minutes.  Serve over hot pasta.

What's for dinner, mom?
Description: 
A rich and creamy sauce that is full of flavor.  Enjoy with your favorite cut of pasta and a crispy, green salad for a quick and easy meal.
