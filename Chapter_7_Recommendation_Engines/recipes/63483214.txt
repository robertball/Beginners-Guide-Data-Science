Title:
Brine for meat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup kosher salt
7 cups hot water
2 cups cider vinegar, apple cider or hard cider
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 Tbsp peppercorns
16 dried juniper berries
1 Tbsp allspice
1 Tbsp crushed red pepper flakes
4 bay leaves
2 celery ribs, chopped
1 carrot, sliced
8 cloves garlic, minced
1 onion, sliced

Directions:
In an 8 quart stockpot, dissolve salt in water.  Add cider, sugar, peppercorns, juniper berries, allspice, pepper flakes, bay leaves, celery, carrot, garlic and onion.  Bring to a boil over high heat.  Remove and cool.  Add meat and refrigerate for recommended time:
Boneless pork: 4 hrs
Tenderloin: 8 hrs
Roast: 48 hrs
5-7 lb chicken: 48 hrs
10 lb turkey: 48-72 hrs
