Title:
Joan's Scalloped Potatoes and Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5lbs. Potatoes (russets or reds) peeled and sliced 1/4 inch thick
8 Center cut pork chops breaded and browned
1 C. Chopped onions
2 Sticks butter
1 C. Flour
1/2 Gallon milk
3 Chicken boullion cubes
Parsley flakes
Garlic powder
Pepper
Salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to *325
Butter and flour a large roasting pan.
Dredge in flour and brown chops in small amount of oil in a large skillet and set aside.
Add onions to skillet and saute until soft, craping up the pan drippings.
Layer 1/4 of the potatoes in bottom of roaster. sprinkle 1/4 of the flour, onions and drippings, parsley flakes, garlic powder, pepper and easy salt. Cut 1/2 stick of butter in slices and distribute evenly. Repeat. Next, layer chops and another repeat of 1/4 of the potatoes, onions, flour, seasonings and butter,. Repeat once more until all of the ingredients except the milk and boullion cubes are layered in the pan. Cover and bake for 1 Hr.
After one hour add cubes and pour milk over the layers, cover and bake 1 more hour.
Remove lid and bake until brown and bubbly. (About another 1/2 hour).
