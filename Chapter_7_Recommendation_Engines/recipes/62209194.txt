Title:
Clove Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1 egg
1 cup flour
1 tsp. ground cloves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degree F.
Mix butter and sugar together; add vanilla.
Beat in egg until mixture is smooth.
Stir flour and coves together; blend into butter mixture.
Drop teaspoonfuls of dough 2" apart onto a lightly greased baking sheet.
Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned.  Cool slightly before removing from cookie sheets.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
Cloves make cookies spicy and fragrant.  Great for the holidays or with a cup of tea.
