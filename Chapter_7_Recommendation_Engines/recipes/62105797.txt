Title:
Moelleux au Chocolat (Chocolate Lava Cake) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/401110.jpg
Ingredients:
6 oz. Semi-Sweet chocolate
6 oz. Butter
3 Eggs
1/2 cup Sugar
1/3 cup Flour
Butter for Ramekins
Confection sugar (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375°F
Melt chocolate on low heat along with the butter.
In another bowl, beat eggs and sugar, until it starts to whiten.
Stir in melted chocolate and then the flour.
Butter 6 individual ramekins, and pour in chocolate batter.
Place ramekins on a cookie sheet and cook for about 15 minutes.
Tip ramekins upside down onto dessert plates.
You can serve it either dusted with confection sugar or alongside a scoop of ice-cream. Serve immediately. Voilà!
