Title:
Ultimate Bolognese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 carrots, cut into large pieces
1 large onion, cut into large pieces
3 ribs celery, cut into large pieces
4 garlic cloves, peeled
4 fresh sage sprigs, leaves only
Olive oil
1 pound ground beef
1 pound ground pork
salt and pepper to taste
2 T flour
1 bottle dry red wine
1 28 oz can San Marzano tomatoes
1/2 cup whole milk
1 t ground cinnamon
1 cup grated parmesan
2 T finely chopped parsley

Directions:
Put onion, carrots, garlic, celery and sage in a food processor and process until you have a smooth vegetable pulp. Coat a Le Creuset with olive oil and set over medium heat. Add vegetable pulp and saut√© until fragrant and some moisture is absorbed. Push veggies to the side and add the meat. Cook until no longer pink. Season heavily with salt and pepper. Dust with the flour before adding the wine, tomatoes, milk and cinnamon. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 1 1/2 hours, uncovered, until sauce is thick. Add parsley and parmesan and season with more salt and pepper if needed.

Description: 
A delicious twist on traditional Bolognese- the cinnamon makes it a little different but the flavors are amazing together. Enjoy!
