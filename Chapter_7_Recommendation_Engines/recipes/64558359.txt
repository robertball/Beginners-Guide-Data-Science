Title:
Indian Broccoli Junka Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3417316.jpg
Ingredients:
1 whole broccoli (chopped)
1 small onion(chopped)
1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
1-2 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon turmeric power
3 tablespoon oil
3-4 tablespoon Gram flour
2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon oil for serving
2 tablespoon water

Directions:
Heat the oil in a pan and add the mustard seeds to it. When they crackle, add cumin seeds and chopped onion and fry for some time till they turn translucent.
Add ginger-garlic paste, chili powder and turmeric powder and sauté for 1 to 2 minutes.
Add chopped broccoli and sauté for 8 to 10 minutes.
Add 2 teaspoon water and salt and cook for 1 minute.
Add gram flour and mix it well and fry 2-3 times so it will not stick to pan for 5 -8 minutes and here jhunka is ready.
Serve jhunka with oil on it with Indian bread.
