Title:
Cheese and onion dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayo
3/4 stick cheddar cheese
1 medium onion, minced

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degress.
Mix all the ingredients well in a small baking dish.
Bake for 20 minutes, or until brown and bubbley.

Submitter: 
AddiesMom6
Description: 
Super easy, delicious dip. Growing up we had this at every single party my Grandma Dalpe threw. This is especially good with Bugle chips.
