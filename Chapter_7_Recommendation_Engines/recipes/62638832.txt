Title:
Mallow-Whipt Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups kraft mini marshmallows
2 (17 ounce) cans sweet potatoes, drained and mashed
1/4 cup Parkay margarine, melted
1/4 cup orange juice
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Reserve 1/2 cup marshmallows.
Combine remaining ingredients; mix lightly. Spoon into a 1-qt. casserole.
Bake at 350 F for 20 minutes
Sprinkle with reserved marshmallows; broil until lightly browned.
