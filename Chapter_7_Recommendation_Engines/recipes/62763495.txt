Title:
Fry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. mayo
1 tsp. ketchup
Garlic powder
Onion powder

Directions:
Stir the mayo and ketchup together. Sprinkle in garlic powder and onion powder to taste (it doesn't take much). Serve along some fries!

Submitter: 
Amanda
Description: 
I got tired of dipping my fries in ketchup or mayo or BBQ sauce, so I made up a new one!
