Title:
Blueberry Croissant Puff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large croissants, cubed
1 and 1/2 c. fresh or frozen blueberries
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
2/3 c. sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 and 1/4 c. milk
powdered sugar, optional

Directions:
Evenly spread croissant pieces into a 9" square greased baking dish.
Sprinkle with blueberries.
Place remaining ingredients (except 10x) into a blender and blend until smooth.
Pour evenly over the croissant pieces.
Let stand 20 minutes.
Bake at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes or until set in the center and golden brown.
Dust with powdered sugar if desired.

Submitter: 
Beth Stone Strachan
Description: 
I found this recipe at our local grocery store. Makes a great dessert or brunch dish. Serve warm or cold.
