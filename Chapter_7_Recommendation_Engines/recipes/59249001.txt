Title:
Baby Back Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Rib Rub
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 1/2 T. paprika
1 1/2 T. black pepper
1 T. salt
1/2 T. garlic powder
1/2 T. onion powder
1/4 t. cayenne pepper
Barbecue sauce
3 pounds pork baby back ribs

Directions:
Tear off 2 pieces of aluminum foil large enough to enclose rack of ribs. Spray each piece of foil with vegetable cooking spray.  Remove membrane from back of ribs using a butter knife beginning at thin boney end on bottom part of ribs.  When separated membrane is large enough to grab with a paper towel, pull and remove from ribs, discard membrane.
Mix first 7 ingredients and rub into ribs. Place the ribs on the foil and brush with barbecue sauce.   Wrap tightly and refrigerate for at least 8 hours, or overnight.
Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
Place wrapped ribs on a baking sheet.  Bake ribs  at 300 degrees F (150 degrees C) for 2 1/2 hours. Peel back foil and add more sauce, if desired.  Place under broiler for 5-10 min.  Enjoy.
