Title:
Quinoa and Avocado Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup red quinoa
2 cups chicken broth
3 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
1-1/2 cups diced cucumber
2 Tb diced red onion
4 Tb lime juice
1 1/2 Tb olive oil
1 bunch fresh basil (or sub 1/2 tsp dried)
1-1/2 teaspoons ground cumin seed
1/2 tsp. ground corriander
1/4 tsp. red pepper flakes
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 C. parmesan cheese (or feta cheese)
3 avocado - peeled, pitted and sliced

Directions:
Bring quinoa and broth to a boil. Cover and let boil on medium for about 20 min, or until liquid is absorbed. Combine all other ingredients (except avocado and cheese) and allow to chill for at least an hour. When ready to serve, top with avocado and cheese.
