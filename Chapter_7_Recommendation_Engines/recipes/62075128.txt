Title:
Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/8 lbs potatoes
3 tbs extra virgin olive oil
6 garlic cloves
1 1/2 tbs chopped fresh rosemary
salt (as needed)
pepper (as needed)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Wash and dry potatoes then cut in wedges.
Toss potatoes in oil and seasoning.
Cook 25-30 minutes.
Shake the pan occasionally for even roasting.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Angela
Description: 
Cut potatoes roasted in olive oil, rosemary and garlic.  Great side for dinner!
