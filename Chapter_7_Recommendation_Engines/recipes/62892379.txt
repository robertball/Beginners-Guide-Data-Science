Title:
Iskender Kebab my way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - chopped
2 medium onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 (10.75 ounce) can tomato puree
1 can Rotel
ground cumin to taste (a lot)
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
1/2 cup butter, melted
tzatiki sauce to serve with it
white rice, cooked to serve with it

Directions:
Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in the chicken, onion, and garlic, and cook until chicken juices run clear. Mix in tomato puree & Rotel. Season with cumin, salt, and pepper. Continue cooking 10 minutes.
Serve over white rice with tzaziki on side.
