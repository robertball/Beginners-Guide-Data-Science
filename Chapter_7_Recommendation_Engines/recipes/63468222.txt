Title:
Chicken and Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Boneless skinless chicken breasts
2c Uncooked minute rice
1 pkg. onion soup mix
1 can of cream of chicken soup
1 can of cream of celery soup
1 (soup can) of water.

Directions:
Mix together soups, 1 can of water, and 2 cups of minute rice (uncooked.)  Pour into a buttered casserole dish.
Lay chicken breasts on top of rice mixture. Cover with tin foil.
350-375 for 1-1 1/2 hours.

Submitter: 
Sidnie Anderson
Description: 
Easy recipe to throw together and have a filling dish.
