Title:
Semi-Homemade Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Kraft Macaroni and Cheese
*4 Tbls butter
*1/4 cup (and a little more)milk
1/4 cup pkged, shredded chedder cheese
1/4 ts ground mustard
1/4 ts onion powder
1/4 ts garlic powder
3/4 cup brocoli steamed, chopped (size-->your preference)
3/4 cup meat, cut into bite sized pieces (cooked ham, hotdogs, etc...)
stale bread/bread crumbs (optional)

Directions:
*Make macaroni according to box directions
Stir in all other ingredients
