Title:
Basil & Garlic Breaded Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBSP fresh minced garlic
2 TBSP Fresh Chopped Basil*
1/3 cup Parmesan Cheese
1/3 cup Bread Crumbs
fresh ground pepper to taste
3 tablespoons Olive Oil
2 eggs
1/4 cup milk
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
Combine first 4 ingredients
Drizzle olive oil over coating mix
Toss well to combine
Beat eggs with milk
Dip chicken breasts in egg wash.
Dredge in coating mix, pat well to adhere coating to chicken
Bake at 400 degree for 20 minutes

Submitter: 
laughingmagpie
Description: 
Easy baked breaded chicken that browns nicely. Great for family dinner or served with some Chevre Cheese Tasty enough for entertaining.
