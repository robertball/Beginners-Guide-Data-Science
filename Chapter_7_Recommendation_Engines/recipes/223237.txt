Title:
Pea Salad With Pimentos and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2211570.jpg
Ingredients:
3 (15 ounce) cans peas, drained
1 (4 ounce) jar pimento peppers, drained and chopped
1/2 pound Colby cheese, cut into 1/4-inch cubes
1/2 onion, diced
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Mix peas, pimento peppers, Colby cheese, onion, mayonnaise, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl with a lid. Cover and refrigerate for at least 12 hours before serving for best flavor; stir again before serving.

Submitter: 
Dean
Description: 
This is a quick and easy - and tasty - recipe that was a standard at my extended family's special dinners. Great Aunt Betty almost always brought this as a dish to pass, and it was always gone by the end of dinner.
