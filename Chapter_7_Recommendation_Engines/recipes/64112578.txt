Title:
Tex-Mex Seasoning/Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons canola oil
heaping tablespoon of minced garlic
2 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
half of a 15 oz can of chopped tomato
dash of salt
dash of pepper

Directions:
Mix in a plastic bag. Use to season any tex-mex dishes or for meat marinade before cooking. If using as marinade; marinate in a refrigerator for at least 2 hours or overnight.
