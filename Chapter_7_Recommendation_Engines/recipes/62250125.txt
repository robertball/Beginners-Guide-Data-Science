Title:
Grilled Sea Bass Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/470197.jpg
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon minced onion
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/4 tsp. lemon pepper to taste
1/4 tsp. kosher salt
2 sea bass steaks
3 tablespoons butter
2 large cloves garlic, chopped
1 tablespoon dried parsley
1/2 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Preheat grill for medium high heat.
In Ziploc bag, combine the garlic powder, minced onion, paprika, lemon pepper, and salt. Shake each fish steak individually in the bag.
In a small saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter with the garlic, parsley and olive oil. Remove from heat when the butter has melted, and set aside.
Oil grill grate. Grill fish for 8 minutes, then turn and baste with garlic butter. Continue cooking for 8 minutes, or until easily flaked with a fork.
