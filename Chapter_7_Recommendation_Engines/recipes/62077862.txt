Title:
Easy Beefy Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. ground meat
1/4 cup onion
1/2 clove garlic
1/2 cup elbow macaroni uncooked
1/4 cup bell pepper
1/4 cup olive oil
1 can (12 oz) tomato juice
3/4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. Worchestshire

Directions:
Saute beef, uncooked macaroni, onion, bell pepper and garlic in hot oil until macaroni turns yellowish and meat is cooked. Drain fat if needed and then add tomato juice and other seasonings.  Bring to a boil, cover and simmer 20 minutes then uncover and simmer a bit longer till sauce is desired consistency.
