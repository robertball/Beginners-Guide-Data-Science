Title:
Cranberry Walnut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of butter
1 cup of sugar
3/4 cup of firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 cup of milk
2 tbl. of orange juice
1 egg
3 cups of flour
1 tsp. of baking powder
1/2 tsp. of cinnamon
1/2 tsp. of salt
1/4 tsp. of baking soda
1 cup of chopped walnuts
2-1/2 cups fresh cranberries
Powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375°.
Wash and cut cranberries in half.  Cream butter, sugar, and brown sugar.  Beat in milk, orange juice and egg.
Sift flour, baking powder, cinnamon, salt, and baking soda.  Blend dry ingredients into butter mixture.  Stir in cranberries and walnuts.
Drop by the teaspoon full onto a greased cookie sheet and bake at 375° for 10-15 minutes.
Cool and sprinkle with powdered sugar.
