Title:
Prince of Peas Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup sour cream
1/4 finely minced onion
3/4 tsp sugar
pinch of salt
4 cups fresh baby peas - OR - 2 (10 oz) packages frozen baby peas, thawed but uncooked
6 oz cubed cheddar cheese
4 or 5 crisp bacon slices, crumbled

Directions:
Whisk together mayonnaise and sour cream in a small bowl. Add onion, sugar, and salt.
Stir together peas, cheese, and bacon in a large bowl. Spoon on mayonnaise mixture and toss lightly until combined. Add more sugar or salt to taste if desired.
Refrigerate 1/2 to 12 hours before serving.
