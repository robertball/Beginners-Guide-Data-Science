Title:
Homemade Western Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bottle Ketchup (36 oz)
2 cups sugar
1/4 cup canola oil
1 tsp paprika
1/4 cup cider vinegar

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in mixer and store in container in the refrigerator until ready to use.

Submitter: 
Vickey Londrie Hawkins
Description: 
Western dressing is a favorite with my family but is expensive to buy.  I came up with my own recipe that tastes better than the stor bought kind.  If you like sweet~ you'll love this one.
