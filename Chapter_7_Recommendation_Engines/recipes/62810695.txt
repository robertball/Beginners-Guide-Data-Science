Title:
Southwestern Vegetarian Chickpea Sandwich Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (19 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
1 small jalapeno, seeded and minced
1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
1/4 cup onion, chopped (or 2 scallions)
1/4 cup chopped cilantro
2 tablespoon mayonnaise
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 teaspoon cumin
1/2 tsp ground coriander
1/2 tsp chili powder (I used ancho chili powder)
1/2 tsp Mexican oregano
1/8 tsp cayenne (or to taste)
salt and pepper to taste
Diced or sliced avocado

Directions:
Drain and rinse chickpeas. Pour chickpeas into a medium size mixing bowl and mash with a fork. Add other ingredients except avocado and combine well.  Serve garnished with diced or sliced avocado, lettuce and tomato.

Serve this tasty sandwich spread on crusty whole grain rolls or pita bread, with avocado,lettuce and tomato.Modified from: Vegetarian Chickpea Sandwich Filling
