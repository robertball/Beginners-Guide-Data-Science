Title:
Red Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar grape or apple jelly
1 bottle chili sauce
1 tsp lemon juice
1/2 bag premade meatballs

Directions:
place meatballs in crockpot or pan
cover with sauce
crockpot on high for 4 hours low for 8 hours
cook until internal temp is over 160 degrees
serve over rice
serve alone as an appetizer
