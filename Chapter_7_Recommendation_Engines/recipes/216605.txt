Title:
Slow-Cooker Tex-Mex Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992766.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch-wide strips
2 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
2 tablespoons flour
1 green pepper, cut into 1-inch-wide strips
1 red pepper, cut into 1-inch-wide strips
1 cup frozen corn
1 1/2 cups chunky salsa
1 cup KRAFT Tex Mex Shredded Cheese

Directions:
Toss chicken with seasoning and flour in slow cooker. Stir in all remaining ingredients except shredded cheese; cover with lid.
Cook on LOW 6 to 8 hours (or on HIGH 3 to 4 hours).
Stir before serving. Top with shredded cheese.

Submitter: 
KRAFT Shredded Cheese
Description: 
Let your slow cooker do the work! You can serve up this zesty chicken dish with peppers, corn, salsa and shredded cheese with or without hot cooked rice.
