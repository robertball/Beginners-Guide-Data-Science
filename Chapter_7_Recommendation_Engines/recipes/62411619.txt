Title:
Marty's Caribbean Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/438002.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. ribs
2 T. instant onions
1 t. ground mustard
1 t. crushed red pepper flakes
½ t. allspice
½ t. cinnamon
½ t. garlic powder
1 medium onion, sliced
1/2 c. water
1 1/2 c. barbecue sauce

Directions:
Mix together and rub on meat.  Layer with onions with meat.  Add water.  Cook on low 8-9 hours.  Pour liquid off.  Pour  BBQ sauce in crock.  Cover and cook 1 more hour.

Submitter: 
KramerGirls
Description: 
Linda discovered this recipe and it became Marty's favorite ribs.  It has a sweet taste because of the spices but contains no sugar (except what's in the BBQ sauce)  They are delicious and ultra tender!  Marty has good taste!
