Title:
Bread Maker French Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/811503.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups water
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1 1/2 Tbsp. vegetable oil
4 cups all-purpose/Bread Machine flour
1 Tbsp. sugar
1 tsp. quick-rise yeast

Directions:
Measure and add liquid ingredients to the bread pan.
Measure and add dry ingredients (except yeast) to the bread pan.
Use your finger to form a well (hole) in the flour where you will pour the yeast. Yeast must NEVER come into contact with a liquid when you are adding ingredients. Measure the yeast and carefully pour it into the well.
Twist the baking pan into the breadmaker and close the lid.
Press "Select" button to choose the French setting.
Press the "Crust Color" button to choose light crust.
Press the "Start/Stop" button.
