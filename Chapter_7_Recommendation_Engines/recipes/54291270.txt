Title:
Blueberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup white sugar
5 tablespoons flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 TBSP lemon juice
5 cups fresh blueberries
1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Mix sugar, flour, salt, and cinnamon, lemon juice, and sprinkle over blueberries.
Line pie dish with one pie crust.  Pour berry mixture into the crust, and dot with butter.  Cut remaining pastry into 1/2 - 3/4 inch wide strips, and make lattice top.  Crimp and flute edges.
Bake pie on middle shelf of oven for about 60 minutes, or until crust is golden brown.
