Title:
Holiday Stuffed Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Stuffing:
2 Tbsp veg oil
1 onion, chopped fine
10 cloves garlic, minced
8 oz pork
1 or 2 slices white bread, pulsed to coarsely ground (1 Cup)
3 Tbsp fresh parsley, finely chopped
1 tsp fennel seeds
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1 tsp salt
1 lg egg, lightly beaten
Pork:
2 Tbsp brown sugar
1 Tbsp salt
1 Tbsp pepper
1 (4-5 lb) boneless pork shoulder roast

Directions:
Stuffing:
Heat oil in lg skillet over md heat until shimmering. Cook onion until golden, about 8 min. Stir in garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 sec; Transfer to lg bowl and let cool. Add ground pork, bread crumbs, parsley, fennel, pepper flakes, salt and eggs to bowl onion mixture and knead with hands until well combined.
Pork:
Adjust oven rack to middle position and heat oven to 300 degrees. Combine sugar, salt and pepper in sm bowl. Butterfly and pound pork to 3/4 inch. You should have about 15 by 8 inches. With long side facing you, cut pounded pork in half crosswise. Spread stuffing in even layer over each half of pork, leaving a 1 inch border around edges. Roll and tie each half securely with kitchen twine at 1 inch intervals. Rub boasts evenly with sugar mixture and transfer to rimmed baking sheet. Cover tightly with foil and roast until meat registers 170 degrees, about 2 hrs.
Remove foil and increase temperature to 400 degrees. Cook until well browned and meat registers 190 degrees, about 30 min. Transfer to cutting board, tent with foil, and let rest 20 min. Remove twine. Slice and serve.
