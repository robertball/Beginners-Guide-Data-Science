Title:
Hearty Lentil Stoup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/719246.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium onion - peeled
2 cloves garlic - peeled
3 carrots - peeled
3 stalks of celery
2 tablespoon oil
10 to 14 oz. package of Kielbasa - cut into cubes
64 oz. chicken broth
1 (16 oz.) bag brown lentils - rinsed
1 bay leaf
1/2 cup ditalini pasta - dry
1 (14 oz.) bag of fresh baby spinach - chop course
cracked black pepper to taste
grated Italian cheese

Directions:
In a food processor mince the onion, garlic, 2 carrots and 2 stalks of celery.
In a large soup pot add oil, Kielbasa and minced vegetable mixture.
Sautee on medium heat till vegetables are tender and meat begins to brown (about 10 minutes).
Add chicken broth, lentils, 1 diced carrot, 1 diced stalk of celery, bay leaf.
Bring to boil then reduce heat and simmer uncovered till lentils are soft (about 30 minutes).
Add ditalini pasta and spinach. Stir into soup well and cover with lid.
If too thick adjust by adding a little water.
Turn off heat and let sit for 20 minutes.
Season with cracked black pepper and grated cheese to taste per individual serving.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
This stew-like soup is thick and hearty with every spoonful overflowing with rib-sticking satisfying goodness. The Kielbasa adds lots of flavor so use a brand and blend you like best.
