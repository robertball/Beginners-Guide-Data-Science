Title:
Golden corn and broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5lbs fresh broccoli, cut up.
1 cup corn
1 cup water
1 can cream of celery
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 cup milk
generous dash of pepper

Directions:
In 3 quart saucepan, combine broccoli, corn and water. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low. Cover; cook 8 minutes or until broccoli is tender-crisp, stirring occasionally. Drain in colander.
Combine soup, cheese, milk and pepper. Return broccoli and corn to saucepan. heat through, stirring occasionally.
