Title:
Sheboygan Bratwurst Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 bratwurst
3 (12 oz) beer
1 1/2 cup water
2 lg onions, sliced thinly
1 tsp fresh ground pepper
12 hard rolls
coarse-grain mustard, ketchup, pickles, relish, sauerkraut (optional)

Directions:
Prick each brat in several places. Put them in a large saucepan with beer, water, onions, and pepper. Bring to a boil and simmer for 30 mins.
Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil grate.
Remove the bratwurst and transfer to the grill. Drain liquid from pan, reserving onions.
Grill brats over high heat for 5 to 7 minutes, turning to brown on all sides. Serve with onions.
