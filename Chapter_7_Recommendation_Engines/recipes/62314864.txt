Title:
Oatmeal Batter Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c flour
1 1/2 T baking powder
1 tsp salt
1 1/4 c rolled oats
1/2 c raisins, if desired
1 egg
1/4 c honey
1 1/2 c milk
1 T melted butter

Directions:
Sift together flour, baking powder, and salt.  Add oats and raisins, if desired In a separate bowl, beat egg lightly, then add honey and milk.  Beat until well blended.  Add dry ingredients and stir until all ingredients are moistened.  Turn into a greased 9 x 5 loaf pan.  Brush top of loaf with melted butter.  Bake at 350F for 1 hour to 1 hour fifteen minutes.  Makes one loaf.
