Title:
Braised beef with garlic mashed potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ¼ pounds blade or cross rib steak, cut into four portions
Salt and pepper, to taste
1/3 cup flour
2 Tbs olive oil
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 med. onions, halved and sliced
½ tsp dried thyme
2 cups (500 ml) low sodium beef stock
¼ cup dry red wine, optional
For the potatoes
2 ½ pounds baking potatoes, peeled and halved
4-6 sliced garlic cloves
¾ cup warm  milk
2 Tbs melted butter
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325%. Season the beef with salt and pepper, and then coat with flour, shaking off excess. Reserve the excess flour.
Heat oil in a skillet set over med. high heat. Add steak portions and brown on both sides Transfer to a casserole. Add garlic and onions to the skillet and cook for 3 to 4 minutes. Stir in thyme and 3 tbs of the reserved flour until well combined. Slowly pour in, stirring constantly, the beef stock and wine if using. Bring to a simmer. Season to taste, and then pour over the steaks. Cover and cook in the oven 90 minutes or until very tender.
30 minutes before the meat is done,gently boil the potatoes and garlic until tender. drain well and thoroughly mash. Whip in the remaining ingredients until combined and mixture is smooth.
Serve steaks on a bed of mashed potatoes. Spoon over pan juices and serve
