Title:
Moist and Tasty Baked Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
(1) 9-10 lb. Smoked Ham, Bone-In
1 TBSP Whole cloves
1/2 c. Brown sugar, divided
Fresh Pineapple cut into 1/2" rings
Toothpicks
1/2 c. Molasses
2 c. Water

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
2. Place ham in lg. roasting pan cut side down.
3. Stud ham with cloves starting from the top and working your way down leaving appx. 2-3" gaps.
4. Rub 1/4 c. brown  sugar over outside of clove-studded ham.
5. Using toothpicks, secure pineapple rings to outside of ham, try to cover all exposed meat.
6. In small pot over med. heat, stir molasses, remaining brown sugar, and water.  Bring to a simmer and reduce by about 1/2.  Drizzle reduction over entire ham.
7. Place in preheated oven and bake for 5 hours, checking ham after 3 hours and basting every hour to follow.
8. Turn oven down to 200 degrees and cook ham for two more hours, basting frequently.
Allow fifteen minutes to cool before serving.

Submitter: 
BrookeMT
Description: 
Very tasty, moist recipe for bone-in ham.  Quick preparation time and spends most of the day in the oven.
