Title:
Baked rice & pork chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pork Chops, bone-in (approx. 6 depending on how thin/big they are)
1 cup regular white long grain rice
1 stick melted butter
1 can beef broth-low sodium  (or you can use consume)
1 can French Onion soup
1 small can (or jar) of mushrooms with liquid

Directions:
Brown pork chops in skillet, set aside.
Meanwhile preheat oven to 350.
In a glass rectangular baking pan; place all remaining ingredients.  Top with browned pork chops.  Cover tightly with foil; bake for 45-60 minutes.
