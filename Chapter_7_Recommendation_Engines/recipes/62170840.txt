Title:
Quick and easy lower fat spinach quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 eggs, beaten
1 package frozen spinach
1/2 medium yellow onion, chopped
1 green onion, chopped
1/4 large red bell pepper, chopped
1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
1/4 cup skim milk
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp olive oil
Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 325°F.
2.  Thaw frozen spinach and drain as much excess water as possible by squeezing the spinach.
3.  Sauté chopped yellow onion and red pepper with olive oil in frying pan over medium heat until onion becomes translucent.
4.  Mix beaten eggs, frozen spinach, sautéed onions and red pepper, green onion, cheddar, milk and baking soda.  Add salt and pepper to taste.
5.  Pour into greased pie plate and bake for 40 minutes or until a toothpick stuck into the centre comes out clean.

Submitter: 
mms09
Description: 
Unlike most quiches, this quiche does not have a crust and cuts out much of the cheese.  This spinach quiche is delicious, quick and super easy!
