Title:
Hazelnut  Crusted Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. salmon filet
1/2 cup nonfat mayonnaise
1/2 cup chopped hazelnuts
2 tsp. minced fresh tarragon or 1 tsp. dry
1/2 tsp. dried orange peel
1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Cut salmon filet into 4 equal-sized pieces. Place on baking sheet coated with cooking spray. Spread mayonnaise on filets (about 2 tablespoons on each). Top with chopped hazelnuts.
Combine remaining ingredients and sprinkle evenly over filets. Bake in preheated oven 20 minutes or until fish flakes easily with fork.

Submitter: 
LINDASUE52
Description: 
A wonderfully moist, low-fat recipe easy enough for every day, but fancy enough for company. You may substitute almonds or pecans in place of the hazelnuts.
