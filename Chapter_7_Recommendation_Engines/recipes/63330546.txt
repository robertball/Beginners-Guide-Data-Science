Title:
MeMe's Best Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 LG CANS OF VAN KAMP’S PORK N BEANS
½  TO 1 CUP OF CHOPPED ONION (PARTIALLY SAUTEED)
5 OR 6 STRIPS OF COOKED BACON, DRAINED & CRUMBLED
½ CUP OR MORE TO TASTE OF DARK BROWN SUGAR
2 TBLS OF MOLASSES OR MORE TO TASTE
1 to 2 TBLS YELLOW MUSTARD TO TASTE

Directions:
PREHEAT OVEN TO 400º    NEED A 13X9 PAN OR CASSEROLE DISH
COOK BACON, DRAIN AND CRUMBLE, SET ASIDE.  SAUTE ONIONS ½ WAY AND THEN OPEN PORK ‘N BEANS DRAIN OFF MOST OF JUICE, LEAVE JUST A LITTLE THEN MIX TOGETHER ALL INGREDIENTS, COVER WITH ALUMINUM FOIL.  BAKE APPROXIMATELY 30 MINUTES.  REMOVE ALUMINUM FOIL & BAKE ANOTHER 15 MINTUES OR UNTIL DONE.
