Title:
Subway Orchard Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C white chicken meat
1/2 C chopped celery
1/4 C cherry juice infused craisins
1/4 C golden raisins
1 chopped red apple
1 chopped granny smith apple
1 to 1-1/2 C lite mayonnaise
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix together and serve on bread. Can add toasted slivered almonds and/or chopped red onions.
