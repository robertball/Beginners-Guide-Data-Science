Title:
Low Carb Stuffed Bell Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb ground Beef
1 can stewed tomatoes
1/4 c chopped onion
1 c riced cauliflower and broccoli or just riced cauliflower
1 tsp minced garlic
2 Bell Peppers
1/4 c Parmesan  Cheese
1 c shredded cheddar cheese
Garlic salt and pepper  to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Place ground Beef in pan. Crumble up and fry with onions and minced garlic. When almost done pour in riced cauliflower and broccoli. Simmer as you bring a large pot of water to boil. Remove tops and seeds from Bell peppers and rinse them.  Place them in boiling water for approximately 3 - 5 min.
Grease small casserole dish.
Remove Bell Peppers,drained, to casserole dish. Mixed ground beef mixture with salt and pepper , 3/4 of cheddar cheese and all of Parmesan Cheese.  Spoon into Bell Peppers putting extra around Peppers in casserole dish. Sprinkle remaining cheddar cheese on top.
Place in oven for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Laury
Description: 
Stuffed Bell Peppers made with riced cauliflower and broccoli instead of Rice for low carb diet. Riced cauliflower and broccoli can now be found in most produce dept.
