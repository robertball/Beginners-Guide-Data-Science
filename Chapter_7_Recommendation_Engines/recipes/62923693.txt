Title:
My Meat Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/713766.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb Of Ground Beef
1 Lb Of Ground Pork
1 Tablespoon of paisley
4 tablespoons of white wine
8 tablespoons of milk
4 tablespoons of graded Parmesan
4 Cloves of garlic, minced
6 tablespoons of breadcrumbs

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl
roll into meat balls of your desired size
Fry in a pan till brown

Submitter: 
Mark Farmer
Description: 
One of the first meal my wife and I had when we got married about 35 years ago at a restaurant\deli in Wildwood, NJ. called A&LP (It is Still there) it was the best meat ball hoagie I have ever had. So meat balls, and meat ball hoagies have always had a special place in my heart.
