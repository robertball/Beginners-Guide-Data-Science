Title:
White Bean Chicken Chili 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cans (15 oz. each) white beans (cannellini, Great Northern, etc.)
1 can (15 oz) black beans (low sodium preferred)
1/2 package frozen corn
1 medium onion, chopped/diced
6 cloves garlic, minced or pressed
2 Pablano Peppers (Hatch Chili peppers if available), chopped/diced without seeds
1 Red Pepper, chopped/diced without seeds
1 Packet Lowry's Taco (preferred) or Chili Seasoning
1 Packet Hidden Valley Ranch powder
1 tsp dried oregano leaves
2-4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
2 1/2 cups chicken broth
OPTIONAL - 2 Serrano Peppers to add extra heat

Directions:
Rinse and drain beans
Cube chicken and
Combine in a large pot with garlic, onion, peppers, and oregano and saut√© on medium-high heat until onions are translucent and/or chicken is browned.
Pour in chicken broth and add beans, corn, and seasonings.
Bring to a broil and let simmer until chicken is cooked through, about an hour.
Serve with tortilla chips/strips, jack cheese, hot sauce, sour cream, avocado, etc.
