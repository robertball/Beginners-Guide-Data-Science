Title:
Butterscotch Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 sticks butter
1 cup sugar
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
2 eggs
4 tsp vanilla
1 tsp salt
2tsp baking soda
2+ cups flour
5 cups oatmeal
2-3 cups butterscotch chips

Directions:
Cream butter, sugar and brown sugar.
Then add eggs adn vanilla.
Mix salt, soda and flour together then add to other mixture.
Finally add the oatmeal and chips.
Bake at 375* for 12-14 minutes.
