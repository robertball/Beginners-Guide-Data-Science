Title:
Not Julia's Beef Bourguignon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C.  unbleached all-purpose flour
1 tsp. kosher salt
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1 to 1 1/4 Lbs. Chuck, cut in 1-inch pieces
2 Tbs. olive oil
1 C. leaks, white and light green only, cleaned and coarsely chopped
1 C. carrots, peeled and coarsely chopped
1 C. celery, coarsely chopped
1 large clove garlic, minced
1 C. red wine
1 bay leaf
4 C. beef stock or vegetable broth
1 tsp. dried herbs, or 1 Tsp. fresh herbs
3 carrots, peeled and sliced into 3/4 inch pieces
6 medium mushrooms, sliced or quartered
2 or 3 Maine potatoes, peeled and cubed
1/2 C. pearl onions, fresh or frozen
1 Tbs. parsley, chopped

Directions:
Mix flour, salt, and pepper in large plastic resealable bag.
Dredge Chuck in flour mixture
Heat oil in large Dutch oven or pot over medium heat. Add chuck and brown on all sides - don't crowd or Chuck will get all gray and tough. Remove meat and set aside on a plate r in a bowl.
Add a little more oil if needed and cook chopped leaks, carrots, and celery until somewhat softened, about 5 minutes.
Add garlic and cook 1 to 2 minutes.
Add wine ( or beer) and scrape up all the brown bits. Return chuck to the dutch oven or pan along with any accumulated juices.
Add bay leaf, broth, and herbs.
Bring to a simmer and add sliced carrots, mushrooms, potatoes, and pearl onions.
Recover and simmer for an additional hour, until chuck is tender and vegetables are cooked.
Discard bay leaf
Top with chopped parsley when served.
