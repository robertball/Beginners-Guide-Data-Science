Title:
Salsa Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985470.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups water
1 1/2 cups chunky salsa
2 cups uncooked instant rice
1 cup shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
In a saucepan, bring water and salsa to a boil. Stir in rice. Remove from the heat; cover and let stand for 5 minutes. Stir in cheese; cover and let stand for 30 seconds or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Molly  Ingle
Description: 
It's a snap to change the spice level in this popular rice side dish by choosing a milder or hotter salsa. 'It's an easy accompaniment to burritos or tacos,' suggests Molly Ingle of Canton, North Carolina.
