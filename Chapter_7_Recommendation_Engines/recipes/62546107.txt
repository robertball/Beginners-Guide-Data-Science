Title:
Cyndie's Deluxe Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 ounce bag frozen peas, thawed
1 cup raw cauiflower, broken into small pieces
1/4 cup diced onion
1/2 cup sour cream
1 cup Ranch dressing
1 cup chopped celery
1/3 cup salad dressing
1/2 cup crumbled bacon
3/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/4 cup cashews, broken

Directions:
Mix everything except for the nuts until well coated.  Chill well.  Just before serving, add the cashews.

Submitter: 
Lilcat
Description: 
A fancy pea salad with a nice presentation that gets rave reviews whenever I serve it.
