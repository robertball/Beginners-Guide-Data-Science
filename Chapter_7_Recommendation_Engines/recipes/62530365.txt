Title:
Pie Crust Pecan Roll-ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Frozen pie crusts
1 stick melted butter (unsalted)
2 cups pecan pieces
1 1/2 cups raw sugar or Turbinado sugar
Cinnamon

Directions:
Thaw pie crusts.
Brush each with melted butter
Spread to the edge - like a pizza with the pecans and sugar.
Sprinkle with cinnamon.
Roll into log.
Slice into 1/2 inch and place on cookie sheet.
Bake at 425 degrees for 18-20 mins.
Immediately remove from backing pan and place on cooling rack.
Best when room temp!
