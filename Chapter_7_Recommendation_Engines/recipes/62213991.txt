Title:
Tuna Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can tuna, drained and flaked
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
3/4 cup chopped pecans
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 TBSP parsley flakes
1/4 tsp seasoning salt

Directions:
In a medium bowl, blend tuna, cream cheese, 1/2 the pecans and onion. Shape into a ball or loaf. Let firm up in refrigerator and then coat with remaining pecans. Refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
JILLENA
Description: 
There's nothing fancy about the Tuna Ball, but it tastes great at parties and other get togethers. Chopped pecans give this blend of tuna, cream cheese and onion a light crunch. I love this appetizer with veggies, crackers and veggies.Modified from: Tuna Ball
