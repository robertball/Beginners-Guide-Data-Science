Title:
Ham and Cheese Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 oz.) carton sour cream
1 (8 oz.) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 (2.4 oz.) package spring vegetable soup mix (1 envelope)
1 (10 oz.) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and well-drained
12 (8-inch) flour tortillas
12 thin slices deli cheese
12 thin slices deli ham (or turkey)

Directions:
In medium bowl, stir together sour cream, cream cheese, soup mix, and spinach. Spread about 1/3 cup sour cream mixture on 1 side of each tortilla. Top each tortilla with a slice of cheese and a slice of ham. Roll up. Wrap tightly in plastic wrap. Chill at least an hour. Cut each roll into 8 pieces. Secure with toothpicks, if necessary.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
Tortillas rolled up with cream cheese, ham, and all sorts of goodness. Cut them up to take to a party or get-together or as a great appetizer.
