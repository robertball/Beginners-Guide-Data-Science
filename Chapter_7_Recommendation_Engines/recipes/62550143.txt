Title:
Vegetarian Sliders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp oil
1/2 onion, chopped fine
1/2 red pepper, chopped fine
1 jalapeno, chopped fine
4 cloved garlic, chopped
19oz can black beans
19oz can chickpeas
4 Tbsp black olived, chopped
4 Tbsp artichokes, chopped
2 Cups rolled oats
4 Tbsp bread crumbs
4 Eggs
1.5 tsp paprika
1.5 tsp chili powder
3 tsp oregano
1.5 tsp red pepper flakes
1.5 tsp cumin
1.5 tsp celery salt
3/4 tsp sage
1.5 Tbsp basil

Directions:
SAUTE veggies in oil
PULSE beans and oats until smaller but still chunky
MIX all ingredients with spices
SHAPE into golf ball sized balls and flatten slightly.
COOK in frying pan sprayed with oil

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
WOW!  This is one of the best vegetarian burgers I've ever tasted.  Serve these with Chipotle Mayo (see recipe).  Sounds difficult but it's not - just time consuming.  Recipe yields about 22 sliders so you can make and freeze for later!
