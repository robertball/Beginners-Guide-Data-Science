Title:
Phycho Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chocolate chips and 1/4  margarine melted together and stirred
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 Tbsp molasses
1/4 margarine
3 eggs
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour
1/3 cup cocoa powder
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
beat together sugar, molasses, margarine, eggs and vanilla.
then add the melted chocolate with margarine
sift together flour, cocoa powder,  baking powder  and salt
Stir into beaten ingredients,  mixing only till combined
Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 25 minutes
place 1 1/2 Tbsp of cookie dough for each cookie on greased or parchment papered  cookie sheet
Bake 10 minutes at 350 degrees ( do not over bake)
