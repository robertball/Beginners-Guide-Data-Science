Title:
Cheese Stuffed Beefsteak Tomato with Crispy Pancetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
4 rashers (slices) Pancetta (or unsmoked bacon)
120g/4 oz Cheese Mixture (Parmesan Reggiano/Cheddar/Gouda/Emmental etc) - grated
60g/2 oz fresh White Breadcrumbs
2tbsp fresh flatleaf Parsley - chopped
2tbsp fresh Basil - chopped
1 Beef Tomato - halved with flesh and seeds removed
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
Extra Virgin Olive Oil - for drizzling

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.
In a bowl, mix together half of the cheese, breadcrumbs and parsley and season with salt and pepper;
Load the mixture into the hollowed-out tomato halves;
Top with the remaining cheese and drizzle with olive oil;
Place onto a baking tray and bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbling and starting to turn golden-brown;
Meanwhile, heat 1tbsp oil in a saut√© pan, add the pancetta rashers and cook for some 4 to 5 minutes until crispy;
Remove tomatoes from the oven and top each half with 2 crispy pancetta slices
Serve

Description: 
A cheesy bacon style treat starter for a sunny afternoon BBQ. Sets the right tone for the rest of the day.
