Title:
roasted sage potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
olive oil - enough to cover cast iron pan
sage leaves
salt
nugget potatoes

Directions:
cover the bottom of the cast iron pan with olive oil
cover the oil with a single layer of fresh sage leaves
sprinkle with salt
slice nugget potatoes in half and place cut side down in a single layer on top of the sage leaves
bake at 425 degrees for 15 minutes

Submitter: 
Esther
Description: 
crispy nuggets of potato with a creamy centre that dress up any dinner
