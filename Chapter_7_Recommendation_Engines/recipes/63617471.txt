Title:
Big Ray's Cheesy "Pizza" Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1006145.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups broccoli (stem and florets attached)
2 Tbs olive oil
1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
6 Tbs grated Parmesan cheese
1 tsp dried chili flakes
1 tsp oregano
Juice of 1/2 lemon
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat broiler.
Steam broccoli (about 5-6 minutes.
Drain broccoli and place in 8"x 10" baking dish.  Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with cheeses, chili flakes and oregano.
Broil until cheese is golden brown, about 2-3 minutes.
Squeeze lemon juice onto broccoli and season with salt and pepper.
