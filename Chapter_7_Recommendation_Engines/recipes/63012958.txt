Title:
Hot Crab Dip III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg (250g) cream cheese, softened
1 can (120g) crabmeat, drained
1/4 cup mayonnaise
3 tbsp minced green onion
4 tsp seafood sauce, ot to taste
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 cup shredded mozzeralla cheese
chopped fresh parsely to garnish

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F
In medium bowl mix together cream cheese, crab, mayo, onion, seafood sauce, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce and garlic powder. Season with salt to taste. Spread mixture in deep 2 cup casserole dish.Top with cheese. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until bubbly and heated through.
Garnish with chopped fresh parsley

Submitter: 
luci
Description: 
This easy dip is great for parties or snacking. For a chunky dip, fold in crabmeat at the end of mixing
