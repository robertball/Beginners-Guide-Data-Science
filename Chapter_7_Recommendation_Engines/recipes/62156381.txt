Title:
Pink Lemonade Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter (not margerine)
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1 egg
1/3 cup frozen pink lemonade concentrate, thawed
1 3/4 cup flour
Pink Lemonade Icing
2 Tbsp butter, softened
2 cups icing sugar
2 Tbsp pink lemonade concentrate, thawed (+ a little extra if needed)
red food coloring (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Beat 1/2 cup butter until smooth. Add sugar, baking powder, and baking soda; beat until combined. Beat in egg and 1/3 cup pink lemonade concentrate. Beat in flour.
Drop by rounded spoonfuls 2" apart on cookie sheet lined with parchment paper.
Bake in 350 oven for 10 - 12 minutes, or until edges are golden. Cool on cooling racks.
For Pink Lemonade Icing:
Beat 2 Tbsp butter, 2 cups icing sugar,and 2 Tbsp pink lemonade concentrate until smooth. If necessary add more pink lemonade concentrate until reaching desired consistency. If desired, add red food coloring to increase the pink coloring of the icing.
Frost cooled cookies.
