Title:
Italian Butter Bean Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/468892.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 lb. sweet Italian sausage
1 red bell pepper, diced
1 orange bell pepper, diced (can substitute green)
1 yellow bell pepper, diced
1/2 sweet onion, diced small
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp black pepper
2 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
1 can butter beans
2 cans diced tomatoes
4 cups penne pasta
1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Begin by putting a pot of water on to boil for the pasta. While that comes to a boil, add the olive oil in a large non-stick skillet and heat over medium high heat.
Chop all the veggies. Brown the sausage together with the vegetables and the seasonings over medium heat. Onions should be somewhat translucent.
Add the beans and the tomatoes. Reduce heat to simmer.
Put the pasta in the boiling water. Cook pasta for 10 minutes. Drain well and add to the simmering skillet.
Adjust seasonings and add salt to your taste.
Serve with grated Parmesan cheese.
