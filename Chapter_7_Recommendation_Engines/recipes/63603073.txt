Title:
Steaks Inside Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless steaks, 1 1/2 inches thick
Olive oil, to coat
Kosher salt and ground black pepper

Directions:
--
Cook Time:
5 min
Level:
Easy
Serves:
1 to 2 servings
Ingredients
Bring steaks to room temperature.
Place dual window fan in kitchen window blowing out.  Drape sheet over chin up bar to seal off kitchen smoke.
Place cast-iron skillet in the oven and heat the oven to 500 degrees F.
Coat the steak lightly with oil and sprinkle both sides with a generous pinch of salt. Grind on black pepper.
When the oven nears temperature, turn stove burner on high (3 minutes before transfer).  When oven reaches temperature, remove the skillet and place on the range over high heat burner.
Immediately place the steak in the middle of the hot, dry skillet. Cook 30 seconds without moving. Turn with tongs and cook another 30 seconds, then put the iron pan with steaks straight into the oven for 2 minutes. Flip the steak and cook for another 2 minutes. (medium rare.)
Remove the steak from the skillet, cover loosely with foil and rest for 2 minutes. Serve whole or slice thin and fan onto plate.
