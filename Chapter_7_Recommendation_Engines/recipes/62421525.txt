Title:
Skinny Taco Salad with Spicy Ranch Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/444961.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound 90 percent lean or better ground beef
1 tablespoon Smoky Southwest Spice Blend (see my recipe for this or use your own or store-bought taco seasoning)
8 ounces shredded lettuce
2 medium to large red tomatoes, diced
4 ounces shredded low-fat cheddar cheese
1 large onion, halved and sliced
1 sweet red pepper, cut into thin strips
1 sweet yellow pepper, cut into thin strips
1 cup salsa
4 ounces baked tortilla chips
1 cup Spicy Ranch Dressing (see my recipe or use store-bought light ranch)

Directions:
Brown ground beef with seasoning mix.
Crush and spread chips across a plate, top with lettuce, meat, tomatoes, cheese, peppers onions, salsa and dressing.

Submitter: 
Scott Koeneman
Description: 
This home-made version has all the flavor of a high-fat restaurant salad, with out the calories,  and is easy to make. Baked tortilla chips are topped with lean seasoned beef, tomato, onion, sweet peppers, salsa, shredded low-fat cheese and a low-fat home-made spicy ranch dressing. It has the crunch of the chips and fresh peppers and onions, the spicy beef and sauces, the mellow cheese and cool tomatoes all for only 360 calories with 11 grams of fat and 4 of fiber.
