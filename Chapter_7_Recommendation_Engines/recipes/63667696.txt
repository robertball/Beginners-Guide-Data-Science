Title:
Great Pudding Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ox container Cool Whip
1 pkg Instant Vanilla Pudding ( best to use sugar free) can use ny flavor
milk ( 1/2 the milk called for on the pkg directions)

Directions:
in bowl pudding and 1/2 milk the directions call for...whisk until it begins to thicken.
Then fold in 1 8 oz container of Cool Whip.
A great frosting ....spread on cakes and piped onto cupcakes, a tasty filling in crepes or on waffles along with some fruit...way less sugar too!
