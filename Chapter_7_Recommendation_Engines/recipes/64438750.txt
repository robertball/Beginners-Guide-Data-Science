Title:
Cayman Custard-Topped Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2354535.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of cornmeal
1 cup of all purpose flour
3 cups of white sugar
1 Tablespoon of baking powder
1 Tablespoon of cornstarch
1 nutmeg, grated
dash of cinnamon
1 can of evaporated milk
1 can of coconut milk
2 cans of water
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 stick of butter, melted
1 tsp of vanilla flavouring

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 F
2. Grease a 9x13 pan
3. Mix all dry ingredients together with a whisk.
4. Add the coconut milk and evaporated milk. Fill each can with water and add.
5. Add beaten egg, melted butter and vanilla flavouring.
6. Use a mixer to mix well.
7. Pour immediately into greased pan (DO NOT LET SIT)
8. Bake for 1 hour and 30 min.

Submitter: 
Sharice McLean
Description: 
You won't find the recipe anywhere other than the Cayman Islands! One of my favourite desserts growing up in Cayman. Very sweet and you will be wanting more!
