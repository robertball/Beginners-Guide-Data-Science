Title:
Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/729992.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lg can fruit coctail, mostly drained
1 sm can manderin oranges, drained
1 apple, peeled cut into bite size pieces
1 banana, peeled cut into bite size pieces
1 sm container cool whip
1 cup coconut
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
In med sized bowl, add all ingredients and mix. Chill and serve.
