Title:
Powdered sugar cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups buttered flavored shortening
2 eggs
2 cups powder sugar
2 t. vanilla
2 t. cream of tarter
1 t. salt
2 t. baking soda
2 cups flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix Shortening, eggs, and powdered sugar. Stir in the rest of the ingredients.Mix well; roll dough into 1 inch balls. Place on cookie sheets. Press down with a glass cup (first make sure the bottom of the cup is dipped in powdered sugar)Bake in 350f oven, For 10 minutes or until golden brown. Cool slightly before removing from cookie sheets.
