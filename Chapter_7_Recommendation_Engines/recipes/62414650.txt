Title:
Sazon Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon Ground Corriander
1 Tablespoon Ground Cumin
1 tablespoon Ground annatto seeds
1 tablespoon Garlic powder
1 tablespoon Salt

Directions:
1.Mix all of the ingredients together and store the sazón in an airtight container. It will keep fresh for about 3 months.
2.In recipes, use about 1 1/2 teaspoons for each packet of sazón called for.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This is commonly used in Mexican and Spanish cooking.  I usually buy the Sazon packets at the store but it was brought to my attention they have a lot of MSG.  So here is a homemade version without all the MSG.
