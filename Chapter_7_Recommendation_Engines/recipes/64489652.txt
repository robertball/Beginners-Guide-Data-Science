Title:
Ground Pork Cellophane Curry Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2576893.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups of water
1 pound of ground pork or any kind of meat
2 pieces of garlic, minced
1 can of chili oil bamboo shoots
1 bunch of green onions, chopped
1 bunch of cilantro, chopped
1 tablespoon of hoisin sauce
1 tablespoon of siracha sauce
1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
1 small can of red curry paste
1 can of coconut milk
1 pack of cellophane/beanthread noodes
1 Lime
Fish Sauce for flavoring

Directions:
Place water into a pot and bring to a boil on high heat.
Add the cellophane noodles and cook until soft
Drain the noodles in a strain and run cold water over the noodles
Use a kitchen scissor to cut the noodles and set aside
In a pot pour the oil from the chili oil bamboo and the minced garlic.  Sautee the garlic until golden.
Add in the meat and cook  until the meat is ready
Add in chopped green onions and bamboo shoots.  Cook for 5 minutes.
Add in the oyster, hoision and sirachi sauce and stir for 1 minute.
Add the curry past and coconut milk.  Mix and stir for 5 minutes.  Until curry paste have been completely dissolved.
Turn the heat down to low and add in the noodles.  Cook until the noodles are sticky and transparent.
Add in cilantro and lime and fish sauce for taste
