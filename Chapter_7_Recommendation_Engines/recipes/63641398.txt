Title:
Chipotle Aioli (Spicy) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 1/2 tbls. chopped chipotles in adobo sauce
3 tbls. fresh lime juice
1 large clove of garlic, minced
1/8 tsp. salt

Directions:
Chop Chipotles into little bits
Mix all ingredients in bowl
Let chill in fridge

Submitter: 
Miller
Description: 
Great on chicken or tuna sandwiches...  or as a dip..
