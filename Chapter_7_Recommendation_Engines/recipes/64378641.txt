Title:
Beef Barley Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs Beef cut into 1/2-3/4 inch dice.  Use the best you can buy
1 medium large onion chopped into fine dice
4 medium carrots cut into fine dice
48-72 ounces beef broth
1-1.5 cups uncooked barley (Small pearled)
2 bay leaves
2-3 cloves of cloves
1/4-1/2 tsp. fresh ground nutmeg
Salt and peper to tast

Directions:
Use a large heavy bottom 6-8 qtr. pot or Dutch oven
Heat pot and 1-2 tablespoons olive oil till shimmering and brown beef well in batchs and remove from pot
Add vegetables and cook stirring until onions have wilted and started to brown
Add beef broth and bring to a slow boil
Add bay leaves, 15 grinds of pepper from a pepper mill, 2 cloves of clove and nutmeg
add 1 cup barley and return to boil stirring occasionally then lower heat to simmer and cover
Cook gently for 45-60 min stirring occasionally
Uncover and check seasonings adding salt and pepper as necessary
