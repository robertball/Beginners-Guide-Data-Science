Title:
ez crab meat appetizer Recipe

Ingredients:
1)1-can (6-ounce) crabmeat,drained  2)1-jar cocktail or chilil sauce  3)8-ounce cream-cheese,softened  4)1-teaspoon worcestershire sauce  5)1-Tablespoon fresh-squeezed lemon juice  6)2-Tablespoons mayo  7)garlic salt,to taste  optional-can also use drained canned shrimp-mashed along with crabme

Directions:
1) combine everything together in a medium bowl,and put into a small container and chill. serve with crackers. , drain crabmeat and mash drained shrimp with fork and combine with above ingredients.
