Title:
Favorite Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups smooth peanut butter
1/2 cup butter or margarine
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla
1 cup flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. baking soda

Directions:
Mix PB, margarine and sugars together well.
Blend in eggs and vanilla.
Mix flour, soda and salt; stir into PB mixture.
The dough will be very soft.
Drop by spoonfuls onto cookie sheets and flatten slightly with a fork dipped in flour.
Bake @ 350 x 10-12 min. or until centers are still soft to the touch.
Cool on pans x at least 5 min. before removing to wire racks to cool.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
These are my favorite PB cookies. They're soft and delicious and stay fresh longer than some other recipes.
