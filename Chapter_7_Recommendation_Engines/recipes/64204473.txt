Title:
Big Ray's Slow Cooker Chicken Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1617056.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts (or thighs)
1 can chili pinto beans, undrained (15 oz)
1 1/2 cup salsa
1 can corn, drained
12 flour tortilla
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
chopped lettuce
chopped tomatoes
sour cream

Directions:
Place chicken, beans and salsa in of slow cooker, stir to coat chicken.  Cook on high 2 to 3 hours, adding corn final 1/2 hour.
Shred chicken.  Spoon on tortilla and sprinkle cheese on top.  Add additional topping if desired.  Fold over tortilla or roll up

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
The slow cooker melds the flavors of the chicken, beans and salsa and keeps the chicken moist.  You can add additional heat by adding chopped jalapeno with the salsa before turning on slow cooker.
