Title:
Chocolate Peanut Butter Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag microwave popcorn
7 oz. Peanut Butter
4-1.55oz. Chocolate Bars
1 small mixing bowl (microwave safe)
1 large serving bowl

Directions:
Pop the microwave popcorn (or air pop if you prefer).  Using a microwave, melt the chocolate bars and peanut butter together in a microwave safe bowl (usually take about a minute with stirring every 30 secs).  Pour the chocolate/peanut butter sauce over the popcorn in a large bowl.
