Title:
Maryland Crab Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of whole tomatoes cut into small pieces
3c. water
2 c. chicken broth or base
1 c. chopped carrots
1 c. chopped carrots
1 c. yellow sweet corn
2 Tblsp. Instant Chopped Onion
1 Tbsp. Old Bay Seasoning
1 Lb. Crab meat
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Place all ingredients except crab meat into a 4 quart saucepan. Heat to boil, cover and cook for 5 min. Reduce heat to low, add crab meat and simmer 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Patti
Description: 
Don't look for creamy crab soup on the menu in Maryland. This recipe for Maryland-Style Crab Soup calls for a  tomato base chock full of vegetables
