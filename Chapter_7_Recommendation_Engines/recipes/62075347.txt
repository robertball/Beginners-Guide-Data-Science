Title:
Ceasars Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large Head Romaine Lettuce
1 cup Large Seasoned Crutons
Freshly grated Parmesan Cheese
2 Eggs
1 tsp Garlic Salt
3 tbsp Fresh Lemon Juice
Freshly Ground Pepper to taste
Enough Vegetable Oil to make 3/4 cup dressing

Directions:
Wash and dry Romaine lettuce. Tear into large bite size pieces. Place into salad bowl.
Grate Parmesan Cheese onto lettuce
Place crutons on top of cheese.
In small bowl, wisk eggs and add lemon juice, garlic salt, and ground pepper.
Wisk until well incorporated.
Add enough vegetable oil to the lemon and egg mixture to make 3/4 cup dressing.
Wisk until well blended. Pour over lettuce and toss, just before serving.

Submitter: 
Andrea F
Description: 
This recipe is from a San Fransico Restaurant Cookbook my mother got about 50 years ago. It has been altered a bit over the years, but is a favorite and often requested.
