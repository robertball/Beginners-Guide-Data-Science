Title:
Blueberry Upside Down Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tbsp. butter, softened and divided
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 cups fresh blueberries (if using frozen - do not thaw before using)
3/4 cup sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/4 cups cake flour
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
In small saucepan, melt 2 tbsp. butter and stir in brown sugar.
Spread into an ungreased 8" baking dish.
Arrange blueberries in a single layer over brown sugar mixture.
In large bowl, cream remaining butter and sugar.
Add egg and vanilla and mix well.  Combine flour and baking powder and add to mixture alternately with milk.
Carefully pour over blueberries.
Bake for 45-45 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
Immediately invert onto serving platter.
Cool and serve (with whipped cream if desired).
