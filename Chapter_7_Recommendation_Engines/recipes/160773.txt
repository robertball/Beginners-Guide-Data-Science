Title:
The Champagne Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/408339.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 fluid ounce pear brandy
5 fluid ounces Champagne or other sparkling white wine
1 tablespoon whole-berry blackberry preserves

Directions:
Pour the brandy into a champagne flute. Top with champagne. Gently drop in preserves.

Submitter: 
c-biskit
Description: 
With a little pear brandy and a bit of fruit compote, this makes a great pre-dinner cocktail. Serve it in a tall champagne flute.
