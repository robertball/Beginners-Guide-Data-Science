Title:
Very Easy Korean Pork Bulgogi (Lovely Spicy) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4561524.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound pork (1/2 inch cut)
3 spoons of A1 sauce
2 spoons of All Purpose hot & spicy sauce (Ajumma Repuclic)
1 whole onion

Directions:
Slice onion
cook the pork in fry pan
put A1 sauce and All Purpose hot & spicy sauce together (that's it)

Submitter: 
Jae Kim
Description: 
You can enjoy authentic Korean Bulgogi in home. It comes with familiar A1 sauce.
