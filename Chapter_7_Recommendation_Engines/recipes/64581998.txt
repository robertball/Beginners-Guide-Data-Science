Title:
Buttermilk Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp soda
1 TBS sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1 egg
1 c buttermilk
2 TBS oil

Directions:
Sift dry ingredients together in mixing bowl.  Combine wet ingredients and stir into dry ingredients just until moistened.  Cook on medium hot griddle until done.
