Title:
Brushetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 plum tomatoes
4 garlic cloves
bag of fresh basil-no stems
1 red onion
1/8 cup olive oil
2 loaves French bread

Directions:
1.) Chop up the garlic, basil and onion. Dice the tomatoes; salt and pepper the tomatoes on both sides.
2.) Soak the vegetables in olive oil overnight.
3.) Slice the bread on an angle and baste one side with olive oil.
4.) Bake at 350 for 10 minutes.
5.) Scoop on the vegetables.
