Title:
Sam I am, Green eggs and Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 thick ham slices
2 string cheese
2 tbsp butter
2 drops green food coloring.
1-2 tsp water
2 toothpicks
1 tbsp honey

Directions:
Place 1 tbsp of butter and 1 drop of food coloring in a bowl and microwave for 15 to 20 seconds. When melted, mix and set aside.
Eggs: In a large skillet melt the remianing 1 tbsp of butter and 1 drop of food coloring into the pan. Have it between medium and high heat. Break eggs into skillet. When egg whites are set, add water. Cover skillet and cook eggs for 3 to 4 minutes or until egg yolks are cooked. to reach desired color brush on additional butter  mixture. Salt and pepper to taste.
Ham: Take ham slices and place in skillet at medium heat and brown on each side then brush on butter and food coloring mixture to reach desired color. Take out of the pan and place on cutting board. Put a string cheese stick on one end of the ham slice and roll it up in a cornucopia shape and use a toothpick to keep together. Place the ham rolls into the skillet again at medium heat for about a minute to let the cheese melt.
Remove toothpicks when serving. Garnish with a drizzle of honey over ham.

Submitter: 
ANGELA O.
Description: 
A breakfast fit for a ultimate Dr. Suess fan and kids of all ages.
Overeasy eggs and a sweet, cheesy ham roll that will put a smile on your faces.
I make this dish for my kids right after we read the book. How much fun is that!
