Title:
Malty Maple Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup coarse ground cornmeal
1 cup porter or dark ale
1/4 cup maple syrup
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon baking powder
2 eggs
1/4 cup melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400Â°F with the greased cast iron pan inside.
Combine cornmeal, porter and maple syrup in small bowl; set aside.
Sift flour, salt and baking powder into large bowl.
Add cornmeal mixture, eggs and melted butter. Stir until well blended.
Pour batter into greased cast iron baking pan and bake until wooden pick inserted into center comes out clean, about 20 to 25 minutes.
Cool 10 minutes. Slice and serve.
