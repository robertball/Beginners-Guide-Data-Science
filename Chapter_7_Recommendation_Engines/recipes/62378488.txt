Title:
Smoky Swiss and Bacon Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 slices bacon cooked, drained and crumbled
1/2 cup chopped smoked almonds
2 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, room temperature
2/3 cup mayo
3 cups (8 ounces) shredded Swiss cheese
1/4 cup chopped green onion
2 dashes hot sauce
Pepper to taste
Assorted crackers, apple slices or bread sticks

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, beat together the cream cheese and mayo until smooth.  Stir in the Swiss cheese, green onion, bacon and almonds.  Season with hot sauce and pepper.  Spread in 1 quart baking dish or 9 inch pie plate. Bake for 20 minutes until slightly brown on top.  Serve with crackers, apple slices or bread sticks.
