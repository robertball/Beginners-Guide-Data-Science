Title:
Blue Cheese Filled Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/589657.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs. ground beef
1/4 cup chopped onion
3 tbsp. Tabasco sauce
2 tbsp. honey
1 tbsp. garlic salt
1 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
1 tsp. black pepper
6 ozs. blue cheese, cut into 6 pieces
6 hamburger buns, toasted
Roasted red peppers (from jar)
6 Portabella mushrooms, grilled

Directions:
Preheat drill to medium-high heat.
Combine ground beef, onion, Tabasco sauce, honey, garlic salt, red pepper flakes and pepper in a large bowl; mis well.
Divide into 6 portions; form into patties around pieces of blue cheese.
Grill 8 to 10 minutes or until done.
Place burger on bun and top with roasted red peppers and portabella mushrooms.

Submitter: 
Lance Hinkle
Description: 
Here's a little twist on your typical cheeseburger.  Kids love the suprise they get when they bite into this burger.
