Title:
Simple Green Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp oil,
3 tbsp water,
1/2 tsp ground mustard,
3 level tsp of dried herbs eg: parsley, chives, basil, dill, oregano, thyme,
1 iceberg lettuce,
2 medium sized carrots,
10 cherry tomatoes (quartered)
1/2 cucumber
1 hard boiled egg
1/4 red pepper
1/4 yellow pepper

Directions:
Mix the oil, water, mustard and herbs in a large salad bowl.
Chop the lettuce, carrot, cucumber, egg, and peppers into smaal equal sized chunks.
Add to the dressing and stir in the tomatoes

Submitter: 
sasa
Description: 
With 3 easy steps make a healthy salad lettuce, carrot, tomato, cucumber, boiled egg, red pepper and yellow pepper combined with tasty herbs using only 3 tbsp of oil!
