Title:
Fettucine Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1126122.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 lb fettucine noodles
4 tsp. butter
2 tbsp. flour
2 cups of 2% per cent milk
1/4 cup of parmesan cheese
1/4 tsp. nutmeg

Directions:
Cook fettucine in boiling salted water until done, drain and place in serving bowl.
Meanwhile, melt butter in a small saucepan. Add flour and cook. stirring for 30 second. Add milk and cook on medium heat, stirring constantly until thickened, about three minutes.
Add cheese, nutmeg, salt and pepper.
Stir until combines, pour over pasta and toss well.

Submitter: 
Andree Vaillancourt
Description: 
A healthy and easy fettucine alfredo that everyone will enjoy!
