Title:
Chocolate Fondant Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz semi sweet chocolate
3 eggs
1/3 cup + 1 Tbsp sugar
3.5 Tbsp butter
1 Tbsp flour
4 Hershey's kisses or caramels

Directions:
Preheat oven to 475Â°. Grease and flour 4 large muffin tins.
Melt the butter and butter over low heat in a small saucepan or in the microwave. Stir until smooth and shiny.
Mix together the sugar and flour, add eggs one at a time.
Gradually add in the melted chocolate and butter until well mixed.
Pour into the prepared muffin tins and add a chocolate or caramel candy in the center (covered by the chocolate mixture.)
Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, allow to cool at least 10 minutes before eating.

Submitter: 
Lynn Pennec
Description: 
These are decadent french chocolate wonders. Hardly any flour and unbelievably easy to do. Sometimes I make them towards the end of dinner when unexpected friends drop in.
