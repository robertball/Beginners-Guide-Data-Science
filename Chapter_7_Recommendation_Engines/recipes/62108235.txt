Title:
Tango Mango Freeze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 mangos
1 liter sweet sparkling white wine, Asti Spumante
1/2 C. sugar more or less depending on sweetness of the mango
Juice of 1 lime
2 egg whites

Directions:
Peel the mango and cut into chunks. Freeze for 3 hours or over night or until ready to prepare.
Place the frozen mango in the food processer and process for a few minutes until nicely chopped. Add the  1/2 the sugar and lime juice and continue processing until the thick and creamy. Taste for sweetness and add more of the sugar, if needed and beat in egg whites. Taste for sweetness and add more sugar if needed.
Freeze in a hard plastic container with a tightly fitted lid until ready to serve.
Using Champagen flutes, place a scoop or two of the frozen mango in each flute and pour the sweet sparkling wine, (Asti Spumante). Garnish with a sprig of mint or a thin strip of lime peel.
Or serve like frozen custard with biscotti or a crisp sweet cookie.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
A frozen dessert or a palate cleanser for a multi-course dinner.
Simple to make, elegant presentation and unusual combination.
