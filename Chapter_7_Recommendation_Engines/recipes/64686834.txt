Title:
Ham Breakfast Burrito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 diced onion
12 oz diced ham
18 eggs
1 6 oz can green chilies
1 6 oz  roasted red peppers
3 tbs chipotle salsa
2 tsp salt
2 tsp pepper
5 cups grated cheese
20 tortillas

Directions:
Saut√© onion in olive oil, add diced ham when onion is almost translucent.  scramble 18 eggs and add to onion and ham mixture with can of diced mild green chilies.  Dice roasted red peppers and add to mixture.  Add in salt pepper and salsa and mix well.
Take 1/4 cup of mixture and 1/4 cup of cheese and roll up in tortilla.
Wrap burrito in sandwich wrap and Store in refrigerator until ready to use.
Warm in microwave for 1.5 minutes and then place on grill until cheese is melted and tortilla is crisp.

Submitter: 
Dave Johnson
Description: 
A quick and easy burrito that can be stored for daily use.
