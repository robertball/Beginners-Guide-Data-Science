Title:
Hula's Hot Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cocoa powder
3 cups sugar
3 tsp salt
4 cup heavy whipping cream
8 TBSP butter

Directions:
Combine 4 cups cocoa powder + 3 cups sugar + 3 tsp salt mix together
Heat 4 cups heavy whipping cream to a boil - stir constantly
Add dry ingredients to heavy whipping cream in 3 parts - stir to blend well
Reheat to boil until smooth - stir constantly
Add 8 TBSP of butter and remove from heat - stir well to blend
