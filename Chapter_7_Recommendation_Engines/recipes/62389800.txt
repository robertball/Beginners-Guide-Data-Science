Title:
Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb ground chuck
1 egg
1 C milk
C dry bread crumbs
1 T minced dried onion
1 T dried parsley

Directions:
Mix together and pat into loaf shape. Bake in loaf pan 1 1/2 hours. Metal pan - 350°, glass - 325°.

Submitter: 
Lynn Pennec
Description: 
My mother's recipe handed down from her mother. Good simple Pennsylvanian staple dinner.
