Title:
Three Seed Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/510738.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 Cup all purpose flour
1 Cup whole wheat flour
1/4 Cup sunflower seeds
1/4 Cup flaxseeds
1/4 Cup wheat germ
1 tbsp granulated sugar
1 tbsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 Cup cold butter, cubed
1 Cup milk
1 Egg, lightly beaten
2 Tbsp sesame seeds

Directions:
1.  Whisk together all-purpose flour, whole wheat flour, sunflower seeds, flaxsees, wheat germ, sugar, baking powder and salt.  Using pastry blender or 2 knives, cut in butter until in coarse crumbs.  Stir in milk to form slightly sticky dough.
2.  With floured hands, turn out onto lightly floured surface; knead gently 10 times.  Pat out to 7 inch square.  Cut into quarters; cut each quarter into 3 strips.  Place, 1" apart on ungreased baking sheet.  Brush tops with egg; sprinkle with sesame seeds
3.  Bake in centre of 425F oven until golden; about 12 minutes.  Let cool on pan rack.
