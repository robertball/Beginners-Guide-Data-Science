Title:
Spicy Mango Sweet Potato Tempeh Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1126286.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cubed peeled sweet potatoes
2 tsp coconut oil
4 - 6 slices tempeh, optional
1/4 - 1 green pepper, diced
2-3 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
6 T sweetened soy sauce (or just regular soy sauce)
3/4 cup water
1 tablespoons hot sauce, or to taste
1 tsp. salt or to taste
1 T. cornstarch
1 ripe mango, peeled and cubed
2 T. cilantro, opt.

Directions:
Bake whole or peeled and cubed or steam sweet potatoes til just tender. This can be done ahead of time.
Meanwhile, heat oil in a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Add green pepper if using. Brown tempeh slices; cut into pieces and set aside. Stir garlic and pepper flakes into the skillet, and cook for a few minutes, until fragrant. Mix the liquids in a measuring cup, taste for seasoning, stir in corn starch, add to skillet and bring to a simmer, then stir in the sweet potato, tempeh, and mango. Cook and stir until hot and thickened. Sprinkled chopped cilantro over top, if using.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins

Honey and mango lend the sauce it's sweetness, but it's nicely countered with salty tamari and spicy hot sauce.Modified from: Spicy Mango Sweet Potato Chicken
