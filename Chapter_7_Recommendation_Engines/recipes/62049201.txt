Title:
Cod Fish Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cooked cod fish (flaked)
3 cups mashed potato
1 egg
1 teaspoon seasoning salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon thyme leaves
2 tablespoons chopped parsley
1 small onion (chopped or grated)
cooking oil
flour

Directions:
Beat egg in a bowl.
Add flaked cod fish and mashed potato. Mix well.
Add salt, pepper, thyme, onion and parsley.
Form mixture into icecream scoop sized balls and dust/coat with flour.
Cook in pan with cooking oil.
Flatten into cake about 1/4 inch thick before removing from pan and golden on both sides.
Drain on paper towels.
*These go great with baked beans, lettuce and tomato.*
