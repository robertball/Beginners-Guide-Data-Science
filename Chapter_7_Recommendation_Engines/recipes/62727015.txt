Title:
micky's swiss steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Micky's SWISS STEAK
1/2 teaspoon FIESTA brand fajita seasoning
2 tbsp oil
1 chopped onion
1 chopped bell pepper
1 can stewed tomatoes
1 can tomato soup(dilluted with one can full of v8 juice)
1/4 tsp marjoram
1/4 tsp thyme
1 tsp dried minced garlic
2 tsp knorr tomato/chicken boullion
2 bay leafs
1/4 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp celery salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 cup dried mushrooms
1 cup frozen grean beans
1 stalk celery
1 cup water

Directions:
season 2 pounds of round steak cut into single servings with FIESTA brand fajita seasoning and dredge in flour.
quickly brown each steak in 2 tbsp oil and remove.
add to drippings...
1 chopped onion
1 chopped bell pepper
saute for 3 mins.
add
1 can stewed tomatoes
1 can tomato soup(dilluted with one can full of v8 juice)
1/4 tsp marjoram
1/4 tsp thyme
1 tsp dried minced garlic
2 tsp knorr tomato/chicken boullion
2 bay leafs
1/4 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp celery salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 cup dried mushrooms
1 cup frozen grean beans
1 stalk celery
1 cup water
heat to boiling then reduce heat to low cover and slow simmer for at least 1 1/2 hours till steak is fork tender.  remove steak and bay leafs.  thicken sauce with flour/water to make gravy.  return steak to gravy and serve.
