Title:
best chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2   tablespoons vegetable oil
2   large onions, diced
4   cloves garlic, minced
2   pounds ground beef
3   (15 ounce) cans beans (I use black)
3   (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes,
drained
1   package of chili seasoning
1   teaspoon cumin
2   bay leaves
1   liter bloody mary mix
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
2   tablespoons sugar
optional toppings:
sour cream
shredded cheese
cilantro
diced onion
fresh tomatoes

Directions:
Saute onions in oil over medium heat until soft.  Add garlic and cook for about one more minute.  Set aside.
Cook beef until no longer pink.  Drain if desired.
With onion mixture and beef in the pot, add remaining ingredients and cook for 1 hour.
Serve with toppings of your choice.

Submitter: 
QUEENREYNEY
Description: 
This chili is a favorite in my house.  I make it at least 3-4 times every fall.
It also works well in the slow cooker.
