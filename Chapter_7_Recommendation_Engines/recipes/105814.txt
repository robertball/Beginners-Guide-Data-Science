Title:
Grilled Portabella Mushroom Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983407.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Archer Farmsâ„¢ ciabatta loaf, halved lengthwise
2 (6 ounce) packages portabella mushrooms
1 teaspoon steak seasoning
4 ounces deli-sliced Havarti cheese
1/2 (16 ounce) jar mayonnaise
2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil

Directions:
Heat grill. Sprinkle mushrooms with steak seasoning.
Place bread, cut side down, and mushrooms on grill over medium heat.  Cover and cook bread about 3 minutes or until toasted; cook mushrooms 6 to 8  minutes or until tender.  During last 2 minutes of cooking top each mushroom with cheese.
Meanwhile, mix mayonnaise and basil. Spread on toasted bread halves.
Slice cooked mushrooms into 1/2-inch strips.  Place on bottom half of loaf.  Add your favorite burger toppers, like lettuce and tomato slices, if you have them on hand.  Add top half of loaf.
