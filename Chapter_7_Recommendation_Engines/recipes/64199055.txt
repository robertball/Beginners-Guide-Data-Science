Title:
Yummy Marshmallows Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 pkg caramels
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1-2 packages large marshmallows
Rice Krispies
Salted peanuts, chopped

Directions:
Melt butter, caramels and sweetened condensed milk.  Cool slightly.
With toothpicks, dip marshmallows in mixture.  Then roll in chopped salted peanuts or Rice Krispies.  Let set on waxed paper.
