Title:
Fresh Honey and Basil Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1284832.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups sliced cucumber (skins on or off)
1/2 c. seasoned rice wine vinegar
1/4 c. honey
4 Tbsp fresh basil leaves (chopped fine)
2 Tbsp fresh chives (chopped fine)
pinch salt (optional

Directions:
Place all in bowl and toss gently with rubber spatula.  Allow to marinate for one hour before serving.  Garnish with additional fresh basil if you like!

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
Great summer garden salad with a lightly sweetened twist.  The fresh basil takes the flavor to another level!
