Title:
Avocado and Mushroom Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/8 cup olive oil
1/4 cup whole grain mustard
1/2 cup sherry vinegar
1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
1 pound sliced fresh mushrooms
12 ounces diced green bell pepper
1 head butterhead lettuce
12 ounces diced red bell pepper
1 avocado - peeled, pitted and sliced

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together the oil, mustard, vinegar, basil, bell peppers and mushrooms. Set aside and allow to marinate for 10 minutes.
Serve each salad portion on a leaf of lettuce. Fan sliced avocado on top and serve with two slices of grilled Italian bread.

Submitter: 
Avocado Commission
Description: 
This salad is dressed with a basil and mustard vinaigrette and served with lettuce and grilled Italian bread.
