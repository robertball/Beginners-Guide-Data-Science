Title:
Deviled Pickled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans ( 1 lb.) 4-oz. size beets
1 cup of beet juice
1 cup of sugar
3/4 cup of vinegar
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 t. pepper
2 bay leaves
10 whole cloves
1/4 cup mayo
1 dozen of boiled eggs
1 t. sweet relish
1 t. prepared mustard

Directions:
Heat cloves, beet juice, sugar, vinegar, salt, pepper, bay leaves to boiling, then reduce heat and cook slowly for 5 minutes.
Poor hot liquid over beets and eggs.   Cool and let stand in refrigerator for 3 days.
The eggs are ready to fill after they have been halved lengthwise. (Save the yoke)
In a bowl mix the egg yoke, mayo, sweet relish, and mustard well.
Then fill the eggs and ENJOY!
