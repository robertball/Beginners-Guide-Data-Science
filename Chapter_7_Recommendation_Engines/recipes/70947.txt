Title:
Thai-Style Chicken Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3/4 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast halves, cut into thin slices
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 (4.9 ounce) package RICE-A-RONI® Chicken & Broccoli Flavor
1 3/4 cups water
2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter
1 garlic clove, pressed
1 1/2 cups frozen pea pods
1/2 cup red pepper strips, cut into 2-inch pieces
Peanuts (optional)

Directions:
Toss chicken with soy sauce; set aside.  In large skillet over medium heat, saute rice-vermicelli mix according to package directions.
Slowly stir in water, seasonings, peanut butter and garlic.  Stir in chicken; bring to a boil. Cover; reduce heat to low.  Simmer 12 minutes.
Stir in pea pods and bell pepper; return to a simmer. Cover; simmer 3 to 5 minutes until vegetables are crisp-tender.  Stir; let stand 3 minutes. Top with peanuts, if desired.

Submitter: 
RICE-A-RONI®
Description: 
Try a new twist on cooking with this exotic Thai-inspired dish. Chicken, rice and broccoli are steamed with snow peas and red peppers in an exotic peanut sauce. This is very impressive thirty minute dish.
