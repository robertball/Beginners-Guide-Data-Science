Title:
Cherry Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. hot water
3/4 c. sugar
4 c. cold water
1/2 c. fresh lemon juice
1 (6 oz.) jar maraschino cherries, undrained

Directions:
In a large pitcher, combine hot water and sugar; Stir util sugar is dissolved.
Add cold water, lemon juice, and cherries with their juice.
Mix well and serve over ice.
