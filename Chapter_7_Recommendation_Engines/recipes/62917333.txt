Title:
Best Ever Banana Bread with extra goodness Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs, beaten
1/3 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
1 cup mashed bananas
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Spray one 9x5 inch loaf pan with non-stick spray coating.
Blend together the eggs, buttermilk, applesauce, vanilla and bananas.
Sift together the sugar, flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt. Add to banana mixture. Mix well.
Pour into prepared loaf pan and sprinkle top with chocolate chips.
Bake 1 hour and 20 minutes or until a cake tester inserted in the center comes out clean.
