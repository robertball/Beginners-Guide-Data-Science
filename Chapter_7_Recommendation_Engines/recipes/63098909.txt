Title:
Glitter Play Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Cups Flour
4 Tablespoons Vegetable Oil
4 Cups Water
2 Cups Salt
6 Teaspoons Cream of Tarter
Food Coloring
1/4 Cup Glitter
Airtight Container or Sealed 1 Gallon Plastic Bag

Directions:
Mix all ingredients (except glitter) in a large saucepan (without heat).  Stir until smooth (no lumps).  Then, over medium heat, stir mixture constantly until it forms a ball.  Remove dough from saucepan, add glitter and knead until smooth. Allow to cool 15 minutes before placing in air-tight container or sealed 1 Gallon plastic bag. Glitter play dough will be good for several weeks stored in a tightly sealed container at room temperature.  Do not refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Alley
Description: 
Great economical recipe for play dough! Fun to make with kids.  Lasts a long time (for months) before drying out.
