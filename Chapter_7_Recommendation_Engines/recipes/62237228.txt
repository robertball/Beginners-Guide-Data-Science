Title:
Seafood Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 to 4 stalks of celery (leaves too) chopped
2 or 3 carrots chopped
One large (red or sweet) onion chopped
Two large potatoes chopped
2 large cloves of garlic grated or finely minced
2 cans condensed clam chowder (I uses Campbell’s soups)
2 cans condensed cream of shrimp soup
2 cans cream of mushroom or cream of celery soup
6 cans of milk (use above cans)
1 lb bay scallops
l lb shrimp (cut in 1/2 or 1/3 if large)
1/2 lb tilapia fillets (or Grouper) cut to 1" size
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup fresh chopped Parsley

Directions:
Simmer vegetables in soups and milk on LOW heat until potatoes are fork tender. Add the seafood. Simmer LOW & SLOW (be careful not to scorch the milk!) for 30 minutes or until seafood is cooked to your liking, stir frequently.  Add Salt, pepper and parsley. Serve with your choice of bread or cheese toast.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Merrianne
Description: 
I made up this recipe for my extended family for Christmas Eve dinner, it now is a tradition!  It is a delicious milk based stew with several different types of seafood that can be changed with whatever type you like.  It’s a hearty and satisfying stew on a cold night.
