Title:
Best Burgers Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground sirloin
2 tbs Worcestershire sauce
1 tbs minced garlice
1 egg
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
3 tbs butter; sliced into 6 X 1/2 TBS butter patties - frozen

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except for the butter in a bowl and mix well.
Shape ground sirloin mix into 1/3 pound balls, inserting frozen butter patty in the middle of each burger
Gently flatten balls into patties about an inch thick.
Grill to desired temperature - watch grill for flare up if the butter escapes your burgers.
Put your favorite cheese on shortly before removing burgers from grill - you don't want the cheese to melt completely, just to get a little soft.
Serve on grilled buns with your favorite condiments!
