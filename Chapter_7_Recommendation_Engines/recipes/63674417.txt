Title:
Hearty Ground Beef Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1 stalk celery, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 green bell pepper, chopped
1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes with chiles
3 tablespoons chili powder, or to desired heat
1 (15.5 ounce) can light red beans, drained
1 (15.5 ounce) can dark red beans, drained
1 (15.5 ounce) can black beans, drained
Sea salt to taste
Black pepper to taste
2 teaspoons white vinegar
Shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add ground beef, cooking it and breaking it up into crumbles. Cook until the meat is no longer pink, about 10 minutes.
Add celery, onion and green pepper. Cook until the onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
Add tomatoes, chili powder and beans. Bring mixture to a boil, then simmer on low for an hour, or until the chili has started to thicken.
Season with salt, pepper and vinegar.
Garnish with shredded cheese.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
After searching through several recipes, I decided to put this recipe together by using my favorite ingredients. The resulting chili is flavorful and hearty. It's excellent when served with home-made corn bread.
