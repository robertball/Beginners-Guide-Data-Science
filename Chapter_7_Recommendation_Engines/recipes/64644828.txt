Title:
Taco soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 small onion, chopped
1 (15oz) can kidney beans, undrained
1 (15oz) can black beans, undrained
1 (15oz) can corn kernels, undrained
1 (15oz) can diced tomatoes
1 (8oz) can tomato sauce
1 pouch (2 tbsp) taco seasoning

Directions:
Brown the ground beef and onion in a large pot, until cooked through and crumbled. Drain any excess fat.
Add the remaining ingredients to the pot and stir to combine. Cook over medium heat, letting simmer for about 10 minutes to let flavors blend.
Serve with any or all of these optional taco toppings: shredded cheddar cheese, sour cream, diced tomatoes / pico de gallo, sliced avocado, corn chips, lime wedges, chopped cilantro Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Sudie B
Description: 
A very easy soup that reminds your taste buds of tacos.  Great for days when you'd rather not cook.  Not kid-tested, but I think they would approve!
