Title:
Potato leek cauliflower soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2T butter
4-5 garlic cloves
2 leeks
1/2 cauliflower
6-8 red potatoes
2 c water
2 c  broth used veg
4 bay leaves
Rosemary
Handful fresh parsley

Directions:
Sauté butter garlic leeks cauliflower
Peel potato and microwave 5 min
Add potato
Add herbs
Add broth
Simmer
Purée
