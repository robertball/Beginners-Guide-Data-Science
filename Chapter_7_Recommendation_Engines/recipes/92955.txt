Title:
Cheesy Barbecued Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Kellogg's速 Corn Flakes速 cereal
2 eggs
1/3 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1/2 pound ground beef
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup ketchup
3/4 cup water
3 tablespoons firmly packed brown sugar
1 tablespoon instant minced onion
1 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons vinegar
1 teaspoon celery seed
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Crush KELLOGG'S CORN FLAKES(R) cereal slightly in medium mixing bowl. Add eggs, milk, salt and pepper. Mix well. Let stand about 5 minutes or until cereal softens.
Add beef and cheese, mixing until combined. Shape into 1-inch meatballs. Place in single layer in shallow baking pan coated with cooking spray.
Bake at 400 degrees  F about 10 minutes or until browned. Remove from oven and immediately loosen meatballs from pan.
To make sauce, combine remaining ingredients in 2-quart saucepan. Bring to boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally. Reduce heat to low. Add meatballs and cook uncovered for 15 minutes. Serve hot as an appetizer or over rice or noodles.
