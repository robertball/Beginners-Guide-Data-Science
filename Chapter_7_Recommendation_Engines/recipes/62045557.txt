Title:
spaghetti primavera Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup bottled italian salad dressing
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 green pepper diced
1 red pepper diced
1 small zuccchini diced
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1 small onion diced
2 garlic cloves chopped
3 tablespoons sliced black olives
8 ounces thin spaghetti cooked and drained
3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
In skillet combine first 9 ingredients. Simmer vegetables about 10 minutes until tender crisp. season with salt and pepper. Stir in pasta and parsley. Top with cheese.
