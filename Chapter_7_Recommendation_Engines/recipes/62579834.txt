Title:
Cream frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/547679.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups heavy whipping cream
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup Herseys special dark cocoa
1 tsp pure vanilla extract

Directions:
in large mixing bowl add 2 cups heavy whipping cream,1/2 cup white sugar,1/2 cup Herseys special dark cocoa powder, and 1 tsp pure vanilla. on medium high speed mix ingredents untill stiff peaks form or to the consistancie you like and spreadable.
