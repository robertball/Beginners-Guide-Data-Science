Title:
PROGRESSO CRISPY BAKED CHICKEN/PORK CHOPS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Progresso Italian Style Bread Crumbs
2 tbsp margarine or butter, melted
1 egg
6 boneless skinless chicken breast halves or 6 pork chops (1/2 in. thick)

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 F. Spray 15 X 10 X 1 in. baking pan with nonstick cooking spray. In shallow bowl, combine bread crumbs and margarine/butter. In another shallow bowl, beat egg with fork. Dip chicken/pork into egg; coat evenly with bread crumb mixture. Place in sprayed pan. Bake for 20 to 30 min or until no longer pink when cut in thickest part, turning once.
