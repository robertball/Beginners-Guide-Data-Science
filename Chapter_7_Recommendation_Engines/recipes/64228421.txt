Title:
Scalloped Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can whole kernel corn
1 can creamed corn
1 cup crushed saltines
1 cup milk
2 eggs
salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 deg.  Drain whole kernel corn.  Butter casserole dish.  Lightly beat eggs then combine with other ingredients.  Pour into dish and bake for 45 min until set in center.
