Title:
Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5959569.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbl. Butter
1 c. chopped green bell pepper
1 c. chopped onion
½ tsp. ground cumin
2 (14 oz) cans chicken broth
1 (16 oz) jar Salsa
1 c. whole kernel corn
1 lb. ground beef cooked and drained - or -
1 lb. shredded cooked chicken - or -
1 lb. cooked white fish
1 Tbl. vegetable oil
6 corn tortillas cut into 1/2” strips
¾ c. 4-cheese Mexican blend
6 Tbl. sour cream

Directions:
Melt butter in medium saucepan over medium heat.  Add bell pepper, onion and cumin; cook for 3-4 minutes or until tender.  Stir in broth, salsa and corn and meat.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to low; cook for 5 minutes.
Heat vegetable oil in medium skillet over medium-high heat.  Add tortilla strips; cook, turning frequently with tongs, for 2-4 minutes or until light golden brown and crisp.  Remove from skillet; place on paper towels to drain.
Serve in soup bowl. Top with tortilla strips, cheese, and a dollop of sour cream.
