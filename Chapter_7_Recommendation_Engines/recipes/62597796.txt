Title:
Tater Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs hamburger
1 onion
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
2 bags of frozen mixed veggies
3 Tbsp. flour
3 Tbsp. butter
1 cup milk
1/2 lb. Velveeta cheese

Directions:
Fry hamburger and onion.
Cook veggies; drain and season to taste.
Mix burger, veggies and soups together and pour into 9x13 pan.
Melt butter in sauce pan.
Add flour and milk stirring occasionally until it begins to thicken.
Cut Velveeta into cubes and stir into white sauce until melted.
Cover meat with cheese sauce.
Cover cheese sauce with tater tots.
Bake at 350 until warm.

Submitter: 
Jessica Kathleen
Description: 
Beef, veggies, cheese & tater tots make this a delicious one-dish meal!
