Title:
Breakfast Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup oats
1/2 cup liquid egg whites
2 T unsweetened almond milk

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a bowl.
Cook in a microwave 1-2 minutes or until all the liquid is absorbed by the oats.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
Top with natural peanut butter, almond butter, or sugar-free syrup.
