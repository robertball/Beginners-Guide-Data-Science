Title:
Crock-pot Apple butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/488918.jpg
Ingredients:
Enough Apples to fill your crock pot (your fave)
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup white sugar
1 tsp cin.
1 tsp all-spice
1 tsp nutmeg
1 tbsp lemon juice

Directions:
core and slice apples, put all ingreidients in crock pot and let it go for 24 hours.  Vent lid slightly to let out extra steam. After 24 hours blend in blender, remove from blender and put in jars.  These make great hostess gifts.
