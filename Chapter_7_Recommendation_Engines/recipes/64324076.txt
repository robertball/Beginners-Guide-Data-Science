Title:
Smoky Cardamom Eggplant Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
olive oil
3 long chinese eggplant
3 portabella mushrooms
salt
coconut oil
1 large yellow onion
1-2 T ginger
5-8 garlic cloves
1.5 tsp plus more cardamom
1 T turmeric
1 tsp plus more coriander
1/2 tsp or more smoked paprika
1/4 tsp chili powder
dash cayenne powder
1 tsp curry powder or to taste
sprinkle of cumin to taste
black pepper
smoked sea salt
1 28 oz can diced tomatoes (or fresh; or fire roasted?)
1 can full fat coconut milk
1 can light coconut milk
cilantro for serving

Directions:
roast eggplant and mushrooms in olive oil/salt at 400 for 20-30 min, flipping once or twice
heat coconut oil, add onion, ginger, garlic to soften
add herbs
add roasted eggplant, mushrooms, tomatoes, coconut milk
add water as needed
simmer 30 min or more
serve with rice and cilantro
