Title:
Pudding Shots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk 2%
1/2 cup Irish cream liqueur (eg. Bailey's®)
1/2 cup vodka (eg. Smirnoff®) flavored
1 (4 serving size) package instant chocolate pudding mix
1 8 oz container Cool Whip- sugar free

Directions:
Whisk together the milk, Irish cream liqueur, vodka, and instant pudding mix in a bowl until combined. Continue whisking for 2 minutes. Add Cool Whip. Gently fold into the mixture,
Spoon the pudding mixture into shot glasses or disposable 'party shot' cups. Chill until set, about 30 minutes. The shots will have the consistency of mousse. Shots can be put in freezer.
Original recipe from Dani Hig
Feel free to experiment with different combinations of pudding mixtures and vodka flavor combinations. One of the best is vanilla vodka and white choc mousse pudding with Bailey's mint. Another is butterscotch pudding, vanilla vodka and Bailey's caramel. WOW. The kids will love it.    xoxoxo jj
