Title:
Boursin Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cloves garlic, minced
8 oz. unsalted whipped butter or margarine, at room temperature
16 oz fat free cream cheese, at room temperature
½ teaspoon each salt, basil, marjoram, dried chives
¼ teaspoon each thyme, pepper
1 teaspoon dried dill weed

Directions:
Place garlic, butter and cream cheese in a food processor and process until combined. Add herbs and process again. Store in the refrigerator and serve on crackers. Best if made the day before serving.

Submitter: 
Cindy in Pensacola
Description: 
Cream cheese spread for appetizers!
