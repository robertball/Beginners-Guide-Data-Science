Title:
Chicken Tinola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, sliced
1 clove garlic, crushed
2 tsp ginger root, strips
1 Tbsp oil
3 lb chicken, cut up & skin removed
2 Tbsp soy sauce
1/2 tsp salt
5 cups water
2 cups unripe papaya, cubed
1/2 lb fresh spinach

Directions:
In large saucepan saute onion, garlic and ginger in oil for 1 minute.  Add chicken and saute until chicken colors slightly.  Season with soy sauce and salt.
Add water.  Bring to a boil, lower heat and simmer for 30 minutes or until chicken is tender.  Add papaya and cook for 5 minutes or until papaya is tender.  Add spinach, cover and remove from heat.  Let stand for 5 minutes
