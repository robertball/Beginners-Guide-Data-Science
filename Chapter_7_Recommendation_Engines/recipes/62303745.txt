Title:
Fluffy Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup of cocoa powder
1/2 cup of sugar
1/3 cup of hot water.
4 cups of milk
1/2 cup of chocolate chips
1 cup of marshmallows.

Directions:
combine 1/4 cup of cocoa powder and 1/2 cup of sugar and 1/3 cup of hot water. Bring to a boil and boil for 2 min. Add 4 cups of milk, 1/2 cup of chocolate chips and 1 cup of marshmallows. Cook and stir over medium heat until marshmallows and chocolate is melted and incorporated well. DO NOT BOIL
