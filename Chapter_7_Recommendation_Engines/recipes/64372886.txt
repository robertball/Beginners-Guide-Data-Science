Title:
Big Ray's Peanut Butter Oatmeal Energy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2181409.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup oats
1/4 cup toasted shredded coconut
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup mini chocolate chips
1/4 cup honey
1 Tbsp chia seeds
1 Tbsp flax seed
1/2 cup ground flax seed
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.
Roll and compress the mixture into 1 inch rounds and place on parchment paper
Refrigerate a few minutes until firm and then transfer to an air tight container.
Refrigerate and enjoy for up to one week.
