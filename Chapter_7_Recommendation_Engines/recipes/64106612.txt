Title:
Mama Collie's Pink Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Boiling water
1 small package each lemon and raspberry gelatin
1 can evaporated milk
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 cup cottage cheese
1 small can crushed pineapple
1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Drain pineapple juice. Add enough water to make one cup. Bring to boil.
Dissolve gelatins into this boiling mix. Remove from heat and cool slightly.
Add evaporated milk, mayo and cottage cheese.
Place in fridge and monitor. When it starts to congeal, add pineapple and nuts.
Pour into mold and allow to fully congeal.

Submitter: 
aunt star
Description: 
Can be serviced as dessert, prettily topped with whipped cream and a cherry. Or, place it on a bed of lettuce as a side dish with cool luncheon salads.
