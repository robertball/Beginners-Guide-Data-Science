Title:
Epicure - Pueblo Black Bean and Salsa Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Can black beans
1 Can mixed beans
4 Cups chicken broth
1 can diced tomatos
1 can corn
1 Tbsp Epicure Poco Salsa
1 Tbsp Pueblo Bean Dip

Directions:
1 Rinse beans.
2. Mix all ingredients in a large soup pot
3. Boil, reduce heat and simmer for 20mins
