Title:
Brown Sugar Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup ketchup
1 T Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1/2 cup milk
1 eggs
2 cloves ground garlic
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 small onion, chopped
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
3/4 cup finely crushed saltine cracker crumbs or breadcrumbs
1 cup diced veggies (frozen veggies that have been thawed or boiled work well)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 5x9 inch loaf pan.
Mix brown sugar, ketchup and Worcestershire sauce - will be used as glaze.
In a mixing bowl, mix thoroughly all remaining ingredients and shape into a loaf. Place loaf in a casserole dish.
Pour glaze over meatloaf.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour or until juices are clear.

Submitter: 
Laurie Sueko Lau
Description: 
This tasty meatloaf flavored with ginger, has a sweet brown sugar and ketchup glaze.Modified from: Brown Sugar Meatloaf
