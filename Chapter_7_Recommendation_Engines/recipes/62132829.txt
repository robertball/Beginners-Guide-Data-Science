Title:
Rotini and Sausage Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz. box rotini pasta
1 pkg. kielbasa sausage (cut in chunks)
1/2 onion (thinly sliced)
1 tsp. olive oil
3 tbs. honey
1 1/2 tbs. soy sauce
3-4 garlic cloves (minced)

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions on package.
Drain and set aside.
While pasta is cooking, place a pan over medium heat and sautee' the onions in olive oil till tender and golden brown.
Add the minced garlic to the onions and cook for another 2 minutes.
Place the onion mixture in a small bowl and set aside.
In the same hot pan, cook the sausage until browned.
Add the onion mixture, honey and soy sauce to the sausage.
Cook for an additional 2 minutes.
Add the sausage mixture to the pasta.
Serve hot.
