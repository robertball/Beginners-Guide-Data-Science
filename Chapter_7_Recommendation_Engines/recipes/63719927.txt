Title:
Queso Fresco Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz Tomato Concassee'
Black pepper, to taste
3 oz Parmesan grated
8 oz puff pastry
Olive oil, as needed
2 oz salsa, hot
4 oz Queso fresco
4 oz Zucchini, shredded

Directions:
Season tomatoes with pepper and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese
Roll out pastry until 1/4" thick, then cut into 1" circles.
Brush muffin tin with EVOO and line with the puff pastry.
Add salsa
Add Queso fresco
Add enough shredded zucchini to nearly fill cup.
Top with Concassee' and remaining Parmesan.
Bake at 375 degree for 15 to 20 minutes.
