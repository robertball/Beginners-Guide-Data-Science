Title:
Swedish Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/925211.jpg
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1 1/2 to 2 cups milk
1/4 cup oil
1/8 tsp salt
3/4 tsp sugar
1 1/2-2 cups flour
1 roll of Jimmy Dean Sausage cooked crumbled
1-2 packages frozen strawberries thawed
Whipped Cream

Directions:
In a blender, put milk, eggs oil salt and sugar
Blend on low
gradually add flour and blend until batter reaches the consistency of a medium gravy.
Heat a flat pan until water spits when dripped on the pan
Pour a small amount of batter into the center of the pan and swirl to spread thinly.
When the top of pancake looks dry, carefully loosen and flip
10-20 seconds later flip again to check doneness.
flip onto a heated plate in the oven to keep warm.
Serve with sausage down the middle and then roll and top with strawberries and whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Susan Murdoch
Description: 
When my kids were small these were the pancakes I would make them on Christmas Morning as we always spent Christmas Eve with Dave's family and Christmas Day with the Scarbroughs.  It became a tradition.
