Title:
Roasted Chocolate Covered Pecan Clusters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter or margarine
6 cups pecan halves
24 oz. chocolate flavored almond bark

Directions:
Melt butter in a 15x10x1 inch jelly roll pan.  Spread pecans evenly in pan.  Bake at 300 degrees for 30 minutes, stirring every 10 minutes.  Melt the bark according to the directions for microwave on package.  Add pecans and stir until coated.  Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls (about 3 halves at a time) onto wax paper.  Let cool completely.  This makes a large batch and they keep well.

Submitter: 
Shults3
Description: 
Roasting the pecans brings out the true flavor of the nut and dipping them in chocolate is the icing on the cake!!  These are great at Christmas time or any time.
