Title:
Baked Crab Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup bread crumbs
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp dried parsley
1 tsp mustard powder
1/8 tsp black pepper
2 tsp Old Bay
1 tb mayo
2 tb butter, melted
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
1-2 eggs
1 lb. lump crab meat

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Combine bread crumbs, baking powder, parsley, mustard powder, pepper, and Old Bay.
In another bowl, stir together mayo, butter, Worcestershire, and egg until smooth.
Fold crab meat into mayo mixture.
Fold in bread crumb mixture until well blended.
Drop mixture from tablespoon onto greased (or parchment paper lined) baking sheet.
Bake in preheated oven 10 minutes.
Turn over and bake an additional 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
These crab cakes are baked and can even be frozen after baking for a quick meal when days are crazy and rushed.  Salmon can be subbed for the crab.
