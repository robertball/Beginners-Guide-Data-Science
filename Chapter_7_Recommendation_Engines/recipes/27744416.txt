Title:
Chicken Scaparella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices of bacon coarsely chopped
2 TBS. Olive oil
1 large chicken breast, split
1/2 cup quartered mushrooms\1 small garlic clove, minced
1 cup chicken broth
1 TBS. red wine vinegar
8 white small onions, peeled
4 small new potatoes, halved
1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
1 TBS. flour
2 TBS. chicken broth
chopped parsley

Directions:
In skillet, cook bacon.  Remove and set aside.  Add oil and chicken.  Brown well on all sides.  Add mushrooms and garlic.  Saute several minutes, stirring occasionally.  Add next 6 ingredients.  Cover and simmer 35 minutes until chicken and vegetables are tender.
To thicken sauce, dissolve flour in remaining 2 TBS. chicken broth.  Stir into sauce.  Cook, stirring, until thickened and smooth.  Garnish with parsley.
