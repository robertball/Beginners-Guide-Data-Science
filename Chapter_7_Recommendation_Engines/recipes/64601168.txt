Title:
Carrot Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3938838.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 cup onion, chopped
1 pound lean ground beef
1 Tbsp garlic, minced
2 cups of carrot, grated
1 cup of celery, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
2 cans of Cream of Celery soup
2 liter V8 juice
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup grated cheddar cheese (optional)

Directions:
In a large pot, saute the onion over medium heat until they just begin to soften.
Add the ground beef and cook until no longer pink and drain if there is a lot of fat.
Add the garlic, carrot, celery and peppers and stir into the beef continue to let it cook for a few minutes.
Add both cans of soup and the V8 juice.
Season with salt and pepper.
Bring to a boil then reduce heat so that the soup is just simmering.
Simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
Pour into bowls and sprinkle with grated cheese.

Submitter:

Description: 
Carrot Chowder? Yes, it is a thing and it's delicious.  This is a great soup to make on a cool evening or when you just don't want to make something that requires a lot of work. My mum found this recipe years ago and it's been a favorite ever since.
