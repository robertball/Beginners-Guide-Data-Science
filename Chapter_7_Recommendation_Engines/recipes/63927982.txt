Title:
Beer Braised Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3lb. Pork Roast
2 Tbsp Olive Oil
1 Large Yellow Onion, chopped
2 Large Carrots, chopped
1 large Parsnip, chopped
2 Bay Leaves
4 Whole Cloves
2 Garlic Cloves, smashed
12oz. Dark Ale Beer
Salt/pepper

Directions:
1. Salt/pepper the roast liberally. Heat oil & sear meat on all sides.
2. Remove meat & sauté onions, carrots, parsnip & garlic (in same pot) until tender - about 4 minutes.
3. Return the meat to the pot. Add beer, cloves & bay leaves. Roast in oven at 300, for 1 1/2 hours.
4. Remove meat & bay leaves. Remove meat from pot and slice. Remove bay leaves. Purée veggie/beer mix until smooth.
5. Return to pot with pork. Bring to a simmer and reduce until thickened.

Submitter: 
Samantha Jones
Description: 
I created this recipe when my guy shared with me how a lot of meat dishes, back home in the UK, are cooked with or soaked in beer, prior to the cooking process. And he loved this dish. I served this with steamed rice and sauteed broccoli.
