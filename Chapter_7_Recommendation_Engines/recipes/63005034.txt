Title:
Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/750080.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1/3 cup cocoa
2 TBS oil
1 tsp vanilla
1/3 cup butter
2/3 cup flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 cup nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
Beat together sugar, eggs, cocoa, oil, vanilla, and butter until creamy
Mix in dry ingredients and nuts
Pour into 8" greased square pan
Bake 25-30 minutes
