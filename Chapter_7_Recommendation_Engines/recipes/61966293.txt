Title:
Honey-Mustard Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb. chicken pieces
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup honey
1/4 cup Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. curry powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place chicken pieces in shallow baking pan.
Combine butter, honey, mustard, salt, garlic powder, and curry powder. Mix well, and pour over chicken.
Bake 1 1/4 hours, basting every 15 minutes, until chicken is tender and nicely browned.
