Title:
Slow Cooker Clam Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3(6 ounce) cans minced clams
1/2 C. bacon bits (real thing)
5 potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 large chopped onion
2 carrots, grated
2 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 (12 fluid ounce) cans evaporated milk

Directions:
In a small bowl, drain the clams and reserve the juice. Add water to the juice as needed to total 1 3/4 cups liquid. Cover the clams and put in refrigerator for later.
In a slow cooker combine the bacon, potatoes, onion, carrot, soup, pepper, and reserved clam juice with water. Cover and cook on low setting for 8 hours OR on high setting for 4 to 5 hours. Add the clams and evaporated milk and cook until heated through. Serve with garlic cheese bread.
