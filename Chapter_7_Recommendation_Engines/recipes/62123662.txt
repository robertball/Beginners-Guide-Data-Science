Title:
rum raisin ice cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup raisins
1/4 cup dark rum
1/4 gallon vanilla ice cream
1/8 teaspoon nutmeg

Directions:
Soak raisins in rum over night. Allow ice cream to soften and scoop into mixing bowl. Add rum, raisins, and nutmeg; mix. Rum will soften ice cream and make mixing easier. Place everything back into the ice cream carton and refreeze.
