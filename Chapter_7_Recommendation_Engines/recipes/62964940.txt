Title:
Terry's Basic Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground Round (not the lean stuff)
1 28oz can of diced tomatoes (I prefer organic fireroasted if possible)
1 12oz can Kidney beans, drained
1 12oz can Black beans, drained
3 PBR's, 1 for chili and 2 for drinking during prep.
1/4 cup tomatillo salsa
1 Onion, at least medium chopped
1 Green pepper choppped
1 Jalapeno or serrano (figure out what you like)
2 - 4 cloves garlic minced
2 Tbsp chili powder
1 Tbsp cumin
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp worchester or balsamic

Directions:
Brown meat and drain (or not)
saute onion, peppers and garlic
Dump it all in slowcooker and cook for 3 -4 hours on high

Submitter: 
Fooddude
Description: 
A good basic recipe in the slowcooker.  You can build many variations from this formula
