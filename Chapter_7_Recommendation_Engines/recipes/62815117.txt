Title:
Garlicy Grilled Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 C Kikkoman Teriyaki Marinade
4 cloves of garlic, crushed
Juice of 1/2 a lime
3/4 Lb. Salmon filet
1/2 C Orange Juice

Directions:
In a non-metallic loaf pan add garlic, Lime juice and Kikkoman. Add salmon flesh side down. Marinade 35 min. Add OJ marinade for another 25 minutes. Grill over indirect medium-high heat rotating 180 degress one time for 20 min or until fish flakes easily with a fork
