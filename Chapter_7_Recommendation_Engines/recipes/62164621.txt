Title:
Spinach Alfredo Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium red potatoes with skin
1/2 pint heavy whipping cream
1/2 pint light cream
1 8oz package of Italian 6 cheese blend
6 cloves fresh garlic
1/2 tspn dill
salt
pepper
2 tspn butter

Directions:
Slice the potatoes and put them in a large mixing bowl.
In medium sauce pan, add butter and put on low heat. Press or mince your garlic cloves and add to pan. let simmer on low for about 5 minutes. Pour the heavy and light cream into the pan and turn the heat up to medium. Once the cream starts to bubble, add the cheese and turn the heat back down. Stir consistently until the cheese is melted and the sauce starts to thicken.
Add the sauce to the potatoes and stir. Add the dill and salt and pepper to taste.
Pour the mixture in to a 9" round glass baking pan and bake at 400 for 60 minutes.
