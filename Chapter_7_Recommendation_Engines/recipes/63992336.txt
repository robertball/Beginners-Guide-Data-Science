Title:
Whole Wheat Oatmeal Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup whole wheat Flour
1/2 Cup quick cook oats
1/4 tsp salt
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp cinnamon
1 large egg
1 Cup milk (*almond,soy,rice is fine)
2 Tbsp brown sugar
1/4 Cup Greek yogurt
1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients in large bowl
Wisk wet ingredients in seperate bowl
Make well in dry ingredients and pour wet ingredients into well
Mix gently - do not over mix
Using soup ladel, ladel onto hot griddle

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
These are delicious and dense.  They taste like an oatmeal cookie!  Recipe came from Sallysbakingaddiction.com
