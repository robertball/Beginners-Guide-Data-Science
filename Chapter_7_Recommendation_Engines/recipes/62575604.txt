Title:
Cranberry Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One 750 ml bottle Smirnoff Cranberry vodka, chilled
Two 1 liter bottles of O Organics Pomegranate Acai Italian Soda (from Genuardi's)
6 cups cranberry juice, chilled
3 cups orange juice, chilled
Juice of 1 lemon
1 orange, thinly sliced
1/2-3/4 cup fresh raspberries

Directions:
In a punch bowl, combine the vodka, italian soda, cranberry juice, orange juice and lemon juice.
Float the orange slices and raspberries on top.

Submitter: 
Sarah
Description: 
I served this during hors d'oeuvres for Christmas. Everyone raved about it and went back for more!
