Title:
Easy Strawberry Shortcake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Angel food cake (with round center)
1 (16 oz) pkg. frozen strawberries, reserve 1/2 cup
1/4 cup sugar
1 (16 oz) container Cool Whip, reserve 1 cup

Directions:
Place cake on a pretty serving tray.
Cut the cake in half (save the top) and scoop out the middle circumference of the cake (save everything you have scooped out).
Put frozen strawberries in a bowl and add enough sugar to desired sweetness. Mash.
Put strawberry mixture into scooped out area (saving some to fill the center of the cake).
Put cake top back on.
Fold together the cake you scooped out and about 1 cup of Cool Whip and remaining strawberries. Fill the center with this mixture.
Frost cake with remaining Cool Whip.
