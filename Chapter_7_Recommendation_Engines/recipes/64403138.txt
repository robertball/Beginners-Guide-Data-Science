Title:
Easy Peasy Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1      Egg
1 cup  Sugar
1 cup  Peanut Butter, creamy of crunchy
1 tsp  Vanilla Extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease a large baking sheet or use parchment paper or a silicon mat.
In a bowl, combine the egg and vanilla, whisk until blended, add in the sugar then the peanut butter, mix well.  Roll the dough into balls and place on baking sheet.  With a fork dipped in sugar, press a crisscross design in to each cookie.  Bake for 12 mins, remove from oven, sprinkle cookies with some sugar. Cool slightly before removing from pan.
