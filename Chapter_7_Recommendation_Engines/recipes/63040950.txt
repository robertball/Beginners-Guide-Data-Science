Title:
Chocolate Chunk Skillet Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (18 oz) refrigerated chocolate chip cookie dough
½ cup semi-sweet chocolate chunks or morsels
½ cup walnut or pecan halves
2 bars (1.5-2 oz ea.) favorite chocolate candy
Snickers, chocolate covered caramels, or peanut butter cups are great choices

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Using our 12" Executive Skillet - Press cookie into bottom of skillet. Top with chocolate morsels. Using our Food Chopper - coarsely chops walnuts. Using our 5" Forged Cutlery Utility Knife - cut candy bars into ¼ inch-thick slices; arrange over dough. Bake 25-30 minutes or until edges are deep golden brown. Remove from oven. Cool 10 minutes. Eat warm and or with ice cream.
