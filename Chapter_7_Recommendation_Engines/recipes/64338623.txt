Title:
"Green Stuff"  (Pistatcho Pudding Salad) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Pistachio instant pudding mix
8oz. can crushed pineapple
8oz. container cool whip
5oz. miniature marshmallows

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the pineapple and dry pistachio pudding mix.
Mix in thawed cool whip.
Fold in marshmallows until well mixed.
Refrigerate until chilled and serve
