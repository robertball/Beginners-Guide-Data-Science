Title:
Simple Slow-cooker Chicken Barley Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/930033.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups homemade chicken broth
½ cup chopped onion
½ cup chopped celery
1 cup diced carrots
1 bay leaf
½ cup barley
1 ½ cup well shredded cooked chicken
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Put all the ingredients but the cooked chicken in a crock pot and cook on high for 4 hours.  Be sure to use a good chicken broth.   Add the shredded chicken the last half hour of cooking.   Remove the bay leaf before serving and salt & pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Deb C
Description: 
Very easily made with simple ingredients, this soup is perfect for those busy days when you want healthy comfort food.  Be sure to use a good quality, flavorful broth.  I use my homemade chicken broth that has already been seasoned.
