Title:
Grilled Turkey Reuben Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices swirl rye bread
2 Tbsp butter, divided
10 ounces of turkey lunch meat
4 slices of swiss cheese
1 cup drained sauerkraut
4-6 Tablespoons Thousand Island Dressing

Directions:
Preheat skillet over medium-low heat
Generously butter one side each slice of bread.
In a separate bowl, warm sauerkraut in the microwave for 30 seconds.
On a separate plate, warm turkey in microwave for 30 seconds.
For each sandwich, lay one piece of buttered bread butter-side down, spread 2 Tablespoons of Thousand Island dressing on the bread, and stack 1/2 cup sauerkraut, 2 slices of swiss cheese, 5 ounces of turkey. Top with additional Thousand Island dressing if desired, and place second piece of bread on top, butter side up.
Grill until lightly browned and flip over; continue grilling until cheese is melted and other side is lightly browned.

Submitter: 
The-Baby-Bow-Lady
Description: 
The restaurant I worked at in college made these amazing turkey reuben sandwiches. They are a hit with everyone, and surprisingly easy. Feel free to adjust the sauerkraut to taste, but don't skimp on the thousand island dressing!
