Title:
Salmon Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/964548.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Large Can Salmon
1/4 cup Cracker or Bread Crumbs
1 egg
1/4 tsp Old Bay Seasoning
Salt & Pepper to taste
Olive Oil
Lemon Dill AOLI
3 Tbsp Mayonaise
1/2 Tbsp Lemon Juice
1/2 Tsp Dill

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and form into patties.
Fry in Olive Oil
Serve with Aoli
