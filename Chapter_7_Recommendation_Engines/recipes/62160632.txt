Title:
Bacon Cheddar Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 & 1/2 lbs ground chuck
2 teaspoons onion powder
2 teaspoons salt
1 lb pkg hickory smoked bacon, cooked crispy
2 to 3 cups bottled barbeque sauce
1 cup sharp cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Cook ground chuck seasoned with onion powder and salt in large skillet until no longer pink,  then drain.
Meanwhile, cook bacon until crispy then chop.
Add cooked bacon and barbeque sauce to hamburger in skillet.
Simmer for 5-10 minutes until flavors combine.
Remove from heat and stir in cheese.
Top your favorite sandwich bun with meat mixture, add extra cheese as desired.

Submitter: 
DRFISH
Description: 
My 9 year old son invented this recipe when we couldn't grill his favorite burgers out on the grill because of rain.  He turned it into a delicious sloppy joe that we love to serve with hash brown casserole.  Definitely a kid pleaser!
