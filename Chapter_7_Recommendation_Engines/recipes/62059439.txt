Title:
Jalepeno Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 roll frozen cream corn (golden or white)
1 bag frozen niblet corn
4 - 6 tablespoons margarine or butter
1 8 oz. cream cheese
Salt & pepper to taste
Sliced jalepenos to taste

Directions:
In medium microwavable bowl, combine roll of cream corn, margarine, & cream cheese.
Microwave on high for 5 minutes.
Stir & add niblet corn and salt & pepper.
Microwave on high at 3 or 4 minute intervals, stirring in between until heated throughout.
Add sliced jalepenos (I also add a LITTLE of the juice) to your taste.  Stir and microwave for another 2-3 minutes.
Stir again & serve.

Submitter: 
BAYOU GAL
Description: 
This is one of those great recipes that is super easy and is always requested for special occasions. Creamed and niblet corn cooked in cream cheese & butter sauce with jalepenos added for that extra bite!  If you like spicy and different, this one's for you!
