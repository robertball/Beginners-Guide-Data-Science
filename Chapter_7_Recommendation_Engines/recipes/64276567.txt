Title:
Salsa Chicken Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups uncooked white rice
1 small can Ortega chili's
1/2 package chicken taco seasoning mix
1 garlic clove pressed
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 cup shredded Pepper Jack cheese
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 onion, chopped
1 1/2 cups mild salsa

Directions:
Place rice and water in a saucepan, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
Meanwhile, saut√© the cubed chicken breast with 2 tsps. of chicken taco seasoning and garlic on medium.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In a medium bowl, combine Monterey Jack and Cheddar cheeses. In a separate bowl, mix together cream of chicken soup, cream of mushroom soup, Ortega chili's, onion, and salsa. Layer 1/2 of the rice, 1/2 of the chicken, 1/2 of the soup and salsa mixture, and 1/2 of the cheese mixture in prepared dish. Repeat layers, ending with cheese.
Bake in preheated oven for about 40 minutes, or until bubbly.
