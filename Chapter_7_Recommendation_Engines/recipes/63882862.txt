Title:
Cheesey Broccoli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup margarine or butter
1/4 cup unsifted flour
3 cups water
2 10 oz packages of frozen chopped broccoli, thawed and drained
4 tbsp chicken broth
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
3 cups shredded cheddar cheese
2 cups cream

Directions:
In a large saucepan or Dutch oven, cook onion in margarine until tender; stir in flour.
Gradually stir in water, then broccoli chicken broth and Worcestershire sauce.
Cook over medium heat and stir until thickened and broccoli is tender - about 10 minutes.
add cheese and cream. Cook and stir until cheese melts and soup is hot - do -not- boil. Garnish as desired and refrigerate left-overs.

Description: 
This soup is a great dish for a cold day, or just as a healthy comfort food.
