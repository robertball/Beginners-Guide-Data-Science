Title:
Simple Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1/2 head of cabbage sliced thin
-6 carrots grated
-1/2 of a small red onion
-3Tbsp mayo
-3Tbsp apple cider vinegar
-2Tbsp white sugar
-1Tbsp grainy mustard
-1/4tsp cumin powder
-1/4tsp coriander powder
-1/4tsp of salt
-1/4tsp of pepper

Directions:
Add the cabbage, carrots and onion to a large bowl
In a separate smaller bowl, mix the remaining ingredients until smooth.
Mix all the ingredients together until everything is well incorporated.

Submitter: 
thebioteacher
Description: 
I make this slaw every time I make pulled pork sandwiches.  Just slice some bread, add pulled pork (see my Faux pulled pork recipe), add your favorite BBQ sauce, and top with this coleslaw. Hope you enjoy :).
