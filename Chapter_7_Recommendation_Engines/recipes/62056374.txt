Title:
Chicken Muffinettes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked rice (any kind)
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup diced, cooked chicken OR 1 can flaked chicken
3/4 cup sliced black olives
1 tbs minced onion flakes
1 tbs parsley flakes
1 tsp seasoning salt
2 eggs, beaten
2 tbs milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Combine rice, chicken, olives, onion flakes, parsley and seasoning salt. Stir in eggs and milk, mixing thoroughly.
Spray six muffin cups with cooking spray. Divide mixture evenly among cups. Bake at 375 for 15 minutes or until lightly browned. Loosen with spatula and serve.
Optional butter sauce: Combine 1/4 cup butter, 1 tbs lemon juice and 1/2 tsp seasoning salt. Spoon over muffins.

Submitter: 
MRSMTAYLOR
Description: 
This recipe is very versatile. Tuna can be substituted for the chicken, and and type of rice can be used. (I prefer wild rice.) A good recipe you can prepare ahead of time for a 'grab & go' lunch the next day.
