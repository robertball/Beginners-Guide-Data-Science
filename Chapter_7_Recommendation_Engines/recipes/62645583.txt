Title:
Red, White & Black Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bean mix:
1 can of black beans
1 can of butter beans
1 cup chopped red onion
1 1/2 cups of chopped red pepper
1 1/2 cups of cooked frozen corn
Dressing:
1/4 cup cider vinegar
3 tablespoons lime juice
2 cloves of garlic (medium size)
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup fresh cilantro

Directions:
Cook the corn.  Chop the onion and red pepper.  Drain and rinse the beans.  Mix these items together.
Add all the dressing ingredients to a blender, and run the blender until homogeneous.
Pour the dressing over the bean mix and blend the dressing in.  Refrigerate overnight.
Serve at room temperature.

Submitter: 
gtsdls1
Description: 
This is an adaptation of several bean recipes from different sources.  It is best after leaving overnight for the flavors to meld.  Serve at room temperature.
