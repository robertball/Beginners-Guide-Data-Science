Title:
Pheasant in Beer Batter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 oz of Pheasant,  ( plucked and Gutted)
Celery Root/Stalk
for the Batter:
5 oz Flour
8oz of Beer
2 Eggs
half a bunch of fresh chopped :   each;  Parsley, Dill, and Gren Onions
1 Tsp.of Gelatine
Salt & Pepper.

Directions:
Dice the Celery. Place the Pheasant and Celery into a Pot and fill with enough
water to cover. Salt.  Boil for appx. 40 minutes.  Remove the Bird; cool and cut into portion
size pieces. (Remove al Bones).  In the meantime prepare the Batter with the rest of the ingredients
Pour over the Cooled Bird, mix, and Chill for 30 min.  Bake, deep-Fry in Batches to a Golden Brown
Color.  Serve with favorite Sauce , side dish and Garnish.  ( Rice, Fries, Green Salad )
