Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 chicken breasts (about 2 pounds) cooked and shredded
2 10-oz. cans green enchilada sauce (Market Pantry brand)
1 16-oz. jar green salsa (Market Pantry brand)
1 4-oz.  can diced green chilies
1 jalapeño pepper, finely chopped
1 5-oz. tub whipped cream cheese
8 oz. shredded cheddar or Mexican blend cheese
10 medium-sized soft tortillas

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
Mix the enchilada sauce, salsa, chilies and jalapeño in a bowl.
Mix the cream cheese and 4 oz. of the shredded cheese together.
Add 1 cup of the green sauce to the cheese and mix well.
Add the chicken to the cheese and green sauce and mix well.
Spoon the mixture evenly onto the tortillas, roll up  and place in a 9x13 baking dish. Bake for 20 minutes, or until heated through.  Sprinkle the remaining shredded cheese over the enchiladas and bake an additional 5 minutes, or until the cheese is melted.
Heat the remaining green sauce and serve on top of cooked enchiladas.

Description: 
Green enchilada sauce, salsa, cheese and chicken combine to create an enchilada everyone will love.
