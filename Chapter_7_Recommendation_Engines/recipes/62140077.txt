Title:
Nona's Antipasto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound of cauliflower
3 cans pitted balck olives, drained
3 jars of stuffed green olvies, drained
1 small jar of sweet pickled onions, drained
5 green and red peppers, chopped
1 1/2 cup olive oil
3/4 cups cider vinegar
3 cans chunk tuna, drained
1 1/2 pounds of shrimp
2 cans sliced mushrooms, drained
3 L of ketchup

Directions:
In a large saucepan, pre-cook cauliflower for abour five minutes, drain.
In the same pot with the drained cauliflower, add olives, onions, ketchup, peppers, oil and vinegar.  Simmer gently for 10 minutes.
Add tuna, mushrooms and shrimp and simmer gently for 30 minutes.
Once done place into sterilzed jars and wait until they seal before place in the fridge.
Storage time is 1 month in the fridge.
