Title:
Poppy Seed Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CAKE BATTER:
6 Tbsp poppy seeds
1 cup buttermilk
1 cup butter, softened
1-1/2 cups sugar
4 eggs, separated
2-1/2 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
FILLING:
1/3 cup sugar
2 tsp cocoa
1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Soak poppy seeds in buttermilk for 2 hours.
In a large bowl, cream butter and 2-1/2 cups sugar until fluffy.  Add egg yolks and beat well.
In another bowl combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.  Add alternately with buttermilk/poppy seeds to butter mixture.
In another bowl beat egg whites until stiff.  Fold into batter and set aside.
Make filling by Combining 1/3 cup sugar, cocoa and cinnamon.  Sprinkle 1/3 of filling in bottom of greased and floured bundt pan.  Pour in half of batter, swirl in half of remaining filling.  Add remaining batter and filling, again swirling together.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 1 hour.  Turn out immediately onto wire rack.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
The cocoa cinnamon filling makes this bundt cake special.
