Title:
Mom's Scalloped Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can creamed corn
1 egg
1 c. cracker crumbs
1/4 c. chopped onion
salt & pepper

Directions:
Beat egg and combine with corn and onion.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Add cracker crumbs to reach desired consistency.  Bake at 350 for 20 min.
