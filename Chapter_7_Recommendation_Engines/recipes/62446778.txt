Title:
Baked Eggplant Like Mom's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Eggplant, halved and stewed until tender
1 onion, chopped
2 T. butter or olive oil
1 can mushroom soup
salt and pepper to taste
cracker crumbs or breadcrumbs

Directions:
Boil egglant in salted water until tender. Scoop out eggplant from shell. Save shell for stuffing or discard and bake eggplant in casserole dish.
Drain and chop eggplant
Saute onion in butter until tender
Mix in eggplant and mushroom soup.
Add salt and pepper
Add cracker crumbs to make stuffing consistency.
Put in sprayed baking dish or eggplant shell
Sprinkle with more crumbs and paprika
Bake 375 degrees for 30-35 min

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Mom used egg, cracker crumbs, milk, onion and butter. This recipe has mushroom soup added.
