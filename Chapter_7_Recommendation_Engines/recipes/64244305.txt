Title:
Turkey Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1757748.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded Cheddar and Monterrey cheese blend
1 onion, chopped
1 (2 ounce) can sliced black olives
1 (2 ounce) can diced green chili's (mild)
1 (16 ounce)can refried beans
1 cup of light sour cream
24 (6 inch) corn tortillas
1 (19 ounce) can red enchilada sauce
4 cups cooked turkey, shredded
3 cups water
1 tbsp butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Pour 3 cups of water in a pot, add the shredded turkey and unions, butter and taco seasoning. Simmer until the onions are clear.
In a small bowl, combine the cheese, onion, green chili's, sour cream, refried beans and black olives.
In a small skillet, heat enough oil to lightly coat one tortilla, and cook until soft. Remove and dip in enchilada sauce to coat using a small plate.
Add a spoon of turkey to center of tortilla, add a spoon of cheese mixture, roll and place in the prepared dish.  Repeat until pan is covered with enchiladas, can be 2 layers. Spread remaining sauce on top and sprinkle with cheese. Save the remaining broth.
Bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until cheese is melted.
