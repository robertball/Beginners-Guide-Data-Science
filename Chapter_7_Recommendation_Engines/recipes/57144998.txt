Title:
Pasta Fagioli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (28 ounce) can RED GOLD速 DICED TOMATOES or REDPACK速 DICED TOMATOES in JUICE
2 (15 ounce) cans great Northern beans
1 (14.5 ounce) can chopped spinach, drained
2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
1 (29 ounce) can RED GOLD速 TOMATO SAUCE or REDPACK速 TOMATO SAUCE
2 cups water
2 cloves garlic, minced
cooked itialian sausage
1 TBL. dried parsley
1 teaspoon garlic powder
Salt and black pepper to taste
1 tablespoon fresh chopped basil
8 ounces small seashell pasta
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a large stockpot, combine all above ingredients except pasta.  Bring to a boil; cover, and simmer for 40 minutes.
Add pasta and cook uncovered until pasta is tender, about 10 minutes.  Ladle soup into individual serving bowls and sprinkle with cheese.

Submitter: 
Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
Description: 
A traditional Italian soup. Serve with a crisp salad and a hot loaf of garlic bread and you have a meal!  Sprinkle top with grated Parmesan cheese.Modified from: Pasta Fagioli Soup
