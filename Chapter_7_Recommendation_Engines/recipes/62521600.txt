Title:
Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can diced tomatoes
1 can corn with juice
1 can beans of choice  drained
1 can green beans with juice
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 pkg taco seasoning mix
2 cups cooked chicken

Directions:
mix all ingredients in saucepan
cook until boiling hot
option: cream of taco soup: leave out chicken and blend all ingredients in blender prior to cooking, add chicken and bring to boil.
serve with corn chips, sour cream and shredded cheese
