Title:
Spicy baby potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 baby potatoes
3-4 tbsp olive oil
5-6 cloves garlic, cut in large pieces
2 tsp rosemary
1 tsp garlic salt
1 tbsp parsley
1 tbsp grated Romano cheese (optional)

Directions:
Put olive oil, garlic cloves, rosemary and garlic and garlic salt in heavy skillet.
Heat on low for 20 minutes.
Parboil potatoes for approximately 10 minutes. When done, remove and dry on paper towels.
Remove garlic cloves and increase heat to medium.
Add potatoes and saute about 10 minutes or until just browned, turning occasionally.
Dust with chopped parsley and grated Romano cheese.
