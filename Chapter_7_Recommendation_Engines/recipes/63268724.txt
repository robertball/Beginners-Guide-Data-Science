Title:
Easy Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
2 eggs
1/2 c. shortening
1 t. vanilla
2 c. flour
1/2 t. salt
1 t. baking soda
1 c. sour milk OR buttermilk
TOPPING:
1 c. chopped nuts
1 t. cinnamon
1/2 c. brown sugar
1/4 c. white sugar

Directions:
Beat the first 8 ingredients hard for 1 minute.
Pour half into a greased 9x13 pan. Cover with 1/2 topping mixture. Cover with remaining batter, then cover with remaining topping.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes.
