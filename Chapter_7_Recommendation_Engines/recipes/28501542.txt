Title:
Brunswick Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 boston butt
1 whole chicken
1 lb. ground beef
1 tbsp olive oil
1 16 oz. can tomato paste
3 cans butter beans
3 cans diced potatoes
3 cans whole kernel corn
3 cans cream corn
1/2 bottle ketchup
1/2 bottle hickory bbq sauce
1/2 bottle hot sauce
2 tsp celery salt
2 tsp onion powder
2 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Pressure cook and debone the boston butt and chicken.
Place olive oil in a large stock pot, and get hot.
Add ground beef, and brown.
Add celery salt, onion powder and black pepper.
The ingredients will need to be stirred often throughout the entire cooking process.
Bring to a simmer, and add all can ingredients, except cream corn.
Rinse each can with a little water and pour into pot.
Add ketchup, and bbq sauce.
Add about half of the bottle of hot sauce.
Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to simmer on low for 1 to 1 1/2 hours, stirring often to eliminate ingredients from sticking.
Add cream corn about 10 minutes prior to serving.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
A brunswick stew recipe handed down through many generations. This meat and vegetable stew is a true comfort food here in the south.
