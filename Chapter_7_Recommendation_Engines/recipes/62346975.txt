Title:
Mom’s Favorite Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/396293.jpg
Ingredients:
•	2 - 29 ounce cans of peach slices
•	1 ½ tablespoons cornstarch
•	1/3 cup brown sugar
•	½ teaspoon cinnamon
•	½ cup juice from canned peaches
•	¼ cup butter
Batter Topping
•	½ cup sifted flour
•	½ cup sugar
•	½ teaspoon baking powder
•	¼ teaspoon salt
•	2 tablespoons butter, softened
•	½ teaspoon vanilla
•	1 egg, slightly beaten

Directions:
Drain cans of peach slices and reserve ½ cup of juice. Put peach slices into a 9 x 12 baking dish.
Mix cornstarch, brown sugar, cinnamon with juice and put in microwave for 1 minute on high. Pour over peaches then dot with butter.
Combine Batter Topping ingredients and beat with a spoon until the batter is smooth.
Drop by spoonfuls onto the peach mixture. The topping spreads while baking.
Bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes or until lightly browned.

Submitter: 
distasi
Description: 
This recipe is a family favorite. What makes this cobbler a bit different than others is the batter topping. It is so easy and just delicious. You can top it with Vanilla Ice Cream or a non-dairy topping when serving.
