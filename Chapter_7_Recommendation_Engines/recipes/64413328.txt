Title:
Spinach Power Pesto Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2282794.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups macaroni
2 tbsp. butter
2 tbsp. prepared pesto
8 ounces or more of fresh and washed baby spinach
small onion minced
2 garlic cloves minced
2 tbsp. flour
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground pepper
2 cups milk
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar
1/2 cup shredded parmesan
1/2 cup shredded asiago
Topping:
1 tbsp. butter
2 tbsp. prepared pesto
1/4 cup shredded cheddar
1/4 cup shredded asiago
1/4 cup shredded parmesan
4 slices multigrain bread cut into small cubes including crusts

Directions:
Cook macaroni in slightly salted water according to package directions;add spinach the last minute of cooking and drain well.
Melt butter over medium heat, add pesto sauce and cook onion and garlic cloves until soft.  Stir in the flour, salt, and pepper, gradually whisk in milk and stir until somewhat thickened.  Add cheeses, stir and removed from heat. When cheeses have melted, mix with the macaroni/spinach mixture and put in a greased casserole dish.   Cook at 350 degrees for 25 minutes, covered.
Melt the butter for the topping in a small saucepan and add pesto, cheeses, bread and stir well.  Uncover the casserole dish, add the topping and cook at 450 degrees for 10 minutes.
