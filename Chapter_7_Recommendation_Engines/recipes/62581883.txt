Title:
Never Fail Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 c sugar
12 oz evaporated milk
1/4 c butter
12 oz bag good quality chocolate chips
7 oz jar marshmallow cream
2 c pecans
1 T vanilla

Directions:
Mix 1st 3 ingredients & stir constantly over med heat till it comes to a rolling boil. Start timing & cook exactly 6 min. Take off heat & don't beat. Add chocolate chips & marshmallow cream. Stir till both are melted & incorporated. Add nuts & vanilla. Pour into a buttered 9 by 13" pan. Cool & cut into squares.

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
This is the recipe my great grandmother used every year to make fudge& now I make it every year for my kids. It really is a no fail recipe as I've made mistakes through the years & it still turned out great!
