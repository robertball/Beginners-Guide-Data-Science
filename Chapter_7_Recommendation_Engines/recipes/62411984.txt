Title:
Irish Potato Casserole 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/525816.jpg
Ingredients:
8-10 Medium Potatoes
1 (8 oz) Cream Cheese
1 (8 oz) Sour Cream
1 Stick Butter
1/4C Chives
1/2t Salt
Paprika

Directions:
Peel and cut potatoes. Cook in boiling water until done. Drain. Pour in mixer bowl with all ingredients except paprika. Mash until creamy. Put in 2 Qt. casserole dish. Garnish with chives and paprika. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.

Description: 
A new twist for mashed potatoes. Potatoes with cream cheese, sour cream, chives, buttter, and paprika.. Delicious!!
