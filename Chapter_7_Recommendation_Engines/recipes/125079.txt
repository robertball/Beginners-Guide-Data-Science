Title:
Sweet and Spicy Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/284197.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon salt
dash cayenne pepper
6 cups plain popped popcorn

Directions:
Place sugar, chili powder, cinnamon, salt and cayenne pepper in a zip-type plastic bag or other 2-qt. airtight container. Mix. Add popcorn. Spray popcorn with nonstick cooking spray. Close bag and shake. Repeat one or two times until popcorn is coated.

Submitter: 
Flo  Burtnett
Description: 
'This crispy snack has a fun and different flavor,' shares Flo Burtnett of Gage, Oklahoma. 'I took some to my neighbor's one evening when we were visiting and it didn't last long!'
