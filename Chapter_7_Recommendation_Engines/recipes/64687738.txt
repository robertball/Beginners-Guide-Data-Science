Title:
Crab Shrimp Crawfish Spinach & artichoke Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup yellow onions
1/4 cup green onions
garlic to taste
2 10 oz pkg of spinach
1 pint sour cream
worstershire sauce -dash
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
Tabasco sauce to taste
7 oz chopeed artichoke hearts
1 bag shrimp
1 bag ceawfish
1 pkg crab meat
1 stick butter

Directions:
Saute 1 cup yellow onions with 1/4 cup green onions & garlic in 1 stick of butter
cook & drain chopped spinach
Mix onions & spinach & add 1 pint sour cream ,worchester sauce Parmesan, simmer 3 minutes
add tabasco, artichoke& simmer 5 more minutes
fold in fresh seafoodPut in casserole dish & top with buttered bread crumbs
Bake in oven at 350 degrees till warm & bubbly about 30 min
