Title:
Friday Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 medium carrots
2 stalks celery
2 large leeks
2 14 oz cans diced tomatoes
1 14 oz can pumpkin
2 cups water
2 t worcestershire sauce
seasoning to taste

Directions:
Finely chop carrots, celery and leeks in food processor or by hand.  Saute until leeks are softened, about 5 min.  Combine all ingredients and simmer 15 - 20 minutes.  Blend with an immersion blender if desired.
