Title:
Rosie's Avocado & Cheek Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1118400.jpg
Ingredients:
4 taco salad shells.
4 C. Romain lettuce
2 Avocados from Mexico
1 C. each corn, black beans, tomatoes
1/2 C. red onions, black olives, cilantro - chopped
2 lbs. Beef Cheeks - cubed.
6 garlic cloves.
3 bay leaf
3 tbls dry onions
2 tlbs. beef boullion
2 tbls chipotle powder
1 tbls Worcestershire sauce
1 tsps black pepper
2 bottle of dark beer
Garnish ::
Salsa
Sour cream

Directions:
Heat oven to 300 degrees
Sear off cheeks in a hot Dutch oven, with a bit of lard
Add the next 9 ingredients.
Pop in the oven for 3-4 hours or until the meat is fork tender
Check for seasoning, cool & build your salad
Top your taco salad with Delicious Avocados from Mexico & sour cream
