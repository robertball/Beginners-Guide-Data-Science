Title:
Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbls. Corn Starch
1/4 C. Brown Sugar
1/2 C. Cold Water
3 C. Peaches
1 Tbls. Butter
Topping:
1 C. Flour
1/2 C. Sugar
1 1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Salt
1/2 C. Milk
1/4 C. Butter, softened

Directions:
Mix first three ingredients, add fruit.  Cook until mixture thickens.  Add 1 Tbls. Butter
Topping:
Mix dry ingredients.  Add the butter and milk.  Beat it until it is smooth.  Spoon it over the fruit.  Sprinkle 2 Tbls. Sugar over the top and bake for 30 min. at 350.

Submitter: 
Kelly Norton
Description: 
This is my Mom's recipe that she would make when we camped.  It is wonderful camping or at home! Especially with a little vanilla ice cream or whip cream!
