Title:
Slow Cooker Adobo Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/795293.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small sweet onion, sliced
4 tsp garlic, minced
¾ cup low sodium soy sauce
½ cup vinegar
3 pounds chicken pieces
2 bay leaves

Directions:
Place chicken in a 4 to 6 quart slow cooker. In a small bowl mix the onion, garlic, soy sauce, vinegar and bay leaves then pour this over the chicken in the crock pot. Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours or on high for 4 to 5 hours.

Submitter: 
Michael St. Laurent
Description: 
An easy slow cooker recipe for something which is so very good.  A whole chicken cut into pieces works well as do thighs or breasts (bone in for best flavor).
