Title:
Butterflied cuban style pork chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c fresh orange juice
1/2 c fresh lime juice
1/3 c oregano leaves, coursely chopped
1 tsp cumin
1/4 c canola oil
4 pork chops butterflied and thinly pounded
salt and freshly ground pepper
8 slices of swiss cheese 1/4 inch thick
8 slices boiled ham slies, 1/4 inch thick
2 sour dill pickles thinly sliced
2 T chopped cilantro leaves
1/4 c olive oil

Directions:
heat the grill to high
whisk together 1/2 c orange juice, 14 c lime juice, 3T oregano, the garlic, cumin and canola oil in a large baking dish.  add the pork and turn to coat - cover and let marinade for atleast 15 minutes and up to 2 hours in the refrigerator
remove the pork from the marinade and pat dry - place the  chops on a flat serface, cut side up and season with salt and pepper - place 1 slice of cheese, 2 slices of ham, a few slices of pickles and another slice of cheese on 1 half of the chop - fold over, brush the top with oil and season with salt and pepper - repeat with remaining ingredients
place the chops on the grill, oil side down and grill until golden brown, 3 to 4 minutes - flip the chops over and continue grilling until the bottom is golden brown and the cheese has melted, 2 to 3 minutes longer
whisk the remaining orage and lime juices, oregano and cilantro with the olive oil and salt and pepper, to taste - spoon over the cops and serve
