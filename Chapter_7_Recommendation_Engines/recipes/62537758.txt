Title:
Lime Cottage Cheese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C boiling water
1 pkg (3oz) lime jello
2 C miniature marshmallows
1 pkg (3oz) cream cheese
pinch of salt
1 can (8oz) crushed pineapple
1 C small curd cottage cheese
1 C whip cream - whipped
1/2 C chopped nuts

Directions:
Dissolve gelatin in hot water
Add marshmallows and cream cheese cut in small pieces
Stir until melted (I use microwave very briefly)
Chill until slightly thickened
Put the cottage cheese in a sieve and stir with a spatula to remove the liquid
Stir together chilled first mixture with salt, pineapple and cottage cheese
Fold in whipped cream and nuts
Pour into pan or mold
Chill until firm
