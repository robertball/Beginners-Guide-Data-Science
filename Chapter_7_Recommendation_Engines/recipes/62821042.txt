Title:
(ellenc) Linda's Really Delicious Carrot Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs carrots, sliced
1/2 cup salad oil
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon vinegar
1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 (10.5 oz) can condensed tomato soup
1/4 cup white or sweet yellow onion, finely diced (optional)
1/2 teaspoon celery seeds
1/2 teaspoon sweet basil, chopped
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil carrots about 10-20 minutes, just until crisp tender. Shock with cold water, drain, transfer to a covered container and set aside.
In a separate bowl, combine remaining ingredients in order; mix well.
Pour mixture over carrots and toss gently. Cover and marinate overnight.

Submitter:

Description: 
A friend brought this delicious salad to the 4th of July party I went to. My modification is the addition of the chopped onions.
