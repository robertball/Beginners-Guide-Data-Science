Title:
Syllabub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c white wine
1 tbs honey
3 tsp sugar
1/8 tsp nutmeg
3/4 c milk

Directions:
Mix the wine, honey, sugar and nutmeg in a glass. Beat together with a spoon or a small whisk till the sweeteners are mostly dissovled.
Add the milk. Continue to beat swiftly until the sweeteners are completely dissolved, and the milk is somewhat thickened.
Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
GODGIFU
Description: 
An authentic 18th century drink. For a stronger version, use equal parts milk and wine to make 1 cup of liquid.
