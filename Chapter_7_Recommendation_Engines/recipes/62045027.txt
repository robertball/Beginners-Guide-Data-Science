Title:
Simple Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp butter
2 tbsp flour
1/2 tsp dry mustard powder
2 cups milk
1 1/2 cups grated cheddar cheese

Directions:
1. Grate the cheddar cheese and measure out the milk, set aside.
2. Melt the butter over medium heat in a saucepan over the stove, stirring constantly.
3. Add flour and dry mustard powder, stir to mix in.  Heat until bubbly.
4. Immediately stir in milk.  Continue to heat and stir over medium-high heat until mixture thickens and begins to bubble.
5.  Remove from heat, stir in cheese until melted.  Pour over hot cooked vegetables.
Note -- to make using microwave, melt butter in microwave on high for 10-15 seconds.  Add flour and mustard powder, mix well, heat on high for another 10-15 seconds.  Add milk and stir well.  Continue to heat on high for 2-5 minutes, stirring every 30 seconds, until mixture gets thick and bubbly.  Stir in cheese and serve.

Submitter: 
MONICA DEREGT
Description: 
A wonderfully easy, velvety cheesey sauce for vegetables. Can be made on the stovetop or in microwave in minutes, to add some instant flavour and class to your side dishes.
