Title:
The "Whiz-Wit" Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb thinly sliced/shaved rib-eye or top round steak, chopped
1 tbsp olive oil
1 15 oz jar Cheese Whiz
1 large onion, diced
8 oz Philadelphia Cream Cheese
2 oz jarred, jalapeno peppers, diced (optional)
2 oz jarred pimiento peppers or roasted red peppers, diced (optional)

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Toss in onions and saute until tender.
Remove onions place to the side.
Cut chip steak into pieces and place in the skillet. Cook until meat is no longer red, and all liquid is cooked away. Stir frequently, not allowing the meat to burn.
Add onions back to the skillet and mix thoroughly. (If you are adding peppers, add here)
Add cream cheese and mix until heated through and cream cheese is melted.
Add Cheeze Whiz sauce and mix thoroughly again until heated through.
Serve in crockpot with cut up hoagie/sub rolls or Italian bread.

Submitter: 
JJsMama
Description: 
If you live in Philadelphia, or have visited, you have probably gone to the famous Pat's or Geno's Steaks. When you order, you have to order a Whiz Wit or a Whiz Wit-out This means a cheesesteak with cheeze whiz and with or without fried onions. This is a cheesesteak in a dip. Perfect for tailgating or football games and is best served with cut up hoagie/sub rolls. Enjoy!
