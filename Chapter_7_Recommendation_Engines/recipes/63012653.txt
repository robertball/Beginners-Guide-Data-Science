Title:
Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
a dozen hard boiled eggs (cook fresh eggs, immediate put in box and refrigerate)
1 t prepared mustard
3 T Miracle Whip Lite
3 T diced sweet pickles
dash of salt
dash of Splenda
dash of paprika

Directions:
Boil and half eggs.
Crush yolks.
Add everything but paprika and mix.
Put mixture in egg halves.
Sprinkle paprika.
