Title:
Caramelized Green Beans and Sweet Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium Sweet Onion
3 Tbsp Olive Oil
12 oz Green Beans, trimmed and halved
16 â€“ 20 fresh Sage leaves
3/4 tsp Salt
2 Tbsp Orange Juice

Directions:
Heat oil in a wok over medium heat. Add the onions, green beans, sage leaves and salt. Stir and flip the vegetables until well coated with olive oil. Reduce heat to medium-low. Stir and cook vegetables until well browned and tender (20 to 24 minutes). Remove from heat, add orange juice and stir well. Add additional salt if necessary. Enjoy!!

Submitter: 
Alyse Ellienne
Description: 
This works great as a side dish or served on top of brown rice.
