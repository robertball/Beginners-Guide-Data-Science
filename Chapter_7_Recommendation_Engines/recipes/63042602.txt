Title:
Blueberry Yogurt Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups frozen blueberries (thawed)
1/2 cup sugar
1 package unflavored gelatin
2 cups vanilla yogurt
Oreo flavored pie crust

Directions:
Pour sugar over blueberries to make juice. You may have to mash the berries a little.
Pour off juice.
Mix gelitan with juice in a medium sauce pan and heat until gelitan is dissolved.
Mix yogurt with juice and gelatin, then add remaining blueberries.
Pour into crust.
Refrigerate for 2 hours.
