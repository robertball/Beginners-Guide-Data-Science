Title:
Famous House Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/792760.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup powdered sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 tbsp mustard
1/2 cup vegetable oil (Soy or canola is also ok)
1/3 cup vinegar
1/3 cup lemon juice
1/4 cup flour

Directions:
Combine ingrediants in a blender and pulse until well blended.
Best Served on a bed of iceberg/romaine lettuce topped with tomatoes, cucumbers, sliced hard boiled eggs topped with mozzerella chese, dressing and fresh ground pepper.
*Tip for perfect Hard boiled eggs. Add water to medium saucepan and turn on burner to Medium-High. Once boiling cover and turn-off burner. Let sit for 20 minutes. That's it, perfect every time. Thanks LB!

Submitter: 
ANGELSTAR
Description: 
This is exactly like the house dressing at that famous fondue restaurant, which is a combination Honey Mustard/Sweet N Sour except SO much better! (I like neither Honey Mustard or sweet and sour, but this I LOVE)
