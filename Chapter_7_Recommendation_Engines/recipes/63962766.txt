Title:
Grandma Mason's Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound margarine
6 ounces cream cheese
1 glass Kraft Old English cheese
1 glass Kraft American cheese
2 glasses Kraft Pimento cheese
4 ounces blue cheese
2 tablespoons cream
1 clove garlic
nuts, chopped

Directions:
Cream altogether until smooth.  Roll in nuts. Chill until firm.
