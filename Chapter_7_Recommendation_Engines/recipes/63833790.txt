Title:
Brussels Sprouts with Balsamic Vinegar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065472.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs Brussels Sprouts
1 TBLS Olive Oil
2 Cloves Garlic peeled and crushed
1/4 C Balsamic Vinegar
2 TBLS Butter
1 med Onion diced
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Trim Sprouts and remove limp leaves.
Place in Ice Water Bath for 1 hour or overnight.
Remove Sprouts from Ice Water bath and blanch in boiling water to cover for 7-10 mins and return to Ice Water bath.
Heat a large frying pan add Olive Oil and sauté onions and garlic until onions are just tender.  Add
Sprouts (Larger sprouts may be halved) and sauté until cooked to your liking. Add Butter and Balsamic Vinegar, Salt and Pepper to taste and toss together.

Submitter: 
Jack
Description: 
This delicious variation on the winter sprout will change some minds.  Those convinced that they do not like Brussels sprouts will enjoy these.
