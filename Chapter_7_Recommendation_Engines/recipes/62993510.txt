Title:
Roasted Garlic Butternut Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/745023.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 tsp dried minced garlic
1 tsp olive oil
1 medium butternut squash (3 lbs), peeled and cubed
1 medium sweet potato, peeled and cubed
1 large onion, chopped
2 TBS butter
3 1/4 cups water
1 can (14 1/2 oz) chicken broth
1 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp  pepper
9 TBS crumbled blue cheese

Directions:
Saute the squash, sweet potato, and onion in butter in large pot over high heat until crisp-tender (about 10 minutes).
In small pot, boil chicken broth, add paprika, pepper, salt, and garlic into pan - cook 7 minutes.
Add water and broth mixture to vegetables; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 25 minutes or until vegetables are tender. Cool slightly.
In a food processor, process soup in batches until smooth. Return all to pan and heat through. (Or use hand/stick blender in pot). Ladle into bowls; top with blue cheese.
