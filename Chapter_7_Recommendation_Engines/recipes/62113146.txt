Title:
Chicken and white bean soup with pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lg onion chopped
1 lb skinless, boneless chicken thighs or breast cut into bite size pieces
3-4 stalks celery, chopped
2 carrots, chopped
1 red bell pepper, seeded and chopped
1 small zuchini, chopped
3 big cloves of garlic, crushed
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1 19oz can white kidney beans, drained
3 - 5 cans chicken broth (enough to just cover beans & veggies)
a few drops Tabasco sauce
1/4 cup basil pesto
salt and pepper
Parmesan Cheese for garnish

Directions:
Heat a drizzle of oil in a large pot set over medium heat.
Cook the onion and chicken pieces for about 5 minutes, until the onions are starting to brown and the chicken is opaque.
Add the celery, carrots, zuchini and red pepper and cook for a few more minutes, until the vegetables begin to soften.  Add the garlic and cumin and cook for another minute.
Add the beans, chicken broth, Tabasco sauce and some salt and pepper and bring to a simmer. Turn the heat down to low, cover and simmer for about 45 minutes.
Stir in the pesto and serve, topped with Parmesan cheese.
