Title:
Hearty Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium potatoes (peeled and diced)
2 carrots (diced)
4 celery stalks (diced)
2 qts water
1 onion (chopped)
6 Tbsp butter or margarine
6 Tbsp flour
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups milk
1 cup Velveeta cheese (cut up)
2 cups ham (diced)
2 cans mushrooms

Directions:
In a large kettle, cook potatoes, carrots, and celery in water until tender, about 20 minutes.   Drain, reserving liquid and setting vegetables aside.   In the same kettle, saute' onion in butter until soft.  Stir in flour, salt, and pepper; gradually add milk, stirring constantly until thickened.  Put the cheese in stirring until it melts.   Gently stir in cooked vegetables, ham, and mushrooms.  Add 1 cup or more of the reserved cooking liquid until soup is desired consistency.  Enjoy!
