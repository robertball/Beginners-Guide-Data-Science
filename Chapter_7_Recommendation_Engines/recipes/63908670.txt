Title:
Meatballs in Tomato Sauce with Fresh Basil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground pork
1 lb lean ground beef or veal
1 lb lean ground chicken or turkey
1 egg
1 cup bread crumbs
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup Parmesan or Romano
2 tsp oregano
2 tbsp finely chopped fresh basil
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
Sauce:
2 tbsp olive oil
4 large garlic cloves
2-28oz cans crushed/diced tomato
1 large bay leaf
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
1/4 cup grated Parmesan/Romano
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450. Grease a large baking sheet or coat with cooking spray. Combine pork, beef, and chicken in a large bowl. Add egg, bread crumbs, water, Parmesan, oregano, basil, salt and pepper. Work mixture using your hands until thoroughly combined. Form mixture into small balls about 1" in diameter. Place slightly apart on baking sheet. Bake in two batches if necessary. Bake 15 minutes or until meatballs are slightly browned.
To make sauce, heat oil in a large pot. Add garlic. Saut√© 2 minutes or until fragrant. Add tomatoes, bay leaf, oregano, sugar and salt. Bring to a boil, stirring frequently. Add meatballs plus juices from baking tray. Reduce heat and simmer 1 to 1 1/2 hours or until sauce is thick. Remove bay leaf. Stir basil, Parmesan and black pepper into hot sauce. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
