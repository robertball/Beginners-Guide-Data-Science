Title:
Shrimp & Sausage with Savory Grit Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/962759.jpg
Ingredients:
Grit Casserole:
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 c. onion, finely chopped
1/2 jalapeno, finely chopped
1 fire roasted red pepper, finely chopped
1 c. milk
1 c. chicken stock
1 tsp. cajun seasoning
Dash black pepper
1/8 tsp. cayenne pepper
1/2 c. grits (not instant)
1/4 c. butter, melted
1 egg, beaten
3/4 c. Sargento shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 c. Sargento shredded parmesan cheese
Shrimp & Sausage Mixture:
1 Tbl. oil
2 andouille sausage links, sliced diagonally
1 Tbl. butter
1 cup colored peppers, thinly sliced (orange, red, yellow or green)
1/2 c. onion, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
Dash black pepper
1 Tbl. flour
1 can diced fire roasted tomatoes (14.5 oz)
1/2 c. white wine
1 tsp. cajun seasoning
1/2 lb. shrimp, peeled and deveined

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease a 1 quart casserole dish.  For grit casserole:  Combine garlic, onion, jalapeno, roasted red pepper, milk and chicken stock to boiling.  Slowly add grits, cover and reduce heat to low.  Add cajun seasoning, black pepper and cayenne pepper.  Simmer for 20 minutes until thick.  Add melted butter, beaten egg, 1/2 c. cheddar cheese and 1/2 c. parmesan cheese.  (Reserve 1/4 c. cheddar cheese for topping.)  Place in greased casserole and bake for 30 minutes.  Add 1/4 c. cheddar cheese to the top of casserole during the last 5 minutes of baking.
Shrimp & Sausage:  Sprinkle shrimp with cajun seasoning and set aside.  Heat oil in skillet.  Saute sausage slices and remove.  Add butter, colored peppers, onion, and garlic.  Saute until tender.  Sprinkle vegetables with flour.  Add tomatoes and wine.  Simmer  until mixture is thickened, approx. 10 minutes.  Return sausage slices and add shrimp.  Cook for 2-3 minutes or until shrimp is pink.  Serve with grit casserole.
