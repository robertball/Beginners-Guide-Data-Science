Title:
Mosley Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/8204163.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can croissant rolls
1 lb roll breakfast sausage, browned
8 large eggs
8oz package cream cheese, softened
2 cups shredded cheese
½ cup milk
½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300° F
Grease 9” x 13” pan and line the bottom with the croissant rolls
Mix together breakfast sausage, softened cream cheese & 1 cup shredded cheese
Spread over croissant rolls in bottom of pan (leave in fridge overnight if desired)
Whisk eggs, milk & pepper together and pour over sausage mixture
Top with remaining cup of shredded cheese
Bake 25-35 minutes or until golden brown.  Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Chris Mosley
Description: 
This casserole is a family favorite!  It has been a huge hit both at home and in the office.  The best part is that it can be partly prepped the night before which helps speed the whole process up.  Enjoy this delicious casserole that your family will have trouble putting down.
