Title:
Protien Breakfast Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/375637.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 cups rolled oats
4 Scoops of Chocolate Whey protien powder (use serving scoop provided in container)
1 slightly over ripe banana cut in large chunks
1.5 cups fat free cottage cheese
14 egg whites
1/3 cup natural peanut butter (chunky or smooth)
1/4 cup chopped or sliced almonds (optional)

Directions:
Place oats in blender and blend until it is the consistency of flour.
Empty oats into a large bowl
Add Whey Protien to oats and set aside
In blender, combine cottage cheese and banana, blend until smooth.
Add egg whites to blender and blend approximately one minute.
While blending egg whites to banana mixture place peanut butter in microwave for 30 seconds, or until it is half way melted.
Empty egg mixture and melted peanut butter into bowl with oat mixture. Mix well.
Coat a 13x9x2 baking pan with cooking spray.
Pour batter into greased baking pan. The batter will be slightly runny.
Top with almonds if desired.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until middle is set and starting to brown.
Remove to cooling rack and let cool completely before cutting.
Cut into 6 squares, then cut each square in half diagonally into triangles.

Description: 
I was trying to make a protien rich yet satisfying breakfast food that I could grab and go.  Its great in the toaster or right out of the fridge.
