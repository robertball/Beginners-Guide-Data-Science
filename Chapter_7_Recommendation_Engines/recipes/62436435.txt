Title:
Nana's Favorite Fruit Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/458189.jpg
Ingredients:
For the filling:
18 oz Blueberries
4 - 5 Peaches or Nectarines - peeled and sliced
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 T Corn Starch
1/4 C butter, cubed
For the Topping:
1 Cup flour
3/4 Cup sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp Baking Powder
1 egg
1/2 Cup oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Spray a deep dish pie pan with cooking spray.
Add all the fruit to the pie pan.
Mix together the brown sugar & corn starch and sprinkle over the top of the fruit.  Gently mix the fruit.  Add the little cubes of butter to the top of the fruit mixture.
In a mixing bowl combine the flour, sugar, salt, baking powder cinnamon.  Stir to combine.
Make a well in the flour and add the egg and oil.  Stir to combine.
Crumble the dough over the top of the fruit.
Bake at 350 for about 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
This is unlike most cobblers in that the crust is a little like a sugar cookie.  Any combination of fruit will work.  Use your imagination.  This is an absolutely delicious dessert.  Easy too!
