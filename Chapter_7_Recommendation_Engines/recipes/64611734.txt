Title:
Grandma's Creamed Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar (15 oz) whole onions (Aunt Nellie's)
1/4 c unsalted butter
1/4 c all-purpose flour
2 c half and half
2 Tbsp dry sherry
2 Tbsp grated parmesan cheese
1/4 tsp dry mustard
pinch nutmeg
salt and ground white pepper
1/4 tsp paprika

Directions:
Drain onions.  Melt butter in 2 quart saucepan over medium heat.  Stir in flour; cook and stir 1 min; until bubbly.  Stir in half and half and sherry; cook and stir 2 min or until sauce thickens.  Stir in cheese, mustard, nutmeg, salt and pepper.  Add onions; pour into  1 1/2 quart baking dish sprayed with nonstick cooking spray; sprinkle with paprika.  Bake at 350 degrees 15 to 20 min, uncovered, until bubbly.
Makes way too much sauce
to make one day ahead, cover and refrigerate.  Let stand 1 hour before baking, uncovered at 350 degrees for 25 min
