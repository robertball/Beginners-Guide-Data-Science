Title:
Grandma Endicott's Classic Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter
1 cup crunchy peanut butter
1 cup white sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 t vanilla
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
3/4 C chopped peanuts (if desired)

Directions:
Cream together butter, peanut butter and sugars. Beat in eggs.
In a separate bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and   salt. Stir into batter. Put batter in refrigerator for 1 hour.(I don't do this step)
Roll into 1 inch balls and put on baking sheets.  Flatten each ball with a fork, making   a criss-cross pattern.  Bake in a preheated 350 degrees F oven for about 10 minutes or until cookies begin to brown. Do not over-bake.

Submitter: 
Becky Cleveland
