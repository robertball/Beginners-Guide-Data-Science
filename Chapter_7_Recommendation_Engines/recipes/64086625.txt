Title:
Blueberry Almond Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup bisquick heart smart mix
1/2 cup steel cut rolled oats
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup toasted sliced almonds
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
1/2 cup cold butter
1/4 cup organic cane sugar
2 tbsp all-purpose flour
5 cups fresh or frozen blueberries
2 tbsp lemon juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Lightly spray an 11x7 inch baking dish with cooking spray.
In a large bowl, stir together Bisquick, oats, brown sugar, almonds, cinnamon, and nutmeg.  Cut in butter until mixture is crumbly and begins to stick together.
In a medium bowl, mix sugar and flour.  Add blueberries and lemon juice and toss to coat.  Spoon into baking dish and top with oat mixture.
Bake 50 minutes until filling is bubbly and topping is golden brown.  Serve warm.
