Title:
Jingle Bell Cookie Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. refrigerated chocolate chip or chocolate chunk cookies
1 ½ cups miniature marshmallows
½ cup red and green m&m’s
½ cup hot fudge topping, warmed

Directions:
Heat oven to 350F. Press out dough onto a greased 12-inch pizza pan.
Bake about 15 minutes or until light golden brown. Sprinkle marshmallows and candles evenly over crust and bake 2-3 minutes longer or until marshmallows are puffed.
Drizzle hot fudge topping over cookie pizza. Let cool and cut into wedges to serve.
