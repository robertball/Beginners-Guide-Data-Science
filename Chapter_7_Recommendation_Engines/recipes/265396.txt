Title:
Quick Vegan Breakfast Bowl with Fruit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5454502.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) container vegan strawberry yogurt
1 teaspoon spirulina powder
2 tablespoons cornflakes cereal
3 fresh strawberries, halved
1/2 large kiwifruit, peeled and sliced
1 tablespoon fresh blueberries
1 tablespoon chopped pecans

Directions:
Combine yogurt and spirulina powder in a bowl; mix well. Top with cornflakes, strawberries, kiwifruit, blueberries, and pecans.

Submitter: 
eloline
Description: 
A quick and easy vegan breakfast that is full of vitamins - I prefer to use berry-flavored soy yogurt, but any flavor will do. You can add any kind of fruit topping you like.
