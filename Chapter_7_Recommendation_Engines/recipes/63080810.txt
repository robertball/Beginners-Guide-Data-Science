Title:
Cut out Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine (softened)
1 cup sugar
1 egg
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp. baking powder
2 T. orange juice
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Combine butter, sugar and egg.  Beat until light and fluffy.
Stir in flour, baking powder, orange juice and vanilla until smooth and well combined.
Roll out dough 1/2 at a time on well floured surface. Cut out w/cookie cutters.
Bake 6-10 min. or until light golden brown.
Cool, frost and decorate
