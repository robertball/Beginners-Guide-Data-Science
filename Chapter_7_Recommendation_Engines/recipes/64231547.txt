Title:
Thai Spring Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup finely shredded Napa cabbage
3/4 c. shredded chicken meat
3/4 c. cooked baby shrimp
1/4 cup shredded carrots
1/4 c. chopped cucumber
1/2 c. fresh basil, chopped
1/4 c. fresh mint, chopped
20 rice wrappers (6.5 inch diameter)
1 cup peanut sauce for dipping

Directions:
Cut up the chicken and vegetables. In a medium bowl, mix the cabbage, chicken meat, shrimp,basil, and carrots.
Fill a small bowl with hot water. Dip rice wrappers quickly, one at a time, into the water, then remove and drain. As wrappers are removed from the water, fill each with 2 tablespoons of the cabbage mixture, and a few basil leaves.
Roll the wrappers and chill covered until with a damp towel until serving.
