Title:
Loco Moco II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 lbs ground beef
1 egg
1/4 cup brown gravy
1 1/2 cups white sticky rice; cooked
1 dash tobasco sauce; optional
1/4 teaspoon black pepper; optional

Directions:
Form the ground beef into a patty. Cook on grill (or frying pan) until medium-to-well done. And Set aside.
Fry the egg in the grease from the hamburger patty. Cook the egg sunny-side up or over easy. And set aside.
Assemle the plate by placing the patty on top of a heap of rice. Then top with fried egg. And pour gravy on top of the that.

Submitter: 
The Big E
Description: 
This is a great breakfast. And although there are many variations, this is the traditional loco moco.
It should be served with tobasco sauce, black pepper, ketchup, and soy sauce. Or use any combination of these toppings according to your preference.
To eat it properly, one should break the egg. And blend all the different flavors together in each bite.
