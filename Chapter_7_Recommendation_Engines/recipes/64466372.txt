Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2441243.jpg
Ingredients:
1. 3 cups Fresh popcorn
2. 1 package Chocolate chips
3. 1 cup Peanut butter chocolate bites
4. 1 bag Mini marshmallows (reserve a cup for toppings)
5. Kale Syrup (optional, but highly recommended)

Directions:
Combine popcorn, peanut butter bites and marshmallows in a bowl.  Melt half the chocolate chips and mix into the mixture.  Fold in the remaining chips and top with reserved marshmallows.  Drizzle with optional kale syrup.

Submitter: 
Wendy Allen Dettmer
Description: 
Even Dinomite needs to have a tasty snack on the go.  This is his favorite go to recipe to satisfy his sweet tooth!
