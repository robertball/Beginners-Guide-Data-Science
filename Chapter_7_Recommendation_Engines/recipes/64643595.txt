Title:
PEANUT BUTTER CORNFLAKE COOKIES (NO BAKE) Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients1 cup granulated sugar1 cup light corn syrup1 cup creamy peanut butter1 teaspoon vanilla extract6 cups corn flakesI

Directions:
In a large saucepan, combine the sugar, corn syrup, and peanut butter. Cook over medium high heat until center starts to boil, make sure to stir constantly so the sugar doesn't burn.As soon as the sugar mixture comes to a boil, remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract and corn flakes, mix until corn flakes are evenly coated in sugar mixture.Use an ice cream scoop to drop cookies onto wax paper as quickly as possible before mixture starts to cool and harden.Let cool on wax paper for 20 to 30 minutes before enjoying.

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
This recipe is made with just 5 ingredients: peanut butter, corn flakes, sugar, corn syrup, and vanilla. Theyre made right on the stove top and are ready in minutes, and I promise the whole family wont be able to get enough of them! Peanut Butter Cornflake Cookies are an easy and sweet treat with no baking required! Everyone will love these chewy, sweet, and salty cookies that are ready in no time at all!
