Title:
Grandma Ora Heath's Applesauce Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 cup applesauce
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup shortening
1/4 teaspoon cloves
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup raisins
1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
In a small bowl, mix together flour, baking soda and salt. Set aside.
In a mixing bowl, cream together shortening and sugar. Beat in egg and add cloves and cinnamon. Slowly mix in flour mixture and then the applesauce. Fold in raisins and walnuts.
Spoon tablespoon sized portions of cookie dough onto lightly greased baking sheet. Bake for 12-14 minutes.

Description: 
This recipe has been passed down in my family for several generations. They are wonderful soft and moist cookies!
