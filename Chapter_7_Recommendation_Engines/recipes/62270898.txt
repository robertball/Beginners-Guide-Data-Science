Title:
Chicken Confetti Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless skinless chicken breast cubed
1 tb olive oil
1 lb spagetti noodles
16 oz package of frozen broccili and cauliflour
1 10.5 can of cream of chicken soup
1 10.5 can of cream of mushroom soup
1 5 oz container of shredded parmeson cheese
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet.
Add chicken and season to taste.
Cook until chicken is no longer pink in the middle.
While chicken is cooking put a large pot of  salted water on to boil.
When boiling add spaghetti and cook for five minutes.
Add veggie mix to pot and cook for an additional five minutes.
Drain spaghetti and veggie mix.
Return to hot pot.
Add chicken, soups and cheese and mix well.
Season to taste.

Submitter: 
Todi
Description: 
Chicken, pasta and veggies come together in this meal to create a one pot meal complete with protien, starch and veggies! Serve with a salad and crusty bread for an easy and delicious weeknight meal.
