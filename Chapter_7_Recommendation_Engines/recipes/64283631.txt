Title:
Chinese Beef Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (14 ounce) can bean sprouts, undrained
1 (8 ounce) can sliced water chestnuts, drained
1 cup uncooked long grain rice
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 cups frozen peas, thawed
1 (5 ounce) can chow mein noodles

Directions:
In large skillet, cook beef, onion and celery until meat is no longer pink, then drain.
Stir in soup, bean sprouts, soy sauce and pepper. Bring to boil.
Add rice and water chestnuts and transfer to 3 quart baking dish.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
Uncover; stir in peas and sprinkle with noodles. Continue baking for 15-20 minutes more.
