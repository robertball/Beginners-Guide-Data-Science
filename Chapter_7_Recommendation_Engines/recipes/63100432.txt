Title:
Clay pot Turkey breast with fresh herbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 or 3 pound bonless turkey breast. fresh herbs 1 full sprig of thyme 2 sprigs of fresh Sage 3 whole garlic cloves,2 or 3 sprigs of parsley chopped 1/8th teaspoon ground black pepper 1/8 teaspoon salt 1 to 2 pinches of italian seasoning. Dump water out of clay pot before putting in your turkey in clay pot

Directions:
while working on the Turkey breast clay pot needs to soak with water.
Butterfly turkey breast, Spices will go between the butterfy breast spinkle with salt  pepper lay your herbs in between and fresh minced garlic and chopped parsely. spinkle with itlian seasoning now close your butterflied turkey and all herbs are in between. My sugestion is leave the skin on till ready to serve, it will keep the moisture in while baking on hot oven @ 400 degrees for 1 1/2 hrs turkey is ready to go into clay pot that has been soaked with cold water. clay pot must be  put in cold oven.
