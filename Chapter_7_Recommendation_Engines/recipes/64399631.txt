Title:
Karen's Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans good quality tuna, drained
2 ribs celery, chopped
1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
3 tablespoons mayonnaise
1/2 teaspoon lemon pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried dill

Directions:
Mix all ingredients until blended well.
Add more or take away some of the mayo, depending on your taste.

Submitter: 
Karen Hibbert
Description: 
We love this tuna salad. It is easy, light, and full of flavor. The fresh parsley is a must!
