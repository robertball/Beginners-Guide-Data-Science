Title:
Turkey Piccata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 turkey breast tenderloin (13-15oz.)
2 T all-purpose flour
1/4 t salt
1/4 t pepper
2 t EVOO
1C chicken broth
2 t finely shredded lemon peel
2 T lemon juice
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 t dry mustard
1/4 C sliced green onions

Directions:
Split turkey breast tenderloin in half horizontally; cut each half crosswise to make 4 portions. Place one of the turkey portions between two pieces of plastic wrap. Using the flat side of a meat mallet, lightly pound turkey to about 3/8-in. thickness. Remove plastic wrap. Repeat with remaining turkey portions
In a shallow dish, combine flour, 1/8 t. salt and pepper. Coat each turkey portion with flour mixture, turning to coat evenly. Shake of excess flour mixture.
In a large nonstick skillet, heat oil over med-high heat. Add turkey portions; cook about 6 min. or until browned, turning once to brown evenly. Add broth, 2 t. lemon peel, lemon juice, garlic, dry mustard and remaining 1/8 t. salt to skillet. Bring to boiling; reduce heat. Simmer, uncovered, about 10 min. or until turkey is no longer pink and cooking liquid is slightly reduced. Add 1/4 C green onions. Cook for 2 min. more. Serve with some of the cooking liquid.
