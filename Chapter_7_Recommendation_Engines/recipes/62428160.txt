Title:
Grammie's Rainbow Peanut Butter Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 small pkg butterscotch chips
3/4 cup peanut butter
1 pkg miniature marshmallows (use colored ones only!)

Directions:
Put butter, peanut butter and chipits in pot. Melt over low heat stirring constantly. Once all melted, remove from heat and let sit for 5 minutes. Stir in marshmallows (make sure they don't melt). Pour mixture into 9x9 pan. Put in refrigerator for 2 hours and then serve. (Best if cut into small squares)
