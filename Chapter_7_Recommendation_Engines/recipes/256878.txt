Title:
BLT Unwrap & Roll® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4462955.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces BelGioioso Unwrap & Roll® fresh mozzarella sheet
3/4 cup spring green lettuce
1 tomato, thinly sliced
4 slices cooked bacon

Directions:
Unroll BelGioioso Unwrap & Roll(R). Layer lettuce, tomato and bacon onto sheet. Roll and slice into pinwheels for a hearty and delicious appetizer.

Submitter: 
BelGioioso Cheese
Description: 
Tomato slices, spring greens, and cooked bacon are layered on a sheet of fresh mozzarella. Roll it up and slice it, and you've got BLT pinwheels!
