Title:
Salade Lyonnaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Frisee, Escarole, Romanie, Radicchio or other sturdy, bitter greens. Washed, torn and dried.
Eggs
Bacon or Lardons
Shallot
Vinegar
Dijon Mustard
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Fry the bacon
Add the shallot
cook unitl shallot is soft
Stir in vinegar and dijon
Turn off heat and set aside
Poach eggs
Dress greens with bacon/shallot mixutre.
Plate the greens and top each with a poached egg.
Dressing and egg should be warm. Egg yolk should be runny.
Option: homemade croutons
