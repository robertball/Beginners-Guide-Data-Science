Title:
Butternut Squash Caprese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium butternut squash
1 cup cherry tomatoes (or small heirloom tomatoes)
1 ripe avocado
1 cup smoked mozzarella
1/2 cup basil
Dressing
1 1/2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar.
1/2 tablespoon Dijon mustard.
1/2 garlic clove, minced.
1/4 cup olive oil.
Salt and freshly ground pepper.

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven on roast to 425*
2.  Peel and cube squash into 1 in cubes, line a baking sheet with foil.  Toss squash with 2 tsp olive oil, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Roast for 25-30 minutes at 425*.
3. Halve tomatoes
4. Cube avocado
5. Cube cheese
6. Chop basil
7. Mix dressing ingredients.Toss squash, tomatoes, avocado, mozzarella, and with dressing.
