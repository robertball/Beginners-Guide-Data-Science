Title:
Cheese Garlic Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1076678.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c bisquick
2/3 c milk
1/2 c shredded cheddar cheese
2 t butter
1/8 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Heat oven to 450. Stir bisquick, milk & cheese until soft dough forms.
Drop dough by 9 spoonfuls onto a ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 8-10 mins. or until golden brown.
Stir together butter & garlic in a bowl. Brush over warm biscuits.
