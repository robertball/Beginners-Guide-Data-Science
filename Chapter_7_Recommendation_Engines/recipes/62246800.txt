Title:
Spicy Baked Picnic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup buttermilk (optional lowfat)
1 package taco seasoning mix - divided
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
1 cup bread crumbs
1/2 tsp onion powder
1 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/4 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt (optional)
1/4 tsp pepper
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees.
Spray broiler pan with non stick cooking spray.
Combine buttermilk, tsp of taco seasoning, and 1/8 tsp red cayenne pepper.  Blend well.
In another bowl, combine remaining ingredients and mix well.
Dip chicken pieces in buttermilk mixture, coat with bread crumb mixture and place on broiler pan.
Bake at 375 until chicken is firm to touch.  Approximately 30-35 minutes.
