Title:
Big Ray's Honey Garlic Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3608308.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/4 cup honey
3 Tbsp spicy yellow mustard
1 Tbsp lemon juice
2 zucchini
2 yellow squash
2 Tbsp olive oil
2 red bell pepper, diced
3-4 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
salt and pepper, to taste
1 1/2 pounds shrimp, peeled, deveined, tails removed
Chopped parsley, for garnish

Directions:
Combine broth, honey, Spicy Yellow Mustard, and lemon juice. Set aside.
Cut zucchini and squash into thin strips using a mandolin or spiralizer.
Diced red bell pepper.
Heat olive oil over medium high heat. Add red bell pepper and cook until edges are slightly blackened. Add garlic and red pepper flakes and stir until fragrant and combined, about 30 seconds.
Add shrimp. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for 1 minute.
Add honey mustard mixture, zucchini, and squash. Cook for 2-3 minutes, until veggies have cooked down and shrimp is opaque. Top with parsley and serve.
