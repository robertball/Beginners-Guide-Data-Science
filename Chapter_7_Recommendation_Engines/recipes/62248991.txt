Title:
Shrimp and Crab Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
2 cups uncooked white rice
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 pound crab meat
1/2 cup minced celery
2 TABL. butter
2 (12 ounce) packages frozen shrimp, thawed
2 cups mayonnaise
2 cups milk
1 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
1/8 tsp red pepper flakes crushed
1/2 cup sliced almonds
1 cup shredded monterray jack cheese
10 buttery round crackers, crushed
1/8 cup parmesean cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 3 quart casserole dish.
In a saucepan, bring water to a boil. Add rice and stir. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
Combine butter, onion, celery, sautee in pan until tender, add 2 cloves minced garlic cooked just until you can smeill it dont brown it, set aside. Combine crab meat, shrimp, cooked rice, mayonnaise, milk, hot sauce, red pepper flakes, almonds & jack cheese. Mix well, add onion mixture. Place in casserole dish.
Cover top of casserole with crumbled crackers & parmesean cheese mixture. Bake, uncovered, in preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes, or until casserole is bubbling.

Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
