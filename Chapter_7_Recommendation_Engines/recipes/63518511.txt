Title:
Pretzel Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese softened
1/2 c. granulated sugar
1 crushed pineapple (20 oz can), drained
1 Cool whip (16 oz)
1 c. crushed pretzels
1/2 c. granulated sugar
1/2 c. melted butter

Directions:
Beat cream cheese with sugar smooth. Add pineapple & fold in Cool whip. Mix pretzels, sugar & melted butter and bake at 400 degrees for 7-8 minutes. stir immediately after taking out of oven to break up into pieces. Cool. Add to Cool whip mixture before serving.
