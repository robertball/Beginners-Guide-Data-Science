Title:
Christmas Morning Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/342501.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound bulk pork sausage with sage, cooked and crumbled
1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
2 (9 inch) unbaked pastry shells
6 eggs, lightly beaten
1 cup milk
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/3 cup chopped sweet red pepper
1/3 cup chopped green pepper

Directions:
In a bowl, combine sausage and cheese. Place half of mixture in each pastry shell. Combine eggs, milk, onion and peppers. Pour half over sausage in each shell. Bake at 350 degrees F for 55-60 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean. Let stand 5 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
Sally Harlan
Description: 
I first made this festive breakfast pie when entertaining friends on Christmas morning. Each year I was asked to make my famous 'Christmas Morning Pie' and the name stuck! But because of its great flavor, I make it year-round.
