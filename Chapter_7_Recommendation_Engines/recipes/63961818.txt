Title:
Chocolate Strawberry Sapodilla Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1099407.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ripe sapodilla
6 frozen strawberries
1.5 handfuls of Ice
1/4 cup vanilla almond milk
1 cup chocolate almond milk

Directions:
Cut open the sapodilla, scoop out the pulp, and place it into the blender. Add 6 strawberries, ice, and almond milks into the blender. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
claytical
Description: 
Sapodilla tastes kind of like caramel flan to me. The strawberries and chocolate almond milk turn this smoothie into a delicious drink with various textures.
