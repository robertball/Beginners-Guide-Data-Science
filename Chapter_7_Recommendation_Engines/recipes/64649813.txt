Title:
Broccoli Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T butter
3/4 c onion
6 c water
6 chicken bouillon cubes
8 oz thin noodles
1 t salt
20 oz broccoli
1/4 t garlic powder
6 c milk
1 lb cubed velveta cheese

Directions:
1.  Saute onions in butter in a large pot until tender.
2.  Add water and bouillon cubes to the pot with the onion.  Dissolve cubes.
3.  Add salt and noodles.  Simmer until noodles are done.
4.  Add broccoli.  Add milk and cheese.  Cook until cheese melts.

Submitter: 
Renee Heston
Description: 
If you live in Northeast Ohio, this recipe will remind you of The Barn restaurant's famous soup.
