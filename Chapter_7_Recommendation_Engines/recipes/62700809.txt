Title:
Asian Tuna Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cans tuna, drained
2 eggs beaten
1 sleeve wheat salted crackers
4 green onions diced
1 tablespoon Asian blend seasoning
1 tsp curry powder
1 Tablespoon Seseme Oil
2-4 Tablespoons Soy sauce to taste

Directions:
Crush the entire sleeve of crackers & split the crumbs in two. Combine the drained tuna, half the sleeve of crackers, eggs, onions, oil, Soy & seasonings together in a large bowl. Heat up a non stick skillet over med-high heat & add a few tablespoons of oil. Divide the tuna mixture into 8 balls & flatten each ball in your hand & dredge in the remaining cracker crumbs. Place in the skillet & cook about 3 minutes on each side until golden brown. Take out & place on paper towels to drain.

Submitter:

Description: 
My kids love tuna patties & I love changing the flavors up in some of my most basic recipes. We have been experimenting with Asian flavors in our cooking & this simple recipe is the result of some of that.
