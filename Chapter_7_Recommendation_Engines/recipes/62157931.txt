Title:
Chlodnik - Cold Polish Beet Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint buttermilk
1/2 cup sour cream
juice from 1/3 lemon
1 hard boiled egg, chopped
3 medium beets with leaves and stems
1 small cucumber, grated
1/3 cup fresh dill, chopped
3 small green onions, sliced very thin
1 cup chicken stock
1/2 tsp ground pepper

Directions:
Scrub the beetroots really well, and wash the leaves.
Chop the beets into 1/4 inch cubes and slices the leaves and stems thinly.
Boil the beets in a soup pot with enough water to barely cover them. Cook until soft, about 15 minutes. Take off heat.
Add the chicken stock and allow the beats to cool.
Meanwhile, prepare the egg, cucumber, dill and green onions. Mix together in a bowl and set aside.
In a large bowl whisk the buttermilk. Add the sour cream and continue whisking until smooth.
When beets are cool, stir in vegetables and buttermilk and pepper.
Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving. Good prepared a day ahead.

Submitter: 
becky joslin
Description: 
Cold Polish beet soup is a refreshing and healthy soup that can be easily prepared the day before.
