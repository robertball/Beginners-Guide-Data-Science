Title:
Texas Caviar III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 oz. can sweet corn
15 oz. can black eyed peas
15 oz. can black beans
1/2 c. chopped onion
1 c. chopped celery
4 oz. can chopped green chilies
1/2 c. olive oil
3/4 c. sugar (I use Splenda)
1/2 c. apple cider vinegar

Directions:
Rinse and drain the corn, black eyed peas and black beans. Pour mixture into medium bowl. Add onion, celery and chilies. In separate bowl combine oil, sugar and vinegar. Pour over bean mixture and stir well.
Refrigerate and marinate overnight or 4-6 hours before serving.
Drain excess liquid before serving.

Submitter: 
Anne Z.
Description: 
This is a light, sweet and tangy mixture that is great as an appetizer with tortilla or corn chips but is also really good as a side salad at a cookout.
