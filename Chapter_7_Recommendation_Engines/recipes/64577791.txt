Title:
Blueberry Cabbage Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3625256.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups red cabbage
1/2 cup blueberries
2 cups cucumber
1 medium apple

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a juicer. Pour juice into a glass, add a handful of ice cubes and enjoy!
If you do not have a juicer, place all ingredients in a blender. Blend until smooth, then place a mesh strainer over a bowl and pour blended juice over the strainer. Discard the pulp and pour juice into a glass.

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
This Blueberry Cabbage Juice is a refreshing, luscious and sweet drink laced with hints of apple and blueberry flavors and zero taste of cabbage. A warm weather drink that will quench your thirst and make your taste buds sing!
