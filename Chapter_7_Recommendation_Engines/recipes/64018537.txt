Title:
Chicken Divan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4  chicken breasts
2 teaspoons olive oil
1 cup water
2 heads of broccoli, cut up
1 can mushroom soup
1 1/2 cup Minute Rice
Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Cut chicken into bite-size pieces. Sprinkle salt and pepper over the chicken breast pieces and brown in oil turning occasionally. Meanwhile mix soup and water in a bowl and add the broccoli pieces. Stir this mixture into the cooked chicken and bring to a boil. Simmer until broccoli is tender crisp. Stir in rice and cover. Move from heat and let stand five minutes. Serve on the bed of fresh spinach and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
