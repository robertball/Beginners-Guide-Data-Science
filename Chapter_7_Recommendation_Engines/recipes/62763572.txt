Title:
Cancun Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 oz. Tequila (I use Jose Cuervo)
.5 oz. Coffee Liqueur (I use Trader Vic's)

Directions:
Pour Tequila and coffee liqueur in a shaker with ice.
Shake until cold and strain into a martini glass.
Garnish with a maraschino cherry.

Submitter: 
HYGEIA
Description: 
A mix of tequila and coffee liqueur that produces a unbelievable, not too sweet, exotic taste. You will have to give this a try, even if you aren't fond of tequila!
