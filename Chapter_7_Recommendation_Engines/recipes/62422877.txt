Title:
Jan's Stuffed Fried Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/445956.jpg
Ingredients:
8 - 3 to 4 inch banana or fryer peppers
1 - 6 1/2 ounce package of stuffing mix.
1 cup boiling water.
1/4 cup canola oil.

Directions:
Wash peppers and remove tops.   Clean out seeds and pulp.
Mix dry stuffing mix with 1 cup boiling water and let sit for 10 minutes.
Fluff stuffing with fork.
Place small amounts of stuffing into each pepper trying to fill as well as you can.  Fill until about 1/2 inch remains unfilled.
Place filled peppers into frying pan with the oil.
Brown peppers over low to medium heat so they do not burn.  Turn occasionally so all sides brown evenly.
Drain on Paper towels.
Serve hot or cold.  You may wish to remove outer skin before eating.
Store in airtight container.
