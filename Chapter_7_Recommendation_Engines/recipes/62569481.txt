Title:
Leftie Overs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Large Potatoes
1 Tbsp Olive Oil
1 Tbsp Melted Butter
1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
1 Tbsp Dried Rosemary, crushed
Salt to taste
Leftover, Cooked Meat (Roast Pork, Beef, Chicken, Turkey), diced

Directions:
Preheat oven at 425F
Wash, peel and dry potatoes. Cut into 2" cubes and place in a large bowl. Add olive oil, butter, garlic, rosemary and salt. Mix well.
Place a sturdy baking sheet, with sides, in the oven for 5 minutes. Quickly dump potatoes onto hot tray and give it a shake to ensure a single layer.
Roast potatoes for 25 minutes, without disturbing. Turn poatoes with a spatula. Continue to roast until lightly browned, about 15 minutes. Add diced meat and toss with spatula. Roast 10 more minutes.
