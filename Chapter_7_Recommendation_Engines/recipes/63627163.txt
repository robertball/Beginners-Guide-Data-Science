Title:
Barbecue Maple Chipotle Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
3/4 cup maple syrup
2 tbsp chipotle powder
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1/4 cup ketchup
1/8 cup soy sauce
1tsp oregano
1/4 cup sesame oil
3 cloves minced garlic
1 dash each of paprika, cayenne pepper and celery salt

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a marinating container and let chicken marinate 1 hour.
Heat barbecue on medium
Place marinated chicken breasts on center of grill and flip over after 10 - 15 minutes.
Spread remaining marinade with a basting brush every few minutes to properly glaze.
Cook for another 10-15 minutes until a slice in the middle of the chicken leaks only clear juices.

Submitter: 
Amelia
Description: 
This is a great weeknight recipe as it's quick to make.  The chicken can be left to marinate over night or can be cooked shortly after mixing.  A favorite in our house!
