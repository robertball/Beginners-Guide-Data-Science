Title:
Pasta with chicken, brocolli and cannellini beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1/2 of your favorite pasta
-2 garlic cloves
-1/2 of one sweet onion
-2 tbs extra virgin olive oil
-a little chicken stock or water
-1 boneless, skinless chicken breat cut into edible pieces
-1 head of brocolli,  cut into edible flourettes
-1 can of cannellini beans
-1 can cream of brocolli soup (I prefer the Campbell's 95% fat free)
-1 T dijon mustard
-A couple tablespoons of parmesean cheese

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large skillet on high.  Add garlic and onion and cook until the onion is tender.
Add the chicken and a little chicken stock or water, stirring occaisionally until the chicken is no longer pink.
- Add brocolli and cook, stirring occaisionally, until cooked to your liking.
-Stir in the cream of brocolli soup and mustard to make the sauce. Add more chicken stock to create the right consistency.
-Add the beans and cook until just heated through.
-Season with salt and pepper to taste.
-Toss in your favorite cooked pasta and serve with generous amounts of parmesean cheese.

Submitter: 
D. Morello
Description: 
Cream of brocolli soup  makes this pasta delicously creamy, while keeping it very healthy! Easy to throw together for a hearty meal.
