Title:
Cunchy Crab Sandwich Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2182260.jpg
Ingredients:
1 120 g can crab meat (or equivalent if fresh crab meat is used)
325 ml chopped cabbage
60 ml diced onion
45 ml Life Smart Chipoltle-Lime dressing (to taste as this is quite zesty)
45 ml low-fat mayo

Directions:
combine crab meat cabbage and onion, stir to mix thoroughly, then add the dressing and mayo stirring until blended

Submitter: 
Tony Carriere
Description: 
A very Delicious and easy to make a crunchy sandwich filling which can be just as easily served as a salad on a plate with lettuce and fresh vegetables
