Title:
Thin Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4c all purpose flour
1t salt
1t baking soda
pinch baking powder
1 egg
2oz milk
1 1/2t vanilla
2 sticks of butter at room temp
1c sugar
1/2c brown sugar
2c chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Sift flour, salt, and soda.
Combine wet ingredients and bring to room temp.
Cream butter, add sugars and wet mix.
Add dry mix and chips.
Bake 13-15 minutes at 375.
