Title:
Tequila Slow cooked beef enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/880613.jpg
Ingredients:
beef
3 pound beef roast
3 cups tequila
3 seeded and diced fresh jalapenos
6 cloves garlic smashed and diced
filling
3 cups uncooked rice
2 cans whole black beans
1 can refried black beans
2 large can diced green chilies
1 large bag shreaded mexican style cheese
sauce
4 small cans hot enchilada sauce
2 large can mild enchilada sauce
2 can tomato paste
12-24 12 inch flour tortillas

Directions:
In a slowcooker put roast, tequila, jalapenos, and garlic. cook for 12-18 hrs
Shread the beef in the juices and let it cool. remove the meat but keep the juices.
Use the left over juice from the meat to cook the rice. Set aside and  let cool.
Mix the whole and refried black beans with one can diced green chilies.
Mix all of the encilada sauce and tomato pastes together in a large container until there are no tomato paste lumps.
On the bottom of a deep baking dish spread a thin layer of sauce.
Lay out a tortilla layer 1/4 cup bean mixture, 1/4 cup rice, 1/2 cup shreaded beef, 1 table spoon sauce, and 1/8 cup chese on top and roll up. Place fold side down. Continue until pan is full cover with a thick layer of sauce and completely cover the top with cheese. spread last can of chilies over the top of the cheese.
pre-heat oven to 350F bake 45min - 1hr or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
