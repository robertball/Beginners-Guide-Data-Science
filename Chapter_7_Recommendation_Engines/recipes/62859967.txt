Title:
Mmmm Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole grain wrap
1/2 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, sliced
2 tablespoons shredded cabbage
1 dill pickle, chopped
mmmm sauce

Directions:
Add salt and pepper to black beans to taste.
Layer beans, cabbage, tomato, pickle and mmmm sauce inside tortilla.
Wrap and eat
