Title:
Tropical Island Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984256.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (11.5 fl oz) can Guava KERNS® from LIBBY'S® All Nectar
4 cups water
2 cups PERRIER® Sparkling Water
3/4 cup Lemon Flavor NESTEA® Sweetened Iced Tea Concentrate
1/4 cup honey
6 slices pineapple or kiwifruit (optional)

Directions:
FOR NECTAR CUBES: POUR nectar into one ice cube tray. Freeze until cubes are set.
FOR TEA MIXTURE: PLACE water, sparkling water, Nestea and honey in large pitcher. Stir until combined. Serve over nectar cubes. Garnish with pineapple or kiwifruit slices.

Submitter: 
Nestea®
Description: 
This tropical beverage will make you feel like booking a trip to an island paradise.
