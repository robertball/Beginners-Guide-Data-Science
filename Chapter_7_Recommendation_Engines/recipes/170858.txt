Title:
Monte Cristo Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986713.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups torn salad greens
2 (14.5 ounce) cans RED GOLD® PETITE DICED TOMATOES or REDPACK® PETITE DICED TOMATOES, drained
1 cup croutons
1 (9 ounce) package shaved smoked turkey breast, cut into strips
4 slices Swiss cheese, cut into strips
2 hard-cooked eggs, quartered
1/4 cup honey Dijon salad dressing

Directions:
Combine greens, RED GOLD(R) or REDPACK(R) PETITE DICED TOMATOES and croutons in a large serving bowl.  Add remaining ingredients and toss lightly.

Submitter: 
Red Gold®, Inc.
Description: 
Enjoy the luscious flavors of a Monte Cristo Sandwich in this wonderful, low fat, salad.
