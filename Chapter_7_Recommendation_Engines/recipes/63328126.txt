Title:
Chocolate "Lunchbox" Snack Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/882237.jpg
Ingredients:
1 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup plain cocoa
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup water
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 tsp. of vinegar
1/2 tsp. of vanilla
1/2 cup of mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees
Sift dry ingredients, the flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt into a medium bowl. Add brown sugar and mix well.
Spray an 8x8x2 pan with pan spray and wipe out excess  with a paper towel.
Stir remaining ingredients into bowl except chocolate chips.
Pour into the prepared pan and spread slightly, if needed, to make smooth.
Gently sprinkle chocolate chips evenly over cake.
Bake 35 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
Cool before cutting into 9 even pieces.

Submitter: 
Holiday Baker
Description: 
A quick to make cake, that needs no frosting and works great for lunches.
