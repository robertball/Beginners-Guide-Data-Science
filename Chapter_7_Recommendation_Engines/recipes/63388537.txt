Title:
Basic Chocolate Ganache Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup heavy cream
8 oz semi sweet or bittersweet chocolate

Directions:
Coarsely chop chocolate and place in medium bowl.
In a small saucepan, bring the heavy cream to a boil. Immediately, pour over chocolate and let sit for five minutes.
Whisk chocolate until thoroughly melted and use immediately(for glaze), or chill and whip for frosting or filling.Keep chilled.

Submitter: 
Cocoanut
Description: 
Use as an icing, or chill and whip it up for a creamy frosting.
