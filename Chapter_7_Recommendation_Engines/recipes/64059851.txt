Title:
Noodles & Co Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Spinach leaves
crumbled bacon
Pecans, chopped
Croutons
red onion, sliced
Feta Cheese
Apples, chopped
Balsamic vinegar dressing:
2/3 cup sugar
1/3 cup balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Salad: Mix first five ingredients together. Add cheese and apples on top. Serve with dressing
Dressing: Mix sugar and vinegar together. Let stand 1/2 hour, stir and serve.
