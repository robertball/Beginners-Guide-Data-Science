Title:
Apple Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 medium pork chops
2 cups BBQ sauce
1/2 cup clover honey
1 cup apple sauce (no cinnamon)
1 tbs ketchup
1 tsp lemon juice
2 tbs onion powder
1 tbs pepper
1/2 tbs salt

Directions:
Mix all wet ingredients in large mixing bowl
Mix all dry ingredients thouroughly.
Combine all ingredients (except chops).
FOR BAKING:
Preheat oven to 350.
Line baking dish with tinfoil and spray with cooking spray for easy clean-up.
Brush both sides of chops with sauce and place in dish.
Cover the chops with the remainder of sauce.
Cover dish and bake until chops are completely done, an hour or two. DON'T OVER COOK!
FOR BROILING:
Pre-heat broiler.
Line pan with tinfoil and spray with cooking spray.
Brush chops on both sides generously with sauce.
Place chops on pan and broil until completly done, 10 min on each side. BE SURE TO CHECK FOR DONENESS!
FOR GRILLING:
Prepair your grill (However you do it).
Grease your rack so the chops won't stick.
Brush chops generously on both sides with sauce.
Grill until done. If you want, you can keep brushing chops with sauce until desired sauc-y-ness. :]
NOTE: For a bolder taste, you CAN marinade chops in sauce. If you do though, besure to make sure it gets cooked to reduce risk of food poisoning.

Submitter: 
Miss Blurton
Description: 
This unique and tasty dish compliments apple desserts very well. It can also be cooked three ways: Grilled, baked, or broiled. Please enjoy! :]
