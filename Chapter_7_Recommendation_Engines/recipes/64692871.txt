Title:
Curry English Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. English Muffins
1 cup mayo
1/4 cup parm
1.5 cup shredded cheddar
1/3 cup green onions
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1/3 cup bacon bits
1/4 tsp. dry mustard
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp. curry

Directions:
Mix ingredients and broil on english muffins
