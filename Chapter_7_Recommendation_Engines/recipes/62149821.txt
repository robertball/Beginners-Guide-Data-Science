Title:
Tomato & Feta Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c long grain rice, like basmati
1.5c water
1/2 white onion, finely chopped
1/2 tsp minced garlic
1/2 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp oregano
1 tsp basil
1/2 tsp salt
2 roma tomatoes, roughly chopped
1 red pepper, finely chopped
1/4c frozen corn
1/2c crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
Place the rice & the water in a pot. Over high heat bring the rice to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover and do not remove the lid for 20 minutes. After 20 minutes, stir the rice to see if it is still moist. If so, add another few minutes to the cook time.
Over medium-high heat, spray a pan with cooking spray & add the onion and garlic. Saute until the onion in transparent, about 3 minutes. Add the thyme, oregano & basil to the onions. Cook for 2 more minutes, until all the dried herbs have softened. Add the pepper, tomatoes, corn and cook for 2 more minutes.
Add the rice & salt to the vegetable mixture. Stir until well blended.  Mix in feta cheese and serve.
