Title:
Pressure cooker beef stock Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3112846.jpg
Ingredients:
1-2 lb beef bones
4 large carrots cut in to 4" pieces
4 celery stalks cut in to 4" pieces
1 large sweet onion cut in half and un-peeled
4 cloves garlic peeled and cut in half
1 TBSP salt
1 tsp peppercorns
1 tsp dried thyme seasoning
2 bay leaves
1 bunch fresh flat parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Place beef bones on large baking sheet and place in oven, save room for onion halves.  Bake for 15 minutes.
Add onion halves to baking sheet, bake for 45 more minutes.
Add beef bones, onion, and all other ingredients to pressure cooker.  Top with water up to 6 quarts
Cook in pressure cooker for 1 hour.  ***If you do not have a pressure cooker you may boil for 6 to 8 hours covered for similar results.***
