Title:
Shredded Apple Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup white sugar
1/4 cup butter, melted
3 tbsps flour
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1 large egg
3 large apples (I prefer Granny Smith or Empire)
18 3 inch tart shells

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
In a large bowl whisk together sugars, butter, flour, vanilla, spices and egg, set aside.
Peel and core apples, cut apples into 1/4's, with a food processor or cheese grater, grate the apples, squeeze the apples to remove some of the liquid.
Fold grated apples into spice mixture.
Prebake empty tart shells for 1 minute.
Fill tart shells with apple mixture.
Bake for 12 minutes, turn pan and bake for another 12 minutes.
Let cool for 10 minutes.
