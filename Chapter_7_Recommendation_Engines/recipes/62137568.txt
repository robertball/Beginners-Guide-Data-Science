Title:
Ginger Soy Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 to 2 large garlic cloves, crushed
1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
1 cup beef broth
1/4 cup sugar
2 tsp powdered ginger
1/2 cup dry sherry
1 1/2 Tbsp cornstarch

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan.
Put over medium heat and bring to a boil.
Simmer for 10 minutes.
Serve hot, in individual ramekins.

Description: 
Excellent dipping sauce for fondue - shrimp or beef
