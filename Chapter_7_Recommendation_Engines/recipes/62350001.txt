Title:
Anasazi Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/397119.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups Anasazi Beans
6 Cups Water
1 Onion
2 Jalapenos
3 garlic cloves
Tortillas
Cheese
Green Enchilada Sauce
Red Onion
Green Pepper
Fresh Tomatoes
Cilantro

Directions:
Cook beans, onion, garlic and hot peppers in an electric pressure cooker on high pressure for 60 minutes.  Quick release the pressure and drain the beans.
Use an immersion blender or food processer to puree the beans.
Spoon 1/2 Cup of beans into tortillas and roll.
Place in an 9X13 pan and pour Green enchilada sauce.
Bake for 20 minutes
Top with Cheese, red onion, green pepper, and fresh tomatoes and cilantro.

Submitter: 
Heidi Heninger Shaw
Description: 
The Anasazi beans were actually found in the ancient ruins of the Four Corners area of the United States.  Scientist and foodies were delighted when they grew into beautiful dark red and white beans.
