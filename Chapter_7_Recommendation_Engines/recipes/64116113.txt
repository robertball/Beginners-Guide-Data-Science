Title:
kalenutsco Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 whole kale leaves washed
2 cups coconut water
1/2 cup of sweetened coconut
1 cup of walnuts

Directions:
1. add all ingredients to a blender
2. blend
3. refrigerate for no more then 1 hour
4. pour and enjoy

Submitter: 
Edward Fine
Description: 
I found this recipe in my house and made it, I liked for its sweet taste.
