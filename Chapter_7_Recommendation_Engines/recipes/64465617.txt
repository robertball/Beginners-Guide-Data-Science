Title:
Egg Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 eggs
2 cups ice
2 - 4 Tbsp Miracle Whip
1/4 - 1/2 tsp onion powder
salt and pepper
crackers or sandwich buns

Directions:
Place eggs in a large saucepan and cover with cold water to one inch above eggs. Cover. Bring to a boil over high heat.
Remove from heat and let sit for 10 minutes.
Meanwhile, fill a large bowl / container with water and 2 cups ice.
Transfer eggs with slotted spoon to the ice bath. Let sit for 10 minutes.
Peel and slice eggs into a large bowl. Add onion powder, salt and pepper and stir with fork.
Stir in 2 Tbsp Miracle Whip. Add additional Miracle Whip until desired creaminess.
Serve with crackers or on sandwich buns.
