Title:
Martha's Vietnamese Kebabs with Sugarcane Skewers, Dipping Sauce and Lettuce Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dipping Sauce
1/4 cup Asian fish sauce (such as nam pla)
1/4 cup water
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice (from 2 limes)
2 teaspoons
1 teaspoon rice-wine vinegar (not seasoned)
2 garlic cloves, crushed
1 teaspoon finely chopped Thai chile
Kebabs
Vegetable cooking-oil spray
1-1/2 pounds Martha's Ground-Meat Mix
3 tablespoons finely chopped fresh lemongrass, pale-green parts only
3 tablespoons chopped fresh Thai or regular basil
3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1 teaspoon finely chopped Thai chile
12 skewers (about 5 inches each) fresh sugarcane
1 head Boston lettuce
Fresh mint, Thai or regular basil, and cilantro for garnish

Directions:
Make dipping sauce: Mix together all ingredients in a bowl. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
Preheat oven to 450-degrees with rack in top third of oven. Make kebabs: goat a bakinf sheet with cooking spray. Gently mix together meat, lemongrass, basil, cilantro, and chile. Form meat into 12 narrow loaves (about 1/4 cup each) shaping each around a surgar cane skewer, leaving 1 inch of skewer exposed. Transfer to baking sheet.
Bake for 11 minutes. Heat broiler. Broil until deep golden brown, about 3 minutes. Discard garlic from sauce. Place kebabs in lettuce leaves. Garnish with herbs. Serve with dipping sauce.
