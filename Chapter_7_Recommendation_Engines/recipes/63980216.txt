Title:
Applesauce Oatmeal Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1104453.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking powder
1/2 tsp. Baking soda
2 tsp. Dry ginger
1 C. Table cream
2 Eggs, beaten
1 C. Granulated sugar
1 C. Rolled oats
1 C. Applesauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
Grease a 9" by 5" by 3" loaf pan and a small 4" round souffle dish. Set aside
Blend flour, B. powder, B. soda and ginger together in a small bowl.
In a small bowl beat the eggs until light and frothy.
Combine beaten eggs, light cream, sugar, applesauce, and rolled oats together and mix just until ingredients form a smooth batter
Add dry ingredients a small amount at a time and then pour into round and rectangular loaf pans filling 2/3 full with a small space around the top of loaf pans.
Bake in oven for 50 minutes or until loaves test done in the middle

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A fibrous loaf with oats and applesauce with a spicy ginger flavour to make a good loaf for tea time or lunch.
