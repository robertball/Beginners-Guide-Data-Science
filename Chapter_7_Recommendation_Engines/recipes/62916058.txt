Title:
Oven Baked Crunchy Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Thick white fish like cod or haddock
4 slices bread
2T butter
salt and pepper
Small chopped shallot
2 T parsley
2 eggs
3 T mayo
2 t salt
1/2 t paprika
1/4 t cayenne pepper
pepper
3 T flour

Directions:
Dry fish with paper towels. Season with salt and pepper.
Preheat oven to 400.
Make crumbs with bread, softened butter, salt and pepper. Pulse in food processor until fine crumbs. Toast in oven until golden brown. To toasted crumbs, add shallot and parsley.
Make batter with eggs, mayo, salt, pepper, paprika and cayenne pepper.
Increase oven temp. to 425.
Dip dried fish in flour, pat off extra. Then dip in batter, followed by crumbs. Set fish pieces on cooking sprayed baking sheet. Bake for 18-22 min or until internal temp is 140 degrees.
