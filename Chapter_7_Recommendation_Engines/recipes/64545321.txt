Title:
French 75 Jell-O Shots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3219563.jpg
Ingredients:
44g Lemon Jello (1/4 packet)
.25 Cup Gin
14 oz. Sparkling wine (divided)
3 envelopes unflavored gelatin
1 drop red food coloring

Directions:
Add 1 cup of boiling water to lemon Jello and stir
Add Gin
Pour lemon mixture into 16 2-ounce cups
Place each cup into a spot in a muffin tin, tilted onto its side
Refrigerate for four hours
In a small pot, pour 10 ounces of wine and the gelatin, let sit for 3 minutes
Put low flame under pot and stir until gelatin is dissolved (less than 5 minutes)
Add last four ounces of wine and drop of food coloring and stir
Upright the cups and add wine mixture up to the top of lemon mixture
Refrigerate four hours
