Title:
Happy Heart Grilled Salmon Radicchio Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12  whole medium radicchio leaves
1 lb  grilled salmon without skin, chunked
PICO DE GALLO
1 md  tomato, seeded and diced
1/2 md  red bell pepper, seeded and diced
1/2 c  chopped red onion
juice of 1 lime
CREAM SAUCE
2/3 c  plain greek yogurt
2 Tbsp  skim milk
1/2 tsp  everglades seasoning

Directions:
To prepare pico de gallo:
Mix pico de gallo ingredients together in a bowl. Cover and refrigerate until ready to use.
To prepare cream sauce:
Mix all of the cream ingredients together in a bowl. Adjust seasoning to suit your taste. Cover and refrigerate until ready to use.
To assemble radicchio wraps:
Place some grilled salmon chunks in a radicchio leaf. Top with pico de gallo and cream sauce.

Submitter: 
A. Smith
Description: 
This recipe is heart health conscious. All that good omega 3 from the salmon and greek yogurt and lycopene from the radicchio, tomatoes, and red bell peppers. Radicchio also is high in antioxidants, which boosts heart health and increase blood vessel elasticity.
