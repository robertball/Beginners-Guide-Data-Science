Title:
Party Mints from EAGLE BRAND® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 (32 ounce) package confectioners' sugar
1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract
Assorted colored granulated sugar or crystals

Directions:
In medium bowl, beat sweetened condensed milk and half of confectioners' sugar until blended. Gradually add remaining confectioners' sugar and peppermint extract, beating until stiff.
Roll mixture into 1/2-inch balls; roll in desired sugar, and place on parchment paper.  Let stand 8 hours.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
These party mints are great for special occasions, and they can be made ahead of time for convenience. Roll into balls, or press into molds to match the celebration.
