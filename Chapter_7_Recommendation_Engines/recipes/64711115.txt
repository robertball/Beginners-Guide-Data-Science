Title:
Panang Curry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. Olive Oil
1-2 Tbsp. Panang Curry Paste (I add more)
2 lbs. Chicken Breasts, Thinly sliced in 1/8" strips
2 Red Bell Peppers,sliced
1 White Onion, sliced
3 Carrots, sliced
3 Celery stocks, sliced
2 tsp. freshly grated ginger
4 garlic cloves, minced
2 14 oz. cans of coconut milk
1 tbsp. Corn Starch
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tbsp. fish sauce
1 tbsp. lime juice
1/2 tsp. Lime zest , grated
1 Bay leaf
1/4 tsp. dried thyme
1 cup loosely packed basil leaves, roughly cut
Garnish (optional)
fresh basil
fresh lime juice
Sriracha sauce

Directions:
Heat oil over medium high heat in a large non-stick skillet.
Add curry paste and sauté for 1 minute.
Add sliced chicken and cook just until chicken is no longer pink inside.
Add ginger and garlic and sauté for 30 seconds.
Add 1 & 1/2 cans of coconut milk.
Wisk cornstarch into remaining 1/2 can of coconut milk then add to the skillet.
Stir in brown sugar, fish sauce, lime zest & juice, bay leaf, thyme and salt(optional).
Simmer for 5 minutes.
Add red peppers, carrots, onions, celery & basil and simmer for another 3 minutes or until vegtabes are crisp tender.
Discard Bay leaf.
Garnish with additional optional fresh basil, lime juice and Sriracha to taste
Serve with rice.
