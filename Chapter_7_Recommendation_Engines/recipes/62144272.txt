Title:
Spicy Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of mini penne pasta
1 chopped poblano pepper (use the seeds)
1 cup of cheddar cheese
1 cup of pepper jack cheese
1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
1 tablespoon of margarine
1 cup of milk or enough milk to almost cover the pasta
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Put noodles, water, salt, and pepper in a pot
2. Boil noodles, not all the way, you want them to be slightly hard.
3. Drain noodles and rinse well with cold water
4. Butter or spray baking pan
5. Put noodles in pan and cover with milk
6. Add a little margarine, salt, pepper, and cheese
7. Bake at 350 for about 40 minutes.
8. When it is done, take out of oven, mix, and put back in for about 10 minutes.
Note: It is also really good if you add cut up, left over hot dogs or cheddar metts.

Submitter: 
Staci Booth
Description: 
This is my Grandma's recipe for Macaroni and Cheese with some spice added to it. The measurements are totally variable. You can add more cheese, less milk, more poblano, etc. to your liking.
