Title:
Beef N Potato Pasties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
pie crust (seven 6 inch rounds)
1/2 pound ground beef, cooked
1/4 pound ground sausage, cooked
2 cups potatoes, raw, diced
2 tablespoons Worcestershire
7 pats butter
1 egg white
1 tablespoon water

Directions:
Prepare seven 6 inch pie crust rounds and set aside.  Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  In bowl, combine ground beef, sausage, potatoes, and Worcestershire sauce to make filling.  Place 1/2 cup filling off-center on pie crust round.  Top with pat of butter.  Fold crust in half to make a half moon and pinch edges.  Place on baking sheet.  Repeat with remaining filling and crusts.  Mix egg white and water.  Brush top of pasties with egg white mixture.  Use fork to make steam vents.  Bake at 375 degrees for 15 minutes.  Reduce heat to 350 degrees and bake for 60 minutes.

Submitter: 
jillbont
Description: 
My meat-and-potatoes husband loves these pasties (pass-teez).  While they require a bit of preparation, the ingredients are cheap and simple.  Traditionally served with ketchup, kids love it too!
