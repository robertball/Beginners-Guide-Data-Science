Title:
My Chicken & Dumplin's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
chicken, carrots, potatoes, lima beans, celery, savory herbs, flour, cornstarch, butter, water

Directions:
boil chicken pieces, add savory herbs, salt & pepper, veges and return to boil. Make dumplin's with 1/4 cup butter melted,1 1/2 cup flour, 1/2 cup soy milk.mix well and knead. Roll into balls and drop into boiling water. Take 3TBS butter and melt. Add 3 TBS of the boiling water and 3 TBS cornstarch and mix well. Add to main pot.

Submitter: 
Jena LaBree
Description: 
True homemade chicken & dumplin's. It's so easy why cut corners with processed foods! Nothing beats the flavor of fresh!
