Title:
Coconut Macaroons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (7 oz.) Shredded coconut
3 Tbsp. Flour
1/8 tsp. Salt
2 Egg whites
1/2 tsp. Almond extract
1/3 Cup Sugar

Directions:
Whip egg whites in small bowl, then add almond extract.
In larger bowl mix coconut, sugar, flour, and salt.
Add the egg whites and almond extract to the larger bowl and lightly mix the two together.
Drop by tablespoons onto greased cookie sheets.
Bake at 325 degrees F. for about 20 minutes or until edges start to brown.
Remove cookies onto wire racks and let cool.
Enjoy

Submitter: 
MMS
Description: 
A great recipe that makes light and fluffy coconut macaroons.
