Title:
Blueberry Stuffed French toast bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/637759.jpg
Ingredients:
1 loaf white bread, No crusts.
1/2 loaf french bread
6c. frozen blueberries
8 oz neufchatel or cream cheese
1/2 c. sour cream
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
7 eggs
1 1/2 c milk
1 cup half n half
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 c powder sugar

Directions:
Cut bread into cubes and place in greased 9x13.  Sprinkle blueberries over this.  In bowl, microwave cream cheese about a minute until soft and add 1/2 c. sugar and the sour cream and vanilla.  Spread mixture over blueberries. Cut french bread into 10 1inch thick slices. Place over sour cream. Beat half n half, eggs, milk and spices til blended. pour over bread.  Cover and put in refridgerator overnight. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes covered.  Uncover bake another 15 minutes. Best if let to sit before slicing. sprinkle serving with poweder sugar just before serving.

Submitter: 
Jacquie
Description: 
Yummy brunch casserole.  Easy to prepare.  Do it the night before, through it in the oven and enjoy. Great with maple syrup
