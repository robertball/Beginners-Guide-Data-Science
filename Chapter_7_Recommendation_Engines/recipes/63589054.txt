Title:
Italian Sausage Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/998265.jpg
Ingredients:
3-4 links Johnsonville Italian Mild Sausage
1/2 large onion, diced
1 carrot, sliced
1 cup cabbage, diced
2 potatoes, cubed
1 14.5 ounce can diced tomatoes
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 cup uncooked bow tie pasta
2 beef bouillon cubes
water

Directions:
Completely cook sausage in a skillet.   Remove and dice and add to a 3 quart kettle.
Add 2 cups of water to the skillet and bring to a boil to deglaze.   Pour the fond into the kettle with the sausage.
Add to the skillet the onions, carrot, cabbage, tomatoes, pepper and bouillon.
Bring to a boil and then immediately return to simmer.
Once the carrots and cabbage just start to soften, add in potatoes.
Once potatoes just start to soften, add in the bow tie pasta.
Remove from heat and serve when the pasta is done.
