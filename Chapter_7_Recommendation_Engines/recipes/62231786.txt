Title:
Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Brick cream cheese softened to room temperature
Brown Sugar
Carmel Sauce
Chocolate Sauce
Heath Bar candy bits

Directions:
Mix cream cheese and as much brown sugar that "tastes right to you". Spread cream cheese mixture in a shallow dish. Top with carmel sauce, chocolate sauce, and Heath Bar candy bits.  Serve with fruit and/or flat pretzels.  Chill before serving.
