Title:
Bill Knapp's Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lb frozen hash browns (let thaw half a day)
1/2 stick butter, melted
1/2t salt
1/2t pepper
2T minced onion
1 can cream of chicken soup
16 oz sour cream
2c shredded cheddar
2c crushed Ritz crackers

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.
Mix crushed crackers and butter.
Add cracker mixture to top of potatoes.
Bake at 350 for 45-60 minutes.
