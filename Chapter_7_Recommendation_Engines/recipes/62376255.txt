Title:
Zippy Tofu Stir-fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/456326.jpg
Ingredients:
1 350g block extra firm tofu, cubed
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup orange juice
1 tbsp sriracha sauce
1 tbsp brown sugar or honey
1 tsp grated ginger
2 tbsp canola oil
1 small onion chopped
2 medium carrots cut into 3 inch strips
5 cloves garlic, minced
1 red bell pepper cut into thin strips
1 green bell pepper cut into thin strips
1 cup broccoli florets
1 mango, pitted, peeled and roughly chopped
1/2 cup toasted cashews, roughly chopped
1/8 cup fresh cilantro, minced
1/8 cup lime juice
salt to taste

Directions:
mix togther soy sauce, orange juice, sriracha sauce, brown sugar and grated ginger, pour over cubed tofu, set aside while you are chopping vegetables.
Heat oil in a frying pan on medium high and add onions, sautee 2-3 minutes until softened.  Add carrots and sautee 3-5 minutes.  Add garlic, peppers and broccoli, sautee, stirring frequently 5 more minutes.
Add tofu mixture to pan and cook stirring frequently until the liquid has reduced and the vegetables are at the desired softness.
Salt to taste.  Toss with fresh mango, cilantro, lime juice and cashews to serve.

Submitter: 
Sarah-May
Description: 
This colourful vegan dish is a snap to prepare.  Serve hot over rice or rice vermicelli.
