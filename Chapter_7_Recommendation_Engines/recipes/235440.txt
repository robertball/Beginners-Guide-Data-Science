Title:
Best Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5645401.jpg
Ingredients:
Casserole:
3 cups mashed cooked yams
2 eggs
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup flaked coconut
1/4 cup milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Topping:
1 cup chopped pecans
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup all-purpose flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Whisk yams, eggs, 1/2 cup butter, white sugar, coconut, milk, and vanilla together in a bowl until smooth; pour into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
Stir pecans, brown sugar, butter, and flour together in a separate bowl until crumbly; sprinkle over the yam mixture.
Bake in preheated oven until the topping is slightly browned, about 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sandy Scott
Description: 
This casserole is easy to make but absolutely delicious. Even guests who hate sweet potatoes love this dish!
