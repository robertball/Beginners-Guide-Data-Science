Title:
Chocolate Cake with Chocolate Sour Cream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c. butter, softened
3 eggs
2 c. flour
3/4 c. unsweetened cocoa
1 tsp. baking soda
3/4 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
2 c. sugar
2 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 c. milk
Frosting:
2 c. semisweet chocolate chips
1/2 c. butter
8 oz. sour cream
4 1/2 c. powdered sugar

Directions:
Let butter and eggs sit out at room temp for 30 minutes.  Grease 3 8 inch cake pans or 2 9 inch cake pans, then line the bottoms with wax paper.  Lightly grease and flour the sides of the pans.
Stir together flour, cocoa powder, baking soda and powder, and salt; set aside.  Preheat oven to 350.
Beat butter for 30 seconds on high speed.  Gradually add sugar, about 1/4 c. at a time until combined.  Add eggs one at a time and beat, add in vanilla and beat.
Add flour mixture and milk alternately beating on low after each addition.  Spread in pans.
Bake 30-40 mins until center comes out clean with a toothpick.
Cool cake layers in pans for 10 minutes.  Peel off waxed paper.  Frost.
Frosting:
Melt the chocolate chips and butter over low heat.  Cool for 5 minutes.  Stir in sour cream.  Gradually add the powdered sugar beating until smooth.
