Title:
Curried Chicken Divan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups cooked chopped broccoli
2 cups shredded, cooked chicken meat
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 teaspoon curry powder
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 bag of seasoned croutons

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Arrange cooked broccoli in a lightly greased 3 quart casserole dish with a little water and microwave for 4 minutes. Arrange chicken over broccoli. In a medium bowl combine the soup, mayonnaise, curry powder. Mix together and pour mixture over chicken and broccoli. Put croutons on top and cover with cheese  Bake for 30 minutes
