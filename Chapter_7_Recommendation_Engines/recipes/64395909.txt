Title:
Roasted White Asparagus Herbs de Provence Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Bunch of White Asparagus
2 TBSP Olive Oil
1/2 TSP Herbs de Provence
1/4 TSP Salt
1/8 TSP Ground Pepper

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 400 Degrees
Trim the bottom of the White Asparugus approx. 1.5 " off the bottom
Use a vegetable peeler to peel the skin off each Stalk, do this on a on a cutting board as the Asparagus may break otherwise
Lay Asparagus in Casserole Dish or on Baking Sheet
Brush with Olive oil
Season with Salt Pepper and Herb du Provence
Cook for 20 min or until asparagus lightly brown

Description: 
White Asparagus Roasted with Herbs de Provence.  Nice accompaniment to your favorite French Dish.
