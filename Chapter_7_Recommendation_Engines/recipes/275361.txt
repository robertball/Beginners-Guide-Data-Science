Title:
Vegan Raspberry-Mint Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6931654.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pint fresh raspberries
2 pinches fresh mint
1 cup vanilla-flavored sweetened almond milk
1 cup dairy-free vanilla ice cream

Directions:
Combine raspberries, almond milk, ice cream, and mint in a blender. Blend until smooth and you no longer see large pieces of berries or mint, scraping down the sides of the blender during the process as needed.
Serve in 2 medium glasses.

Submitter: 
Manda
Description: 
This is a delightfully refreshing vegan mint-raspberry milkshake that is sure to please everyone. Top with additional raspberries and mint, if desired.
