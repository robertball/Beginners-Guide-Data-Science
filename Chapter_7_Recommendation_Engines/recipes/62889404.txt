Title:
Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/697782.jpg
Ingredients:
6 oz Ground Beef
2 Cups Of Shredded Lettuce
2 Oz Of Black Olives
2 Oz Of Sour Cream
2 Oz Of Shredded Cheddar Cheese
4 Oz Of Salsa (Fresh Or Goya Salsa Pico de Gallo)
1 Tomato, Diced
1 Flour 12 Inch Tortilla Shell
4 Oz re-Fried Beans
2 oz Onions, Chopped
FOR THE RED SAUCE
1/4 Teaspoon Cumin
1/8 Teaspoon of Pepper
2 Cups Tomatoes
1 Jalapeno Pepper
5 Cloves of Garlic
1/4 Teaspoon of Oregano
1/4 Cup of Chicken Stock
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 onion, chopped

Directions:
Pre-Heat Deep Fryer On 375
In A Pan Cook Red Sauce (10-15 min), Cumin, Pepper, Tomatoes, Jalapeno Pepper, Garlic, Oregano, Chicken Stock, salt, onion. Put In Blender And Puree, Or Use An Immersion blender, Strain.
as your cooking the red sauce brown ground beef in a 10/12 Inch fry pan, drain off all oil
In A Pan Cook Red Sauce (10-15 min), Cumin, Pepper, Tomatoes, Jalapeno Pepper, Garlic, Oregano, Chicken Stock, salt, onion. Put In Blender And Puree, Or Use An Immersion blender, Strain.
Mix Red Sauce In With Hamburger, And Cook Into Hamburger, till thickened.
When Oil Is Ready Take Tortilla Shell And Put In Taco Salad Form, And Dunk In Deep Fryer For 30 Seconds. Pull Form Out And Allow All Oil To Go Back Into Deep  Fryer. Then Dunk Back Into Deep Fryer For Another 30 Seconds. This Allows For The Cooler Oil To Mix Back Into The Hotter Oil.
Take Shell, Layer With Re-Fried Beans, Add Half Of The Ground Beef, Then Add Lettuce, Top With The Rest Of The Ground Beef In Center, Add Diced Tomatoes, Black Olives, Cheddar Cheese, Sour Cream, Onions, Salsa
Taco Salad, Done
