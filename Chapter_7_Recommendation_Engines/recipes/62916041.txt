Title:
Mini Greek Pizza Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T EVOO
2/3 C finely chopped onion
2/3 C finely chopped red bell pepper
1/3 C whole wheat pastry flour
1/3 C all-purpose flour
2 t baking powder
1 1/2 t chopped fresh oregano or 1/2 t dried
1 t sugar
1/4 t garlic powder
1/4 t salt
1/3 C low-fat milk
1/3 C crumbled feta cheese
1 lg. egg, well beaten
2 T tomato paste
2 T chopped kalamata olives

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet over med. heat. Add onion and bell pepper;cook, stirring often, until onion is tender, about 5 min. Transfer to a large bowl and let cool for 10 min.
Meanwhile, preheat oven to 400 degrees. Coat a mini-muffin pan with cooking spray.
Whisk whole wheat pastry flour, all-purpose flour, baking powder, oregano, sugar, garlic powder and salt in a med bowl.
Stir milk, feta cheese, egg, tomato paste and olives into onion mixture. Make a well in the dry ingred.; add the wet ingred. and stir until just combined. Fill the prepared muffin cups two-thirds full.
Bake the muffins until lightly browned, 13-15 min. Cool in the pan for 5 min. before turning out onto a wire rack. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
All the flavors of a Greek pizza make these savory muffins bite-size treats for adults and kids alike
