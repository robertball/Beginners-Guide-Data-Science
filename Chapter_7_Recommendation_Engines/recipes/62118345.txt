Title:
Healthy Breakfast Cereal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
200g oatmeal
200g millet flakes
100g sesame seeds
4 tblsp honey
4 tblsp rapeseed oil
3 tblsp cocoa powder
milk

Directions:
in a large non-stick frying pan mix together oatmeal, millet flakes and sesame seeds.
roast for about 10 minutes stirring constantly.
mix oil and honey
pour over granola mixture and roast for another five minutes until mixture is a golden brown stirring constantly.
Turn off flame immediately and mix in cocoa powder.
Wait for the granola mixture to cool off completely and then pour in a big glas with srew top to store.
Enjoy with milk.
