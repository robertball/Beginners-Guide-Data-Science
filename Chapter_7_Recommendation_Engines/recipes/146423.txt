Title:
BUSH'S® Black Beans and Rice with Mango Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990362.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
3/4 cup finely chopped onion
1/2 cup finely chopped green pepper
1 cup diced tomatoes
1 (15 ounce) can BUSH'S® Black Beans, drained, juices reserved
1/2 teaspoon thyme
1 teaspoon garlic salt
3 tablespoons cider vinegar
1/2 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
2 cups cooked white rice
Garnish:
Mango, sliced and grilled
Lime wedges (optional)

Directions:
In large skillet heat olive oil; cook onion and green pepper until crisp tender; stir in tomatoes, beans, thyme and garlic salt, cook 3 minutes; add vinegar, pepper sauce, and reserved juices, continue to cook 5 minutes. Serve over rice.
Garnish with grilled mango slices and lime wedge.

Submitter: 
BUSH'S® Beans
Description: 
You don't have to sacrifice taste to eat well. Here is a fresh and light salad that blends black beans and mangos.
