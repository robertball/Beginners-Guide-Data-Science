Title:
bacon infused brussel sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs of fresh Brussels’ sprouts, ends cut off and halved
8 oz of good bacon (peaces and ends if you can find them) cut into a medium dice
1 medium onion (medium dice)
2 table spoons of minced garlic (you can use the jar kind to save time)
Pepper to taste
Olive oil

Directions:
Place the bacon in a sauté pan and begin to brown and render fat.
When the bacon is about half done add onions and garlic cook for 4 minutes on medium heat
Place all the Brussels in a 9 by 12 baking dish
Cover sprouts with bacon and onion mixture
Add oil and toss to coat all the sprouts (if there is enough bacon grease omit this step)
Pepper to taste
Place baking dish under a broiler on high for 3 minute then toss mixture, keep tossing mixture every 3 to 4 minutes until Brussels are fork tender, some of the leaves will get brown this is desirable (about 12 minutes)

Submitter: 
jon
Description: 
using bacon, onion and garlic and a broiler you can take brussels to another level.
