Title:
Homemade Pancake Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744808.jpg
Ingredients:
5 Cups      All-Purpose Flour
1 1/4 Cups  Instant Dry Milk
1/4 Cup     Sugar
4TBL        Baking Power
1TBL        Salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.  Stir together to blend well.  Put in a large air tight container.  Use within 6 to 8 months.
TO MAKE PANCAKES OR WAFFLES:
2 Cups Pancake Mix
1 Cup water
1 Egg
2 tbsp.  Oil  (waffles - 3 tbsp)
Beat smooth with a wire whisk or fork.  This makes 8 (4 inch) pancakes or four large waffles.
