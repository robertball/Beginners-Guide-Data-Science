Title:
Homemade Chivettas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 cup cooking oil
2 cups vinegar
3 tablespoons salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/8 teaspoon onion powder
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon celery salt
1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
Dash of ground cloves and cayenne pepper

Directions:
Beat egg. Add oil, beat again. Add all other remaining ingredients and mix well
