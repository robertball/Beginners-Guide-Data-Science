Title:
Cookies and Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
19.1 ounces chocolate sandwich cookies with creme filling (family size package, 1 lb 3 oz)
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup confectioners' sugar
2 (3.5 ounce) packages instant vanilla pudding mix
3 cups milk
1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
14.3 ounces chocolate sandwich cookies with creme filling (regular size package)

Directions:
Chop the family size package of cookies very fine in food processor. The white cream will disappear.
Mix butter and 2/3 of the crumbs and press into the bottom of a 9x13 pan.
In a large bowl mix, milk and pudding and whipped topping together.
Combine pudding mixture and cream mixture together and let sit until thickened, about 5 minutes.
Add cream cheese and sugar to the bowl, and mix until thoroughly incorporated.
Gently fold in the whipped topping.
Roughly chop the remaining package of cookies or place in a gallon size bag a roughly break up using a rolling pin, the bottom of a pan, can of veggies, etc.
Gently fold in the broken up cookies into the bowl.
Spread the mixture on top of the cookie bottom crust.
Top with the remaining crumbs.
Chill until ready to serve.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Cori
Description: 
If you love cookies and cream, this is the dessert for you!  Once fully chilled, you can cute the crust and serve this up in squares on a plate.
