Title:
Chicken Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 lb. spagetti- cooked drained and rinsed.
*2-4 cooked boneless, skinless chicken breasts
*2 cans cream of chicken soup (can use any kind)
*1 cup chicken broth
*1/2 cup chopped green onions
*2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
1. Pour cooked spaghetti into 13x9 baking dish.
2. Combine chicken, soup, broth, onions and 1 1/2 cup shredded cheese.
3. Pour over top of spaghetti
4. Sprinkle the top with remaining cheese.
5. Bake for 30 minutes until bubbly at 375 degrees.
