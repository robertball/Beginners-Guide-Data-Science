Title:
Pork Chops with Creamy White Wine Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless Pork Chops
4 Tbls. Butter (Divided 2 & 2)
1/4 Cup All Purpose Flour
1/2 tsp.Garlic Powder
1/2 tsp. Onion Powder
2 tsp. Italian Seasoning (Divided)
2 Cloves Garlic, Minced
1.5 tbsp. leftover flour from dredging
1/2 Cup Dry White Wine
Juice from 1/4 of a Lemon
1/2 Cup Chicken Stock
1/2 Cup Heavy Cream
4 Sprigs Fresh Thyme (Leaves Only)
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Pork Chop Dredging Mixture: In a large bowl, whisk together flour, garlic powder, onion powder, 1 tsp. Italian seasoning, and season generously with salt and pepper.
2. Dredge pork chops thoroughly in dry mixture, then heat 2 tbsp. butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Once hot, add pork chops and sear them on each side for 3-4 minutes. Remove from heat and cover to keep warm.
4. Reduce skillet heat to medium and add 2 more tbsp. butter and minced garlic. Cook for 1-2 minutes. Add 1 heaping tbsp. of the dredging mixture and blend it with the butter.
5. Once butter and flour are combined, slowly stir in the wine. Next add the chicken stock and heavy cream. Squeeze the 1/4 lemon wedge juice into the sauce. Add the fresh thyme leaves and 1 tsp. Italian seasoning. Season with salt and pepper to taste and simmer for 2-3 minutes. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
6. Reduce heat to low and return pork chops to the pan. Spoon sauce over the pork chops and allow them to reheat in the sauce for 3-5 minutes or until pork chops are done.
