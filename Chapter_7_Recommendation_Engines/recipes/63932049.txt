Title:
Baked peaches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz frozen peaches
1/3 cup brown sugar sub=1/3 cup brown sugar
1 Tbl flour
1/4 cup pecans
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Put peaches in single layer in baking pan. Mix other ingredients together & top peaches. Bake at 350 for about 20-30 min.
