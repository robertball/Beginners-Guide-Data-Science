Title:
Kahlua Irish Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992130.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce Kahlua
1 fluid ounce Jameson Irish Whiskey
3/4 cup hot coffee
Whipped cream

Directions:
Pour the Kahlua and Jameson Irish whiskey into a mug of hot coffee, top with whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Kahlua
Description: 
Irish coffee gets a flavor boost from Kahlua, whiskey, hot coffee, and a dollop of whipped cream.
