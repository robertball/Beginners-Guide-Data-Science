Title:
Galopita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup farina
2 quarts milk
2-1/2 cups sugar
3/4 stick butter
10 eggs, well beaten
2 teaspoons vanilla

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, except eggs, in large pot.  Bring to boil, stirring constantly until thickened.  Remove pan from heat and let cool.  When cool fold in beaten eggs.  Pour mixture into greased 9 x 13 pan.  Preheat oven to 350 degreed and bake for one hour.  Sprinkle with powdered sugar and cinnamon.  Cut into small diamond shaped pieces.  Best served lukewarm or room temperature.

Submitter: 
Kathy W.
Description: 
This is simple Greek dessert that I always get rave reviews on.  The recipe goes a little quicker if you let the milk stand at room temperature for a while.  Enjoy!
