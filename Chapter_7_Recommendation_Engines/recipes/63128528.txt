Title:
Sesame Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/797822.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound angel hair pasta
3 1/2 tablespoons sesame oil
3 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 1/2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
2 tablespoons sugar
2 teaspoons salt
3/4 tablespoon hot chili oil (optional, but good)

Directions:
Cook spaghetti as you normally would.
Mix all liquid ingredients and toss in warm noodles.
Add green onions, red peppers or any other veggies or chicken.

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
Martha Belski introduced this side dish to us in the Summer of 2005, while we were all vacationing in South Fork Colorado.
