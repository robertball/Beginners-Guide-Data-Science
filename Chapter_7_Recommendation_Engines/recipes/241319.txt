Title:
Cheesy Chicken & Broccoli Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/6834381.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) package STOVE TOP Stuffing Mix for Chicken
1 ½ pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 (16 ounce) bag frozen chopped broccoli, thawed
½ pound VELVEETA(<sup>&reg;</sup>), cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1 (10.75 ounce) can reduced-sodium condensed cream of chicken soup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Prepare stuffing mix as directed on package; set aside.
Mix chicken, broccoli, VELVEETA and soup in 13x9-inch glass baking dish; top with the stuffing.
BAKE 30 min. or until chicken is cooked through.

Submitter: 
VELVEETA Cheese
Description: 
Classic comfort comes in the form of chicken, broccoli and-you'll love this-stuffing, all in an ooey-gooey cheese sauce. Get a big helping of melty-meets-hearty in every bite.
