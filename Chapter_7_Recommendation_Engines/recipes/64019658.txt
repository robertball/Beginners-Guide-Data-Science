Title:
Keith's Favorite Beef Egg Roll Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground beef
2 cloves garlic, finely grated
1/4 cup finely diced red bell pepper
2 Tbsp finely chopped onion
1/2 tsp finely grated fresh ginger
2 Tbsp brown sugar
1 Tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp toasted sesame oil
1/4 tsp kosher salt
1 cup slaw mix

Directions:
Place all the filling ingredients, except the cabbage, in a mixing bowl and mix with a fork.
Heat a non-stick skillet over high heat. Add the meat mixture and cook, breaking it up with a wooden spatula. Once it is almost fully cooked and broken into small pieces add the shredded cabbage. Cook until the cabbage is softened.
Fill egg rolls wrappers as directed on package.
