Title:
Mauigirl's Herb Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1018370.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup olive oil
1/3 cup red wine vinegar
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1 tablespoon sugar
1 clove peeled garlic
1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves or 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
8 fresh basil leaves or 1/2 teaspoon dried basil

Directions:
Add all ingredients to your blender pitcher or a food processor bowl. Blend until well combined. Chill for 1 hour before using. If it separates, shake to combine.

Submitter: 
mauigirl
Description: 
This is a simple and quick salad dressing that you can easily throw together with available ingredients.
