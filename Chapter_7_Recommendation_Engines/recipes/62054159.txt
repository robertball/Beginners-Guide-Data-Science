Title:
Shrimp Creole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked white rice
4 tablespoons butter
1 medium onion, diced
1 medium green pepper, cut in strips
1 crushed bayleaf
1 teaspoon parsley
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
3 tablespoons sugar
1-6 ounce can of tomato paste
1-16 ounce can diced tomatoes
2 cups water
1.5-2 pounds uncooked peeled, deveined shrimp

Directions:
Cook rice according to directions on package.  In a large pan, melt butter, add onion and green pepper and cook until tender.  Stir in remaining ingredients except shrimp and rice.  Cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes.  Add shrimp and heat through, for about 5 minutes or until pink.  Serve over white rice.

Submitter: 
Irlandes
Description: 
This is one of my all time favorite dishes.  It's not as spicy as normal creole dishes, but it's a wonderful dish reheated.  Eat with a baguette to sop up the wonderful sauce!
