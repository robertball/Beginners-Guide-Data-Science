Title:
Grape Nuts Energy Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup peanut butter
1 Cup honey
2 Cups large marshmallows
1 Cup Grape Nuts cereal
1 Cup rolled oats

Directions:
Mix peanut butter, honey and marshmallows in saucepan, cooking slowly until all are melted and stirred together. Remove from heat and add Grape Nuts cereal and rolled oats. Stir well. Pour into parchment lined pan, pat evenly. Cool. Cut into 20 bars. I wrap these individually in plastic wrap for a ready to go treat or breakfast!

Submitter: 
CHEFROMANO
Description: 
A crunchy, chewy, sweet breakfast or anytime you need a pick me up bar!
