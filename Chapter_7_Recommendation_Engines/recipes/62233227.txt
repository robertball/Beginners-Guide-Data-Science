Title:
Sticks and Stones Candy Bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 (11 ounce) packages NESTLE速 TOLL HOUSE速 Butterscotch Flavored Morsels, divided
1 1/2 packages NESTLE速 TOLL HOUSE速 Semi-Sweet Chocolate Morsels
3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
2 cups thin pretzel sticks
3/4 cup dry roasted peanuts
1 cup craisins

Directions:
BUTTER a 9x13 inch glass baking dish.
MICROWAVE all but 1/2 cup butterscotch morsels, semi-sweet morsels and peanut butter in large, microwave-safe bowl on HIGH (100 percent) power for 1 minute; stir. Microwave at additional 10- to 20-second intervals, stirring until smooth.
ADD pretzels, peanuts and craisins; stir well to coat. Spread into prepared baking dish.
Sprinkle with remaining butterscotch morsels.
REFRIGERATE for 1 hour or until firm. Break into bite-size pieces.
