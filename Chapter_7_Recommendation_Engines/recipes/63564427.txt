Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
2 tablespoons butter
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of chicken soup
2 cups chicken broth
1 onion, finely diced
2 (10 ounce) packages refrigerated biscuit dough, torn into pieces

Directions:
Season chicken with Montreal chicken seasoning.Place the chicken, butter, soup, and onion in a slow cooker.  Pour in chicken broth
Cover, and cook for 5 hours on high. About 90 minutes before serving, place the torn biscuit dough in the slow cooker and push down into broth.

Submitter: 
stacy voss
Description: 
Easy, creamy chicken and dumplings made from refrigerator biscuits, slow cooked to comfort-food perfection.Modified from: Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings
