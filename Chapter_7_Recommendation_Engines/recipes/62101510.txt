Title:
Homemade 3 cheese Mac & cheese with sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound sausage
1 small onion (diced)
6 cloves (fresh) garlic minced
1 pound Cellentali pasta (or elbow)
2 cups Smoked provolone cheese (shredded or diced)
1 cup Asiago cheese (shredded)
1 cup Parmesan cheese (shredded)
2 Tbsp parsley
2 cups chicken stock
2 cups half & half
2-3 cups garlic butter croutons (coursely crushed)
2 Tbsp flour
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Spray 9x13 baking dish with cooking spray, set aside.
Crush croutons in gallon size storage bag. Mix in Parmesan cheese and parsley. Set aside.
Cook pasta according to package directions.Drain.
In a large heavy pot. Crumble and saute sausage over medium high heat. While the sausage is browning dice onion, and mince garlic.
When the sausage is browned move it to the outer edges of the pan (keeping drippings in pan).
Add onion and saute until translucent.
Add garlic. Mix with sausage and onion. Stir in flour to make a roux.(cook for approx. 1 minute)
Add salt and pepper to taste.
Add chicken stock and half & half. Stirring often. Bring to a boil. Boil for aprox. 2-3 minutes. stirring so that the mixture will not scortch. Liquid will thicken.
Remove from heat and add the provolone and Asiago cheeses. Stir until cheeses are melted and smooth.
Add pasta.
Stir until evenly coated.
Pour mixture into prepared baking dish.
Cover evenly with crouton mixture.
Bake in 350 degree oven for 30 minutes. or until lightly browned on top.

Description: 
This is not your Mother's Mac & Cheese. This easy recipe will win over even the pickiest of eaters. Serve with a green salad.
