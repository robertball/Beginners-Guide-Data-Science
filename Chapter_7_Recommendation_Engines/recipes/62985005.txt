Title:
Susan's Millionaire Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (14 ounce)can sweetened condensed milk
1 tsp pure vanilla
1 (12 ounce)container frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 (20 ounce)can crushed pineapple in syrup, drained
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 (6 ounce)bag shredded coconut
1 1/2 cups  chopped pecans
2 (9 inch)  prepared graham cracker crusts

Directions:
Cream together the cream cheese, vanilla and condensed milk; Stir in the crushed pineapple, coconut and pecans; Stir in lemon juice. Gently fold in the whipped topping. Scoop evenly into pie crusts and refrigerate for 3 to 4 hours.

Submitter: 
TALK2MOI
Description: 
In this recipe making two pies, graham cracker crusts are filled with a creamy pineapple, coconut and pecan mixture made with sweetened condensed milk, cream cheese and whipped topping.Modified from: SueBee's Millionaire Pie
