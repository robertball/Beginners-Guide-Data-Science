Title:
Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ozs. vodka
4 oz. pineapple juice
1 oz. cranberry juice cocktail
splash of orange juice
splash of seltzer water
a quick squeeze of fresh lemon and lime
lemon and lime wedge for garnish
ice cubes

Directions:
Put ice cubes in a glass and add vodka and all fruit juices and seltzer.  Stir and put the lemon and lime garnish on the rim of the glass.

Submitter: 
SANDI149
Description: 
I was just experimenting on day with some vodka and a whole bunch of fruit juices and came up with this wonderful summer drink.
