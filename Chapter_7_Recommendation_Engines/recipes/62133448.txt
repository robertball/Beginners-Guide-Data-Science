Title:
Pineapple Picnic Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 block of cream cheese (softened)
1 20 oz. can of crushed pineapple
1 2.1 oz. box of instant vanilla pudding
1 16 oz. tub of Cool Whip (thawed)

Directions:
In a large bowl,combine the cream cheese and crushed pineapple with a mixer.  Mix in the pudding.  Fold in the Cool Whip.  Chill till ready to serve.
Variations:  You can use all low fat/sugar free substitutes.  You may also use the cheese cake flavored pudding.  We have added sweetened coconut as well.
