Title:
Breakfast Parfait Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz vanilla 0% Greek yogurt
1 tablespoon creamy peanut butter
1/2 banana, sliced
1/4 cup crispy rice cereal

Directions:
Mix yogurt and peanut butter until combined.
Layer half of yogurt in the bottom of a parfait glass or bowl.
Top with half of the banana slices.
Pour cereal over banana slices.
Add the rest of the yogurt and top with the remaining banana slices.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
This recipe from the Gastroparesis-Friendly Kitchen is an easy, high-protein breakfast with about 8 grams of healthy fat.
