Title:
Big Ray's Sweet and Saucy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1173941.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
2/3 cup water
1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
1/3 cup soy sauce
2 Tbl ketchup
1 medium onion, minced
1 tsp Dijon mustard
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Place chicken in a 3-qt. slow cooker. In a small saucepan, combine brown sugar and flour. Stir in the water, vinegar and soy sauce. Add the ketchup, onion, mustard, garlic powder, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 1-2 minutes or until thickened.
Pour over chicken. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or until chicken juices run clear.
