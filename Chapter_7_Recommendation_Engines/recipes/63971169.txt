Title:
Karen's Meatlof Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 lbs ground beef
1, 10 3/4 oz can french onion soup
4 oz Pepperidge Farm Stuffing Mix
2 eggs
Topping:
1/2 -3/4 cup ketchup
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 tsp dry mustard

Directions:
Mix together beef, soup, stuffing mix, and eggs.  Shape into loaf and bake at 350 degrees for 90 minutes, until brown and firm.  During the last 20 minutes, mix the ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard in a bowl.   Spread mixture on top and return to oven for 15 minutes.
