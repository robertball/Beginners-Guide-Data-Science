Title:
Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package fresh cranberries
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup orange juice
Zest from One Orange
1/8 tsp cinnamon
Pecan Pieces

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over medium heat, dissolve the sugar in the water and orange juice. Stir in the cranberries and orange zest, cooking until cranberries start to pop (about 10-20 minutes). Stir in cinnamon and pecan pieces.  Remove from heat, and transfer to a bowl. Cranberry sauce will thicken as it cools.
