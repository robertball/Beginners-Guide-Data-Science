Title:
Zippy Cauliflower and Cheese Soup (Epicure) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4538964.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large cauliflower, coarsely chopped
1 large onion, diced
1 tsp (5 ml) Epicure's Minced Garlic
4 cups (1 L) prepared Epicure's Chicken Bouillon
2 cups (500 ml) water
1 tsp (5 ml) Epicure's Mild Smoked Spanish Paprika
Epicure's Sea Salt, to taste
Epicure's Black Pepper, to taste
2 Tbsp (30 ml) butter
2 Tbsp (30 ml) allpurpose flour
2 Tbsp (30 ml) Epicure's Cheese, Chives & Bacon Dip Mix OR Chipotle, Bacon and Cheddar Dip Mix
1 cup (250 ml) cream
1 cup (250 ml) grated low fat cheddar cheese

Directions:
1.Simmer first 8 ingredients together until tender in Epicureâ€™s large MultiPurpose Pot. Drain, reserving broth. Set vegetables aside to cool.
2.Melt butter over medium-high heat. Stir in flour and Cheese, Chives & Bacon Dip Mix, stirring until bubbly.
3. Add reserved broth, stirring continuously. Add cream, stir and bring to a simmer, stirring frequently, until thickened. Add cheddar and stir until melted.
4.Mash cauliflower into small pieces. Add to cheese sauce. Bring soup to a simmer and serve
