Title:
Avocado Scrambled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/446362.jpg
Ingredients:
8 eggs
1/2 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 medium ripe avocado, peeled and cubed
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
6 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled

Directions:
In a bowl, beat eggs. Add milk, salt and pepper; stir in avocado.
In a skillet over medium heat, melt butter. Add egg mixture; cook and stir gently until the eggs are completely set. Sprinkle with bacon.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home's Fast Family Favorites
Description: 
Bacon and avocado blend nicely in these easy eggs, which are great for breakfast or brunch.
