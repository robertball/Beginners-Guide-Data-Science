Title:
Apple Cranberry Relish from Mott's® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1477689.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup Mott's® Natural Applesauce
1 1/2 cups whole berry cranberry sauce
1/3 cup minced red onion
1 dash salt
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Puree all ingredients in a food processor until smooth. Keep refrigerated for up to 1 week.

Submitter: 
Mott's
Description: 
A new twist on a holiday favorite. The Applesauce adds great flavor and texture.
