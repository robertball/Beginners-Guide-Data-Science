Title:
Turnip Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lg turnip, peeled, cubed
2 Tbl butter
pinch of salt and pepper
2-3 Lg apples, diced
2 Tbl brown sugar
pinch of cinnamon
Topping:
1/4 C flour
1/4 C brown sugar
2 Tbl butter, softened

Directions:
Preheat over to 350. Grease casserole dish. Cook and mash turnip. Add butter, salt, pepper.
Toss apples with 2 Tbl sugar and the cinnamon.
Cover the bottom of casserole dish with some of the turnip mixture, cover with a layer of apple mixture. Continue layering, ending with turnip.
Mix topping ingredients by hand, until it resembles a coarse meal. Sprinkle over casserole.
Bake for 1 hour.
