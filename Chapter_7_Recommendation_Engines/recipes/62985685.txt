Title:
Chicken and Shiitake Marsala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breasts
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
2 - 3.5 oz. packages sliced shiitake mushrooms
1/2 cup Marsala wine
2 scallions, finely chopped - about 1/3 cup - divided
2 tbsp. butter

Directions:
Pound chicken to 1/2 inch thickness.
Heat large skillet over medium high heat and coat with cooking spray.
Sprinkle salt and pepper on chicken and place in pan.
Cook 5-6 minutes on each side, or until done and remove from pan, keeping warm.
Spray pan with cooking spray and add mushrooms.  Cook about 2 minutes or until tender.
Add wine and 3 tbsp. of scallions and cook 30 seconds over high heat.
Reduce heat, add butter and stir until it melts.
Add chicken and drippings to pan, stirring gently.
Serve chicken with sauce spooned over it and sprinkle remaining scallions on top.
