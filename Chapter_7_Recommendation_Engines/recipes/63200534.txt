Title:
Tabbouleh II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup quinoa
1 cup water
2 cups chopped parsley
1/2 cup chopped fresh mint leaves
7 tomatoes, diced
1 large red onion, finely diced
4 teaspoons olive oil
2 lemons, juiced
salt to taste

Directions:
Sprout quinoa in water.
Combine the parsley, mint, tomatoes, onion, olive oil, lemon juice and salt. Add the quinoa; mix well and serve.

Submitter: 
KESSIANNE
Description: 
Such a refreshing salad and it holds nicely in the refrigerator if you want to make it ahead. Quinoa is soaked in water until it 's fluffy and ready to be combined with parsley, mint, tomatoes, and a bit of oil and freshly squeezed lemon juice. Serves four.Modified from: Tabbouleh II
