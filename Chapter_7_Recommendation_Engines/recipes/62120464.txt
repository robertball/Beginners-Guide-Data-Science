Title:
Shredded BBQ Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 2 pounds boneless chicken breast
* 1 tablespoon BBQ dry rub seasoning
* 1 medium onion, chopped
* 4 cloves garlic, crushed
* 2-3 cups chicken broth
* 1 bottle BBQ sauce of your choice
* 8 Kaiser rolls or burger buns

Directions:
1.Season chicken breasts on both sides with the BBQ rub and place in a slow cooker.
2. Add the onion, garlic,and broth(just enought to cover the chicken) to the slow cooker.
3. Cover and cook on High for 6 hours, or until chicken can be easily shredded. Shred the chicken in the slow cooker and add the BBQ sauce.  Slow cook at least 30 more minutes.
4. Serve on Kaiser rolls or hamburger buns.

Description: 
Chicken breast slow cooked in tangy BBQ sauce gives you a tender, delicious sandwich that will be the hit of any potluck or party!
