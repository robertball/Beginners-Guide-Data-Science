Title:
pear and necterine smootie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 peeled necterines
2 peeled pears
2 1/2 cups pure unsweetened apple juice
1 tray ice cubes

Directions:
combine necterines, pears,apple juice and ice in a blender and blend together.

Submitter: 
babybluangel
Description: 
Delicious on a hot summer day.  To make it thinner add more apple juice.
