Title:
Oatstanding Choco-chip Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ¼ cups quick-cooking rolled oats
1 ½ cups boiling water
1 ½ cups flour
¾ cups unpacked brown sugar
¼ cup sugar
1 ½ tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
½ tsp. salt
1 tsp. ground cinnamon
2 egg whites
¼ cup pureed canned peaches (puree canned peaches, without juice, in blender)
¼ cup butter/margarine, melted
1 tsp. vanilla
½ cup mini choc. chips

Directions:
Pour boiling water over oats in medium bowl. Stir and let stand 20 minutes.
Combine flour and next 6 ingedients in a large bowl. Set aside.
In a small bowl, combine egg whites, pureed peaches, butter, and vanilla. Beat with a whisk until well mixed.
Add peach mixture to oats and stir until smooth. Add oat mixture to flour mixture and stir until dry ingredients are moistened. Batter will be thick. Fold in chocolate chips.
Bake at 325 for 25 minutes.
