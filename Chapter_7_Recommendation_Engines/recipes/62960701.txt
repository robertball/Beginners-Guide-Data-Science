Title:
Lighter Homestyle Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lb fresh green beans
2 TBL light margarine
1/4 cup all purpose flour
1-1/2 cups skim milk
1/2 cup nonfat buttermilk
1 TBL Ranch Dressing seasoning mix
2 tsp fresh thyme
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 tsp margarine
4 strips turkey bacon
1 (8 oz) package sliced fresh mushrooms
Cooking spray
1 cup french fried onions
1/2 cup panko bread crumbs
2 roma tomatoes, seeded and chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Cook green beans in boiling water 4 to 6 minutes.  Then immediately plunge the green beans into cold water to stop them from cooking.
Melt margarine in dutch oven and whisk in flour and stir.  Add milk and stir constantly.  Remove from heat.  Add buttermilk and next 4 ingredients.
Melt 1 tsp butter in medium skillet and saute mushrooms.  Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes.  Toss green beans and mushrooms into the buttermilk sauce.
Pour everything in a 9 x 13 pan.  Combine the french fried onions and the next two ingredients.  Sprinkle over the top of the casserole.
Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.
