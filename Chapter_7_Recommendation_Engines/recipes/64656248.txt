Title:
Big Ray's Corn Gazpacho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4551324.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups fresh corn kernels
1 1/2 cup coursley chopped English cucumber plus 1 cup diced peeled English cumber
1 clove, smashed garlic
1/4 cup lie juice
2 Tbsp canola oil
1/2 tsp kosher salt
1 cup halved cherry tomatoes
1 cup diced bell pepper
1 Tbsp minced serrano chili with seeds
Garlic croutons and shredded fresh basil for garnish

Directions:
Bring a medium saucepan of wter to a boil, add the corn and boil for 1 minute.  Drain the corn into a colander and run cold water over it to cool.  Set aside 1 cup of the kernels and in a blender combine the remaining corn, chopped cumber, garlic, lime juice, oil and salt and puree until smooth.
Transfer the puree to a bow land stir in remaining corn, diced cumber, tomatoes, bell pepper and chili.  Taste and adjust seasoning.  Chill for at least 2 hours and up to 2 days ahead before serving.  To serve, divide into 4 bowls and top with croutons and shredded basil.

Description: 
Simple and delicious straight out of the garden, abundant corn on the cob is one of the great things about summer.  The supporting ingredients of this soup are your usual gazpacho players- tomato, cucumbers, peppers come from as well.  A great way to use up that garden bounty.
