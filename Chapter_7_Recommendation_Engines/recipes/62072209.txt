Title:
girly chicken salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 13oz. can chicken breast
3/4 cup mayo
1/2 cup cranberries or grape halves
1/4 cup chopped green onions
1/4 cup sliced almonds
1/4 cup celery chopped
salt to taste
garlic powder to taste

Directions:
Drain then flake chicken.  Stir in mayonaise until blended.  Add cranberries, onion, almonds, celery, salt and garlic.
Excellent served on crescents, wheat bread with sprouts and cream cheese, or even in cream puff shells served as appetizers.

Submitter: 
Sara Slade
Description: 
This is the best chicken salad you may have in your lifetime.  This was a pregnancy invention, and one that hasstuck.  I get many request for this recipe.  My husband though calls it girly chicken salad because it has fruit and other good things in it.
