Title:
Mom's Cabbage Roll Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lrg can tomato juice
1/4 cups brown sugar
1 Tbsp Worcestshire sauce
1/2 cup water (optional)
1 Tbsp cornstarch (optional)

Directions:
Blend tomato juice, brown sugar and worcestshire sauce.
If you wish thicker sauce add a mixture of cornstarch and water.
Heat on stove until boiling then pour over cabbage rools and continue to cook as per normal.

Submitter: 
CLField
Description: 
Best sauce for German-style cabbage rolls. My brother's favourite food!
