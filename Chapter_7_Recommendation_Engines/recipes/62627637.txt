Title:
Pinas Penne a la Vodka Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp butter
1 pkg pancetta
1/3 cup vodka (If you wish to add more..add more...)
5 cups tomato puree ( Remember, if you use one jar of purée, add one jar of water, or one jar of purée and 1 can diced tomatoes and 1/2 jar water)
Chopped parsley
chili pepper flakes
cooking cream ( buy the small quart)
parmeggiano cheese
Penne

Directions:
In pot, brown the pancetta then add the butter. Add the vodka, cook for 1-2 minutes.
Next add your tomato purée, season with salt&pepper, parsley, chili pepper flakes.
Cook until sauce thickens, about an hour.
Lastly add the whipping cream.
Give it a stir, sauce should be pinky, if not pinky enough, add some more cream. Continue to cook for another 10-15 minutes, then add some parmeggiano.
