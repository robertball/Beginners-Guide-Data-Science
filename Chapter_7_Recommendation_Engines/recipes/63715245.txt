Title:
SPICY FRIED CHICKEN Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups buttermilk
1/2 cup Frank's Hot Sauce
1 (4 lb.) frying chicken, cut into pieces
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup panko bread crumbs
1 tsp.  seasoned salt
1 tsp. chili powder
1 tsp. paprika
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. onion powder
1/2 tsp. celery salt
1/2 tsp.  white pepper
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
vegetable oil for frying
butter for frying

Directions:
In a large resealable plastic bag, combine the buttermilk and hot sauce, add the chicken pieces and massage to coat. Remove as much air as possible and seal. Refrigerate at least 30 minutes.
In another plastic bag, combine the flour, bread crumbs, seasoned salt, chili powder, paprika, garlic and onion powders, celery salt, white and cayenne peppers. Shake to mix.
Remove the chicken from the buttermilk a few pieces at a time and place in the flour mixture bag. shake to coat. Place coated chicken on a cooling rack.
Fill a large, covered skillet 1/3-1/2 full with equal parts vegetable oil and butter. Heat to very hot but not smoking. Fry the chicken, covered,  turning occasionally for even cooking, until the skin is browned and crispy and the chicken is cooked through, about 25-30 minutes. Drain the chicken on paper towels and serve.
