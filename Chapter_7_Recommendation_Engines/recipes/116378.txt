Title:
Lemon-Thyme Cornish Hens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/353079.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
1/4 cup lemon juice
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 teaspoons dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/8 teaspoon paprika
2 (1 1/4 pound) Cornish game hens

Directions:
In an bowl, combine the first seven ingredients. Place hens on a rack in a shallow baking pan. Tuck wings under hen. Spoon half of butter sauce over hens. Bake, uncovered, at 425 degrees F for 40-45 minutes or until juices run clear and a meat thermometer reads 180 degrees F, basting occasionally with remaining sauce.
