Title:
Fudge Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup flour
1/1/2 cup buttermilk
1 1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup cocoa
1/4 cup plus 2 Tablespoons soft margarine
2 large eggs
2 teaspoons baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
In a food processor:
Place all ingredients and pulse for 10 seconds
Pour into greased bundt pan
Bake for 40 minutes
Cool.
