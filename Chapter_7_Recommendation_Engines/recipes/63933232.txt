Title:
Big Ray's Pizza Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1090455.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tubes refrigerated pizza crust
1 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 cup chopped pepperoni
1/2 cup warmed spaghetti sauce (optional)

Directions:
On a lightly floured surface, roll dough into a 16-in. x 10-in. rectangle. Sprinkle with cheeses and pepperoni.
Roll up jelly-roll style, starting with a long side. Cut into 2-in. slices. Place cut side down in a greased 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan; lightly press down to flatten.
Bake at 400Â° for 8-10 minutes or until golden brown. Serve with spaghetti sauce if desired.

Description: 
A nice break from making pizza. No down sides to this cheap, fast, easy and delicious recipe! Add whatever other toppings you'd like. But be forewarned, they will go fast so you may want to make a double batch!
