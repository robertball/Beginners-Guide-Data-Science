Title:
Creamy Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/487607.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz. (250 g.) cream cheese
1/4 cup sour cream
3 tbsp. salsa
3 tbsp. salsa verde
3 tbsp. minced fresh onion
2 tsp. fresh lime juice
1-2 jalapenos seeded
1/4 tsp. Tajin (Mexican chili powder with lime)

Directions:
Bring the cream cheese to room temperature.
Add the remaining ingredients.
Stir until well blended.
Let sit about an hour for the flavours to blend.

Submitter: 
TJ
Description: 
I made this cream cheese based dip to go with tortilla chips at a lakeside bbq.  When I checked to see if it was a success, I found the bowl nearly empty and people wiping it with their fingers - I guess you could say finger licking good.
