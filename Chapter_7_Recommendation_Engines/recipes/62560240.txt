Title:
Spritz!!! mmmmm Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535928.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cups Butter
1 cup Sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp Vanilla
1/2 tsp Almond extract
4 cups Sifted Flour
1 tsp Baking Powder

Directions:
Preheat oven 400 degrees
Cream Butter & Sugar
Add Eggs, Vanilla & Almond Extract
Sift Flour & Baking Powder
Add gradually to creamed mixture
Bake 7-8 mins
DO NOT CHILL
