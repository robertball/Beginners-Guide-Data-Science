Title:
Layers of Love Cherry-Choco Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990537.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (21 ounce) cans LUCKY LEAFÂ® Regular or Premium Cherry Fruit Filling
1 (18.25 ounce) package chocolate cake mix, prepared
1 (4 serving size) package instant chocolate pudding mix, prepared
2 cups frozen whipped topping, thawed
Chocolate curls (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Prepare cake mix according to package directions and bake in a 13x9-inch baking pan. Let cake cool completely and cut into 1-inch cubes.
Prepare pudding mix according to package directions. Chill at least 30 minutes. Place half of cake cubes in a 3- to 4-quart trifle bowl. Spoon one can of the LUCKY LEAF Cherry Pie Filling over cake; spread 1 cup pudding over cherries and top with half of whipped topping. Repeat layers.
Top with chocolate curls, if desired. Cover and chill at least 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Lucky Leaf Fruit Filling
Description: 
Chocolate cake cubes, cherries, chocolate pudding, and whipped topping are layered in this elegant dessert.
