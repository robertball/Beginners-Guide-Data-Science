Title:
Brunch Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 thick slice Canadian bacon or ham
1 slice Swiss cheese
1 egg
4 t whipping cream
1 1/2 t grated Parmesan cheese
1 slice toast (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Place Canadian bacon in a greased ramikin or oven proof dish.
Top with Swiss cheese.
Carefully break an egg over the cheese.
Pour cream over egg and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
Bake, uncovered, for 25-30 minutes or until egg reach desired doneness. Let stand for 5 minutes. Serve with toast if desired.
