Title:
beef chop suey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of mushroom soup, 1 can cream of celery soup,1 1/4 cup water, 1 lb. ground beef browned with 1 small yellow onion, 1 cup chopped celery, 3/4 cup rice(uncooked),4 T. scant soy sauce, 1 can undrained bean sprouts1 can buttered mushrooms, chow mein noodles to go on for last 15 minutes baking time.

Directions:
Dump all together and bake at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hrs. Top with chow main noodles and bake for 15 more minutes.

Submitter: 
JAZZY-GIRL
Description: 
This recipe ccame from a German grandmother who loved to experiment in the kitchen. This was one of my favorites as a child.
