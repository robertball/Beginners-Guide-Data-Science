Title:
Three Amigos Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2467251.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Haas avocado, cut into bite-sized pieces
1 mango, cut into bite-sized pieces
1 grapefruit, cut into bite-sized pieces
1/2 lime

Directions:
Place the avocado, mango, and grapefruit into a mixing bowl. Squeeze the lime over the fruit, and stir gently until mixed.

Submitter: 
geekchef
Description: 
Southwestern fruit salad. Great flavors combination and wonderful for dieters. These three fruits blend perfectly together for a special, easy to make salad. Serve with cottage cheese for a complete diet meal. Refrigerate if making ahead, keeps 2 days.
