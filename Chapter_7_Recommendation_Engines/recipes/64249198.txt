Title:
Fruity Curry Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 stalk celery, diced
4 green onions, chopped
1 Golden Delicious apple - peeled, cored and diced
1/3 cup golden raisins
1/3 cup seedless green grapes, halved
1/3 cup Walnuts, chopped small
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon curry powder
1 cup light mayonnaise

Directions:
Cut up raw chicken in chunks and bring to a boil in chicken broth. Boil for just a couple of minutes and then put a lid on, take off stove and let it sit for 15 minutes or so. Then, shred in food processor.
In a large bowl combine the chicken, celery, onion, apple, raisins, grapes and walnuts.
In a separate bowl, mix pepper, curry powder and mayonnaise. Fold into the chicken and other ingredients. Allow to sit in fridge for at least 1 day before serving.
