Title:
Mississippi Sweet Potato Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ounces butter, softened
2 cups cooked and mashed sweet potatoes
2 cups granulated sugar
1 small can (5 ounces, about 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons) evaporated milk
1 teaspoon vanilla
3 eggs, beaten
1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
2 prepared pie shells (shallow)  or 1-deep dish pie shell, unbaked

Directions:
Mix butter, potatoes, sugar and evaporated milk until well blended.
Add vanilla, eggs, and cinnamon; mix well.
Pour into the prepared pie shells. Bake in a 350Â° oven for about 1 hour, until set.
Makes 2 pies.
