Title:
Pinchos Morunos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/685329.jpg
Ingredients:
•3.5 lb. Pork tenderloin, cut into small cubes
•7 garlic gloves, finely chopped
•2 teaspoons salt
•1 tablespoon mild curry powder
•1 1/2 teaspoon coriander seeds
•1 tablespoon Spanish paprika
•3/4 teaspoon dried thyme
•Freshly ground black pepper
•6 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
Preparation
Crush the garlic with the salt in a mortar (or with the flat of a knife on a board), then work in the other ingredients.
Place in bowl and marinate them overnight turning so they are will coated.
Skewer the pork, 3-4 cubes to a wood skewer soaked in water for an hour. Spread the pinchitos out well on a barbecue or on foil under your broiler. Cook them over a high heat for about 3 minutes on each side. Serve hot or at room temp.
