Title:
Karyn's King Ranch Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 1/2 cups chicken broth, divided
1 (10 ounce) can diced Rotel tomatoes
1 can of black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (12 ounce) package corn tortillas
3 cups cooked, diced chicken (rotisserie)
1 onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
3 cups shredded Montrey Jack /Colby cheese
1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon of black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Combine the chicken soup, mushroom soup, chicken broth, Rotel Tomatoes(You choose the heat) and black beans. Set aside. In separate bowl mix pieces of chicken with chili powder, cumin, black pepper and garlic powder.  Lightly grease a shallow 3 quart casserole dish. Dip each Tortilla into 1/2 cup of chicken broth and tear or cut in strips or pieces.  Layer 1/2 the tortillas, 1/2 the chicken, 1/2 the onion, 1/2 the bell pepper and 1/2 the cheese in the casserole dish. Pour 1/2 of the soup mixture over the layers. Repeat the layers of tortillas, chicken, onion and bell pepper; pour the remaining soup mixture over the top, then top with the remaining cheese. Bake for 45 minutes to one hour.

Submitter: 
Karyn Osborne
Description: 
A popular Tex-Mex casserole of chicken with creamy tomato sauce, tortillas, vegetables and Cheddar.Modified from: King Ranch Chicken Casserole III
