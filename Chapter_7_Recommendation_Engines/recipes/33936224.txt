Title:
Chicken and Broccoli Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Chicken breast - large, cut in strips
125g Pasta Rigatoni
100g Mushrooms, finely chopped
50g Broccoli (top florets-no stalks)
1 garlic clove, finely chopped
2tbsp grated parmesan or peccorino
125g cream cheese
1tbsp butter
125ml milk
1/2 onion, finely chopped
salt & pepper

Directions:
Cook pasta per packet instructions and drain
Melt the cream cheese and butter on low heat. Stir until smooth.
Add Milk, veg, parmesan and garlic.
Cook for 2-3 mins, stirring constantly. Add Salt and pepper.
Add the chicken, cook until ready.
Mix in the pasta, stir and serve.

Submitter: 
Oisín Ó Searcóid
Description: 
Lightly creamy and cheesey. Very tasty!
Cook on a low heat throughout for best results. It takes longer, but it's worth it!
