Title:
Short Ribs and Squash Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb short ribs meat, cut small
2 cups flour, for coating
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp sesame oil
2 cups maize pozolero
1 cup onion, chopped
2 cups squash (any variety), cut bite size
1 cup tomatoes,diced
1 mild chili, chopped
2 cups beef broth
2 quarts water
1 tsp salt (or to taste)

Directions:
Mix flour with salt, pepper, and garlic powder in a bowl. Coat short rib meat with flour mixture.
Brown meat in soup pot.
Roast maize in a pan with sesame oil.
Add onion to maize and saute.
Add remaining vegetables and saute lightly.
Combine vegetables with the meat in the soup pot.
Add broth and then water and salt.
Simmer low for one hour.

Submitter: 
Jesse Anne
Description: 
Simple flavors of squash and corn in a hearty stew.
