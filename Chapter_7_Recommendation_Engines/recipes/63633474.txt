Title:
Grilled Curried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breasts
1 TB vegetable oil
2 tsp sweet curry powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp granulated garlic powder
1/8 granulated onion

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine the oil with the curry, salt, garlic and onion. Add the chicken pieces and coat well. Preheat your grill to medium hot.
Lay out the chicken in a single layer once the grill is hot and cook the chicken for about 5 minutes. Turn the chicken and cook an additional 4-5 minutes. Continue cooking until done.
Remove the chicken from the grill and serve with steamed rice and mango chutney.

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
Try this quick and easy grilling recipe for something new and different from your grill!
