Title:
Easy Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/835885.jpg
Ingredients:
4 (0.13 ounce) packages unsweetened strawberry-flavored drink mix powder
2 (46 fluid ounce) cans pineapple juice
3 cups white sugar
4 quarts water
2 liters ginger ale

Directions:
In a large punch bowl combine drink mix, pineapple juice, sugar and water. Stir until dissolved. Stir in the ginger ale.

Submitter: 
Donna
Description: 
I served this at my grandmother's 90th birthday and everyone wanted the recipe. Use any flavor of drink mix. One variation is to freeze the punch overnight and then set it out an hour before the event.
