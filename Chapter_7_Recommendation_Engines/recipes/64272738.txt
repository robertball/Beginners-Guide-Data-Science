Title:
Warm Cheese/Bacon Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. softened cream cheese
2 cups sour cream
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
6 or more slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
1/2 cup sliced green onions

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Combine all ingredients, reserving 2 slices bacon for topping.
Spoon mixture into a quart baking dish.
Bake 25-30 minutes or until cheeses are bubbly.
Serve with bread slices, crackers or veggies.
