Title:
Breakfast Rice from Japan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1tsp vegetable oil
1-2 cups chilled white or brown rice
1-2 eggs
2-4 green onions  white and green parts sliced
Soy sauce

Directions:
Heat oil in frying pan over medium high heat
Add rice, patting flat in pan
When rice is crisping stir 1-2 eggs into rice
Top rice/egg mixture  with green onions just before serving
Season with Soy Sauce

Submitter: 
caquilter
Description: 
Healthy and quick breakfast I learned from friends in Tokyo. Use chilled  white or brown rice. Adjust ingredients for your hunger needs and enjoy!
