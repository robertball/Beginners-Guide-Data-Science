Title:
A&W Root Beer Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/768568.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/3 Cup Granulated Sugar
1 Cup Very Hot Water
1 Cup Corn Syrup
1 tsp McCormick Root Beer Extract

Directions:
Dissolve sugar in hot water.  Add corn syrup and extract and mix well.  Refrigerate until cool.  Add 1/4 Cup syrup to 10 oz. cold soda water.
