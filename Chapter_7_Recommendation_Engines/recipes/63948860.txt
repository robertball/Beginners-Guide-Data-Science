Title:
Cucumber Salad (Korean) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Englash cucumber, halved lengthwise and diagonally sliced thin
1 teaspoon rice vinegar
1 teaspoon dark sesame oil
1 teaspoon low sodium soy sauce
fresh cilantro
1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Combine vinegar, sesame oil and soy sauce in medium bow; add cucumber gently stir.  Sprinkle with cilantro and salt, toss to gently coat.
Let flavors combine while cooking your steak.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
This cucumber-cilantro side dish is to serve alongside any Asian type steak you would grill.
