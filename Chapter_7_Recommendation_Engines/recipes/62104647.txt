Title:
Sport Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1 scoop of Gatoraid powder (approx 1 tbs)
-10 ice cubes
-1/4 cup water
-1 scoop vanilla ice cream
-optional:2 tbs of frozen concentrated juice of your choice

Directions:
Blend the ice cubes and water then add the Gatorade powder, juice concentrate and ice cream then blend again.
If you don't have Gatorade powder, put you're favourite sports drink in the freezer (put in ice cube trays for easy removal)and replace these in the recipe where it calls for the 10 ice cubes. Blend this along with the water, juice concentrate and ice cream.

Submitter: 
J9
Description: 
A sports drink in the form of a smoothie. Great to have in your water bottle on a hot day!
