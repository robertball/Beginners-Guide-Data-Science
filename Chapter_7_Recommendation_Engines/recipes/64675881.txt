Title:
Big Ray's Quinoa Tabbouleh Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4671218.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup quinoa, rinsed
1 can (15 ounces) black beans, rinsed and drained
1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
1 small cucumber, peeled and chopped
4 green onions, chopped
1 small bell pepper, chopped
1/3 cup minced fresh parsley
1/4 cup lemon juice
2 Tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pep

Directions:
In a large saucepan, bring broth to a boil. Add quinoa. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 12-15 minutes or until liquid is absorbed. Remove from the heat; fluff with a fork. Transfer to a bowl; cool completely.
Add the beans, cucumber, tomatoes, onions,bell pepper and parsley. In a small bowl, whisk the remaining ingredients; drizzle over salad and toss to coat. Refrigerate until chilled.

I made a slight modification and substituted quinoa for couscous in this tabbouleh. My wife loves to take the leftovers for to work for lunch.
