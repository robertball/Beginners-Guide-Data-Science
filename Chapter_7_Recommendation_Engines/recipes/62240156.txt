Title:
Irene's Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Tomato Sauce:
2 medium sized Onions
1 stalk Celery
1/2 Green Pepper
2 cloves Garlic (or to taste)
1 16 oz can of Crushed Tomatoes
18 oz of Tomato Paste
18 oz Water
1/2 cup red wine
2 tablespoons Sweet Dry Basil
1/2 teaspoon Oregano
2 tablespoons Sugar
Salt and Pepper to taste
Olive Oil
Meat Sauce:
1 pound Ground Turkey or lean Ground Beef (2 lbs if made without sausage)
1 pound Sausage
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
Olive Oil

Directions:
Finely chop onions, celery, green pepper and garlic.  Saut√© in olive oil until soft.
Add crushed tomatoes and juice, tomato paste, red wine, basil, oregano, and sugar.
Cook on low heat for about an hour, stirring often so that ingredients do not burn on bottom of pot.
Add meat to sauce if desired.
Cook for an additional 30 minutes to blend flavors.
Serve with spaghetti noodles or use with lasagna.
For Meat Sauce, heat olive oil.
Add ground meat in pieces continuing to break into smaller pieces
Salt and pepper to taste.
When meat is browned, drain with slotted spoon and add to sauce.
For sausage, heat olive oil.
Cut sausage into quarter slices and add to olive oil.
When browned, add with slotted spoon to tomato sauce.
