Title:
Great Blueberry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
2 tablespoons cornstarch
1 cup water
4 tablespoons lemon flavored Jell-OÂ® mix
4 cups fresh blueberries
1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, mix together the sugar and cornstarch. Pour in water and bring to a full boil. Turn off heat and add in gelatin; stir until completely dissolved. Let cool.
When mixture is cooled fold in blueberries. Gently pour into the graham cracker crust. Cover and refrigerate for about an hour or until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Nesto
Description: 
This is a superb, easy dessert made with all-fresh blueberries during the peak of their season.  You're gonna love it!  At serving time, garnish with whipped topping. Use the Graham Cracker Crust recipe for the crust!Modified from: Jan's Fresh Blueberry Pie
