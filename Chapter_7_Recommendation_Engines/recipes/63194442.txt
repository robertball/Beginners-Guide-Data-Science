Title:
Beef O's Soup - Gluten Free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c Water
4 Beef Boullion Packets
Shredded Beef Roast Pieces
Large Carrot, sliced
Minced Onion, tbsp
Dash of Minced Garlic
1/2 Celery stalk, diced
3/4 c frozen corn
3/4 c frozen green beans
3/4 c gluten free Schar Anellini noodles

Directions:
Bring water to a boil with Boullion packets, onion, garlic, carrots and beef. Add noodles, celery, corn, and green beans. Boil for 4 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jessica Schultz
Description: 
Easy gluten free beef noodle soup using left-over beef from a roast.
