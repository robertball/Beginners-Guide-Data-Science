Title:
No Steak Sauce Required Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup dry sherry
1/3 cup robusto italian dressing (such as Wishbone)
1/3 cup low sodium soy sauce
1 teaspoon prepared ground ginger
1 teaspoon brown sugar
1 teaspoon prepared minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Add the ingredients into a measuring cup and stir.  Place steaks in zip-lock bag and add marinade.  Marinate 8-12 hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
This is the easiest and most delicious marinade for steaks!  Grill your steaks to perfect doneness and enjoy moist and flavorful beef.  No steak sauce required!
