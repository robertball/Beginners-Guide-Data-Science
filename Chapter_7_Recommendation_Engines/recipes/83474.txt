Title:
South of the Border Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980996.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups Cabbage, shredded
1 cup Radishes, shredded
1 medium Red, Yellow or Orange Bell Pepper, trimmed, seeded and chopped
1 small Sweet Onion, chopped
1/2 cup crushed Tortilla Chips
1/2 cup Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
1/2 cup Salsa mild, medium or hot

Directions:
In large bowl, combine Cabbage, Radishes, Pepper, Onion and Tortilla Chips; toss gently.
In small bowl, combine Marzetti(R) Slaw Dressing and Salsa; mix until well blended.  Add dressing to vegetables, mix and serve.  Garnish with additional crushed Tortilla Chips.

Submitter: 
Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Description: 
With just a hint of Salsa, this slaw is a tasty accompaniment to grilled Fish and Chicken.
