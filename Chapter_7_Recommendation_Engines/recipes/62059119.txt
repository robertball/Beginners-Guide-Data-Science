Title:
Mega Garlic Mushroom Chicken Saute' Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breast
10 cloves of garlic minced or chopped
2 (small) cans of mushrooms
1 1/2 tbs parsley
1 tbs of garlic salt
1/2 onion minced
2 1/2 tbs of olive oil
1/2 tbs of worceshire sauce
1 1/2 cup of chicken broth

Directions:
Warm Large Saute' Pan on medium heat with 2 1/2 tbs of olive oil. Wait until oil is hot add minced garlic, mushrooms, parsley, onions, & garlic salt. Saute for 2 minutes. Add chicken breast (make sure there is not to much between bottom of pan and chicken.  Add Worceshire Sauce. add chicken broth after 2nd side is white in color (7-8 min after)Cook on each side for 10 - 12 minutes. Stir around ingredients occasionally.
