Title:
Savory Rice & Quinoa Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups basmati rice
1 cup quinoa
6 cups broth
1 t salt
1 t pepper
1/2 t sage
1/2 t allspice
1 T chopped parsley
tomatoes chopped (optional)
1/4 c raisins (optional)

Directions:
Bring the broth to a boil and add all the ingredients. Reduce heat to simmer for 20 minutes - done!
Freeze left overs for a quick side dish ready in minutes.
