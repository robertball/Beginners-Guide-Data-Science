Title:
Lemongrass Chicken w/ pan gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lemon grass stalks
3 garlic
1 shallot
4 seeded hot chilies
1 T sugar
1/2 t salt
4 T fish sauce
3 lb roasting chicken
1/4 c veg oil
1/2 c onion
BASTING SAUCE
2 T peanut oil
1 T lime juice
1/4 t salt
pan juices
1/4 carrot
1/4 c celery
1 T flour
1 1/2 c chicken broth

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400*
Make a paste with first 7 ingredents. Rub 1/2 paste under skin, and remaning on skin.
Roast for 40 min breast side up, trussed, on a rack.
Mix together basting juice and pour over chicken.
Add veggies, roast for 30 min., basting occasionaly.
Let stand 15 min before carving.
Place roasting oan on stove top and brown veggies. Pour off all but 2 T fat, add flour and cook for 5 min. Whisk in brothand simmer 12 min., strain through a sieve.
