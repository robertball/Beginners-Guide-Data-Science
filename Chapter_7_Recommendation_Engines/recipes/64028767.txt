Title:
Spumoni Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 1/2 cups sugar
3/4 cup butter - melted
Pinch of salt
5 eggs - lightly beaten
1/2 tsp. vanilla
11 oz. evaporated milk
3 cups flour
3 tsp. baking powder
3 drops green food coloring
3/4 cup chopped walnuts
3 drops red food coloring
Small jar of maraschino cherries - chopped
1 1/2 squares of unsweetened chocolate - melted
Frosting:
8 oz. package of cream cheese - softened
3/4 stick of butter - softened
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Cake:
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine sugar with melted butter and salt.
Add beaten eggs, vanilla and evaporated milk.
Sift flour, add baking powder and add to egg mixture, blending well.
Divide batter into 3 parts.
Add red food coloring and cherries to 1 portion.
Add green food coloring and walnuts to another portion.
To the third portion, add the melted chocolate.
Grease a 13x9 baking pan.
Put green batter in first, then red batter, then chocolate batter.
Bake 35-40 minutes or until cake tests done.
Cool in pan.
For frosting:
Mix cream cheese and butter until smooth.
Add vanilla then gradually add powdered sugar.
Spread over cooled cake and top with chopped walnuts if desired.
