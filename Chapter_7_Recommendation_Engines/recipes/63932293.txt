Title:
Shelly Hospitality's Bake-A-Filling Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1090227.jpg
Ingredients:
Cake:
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1 cup butter
2 eggs, separated
2 1/2 cups Pillsbury's Best Flour*
(Regular or Instant Blending)
1 1/2 cups applesauce
1/3 cup instant butterscotch pudding mix
3/4 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup ready-to-use diced dates
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon cinnamon
Filling:
1/3 cup butterscotch instant pudding mix
1/2 cup milk
Glaze:
1 cup confectioners sugar
3 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons lemon juice

Directions:
Oven 350°                       Two 9-inch layers
Grease bottoms of two 9-inch round layer pans, line with waxed paper and grease again.
Gradually add brown sugar to 1/2 cup butter in large mixer bowl, creaming until light and fluffy.  Add egg yolks, 1/2 cup flour, 1/2 cup applesauce, pudding mix and 1/4 teaspoon salt; mix well. stir in dates and walnuts.  Pour into pans.
In large mixer bowl, combine 2 cups flour, 1/2 teaspoon salt, sugar, baking soda and cinnamon.  Cut in 1/2 cup butter.  Add 1 cup applesauce and egg whites. Beat 3 minutes at medium speed of mixer until well blended. Pour batter over filling in pans, spreading carefully to cover.
Bake at 350° for 45 minutes until cake springs back when lightly touched in center. Cool 10 minutes. Remove from pans carefully onto wire cooling racks.
Mix 1/3 cup butterscotch instant pudding mix with 1/2 cup milk. Spread between bottom layers. Place bottoms together. (Variation: Substitute 1 cup (6 ounce package) butterscotch pieces, melted, for the instant butterscotch pudding mix.)
Combine 1 cup confectioners sugar, 3 tablespoons butter and 2 tablespoons lemon juice until the consistency of a glaze.
While warm, frost with glaze allowing to drip down sides.

Submitter: 
Shelly Hiddleson
Description:

Below is her recipe. Yummy memories of this cake at family parties, enjoy!
