Title:
Perfect Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 lime juiced
4 avocados peeled pitted and mashed
2 tbsp. diced onion
2 tbsp. chopped cilantro
1 plum tomato diced
1 tsp. minced garlic
2 dashes red pepper

Directions:
In a medium bowl add the salt and lime juice. Add the avocado and mash. Add onion, cilantro, tomato, garlic and red pepper and mix. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Jordan VanderLaan
Description: 
The trick with Guacamole is the proportions. This is about as closes as I've come to perfection.
