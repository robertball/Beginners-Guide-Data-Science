Title:
Dog Vomit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound chicken, or fish diced and grilled  or leftover peices
2 tbsp margarine or butter
2 tsp chicken oxo in a mug
3 tbsp corn starch
1 cup milk
1.5 cup hot water
pepper to taste
2 tbsp curry powder or to taste

Directions:
grill, fry, or use left over chicken, or fish
Melt margarine in saucepan, remove from heat
Immediately add chicken oxo, corn starch, pepper and curry powder.
Whisk together.
Put back on heat, and slowly add water and milk.
Heat on medium low while whisking until thickened, and lightly bubbling(to make sure its finished thickening).
If too thick, add small amounts of milk until desired thickness.  If too thin, mix 1 tsp of corn starch with 1 tsp of water, add, and simmer til thicker, repeat until desired thickness.
When sauce is ready, pour over chicken and fish peices.  Should be bright yellow, hence the recipe title.
Pour over rice, or noodles.
