Title:
Eggs Bene-Chick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/729280.jpg
Ingredients:
2 light English muffin
4 large eggs
4 slices extra-lean ham
4 tablespoons fat-free mayonnaise
4 teaspoon Hellmann's/Best Food Dijonnaise
4 teaspoon light buttery spread, softened
4 teaspoons plain yogurt with squirt of lemon juice

Directions:
To make sauce, combine mayo, Dijonnaise, butter, and yogurt. Set aside.
Crack eggs, poach in egg poacher.
Heat ham and toast muffins.
Heat sauce in microwave for about 20 seconds and give it a stir (add water for a thinner sauce).
Top muffin with ham and egg, then cover with sauce.
Serve and enjoy!
