Title:
Sausage and Corn side dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbs butter
1 medium shallot, finely diced
2 cloves garlic
1/2 tsp onion powder
1 lb. Smoked sausage, diced into 1/4" thickness
1 16 oz can corn (wtih juices)
1 14.5 oz can chicken broth
3 TBS Parmesan cheese
1/3 cup cubed cheddar cheese

Directions:
Melt butter in 10 inch non-stick skillet over medium heat
Add shallot, garlic, onion powder and sausage
Cook until sausage starts to brown
Add corn and chicken broth
Let softly boil until 1/4 cup liquid remains
Remove skillet from heat
Add Parmesan and cheddar cheese
cover skillet until cheese melts
Serve over rice, noodles, or all by itself.

Submitter: 
Scott Simmons
Description: 
This side dish is SO addicting, you might just turn it into a main dish.
