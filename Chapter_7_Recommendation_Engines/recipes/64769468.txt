Title:
Spanish Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb hamburger meat
1 green pepper, chopped
1 onion, diced
1 pkg egg noodles
1 tbsp chili sauce
2 small cans Tomato paste
1 lg jar sliced mushrooms
16 oz shredded cheddar cheese
1 small can sliced black olives, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Brown hamburger meat with green pepper and onion, then drain off grease.
Boil the noodles per package directions.
Add mushrooms, tomato paste and chili sauce to the hamburger and simmer 10 minutes.
Combine cooked noodles and meat mixture then spread into a 9 X 13 baking dish.
Bake for 20-30 minutes.
Cover with cheese and black olives and bake until cheese is melted.

Description: 
Mom's recipe that she use to make. Serve with garlic bread and corn.
