Title:
Shellie's Carne Guisada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1044861.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lbs stew meat (small cubed)
1 small sweet onion, diced
4 large garlic, minced
1 beef bouillon cube
2 tbs oil
2 tbs carne guisada seasoning (Fiesta Brand)
small flour tortillas
shredded mexican cheese
fresh cilantro, chopped
taco sauce
2-3/4 cups water

Directions:
In a large pan add oil, onions and garlic over medium heat.
Add beef bouillon cube and crush until mixed completely.
Stir well and allow onions to soften and brown (about 4 minutes)
Add stew meat and brown.
Add 2 cups of water, lower heat to simmer and cover pan.
Allow beef to simmer until tender (about 2 hours).
Mix 3/4 cups of water with carne guisada seasoning. Add to pan of meat.
remove lid and turn heat up to medium. Let sauce thicken (about 15 minutes).
Serve with warm tortillas, taco sauce, shredded cheese and cilantro.

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
This is so EASY and practically cooks itself. Simple and delicious recipe. Also freezes well.
