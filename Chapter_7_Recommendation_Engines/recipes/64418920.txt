Title:
Cedar Plank Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 pounds pork ribs
Apple cider vinegar
1 clove garlic
1 tbsp olive oil
Yellow prepared mustard
Your favorite dry rub
Your favorite BBQ sauce

Directions:
Soak the plank in apple cider vinegar for 1 hour
Cut white membrane off back of ribs
Coat back ribs with mustard
Sprinkle and pat rub over entire back ribs
Dry plank, cut garlic in half and rub top side of plank
Lightly coat topside of plank with olive oil
Lay rub coated back ribs meat side down on plank
Prepare barbeque for indirect grilling (no heat under plank)
Place plank on grill and close lid
Cook on low heat 230-250°F (110-120°C) for 3 to 4 hours
Turn ribs over after first hour and lightly spray with apple cider
Spray apple cider every half hour for flavour and to keep ribs moist
20 minutes before serving, brush BBQ sauce on back ribs
Turn up the indirect heat a little to help caramelize the sauce on the back ribs

Description: 
A rib recipe you will really love. The cedar plank infuses great flavor into the ribs.
