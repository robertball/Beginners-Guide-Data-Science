Title:
Grandma Westcott's Chocolate Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1078642.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust:
1-1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter - melted
Chocolate filling:
3 cups whole milk
1 cup sugar
1/8 tsp salt
3 Tbs cornstarch
2 eggs
1/3 cup cocoa
2 tsp. vanilla extract

Directions:
Crust: In a 9" x 2" glass pie dish mix graham cracker and sugar. Add butter and mix evenly. Press mixture firmly across pie dish and up sides to cover entire pie dish and sides. Set aside.
Chocolate filling: Mix all ingredients EXCEPT for vanilla together with a blender or mixer. Pour into a pot and cook over medium-high heat stirring constantly until boiling. Allow mixture to boil for one minute. Take off heat and stir in vanilla extract. Pour into crust and cover with cling wrap immediately. Let cool on counter to room temperature. Place in fridge overnight.

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
This is a tradition that we make every holiday and is so delicious! Rich cocoa flavor, deep brown in color and creamy delicious. Taste's great with homemade whip cream.
