Title:
Beef Chili in Crock Pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp oil
1 lb stew meat cut into small chunks
1 Medium onion chopped into small pieces
1 small green or red pepper chopped into small pieces
1 clove garlic minced
1 8 oz can of tomatoes drained and chopped
1 can kidney beans
1 can pork and beans
2 Tbsp soya sauce
1 Tbsp worchesershire sauce
1 1/2 Tbsp chili powder
1/2 tsp each cumin and Thyme

Directions:
Brown stew meat in oil put in Crock pot
cook onion in oil about 10 minutes add to Crock pot
Add all other ingredients to crock pot and stir well
Cook on High 6 hours (or low overnight)
