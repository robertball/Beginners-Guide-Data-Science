Title:
Cream of Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. margarine
2 Tbsp. onion, chopped
3 Tbsp. flour
2 tsp. sugar
1 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
dash of garlic salt, basil, oregano, thyme
2 cups tomato juice
2 cups cold milk
3/4 cup cheese, grated or in small cubes (optional)

Directions:
Saute margarine and onion.
Blend in flour, sugar, salt, pepper, and spices.
Remove from heat.  Gradually stir in tomato juice.
Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.  Boil 1 minute.
Stir in milk.
Heat almost to boiling and serve.
Add cheese to each bowl if desired.

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
This is great comfort food on a cold winter evening.
