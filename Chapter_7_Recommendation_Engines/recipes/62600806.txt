Title:
Spinach salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
DRESSING:
1/2 c. olive oil
2 T. lemon juice
2 T. vinegar
1/2 tsp. each paprika, tarragon, and salt
1 small clove garlic, crushed
1 tsp. Dijon
SALAD:
4 oz. thin-sliced mushrooms
5 pieces bacon, crumbled (or Fakin bakin)
grated parmesan
1 # fresh spinach torn in bite-sized pieces
4 hard-boiled eggs, in wedges

Directions:
Whisk together dressing ingredients. Mis salsd ingredients.  Heat dressing and pour over spinach to wilt.  Sprinkle with bacon, parmesan, and pepper.

Susie
Description: 
great dressing for any salad; still good without wilting the spinach.

(Cooking time is only for the eggs)
