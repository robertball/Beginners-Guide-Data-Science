Title:
Almond Cheddar Appetizers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
2 tsp worchestershire
2 or 3 green onions, finely chopped
1 cup shredded old cheddar cheese
3/4 cup finely chopped silvered almonds
6 bacon strips, cooked & crumbled
1 loaf baguette

Directions:
Mix everything except baguette in bowl.
Cut baguette into 1 to 2 inch slices.
Spread mixture onto sliced bread.
Bake at 375 for approx 8-10 minutes.
Can be frozen after baking

Submitter: 
FRIENDLEE
Description: 
Not sure where this originated from, but I always get plenty of compliments when I make this easy and delicious appetizer!
