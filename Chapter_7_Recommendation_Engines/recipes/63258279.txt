Title:
Blueberry Yogurt Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup skim or lowfat milk
1/2 cup fat free plain or vanilla yogurt
1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries
1-2 tsp honey
Ice (optional)

Directions:
Blend milk, yogurt and blueberries in a blender. Sweeten with honey as desired. Add ice for a frosty treat.
