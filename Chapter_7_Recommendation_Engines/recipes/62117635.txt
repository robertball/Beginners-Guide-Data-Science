Title:
Croque Monsieur/Madame Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices of white sandwich bread
2 slice of ham
3 slices of cheese
butter
egg (for croque madame)

Directions:
Butter a slice of sandwich bread, add a slice of ham and a slice of semi-soft cheese and finally cover with another slice of sandwich bread. Put in oven for 10 minutes and serve right away.
For Croque Madame, just add an egg on the bread.

Submitter: 
dasha
Description: 
I love Paris and speaking french fluently I have been there a number of times. Croque monsieur and croque madame are traditional snacks in France. They are really easy and quick to make and taste great!!
