Title:
So-Lo Baked Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6	small apples
16 oz	sugar free fruit soda

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Wash apples and cut in half.
Remove core and stem (do not peel)
Place cut side down in baking dish.
Pour soda into dish.
Bake about one hour or until tender.
Divide into 6 equal portions.

Submitter: 
Candy
Description: 
Easy baked apple recipe that is very low in sodium. Great desert for people who are on a strict lo-so diet.
