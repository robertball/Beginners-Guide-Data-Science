Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound chicken, boiled and cut up
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can green chili peppers
1 cup sour cream
1 package corn tortilla
1 pound cheese, jack, cheddar or pepper jack

Directions:
Mix soups, sour cream, chilies and chicken  together.  Put half in bottom of 9x13 pan.  Cut up tortillas. Put 1/2 of tortillas on top of soup mix.  Put 1/2 of cheese on tortillas. Layer remaining soup, tortillas and top with cheese.    Cook covered at 350 for 30-40 minutes or until warm.  Uncover for last 10 minutes to brown.
