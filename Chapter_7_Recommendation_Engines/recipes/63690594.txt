Title:
Restaurant Style Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1026030.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can (28 ounce) Whole Tomatoes with Juice
2 cans (10 ounce) Rotel -diced Tomatoes & Green    Chilies
1/4 cup Chopped Onion
1 clove Garlic, minced
1 whole Jalapeno, quartered, deseeded and diced
1/4 teaspoon Sugar
1/4 teaspoon Salt
1/4 teaspoon Ground Cumin
1/2 cup Cilantro (more to taste if desired)
1/2 whole Lime Juice

Directions:
Combine whole tomatoes, Rotel, onion, jalapeno, garlic, salt, sugar, cumin, lime juice and cilantro in a blender or food processor.  Pulse until you get the salsa to the consistency you like.  About 10 - 15 pulses.  Test seasoning and adjust as needed. Refrigerate salsa for at least an hour.

Quick and easy salsa that is packed with great flavor.
