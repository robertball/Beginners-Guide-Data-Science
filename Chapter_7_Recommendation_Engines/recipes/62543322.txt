Title:
Angela's Slow Cooker Tin Can Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/525282.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound fully cooked kielbasa or your favorite link sausage.  (cut into 1/4 inch slices)
1 can(15oz) Ranch Style beans,do not drain
1 can(16oz)kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 can(15 1/2oz)great northern beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (15oz)black beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (15oz) tomato sauce
1 can (15oz) Le Sueur Brand whole baby carrots
1 small onion, sliced into rings
1 can beef broth (+/- for desired consistancy)
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 tsp chili powder

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients in slow cooker.  Cook on low for 4 hours or until beans and carrots are tender, but not mushy.  Serve with corn bread or garlic bread and a tossed salad.

Submitter: 
Angela Naumann
Description: 
This hearty stew is the perfect combination of your favorite beans and link sausage.  It has a rich tomato base that brings all the flavors together. It uses canned ingredients for easy preparation.  I like to  prepare it in a slow cooker for a delicious easy week night meal.
