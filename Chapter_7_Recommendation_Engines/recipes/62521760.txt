Title:
Chocolate chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c+2 T sifted flour
1/2 t baking soda
1/2 t salt
1/2 c sugar
1/4 c light brown sugar
1 egg
1 t vanilla
1/2 c soft butter or margarine
6 oz chocolate chip morsels
6 oz semi-sweet chocolate morsels

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 375
2.  Sift flour/soda/salt into large bowl
3.  Add sugars,egg,vanilla, and butter.  Mix w/ wooden spoon or electric mixer at medium speed until well blended- about  1 minute.
4.  Stir in chocolate morsels
5.  Drop by teaspoonfuls, about 2 inches apart, onto ungreased cookie sheets.
6.  Bake 10-12 minutes, or until golden.  Remove to wire rack to cool
