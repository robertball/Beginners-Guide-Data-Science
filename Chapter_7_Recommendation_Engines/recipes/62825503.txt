Title:
Brown Beauty Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/670377.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white flour
1/2 cup oatmeal
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 to 3/4 teaspoon salt
2 Tablespoons sugar
1 cup buttermilk
1 egg
3 tablespoons oil

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients
Add buttermilk, oil and egg
Bake on medium low grill

Submitter: 
Rebecca Joy Mahoney
