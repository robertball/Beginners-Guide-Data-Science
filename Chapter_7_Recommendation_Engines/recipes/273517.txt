Title:
Skinny White Chocolate Coconut Iced Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4548401.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cold coffee
3/4 cup coconut milk beverage (such as Silk速)
1 tablespoon sugar-free white chocolate-flavored syrup (such as Torani速)
1 tablespoon sugar-free coconut-flavored syrup (such as Torani速)
1 packet stevia sugar substitute (such as Truvia速)

Directions:
Pour coffee in ice cube tray(s); place in freezer until frozen solid, 2 to 4 hours.
Combine coffee cubes, coconut milk, white chocolate syrup, coconut syrup, and sugar substitute in a blender; blend until icy and frothy.
