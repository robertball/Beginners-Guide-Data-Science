Title:
Garlic Spinach Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2060314.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups crushed seasoned stuffing
1 cup finely chopped onion
4 eggs, lightly beaten
3/4 cup butter, melted
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 garlic clove, minced
1 1/2 teaspoons dried thyme
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the first nine ingredients. Stir in spinach until blended. Roll into 1-in. balls. Place in a greased 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees F for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.

Amy Hornbuckle
