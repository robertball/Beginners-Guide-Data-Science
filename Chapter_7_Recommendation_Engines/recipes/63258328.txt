Title:
Backstage Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 1oz packet ranch dressing & seasoning mix
2 TBL water
2 onions
6 Kaiser rolls
1/2 cup bbq sauce

Directions:
Heat grill until coals are red.
While waiting for the charcoal, combine ground beef, ranch & 2 TBL water in large bowl. Form the mixture into 6 burger patties.
Slice the onions into 1/2" slices.
Place the burger patties & onion slices onto the hot grill. Cook for about 4 minutes or until meat is firm around the outside and juices start to pool.
Flip the burgers & onion slices and grill for another 3 minutes or until meat is done to your liking.
Serve the burgers and onion slices (cut in half if too large) on a roll brushed with bbq sauce.
