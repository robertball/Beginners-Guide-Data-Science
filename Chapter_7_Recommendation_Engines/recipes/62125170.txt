Title:
Seafood and Wildrice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T chopped bell pepper
4 T chopped onion
4 T melted butter
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
2 T lemon juice
4 cups cooked wildrice
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
1 tsp dried mustard
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 lb cubed medium cheese
2 lb seafood (shrimp, crab, or combination)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Spray 13 x 9 dish with cooking spray
Saute bell pepper and onion in butter
Combine remaining ingredients and place in baking dish
Bake 30-35 minutes

A delicious combination of seafood, rice and cheese.
