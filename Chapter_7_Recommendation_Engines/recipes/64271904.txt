Title:
Buffalo Wing Shrimp Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1876199.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1/2 teaspoon grated or minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 lb. large peeled shrimp, patted dry
2 Tbsp. Frank's hot sauce
2 Tbsp. unsalted butter
1 1/2 teaspoons brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon cider vinegar
1/8 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 cup chopped celery
Small romaine lettuce leaves for serving
Ranch sauce

Directions:
Combine olive oil, garlic, chilli powder and salt in a bowl. Add shrimp and toss to coat. Thread onto metal or soaked wooden skewers.
Combine Frank's, butter, sugar, vinegar and Worcestershire in a small pot over medium-low heat. Stir until butter s need and ingredients are combined. Set aside.
Grill shrimp 1-2 minutes on BBQ (on each side). Toss shrimp with pepper sauce and chopped celery, pile into lettuce leaves and drizzle with ranch sauce.
