Title:
SmartPot Cheese Cake Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
Crust
10 graham cracker sheets ( or about 1 full package )
4 tbsp melted butter
pinch of salt
2 tsp brown sugar
Cheesecake batter
2/3 cup sugar
2 tbsp corn starch
2 pinches salt
16 oz cream cheese
1/2 cup sour cream
2 tsp vanilla
2 eggs
Topping
2  "big huge"  tbsp sour cream
2 tsp sugar.

Directions:
Pressure cook 26 min
Rest 10 min
Refrigerate at least 4 hours ~ even better overnight
