Title:
Susan's Hot Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans (large, 14 oz) artichoke hearts (non-marinated, drained and coarsely chopped)
1 - 1/3 cup mayo
1/2 teaspoon celery salt
1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
1/2 cup sour cream or cream cheese (4 oz) - cream cheese preferred.
1 cup parmesan Cheese, grated
Extras can add:
jalapenos - half to one depending on spicy taste
garlic - 1 clove minced or spoonful of premade
sprinkle paprika on top

Directions:
Mix all together well and bake at 350 degrees for approx. 25 min til bubbly and slightly browned.
can split the recipe into 2 smaller dishes or full recipe fits nicely in a ceramic pie pan.
Extras can add:
jalapenos - half to one depending on spicy taste
garlic - 1 clove minced or spoonful of premade
sprinkle paprika on top
