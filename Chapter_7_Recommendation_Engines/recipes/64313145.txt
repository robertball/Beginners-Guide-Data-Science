Title:
Luscious Lime Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 teaspoon oil, olive, extra-virgin
1 tablespoon cilantro, fresh chopped
1 1/2 teaspoon lime juice plus 1 tablespoon
1/4 teaspoon garlic minced
1/2 small scallion(s) (green onions) chopped
1 1/2 tablespoon hoisin sauce
1 pinch pepper, white
1/2 pounds shrimp, large
1/2 tablespoon pepper(s), red, bell minced
2 leaves lettuce, bibb to line plates

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk the olive oil, cilantro, lime juice, garlic, green onion, hoisin sauce and white pepper together to make a marinade.
Peel, devein, and clean the shrimp, rinse under cold water, and pat dry.
Heat 1 tablespoon of the marinade in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the shrimp and cook until pink, 2 to 3 minutes. Transfer the skillet contents to the marinade bowl, add the bell pepper, and toss.
Cover the bowl and chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes. To serve place a Bibb lettuce leaf on each of 2 serving plates, spoon the shrimp with some bell pepper and marinade onto the lettuce.
