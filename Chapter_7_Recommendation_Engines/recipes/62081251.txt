Title:
PERFECT PARTY PUNCH Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 46 OZ CAN GOOD QUALITY PINEAPPLE JUICE
1 CAN FROZEN CONCENTRATED PINEAPPLE-ORANGE JUICE
2 LITER GINGER ALE
1 LITER CARBONATED FRUIT PUNCH DRINK (LIKE TAHITIAN TREAT, BUT GENERIC WORKS FINE)
1 LITER PEACH FLAVOURED SODA
1 GALLON OF RAINBOW SHERBET

Directions:
POUR FROZEN JUICE IN THE BOTTOM OF A PUNCH BOWL AND ADD DRINKS, ENDING WITH THE GINGER ALE. STIR WITH SPOON/LADLE TO BE SURE FROZEN CONCENTRATE IS MIXED IN. ADD LARGE DOLLOPS OF SHERBERT TO THE FINISHED PUNCH, AS MUCH OR AS LITTLE AS YOU LIKE.

Submitter: 
TIANA1T
Description: 
I MAKE THIS PUNCH FOR ALL OF OUR GATHERINGS. IT'S QUICK, EASY, CHEAP, AND DELICIOUS. YOU CAN EVEN LEAVE OUT THE SHERBERT AND IT IS STILL GREAT.
