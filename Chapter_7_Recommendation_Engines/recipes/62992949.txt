Title:
Chex® Holiday Party Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup butter
1 Tablespoon pumpkin pie spice
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
4 teaspoons vanilla
3 cups Cinnamon Chex® cereal
3 cups Wheat Chex® cereal
3 cups Honey Nut Chex® cereal
1 cup pecans
1 cup peanuts
1 pkg. Sugar Babies
1 pkg. M&M;'s
2 cups small pretzels

Directions:
In small microwave-safe dish, microwave sugar, butter,spices and vanilla until butter is melted.  In large bowl, mix all ingredients except candies. Pour butter mixture over cereal mixture, stirring until evenly distributed.
Pour butter mixture over cereals and stir until coated.
Pour onto large baking pans or roaster and bake at 250° for one hour, stirring every 15-20 minutes. Turn off oven,leaving pans in oven until cools.  Store in airtight container.  Makes great hostess gifts.
