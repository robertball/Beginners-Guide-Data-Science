Title:
KFC Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 head cabbage, shredded
2 T carrots, shredded
2-4 T sugar (depending on taste)
3-4 T milk (mix of buttermilk & regular is great)
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 T white vinegar
1 T lemon juice (lime juice is nice too)
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Shred cabbage and carrot;  Mix lightly and dust with 1 T of sugar;  Set in refrigerator and chill 1 hour;
Combine all else and mix well.  Taste and add more sugar if desired;  Pour over cabbage mixture and chill before serving
