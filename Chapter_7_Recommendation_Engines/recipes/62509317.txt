Title:
Banana Split Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C graham cracker crumbs
1/2 C butter, melted
1 (8 oz.) package cream cheese
2 C powdered sugar
4 large bananas
1 (20 oz.) can crushed pineapple, drained
1 C whipping cream, whipped with sugar to taste
8 medium strawberries, halved
1/4 C pecans, chopped (optional)
1 T chocolate syrup

Directions:
Combine graham cracker crumbs and melted butter.
Press evenly and firmly into the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking pan.
In a med. bowl, combine cream cheese and sugar; blend until smooth. Spoon evenly over crumb crust.
Slice bananas and arrange on top of cream cheese layer.
Spoon the drained crushed pineapple over bananas.
Spread the whipped cream over pineapple.
Arrange the strawberry halves in rows across the tops.
If desired, sprinkle with nuts. Drizzle with chocolate syrup.
Cover and refrigerate 4-8 hours.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
Cool and creamy. This is a great summer dessert.
