Title:
Mexican Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/356642.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 onion, finely chopped
1 teaspoon garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chiles
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
1/2 cup hot water
1 (11 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1 (15 ounce) can seasoned black beans, undrained
2 (8.5 ounce) package corn muffin mix
1/2 cup sliced green onions
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Spray a 9x13 inch baking dish with cooking spray.
Place the beef in a skillet over medium heat. Cook until beef is evenly brown; drain grease. Add onion, season with garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix in the tomatoes, and cook 5 minutes. Stir in the taco seasoning, water, corn and black beans. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and continue cooking 5 minutes, until thickened. Transfer to the prepared baking dish.
Prepare the corn muffin mix according to package directions, add green onions. Spread evenly over the beef mixture in the baking dish.
Bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until puffed and golden. Top with Cheddar cheese and bake 5 minutes or until cheese melts.
