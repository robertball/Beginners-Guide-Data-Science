Title:
Apple Crumble Pie-Double this and use for Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (9 inch) deep dish pie crust
5 cups apples - peeled, cored and thinly sliced
1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
l teaspoon vanilla
TOPPING;
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon vanilla
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
8 tablespoons butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix apple slices in bowl with  1/2 cup sugar, 1/2 cup brown sugar,cinnamon, and vanilla.
Cover with chopped walnuts.
CRUST;
Mix 1/2 cup sugar, 1/2 cup brown sugar,l teaspoon cinnamon, and l teaspoon vanilla with flour.
Cut in butter until crumbly. Spoon mixture over apples.
Bake in preheated oven until apples are soft and top is lightly browned, about 60 minutes.
