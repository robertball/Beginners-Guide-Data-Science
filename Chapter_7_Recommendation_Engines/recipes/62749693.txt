Title:
Full Bodied Ham and Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pound dry great Northern beans
8 cups chicken stock
1 ham hock
2 cup chopped carrots
6 stalk celery, chopped
2 cup chopped onion
2 teaspoon minced garlic/or more to taste
2 teaspoon mustard powder
4 bay leaves
2-4 cups chopped ham
1 tbs herbs provence
2 teaspoon ground white pepper

Directions:
Rinse the beans, sorting out any broken or discolored ones. In a large pot over high heat, bring the chicken stock to a boil. Add the beans and remove from heat. Let beans sit in the hot water for at least 60 minutes.
After the 60 minutes of soaking, return the pot to high heat and place the ham bone, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, mustard and bay leaves in the pot. Stir well, bring to a boil, reduce heat to low and simmer for 60 more minutes.  Then remove about 1/3 of the mixture, season with herbs provence and set aside. Remove ham bone and discard. Stir in the chopped ham.  Using blender or immersion blender puree reserved soup and return to pot and simmer for 30 more minutes or longer. Season with ground white pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Sandy Wolf Pardington
Description: 
This is a hearty ham, white bean soup using chicken stock for extra flavor.  Chop veggies into bite size pieces.Modified from: Basic Ham and Bean Soup
