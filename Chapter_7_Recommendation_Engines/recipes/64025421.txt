Title:
Herbed Basmati Rice-Vermicelli Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T butter
1 rice cooker measure basmati rice
1/2 c finely broken angel hair pasta
1/2 c chopped onion
Chicken broth
1/2 t salt
1/2 t black pepper
1 T snipped fresh parslety
1/2 snipped fresh thyme
1/4 c currants
1/4 c craisins

Directions:
In a saucepan, melt butter.
Add onions and pasta and cook until browned.
Fold in rice and stir for 2-3 minutes.
Transfer mixture to rice cooker.
Add salt and pepper.
Add chicken broth to the "2" line and start
When rice is done, fluff and fold in parsley, thyme, currants and craisins.
