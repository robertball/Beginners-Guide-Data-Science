Title:
Bhut Jolokia Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp olive oil
20 Bhut Jolokia peppers
5 garlic cloves
1 small yellow cooking onion
3/4 tsp salt
1 cup water
1/8 cup distilled white vinegar
1 tsp sugar

Directions:
Finely mince to liquefy peppers, garlic, and onion in a blender or food processor.
Heat oil in a large saucepan to medium high heat and add peppers, garlic, onion, and salt. Stir frequently for 5 min to a smooth consistency.
Add water and cook 20 minutes to a medium thin consistency. Stir regularly.
Allow ingredients to cool and transfer to blender. Add vinegar and sugar and blend well.
Keep sauce refridgerated.

Submitter: 
Josh Butcher
Description: 
A sauce for the brave or insane. The Bhut Jolokia or Ghost pepper is extremely hot. Use caution and protection when handling.
