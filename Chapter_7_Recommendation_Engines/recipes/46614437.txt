Title:
Breakfast Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package frozen SHREDDED hash brown potatoes, THAWED
2 CUPS diced cooked ham, BACON OR SAUSAGE, OR A COMBINATION
3-4 GREEN ONIONS, CHOPPED
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 T. BUTTER
1 (4.5 ounce) can diced green chile peppers
2 CUPS shredded Cheddar cheese, divided (MEXICAN CHEESE BLEND)
8 EGGS, BEATEN
2 T. BUTTER
1 CUP SOUR CREAM
1/2 CUP CREAM
2 (14 OZ.) CANS green chile enchilada sauce, DIVIDED
8-12 (10 inch) flour tortillas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).  SOFTEN TORTILLAS IN MICROWAVE AND KEEP WARM.
Brown hash browns, ONIONS and ham in 1 tablespoon of oil AND 2 T. BUTTER in a medium skillet over medium high heat. Stir in diced green chilies and 1 cup of cheese.  SET ASIDE.
SOFT-SCRAMBLE EGGS IN 2 T. BUTTER.  SET ASIDE.
COMBINE 1 CAN GREEN SAUCE WITH 1/2 CUP SOUR CREAM AND 1/2 CUP CREAM AND SET ASIDE.
Coat the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish with a small amount of PLAIN enchilada sauce. Dip each tortilla in remaining PLAIN sauce, and fill with potato-ham mixture, TOPPING WITH SOME OF THE SCRAMBLED EGGS AND CHEESE BEFORE ROLLING.  Roll each as tightly as possible and place in the baking dish, seam side down. Top with SOUR CREAM/ENCHILADA sauce MIXTURE and REMAINING cheese, and cover with foil.
Bake, covered, in the preheated oven for approximately 20 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 10 minutes, or until lightly browned on top. DOLLOP WITH SOUR CREAM, IF DESIRED.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Wendy Gayle Wright Epps
Description: 
A hearty breakfast dish that includes hashbrowns, cheese, ham and diced green chilies rolled into flour tortillas. A new favorite for your next brunch or office get together that can be made in advance and reheated easily the next day.Modified from: Breakfast Enchiladas
