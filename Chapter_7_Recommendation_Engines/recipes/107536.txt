Title:
Creamy Tuna Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978070.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package medium seashell pasta
1 (12 ounce) can tuna, drained
1/2 cup chopped dill pickles
2 tablespoons dill pickle juice
2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
1/2 small red onion, finely diced
1 cup mayonnaise
salt
freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Place pasta in the pot, cook for 8 to 10 minutes, until al dente. Drain and allow to cool slightly in refrigerator.
In a large bowl, whisk together the tuna, pickles and juice, dill, onion and mayonnaise. Toss with cooked pasta and season with salt and pepper; serve.

Submitter: 
Pam Anderson
Description: 
Crunchy red onion, dill pickles and fresh dill make a delicious pairing with tuna and pasta shells for a quick and easy salad.
