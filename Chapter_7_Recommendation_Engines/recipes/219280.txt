Title:
Cherry Bliss Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984014.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (21 ounce) can LUCKY LEAFÂ® Regular or Premium Cherry Fruit Filling
1 (19.5 ounce) package brownie mix
1/2 cup vegetable oil
3 eggs, lightly beaten
1/4 cup water
1 (8 ounce) package softened cream cheese
5 tablespoons butter, softened
1/3 cup sugar
2 eggs, lightly beaten
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
3/4 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. In a large bowl prepare brownie mix according to package directions, using oil, eggs, and water.
Spread batter in a 9x13-inch greased pan. Set aside.
Beat cream cheese and butter in large bowl until fluffy. Add sugar and beat in additional 2 eggs, flour, and vanilla. Drop spoonfuls of the mixture over brownie batter in pan. Use a knife to gently cut through batters to create a marble pattern.
Spoon LUCKY LEAF Cherry Pie Filling over top. Bake for 50 to 60 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean. Cool completely before serving.

Submitter: 
Lucky Leaf Fruit Filling
Description: 
Satisfy your sweet tooth with these brownies marbled with sweetened cream cheese and baked with a topping of cherry pie filling.
