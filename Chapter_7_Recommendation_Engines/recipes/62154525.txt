Title:
Summer Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. Blu Cheese
1/4 c. raspberry vinaigrette
1/2 med. cucumber sliced
1/2 c. deli sliced roast beef chopped
1/4 c. pears diced (about 1/2 pear)
1/2 head of lettuce chopped

Directions:
Slice cucumber thinly and set aside, cut 1/2 a pear into thin chunks, chop lettuce to your choosing, chop up roast beef for long thin slices .
Add lettuce in a medium bowl; toss in vinaigrette, blue cheese, roast beef, pears, and cucumber.

A very quick summer salad.
