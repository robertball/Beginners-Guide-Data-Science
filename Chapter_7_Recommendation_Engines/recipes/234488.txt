Title:
Yummy Mango Citrus Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2072031.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana, peeled
1/2 lemon, peeled
1 mango - peeled, seeded, and cut into wedges
1/2 orange, peeled
2 apples, cut into chunks
2 slices fresh ginger root

Directions:
Process banana, lemon, mango, orange, apples, and ginger through juicer.

Description: 
This is a modified version of one of the expensive all natural drinks sold commercially!
