Title:
Utterly Sinful Mocha Almond Cheesecake Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/404287.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz.) package cream cheese
1 1/3 cups sugar, divided
3 eggs
2/3 cup butter
1 tsp. instant coffee granules
6 tbs. cocoa
3/4 cup flour
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup chopped almonds
1/2 cup miniature semisweet chocolate chips
1 tsp. milk

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and 1/3 cup sugar until smooth and light. Add one egg; beat until smooth. Set aside.
In a large mixing bowl, cream butter with remaining sugar until fluffy. Add remaining eggs, one at a time,beating well after each addition. Beat in cocoa and coffee granules. Sift together flour, baking powder and salt onto wax paper. Add all at once to chocolate mixture. Stir until blended. Add vanilla; set 1/3 cup of batter aside. Fold nuts and chocolate chips into remaining batter. Pour batter into a greased and foil lined 9"x9" square baking pan. Carefully spread cream cheese mixture over batter. Place reserved batter into a small bowl; add milk.Drop teaspoonfuls of reserved batter on cream cheese layer; run a clean knife through cheesecake and batter to create heart-shapes or swirls. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes or until toothpick inserted halfway to center of the pan comes out clean and cheesecake topping is set. Cool on wire rack.

This prize-winning recipe combines a dark,moist mocha brownie with a thick and creamy cheesecake layer to create a decadant and beautiful dessert.
