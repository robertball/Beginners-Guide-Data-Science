Title:
yeah i lived in Texas Smoked Brisket. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1113905.jpg
Ingredients:
10-12 lb. beef brisket.
1/4 cup paprika
1/4 cup Kosher salt
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup cumin
1/4 cup chili powder
1/4 cup fresh cracked black pepper
1/4 cup garlic powder
1/4 cup onion powder
1/4 cup cayenne pepper
Wood Chips I use a mix of Mesquite, Pecan, Hickory, and oak.  but lean more with Mesquite and pecan wood chips

Directions:
1st Soak your wood chips at least 8 hours
2nd make your rub by combine 1/4 cup paprika 1/4 cup Kosher salt 1/4 cup sugar 1/4 cup brown sugar 1/4 cup cumin 1/4 cup chili powder 1/4 cup fresh cracked black pepper 1/4 tsp garlic powder 1/4 tsp onion powder  4 Tbsp cayenne pepper
3rd Rub the Rub in to the 10-12 lb. beef brisket. (I prefer to rub down my brisket 24hr before smoking and store in refrigerator)
4th smoke  @ 220-230 1 hour 15 minutes per pound
5th When the inside temp of the brisket hits 165 wrap tightly in butcher paper or heavy duty aluminium foil place back in smoker.
6th when the inside temp of the brisket is 185 pull from heat place in a coller to trap heat and finish cooking.
