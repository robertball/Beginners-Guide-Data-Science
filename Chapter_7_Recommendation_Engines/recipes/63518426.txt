Title:
Crockpot Venison Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-4 lb. venison roast (or beef, if you wish)
2 T oil
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 envelope french onion soup mix
2 cups water, divided
6 med potatoes, cut in large bite size pieces
8 carrots, cut in 2" pieces
2 onions, chopped
1 bay leaf
2 cloves garlic
ground pepper, to taste
2 T. flour, mixed with 1/2 cup cold water
1/2 c. sour cream

Directions:
In a large pan, brown roast in oil; remove and set aside. Pour off excess oil.
In the same pan, combine mushroom soup, onion soup mix, and 1 cup water. Heat to boiling. Add potatoes, carrots, onions, garlic and bay leaf and remaining 1 cup water. Place veggies in crock-pot, top with roast. Cover and refrigerate until ready to cook. Cook on low for 6-8 hours.
When ready to serve, remove meat and veggies to a serving dish and cover. Put liquid in a sauce pan.
Gradually stir the flour and water mixture into the sauce in the pan, stirring until sauce boils and thickens. Add sour cream just before serving.
