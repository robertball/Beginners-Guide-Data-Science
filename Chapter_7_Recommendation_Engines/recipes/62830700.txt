Title:
Slow Cooker Chile-Chicken Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
1 envelope taco seasoning mix
1/2 cup chopped green chiles
1 envelope of onion soup
1 envelope of brown gravy
1 cup water
1 clove garlic minced
4 medium green onions, sliced (1/4 cup)
24 taco shells

Directions:
Spray 3- to 4-quart slow cooker with cooking spray. Place chicken thighs in cooker. Sprinkle with taco seasoning mix, onion soup, & brown gravy.
Add water, green chiles, & garlic. Cover and cook on Low heat setting 6 to 7 hours. Place chicken on cutting board; use 2 forks to pull chicken into shreds. Return chicken to cooker. Stir in green onions. Cover and cook on high heat setting about 15 minutes more. Serve chicken mixture in taco shells with lettuce, tomatoes,sour cream, and cheese.
