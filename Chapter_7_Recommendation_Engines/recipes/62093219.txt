Title:
Super Easy Onion Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package fat free cream cheese, softened
1 (16 ounce) container fat free sour cream
1 3/4 cup onion, finely chopped
salt to taste

Directions:
Beat together cream cheese and sour cream on medium speed until well combined.
Stir in onion.
Sprinkle with salt, if desired, and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
EMILY052005
Description: 
With only 4 common ingredients, you can create an amazing dip that no one will believe is fat free!  It is much better refrigerated and served the next day.
