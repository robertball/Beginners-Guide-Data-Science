Title:
Shrimp Diablo Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1133214.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound raw shrimp, deveined and peeled
2 Tablespoons Butter
2 Tablespoons All Purpose Flour
1/2 large onion, diced
5 garlic cloves, minced
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced
1/2 sweet red pepper, diced
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 cups heavy cream
1 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon ground oregano
2 Tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
1/2 cup Pepper Jack cheese, shredded
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, shredded
1/2 cup Romano cheese, shredded

Directions:
In a large sauce pan over medium heat, melt butter.
Saute onions until they start to soften.
Add in garlic, jalapeno and sweet red pepper and cook until garlic is fragrant.
Stir in flour and cook for about 5 minutes.
Add in milk, cream, red pepper flakes, salt, pepper, oregano and basil and stir until the mixture starts to bubble, reduce heat to low and simmer, stirring occasionally, until it starts to thicken.
Add in all the cheese and the shrimp and continue to simmer, while stirring, until the shrimp is fully cooked and cheese is melted.
Serve over prepared pasta.

Description: 
This is a wonderful Alfredo sauce with just a little kick.   Two bites into this my family was requesting that I save the recipe and make it again.  I would feel free to substitute in other cheeses for the Parmesan and Romano based on whats in your cheese drawer, but the Pepper Jack is important for the kick factor.
