Title:
Lemony Cucumbers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/215875.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cucumbers, sliced
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
2 tablespoons white sugar
2 teaspoons celery seed
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
3/4 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons chopped onion
1 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine the cucumbers, vinegar, sugar, celery seed, pepper, salt, chopped onion and lemon juice. Toss, cover and chill for 4 hours.
