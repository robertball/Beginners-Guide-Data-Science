Title:
Kid Friendly Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1081618.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 cup ketchup
3 lbs of lean ground beef
1 1/2 cups of saltine cracker - finely crushed
4 eggs
2 1/2 cups of milk
1 package of Lipton Onion Soup Mix
1/2 an onion chopped
1 Tbls worcestershire sauce
1 tsp of granulated garlic
2 tsp of all purpose salt
1 tsp of pepper

Directions:
Preheat over 350
Foil line 2 9x5 pans and spray with Pam
Spread 1/4 cup brown sugar in each pan
Spread 1/2 cups ketchup in each pan over the brown sugar
Place the ground beef in a large bowl and add the remaining ingredients.
Mix the meat mixture to combine, but do not overmix or the meat will be tough
Bake 60 minutes or until a meat thermature reads about 160
Gently drain any fat from the pan and then carefully turn onto a serving platter.

Submitter: 
Melissa Lindsey Gibson
Description: 
This was created to please all 5 of my children. If you are looking for a more savory or spicy meatloaf, this one is not for you. I serve the first meatloaf with mashed potatoes and the second one as meatloaf sandwiches.
