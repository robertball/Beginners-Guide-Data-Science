Title:
Feta Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 red peppers (may use orange and yellow as well)
Olive oil
¼ cup chives
¼ cup parsley
1 cup crumbled feta

Directions:
Cut red peppers into boats, rub with olive oil and line on a cookie sheet.
Combine chives, parsley and feta in a bowl and stuff pepper boats.
Bake at 375 for 20-25 minutes.
