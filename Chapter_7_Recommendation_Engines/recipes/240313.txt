Title:
Blue Cheese Biscuit Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3316906.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated biscuit dough, separated and cut into thirds
1 cup melted butter
1 cup crumbled blue cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Arrange biscuit pieces on a baking sheet. Drizzle butter over biscuit pieces and evenly top with blue cheese.
Bake until biscuits are cooked through and cheese is melted, 10 to 13 minutes.

Submitter: 
Rachel k.
Description: 
Biscuits are torn into thirds, drizzled with melted butter, and topped with crumbled blue cheese. Yummy, even for anyone like me who hates blue cheese dressing!
