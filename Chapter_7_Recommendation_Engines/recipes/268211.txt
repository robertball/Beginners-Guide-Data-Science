Title:
Strawberry-Banana Smoothie Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5772072.jpg
Ingredients:
1 frozen banana, cut into chunks
1 cup strawberries, chopped
Topping:
1/2 tablespoon unsweetened coconut flakes
1 teaspoon chia seeds
1/4 banana, sliced
2 strawberries, chopped
2 tablespoons fresh blueberries

Directions:
Defrost banana chunks for 5 minutes. Combine banana and 1 cup strawberries in a blender; blend until smooth. Pour into a bowl.
Top with coconut flakes, chia seeds, fresh banana slices, strawberry pieces, and blueberries.
