Title:
White cream filling (holland Cream version) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/698492.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup water
1 cup vegtable oil
2 cup shortening
8 cup powder sugar
1 tsp. clear vanilla

Directions:
take sugar and water and boil til sugar dissolved. let cool completely. After cooled mix at high speed.the cooled syrup,vegtable oil,shortening and vanilla.once blended well slowly add the powder sugar one cup at a time until well blended.
if to thick of consistency use extra water 1 tablespoon at a time until you get the consistency you like.

Submitter: 
Melissa 3dcakery
Description: 
This is the white cream filling used in Smear Faces,Cream Horns etc. I have looked forever for the Holland cream recipe for donuts.I have never had any luck. I haved worked on this recipe for a long time.I use this in my donuts at my cake shop.
