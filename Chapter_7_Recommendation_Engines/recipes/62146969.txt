Title:
Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Noodles:
7 small eggs
1/2 t salt
3 1/4 c flour
Sauce Mix:
1 1/2 lb diced chicken
4 water
1/2 onion cut into large chunks
2 stalks celery cut into large pieces
1t poultry seasoning
1 t salt
1/2 t garlic powder
3 cans chicken broth
24 oz evaporated milk
1/2 t pepper
2 cans bicuits

Directions:
For noodles:
mix eggs and salt
add 1 c flour and whisk
add 1 c flour and whisk dump onto heavily floured surface
knead in 1/2 c flour (be sure your hands are floured as well)
knead in remaining 3/4c flour
roll with a floured rolling pin into desired thickness
cut into 1/2 inch strips with a pizza cutter
For Broth:
place chicken, water, onion, celery, poultry seasoning, salt, and garlic powder in a large, thick bottomed stock pot
boil 20 minutes on high
remove celery and onion
add chicken broth, evaporated milk, and pepper
reduce heat to medium and bring to a boil
add noodles one at a time while stirring
stir in all flour that is leftover on the counter from the noodles (it thickens the sauce)
boil 10 minutes uncovered, stirring frequently
meanwhile cook biscuits half the time (you don't want them done)
cut biscuits into quarters and put on top of sauce in pot
cover loosely and boil for 10 minutes

Submitter: 
Melissa Arenson
Description: 
This recipe is at least 5 generations old. It is the best recipe I've ever had, but it does take a while to make.
