Title:
Grandma Raap's Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
11/2 lbs ground beef
1 package stuffing
1 medium onion (chopped)
1 cup chopped Celery
2-3 eggs
poultry seasoning
2 cups Chicken broth

Directions:
Sweat onions & celery in butter over medium heat
Add ground beef & brown
Add broth & bring to a boil
Add stuffing  and mix
Add eggs
Cover & Cook over low heat.
