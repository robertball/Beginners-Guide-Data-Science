Title:
Gingerbread Latte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup water
1 tablespoon instant coffee
1 tablespoon sugar
sprinkle ground ginger
sprinkle ground cinnamon
sprinkle ground cloves
sprinkle ground nutmeg
dollop whipped cream

Directions:
Whisk all ingredients together in a small, microwaveable bowl. Heat in the microwave for 2 minutes. Top with a dollop of whipped cream.

Submitter:

Description: 
A quick, warm and cozy coffee drink spiced just nice for the cold winter months or anytime.
