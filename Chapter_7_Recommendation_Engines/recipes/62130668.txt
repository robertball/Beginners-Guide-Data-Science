Title:
Moist and Delicious Banana Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter,softened, or margarine
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1 cup granulated sugar
2 eggs
2 cups sifted all-purpose flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1 (3 3/4 oz.) pkg. instant banana cream pudding mix
2 ripe bananas, mashed
1/3 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease & flour one tube cake or bundt cake pan .  In a large mixing bowl, cream butter or margarine; beat in vanilla extract; add sugar and eggs, one at a time, beating well after each.  In separate bowl, sift together the next five dry ingredients.  Add sour milk to mashed bananas & mix well.  Alternately add flour mixture and banana mixture to butter mixture by stirring only enough to combine thoroughly.  DO NOT BEAT!  Pour into prepared pan and bake in preheated oven for 45-55 minutes.

Submitter: 
Virginia Mom
Description: 
A moist, dense cake made with pudding.  Hard to resist, it tastes even better the next day.  Freezes well.
