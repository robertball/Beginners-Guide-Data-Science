Title:
Tangy (Not Sweet) Tartar Sauce II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon minced onion
1 tablespoons minced green olives, pitted and not stuffed with anything
1/2 tablespoon green olive brine
salt and ground black pepper to taste
celery salt to taste

Directions:
Stir the mayonnaise, onion, olives, and olive brine together in a small bowl. Season with salt, black pepper, and celery salt to taste. Cover, and refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.
