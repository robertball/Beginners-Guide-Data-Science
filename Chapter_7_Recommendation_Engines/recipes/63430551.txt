Title:
Sugar Cookies - Sugar Free :P Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/929275.jpg
Ingredients:
2 3/4 cups Brown Rice Flour
1 tsp Baking Soda
1/2 tsp Sea Salt
1 1/4 cups Butter
2 cups Xylitol (equivalent to 2 cups Sugar)
2 Eggs
2 tsp Vanilla Extract (No Corn Syrup, Gluten Free)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°
In a large bowl, cream together butter and 2 cups xylitol until light and fluffy
Beat in the eggs one at a time
Add vanilla extract
On top of the moist ingredients, carefully stir together flour, baking soda, and salt
Then stir the dry ingredients in until just blended
Roll the dough into walnut sized balls
Place cookies 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheets and flatten slightly
Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, until lightly browned at the edges
Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely

Submitter: 
Zan
Description: 
The only sugar free sugar cookie I've successfully made so far. It tends to be thicker than a normal cookie, much bigger, and a little hard, but it tastes great!
