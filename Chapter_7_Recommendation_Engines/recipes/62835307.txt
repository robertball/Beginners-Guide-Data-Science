Title:
slow cooked beef loin tri tip roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.24lb Beef loin tri top seasoned roast
one fresh clove of garlic
one yellow onion cut in half then sliced
olive oil
Goya adobo a dash or two dependent on taste
1 beef bouillon cube

Directions:
Preheat crockpot dripping a small enough olive oil to coat the bottom of the crockpot.
In a skillet heat up olive oil placing roast in browning entire roast from top to bottom and side.
By now your roast may begin to stick and pop in the pan it is normal give it at least five minutes browning time for each area of the roast place onion slices in while browning the final area of the roast.
transfer the roast to crockpot.
Continue to cook onions adding a half cup of liquid returning the skillet to the heat crumble the beef bouillon cube into the skillet stirring till the beef bouillon dissolves, then pour it over roast in crockpot cooking on high for four hours or until done.

Description: 
A 2.24lb beauty of a roast I had read other recipes for cooking a roast in slow cooker. None of which sounded like my goal recipe. So here it is a new take on a slow cooking roast recipe.

The roast is a 2.24lb beef loin tri tip roast seasoned, this one had very little fat.
