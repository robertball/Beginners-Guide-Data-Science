Title:
Tuna Salad ala Jackie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12oz box small shell macaroni
1 half inch slice deli ham
1 8oz brick sharp cheddar cheese
1 large can white albacore tuna
1 12oz bag frozen peas
1 cup salad dressing (not mayonnaise)
1 tsp. worchestershire sauce
1 Tbs. mustard
1/2 tsp. sugar
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Pour macaroni in boiling salted water and boil until tender.
While macaroni is cooking, chop ham and cheese into cubes and set aside.
Put peas in colander and pour cooked macaroni over top.
Rinse with cold water and shake both until most of the water is drained and pour into bowl.
Put tuna, ham, cheese and salad dressing ingredients in with the macaroni and peas and mix well.

Submitter: 
Jacqueline Allison
Description: 
A family favorite for warm summer evenings.  Serve with chips and fruit.
