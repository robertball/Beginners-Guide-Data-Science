Title:
Standing Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/942307.jpg
Ingredients:
3-4 pound whole chicken, rinsed
salt, to taste
pepper, to taste
thyme, to taste
4 cups of water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Rinse whole chicken
Sprinkle skin with salt, pepper, and thyme
Place on a chicken stand, upright in a roasting pan
Place in oven
Pour water in base of roasting pan
Bake for 1 hour, or until the breast meat reads 165 on a thermometer
Remove, and let rest 15 minutes before serving

Submitter: 
Moonharehayashi
Description: 
I can't remember when I started using a chicken stand to make baked chicken, but I haven't made it any other way in years. It makes a thin, crisp skin, juicy meat, and the water at the base becomes great chicken stock. Only the bones of this chicken ever go to waste.
