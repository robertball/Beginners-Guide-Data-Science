Title:
Authentic Chicken Marsala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast
1 1/2 cups tinly sliced baby bella mushroom
3/4 cups Marsala wine
1/4 water
6 TBSP Butter
1/4 tsp Rosemary
1 cup flour
1 TBSP flour

Directions:
Sautee mushrooms in 2 TBSP of butter
once tender, remove from heat, set aside.
Rinse chicken and fillet lengthwise.
Melt 2 TBSP of butter on med-high heat and
Lightly dredge breast fillets in flour
Brown fillets in butter, 3-4 minutes/side.
Once cooked through, remove from heat
place fillets on a deep serving platter
Reduce heat to med-low
In same pan melt remaining 2 TBSP butter
whisk in 1 TBSP of flour to form roux.
Add Marsala and water slowly while whisking
add 1/4 tsp crushed rosemary
return mushrooms to pan
Simmer sauce until it thickens slightly
Pour sauce over chicken and serve

Submitter: 
Karen D.
Description: 
This is a dish I have been making for years and is the most requested dish of friends and family at dinner parties. I serve it with herbed-buttered penne and sauteed zucchini.
