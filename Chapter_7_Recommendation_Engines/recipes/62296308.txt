Title:
Authentic Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Marinade
1.5 lbs of chicken or sirloin
1/4 cup canola oil
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp oregano OR basil OR tarragon
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
Fajita ingredients
2 TBSP Oil
1 onion thinly sliced
1 bell pepper thinly sliced( I prefer red but you can use any)
Fajita Toppings
Cheese
Salsa
Avocado
Sour Cream

Directions:
Combine all marinade ingredients and place in a glass dish with meat of your choice.
You can puncture meat with a fork so that the marinade soaks in better as well.
Let this sit over night.
For Beef: broil or grill until done (reserve marinade)
Thinly slice when meat is cooked to your liking.
For Chicken: Bake at 400 degrees for 45 minutes.
cook chicken in marinade.
After it is done, shred chicken and place back in marinade.
Saute onion and pepper in 2TBSP of oil for a few minutes.
Place meat of choice and marinade in the pan with your veggies and cook until heated through and liquid has mostly evaporated.
Pile up your fajita mixture onto a flour tortilla serve with sour cream, cheese, salsa and avocado.
