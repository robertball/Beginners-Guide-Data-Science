Title:
Slow Cooker Salsa Shredded Chicken for Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
24 ounces Salsa
1 packet of taco seasoning mix

Directions:
Mix salsa and taco seasoning mix. Place chicken breasts in the bottom of slow cooker. Pour salsa mixture on top. Cook on low for 7-8 hours. Strain chicken, reserving the salsa. Shred the chicken and serve on tacos with reserved salsa, lettuce, cheese, and sour cream if desired.

Submitter: 
CUPYCAKESMURF
Description: 
Chicken breasts are slow cooked to perfection all day long in salsa and seasonings to make the perfect chicken taco!
