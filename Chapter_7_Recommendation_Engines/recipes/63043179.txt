Title:
Power Blend Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups PC Organics Power Blend Frozen Fruit Mix (dark cherries, blackberries, blueberries)
1.5 cups vanilla yogurt (I use Astro Balkan Style)
3 ice cubes
1 banana

Directions:
Put all ingredients in the blender or magic bullet and blend until smooth and no ice chunks left.
Pur into a tall glass and use a straw for fun!

Submitter: 
Shannon O'Keefe
Description: 
This smoothie is just sweet enough, no added sugar and best of all tastes great!
