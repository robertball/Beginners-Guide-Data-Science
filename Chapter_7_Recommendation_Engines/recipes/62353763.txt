Title:
Linguini with Mushrooms and Sugar Snap Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb linguini
10 oz baby bella mushrooms, halved
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tbsp olive oil
2 cups sugar snap peas
2 tbsp butter
1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan
2 tbsp chopped basil
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook linguini according to package directions.
Meanwhile, heat olive oil in large skillet over medium heat; add mushrooms and garlic and saute 5 minutes.  Add sugar snap peas and saute 2 to 3 minutes.  Stir in butter until melted.
Drain linguini and toss with sauce.  Add parmesan, basil and season to taste with salt & pepper.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
Portobello mushrooms (or baby bella) and sugar snap pease are sauteed in garlic and olive oil, then finished with butter and tossed with cooked linguini and fresh parmesan.
