Title:
Antipasto Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages of Pillsbury crescent rolls
1/4 lb. sliced ham
1/4 lb. sliced provolone
1/4 lb. sliced salami
1/4 lb. sliced Swiss cheese
1/4 lb. sliced pepperoni
12 oz. jar of roasted red peppers- drained well
3 eggs
3 tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 325.
Place one package of rolls in a 13x9 pan.
Layer meats and cheeses (except Parmesan) in the order listed above then top with the roasted peppers.
Beat eggs with the Parmesan cheese and pour evenly over ingredients in pan.
Place second package over the ingredients then cover and bake for 25 minutes.
Uncover and bake for 10 more minutes or until golden brown.
Cut into squares to serve.
