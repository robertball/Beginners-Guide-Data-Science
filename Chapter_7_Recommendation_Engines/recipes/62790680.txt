Title:
Mimosa's Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cucumbers, sliced, excess seeds removed
1 small sweet onion, thinly sliced
1 cup apple cider vinegar
1/2 cup water
1 teaspoon kosher salt
3/4 cup white sugar
1 tablespoon celery seed, lightly cracked

Directions:
Toss together the cucumbers and onion in a large bowl. Combine the vinegar, water, salt,and sugar in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Bring to a boil, and pour over the cucumber and onions. Stir in celery seed, cover, and refrigerate until cold. This can also be eaten at room temperature, but be sure to allow the cucumbers to marinate for at least 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Jay Lynne Stuart
Description: 
This cool and crisp cucumber and onion dish is the perfect side to any Summer meal.Modified from: Adrienne's Cucumber Salad
