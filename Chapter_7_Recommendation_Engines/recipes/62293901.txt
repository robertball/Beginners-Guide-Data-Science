Title:
Spicy Cheesy Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2 lb Block velveeta
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/2 pound cooked and peeled shrimp (chopped or whole)
3 diced or sliced jalapenos

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together melted velveeta and
mayonnaise.
Mix in onion and shrimp meat.
Cover bowl, and
refrigerate before serving.
Serve with rippled potato chips
