Title:
Tuna Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 6 oz cans of chunk light tuna in water
1 10 oz can of reduced fat cream of chicken soup
1 10 oz package of mixed frozen vegetables
salt and pepper to taste
1 package of refrigerator biscuits (5 in package)

Directions:
In a bowl, combine tuna, canned soup, mixed vegetables, salt and pepper, mix thoroughly.
Pour into a 1 1/2 quart casserole dish
place pieces of the rolls across the top to cover
Bake in oven for 40 minutes or until top appears lightly browned and mixture is bubbly.
