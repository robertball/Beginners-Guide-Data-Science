Title:
Southwestern Turkey Chili from Del Monte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990844.jpg
Ingredients:
Cooking spray
12 ounces ground turkey
1/2 cup diced onion
1 (14.5 ounce) can DEL MONTE® Diced Tomatoes, No Salt Added
1 (15.25 ounce) can DEL MONTE® Whole Kernel Corn, No Salt Added, drained
1 (15 ounce) can low-sodium kidney beans, drained
1 (8 ounce) can Del Monte® Tomato Sauce, No Salt Added
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 Lime (optional)
Light sour cream (optional)
Cilantro (optional)
Salt and pepper to taste (optional)

Directions:
Heat a large skillet coated with cooking spray over medium-high heat. Cook turkey and onion for 5 minutes or until onion is soft, stirring frequently.
Stir in tomatoes and its juices, corn, beans, tomato sauce and chili powder. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer 12-15 minutes or until slightly thickened.
Serve with lime wedges and top with sour cream and cilantro, if desired. Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Del Monte
Description: 
When you have a craving for a hot bowl of chili but don't have lots of time, this recipe is hearty, delicious and fast.
