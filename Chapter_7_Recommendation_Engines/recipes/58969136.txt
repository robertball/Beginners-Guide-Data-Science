Title:
Indian Style Sheekh Kabab Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground lamb
2 onions, finely chopped  (try dry minced)
1/2 cup fresh mint leaves, finely chopped
1/2 cup cilantro, finely chopped
1 tablespoon ginger paste
1 tablespoon green chile paste  (never could find this)
2 teaspoons ground cumin
2 teaspoons ground coriander
1 teaspoon allspice (optional)
2 teaspoons paprika
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
2 teaspoons salt

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix ground lamb, onions, mint, cilantro, ginger paste, and chile paste. Season with cumin, coriander, paprika, cayenne, and salt. Cover, and refrigerate for 2 hours.
Mold handfuls of the lamb mixture into patties. Make sure the meat is spread to an even thickness. Refrigerate until you are ready to grill.
Preheat grill for high heat.
Brush grate liberally with oil, and arrange patties on grill. Cook for 10 minutes, or until well done, turning as needed to brown evenly.

Submitter: 
Merrie Brucks
