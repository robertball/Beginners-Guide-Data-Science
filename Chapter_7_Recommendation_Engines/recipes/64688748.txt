Title:
Instant Pot Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBS olive oil
1 onion diced
4 cloves garlic, minced
3 tomatoes diced (about 2 cups)
1/8 tsp celery seed
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp dried basil
1 packet Italian dressing and seasoning mix
3 carrots chopped
1 zucchini chopped
1 C elbow macaroni
32 oz Vegatable Stock
1 can white beans, drained
1 can corn, with liquid
1 5 oz bag spinach, chopped
Parmesan cheese to taste

Directions:
Turn instant pot to sauté mode, normal setting and heat up oil.
Add onion, and garlic and cook until translucent.  Add in the celery seed, oregano,basil and dressing mix.  Sauté until fragrant about 2 minutes.
Add tomato, carrots, zucchini stir to coat.
Then add macaroni, beans, corn and stock.
Make sure macaroni is cover by the stock.
Press cancel and set to soup setting for 15 minutes.   When the cycle ends, let pot release  naturally for 5 minutes, then manually release the pressure.
Stir in chopped spinach and serve.
Add Parmesan cheese to individual bowls to taste.
