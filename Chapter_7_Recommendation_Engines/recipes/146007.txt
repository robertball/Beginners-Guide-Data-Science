Title:
Big Star Cactus Cooler Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes
3 fluid ounces cactus juice schnapps
1 fluid ounce light rum
3 fluid ounces pineapple juice
1 fluid ounce cranberry juice

Directions:
Fill a tumbler glass with ice cubes. Pour in the schnapps, rum, and pineapple juice. Add enough cranberry juice to turn the drink from yellow-orange to a salmon pink color.

Submitter: 
lauri28
Description: 
A very tasty drink, good for summer outings.  The Cactus Cooler tastes more like fruit juice than an alcoholic beverage.  Quite intoxicating -- make sure to designate a driver.
