Title:
FAT GRANNYS CREOLE CHICKEN AND RICE SOUP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
---seasonings---
1 tsp each:
dried oregano,thyme and basil.
granulated onion and garlic.
celery seed
salt and pepper
red pepper flakes
---for the soup---
1 whole roasted chicken,meat removed and diced
1 cup cooked long grain and wild rice
4 cups chicken stock
3 tbl olive oil
1 stick of butter
1 large onion diced
2 stalks celery diced
1 red and 1 green bell pepper diced
1 tbl minced garlic
2 tsp worcestershire sauce
1 tbl franks hot sauce
1 (15oz)can of diced tomatoes in tomato sauce

Directions:
In a small bowl add all of the seasonings,set aside.(this will save time later on)
In a large sauce pan on medium high heat add the olive oil and melt the butter.stir in the onions,celery and peppers.saute for 2 to 3 min.stirring constantly.add the garlic,tomatoes,seasonings,worcestershire sauce,hot sauce and stir.add the chicken stock and stir well.
bring to a boil,reduce heat and simmer for 15 to 20 min.stirring occasionally.remove from heat,add the chicken and rice.stir to combine.let simmer on low for 5 min and serve.

a savory blend of fresh vegetables,seasonings,chicken and wild rice in a flavorful broth.
