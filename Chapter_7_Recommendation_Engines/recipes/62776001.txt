Title:
Stuffed cheeseburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/645240.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs 80/20 burger meat
diced jalapenos
diced fried bacon
diced sauteed mushrooms
minced garlic
minced green pepper
diced sauteed onion
blue cheese crumbles
american cheese slices
pepper jack cheese slices

Directions:
Seperate hamburger into 8 oz pieces.  Press 7 oz into bottom of burger press and make well.  Fill burger with choice of toppings.  Press remaining 1 oz of hamburger on top and seal.  I baked at 400 in oven, but you can do on grill also.

Submitter: 
shellbellc
Description: 
Using a widely available stuffer made the best stuffed burgers ever! Offer many different filling choices so people can choose what they want in their burger.
