Title:
Big Ray's South African BBQ Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1033530.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lb.  boneless skinless chicken thighs
1 1/2 cups barbecue sauce
1 cup  chicken broth
1 onion, chopped
1/2  cup  dried apricots, quartered
1/2 cup  golden raisins
1 Tbsp.  curry powder
3 cloves  garlic, minced
6 cups  hot cooked long-grain brown rice

Directions:
Place chicken in slow cooker. Combine all remaining ingredients except rice; pour over chicken. Cover with lid.Cook on LOW 8 hours (or on HIGH 5 hours).Serve over rice.
