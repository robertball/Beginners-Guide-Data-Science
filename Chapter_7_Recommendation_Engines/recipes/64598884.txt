Title:
Pierogies Miami Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3910158.jpg
Ingredients:
Frozen Pierogies, about 4 per person.
2 cups of frozen sofrito (or dice onions, red and green bell peppers)
4 slices of bacon
1 green tomato, thinly sliced.
1 tbs dried parsley
1/4 tsp chipotle pepper
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tbs minced garlic
1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Defrost the frozen pierogies in a microwave steamer for about 4 minutes on high. They should not cook in the microwave, and they can still be cold to the touch.
Cook the bacon in a large skillet until crispy.
Remove the bacon from the skillet and set aside. Add the frozen sofrito (onion and bell pepper mixture) into the bacon grease. Fry on medium high heat for about 3 minutes or until frozen vegetables start to brown.
Add green tomato slices to the mixture. Add spices and stir. fry for 2 more minutes.
Lower heat to medium and add pierogies. Stir well covering the pierogies with the sofrito mixture. Cover and let simmer for about 4 minutes.
Reduce heat to low. Flip the pierogies. Crumble bacon over the pierogies. Cover and simmer for about 4 more minutes.
You're done! Take a picture, share and serve!
