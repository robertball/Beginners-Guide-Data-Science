Title:
Mustard Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c Dijon Mustard
1/2 c White Wine Vinegar
1 1/2 c Olive Oil

Directions:
In a blender mix the mustard and vinegar until well blended. Then add 1/2 cup of olive oil. When fully blended add the rest of the olive oil. Add salt and pepper to liking.

Submitter:

Description: 
Perfect for salads or as a marinade!
