Title:
Cheesy Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup diced onion
4 cups peeled white new potatoes, diced
3 cups chicken stock
1 cup half and half
2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1/4 teaspoon dried dill weed
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper

Directions:
Bring stock to boil and stir in 2.5 cups of potatoes and onion.  cover, reduce heat and simmer 15 to 20 minutes, until potatoes are tender. In separate pan boil 1.5 cups of diced potatoes in chicken broth.
Puree potato mixture in a blender or food processor or using an immersion blender; return to pot over medium heat.  Stir in cream, cheese, dill, pepper, salt and cayenne.  Bring to a low boil and cook, stirring, until thickened, 5 minutes.

Description: 
Cream and sharp Cheddar are added to pureed potatoes and the soup is seasoned with dill and cayenne for a creamy, thick meal.Modified from: Cheesiest Potato Soup
