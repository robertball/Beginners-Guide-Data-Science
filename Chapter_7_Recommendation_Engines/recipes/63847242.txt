Title:
Chocolate Turtle Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068564.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c chocolate chips
2 c pecans
3/4 c melted butter
1/2 c evaporated milk
1 pkg (14 ounce) caramels
1 1/3 c water ( or as called for by your cake mix)
1/3 c oil ( or as called for by your cake mix)
3 eggs ( or as called for by your cake mix)
1 box (18 ounce) German chocolate cake mix

Directions:
1. Prepare cake mix as directed.
2. Pour 1/2 of the batter in a 13x9 inch pan.  Bake at 350-degrees for 15 minutes.
3. Remove and let cool.
4. In a double boiler, add caramels, milk and butter and stir constantly till melted.
5. Pour melted caramel over cooling cake.
6. Sprinkle on top 1 cup of pecans& chocolate chips.
7. Pour remaining batter on top and bake for an additional 20 minutes.
