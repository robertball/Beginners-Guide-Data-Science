Title:
Lemony-ious Lemon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/870228.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Lemon Cake mix
1 can Lemon Pie Filling
4 eggs
2 Cups Confectioners Sugar
4 TB Butter or Margarine
4 TB Lemon Juice

Directions:
Mix together the Cake Mix, Pie Filling, and eggs.  Pour into a greased 15x10x1 pan, and bake in a 350 degree oven 30-35 minutes.  Cool 15 minutes.
Mix together the Confectioners Sugar, butter, and Lemon Juice and frost cake.  Enjoy!!

Submitter: 
Phyllis
Description: 
3 Ingredients is all you need to make these bars, that taste like a million bucks.  Will surely satisfy the lemon craving in everyone!!  Makes a large pan, so is  great for picnics and family gatherings.
