Title:
Penne with Wild Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. olive oil
1 tsp. butter
3/4 cup thinly sliced leek
2 tsp. fresh sage - minced
6 cups wild mushrooms - coarsely chopped
3/4 cup half and half
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
8 oz. pasta

Directions:
Cook pasta to al dente.
While pasta is cooking, heat oil and butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
Add leek to pan and cook until tender - about 2 min.
Add sage and cook about 30 sec.
Add mushrooms and cook about 10 min., or until liquid evaporates - about 10 min.
Add half and half and cook until liquid is reduced to about 6 tbsp. - about 2 min.
Stir in salt and pepper.
Toss mushroom mixture with pasta and serve immediately.
