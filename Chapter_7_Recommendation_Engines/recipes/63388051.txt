Title:
Garlic Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. buttermilk baking mix
2/3 c. milk
1/2 c. shredded cheddar cheese
Topping:
1/4 c. butter, melted
1/2 tsp. garlic powder

Directions:
Combine baking mix, milk and cheese with a wooden spoon until soft dough forms.
Beat for 30 seconds more.
Drop dough by heaping Tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake at 450 degrees for 8-10 minutes.
Topping: Combine butter and garlic powder.  Brush over warm biscuits before removing them from cookie sheet.
