Title:
Jamaican Style Curry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts
1/4 cup oil
1 cups water
1 tomatoes, chopped
1 onions, chopped
1 carrot chopped
1 potato chopped
1 cup peas
2 tablespoons Jamaican Style curry powder
1 clove garlic, chopped
1/4 tsp thyme
2 slices hot pepper (optional)
1/2 tsp Salt

Directions:
Cut chicken breast into 1 1/2 inch cubes. Brown (fry) curry powder, onions, garlic, tomatoes, thyme and pepper in oil. Add chicken and brown (fry) for five minutes. Finally add water, lower heat, cover and simmer for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Ed G II
Description: 
This is a recipe that I have put together over some various websites, verbal consultations and just experimenting on my own and with my family. Rich and hearty with a great flavor when you can't get the real thing in the islands.
