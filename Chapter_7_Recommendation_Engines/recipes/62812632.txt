Title:
Gluten Free Strawberry Rhubarb Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C Sugar
8 ounces cream cheese
3 eggs
1 C strawberry puree*
1 C rhubarb puree*
2 C sliced strawberries
1 C sliced rhubarb (1/4-1/2" slice)
3 eggs
1/4 C oil or melted butter
2 1/2 C brown rice flour
1/4 C coconut flour
1/2 C garfava flour
1/2 C tapioca starch
3 tsp guar or zanthum gum
2 T baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 C chopped walnuts (optional)
1 tsp vanilla(optional)
*(2 C strawberry-rhubarb jam may be substituted for purees.  Just decrease sugar to 1/2 C.)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Grease (not spray, this bread is sticky) 2 loaf pans.
Beat sugar with cream cheese.
Add eggs one at time, beating between each egg.
Add oil.  Beat until fluffy.
Combine with purees and sliced rhubarb and strawberries. Stir gently to combine.
In another bowl, whisk together dry ingredients.
Add dry ingredients to the fruit mixture along with the optional nuts and vanilla.  Stir to combine thoroughly.
Pour into prepared pans.
Bake 50-60 minutes, or until toothpick comes out clean.
Let cool completely before slicing to help prevent crumbling.
