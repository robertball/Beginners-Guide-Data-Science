Title:
Amazing Breakfast Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Serrano pepper
6 Eggs
Sausage or Bacon (optional)
2 large potatoes or 4 small potatoes
Seasoning Salt
1/2 small onion
Burrito size Tortilla's
1 TBSP Vegetable oil

Directions:
Cut potatoes into cube size chunks. Cut up Serrano peppers and onion and put them aside for later. Heat oil in a fry pan. Add potatoes. Sprinkle the seasoning salt over potatoes. Cover potatoes for 15 minutes stirring occasionally. Now if desired cook your meat of choice in separate pan. After 15 minutes check on softness of potatoes. When the potatoes are soft to your liking add the Serrano and Onion and uncover the potatoes. Also taste a potato and see if you need to add more Seasoning Salt. Start cooking you eggs. Heat the tortilla and add eggs and potatoes and meat if desired. Enjoy this makes a great breakfast.
