Title:
Leftover Ham Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box AuGratin potato mix...2 cups boiling water
1/2 c milk
2 talb butter
2-3 cups cut up ham
2 cups cheddar cheese grated + 1 cup for top
1 can asparagus...may use string beans or frozen broccoli or corn
crumb topping- ritz crackers crushed with melted butter...or bread crumbs melted butter...I used crushed potato chips
garlic powder

Directions:
oven set at 375
9x12 casserole pan
follow directions on the box augratin potato mix for oven cook...boiling water/milk/butter...add garlic powder and seasonings..mix all in casserole dish
add ham on top and spread evenly
add cheddar cheese ( 2 cups)
add vegetable - I used asparagus and string beans..
add rest of cheese
top with your favorite crunchy toping ( potato chip crumbs..or ritz crackers crushed with melted butter or seasoned breadcrumbs and melted butter)season your crumbs with garlic powder..herbs
cover with tin foil and bake 30 - min take foil off bake 10 more min

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
what to do with all that ham?...and box mix of AuGratin potatoes....make a casserole...you can add your leftovers ( corn,,,broccoli...spinach) just stick to the basic base
