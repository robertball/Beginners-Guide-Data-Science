Title:
Dorito Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups whole nacho cheese Doritos, crushed
1 10.75 oz. can condensed cream of chicken soup
1 14.5 oz. can diced tomatoes, undrained
1 4 oz. can diced green chiles
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1/4 cup sour cream
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
1 cup shredded pepper jack cheese, divided
1 lb. ground pork
1 lb. ground chicken

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine crushed Doritos, cream of chicken soup, tomatoes, chiles, green pepper, sour cream, 1/2 cup Cheddar and 1/2 cup pepper jack.  Stir to combine all ingredients.
Preheat oven to 350 F.  In a large skillet, cook the ground pork until no longer pink.  Do not drain.  Transfer pork to bowl with other ingredients.
In the same skillet, cook the chicken until no longer pink.  Transfer chicken to bowl with other ingredients and mix well.
Spray a 9 x 13-inch cooking dish with cooking spray.  Pour ingredients into prepared pan and top with remaining cheese.
Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes.  Let stand 5-10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Tawny M.
Description: 
This recipe was created after a couple of years of tweaking.  I've successfully served it to people ranging in age from 6 to 60!  My husband loves it so much that he insists I make it for him every week.  It also keeps well as leftovers.
