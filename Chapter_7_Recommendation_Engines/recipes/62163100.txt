Title:
Sugar Free Fruit Platter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Kiwis, peeled and sliced
10 Strawberries, sliced and tops removed
2 cups wipped topping (prefered: cool wip)
2 teaspoons sugar subsitute (prefered: splenda)
4 tablespoons sugar free chocolate syrup

Directions:
Put wipped topping in the middle of a large platter.
Put slices of kiwi and strawberries around it.
Sprinkle fruit with sugar subsitute.
Drizzle the platter with chocolate.
Dip fruit in wipped topping and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Chef HamDog
Description: 
A delicious platter of kiwis and strawberries with a wipped topping dip.  Sprinkled with sugar subsitute and drizzled with sugar free chocolate syrup.
