Title:
Spice Islands® Spicy Golden Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 medium Braeburn apples, peeled and chopped
1 medium mango, peeled and cubed
1/3 cup red bell pepper, chopped
1 cup onion, chopped
1 cup golden raisins
1/2 cup candied pineapple OR dried fruit
1 medium fresh lime, zest and juice
1 cup orange juice
1 1/4 cups cider vinegar
2 cups white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 teaspoons mustard seed
2 teaspoons Spice Islands® Saigon Cinnamon
2 teaspoons Spice Islands® Crystallized Ginger
1 teaspoon Spice Islands® Ground Ginger
1 teaspoon dehydrated minced garlic
1 teaspoon Spice Islands® Coriander Seed
1 teaspoon Spice Islands® Dried Crushed Red Pepper
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon Spice Islands® Ground Turmeric

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a heavy large saucepan or Dutch oven. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered for 1-1/2 to 2 hours, until fruit becomes transparent and mixture is thickened.  Stir occasionally. Transfer, in batches, to food processor or blender and pulse to desired consistency.

Submitter: 
Spice Islands®
Description: 
Apples, mango, bell peppers, lime and lots of spices make this chutney a delightful accompaniment to a variety of savory dishes.
