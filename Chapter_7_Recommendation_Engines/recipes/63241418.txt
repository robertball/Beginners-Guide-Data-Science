Title:
French Onion Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1001016.jpg
Ingredients:
4 whole, frozen chicken breasts
8 oz sliced, fresh mushrooms
1 (10.75 oz) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (1.0 oz) packet Onion Soup Mix
8 oz sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Lightly spray the bottom of a 3 quart casserole dish with oil (optional).
Place frozen chicken breasts in 3 quart casserole dish.
Top chicken with sliced mushrooms.
Mix remaining ingredients in a bowl then pour over chicken and mushrooms.
Cover dish with foil and bake for an hour.

Submitter: 
Pamelia
Description: 
Tasty meal for a family on the go.  This is one of my go-to meals when it's dinner time and I haven't had the time to plan for our meal.
