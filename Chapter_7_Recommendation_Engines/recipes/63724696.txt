Title:
Honey Walnut Cream Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1034553.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package Philadelphia cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup orange blossom honey
5 whole walnuts, halved and slightly toasted

Directions:
In a ziploc bag, roughly crush the toasted walnuts.
Place all the ingredients in a small food processor and pulse until creamy.
Spread a layer on a slice of your favorite bread and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Cute Brunettes
Description: 
We came up with this recipe after trying Trader Joe's honey walnut cream cheese and this was much better since we used orange blossom honey mom buys from local farmers market. She even suggested toasting the walnuts before mixing them in. Yum!
