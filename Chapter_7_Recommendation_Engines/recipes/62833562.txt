Title:
Baked Beans On Crack Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs B&M Baked Beans
1 Cup Brown Sugar
1 Can Red Kidney Beans Drained
1 Cup Ketchup
1/2 tsp Pickle Juice
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1 1/4 lb ground chuck

Directions:
Cook the ground chuck and drain well.  Mix all ingredients together and bake at 350 for 1 hour

Submitter: 
ltlmsmfft
Description: 
This is a twist on ordinary baked beans.  They are SO good you can't possible take just one bite which is where the name comes from.  They include ground chuck which makes them more of a meal; but, they still suffice as a side dish.
