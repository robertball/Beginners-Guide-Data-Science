Title:
Italian Skillet Sammies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pack of 5 Italian Sausage
1 can tomato paste
1 can of water
1/2 cup apple juice
1 tsp garlic powder
1 Med Onion
1 Green Pepper
6 hot dog buns or 4 hoagie
1 cup mozarella cheese

Directions:
Cut the Sausage in 1/2 inch pieces and put in heated skillet (med heat)
Slice Onion and Green pepper add to skillet and continue to stir.
In bowl mix tomatoe paste, can of water, apple juice, garlic powder
Pour over sausage in skillet, mix, turn down heat to med/low and cover for 30 minutes.
Assemble sandwich with cheese on bun then sausage mixture, cheese.
Will make either 6 on hot dog buns or 4 hoagies

Description: 
I wanted something quick, but tasty.  This is now a staple in our house.  Its a good quick sandwich with flavor.
