Title:
Hemp Seed Eggnog Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2996269.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
1/2 cup sweet n cinnamon seasoned hemp
3 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp organic sugar
1 1/2 cups eggnog
1 egg
2 tbsp hemp seed oil
1 tbsp avocado oil

Directions:
Mix dry goods together.
Blend eggnog, eggs and oil separately.
Add wet and dry ingredients mixing well.
Drop by spoonfuls onto a pre-heated pan on medium heat. Flip pancakes when bubbles form and pop on top of the pancakes.

Submitter: 
HempE Recipes
Description: 
Golden holiday season pancakes.  Delicious with coconut oil (instead of butter) and sweet n cinnamon seasoned hemp on top!
