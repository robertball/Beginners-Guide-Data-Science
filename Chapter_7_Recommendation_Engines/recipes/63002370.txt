Title:
Luscious Pineapple Cream Cheese Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces cream cheese (room temp)
1.5 cups icing Sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp lemon zest
3 tblsp. of pineapple juice from can of tidbits
1/4 cup of tidbits
1/4 cup of toasted coconut

Directions:
1. Beat cream cheese with electric mixer on med until smooth.
2. Add in sugar .5 cup at a time, until well mixed.
3. Add vanilla, lemon zest, pineapple juice and tidbits to cheese mixture and blend with mixture.
4. Blend on high to break up the pineapple pieces.
5. Add in the toasted coconut.
