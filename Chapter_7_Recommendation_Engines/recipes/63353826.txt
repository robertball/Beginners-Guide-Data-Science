Title:
Chicken Sausage and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Alfresco Chicken Sausage - I used Spinach and Feta
Jasmine Rice
1/2 diced vidallia onion
1 cup water cress sliced
1/2 cup portabella mushrooms chopped
Asparagus steamed
1 tsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Directions:
Cook Jasmine rice per package
Steam asparagus
Stir Fry Olive oil and  Chicken Sausage. Cook thru.
Add in Water Cress, Onion and mushrooms being careful not to over cook.
Put in a bowl over Jasmine rice and steamed asparagus.

Submitter: 
Cathy Chace
Description: 
Alfresco Chicken Sausage and items I found left over in my fridge. Feel free to vary and use what you have in the fridge.
