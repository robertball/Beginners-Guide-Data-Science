Title:
Cheesy Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14-16 oz frozen broccoli
4 oz cream cheese, softened
4 oz shredded cheddar cheese
4 green onions, chopped
salt and pepper to taste
3 pieces bacon, chopped and fried until crisp

Directions:
Cook broccoli 4-5 min or until tender-crisp; drain well.
Mix in cream cheese, cheddar, green onion, salt, pepper, and bacon
Put in greased 2-qt. casserole dish and bake 350 degrees for 20-30 min, until bubbly

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Frozen broccoli, cream cheese, cheddar cheese and bacon for a low carb side dish
