Title:
Bodacious Brats and Beer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 bratwurst
1 large yellow onion, sliced thin
2 cans beer
yellow mustard
4 hot dog buns

Directions:
Pour beer into a large aluminum foil pan.  Place onions and bratwurst into  the pan and cover and refrigerate for 4-8 hours.  Light the charcoal grill and sit the marinade on the grill too.  Alternate the brats and cook on the grill and in the marinade until they're no longer pink inside.  Brush buns with mustard and place brats in bun and top with grilled onions.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
Marinating bratwurst sausage in beer and onions give them a awesome flavor!  I used to live in Milwaukee where I first had these and have been fixing them this way ever since.
