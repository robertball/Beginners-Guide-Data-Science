Title:
Pasta Pomodoro Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 packages angel hair pasta
1/4 cup olive oil
1/2 onion, chopped or 2 shallots chopped
6 cloves garlic, minced
2 cups roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup low-sodium chicken broth
1/4 cup white wine
crushed red pepper to taste
freshly ground black pepper to taste
2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8  minutes or until al dente; drain.
Pour olive oil in a large deep skillet over high-heat. Saute onions and garlic until lightly browned. Reduce heat to medium-high and add tomatoes, vinegar and chicken broth; simmer for about 8 minutes.
Stir in red pepper, black pepper, basil and cooked pasta, tossing thoroughly with sauce. Simmer for about 5 more minutes and serve topped with grated cheese.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
Easy and light pasta with tomatoes and garlic. Cooked chicken breast chunks or shrimp can also be added to sauce for a great main dish!Modified from: Pasta Pomodoro
