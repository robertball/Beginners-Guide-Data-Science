Title:
Quicky and Easy Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/952765.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds cooked salad shrimp
6 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
1 pint sour cream
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle chile sauce

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, blend together the shrimp, cream cheese, sour cream and chili sauce.  Season to taste with salt and ground pepper, serve with favorite crackers.

Submitter: 
Golda Szydlowski
Description: 
My mom serves this appetizer every Thanksgiving with fresh cut carrots, cauliflower, celery, and red peppers!
