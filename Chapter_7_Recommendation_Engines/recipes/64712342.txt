Title:
Raw Jammin' (Fresh Strawberry Chia Jam) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5624273.jpg
Ingredients:
•	14 ounces (400g) fresh strawberries, washed and cored
•	1/3 cup agave syrup
•	2 tsp lime juice
•	1/4 cup chia seeds
•	2 tbsp chia seeds

Directions:
1.	On low speed, pulse the strawberries, agave syrup, lime juice and 1/4 cup chia seeds with an immersion blender until the strawberries are chunky. Add remaining 2 tbsp chia seeds and mix well with a wooden spoon.
2.	Pour the mixture into two half-pint canning jars. Allow to set in the refrigerator or freezer for at least 8 hours before consuming.
3.	Keep in the refrigerator for up to a week or in the freezer up to six months.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Raw strawberry chia jam with none of the funky stuff. The chia seeds help the blended fruit gel, creating a spreadable fruit for your toast or sandwich.
