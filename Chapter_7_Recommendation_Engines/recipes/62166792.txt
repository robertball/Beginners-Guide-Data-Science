Title:
The Ultimate Milkshake! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint Chocolate Ice Cream
Milk
1 sliced Banana
1/4 cup peanut butter
2 crushed graham crackers

Directions:
Add Ice cream, banana, peanut butter to blender. While blending add milk to desired thickness. Stir in crushed graham crackers by hand. Pour into glasses and serve.
