Title:
Spicy Cheesy Shrimp Sausage Fettuccine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1 pound package fettuccine pasta
1/4 cup (1/2 stick) unsalted butter
1 medium yellow onion, chopped
1 medium green bell pepper, chopped
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon of minced garlic
1/2-1 teaspoon of cajun seasoning
1 pound package smoked sausage, chopped and cooked
3/4 lb peeled and deveined medium sized cooked shrimp(also remove tails)
1/2 cup half and half, can use more if needed
1-1 pound package jalapeno velveeta cheese, cubed
freshly shredded parmesan cheese for topping

Directions:
Bring a pot of well salted water to a rolling boil and cook the fettuccine to al dente according to the package directions. Drain, rinse well, and set aside to fully drain.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet over medium heat, melt the butter.
Add the chopped onion and bell pepper and cook until softened.
Stir in the flour and cook for about 3 minutes.
Add the garlic and cook another 2 minutes.
Add the cajun seasoning and shrimp and sausage and cook on a low simmer for about 5 minutes.
Add in the half and half and cubed velveeta, and continue cooking over low until the cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
Add the cooked and drained fettuccine and stir well to combine. Top with parmesan cheese prior to serving.
