Title:
Sorrelade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6581211.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1 1/2 cups Sorrel (Oxalis) leaf/stem/flowers/seedpods
1/4 cup agave nectar or honey
dash of salt
2 cups ice cubes
Mint leaves for a garnish

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a blender
If possible, let sit overnight in refrigerator
Garnish with mint springs and enjoy!
Serves 3
