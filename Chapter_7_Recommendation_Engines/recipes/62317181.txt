Title:
Casatiella (Italian Easter Bread) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkg. Dry Yeast          1/2 T. Black Pepper
2 C. Warm Water           1/2 C. Crisco or Lard
1 T. Salt                 6 Eggs (Romm Temp.)
8-1/2 C. Flour
1/2 C. Grated Parmigiana Cheese

Directions:
Soften yeast in 1 C. Warm Water (not hot).  Add salt & remaining water.   Stir in 4-5 C. Flour.  Then work with hands.  Knead until dough is smooth & satiny.  Place in an ungreased bowl, cover with clean towel.  Let rise in warm (80-85) place until double in bulk.  Punch down dough.  Sprinkle w/ pepper, cheese, shortening, eggs & remaining flour. (about 2-3 C.)  Work in the dough until thouroughly blended.  Divide dough ball into 3 part & braid to form 1 large ring or 2 smaller rings. Place on cookie sheetor cake pan,that has been greased & floured.*(At this point if you wish you can place 3 or 4 hard boiled eggs into the ring) cover with clean towel again & let rise until double in bulk.  About 1 Hr.  Bake in preheated oven (350) for about 1 Hr. or until done. Cool
