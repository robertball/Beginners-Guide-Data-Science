Title:
Cinnamon applesauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2- 3 pkg cherry jello
1/2c red hots
3c water
2c unsweetened applesauce
1T lemon juice
1/4t salt

Directions:
Boil water with redhots until melted, stirring constantly.
Add jello and stir until dissolved.
Add other ingredients, pour into 8x8 dish and chill.
