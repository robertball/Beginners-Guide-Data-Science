Title:
Thai Cabbage Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups packaged shredded cabbage with carrot (coleslaw mix)
1 cup sugar snap peas, cut into pieces
1 can sliced water chestnuts, drained
1/4 cup thinly sliced green onions
1/4 cup sliced or slivered almonds
Fresh cilantro (optional)
for the dressing:
1/3 cup peanut butter
1 t. curry powder
1/2 t. salt
1/2 t. garlic powder
1/2 t. ground ginger
1/3 cup water
2 T. lemon juice
2 T olive oil

Directions:
In a medium sized bowl, whisk together dressing ingredients.
In a large bowl combine all ingredients, then pour dressing over and mix well.

Submitter: 
Jo Bice
Description: 
This is one of our favorite summer main dish salads when I add chicken.  It's also delicious without chicken as a side salad.
