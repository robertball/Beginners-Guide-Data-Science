Title:
PeepSmoothies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1109913.jpg
Ingredients:
8 cubes ice cubes
1 cup milk
6 fluid ounces frozen lemonade concentrate
3/4 cup water
3 yellow marshmallow chicks (such as Peeps®)
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Blend ice cubes, milk, lemonade, water, marshmallow chicks, and vanilla extract in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
thellamaman
Description: 
PeepSmoothies are the best for a quick, deliciously cool drink. Select your frozen juice and Peeps® carefully to coordinate colors. You can use any frozen juice concentrate.
