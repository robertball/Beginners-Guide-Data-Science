Title:
Slow Cooker Corned Beef and Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 handfuls of baby carrots , to fill the crock
5 medium red potatoes, cut into bite-sized pieces
1 onion, peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces
Water to top the crock
1 (4 pound) corned beef brisket with spice packet
1 bottle Guiness or other tasty non wheat beer
1 head cabbage, coarsely chopped

Directions:
Place the brisket in the crock.  Pour the beer over the brisket.Top with enough water to submerge beef to an depth of an inch. Sprinkle on the spices from the packet, cover, and set the cooker on low.
Cook the brisket for about 4 hours. Then add potatoes.  Add enough carrots to fill the crock. 20 minutes before serving, pull off a cup of liquid braise the cabbage in the liquid with the lid on the pot. Serve the juice along with the  cabbage and place the potatoes , carrots and pieces of beef onto the cabbage in a bowl.  Serve with hot buttered bread and a salad.  For a kick, use horseradish sauce.
