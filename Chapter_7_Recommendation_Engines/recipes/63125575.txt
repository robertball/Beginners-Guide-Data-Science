Title:
Broccoli and Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/796558.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 medium whole onion, chopped
1 16 oz frozen chopped broccoli
4 14.5 oz cans chicken broth
1 pound velveeta cheese, cubed
2 cups millk
1 teaspoon garlic powder
2/3 cup cornstarch
1 cup warm water

Directions:
In a stockpot melt butter over medium heat. Cook onion in butter until softened. Stir in broccoli, and cover with chicken broth. Simmer until broccoli is tender. 10 to 15 minutes.
Reduce heat and stir in cheese cubes until melted. Mix in milk and  garlic powder.
In a small bowl, stir cornstarch into warm water until dissolved. Stir into soup; cook stirring frequently until thickened.

James Noble

Serve with crusty french bread.
