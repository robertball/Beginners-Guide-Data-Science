Title:
Not your regular Banana Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 to 10 - medium size banana ripe
2 - packages - Lorna Dune cookies.  1 package you'll need to make into crumbs with a to make about 2 cups of crumb.  Save the rest of the cookies for the pudding layering.
2 - tablespoons - granulated sugar
1/2 cup melted butter
1 - 8oz cream cheese - room temperature
1 - 12oz can sweetened condensed milk
1 - 12oz. whipped topping - defrosted
1- 5oz. size instant vanilla pudding or banana pudding
2 - cups milk (regular)
2 cups whipping creme - unwhipped
4 tablespoons - granulated sugar

Directions:
Preheat your oven to 350 degrees.  Spray a 13 x 9 glass or ceramic pan with no stick spray.  Mix together cookies crumbs, melted butter and sugar by hand.  Pat mixture into pan and bake for 10 minutes.  Cool in pan.
In a mixer the pudding and milk.  Beat until it's smooth and thickened.  Set aside.  In another mixer, beat cream cheese and condensed milk until smooth.  Mix in whipped topping.  Fold in vanilla pudding mixture. Slice bananas 1/2 inch and layer them onto the crust.  Add half of pudding mixture on top of bananas. Take the rest of the cookies and make one layer on top of budding.  Add another layer of bananas and pour the rest of vanilla pudding mixture over bananas and smooth out.
Put in refrigerator overnight.
Next day in a mixer, beat whipping creme and sugar.  Whip until creme reaches the firm peak stage.  Pipe over banana mixture and refrigerate until ready to serve.
