Title:
Stuffed Dates Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz pkg. soft cream cheese
3 oz Gorgonzola cheese crumbles
8 oz bag of pitted dates (approx. 36)
36 walnut pieces

Directions:
Mix together the two cheeses until blended.
Spread the dates open, make a new slit if needed.
Using a knife spread some cheese mixture into date,
then top with a pieces of walnut.

Submitter: 
Grace Carolyn Bowers-Reimondo
Description: 
Sweet dates stuffed with the tang of Gorgonzola cheese and a walnut piece for some crunch.  Hmmmm
