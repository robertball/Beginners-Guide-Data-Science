Title:
Ti Amo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pck Gnocchi
8 oz smoked salmon
2 cups lights cream
salt, pepper
nutmeg
cayenne pepper

Directions:
Slice salmon in small bitesize pieces
In a saucepan combine salmon and cream. Bring to a boil.
Season with salt, pepper and nutmeg.
Cook gnocchi according to direction on package.
Arrange gnocchi on dinner plates and top with sauce and salmon.
Use some cayenne pepper for decoration.

Submitter: 
Nuttylicious
Description: 
Gnocchi and Salmon topped with a light creamy sauce.
