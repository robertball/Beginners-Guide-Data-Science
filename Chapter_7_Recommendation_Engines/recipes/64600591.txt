Title:
Whole Wheat All-Purpose Baking Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3931935.jpg
Ingredients:
720 grams milled Soft White Wheat Berries (6 cups whole wheat flour)*
6 Tablespoons Sugar
4 Tablespoons Baking Powder
1 ½ teaspoons table salt
¾ cup plus 1 ½ Tablespoons (6 oz/172 g) Vegetable Shortening
*Remember when using fresh milled flour it is best to use weighted measurements rather than volume

Directions:
Mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a stand mixer or by hand until well blended.
Add vegetable shortening and mix until completely incorporated.
Store in refrigerator or freezer for best results.
