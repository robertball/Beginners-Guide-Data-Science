Title:
Monterey Nacho Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987033.jpg
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons margarine or butter
2 tablespoons Lea & Perrins Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1 tablespoon lime juice
6 drops hot sauce
4 cups Cheez-It® White Cheddar Baked Snack Crackers
4 cups corn chips
2 cups mini pretzels

Directions:
Heat oven to 250 degrees.
Melt margarine in a large baking pan with sides.  Add Worcestershire Sauce to margarine and mix well.  Add remaining ingredients and gently stir until evenly coated.
Bake 45 minutes, stirring frequently.  Spread on paper towel to cool.  Store in airtight container.

Submitter: 
Keebler® Kitchens
Description: 
Chili powder, lime juice, and zesty cumin coat Cheez-It® White Cheddar crackers, corn chips, and mini pretzels in this power-packed snack mix.  Not intended for novice snackers.
