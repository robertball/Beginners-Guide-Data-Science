Title:
Chorizo Stuffed Poblano Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6860547.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound chorizo sausage
1 small carrot, grated
1 small celery stalk, grated
1 small onion, diced
2 TBS minced garlic
4 poblano peppers, halved and seeded
1 can diced tomatoes with green chilies
1 tsp Adobo seasoning
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 TBS fresh parsley
1 tsp dried basil
1 tsp dried cilantro
1 cup cooked long grain rice
1/8 cup shredded cheddar
1/8 cup shredded mozzerella
1/8 cup shredded pepper jack
Olive Oil spray

Directions:
In a skillet, cook chorizo completely and drain.
Add meat back to skillet, add onion, garlic, celery, carrot, canned tomatoes, Worchestershire sauce, basil, cilantro and Adobo seasoning.
Simmer 5 minutes.
Add rice and simmer 5 more minutes.
Turn off heat, add fresh parsley and let rest.
Preheat oven to 350*F.
Cut peppers in half lengthwise and remove ribs and seeds.
Spray baking dish with olive oil.
Stuff each pepper half and lay in baking dish.
Add 1/2 cup water to the bottom of the dish.
Loosely cover with aluminum foil and bake for 1 hour.
Remove foil and sprinkle each pepper with the mixed cheeses.
Turn oven to broil and broil the peppers for 3-4 minutes until the cheese is brown and bubbly.

Description: 
Spicy chorizo and rice stuffed into poblano peppers and roasted to perfection.
