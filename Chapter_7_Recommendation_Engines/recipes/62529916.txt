Title:
Butternut Squash Soup V Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/517253.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1 small onion, chopped
1 medium carrot, chopped
2 medium sweet potatoes, cubed
1 medium butternut squash
Dash of paprika, cloves and any other spice you like.
1 (32 fluid ounce) container chicken stock
salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut the squash in half and remove seeds, bake face down on foil for one hour at 400 degrees and let cool.  Melt the butter in a large pot, and cook the onion,carrot, and potatoes, for 5 minutes, or until lightly browned. Peel skin off cooled squash and add pulp to vegies. Pour in enough of the chicken stock to cover everything. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, add spices, cover pot, and simmer 40 minutes, or until all vegetables are tender.
Transfer the soup to a blender, and blend until smooth. Return to pot, and mix in cream to desired consistency. Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Kim Carvalho
Description: 
Squash, potatoes, carrots, onion, spices and cream make for a thick, delicious soup.Modified from: Butternut Squash Soup II
