Title:
Rubarb Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups chopped rubarb
1 cup white sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1 cup flour
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup butter

Directions:
Cut the rubarb in 1/4th inch pieces.  Mix the first three ingredients together put into a 8x11 cake pan.
Mix the next four ingredients until they resemble medium size crumbs.  Sprinkle over the rubarb.
Bake at 375o for 45 min.

Submitter: 
Linda Peterson
Description: 
A crisp without the oatmeal.  Still has that great crumble topping.
