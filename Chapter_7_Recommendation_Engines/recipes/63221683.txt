Title:
Pampered Chef Apricot Almond Baked Brie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. finely diced dried apricots
1/4 c almonds, silvered
2 T apricot preserves
1 tsp. Dijon mustard
1 pkg. Refrigerated Crescent Rolls
1 4 inch (8oz.) Brie Cheese rind included
1 egg lightly beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine apricots, almonds, preserves and mustard.  Mix well.
Unroll crescent dough on lightly floured cutting board.  Separate dough crosswise into two squares, pressing seams to seal.  Place Brie on one square of dough; set aside remaining square of dough.  Trim courners from first dough square, leaving about an inch border around the Brie.  Scoop apricot mixture evenly over top of Brie.
Using a small cookie cutter cut one shape from each corner and center of remaining square of dough;  set shapes aside.  Trim courners of dough.  Place dough over Brie, pinching seams, if necessary fold and crimp edge of dough to seal.  Trasfer Brie to Mini Baker.  Brush egg wash over entire surface of dough.  Place cut out shapes over dough and brush with egg wash.  Bake for 30-35 minutes  or until crust is golden brown.  Remove from oven, let stand and then serve.
