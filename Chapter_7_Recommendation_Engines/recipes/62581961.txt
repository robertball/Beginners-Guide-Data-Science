Title:
Coffee Shop Corn Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/792299.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c cornmeal
1 c flour
1/3 c brown sugar
1/3 c sugar
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 egg
1 c buttermilk
3/4 c vegetable oil

Directions:
In a bowl, combine cornmeal, flour, sugars, baking soda & salt. In another bowl beat egg, buttermilk & oil & stir into dry ingredients just until moistened. Fill greased or paper lined muffins cups 3/4 full. Bake @ 425 for 12-15 min, Cool in pan 10 min before removing to a cooling rack.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is our favorite corn muffin, they turn out perfectly every time.
