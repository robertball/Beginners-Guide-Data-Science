Title:
Sate with Peanut sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1127684.jpg
Ingredients:
Sate:
Pork shoulder or Pork Butt, cut into cubes
Wooden skewers soaked in water overnight.
Marinade:
1 Onion sliced
3 toes of garlic, finely sliced
3 tsp Sambal Oelek (Asian Isle, Jar has a green lid)
1 fresh lemongrass, split in 2 pieces
1 inch fresh Ginger cut in slices
ABC Ketjap (Ketjap Manis) = sweet Soysauce, enough to cover meat.
Peanut sauce:
Above Marinade
Peanut butter
Coconut Milk

Directions:
Make the marinade by combining all the ingredients
Add the cubed meat and let marinade overnight (stir occasionally).
Skewer the meat on the wooden skewers (3 or 4 pieces)
(You can alternate with pieces of bell pepper or onion in desired)
Use the marinade to make the sate sauce.
Warm the marinade in a sauce pan. Do NOT bring to a boil.
Add peanut butter (enough to make a thick sauce)
Use a little coconut milk to get the right thickness (when sauce cools it will get thicker as well)
