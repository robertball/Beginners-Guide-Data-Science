Title:
Wacky Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988353.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package Wacky Mac®
1 1/2 cups cooked chicken strips
2 medium carrots, shredded
1/2 cup red pepper, cut into julienne strips
1/2 cup yellow pepper, cut into julienne strips
1/2 medium red onion, sliced thin
1/2 cup celery, sliced thin
1/2 cup parsley, chopped
1/2 cup honey mustard salad dressing

Directions:
Prepare Wacky Mac according to package directions; rinse with cold water; drain. In large bowl, toss Wacky Mac with remaining ingredients; cover and chill thoroughly. Toss gently before serving. Garnish as desired.
