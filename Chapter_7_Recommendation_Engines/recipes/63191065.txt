Title:
Zach's Famous Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp paprica
2 tsp cumin
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp crushed red pepper
1 tsp kosher salt
4 garlic cloves minced
4 tbs brown sugar
1-2 lbs salmon

Directions:
Mix together all ingredients, rub on both sides of the salmon. Wrap in foil and slow cook 2-4 hours
