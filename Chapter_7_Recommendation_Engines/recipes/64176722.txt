Title:
Rhode Island Johnny Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. corn flour or cornmeal
1/2 tsp. salt
1 level tsp. sugar (optional)
1 1/3 c. boiling water

Directions:
Pour water over meal, salt and sugar.
Mix well. Thin down with milk to consistency of mashed potatoes.
Drop by spoonfuls on hot griddle or frying pan. Cook 5 minutes. Turn over, and cook 5 minutes more. Serve with jam or syrup.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
Gluten free, added fat free breakfast pancakes or have as brunch or snack.
