Title:
Vanilla Cookie Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups pancake mix
1 cup milk
2 eggs
1 tbsp vanilla extract
2 tbsp splenda or sugar
**add in's banana slices, chocolate chips, raisins, dried fruit, etc.

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except for add in's.
On a hot griddle, ladle on some batter. When outer edge stops spreading and becomes not shiny, add your add in's then flip!
Continue process until all batter is gone or you have made enough pancakes for your family.

Submitter: 
Katy Blaylock
Description: 
I was just playing around one morning and though I would just add vanilla and splenda to the normal pancake batter mix. I have been TOLD I cannot make them the normal way anymore :)
