Title:
Chili Sauce for Hotdogs and Hamburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion chopped
1 clove garlic
1 tsp oil
1 pound ground beef
1 cup water
1/4 cup tomato paste
1 TBl brown sugar
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp allspice
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp ground cloves
1/2 tsp salt,or to taste

Directions:
In a large skillet,fry onion and garlic in oil until soft.Add meat ,stirring constantly with fork to keep the beef broken up.Once the meat browns,add water and tomato paste.Simmer 10 minutes.Go through it with and immersion blender a little to get it a finer texture.Add sugar and spices.Simmer 1 hour,adding water if necessary to keep it moist(not soupy )

Submitter: 
Lindalou
Description: 
This is a chili hotdog sauce that is close to a local restaurants recipe.And taste great on hamburgers too.Adjust the cayenne to your taste.
