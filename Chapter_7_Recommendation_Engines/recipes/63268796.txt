Title:
Lemon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. flour
1 c. margarine
1/2 c. powdered sugar
4 eggs, slightly beaten
2 c. white sugar
6 T. lemon juice
4 T. flour
1 t. baking powder

Directions:
Mix flour, margarine, and powdered sugar; pat into a 9x13 pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
While baking, beat eggs slightly; add sugar, lemon, flour, and baking powder. Pour over crust while still hot.
Bake for another 25 minutes at 350 degrees. While still warm, sift powdered sugar over the top.
