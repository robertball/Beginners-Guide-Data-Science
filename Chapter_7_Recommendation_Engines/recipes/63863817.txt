Title:
Vegan Pancakes (GF) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons Chia Seeds
1 Tablespoon Flax Seeds
2 cups hot water
2 tablespoons sucanat.
1 cup millet flour
1 cup brown rice flour
2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup almond milk

Directions:
In a bowl, mix flours, baking powder and salt together.
Sift mixed flours into another bowl.
Add chia seeds and flax seeds to water.
In a blender, blend flax, chia seeds and sucanat together.
Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients.
Add almond milk
