Title:
Quick and Easy Little Cherry Cheesecakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/551905.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 oz.) cream cheese
4 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup milk
4 tsp. cornstarch
1 cup sugar
cinnamon to taste
1 (16 oz.) can cherry pie filling

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Line mini cupcake pans with baking cups.
In a food processor, combine the cream cheese, eggs, vanilla, milk, cornstarch, and sugar.  Cover and process until smooth.  (about 2 minutes)
Pour into baking cups about 3/4 full.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
Sprinkle with cinnamon and allow to cool in the pan before removing.
When cool top with cherry pie filling and refrigerate.
Serve cold.

Submitter: 
SHORECOOK
Description: 
All the ingredients get put in a food processor and blended until smooth.  What sets these little cheesecakes apart from others is there is no cookie involved and they are mini cupcake size.  The cinnamon sprinkling adds a delightful flavor.
