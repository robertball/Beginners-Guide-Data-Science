Title:
Chilli Burger I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2241265.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground chicken breast
1 Egg
1 Jalapeno pepper, diced finely
1 6 oz can tomato paste
4 to 5 Slices whole grain bread, crumbled
2 Tbsp. Chilli powder
1 tsp. Dry ginger
1/2 tsp. Salt

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl combine ground chicken meat, onion, egg and tomato paste.
Mix all ingredients together well with a large spoon making sure tomato paste binds well with other ingredients
In a food processor place crumbled bread pieces and pulse until crumbs are of fine consistency
Add spices and seasoning to meat mixture and combine all ingredients into a mould.
In a frying pan over medium heat form patties with meat mixture and place in frying pan with 2 Tbsp. Canola oil coating the surface.
Cook in frying pan until juices run clear and burgers are browned nicely
Place on serving plate and serve with hamburger buns and your favourite condiments

Description: 
A hot and spicy burger that goes well with your favourite salad for dinner.
