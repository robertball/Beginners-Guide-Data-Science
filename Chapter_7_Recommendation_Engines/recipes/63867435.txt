Title:
My Mom's Cranberry Orange Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cup all purpose flour
1/4 tsp salt
2 tsp baking powder
1 1/4 cup sugar
1/2 tsp baking soda
3 T shortening (canola oil or butter melted)
2 eggs, beaten
2 T orange zest
1 cup orange juice
1 1/4 cup sliced cranberries
1 cup chopped pecans or English Walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Mix together flour, salt, baking powder, sugar and soda.  Add shortening, eggs, orange zest and orange juice in and stir till just moistened.  Fold in berries and nuts.  Pour into 12 lined muffin tins and bake for 18 -25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Cat Hill
Description: 
This is my Mom's recipe from the 1980's.  It's been so long since I have made it but I remember the sweetness from the orange juice and the tanginess of the cranberry in a nice light muffin.  Perfect for a snack or breakfast.
