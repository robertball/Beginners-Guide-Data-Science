Title:
Classic Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/277588.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound uncooked cellantani or rotelle pasta
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup distilled white vinegar
2/3 cup white sugar
2 1/2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 stalks celery, chopped
1/2 pound sugarsnaps or snowpeas, trimmed and cut into bite-size pieces
1/4 cup grated carrot

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add the noodles, and cook until tender, about 8 -10 minutes adding sugarsnaps during the last minute of cooking.
In a large bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, mustard, salt and pepper. Stir in the celery, carrot, peas, and pasta. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving, but preferably overnight.
