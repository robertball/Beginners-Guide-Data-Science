Title:
Nacho Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/259007.jpg
Ingredients:
10 cups popped popcorn
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
1 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Place popcorn in a large bowl. In a small bowl, combine butter, paprika, cumin and red pepper flakes. Pour over popcorn and toss to coat. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and toss again.

Submitter: 
Kay Young
Description: 
When I allow myself to indulge in a snack, I make this spicy popcorn. One batch doesn't last long at our house. You can add more or less red pepper flakes to suit your family's taste.
