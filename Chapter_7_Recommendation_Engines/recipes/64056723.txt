Title:
Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz rotini, cooked
2 cups chopped celery
1 small red onion chopped
4 oz pimento
6 oz marinated artichoke hearts
3 cups  ham chopped and seared
5 large mushrooms, chopped
1 small can sliced olives
5 oz frozen peas
1 bunch green onions, chopped
1/2 bunch broccoli, chopped
1/2 cup Monterey jack cheese
1/2 bottle herbed Italian dressing
6 oz cashews

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except cashews. Let stand for several hours. Add cashews right before serving.
