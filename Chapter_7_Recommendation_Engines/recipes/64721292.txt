Title:
Hamburger Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 # ground beef
1 Small onion finely chopped
2 T. minced green pepper (Optional)
1 C. chopped celery
3 beef bouillon cubes
1 t. Kitchen Bouquet
1 1/2 t. salt
1 1/2 C. water divided
3 T. Flour

Directions:
1.  Brown the meat with the onion. Drain.
2.  Add celery, 1 C. water, Kitchen Bouquet and seasonings.
3. Mix the flour with 1/2 C. of water and add to mixture.
4.  Simmer for about 20 min. until celery is somewhat softened.
5.  Serve over mashed potatoes.

Submitter: 
Jim
Description: 
A fantastic meat and sauce mix served over mashed potatoes.  This was served in the Bloomington, MN schools in the 60's and was a favorite of all.
