Title:
Sheep herders hors d'oeuerves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 saltine crackers
1/2 sm onion, rings cut into 1 inch long pieces
About 3 oz. cheddar cheese cut into squares
2 oranges, seedless

Directions:
Onto each cracker, place a square of cheese
Layer a piece of onion on top of cheese
Layer a small piece of orange on top of onion
Enjoy!
(To have some fun, keep these treats hidden, and make your guests try them without looking and then ask the guest to name the ingredients.)

Submitter: 
Carol Prather
Description: 
An interesting combination, and the name means you can tease your guests before they see it.
