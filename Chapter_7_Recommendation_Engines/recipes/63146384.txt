Title:
BBQ 'pulled' tuna sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sliced red onion
3 cloves garlic, diced
3 cans tuna, drained
1 tsp worcesterchire sauce
1 tbsp steak seasoning
3/4 to 1 cup bbq sauce
4 large soft buns

Directions:
In a skillet saute the onions and garlic in water until tender (about 5 mins). Add the tuna, worchestershire sauce, and steak spice and mix breifly to combine everything. Add the desired amount of bbq sauce depending on how saucy you like your sandwiches. Allow to cook on low heat for about 5 minutes to allow the flavours to come together.
Heap tuna mixture onto 4 warm buttered buns and serve. Goes excellent with a nice crisp coleslaw!

Description: 
Pulled pork sandwiches are one of my favorite things in the world and I made this recipe to sort of capture the taste with less less time and work! Also healthier!
