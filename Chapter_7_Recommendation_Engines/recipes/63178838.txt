Title:
Grandmother B's sauce for Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (284 ml) can of jelled cranberry
Juice of 1 orange and zest
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 tablespoon prepared mustard
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tablespoon honey
2 whole cloves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degree F
Boil all the ingredients for 10 mins or until fully dissolved
Place ham face down and score the ham so the sauce soaks into the ham
Cook for 2 hours

Submitter: 
Maryet Mann
Description: 
This recipe has been in the family for years. We always make this for Easter dinner.
