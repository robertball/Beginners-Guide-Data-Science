Title:
Bacon Wrapped Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Chicken Breasts
1 8 oz Container Chive & Onion Cream Cheese
1 Pkg of Bacon
Salt to tast

Directions:
Pound chicken between plastic wrap until 1/2 inch thick. Lightly salt one side of chicken. Butter the same side with Cream cheese. Roll chicken up. Wrap Chicken with bacon and place in a caserole dish.
Bake at 350 degrees for 35 min. Drain off Grease and broil for 10 more min to crisp up the bacon.
