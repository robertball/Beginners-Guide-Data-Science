Title:
Big Ray's Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1129438.jpg
Ingredients:
2 packages (8 ounces each) cream cheese, softened
1 envelope ranch salad dressing mix
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheeese
5 green onions, chopped
1 can (4 ounces) chopped green chilies, drained
1 can (3.8 ounces) sliced ripe olives, drained
1 celery rib, chopped
1/4 cup chopped sweet red pepper
4 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
8 large slices of ham
8 flour tortillas (10 inches)

Directions:
In a small bowl, beat cream cheese and dressing mix until blended. Beat in the onions, green chilies, olives, celery, red pepper and bacon.  Mix in cheese.  Spread over tortillas. place slice of ham on top of spread.  Roll up and wrap in plastic wrap.  Put into refrigerator for 2 hours before trying to cut each into 1-in. slices. Refrigerate leftovers.
