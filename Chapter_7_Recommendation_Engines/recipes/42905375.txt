Title:
Jalapeno White Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Whipping Cream
1 cup Sour Cream
1 tsp Chicken Base
2 Tbsp Calrified Butter
1 Tbsp Flour
1 Jalapeno, Minced
1 Tbsp Juice from Bottled Jalapenos
2 oz Shredded Cheese - Eual parts Jack & Cheddar

Directions:
Heat Whipping cream in a heavy saucepan over high heat
When the cream is ready to boil, stir in sour cream.
After Sour cream dissolves, reduce heat to medium. Stri in chicken base and jalapeno juice and simmer.
While cream is heating make a roux by warming butter in a saucepan over medium heat, adding flour, and mixing with a wire whip until mixture starts to turn pale gold.
Just before the cream mixture is ready to boil again, add roux, whisking briskly and constantly until roux is incorporated.
Remove from heat; stir in minced jalapeno and the cheese mixtrue
