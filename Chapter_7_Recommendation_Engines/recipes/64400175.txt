Title:
Crustless Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup flour
3/4 cup milk
1 lb. cheese
4 oz. cream cheese
2 cups cottage cheese
10 eggs
Add. Bacon, ham, veggies of choice

Directions:
Melt butter, add flour,add rest of ingredients - bake or refrigerate overnight.  Bake 350 degrees for 45-60 minutes
