Title:
Create Your Custom Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1528392.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (21 ounce) cans LUCKY LEAFÂ® Premium Fruit Filling--Apple, Cherry or Blueberry
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 1/2 cups quick oats
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup finely chopped or sliced nuts of your choice
You Choose:
Coconut, chocolate chips, cinnamon chips, dried fruit

Directions:
Pick your favorite LUCKY LEAF(R) Premium Pie Filling: Apple, Cherry or Blueberry.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Place two cans of pie filling in bottom of 9x13-inch pan.
Blend softened butter, oatmeal, brown sugar and nuts until crumbly. Now's the time to add in anything else you'd like to include in your crisp.*
Sprinkle mixture over pie filling. Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
*ADD IN YOUR FAVORITES: coconut, chocolate chips, cinnamon chips, dried fruit, etc. The options are endless and the choice is yours!

Submitter: 
Lucky Leaf Fruit Filling
Description: 
Choose your favorite fruit pie filling, sprinkle with a buttery oat, nut, and brown sugar mixture (with the addition of your favorite 'secret' ingredient) for a fruit crisp customized to your personal taste.
