Title:
Lemon pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1pkg lemon jello pie filling
2/3C sugar
1/4C water
3 eggs seperated
2C water
2Tbsp lemon jucie
2Tbsp butter
1 9in prebaked pie crust
6Tbsp sugar

Directions:
Combine the pie filling, sugar and 1/4C water in a sauce pan. Heat on medium. Mix in 3 eggs yolks and then add 2C water. Cook and stir to a full boil.(about 5min) Then let cool. Once cool blend inn the lemon jucie and butter. Pour into pie shell. In another bowl add the egg whites and beat until foamy.
Gradually add 6Tbsps of sugar, beating until stiff peaks form. Spread over the pie filling. Bake at 455 for 5-10min or until mering is lightly browned.
