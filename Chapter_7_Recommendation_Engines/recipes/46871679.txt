Title:
Spaghetti Con Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# spaghetti,cooked
24 oz. half and half cream
1/4 C butter
1/2 # broccoli florets
1 C sliced mushrooms
1/4 C minced parsley
1 1/2 t. fresh minced garlic
1 T black pepper
2 t salt
3/4 C freshly grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Combine cream, butter, mushrooms, parsley and seasonings in sauce pan over med. heat for 3-5 min.
Increase heat slightly and bring to boil
When boil reached, add cooked and drained spaghetti.
Reduce over med. heat until sauce clings to speghetti.
Remove from heat and toss in parmesan cheese a little at a time until desired consistency
