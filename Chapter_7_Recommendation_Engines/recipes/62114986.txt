Title:
Rise 'N' Shine Baked Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices of bacon, chopped
4 slices of ham, chopped
8 oz. sliced mushrooms
1/2 green bell pepper, chopped
6 green onions, chopped
16 oz. frozen shredded hashbrown potatoes, thawed
12 eggs
1/2 cup half & half
1/4 cup milk
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
8 oz. shredded sharp cheddar cheese
3/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large frying pan, cook bacon until almost crisp. Add ham, mushrooms, bell pepper and green onions. Cook over medium high heat until vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and add shredded hashbrowns, stirring until combined.
In a large bowl, beat eggs, half & half, milk, salt and pepper.
Transfer the bacon mixture to a 13x9x2-inch pan and spread evenly over bottom. Pour egg mixture into baking pan. Sprinkle cheeses on top of casserole and bake in preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until cheeses are melted on top and eggs are set.

Submitter: 
BlondeCook
Description: 
A simple and hearty egg casserole. A wonderful start to a weekend morning or for a special holiday breakfast or brunch.
