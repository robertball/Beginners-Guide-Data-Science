Title:
Grilled Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Salmon fillet
Lemon juice
White wine
Mrs. Dash seasoning
Fresh rosemary
Fresh basil

Directions:
Place salmon fillet on a double layer of foil large enough to fold over.
Liberally sprinkle lemon juice and then white wine over the Salmon.
Sprinkle seasoning and lay herbs over Salmon.
Fold tin foil over to package up the Salmon.
Grill on medium or bake at 350Â° for 20 minutes.
