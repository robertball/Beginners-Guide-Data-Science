Title:
Pancit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. oil
1 small onion - minced
2 cloves garlic - minced
1 lb. boneless chicken breast - sliced
1 cup carrot - julienned
2 cups thinly sliced cabbage
1 1/2 cups water
1 to 1 1/2 tbsp. soy sauce
8 oz. pancit noodles

Directions:
Place oil in a large saute pan.  Add onions and cook until translucent.
Add garlic and cook about 1 minute.
Add chicken and cook until lightly browned.
Add carrots and cabbage and stir to mix.
Add water and simmer for 1 minute.
Add soy sauce and noodles.
Simmer until liquid is absorbed and noodles are tender - about 5 to 10 minutes.
Season to taste.
