Title:
Dad's Famous Easy-Rise Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups flour
1 heaping tablespoon kosher salt
1 packet rapid rise yeast
2 cups cold water

Directions:
Use electric mixer with dough hook or food processor with dough blade.
Combine flour, salt, and yeast in mixer or food processor for one minute on low.
Mix for one minute more and slowly pour in the cold water over 60 seconds.
Let dough rest for five minutes.
Mix for another 60 seconds then remove dough and place in a large bowl.  Cover with plastic wrap and let rise for 3-6 hours.
Sprinkle surface with flour, punch down dough, form into a ball.
Place dough ball on a pizza pan sprinkled with corn meal.  Allow to rise for another hour.
Heat oven to 400 degrees.  Bake for 35 minutes.  While baking, throw in 1/4 cup of water on the oven floor.  Set for two minutes and then throw in another 1/4 cup of water.

Submitter: 
Megan Fellows
Description: 
Great served warm with butter for dinner or in the morning as toast.
