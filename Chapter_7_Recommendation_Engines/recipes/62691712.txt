Title:
Caesar Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/610273.jpg
Ingredients:
a couple of cloves of galic, peeled
1 tbs white vinegar
1 tbs yellow mustard
1 cup mayonaise (Hellmann's)
1 cup of olive oil or less (depending on how thick
you want the consistency)
1/2 tbs lemon juice
1 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Throw the garlic into a mini food processor or blender  and turn on until all the garlic is minced.
Add the vinegar, mustard and mayonaise. Turn on the processor and slowly add 1/2c to 1 c of olive oil. (how much depends on how thick you want the dressing)
Once oil is mixed in add the lemon juice and parmesan cheese.
