Title:
Chicken Rice Soup with Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. olive oil
2 cloves of garlic - minced
3 - 14.5 oz. cans of chicken broth
1 1/2 cups roma tomatoes with basil
1/2 cup chopped onion
10 oz. fresh spinach - torn
3/4 cup instant rice
3 cups cooked chicken

Directions:
Saute onion and garlic until soft.
Add broth and tomatoes and bring to a boil.
Add rice, spinach and chicken.
Cover and remove from heat and let sit 5 minutes until rice is tender.
