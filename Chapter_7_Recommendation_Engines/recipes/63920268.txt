Title:
Best No Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1087137.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes
5 cup coffee
1 can dark (porter style) beer
2 (6 oz) cans tomato paste
1 (10oz) can of beef broth
4 tablespoons chili sauce
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 tablespoon cumin
1 teaspoon cocoa
1 teaspoon oregano
1 teaspoon cayenne
1 teaspoon coriander
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons Ancho chili powder
4 tablespoons jalapeno chopped peppers
1/2 cup corn meal
2 teaspoons olive oil
2 onions - chopped
3 cloves garlic - minced
2 lbs lean ground beef (80%+)

Directions:
Place double boiler pot or slow cooker setting to "high" and add:   Coffee, tomatoes, tomato paste, beef broth, beer, chili sauce, chili powder, jalapeno peppers, cumin, cocoa, oregano, cayenne, coriander, salt and the chopped onions to the pot.  Heat the oil in large frying pan and cook the garlic until brown then pour it into your pot.  Cook meat in frying pan till brown.  Drain off grease and add to pot. Make sure there are no lumps of meat.  Slowly add the corn meal to pot while stirring until well mixed.  Lower the pot's heat setting to "simmer" and cook for two (2) hours covered. Stir mixture every 30 minutes.  Serve. Yum!
