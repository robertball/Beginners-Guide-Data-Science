Title:
Creamy Chocolate Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988149.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups cold milk
2 pkg. (4 serving size)  JELL-O Chocolate Instant Pudding
1 (8 ounce) tub COOL WHIP Whipped Topping, thawed
1 OREO Pie Crust (6 oz.)

Directions:
Pour milk into large bowl. Add dry pudding mixes. Beat with wire whisk 2 min. (Mixture will be thick.) Gently stir in whipped topping.
Spoon into crust.
Refrigerate 4 hours or until set. Store leftover pie in refrigerator.
