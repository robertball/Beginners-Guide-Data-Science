Title:
Crescent Wrap Chicken Parm Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 uncooked chicken tenderloins (about 1 1/4 lb)
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper SAVE $
3 tablespoons butter SAVE $
2 oz deli mozzarella cheese
1 can (8 oz) Pillsbury™ refrigerated crescent dinner rolls SAVE $
3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
1 cup marinara sauce

Directions:
1. Heat oven to 375°F. Spray cookie sheet with cooking spray; set aside.
2. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
3.  In 10-inch nonstick skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of the butter over medium-high heat; cook chicken in butter 8 to 10 minutes or until chicken is well browned on all sides. Remove from skillet; cool slightly.
4. Cut mozzarella cheese into 8 pieces; cut small slits in side of each chicken tenderloin, and insert piece of mozzarella cheese.
5. Unroll dough; separate into 8 triangles. Place piece of chicken on wide end of triangle; roll dough around chicken, and place on cookie sheet. Repeat for remaining dough and chicken.
6. In small microwavable cup, microwave remaining 1 tablespoon butter uncovered on High 15 to 30 seconds or until melted.
7. Brush crescents with melted butter; top each with about 1 teaspoon Parmesan cheese.
8. Bake 14 to 18 minutes or until golden brown.
9. In microwavable measuring cup, heat marinara sauce covered on Medium-High (70%) 1 to 2 minutes or until heated through.
10. Serve chicken with heated marinara sauce.
