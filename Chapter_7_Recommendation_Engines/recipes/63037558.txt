Title:
Ultimate Party Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can Ocean Spray® Jellied Cranberry Sauce
1 (14ounce) can ocean spray whole cranberry sauce
2 (12 ounce) bottle Heinz® Chili Sauce
1 tsp granulated garlic
2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
2 cups brown sugar
1 (2 pound) bag frozen, pre-cooked, cocktail-size meatballs

Directions:
Combine sauces in a large saucepan.  Cook over medium-low heat, stirring until smooth.  Add meatballs and sauce in crockpot.  Cook until done.

Submitter: 
RAEVYAN
Description: 
A new twist to a classic party appetizer, these easy to make meatballs are cooked in a cranberry-chili sauce and ready to serve your guests in minutes.Modified from: Ultimate Party Meatballs
