Title:
Homemade Burrito Bowls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. marinated carne asada (available at Trader Joes)
1 can pinto beans
Shredded Mexican Cheese
Spanish rice (use Best Spanish Rice recipe)
Lettuce
Salsa
Sour cream
Guacamole

Directions:
Grill steak on high, about 3-4 minutes on each side.
Prepare spanish rice according to recipe.
Heat beans in microwave.
Prepare your own burrito bowl, using whatever toppings you would like. Serve with chips and guacamole.

Description: 
A delicious mexican entree that is easy and satisfying. Perfect with homemade margaritas!
