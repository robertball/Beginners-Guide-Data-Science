Title:
Easy Au Gratin Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 russet potatoes, sliced into 1/4 inch slices
salt and pepper to taste
1/3 cup butter
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
3/4 teaspoon salt
2 cups half and half
1 cup milk
2 cups Shredded Gruyere cheese
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Butter a 1 quart casserole dish.
Layer potatoes into bottom of the prepared casserole dish.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.
In a medium-size saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Mix in the flour and salt, and stir constantly with a whisk for one minute. Stir in cream and milk. Cook until mixture has thickened. Stir in cheese all at once, and continue stirring until melted, about 30 to 60 seconds. Pour cheese over the potatoes, and cover the dish with aluminum foil.  Take foil off for last 20 minutes to brown the top.
Bake 1 1/2 hours in the preheated oven.
