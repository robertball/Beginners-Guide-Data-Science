Title:
Cheese Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 bag ore ida potatoes o'brien potatoes, thawed
- 1 can cream of celery soup
- 1 bag shredded cheddar cheese
- 3 tbsp. melted butter
- 1 small container sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Mix together potatoes, soup, 1/2 bag of cheese, butter, and sour cream.  Spread into greased casserole dish.  Top with remaining cheese.  Cook for 1 hour or until brown around the edges and cheese is melted on top.

Submitter: 
Eric
Description: 
Deliciously cheesy recipe thats quick and easy.  My stepmom used to make me this and we always had to make 2 batches for the whole family.  A definite for potlucks!
