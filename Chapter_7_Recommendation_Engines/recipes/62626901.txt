Title:
French Toast Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/574795.jpg
Ingredients:
For Pancakes
2 cups Bisquik
2 eggs
1 cup 1% milk
For French Toast Batter
4 eggs
1/4 cup 1% milk
2 tbs orange juice (can use Grand Marnier instead)
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
combine ingredients for french toast batter in a shallow dish and whisk until well combined. set aside. Combine ingredients for pancakes in a large mixing bowl and beat until smooth & fluffy.
Make slightly larger pancakes than usual. When pancake is done, put it right into french toast batter to soak. Then transfer to buttered fry pan/griddle on medium heat. Cook until golden brown with a slight crisp on both sides.

Submitter: 
Doug Matthews
Description: 
You can make french toast out of anything so why nit hot fluffy pancakes dipped in a flavorful french toast egg batter then grilled in butter. Decadent any way you say it.
