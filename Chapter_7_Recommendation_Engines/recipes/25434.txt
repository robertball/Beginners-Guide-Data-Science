Title:
BBQ Sauce to Live For Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4971102.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup barbeque sauce
1 cup duck sauce

Directions:
In a medium bowl, blend barbeque sauce and duck sauce. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.

Submitter: 
Jaynine
Description: 
A slightly sweet and oh so tasty barbeque sauce that will have you licking your plate. The combination of ingredients is so simple, you'll be shocked. It's great for coating chicken or ribs, mixing with plain noodles or on top of white or yellow rice.
