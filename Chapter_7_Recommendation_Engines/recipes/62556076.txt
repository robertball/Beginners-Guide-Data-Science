Title:
Easy Pumpkin muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/533144.jpg
Ingredients:
Muffin mix:
1 (18 ounce) box spice cake mix
1 (15 ounces)can pumpkin puree (make sure it says pure pumpkin and not pumpkin pie mix)
1/2 cup water
Topping:
1 Cup Brown Sugar
1 Stick Margarine
1 Cup Flour
Stir together flour and brown sugar, breaking up any lumps. Cut in margarine. Spoon or sprinkle on top of muffins, pat down slightly. Bake at 350 for 20-24 minutes.
*Any leftover topping can be refrigerated for several weeks or frozen for several months until needed.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Grease a 12 cup muffin tin.
In mixing bowl, place all ingredients. Mix until well blended.
Scoop into muffin tin.
Make topping

Submitter: 
Jessi Renee Bigney
Description: 
This is a very simple pumpkin spice muffin with a streusel topping that is both tasty and hard to mess up even for children.
