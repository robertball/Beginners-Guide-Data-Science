Title:
Eggplant and Pasta Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium eggplants, washed and cut into 2" chunks
Salt
1/4 C. flour
16 oz. can fire-roasted diced tomatoes
1 small onion, diced
4 cloves of garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. dried basil
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. cracked black pepper
Cayenne to taste
1/3 C. olive oil
2 C. shredded mozzarella or provolone (or a mix)
1/2 lb. whole grain penne or rigatoni pasta, cooked al dente
1 C. pasta water*
1/2 C. Panko bread crumbs
1/2 C. Parmesan Cheese
Olive oil

Directions:
Place the eggplant in a large bowl
Sprinkle the eggplant with salt and allow the eggplant to sit for about 30 minutes--permitting the water to drain from the eggplant
PREHEAT oven to 350
Put the flour into a large Ziploc bag and add the drained eggplant
Shake to coat
Heat, over medium high heat, 3 Tbl. of olive oil in a non-stick Dutch oven**
Add the eggplant and lightly brown on one side for about 3-5 minutes
Sprinkle the onion and garlic over the eggplant and STIR gently
Brown about 3 minutes
Remove the eggplant from the heat
Add the canned tomatoes, the remaining olive oil, the basil, salt, pepper and cayenne to taste and gently stir
Meanwhile, boil to al dente the pasta and drain well and KEEP about 1 Cup of the pasta water
Add the pasta to the eggplant mixture
Top with 2 C. shredded mozzarella or provolone and gently stir to incorporate
Add 1 Cup of the starchy pasta water and stir
Top with Panko bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese
Drizzle with olive oil
BAKE for about 30 minutes, uncovered, until the casserole is bubbly and the bread crumbs begin to brown

Submitter: 
Sally Anne
Description: 
A deconstructed Eggplant Parmesan served prepared and baked in the same pot.
