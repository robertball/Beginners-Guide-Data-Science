Title:
Smoked Turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 pound turkey
olive oil
sea salt
Traeger apple pellets (do not's substitute)

Directions:
Rub skin with olive oil.  Sprinkle with sea salt.  Smoke for 4-6 hours, turning every 2 hours.  Wrap with plastic wrap.  Wrap with heavy duty aluminum foil.  Refrigerate until ready to finish.  Cook on high until you the internal temperature is reached.
