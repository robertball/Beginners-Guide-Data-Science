Title:
Mexican Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkg (8-1/2oz) corn bread/muffin mix
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cups (8oz) shredded cheddar cheese
1 can (14-3/4oz) cream-style corn
1-1/2 cups (12oz) sour cream
4 eggs beaten
1 can (4oz) chopped green chilies
1 TBS finely chopped jalapeno pepper
1/3 cup veggie oil

Directions:
In a bowl, combine corn bread mix and onion.  Combine the remaining ingredients; add to the corn bread mixture just until moistened.  Pour into a greased 13x9x2 baking dish.
Bake at 350 for 50-55 minutes or until lighlt browned and the edges pull away from sides of pan.  Serve warm.
