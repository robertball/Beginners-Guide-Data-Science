Title:
Adrian Leon's Chili Piquin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pinch ground allspice
1 pinch cinnamon
1 pinch salt
1 pinch pepper
1/2 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1 teaspoon chili piquin or ground cayenne
1/2 cup mixed nuts
1 egg white

Directions:
Pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees F.
Mix all the dry spices and SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener in a bowl.
Whip the egg white in a separate mixer bowl on medium high until soft peaked, then stir in the mixed nuts and other dry ingredients.
Spread the nuts over a baking sheet and bake at 350 degrees  for 10 minutes.
Remove from oven cool and serve.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
These sweet and spicy nuts are seasoned with allspice, cinnamon and chili piquin.
