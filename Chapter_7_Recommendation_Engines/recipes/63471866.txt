Title:
Smoky Gouda Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. shredded Smoky Gouda Cheese
1 C. shredded Boar's Head Chipotle Gouda Cheese (also known as "Red Hot Dutch" available at Whole Foods)
2 C plus 2 T mayonnaise
3/4 C chopped onion
4 oz. cooked chopped bacon

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl.  Place mixture in an ovenproof dish.  Bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes until cheese mixture is bubbly and lightly brown along the edges OR microwave 3-4 minutes on high until bubbly then broil on low for several minutes until lightly brown.
Serve warm with baguette slices, crackers, or pita chips.

Submitter:
