Title:
Gourmet Gelly Shots: Malibu Goodness Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/875198.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup boiling water
1 (6 ounce) box strawberry-banana-flavored Jell-O® mix
1 cup coconut-flavored rum (such as Malibu®)

Directions:
Arrange 12 2-ounce plastic cups on a baking sheet.
Slowly pour boiling water over gelatin mix in a bowl while whisking. Continue whisking while streaming rum into the gelatin mixture. Pour mixture into plastic cups.
Refrigerate until set, 8 hours to overnight.
