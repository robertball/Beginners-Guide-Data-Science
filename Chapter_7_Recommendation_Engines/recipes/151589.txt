Title:
Creamy Mushroom-Garlic Chicken Recipe

1 tablespoon vegetable oil
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Cream of Mushroom with Roasted Garlic Soup

Directions:

Add soup and milk. Heat to a boil. Cover and cook over low heat 5 minutes or until done.
