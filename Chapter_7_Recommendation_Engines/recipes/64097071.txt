Title:
Big Ray's Scrambled Egg  Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1215117.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb bulk pork sausage
12 eggs
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped green pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°. In a large skillet, cook sausage over medium heat until no longer pink; drain.
In a large bowl, beat eggs. Add onion, green pepper, salt, garlic powder and pepper. Stir in sausage and cheese.
Spoon evenly into  jumbo muffin cups coated with cooking spray. Bake 20-25 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.

Description: 
One of the great things about this recipe is these freeze well so they are ready when you are on the go.  Grab a cup of coffee and heat one of these and you are out the door.
