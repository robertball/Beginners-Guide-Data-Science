Title:
Grilled Bacon Wrapped Quail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3725128.jpg
Ingredients:
-quail breasts (at least 2 per person)
-fresh jalapeños
-bacon
-toothpicks
-black pepper
-Italian dressing

Directions:
-let quail marinate in Italian dressing for at least a few hours
-cut jalapeños lengthwise into strips (4strips per pepper)
-place each jalapeño strip skin side down into the breast of each bird
-tightly wrap a slice of bacon around bird and secure with toothpick
-sprinkle with black pepper
-grill each side 5-6 minutes (don't overcook because quail will dry out)
