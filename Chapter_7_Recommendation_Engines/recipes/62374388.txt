Title:
Mike's Amazing Enchilada Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/413421.jpg
Ingredients:
3 T olive oil or animal fat such as butter or bacon grease
3 T flour
1/4 C chili powder
1/2 yellow onion, minced fine
or 3 T dried minced onion
3 cloves of garlic, minced
dash of chipotle powder
1/2 C El Pato Mexican style tomato sauce
Chicken stock or Caldo de Pollo dissolved in water as needed to create right consistency

Directions:
Melt fat in skillet
If using fresh onion, saute onion in fat until translucent
Add flour and saute until flour is medium brown
Add chili powder, garlic, chipotle powder
Carefully add El Pato tomato sauce ... it will boil immediately!
Add caldo de pollo or chicken stock to desired consistency
Whisk until smooth
Simmer 10 minutes
