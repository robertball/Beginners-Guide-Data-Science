Title:
BBQ pork over noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb. boneless pork
1 1/2 c. ketchup
1 c. water
4 T lemon juice
22 T vinegar
3 T W. sauce
3 drops tobasco
2 T brown sugar
1/4 c. sugar
1 t. chili pwd
chopped onions
cooked noodles

Directions:
Cook pork and chop into small pieces. Combine other ingredients. Place meat in 9 X 13 pan and pour sauce over. Bake at 350, covered for 1-2 hours.  OR can pressure cook pork roast about 50 minutes, cool, cut up. Put all in pressure cooker and cook for 20 minutes. Serve over noodles.
