Title:
Cream of Mushroom soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup chopped red pepper
1 tbsp flour
3 cups beef broth
1 1/2 cups light cream
1 pakage sliced various mushrooms (cremini, white, baby portabellos)
salt/pepper
tarragon

Directions:
Saute onion/celery/red pepper in butter til about 3/4 done.  Throw in pot with 3 cups beef broth.  Saute mushrooms til about 3/4 done then throw them in too.  Chop finer if you wish beforehand.  Season and bring this to simmer, simmer for a while.
Let soup cool.  Whisk flour into cream add, reheat soup and blend.  Enjoy
