Title:
Waffles I* Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/552829.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 3/4 cups milk
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 teaspoons white sugar
1 teaspoon (1 packet) Splenda®
4 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon Morton Lite® salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat waffle iron. Beat eggs in large bowl with hand beater until fluffy.  Beat in flour, milk, vegetable oil, sugar, baking powder, salt and vanilla,  just until smooth.
Pour in waffle iron 1 cup at a time (2 cups) for perfect "four-waffle" square of Belgian waffles.

Submitter: 
Tracy Ann
Description: 
You can keep the batter, covered, in the fridge for up to a week.  Just be sure to whisk well before using again. You may also use whole wheat flour for this recipe.Modified from: Waffles I
