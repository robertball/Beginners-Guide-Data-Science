Title:
Baked Pineapple Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (20-ounce) can pineapple chunks in juice
1/2 cup of sugar
3 TBSP all-purpose flour
1 cup shredded chedar cheese
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
1/2 cup crushed ritz crackers

Directions:
Drain pineapple, save 3 tablespoon of juice.
Combine sugar and flour; add cheese, pineapple and pineapple juice.
Mix well.  Put in greased baking dish.
Mix crused crakers and melted butter together; sprinkle over pineapple mixture.
Bake for 30 minutes at 350

Submitter: 
Shannon Campbell
Description: 
A perfect side dish for baked ham
