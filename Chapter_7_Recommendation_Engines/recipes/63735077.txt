Title:
Crab Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. sour cream
1/2 c. mayonnaise
1 T. lemon juice
1 T. honey mustard
1 T. dill
1/2 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
12 oz. uncooked pasta
1 lb. crab (real or imitation)
1/2 c. green onion, diced

Directions:
Pre-cook pasta until al-dente. Drain, and set aside.
In a bowl, mix together all other ingredients.
Add pasta, and stir well.
Chill before serving.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
A creamy, delicious mixture of goodness sure to lure even the skittish.
