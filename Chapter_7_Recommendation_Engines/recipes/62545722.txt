Title:
Lebanese Zaatar (Za'atar) Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3  cups Warm (not boiling) Water
1  teaspoon Salt
1  teaspoon Sugar
1  package Yeast
1  cup Corn Oil
2  cups  Olive Oil
2  cups  Whole Wheat Flour
8  cups  All Purpose Flour
2-3  cups  Fresh Za'atar (may substitute fresh Oregano but use less)

Directions:
Mix water, salt, sugar, and yeast together in large bowl.
Add oils and flour and mix well with hands. Add more flour if needed.
Add za'atar and mix to combine.
Shape handfuls of dough into rounds on floured work surface.
Bake in 350-400 F oven for 25 minutes.
May place under broiler to brown tops.

Submitter: 
Stacey
Description: 
Traditional Lebanese bread. Crispy, flaky cracker like bread infused with fresh za'atar (similar to oregano).
