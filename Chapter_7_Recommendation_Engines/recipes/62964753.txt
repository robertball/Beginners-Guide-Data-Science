Title:
Morroccan Lamb and Winter Vegetable Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/4 cup flour
1 tbsp ras el hanout
1 tsp sweet paprika
salt & pepper
1 pound lamb stew meat, cubed
1 medium onion, thinly sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 cups roasted vegetable stock (or beef)
1 15-oz can diced tomatoes (about 1 1/2 cups)
1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
Salt & pepper
2 cups peeled, seeded, and cubed buttercup squash
1 cup peeled, sliced parsnips
1 cup peeled, diced carrots
1/2 cup sliced celery
1/2 cup sour cream or yogurt (for garnish)
Chopped fresh mint

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large saucepan.  combine the flour, ras el hanout, paprika and salt & pepper; toss lamb cubes in flour mixture.  Brown the lamb meat on all sides. Add onions and garlic and cook for a few minutes. Stir in the stock and tomatoes. Season with thyme, salt, pepper, and bay leaf. Bring the mixture to a boil.
Add squash, parsnips, carrots and celery. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 20 to 40 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
Remove bay leaves before serving.
Garnish with sour cream or yogurt and fresh chopped mint when serving.
