Title:
P.A.M. Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 pineapple, peeled and cored
2 Granny Smith apples, halved
4-5 Mint leaves
1 oz lime juice
Water, as need for blending

Directions:
Add all ingredients to blender or juicer. Blend until smooth. Strain if desired.

Submitter: 
Brittany Curtis
Description: 
Great when used as part of a healthy cleanse.
