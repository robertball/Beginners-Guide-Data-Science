Title:
Cheesy Apple Bacon Brunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. sliced bacon
1 C. apple, peeled, chopped
2 Tbs. sugar
1 1/2 C. Original BisquickÂ® pancake mix
1 1/2 C. milk
4 eggs
2 C. shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees. Spray an 11x7-inch (2-quart) glass baking dish with cooking spray. In 12-inch skillet, cook bacon over low heat 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until crisp. Drain on paper towels. Crumble bacon; set aside. In small bowl, mix apple and sugar; spread in baking dish. In medium bowl, stir Bisquick mix, milk and eggs until blended; pour over apple. Sprinkle with cheese and bacon. Bake uncovered 30-35 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
