Title:
Hot Carmel Apple Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1078185.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups apple juice
3 tablespoons caramel syrup (such as Hershey'sÂ®)
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 cup whipped cream, or to taste (optional)

Directions:
Whisk apple juice, caramel syrup, cinnamon, and nutmeg together in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Cook and whisk briskly until mixture is hot but not boiling, about 3 minutes. Add vanilla; cook and stir for 1 more minute. Pour into mugs and top with whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Kelly Provost
Description: 
A great warm fall drink. Great replacement for a winter's hot cocoa! My children love it. I came up with it when I wanted something warm and sweet but didn't want the caffeine. Like hot apple pie in a mug! Top with whipped cream.
