Title:
Banana Coconut Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2257033.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ripe frozen banana, peeled then frozen
1 cup cold coconut water
2.5 oz baby spinach (about 1-2 handfuls)
1 heaping tap natural, unsalted almond butter (or Nutzo from Costco)
1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Add all ingredients to blender and puree until smooth.  (If your blender has some issues blending everything properly, try adding coconut water, vanilla extract, and frozen bananas - blend until smooth - then add spinach and almond butter)
