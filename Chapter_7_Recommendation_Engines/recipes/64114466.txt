Title:
zucchini boats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium zucchini
1/2 c. chopped onion
2 T. butter
1 c. chopped tomato
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp poultry seasoning
dash pepper
6 oz. shredded sharp cheddar
4 slices crisply cooked bacon, crumbled

Directions:
Trim ends of zucchini and cook, covered, in boiling salted water 5- 8 minutes; drain. Cut in half lengthwise. When cool, scoop out centers and chop.
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine chopped zucchini and tomatoes.  Allow to drain for a few minutes.
Cook onion in butter till tender;  Add to tomatoes, zucchini and remaining ingredients.
Fill zucchini. Arrange in 13 x 9-inch baking pan.
Bake 30 minutes.
