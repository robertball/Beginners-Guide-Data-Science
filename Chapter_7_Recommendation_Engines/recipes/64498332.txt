Title:
Cheesy Mushroom Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2612016.jpg
Ingredients:
Chicken:
16 oz fresh sliced mushrooms
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 lb cooked and crumbled bacon
1 cup honey mustard sauce (see below)
1 1/2 cup Colby jack cheese, shredded
Honey Mustard Sauce:
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup honey
2 tbls Dijon mustard

Directions:
First, in a food processor, mix the honey mustard sauce and set in fridge.
Preheat oven to 350.
In baking dish layer mushroom on bottom and top with chicken, cover and place in oven for 20 Minutes. Remove and top with bacon, 3/4 of the honey mustard sauce and the shredded cheese,
place back in oven, uncovered for 45 to 55 minutes until chicken is done.
Serve with left over honey mustard as a dip. This is an EPIC dish.
