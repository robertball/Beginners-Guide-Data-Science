Title:
Chicken & Swiss Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 9 oz. pastry/pie shell
1 cup shredded swiss cheese
2 TBSP flour
1 TBSP chicken bouillon
2 cups chicken, cubed (perdue short cuts, original, work well)
1 cup milk
3 eggs, well beaten
1/4 cup onions, finely chopped
2 TBSP green peppers, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ยบ.  Place pastry shell in pie pan.  Mix remaining ingredients together in bowl.  Pour into pie shell and bake for 40-45 minutes.
