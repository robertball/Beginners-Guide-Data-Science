Title:
Plain Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup short grain rice
1 and ½ cup water

Directions:
In 3 Qt Instant pot
Layer 1: 1 cup rice
Layer 2: 1 and ½ cup of water
Select steam more mode ,set time for 5 mins. keep Vent in sealed position
Do a QR(Quick release of Pressure) after its completed.
Enjoy steam hot rice
Steam more mode : click on steam button twice.
