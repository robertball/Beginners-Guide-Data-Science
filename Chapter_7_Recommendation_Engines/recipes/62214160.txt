Title:
Crater Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c butter
2 c white sugar
2 eggs beaten
1 tsp vanilla
3 ripe bananas mashed
3 cups flour
2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp baking powder
1 c sour cream
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 c brown sugar packed
1/2 to 1  c choc chips
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Cream together the butter and sugar, add beaten eggs and beat until smooth. Add vanilla and bananas. Sift together flour, baking soda, baking powder. Alternatly mix into banana mixture with sour cream (1/4 of the flour, then 1/4 of sour cream than 1/4 flour ...etc) ending with dry ingredients. Pour half of the batter into a 9X13 in pan. Combine Cinnamon and brown sugar, sprinkle 1/2 of the brown sugar and cinnamon mixture over the bottom layer. Pour the rest of the batter into the pan try to cover the bottom layer as much as possible. Sprinkle the remaining sugar mixture over the top then sprinkle chocolate chips and walnut over the top. Bake at 350F for 45 to 50 minutes. Test cake in center with toothpick, when comes out clean remove from oven and let cool. Remember that there will be a slight indent in the center but it does not mean your cake is bad, it actually means you have done it totally right!!!

Submitter: 
C.Lynn
Description: 
Banana's, brown sugar and Chocolate make this unique cake simply wonderful. Dont be suprised when there is a small crater in the center of the cake.....its just does that everytime....This cake recipe was my son Justin's favorite when he was a little boy.....
