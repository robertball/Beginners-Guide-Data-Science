Title:
Creamy Mushroom and Asparagus Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups elbow macaroni
2 cups fresh asparagus cut into pieces
1 can of mushrooms or 1 cup fresh mushrooms
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup lowfat milk
1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
1/3 cup shredded cheese (part mozarella, part cheddar)
1 tsp ground black pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp seasoned salt, or to taste

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Boil pasta until el dente and drain. While pasta is boiling:
Heat soup, milk, cream, cheese and seasonings in a separate pan until cheese is melted. Mix the mushrooms and asparagus into the cream mixture and heat for a few more minutes.
Add cooked pasta to the cream mixture and mix well.
Pour into a greased 9 x 12 inch pan and top with extra shredded cheese.
Bake covered with aluminum foil for 45 minutes.
More pepper and salt can be added to taste when served.

Submitter: 
Heidi Habeck-Johnson
Description: 
I created this recipe today and turned out very good so I thought I would share it. Other vegetable such as broccoli can be substituted for asparagus.
