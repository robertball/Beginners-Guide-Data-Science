Title:
Chocolate Billionaires Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (14 ounce) package caramels
1/4 cup + 2 tablespoons water
2 1/2 cups chopped pecans
2 cup crisp rice cereal
6 cups milk chocolate chips
1 Tbsp shortening

Directions:
Line baking sheets with parchment paper; grease the paper and set aside. In a large heavy saucepan, combine the caramels and water; cook and stir over low heat until smooth. Stir in pecans and cereal until coated. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto prepared pans. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
Meanwhile, in a small heavy saucepan, melt chocolate chips and shortening over low heat; stir until smooth. Dip candy into chocolate, coating all sides; place on prepared pans. Refrigerate 30 mnutes until set. Store in an airtight container.
