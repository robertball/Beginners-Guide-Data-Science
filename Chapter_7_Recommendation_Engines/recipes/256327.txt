Title:
Twisted Ham and Turkey Grilled Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5194440.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons fresh corn kernels
2 teaspoons softened butter, divided
salt and ground black pepper to taste
1 teaspoon mayonnaise, or to taste
2 thick slices Italian bread
2 slices honey ham
2 slices honey turkey
1 slice Lacy Swiss cheese
1 slice Amish Swiss cheese
1 slice American cheese
2 tablespoons shredded Cheddar cheese, or to taste
1 Roma tomato, seeded and chopped
1 tablespoon chopped sweet onion
1 green onion, chopped

Directions:
Mix corn kernels with 1 teaspoon butter, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
Spread 1/2 teaspoon butter and 1/2 teaspoon mayonnaise over 1 bread slice; flip. Layer honey ham, turkey ham, Lacy Swiss cheese, Amish Swiss cheese, American cheese, and Cheddar cheese on top. Cover with corn mixture, tomato, sweet onion, and green onion. Cover with second slice of bread. Spread remaining 1/2 teaspoon butter and 1/2 teaspoon mayonnaise on the outside.
Preheat a skillet over medium-low heat. Cook sandwich until cheeses melt and bread turns golden brown, about 6 minutes per side.

Submitter: 
Kristin Licavoli
Description: 
This recipe combines honey turkey and ham, red onion, tomatoes, fresh corn on the cob, 4 kinds of cheese, and is made using mayo and butter to brown. A unique sandwich. Feel free to add a Mexican twist using cilantro and peppers! Serve with tomato soup.
