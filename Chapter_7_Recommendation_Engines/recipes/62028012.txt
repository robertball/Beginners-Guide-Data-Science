Title:
Garlic Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 garlic cloves, minced
1 Tbs. butter or olive oil
1 1/2 Tbs. Cornstarch
3 cups low fat milk or skim milk
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp. grated nutmeg
2/3 cup Parmesan Cheese, preferably fresh, not the canned stuff
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
In the butter or olive oil, saute the garlic for two minutes, over medium heat. Add the milk, reserving 1/4 cup. Mix the 1/4 cup milk with cornstarch. Set aside. Reduce the heat to medium low and bring to a low simmer. Add the reserved milk mixture and the pepper and nutmeg. Cook until thickend. Toss with your favorite pasta and the Parmesan cheese and sprinkle with the parsley.

Submitter: 
SABRYSON
Description: 
This is really good and a lot healthier than traditional alfredo sauces. I often serve this with chicken strips seasoned with pepper, garlic, rosemary, and minced onion and then sauted.
