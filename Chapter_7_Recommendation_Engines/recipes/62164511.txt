Title:
Healthy Vegan Breakfast Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
WET:
2cup Brown Sugar
2/3 cup organic cane sugar
1 1/2cup veg oil
1cup rice milk, 2 cup soy milk (or 3 cups soy or rice milk)
1/2c water w/ 2tbsp Chai  mixed in glass to thicken (10min)
3/4cup applesauce
6 Tbsp Molasses
1 Tsp Vanilla Extract
DRY:
4 cup Bran
1 cup W/W Flour
3/4cup Whole Grain Flour
3/4cup Soy Flour
1 1/2cup Wheat Germ
3 Tsp Baking Soda
1 Tbsp Arrowroot Powder
21/2 Tsp Cinnamon
3/4cup Flax Seeds
3/4cup Sesame Seeds
2 Tbsp Chai & 3 Tbsp Hemp Hearts
2 1/2cup Raisins & Dried Cranberries

Directions:
400 Degrees for 15 minutes
Makes about 50 muffins
Mix 1/2cup water with 2Tbsp Chai seeds in glass and let sit to thicken.
Mix wet ingredients in large bowl.  Mix dry ingredients in another large bowl.
Add chai thickener to wet ingredients.  Mix wet into dry.  Bake 400 for 15 minutes.
Cool on cookie racks. They freeze very well.  Just grab one and pop into microwave to warm.

Submitter: 
Deanna Hunter
