Title:
Mary's Whole Wheat Bread for Bread machine Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1.25 cup hot water
2 tablespoons melted Butter
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 tablespoons white sugar
1 teaspoons salt
1 1/4 cups bread flour
1 1/4 cups whole wheat flour
1 1/2 teaspoons Yeast

Directions:
Put all ingredients in bread machine in the order your bread machine calls for
