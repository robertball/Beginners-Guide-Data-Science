Title:
Pepperidge Chicken from Nina Brooks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Pepperidge Farms Stuffing
2 Cups Cooked Cut up Chicken
1 Can Cream of Chicken
1 Cup Milk
1/4 C. Sliced Almonds
1 T Margarine
1/4 Cup Chicken Broth

Directions:
Place 2/3 Cup stuffing in greased baking dish.  Add layer of chicken and almonds.  Blend Milk and soup.  Pour over mixture.  Add 1/4 Cup of chicken broth, and 1 T margarine to remaining stuffing.  Sprinkle on top.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
