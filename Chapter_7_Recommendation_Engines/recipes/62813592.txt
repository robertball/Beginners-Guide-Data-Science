Title:
Alamo City Chicken Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 onion, chopped
2 tsp diced garlic
2 cups chopped, pre-cooked frozen pre-seasoned chicken fajita thighs
2 cups chicken stock
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed 98%FF cream of chicken soup
1 Tbs. chili powder
1 Tbs. comino (cumin)
2 Tbs. fresh diced cilantro or 1 tsp dried
1 tsp onion powder
1-2 tsp garlic pepper (to taste)
2 tsp Spanish paprika
1.5 tsp garlic powder
Shredded Pepper Jack cheese
2-3 slices avacado
1 Tbs sour cream (optional)
Nacho style corn chips

Directions:
In a saucepan over medium heat, heat oil.  Cook green bell pepper, onion, and garlic until onion is translucent and tender, stirring often.
Add chopped chicken, stock, and tomatoes.  Bring to a simmer.
Meanwhile, in a separate bowl cream the soup and remaining spices thoroughly and whisk into simmering pot.  Stir and heat through.
Top with shredded Pepper Jack cheese and dollop of sour cream.  Lay avacado slices on the inside of one side of the bowl and serve with nacho chips.

Description: 
This tastes like it's right off the San Antonio Riverwalk.  It's really quick using pre-cooked frozen chicken fajitas.  Want to be super-indulgent?  Top with a dollop of sour cream!Modified from: Chili Chicken Stew
