Title:
Gourmet Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/273268.jpg
Ingredients:
2 avocados, chopped
2 tablespoons salsa
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
1/4 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
salt to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together avocados, salsa, mayonnaise, chili powder and black pepper. Mix well and salt to taste before serving.

Submitter: 
April
Description: 
This is a delicious and easy guacamole dip with salsa, mayonnaise and spices!
