Title:
Jiffy Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cooking oil 1 tbsp
Chopped celery 1 cup
Chopped onion 1 cup
Chopped green pepper 1 cup
spicy sausage cut in 1/2 inch slices 140g
can of diced tomatoes 14 Oz
Chicken broth 1 1/2 cup
long grain white rice 1 1/4 cups
cajun seasoning 1 tbsp
cayenne pepper 1/2 tsp
pre-cooked chicken ,2 1/2 -3 lbs

Directions:
heat oil, on medium high. add celery onion pepper and sausage cook until sausage is cooked
Add diced tomatoes, chicken broth, rice, cajun spice, and cayenne. bring to aboil then reduce heat to medium low. simmer covered for about 20 minutes. until rice is tender and absorbed all liquid. let stand 5 minutes then fluff with fork.
Add chicken in small pieces to mixture. fluff together and serve...
