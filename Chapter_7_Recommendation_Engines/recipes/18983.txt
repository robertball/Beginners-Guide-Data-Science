Title:
Apple Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/58053.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (21 ounce) cans apple pie filling
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
1/2 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Pour apple pie filling into a 9x13-inch pan.  Sprinkle cake mix over apples.  Drizzle melted butter on top, stirring slightly to evenly moisten dry cake batter.  Bake 30 minutes.  Serve hot or cold.

Submitter: 
Paula  Anderson
Description: 
For a quick-to-make excellent treat, try this yummy apple dessert that melts in your mouth.
