Title:
Refreshing Tangy Cucumber Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2414679.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cucumbers, peeled and cut into small pieces
1 cup water, or as needed
1 green chile pepper
6 fresh mint leaves
salt to taste
2 teaspoons lime juice

Directions:
Blend cucumbers, water, green chile pepper, mint, and salt together in a blender until juice is smooth. Pour juice into glasses and add 1 teaspoon lime juice to each glass.

Submitter: 
vasma2000
Description: 
This summertime beauty and health drink is one of my favorite drinks in summer. Drink chilled to get refreshingly cool in the summer. It prevents dehydration in the body.
