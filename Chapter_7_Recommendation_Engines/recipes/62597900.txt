Title:
Crockpot Chicken and Dumplins' Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/557781.jpg
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed cream of chicken soup
1 onion, finely diced
2 carrots, sliced
1 celery stalk, sliced
1 tsp dried parsley
1/2 tsp garlic powder
Black pepper to taste
2 cups chicken broth
1 (10 ounce) package refrigerated biscuit dough, formed into coin sized balls

Directions:
Place the chicken, vegetables, soup, seasoning and broth in slow cooker.
Stir.
Cover, and cook on high for 6 hours.
After 5 hours shred chicken and top with biscuit dough.
Cover and cook for remaining hour.
