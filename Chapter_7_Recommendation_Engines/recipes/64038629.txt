Title:
Perfect Fudge Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
2 cups sugar
4 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
3/4 cup cocoa
1 1/2 cup plus 2 TBSP flour
3/4 cup pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees, oil 9x13 cake pan.
Cream butter and sugar,  add eggs and vanilla. Add rest of ingredients. Bake.
