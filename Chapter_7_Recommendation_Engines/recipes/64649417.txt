Title:
Magic Oreo™ Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (16 oz) Pillsbury™ Ready to Bake!™ refrigerated chocolate chip cookies
2 cups coarsely chopped Oreos™ (about 16 cookies)
1 cup semisweet chocolate chunks
1/2 cup milk chocolate chips
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk (not evaporated)
1/2 cup white vanilla baking chips

Directions:
1. Heat oven to 350°F. Line 13x9-inch pan with foil, extending foil over edges; spray with cooking spray. Place cookie rounds in pan; press dough evenly in bottom of the pan. Bake 15 minutes.
2.   Top dough evenly with 1 cup of the Oreos™, the chocolate chunks, milk chocolate chips and pecans. Pour sweetened condensed milk evenly over bar base. Sprinkle evenly with remaining 1 cup Oreos™ and vanilla baking chips.
3. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until center is set and edges are golden brown. Cool completely, about 2 hours. Use foil to lift from pan; pull foil edges away. For bars, cut into 6 rows by 4 rows.
