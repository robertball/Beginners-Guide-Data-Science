Title:
Zucchini Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Green Zucchinis Peeled
1/2 Pint of cherry tomatoes quartered
1 cup of medium pitted black olives
1 6oz jar of quartered artichoke hearts
2 Tablespoons of white wine vinegar
1 lemon
Feta Cheese

Directions:
Peel off the green on the Zucchinis and then into 2 bowls completely peel the zucchini into thick strands. Quarter the tomatoes and olives and separate between the two salad bowls. Strain and cut the artichoke hearts into even smaller pieces and split between the two bowls. Each bowl gets 1 tablespoon of white wine vinegar and half a squeezed lemon. Sprinkle feta cheese on top snd you're done!
