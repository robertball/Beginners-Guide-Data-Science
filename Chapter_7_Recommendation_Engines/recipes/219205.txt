Title:
Mom's Carrot and Raisin Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/848008.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package carrots, peeled and shredded
3 tablespoons orange juice
1/3 cup raisins

Directions:
Stir the shredded carrots, orange juice, and raisins together in a bowl.

Submitter: 
Julie Nebeker
Description: 
Colorful, tasty, and easy cold salad that has only 3 ingredients. This is one of my mom's recipes. A great salad to bring to a picnic or to make for Halloween.
