Title:
Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs yellow squash, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
3 Tbsp melted butter
2 eggs, beaten
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 tsp garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
Paprika

Directions:
Cook squash in small amount of water until tender, drain. Combine in bowl with butter, garlic, salt, pepper, eggs and onion. Stir in half the cheese. Place in buttered 8x8 baking dish. Bake at 350 for 30 miutes or until set.
