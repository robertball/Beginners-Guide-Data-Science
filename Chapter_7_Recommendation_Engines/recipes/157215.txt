Title:
Pomosa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/824966.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup brut Champagne
2 tablespoons bottled pomegranate juice
1 teaspoon pomegranate seeds (optional)

Directions:
Pour champagne into a flute, filling about 3/4 full. Top with pomegranate juice. Garnish with a few pomegranate seeds.

Submitter: 
Barb the Bee
Description: 
A twist on the traditional, pomegranate juice stars in this version of the classic mimosa drink.
