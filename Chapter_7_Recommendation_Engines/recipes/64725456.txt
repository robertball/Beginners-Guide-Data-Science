Title:
Tomato Bruschetta (Meghan's recipe) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 containers grape/cherry tomatoes
3+ garlic cloves  crushed or minced
3/4 C EVOO
2 pkgs. basil
8 oz. feta cheese
Baguettes to serve

Directions:
Slice tomatoes in half
chiffonade basil  (Little ribbons)
add tomatoes, garlic, oil and basil tp small casserole
Bake 350 for 45 mins.  Periodically smash tomatoes with masher
remove from oven  S & P to taste
add crumbled feta
serve with baguettes
Leftover good with pasta or chicken
