Title:
Cocoa Coffee Crumbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tbsp. melted butter
3/4 cup almond flour or almond meal
1/3 cup flour
1/3 cup sugar
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 cup ground coffee
1 tsp. kosher salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.
Combine butter, almond flour, flour, sugar, cocoa powder, ground coffee and salt in a medium bowl until well combined.
Spread crumbs on a baking sheet and bake, stirring occasionally, until fragrant and dried out - about 15-20 minutes.
Let cool.
