Title:
Jennifer's Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless chicken breasts, cooked and cubed
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 c. sour cream
1 c. Ritz crackers (about 25) crushed.
2 T. butter, melted
1 tsp. celery seed

Directions:
Combine first 3 ingredients and pour into a greased 9x13 casserole.
Combine crumbs, butter, and celery seed and sprinkle over the top.
Bake at 350, uncovered, for 30 - 35 min.
Serve over rice

Karen Martin
