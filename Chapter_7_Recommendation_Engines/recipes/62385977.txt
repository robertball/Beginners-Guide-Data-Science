Title:
Cream of Chicken Soup Substitute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can condensed chicken noodle soup

Directions:
Place soup (undiluted) in a blender and puree. Use in place of 1 can condensed cream of chicken soup.

Submitter: 
4givnCyn
Description: 
Desperate to replace the cream of chicken I didn't have for a recipe,  I spied the chicken noodle and thought it was worth a try.  It worked!
