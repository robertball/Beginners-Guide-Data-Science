Title:
Fiesta Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/844427.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. chicken breasts, cut into small pieces
1 large green pepper, chopped
1 large red pepper, chopped
1 Tbsp. chili powder
3/4 cup salsa, divided
1/4 cup cream cheese
3/4 cup grated cheddar cheese
8-10 small flour tortillas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F.
Heat a large skillet sprayed with cooking spray on med heat. Add chicken, peppers, and chili powder; cook and stir 8 minutes or until chicken is done. Stir in 1/4 cup salsa and cream cheese spread; cook and stir 3-5 minutes or until cream cheese is melted and mixture is well blended. Stir in 1/4 cup shredded cheese.
Spoon heaping 1/3 cup chicken mixture down center of each tortilla; roll up. Place, seam sides down, in 13 x 9 inch baking dish sprayed with cooking spray; top with remaining salsa and shredded cheese. Cover.
Bake 20 minutes or until heated through.
