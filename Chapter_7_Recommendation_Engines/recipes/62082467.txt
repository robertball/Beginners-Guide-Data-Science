Title:
Caesar Dip with Parmesan &  Anchovies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 ounce grated Parmesan cheese, (1/2 cup)
1 tablespoon lemon juice from 1 lemon
1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley leaves
2 medium cloves garlic , pressed through garlic press or minced (about 2 teaspoons)
2 anchovy fillets, minced to paste (about 1 teaspoon)
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in medium bowl until smooth and creamy. Transfer dip to serving bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate until flavors are blended, at least 1 hour; serve cold with crudit√©s or crackers. (Can be refrigerated in airtight container for up to 2 days.)

A manly dip...one of my husband's favorite for Superbowl parties.
