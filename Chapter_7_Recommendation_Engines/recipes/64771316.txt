Title:
Basic Cheese/Beer Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. milk
1 c. beer
1 T. Worcestershire sauce
2 t. ground mustard
1 clove garlic
3 T. flour
6 C. shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In medium saucepan over low heat, mix together milk, Worce sauce, mustard, garlic and flour.  Heat until almost boiling.  Gradually stir in cheddar cheese.  Continue heating until all cheese melts.  Keep warm in fondue dish
