Title:
Slow cooked Greens and Turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3684367.jpg
Ingredients:
8oz Bagged Kale Greens (1/2 a bag)
1 box frozen spinach, thawed
1 tbs. salt
1 tsp pepper
1 smoked turkey drumstick
1 small onion, chopped
4 cups of water

Directions:
1.  Add kale, spinach,  onion, salt, pepper, and smoked turkey to crock pot.
2. Turn on High.
3. Add water.
4. Cover and cook for 6 hours.
5. Add additional salt to taste.

Submitter: 
Eva Maria
Description: 
This is my take on Greens and Ham Hocks. It's a lighter version that won't leave you feeling heavy. Perfect if you want some good home cooking that's light for summer time dining.
