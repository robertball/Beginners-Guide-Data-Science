Title:
Axle's Easy Barbeque Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup ketchup
1/2 Cup lemon juice
1/4 Cup water
1/4 Cup brown sugar
3 Tbsp olive oil
2 Tbsp paprika
1 Tbsp chili powder
4 Tbsp garlic powder
1 Tsp cayenne
2 1/2 Tbsp honey
2 Tsp salt
1 Tsp dry mustard
1/2 Tsp pepper
1 Tsp onion powder
1 Tsp worcestershire sauce
3 drops hot sauce

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a large saucepan over medium heat.  Simmer for 45 minutes to 1 hour.  Remove from heat before serving.

Submitter: 
AxleHellfire
Description: 
I created this recipe in college as a way to cook a great BBQ sauce from the spices in my kitchen without having any fresh ingredients available.  Substituting fresh alternatives can only improve the recipe!
