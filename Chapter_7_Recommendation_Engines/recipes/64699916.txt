Title:
Hula's Pumpkin Ice Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
= For Pie Crust and Topping =
1 1/4 cups Flour
1/4 cup Sugar
1/4 tsp Salt
2/3 cup Butter
1/2 cup Finely Chopped Nuts
= For Ice Cream Filling =
1 cup canned Pumpkin
1/2 cup packed Brown Sugar
1 tsp Cinnamon
1/4 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp Nutmeg
1/4 tsp Cloves
1 quart soft Vanilla Ice Cream

Directions:
Sift flour, sugar and salt.
Cut in butter to form crumb mixture.  Stir in nuts.
Place 1/2 cup crumb mixture on a tray to toast in oven.
Press rest of mixture into bottom and sides of a 9" pie pan.
Bake at 375 until delicately browned. Crumb - 10 mins.  Base - 15-20 mins.
Cool!
Combine pumpkin, sugar and spices in pan and bring to a boil.  Cook, stirring constantly for 1 minute.
Cool!
Beat cooled pumpkin mix into softened Vanilla ice-cream.
Pour into baked crust.
Top with crumb mixture.
Freeze.

A chilly alternative to a warm holiday favorite.
