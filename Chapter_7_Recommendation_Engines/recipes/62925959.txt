Title:
Chicken & Gnocchi Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/714780.jpg
Ingredients:
2-3 chicken breasts, cooked and diced
1 Tbsp olive oil
1/2 clove garlic, chopped
1 stalk celery, diced
1/2 carrot, shredded
1/2 small onion, diced
1 Tbsp cornstarch
4 cups chicken broth or stock
1 cups half-and-half
1 cup milk
1/2 tsp. thyme
salt & pepper to taste
8 oz. potato gnocchi
1 cup fresh spinach, chopped (or whole baby spinach)

Directions:
1. Saute the celery, onion, garlic, and carrot in olive oil for about 3 minutes or until onion is translucent.  Add constarch (and if necessary a little more olive oil) and stir.
2. Add chicken broth, half-and-half, milk, thyme, salt, and pepper.  Heat to boiling, then add gnocchi. Boil gently for 4 minutes, then turn down and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
3. If your soup is not thick enough, mix 1 Tbsp each of water and cornstarch, add to soup, and simmer for a couple more minutes.
4. Add spinach and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
5. Serve!
