Title:
Chicken with Pineapple Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2	tablespoons brown sugar
1	tablespoon cornstarch
2	cans (8 ounces each) crushed pineapple
¼	cup soy sauce
¼	teaspoon minced or crushed garlic
¼	teaspoon ground ginger
6	boneless skinless chicken breast halves (4 ounces each)
Minced chives, optional

Directions:
1.	Ina  large saucepan, combine the brown sugar and cornstarch.  Stir in pineapple, soy sauce, garlic, and ginger.  Cook and stir over low heat until thickened.
2.	Place chicken in a greased 9 inch square baking dish.  Pour half of the sauce over chicken.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.  Bake 10 minutes longer or until chicken juices run clear, basting several times with the remaining sauce.  Sprinkle with chives if desired.
