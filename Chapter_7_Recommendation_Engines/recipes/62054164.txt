Title:
Perfect Pork Tenderloin Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Two 3/4 pound pork tenderloins
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup oyster sauce (Asian section of store)
3 tablespoons packed brown sugar
1 tablespoon fresh minced gingerroot
2 tablespoons minced garlic
1 tablespoon ketchup
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon cayenne
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
In a large, strong ziplock bag, combine all ingredients ... except for pork. Close top of bag and squish around until all ingredients combine. Place pork in bag with marinade. Chill pork, marinate for at least 8 hours.
Prepare grill. Grill pork, basting with reserve marinade and turning every 3 minutes, 10 minutes total. Discard marinade. Continue to cook pork, turning every 3 minutes until thermometer inserted 2 inches registers 155 degrees. (approx. 10 minutes more) Let pork stand 5 minutes before slicing.
