Title:
The Hipsters Spam Musubi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups un-cooked Calrose rice
3 cups water
6 sheets of sushi nori (dried seaweed)
1 can of spam
4 eggs
2 table spoons of oyster sauce
1/4 cup cooking oil
salt to taste

Directions:
Either in a small pot or rice cooker pot, place rice and water
Rinse rice thoroughly until water is clear and not cloudy
If using stove top bring water to a boil
Lower heat and cover till water is gone
Set aside and allow to cool
Slice spam in 1/8" thick slices
drizzle oil in frying pan and cook till slightly brown
beat eggs in small bowl
add oyster sauce and mix some more
Do not scramble eggs just gently fold while cooking
Take out Musubi Maker and moisten insides slightly
Pack insides of musubi maker with cooked rice to form rectangle
Cut strips of nori approx. 3" wide the length of nori sheet
Cut eggs into size of musubi maker
Place rectangular rice over 3" wide nori
Place egg ontop of rice
Place spam on top of egg
Wrap nori around itself and moisten end with water to adhere to itself

Submitter: 
Cooking from the Hip
Description: 
Growing up in Hawaii, I had the chance to experience lots of different cultures, one of them being the asian culture. There are MANY variety's of the Musubi and this is just ONE of mine and how I grew up learning to make them.
