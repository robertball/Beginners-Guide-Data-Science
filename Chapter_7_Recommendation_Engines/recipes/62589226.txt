Title:
Easy Pork Loin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pork loin
2T steak seasoning
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup olive oil

Directions:
Place all in plastic bag and marinade 2 hours or overnight.
Bake at 350 for an hour.
Allow to sit 10-15 minutes before slicing.
