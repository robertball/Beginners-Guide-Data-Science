Title:
Two Rock Rueben Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/618452.jpg
Ingredients:
2 oz. smoked turkey breast
2 slices swiss cheese
1/4 c.saurkraut
1 tbs. lowfat thousand island dressing
2 slices pumpernickel/ rye bread
1 tbs butter melted

Directions:
Brush one side of each slice of bread with butter. on the unbuttered side layer turkey,cheese, kraut,and dressing, top with 2nd slice of bread, butter side up. place on hot griddle until golden brown on each side.  I make mine in my NuWave. hi power for 7 min (no need to turn)

Submitter: 
Terry Goff Tripp
Description: 
I love a good rueben, and crave them often.  one day I just had to have one.  This is my Pantry version of a classic favorite.
