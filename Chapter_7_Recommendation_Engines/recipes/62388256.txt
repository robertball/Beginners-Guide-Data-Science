Title:
Hillyhils Mushroom Sherry Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 thin pork chops
1 T butter
1 can cream of mushroom soup
4 cloves of garlic, minced
1/2 onion, minced
2 T cream sherry

Directions:
Melt butter over low heat in skillet. Add pork chops and sear until lightly brown on all sides. Remove chops and keep warm. Add garlic and onion and saute in the pan drippings until tender, about 6 minutes. Add sherry and scrape up the pan bits. Reduce to a syrup. Put chops back in the skillet and spread the mushroom soup evenly over the top. Cover and cook over low for 35 to 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
hils
Description: 
Simple variation of pork chops in cream of mushroom soup, but with a twist of sherry pan sauce.
