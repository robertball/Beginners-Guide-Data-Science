Title:
Rubbed Prime Rib with Chili & Mustard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lb.prime rib roast
1/4 cup flour
2 1/2 Tbsp.dry mustard
1 tsp.thyme
1 tsp. sea salt
1 tsp.chili powder
1 tsp.fresh ground pepper
1/2 tsp.granulated garlic
1/2 tsp.onion powder
2 Tbsp.worcestershire sauce
2 onions, chopped
4 garlic cloves
2 carrots,chopped
2 celery sticks,chopped
2 fresh bay leaves
1 Tbsp.soy sauce
1 cup red wine
1/2 cup beef broth

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
In mixing bowl,combine flour,mustard,thyme,salt,chili powder,pepper,garlic powder and onion powder,mix well.
Rub mixture evenly over roast.
Place roast into roasting pan and pour worcestershire sauce over roast.
Add vegetables and bay leaves to pan and top with remaining liquids.
Roast in oven untill desired doneness,about 2 1/2 hours for rare.
