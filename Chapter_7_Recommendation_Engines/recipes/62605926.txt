Title:
Mexican Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cooking spray
12 oz cooked chicken, cut into 1-in cubes (about 3 cups)
1 cup sour cream (low-fat)
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese (regular or low-fat), divided
1 cup shredded Cheddar (regular or low-fat), divided
1/2 cup milkd, medium, or hot salsa
1 (4-oz) can chopped green chiles
2 Tbsp chopped fresh cilantro leaves
1 tsp ground chili powder
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper, or to taste
8 (6-inch) corn tortillas, cut into 6 wedges

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Coat an 11 by 7-inch baking pan with cooking spray.
In a large bowl, combine chicken, sour cream, 3/4 cup of the Monterey Jack cheese, 3/4 cup of Cheddar, salsa, chiles, cilantro, chili powder, and cayenne pepper.
Mix well and set aside.
Arrange half of the tortilla pieces in the bottom of prepared pan, overlapping pieces slightly to cover the surface. Top with half of the chicken mixture and smooth over with the back of a spoon to even the top.
Layer remaining tortillas over top, and spoon over remaining chicken mixture.
Top with remaining 1/4 cup each of Monterey Hack and cheddar cheese.
Cover with foil and bake 30 minutes. Uncover and bake 30 minutes more, until top is golden and bubbly.
Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
To reheat, bake in a preheated 375 degree oven for 15 minutes.
