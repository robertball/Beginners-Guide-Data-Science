Title:
Hearty Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/373396.jpg
Ingredients:
1 6oz. can of tomato paste
1 8 oz. can of tomato sauce
1/2 Tablespoon of dried oregano
1/2 Tablespoon of olive oil
1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon of fresh cracked pepper

Directions:
Mix tomato paste and tomatos sauce together well. Continue to mix in dried oregano, olive oil, gsrlic powder, and fresh cracked pepper. Let the mixture sit for 20 minutes so the flavors can mix together and it will be ready to use or store in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
House of Aqua
Description: 
This is a hearty pizza sauce I make for homemade pizzas or english muffin pizzas.
