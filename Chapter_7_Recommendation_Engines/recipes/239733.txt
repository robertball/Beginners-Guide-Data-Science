Title:
Slow Cooker Cranberry Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (750 milliliter) bottle spiced apple wine
2 cups cranberry juice
1/2 cup white sugar
12 whole cloves, or as desired
1 orange
3 cinnamon sticks
1 apple, sliced

Directions:
Combine apple wine, cranberry juice, and sugar in a slow cooker.
Press whole cloves into the orange. Float the orange, cinnamon sticks, and apple slices in the wine mixture.
Heat on Low until wine mixture is heated through, at least 30 minutes.
