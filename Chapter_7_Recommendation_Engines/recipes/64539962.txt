Title:
Pantry Staples Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2/3 cups flour
1 1/3 cups yellow corn meal
1 cup sugar
4 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
2 eggs
1/2 cup margarine, melted
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup honey
1 can of corn, blended with liquid
1 tsp vanilla
2 Tbsp maragrine for the baking dish

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350
2. put 2 Tbsp margarine in baking dish and place in the oven to melt while making the batter.
3. Put all the dry ingredients into a large bowl and whisk to combine well.
4. Add blended corn, eggs, margarine, honey, milk and vanilla. Whisk until all ingredients are fully incorporated.
5. Add completed batter to hot pan and bake for 40-50 minutes or until skewer inserted in the middle comes out clean.
Serve with whipped honey margarine. (1 part honey, 2 parts margarine) or with any toppings you prefer.

Submitter: 
Danielle Lockley
Description: 
A truly easy and delicious sweet cornbread recipe made from things most people keep on hand all the time.
