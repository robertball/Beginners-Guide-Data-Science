Title:
Lemon Bars - Gooseberry Patch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c allpurpose flour, divided
1/2 c powdered sugar
1/4 t salt
3/4 c butter, softened
4 eggs
1 1 /2 c sugar
1/2 c lemon juice
Opt: zest of one lemon
Garnish:  powdered sugar

Directions:
For crust:  Combine 1 1/2 c flour, powdered sugar and salt in a medium bowl.  Cut in butter with a fork until mixtures resembles coarse crumbs.  Press into a greased 9 x 9 baking pan in an even layer.  Bake at 350 degrees for 15-18 minutes, until just golden at edges.  To make filling:  Whisk together eggs and sugar in a medium bowl until smooth.  Stir in lemon juice, optional zest, then remaining flour.  Pour filling over baked crust.  Reduce heat to 325 degrees and bake 25-30 minutes, or until the filling has just set.  Let cool for 30 minutes.  Cut into 2-inch squares; sprinklw with powdered sugar.  Makes 16.
