Title:
Strawberry Margarita Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1010472.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups hulled strawberries
3 tablespoons lime juice
1 tablespoon honey
1 tablespoon tequila

Directions:
Blend strawberries, lime juice, honey, and tequila in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Angela Sackett  Superhotmama
Description: 
This fresh strawberry sauce is delicious served cold over desserts or even savory dishes like grilled fish or chicken. If desired, refrigerate overnight.
