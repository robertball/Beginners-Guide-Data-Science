Title:
Egg Free, Milk Free, Soy Free and Nut Free - Orange Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 cup white sugar
3 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup orange juice
1/3 cup vegetable oil
2 Tablespoons cornstarch
1 whole orange zest
Juices from the zested orange

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees F.
Put all ingredients in a mixing bowl and mix until well blended
Line muffin pan with baking cups
Fill cups 2/3 full
Bake for 20 minutes or until toothpick inserted into the center come out clean
Let cool then enjoy!

Submitter: 
ANDYTAMI
Description: 
My friend's son is allergic to Eggs, Milk, Soy and Nuts. Imagine birthday parties and school parties and you can't eat the cake, ice cream or cookies. This is an easy and delicious muffin that everyone will love and it is Egg, Milk, Soy and Nut Free.
