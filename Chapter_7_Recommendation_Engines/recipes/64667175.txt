Title:
Dee's Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1.5 lbs boneless skinless chicken breast, cooked (or use 1 cut up rotisserie chicken)
1 can Cream of Chicken
1 C. Sour Cream
4-6 oz. Green Chiles
1 sweet onion, chopped
Salt
Pepper
2 Cups Monterrey Cheddar shredded cheese
8-10 flour tortillas

Directions:
Cook chicken and onions together and shred
In a large bowl, mix cooked chicken, onions, green chilies, sour cream, cr of chicken soup, and 1 cup of shredded cheese together
Spray a glass 11 x 13 dish with cooking spray
Spread 1/2 cup mixture on the bottom of the pan
Fill 8-10 tortillas with mixture, roll up, and place vertical in baking dish
Save approx 1/2 mixture to spread over the top of the tortillas
Top with remaining 1 cup of cheese
Bake for 40-45 mins at 400.
