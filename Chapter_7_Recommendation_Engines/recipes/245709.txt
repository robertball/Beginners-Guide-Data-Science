Title:
Acai Pomtini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce citron vodka
1 fluid ounce orange-flavored liqueur (such as Cointreau®)
1 fluid ounce raspberry-flavored liqueur (such as Chambord®)
1 fluid ounce acai-pomegranate juice
1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lime juice
4 ice cubes
3 seedless red grapes, halved

Directions:
Pour vodka, orange-flavored liqueur, raspberry-flavored liqueur, acai-pomegranate juice, and lime juice over ice cubes in a cocktail shaker; cover and shake until well-chilled. Strain into a martini glass and garnish with 3 grapes.

Submitter: 
MSDOLY
Description: 
This is a delicious twist to your traditional cosmo. It is sure to be a hit at your next dinner party. Three grapes on a swizzle stick makes a nice garnish.
