Title:
White Chicken Chilli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp.  canola oil.
1 medium onion diced.
4 cloves garlic, minced.
3 cups, low-sodium chicken broth.
1ea. 4oz can diced green chiles.
2ea.  15 oz. cans cannelli beans, drained and rinsed.
1 ea.  15oz. can navy beans, drained and rinsed.
3 cups cooked, diced rotisserie chicken meat.
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper.
2 tsp. cumin.
1 tsp. oregano.
sea salt and pepper to taste.
1 cup shredded monterey jack cheese. (optional)
4ea. green onions, sliced. (optional)

Directions:
Heat oil over medium heat in a dutch oven or heavy sauce pan.  Add onion and garlic.  Cook 2-3 minutes,  Process one cup of cannellini beans in  a blender or food processor until smooth.  Add to onion and garlic mixture.  Stir in remaining ingredients then turn heat down to medium -low.  Simmer 20-30 minutes.
Garnish with shredded cheese and green onions and serve with corn bread.
