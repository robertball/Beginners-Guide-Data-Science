Title:
Oatmeal Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 T butter, softened
7 T shortening
3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 t vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour
1 t baking soda
1 t cinnamon
1/2 t salt (optional)
3 cups oats, uncooked
1 cup raisins

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
In large bowl, beat butter, shortening and sugars on medium speed until creamy.
Add eggs and vanilla; beat well.
Add combined flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt; mix well.
Add oats and raisins; mix well.
Drop cookie dough by rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
Bake 8-10 minutes or until light golden.  Cool 1 minute on cookie sheet; remove to cooling rack to cool completely.
Store in tightly covered container

Submitter: 
Karen Semmelrock
Description: 
Wonderful oatmeal cookies!  Always soft and chewy!  They are also great with craisins and chopped walnuts!
