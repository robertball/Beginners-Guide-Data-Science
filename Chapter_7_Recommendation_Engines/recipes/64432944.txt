Title:
Coconut and Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2344294.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup coconut milk
2 scoops vanilla protein
1 ripe bananas
2 teaspoons honey (optional)

Directions:
Blend coconut milk, vanilla protein, bananas, and honey in a blender or food processor until smooth.

Submitter: 
Joseph Alvarado
Description: 
Bananas and coconut milk make this vanilla ice cream smoothie extra yummy.Modified from: Coconut and Banana Smoothie
