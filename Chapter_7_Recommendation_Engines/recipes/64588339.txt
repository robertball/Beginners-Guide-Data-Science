Title:
Easy King Ranch Chicken Caserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3740494.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cooked rotisserie chicken, deboned and torn up
1 can Rotel
1 can chicken broth
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
12 corn tortillas cut in squares
2 cups mexican blend cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven at 350
Mix all ingredients except cheese in a large bowl
Put in 13 x 9 pan and sprinkle cheese on top
Bake for 20 minutes
