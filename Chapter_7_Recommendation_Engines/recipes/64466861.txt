Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2444405.jpg
Ingredients:
A large bag of marshmallows
A box of ricecrispies
A bag of chocolate chips (milk or white) or jelly beans
Kale (optional)
Edible glitter

Directions:
Melt the marshmallow until liquid or mixable.
Mix into the  ricecrispies, ensuring that all are covered all over.
Mix in the chocolate chips of your choice, and kale if you choose to add it along with the edible glitter.
Sculpt into individual bite size balls.
Place on a plate and place in the fridge to set.
Once set, enjoy!
