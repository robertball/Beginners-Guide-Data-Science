Title:
MUSHROOM SAUCE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp.butter
1 lb.sliced fresh mushrooms
2 cloves garlic,minced
1/4 cup sherry
3/4 tsp.salt
1/4 tsp.pepper
1 cup heavy cream
1 Tbsp.chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
In large frying pan over high heat,melt butter.Add mushrooms and cook untill liquid has evaporated and they start to brown,about 12 min.
Stir in garlic and cook till mushrooms are completely browned,about 3 min.Stir in sherry,salt and pepper.Cook untill sherry is almost gone,about 1 min.
Add cream and cook untill reduced and slightly thickened,about 5-7 min.Stir in parsley and serve.

Submitter: 
koko
Description: 
This creamy sauce is full of flavor,with sherry and lots of mushrooms.
