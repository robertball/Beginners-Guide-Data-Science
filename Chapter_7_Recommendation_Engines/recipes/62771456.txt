Title:
SEASONED GREEN BEANS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Fresh Green Beans (cut into pieces)
1 Â½ cup water
1 tbls. Vegetable base
1 tsp. Minced Garlic
2 -3 strips Chopped Bacon (cooked till crispy, reserve fat)
1/8 tsp. White Pepper
1/8 tsp. Sea Salt

Directions:
Put beans in small sauce pot.
Mix water and base together, add to beans
Add garlic, bacon (w/fat), salt and pepper.
Put over medium high heat till the beans come to a boil.
Turn heat down to low and let cook till done.

Submitter: 
Roger Paul Hass
Description: 
This was my mother's recipe using canned green beaans. I took the recipe and tweeked it using fresh green beans.
