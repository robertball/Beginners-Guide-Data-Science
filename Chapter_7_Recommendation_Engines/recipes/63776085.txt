Title:
Mediterranean Three Cheese Omelette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1046473.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large Eggs
1 tablespoon Shredded sharp cheddar
1 tablespoon Shredded mozzarella
1 tablespoon Ricotta Cheese part skim
1 clove Garlic; minced
2 tablespoons Red onion; diced
3 whole Cherry tomatoes; diced
1 teaspoon Fresh basil
2 teaspoons Fresh parsley
1 teaspoon Onion powder
1 tablespoon Olive oil
Salt & pepper; to taste

Directions:
Heat medium size pan to medium-low heat. Add in olive oil and saut√© the diced red onion and minced garlic for 2 minutes, or until garlic begins to brown. After mixing together three eggs, pour the egg into the pan and distribute evenly.
Sprinkle the cheddar and mozzarella, and dollop small portions of the ricotta all around the egg evenly. While the egg is cooking and the cheeses are melting, add in the tomato, basil, and parsley. Season with onion powder, salt, and pepper.
After egg is cooked through, use a spatula to fold the omelette in half and transfer to a plate.Garnish with more parsley and diced tomato.
