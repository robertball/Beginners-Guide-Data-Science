Title:
Easy Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 brick of Velveta PeperJack Cheese.
1/2 brick of Habanero Cheese.
1/4 cup milk
Any Nacho chips of your choice

Directions:
Dice the Habanero Cheese and the peperjack cheese into 1/4 in cubes
Place together in a microwave safe bowl or dish
Microwave on High for 2 minutes.
Take the melted cheese out of the microwave stir slightly  and add the milk
Place back in the microwave for 1 minute longer and enjoy

Submitter: 
David Pfeffer
Description: 
This is a great feel good snack great with chips.
