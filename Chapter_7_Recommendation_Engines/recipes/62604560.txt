Title:
Octodogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/561883.jpg
Ingredients:
4 hotdogs
1/4 cup oil, for frying
favorite hotdog dips (kethcup, mustard, hotsauce)

Directions:
In pan heat oil until warm.
Cut hotdogs into 3 equal peices.  Stand the 3 peices on there ends, make a cross shaped cut half way into the hotdogs.  Repeat until there all done.
When oils is warm place hotdogs cut side down in oil.  Watch them turning occasionally, not to burn.  Once slightly crisp on the outside, remove and set on paper towel to remove excess oil.  Cool and serve with favorite dips.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
These are fun hotdogs that look like octopuses once fried in oil.  Their one of the kids favorite lunches.
