Title:
Cheesy Meaty Manicotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2894484.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 (24 oz.) container ricotta cheese
8 oz. shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup mayonnaise
12 manicotti (cooked and drained)
1 (16 oz.) jar Pasta sauce
1/2 tsp dried oregano leaves
1/2 cup parmesan cheese, shredded

Directions:
Brown beef and garlic, drain fat.
Mix ricotta, mozzarella, salt and mayonnaise in bowl.
Stir in beef.
Spread 1 cup of pasta sauce in bottom of 9x13" baking dish.
Fill manicotti with beef and cheese mixture and place in baking dish.
Pour remaining pasta sauce over top.
Sprinkle with oregano and parmesan cheese.
Cover with foil.
Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes. Remove foil and bake 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
DebbyJean
Description: 
I have been making this for over 20 years. We love this recipe because it has meat and cheese in the filling. Serve with a nice salad and some Italian bread.
