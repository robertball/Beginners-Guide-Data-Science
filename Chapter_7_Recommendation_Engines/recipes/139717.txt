Title:
Mediterranean Lemonade Spritzers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/687057.jpg
Ingredients:
SYRUP:
3 cups pineapple juice
3 lemons, juiced
1 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/2 cup honey
Ice cubes
6 slices lemon
1 (2 liter) bottle carbonated water

Directions:
To make the pineapple lemon syrup:  In a large saucepan over medium heat, combine pineapple juice, lemon juice, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener and honey. Bring to a boil, and cook for 1 minute. Allow to cool, then refrigerate overnight.
Fill 6 glasses with ice. Place a slice of lemon in each glass. Pour in 2 fluid ounces pineapple lemon syrup. Fill glasses to the top with carbonated water; stir.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
A refreshing alternative to lemonade. A syrup made with SPLENDA® Granulated Sweetener  and fresh fruit juices is mixed with carbonated water and served over ice. Garnish with fresh mint leaves.
