Title:
Butterscotch Banana Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Shortening
1 Cup Butter
1 Cup White Sugar
1 Cup Brown Sugar
4 Eggs
4 Cups Flour
1 tsp Baking Powder
1 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp Maple extract
1 1/2 Cups Evaporated Milk
1/2 Cup Prepared Butterscotch Pudding (1 Jello pudding cup)
2 Cups Mashed Bananas
1 Cup Butterscotch Chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to  375.
Cream shortening and butter until light and fluffy. Add both sugars gradually. Beat in eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
Add maple extract to evaporated milk and pudding in a small bowl.
Alternately add flour and liquid ingredients to the cream mixture. Blend in bananas and butterscotch chips.
Pour into prepared muffin tins and 9 X 13 pan.
Bak in 375 oven until toothpick comes out clean and top is golden brown. (Depending on pan size, anywhere from 15 to 40 mins)

Submitter:

Description: 
Moist and delicious, a great way to use up overripe bananas. Makes a dozen cupcakes AND a 9 X 13 cake.
