Title:
Acorn candy-cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/506949.jpg
Ingredients:
24 Hershey Kisses
24 Mini Nilla Wafer Cookies
24 butterscotch or Peanut Butter baking chips
small amount of chocolate frosting, about 1 tablespoon.

Directions:
Unwrap kisses.
Place a small dollop of frosting on bottom of kiss.
Push kiss onto bottom of cookie.
Put a small dollop of frosting on the bottom of the baking chip and stick on the top of the cookie.
Let sit until set - about 30 minutes.
Enjoy as is or use to decorate cupcakes.
If you are good at piping try adding some colored leaves made of frosting to the top of the Nilla Wafer.
