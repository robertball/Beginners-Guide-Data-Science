Title:
Kik Wat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup canola oil (or any oil of your choosing)
5 medium yellow onion minced
5 medium red onions minced
24 cloves of garlic minced
.6 cups of Berbere
2 1/2 cup crushed tomato
1/2 tbsp cardamom
5 cup split red lentils
10 cups water
Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
In large pot, saute onion and garlic, 1-2 mins.
Add Berbere and vegetable oil, stirring with each addition for 3 more mins
Add crushed tomato and let cook for 15 mins
Add lentils and cook for 5 mins
Add water. Continue to simmer for about 15 minutes at low heat, stirring occasionally until lentil fully cooked.
Add cardamom, salt and pepper in the last few mins of cooking.
