Title:
ALBONDIGAS (MEATBALLS) EN CHIPOTLE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
The broth -
5 tomatoes (or used canned, chopped tomatoes)
1 clove garlic
1 small onion
1 TBS canned adobado sauce from canned chipotles in adobado
3 TBS oil
3 chicken stock. If you can find tomato bouillon, use this for half of the liquid
The meatballs -
½ kilo (one pound) ground beef
½ kilo (one pound) ground pork
1 cup cooked rice
4-5 tomatillos (tomates verde or green tomatoes)
2 eggs
5 leaves chopped cilantro
2 chipotle chiles in adobado sauce
2 cloves garlic
1 small onion
1 TBS chicken or beef instant bouillon

Directions:
The broth:
In a blender or food processor, blend the tomatoes with the garlic, onion and chipotle sauce.
Fry in the oil for about 10 minutes to mix the flavors.
Add the broth and cook for 10 minutes.
The meatballs:
Mix the chopped tomatillos, garlic, onion, cilantro, eggs, instant bouillon and chiles in the blender or food processor.
Put the  meat in a bowl and add the rice and stock granules. Stir in the blended mixture and mix well.
Shape into balls the size of walnuts and cook in the tomato broth for 15-20 minutes. Serve with warm tortillas and a salad.
