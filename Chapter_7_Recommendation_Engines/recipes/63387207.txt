Title:
Thumbprint Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c butter, softened
1 c sugar
1 t vanilla
1/2 t salt
3 1/2 c all purpose flour
jelly

Directions:
Preheat oven 350'.  In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugar and stir in the vanilla.  In a med sized bowl, mix the salt and flour.  Add the flour mixture to the butter mixutre until the dough is crumbly but holds together when pinched.  Take a walnut size piece of dough in one hand.  With the thumb of the other hand, gently knead it 3 or 4 times.  Roll the piece into a ball and place it on an ungreased cookie sheet.  With your knuckle or finger tip, press a well in the center and fill with 1/2 t of jelly.  Bake for 10 to 15 minutes.
