Title:
Coleslaw with a Twist Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ White Cabbage - finely shredded
¼ Red Cabbage - finely shredded
2 Carrots - grated
1 Red Onion - finely sliced
2 Spring Onions - chopped
1 Red Chilli - seeds removed to taste for heat - finely chopped
180g/6oz Good Quality Mayonnaise
1 Lemon - juice only - to taste
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
Optional: 1tbsp Caraway Seeds

Directions:
Mix all the Ingredients together well, ensuring a well - balanced, creamy consistency;
Taste for seasoning and flavour.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
A creamy, spicy, piquant delight to accompany any salad, grilled/BBQ meat, fish, vegetable dish throughout the summer months.
