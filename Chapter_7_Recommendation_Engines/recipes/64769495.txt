Title:
Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box rolled oats
2 cups raisins
2 cups brown sugar
1 1/2 cup water
1 cup oil
2 tsp vanilla
2 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees.
Combine raisins, oats, and brown sugar in a large bowl.
Mix water, oil, vanilla, and salt.
Combine all ingredients.
Bake at 250 degrees for 45 mins, stir then bake another 45 mins.
