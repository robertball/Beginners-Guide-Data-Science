Title:
Slow Cooker Creamy Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp butter
1 tsp dried basil
2 cloves garlic,minced
1 cup heavy cream
1 cup shredded cheese,your choice
1 cup parmesan cheese
1 cup broccoli florets
1 small red pepper chopped
1 zuchinni,sliced
1/2 red onion,chopped
1 cup sliced mushrooms

Directions:
Rub inside of slow cooker pot with butter.Add vegetables,garlic,basil & parmesan cheese & stir well.Add any other veg you like or have on hand.Cook on high for 2 hours,then turn to low,add shredded cheese & cream & stir well & heat through til cheese is melted.Mix with any pasta noodle & sauteed chicken breast(opt.)

Submitter: 
Deb Hughes Cormier
Description: 
This easy & simple pasta dish is great for everyday family dinner yet impressive enough for company.Takes much less time than a typical slow cooker recipe,chop it up & throw it in for 2 hrs.YUMMY!!
