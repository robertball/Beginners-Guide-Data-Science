Title:
Stacy's Oatmeal Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c oatmeal
2 c boiling water
2/3 c warm water
2 tsp sugar
2 pkg. dry yeast
2 tbsp margarine
1/2 c honey
6 c flour
2 tsp salt

Directions:
1.  Mix oatmeal and boiling water, let stand 30 min.
2.  combine warm water, sugar and dry yeast to soften.
3.  Melt margarine and honey.
4.  Combine yeast mixture, oatmeal, and honey.
5.  Add flour one cup at a time until you can't add any more.  Turn onto a floured surface and knead until smooth.
6.  Let rise 2 hours. Knead again.  Divide into two parts and form into two loaf pans.
7.  Let rise 1 hour.
8.  Bake 45 min. at 325 degrees.
