Title:
Tilapia Filets with a Crunchy Curry Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tilapia Filets
1/4 cup coconut milk
3 Tbsp corn meal
2 tsp curry powder
1/4 tsp red pepper or ancho chile pepper
Salt and pepper to taste
Canola oil spray

Directions:
Heat a large skillet or griddle over medium high heat.  Pat the fish filets dry, and place them in a large dish.  Add the coconut milk and turn the fish to coat.  Mix the corn meal and seasonings together, then sprinkle over the fish, turning to coat.  Spray the skillet with the canola oil, and add the fish to the skillet.  Cook for about 5 minutes on both sides.

Submitter: 
toastyfrenchy
Description: 
Tilapia filets coated in coconut milk and a cornmeal crust seasoned with curry powder, then cooked in a skillet.
