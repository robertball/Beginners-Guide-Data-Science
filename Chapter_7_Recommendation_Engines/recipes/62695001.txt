Title:
Maple Whole Wheat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups water
4 tablespoons maple syrup
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/3 teaspoon salt
1 cups whole wheat flour
2 cup bread flour
4 teaspoons wheat gluten
2 tablespoons flax seed
2 teaspoons active dry yeast

Directions:
Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select (no 1) white bread cycle; 1-1/2 lb loaf; dark crust; press Start.  Let cool before slicing although not eating the warm bread with butter is the hardest part of the recipe.

Description: 
Chewy,LIGHT but hearty, whole wheat bread for bread machines. Flax added for extra fiber.Modified from: Maple Whole Wheat Bread
