Title:
Penne Pasta with Chicken and Feta Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/ 16 ounce package penne pasta
1-1/2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons of olive oil
1 cup chopped red onion
6 cloves garlic, minced
1-1/2 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
1/ 14 ounce can artichoke hearts in water
1/ 14 ounce can of diced tomatoes
1 cup or 195 grams of crumbled feta cheese
8 ounces of mushrooms, sliced
6 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
Juice of 1 large lemon
2 tablespoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon red pepper flakes or to taste (optional)
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large pot with boiling salted water cook penne pasta until al dente. Drain.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet over medium-high heat melt butter,add olive oil, add onion and garlic and cook for 2 minutes. Add chopped chicken and continue cooking, stirring occasionally until golden brown or done, about 5 to 6 minutes.
Reduce heat to medium- low. Drain and chop artichoke hearts and add them, tomatos, feta cheese, mushrooms, fresh parsley, lemon juice, dried oregano, red pepper flakes, and drained penne pasta to the large skillet. Cook until heated through, about 2 to 3 minutes.
Season with salt and ground black pepper. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
I've increased the amount of chicken to make it a heartier meal and have subsequently increased the amounts of seasonings accordingly.  I've also added some red pepper flakes to give this recipe a little punch.Modified from: Greek Penne and Chicken
