Title:
Yummy Shrimp Dip-hot from the oven Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/773011.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg.cream cheese
1 can small or broken shrimp
garlic salt to taste
2 Tbsp.green onion
2 tsp.milk
paprika

Directions:
Rinse shrimp and dry well.Cream together shrimp and cream cheese,add milk green onions and garlic salt and pepper.Spoon into well greased dish,Sprinkle with paprika.Bake at 425 for 30-45min.Serve with crackers.
