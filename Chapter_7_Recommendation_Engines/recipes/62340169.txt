Title:
Snickers Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
First layer:
1 c. chocolate chips
1/4 c. peanut butter
1/4 c. butterscotch chips
Second Layer:
1 c. sugar
1/4 c. milk
1/4 c. butter
1/4 c. peanut butter
1 1/2 c. marshmallow creme
1 tsp. vanilla
Third layer:
40 Kraft caramels
4 tbsp. heavy cream
Fourth Layer:
1 c. chocolate chips
1/4 c. peanut butter
1/4 c. butterscotch chips

Directions:
First layer - melt chips and peanut butter together and spread in 9x13 pan lined with wax paper, let cool.
Second layer - combine sugar, milk, and butter, boil for 5 minutes.  Add remaining ingredients and spread over first layer.
Third layer - Melt and spread over second layer.
Fourth layer - melt and spread over caramel layer, cool and cut into squares.
