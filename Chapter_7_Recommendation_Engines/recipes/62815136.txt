Title:
Peanutbutter Fudge Dessert Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 oz unsweetened chocolate
1/8 to 1/4 cup chunky peanut butter
7 oz sweetened condensed milk
1/2 t vanilla

Directions:
Chop chocolate for easier melting
Combine chocolate with peanut butter and condensed milk in saucepan.
Cook over medium-low heat, stirring constantly, until chocolate is melted.
Add vanilla and stir well to incorporate.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Thick, gooey & chunky.  A perfect topping for ice cream, brownies, etc.  For a creamier topping, simply use regular peanut butter.
