Title:
Sunday Clark's Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup oil
1 box Jiffy muffin mix
2 eggs
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup sugar
8 oz. Sourcream

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.
Put oil in pan and coat bottom and sides of pan and set aside.
In medium bowl mix remaining ingredients until just mixed.
Pour in greased baking pan.
Bake till golden brown.
Remove from oven then slice and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Dawn Raegene Mendenhall Scott
Description: 
Moist and full of flavor.  Jiffy Cornbread mix with a unique twist that will have you coming back for more and more.
