Title:
Jim's Chicken Tender Nuggets w/ dipping sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/371813.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup seasoned bread crumbs
3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder
2 eggs beaten to dip
1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch long fingers
Dipping sauce
1 cup mayonnaise
3 tablespoons prepared horseradish
1/2 cup sour cream
1 dash Worcestershire sauce
3 tablespoons prepared mustard

Directions:
In a large resealable plastic bag, combine the bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese, pepper and cayenne pepper. In a shallow bowl, beat the eggs. Dip chicken pieces in eggs, then place in bag and shake to coat. Place in a 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan coated with nonstick cooking spray. Bake, uncovered, at 400 degrees F for 12-15 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink, turning once.
Dipping sauce
mix the mayonnaise,horseradish,sour cream,Worcestershire sauce and mustard in a bowl. Serve chilled for dipping
