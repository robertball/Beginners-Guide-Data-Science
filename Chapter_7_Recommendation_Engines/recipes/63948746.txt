Title:
Cindy's Alfredo Sauce/Cheesy Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1095233.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Tablespoons butter
1 cup heavy cream
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 teaspoon italian seasoning
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
1/2 cup shredded parmesan
1/2 cup shredded mozzerella

Directions:
Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan.
Add the cream and the rest of the ingredients except for the cheeses.
Bring to a simmer and add the 2 types of cheese and let them melt and let it come up to simmer again.
Use as you want!

Submitter: 
Cindy in Pensacola
Description: 
Found something like this on Pinterest, made it my own and it sooo good, easy and almost always have the ingredients on hand.
