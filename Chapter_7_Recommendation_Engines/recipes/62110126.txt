Title:
PJ's Sweet Potato Mash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 sweet potatoes (2 lbs)
1- 1 1/2 Tbsp prepared horseradich
1-2 Tbsp honey
2 Tbsp butter or butter spread
1/2 tsp. salt
cream or milk

Directions:
Cook sweet potatoes by baking in 400 F. oven for 40-50 minutes or boil for 25-35 minutes.
Place the cooked sweet potatoes in a large mixing bowl and add the remaining ingredients.  Whip until mixture comes together then add a few drops of cream or milk if needed for a whipped texture.
Serve hot or keep warm in the oven until ready to eat.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Mashed sweet potato or yams with a kick of horseradish & honey. Excellent with pork or beef or poultry.
