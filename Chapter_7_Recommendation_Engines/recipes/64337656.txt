Title:
Grandma's Pepper Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium head green cabbage, shredded
2 green or red bell peppers, diced
salt to taste
1 cup vinegar
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 tablespoons celery seed

Directions:
Toss the cabbage and bell pepper together in a large bowl. Season the mixture liberally with salt; stir. Allow to sit 45 minutes (opt); drain and return to the bowl.
Bring the vinegar and sugar to a boil together in a saucepan or in a cup in the microwave for about 40 seconds. Immediately remove from heat and stir into the cabbage mixture. Add the celery seed; mix thoroughly. Chill completely before serving.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
This recipe for sweet and sour cabbage slaw couldn't be simpler to make! Try it as a side dish for grilled burgers, hot dogs, roast pork, or beef.Modified from: Grandma's Pepper Slaw
