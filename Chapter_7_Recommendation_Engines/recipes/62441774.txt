Title:
Orange Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 orange with peel, cut up
½ cup orange juice
1 egg
½ cup butter/margarine
1 ¾ cup flour
¾ cup sugar
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda

Directions:
Cut orange into 8 pieces and remove seeds. Put in blender and puree orange and juice. Add egg and butter.
Measure flour, sugar, baking powder and baking soda in separate bowl. Mix thoroughly.
Pour over top of orange mixture and mix.
Bake at 400 for 20 minutes.
