Title:
Protein Parfait Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1/2 cup of Greek Yogurt
1/2 tsp agave sweetener
1-1/2 scoops of low carb protein powder
Fresh Berries of your choice

Directions:
Mix yogurt, sweetener and protein powder thoroughly
Add Berries
Enjoy
