Title:
real buttermilk ranch dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/461598.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups buttermilk
2/3 cup mayonnaise
2/3 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon dried dill
1 teaspoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
whisk all ingredients until combined. cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to let all the flavors mature.

Submitter: 
paralee
Description: 
this is a guilty pleasure dressing; no low fat ingredients here! great on a summer salad, hot wings, pizza, even watermelon!
