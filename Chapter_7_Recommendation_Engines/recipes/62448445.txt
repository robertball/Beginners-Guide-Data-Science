Title:
Peanut Brittle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ½ tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. water
1 tsp. vanilla
1 ½ cups sugar
1 cup water
1 cup light corn syrup
3 Tbsp. margarine/butter
1 lb. (2 cups) shelled unroasted peanuts

Directions:
Butter 2 cookie sheets (15 ½ x 12 inches), keep warm.
Mix baking soda, 1 tsp. water and vanilla; reserve.
Mix sugar, 1 cup water and the corn syrup in 3-quart saucepan. Cook over med. Heat, stirring occasionally, to 240 F on candy thermometer or until soft ball stage.
Stir in margarine and peanuts.
Cook, stirring constantly, to 300F. Immediately remove from heat; stir in baking soda mixture.
Pour ½ mixture onto each cookie sheet and spread quickly about ¼ inch thick.
Cool; break into pieces
