Title:
Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Mega-Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2717983.jpg
Ingredients:
4 eggs
2 cups sugar
1 can pumpkin
1.5 cups oil (I mix coconut & olive)
3 cups flour (I use whole wheat)
2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp baking powder
1.5-2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp salt
2 cups dark chocolate chips
2 cups hemp hearts
1/2 cup of both wheat germ & ground flax seeds
1 packet hemp powder
1/2 cup+ Brewers yeast
1/4 cup Black strap molasses

Directions:
Mix eggs, sugar, pumpkin and oil.
Blend in dry ingredients.
Place in muffin tins, in oven set at 400ยบ for 15-18 min
Enjoy!
