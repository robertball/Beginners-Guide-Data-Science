Title:
Super Moist Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Brown Sugar
2 Eggs
1/2 cup White Sugar                                       1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 Canola Oil                                              1 tsp Cinnamon
1 cup Whole Wheat Pastry flour
1/4 cup chopped Walnuts
1 cup Grated Carrot
8 ounces canned plums, drained(or other canned fruit,)
1 tsp Baking Powder
3/4 tsp Baking Soda

Directions:
Mix sugar, eggs, salt, oil, and cinnamon. Add other ingredients, and mix well. Bake at 350F in a greased 8"x8" or 9"x9" pan until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. About 40 minutes.
