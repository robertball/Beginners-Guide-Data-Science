Title:
Hot Artichoke-Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese
1 can 14 oz. Artichoke Hearts, drained, coarsely chopped
1/2 C. frozen chopped Spinach, thawed and drained very well
1/4 C. real(only) Mayonnaise
1/4 C. Romano Cheese
1/4 C. Parmesan Cheese
1 clove finely minced garlic
1/2 tsp. dried Basil
1/4 C. grated Mozzarella Cheese
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Allow cream cheese to come to room temperature. In mixing bowl, blend
cream cheese with mayonnaise, Parmesan, Romano cheese, garlic, basil, and
Salt and pepper Mix well. Add the artichoke hearts and spinach and mix until well-blended.  Store in refrigerator until ready to bake. Spray baking dish with spray oil, add mixture, and top with Mozzarella cheese. Bake at 350Â°for 25 minutes or until the top is browned. Serve with bread pieces or chips.
