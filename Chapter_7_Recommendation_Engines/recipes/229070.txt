Title:
Honeymoon Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1004044.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups ketchup
1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
1 1/4 cups white sugar

Directions:
Beat ketchup, vegetable oil, and sugar together in the bowl of a stand mixer on low speed until creamy, 30 to 45 seconds.
