Title:
Leeks, Potato and Bacon with Dill Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz bacon, cooked and crumbled
4 leeks, thinly sliced, whites and some of green parts
1# potatoes, scrubbed and cubed
1 qt. chicken broth
2 bay leaves
2 C. milk
dill, fresh or dried, about 1 teaspoon, or to taste
sea salt and white pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook leeks in oil until softened.
Add broth and potatoes; cover and cook over med.-low heat until potatoes are softened, about 30 min.
Remove from heat, cool slightly.
Add milk and dill. Season to taste.
Serve topped with bacon crumbles.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
I thought this was very good.  I used frozen leeks and low fat milk.  Was easy, quickly made and very tasty.
