Title:
Grandmama's Lazy Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sliced peaches, fresh or canned,
2 cups water,
2 cups sugar,
1 cup flour,
1 cup milk,
1 stick butter.

Directions:
Cook on stove the peaches with water, and one cup of sugar until tender, about 20 minutes.  Mix remaining ingredients together well, except butter.  Place butter in a 9x9 baking dish, and put in a 350 degree oven til melted.  Pour peaches into baking dish onto butter.  Pour batter into this.  Bake 40-45 minutes or until crust is golden brown.  Serve alone or with whipped cream, or ice cream.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
Granmama would make this at least once a week, because we all loved it.
