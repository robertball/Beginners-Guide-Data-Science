Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken with Sun Dried Tomatoes, Artichokes and Garlic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 - 6 large chicken breasts on the bone
1/2 - 1 cup sliced sun dried tomatoes (not packed in oil)about two large handfuls
5 cloves of garlic - just smashed once on the cutting board with your hand
1 can artichoke hearts, drain them and cut into fourths
1 can chicken broth
1/2 cup white wine
1/2 cup Newman's Own Light Sun Dried Tomato Dressing
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Place about 1/2 the tomatoes on the bottom of the crock pot. Place chicken breasts skin side down on top. Add remaining tomatoes, artichokes, garlic, salt and pepper. Mix broth and wine with dressing and pour over. Season lightly with salt and pepper.
Cook on slow for 6 - 8 hours.
Taste and adjust seasonings.
Serve with rice.  Yummy.

Submitter: 
DASKDS
Description: 
I had a large bag of sliced sun dried tomatoes and was experimenting with recipes to use them up.  This was a great hit at the last pot luck dinner.
