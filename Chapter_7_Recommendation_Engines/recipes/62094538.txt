Title:
Lemon Asiago Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 medium zucchini
1 tbs. olive oil
1 tbs. lemon juice
1 tbs. asiago cheese
1 tbs. italian-style bread crumbs

Directions:
Wash and slice zucchini into coins. In a veggie steamer, cook zucchini until tender - no more than 10 minutes.
Once zucchini is cooked, place in serving bowl. Spinkle lemon juice, olive oil, bread crumbs and cheese over zucchini and toss. Enjoy.

Submitter: 
mkecupcakequeen
Description: 
A flavorful use for all that summer zucchini that is easy and delicious. I just tossed it together one night with stuff I had in the fridge.
