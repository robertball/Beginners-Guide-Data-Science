Title:
Cold Cucumber and Avacado Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium cucumber,
1 ripe avocado,
1 garlic clove,
2 T. fresh coriander leaves,
2 T. fresh lemon juice,
1/2 - 1 C. chicken stock,
1/4 yogurt,
Salt and Pepper to taste.
Garnish: 2 fresh cilantro sprigs
Optional: Ground red pepper

Directions:
Peel cucumber and cut into 4 pieces. Quarter avocado, removing pit, and peel. In a blender puree all ingredients until smooth and season with salt and pepper.
If you like it spicy, serve soup with a pinch of red chili powder to the top of the soup.  Otherwise just serve soup garnished with cilantro.

Submitter: 
MattyHam
Description: 
This is the perfect simple soup for a hot summer day.  It's light, fresh, and super easy.  It is also very good for an alternative to sour cream on gazpacho soup.
