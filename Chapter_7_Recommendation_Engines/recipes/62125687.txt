Title:
Hearty Pumpkin Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28 oz. can of Pumpkin
1 cup celery chopped
1 cup carrots chopped
1 cup cream
1 med. apple peeled cored and diced
1 med onion chopped
2 tbs olive oil
2 cloves garlic chopped
1 32 oz. box of broth (veg/chicken)
1 tsp fresh ground nutmeg
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp celery salt
1 tsp onion salt
1 tsp marjoram
1 tbs cumin
1 tbs chili powder
1 cup cream

Directions:
Sautee onion celery carrots apple and garlic until soft adding enough broth to keep moist.
Puree ingredients with submersible blender.
Add pumpkin and remaining broth.
Serve in bowls and swirl cream on top.

Submitter: 
ROBYNSHERE
Description: 
A simple and forgiving recipe that can be ready in a jiffy, but allow time to simmer and your home will smell marvelous.
