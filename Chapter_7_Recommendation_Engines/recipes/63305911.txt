Title:
Rockin's Strawberry Shortcake Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16oz angel food cake torn in bite size pieces(Split into two 9x13 pans or one large steamer pan)
2 6oz strawberry jellos
4 cups of boiling water
3 cups cold water
2 24oz containers frozen sliced strawberries in sugar
16oz container cool whip thawed

Directions:
Boil the water in a large pot. Add the 2 jellos to the boiling water and stir to disolve.
Put the frozen strawberries in the jello and stir until the strawberries are broken up and the frozen juice is melted.
Add the additional 3 cups cold water.
Place pan in the refrigerator and leave untill jello begins to thicken but not totally set. (this may take an hour or more)
Pour the thickened jello over the angelfood cake that has been prepared in the pan.(or pans)
Stir gentally to coat all the cake pieces.
Place pan in the refrigerator for another half hour until set.
Frost with the cool whip.
Keep chilled!

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
This is always a huge hit! I can take it to a summer picnic or to a covered dish in the middle of winter and it's a goner. Can be made lite as well!
