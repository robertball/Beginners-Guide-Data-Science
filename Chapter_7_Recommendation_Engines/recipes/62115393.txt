Title:
Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium Yukon potatoes, cubed small
3 carrots, diced small
3 celery stalks, diced small
1 medium onion, chopped
1 T. parsley flakes
5 c. water
4 chicken bouillon cubes
1 (12-oz.) can evaporated milk
2 T. butter

Directions:
Boil gently:  potatoes, carrots, celery, onnions, bouillon cubes and parsley flakes in water until done.  Add evaporated milk and butter.  Simmer a little longer and serve.

Submitter: 
Linda Smith
Description: 
Low calorie, but delicious homemade potato soup.
