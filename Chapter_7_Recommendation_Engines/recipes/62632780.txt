Title:
Spinach Fritatta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/577943.jpg
Ingredients:
12 eggs
2 tbsp olive oil
16 oz sausage
9 oz spinach bag
1 medium onion
1 medium green pepper
1/2 cup milk
1 tbsp sea salt
1 tbsp Italian seasoning

Directions:
-Preheat the oven to 350 F.
-Chop the green pepper and onions and set aside
-In an iron skillet, brown the sausage into crumbles. -Remove the sausage from heat into a dish temporarily
-Cook the onions and pepper til carmelized
-Add the entire bag of spinach and fold until withered
-Beat all the eggs and milk and stir in the sausage
-Pour into skillet and cook on medium for 5 minutes
-Place into oven for 25 minutes
-Broil for 2 minutes to brown the top
-Let sit for 15 minutes to firm.
