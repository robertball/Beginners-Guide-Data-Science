Title:
English Toffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
ENGLISH TOFFEE
1 Cup sugar
1 Cup butter
3 T water
1 - 2 tsp vanilla
8 oz. chocolate bar
1/4 - 3/4 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Heat butter, sugar and water (I also add 1/3 cup slivered almonds) in heavy pot to hard crack stage - can remove from heat at 300 degrees.  Add the vanilla, stir and pour onto cookie sheet and spread.  Top with chocolate pieces.  After choc. is melted, spread with spatula, and then top with nuts.  Cool to set.
