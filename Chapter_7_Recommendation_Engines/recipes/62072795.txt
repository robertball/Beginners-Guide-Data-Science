Title:
Hamburger Cheesy Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb hamburger, browned and drained
1 jar cheese whiz
1 can diced green chilis (optional)
salt and pepper to tasted
Tortilla chips

Directions:
Brown hamburger and drain.  Add cheese whiz and heat til melted, stirring constantly.  Add green chilis, if desired.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Serve warm with chips.

Submitter: 
anmlnrs
Description: 
An easy warm dip for tortilla or regular chips.  Add diced green chilis or jalapeno for an extra bite.
