Title:
Lemon-Garlic Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 teaspoons olive oil
1 pound green beans, trimmed and cut into 2-inch pieces
juice from 1/2 lemon
1/4 teaspoon coarsely ground pepper
1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a large nonstick skillet coated with nonstick cooking spray, cook the garlic in oil over medium heat for 30 seconds.
Add the beans; cook and stir for 10-13 minutes or until crisp-tender.
Stir in the lemon juice, pepper and salt.

Submitter: 
Liora Hess
Description: 
I changed the tablespoon of lemon juice to the juice from half of a lemon to avoid having to measure. It worked out great.Modified from: Lemon-Garlic Green Beans
