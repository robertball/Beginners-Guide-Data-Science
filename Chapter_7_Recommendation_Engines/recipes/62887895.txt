Title:
Hungry Girl's Broccoli Slaw Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag dry broccoli slaw mix with shredded broccoli, carrots, and red cabbage
1/2 cup Newman's Own Lighten Up! Low Fat Sesame Ginger Dressing

Directions:
Add broccoli slaw mix to a bowl.  Add the dressing and stir until the dressing is thoroughly distributed.  Cover and refrigerate for at least 15 minutes.  The longer it sits the better the flavor.
