Title:
Egg Nog Banana Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 in graham crust
5 oz vanilla instant pudding mix
2 cups egg nog
1 envelope dream whip
2 tsp rum
2 bananas
8 oz cool whip
nutmeg

Directions:
Combine dry pudding mix, egg nog, dream whip & rum. Beat 5 min until peaks are formed. Slice bananas & arrange on the bottom of the pie crust. Pour pudding mixture over bananas. Chill several hours before serving. Spread with cool whip & sprinkle with nutmeg
