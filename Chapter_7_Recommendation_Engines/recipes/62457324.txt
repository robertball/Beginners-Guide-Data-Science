Title:
Saffron: Cauliflower soup with Charmoula Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cauliflower soup:
5 cups of cauliflower florets (1-2 heads)
1/2 stick (4T) butter divided
1 medium onion, diced
10 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
1 large potato, peeled and diced
1/4 cup whole milk
Water
Salt
Charmoula:
1 t saffron
1/2 cup lemon juice
1 T coriander seeds
1 T fennel seeds
1 T plus 1 t cumin seeds
1 T plus 1 t caraway seeds
1 T sweet paprika
1 t cayenne
2 garlic cloves
EVOO
salt

Directions:
1. Make the soup: Melt 2 T of butter in a pan, add onion and garlic, sweating over low heat for 3 minutes. Add cauliflower and continue over low heat for 2 minutes, add salt. Add potato and enough water to cover 3/4 of veggies, simmer over moderately low heat for 10 min. Add milk, simmer until potato soft. Puree. Add 2 T of butter, salt.
Charmoula: Toast seeds until fragrant. Grind. Blend garlic, saffron, cayenne and sweet paprika. Combine the two mixtures together in a bowl. Slowly mix in EVOO until sauce-like consistency. Salt. Think texture of chimichurri.
