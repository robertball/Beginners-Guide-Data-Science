Title:
Reuben slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5979994.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 head small green cabbage, cut julienne.
1 lb Pastrami, chopped up.
1/2 lb of Swiss Cheese, cut julienne.
1 Cup Avocado Oil Mayo (I used olive oil mayo)
1/2 Cup Dill Pickles, chopped.
1 Tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
1 tsp Monkfruit Sweetener.
1 dash cayenne pepper
1 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in large mixing bowl.
Chill until ready to serve, good up to 3 days in fridge.

Submitter: 
momscaasi
Description: 
This keto friendly Reuben slaw satisfies both coleslaw cravings and Reuben cravings.
