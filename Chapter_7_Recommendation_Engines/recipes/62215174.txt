Title:
Charlene's Chocolate Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sugar
3 tbs. cocoa
1 cup milk
3 tbs. marshmellows
1 cup nut meats
3 tbs. butter
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Cook sugar, milk, cocoa, until it makes a soft ball in cold water, add butter, nut meats, vanilla & marshmellow.  Beat until it starts to thicken.
Pour in cake pan that has been greased with butter so it doesn't stick.
Cut when cool.

Submitter: 
Exhibits
Description: 
This recipe came from a friend in Florida that I play golf with.  She always makes this at Christmas.  I like it because it is not the soft creamy kind, but rather the harder old fashion fudge.
