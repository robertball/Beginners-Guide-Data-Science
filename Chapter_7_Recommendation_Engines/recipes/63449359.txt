Title:
BBQ Pit Boys Bacon Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb thick-cut bacon
2 sleeves buttery crackers coarsely crushed
1 T. garlic, minced
1/4 c. celery, chopped
1/4 c. green onion, chopped
2 T. parsley, chopped
1 pinch salt
1/8 tsp. ground pepper
1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
1/4 c. chicken broth
juice of one lemon

Directions:
Bake, Fry or grill bacon to desired crispiness.
Coarsely chop bacon.
In a large bowl, mix together chopped bacon, garlic, celery, onion, parsley, salt, pepper and buttery crackers.
Add chicken broth and butter, and stir to incorporate.
Cook as you would any other stuffing.

Submitter: 
Mark Adams
Description: 
Need a special holiday stuffing for Shrimp, Clams or for a side dish? Stuffing out of a box don't cut it? We've got you covered. Check out this easy to do smoked Bacon stuffing recipe by the BBQ Pit Boys.
