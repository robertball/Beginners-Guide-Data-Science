Title:
Navy Beans and NOT ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/581935.jpg
Ingredients:
1	package dried navy beans
1	tablespoon EVOO
1	large white or yellow onion, chopped
2	large cloves garlic, pressed
¼	teaspoon kosher salt
¼	teaspoon pepper
1	32 ounce container Unsalted chicken broth
6	cups water
1	smoked turkey wing

Directions:
In a 4-6 quart Dutch oven or stock pot, or saucepan over medium heat, add  the onion and garlic sauté for 3-5 minutes.  Add chicken broth water, beans, salt pepper and the turkey wing.  Bring to a boil; turn down to a simmer and let simmer for about 2 to 12 ½ hours or until beans are done.  When the meat is falling off the bone, remove from the turkey from the pot; take the meat from the bone, chop and return meat to the pot and finish.
