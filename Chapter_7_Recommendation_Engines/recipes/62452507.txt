Title:
sweet and sour (chicken or pork) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
coat crock pot with oil on low setting
2 lb lean pork (or chicken)cube it to bit size
1/2 c flour
1 tbs ginger
salad oil
1 can pineapple cunks
1 can water chestnuts
bean sprouts (if you like)
1 cut cup carrot (even cut up small carrots to bite size)
small pepper cut into bite size chunks
THEN SAUCE
1 c pineapple juice
1/2 c vinegar
1/2 c soy sauce
2 tsp worcesterchire sauce
1/2 c sugar
(mix them all for sauce, blend well)

Directions:
coat meat with flour/ginger mixture and very lightly brown then place in crock pot.
use the rest of the flour mixture and add the sauce ingredients to it, pour over meat in crockpot and set on low for 6 hours,
on the 6th hour, add the vegetable, and add the drained pineapples, cook for one more hour
serve over rice
