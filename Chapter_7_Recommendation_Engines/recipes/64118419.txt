Title:
Cream Cheese Rhubarb Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1/4 cup cornstarch
* 1 cup sugar
* Pinch salt
* 1/2 cup water
* 3 cups sliced fresh or frozen rhubarb (1/2 inch pieces)
* 1 unbaked pie shell (9 inches)
Topping:
* 1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened
* 2 eggs
* 1/2 cup sugar
* whipped cream and sliced almonds

Directions:
1)In a saucepan combine cornstarch, sugar, and salt. Add water; stir until well mixed. Add rhubarb cook, stirring often, until mixture boils and thickens. Pour into the pie shell; bake at 425 Degrees F (i cook it at 350 Degrees F) for 10 minutes.
2)Meanwhile for topping, beat cream cheese, eggs and sugar until smooth. Pour over pie. Return to oven; reduce heat to 325 Degrees F. Bake for 35 minutes or until set. Cool then chill several hours or overnight. Garnish with whipped cream and sliced almonds.
