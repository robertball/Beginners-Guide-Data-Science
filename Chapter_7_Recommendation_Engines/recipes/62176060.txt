Title:
Baked Donuts using the Donut Pans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 cup white sugar
Optional: 1/2 cup cocoa powder
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup buttermilk (substitution: add 1 Tbl vinegar to reg. milk - let sit 5 minutes)
2 eggs, beaten
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 Tablespoons melted butter
-
-
Icing #1 - Almond Extract:
1 cup confectioners' sugar
2 tablespoons hot water
1/2 teaspoon almond extract
Optional: Food Coloring
-
-
Icing #2 - Vanilla Extract:
1 cup powdered sugar
2 Tablespoons hot water
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
Optional: Food Coloring
-
-
Icing #3 - Chocolate:
1 cup powdered sugar
2 Tablespoons hot water
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup melted chocolate

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Lightly grease a doughnut pan.
In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, nutmeg, salt & cocoa (if using). Stir in buttermilk, eggs, vanilla and melted butter. Beat together until well blended.
Fill each doughnut cup approximately 3/4 full. Bake 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, until doughnuts spring back when touched. Allow to cool slightly (about 1 minute) before removing from pan.
To make glaze, blend ingredients listed above in a small bowl. Dip doughnuts in the glaze when serving.
