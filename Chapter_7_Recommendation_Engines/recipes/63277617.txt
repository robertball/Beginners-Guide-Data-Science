Title:
Lighter Cajun Chicken Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz linguine
1 1/2 lbs chicken breast strips
2 tsp Cajun seasoning
2 tsp garlic powder, divided
pepper to taste
1 red pepper, sliced
1 yellow pepper, sliced
8 oz sliced mushrooms
1/2 red onion, sliced
1 medium tomato, diced
1 1/2 tsp minced garlic
1 cup chicken broth
1/3 cup skim milk
1 Tbsp flour
3 Tbsp cream cheese
2 scallions, chopped
Cooking spray

Directions:
In blender, combine milk flour and cream cheese; set aside. Season chicken with Cajun seasoning and 1 tsp garlic powder. Prepare pasta according to package directions. Spray skillet with cooking spray and saute chicken over medium-high heat until cooked through; set aside. Add olive oil to skillet and reduce heat to medium. Saute peppers, onion and garlic for 3-4 minutes. Add mushrooms and tomato and saute another 3-4 minutes. Season with remaining garlic powder and pepper. Reduce heat to medium low; add chicken broth and milk mixture; stir 2 minutes. Return chicken to skillet; stir until hot. Add linguine; toss well to coat. Top with chopped scallions and serve.
