Title:
Mini Pork Meatball Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. olive oil
2 carrots - diced
2 ribs of celery - diced
1 medium onion - chopped
2 bay leaves
1 lb. ground pork or pork/beef/veal mixture
1 egg - lightly beaten
1 clove of garlic - finely minced
1/2 cup grated Parmesan
1/2 cup breadcrumbs
6 cups chicken stock or broth
2-3 chicken bouillon cubes
2 cups water
1 1/2 cups uncooked ditalini or other small pasta
1 lb. fresh spinach, kale or escarole
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large pot and add onion, celery and carrot.
Cook for 5 or 6 minutes.
While this cooks, combine egg, meat, garlic, breadcrumbs, Parmesan and salt and pepper.
Add stock and water to vegetables and bring to a boil.
Roll meat mixture into small balls and add to soup.
When meatballs start to firm up, add pasta to pot and stir.
Reduce heat and cook until pasta is tender.
Add spinach (or greens of choice)to pot and cook until wilted.
Adjust seasoning and serve!
