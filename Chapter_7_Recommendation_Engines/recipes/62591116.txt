Title:
Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871611.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package McCormick Seasoning Mix
1 pound lean ground beef for chili
1 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 (16 ounce) can kidney beans

Directions:
Brown meat in skillet. Stir in remaining ingredients. Bring to boil. Cover. Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionaly
