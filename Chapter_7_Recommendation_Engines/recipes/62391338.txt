Title:
Skillet Chicken (with tomato/Shiraz reduction) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C olive oil divided
1 lb ripe tomatoes coarsely chopped
2 cloves of minced garlic
2 chicken breasts (2 inches thick)
garlic salt
pepper
2 cups red wine (Shiraz)
2 tbs brown sugar
1 tbs butter
1 cup fresh baby spinach

Directions:
Divide oil into a medium and large skillet and heat over medium heat.
In large skillet add garlic and tomatos- toss to coat.
In medium skillet turn up heat to medium high.  Season chicken breast to taste with pepper and garlic salt and place into hot oil.  Cook each side until brown about 7 minutes each side.
Meanwhile, add wine to tomato mixture and turn heat up to high.  Crush the tomatoes that have not reduced on their own gently. Bring to a rolling simmer. Reduce liquid by half.
Once reduced add butter and brown sugar mix well, reduce heat to low and cover.
Once chicken has browned on both sides place the chicken into the tomato wine reduction and simmer for 15 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink; turning chicken once.
Spread fresh baby spinach over a serving platter.  Place chicken on top of the spinach and spoon sauce over the chicken.
