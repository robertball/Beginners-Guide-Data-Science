Title:
Venison Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Venison Roast
1 Tablespood chopped Garlic
2 cups Beef Gravy(or bulion)
1 Tablespoon Montreal Steak Seasoning
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup flour
1 teaspoon chili powder

Directions:
Slice roast into thin slices(cross grain)
Combine Roast, Garlic, Gravy, Montreal, and Chili Powder into caserolle dish.
Bake on 350 for 1 hour.
Take 1 1/2 cup of broth from roast and add butter, and flour in medium sauce pan.
Simmer until thick.
Introduce meat into gravy and serve

Submitter: 
Ron
Description: 
A quick and easy way to prepare a venison roast(even the not so tender of cuts)
