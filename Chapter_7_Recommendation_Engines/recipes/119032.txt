Title:
Cheesy Corn Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983540.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (11 ounce) can Mexicorn, drained
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chilies, undrained
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and finely chopped
1 tablespoon ground cumin
1 tablespoon picante sauce
3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup chopped sweet red pepper
Assorted crackers

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first eight ingredients; stir in cheese.  Sprinkle with red pepper.  Cover and chill for 4 hours.  Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Jan Henderson
Description: 
Canned Mexicorn lends a sweet twist to this cheesy crowd-pleaser from Jan Henderson of Smackover, Arkansas.
