Title:
Chicken & Green Bean Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons margarine or butter
3/4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves, cut into 1 inch pieces
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 1/4 cups water
1/2 cup milk
1 (4.7 ounce) package PASTA RONI® Fettucine Alfredo
1 1/2 cups frozen cut green beans
3/4 cup canned French fried onions

Directions:
In large skillet, melt 1 Tbsp. margarine or butter over medium-high heat. Add chicken; cook and stir 4 to 5 minutes or until browned. Sprinkle with black pepper.
Stir in water,  milk and 2 Tbsp. margarine or butter. Bring to a boil. Stir in pasta, seasonings and green beans. Return to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low. Gently boil uncovered 5 to 6 minutes, or until pasta is slightly firm, stirring frequently.
Remove skillet from heat. (Sauce will be thin.) Let stand 3 to 5 minutes for sauce to thicken. Sprinkle with onions.

Submitter: 
RICE-A-RONI®
Description: 
This is a fast and tasty way to whip up some dinner Alfredo style. Green beans, chicken and pasta make this a complete meal in no time flat.
