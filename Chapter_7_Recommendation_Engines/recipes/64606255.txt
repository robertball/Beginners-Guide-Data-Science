Title:
Big Ray's Artichoke Crack Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4049138.jpg
Ingredients:
2 jars artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1/2 jar sun dried tomatoes, patted dried and chopped
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup sour cream
1 clove garlic, chopped
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 tsp hot sauce

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and pour into a greased (sprayed) 2 qt casserole dish. Bake for 40 minutes at 350 until bubbly.  Serve with veggies or cracker that can handle the fight.
