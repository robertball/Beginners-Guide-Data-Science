Title:
Pumpkin Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Devils food cake mix
1 can of pumpkin
3 TBL water

Directions:
pre head oven to 350 degrees
Mix with spoon, pumpkin water and cake mix in bowl just until all incorperated.
Fill muffin tins about 1/2 full and bake for about 10 min. Cool on wire rack!

Submitter: 
Belinda Owens McMath
Description: 
Kinda like a muffin and kinda like a brownie, My kids love them!!
