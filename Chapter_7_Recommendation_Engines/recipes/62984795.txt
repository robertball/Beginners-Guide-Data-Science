Title:
Feta Stuffed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts
1/2 cup of sour cream
1/2 brick of feta cheese
1/2 cup of green onion, chopped
1/2 cup of bread crumbs
Egg wash or italian dressing for breading the chicken
Pepperoncini for topping

Directions:
Thaw chicken
Slice chicken breasts to create a pocket for stuffing
Mix sour cream, feta, and green onions
Bread the chicken
Stuff the chicken
Top with pepperoncini and close with toothpick
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes
