Title:
Moreland's scrambled eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1 1/2 TB worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup smoked Cheddar cheese, shredded
1/2 cup fresh mushrooms, chopped
Salt & pepper to taste
1 TB butter

Directions:
Beat eggs.  Add remaining ingredients.  Meanwhile, in a medium size skillet, melt butter.  Add egg mixture and stir until eggs are cooked thoroughly. Serve immedialtely.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
I first had these eggs at our friend, Moreland's house years ago.  My husband and I still fix them and call them Moreland eggs after the creator.  Worcestershire sauce gives them an added kick.
