Title:
Quesadilla Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/296343.jpg
Ingredients:
1 hamburger patty
2 (10 inch)  flour tortillas
1 tablespoon
1/3 cup shredded monterey jack pepper cheese
1/3 cup shredded cheddar cheese
diced onions
diced canned jalapenos
bacon (cooked and crumbled)
Zesty Seosoning Mix...recipe below
Zesty Seosoning Mix
2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 tablesppons garlic powder
1 tablespoon dried oregano
2 tablespoons paprika
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
crushed red pepper flakes (to taste)
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Combine 1 tablespoon of Zesty Seasoning Mix per pound of meat.  Cook burger as usual, set aside.  Butter one side of each tortilla.  Over medium heat, place tortilla butter side down.  Sprinkle cheese in the middle, top with crushed bacon, onions, tomatos, jalepenos, and burger.  Top with other tortilla, butter side up.  When the bottom tortilla is brown, carefully flip.  Cook until other tortilla browns.

Submitter: 
Meat Legs
Description: 
A different twist on the regular ole hamburger.  Served on torillas with pepper jack and cheddar cheese, with jalepenos for a little spice!
