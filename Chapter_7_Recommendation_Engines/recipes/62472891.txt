Title:
Dump-It Dessert Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 oz) can crushed pineapple
1 (21 oz) can cherry pie filling
1 (18.25 oz) package yellow cake mix
1 stick (1/2 cup) unsalted butter, grated
1/2 - 3/4 cup coconut
Vanilla ice cream or whipped cream, to serve (if desired)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. In a lightly greased 9 x 13" baking dish, spread pineapple; top with filling.
Sprinkle with dry cake mix over this. Grate butter over top. Cover all with coconut.
Bake for 1 hour or until golden. Serve with ice cream or whipped cream, if desired.

Submitter: 
Jessica Paige
Description:
