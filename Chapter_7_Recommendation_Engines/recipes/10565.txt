Title:
German Wreaths Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup shortening
1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
2 hard-cooked eggs, chopped
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 eggs, separated
1/3 cup confectioners' sugar for decoration

Directions:
In large bowl cream together shortening and confectioners' sugar. Beat in uncooked egg yolks and hard-boiled eggs.  Gradually blend in flour. Cover dough and chill for 8 hours or overnight.
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and lightly grease two cookie sheets.
Roll out dough on floured surface to 1/4 inch thickness. Cut into pencil thick strips about 6 to 8 inches long.
For each cookie, braid three strips and form into a circle pinching ends together. Place cookies 1 inch apart on cookie sheets.  Brush with the beaten eggwhites.  Sprinkle with confectioners' sugar.
Bake for 12 to 15 minutes until lightly colored. Cool on wire racks.

Description: 
Made with hard-boiled eggs! Often a Christmas cookie but good any time.
