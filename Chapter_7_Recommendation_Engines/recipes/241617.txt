Title:
Turkey Rub (Poultry Rub) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2595755.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon dried thyme
1 tablespoon dried rosemary
1 tablespoon dried marjoram
1 teaspoon coarse salt (such as Himalayan)
3 tablespoons molasses

Directions:
Grind thyme, rosemary, marjoram, and salt together with a mortar and pestle. Add molasses and mix until well-combined.

Submitter: 
Kimberli_Noel
Description: 
This was a hit for Thanksgiving. The meat had a pleasant and light flavor. It was extremely moist and tender.
This rub can be used with any poultry.
