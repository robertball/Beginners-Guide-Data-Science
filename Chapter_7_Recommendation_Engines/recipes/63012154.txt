Title:
Chocolate Marshmallow Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 3/4 cup butter
2. 1 1/2 cups sugar
3. 3 eggs
4. 1 tsp vanilla
5. 1 1/3 cups flour
6. 1/2 tsp baking powder
7. 1/2 tsp salt
8. 3 Talb cocoa
9. 1/2 cup chopped nuts (walnuts or pecans)
9.5 4 cups miniature marshmallows
topping
10. 1 1/3 cups semisweet choc chips
11. 3 Talb butter
12. 1 c peanut butter
13. 2 cups crisp rice cereal

Directions:
in mixing bowl cream 1. 2. 3. add 4. 5. beat till fluffy.add 6. 7. 8. 9. to creamed mixture
Spread in Greased 15"x10"x1" pn
Bake 350 * 15-18 min
Sprinkle 9.5 evenly over hot cake ...return to the oven 2-3 min( use knife dipped in hot water to spread)cool
Topping:
in small saucepan combine 10. 11. 12. low heat till melted and blended...remove from heat and add 13. spread topping over colled bars Chill
