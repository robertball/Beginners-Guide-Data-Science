Title:
Chocolate - Harlequin- Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter
1/4 c. water
3 TBSP baking cocoa
-------------
1 c. all purp flour
1 c. sugar
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
-------------
1 egg
1/4 c. buttermilk
1 tsp vanilla extract
-------------
TOPPING:
1 c. heavy whipping cream
3 TBSP sugar (powdered)
* toasted sliced almonds  optional

Directions:
In small saucepan, boil water, butter, and cocoa, stirring constantly
Remove from heat; set aside.
In sml bowl, combine dry ingred.
Add egg, butrmilk, & vanilla; mix well.
Beat in cocoa mixture.
Pour into greased & floured 9-in round pan.
Bake at 350o for 35-40 min.
Cool for 10 min.
Remove from pan to wire rack to cool completely.
In sml bowl, beat cream and sugar till soft peaks form.
pile it on the cake and dig in.
Or, pipe it on pretty.
