Title:
Katie's Caprese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tomatoes
1 ball fresh mozzarella
1 TBL minced fresh basil
3 TBL olive oil
2 TBL red wine vinegar
1 tsp dried oregano
1 large pinch white sugar
salt to taste

Directions:
Cut the tomatoes and mozzarella into 1/4 inch slices. Place tomato slices on plate and top with 1 slice each of mozzarella.
Dice or confetti fresh basil and sprinkle on top of mozzarella-tomato slices.
In a bowl or cup, combine remaining ingredients and whisk with a fork diligently for 1 minute. Dressing should be cloudy.
Drizzle vinaigrette over mozzarella-tomato slices right before serving.

Submitter: 
bignosekate
Description: 
This is a very simple, but always delicious mozzarella & tomato salad with a slightly sweet vinaigrette.
