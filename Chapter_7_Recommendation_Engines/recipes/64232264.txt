Title:
Cheese Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 eggs, beaten
4 c. milk
1/2 T. salt
1/2 T. dry mustard
8 slices white bread, cubed
1 lb. shredded cheddar cheese
1 1/2 c. crushed corn flakes (optional)

Directions:
Mix eggs and milk together in a bowl and set aside.
Place bread cubes in greased 9x13-inch casserole pan.
Mix and sprinkle salt and dry mustard evenly over bread cubes.
Cover contents of pan with cheese.
Refrigerate overnight.
Bake at 325 degrees for 90 minutes.
