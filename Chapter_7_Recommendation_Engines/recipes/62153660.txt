Title:
Maryland-style Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. lump crab meat
2 (8 oz.) packages cream cheese
1/2 pint Sour cream
4 T mayonnase
juice of 1/2 lemon
2 T "Old Bay" seafood seasoning
2 tsp worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp garlic salt
1 tsp dry mustard
1 c shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
Mix ingredients together in order given, adding and evenly blending each ingredient. Add only 1/2 cup of the cheddar cheese to mixture when blending.
Place in greased baking dish and top with remaining cheddar cheese. Bake for 45 to 50 minutes or until bubbly.

Submitter: 
FoodJunkie
Description: 
This creamy, spicy Eastern Shore style crab dip is a favorite staple at Maryland functions and parties. It's always one of the first dishes to be eaten up! Served hot, It can be accompanied with crackers, or you can slice up some french baguettes, depending on preference.
