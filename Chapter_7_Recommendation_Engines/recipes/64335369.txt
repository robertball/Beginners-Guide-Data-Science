Title:
Pineapple Upside Down Cake Lesa's Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Round Unsweetened Pineapple
Yellow Butter Cake (Duncan Hines)
1 can #10 crushed unsweetened pineapple
1 stick butter
1 Cup Brown Sugar
Cherries

Directions:
Drain Pineapple, keep juice.
Make cake per recipe, substituting pineapple juice for water.
Melt stick of butter in small pan and put it into 10 inch skillet add brown sugar.
Add rings, cherries, then crushed pineapple.
Add cake batter.
Bake at 350 degrees for 40 - 45 minutes, let cool 5 minutes.  Run a knife around edge of cake and immediately turn over onto plate.
Enjoy!
