Title:
Toll House® Turtle Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976347.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18 ounce) package NESTLE® TOLL HOUSE® Brownie Bar Dough
4 (1.76 oz) package NESTLE® TURTLES® Candies
1/4 cup NESTLE® TOLL HOUSE® Semi-Sweet Chocolate Morsels

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 350 degrees F. Grease 8-inch-square baking pan.
PRESS dough onto bottom of prepared pan. Place turtles on top; press down slightly.
BAKE for 30 to 35 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool completely in pan on wire rack.
MICROWAVE morsels in small, heavy-duty plastic bag on HIGH (100%) power for 30 seconds; knead. Microwave at additional 10- to 20-second intervals, kneading until smooth. Cut tiny corner from bag; squeeze to drizzle over brownies.

Submitter: 
Nestle® Toll House®
Description: 
By using refrigerated brownie dough, you will have more time for decorating.
