Title:
Kidney Bean and Corn Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can kidney beans, rinsed, drained
1 8oz can corn, drained
1/4 cup finely diced red onion
1 TB honey
1 TB lime juice
1 TB chili powder
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1 TB Tabasco, or to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, chill 1 hour or overnight.

Description: 
Light, refreshing colorful salad, great year round.
