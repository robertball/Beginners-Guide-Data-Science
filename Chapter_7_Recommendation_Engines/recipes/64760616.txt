Title:
Chicken Tequila Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground chicken
4 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro leaves
2 cloves garlic, minced
4 oz can mild green chilis
1/2 cup Panko or 1/4 cup plain bread crumbs
2 tablespoons tequila
3/4 teaspoon salt
pepper to taste
shredded lettuce or lettuce leaves
(Optional: 1 sliced bell pepper & 1 sliced small onion)
Cilantro Lime Sauce:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro leaves
1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives
1-2 teaspoons fresh lime juice

Directions:
Mix all the burger ingredients (except the bell peppers, onions and lettuce) in a bowl, just until combined.
Form into 4 equal sized burger patties.
Grill on pre-heated grill on medium-high heat or 350-400 degrees with lid closed for 4 - 5 minutes per side or until cooked through and meat themometer inserted in middle registers 165.
Remove from grill and keep warm.
If desired grill bell peppers and onions 4 - 5 minutes until tender.
While burgers are cooking make sauce by combining all the sauce ingredients in a small bowl. Serve burgers on buns with Cilantro Lime sauce and lettuce. (And grilled bell peppers and onions if desired.

Submitter: 
FunwithFood
Description: 
Something a little bit different then your typical burger. Even though the burger has tequila in it, it is very subtle in flavor and alcohol does cook out during grilling.  The creamy sauce just brings this burger over the top.
