Title:
T-Bone Tomato Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 t-bone steaks
2 tbsp olive oil
1 red bell pepper, chopped
4 tbsp minced garlic
1 tsp dried minced onion
1 tsp ground black pepper
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp ground cloves
1 (14.5 oz.) can diced tomatoes
1 (14.5 oz) can tomato soup
2 cups water
1 (6oz.) can tomato paste

Directions:
In a large Skillet over medium-high heat, saute red bell pepper, minced garlic and dried minced onion in olive oil for about five minutes. Season with cayenne pepper, salt, ground black pepper and ground cloves.  Add tomato soup, diced tomatoes, tomato paste and water, set aside. Brown t-bone steaks in a skillet for 2 to 4 minutes per side, set aside. Bring tomato mixture to a boil, add steaks and simmer for 20 minutes. Serve with tomato mixture spooned over steak.

Submitter: 
JOHN MITSCHKE
Description: 
T-bone steaks cooked in diced tomatoes & tomato soup with a hint of garlic and cloves.
