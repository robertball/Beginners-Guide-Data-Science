Title:
Chicken Fried Steak with Pan Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cube steaks
flour
olive oil
1 cup buttermilk
1 1/2 cups cream
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon pepper
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
1. Heat olive oil in tall skillet, about 1/8 of an inch.
2. Combine flour, salt, cayenne pepper, and paprika.
3. Dredge a steak in flour mixture, dip in buttermilk, and then dredge back in flour mixture. Repeat with second steak.
4. Place the steaks in the hot oil and cook for 4 minutes. Flip and cook on the other side for another 4 minutes.
5. When steaks are fully cooked, remove from heat.
6. Keeping skillet over heat, whisk in flour 1 tablespoon at a time until a roux forms. (Note: Not that much oil is needed to create the roux. If needed, pour out extra oil until a thin layer left.)
7. After roux formed, slowly pour in cream, continuing to whisk.
8. Add pepper and let gravy cook for 2 minutes. Stirring constantly.
