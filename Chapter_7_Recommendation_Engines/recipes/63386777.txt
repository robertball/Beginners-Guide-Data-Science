Title:
Alfredo Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6oz uncooked pasta
1 jar Alfredo sauce
2 lbs uncooked chicken, cut into strips
1 pkg Italian dressing mix
1 tbsp garlic powder

Directions:
Cook and drain pasta
Season chicken with dressing mix and garlic powder.
Place chicken on a griddle or in a frying pan and cook until done,(no pink shows.)
Combine chicken, pasta and Alfredo sauce and serve.
