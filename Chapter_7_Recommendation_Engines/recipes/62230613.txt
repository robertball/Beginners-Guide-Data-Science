Title:
Kelli's Corn Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz box of Jiffy Corn Muffin Mix
1 c. Sour Cream
2 Eggs
1/2 c. Margarine
1 can Creamed Corn
2-3 Tbl. Sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Put margarine in baking pan and melt in oven.  Mix all ingredients together.  Pour into pan with melted margarine.  Bake for 45-50 minutes, until the top cracks and the sides are brown.

Submitter: 
Tiffany Donnelly
Description: 
Corn bread with chunks of creamed corn and creamy souffle texture.
