Title:
Sizzling Southwest Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1008957.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Lbs. Lean Ground Beef
3/4 Cup Soft Bread crumbs
1/4 Cup Finely Minced Onions
1/2 Cup Herdez Traditional Chipotle Mexican Cooking Sauce
Garlic Salt, to taste
8 slices Monterey Jack Cheese
1 Avocado, sliced
1 Large Tomato, sliced
1 Bunch Fresh Cilantro
Sandwich Buns, Toasted
Sauce:
1 Cup Mayonnaise
1/4 Cup Herdez Traditional Chipotle Mexican Cooking Sauce

Directions:
Preheat grill to medium.
Combine 1/4 cup Herdez Traditional Chipotle Mexican Cooking Sauce and Mayonnaise in a small bowl and keep chilled until ready to serve burgers.
Toast buns lightly and reserve.
Combine ground beef, bread crumbs, minced onions and 1/2 cup Herdez Traditional Chipotle sauce. Form mixture into eight burger patties. Sprinkle burgers with garlic salt to taste.
Grill to desired doneness. Top burgers with cheese.
Serve each burger on a toasted bun with a couple of Tablespoons of Chipotle mayonnaise. Garnish with avocado, tomato and a handful of cilantro.

Description: 
Chipotle has a reputation for heat, but this recipe tames the heat and brings out the smoky and rich flavor of the chipotle with just a hint of spice. We love it because it is a quick and easy way to add all the trimmings of a Mexican feast to a simple burger.
