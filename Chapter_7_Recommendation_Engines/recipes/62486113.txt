Title:
Quick Softened Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/491056.jpg
Ingredients:
Butter, amount needed for recipe, cut up

Directions:
Place butter pieces in microwave-safe bowl and microwave on low (power level 3) in 20-second intervals, checking in between.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
I never remember to take my butter out of the refrigerator to soften before baking, so I decided to write down this method I found in Real Simple magazine using the microwave.
