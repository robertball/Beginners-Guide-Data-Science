Title:
Pierogi Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5lb Wheat flour
8 Eggs
3 tsp Salt
4 Cups Water
3/4 Cup Riced (Mashed) potato
1 Cup Softened Butter

Directions:
In medium sized mixing bowl mix wet ingredients (egg, and water).
In large mixing bowl mix Flour, salt.  Add Potato.
Create a well in the flour mixture and gradually mix in egg, water mixture.
Need dough mixture and place in bowl, cover with damp towel and let rest for 10-15 minutes.
Roll mixture out to approx. 1/8 inch thickness.
Cut 6 inch circles out of rolled dough.  Re-Roll excess dough to make more circles.
Add 2 Tablespoons of Pierogi Filling onto each and folder over making a half circle.  Pinch the edges together to form a seal.  Flour each generously to prevent sticking.
