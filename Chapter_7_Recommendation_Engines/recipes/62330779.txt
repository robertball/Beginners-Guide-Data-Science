Title:
Greens the Utica way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices of prosciutto, thin slices, chopped
2 cloves garlic, chopped
2 TBLS. Olive oil
1/4 C. Hot cherry peppers, from the jar, chopped
1 Large head of escarole, rough chop
1 C. Chicken broth
1/2 C. Breadcrumbs, plus hand full for the end
1/4 C. Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Clean the escarole as much as humanly possible and dry.  Chop in large pieces.  In a large saut√© pan add the olive oil and heat on medium heat.  Add the chopped garlic and prosciutto and saute for 2-3 minutes.  Add cherry peppers and cook 1 - 2 minutes longer.  Add the escarole and chicken broth.  Let the broth cook down for 3-5 minutes and gradually add in the breadcrumbs and cheese tossing gently until blended.  Place in a casserole dish, add the last hand full of breadcrumbs on the top and place under the broiler for 3-4 minutes.  Serve hot.

Description: 
If you have ever been to Utica, NY, you know how good this dish is.  If you have not been to Utica, then here is a chance to enjoy what you are missing.
