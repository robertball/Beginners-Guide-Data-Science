Title:
Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/385635.jpg
Ingredients:
3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
1 lime, juiced
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup diced onion
3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro/coriander (optional)
2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced or some salsa
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (optional)

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados (with potato masher), lime juice, and salt. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Kathy Higgins
Description: 
Cilantro and cayenne give this tasty guacamole a kick. Makes four servings.Modified from: Guacamole
