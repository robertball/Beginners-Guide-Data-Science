Title:
Chili Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ½ lbs Ground Beef
1 Small Onion Chopped
1 Can Kidney Beans
1 to 2 Tbls Chili Powder (depending on taste)
Salt & Pepper to Taste
1 bottle Heinz Hot Ketchup (w/Tabasco)
8 oz Cheddar Cheese, Shredded
Tortilla Chips

Directions:
Brown ground meat with onion in large skillet.  While the meat is browning, drain kidney beans and mash with a fork.  Drain meat and add kidney beans, chili powder, salt & pepper and stir well.  Add entire bottle of hot ketchup and simmer 5 minutes on low heat.
Transfer meat mixture to 9” glass pie plate or casserole dish; top with shredded cheddar cheese.  Bake in 350 degree oven until cheese is melted (~5-10 minutes).
Serve with tortilla chips

Submitter: 
LynnG
Description: 
Great for football games, etc.  The secret is in the hot ketchup -- just the right kick.
