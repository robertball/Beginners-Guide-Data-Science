Title:
Creamy Basil Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs Russet potatoes, peeled
2 cups Milk
1 cup Cream
1/4 cup Butter
10 fresh Basil leaves, chopped
Salt, to taste
Fresh ground black pepper, to taste

Directions:
Lightly salt boiling water and cook potatoes until tender; drain well.
Bring milk and cream to a simmer in a pot over low heat. Add potatoes to pot and mash well. If necessary, add potatoes in batches for easier mixing.
Stir in butter and basil, and mix well. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
MIKI
Description: 
These creamy potatoes make a great side dish for pork tenderloin.
