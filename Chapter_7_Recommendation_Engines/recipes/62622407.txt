Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken, Vegetable and Tortellini Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts (8 ounces each) chopped into 1 inch pieces
3 medium carrots cut into coins
3 cloves garlic (finely chopped)
1 large onion, minced
1 can (19 ounces)white kidney beans
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 can (13 ounces) chicken broth
2 1/2 cups water
1 pkg (9 ounces)refridgerated cheese tortellini
1 tsp dried basil
1 small pkg of baby spinach
parmesan cheese to sprinkle on finished product if desired

Directions:
Layer carrots, onion, garlic, chicken and beans in slow cooker.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper, pour broth and water over all.
Cover and cook on low for 6 to 9 hours until chicken is no longer pink.
30 minutes before serving add spinach, basil and tortellini and turn to high setting.
Sprinkle with parmesan if desired.
