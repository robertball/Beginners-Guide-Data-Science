Title:
Vegan Caesar Dressing/ Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ cup cashews, raw
1/8 cup pine nuts
½ lemon, juiced
2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
1 garlic clove, minced
½ cup water
sea salt as desired
black pepper as desired

Directions:
Throw all ingredients for raw caesar dressing in a blender and blend

Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description:
