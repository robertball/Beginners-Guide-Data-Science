Title:
Avocado with Sweet Red Onion, Olive and Wine Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 Red Onion - Chopped
1tbsp Soft Brown Sugar
Splash Red Wine Vinegar
75ml/2Â½fl oz Red Wine
1 Avocado - halved with stone removed
60g/2oz Pitted Black Olives
1tsp Clear Honey

Directions:
Heat the oil in a frying pan, add the red onion and brown sugar and fry gently for five minutes, or until the onions have softened;
Add the vinegar and red wine and turn up the heat to boil for 4-5 minutes, until the liquid has reduced by half and has thickened;
Scoop out the avocado flesh into a bowl, leaving the skin intact. Add the onion and wine mixture to the avocado flesh and mix well;
Add the olives and mix again;
To serve, spoon the avocado mixture back into the avocado shell and drizzle with honey.
