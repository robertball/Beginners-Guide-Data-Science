Title:
Baked cucumber  chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 med cucumbers
1 tbs olive oil
2 tsp apple cider vinegar or
other flavor vinegar
1 tsp sea salt

Directions:
1 slice cucumbers very thin
2 Remove excess water from slices using a paper towel
3 Put cucumbers in bowl and  toss  with ingredients
4 for dehydrator dry at 126-135 for 10 hours
For oven line pan with parchment paper
dry at 175 for 3-4 hours
