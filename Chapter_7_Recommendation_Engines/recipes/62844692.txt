Title:
Shrimp Soft Taco's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/677486.jpg
Ingredients:
4 soft taco shells
1 tomato diced
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 avocado diced
2-3 Tbsp minced red onion
24 shrimp (peeled, cleaned, de-veined, tails off)
chili powder to season shrimp
1/4 vegetable oil for frying
sour cream (optional)

Directions:
1. Sprinkle shrimp with chili powder on both sides, fry until pink and no longer opaque about 1-2 minutes each side.
2. Dice tomatoes, avocado, and mince red onion.
3. Heat taco wraps in microwave for 12 seconds. When shrimp are cooked put 6 on each shell, along with tomato, cheese, avocado, red onion, and sour cream.

Submitter: 
Cookin Mom 84
Description: 
I was in the mood for shrimp, and tacos, and didn't feel like making the ground meat, this is a healthier version of a soft taco!
