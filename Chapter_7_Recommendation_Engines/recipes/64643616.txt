Title:
Chicken and Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts
12 ounces smoked Andouille sausage, cut into small rounds
1 cup chopped onions2 cloves minced garlic
1 cup low sodium chicken broth1 can (14.5 ounces)
1 can diced tomatoes3  tablespoons tomato paste
1cup tomato sauce
2 teaspoons Creole seasoning
1 teaspoon cayenne extras:
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 14 ounce can black beans, rinsed and drained
2 green bell peppers chopped
1 cup sliced green onions for topping
2 tablespoons almond butter (randomly delicious)salt to tastebrown rice for serving

Directions:
1. Place the essential ingredients in the crockpot.2. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 3 hours.3. Shred the chicken directly in the crockpot to your desired texture. 4. Add the extras, if you want, and cover and cook for another 20-30 minutes. 5. This is also a great time to make your rice, if youre serving it with rice. 6. When everything is ready, taste the chicken mixture and season with salt. 7. Serve the creole chicken over rice and topped with green onions.
