Title:
Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head iceberg lettuce
2 tomatoes
1 green pepper
1 can kidney beans, drained
2 cans pitted olives, optional
6 drops tabasco sauce
1 lb. hamburger
1 pkg. taco mix seasoning
1 bag Old Dutch Nacho Cheese flavored taco chips--or brand of choice
4 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 250ml bottle Catalina salad dressing

Directions:
Following the instructions on the taco seasoning mix, fry with the hamburger.
Allow hamburger mixture to cool before adding to the salad.
Chop & toss the lettuce, tomatoes and green pepper, place into a large mixing bowl.
Drain the kidney beans, then mix together with the olives & tabasco sauce.
Grate the cheddar, and when you are ready to serve, toss all above ingredients together.  Add taco chips (about 1/2 - 3/4 of the bag, unless you like alot of chips in your salad) and pour catalina dressing over top to taste. (usually 1/2 - 3/4 of a bottle) Toss and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Lori Guilderson
Description: 
My aunt brings this delicious salad to our family get-togethers, it's a meal in itself!  Using cheese flavored nacho chips really adds zip.
