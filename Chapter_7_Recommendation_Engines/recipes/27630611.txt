Title:
Turkey Tetrazzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz. uncooked vermicelli
2 tsp. veggie oil
1 lb. turkey breast cutlets
3/4 tsp. onion powder, divided
1/2 tsp. salt, divided
1/4 tsp. pepper, divided
2 TBS. dry sherry
2 - 8oz packages presliced mushrooms
3/4 cup frozen peas, thawed
3/4 cup fat-free milk
2/3 cup fat-free sour cream
1/3 cup grated fresh Parmesan cheese
1 - 10-3/4oz. can reduced-fat cream of chicken soup
Cooking spray
1/3 cup dry breadcrumbs
2 TBS. butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450
Cook pasta according to package directions, omitting salt and fat. Drain.
Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Sprinkle turkey with 1/2 tsp. onion powder, 1/4 tsp. salt, and 1/8 tsp. pepper. Add turkey to pan; cook two minutes on each side or until done. Remove turkey from pan.
Add 1/4 tsp. onion powder, sherry, and mushrooms to pan. Cover and cook for 4 minutes or until mushrooms are tender.
Combine peas and next 4 ingredients in a large bowl. Chop turkey. Add 1/4 tsp. salt, 1/8 tsp. pepper, pasta, turkey, and mushroom mixture to soup mixture, tossing gently to combine. Spoon mixture into a 13x9 inch baking dish coated with cooking spray.
Combine bradcrumbs and butter in a small dish, tossing to combine. Sprinkle breacrumb mixture over pasta mixture. Bake at 450.
