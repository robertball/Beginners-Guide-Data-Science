Title:
The most awesome popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 original lightly buttered popcorn bag, 2 tbsp of cinn. sugar (or more if you like it more sweet!)

Directions:
Pop the popcorn in the microwave (by following the directions on the bag), or if you have  a special popper, pop it the regular way. While it's still hot carefully toss in the cinn. sugar. Enjoy!!
