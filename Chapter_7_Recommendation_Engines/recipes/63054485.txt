Title:
Chicken Dijon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsps. melted butter
1 tbsp. dijon mustard
1 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup Italian bread crumbs
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400*F. Mix butter, mustard, worchestershire sauce and salt in one bowl. Mix bread crumbs, and cheese in a second bowl. Dip chicken in butter mixture and dredge through crumb mixture and set on foil lined pan. Bake covered for 45 minutes.
