Title:
"Fowl" Weather Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Uncle Ben's Long Grain and Wild Rice
2 cups cubed cooked chicken or turkey
1 6 oz. package frozen chopped broccoli
1 teaspoon (+/-) salt
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of celery soup
1 jar sliced mushrooms, drained
1 small can fried onion rings

Directions:
Cook the rice according to package directions, adding 1/4 cup butter in place of the suggested amount.
Thaw the broccoli and combine with the cubed chicken.
Add the mushrooms.
Mix in the soups.
In a large casserole dish, press the rice over the bottom and up the sides with the back of a serving spoon.
Fill the "crust" with the broccoli/chicken mixture.
Cover and bake at 400 degrees for 45 minutes or until bubbling throughout.  Remove lid and cover with onion rings.
Return to oven and bake until onion rings are golden brown, watching carefully.
