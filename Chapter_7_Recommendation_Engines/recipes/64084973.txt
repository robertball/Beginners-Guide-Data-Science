Title:
Buffalo Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. chicken breast tenders
3 T hot sauce
1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese
3 T reduced-fat sour cream
3 T 2% milk
3 T light mayo
1 chopped romaine heart
1/2 head chopped iceberg lettuce
2 stalks celery cut into 2 inch matchsticks
1 cup shredded carrots

Directions:
Coat a large nonstick skillet with nonstick cooking spray. Cook chicken in skillet over medium heat about 8 minutes or until cooked through, flipping at halfway point. Remove from heat and stir together with hot sauce.
In a small bowl, stir together blue cheese, sour cream, milk, and mayo.
Combine romaine heart and iceberg lettuce in a large serving bowl. Add celery and carrots. Drizzle dressing over salad. Toss well to combine. Top with chicken.
