Title:
spicy chicken curry soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbs of peanut oil
1 clove of freshly minced garlic
1/2 carrot peeled and cut into tiny pieces
1 can of coconut milk
2 medium sized boneless, skinless chicken breasts chopped into small cubes
1 tbs of green curry paste
2 cups of chicken stock or broth
1/4 cup chopped green onions
The juice of one lime

Directions:
Over medium low heat, add oil to heavy bottom 2qt sauce pan and let warm for 2 minutes.  Add to that garlic and carrots and let sweat for about five minutes.  Constantly stirring, add 1/4 of the can of coconut milk.  Bring to a light boil.  Stir in the curry paste and chicken and make sure to coat the chicken with the mixture.  Turn heat down to a high simmer and add chicken stock.  After bringing to a light boil again, add the rest of the coconut milk, the juice of one lime, and the green onions.  Simmer for about 10 minutes and serve.

Submitter: 
Alissa Doe-Klinger
Description: 
This is a simple chicken curry soup.  Recipe is quite spicy as is.  Adjust the amount of Green curry paste to lessen the amount of heat if necessary.  This is a very good low-carb recipe.
