Title:
Salted Pecan Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1128733.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cup pretzels sticks, crushed
1/2 Cup butter, melted
3 tsp sugar
1 egg
2 tsp milk
40 carmels
1 tsp vanilla extract
3/4 finely chopped pecans
1/4 tsp coarse salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  line bottom and sides of an 8 in square pan with aluminum foil, allowing 2 to 3 inches to extend over the sides; coat with cooking spray.
Stir crushed pretzels, melted butter, sugar and egg together and press onto bottom of pan.  Bake for 11 minutes.
While crust is baking, combine milk and carmels in a medium saucepan.  Cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes or untill carmels have melted.  Remove from heat and stir in vanilla.
Remove crust from oven and pour carmel mivture over hot crust.  Sprinkle with pecans and salt.  Cool in pan on wire rack for 10 minutes.  Chill for at least 30 minutes.
Lift mixture from pan, using foil sides as handles.  Cut into 20 bars.  Store in refrigerator for up to 1 week (as if they will last that long).
