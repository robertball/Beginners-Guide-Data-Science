Title:
Ice Cream Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988883.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Pop-Tarts® Frosted Strawberry toaster pastry, cut in half
1/4 cup ice cream, softened
1 tablespoon whipped cream
1 strawberry

Directions:
Place one half of the Pop-Tarts(R) Frosted Strawberry toaster pastry on a dessert plate; spread the ice cream evenly over the pastry half and top with the remaining piece. Spoon the whipped cream on the sandwich and top with a strawberry. Enjoy immediately!

Submitter: 
Pop-Tarts
Description: 
This Pop-Tarts® ice cream sandwich makes a great summer treat!
