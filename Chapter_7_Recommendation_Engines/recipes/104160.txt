Title:
Buttermilk Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup buttermilk
1 teaspoon Morton® Nature's Seasons® Seasoning Blend

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, mix well. Keep refrigerated. Stir before serving.

Submitter: 
Morton Salt
Description: 
Creamy, lightly seasoned buttermilk dressing is a perfect salad topper.
