Title:
Secret Ingredient Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. romaine lettuce, chopped
1 banana, frozen
4-6 strawberries, frozen
1/3 c. milk or as much as needed
Vanilla extract(opt)

Directions:
Put your chopped lettuce into the bottom of a blender and pour milk in to cover( around 1/4 c but as much as it needs. Blend this till it is all in liquid form and then while the blender is still going on high, add one at a time a peice of strawberry, then a peice of banana, until they are all blended in. Add more milk as you go if it seems to be getting too thick to run properly. Add vanilla extract if using and make sure its blended in. If it needs more sweetener, you can add start with a tsp adding honey or sugar or agave, what ever you want to sweeten it.
