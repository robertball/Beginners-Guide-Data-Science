Title:
Creamsicle Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1022832.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups orange soda (refrigerated)
2 cups ginger ale (refrigerated)
1/2 cup milk
4 scoops orange sherbet (about 1/3 cup each)
4 scoops vanilla ice cream (about 1/3 cup each)

Directions:
Combine orange soda, ginger ale, and milk.
Put a scoop of orange sherbet and a scoop of ice cream in each glass.  Pour drink mixture over both and serve.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
This is a great refreshing drink for summer parties!  Your guests will be reminded of eating creamsicles as a child.
