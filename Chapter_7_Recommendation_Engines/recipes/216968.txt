Title:
Mango Ponzu Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988012.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons Kikkoman Ponzu
2 tablespoons lime juice
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
2 cups diced mango
1/2 cup diced jicama
2 stalks green onion, chopped
2 tablespoons chopped cilantro

Directions:
In medium bowl, whisk ponzu, lime juice, sugar and pepper until sugar is dissolved. Add mango, jicama, green onion and cilantro; toss to combine.

Submitter: 
Kikkoman
Description: 
The velvety sweetness of mango, the crunch of jicama, and hints of citrus and cayenne make a terrific salsa that pairs well with grilled food.
