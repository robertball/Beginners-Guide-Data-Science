Title:
CHICKEN PAPRIKASH-from the happy hungarian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/705921.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup low fat yogurt
1 Tbsp.flour
4 Tbsp.margarine
4 chichen breast-cut in chunks
1/2 cup flour
1/2 tsp.each salt and pepper
2 tsp.hugarian paprika
1/2 lb.fresh mushrooms,sliced
1 large onion chopped
1 red pepper,sliced
2 tsp.minced garlic
1 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup dry white wine
cooked pasta
2 Tbsp.fresh parsley

Directions:
Strain yogurt using coffee filter in a strainer ,discard liquid.Let stand for 15 min. to get to room temperature.Scrape yogurt into small bowl,mix in 1 Tbsp of flour.
Wash chicken and pat dry.Cut into chunks.Combine flour with salt,pepper and paprika.Roll chichen in flour mixture.
In dutch oven melt margarine over medium heat.Add chicken and brown on all sides.Remove chicken from pan,keep warm.
Add mushrooms, onions,red pepper and garlic,cook till lightly browned.Add the chicken broth and wine.Add the chicken,bring to a gentle boil,reduce heat and simmer for 50-60 minutes.
Remove pan from heat.Stir a little of the hot sauce from the  pan into yogurt then slowly add to the chicken,stirring constantly.
Serve over hot noodles.Sprinkle with fresh parsley.
