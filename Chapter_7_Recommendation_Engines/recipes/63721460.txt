Title:
Lunchbox Cookies III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1033776.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C. All purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
1/2 C. Butter
1 C. Granulated sugar
2 Eggs
1 C. Medium shredded coconut
1 C. Chocolate chips
1/2 C. Sultana raisins
1/2 C. Quick Oats

Directions:
Grease a large cookie sheet and set aside
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Combine flour, Baking Powder and Baking Soda together in a medium size bowl and blend well. Set aside
Mix together sugar and butter to form a smooth paste
Add eggs to  mixture and beat well after each addition
Beat in coconut,  quick oats, chocolate chips and raisins until thoroughly mixed and coated well
Drop by teaspoonful a small amount onto greased cookie sheet and bake for 10 minutes or until lightly browned on top
Place on a cookie rack to cool completely before putting into storage container

Submitter:

Description: 
A gooey chocolate chip cookie with chewy coconut and raisins for snack time.
