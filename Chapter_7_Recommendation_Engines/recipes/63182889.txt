Title:
Cheesecake Filling / Frosting - Sugar Free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/819007.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) Packages Cream Cheese, softened
1/2 cup Butter, softened
3/4 cup Xylitol (equivalent to 3/4 cup Sugar)
1 tsp Vanilla Extract (No Corn Syrup, Gluten Free)

Directions:
Cream together Cream Cheese & Butter until smooth
Mix in the Vanilla Extract
Gradually stir in the Xylitol
Pour into crust and chill or use as frosting

Submitter: 
Zan
Description: 
Easy & Quick No Bake Cheesecake filling that can also be used as frosting.
