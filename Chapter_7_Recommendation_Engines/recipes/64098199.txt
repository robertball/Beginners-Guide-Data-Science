Title:
Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 64 oz. cans tuna
1/2 bunch celery, small diced
3 C mayonnaise
1 large yellow onion, small diced
1 TBSP salt
2 TBSP granulated garlic
2 lemons, juiced
1 TBSP black pepper
2 TBSP Lawry's
1 tsp white pepper
12 eggs, hard-boiled, chopped
1 TBSP yellow mustard
2 TBSP dill

Directions:
Strain tuna and place in a large bowl.  Break up tuna.  Add all other ingredients and mix well until combined.

Submitter: 
Chef Hurley Baird
Description: 
Moist and zesty!  This mouthwatering tuna salad will have you craving more!
