Title:
Fresh Blueberry Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, room temp
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1/4 cup light sour cream
1 tsp vanilla
pinch salt
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 cup all purpose flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 cup bluberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
Beat together butter and sugar until creamy. Add egg, sour cream, vanilla, salt. Beat until smooth.
In a small bowl, whisk together both flours and baking powder. Beat into butter mixture.
Gently fold in blueberries using a spatula or wooden spoon, being careful not to squish them.
Using small cookie scoop (or rounded tablespoons) scoop batter onto baking sheet about 2 inches apart.
Bake at 350 for 10 - 15 minutes.
