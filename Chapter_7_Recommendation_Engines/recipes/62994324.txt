Title:
Turkey & Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBS olive oil
1/2 small onion, finely diced
1/2 carrot, finely diced
1/2 red bell pepper, finely diced
2 cloves garlic, pressed
1 C cubed turkey (white meat)
1 box chicken or turkey stock
1 recipe Pat's Dumplings

Directions:
heat oil in pan, saute onions, carrots, & bell pepper till softened. Add garlic, sautee 1 to 2 minutes. Add turkey and stock, heat till boiling.
Meanwhile make dumpling recipe. Once soup has come to a boil, add dumplings, including side bits (these dissolve and thicken soup bas). Turn down heat to simmer until dumplings are cooked.
