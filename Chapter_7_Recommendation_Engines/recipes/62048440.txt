Title:
Dry Rub for Ribs or Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
brown sugar
ground black pepper
onion powder
garlic powder
paprika
salt
crushed red pepper

Directions:
Mix together thoroughly. Rub well into ribs or chicken and refrigerate overnight. Spicier with optional crushed red peppers.

Submitter: 
Love to cook
Description: 
1/2 cup packed brown sugar 
1 1/2 tablespoons ground black pepper
1 level tablespoon onion powder 
1 level tablespoon garlic powder 
2 level tablespoons paprika 
1 tablespoons salt 

Optional if you want spicier: 
1 1/2 teaspoons crushed red pepper
