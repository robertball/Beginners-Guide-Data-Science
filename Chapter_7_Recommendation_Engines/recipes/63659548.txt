Title:
Oh-So-Good Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 teaspoons olive oil
6 tablespoons sour cream
4 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese
2 cups fresh sliced mushrooms
1 (16 ounce) jar salsa
2 cups cooked white rice
8 ounces boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and diced
1 can drained black beans
optional sliced olives

Directions:
In a skillet, heat oil over medium heat.  Saute mushrooms.
Combine mushrooms with remaining ingredients.  Pour into a greased casserole dish.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 25 to 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Yomama
Description: 
This chicken and rice casserole would be yummy enough with just the salsa, Cheddar and sour cream.  The bounty of sauteed mushrooms is an oh-so-good bonus.Modified from: Oh-So-Good Chicken
