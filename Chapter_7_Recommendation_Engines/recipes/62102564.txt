Title:
Mom's Award-Winning Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Lean Ground Beef
1 12 oz. Tube Jimmy Dean HOT Sausage
1 Cup Chopped Onion
2 Cloves Garlic Minced
2 15 oz. Cans Diced Tomatoes Undrained
1 16 oz. Can Tomato Sauce
2 15 oz. Cans Kidney Beans Drained
2 Tbsp. Chili Powder
1 1/2 Tsp. Ground Cumin
1/2 Tsp. Salt
1/2 Tsp. Pepper

Directions:
Cook ground beef in pan until brown
Add onion and garlic and continue to cook until very brown
Drain and add to slow cooker
Cook sausage until very brown
Do not drain
Add to slow cooker
Stir in remaining ingredients
Cook on low for at least 6 hours
The longer you cook it the better the flavor!

Submitter: 
Jenni Rose
Description:
