Title:
Refreshing Limeade Recipe

Ingredients:
3 limes
2/3 cup SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
2 quarts cold water

Directions:
Cut limes in half and squeeze lime juice into a 2 quart pitcher.  Add SPLENDA(R)Granulated Sweetener and stir until dissolved.  Fill pitcher with cold water, stir and chill in refrigerator.
