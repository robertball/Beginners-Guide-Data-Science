Title:
Sausage Cheese Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb pork sausage
4 c buttermilk biscuit mix
2 c shredded cheddar cheese
1 c water
Seasonings as desired (chili powder, smoked paprika, etc.)

Directions:
1)  Crumble and brown sausage.  Drain.
2)  In a large bowl, combine biscuit mix and cheese.
3)  Add seasonings as desired.
4)  Add cooked, drained sausage and stir until well
blended.
5)  Stir in water until just moistened.
6)  Shape into 1 1/2" balls.  Bake at 375F for about
15 minutes or until golden.

Submitter: 
Elle
Description: 
A savory biscuit/cookie made with cheese and meat.
