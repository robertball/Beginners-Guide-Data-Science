Title:
Lime Honey Soda Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4461038.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups club soda
1 cup fresh lime juice
1/4 cup honey
ice

Directions:
Mix club soda, lime juice, and honey together in a pitcher until honey is dissolved. Serve over ice.

Submitter: 
Yui
Description: 
My favorite drink on hot days. Well, it's hot most of the time of the year in Bangkok! Add chopped fresh fruit if desired.
