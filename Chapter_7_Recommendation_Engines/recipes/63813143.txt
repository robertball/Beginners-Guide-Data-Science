Title:
Impossible Coconut Pie II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups milk
1 cup flaked coconut
4 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
6 tablespoons margarine
3/4 cup Splenda
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Directions:
Place milk, coconut, eggs, vanilla, flour, margarine, and splenda in blender.  Mix well.   Pour into a greased and floured 10 inch pie plate.  Sprinkle nutmeg on top.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Josie
Description: 
Milk, eggs, coconut and a few other nice ingredients are plopped into the electric blender and poured into a prepared pie plate. It then goes into the oven an quickly becomes an amazing coconut custard pie.Modified from: Impossible Coconut Pie II
