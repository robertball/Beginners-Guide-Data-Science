Title:
Southwestern Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of corn
1 can black beans-drained and rinsed
1 16 oz. jar chunky salsa
6 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1c. shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Mix the corn,black beans and 1/2 c.salsa in crock pot. Lay the chicken over the top and top with remaining salsa. Cook on low for 7-8 hrs. or on high 3-4 hrs. 5 min before serving,top with cheese and cover to melt.
served it over brown rice. Our whole family loved it.
