Title:
Roast beef cooked in pasta sauce. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp.olive oil,
1 large onion chopped
1 carrot chopped
1 stalk celery chopped
1tbsp.finely minced fresh rosemary(if dried 1/2 teaspoon,or to taste.
2 bayleaves
dash of salt and pepper
1/2teaspoon oregano(if fresh 2Tbsp.)
1/2 teaspoon Turmeric.
2 cans diced tomatoes put through the blender to make smooth.
1 beef roast about 5 or 6 pounds.
1 can tomotoe paste
2 tbsp.fresh basil

Directions:
Heat oil in roaster or oven proof dish,saute onion,carrot,celery 5 minutes,add Turmeric and stir 1 minute.Add the tomatoes,and the herbs,salt and pepper,bring to a gentle boil,take off heat,add the roast,put in 325 oven, cover,bake for 2 and 1/2-3 hours.The sauce is good with any type of pasta.If you wish take the cover off for the last 1/2 hour.

Submitter: 
manella
Description: 
My mother made her roast this way,it comes out tender and the added bonus is you also get a nice pasta sauce.
