Title:
Cannellini Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon oil, olive, extra-virgin
1/2 tablespoon vinegar, red wine
1/2 tablespoon onion(s), red minced
1/2 teaspoon oregano, dried
1 medium cucumber(s) peeled, seeded, and diced
1/2 can(s) beans, cannellini (15 ounces), drained and rinsed
1/2 medium pepper(s), red, bell finely diced
salt to taste
peppercorns, black freshly ground, to taste

Directions:
Whisk together oil, vinegar, onion, and oregano in a large mixing bowl.
Add cucumbers, beans, and bell pepper; toss to combine. Season with salt and pepper and serve.
