Title:
Big Ray's Irish Country Style Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/996101.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz Guinness (or other dark stout beer)
1/4 cup ketchup
1/4 cup honey
1 tsp ground pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1 bunch green onions chopped
6 lbs bone -in country style ribs
2 Tbl corn starch

Directions:
Mix first 8 ingredients in slow cooker.  Put ribs into sauce, making sure they are covered (push 'em down if needed or add water to cover).  Cook on low for 5 hours.
Heat oven to broil.  Remove ribs to broiling pan.  Put 2 cups of liquid from crock pot into a sauce pan and bring to a boil.  Mix corn starch with several Tbl of additional liquid from crock and add to saucepan, reducing heat and stirring until thickened.  Spoon over ribs and put under broiler until carmelized, basting with addition sauce as needed, and crispy brown about 5-10 minutes.
