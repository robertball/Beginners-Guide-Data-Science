Title:
Laura's Quick Slow Cooker Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 pound ground turkey
1 (10.75 ounce) can  low sodium tomato soup
1 can Rotel
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained
1/2 can water
1/2 medium onion, chopped
1 tablespoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
1/2 tablespoon ground cumin
1 pinch ground black pepper
1 pinch ground allspice
salt to taste
1 can corn, drained

Directions:
Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat. Place turkey and onion in the skillet, and cook until evenly brown; drain.
Coat the inside of a slow cooker with cooking spray, and mix in turkey, tomato soup, Rotel, kidney beans, black beans, water and onion. Season with chili powder, red pepper flakes, garlic powder, cumin, black pepper, allspice and salt.
Cover, and cook 8 hours on Low or 4 hours on High. Before last hour, mix in corn.

Submitter: 
Kelly K.
Description: 
This is an easy chili recipe that you can throw in the slow cooker and forget about all day.  Great topped with Cheddar cheese, crushed corn chips, and a dollop of sour cream.Modified from: Laura's Quick Slow Cooker Turkey Chili
