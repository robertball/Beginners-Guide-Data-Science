Title:
Beef Lo Mein Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/717859.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz of beef
2 Green Onions, 1 to 2 inch strips
1/2 Onion, chopped
2 stocks of celery, chopped
1 table spoon of fresh ginger, chopped
2 cloves of garlic, minced
2 cups of napa cabbage
1 carrot, sliced (optional)
1/2 cup of broccoli (optional)
1/2 cup of snow peas (optional)
1/2 cup of peas (optional)
Lo Mein oriental noodles
Finishing sauce
1/4 cup of Beef stock
1 tablespoon of soy sauce
1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
1 tablespoon of hoisin sauce
1 tablespoon of dry sherry (optional)
1 tablespoon of sesame seed oil

Directions:
Mix all liquids into a measuring cup, add and sir in corn starch.
fill 3 quart pot with water, boil, add Lo mein noodles, only cook for 2 mins. remove, and rinse the noodles. If you cook them too long they become soggy and fall apart when you fry them.
Heat up a fry pan with some vegetable oil or olive oil in the pan.
fry the beef until the beef is browned, remove beef and set the beef aside.
Make sure there is some oil in the pan, and add, onions, green onions, garlic, ginger, cabbage, celery, and any other vegetables you wish. fry and stir vegetables until they are cooked to your desired taste.
Add noodles, and beef and stir till hot
Add finishing sauce, and stir into the vegetables, and noodle mixture, the fishing sauce should thicken up rather quickly.
remove and dish
