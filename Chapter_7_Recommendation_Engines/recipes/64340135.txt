Title:
Raw chocolate mousse, lactose free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2080731.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup cashews (Preferably soaked in water over night. It is optional)
2 tablespoons cocoa powder
3 tablespoons agave syrup
2 tablespoons coconut cream

Directions:
If you have soaked your cashews â€“ rinse them and let them dry as much as possible.
Put the cashews in a blender and blend for a couple of minutes until you have a smooth nutbutter.
Add the rest of the ingredients and blend until you have a smooth chocolate mousse.
Taste and add more agave syrup or cocoa powder if needed.
Enjoy straight away or chill in the refrigerator for a while. It will thicken when it is chilled.

Submitter: 
AngelasHeaven.com
Description: 
This raw chocolate mousse is smooth, creamy and soft. It just melts in your mouth. With only a few ingredients you will make this healthy dessert in a couple of minutes.
