Title:
Tomato Pesto Chicken Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/802232.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium-sized tomatoes
1 3/4 lb boneless, skinned, chicken breasts
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 tablespoons canola oil
6 tablespoons sun-dried tomato pesto
3/4 cup shredded cheddar-jack cheese

Directions:
Cut six 1/4 inch thick slices from the tomatoes. Preheat large saute pan on medium high for 2-3 minutes. Cut each chicken breast in half, season with salt and pepper.
Place oil in pan, then add chicken. Cook 8-10 minutes, turning only once, or until golden.
reduce heat to medium low. Top chicken with pesto, tomatoes, and cheese. Cover and cook 10-12 minutes or until 165 degrees F. Serve.
