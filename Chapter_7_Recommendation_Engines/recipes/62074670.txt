Title:
Marnie's Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 bunch of cilantro
1 tomatateo
1 white onion
1 jalepeno
1 large can of whole tomatoes
salt
pepper
1 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
1.Husk tomateo
2.Chop first four ingredients
3.Pour half can of tomatoes into blender add all ingredients except for onion, then pour rest of can then turn on blender on low speed just to mix ingredients.
4.Add chopped onion and mix
5.Add garlic powder then salt and pepper to taste mix with and serve cold.

Submitter: 
mjhutch
Description: 
A very authentic resturant style salsa. It has just the right amount of spice and is bursting with flavor. You'll be asked for this recipe, for sure!
