Title:
Creamy Kale Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/834214.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Cups Kale, chopped and stem removed
1/4 C red onion, chopped
3 Tbsp Raisons
3 Tbsp Sunflower seeds
1/4 Cup Mayonnaise
1/2 Cup plain Greek Yogurt
Juice of one lemon
Zest from lemon
2 tsp Sugar

Directions:
Wash Kale in a salad spinner.  use a sharp knife to remove kale from its stem and chop.  Wash and chop the onion.  Mix the kale, onion and raisons in a large bowl.  Whisk the mayonnaise, yogurt, lemon and zest, and sugar and pour over kale mixture and mix together. chill for 2 hours or more (best if left to sit overnight).  Add the sunflowers and mix in before serving.
