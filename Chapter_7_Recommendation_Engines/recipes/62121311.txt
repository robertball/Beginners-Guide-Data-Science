Title:
Warm Winter Citrus Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium ruby red grapefruit, peeled, with sections cut from the membrane.
2 large oranges, peeled, with sections cut from the membrane.
1 cup fresh pineapple chunks, or one 8 oz can pineapple chunks, drained.
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
2 Tablespoons butter, softened.

Directions:
Combine grapefruit sections, orange sections and pineapple chunks in a 1-quart broiler safe baking dish.
In a small bowl, stir together brown sugar and butter until well mixed; sprinkle the sugar mixture over the fruit.  Broil about 4 inches from the heat source for 5 to 6 minutes  until sugar is bubbly

Submitter: 
Dianemwj
Description: 
This is a lovely blend of citrus fruit and pineapple.  Just right for after all the over-indulgence of holiday desserts.  Light, healthy and delicious!
