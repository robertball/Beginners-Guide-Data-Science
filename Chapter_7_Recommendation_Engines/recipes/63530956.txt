Title:
FAUX CHILI Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 can of diced/stewed tomatoes
- 1 can of corn, undrained
- 1 small can of tomato paste (2 if too watery)
- 1lb of ground beef
- 2 cans of kidney beans, undrained
- 1 can of black/pinto beans, undrained

Directions:
Brown hamburger and drain.
Dump beef and all contents (with their liquids) in a large pot and turn the heat to Medium or Medium-Low.
Let simmer, stirring occasionally, for 40-60 minutes or until beans are soft.
**if too watery add 1 can of tomato paste
**if too tomato-y add some chili powder or chili/taco seasoning.
Excellent served on top of rice.

Submitter: 
Sidnie Anderson
Description: 
A fast alternative to a 'sweet' chili
