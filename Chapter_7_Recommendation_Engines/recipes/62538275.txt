Title:
Cranberry spice pound cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/522162.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of dried cranberries
1/2 cup coarsely chopped walnuts
1/2 cup dark sugar
2 tablespoons of honey
2 tablespoons flour
4 cups flour
1 1/2 tablespoons of baking powder
1/2 tablespoon of grund cinammon
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/8 teaspoon groun clove
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup melted butter (2 sticks)
4 eggs
1/2 cup dark sugar
1/2 cup honey
1/2 cup milk
Butter and floured ring mold or spring mold.

Directions:
Oven at 350° F. (175°C)
In a small bowl mix together the cranberries, walnuts, the half cup of dark sugar, the two tablespoons of honey and the two tablespoons of flour.
Cover de bottom of a ring mold with the mix and pack tighly.
In a large mixing bowl mix all the dry ingridients: 4 cups of flour, baking powder, cinammon, nutmeg, clove and salt.
In the blender at medium speed blend the melted butter and the 4 eggs until a mayonaise like mixture forms.
Add the half cup of dark sugar, the half cup of honey until sugar is disolved
Incorporate the milk.
Add the mixture to the dry ingredients and mix with a spatula.
Fill de buttered and floured pan.
Bake until golden brown and a toothpick comes out clean, around 40 minutes.

A rare lazy afternoon produced this spiced pound cake textured bread topped with a cranberries and walnuts with ingredients that were availeable in the pantry.
