Title:
Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 hard boiled eggs, halved
Yolks of the eggs
Mayonnaise about 1/3 cup
Paprika,optional
Pepper for taste
1 tbs. sweet relish

Directions:
Cut the hard boiled eggs in half. Separate the yolks from the eggs and put them in a small bowl. Add mayo, more can be added to your liking. Mash with a fork until well blended. Add pepper and sweet relish. Spoon the mayo mixture into the halved eggs. Place on a platter or deviled egg holder. Sprinkle with paprika. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
thepurplebaker
Description: 
Deviled Eggs are a delicious and quick snack. They are especially good for parties and holiday meals.
