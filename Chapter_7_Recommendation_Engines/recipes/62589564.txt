Title:
Breakfast Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
seasoned croutons
1 pound Jimmy Dean sausage, browned and drained
1 c swiss cheese, shredded
1 c cheddar cheese, shredded
1/2 c milk
6 eggs
1T dry mustard
1 c milk
1 can cream of - soup

Directions:
Spray 13/9 pan.  Layer with seasoned croutons.
Layer sausage and cheeses.
Mix 1.5 c milk and eggs and mustard. Pour over cheese.
Refrigerate overnight.
Mix soup and 1c milk, pour over.
Bake at 300 for 1 hour covered with foil, 30 minutes uncovered.
