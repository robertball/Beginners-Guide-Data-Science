Title:
Sugared Almonds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Whole blanched almonds
1/2 cup white sugar
2 tablespoons butter
1/2  tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Heat almonds, sugar and butter in a heavy skillet over medium heat.
Stirring constantly until almonds are toasted and sugar is golden brown about 15 minutes
Stir in vanilla
Spread on aluminum foil
Cool and break into clusters.
