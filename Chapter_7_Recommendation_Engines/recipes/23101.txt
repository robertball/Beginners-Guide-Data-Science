Title:
Ann's Sister's Meatloaf Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/101268.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground beef
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups dry bread crumbs
1/4 cup ketchup
1 teaspoon monosodium glutamate (MSG)
1/2 cup warm water
1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
2 slices bacon
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl combine the beef, eggs, crumbs, ketchup, MSG, water and soup mix. Mix well and spoon mixture into loaf pan. Cover with 2 strips of bacon, then cover with tomato sauce.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour.
