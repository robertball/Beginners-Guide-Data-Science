Title:
Big Ray's Mexican Beef & Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6092462.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb.  ground beef
1 large onion, chopped
1 bell pepper, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup Mexican Style Shredded Four Cheese
1 pkg. (1 oz.) taco seasoning mix
1 cup long-grain white rice, uncooked
1 cup frozen mixed vegetables (carrots, corn, peas)
1 can (14.5 oz.) black beans, drained & rinsed
1 can (14.5 oz.)  diced tomatoes, undrained
1 cup  salsa
1 cup chicken broth

Directions:
Brown meat, onions, bell peppers and garlic in large nonstick skillet with lid; drain. Return meat to skillet. Add remaining ingredients to meat; mix well and bring to a boil. Put on a lid and simmer for about 25 minutes or until rice is tender. Add the cheese on top, put the lid back on for a few minutes until it melted.
