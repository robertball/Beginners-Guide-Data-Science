Title:
Baked Tomato Slices Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 small tomatoes, cores removed
pinch salt
pinch pepper
3Tbsp. olive oil
2Tbsp. red wine vinegar
1/2tsp. basil
24 thin slices of cheese (provel, romano, provelone, mozzerella, or parmesan)

Directions:
Place oven on "broil"
Cut tomatoes into 4-1/4 inch slices
Place slices on lightly greased baking dish
Season tomatoes with salt and pepper
Drizzle slices with olive oil and vinegar
Sprinkle basil on top of slices
Top tomatoes with cheese slices
Broil 5-7 minutes or until cheese is bubbly
Serve immediately

Submitter: 
SPEARL20
Description: 
Delicious, quick way to enjoy fresh summer tomatoes with just a few ingredients you already have on hand!  If you have fresh basil available, this is even better.  I recommend using provel cheese if you can find it.
