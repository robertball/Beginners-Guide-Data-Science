Title:
Pumpkin Pecan No-Bake Cheesecake Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/332742.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8oz)pkg 1/3 less fat cream cheese, softened
3/4 cup sugar
1 (15oz) can Libby's 100% Pure Pumpkin
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
1/4 tsp ground clove
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 cup chopped pecans, divided
1 standard graham cracker pie crust
Whipped topping (optional)

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl cream, cream cheese & sugar together, mixing thoroughly until smooth.
Add pumpkin, cinnamon, nutmeg, clove, ginger and vanilla. Mixing and scrapping the sides of the bowl thoroughly until all is combined and smooth.
Add 1/3 cup of chopped pecans into mixture, then spread pie mixture into the pie crust and sprinkle remaining pecans on top of mixture.
Cover with lid and refridgerate for 4 hours. Top each slice with whipped cream as a garnish.

Submitter: 
Melissa Goff
Description: 
Trying to come up with a pumpkin cheese ball, didn't work out into my favor but came out with a wonderful pie instead. With Cream cheese, pumpkin, pecans, and spices mixed in pure taste bud heaven served in a graham cracker crust. Such a wonderful turn out on a mistake.
