Title:
Tempura Batter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Cups Flour
8 Cups Soda Water
2 Teaspoons Salt

Directions:
1.  Pour soda water into a large bowl (Disconnect the Coke syrup dispenser, dispense Coke until it runs clear, then measure the soda water.)
2.  Slowly add flour and whip together until there are no lumps. Gradually add salt.

Submitter: 
Chef Hurley Baird
Description: 
Produces a light and crispy coating.  Never greasy!
