Title:
Hot Cocoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 heaping teaspoon unsweetened powdered cocoa
2 teaspoons sugar
8 oz milk

Directions:
In a small pot, mix the sugar and cocoa.
Slowly stir in about 1 tablespoon of milk until all the powder is moist and somewhat syrupy.
Slowly and gradually while stirring constantly, add the remaining milk.
Heat on medium heat until hot but be careful not to boil.

Submitter: 
Clara <3
Description: 
A traditional cocoa recipe for those of us who like our chocolate richer than water could ever allow.  Perfect for those cold, rainy nights in front of a fireplace with a good book.
