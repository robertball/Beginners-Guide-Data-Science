Title:
Amazing Alize Bleu Lemonade Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. Alize Bleu (a blue French Vodka)
1 Lemon
4 teaspoons Sugar

Directions:
Cut lemon into quarters.  Squeeze lemon juice into shaker & place lemons (rind & all) into shaker.  Add sugar & mash it all together.  Add Alize Bleu & shake.  Strain into 2 glasses.  A small lemon zest can be added to rim of glass.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Stefany Grider Baldwin
Description: 
Great drink for a hot summer day!
