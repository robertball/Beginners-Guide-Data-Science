Title:
Ham Salad with a Schmear Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5978879.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups cubed fully cooked ham
1/2 onion, cut into chunks
3 cloves garlic, or more to taste
1 1/2 cups mayonnaise
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 tablespoon sweet pickle relish, or to taste
1 tablespoon stone-ground mustard, or to taste

Directions:
Pulse ham, onion, and garlic in a food processor until finely chopped; transfer to a bowl.
Place mayonnaise, cream cheese, relish, and mustard in food processor and process until smooth.
Stir cream cheese mixture and ham mixture together in a bowl.

Submitter: 
badbabybeth
Description: 
I use this recipe when I have leftover ham. It's simple, quick, and delicious; not to mention the easy clean up! You can also add a little shredded Cheddar cheese or American cheese, if desired. Serve on sandwiches or with crackers as an appetizer.
