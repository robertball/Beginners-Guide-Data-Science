Title:
Texas Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz) package egg noodles
2 lb. ground beef
3 tsp. butter or margarine
2 8 oz. cans tomato sauce
2 tsp. garlic powder
1 block cream cheese
1 c cottage cheese
1 c shredded mozarella

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit. Grease a large 9x13" baking dish.
Brown beef in skillet and drain. Add margarine, tomato sauce and garlic powder and simmer for 5 to 7 minutes on low heat, stirring often to blend flavors.
Boil egg noodles until tender. Drain. Add cream cheese and cottage cheese to drained pot of noodles and stir until melted.
Pour noodle mixture into greased dish. Add meat sauce mixture on top. DO NOT MIX TOGETHER!
Sprinkle with mozarella cheese and bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 20 to 30 minutes or until bubbly.

Submitter: 
FoodJunkie
Description: 
Although the name conjures the image of a spicy, tex-mex style dish, it tastes very similar to lasagna, although a bit easier to make.
