Title:
Potato Bake with Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 c. instant mashed potatoes
1 lb. breakfast sausage cooked
1-2 onions minced
1-2 garlic minced
3 c. shredded cheese (mozzarella)
1 can evaporated milk
1/8 t. cayenne pepper
1/8 t. nutmeg

Directions:
Brown onions and garlic in butter.  Mix with sausage.  Layer potatoes, sausage mixture, and cheese in buttered casserole dish.  Pour milk with nutmeg and cayenne over it.  Let set about 30 min.  (Can be skipped but worked the first time...)  Bake at 375* for about 30-45 min until browned on top.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Lebow
Description: 
I was inspired by a recipe in Family Circle.  Included is he original but everyone loved what I could offer because we didn't have most of what the original called for!
