Title:
Gourmet Jell-O Shots: Chocolate-Covered Cherries! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/835845.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) box of Cherry Jell-o
1 cup boiling water
1/2 cup (good) Vodka
1/2 cup Godiva Chocolate Liqueur

Directions:
Place empty 2oz plastic cups on a cookie sheet that will fit in the refrigerator.
Boil water.
In a mixing bowl, empty the contents of the Jell-o packet.
Slowly add water, mixing with a whisk.
Slowly add 1/2 cup vodka and 1/2 cup Godiva Chocolate liqueur, continuing to mix.
Pour mixture into 2 oz. plastic cups (available at party supply stores).
Place cookie sheet in the refrigerator and let jell-o set  for several hours, preferably overnight.
Enjoy!
