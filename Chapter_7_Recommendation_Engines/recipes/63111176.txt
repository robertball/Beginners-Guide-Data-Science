Title:
Chicken pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz portion fresh pizza dough
2 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
12 oz cubed chicken breast
4 oz sweet Italian sausage
1/4 cup sliced onion
1 tablespoon mushrooms sliced
2 teaspoons cornmeal
4 oz shredded cheese
3/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
2 tablespoons parsley
2 teaspoons thyme

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450. Heat skillet with 1 1/2 teaspoons olive oil, chicken and sausage and cook for 5 minutes. Remove mixture from pan and set aside.  Add 1 tablespoon oil to pan and add mushrooms, onions, and garlic and cook for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.  Arrange dough into a 14 inch circle on baking sheet or pizza pan.  Brush dough with 1 tablespoon of olive oil.  Cook for 5 minutes and remove from oven.  Sprinkle with 2 ounces of cheese.  Arrange chicken mixture, vegetable mixture over cheese leaving a border.  Top with remaining cheese and pepper flakes.   Bake at 450 for 20 minutes or until golden brown. Sprinkle with herbs now ( could include basil). Cut into slices.
