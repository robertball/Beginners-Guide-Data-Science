Title:
Catalan Style Baked Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
600g Potatoes - cut into 1/8" slices
1 clove Garlic  finely chopped
1 medium Onion - finely sliced
60ml/4tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
150g Tomatoes - sliced in very thin wedges
1kg Rosada /Turbot/Halibut/Tilapia/Cod steaks (or other firm flesh white fish)  2.5cm thick
2tsp fresh Thyme - leaves only (or ½tsp dried thyme)
1½tsp fresh rosemary - finely chopped (or ¼tsp dried rosemary)
2tsp fresh Oregano - finely chopped (or ½tsp dried Oregano)
2tbsp fresh Parsley - finely chopped
1 lemon  Juice only - to taste
150ml dry White Wine
1 or 2tbsp Dried Bread Crumbs
Sea Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper - to taste

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 180c/350f/gas 4
Arrange the potatoes and onion in layers in a greased baking pan, sprinkling each layer with oil (a total of 3tbsp),  garlic, salt, and pepper;
Scatter the tomato wedges over the potatoes, cover tightly with foil, and bake for some 45 minutes or so, or until the potatoes are almost tender;
Meanwhile, sprinkle the fish steaks on both sides with a little salt and let sit;
Place the fish over the potatoes, brush the fish with about 1tbsp oil, sprinkle thyme, rosemary, oregano, parsley, and lemon juice over the fish and potatoes;
Pour in the wine;
Dust the fish with bread crumbs and cook until the fish is just done, about 15 minutes, basting several times.
