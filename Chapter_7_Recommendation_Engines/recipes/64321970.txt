Title:
Oatmeal with Ricotta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup(s) oats, old-fashioned
2 dash(es) cinnamon, ground
2 teaspoon milk, fat-free
1/2 cup(s) cheese, ricotta, part-skim (may substitute 1% or fat-free cottage cheese)
sugar substitute, to taste

Directions:
Cook oatmeal according to package direction, adding cinnamon and milk. Cook until done. Pour into bowl. Add ricotta and sugar substitute to taste.
