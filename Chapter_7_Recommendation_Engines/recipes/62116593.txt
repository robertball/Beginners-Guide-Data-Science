Title:
Pumpkin Bread old recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 Cups Flour
3 Cups Sugar
2 tsp Baking Soda
1 1/2 tsp Salt
1 tsp Cinnamon
1 tsp Nutmeg
1/2 tsp Cloves
1/2 tsp Ginger
1 cup Canola Oil
2 cup puree pumpkin
4 Eggs
2/3 cup Water
Nuts optional...

Directions:
Mix pumkin, oil, water and eggs in bowl
Add sugar, baking soda, salt, cinn, nutmeg, cloves, and ginger with pumpkin mixture
Add flour..
Using Pam or coating for loaf tins pour mixture into loaf pans 3/4 full...
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
Cool 15 mins..
Enjoy...
