Title:
Mom's Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked macaroni
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 cup milk
4 oz Velveeta, cut into 1/2-inch cubes

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to package directions.
Cook and stir butter, salt and pepper until butter is melted. Blend in flour. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until mixture is smooth and bubbly. Remove from heat. Stir in milk. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil and stir 1 minute. Remove from heat and stir in cheese.
Place macaroni in an ungreased 1-quart baking dish. Pour cheese sauce over macaroni and stir to combine. Bake uncovered at 375 for 20 minutes.
