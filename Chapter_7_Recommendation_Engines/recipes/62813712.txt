Title:
Delicious Candy Apple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/701120.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups Heavy Whipping Cream
1 Packet of Whipped Cream Stabilizer
4 Tablespoons Powdered Sugar
2 Teaspoons Pure Vanilla
8 Fun Size Milky Way Candy Bars
2 Large Green Apples, chopped
1/2 Cup Caramel Sauce

Directions:
1.) Make whipped cream by whipping heavy cream, stabilizer, powdered sugar, and vanilla together until it forms stiff peaks. Set aside.
2.) Chop Milky Way Candy Bars and green apples.
3.) Pour half of the whipped cream into a serving dish. Add half of the chopped apples and Milky Way on top of the whipped cream without mixing it together. Repeat this step with the remaining whipped cream, apples, and Milky way one more time.
4.) Drizzles caramel sauce over the top. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
House of Aqua
Description: 
I created this recipe one day on a whim and it reminds me of a candy apple. Great for a light sweet snack or to take to a potluck. Drizzle caramel sauce across each serving before eating.
