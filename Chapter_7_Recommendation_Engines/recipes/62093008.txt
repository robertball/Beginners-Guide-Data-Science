Title:
Easy Cranberry-Orange Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 cup + 2 tbsp sugar
2 tbsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1 cup sweetened dried cranberries
1/4 cup butter, melted
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 1/4 cup orange juice, with some pulp
2 tsp pure orange extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Grease muffin tin or line with paper cups.
In a large bowl, combine flours, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
Stir in the cranberries to coat with flour mixture.
In a separate small bowl, melt the butter, then add the egg, orange juice, and pure orange extract.  Add this to the flour mixture.
Stir juice mixture into flour mixture until thoroughly moistened.  Batter will be thick and lumpy.
Spoon batter into muffin cups, about 3/4 full.
Sprinkle muffin tops with extra sugar.
Bake at 400 degress for 20-22 minutes.

Description: 
These muffins are fragrant, moist, and the perfect combination of sweet and tart.  (Also a source of whole grain!)
