Title:
Buffalo Chicken Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1039109.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (32oz) bag frozen shredded hashbrowns
2 cans Cream of Chicken soup
2 (12oz) containers of Buffalo Dip
4 cups chopped cooked chicken
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
2 cups crushed Fritos

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Combine hashbrowns, chicken soup, buffalo dip, chicken, and cheese.
Spread into a lightly greased 9x13-inch pan.
Top with crushed Fritos.
Bake for 30 minutes.
