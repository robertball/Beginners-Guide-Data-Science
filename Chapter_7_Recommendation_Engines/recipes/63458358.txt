Title:
Raw Raspberry Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups dried Raspberries (Just Tomoato's brand)
1/2 cup agave
1/4-1/2 cup water

Directions:
Combine ingredients in high speed blender.
Blend until desired consistency.
Transfer to a jar, refrigerate.
