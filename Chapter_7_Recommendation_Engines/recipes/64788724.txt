Title:
Garlic Parmesan Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tbsp salted butter, divided
2 tbsp olive oil
2 cup Jasmine rice, rinsed and drained
2 tsp garlic salt
2 tsp dried parsley
1 1/4 cups water
1 1/2 cup milk, divided
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
salt and pepper

Directions:
Add 2 tbsp butter and olive oil to a pan over medium heat. Add rice, garlic salt and parsley and toast rice until just lightly browned.
Add in water and 1 cup milk. Manuel set for 20 minutes. Natural release.
Stir in the remaining 2 tbsp butter, 1/2 cup milk and parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Christine Denstaedt
Description: 
This quick and easy Garlic Parmesan Rice is a flavorful side dish recipe that pairs perfectly with anything!
