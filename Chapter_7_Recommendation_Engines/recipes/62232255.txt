Title:
Popper Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese
1/2 c. mayo
1/2 c. sour cream
4 oz. diced green chiles
3 fresh jalapenos or serranos, seeded and chopped
1/2 c. grated parmesan
1 c. shredded cheddar

Directions:
Mix all but 1/2 c. cheddar.
Put in a baking dish.
Sprinkle 1/2 c. cheddar on top.
Bake 375 25 min.
Excellent with Triscuits!!
