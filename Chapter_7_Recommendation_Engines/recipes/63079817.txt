Title:
Fudpuckers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. peanut butter cookie dough - cut in half
40 Reese's peanut butter miniature cups
Chopped peanuts or Heath Bars

Directions:
Cut each half of cookie dough into 5 slices
Cut each slice into quarters or 4 pieces
Place each piece in mini-muffin pan and bake according to directions on cookie dough pkg.
Remove from oven and push a peanut butter cup in center of each cookie and sprinkle top with nuts.
Let cookies cool 1-2 min. in pan, then remove to foil to finish cooling
