Title:
Light Curried Chicken & Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 whole boneless chicken breasts, cooked (can be whole, strips or cut into chunks)
1 large bag frozen broccoli (spears or florets)
1 can cream of chicken soup
2/3 c. mayo
1/3 c. evaporated milk
1/2 grated colby cheese
1 t. lemon juice
1/2 tsp. curry powder
2 c. bread crumbs
1 stick butter melted.

Directions:
Cook chicken however you like.  Place broccoli in 9X13 casserole dish (or larger) and top with chicken.  Combine soup with mayo, milk, cheese, lemon juice and curry.  Pour over chicken.  Mix melted butter with butter bread crumbs and sprinkle on top of casserole.  Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.
This dish may be frozen before baking.  To serve, return to room temperature and bake.

Submitter: 
jmacsaunders
Description: 
I do not like curry in general. However I grew up eating this because it was light on the curry- to enhance not overpower.  Great to top onto egg noodles or rice. Family favorite!
