Title:
Luscious Lemon Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups skim milk
(2) 3.4oz each packages lemon instant pudding
8oz tub of whipped cream, thawed, divided
1- 9in prepared angel food cake, diced in 1in pieces
2 cups fresh blueberries
1 cup toffee bits

Directions:
1. Pour milk into a large bowl. Add pudding mixes. Beat until well blended.
2. Gently stir in 1 cup whipped cream.
3. Place half the angel food cake cubes into a 2-quart clear bowl. Top with half each of the pudding mixture, blueberries and toffee bits.
4. Repeat layers. Top with remaining whipped cream.
5. Refrigerate at least 1 hour or until ready to serve.
