Title:
Chicken Cordon Blue Mac & Cheese Recipe
Image:
NA
Ingredients:
6  fully cooked Southern-style chicken breast tenderloins, heated according to package directions
3 slices HO<sup>&reg;</sup>MEL(<sup>&reg;</sup>) NATU<sup>&reg;</sup>AL CHOICE(<sup>&reg;</sup>) Honey Deli Ham. cut in half
1 (20 ounce) package HO<sup>&reg;</sup>MEL(<sup>&reg;</sup>) Side Dishes Elbow Macaroni & Cheese
1 cup shredded Gouda cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees F. Grease 11x8-inch baking dish.
In baking dish, arrange chicken in single layer. Arrange ham over chicken. Spoon macaroni and cheese over ham. Sprinkle with shredded cheese.
Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until cheese is melted. Serve with salad, if desired.

Submitter: 
HORMEL NATURAL CHOICE
Description: 
Prepared chicken breast tenderloins are topped with ham, macaroni and cheese, and shredded Gouda cheese then baked for this quick, cheesy family weeknight dinner.
