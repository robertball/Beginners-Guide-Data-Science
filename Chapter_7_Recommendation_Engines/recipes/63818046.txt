Title:
Homemade Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion
4 cloves onion
1/4 red wine
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp italian seasoning or basil
28 0z crushed tomatoes
kalamata olives optional
3 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
chop onion and saute in olive oil until slightly browned. add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
Add wine and cook down until reduced. add salt, pepper, season and tomotoes and cook until thick.. olives are optional add with seasonings..
