Title:
Chili Cheese Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground Beef
1 c. Chopped Onion
1/4 c. Chopped Green Pepper
8 oz. Tomato Sauce
1 can petite diced tomatoes drained
1 tablespoon Chili Powder
1 tsp. Garlic Powder
1/4 tsp. onion powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 tsp. sugar
1 canned of large flaky layered Biscuits
2 c. Cheddar Cheese shredded

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
2. Brown beef with onion and pepper. Drain.
Add tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper and sugar.. Quarter biscuits and sprinkle 1/2 on bottom of greased 13x9 inch baking pan. Top with beef mixture. Add remaining biscuits on top.
3.  Bake for 20 minutes.
4.  Remove from oven and sprinkle with cheddar cheese. Bake an additional 5 - 10 minutes until cheese is melted and begins to brown around edges.

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
This delicious and flavorful casserole is comfort food perfection.  The seasoned ground beef is topped with flaky canned biscuits and a blanket of cheese.
