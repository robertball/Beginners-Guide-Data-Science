Title:
Buffalo Wings - That's a Wrap! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meat from one rotisserie chicken, shredded
1 cup finely diced celery
Texas PeteÂ® hot sauce
8 (10") flour tortillas
Bleu cheese dressing for serving

Directions:
Mix together the meat, hot sauce to taste, and the diced celery.
Spread a decent amount of the mixture down the center of a tortilla.  Roll up and wrap tightly in waxed paper. Secure with toothpick.
Repeat until all tortillas are filled.
Refrigerate overnight.
Remove toothpick, but leaved wrapped.
To serve, cut each roll in half and place on platter with a bowl of bleu cheese dressing in the center.
