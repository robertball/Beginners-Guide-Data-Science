Title:
Easter Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg yeast
1/4 c. warm water
1 3oz pkg of instant lemon pudding mix
1/4 c. margarine
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 c. scalded milk
4-4 1/2 c. flour
4 eggs
1 tbsp water
multicolored sprinkles (optional)

Directions:
Dissolve yeast in warm water.  Mix pudding mix, margarine, and salt in a bowl: pour milk over pudding mixture.  Mix until margarine is melted and pudding is dissolved.  cool mixture to lukewarm.  Add 1 1/2 cups of flour to pudding mixture, mix well.  Beat in yeast and 3 eggs with an electric mixer.  Add enough remaining flour to form a soft dough.  Turn dough onto a flour surface and allow to rest for 10 min.  Knead for 10 min by hand.  place in a greased bowl, cover and allow to rise for 1 hour.  Divide dough into 6 equal parts; shape each part into a rope.  Make 2 braids, using 3 ropes each.  Bring the ends of each finished braid together to form a circle and place each braid in a 9 inch round cake pan. Allow to rise for 1 hour. Heat oven to 375. Beat the remaining egg and water together and brush the top of each braid.  Shake on sprinkles if you wish.  Bake in preheated oven for 25 min or until golden.

Submitter: 
Christine Douglas- Kaiser
Description: 
I'm not sure where I got this recipe, I've been carrying it around for about 20 years.  It is a sweet braided bread with a surprising lemon twist.  My family asks for it every year.
