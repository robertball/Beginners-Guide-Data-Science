Title:
Angela's Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Leftover chicken filling from the enchilada recipe mentioned above. You may need to adjust the base to your preference.
1 (10 oz)can sweet corn drained
1 (14 oz)can black beans rinsed and drained
1 (14 oz)can chicken broth
1 (16 oz)can mild chunky salsa
1 t. lemon juice or lime juice
1 t. cumin
1 t. garlic

Directions:
Put everthing in a pot simmer for 30 minutes.
Serve with cheese, sour cream and tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
4GiddyGirls
Description: 
Using leftover chicken filling (from Angela's Awesome Enchiladas-found on this sight and fabulous btw!) I created this simple but amazing soup by adding a few canned goods. Simple, easy, filling and delicious.
