Title:
Quinoa Crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Quinoa Flower
1 cup Almond milk
2 large eggs
dash of sea salt
teaspoon of honey
2 tablespoons of melted butter

Directions:
Mix 1 cup Quinoa Flower, 1 cup Almond milk, 2 large eggs, dash of sea salt and teaspoon of honey in a large bowl. Add 2 tablespoons of melted butter right before ready to put on griddle. Spread on low to medium griddle like an omlette and cook for 2 minutes; flip and cook other side for 30 seconds.
