Title:
Chicken & Sausage Casserole - Low Carb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/951099.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb pork sausage
1/4 cup chopped onions
12 oz of sliced mushrooms drained (optional)
4 cups diced chicken
16 oz frozen cauliflower - cooked and drained
8 oz cheddar cheese shredded
8 oz cream cheese softened
1 stalk celery (optional)

Directions:
Brown the sausage with onion and mushrooms
Stir in softened cream cheese and blend well
Add coarsely chopped cauliflower
Add chicken and cheddar cheese minus 1/2 cup
Place in greased 9 x 13 baking dish and top with 1/2 cup cheddar
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes covered with foil
Uncover and bake additional 20 minutes or until top is slightly browned
