Title:
Big Ray's Raspberry Barbecue Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1129655.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup barbecue sauce
2/3 cup seedless raspberry jam
3 Tbl finely chopped onion
1 to 2 jalapeno peppers, seeded and finely chopped
2 tsp minced garlic, divided
2 tsp liquid smoke, divided
1/4 tsp salt
3 lbs chicken, cut up
1 small onion, sliced
1 cup water

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine barbecue sauce, jam, chopped onion, peppers, 1 teaspoon garlic, 1 teaspoon liquid smoke if desired and salt; mix well. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours to overnight.
Preheat oven to 350Â° (or heat grill to medium). Place chicken in a greased 15x10x1-in. baking pan. Top with sliced onion and remaining garlic. Combine water and remaining liquid smoke; pour over chicken. Cover and bake at 30 minutes or until juices run clear.
Transfer chicken to a greased foil lined broiler pan (or grill); brush with sauce. Broil 4-6 in. from heat 20-25 minutes, turning and basting every 5 minutes or until chicken is well coated.
