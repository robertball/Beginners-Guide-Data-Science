Title:
Shrimp and Scallop Scampi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. scallops
½ shrimp
2 Tbsp. butter melted
4 Tbsp. olive oil
6 cloves garlic finely chopped
1 Tbsp. fresh parsley finely chopped
½ c. white wine
½ c. chicken stock
1 tsp. salt
Pepper
½  lb. angel hair pasta
Corn starch
Big Green Egg Maple Season (or favorite all seasoning)
3 pieces of green onion finely chopped
½ of a lemon

Directions:
1.	Boil pasta.
2.	Mix butter, 5 cloves garlic, 2 Tbsp olive oil, salt, pepper, parsley set a side.
3.	Season  scallops with favorite all seasoning.  Pour 2 Tbsp. olive oil in pan add 1 cloved finely chopped garlic.  Sear scallops for about 3-4 minutes on each side.  Remove from pan.
4.	Over medium heat, ad d butter mixture to pan. Cook garlic, but make sure it does not brown.  Add shrimp. Once shrimp is cooked , add scallops.  Squeeze lemon over shrimp and scallops. Pour in wine and chicken stock.  Added green onion. Reduce heat to simmer.
5.	Thicken sauce with cornstarch.
6.	Drain pasta and added to shrimp and scallop mixture. Serve.

Submitter: 
Randi Turban
Description: 
This is a delicious, easy meal to make that tastes like it came from your favorite restaurant.
