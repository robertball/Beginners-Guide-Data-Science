Title:
Spicy  Orange  Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986660.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon Lawry's速 Seasoned Salt
1 teaspoon Lawry's速 Seasoned Pepper
2 pounds chicken wings, tips removed and wings cut in half at joint
2 tablespoons Shedd's Spread Country Crock速 Spread
3/4 cup orange marmalade
Hot pepper sauce, to taste

Directions:
In large resealable plastic bag, combine Lawry's(R) Seasoned Salt and Lawry's(R) Seasoned Pepper; add chicken. Close bag and toss to coat.
In 12-inch nonstick skillet, heat Spread over medium heat and cook chicken, turning occasionally, 25 minutes or until thoroughly cooked. Remove chicken to platter and keep warm. Drain skillet, if desired.
In same skillet, bring marmalade and hot pepper sauce to a boil over high heat, scraping up brown bits from bottom of pan. Return chicken to skillet. Reduce heat to low and simmer, tossing to coat with glaze, 5 minutes or until heated through. Garnish, if desired, with orange slices.
