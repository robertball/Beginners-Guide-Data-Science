Title:
Super lemon garlic dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
11 oz lemon juice
3 oz canola oil
1 oz olive oil
1 teaspoon dijon mustard
3 cloves garlic
3 tablespoons chopped cilantro

Directions:
Mix it all together and let it sit in the fridge a few hours or overnight (better).
Shake hard before pouring.

Submitter: 
Ruby
Description: 
Adapted from the salad dressing my mother made when I was a kid. I increased the lemon juice ratio to to reduce the fat and calories.

PRO TIP: Store in a bottle with a narrow opening (I recycle the lemon juice) so that you shake it when dispensing.
