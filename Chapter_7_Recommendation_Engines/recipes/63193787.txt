Title:
Leftover Chicken Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/823843.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise/miracle whip
2 tablespoons honey mustard
1 clove garlic, chopped or 1 tsp. garlic powder
2 cups cooked chicken , shredded
1 cup Italian seasoned breadcrumbs
1/4 cup mayonnaise/miracle whip
1 egg , lightly beaten
1/4 cup prepared basil pesto
2 teaspoons honey mustard
Oil for frying

Directions:
Whisk together 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 2 tbsp mustard and garlic, cover and refrigerate.
In a large bowl, mix together chicken, 1/2 cup bread crumbs, mayo, egg, pesto, 2 tsp mustard.
Make patties the size you want and coat lightly with remaining bread crumbs.
Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium high heat.
Cook cakes until golden brown on both sides and drain on paper towels.
