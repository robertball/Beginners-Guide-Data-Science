Title:
Creamy Seafood Hot Pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2105750.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup orzo pasta, uncooked
1/2 teaspoon Penzey's Herbes De Provence
1/2 teaspoon Penzey's Florida Seasoned Pepper
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon parsley
1 teaspoon garlic, minced
3/4 cup onion, diced
3/4 cup portabella mushroom, diced
3/4 cup shredded carrots
4 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup romano OR parmesan cheese
2 cups milk
1 cup frozen patagonian scallops
1 1/2 cups frozen salad shrimp, peeled and deveined

Directions:
Combine everything in a medium large saucepan.
Cover and bring to a low boil.
Stir and reduce heat to low.
Recover pot and cook for 15 minutes.
Stir after 15 minutes and serve or, for a thicker hot pot, continue cooking with no lid for 2 to 3 minutes until thickened slightly.
Sprinkle with sea salt before serving.

Submitter: 
Carolyn Chieffe Perrine
Description: 
This is hands down the easiest dish you can make during a hectic week.  Just throw everything in a pot, uncooked and frozen solid, and come back in 15 minutes to a delicious dish.  Add a crusty baguette and salad for a complete meal.
