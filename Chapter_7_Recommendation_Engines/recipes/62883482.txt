Title:
Kathy's Blueberry-Lemon Jello Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 21 oz. can of blueberry pie filling
1 box, yellow cake mix
2 T. lemon juice
The zest of one lemon
1 C. boiling water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Spray a 9 x 13" pan with non-stick spray then lightly flour the pan.
Mix the cake as directed on the package.
Put pie filling in the bottom of the prepared pan.
Carefully pour cake mix over the blueberries.
Dissolve Jello in 1 Cup boiling water, then add lemon juice and lemon zest.
Pour the Jello mixture gently and evenly over the cake batter.
Bake at 375 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes.
