Title:
Tuna Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978295.jpg
Ingredients:
Quaker® Salt Free or Lightly Salted Rice Cake
1 (6 ounce) can tuna in brine or water
1/2 onion cut very fine
1/2 tomato cut into cubes
3 cups English cucumber
1/2 green crispy apple cut into cubes
1 pinch coarsely ground peppercorns
1/2 green chili cut finely
low fat mayonnaise

Directions:
Add all the above ingredients together and mix together with a little low fat mayonnaise.
Use as a filling for your rice cakes.

Submitter: 
Quaker® Rice Snacks
Description: 
Canned tuna mixed with crispy apple, cucumber, and tomato makes a delightful tuna salad and a perfect topping for crunchy rice cakes.
