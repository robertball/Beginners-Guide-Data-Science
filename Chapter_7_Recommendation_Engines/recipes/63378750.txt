Title:
Grilled Ham and Cheese Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pretzel hamburger buns
1 lb. deli ham
Sliced colby jack cheese
Dijon mustard
Mayonnaise
Tin foil

Directions:
Turn grill to medium heat.
Slice pretzel buns in half. Spread mayonnaise on bottom half of bun, then spread mustard on the top half of the bun.
Place 3 slices of ham and a slice of cheese on bottom half of bun and place top half on top. Repeat for all 4 buns.
Wrap each sandwich in tin foil and place directly on grill.
Cook for about 10 minutes, flipping sandwiches halfway through.
