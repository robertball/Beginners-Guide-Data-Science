Title:
Grandma's Date Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup dates
7 prunes
1/2 cup raisins
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 cup unsalted butter
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 egg
1 3/4 cup flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans

Directions:
Preheat over to 350 degrees F.
Chop dates and prunes and place in a large bowl.
Pour 1 1/2 cup boiling water over raisins. Let them soak for aout 20 minutes. Drain water, bring to a boil, discard raisins.
Add baking soda to 3/4 cup boiling water.  Pour over fruit.
Add butter, stir until melted.  Stir in sugar and vanilla.
When cool add 1 well beaten egg.
Mix flour and salt, add to fruit mixture. Stir to blend.
Add walnuts or pecans
Pour into a loaf pan which has been greased with butter.  Bake 50 - 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Suzanne
Description: 
This is the recipe for date-nut bread that my grandmother always made around Christmas time. Definitely a family favorite!
