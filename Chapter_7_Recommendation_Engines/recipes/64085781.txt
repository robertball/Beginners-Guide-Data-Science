Title:
Banana Banana Bread ll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Per Loaf:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup butter
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 eggs, beaten
2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas
cinnamon,use all baking powder not soda (Ecuador doesn't sell soda), and for topping/bottom per loaf, 2 tbsp butter, cin., brown sugar mix -- pour in after greasing pans.  Make two loaves.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (150 degrees C). Lightly grease 2 9x5 inch loaf pans or bundt pan
In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
