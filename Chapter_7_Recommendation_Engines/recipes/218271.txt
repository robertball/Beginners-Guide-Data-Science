Title:
Wacky Mac®, Pork and Tomato Salad with Chile Vinaigrette Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991563.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 pound) pork tenderloin, cooked and cut into bite-size pieces
2 medium tomatoes, cored and diced
1 large scallion, trimmed and chopped
1/4 cup sliced pimiento-stuffed olives
1 large celery rib, trimmed and chopped
1 tablespoon minced fresh basil
2 cups cooked Wacky Mac® (prefer Veggie Spirals), rinsed under cold water; drained well
2 tablespoons olive oil, plus
1 teaspoon olive oil
4 teaspoons white wine vinegar
1/4 teaspoon crushed dried oregano
1 serrano chile, cored, seeded and minced
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Combine pork, tomatoes, scallion, olives, celery and basil in large salad bowl. Add cooked Wacky Mac; toss gently but well.
Stir together olive oil, vinegar, oregano, chile, salt and pepper in small bowl. Pour over salad just before serving. Toss well.

Submitter: 
Wacky Mac®
Description: 
Pork, tomatoes and olives mingle with Wacky Mac and Chile Vinaigrette for a truly unique salad, perfect for any summer BBQ.
