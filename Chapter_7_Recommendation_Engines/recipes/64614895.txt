Title:
Georgia Girl Brunswick Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 stick butter
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 large sweet onion, finely chopped
1 28 ounce can fire roasted tomatoes (with their juices)
4 Tbs tomato paste
1 Tbs liquid smoke
1 32 oz box chicken stock
1 cup Carolina barbecue sauce
2 Tbs Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1 pound smoked pulled pork, shredded
1 rotisserie chicken, shredded
4 golden potatoes, chopped fine
1 bag frozen corn kernels
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1/2 cup white wine vinegar
Hot sauce, for serving

Directions:
Melt the butter in a large Dutch oven over medium-high heat, add the garlic and onions, saute until soft, about 4 minutes.
Stir in the tomatoes, tomato paste, liquid smoke, chicken stock, barbecue sauce, Worcestershire, cayenne, pork, chicken, potatoes, corn, salt, pepper, paprika, mustard, and vinegar.
Bring the mixture to a boil, reduce to a simmer and cook over medium-low until thick, about 1/2 hour, stirring occasionally.  Transfer to slow cooker, simmer on high at least an hour.
