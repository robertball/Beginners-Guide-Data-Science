Title:
Dad's Crab & Broccoli Bisque Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/929478.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup leeks, finely chopped
1/2 cup onions, finely chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 cup mushrooms, finely chopped
1 cup broccoli, finely chopped
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 tsp dried thyme
1/8 tsp salt
1/8 tsp black pepper
1 small bay leaf
3 cups chicken broth
1 cup light cream
3/4 cup shredded Jarlsberg or Swiss cheese (3 ounces)
1 6-ounce package shredded crab meat

Directions:
In a large saucepan over medium high heat, saut√© leeks, onions, and garlic in butter.  After they are cooked halfway through, add broccoli and mushrooms.  Continue cooking until all ingredients are cooked through and onions are soft and clear (approximately 5-10 minutes total).
Blend in flour, thyme, salt, pepper, and bay leaf.  Add chicken broth and cream.  Cook and stir until thick and bubbly.  Cook and stir 1 minute more.
Add cheese.  Stir until cheese is melted.  Add crabmeat and heat through.
Garnish with sprinkle of thyme and cheese.  Serve with warm crusty bread.
