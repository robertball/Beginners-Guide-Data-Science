Title:
Artichoke & Spinach Dip Restaurant Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cloves garlic
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed, drained and excess liquid squeezed out
1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1 (10 ounce) container Alfredo-style pasta sauce
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
3 Tbs. milk

Directions:
Cream cream cheese. Add alfredo sauce & roasted garlic then blend well.  Stir in spinach, artichoke hearts, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese & milk; mix well.
Cover and bake in the preheated oven 30 minutes, or until cheeses are melted and bubbly. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Virginia Mom
Description: 
Alfredo sauce brings a rich, creamy twist to an old favorite. Serve this hot dip with bread sticks, chips or crackers.Modified from: Artichoke & Spinach Dip Restaurant Style
