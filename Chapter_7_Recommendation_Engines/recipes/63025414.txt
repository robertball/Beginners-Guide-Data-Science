Title:
Mom's Cornbread Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cornmeal
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
5 eggs, beaten
1 (12 fl oz) can evaporated milk
1/4 cup vegetable oil	2 tablespoons butter
1/2 cup chopped celery
1 small onion
1 cup chopped mushrooms
2 cups chicken stock
2 tablespoons dried sage
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Directions
1 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish
2 In a 9 inch iron skillet, mix cornmeal, white sugar, baking powder, salt, 2 eggs, evaporated milk and vegetable oil.
3 Bake cornmeal mixture in the preheated oven 35 to 40 minutes, or until lightly browned. Allow to cool.
4 Prepare the dry corn bread mix according to package directions. Cool and crumble.
5 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease one 9x13 inch baking dish.
6 In a large skillet over medium heat, melt the butter and saute the celery and onion until soft.
7 In a large bowl, combine the celery, onions, 3 cups crumbled corn bread, eggs, chicken stock, sage and salt and pepper to taste; mix well.
8 Place into prepared dish and bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes
