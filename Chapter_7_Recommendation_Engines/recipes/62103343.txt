Title:
Tyler's 4-cheese Vermicelli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 500g package vermicelli
¼ c Butter
¼ c Milk
½ tsp olive oil
½ lb Cheddar cheese (old)
1/4lb Provolone
1/4lb Jalepeno Havarti
¼ c Cream cheese
1/4c sour Cream
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp spicy peppers or cajun spice mix
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1tsp ground white pepper
salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees
Bring large pot of salted water to a boil
boil pasta at high heat for 8 minutes or until adente
drain.
Return to pot and stir in olive oil to coat pasta and prevent sticking
Set heat of element to low
add ¾ of the cheese and all of the seasonings, stir until cheese is melted, adding milk/butter(recommend premelting butter in microwave) while stirring to prevent gooping.
Add sour cream and stir.
place in large oven safe glass dish
add remaining cheese to the top of the pasta
bake at 375 10-15 minutes until all chese is melted and bubbling
dab with paper towel to remove excess grease, serve.

Description: 
A wonderful blend of cheese cheese cheese and fast cooking vermicelli. With less than 5 minutes of prep, and 30 until done, its super simple.
