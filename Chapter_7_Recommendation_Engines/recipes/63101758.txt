Title:
AppleOne Crisp Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/786615.jpg
Ingredients:
10 Apples, peeled & cored
1 cup white sugar
1 tablespoon of pulverized Fiber One Original Cereal
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1/8 teaspoon salt
TOPPING
2 Cups of Fiber One Original Cereal
1/2 Cup All Purpose Flour
1 Cup Packed Light Brown Sugar
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 cup butter, cold and cubed into small pieces
Optional:
Pecans or Walnuts and Raisins, added to the topping mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
In a food processor,  pulverized 1/4 cup of Fiber One Original Cereal to make 1 Tablespoon
In a large mixing bowl, combine apples with sugar, nutmeg, cinnamon, salt and the pulverized Fiber One Original cereal. Mix thoroughly, until all the apples are coated.
Turn them out into a 9x12 baking dish
Make the Topping:
In a separate bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and 2 cups of FiberOne Original Cereal. Add small cubes of butter and blend with a pastry fork.
Sprinkle this generously over the top of the apples, adding more small cubes of butter over the top.
Bake for 40-45 minutes at 350

Submitter: 
Kitchenista Jane
Description: 
An interesting alternative to a time-honored apple crisp using a crunchy topping with Fiber One Original Cereal.
