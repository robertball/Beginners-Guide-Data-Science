Title:
Bucket of Blood Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990112.jpg
Ingredients:
Blood:
1 (26 ounce) can condensed tomato soup
1 (14 ounce) can vegetable broth
1 1/4 cups water
1/2 teaspoon red food coloring
4 drops blue food coloring
Body Parts:
2 cups cooked orechiette pasta (ears), or medium shells (noses), or ziti (intestine)
1 cup cooked canned peas or pearl onions (eyes)
1 cup canned white corn (teeth)

Directions:
Mix the soup, broth, water and food coloring in a large saucepan or small Dutch oven. Simmer and add the body parts; heat through. Serve in a new and cleaned childs' beach bucket (at least 1/2 gallon capacity), or from the pot.

Submitter: 
Mealtime.org
Description: 
This spooky and nutritious Halloween recipe is easy to prepare with the help of canned soup, a variety of pastas and canned peas.
