Title:
Dorothy's Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds beef chuck, trimmed of visible fat and cut into 1 1/2-inch cubes
1/3 cup tomato paste
1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
2 tablespoons Worchestershire sauce
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
Coarse salt and ground pepper
1 large onion, cut into 1-inch chunks
1 pound small white or red new potatoes, well scrubbed, and cut into chunks
1 pound carrots, cut into 1 1/2-inch lengths
6 garlic cloves, smashed
2 bay leaves
1 quart beef stock

Directions:
Place beef in a 5-quart slow cooker. Distribute tomato paste, vinegar, and flour over beef; season generously with salt and pepper. Add onions, potatoes, carrots, garlic, and bay leaves. Cover contents of crockpot with beef stock; cook on high until beef is fork-tender, about 5 hours (or cook on low heat 8 hours).
