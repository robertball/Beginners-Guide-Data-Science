Title:
Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground beef
1/4 cup onion
2 garlic cloves, minced (or 1/2 tsp garlic powder)
1/2 cup catsup
1/2 Tbsp red wine vinegar
1/2 Tbsp brown sugar
1/2 Tbsp lemon juice
1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1/8 tsp chili powder

Directions:
Brown ground beef, onion and garlic.  Add remaining ingredients and simmer for 10 minutes or more.  Serve on hamburger buns.
