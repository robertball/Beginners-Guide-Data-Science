Title:
Tim O'Toole's Famous Stuffed Quahogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Quahogs
1 tall canister of chicken flavored Stove Top Stuffing
1 large onion
1 lb of Chorico
2 ounces Butter

Directions:
Boil the Chorico for 10 minutes
Keep the broth in the pot
Boil the Quahogs in the Chorico broth for about 10 minutes until they open
Save the chorico/Quahog broth to make the stuffing.
Remove the cooked Quahogs and save the shells. Separate them into halves and wash them if necessary.
Food process the cooked chorico and the cooked quahogs
Food process the raw onion
Make the full container of stuffing according to the recipe on the container but substitute the chorico/clam broth for the water
Mix the stuffing, chorico, onion, and Quahogs together
Fill the shells with stuffing mixture
Put small pat of butter on top of each stuffed Quahog
Place in baking pans.
Bake for about 15-20 minutes at 350 degrees until toasty brown on top.
Or wrap in foil before baking and refrigerate or freeze for later. Heat them in foil for soft mushy stuffies of without foil for a crisper top.

Description: 
Classic Modern Stuffed Quohog Recipe, Our grandmothers used stale bread and lots of seasoning,  my grandmother would have appreciated the stove top stuffing adaption.
