Title:
Jalapeno Popper Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 cup mayonnaise
6 jalapeno peppers
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 c panko bread crumbs

Directions:
Stir together cream cheese and mayonnaise in a large bowl until smooth. Stir in green chiles and jalapeno peppers. Pour mixture into a microwave safe serving dish, and sprinkle with Parmesan mixed with panko.
bake 375* for 30 min
serve with butter crakers or toasted french bread.

Submitter: 
Zhora Autumn
Description: 
This will definitely be the biggest hit at the party!  This is such an easy appetizer to make and it tastes just like the jalapeno poppers you wish you could make yourself!  The hardest part of this recipe is not eating it all by yourself! Serve with sliced sourdough baguettes, tortilla chips or crackers.Modified from: Jalapeno Popper Spread
