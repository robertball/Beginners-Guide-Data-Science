Title:
Matt's Favorite Cornbread Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup cornmeal
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 tablespoons baking powder
4 eggs
1/3 cup sugar
7 ounces creamed corn (1/2 can)
2 ounces diced green chilies, drained well (1/2 can)
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup shredded Monterrey jack and cheddar cheese blend

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400Â°F; grease 8" or 9" iron skillet
Mix all ingredients together.
Pour into skillet.
Bake 35 minutes; check for clean toothpick, check every 5 minutes until done.
Serve warm.
