Title:
Meat loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs hamburger
2 lightly beaten eggs
1 envelope onion soup mix
1/2 tsp poultry seasoning
1/3c ketchup
3/4c water
1/2c bread crumbs
Glaze:
3 tbsp brown sugar
1/4c ketchup
1/2tsp nutmeg
1tsp dry mustard

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.
Mix glaze ingredients in a small bowl and baste the top of the meatloaf.
Bake at 350 for one hour.
