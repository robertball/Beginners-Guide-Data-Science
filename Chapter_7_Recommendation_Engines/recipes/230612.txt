Title:
Spaghetti with Fennel and Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989391.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box Barilla PLUSÂ® Spaghetti
1 (24 ounce) jar Barilla Marinara - Traditional Sauce
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 clove garlic, sliced very thin
1 bulb fennel, trimmed, sliced thin (include some fronds)
1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 cup fresh basil, torn
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and fennel. saute 2 to 4 minutes or until fennel begins to soften.
Add shrimp and oregano to skillet. Raise heat slightly and saute for 3 to 5 additional minutes or until shrimp is cooked. Add marinara sauce, basil, salt and pepper.
Cook Plus(R) Spaghetti according to package directions. Drain. Toss pasta with sauce in skillet while cooking and additional 3 to 5 minutes.
Transfer hot Plus(R) Spaghetti to a large serving platter and serve immediately.
