Title:
Basic Chicken Fajita's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Bell Peppers cut in strips (any color will do)
1 Onion sliced
3 Cloves of garlic minced
1/4 cup Worcestershire Sauce
1 Lime
1 1/2 tbs Canola Oil
Salt and Pepper to taste
1 lbs Chicken cut in strips

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and marinade chicken in the mixture for 2 hours.  Transfer the marinade and contents to a saute pan.  Saute over medium high heat until chicken is fully cooked and the vegetables are tender.
