Title:
Zippy Mango Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 mango, peeled and diced small
1 red papper, diced
1 cucumber, peeled, seeded and diced
1 large tomato (I used Campari) diced small
1 red onion, diced
1 or 2 Jalapeno peppers (2 for very spicy) seeded, white vein removed and diced very small
1/2 cup Cilantro, chopped (no stems)
1 lemon, Juiced
1 or 2 limes, Juiced
1/2 tsp lemon zest or lime zest (your choice)

Directions:
Wash, dice or chop all ingredients. Toss together in a large mixing bowl and sprinkle with lemon and lime juice.  Add zest and let stand for 1 hour in refrigerator to chill.
Serve with Taco chips, Corn chips  or tortilla chips. Also can be served with grilled chicken and fish.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Spiced up with hint of Jalapeno pepper, this Zippy Salsa Mango is addictive. Everyone was asking for the recipe and for more salsa. Served with Frito or Doritos' as an appetizer or snack. Served with grilled chicken breast or fish makes a tasty topping.
