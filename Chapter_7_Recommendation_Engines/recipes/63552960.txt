Title:
Vegan Truffles - Almond Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/970709.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. raw almonds
5 Medjool dates, pitted
1 c. raw cacao powder
2 t. coconut oil
3 T. or to taste raw agave
2 t. chia seeds
2 t. flax
1/4 c. water
Pink Himalayan salt for garnish

Directions:
1. In a food processor, chop almonds into a flour.
2. Blend Medjool dates in food processor with almond flour until incorporated.
3. Melt coconut oil in a bowl then combine with almond-date flour and all other ingredients. Mix with a sturdy wooden spoon or spatula (dough will be very stiff).
4. Roll dough into Tablespoon-sized balls with your hands, coat in extra cacao powder then dip lightly in pink salt before placing on a parchment-lined cookie sheets.
5. Store in fridge or freezer until hardened before packing.
Keeps for 3 days
