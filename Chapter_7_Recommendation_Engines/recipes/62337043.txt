Title:
Strawberry Banana Oat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/388767.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon of cinnamon
1 teaspoon of baking soda
1 cup of flour
1 cup of oats
1 1/2 cups of mashed ripe banana
1/4 cup of milk
1/2 cup of walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven at 350 degrees
In a large bowl cream the butter with the sugars until light and fluffy.  Beat in the eggs.
In a separate bowl mix the flour, oats, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.  Add to the wet mixture alternating with banana and milk.
Stir in nuts and then pour into a loaf pan (9x5x3).
Bake for one hour then turn out and allow to cool completely.
