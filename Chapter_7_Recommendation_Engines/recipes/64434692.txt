Title:
Spiced Apple and Cranburry Compote Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 Cups Cranberry Juice Cocktail
1 Package (6 ounces) dried apples
1/2 cup (2 ounces) dried Cranberries
1/2 cup Rhine wine or apple juice
1/2 cup honey
2 cinnamon sticks, broken into halves

Directions:
Mix juice, apples, cranberries, wine, honey, and cinnamon stick halves in 2-3 quart slow cooker.  Cover and cook on low 4-5 hours or until liquid is absorbed and fruit is tender
Remove and discard cinnamon stick halves.  Ladle compote into bowls.  Serve warm, at room temperature or chilled with a scoop of frozen yogurt or ice cream, if desired.  Garnish with additional cinnamon sticks, if desired
