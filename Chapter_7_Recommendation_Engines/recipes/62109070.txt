Title:
2-Way Berry Berry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup of water (if needed)
1/2 cup of pineapple juice
1/2 can of pineapple tidbits
3/4 cup of fresh or frozen whole rasberries
1/2 cup of fresh or frozen whole strawberries
3 tablespoons of honey
*extra honey for garnish
10-15 ice cubes

Directions:
Combine, in blender, about 5-6 pieces of ice along with the pineapple, pineapple juice, and the honey. Blend well.
Add remaining ice. Blend well.
Add about 3/4 of the rasberries and half of the strawberries. Blend Well.
Add remaining strawberries. Blend Well.
Pour into cups and garnish with remaining rasberries and extra honey.
*If you want to take the chill factor even farther, spoon into a 9x13 cake pan and put in freezer. When completely frozen, use a spoon to scrape lenght wise creating a slush. Serve in cups and enjoy!!!

Submitter: 
Chocolate
Description: 
This smoothie is cool, refreshing, and easy for the whole family to make and enjoy. This is one for those hot summer nights!
