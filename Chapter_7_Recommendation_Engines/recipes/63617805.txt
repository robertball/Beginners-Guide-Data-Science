Title:
Tudie's Pumpkin Peanut Butter Dog Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
1/2 cup whole oats
2 eggs
1 cup canned pumpkin
3 tablespoons peanut butter
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 cup ground flax seed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Blend together the pumpkin, peanut butter, eggs, ground flax seed and cinnamon in a bowl. Stir in the whole wheat flour and oats. The dough should be dry and stiff. Roll the dough into a 1/2-inch-thick roll. Cut into 1/2-inch pieces.
Bake in preheated oven 20 minutes. Turn over and bake 20 minutes more.  Turn off oven and leave biscuits in until oven is cool and biscuits are dry and hard.
