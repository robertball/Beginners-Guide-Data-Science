Title:
Simmering Beer Hot Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds hot dogs cut into pieces
2 cups brown sugar
2 cups ketchup
1 12ounce can of beer

Directions:
put all ingredients in a large saucepan and gently mix over low heat and cover
simmer about 45 minutes

Submitter: 
LillerFamily
Description: 
This makes for a good cook-out side dish.
