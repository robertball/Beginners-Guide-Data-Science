Title:
Baked Bananna Jumble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ripe yellow banannas( not too many brown spots).
1 1/4 cups shredded coconut.
1 1/4 cups fresh chopped red cherries.
1 cup canned diced pineapple, drained.
1 cup butter, melted.
1 1/4 cups dark brown sugar.
1/8 cup dark rum, optional.*
1 cup mini marshmallows, optional*

Directions:
Place the following in a medium size baking pan/dish:
Peel the banannas, slice each one in half lengthwise, then slice those halves into 3-4 pieces.
Add the cherries, pineapples, coconut, brown sugar, and melted butter.
Mix very well.
Bake 20-25 minutes, or until fruits are soft.
*For an added treat the kids love, add the mini marshmallow directy after removing from oven. Mix well again to allow the marshmallows to melt in.
*For an adult treat, add the dark rum after removing from oven.
Serve warm.

Submitter: 
La AfroCubana
Description: 
This warm dessert recipe is a hit with my kids, and at adult parties when you add the optional dark rum. This is also a very quick recipe, as well as low cost.
