Title:
Frosty Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2oz Bacardi Razz
1/4oz Creme de Cacao
1/4oz Blue Curacao
2oz Half & Half
Ice Cubes

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker and shake until cold and frothy. Strain into  a chilled martini glass
