Title:
Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. lasagne noodles
1 jar spaghetti sauce
1 bag mozzarella cheese
2 lbs. ground beef
1 bag Italian cheese

Directions:
Cook ground beef.
Preheat oven to 300F.
Mix ground beef with spaghetti sauce. Put a thin layer of sauce on bottom of baking dish. Add a layer of pasta noodles. Add layer of sauce, cheese, then noodles. Repeat until dish is full ending with layer of cheese.
Bake for 45 minutes.
