Title:
Plum n' Blueberry Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/468590.jpg
Ingredients:
6-7 Black Plums - Sliced, skin and cores removed
1 - 15oz Can Blueberries in light syrup - drained (or 1 pint fresh)
1 Cup Flour
1 Cup Old Fashioned Oats
1 Cup Brown Sugar
1 Stick Butter melted
3/4 Teapsoon Cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat 350 degrees.  In 8 x 8 baking dish layer bottom with sliced plums. place drained/fresh blueberries on top of plums.
Combine flour, oats, brown sugar and cinnamon; mix together.  Slowly add butter, mixing through.  Place mixture on top over berries.
Cook in oven for 45-60 minutes until golden brown on top.  Cool and serve

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This is similar to apple crisp only with plums and blueberries.  There's a nice balance of bitter and sweet.  You may sub 1 pint fresh blueberries if you have them on hand.
