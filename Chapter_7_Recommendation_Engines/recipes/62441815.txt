Title:
Zucchini Creme Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/458273.jpg
Ingredients:
Crsut:
1 c flour
1/2 c sugar
1/2 c very soft butter
Filling:
3 c cooked zucchini (boiled and drained)
2 c evaporated milk
2 eggs
1 1/2 c sugar
4 T flour
1 T butter
2 t vanilla

Directions:
1) Pre-heat oven to 350 and grease 9 x 13 pan
2) Crust: mix together crust ingredients until moist and crumbly. Press into bottom of greased 9 x 13 pan.
3) Put all ingredients for filling into the blender and blend until smooth. Pour over crust. *Optional: sprinkle cinnamon sugar mixture over top of filling.
4) Bake for 45 min or until middle is set.
5) Let cool and store in refrigerator.
