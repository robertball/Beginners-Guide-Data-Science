Title:
Jen's Favorite Green  Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons vegetable oil
1/2 cup chopped onions
1 10 3/4 ounce can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup sour cream
8 butter crackers, (Hi-Ho, Ritz, etc.) crumbled
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/8 teaspoon dry mustard
2 16 oz. cans French style green beans, drained
1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded and divided
1 3 oz. package French fried onions

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.  Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.  Add onion and saute for 5 minutes.  Remove from heat.
Combine soup, sour cream, cracker crumbs, pepper and mustard in a large bowl: mix well.  Add green beans and saut√©ed onion, mix well.  Stir in 1/2 cup Cheddar.
Spray a 9-inch square baking pan with nonstick cooking spray (If doubling recipe, use a 9" x 13" pan).  Spoon bean mixture into prepared casserole.  Bake for 20 minutes (or until edges are bubbling).
Remove casserole from oven.  Sprinkle French-fried onions and remaining Cheddar over top.  Bake until golden, about 10 minutes longer.
