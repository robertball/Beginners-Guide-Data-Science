Title:
Fourth of July Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds potatoes, peeled and diced
1/3 cup cider vinegar
2 teaspoons white sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons  mustard
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
3/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 cup mayonnaise
3/4 cup chopped onion
2 celery ribs
6 hard-cooked eggs, peeled and chopped

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, add the peeled potatoes and let cook until tender. While the potatoes cook, chop up the onions and celery.Then in a large bowl, whisk together the vinegar, sugar, mustard, salt and pepper,mayo and sugar. add the sauce to the potatos after they have cooled. Mix well
