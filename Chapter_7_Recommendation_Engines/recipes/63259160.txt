Title:
Rhonda's Easy But Satisfying Pomegranate Margaritas!!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 liters lemon lime soda
1.75 liter Gold Tequilla
4 cups Triple Sec
4 cups Pomegranate juice

Directions:
Blend all together and serve over ice.
For Frozen Margaritas put crushed ice in blender bowl and blend till fine then and as much of the concotion as your blender will allow!

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
These are perfect for those large Beautiful Drink dispensers with the spigots! Mine will hold more than 3 Gallons so I double this recipe =-)
