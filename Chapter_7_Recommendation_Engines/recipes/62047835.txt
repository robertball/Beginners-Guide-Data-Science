Title:
Sweet Kielbasa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.1 pkg Kielbasa sliced
2.1 small bottle Ketchup
3.about 3/4 brown sugar (to taste)
4.1/2 cup water (or beer)to desired thickness.

Directions:
1. Slice Kielbasa and put in slow cooker.
2. Mix in entire bottle of Ketchup, 3/4 cup brown sugar (more or less to taste) and water (or beer)
3. Cook on low for first 2 hours and then on high for the last hour.

Submitter: 
Amanda
Description: 
Sliced Kielbasa with a simply tangy sauce cooked in a slow cooker. Great for Potlucks!
