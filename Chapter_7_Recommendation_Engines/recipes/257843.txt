Title:
RITZ Caprese Ladybug Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4506117.jpg
Ingredients:
24 RITZ Crackers
6 bocconcini (1-inch fresh mozzarella cheese balls), each cut into 4 slices
24 fresh basil leaves
12 cherry tomatoes, halved
6 pitted black olives, quartered
1 green onion, green part only cut into 48 thin strips
2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
1/4 teaspoon black sesame seeds

Directions:
Top each cracker with 1 EACH cheese slice and basil leaf.
Add 1 tomato half to each for the lady bug's body, 1 olive piece for the head and 2 green onion strips for the antennae.
Brush with balsamic glaze; sprinkle with sesame seed.

Submitter: 
RITZ Crackers
Description: 
Caprese salad ingredients--tomatoes, basil, mozzarella cheese--arranged creatively on Ritz crackers make adorable ladybug bites.
