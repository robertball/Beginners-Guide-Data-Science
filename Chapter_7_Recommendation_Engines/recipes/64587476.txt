Title:
TEA PUNCH Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 individual size tea bags
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup fresh lemon juice
1 cup pineapple juice
1/4 cup grenadine
1 quart chilled ginger ale

Directions:
Combine the tea bags with 2 cups of boiling water and steep.
Discard the bags.
Add the sugar and stir to dissolve.
Add the fruit juices and grenadine.
Chill for several hours.
When ready to serve, add the chilled ginger ale.
If you have fresh mint, use a sprig for garnish.

Submitter: 
CajunGrocer.com
Description: 
Delicious Tea Punch to cool down on a hot southern summer day!
