Title:
Bacon-Wrapped Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/387482.jpg
Ingredients:
10 spears fresh asparagus, trimmed
1/8 teaspoon pepper
5 bacon strips, halved lengthwise

Directions:
Place asparagus on a sheet of waxed paper; coat with nonstick cooking spray. Sprinkle with pepper; turn to coat. Wrap a bacon piece around each spear; secure ends with toothpicks. Grill, uncovered, over medium-low heat for 8-12 minutes or until bacon is crisp, turning occasionally. Discard toothpicks.

Submitter: 
Patricia Kitts
Description: 
'My husband and I grill dinner almost every night, and I love grilling asparagus for a side dish,' relates Patricia Kitts of Dickinson, Texas. 'I serve these bacon-wrapped spears with grilled meat and sliced fresh tomatoes for a wonderful meal.'
