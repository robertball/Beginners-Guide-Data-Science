Title:
coconut bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2cups sweetened coconut flakes  2cups of unsweetened coconut flakes 1 can 14oz sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
heat oven to 350 In a large bowl combine both kinds of coconuts then add milk mix until combined until all coconut is covered with the sweetened condensed milk set aside then in a glass square dish baking pan grease with cooking spray then add coconut mixture in dish make sure you spread batter evenly bake 7-10 min until golden brown on edges. do not burn .
