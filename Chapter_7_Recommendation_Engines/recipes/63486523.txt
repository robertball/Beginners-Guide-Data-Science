Title:
Sugar Plum Balls (without the sugar!) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped almonds
1/4 cup prunes (about 5), or dried mangos
1/8 cup chopped dates
1/8 cup raisins
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon anise seed
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/8 teaspoon almond extract
pinch of salt
1 tablespoon honey
1/4 cup coconut flakes

Directions:
Combine nuts and fruit in a food processor and pulse until it becomes finely chopped but before it starts to form a ball.
Transfer mixture to a medium bowl and add remaining ingredients. Combine/mix together with hands.
Roll into balls and set aside.
Place coconut flakes in food processor and process till it becomes a powder.
Add coconut to a shallow ball and roll balls until completely coated.
