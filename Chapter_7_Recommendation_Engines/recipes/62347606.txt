Title:
Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick margarine
2 cups of rice
1/2 tsp of seasoned pepper
1/2 tsp of paprika
5 cans cream of chicken soup
1/2 tsp of powdered garlic
1 1/2 soup cans of water
Chicken

Directions:
Melt margarine, mix ingredients together except for chicken, then place chicken on top of rice mixture, sprinkle with paprika, cook covered with foil at 300 degrees for 3 hours
