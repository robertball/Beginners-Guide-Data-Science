Title:
Berry Blue Cheese Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2078416.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup crumbled blue cheese
3/4 cup raspberry vinaigrette
1/2 cup sunflower kernels
1/3 cup crasins
2 cups baby spinach

Directions:
In a bowl mix crumbled blue cheese and raspberry vinaigrette. Set aside. In large salad bowl combine spinach, crasins, and sunflower kernels and pour on desired amount of blue cheese raspberry vinaigrette dressing. Toss and enjoy!
