Title:
Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/303552.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups self-rising flour
1 tsp. of vanilla
1 tsp. of cinnamon
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
3 large bananas, mashed
3/4 cup Splenda
1 egg
1/3 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Coat muffin pans with non-stick spray, or use paper liners. Sift together the flour; set aside.
Combine bananas, Splenda,vanilla,cinnamon,nutmeg egg, and melted butter in a large bowl. Fold in flour mixture, and mix until smooth. Scoop into muffin pans.
Bake in preheated oven. Bake mini muffins for 10 to 15 minutes, and large muffins for 25 to 30 minutes. Muffins will spring back when lightly tapped.
