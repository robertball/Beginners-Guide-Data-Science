Title:
Protein-Packed Rice Bowl with Chicken Recipe
Image:
NA
Ingredients:
½ cup water
½ cup uncooked instant rice
3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided
1 (3 ounce) chicken breast, cubed
1 large egg
4 tablespoons skim milk
¼  onion, finely chopped
salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring water to a boil in a saucepan and stir in rice according to directions. Cover, remove from heat, and let stand for 5 minutes.
Meanwhile, heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken and onion and cook until onion is soft and translucent and chicken is no longer pink inside, about 5 minutes.
Crack egg into a bowl, add milk, and beat to combine. Pour into the skillet with chicken. Season with salt and pepper. Cook and stir until egg is scrambled and set, about 5 minutes. Add cooked rice and cook for 5 more minutes. Drizzle remaining tablespoon olive oil on top and season with additional salt and pepper to taste.
