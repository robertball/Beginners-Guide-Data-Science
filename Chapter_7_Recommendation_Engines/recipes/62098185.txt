Title:
Asian Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 large cabbage
1 bunch green onions
1 cup slivered almonds
1/4 cup white toasted sesame seeds
3 tablespoons sesame oil
3 tablespoons rice vinegar (4% acidity = sushi vinegar)
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon grated ginger (optional)
1 tablespoon sugar (optional)
1 clove minced garlic (optional)

Directions:
Cut cabbage in half and cut out the core.  Slice as thin as possible to shred the cabbage.  If pieces are very long, cut in half length-wise.
Slice green onions small.
Put cabbage, green onions, and almonds in a large dish.
Grate ginger.
Mince garlic.
Combine oil, vinegar, salt, pepper, ginger, sugar and garlic in a small bowl or up to make the dressing.
Pour dressing over the cabbage salad.  Toss and mix well!
Serve as cole slaw salad with any meal.
