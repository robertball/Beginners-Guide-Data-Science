Title:
Terriffic Breakfast Tacos ! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 flour tortillas
20 bacon strips
1 bag hashbrowns
1 lb pork sausage
8 eggs
1 small onion
1 tbsp garlic powder
5 tbsp veggie oil
1/4 milk or water
Salt & Pepper to taste for potatoes
Toppings:
grated cheese
sour cream
salsa

Directions:
In a large skillet, mix hashbrowns, onions, garlic powder and oil. Cover. Stir occasionally until hashbrowns and onions are cook til soft and golden brown.
In small skillet, cook sausage. Remove sausage, reserve grease.
Wisk eggs with milk or water. Pour into sausage grease and scramble.
Cook bacon til crisp.
Fill tortillas to liking and add toppings of your choice.

Submitter: 
Katy Blaylock
Description: 
My dad made these for years, then I started making them. We do these for dinner too, mainly so we can make some extras for the work week breakfast! So much better thatn toaster pastries..LOL
