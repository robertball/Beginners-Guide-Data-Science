Title:
Whole grain bread with soft cheese spread and raspberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 ounce soft goat cheese or part skim ricotta cheese
1 1/2 tsp non fat plain greek yogurt
1 slice whole grain bread
1/3 cup raspberries
1/2 tsp honey
pinch of pepper

Directions:
In a small bowl place the cheese, yogurt and pepper. Use a fork to mash and mix the cheese and yogurt together until well combined. Toast bread. Spread the cheese and yogurt mixture on the toast. Place the raspberries in a single layer on top and mash them with a fork. Drizzle with honey and serve.
