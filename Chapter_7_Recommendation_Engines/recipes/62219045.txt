Title:
Orange Stuffing for Turkey or Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c fresh or frozen cranberries
3/4 c sugar
1 1/2 c orange juice
1 (14 1/2 oz) packaged herb seasoned stuffing cubes (about 8 1/4 cups)
1 c butter, melted
6 celery ribs, sliced 1/2 inch
2 medium onion, chopped (1 cup)
2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp allspice

Directions:
Stir together cranberries, sugar and 1 1/2 c orange juice in a 3 quart saucepan. Cook over medium high heat, stirring occationally, until cranberries pop and sugar dissolves about 9 to 11 minutes, cool 15 minutes
Meanwhile combine all remaining stuffing ingredients in large bowl. Stir in cranberry mixture. Preheat oven to 325F. Place stuffing in a greased 3 quart casserole dish. Bake uncovered in oven for 60 to 70 minutes or until celery is tender.
