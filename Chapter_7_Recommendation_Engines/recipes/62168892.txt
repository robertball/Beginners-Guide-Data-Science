Title:
Hot Carmel Apple Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of apple juice
3 tablespoons Hersheys carmel syrup
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
whip cream (optional)

Directions:
Mix apple juice, carmel syrup, nutmeg, cinnamon in a saucepan in medium-high heat. Stir or whisk briskly until well heated but not boiling (about 3 minutes) add vanilla and stir for another minute. Pour in two mugs. top woth whip cream - if you like!

Submitter: 
Kelly Provost
Description: 
A great warm fall drink. Great replacement for a winter's hot cocoa! My children love it. I came up with it when I wanted something warm and sweet but didnt want the caffeine. Like hot apple pie in a mug!
