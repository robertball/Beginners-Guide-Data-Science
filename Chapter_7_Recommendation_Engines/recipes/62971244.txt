Title:
Peanut Butter Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/773686.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
15 oz can chicken broth
3/4 cup peanut butter
4 oz thai noodles
6 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbsp corn starch
3 vegetables of your choice (i.e. 1 green pepper, 1 red pepper, 1 sweet onion, or 1 cup green beans, 1 cup chopped carrots, 1 cup snap peas.)

Directions:
Cook chicken and vegetables in pan.
Boil noodles in pot.
Add ingredients at medium heat and cook until sauce is thickened.
