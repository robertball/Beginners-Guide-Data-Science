Title:
Fool Proof Barbecue Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C Open Pit Barbecue Sauce, original
1 C ketchup
1 envelope Lipton's Onion Soup Mix
1 large onion diced
3 cloves of garlic, minced
1/2 C cider vinegar
1/2 C brown sugar
1/2 C pure maple syrup
1 T Worcestershire Sauce
1 T yellow mustard
1 T chili powder
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. black pepper
1 bay leaf

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a medium sauce pan.  Simmer over medium heat until the sauce thickens, about 30 minutes. Store in a 1 quart Mason jar.  Keeps about a week in the refrigerator.  Great over chicken, ribs or as a base for sloppy joes.
