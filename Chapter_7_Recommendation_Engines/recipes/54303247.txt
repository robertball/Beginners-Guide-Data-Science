Title:
Thai Pineapple Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup red curry paste
2 (13.5 ounce) cans coconut milk
4 lg skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into thin strips
2 tablespoons fish sauce
1/2 cup white sugar
2 cups sliced bamboo shoots, drained
1 red bell pepper, julienned
2 cup pineapple chunks, drained

Directions:
In a large sauce pan, mix coconut milk, chicken, fish sauce, sugar, and bamboo shoots. Bring to a boil, and cook 15 minutes, until chicken juices run clear.
Add the red bell pepper, continue cooking 10 minutes. Remove from heat, and stir in pineapple. Serve over cooked rice or noodles.
