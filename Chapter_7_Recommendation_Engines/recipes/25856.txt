Title:
Paralyzers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1804387.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce vodka
1 fluid ounce coffee flavored liqueur
1/2 cup milk
1/8 teaspoon cola-flavored carbonated beverage

Directions:
Fill a highball glass with ice, and pour in vodka, coffee liqueur, milk, and cola; stir.
