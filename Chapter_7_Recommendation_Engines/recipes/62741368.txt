Title:
Most Excellent Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground pork sausage or
1 pound bacon
4 cups milk
8 eggs
1/4 tsp. pepper
12 slices white bread, crusts trimmed
2 tablespoons butter, softened
1/2  onion, chopped & sauted
8 oz. can mushrooms
6 oz. shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Place sausage or bacon in large skillet & onion add mushrooms toward end of cooking. Cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown. Drain, crumble and set aside.
Butter one side of six slices of the bread. Placed slices butter side up in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking pan.
In a large bowl, layer half of meat mixture over bread, sprinkle with 1/2 of cheese,  Then add another six slices of bread (no butter).  Add remaining meat mixture, sprinkle with the rest of cheese. Beat together milk, eggs, & pepper; pour over top of casserole. Cover with foil and refrigerate overnight.
The next morning: preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Bake, covered, in preheated oven for 45 then uncover and bake additional 10 to 15 minutes to brown the top.
