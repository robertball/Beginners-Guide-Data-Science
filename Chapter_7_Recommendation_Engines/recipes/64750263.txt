Title:
Cheesecake from "The Manor" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese (room temperature)
1 (1 oz.) box instant vanilla pudding mix(sugar free)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups almond milk(regular- unsweetened)
spray  oil for pie plate

Directions:
With hand mixer- mix cream cheese til smooth
add vanilla and pudding mix- mix well
add milk- mix til thickened
put in sprayed 8 inch pie plate and chill til cold- (about a half hour)- could put in ramekins if you wish individual servings
serve with favorite berries- either cooked or fresh
