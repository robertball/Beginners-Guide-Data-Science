Title:
Trish's Quick Marbleized Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Jiffy brownie mix (follow box directions)
1 8 oz pkg cream cheese - quartered
1 egg
1/4 c powdered sugar
1/4 c Heath brickle bites
Raspberry preserves

Directions:
Prepare brownie mix.  Add 1/4 c. Heath brickle bites to the mix.
In food processor, cream cheese, powdered sugar, and egg together.
Oil 9x11 baking pan, preheat oven to 350 degrees, put brownie mix into the dish.  Spread mix evenly in the pan. Drop dollops of cream cheese mixture and raspberry preserves atop the brownie mix.  Drag a kitchen knife through the mix, making a pretty pattern.  Bake for 30-35 minutes. Slice them and chill in the refrigerator for 2 hours.
