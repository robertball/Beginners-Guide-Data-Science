Title:
Broccoli Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 El Preferido Original dip
1 ½ cups cooked Rice
2 cups chopped broccoli
1 12.5 oz can chicken, or 1 ½ cups precooked shredded chicken
½ medium sized onion diced
1 tbsp butter/margarine
¼ cup chicken broth
½ tsp garlic salt
Shredded cheddar cheese for topping.

Directions:
Sauté onion and butter in a skillet till the onion becomes translucent
Once the onion is cooked mix all ingredients together except the cheddar cheese.
Pour into a baking dish and sprinkle the top of casserole with cheddar cheese.
Bake at 375 for 30 min, then broil for about 3 minutes until golden brown.
