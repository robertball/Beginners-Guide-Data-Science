Title:
Broccoli and Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Minute Rice
2 cups cold water
3/4 cup chopped onion
1 stick soft butter
3/4 cup chopped celery
2 10oz packages thawed chopped broccoli
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1 8oz jar of cheese whiz
1 sm can Durkee French Fried onions(optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 deg.
Spray 9x13 dish with pam
Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl.
Pour out into prepared pan.
Bake in preheated oven for one hour. You may add the optional onions on top after 1/2 hour.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description:
