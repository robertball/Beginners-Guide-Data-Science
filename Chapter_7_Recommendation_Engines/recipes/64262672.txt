Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of enchilada sauce
2-3 cups of cooked chicken
1/2 cup of sour cream
2- 4oz cans of green chiles
1/3 cup of chopped green onions
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
12 tortillas

Directions:
In 9x13 baking dish, pour 1 can of enchilada sauce on the bottom of pan
Mix the chicken, sour cream, chiles, and green onions and 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
Divide and fill 12 tortillas, and place seam side down in baking dish.(You can use a little sourcream to seal the seams.)
Pour second can of sauce over filled tortillas
Top with 1 cup of cheddar cheese.
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes, uncovered.
(You can use one can of enchilada sauce on the bottom and top with salsa instead of enchilada sauce.)
