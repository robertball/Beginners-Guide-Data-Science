Title:
Porcupine Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1/2 cup rice
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon minced onion
1 can tomato soup
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Wash rice.  Combine rice, meat, salt, pepper, and onion.  Shape into small balls.  Combine soup and water.  Drop balls into soup.  Put in crock pot.  Cook on HIGH 3-4 hours, until meat and rice and cooked and tender.

Submitter: 
Karen Monson
Description: 
Another recipe from my mom.  So good and the kids love the porcupine quills sticking out of the meatballs!
