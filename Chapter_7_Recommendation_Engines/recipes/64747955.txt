Title:
Roasted Fennel and Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6645346.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large organic fennel bulbs
14 ounces organic multicolored carrots
2 tbsp evoo
1 tsp Sriracha salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Line a large baking sheet with aluminum foil.
Remove the outer layers of the fennel but leave the fronds.Wash with water. Cut into eighths. Scrub under running water the carrots, leaving about four inches of the green tops. If your carrots are larger than 3/4 inch in diameter, cut them in half.
Arrange the vegetables on the baking sheet. Drizzle the oil and sprinkle the Sriracha salt evenly over the vegetables.
Bake until the vegetables are tender but not too soft between 20 and 25 minutes. Turn on the broiler and brown the vegetables. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Fennel and multicolored carrots are roasted in the oven highlighting their natural flavor and sweetness. I serve this with chickpea cassava couscous for a vegan and gluten free meal.
