Title:
Lemon Turkey Fillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Lemon Turkey FIllets
Yield: 4  servings
4 turkey fillets
1 turkey breast- fillet into four to six pieces
100 g flour
salt
pepper
olive oil
lemon juice
1 bundle fresh parsley - chopped

Directions:
Place flour, salt and pepper to season into a plastic bag.
Shake turkey fillets together with flour mixture in the plastic bag until lightly coated in flour.
Heat a generous amount of olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Keep the level of olive oil wet in the pan. Don't let it dry out.
Add the turkey fillets to the pan and fry on each side until golden brown and cooked all the way through (about 5 minutes).
Place cooked fillets on a platter and squeez lemon juice over the top to add a light flavoring and sprinkle with fresh chopped parsley
Serve hot!
