Title:
Mojito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985236.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 ounces Captain Morgan Original Spiced Rum
12 leaves mint
1 teaspoon sugar
1/2 ounce lime juice
2 ounces soda water

Directions:
Place mint leaves in bottom of glass.
Add crushed ice, Captain Morgan Original Spiced Rum, sugar, and lime juice, and muddle.
Add soda water and garnish with mint leaves.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Fresh mint leaves, lime juice and spiced rum are key to making this classic mojito.
