Title:
Roasted Chili Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c small dried chilies
3 T fish sauce
2 c veg oil
1/3 t salt
8 shallots sliced
6 garlic
1 c dried shrimp
1 T palm sugar
1 1/2 T tamarind juice

Directions:
Fry the shallots and garlic until golden brown, remove and drain. Add dried shrimp and chilies until brown and remove and drain.
Blend all until fine textured.
Store for 4 month in a glass jar in the fridge.
