Title:
Cranberry Cornmeal Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/974880.jpg
Ingredients:
1C A/P flour
3/4C cornmeal
1/3C sugar
2t baking powder
1/4t salt
1 beaten egg
3/4C buttermilk
1/4 veg/canola oil
1/2t lemon or orange peel
1C coarsely chopped cranberries
2T sugar
1/4C finely chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F; grease muffin pans.
Combine flour, corneal, 1/3C sugar, baking powder, salt. Make a well in the center.
Combine egg, buttermilk, oil, lemon peel. Add to flour mixture all at once and stir just until moistened.
In another bowl combine chopped cranberries with 2T sugar. Fold cranberries and walnuts into muffin batter.
Spoon into pan; bake at 400F for about 20 mins.
Cool on rack.

Submitter: 
Helen
Description: 
The cornmeal adds nice texture and lightness to the muffin.
