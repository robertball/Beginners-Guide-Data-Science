Title:
Sausage and Kale Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 onion diced
2 cloves garlic minced
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
5 waxy potatoes 1.5 lbs, peeled an cut into 1/2 inch pieces
43 oz reduced sodium chicken stock (3 14.5 oz cans)
1 bunch (12 oz) kale stemmed and shredded
12 oz smoked chicken sausage cut into 1/2 inch moons

Directions:
In a large (6-8) qt pot heat oil and cook onions until soft, stirring, 2-3 min
Add garlic and pepper flakes, cook until fragrant (1 min)
Add potatoes and broth, bring to a boil and reduce heat simmer 10-15 minutes or until potatoes are tender
Puree half the soup in a blender, return to pot, add kale and sausage. Simmer until kale is wilted, (10-15 min)
Optional: Add some heavy cream (start with 1/2 cup) stir in and cook until heated.

Submitter: 
Soccercoach
Description: 
Hearty warming soup that doesn't take too long to prepare if you use precooked sausage and don't have to take the time to cook the sausage first
