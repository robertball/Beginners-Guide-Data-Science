Title:
Sausage rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb of sausage meat ground- pork mostly mixed with beef - pork fatter than beef, but you can use turkey, lamb whatever - traditional is 80 percent porl 20 percent beef
2 tablespoons of special spice - actually your choice, actually i like nothing or herb de provence but make it your signature dish
roll puff pastry, cut into strips, fill with sausage roll filling and bake at pastry
1 onion chopped finely - use mini chopper
1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon of salt
1/4 teaspoon of pepper
2 tablespoons of parsley flakes
1 egg
1 pkg of puff pastry

Directions:
in a large bowl mix all ingredients except puff pastry
cut pastry IN STRIPS, bake as per the directions for puff pastry
