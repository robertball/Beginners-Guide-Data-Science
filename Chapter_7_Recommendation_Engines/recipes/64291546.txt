Title:
Balsamic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon olive oil
1 yellow onion, thinly sliced
2 large red bell peppers, sliced
4 cloves garlic, minced (may substitute 1 tsp galic powder)
4 [4- to 6-oz]skinless, boneless, chicken breast halves
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes [or to taste]
1/4 teaspoon coarse salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/3 cup balsamic vinegar
1 [15-oz] can diced tomatoes, well drained

Directions:
Coat a large sauté pen with oil, and preheat the pan over medium-high heat.  Add onion and bell pepper and sauté for 5 minutes.
Add garlic [or garlic powder] and red pepper flakes and sauté for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.  Add chicken breasts to the pan and cook for 4 minutes on each side.
Add vinegar and tomatoes, and bring liquids too a boil.  Reduce the heat to medium-low, and simmer uncovered for 25 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Serve with quinoa and sautéed greens.
