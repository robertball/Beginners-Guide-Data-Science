Title:
Shari's Seafood Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Shari's Seafood Lasagna Recipe.
9 cooked lasagna noddles.
The Sauce:
3 T butter, 1/4 c. flour 1 1/2 c. milk 1/2 C dry white wine, 1 t. nutmeg, 2 t. fresh minced garlic, 1/2 t. salt, 1/4 t. pepper. 1/8 t. hot sauce.
The Filling: 2 eggs, 3/4 c. freshly grated parmesan cheese, 1/2 c. fresh chopped parsley, 15 oz Ricotta cheese, 4 oz. chopped pimento, 12 oz. frozen or fresh shrimp, 8 oz, of crab or lobster meat ( rinse & drains seafood), 3 c.shredded swiss cheese and fresh parsley for garnish.

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees, Melt butter in frying pan. Stir in flour and garlic til it bubbles, stir in milk , boil 1 minute. In a bowl mix ricotta filling, in a large baking dish, layer 3 lasagna noodles, 1/3 or sauce, and ricotta , repeat until finished, Cover with foil and bake 20 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 15 - 20 minutes. Let sit for 15-20 minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley. Serve with a caesar salad and a nice white wine. Hope you enjoy!!!
