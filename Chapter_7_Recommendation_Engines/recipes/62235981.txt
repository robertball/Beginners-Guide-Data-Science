Title:
Gluten-free Chocolate Chip Yummies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 egg whites
1/8 tsp salt
1/8 tsp cream of tartar
3/4 C sugar
3/4 tsp vanilla
1/2 C semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 C chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat over to 300 degrees F. Beat the egg whites until foamy. Them add your salt and cream of tartar. Beat until stiff peaks form. Add your sugar a couple tbs at a time. Beat well after each addition. Add vanilla and blend well. Fold in chocolate chips and nutus. Drop teaspoonfulls onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake about 20 minutes or until firm and golden. (max about 23 minutes.) Makes 2 dozen cookies.

Submitter: 
ilovemykids
Description: 
Chocolate chip cookies that are wheat free and so yummy. they are low in cholesterol and they will freeze well if you choose to make bunch and freeze them!
