Title:
Big Ray's Sausage and Cabbage Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1052004.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb pork sausage
1 cup chopped onion
10 cups chopped cabbage
1 1/2 cup sour cream
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 1/2 cup bread crumbs
2 Tbs butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Brown sausage, drain fat.
Add onions and cabbage, cook 10 minutes.
Combine with  sour cream and cheese in a 9 x 13 casserole dish.
Top with bread crumbs and melted butter mixture.
Bake for 20 minutes.
