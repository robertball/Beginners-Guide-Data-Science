Title:
Wild Blueberry Lemon Breakfast Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/809274.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 cup rolled oats
1 cup corn flakes, lightly crushed
1 cup crisp rice cereal
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup dried wild blueberries
zest of one medium lemon
1/2 cup canola oil
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup granulated sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. In a large bowl, whisk together first 6 ingredients. Set aside. In separate bowl, beat lemon zest, oil, sugars, vanilla and egg til well blended. Stir in blueberries. Mix wet into dry ingredients.
Drop by tablespoonful onto insulated cookie sheet. *Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until just starting to brown (see note below). Do not overbake.
