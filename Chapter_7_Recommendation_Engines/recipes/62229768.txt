Title:
my trainer's kale soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
olive oil (about 3 tablespoons)
1 lrg onion diced
5 carrots peeled and sliced
5 stalks of celery chopped
salt (to taste)
pepper (to taste)
2 garlic cloves minced
32 oz chicken stock
1 can black beans rinsed & drained
1 can cannelini beans
kale rough chopped (3 or 4 good handfuls)
apple cider vinegar - 1 tablespoon or to taste

Directions:
heat olive oild over medium heat
add onions & saute until tranluscent
add carrots, celery, salt & pepper & continue to cook until carrots become tender
add garlic & continue to cook for 2 minutes
add chicken stock
add black beans
add 1/2 can of cannelini beans rinsed & drained
add other 1/2 of cannelini beans to a food processor until smooth, then add to soup & stir
add kale & splash of apple cider vinegar
bring to a boil then simmer for at least another 20-30 minutes
serve with crust bread for dipping & enjoy

Submitter: 
sarah
Description: 
so much flavor and so easy to customize with your choice of greens! you can even substitute vege stock to make this a fully vegetarian dish! tastes even better the next day!
