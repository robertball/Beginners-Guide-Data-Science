Title:
Super Slimmer Sausage Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Bob Evans® Original Recipe Sausage Roll
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
2 cups skim milk
Salt and black pepper to taste
8 prepared reduced fat Bisquick™ biscuits

Directions:
Crumble and cook sausage in large skillet over medium heat until browned. Stir in flour until dissolved. Gradually stir in milk. Cook gravy until thick and bubbly. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot over biscuits. Refrigerate leftovers.

Submitter: 
Zanne
Description: 
This is a hearty breakfast favorite. Biscuits served with hot sausage gravy are a sure way to stay satisfied until your next meal.Modified from: Super Sausage Gravy
