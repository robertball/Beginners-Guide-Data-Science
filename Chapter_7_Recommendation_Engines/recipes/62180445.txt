Title:
Festive Mincemeat Pastries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c butter
8 oz cream cheese
1/2 powdered sugar
2 c flour
1/4 tsp salt
1 c prepared mincemeat
1 tbsp rum

Directions:
Beat butter and cream cheese for 30 seconds; add sugar and beat until light and fluffy. Whisk together flour and salt; stir into mixture. Cover; chill dough several hours or overnight.
On a lightly floured surface, roll out dough 1/8" thick, cut wiht a 3" round cookie cutter. Place cookies on a parchment lined baking sheet.
Combine mincmeat and rum; place 1 tsp mincemeat mixture in center of each cookie; bring up 2 adjacent sides of cookie and pinch to seal, forming a cornucopia or "bell".
Bake in 375 degree oven 10-12 mins or till golden brown. Let cool on a wore rack.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
Tender butter cookies filled with mincemeat, in a cornucopia shape.
