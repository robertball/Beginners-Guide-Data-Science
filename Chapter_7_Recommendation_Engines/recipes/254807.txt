Title:
Crack Burrito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4555165.jpg
Ingredients:
peanut butter
1 (8 inch) flour tortillas
1 tablespoon vanilla frosting

Directions:
Spread peanut butter onto the flour tortilla. Layer vanilla frosting onto the peanut butter; roll up tortilla to make a burrito.
