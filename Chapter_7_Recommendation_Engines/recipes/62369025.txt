Title:
Egg White Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/413771.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup egg whites (or use the whites of 4 large eggs)
1/2 cup rolled oats
2 T non-fat cottage cheese
Cinnamon and Splenda to taste
1/2 cup fresh strawberries or blueberries
Sugar-free syrup

Directions:
Mix first four ingredients until smooth, add berries
Pour approximately 1/3 cup batter for each pancake onto heated griddle or non-stick pan (check to see if its hot enough with a splash of water-if it sizzles, its hot enough)
When pancake bubbles, flip and cook the other side
Serve with sugar free syrup.
