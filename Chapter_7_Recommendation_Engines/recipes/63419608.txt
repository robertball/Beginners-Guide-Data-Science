Title:
Mushroom Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of mini phyllo cups
4 Tbs butter
1/2 lb finely chopped mushrooms (portabellas are good)
3 tsp onion
2 tsp flour
1 C light cream
1/2 tsp salt
1 Tbs chopped parsley
1/2 tsp lemon juice
3 Tbs Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat over to 350 degrees
Fill mini muffin pan with Phyllo cups and bake 5 minutes.
Sauté onion and mushrooms in butter until dry. Stir in flour. Add cream and boil until thick. Add parsley and lemon juice.
Put in cups.
Sprinkle with cheese and bake at 350 degrees for 8 – 10 minutes.
