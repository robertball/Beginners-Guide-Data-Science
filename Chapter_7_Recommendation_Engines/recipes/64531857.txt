Title:
Sugar-Free Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 & 3/4 Cups of Sugar-free Shortbread cookie crumbs ( approx. a package and a half of cookies )
1/2 Cup of Butter
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
16 oz. of Cream Cheese
.30 oz pkg. Sugar-Free Lemon Jello
1 oz. pkg of Sugar-Free Cheesecake pudding
1 Cup of Sugar-Free cool whip
1 Cup boiling water
3/4 Cup of Milk
1 tsp. Vanilla extract
Topping (optional)
20 oz. can of Duncan Hines Comstock No Sugar Added Cherry Pie Filling & Topping

Directions:
Crust~
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
In a food processor crumble cookies ( If you don't have a food processor put them in a ziplock baggie, not fully sealed, and use a rolling pin to break down. If you don't have a rolling pin a bottle of wine or a can of pop will work in a pinch.)
Combine Cookie crumbles, Butter, & Cinnamon
Press into a 9 inch Springform pan then
Bake for 8-10 Minutes then allow to cool
Cheesecake filling~
Boil 1 Cup of water
Once it is at a boil add Jello packet and stir until dissolved and remove from heat
Mix softened Cream Cheese, Vanilla extract, Cool whip & pudding then SLOWLY add the milk while mixing, add the hot Jello and mix well
Then pour into pan with crust
Refrigerate overnight
Before serving add Cherry Topping or Topping of your choosing
