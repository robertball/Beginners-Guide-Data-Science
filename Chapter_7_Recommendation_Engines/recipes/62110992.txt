Title:
Quick Salmon Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2T Olive Oil
3 cloves garlic
8 oz Lite Cream Cheese
14 oz jar petite diced italian tomatoes, undrained
1/2 - 3/4 c milk
1 tsp Italian Seasoning
1/2 c parmesan or asiago cheese, additional to garnish
4 oz (or more) smoked salmon
1 c frozen petite peas
8 oz Rigatoni (or other) pasta
Salt & Olive oil to taste

Directions:
Boil rigatoni according to package directions, drain.
With a garlic press, press the garlic cloves into a microwave-safe dish.  Add 2T olive oil and heat on high in the microwave for 1 minute.
Add cream cheese and tomatoes, microwave on high 1 - 2 minutes longer until cream cheese stirs smooth.
Add milk, italian seasoning, cheese, salmon and frozen peas.  Microwave on high till heated through, about 2 minutes.  At this point add 1/4 c more milk if sauce seems too thick.
Toss sauce with cooked pasta, adding salt and olive oil to taste.
Serve sprinkled with parmesan/asiago cheese and pepper if desired.
