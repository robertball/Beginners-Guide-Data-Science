Title:
Slow Cooker Tangy Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/443661.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds boneless pork chops (no need to trim fat)
2 large potatoes cut into big chunks
14 ounces sauerkraut (drained)
1 cups dried berries or dried cherries
1/2 cup carrots (shredded works best)
1 can of Root Beer (regular not diet)
2 Tablespoons Horseradish (prepared is fine)

Directions:
Place all ingredients in slow cooker in order of listing.
