Title:
Potato Soup with Smoked Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TB butter
1 lb. smoked sausage
3 potatoes, peeled and diced
3 carrots, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
1/2 cup diced onions
14 oz. corn with juice
1 cup water
14 oz. evaporated milk
1 TB fresh dill or 1 tsp. dried
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

Directions:
In a large sauce pan, melt butter over low heat
Add potatoes, celery, carrots, and onions and saute over medium heat for approximately 5 minutes.
Add corn with liquid and smoked sausage
Cover and simmer for approximately 30 minutes or until potatoes are tender
Add evaporated milk, dill, cayenne pepper, salt and pepper and heat thoroughly.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
A creamy, rich potato soup with bountiful flavors of smoked sausage with a little kick of cayenne pepper. A quick, easy soup that served along with a green salad and popovers makes a great supper on a cold winters day.
