Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2453850.jpg
Ingredients:
1 boxed cake mix (used chocolate in picture)
1/3 cup melted butter
2 eggs
3/4 cup crumbled frosted flakes
1/2 cup butterscotch chips
colored sprinkles for pizzaz
1/4 shredded kale (optional)
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1/4 cup chopped marshmellows

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees
Mix ingredients together in a bowl using a rubber spatula until combined.
Roll into 1? balls then roll in powdered sugar and drop onto a greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for approx 10 minutes (oven times may vary)

Submitter: 
Denise Skuba
Description: 
For a quick pick me up while running from here to there. To get you through any hunt. If you are low in vegies, add the kale
