Title:
Mason's Mexican Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef (or turkey)
1 small onion, diced
1 Tbls taco seasoning
1/2 Tbls cumin
1 can diced tomatoes
1 can black beans, rinsed
1 can corn, drained
1 cup shredded cheese

Directions:
Brown beef in oven proof skillet, add onion and cook until tender.    Drain and add tomatoes, corn, beans and seasonings.  Cook for 5 min, then transfer to preheated 350 degree oven.  Cook for 15 min, sprinkle cheese on top and cook for 5 more minutes.

Submitter: 
abtomesh
Description: 
Every monday my children get to decide what we eat, this seems to be what Mason always asks for...turkey, corn, black beans.  I spice it up a little bit.  Sometimes we even serve it in tortillas with a little sour cream.
