Title:
Slow Cooker Cheesy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (32 ounce) package frozen Southern-style hash brown potatoes
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  Healthy Request condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated skim milk
1 cup reduced fat sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
1/2 white onion, sliced
1 (14-1/2) oz can italian stewed tomatoes
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
1 tsp italian seasoning
1 tsp minced garlic
1/2 tsp dried basil

Directions:
Grease your slow cooker with cooking spray or shortening. In a large bowl, stir together the hash browns, cheese soup, evaporated milk, and half of the French fried onions. Pour into the slow cooker. Cover, and cook on High for 4 hours, or Low for 8 hours. Top with remaining onions just before serving.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
An easy tasty recipe for cheesy potatoes.  Great for potlucks or the holidays when oven space is limited!  It can easily be doubled if you have a larger slow cooker.Modified from: Slow Cooker Cheesy Potatoes
