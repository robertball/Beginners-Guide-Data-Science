Title:
Bright and Fresh Ham and Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3190242.jpg
Ingredients:
48 ounces broth (vegetable or chicken)
3 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
2 carrots, peeled and diced
1 onion, peeled and diced (Vidalia or yellow)
1 stalk of celery, diced
1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
1 1/2 cups of ham, diced
2 bay leaves
3 sprigs fresh dill
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Pour the broth into stock or crock pot. Prepare all ingredients and add to the broth EXCEPT the dill. Five minutes before serving, pull the small sprigs of dill off of the stems and add to simmering soup.

Submitter: 
Linda Caprice Jordan Diaz
Description: 
This soup features the bright flavor of fresh dill paired with the savory combination of ham, potatoes, and carrots. YUM!
