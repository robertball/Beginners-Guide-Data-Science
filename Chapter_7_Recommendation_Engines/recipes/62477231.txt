Title:
Easy Measure, Large Batch Pumpkin and Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound andouille sausage, diced
2 Tablespoons butter
2 1/2 cups chopped onion
2 teaspoon dried thyme
2 large cans (29 ounce) pumpkin puree
1/2 cup maple syrup
16 cups chicken stock (4 boxes)
1 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup heavy cream

Directions:
Cook diced sausage in skillet with 2 tablespoons butter for 5 minutes.
Add onion and cook until soft. Add thyme and pumpkin OR sweet potatoes and cook 5 minutes.
Add syrup, broth, and brown sugar. Cover and simmer over low heat for 45 minutes, or until pumpkin OR potatoes are tender.
In blender puree soup in batches. Return to pan and stir in cream and remaining 2 tablespoons butter. Warm but do not boil. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
The-Baby-Bow-Lady
Description: 
Makes a very large amount. Split in half, and put half in freezer (prior to adding the broth and cream). This is a different type of spicy soup for the fall season or your next Thanksgiving dinner. Works with pumpkin OR sweet potatoes.Modified from: Pumpkin and Sausage Soup
