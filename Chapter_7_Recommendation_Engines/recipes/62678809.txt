Title:
Mexican Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large skinless boneless chicken breasts (cooked and shredded)
1 can diced green chilies
6-7 sliced jalapeños (diced up finely-jar type)
Salt & Pepper to taste
1 small container sour cream
1 can black beans (drained and rinsed)
1 can refried beans
corn tortillas
corn chips
16 oz shredded mozzarella cheese.

Directions:
Simmer chicken in broth until cooked and tender.  Shread/cube chicken.
In bowl combine &quot;chicken mixture&quot; using:
shredded Chicken
Diced green chilies
6-7 diced jalapeños
Salt &amp; Pepper to taste
1 small container sour cream
1 can black beans (drained and rinsed)
1 can refried beans
Layer in casserole
Single layer corn tortillas on bottom
Layer of &quot;chicken mixture&quot;
Layer of crumbled corn chips
Layer of cheddar/mozzarella mix
Repeat two more times
Bake covered 350 for 30 minutes
Remove cover bake 10 minute
Served with hot sauce and sour cream

Submitter: 
Ruth Jones
Description: 
You can use left over chili in place of the beans.
