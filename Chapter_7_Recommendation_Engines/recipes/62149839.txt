Title:
coconut pineapple teacake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 440 gram tin crushed pineapple
1 cup self raising flour
1 cup sugar
1 cup dessicated coconut (I use lite)

Directions:
Combine all ingrediants into a mixing bowl.
Pour into a well greased ring tin (vegie oil spray is best for greasing).
Bake at 170 degrees celcius for 35-40 minutes.

Submitter: 
purplepiscean
Description: 
such a simple no-hassles recipe. Can be served hot with custard or ice-cream, of cold for afternoon tea, either iced with coconut icing or un-iced. Great for those who cannot take dairy products too.
