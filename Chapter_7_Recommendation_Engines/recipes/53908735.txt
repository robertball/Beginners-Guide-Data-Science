Title:
Dill Wrapped Cream Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) can refrigerated crescent roll dough
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 teaspoon dried dill weed
1 Tbsp. dry ranch dressing
1 tsp. horseradish

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a cookie sheet.
Unroll dough on lightly floured surface and press seams together. Mix cream cheese with dill, ranch dressing and horseradish.  Place cream chees in center of dough. Bring sides of dough up around cheese block and seal to completely enclose.
Can be made into individual bite-sized pieces.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for approximately 15 minutes. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Jean
Description: 
Easy, easy, easy! Dill-sprinkled cream cheese is wrapped in crescent roll dough and baked till golden. Serve this extra special appetizer with apple slices and crackers.Modified from: Dill Wrapped Cream Cheese
