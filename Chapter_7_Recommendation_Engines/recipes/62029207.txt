Title:
Karen's Salmon Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz. box macaroni noodles, cook and drain
2 tomatoes, chopped well
4 green onions, sliced
2 pickles, chopped well
1 can salmon,drained well
salt and pepper, to taste
1/2 cup of reduced-fat mayo, or regular mayo

Directions:
Cook pasta an drain well. Mix rest of ingredients with pasta and chill for 1hr. Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
KAREN325
Description: 
This dish makes a great summer meal. Cool and refreshing, just add bread of your choice and your favorite sorbet. Sit back and relax and enjoy!!!
