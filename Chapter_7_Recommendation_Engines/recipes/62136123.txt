Title:
Candy Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups thinly-sliced peeled apples
2 tbsp. lime juice
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. salt
pastry for double pie crust
2 tbsp. butter
--------------------
TOPPING
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
2 tbsp. heavy cream
1/2 cup chopped pecans
--------------------
PASTRY FOR DOUBLE-CRUST
2 cups all purpose flour
2/3 cup shortening
1 tsp. salt
6 to 7 tbsp. cold water

Directions:
In a large bowl, toss the apples with lime juice.
Combine dry ingredients and add to apples. Toss lightly
Place bottom pastry crust in 9-inch pie plate; fill with apple mixture.
Dot with butter and cover with top crust.  Flute edges high; cut steam vents.
Bake at 400 degrees for 40 to 45 minutes, or until apples are tender.
--------------------
FOR TOPPING:  melt butter in a small sauce pan. Stir in brown sugar and cream; bring to a boil, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat, stir in pecans.  Pour over crust.
Return to oven for 3 to 4 minutes or until bubbly.
--------------------
PASTRY FOR DOUBLE-CRUST:
Place flour and salt in bowl and mix.  Cut in the shortening until pieces are the size of small peas.
Sprinkle 1 tablespoon of the water over part of the mixture; gently toss with fork. Push to side of bowl. Repeat until all is moistened. Divide dough into two equal part.
On a lightly floured surface, flatten with hand.  Roll dough from center to edge, forming a circle about 12" in diameter.
Wrap pasty around rolling pin and unroll on a 9" pie plate.
