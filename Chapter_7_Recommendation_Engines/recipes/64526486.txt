Title:
Garlic Soy Tuna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3352938.jpg
Ingredients:
Fresh Tuna - sliced into stir-fry sizes
One part Soy
One part Lemon
One part Garlic

Directions:
1. Slice tuna into bite-size pieces
2. Mix soy, lemon & crushed garlic in equal parts
3. Marinate for 10 minutes
4. Eat

Submitter: 
Kyle Watson
Description: 
The lemon in this sauce chemically cooks the outside of the tuna,  the soy & garlic add amazing flavours.
