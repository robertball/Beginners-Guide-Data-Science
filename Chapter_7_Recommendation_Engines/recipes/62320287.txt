Title:
Spicy Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
64 oz of baby dill pickles (already made from the store)
2 1/2 cups sugar
1/3 cup cider vinegar
1 1/2 tbsp. red pepper flakes
2 habanero peppers
12 cloves crushed garlic
1 tbsp. pickeling spices

Directions:
Measure out juice from pickles and discard. Rinse pickles and cut into bite size chunks. Mix sugar, vinegar,red pepper flakes, garlic and pickeling spice into sauce pan. Add enought water to equal the amount of pickel juice you discarded. Bring to a boil long enought to disolve sugar. Pack pickle chuncks back into the jar(s) cover with liquid mixture. Slice habanero equaly into jars or jar. Place lid on and refridgerate for 3 weeks. If habanero is too hot for some, jalapeno pepper works well also.

Submitter: 
Carla Murrain-Wilson
Description: 
Got this recipe from my brother. It's easy and great change from regular pickles.
