Title:
Summer Herb Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 garlic cloves
1 cup fresh basil leaves
1 cup fresh Italian parsley leaves
1/2 cup fresh oregano leaves
1 cup fresh spinach leaves
1/2 cup toasted pine nuts or almonds, cooled
1/2 cup grated Parmesan
Salt and freshly ground black pepper
3/4 cup olive oil

Directions:
In a blender or food processor finely chop the garlic, scraping down the sides with a rubber spatula if necessary.
Rinse and dry all the herbs and the spinach.
Add the herbs, spinach, nuts, Parmesan, salt, pepper and olive oil and process until smooth.
Pesto will keep stored in a airtight container and refrigerated for up to one week. Pesto can also be frozen and stored for up to one month.

Submitter: 
thislittlepiggy
Description: 
A fresh tasty pesto you can make with herbs right from the garden. Delicious tossed with pasta, spread on a sandwich or as a dip for veggies.
