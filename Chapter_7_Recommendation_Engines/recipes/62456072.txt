Title:
Oven-Fried Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork chops, trimmed
2 tablespoons butter, melted or olive oil
1 egg, beaten
flour for coating chops
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 cup Italian breadcrumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Pour butter or olive oil into a 9x13 inch baking pan.
Stir together egg and pepper.  Coat chops with flour. Dip pork chops in egg mixture, coat with bread crumbs .Repeat. Place in pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes. Turn chops and bake for another 10 minutes, or until no pink remains in the meat and juices run clear.
