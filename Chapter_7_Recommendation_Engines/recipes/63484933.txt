Title:
Beef Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbs cornstarch
1 tbs water
1 tbs soy sauce
1 tsp sugar
1 tbs oil
stir fry sauce:
2 tbs corn starch
2 tbs soy sauce
1/3 cup water
1 tsp sugar
2 tbs oyster sauce
1 lb beef sliced
1 lb or more frozen or fresh stir fry veggies
1/2 onion chunked or green onions sliced
1 clove garlic minced
1/2 tbs ginger root, minced (optional)

Directions:
heat in 1 tbs oil in skillet and cook onion beef and garlic.
if frozen veg, add veggies and sauce and cover and cook 5 mins while stirring
if fresh veg, remove beef and cook veg in order of length of cooking time or cook before beef
serve over rice
