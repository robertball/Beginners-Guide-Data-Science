Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookies with Avocado Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1566568.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1\4 cups flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
1/2 cup mashed avocado
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1 egg
10 oz. chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Lightly spray cookie sheets.
Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl and set aside.
Mix together butter, avocado, sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla until creamy.
Beat in egg.
Stir in flour mixture a little bit at a time.
Stir in chocolate chips.
Drop by spoonful on to cookie sheets.
Bake 8 to 10 minutes.
Cool on cookie sheet for 2 minutes then remove to wire rack.
Cool completely.

Submitter: 
Erin
Description: 
I read that when baking you can replace half or all of the butter with mashed avocado to make it a little healthier. I got out my favorite cookie recipe from my dad and tried it. I replaced half the butter. They were still very yummy and I couldn't tell there was avocado in them.
