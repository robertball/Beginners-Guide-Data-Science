Title:
10 Minute Chocolate Carmel Pecan Pretzel. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mini Pretzels, Chocolate Rolo's Candy & Pecan Halves thats it!

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 300f. Cover a cookie sheet with wax paper or not. Place Mini Pretzels about a 1/2" apart. Put a Rolo Candy on top. Place in the oven 2 1/2 - 3 minutes. Remove from oven and place a pecan half on top and press slightly. Cool'm off and enjoy.
