Title:
Italian Restaurant-Style Salad Dressing I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/353394.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces olive oil vinaigrette dressing
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1 tablespoon white sugar

Directions:
Whisk together the salad dressing, garlic, basil, oregano and sugar. Refrigerate for 24 hours and serve chilled.

Submitter: 
Colleen
Description: 
This salad dressing is similar to the one served at a popular Italian restaurant chain.
