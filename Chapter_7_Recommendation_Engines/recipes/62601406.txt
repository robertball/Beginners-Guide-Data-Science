Title:
Lentil Pepperoni Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 1/2 c water
2 c dry lentils, rinsed
2 T olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, chopped
1 (6 oz.) pkg. sliced turkey pepperoni, quartered
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1 1/2 t salt
1/2 t dried oregano
1/2 t ground sage
1/4 t cayenne pepper
2 cans petite diced tomatoes
2 large celery ribs, chopped
2 large carrots, chopped

Directions:
Saute onions and garlic in olive oil over med-hi heat.  Add to crock pot.
Add all other ingredients to crock pot, mix well.
Cook on low for 8 hours.

Submitter: 
Andrea Patton
Description: 
Turkey pepperoni nicely spices this thick lentil stew with its rich tomato broth.Modified from: Lentil Pepperoni Stew
