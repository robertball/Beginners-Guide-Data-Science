Title:
Zucchini Saute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 red onion, diced
salt and pepper to taste
4 zucchini, halved and sliced
1 clove garlic, minced
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.  Saute onion with salt and pepper for 2 minutes.  Stir in zucchini. Add garlic and Italian seasoning.  Cook over medium high heat until heated through, but still firm. About 5 mins.

Submitter: 
Anne Soie
Description: 
Zucchini is sauteed with red onion, mushrooms, tomato and herbs.  You can use any veggies you like.  Serve over pasta, if desired.Modified from: Zucchini Saute
