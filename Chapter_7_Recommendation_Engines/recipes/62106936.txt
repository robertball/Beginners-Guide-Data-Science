Title:
Instant Oatmeal Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup milk
1/3 cup sugar
1/4 cup veg. oil
1 cup flour
3 packets of single-serve instant oatmeal (any flavor)
2 tsp baking powder
1 tbsp cinnamon, nutmeg, or some combination of the two
1/4 tsp allspice
1/2 tsp salt
1 tbsp white or brown sugar
(Optional) 1 cup of dried fruit or nuts, such as raisins, currants, chopped apple, dates, and walnuts. Fruit or nuts related to the selected oatmeal flavor work best.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 deg. F
Grease muffin pan or line with paper cups
Combine dried fruit/nuts, milk, 1/3 cup sugar, and oil
In a separate bowl, mx together flour, oatmeal, baking powder, cinnamon/nutmeg, allspice and salt
Combine bowls and stir just enough to mix well
Spoon evenly into muffin cups
Using the remaining 1 tbsp white or brown sugar, sprinkle 1/4 tsp over the top of each muffin batter
Bake for 20 minutes

Bryan
Description: 
I came up with this very easy recipe after testing some recommendations from around the Web. This is the easiest recipe I've seen for producing store-quality muffins.
