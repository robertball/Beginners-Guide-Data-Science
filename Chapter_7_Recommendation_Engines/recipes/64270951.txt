Title:
The best easy holiday stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
I box of cubed seasoned stuffing mix
1 box of stove top stuffing (prepared as directed)
2 large onions -diced
2- bunches of celery hearts -chopped
2 tbs fresh tyme
3 tbs fresh sage -finely chopped
1 lb Jimmy Dean Sage sausage browned
3-4 cups fresh chicken stock
1 1/2 sticks of butter softened and cut into 1
Inch pats

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Prepare stuffing and brown and drain sausage
Combine the rest in a large mixing bowl, add sausage,
And prepared stuffing, mix well.
Transfer to oiled casserole dish to with butter slices
and bake covered for 45 minutes! Yum
