Title:
Cinnamon roll copy-cats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup  white sugar
1/3 cup shortening
1 cup milk
8 tbsp. cinnamon
1 cup whipping cream
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup butter or margerine
1 tbsp. baking powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, 4 tbsp. cinnamon salt, and 1/2 cup sugar. Cut in the shortening until the mixture resembles coarse meal. Gradually stir in milk until dough pulls away from the side of the bowl.
Bake for about 10 min. , then remove from the oven and make a small well in the middle of each biscuit. Pour melted butter into each well and bake for an additional 10 min.
Meanwhile, beat whipping cream until it forms soft peaks. Slowly add in vanilla, remaining sugar and cinnamon. When the biscuits are done let cool and fill each well with sugar and top with cinnamon whipped cream.
