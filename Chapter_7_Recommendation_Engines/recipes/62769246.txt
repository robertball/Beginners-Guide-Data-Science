Title:
Indonesian Layer Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/642223.jpg
Ingredients:
375g butter
170g sugar
1/2 tin of condensed milk
2 tbsp brandy
18 egg yolks
6 egg whites
110g top flour / cake flour
1/2 tsp cream of tartar
1 tsp mixed spice

Directions:
1. Sift top flour and mix it with mixed spice
2. Cream butter together with condensed milk until creamy
3. Whisk egg yolks with 100g sugar until thick
4. Add the remaining sugar and 1/2 tsp of cream of tartar to egg whites. Use high speed to beat up the egg whites till stiff
5. Fold in alternately the egg yolk mixture and the egg white mixture to the creamed butter while adding a little of flour at a time. Lastly, add in the brandy.
6. Grease the bottom and sides of a 9" square tray. Place a piece of greaseproof paper to fit the base of the tin and grease again with butter.
7. Preheat oven at 200°C / 392°F. Place greased tray under grill for 1 minute.
8. Pour 3 tbsp of the batter into tray and spread mixture evenly. Bake for 5-7 minutes or till light brown. Remove tray from grill. Use a skewer or toothpick to prick holes on top of the cake to prevent air bubbles from forming. Repeat this step layer by layer till mixture is used up. Unmould cake at once. Turn it over on a cooling rack to cool for one hour.

Description: 
Indonesian layer cake is commonly known as kek lapis in Malaysia and Indonesia. It is made by adding many layers on top of one another, and meant to be eaten in small portions. So do not be intimidated by the crazy amount of eggs.
