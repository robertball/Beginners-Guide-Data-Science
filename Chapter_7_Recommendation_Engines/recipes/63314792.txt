Title:
Melanie's Smoked Paprika Chicken Thighs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
3 t smoked paprika
1 t cumin
2 t cayenne
2 T olive oil
Salt -- generous amount

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Wash chicken & pat dry, and place in a ziplock bag. In a small bowl mix all the seasoning & oil and pour into ziplock. Once all the chicken is well coated, place on a foil lined cookie sheet or broiler pan.
Cook for approximately 40 min (until crispy and cooked through to the bone).
