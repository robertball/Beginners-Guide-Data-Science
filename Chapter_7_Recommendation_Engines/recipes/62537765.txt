Title:
Jalapeno Infused Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1995806.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Med Sweet Potatoes or 2 Lg.
1-2 Jalapeno peppers (Fresh!)
~1/4 C softened BUTTER (USE THE REAL THING!)
Salt & Black Pepper
3 TBLS melted BUTTER

Directions:
Pre-Heat oven to 350F
Bake whole, Un-peeled Sweet Potatoes until tender (about 1 Hr)
Broil Jalapeno pepper until skin pops and loosens (not more that 10 min), place in paper bag and allow to cool. Remove skin, split and remove ALL white pith and ALL seeds! Dice Jalapeno pepper and set aside. (DO NOT TOUCH YOUR MOUTH, NOSE, OR EYES!! FOR THE REST OF THE DAY!!)
Peel & Mash cooked, slightly cooled Sweet Potatoes with softened butter - until smooth & creamy. Fold-in diced peppers, add salt and Blk pepper to taste.
Transfer to a Buttered baking dish and dot with  butter.
Bake until hot in the center - about 30+ mins.
