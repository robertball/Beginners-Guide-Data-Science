Title:
Pumpkin cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 yellow cake mix packet
1/3 cup veg. oil
1 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
Icing:
1 cup pumpkin puree
1 3/4 cup icing sugar
1/2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
a small amount of water (if needed)

Directions:
Combine all of the ingredients. Cook at 350 for 12 minutes, or until golden. Let cupcakes sit until they are room temperature. Arter sitting, drizzle the pumpkin icing on the top. Happy baking!!!!
