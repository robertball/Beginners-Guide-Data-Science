Title:
Berry Hemp Protein Smoothie Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3434539.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen blueberries
1/4 cup frozen strawberries
1/2 cup plain nonfat Greek yogurt
1 scoop hemp protein powder
Sweetener of choice

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a blender and blend on high until creamy and smooth.
Add smoothie to a bowl. Top with additional toppings as desired and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Wake up to a creamy Berry Hemp Smoothie Bowl laced with sweet berries, Greek yogurt and hemp protein powder! This gluten free smoothie bowl is a nutritious and refreshing meal or snack for warm weather and great for post workout recovery!
