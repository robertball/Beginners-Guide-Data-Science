Title:
Delicious Pulled Pork with a Kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4501016.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T cumin
1 T garlic powder
1 T onion powder
1 T Chili powder
1 T cayenne pepper
1 T salt
1 T pepper
1 T paprika
1/2 c. brown sugar
1 5-7 lb. pork roast
2 cans beef broth
3 T Worcestershire sauce
1 onion diced in large pieces
4-5 cloves garlic

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees
Combine the first 9 ingredients in a bowl
Place the pork roast in a large roasting pan
Generously rub the seasonings all over the pork roast, you may have extra rub left over
pour beef broth and Worcestershire sauce in the pan around the roast, not over the top
Put onions and peeled garlic in the broth
Bake in 300 degree oven 4-5 hours until it is easily shredded
Shred the pork in the broth and bake another 30 minutes to an hour until the meat soaks up the broth

Submitter: 
Jen Cooks For Fun
Description: 
Delicious pulled pork recipe with a little cayenne kick. This pork is great on commercials, sandwiches or right out of the pan!
