Title:
Liz's Nutella Truffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup hazelnuts, roasted
1/2 cup confectioner's sugar
14 oz chocolate wafer cookies
20 oz Nutella
12 oz semisweet chocolate chips
3 tbsp Crisco

Directions:
Mix first 4 ingredients, roll into balls, melt last 2 ingredients, dip former into latter.
