Title:
Completely citrus cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
3 cups flour
2 cups sugar
1 Tbsp baking powder
1 1/2 cups milk
1/2 cup soft butter
2 tsp finely grated lemon peel
2 1/2 Tbsp of lemon juice or juice of 1 lemon
Lemon curd filling:
3 egg yolks
1 cup sugar (divided)
6 Tbsp cornstarch
1/4 tsp salt
2 cups water
2 Tbsp Butter
2 tsp grated lemon peel
5 Tbsp lemon juice

Directions:
Cake:
Combine dry ingredients together including the lemon peel.
Add milk and butter. Beat until combined. Add the eggs until well combined.Add the lemon juice and mix in well. Pour into two greased and floured 9 inch pans. Bake at 375f for 25-30 min. Cool on a rack for 10 min before removing from pans. Remove from pans and cool completely on a wire rack.
Curd:
In a double boiler place 1/2 cup sugar, cornstarch, salt and water.
Cook until thickened stirring constantly
Add the egg yolks to the other 1/2 cup of sugar.
When the first mixture is thick add a large Tbsp of it to the egg and sugar mixture and then immediately add it to the rest of the double boiler, stir well and cook about 2 min. Remove the double boiler from the heat and add the lemon juice, peel and butter stirring it in quickly. remove the insert from the double boiler and set aside to cool.
