Title:
Home Made Chimichurri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup olive oil
2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar (white is nice)
2 tablespoons lime juice
2 cloves garlic or to personal taste
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon basil
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon sea salt
3 cups of loosely packed Italian (flat leaf) parsley leaves without the stems (about one bunch)

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a blender.
Blend well.

Submitter: 
TJ
Description: 
This is my version of a meat sauce popular with Argentineans.  I also use it for dipping bread and it's good poured over sliced tomatoes and cheese cubes or slices.Modified from: Tracy's Chimichurri
