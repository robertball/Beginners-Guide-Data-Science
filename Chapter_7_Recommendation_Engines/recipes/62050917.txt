Title:
Country Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 eggs
2 1/4 c. sugar
1 qt 1/2 and 1/2
5 c. milk
4 1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Beat eggs for about 5 minutes.  Add sugar and beat until mixture is very stiff.  Add rest of ingredients and pour into gallon freezer and freeze as directed.

Submitter: 
JAN
Description: 
This is the BEST and EASIEST I've ever made. You can make it with no fat 1/2 N 1/2 and it tastes just as great. I use 2% milk.  Add fruit or leave plain, excellent either way.
