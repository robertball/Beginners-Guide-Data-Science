Title:
beer boiled corn on the cob Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 ears of corn
1/4 tsp salt
2 TBS of butter
3/4 cups of beer
1/4 tsp of old bay seasoning

Directions:
Bringing beer to a boil in a large pot add 6 ears of corn salt butter beer and old bay seasoning reduce heat to low and steam for 15 minutes or until tender
