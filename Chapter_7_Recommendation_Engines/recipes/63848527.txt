Title:
Tequila and Orange Liqueur Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Kilo boneless skinless chicken breasts
6 large cloves of garlic, minced
The juice of 6 large limes
6 Tbsp olive oil
1/4 cup tequila
1/4 cup orange liqueur
1/4 cup cilantro, minced
1/4 cup parsley, minced
1/4 cup onion, minced
1/2 tsp cayenne
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Perforate chicken breasts with a fork to facilitate marinating process.  Set aside.
Mix all other ingredients in a mixing bowl.
Place chicken breasts into a zip loc bag and pour marinade over chicken.
Marinate for up to 6 hours in the refrigerator.
Pre heat bar b q to 375* F.
Grill over medium hot bar b q for 8 minutes a side or until juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
A great marinade for chicken or seafood.
