Title:
Javanese Pork Tenderloin with Spicy Peanut Slather Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pork tenderloins about 12 oz each
Cooking oil to brush on rack
Peanut slather...
1/2 c chunky peanut butter
1/4 c rice wine or dry sherry
2 tbsp dark soy sauce
2 tbsp white wine vinegar
2 tbsp honey
2 tbsp canola oil
1 tbsp dark sesame oil
1 tbsp Sambai Badjsk fresh ripe chili paste with onion or other Asian chili sauce
2 tbsp finely minced ginger
1/4 c minced green onion
1/4 minced cilantro

Directions:
Trim and discard excess fat from tenderloins. Make one cut lengthwise from end to end of tenderloin cutting half-way thru the meat. Flatten pork and transfer to a container.
In small bowl, combine all ingredients for slather.
30 minutes before grilling, bring meat and slather to room temperature. Use half of slather to spread on meat
Preheat grill to 350 degrees and brush rack with oil. Lay pork flat or open on grill. Cover grill and regulate to remain medium. Grill until meat thermometer registers 150 degrees, @ 12-15 minutes.
Let pork rest for 5 minutes.
Slice pork and serve with remaining slather.
