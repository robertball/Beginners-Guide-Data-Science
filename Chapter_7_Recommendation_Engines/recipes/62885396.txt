Title:
Taco Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (16 ounces) refried beans
1 package (1.25 ounces) taco seasoning mix
1 container (8 ounces) sour cream
1 cup (4 ounces) shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup salsa, any variety
1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilies
1 large green onions, sliced
tortilla chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Combine beans and taco seasoning mix in a small bowl. Spread bean mixture in 8-inch square baking dish or shallow serving dish.
Top with salsa, sour cream, cheese, green chilies, and green onions.
Bake for 15 minutes in preheated oven.

Submitter:

Description: 
Quick and easy and great for parties. Serve with tortilla chips.
