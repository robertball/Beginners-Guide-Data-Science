Title:
Kathy's Harvest Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T oil
1/4 c chopped onion
1/2 c chopped lean ham (optional)
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 c. Fresh green beans, cut diagonally in 2 inch pieces
3. Carrots, sliced
6 green onions, white part only, sliced
1 stalk celery, chopped
3 cans (14.5 oz each) reduced sodium chicken broth
1 T chopped fresh parsley
1/4 t dried thyme
1/4 t dried basil
1/4 t white pepper
1/2 black peppercorns, cracked
2 chicken breast halves
1 T prepared mustard. (yellow)
2 c rice. (experiment).

Directions:
Heat oil in soup pan; add onion, ham, and garlic.  Cook 2-3 minutes. Add green beans, carrots, green onions, celery, rice, and broth. Bring to a boil. Add parsley, thyme, basil, pepper, and peppercorns. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 20 minutes or until vegetables are desired tenderness.  Cut chicken into bite sized pieces, add chicken and mustard to soup; simmer about 10 minutes or until chicken is tender.
