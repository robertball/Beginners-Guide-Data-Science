Title:
Barbie's Turkey White Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz ground turkey
48 oz jar Great Northern Beans
2 16 oz jar garbanzo beans
1/2 can sweet yellow corn, drained
1 can chicken broth
2 10 oz. cans RoTel Diced tomatoes Original Diced Tomatoes & Green Chilies
12 oz block pepper jack cheese, cubed

Directions:
In a skillet on medium heat, brown ground turkey until cooked through.  In a large pot, combine ground turkey with remaining ingredients and cook over medium heat until cheese is melted, approximately 20 min. Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Barbie Russell
Description: 
This is a quick recipe that's easy to make when serving lots of people. It's also a good one to make ahead and freeze in lunch sized portions. My husband and I love it!
