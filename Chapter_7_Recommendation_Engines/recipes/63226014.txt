Title:
Mrs. Flick's Creamy potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium potatoes, sliced and peeled
3 T. butter
1 small onion(about 1/3 c.),minced
3 T. flour
2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
2 1/2 c. milk

Directions:
Saute onions and butter until tender crisp.
Add flour, salt, and pepper to onions.
Spread sliced and peeled potatoes in a foil-lined baking dish.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
