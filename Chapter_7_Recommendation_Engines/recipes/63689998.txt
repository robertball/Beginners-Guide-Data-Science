Title:
Big Ray's Bacon Spinach Quiche on Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1130198.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups mashed potatoes
3 ounces bacon
1/2 small onion
1/2 Tbl olive oil
2 eggs
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup heavy cream
2 cups fresh spinach
3 ounces fetta cheese, crumbled
1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F. Butter and flour 9 inch pie pan.
Spread mashed potatoes into the prepared pie pan. Press firmly on the bottom of the pan and bake for 15 minutes.
As crust is baking, slice bacon and onion thinly. In a skillet, heat 1/2 tablespoon olive oil and cook onion and bacon until tender. Turn off the heat and add fresh spinach. Stir lightly and remove from the heat.
In a bowl, beat eggs and add milk, heavy cream, salt and pepper. Whisk well.
When mashed potatoes crust has baked, put crumbled fetta cheese on the mashed potatoes crust, put saut√©ed bacon, onion and spinach, pour egg mixture and sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese. Bake at 400 degrees F for 20 minutes.
