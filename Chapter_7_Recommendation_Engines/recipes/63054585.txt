Title:
Crock Pot Czech Beef Stew with Gherkins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup crisp bacon pieces
2 1/2 lbs. stewing beef
salt and pepper
2 cups sliced mushrooms
1 large onion, sliced
1 tbsp. paprika
1/2 cup dry red wine
1 1/2 cups beef broth
1 small jar gherkins, cut in half lengthwise

Directions:
Saute onions and mushrooms in a little olive oil until they start to brown. Add to crock-pot. Add all the ingredients to the crock-pot except the gherkins and bacon. Cover and cook for 4-5 hours on medium. Stir in pickles and bacon. Thicken if you wish and serve over hot rice.
