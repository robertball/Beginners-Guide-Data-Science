Title:
DILL AND GARLIC REFRIGERATOR PICKELS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/700998.jpg
Ingredients:
12 cups water
4 cups white distilled vinegar
2/3 cup canning salt
1/2 cup sugar
12 cloves minced garlic
fresh dill[3 sprigs per container or 2 per jar]
2 Tbsp.dried chilies[omit if you don't want them spicy.
16 cups cucumbers
1 1/4 tsp.Alum

Directions:
Combine water,vinegar,salt and sugar,bring to a boil and keep hot.
Scrub cucumbers and slice or cut into wedges[if using jars.]
Layer cucumbers, dill and garlic in large container.If using jars fill with wedged cucumbers[or sliced]
Sprinkle chilies and alum over the top.
Pour hot brine over cucumbers,cover or seal jars.
Let cool,refrigerate.
Refrigerate for at least a week before sampling.
If kept in a airtight container,pickles will keep for months.
If doubling recipe in a container, no need to double brine.
