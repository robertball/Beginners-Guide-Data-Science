Title:
Barley Hot Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb Ground Beef or Turkey
1 Medium Onion
1-2 Cloves Garlic (to taste)
1 Can Whole Corn drained drained
1 Can Tomato Sauce
2 Can Tomato Soup
2 Can Cream of Mushroom soup
1 Can Peas and Carrots drained
1 Can Diced Tomatoes Italian
1 Can Mushrooms drained
1 Can Green Beans drained
1 Cup Barley / Rice / Pasta uncooked

Directions:
Cook Barley / Rice / Pasta according to package directions
Brown the ground beef / turkey with onion and garlic. Drain grease.
Combine all ingredients in large casserole dish (9X13 or bigger)
Cover with foil and cook in the oven at 375 for 60 minutes (place a cookie sheet underneath to catch any splatters). Will thicken as you cook.
*You can also cook it in a large stock pot on the stove top for 60 minutes or in crock pot on low for 6-8 hours.

Submitter: 
Beth
Description: 
This is a very thick, healthy and filling stew type dish. It also freezes very well to be re-heated for a quick meal. It can be cooked in the oven, on the stove top or in a crock pot. You can subsitute Rice or Pasta for the Barley.
