Title:
greek spagetti and meatsauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 medium fine chopped onion
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 tablespoons tomato paste
1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
dash  of allspice
salt,pepper to taste
1 box spagetti

Directions:
prepare spagetti as directed on box.drain,toss with butter
saute onion with 1 tablespoon of olive oil till soft,add ground beef and brown ,drain oil.
return to heat add tomato paste ,garlic, salt, pepper and allspice along with 4 ozs water,reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 mins .
serve over spagetti
