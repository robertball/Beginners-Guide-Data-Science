Title:
Thanksgiving in a Pan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991120.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) package stuffing mix
2 1/2 cups cubed cooked turkey
2 cups frozen cut green beans, thawed
1 (12 ounce) jar turkey gravy
pepper to taste

Directions:
Prepare stuffing mix according to package directions. Transfer to a greased 11-in. x 7-in. x 2-in. baking dish. Top with turkey, beans, gravy and pepper. Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 30-35 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Lynne  Hahn
Description: 
'This meal-in-one tastes like a big holiday dinner without the work,' writes Lynne Hahn of Temecula, California. It's a great way to use up leftover turkey, but Lynne says she often uses thick slices of deli turkey instead.
