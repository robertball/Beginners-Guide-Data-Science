Title:
Gluten Free AP Mix with Sorghum Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c brown rice flour
1c white rice flour
1c sorghum flour
1 and 1/3c potato starch
2/3 cup tapioca starch
10g agar agar powder

Directions:
Mix together with whisk attachment on KA mixer setting 2 for 10 minutes. Keep in airtight container.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
All-purpose flour to be used for most all baking such as; biscuits, breads, pizzas, crackers, cookies, brownies, etc.  To make into cake flour add 1 cup corn starch and 1/2 cup sweet rice flour and sift twice.
