Title:
Whipped Feta Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5755427.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup crumbled feta cheese
1 cup cream cheese
2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley
1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
1 teaspoon minced fresh marjoram
1/2 teaspoon minced fresh thyme
1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine feta cheese, cream cheese, parsley, lemon juice, marjoram, thyme, garlic, and pepper in a food processor. Process until well mixed; spread will have a grainy look.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
This is an amazing feta spread. Great to serve as an appetizer for a party. It makes a lot! Serve with thinly sliced baguette, toasted.
