Title:
Tarragon Scallops in a Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 large sea scallops, rinsed
2 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp butter
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tbsp fresh tarragon, chopped
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 cup white wine
1/2 cup cream

Directions:
In a large skillet, melt butter and olive oil together over medium heat.  Add garlic and tarragon and saute until garlic is slightly brown, about 5 minutes.  Stir in salt and pepper.  Place scallops in the skillet and cook until brown on each side, approximately 4 minutes per side.  Remove scallops from skillet.  Add wine to skillet and deglaze the pan.  Bring to a boil and reduce the liquid to half.  Remove from heat and gradually stir in cream.  Return skillet to heat and cook over medium heat for 5 minutes.  Add scallops and heat through.  Serve with risotto or rice.

Submitter: 
RJG
Description: 
Sauteed scallops with tarragon, white wine and cream.  Wonderful with risotto and fresh asparagus.
