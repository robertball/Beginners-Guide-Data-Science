Title:
Taco-Stuffed Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Medium Zucchinis, Ends Trimmed, Cut in half lengthwise
1 TBSP Coconut oil or Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 lb Lean Ground Beef
1/2 cup Finely Chopped Onion
2 Tsp Chili Powder
1 Tsp Ground Cumin
2 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped
1 TBSP Tomato Paste
1/2 Cup Mild Salsa
Sea Salt, to taste
For Garnish:
Chopped Scallions
Chopped Cilantro or Parsley
Slices Black Olives
Diced Avocado

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375F. Bring a large pot of water to boil.
Scoop the seeds from the zucchini halves using a spoon or melon baller, leaving a 1/4-inch-thick shell. Place the zucchini in the boiling water and cook 1 minute. Remove from the water and arrange the zucchini, hallow side up, in a large glass baking dish or on a rimmed baking sheet.
Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Add the ground beef. Cook,breaking up with wooden utensil, until almost cooked through-about 5 minutes. Reduce the heat to medium and add onion, chili Powder,cumin, garlic and tomato paste. Cook Stirring often, for 3-5 minutes. Stir in the salsa. Remove from the heat and season to taste with salt.
Divide the meat among the hollowed-out zucchini halves. Cover with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes. Sprinkle with garnish and serve.
