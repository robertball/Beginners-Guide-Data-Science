Title:
Perfect Pitcher of Pink Raspberry Cosmopolitans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/584678.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup raspberry vodka
1/2 cup triple sec
1/2 cup sweetened lime juice (such as Rose'sÂ®)
3 cups cran-raspberry juice
1 lime, sliced

Directions:
Fill a pitcher with ice, and pour in the vodka, triple sec, lime juice, and cran-raspberry juice.  Stir to mix.  Serve garnished with a lime slice that has been rubbed around the glass edge.

Submitter:

Description: 
Having the girls over?  This is the perfect party drink and you can count on it disappearing.  Using Rose's lime juice prevents the cloudiness of real lime, it's so pretty.  Sweet without the harsh taste of alcohol.  Be careful of addiction!  It's that good.
