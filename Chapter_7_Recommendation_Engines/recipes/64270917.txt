Title:
Quick Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can, crushed tomatoes (drain mostly)
1 can Rotel, original (hot if that is the goal)
1 tbsp dehydrated onions, reconstituted and drained
1 tsp dehydrated bell pepper, reconstituted and drained
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cinnamon
pars;ey, cilantro, rosemary to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together thoroughly.
Let the mixture sit for at least an hour to meld flavors.

Bryant Evans

A quick recipe that's faster than going to Walmart.
