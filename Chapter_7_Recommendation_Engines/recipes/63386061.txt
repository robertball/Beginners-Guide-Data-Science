Title:
Spiced Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 T water
1/8 t turmeric
2 c cooked rice
1/2 t oil
1/2 t mustard seed
1 small onion,chopped
dash ginger powder
1/8 t ground cumin seed
1/4 t coriander
1/2 t chili powder
1 clove garlic, chopped small
1/2 c tomato, diced
1/4 c cilantro, chopped small

Directions:
Put oil in pan, saute the mustard seed at medium heat until they start moving.  mix water with turmeric powder in a bowl, then pour cooked rice.  stir, set aside.  as soon as mustard is sauted, add remaining spices and mix together.  Add garlic and saute.  Add tomato and cilatro and stir.  Add rice together.  Heat thoroughly.
