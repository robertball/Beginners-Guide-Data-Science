Title:
Greek Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp oregano
3-4 tbsp salt
4-5 tbsp pepper
1/4 cup olive oil
6 cups diced yukon gold potatoes

Directions:
wash and chop potatoes into cubes and mix in bowl with all above ingredients. Cook in oven at 425 degrees for 45 minutes.
