Title:
Watermelon Vidalia® Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/657439.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups diced watermelon
1 cup chopped sweet onion (such as Vidalia®)
1 serrano chile pepper, diced
1 serrano chile pepper, seeded and diced
1 clove garlic, minced
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1/4 teaspoon coarse salt
2 pinches garlic powder

Directions:
Combine the watermelon, onion, serrano chile peppers, garlic, and cilantro in a large bowl; add the salt and garlic powder and stir to combine. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
GRECKLE
Description: 
A salsa made with watermelon and Vidalia® onion. To make it less spicy, remove all seeds from the peppers -- or you could use an equal quantity of a milder pepper such as poblano.
