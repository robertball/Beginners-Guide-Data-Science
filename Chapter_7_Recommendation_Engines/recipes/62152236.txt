Title:
Gluten Free Savoury Zucchini Slice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Medium Zucchinis coursley grated
1 medium carrot, grated
1 cup of grated tasty cheese
1 Medium onion chopped
1/2 cup of olive oil
1 cup of Gluten Free Self Raising Flour
5 Lightly Beaten eggs
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.  Pour into a greased slice tray and bake for 30-40 minutes at 180degrees.  Allow to cool before removing from pan.

Submitter: 
CZARDAS
Description: 
This is really easy to make and handy to cut into serves and freeze for lunches during the week.  Ideal for people with Celiacs disease.  Make sure to use Gluten Free flour though
