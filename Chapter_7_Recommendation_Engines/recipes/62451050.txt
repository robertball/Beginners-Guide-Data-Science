Title:
Smoked Salmon Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. sweet potatoes cut int 1/2-inch cubes
1 T. no-salt Cajun seasoning
1 1/2 T. olive oil
2 tsp. tamari
6 c. mixed salad greens (3-4 oz)
1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
1/2 yellow bell pepper, sliced
1/4 small red onion, very thinly sliced
6 sun-dried tomatoes (oil packed), thinly sliced
3 oz sunflower sprouts
3/4 c. pecans, toasted and chopped
6-8 oz smoked salmon
dressing:
2 T. olive oil
1 1/2 T. basalmic vinegar
1 T. whole-grain mustard
1/4 tsp. salt; dash pepper
1 tsp. agave

Directions:
Steam potatoes until cooked but not mushy.  (10 min.?) Place in a bowl to cool and dry for about 10 minutes.  Sprinkle Cajun, oil, and tamari over potatoes and stir.
Heat a skillet over medium-high heat.  Pour sweet potato mix into skillet and cook, stirring often and carefully until potatoes are toasted.
Place salad greens in a large bowl.  Add peppers, onion, tomatoes, and sprouts.  Add cooled sweet potatoes and pecans.  Cut salmon into bite-sized pieces and add.
Combine dressing ingredients and blend well.  Pour over salad and toss.  Serve
