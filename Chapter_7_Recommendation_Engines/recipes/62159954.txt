Title:
California Club Turkey Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Slices Toasted Wheat Bread
3-4 Slices Sliced Smoked Turkey Meat
3 Slices Avocado
1-2 Tbs Cream Cheese
1 Tbs Sunflower Seeds
1 tsp Mayo

Directions:
Assemble Mayo and turkey on one side of bread, and cream cheese, sunflower seeds, and avocado on the other, put together, and serve with chips or a pickle.  Great summer sandwich!

Submitter: 
Brooke129
Description: 
Not your typical Turkey Sandwich!  With avocado, cream cheese, and sunflower seeds, on toasted wheat bread, your turkey sandwich won't know what hit it!
