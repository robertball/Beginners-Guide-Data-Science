Title:
Spicy Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1126695.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Pounds Extra Lean Ground Beef, 1 Sleeve Ritz Crackers (crushed), 1/2 cup diced onions, 1/2 cup diced jalapenos, 2 cups Shredded pepper jack cheese, 1 pkg Hot taco seasoning mix, 1 pkg Fiesta ranch mix (dry), 2 eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cover cookie sheet with aluminum foil. In a large mixing bowl add beef, ritz crackers, onions, jalapenos, shredded cheese, taco seasoning, ranch packet, and eggs. Mix all the ingredients together until fully mixed.

Submitter: 
Val's Mom
Description: 
I don't like meatloaf, but my roommate does, so we got together and chose ingredients to add flavors we both like. Everyone I serve this to says it is the first meatloaf they did not smother in ketchup.
