Title:
Ginnies potato salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10-15 potatoes
8 eggs
2 Tbl vinegar
1/4 C sugar
salt pepper
1 Tbl mustard
1 C mayo
1/4 C onion grated
1 can peas
1-2 grated carrots

Directions:
Cook potatoes with skins on and boil eggs. Let cool in refrigerator over night. Combine the rest of the ingredients except the peas (sauce). Mix thoroughly. Chop the eggs and potatoes. Pour the sauce over the potatoes and eggs. Fold in the peas.
