Title:
Chicken Pesto Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground chicken breast (give or take)
2 tablespoons basil pesto
1 clove minced garlic
1/4 cup minced onion
1/4 cup grated parmesan
1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
Pinch red pepper flakes
1/4 cup bread crumbs
Salt & pepper

Directions:
Mix together allingredients in a bowl until evenly blended.
Form into 4 patties.  Grill pesto burgers until no longer pink in the center, about 5 minutes per side.

Description: 
Prepared pesto adds great flavor to ground chicken in these moist and delicious burgers.Modified from: Pesto Turkey Burgers
