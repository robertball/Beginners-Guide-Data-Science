Title:
Traci's Apricot Bellini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 ounces champagne
1 ounce apricot liqueur

Directions:
Chill champagne and liqueur until cold, at least 1 hour. Pour liqueur into fluted glass; add champagne to the glass and stir.

Submitter: 
Traci-in-Cali
Description: 
This is a delicious cocktail, looks elegant in flute glasses and can also be made using other flavors of liquor.
