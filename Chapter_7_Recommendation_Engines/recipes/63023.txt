Title:
Raspberry Peach Blender Breakfast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups soy milk
1 cup lowfat plain yogurt
1 cup frozen unsweetened sliced peaches
1 cup frozen unsweetened red raspberries
1/2 cup Equal® Spoonful*
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 teaspoon almond extract
Fresh mint (optional)

Directions:
Place all ingredients in blender container. Cover with lid. Process until smooth. Serve immediately in tall glasses. Garnish with fresh mint, if desired.

Submitter: 
EQUAL®
Description: 
A rosy colored breakfast drink that will help you welcome the day. It's easily prepared with frozen unsweetened sliced peaches and red raspberries to flavor and add texture to plain yogurt and soy milk.
