Title:
Honey-Walnut Pound Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package Betty Crocker ® SuperMoist butter recipe yellow cake mix
1 cup water
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/3 cup butter, softened
3 eggs
1/4 cup honey
1 (16 ounce) can Betty Crocker ® Rich and Creamy butter cream ready-to-spread frosting

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour 2 loaf pans, 8 1/2x4 1/2x2 1/2 or 9x5x3 inches.
Beat cake mix (dry) and remaining ingredients except frosting in large bowl on low speed 30 seconds. Beat on medium speed 2 minutes. Pour into pans.
Bake 8-inch loaves 50 to 60 minutes, 9-inch loaves 40 to 45 minutes, or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 10 minutes; remove from pans. Cool completely, about 1 hour.
Place frosting in microwavable bowl. Microwave uncovered on Medium (50%) 15 seconds. Spread frosting over tops of loaves, allowing some to drizzle down sides.

Submitter: 
Betty Crocker®
Description: 
Pound cakes freeze beautifully! Wrap the unfrosted cakes tightly and label them. Freeze up to 3 or 4 months. To thaw, loosen the wrap and thaw at room temperature 2 to 3 hours.
