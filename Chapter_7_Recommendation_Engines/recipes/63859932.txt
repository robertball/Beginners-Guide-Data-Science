Title:
Pumpkin Glaze for Cookies, Cakes and Donuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1071218.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound (4 1/2 cups) powdered sugar, sifted
2 teaspoons light corn syrup
1/4 teaspoon table salt
1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
1/4 cup canned pumpkin
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 cup hot whole milk

Directions:
Whisk together all the ingredients except for the milk.
Slowly add the hot milk, whisking constantly, until the mixture is smooth.
This step can also be done in the bowl of a stand mixer or by hand.

Submitter: 
Dianna Jacobs-Fresh
Description: 
This glaze I use on my chocolate pumpkin bunt cake and its amazingly delicious.
