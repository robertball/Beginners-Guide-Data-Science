Title:
4B's Cream of Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/636448.jpg
Ingredients:
1 - 28 ounce can diced tomatoes, including juice
2 cups chicken broth
2 tbs butter
2 tbs sugar
1/2 cup diced white onion
Pinch baking soda
16 oz sour cream
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients EXCEPT cream into a large pot.
Cover and simmer for 1 hour, checking occasionally/stir.
Heat cream until it is almost ready to boil. Pour into tomato mixture and stir.
Add salt and pepper to taste and serve.

Submitter: 
Nicole Skorka
Description: 
If you're from Montana you will know about this soup!  The 4B's restaurant cream of tomato soup is famous and now you can make it at home!  If you know somebody who doesn't like tomato soup, get them to try this, it will change their mind!
