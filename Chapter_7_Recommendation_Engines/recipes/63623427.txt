Title:
Chocolate (Coconut) Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box German chocolate cake
2 packages coconut pudding
3/4  cup coffee cooled to room temperature
16 oz. cool whip
1 cup chopped Mounds bars
(Make sure to check out the alternatives!!)

Directions:
Bake chocolate cake according to directions on box. Let it cool once done.
Prepare the pudding according to the box.
Crumble a third of the cooled cake into a large glass bowl.
Pour a third of the coffee on top of the cake, then spoon a third of the pudding on top of that.
Add a third of the cool whip on top of the pudding, and sprinkle some of the chopped Mounds bars on top of the cool whip.
Repeat three times until all ingredients are layered in the bowl.

Submitter: 
Julia L
Description: 
I am absolutely in love with trifle, and this is definitely the reason why! If coconut is not your thing, there's an alternative recipe at the bottom so everyone can enjoy this delectable dessert.
