Title:
Rudy's Mexican Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/351873.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup long grain white rice
2 cups water
4 oz of canned tomato sauce
1/3 cup cooking oil
3/4 cup spanish onion, diced
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Saute rice in oil first until lightly browned
Add diced onion, saute mixture until rice is golden brown
Add tomato sauce, stir for 1 minute
Add water, bring to boil, stir
Add salt
Simmer for about 20 minutes.
Add 1/4 cup of cold water, simmer for another 5 minutes.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Rudy Torrijos III
Description: 
This is my grandmother's mexican rice recipe. The simplest is the best when it comes to authentic Mexican food.
