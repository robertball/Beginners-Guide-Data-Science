Title:
Simon and Garfunkel Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1-3 Pound Roast
2 12oz Jars of Heinz Beef Gravy
1/8 Cup extra virgin olive oil
1 tablespoon thyme
1 tablespoon rosemary
1 tablespoon sage
1 tablespoon onion salt
Vegetables as desired

Directions:
Pour the 2 jars of gravy into the slow cooker, then add two jar fulls of water to it. Add in HALF of the dry spices and the vegetables. In a seperate bowl, combine the other HALF of the spices and the olive oil. Rub the roast with olive oil and spices. Slow cook on low heat 8-9 hours, high heat 4-5 hours.
