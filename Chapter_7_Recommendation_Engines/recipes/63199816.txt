Title:
Mrs. Fortins Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can whole sweet corn
1 can cream corn
1 pkg softened philly cream cheese
1 stick softened butter (real)
1 egg
1 pkg jiffy corn muffin mix
shredded cheddar cheese (top)

Directions:
mix the sweet corn (undrained), cream corn,egg philly cream cheese and butter and jiffy corn muffin mix together; using a mixing beater. Using a glass baking dish lightly buttered top with shredded cheddar cheese cover with aluminum foil ( losely)and bake 350 degrees for 1 hour

Submitter: 
Chef Scott
Description: 
A savory blend of corn /corn meal and cheese for a savory side dish with meatloaf,ham or turkey dinners.
