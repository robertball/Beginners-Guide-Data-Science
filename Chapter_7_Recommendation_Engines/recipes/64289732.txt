Title:
Spicy Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 10 ounce can diced tomatoes and green chiles
1 (14 ounce) can petite diced tomatoes
4 cups chicken broth
1 cup whole corn kernels, cooked
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
2 boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cut into bite-sized pieces
crushed tortilla chips
sliced avocado
shredded Monterey Jack cheese
chopped green onions

Directions:
Saute onion and garlic in hot oil in a medium stock pot until soft.  Add chili powder, oregano, diced tomatoes and chiles, tomatoes, and broth. Bring to a boil, and simmer 5 to 10 minutes.
Stir in corn, beans, cilantro, and chicken. Simmer for 10 minutes.
Ladle soup into individual serving bowls, and top with crushed tortilla chips, avocado slices, cheese, and chopped green onion.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
This is my favorite soup to make when a good cold snap sets in. The spicy chilies makes it double warming!Modified from: Chicken Tortilla Soup I
