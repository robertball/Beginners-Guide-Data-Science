Title:
cubed steak and wild rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Uncle Ben's) Long grain and Wild Rice (or equivilent brand)
1 package cubed steak (minute steak)
4 or 5 Mushrooms
2 Tbs. Worcestershire Sauce
2 Tbs. Garlic Powder (or more)
1 Tbs. Onion Powder
2 Tbs, Butter or Margarine
2 cups water

Directions:
Cut cube steak into bite size pieces
Saute cubes of beef in butter, till done.
Add Rice, Spices, Worcestershire Sauce, Mushrooms and Water.
Cover and simmer till water is absorbed, about 25 minutes.
That's all there is to it!
Told you it was quick and easy.

Submitter: 
GRAYJETMAN
Description: 
A very quick, easy and inexpensive main dish to prepare.
You can top this wih sour cream before serving, if you'd like.
Depending who will dine with me, I add some red pepper flakes for added pizzaz.
