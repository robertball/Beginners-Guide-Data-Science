Title:
Broccoli Pasta Toss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup small broccoli florettes
1 1/3 cup cooked shell pasta (or bowtie)
1/3 cup chopped red onion (aka bermuda onion)
1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
1/2 cup raisins
Dressing
1/3 cup reduced calorie mayonnaise
1 TBSP apple cider vinegar
1/2 tsp sugar
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Cook pasta per recipe and cool
2. Combine all salad ingredients
3. Combine all dressing ingredients
4. Toss salad and dressing ingredients
