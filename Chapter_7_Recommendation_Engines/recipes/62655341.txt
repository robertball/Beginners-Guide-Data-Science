Title:
Pot de Creme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup (dark) chocolate chips
2 T sugar
pinch salt
1 egg
1 t. vanilla
3/4 cup half & half (or milk)

Directions:
Put first 5 ingredients in blender and turn off/on a few times to distribute egg. Heat half & half just to boiling. Turn on blender and gradually add heated half and half. Blend one minute. Pour into dessert dishes and chill in refer. Can garnish with whipped cream.
