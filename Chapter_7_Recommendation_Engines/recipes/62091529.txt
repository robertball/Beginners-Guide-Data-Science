Title:
Rice with Veggies and Chorizo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp vegetable oil
1 large onion, diced
2 red bell peppers, diced
1 green bell pepper, diced
4 chorizo sausages, cut into medium chunks
2 cups white rice
4 cups beef broth
2 tomatoes, seeded and diced
1 cup frozen peas
1 cup frozen corn
1 cup

Directions:
Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.  Add the onion and red and green bell pepper and cook until the onions begin to soften.  Add the chorizo and cook a few mintues more.
Stir the rice into the pot with the onions, peppers, and chorizo.  Cook, stirring frequently, for about 2 minutes.  Add the 4 cups of beef broth.  Increase the heat and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to low and simmer, covered for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally and adding water as needed.  Stir in the frozen corn and peas and the chopped tomatoes.  Simmer about 10 minutes more, stirring occasionally and adding water as necesssary, until the rice is cooked through.

Submitter: 
Dulce
Description: 
I used to eat this hearty rice for lunch almost daily.  It reheats well for easy leftovers.  Use flavorful chorizo for best results.  Add a jalapeno or two if you're feeling spicy!
