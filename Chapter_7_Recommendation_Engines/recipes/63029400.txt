Title:
Festive Frangelico Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/759797.jpg
Ingredients:
Festive Frangelico Cake
1 (18.25 ounce) box yellow cake mix
1 small box instant vanilla pudding mix
4 eggs
1/2 cup cold water
1/2 cup oil
1/2 cup Frangelico
Glaze:
1/8 pound butter
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/8 cup water
1/4 cup Frangelico

Directions:
Combine cake ingredients in a large bowl; beat at medium speed for 3 minutes (until very pale yellow). Pour into a greased and floured 10-inch pan. Bake at 350 degrees F for 45-60 minutes.  My over takes right @ 46 minutes.
Glaze:
Melt butter in saucepan. Stir in water and sugar. Boil 3 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Stir in Frangelico.
Set on rack to cool (no more than 5 minutes). Invert on serving plate. Prick cake all over with wooden pick; brush glaze evenly over top and sides.
