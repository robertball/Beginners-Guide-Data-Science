Title:
Fruit Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRUST:
1/2 cup oil
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 egg
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp cream of tartar
1/4 tsp salt
2-1/4 cups flour
TOPPING:
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
GLAZE:
1 cup sugar
2 Tbsp cornstarch
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 cup orange or pineapple juice
1/2 cup water
Dash of salt

Directions:
Crust:  Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.  Combine oil, sugar, powdered sugar and butter and mix well.  Mix in egg.  Add baking powder, cream of tartar, salt and flour and mix well.  Spread on large baking sheet and bake for 12 minutes.  Cool.
Topping:  Mix cream cheese, sugar and vanilla.  Spread over crust.  Then top with assorted fruit (berries, melon, peaches, oranges, bananas).
Glaze:  In a small saucepan, combine glaze ingredients. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly.  Cool slightly and pour over fruit pizza.  Refrigerate overnight if possible.
