Title:
Overnight Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup steel cut oats
1-2 cups dried fruit (whatever you prefer; cranberries, cherries, figs are some of my favorites)
2 cups almond milk
2 1/2 cups water
3 T. brown sugar (you can also use maple syrup or leave this out completely, depending on how sweet you like your oatmeal)
1 t. vanilla
1 t. cinnamon
optional:
1 cup applesauce or pumpkin

Directions:
In a slow cooker, combine all ingredients and set to low heat. Cover and let cook for 8 to 9 hours.

Submitter: 
holly
Description: 
throw this in the crockpot the night before and have a delicious breakfast waiting for you in the morning. reheats well too!
