Title:
Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 c. peeled & sliced apples.
1/4 c. water
1/2 c. flour
1/2 c. brown sugar
1/4 c. sugar
1/2 t. cinnamon
1/2 t. salt
1/4 c. margarine or butter

Directions:
Put apples in 8x8x2 baking dish and pour water over them.
Mix together the flour, sugars, cinnamon, and salt.
Cut 1/4 c. margarine or butter into dry mixture.
Blend/mash with fork until mixture is like corn meal.
Put on top of apples.
Bake at 350 for 40 minutes or until apples are done.
