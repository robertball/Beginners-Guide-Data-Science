Title:
Hedgehog Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982262.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup finely chopped walnuts
1/2 cup finely chopped dates
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 cup flaked coconut, divided
1 egg, lightly beaten

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the walnuts, dates and brown sugar. Add 1/2 cup coconut and the egg; mix well. Shape into 1-in. balls; roll in remaining coconut. Place on greased baking sheets. Bake at 350 degrees F for 12-13 minutes or until lightly browned. Remove to wire racks to cool.

Submitter: 
Sandra Pichon
Description: 
These little treats are as cute as they can be and delicious, too. A lady from my garden club shared this recipe one Christmas, but they're good for any occasion. They're a hit with both kids and grown-ups.  -Sandi Pichon, Slidell, Louisiana
