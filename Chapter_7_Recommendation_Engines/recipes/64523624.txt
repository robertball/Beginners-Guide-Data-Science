Title:
Thai Red Curry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2949034.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbsp red curry paste
1 (15 ounce) can coconut milk
1/4 cup fresh basil
1/4 cup bamboo shoot
2 tsp fish sauce
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1/3 cup chicken stock
1/2 cup shitake mushroom
1/2 red bell pepper - chopped 1" pieces
1/2 green pepper - chopped 1" pieces
1/2 yellow pepper - chopped 1" pieces
1/2 onion (yellow or white) chopped
2 chicken breasts

Directions:
In a large saucepan saute chicken breasts until partly cooked through.  Add the onions, and peppers and continue saute until chicken is cooked through.
Add Red Curry paste and coconut milk. Simmer for 5 minutes. Add basil, bamboo shoots, Fish Sauce, brown sugar, chicken stock, and mushrooms . Simmer for another 10 to 15 minutes.
