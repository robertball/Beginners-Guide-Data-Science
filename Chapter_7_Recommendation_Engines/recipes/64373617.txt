Title:
Wendy's Spicy Chicken Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup Frank's RedHot sauce
2/3 cup water
1 cup flour
4 tsp. cayenne pepper
1 tsp. black pepper
1 tsp. onion powder
1/2 tsp. paprika
1/8 tsp. garlic powder
4 chicken cutlets
Oil for frying

Directions:
Pour oil into large skillet and begin to heat while preparing chicken.
Combine RedHot sauce and water in a small bowl.
Combine flour, salt, cayenne pepper, black pepper, onion power, paprika and garlic powder.
Pound each piece of chicken to about 3/8 inch thick
Coat each piece of chicken with flour mixture, dredge in diluted pepper sauce and then again in the flour mixture.
When all chicken has been coated, fry in hot oil for about 8-12 minutes or until light brown and crispy.
Remove from pan and drain.
Prepare sandwich as you like.
