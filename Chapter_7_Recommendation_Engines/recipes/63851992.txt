Title:
Creamy Pesto Shrimp or Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb uncooked linguini pasta
1/4 cup butter
1 cup heavy whipping cream
1/4 tsp fresh ground black pepper
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/6 cup basil pesto
1/2 lb large shrimp - peeled & deveined

Directions:
Cook pasta al dente
Saute the shrimp in olive oil and set aside.
In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
Stir in the cream and pepper.  Cook, stirring constantly, for 6-8 minutes or until hot.
Add the Parmesan cheese, stirring until thoroughly mixed.  Stir in the pesto and cook 3-5 minutes, until thickened.
Add back the shrimp and heat through.
Serve immediately over hot linguini pasta.
