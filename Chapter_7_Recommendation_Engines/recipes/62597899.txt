Title:
Christmas Log Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz unsalted butter
4 oz powdered sugar
1 pack graham crackers
Milk
Red, white and green food coloring
Nestle Quick powder (to taste)
Instant coffee (to taste)

Directions:
Soften butter, then mix butter and sugar together. Separate into four parts for red, white, green and brown (from coffee and chocolate)
Use 1 or 2 tablespoons of warm water and mix in coffee and chocolate.
Add coffee/chocolate to cream mixture (one bowl set aside for brown).
Quickly dip graham crackers in milk; layer graham crackers and brown butter mixture. Save enough butter mixture to frost over graham crackers into log shape.
Chill in fridge for 30 minutes.
Remove from fridge and use a fork to make "ridges" in the log.
Chill in fridge for 30 minutes.
Remove from fridge and decorate with red, green and white butter mixture.
Suggestions: use paper funnel; green for vines; red for berries, white for flowers.
Chill and serve.
