Title:
QUICK Chocolate Fruit & Nut Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 18 oz. box brownie mix (your favorite)
1-1/2 c. Craisins
1 c. whole pistachios (salted or unsalted)
1/2 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Coat 8 x 12 baking pan with cooking spray or grease bottom of pan only with shortening.
Prepare brownie mix according to package directions.
Add remaining ingredients.
Bake 30-35 minutes.
Cool completely before cutting.

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
All the delicious flavor of your favorite fruit 'n nut bar in an 8 x 12 pan!  Need a last-minute dessert for an event?  This is the go to recipe!
