Title:
Hash Brown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2 lb.) bag frozen hash browns, thawed
1 large onion, chopped
Salt and Pepper to taste
1½ sticks butter, divided
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 pint sour cream
8 oz. Velveeta, cut into cubes
6 slices lean bacon, fried and crumbled (optional)
1 tube buttery crackers (like Ritz or Keebler Club), coursely crushed

Directions:
In large bowl mix hash browns, onions, salt and pepper.  Melt butter and blend one half into potatoe mixture.
In heavy saucepan, mix soup, sour cream, and Velveeta, stirring until Velveeta is melted.  Blend mixture into potatoes; add bacon and placein greased 15x10x3 inch casserole.
Mixed reserved metled butter with crackers and sprinkle on potatoes.  Bake at 325° for 1 hour.
