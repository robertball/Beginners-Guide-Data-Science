Title:
Old-fashioned Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups mayonnaise (NOT Miracle Whip!)
4-5 T vinegar
2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 T. sugar
2/3 cup finely chopped onion
2 cups sliced celery
10 cups cooked, peeled, cubed potatoes

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients in a large mixing bowl and whisk to blend.
Add onions, celery, potatoes and mix gently until evenly blended.
Season with more salt and pepper to taste, if desired.
Garnish with fresh parsley, sliced tomato, or sliced hard-boiled egg.
Chill at least 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Mrs. L
Description: 
A cool, creamy traditional potato salad that is great for summer barbecues.
