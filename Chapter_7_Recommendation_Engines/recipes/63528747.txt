Title:
Louise's Chocolate Wafers and Fruits Layered Cool Whip Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boxes of chocolate wafers
2 containers of cool whip
1 can of mandarin, drained
1 can of crushed pineapple, drained
2 bananas soaked in lemon juice

Directions:
Layer in a 10 inch spring pan:
chocolate wafers
cool whip
sliced banana soaked in lemon juice
chocolate wafers
cool whip
mandarin orange (drain the juice)
chocolate wafers
Cool whip
Crushed pineapple (drain the juice)
chocolate wafers
cool whip
crush chocolate wafers and sprinkle on top
