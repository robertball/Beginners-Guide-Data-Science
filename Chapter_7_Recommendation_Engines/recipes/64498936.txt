Title:
Blueberry, Orange Scones with White Chocolate Chunks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  1/2 cups white flour
1 tbsp. baking powder
1 tsp. sugar
1/2 c milk
1/3 c. blueberries
1 /2 tsp. orange zest
1/2 good quality white chocolate bar, broken into chunks OR
1/3 C white chocolate chips
4 tbsp.  shortening

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 F
2. In large bowl, combine 2 c flour, baking powder and sugar. Place in blender and add shortening. Pulse until mixture has the consistency of fine crumbs.
3.  Mix in blueberries, white chocolate chunks and orange zest.   Gradually add milk, stirring as you do, until mixture forms  a smooth batter.
4.  Knead mixture adding remaining flour , until the dough is smooth and not too sticky.
5.   Roll to 1/2  in  and cut out desired size OR use a small scoop (or hands) to make small balls.  Bake at  350 F for 15-17 minutes, until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Sarah Bowdidge
Description: 
A triple combination of deliciousness.  These scones are sure to vanish quickly.
