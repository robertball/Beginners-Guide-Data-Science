Title:
Potato Bombs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
potatoes
cheese
bacon

Directions:
using an apple corer, remove the centre meat of the potatoes.  Save the cores.
Stuff the potatoes with your favourite deli meats, cheese or anything you desire.  Now plug them with the potato plugs, wrap with bacon and then foil.
Bake indirect for an hour, or until done
