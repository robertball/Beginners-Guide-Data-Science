Title:
Kafta (BBQ) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1 medium onion, grated  (or equivalent dry minced onion)
1 clove garlic, finely chopped (or equivalent dry minced garlic)
1/2 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
Make into patties for grilling

Directions:
Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, onion, parsley, cayenne, allspice, salt and pepper until evenly blended. Divide into 6 portions and make patties.
Grill for 10 to 15 minutes, turning once, until meat is no longer pink.
