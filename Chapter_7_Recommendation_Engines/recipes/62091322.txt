Title:
Hot Cranberry Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. cranberry juice cocktail
½ C. packed brown sugar
2 ½ C. unsweetened pineapple juice
½ C. water
½ t. ground cloves
cinnamon sticks
½ t. ground allspice

Directions:
Combine cranberry juice, pineapple juice, water, and brown sugar in a saucepan and mix well.  Stir in the cloves and allspice and add three cinnamon sticks.  Bring to a boil over medium heat.  Reduce heat to low and simmer for 10 minutes.  Pour into mugs, discarding the used cinnamon sticks.  Garnish with fresh cinnamon sticks.

Submitter: 
Miss Moneypenny
Description: 
Cranberry and Pineapple juices, warmed and spiced, for a lovely winter drink.
