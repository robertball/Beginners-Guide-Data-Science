Title:
Jalapeno Beans & Kielbasa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 15oz cans Ranch Style Beans with Jalapenos
1 Beef or Pork kielbasa
1/2 cup BBQ sauce
3Tbs your favorite BBQ rub

Directions:
Empty beans into large non-stick sauce pan
Diagonally slice kielbasa into 1/4 inch slices and place  with beans
Add BBQ sauce and rub to beans and kielbasa and stir to combine
Heat on medium-low for 2 hours. Stir occassionaly
If using a slow cooker, cook on low for 6 hours.

Submitter: 
Evan
Description: 
I won 1st place in the appetizer/side dish category at a local BBQ competition with this recipe. I make them just about anytime I grill or BBQ as a side dish but they can stand alone as a main dish as well. The result is a spicy sweet dish.
