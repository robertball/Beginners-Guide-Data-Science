Title:
Best Ever Peanut Butter Chippers - Gluten Free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1045533.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Peanut Butter (Crunchy or Creamy)
1 Cup Firmly Packed Brown Sugar
1 Egg
3/4 Cup Milk or Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
Granulated Sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In a mixing bowl, combine all ingredients until well incorporated.  Roll into 1-2 inch balls and place about 2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet.
Using a fork dipped in granulated sugar make a cross shape on each cookie to flatten slightly.  Sprinkle with additional sugar if desired.
Bake 10-12 minutes until set - NOT BROWNED.
Cool 2 minutes on cookie sheet before removing to wire rack to cool.
