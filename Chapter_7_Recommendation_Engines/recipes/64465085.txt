Title:
Creamy Garlic Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 small / medium red potatoes (2.5 lbs), quartered
1 large clove garlic, minced
1/4 c. milk
3 Tbsp butter
4 ounces cream cheese
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place potatoes and minced garlic in a medium sauce pan and fill with water to one inch above potatoes. Boil 20 - 25 minutes or until tender. Drain.
Return potatoes to pan and add milk, butter, cream cheese, salt and pepper. Mash.
