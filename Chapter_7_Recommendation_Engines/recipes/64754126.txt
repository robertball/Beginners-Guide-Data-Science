Title:
Banana Bars (mom’s) with Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6875142.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c Flour
1 1/2 c sugar
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
***
2 eggs, beaten
2 bananas, mashed
1/2 c + 2 tbsp canola oil
1/4 c + 1 tbsp buttermilk
2 tsp vanilla extract
2 tsp real lemon
1 tsp almond extract
***
Frosting
6 tbsp butter, softened
4 oz cream cheese, softened
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp almond  extract
1/2 c (@) powdered sugar

Directions:
Set oven to 325°, and grease and flour 9x13 backing pan
In a  bowl, combine the  dry ingredients
In another large bowl beat the eggs and bananas together, mix in the remaining wet ingredients and beat well
Add the dry ingredients to wet and mix well
Bake at 325° for 40 minutes
When sufficiently cooled, mix frosting ingredients well and frost the bars.
