Title:
Brushetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayo
1 cup shredded mozza (or other) cheese
2 med. tomatoes, seeded, diced
1/4 cup chopped black olives
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
1 tsp. oregano
1/4 tsp. basil
1/2 tsp. pepper
baguette bread
margarine

Directions:
Combine first 8 ingredients.
Spread margarine on baguette slices.
Top with mixture.
Bake x 15 min. @ 350
