Title:
Cheddar Broccoli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 shallot, minced
1 tablespoon butter
2 cups chicken broth
2 cups milk
16 ounces broccoli
1/4 cup flour
16 oz cheddar cheese, shredded
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute shallotss in butter, in medium saucepan, 3 minutes.
Add 1 cup chicken broth, then 2 cups milk. Bring to boiling.
Add broccoli. Return to boiling. Reduce heat to medium, cook about 6 minutes or until almost tender.
Whisk together flour and remaining 1 cup chicken broth in small bowl.  Stir into saucepan.  Bring to boiling.  Cook, stirring occasionally 2-3 minutes or until thickened.
Remove from heat. Add cheese, stirring until melted.
Season with salt & pepper.
Serve warm.
**For a more mild flavor, you can use 8 oz. of cheddar cheese and 8 oz. of  Monterey Jack.**
