Title:
Slow Cooker Swiss Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. beef round steak, tenderized
1 can (10 oz.) tomato soup
1 small onion, finely chopped
2 carrots, grated
1 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
cracked pepper
1 can (15 oz.) stewed tomatoes
1/2 cup flour
2 Tbsp. canola or olive oil, divided

Directions:
1. Trim and discard excess fat from steak. Cut meat into serving-sized pieces; set aside (or refrigerate overnight).
2. Prepare sauce in a medium bowl, mixing tomato soup, onion, carrots, Worcestershire sauce, and pepper to taste. Chop stewed tomato pieces into small dice and add to soup mix along with juices. Stir to blend and set sauce aside (or refrigerate overnight).
4. Spoon flour into a shallow container and coat steak pieces.
5. Heat 1 Tbsp. oil in a large skillet till hot; brown half the meat. Repeat with remaining oil and meat. Layer steak pieces in crock pot.
6. Pour sauce over steak. Cover and cook on low for 6-9 hours. Serve steak and sauce with mashed potatoes, cooked noodles, or rice.

Submitter: 
RCKim
Description: 
Easy crock pot recipe yields tender beef in a thick tomato-based sauce. Easy to assemble in the morning (or even the night before). Perfect with mashed potatoes and a green veggie on the side.
