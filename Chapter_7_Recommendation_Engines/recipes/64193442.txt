Title:
Blackberry Sorbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups fresh blackberries, sub forzen
1/2 cup water
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2/3 cup sugar
1 tablespoon vodka

Directions:
blend blackberries, water and lemon juice until mixture is smooth
transfer the berries and mixture to a large saucepan and stir in sugar
bring mixture to a boil and remove from heat
allow berries to cool at room temperature for 5 minutes
press tem thru a fine mesh sieve
discard the berry solids and mix in vodka
allow to cool then freeze

Submitter: 
M. Karen
Description: 
for a true taste of summer, it uses less sugar but the result is of freshly picked blackberries
