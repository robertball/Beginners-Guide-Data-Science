Title:
Key Lime Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Egg Yolks
4 tsp. Grated Lime Zest
14 oz. Sweetened Condensed Milk
1/2 C. Fresh Key Lime Juice
Graham Cracker Pie Crust
Whipping Cream For Garnish
1/2 C. Whipping Cream
2 tsp. Sugar
1/4 tsp. Vanilla

Directions:
1. Whisk together egg yolks and lime zest (2min.)
2. Beat in sweetened condensed milk, then lime juice.
Set aside to thicken
3. Preheat oven to 325 degrees, pour into pie crust.
Bake 15 minutes, Cool to room temp, Refrigerate
3 hours.
4. Whip together whipping cream ingredients and serve with pie.

Submitter: 
Lisa Harmon
Description: 
This is a nice mild key lime pie. I don't like them too tart and this one seems just perfect.
