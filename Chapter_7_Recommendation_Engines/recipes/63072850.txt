Title:
Orange Jello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/782101.jpg
Ingredients:
1 6oz. Pkg. Orange Jello
2 Cups Boiling Water
1 Small Can Crushed Pineapple (Drained - Save Juice)
1 Large Can Mandarin Oranges (Drained - Save Juice)
1 6oz. Can Frozen Orange Juice Concentrate
1 Cup Fruit JuiceFrom Pineapple and Mandarin Oranges

Directions:
Dissolve orange jello in boiling water.  Add 1 cup fruit juice from canned pineapple and mandarin oranges (if not enough fruit juice, add enough water to make 1 cup).  Add frozen orange juice and stir to dissolve.  Immediately add crushed pineapple and mandarin oranges.  Pour into jello mold or into serving bowl.  Refridgerate for 3 hours, or until firm.

Submitter: 
Alley
Description: 
This is a vintage jello recipe that is loved by both kids as well as adults.  My mom used to make it often when I was growing up.  It has a rich orange flavor and goes very well with ham, pork or turkey.  Enjoy!
