Title:
Crunchy Tossed Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123229.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 c. vegetable oil
1/4 c. sugar
2 T. vinegar
1t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
1 large head iceberg lettuce, torn apart
6 bacon strips, cooked & crumbled
1/3 c. sliced almonds, toasted
1/4 c. sesame seeds, toasted
4 green onions, sliced
3/4 c. chow mien noodles

Directions:
In a jar with a tight fitting lid combine oil, sugar, vinegar, salt & pepper.
Shake well.
Chill for 1 hour.
Just before serving, combine the lettuce, bacon, almonds, sesame seeds & onions in a large bowl.
Add dressing & toss.
Top with chow mien noodles.
