Title:
Triple Delicious Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup sugar
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 tsp. salt
3 cups milk, divided
3/4 tsp. vanilla
1 cup heavy cream
1 square bittersweet chocolate
1 square white chocolate (1 oz)
3/4 cup whipped topping
6 tsp. mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Combine sugar, cocoa powder, salt and 1/2 cup milk in medium sized bowl.   Stir until smooth.  Pour into slow cooker.
Add remaining 2 1/2 cups milk and vanilla to slow cooker.  Cover. Cook on low 2 hours.
Stir in cream.  Cover.  Cook on low 10 minutes.
Stir in bittersweet and white chocolates until melted.
Pour hot chocolate into 6 mugs.   Top each with 2 tbsp. whipped topping and 1 tsp. chocolate chips.

Submitter:

Description: 
All the different chocolates make this a dreamy cup!
