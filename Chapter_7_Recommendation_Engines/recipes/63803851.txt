Title:
Better than Zuppa Toscana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb mild pork sausage
1/2 lb bacon
1 onion, finely diced
6 cups chicken broth
1 Tablespoon Garlic
6 large red skin potatoes, sliced
1 tsp red pepper flakes
1/2 bunch (approx 4 cups) Kale, coarsely chopped
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook the bacon, crumble and set aside.
Pan fry the sausage, onion, & garlic.
Combine the cooked sausage mixture in a large soup pot with the bacon, chicken broth, sliced potatoes, & red pepper flakes.
Simmer until the potatoes are soft, approx 15-20 minutes.
Add Kale, sour cream, and Parmesan, then cook 5 minutes until Kale is wilted.
Serve warm with extra Parmesan as a garnish if desired.

Submitter: 
Jeanne Garber
Description: 
After many attempts and revisions I think my version of this hearty soup is much better and lower in fat than the restaurant dish that inspired it.  Enjoy!
