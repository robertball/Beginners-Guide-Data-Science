Title:
Tami's Best Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup salted butter, softened
1 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
pinch of cinnamon powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup vanilla pudding
1/2 cup chopped walnuts or chocolate chips (optional)
1 cup over-ripe banana, mashed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar. Add the eggs, lemon juice and vanilla, mix well.
Combine the flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt, stir into the butter mixture until smooth. Finally, fold in the pudding, walnuts (chocolate chips) and bananas. Spread evenly into the prepared pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean.
Cool loaf in the pan for 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
