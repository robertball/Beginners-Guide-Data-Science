Title:
Andrea's Low-Fat Pimento Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/953896.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup grated 2% sharp Cheddar cheese
1 cup grated 2% Monterey Jack cheese
1/2 cup reduced-fat mayonnaise
1/4 cup nonfat plain yogurt
2 tablespoons pimento peppers, smashed, or more to taste
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 dash hot sauce (such as Tabasco®), or more to taste (optional)
1 pinch paprika, or to taste (optional)

Directions:
Combine Cheddar cheese, Monterey Jack cheese, mayonnaise, yogurt, pimento peppers, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, hot sauce, and paprika in a bowl. Mix until well blended. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate, 8 hours to overnight, before serving.

Submitter: 
AndreaAudish
Description: 
This is something I made while just playing around since I didn't have cream cheese. The yogurt makes it really have a nice sharp taste. Serve on Ritz® crackers, as sandwich filling, or in a veggie wrap.
