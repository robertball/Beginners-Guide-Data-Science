Title:
breakfast cookie bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C. Whole Wheat flour
1/2 C. flour
1/2 tsp. Baking soda
1 tsp. ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp. ground nutmeg
1/4 tsp. salt
2 tbs. butter
1/3 C. brown sugar
3 tbs. sugar
2 tbs. milk
3 tbs. applesauce
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 tsp almond extract
1/2 rolled oats
1/3 C. dark chocolate
1/4 C. dried cranberries and/or rasins
1/3 C. Walnuts
1/8 C. grated zucchini
1/8 C. grated carrot

Directions:
in large mixing bowl combine flours, baking soda, and spices. mix well and set aside.
In a diffirent bowl beat the butter, sugars, milk, applesauce, and extracts once fluffy add egg and beat
Combine flour mixture and sugar mixture then fold in the chocolate, walnuts, cranberries or rasins rolled oats and grated zucchini and carrot.
Place 12 large heaping spoonfuls onto a greased cookie sheet and bake for 12 min at 350 F

Description: 
if you've ever wanted a gigantic, tasty, healthy cookie this is it!! I make it every sunday to have handy during the week. (although their usually gone by tuesday).
