Title:
Avocado Salad with Avocado Lime Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/969962.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ripe medium size Haas Avocados
4-5 cups leafy green lettuce
1/4 cup very thinly sliced red onion
1 lime
1 clove garlic finely minced
salt and pepper

Directions:
wash, rinse and dry lettuce and cut into bite size pieces and arrange in a serving dish
slice the avocados into about 1//2 inch slices and toss with the juice from 1/2 lime(reserve about 3 slices for vinaigrette)
slice the red onion into very thin slices, you need only about 1/4 cup so you don't overpower the avocados
arrange all the avocados on top of the greens and sprinkle the red onion slivers over that and salt and pepper to taste
for the vinaigrette 3 slices of the avocado from above with the lime juice, mashed really well with a fork
add 3 to 4 tablespoons of rice wine vinegar, or white wine vinegar
juice from the rest of the lime
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 clove garlic very finely minces
with a whisk mix very well until emulsified
salt and pepper to taste
