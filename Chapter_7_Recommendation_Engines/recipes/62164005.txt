Title:
Red Velvet Wonder Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups flour
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup butter, softened
2 cups sugar
4 eggs
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup milk
1 bottle (1 ounce) Red Food Color
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
Cream Cheese Frosting
1 cup finely chopped pecans
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup butter, softened
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups powdered sugar

Directions:
Mix flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt together.
Set aside.
Beat butter and sugar in a separate bowl on medium for 5 minutes.
Beat in eggs, one at a time.
Add the sour cream, milk, food color, and vanilla to the butter and sugar mixture.
Gradually beat in flour mixture on low speed.
Pour batter into two greased, nine inch round pans.
Bake at 350 for 20 to 25 minutes.
Cool completely.
For Frosting:
Beat cream cheese and butter on medium speed until fluffy. Beat in powderedvsugar and then vanilla.
Add the pecans.
Freeze if the frosting is a little liquid-y.
Frost cake.

Submitter: 
ChefMandy
Description: 
This cake has always been present at Christmas dinners and still continues to after all these years. There are never any leftovers to take home afterward so I suggest going for dessert first before the meal.
