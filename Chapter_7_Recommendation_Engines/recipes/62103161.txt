Title:
Chicken Pot Pie Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Bag Egg Noodles (I like dumpling style)
4 Chicken Breast Halves
Salt
Pepper
Garlic Salt
2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
1/3 cup of Butter
2/3 cup Flour
1 Quart Heavy Cream
1 Box Chicken Broth (32 oz)
1 Tablespoon Minced Garlic
1/2 Cup Small Red Potatoes (cubed)
1/2 Cup Carrots (diced)
1/2 Cup Frozen Peas
1/2 Cup Frozen Corn

Directions:
Chicken:
Season chicken with salt pepper and garlic salt.
Heat oil in a Large skillet over medium-high heat.
Add Chicken and saute until cooked through.
Remove from heat and cut into cubes.
Set aside.
(Alternatively you may use pre-cooked chicken.)
Noodles:
Boil Noodles as instructed on package.
Drain and set aside.
Filling:
Precook potatoes and carrots.
In a LARGE saucepan, melt butter.
Slowly add flour, stirring until the consistancy of penut butter but not brown like a roux.(you may not need all the flour)
If it gets clumpy, that is ok.
Slowly add cream and keep stirring.
Add Chicken Broth and garlic.
Stir until Smooth.
Add peas and corn, still frozen.
Add precooked carrots, potatoes, chicken and noodles.
Stir and cook until veggies are done.
Remove from heat and serve.
This is a big recipe.
It will thicken as it cools, but is as good the next day for leftovers.

Description: 
A Hearty Soup that is easy and quick to make.
It started out as a pot pie recipe and was altered to make into a soup. Just delicious!
This recipe makes A LOT! It is easy to cut in half to make less if needed.
