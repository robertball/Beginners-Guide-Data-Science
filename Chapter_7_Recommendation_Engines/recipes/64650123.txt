Title:
Mexican Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4  cup yellow cornmeal
1/4 cup self rising flour
1/4 cup sugar (I use a little less)
1/4 teaspoon salt
3 teaspoons of baking powder if not using self rising flour
1 can cream style corn
1 cup buttermilk
2/3 cup vegetable oil
2 eggs
7 ounce can chopped green chilies
2-3 chopped jalapenos
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese ( I us more)
1/2 of a onion chopped up

Directions:
Combine all dry ingredients in a large bowl. Stir in cheese and coat well. Combine all wet ingredients.
Add wet ingredients to the cornmeal mixture and stir just until moistened.
Spray  large iron skillet with pam. pour cornbread mixture into pan and bake at 425 degrees for 35-40 minutes until done.

Submitter: 
franny
Description: 
A delicious alternative to regular cornbread. This cornbread has a great flavor and is delicious with all sorts of soups.
