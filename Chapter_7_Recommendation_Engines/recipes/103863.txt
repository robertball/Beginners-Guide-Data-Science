Title:
Citrus-Mint Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985369.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups boiling water
5 regular-size tea bags
1/2 cup loosely packed fresh mint leaves
1 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
6 cups water
1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
1 cup fresh orange juice
Garnish: lemon slices, orange slices, fresh mint sprigs

Directions:
Pour boiling water over tea bags and mint leaves; cover and steep 5 minutes. Remove tea bags and mint, squeezing gently. Stir in SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener and remaining ingredients. Serve over ice. Garnish with lemon slices, orange slices and fresh mint sprigs.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
A refreshing drink to keep on hand for the family and a favorite for summertime entertaining.
