Title:
Keto Instant Pot PORK TENDERLOIN Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Seasoning
1 TBSP Olive Oil
2.5 lbs thin pork tenderloin
1 Cup Beef Broth

Directions:
1. Season pork tenderloin with salt.
2. Turn the Instant Pot to the saut√© setting, add the oil to the insert and when hot, brown the pork tenderloin on all sides (2-4-mins).
3. Add the stock mixture to the Instant Pot insert and scrape any bits stuck to the bottom of the pot from frying with a silicon or wooden spoon.
4. Cover your Instant Pot, set the vent to SEALING, select the 'manual' or 'pressure cook' setting and set the timer to 7 mins.
5. Cook till done then do a quick release
