Title:
Baked Pineapple Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
3/4 cup butter
4 eggs
20 oz can crushed pineapple
5 slices white bread cubed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Butter a 1 1/2 quart oven-safe dish.
Cream butter and sugar. Add 4 eggs and mix.  Add pineapple and bread, mix.
Pour into prepared oven safe dish. Bake for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Noel Keen
Description: 
Delicious side dish to ham or for dessert with vanilla ice cream.
