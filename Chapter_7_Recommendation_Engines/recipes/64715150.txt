Title:
Quick Pickled Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5718780.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1/2 cup Apple cider vinegar
3 tablespoons white sugar
1 teaspoon salt
I  large onion sliced thinly
I large mason  jar with lid.

Directions:
In a small pot heat the water,Apple cider vinegar,sugar and salt.
Heat until boiling.
Place the thinly cut onion in the mason jar.
Pour boiling liquid over onion.
Seal the jar.
Allow to sit on the counter 1 hour and then refrigerate  over night.
Can be eaten after the hour but I find it much better to leave it
over night in the fridge.
This can be doubled.

Submitter: 
manella
Description: 
A great way to use up your garden onions.
Very tasty over eggs or on their own as a side dish with any protein
Great to take on picnics .
