Title:
Joy Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Dark Chocolate Premium Brownie Mix
1 small can of sweetened condensed milk
1 cup sweetened coconut
½ cup chopped nuts (any type)
1 tbsp. flour
1 egg

Directions:
Baking Instructions
Preheat oven to 350°F.
Mix brownies according to directions on box. Pour ½ of the batter into the greased pan. Reserved the other half of the batter.
Filling - Mix together the coconut, the sweetened condensed milk, 1 tbsp. flour, one egg and the nuts.
Spread filling over first layer of chocolate brownie.
Spread remaining chocolate brownie on top of coconut layer.
Bake brownies for 35-40 minutes until firm in center.
Allow to cool. Cut and enjoy.
Tips:
Garnish with additional sweetened coconut
For easy clean up line the bottom of baking pan with parchment paper.
Do not grease the sides of a brownie pan, only grease the bottom
