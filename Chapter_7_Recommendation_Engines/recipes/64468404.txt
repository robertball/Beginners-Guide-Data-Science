Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2453244.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter
1/3 cup white sugar
1/3 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup powdered sugar
1 egg
1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Line cookie sheets with wax or parchment paper.
Mix together all ingredients except for chocolate chips.
Roll into teaspoon sized balls and place on cookie sheet, one inch apart.
Bake until cracks start to form in the cookies, about ten minutes.
Place four chocolate chips on each cookie while still warm in a square, as seen in the picture.
Let cool, serve, and enjoy!
