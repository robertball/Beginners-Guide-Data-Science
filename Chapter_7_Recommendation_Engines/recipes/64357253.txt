Title:
Mom's Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb sweet potatoes, baked and peeled (firm)
1 lb canned apricots, reserve syrup
1 cup brown sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp cinnamon
1-1/2 Tbsp cornstarch
2 Tbsp butter
1/2 cup pecan halves

Directions:
Stir 1 cup reserved syrup with brown sugar, salt, cinnamon and cornstarch.  Cook until it boils.  Add apricots and butter.
Grease large baking dish and arrange sweet potato pieces in baking dish.  Pour apricot mixture over sweet potatoes.  Sprinkle with pecans.
Bake at 375 degrees for 25 minutes.

Barbara Bartz

A family favorite at Thanksgiving.
