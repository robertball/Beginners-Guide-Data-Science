Title:
Ranee's Favorite Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups shredded chicken (approximately 1 rotisserie chicken)
1/2 cup mayo
1/2 cup light sour cream
3 Tablespoons Dijon mustard
1 Tablespoon honey
2 green onions, thinly sliced
1/2 cup pecan pieces, toasted
1 tsp seasoned salt
1/2 teaspoon ground thyme
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1 Tablespoon red wine vinegar or lemon juice

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together except chicken.
Fold in shredded chicken.
Can be served immediately, but tastes better if refrigerated for at least 30 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
QUEENREYNEY
Description: 
This creamy chicken salad recipe with pecans, green onions, and less mayo than most is a much requested staple in my family.
