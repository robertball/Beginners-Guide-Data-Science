Title:
You Want Me to Eat This Taco Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T Masa harina
1 T Chili Powder
1 t dried minced onions
1/2 t salt
3/4 t paprika
1/8 to 1/4 t Hot Hungarian paprika**
1 t cumin
1/4 t garlic granuales
1/2 t oregano

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a zip lock bag and shake until completely mixed. You can add anywhere from 3T to the entire mix to 1 pound browned ground beef along with 1/2-3/4 cup water and cook until reduced to desired consistency.
