Title:
PBnJ "Workday" Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 frozen strawberries
1 whole frozen sliced banana
Add up to 1 c of water to desired thickness
2 tbsp Creamy peanut butter
1/4 c Vanilla nonfat yogurt
Optional**
2 packets of equal (or your choice of sweetener to desired sweetness)

Directions:
Place strawberries, bananas, yogurt in blender with about 1/2 a c of water.  Just enough to blend smoothly.  Add additional water slowly to achieve desired thickness.  After it's been thoroughly blended, add peanut butter and sweetener (if desired), then blend a moment longer.

Submitter: 
Cindylee Bloomer
Description: 
My lunch time smoothie staple.  Peanut butter and jelly in a glass, yum!  I like mine thick enough to eat with a spoon add more water slowly to thin to your preferred consistancy.
