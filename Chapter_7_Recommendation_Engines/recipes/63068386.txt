Title:
Caramel Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123078.jpg
Ingredients:
2 T. sugar
1/4 c. chopped pecans
2- 8 oz. pkg.refrigerated biscuits
1/3 c. margarine, melted

Directions:
Sprinkle sugar on bottom of well greased 9"layer pan.
Cover with nuts.
Dip biscuits in margarine & arrange in pan with 15 biscuits overlapping around outer edge & 5 in center.
Bake at 400 degrees for 23-25 minutes.
Let stand 5 minutes.
Invert onto serving plate.
Serve warm.
