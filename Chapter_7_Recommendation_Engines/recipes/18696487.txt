Title:
Cafe Rio Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 buttermilk ranch dressing packet (make as per recipe)
2 tomatillos (tomato like vegetable with a husk around them)
½ bunch of cilantro
1 clove garlic
Juice of 1 lime
1 jalapeño (Use the seeds too if you like it spicy. You could substitute a few drops of green tobasco for the jalapeno.)

Directions:
Use a food processor to blend all the ingredients well. Refrigerate.

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
SSSOOO good!!  This is from a fabulous Mexican restaraunt in Utah.  The dressing is a spicy, ranch, lime, cilantro flavor.  All Wallace's will love.
