Title:
Real German Baked Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 red, sweet and big apples
1 egg white
1 teaspoon cinnamon
2 teaspoons raisins
1 teaspoon brittle
1 teaspoon white sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 190Â°C
1. Hollow out the apple core with a sharp knive. Do not break the apples into pieces!
2. Whip the egg white until it is stiff.
3. Mix the other ingredients with the egg white and fill the apples with the mixture.
4. Put them into the preheated oven for 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
MARYANDFRANZI
Description: 
This really delicious Baked Apple and easy-to-make recipe is perfect for the season. Cinnamon and raisins warm you during cold and windy winter evenings.
