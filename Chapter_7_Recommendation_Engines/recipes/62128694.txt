Title:
Tiny Cream Cheese Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz.) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 cups butter or margarine, softened
1 cup self-rising flour

Directions:
Beat cream cheese and butter at medium speed of an electric mixer for two (2) minutes or until creamy.  Gradually add flour, beating at low speed until just blended.  Spoon dough into miniature ungreased muffin pans, filling full.  Bake at 400 degrees for 15 - 17 minutes or until golden brown.  Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Shults3
Description: 
Only three ingredients but they sure are tasty little  biscuits that are good with just about anything!
