Title:
tomato basil soup dairy-free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14.5 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (11.5 ounce) can tomato-vegetable juice cocktail (V8)
1 tbsp minced garlic
1 tsp vinegar
1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
20 fresh basil leaves, cut into thin strips

Directions:
Put whole can of crushed tomatoes and half the can of diced tomatoes and most of the basil in blender and get them nice and mixed. Saute the garlic a bit in the bottom of a saucepan. Transfer blender ingredients to saucepan, add V8, vinegar, chicken broth, and the rest of the basil.Bring to a boil, cook for 30 minutes. Remove from heat, serve immediately.
