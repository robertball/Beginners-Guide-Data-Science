Title:
Keto Ranch Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBLS parsley
1/2 tsp dill
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp minced onions
1/2 tsp basil
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup heavy whipping cream
1/3 cup sour cream
1 medium avocado

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a blender or food processor.

Submitter: 
Loretta Ann Zazo
Description: 
.6 net carbs per 2 TBLS serving
I usually mix up a large amount of the dry ingredients to keep in hand. I’ll use 3 TBLS of dry ingredients to the mayo, HWC, sour cream, avocado mix. I’ll store it in the fridge.  Interestingly, it does not turn brown like avocados usually do.
