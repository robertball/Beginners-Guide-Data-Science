Title:
Oatmeal with apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c oatmeal
4 c water
1 c tiny chopped apples

Directions:
1.  Heat water in medium saucepan till boiling.
2.  Add oatmeal.  Stir for 30 seconds.  Turn off heat and let sit for one minutes.
3.  Serve hot with apples in bowl on the table to top with.
NOTE:  You can use bacon too or both.
