Title:
Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey
1 cup chopped onions
1 can kidney beans
1 can northern beans
1 can tomatoes (petite diced)
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 bell pepper, chopped
1 cup chopped celery
1 can tomato sauce
1 pkg. chili seasonings
2 tbsp sugar
1 tsp salt (to taste)
1 tsp pepper (to taste)

Directions:
Brown meat in large skillet.
Add celery, onion, and bell pepper, cook until almost done.
Drain and place in crock pot.
Add remaining ingredients to crock pot and stir to mix.
cook 3-4 hours, until done. cook on low.
