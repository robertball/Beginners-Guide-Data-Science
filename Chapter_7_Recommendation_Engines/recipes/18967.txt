Title:
Really Raspberry  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/448437.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup firm tofu
2 cups apple juice
2 cups frozen raspberries
4 tablespoons raspberry jelly
3 bananas, frozen and chunked
1/2 teaspoon almond extract

Directions:
In a blender combine tofu, apple juice, frozen raspberries, jelly, frozen bananas and almond extract. Blend until smooth.

Submitter:

Description: 
A pretty pink blender made with tofu - but don't tell anyone, and you'll be the only one who knows!
