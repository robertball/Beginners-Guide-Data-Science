Title:
Orange Fluff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886971.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) package orange jello
2 1/2 cups boiling water
2 (11 ounce) cans mandarin oranges, drained
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple with juice
1 (6 ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
1 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup milk

Directions:
Combine the jello and boiling water, stir until dissolved.
Add the mandarin oranges, pineapple and orange juice; pour into prepared dish and refrigerate until chilled.
Beat together the pudding, cream cheese and milk. Spread over jello and refrigerate until chilled.
