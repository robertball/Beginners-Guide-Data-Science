Title:
OREO Snowman Cookie Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2739264.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package brick cream cheese, softened
36 OREO Cookies, finely crushed
3 (4 ounce) packages white baking chocolate, melted
1 teaspoon black decorating gel
2 teaspoons orange decorating icing

Directions:
Mix cream cheese and cookie crumbs until blended.
Shape into 48 (1-inch) balls. Freeze 10 min. Dip balls in melted chocolate; place in single layer in shallow waxed paper-lined pan. Decorate with remaining ingredients to resemble snowmen.
Refrigerate 1 hour or until firm. Keep refrigerated.

Submitter: 
Oreo
Description: 
Easy to make, nothing to bake! These OREO Snowman Cookie Balls are the perfect treats for your holiday parties.
