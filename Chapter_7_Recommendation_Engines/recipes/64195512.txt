Title:
Kevin's Slow Cooker Brunswick Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 pound pulled pork
1 onion, chopped
1 roasted chicken, deboned and shredded
2 teaspoons minced garlic
1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 cup BBQ sauce (I use Head Country)
1/2 cup cider vinegar
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon hot sauce
1 small lemon, juiced
2 teaspoons Penzey's chicken soup base
1/2 tablespoon ground black pepper
2 (15 ounce) can creamed corn
2 cup frozen lima beans
2 cup frozen sliced okra

Directions:
Heat the vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat. Place onion in the skillet, cook until tender, and transfer to the slow cooker.
Place the chicken in the slow cooker, and mix in garlic, tomatoes, BBQ sauce, cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, lemon, chicken soup base, pepper, corn, lima beans, and okra.
Cover, and cook 6 - 8 hours on low.
