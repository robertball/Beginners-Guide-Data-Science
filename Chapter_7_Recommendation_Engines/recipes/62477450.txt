Title:
Porketta Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon dill seed
1 tablespoon fennel seed
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon lemon pepper
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
4 pounds boneless pork roast
1 jar pickle relish

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
In a small bowl, combine the dill seed, fennel seed, oregano, lemon pepper, onion powder and garlic powder. Mix well and apply to the roast. Place roast in a 10x15 inch roasting pan.
Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 1 to 1 1/2 hours, or until internal pork temperature reaches 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
When done, slice thin or shred, add relish,mix well, place in crock pot on med. heat. Serve on burger bun or kaizer roll.

Submitter: 
Joseph Brojakowski Jr.
Description: 
A spicy, tasty and extremely moist boneless pork roast. This recipe uses a dry rub combination that you rub on the meat before you roast it. The rub includes dill seed, fennel seed, oregano, lemon pepper, garlic and onion powder.Modified from: Porketta Roast
