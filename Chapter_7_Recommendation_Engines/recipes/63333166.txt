Title:
Rachael Ray's Pumpkin Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 medium onion, finely chopped
3 cups vegetable stock
1 15 oz can diced tomatoes, in juice
1 15 oz can black beans, drained
2 15 oz cans pumpkin
1 Tbsp curry powder
1 1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1 cup 1/2 & 1/2 or cream
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in soup pot.  Saute onions until soft.  Add curry, cumin, cayenne.  Add broth, tomatoes, beans and pumpkin.  Bring to a boil, reduce, simmer 30-45  minutes.    Remove from heat.  Stir in cream.  Salt and pepper to taste.
