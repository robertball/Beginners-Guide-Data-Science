Title:
Cayenne Pepper Bread Chicken Drumsticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6201240.jpg
Ingredients:
4 drumsticks, cayenne pepper, bread crumbs, eggs, butter spray

Directions:
Take 4 c to 6 chicken drumsticks, rinse under water then roll til covered in egg mixture, then roll in bread crumbs, then place in baking pan , sprinkle with lots of cayenne pepper til well coated then place in oven at 350 degrees for an hour
