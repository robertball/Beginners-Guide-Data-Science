Title:
Jill's meatball Bolognese sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the meatballs:
1/4 lb ground beef
1 tbl bruchetta spices
1 egg
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tbl ketjap
1/2 bag of pizza chili peppers (or full tsp.)
1/2 cup of bread crumbs
salt & pepper
For the spagetti sauce:
1 red onion
2 garlic toes
1/4 lb ground beef
28 oz. can of stewed tomatoes
7 oz. can of tomato paste
16 oz. can of tomato sauce
1 tsp of basil (or fresh)
1 tsp of thyme (or fresh)
6 bay leaves
1/2 cup of white wine (or what ever is open :-) )
1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Meatballs:
make the meatballs a little smaller than a ping-pong ball
Add a little oil to the pot and brown the balls until they are dark brown.
Remove from pan and set aside.
Bolognese Sauce:
Brown the ground beef.
add the onion and garlic and stir for a couple of minutes
add the spices
add all 3 cans of tomato
add the wine.
add the meat balls
Stir and simmer. Add salt & pepper to taste
top with grated parmesan cheese.
