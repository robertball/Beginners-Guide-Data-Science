Title:
baked cheese spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz green onions, chopped; 1 medium tomato, chopped; 2 packages cream cheese, softened; 1/2 cup mayonnaise; 2 tbls garlic, minced; 2 cups fresh basil, minced; 12 oz fresh mozzarella, chopped small; 1 loaf of garlic sourdough bread

Directions:
1. set oven to broil
2. put cream cheese in microwavable bowl and nuke for 1-2 minutes until cream cheese is soft, then add mozzarella and mayonnaise and nuke again for 2 minutes to melt cheese.
3. mix in basil, tomatoes, garlic, green onions, then spread on sourdough bread and broil for about 10 minutes till cheese topping is golden brown and bubbly. pull out and let cool for a few minutes then serve.

Submitter: 
April Kammerzell
Description: 
This is a little experiment I made that wound up being a total hit at a company party. I really just started throwing stuff together that sounded good and cheesy for an appetizer because I forgot to make one.
