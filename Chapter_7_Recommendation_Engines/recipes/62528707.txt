Title:
Smoked Bricket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lb. brisket
Prime Rib Rub

Directions:
Rub prime rub seasoning on the brisket.  Smoke on grill with Hickory pellets for 6 hours, turning every 2 hours.  Finish cooking on 350 degrees until it reaches the internal temperature of 190 degrees.
