Title:
Instant pot egg bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5549779.jpg
Ingredients:
1 dozen eggs
1 cup chopped spinach
1/2 cup chopped onion
3/4 cup shredded Muenster cheese
1 tsp granulated garlic
1 1/2 tsp salt (to taste)
1 1/2 tsp black pepper (to taste)

Directions:
In a medium bowl, beat eggs until yellow and frothy.
Add the granulated garlic, salt and black pepper.
Toss the onions, spinach, and cheese together in a small bowl.
Divide vegetables and cheese evenly among the egg mold cavities. Pour the beaten eggs into each cavity, filling 3/4 way.
Cover the molds with foil. Place the mold into the instant pot - pour 1/2 cup water into the pot.
Cover the instant pot with lid, sealing vent. Select steam, and a time of 13 minutes.
Allow pressure to naturally release.

Submitter: 
thedailygourmet
Description: 
These egg bites are perfect for breakfast on the go. Packed with veggies and cheese it packs a lot of flavor. Feel free to interchange vegetables for more variety.  This uses a silicone egg mold available through online retailers like Amazon.
