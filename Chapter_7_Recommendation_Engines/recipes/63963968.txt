Title:
Manicotti Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 pieces manicotti pasta (one 8 ounce pkg), uncooked
1 pound extra lean ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, seeded and diced
4 ounces chopped green chiles, drained
3/4 cup nonfat sour cream, divided
1/2 teaspoon salt
16 ounces picante sauce, heated
1/2 cup low-fat cheddar cheese, grated
1/4 cup green onions or red onion, sliced

Directions:
Prepare pasta according to package directions, being careful not to overcook.  While pasta is cooking, saute beef, onion and green pepper in large skillet until browned; drain well.  Stir in chilies, 1/2 cup of sour cream and the salt.  When pasta is done, drain well.  Fill manicotti with beef mixture.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Coat bottom of a glass 9" x 13" baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.  Spread 1/2 cup of the picante sauce over bottom of dish.  Place filled manicotti side by side on top of sauce.  Pour remaining sauce evenly over manicotti.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Cover with aluminum foil; bake 20 to 25 minutes or until done, drain well.  Garnish with remaining 1/4 cup sour cream and sprinkle green onions on top.  Serve immediately.
