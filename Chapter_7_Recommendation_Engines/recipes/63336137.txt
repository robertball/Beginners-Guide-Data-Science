Title:
Crystal's Hamburger Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 lbs. ground beef
1 Tbsp olive oil
2 cups chopped onion
2 cups diced potatoes (Russet)
2 cups sliced carrots
2 cups shredded cabbage
56 oz, canned tomatoes
1/2 cup uncooked brown rice or barley
6-10 cups water
1/2 tsp basil
1/2 tsp thyme
2 bay leaves
1 1/2 -2 1/2 Tbsp salt

Directions:
Brown ground beef in oil.  Drain fat.  Return to pot and add remaining ingredients.  Bring to a boil, cover, reduce heat and simmer about 2 hours.  Adjust for salt at the very end.  Between 7000 and 8000 mg Na+ is needed for proper flavor.
