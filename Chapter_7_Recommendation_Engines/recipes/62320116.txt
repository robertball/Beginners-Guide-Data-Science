Title:
Rhonda's Chicken Paprikas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-split chicken breast(1/2)
3-qrts water
4-1 pound packages of spatzels (Sopie's are usually found in the meat case)
4-Tbls butter
1-lge onion chopped
2-Tbls paprika
salt and pepper to taste
1- 16oz con. sour cream

Directions:
Bring chicken to a boil in water , cook 20-30 min.
Place chicken on a plate to cool. Reserve broth.
Once cool pull meat away from bone and cut into bitesize pieces.
In a heavy dutch oven melt butter and saute' onions 5min. till soft.
Add paprika and continue to saute' over low heat 5 min.
Add 2cups of broth to onions and continue to saute' 5min.
Add sour cream and stir to blend.
Add spatzels and enough broth to cover dumplings. Mix meat into dumpling mixture, simmer 10 min. over low heat. Salt and pepper to taste. Add more broth for desired consistancy.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
This recipe is totally inspired by Grandma Ruth (one of my favorite ladies). I use the convenience of store bought spatzels though I wonder what Grandma would have thought!
