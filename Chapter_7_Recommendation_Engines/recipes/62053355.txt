Title:
Peanut Butter Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup creamy peanut butter,
1 cup confectioners sugar,
8 ounces softened cream cheese,
16 ounces whipped topping,
2 bars milk chocolate,
2 graham cracker pie crusts.

Directions:
Whip peanut butter, sugar, and cream cheese until smooth.  Fold in whipped topping, and divide into each crust.  Shave chocolate and sprinkle one bar on each pie.  Chill at least 5 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
This is a tasty pie.  I try to cook sweets every Sunday, and have family over.  I made this one just recently, and everyone raved.
