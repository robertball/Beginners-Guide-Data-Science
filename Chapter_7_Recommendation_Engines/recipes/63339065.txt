Title:
Fatty Natty's Peanut Butter Fudge Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tbsp. butter, melted (microwave 20 secs.)
1 cup peanut butter
1/8 cup cocoa powder
2 cups confectioner's sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
2 Tbsp. marshmallow creme (fluff)

Directions:
1. Mix all ingredients until smooth. Roll into 2 inch balls. Place in sealed container and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Natalie Shollenberger
Description: 
Yummy chocolate-peanut butter balls! Melts in your mouth.  Quick and easy! No baking required!
