Title:
Broccoli Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz to 10oz broccoli, chopped (I used 4oz for a half recipe)
2-4 T onion, chopped
1 T butter
2 cup cooked rice
1 can cream of chicken soup
3 or 4 mushrooms, chopped
1 cup Velveeta cheese, cubed

Directions:
Saute onion in butter until softed.
Add remaining ingredients and stir until well combined.
Bake in 8x8" pan for 20-30 minutes at 350.
*I made a half recipe baking in mini pans using 2 mushrooms and
3 1/4oz broccoli - John went nuts.  Very nice.
