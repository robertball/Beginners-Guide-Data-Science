Title:
Cheeseburger Soup I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound ground beef
3/4 cup chopped onion
3/4 cup chopped carrots
3/4 cup chopped celery
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon dried parsley
Garlic Powder to taste
Sea Salt to taste
Black Pepper to taste
Worcestershire Sauce to taste
4 tablespoons butter
3 cups chicken broth
4 cups cubed potatoes
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
2 cups cubed Cheddar cheese
1 1/2 cups milk
1/4 cup sour cream

Directions:
Chop vegetables into very small pieces. In a large pot, melt 1 tablespoon butter or margarine over medium heat: cook and stir vegetables and beef , until beef is brown.
Stir in seasonings. Add broth and potatoes. Bring to a boil, then simmer until potatoes are tender, about 10-12 minutes.
Melt the remainder of butter and stir in flour. Add the milk, stirring until smooth.
Gradually add milk mixture to the soup, stirring constantly. Bring to a boil and reduce heat to simmer. Adjust seasonings to desired taste. Stir in cheese. When cheese is melted, add sour cream and heat through. Do not boil.
