Title:
Coconut Dulce de Leche (Dairy-Free Caramel Sauce) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can coconut milk
1 can coconut cream
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp kosher salt

Directions:
Mix coconut milk, coconut cream, sugar, and salt in heavy pan over medium heat--stir constantly until sugar is completely dissolved.
Increase heat to medium-high and boil until mixture is reduced to about half--stir occasionally, this should take about 20 min.
Cool completely before use.  Store covered in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Julie Julie
Description: 
This dairy-free caramel sauce makes me forget how much I love dairy!  Put it on cookies, ice cream, or just your spoon.
