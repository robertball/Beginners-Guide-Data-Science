Title:
Chinese Five Spice Air Fryer Butternut Squash Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5999336.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large butternut squash, peeled and cut into “fries”*
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 teaspoons sea salt
1 tablespoon Chinese five spice powder
1 tablespoon minced garlic
2 teaspoons black pepper

Directions:
Place cut squash in a large bowl and toss with oil and seasonings.
Place your butternut squash fries in your air fryer at 400 degrees for 15-20  minutes; shake every 5 minutes.
Once your fries are to your desired crispness, remove from fryer and season to taste with additional sea salt.

Submitter: 
Angela Sackett  Superhotmama
Description: 
These fries made with Chinese five spice are sweet and spicy, and they're a fiber-ful, lower carb alternative to french fries.  Made in the air fryer, they're less mess and can cook up while the rest of your meal is in the making.
