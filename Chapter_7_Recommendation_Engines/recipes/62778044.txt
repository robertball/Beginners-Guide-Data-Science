Title:
Tangy Cucumber  Tomato and Avocado Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium cucumbers, cubed
2 avocados, cubed
10-12 Cherry Tomatoes halved
4 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1 clove garlic, minced
2 tablespoons minced green onions (optional)
1/4 teaspoon salt
black pepper to taste
1/4 large lemon
1 lime

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine cucumbers, avocados, and cilantro. Stir in garlic, onions, salt, and pepper. Squeeze lemon and lime over the top, and toss. Cover, and refrigerate at least 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
ENAJE
Description: 
A great, fresh-tasting picnic salad, it combines avocados, cucumbers, tomatoes, garlic, and green onions with chopped cilantro and the bright flavors of lemon and lime juice. Add Cherry Tomatoes for color and taste.Modified from: Tangy Cucumber and Avocado Salad
