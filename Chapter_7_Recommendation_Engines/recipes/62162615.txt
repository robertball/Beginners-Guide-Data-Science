Title:
Southern Style Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Quarts berries or fruit
1/2 stick butter
1 cup flour
1 cup sugar
1 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Directions:
Place the butter in 13x9 pan and put in the oven. Preheat to 350. Stir together the flour, sugar, milk, and baking powder. When the butter is melted, remove pan from oven and pour in fruit. Pour batter over the fruit. Place in oven and bake for 45 minutes or until dough is done.

Submitter: 
Devin
Description: 
A luscious summer treat that is great warm with vanilla ice cream. I always make this with blackberries we pick in our backyard or peaches from our family in Georgia.
