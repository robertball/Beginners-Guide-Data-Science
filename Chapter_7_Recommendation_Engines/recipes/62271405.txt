Title:
Olive Croutons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bread cubes - best is Ciabatta bread, 4 medium slices, cubed
Extra Virgin Olive Oil - 1 Tablespoon
Olive Paste - 1 Tablespoon or more depending on your taste

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 190°C/375°F.
Put all the ingredients into a bowl and stir around to get a good coating.
Arrange the croutons on a baking sheet and place in the oven for 8 to 10 minutes or until golden.
Leave to cool on the baking sheet.

Submitter: 
Swiss Phil
Description: 
Another garnish-type recipe which I also serve with my Roasted Tomato soup. Great also in salads, particularly Greek ones.
