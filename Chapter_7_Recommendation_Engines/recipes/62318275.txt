Title:
smore sandwhich cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/376647.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (8 oz.) PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened
1 cup  powdered sugar
3/4 cup (1-1/2 sticks) butter, softened
1/2 tsp.  vanilla
2 cups flour
1/2 tsp.  baking soda
4 tsp. colored sugar
1/3 cup  JET-PUFFED Marshmallow Creme
2 squares BAKER'S Semi-Sweet Chocolate, melte

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 325ÂºF
Beat cream cheese powdered sugar butter and vanilla with electric mixer on medium speed until well blended
Add flour and baking soda
mix well.
Cover
Refrigerate 1 hour
ROLL dough to 1/8-inch thickness on lightly floured surface
Cut into 66 cookies using 2-inch festive cookie cutters
Sprinkle half of the cookies with colored sugar
Place all cookies 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets
BAKE 12 min or until lightly browned
Cool on baking sheets 1 min
Remove to wire racks cool completely.
SPREAD bottom side of each of the plain cookies with about 1 tsp. marshmallow creme
cover with remaining sugar cookies bottom-sides downto form sandwiches
Drizzle with melted chocolate.
