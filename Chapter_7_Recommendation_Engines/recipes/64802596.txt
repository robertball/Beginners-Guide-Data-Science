Title:
Pasta with Ricotta and Turmeric Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
160 grams fusilloni pasta
1 cup fresh ricotta, drained
1/4 grated Grana padano
1 tsp ground turmeric
1/2 tsp course ground black pepper
Salt to taste

Directions:
Bring to a boil a large pot of salted water. Cook the pasta until al dente as directed on the package.
Meanwhile, in a large mixing bowl, whisk the ricotta, grated Grana padano, turmeric, black pepper, and a bit of pasta water until creamy.
Drain the pasta, reserving a bit more pasta water. Fold the pasta into the sauce, adding a bit of pasta water if necessary. Add salt if desired.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
A quick and healthy meal for two is just minutes away. While fusilloni pasta cooks, whip up a sauce made with creamy fresh ricotta, ground turmeric, and add a touch of pepper.
