Title:
Oatmeal Scotchies Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/317292.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups sweet cream butter, at room temperature
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt (omit if using salted butter)
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
3 cups quick cook oatmeal
1 cup butterscotch chips
1/2 cup coconut flakes (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Beat together the butter, sugars, vanilla, and egg until semi-smooth. Slowly add flour, soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg and the oatmeal to the mixture. Beat until combined well. Fold in Butterscotch chips (and coconut flakes, if using). Drop by tablespoonfuls onto cookie sheet and bake for 7 to 8 minutes (If you prefer crisp & crunchy cookies, let them bake for 9-10 minutes).

Submitter: 
Chris Diaz
Description: 
Don't even thinkg that you can eat only ONE! Boy, are these cookies fabulous! They are so moist and chewy. If you prefer a crisper cooking, just bake for a couple of minutes longer. These are great cookies to send overseas to our troops.
