Title:
Sahara's Mango Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6013439.jpg
Ingredients:
1 mango peeled, seeded and cut into bite size cube chunks.
1/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
1/2 cup chopped cherry or grape tomatoes
1/2 tbsp green onion finely chopped
1/4  cup red onion finely chopped
1 small fresh jalapeno; seeded and finely chopped
2 Tbsp lime juice
1 tsp lemon juice
1 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
In a mixing bowl combine all ingredients. mix well then cover and refrigerate for an hour so flavors marinate. I like served with grilled chicken, pan seared pork chops,  tilapia or just scoop up with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Sahara B
Description: 
This spicy, fruity mix will pump up any dish. It's quick, easy and fantastic year round. A colorful, flavorful balance that is a great accompaniment with chicken, pork chops or pan seared tilapia.
