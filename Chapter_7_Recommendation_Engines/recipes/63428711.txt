Title:
White Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb powdered sugar
1 8 oz pkg cream cheese
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp vanilla
1 20oz can crushed pineapple, drained
1/2 cup coconut
1 cup chopped pecans
1 16 oz carton cool whip
2 graham cracker crusts

Directions:
Combine powdered sugar, cream cheese, & salt and beat until light. Stir in the next 5 ingredients. Pour into the graham cracker crust. Chill.
