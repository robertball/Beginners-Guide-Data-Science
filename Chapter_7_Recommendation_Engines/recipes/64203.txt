Title:
Asian Pilaf Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986054.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6.09 ounce) package NEAR EAST® Rice Pilaf Mix
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1/2 cup carrots, diced
1/4 cup peanuts, chopped
1/4 cup green onions, chopped
2 tablespoons red bell pepper

Directions:
Prepare Near East rice according to package directions, except omit butter or olive oil.
Stir in remaining 8 ingredients. Chill for 2 to 4 hours. Top with extra chopped peanuts right before serving, if desired.

Submitter: 
NEAR EAST®
Description: 
This is a cold rice salad with spicy undertones. Carrots, green onion, and peanuts help punctuate this salad with authentic Asian flavor.
