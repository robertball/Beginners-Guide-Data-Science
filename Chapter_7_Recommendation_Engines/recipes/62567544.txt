Title:
Candied Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. diced sweet potatoes
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup apricot juice or nectar
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Place sweet potatoes in a large saucepan with enough water to cover; bring to a boil and cook for 15 minutes, or until tender but firm. Drain and set aside.
In a large skillet over low heat, melt the butter. Stir in the brown sugar, apricot juice or nectar, and salt.  Add the sweet potatoes, and stir to coat.  Cook, stirring gently, until sweet potatoes are heated through and well glazed.
