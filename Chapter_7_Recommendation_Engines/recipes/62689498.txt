Title:
Flourless Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 oz. really good (45-50% cocoa)semisweet chocolate
3/4 cup butter
1 cup sugar, divided
4 eggs, separated

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees F
Melt chocolate and butter in a double boiler.
Stir together half of the sugar and four egg yolks, add to melted chocolate concoction.
Using an electric mixer, beat the egg whites; slowly add remaining 1/2 cup sugar until stiff peaks form.
fold mixture into chocolate.
pour into a greased 9-inch cake pan.
