Title:
Cofer Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ready to use pie crusts
2 cups chopped cooked chicken
1 cup frozen peas & carrots
1/2 can (15 oz) cut green beans
1 cup leftover mashed potatoes
10 oz can cream of chicken soup
1 tsp celery flakes

Directions:
Unwrap one pie crust & place in bottom of pie plate.  Combine remaining ingredients, mix well.  Pour into pie plate & top with remaining crust.  Vent top crust.  Bake 35 to 40 minutes at 400 degrees till crust is golden.  Let stand 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
ccofer
Description: 
We make this when we have rotisserie chicken left to use.  Always tasty.
