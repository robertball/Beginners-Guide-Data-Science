Title:
Oreo® Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6335643.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup shortening
1/3 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
2 tablespoons heavy cream, or more as needed
8 chocolate sandwich cookies (such as Oreo®), finely crushed

Directions:
Combine butter, shortening, and cream cheese in a bowl; beat with an electric mixer until creamy. Add vanilla extract and salt. Beat in confectioners' sugar, 1 cup at a time, until well incorporated. Add enough heavy cream to thin frosting to desired consistency. Fold in crushed cookies.

Submitter: 
metread
Description: 
This super yummy Oreo® frosting goes perfectly with a deep, dark chocolate cake. Makes enough to frost a 2-layer cake or 24 cupcakes.
