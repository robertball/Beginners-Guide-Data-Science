Title:
Louise's Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 10 oz. packages frozen brocolli spears, thawed and cut up
10-3/4 oz. condensed cream of celery soup
1-1/4 cups grated sharp cheddar cheese divided
1/4 cup minced green onions
1 cup crushed saltine crackers or potato chips

Directions:
Grease slow cooker. In large bowl combine broccoli, celery soup, 1 cup cheese and onions. Pour into crock pot. Sprinkle top with crushed chips/crackers, then with remaining cheese. Cover and cook on low 5-6 hours or on high 2-1/2 to 3 hours.
If desired, casserole may be spooned into a baking dish and garnished with additional grated cheese and broken potato chips; bake 5-10 minutes at 400 degrees.
