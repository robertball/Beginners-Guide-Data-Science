Title:
CLASSIC BEEF STROGANOFF Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp. salt			1 pound beef tenderloin, cut in 1/4-inch strips
2 Tbsp. butter
1 package sliced mushrooms
1/2 chopped onion
2 Tbsp. dry sherry
1 clove garlic, minced
2 Tbsp. butter
3 Tbsp. all-purpose flour
1 Tbsp. tomato paste
1 10 1/2 ounce can condensed beef broth
1 cup sour cream
6 ounces noodles cooked

Directions:
Combine 1 Tbsp. flour and salt; coat meat with mixture.  Heat frying pan, then add 2 Tbsp. butter.  When melted, add beef tenderloin strips and brown quickly on both sides.  Add mushroom slices, onion, and garlic; cook 3 or 4 minutes or until onion is crisp-tender.  Remove from pan.
Add 2 Tbsp. butter to pan drippings; blend in 3 Tbsp. all-purpose flour.  Add tomato paste.  Stir in cold meat stock.  Cook and stir over medium-high heat till thickened and bubbly.
Return browned meat, mushrooms, onion, and garlic to frying pan.  Stir in sour cream and sherry.  Cook slowly until heated through.  Do not boil.  Serve over hot buttered noodles.
