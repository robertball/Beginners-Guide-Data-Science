Title:
shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
1/2 cup cornstarch
1 pound (2 cups) butter
1 cup icing sugar

Directions:
Cream butter - add sugar & work together thoroughly until creamy.
Sift flour and corn starch & add
Hand beat with spoon until mixture breaks.
Drop by spoonful or add little flour to knead and roll out, cut into shapes.
Bake at 325 degrees.
