Title:
Mushroom, Beef and Barley Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/776575.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb Sirloin Steak, cut into 1 1/2 inch pieces
2 Tbsp Olive Oil
1 cup diced onion
1 cup diced carrots
1 12 oz pkg mushrooms, coarsely chopped
1/2 cup uncooked barley
1 (10 1/2 oz) can fat free, lower-sodium beef broth
5 cups water
1 tsp coarse salt
1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
2 cups chopped Swiss Chard (optional)

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat.  Add beef and saute until brown.  Add onion and carrots; saute about 7 minutes.  Add mushrooms; cook about 8 minutes.
Add barley, broth, water, salt and pepper; stir to combine.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer 40 minutes or until barley is tender.  Stir in Swiss chard, if using, and cook 5 minutes.  Maqkes about 6 cups.  Serves 6.

Nutritious Barley star in this comforting soup, perfect for knocking the chill out of cold winter days.  Recipe from www.relish.com and photo by Becky Luigart-Stayner.
