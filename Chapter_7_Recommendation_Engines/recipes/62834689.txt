Title:
Katie's Mango Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 fresh mango - peeled, pitted, and chopped
1/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
1 tablespoon red onion, finely chopped
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
1 tablespoon lime flesh, minced, OR 2 tablespoons lime juice

Directions:
Combine the mango, bell pepper, red onion, cilantro, and jalapeno pepper in a bowl; stir. Add the lime juice. Chill in refrigerator 1 hour.
Serving size about 1/2 a cup per person.

Submitter: 
greenthumb
Description: 
Rainbow-hued mango salsa is made with the flavors of red pepper, cilantro, red onion, jalapeno, and lime for a delicious accompaniment to fish.Modified from: Blackened Tuna Steaks with Mango Salsa
