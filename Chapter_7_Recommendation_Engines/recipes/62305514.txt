Title:
Baked Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz macaroni
8 tbsp butter
2 c Panko breadcrumbs
1/2 tsp ground mustard
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp salt
2 tbsp flour
2 c milk
8 oz sour cream
2 c sharp cheddar
2 c mild cheddar
4 slices American cheese slices

Directions:
Boil noodles in salted water for 8 minutes, set aside.
In saucepan, melt 4 tbsp butter, add breadcrumbs and toss to coat and lightly brown.  Set aside.
In saucepan melt 4 tbsp butter, blend in mustard, S&P, whisk in flour.  Cook for a minute or so, add in milk and sour cream.  Allow to cook until blended and slightly thickened.  Remove from heat.  Stir in cheeses and allow to melt.  Pour over noodles in baking dish.  Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
chefmom
Description: 
Angie's baked mac and cheese.  One recipe feeds 10.  A triple recipe will fill one rectangular chafer and will feed 30 people.  Saucy and creamy!
