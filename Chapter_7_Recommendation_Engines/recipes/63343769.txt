Title:
Simple Turkey & Veggie Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon olive oil
1 pound ground turkey
1 onion, chopped
1 cup water
1 (28 ounce) can canned crushed tomatoes, undrained
1 (16 ounce) can canned kidney beans - drained, rinsed, and half mashed
1 (16 ounce) can canned black beans - drained, rinsed, and half mashed
2 large carrots, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 (6 ounce) can diced chiles
1 tablespoon garlic, minced
2 tablespoons chili powder
1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
1 Tablespoon ground cumin

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat. Stir in half of onion, and cook until tender. Add turkey and cook until browned, breaking up larger chunks. Pour water into the pot. Mix in tomatoes, carrots, beans, and garlic. Season with chili powder, paprika, oregano, cayenne pepper, cumin. Bring to a boil. Add remaining onion and bell peppers. Reduce heat to low, and simmer 30 minutes.
