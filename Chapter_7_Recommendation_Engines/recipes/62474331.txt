Title:
jack-o-lantern pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds of hamburger
1 packet of taco seasoning
2 c. cheddar cheese, shredded
1 prepared pie shell for 2 pies
1 egg
1 medium onion, diced
1 (16 oz) can refried beans (optional)
1 (4 oz) can of mild chilies diced (optional)
lettuce
sour cream
salsa
black olives

Directions:
Preheat oven according to pie package directions.
Press one pie shell in bottom of pie pan, prick with fork and slightly bake.
Meanwhile cook hamburger with diced onions and chilies. Drain.
Add taco seasoning according to directions on pkg.
Layer refried beans on bottom of cooked pie shell.
Then layer hamburger mixture.
Top with cheese.
Cut a Jack-o-lantern face on the remaining pie shell and place on top of the layered pie.  Seal edges and brush top with egg.
Bake according to pie directions.
Serve with salsa, sour cream, lettuce and black olives

Submitter: 
4GiddyGirls
Description: 
Taco meat, veggies and cheddar cheese are layered in a Jack-o-lantern pie crust creating a healthy kid friendly meal for goblins of all ages.
