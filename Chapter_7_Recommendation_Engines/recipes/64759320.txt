Title:
Zucchini Lemon Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 cup vegetable oil (I like canola)
grated rind from 1 med. lemon
2 cups flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup sunflower seeds, shelled (raw or toasted)
1 cup sugar
1/4 cup lemon jc.
2 cups grated zucchini, unpeeled & unseeded (remove any skin blemishes)
2 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. baking powder

Directions:
In large bowl, beat eggs, sugar, oil, jc. and rind, using mixer until well blended.
By hand, fold in zucchini.
Mix flour, baking soda, salt and baking powder and fold gently into mixture.
Fold in sunflower seeds.
Bake in well-greased 9" x 13" cake pan @ 350 x ~ 50 min. or till tests done.
Cool & frost, if desired.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A delicious way to use all those zucchinis your neighbors & friends are trying to find good homes for! Top with a lemon glaze/icing for extra deliciousness (could even use a lemony cream cheese icing!!!)
