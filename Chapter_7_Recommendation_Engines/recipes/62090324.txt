Title:
Simply the Best Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf good Italian bread
1/4 c olive oil
2 garlic cloves
1/4 c. parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
Slice the loaf of bread into slices of desired thickness.
Brush each slice, front and back, with olive oil.
Bake on cookie sheet, approximately 15 min at 350 degrees, until toasted.
Rub toasted bread with garlic clove.
Sprinkle with parmesan cheese. Serve immediately.

Submitter:

Description:
