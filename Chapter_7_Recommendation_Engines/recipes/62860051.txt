Title:
Roasted Chickpea Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
1 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon lime juice
2 tablespoon water
1 can of chickpeas/drained and rinse
8 taco shells
lettuce, tomatoes, sour "cream" for toppings

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
In a large bowl, combine chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, cumin, salt lime juice and water.
Add chickpeas and marinate for at least 30 minutes
Place chickpeas on a baking sheet that has been sprayed
Bake for 20-30 minutes, stirring once during cooking, until chickpeas are slightly crispy.
Prepare taco shells according to package directions.  fill with chickpeas and toppings
