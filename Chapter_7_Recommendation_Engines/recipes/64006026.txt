Title:
Beef and Irish Stout Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean beef stew meat, cut into cubes (eye of round steak works well)
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
1 tsp chili powder
2 large onions, chopped
1 large rutabaga
2 cups chopped carrot
2 sticks of celery
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 can tomato paste
pinch of thyme
1 can beer

Directions:
Toss the beef cubes with the oil. In a separate bowl, stir together the flour, salt, pepper, and chili powder. Dredge the beef in this to coat. Put in slow cooker. Add the onions, rutabaga, carrots, celery and garlic. Stir the tomato paste and thyme into the beer pour over meat and vegetables the slow cooker. Cook on high 4 hours stirring occasionally. reduce to low heat for another hour.  Taste and adjust seasoning before serving.

Submitter: 
TJ
Description: 
This stew is great for St. Patrick's Day.  The mixture of the beef and beer (Guinness if you like) is awesome!  So good when served OVER mashed potatoes.Modified from: Beef and Irish Stout Stew
