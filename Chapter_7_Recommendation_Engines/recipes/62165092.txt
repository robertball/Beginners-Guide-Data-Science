Title:
Sunrise Strawberrry-Pineapple Tropical Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen pineapple chunks
1 cup frozen strawberries
1 cup crushed ice
1/2 cup orange sherbert
1 can frozen apple juice concentrate
5-7 large fresh strawberries

Directions:
First combine the first 5 ingredients in the blender.
Blend until well blended.
Add the fresh strawberries and pulse until the strawberry bits are dispersed.

Submitter: 
Sweet Southern Charm
Description: 
I came up with this one day when I had the urge for a smoothie and was tight on cash.It turned out better than I would have ever expected and it is so incredibly easy. This tropical delicious smoothie will satisfy any smoothie craving without breaking the bank.
