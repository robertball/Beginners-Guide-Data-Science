Title:
Pepper Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. Round Steak (3/4" thick) trimmed
1 Tbl. Vegetable oil
1/2 c. Onion - chopped
1 clove garlic - minced
1 beef bouillon cube
3/4 c. boiling water
1/8 tsp. pepper
1 can (14 1/2 oz) stewed tomatoes
1 med. green bell pepper - cut into rings
1/4 c. cold water
2 Tbl. cornstarch
2 Tbl. soy sauce
Hot cooked noodles

Directions:
Cut meat into 2" x 1" strips, brown in a skillet in oil for 10 minutes.  Add onion and garlic, cook for 3-4 minutes.
Dissolve bouillon in boiling water, pour into skillet.
Sprinkle meat with pepper.  Cover and simmer 35 - 40 minutes or until meat is tender.
Add tomatoes and green bell pepper.  Cover and simmer for 10 minutes.  Combine cold water cornstarch and soy sauce, stir into broth.
Bring to a boil, cook and sitr for 2 minutes.  Serve over noodles.
