Title:
Bruschetta I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/649376.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large tomatoes, seeded and chopped
2 T chopped fresh parsley
2 T chopped fresh chives
2 T balsamic vinegar
2 cloves garlic, minced
4 T minced fresh basil
1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
salt and pepper to taste
French Bread

Directions:
In a medium bowl combine all ingredients except bread.  Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, allowing flavors to meld before serving.  Toast bread before topping with mix.

Submitter: 
Tamara Gray
Description: 
Serve on toasted French bread with pesto, or enjoy it all by itself.Modified from: Bruschetta I
