Title:
zucchini chocolate chip cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup shredded zucchini
1 1/2 cup flour
3/4 cup sugar
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup chocolate chips(can be mini chips)
1 egg
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup milk
1 teaspoon vinnila extract
1 Tablespoon lemon juice

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
Line a 12 cupcake tin with paper liners
Mix all the wet ingredients together in a bowl. Keep the zucchini in a seperate bowl.
Combine all of the dry ingredients together.
Put the dry ingredence in the wet ingredence bowl gently fold them together.
Add the zucchini in.
Bake for 20 minutes
