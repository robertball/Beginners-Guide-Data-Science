Title:
Mom's Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small head cabbage, shredded
3 medium carrots, shredded
1/2 cup of green and sweet onion chopped
1 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup sugar
1/4 cup cider vinegar
1 teaspoon dry mustard
cracked pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine cabbage, carrots and onion. In a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise, sugar, dry mustard, pepper and vinegar. Pour over cabbage mixture and toss to coat. Serve with a slotted spoon.
