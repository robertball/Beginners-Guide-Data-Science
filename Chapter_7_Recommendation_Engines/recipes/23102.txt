Title:
Dill Sauce for Hamburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/491583.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
2 tablespoons dried dill weed

Directions:
In a small bowl combine mayonnaise, Worcestershire sauce and dill weed; mix well.

Submitter: 
FOXMOORQHS
Description: 
Yummy sauce to add to a plain hamburger and dress up the flavor.  My whole family loves it!
