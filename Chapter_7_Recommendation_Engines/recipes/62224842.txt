Title:
Cereal Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup corn syrup
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 cup peanuts
3 cups cheerios
3 cups corn flakes
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
In a big bowl combine the cheerios, corn flakes and peanuts.  In a pot combine the sugar, corn syrup and butter and bring to boil, remove from heat and add peanut butter, and vanilla stir well.  Pour over cereal mix and blend well.  Pour into a 9 x 13 inch dish and refridgerate until set, cut into squares and enjoy.
