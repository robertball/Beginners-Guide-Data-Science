Title:
White Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. Flour
2 Tbsp. Butter
1 Cup Milk
Dash Salt
Dash Pepper

Directions:
Melt butter in small sauce pan.
Add Salt and Pepper.
Slowly add Flour whisking until smooth and thickened.
Sauce will thicken upon standing.
