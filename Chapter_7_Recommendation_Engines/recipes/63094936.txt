Title:
Emeril's Essence Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 T paprika
2 T or less salt
2 T garlic powder
1 T black pepper
1 T onion powder
1 T cayenne pepper
1 T oregano
1T thyme

Directions:
Measure all ingredients into a glass jar.  Put the lid on and shake it up.

Submitter: 
None
Description: 
Great mix to use on meat, in sauces or soups.
