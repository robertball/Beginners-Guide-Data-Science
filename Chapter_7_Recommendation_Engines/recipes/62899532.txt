Title:
Best Chocolate Chip Cookie Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups butter, softened
1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
1 1/4 cups packed brown sugar
1 T vanilla
2 eggs
4 cups all-purpose flour
2 t baking soda
1/2 t salt
2 cups coarsely chopped nuts
1 package (24) ounces semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Heat oven to 375.  Beat butter, sugars, vanilla and eggs in large bowl with electric mixer on medium speed, or mix with spoon. Stir in flour, baking soda and salt (dough will be stiff).  Stir in nuts and chocolate chips.
Drop dough by level 1/4 cupfuls about 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheet. Flatten slightly with fork.  Baking 13 to 15 minutes or until light brown (centers will be soft.)  Cool 1 to 2 minutes; remove from cookie sheet to wire rack.

Submitter: 
DuffMan
Description: 
I've tried lots of chocolate chip cookie recipes and this is the best!  Make sure you measure out the cookie dough so that all the cookies are the same size.
