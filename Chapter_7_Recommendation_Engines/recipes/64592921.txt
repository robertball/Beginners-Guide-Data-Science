Title:
Pineapple Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1/4 cup sugar
1 1/4 cups flour
2 eggs
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup crushed canned pineapple, drained (save juice for icing)
1/2 tsp. almond extract
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
2 tbsp. flour
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
Icing:
1 1/2 cups icing sugar
3 tbsp. butter or margarine
5 tsp. pineapple juice

Directions:
Bottom layer:
Mix 1st 3 ingredients in bowl till crumbly.
Pack into an ungreased 9"x9" pan.
Bake @ 350 x 15 min.
Top layer:
Beat eggs slightly; add to remaining ingredients, stirring to mix.
Spread over shortbread base.
Bake @ 350 x 30 min. or until set and a rich brown color.
Icing:
Beat icing ingredients together, adding more juice a few drops at a time to get a nice spreading consistency. Spread over bars.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
These shortbread based pineapple bars always made an appearance anytime ppl gathered for coffee & goodies - Tupperware parties, potlucks, baby showers...
