Title:
Lasagna for Fifty Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lb AP Ground Beef
12 oz onions, finely chopped
2 cloves Garlic Cloves, minced
3 qt tomato sauce
1 qt tomato paste
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp basil, dried, crumbled
1 tbsp oregano, dried, crumbled
2 lb 8 oz lasagna noodles, dry
2 gal water, boiling
2 oz salt
2 tbsp vegetable oil
2 lb 8 oz mozzarella cheese, shredded
6 oz parmesan cheese, grated
2 lb 8 oz ricotta cheese or cottage cheese, dry or drained

Directions:
Cook beef, onion, and garlic until meat reaches an internal temperature of 155°F. Drain off fat.
Add tomato and seasonings to meat. Simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Cook noodles according to directions on p. 507. Store in cold water to keep noodles from sticking. Drain when ready to use.
Combine cheeses. Arrange in two greased 12 x 20 x 2-in counter pans in layers in the following order:
Meat sauce, 1 qt
Noodles, overlapping, 1 lb 12 oz
Cheeses, 1 lb 4 oz.
Repeat sauce, noodles, and cheese. Spoon remainder of meat sauce on top.
Bake at 350°F for 45 minutes - 1 hour. Let stand 15-20 minutes before cutting. Cut 4 x 6.
