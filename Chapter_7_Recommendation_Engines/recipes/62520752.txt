Title:
Oatmeal Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 Nutmeg
3 cups quick cooking oats
2 eggs
1 cup packed light brown sugar
1/2 cup granulated sugar
2 sticks butter
1/4 cup milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Beat butter and both brown and granulated sugars together until creamy.  Add the eggs, vanilla, milk, and mix until smooth. In a separate bowl combine flour, Cinnamon, salt, Nutmeg, and baking soda, mix with a fork.  Add to the butter mix slowly allowing it to mix well.  Add the Oats one cup at a time.  Mix well, then add the raisins and mix well.  Place rounded tablespoons onto a wax papered cookie sheet.  Bake for 10 min or until no longer gooey in the center.  Let cool for a minuet or two on a wire wrack then serve.

Submitter: 
Shannon Pavek

Hardy cookie with plump raisins and sweetened with brown sugar - a childhood favorite.Modified from: Mom's Raisin Oatmeal Cookies
