Title:
Simple and Flavorful Balsamic Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/384568.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1 teaspoon dijon mustard
1 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
freshly ground black pepper to taste
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk together balsamic vinegar, dijon mustard, sugar, salt, black pepper, and garlic until sugar and salt are dissolved.
While continuously whisking, slowly add the olive oil in a very thin, steady stream until emulsified.
Add more salt & ground black pepper to taste if necessary.

Submitter: 
TTV78
Description: 
It's so easy to make your own fresh & flavorful vinaigrette, you'll never waste your money on the store-bought stuff ever again!  This is such a versatile dressing.  Try it on mixed green salads, arugula, spinach, tomato slices, or steamed asparagus!  Any leftover vinaigrette will keep up to two weeks in the fridge.  Enjoy!
