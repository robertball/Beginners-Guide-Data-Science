Title:
Lemon Kamikaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/930854.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vanilla vodka
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger triple sec
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger limoncello liqueur
1 fluid ounce lemon juice
1/2 fluid ounce lime juice
1 lemon twist

Directions:
Pour the vodka, triple sec, limoncello, lemon juice, and lime juice into a cocktail shaker over ice. Cover, and shake until the outside of the shaker has frosted. Strain into a chilled martini glass, and garnish with a twist of lemon to serve.
