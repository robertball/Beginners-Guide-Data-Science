Title:
Sour Cream Blueberry Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2C soft butter or margarine
1C sugar
3 eggs
2C all-purpose flour
1t baking powder
1t baking soda
1C sour cream
1t vanilla
1t ground cardamon, opt.
2C blueberries, fresh or frozen (no need to defrost)
1/2C brown sugar

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar; add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. With wire whisk, mix dry ingredients together; add gradually to egg mixture, alternating with sour cream, ending with flour mixture. Stir in vanilla and cardamon. Fold in 1 cup blueberries. Pour half the better into well greased and floured pan, 9 x 13 x 2 inches. Cover with remaining blueberries. Sprinkle with brown sugar. Top with remaining batter. Bake at 325 degrees for 45-50 minutes or until cake tests done. Cool in pan 10 minutes. Remove pan to rack to finish cooling.
