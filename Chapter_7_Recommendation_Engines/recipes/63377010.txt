Title:
Rotisserie Ranch Chicken Taco Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/904372.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.25 ounce) package dry taco seasoning mix or 3 TBL homemade taco seasoning mix (such as Taco Seasoning I recipe from this site)
1 (1 ounce) package dry ranch dressing mix
2 cups reduced sodium chicken broth
3 cups cooked, shredded rotisserie chicken

Directions:
Mix together chicken broth, taco seasoning, and dry ranch dressing mix in a bowl.
Place shredded chicken in a slow cooker and pour chicken broth mixture over chicken.
Cover and cook on Low for about 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
This is a delicious, easy and healthy way to use up leftover chicken and stretch your grocery dollar.  Leftover rotisserie chicken works great in this.  The ranch dressing mix gives the chicken tacos a different but delicious twist.  All you need are four ingredients and a slow cooker for some awesome chicken ranch tacos.Modified from: Chicken Taco Filling
