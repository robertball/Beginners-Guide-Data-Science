Title:
zucchini wrapped pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small zucchini
12-16 oz pork tenderloin
olive oil
1/3c basil pesto

Directions:
preheat oven 450,line 15x10x1 baking pan with foil,cut zucchini lengthwise in thin slices(8),cut pork crosswise into 4 pieces,press to flatten
wrap pork with zucchini,place in pan,brush with oil
roast uncovered 18-20 min for 12oz,25-30 min 16oz(160)
spoon pesto over before serving
