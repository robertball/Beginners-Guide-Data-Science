Title:
Texas Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling:
4 cups peeled, sliced ripe peaches (about 2 lbs.)
2 tbsp. sugar
1 tsp. fresh lemon juice
1/4 tsp. vanilla
Batter:
6 tbsp. butter
1 1/4 cups all purpose flour
3/4 cup sugar
2 tsp. baking powder
1/8 tsp salt
Dash of cinnamon
1 cup milk
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 tbsp. turbinado sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine filling ingredients in a bowl and set aside.
For batter, place butter in an 8" square baking dish and place in oven for about 5 minutes or until butter melts.
In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder and cinnamon.
Combine milk and vanilla and add to flour mixture.
Stir until just moist.
Spoon batter over butter, spreading evenly. (Do not stir)
Spoon peach mixture over batter, gently pressing peaches into batter.
Bake for about 40 minutes, then sprinkle with turbinado sugar and bake for another 10 minutes or until crust is golden.
