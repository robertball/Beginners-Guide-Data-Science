Title:
Grape & Tarragon Chicken w/cream sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts, halved, boned, skinned (6 halves, about 3 lbs in all)
2 Tbsp butter (or olive oil)
1 Tbsp orange marmalade
1/4 tsp tarragon, crumbled
1/4 finely chopped yellow onion
1/3 cup dry white wine
1 cup seedless red or green grapes
1/2 cup heavy cream
1 tbsp corn starch
1 tsp fresh orange rind, grated (optional)
salt
peper

Directions:
Sprinkle chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Brown lightly in heated butter in a large frying pan.
Add marmalade, tarragon, onion and wine. Cover, reduce heat to low and simmer 10 minutes.
Add grapes, cover again and continue cooking about 10 minutes longer, until chicken is cooked through.
Using a slotted spoon, remove chicken to a warm serving dish; keep warm.
Combine 1 tablespoon of cream and cornstarch in a small dish. Add remainder of milk to liquid in pan, stir. Then add cornstarch & milk mixture, stir.
Bring to a gentle boil, stirring constantly.
Cook over medium heat until thickened, stirring frequently. Salt to taste. Pour sauce over chicken.

Really unique chicken w/cream sauce. I watched my dad make this back in the early 90s many times.  The cream sauce tastes great over white rice.
