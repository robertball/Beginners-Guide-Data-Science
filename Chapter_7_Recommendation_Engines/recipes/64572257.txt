Title:
Big Ray's Smashed Fingerlings with Jalapeños Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3608473.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds fingerling potatoes, halved crosswise if large
1/2 cup olive oil, divided
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
1/4 cup Sherry vinegar or red wine vinegar
1 Tbsp whole grain mustard
1  jalapeño, thinly sliced into rounds, seeds removed if desired
1/4 cup (lightly packed) torn flat-leaf parsley leaves

Directions:
Heat oven to 450°. Toss potatoes with 1/4 cup oil on a rimmed baking sheet; season with salt and pepper. Roast, tossing once, until golden brown and tender, 30–35 minutes. Let cool slightly, then lightly flatten with fork.
