Title:
Lasagna Toss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10oz package large shell macaroni, cooked according to package directions
1lb ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
16oz spaghetti sauce
1 Tbl parsley
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
seasoning to taste

Directions:
Brown beef with onion and preferred seasonings i.e. garlic powder, salt, pepper. Drain.
Mix spaghetti sauce and cooked pasta into the meat mixture.
Pour half of mixture into a 2 quart casserole dish.  Layer with cottage cheese and 1 cup of the mozzarella.  Sprinkle with parsley.
Add remaining half of the pasta and meat mixture. Top with rest of Mozarella.
Cover and bake at 350. for 30 minutes.
