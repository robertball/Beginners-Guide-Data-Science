Title:
falafel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sauce:
6 oz. yogurt
1/2 cucumber- peeled, seeded, finely chopped
1 tsp. dried dill weed
salt and pepper to taste
1 T. mayo
falafel:
2 c. chickpeas
1 onion, chopped
1/2 c. chopped parsley
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1 egg
2 tsp. cumin
1 tsp. coriander
1 tsp. salt
dash pepper
pinch cayenne
1 tsp. lemon juice
1 tsp baking powder
1 T. olive oil
1+ c. dry bread crumbs

Directions:
Combine sauce ingredients and chill at least 30 minutes.
In a large bowl, mash chickpeas till thick and pasty.  In a blender, process onion, parsley, and garlic till smooth.  Stir into chickpeas.
In a small bowl, combine egg, cumin, coriander, salt, pepper, cayenne, lemon juice, and baking powder.  Tir into chickpea mix along with oil.  Slowly add bread crumbs till mix in not sticky but will hold together; add more bread as needed.
Form 8 balls and flatten into patties.  Fry patties in 1 inch of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat till brown on both sides.
