Title:
Big Ray's Artichoke Linguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4496885.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz linguine
3 cups spinach
1 6 oz jar marinated artichokes
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup olive oil
2 Tbsp red wine vinegar
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper

Directions:
Cook linguine in salted water until al dente (about 8 minutes), drain.  Drain artichoke and roughly chop.  Toss linguine, spinach, artichokes, cheese, oil, vinegar, salt and pepper together in large bwl, serve.

Description: 
They may not look like much, but they can can completely transform a dish.  A little pasta, some spinach and a jar of marinated artichokes and you'r done!
