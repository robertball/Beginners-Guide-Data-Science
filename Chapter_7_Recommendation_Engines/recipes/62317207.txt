Title:
Ulimate Potato Leek Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/376041.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb hot Italian sausage, casings removed
2 tbs butter
2 tbs olive oil
2 leeks, light green and white part sliced thin
1 onion, chopped
4 large cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp dried basil
6 medium russet potatoes, sliced 1/4 inch
5 cups broth, or enough to just cover potatoes
1 cup whipping cream
1 bunch kale, leaves removed from stems, torn into bite size pieces
kosher salt
fresh ground black pepper

Directions:
Break up and brown the sausage in a large pot on med-hi.  Remove sausage from the pot and set aside in a bowl.
Turn the heat to med and add the butter and olive oil to the pot until melted.  Add the sliced leeks and a pinch of salt.  Cook for several minutes.
Add the chopped onion and a pinch of salt, cook for several more minutes or until the onions and leeks start to brown lightly.
Add the garlic and pinch of salt; cook for one min.  Add the basil and pinch of salt; cook for one min.
Add potatoes to the pot and cover with chicken broth.  Cook until potatoes are fork tender.  Mash to desired consistency.
Add sausage, cream, and kale and heat through.
Serve with crispy, buttered and broiled Italian bread.
