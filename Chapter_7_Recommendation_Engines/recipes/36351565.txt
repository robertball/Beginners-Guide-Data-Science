Title:
Chicken with Parmesan Fettuccine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2	tablespoons butter, divided
2	to 3 frozen boneless chicken breasts, (total about 1 pound), thawed, cut into bite size pieces
1	clove garlic, minced
3 ½	cups (28 ounces) chicken broth
6	ounces uncooked fettuccine, broken in half
½	cup whipping cream
½	cup grated Parmesan cheese
½	cup finely chopped green onion (green and white parts)
¼	teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
1.	Place large saucepan over medium heat.  Add 1 tablespoon butter.  When melted, add chicken and garlic.  Cook 3 minutes or until minutes or until white, stirring frequently.  Remove chicken and juices from saucepan; set aside on platter.
2.	Add broth to saucepan.  Bring to boil over high heat.  Add pasta.  Return to boil; reduce heat.  Cover and simmer 10 minutes or until just tender.  Drain, reserving 2 tablespoons broth.
3.	Return drained pasta to saucepan.  Add chicken and any accumulated juices and cream.  Gradually add Parmesan, stirring constantly.  Stir in onion, black pepper and remaining 1 tablespoon butter.  Cook 2 minutes or until thickened, stirring constantly.  Add reserved broth to thin, if necessary.
