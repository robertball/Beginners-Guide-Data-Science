Title:
Chocolate-Banana Soy Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4461302.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup chocolate soy milk
1 packet Rich Milk Chocolate Flavor CARNATION BREAKFAST ESSENTIALS® LIGHT START™ Complete Nutritional Drink
1 banana
1/2 cup ice cubes

Directions:
Place soy milk, Carnation Breakfast Essentials Drink, banana and ice in blender; cover. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Carnation Breakfast Essentials
Description: 
Try soy milk with Carnation Breakfast Essentials® Light Start™ to start off your morning right. Take a look at this Chocolate-Banana Soy Smoothie today!
