Title:
Trish Yearwood - Easy Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small frozen pie crust
1/2 basket cherry tomatoes cut in halves and salted with sea salt
1 pkg any sausage you like (turkey or pork)
6 eggs
1 tsp baking soda
salt/pepper
2 cups grated cheese

Directions:
Brown sausage
Mix together the following:
eggs
tomatoes
baking powder
cheese
salt/pepper
Add all to 2 small pie crusts
Bake 350 degrees
