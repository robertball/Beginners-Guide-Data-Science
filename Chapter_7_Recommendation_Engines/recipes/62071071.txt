Title:
raspberry-mandarin whip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese
1/2 cup sugar
12 oz cool whip
2 sliced bananas
1 small can mandarin oranges, drained
1 1/2 packages frozen raspberries

Directions:
Mix together the cream cheese and sugar.  Fold in cool whip.  Add fruit.  Spread into 9x13" pan.  Place in freezer for at least 4 hours.  1/2 hour prior to serving, remove from freezer.

Submitter: 
Chmeliar
Description: 
This is great as a sweet compliment to a meal.  It works well for pot lucks.
