Title:
Quick & Spicy Brown Rice with Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 medium onion
1/2 tbsp. vegetable oil
2 cups Instant Brown Rice
1 3/4 cups water
3/4 cup medium salsa
2 dashes chili powder
1/2 cup sharp cheddar

Directions:
1. Sautee onion in oil until transluscent in a medium saucepan
2. Add water and bring to boil. Add rice and cover. Reduce to simmer and cook for 5 minutes.
3. Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes or until water is absorbed.
4. Add cheese, stir to melt and serve!

Submitter: 
Sola
Description: 
Versatile recipe that can be served with Mexican food or even fish sticks. Adjust the amount of chili powder or type of salsa for your desired spiciness! Take away the cheese for a completely vegetarian meal.
