Title:
Cajun Chicken Andouille Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound chicken, cut into 3/4" pieces
4oz andouille sausage, fully cooked, halved lengthwise and cut into 1/2" pieces
1 can crushed tomatoes
1-1/2c fresh or frozen/thawed corn
1c sliced okra (fresh or frozen/thawed)
1 medium onion, chopped
1 medium green pepper, chopped
2 ribs celery, sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
3/4t salt
1/2t Cajun seasoning
4c chicken stock

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in slow cooker.
Cover and cook 6-8 hours on low.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
