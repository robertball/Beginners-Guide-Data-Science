Title:
Lemon Verbena/Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup sugar
1/3 cup chopped, fresh lemon verbena leaves
1/4 cup cold butter
1 and 3/4 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1 cup blueberries (I used frozen, wild)
1 egg
3/4 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Process sugar, lemon verbena, and flour in a food processor.
Add butter, salt and baking powder. Pulse twice.
Toss blueberries into flour mix.
Beat egg, milk and vanilla.
Stir in dry ingredients just until mixed.
Fill greased muffin tins 2/3 full.
Bake at 400 for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
SIXMITTS
Description: 
Lemon verbena smells fabulous in the garden and can be dried for potpourri, but it is fragrant and tasty in baked goods as well.
