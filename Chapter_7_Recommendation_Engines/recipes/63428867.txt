Title:
Large sugar cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2c.sugar
3 eggs
1 t. Soda
1/2 t salt
5c flour
1c butter
1c sour cream
3 t. Baking powder
1 T vanilla

Directions:
mix sugar and butter until smooth.  Add sour cream and mix well.  Add eggs and beat until smooth.  Add vanilla.  Add dry ingredients and chill.  Roll dough 1/2 in thick.  Cut with cookie cutter.  Bake at 350 for 7-8 minutes.
