Title:
Mother Earth's Creamy Pistachio fruit fluff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/480931.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (20 oz.) can crushed pineapple (undrained)
2 (3.4 oz.) boxes instant pistachio pudding mix (unprepared)
8 oz.sour cream
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
1 (8 oz.)  container extra creamy cool-whip
2 (15 oz.) cans mandarin orange segments (drained)
1 (16 oz.)  jar maraschino cherries (drained and halved)
2 bananas (sliced)

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine both boxes of the dry pistachio mix with the 2 cans of undrained pineapple. Stir together until well blended.
Add 8 oz. sour cream and mix well.
Stir in walnuts.
Fold in the cool-whip.(make sure cool-whip is completely thawed before adding.)
Stir in the drained mandarin oranges.
Drain the maraschino cherries and slice in half. Add to the mixture.
Chill for a minimum of 2 hrs. Add the sliced bananas into the fluff right before serving. (prevents them from browning before presenting to guests.)

Submitter: 
Terra
Description: 
A classic fluff gets a yummy twist with adding sour cream to the mix. A creamier and richer version that has a taste that is sure to please. Incredibly simple to make. This recipe makes enough for a potluck or a large family gathering.
