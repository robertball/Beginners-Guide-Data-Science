Title:
Heavenly Rice Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cooked and salted rice, cooled
2 pkgs (3 oz) lemon or lime jello, can use sugar free
1 small can crushed pineapple, drained
1 cup whipped cream or topping

Directions:
Mix up your jello, according to box directions and let stand until set.  Mix in cooled rice and 1 small can pineapple.  Stir in whipped cream or topping and refrigerate until set.

Submitter: 
REDTEETOP
Description: 
An easy rice salad dish for hot summer days and great for potluck dinners.  Rice is a big surprise and everyone loves this.  I like to make this the night before using leftover rice.  My mom gave me this recipe when I was a kid.
