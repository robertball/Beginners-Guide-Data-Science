Title:
Crock Pot Stuffed Green Pepper Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Large Green Peppers, Cut into 1 inch cubes
1 Pound Ground Beef
1 28 oz Jar Spaghetti Sauce
1 28 oz Can of diced tomatoes (undrained)
1 32 oz Can of Beef Broth
1 Medium Onion, diced
1 Cup Uncooked Rice

Directions:
Brown ground beef and drain. Add ground beef and all remaining ingredients to crock pot, stir and set on low for 8 hours. Voila!!

Submitter: 
The Richmond Inn
Description: 
Easy Stuffed Pepper Soup - All the ingredients, less than half the work!! I like this recipe because you do not have to precook the rice.
