Title:
Grandma's Sweet Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups onion, chopped
4 cups cabbage, chopped
4 cups green tomatoes, chopped
4 cups green peppers, chopped
1/2 cup salt
1 1/2 Tbsp. mustard seed
3/4 Tbsp. celery seed
4 cups sugar
3 cups white vinegar
1 cup cold water

Directions:
Combine first 4 ingredients.  Rinse, drain and place in large bowl or pot.
Sprinkle with salt and let stand overnight.
Rinse and drain.  Pour into large pot.
In another large bowl, combine remaining ingredients and pour over chopped mixture.
Heat to a full boil.  Boil and stir for 5-10 minutes.
Place in hot canning jars and seal.
Makes 9 pints.
