Title:
Shredded Mexican Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 stalks of celery washed and cut in half
1 10 oz can of Rotel
1 14 oz can of beef broth
1/2 cup of white wine
5 whole cloves of garlic peeled
2 bay leaves
1 tsp cumin
1.5-2 lb top sirlion steak
water as needed

Directions:
In a dutch oven place first 7 ingredients into pot and mix well; Place beef on top.
Cover and heat on medium-low heat for 3 hours; stirring occasionally- adding water to pot if needed.
Remove beef and set on a plate to cool; slightly.  Meanwhile remove celery ribs and bay leaves from Dutch oven.  Using an immersion blender blend broth until it is smooth.
Shred beef with a fork and return to the pot.
Heat over medium low heat uncovered for 1 hour to reduce liquid.
