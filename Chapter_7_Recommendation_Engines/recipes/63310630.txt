Title:
Pumpkin Pie Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/875272.jpg
Ingredients:
•1 banana (peeled, frozen, diced)
•~1/3 cup pumpkin puree
•~1/3 cup FF vanilla Greek yogurt
•~3/4-1cup lt vanilla soy milk (or milk of choice)
3 Tbsp Sugar (or Splenda, Stevia, Truvia)
1 tsp of pumpkin spice (Cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and allspice)
•Few shakes of cinnamon
•1-1/2 tsp vanilla
•4-5 ice cubes

Directions:
1. Combine all ingredients in blender.
2. Blend until smooth.
