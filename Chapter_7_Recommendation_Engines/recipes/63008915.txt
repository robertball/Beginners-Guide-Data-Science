Title:
Turkey Noodle Soup (WWPP=3) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup each chopped onion, celery, carrot
4 cans chicken broth
1 can 98% free cream of chicken soup
2 cups cubed turkey or chicken
6 ozs. egg noodles

Directions:
Soften veggies in 1 cup broth by simmering until tender.  Add the rest of the broth and all other ingredients.  Add salt and pepper to taste. Cook until noodles are tender. You may want to add more broth if too thick.
