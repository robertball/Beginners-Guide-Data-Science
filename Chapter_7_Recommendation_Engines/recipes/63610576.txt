Title:
Amaranth Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup rice flour
1/2 cup sorghum flour
1/2 cup amaranth
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 egg
1/2 cup unsweetened apple sauce
1 cup almond or soy milk

Directions:
Heat a griddle on medium heat.
Combine the rice flour, sorghum flour, amaranth, baking powder and cinnamon.  Add the egg, apple sauce and almond milk, stir to combine.
Spray griddle with vegetable spry oil, Pour about 1/4 cup batter for each pancake onto preheated griddle.  Cook about 3 minutes, flip pancakes, and cook approximately another three minutes. Continue frying pancakes with remaining batter.

Submitter: 
sueb
Description: 
These pancakes were developed to be dairy and gluten free.  The slight crunch of amaranth makes these memorably delicious!
