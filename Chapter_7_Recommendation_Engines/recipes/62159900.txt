Title:
Aunt Nancy's Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 LB      Ground Beef
½ LB     Bacon
1 Med.  Onion
(2 ) 1LB  12 OZ can   Baked Beans (Bushes or M&M)
1- 15 OZ can   Kidney Beans– drained
1 - 15 OZ can   Butter Beans —  drained
½ Cup   Dark Brown Sugar
½ Cup   granulated Sugar
½ Cup   Ketchup
½ TBsp  Dry Mustard
2 TBsp   Dark Molasses

Directions:
Cook beef in skillet and drain off fat drippings.  Cut up bacon in bite size pieces and cook.
Drain off drippings. Discard all drippings.
Open cans of beans and drain off Kidney and Butter beans. DO NOT DRAIN BAKED BEANS.
Chop onion.
Mix all ingredients together and place in a 9 X 13 Aluminum pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes covered and 10 minutes uncovered.

Submitter: 
Lindsay
Description: 
A wonderful rich and flavorful dish that leaves people coming back asking for more!  I usually make this for family functions in honor of my Aunt Nancy (it was her recipe) and people always ask for the recipe.
