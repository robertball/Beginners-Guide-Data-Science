Title:
Green Chili Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 small can evaporated milk
1/2 lb. Velveeta Cheese, cubed
1 sm. can pimentos
1 small can chopped greed chilies
1 lb. ground beef, lightly browned
1 dozen tortillas (taco fold)
1/2 lb. longhorn cheese, grated
1 c. chopped onions

Directions:
Lightly brown meat and onions; add longhorn cheese.
Fill taco shells with meat mixture and place in 9 x 13" baking dish.
While meat is cooking, heat soup, milk, and Velveeta cheese, in top of double broiler until melted, add chillies and pimentos.
Pour sauce over top or tortillas, cover with foil.
Bake 30 minutes at 350.  Serve with brown beans and green salad.
