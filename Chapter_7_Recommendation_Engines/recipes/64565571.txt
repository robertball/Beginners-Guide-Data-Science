Title:
The Best Waffle Iron Grilled Cheese Sandwich!!!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices of bread
1 slice of cheddar cheese
1/4 stick of butter (I always put more, of course)

Directions:
Turn on the waffle iron.
Spread butter on one piece of bread and lay it face down on the waffle iron.
Put the slice of cheese on top of the slice of bread.
Take the second slice of bread and butter both sides.
Lay the slice of bread on top of cheese and close waffle iron.
Let cook for 5-7 minutes or until golden brown.
Eat it.

Submitter: 
Tiane
Description: 
I love grilled cheese, and I wanted a faster and yummier way to make it, so one day I decided to make one in a waffle iron! Best decision I have ever made!!!
