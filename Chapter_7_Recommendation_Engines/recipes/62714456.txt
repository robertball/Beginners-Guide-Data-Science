Title:
CHILLED VEGETABLE SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
¾ cup vinegar
¼ cup oil
1 medium bell pepper, chopped fine (I used ½ bell pepper)
1 medium onion, chopped  fine (I used ½ large onion because larger onions are milder)
3 celery ribs, diced fine
1 (7 ounce) jar pimento, undrained
1 (15 ounce) can small sweet green peas, drained   (LeSeur brand)
1 (14 ounce) can French-cut green beans, drained then, rinse and drain  (always rinse and drain canned green beans to get the can taste out)
1 (11 ounce) can white shoepeg corn, drained

Directions:
Bring first 3 ingredients to a boil in a small saucepan over medium heat; simmer, stirring often 5 minutes or until sugar dissolves.  Remove dressing from heat, and cool for 30 minutes.
Stir together chopped bell pepper and next 8 ingredients in a large bowl.
Gently stir in dressing.
Cover and chill salad in refirgerator for at least 8 hours or overnight.
Serve with a slotted spoon.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
The ingredients for this salad can be kept on hand to make without having to go to the grocery store.   The vinegar based mariade can be kept in the refrigerator for a long time.  This salad needs to be made one day in advance.
