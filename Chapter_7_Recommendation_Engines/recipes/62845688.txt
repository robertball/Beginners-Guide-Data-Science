Title:
Vegan Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/828124.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Romaine head lettuce
1/2 bag coleslaw
2 Roma tomatoes
1 red onion
1 avocado
1 red pepper
1 sm can sliced black olives
1 cup frozen corn
Tortilla strips bag (in store with croutons)
1 can black beans
1 can chopped tomatoes with chiles
1 can pinto beans
1 sm can chopped green chiles
1 pkg taco seasoning

Directions:
Clean, remove ribs, & chop Romaine. Remove seeds & stems from tomatoes and pepper. Chop red onion. Slice avocado chunky and soak in lemon water for 2 minutes then drain. Rinse beans & drain olives.
Mix Romaine & coleslaw together and lay on large serving plate. Top with prepared veggies: tomatoes, onion, avocado, pepper, olives, and corn. Sprinkle tortilla strips over top.
Mix together Rotel, rinsed beans, chiles, and seasoning.
Serve as a side. Consider serving a Pico de Gallo as well.

Submitter: 
Margaret Havenar
Description: 
A healthier alternative to the usual Mexican salad. This recipe is easy to tweak so you'll have your own variation to suit your taste in no time!
