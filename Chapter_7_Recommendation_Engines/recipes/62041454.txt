Title:
Snuggler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup skim milk
1/4 cup irish cream liquor
1 tbs chocolate nesquick
1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Heat milk for 1 minute on high in the microwave (or you could heat it on the stove if you want.) Milk should be at a slightly higher temprature than you would want to drink it at.
Add remaining ingredients and combine well.

Description: 
A warm, slightly alcoholic beverage. Nice to drink on a cold evening.
