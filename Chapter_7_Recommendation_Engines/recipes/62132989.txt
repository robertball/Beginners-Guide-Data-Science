Title:
Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 to 10 medium cucumbers
2 to 3 tablespoons salt
1 cup salad dressing (Miracle Whip)
1 tablespoon vinegar
2 tablespoons sugar
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Wash, peel and slice cucumbers as thin as possible.
In a large bowl heavily salt cucumbers and let sit for 30 minutes.
Drain cucumbers for 15 to 30 minutes.
Meanwhile mix salad dressing, vinegar, sugar, garlic powder and black pepper.
Put cucumbers in large bowl and mix with dressing.
Cover and refrigerate overnight.

Submitter: 
Nita Rockwell Minto
Description: 
Cucumbers in a creamy dressing makes the an easy and delicious, make ahead, dish for any meal. Best if refrigerated overnight.
