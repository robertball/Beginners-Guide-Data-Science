Title:
Potato Bacon Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/500391.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups peeled, cubed potatoes
1 cup chicken broth
8 bacon strips
1 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped celery
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup, undiluted
1 3/4 cups milk
1 cup sour cream
1/2 teaspoon salt
Dash pepper
Cheddar cheese
French fried onions

Directions:
In a covered 3-qt. saucepan, cook potatoes in broth until tender. Meanwhile, cook bacon in a skillet until crisp; remove to paper towels to drain. In the same skillet, saute onion and celery in drippings until tender; drain. Add to undrained potatoes. Stir in soup, milk, sour cream, salt and pepper. Cook over low heat for 10 minutes or until heated through (do not boil). Crumble bacon add to soup.  Put soup in bowl, sprinkle cheese and fried onion on top.
