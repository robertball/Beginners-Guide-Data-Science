Title:
Old Fashioned Baked Potato Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
olive oil
salt
potatoes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Wash potatoes, poke them with holes, coat with oil and salt.
Place on top rack of oven.
Put cookie sheet underneath to catch drippings.
Cook for 1.25 to 1.5 hours.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
Baked potatoes are so much better in the oven. Give it a try.
