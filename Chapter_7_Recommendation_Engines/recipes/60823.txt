Title:
Coco Lopo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4502194.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup gin
1 cup 151 proof rum
1 cup vodka
1 cup tequila
1 cup grenadine syrup
1 (8 ounce) can cream of coconut

Directions:
Pour the gin, rum, vodka, tequila, grenadine, and cream of coconut into a blender; fill with ice; blend until smooth.
