Title:
Mediterranean Zucchini Shrimp "Pasta" Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 clove garlic
2 tbs evoo
1 zucchini
1 C cherry tomato
1/2 lb medium size cooked cleaned shrimp
1/4 C fresh basil
1 tbs capers
8 black olives
2 tbs feta cheese

Directions:
Mince the garlic.
In a small bowl combine it with the evoo and set aside.
Slice the zucchini into thin ribbons.
Halve the cherry tomatoes.
Chiffonade the basil.
Drain the capers.
Slice the olives
In a large bowl combine the tomato, shrimp, basil, capers, and olives.
Fold in the zucchini "pasta" and feta cheese crumbles.
Drizzle the evoo and garlic mixture, tossing gently, and serve.

Submitter: 
Dragonfly
Description: 
Slicing the zucchini into thin ribbons makes it look like a pasta salad.
