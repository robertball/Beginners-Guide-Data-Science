Title:
Kale banana protein smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5160201.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana (without peel)
1 cup kale
1/3 cup blueberries
6 oz almond milk (unsweetened)
6 oz ice
1 scoop protein powder

Directions:
1. Combine in a blender: banana, blueberries, kale and almond milk.  Use ice crushed mode or food processor mode to blend all ingredients to where kale is breaking down. About two minutes total.
2. Add in protein and blend for up to two more minutes.
3.  Add in ice. Blend until ice broken up and smoother.
4. Pour into glass and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Luette Davis Drumheller Kanas
Description: 
Delicious and healthy smoothie... perfect for breakfast or after workout. Added blueberries for an antioxidant boost. A new look for my favorite Belgian beer glass!
