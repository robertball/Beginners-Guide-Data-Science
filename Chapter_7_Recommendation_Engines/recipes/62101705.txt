Title:
Blackberry Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
2 tablespoons cornstarch
6 cups blackberries
2.5 cups flour
1 tablespoon fresh baking powder
1 tsp salt
1.5 cups sugar
1/2 cup butter melted (divided)
1 tablespoon vanilla
2 cups milk

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees
Whisk together sugar and cornstarch and toss with blackberries.  Pour blackberries into a lightly oiled 13x9x2 inch pan. Drizzle berries with 1/4 cup of butter
Whisk together flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder.  Add milk, vanilla and 1/4 cup butter and stir till combined but still slightly lumpy.  Pour batter over berries
Bake at 350 degrees 55-60 minutes till crust is golden brown
Serve warm with heavy or whipped cream

Submitter:

Description: 
Easy blackbery Cobbler My father-in-law grows huge blackberrys and always has tons. I tried a number of recipes and none really hit the spot. So created this one and it worked so well that my fiance said it was the best she had ever had
