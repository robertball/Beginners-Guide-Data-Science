Title:
Champagne Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
4 oz Butter
6 oz Champagne
1/2 cup Mushrooms, sliced
1/4 cup Tomato, diced
1/3 cup Cream
1 tsp Garlic, minced
1 tsp Shallots, minced
1 tsp Capers
3 tbl Scallions, minced
1 tsp Fresh parsley, chopped
Lemon juice, 1 squeeze or to taste
Salt, to taste
Fresh ground black pepper, to taste

Directions:
Melt butter in pan over medium high heat, but do not brown. Add shrimp, garlic and shallots. Saute until shrimp turn opaque.
Add tomato, mushrooms, scallions, and capers. Saute for 1-2 minutes.
Deglaze pan with champagne. Simmer liquid until reduced by about half. Add cream, and simmer until sauce thickens slightly. Season to taste with lemon juice, salt and fresh ground pepper.
To serve, top with chopped fresh parsley.

Description: 
This heavenly shrimp is wonderful for special occasions. Serve over angel hair pasta for special meal.
