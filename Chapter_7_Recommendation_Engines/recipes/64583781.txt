Title:
Spicy Korean Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3685161.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp Korean chili paste (Gochujang)
2 Tbsp White vinegar
1 Tbsp Raw sugar
1 Tbsp Honey
1 tsp sesame seeds
1/4 tsp minced garlic

Directions:
Mix the above ingredients in a bowl well.
Stir until the sugar dissolves.
Store in fridge.

Submitter: 
Lori Rowley-Sipple
Description: 
I can't get enough of Korean food! This dipping sauce is great for ANYTHING! And this is amazing fast!
NOTE: If you can't have soy, you could replace the Gochujang with Sambal Olek.
