Title:
Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Gorgonzola cheese sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
SPROUTS:
2lb. Brussels Sprouts, trimmed and halved
3tbsp. olive oil
salt and black pepper
SAUCE:
3  tbsp. butter
4 slices of bacon, diced
2 cloves of garlic, minced
1 cup heavy cream
3 oz. Gorgonzola cheese, crumbled
2 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
zest of 1 lemon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 with a foil-lined baking sheet inside. Trim sprouts by cutting off ends and halving through the root end. Toss Brussels Sprouts with oil, salt and pepper in a large bowl. Transfer sprouts to hot baking sheet and roast until beginning to brown about 10 minutes; stir and roast 10 minutes more. Some leaves with blacken that's ok.
In a large saute pan over medium heat cook bacon until crisp, remove from pan and drain on paper towel. Return pan to heat and melt butter, add garlic and cook but do not brown. Stir in cream add bacon and simmer for 2 minutes. Add Gorgonzola and lemon juice. simmer for anther 2 min thicken up should coat the back of a spoon. Toss roasted Roasted Brussels Sprouts with sauce to coat, then season with salt and pepper, Garnish Brussels spouts with lemon zest.

Submitter: 
Krissy16
Description: 
Roasting Brussels Sprouts helps take away the bitter flavor then they are added with cream, garlic, bacon and Gorgonzola cheese. This makes a wonderful side dish for special occasion.
