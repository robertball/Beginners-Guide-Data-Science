Title:
Cornstarch Pudding (Vintage Vanilla Pudding) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
3 tablespoons Corn Starch
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 cups milk
2 egg yolks, slightly beaten
1-1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Combine sugar, corn starch and salt in medium saucepan. Gradually stir in milk until smooth.
Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly, and boil 1 minute.
Stir in vanilla.
Add egg yolks. Pour in a serving bowl OR pour equal amounts into 4 individual serving bowls.
Cover and chill a minimum of 30 minutes before serving
