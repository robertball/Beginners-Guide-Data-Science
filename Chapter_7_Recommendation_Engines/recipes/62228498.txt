Title:
Breakfast Casserole II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package ground pork breakfast sausage
12 eggs
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 1/4 cups milk
3/4 onion, minced
6 cloves garlic, minced
1 (8 ounce) sliced mushrooms,
1 (32 ounce) package tater tots
1/2 cup shredded pepper jack cheese

Directions:
Thaw tater tots and layer on bottom of greased 9x13 baking dish.
Place sausage in a skillet over medium-high heat, and cook until evenly brown. Saute onion and garlic.  Drain.  Layer on top of potatoes.
Saute' mushrooms and layer on top of potatoes.
Add sliced or shredded pepper jack cheese on top.
In a large bowl, beat together the eggs, condensed cream of mushroom soup, and milk and pour over all.
Can now proceed to baking or let sit overnight.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour to 1 hour and 15 minutes or until cheese is melted and eggs have set.
