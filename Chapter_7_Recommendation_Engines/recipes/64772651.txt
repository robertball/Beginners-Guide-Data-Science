Title:
Eastern Shore Chicken Chesapeake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large chicken breasts
2 large tomatoes
8 ounces cream cheese softened
1/2 c sour cream
2 TB Mayo
1 TB lemon juice
1 Tea Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tea dry mustard
1/2 tea garlic salt
1 TB milk
1 TB Old Bay Seasoning
1/2 pound lump crab meat

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
1.  Flatten chicken breasts and slice in half
2.  Marinate chicken for 30 minutes in lemon/garlic/olive oil
3.  While chicken marinates, combine crab imperial ingredients and set aside
4.  Cook chicken in skillet in light olive oil, 4 minutes each side
4.  Grease 1 quart casserole dish
5.  Place chicken in casserole dish
6.  Top chicken with sliced tomatoes
7.  Top with Crab imperial
8.  Bake at 350 for 30 minutes or until crab sauce is slightly brown and bubbly
Crab imperial sauce -   Blend softened Cream cheese, sour cream, mayo, Worcestershire, dry mustard, salt, milk, old bay....Fold in crab meat

Submitter: 
Lynn Mauk
Description: 
Blending local spices using Chesapeake Blue Crab lump, local tomatoes and Old Bay creamy hot crab dip
