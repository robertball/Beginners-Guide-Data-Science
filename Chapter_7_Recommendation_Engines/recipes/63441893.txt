Title:
Rockin's Marvelous Mint Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/937286.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup softened butter
2/3 cup sugar
1 egg
2 1oz. squares unsweetened chocolate squares, melted and cooled
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 3/4 cup flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
24 Andes Mints halved
2 1oz. squares white baking chocolate

Directions:
In large mixing bowl cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
Add the egg, cooled unsweetened chocolate and vanilla and mix well.
Combine the flour and the salt and gradually add to the creamed mixture and mix well.
Cover and refrigerate one hour.
Divide dough in half and roll into a one inch log. Slice each log into 12 equal pieces.
Roll each piece into a ball and place on a greased cookie sheet two inches.
Dip a glass with a flat bottom in some granulated sugar and flatten each dough ball slightly.
Bake at 400? for 5-7 minutes or untill firm.
When the cookies are done and fresh out of the oven place an opened Andes mint on each one.
Once the chocolate melts take the tip of a knife and swirl the mints slightly spreading a little to cover the cookie top.
Melt the white chocolate and drizzle some on each cookie with the back of a spoon.
Remove from the pan when cooled.
