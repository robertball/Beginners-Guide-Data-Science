Title:
Thai Fried Bananas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cooking bananas
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup rice flour
1/4 cup sesame seeds
1/2 cup coconut
1/3 cup water
1/8 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 cup sugar
Oil for frying

Directions:
Cut cooking bananas into fourths.  Mix remaining ingredients.  Coat bananas with mixture.  Fry in hot oil until lightly brown.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
Fried bananas without the wrapper.
