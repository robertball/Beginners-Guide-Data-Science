Title:
JR'S Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Roma tomatoes seeded & diced.
1/2 of a small white onion diced.
3 cloves of Galric diced.
2Jalepeno's seeded and diced.
6 Serrano chiles diced.
1'4 tsp. of lime zest.
2 Tablespoons of diced cilantro.
Coarse salt and freshly pepper to taste
1'4 teaspoon Chili powder.                             The juice from the lime.

Directions:
In a medium mixing bowl, toss in all ingredients.Add salt and pepper to taste.Cover with plastic for an hour in the fridge or for more intense flavor,let it stay over night :)

Description: 
A mild salsa that my other half love's.
