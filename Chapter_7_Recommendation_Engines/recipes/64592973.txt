Title:
Blueberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5 cups blueberries
1/4 cup water
1/2 cup OJ
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup cold water
1.5-2 tablespoons cornstarch
1/2 teaspoon almond extract (optional)
1/8 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)

Directions:
1. In a saucepan over medium heat combine blueberries, water, OJ, sugar stir gently and bring to a boil.
2. Using a potato masher crush some of the blueberries
3.  Add cinnamon and almond extract if desired
4. In a small prep bowl mix cornstarch and water till smooth and add to blueberry mixture.
5. Stir gently and simmer till thickened.  Remember that sauce will thicken as it cools

Good sauce for shortcake or ice-cream or pancakes
