Title:
Autumn Pear Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984947.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium firm pear
1/4 cup whipped topping
3 tablespoons raisins
2 tablespoons toasted, chopped pecans

Directions:
Peel the top third of the pear; core from the bottom, leaving stem intact.  Place on a microwave-safe plate; cover and cook on high for 2-3 minutes or until tender.  Immediately transfer to a serving plate.  Serve warm with whipped topping, raisins and pecans.

Submitter: 
Patricia  Olson
Description: 
After a filling meal, this elegant little dessert hits the spot...and it's a snap to fix! Patricia Olson from Germantown, Maryland serves a warm pear with a few simple ingredients. 'For a lighter dessert, substitute vanilla yogurt for the whipped topping,' she suggests.
