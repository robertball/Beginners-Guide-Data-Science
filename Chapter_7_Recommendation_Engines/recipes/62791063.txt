Title:
Almond Cupcake with a Salted Caramel Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/651858.jpg
Ingredients:
Cupcake:
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup margarine
2 eggs
1 tsp Vanilla
1 tsp Almond Extract
1 1/2 cup all purpose flour
1 3/4 baking powder
3/4 cup homo milk
--------
Caramel:
1/2 cup Brown sugar
1/2 cup margarine
2 tbsp Light corn syrup
1 tbsp Vanilla
1/2 cup cream
1 pinch salt
--------
Buttercream:
3/4 cup salted butter
2 cups icing sugar, sifted

Directions:
Cupcake:
Preheat oven to 350.
-Cream margarine and sugar together really well. Beat in eggs 1 at a time, making sure to beat real well after each egg. Stir in Vanilla and Almond extracts.
-Combine Flour and Baking Powder in a seperate bowl and then add it to the creamed mixture. mix well. Stir in milk until smooth.
Bake 20-25 min.
Caramel:
Bring sugar, margarine, corn syrup and vanilla to a boil over medium heat and let simmer for 3-4 minutes until thickened. Remove from heat. When cooled to a warm temperature add the cream a little at a time until it has become the consistency of honey give or take when at room temperature (you may need more or less cream). Then add the pinch of salt. Do not add to the buttercream until it is room temperature or cooler.
Buttercream:
Cream/beat together until fluffy, slowly add cold caramel and continue to beat.
You can also save some of the caramel for a drizzle over top of the cupcake.
