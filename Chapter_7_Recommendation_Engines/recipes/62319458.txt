Title:
Dark Chocolate Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/377435.jpg
Ingredients:
3 TBSP Dark Chocolate Cocoa (Dutch processed)
2 TBSP Butter, melted
1 C Confectioners Sugar
2-4 TBSP of HOT Water

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, adding the Hot water one TBSP at a time, until desired consistency is reached.
Stir until smooth.
Glaze your favorite dessert!

Submitter: 
Lisa D
Description: 
A simple, versatile, decadent dark chocolate glaze.  Great for topping bunt cakes, cookies or even your favorite doughnut!
