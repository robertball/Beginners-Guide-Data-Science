Title:
Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked rice (leftover/cold 2-3 days)
3 tbs sesame oil
1 cup frozen peas/carrots (thawed)
1 small onion, chopped
2 tsp minced garlic
2 eggs, slightly beaten
1/4 c dark soy sauce (or low sodium)

Directions:
Heat oil in wok on medium high heat.  Add peas/carrots mixture, onion and garlic.  Stir fry until tender.  Lower heat to medium low.  Push veggies off to one side of the wok and pour eggs on the other side.  Scramble the eggs.  Add rice and soy sauce and blend together until heated through.  Garnish with green onion and serve.
