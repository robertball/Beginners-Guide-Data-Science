Title:
Hot Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1443431.jpg
Ingredients:
5 C. cooked chicken, cubed
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 1/2 C Hellman's mayonnaise
1 tsp. salt
8 oz. water chestnuts, sliced
1/2 C chopped onions
2 C chopped celery
4 oz. pimento
8 oz. sliced mushrooms, drained
5 tsp. lemon juice
2 C cooked rice
Cracker crumbs
3 Tbsp. butter melted

Directions:
Mix all except cracker crumbs and butter and put in 13 x 9 inch pan.  Mix melted butter and cracker crumbs and sprinkle over top.  Refrigerate over night.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 min., until brown and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Kathy
Description: 
All-in-one casserole that has been a family favorite for years.  Either make a day ahead as recipe suggests or if short on time make and bake right away.  It also freezes well.
