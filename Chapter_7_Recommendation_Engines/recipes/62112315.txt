Title:
Pineapple Cheese Mold Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C sugar
1/2 C pinappple syrup
1 tbsp. Knox gelatin
1/4 C. cold water
1 C. crushed pineapple, drained and reserved syrup
1 C. American cheese, shredded or grated
1 C. whipping cream, whipped. (1/2 pt.)or

Directions:
Dissolve the sugar in pineapple syrup over low heat.
Soften gelatin in cold water and combine with syrup while still warm.
Chill until partially set.
Add pineapple and cheese and fld in whipped cream.
Chill in mold
Note: 1 small Cool Whip can be substituted for real whipped cream.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
We serve this for holiday celebrations and buffet parties.It's always well received and be prepared to share the recipe.
Louise Hasbrock gave me this delicious recipe and she is remembered whenever I made this gelatin salad.
