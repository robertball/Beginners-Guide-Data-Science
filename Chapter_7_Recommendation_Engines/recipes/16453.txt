1 (16 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 (6 ounce) package strawberry flavored Jell-OÂ®
3 (15.25 ounce) cans fruit cocktail, drained

2 cups grapes
2 cups miniature marshmallows

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the thawed whipped topping, gelatin, fruit cocktail, oranges, grapes and marshmallows. Mix together well and refrigerate until chilled. Stir again before serving.
