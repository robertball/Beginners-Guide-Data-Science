Title:
Hot Reuben Delights Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One loaf Italian bread, split horizontally
2 Tbsp Russian dressing
8 oz thinly sliced corned beef
4 thick slices Swiss cheese
2 1/2 cups deli coleslaw
1/2 cup thinly sliced red onion
1/2 cup russian dressing

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees
In a small bowl, marinate onions in 1/2 cup russian dressing for about 20 minutes.
Place loaf halves on baking sheet. Spread top and bottom with 1 tablespoon dressing each. Top one half with the beef and cheese.
Place baking sheet in oven. Bake until the cheese is melted, about 4 minutes.
Top cheese with the coleslaw and marinated onions. Top covered half of loaf with other half. Cut into fourths and serve.

Submitter: 
Hugatunity
Description: 
These sandwhiches make an easy and cheap dinner. Serve with chips and a salad for a weeknight meal with a Russian flare.
