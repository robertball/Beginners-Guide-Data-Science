Title:
No Powdered Sugar Peanut Butter  frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2091372.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cream cheese
1 cup peanut butter
1/4 cup butter
4 TBSP honey

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together until smooth.

Submitter: 
Bobbie Susan
Description: 
This was inspired by my son whose favorite foods are peanut butter and honey.  One day he wanted banana cupcakes so I decided to surprise him with this frosting on top.  He now asks for this frosting on every cake I make.
