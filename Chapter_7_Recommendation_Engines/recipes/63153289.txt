Title:
Chicken (White) Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cubed
1 T canola oil
2 medium yellow onions, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
28 oz chicken stock
4 15-oz cans cannellini beans, drained
4 4-oz cans Ortega fire-roasted mild green chilies
2 tsp salt
2 tsp crushed cumin seeds
2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp cayenne
1/2 c half and half
8 oz lowfat sour cream

Directions:
Sear the chicken in the oil.
Add onions and saute until translucent.
Add garlic and saute briefly.
Deglaze with stock.
Add beans, spices, and chilies. Simmer for at least 30 min.
Remove from heat. Stir in sour cream and half-and-half. Serve with grated cheddar and chips.
