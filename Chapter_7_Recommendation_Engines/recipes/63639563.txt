Title:
Mock Marscapone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese, room temp
2 T vanilla flavored Greek yogurt
1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
In a small bowl, cream together the cream cheese, yogurt and vanilla extract with a mixer until well blended.
Refrigerate if a firmer texture is desired. Use in recipes as an inexpensive alternative to mascarpone
