Title:
** Overnight Gooey Pecan Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
SMALL BATCH:  6x10 pan  (6 servings)
12 frozen dinner roll dough balls
1/2 small pkg. regular butterscotch or vanilla pudding mix (not instant)
1/2 c. brown sugar
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 c. butter
1/2 c. chopped pecans
LARGE BATCH:   11x13 pan  (12 servings)
24 frozen dinner roll dough balls
1 small pkg. regular butterscotch or vanilla pudding mix (not instant)
1 c. brown sugar
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 c. butter
1 c. chopped pecans

Directions:
Grease pan & sprinkle pecans over bottom. Arrange frozen dinner roll dough in pan.
Mix together pudding mix, brown sugar, cinnamon, & butter & heat in microwave until butter melts & bubbles.
Pour mixture over frozen rolls. Cover pan & let set 8 hr. or overnight.
To bake: preheat oven 350 degrees; bake uncovered 20-30 min. Turn out onto platter to serve.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
Made with frozen dinner roll dough (like Rhodes) & butterscotch pudding mix.  Easy breakfast for a crowd.  When they were little, the kids loved them for dinner with chili (still do).
