Title:
Vinegar and Oil Wonder Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/862738.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 head green cabbage
1/4 cup canola oil
1/4 cup white vinegar
salt and pepper, or Tony Chachere's* to taste

Directions:
Cut the cabbage into very thin strips 1/8 - 1/4"
Leave it in long lengths
Mix in a bowl with the rest of ingredients
Let set for 5-10min before eating
Eat within 1-3 days or it will begin to pickle

Submitter: 
Wonder Becky
Description: 
This is the coleslaw we ate with Cajun food when I was a kid.  It is VERY simple and easy to make
