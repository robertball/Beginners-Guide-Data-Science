Title:
Black Bean & Roasted Red Pepper Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982900.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 ounce) package Lawry's® Taco Spices & Seasonings
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (7 ounce) jar roasted red peppers, drained
12 ounces cream cheese, softened
1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
1 1/2 teaspoons lime juice

Directions:
In food processor, process all ingredients until smooth. Garnish, if desired, with chopped tomato, chopped bell pepper and chopped fresh cilantro and serve with tortilla chips or assorted fresh vegetables.

Submitter: 
LAWRY'S®
Description: 
A little effort yields big flavor in this quick and easy bean dip.
