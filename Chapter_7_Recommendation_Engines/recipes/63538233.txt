Title:
QUICK AND EASY LEMON LOAF Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4544102.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1cup granulated white sugar
2eggs
1 and 1/2cups flour
1tsp.baking powder
1/2tsp.salt
1/2cup milk.(At this point you could add more lemon juice to the batter if you wish a stronger  lemon flavor.)
Topping
1/4cup granulated white sugar
juice of one lemon.

Directions:
Put the flour in a bowl,cut the shortening in.Add the rest except the Topping and mix together.  .Pour into a greased or sprayed loaf pan lined with parchment paper and bake at 325 for 45 minutes.
Mix the lemon juice and sugar for the topping together and pour over the loaf while still warm. At this point you can poke holes in the loaf so the juice can seep in.Leave in pan 15-20min,then put on wire rack to finish cooling.
