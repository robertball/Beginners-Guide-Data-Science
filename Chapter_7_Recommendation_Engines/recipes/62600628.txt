Title:
Honey chile chicken wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds chicken wings
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
salt and freshly ground pepper
1/4 cup unseasoned rice vinegar
1 tsp crushed red pepper
1/2 cup honey
2 tbsps soy sauce
2 scallions, thinly sliced

Directions:
Preheat broiler and set a rack in the center of the oven.In a large bowl, toss the chicken wings with the olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Arrange the wings on a wire rack set over a large, sturdy baking sheet. Broil 45-50 minutes, turning once or twice, until the wings are cooked and crisp.
Meanwhile, in a sauce pan, combine the vinegar and crushed red pepper and simmer for 1 minute.Let cool, then whisk in the honey and soy sauce.
In a large bowl, carefully toss the chicken wigs with the honey-soy mixture.Transer the wings to a platter, sprinkle with the scallions and serve
