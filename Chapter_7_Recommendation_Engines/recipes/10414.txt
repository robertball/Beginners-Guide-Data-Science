Title:
Pink Valentine Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1131168.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup sifted confectioners' sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 tablespoon water
2 drops red food coloring

Directions:
Blend sugar, salt and flavoring.  Add just enough water to make it easy to spread.  Add 2 drops red food coloring and mix well.
