Title:
Smoked Salmon Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ounces smoked salmon
3 T. light cream cheese
1 tsp. lemon juice
2 tsp. fresh chopped dill or 1/2 tsp. dried dill
2 tsp. minced red onion
4 slices pumpernickel bread
Sliced tomatoes and cucumbers

Directions:
Combine first 3 ingredients till smooth.  Stir in dill and onion.  Spread on 2 bread slices and top with tomatoes and cukes.
