Title:
Jalapeño Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can garbanzo beans drained reserve juice
3T Tahini
4 cloves garlic chopped
4T fresh lemon juice
3 Slices jarred or canned jalapeño
Splash juice from jalapeños
1t ground cumin
1t curry powder

Directions:
In blender add all ingredients. Blend till smooth. If it’s too thick add some of the reserved bean juice.

Submitter: 
ChrisB
Description: 
Spicy hummus. Can add more or less of the jalapeños to your taste. As written it’s slightly spicy.
