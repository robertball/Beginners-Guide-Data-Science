Title:
Maple Butter Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/1873134.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups confectioners' sugar
¼ cup butter, softened
3 tablespoons cream
½ teaspoon maple flavored extract

Directions:
Beat the butter or margarine, confectioner's sugar, milk, and maple flavoring in a bowl until smooth. Add more milk or confectioner's sugar as need.  Makes enough to frost one 8 or 9 inch round cake or 1 8x8 inch square cake.

Submitter: 
Carol
Description: 
Maple flavored frosting that tastes great on the Maple Nut Cake or try it on a plain yellow or chocolate cake, yummy!  Increase the amount of maple extract (gradually!) to suit your taste.
