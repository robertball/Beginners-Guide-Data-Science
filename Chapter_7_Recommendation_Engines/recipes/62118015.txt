Title:
Easy Cheesy Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups redskin potatoes (boiled until soft, cut into cubes)
10 3/4 ounce can cream of chicken soup
1 cup sour cream
1 cup shredded cheddar
1/3 cup butter
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
Combine all ingreadients in crockpot.
Cook on low for 3-4 hours.

Submitter: 
Crystal Miller
Description: 
So delicious and so easy. Used to make this for every holiday dinner, but since everyone in the family has gotten the recipe off of me they make it themselves!
