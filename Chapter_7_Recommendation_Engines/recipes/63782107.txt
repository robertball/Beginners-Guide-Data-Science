Title:
Texas Cavair Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans blackeyed peas
1 garlic clove minced
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 red and 1 green pepper, diced
1 green onions minced
1 tomato, chopped
1 can shoepeg corn
1/4 cup of chopped cilantro
1 package of good season garlic and herb dressing or zesty italian dressing. Make dressing as recipe directs.

Directions:
Rinse and drain peas and corn. Combine remaining ingredients with salad dressing and refrigerate 24 hours. Toss before serving. Serve with Fritos or Tostitos.
