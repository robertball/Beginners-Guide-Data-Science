Title:
Authentic Chai with Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of Water
2 tablespoons of Yellow Label Lipton tea (or any red tea)
3-4 Whole cloves
1-2 cinnamon stick
4 Cardamom pods (cracked)
1/2 can Evaporated milk

Directions:
Bring water to a boil and add the next 4 ingredients. Allow to steep for 5 minutes, then add the Evaporated Milk to desired lightness.

Submitter: 
Marie Hernandez
Description: 
Perfect after a meal, to serve to guests or as a warming treat. Powdered versions of the spices may work as well as regular milk if Evaporated is not available.
