Title:
Treebeard’s Red Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1lb. dried light red kidney beans
-2 qts. water
-2 medium onions, chopped
-2 stalks of celery with leaves, chopped
-1/2 large bell pepper, chopped
-2-1/2t salt
-1-1/2t ground thyme
-1-1/2t dried oregano
-1-1/4t garlic powder
-3/4t cayenne pepper
-2 bay leaves
-1lb. smoked sausage, cut into 1/4” slices
-white rice

Directions:
-Wash and drain beans and then place in 4 qt. saucepan and add 2qts. water.  Let soak overnight. Don’t drain!!!!!!!
-(If in a rush you can also place beans in 4 qt. saucepan and add 2 qts. water.  Bring to a boil and then cover. Reduce heat and simmer 2 minutes.  Remove from heat and let stand for one hour.  Don’t drain!!!!!!!)
-Add onion, celery, and bell pepper and bring to a boil.
-Stir in seasonings, cover, reduce heat, and simmer 1-1/2 hours or until beans are almost tender.
-Add sausage and simmer another 30 minutes or until beans are tender and begin to thicken.
-If you like beans thickened, remove cover during the last 30 minutes of cooking. Or, after beans have cooked, remove 1/2 cup of the beans, mash, return to pot, and stir in.
-Serve with white rice
