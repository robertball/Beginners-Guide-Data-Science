Title:
Savoy cabbage  vegetable salad with lemon vinagarette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBLSP of oliveor canola oil
1/4 cup of shredded carrrots
3 cloves of garlic sliced thin
1 zuchinni chopped into 1/4 in cubes
1 small sweet onion or 1/2 large onion chopped
1 small head of savoy cabbage finely shredded
salt and pepper to taste
1 tsp of apple cider vinegar
1 juice of one lemon
2 TBSP of sesame seeds
1/2 tsp of oregano
1/4 tsp of thyme

Directions:
1. Heat oil in a medium size skillet on low to medium heat.
2. Add carrots, zuchinni, onion and garlic to pan. Saute until onions are clear and vetables begin to get tender. This should take about a 2 minutes.
3. Add cabbage and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Cook cabbage for three minutes and then toss with lemon juice and apple cider vinegar.
4. When cabbage is cooked  for about 3-4 minutesbut still has a slight crunch remove mixture from heat and toss with oregano, thyme and sesame seeds.
5. Toss and  Serve.

Submitter: 
cookin' social worker
Description: 
This is a nice alternative to regular tossed salad. Lemon juices gives brightness and sesame seeds provide a nice crunch.
