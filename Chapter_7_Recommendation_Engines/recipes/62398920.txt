Title:
Chas Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head Bib lettuce
1 avacado, sliced
1 lb bay shrimp
1 can hearts of palm, sliced
croutons
Dressing:
3/4 c olive oil
1/4 c wine vinegar
2 T dijon mustard
salt
pepper
1 clove garlic
grated parm cheese

Directions:
Mix all ingredients for dressing in jar. Place salad ingredients on plate and top with dressing.
