Title:
No Bake- Chocolate Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/500083.jpg
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
2 cups white sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 cup peanut butter
3 cups rolled oats

Directions:
Melt butter and stir in sugar, milk, cocoa, vanilla and salt and bring to a boil in a saucepan over medium heat, stirring constantly. When mixture comes to a boil, let it boil for 2 minutes.(any longer your cookies will be crumbly, any shorter, your cookies will be gooey)
Remove from burner and mix in peanut butter and rolled oats.
Dip spoonfuls of the mixture onto parchment paper, waxed paper,or aluminum foil and let cool on counter top.

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
An easy to make, easy to disappear, chocolate oatmeal cookie.
