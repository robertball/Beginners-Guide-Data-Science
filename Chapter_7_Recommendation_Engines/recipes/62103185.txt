Title:
Southwestern Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons. oil
1 lb. chicken breast, cut into bite-size pieces
1 large chipotle pepper in adobo sauce, minced
salt and pepper to taste
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 large onion, I prefer red but can use any, chopped
1 bag super sweet frozen corn
1 tablespoon ground cumin
2 teaspoons ground coriander
1 tablespoon flour
1 large can of black beans, drained and rinsed
6 cups water
4 cubes Knorr Low Sodium Caldo de Pollo
1 handful, chopped fresh cilantro
Juice of 1 lime
unsalted corn tortilla chips
shredded cheddar or mexican blend cheese

Directions:
Heat oil in pan and add chicken and chipotle peppers.  Brown the chicken lightly.
Add the onions, garlic and bell pepper, cumin, coriander, salt and pepper.  When juices are released add the corn and heat through.  Then add the black beans and heat through.
Sprinkle with flour and cook for 1 minute.
Add water and bring to a boil, then add the Knorr cubes.  Simmer about 15 minutes.
Turn off heat and add the lime juice and the chopped cilantro right before serving.  Garnish with shredded cheese, and crunched tortilla chips.
