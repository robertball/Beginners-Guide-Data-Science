Title:
Carrot Cake Remake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1006148.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups white whole wheat flour
1 cup Whey Low granular sugar substitute
1/2 cup Whey Low gold brown sugar substitute
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
2 tsp cinnamon
2 eggs
2 tbsp (no sugar added) applesauce
2 tsp pure vanilla
2 cups shredded carrots
1/4 cup flaked organic reduced fat coconut
20 oz. can crushed pineapple in juice, drained
1/4 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
For the Cream Cheese Frosting:
2 tablespoons butter
8 oz 1/3 less fat Cream Cheese
2 cups Whey Low powdered sugar substitute
1 tsp pure vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
In a large bowl mix together the white whole wheat flour, white and brown sugar, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.
In a medium bowl mix together eggs, applesauce, and vanilla.  Fold in the carrots, coconut and pineapple.  Mix well.
Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until well combined.   Stir in chopped walnuts, if desired.
Pour the batter into a prepared a deep 8"x 3" cake pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
Meanwhile make the cream cheese frosting by  mixing together the butter, cream cheese, and vanilla.  Gradually add in the Whey Low Powdered Sugar substitute.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
This is a deliciously moist carrot cake that is lower in fat and calories that your typical carrot cake.  It uses white whole wheat flour and Whey Low sugar substitute which has about 75 - 80% fewer calories than sugar but still tastes like sugar.  It is a perfect dessert for family get togethers and no one would suspect it was a healthier version of carrot cake.  Indulge in dessert without all the guilt.
