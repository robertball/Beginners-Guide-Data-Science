Title:
Scotch-A-Roo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 cup corn Syrup
1 cup peanut butter
6 cups rice krispies
1 cup chocolate chips
1 cup butterscotch chips

Directions:
bring sugar and corn syrup to a boil, remove from heat and then add the peanut butter.  Pour this over 6 cups of rice krispies (or you can do 3 cups rice krispies and 3 cups corn flakes) in a buttered 9X13 pan.  For the icing nuke 1 cup chocolate chips and 1 cup butterscotch chips.  Pour over everything and let set.
