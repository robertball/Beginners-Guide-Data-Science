Title:
Mushroom Barley Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. fresh mushrooms or 2 cans (6 to 8 ounces each)
1 cup pearl barley (check family cookbook)
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1 cup onion, coarsely chopped
1 4 oz. can pimiento, coarsely chopped, or 1/2 red pepper chopped
2 cups chicken broth
1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Heat over to 350 degrees.
Rinse, pat dry and slice fresh mushrooms (makes about 5 cups) or drain canned mushrooms.
Heat butter in a large saucepan.  Add mushrooms and onions; saute 4 to 5 minutes.
Add barley to mushroom misture and cook until barley is golden brown.
Stir in remaining ingredients.  Transfer to large casserole.
Cover and bake for 50 to 60 minutes, or until barley is tender and liquid is absorbed.  Add more chicken stock or water if barley seems dry during baking period.

Submitter: 
daf
Description: 
Source: Oregonian, 10/29/75. This is also in the Eldert family cookbook. Great side dish with roasted chicken or pork tenderloin.  As a vegetarian dish, stir in some grilled tofu cutlets in the last 10 minutes of cooking.  Can substitute a little wine or beer for the stock.
