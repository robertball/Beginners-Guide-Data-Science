Title:
beef stroganoff and pilau rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1447092.jpg
Ingredients:
(rice)
400g rice
80 cl water
1 medium onion
1 clove garlic
pepper salt and seasoning to liking
(beef)
500 gr. beef
200 gr. mushrooms
10 small (pink size) pickled gherkins
1 clove garlic
10 cl. vodka
40 gr. tomato puree
25 cl low fat cooking cream (or sour cream)
20 gr. butter
pepper salt and seasoning to liking

Directions:
peel and chop onion
peel and squash garlic
cut mushrooms in quarters
cube beef no larger than cubic inch
melt butter in frying pan and fry-crust and season beef
set beef aside to rest
reuse pan with new butter and beef juices to cook mushrooms. season
add chopped onion and garlic
stir in tomato puree and season with paprika powder
lower heat and add beef again
add chopped gherkin and vodka
light flame to burn away the alcohol
lower heat and add cream
stir slowly to get smooth sauce
taste and season as needed
bon apetit !
