Title:
Slow Cooker Turkey Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 pound) bone-in turkey breast
3 celery stalks
1/2 onion,halved again
salt and pepper
1 tsp dried sage
1 tsp poultry seasoning
1/4 cp softened butter
1 chicken boullion cube,dissolved in 1/2 cp water

Directions:
Rinse the turkey breast and pat dry. Cut off any excess skin, but leave the skin covering the breast.Mix all the seasoning except butter and boullion mix. Rub seasoning mixture all over outside of the turkey and under the skin.Place half of butter UNDER skin and remainder of butter rubbed onto outside of breast. Place in a slow cooker.Pour boullion mixture around breast. Cover, and cook on High for 1 hour, then set to Low, and cook for 7 hours.(Optional)Gravy; Pour drippings into skillet and simmer.Make a slurrey of equal parts cornstarch & water and add to skillet. Simmer 5-10 mins to thicken,whisking often.
