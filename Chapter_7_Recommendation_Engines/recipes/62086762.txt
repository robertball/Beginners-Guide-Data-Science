Title:
Buttermilk Chocolate Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter{no substitutions}
4-6 cups powdered sugar
2 ts almond extract
1/2 ts vanilla
1/8 ts salt
2/3 cup cocoa
1/3-1/2 cup buttermilk

Directions:
Cream butter, add flavorings, 2 cups pwd. sugar and cocoa.
Add buttermilk in small amounts until well combined, add more sugar and buttermilk to reach the desired consistency and quantity.
For use with the 3-layer Devil's Food Cake I submitted you will probably need all 6 cups of sugar and 1/2 cup of buttermilk give or take a Tablespoon or two

Submitter: 
melis
Description: 
Perfect Buttercream to frost Devil's Food cake, rich buttery and chocolate with just a hint of almond, YUMMY. It took me awhile to get this frosting right. Keep in mind that amounts of milk and confectioners sugar should be adjusted according to the desired consistency
