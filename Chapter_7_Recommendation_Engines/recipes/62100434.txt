Title:
Angel Food Cream Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 round angle food cake
2 - 8oz pks of cream cheese (softened)
1 large or 2 small containers of cool whip
1 c. powdered sugar
2 cans of pie filling (I use cherry)

Directions:
Cream powdered sugar, cool whip, and cream cheese together.
Add angel food cake broken into small pcs. Mix together well.
Put in a 9 x 13 pan. Spread pie filling on top.
Cover and chill for at least 1 hour. Serve. Store in Refrigerator.

Submitter: 
3D's Mom
Description: 
This is a very easy, no cook recipe. It has been a crowd pleaser each time I have made it.
