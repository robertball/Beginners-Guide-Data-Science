Title:
Crock Pot Salsa Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 lbs sirloin steaks
1 1/2 pints or 24 oz of salsa
1 large onion chopped
1 green pepper chopped
8 oz sliced mushrooms
2 Tbs flour
2 Tbs olive oil
salt & pepper
1/2 cup beef broth

Directions:
Cut the meat into 1" pieces  and cover with seasoned flour. Brown meat until all sides are browned. Pour into crock pot. In same pan as meat, saute chopped onions, green pepper  and mushrooms until onions are pearly. Add to crockpot.
Add 24 oz or three half pints of  salsa to crock pot
Add beef broth and stir
Cook 4-5 hours
Serve over  Jasmine rice.

Submitter: 
Aven
Description: 
My Salsa Steak recipe evolved from having too much left over salsa at the end of the season. I first brown the steak peices and onions, mushroom and green pepper. Then I add the salsa and other things to the crockpot.
