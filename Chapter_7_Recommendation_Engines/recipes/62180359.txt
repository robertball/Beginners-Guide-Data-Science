Title:
Festive Chocolate Dipped Prezel Rods Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pckg backers sweet chocolate, semi-sweet chocolate, premium white chocolate, or bittersweet chocolate.
24 pretzel rods

Directions:
Microwave chocalate in a mcorowavalbe bowl on high for 2 mins or until chocolate is almost melted. Stir until chocolate is completly melted.
Dip rods in chocolate; let excess drip off.
Let stand at room tempor refridgerate on wax paper-lined cookie sheet 30 mins or until chocolate is firm.
Drizzle with addtional melted chocolate or colored sprinkles.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
Pretzel rod dipped in chocolate and drizzeld with opposite chocolate and sprinkled with colored sugar crystals, or not. Very easy delicious, sweet and salty treat.
