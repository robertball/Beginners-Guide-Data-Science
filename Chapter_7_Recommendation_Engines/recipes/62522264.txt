Title:
Marinated Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
DRESSING:
1/3 c. herbed or cider vinegar
2 T. sugar
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 T. chopped fresh parsley
2 T. olive oil
SALAD:
1 can each of garbanzo, kidney, and pinto beans , drained and rinsed (or your choice of beans)
1 (10 oz) jar pepperoncini peppers, drained, seeded, sliced
1 c. red onion strips (2 x 1/8 inch)
1 c. red pepper strips (1 x 1/4-inch)

Directions:
In small bowl stir together all dressing ingredients.
In large bowl carefully stir together all salad ingredients.  Pour dressing over bean mixture; toss to coat.
Cover and refrigerate, stirring every hour for at least 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Susie
Description: 
Cold bean salad for a hot summer day.  Serve with crusty bread.
