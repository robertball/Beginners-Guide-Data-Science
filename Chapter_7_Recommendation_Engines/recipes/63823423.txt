Title:
Queso Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Velveeta
1 can (10 oz) Rotel diced tomatoes and chilies, undrained

Directions:
Mix in a 2 quart micro-safe bowl.
Microwave on high 5 min or until Velveeta is completely melted, stirring after 2-3 min.

Submitter: 
Karen Zastrow
Description: 
This is the famous dip that is on the Velveeta box.
