Title:
Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4502427.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8 oz container of Salted Cashews
2 bags Frozen peas
1 pkg traditional bacon
1 1/2 cups of raisins
1 small red onion
1 to 1 1/2 cups of mayonnaise
Red wine vinegar
Shredded Chedder Cheese

Directions:
Cook bacon thoroughly; until crispy. Boil or steam peas and then drain. Dice red onion into finely chopped peaces. Combine mayonnaise and red wine vinegar to taste. In large bowl combine peas, cashews, raisins, red onion, bacon and mix in the sauce. Top with shredded chedder cheese. Cover and shake to mix. Refridgerate for at least 1 hour for tasty, crunchy goodness :)
