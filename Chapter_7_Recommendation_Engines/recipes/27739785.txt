Title:
Two Great Tastes Pudding Parfaits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pack (4-3/4oz) vanilla pudding
3-1/2 cups milk
1 cup Reese's Peanut butter chips
1 cup Hershey's Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
Whipped topping
*Do not use instant pudding mix*

Directions:
In a large heavy saucepan, combine pudding mix and milk.  Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture comes full boil.
Remove from heat; divide hot mixture between 2 heatproof  medium bowls.  Immediately stir peanut butter chips into mixture in one bowl and chocolate chips in other bowl.  Stir both mixtures until chips are melted and mixture is smooth.  Cool slighty, stirring ocasionally.
In parfait glasses, alternately layer peanut butter and chocolate mixtures.  Place plastic wrap directly onto surface of each dessert; refrigerate several hours.  Garnish with whipped topping if desired.
