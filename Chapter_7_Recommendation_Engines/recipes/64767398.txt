Title:
Michael's Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7347817.jpg
Ingredients:
10 c      Quick Oats
1 1/2 c Brown Sugar
2 t         Cinnamon
1/2 t     Nutmeg
1 1/2 c Raisins
6 oz      Dried Dates
5 oz      Dried Cherries
1 c         Chopped
Pecans or Walnuts

Directions:
We put our mix in a
12 c container from Dollar Tree.
Put 9 c of oatmeal in the container, add the brown sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg.
Shake vigorously to mix then add fruit and shake again. Add another ounce or two of oatmeal if there is room and shake once more.
To make a bowl of oatmeal:
Put 1 1/2 c of mix in the bowl and add 1 c of milk and stir well. If needed, add more milk to make the top of the bowl have several liquid spots. Cook in microwave for 2 minutes. Take out and stir well and enjoy.
You may substitute any amounts to suit your taste.  You may also add apricots, cranberries, blueberries, almonds, etc.
