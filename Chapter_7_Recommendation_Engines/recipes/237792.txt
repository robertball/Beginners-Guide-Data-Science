Title:
Frozen Raspberry Margaritas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2263551.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen unsweetened raspberries
3/4 cup gold tequila
3/4 cup fresh orange juice
1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
1/4 cup brandy-based orange liqueur (such as Grand MarnierÂ®)
2 tablespoons superfine sugar
4 mint leaves
4 ice cubes

Directions:
Blend raspberries, tequila, orange juice, lemon juice, orange liqueur, superfine sugar, and mint leaves in a blender until smooth. Add ice cubes individually, crushing each one before adding the next. Pour into chilled cocktail glasses to serve.

Refreshing and delicious combination of frozen raspberries, orange juice, and mint, with a kick.
