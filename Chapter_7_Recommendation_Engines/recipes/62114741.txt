Title:
Cowgirl Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 ½ c. margarine
¾ c. sugar
¾ t. salt
2  ¼ c. brown sugar
1 t. vanilla
2 t. almond flavor
3 eggs
1 ½ t. baking soda
3 c. oatmeal
3 c. flour
1 ½ c. coconut
4 squares white chocolate (cut up almond bark)
4 oz. jar broken macadamia nuts or 1 ½ cups slivered almonds
Cream margarine and sugars.  Add eggs, beat well.
Mix in remaining ingredients- chill.
Bake 350º for 15 minutes or until center is set.
Let cool a few minutes on pans.
Note- don’t bake well on air bake pans

Directions:

I have the best luck with pre-heated pans and using pampered chef stones.

Submitter: 
Betty
Description: 
Reminiscent of a white chunk macadamia cookie but better.  Using the almonds is a more economical alternative.  Follow directions for baking.  These can be a little tricky to get right on the first pan, unless preheated might not turn out.
