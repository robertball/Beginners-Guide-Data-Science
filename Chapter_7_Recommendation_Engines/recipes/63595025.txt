Title:
Chicken Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken:
1 whole cooked fryer chicken, cooled and cut up
1 quart broth
4 Tbls flour
4 Tbls fat
Dressing:
1 1/2 quart cubed bread
4 Tbls chopped onion
3/4 cup butter
1 stalk celery, chopped
1/4 tsp sage
1/2 cup broth
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Put cut up chicken in baking dish.
Put dressing on top.
Pour Gravy on top.
Bake for 35 minutes.
