Title:
Broccoli Cauliflower Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz broccoli chopped (1 head is 10oz?)
10 oz cauliflower chopped (1 head is 10oz?)
1 can condensed cream of chicken soup
6 oz shredded mild cheddar cheese
2 tbsp milk
1/4 cup crushed ritz crackers (1/2 sleeve)

Directions:
Steam or microwave veggies (in a glass bowl with 1/4 cup of water) until crisp tender (15 minutes).
Drain veggies & combine with soup, cheese and milk.
Spread into greased or oil sprayed 9 x 13" pan.
Cover with crushed ritz.
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
