Title:
Chipotle Pork Pozole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T. olive oil
1 pork tenderloin about 1 lb halved crosswise
salt and pepper
1 medium chopped onion
2 minced garlic cloves
1 T. minced chipotle chiles in adobo
2 14.5oz. cans chicken broth
2 15.5 oz cans hominey, rinse and drained
1 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes in juice
1/2 cup chopped cilantro

Directions:
Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Season pork with salt and pepper. Cook until browned on all sides, about 7 minutes. Move pork to a plate, reserving pan. Place onion and garlic in pan and season with salt and pepper. Cook over medium heat about 4-5 minutes until onion is softened.
Add chiles and cook about 1 minute.  Add broth, hominy, tomatoes, poek and 2 cups water.  Bring to a boil then reduce to a simmer.  cook until an instant read themometer inserted in the center of the pork registers 145 degrees.
Transfer pork to a work surface and shred with 2 forks.  Return to pan, top with cilantro.

Joanie
Description: 
This is a quick version of a traditional Mexican soup made with canned hominy.  It is so delicious!
