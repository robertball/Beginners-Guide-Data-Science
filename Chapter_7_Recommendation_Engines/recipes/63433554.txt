Title:
Creamy Jello Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3 oz pkg jello (orange or raspberry)
1 3 oz pkg regular vanilla pudding
1 3 oz pkg tapioca
3 c water
1 large can mandarin oranges OR fresh raspberries

Directions:
Stir all ingredients but fruit together.
Bring to boil, then chill.
When chilled, add in corresponding fruit.
Serve with whipped topping.
