Title:
Sex in The Pan - My way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups crushed graham crackers
1/2 cup sugar
2/3 cup butter, melted
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup confectioners' sugar
1 cup whipping cream (or 1 medium pack of Cool Whip)
1 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix
1 (3.9 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
4 cups milk divided
coco powder (optional)

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the crumbs and sugar; add butter and blend well. Press onto the bottom of a 9x13 pan.  Refrigerate for 30 minutes before filling, or bake at 375 degrees F for 5 - 8 minutes. Cool on a wire rack before filling.
Beat together cream cheese and confectioners’ sugar until smooth. Spread over cooled crust.
In mixing bowl mix instant vanilla pudding with 2 cups of milk.   Also to sit for a couple of minutes.  Spread on top of cream cheese.
Whip whipping cream to stiff peaks.  Spread ½ on top of the pudding.   (if using Cool Whip use ½ container)
In mixing bowl mix instant chocolate pudding and 2 cups of milk.  Allow to sit for a couple of minutes.  Spread on top of the whipped cream.
Top with remaining whipped topping.  (if using cool whip add remaining)
If you wish sprinkle a little coco powder on top

Nadine
Description: 
This desert has been a hit and often requested.  Not like the normal sex in pan with the flour crust.
