Title:
Tofu Mayonnaise - low fat, low sodium Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 package firm tofu well drained
1 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tsp chives minced
1/2 tablespoon minced garlic
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon sugar
1/4 teaspoon vinegar

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in a food processor or blender until smooth and creamy. Chill before serving. Use promptly.

Submitter: 
kcryss
Description: 
A delicious low fat, low sodium creamy mayonnaise substitute comparable to a salad dressing like Miracle Whip.
