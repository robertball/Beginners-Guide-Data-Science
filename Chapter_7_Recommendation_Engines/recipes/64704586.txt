Title:
Easy Bruschetta Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5447798.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breast
3 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil, divided
1 tbsp. dried oregano
1 tsp. garlic powder
kosher salt and fresh ground black pepper
2 cups chopped tomatoes ( I prefer Roma)
1/2 small to medium shallot, minced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup fresh chopped basil
1/2 small sweet onion, chopped
shaved Parmesan, for garnish
Balsamic glaze, for drizzling/garnish
1 small loaf Italian bread cut into 1 inch slices on the diagonal

Directions:
On a plate, season chicken breast with oregano, garlic powder, salt and pepper each side of chicken breast.
In large skillet on medium heat, heat Tbsp olive oil.
Add chicken to skillet and cook until golden and no longer pink. (approximately 7-8 minutes per side) remove from pan and set to side and tent with foil.
In same pan make Bruschetta topping; mix chopped tomatoes, sweet onion, remaining olive oil, shallots, garlic and chopped basil. Season generously with salt and pepper to taste and cook through approximately 5-7 minutes.
Top chicken on lightly broiled/toasted Italian bread with Bruschetta topping and shaved Parmesan. Drizzle with some balsamic glaze.
For Italian bread:
slice small Italian loaf of bread on diagonal approximately 1 inch thick.
brush each slice with fresh chopped garlic and olive oil (both sides)
place bread slices on bake sheet and broil till lightly toasted each side. (2-3 mins per side, depends on broiler)
