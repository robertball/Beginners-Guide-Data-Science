Title:
BBQ Chicken Tortilla Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/536256.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Whole Wheat, low carb tortilla
1-2 Tbsp Honey BBQ Sauce
1/4 c diced red onion
1 Tbsp chopped cilantro
4 oz cooked, shredded chicken
1/4 c or 1/2 oz  finely shredded low-fat mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Spread 1 Tbsp BBQ sauce on th tortilla.
Toss remaining bbq sauce with chicken.
Place chicken, onion, 1/2 the cilantro and 1/2 the tomatoes on the tortilla.  Top with the cheese then remaining tomatoes and cilantro.
cook it in the oven on 400 degrees for 5-7 minutes until the cheese is melted and the tortilla is crispy

Submitter: 
SlimCookins
Description: 
Little individual tortilla pizza with shredded chicken, honey BBQ sauce, onions, tomatoes and mozzarella cheese
