Title:
THE Brownie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
2 ounces unsweetened chocolate
1 cup sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup toasted pecans, chopped
1/4 cup flour
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325°.
Spray the bottom and sides of an 8" x 8" baking pan with vegetable spray. Line the pan with parchment paper; spray the parchment and set the pan aside.
Place butter and chocolate in a microwavable bowl. Place in microwave and cook 45 seconds, stir. Repeat until the chocolate is melted and the mixture is smooth.
Mix eggs and vanilla and mix until smooth.
Add the chocolate mixture, pecans, flour, and salt; stir until blended. Spread batter into pan and bake 40–45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the middle, comes out clean. Cool before cutting,
