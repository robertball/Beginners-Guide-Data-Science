Title:
Dessert Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 8oz pkg. cream cheese
1 egg
1/3 c. sugar
dash of salt
1 - 6oz pkg. chocolate chips
1 1/2 c. sugar
2 1/4 c. flour
1 1/2 t. baking powder
1 1/2 t. vanilla
3/4 t. salt
1/2 c. vegetable oil
1 1/2 c. water
1 T. vinegar
6 T. cocoa

Directions:
Cream together the first 5 ingredients; add chips and set aside.
Mix remaining ingredients together and pour 1/3 into a greased 9x13 pan.
Drop cream cheese mixture by teaspoonfuls into the batter.
Pour remaining batter over cream cheese mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.
