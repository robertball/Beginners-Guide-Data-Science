Title:
Hamburger Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/330204.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 small onion, chopped (or use minced onion flakes)
3 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
1 (14.5 oz.) can diced tomatoes with garlic and onion
1 can condensed tomato soup
1 can whole kernel corn
1/2 to 3/4 can tomato sauce
3/4 tsp. sugar
3/4 tsp. salt
1/3 tsp. oregano
black pepper to taste
1 cup shredded Italian cheese blend

Directions:
Cook macaroni, drain, rinse, and set aside. Brown ground beef and onion. Drain fat from beef. Add macaroni, corn (drained), tomatoes (not drained), tomato soup, sugar, and seasonings. Mix well. Add tomato sauce, amount depends on your preference. Transfer mixture to 2 quart dish and bake at 350 for 45 minutes. Sprinkle with cheese 10 minutes before casserole is done baking.
