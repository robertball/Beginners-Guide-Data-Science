Title:
creamed peas, mushrooms and onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large bag frozen green peas
1 bag frozen pearl onions
8 oz sliced fresh mushrooms
1 clove of garlic, minced
2 cups half and half
3 tablespoons butter
3 tablespoons of flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

Directions:
run peas and onions under cold water to defrost.
in a large frying pan, melt butter and add mushrooms and garlic and salt and pepper.
saute until mushrooms are cooked.
add flour and mix until a light brown color is on flour, about 2 minutes
gradually add half and half, whisking constantly.
bring to a slight boil, reduce heat to low
add peas, onion and nutmeg.
simmer for 5 minutes
taste for salt and pepper

Submitter: 
merri rosenthal
Description: 
this is a wonderful side dish for any occasion. my family will eat peas this way.
