Title:
Tomato and Basil Meatball Sliders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 oz) pkg al frescoÂ® Tomato Basil Chicken Meatballs
1 1/2 cups tomato & garlic red sauce
16 dinner rolls, small, 2" x 2"
4 basil, fresh, leaves

Directions:
Pour the tomato sauce in a small sauce pan, add the meatballs, cover and heat over medium heat for 8 - 10 minutes. Gently stir to coat the meatballs with the sauce.
Slice the small rolls in half, and place a tomato & basil meatball in each roll. Assemble on a serving platter. Top each meatball with a dollop of the remaining heated tomato sauce.
Garnish with sprigs of fresh basil and serve immediately.
