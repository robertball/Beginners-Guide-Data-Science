Title:
Sweet Potato Walnut Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium sweet potatoes
8 ounces of frozen unsweetened peach slices
1/4 cup of packed brown sugar
1/2 tsp. ground ginger
1 and 1/2 Tablespoons butter
1/4 cup of chopped Walnuts

Directions:
Peel and slice potatoes into 1/4 inch slices.
In a 2 Quart baking dish, layer half the potato slices and half the peach slices.
In a small bowl, combine brown sugar and ginger.
Sprinkle half of mixture over potatoes and peaches.
Repeat layers
Dot with butter.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes, stirring once.
Uncover and sprinkle with walnuts.
Bake uncovered for about 15 additional minutes or until potatoes are tender.
Spoon the cooking juices over potatoes and peaches before serving.

Submitter:

Description: 
Not sure where this originated from; but my Aunt always made it around the holidays.  I have scaled it down to fit our family; but this could easily be doubled.
