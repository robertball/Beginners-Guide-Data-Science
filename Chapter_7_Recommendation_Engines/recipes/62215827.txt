Title:
Spicy crab quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 9-inch unbaked pie shell
1/2 cup crabmeat
1 shallot, minced
1 TB olive oil
1 garlic clove, minced
1/4 cup jalapeno peppers, chopped
1/2 tsp. hot sauce
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 cup pepper jack cheese, grated
1/2 tsp. worchestershire sauce
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
1 cup swiss cheese, grated
1 cup sharp cheddar cheese, grated
1 cup half and half
4 eggs

Directions:
In a little saute pan, heat olive oil and saute shallots until soft.
Set aside.
In a mixing bowl, add the crabmeat, garlic, jalapeno peppers, hot sauce, mayonnaise, salt, worcestershire sauce, pepper jack cheese and the Parmesan cheese
Mix well and place in the pie shell
Place the shallots on top of crab mixture.
Add the swiss cheese and sharp cheddar cheese on top of crab mixture.
In the meantime, whip eggs with the half and half in a small measuring cup.
Pour egg/half and half mixture over the cheeses.
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Bake quiche in preheated oven for approximately 45 minutes or until set.  Let cook for about 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
A crab quiche made with pepper jack cheese and a little hot sauce for a little kick.  I used canned crab claws but the imitation crab could be used.
