Title:
Cheese & Apple Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz cream cheese, softened
1/2 c. miracle whip
1/2 c. shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 c. finely chopped apples

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.
Chill
Serve with crackers.
