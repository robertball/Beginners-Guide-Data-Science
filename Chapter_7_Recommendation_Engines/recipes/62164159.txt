Title:
Car Dip by cooks4forty Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (5.2 oz.) package Boursin Garlic & Fine Herbs cheese
1 (8 oz.) package light cream cheese
1 lemon

Directions:
Zest and juice the lemon, and add the cream cheese and Boursin Cheese.  Mix until combined.  Serve with crackers or vegetables.

Submitter: 
cooks4forty
Description: 
This refreshing cheese dip is so easy that the first time I made it, I assembled it in the car on my way to a party!  The name stuck, and it's requested often.
