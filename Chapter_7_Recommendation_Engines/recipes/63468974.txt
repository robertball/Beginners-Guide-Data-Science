Title:
Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans shrimp
1 small onion, chopped fine
Dash Worcestershire sauce
8 oz block of cream cheese +
3 oz block of cream cheese
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp. horseradish
2 tbsp ketchup
2 tsp. dill weed

Directions:
Prepare one day in advance. Soften cream cheese, and beat well with an electric mixer. Drain shrimp. Stir shrimp and remaining ingredients into the softened cream cheese. Cover and refrigerate until serving. Enjoy with crackers.
