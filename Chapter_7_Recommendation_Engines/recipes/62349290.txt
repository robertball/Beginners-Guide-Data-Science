Title:
Spicy Jalepeno Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 Cloves of Garlic
Handful of Canned Jalepenos plus pickling juice
1-2 Tablespoons of Lemon Juice
1-2 Tablespoons of Lime Juice
1-2 Tablespoons of Oil
1/2 small can of green chiles
Dash of Cumin
Dash of Chili Powder
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into food processor and blend until smooth. Refridgerate.

Submitter: 
LaurenSyd
Description: 
A great salad dressing for Mexican meals, can also be used as a salsa or dressing for tacos or enchiladas.
