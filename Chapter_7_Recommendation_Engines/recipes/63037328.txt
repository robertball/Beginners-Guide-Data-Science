Title:
Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
1/4 cup chopped celery (optional)
1 small onion, chopped
**note: use equal amounts of mayonnaise and yogurt, less of each the drier you want your salad to be
2-4 tablespoons nonfat mayonnaise
2-4 tablespoons nonfat plain yogurt
ground black pepper to taste
salt to taste

Directions:
1. Boil chicken to cook it, shred it once it's cooled off
2. Stir the chicken, celery, onion, mayonnaise, yogurt, pepper, and salt in a medium bowl.
