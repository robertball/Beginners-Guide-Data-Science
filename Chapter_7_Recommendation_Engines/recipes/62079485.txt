Title:
Mammie Eisenhower Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. chocolate chips
14 oz. marshmallow creme
12 oz. German chocolate chopped
2 cups chopped nuts
4 1/2 cups sugar
2 Tablesoon butter
4 teaspoons vanilla
1/8 teaspoon salt
1  12 oz.can evaporated milk

Directions:
Step 1  place in large mixing bowl
chocolate chips,marshmallow creme,German chocolate and chopped nuts
Step 2  In large sacepan bring to a boil over medium heat, sugar, butter, vanilla,salt, and evaporated milk. Boil for 6 minutes  Pour boiling syrup mixture over ingrediants in mixing bowl. Beat until chocolate is melted. Pour into 2 buttered 8x8 inch pans. Let stand several hours till cool and set. cut and store in refridgerator.
