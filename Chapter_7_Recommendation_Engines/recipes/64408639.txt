Title:
Fresh Balsalmic peach chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 t olive oil
1/2 c finely diced red pepper
1/3 c freshly diced red onion
3 ripe peaches, peeled, pitted, diced
1 t sugar
1/4 c balsamic vinegar
1 t fresh thyme
salt and pepper

Directions:
heat oil in a skillet over medium heat, add the red pepper and onion and cook until softened.  Add the peaches and continue cooking until softened.  Stir in sugar until disolved 2 to 3 minutes.  mix in the vinegar and bring to a simmer, cook until sauce thickens, about 10 to 12 minutes
remove from heat, season the thyme, salt and pepper
