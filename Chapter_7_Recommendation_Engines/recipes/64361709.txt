Title:
Can't Leave Them Alone Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 box white cake mix
* 2 eggs
* 1/3 cup oil
* 1 can sweetened condensed milk, fat free or regular
* 1/3 or 1/2 stick of butter
* 1 cup of chips of any flavor (semisweet chocolate, white chocolate etc.)

Directions:
1) Mix cake mix, oil, and eggs together. Lightly spray a 13x9 cake pan with whatever spray you use. Press 2/3 of the mixture into the bottom of the pan, reserving the rest. Melt the butter, chips and milk in microwave for 1 minute and 20 seconds. Stir until well mixed. Pour over dough in pan. Pat out the rest of the dough in strips of 3 and place on top of milk mixture. Bake at 350 for 18 minutes or until the top is lightly browned. Cool and Enjoy!
