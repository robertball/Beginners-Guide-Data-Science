Title:
Grilled Garlic Fury Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb Uncooked and peeled/de-veined Shrimp
1/4 cup Margarine or Butter
1 Tbl spoon Olive Oil
3 Tbl spoons White Wine
1 Tsp Old Bay
2-3 Tbl spoons (or to taste) fresh or dried minced Garlic
1 Tbl spoon (or to taste) Garlic Powder
Salt and coarse grind Pepper to taste

Directions:
Place all ingredients except for the shrimp in a medium size bowl large enough to hold the shrimp too.
Microwave the mixture until it is melted but not scalding hot (you want to marinate the shrimp-not cook them in the mixture). Place Shrimp in mixture and marinate for a few hours (less if desired) in fridge stirring occasionally.
Grill Shrimp over Med to Med Hi heat on skewers or in a grilling basket for 2-3 minutes each side, add marinade while they cook. Grill untill the edges brown/char for best flavor-Enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
Marinated Crazy Garlic/Butter Shrimp that everyone will love! Quick and easy to prepare and cook, marinate for as little as 30 minutes. This one is for the Garlic Lovers out there!!
