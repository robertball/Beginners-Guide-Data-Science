Title:
Irish Potato Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. sugar
1 c. oleo or butter
4 eggs
1 c. nuts
2 c. flour
1/2 c. cocoa powder
1 c. mashed potatoes (not instant)

Directions:
Cream oleo and sugar, add eggs, one at a time, mix well after each addition.  Add dry ingredients.  Mix well.  Fold potatoes in last.  Grease and flour 9x13 pan.  Bake 350 degrees F. for 30 min.  Frost with quick fluffy frosting or leave plain and sprinkle with powdered sugar.

Submitter: 
Cindy Carnes
Description: 
This cake recipe was given to me by a dear lady in Colorado in the early 1980's.  I have used my leftover mashed potatoes this way for years!!!
