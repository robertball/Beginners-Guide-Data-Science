Title:
This is so good..... Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb Ground Beef
1/2 Lb Ground Itailian Sausage, I use hot
1 28oz. Can Crushed Tomatoes
1 12oz. Can Tomato Paste
2 Tea. Garlic Powder
2 Tea. Oregano
1 Tea. Basil
1 Cup Cottage Cheese
1 Cup Ricotta Cheese
1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
4 oz. Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
4 oz. Shredded Provolone Cheese
12 oz. Lasagna Noodles, uncooked
1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Cook meats till brown, drain fats.
Add tomatoes, tomato paste, garlic powder, oregano, and basil and simmer about an hour or more.
In a bowl - stir together Cottage Cheese, Ricotta Cheese, and 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese.
In a seperate bowl - Mix together the Mozzzrella and the Provolone cheese.
Heat oven to 350.
Lightly grease the bottom of a 13x9 inch pan.
Add a little sauce to the bottom on the pan.
Layer ingredients starting with the noodles, (wet noodle slightly) - then the meat sauce; then lightly spread out the cottage/ricotta cheese mixture; then sprinkle on the mozz/provolone cheese mixture.  Repeat for the second layer, then end with a third layer of noodles, meat sauce and the shredded cheeses.
Sprinkly with the remianing 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese.
Bake uncovered for 30 minutes.
Let sit for 15 minutes before serving.
NOTE:  You will need to break the noodles to fit the pan. Its usually 3 across and a half one at the bottom.

This has been a family favorite for over 20 years.  My family will not order Lasagne anywhere cause they like this so much.
