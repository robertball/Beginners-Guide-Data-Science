Title:
Mexican Wedding Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup finely chopped pecans
2 sticks (1 cup) of unsalted butter, softened
4 Tb granulated sugar
2 cups of cake flour
2 tsp vanilla extract
Powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees F
Cream together the pecans, butter and granulated sugar
Beat in the flour and vanilla.
Form into 1" balls and put on uncreased or parchment-lined cookie sheet.
Bake for 35 minutes, until lightly browned.
While still hot, roll in powdered sugar and place on rack to cool completely.
