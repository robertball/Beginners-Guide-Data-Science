Title:
Lemon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinned and boned chicken breast(about 11/2lb.)
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/3 cup fl
4 tbs butter divided
2 tbs olive oil
1/4 cup chicken broth
1/4 cup lemon juice
8 lemon slices
1/4 cup chopped parsley
garnish with lemon slices
cup of sliced mushrooms

Directions:
Cut each chicken breast in half lengthwise.  Pound chicken until flatten to 1/4 inch thickness.
Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper. Lightly dredge chicken in flour. .
Melt 1 tbs butter with 1 tbs olive oil over med heat in skillet. cook half the chicken for 2-3 mins
on each side until golden brown.  Transform chicken to platter and keep warm. Repeat with rest of chicken pieces.
Add broth and lemon juice to skillet with slice mushroom . cook 1-2 mins until sauce it slightly
thickened. loosen particles from bottom of skillet. Add 8 lemon slices.
Remove skillet from heat and add parsley and remaining 2 tbs butter, stir until butter melt. Pour over chicken. Server immediately
