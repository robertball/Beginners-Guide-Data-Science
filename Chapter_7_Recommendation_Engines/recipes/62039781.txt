Title:
Grannies Holly Leaf Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
30 marshmallows (regular size)
1 1/2 tsp green food coloring
3 cups corn flakes
cinnamon candies

Directions:
1.  Melt butter and marshmallows over double boiler.
2.  Add food coloring.
3.  Add corn flakes.
4.  Mix well.
5.  Drop by tablespoons on waxed paper.
6.  Add about 2 or 3 cinnamon candies to each to give holly berry effect.

Submitter: 
ERLENSEE_GERMANY
Description: 
These cookies make our family Christmas.  I don't think that we have ever had a Christmas without them.  We always end up needing more.
