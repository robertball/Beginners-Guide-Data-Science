Title:
Grandma Grates's Bavarian Cream Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
6 eggs
1 c sugar
1 c cake flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. vanilla
Filling:
1 pint coffee cream
2 tsp. Know gelatin (1 packet)
1/2 c cold water
8 egg yolks
1 c sugar
1 pint whipping cream
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 pound blanched almonds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Instructions for cake:
1-Separate eggs.
2- Beat yolks in with sugar.
3-Sift flour, add baking powder and fold eggs into sugar.
4- Beat whites until stiff then add vanilla beat a bit longer. Fold into flour mixture.
5- Divide between two cake rounds and bake for 30 minutes.
Instructions for filling:
1- Heat coffee cream to a boil.
2- Soak gelatin in 1/2 c cold water. When cream boils, add gelatin and let boil for 2 minutes.
3- Beat egg yolks with sugar. Add to cream and boil again.
4- Cool. Add whipped cream and vanilla to the filling. Let cool in refrigerator.
Putting cake together:
Cut each round in a horizontal section when cooled. Spread filling between layers, sides and top of cake.
Roast almonds in butter until golden brown and then chop them finely. Sprinkle on tops and sides of cake

Description: 
Grandma Grates made this torte for us when we were growing up. All the adults used to just go nuts when her cakes would appear. I remember it was very rich but absolutely scrumptious!
