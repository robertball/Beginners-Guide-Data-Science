Title:
Spicy Doodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/642338.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C. all purpose flour, 1 tsp. cinnamon, 1/2 tsp. baking soda,1/2 C. shortening melted, 1/3 C.sugar, 1/3 C. brown sugar, 1 beaten egg, 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract. 2 Tbls. sugar, 1/4 tsp cinnamon for rolling.

Directions:
In a medium bowl combine flour,1 tsp. cinnamon and baking soda, set aside.In a large mixer bowl combine the melted shortening,and sugars until combined. Mix in the beaten egg, and vanilla. Stir in the spiced flour mixture. Shape dough into 1 inch balls. Roll each ball into the sugar cinnamon mixture. Place on lightly greased cookie sheet 2 inches apart. Flatten each ball by criss crossing with a fork. Bake 375 degrees 6 to 8 minutes or until edges are golden. Remove and cool on wire racks.

Submitter: 
Janet Shum
Description: 
This is a variation of a snickerdoodle that my family just loves. Simple and delicious.
