Title:
Chicken & Broccoli Stuffed Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2366294.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Box of Jumbo Shells
2 TBL Veg Oil
2 Chik Breast (cut into squares)
1/2 Onion (I use Green Onions-personal)
4 cloves Garlic (minced)
1 12oz. bag Frozen Broccoli
3 TBL Flour
2 Cps Milk
1/3 cp Grated Parmesan
1 Cp Mozzarella Cheese

Directions:
1. Preheat Oven to 375*
2. Lightly grease 9X13" Casserole Dish, set aside
3. Bring Large pot of water to boil. Salt water GENEROUSLY & add Shells. Cook (aldente), set aside
4. Heat oil in Large skillet over Med-high heat. Once hot, add Chik. Season with Salt, Pepper & Season salt to taste. Allow to SEAR by NOT moving Chik for a few minutes. Once SEARED, toss Chik. Add in onion & garlic. Saute a few minutes till onions are tender. Add more seasoning to taste. Sprinkle Parmesan over mixture & stir in. Let sauce cook a few minutes on low. Spoon some of sauce in bottom of Casserole dish. Take each shell & fill with Chik mixture. Place each shell in dish. Sprinkle Mozzarella over the top.
