Title:
Asparagus Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
1/4 cup olive oil
2 cloves garlic
1/2 onion, chopped
1 pound fresh asparagus spears, trimmed
1/3 to 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt butter with olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic , onion and asparagus spears, and cook, stirring occasionally for about 10 minutes, or to desired firmness. Drain off excess oil, and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper.

Description:
