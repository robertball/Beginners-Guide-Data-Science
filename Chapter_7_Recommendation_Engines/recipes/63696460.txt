Title:
Family Style Tuna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
225g/8oz Linguine - cooked al dente and well drained
6tbsp Butter
450g/1lb Mushrooms - thinly sliced
4 Spring Onions - thinly sliced
4tbsp Plain Flour
225ml/8 fl oz Milk
½tsp Salt or to taste
¼tsp freshly ground Black Pepper
2 x 120g/4oz tins Tuna - drained and flaked
225g/8oz frozen Peas - cooked and drained
120g/4oz Parmesan Cheese - finely grated
225g/8oz fresh Bread Crumbs

Directions:
Pre -Heat oven to 180c/350f/gas 4 to 5;
Grease an Oven baking dish;
Melt 4 tbsp butter in a large saucepan over medium-low heat;
Add mushrooms and sauté, stirring occasionally, until browned and tender;
Add spring onions, stir in well, and continue cooking for 1 minute;
Stir in the flour until well mixed;
Gradually, stirring continually, stir in the milk;
Continue cooking, stirring continually, to avoid lumps, until thickened;
Add salt, to taste, and pepper;
Stir in the tuna and peas and mix well;
Add the linguini and mix in well;
Pour the mixture into the baking dish and sprinkle over with Parmesan;
Melt the remaining 2tbsp butter and toss with the bread crumbs;
Sprinkle the crumbs over the top of the oven mixture;
Bake for 25 to 30 minutes or until lightly browned and bubbly.

A simple, tasty Tuna treat for all the family.
Added bonus - NO soup.
