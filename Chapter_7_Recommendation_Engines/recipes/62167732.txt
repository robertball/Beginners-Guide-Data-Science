Title:
best mocha drink, EVER! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 heaping tbs. instant coffee
2 cups milk
3 tbs. sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 tbs. chocolate syrup
2 tbs. evaporated milk
whipped cream (optional)

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender.
blend on high.
Drink over ice, or with whipped cream on top.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
geranium
Description: 
This yummy mocha drink, was created by my brother and I. It is best topped with whipped cream!
