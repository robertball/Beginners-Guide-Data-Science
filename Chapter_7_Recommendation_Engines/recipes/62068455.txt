Title:
Best-Ever Ginger Snaps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
2 c. four
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. ground ginger
1/2 tsp. ground cloves
3/4 c. molassas
1 egg, slightly beaten
sugar

Directions:
Combine 1 cup sugar, flour, salt, soda, and spices; stir lightly.  Cut in shortening to resemble coarse crumbs.  Stir in egg and molassas.
Shape into 1 inch balls and roll into sugar.  Place on ungreased baking sheet.  Bake at 350 for 9 mins.
