Title:
Wicked Whole Wheat Orange Carrot Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2622011.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup of white flour
1 cup of whole wheat flour
1 tablespoon of baking powder
2 teaspoons baking soda
1/5 teaspoons of ground cinnamon
4 eggs
1/3 cup of oil
2/3 cup of unsweetened applesauce
3/4 cup of brown sugar
3 cups of shredded carrots
the zest of one orange

Directions:
1.) Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (175 degrees Celsius).  Grease or line muffin tray with paper muffin liners.
2.) Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and cinnamon.
3.) In a separate bowl, mix together eggs, oil, applesauce, and brown sugar.
4.) Combine dry ingredients with wet, mix until just moistened.
5.) Fold in carrots and orange zest.  Spoon into muffin cups.
6.) Bake in preheated oven for 30-35 minutes.  Enjoy!
