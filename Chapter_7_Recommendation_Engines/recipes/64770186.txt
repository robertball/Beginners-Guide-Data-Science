Title:
Keto Friendly "sugared" nuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 egg whites
1/2 C Swerve sweetener (Granular or Brown Granular) or erythritol
1 pound nuts of your choice (walnuts, pecans, hazelnuts, whole almonds, sliced almonds, brazil nuts)
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C). Grease one baking sheet.
Put Swerve sweetener in a small mixing bowl
In a separate mixing bowl, beat the egg whites until frothy, add  salt, and cinnamon.
Add nuts to egg white spice mixture , stir to coat the nuts evenly. Remove the nuts, and toss them in the Swerve "sugar" until coated. Spread the nuts out on the prepared baking sheet.
Bake at 250 degrees F (120 degrees C) for 1 hour. Stir every 15 minutes

Description: 
I make these over the Holidays, and I follow a low carb diet, these slow roasted mixed nuts are addictive!  The smell coming out of your kitchen will be spectacular!  It is perfect for anyone on a reduced carb, or diabetic diet, and it is totally customizable, as you can put in any combination of nuts!
