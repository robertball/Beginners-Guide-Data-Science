Title:
Ice Cream Cone Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Betty Crocker SuperMoist white cake mix
Water, veggie oil, and eggs called for in the cake mix
24 flat bottom ice cream cones
2 containers of Betty Crocker Frosting
Betty Crocker assorted candy sprinkles

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 (325 for a dark or nonstick pan)  Using a regular sized, 24ct muffin pan, place a paper baking cup in each compartment.
Make cake batter as directed on box, using water, oil and eggs.  Fill each cup 2/3 full of batter.  Place ice cream cone upside down on batter in each cup.
Bake 15-20 min or until toothpick inserted in cake comes out clean.  Remove from pan.  Cool completely, about 30 min.  Remove paper baking cups.  Frost cake portions with icing.  Decorate with candy sprinkles.
