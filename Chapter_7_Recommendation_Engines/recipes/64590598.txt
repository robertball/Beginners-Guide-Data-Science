Title:
Pindang Telur (Eggs in Soy Sauce) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 small onion, finely sliced
1 fresh red chili, seeded and sliced
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon finely grated fresh ginger
1/2 teaspoon dried shrimp paste (trasi)
1 large ripe tomato, diced
1 tablespoon white vinegar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon palm sugar
3 tablespoons soy sauce
1/2 cup water
4 hard boiled eggs, peeled and halved

Directions:
Heat oil in a saucepan over medium heat and fry the onion, chili, garlic and ginger until onion is soft and starts to turn golden.
Add shrimp paste and fry, mashing with the back of a spoon.  Add tomato and cook until tomatoes is pulpy.
Add vinegar, salt, sugar, soy sauce and water.  Cover and simmer until sauce is thickened and smooth.
Add egg halves and heat through.

Submitter: 
mikekey
Description: 
An Indonesian dish.  Serve as an accompaniment to chicken dishes.  Shrimp paste can be found in Asian markets.
