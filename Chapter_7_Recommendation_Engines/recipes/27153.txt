Title:
Fruit Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992884.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups chopped apple
1 1/4 cups sliced banana
1 1/4 cups seedless green grapes
1 1/4 cups sliced strawberries
2 tablespoons orange juice
1 package (12) ORTEGA® Taco Shells
1 (8 ounce) container lowfat strawberry yogurt
1/2 cup toasted coconut (optional)
1/2 cup sliced almonds (optional)

Directions:
COMBINE fruit and orange juice in large bowl.
FILL taco shells with fruit mixture.  Top with yogurt, coconut and almonds.

Submitter: 
Ortega®
Description: 
These tangy fruit tacos are a unique and delicious dessert idea for your family or for a dinner party. And, they are lowfat and nutritious to boot!
