Title:
Parslied potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs small, new red potatoes, scrubbed
1 tbs vegetable oil
1 med. onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 c chicken broth
3/4 c chopped fresh parsley
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
Peel a strip of skin around the middle of each potato.  Set potatoes in bowl of cold water
Heat oil in a large skillet over med / high heat.  Saute onion & garlic for 5 minutes.  Add broth and 3/4 c parsley.  Mix and bring to a boil.
Drain potatoes and place in single layer in skillet.  Bring to boil and simmer, uncovered until potatoes are tender (10 minutes)
Remove potatoes from skillet into serving bowl.  Add pepper to pan and stir.  Pour sauce over potatoes and serve immediately.

Submitter:

We love this quick, simple side dish that is easy to prepare and looks elegant on the table.
