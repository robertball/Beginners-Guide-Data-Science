Title:
Apple Pan Dowdy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
PASTRY:
2 cups flour
1 tsp salt
2/3 cup shortening
4-6 Tbsp water
FILLING:
1/4 cup sugar
1/8 tsp salt
1/8 tsp cinnamon
5 cups apples, peeled and sliced
1/3 cup molasses
3 Tbsp water
2 Tbsp butter, melted

Directions:
Mix flour and 1 tsp salt.  Cut in shortening.  Add enough water Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
In large bowl, mix flour and 1 tsp salt.  Cut in shortening.  Add enough water to hold ingredients together.
Roll out 2/3 of pastry and line 7-1/2 x 11-1/4 x 1-1/2 inch pan.
In another large bowl, mix together sugar, salt, and cinnamon.   Combine with apple slices and pour into pan.
In a small bowl, combine molasses, water and melted butter.  Pour over apples in pan.
Roll out remaining pastry and cover apple mixture.  Trim excess pastry and pinch edges together with fingertips.  Prick top with fork.
Bake at for 45 minutes, or until crust is delicate brown and apples are soft.  Remove from oven and chop mixture with a knife until pastry is blended in the apples.  Serve with custard sauce.
