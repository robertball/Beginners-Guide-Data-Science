Title:
The Tequila Squeeze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1005660.jpg
Ingredients:
1 shot Sauza brand Blue Agave Silver Tequila
1 shot Amaretto almond flavored liqueur
1 shot diet lemon/lime soda
1 squeeze fresh lemon juice, about 2 tsp.
1 lemon slice - garnish

Directions:
Mix together the tequila, Amaretto, diet soda and fresh lemon juice.
Fill a cocktail glass half way with crushed ice.
Pour mixture over ice and garnish with a lemon slice.
Sip & Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Lillian
Description: 
This drink has a slight sweet almond essence with a little fizz and light citrus flavor!
It isn't a strong drink, but it isn't wimpy either!
