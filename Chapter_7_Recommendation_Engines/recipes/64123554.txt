Title:
Aunt Bea's Refrigerator Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6875180.jpg
Ingredients:
8 c sliced cucumbers
1 c sliced onion
1 c chopped celery
1 tbsp salt
1 c sugar
1/2 c vinegar
1/2 tbsp celery seed
1/2 tbsp mustard seed

Directions:
Prepare first three ingredients, sprinkle with salt and let stand for 30 minutes. Drain well.
Mix final four ingredients well and pour over vegetables. Refrigerate.
