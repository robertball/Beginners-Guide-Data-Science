Title:
Sheila's Greek Style Avocado Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/106452.jpg
Ingredients:
1 avocado - peeled, pitted and diced
1 clove garlic, minced
2 tablespoons lime juice
1 roma (plum) tomato, seeded and diced
1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
Mash together the avocado, garlic, and lime juice in a bowl until nearly smooth. Fold in the diced tomato and feta cheese.

Submitter:

Description: 
Healthy and delicious as a dip or afternoon snack.  A tasty blend of Greek salad and Mexican guacamole flavors make this a different and healthful choice for a party appetizer as well!
