Title:
Cabin Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 medium potatoes - peeled, cooked and cubed
3 cups turkey Keilbasa
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup butter or margarine
2 Handfuls of Spinach leaves
8 eggs - cracked and whisked
salt and pepper to taste
Sour cream

Directions:
In a large skillet warm potatoes, Keilbasa and onion in butter, stirring frequently, until potatoes are lightly browned. Add eggs; cook through, fold in spinach leaves.  Season with salt and pepper. Serve with sour cream if desired.

Submitter: 
MONIQUEWS
Description: 
My family named this dish because I served it when we vacationed at our cabin on the Mississippi River. Now it's become such a favorite that I often make it when we're home.Modified from: Cabin Hash
