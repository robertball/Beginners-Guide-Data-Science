Title:
Mediterranean Tomato Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (17oz) frozen puffed pastry, thawed
1 1/2 C (6oz) shredded Gouda cheese
6 plum tomatoes, cut into 1/4-in slices
1/4 C pitted ripe olives, coarsely chopped
1 C (4oz) crumbled feta cheese, or other choice
Minced fresh basil and oregano

Directions:
Unfold puff pastry; cut each sheet into 16 squares. Transfer squared to greased baking sheets. Sprinkle with Gouda cheese; top with tomatoes, olives and feta cheese.
Bake at 400 degrees for 14-18 min. or until golden brown; sprinkle with herbs. Serve warm or at room temperature

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Great summer appetizer, like a mini pizza made with fresh tomatoes, fresh herbs, olives and cheeses
