Title:
Mom B's Peanut Blossom Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c shortening
1/2 c peanut butter
1/2 c sugar
1/2 c packed brown sugar
1 beaten egg
2 T milk
1 t vanilla
1 3/4 c flour
1 t baking soda
1 t salt

Directions:
Cream together first four ingredients.  Add egg, milk, and vanilla.  Beat well.  Sift flour, soda, and salt.  Blend into others.  Shape into balls.  Roll in sugar.  Bake for 8 minutes, until cracks just begin to form.  Remove and place Hersheys Kiss on top.  Bake 2 more minutes, then cook on rack.
