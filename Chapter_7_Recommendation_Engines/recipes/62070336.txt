Title:
Mom's Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 lb pot roast
Salt and Pepper to taste
1 tsp garlic powder
1 can condensed Golden Mushroom Soup
1 can water
1 small yellow onion
1/2 cup beef broth as needed
8 small red potatoes, halved

Directions:
Season pot roast with Garlic powder, salt and pepper and place in slow cooker.
Slice onion into thin rings and surround roast.
In seperate bowl, mix golden mushroom soup with can of water and  beef broth, pour over roast and onions.
Cook for about 8 hours for smaller roast and 10 for larger one, making sure that the lid remains on.  If potatoes are desired, add them, pushing them down into the gravy, about 30 minutes before the cooking is complete.

Submitter: 
MRSPARKERII
Description: 
It doesnt get easier than this!  This pot roast is tender and flavorful with a perfect gravy for serving with potatoes. The soup portion can be doubled for more gravy!
