Title:
Snickerdoodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2c    butter softened
1/2c    shortening
1 1/2c  sugar
2       eggs
2 3/4c  flour
2tspn   cream of tartar
1tspn   baking soda
1/4tspn salt
2Tbspn sugar and 2Tbspn cinnamon mixed together to roll cookie balls in prior to baking

Directions:
heat oven to 400
Cream butter, shortening, sugar and eggs.
Blend in dry ingredients until a soft dough forms
Roll into into balls about a tspn at a time and roll them in the sugar cinnamon mixture
Bake 8-10 minutes
