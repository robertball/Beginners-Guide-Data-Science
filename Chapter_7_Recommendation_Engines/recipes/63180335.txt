Title:
Broccoli and Cheddar Nuggets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound frozen chopped broccoli, cooked, drained and chopped fine
1 cup bread crumbs
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
3 eggs
1/2 tsp basil
1/2 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Lightly coat a baking sheet with oil and set aside.
Combine all ingredients and mix well.
Shape mixture into nuggets or fun shapes and place on a baking tray.  Bake for 20-25 minutes, turning after 15 minutes.
Serve warm.

Submitter: 
goodeats
Description: 
Yummy alternative to chicken nuggets for the kids.  I'm not a broccoli fan in general, but this is really tasty.
