Title:
Easy Chicken Stir-Fry with Broccoli LGLS 176 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts,bnls, sknls and in thin strips
6 cups of broccli florets and stems chopped
1 pound fresh mushrooms sliced
4 cups of cabbage, red or white sliced
2 tbsp fresh gingerroot, peeled and minced
2 tbsp evoo
2 tbsp dry sherry or cooking wine
1 cup homemade chicken stock or 1 cube bouillon no msg
2 tbsp Bragg soy
2 tbsp homemade mayo page 38-40
2 tbsp water
black pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Heavy skillet heat oil very hot, add chicken and 1 tbsp ginger remove chicken and set aside.
2. Reheat skillet add brocoi, mushrooms and remaining gingerroot, and stir for 2 minutes add water for sticking
3. Mix sherry or wine and stock or bouillon, soy  and pour over skillet let steam for 2 minutes.
4. Stir in Chicken mix, mayonnaise and water and boil.
5. Add cabbage, stir for 1 minute to crisp and then season with pepper and serve hot.
