Title:
Slush with Vodka Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Large tin of pineapple juice
Bottle of chi-chi mix
1/2 tin of coconut snow....(milk)
1 26 oz bottle of vodka
Ginger-ale

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in shift in blender. Pour into pail with lid and freeze over night. To serve use ice cream scoop and put into glass 1/2 slush 1/2 ginger-ale.
For kids replace the vodka with ginger-ale and then freeze.....remember to write on the lid which one has the vodka and which one doesn't or you could have a problem haha

Submitter: 
C.Lynn
Description: 
Wonderful drinks for family occations or parties.....great in the summer when its hot and  you want a nice drink to cool you off.....
