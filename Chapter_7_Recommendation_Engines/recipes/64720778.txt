Title:
Amish barbeque Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1cup butter
1cup cider vinegar
2cups water
2Tablespoons worcestershire sauce
1Tablespoon salt
1teaspoon pepper
10-12 pieces of chicken

Directions:
Combiner ingredients in a sauce pan and simmer till butter is melted and salt dissolved. pour over chicken and allow to soak for a few hours. Grill and baste as you grill!
