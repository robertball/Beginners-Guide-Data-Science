Title:
Pina Colada Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 C Canned Crushed Pineapple w/ Juice
1/2 C Water
3 T Rum
2 T Margarine or Butter, softened
3 1/4 C Bread Flour
1/2 C Shredded Coconut, toasted
2 T Sugar
1 tsp. Salt
1 3/4 tsp. Active Dry Yeast

Directions:
Place all ingredients in the bread maker's bread pan in the order listed
Bake on Sweet setting, Light color until done.
