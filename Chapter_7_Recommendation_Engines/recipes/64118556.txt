Title:
Chocolate Cherry Cake Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1307212.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup coconut flour
3/4 cup tapioca flour
1/2 cup coconut sugar
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
3 large eggs
1 cup unsweetened applesauce
1/2 cup melted coconut oil, cooled
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
3/4 cup sliced fresh cherries, pitted and halved
Chocolate Ganache
1 cup good quality chocolate
1/2 cup heavy cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
I a medium mixing bowl combine coconut flour, tapioca flour, coconut sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda and baking powder.
In a large mixing bowl, whisk together eggs, applesauce, coconut oil and vanilla extract.
Add dry ingredient to wet ingredient and mix together until well mixed.
Fold in chocolate chips and fresh cherries.
Pour batter into a 9Ã—13 baking dish for 22 minutes
Top with chocolate ganache then enjoy!
Chocolate Ganache
Put all ingredients in a microwave safe bowl. Set on high for 1 minute 30 seconds. Remove from microwave and mix with a small sized rubber spatula until chocolate is melted and creamy. Drizzle over your cake!

Cindy Anschutz Barbieri
Description: 
I want to cook and invited my lake neighbors over for a paleo chicken marsala dinner and knew there had to be a chocolate dessert. Looking around the kitchen I took out all of the ingredients and put them together for a yummy Chocolate Cherry Cake Squares Paleo dessert!
