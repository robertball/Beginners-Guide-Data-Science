Title:
Cranberry and Cream Cheese Crescent Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz cream cheese, softened
6 tablespoons chopped dried cranberries
2 tablespoon chopped fresh chives
2 teaspoon finely diced seeded jalapeño chile
2 can (8 oz) Pillsbury™ refrigerated crescent dough sheet or 1 can (8 oz) Pillsbury™ refrigerated crescent dinner rolls .

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees
Line cookie sheets with parchment paper
Mix Cream Cheese, Cranberries, chives and jalapeno chile.
Roll and Press into  2 12x8 inch rectangles.
Cut into 6 x4 rows to make 24 squares out of each crescent rolls
Place 1 teaspoon cream cheese mixture onto center of each square.
Bring 4 corners together to overlap slightly in center at top of each bundle.
Twist and pinch to seal, leaving small gaps between seams.
Bake
