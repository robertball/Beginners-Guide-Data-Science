Title:
Outrageous Ranch Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
3 cups shredded lettuce
1/2 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing
8 large taco shells, heated
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 medium tomato, diced (optional)

Directions:
Brown beef on stovetop, crumbling into small pieces; drain.  Toss lettuce with Ranch.  Fill each taco shell evenly with ground beef, lettuce and cheese.  Top with tomatoes, if desired.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
Tacos topped with lettuce and creamy Ranch dressing provides a unique twist on tacos.
