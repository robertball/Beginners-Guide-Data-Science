Title:
Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068354.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup butter, softened
2 cups sifted confectioners' sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a medium bowl, cream together the cream cheese and butter until creamy.
Mix in the vanilla, then gradually stir in the confectioners' sugar.
Store in the refrigerator after use.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
This cream cheese icing goes great on pumpkin bread, carrot cake, chocolate cake, and cookies. Just four ingredients!Modified from: Cream Cheese Frosting II
