Title:
Layered Lettuce Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bags shredded lettuce
3 hard boiled eggs
1 cup frozen peas
Supreme salad seasoning
1 lb bacon- cooked
Mayo
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Place lettuce in the bottom of a 9X13in pan.  Chop eggs and place on top of lettuce.  Crumble bacon and place on top of eggs.  Do the same with the peas then sprinkle with salad supreme.  In a separate bowl, whip the may until smooth and spread on top of salad.  Then sprinkle with Parmesan Cheese.  Chill until ready to serve.
