Title:
Jim's Quinoa Brown Rice Mediterranean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup quinoa
1 Cup brown rice
4 cups water
1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
1/4 c lime juice
1/4 c- lemon juice
1- T red wine vinegar
1-T ground cumin
1- t minced garlic
1- t teaspoon red pepper flakes
1- dry pint halved cherry tomatoes
1/2 small onion diced
1/4 cup mint diced
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring quinoa and brown rice in water to a boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until quinoa/rice is tender and water has been absorbed, 10 to 15 minutes. Set aside to cool.
Whisk olive oil, red wine vinegar, lime juice,lemon juice, cumin,mint,garlic and red pepper flakes together in a bowl. Cut the tomatoes in half and add including juice from the tomatoes.
Combine with tomatoes, onions and mint together in a bowl. Pour over quinoa/rice, toss to coat. Season with salt and black pepper. Chill in refrigerator for 1 hour or serve immediately

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
I'm not usually a fan of mint buy it gives this dish the taste of spring. It's healthy and great as a side or just make a meal of it!
