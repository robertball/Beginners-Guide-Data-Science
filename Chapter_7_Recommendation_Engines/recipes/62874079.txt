Title:
Easy White Chicken Enchiladas My Version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ounces cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup green salsa
4 Boneless/Skinless Chicken Breasts
1 cup shredded Mexican-style cheese blend
1 cup Tostito brand white queso.  (found in chip aisle normally)
1/2 package of taco seasoning
Weber N'Orleans Cajun Seasoning (or any blackening spice will do)
6 (8 inch) flour tortillas

Directions:
Sautee chicken breasts in olive oil (or grill if you have the time).   Sprinkle with blackened seasoning.  Once cooked, cut up into small pieces.  Set aside.
In small bowl mix sour cream, cream cheese, green salsa, taco seasoning and shredded cheddar. Add the cubed chicken. Mix well.   Lay out the tortilla and spread 1/6 of the mixture into the center of the tortilla.  Roll tightly into cylinder.  Repeat with remaining tortillas.  Place in a greased (Pam) 13 x 9 x 2 baking dish. Drizzle queso over the top. Cover with foil and bake on 350 for approx 20-30 minutes.  Remove foil.  Add some shredded cheddar.  Bake for another 10 minutes.

Description: 
Wonderful way to serve Restaurant Quality Enchiladas at home.  The taco seasoning adds the perfect zing.  Serve with Mexican Style Corn or Refried Beans. Enjoy!Modified from: Easy White Chicken Enchiladas
