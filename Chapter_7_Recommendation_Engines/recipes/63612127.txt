Title:
Ali's Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1004696.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb. southern style hash browns
1 lb. block Velveeta cheese (cubed)
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
16 oz. sour cream
1 stick melted butter
2 cups corn flakes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Lightly grease a 13 by 9 pan.
Mix first four ingredients, put into pan.
Mix melted butter and corn flakes and use to top mixture in pan.
Bake at 350 for one hour or until bubbly.
