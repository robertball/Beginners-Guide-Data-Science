Title:
lemon pepper salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. salmon
2 TBSP lemon pepper
1\2 cup olive oil

Directions:
Mix olive oil and lemon pepper in small bowl. Put salmon in a sealable container. Top with lemon pepper sauce. Cover, refrigerate at least 2 hours. Preheat oven to 350. Put salmon and sauce in casserole dish. Cover with aluminum foil. Bake for 25 min

Submitter:

Description: 
All you need is olive oil and lemon pepper to make an unforgetable juicy salmon
