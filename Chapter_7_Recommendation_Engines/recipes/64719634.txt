Title:
Butterscotch Carmel Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6295884.jpg
Ingredients:
24 Frozen Dinner Rolls
1 Small Package Butterscotch Pudding, not instant
1/2 C Packed Brown Sugar
1 t Cinnamon
1 Stick Butter

Directions:
Place frozen rolls in pan.  Mix dry ingredients and sprinkle over top of rolls.  Cut butter in small pieces over top and cover.  Leave overnight.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes.
Serve hot.
