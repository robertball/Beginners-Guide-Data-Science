Title:
Easy Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked rice
1 small onion, chopped (about 1/4 cup)
1 lb. ground beef
16 oz. can tomato sauce
8 oz. can sliced mushrooms, drained
1 tsp. celery salt
1 tsp. oregano
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook rice in a medium saucepan according to package instructions.
While the rice is cooking, cook ground beef and onion in a large skillet over medium heat until beef is no longer pink; drain if necessary.
Stir in tomato sauce, mushrooms, celery salt, oregano, salt and pepper.  Bring to a boil.
Stir in cooked rice.
Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
scarlett
Description: 
This was a favorite of ours when we were kids.  My brother and I would use our forks make roads through the rice while we ate.
