Title:
Lemon Lush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup margarine
1-1/2 cups flour
1 Cup chopped walnuts
8 Oz. Cream Cheese
1 Cup confectioners sugar
12 oz of Cool Whip
2 small pkgs Instant Lemon Pudding mix
2 cups of COLD milk

Directions:
CRUST:
Blend margarine and flour well. Mix in one cup of chopped walnuts (optional). Spread into a buttered 9"x13" pan.  BAKE at 400 degrees for 15 -20 minutes until crust turns a light brown. If your oven runs hot, reduce heat to 375 degrees.  COOL THOROUGHLY BEFORE PREPARING NEXT LAYER.
FIRST LAYER:
Beat the cream cheese with confectioners sugar until smooth. Fold in 1-3/4 cups of Cool Whip. Spread over the cooled crust.
SECOND LAYER:
Combine 2 small packages of instant lemon pudding with 3 cupes of cold milk. Beat until smooth and spread over the first layer.
THIRD LAYER:
Spread the remaining Cool Whip over the lemon mixture.
REFRIGERATE for at least several hours. Can be prepared the day before needed.

Submitter: 
Sandra G
Description: 
A refreshing light lemony dessert - Great on a hot summer day.  A favorite dessert at family gatherings.
