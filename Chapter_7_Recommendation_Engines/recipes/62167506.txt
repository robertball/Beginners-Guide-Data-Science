Title:
STEVES CUCCUMBER SOUP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 seedless cucumbers - peeled and grated
2 tablespoon fresh lime juice
zest of 1 lime
1 bunch fresh mint leaves, chopped
1 bunch fresh dill, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 cups plain yogurt
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 teaspoon salt
Sour cream

Directions:
Combine the grated cucumber, lime juice, zest, mint, dill, garlic, yogurt, olive oil, and salt in a large mixing bowl
Stir with a large spoon.
Pour the mixture into a blender; blend on high speed until smooth.
Chill in refrigerator until ready to serve.
Divide the soup between four bowls
Top each serving with about 1 tablespoon sour cream
