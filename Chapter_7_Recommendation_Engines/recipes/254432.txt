Title:
Chicken and White Cheddar Queso Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4167346.jpg
Ingredients:
3 (14.5 ounce) cans great Northern beans, drained and rinsed
4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
1 1/2 cups chopped cooked chicken breast
3/4 cup salsa verde
1 (5.6 ounce) package rice mix with white Cheddar sauce (such as Knorr® Rice Sides™)
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Combine beans, chicken broth, chicken breast, salsa verde, rice mix, chili powder, cumin, and garlic powder in a large saucepan over medium heat. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer, covered, until rice is tender, about 7 minutes. Stir and remove from heat. Let stand before serving, about 2 minutes.

Submitter: 
Bibi
Description: 
Take some everyday pantry items and just a few minutes to serve your family a warm, comforting bowl of chicken chili! Suggested garnishes: fresh cilantro, chopped scallions, tortilla chips. This would be very good with a pan of cornbread!
