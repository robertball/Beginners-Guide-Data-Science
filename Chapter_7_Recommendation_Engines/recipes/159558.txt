Title:
Peppermint Patty Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/871380.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ice cubes
2 fluid ounces coffee flavored liqueur
1 fluid ounce peppermint schnapps

Directions:
Place the ice cubes into a chilled rocks glass, and pour the coffee liqueur and schnapps overtop. Bottoms up!

Submitter: 
Dr.Owen L Falk
Description: 
This makes a great dessert or an after dinner drink.
