Title:
Chicken Stuffed Crescents with Chicken Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pkg crescent rolls
3 c. cooked (cubed or shredded) chicken
2 c. shredded cheddar cheese
6 oz pkg cream cheese, softened
1/2 c. finely chopped celery
1/2 c. finely chopped onions
1 tsp. seasoned salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/4 c. melted butter
1 c. croutons, ground up

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Combine all ingredients except the rolls, butter, and croutons. Lay out the crescents and put approximately 1 tbsp of the mixture on the fullest part of the crescent. Fold and pinch together the edges.
Place on baking sheet. Baste with melted butter and sprinkle on ground croutons.
Bake at 375 for 15-20 minutes. Serve with chicken gravy.
Chicken Gravy:
4 Tbsp flour
4 Tbsp butter
1 c. chicken broth
1 c. milk
Mix the flour and butter. Gradually add the liquid to keep it smooth. Cook over medium until thickened.
