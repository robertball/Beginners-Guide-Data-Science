Title:
Stuffed Baked Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 medium baking potatoes
1/4 cup butter (or margarine); softened
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese; divided
3/4 cup sour cream
1 envelope ranch salad dressing mix
1 tablespoon chives; snipped
1 clove garlic; minced
cooked bacon; crumbled
green onions; chopped

Directions:
Bake potatoes at 400 for 1 hour or until tender.
Reduce heat to 375.
Cut each potato in half lengthwise; scoop out the pulp, leaving a thin shell.
In a large mixing bowl, beat pulp with butter. Stir in 1 cup cheddar cheese, sour cream, salad dressing mix, chives and garlic.  Spoon into potato shells. Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
Place on baking sheet. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until heated through.
Top with bacon and green onions.
