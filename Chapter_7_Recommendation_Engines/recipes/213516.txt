Title:
Vegetable Tortilla Triangles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987736.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell's® Healthy Request® Condensed Cream of Celery Soup
1 medium tomato, chopped
1 small green pepper, chopped
2 green onions, sliced
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and finely chopped
8 (8 inch) flour tortillas
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Mix soup, tomato, green pepper, onions and jalapeno pepper.
Place tortillas on 2 baking sheets. Spread about l/3 cup soup mixture on each tortilla to within 1/2-inch of edge. Top with cheese.
Bake 10 min. or until tortillas are crisp. Cut each into quarters. Makes 32 appetizers.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Jalapeno, green pepper, tomato and green onion are stirred into a creamy sauce to fill tortillas. Baked crisp and cut into wedges, these veggie-filled quesadilla bites are perfect for a snack or a quick meal.
