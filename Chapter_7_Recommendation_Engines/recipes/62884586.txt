Title:
Rhubarb Crunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup sifted flour
3/4 cup rolled oats
1/2 cup melted margarine
1 tsp.cinnamon
4 cups diced rhubarb
1 cup sugar
2 Tbsp.cornstarch
1 cup water 1 tsp.vanilla

Directions:
Thoroughly mix together brown sugar,flour,oats,melted margarine and cinnamon.
Press half of mixture into a 9x9  greased pan.
Cover with the rhubarb.
Combine sugar and cornstarch,stir in water,cook till clear,add vanilla.
Pour over rhubarb.
Top with remaining brown sugar mixture.
Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
