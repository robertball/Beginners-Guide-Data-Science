Title:
Momma's Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2613081.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. Ground chuck
1 diced onion
2 tsp. Garlic powder
1 1/2 tsp. Italian seasoning
2 tsp.salt
2 tsp. Black pepper
2 eggs
2 c. Cottage cheese
1 c. Parmesan cheese
1 1/2  c. Shredded Colby Jack
6 lasagna noodles
1- 8 oz.package of shredded pizza cheese
1- 8 oz. package of shredded casserole cheese
1- 8 oz. package of shredded colby jack cheese
1- jar prego spaghetti with mushrooms

Directions:
Meat Layer:
1 1/2 lbs. Ground chuck
1 diced onion
2 tsp. Garlic powder
1 1/2 tsp. Italian seasoning
2 tsp.salt
2 tsp. Black pepper
Cheese Layer:
2 eggs
2 c. Cottage cheese
1 c. Parmesan cheese
1 1/2  c. Shredded Colby Jack
Additional ingredients include:
6 lasagna noodles
1- 8 oz.package of shredded pizza cheese
1- 8 oz. package of shredded casserole cheese
1- 8 oz. package of shredded colby jack cheese
1- jar prego spaghetti with mushrooms
Prepare skillet with ground chuck,diced onion,garlic powder,Italian seasoning,salt,and pepper.Brown meat and drain grease.Add spaghetti sauce and set aside.
Boil and cook lasagna noodles.While noodles are boiling prepare cheese layer.
Whisk two eggs,add cottage cheese,parmesan cheese,and colby jack cheese.
Preheat oven to 375 F
After noodles finished cooking through run beneath cold water.
Grease 8x10 baking pan
Begin layers:
Meat
all shredded cheeses mixed
Noodles
Cheese Layer
All shredded cheeses mixed
Repeat all layers until meat and cheese are gone.
Cover baking pan with aluminum foil and bake for 20 mins.
After 20 mins uncover baking pan and bake for another 15 mins or until cheese is lightly browned on top.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a very filling lasagna recipe that I re-created since my mother's passing years later. My favorite dish that she seldom made but was always my favorite!! Add Italian sausage to this as well and it's magnificent!
