Title:
Citrus Chicken Stir-fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980338.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package TysonÂ® Frozen, Fully Cooked Breaded Chicken Tenderloins
1 tablespoon cooking oil
1 (16 ounce) package frozen stir-fry vegetables
1/2 cup stir-fry sauce
1/2 cup orange juice
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 cups hot cooked rice
1/4 cup sliced almonds

Directions:
PREP: CLEAN: Wash hands. Prepare chicken according to package directions. Cut tenders in half crosswise.
COOK: In a large skillet heat oil to medium-high; add vegetables and stir-fry 5 minutes. Add stir-fry sauce, orange juice and brown sugar. Continue cooking over medium heat 3 minutes. Toss cooked chicken with the vegetable mixture.
SERVE: Serve over rice. Top with almonds.
CHILL: Refrigerate leftovers immediately.

Submitter: 
Tyson Foods
Description: 
A tangy, orange citrus stir-fry made with boneless skinless chicken tenderloins and your favorite stir fry vegetables.
