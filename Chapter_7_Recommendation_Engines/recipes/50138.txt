Title:
French Vanilla Mocha Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986134.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup hot water
1 tablespoon TASTER'S CHOICE® 100% Pure Instant Coffee
1/4 cup French Vanilla NESTLE® CARNATION® COFFEE-MATE® Liquid Coffee Creamer
1 tablespoon Chocolate Flavor NESTLE® NESQUIK™ Syrup
Whipped cream (optional)

Directions:
COMBINE water and Taster's Choice in large mug; stir until coffee is dissolved. Stir in Coffee-mate and Nestle Nesquik. Top with whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Nestle® Carnation® Coffee-mate®
Description: 
An elegant and rich hot coffee beverage made with both chocolate and French vanilla.
