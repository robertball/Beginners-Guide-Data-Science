Title:
MauiGirl's Classic Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/664677.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
1 cup mayonnaise
2 1/2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/2 teaspoon celery seed
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 bunch green onions, sliced
2 stalks celery, chopped
1/4 cup sweet pickle relish
1/4 cup grated carrot (optional)
1 lobster, steamed, cleaned and chop the meat
Reserve the body and claws for decorating the salad

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add the macaroni, and cook until tender, about 8 minutes. Rinse under cold water and drain.
In a large bowl, mix together the mayonnaise,  mustard, celery seed,salt and pepper. Stir in the onion, celery,  carrot, lobster and macaroni. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving, but preferably overnight.
