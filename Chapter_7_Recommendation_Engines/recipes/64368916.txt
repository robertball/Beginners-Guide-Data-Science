Title:
Basic Microwave Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons butter
1 clove garlic, minced
1 onion, chopped
1 pint mushrooms
1 1/2 cups vegetable broth
1 cup uncooked Arborio rice
1/2 cup white wine
1/2 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Saute onion, garlic and mushrooms in butter until tender.  Add the vegetable broth and heat thoroughly, then add the rice.  Move it to a 3 quart, microwave safe casserole dish. Cover the dish tightly and cook on high for 6 minutes.
Stir wine and cream into the rice. Cover and cook on high for 10 minutes more. Most of the liquid should boil off. Stir the cheese into the rice. Add more cream if needed and serve.

Submitter: 
Hudson's Mama
