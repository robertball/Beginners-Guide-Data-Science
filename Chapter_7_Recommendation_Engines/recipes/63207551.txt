Title:
Summer Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 fresh ears of sweet white corn
1/2 red bell pepper chopped
8 ounces canned white beans, drained & washed
3 leaves of fresh spinach chopped
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1 Roma tomato chopped
1 tablespoon Gorgonzola cheese
1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 teaspoons white vinegar
1 teaspoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
salt & pepper to taste
1 fresh avocado chopped (optional)

Directions:
Husk & remove silk from corn.  Boil for 6 minutes. Char the outside on the bbq. Allow to cool.  Cut the kernels from the cob, into a bowl. Add the next 7 ingredients and stir.
In a small bowl mix the: olive oil, vinegar, lemon juice, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Pour this mixture over the bowl of veggies & stir.
Cover and refrigerate for at least an hour.
Add chopped fresh avocado before serving, if desired.

Submitter: 
peasy
Description: 
An abundance of fresh garden veggies inspired this one. My husband deemed it summer in a bowl.
