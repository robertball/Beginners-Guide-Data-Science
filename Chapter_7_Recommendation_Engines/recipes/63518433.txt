Title:
Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 LB Sirloin steak cubed
1 LB lean ground beef
1 Onion chopped
3 Cloves Garlic
1 14 oz can diced tomatoes
1 can Rotel Tomatoes with mild chilies
1 can chili beans (hot)
1 can chili benns (mild)
1-2 TBS chili powder (more if desired)
1/4 TSP red pepper flakes (or more)
1 TBS taco seasoning
salt and petter to taste

Directions:
Heat crock pot on low
Cut sirloin into bite size pieces,sear over medium heat and put into crock pot to keep warm
Brown ground beef, onion and garlic
Drain any fat and put into crock pot
Add beans, tomatoes and seasoning
Cook on low for 8 hours or high for 4 hours

An easy to make meaty chili that will be my go-to chili recipie from now on.
