Title:
Yogurt Soup with Apricots & Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups water
1/2 cup of dried apricots chopped
2 yellow onions
1/2 cup yellow split peas
1/2 lb spinach
10 green onions chopped
1 tsp salt
Black pepper to taste
1 cup uncooked white rice
2 cups yogurt
1 Tbsp olive oil
2 Tbsp fresh mint finely chopped

Directions:
Place the water, onions, split peas, spinach, green onions, salt and pepper in a sauce pan.
Bring to boil, cover and simmer for 40 minutes.
Stir in the rice and cook over moderate heat for 20 - 25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add the chopped apricots.
Add the yogurt.
Stir 1 minute without boiling and remove from heat.
Heat oil in a frying pan, add the mint and stir for 1 minute.
Add to soup and allow to reach room temperature.
Chill for 1 - 2 hours in refrigerator and serve cold.
