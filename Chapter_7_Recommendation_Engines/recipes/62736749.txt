Title:
Pride o'Gloucester Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# white fish fillets
3/4 c bread crumbs
1/4 c grated Parmesn/Romano cheese
1 lg clove garlic, minced
1/4 tsp pepper
1 tsp salt
2 tblsp chopped parsley
2 beaten eggs
2 c onions, sliced
2 c green pepper, sliced
2 c stewed tomatoes
3/4 c oil
Old Bay (~1 tsp)
Paprika
8 oz Montery Jack, shredded

Directions:
Fits an 8-in square casserole
Heat oven to 350 F
1)  Mix crumbs, cheese, garlic, pepper, salt, parsley, Old Bay in a bowl
2)  Dip fish in the egg, then crumbs
3)  Sprinkle with paprika and brown in the oil; remove fish and place in baking dish
4)  In the oil, saute onions + peppers; drain oil and add tomatoes.  Saute until vegies are tender.  Pour over fish.
5)  Bake covered at 350 for 30 min.  Sprinkle with Parmesan/Romano
6)  Sprinkle cheese over fish and cook additional ~8 min.
