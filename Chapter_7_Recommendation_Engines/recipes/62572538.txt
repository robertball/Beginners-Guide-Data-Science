Title:
Rick's Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup apple juice
1/2 cup rapunzel sugar
1/2 cup butter
6 to 10 apples - peeled, cored and sliced
1/2 cup rapunzel sugar
2 heaping tablespoons cornstarch
2 (9 inch)  unbaked pie crusts
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1 heaping teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Combine the apple juice, 1/2 cup white sugar and the butter in a sauce pan and heat until melted. Add the apples and cook until fruit is tender.
Combine 1/2 cup of the sugar with the cornstarch, nutmeg & cinnamon. Stir into the fruit mixture, then cook until thickened. Cool then pour into the pie shell. Cover with the top crust and seal.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) until golden brown & apples bubbling a little [about 35-45-minutes]

Submitter: 
RICKMCCJ
Description: 
You get twice the apple goodness with this recipe. Golden Delicious  apples are sliced and cooked in a pan with apple juice, butter and sugar until tender. A bit of cornstarch is added to thicken it. Then the filling is poured into the bottom crust, covered with the top crust, and baked until golden brown.Modified from: Apple Pie II
