Title:
Hot Corn Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded cheddar
1 cup shredded Monterey jack
2 T chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, diced(the more you add the spicier it will be so adjust to your liking)
1 4 oz can diced green chiles
1/2 cup mayo
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1 11oz can corn, drained
1 small tomato, diced
2 T cilantro, chopped
2 T green onions, sliced thin

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix the cheeses, chipotle peppers, green chiles, mayo, garlic powder, and corn together. Pour into a glass dish (8x8).
Bake for 20 minutes or until bubbly.
Top with diced tomatoes, green onions, and cilantro.
Serve immediately with tortilla chips.
