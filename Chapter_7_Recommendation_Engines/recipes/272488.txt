Title:
14 Hands Frosé Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6531141.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (750 milliliter) bottle 14 Hands Rosé
2 tablespoons grenadine
4 cups ice
4 cups frozen strawberries
1 lemon, juiced
1/8 cup agave nectar, or to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender; blend to desired consistency.
Pour into glasses and enjoy, or set in the freezer for 1 hour to freeze for thicker consistency.
