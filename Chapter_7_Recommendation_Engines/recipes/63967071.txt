Title:
Barbecue Pork Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
see below

Directions:
2 lbs boneless pork top loin, or rib roast
1 1/2 cups onions, chopped
1 cup diet soda
1 cup barbecue sauce
1.  Place pork on slow cooker.  Combine all ingredients and pour over top of pork.
2. Cover. Cook on high for 4-6 hours or until meat is tender.
3.  Take pork out of slowe cooker.  Slice or shred pork. Put back into the crockpot with the sauce.
4.  Serve on buns
If you are using your Nesco to do this, you'll want to set the temp to 350 degrees. That is the equilivant of a crockpot temp.
This recipe makes 8 servings. If you want

Submitter: 
Dawn Yoder Holycross
Description: 
Awesome slow cooker barbecue pork sandwiches!  I've made this for several family gatherings and it's always a hit!!
