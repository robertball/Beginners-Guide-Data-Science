Title:
Lazy Cake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box yellow or white cake mix
2 eggs beaten
5T melted butter
2 cups M&M's or mini choc chips

Directions:
Mix all together, put in greased 9 X 13 pan and bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
