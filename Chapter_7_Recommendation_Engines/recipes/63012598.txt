Title:
Italian chicken soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
oil
2 whole chick breast, cooked and in chunks
1/2 cup onion
1/4 cup celery
2 cloves minced garlic
1 tsp dried basil
1/4 tsp pepper
1 cup water
1 can chik broth
1/3 cup vermecillie broked into 2"
2TB parm
1 cup frozen corn
2 cans tomatoes

Directions:
cook chicken, add onion, celery and agarlic.  cook and stir until crisp tender.
Stir in basil, pepper, water broth and tomatoes.  Brign to boi.  Reduce heat and cover and simmer 5 min.  stir in corn and verm.  Cover and simmer 10 min.
