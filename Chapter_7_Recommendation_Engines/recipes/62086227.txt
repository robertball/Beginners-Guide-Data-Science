Title:
Supermom's Southwestern Sweet Potato Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Topping:
2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled  		2 t sea salt
and cut into 2-inch chunks		1 ½ T butter (may use soy)
¼ c skim or soy milk			dash cayenne pepper
Filling:
1 ½ lbs lean ground turkey			1 t ground cumin
½ c chopped onion				1 T chili powder
1 can rinsed, drained black beans		¼ t cayenne
1 vine-ripe tomato, chopped			¼ t cinnamon
¼ c ketchup					2 t minced garlic
¼ t ground black pepper			add’l salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large saucepan, cover the potatoes with cold water.  Cook until very soft, about 10 minutes.  Drain well and return the potatoes to the saucepan.  Stir in 1-1/2 T Butter, the milk, the salt, and the cayenne.  Mash until smooth and taste for seasoning.  Set aside.
Preheat oven to 425.  In a large skillet, cook turkey until almost browned.  Add onion and garlic, cook until soft, about 3 minutes.  Add cumin, chili powder, cinnamon, and cayenne, cook nntil turkey is browned and drain any fat.  (Usually there isn’t any.)  Stir in black beans, tomato, and ketchup.  Taste, add salt or pepper to taste.  Cook 5 minutes longer and spread evenly in 2 qt casserole dish.  Spread the sweet potatoes on top and bake until the potatoes start to brown and the meat is bubbling underneath, about 20 to 25 minutes.

Description: 
A yummy sweet-and-spicy dish that's a twist on  a favorite comfort food.  Made even more healthy with ground turkey!
