Title:
The Taco Omelete Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz of cooked ground beef
3 eggs,
3 slices sharp ched cheese
half cup of salsa
tablespoon of sour cream
1 tortilla wrap

Directions:
1. Cook down ground beef add half of salsa to ground beef mix set aside
2. scramble 3 eggs place on griddle or pan place ched cheese in the middle
3. Place beef mixture on top of the cheese
4. Fold omelete cook to your liking
5. Place cut( in 4s or 8ts) tortills wrap in a pre heated oven to toast to your liking
6. Plate omelete up, top w/remaining salsa and top with sour cream garnish with toast wedges   Enjoy!!!
