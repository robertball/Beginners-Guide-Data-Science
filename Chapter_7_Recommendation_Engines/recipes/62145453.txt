Title:
Cheesecake Truffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 CUP white chocolate chips
1 CUP butterscotch candy (crushed or chopped)
¾ CUP sifted powdered sugar
½ CUP sour cream
1 tsp grated lemon rind
¼ tsp salt
1 ¾ CUP vanilla wafers (crushed to make crumbs)
¾ CUP sliced almonds, chopped

Directions:
Melt chocolate over hot water in a double boiler.
Remove from heat.
Chop or crush Butterscotch to resemble small crumbs and pour into melted chocolate. Stir to combine.
Add the powdered sugar, sour cream, lemon peel, and salt, and stir to combine.
Blend in wafer crumbs. Chill 15-20 min or until firm.
Shape into balls using a teaspoon.
Roll balls in chopped almonds.
Store truffles in a tightly covered container in the refridgerator. Can be frozen.
