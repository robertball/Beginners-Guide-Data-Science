Title:
Pumpkin Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sugar
5 cups flour
1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
1 teaspoon cloves
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon allspice
4 teaspoons baking soda
3 1/2 cups pumpkin pie filling
1 cup oil

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well.  Add craisins and nuts if desired.  Put in 4 loaf pans.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes
