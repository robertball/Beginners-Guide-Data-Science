Title:
Meatloaf with a Twist Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs lean ground beef
5 oz package garlic flavored croutons
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp dried thyme
2 tbsp brown sugar
1/2 cup ketchup
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
3/4 cup milk
1 egg
1 small onion, finely chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Pulse croutons in a food processor until they are crumbs.
Combine beef, onion, salt, chili powder, pepper, thyme, brown sugar, ketchup and worcestershire sauce.  Add the croutons slowly while mixing.
Beat the egg and mix with the milk, then add to the beef mixture.
Pack the beef mixture into a 10-inch loaf pan and bake at 350 degrees for 50-60 minutes or until the internal temperature registers at 155 degrees.
Remove from oven and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Zest to Impress
Description: 
This is a simple meatloaf recipe with a special substitution for bread crumbs.  The idea was integrated from an Alton Brown recipe.
