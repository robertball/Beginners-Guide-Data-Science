Title:
Bone Broth Protein Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 packet bone broth protein powder
1.5 c nut milk unsweetened
1 c water
1 egg
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp (or less) salt
1/4 tsp (or less) monk fruit

Directions:
combine all in vitamix
put in ice cream maker
turn on until ice cream forms
