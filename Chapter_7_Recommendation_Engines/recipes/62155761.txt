Title:
Greek Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8oz) pkg. cream cheese,softened
2 tbsp milk
1 tsp dried oregano leaves
1/4 tsp. pepper
1(8oz)container hummus
3/4 cup chopped cucumber
1 plum tomato, chopped
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
1/4 cup  sliced ripe olives
2 tbsp sliced green onion
Purchased assorted cracker

Directions:
1.In a small bowl stir together cream cheese,milk,oregano, and pepper.2.Spread cream cheese mixture on a 10 inch serving plate.Gently spread hummis on top.Sprinkle with cucumber,tomato,feta cheese,olives,and green onions.3.Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour.Serve with crackers.
