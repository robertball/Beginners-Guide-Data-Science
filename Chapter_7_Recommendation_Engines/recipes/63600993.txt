Title:
Whole Grain Chocolate Chip Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1001826.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup margarine
1-1/2 cups brown sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
1 cup white flour
1cup whole wheat flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 cup oats
1-1/4 cups whole grain cereal (ie. Red River Cereal or St. John Valley Cereal )
1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cream together margarine and brown sugar.  Add eggs and vanilla and mix well.
In a separate bowl, stir together flours, baking soda, salt, oats and cereal.
Add dry to wet and stir until blended.
Stir in chocolate chips.
Drop by tablespoon onto cookie sheet.
Bake for 10  - 12 minutes.
