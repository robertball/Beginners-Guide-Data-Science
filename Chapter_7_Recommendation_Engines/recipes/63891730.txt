Title:
Cuisinart Avocado Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ to ¾ cup coconut water, chilled
¼ cup coconut milk from a can (not lite), chilled
1 tablespoon honey, preferably local
¾ teaspoon fresh lime juice
¼ of a medium ripe avocado, cut into chunks
½ of a medium banana, cut into chunks

Directions:
Put all ingredients into blender, in order listed. Blend until smooth. If a thinner
consistency is desired, add more coconut water and blend until fully combined. Serve
immediately.
