Title:
Jim C's Favorite Pimento Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/680915.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces extra sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
8 ounces processed cheese food (such as Velveeta®), cubed
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 teaspoon hot pepper sauce (such as Tabasco®)
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 (4 ounce) jar pimentos, drained
1/4 cup chopped pecans
1 red chile pepper, seeded and chopped

Directions:
Place the Cheddar cheese, processed cheese, mayonnaise, pepper sauce, onion, Worcestershire sauce, pimentos, pecans, and red chile pepper in a food processor. Blend until smooth. Chill overnight for flavors to blend.

Submitter: 
Sydney Young
Description: 
This recipe has just the right amount of spice, and just the right amount of creamy.
