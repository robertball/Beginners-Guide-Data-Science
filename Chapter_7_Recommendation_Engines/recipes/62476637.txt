Title:
Supermoist Cornbread Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked - Yellow Squash (steamed and pureed)
1 pkg - Cornbread mix (bake according to package directions)
1/2 stick - Butter
1 can (10.5 oz) - Cream of Chicken soup
1 - Onion (chopped)
3 - Celery stalks (sliced)
1T - Ground Sage
1 - Egg (beaten)
Salt and Pepper to taste
Pecan halves (optional)

Directions:
Bake Cornbread according to package directions. Cool and crumble.
Preheat oven to 350.
Slice and steam squash until soft, then puree.
On stove top melt butter over medium heat in a 2 quart casserole dish. Saute onions and celery until onions are clear. Add cream of chicken soup.  Add crumbled cornbread. Season to taste. Add beaten egg and fold into mixture. Use casserole dish (45 minutes) or pour into "13 x 9" baking dish (35 minutes). To make a great presentation and added flavor place pecan halves on each serving.  Bake at 350 until knife comes out of center clean.

Submitter: 
Cindy
Description: 
Perfect everytime. This is a recipe passed down the generations. Plus, it's a perfect way to get your kids to eat veggies, because squash is the secret to a moist dressing.
