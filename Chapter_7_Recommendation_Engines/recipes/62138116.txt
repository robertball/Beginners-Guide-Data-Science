Title:
Vegan Chocolate Chip Oatmeal Nut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup peanut butter
2 tablespoons canola oil
1 cup sugar
1/3 cup soymilk
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup rolled oats
1/2 cup vegan semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Oil a large baking sheet; set aside.
Whisk together peanut butter, canola oil, sugar, soymilk and vanilla extract until very smooth.
Combine flour, oats, soda and salt separately.
Add dry ingredients to the mixture with chocolate chips and nuts.
Stir to combine.
Drop batter by large spoonfuls onto prepared baking sheet.
Bake for about 8 minutes.
Remove and let cool for 10 minutes before moving cookies.
