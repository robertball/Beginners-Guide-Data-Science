Title:
Cider Turkey with Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. skinless turkey, cut into 1-inch cubes
2 tablespoons no-salt chicken broth
4 cups mushrooms, sliced
1/4 cup red peppers, diced
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1/8 cup chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Cook turkey in broth in skillet until cooked through.  Add veggies until soft.  Garnish with parsley.
