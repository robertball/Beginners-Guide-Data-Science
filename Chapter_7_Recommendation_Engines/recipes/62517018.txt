Title:
Holiday Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/510332.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag potato bread cubes
1 box Chicken Stock
1 Stick of Margerine or Butter
1 egg
sauted Onion, Celery, Apple, Cranberry, and Pear
Pepper, Sage and Rosemary to taste

Directions:
1.Prepare Stuffing according to package directions
2.Add Egg and Sauted Vegetables and Fruit
3.Season with Rosemary, Sage and Pepper
4.Bake in Baking dish for 25 to 30 mins
