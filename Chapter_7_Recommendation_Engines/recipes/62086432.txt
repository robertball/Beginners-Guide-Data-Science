Title:
Homestyle Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/292327.jpg
Ingredients:
5 CUPS SKIM MILK
6 TABLESPOONS BUTTER OR MARGERINE
6 EGGS
1 CUP GRANULATED SUGAR
1/2 CUP BROWN SUGAR
1/2 TSP. SALT
1 TSP. CINNAMON
1 TABLESPOON VANILLA EXTRACT
12 CUPS SOFT BREAD CUBES

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Add butter to milk and scald.  Let cool down 10 minutes or more.  In mixing bowl, beat eggs; add sugars, salt, cinnamon and vanilla.  Mix well.  Stir into milk mixture.  Then add the bread cubes, stir to mix.
Spray 9"X13" pan with non-stick vegetable spray. Pour bread mixture into pan.  Cover with foil and seal edges.  Place into a larger pan of hot water (1 inch deep). Bake at 350 degrees for 50-55 minutes, or until toothpick comes out clean.
Can be served either warm or cold.  Top with desired pie filling prior to serving.
Optional idea:  Stir in 1 cup of raisins to mixture prior to baking.

A custard based bread pudding that is moist and delicious and very easy to make.
