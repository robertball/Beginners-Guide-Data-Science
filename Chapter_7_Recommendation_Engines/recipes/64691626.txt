Title:
Gluten -Lactose Free  Vegan Pumpkin Oatmeal Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5144026.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Robin Hood Gluten Free flour
1 cup white sugar
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
1 and 1/2 teaspoons gluten free baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon  cinnamon
1 teaspoon nutmeg
2 and 1/2 cups pumpkin purée
1 cup lactose free milk ( I use Natrel)
4 eggs beaten
1 /2 cup maple syrup
1 and 1/2 cups gluten free oats ( Bob Mills)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375°
Line a 12 cup muffin pan with liners or spray with cooking spray
In a bowl or a KitchenAid mixer add all the dry ingredients and mix until thoroughly combined.
Add the pumpkin purée, milk, eggs,and maple syrup in to the dry ingredients
Stir until the batter is smooth, then fold in the oats.
Add batter into the lined or sprayed muffin cups.
Bake in preheated oven 25- 30 min. or until a toothpick comes out clean.
These are good hot, With Earth Balance  or peanut butter...

Description: 
A great muffin that is good out of the oven spread with Earth balance or cold with peanut butter;
Great for breakfast or as a snack with a hot drink.
