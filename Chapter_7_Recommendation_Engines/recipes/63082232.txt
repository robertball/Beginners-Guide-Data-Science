Title:
Pasta Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/778976.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb bulk sausage
2 tsp minced garlic
1/2 TBS extra virgin olive oil
2  14.5 oz cans fire-roasted or spicy red pepper diced tomatoes
1 can navy or great northern beans, drained
3 ½ cups beef stock
3 ½ cups chicken stock
1 tsp. basil
1 tsp. oregano
red pepper flakes-to taste
1 ½ cups frozen chopped spinach
1 cup uncooked Ditalini or other small pasta like orzo

Directions:
Brown sausage with garlic and olive oil in stock pot. Drain grease, if needed.
Add tomatoes, beans, basil, oregano and red pepper flakes to taste.
Add beef and chicken stock. Bring to a boil.
Add pasta and spinach. Continue to simmer until pasta is cooked.
Sprinkle each serving with Parmesan cheese and serve with a hearty bread.
