Title:
On Golden Prawns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp olive oil
1/2 cup white onion, chopped
1/2 cup red pepper, chopped
1 tsp garlic, minced
1 tsp cumin, ground
3/4 tsp coriander
1/2 tsp curry
1 cup coconut milk
1 tsp sugar, granulated
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes, crushed
1 lb shrimp, jumbo uncooked peeled, deveined and tails removed
1 tbsp corn starch
2 tbsp cilantro, fresh minced
4 cups hot cooked brown rice

Directions:
Heat oil in deep 10" skillet over medium-high heat.
Add onions, red pepper and garlic.
Cook and stir until vegetables begin to soften, about 3 minutes.
Add Cumin, coriander and curry powder.
Cook for 1 minute.
Add coconut milk,sugar and crushed red pepper flakes.
Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered for 2 minutes
Stir in shrimp.
Increase heat to medium-high.
In a small bowl, combine cornstarch with 1 tbsp water.
Add to shrimp mixture.
Cook until sauce is bubbly and has thickened, about 1 minute.
Stir in cilantro and remove from heat.
Serve shrimp and sauce over hot rice.
