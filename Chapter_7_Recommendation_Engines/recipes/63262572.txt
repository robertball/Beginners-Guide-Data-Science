Title:
Rhonda's Pierogie Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12oz Bowtie Pasta (cooked and drained accordingly)
13.7oz Idaho instant potatoes(6 servings)
5 cups water
2 cups milk
8oz velveeta cheese (cut in chunks)
1 stick butter divided in half (1/4 cup each)
3 medium onions chopped
8oz cheddar cheese shredded

Directions:
Melt 1/4 cup butter and add to the already prepared pasta
Place the other 1/4 cup of butter in large skillet and saute the choppped onions
Bring the water to boil and add the velveta chunks ans stir till it melts.
Stir in the potato flakes and then the milk.
Place 1/2 of the noodles and butter in bottom of your casserole.
Sprinkle with 1/2 of the sauted onions.
Spread 1/2 of the potato  mixture over the onion layer.
Repeat this layering one more time then top with the shredded cheddar cheese.
Bake at 350 deg. for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
I have made this recipe for years. It's a favorite at family get togethers. Perfect comfort food! 9x13 pan baked at 350 deg for one hour.
