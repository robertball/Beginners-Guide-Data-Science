Title:
Passover Matzo Kugel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6040750.jpg
Ingredients:
1 matzo per person (12 matzos)
1/2 lb butter per 12 matzos
6 eggs
1 cup sugar
4 apples peeled and chopped
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Soak Matzo in hot water til soapy looking.
squeeze out the water.
mix together the eggs, sugar, and melted butter. Pour into matzo mixture and add the apples and nuts. Put into a 9x13 pan. Bake at 350 for 45mins
