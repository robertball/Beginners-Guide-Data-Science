Title:
Teriyaki Sauce Baste, Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2304444.jpg
Ingredients:
Sauce:
1/4	cup Low sodium soy sauce
1/2	cup water
2 	tablespoons sweet rice wine
1 	tablespoon, plus 2 teaspoons brown sugar
1 ½	teaspoons minced garlic
1 ½	teaspoons minced ginger
Glaze:
2	teaspoons cornstarch
1 	tablespoon water

Directions:
1.	Combine ingredients in a saucepan on medium heat until the sugar is dissolved.
2.	To make glaze: Mix cornstarch with water set aside. Heat sauce on medium high heat until it starts to bubble, whisk in cornstarch water mixture. Simmer until thickened. about 1 to 2 minutes

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
Wonderful Sauce that you can control the sugar and salt in.  I wanted to get a way from bottled Teriyaki sauce so this one came about.
