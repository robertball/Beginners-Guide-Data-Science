Title:
Momma's Fruit Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 big cans Hawaiian Fruit Punch (about 46 oz. EACH)
1 big can (46 oz.) pineapple juice
1 12 oz. can frozen orange juice, mixed as directed on can (pulp-free)
1 1/4 c. sugar (or to taste) for EACH gallon

Directions:
Makes 2 gallons.  For each gallon, mix 46 oz. Hawaiian Punch, 1/2 of the orange juice (after mixing as directed on can), 1/2 of the pineapple juice and 1 1/4 c. sugar per gallon.  Mix well with water to make 2 full gallons.  Best if chilled overnight.

Submitter:

Description: 
A simple and delicious fruit punch that's always a big hit.
