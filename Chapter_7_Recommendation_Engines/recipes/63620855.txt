Title:
Chicken and Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breast fillets, 1 can cream of chicken soup, 1 can cream of celery soup, 1 can cream of mushroom soup, 2 cups instant white rice, 2 cups water, 1 stick butter or margarine (whichever you prefer), salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Grease sides and bottom of a casserole dish. Combine the 3 soups, water, and rice and stir together. Cut the chicken breast in to squares and mix in with the rest of the ingredients. Slice the stick of butter in to pats and place spread out all over the top. Bake for 1 hour- 1 hour 15 mins. Let sit for 15 minutes or so then serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
I grew up on this recipe, my Grandmother got it from a lady from church at a pot luck a long time ago! She passed it down to my mom and it's always been a family favorite. Quick and easy for school nights and the leftovers are just as good!
