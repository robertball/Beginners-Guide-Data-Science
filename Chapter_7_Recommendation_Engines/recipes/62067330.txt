Title:
World's Best Vegan Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pancake batter:
4 cups self-raising flour
2 1/2 cups soy milk
1 tbsp sugar
1 tbsp custard powder
Makes about 12 pancakes
Optional:
You can add your favorite fruits like bananas, blueberry or raisins etc. You can also add vanilla flavouring or cinnamon powder if you like.

Directions:
1.Mix pancake batter ingredients in a big bowl and whisk until there are no lumps.
2.Pour batter onto a lightly greased griddle and cook until bubbles form then flip over and cook until other side is browned.
3.Serve with jam or syrup.
World's best vegan pancakes!

Submitter: 
Timothy Tang
Description: 
This vegan pancake recipe doesn't use any milk nor eggs. The secret to it looking and tasting so similar to regular non-vegan pancakes is the use of custard powder. Also, it is cakelike and non soggy like other vegan pancakes.
