Title:
Parboiled & Wild Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups water
1 cup rice mix (parboiled long grain rice, wild rice)
1 tbsp. butter
1 tsp. salt

Directions:
Bring water to a boil.
Add remaining ingredients.
Cover saucepan tightly and cook over low heat until all the water is absorbed, about 25 minutes.
