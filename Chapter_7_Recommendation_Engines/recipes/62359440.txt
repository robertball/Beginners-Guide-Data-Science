Title:
Pea, Crab & Bacon Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups uncooked medium pasta shells or pasta of your choice
2 1/2 cups fresh baby spinach
1 (10 ounce) package frozen peas, thawed
1 1/2 cups imitation crabmeat
4-6 slices of crumbled bacon
1/2 cup diced red or other sweet onion
1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
1/2 cup or to taste Italian salad dressing or
1 cup of mayonaise

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a large bowl, combine the spinach, peas and crab. Drain pasta and rinse in cold water. Stir into spinach mixture. Sprinkle with feta cheese. Drizzle with dressing and toss to coat.

Submitter:
