Title:
Easy Italian Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Leftover roast beef
1-2 packs of italian dressing depending on how much beef you have
Beef broth

Directions:
Shread up your leftover beef and put in pot.  Add italian dressing and broth.  Heat until warm and serve.

Description: 
Quick and easy italian beef using leftover roast beef.  My hubby loves it.
