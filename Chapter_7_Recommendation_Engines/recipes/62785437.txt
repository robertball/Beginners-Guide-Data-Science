Title:
Brussels Sprouts Pan Fried with Bacon and Morel mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved lengthwise
2 tablespoons olive oil
sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
4 slices bacon, chopped
1 shallot, chopped
7 Morel mushrooms, cut in half, or quartered
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup cooking sherry

Directions:
Steam Brussels sprouts for 6 minuets, drain and place on paper towel. Cook the bacon in a large skillet over medium-high heat until crisp; drain on paper towels and crumble.
Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook garlic and onion until soft; stir in the bacon and cook until bacon is heated through. Add the Brussels sprouts and mushrooms; cook and stir until the sprouts are browned, 7 to 10 minutes.
Stir in the sherry, toss to coat, and season to taste with salt and black pepper.
