Title:
Apple and Buffalo Monterey Jack Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1365388.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
1 onion roll, split
1/4 apple, thinly sliced
1 slice Buffalo sauce-flavored Monterey jack cheese

Directions:
Spread mustard onto cut-sides of onion roll halves. Top one half with apple slices and cheese; sandwich with remaining half.

Submitter: 
Carrie Reed
Description: 
Super quick, healthy, and absolutely delicious. I use an Enterprise apple.
