Title:
Honey and Ginger Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large boneless skinless chicken breasts
2 T honey
1-2 t. Dijon mustard
4 T water
2-4 cloves garlic, peeled and crushed
2-3 t ground ginger, or 1 T grated fresh ginger
salt and pepper to taste
1/2-1 t cayenne pepper (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Spray lg. pan w/non-stick spray
Mix honey, mustard, water, ginger, crushed garlic (and cayenne pepper)
Put chicken in pan and pour sauce over, easing them up slightly so mixture runs underneath them
Season with salt and pepper
Bake 45-60 min.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Glazed skinless boneless chicken breasts coated with Dijon mustard, a little honey, and more or less garlic to suit your taste
