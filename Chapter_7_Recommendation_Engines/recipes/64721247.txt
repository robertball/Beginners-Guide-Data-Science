Title:
Chickpea and Root Vegetable Sheet Pan Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5934981.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked chickpeas (or 2 cans of chickpeas, rinsed and drained)
1/2 butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and cut into 1" cubes
1 onion, diced
1 sweet potato, peeled and cut into 1" pieces
2 large carrots, cut into 1" pieces
3 medium russet potatoes, cut into 1" cubes
3 tbsps vegetable oil
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp ground fennel seeds
1 tsp rubbed sage
chopped green onion (optional)

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a large sheet pan.
2. Place chickpeas, butternut squash, onion, sweet potato, carrots, and russet potatoes on the baking sheet. Sprinkle with the vegetable oil, and toss to coat.
3. Combine salt, black pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, ground fennel seed, and rubbed sage together. Sprinkle over vegetables on the baking sheet, and toss to coat.
4. Place baking sheet in preheated oven and bake for 25 minutes, stir, and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes or until veggies are soft and lightly browned and chickpeas are slightly crisp. Season with additional salt and black pepper to taste, and top with chopped green onion, if desired, before serving.

Submitter: 
Kim
Description: 
Adding chickpeas to root vegetables on a sheet pan makes for an easy one-pan supper! Make sure to cut all the vegetables the same size or they won't cook properly!
