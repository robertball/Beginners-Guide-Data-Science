Title:
Zuchinni Bread from Shirley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. baking powder
2 eggs, beaten
2 cups sugar
2 cubes margarine, at room temperature
3 tsp. cinnamon
3 tsp. vanilla
2 c. grated zuchinni (f using frozen, drain well and pat dry)
1/2 cup nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees; oil two bread pans and lightly dust with flour.
Combine dry ingredients in bowl.
Beat eggs and slowly add sugar, margarine (or butter), cinnamon, and vanilla until creamy (vigorously by hand or with hand mixer).
Alternately, mix in dry ingredients and zuchinni, add nuts if wanted.  Mixture will be fairly wet.
Divide mix between two prepared bread pans and bake at 350 degrees for approximately 1 hour.  Check for doneness, then cool at least 30 minutes before slicing.
To freeze, cool thoroughly, wrap tightly in foil or saran wrap, then freeze in a freezer bag.

Description: 
From Shirley Foreman, 10/10/73, in my red My Favorite Recipes notebook.  As always with Mom, ingredients but limited instructions on how to assemble.  This was pretty easy to figure out.
