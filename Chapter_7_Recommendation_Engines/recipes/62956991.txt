Title:
Black Bean Brownies by deb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15.5 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
3 eggs
1/2 c applesauce
1/4 c Greek yogurt
1/4 cup cocoa powder
1 pinch salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3/4 tsp stevia

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease an 8x8 square baking dish.
Blend the black beans, then add eggs, applesauce, cocoa powder, salt, vanilla extract, stevia  in a blender; blend until smooth; pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle the chocolate chips over the top of the mixture.
Bake in the preheated oven until the top is dry and the edges start to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 30 minutes.
Let cool completely
