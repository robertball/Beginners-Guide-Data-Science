Title:
Tomato Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup oil
2 cups chopped onion
1 tbsp. garlic powder
10 cups peeled tomatoes
13 oz can tomato paste
2 green peppers, chopped
2 tbsp. oregano
2 tbsp. basil
1 tbsp. salt
1 bay leaf (remove before canning)
2 tbsp. sugar
1/2 tbsp. pepper

Directions:
Boil first 6 ingredients together x 10 min.
Add remaining ingredients
Boil all for 1 hr or longer, stirring every 15 min., until sauce is a thick as preferred
Put into jars. Makes ~ 2 qt or more
