Title:
Buffalo Ranch Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts
1/2 cup buffalo sauce
1 packet ranch dressing mix
1 stick of butter
8oz cream cheese
1 1/4 cup milk
1 box short/fun pasta
Mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package instructions
Cube chicken and coat with 1/4 cup buffalo sauce and 1/2 of the ranch seasoning
Cook chicken thoroughly. Set aside
Combine butter, cream cheese, buffalo sauce, and ranch seasoning. Stir until all ingredients are melted/fully combined
Slowly add milk, whisking until sauce forms
Combine pasta, sauce and chicken
Serve topped with mozzarella cheese
