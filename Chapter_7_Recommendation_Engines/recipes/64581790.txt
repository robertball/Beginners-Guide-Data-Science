Title:
Crock Pot Steel Cut Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3657343.jpg
Ingredients:
Ingredients
1 cup milk
2 cups water
1 peeled and chopped fresh apple
1 cup uncooked steel-cut oats
2 tablespoons brown sugar (or substitute maple syrup or other desired sweetener)
1-1/2 tablespoons butter, cut into 5-6 pieces (optional)
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon salt
Optional garnishes: chopped nuts, raisins, maple syrup, additional milk or butter

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in crock pot. Turn on LOW for an hour (to get it started). Reduce heat to SIMMER or WARM for 7-8 hours. Serve topped with dried fruit or nuts - especially if you don't have an apple to add to the cooking process!

Delicious oatmeal dish that is hot and ready in the morning!
