Title:
Nana's Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
White Lilly Self rising Corn Meal
2 Eggs
Buttermilk
1/4 cup of Oil
Salt to taste

Directions:
Oven to 410 degrees
Place Oil in a baker
Place in oven to heat to extremely hot temp
Coat baker with oil
Pour hot oil into the meal mix
Mix well
Pour mix into baker
cook for 25 minutes until top is golden brown
