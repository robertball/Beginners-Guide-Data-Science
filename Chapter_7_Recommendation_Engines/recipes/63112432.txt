Title:
Dutch Oven Peach Pecan Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans (21 oz.) peach pie filling
1 white or yellow box cake mix
1 c. chopped pecans
1 stick butter or margerine
12 oz. bag toffee chips

Directions:
Place parchment liner in 12-inch Dutch oven.  Pour peaches into oven and level out.  Sprinkle dry cake mix over peaches, then sprinkle half the pecans over the cake mix.  Stir slightly.
Dot the top with butter or margerine, and sprinkle the remaining pecans and all toffee chips  over top.
Cover with lid and cook with medium coals on top and beneath for 30 - 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Melissa Burnett Mandrona
Description: 
Great scout or family camping recipe shared by a veteran Scout leader.  Use parchment dutch oven liners to speed cleanup.
