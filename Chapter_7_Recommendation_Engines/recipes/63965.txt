Title:
Orange-Pineapple Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983489.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (46 fluid ounce) can pineapple juice, chilled
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 (6 ounce) can orange juice concentrate, thawed
1 (2 liter) bottle ginger ale, chilled
Orange sherbet
Orange slices
Mint leaves

Directions:
Combine pineapple juice, sweetened condensed milk and orange juice in a large punch bowl.
Add ginger ale gradually just before serving.  Stir gently to mix. Top with scoops of sherbet, orange slices and mint.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
Orange - Pineapple Punch is a great flavor for any time of year.  Top punch bowl with mint leaves or fresh cut orange slices for refreshing taste.
