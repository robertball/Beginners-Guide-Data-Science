Title:
French onion Mac' n' Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4Tbsp butter
2 large onions, very thinly sliced
1tsp ground thyme
1 bay leaf
salt and pepper
1pd penne rigate or elbow noodles
2Tbsp flour
1C beef stock
1C milk
Freshly grated nutmeg
3/4pd Gruyere cheese, shredded

Directions:
In a large skillet, melt 2Tbsp butter over medium-high heat. Stir in the onions, thyme and bay leaf for 5 minutes; season with salt and pepper. Lower the heat and cook the onions until the onions are browned, 10-15min. Transfer to a plate and discard the bay leaf. Reserve the skillet. Meanwhile bring a large pot of water to boil, salt it, add the pasta and cook until al dente. Drain and retun to the pot. In the reserved skillet, melt the remaining 2Tbsp butter over medium heat. Whisk in the the flour for 1 minute, then whisk in the beef stock and milk and bring to a boil; season with salt and pepper and nutmeg. Lower the heat and cook until thickened, about 5 minutes. Stir in the cheese until melted. Combine the onions and sauce with the pasta and toss well.
