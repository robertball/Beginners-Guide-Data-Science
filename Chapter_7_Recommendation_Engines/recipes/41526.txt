Title:
Smoked Salmon and Dill Vegetable Bites Recipe

Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package whipped cream cheese
3 ounces sliced smoked salmon, finely chopped
2 teaspoons lemon juice
1 1/2 teaspoons McCormick速 Dill Weed
1 1/2 teaspoons McCormick速 Minced Onion
1 teaspoon McCormick速 Parsley Flakes
cherry tomatoes, hollowed out
red and green bell pepper pieces
cucumber slices
celery pieces

Directions:
In a bowl, stir together all ingredients except vegetables.
Spoon or pipe with pastry tube a small amount of cheese mixture in/on each piece of vegetable.
Place on serving plate; cover and chill at least 2 hours or up to 24 hours.
