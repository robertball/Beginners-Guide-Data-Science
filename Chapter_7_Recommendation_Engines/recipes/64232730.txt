Title:
Savory Cauliflower Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1825731.jpg
Ingredients:
1lb frozen cauliflower thawed out.
1 tsp butter
1/4 cup plain low fat yogurt
1 tsp salt
3 tbs grated Parmesan
1 tsp chives (optional)

Directions:
Microwave cauliflower in a microwave safe bowl for 5 minutes.
Blend or pulse a few times in a blender.
Add all ingredients one at a time, stirring after each one is added.
Microwave for 3 minutes.
Stir together. Serve.
