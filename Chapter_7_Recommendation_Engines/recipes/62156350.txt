Title:
FRENCH WATERMELON Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole large watermelon
1/2 cup of sugar
15 mint leaves chopped (or to your flavor)
1 cup vodka

Directions:
Cube the watermelon.  Stack the mint leaves and sliver them or just chop them.  Depends on how fancy you want it to look.  Combine all ingredients.  Let sit in fridge covered for about 1-2 hours.

Submitter: 
PAUL
Description: 
This is a simple summer watermelon recipe.  Great at parties and picnics!
