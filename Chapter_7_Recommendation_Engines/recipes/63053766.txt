Title:
Pork Normande Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
3 pork tenderloins
4 shallots, minced
10 ozs. consomme
1/2 cup apple juice
2 cooking apples, peeled, cored and sliced
1/3 cup cream, 10%
salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*F In a saute pan, melt butter and add tenderloins. saute until medium brown, then remove and place in a lightly greased baking dish. Roast for 30 minutes, uncovered. Saute shallots for 2-3 minutes then add consomme, apple juice and cream. Bring to a gentle boil then cook slowly until thickened (20 mins)
Pour  over tenderloins and serve with sliced apples that have been poached in a little apple juice. Great served with wild rice, asparagus and a green salad.
