Title:
Western Beat Scramble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/536320.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
2 tbsp chopped green onion
1 oz thinly sliced smoked ham, chopped
1/3 cup eggbeaters
hot sauce to taste
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Spray a medium skillet with cooking spray and sautee mushrooms, bell pepper, green onion, and ham for 5 minutes on medium heat. Push to one side of the pan.
Add eggbeaters to open side of hot pan, and scramble. Mix egg and vegetables together. Remove from heat and stir in hot sauce, salt and pepper. serve.
