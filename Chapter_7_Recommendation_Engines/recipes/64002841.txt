Title:
Incredible White Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans white beans (Great Northern)
2 T. butter
1 1/2 tsp. dried parsley
1 tsp. dried onion flakes
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1/8 - 1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper (depending on preference)
1/8 tsp. white pepper
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack Cheese
1/4 cup bottled Alfredo Sauce
3 strips crispy fried bacon, crumbled
salt to taste

Directions:
Combine beans and butter in a saucepan, heat until bubbly and beans have softened. Use a potato masher to lightly mash beans. Add remaining ingredients, reserving bacon to sprinkle on top. Warm through until cheese melts. Let flavors marry for about 15 minutes before serving. Serve with crispy tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Dhyana
Description: 
In a world full of bean dips this unique version will have your guests clamoring for the recipe. Crushed red pepper flakes gives it the kick you're looking for while the melted Jack cheese provides that 'comfort' of food we all love.
