Title:
Lemon Grape Cooler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 cup lemon juice
1 cup white grape juice
2 tablespoons unsweetened instant tea
Water

Directions:
In a gallon container, combine the sugar, juices and tea. Add water to measure 1 gallon. Cover and refrigerate until chilled.

Submitter:

Description: 
This refreshing grape beverage has hints of lemon and tea.
