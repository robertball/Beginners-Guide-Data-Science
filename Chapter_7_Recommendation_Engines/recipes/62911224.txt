Title:
Holiday Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/707777.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounce(s) of Philadelphia cream cheese
1/2 cup(s) of sour cream
2 ounce(s) of Armour dried beef
1 tbsp. of diced onion
1 tbsp. of diced green pepper
1/2 tsp. of black pepper
2 tsp. of milk
1/2 cup(s) of chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Warm cream cheese for 20 seconds in a microwave to soften it.
Mix cream cheese, sour cream, chopped dried beef, diced onion, diced green pepper, black pepper, and milk in a bowl.
Spread mixture into an 8x8 baking dish.
Top with chopped pecans.
Bake 15 minutes at 350 degrees.
Serve warm dip with Ritz crackers.

Submitter: 
TARAWVU
Description: 
This is a staple in our family during the holidays.  It's warm and comforting and disappears within minutes.  I've seen people at parties literally stand around this dip just to get more.
