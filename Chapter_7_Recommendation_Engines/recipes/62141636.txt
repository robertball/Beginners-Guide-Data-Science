Title:
Old Grandads cough medicine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teabag
1/2 shot Old Grandad's whiskey
1/2 shot Grand Marnier
1 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp honey
water

Directions:
Boil water and brew the tea.
Add the whiskey and Grand Marnier.
Stir in the Lemon and Honey.

Submitter: 
The Drunken Chef
Description: 
Best not actually drunk with a cold, as alcohol weakens the immune system and dehydrates the body.  This version of a Hot Toddy makes a nice night cap, or a sipping drink with a book on a cold night by the fire.
