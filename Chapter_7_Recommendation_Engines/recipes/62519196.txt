Title:
Linquine with Mushrooms and bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Two serving of Linquine cooked
Any type Mushrooms chopped or pulled apart
1/2 lb of chopped bacon
2-3 garlic cloves chopped
2 tbs margarine
1tbs thyme, basil, fresh or dried
salt and pepper for taste
chili pepper flakes for taste
1 lemon squeezed
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a large skillet fry bacon until crisp. add margarine and mushrooms, garlic,herbs, chili pepper, and salt and pepper. Saute until mushrooms are tender. Add 1/4 cup of water and cook for about 5 minutes until reduced. then add a squeezed lemon and cooked linquine and parmesan. Add more water and toss. You can add any vegetable like broccoli, spinach, arugela.
