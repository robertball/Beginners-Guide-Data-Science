Title:
Velveeta-Free Queso! Ole! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp flour
1 Tbsp butter
2 cups cream or half and half
8 ounces shredded Monterey Jack cheese
4 ounces shredded cheddar cheese
1 small can green chiles, drained
1/2 can Rotelo tomatoes w/green chiles
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp onion powder
1 tsp cilantro

Directions:
Melt butter in saucepan over low heat. Add flour; whisk until free of lumps. Ad cream and stir to combine, heat until thickened, about 5 minutes. Stir frequently, do not boil. Add cheeses and whisk until melted. Add remaining ingredients and stir until well combined.
