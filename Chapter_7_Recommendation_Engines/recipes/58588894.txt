Title:
Homemade Italian Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 ripe plum tomatoes
4 tablespoons canola oil
1 onion, chopped
2 carrots, grated
2 celery stalks, grated
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup chopped flat leaf parsley
1/2 teaspoon dry oregano
1/2 teaspoon dry basil
1/4 cup Burgundy wine
1 bay leaf
6 tablespoons tomato paste
2 teaspoons kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Cut tomatoes in half and squeeze out seeds.  Chop tomatoes and puree in blender or food processor.
In a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat, cook onion,   carrot, celery and garlic in canola oil until onion starts to soften, 5 minutes.  Pour in pureed tomatoes.  Stir in oregano, parsley, basil and wine.  Add bay leaf.  Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover and simmer 2 hours.  Stir in tomato paste and simmer an additional 2 hours.  Discard bay leaf and serve over pasta of your choice.
