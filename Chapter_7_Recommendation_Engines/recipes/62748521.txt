Title:
So Tasty Eggplant Italino Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large eggplant, peeled, cut crosswise in 3/4 inch slices.
1/2 c. Italian dressing, your choice
1/2 tsp. rosemary
1/2 tsp. oregano
1 c. tomato sauce, your choice
6 tbsp. bread crumbs

Directions:
Marinade eggplant for at least 1 hour with dressing, rosemary and oregano. Drain eggplant. Place in baking dish and broil for about 5 mins. per side until tender and lightly browned. Arrange eggplant and tomato sauce in layers in 8" pan. Top with bread crumbs, put under broiler for about 2 mins or until bread crumbs are toasted. Serve immediately.

Eggplant has a bad rep but this recipe will convert you.
