Title:
Simple Molten Iced Chocolate Latte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5543296.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups strong brewed coffee, cold
2 tablespoons chocolate sauce
2 tablespoons cream cheese, softened
1 tablespoon demerara sugar
1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
4 ice cubes, or as desired

Directions:
Combine coffee, chocolate sauce, cream cheese, demerara sugar, cream, and ice cubes in a blender or Ninja(R) Bullet; blend until cream cheese is blended smoothly.

Submitter: 
keysgate
Description: 
Iced easy latte. Top with whipped cream and chocolate sprinkles if desired.
