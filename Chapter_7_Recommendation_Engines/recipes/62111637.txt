Title:
Tomato Aspic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 envelope or 1 Tbsp unflavoured gelatin
¼ C boiling water
2 C vegetable juice e.g. V-8
1 bay leaf
2 cloves
worcestershire sauce (optional)
½ C chopped onion
½ C chopped celery

Directions:
Fully dissolve gelatin in boiling water in mixing bowl
Heat to boiling the vegetable juice with the bay leaf and cloves
Set heat to low and simmer 10 minutes
Remove seasonings
Add chopped onion and celery to juice mix
Pour over dissolved gelatin
Stir gently until FULLY blended
Refrigerate until set

Submitter: 
Barbara Zavadil
Description: 
A refreshing taste of vegetable juice with crisp chopped celery and onion.  Lovely served as a side to my Ham Salad for Two, or any other salad one wants. Makes a lovely colourful touch to a summer evening salad plate.
