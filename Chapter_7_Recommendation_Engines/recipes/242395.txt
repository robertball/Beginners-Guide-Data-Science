Title:
Morning Matcha Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2258956.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1/2 avocado, peeled and pitted
1/2 cup ice
2 tablespoons vanilla protein powder
1/2 teaspoon matcha green tea powder

Directions:
Blend milk, avocado, ice, vanilla protein powder, and matcha green tea powder together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
Matcha and avocado are both powerhouses of nutrients making this smoothie ideal to start the day. Adding a little vanilla protein powder adds not only additional protein but a subtle sweet vanilla flavor.
