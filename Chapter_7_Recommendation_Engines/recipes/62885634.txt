Title:
Carolyn's Classic Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups sliced cooked red potatoes (peeling optional)
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp white or cider vinegar
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped celery
3/4 cup salad dressing (Miracle Whip)
2 hard cooked eggs chopped

Directions:
1. Sprinkle potatoes with sugar and vinegar. Add onion, seasonings and salad dressing. Mix well, then fold in eggs.
2. Chill.

Submitter: 
Lynn Pennec
Description: 
Just a simple potato salad that never stays around too long at our house. Always good and always a great compliment to smoked brisket or chicken.
