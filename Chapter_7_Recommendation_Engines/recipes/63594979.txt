Title:
Meat Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box lemon Jell-o
1 3/4 c hot water
1 1/2 c celery, diced
1 can (#2) corned beef, chopped
1 tsp onion
3 hard boiled eggs
1 small green pepper
3 tsp pimiento
1 c salad dressing

Directions:
Pour hot water in jell-o and cool.
Mix meat, eggs, celery, onions, pimiento and green peppers with salad dressing.
Pour in cooled jell-o.
Mix well and chill until set.
