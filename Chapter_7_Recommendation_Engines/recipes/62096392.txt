Title:
Twice Baked Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-9 medium unpeeled Russett potatoes, baked
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 pound sliced bacon, cooked and crumbled
3 cups (24 ounces) sour cream
2 cups (8 ounces) shredded Monterrey Jack cheese
2 cups (8 ounces) shredded cheddar cheese
5 green onions, chopped

Directions:
To bake potatoes, preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Scrub potatoes and prick with fork.  Place on baking sheet and bake 45 â€“ 60 Minutes, until done.  Cool completely and cut baked potatoes into 1-in. cubes. In a large bowl, combine sour cream, cheeses, bacon, green onion, salt and pepper.  Add potatoes.  Spoon in to a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish. When ready to cook, preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Bake, uncovered for 40 minutes or until cheese is melted.
