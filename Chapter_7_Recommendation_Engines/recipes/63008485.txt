Title:
Mexican Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c cornmeal
1c buttermilk
2 eggs
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 c oil
1 can cream corn
1 med chopped onion
Mix the above together.
1/4 lb sharp cheddar cheese
jalopena peppers chopped to taste

Directions:
Pour 1/2 mixture in dish and top with 1/2 cheese and peppers.  Repeat layers and bake at 350 for 45 min.  cool for 1 hr and then cut.  I usually double this recipe.  It fits nicely in large pyrex dish.
