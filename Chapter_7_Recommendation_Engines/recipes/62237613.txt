Title:
Stuffed Baked Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/325102.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large baking potatoes
1/2 cup sliced green onions
2 tbsp butter
1/2 cup half and half cream
1/2 cup sour cream
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp white pepper
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
paprika

Directions:
Scrub and pierce potatoes. Bake at 400 degrees for 50-75 minutes or until tender. Cool.
When cool enough to handle, cut each potato in half lenghtwise. Scoop out pulp, leaving a thin shell. Place pulp in a large bowl and mash.
Stir in next 6 ingredients. Fold in cheese. Spoon into potato shells and sprinkle with paprika.
Place on a baking sheet and bake uncovered at 375 degrees for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kelli F
Description: 
This recipe is nice because you bake only the servings you need and freeze the remaining ones to use another time.
