Title:
Peruvian Arroz Tapado Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 lb ground beef
2 tomatoes, chopped
2 tbsp tomato paste
1 packet Sazon Goya
1 cup chicken broth
1 tablespoon raisins
2 hard boiled eggs, finely chopped
2 cups cooked rice
non stick spray

Directions:
Brown beef and drain fat.
Add onion and green pepper and saute for a few minutes. Add garlic, tomatoes, chicken broth, tomato paste, sazon goya packet, hard boiled eggs, and raisins. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
Spray a cup with non stick spray and fill with rice, hallowing out the center.  Fill the center with the meat mixture. Turn cup upside down over a plate and let the rice mound slide out of the cup.
Repeat for each serving.

Submitter: 
Jessica Weber
Description: 
A delicious tomato based mixture of ground beef, hard boiled eggs, and raisins stuffed into a cone shaped rice mound.
