Title:
Kathy's low sugar waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
2 TBS sugar
3 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
2 eggs beaten
1 1/2 cups milk
1/4 cup butter melted
1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Heat waffle iron as directed.
Sift together dry ingredients. combine beaten eggs, milk, and melted butter.  Add liquid to dry.  Beat only until smooth.
Pour batter onto hot iron.  About 1 cup for 9 inch square, and 1/2 cup for 7 inch round pan.
Bake until the waffles stop steaming.
