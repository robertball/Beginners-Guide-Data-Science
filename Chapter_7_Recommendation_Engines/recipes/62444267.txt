Title:
Crispy Spicy Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/460059.jpg
Ingredients:
Package of chicken wings
1 Cup of Franks Hot Sauce
1/2 cup of Melted Butter
1 Package of Shake and Bake Extra Crispy

Directions:
Roll chicken in butter, then hot sauce and set aside.
Once all chicken is rolled in butter and hot sauce, shake and bake them individually.
Line your baking sheet with tinfoil and place coated chicken on sheet. Bake at 425 for about 35 minutes, or until juices run clear. SO SIMPLE AND SO GOOD.

The taste of yummy wing sauce but the crunchy skin you want.
