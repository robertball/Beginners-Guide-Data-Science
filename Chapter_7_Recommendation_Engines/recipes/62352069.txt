Title:
Oven Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup oil
1/4 cup soya
2 cups long grain rice
4 cups chicken broth
1/2 cup celery
1/4 cup onion
1 cup sliced mushroom
1/2 package onion soup mix

Directions:
mix all ingredients together and bake at 325 for 90 minutes.
