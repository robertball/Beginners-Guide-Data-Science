Title:
Super easy cold Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cans of crabmeat
1 block of cream cheese softened
2 T of mayo
2 T of finely chopped white onion
3 T of ketchup
1 to 2 tsp of horseradish (to your liking). You can also omit the horseradish and it is still delish.

Directions:
Combine ingredients and serve chilled with crackers of your choice!

Submitter: 
Muffintopmommy
Description: 
A very delicious, very simple to make crab dip that you serve chilled with crackers. This is the dip that all your friends will ask you how to make!
