Title:
Sunset Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. butter
1/3  c. sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
2 1/4 c. flour
Red & Green Sugar

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar; add vanilla. Blend in flour gradually. Shape into four rolls 1 1/2 inch diameter. Roll in colored sugar (red and green) on waxed paper. Wrap each roll separately in Saran Wrap. Refrigerate overnight. Let dough stand at room temperature a few minutes before slicing into 1/4 inch slices. Bake on ungreased sheet 12-15 minutes at 375 degrees. Cookies bake very light colored.
