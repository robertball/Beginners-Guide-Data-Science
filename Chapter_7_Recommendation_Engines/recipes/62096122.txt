Title:
Banana Snack Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup water
1/3- 1/2 cup mashed ripe bananas (about 1 medium)
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
confectioners sugar

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine flour and baking soda.
In another bowl, whisk the brown sugar, water, oil, vanilla and banana.
Stir into dry ingredients just until moistened.
Transfer to a greased 8 in. square pan.
Bake at 350' for 30-35 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
Cool on a wire rack.
Dust with confectioners Sugar if desired before serving.

Submitter: 
Jodster
Description: 
This is a nice way to use up a few overripe Bananas and it makes a small cake which is nice for smaller families.
