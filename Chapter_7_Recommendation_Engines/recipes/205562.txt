Title:
Thanksgiving Drumstick Treats™ Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990821.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons butter or margarine
1 (10 ounce) package JET-PUFFED Marshmallows*
6 cups KELLOGG'S® RICE KRISPIES® cereal
Peanut butter
KELLOGG'S® COCOA KRISPIES® cereal

Directions:
In large saucepan melt butter over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat.
Add KELLOGG'S RICE KRISPIES cereal. Stir until well coated.
Cool slightly. Using buttered hands shape mixture into twelve small drumsticks.
Spread peanut butter on large end of each drumstick, then dip in KELLOGG'S COCOA KRISPIES cereal. Refrigerate until firm. Best if served the same day.

Submitter: 
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies®
Description: 
Everyone will clamor for a drumstick when you serve this all-time-favorite recipe.
