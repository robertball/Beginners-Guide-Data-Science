Title:
Marzetti®'s Asian Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977718.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package classic coleslaw mix
4 cups sliced red cabbage
3 cups mung bean sprouts
1 bunch green onion, 4-inch section, both green and white parts, chopped
1 jalapeno, minced (with or without the seeds)
1 tablespoon fresh minced ginger
1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar
3/4 cup Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
2/3 cup chopped salted peanuts

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, combine all ingredients except peanuts. Add Marzetti Slaw Dressing and toss well. Chill 1 hour and serve. Stir in peanuts just before serving.

Submitter: 
Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Description: 
Shake up tradition by bringing the flavors of the Far East to slaw with sprouts, ginger, and rice wine vinegar.
