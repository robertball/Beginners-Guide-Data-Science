Title:
Complete & Utter Oatmeal Insanity Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 packet plain instant oatmeal
1/4 cup Fiber One bran cereal (original)
1/4 cup light vanilla soymilk
2 tablespoons canned pure pumpkin
2 tablespoons Fat Free Reddi-wip
1 tablespoon sugar-free pancake syrup
1/4 tsp pumpkin pie spice
5 sprays I Can't Believe It's Not Butter! Spray
1 no-calorie sweetener packet
Dash salt

Directions:
Place all ingredients except for Reddi-wip in a medium microwave-safe bowl.
Add 1/4 cup water and stir.
Microwave for 45 seconds, then mix well.
Microwave an additional 30 to 40 seconds. Allow oatmeal to thicken before removing from the microwave.
Add Reddi-wip and stir well. Then enjoy the oatmeal!
