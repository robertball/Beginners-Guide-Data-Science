Title:
Scalloped Potatoes with a Dash of Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/288256.jpg
Ingredients:
4-5 small to medium white potatoes sliced in 1/2 cm thick slices
1/4 of a Spanish onion sliced
1/2 tbsp of garlic powder or a few cloves of real garlic
1/2 tsp of curry powder
salt & pepper to taste
1-2 tbsp of olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F
Slice potatoes into 1/2cm thin pieces
Put potatoes in a mixing bowl with the onions, garlic, salt, pepper, curry and olive oil & toss until it's well coated.
Layer the mixture into a casserole dish.
Cover with foil and bake for 1 hour. Remove cover and bake for 10 more minutes with a couple minutes on broil.

Submitter: 
Raquel Teixeira
Description: 
This is just one of many spins on a classic dish. You won't find the traditional milk, flour, butter or cheese in this recipe, and yet somehow it turns out great every time.
