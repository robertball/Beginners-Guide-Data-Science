Title:
Rudy's Re-fried Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/351866.jpg
Ingredients:
2 x 15.5 oz cans Goya pinto beans
2 oz. Monterey Jack Cheese, hand shredded to taste
Rendered fat of 3 strips of Bacon

Directions:
Heat beans in a medium pot
Mash beans to semi-smooth consistency
Add cheese and Bacon Fat, stirring continuously
Let cook on low for 5-10 minutes until cheese is completely melted, continuously stirring.
Let cool for at least 30 minutes
Reheat for refried beans, add water if necessary.
Serve immediately.

Submitter:

Description: 
The simplist recipe you'll ever see for 5 star Mexican refried pinto beans.
