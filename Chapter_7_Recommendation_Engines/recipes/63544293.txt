Title:
thekruser's Shrimp Stock Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Shells and tails (and heads if you have them) from 2 lbs of shrimp
2 1/2 c water
1/2 c chopped onion
1/4 c chopped celery
2 cloves garlic
1 lemon, sliced
2 fresh bay leaves (or 3 dried leaves)
3 sprigs fresh thyme (or 3 t dried)
1 t black peppercorns

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a 2 qt sauce pan.
Bring to almost a boil, reduce heat to a low simmer.
Simmer for 45 min to an hour.
Strain.
