Title:
Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Pounds potatoes cooked and cubed
1 small onion finely chopped
1 carrot grated
2 Boiled eggs cooled and chopped up
Sauce:
1 Cup of Mayonnaise
1 Tbsp Dijon Mustard
1 tsp curry powder
1 TBSP pickle juice
1/8 tsp pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
1 TBSP sugar
Salt to taste

Directions:
1.  Place potatos, onions, carrots and egg in a large bowl.
2. In a medium bowl add the remaining and mix.  Add to potato mixture.
