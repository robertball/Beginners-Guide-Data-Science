Title:
Creamy Caramel Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Sugar
6 Tablespoons Butter
1/2 cup Heavy Cream

Directions:
Whisk 1 cup of sugar in a saucepan over medium-high heat until sugar dissolves. Once the sugar starts boiling stop whiskingand swirl the pan. When the sugar turns amber in color, add the butter and swirl until melted. Remove pan from heat and slowly pour cream into the pan, swirling until mixed. Whisk until mixture is smooth. Pour into glass jar and allow to cool.
Refrigerate for up to 2 weeks.
