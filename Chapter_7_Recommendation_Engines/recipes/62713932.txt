Title:
Ground Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/619000.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 pkg. fresh cremini mushrooms,sliced
1tablespoon caraway seeds
1 tablespoon beef concentrate- "better than boullion"
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon worcestshire sauce
1/2 teaspoon mustard powder
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup beef broth
1 (8 ounce) package wide egg noodles

Directions:
Break up ground meat into a large skillet, and add onion, mushrooms, caraway seeds, beef concentrate, garlic powder, and mustard powder, worcestshire sauce.  Cook and stir over medium heat until the meat is browned and onion is tender.  Drain off excess fat.
Add beef broth and stir to combine and cook over low heat for 10 to 15 minutes.  Do not stir too much.
Meanwhile, cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water until done. Drain. Add sour cream to meat mixture and heat through.  Serve sauce over hot noodles.
