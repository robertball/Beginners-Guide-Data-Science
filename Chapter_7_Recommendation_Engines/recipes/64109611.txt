Title:
Classic Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds of beef stew meat
1 to 2 cups of water
1/2 pound to 1 pound of sliced mushrooms
3 to 4 cloves of minced garlic
1 Large onion chopped
2 cans of condensed beef broth
1/4 cup of catchup
6 tablespoons of flour (add more if a lot excess water is still in the pan, maybe 1 or 2 more tablespoons)
1 to 2 teaspoons of salt
16 ounces of reduced fat sour cream
1 pound of uncooked noodles

Directions:
Add meat,mushrooms, onion, garlic and water to large dutch pan on the stove and simmer until meat is tender (couple of hours)and can be cut up or shredded inside pan.
Reserving 2/3 cup of the broth, stir in remaining broth, catchup and salt and simmer for about 10 to 15 minutes.
Blend reserved broth and flour and stir into meat mixture. Heat to boiling. Reduce heat and stir in sour cream until heated through. Serve over cooked noodles.

Submitter: 
Brenda Brown
Description: 
This is a family favorite made easier from Betty Crocker's cookbook. Great flavor and simple to make!!
