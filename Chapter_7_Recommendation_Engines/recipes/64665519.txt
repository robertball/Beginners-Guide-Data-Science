Title:
Torta de Bolacha (Brazilian Chocolate Cookie Pie) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4569688.jpg
Ingredients:
4 sticks of unsalted butter @ room temperature
4 eggs - separate yolk and white
500mg of Brazilian “Nescau” chocolate powder
2 cans of table cream (creme de leite)
3 sleeves of Maria cookies
9x13 freezer safe dish w/ lid

Directions:
Mix egg yolk and butter on mixer well. Then add chocolate powder. Once fully blended, add table cream by spoonfuls without water. Mix well until smooth.
On separate bowl, beat egg whites into merengue. Add 8 spoonfuls of sugar and continuing beating until peaks form. Then gently fold merengue onto chocolate cream.
Layer chocolate cream and Maria cookies into dish. Moisten cookies in white milk briefly prior to layering.
Decorate and freeze for keeping.
Allow to thaw 30minutes prior to serving.
