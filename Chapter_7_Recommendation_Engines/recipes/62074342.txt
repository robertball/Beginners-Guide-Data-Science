Title:
Scrumptious Sweet Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-40 oz can sweet potatoes in syrup, drained
2 stalks celery, finely chopped
3 sweet pickles, finely chopped
1 lrg or 4 baby dill pickles, chopped
2 green onions, tops included, finely chopped
3 hardboiled eggs, chopped
1/4 cup(1/2 med) red onion, finely chopped (opt)
1/2 c miracle whip light
1/3 c mayo or light mayo
3 T durkee's dressing or dijon mustard
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In medium pot, cover eggs with cold water, bring to boil, and set aside for 20 min.  In large bowl, mash drained sweet potatoes.  Add all celery, pickles, onion(s); chop egg and add, mixing well.  In separate bowl mix dressing ingredients and add to sweet potato mixture, mixing thoroughly.  Serve immediately or, for best taste, refrigerate 1-24 to allow flavors to mingle.  Re-stir before serving.

Submitter: 
RANCHGIRL_59714
Description: 
This salad is an awesome use of leftover sweet potatoes, and a quick, delicious side dish for summer potlucks and BBQs! My own twist on the recipe from a deli where I once worked.
