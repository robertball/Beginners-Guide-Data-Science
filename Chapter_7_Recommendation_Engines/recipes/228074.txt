Title:
Cucumber Feta Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/970428.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large cucumber, finely chopped
1/2 cup finely chopped red onion
4 ounces crumbled feta cheese
1 pinch dried dill, or to taste (optional)

Directions:
Mix cucumber, red onion, feta cheese, and dill together in a bowl until thoroughly combined.

Submitter: 
GourmetLisa
Description: 
A very simple fresh salsa that goes great with tortilla chips.
