Title:
Mama's Lasagna Scramble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground beef
1/2 lb. Cellentani pasta(or any other pasta)
1 14.5 oz. can Diced tomatoes
1 15 oz. can Tomato sauce
1 6 oz. Container of Feta cheese
1/2 tbsp. Pepper
1/2 tbsp. Onion salt
1/2 tbsp. Garlic powder
1/2 tbsp. Basil leave flakes
1/2 tbsp. Oregano flakes
1/2 tbsp. Parsley flakes
1/2 tbsp. Thyme

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Bring water to a boil for Cellentani pasta.  Cook pasta for 9 minutes until al dente.
In a large frying pan brown ground beef.  After meat is cooked through add the tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, feta cheese, pepper, onion salt, garlic powder, basil leaves, oregano, parsley and thyme.  Lower temperature to a simmer.
After the pasta is done, drain and place in a large casserole dish.  Pour the meat mixture over the pasta, do not mix.
Bake for 20 minutes uncovered.  Let it cool for 5 minutes and serve.

Submitter: 
Lisa
Description: 
I came up with this on the fly when I did not want to take the time for full blown lasagna.  My kids are all picky eaters and all asked for seconds!
