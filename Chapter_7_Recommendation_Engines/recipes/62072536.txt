Title:
Macaroni and Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-10 oz. macaroni, cooked and drained
12 oz. grated mild Cheddar cheese
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup sour cream
1 cup mayonnaise
1 small onion, chopped
Cheez-Its crackers, crushed
Margarine

Directions:
Mix soup, sour cream, mayonnaise, and onion together.  Butter a 9 x 13 inch dish.  Layer half of the macaroni, chhese, and soup mixture; repeat layers.  Place crushed crackers on top and dot with margarine.  Bake at 325 degrees for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
UCMOM
Description: 
This is a dressed up version of macaroni and cheese.  I always prepare this at Christmas and Thanksgiving.
