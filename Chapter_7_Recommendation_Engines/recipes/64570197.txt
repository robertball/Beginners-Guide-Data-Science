Title:
Orange Carrot Ginger Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3528911.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup carrots
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 large orange
1 inch piece of ginger

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a juicer and blend until smooth.*
Transfer to a glass, add ice and a straw and enjoy!
*If you do not have a juicer, place all ingredients in a blender (just be sure to peel the carrots & orange before). Blend until smooth, then place a mesh strainer over a bowl and pour blended juice over the strainer. Discard the pulp and pour juice into a glass.

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Boost your immune system with an Orange Carrot Ginger Juice packed with healthy citrus and carrots. This juice has tons of flavor, a zing of tartness and is full of nourishment. The perfect juice to spring clean your diet!
