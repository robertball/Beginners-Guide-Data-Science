Title:
"Ohmigawd" Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Brownies:
1 box of your favorite plain boxed brownie mix
1 cup of milk chocolate chips
Topping:
1 7oz. jar Marshmallow cream
1 8oz. package cream cheese, softened
1/2 Cup chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
1.)Prepare the brownie batter as directed on the box. Mix the choclate chips into the batter and bake according to box directions.
2.)While they are baking, use a mixer to blend the marshmallow cream and cream cheese until light and smooth (no lumps).
3.)Allow the brownies to cool and top with the cream cheese mixure.
Sprinkle chopped nuts over the cream cheese mixture.
*if not serving immediately, cover and keep cool*

Submitter: 
MLALAK
Description: 
These Brownies are very decadent, but made from store-bought components that make any baker's life easy! The name came from my friend who said Ohmigawd, I want that recipe!.
