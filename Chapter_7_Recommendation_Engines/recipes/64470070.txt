Title:
Grilled Halibut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter, softened
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1  teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon Old Bay
1/2 teaspoon Seasoned Salt
4 inch-thick halibut steaks

Directions:
Mix together butter, lemon juice and seasonings.  Smear on both sides of fish.  Place in tin foil and seal carefully to make a packet.  Grill for 5-7 minutes per side.

Submitter: 
Jacqueline Bedsaul Johnson
Description: 
Making a quick compound butter to spread on your halibut steaks is a perfect way to get a tasty grilled fish.Modified from: Grilled Halibut
