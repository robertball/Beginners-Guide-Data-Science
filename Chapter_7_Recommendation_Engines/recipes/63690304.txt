Title:
Granola Bars with Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. oats
1 c. brown sugar
2/3 c. melted butter OR oil
1/2 c. corn syrup
1/4 c. crunchy peanut butter
1 t. vanilla extract
1 pkg. chocolate chips
1/4 c. peanut butter

Directions:
Mix all ingredients up to and including the vanilla extract.
Press into a greased 9"x13" pan.
Bake at 375 degrees for 10 minutes.
Cool completely.
Melt together chocolate chips and peanut butter.
Pour and spread over cool bars.
Cut when hard.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
A delicious snack for on-the-go. Say good-bye to pre-packaged granola bars, and hello to your own flavor combinations!
