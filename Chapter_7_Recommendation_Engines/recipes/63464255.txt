Title:
Christmas Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Jimmy Dean pork sausage
1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent roll dough
8 eggs, beaten
1/2 cup Milk
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Place sausage in a skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown. Drain, crumble, and set aside.
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Line the bottom of the prepared baking dish with crescent roll dough, and sprinkle with crumbled sausage. In a large bowl, mix beaten eggs,milk, salt, pepper, and Cheddar. Pour over the sausage and crescent rolls.
Bake 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
