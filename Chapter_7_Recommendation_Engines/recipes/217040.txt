Title:
Dannon Sunshine Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988348.jpg
Ingredients:
1 mango, peeled, seeded, and cut into chunks
1 banana, peeled and chopped
1 cup orange juice
1 cup ice cubes
1 cup Dannon® All Natural yogurt

Directions:
Place mango, banana, orange juice, ice and Dannon All Natural in a blender. Blend until smooth. Pour into a glass and enjoy

Submitter: 
Dannon® All Natural
Description: 
Start the day with a glassful of sunny fruit--mango, banana, orange juice and tangy yogurt fuel a busy day.
