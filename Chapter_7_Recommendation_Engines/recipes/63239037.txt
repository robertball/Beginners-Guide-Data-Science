Title:
Garlic Roasted Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/844334.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Bunch of Asparagus
1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
2 Teaspoons Kosher Salt
1 Teaspoon Freshly Ground Black Pepper
2 Garlic Cloves, Minced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Slice the ends off of each asparagus stick. Rinse and pat dry.
Drizzle with oil, and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
Transfer to a baking sheet, and season with minced garlic.
Bake at 400 degrees for 20-25 minutes until golden brown. No need to turn!

Description: 
Crispy, healthy, and delicious take on the every day asaparagus!
