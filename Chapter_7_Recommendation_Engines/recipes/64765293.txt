Title:
Chicken Piccata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/2 tsp. kosher salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper
2/4 cup chicken stock
2 oz flour (about 1/2 cup)
2 Tbsp. butter, divided
2 Tbsp. olive oil, divided
1/4 cup finely chopped shallots
Lemon slices
4 medium garlic cloves, thinly sliced
1/2 cup dry white wine
2 Tbsp. lemon juice
1 1/2 Tbsp. drained capers
3 Tbsp. fresh parsley

Directions:
Place each chicken breast between 2 sheets of heavy duty plastic wrap and pound thin.
Sprinkle both sides evenly with salt and pepper.
Combine 1/4 cup stock and 1 tsp. flour in a bowl and set aside
Place remaining flour in shallow dish and dredge chicken
Heat 1 Tbsp. of butter and 1 Tbsp. oil in large skillet over medium high
Add chicken to pan; cook 4 minutes on each side and remove from pan
Add remaining oil to pan; saute shallots and lemon slices
Add garlic; saute 1 minute
Add wine, cook 2 minutes until all liquid is almost evaporated
Add remaining 1/2 cup stock; bring to a boil.  Cook 5 minutes.
Stir in reserved stock and flour mixture; cook 1 minute
Remove from heat; stir in butter, juice and capers.
Place chicken breast on plate and top with sauce.  Sprinkle with parsley
