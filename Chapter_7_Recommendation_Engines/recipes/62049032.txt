Title:
Cookie Monster Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1 lb. brown sugar
1/2 tbsp. vanilla
1/2 cup honey or cornsyrup
1/2 lb. butter, softened
1/2 lb. peanut butter (creamy)
4 tsp. baking soda
9 cups raw oatmeal
1/2 lb. chocolate chips
1/2 lb. M&M;'s (plain or peanut)
Raisins (optional)

Directions:
Mix ingredients in order given, using a large spoon.  Put onto a cookie sheet with an ice cream scoop or large spoon.  Bake at 350 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes.  Cool on wire rack.
NOTE:  The dough will become difficult
to stir towards the end, but the
results are definitely worth it!

Submitter: 
SARA R
Description: 
I got this from a Marine Corps wife while my husband & I were stationed overseas in Okinawa.  You might think wow when you read the ingredients, but the cookie is definitely worth the effort!!!
