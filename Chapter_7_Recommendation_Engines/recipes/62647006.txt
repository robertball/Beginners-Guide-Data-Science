Title:
Easy Italian Sausage Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb sweet Italian sausage
1 onion, chopped
1 bell pepper, chopped
1 TBSP minced garlic
1 lb sliced mushrooms
1 can sliced olives, drained
1 TBSP olive oil
2 large cans tomato sauce
1 small can tomato paste
2 med diced tomatoes
Grated parmesean cheese
Oregano
Basil
Spaghetti noodles
Salt

Directions:
1. Brown sausage, bell pepper, onion, mushrooms, and garlic in olive oil, 20 min
2.  Add tomato sauce, paste and diced tomatoes and olives.  Season to taste with oregano and basil.  Simmer about 30 min over low heat to infuse flavors.
3.  Boil noodles in salted water.
4.  Serve sauce over noodles topped with parmesean.
