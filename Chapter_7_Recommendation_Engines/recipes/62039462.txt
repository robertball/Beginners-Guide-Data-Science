Title:
Whipped Cream Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14oz can of Sweetened Condensed Milk
1/2 Cup Half & Half
1 1/2 Cup Heavy Cream
1/2 tbs Vanilla

Directions:
Combine ingredients into a chilled bowl and whip on medium to high for a couple of minutes. Transfer mixture to ice cream freezer and follow manufacturer's instructions. Chill in freezer for 1 to 2 hours before serving for best reslults but can be served immediately too.

Submitter: 
Doug Matthews
Description: 
The hint of vanilla makes this an easy A La Mode ice cream for pies and cakes.
