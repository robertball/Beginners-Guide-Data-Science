Title:
Wainachsrollen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886723.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, melted
1 cup lard, melted
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar, packed
3 egg
4 1/2 cups flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
8 ounces almonds, chopped

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir together the melted butter, melted lard, sugar, brown sugar, and eggs. Mix until well blended.
Stir in the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and almonds.
Roll the dough into 2 logs about 2 1/2 inches in diameter; refrigerate overnight.
Let the dough logs thaw for about 5 minutes.
Slice dough into slices about 1/4 inch thick.
Bake at 350ยบ for 8 to 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
German recipe that is over 100 years old.  You can use a cookie mold for these cookies.  After you have chilled the dough, roll out the dough to 1/4 - 1/2 thick.Modified from: Wainachsrollen
