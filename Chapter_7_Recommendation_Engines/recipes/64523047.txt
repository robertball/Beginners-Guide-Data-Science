Title:
Gelato al Fior di Latte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
500ml whole milk (latte intero)
500ml heavy cream (panna)
150g super-fine granulated sugar (zucchero fine)
50g (not ml) brown rice syrup

Directions:
Add sugar and rice syrup to a saucepan. Add the milk and whisk until completely combined. Heat the mixture over low heat. Stir constantly until the sugar is dissolved. About 1 minute. Remove from the heat. Allow to cool and add the cream to the mixture. Allow to cool thoroughly in fridge. About an hour.
Pour into gelateria and start.

Plain gelato without eggs.
