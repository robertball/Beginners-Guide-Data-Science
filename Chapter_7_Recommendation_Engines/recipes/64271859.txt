Title:
Macaroni and Cheese with Ham Cubes (Double Batch) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 batches WHITE SAUCE (prepared as directed)
1 teaspoon dry mustard
16 ounces shredded cheddar cheese (4 cups once shredded)
16 ounces elbow macaroni
3 cups ham cubes

Directions:
Stir dry mustard into white sauce; add 3 1/2 cups of the grated cheese and stir until melted (reserve 1/2 cup cheese for topping).  Cook elbow macaroni for six minutes; drain.  Add cheese sauce to macaroni; stir.  Turn into 2 greased, 2 quart casserole dishes; stir in ham cubes (1 1/2 cups in each).  Top with reserved grated cheese.
To freeze:  Cover casseroles with aluminum foil; seal dishes in jumbo freezer bag. Freeze.
To prepare: Place frozen casserole (covered with Aluminum foil) in preheated 375 degree oven.  Bake for 25 minutes; remove aluminum foil.  Bake 25 minutes more, or until hot and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Hanson
Description: 
This is one of those thank GOODNESS I've got something in the freezer for dinner! recipes. Makes two casseroles. One for now and one for later!
