Title:
Alabama White Barbecue Sauce II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c light mayonnaise
3 T low-fat buttermilk
2 T cider vinegar
2 t Dijon mustard
1 t sugar
1/2 t coarsely ground pepper
1/2 t salt
2 garlic cloves, minced

Directions:
In a medium bowl, whisk ingredients together.  Use as a 15-minute marinade or dipping sauce for chicken or pork, a sandwich spread or a coating for grilled corn.
