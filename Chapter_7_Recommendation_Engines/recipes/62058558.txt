Title:
Lamb and Bulgar Soup (Shorba Freek) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb lamb leg or lamb shoulder, with bones, cut into small pieces
1T olive oil
1 small onion diced
2 cloves garlic
2 cinnamon sticks
1/2 14.5 oz can chickpeas
1 14.5 oz can whole tomatoes
1/8-1/4 C bulgar (cracked wheat)
7 C water
salt and pepper to tasted
fresh parsley and dried mint to garnish

Directions:
Put olive oil in stockpot and over medium high heat add lamb pieces, onions, garlic, cinnamon and salt and pepper. cook until meat is brown and onions almost tender.  Add water and chickpeas.  Bring to a boil and skim the scum off the top. Reduce to a simmer and cook till meat is tender. About 45-60mins.  Meanwhile put canned tomatoes in food processor and blend till smooth consistency.  When meat and chickpeas are ready add the tomatoes and bulgar. cook for 5-15 minutes till bulgar has puffed and absorbed some moisture. remove meat with a slotted spoon. Cut off the bones and fat and return to soup.  Transfer to a soup tureen and top with fresh parsley and dried mint. ENJOY

Submitter: 
Maryam
Description: 
This tasty soup begins with lamb shoulder,cinnamon, onions, garlic and chickpeas. Finished with tomatoes, bulgar and some fresh parsley and mint. Wonderful on cool nights!!
