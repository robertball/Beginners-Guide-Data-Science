Title:
Hummingbird Cake III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
2 cups white sugar
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
3 eggs, beaten
3/4 cups vegetable oil
3/4 cup applesauce
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
1 cup chopped pecans
1/2 - 2 cups diced bananas
1/2 cup chopped pecans (optional)
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 3.4 oz box instant vanilla pudding mix
1 1/2 cups cold milk
8 oz cool-whip
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained well (for inner layer)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans.
Combine flour, white sugar, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon in a large bowl.  Add eggs and oil; stir until moistened.  Do not beat.  Stir in bananas, 2 teaspoons vanilla, pineapple, and 1 cup pecans.
Pour equally into the two cake pans. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until done. Cool on a wire rack.
Beat the cream cheese and 1/2 cup pecans until blended. Add milk and pudding mix. Beat until smooth. Fold in the Cool-Whip.
Spread crushed pineapple over the top of one of the cake layers.Top it with the cream cheese frosting. Put second cake layer on top and spread the frosting over the full cake.
