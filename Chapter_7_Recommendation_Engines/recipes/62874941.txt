Title:
Chocolate Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup soft butter
3 eggs
4 very ripe bananas
1 1/4 cups buttermilk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 cups all-purpose flour
2 tsp cinnamon
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
24oz bag of Semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Cream together butter and sugar.
Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
Mix in bananas, buttermilk, and vanilla.
Mix in flour, cinnamon, baking powder, and soda.  Stir in chocolate chips.
Pour batter into two greased 9x5 inch pans.
Bake for 50 to 60 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean (excluding chocolate).

Description: 
Buttermilk and vanilla make for two moist, well-flavored loaves.Modified from: Banana Nut Bread I
