Title:
Spanish Lime Rice I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T Canola oil
2 yellow onion finely choppoed
4 C rice
2 C chopped cilantro leaves or chives
4 cloves of garlic chopped
2 tsp lime zest
8 C chicken broth
salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in a medium saucepan.  Add the onions and garlic, cook until soft.  Add the rice, toss to coat in teh oil and onion mixture. Cook for 2 minutes.  Add the chicken broth. lime zest and cilantro (or chives). Season the rice with salt and pepper and stir well.  Reduce heat, cover pan, and cook until rice is just cooked through (about 14 to 16 minutes).
