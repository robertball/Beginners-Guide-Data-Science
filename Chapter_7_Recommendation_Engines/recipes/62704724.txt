Title:
BFF's Tasty Corn Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/620189.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Eggs
6 tbsp melted butter
2 tbsp flour
1 tbsp sugar
17 oz creamed corn
1 cup heavy cream
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp nutmeg
dash hot sauce
1 3/4 cup frozen corn

Directions:
Grease 1 1/2 Qt baking dish.  Beat eggs. Stir in butter, flour, spices until blended.  Stir in remaining ingredients. Pour into greased dish. Bake at 325 degrees for 1 hour 15 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
We had this at my BFF's house in Florida and she gifted me the recipe. It is so good.
