Title:
Host's Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz rigatoni
1/2 lb boneless chicken breast, cubed
1/2 lb shrimp
2 tsp Tastefully Simple Seasoned Salt
1/2 red bell pepper, diced
1 cup Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
Sauce
1/2 c light sour cream
1/4 fat free mayo
1 TB Tastefully Simple Wahoo Chili
1 tsp Tastefully Simple Garlic Garlic

Directions:
Mix together sour cream, mayo, Wahoo Chili and Garlic Garlic.  Set aside.
Toss chicken with seasoned salt; set aside
In non-stick skillet, heat olive oil, saute red pepper until softened.  Add seasoned chicken, saute.  Add TS sauce, stir and heat until chicken is done.
Add shrimp just before serving, stir until cooked through.  Stir in jack cheese just before serving.
