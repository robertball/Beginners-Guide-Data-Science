Title:
Easy French Dips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound deli beef, thinly sliced
4 beef bouillon cubes
3 cups water
3/4 teaspoon onion powder
3/4 teaspoon garlic powder
3/4 teaspoon dried oregano
3/4 teaspoon dried thyme
1 small bay leaf
4 6-inch sliced French rolls

Directions:
Layer beef in 8x8 baking dish.
Mix all remaining ingredients (except rolls) in saucepan.   Bring to a simmer.
Pour mixture over beef and bake beef in 350 degree oven for 20 minutes.
