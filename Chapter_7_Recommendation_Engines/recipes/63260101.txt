Title:
Honey-Lime Grilled Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/853464.jpg
Ingredients:
8 boneless, skinless chicken thighs, trimmed of fat
For the marinade:
1 egg
1 Tbsp smoked sweet paprika (see notes)
1/4 tsp sea salt
1/4 cup honey
2 tsp sesame oil
2 Tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
juice of 1 lime

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together all the marinade ingredients. Add the chicken. Cover and marinate in fridge overnight or for at least 4 hours.
Heat the grill at medium heat. Grill chicken for approximately 18 - 20 minutes, or until done. Remove chicken from grill and serve!

Submitter: 
Sanderson32
Description: 
This chicken recipe makes extremely moist chicken.  Our whole family loves this recipe, we make it a lot in the summer.  I like to serve it with a side of brown basmati rice and some stir-fried veggies for a complete meal.
