Title:
Party Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/479737.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix
6 green onions, minced
5-6 (12 inch) flour tortillas
1 red bell pepper, diced
1 green pepper, diced
1 (6 ounce) can black olives, chopped
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Combine all ingredients. Spread onto tortillas. Roll up the tortillas, then wrap them tightly in aluminum foil. Chill 2 hours or overnight. Cut off ends of the rolls, and slice the chilled rolls into 1 inch slices.

Submitter: 
FROGHOPPER
Description: 
It's easy to make lots and lots of these cream-cheese-based rounds: Simply use extra-large tortillas and roll them up with the smooth and crunchy filling.Modified from: Party Pinwheels
