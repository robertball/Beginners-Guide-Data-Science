Title:
Spicy Sausage Pasta Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987002.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (19.76 ounce) package JohnsonvilleÂ® Italian Mild Sausage Links prepared according to package directions and coin-sliced
1 medium sweet bell pepper, diced
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 cup red wine
1 (16 ounce) jar Alfredo sauce
1 pound penne pasta, cooked and drained
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a large skillet, saute sausage, pepper, mushrooms and garlic. Add wine, continue to cook until 1/2 of the liquid has evaporated. Stir in sauce, pasta and cheese until blended.
Serve immediately.
