Title:
Spinach Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/522134.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pkg. frozen chopped spinach
1 (14) oz. can artichoke hearts, drained
1 1/4 sticks butter
8 oz cream cheese
8 oz water chestnuts, sliced and drained
garlic powder
1/2 cup bread crumbs

Directions:
Cook spinach according to directions.  Drain the spinach well before combining it with the other ingredients. Cut artichoke hearts in half.  Melt 1 stick butter in saucepan.  Aded cream cheese, spinach, water chestnuts, and garlic powder.  Line baking dish with artichoke hearts and cover with spinach mixture.   Sprinkle with bread crumbs and dot with remaining (1/4) cup of butter.  Bake at 400 for 20 to 25 minutes until heated through.
I also add a little parmesan cheese to the spinach mixture.
