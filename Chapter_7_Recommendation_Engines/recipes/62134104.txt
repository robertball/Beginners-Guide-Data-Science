Title:
chewy gooey chocolate chip bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 c butter (salted)(softened)
1/2 c white sugar
1/2 c light brown sugar
1 large egg
1 tbs vanilla extract
1 c semi sweet chocolate chips
1/2 c walnuts (optional)

Directions:
combine butter and sugars until fluffy
add egg and vanilla, beat well.
combine flour salt and baking soda in seperate bowl
add wet ingredients to the flour mixture, fold in chocolate chips and nuts.
chill for 20 min
press into greased 9x13 pan
bake for 17-20 min or until edges are golden and a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
DON'T OVERBAKE!
Allow to cool (if you can!) 15min before cutting.
Store at room temperature. Keeps well if you can resist thier chewy gooeyness when you want something sweet!

Submitter: 
Michelle Keith
Description: 
All the best of a warm chocolate chip cookie... in a bar!
