Title:
Better-Than-Anything Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box German Chocolate Cake Mix
1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
2 cups caramel topping, homemade or otherwise
1 container (8 oz) frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 cup chopped Butterfinger's candy bar

Directions:
1 Heat oven to 350°F (325°F for dark or nonstick pan). Make and bake cake as directed on box for 13x9-inch pan. Cool 15 minutes.
2 Poke top of warm cake every 1/2 inch with handle end of wooden spoon. Drizzle condensed milk evenly over top of cake; let stand until milk has been absorbed into cake. Drizzle with caramel topping. Run knife around sides of pan to loosen cake. Cover and refrigerate about 2 hours or until chilled.
3 Spread whipped topping over top of cake. Sprinkle chopped candy bar on top. Store covered in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
grainfreegirl96
Description: 
Simple and delicious!! Everyone loves this cake!! It is very easy to throw together for company or a party. Any type of candy bar can be substituted for the Butterfinger. Hope you enjoy:)
