Title:
Mushroom Lovers Fantasy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975960.jpg
Ingredients:
1 clove garlic, minced
2 tablespoons oil
2 cups sliced mushrooms (combination of portabello, shitake, white or brown)
1/4 cup dry white wine
1 (10 ounce) package DI GIORNO Alfredo Sauce
1 (9 ounce) package DI GIORNO Portabello Mushroom Tortelloni

Directions:
Cook and stir garlic in oil in skillet on medium-high heat 1 minute.  Add mushrooms and wine; cook 1 to 2 minutes or until mushrooms are tender.  Reduce heat to medium-low.  Stir in sauce; cook until thoroughly heated.
Toss with hot pasta.
