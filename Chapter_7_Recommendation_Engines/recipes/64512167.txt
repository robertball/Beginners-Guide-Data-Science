Title:
Slow Cooker Spaghetti and Mozzarella Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2756237.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 lb italian sausage
1/2 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1 cup italian bread crumbs
1/4 cup parmesan
2 eggs
1/2 cup whole milk
1/2 cup parsley
mozzarella cheese
marinara sauce

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except the mozzarella and marinara in a bowl.
Roll meat mix into balls.
Cut mozzarella into 1/2 cubes.
Stuff 1 cube mozzarella into each meatball.
Pour layer of marinara into slow cooker, place layer of meatballs, cover in marinara layer, continue until out of meatballs, top with marinara.
Slow cook on high for 2-2.5 hours.
