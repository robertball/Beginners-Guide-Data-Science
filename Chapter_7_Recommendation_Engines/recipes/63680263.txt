Title:
Tropical Blend Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 mangoes, halved, seeded, & peeled
3 kiwi fruit, peeled
1/2 small pineapple, peeled & halved
1/2c. fresh mint leaves
1c. crushed ice

Directions:
Process mangoes, kiwi fruit, pineapple, & mint through Juice Fountain™ using speeds 2 & 5.
Scoop ice into 4 glasses, pour in juice, mix well to combine. Serve Immediately.

Submitter:
