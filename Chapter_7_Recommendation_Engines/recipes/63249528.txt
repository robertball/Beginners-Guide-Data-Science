1/4 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar (packed)
1/3 cup soy sauce
2/3 cup brandy
1 1/3 tablespoons prepared mustard
3 (8 ounce) packages chicken drummettes	 (the little chicken legs..haha)
Directions:
1
In saucepan melt butter and mustard; add brown sugar until dissolved. Add soy, brandy, and stir until warm and thoroughly blended.
2
Place chicken in a 9 x 12 pan. Pour mixture over drummettes, and bake until brown at 275° for 3-4 hours. Baste about every 45 minutes.

Directions:
1
In saucepan melt butter and mustard; add brown sugar until dissolved. Add soy, brandy, and stir until warm and thoroughly blended.
2
Place chicken in a 9 x 12 pan. Pour mixture over drummettes, and bake until brown at 275° for 3-4 hours. Baste about every 45 minutes.
3
Transfer to electric skillet to cook down sauce.

Submitter: 
Carol Roberts
Description: 
All day recipe.  Cook and baste the four hours as the recipe calls for.  Then transfer to electric fry pan to cook down and thicken the sauce.
