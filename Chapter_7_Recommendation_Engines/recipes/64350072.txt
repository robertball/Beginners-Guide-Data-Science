Title:
Pumpkin Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg spice cake mix
1/2 cup oil
1/2 cup water
1 cup pumpkin
3 eggs, beaten
3 oz pkg instant vanilla pudding mix
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In large bowl, mix all ingredients (except walnuts) and beat with electric mixer for 5 minutes.
Fold in walnuts.  Pour into greased and floured bundt pan.
Bake for 50 minutes or until done.  Cool 20 minutes in pan, then invert on a rack.
Sprinkle with confectioner's sugar or frost with glaze.
