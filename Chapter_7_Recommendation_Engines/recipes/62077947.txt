Title:
Suzy Q icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c powdered sugar
1/2 c veg oil
1/2 c shortening
1/2 c butter
1 egg white
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Mix powdered sugar, oil, butter, and shortening for 2 minutes. Set aside. Beat egg white and vanilla until fluffy. Fold into sugar mixture. Ready to use.

Submitter: 
Jamie Beth
Description: 
So creamy and delicious. It's dangerous!!! Tastes so good inside chocolate cupcakes.
