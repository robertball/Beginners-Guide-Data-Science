Title:
Goat Cheese and Olive Tapenade Flat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 6 ox package of honey infused goat cheese- room temperature
1- jar of black olive tapenade
1- flat bread such as focaccia or naan- herb infused is best

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 250 degrees
2. On a pan sprayed with olive oil place the bread. Spread evenly with goat cheese.
3. Bake in oven for 12-15 minutes until cheese is warm and smooth- not hot.
4.Spoon olive tapenade over entire loaf.
5. Cut with a pizza cutter into desired servings and serve immediately.
