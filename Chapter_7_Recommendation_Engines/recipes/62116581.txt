Title:
Juicy Onion Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef, no leaner than 85%
2 medium onions
2 big garlic cloves
1/2 cup flat-leaf parsley
2 teaspoons salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Rough chop the onion and put in the food processor with the garlic.  Pulse several times. Add parsley and continue to pulse until you have a fine mince but not a puree. In a large bowl, add beef and sprinkle over salt and pepper.  Add onion mixture and mix well.  Make 8 big patties and grill over medium-high heat for several minutes each side.

Submitter: 
Noor
Description: 
Simple ingredients make one great burger!  Don't forget to butter and toast your roll to make the burger taste even better.
