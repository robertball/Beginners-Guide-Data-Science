Title:
Alysha's Blue Cheese Pecan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
½ cup crumbled good blue cheese
3 cups whole pecans

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, melt the butter and blue cheese together.  Stir in pecans, and cook until they are coated. Spread nuts on a cookie sheet and bake in the oven at 350º for approximately 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
PAMMMY
Description: 
These savory nuts are tangy from the blue cheese.  Great for parties!  These are so good, I had to submit the recipe!
