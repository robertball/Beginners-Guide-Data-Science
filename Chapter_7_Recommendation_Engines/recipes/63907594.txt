Title:
Crispy Spring Veggie Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup grated celeriac (celery root)
1 cup grated fennel
2 tablespoons grated onion
2 cups spinach
1 / 2 teaspoon lemon zest
1 tablespoon chopped sage
1 large egg
2 tablespoons brown rice flour
1 / 3 cup bread crumbs *
2 teaspoons olive oil

Directions:
Place vegetables in a large bowl. Finely chop spinach, and add to vegetables with lemon zest, sage, egg, flour, and bread crumbs. Toss to combine.
In a large skillet over medium heat, heat olive oil. Using an ice cream scoop 1/4 cup, spoon vegetable mixture into pan. Allow about 1 inch between each vegetable cake and let cook 2 to 3 minutes. Flip and cook an additional 2 to 3 minutes on the other side. Cakes should be brown and crispy on each side and warm and tender in the center, but cooked through.
Serve warm.
