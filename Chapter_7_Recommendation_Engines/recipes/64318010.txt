Title:
Tasty Pecan Pepper Appetizer Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
4 cups finely chopped pecans
2 cups finely shredded cheese
1 cup finely chopped green onion
Mayo enough to hold it together
Large jar of Raspberry Jalape√±o jelly

Directions:
Chop the chopped pecans further in small blender or chopper. Mix pecans, cheese, onion and add enough mayo to hold it together, approximately 1/2 to 1 cup.  Spread on large plate and top with jelly.  Serve with crackers.
