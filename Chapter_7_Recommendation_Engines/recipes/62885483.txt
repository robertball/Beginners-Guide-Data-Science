Title:
Moong/Mung Dal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup green moong dal
1 tomato
Salt
½ tsp red chilli powder
½ tsp tumeric powder
1 small red onion diced
1 green chilli diced
2 cloves of garlic chopped length ways
Thumb size ginger finely chopped or grated
2 tbsp garlic grated or chopped finely

Directions:
In pressure cooker add in washed moong dal and 2 cups of water
Add in diced tomato, salt, green chili and tumeric
Pressure cook for 6 whistles
Let cool
Open cooker
Over medium heat, whisk the dal until the dal grains are mashed up
Add in red chilli powder, taste for salt after mixing
In a small sauce pan, heat up oil , add in garlic and when golden brown add to moong dal
Add more water if the dal is too thick
Should be a  thick soup consistency.

Description: 
This is one of the healthier dals/lentils out there. This tastes really great and makes a great meal.
