Title:
Cheesy Bacon Crescents Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984821.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) can Pillsbury速 refrigerated crescent dinner rolls or Pillsbury速 Crescent Recipe Creations速 refrigerated seamless dough sheet
8 slices cooked bacon, cut in half
1/2 cup shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese blend
2 tablespoons mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/4 cup chopped tomato

Directions:
Separate or cut dough into 4 long rectangles (if using crescent rolls, press perforations to seal). Place bacon on center of each rectangle.
In bowl, mix cheese and mayonnaise. Spoon cheese mixture over bacon; sprinkle with tomato. Fold short sides of dough to center of each rectangle; press edges to seal. Place seam side down on ungreased cookie sheet; press short sides with fork to seal. Cut 2 slits on top.
Bake at 375 degrees F 12 to 15 minutes.
