Title:
Basil-Buttered French Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986516.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
4 (1 inch thick) slices French bread

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine butter and basil. Brush butter mixture over one side of each bread slice. Place buttered side up on an ungreased baking sheet. Bake, uncovered, at 400 degrees F for 5 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Dixie  Terry
Description: 
Dixie Terry makes this warm bread when she wants to round out quick dinners at her home in Goreville, Illinois. 'Just pop it in a hot oven for a few minutes to have it ready when the rest of your meal is served,' she notes
