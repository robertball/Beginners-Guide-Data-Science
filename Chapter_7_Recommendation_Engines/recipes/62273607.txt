Title:
Pumpkin Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5 Cups Chicken Broth
1 Can Pumpkin
.5 Onion, chopped
1 Clove Garlic, minced
1 tsp Cajun seasoning
1/2 Cup Heavy Cream

Directions:
Mix together broth, pumpkin, onion, Cajun seasoning,  and garlic in a saucepan.  Heat to boiling, then lower heat and simmer for 45 minutes to an hour, stirring every 15 minutes.  Add cream at the very end, stirring until mixed.  Serve warm.
