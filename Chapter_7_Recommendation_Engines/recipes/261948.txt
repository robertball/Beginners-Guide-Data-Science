Title:
Low-Carb Bacon Lettuce Turkey Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5009375.jpg
Ingredients:
4 large lettuce leaves
1/4 cup mayonnaise
8 slices bacon
12 slices deli smoked turkey
4 slices Swiss cheese
(8-inch) lengths of baking string

Directions:
Dry lettuce leaves on paper towels. Spread each with 1 tablespoon mayonnaise.
Place bacon between paper towels on a microwave-safe plate. Heat in the microwave until crispy, 4 to 6 minutes.
Top each lettuce leaf with 3 slices turkey, 2 slices bacon, and 1 slice Swiss cheese. Roll up and tie each wrap with a length of baking string.

Tim Unger
Description: 
A gluten-free version of this popular lunch meal.
