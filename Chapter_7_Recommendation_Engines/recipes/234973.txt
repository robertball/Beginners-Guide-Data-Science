Title:
Delicious Chicken Fajita Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6494529.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1/3 cup orange juice
1/4 cup olive oil
1 lime, juiced
3 cloves garlic, crushed
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Whisk cilantro, orange juice, olive oil, lime juice, garlic, salt, cayenne pepper, cumin, and black pepper together in a bowl.

Submitter: 
SandraJ
Description: 
Delicious fajita marinade for chicken or beef. Makes enough marinade for about 2 pounds of meat. Try it with shrimp too, just reduce the marinade time from 2 hours to 30 minutes.
