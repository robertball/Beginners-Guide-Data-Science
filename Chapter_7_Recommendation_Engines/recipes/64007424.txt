Title:
All Purpose Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1288437.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c all purpose flour (7 1/2oz)
1 cup cornmeal (5oz)
2 tsp baking powder
3/4 tsp table salt
1/4 tsp baking soda
1 cup buttermilk
3/4 c frozen corn, thawed
1/4 c light brown sugar, packed (1 3/4oz)
2 large eggs
8 T (1 stick) butter, melted and cooled

Directions:
Spray Pyrex 8x8 glass pan - oven to 400, rack in middle position.
Whisk flour, cornmeal, baking powder, baking soda and salt in medium bowl.
In food processor, process corn kernels, brown sugar and buttermilk until combined (5 sec).  Add eggs and process until well combined, another 5 seconds longer.
Make a well in center of dry ingredients and pour the wet into well.  Fold dry into the wet, giving mix only a few turns to barely combine.
Add melted butter and continue folding until just moistened.
Pour batter into baking dish, smooth surface with spatula, and bake until golden brown (25-35 minutes).
Cool in pan on rack 10 min; removed from pan and cool another 10 minutes longer.

Submitter:

Description: 
Flavor enhanced by the addition of pureed corn kernels, buttermilk, and light brown sugar.  If baking in conventional heated cast iron skillet pan, reduce heat to 350.
