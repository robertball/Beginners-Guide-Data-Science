Title:
Dilly Blue Corn on the Cob Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5532242.jpg
Ingredients:
6 ears corn, husks and silk removed
4 Tbsp. unsalted butter, softened
2 tsp. dried dill
2/3 cup crumbled blue cheese

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
2. Combine dried dill with softened butter forming a paste. Rub each corn evenly with paste and wrap individually in aluminum foil. Arrange corn on a baking sheet.
3. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes.
4. Remove corn from oven and carefully open each aluminum packet. Sprinkle each corn evenly with crumbled blue cheese. Return to oven and bake an additional 5 minutes or until melted.

Submitter: 
Chef Mo
Description: 
Amaze your guests with this simple roasted, but over the top corn on the cob.
