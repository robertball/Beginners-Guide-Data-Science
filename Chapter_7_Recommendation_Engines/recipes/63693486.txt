Title:
Lobster Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Kosher salt
3 pound lobster meat
1 celery stalk, finely chopped
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives
2Â–3 tablespoons olive oil miracle whip
6 wheat rolls or Italian bread rolls
2 tablespoons unsalted butter, room temperature

Directions:
Try to locate fresh lobster meat, it has already been decalwed and makes this process so much quicker, go to your local fish or seafood market and just request "lobster meat" .
Mix lobster, celery, lemon juice, chives, and 2 tablespoons mayonnaise in a medium bowl; season with salt and pepper and add more miracle whip, if desired.
Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Spread flat sides of buns. Cook until golden, about 2 minutes per side; fill with lobster mixture.
