Title:
Butternut Squash Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Red Onion (chopped)
1 Jalapeno Pepper (seeded and chopped)
2T. Olive oil
4 Cloves Garlic (minced)
1T. Cumin (ground)
1T. Tomato Paste
2 Lbs. Butternut Squash ( peeled,seeded and 1/2 " cubed)
1 14.5oz can Tomatoes (petite dice)
6 C. Chicken Broth
2 C. Cilantro (fresh and chopped)
Tortilla Chips
Cooked Chicken (shredded)
Feta Cheese (crumbled)
Avocado (diced)
Sour Cream
Lime Wedges

Directions:
Saute onion and Jalapeno in oil over med. heat for 5min. or till tender.
Add garlic, cumin, and tomato paste; saute 2 min.
Add Butternut squash and tomatoes and cook 10 min. stir often
Add chicken broth; bring to boil, reduce heat and simmer 20 min.
Add Cilantro and remove from heat
Top with chicken, tortilla chips, cheese, avocado, and/or sour cream.
Lime wedges on the side.

Submitter: 
Mad Butcher
Description: 
Southwest flair with multiple options. Don't second guess the cumin and cilantro it is a lot but needed. Jalapeno is only mild with this amount.
