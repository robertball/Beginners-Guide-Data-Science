Title:
Banana Poke Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Box Yellow Cake Mix
2 3.4 oz Instant Banana Pudding
4 Cups Milk
1 8 oz tub Cool Whip
20 Vanilla Wafers Crushed
Sliced fresh Bananas

Directions:
Prepare cake mix according to directions for a 9X13 cake. Once cake comes out of oven, allow to cool for just a couple
of minutes. with a wooden spoon handle begin poking holes in the cake.  In a bowl whisk together instant pudding with 4 cups milk. Stir til all the lumps are gone.  Let pudding sit for 2 minutes.  Pour pudding over cake get in holes. Refrigerate. when cool spread cool whip over cake. Serve with fresh sliced Bananas. Keep refrigeratored.
