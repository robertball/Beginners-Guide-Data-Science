Title:
Sweet Potato Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Krusteaz Belgian Waffle Mix
1/2 cup oat flour
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup mashed sweet potato
1 egg
1 tbsp oil
3/4 cup water

Directions:
Heat electric fry pan or griddle to 350 degrees.
Mix all ingredients just until combined and moistened.
Cook on heated griddle.
Serve with maple syrup or other toppings as desired.
