Title:
Spinach and Feta Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs, beaten
2 Tablespoons Worchestire sauce
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup chopped onion
6 ounces feta cheese
1 (10 ounce) box frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
2 pounds ground turkey

Directions:
Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil grate.
Saute garlic, onion and spinach until onions are translucent.
While the grill is preheating, mix together eggs, Worchestire sauce, onion mixture, feta cheese, and turkey in a large bowl until well combined; form into 8 patties.
Cook on preheated grill until no longer pink in the center, 15 to 20 minutes.
