Title:
Great Grandma's Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter (room temp)
1 cup sugar
2 eggs (room temp)
1 cup flour
1/2 cup butter milk
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
4 large very ripe bananas, mashed
1 Tbs. melted butter
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. sugar

Directions:
1. preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. Cream together sugar and butter
3. beat in eggs, one at a time, beating well after each
4. sift together flour,salt and baking soda
5. add half flour mixture to butter mixture, alturnating with the butter milk
6. stir in bananas
7. let the dough rest, covered for 20 minutes
8. bake bread until tooth pick inserted comes out clean, about 50 minutes
9. when bread comes out of oven brush with melted butter and sprinkle the cinnamon and sugar on top

Submitter:

Description: 
This is my great grandmothers recipe and it makes the BEST banana bread! Very tender, very moist and VERY good! ENJOY!
