Title:
Avocado, Blueberry, Banana, and Chia Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3498426.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup vanilla-flavored almond milk
1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and halved
1 cup fresh blueberries
1 banana
1 cup ice
1 tablespoon chia seeds

Directions:
Combine almond milk, avocado, blueberries, banana, ice, and chia seeds in a blender; blend until smooth.
