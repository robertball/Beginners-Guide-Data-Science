Title:
Luscious Coconut Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package butter cake mix, prepsred
1 (8-oz.) package cream cheese, room temperature
1/2 cup powdered sugar
Â¼ cup milk
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tsp. coconut flavoring
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 (12-oz.) tub whipped topping, thawed
2 cups shredded coconut

Directions:
Prepare cake mix according to package directions for 2 8-inch cake pans; cool.
Prepare frosting by combining cream cheese, sugar, milk, lemon juice, flavoring and vanilla with an electric mixer until smooth.
Gently fold in whipped topping with a spatula; chill until ready to frost cake.
Frost cake and coat with coconut. Store cake in refrigerator until serving time.

Submitter: 
Melissa Dommert
Description: 
This rich, moist cake is creamy coconut perfection!
