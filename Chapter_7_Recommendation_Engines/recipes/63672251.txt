Title:
Dark Chocolate Heart Healthy Cookies (Gluten Free/ Vegan) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1029401.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons Chia seeds
3 Tablespoons Flax Seeds
1 1/2 cup hot water
1 teaspoon vanilla
4 ounces Tofu
1/2 cup agave
1/2 cup cacao nibs
1/2 cup warm water
1/2 cup almond meal flour
1 cup millet flour
3/4 cup cacao powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup raw sugar
1 cup dried raspberries
Cacao nibs for topping cookies

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
Soak Chia and Flax Seeds in hot water in blender for 10 minutes.
Blend mixture on high.
Add tofu,vanilla, agave, cacao nibs, water, blend in high speed blender until well mixed.
In a large bowl, mix almond meal flour, millet flour, raspberries, cacao powder, baking soda, salt and sugar.
Stir in raspberries.
Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients.
Spoon out cookies onto parchment paper.
Top cookies with a few nibs.
Bake for 12 minutes.

Description: 
These healthy treats are rich in antioxidants. Dark chocolate increases blood flow to the brain as well as to the heart, so it can help improve cognitive function. Dark chocolate also helps reduce your risk of stroke.
