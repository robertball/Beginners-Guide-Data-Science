Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Broth Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c (2 med ribs) celery, coarsely chopped
1 c (2 med) carrots, coarsely chopped
1 c (2 med) onions, coarsely chopped
2 tsp fresh thyme, chopped
2 tsp salt or to taste
1/4 tsp pepper
one 3- 3 1/2  broiler/fryer chicken, cut up

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a 6 quart cooker.  Cook on low 6-8 hours.  Remove chicken and vegetables from broth, cool.  Strain through cheesecloth or lined colander.  Discard chicken bones, chicken skin & vegetables.  Refrigerate chicken and broth separately to use as desired.
