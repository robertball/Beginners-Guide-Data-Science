Title:
Fabulous Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T. butter
1 small red pepper (optional)
1 c. diced fresh mushrooms
1 c. frozen peas
1/4 c. flour
2 1/2 c.milk
2c. shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 lg. can albacore tuna
12 oz. egg noodles cooked
1/2 t. salt
1/2 t. pepper

Directions:
Heat water for noodles.  Meanwhile melt butter in lg. skillet.  Add bell pepper, onion, and mushrooms, and saute 5 min. or until tender.
Whisk together flour and milk; stir into veggies.  Cook, stiring constantly til thickened.
Cook noodles intermittently.
Add cheese to veggies.
Drain noodles.  Add creamy veggie mixture to noodles.
Add tuna, salt and pepper, and frozen peas.
Spoon into casserole dish.
Bake covered at 350 for 25 minutes.

Description: 
Creamy delicious blend of mushrooms, tuna, peas and egg noodles.  A personal favorite.
