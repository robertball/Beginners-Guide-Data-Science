Title:
Heaven Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
L1	2 C. Flour
2 sticks butter
1 C. chopped pecans or walnuts
L2	8 oz. Cream cheese
2 C. pdr. Sugar
2 C. cool whip
L3	1 sm. pkg. instant chocolate pudding mix
1 sm. pkg. instant vanilla pudding mix
3 C. cold milk
L4	8 oz. Cool whip
1 chocolate bar, chilled

Directions:
Layer 1	Mix ingredients in med MB
Press into BD. Bake for 20 min. at 325Â°
Allow to cool before adding 2nd layer.
Layer 2	Blend cream cheese and pdr. sugar in med. MB until creamy.
Fold in cool whip. Spread over cooled crust.
Layer 3 Beat puddings and milk in med. MB for 3 min.
Pour and spread over cream cheese mixture.
Layer 4	Spread cool whip over pudding.
Grate chocolate bar over cool whip.
Refrigerate. Serve after 1 hour or so.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
A nutty crust topped with layers of cool whip, cream cheese, and chocolate/vanilla pudding.
