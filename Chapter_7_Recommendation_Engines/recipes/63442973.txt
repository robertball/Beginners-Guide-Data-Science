Title:
Best Chocolate Bar loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/934330.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
1/2 C. Cocoa powder
1 C. Granulated sugar
2 tsp. Baking Powder
1 tsp. Baking Soda
1 C. Boiling water
3 Eggs
1/2 Ritter Sport (cocoa mousse bar) German chocolate

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
Slice chocolate into narrow slices and set aside
In a large mixing bowl blend dry ingredients together well.
Add the eggs and boiling water beating with an electric mixer until mixture is smooth and creamy
Stir in chocolate pieces and combine them well into the batter.
Put into a greased 9" by 5" by 3" loaf pan and set in the oven
Bake for 35 to 40 minutes testing with a toothpick to make sure it is done in the middle.

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very chocolate cake loaf that has bits of German chocolate in the middle to make it very decadent.
