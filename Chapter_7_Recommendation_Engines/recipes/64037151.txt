Title:
One Pot Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. pasta of your choice (except spaghetti)
15 oz diced tomato with liquid
1 large onion cut up
4 cloves garlic
2 tsp. oregano
2 sprigs basil
4 1/2 cups vegetable broth (low sodium)
2 tbs. olive oil
1/2 tsp. red pepper flakes

Directions:
1. Place pasta, tomatoes, garlic, onion and basil in pot.
2. Pour in broth
3. Sprinkle with pepper and oregano
4. Drizzle top with oil
5. Cover bring to a boil and simmer 10 minutes stirring constantly.

Submitter: 
Helen Lang Rosenbaum
Description: 
This is the easiest pasta recipe I have ever made.  My favorite pasta to use with this recipe is gemelli and penne.  Also, don't limit yourself to just the pasta, you can also add 1lb of chicken or sausage to make it a true one pot meal!
