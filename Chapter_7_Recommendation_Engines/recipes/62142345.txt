Title:
CROCKPOT PINTO BEANS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb dry pinto beans, soaked overnight
2 slices (or equal to about a pound) black forest (or any deli type) ham - cube style
1 onion
2 celery ribs
3 garlic cloves
¾  green bell pepper, diced
2 bay leaves
1  2 tsp. cumin
1  2 Tbls. oregano
1 Tbls.  lard (or manteca)
5 C. chicken broth (enough to cover)

Directions:
Place vegetables in slow cooker first, followed by ham, then the beans, the seasoning, bay leaves, and the lard.
Pour chicken broth over the top.
Cook on high for 5-6 hours.

Submitter: 
Corena
Description: 
These beans turn out fabulous in the crockpot! I developed this recipe after finding too many hard beans aftter cooking with a pot on the stove.
