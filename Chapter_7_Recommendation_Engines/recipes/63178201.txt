Title:
Kale and Mushroom Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/816952.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small yellow onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced (I used garlic from refrigerated can)
Dash of salt and freshly ground black pepper
2 cups kale (inner stem removed) and chopped?
1 cup mushrooms, sliced?
Olive oil?
1 ½ tsp Herbs de Provence (or substitute dried rosemary, thyme, etc.)
¾ cup Egg Beaters Eggs or 4 large eggs
¾ cup Egg Beaters Egg Whites or 4 large egg whites
½ cup lite Jarlsberg or Swiss cheese, shredded

Directions:
1.	Preheat oven to 350.
2.	Heat non-stick skillet over medium heat with oil and sauté garlic and onion until fragrant and tender.
3.	Season with salt and pepper.
4.	Add kale and mushrooms. Sauté 10 minutes over medium low heat until tender. Add a bit more olive oil if necessary to be prevent burning.
5.	In a small bowl whisk eggs and egg whites with dried herbs and a teeny tiny bit more salt. Pour egg mixture over vegetables in skillet.
6.	Continue cooking until the frittata is set around the sides, about 8 minutes.
7.	Top with Swiss cheese and place in the oven to bake for 15 minutes.
8.	Remove from oven, cool for a minute, dig in and enjoy! Serve with toast and a fresh fruit salad for a delicious meal!
