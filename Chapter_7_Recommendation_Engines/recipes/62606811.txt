Title:
Quinoa with Lentils and Chickpeas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon vegetable (olive) oil
1 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, peeled and chopped
3/4 cup uncooked quinoa
1 1/2 cups vegetable broth
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp dried Mexican oregano
salt and pepper to taste
1 1/4 cups cooked lentils
1 (15 ounce) cans chick peas, rinsed and drained
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat.  Add onion and garlic and cook until golden brown, about 10 minutes.
Add quinoa, broth and seasonings.  Bring to boil, then reduce heat and cover.  Simmer 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Stir in lentils, chickpeas and fresh cilantro and heat through.

Description:
