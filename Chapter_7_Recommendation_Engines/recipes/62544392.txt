Title:
Baked Cauliflower Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large head of Cauliflower.
1 Large onion chopped and browned.
1 Can of sliced mushrooms, drained.
1 Can of diced chilis (optional)
1 Can diced olives.
16oz Cottage cheese
1 Cup of shredded Cheddar cheese
1 Cup of shredded Monterey Jack cheese
6 Eggs
2 Tbs of flour
1/2 Tsp of salt
1/4 Tsp of pepper
1 cup fresh bread crumbs (optional)

Directions:
1. Preheat the oven to 350F
2. Chop the Cauliflower into 1/2-inch pieces, brown the onion in a skillet. Combine the cauliflower & onions with the mushrooms, chilis, and olives, set aside.
3. Beat the eggs with the flour, stir in the cottage cheese, shredded cheeses, salt & pepper.
4. Combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl and mix very well.
5. Spray an 8" x 13" with a non-stick cooking spray, and spread the cauliflower mixture evenly on the bottom.  Sprinkle the bread crumbs on the top and bake for 60 minutes.

Submitter: 
Stan
Description: 
This makes a great breakfast entry or as a nice side dish.  Most people will not know they are eating cauliflower.
