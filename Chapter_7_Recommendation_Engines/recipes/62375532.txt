Title:
3BC (best-baked-bean casserole) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/528477.jpg
Ingredients:
28 oz of Baked Beans ( I prefer Bush's original)
2 tsp Worcestershire
1 tblsp Ketchup
1 tsp Yellow Mustard
1 c Brown Sugar (set aside ¼ c )
½ lb sliced bacon
1 small onion (red or yellow) - diced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400°F.
In a small pan, sauté diced onion in butter (you can add worcest.sauce for more flavor) on low heat for 10 to 15 min., stirring occasionaly.
Cook bacon, drain grease and cut into bite size chunks. Set aside about a third of your bacon.
In a 2 qt casserole dish mix beans, sautéd onion, ketchup, mustard, worcestershire, ¼ c brown sugar, and the third of bacon you set aside. Mix well.
Sprinkle bean mixture with the remaining bacon and top with brown sugar.
Bake for 45 min.
