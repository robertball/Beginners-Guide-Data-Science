Title:
Chicken Marsala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/277601.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup all-purpose flour for coating
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded 1/4 inch thick
4 tablespoons butter
4 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons lemon juice
2/3 cup chicken broth

Directions:
In a shallow dish or bowl, mix together the flour, salt, pepper and oregano. Coat chicken pieces in flour mixture.
In a large skillet, melt butter in oil over medium heat. Place chicken in the pan, and lightly brown.  Turn over chicken pieces. Pour in chicken broth and lemon juice.  Cover skillet; simmer chicken 10 minutes, turning once, until no longer pink and juices run clear.
