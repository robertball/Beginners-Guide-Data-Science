Title:
Dill Oyster Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c vegetable oil
1t dill weed
1/2t garlic powder
1pkg ranch dressing mix
2-12oz packages oyster crackers

Directions:
Mix all dressing ingredients and pour over crackers.
Mix well and let sit for at least an hour.
