Title:
Apple Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Granny Smith apples, thinly sliced
4 cups shredded green cabbage
4 cups shredded red cabbage
1 medium cucumber, seeded and sliced
2 large jalapeno peppers, seeded and minced
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1/3 cup honey
1/3 cup veggie oil
2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Combine apples and next 4 ingredients in a large bowl.
Whisk together vinegar and next 4 ingredients. Toss with apple mixture. Cover and chill at least 1 hour or up to 4 hours.
