Title:
Crunchy light chocolate chips cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup flour
1/2 t baking soda
1/4 salt
1/2 cup softened butter
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 t vanilla
2 cups Rice Krispies
1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Sift flour, baking soda, salt & set aside.
In a large bowl, beat butter and sugar for 4 minutes or until creamy.  Add egg and vanilla and beat well.  Add sifted dry ingredients and mix until combined.  Stir in Rice Krispies and chocolate chips until combined.
Drop by level teaspoon onto baking sheet.
Bake 12 minutes or until lightly browned.
Remove immediately from cookie sheet and cool on wire rack.
(Even better - after cooled - put in large zip lock bag and freeze and eat straight from the freezer).
