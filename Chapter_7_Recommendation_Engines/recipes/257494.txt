Title:
Strawberry Watermelon Agua Fresca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4518993.jpg
Ingredients:
2 chunks fresh watermelon, seeds removed, or more to taste
2 fresh strawberries, hulled, or more to taste
2 packets Wholesome Organic Stevia, or more to taste
2 basil leaves
Ice
Sparkling water to taste

Directions:
Muddle fresh fruit, Wholesome Organic Stevia and basil.
Top with ice and sparkling water.

Submitter: 
Wholesome
Description: 
This refreshing blend of strawberries and watermelon is sweetened with organic stevia and finished with sparkling water and ice. A perfect drink for a hot summer day.
