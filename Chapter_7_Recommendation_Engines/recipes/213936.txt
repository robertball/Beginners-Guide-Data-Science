Title:
Caprese Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989449.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Sargento® Reduced Sodium String Cheese Snacks
24 grape tomatoes
24 fresh basil leaves
Extra virgin olive oil, optional (optional)

Directions:
Cut each cheese snack crosswise into 4 pieces. Alternate on skewers with tomatoes and basil. Drizzle with oil, if desired.

Submitter: 
Sargento®
Description: 
These skewers make for a great party snack. Simply combine Reduced Sodium string cheese, grape tomatoes, and basil leaves.
