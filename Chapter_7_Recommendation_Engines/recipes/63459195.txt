Title:
Dark Chocolate Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 oz. semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 can sweetened condensed milk
dash salt
1 tsp. vanilla extract
chopped nuts (optional, between 1/2 and 1 cup)

Directions:
Melt chocolate chips with condensed milk and salt in saucepan over low heat. Remove from heat, stir in vanilla and nuts if desired. Pour into foil lined 8x8 or 9x9 pan. Cool in refrigerator until firm and cut into squares.

Submitter: 
Cupcake
Description: 
Quick and easy fudge without the marshmallow fluff.
