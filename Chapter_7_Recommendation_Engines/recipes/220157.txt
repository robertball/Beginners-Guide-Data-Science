Title:
Delicious Ranch Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5931535.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup mayonnaise made with olive oil
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 teaspoon minced garlic
2 teaspoons chopped fresh tarragon
2 teaspoons chopped fresh basil
2 teaspoons chopped fresh cilantro
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix mayonnaise and buttermilk together in a bowl until smooth.
Stir in garlic, tarragon, basil, cilantro, salt, and black pepper.
For best flavor, refrigerate until chilled, at least 1 hour.
