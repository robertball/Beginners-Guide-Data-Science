Title:
Hearty Ham, Barley, Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/748006.jpg
Ingredients:
2 litres chicken broth
1 large ham steak, cooked and cubed
3 - 4 russet potatoes, peeled, diced
1 large can diced tomatoes in juice
1 medium onion, chopped
2 medium carrots, peeled, chopped
1 cup chopped celery
2 cups cut green beans (fresh or frozen, NOT canned)
3/4 cup pearl barley, rinsed
2 teaspoons dried oregano

Directions:
Bring broth to boil in large pot. add cooked ham (cubed), reduce heat to medium-low and simmer 15 minutes.
Add diced potatoes, tomatoes with juices, onion, carrots, celery, beans, barley, and oregano. Bring to boil.
Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer uncovered until meat and barley are tender, about 1 hour.
Season soup to taste with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Catherine Parnell-Proulx
Description: 
This is an easy and delicious soup - ideal for beginners.  It can be made quickly and eaten right away - perfect for a weekday meal.
