Title:
Easy wholewheat bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup warm water (110 to 115 F)
2 tbs. vegetable oil
2 tbs. sugar
1 tsp. salt
1 cup white bread flour
1 cup wholewheat flour
1 tbs. powdered egg substitute (optional)
1 tsp. rapid or quick yeast

Directions:
If you're using the egg substitue, mix that well with the warm water first.
Add ingredients into your bread machine as suggested by the manufacturer.
Select "wholewheat" and then "medium" with a "regular" bake time on your  bread machine.
After 5 minutes check how the dough is kneading, it may need either 1 tbs. of flour or 1 tbs. of water added, depending on the consistency.
Once the bread is done, allow it time to cool on a wire rack before cutting.

Submitter: 
Petal
Description: 
An easy basic recipe for wholewheat bread that is great for sandwiches and for toasting!
