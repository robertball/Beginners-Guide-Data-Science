Title:
Marshmallow Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups sugar
3/4 cup evaporated milk
1 1/2 cups miniature white marshmallows
1/4 cup butter (no substitutes)
1/4 tsp. salt
1 cup chocolate chips (I prefer dark)
1/3 cup chopped nuts, your choice
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
In large heavy saucepan, combine the first 5 ingredients.
Cook and stir over med. heat until mixture comes to a boil.
Boil and stir 5 minutes longer.
Remove from heat.
Stir in chocolate chips until melted.
Stir in nuts and vanilla.
Spread in a buttered 8" x 8" pan.
Cool.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Quick, easy fudge that can be altered with different chocolate darkness, nut types and additions.
