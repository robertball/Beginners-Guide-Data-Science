Title:
Sweet Smoky Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/993441.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Granny Smith apples, cut into thin slices
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/2 cup soft cream cheese with honey and nuts
8 slices whole grain bread
12 ounces thinly sliced smoked turkey breast

Directions:
TOSS apple slices with lemon juice; drain.
SPREAD cream cheese evenly on 1 side of each bread slice; top 4 slices evenly with apple slices and turkey.
TOP with remaining bread slices, cream cheese side down.

Submitter: 
Southern Living magazine
Description: 
Cream cheese and apples add a different twist to the turkey sandwich.
