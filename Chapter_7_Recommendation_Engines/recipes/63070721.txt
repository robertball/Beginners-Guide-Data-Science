Title:
Strawberry Pretzel Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886734.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups pretzels, crushed
3/4 cup butter, melted
3 tablespoons sugar
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup sugar
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
2 (3 ounce) packages strawberry flavored gelatin
2 cups boiling water
2 (10 ounce) packages frozen strawberries

Directions:
Stir together crushed pretzels, melted butter and 3 tablespoons sugar; mix well and press mixture into the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Bake at 400ยบ for 8 to 10 minutes, until set.  Set aside to cool.
In a large mixing bowl cream together cream cheese and 1 cup sugar.  Fold in whipped topping.  Spread mixture onto cooled crust.
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water.  Stir in still frozen strawberries and allow to set briefly.  When mixture is about the consistency of egg whites, pour and spread over cream cheese layer.
Refrigerate until set.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
A pretzel crust topped with a sweet cream cheese layer and a strawberry topping.Modified from: Strawberry Pretzel Salad
