Title:
Oreo™  Cookie Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) package chocolate sandwich cookies (5 packages)
1/2 cup butter
3 cups whipping cream, whipped with a bit of powdered sugar
1 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
1 (3.5 ounce - 100 grs.) package chocolate pudding mix
milk

Directions:
Crush 4 1/4 packages cookies into fine crumbs.  Reserve 3/4 package for top (cut into bite size pieces).  Melt butter and mix with rest of cookies.  Press into 9x13 pan.  Put in freezer for 5 minutes.
Blend 1/2 of the whipped cream, all of the cream cheese and confectioners' sugar. Spread over crust and place cake back in freezer.
Prepare pudding with the milk according to package directions then spread over top of cake. Spread the remaining whipped topping on top of the pudding then sprinkle with the remaining cookies. Keep cake refrigerated.
