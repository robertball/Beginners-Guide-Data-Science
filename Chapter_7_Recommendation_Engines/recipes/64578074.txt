Title:
Easy Tom Kha Gai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chicken broth
2 cans coconut milk
2 Tbsp minced fresh ginger
1 1/2 tsp Sriracha
1-2 chicken breasts sliced into very thin strips
2 Tbsp lime juice
1 lime grated for the rind
1/4 of a lemon grated  for the rind
1 med-large tomato cut into small cubes
1 Tbsp brown sugar
1 Tbsp fish sauce
cilantro

Directions:
Bring the broth and coconut milk to a boil and add the ginger and Sriracha  and simmer 10 mins, add the chicken and  simmer till chicken cooked. Add lime juice and the lemon and lime rinds and tomato.  Taste and add more ginger or Sriracha if necessary.
When you are ready to serve, add the brown sugar, fish sauce and cilantro to taste.
Eat right away or refrigerate for  the flavors to be enhanced.
