Title:
Tequila Sunrise (Cuervo Sunrise) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992446.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Jose Cuervo Especial® Tequila
1/2 ounce grenadine
3 ounces orange juice
1 slice orange

Directions:
Pour Jose Cuervo Especial(R) Tequila, grenadine, and orange juice into a cocktail glass halfway filled with ice.
Stir well and garnish with a slice of orange.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
When creating the archetypical tequila drink, get authentic flavor with the gold standard in tequila, Jose Cuervo Especial® tequila.
