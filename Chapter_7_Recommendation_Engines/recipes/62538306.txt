Title:
Slow-Cooker Beef Tips & Mushrooms in Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/522540.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds cubed beef tips
1 tsp. Adolf's Meat Tenderizer
1 onion, chopped
1 (8 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
1 (8 ounce) can sliced water chestnuts, drained
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's Healthy Request® condensed mushroom soup
1/2 cup skim milk
2 Tbsp cornstarch
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1 Tbsp Heinz worcestershire sauce
1 tsp La Choy® soy sauce
1 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Sprinkle Adolf's Tenderizer on bite-sized sirloin tips then put the sirloin tips, chopped onion, mushrooms, and water chestnuts in the crockpot of a slow cooker. In a separate bowl mix the soup, milk, cornstarch, balsamic vinegar, worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, and pepper. Pour the mixture over the beef and stir. Cook on Low for 8 hours, or on High for 4 hours. Serve over rice or noodles.

Submitter: 
Tracy Ann

This hearty beef with mushroom gravy is really easy to make with the help of your crockpot.  Serve over rice or noodles.Modified from: Slow Cooker Beef and Mushrooms
