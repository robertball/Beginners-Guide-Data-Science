Title:
Hamburger Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pounds lean ground beef
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
2 1/2 cups milk
1 Tablespoon beef bouillon
1 Tablespoon butter
1 teaspoon ground sage
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup all-purpose flour

Directions:
Brown ground beef and onion.  Drain grease from meat and remove meat from pan.  Set aside 3/4 cup of the milk and pour remaining milk in the pan.  Stir to scrape up any residue left in pan. Stir in bouillon, butter, and seasonings.  Bring to a boil and cook for 5 minutes.  Whisk flour into the 3/4 cup of milk until smooth.  Stir into the hot milk mixture.  Simmer until mixture thickens and coats the spoon.  Stir in hamburger.  Taste and adjust seasonings before serving.  This recipe can easily be doubled.

Submitter: 
Melody
Description: 
A quick, inexpensive meal that can be served over biscuits, noodles or rice.  Add a green salad or steamed broccoli for a complete meal.Modified from: Dad's Hamburger Gravy
