Title:
Lemon poppy seed cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/703086.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. lemon cake mix
1 pkg. lemon instant pudding
1 cup water
1/2 cup oil
1 tsp.lemon extract
4 eggs
1/2 cup poppy seeds
Glaze:
1 cup icing sugar
2 Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2 tsp.poppy seeds

Directions:
Grease and flour bundt pan or angel food pan.Pre heat oven to 350f.        Mix together cake mix,pudding,water,oil,lemon extract and eggs.Mix at medium high for 4 min.Add the poppy seeds.Pour into prepared pan,bake 45-50min.When done let cool completely.Tranfer to plate.
Glaze:
Mix together the icing sugar and lemon juice,add poppy seeds.Slowly pour along the top of the cake,let it drizzle down the sides.
