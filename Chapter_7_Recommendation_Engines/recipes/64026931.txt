Title:
Homemade Rye Bread (Emeril Lagasse 2005) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 oz dry yeast
1 tbs sugar
3 tbs melted butter
1 egg
1 cup warm milk (110 degrees F)
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 cup rye flour
2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 tbs caraway seed
1 tsp vegetable oil
1 large egg, beaten

Directions:
Combine yeast, sugar, melted butter, egg, and milk in bowl of mixer with dough hook. Beat on low speed for 1 min. Add salt, rye flour, all purpose flour, and caraway seeds. Beat at low speed until all of flour is incorporated. Then, beat at medium speed until mixture forms a ball. Remove dough from bowl. Use hands to smooth dough into ball. Lightly oil bowl, place dough in bowl and turn it to oil all sides.  Cover with plastic wrap and set aside in a warm. draft-free place until double in size, about 1 hour.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees and lightly grease 5 1/2 by 9 inch baking pan.
Remove dough from bowl and invert onto lightly floured surface. Gently knead the dough several times. Tuck and roll so that any seams disappear into the dough and place in prepared baking pan.  Cover with plastic wrap and set aside until double size, about 1 hour. Use pastry brush to brush egg lightly over dough. Bake about 45 min until lightly browned. Remove from oven and cool on rack.
