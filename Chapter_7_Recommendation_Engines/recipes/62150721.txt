Title:
Chocolate Raspberry Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 11.5 oz pkgs milk chocolate chips
1/2 cup raspberry preserves (best to NOT substitute jam or jelly)
1/2 cup whipping cream
Toothpicks or fondue forks
Bite sized servings of strawberries, cubed rice krispy treats, marshmallows, pound cake, etc. for dipping.

Directions:
Heat chocolate chips in microwave or double boiler until almost melted.
Add preserves and stir, continuing to cook on low heat until preserves are absorbed into chocolate mixture.
Slowly add whipping cream, stirring constantly, until mixture is at dipping consistency.
Place in fondue pot or mini-slow cooker and serve with assorted condiments.
