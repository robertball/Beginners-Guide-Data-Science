Title:
Banana  Bread - Quick Bread for Machines Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
3/4 cup white sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
splash of vanilla
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 eggs
3 bananas, peeled and halved lengthwise

Directions:
Place all ingredients in the pan of the bread machine.
Set to Quick Bread setting.
In the first few minutes of mixing, use a plastic spatula (avoiding the blade) to incorporate flour that may stick to the side.
Once cycle is complete, remove pan from breadmaker and allow adequate time to cool. Use a plastic spatula around the edges to prevent sticking.

Submitter: 
Misty Sutton
