Title:
Cheese Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound shredded cheese (swiss, jarlsberg, cheddar)
2 tbsp cornstarch
1 cup dry white wine or apple juice
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
Dash nutmeg
1 loaf bread cut into cubes
Veggies cut bite size
Apple cored and cup in wedges

Directions:
Toss cheese with cornstarch to coat.  Place in heavy saucepan over low heat.  Stir from bottom as cheese begins to melt.  When softened and smooth, gradually add wine, stirring vigorously after each addition, until wine is absorbed.  After all wine is added and fondue is creamy, stir in salt, pepper and nutmeg.  Pour into cheese fondue pot.
