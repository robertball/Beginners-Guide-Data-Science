Title:
Sweetened Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/954760.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup cornmeal
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 cup sour cream or greek yogurt
1/3 cup milk
1/4 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (205 degrees C). Lightly grease an 8-inch square baking dish.
Combine wet ingredients in a medium bowl. Add dry ingredients and stir until just fully incorporated. Pour into prepared baking dish.
Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes. Serve warm.
