Title:
Peach Strawberry Surprise Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup frozen peaches
4 frozen or fresh strawberries
1/4 cup frozen or fresh mango
1/3 cup orange-pineapple juice
1/4 cup plain yogurt

Directions:
Put in all ingredients in blender. Blend until it is smooth.
Enjoy!
