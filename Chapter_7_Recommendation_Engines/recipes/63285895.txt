Title:
Tex Mex Party Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups small pretzels [twists]
1 1/2 cups corn chips
1 1/2 cups oyster crackers
1 1/2 peanuts [dry roasted]
1 1/2 cups cheese crackers
2 tbsp melted butter
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp garlicpowder
1/4 tsp red pepper

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients in a large roaster.  Combine remaining ingredients in a small bowl, mix well. pour mix over pretzel mix and toss to coat.  Bake st 300 degrees for 25 min, or until golder brown, stirring half way through.  Pour out on wax paper to cool.  Store in sir tight container.
