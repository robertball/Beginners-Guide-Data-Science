Title:
Sweet & Sour Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup water
2 tsp soy sauce
4 Tbsp sugar
4 Tbsp vinegar
2 Tbsp cornstarch, dissolved in 1/3 cup water
1 tsp oil

Directions:
In a small saucepan, combine water, soy sauce, sugar, and vinegar.  Bring to a boil.  Stir in cornstarch mixture.  Cook, stirring constantly, until sauce becomes thick and transparent (about 2-3 minutes).  Stir in oil.
