Title:
Muriel's Turnip Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/900225.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium Turnips (Rutabagas)
1 C Applesause
6 tbsp Butter
4 tsp Brown Sugar
3 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp Pepper
2 Eggs
1 3/4 C Soft Bread Crumbs - Divided
2 tbsp Melter Butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Cook diced turnip in boiling water until tender.
Drain, mash, and mix with remaining ingredients, except for half the bread crumbs and the melted butter.
Spoon into a greased 2 quart casserole.
Toss remaining bread crumbs with melted butter and sprinkle over turnip mixture.
Cook uncovered at 350F for 30 minutes, then refrigerate until needed.
Heat in a 350F oven for 1 hour or until hot.
