Title:
Rise To The Sun Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/615166.jpg
Ingredients:
4 orange, juiced
2 lemons, juiced
4 tablespoons orange liqueur
4 fluid ounces pineapple juice
8 fluid ounces lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
1 dash grenadine syrup
4 red maraschino cherries
4 green maraschino cherries
4 twists orange zest

Directions:
In a cocktail shaker, combine orange juice, lemon juice, orange liqueur, pineapple juice, lemon-lime soda and grenadine. Shake well and pour into 4 chilled wine glasses. Garnish each with a green cherry, a red cherry and a twist of orange zest.

Submitter: 
Louise
Description: 
I like to serve this drink during sunset as an aperitif to my guests because my apartment's balcony faces the sea -- hence the name. It's easy to make and high in vitamin C!
