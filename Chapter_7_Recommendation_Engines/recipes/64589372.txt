Title:
Crescent Berry Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. 1/3 fat cream cheese, softened to room temperature
1/3 cup sugar, plus extra for sprinkling
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 cup milk
2 eggs
10 oz frozen mixed berries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cream sugar, cream cheese and vanilla extra until well mixed.
Add in milk and eggs.  Mix well.
Roll crescent rolls according to package directions, and place spaced out in an ungreased 7.5x11 or 9x9 inch casserole dish.
Pour mixture evenly over the top, and sprinkle the top with sugar.
Bake at 350 degrees for 35 mins, cover with foil, and continue to cook for another 10-15 minutes until set.
Cool slightly and serve.

Submitter: 
Cori
Description: 
Easy, yummy breakfast bake!  I like using fresh berries when I have them, but I always have frozen berries in the freezer.
