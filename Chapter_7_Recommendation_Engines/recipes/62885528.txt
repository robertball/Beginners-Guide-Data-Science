Title:
Orange Walnut Date Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all purpose flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp salt
1/3 cup soft magarine
3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
1 egg
1 tbsp grated orange rind
2 tbsp orange juice
1/2 cup chopped dates
3/4 cup chopped walnuts
for the icing:
1/4 cup soft margarine
1 tsp grated orange rind
1 egg yolk
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
2 Tbsp orange juice
1/4 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
1. Sift the flour, soda, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt into a medium sized bowl. Set aside.
2. Cream 1/3 C margarine and 3/4 C brown sugar in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Beat in one egg.
3. Add flour mixture to margarine/sugar mixture until smooth. Stir in dates and walnuts by hand.
4. Spread in greased and floured 11"x17" pan. Bake in 350Â° oven for 25-30 minutes until brown.
5. Allow cake to cool in pan.
6. Cream 1/4 C margarine, orange rind, egg yolk and vanilla until well blended. Gradually add powdered sugar. Beat well. Add orange juice.
7.Frost cooled cake and cover frosting with the remaining 1/4 C chopped walnuts. Push in lightly with fingers. Refrigerate or freeze.
6. Ice cooled
