Title:
Turkey, Sweet Potato, and Cranberry Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds turkey drumsticks, skin removed
1 small onion, diced
1T honey
1T cider vinegar
1-1/2t finely chopped fresh ginger
1t salt
1/2t black pepper
1c chicken stock
1 lb sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1" chunks
1/2c dried cranberries
2T flour

Directions:
Combine turkey, onio, honey, vinegar, ginger, salt, pepper, and stock in slow cooker.
Cover and cook on low 5 hours.
Add potatoes and cranberries.
Cook until potatoes are tender, about 45 minutes more.
Remove turkey and pull meat from bones.
Pour one quarter of the liquid into small pot and whisk in flour until smooth.
Whisk in remaining liquid.
Toss meat, potatoes, and cranberries with gravy and reheat if necessary.
