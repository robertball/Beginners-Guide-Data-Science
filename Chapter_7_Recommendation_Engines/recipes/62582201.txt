Title:
Buffalo Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs Chicken Wings (flappers, drummetes mixed)
1 12oz Bottle Franks Redhot Sauce
1 Packet Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing Mix (not dip)
1/2 Cup of unsalted butter
1 tbsp of white vinegar (if you want it more tangy)
Enough oil to fry your wings

Directions:
Cook chicken wings with or without breading in 375*F oil for about 15 minutes in batches of about 9 at  time unless you have a bigger fryer then do more. Meanwhile melt down butter over low heat, after butter has melted add in the dressing mix, franks, and vinegar. Mix well and allow to heat up. Cover wings in the sauce and enjoy!
