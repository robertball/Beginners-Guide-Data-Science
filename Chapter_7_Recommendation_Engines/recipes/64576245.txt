Title:
Banana Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Two ripe bananas, sliced
Half cup of milk
Half teaspoon cinnamon
Two teaspoons sugar

Directions:
Put bananas and milk in a saucepan on low heat
Stir constantly until desired consistency-anywhere from oatmeal-y to smooth.
Remove from heat
Add cinnamon and sugar

Submitter: 
Cinnamon Cardamom Cloves
Description: 
This isn't actual gravy-it just looks like it. It's great on biscuits, pancakes, or waffles. Easily halved or doubled
