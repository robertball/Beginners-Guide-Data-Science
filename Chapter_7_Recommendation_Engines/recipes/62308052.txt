Title:
Tasty Beets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/369592.jpg
Ingredients:
•4 Medium beets, cut up into 3/4 inch cubes or pieces
•Pinch Sea Salt
•1 Teaspoon thyme (optional)•2 Tablespoons of olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425
Cut up beets in 3/4 inch cubes or pieces.  Place olive oil in bowl and cut up beets.  Toss beets in bowl until coated.
Place beets on cooking stone every spaced, very lightly sprinkle a little sea salt and put in oven on middle rack position.
Cook from 10 to 20 minutes (done when you can stick a fork in them and it comes out easily.
Makes 4 servings.
Nutrition Faces 1/2 cup (1 medium beet): 35 calories, Fat .1g, Cholesterol 0, Sodium  5%, Carbohydrate 7.8 g, Dietary Fiber 2.3g, Sugars 5.5g
