Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2452950.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all purpose flour
1 cup buttermilk
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 packet instant hot cocoa mix
1 cup M&Ms; or chocolate chips

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375ยบ
Oil baking sheet (cast iron skillet also works)
Mix the dry ingredients (flour, baking soda, salt) in a large bowl
Add the buttermilk and mix just until it starts to hold together -- do not knead
Take rounded teaspoonfuls of dough and flatten slightly
Tuck two or three M&Ms or chocolate chips into the dough and close the dough around them
Roll the dough ball in the dry cocoa mix powder and set on baking sheet
Balls should not touch on baking sheet, but can be close to each other
Bake for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Alexa Offenhauer
Description: 
Fun for kids to make and eat! Includes chocolate without being overly sweet. A good afternoon project.
