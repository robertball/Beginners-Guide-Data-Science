Title:
Chubby Hubby Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2cups all purpose flour
1tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 cup unsalted butter
1 cup light brown sugar
1/2 cup granulated sugar
2 large eggs
1 Tbsp vanilla extract
1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
1 cup semi sweet chocolate chips
1 1/2 cups coarsely chopped pretzels
1 cup salted peanuts
1/4 cup peanut butter chips for top
1/4 cup chocolate chips for top
1/4 cup chopped pretzels for top

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
foil line a 9x13 pan and then spray with cooking spray
In a bowl combine flour, baking soda and salt. In separate bowl blend butter and both sugars. Add in vanilla and eggs to sugar mix. Beat on low speed until blended.  Add peanut butter and continue to blend till creamy.
Add 1 cup of chocolate chips, 1 1/2 cups of pretzel pieces and salted peanuts to flour mixture.
Combine flour to butter mixture.
Spread evenly in pan
Sprinkle peanut butter chips, remaining chocolate chips and remaining pretzel pieces on top
Bake for about 30 min or until golden brown
Cool completely before serving
