Title:
Corkscrew Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/960301.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups corkscrew noodles (16 oz pkg), cooked
2 cups tomatoes, chopped
1 cup zucchini, thin sliced
1/2 large red pepper, julienned
1/2 cup green peas, frozen, thawed
dressing:
1/3 cup tarragon vinegar
2 tablespoons water
1 clove garlic, minced
1 teaspoon dill weed

Directions:
Combine the salad ingredients. Mix the dressing ingredients in a small bowl and beat with fork. Pour dressing over salad; toss well. Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
lamorris
Description: 
The dill weed makes the great taste for this pasta salad! I have made this for many years and it's always a favorite.
