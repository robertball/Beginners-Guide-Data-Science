Title:
White Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/488535.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans white kidney beans, drained
2 cans whole kernal corn, drained
2 large cans diced chicken
2 cans chicken broth
2 Tbsp. green chilies
2 tsp. garlic salt
1 cup onion, chopped
8 oz. salsa
8 oz. cream cheese
2 cups monterey jack cheese

Directions:
Place kidney beans, corn, chicken, broth, chilies, garlic salt, and onion in crock pot.
Heat on low for 6-8 hours.
About an hour before serving, add salsa, cream cheese, and monterey jack cheese; change heat to high.
Heat until cheeses are melted and serve.
