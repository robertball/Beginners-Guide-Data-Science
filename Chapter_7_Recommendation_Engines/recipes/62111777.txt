Title:
Rosie's Pumpkin Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. can Pumpkin
8 oz. cream cheese ( your choice low fat may be used)
2 C. powdered sugar, sifted
1 1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
Beat pumpkin and cream cheese until fluffy. beat in the sugar and spice.
Serve with gingersnap cookies.
