Title:
Pecan Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/719355.jpg
Ingredients:
6-8 Medium size apples
2 tbsp lemon juice
2 tbsp honey
1.5 tbsp flour
1 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp sugar
3/4 cup granola
1/4 cup pecans
1/4 cup dried cherries, chopped into small pieces

Directions:
Core and cut apples into small wedges. In a large bowl add apples, lemon juice, flour and honey.  Transfer to a well-oiled pie plate. Combine Pecans and granola in ziplock bag and pound until small.  Add cherries, nutmeg, cinnamon and sugar and mix.  Pour granola topping over apples, cover with foil and cook for 30 minutes.  Take foil off an let cook another 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Michele McCormick
Description: 
A twist on a fall classic, easy to make and the house will smell amazing!
