Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cubed
2 tablespoons butter
1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup white wine
2 tablespoons butter
1/2 chopped onion
3 garlic cloves, minced
6 ounces sliced mushrooms
1/2 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon BTB

Directions:
Put chicken, margarine and dressing mix in slow cooker; mix together and cook on low for 5 to 6 hours.
Last hour of slow-cooking, melt 2 tablespoons butter in saucepan, sautee onion, garlic and mushrooms for 5 minutes. Add this and 1/2 cup white wine to slow cooker and stir.
Add cream cheese,soup,sour cream and BTB
mix together and cook on high for another 1/2 hour or until heated through and warm.

Submitter: 
Cathy Myers
Description: 
Ultra creamy with cream cheese and cream soup, this crock pot dinner comes together quickly with the addition of chicken and Italian salad dressing mix.Modified from: Slow Cooker Chicken Stroganoff
