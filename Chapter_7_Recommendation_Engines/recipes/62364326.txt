Title:
Casa Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup milk
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 tablespoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
(add cayenne pepper to taste for a little kick)

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, milk, and lime juice. Season with cumin, salt, and pepper. Stir in cilantro just before serving.

Submitter: 
B L
Description: 
This tangy dressing is accentuated with lime and cilantro. It is a simple, refreshing topping for a green salad, and makes a great accompaniment to a spicy Mexican dinner.

My changes: light miracle whip, lowfat evaporated milk plus water, used a couple of tsps dried cilantro, and added a couple pinches of cayenne pepper.Modified from: Casa Dressing
