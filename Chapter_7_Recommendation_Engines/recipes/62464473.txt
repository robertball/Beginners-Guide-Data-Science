Title:
Mexican Adobo Marinated Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/474135.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts
3 Guajillo chiles~ Stems removed, toasted lightly
3 Arbol Chiles~ Stems removed, toasted lightly
4 Garlic Cloves
1/2 Teaspoon Cumin
1/8 Teaspoon Coriander
1/4 Teaspoon Ground Cloves
1/2 Teaspoon Mexican Oregano
2 Tablespoons Cider Vinegar
1/4 Cup Water
Salt to taste (about 1/2 teaspoon)

Directions:
Place toasted chiles, galic, cumin, coriander, cloves, oregano, water, vinegar and salt into blender; blend until smooth.
Pound chicken to about 3/4 inch thickness.  Rub marinade in chicken generously, pouring extra over top.  Cover and let stand at room temperature for 1 hour.  Or refridgerate for up to 1 day.  Bring to room temperature if necessary.
Heat grill to medium-high and grill chicken turning once, until charred in places and cooked through. About 10 minutes.  Remove chicken and wrap in foil and let stand 5 minutes before slicing and serving.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This is a simple marinade for chicken.  The nuttiness of toasted chiles is backed up by the fragrance of cumin and garlic and the bite of the vinegar.  The recipe can easily be doubled or tripled.
