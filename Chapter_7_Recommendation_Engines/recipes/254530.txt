Title:
Easy Microwavable Bacon and Egg Breakfast Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3769329.jpg
Ingredients:
cooking spray
2 small flour tortillas
4 slices bacon, cut into halves
1 egg

Directions:
Spray the inside of a microwave-safe mug with cooking spray.
Layer the tortillas in the mug. Place 4 bacon halves on top of the tortillas. Crack egg over bacon layer and top with remaining bacon halves. Cover mug with a paper towel.
Cook in the microwave until egg is set and bacon is cooked, about 4 minutes. Remove tortilla bowls from mug.

Description: 
Delicious breakfast bowl, great for quick breakfasts! Top with shredded cheese, if desired.
