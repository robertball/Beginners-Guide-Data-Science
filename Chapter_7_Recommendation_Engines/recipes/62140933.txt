Title:
Sweet Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/509929.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c self-rising flour
1 c self-rising yellow cornmeal
1 c milk
2 eggs, beaten
6 Tbsp salted butter
1/2 c sugar

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 F
Mix flour, meal, and sugar.
Cut in butter until mixture is the size of coase peas.
Stir in milk and eggs. Pour into a well-greased cast iron skillet.
Bake for about 30 minutes, or until golden brown on the top.
Allow to cool for about 10 minutes before releasing from skillet.

Submitter:

Description:
