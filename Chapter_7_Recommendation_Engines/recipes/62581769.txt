Title:
Celine LaPointe's Riz Jamaicain Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c cooked cold rice ( cooked in chicken broth)
1 can red beans ( drained)
1 green bell pepper, diced
1 yellow bell pepper, diced
1 red bell pepper, diced
4 french onions
1 red onion
Dressing:
1 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp parsley
salt & pepper to taste
1/3c oil
juice & zest of 2 limes

Directions:
Mix all salad ingredients together & in a separate bowl combine dressing ingredients. Toss together & chill until served.

Submitter: 
StevenandMaggie McClain
Description: 
This is a cold rice salad recipe that I got from a friend many years ago.
