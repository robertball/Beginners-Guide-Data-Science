Title:
Gluten Free Summer Squash Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup grated summer squash
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 jalapeno pepper, chopped
3/4 cup rice flour
1/2 cup gluten free flour
2 T ground flax seed
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 cup water

Directions:
Place griddle on medium heat.
Combine vegetables in small bowl.
Combine rice flour, gluten free flour, ground flax seeds and baking powder in medium bowl.  Stir in water
Add vegetables, coat with flour mixture.  It should be moist but not liquidy.
Spray griddle.
Scoop dough onto griddle.  Fry about 3 minutes, flip, fry another 3 minutes.(Keep on heat longer if you want a more browned edge.) Repeat until all dough is used.

Submitter: 
sueb
Description: 
These gluten free, egg free and dairy free summer squash patties make an interesting breakfast or a side for a meal.
