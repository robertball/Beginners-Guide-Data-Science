Title:
Grandma Irena's Palacsinta (Hungarian Crepes) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pancake Mix:
2 cups flour
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1 cup of soda water (or regular water)
1/2 cup vegetable oil
salt to taste
Filling:
1 cup chopped almonds
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup milk
1 or 2 drops of vanilla
1-2 tsps of rum (optional)
Chocolate topping:
1/4 cup water
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup of dark chocolate
1/4 stick of margarine

Directions:
Combine flour and eggs until smooth.
Add milk, soda water, salt, and olive oil and mix until smooth.
Refrigerate overnight.
Mix upon removing from fridge.
Warm a small amount of vegetable oil in frying pan on medium heat, pour 1/4 cup of mixture on to pan
Flip, cooking for ~1 minute on each side.
Remove from pan and place flat on wax paper - keep pancakes separate with wax paper.
To make filling combine almonds, sugar, milk, vanilla, and rum (if desired) in a small pot on low heat and mix until creamy. Allow mixture to cool a bit before filling pancakes.
Spread 1 heaping tbsp of mixture onto pancakes, roll them up and place on a platter
To make chocolate topping combine water, sugar and chocolate in pot on low heat and mix just until chocolate melts.
Remove from heat and add margarine, mixing until melted.
Pour topping onto rolled pancakes.
Warm in microwave a bit before serving.

Submitter: 
Michele Avissar-Whiting
Description: 
My Hungarian Grandmother has made this for us ever since we were kids...certainly not health food but maybe one of the tastiest things you'll ever eat! Make it for breakfast or dessert.
