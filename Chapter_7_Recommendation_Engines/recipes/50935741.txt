Title:
Chocolate Mint Dessert Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box brownie mix
ingredients for brownie mix
2 cups confectioners' sugar
1/2 cup butter, softened
2 tablespoons creme de menthe liqueur
1 cup semisweet mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Mix brownies according to box directions. Coo. Cool.
Bake for 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until top springs back when lightly touched. Cool completely in the pan.
In a small bowl, beat the confectioners' sugar, 1/2 cup butter and creme de menthe until smooth. Spread evenly over the cooled brownies, sprinkle with mini chocolate chips, then chill until set.
. Cover, and chill for at least 1 hour before cutting into squares.
