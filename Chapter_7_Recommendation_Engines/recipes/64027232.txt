Title:
Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can whole kernel corn, drained
1 can creamed corn
2 eggs beaten
1 stick butter, melted (1/4 Lb)
4 heaping T. all purpose flour
2 T. sugar
1/4 C. diced bell pepper (you can use red, yellow orange or green in any combination)
2 C. shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 tsp each salt and pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine all ingredients and divide into 2 greased 5" x 7" casserole dishes.
Bake for about 45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
