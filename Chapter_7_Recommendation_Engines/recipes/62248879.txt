Title:
Gulliver's Cream Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (20 ounce) packages frozen corn kernels, thawed
1 cup whipping cream
1 cup evaorated milk
1 teaspoon salt
white or cayenne pepper
2 tablespoons flour
2 tablespoons butter, melted
Parmesan cheese
dry bread crumbs
1 T butter

Directions:
Place corn kernels, whipping cream, milk, salt,and white or cayenne pepper in a large stockpot. Bring to a slow, low boil beginning on medium heat, stirring frequently. Simmer for 5 minutes on low heat.
Melt butter in the microwave, or in a small saucepan on low heat. Whisk in flour until smooth. Stir into the corn until well blended. Sprinkle some parmesan cheese on top, then some dry breadcrumbs and melted butter. Broil to brown lightly. Enjoy!
