Title:
SCALLOPED POTATOES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. butter
2 medium white onions, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cracked black pepper
1 tsp. dried oregano
1/2 cup white wine
3 cups whipping cream
4-6 large potatoes, sliced
3 cups grated Emmenthal cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, salt, pepper and oregano.  Cook and stir for 3 to 5 minutes, or just until onions turn translucent. Add white wine and stir to loosen bits from bottom of the saucepan. Reduce heat to low.
Add whipping cream and simmer for about 30 minutes until it thickens and reduces a bit. Spread one quarter evenly on the bottom of a 13 x 9 baking pan.
Add layer of potatoes, spread another layer of cream mixture over potatoes; sprinkle with 1/3 of cheese. Repeat layering finishing with cheese on top. Cover with aluminum foil and bake in oven for 1 hour.
Remove foil and continue baking for 30 minutes.
