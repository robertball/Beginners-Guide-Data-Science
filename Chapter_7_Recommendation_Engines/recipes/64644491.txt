Title:
Mom'sCreamy Lemon Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust
4 tablespoons butter melted and cooled
1-1/2 cup graham crackers
1/4 cup sugar
Filling
2 Large Egg Yokes
1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup of fresh lemon juice (4 lemons)

Directions:
Over 350 degrees
Brush baking dish with melted butter
Crush Graham Crackers
Add sugar and butter to graham crackers
Press Mixture into the baking dish
Bake crust until lightly brown (8 to12 minutes)
Cool Crust for 30 minutes
Wisk yokes and condensed milk together
Add Lemon Juice and wisk
Pour on to crust
Bake until mix is set (~15 min)
Cool on pan rack
Chill for 1hr before serving
Serve with Whipped Cream
