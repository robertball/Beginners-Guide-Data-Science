Title:
Whole Wheat Yogurt Sesame Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c white bread flour
1 1/4 c whole wheat bread flour
1/4 c rolled oats
1/2 c hand hot water
1/2 c vanilla yogurt
1 tbsp sesame oil
1 tbsp corn syrup
1 tbsp honey
1 tbsp brown sugar (or Splenda)
1 tsp salt
3/4 tsp dry yeast

Directions:
Set bread machine to basic and light crust. Crust will be plenty brown at this setting.
Spray loaf pan with butter flavor cooking spray.  Pour in water, add yogurt and sesame oil.  Add both flours and then the oatmeal, sprinkling them in over the top of the liquids. Add honey and brown sugar. Make a well in the center until you feel the liquid, pour in the yeast. Sprinkle the salt lightly over the top and then start.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
Crusty, moist, slightly nutty and sweet bread that is good for toast or sandwiches.  Or, served warm with butter with your evening meal.
