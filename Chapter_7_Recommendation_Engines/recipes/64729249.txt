Title:
Chimichurri Mahi Mahi in parchment Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chimichurri from “Roasted New York Strip Steak with Chimichurri Sauce” on allrecipes.com
6-4 oz fillets of Mahi Mahi
Seasoned salt
6 tsp butter
Honey

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Place a piece of  fish on parchment paper.
Sprinkle seasoned salt on top of fish.
Smooth 3 tsps of  chimichurri across the  fish filet and place a pat of butter on top.
Drizzle with a tsp of honey.
Fold parchment over the fish and tightly close the sides.
Repeat with each fillet.  Place on a sheet pan and bake for 15 minutes or until fish is cooked to desired doneness.

Description: 
We love Mahi Mahi, it’s our families favorite fish.  I had some homemade chimichurri left over that needed to use and came up with this. We liked it a lot.  You don’t have to used parchment but it keeps the fish super moist.
