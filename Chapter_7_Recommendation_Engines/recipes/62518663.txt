Title:
Traditional Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/511306.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups  sugar
1 teaspoon  salt
2 teaspoons  ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon  ground ginger
1/2 teaspoon  ground cloves
4 each  eggs large
1 can 29 oz.  libby's pure pumpkin
2 can 12oz  carnation evaporated milk
2 each  pie shells (9 inch), unbaked

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425° F.
Mix first five ingredients in a small bowl.
Beat eggs in another large bowl.
Sti in pumpkin and sugar spice mixture. Then gradually stir in evaporated
milk.
Pour into pie shells and bake for 15 minutes.
Reduce temperature to 350° and bake for 40 to 50 minutes, or until inserted knife in center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
This is a traditional pumpkin pie recipe served at holiday dinners. Filled with aromatic spices like nutmeg, cloves, cinnamon, and ginger; it is truly a  delicious pumpkin pie everyone will love!
