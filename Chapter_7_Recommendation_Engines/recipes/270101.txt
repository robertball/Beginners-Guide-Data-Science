Title:
CAMPBELL'S® Tuna Pasta Salad with Celery and Herbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6297001.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (900 ml) carton CAMPBELL'S® 30% Less Sodium Ready To Use Seafood Broth
1 (16 ounce) package medium pasta shells
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
1 (6 ounce) can tuna
2 stalks celery stalks with leaves, sliced
1/2 medium red onion, finely chopped
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon fresh cracked black pepper
1/4 cup finely chopped parsley

Directions:
In a large pot, bring broth and pasta to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until pasta is al dente and most of the liquid has been absorbed, about 10 minutes. Set aside.
In a large bowl, whisk together mayonnaise and lemon juice. Add the tuna, celery stalks and leaves, onion, and cooled pasta; season with salt, pepper, and parsley. Toss to combine.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Canada
Description: 
Broth adds lots of flavor to this summer classic that is sure to be a hit at your next BBQ.
