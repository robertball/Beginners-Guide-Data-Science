Title:
Chicken Balls With Couscous and Mushroom Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T olive oil
1 lb ground chicken
1/3 c chopped celery
1/3 c chopped onion
1/2 c bread crumbs
1 t dried sage
1/2 t garlic powder
1/2 t salt
1 T butter
1 lb button mushrooms, sliced
1 1/2 c skim milk
1/2 t pepper
2-3 T flour
1 10-oz pkg plain, instant couscous

Directions:
Over medium heat, warm the olive oil in a large skillet.  In a bowl, mix together the chicken, celery, onion, bread crumbs and spices.  Form into 1 inch balls.  Place in skillet and sautee for 15 minutes, rolling around periodically to cook all sides evenly, or until chicken is cooked.  Balls will be light brown.
Meanwhile, melt butter in another pan and sautee mushrooms until soft over low-medium heat.  Add milk and pepper; bring to a boil.  Stir in flour until dissolved, reduce heat to low and stir occasionally; allow sauce to thicken.
Prepare couscous according to package directions.
To present, dish couscous onto plate, top with several chicken balls, then cover with the mushroom sauce.

Description: 
Onions, celery and spices flavor ground chicken sauteed as balls.  Served over couscous with a creamy mushroom sauce, this is an easy, healthy meal that comes out totally gourmet!
