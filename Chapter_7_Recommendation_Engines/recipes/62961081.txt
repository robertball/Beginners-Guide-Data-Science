Title:
Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large sweet potatoes (peeled and boiled or baked until soft and mashed)
1/4 cup milk
2 TBLS butter
2 TBLS brown sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/8 tsp nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
In a large bowl mix together mashed sweet potatoes with milk, margarine, brown sugar, salt, cinnamon and nutmeg.
Transfer the sweet potato mixture to an 9 x 9 casserole dish.
Bake in a preheated oven for approximately 30 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
This is a traditional sweet potato casserole made lighter by using reduced fat margarine and almond milk.  Most likely no one will even know that you used lower fat ingredients.
