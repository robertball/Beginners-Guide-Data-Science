Title:
Chicken Rice Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups rice -cooked
1 lbs chicken cooked
2 cups salsa
1 pkg taco seasoning
8 large tortillas

Directions:
Cook rice according to directions and cook and shredd chicken.  Mix everything together and spread in tortillas.  Microwave until heated through.  Can be frozen
