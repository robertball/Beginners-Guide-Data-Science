Title:
Easy Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint heavy whipping cream
1/2 cup butter
1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 t. garlic powder

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, melt butter.
Add whipping cream and shredded Parmesan by 1/2 Cupfulls.
Add 1/2 t. garlic powder
Cook over medium  heat until smooth. Remove from heat. Sauce will thicken upon standing.

Submitter: 
Dorothy Clark
Description: 
A  convergence of whipping cream, butter and Parmesan cheese cooks down effortlessly into a velvety, classic Alfredo sauce.Modified from: Easy Alfredo Sauce I
