Title:
Crab and spinach dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup finely chopped yellow onion
2 tsp chopped garlic
1 10 oz package frozen spinach, defrosted and drained well
1 pound lump crab meat
2 cups heavy cream
1/4 c white wine
3 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
1/8 cup chopped parsley
1/8 cup chopped chives
pinch cayenne pepper
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
In heavy sauce pan, over medium heat saute onions and garlic for 3 to 5 minutes but do not brown.
Add the butter and allow to melt.
Add white and reduce to half its volume.
Add the spinach and warm through for about 3 minutes
Add cream and cook until it reduces about half and begins to coat the back of the spoon.
Add the crab meat and stir gently for about 3 minutes.  Try not to shred crab meat.
Stir in mustard, parsley, chives, cayenne pepper, salt and pepper.
Transfer to a casserole dish and sprinke with cheese
Bake 8 to 10 minutes until cheese is golden brown.

Description: 
This is a delicious dip for tailgating, girls night in or when having company.  I am always asked for this recipe. Serve with either tortilla chips or cut fresh bread like french.  You can always add more cheese to thicken the sauce.
