Title:
Kristin's Kale Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/718369.jpg
Ingredients:
Kale
Extra Virgin Olive Oil
Celtic Sea Salt
Garlic Powder
Chili Powder
*amounts to come

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
Using your hands carefully remove the leaves from the thick stems and tear into bite size pieces (approximately the size of your palm, they shrink) and place into colander.
Rince well and pat dry with paper towels.
In a large plastic container, that has a lid, mix extra virgin olive oil, celtic sea salt, garlic powder & chili powder with a spoon.
Add kale to the container and attach lid. Shake well until all leave have been covered in oil mix.
Using a thin baking sheet covered in aluminum foil bake until the edges are brown, 13-15 minutes.
