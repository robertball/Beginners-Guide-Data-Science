Title:
Lean & Green Egg Mug * Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼  cup diced zucchini
½ cup chopped spinach leaves
¼ cup chopped asparagus
¾ cup fat-free liquid egg  substitute (like Egg Beaters)
1 tbsp. chopped scallions
1 wedge The Laughing Cow Light Creamy Swiss Cheese

Directions:
In a large microwave-safe mug sprayed with nonstick spray, combine zucchini, spinach, and asparagus. Microwave for 1 minute, or until spinach has wilted, and zucchini and asparagus has slightly softened.
Blot away excess moisture. Add egg substitute, stir, and microwave for 1 minute.
Mix scallions and cheese wedge, breaking the wedge into pieces as you add it.
Microwave for 1 minute more, or until set. Enjoy!
