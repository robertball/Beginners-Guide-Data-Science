Title:
Three Sisters Vegetarian Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# dried beans
1 medium chopped onion
1/2 c. chopped green pepper
2 cloves chopped garlic
1 15 oz. can diced tomatoes
1 15 oz can pumpkin
1 Tbl. pumpkin pie spice
1 Tbl. chili powder
1 tsp. cumin
1 tsp. salt
2 c. frozen corn

Directions:
rinse beans, cover with water and soak overnight
discard soaking water, rinse beans, cover with water, heat to boiling and simmer 10 minutes.
put the beans along with about 1 cup of the cooking water into a crock pot
add the rest of the ingredients except for the corn
cook on low for about 8 hours.  Add more water if beans become dry.
about 1 hour before you want to serve, add the corn
add water to desired consistency before serving

Submitter:

Description: 
I developed this dish for our Native American food ways celebration.  It is always a big hit.
