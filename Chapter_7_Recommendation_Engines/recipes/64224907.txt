Title:
WHIPPED SHORTBREAD COOKIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 POUND OF BUTTER
1/2 CUP CORNSTARCH
3 CUPS FLOUR
1/2 CUP ICING SUGAR
1 TEASPOON VANILLA

Directions:
WHIP BUTTER WELL AND THEN ADD ALL THE REST OF THE INGREDIENTS AND MIX VERY WELL.  ROLL INTO BALLS AND PUT A PIECE OF CANDIED RED OR GREEN CHERRY ON TOP AND PUSH IN TO SPREAD THE COOKIE A LITTLE.  BAKE THESE COOKIES UNTIL JUST VERY SLIGHTLY GOLDEN ON THE BOTTOM BUT WATCH THAT THEY DON'T BROWN.  TAKE OUT OF OVEN AND LET SIT ON COOKIE SHEET FOR A FEW MINUTES AND THEY WILL KEEP BROWNING.  TRANSFER TO COOLING RACK

Submitter: 
Debbie Owens
Description: 
THIS RECIPE HAS BEEN PASSED DOWN FROM FAMILY AND I BAKE THEM EVERY YEAR AT CHRISTMAS.  THEY ARE MELT IN YOUR MOUTH GOOD.
