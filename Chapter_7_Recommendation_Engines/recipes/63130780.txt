Title:
Meat Loaf Wellingotn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 cup meatless spaghetti sauce, divided
1/4 cup dry bread crumbs
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 pounds ground meat
2 cups (8 oz.) shredded part skim mozzarella cheese
1 Tbs. minced fresh parsley
1 tube (8 oz.) refrigerated crescent rolls

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the eggs, 1/3 cup spaghetti sauce, bread crumbs, salt and pepper.  Crumble beef over mixture and mix well.
On heavy duty foil or wax paper pat beef mixture into a 12X8 inch rectangle.
Sprinkle 1 cup of cheese and parsley to within 1 inch of edges.  Roll up jelly roll style starting with a long side and peeling foil or wax paper away while rolling. Seal edges and place seam side down in a greased 13x9x 2 in. baking dish.
Bake uncovered at 350 for 1 hour. Drain.
Unroll crescent dough, seal seams and perforations.
Drape dough over meat to cover top, sides, and ends. Seal ends
Bake 15-20 minutes longer until crust is golden brown.
Let stand 5 minutes
Transfer to serving platter and sprinkle with remaining cheese.  Serve with remaining spaghetti sauce.
