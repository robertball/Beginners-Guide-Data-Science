Title:
Thai Peanut glazed Grouper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 7oz. portion Local grouper
1 avocado
4 tomatillos
1/2 red onion
1tbs olive oil
4oz yucca (casava)
3oz peanut butter
1tsp sesame oil
1tbs red curry paste
1/2 qt. heavy cream
4 lemons(juice)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Dice avocado, onions, tomatillo place in mixing bowl.
2. add 1/2 of the lemon juice and olive oil. set aside in cooler
3. cut yucca into 1/2 in.wide by 4 in.long blanch for 8-9 mins.,and fry till crispy.
4. combine heavy cream, sesame oil, curry, and peanut butter in a pot and reduce by 1/2.then wisk in the remainder of lemon juice.
5. Pan sear grouper on med to high heat for 4-5 mins on each side.
6. cook in oven for 8-10 mins.

Submitter: 
Jdorian Garcia
Description: 
Thai Peanut glazed grouper over avocado,tomatillo ceviche, crispy yuca fries, topped with thai peanut froth.
