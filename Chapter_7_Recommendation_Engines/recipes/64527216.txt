Title:
Mulled Wine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups sugar
2 cups water
2 tsp. whole cloves
2 cinnamon sticks, broken
3 star anise
1.5 l dry red wine
orange slices

Directions:
Bring water, sugar and spices to boil. Simmer uncovered x 10 - 15 min. Stir occasionally, until sugar is dissolved.
Add wine.
Heat but do not boil. Spices can be removed after the wine is heated.
Add 1/2 of an orange slice in each mug to serve.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A delicious, sweet, warm beverage with just enough spice - esp. nice on a winter evening around the fire.
