Title:
Mediterranean Chicken II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large boneless chicken breasts and 4-6 boneless thighs (pound chicken to flatten/tenderize and cut into uniform pieces to cook evenly)
1 c. Kalamata olives and juice
1 c each apricots, figs, dates, chopped
1/4 c oregano (dried)
2T cumin
1 whole garlic head, chopped
1/2 c (approx, little less) olive oil
1/3 to 1/2 c brown sugar
1 c apple cider/white vinegar
1/2 c. approx white wine
1/2 to 3/4 c plus more red wine
extra bay leaves (10?)
parsley

Directions:
combine all ingredients
marinate 5-6 hours or more/less
bake uncovered 350 degrees for 1 hour
sprinkle with fresh parsley to serve
