Title:
Exquisite Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
4 fluid ounces warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 tablespoons honey
3/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
1/4 teaspoon dried marjoram
1/4 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
a tiny pinch of cayenne pepper
maybe a little olive oil
salt to taste

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine tomato paste, water, garlic, honey, onion powder, oregano, marjoram, basil, ground black pepper, cayenne pepper, red pepper flakes and salt; mix together, breaking up any clumps.
Sauce should sit for 30 minutes to blend flavors; spread over pizza dough and prepare pizza as desired.
