Title:
Pork 'n Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean fresh pork, cut into 1-inch cubes
1 medium onion chopped
1 can (31 oz) pork and beans
1/4 cup molasses
2 tbsp packed brown sugar
2 tbsp prepared mustard
2 tsp dried parsley flakes
2 tsp worchestershire sauce

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a saucepan. Cook over medium heat until heated through.

Submitter:

Description: 
Tastes like baked beans without the fuss.
