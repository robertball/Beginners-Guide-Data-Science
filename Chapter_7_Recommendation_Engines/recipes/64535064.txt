Title:
Hemp Seed Energy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3092608.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup GF rolled oats
1/2 cup nut butter
2 tbsp + 1/8 cup Manitoba Harvest Hemp Hearts, divided
1/4 cup reduced sugar maple syrup
1/4 cup dairy free mini chocolate chips
1 tsp vanilla extract
dash of sea salt

Directions:
To a bowl, add all ingredients reserving the 1/8 cup of hemp hearts
Combine with a spoon until ingredients are fully combined
Roll into golf sized balls
Place 1/8 cup of hemp hearts on a plate & roll balls over to coat
Place bites on a plate lined with parchment paper & refrigerate 2-3 hours prior to enjoying
Refrigerate up to a 1 week or freeze up to 1 month*

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Sink your teeth into a 130 calorie Hemp Seed Energy Bites made with creamy peanut butter, chocolate and oats. These nutritious snacks are packed with protein and omegas a healthy refuel snack!
