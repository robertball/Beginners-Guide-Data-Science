Title:
Slow Cooker Peking Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4	Bone-in Skinless Chicken Thighs
¾ cup	Green Onions, sliced
4 cloves	Garlic, minced
¼ cup	Low-sodium Soy Sauce
2 Tbsp	Honey
1 Tbsp	Fresh Ginger, grated
¼ tsp	Sesame Oil
½ tsp	Red Pepper Flakes
1 Tbsp	Fresh Lime Juice
2 Tbsp	Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Directions:
1.In a large bowl, combine green onion, garlic, soy sauce, honey, ginger, sesame oil, red pepper and lime juice. Mix until ingredients are well combined.
2.Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Sear chicken thighs on both sides until well browned.
3. Place chicken thighs in slow cooker and top with sauce mixture. Cook on low heat for 4 hours
