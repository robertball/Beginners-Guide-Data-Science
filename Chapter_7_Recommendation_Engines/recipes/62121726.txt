Title:
Mashed Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium heads cauliflower, florets cleaned and broken off
2/3 cup skim milk
1/4 cup softened butter
1/4 cup instant potatoes
1/2 cup fat free cottage cheese
1/4 cup light cheddar cheese
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Steam cauliflower florets (no stems) until extremely tender, about 30 minutes.
Stir in milk, softened butter, and instant potatoes.  With a hand mixer, beat until mostly smooth.
Add fat free cottage cheese, light cheddar cheese, and salt to taste.  Beat with mixer to fully combine.
Serve in a bowl warmed in the oven.

Submitter: 
Brooke Elizabeth
Description: 
This delicious dish is healthier than traditional mashed potatoes, but tastes just as wonderful!  It is also a great way to incorporate a non starchy vegetable and lots of protein.
