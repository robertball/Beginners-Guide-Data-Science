Title:
Zucchini Rotini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Rotini Pasta
3 Zucchini - sliced into 1/4" pieces
4 Large Carrots - peeled and sliced into 1/4" pieces
1 Tbs Olive Oil
2 14.5 oz cans Stewed Tomatoes
1 small can Tomato Paste
2 tsp. Italian Seasoning
1 tsp. Garlic Powder
1 Tbs Sugar
Salt to taste

Directions:
Cook Rotini pasta in boiling water 8-10 minutes.
Saute' zucchini and carrots in olive oil until soft. Add stewed tomatoes, tomato paste, sugar and seasonings and cook covered 20-30 minutes. Add cooked rotini, and mix well. Serve in bowls, and top with freshly grated Parmesan cheese.

Description: 
Delicious vegetarian pasta dish. So rich and delicious, you won't even miss the meat!
