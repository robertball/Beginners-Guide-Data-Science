Title:
Lemon Coconut Scones for Vegans/Vegetarians/Lactose Intolerants Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
3 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup margarine, softened
1/2 cup flaked coconut
1 tbsp. grated lemon peel
2 tbsp. lemon juice
1 tsp. almond extract
3/4 - 1 cup vanilla soy milk
dried cranberries (craisins) Optional
Lemon Sugar Glaze
1/4 cup confectionary sugar
1/4 cup reg. granulated sugar
2 tbsp. lemon juice
some grated lemon rind

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Combine first 4 dry ingredients and then cut in the margarine. Crumble with your fingers until it resembles lumpy corn meal (1 min)  Mix the soy milk with the lemon peel, juice and extract. Mix into the flour mixture just until blended then fold in  the coconut and the cranberries if desired. It should be a stiff batter. Spoon a non-stick cookie sheet approx 9-12 heaping mounds of the batter and bake for 15 - 20 minutes or until light brown. Let them cool for 10 min then brush on lightly the Lemon sugar glaze.
Glaze-
Mix the sugars with the rind then add the juice.

Description: 
This is a great light breakfast treat I make for my son who is lactose intolerant. Its a nice combination of flavors thats real easy and fast to make. The glaze is optional depending on how sweet you like your scones. These are delicious!
