Title:
Grandma's Simple Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb lean ground beef
1 small onion
1 cup of carrot coins
1 celery stalk chopped
3 cups beef stock or bouillon
2 cups water
1 tsp salt
2 cups broad noodles

Directions:
Brown ground beef in your soup pot with onions and celery.  Drain off fat and return to pot.
Add stock, salt and water with carrot coins and bring to the boil.  Simmer on medium heat for 10 minutes.
Add noodles and continue to simmer until noodles are done.
Serve with warm biscuits.

Submitter:

Description: 
Ready in less than 30 minutes this flavourful beef soup will please even picky eaters.
