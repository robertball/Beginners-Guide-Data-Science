Title:
Cocktail Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pk small ritz or oyster crackers
3/4 C oil
1 pk Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing
3 t  garlic powder
2 t dill weed
2 T parsley flakes.

Directions:
Mix all together in bowl. Add crackers. and stir well. Keep in airtight container.
