Title:
Michele's Fresh Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/705757.jpg
Ingredients:
6 heads fresh broccoli ( Just Florets Not Stems)
1/2 to 3/4 red onion
1/2 pound bacon
3/4 cup  Golden raisins
1/2 Cup Sunflower seeds
3/4 cup Honey Roasted Sliced almonds
1 cup Mayonnaise or Miracle Whip
1/3 or 1/2 cup white sugar
2 tablespoons Balsalmic vinegar

Directions:
Place bacon in a deep skillet and cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Cool and crumble.
Cut the broccoli into bite-size pieces and cut the onion into thin bite-size slices. Combine with the bacon, raisins, Honey Roasted Almonds or Cashews and mix well. Let sit in Fridge ovenight
To prepare the dressing, mix the mayonnaise, sugar and vinegar together until smooth. Stir into the salad, let chill and serve.
