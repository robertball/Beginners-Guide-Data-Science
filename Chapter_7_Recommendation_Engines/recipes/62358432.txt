Title:
Sweet Potatoe Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkgs refigerated cresent rolls
8 frozen yam patties cut in half
1 1/2 cup sugar
1 1/2 sticks butter
1 1/2 cups water
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla flavoring
ground nuts (I use pecans) for sprinkling on top
1 tsp each nutmeg and cinnamon

Directions:
Unroll cresent rolls into triangles and place half yam into each and reroll to cover yam.  Place in greased 9 x 12 baking pan (I prefer glass).  Heat sugar, butter and water just unitil disolves then add vanilla, nutmeg and cinnamon.  Stir mixture and then pour over rolls, then top each roll with some nuts.
Bake according to cresent roll pkg about 18 mins.  or until golden brown!!  Serve warm or cold  refigerate leftovers if there are any!  I sometimes half recipe too!!
