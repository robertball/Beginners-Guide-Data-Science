Title:
Sweettreats's Holiday Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2829644.jpg
Ingredients:
12 ounces fresh Cranberries
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup light brown sugar, packed
1/2 cup orange juice
1/2 cup water
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
In a medium sized saucepan add all of the ingredients except the vanilla and cook for 15 to 20 minutes. As the cranberries cook they will start to pop. Once the cranberries have cooked and the sauce starts to thicken remove from heat and add the vanilla. Chill for two hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Angela Welch
Description: 
This recipe has been a family favorite for many years.
