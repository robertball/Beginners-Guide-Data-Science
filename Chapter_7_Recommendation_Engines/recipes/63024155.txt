Title:
Sausage and Pepperoni Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Lasagna Noodles
1 jar of high-quality marinara sauce
1 container of ricotta cheese
1 package of low-moisture mozzarella shredded cheese
3/4 cup of shredded Parmesan cheese
1 egg (optional)
1/2 roll of hot Jimmy Dean sausage, browned and crumbled
1 stick of pepperoni, diced
1/2 onion, diced
4 cloves of garlic, minced
1 tablespoon of italian seasoning
Dash of garlic powder, salt and pepper to season ricotta

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Boil water for lasagna noodles, cook until al dente, drain and set aside
Chop onions and heat up stick of butter in a large skillet
Brown sausage and onions until cooked through, throw in garlic for the last minute or so and remove all three ingredients from skillet, set aside
Brown pepperoni in the same skillet and set aside with sausage after draining off the grease
Mix ricotta, egg, mozzarella, parmesan, salt, pepper, italian seasoning, and garlic salt in a mixing bowl
Cover bottom of baking dish with spaghetti sauce
Trim lasagna noodles as needed and layer, overlapping, to cover the bottom of the dish
Layer cheese mixture, meat mixture, sauce, and noodles until used up
Top lasagna with remaining parmesan and sauce
Bake for 35 - 40 minutes, turning halfway through
Let cool for 10 minutes and serve
