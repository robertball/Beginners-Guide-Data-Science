Title:
Jalapeno And Lime Pork Loin With A Pineapple-glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/298482.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup pineapple juice
3/4 cup sugar
2 jalapenos chopped
3/4 tsp. allspice
juice from 1 lime
(2)- 3/4 lb  pork loin
S&P; to taste
chopped cilantro for garnish

Directions:
Preheat oven to 500.  Bring 3/4 of the cup of pineapple juice and sugar to a boil in a saucepan over high heat, stirring until sugar dissolves.  Cover and boil until syrup is a deep brown, about 8-10 minutes.  Remove from heat and add the 1/4 cup of pineapple juice, jalapenos and allspice then last lime juice.  Season with salt and pepper.  Reserve 1/4 of the glaze for later in separate bowl.
Cover a baking sheet with foil and add pork loin.  Season with S&P.  Brush glaze onto pork loin from saucepan.  Bake for 15-20 minutes or so until about medium or 145 F.  Slice pork and drizzle with glaze and sprinkle with cilantro!
