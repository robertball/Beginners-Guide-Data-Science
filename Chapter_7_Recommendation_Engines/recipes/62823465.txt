Title:
Jalapeno Rosemary Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. Olive Oil
1 c. Heavy Cream
1/2 Parmesan
1 Jalapeno
2 Cloves Garlic Minced
1 tsp. Dried Rosemary

Directions:
Saute garlic and jalapeno in the olive oil.  Add the heavy cream and bring to a simmer.  Reduce heat and add parmesan cheese.  Continue cooking on low until cheese is melted.  Add salt and pepper as desired.

Submitter: 
SaraLinh
Description: 
A creamy spicy alfredo, great for a dipping sauce!
