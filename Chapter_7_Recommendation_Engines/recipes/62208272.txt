Title:
Spicy Italian Sausage and Lentil Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the lentils:
1 cup black beluga lentils
2.5 cups water
pinch salt
1 tblsp olive oil
1 medium onion chopped
1lb (4) spicy Italian sausage
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 small eggplant chopped
1 small hot pepper chopped
1/2lb baby bella mushrooms
ground black pepper
salt
14oz can chopped tomatoes

Directions:
Put the lentils on to cook with the water and salt. Bring to the boil and simmer till tender and dry. About 25 minutes.
Meanwhile:
Heat the olive oil and saute the onion.
When slightly browned add the sausages and brown all over.
Add the garlic and till fragrant.
Add the eggplant, pepper, mushrooms, ground pepper and salt to tast and cook till all getting some colour.
Add the tinned tomatoes.
Turn the heat down, cover and simmer till sausage pretty much cooked and lentils done.
Remove sausages, cut them into chunks and return to pan with the lentils.
Cook uncovered until fuly cooked and flavours blended - about 5 minutes.
