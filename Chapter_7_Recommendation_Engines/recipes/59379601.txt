Title:
delicious angel hair pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz angel hair pasta
1 tbs. extra virgin olive oil
1 tsp. chopped roasted pimentos
2 tbs. lemon juice
1 tsp. white wine vinegar
1 tsp. white sugar
2 tbs. fresh chopped parsley
1 tbs. fresh chopped basil
1 tbs. fresh chopped oregano
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1/4 tsp. onion powder
3 tbs. Parmesan or Romano Cheese
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
cook angel hair according to package's directions, drain
heat a nonstick pan over medium-high heat; add: oil, peppers, lemon juice, vinegar, sugar, 1 tbs. parsley, basil, oregano, garlic powder, and onion powder. Cook until heated through and fragrant. Add cheese,and  pasta, salt, and pepper to taste. Plate; garnish with remaining parsley, and serve immediately with some fresh chopped tomato.

Submitter:

Description: 
Angel hair pasta cooked, and served with essentially a hot pasta salad dressing and cheese.
