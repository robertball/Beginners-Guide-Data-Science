Title:
Easy Ham & Swiss Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chopped ham
1/2 cup chopped or shredded swiss cheese
2 1/2 cups uncooked egg noodles
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/4 cup water
1/2 cup white wine
1 cup dry Stovetop stuffing

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
Boil the egg noodles, drain and set aside.
Mix the water, wine, and cream of mushroom soup.
In a large greased casserole dish, pour the soup mixture over the egg noodles, ham and cheese and mix together.
Sprinkle the dry Stovetop stuffing over the top of the dish.
Bake uncovered at 375 degrees for 30 minutes.
This is great served with broccoli, asparagus or a salad!

Submitter: 
Mallory Brown
Description: 
This casserole is perfect comfort food for the day after a holiday when you have leftover ham but don't feel like cooking!
