Title:
Wheat Bran Quick bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c Unbleached wheat flour
1   c whole wheat flour
1   c wheat bran
3/4 c brown sugar
1   tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 c chopped walnuts
1/4 c chopped pecans
1/2 c dried cranberries
1/2 c dried apricots
1 egg
1/2 c milk
1 c orange juice
1/3 c oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Grease  9'5'3 loaf pan
In a large bowl, mix together egg, milk, orange juice, and oil.
In a separate bowl, combine flours, bran, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, buts, and cranberries.
Add dry mixture into wet and stir until just combined (don't overmix). Pour into prepared loaf pan. Bake 55-60 minutes or until the bread is firm in the center.
Cool in pan 10 minutes, remove to rack and cool before slicing.
