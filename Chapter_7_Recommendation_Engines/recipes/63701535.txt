Title:
Spinach & Artichoke Dip! Recipe

Ingredients:
1 package (10 oz./300 g) frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
1 package (8 oz.) cream cheese, softened
3/4 cup Hellmann's Mayonnaise (I ended up using Hellmann's Mayo with Olive Oil)
1 1/2 cups Monterey Jack cheese1 package Knorr Vegetable recipe mix
1 can (14 oz.) artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
2 cloves garlic

Directions:
1.) Preheat oven to 350Â°.
2.) Combine all ingredients except 1/2 cup of cheddar cheese. Spoon into a 2-quart casserole, then top with remaining 1/2 cup of cheese.
3.) Bake for 30-35 minutes or until dip is hot.
