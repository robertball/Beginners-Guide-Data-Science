Title:
Baked Ziti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744732.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb dry ziti pasta
salt
3 1/2 cups quick marinara sauce (recipe follows)
1/2 cup grated pecorino romano
1 lb mozzarella, 1/2 cut into 1/2" cubes,1/2 thinly sliced
pepper and rep pepper flakes to taste
Quick Marinara Sauce:
2 TBS olive oil
1/4 medium onion, diced (about 3 TBS)
3 garlic cloves, chopped (or 1/4 + 1/8 tsp garlic powder)
3 1/2 whole, peeled, canned tomatoes in puree (28 oz can), chopped
1 tsp thyme
1 tsp basil
2 tsp salt
pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
Cook pasta al dente, drain, toss with sauce, 1/4 cup parmesan, romano, cubed mozzarella, and black and red pepper
Put in oiled 9" x 13" pan, cover with remaining cheese, bake 30 minuted or until lightly browned
Quick Marinara Sauce:
Heat oil on medium-high heat, saute onion and garlic until brown, add tomatoes and herbs, lower heat; simmer covered 10 minutes. Add salt and pepper
