Title:
Buster Bar Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust
1 lb Oreo Cookies, crushed
1/2 c butter, melted
Filling
1/2 gallon vanilla ice cream, softened
1 1/2 Spanish Peanuts
2 c powder sugar
2/3 c choc chips
1 1/2 c evaporated milk
1/2 c butter
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Make Crust by mixing together the cookie crumbs and melted butter.
Press into 9 X 13 pan.
Spread ice cream over crust and cool over night.
Put peanuts on top of ice cream.
Combine in saucepan: powdered sugar, choc chips, evaporated milk and butter.
Bring to a boil and cook for 8 minutes, stirring.
Remove from heat and add vanilla.
Let stand until semi-cool.
Pour mixture of ice cream and cool overnight.
