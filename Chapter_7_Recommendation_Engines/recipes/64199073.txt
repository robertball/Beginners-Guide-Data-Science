Title:
Chicken BBQ Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast halves (skinless & boneless)
1 Tbsp Dijon mustard
2 Tbsp vinegar
1/4 cup catsup
2 Tbsp molasses
1/4 tsp Worcestershire sauce
2 cloves garlic, minced
Dash Tabasco sauce

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except chicken.
Pour 1/2 of marinade over chicken and marinate in refrigerator for 1 hour.  Remove chicken and reserve marinade.
Broil or grill for 10 minutes.  Turn, coat chicken with marinade and continue cooking until done.  Heat remaining 1/2 marinade to boiling.  Simmer 2 minutes and spoon over chicken before serving.
