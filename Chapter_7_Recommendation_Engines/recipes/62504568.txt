Title:
Chicken Puffs for 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cubed (or beef for more of a patsie)
1.5 tablespoons chopped onion
1 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
diced carrots
3 oz package cream cheese
2 tablespoons butter
1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated crescent roll dough
potato & celery would be good additions also.

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over medium heat, slowly cook and stir skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, onion and garlic. Cook until onions are tender and chicken is lightly browned.
In a medium bowl, blend chicken mixture, cream cheese and butter until creamy.
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Unroll croissants and divide in half to create 4 rectangles. Place approximately one tablespoon of the chicken mixture on each rectangle. Fold into balls.
Arrange balls on a large baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven 15 minutes, or until golden brown.
