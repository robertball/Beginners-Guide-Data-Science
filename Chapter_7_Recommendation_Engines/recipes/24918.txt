Title:
Cold Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/69549.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
1/2 pound cooked baby shrimp

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix sour cream, dry Italian-style salad dressing mix and baby shrimp. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.

Submitter: 
WENGERT
Description: 
This unassuming dip, loaded with shrimp and Italian-style seasonings, is simple to make and hard to resist. It is best served with rippled potato chips. Enjoy!!
