Title:
chocolate and peanut butter cracker cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
ritz crackers
creamy peanut butter
chocolate almond bark

Directions:
melt almond bark according to package directions.
spread peanut butter on one Ritz cracker and place another craker on top of it.
dip crackers in almond bark and let set until almond bark is cool.

Submitter: 
DEBOKC
Description: 
these taste just like Reeses peanut butter cups and are so easy to make!!!
