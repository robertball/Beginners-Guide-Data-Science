Title:
Alexander's 5 Ingredient Blender Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter
2 medium sized bananas
2 large eggs
Â½ teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
2 Tablespoons honey, optional
dash of vanilla, optional
mini chocolate chips, optional

Directions:
1. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees.
2. Lightly grease your muffin pan.
3. Put all the ingredients into a blender except the chocolate chips (if using) and blend until well mixed.
4. Stir in chocolate chips.
5. Pour batter into muffin pan.
6. Drop a couple of chocolate chips onto the top of each muffin.
7. Bake 13-15 minutes.
8. Muffins will puff up quite a bit but will level after cooling.
9. Serve and enjoy!
