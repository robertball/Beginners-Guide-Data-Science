Title:
Strawberry Kiwi Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2260802.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups fresh ripe strawberries, stems removed
1 1/2 cups vanilla yogurt
1 cup orange juice
2 bananas
2 kiwis, peeled
1/4 cup honey

Directions:
Blend strawberries, yogurt, orange juice, bananas, kiwis, and honey together in a blender until smooth.

Delicious and refreshing beverage, great for any time of the day!
