Title:
Moroccan Couscous Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups diced butternut squash
2 onions diced
1 1/2 cups diced carrots (4)
1 1/2 diced zucchini (2)
2 tbsps. olive oil
salt & pepper
1 1/2 cups chicken broth
2 tbsps. butter
1/4 tsp. cumin
1/2 tsp. saffron threads
1 1/2 cups couscous
2 scallions, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375*F Toss squash, onions and zucchini with olive oil and salt and pepper. Roast for 25-30 minutes, turning once. In a small saucepan bring broth to a boil and turn off heat.  Add the butter, cumin and saffron threads and allow to steep for 15 minutes. Bring back to boil. Place couscous and veggies together in a large bowl. Pour chicken broth over couscous, cover with plastic wrap and let stand for 15 minutes.  Add the scallions and toss together with a fork. Serve warm.
