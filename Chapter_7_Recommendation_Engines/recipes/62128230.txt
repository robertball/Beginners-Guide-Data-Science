Title:
Dark Chocolate Cake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*  1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
* 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
* 1/4 teaspoon salt
* 2 large bars of Lindt Dark Chocolate
* 1/2 cup walnuts or pecans (optional)
* 6 tablespoons butter, softened
* 1 cup white sugar
* 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
* 2 eggs

Directions:
1.   Combine flour, baking powder and salt. Set aside.
2. Melt chocolate over low heat. Cream butter and sugar. Add vanilla, and eggs add flour mixture and remaining chocolate chips.
3. Wrap in plastic wrap, and freeze for 20 minutes.
4. Make small balls. Place on a non greased cookie sheet and bake for 10 minutes at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C, Gas Mark 4) Cool on a wire rack.
