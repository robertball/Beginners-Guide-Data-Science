Title:
Great White Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1273815.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 fluid ounces vodka
1 fluid ounce white cranberry juice
1/2 fluid ounce triple sec
1/4 fluid ounce lime juice
1 cup ice
3 drops grenadine syrup (optional)

Directions:
Combine vodka, cranberry juice, triple sec, and lime juice in a cocktail shaker. Add ice; cover and shake. Strain into a cocktail glass. Add grenadine to drink, which will sink to the bottom.

Submitter: 
sonjagroset
Description: 
This is basically a white Cosmopolitan, but a fun and delicious drink to serve during Shark Week.
