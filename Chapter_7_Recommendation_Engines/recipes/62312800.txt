Title:
Chinese Jicama Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound jicama
1 tablespoon black or red rice vinegar
1 teaspoon sugar
1 pinch salt
1 small drizzle sesame oil

Directions:
Peel jicama and shed. Combine all incredients in bowl. Toss. Leave to marinate 4 hours or overnight.
