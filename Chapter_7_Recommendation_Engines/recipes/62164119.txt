Title:
Pineapple Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (28 ounce) can of crushed pineapple in natural juice
1 cup of sugar
2 cups of Ritz Cracker Crumbs
1 stick of melted butter
2 1/2 cups of grated sharp cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a saucepan at medium low heat combine the pineapple in its own juice along with the sugar, still and heat until sugar is completely dissolved.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In a bowl combine the pineapple mixture, ritz cracker crumbs, melted butter and 2 cups of cheese, mix well. Pour mixture into a casserole dish.  Bake uncovered for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown. Add the rest of the 1/2 cup of cheese to the top and let it sit in the oven until melted. Serve immediately. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
K. McKenzie
Description: 
I cooked this a few years back for Thanksgiving and my family loved it! It is great as a side dish or even something sweet!
