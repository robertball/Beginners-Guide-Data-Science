Title:
Classy Ham Caserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16oz of your favorite Pasta style
3/4 cup of Mayonaise
2 cups cubed Ham
1 Medium diced Sweet Onion
1 Bundle of Asparagus
16oz shredded Chedder Cheese
1 cup Breadcrumbs

Directions:
Use the directions on the pasta to cook it appropriately and set aside.
Cube 2 cups of Ham
Dice 1 medium Sweet Onion
Chop 1 bundle of Asparagus in bite sized pieces
Mix in 12oz of the shredded Chedder Cheese
Combine the Pasta, Mayonaise, Ham, Onion, Cheese and Asparagus in a large bowl and mix thoroughly. Add salt and pepper according to your preference.
Preheat the Oven to 350 degrees.
Put the mixture into a 9x13 baking dish. Top with the remaining 4oz of shredded Chedder Cheese and then with 1 cup of Breadcrumbs.
Bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees.
***Alternative - If you would like to save a few dollars, you could substitute a bag of frozen peas for the asparagus.***
