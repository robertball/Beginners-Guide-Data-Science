Title:
Jim's Shrimp Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 lb butter softened
1/4 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup grated parmesan
5 slices bacon cooked and broken
1 pound shrimp
i - 14 oz package egg noodles

Directions:
Saute shrimp in small amt of butter.
Cream butter...beat in cream little at a time & cheese.
Toss at room temperature.
Boil egg noodles al dente then rinse well in hot water.
Place sauce  shrimp and bacon on top and stir. ENJOY!
