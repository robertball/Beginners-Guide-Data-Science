Title:
Tori's Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ground beef
Onions, diced
Green bell peppers, diced
Dried basil
Garlic powder
Paprika
Cayenne pepper
Salt
Black pepper
Hot sauce
1 egg

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a medium bowl. Spices, hot sauce, peppers and onions are to taste.
Form patties into handful-sized balls. Flatten slightly and press indent into middle of one side of each ball.
Grill with indent side down first, until patties are brown and dripping. Flip and grill for an additional 5-7 minutes.
For sliders: Follow recipe as above, except form the patties about half the regular size.

Submitter: 
Tori Spice
Description: 
Juicy and delicious! Flavoring is to taste - make as spicy or as mild as desired.
