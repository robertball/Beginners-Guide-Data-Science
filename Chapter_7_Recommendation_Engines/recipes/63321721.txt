Title:
Homemade Soap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs of fat
1 can of lye (Sodium hydroxide)
1 quart of cold water
2 gallon enamel ware vessel
a long handled wooden spoon
a pair of rubber gloves
1/2 cup boarax dissolved in 2/3 cup water
1/2 cup ammonia
perfume

Directions:
Wash Grease: 1. Melt and pour into a vessel with 2 to 3 quarts of water. 2. Bring to a boil. Stir frequently. 3. Cool until fat can be lifted off the water. 4. If fat is not "clean", repeat steps through 3. Place the enamel-ware container on the floor (floor should br covered with newspapers). Very slowly pour the lye into the water. Let stand intil cool. Add to the lye water about one pound of the melted lukewarm grease. Add ammonia and stir. Add borax and stir. Add grease and stir (5 to 10 minutes each time) until all 5 pounds has been used. Add perfume. Stir 20 or 30 minutes longet until soap is consistency of fudge. Pour into glass pans until soap is about 1-in thick. Allow to stand until soap is firm to the touch. Score with a sharp knife. Allow the soap to set for 6 or 8 hours before removing from pans. Remove from pans and stack on trays. Turn soap daily. Allow to dry for 2 or 3 weeks before using.
