Title:
Irish Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 24oz bag frozen potato Wedges
1 16oz can of refried beans
1 8oz Salsa
1 can Frito Lay mild cheddar cheese dip
1 tomato diced
Chopped green onions
sliced Black olives
sour cream

Directions:
Bake potato wedges according to package directions. Arrange wedges on a large serving plate in a circle. Top with heated beans. Pour salsa and heated cheese over beans and potatoes. Sprinkle with tomatoes green onion and olives then a dollop of sour cream.
