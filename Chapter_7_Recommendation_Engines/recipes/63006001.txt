Title:
Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2# Ground Beef
2 slices white bread for soft crumbs
1/4 C. Milk
1/2 C. Onion(chopped in food processor)
1/4 C. Red Pepper(chopped in food processor)
1/4 C. Celery(chopped in food processor)
2 Eggs(slightly beaten)
1 t. Seasoning Salt(heavy teaspoon)
1/4 t. Pepper
1 t. Oregano
1/2 t. Granulated Garlic
2-3 Bay Leaves
1/4 C. Ketchup
2 T. Brown Sugar
1 T. Prepared Mustard

Directions:
Combine bread crumb and milk;let set for 5 min. Add Gr. Beef, eggs,onions, red pepper, celery,seasoning salt, pepper,oregano, garlic and combine thoughly by hand. Form a loaf in a shallow casserole and top with bay leaves. Cook for 1hr. at 375F. Combine ketchup,brown sugar,and mustard. Spoon over meat loaf after 1st hr. of cooking. Return to oven for another 30min. If roasting potatoes with the meat loaf;arrange around meat loaf when you first put them in the oven.
