Title:
Whipped Scotch Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6000238.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound butter at room temperature.
1 cup icing sugar, also called confectioners sugar.
1/2 cup cornstarch.
3 cups of All Purpose flour.
1/2 teaspoon salt.
1 teaspoon vanilla.
Cherries, the kind you get in a jar( optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Cream the butter.
Add the rest of the ingredients and beat until fluffy with a wooden spoon.
It does come together, just takes a couple of minutes.
Drop from teaspoon onto greased or parchment paper lined cookie sheet.
Press a bit of red or green or red cherry on top.
Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes or lightly browned.
If your using the cherries, drain them on a paper towel before putting them on the cookie.
FOOTNOTES::
I am lactose intolerant and i found using  Natrel lactose free butter works very well.
These freeze very well, but if you are  like me you will eat them  right out of the freezer.
