Title:
Brown Sugar Ham and Potato Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ham cut into strips
1 Tablespoon butter
1 Tablespoon brown sugar
1 can cream of mushroom soup
2/3 c evaporated milk
1/3 c water
1/4 c chopped onion
1/2 t salt
1/8t pepper
3 cups potatoes sliced thin
1 c carrots cut into bite size pieces

Directions:
In skillet melt butter, add brown sugar and brown ham strips. Remove ham and in same skillet add the soup, milk,water, onion, salt and pepper. Mix, and then add sliced potatoes and carrots. Cover and simmer over low heat stirring once in awhile, until vegtables are tender. (35 minutes)Place ham on top and simmer 10 minutes more. Serve.

Submitter: 
Julia
Description: 
This has a sweet and salty taste balanced out by the creamy potatoes. My kids have loved this dish from an early age, and still love it as teenagers.
