Title:
Hash Brown Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/819596.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups shredded hash browns, thawed & drained
1/2 stick butter
3 large eggs, beaten
1 cup half-and-half
3/4 cup diced cooked ham
1/2 cup diced green onions
1 cup shredded cheddar
salt
freshly ground pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Gently press drained hash brown between paper towels to dry them as best as possible.
In a 9-inch pie pan, toss the hash browns with butter into plate.
Press into the bottom and up the sides to form a crust.
Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown and starting to crisp.
Meanwhile, in a large mixing bowl, combine the remaining ingredients.
When the has brown crust is ready pour the egg mixture over it and return to oven.
Lower the oven temperature to 350 degrees and bake for 30 minutes until the quiche is light golden brown on top and puffed.

This is a good recipe using ham, cheddar, eggs, hash browns and half-and-half. We made it for Cotton Bowl 2011 and everyone loved it!
