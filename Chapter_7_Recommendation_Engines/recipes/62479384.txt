Title:
Popa's Pickled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/833841.jpg
Ingredients:
1 12oz jar of hot yellow chili peppers (including brine)
2 tablespoons pickling spice (I use McCormick)
1 cup white wine vinegar
1 1/2 cups water
1 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon turmeric
2 teaspoons salt
8 eggs hard-boiled and peeled

Directions:
Combine chili peppers (including brine), pickling spice, vinegar, water, sugar, turmeric and salt. Pour into a 1/2 gal mason jar.
Place eggs in mixture and seal, place in refrigerator. Eggs will be ready after 2 days. Better if longer.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
These simple to make pickled eggs are a great snack along with pretzels.
