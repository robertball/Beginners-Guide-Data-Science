Title:
Vegetable Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick Butter, separated by tablespoons
5 Tbsp Flour
2 cups Milk
2 1/2 cups Sharp Cheddar Cheese or Monteray Jack Cheese, shredded
1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 tsp Pepper

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Remove from heat and swirl, if necessary, to be careful not to burn the butter.
Whisk in flour until smooth.
Gradually whisk in a 1/4 cup of milk. Making sure to stir in the flour at the corners of the pan.
Stop stirring, then after about a minute, whisk in another 1/4 cup of milk; continue process until all the milk is stirred in.
When sauce is at the desired thickness, whisk in cheese a little at a time until well blended.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
Serve over vegetables.

This is a quick and easy sauce that tastes delicious on vegetables.
