Title:
Italian Tomatoe Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 on the vine ripe tomatoes or hot house tomatoes
1/2 English seedless cucumber
1/2 red onion sliced thin
salt and pepper to taste
1 tbsp chopped fresh oregano
1/4 cup or less extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
mix all ingredients in large glass bowl toss to combine. Let sit 30min to blend flavours.
