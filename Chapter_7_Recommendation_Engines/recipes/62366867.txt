Title:
tommys favorite banana bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/408398.jpg
Ingredients:
preheat  oven to 350 cook 1 hour
1 cup of butter
2 cups of sugar
4 eggs
3 cups of flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon of salt
2 cups mashed bananas
1 cup of sour cream or plain yogurt
2 teaspoons of vanilla
chopped nuts(optional)

Directions:
cream butter, sugar,eggs together.combine dry ingredients blend both mixtures together until well mixed.Add mashed bananas,sour cream vanilla. pour into greased loaf pan(makes 1 large, or 3 small
loaves.) sprinkle top with sugar.

Submitter: 
Diane Dizacomo
Description: 
a favorite family recipe that calls for sour cream or plain yogurt. Delicious! This is the double batch version.
