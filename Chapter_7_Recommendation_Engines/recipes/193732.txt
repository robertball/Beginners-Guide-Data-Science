Title:
Saucy Baked Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990500.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups FRENCH'S® Original or Cheddar French Fried Onions
1 (1.8 ounce) package white sauce mix
2 cups milk
2 cups shredded Cheddar or cubed American cheese
3 cups cooked elbow pasta
1 cup diced boiled ham
1/2 cup frozen peas

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Crush French Fried Onionss in plastic bag; set aside.
Prepare white sauce mix according to package directions using 2 cups milk. Stir in cheese and cook until cheese melts.
Combine pasta, ham, and peas in 2-quart baking dish. Stir in cheese sauce and sprinkle with crushed onions. Bake 30 minutes or until hot and bubbly.

Submitter: 
French's®
Description: 
Baked macaroni and cheese is made into a meal with ham, peas and a crunchy topping of crushed French Fried Onions.
