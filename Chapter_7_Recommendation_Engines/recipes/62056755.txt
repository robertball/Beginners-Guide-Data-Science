Title:
Corn with Jalapenos and Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1 16oz bag frozen corn, thawed
-1/4 cup margarine or butter
-1 small onion, finely chopped
-2 Tbs pickled jalapeno, finely chopped
-2/3 cup cream cheese (lowfat ok)
-1/3 cup freshly grated parmesean
-salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt butter over medium heat in 2 qt saucepan, add onion and sautee for 4-5 min, until translucent.  Add corn and remaining ingredients, cook on low heat until heated thru and cheeses have melted.

Submitter: 
DARLA
Description: 
Spicy corn with a thick sauce of cream cheese and parmesean.  Quick and easy. The pickled jalapenos have more heat than fresh, so taste before you get too brave.
