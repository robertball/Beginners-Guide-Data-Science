Title:
Thai Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lbs flank steak
1 bottle Thai peanut stir fry and dipping sauce
1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
1 lb peeled baby carrots
6 green onions
1/4 cup coarsely chopped peanuts

Directions:
Trim flank steak of all visible fat.  Cut into thin strips.
Cut green onions, including green parts, into 1 inch pieces
Place steak in crock-pot
Add all other ingredients except coconut milk and peanuts
Cook on low for 8-10 hours or high 4-5 hours.
30 mins before serving, add coconut milk and peanuts.
Serve over steamed rice.

Submitter: 
Marty C
Description: 
Thai beef recipe that is easy to make in a crock-pot and is a pot-luck favorite.
