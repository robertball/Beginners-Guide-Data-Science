Title:
Beer for my Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef chuck
1/2 large white onions, chopped
2 (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes with juice
4 (small cans) tomato sauce
1 (12 fluid ounce) can beer
2 (15 ounce) cans spicy chili beans
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
3 tablespoons hot pepper sauce (e.g. Tabascoâ„¢),  (optional)
1/4 cup chili powder
3 tablespoons red pepper flakes, or to taste (optional)

Directions:
Crumble the ground chuck into a skillet over medium heat. Cook, stirring occasionally until evenly browned. Drain grease. Transfer the beef to a large soup pot. Add onions, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, beer and chili beans. Season with Worcestershire sauce, hot pepper sauce, chili powder, and red pepper flakes, if using.
Cover the pot, and simmer over low heat for 1 hour. Turn off heat and serve.
