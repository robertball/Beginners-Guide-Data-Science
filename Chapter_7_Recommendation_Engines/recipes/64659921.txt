Title:
Cornbread Honey Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages corn bread muffin mix
1/8 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup milk
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1 (15 ounce) can canned cream-style corn
2/3 cup honey

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
Thoroughly grease two standard 12-cup muffin tins.
Place the muffin mix and baking soda in a large mixing bowl .
Stir in the milk and egg.
Mix in the corn and honey.
Pour batter into prepared muffin tins.
Bake in preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, rotating pans halfway through baking time.
Muffins are done when a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
Add 6 tablespoons canned or jarred jalapenos, diced and well-drained and 2 cups cheddar cheese to make jalapeno cheddar cornbread.

Submitter: 
Mark Adams
Description: 
Sweet, moist corn muffins using corn bread muffin mix and honey.
