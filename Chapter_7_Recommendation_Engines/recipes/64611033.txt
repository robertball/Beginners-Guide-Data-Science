Title:
BACON & SMOKED GOUDA CAULIFLOWER MASH (LOW CARB AND GLUTEN FREE) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4169492.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups cauliflower florets
3 Tbsp heavy cream
2 Tbsp butter
½ tsp kosher salt
¼ tsp black pepper
¼ tsp garlic powder
4 slices of cooked bacon
? cup shredded Smoked Gouda cheese
salt and pepper to taste
Approximate nutrition info per cup: 282 calories, 22g fat, 6g net carbs, 12g protein

Directions:
Place the cauliflower, heavy cream, butter, salt, pepper, and garlic powder in a pot and boil on medium-high heat for 18 - 20 minutes, or until soft. Transfer the cauliflower and liquid to a magic bullet or food processor. Add the bacon and Smoked Gouda. Blend until smooth and creamy. Season with additional salt and pepper as desired.
