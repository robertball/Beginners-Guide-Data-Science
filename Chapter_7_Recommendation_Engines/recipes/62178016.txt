Title:
Vanilla Sugar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c sugar
1 whole vanilla bean

Directions:
If vanilla bean is whole, slice down side of bean with back of knife and scrape seeds into airtight container with the sugar. Bury bean in sugar and seal tightly with lid. Let sit for 1 to 2 weeks.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
A lot of european cookie reciepe uses vanilla sugar in place of vanill extract. My mother would make a large conister in august, in prep for her cookie baking season (between Thanksgiving and the beginning of the christmas holidays)
