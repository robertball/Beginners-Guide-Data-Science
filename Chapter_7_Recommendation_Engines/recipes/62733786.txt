Title:
Tilapia Twist Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 to 5 thawed talapia fillets (4 ounces)
1 orange bell pepper (diced)
1/4 cup red onion (diced)
1/2 can diced tomatoes (drained)
garlic salt
McCormick perfect pinch cajun
McCormick lemon pepper california style
salt
2 tbs olive oil extra light

Directions:
Saute peppers and onions for 5 minutes over medium heat in a large skillet. Add tomatoes and season to taste. Cook for 10 minutes longer stirring frequently. Add Talapia and spoon sauce over Talapia.
Lower heat and cover for 5 minutes then flip talapia and cook for 5 more minutes.

Submitter: 
Gene03
Description: 
Tilapia served with orange bell peppers, red onions and tomatoes, a nice twist on Talapia!
