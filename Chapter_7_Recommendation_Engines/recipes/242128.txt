Title:
Olive and Parsley Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3533310.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup pitted olives
1 cup fresh parsley
1/3 cup walnuts
1/4 cup olive oil
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
1 clove garlic

Directions:
Blend olives, parsley, walnuts, olive oil, Parmesan cheese, and garlic in a food processor until evenly mixed.
