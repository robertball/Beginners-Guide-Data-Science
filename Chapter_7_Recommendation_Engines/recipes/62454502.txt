Title:
Italian Chicken Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small cans v8
3 chicken breasts, chopped
1 can tomato sauce
1 box chicken broth
1 cup organic wild rice
3 chopped tomatoes
1 chopped bell pepper
2 chopped zucchini
1 chopped yellow squash
3 chopped carrots
1 chopped onion
Italian seasoning (thyme, oregano, basil)

Directions:
Cook on high in crock pot for 4 hours.
Cook on low in crock pot for 2 hours.
Serve with fresh croutons and corn bread.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb

Great on cool fall nights.
