Title:
Shrimp Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz of rice
10 oz bag of frozen green peas
2 large eggs
1 TB canola oil
1 C chopped green onions
1 TB bottled fresh ginger
1 lb medium, peeled and deveined shrimp
2 TB rice vinegar
2 TB low sodium soy sauce
1 tsp dark sesame oil
1/4 t salt
pinch of crushed red pepper

Directions:
Cook rice in pan. Add peas to pan, stirring well, keep warm.
Coat sides of wok with spray. add eggs, cook one minute or until set.  remove eggs and coarsely chop. add oil to pan and add onions and ginger, saute 1 min.  Add shrimp, saute 2 min or until done.  add rice, peas, and eggs in with the shrimp.  Combine vinegar and remaining ingredients, stir well.
