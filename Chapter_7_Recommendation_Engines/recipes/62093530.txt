Title:
Mississippi ice tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 oz disaronno
1 oz triple sec
1 oz unflavored rum
1 12 oz can of cola

Directions:
Fill a large glass with as much as you like.  Then add the disaronno, triple sec, and the rum.  Finnaly stir all of ingredients together until mixed together and enjoy.

Submitter: 
streetracer
Description: 
it is sort of like a long island ice tea but a different taste. Be careful though you may not realize how much alcohol you are are consuming
