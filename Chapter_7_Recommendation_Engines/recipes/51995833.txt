Title:
Pomantini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz orange grey goose vodka
1/4 cup pom juice
1/2 lemon
grenadine
sugar rimmer

Directions:
Place sugar on rim.
Place grenadine in bottom of martini glass.
Mix vodka, juice, and squeezed half of lemon in martini shaker with ice.
Pour over grenadine.
