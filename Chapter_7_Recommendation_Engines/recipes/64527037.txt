Title:
Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter, softened
1 cup white sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups all-purpose flour plus a tablespoon
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon salt
3 cups rolled oats
3/4 cup raisins
3/4 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
In large bowl, cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
Beat in the eggs and vanilla until fluffy.
Mix raisins, chocolate chips and a tablespoon or so of flour.
Stir together flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
Gradually beat into butter mixture. Stir in oats and raisins.
Place mixture in fridge for about 30 minutes.
Drop by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
Bake 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown.
Cool for 5 minutes on cookie sheet.
Remove from sheet to wire rack.
