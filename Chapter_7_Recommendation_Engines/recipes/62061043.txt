Title:
Veggie Stuffed Steaks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 lb. boneless beef round steak, pounded thin
1/3 cup French salad dressing
1/2 cup shredded carrots
1/2 cup finely chopped onions
1/2 cup finely chopped green pepper
1/2 cup finely chopped celery
1/2 cup water
2 TB vegetable oil
1/2 cup beef broth
1 TB cornstarch

Directions:
Cut meat into four rectangular portions.  Brush with salad dressing and let marinate for at least one hour.  In a saucepan, combine vegetables and water and simmer, covered 7-8 minutes or until crisp and tender.  Drain.  Place about 1/4 cup of vegetables on top of beef and roll up jely roll style and secure with toothpicks.  In a heavy skillet, add oil and place meat in and brown. Add broth.  Cover, simmer about 45 minutes or until tender.  Remove beef rolls and keep warm.  Reserve 1 cup broth.  Combine cornstarch with 1 TB cold water and stir into reserved broth.  Cook and stir until thickened and bubbly.  Serve with meat rolls.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
Round steak is marinated in french dressing and stuffed with vegetables. I like to prepare these in my pressure cooker using 1 1/4 cups of water and one beef buillon cube.  I serve these with mashed potatoes.
