Title:
Linguine with White Clam Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbs Butter, 1 Tbs Oil, 3 Cloves Garlic, 1/2 cup Onion 1/4 in chop, 4 Tbs Flour, 1/2 Cup Dry White wine, 1 Cup Bottled Clam Juice, 1 Tbs Lemon Juice, 1/2 Cup cream or milk, 1 tsp dryed Marjoram, 5 Green Onions Choped, 2-6 oz Cans Choped Clams, 1 lb Linguine, 2 Tbs Choped Parsley, 1 lb Raw pealed and devained Shrimp, Mussels, and any other seafood you like.

Directions:
Start water for pasta, then start pasta. as soon as you start pasta start sauce. Heat large wok or frying pan add oil, butter, garlic, and onion cook onion until tender and clear. Add flour stir for 1 minute. Add wine, clam juice,juice from canned clams and lemon juice. Simmer until slightly thickened add cream and Marjoram. add green onion simmer uncovered 3 minutes until thickened, Add seafood and canned clams until seafood is cooked. Stir finished Linguine into sauce and serve. Sprinkle choped Parsley on top and Serve!.
