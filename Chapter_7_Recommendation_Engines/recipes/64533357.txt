Title:
Cranberry And Pistachio relish recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pack cranberries
1 cup sugar
1 cup pistachios
1 zest of a navel orange
Wedges of the Navel orange (all pith removed)

Directions:
Put the wedges of the orange (No white) in food processor with everything and Process till you achieve the texture you like.
Remove from processor and place in a Bowl
Store in fridge for 2 hours or Overnight..
Serve cold.

Submitter: 
Darin Terwilliger
Description: 
Best holiday relish recipe I have ever made, no left overs had!
