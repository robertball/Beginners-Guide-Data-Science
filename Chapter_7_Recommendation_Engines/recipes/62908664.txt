Title:
WW Cuban-Style Pork and Sweet Potato Slow Cooker Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb. sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1 Lb. lean pork loin, cut into 1 inch pieces
14 1/2 oz canned diced tomatoes, with green chiles
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 cup orange juice
2 medium scallions, chopped (green parts only)
1/2 tsp table salt
1/4 tsp ground cumin
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 1/2 Tbsp fresh lime juice
2 Tbsp Cilantro, fresh, chopped

Directions:
Place potatoes, pork, tomatoes, garlic, orange juice, scallions, salt, cumin & pepper in a 4 to 5 quart slow cooker; cover and cook on low setting for 7 hours.
Stir in lime juice and cilantro; cover slow cooker and cook for 5 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Shannon Campbell
Description: 
Both a little spicy and a little sweet.  Serve with a salad of black beans, orange and red onion tossed with low-fat vinaigrette
