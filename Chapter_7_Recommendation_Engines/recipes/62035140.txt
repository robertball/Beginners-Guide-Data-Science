Title:
marinated pork chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c vegtable oil
1/3 c soy sauce
1/4 c vinegar
2 T worcestershire sauce
1 T lemon juice
1 T prepared mustard
1 t salt
1 t pepper
1 t dried parsley flakes
1 or 2 cloves garlic, minced
6 pork chops

Directions:
Combine all ingredents in a resealable plastic bag. Refridgerate overnight. Discard marninade and grill 20-25 min.

Submitter:

Description: 
Overnight marinating yeilds a tender chop, and very tasty as well!
