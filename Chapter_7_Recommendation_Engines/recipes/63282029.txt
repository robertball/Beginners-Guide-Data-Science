Title:
Refreshing Fruit Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/863621.jpg
Ingredients:
~2 cups water
~1 liter ginger ale
~2 cups white sugar
~1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate
~1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate
~1 pint orange sherbet
~4 cups ice
~1 liter carbonated water
~1 orange or lemon, cut

Directions:
Bring the water to a boil. Add the sugar and boil until it becomes a syrup. Let the mixture cool. In a punch bowl, combine the lemonade, orange juice, ginger ale, and carbonated water. Mix in the sugary “syrup”. Add the ice and sherbet. Cut up thin slices of orange/lemon and put on top of your now finished and delicious, refreshing fruit punch!!! ENJOY!!!!!
