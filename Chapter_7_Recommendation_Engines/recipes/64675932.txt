Title:
Hawaiian Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8oz blocks of cream cheese, softened
3-4 Tbs crushed pineapple and a little juice
5-6 Green onion stems, chopped
1 c pecans

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except for the pecans.  Place in plastic wrap and shape into a ball.  Allow to chill before rolling it in the pecans.
