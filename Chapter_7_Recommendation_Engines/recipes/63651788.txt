Title:
Grand Mother's Holiday marshmallow fruit salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 sliced bananas
bunch of red grapes cut in halves
4 cans lite large cut fruit cocktail
pint old fashioned Whipping cream
1/2 bag mini marshmellos
optional diced pears peeled, peaches...

Directions:
Drain fruit well and combine fruit ingredients.
Whip cream till very stiff add a pinch or 2 of sugar. Toss with fruit.
Stir in marshmallows
refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Deborah Ames
