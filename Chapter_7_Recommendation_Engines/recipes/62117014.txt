Title:
Microwave Hot Broccoli Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box frozen chopped broccoli
1 onion chopped
1 stick butter
1 roll garlic cheese(found in the refrigerator section)
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can sliced mushrooms-drained
1 sm package of sliced almonds

Directions:
Remove wrapper from frozen broccoli.  Microwave on high for 8-10 minutes.  Saute onion in butter for 3-4 minutes at 70%.  Add broccoli, cheese, and soup.  Heat 6  minutes at 70%.  Add mushrooms and almonds.  Heat well.  Serve with tortilla chips or corn chips.
NOTE:  The garlic cheese is in a small roll and is found in the refrigerated section near the cream cheese.

Submitter: 
SHARONAIR
Description: 
Hot appetizer dip with garlic cheese, mushrooms, onions, broccoli and almonds!  My family has been making it for years.  My sister doubles the recipe and it is always gone within 15 minutes!
