Title:
Buttermilk Mashed Taters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds red skin potatoes- washed- cubed and boiled- drained-(saved 1/2 cup water)
1/3 stick butter
1/3 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup milk
1 Tablespoon dry ranch mix- i used homemade- but packeged is fine

Directions:
mashed potatoes adding 1/4 cup of the boiling water- add other ingredients- mash again- add more water if needed- salt and pepper to taste
