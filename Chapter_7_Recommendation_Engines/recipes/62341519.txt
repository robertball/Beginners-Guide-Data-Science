Title:
Grilled Company Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 baking potatoes scrubbed and dried
4 tblsp margarine OR butter
1 small chopped onion
4 tblsp cream cheese
2 slices cooked bacon
1/8 c cheddar cheese
3 pinches salt
3 pinches pepper

Directions:
With a pointed small knife cut a small hole in the end of the fattest part of the potato turn the potato with your knife inserted into the hole to make a clean hollow cut out of the potato.it should hollow out to about 1/2 the size of your potato and go halfway through to the middle. combine ingredients in a small bowl stuff potato with remainder of ingredients. put end bit of the potato back on to cover stuffing. wrap in foil and grill until done. when finished allow to cool slightly before unwrapping. slice down middle to serve top with any leftover stuffing. if you dont want to throw the hollowed potato out wrap in the foil.

Submitter: 
soupisgoodfood33
Description: 
A change of pace to your ordinary potatoes on the grill. These are also good in the oven. You can change what goes inside to suit your tastes.
