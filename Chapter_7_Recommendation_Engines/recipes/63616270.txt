Title:
Orange Guajillo Glazed Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1005836.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Herdez Red Guajillo Mexican Cooking Sauce
1/4 cup orange marmalade
1 tablespoon honey
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
3 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced into cubes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Cover a baking sheet with foil.
In a medium sauce pan over low heat mix together all ingredients, except the sweet potatoes.  Stir until the sauce is smooth and heated through.  Remove from the heat.  Toss in the sweet potatoes and cover thoroughly with the glaze.
Pour out the sweet potatoes and glaze onto the foil covered baking pan.  Cover the pan with foil.
Bake for 20 minutes and then remove the foil increase oven to 400 degrees for 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
These sweet and spicy roasted Mexican sweet potatoes add a different and delicious twist to a favorite side dish.
