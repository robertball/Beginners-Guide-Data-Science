Title:
taco casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1/2 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup salsa
1 (80z) can tomato sauce
1 envelope taco seasoning
1 box corn hard shell tacos, broken
1 cup frozen corn
1 cup chopped black olives
1/2 cup sliced black olives
1 bag taco seasoned cheese blend
toppings of choice: tomato, lettuce, chilis, green onions, etc.

Directions:
preheat oven to 375 degrees.
in sauce pan over medium heat cook hamburger and onion untill no longer pink, drain.
add garlic, tomato sauce, corn and olives.
combine 1/2 can of water with taco seasoning and add to meat mixture, simmer 10 minutes.
break shells and use 6 shells on bottom of a 13x9 baking dish.
layer with meat mixture.
top with remaining shells,top with cheese and sliced olives.
bake 20-25 minutes or untill hot and bubbly.
serve with desired toppings.

Submitter: 
mistycolleen
Description: 
a very easy layered taco dish with black olives and corn.a favorite in our house never leaving any leftovers.
