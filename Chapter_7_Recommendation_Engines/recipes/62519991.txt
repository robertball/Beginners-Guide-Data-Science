Title:
Roasted Green Cabbage Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small head green cabbage (about 2 1/2 #), cut into 8 wedges, core intact
1/2 T EVOO
coarse salt and freshly ground pepper
1 lemon, cut into wedges

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450. Arrange cabbage on a rimmed baking sheet. Brush both sides of wedges with oil. Season w/salt and pepper. Roast, flipping halfway through, until edges are brown and crisp, 25-30 min. Squeeze lemons over cabbage
