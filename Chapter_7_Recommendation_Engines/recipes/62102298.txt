Title:
Dad's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meatloaf:
1 1/2 lb ground beef
1 egg
2 cups seasoned stuffing mix (such as Stove Top)
1/3 cup milk
1 small onion, chopped
Sauce:
1 cup ketchup
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
1 Tablespoon garlic powder
1 Tablespoon lemon pepper
1 Tablespoon cooking sherry

Directions:
For meatloaf:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Grease an 8x5 loaf pan
Combine milk, egg, hamburger, stuffing and onions.
Mix well.
Pat into loaf pan and bake for 35 minutes.
Remove from oven.
Drain grease.
Top with sauce and bake for another 10 minutes, or until sauce is hot.
Make the Sauce:
Combine all  sauce ingredients in a small saucepan.
Heat on low heat for 6-8 minutes, stirring often, until heated through.
Pour on top of meatloaf.
May store leftover sauce in fridge for up to 2 months.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Kotz Walls
Description: 
My dad came up with this recipe for me when I was little because I hated ketchup! Now my kids ask for this all the time. We also use the sauce as a steak sauce. Much tastier (and cheaper) than the stuff you buy
