Title:
Butter Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1990581.jpg
Ingredients:
Filling:
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup butter
2 eggs
1/2 cup maple syrup
1 tbsp. flour
1 tbsp. vanilla extract
pinch of salt
1 cup raisins-soaked in your favorite rum if you like
Pastry: enough for 16 tarts-I use the Tenderflake recipe on the box and freeze any leftover dough or you can purchase the premade tart shells in the freezer section of the grocery store

Directions:
Preheat oven at 350 degrees
Cream ingredients in the order given
Place 1 tsp. of raisins in the bottom of each tart shell
Fill each shell 3/4 full with filling
Bake for 16-18 minutes turning half way for an even finish.

Submitter: 
Inez Quinn
Description: 
These are a big hit every time I make them.  The are an addition in a basket donated at a local fundraiser full of maple products.
