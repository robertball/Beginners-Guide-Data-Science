Title:
Steak and potato wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. sirloin or round steak
7 medium potatoes
2 fresh Jalepeno peppers seeded and chopped
1 med. diced onion
2 15oz cans of rotel tomatoes w/ green chili's
16 thin corn tortillas
shredded cheese
sour cream
1/4 c. vegetable oil

Directions:
heat vegetable oil in a skillet on med/low heat. Cut steak and potatoes into 1" bite size pieces. add potatoes to oil and let simmer on low heat for 10 minutes stirring occasionaly. Add steak, onion and jalepeno. Cover and let simmer until steak is done and potatoes are tender. Drain. Drain the juice out of one can of rotel tomatoes and add that and the other whole can of tomatoes to the meat and potatoes. simmer until hot. serve topped with shredded cheese and sour cream. Heat 4 tortillas wrapped in a damp paper towel for 30 seconds in microwave. Spoon mixture into tortillas and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Kathie Stacy
Description: 
Easy and delicious, tex-mex, stew type dish with a spicy kick. My whole family loves this dish.
