Title:
Holly's "So Good it Will Make You Slap your Mama" Chicken Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup salt
1/3 cup paprika
2 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons onion powder
2 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons dried thyme
2 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons white pepper
1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon black pepper
1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together salt, paprika, onion powder, thyme, white pepper, black pepper, cayenne pepper, and garlic powder. Store in an airtight container.
