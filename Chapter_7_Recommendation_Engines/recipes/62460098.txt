Title:
Spicy Prawn Linguine with Garlic, Tomato and Herbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
¾ Onion - finely chopped
2 cloves Garlic - crushed or finely chopped
¼ to ½ tsp (to taste) dried crushed Chillies
20 or so Langostines (8 Tiger Prawns ) - Shelled and Devined)
10 Cherry Tomatoes - halved
120ml dry White Wine
2 tbsp fresh flatleaf Parsley  - chopped
3 tbsp fresh Basil - chopped
1 tbsp fresh Oregano - chopped
1 Lemon - juice only to taste
250g to 300g Linguine - cooked and drained

Directions:
Heat the olive oil in a pan and fry the onion and garlic for 3-4 minutes, or until softened;
Add the dried chilli and prawns and cook until the prawns turn pink;
Add the tomatoes and wine and cook for 3 to 5 minutes or until wine has reduced a bit;
Stir in the herbs and lemon juice;
Add the cooked linguine and stir to combine well;
Serve.
