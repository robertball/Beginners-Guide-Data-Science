Title:
Big Ray's Toffee Paper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1073785.jpg
Ingredients:
35 saltine crackers
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
2 pkg.(4 oz. each)Semi-Sweet Chocolate, chopped
1 cup  chopped walnuts, toasted

Directions:
Heat oven to 400ÂºF.
Place crackers in single layer in foil-lined 15x10x1-inch pan.
Cook butter and sugar in saucepan on medium-high heat until butter is melted and mixture is well blended, stirring occasionally. Bring to boil; cook 3 min. (Do not stir.) Spread onto crackers.
Bake 5 to 7 min. or until topping is golden brown. Immediately spread on peanut butter while hot, let soften, then spread, put in freezer or fridge to harden. Melt chocolate in microwave and spread over cooled peanut butter. Spread over crackers; top with nuts. Cool. Break into pieces.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
A family favorite for years. Sometimes I will use chocolate, white chocolate or butterscotch chips instead of bar chocolate and substitute  pecans instead of walnuts. Everyone loves it and no one can believe it's made with saltines.
