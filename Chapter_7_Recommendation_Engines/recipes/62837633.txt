Title:
Texas Caviar Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
2   15.5oz cans black eyed peas, rinse and drain
1  10 oz. can diced Rotel tomatoes with green chilies, drained
one sm. green pepper, diced
3/4 cup zesty Italian dressing
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 purple onion, chopped
two avocados diced and covered in lime juice
fresh cilantro to taste

Directions:
Stir together and chill.  Serve with taco chips
