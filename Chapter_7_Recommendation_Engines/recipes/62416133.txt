Title:
Authentic Ceviche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 ounces of raw tilapia, diced
1/3 cup diced onion
14-16 limes
1 cup of corn
1 1/2 roma tomatoes, chopped
1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
1/4 cup clam juice
1/4 cup Clamato
1/2 teaspoon of salt or to taste
tostadas, for serving

Directions:
Combine tilapia and onion in a large bowl.
Squeeze limes into bowl until fish and onion are covered.
Refrigerate, covered with aluminum foil, for 2-3 hours or until tilapia is opaque throughout.
Drain lime juice from bowl and reserve.
Add corn, tomatoes, and cilantro to bowl.
Add 1/4 cup reserved lime juice to bowl.
Add clam juice, Clamato and salt.
Refrigerate uncovered until ready to serve.
Serve on tostadas.
