Title:
Natasha's Smoked Sausage, Potato and Green Bell Pepper Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages smoked sausage, sliced
6-8 Medium potatoes, cubed
1-2 green bell peppers, diced
4 tablespoons butter or margarine
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine sausage, potatoes, and green bell peppers in a greased 9X11" baking dish.
Spoon butter on top of mixture. Cover with aluminum foil.  Bake for 45-60 minutes until potatoes are tender.
Remove from oven, sprinkle cheddar cheese on top, return to oven for 5 minutes until cheese is melted.  Serve!
