Title:
Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pound cubed beef stew meat
2 cloves garlic chopped
3 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed golden mushroom soup
1 chopped onion
1 1/2 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp beef bullion
1 pkg lipton onion soup
3/4 cup water
4 ounces cream cheese
4 ounces sour cream
8 oz. sliced fresh mushrooms
1 tbl butter
salt/pepper to taste
1 lb. egg noodles

Directions:
In a slow cooker, combine the meat, garlic, soup, onion,onion soup mix, bullion, Worcestershire sauce and water.
Cook on Low setting for 5 hours, or on High setting for about 2 1/2 hours. Saute mushrooms in butter and add to beef mixture along with salt and  pepper to taste and cook an additional 1/2 hour. Stir in cream cheese and sour cream just before serving. Serve over cooked egg noodles.
