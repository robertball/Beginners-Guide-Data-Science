Title:
Peanut Butter Chocolate Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup butter or margarine
1 lb box powdered sugar
1 12oz bag semi sweet chocolate chips
1 12oz bag milk chocolate chips

Directions:
Combine peanut butter and butter in a microwave safe bowl.  Place in the microwave and heat for 15-30 seconds at a time, stirring after each time until mixture is melted.
Pour in box of powdered sugar.
Stir mixture well until there are few or no lumps left.
Roll into balls and place in muffin cups.
Melt both bags of chocolate chips together and mix.
Pour chocolate generously over peanut butter balls.
Place in frige and let sit a couple of hours or until chocolate has hardened.
