Title:
Chocolate Sauerkraut Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pkg Betty Crocker SuperMoist Devils Food Cake Mix
2/3 Cup Water
1/2 Cup Vegetable Oil
3 Eggs
1 Cup Sauerkraut, rinsed drained and finely chopped
1 Can (15 oz) Cherry Pie Filling

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Grease & flour a 13x9x2 pan.
Beat cake mix (dry), water, oil and eggs in large bowl on low speed 30 seconds.
Beat on medium speed 2 minutes. (Batter will be thick and grainy.) Stir in sauerkraut.
Pour into pan. Bake 30 to 40 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
Cool completely.
Serve with pie filling.
