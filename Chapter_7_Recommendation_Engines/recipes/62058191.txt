Title:
No Pasta Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 26oz. jar meatless pasta sauce (your favorite brand/variety)
10 oz. sliced italian beef from the deli
2 cups shredded mozerella
1 15oz container part skim ricotta
1 cup grated parmesan
1 Tbls Italian Herb Blend
2 Eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F
In a small bowl mix together ricotta, eggs, herbs, and 2/3 cup parmesan.
In a 9 x 13 pan, begin layering as follows:
3/4 cup pasta sauce
5 oz. beef
1/2 ricotta mixture
1 cup pasta sauce
1 cup mozerella
5 oz. beef
1/2 ricotta mixture
remaining pasta sauce
remaining mozerella
remaining parmesan
Bake at 350F for approximately 30 minutes until heated through and top is melted and golden.

Submitter: 
LARANEFF
Description: 
This simple to assemble recipe layers a lasagna with italian beef, rather than pasta, to cut the carbs, but has enough texture and flavor, that you won't miss it.
