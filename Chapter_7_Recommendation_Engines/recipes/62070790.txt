Title:
Butterfinger Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz. cream cheese, softened
1 8oz. whipped topping
1/3 cup powdered sugar
5 Butterfinger candy bars, crushed
1 graham cracker pie crust

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, whipped topping, powdered sugar, and half of the candy bars.
Pour into pie crust.
Sprinkle with remaining candy bars.
Refrigerate at least 2 hours.
