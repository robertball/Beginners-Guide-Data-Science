Title:
Cape Cod and White Zinfandel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1008067.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vodka
2 fluid ounces cranberry juice
2 fluid ounces white Zinfandel
1 wedge lime

Directions:
In a highball glass with ice, pour vodka, followed by cranberry juice and white Zinfandel. Squeeze the lime wedge over and drop in for garnish. Stir.

Description: 
The traditional cranberry and vodka cocktail with a twist.
