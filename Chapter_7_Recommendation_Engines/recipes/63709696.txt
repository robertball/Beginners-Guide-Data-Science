Title:
Broccoli Bacon Cheese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 head fresh broccoli, chopped
3/4 cup sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
2 hard boiled eggs, chopped
1/4 sweet white onion, finely minced
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1 tablespoon white sugar
1/2 teaspoons ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1/2 teaspoon fresh lemon juice

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk together the vinegar, sugar, salt, pepper, mayonnaise and lemon juice.  Stir in the broccoli, cheese, bacon egg and onion.  Cover, and chill for one or two hours.
