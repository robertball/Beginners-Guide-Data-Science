Title:
Clam Chowder Tart with potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (9 inch) pie shell, partially baked
48 small soft-shell clams
4 eggs
1 cup fat free half and half
1/4 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon white pepper
4 slices turkey bacon
2 tablespoons minced onion
1 diced celery stalk
1 diced russet potato

Directions:
Place oven rack on highest level. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Chop the clams finely, and reserve 1/2 cup of the liquid.  Beat the eggs well, and add  clam liquid, cream, nutmeg, salt, and pepper.  Beat until smooth, and stir in the clams.
Fry the bacon over moderate heat until crisp, and then remove from pan. Leave a thin layer of fat in the pan, and fry the onions, celery and potatoes until potatoes are soft. Scrape vegetables into the clam mixture. Chop the bacon and add into mixture, then pour into mixture into pastry shell.
Bake on the top rack of preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
AZ
Description: 
Crumbly bacon and sauteed onions are stirred into a cooked custard of clams, eggs, cream and nutmeg. This concoction is then poured into a pastry shell and baked. Serve hot or at room temperature.Modified from: Clam Chowder Tart
