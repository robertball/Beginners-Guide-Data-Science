Title:
Lil's Fresh Coconut Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Fresh Coconuts: Cracked and Peeled
3 Egg Whites
6 Egg Yolks
1 1/2 Cups Unsalted Butter
2 1/2 Cups Sugar
1 Teaspoon Vanilla
2 Cups Milk
4 Cups Cake Flour
1/2 Teaspoon Salt
4 Teaspoons Baking Powder
-----
Filling:
2 Cups of Lemon Curd
-----
Frosting:
3 Cups Sugar
1 1/2 Cups Water
3 Egg Whites
1/8 Teaspoon Salt
1 Teaspoon Vanilla

Directions:
Grease 4 8" cake pans.  Cover the bottom of all four with waxed or parchment paper.
Shred coconut meat with the using a fine grater or food processing disk.
Whip three of the egg whites until foamy.  Add 2 Tablespoons of sugar and whip until stiff and glossy.  Put aside.
Cream together butter and sugar.  Add vanilla and egg yolks.
Sift togther flour, salt, and baking powder.
Add flour mixture and milk alternatly to butter and sugar - begining and ending with the flour mixture.
Fold 2 cups of the fresh coconut to the cake batter.  Gently fold in egg whites.
Divide the batter equally between the four pans.
Bake for 20 minutes, or until middle is set.  The cake should not pull from the sides of the pan until it has been removed from the oven!
Cool in pan 5 minutes, then remove cakes to rack and cool completely.
Fill with Lemon Curd.
FROSTING:
Bring sugar and water to a boil.
While sugar and water are cooking, beging to beat egg whites and salt until soft peaks begin to form.
When syurp reaches 238 - 240, of begins to spin a fine thread, remove from heat.
With the mixer running, add the syurp to the egg whites in a thin stream.  When the two mixtures are combined, stir in vanilla.
Frost Cake immediatly.  Sprinkly remaining coconut liberaly over sides and top of cake.
