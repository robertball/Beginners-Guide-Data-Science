Title:
black bean burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 oz) can black beans, drained and rinsed
1/2 bell pepper
1/2 Vidalia onion
3 cloves garlic
1 egg
1-2 tbl chili powder
1-2 tbl cumin
1/2 C whole wheat panko or quinoa

Directions:
pulse black beans in food processor until thick & pasty and put into bowl
finely chop bell pepper, onion, & garlic in food processor
cook in frying pand and add to bowl w black beans
in sm bowl, stir together egg, chili powder, cumin
min in panko until mixture is sticky & holds together
divide mixture into 4 patties
bake in oven at 350 - 375 on baking sheet on parchment paper for about 10 min each side
add cheese, roll, lettuce & tomatoe, avocado...
