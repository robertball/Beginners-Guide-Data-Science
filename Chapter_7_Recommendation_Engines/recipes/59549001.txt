Title:
Quick and Easy Seven Layer Taco Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/268766.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) can spicy refried beans
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
1 (16 ounce) jar salsa
1 large tomato, chopped
1 8 ounce can green chiles or jalapenos
1 bag shredded iceberg lettuce
1 (6 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained
2 cups packaged pre-shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Spread the spicy refried beans onto a large serving platter.
Spread softened cream cheese on top of beans then cover with layer of sour cream.
Add layer of salsa; top with tomato, green chilies, and shredded lettuce. Top with Cheddar cheese. Garnish with black olives (optional).
