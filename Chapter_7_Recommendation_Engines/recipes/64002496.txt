Title:
Coconutty Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup almond flour
1/4 cashew butter/peanut butter/almond butter
1/4 cup mashed banana
3 Tbsp. grated coconut
extra grated coconut or crushed nuts for rolling

Directions:
Mix almond flour, nut butter, mashed banana, and grated coconut in small mixing bowl.
Roll into bite-sized balls.
Roll in more grated coconut or crushed nuts.
Chill if desired.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
This easy little snack is sugar free (if you use unsweetened coconut) and gluten free.  It is fun for kids to make and requires no baking.  (For a tasty but less healthy variation, add chocolate chips.)
