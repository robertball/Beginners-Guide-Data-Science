Title:
Two Sauce Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 Uncooked Lasagna Noodles
1/2 Container (15oz) Ricotta Cheese
1 Package (5oz) Baby Spinach
1 Cup Italian Blend Cheese
1/4 Cup Parmesan Cheese, Separated
1 Egg
1/2 lb Hot Italian Sausage
3 Cups Tomato Sauce
2 Cups Tomato Cream Sauce or Alfredo Sauce

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 Degrees F (175 C). In an electric mixer, mix together ricotta cheese, spinach, italian cheese blend, 2 Tbsp of parm and eggs. Cover and set aside.
2.In a large skillet over medium high heat cook hot italian sausage until browned and small crumbles form, 4-6 minutes.
3. Spread 1 Cup Tomato sauce evenly over bottom of 8 x 8 pan. Layer with 3 lasagna noodles, half of ricotta cheese mixture, 1 cup of tomato sauce and 1/2 of cooked sausage. Repeat layers.
4. Top with remaining 3 lasagna noodles. Spread alfredo sauce evenly on top of noodles and sprinkle with remaining 2 tbsp parm.
5. Cover tightly with aluminum foil and bake 40 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
6. Remove from oven and allow to rest for 5 to 10 minutes before serving.
