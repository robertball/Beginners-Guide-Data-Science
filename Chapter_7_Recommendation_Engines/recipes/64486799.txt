Title:
Corn Ratatouille Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 onion, chopped
1 large zucchini, sliced and quartered
1 small eggplant, sliced and quartered
1/2 red pepper, chopped
1/2 yellow pepper, chopped
2 large tomatoes, chopped
6 cobs sweet corn, husked
1 cup white mushrooms, sliced
2 tsp dried basil
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp dried thyme leaves
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp dried parsley

Directions:
In a large, non-stick skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Add garlic and onion; cook, stirring occasionally until softened, about 5 minutes. Add zucchini, eggplant, peppers and tomatoes. Cover and cook over medium- low heat, stirring occasionally, until thickened about 15 minutes.
Meanwhile, with a shape knife, cut kernels from corn, set aside.
Add mushrooms, basil, salt, thyme, pepper and corn kernels to skillet. Cover and cook until corn is just tender, about 5 minutes. Garnish with basil leaves.

Submitter: 
Adam Venn
Description: 
This is a fantastic way to use several of the healthy vegetables growing in your garden, or purchased from your local farmer's market!
