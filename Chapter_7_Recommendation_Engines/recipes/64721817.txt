Title:
Baked Chop Suey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb hamburger
1 onion, chopped
1.5 cups chopped celery
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can mushrooms
1/2 cup raw rice
2 cans water
3 Tbsp soy sauce
Salt
Pepper
crunchy Chow mein noodles

Directions:
Brown the hamburger and onion together.
Mix everything together except for the chow mein noodles & put in 9x13 casserole dish.
Bake at 350 for 1 1/2 hours. Top with chow mein noodles and bake for 10 more minutes.
