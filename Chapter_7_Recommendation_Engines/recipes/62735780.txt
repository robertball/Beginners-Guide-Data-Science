Title:
Best Baked Chicken Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c. honey
1/4 c. brown sugar
3 tbsp. water
3 tbsp. balsamic vinegar
1/4 c. Dijon mustard
1 tsp. minced garlic
1 tbsp. olive oil
4 lbs. chicken legs or breast or thigh or combo
1 tsp. kosher salt
1/2 tsp. ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425. Spray baking sheet with cooking spray. Whisk honey, sugar, water vinegar, mustard, garlic, and oil. Add chicken and toss to coat. Place on prepared pan and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Bake for 30-35 min. or until juice runs clear. You can turn over at this point and cook longer if needed.

Submitter: 
Deborah Norris

A tasty chicken piece that smells and tastes as good as it looks.
