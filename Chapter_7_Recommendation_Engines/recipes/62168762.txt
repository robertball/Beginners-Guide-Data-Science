Title:
Spicy sausage and peppers over penne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz package of penne
1 package smoked sausage or kielbasa, sliced thin
1 clove garlic, minced
1 onion, cut into strips
1 green pepper, cut into strips
1 red pepper, cut into strips
2 tsp olive oil, divided
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 tsp dried basil
1/4 tsp dried parsley
Salt, pepper and garlic powder to taste

Directions:
Cook penne pasta as directed. Set aside.
Heat 1 tsp olive oil over medium high heat. Sautee garlic for 1 minute.
Add onions, green and red peppers and saute for 4-5 minutes.
Add sliced sausage and saute for 10 minutes or until lightly browned.
Add red pepper flakes, basil and parsley. Cover and simmer for 5 minutes.
Mix sausage mixture with penne pasta.
Add remaning olive oil and Parmesan cheese.
Season with salt, pepper and garlic powder.
Toss and serve.
