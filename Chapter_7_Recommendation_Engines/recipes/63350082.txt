Title:
Herb Roasted Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon rubbed sage
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 (5 pound) boneless pork loin

Directions:
In a bowl, combine sage, salt, pepper, and garlic. Rub thoroughly all over pork. Place pork in crock pot for the day
