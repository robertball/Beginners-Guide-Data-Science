Title:
Lemon Supreme Cake with Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box lemon cake mix
1 cup apricot nectar
3/4 cup oil
4 eggs
1/3 cup sugar
GLAZE:
1 cup powdered sugar
1 lemon, juiced

Directions:
Combine cake mix, sugar , oil and nectar.  Add eggs one at a time.  Bake in tube pan at 325 degrees for 50 minutes to 1 hour.
For glaze:  Mix powdered sugar and juice of one lemon.  Pour over hot cake.

Submitter: 
Karen Monson
Description: 
I love this so much that for a number of years I took this to people everytime I took someone dinner.
