Title:
Melissa's Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 avocados, cut up
1 med. onion, finely chopped (1/2 cup)
1 or 2 green chili peppers, finely chopped
1 Tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground pepper
1/2 tsp ascorbic acid mixture
1 medium tomato, finely chopped (3/4 cup)

Directions:
Beat avocados, onion, peppers, lemon juice, salt, pepper, and ascorbic acid until creamy.
Stir in tomatoe.
Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour.
Serve with corn chips.
