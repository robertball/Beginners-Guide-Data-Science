Title:
Classic American Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992363.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Morningstar Farms® Grillers® Prime Veggie Burgers
2 (1 ounce) slices reduced-fat Cheddar cheese, mozzarella, or Swiss cheese
2 leaves lettuce
2 hamburger buns, split and toasted
6 slices dill pickle
2 slices tomato
1 thin slice red onion, separated into rings
Ketchup and mustard

Directions:
Cook Morningstar(R) Farms Grillers Prime(R) Veggie Burgers according to package directions. Immediately top each burger with one slice of cheese, if desired. Let stand for 1 minute to allow cheese to melt.
Place one lettuce leaf on each bun bottom. Top with burgers, pickles, tomato slices, onion rings and bun tops. Serve with ketchup and mustard, if desired.
ON THE GRILL: Preheat grill. Use a food thermometer to be sure patties reach minimum internal temperature of 160 degrees F.

Topped with all the fixings of the quintessential burger.
