Title:
Basic Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds Russet potatoes, cut into one inch pieces
1/4 cupbutter, melted
1 cup milk or half and half, warmed
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place cut poatoes in large pot snd cover by about 2 inches with cold water.  Bring to a boil and throw in a large amount of salt (water should be as salty as the ocean).  Continue boiling until poatoes are fork tender.  Drain and return to cooking pot.
In a small saucepan heat milk over low heat until steaming.  Using a potato masher, smash potatoes with melted butter.  Pour in milk a little at a time until desired consistency and texture is reached.   Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Melissa Gardner
Description: 
Potatoes are blended with warmed butter and milk to create those perfect, smooth mashed potatoes everyone loves.Modified from: Basic Mashed Potatoes
