Title:
Pretty Stuffed spring peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1pkg (8oz) cream cheese
2tsp minced chives
1tsp dried basil
1 tsp minced garlic
1/2tsp  caraway seeds
1/2tsp dill weed
1/4tsp lemon pepper seasoning
36 fresh snow peas(trimmed)

Directions:
In large bowl combine the first 7 ing. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
Let filling stand at room temp for 30 minutes. Meanwhile,in a large sauce pan, bring 6C water to a boil. Add snow peas, cover and boil for 1-2minutes.
Drain and immediately place peas in ice water. Drain and pat dry. Gently split peas open; pipe about 1tsp of the filling into each pod.
