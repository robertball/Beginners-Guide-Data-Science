Title:
Kale Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1914374.jpg
Ingredients:
16 fluid ounces coconut water, chilled
1 banana
1/2 avocado, peeled and pitted
1/2 cup packed kale
1/8 lemon, juiced
1 pinch cayenne pepper

Directions:
Put coconut water, banana, avocado, kale, lemon juice, and cayenne pepper in blender; blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
