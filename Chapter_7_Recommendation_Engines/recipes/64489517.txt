Title:
Loaded Baked Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2600955.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lbs Russet potatoes baked till soft and cubed
2 Onions diced
1 LB bacon diced (reserve 4 strips to bake and crumble for garnish)
4 C Chicken broth
4 C water
8 oz block cream cheese
8 oz melting cheese such as velteeta
Salt and pepper as desired
Shredded cheese for garnish
Sour cream for garnish
chopped chives for garnish if desired

Directions:
In a large stock pot saute diced bacon till half the fat is rendered.
Add Onions to the bacon and cook till onions are soft and bacon begins to crisp.
Add Chicken broth and Water bring to a simmer.
In a separate pot  melt cream cheese and melting cheese together stir till smooth.
Add cheese mixture to the soup.
Salt and Pepper to taste.
Add potatoes
Simmer till potatoes warm through
Serve with shredded cheese, crumbled bacon, sour cream and chives if desired

Submitter: 
Michelle Jenkins
Description: 
It's all the yumminess of a loaded baked potato in a soup
