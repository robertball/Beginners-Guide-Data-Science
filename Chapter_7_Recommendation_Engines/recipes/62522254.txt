Title:
Pita pockets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
TAHINI SAUCE:
1 c. plain yogurt
2 T. sesame tahini
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp. lemon juice
salt to taste
PITA POCKETS:
4 large whole wheat pita breads
8 oz. soft tofu, well-drained and cubed
4 ripe plum tomatoes, diced
1 c. spinach leaves, torn into bite-sized pieces
1 c. sprouts
1 large ripe avocado (peeled) cut into thin wedges

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix all sauce ingredients.
Slice an edge from each pita and open pockets.
In a medium bowl, combine tofu, tomatoes, and spinach.  Add 1/4 c. sauce and toss gently to mix.
spoon the mixture into each pocket and divide sprouts and avocado among the sandwiches.  Drizzle each with 1 T. sauce.
