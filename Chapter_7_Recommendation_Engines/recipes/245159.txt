Title:
Delicious, Gooey,  Whole Wheat Protein Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
cooking spray
1 apple, cut into 1/4-inch pieces
1 banana, mashed
1 cup non-fat Greek yogurt
3/4 cup almond butter
2 eggs
2 cups whole wheat flour
1 cup dark chocolate chips
3/4 cup whey protein powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray an 8-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
Mix apple, banana, yogurt, almond butter, and eggs together in a bowl. Mix flour, chocolate chips, and protein powder together in a large bowl. Stir apple mixture into flour mixture until well combined; pour into the prepared baking dish.
Bake in the preheated oven until cooked through, 15 to 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Noah Greenwald
Description: 
This is a recipe I came up with by picking and choosing my favorite ingredients from other protein bars I've tried. Healthy, delicious, and easy to make!
