Title:
Italian Paleo Chicken Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/459164.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Carrots
1 Celery Stalk
1/4 Large Onion
2 Cloves Garlic
1 Tbsp. Italian Seasoning
1 Tsp. Black Pepper
4 Eggs
1 8oz. Can Sodium Free Tomato Sauce
7-8 Chicken Tenderloins

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350.
Mince veggies and spices together in a food processor. Set aside in a mixing bowl. Process uncooked chicken tenderloins in the food processor until it looks ground. Set aside. Crack 4 eggs into the mixing bowl with veggies and spices and mix with a fork. Add half of the tomato sauce to the mixture. Place chicken into mixing bowl with everything else and mix with fork. Pour into a greased loaf pan and into preheated oven.
Bake for 1 hour & 30 Minutes. Remove from oven, spread remaining tomato sauce on top and continue to bake for 30 more minutes.
Remove from oven and let cool for 20 minutes before carefully slicing and serving.

Submitter: 
CookingThePaleoWay
Description: 
This is a low fat Paleo friendly recipe for Chicken Meatloaf I came up with while trying to create something simple, Paleo, but most of all healthy. Enjoy!
