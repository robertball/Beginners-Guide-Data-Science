Title:
Devilish Devils Food Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CAKE
1 1/2 c. All-Purpose Flour
1 1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
3/4 tsp. Salt
1/2 c. Cocoa
1/2 c. Margarine
1 1/3 c. Sugar
3 Eggs
1 tsp. Vanilla
ICING
1/4 c. Margarine
3 c. Icing Sugar
Dash of Salt
1 tbsp. Vanilla Extract
3-5 tbsp. Milk
3 tbsp. Cocoa

Directions:
CAKE
Preheat oven to 350F (180C). Grease 2 8-9 inch round cake pans.
Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and cocoa.
In a separate bowl, cream margarine with an electric mixer. add in sugar and blend well. Blend in eggs and vanilla. add dry ingredients alternately with milk (3 dry 2 wet).
Pour equal amounts into each cake pan. Bake 30-35 minutes or until a cake tester inserted in the center comes out clean. let cool 5 minutes in the pans then remove to cool completely on wire racks.
ICING
Cream margarine with electric mixer. add icing sugar, salt, vanilla and enough milk to make it come together. blend until smooth. mix in cocoa.
TO ASSEMBLE
Spread a third of the icing on top of the first layer. Place second layer on top of the first layer and ice with remaining icing. Decorate if desired.
