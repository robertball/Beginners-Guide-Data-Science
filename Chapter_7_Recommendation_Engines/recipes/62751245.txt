Title:
Lemon Blueberry Ginger Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups fresh or thawed frozen blueberries
1 (32 ounce) container plain Greek yogurt
Juice of one lemon
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
4 packets Stevia sweetener
1 8 oz container low fat Cool Whip
20 gingersnaps, crumbled
Lemon slices and a few blueberries for garnish.

Directions:
In a large bowl gently mix Greek yogurt with the juice of one lemon, the vanilla and the Stevia packets.  Gently fold in the Cool Whip.
In Trifle Bowl layer first 1/3 of the yogurt/Cool whip mixture, then 1/3 of the crumbled ginger snaps, then a layer of 1/2 of the fresh blueberries.  Repeat layering order again, and then end with the last 1/3 of the yogurt/Cool Whip mixture and sprinkle with remaining ginger snaps and a few blueberries on top.  Garnish with lemon slices.  Chill for an hour or two and serve cold.
