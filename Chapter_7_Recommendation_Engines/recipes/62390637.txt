Title:
Simple Ham, Potatoes, and Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 14.5 oz cans green beans, kitchen cut
2 14.5 oz cans sliced potatoes
1 16 oz package cooked ham, diced
1 medium onion, diced
1 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon crushed red pepper

Directions:
Pour cans of potatoes in a large pot with the liquid. Drain one can of green beans and add to pot. Add second half of green beans with the liquid. Add the ham, diced onions, pepper, and crushed red pepper. bring to a boil and then cooked on medium heat for 15 minutes. May be cooked longer at a lower heat or in the crock pot for several hours.

Submitter: 
Jessica Weber
Description: 
This is a quick and easy spin on the traditional Pennsylvania Dutch recipe. You can substitute the canned ingredients for fresh ingredients, if you are so inclined. This version is meant to be quick, easy, and inexpensive.
