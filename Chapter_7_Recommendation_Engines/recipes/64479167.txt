Title:
Black Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup black rice
3 cups water
1 cup raisins or prunes
1 cup coconut cream
1/2 cup pistachios, shelled
1 cup strawberries, halved

Directions:
Rinse rice well in cold water, then place in heavy saucepan and cover with the water.
Add raisins or prunes.
Bring to boil and simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally until water is absorbed, rice is tender, and fruit has softened.
Spoon into bowls and top with coconut cream, pistachios, and strawberries.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
Black rice is chock-full of antioxidants, and, coupled with the sweetness of dried fruit, makes for a rich, filling pudding.
