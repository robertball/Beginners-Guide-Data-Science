Title:
Swedish Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1 cup sour cream
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
dash tobasco sauce, or any other kind of hot sauce, according to taste
1/4 cup milk
2 pounds meatballs

Directions:
Place meatballs in slow cooker.
Combine remaining ingredients, pour over meatballs, stirring to coat.
Cook on high 30-45 minutes and gently stir.
Reduce heat to low. Stir occasionally to prevent burning outer layer.

Submitter: 
Beth Mower Dodge
Description: 
I have always gotten a great response when serving these. They are not quite Swedish meatballs, but definitely work.
