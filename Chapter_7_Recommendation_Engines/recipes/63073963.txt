Title:
Crawfish Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/2 bunch green onions, chopped
2 pounds frozen crawfish or shrimp, cleaned
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of potato soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of shrimp soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 bag frozen corn, roasted
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
2 cups half-and-half cream
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
Melt 1/4 cup of butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute crawfish or shrimp and green onions in butter until fish is cooked, about 5 minutes. In a large pot over medium heat, combine potato soup, shrimp soup, mushroom soup, corn, and cream cheese. Mix well, and bring to a slow boil. Stir in half-and-half, sauteed green onions, and crawfish or shrimp. Season with cayenne pepper. Bring to a low boil, and simmer 5 minutes to blend flavors.

Submitter: 
Lish
Description: 
A creamy chowder consisting of green onions, corn, crawfish, cream of potato and mushroom soups, and a bit of cayenne pepper for a little kick. Serve with cornbread, or some crusty French bread.Modified from: Crawfish Chowder
