Title:
Onion Roasted Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (1 ounce) packages dry onion soup mix or sweet & sour mix
2 pounds sweet potatoes or red potatoes, peeled and diced
1/3 cup evoo

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (230 degrees C).
In a large bowl, toss the dry onion soup mix, sweet potatoes and vegetable oil until the sweet potatoes are well coated.
Arrange the mixture on a large baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven 40 minutes, or until the sweet potatoes are tender.
