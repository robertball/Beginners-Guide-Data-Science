Title:
Reeses Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup margarine
1 1/2 cups graham wafer crumbs
2 cups icing sugar
Topping:
1 cup chocolate chips
1/2 cup margarine

Directions:
Melt peanut butter and first margarine.
Add sugar and wafer crumbs.
Press into a 9" x 13" pan.
Melt topping ingredients together.
Spread over top of layer in pan.
Cool.
