Title:
French Onion Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 onions, thinly sliced
1/4 C. butter
2 Tbsp. flour
2 10.5-oz. cans beef broth
2 1/2 C. water
1/2 C. Parmesean cheese, grated
1/2 C. Swiss cheese, grated
Sour cream
Sliced French bread

Directions:
1. In a pan, saute onions in butter over medium heat until the onions are carmelized, approximately 20 minutes. 2. Add the floor and mix in well. Gradually add broth and water. Boil for approximately 25-30 minutes. 3. While the soup is boiling, toast the French bread slies. 4. Place a piece of toasted bread in each bowl. Top the bread with cheese and a dollop of sour cream, pour soup over bread, and enjoy!
