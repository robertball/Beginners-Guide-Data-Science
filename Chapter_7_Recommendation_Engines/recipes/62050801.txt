Title:
Creamy curry tomato soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can condensed tomato soup, prepared with 1% milk rather than water
1 cup roasted sweet and yukon gold potatoes (see recipe)
1 handful dried sweetened cranberries
3-6 shakes cayenne pepper
1 tsp curry powder (to taste)
1-3 shakes nutmeg

Directions:
Prepare soup according to directions, adding the rest of the ingredients until the flavors marry.  Simmer and enjoy!  Adjust spice to taste.

Description: 
An easy, delicious way to jazz up tomato soup using ingredients you already have on hand!
