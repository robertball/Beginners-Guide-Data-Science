Title:
Spicy Black Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30 oz. black beans, rinsed and drained
15 oz. whole kernel yellow corn, drained
1 red onion, diced
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons lemon or lime juice
1 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
1/2 cup olive oil
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
red salsa, about 3/4 cup, add to your liking

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl.  Cover and refrigerate for up to 2 days.  Serve chilled.

Kelly Michael Hammer
