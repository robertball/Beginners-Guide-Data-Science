Title:
Sue's Popovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 cup milk
2 Tbsp honey
1 Tbsp melted butter
2 large eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425. Grease a 12 cup muffin tin or popover pan.
Mix together flour and salt. Mix in milk, honey and butter. Beat in eggs until well blended.
Pour into prepared muffin tins. Do not fill muffin cups more than half full or they will not rise properly.
Bake in 425 oven for 25 to 30 minutes. DO NOT open the oven prior to the 20 minute mark or they will fall. Remove from oven and enjoy hot.
