Title:
Gnooki (Knee-oh-key) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium potatoes
2 egg yolks
1/2 cup flour
2 tbs. Parmesan cheese
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1 tsp parsley

Directions:
Heat boiling water in pot.
Cut peeled potatoes into small cubes and add to boiling water.
Boil potatoes until slightly soft, strain and press softened potatoes through strainer (potato ricer works best) into bowl.
Bring another pot of water to a boil.
Mix in remaining ingredients w/potatoes.
Pull of small pieces of potato mixture (not nec. to ball up).
Drop into boiling water and cook until they float.
Strain and serve.

Italian Potato dumplings. Excellent in alfredo recipes as an alternative to meats.
