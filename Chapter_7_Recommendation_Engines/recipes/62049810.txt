Title:
Baked Chicken Chimichangas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups cooked chicken
2 cups Pace Picante sauce
1 1/2 cups chopped green onions (add as much or as little as you like)
1/2 tsp. oregano
1/4 tsp. basil
1/4 cup melted butter or margarine
1 cup Cheddar cheese, shredded
8-large flour tortillas
Guacamole
Sour cream

Directions:
Combine the first 5 ingredients & simmer 10 min's. Brush shells with melted butter & put some of the chicken mixture on each shell. Top with the cheese. Fold the shells & place in a large greesed baking dish with the seam side down. Bake at 475 for 15 min's. Serve with guacamole & sour cream.

This is quick & easy to make yet very satisfying to eat. Add a salad and your dinner is ready!
