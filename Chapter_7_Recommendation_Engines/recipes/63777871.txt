Title:
Chicken, Bacon, Barbecue, Ranch Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1046989.jpg
Ingredients:
8	cups chopped romaine lettuce, or shredded cabbage or slaw mix
2	cups diced cooked chicken
8	tablespoons cooked chopped bacon or real bacon bits
8	tablespoons ranch dressing
6	tablespoons barbecue sauce (I like Sweet Baby Ray’s honey barbecue sauce)
Garnish with  fried tortilla strips.

Directions:
Divide lettuce, or cabbage, chicken and among 4 bowls.  Top each with 2 tablespoons ranch dressing, and 1 ½ tablespoons barbecue sauce, sprinkle each with 1 to 2 tablespoons bacon bits or to taste.  Garnish with tortilla strips.
