Title:
Shrimp in Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 lb. Cheddar cheese
2 tbsp. butter
1/2 pt. sour cream
1 (4 oz.) can mushrooms
1 (10 oz.) pkg. shrimp, cooked
3/4 tsp. salt
Dry white wine

Directions:
Melt cheese in butter.
Stir in sour cream and a bit of juice from the mushrooms.
Add salt.
Add wine to taste.
Heat, but do not boil.
Add shrimp.
Heat thoroughly.
Serve with rice or noodles.
