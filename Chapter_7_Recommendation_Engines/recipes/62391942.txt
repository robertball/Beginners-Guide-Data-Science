Title:
Mozza Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. extra lean ground chicken
1 egg
1/4 cup of panko flakes
1 jalapeno seeded and chopped
2 cloves of garlic chopped
1 1/2 tsps. of Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese (can be generous and add a bit more)
15 grams of powdered Ranch salad dressing mix - about half the pouch

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a bowl and mix very well.  Divide into four balls and flatten into desired size of patty

Submitter: 
lkane47
Description: 
While trying to stay away from beef I came up with this delicious chicken burger.  It's great on thinfuls which are new hamburger buns, less bun and better for you.
