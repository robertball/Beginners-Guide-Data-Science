Title:
Golden Potato Soup with bacon sauteed onion, celery and garlci Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups peeled and cubed potatoes
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 cube chicken bouillon
1 cup water
1 tbs fresh parsley
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 pinch ground black pepper
2 teaspoons all-purpose flour
1 1/2 cups milk
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
8 slices bacon

Directions:
In a skillet cook bacon and reserve about 2 tbsp of drippings. Saute onions and celery, add garlic shortly before onions and celery become transluscent. In a large stock pot add potatoes,  chicken bouillon, water and parsley flakes.  Season with salt and pepper and simmer about 10 minutes and add sauteed vegetables. After potatoes soften and in a separate bowl mix flour and milk.  Once it is well blended, add to soup mixture and cook until soup becomes thick.
Stir in cheese and bacon and simmer until cheese is melted.
