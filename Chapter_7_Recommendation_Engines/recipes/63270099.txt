Title:
Turkey and Spinach Meat Loaf and Braised Greens with Red Onion and Sun Dried Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meatloaf
1/2 C pecans
1 lb lean ground organic turkey
10 oz frozen spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
2 organic eggs
1 tbs extra virgin olive oil
1/2 small onion, diced
1 tsp dried basil
1/4 tsp sea salt
pinch of black pepper
-------------------------------------------------
Braised Greens w/Red Onion and Sun Dried Tomatoes
1/2 red onion, sliced
2 tbs sun dried tomatoes, sliced
4-6 C greens(kale,chard,collard)thinly sliced
1/2 C water
1 tbs extra virgin olive oil
splash of balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Meatloaf
Preheat oven to 375*.  In food processor, grind the raw pecans to a medium-fine texture.
In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground pecans with the remaining ingredients. Mix well. Place into an oiled loaf pan and bake for 30 minutes.
Remove from the oven and allow to cool for 5 minutes before serving.
Serve with Braised Greens recipe that follows.
Place the onion, tomatoes,and greens in a saute pan with the water.
Bring to a boil, reduce to a low simmer until the greens are soft.
Splash with olive oil and balsamic vinegar before serving.
