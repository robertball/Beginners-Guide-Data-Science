Title:
Chicken Casablanca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion, sliced
1 tsp fresh ginger, grated or 1/2 tsp ginger paste.
2 cloves garlic, minced
3 large carrots, diced
2 large potatoes, peeled and diced
1 medium zucchini, sliced 1 in. thick
1 15 oz. can garbanzo beans, drained
3 lbs. boneless skinless chicken cut into chunks
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp turmeric
1/2 tsp salt and pepper
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1 15 oz. can diced tomato
2 tbl chopped parsley
1 tbl chopped cilantro

Directions:
1.  Combine first 8 ingredients in crockpot.
2.  Combine all the spices in a small bowl and mix together.  Sprinkle spice mixture over ingredients in crockpot and mix.
3.  Add chopped tomatoes.  Cover and cook on Low 8-10 hrs. or High 4-5 hrs.  Stir in parsley and cilantro before serving.  Serve over cooked rice.

Submitter: 
IMANKAY
Description: 
A tasty dish with chicken, zuchini, garbanzo beans, potatoes, and carrots with cumin, cayenne, and cilantro tossed in a crock pot to slow cook to perfection.  You will be amazed at how easy and fun this recipe is.
