Title:
2 Minute Delicious Philly Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup cream cheese spread
1 TBL Catalina dressing
2 TBL sliced black olives
12 Triscuit Thin Crisps

Directions:
Mix cream cheese spread, dressing and olives until well blended. Serve with crackers.
