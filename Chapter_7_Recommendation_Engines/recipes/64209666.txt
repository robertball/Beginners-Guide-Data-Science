Title:
Easy Nut Brittle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup granulated sugar
1/2 lb. butter, softened
2 tsp. water
1 cup chopped nuts
2 Hershey's (1.55oz) chocolate bars, crumbled (optional)

Directions:
Spread nuts in 8X10 pan.
In a deep heavy sauce pan, boil water, sugar and butter over high heat. Constantly stir till caramel colored, be careful not to burn.
Pour evenly over nuts.
While hot sprinkle chocolate on top and smooth melting chocolate with spatula till entire top is covered. Let cool completely.
Break into pieces.

Submitter: 
Celeste
Description: 
Quick and easy brittle. This cooking method produces a softer brittle, meaning it won't break your teeth ;)
