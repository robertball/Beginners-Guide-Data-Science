Title:
Venison Summer Sausage II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Lbs Ground Venison
4 Tbs Mortons Tender Quick
2 Tbs Cracked peppercorns
1 Tbs Mustard seed
2 tsp Garlic Powder
2 tsp Liquid Smoke

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together thoroughly for at least 5 min.Keep in refrigerator 24-48 hours to cure.
Shape meat in rolls about 3in in diameter.
place on cookie sheet or broiler pan and bake 175 degrees for 8 hours.
Refrigerate 24 hrs before serving.
