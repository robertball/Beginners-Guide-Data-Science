Title:
Very Easy Microwave Pralines Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/309689.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups packed brown sugar
1 cup heavy whipping cream
1 stick butter / 1/2 cup
2 cups chopped toasted pecans
1 teas. vanilla

Directions:
In a deep, microwave-safe bowl, mix together brown sugar, whipping cream.  Microwave on High for 8 1/2 minutes.
Stir well
Microwave on High for 8 1/2 minutes.
Stir well
Mix in butter and vanilla until well blended. Then stir, stir, and stir until mixture begins to cool and get creamy. Stir in chopped nuts. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto waxed paper to cool. If mixture gets to hard,add a couple drops water.
