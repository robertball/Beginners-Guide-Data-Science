Title:
Gorgonzola Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup heavy or whipping cream
1/2 pound Gorgonzola cheese
2 Tsp. butter
1/4 freshly grated nutmeg
Pinch of salt
1/4 tsp. freshly ground black pepper
1/3 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat cream at low heat in a saucepan to a gentle boil until it thickens slightly.
Crumble the Gorgonzola cheese and add into the cream.
Gently mix together.
Add in butter, nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
Continue mixing gently over extremely low heat.
Gradually stir in Parmesan cheese.
Combine with hot pasta.

Submitter: 
IrvineHousewife
Description: 
This is a quick and easy white sauce that I use with my linguine or fettuccine when my kids don't feel like having Alfredo sauce.
