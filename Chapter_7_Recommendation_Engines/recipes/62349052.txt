Title:
Zippy Barbecue Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup ketchup
2   tablespoon molasses
1   tablespoon wine wine vinegar
1   tablespoon Dijon mustard
1   tablespoon light brown sugar
2   tablespoon Worchestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon   kosher salt
1/4 teaspoon   Tabasco sauce
1/4 teaspoon   granulated garlic
1/4 teaspoon   freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
In a small, heavy saucepan, whisk the ingredients with 1/2 cup water. Bring to boil over medium heat, then reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
