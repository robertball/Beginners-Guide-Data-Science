Title:
Lemon Yogurt Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c flour
3/4 c sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp baking powder
1 egg
1 c lemon yogurt
1/3 c canola oil
1 tbsp lemon juice

Directions:
In large bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, b soda, and b powder.
In another bowl, combine egg, yogurt, oil and lemon juice.
Stir wet into dry ingredients until just moistened.
Pour into 8x4 greased baking dish.
Bake at 325 degrees for 45-50 minutes.
