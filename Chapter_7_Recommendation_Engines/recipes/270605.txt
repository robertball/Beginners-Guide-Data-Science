Title:
Instant Pot® Vegan Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6555371.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups almond milk
2/3 cup jasmine rice, rinsed
1/3 cup granulated sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine almond milk, rice, sugar, and salt in a multi-functional pressure cooker (such as Instant Pot(R)). Close and lock the lid. Select high pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; set timer for 18 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.
Release pressure using the natural-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, about 10 minutes.  Unlock and remove the lid. Stir in vanilla extract. Serve warm or chilled. Rice pudding will thicken as it cools.

Description: 
A simple Instant Pot® recipe for a gluten-free, dairy-free, egg-free rice pudding.
