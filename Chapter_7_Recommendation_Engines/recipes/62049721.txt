Title:
Double Chocolate Toffee Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick (1/2 c) frozen butter
1 1/4 c flour
1/4 t baking soda
1/8 t salt
3 T cocoa powder
1/2 c brown sugar, firmly packed
1/4 c white sugar
1 large egg
1 t vanilla
1/2 c bittersweet chocolate chips
1/2 c toffee bits

Directions:
* Preheat oven to 300.  Butter cookie sheet.
* Place frozen butter in microwave-safe bowl and microwave 60 sec or till mostly melted.  Cool to room temperature.
* In medium mixing bowl combine flour, soda, salt and cocoa.  Mix thoroughly with wire whisk.
* In large bowl, mix sugars on medium speed with electric mixer.  Add butter and mix to form a grainy paste.  Add egg and vanilla and continue mixing thoroughly.
* Add flour mixture to egg mixture in two additions.  Do not overmix.
* Fold in chocolate chips and toffee bits.
* Roll heaping tablespoon into balls and place on prepared cookie sheet 1-2" apart.
* Place cookie sheet with cookies in freezer for 10-15 minutes, until cookie dough appears matte.
* Bake at 300 for 15 minutes.  Cool slightly and then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.

Submitter: 
ABROSEN
Description: 
Soft, gooey and oh-so-sweet.   Best right out of the oven, drenched in milk.
