Title:
Best of Bridge Nest Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2314488.jpg
Ingredients:
2-3 slices Black Forest ham
1 egg
1 Tbsp. cream
1 heaping Tbsp. grated Swiss cheese
Sprinkle dried basil
English Muffin

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F and grease large muffin tins.
Line with ham and break egg over top. Add cream and sprinkle with cheese and basil.
Bake 12-15 minutes and serve on half of a toasted English muffin.
