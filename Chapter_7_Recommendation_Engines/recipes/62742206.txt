Title:
Mango Salsa and Turkey Breast Cutlets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/631034.jpg
Ingredients:
4 turkey breast cutlets
1 cup Wondra flour
1 cup broth
1 egg
1TB olive oil
2 mangoes, chopped
1 cup pineapple, chopped
4 small tomatoes, chopped
1/2 cup sweet pepper, chopped
1/4 cup onion, chopped
1TB cilantro
1TB ginger, chopped
1TB rice vinegar
.5TB olive oil
squirt of lime
dash of mint
dash of pepper

Directions:
Coat turkey with egg and flour. Brown in olive oil on stovetop. Add broth, bring to boil. Reduce heat and cook for 10 minutes. Check for temperature of 170.
Mix remaining ingredients and serve over cutlet.
Serve with brown rice.
