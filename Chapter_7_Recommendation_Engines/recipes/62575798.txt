Title:
Lorinda's Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb roast
bag of baby carrots
5 potatoes
1 pkt lipton french onion soup
4 cups water
1 can cream of mushroom
1 can cream of celery
pepper and salt to taste
essence
1/4 cup of flour
1/4 cup vegetable oil
dash of worstishire sauce

Directions:
coat the roast in flour salt and pepper shake excess and brown in oil.  Brown both sides.  Then add 4 cups of water and lipton soup mix dash of worstishire and essence.  Cook for 2 hours.  Add the carrots and cook for half hour.  Add the potatoes and canned soups and cook an addl 40 minutes or until tender.

Description: 
Yummy pot roast that cooks up in its own gravy with potatoes and carrots.
