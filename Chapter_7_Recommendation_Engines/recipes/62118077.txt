Title:
Easiest Peanut Butter Blossoms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup creamy peanut butter
1 egg
1/2 cup sugar
24 hersheys kisses

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine sugar, peanut butter and egg in a medium size bowl.
Roll teaspoon size amountof dough into small balls and roll in remaining sugar.
Place 1 inch apart on cookie sheet and bake for 8 - 10 minutes.
Unwrap kisses while cookies are baking.Smash them in the middle of each cookie immediatly after the ycome out of the oven to flatten the cookie by about half.
Allow cookies to cool. Kisses will stay formed, but will be soft.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Alesha
Description: 
A super easy and super fast peanut butter blossom recipe. These peanut butter cookies have that almost done consistency that everyone loves! The hersheys kisses make these irresistable to kids and adults! Enjoy!
