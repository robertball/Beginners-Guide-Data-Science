Title:
Artisian  Cola Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/396845.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 Cups Cola - warmed to 110 degrees
2 1/2 teas. Yeast
2 T. Molassas
1 T. Dark Brown Sugar
1 Cup Whole Wheat Flour
1 Cup Rye Flour
1/4 Cup Vegetable Oil
1 T. Kosher salt (or 1 1/2 tsp regular salt)
2 Cups Bread flour
1 egg white beaten with a splash of water for a egg wash
A few sprinkles of Oatmeal.

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl add cola, yeast, molassas and dark brown sugar.  Stir and allow to sit for 10 minutes to proof the yeast.
Add the Whole Wheat flour, the Rye flour, oil and salt.  Mix until combined.
Add 1 cup bread flour and mix.
Add the additional cup of flour a little at a time until the dough is elastic and is still a bit sticky.
Knead for about 8 minutes until dough is smooth and elastic.
Turn into greased bowl and cover.  Let rise until doubled in size.  About 1 hour.
Turn dough onto work mat and divide into 2 equal pieces.  Without over working the dough form into 2 free form rounds.
Place on parchment lined cookie sheet.  Lightly mist dough with cooking spray and cover with plastic wrap.
Pre heat oven to 375
Allow the loaves to rise until doubled in size.
Bake for 25 mins at 375.
Quickly brush the bread with the egg wash and sprinkle with the oatmeal.  Return to the oven and bake another 5 minutes.
Bread should register 180 degrees in the center when done.
Allow bread to cool on a wire rack for 20 minutes before slicing.

Submitter:

Description: 
Beautiful dark bread.  Cola as the secret ingredient is sure to surprise your guests.
