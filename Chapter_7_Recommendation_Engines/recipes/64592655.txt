Title:
Honey Lime Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 540ml jar pineapple chunks
1/2 jar (270 of 540ml) mandarin oranges, drained
1 large banana, sliced
1 kiwi fruit, peeled, halved, and sliced
1 cup fresh or frozen strawberries
1/4 tsp. grated lime peel (optional)
2 tbsp. lime juice
1 tbsp. honey

Directions:
Drain pineapple, reserving 1/4 cup of the juice.
Combine pineapple, oranges, banana, kiwi, and strawberries in large serving bowl.
Stir together reserved pineapple juice, lime peel and juice, and honey in small bowl.
Pour over salad. Toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A quick fruit salad made with fresh, canned and/or frozen fruit tossed with a refreshing dressing. Substitute seasonal or favorite fruits.
