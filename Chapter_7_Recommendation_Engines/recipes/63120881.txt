Title:
WW Lemon-Glazed Chicken with Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. chicken cutlets (about 8 cutlets)
1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
3 TBSP fresh lemon juice, divided
Cooking Spray
1 (8oz) pkg sliced mushrooms
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 cup fat-free, less sodium chicken broth
2 TBSP chopped fresh parsley
1 TBSP drained capers
Lemon wedges (optional)

Directions:
Sprinkle chicken with salt & pepper; drizzle with 2 TBSP lemon juice
Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat
Coat pan with cooking spray.  Add chicken; cook 2 to 3 minutes on each side or until chicken is browned.  Remove chicken from pan, and keep warm.
Add mushrooms and garlic to pan; sauti 3 minutes or until mushrooms are tender.  Add remaining 1 TBSP lemon juice, chicken broth, parsley, and capers.  Return chicken to pan.  Cook uncovered, 2 to 3 minutes or until thoroughly heated.  Garnish with lemon wedges.
