Title:
Josh's sauerkraut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cabbage, thinly shredded
1/2 T. Salt

Directions:
Place cabbage in bowl and knead the salt in for 5 minutes. Put in crock and cover and weigh down.  Wait several days, then enjoy!
