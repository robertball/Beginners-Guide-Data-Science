Title:
Caribbean Collards & Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3c cooked brown rice
1md zucchini, diced
1md red pepper, diced
1sm onion, peeled & diced
16oz can chickpeas
10oz frozen chopped collards
canola oil
smoked salt
black pepper
nutmeg
marjoram

Directions:
Combine the zucchini, red pepper, and onion in a frying pan with just enough oil to coat them.  Place on high heat and cover.  Stir occasionally.
After 10-15 minutes, when they are fully cooked, add all other ingredients, and seasonings to taste.  Stir and serve.
