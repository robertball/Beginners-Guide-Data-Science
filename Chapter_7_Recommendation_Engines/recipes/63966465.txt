Title:
SWEET POTATO MUFFINS TO SATISFY & END SUGAR CRAVINGS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C SWEET POTATOES
4 ORGANIC EGGS
1/2 C UNSWEETENED COCONUT MILK
2 C FILTERED WATER
1/2 T CINNAMON
PINCH SEA SALT

Directions:
1.COMBINE ALL IN BLENDER UNTIL SMOOTH, SCRAPING DOWN SIDES A FEW TIMES
2.YOU MAY NEED TO ADD MORE WATER TO GET A THICK, POURABLE CONSISTENCY. ADD 1/4 C AT A TIME
3.POUR INTO BAKING CUPS. BAKE AT 350 FOR 15-20 MINUTES UNTIL FIRM AND SLIGHTLY BROWNED
