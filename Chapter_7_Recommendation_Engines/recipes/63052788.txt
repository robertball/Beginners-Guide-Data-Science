Title:
Fluffy Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
3 1/2 teaspoons Baking Powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 Tablespoon white Sugar
1 1/2 cups Milk
1 egg
3 tablespoons Butter melted

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients together then add wet ingredients and mix well until batter is fluffy.
