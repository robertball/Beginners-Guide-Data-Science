Title:
Cheesy Cauliflower Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Heads Cauliflower
1 Small to Medium Yellow Onion, Diced
1 1/2 Cups Sour Cream
1 1/2 Cups Cheddar Cheese, Shredded
1 Teaspoon Salt
1/2 Teaspoon Black Pepper
1 Tablespoon Butter or Margarine
1 Cup Bread Crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease one 9x13 inch casserole dish.
Break cauliflower into florets, place in large covered saucepan with diced onions and steam until tender.
Place cauliflower in casserole dish; gently stir in sour cream, cheese, salt and pepper.
In a saute pan over medium heat, melt butter; add bread crumbs and evenly coat.
Sprinkle bread crumbs over cauliflower mixture and bake for 25 to 30 minutes.
