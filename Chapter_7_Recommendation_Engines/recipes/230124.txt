Title:
Thanksgiving Any Day Rollups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3441305.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package reduced-fat cream cheese
1/2 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese
12 meatless turkey-flavored deli slices (such as Tofurky®), chopped
1/3 cup dried cranberries
1/4 cup pecans
1 celery stick, chopped
2 green onions, chopped
1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives
salt and ground black pepper to taste
3 large flour tortillas

Directions:
Combine cream cheese and Cheddar cheese in a food processor; process until blended. Add meatless turkey slices, cranberries, pecans, celery, green onions, chives, salt, and black pepper. Process until smooth.
Spread cream cheese mixture onto each tortilla and roll up. Serve immediately or refrigerate until serving time.

Submitter: 
FROGERINA
Description: 
With delicious holiday ingredients, you can have an easy, healthy, and portable taste of Thanksgiving any day! Sliced, cooked turkey can be substituted for the Tofurky® if desired.
