Title:
Hot Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887179.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup sour cream
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon onion, grated
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
3/4 teaspoon dry mustard
1/4 teaspoon garlic
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
1 pound crabmeat
paprika

Directions:
Combine first 7 ingredients, stir well.
Stir in cheese and crabmeat.
Spoon the mixture into a 1-1/2 qt. casserole dish coated with cooking spray, and sprinkle with paprika.
Bake at 325ยบ for 30 minutes or until heated well. Serve warm.
