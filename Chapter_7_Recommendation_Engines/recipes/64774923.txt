Title:
Egg Drop Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7595377.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbsp dry minced onion
1 tbsp avocado oil (or sesame oil)
1 tsp soy sauce
2 14 oz cans of chicken broth
4 eggs (beaten)
Salt to taste
1/4 tsp garlic powder
dash of red pepper flakes (optional)
Â½ tsp ground pepper (or white pepper)
1/8 tsp fish sauce (optional)
2 tbsp green onions (optional)
1/8 tsp ground ginger (optional - can also use a couple of thin slices of fresh ginger)

Directions:
Add  oil to a medium-sized pot and warm over medium heat. Add onions and saute until browned.
Add the chicken broth,  soy sauce,  salt, spices and fish sauce (if using). Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
Stir in a brisk circular motion while pouring in a thin drizzle of whisked egg into the chicken broth.
Taste and add additional salt if needed.
Season with chives before serving if desired.
