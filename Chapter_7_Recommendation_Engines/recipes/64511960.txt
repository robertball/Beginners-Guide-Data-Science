Title:
Quick Apple Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium Honeycrisp apples, peeled and sliced thinly
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
2-3 tbs lemon juice
1 ready made pie crust
3-4 tbs cream or butter for crust glaze
raw sugar

Directions:
Peel and slice apples thinly
Mix apples with sugars and lemon juice until evenly coated
Line lipped cookie sheet with parchment paper
Roll out pie crust and place apple mixture in center
Fold edges in overlapping pattern, leaving about 4" uncovered in the middle
Brush the exposed crust with cream or melted butter and sprinkle with raw sugar
Bake at 375 for 35-40 minutes
I let it cool for 10 minutes and then cut into 4 servings or more.  Top with your favorite ice cream.
