Title:
Herb Rubbed Sirloin Tip Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 tablespoons paprika
1 tablespoon kosher salt
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
2 cloves, minced garlic
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 Bay leaves
1/2 can Vegetable broth
1 can golden mushroom soup
1 (3 pound) sirloin tip roast

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix the paprika, kosher salt, garlic powder, black pepper, onion powder, cayenne pepper, oregano, thyme and garlic. Stir in the olive oil, and allow the mixture to sit about 15 minutes.
Spread rub on meat and sear in cast iron pan coated with olive oil.
Meanwhile, put bay leaves and mushroom soup in crock pot.
Place the roast in crock pot, and deglaze pan with broth and pour over roast.
Cook on low 6-8 hours or to a minimum internal temperature of 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
Let sit 15 minutes before slicing.
