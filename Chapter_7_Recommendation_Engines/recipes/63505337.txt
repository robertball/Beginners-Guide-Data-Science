Title:
Hot Potatoes au Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/961214.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cup heavy whipping cream
2 garlic cloves, minced
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 jalapeno, seeded and diced
1/2 to 3/4 cup chopped leeks (or chopped onion)
1 1/2 cups shredded Sargento extra sharp Cheddar cheese, divided use
3/4 cup shredded Sargento mozzarella cheese
2 large or 3 medium russet potatoes, peeled, sliced 1/8" thick

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Spray 8x8 casserole dish with nonstick spray.
In saucepan on medium heat, bring cream to simmer.
Add garlic, salt, and pepper to cream and remove from heat; add 1 cup Cheddar cheese and 3/4 cup mozzarella cheese and stir until creamy.
Place jalapeno and leeks in large mixing bowl and stir in cream mixture.
Add potatoes to cream mixture and then spread potatoes and sauce evenly into casserole dish.
Cover with foil and bake about 60-75 minutes.
Remove foil and cook 15-20 minutes more.
Sprinkle with reserved cheese and brown for 5-10 more minutes.  Allow the dish to sit at room temperature for at least 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Marianne
Description: 
These rich potatoes make an excellent side to roasted chicken or some other main dish that is low in fat.
