Title:
Joel's Maple Mesquite Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/666067.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lbs. Hamburg
3 14.5oz cans diced tomatoes
1 6oz can tomato paste
2 14.5oz cans golden sweet corn
1 14.5oz can red kidney beans
1 14.5oz can black beans
1 large green pepper
1 large red onion
2 jalapeno peppers
3 Tbl. spoons minced garlic
1/2 cup maple syrup
1/2 cup lightly packed brown sugar
4 Tbl. spoons chili powder
2 Tsp. worcestershire sauce
1 Tbl. spoon McCormick Mesquite mix
1 Tbl. spoon soy sauce

Directions:
Brown Hamburg and drain.
Dice all vegetables.
drain and rinse corn and beans.
mix all ingredients in crock pot and stir well.
cook on low 8-12 hours, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Joel Lacoss
Description: 
My homemade sweet, mesquite style chili with a just enough spice and sweetness; accompanied by plenty of meat, sweet corn, maple syrup, and other vegetables and spices.  As always with chili, the longer you brew it, the better.
