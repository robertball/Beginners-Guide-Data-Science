Title:
ORANGE SLUSH Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. can of frozen lemonade
12 oz. can of frozen orange juice
4 cans water (use the empty juice cans)
3/4 sugar
2 c. tea
8-12 oz. bourbon

Directions:
Mix and freeze for 6 hours.
Stir and refreeze.  (Let it thaw a little before serving)

Submitter: 
Steph T.
Description: 
A simple, fun twist on the fuzzy navel.  Great to make ahead and to enjoy at a party.
