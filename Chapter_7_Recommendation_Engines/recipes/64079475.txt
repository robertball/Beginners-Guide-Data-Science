Title:
Simple Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1148827.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz. elbow Macaroni
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup flour
1/2 tsp. salt
pepper to taste
2 cups milk
2 cups cheddar cheese (8 oz.)

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to package directions.
In medium saucepan, melt butter over medium heat; stir in flour, salt and pepper; slowly add milk.
Cook and stir until bubbly.
Stir in cheese until melted.
Drain macaroni; add to cheese sauce; stir to coat

A VERY quick and easy fix to a tasty side-dish. Fancy/designer Mac & Cheese often costs forty or fifty dollars to prepare when you have so many exotic and expensive cheeses, but they aren't always the best tasting. This recipe is CHEAP and TASTY.
