Title:
Wheat-Corn Spiced Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1½ c. whole-wheat flour
½ c. toasted wheat germ
¾ c. cornmeal
½ c. packed brown sugar
1 tsp. salt
2 tbsp. baking powder
1/8 tsp. ground allspice
1/8 tsp. ground cloves
½ tsp. vanilla
1½ c. skim milk

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients in bowl with spices.  Add vanilla and milk, stir to mix.  Bake in greased muffin tins for 15 minutes at 400 degrees.  Yields 12 muffins.

Submitter: 
CLOUDSSUNRAIN
Description: 
Easy-to-make, lightly spiced muffins with whole-wheat flour, wheat germ, and cornmeal.  They are very healthy, too!
