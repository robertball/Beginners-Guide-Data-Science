Title:
Red Smothered Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups chopped meat of choice
2 cups rice
1 can corn drained
1 can black beans drained
8 tortillas
1 can cream of chix or mushroom soup
1 can condensed tomato soup
2 cups grated cheese

Directions:
mix soups together in bowl
in separate bowl mix remaining ingredients (except tortillas) and only one cup shredded cheese
spray 9 x 13 pan with pam
skim bottom of pan with sauce
add and mix 1/2 cup sauce to rice and chix mixture
fill each tortilla with rice mixture and roll
place rolled tortillas in pan
pour remaining sauce over rolled tortillas
sprinkle with remaining cup of grated cheese
cook 350 degrees x 45 min
