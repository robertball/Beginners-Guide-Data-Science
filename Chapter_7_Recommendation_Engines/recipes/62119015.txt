Title:
4-Layer Peppermint Brittle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 3/4 tablespoons white chocolate chips
2 1/2 tablespoons semi-sweet chocolate chips
2 peppermints

Directions:
Cover a flat surface with a sheet of wax paper or foil.
Crush 1 peppermint into small chunks.
To make the first layer, melt 1 tablespoon semisweet chocolate chips in microwave oven until creamy.
Smear melted chocolate chips on wax paper until thickness desired is accomplished.
To make the second layer, melt 1 tablespoon white chocolate chips with 1/2 tablespoon semi-sweet chocolate chips until creamy enough to mix together.
Mix in crushed peppermint.
Smear on top of first layer when first layer has hardened.
To make the third layer, melt one tablespoon white chocolate with remaining semi-sweet chocolate until creamy enough to mix together.
Smear on top of second layer when second layer has hardened.
Crush second peppermint into small chunks.
Melt remaining white chocolate.
Stir in second peppermint.
Smear on top of third layer when third layer has hardened.
Wait for all of the small bar to harden.
