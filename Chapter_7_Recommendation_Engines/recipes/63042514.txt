Title:
Spicy Coffeebar Chai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups water
4 black tea bags
1/3 cup honey
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cinnamon stick
5 whole cloves
1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1 pinch ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon peppercorns
2 cups milk

Directions:
In a saucepan, bring water to a boil. Add tea, honey and vanilla. Season with cinnamon, cloves, cardamom, ginger, nutmeg, and peppercorns. Simmer for 5 minutes. Pour in milk, and bring to a boil. Remove from heat, and strain through a fine sieve before serving.  If you want it cold, chill the mixture and serve over ice.
