Title:
Sugar-Top Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/930567.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg
3/4 cup sugar
1 tablespoon melted butter or margarine
1 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoons flour
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
2 tablespoons softened butter

Directions:
Beat egg until frothy; beat in sugar and 1 tablespoon melted butter.  Cream until light and fluffy.  Add sour cream and vanilla; blend well.
Sift together 1 1/2 cups flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt; add to sour cream mixture.  Blend well.  Pour into a greased 8" square pan.
Mix together until crumbly, brown sugar, 2 tablespoons flour, cinnamon and 2 tablespoons butter.  Sprinkle over top of batter.
Bake in moderate oven; 375 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes, or until cake tests done.
