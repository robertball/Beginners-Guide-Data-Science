Title:
Authentic pizza Dough Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
500gr Bread Flour or 00 Italian flour
325gr water luke warm
10gr salt
3gr active dry yeast

Directions:
Mix in a stand mixer for 2 minutes on slow then faster for 5 minutes.  Cover and let stand for 1 hour till it doubles in size.  Cut into 4 equal size pieces and let rise again.  Store in a refrigerator after balls double in size.
