Title:
PEBBLES Dino S'Mores Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978058.jpg
Ingredients:
20 squares HONEY MAID Honey Grahams, divided
1 (8 ounce) package BAKER'S Semi-Sweet Baking Chocolate
6 tablespoons butter or margarine, divided
1 (10 ounce) package JET-PUFFED Miniature Marshmallows
1 (13 ounce) package POST Cocoa PEBBLES Cereal
15 large JET-PUFFED Marshmallows, halved

Directions:
Line 13x9-inch pan with foil; grease foil. Place 15 graham squares on bottom of pan, cutting to fit if necessary. Coarsely crush remaining graham squares; set aside. Microwave chocolate and 2 tablespoons of the butter in microwaveable bowl on HIGH 2 minutes; stir until chocolate is completely melted and mixture is well blended. Spread half of the melted chocolate in thin layer over grahams in pan.
Microwave remaining 4 tablespoons butter in 4-quart microwaveable bowl on HIGH 45 seconds or until melted. Add miniature marshmallows; toss to coat. Microwave 1-1/2 minutes or until marshmallows are completely melted, stirring after 45 seconds. Add cereal; mix well. Place over chocolate-coated grahams; press gently to form even layer.
Top with large marshmallow halves, cut sides down. Sprinkle with crushed grahams; drizzle with remaining melted chocolate. Cool. Cut into 30 squares.

Submitter: 
BAKER'S Chocolate
Description: 
Get the taste of s'mores any time of year with these gooey and crunchy cereal dessert bars.
