Title:
Quick Homemade Mushroom Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 large onion, finely chopped
2 gloves garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon tarragon
pinch of nutmeg
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup flour
4 cups beef or veggie broth
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup cream
1/2 cup evaporated milk
tsp lemon juice
dash of hot sauce
salt and pepper

Directions:
In a dutch oven or soup kettle, saute the mushrooms, garlic, onion, tarragon, and nutmeg in the butter until veggies are tender. Stir in flour until smooth. Gradually add broth, bring to a boil, stirring constantly.
Reduce heat to low, slowly add sour cream. Cook and stir until smooth. Stir in cream and milk. Add lemon juice, hot sauce, salt and pepper. Heat through but do not boil.
