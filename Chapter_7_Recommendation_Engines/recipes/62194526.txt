Title:
Jim's Ham Salad Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups ground fully cooked ham
1 hard-cooked egg, chopped
2 tablespoons finely chopped celery
2 teaspoons finely chopped onion
2 teaspoons sweet pickle relish
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon prepared mustard
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp salt
1/4 cup red bell pepper diced

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first seven ingredients. Combine mayonnaise and mustard; add to ham mixture and mix well. Refrigerate until serving. Serve with crackers or as a sandwich

Submitter:

Description: 
You will enjoy this ham salad spread for years. It came to be an unexpected leftover every time we had ham for a special dinner.Modified from: Ham Salad Spread
