Title:
Roasted Butternut Squash with Arugula, Pumpkin Seeds, and Browned Butter-Apple Cider Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Squash Salad:
1 butternut squash, partially peeled (optional), cubed
1-2 tsp olive oil
salt
1 T olive oil
1 medium yellow onion, diced
4-6 garlic cloves, chopped finely
6oz Arugula
1/2-1 c finely chopped green onion
1/4 c toasted pumpkin seeds
Vinaigrette
1/2 c butter
3/4 c apple cider vinegar
2-4 cloves garlic, chopped
salt to taste

Directions:
peel (or not) squash and chop into 1 inch pieces or 1 inch slices
toss squash with little olive oil and salt
roast squash for 30-40 min until tender and browning, turning couple times
saute garlic and onion to soften (add little salt to sweat)
toast pumpkin seeds until fragrant and starting to brown (optional and to taste)
brown butter over high/medium heat (be careful not to burn), stir constantly/frequently
reduce vinegar over high/medium heat
add garlic to vinegar, add butter to vinegar, salt to taste
toss squash, onion/garlic, arugula, pumpkin seeds and vinaigrette
season to taste
serve over rice or quinoa
