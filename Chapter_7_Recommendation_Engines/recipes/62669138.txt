Title:
Texas Caviar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 Tablespoon water
3/4 cup apple cider vinegar
salt & pepper to taste
1 can of black beans (drained and rinsed)
1 can of black eye peas (drained and rinsed)
2 cans of shoepeg corn (drained and rinsed)
1 small jar of chopped pimento (drained and rinsed)
1 small can of green chiles (drained and rinsed)
1 small onion, chopped fine
2 cloves of garlic, minced

Directions:
1.  Heat sugar, oil, water, vinegar, salt &amp; pepper to boiling.  Cool slightly.
2.  Combined all remaining ingredients.
3.  Pour vinegar mixture over and refrigerate overnight.
