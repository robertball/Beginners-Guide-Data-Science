Title:
Instant Pot Beef Burrito Bowls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5629584.jpg
Ingredients:
1lb ground beef (I use 85%)
1 small onion diced
2 TBS olive oil
1 Cup uncooked long grain rice
1 14.5oz can roasted diced tomatoes (drained)
1 15oz can black beans (drained & rinsed)
5-6 TBS Taco Seasoning (or 2 packets)
2 1/4 cups vegetable broth or low sodium chicken broth
2 Cups Mexican Cheese
Fresh diced tomatoes
Diced green onions
Chopped cilantro
Sour cream
Avocado
Burrito Wrappers

Directions:
1.  Turn Instant Pot on and hit Saute button, then hit adjust button to set to More.  Add ground beef and Season with 1 teaspoon of kosher salt and 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper.
3.  Once beef is browned, add onions and cook until they start to soften.
4.  Add additional tablespoon of olive oil and uncooked rice and toast uncooked rice for about 2 minutes or just until some grains start to turn golden brown.
5.  Stir in black beans, canned tomatoes, vegetable or chicken broth, and taco seasoning.
6.  Lock Instant Pot lid in place and set to Manual, High Pressure for 7 minutes.
7.  Use Quick Pressure Release, and once pressure pin drops, open Instant Pot and fluff Beef Burrito Bowl with fork.  Add fresh cilantro while fluffing.
8.  Serve in burrito wrapper with cheese, garnishing with fresh tomatoes, green onions, sour cream, and avocado.
