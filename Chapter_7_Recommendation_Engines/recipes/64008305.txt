Title:
Fire Fitness Chocolate Protein Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup margarine
1/2 cup almond milk
2 scoops chocolate protein powder (6 tablespoons) I use Apex 110 calories/per scoop
1 1/2 cups Stevia (sugar substitute)
1 cup peanut butter
3 1/2 cups oats
1-2 tablespoons vanilla
*for chocolate lovers add 1/2 cup dark chocolate or semi sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
1. Melt margarine in a saucepan and add milk, protein powder, Stevia, and dark chocolate.
2. Bring to a boil for no more than a minute or until thick.
3. Remove from heat and add in the oats, peanut butter and vanilla.
4. Mix well.
5. If too crumbly add a touch of water, but this should not be needed.
6. Press into cookies and set to cool on wax paper.
