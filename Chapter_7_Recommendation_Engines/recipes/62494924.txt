Title:
Annie's Pumpkin Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/494864.jpg
Ingredients:
1c + 2 T all-purpose flour
11/2 c white sugar
1 t baking soda
1/2 t baking powder
1 T pumpkin pie spice (1 1/2 t cinnamon, 3/4 t ginger, 1/4 t allspice, 1/8 t cloves, 3/8 t nutmeg)
1 1/2 c pumpkin puree
5 med eggs
1 1/2 t lemon juice
1 c walnuts, chopped (optional)
3 T confectioners' sugar
1 (8oz) cream cheese, softened
1/4 c butter
1 t vanilla
1 c confectioners' sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Grease a 17 x 11 cookie sheet pan, lay in a sheet of wax paper, then grease and flour the wax paper.
In a large bowl, mix together flour, sugar, baking powder/soda, and pumpkin pie spice. Stir in pumpkin puree, eggs and lemon juice. Pour mixture into prepared pan. Spread mixture evenly. Sprinkle with optional nuts if desired.
Bake at 375' for 15 minutes.
Place linen towel on counter, sprinkle it with confectioner's sugar, and turn the cake onto the towel. Pull off wax paper. Carelfully roll the towel up with the cake in it to make a log. Place the cake-in-towel on a cooling rack and let it completely cool.
Make the incing: In a medium bowl, blend scream cheese, butter, vanilla and sugar with mixer until blended. Place in refrigerator until ready to use.
When the cake has cooled, unroll it and spread icing onto it. Immediately re-roll (without towel), and wrap it with plastic wrap. Keep the cake refrigerated or freeze it for 2 weeks in aluminum foil. Dust with confectioner's sugar if desired. Cut the cake in slices just before serving.
