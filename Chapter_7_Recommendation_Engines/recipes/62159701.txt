Title:
Tofu Walnut Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag walnuts, crushed
1/2 package firm tofu
1 egg
1/2 cup guinness beer
1 tbsp soy sauce
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix together crushed walnuts and egg, and beer.  Crumble in tofu until it forms a nice sticky consistency.  Mix in soy and spices.  Form into patties and grill for 5-7 minutes per side.  Serve on hamburger buns with your favorite toppings.

Submitter: 
AmieO
Description: 
A spectacular vegitarian burger option, or for anyone looking for something different.  A tofu loving friend and I dreamed this up for a memorial day grill out.
