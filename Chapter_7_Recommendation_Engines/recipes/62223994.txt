Title:
Potato Bacon Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•	3 medium Onions, small diced
•	1 bunch Celery, small diced
•	1 Red Pepper, small diced
•	2 # frozen Corn
•	½ # Real Butter
•	½ # Flour
•	1 gallon Heavy Cream
•	1 # Bacon, small diced, raw
•	3 tbsp Garlic, small chopped
•	Salt & Pepper
•	2 tbsp Red Pepper, crushed
•	18 medium size Potatoes, peeled, medium diced

Directions:
In a large heavy bottom pot, cook bacon until crispy. Add onions and celery, stir well, add garlic.  Add 1 quart water, let the vegetables steam. Add an additional quart of water and the heavy cream.  Reduce heat to medium.  In a med sauce pan add melted butter and flour to make a roux.  Stir into a paste. Add potatoes and cook just until the raw taste is gone.  Add roux slowly and stir. In a cast iron skillet, sauté the corn and red peppers until they are golden brown. Once roux mixture is smooth and temperature is 170-180 degrees, add corn and red peppers.  Taste, add salt and pepper.  Let the flour taste cook out.  Add a few drops of hot sauce.
Thank you and enjoy!
