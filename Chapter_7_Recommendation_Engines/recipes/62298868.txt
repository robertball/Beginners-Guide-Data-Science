Title:
Lasagna Primavera Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons butter (reserve 3)
8 ounces of sliced mushrooms (fresh or canned)
1 pound shredded carrots
1 medium onion chopped
2 garlic cloves minced
1 (14 1/2 ounce) Italian Style stewed tomatoes chopped
1 (6 ounce) can tomato pasted
1 (16 ounce) package of Mozzarella cheese shredded
1 (16 ounce) container of cottage cheese or ricotta
1 (10 ounce) package of spinach thawed and minced
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
8 lasagna noodles

Directions:
Grease a 13 x 9 baking dish and set aside.
Cook mushrooms in 1 tablespoon butter over medium high heat until tender.  Remove from skillet.  Cook and stir carrots, onions, garlic, and remaining 3 tablespoons of butter until the carrots are tender.  Reduce heat to medium, add tomatoes and paste.  Simmer for 5 minutes, then stir the mushrooms back in.
In a medium bowl mix 2 cups mozzarella, cottage or ricotta cheese, spinach and parmesan cheese.
Arrange 4 noodles in bottom of the baking dish.  Top with 1/2 of the spinach mix and 1/2 of the tomato mix.  Repeat layers and top off with rest of mozzarella cheese.
Bake for 40 -  45 minutes until cheese melts.  Let stand for about 10 minutes and eat!
