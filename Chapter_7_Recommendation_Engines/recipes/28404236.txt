Title:
Black-Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 oz. can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 small tomato cut up
1 garlic clove peeled
1 TBS. diced red onion
2 TBS. lime juice
1 TBS. fresh cilantro leaves
salt and pepper to taste
Sour cream for garnish
(for dipping use pita bread, bit size pieces of cooked steak, or carrot and celery sticks)

Directions:
Put the beans, tomato, garlic, onion, lime juice, and cilantro in a food processor.  Pulse until mixture is smooth, about 1 minute.  Season with salt and pepper.
Transfer to a bowl and top with sour cream, if using.  Serve.
