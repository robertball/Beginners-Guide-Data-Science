Title:
Budget Chocolate Covered Strawberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/271115.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (11.5 ounce) package milk chocolate or bittersweet chocolate chips
1 pound fresh strawberries with leaves

Directions:
In a double boiler, melt the chocolate. Make sure that the water is only hot and not simmering.  Remove chocolate from heat once melted.
Holding the strawberry from the base of the leaves, dip into melted chocolate, drag the back side of the berry along the side of the bowl, place berry on to parchment paper to harden.

Submitter: 
CookingOnABudget
Description: 
Simply melt the chocolate over a double boiler, no need to add the shortening to the chocolate, which can actually make the chocolate taste greasy.  Shop your local grocer fliers for sales on cartons of strawberries.Modified from: Chocolate Covered Strawberries
