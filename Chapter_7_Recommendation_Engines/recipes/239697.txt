Title:
Manhattan Cherries! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3277923.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup dried cherries, or as desired
1/2 cup bourbon, or as needed

Directions:
Fill a small jar with dried cherries and pour in enough bourbon to cover cherries. Cover jar tightly with a lid and place in the refrigerator until cherries are hydrated and infused with bourbon, at least 2 weeks.

Submitter: 
JJOYB53
Description: 
I am a Manhattan fan - on the dry side, please! I came up with this idea when my husband was on a kick of making infused vodkas. Not so sweet as maraschino cherries, for those of us who like the 'dry side'! Use in Manhattans as you would maraschino cherries.
