Title:
American-Style Creamy Greek Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1316693.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cucumber, chopped
1/2 (4 ounce) package brine-packed feta cheese
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup sour cream
3 tablespoons milk
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder, or to taste
1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
1/2 teaspoon onion powder, or to taste
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste

Directions:
Process cucumber, feta cheese, mayonnaise, sour cream, milk, olive oil, garlic powder, salt, onion powder, and black pepper in a food processor until smooth.

Submitter: 
Lea Davis
Description: 
This is a dressing I made for an American-style Greek salad, one with a hearty lettuce not typically found in a traditional Greek salad.
