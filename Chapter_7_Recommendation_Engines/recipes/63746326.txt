Title:
simmered lentils Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c lentils
1 tbsp olive oil
1//2 chopped onion
2 diced carrots
2 cloves minced garlic
1 1/2 c low-sodium chicken broth or water
2 bay leaves
1/2 tbsp red wine vinegar
salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:
rinse lentils and pick through, discarding and stones.
heat the olive oil in a pot over med heat. cook the onion carrots and garlic until softened, 5-7 mins. add the lentils broth and bay leaves. simmer until the lentils are just tender, about 20 min. season with the vinegar, salt and pepper. discard the bay leaves
