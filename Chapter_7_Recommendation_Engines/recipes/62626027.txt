Title:
Al's Awesome Marinara Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 Large Onion
2 cloves of garlic, minced
3/4 Cup white wine
1 32 oz can San Marzano tomatoes, crushed
1 32 oz can tomatoe puree
2 Tablespoons brown sugar
1 Tablespoon dried Oregano
2 Tablespoons dried basil
2-3 Bay leaves
1 Cup water
1 Tablespoon kosher salt

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a 6 quart dutch oven. Add diced onion and minced garlic and a pinch of kosher salt. Cook until translucent, about 10 minutes. Deglaze pan with white wine. Add both cans of tomatoes and the remaining ingredients. Simmer for about 1 hour.
