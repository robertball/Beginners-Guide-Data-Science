Title:
Braised Cabbage, Kielbasa, and Spuds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. green cabbage
4 large potatoes
1 cup vegetable broth
1 Tbsp. mustard
1 lb. kielbasa, thinly sliced
mustard to serve

Directions:
Cut cabbage and potatoes into 1-inch chunks. Mix chicken broth with mustard in a large saucepan. Add cabbage and potatoes; bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.
Add kielbasa, cover, and cook 5 minutes more until hot.
Serve with mustard.
