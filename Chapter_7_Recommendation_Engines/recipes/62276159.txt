Title:
Chicken Jalfrezi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bit-sized pieces
1 onion, thinly sliced and cut to about 2-inch lengths
1/2 green pepper, thinly sliced and cut to about 2-inch lengths
1/2 red pepper, thinly sliced and cut to about 2-inch lengths
4 cloves garlic, grated
1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger root, grated
3 teaspoons ground turmeric
1 teaspoon chili powder
3 teaspoons ground cumin
3 teaspoons ground coriander
2 (14.5 ounce) cans peeled and diced tomatoes

Directions:
Heat 2 tablespoons of the oil in a large deep skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink on the outside.  Remove chicken and set aside.
Heat remaining oil in the skillet over medium heat.  Add onions and peppers and sautee until no longer crisp.
Add garlic, ginger and spices and stir for about 1 minute until very fragrant.
Deglaze the pan with a small amount of broth or water.
Add the tomatoes and return the chicken and any juices to the skillet.  Cover and simmer over low heat for one hour.
Serve over brown or basmati rice.

Submitter: 
fullerla
Description: 
Chicken, peppers, onions and spices combine to create an aromatic, hearty dish perfect served over brown or basmati rice with steamed cauliflower on the side.
