Title:
Italian Spaghetti Sauce with Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
MEATBALLS
1 pound lean ground beef
1 cup fresh bread crumbs
1 teaspoon dried parsley
1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon Italian style saesoning
1/8 teaspoon chrushed red pepper flakes
3/4 teaspoon fennel seeds
1/2 teaspoon dried minced onions
1 teaspoon salt
1 egg, beaten
SAUCE
3/4 cup chopped onion
5 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup olive oil
2 (28 ounce) cans whole peeled tomatoes, pureed
2 teaspoons salt
3 Tablespoons brown sugar
1 bay leaf
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
3/4 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 teaspoons Itailian style seasoning

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine ground beef, bread crumbs, spices and beaten egg.  Mix well and form into 24 balls.  Store, covered, in refrigerator until needed.
In a large saucepan over medium heat, saute onion and garlic in olive oil until onion is translucent.  Stir in tomatoes, salt, sugar, Italian style seasoning, and bay leaf.  Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer 90 minutes.  Stir in tomato paste, basil, 1/2 teaspoon pepper and meatballs and simmer 30 minutes more.  Serve.
