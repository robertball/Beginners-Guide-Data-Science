Title:
4-3-2 microwave cashew brittle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups salted roasted cashews
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup light corn syrup
1 Tblsp. butter
1/2 tsp. butter flavoring
1 tsp. real vanilla
1 tsp. baking soda

Directions:
Mix cashews,sugar and corn syrup in a medium pirex bowl. Microwave on high for 4 minutes. Take out and stir and return to microwave for 3 more minutes. Take it out and add butter, butter flavoring and vanilla. Stir and return to microwave for 2 minutes. Take out and add the baking soda. Stir and fold until mixture is fluffy. Work quickly and spread out evenly on a lightly buttered cookie sheet. Let it sit until completely cooled. Break into pieces and keep in a airtight bag or container.

Submitter: 
Marie Berron
Description: 
Easy buttery brittle made in the microwave in just about 10 minutes. Every time I make this it disappears very fast. WARNING: once you start you can't stop.
