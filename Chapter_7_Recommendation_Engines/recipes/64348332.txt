Title:
Joy's Salisbury Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
1 packet Lipton Onion Soup mix
1 TBS Worcestershire Sauce
1 TBS Ketchup
1/4 C Seasoned Bread crumbs
1/4 C Parmesan Cheese shredded
1 TBS water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325*
Shape beef into 5 small oval loaves
Heat 1 TBS olive oil in an oven proof skillet
Lightly brown patties on both sides without cooking through and remove from pan
Add 1 TBS olive oil and sautee' 1 medium onion sliced and 1 can or 8 oz fresh mushrooms
Put patties back in pan and top them with some of the onion/mushroom mixture
Add 1 C beef broth to pan, cover and cook in oven for 30 minutes
Remove patties and vegetables and pour juices into a saucepan. Add enough broth to equal 2 C liquid to use for gravy.
Stir 2 TBS flour into cold water to form a liquid slurry not a paste
Bring liquid in saucepan to a boil, stir constantly
while adding slurry and boil until thickened.
Serve Salisbury Steak topped with onion/mushroom mixture and gravy

Submitter: 
Joy Rogers
Description: 
Serve this with mashed potatoes and a vegetable for a delicious comforting meal.
