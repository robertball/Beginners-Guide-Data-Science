Title:
Skillet Basil Cream Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c milk
1/4 c dry bread crumbs
1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast (4 halves)
3 tbsp butter
1/2 c chicken broth
1 c heavy cream
1 can (4 oz)
Pimentos
1 c or 1 can diced tomatoes
1/4 c fresh basil, cut up
1/2 c grated Parmesan cheese
2 tbsp Boursin cheese
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Heat large skillet over medium heat. Add butter to skillet.
Place milk and bread crumbs in separate shallow bowls.
Dip chicken in milk, then coat with crumbs, then place in skillet to cook through, about 6 minutes each side.
Transfer chicken to a separate plate.
Add broth to skillet and bring to boil.
Stir in cream, pimentos, and tomatoes. Boil and stir 1 minute.
Reduce heat and add Parmesan cheese, Boursin cheese, basil, and pepper. Cool and stir until heated through.
Add chicken back into sauce.
Serve with rice or pasta.
