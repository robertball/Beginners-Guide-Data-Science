Title:
Greek Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb fresh ground turkey
1 tsp olive oil
1 large onion, chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
1 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp dried thyme
2 bay leaves
1/4 tsp pepper
2 cans Italian style stewed tomatoes
2 14 oz cans diced tomatoes
1 14 oz can artichoke hearts (not marinated), drained
1 19 oz can chickpeas (garbanzo  beans), drained and rinsed
1 2 oz can sliced black olives (may also add green olives)
Crumbled feta cheese, for serving

Directions:
In stock pot, heat oil on medium.
Saut√© onions and garlic.
Add turkey and spices, and cook until turkey is no longer pink, stirring regularly.
Add tomatoes, artichoke hearts, chickpeas, and olives.
Bring to boil, reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes.
Garnish with feta cheese.
