Title:
Orange Pear Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/328207.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pear, cored and chopped
2 oranges, peeled and seeded
1/4 cup milk
3/4 cup vanilla ice cream

Directions:
In a blender, combine pear, oranges, milk and ice cream. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
Tyler Broska
Description: 
A great tasting drink that is easy to make.
