Title:
Christmas cookies- Swedish butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2lb butter
1/2C sugar
1 egg yolk
1Tbsp milk
1tsp vanilla
2C flour
1/2tsp baking powder
1/2C chopped pecans
cherries (quarted)

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar, add the egg, milk and vanilla. Once mixed well add the flour and baking powder.  Form dough into balls and dip into beaten egg white and then firmly ground nuts. Put adn indent in the middle of the cookie and then put the cherry in the center. Bake at 350 for 20min.
