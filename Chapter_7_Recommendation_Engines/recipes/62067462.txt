Title:
"Get Better" Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. olive oil
3 tbsp. butter
1 med. yellow onion, chopped
3 carrots, peeled and chopped
5 stalks celery, chopped
3 baby red potatoes, quartered
1/4 cup flour
4 cloves garlic, minced
3-4 chicken breasts, boiled and shredded
8 cups chicken broth
3-4 sprigs fresh parsley
2 tbsp. marjoram
2 tbsp. fresh or dried sage
3 bay leaves
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup short noodles (shells, short egg noodles or elbow macaroni)
1 can dark red kidney beans, drained (optional)

Directions:
In a large pot, heat olive oil and butter.
Add onion and cook for 10 minutes or until softened.
Add carrots, celery and potatoes and cook for 10 more minutes.
Add garlic and cook for 2-3 minutes.
Sprinkle with flour and stir, scraping up brown bits from the bottom.
Add the broth, chicken, salt, pepper, marjoram and parsley.
Simmer for 1 hour.
Turn up the heat a bit and add the noodles and beans if using, and sprinkle additional salt and pepper and sage.  Cook for 10 minutes more or until noodles are al dente.
Serve immediately.
