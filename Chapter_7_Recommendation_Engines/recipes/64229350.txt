Title:
Pancit Canton Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chicken, cut up and skinned.
1 Tbsp oil
1/2 onion, diced
5 cloves garlic, minced
2 carrots, cut in thin strips
1 cup green beans, French cut thinly sliced
2 tsp chicken bouillon
2 Tbsp soy sauce
400 gm canton noodle
1/2 small cabbage, thinly sliced
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
In Dutch oven, cover chicken pieces with water and boil until tender and broth develops.  Remove chicken and reserve broth.
In same pot, saute onion and garlic in oil.  Add chicken pieces and saute for 2 minutes.  Add carrots and saute for 1 minute.  Add green beans and saute for 1 minute.  Add 2 cups broth from chicken.  Stir, cover and bring to a boil.  Add chicken bouillon cube, cover and cook for 10 minutes.
Add soy sauce.  Break up canton noodles and add to mixture.  Stir in slowly, stirring noodles in broth to absorb moisture.  Continue stirring intil broth is absorbed.  Add cabbage and stir.  Cover and turn off heat.  (Add more broth if needed).  Cooking time approx. 30 minutes.
