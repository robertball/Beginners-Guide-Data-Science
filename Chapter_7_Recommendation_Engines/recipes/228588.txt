Title:
Curried Honey Mustard Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6596450.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup honey
1/4 cup Dijon mustard
1/2 teaspoon curry powder

Directions:
Mix mayonnaise, honey, Dijon mustard, and curry powder together in a bowl until completely combined into a smooth sauce.

Submitter: 
STORMY3600
Description: 
My favorite honey mustard sauce is a little different from most. The touch of curry powder makes the difference! Wonderful with chicken. I have made this using brown sugar instead of honey; it's almost as good.
