Title:
The Whiskey Rose Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6399493.jpg
Ingredients:
ice cubes
2 ounces  whiskey
1/2 ounce rose syrup
Orange bitters
Orange peel

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice.  Add whiskey, bitters and rose syrup. Stir with a cocktail spoon  until chilled about 20 seconds. Massage the orange  peel between your fingers, drop into a Martini glass.  Strain liquid into the glass.

Description: 
The Whiskey Rose...I discovered rose syrup on a trip to Grasse, France.  I bought a bottle on vacation and used it to make this cocktail.  You can find rose syrup online. It’s delicious.   If you like sweeter cocktails add more.  You can’t get easier then this.
