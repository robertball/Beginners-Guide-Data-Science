Title:
Hot Chocolate Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup (100g) cocoa
1/2 cup (100g) sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Mix all together. Use 2 scoops (2 tbsp) in 1 cup of steamed milk. Add cayenne if desired. And maybe some rum and whipped cream! If it's not sweet enough, add a few drops of stevia.

Submitter: 
Evie
Description: 
It's so easy to make hot chocolate - why buy from the store?
