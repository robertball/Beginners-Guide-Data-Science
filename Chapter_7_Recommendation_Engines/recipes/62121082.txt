Title:
Snickers Bar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  large eggs
1  cup sugar
1  cup brown sugar
1  cup butter
1  cup peanut butter
2  teaspoons vanilla
3  cups flour
1  teaspoon baking powder
1  teaspoon baking soda
1/2  teaspoon salt
20  Snickers miniature candy bars, unwrapped

Directions:
In large bowl, combine sugars, butter, peanut butter, vanilla and eggs.
Beat well.
Add flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt.
Mix well.
Shape about 1/4 cup of dough smoothly around candy bar making sure bar is completely covered.
Place 4 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake at 375Â°F for 13-14 minutes or until light golden brown and puffy.
Cool 10 minutes and remove from cookie sheets.
Makes 20 HUGE cookies.

This is a wonderful cookie for the Holidays.  The kids love it and always ask for it for their school parties.  Best thing is that it's easy to make.
