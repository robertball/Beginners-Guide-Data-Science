Title:
Spicy Roasted Red Pepper Yogurt Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2497392.jpg
Ingredients:
1 packet Hidden Valley® Greek Yogurt Original Ranch® Dip Mix
1 1/2 cups plain Greek yogurt
1 (12 ounce) jar roasted red bell peppers, drained and pureed
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste

Directions:
Combine dip mix with yogurt. Cover; refrigerate 30 minutes to thicken. Add pepper puree, cumin and cayenne pepper to dip. Cover; refrigerate additional 30 minutes for flavors to blend.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley®
Description: 
Greek yogurt and pureed roasted red peppers is mixed with spices and Hidden Valley® Greek Yogurt Original Ranch® Dip Mix for a real party favorite.
