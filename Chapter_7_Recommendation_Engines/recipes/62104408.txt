Title:
Not Barbecued Short Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 to 8 pounds of boneless short ribs
Salt and pepper to taste
3 tsp dried sage
3 tsp thyme
2 20 oz chunk pineapple with juice
3 cup water
5 Tbs ketchup
7 Tbs Worchestershire sauce
3 large onions, chopped
4 chopped garlic cloves or to taste
1 1/2 tsp dry mustard
1 1/2 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Place chopped onion in large crock pot.  Put short ribs on top of onions.  Mix remaining ingredients and pour over ribs. Cover and cook on high for 4 hours.  You can cook on low for 6-8 hours too.

Submitter: 
Mama Luvs Papa
Description: 
A slow cooked crock of short ribs that are easy to prepare, tasty, very tender and have a Polynesian twist.  Great over rice. Great as a pot-luck dish.
