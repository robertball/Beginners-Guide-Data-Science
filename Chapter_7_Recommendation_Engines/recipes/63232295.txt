Title:
FAT GRANNYS MINESTRONE SOUP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
--seasonings--
1 tsp each of the following:
granulated onion & garlic
celery seed
parsley,thyme,basil,oregano
italian seasoning
salt and pepper
1 bay leave
---now for the soup---
3 tbl olive oil
1 stick butter
1 red and green bell pepper diced
1 lg onion diced
1 lg carrot diced
2 stalks celery diced
1 sm zucchini diced
1 tbl minced garlic
1(28oz)can san marzano tomatoes diced saving the juice.
1(15oz) can tomato sauce
1(15oz) jar 3 bean salad drained and rinsed  well.
1(15oz) pkg frozen french style green beans.
6 cups chicken stock
1 cup cooked elbow macaroni

Directions:
In a small bowl add all of your seasonings and set aside.(this will save you time later on.)
In a large sauce pan(4 or 5 qt)on medium high heat.add the olive oil and butter.next the carrots,celery and onions.saute for 2 to 3 min.add the garlic,peppers, zucchini and seasonings.stir to combine.add the tomatoes with the juice, tomato sauce, beans and the chicken stock.stir well to combine all of the ingredients.
bring to a boil.reduce heat and simmer for 30 min.stirring every 5 min or so.turn off heat,add your pasta,garnish with parmasan cheese, stir and serve.
