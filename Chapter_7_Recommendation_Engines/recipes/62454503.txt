Title:
Fall Veggie Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cluster fresh basil leaves, chopped
1 zucchini, chopped
2 tomatoes, chopped
1 can tomato sauce (add Italian seasoning) or pizza sauce
1 bag low fat mozzarella
Fresh pizza dough

Directions:
Precook pizza crust 5 minutes
Add sauce, veggies, and cheese.
Bake at 450 for 14 minutes.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
Great with fall farmer's market veggies.
