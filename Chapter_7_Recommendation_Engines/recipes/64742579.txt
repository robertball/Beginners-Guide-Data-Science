Title:
Quick and Easy Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of small red skin potatoes (about 1")
2 Tablespoons butter
Hidden Valley Ranch Seasoning and Dressing Mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 435 degrees
Pierce the potatoes and place them  in a microwave safe bowl
Cook on high for about 4 minutes
Turn the potatoes, sprinkle with Hidden Valley seasoning mix and put the butter into the bowl
Continue to cook another 2-4 minutes until soft
Cut the potatoes in half
Toss in the melted butter and seasoning mix
Transfer cut side down onto a rimmed baking sheet
Place in 435 degree oven for 15 minutes until wonderfully browned.
