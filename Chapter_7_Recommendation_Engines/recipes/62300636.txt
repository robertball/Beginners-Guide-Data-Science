Title:
Best Cream Of Broccoli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1 onion, finely chopped (less)
1 stalk celery, finely chopped
3 cups chicken broth
8 cups broccoli florets
3 tablespoons butter
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 cups milk
1 teaspoon salt (for 4 servings)
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg (for 4 servings)
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt 2 tablespoons butter in medium sized stock pot, and saute onion and celery until tender. Add broccoli and broth, cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
Roux: (mix in pan - HOT) In small saucepan, over medium-heat melt 3 tablespoons butter, stir in flour and add milk.  Stir until thick and bubbly, and add to soup.  Season with pepper and serve.

Submitter: 
bluedaisy
Description: 
Onions, celery and broccoli cooked in chicken broth are pureed with milk in this roux-thickened soup.  It should be thick before adding the milk.  Add some nutmegModified from: Best Cream Of Broccoli Soup
