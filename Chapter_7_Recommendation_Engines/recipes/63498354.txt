Title:
Biscuit topped Italian Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb Ground beef
8 oz can of tomato sauce
3/4 cup water
1/4 teaspoon pepper
10 oz package of frozen mixed vegetables
2 cups Shredded cheddar cheese, divided
12 oz package of buttermilk biscuits
1 tablespoon butter or margarine, melted
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano

Directions:
In a large skillet, cook beef; drain. Stir in the tomato sauce, water, and pepper. Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.
Remove from heat and stir in vegetables and 1 1/2 cup cheese.
Transfer to a greased 9X13 baking dish.
Split each biscuit in half and arrange biscuits around the edge of the dish overlapping slightly. Brush with melted buter and sprinkle with oregano. Sprinkle remaining cheese over the meat mixture.
Bake; uncovered at 375 degrees for 25-30 minutes, or until biscuits are golden brown.
