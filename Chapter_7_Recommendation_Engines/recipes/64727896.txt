Title:
Dutch Chocolate Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb butter - melted
1/2 lb graham crackers crushed
1/2 c peanut butter
2 cups flaked coconut
1 cup nuts
2 cups powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla
topping: 1 cup chocolate chips
1 cup butterscotch chips

Directions:
Mix and roll into balls
place a round toothpick in each and freeze
Melt Chips and dip balls
place dipped balls on wax paper
