Title:
Lemon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
Salt and pepper to taste
Flour for dredging
4 tbsp butter
2 tbsp olive oil
1/3 c fresh lemon juice
1 c chicken stock or dry white wine
Corn starch to thicken
1/4 c brined capers
1/3 c fresh parsley, chopped

Directions:
Season chicken with salt and pepper and dredge in flour. Shake off excess.
In large skillet, melt 2 tbsp butter with olive oil over medium high heat,
Add chicken pieces to skillet and cook 3-4 minutes per side until browned. Remove from skillet.
Remove skillet from heat and add lemon juice, wine, remaining butter, and capers. Return to heat and bring to boil. Thicken with corn starch. If desired, add additional salt and pepper and wine or chicken stock.
Whisk and simmer 2-3 minutes, adding chicken back into sauce.
If you wish to prepare in advance, place chicken and sauce in baking dish, to refrigerate overnight. Remove and let sit for 30 minutes, then bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes to heat.
