Title:
Pumpkin Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup canned pumpkin
1 spice cake mix
1/2 Cup vegetable oil
1 box vanilla instant pudding
3 eggs
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 Cup water
1/2-3/4 Cup chopped pecans
2 Tbsp.melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a 12 cup bundt pan.
Melt the butter in a small pan and add chopped pecans. Toast slightly.
Place the buttered pecans in the bottom of the bundt pan and set aside.
In a large mixing bowl place and remaining ingredients in order listed.
Beat 5 minutes on high speed, scraping the sides of the mixing bowl occasionally.
Pour the batter into the bundt pan.
Bake at 350.
Baking time: 40-45 minutes
Cool in the pan 15 minutes then remove from the pan and cool on racks.
Serve as is or top with penuche icing.
