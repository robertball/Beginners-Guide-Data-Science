Title:
Scrumptious Chocolate Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/4 cups oil
3 1/4 cups sugar
2 cups brown sugar
6 eggs
5 1/4 cups flour
2 cups cocoa
3 tsp. baking soda
2 cups choc chips (or however much you want)

Directions:
Mix the oil, sugar, and brown sugar together in a large mixer bowl. Add the eggs, and mix again. Now add the flour, cocoa and baking soda, and give it another stir. Add the chocolate chips. Shape them into balls-how ever large or small you want. Bake at 350 for 8 to 10 minutes. they may look raw, but they are done!
