Title:
Best Ever Sausage with Peppers, Onions, and Beer! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons olive oil
3 pounds Italian sausage links
1 red bell pepper, sliced
2 orange bell pepper, sliced
1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
2 large red onions, sliced
1 package of mushrooms,8 oz
3 cloves garlic, chopped
2 (12 fluid ounce) bottles beer
2 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
3 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large heavy skillet over medium high heat. Cook sausage until browned on all sides. Remove sausage from pan, and set aside.Fry peppers, onions, and mushrooms until soft.Put above in slow cooker. Pour in 1 bottle of beer to deglaze the pan, scraping up any blackened bits from the bottom.  Stir in the remaining beer and the tomato paste. Season with oregano, parsley, salt and pepper. Cover, and simmer for 45 minutes. Slice the sausages into bite size pieces. Cover, and simmer until sausage is cooked through.
