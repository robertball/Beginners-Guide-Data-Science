Title:
Apple Hot Toddy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/931434.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups water
2 tablespoons plus 2 teaspoons honey
1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
2 cups apple liqueur, I used Berentzen
Eight 3-inch cinnamon sticks

Directions:
In small saucepan, bring the water to a boil. Remove from the heat and stir in the honey until dissolved. Stir in the lemon juice and apple brandy. Set a cinnamon stick in each of 8 mugs or heatproof glasses, pour in the hot liquid and serve.

Submitter: 
A Healthy Life For Me
Description: 
A Hot Toddy is usually known as a toasty winter beverage made from a distilled spirit, honey and water.  Now that I knew the basics I chose a Apple Liqueur recipe it seemed like the one I would most want to snuggle up to a fire and drink. Enjoy!
