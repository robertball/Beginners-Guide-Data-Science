Title:
Chile Colorado Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 dried Ancho or Pasilla chiles
1 large onion, chopped
6 cloves garlic crushed or finely minced
3 lbs beef chuck roast in 1" cubes
3-1/2 cups boiling beef broth
3 Tbsp mild chile powder
2 tsp ground cumin
4 Tbsp olive oli

Directions:
Stem,seed and chop ancho chiles, add to beef broth, let stand for 30 minutes
Strain chilies and set aside, save broth
Saute onion and garlic in 1 Tbsp of oil until lightily browned, set aside
In three separate but equal portions, brown beef in remaining oil.
Combine beef and onion/garlic mixture
Add chile powder and ground cumin
Add 2-1/2 cups of saved beef broth
Simmer uncovered for 1 hour
Place remaining beef broth & soaked chiles in a blender, Blend till smooth, add to simmering beef, contine to simmer uncovered for another 30 minutes or until beef is tender.
Garnish with sweet red onion, avocado, lime juice grated cheese, and chopped red bell peppers.
Serve with rice.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
Ancho or pasilla chiles are sweet and fairly mild.  In this recipe, they give the chile a rich dark color and provide a unique slightly spicy flavor.
