Title:
Italian Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef or turkey
1.5 onion chopped
1 cup chopped celery
1.5 cup chopped carrots
5 cloves garlic, minced
2 (14.5 ounce) can peeled and diced tomatoes
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 (19 ounce) cans kidney beans (1 white and 1 red), drained and rinsed
6 cups water
9 beef bouillon cubes
3 tablespoon dried parsley
1 tablespoon dried oregano
1 tablespoon dried basil
2 cups chopped cabbage
1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn
1 (15 ounce) can green beans
1 (9 ounce) bag fresh spinach
1 cup macaroni (cooked separately)

Directions:
Place ground beef in a large soup pot. Cook over medium heat until evenly browned. Drain excess fat. Stir in onion, celery, carrots, garlic, chopped tomatoes, tomato sauce, beans, water and bouillon. Season with parsley, oregano and basil. Simmer for 20 minutes.
Stir in cabbage, corn, green beans Bring to a boil, then reduce heat. Simmer until vegetables are tender and pasta is al dente. Add spinach towards end. Add more water if needed.
Cook the macaroni separate and mix in to each bowl upon serving.

Submitter: 
Chris Ginn
Description: 
Carrots, celery, onions, cabbage, corn, and green beans -- this soup is full of vegetables!  With ground beef, beans, and macaroni, a loaf of warm, crusty bread is all you need for a well-rounded dinner.Modified from: Italian Vegetable Soup
