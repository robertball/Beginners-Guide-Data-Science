Title:
Italian Sausage Soup, the best! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds Italian sausage; one sweet, one hot & one mild
4 onions, chopped
8 garlic cloves
2 - 14.5 ounce cans diced tomatoes (break up tomatoes if needed)
1 bottle Merlot (wine)
3 - 32 ounce cartons beef broth
1 teaspoon basil
1 teaspoon oregano
6 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
2 bell peppers, chopped
2 zucchini, sliced 1/4-inch thick
4 cups spinach noodles, uncooked
salt and pepper, to taste
grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In skillet: brown sausage, drain.
In large soup pot: Saut√© onion and garlic until soft.  Add tomatoes, Merlot wine, broth, basil & oregano.  Simmer uncovered 30 minutes.
Add parsley, bell pepper, zucchini, pasta, salt, pepper and cooked sausage.  Cover, simmer for 25 minutes.
Serve topped with the Parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
This soup  is just the right medicine!  I got the recipe from a good long time friend who was gifted it from her coworker; both nurses.   It takes a little time, not difficult.  It feeds a lot of people, make it for a party or large group.
