Title:
Creamy Blue Cheese 'n Bacon Topped Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices	 Bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 cup	 Onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup	 Mayonnaise
4 ounces	 Crumbled Blue Cheese
2 tablespoons	 Fresh Parsley, finely chopped
1/8 teaspoon	 Ground Black Pepper
8 Hamburgers, grilled or broiled
8 Hamburger Buns, lightly toasted

Directions:
Cook onions over medium heat until golden brown (about 8 minutes).
Combine mayonnaise with blue cheese, parsley and black pepper in a small bowl.
Add onions and bacon.
Arrange burgers on buns, top with bacon mixture and enjoy.
