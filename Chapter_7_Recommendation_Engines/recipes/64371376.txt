Title:
Corned Beef with Spiced Mustard Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4lb Corned Beef Brisket
1/2 cup apple jelly
1 1/2 tablespoon ground seed/or beer mustard
1/4 tsp cloves

Directions:
Place corned beef in slow cooker with spice packet; cover with water and cook on low for 6-8 hours or simmer on stove top for 2.5 hours.
Melt apple jelly, mustard, and cloves together in saucepan or in microwave.
Preheat oven to 350; baste liberally 2 or 3 times with sauce for 30 minutes.
