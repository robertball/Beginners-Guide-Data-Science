Title:
Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound medium red potatoes, quartered
1 boneless beef chuck roast (3 to 4 pounds)
1/4 cup Dijon mustard
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/3 cup chopped onion
1-1/2 cups beef broth

Directions:
Place potatoes in a 5-qt. slow cooker. Cut roast in half. In a small bowl, combine the mustard, garlic salt, thyme and pepper; rub over roast. Place in slow cooker; top with onion and broth. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or until meat is tender. Yield: 6-8 servings.
