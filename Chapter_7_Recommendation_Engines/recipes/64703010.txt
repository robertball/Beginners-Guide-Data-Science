Title:
Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5 Pounds Beef, Top Round, Trimmed and Medium Diced
1 Tbsp 21 Season Salute (From Trader Joes. You can substitute Montreal Steak Seasoning if not available)
5 Tbsp Butter, Salted
1 Cup Flour, All Purpose
Half of an Onion, Vidalia, Medium Diced
1 Pound Mushroom, White Button or Mini Portobello, Halved
1 qt Beef Stock
1 pt Heavy Cream
2 Tbsp Dijon Mustard
1 Tbsp Dill, Chopped
1 Tbsp Parsley, Chopped
8 oz Sour Cream
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Lightly toss diced beef in flour and 21 Season Salute.
Sauté the meat in butter in a deep skillet or pot until cooked through, then remove and set meat aside, keeping in a warm place.
Sauté onions, in the pot used to cook the meat until they begin to caramelize, then add mushrooms and briefly sauté. Add beef stock and return meat to pot.
Slowly add the heavy cream, mustard and sour cream, dill and parsley.
Bring to a simmer and cook until meat is tender.
Tip: Serve over egg noodles. Recipe will yield enough for 1 pound of egg noodles.

Submitter: 
ToriGeib
Description: 
You can also make this ahead until the point where you add the cream and keep it warm in a crock pot to simmer all day.
