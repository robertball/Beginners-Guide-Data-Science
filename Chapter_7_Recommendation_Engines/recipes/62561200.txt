Title:
Pan Fried Garlic Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 4-5oz beef ribeye steaks(delmonico)1/2 inch thick
6 cloves garlic peeled and sliced thin
2 Tablespoons butter
1 15-19oz can cannellini beans
1/4cup parsley

Directions:
Drizzle steaks with olive oil; sprinkle with salt and pepper
Heat 12 inch skillet over med high heat. Add steaks and cook 4 mins on each side or to desired doneness. Remove steaks to warm plate and cover to keep warm.
Add garlic slices to the skillet and fry for a minute to soften. scatter the garlic on top the steaks.
Add the beans and butter to skillet and heat through. Add parsley and cook one more minute.
Serve steaks with the beans.
