Title:
Cherry Muesli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C. whole oats
1/2 C. dried cherries
1/2 C. sliced almonds
2 T. flax seed
2 T. olive oil
2 T. pure maple syrup
1 tsp. sugar, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  Line a cookie sheet with sides with foil and very lightly mist with non-stick cooking spray.  In a large bowl combine all of the ingredients, except the sugar, until well-coated.  Spread the muesli evenly on the prepared cookie sheet.  Evenly sprinkle with 1/2 tsp. sugar.  Bake for 10 minutes, stir gently and sprinkle the remaining sugar over the oat mixture and bake another 10 minutes.  Remove from oven and allow to cool completely.  Store in air tight container.  Keeps for up to one week.

Submitter: 
Sally Anne
Description: 
This is adapted from a Martha Stewart recipe.  A crunchy, low-fat and high fiber addition to cereal or yogurt.
