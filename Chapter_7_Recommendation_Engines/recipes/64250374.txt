Title:
Traditional Cold Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box (10 oz.) frozen chopped spinach, cooked, cooled and squeezed dry
1 container (16 oz.) sour cream or nonfat plain Greek yogurt
1 cup Hellmann's® or Best Foods® Real Mayonnaise
1 package Knorr® Vegetable recipe mix
1 can (8 oz.) water chestnuts, drained and chopped

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and chill about 2 hours. Serve with your favorite dippers to your favorite people.

This the traditional Knorr recipe for spinach dip.
