Title:
Sheppard's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. Gr. Beef
1/2 Onion(chopped)
1T. Butter
1C. Frozen Peas
1C. Frozen Corn
2C. Carrots(steamed and mashed w/nutmeg,milk and    butter)
1 Pkg. Au Jus Mix
1/2 C. Water
1T. Tomato Paste
1t. salt
1/4t.Pepper
1/2t. Oregano
1 envelope instant mashed potatoes(prepared)
1C. Cheddar cheese(shredded)

Directions:
Brown onions in butter. Add Gr. Beef and cook until brown. Stir in Au Jus, water, and tomato paste to skillet. Let simmer 15min. Add peas, corn, oregano,salt,pepper and stir until blended. While onion and Gr. beef are browning steam carrots and drain. Mash carrots with milk, butter, and nutmeg. Move Gr. beef mixture to a shallow 2 qt. casserole and top with mashed carrots. Cover carrots with mashed potatoes,then sprinkle with cheese. bake in 350F. oven for 45 min.

Submitter: 
Mad Butcher
Description: 
Mashed carrots are little different in this version of sheppard's pie.
