Title:
Awesome Lemon Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2469515.jpg
Ingredients:
THE LOAF
1 1/2 cups FLOUR
1/2 teaspoon BAKING SODA
1/2 teaspoon BAKING POWDER
1/2 teaspoon SALT
3 EGGS
1 cup SUGAR
2 tablespoons BUTTER; Softened.
1 teaspoon VANILLA
1 teaspoon LEMON EXTRACT
1/3 cup LEMON JUICE
1/2 cup OIL (recommend coconut oil)
LEMON ICING
1 cup POWDERED SUGAR; Plus 1 Tablespoon.
2 tablespoons WHOLE MILK; I Used 2%.
1 teaspoon LEMON JUICE
2 teaspoons LEMON ZEST (optional)

Directions:
The Cake
Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt in a bowl.
Use a mixer to blend together the eggs, sugar, butter, vanilla, lemon extract and lemon juice in a medium bowl.
Pour wet ingredient into the dry ingredients and blend until smooth.
Add oil and mix well.
Pour batter into a well greased 9x5-inch loaf pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes or until a toothpick stuck into center of the cake comes out clean.
The Icing
Make the lemon icing by combining all the icing ingredients in a small bowl with an electric mixer on low speed.
Add 1/2 teaspoon of lemon zest, stir with spoon until mixed well into the icing.
When the loaf is cool, remove it from pan and frost the top with the icing.
Sprinkle remaining lemon zest lightly
Let the icing set before serving.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a really nice take on the traditional lemon loafs. Icing is optional because this loaf is moist and tasty without icing too.
