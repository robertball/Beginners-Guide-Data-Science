Title:
Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T oil
1 small onion diced
1/4 c grated carrot
2 cloves garlic minced
1 tsp ginger minced
1 lb ground turkey
salt and pepper
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp cumin
1/4 c soy sauce
2-3 green onions sliced
cilantro

Directions:
Heat oil, cook onion and carrots for 3 minutes until begin to soften. Add gurkey and cook. Add seasoning abouty 2 minute. Pour soy cauce adn cook 3-4 minutes until mostly absorbed.
