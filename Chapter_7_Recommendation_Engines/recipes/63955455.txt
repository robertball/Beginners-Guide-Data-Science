Title:
Oreo Cookies (from scratch) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
COOKIE
10 TBSP butter (1 cube plus 2 TBSP)
3/4 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 egg
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 1/4 cup whole wheat flour
FROSTING
1/4 cup butter
4 oz cream cheese (1/2 package)
1/2 to 1 tsp vanilla
2 cups powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Mix together butter, sugar and vanilla until creamy.
Add in egg, salt, baking powder, baking soda and cocoa powder and mix until thoroughly mixed.
Next add in flour until blended well.
Form into 1 tsp size balls. (for football shape, flatten, narrow and point the ends)Bake up to 16 on a greased cookie sheet 375 degrees for soft cookies 6-8 minutes. For crispy cookies 8-10 minutes.
Remove from cookie sheet and completely cool on a rack.
FROSTING:
Mix together all ingredients until creamy.
PUTTING IT TOGETHER:
After cookies are cooled, spread cream cheese filling on the bottom side of one of the wafers and then place another wafer similar in size on top to make a sandwich cookie. Squish together and store in an airtight container.
