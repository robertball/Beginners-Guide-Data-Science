Title:
The Best Tropical Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5446516.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large mango, peeled and chopped
1/2 cup ice
1/3 cup orange juice
1/4 cup vanilla yogurt
1/4 banana
1 teaspoon honey (optional)

Directions:
Place mango, ice, orange juice, yogurt, banana, and honey in a blender. Mix until combined. Pour into a large glass.

Submitter: 
James Knisely
Description: 
Brighten your day with this tropical smoothie that blends mango and banana with orange juice and yogurt.
