Title:
Pamela's Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons butter, melted
1/2 cup sugar or honey
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup banana, mashed
1-3/4 cups Pamela's Baking & Pancake Mix
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 cup nuts (optional)

Directions:
Beat together butter, sugar or honey, eggs and banana. Add remaining ingredients and mix together. Pour into a large greased loaf pan.
Bake in a preheated 350Â° oven for 1 hour, or until an inserted toothpick comes out clean
