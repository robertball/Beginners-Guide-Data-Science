Title:
Spinach Triangles - Fatayer Sabanegh Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg phyllo dough (thawed)
1 pkg (10-12 oz) fresh spinach leaves
1 medium onion, finely chopped
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
2 tablespoons ground sumac
2 tablespoons pinenuts (optional)
juice from 1 lemon (more or less to taste)
2 pinches of salt
1/2 teaspoon of ground pepper
melted butter for phyllo dough

Directions:
heat oven to 400 degrees celcius
chop spinach leaves and transfer to large bowl
add pinch of salt to chopped spinach
work spinach together until it wilts
in a separate bowl, add pinch of salt to chopped onions
work onions together until it wilts
transfer onions to spinach
mix in olive oil, sumac, pinenuts, pepper & lemon juice
let sit for 5 mins
drain liquid from mixture
unroll phyllo dough one sheet at a time
layer 2 sheets per batch
brush melted butter on EACH sheet
cut into 5 strips
place a tablespoon of spinach mixture on each strip
fold strip into triangles
brush completed triangles with butter
transfer to large non-stick baking sheet
bake 12-15 mins per batch or until golden brown
serve warm

Submitter: 
Lucy Mirabella Nino
Description: 
Middle Eastern recipe inherited from my mother-in-law.  These triangles won't even have time to cool down before they are gobbled up.  Even my kids love 'em!!  Enjoy!!
