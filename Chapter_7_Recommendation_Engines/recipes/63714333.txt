Title:
Matthew's Chicken Stuffed with Herbs and Mozzarella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup.  Flour
1 tsp   Kosher Salt
1/2 tsp.  Paprika
1/2 tsp.  Garlic Powder
4 (5-6 oz) Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
1 Tbs Fresh Parsley, chopped
1 Tbs. Fresh Basil, chopped
6 oz.  Mozzarella
3 Tbs EVOO

Directions:
Preheat oven 400.
Combine the flour, salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder - set aside.
Butterfly breasts and pound to flatten.
Combine parsley and basil. Salt and pepper each breast and add 1/4 of the herb mixture to each breast.
Put 1/4 of the cheese to the inside 1/2 of the breast, leaving 1/4 inch edges. Fold together and press gently.
Carefully dredge each breast in seasoned flour. Heat large oven-safe saut√© pan over medium-high heat; add oil.  Place each chicken breast presentation side down and cook 3 minutes, until golden brown.  Using tongs carefully turn breast over and brown another 2 minutes. Transfer pan to oven and cook until breasts are done (aprox 8 minutes).
