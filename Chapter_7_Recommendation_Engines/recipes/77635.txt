Title:
Teriyaki Chicken by CRISCO速 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup CRISCO速 Oil
1/3 cup soy sauce
3 tablespoons honey
1 tablespoon cider vinegar
1 large garlic clove, peeled and minced
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves trimmed and flattened to even thickness
CRISCO速 No-Stick Cooking Spray

Directions:
Combine CRISCO(R) Oil, soy sauce, honey, vinegar, garlic, ginger and pepper in heavy re-sealable plastic bag or mixing bowl. Add chicken breasts; marinate 30 minutes, or longer, if desired.
Heat broiler. Line broiler pan with heavy duty aluminum foil; spray with CRISCO(R) No Stick Cooking Spray.
Remove chicken from marinade. Place on prepared broiler rack. Pour marinade into small saucepan; bring to a boil. Boil until liquid is reduced by one-half.
Broil chicken 5 minutes per side or until chicken is no longer pink in center. Cut chicken into 1/2-inch slices.
Drizzle slices with reduced marinade. Serve immediately.

Hop on the Orient Express with this tasty dish. Marinate overnight for even more great flavor.
