Title:
Chocolate Chip Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. margarine
1 c. sugar
2 eggs
3 mashed banana's
2 c. flour
1 t. baking soda
1/4 t. salt
1/4 c. chopped nuts, optional
1/4 c. chocolate chips

Directions:
Cream margarine and sugar together.
Add eggs and beat well.
Mix dry ingredients and add to batter.
Add remaining ingredients.
Pour into a greased loaf/bread pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

A fun twist on an old classic. My favorite treat as a kid.
