Title:
Chicken Pot Pie Bisquick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups bisquick
1 1/2 c chicken broth
1/4 c butter
1 can cream of soup
1 c sour cream
1/2 c chicken broth
16 oz frozen mixed veggies
3 cups cooked chicken, chopped
2 potatoes, peeled and cubed

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine 2 cups bisquick and 1 1/2 c broth. Mix filling ingredients place in 9 X 13 pan. top with bisquick and butter pats
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (200 degrees C.)
Bake in the preheated oven for 60 minutes, or until pastry is golden brown, and filling is bubbly.
