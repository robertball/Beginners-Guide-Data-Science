Title:
Parmesan Potato Halves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 small potatoes, scrubbed and cut in half
1/4 c butter
Grated Parmesan cheese
Garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Melt butter and pour into 9x13 baking pan.
Generously sprinkle Parmesan cheese and lightly sprinkle garlic powder over butter.
Place potatoe halves face down in butter.
Bake for 40-45 min.
Cool for a full 5 minutes before removing (otherwise Parmesan crust will not stick to potato).
Serve with side of sour cream for dipping.
