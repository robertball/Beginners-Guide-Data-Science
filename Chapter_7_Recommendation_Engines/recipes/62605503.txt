Title:
Italian Cream Cheese Crock-Pot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breasts (whole or sliced)
1/2 stick butter
1 package dry Italian salad dressing mix
1 can cream of chicken soup
8 oz package cream cheese, softened

Directions:
Melt butter and pour over chicken in crock pot.
Sprinkle packet of Italian dressing over chicken.
Cook on high for four hours.
Mix cream soup and cream cheese together.
Stir into chicken.
Cook 30 minutes more.
Serve over angel hair pasta.
