Title:
Creamy Ranch Chicken Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1045829.jpg
Ingredients:
8 slices bacon
1.5 lbs skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 (1oz) package ranch dry salad dressing mix
season salt and pepper to taste
2 cups whole milk
1/4 cup butter or margerine
5 cups dried egg noodles
2 cups finely shredded Parmesan cheese
1 7oz can mushrooms chopped
2 cups frozen broccoli (cooked)
1 cup frozen spinach (cooked)
Bread crumbs (enough to top casserole lightly)

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cut bacon into chunks. In a large skillet cook bacon over medium heat until crisp. Drain bacon on paper towels; discard all but 2 tablespoons drippings.
At the same time heat milk, 1 cup of parm, butter, and mushrooms in a saucepan over medium low heat till cheese is melted.
In the same skillet cook chicken in reserved drippings until tender and no longer pink, turning to brown evenly. Season with pepper and seasoned salt. Sprinkle flour and salad dressing mix over the chicken in the skillet; stir well. Stir in milk mixture. Cook and stir until thickened and bubbly. Cook and stir 1 minute more. Stir in bacon, and vegetables. Meanwhile, cook noodles according to package directions.
Mix chicken mixture with noodles in large greased baking pan/casserole dish. top with remaining Parmesan and bread crumbs. Bake for 15 mins. Let sit for 5 mins before serving.
