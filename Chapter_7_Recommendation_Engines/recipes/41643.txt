Title:
Cranberry Streusel Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups flour
1 tablespoon CALUMET Baking Powder
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon, divided
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 egg
1/2 cup cranberry juice cocktail
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/3 cup oil
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups POST SELECTS CRANBERRY ALMOND CRUNCH Cereal, divided
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1 tablespoon oil

Directions:
Mix flour, baking powder, 1 tsp. of the cinnamon and salt in large bowl.  Beat egg in small bowl; stir in juice, 1/2 cup sugar, 1/3 cup oil and vanilla.  Add to flour mixture; stir just until moistened.  (Batter will be lumpy.)  Stir in 1-1/2 cups of the cereal.  Spoon batter into greased muffin pan, filling each cup 2/3 full.
Mix remaining 1/2 cup cereal, 2 Tbsp. sugar and remaining 1/2 tsp. cinnamon in small bowl.  Drizzle with 1 Tbsp. oil;  stir until crumbly.  Sprinkle evenly over muffins.
Bake at 375 degrees F for 15 to 18 minutes or until golden brown.  Serve warm.
