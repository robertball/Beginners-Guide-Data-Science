Title:
Chef Jeff's Seasoned Butter (2012) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/706987.jpg
Ingredients:
2 sticks salted butter (softened)
2 TBL fresh chopped Italian Parsley (Flat Leaf)
1 TBL dried chives
1 TBL fresh chopped garlic
2 tsp Lawrey's Seasoned Salt
1 tsp white pepper
2 TBL Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2 tsp onion powder
1 TBL fresh squeezed lemon juice

Directions:
First, place butter in a medium size bowl to soften.  Next, place all ingredients into bowl and mix thourghly.  Using vinyl food prep gloves is better for incorporating all ingredients by hand.

Submitter: 
Jeffrey Morris
Description: 
This versitile butter spread is great on finishing grilled chicken, baked fish, toasting French Bread, toasting Italian Bagette or just some good multi grain bread.  It is easy and lasts for a month in the fridge.
