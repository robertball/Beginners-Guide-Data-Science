Title:
Turkey and Bean Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989725.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
1 (16 ounce) can ORTEGA® Refried Beans
1 (7 ounce) can ORTEGA® Diced Green Chiles
1 (1.25 ounce) package ORTEGA® Taco Seasoning mix
1 package (12) ORTEGA® Taco Shells, warmed
Toppings: shredded lettuce, SARGENTO® ChefStyle Shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese, sour cream, ORTEGA® Thick & Smooth Taco Sauce

Directions:
BROWN turkey until no longer pink; drain.  Stir in beans, chiles and seasoning mix.
FILL taco shells with turkey mixture.  Top with lettuce, cheese, sour cream and taco sauce.

Submitter: 
Ortega®
Description: 
Ground turkey is a great substitution for ground beef in tacos. Here, we've added beans and Ortega ® Diced Green Chiles for some additional contrast and flavor.
