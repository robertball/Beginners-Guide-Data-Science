Title:
Strawberry Greek Yogurt Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3190610.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups GF Oat Flour
1 cup nonfat greek yogurt
2 egg whites
1/2 cup baking stevia
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
2 cups fresh, roughly chopped strawberries
1 tsp lemon juice

Directions:
Preheat 400 degrees F
Prepare muffin tin with liners or spray with nonstick cooking spray
Place all ingredients in food processor except strawberries & blend until oats are smooth
Pour into a bowl and gently fold in strawberries
Add batter to muffin tin
Bake 25 minutes
Remove from oven & allow to cool in pan until removing to cool on a rack
Top with Reddi-whip for a yummy snack!

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Bursting with strawberries and creamy greek yogurt, these Strawberry Greek Yogurt Muffins make a delicious low fat snack for just 95 calories.
