Title:
Easy White Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cans of 12 oz Northern Beans
1 Jar(12 oz) of your favorite salsa
2 Large Chicken Breasts
2 Cloves garlic
1 tablespoon of each, Chives, Oragino, Thyme, Bay Leaves(or 1 whole), basil, and salt and pepper.
1 Can chopped tomato
Olive Oil
1 Cup sour cream
Toppings: Cheddar Cheese, Tortilla Chips, Sour Cream, and if you like fresh chopped onion.

Directions:
1. Dice Chicken into bite size chunks, and cook with the 2 cloves of garlic, all spices and olive oil till done.
2. Add in 4 Cans of the Northern Beans and 1 can of Water, the jar of salsa, and the chopped tomato.
3. Let sit for 1 hour on low heat, add in two other cans of beans and 1 cup sour cream.
4. Let bring heat up to med-lo and let sit for 25 minutes.
5. Scoop into a bowl and top with Onion and Chips, then Cheese and Sour Cream. The cheese will melt and ENJOY!

Submitter: 
lelarsen5322
Description: 
This is a quick and easy way to make a great standard white chicken chili. Kids and Adults love it, topped with chips, sour cream and cheese.
