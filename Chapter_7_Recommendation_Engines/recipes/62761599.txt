Title:
Curried Chicken of the Caribbean Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/729652.jpg
Ingredients:
1 chicken,cut into pieces
1 chopped onion
1 sprig thyme
1 green onion,chopped
1/4 tsp.ginger
1 tsp.poultry seasoning
dash seasoning salt
6 tsp.curry powder
1 tsp.poultry seasoning
3 Tbsp.oil
1/4 tsp.cumin
2 cloves garlic,minced
2 chicken bouillon cubes,dissolved in 1 cup water or
1 can chicken broth

Directions:
Wash chicken pieces and season with onions,thyme,green onions,ginger,poultry seasoning,seasoning salt and 2 tsp.of curry powder.
ALLOW TO MARINATE FOR 45-60 MIN.
In sauce pan add oil,cumin,garlic and remainder of the curry powder.Heat till the curry starts to froth. Add marinated chicken and saute for 5-10 min.Add the cup of stock,reduce heat,simmer untill chicken is tender[additional broth may be added.]
For a thicker sauce 2-3 pared and cubed potatoes can be added.
