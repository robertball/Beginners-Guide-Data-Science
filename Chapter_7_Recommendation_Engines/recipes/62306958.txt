Title:
GARLIC CHICKEN WITH CREAMY TOMATO PASTA Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups uncooked rotini pasta
1 tbsp. olive oil
l lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
1 shallot, sliced
1/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
3 cloves garlic minced
1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
1/4 cup heavy whipping cream (I used 5% light cream)
1 tbsp. dried Italian herb seasoning
2 cups packed fresh baby spinach
Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions; drain well, set aside and keep warm.
In a large Dutch oven heat oil over medium-high heat.  Add chicken, shallot, bell pepper, and garlic; saute for 6 minutes or until chicken is done.  Add tomatoes, cream, and Italian herb seasoning; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, and stir in pasta and spinach.  Serve immediately with parmessan cheese if desired.
