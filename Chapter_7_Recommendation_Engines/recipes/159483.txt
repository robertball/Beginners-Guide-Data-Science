Title:
Turkey Parmesan Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975820.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces spaghetti, broken in half, uncooked
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
3/4 cup BREAKSTONE'S or KNUDSEN Sour Cream
1/4 cup milk
1/3 cup KRAFT 100% Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
3 cups frozen broccoli florets, thawed
2 cups chopped cooked turkey

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Cook spaghetti as directed on package; drain.
Mix soup, sour cream, milk, Parmesan cheese and pepper in large bowl. Add spaghetti, broccoli and turkey; mix lightly. Spoon into 2-quart casserole.
Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
BREAKSTONE'S
Description: 
For a quick weeknight dinner serve this creamy turkey noodle casserole with a crisp, mixed green salad.
