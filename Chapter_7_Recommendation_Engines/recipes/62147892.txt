Title:
Grilled Cheese and Ham sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices white bread
2 slices pepper jack cheese
2 slices ham

Directions:
Preheat skillet over medium heat.
Generously butter one side of all four slices of bread.
Place 2 slices of bread butter-side-down onto skillet.
Add 1 slice of pepper jack cheese and one slice of ham to each each slice.
Place the other 2 slices of bread butter-side-up on top of sandwich.
Grill until lightly browned and flip over; continue grilling until cheese is melted and both sides are lightly browned.
