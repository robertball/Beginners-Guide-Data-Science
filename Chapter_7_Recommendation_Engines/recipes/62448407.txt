Title:
Chocolate Sheet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups white sugar
2 cups flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 cup margarine
1 cup water
4 Tbsp. cocoa
2 beaten eggs
½ cup buttermilk (milk with 1 Tbsp. vinegar)
1 tsp. vanilla
Frosting:
½ cup margarine
4 Tbsp. cocoa
6 Tbsp (1/3 cup) buttermilk
1 lb. icing sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Combine first three ingredients well.
Bring margarine, water and cocoa to a boil (microwave 2 minutes, stir, microwave 2 min high again)
Pour boiled mixture over dry ingredients. Add remaining ingredients and mix well.
Pour onto cookie sheet (wax paper on bottom) and bake at 375 F for 20 minutes.
Bring margarine, cocoa and buttermilk to a boil (45 seconds), then remove from heat. Add icing sugar, vanilla and walnuts (optional).
Ice with frosting.
