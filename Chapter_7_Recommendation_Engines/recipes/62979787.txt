Title:
Kristi"s Jerky Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups worchestershire
1/4 cup soy sauce
1 tsp liquid smoke
1/3 cup lemon juice
1/2 cup teriyaki sauce
1 tsp hot sauce
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1 tbsp black pepper
1/2 tsp paprika
1 lb venision or your choice of meat,cut into strips

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, excluding the venison.
Stir well.
Place venison in a sealable container and pour mixture on top.
Seal container and shake.
Place in the refrigerator for 24 hrs.(shake often)
Remove from refrigerator, and place on dehydrator racks.
Cook as instructed.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
For those who like a little kick and alot of flavor.
