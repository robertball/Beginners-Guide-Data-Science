Title:
Cheryl's lemony cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened (no substitutions)
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 TBLSP lemon extract
2-3 drops of lemon juice
1 1/3 cups all purpose flour
3/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven at 350 degrees. Put butter and sugar in an electric mixer and beat til creamy on medium speed. Add egg, lemon extract and lemon juice and continue to beat til creamy. Add flour, salt and baking soda and beat til combined. On an ungreased cookie sheet put teaspoonful and 2 inches apart. Cookies will spread so be sure you place the dough 2 inches apart. Bake for 8-10 min or til sides are golden brown.

Submitter: 
Cheryl's Cooking Now!
Description: 
These are very basic to the vanilla cookie recipe. I substituted vanilla extract for the lemon and added 3 drops of lemon juice.
