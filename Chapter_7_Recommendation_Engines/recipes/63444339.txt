Title:
Crystal's crockpot chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. boneless skinless chicken cut into small cubes
5 medium carrots sliced
1 potato diced
1 clove garlic minced
1 medium onion chopped
1/2 cup water
2 TBS. butter
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 bay leaf
1 (1.2 oz) packet roasted chicken gravy mix
1/3 cup buttermilk, plus extra to taste
2 tsp. flour
2 cups frozen peas thawed and drained
3 or more cups cooked rice
1 can cream of chicken soup

Directions:
Cook first 10 things in crockpot. Cook 8- 10 hours on low. 20 minutes before serving, stir in gravy mix, and remove bay leaf.  Blend buttermilk and flour. Add peas and mix well. Add soup and more buttermilk if needed. Set on high for 15 to 20 minutes or until veggies are cooked.

Submitter: 
iceemama
Description: 
This is sort of like the filling for chicken pot pie but served over rice. The buttermilk gives it a nice tangy flavor.
