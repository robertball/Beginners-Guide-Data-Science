Title:
No Bake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1/3 c. cocoa
1/2 c. coconut
1/2 c. margarine
1/2 c. milk
3 c. Quick Oats
3/4 c. peanut butter (optional)

Directions:
Cook sugar, margarine, salt, milk and cocoa to a boil; boil 1 minute.  Put oats and coconut in large bowl.  Pour the hot mixture quickly over the oats and coconut, then add peanut butter (optional).  beat until mix is good and smooth. Spoon on buttered cookie sheet or waxed paper. Coconut may be omitted.

Submitter: 
Miriam
Description: 
Chocolate, oatmeal cookies that you don't bake.
