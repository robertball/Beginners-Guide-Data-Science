Title:
Beef Stroganoff - Low carb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz ground beef
12 oz broccoli
1 tsp garlic
2 to 3 oz chopped onions
2 oz mushrooms (optional)
1/4 cup red wine
1 tsp beef better than bullion
3/4 cup water
1/4 cup (4 Tbsp) sour cream
2 tsp dijon mustard

Directions:
heat 1/4 water, mix in 1 tsp bullion, 2 tsp mustard, 1/2 cup water, 1/4 wine.
heat oiled pan
brown garlic
brown beef, drain oil
saute mushrooms and onion
add liquid mixture
boil for a while to thicken then add sour cream
serve over cooked broccoli.
