Title:
Carrot soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp butter
1 med onion, chopped
6-7 carrots, thinly sliced
salt and pepper
1/2 tsp basil
1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
3 tbsp flour
5 cups chicken stock

Directions:
Heat butter in large saucepan over med heat. Add onion. Cover and cook 5 min on low heat.
Add carrots, basil and parsley. Season well. Mix and cook 6 minutes, partly covered over medium heat.
Mix in flour until well incorporated. Pour in chick stock, correct seasoning, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and cook 20 minutes.
Pur√©e in blender.
