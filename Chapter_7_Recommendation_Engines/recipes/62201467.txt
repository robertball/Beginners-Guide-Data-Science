Title:
Simple Waldorf Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup real mayonnaise
1 1/2 tablespoons distilled white vinegar
1 lg. apple - peeled, cored, and chopped
1 large stalk celery, diced
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1 T. sugar
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise and vinegar.  Add sugar and whisk again.
Add the apples, celery, raisins and walnuts if desired. Add salt and pepper to taste.
Mix together well and serve chilled.

Description: 
Who doesn 't love this terrific salad. Apples, celery, raisins and walnuts are all folded into a mayonnaise/vinegar dressing and then chilled. Real comfort food.  Double this recipe for a family.Modified from: Simple Waldorf Salad
