Title:
90 min. Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C water, warm
1 T Yeast
1 t. Salt
1 T veg. Oil
2 T sugar
1 egg, beaten
2 1/2-3 C flour (enough to make soft dough)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°
Combine water, yeast, and sugar
Let rise.
In another bowl, combine salt, oil, end egg
When yeast is soft and risen, add to egg mixture
Add Flour to make soft dough
Knead on floured surface until smooth and elastic (~10min)
Form into loaf and put in greased loaf pan.
Cover and let rise until 2x size
Bake at 350° for 20 min. or until golden brown, sounds hollow when tapped.
