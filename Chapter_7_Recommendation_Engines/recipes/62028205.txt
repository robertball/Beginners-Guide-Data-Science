Title:
Sheppard's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-Red Potatoes (or whatever you like)
1 lb. ground beef
1-14.5 oz. can of cream corn
1-14.5 oz. can of whole kernel corn(drained)
1/4 cup of milk
1/4 cup of butter or margarine
2 T. finely chopped onion
1 tsp. kosher salt
1 egg beaten
pinch of black pepper

Directions:
Brown ground beef and drain.
Turn drained beef into a 2L (2 qt.) baking dish to form bottom layer of pie.  Add cream style corn and then whole kernal corn to form second layer.
Peal, boil, drain and mash potatoes while still warm.  Add milk, butter/margarine, onion, salt and pepper.  Beat until potatoes are light and fluffy.  Turn potatoes into baking dish to form the top layer.
Bake in oven at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until potatoes are slightly browned.
