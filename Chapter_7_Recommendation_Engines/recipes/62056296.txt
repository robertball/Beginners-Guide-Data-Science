Title:
Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
olive oil:
1 tsp. minced garlic
1 1/4 cup chopped onion
2 celery stalks, chopped finely
1 carrot, grated finely
1/2 cup bell pepper, chopped
2  3 jalapenos, minced
1 tsp. ground cumin
2 tbs. chili powder
1 tbs. unsweetened cocoa powder
2 tbs. brown sugar
a small can of tomato paste
1 pound of browned ground beef that has been drained.
1 large can of skinless plum tomatoes (cut the tomatoes into small pieces and include the liquid)
1/2 can beer (up to a whole can if you need to thin it)
1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained
1 large basil leaf (chopped)
cooked rice

Directions:
In a pot, brown garlic, onion, celery, carrot, bell pepper, and jalpenos in olive oil:
Once all the ingredients are tender/cooked, add cumin, chili powder, cocoa powder, and brown sugar until well coated.
Then add the tomato paste and mix until well coated.
Mix in browned ground beef.
To this, add the tomotoes, beer, kidney beans, basil, and parsley.
Let simmer for 20 minutes.
Serve over rice.
