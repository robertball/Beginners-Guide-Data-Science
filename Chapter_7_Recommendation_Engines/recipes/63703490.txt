Title:
Peach Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1029283.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups sliced, peeled peaches (about 1 1/2 pounds)
1 cup half-and-half
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup whole milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Place the peaches in a blender or food processor; process until finely chopped. Combine peaches and remaining ingredients in a large bowl. Pour peach mixture into the freezer can of an ice-cream freezer.
Freeze according to manufactuere's instructions.
Spoon ice cream into a freezer-safe container. Cover and freeze for 2 hours or until firm.

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
Ripe peaches at peak season are key to this creamy, refreshing ice cream. It's best eaten the same day as it is prepared.
