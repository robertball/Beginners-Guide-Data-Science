Title:
Greek Souvlaki Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068050.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1/2 clove garlic, minced
1/4 teaspoon dry mustard powder
1 pinch salt
1 pinch pepper
2 teaspoons oregano
2 teaspoons basil
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Whisk together vinegar, garlic, mustard powder, salt, pepper, oregano, basil, and olive oil together until blended.
Pour into a glass dressing container, and allow to stand at least 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
This typical Greek dressing is found in any place that serves souvlaki, it will bring out the flavor of your souvlaki!Modified from: Greek Souvlaki Dressing
