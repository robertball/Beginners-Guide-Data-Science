Title:
Chicken and Papaya - Yamkia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c coconut cream
7 oz chicken
1 c jasmine rice
1 1/3 c coconut milk
2 garlic
3 shallots
3 small red chilies
1 t dried shrimp
2 T fish sauce
8 cherry tomatoes
5 oz green papaya, grated
2 T lime juice
1 1/2 c mint leaves
2/3 c cilantro

Directions:
Simmer chicken in coconut cream for 5 min, let cool for 20 in liquid.
Cook Jasmine rice in coconut milk.
Pound garlic, shallots, red chilies, shrimp, and fish sauce. Add tomatoes to form a paste.
Finley chop herbs.
Mix all and serve.

Submitter: 
Zhora Autumn
Description: 
A very Thai dish with green papaya and coconut!
