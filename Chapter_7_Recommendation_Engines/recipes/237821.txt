Title:
Quick Strawberry Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3221959.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
1 1/2 tablespoons strawberry jam, or to taste

Directions:
Whisk oil, vinegar, and strawberry jam together in a bowl until smooth.

Submitter: 
Ms. Robinson
Description: 
I whipped this up on the fly to toss with a salad of field greens and sliced peaches. Easy, light, and fruity. One batch makes enough for one big salad bowl. This vinaigrette can also be blended in a food processor.
