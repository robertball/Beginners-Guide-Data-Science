Title:
Diana's Famous Sticky Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped Pecan's
1 (12 ounce) package frozen dinner roll dough
1 (5 ounce) package cook n serve butterscotch pudding mix
1 cup brown sugar
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Sprinkle nuts in the bottom of a greased Bundt pan. Mix dry ingredients. Sprinkle a little on nuts (can add raisin's) then cut up dough into pieces and dredge them in dry mixture (try a zip lock bag for this) then lay them on top of nuts in Bundt pan.  Pour rest of dry mixture on top of dough. Pour the melted butter over all.  Cover with aluminum foil and put in refrigerator over night.
In the morning, remove foil and place pan in cold oven. Heat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 40 minutes.  Remove from oven and let sit for 5 minutes, then flip out onto plate and pull apart, or cut.
