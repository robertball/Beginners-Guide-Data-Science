Title:
Spicy Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/380828.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 - 1 cup cup mayonnaise - do not want to be dry
1 squirt Goldens Spicy Brown mustard
1 squirt Horseradish or coarse ground Dijon mustard
1 tablespoon lemon juice
¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
2 ½ cups chopped, rotisserie or smoked chicken
1/2 - 1 stalk celery, chopped

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix mayonnaise, lemon juice, and seasonings. Use your judgement on the mayo. Also use your favorite mustard's. Add chicken and celery.  Serve on warm bread or a bed of lettuce or fresh spinach and pepperoncini's.  Chill for a good 1/2 hour in refrigerator.  So good!

Submitter: 
Cajun Girl
Description: 
This is a nice summer dish. Add some SLAP YA MAMA seasoning or your favorite seasoning and make this chicken salad come alive with flavor!  Easy and satisfying.Modified from: Basic Chicken Salad
