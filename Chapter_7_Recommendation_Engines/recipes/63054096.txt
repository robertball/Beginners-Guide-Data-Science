Title:
Scalloped Apples, Sweet Potatoes and Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups sliced apples
4 small sweet potatoes, peeled and cooked
1/3 cup seedless raisins
1/2 cup light brown sugar, firmly packed
dash of nutmeg
3 tbsps. currant jelly
3 tbsps. prepared mustard
4 lean pork chops 3/4 inch thick trimmed of fat

Directions:
Divide apples into two equal batches. Place apples on each end of a lightly greased casserole dish. Cut sweet potatoes into slices and place in center of baking dish. Sprinkle with a mixture of the raisins, brown sugar and nutmeg. Mix together jelly and mustard until smooth. Place pork chops on top of potatoes and apples.
Top with mustard mixture. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Bake at 350*F for 1 1/2 hours or until the chops are done.
