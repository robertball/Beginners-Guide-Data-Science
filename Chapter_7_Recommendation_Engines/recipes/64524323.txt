Title:
Cinnamon Thumbprint Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
5 tablespoons sugar
2 cups flour
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup sugar
2 teaspoons cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
1.  Cream butter and sugar.
2.  Mix in vanilla.
3.  Mix in flour until a nice dough forms.
4.  Roll into balls place on cookie sheet and make an indentation in each dough ball with your thumb.
5.  Bake for 20 minutes.
6.  While warm, roll in cinnamon/sugar mixture.
