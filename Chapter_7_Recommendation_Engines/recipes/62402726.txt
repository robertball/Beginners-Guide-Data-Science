Title:
White & Wild Rice Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Rice Herb Mix:
1 T onion powder
1 Tbsp. garlic powder
1 Tbsp. dried parsley flakes
1 tsp. dried basil leaves
1 tsp. dried thyme leaves
1 tsp. dried marjoram leaves
1 tsp. white pepper
Making the Rice:
1 1/2 t olive oil
1/2 onion, diced
1 carrot, diced
1 stalk celery, diced
1 clove garlic
1 3/4 cup chicken stock
1/8 cup wild rice
1/8 cup brown rice
1/2 cup white rice
1 t rice seasoning mix

Directions:
1. Heat oil in large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add onion, carrots, celery and garlic and cook until tender. Stir in broth, wild rice, brown rice and seasoning mix. Heat to a boil. Cover and cook over low heat 25 minutes.
2. Stir in white rice. Cover and cook over low heat 20 minutes or until rice is done. Stir in parsley
