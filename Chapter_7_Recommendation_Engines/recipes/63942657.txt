Title:
MICROWAVE PECAN PIE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (9") pastry shell, unbaked
1 large egg yolk, beaten
2 Tbsp. dark corn syrup
1/4 cup butter, melted
3 large eggs + 1 egg white
1 cup dark corn syrup
1/3 cup brown sugar, packed
1 Tbsp. all-purpose flour
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 cups toasted pecan halves

Directions:
Fit the pastry into a microwave safe pie plate. In a a small bowl, combine the egg yolk and corn syrup. Brush the inside of the shell with the mixture. Microwave on HIGH for 6-7 minutes, rotating 1/2 turn after 3 minutes, if needed. Set aside.
In a large glass bowl, to the melted butter, whisk in the eggs and egg white until well blended. Mix in the corn syrup, brown sugar, flour and vanilla. Fold in the toasted pecans. Pour into the prepared crust.
Microwave on MEDIUM 12-15 minutes, rotating 1/2 turn every 5 minutes, if needed. The pie is done when the surface appears dry and puffed and the filling is set. Cool completely on a wire rack.
