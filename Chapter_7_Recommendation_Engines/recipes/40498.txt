Title:
Florentine Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981306.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package STOUFFER'S® frozen Creamed Spinach, defrosted
1 (9 inch) pie shell, baked
4 eggs, lightly beaten
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
6 slices bacon, cooked until crisp, drained and crumbled
2/3 cup shredded Swiss cheese
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Combine eggs and flour until smooth in medium bowl. Add Creamed Spinach, bacon, Swiss cheese, milk, onion, nutmeg and pepper; stir well to combine. Pour into pie crust.
Bake for 35 to 40 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Makes 6 to 8 servings

Quiche that is perfect for a special breakfast gathering.
