Title:
Honey-Oat Granola Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. quick-cooking oats
1 c. packed brown sugar
1 c. chopped salted peanuts
1 c. semisweet chocolate chips
1/2 c. sunflower kernels
3/4 c. butter, melted
2/3 c. honey
1 tsp. vanilla extract

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the oats, brown sugar, peanuts, chocolate chips and sunflower kernels.  Stir in butter, honey and vanilla until combined (mixture will be crumbly.)  Press into greased parchment paper lined 15"x10" baking pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes or until browned and bubbly.  Cool for 15 minutes on a wire rack; cut into squares.  Cool completely before removing from pan.
