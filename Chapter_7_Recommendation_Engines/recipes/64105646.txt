Title:
Gluten Free Cake Flour Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 cups white rice flour (675 grams)
1 1/2 cups sweet (glutinous) rice flour (200 grams)
2 cups potato starch (not potato flour) (320 grams)
1 cup tapioca starch (185 grams)
4 Tbsp rice starch

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients thoroughly. Sift four times.
