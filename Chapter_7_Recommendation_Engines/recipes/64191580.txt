Title:
Mom's Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups cabbage, shredded
1 large carrot, shredded
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup sugar
1/8 cup cider vinegar
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon celery seed

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the mayonnaise, sugar and vinegar, pepper, and celery seed. Add cabbage/carrots and combine well. Refrigerate until ready to serve stirring occasionally. Best when made at least an hour before serving.

Submitter: 
kcryss
Description: 
You won't have to fuss with a lot of seasonings to fix this tangy coleslaw from Denise Augostine. 'This speedy salad is an old family favorite,' remarks the Saxonburg, Pennsylvania cook. 'We've shared the recipe with many friends over the years.'Modified from: Mom's Coleslaw
