Title:
Fiesta Lime Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1/3 cup teriyaki sauce
1/2 lime, juiced
3 cloves minced garlic
1 tsp. liquid smoke
1/4 tsp ginger
1/2 tsp. salt
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup sour cream
2 tbsps. chunky salsa
1 tbsp. milk
1 tsp. cajun blackening spice
1/4 tsp. dried parsley
1/4 tsp. hot sauce
1/8 tsp. dill
1/8 tsp. cumin
1 cup shredded colby jack cheese
2 cups corn chips crumbled

Directions:
Whisk together first 7 ingredients. coat chicken breasts in mixture and marinate for at least 2 hours. Mix together next 9 ingredients and set aside covered in refrigerator. Grill the chicken until cooked through. Discard marinade. Brush cooked chicken with reserved dressing and place in casserole dish. Sprinkle with cheese and broil intil cheese has melted. Serve the chicken on a bed of crumbled chips.
