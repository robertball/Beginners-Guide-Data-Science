Title:
Cranberry Salad Mold Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6oz. pkg cherry jello
2 cups boiling water
6 Tbsp. sugar
1 16oz. can whole cranberries
1 pt sour cream
2/3 cup chopped walnuts
2/3 cup finely chopped celery

Directions:
Dissolve jello and sugar in boiling water. Place in refrigerator and chill until well thickened.
When thickened, fold in cranberries, sour cream, walnuts and celery, mixing well.
Pour into lightly greased mold and chill until firm.

Submitter: 
Nurse Ellen so not a
Description: 
My Mom makes this for the holiday's. Incorporates cherry jello with tangy cranberries and walnuts. It's very light and will be sure to please all!
