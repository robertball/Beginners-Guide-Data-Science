Title:
Fantastic Black Bean Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 pound ground turkey
2 (15 ounce) cans black beans, undrained
1 (15 ounce) can refried black beans
1 (14.5 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
1 1/2 tablespoons chili powder
1 tablespoon dried oregano
1 tablespoon dried basil leaves
1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
1/8 tsp red pepper flakes

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large heavy pot over medium heat; cook onion and garlic until onions are translucent. Add turkey and cook, stirring, until meat is brown.  Stir in beans, tomatoes, chili powder, oregano, basil and vinegar.  Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer 60 minutes or more, until flavors are well blended.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
This hearty black bean chili has ground turkey, is flavored with chili powder, oregano and basil, and perked up with a spoonful of red wine vinegar.Modified from: Fantastic Black Bean Chili
