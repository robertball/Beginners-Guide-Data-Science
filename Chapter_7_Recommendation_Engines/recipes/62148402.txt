Title:
Southern Butter Role Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One large pie crust.
two cups of real butter.
two cups of sugar.
one ts of vanilla.
1/4 ts of nutmeg or ciniammon
three cups of heavy cream.

Directions:
Roll out pie crust on to floured surface , very thin.
cut two sticks of butter in the middle of the pastry and add sugar and spices and ts of vinilla then pour heavy cream over contents in the middle and over fold crust and pour remaining cream over crust and bake on 400 degrees until dark golden brown on top and serve hot!

Submitter: 
Great Chef
Description: 
Flakey crust soaking in hot warm cream.
