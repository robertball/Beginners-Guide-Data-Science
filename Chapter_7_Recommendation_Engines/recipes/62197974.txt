Title:
Ultimate Turkey Brine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 gallons water, divided
1 1/2 cups canning or kosher salt
3 tablespoons minced garlic
1 tablespoon ground black pepper
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup brown sugar

Directions:
In a large saucepan, bring 4 C. water and remaining ingredients to a boil.  Simmer until salt is dissolved.  Cool completely.  Place turkey in large bucket or heavy duty trash bag.  Pour in cooled brine, and remaining water.  Twist bag closed, and place in cooler.  Top with 2 bags of ice, and let bring overnight.  If using a bucket, place in refrigerator.  Rinse well inside and out, and pat turkey dry before smoking or roasting.
Note:  Any of your favorite herbs can be added, as well as sliced oranges/lemons, etc.

Submitter: 
Shannon Ellis Timmons
Description: 
Best brine for poultry.  Makes tender, moist white meat, and can be used for roasting and smoking.Modified from: Out of this World Turkey Brine
