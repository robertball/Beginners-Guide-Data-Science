Title:
al fresco Easy Italian Scramble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/885904.jpg
Ingredients:
1 12oz package al freso chicken Italian sausage
1 Tablespoon olive oil
1/2 a small onion, diced
8 Eggs
1/4 cup milk
salt and pepper to taste
2 Tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Slice the al fresco Italian sausage into rings.
In a non stick skillet heat olive oil over medium heat add sausage and brown one side.  Stir in diced onions and cook until and sausage is brown and the onions are tender.
Beat the eggs with the milk, salt, pepper.
Add the eggs to the skillet with the sausage and onions.  Stir and gently cook until the eggs have set.
Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and serve.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
A fast and easy breakfast or week night dinner. Serve with a side of fresh fruit or a green salad for a complete meal in less than 30 minutes.
