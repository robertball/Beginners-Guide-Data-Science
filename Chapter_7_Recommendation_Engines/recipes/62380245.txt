Title:
Velvet Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 C. bananas, mashed
4 eggs
1/2 C. butter, softened
1 1/2 C. sugar
4 C. flour
2 t. baking soda
1 t. salt
1 C. nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°
Put bananas, eggs, sugar, and butter in blender. Blend until smooth
Pour into MB and add dry ingredients. Fold in nuts.
Pour into well greased loaf pans.
Bake at 350° for 45 min.
