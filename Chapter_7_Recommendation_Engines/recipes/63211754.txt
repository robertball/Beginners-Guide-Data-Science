Title:
Chef Jeff's Spicy Fish Taco Sauce (2012) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/832534.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Mayonaisse
1 TBL Sriracha Sauce
1 TBL Sriracha Chile Garlic Sauce
1 TBL Soy Sauce
1 TBL fresh LEMON Juice

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.  Will hold in fridge for a month.

Submitter: 
Jeffrey Morris
Description: 
This spicy and creamy sauce will compliment any seafood taco. Its easy and absolutely delicious. Similiar to Spicy Sushi Sauce.
