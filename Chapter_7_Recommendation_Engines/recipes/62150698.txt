Title:
Creamed Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
3 carrots, peeled and sliced
1 cup frozen peas
3 Tbsp. butter
3 Tbsp. flour
2 cups milk (or more)
salt
pepper

Directions:
Place potatoes and carrots in large pot and fill with water until veggies are just covered. Bring to a boil over high heat, and continue to boil until veggies are fork tender. Drain.
Place pot back on burner on medium heat. Melt butter. Add flour and cook, stirring constantly, until golden brown. Add milk all at once. Stir. Continue to stir and cook until sauce has thickened. Add salt and pepper to taste.
Stir in carrots and potatoes. Stir in frozen peas and heat until peas are warmed through.

Submitter: 
prettykitten
Description: 
A summer staple in our house. Potatoes, carrots and peas in a light cream sauce with plenty of black pepper. Great served alone for a light supper, or with grilled chicken.
