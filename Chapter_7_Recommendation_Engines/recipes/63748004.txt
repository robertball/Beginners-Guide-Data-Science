Title:
Chicken Tetrazzini (Cotton Country) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion
3/4 cup bell pepper, chopped
1 cup celery (Laura doesn't include)
1/2 cup butter
1 tablespoon flour
1 cup sharp cheese, grated
1 can cream of mushroom soup
2 cans chicken broth or milk
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon celery salt
2-3 cups chicken
1 4-ounce can mushrooms
1/2 cup slivered almonds, toasted
1 2-ounce jar pimento, chopped
1 small package spaghetti, cooked

Directions:
Saute onion, bell pepper and celery in butter.  Add flour, cheese and soup.  Stir to blend well.  Add broth or milk.  Blend till smooth.  Stir in remainder of ingredients.  Pouor into large casserole or flat baking dish.  Sprinkle top with cracker crumbs (probably Ritz).  Bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes.  Serves 8.
