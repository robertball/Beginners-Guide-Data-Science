Title:
CARROT CAKE CHEESECAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CHEESECAKE
3-8oz pkgs cream cheese, at room temperature
1 cup granulated sugar
4 eggs
1 cup sour cream
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
3 Tbs all-purpose flour
CARROT CAKE
3/4 cup packed dark brown sugar
2/3 cup canola oil
1 egg
1 cup all purpose flour
3/4 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup grated carrot
1/3 cup chopped walnuts
FROSTING
4 oz cream cheese
2 cups confectioners sugar
1 tsp almond extract

Directions:
Position racks in center and lower third of oven.
Preheat to 350 degrees.
Place roasting pan on lower rack, fill halfway with water.
Coat 9"springform pan with cooking spray.
CHEESECAKE:
At high speed, beat cream cheese until fluffy.  Reduce speed to medium-low.
Add sugar.  Beat 2 minutes.
Beat in eggs, one at a time.  Beat 3 minutes.
Beat in sour cream and vanilla, then flour, until smooth.
CARROT CAKE
In another bowl, at medium speed, beat sugar and oil until combined.
Add egg.  Beat 2 minutes.
Reduce speed to low.
Add flour, cinnamon, baking powder & salt.
Beat until combined.
Stir in carrots and walnuts.
Spread carrot cake batter in pan.
Top with cheesecake batter.
Bake on rack above pan of water, 1 hour 10 minutes, covering with foil if browning too quickly.
Cool 10 minutes.
Run knife around edge to loosen.
Cool in pan on rack.
Refrigerate at least 8 hours.
Remove side from pan.
Mix frosting ingredients, until well blended & frost.  Add food coloring, if desired.
Optional: Top with coconut(toss in food coloring, if desired) &/or chopped nuts.
