Title:
Pork  Loin Cutlets with Bell Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5012619.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium sized red bell pepper
1 medium sized yellow bell pepper
1 cup button mushrooms
1 garlic clove
4 pork loin cutlets( boneless, like you get when you slice up a pork loin)
1 tbsp. tomatoe paste
olive oil
freshly ground black pepper
salt.

Directions:
Clean the peppers, remove the core, seeds and membrane and cut into rings. Clean the mushrooms and slice. Put a little oil in your frying pan and  on medium high heat the garlic without browning. Remove the garlic and use the same oil to fry the cutlets until golden brown, season with salt and pepper.remove the cutlets from the heat and keep warm. Dilute the tomatoe paste in a little water and add to the pan then add the peppers, mushrooms and then add the cutlets back in to the pan. Lower the heat to medium low  and cover the pan and cook for 1/2 hour. serve hot over rice or your favorite pasta. *SEE NOTES*
You can use pork tenderloin and cut it into slices or cubes.
