Title:
Pudding Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/609925.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1 cup butter, melted
1 cup nuts, chopped
1 cup powdered sugar
1 8-oz pkg cream cheese, softened
1 16 oz tub of cool whip, thawed and divided
1 small pkg vanilla pudding
1 small pkg chocolate pudding
2 cups milk
shaved chocolate (optional)
chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*. In 9x13 pan, combine flour, chopped nuts and melted butter. Mix and press into bottom of pan. Bake for 20 minutes. Cool completely.
Beat together cream cheese, powdered sugar and 1 cup of cool whip. Spread over cooled crust.
Beat together chocolate pudding, vanilla pudding and milk. Spread over cream cheese mixture.
Top with remaining cool whip. Sprinkle with shaved chocolate and/or chopped nuts, if desired. Chill until ready to serve.
