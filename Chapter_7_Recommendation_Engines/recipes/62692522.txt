Title:
Easy Deviled Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/8 cup butter
3/4 cup mayonnaise
3 tablespoons prepared dijon mustard
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
salt and pepper to taste
one sleeve crushed Club crackers combined with:
Adobe seasoning to taste
sweet ginger and garlic seasoning (optional)
use whatever seasonings you like

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Melt butter/margarine and mix in a small bowl with mayonnaise and mustard. Pour mixture into a shallow bowl or plate.
Season chicken with salt and pepper to taste. Roll seasoned chicken breasts in mayonnaise mixture, then dip in cracker crumbs and place in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes.
