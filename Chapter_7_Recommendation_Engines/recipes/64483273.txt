Title:
Hot Artichoke and Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg     cream cheese
1 cup     Artichoke (drained, coarsely chopped)
-------
1/2 cup  chopped spinach (frozen or steamed or boiled)
(don't squeeze water too much, so dip doesn't get dry/not soft)
-------
1/4 cup  shredded Romano cheese
1/4 cup  shredded Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup  mayo
1 glove  minced garlic
1/2 tsp  basil (dried)
1/4 tsp  salt & pepper

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients, and pour into sprayed dish.
Cook in oven 25-30 min (350 F)
If using Garlic & Herb light cream cheese do not add garlic and basil.
