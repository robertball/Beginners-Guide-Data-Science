Title:
Buffalo Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1122500.jpg
Ingredients:
8 hard-boiled eggs
2 Tbsp mayo
4 Tbsp plain Greek yogurt
1.5 Tbsp dijon mustard
1 celery stock finely chopped
2 Tbsp finely chopped anaheim pepper
2-3 Tbsp finely grated cheddar cheese
Franks Red Hot buffalo sauce (1 Tbsp or more)
Garlic salt (1/4 tsp)
Onion powder (1/4 tsp)
Black pepper (1/4 tsp)
Paprika (1/4 tsp)

Directions:
If eggs have not been boiled, place them in a pot with water covering them 1/2 to 1 inch. Bring the water to a boil for a minute or two, then remove from heat and allow eggs to cool.
Peel each egg and rinse
Cut each egg in half lengthwise and deposit yolk into medium sized mixing bowl; set whites aside
Add mayo, yogurt, dijon, celery, anaheim or chile pepper, garlic salt, onion powder, paprika and buffalo sauce to the mixture, to desired taste.
Mix ingredients together with a fork, mashing the yolks into a paste; adjust flavors and creaminess to taste
Add grated cheddar to mixture and spoon into eggwhite halves
Garnish with additional buffalo sauce and paprika

Description: 
Easter with a kick! Every year I make deviled eggs, and this year I wanted to try something new. This is my own creation: a good balance of richness and zesty flavor!
