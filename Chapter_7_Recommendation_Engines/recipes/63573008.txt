Title:
Simple Slow Cooker Irish Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs stew beef, browned
4 medium yellow onions, sliced
6 potatoes, peeled & diced
1 1/2 cups water
2 beef bouillon cubes
salt & pepper, to taste

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a slow cooker and cook on low for 8 hours.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
RainbowJewels
Description: 
I received this recipe when I was in the third grade. Our teacher made this for us in class on St. Patrick's Day, and I've made this very simple, hearty meal every year in March since then.
