Title:
Pennsylvania Pork Loin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 lb center cut pork loin
salt and pepper
2 cans bavarian style sauerkraut
2 T brown sugar
1 apple peeled and diced
1/2 can beer

Directions:
salt and pepper pork roast
put one can of sauerkraut in slow cooker and 1 T brown sugar.
Place roast fat side up on sauerkraut. Put other can sauerkraut on top of pork and sprinkle with brown sugar and diced apples. Pour 1/2 can beer on it and cook for 6 hours on low.
