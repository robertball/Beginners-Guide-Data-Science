Title:
Lemon Dijon Flounder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon Dijon Mustard
1 tablespoon margarine/butter (melted)
1/4 teaspoon fresh course ground black pepperer
2 cloves garlic (minced)
1/8 teaspoon salt
4 flounder filets

Directions:
Preheat overn to 425 f
Line a babking pan with aluminum foil
Mix first 6 ingredients together in a small mixing bowl and pour over filets
bake for 10-12 minutes (if frozen)  7-9 minutes if thawed
Fish is ready when meat flakes when tested with a fork
