Title:
Desert Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
l lb turkey
l lb italian sausage - mild or medium
1 onion, chopped
3 garlic cloves
1 15 oz can chicken broth
1 6oz tomato paste
1 8oz can tomato sauce
2 15oz cans diced tomato
1 15oz can black beans
1 15oz can pinto beans
3 tbs chili powder
8 tbs brown sugar
2 tbs worcestershire
2 tsp cumin
2 tsp cayenne pepper
2 tsp rosemary
2 tsp sage
1 cup white wine

Directions:
Brown the meat with onion and garlic, drain and set aside
Drain both cans of beans, put in crock pot or large pot
Add chicken broth, tomato paste, tomato sauce, diced tomatos, wine and all the spices. Mix together and add the brown sugar
Add the meat and simmer for 4 hours or until it reaches the desired consistency
Serve with cheese, sour cream and fresh onions on top
