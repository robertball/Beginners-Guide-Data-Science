Title:
My Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter
2-3 Tbs flour
1 tsp House seasoning
3 oz. cream cheese (fat-free)
1 1/2 to 2 cups milk
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Melt butter, stir in flour and seasoning until blended.  Add cream cheese to melt some before adding milk.  Slowly stir in milk and let cream cheese melt before adding Parmesan cheese.  Whisk until thickened.

Submitter: 
NEgirl
Description: 
This is a compilation of several alfredo recipes from AR.  Took a little from each recipe so though I could call it my own.
