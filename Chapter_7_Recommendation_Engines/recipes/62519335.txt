Title:
Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cooked and crumbled mild bulk sausage (you may also use cubed ham or cooked crumbled bacon)
1/4 cup minced onion
9 large eggs lightly beaten
3/4 cup milk
1/4 cup melted butter (cooled)
1 bag refrigerated hashbrowns (I use Simply Potatoes brand)
salt & pepper to taste
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease a 13 X 9 baking dish. Mix together eggs, butter and milk.  Spread an even layer of cooked meat on the bottom of baking dish, sprinkle with onion. Pour egg mixture carefully over meat. Spread hashbrowns over eggs layer. Sprinkle lightly with salt and pepper. Cover with cheese. Bake uncovered 50-60 minutes, until eggs are set and edges are lighly browned.

Submitter: 
Amy Thomas Link
Description: 
This is a great and easy one dish baked breakfast dish I have been using for years. It can be made with an different combinations of meats and cheese to suit your taste, as well as flavored hashbrowns (such as southwest)
