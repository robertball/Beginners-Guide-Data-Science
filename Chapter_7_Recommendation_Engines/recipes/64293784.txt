Title:
Low Carb Chicken Nacho Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1939368.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large boneless skinless chicken breast, cooked and shredded
1 Tbsp taco seasoning
1 C pico de gallo, divided
1 C sour cream, divided
1 C Mexican blend cheese, divided
8 oz. pkg mini sweet bell peppers, halved lengthwise and seeds removed
2 1/4 oz chopped olives

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Combine chicken, taco seasoning, 1/2 C pico de gallo, 1/2 C sour cream, and 1/2 C Mexican blend cheese.
Spray cooking dish lightly with cooking spray.  Fill each pepper half with as much chicken mixture as possible and place in cooking dish.  Sprinkle filled pepper halves with 1/4 C cheese.  Top pepper halves with chopped olives.  Sprinkle with remaining cheese.  Bake peppers uncovered for 15-20 minutes or until cheese is melted and peppers have begun to soften.
Top each cooked pepper with a dollop of sour cream and pico de gallo.
