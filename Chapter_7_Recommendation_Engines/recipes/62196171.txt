Title:
Golden Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. margarine
1/2 c crisco
3 c sugar
5 eggs 3 c flour
1 t salt
1/2 t baking pdr.
1 c milk
2 t vanilla (or almond extract)
optional: nuts

Directions:
Cream together the sugar, the margarine, and the crisco.
Add the eggs one at a time.
Sift together and then add the dry ingredients to the wet.
Bake in either an angelfood or bundt pan, or 2 loaf pans, buttered and floured.
Bake at 350 until golden on top, about an hour.
Let cool before removing from pan.

Submitter: 
Lisa Scott
Description: 
This is a family recipe. It's a wonderfully rich pound cake with a great almond flavor.  MMMMMM!
