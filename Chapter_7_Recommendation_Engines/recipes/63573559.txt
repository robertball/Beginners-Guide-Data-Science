Title:
Chicken Caesar Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993863.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast, grilled cubes
1/2 cup Croutons
2 Tbsp Parmesan Cheese, grated
1 Romaine Lettuce, chopped
3 Spinach Wraps
optional: BBQ sauce

Directions:
1. Grill chicken and cut into cubes or strips
2. Mix lettuce, dressing, parmesan cheese, croutons and chicken together.
3. Heat wraps (20 sec.)
4. Fill w chicken salad mix and wrap (optional to add a little dressing and BBQ sauce to wrap before wrapping in tightly

Submitter: 
Rachael Welch
Description: 
I love Red Robin's Chicken Caesar Wraps and was excited to see Spinach Wraps available at grocery stores. Now I can make these yummy wraps at home!
