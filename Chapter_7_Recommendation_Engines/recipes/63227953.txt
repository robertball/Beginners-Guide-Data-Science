Title:
Bone dust to Die for Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups brown sugar
1 1/2 cup of paprika
5 tbs of black pepper
4 tbsp chili powder
4 tbs garlic powder
4 tbs onion powder
3 tsp Cayenne pepper
5 tbs course salt

Directions:
combine each ingredient completely with the previous prior to adding the next. Mix completely, take your time.

Submitter: 
George. P. Cooper
Description: 
a combination of spices that is a great rub for smoking a variety of meats- pork, beef and poultry. we make up a large batch and store in an air tight bag for future usage
