Title:
Chicken Cordon Blue Casserole IV Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 chicken breast
12 slices cooked ham
12 slices of swiss or mozzarella cheese
4 tbsp chopped onion
8 tbsp melted butter
8 tbsp. flour
4 c. milk
½ tsp poultry seasoning
½ tsp salt
½ tsp. pepper
2 eggs
½ c. milk
6 tbsp melted butter
1 c. bread crumbs

Directions:
Simmer chicken in water (covered) 20 Min.  Slice as desired.  Saute onions in butter blend in spices and flour gradually add milk.  Cover and cook over medium heat.  Stir until thick and smooth.  remove from heat.
Beat eggs and add 1/2 cup milk.  Stir into hot sauce.  Cook for a few Min.  In a 9 x 13 dish, layer chicken, ham cheese and cover with sauce.  Combine crumbs, 3 tbsp butter.  Sprinkle on top- bake for 30 Min in 350º oven.

Submitter: 
K. Lea
Description: 
In Community Kitchen we would group cook and try out different recipes.  This is a favorite, slightly more expensive than the budget cooking, but worth the expense.  Easy to make ahead and freeze.
