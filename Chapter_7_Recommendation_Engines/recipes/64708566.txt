Title:
Cauliflower "Rice" Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 head of cauliflower
1/2 diced onion
2T olive oil
2T butter
1c sliced mushrooms
1/4c Parmesan cheese
1/2t garlic salt
dash of pepper

Directions:
Place large chunks of cauliflower into food processor and process until about the size of rice. May have to do a few batches.
Melt butter on medium high heat in small frying pan and saute mushrooms until softened and browned, about 5 minutes. Set aside.
Warm olive oil at medium high in large frying pan and saute onion till translucent, about 2 minutes.
Add cauliflower, garlic salt, and pepper to pan. Saute for about 8 minutes.
Add sauteed mushrooms and Parmesan cheese to pan and stir until mushrooms and cheese are warmed through. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
DOMMETT
Description: 
Great way to substitute a veggie in place of a starchy side. The sauteed mushrooms and Parmesan really give the slightly firm cauliflower the punch of flavor it needs. Inspired from Dr. Mark Hyman's Cauliflower rice recipe but put my own spin on it.
