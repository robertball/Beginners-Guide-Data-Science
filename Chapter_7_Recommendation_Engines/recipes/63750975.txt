Title:
Veggie and Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1040700.jpg
Ingredients:
1Medium sized  Rutabaga, diced
1 Onion, finely chopped
2 Tbsp. Butter
3 C. Fresh spinach leaves, chopped
1 14 oz can Pinto Beans, drained
1  32 oz container of chicken broth
1 tsp. Salt

Directions:
Chop rutabaga into 1/2 inch slices and peel the outer skin away
Dice rounds into 1 inch squares and rinse under cold running water. Set aside
Peel and chop onion into nice small chunks for frying
In a medium frying pan saute onion in butter until translucent in colour
Add chicken broth to onions and bring to a boil over medium heat
Stir in diced rutabaga and cook for 10 to 15 minutes to soften rutabaga
MIx in chopped spinach leaves, pinto beans and salt together. Simmer for a half hour until heated through and season to taste with salt and other seasoning to suit your personal preference and taste '
Serve with your favourite crackers or bread slices

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very hearty soup for the fall season to warm up on a cold fall day with root vegetables that are very savoury.
