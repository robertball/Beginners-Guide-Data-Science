Title:
Layered Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of crushed vanilla wafers
1 1/4 stick of butter
1 cup powdered sugar
1 block of cream cheese
1 large tub of cool whip
2 small boxes of instant pudding
3 cups of milk

Directions:
Mix 1 3/4 cup of crushed vanilla wafers with butter (melted) and press into the bottom of a 8x9 pan.
Mix powdered sugar, cream cheese and 1/2 tub of cool whip and pour into pan.
Mix pudding and milk until thickened and pour into pan.
Put remaining cool whip on top and sprinkle with remaining wafers.
Refrigerate for about 2 hours

Description: 
Yummy cream cheese, pudding, vanilla wafer dessert that requires no baking and is easy to make.
