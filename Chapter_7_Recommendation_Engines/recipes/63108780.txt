Title:
Tortellini Primavera with Garlic Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/789279.jpg
Ingredients:
19 oz bag of frozen cheese tortellini
1 Tbsp. butter
1 Tbsp. olive oil
2 cloves of garlic, minced
1/2 red pepper, diced
1/2 yellow pepper, diced
1 C chopped fresh broccoli
4 green onions, chopped
1 C chicken broth
1 C 1/2 & 1/2
2 tsp. cornstarch
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
3/4 tsp dried basil
3/4 tsp dried oregano
Parmesan cheese, shredded
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil tortellini and set aside once cooked. Meanwhile, combine the butter & olive oil over medium-low heat. Add the minced garlic & vegetables and cook til crisp tender. Add the chicken broth and bring to a hard simmer. Combine the cornstarch and 1/2 & 1/2 and add to the broth mixture and cook til slightly thickened. Add the basil, oregano, and pepper flakes. Toss the tortellini and coat the pasta with the sauce, top with salt, pepper, and Parmesan.

Description: 
A light cream sauce featuring vegetables gives ordinary cheese tortellini a nice change of pace. Quick too!
