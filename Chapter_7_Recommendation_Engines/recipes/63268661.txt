Title:
Broccoli Surprise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. chopped celery
1 c. chopped onion
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1 lb. jar of cheese whiz
20 oz. pkg frozen broccoli florets
4 c. cooked minute rice

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well.
Pour into a 9x13 pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 35-45 mins.
