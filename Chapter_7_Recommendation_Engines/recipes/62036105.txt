Title:
Easy Shrimp and Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) bottle of cocktail sauce
1 (8 ounce) cream cheese brought to room temperature
1 (6 ounce) can of tiny shrimp
1 (6 ounce) can of lump white crab meat
1 tablespoon horseradish

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix together cocktail sauce, shrimp and horseradish.  Set aside.  In another bowl, mix the crabmeat and cream cheese to form a ball.  Refridgerate cream cheese mixture for at least 1 hour or until firm.  When the ball is firm, put cocktail sauce mixture over top and serve with crackers, chips or pretzels.

Submitter: 
Irlandes
Description: 
This is a very yummy and quick appetizer that my whole family requests all the time!
