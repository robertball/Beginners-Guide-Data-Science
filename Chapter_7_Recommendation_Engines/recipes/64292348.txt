Title:
My Bolognese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
4 slices bacon, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1 large onion, minced
3 Tlb. minced garlic
1 pound ground beef
1/2 pound ground pork
1 chicken liver, chopped
1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, chopped
2 carrots, chopped
1 stalk celery, chopped
1 (28 ounce) can chopped tomatoes
10 ounces tomato paste
1/2 cup dry red wine
2 Tlb. basil
1 tsp. oregano
salt and pepper to taste
1 pound pasta

Directions:
In a large saucepan, fry bacon until crisp. Remove from pan. Saut√© onion and garlic and mushrooms in bacon grease. Add to that the beef, pork and chicken liver and brown completely.  Stir in bacon, mushrooms, carrots, celery, tomatoes, tomato paste,wine, basil, oregano. Cover, reduce heat and simmer two hours, stirring occasionally.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Season with salt and pepper. Serve sauce over hot pasta.
