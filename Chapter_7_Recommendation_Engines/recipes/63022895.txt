Title:
Mum's  Spicy Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
i Litre of chicken stock
8 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon onion powder
3 tablespoons olive oil
1 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, chopped
1 (16 ounce) jar chunky salsa
2 large  cans peeled and diced tomatoes
1  can  condensed tomato soup
3 tablespoons chili powder
1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
2 (16 ounce) cans chili beans, undrained
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream

Directions:
In a large pot over medium heat, combine stock, chicken, salt, pepper, garlic powder,and onion powder.  Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 1 hour, or until chicken juices run clear.  Remove chicken, reserve broth.  Shred chicken.
In a large pot over medium heat, cook onion and garlic in olive oil until slightly browned.  Stir in salsa, diced tomatoes, whole tomatoes, tomato soup, chili powder, corn, chili beans, and shredded chicken and 5 cups broth.  Simmer 30 minutes.
Serve in bowls with a dollop of sour cream

Submitter: 
Allyson
Description: 
A shredded chicken soup flavored with garlic, onion powder, salsa, tomatoes, tomato soup, chili powder, corn, beans and sour cream.Modified from: Catherine's Spicy Chicken Soup
