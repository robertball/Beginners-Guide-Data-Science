Title:
Bos Bar Be Que Sauce Recipe

Ingredients:
•	2 cup ketchup
•	2 (5.5 ounce) can spicy vegetable juice
•	2 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
•	2 cup honey
•	1/2 cup chopped onion
•	2 tablespoon garlic powder
•	4 tablespoons turbinado sugar
•	2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
•	2 teaspoon ground black pepper
•	3 tablespoons lemon juice
•	3 tablespoons liquid smoke flavoring
•	3 Chipotle peppers and 1 tablespoon of sauce
•

Directions:
1.	In a blender combine the ketchup, tomato juice,Worcestershire sauce, molasses, sugar, onion, garlic powder, ground black pepper, cayenne pepper, lemon juice, liquid smoke flavoring and chipotle peppers. Puree until smooth, and transfer to a saucepan while straining.
2.	Place saucepan on the stove over medium high heat. Bring mixture to a boil; reduce heat to low, and simmer for about 2 hours stirring occasionally.
