Title:
Super Fantastic Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. beef or pork roast, cooked
1 14 oz. can crushed tomatoes, or 3 fresh
1 can tomato sauce
2 1/2 cups beef broth
2 cans green chiles, or about 6 fresh
1/2 can jalapeno peppers, or 2-3 fresh
1 tbl. olive oil
1 small yellow or white onion
1 tbl. minced garlic
1 can red kidney beans with juice
1 can white kidney beans, drained
4-5 tbl. ground cumin
6-7 tbl. chili powder
1 tsp. cayenne pepper
1 tsp. crushed red pepper
1 tbl. dried cilantro
2 tbl. seasoned salt

Directions:
Heat oil in a skillet.  Saute onions and garlic, as well as fresh peppers if used.  Cook until all are tender, adding fresh tomatoes (if used) the last two minutes of cooking.  In the meantime, start adding all other ingredients to a slow-cooker.  Cook on low for desired length of time.

Submitter: 
Michelle
Description: 
I have come up with this recipe over time, borrowing from recipes of family and friends to come up with this unique and delicious version.  It takes some planning ahead as you need to use slow-cooked beef or pork roast, but in a pinch you can use ground beef.  This comes out medium on the spicy scale - you can adjust the peppers to your taste.  Seasonings can also be adjusted to taste.  This can be put in the crock pot in the morning before leaving for work, or for an even better taste, can be started the night before you are going to serve it and cooked for 24 hours.
