Title:
Hot Subs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese, softened
Mayo
Basil
Garlic salt
2 pkg sub rolls
1# deli sliced ham
1# deli sliced turkey
1/2# deli sliced cheddar cheese
1/2# deli sliced pepper jack cheese
Lettuce
Tomato, sliced
Guacamole
Pickles

Directions:
Mix cream cheese with enough mayo to make spreadable.  Add basil and garlic salt to taste.
Split rolls in half.  Spread one side with cream cheese-mayo spread, add a little mustard if desired.  Place deli meat on top of spread, cheeses slices on other half.
Broil rolls, meat and cheese sides up, for 2-4 minutes.  Watch carefully so as not to burn.
Top with lettuce, tomato, guacamole, pickles as desired.
