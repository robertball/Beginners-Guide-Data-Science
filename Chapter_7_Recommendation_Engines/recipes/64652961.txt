Title:
Cheesy Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  10-inch round bread loaves
1 jar marinated artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1 cup sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
1 cup Monterrey Jack cheese, grated
1 cup mayonnaise ( NOT miracle whip)
1 small onion, chopped fine
1 cup high quality parmesan cheese, grated

Directions:
Preheat oven  to 350 degrees F.  Carefully slice the top off of one loaf of bread (set aside) and hollow out , being careful not to break through the bottom.  Cube other loaf of bread and place in storage bag to keep fresh.
Mix together all ingredients well  (you can also throw them all into a food processor and pulse until well mixed).  Spoon into hollowed-out bread.  Replace lid.  Place loaf on tine foil lined baking sheet and bake for 1 1/4 hours (75 minutes) or until cheese is melted completely.  Serve with cubed bread, crackers or veggies.
