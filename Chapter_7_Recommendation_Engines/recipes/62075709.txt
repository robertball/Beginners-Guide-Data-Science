Title:
Bangin' Steak Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 pkg (1.25 oz) dry chili seasoning mix - make sure that chili pepper is the first ingredient.
1 pkg (1.25 oz) dry ranch dip mix
1 tsp garlic salt
1 tsp onion salt (or powder)
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1 tsp McCormick GrillMates Montreal steak seasoning

Directions:
Add ingredients one at a time to a medium bowl. After each ingredient, use a fork to combine mixture.  Be sure that ingredients are thoroughly mixed. Rub should be smooth and granular.  Pat rub onto steak or meat of your choice.

Submitter: 
BROWNSKYNLADY
Description: 
This quick and easy grill rub will make your steak sing! No expensive spices to search for, but lots of flavor!
