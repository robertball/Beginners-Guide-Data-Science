Title:
Pa Pa's Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Bags of Bread Cubes
1 Package of Turkey Giblets
3 Onions
6 Stalks of Celery
1 tsp Sage
1 tsp Poultry Seasoning
1 tsp Ground Oregeno
Salt and Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Cook giblets in water for 1/2 hour. Retain broth. After cooking, pick all meat off bones.
Finely chop onion, celery and giblets in food processor or by hand
Stir bread cubes into giblet broth
Add giblets, celery and onion to bread mixture.
Add seasoning, adjust to taste
Mix well.
