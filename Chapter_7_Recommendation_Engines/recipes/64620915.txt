Title:
White Sweet Potato & Roasted Banana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Jersey White sweet potatoes
1 banana (peel on)
1-2 tablespoons smooth nut butter, optional
splash of skim milk, rice milk, soy milk, or almond milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Wrap sweet potatoes in foil and place on baking sheet. Cook for about 40 minutes.
Put banana, peel on, onto the cookie sheet and cook an additional 15 minutes.
Allow sweet potatoes and bananas to cool a little and then remove peels and skins.
Process in a food processor or blender, adding nut butter if desired and milk as needed to achieve desired consistency.
