Title:
Apricot No Bake Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
155G BUTTER NUT SNAP BISCUITS (or biscuits of your choice)
75g Melted Butter
470g Apricot Nectar
1 tbs Gelatine
375 g Cream Cheese
1/2 Cup Caster Sugar
1tbs Lemon Juice
1 Cup of Whipped Cream (measured after whipping)
TOPPING
1tbs Sugar
3tsp Corn Flour (Corn Starch)
2tsp Rum

Directions:
Crush biscuits and mix with melted butter.  Spread and press down to form a base, into spring form cheescake tin & chill.
Place 1 cup of nectar in saucepan & sprinke with gelatine.  Place on low heat and stir until dissoved.  Allow to cool and thicken.
Beat cream cheese, caster sugar.  Add lemon juice, prepared nectar juice, mix.
Fold in whipped cream.  Pour into prepared biscuit base.  Refrigerate until firm.
TOPPING
Place remaining apricot nectar and cornflour into a saucepan and mix until smooth.  Bring to boil, remove from heat.  Add rum, stir and when cool pour over top of cheesecake.  Place back into fridge until set.

Submitter: 
CZARDAS
Description: 
No need for baking, this cheesecake is whipped up in minutes and then allowed to cool.  The topping has a hit of rum, which can be ommitted if preferred.  AUSTRALIAN MEASUREMENTS
