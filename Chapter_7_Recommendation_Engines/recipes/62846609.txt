Title:
Ancho espresso dry rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 C ancho chile powder
1/3 C Melitta coffee (extram fine grind)
2 Tbs garlic powder
1 Tbs onion powder
1/2 Tbs cinnamon
1 Tbs kosher salt
1 Tbs coarse blk pepper

Directions:
Mix together.

Submitter: 
Chef Lucky
Description: 
You can sub in your fav. cofffee blend. When I worked for a popular hotel chain in Texas we used a rub similar to this one for grilled pork chops and served with a warm peach sauce.
