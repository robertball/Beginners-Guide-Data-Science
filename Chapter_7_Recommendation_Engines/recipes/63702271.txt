Title:
Chili Beans Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of Pinto Beans
1 can Rotel
1 lb ground turkey
1 tbsp chili powder
1/2 tbsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp chicken bouillon powder

Directions:
Cook ground turkey until brown.
Heat 2 cans of pinto beans (undrained) and 1 can rotel in a pot.
Add 1/3 cup of water to the pot.
Add cooked turkey to pot.
Add chili powder, garlic powder, oregano, and chicken bouillon to pot.
Mix well, cover, and bring to boil.
Simmer for 15 minutes.
Serve.
