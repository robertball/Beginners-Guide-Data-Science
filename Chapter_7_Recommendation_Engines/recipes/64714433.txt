Title:
Tortellini, White Bean and Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups chicken stock
1 cup water
1 can (14.5 oz.) Italian-style  diced tomatoes
1 pkg. (9 oz) refrigerated cheese tortellini
1 pkg. smoked sausage
1 can (15 oz.) cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
4 cups  (packed) pre-rinsed, pre-chopped spinach leaves

Directions:
In a medium stockpot or large sauce pan, combine stock, water and tomatoes. Bring to boil. Add tortellini, adjust heat to maintain gentle boil and cook 2 minutes.  Add sausage, beans and spinach. Bring to low simmer and cook 10 minutes. Serve, refrigerating any leftovers.
