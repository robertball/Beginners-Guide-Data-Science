Title:
Vermont-Style Manhattan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1060951.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces spiced rum
1 fluid ounce sweet vermouth
1 maraschino cherry

Directions:
Pour rum and vermouth over ice cubes, place cherry in glass; stir, then sit back and enjoy.
