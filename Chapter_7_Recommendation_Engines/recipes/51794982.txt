Title:
TBaer's Sauerkraut Dog and Brat Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C of sauerkraut, drained and rinsed (use refrigerated bagged kraut instead of canned.
1/4 cup sweet pickle relish
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1 tsp of prepared yellow mustard
1 tsp of prepared Dijon mustard
1 package of Rotella's Brat Buns

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan; cover and cook on low heat until heated through. Serve over hot dogs, brats, or polish sausage in buns.

Submitter: 
TBaer
Description: 
This a very good topping for hot dogs, brats, and polish sausage, but it also stands alone as a side dish as well.Modified from: Sauerkraut Hot Dog Topping
