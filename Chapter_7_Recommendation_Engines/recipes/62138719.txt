Title:
completely comforting chicken casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless skinless chisken breasts
1 pkg frozen mixed veggies(broccoli, cauliflower, and carrot blend)
1 pkg chicken flavored stuffing
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup sour cream
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese if desired

Directions:
preheat oven to 375
prepare stuffing as directed on package
place chicken in a 9x13 casserole, top with vegetables.
in a bowl combine sour cream and mushroom soup. season with salt and pepper if desired.
pour over chicken.
top gravy with stuffing
bake in preheated oven for 35-45 minutes or untill chicken is cooked thru.
if desired, add shredded cheddar to top of casserole with in last 5-10 minutes of cooking.

comfortin layered chicken casserole with lots of veggies, stuffing and a creamy gravy.
