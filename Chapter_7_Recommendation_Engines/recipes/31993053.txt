Title:
Brown Sugar Glaze for Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
2 tbsp flour
1/2 tsp dry or prepared mustard
1/8 tsp cinnamon
3 tbsp dry sherry, vinegar or water

Directions:
Combine all ingredients; mix well.
During last 15 - 20 minutes of baking, spread glaze over ham.
Continue baking ham, occasionally spooning glaze over top.
