Title:
Thin Crispy Dark Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 lb (1 stick) butter (room temp)
1 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
1 egg beaten
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
3 tablespoons dark cocoa powder
1 1/2 cups cake flour

Directions:
mix butter, sugar, salt and vanilla
add egg, oil and cocoa powder
add flour
Drop by teaspoons on lightly greased cookie sheet
Bake at 350 for 12-15 minutes (crispy is 15 minutes)
Cool thoroughly on rack or plates.

Description:
