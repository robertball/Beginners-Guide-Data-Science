Title:
Zero points Crustless Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups canned pumpkin
3/4 c splenda ( i will use stevia)
1/2 c egg beaters
1/2 tea salt
1 &1/2 tea pumpkin pie spice
1 &1/2 cups fat free milk

Directions:
mix all ingredients and pour into greased 9" pie pan
Bake 400* for 15 min then lower oven to 375* and bake for 45 min
only 3 points for the whole pie
