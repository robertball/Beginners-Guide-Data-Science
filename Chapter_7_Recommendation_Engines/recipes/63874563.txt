Title:
Gingerger Bread Cutout Cookies (Carol Hall) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup soft butter
1/3 cup brown sugar
1 egg
2/3 cup honey
2 ¾ cup whole wheat flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon ground ginger (1 tablespoon fresh)

Directions:
Cream shortening and sugar. Add egg and honey and mix well.
Sift dry ingredients and stir into shortening and egg mixture. Blend thoroughly. Chill dough. I only chilled it for about 30-60 minutes. Roll out 1/4" thick and cut into shapes.
Place on lightly greased cookie sheet 1” apart and bake at 375 degrees for 8-10 minutes or until done. Frost with buttercream, cream cheese or royal frosting. Decorate as desired Baked, unfrosted,cookies can be stored in the freezer.

Submitter: 
Apron Appeal
Description: 
Gingerbread cookies for a holiday tradition. Great alternative to sugar cookies.
