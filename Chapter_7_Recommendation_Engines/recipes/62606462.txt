Title:
Mushroom Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups chicken stock, divided
3 tbsp olive oil, divided
1 pound portobello mushrooms, thinly sliced
1 pound white mushrooms, thinly sliced
2 shallots, diced
1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
1/2 cup dry white wine
sea salt and pepper to taste
3 tbsp finely chopped chives
4 tbsp butter
1/3 freshly grated Parmesan

Directions:
In a saucepan, warm the broth over low heat
Warm 2 tbsp olive oil in a large saucepan over med high. Stir in the mushrooms and cook until soft. Remove mushrooms and their liquid and set aside.
Add 1 tbsp olive oil and stir in the shallots. Cook 1 minute. Add rice, stir to coat. When rice is pale gold colour pour in wine and stir until absorbed. Add 1/2 cup broth to pan, stir until absorbed. continue until all broth is used in this manner. About 15-20 min.
Remove from heat, and stir in mushrooms and liquied, butter, chives and parmesan. Season.
