Title:
Steak Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1089728.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp Olive Oil
2 lbs Steak - (Top Round, London Broil, or Flank)
2 tsp Fresh Ground Black Pepper
2 Medium Yellow Onions Diced
2 Tbsp Chopped Garlic
2 Green Bell Peppers Diced
1 Yellow Bell Pepper Diced
1 Red Bell Pepper Diced
2 15oz Cans Kidney Beans - Drained and Rinsed
2 15oz Cans Black Beans - Drained and Rinsed
1 15oz Can Yellow or White Corn - Drained
3 14.5oz Cans Diced Tomatoes
1 15oz Can Tomato Sauce

Directions:
Cut the meat in to small cubes, approximately half an inch in size.
In a large stock pot over medium heat, add the olive oil, meat and black pepper.
Allow the meat to cook, stirring occasionally, while preparing and adding the other ingredients.
Dice and add onions; allow to cook until translucent, approx. 3-4 minutes.
Add chopped Garlic.
Dice and add Peppers one at a time.
Add Kidney Beans, Black Beans, and Corn. NOTE: Beans should be drained and rinsed. Corn should be drained.
Add Diced Tomatoes - Remove any blemished tomatoes or tomato cores, which are not very tasty.
Add Tomato Sauce.
Cook for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Steve Talbot
Description: 
This is a basic recipe for non-traditional Chili using Steak.  My wife is allergic to Chili Powder, so I don't use any in this recipe, but you are welcome to add some if you like.  This is a very tame, full flavor, colorful, chili recipe that even the kids will like.
