Title:
Mexican Style Sausage Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large potatoes, scrubbed and chopped into 1/2" pieces
1 medium onion, chopped
8 oz. bulk sausage
1 small can diced green chiles
2 eggs
salt and pepper to taste
Salsa or hot sauce

Directions:
Place cut potatoes in large nonstick skillet.  You can add one or two teaspoons of water if you would like.  Cover skillet and place over medium heat, stirring on occasion.
When potatoes are about half cooked, remove lid and add onions, sausage, and chiles.  Stir to break up sausage.  Flip every few minutes to make sure mixture is browned on all sides.
When mixture is just browned, create 2 wells in mixture.  Crack an egg into each well.  Season dish with salt and pepper and cover.  Cook until eggs have cooked to desired doneness.
Garnish with salsa or hot sauce
