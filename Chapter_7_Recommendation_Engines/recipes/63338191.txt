Title:
Australian Restaurant Croutons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Slices White bread, cubed
1/4 cup butter, softened
1 tsp cajun seasoning
1 tbsp minced garlic

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
With softened butter (Not melted, croutons will not turn out) add cajun seasoning and garlic. Blend well and set aside
Place bread on cookie sheet and place in oven until toasted golden brown. Remove from oven and allow to cool completely.
Place butter in large mixing bowl, coat bread crumbs gently. Place bread on baking sheet once more.
Bake for 8-10 minutes more, shake pan to be certain all are browning evenly.

Submitter: 
ANGELSTAR
Description: 
This is from that famous down under restaurant. I crave their salads, which does me no good living overseas. So I went in search for the recipe for their crouton to make them myself. I found this online but it was for a large batch, so I just converted it.
