Title:
Whole Wheat Pizza Dough Secret Family Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup warm water
1 T. yeast
1 tsp. sugar
1/4 cup olive oil
1 1/2 tsp. salt
3 cups hard white wheat flour
1 cup additional flour if needed

Directions:
Add the yeast and sugar to the warm water, let it grow several minutes.  Mix with the oil.  Add flour and salt.  Add additional flour if needed to make a non-sticky dough.  Knead for several minutes.  Cover with a damp towel or place in a sealed container.  Allow to rise 20-30 min.  Roll with a rolling pin on pizza stone.  Top with toppings.  Bake at 450 degrees for 15 min.

Description: 
This recipe makes one large pizza crust.
