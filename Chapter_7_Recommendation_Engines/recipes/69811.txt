Title:
Midori Glow Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/824719.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup ice cubes
2 fluid ounces vodka
1 fluid ounce melon liqueur
1 cup lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage

Directions:
Fill a short glass with ice. Pour in vodka and melon liqueur, and stir. Top off with lemon lime soda, and serve.

Submitter: 
JOEBLACK
Description: 
Cool looking and refreshing beverage to drink in a club. You'll love the taste and how it glows with the UV lights in the club!
