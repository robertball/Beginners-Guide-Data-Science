Title:
Beef Ka-Bob Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup oil
3/4 cup wine vinegar
1 tablespoon crushed fresh garlic
1/4 cup Worcestershire Sauce
1 tablespoon salt
1/4 cup sugar
1 tablespoon pepper
Ka-Bob meat; use a good cut of meat, cut into desired size pieces
Ka-Bob vegetables; onion, bell pepper, mushrooms, tomato etc., cut into pieces/wedges

Directions:
Combine all marinade ingredients.
Marinate the beef for one day (over 24 hours) in refrigerator.
Marinate the vegetables in a separate container or bag.
When ready to grill alternate meat and veggies on stick.  Let juices drip off and meat come to room temperature; grill as desired.
