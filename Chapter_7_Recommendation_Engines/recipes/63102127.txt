Title:
Disappearing Fiber One® Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/786743.jpg
Ingredients:
5 Tablespoons butter
1- 10oz. bag marshmallows
1- 16.2oz. box Fiber One® Original Bran Cereal
1/4 cup each milk and white confectionary coating, melted

Directions:
In a large sauce pan, melt butter. Add marshmallows and stir until melted and completely blended into the butter.
Turn the heat down to low and stir in the cereal, mixing until evenly coated.
Pour mixture into a 13x9 baking pan, that has been sprayed with non-stick cooking spray.
Allow to cool completely. Remove from pan and cut into squares. Place on waxed paper.
Drizzle with the milk and white coating. Allow to dry completely. Enjoy!
