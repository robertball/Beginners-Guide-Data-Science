Title:
Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground beef
1 quart spaghetti sauce
1 garlic clove, crushed
1 cup cottage cheese
1 egg
1/3 cup parmesan cheese
1 1/2 cups grated marble cheese
9 - 15 wide lasagne noodles

Directions:
Brown beef in frying pan.
Add spaghetti sauce and garlic. Bring to boil and simmer 10 min.
Put a very small amount of meat sauce in bottom or 9" x 9" pan - just enough to keep noodles from resting on bottom of pan.
Mix cottage cheese, egg and parmesan cheese together well.
To assemble:
1. bit of meat sauce
2. layer of uncooked noodles
3. 1/3 meat sauce
4. 1/2 cottage cheese mixture
5. layer of uncooked noodles
6. 1/3 meat sauce
7. 1/2 cottage cheese mixture
8. layer of uncooked noodles
9. 1/3 cup meet sauce
10. all marble cheese
Cover with foil.
Bake @ 350 x 1 hour or more, until noodles are tender.
Let stand x 10 min. before serving.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A classic recipe of layered meat, noodles and cheese, smothered in tomato sauce.
