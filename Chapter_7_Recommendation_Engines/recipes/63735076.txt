Title:
Marinated Cucumbers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. lemon juice
3 T. vinegar
3/4 c. oil (your choice)
3 T. water
3 T. diced onion
2 1/2 t. parsley
1/2 T. tarragon leaves
1/2 T. sugar
4-6 cucumbers.

Directions:
Slice cucumbers as desired.
Mix together all other ingredients and pour over cucumbers.
Chill at least 3 hours before serving, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
A great new way to eat your cucumbers. Super flavorful.
