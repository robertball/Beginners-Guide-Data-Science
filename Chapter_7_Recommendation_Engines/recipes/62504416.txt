Title:
Aunt Karens Brussel Sprout Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ten ounce packs frozen brussel sprouts,1/4 cup apple cider vinegar,1/4 cup salad oil,1 tsp salt,1/4 tsp pepper,1 medium tomato and 1 table spoon snipped parsley

Directions:
Cook brussel sprouts according to package directions,drain.In a glass jar with lid put vinegar,oil,salt and pepper.Shake well.Pour dressing over hot brussel sprouts turning to coat all.Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.Before serving cut tomato into small pieces and add to brussel sprout mixture. Sprinkle with parsley. Enjoy!!

Submitter: 
mistychesebro
Description: 
This salad goes very well with most pork dishes.Light and refreshing!
