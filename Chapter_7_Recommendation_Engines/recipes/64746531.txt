Title:
Mother Of All Vegetable Soups (MOAVS) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6588731.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 onion
7 medium carrots
7 celery stalks
3 small bell peppers
2 zucchinis
1/2 small cabbage head
1 lb green beans
2 quarts water
2 tablespoons Tone's Chicken Base
2 tablespoons garlic
2 tsp black pepper
2 cans diced tomatoes - any flavor

Directions:
Use a little oil, saute onion, carrots, celery, bell pepper.
Add in the rest of the veggies, the water, seasonings and canned tomatoes.  Bring to slight boil, cover and cook on low simmer for 20 minutes.
