Title:
Steakhouse Ranch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 package hidden valley BUTTERMILK Ranch dry mix
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp paprika
- dash of powdered cayenne

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a bowl.  Adjust cayenne for spice preference.
Chill in refrigerator for 1 hour minimum (over night is better).

Submitter: 
Jacob French
Description: 
After trying bottle after bottle of pre-made ranch dressing and not finding anything to compete, I decided to whip up my own.  This is so close to the restaurant style ranch dressing I can't tell the difference.  Easy, too!  Enjoy.
