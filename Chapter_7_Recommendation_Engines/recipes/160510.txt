Title:
Creamy Layered BLT Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986186.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) container BREAKSTONE'S or KNUDSEN Sour Cream
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1 (2.1 ounce) package OSCAR MAYER Ready To Serve Bacon
1/2 cup KRAFT Shredded Cheddar Cheese
2 medium tomatoes, chopped, divided
1 cup shredded lettuce

Directions:
Mix sour cream and onion powder; spread onto bottom of 9-inch pie plate.
Heat bacon as directed on package; cut into small pieces. Sprinkle over sour cream mixture. Top with cheese, 3/4 cup of the tomatoes and the lettuce; sprinkle with remaining tomatoes.
Serve with RITZ Toasted Chips, toast triangles or fresh vegetable dippers.

Submitter: 
Breakstone's and Knudsen Family of Products
Description: 
Rich flavors make this creamy, crunchy dip a party favorite.
