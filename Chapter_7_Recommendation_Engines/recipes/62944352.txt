Title:
Veggie Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pillsbury pie crust
1 can mixed vegetables
1/2 c frozen broccoli
2 Morning Star Spicy Bean patties,crumbled
1 can low sodium cream of broccoli soup
1/4 cup chopped green pepper
1/8tsp black pepper
1/8 tsp Curry powder
1/8 tsp no salt

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375. Place the pie crust in  pie plate and set aside. Drain can of mixed veggies and place in the bowl. Mix in broccoli, patties and green pepper. Add cream of broccoli soup and  gently mix together.   Place mixture in pie crust then sprinkle with pepper, no-salt, and curry. Place top pie crust over mixture and with a fork press indentations on outer pie crust then make a few  small slits in top and brush lightly with egg and sprinkle with pepper. Place in oven for approx 45-50 min until top turns golden brown.
