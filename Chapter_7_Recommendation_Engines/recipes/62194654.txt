Title:
Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs of ground turkey breast (Make sure it the breast, its healthier)
1 box of chicken flavored Stuffing mix
2 eggs
1 cup of fresh broccoli cooked and food processed until very fine. (it hides it)

Directions:
Mix turkey, stuffing mix, both eggs and broccoli in a large bowl. Shape into 9 burgers about 4 inches in diameter. I put these on my broiler pan and then BAKED them on 350 for 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Rita M.B.
Description: 
I came up with this after modifying my recipe Thanksgiving meatballs that my daughter loves. I think the trick is that these are baked.
