Title:
Turkey Veggie Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey breast (raw or cooked leftovers)
1 48 oz can / container chicken broth
2 qt water
1 pgk dry onion soup mix
2 stalks celery, cut up
1 28 oz can diced tomatoes, and the juice
1 6 oz can of tomato paste
1 pkg frozen mixed veggies (we like corn, carrots and asparagus)
3-4 cloves garlic
1/2 cup small pasta like orzo or tubitini
1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
Pepper
grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a large pot, if raw, saute the turkey until fully cooked.
Add the broth, water, soup mix and celery. Boil for 10 minutes.
Add diced tomatoes (and juice), tomato paste, veggies, pasta, garlic, Worcestershire, and pepper (to taste). Boil for another 20 minutes.
Serve with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.

Submitter: 
AddiesMom6
Description: 
This started out as a replica of a 'goddess' soup from a local restaurant. I only had ground turkey, and I added too much tomato, and voila! An even better recipe was formed.
