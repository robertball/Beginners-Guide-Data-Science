Title:
Baked Brie in Puff Pastry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 (17.5 ounce) package frozen puff pastry, thawed
1 (8 ounce) wheel Brie cheese
1/4 cup sliced almonds
Apricot or peach preserves or raspberry

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degree C).May need to increase to 400 Lightly grease a 9 inch pie pan.
Slice the wheel of Brie in half, horizontally, so that the halves are two, flatter wheels of Brie. Lay the puff pastry in the pie pan. Place half of the Brie (rind-side down) onto the pastry dough. Place almonds and preserves evenly over the top. Place the other half of the Brie (rind-side up) over the almonds and preserves/ Bundle the pastry dough around the Brie. Seal the pastry dough well. Then freeze.
Bake for 15 to 20 minutes. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
