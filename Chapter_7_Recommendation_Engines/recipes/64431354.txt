Title:
Easy Curried RIce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup rice
1 can mushroom soup
1 1/2 can water
3/4 cup grated cheddar
1 pepper cut up
sauted mushrooms (optional)
4 tsp curry powder
1/2 tsp each salt and pepper
1/4 tsp oregano

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and put in a greased casserole dish
Cook 350  for 1 hour stirring occasionally
