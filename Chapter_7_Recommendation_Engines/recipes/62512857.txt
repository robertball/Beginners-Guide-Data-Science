Title:
Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1119717.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs. browned ground beef
1/2 onion
1/2 bell pepper (any color)
1/3 jalapeño or other pepper(optional)
1-28 oz. can kidney beans
1-14 oz. can whole kernel corn
1-28 oz. can diced tomatoes
1-14 oz. can Rotel
1 can tomato soup
1 - 1 1/2 cups water
1-1 oz. pkg. dry Ranch dressing mix
1 pkg. taco seasoning

Directions:
Saute onion, pepper and hamburger. Combine all ingredients in crockpot. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours. Serve with sour cream, grated cheese, green onions, olives, jalapeños, tortillas and corn bread.
