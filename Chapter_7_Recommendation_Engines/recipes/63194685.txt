Title:
General Tso Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz chicken
1  Tbs Red Wine vinegar
2  Tbs Sugar or Splenda for the calorie counter
1 1/2  Tbs Corn Starch
1   Tbs Oil
1/2  Tbs Chopped Ginger
1 tsp Pepper Flakes
3/4  Cup Chicken Broth
2  Cups Broccoli Flowerettes
2 Cloves Minced garlic
2 Med Scallions
2  Tbs Soy sauce

Directions:
(1) Mix, Broth, Starch, Ginger,Soy, Sugar, Vinegar. Set  aside
(2) Cook Chicken ( Batter fry or cook in Wok ) Set aside
(3) 1 Tbs Oil, Add Scallions,Garlic,Pepper Flakes stir for 2 Min, Add Chicken,Stir Broth Mixture then add to Chicken when Broth thickens add Broccoli and serve over Rice.
