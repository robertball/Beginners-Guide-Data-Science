Title:
Slow Cooker Brisket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 pound) beef brisket
1 (12 ounce) bottles chili sauce
1 bottle of Lager
1 (1 ounce) envelope dry onion soup mix
5 cloves of garlic peeled and halved
1 T. Worcestershire sauce
1 Large onion thickly sliced

Directions:
Place the beef brisket into a slow cooker on top of sliced onions. In a medium bowl, mix together the chili sauce, onion soup mix, and lager. Pour over the brisket. place garlic around brisket.  Cover, and cook on the Low setting for 8 to 10 hours. Slice the brisket against the grain, and pour the gravy over the slices.
