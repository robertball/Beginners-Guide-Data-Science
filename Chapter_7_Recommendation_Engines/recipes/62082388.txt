Title:
Helen's famous  Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of cabbage
1 cup of REAL Mayonaise
2 tbs. sugar
3 tbs. lemon juice
1/4 cup white vinegar
1 tsp. salt
Paprika

Directions:
1.  Grate the head of cabbage angel hair thin (you can buy pre-sliced angel hair cabbage in the produce aisle at you local grocery store to save time.)
2.  add mayo, sugar, lemon juice, and salt them mix thouroughly.
3.  Top with sprinkled Paprika
*  Remember to keep this over ice if it's going to be out for a long time as mayo turns very quickly!

Description: 
This is a family favorite that my grandmom Helen makes!  There are no bells and whisltes, but it still tastes great every time.
