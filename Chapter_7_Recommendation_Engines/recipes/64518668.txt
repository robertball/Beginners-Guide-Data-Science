Title:
Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 frozen bag Mr. Dell’s Hash browns  (thawed)
3 c. sour cream
2 1/2 c. shredded Swiss cheese
onion flakes
2-4 Tb. parsley
1 t. salt
1 t. dill weed
1/2 t. pepper
1/2 t. paprika
1 can cream of chicken and a little milk

Directions:
Combine all ingredients together and put in a 9 x 13 pan.  Bake 30-40 min @ 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
US92
Description: 
Nancy Moore joined us to cook this potato casserole that offers a twist on the typical hashbrown casserole by adding Swiss cheese and dill.
