Title:
Christy's Awesome Black Bean and Wild Rice Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
1 medium red onion, finely chopped
1 bunch cilantro, chopped
1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (16 ounce) package cooked wild rice
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1/2 cup roughly chopped cashews
Dressing:
1/2 cup fresh squeezed orange juice
1/4 cup olive oil
3 tablespoons tarragon vinegar
1 1/2 teaspoons dijon mustard
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1/4 teaspoon cumin
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, toss together the red onion, cilantro, black beans, wild rice, dried cranberries, and cashews.
In a small bowl, whisk together the orange juice, olive oil, tarragon vinegar, mustard, thyme, cumin, cayenne, salt and pepper. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat. Chill for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
SeattleFarmersMarketGirl
Description: 
I love this cold salad as a light lunch or as a side dish. It's great to take to potlucks, it is always a winner. The orange and herb vinaigrette is really delicious!
