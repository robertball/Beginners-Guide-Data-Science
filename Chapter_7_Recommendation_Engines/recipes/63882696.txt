Title:
Easy Zucchini Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of lettuce, washed and chilled
1 small bunch of romaine, washed and chilled
1/4 cup olive oil or salad oil
2 medium zucchini, thinly sliced
1 cup sliced radishes
3 green onions, sliced
3 tablespoons crumbled blue cheese(optional)
2 tablespoons tarragon or wine vinegar
1/4 tsp salt
1 small garlic clove, crushed
Dash of pepper

Directions:
In a large salad bowl, tear greens into bite sized pieces.
Toss with oil until leaves glisten.
Add zucchini, radishes, onions and cheese.
Combine vinegar, salt, garlic and pepper, pour over salad mixture and toss

Submitter: 
CBerrier86
Description: 
This one-dish salad is delicious, quick, and simple and will go great with any Italian main dish!
