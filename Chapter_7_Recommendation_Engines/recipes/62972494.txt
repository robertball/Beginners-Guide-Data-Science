Title:
Hot Artichoke Dip with Spinach and Jalapenos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8oz pkgs cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 14oz cans artichoke hearts, drained and pulsed in food processor.
1/4 to 1/3 cup jarred jalapeno peppers, finely chopped
1 10oz pkgs of frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained well.
3 Tbls minced onion
2 cloves garlic minced
1 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Spray a large casserole with non-stick cooking spray.  In large bowl mix the softened cream cheese and mayo until blended.  Mix in all other ingredients until well combined.  Spread evenly in sprayed casserole and sprinkle with a bit more parmesan and parsley before putting in the oven.  Bake for 20-30 mins until starting to be golden brown on top.  Remove from oven and serve with a veggie and tortilla chip platter.

Submitter: 
Mary P.
Description: 
This appetizer is a huge hit at any party.  I've never left a party that I brought it to without a handful of people wanted the recipe.  It's spicy and cheesey!  Serve with veggies and tortilla chips.  Yummy
