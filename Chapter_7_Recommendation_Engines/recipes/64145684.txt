Title:
Stuffed Flounder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1 cup chopped mushrooms
1/2 bag of fake crab meat (7 oz)
1/2 cup saltine cracker crumbs
2 Tbs dried parsley
1/2 teas salt
2 lbs flounder fillets (8)
3 Tbs butter or margarine
3 Tbs of all purpose flour
1/4 teas salt
11/2 cups of milk
1/3 cup dry white wine
1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
1/2 teas paprika

Directions:
(Preheat oven 400)
cook onion in 1/4 cup of butter til soft, add mushrooms and simmer until soft. Mix in fake crab-meat,cracker crumbs, parsley and 1/4 teas. salt and dash of pepper for taste.
Spread stuffing over flounder fillets and place seam side down into 12x71/2x2 inch baking dish. Set aside.
In a saucepan melt 3 tablespoons of butter or margarine, add flour, salt, milk and wine. Cook and stir until mixture thickens and bubbles. Pour over fillets.
Bake in hot oven (400) for 25 minutes, then sprinkle with cheese and paprika returning dish to the oven bake for 10 minutes more or when fish flakes with fork.
