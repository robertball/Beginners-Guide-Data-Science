Title:
mud Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3050114.jpg
Ingredients:
6 oz package instant chocolate pudding
3 cups cold milk
6 whole gram crackers
1 package Chocolate chips (optional)
6 gummy worms (optional)

Directions:
Finely crush the gram crackers in a zip lock bag
Put the chocolate pudding and milk in a jar
Shake it for about two minutes or stir around until blended
Stir in gram crackers
Pour into 6 mugs
Place chocolate chips on top
Place in gummy worms

Submitter: 
Izzy Fiona
Description: 
This chocolatey delightful drink is perfect for a quick snack to satisfy your sweet tooth.
