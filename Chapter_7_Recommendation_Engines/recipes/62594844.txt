Title:
Cheese Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/556159.jpg
Ingredients:
4 T. butter
4 T. flour
1 t. salt
4 c. milk
mustard (add to taste)
1.5 lbs. of grated swiss cheese

Directions:
Melt 4 T. butter over low heat. Blend 4 T. flour, 1t. salt, add butter stirring until mixture is smooth and bubbly.
Remove from heat; stir in 4 cups of milk.
Bring to boil, stirring constantly (takes a while, so be patient.) Boil 1 minute.
Add mustard (to your taste) for a little tang.
Add grated baby swiss cheese about a 1/4 cup at a time and stir until melted over very low heat.
