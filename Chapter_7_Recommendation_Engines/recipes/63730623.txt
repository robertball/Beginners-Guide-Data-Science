Title:
Caramel Sauce II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup heavy cream
4 tablespoons butter
Pinch of sea salt
1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Melt butter in a saucepan.  Mix in brown sugar, cream, and salt.  Cook over medium low for 7 minutes, whisking gently.
Remove from heat, add vanilla, and stir thoroughly.  Return to heat and cook until thickened, approximately 1 minute more.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
Perfect on ice cream, cheescake, in coffee, or a hundred other ways.
