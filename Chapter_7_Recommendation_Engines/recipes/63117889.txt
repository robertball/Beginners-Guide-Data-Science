Title:
Zatarain's Red Beans and Rice Tortilla Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg Zatarain's Red Beans and Rice Mix
1 8 oz can whole kernel corn, drained
1 4 oz can chopped green chiles, undrained
6 flour tortillas (6-8 inch)
(2 cups shredded cooked chicken)
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
Toppings: sour cream, salsa, green onions etc.

Directions:
Prepare rice mix as directed on package.  Add corn and green chiles; mix well.
Spread 1/2 cup of the rice mixture on bottom of 13x9 baking dish sprayed with no stick cooking spray.  Top with 3 of the tortillas, overlapping as needed.  Layer with 1/2 each of the remaining rice mixture, (chicken) and cheese.  Repeat layering with remaining tortillas, rice mixture, chicken and cheese.
Bake in preheated 350 degree oven 15 minutes or until heated through.  Let stand 5 minutes before serving.  Serve with assorted toppings if desired.

Submitter: 
Christal Kuchera
Description: 
Turn leftover chicken into a Mexican-style casserole by layering with red beans adn rice, cheese and tortillas.  Or skip the chicken and make a great meatless Monday dish.
