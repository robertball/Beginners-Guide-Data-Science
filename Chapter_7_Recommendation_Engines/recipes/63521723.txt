Title:
Just Like Wendy's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/967110.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. olive oil
2 lbs. ground beef
2 stalks celery, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1 green pepper, chopped
3 14 oz. cans stewed tomatoes
1 10 oz. can Rotel Diced Tomatoes
1 14 oz. can tomato sauce
1 Cup water
1 14 oz. can kidney beans, including liquid
1 14 oz. can pinto beans, including liquid
2 1.25 oz packages McCormick Mild Chili Seasoning
Salt and Pepper to taste
1-2 tbsp. white vinegar to taste
Finely diced onions (optional)
Shredded cheddar cheese (optional)

Directions:
1. In a large pot, heat oil over medium high heat and press ground beef into the bottom (to make one large patty) - brown significantly, then stir crumbling the browned beef.
2. Add onions, pepper, and celery and saut√© until onions are translucent.
3. Add stewed tomatoes, Rotel tomatoes, tomato sauce and water.
4. Smash up the larger chunks of tomatoes and add the 2 packages of chili seasoning.
5. Add kidney and pinto beans
6. Salt and pepper to taste
7. Simmer uncovered for 60 minutes
8. Stir in white vinegar to taste
Top white diced onions and/or cheese.
