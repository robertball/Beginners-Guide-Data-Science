Title:
Sesame Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package Rice Noodles
6 cloves garlic, minced
4 tablespoons sugar
4 tablespoons safflower oil
4 tablespoons rice vinegar
6 tablespoons soy sauce
2 tablespoons sesame oil
2 teaspoons chili sauce (Sriracha)
6 green onions, sliced
1 teaspoon sesame seeds, toasted

Directions:
Bring a pot of water to boil. Remove from heat and add noodles, soak about 8 to 10 minutes. Drain, and transfer to a serving bowl.
Meanwhile, place a saucepan over medium-high heat. Stir in garlic, sugar, oil, vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, and chili sauce. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly, until sugar dissolves. Pour sauce over noodles, and toss to coat. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds.
Can add cooked vegetables to noodles before mixing with sauce,
