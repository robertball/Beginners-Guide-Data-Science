Title:
Carrot Cake To Die For Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups white flour
2 tablespoons baking powder
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons cinammon
Add:
2 cups granulated white sugar
1 1/2 cups oil
4 eggs
2 cups grated carrots
1 cup drained crushed pineapples (8 oz. can)
1/2 cup chopped pecans or walnuts
Frosting:
1/2 cup butter
8 oz. package cream cheese
1 pound powdered sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Mix well.
Bake at 350 degrees until toothpick comes out clean (about 45 minutes)
Let cool
Beat together frosting ingredients
Can be made as both pan (9" X 13" with high sides) and layer cake

Moist, delicious and (any) leftovers freeze well.
