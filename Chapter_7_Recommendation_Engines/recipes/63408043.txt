Title:
Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. chicken, cooked and diced
1 can water chestnuts
1 can peas
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 c. sour cream
1/2 to 3/4 c. shredded cheddar
1 sleeve Ritz crackers, crushed
1 stick butter

Directions:
Combine chicken, water chestnuts, peas, soup and sour cream and cheese.  Place in greased casserole.  Top with crackers mixed with butter.  Bake 30 min at 350 or until bubbly.
