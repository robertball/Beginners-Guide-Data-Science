Title:
Cottage Cheese Salad Recipe

Ingredients:
1 cup uncreamed cottage cheese (dry white curd)
1/4 cup homemade yogurt
1/4 cup unsweetened pineapple (fresh or canned) or other seasonal fruit

Directions:
