Title:
Summer Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can corn
1 can black beans
1 lime
1 nectarine
1 avocado
1 red bell pepper
1 jalapeno pepper
1/2 sweet onion
12 strawberries

Directions:
Pour corn and black beans into a large bowl
Add juice from the lime
Chop remaining fruits and vegetables
Stir to combine

Submitter:

Description: 
A refreshing and filling combination of fruits and vegetables that won't warm your kitchen in the summer heat.
