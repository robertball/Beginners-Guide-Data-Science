Title:
Aloo Baigan (eggplant & potato curry) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb eggplant, 1" cubes
1 lb potatoes, peeled, 1" cubes
3/4 tsp turmeric
1/2 T ground coriander
1 tsp cayenne
1 tsp salt
4 T oil
3/4 tsp fenugreek seed
1 tsp amchur powder (mango powder)

Directions:
Measure all ingredients and set aside, close at hand
Mix together the turmeric, coriander, cayenne, and salt.
Toss the eggplant cubes with the spice mixture to coat evenly.  Set aside.
Heat oil over high heat.  Add fenugreek seeds and fry about 10-15 secs, til they start to brown.  Reduce heat to med-high
Add the veggies and stir to coat, then let them fry for a couple of minutes before you start to stir again, cooking til the eggplant starts to get soft.
Reduce heat to med, cover, and cook til the veggies are done, turning occasionally as needed.
Remove cover, sprinkle on the amchur powder, and turn to coat well until any remaining moisture has evaporated.
Serve with rice, chappati, puri, or naan.
