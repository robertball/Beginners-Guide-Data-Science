Title:
Norwegian Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2879771.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 Cup granulated sugar
1 Tsp Baking powder
1/3 Tsp sea salt
1 Tsp Ground cinnamon
1/3 Tsp Ground cardamom or Nutmeg
1 Cup + 2 Tbs All-purpose flour
3 Small sized tart apples
1 Egg
3 Tbs Milk
1 Tsp Pure vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350Â°, line bottom of 8" cake pan with baking paper and then butter it.
Peal, core, apples. Dice two apples and thinly slice 1.
Stir all the ingredients together (the mixture will be similar to that of muffins).
Spread into the pan.
Bake for 30-40 minutes, or until browned and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
Cut into wedges and serve with either sweetened whipped cream  or ice cream.
