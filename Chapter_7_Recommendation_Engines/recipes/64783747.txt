Title:
Linda's Corn Bread Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 skillets of cornbread
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
water
onion (diced) (onion powder is an option if you have picky eaters)
1 dozen boiled eggs, peeled and chopped
sage
salt
pepper

Directions:
Crumble the corn bread in a large mixing bowl
Mix the cream of chicken and cream of mushroom soups
Add the chopped onion or onion powder to taste preference. Remember with onion powder: a little goes a long way!)
Add soup mixture to crumbled corn bread
Add in chopped eggs and mix well.
Add water to slightly soupy consistency.
Mix in 2 Tablespoons sage (more to taste) and salt and pepper to taste.
Pour into pan and bake at 350 until browned on top.
Freezes well!

Submitter:

Description: 
Flavorful and fail-proof corn bread dressing recipe. This an adaptation of my mother-in-law's delicious recipe.
