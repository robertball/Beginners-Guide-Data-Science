Title:
Cindy's Slow Cooker French Dip Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3lb boneless chuck roast, or any type of beef roast you have
1 packet of Au Jus dry mix
1 can of Campbells French Onion soup
1 12oz bottle of beer

Directions:
Place roast in the bottom of the slow cooker.
Sprinkle au jus powder over the top.
Pour can of onion soup over the top.
Pour the beer over.
Set on low and cook for 7-8 hours, until tender enough to pull apart.
Serve on toasted rolls and dip in the juice.

Submitter: 
Cindy in Pensacola
Description:
