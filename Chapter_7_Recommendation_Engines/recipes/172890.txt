Title:
Buttery Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1/2 cup olive oil
4 cloves garlic, sliced
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
2 loaves sweet French bread
3 tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
In a small saucepan, melt butter; add olive oil and garlic. Warm over medium heat for 1 minute. Add black pepper and oregano. Cut bread in half lengthwise and brush cut sides with butter mixture. Toast on a grill or under a broiler until golden brown. Sprinkle with parsley and serve hot.

Submitter: 
California Milk Advisory Board
Description: 
This garlic bread is made with fresh garlic, oregano and chopped parsley. Enjoy hot out of the oven with your next Italian feast or with a simple salad dinner.
