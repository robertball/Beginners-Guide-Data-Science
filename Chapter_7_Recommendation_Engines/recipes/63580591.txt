Title:
Dry Brownie Mix for gifting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup flour
1/3 cup cocoa powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking powder
To mix in later and label on the bag/glass jar:
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup canola or vegetable oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix dry and wet ingredients only until combined loosely and pour into a greased 8 x 8 or 9 x 9 pan.  Bake for 20 - 22 minutes or until inserted knife comes out clean.  Allow to cool before cutting into squares.

Submitter: 
Karen Barris Calabro
Description: 
If you're looking for inexpensive and thoughtful gifts, whether during the holidays or as a hostess gift when going to barbecues or dinner parties, this brownie mix in a nicely decorated glass Mason jar with a recipe card attached is a lovely option.
