Title:
Meatballs & Vodka Sauce Over Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/404560.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Lb. lean ground beef
3/4 C Italian breadcrumbs
1 Tbs. grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 large egg, beaten
1 (24 oz.) jar Bertolli Vodka Sauce
1/4 C milk
1 Lb. cooked noodles of your choice
Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
In a medium-sized bowl combine the meat, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, pepper and egg. Roll out about 14-16 meatballs; set aside.
Pour the sauce in a medium-sized saucepan over medium-high heat. Pour the milk in the jar and shake; pour into saucepan and stir. Heat sauce for 5 minutes, reduce heat to medium-low, then add meatballs. Cook 40-45 minutes.
Serve sauce and meatballs over warm noodles of your choice. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, if desired.
