Title:
Chocolate Ganache Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 ounces bittersweet or semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
1 cup heavy cream

Directions:
Heat the cream in a medium sauce pan over medium-low heat. Bring to the point of just beginning to boil. Take off the heat, add chocolate and whisk until smooth.

Submitter: 
www.funfamilydinners.com
Description: 
Ganache is so easy to make and so decadent that you will wonder why you ever used store-bought chocolate frosting.
