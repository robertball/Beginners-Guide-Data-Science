Title:
Tomato Ravioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cooking spray
1 lb. cherry tomatoes, divided
2 shallots cut into wedges
3 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil, divided
2 tbsp. balsamic vinegar
¼ tsp. kosher salt
¼ tsp. black pepper
12 oz. cheese ravioli
2 tbsp. chopped fresh basil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.  Spray jelly-roll pan with cooking spray.
Take ½ of the cherry tomatoes and cut them in ½.  Mix cut and whole tomatoes with shallots and arrange on pan.  Sprinkle 1 tbsp. of oil on top.  Bake in oven for 35 minutes.
Mix reaming oil, vinegar, salt and pepper together.  Mix with the tomato mixture and bake for another 10 minutes.
Cook ravioli per package instructions.   Remove ¼ cup of the water and then drain the rest of the ravioli.
Return ravioli to pan add the tomato mixture and toss.  If you wish add the reserved pasta water.
Garnish with fresh basil
