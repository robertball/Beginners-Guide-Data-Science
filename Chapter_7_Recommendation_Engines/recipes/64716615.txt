Title:
Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb Pork Loin
1/2 Stick Butter
1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
1/2 tsp  Onion Powder
1/2 tsp Old Bay
2 Pinch Black Peper

Directions:
Cut pork loin into 1 inch thick steaks
Season meeet
Heat skillet to med.
Melt butter in pan
add prk chops
cook for 10 mins then flip
cook for an addition 7 mins.
let chops rest for 7-8 mins
serve
