Title:
Dill Pickle Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. dry shell pasta (about 3 cups)
3/4 cup diced pickles
2/3 cup diced cheddar cheese
3 tbsp finely diced onion
2 tbsp fresh dill
1/2 cup pickle juice
Dressing
2/3 cup mayo
1/3 cup sour cream
4 tbsp pickle juice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil pasta, drain and rinse with cold water
Toss cold pasta with 1/2 cup pickle juice, let for 5 min. Drain and discard juice.
combine dressing ingredients and mix well.
Toss all ingredients in large bowl and refrigerate 1 hour
.
