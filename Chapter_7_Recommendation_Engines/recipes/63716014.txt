Title:
Big Ray's Adobo Fish Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1032461.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds tilapia fillets
2 tablespoons lime juice
1 teaspoon season salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon paprika
olive oil
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons lime juice
1 teaspoon olive oil
1 1/2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1 1/2 teaspoons Adobo seasoning
16 (8 inch) flour tortillas
2 cups shredded spinach
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 tomato, chopped
2 green onions, chopped

Directions:
Preheat broiler and grease broiler pan (or line with foil)
Rub tilapia fillets with olive oil and 2 tablespoons lime juice and season with salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and paprika, coating  both sides of each fillet
Combine mayonnaise, olive oil, 2 tablespoons lime juice, cilantro, and Adobo seasoning in a blender; pulse until sauce is well blended. Set aside.
Broil tilapia  until fish easily separates with a fork, about 5 minutes on each side.
Heat each flour tortilla in a skillet over medium-low heat until warm, about 1 minute. Divide fish evenly over tortillas and serve with Mayo-Adbo sauce, spinach, Monterey Jack cheese, tomatoes and green onions.
