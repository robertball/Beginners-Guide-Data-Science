Title:
Fat Free Jalapeno Ranch Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 bunch cilantro
3 tomatillos, washed & quartered
2-4 pickled jalapenos, depending on your desired heat
2 packages dry Ranch dressing mix
16 oz fat free sour cream

Directions:
Place cilantro, tomatillos, and jalapenos in a food processor or blender. Blend until smooth and set aside.
In a medium bowl, stir together sour cream and dry Ranch packages.  Whisk in about 3/4 of the blended green mixture.  Taste salad dressing & add the rest of the blended green mixture for desired heat.

Submitter: 
bbqgirl
Description: 
Cool, creamy, and spicy. Crazy good salad dressing you could also use as a dip. This recipe is a lot like the dip they serve at Chuy's, which is a restaurant we have in Texas. However, this one's FAT FREE!!
