Title:
Horseradish Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup heavy cream(whipping cream)
1/4 cup mayonaisse
1/2 cup horseradish drained well
2 Tablespoons Dijon Mustard
pinch of sugar, salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Whip cream in bowl until soft peaks form.  Combine mayonaisse, horseradish, and mustard in another bowl.  Fold in the whipped cream.  Add sugar, salt and pepper.  Stir well.  Serve. Can be made a few hours ahead and stored in fridge.

Submitter: 
Momof3girls
Description: 
My mother made this every year for St. Patricks Day.  It goes great with Corned Beef and Cabbage and works well on steak sandwiches too.
