Title:
Agua de Jamaica (Hibiscus Water) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Dried Jamaica (Hibiscus flower)
1 Stick Cinnamon
1/16 Teaspoon Cloves
1/16 Teaspoon Nutmeg
1/16 Teaspoon Allspice
1/2 Cup Chopped Piloncillo
1-1/2 Cup White Sugar (you may add more to your taste)
6 Cups Boiling Water
Cold Water

Directions:
Bring 6 cups of water to boil.  Add jamaica, cinnamon, cloves and nutmeg.  Boil on low simmer for about 30-45 minutes until a dark red.
Add the chopped piloncillo and stir constantly until disolved.  Once disolved remove from heat and let cool for about 15-20 minutes.
Place strainer over 4 qt pitcher and pour mixture through to strain flowers.  Remove strainer and add remaining cold water to fill 4 qt pitcher and sugar.  Stir until dissolved.
Serve immediately or let stand overnight for best taste.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This drink is served throughout Mexico.  It is nice and refreshing.  It is similiar to a tart cranberry tea drink.  Enjoy!
