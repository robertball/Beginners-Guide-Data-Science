Title:
Simple Slow Cooked Korean Beef Soft Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3lb beef chuck roast, trimmed
1/2 cup dark brown sugar, packed
1/3 cup soy sauce
10 cloves garlic
1/2 onion, diced
1-inch section of fresh ginger, peeled and grated (optional)
2 tbsp seasoned rice vinegar
1 tbsp sesame oil
1 jalapeno, diced (optional)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place all ingredients in slow cooker.
Cover and cook on high for 8 hrs, or 10 hours on low, or until the meat is tender enough to shred with a fork. Shred the beef and mix with the juices.
Serve with your favorite corn tortillas and toppings.
