Title:
Slow Cooker Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork chops
16 ounces sauerkraut with juice or red potatoes
1 or 2 apples, peeled and sliced
dry onion soup mix
brown sugar

Directions:
Place the chops in the bottom the slow cooker and pour the sauerkraut over the top. Cook on low for 8 to 9 hours or on high for 4 to 5 hours.

Submitter: 
MaxMom
Description:
