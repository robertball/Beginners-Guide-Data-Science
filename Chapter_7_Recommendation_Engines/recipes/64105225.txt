Title:
low carb chocolate fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1246417.jpg
Ingredients:
5-6oz unsweetened baking chocolate, melted
16oz cream cheese, softened
1-1 1/2C sugar substitute

Directions:
in your blender, beat the softened cream cheese.
add in your sugar substitute.
add melted chocolate
mix until well blended.
pour into a glass or metal dish lined with parchment paper.
refrigerate 3-4 hours, cut and serve.

Submitter: 
Mama Wallace
Description: 
One of my first low-carb desserts I have made. Although it was different, it certainly was yummy!
