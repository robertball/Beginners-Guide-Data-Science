Title:
Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/714822.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. spaghetti
1 Tbls. olive oil
3 strips thick-cut bacon, cut in strips (or pancetta)
1/4 c. onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, diced
1/2 tsp. crushed red pepper
1 tsp. smoked paprika
1 egg
1/2 c. parmesan cheese, shredded
1/4 c. half & half
1/2 c. reserved pasta water

Directions:
Cook pasta in boiling water.
While pasta is cooking, add bacon to oil and cook until it starts to get crisp. Remove with a slotted spoon and set aside.
Add onion and saut√© 3-4 minutes. Add crushed red pepper and smoked paprika, then add garlic. Saute 1 minute.
In small bowl or cup, crack egg and beat. Add parmesan cheese with half & half and whisk together. Set aside.
Add bacon back to hot pan with onion and garlic. Add pasta and stir vigorously until well coated, then add egg/cheese mixture and stir continuously until fully incorporated, adding reserved pasta water a little at a time.
Garnish with fresh parsley.
