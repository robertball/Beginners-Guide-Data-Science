Title:
"Silli" Chicken! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package good quality fusilli
2-3 Tablespoons olive oil
1 whole chicken breast, cut into bite sized pieces
2-3 cloves minced garlic
1 Tablespoon fennel seeds
1 can artichoke hearts
1 portobello mushroom, cut into bite sized pieces
1 pint cherry or grape tomatoes
1-2 Tablespoons lemon juice
A handful of assorted fresh herbs, chopped (I used oregano, basil, marjoram, and Italian Parsley)
Sea salt (applewood smoked gives it a great flavor)  and Fresh ground pepper to taste
Parmesan cheese and red pepper flakes for garnish

Directions:
Cook fusilli pasta according to package directions
In a large skillet, brown chicken in olive oil and turn heat down
Add garlic, fennel, artichoke hearts, mushrooms,  tomatoes, and lemon juice and simmer about 15 minutes until tomatoes begin to break down
Add fresh herbs and cook another 5 minutes
Mix in fusilli
Serve with parmesan cheese and crushed red pepper to taste

Submitter: 
Elizabeth
Description: 
Fresh herbs, fusilli pasta and chicken with artichoke hearts...YUM!  My kids loved this because the pasta is so fun!   A nice light dinner on a hot summer night!
