Title:
Texas Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beek, buffalo, or pork
1 large green bell pepper chopped
1 large red bell pepper chopped
1 sweet vidalia/mayan onion chopped
3 roma tomatoes chopped
2 jalapeno peppers finely minced
1 tsp fresh cilantro
salt and pepper to taste
one recipe corn bread (trust me)
shredded cheddar and jack cheese
1 TBSP olive oil

Directions:
Heat oil over medium heat
Sautee bell pepper & onions til aldente
Remove from skillet and set aside.
Add ground meat and cook thuroughly.
Return pepper and onions, add jalapenos
Sautee over medium heat 2 minutes.
Add tomatoes and reduce heat to low.
Simmer 15-20 minutes
Serve over cornbread and top with cheeses

Submitter: 
Karen D.
Description: 
I invented this recipe when I was pregnant with my son; I wanted chili, but not soupy chili. I use ground beef, buffalo, or pork depending on my meat mood; fresh veggies, too. I serve it over sweet cornbread, it's awesome.
