Title:
Whole Wheat Oatmeal Preserve Filled Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup quick oats
1 cup soured milk
2 eggs
1 1/4 cup whole wheat flour
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp cumin
(1/4 cup flax seed)
4 Tbs apricot (or other) preserves

Directions:
Mix oatmeal and soured milk. Add eggs. Combined next dry ingredients and seeds if added. Use paper liners or vegetable oil spray on 12 muffin cups. Fill each muffin cup halfway. Spoon 1 1/2 tsp preserves on to each muffin and fill to top with remaining batter. Bake 20 min @ 375 degrees

Colleen Smith

Healthier and tasty version to replace yummy quick breads.
