Title:
3 + 2 = 1 CAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 oz.) box Angel Food Cake mix
1 (16.5 oz.) box Cake mix, any flavor
2 Tbsp. water

Directions:
In a resealable plastic bag, combine the two cake mixes together, mixing well.
In a small microwave safe bowl, measure out 3 tablespoons of the cake mixture, mix with 2 tablespoons of water, cook on HIGH for 1 minute and you have your own individual little cake. Garnish with a dollop of whipped cream and/or fresh fruit.
Keep the remaining cake mixture sealed in the sealed plastic bag.

Submitter: 
lgmarge
Description: 
Try mixing various flavors of cake mix with the angel food mix. Chocolate, lemon, orange, pineapple, red velvet... the flavor possibilities are endless.
