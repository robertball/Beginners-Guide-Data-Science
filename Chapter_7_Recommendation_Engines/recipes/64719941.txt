Title:
Beef Barly Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5885990.jpg
Ingredients:
Left over Roast Beef
8 sprigs fresh Parsley, plus 3 tablespoons chopped
4 sprigs fresh Thyme
1 Bay leaf
2 tablespoons Tomato Paste
2 tablespoons unsalted Butter
1 1/2 pound Leeks, white and light green parts sliced 1/2-inch thick and washed thoroughly
2 Carrots, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch pieces
2 Celery ribs, cut into 1/4-inch pieces
1 Onion, chopped
1/3 cup Red Wine
2 teaspoons Soy Sauce
5 cups Beef stock
1 cup Water
1 cup Vegetable stock
2 teaspoons Salt
Salt and Pepper to taste
1 clove Garlic, peeled and smashed
3/4 cups Pearl Barley

Directions:
Using kitchen twine, tie together parsley sprigs, thyme, and bay leaf.
Melt butter in large Dutch oven over medium heat. Add leeks, carrots, celery, onion, wine, soy sauce, and 2 teaspoons salt. Cook, stirring occasionally, until liquid ha evaporated and celery is softened, about 10 minutes.
Add broths, water, barley, herb bundle, tomato paste, and garlic; increase heat to high and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, partially covered, for 25 minutes.
Cook until barley is tender, about 18-20 minutes.
Remove pot from heat and remove herb bundle. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
