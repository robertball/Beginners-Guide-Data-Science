Title:
Whole Wheat Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Whole Wheat flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup margarine
1 cup Splenda or sugar substitute equal to one cup sugar
2 eggs
5 medium overripe bananas, mashed
TIP: if your bananas are not overripe enough, place them in a brown paper bag (lunch or grocery) and set them on your counter for a day or two.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray 9x5 inch loaf pan with baking spray.
In a large bowl, sift together first three ingredients.
In a separate medium bowl, cream margarine and Splenda together.  Add eggs and blend.
Add mashed bananas and blend well
Stir banana mixture into flour mixture until just moist, do not over mix! Pour into loaf pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 55-65 min until done. Let cool and enjoy!
