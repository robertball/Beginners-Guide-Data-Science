Title:
Seafood chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped onion
1 clove minced garlic
1/4 tsp dill weed
1 can cream of celery soup
1 can cream of potato soup
1 1/2 soup cans milk
1/2 lb medium shrimp, shelled and deveined.
1/2 lb firm white fish fillets, cut into 2" pieces

Directions:
In 3 quart saucepan over medium heat, cook onion, garlic and dill until onion is tender, stirring occasionally.
Stir in soups and milk. Heat to boiling, stirring often. Cook 8 minutes. Reduce heat to low.
Add shrimp and fish. Cook 5 minutes more or until shrimp turn pink and opaque, and fish flakes easily.
