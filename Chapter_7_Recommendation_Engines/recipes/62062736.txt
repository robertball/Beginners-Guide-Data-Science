Title:
Peachy Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups pancake mix
16 oz. sausage links, browned
16 oz. can sliced peaches, in syrup
1/4 cup sugar
1 tbsp. cornstarch
3/4 cup maple pancake syrup
1 tbsp. margarine

Directions:
1. Prepare pancake mix according to directions but use only 1 cup of liquid.
2. Pour batter into a greased 9 x 13 baking dish.
3. Drain peaches, reserving 1/2 cup of syrup. Set aside.
4. Cut sausages in half and peaches in chunks. Arrange on top of batter.
5. Bake at 350* for 30 minutes.
Peach syrup:
Combine sugar and cornstarch in saucepan. Stir in reserved peach syrup. Cook and stir until thick and bubbly. Mix in maple pancake syrup and margarine. Serve syrup over warm over hot breakfast squares.

Submitter: 
Melissa Kosswig
Description: 
A yummy breakfast casserole that includes peaches and sausage links. Easy to make and delicious!
