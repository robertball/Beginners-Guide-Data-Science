Title:
Cucumber Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium tomatoes, sliced and quartered
6 large cucumber, peeled and sliced
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
3/4 cup canola oil
1/2 cup white vinegar

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the tomatoes, cucumber. In a small bowl,combine salt and pepper. Whisk in the vinegar and water. Pour over vegetables and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Anne Soie
