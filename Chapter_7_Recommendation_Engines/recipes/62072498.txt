Title:
Spinach Ranch Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups baby spinach
1/2 cup cucumber
1 cup brocoli florets, uncooked
1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
1/4 red onion, chopped
2 slices chicken breast, cut into small pieces
1/2 cup low-fat or plain Ranch dressing
preferred amount of bacon bits

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl, except the dressing, then when ready to serve, add dressing and toss.

Submitter: 
JULICHICKY
Description: 
Unsual combination I came up with one night while preparing my lunch for the next day. Was scared to try it but it turned out to be good! You could replace any ingredient to make it your own.
