Title:
Chicken Flautas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tbsp Butter
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup fine diced onion
1 tbsp oil
1/4 tsp Paprika
1/8 tsp Pepper
1/8 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups cooked, Pulled apart Chicken
2 tbsp Lemon Juice
1/4 cup chopped black olives
8 Flour tortillas
oil for frying
toothpicks

Directions:
In frying pan melt butter
add flour mix together, add onions saute.
Add oil, paprika,pepper, salt, lemon juice, black olives.
Heat for about 2 minutes.
Add chicken, remove from heat and cover.
Heat oil in deep skillet. Fill tortillas, roll up tight use toothpick to keep in place.
Fry for about 2 minutes or till just browned.
Serve with Guacamole or sour cream and cheese.
