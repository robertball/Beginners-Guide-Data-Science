Title:
Chicken Cordon Bleu Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1683032.jpg
Ingredients:
½	cup chicken broth
½	cup sour cream
1	(12 oz.) jar chicken gravy
5	oz. (3 cups) uncooked egg noodles, bowtie, or other pasta.
½	cup diced cooked ham
½	teaspoon paprika
¼	teaspoon pepper
2	to 3 cups chopped or shredded leftover cooked chicken
½	teaspoon seasoned salt
8	slices Swiss cheese, halved

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 F.  In large bowl, combine broth, sour cream and gravy; mix well with wire whisk.  Stir in uncooked noodles, ham, paprika and 1/8 teaspoon of the pepper. Spoon into ungreased shallow 2-quart casserole.
Bake covered at 375 F.  For 30 minutes.
Uncover casserole; Sprinkle chopped cooked chicken over top of casserole; bake 20 minutes or until chicken is warmed thgourh, and noodles are tender and thoroughly heated.
Place 4 to 5 cheese slices over top of casserole (covering top of casserole).  Bake an additional 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.
