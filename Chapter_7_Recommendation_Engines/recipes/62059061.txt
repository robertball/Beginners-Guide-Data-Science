Title:
Scallops and Roasted Red Pepper Sauce Served over Linguini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs	Sea Scallops (dry).
1/4 lb		Prosciutto,  julienned
2 10 oz. jars	Sweet* roasted red peppers
1 28oz can	Tomatoes
Extra virgin olive oil
2-4 tbs		Butter
1 cup		Dry white wine (or poultry/beef stock)
1		Shallot, chopped
2 cloves	Garlic
Flat leaf parsley
6 large leaves	Basil
1/2cup +	Parmigiano reggiano (finely shredded / grated for garnish)
1/2 cup		Sour cream
Salt & pepper
* If using regular (non-sweet) roasted red peppers, 1/2 - 1 tbs of sugar may be added to sauce mixture to reduce acidity from tomatoes.

Directions:
Puree both jars of roasted red peppers, tomatoes, shredded parmigian, basil and 2 cloves garlic in  a food processor or blender.
Water to boil for pasta.
Over medium heat and 1 1/2 tbs oil to large pan and 1/2 cup finely chopped shallots.  Cook until lighlty browned,  1-2 minutes.  Add proscuitto, cook additional 1-2 minutes.  Add wine (or stock), remove any proscuitto bits from bottom of pan, increase heat to med-high and reduce liquid to half.  Add pureed red pepper mixture.  Low simmer for 15-20 minutes. (Or when pasta and scallops are done).  Salt and pepper to taste.  **Just before serving, stir in sour cream and chopped parsley**
Add linguini to boiling water. Cook to package directions.
Cook Scallops (Two batches)
Rinse scallops, cut extra large scallops in half and place in bowl. Season with salt & pepper.
Heat large saut√© pan over med-high heat until hot.  Add ~ 1 tbs butter to coat bottom.  When butter has slightly browned, add enough scallops (one at a time) to fill pan but be sure sides of scallops do not touch.  Cook until browned on bottom.  (Depending upon size of scallops, time will be approximately 1-2 minutes).  Turn scallops one at a time to brown opposite side.  Cook to medium-rare; all but middle-third of scallop is opaque. Place scallops on dish and repeat with remaining scallops.
Rinse linguini.  In large bowl mix linguini with red pepper sauce.  Top with scallops and freshly grated parmigian. Bon Appetito!
