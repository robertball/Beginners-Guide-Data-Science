Title:
Basil Mint Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 tsp. yeast (1 pkg.)
1/2 tsp. salt
3 cups whole wheat flour
1/2 cup quick cooking oats
1 cup Greek yogurt
1 egg
3 T butter, melted
2 T honey
1/4 cup firmly packed chopped fresh basil
1/4 cup firmly packed chopped fresh mint
2 T lemon juice
grated zest of 2 lemons

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.  Knead on floured surface, dough might stay sticky!
Let rise until doubled in greased bowl.  Punch down,
Press into greased angel food or Bundt pan.  When doubled, bake in 350 oven for 45-55 minutes.

Submitter: 
sueb
Description: 
Heavy, hearty bread with plenty of fresh herb flavor!
