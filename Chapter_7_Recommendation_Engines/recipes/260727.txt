Title:
Kahlua® Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 cups confectioners' sugar
1 cup butter, softened
3 tablespoons coffee-flavored liqueur (such as Kahlua®)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine confectioners' sugar, butter, coffee-flavored liqueur, and vanilla extract in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment. Beat until frosting is smooth and fluffy, 3 to 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
*DW*
Description: 
A tasty (and boozy) version of frosting! Great on cakes or cupcakes.
