Title:
Coffee Cake with Fruit Pie Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1 3/4 cups sugar
2. 1 cup margarine
3. 4 beaten eggs
4. 3 cups flour
5. 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
6. 1 teaspoon vanilla ( or almond with cherry filling) flavoring
7. 1 can fruit pie filling ( apple is what I use)or cherry
8. 1/4 cup powdered sugar ( optional)

Directions:
Cream together 1. 2. add 3. then mix...add 4. 5. 6....set aside 2 cups of the batter..
Spread rest of  mix on 10x15" jelly roll pan Greased....Spoon 7. over pan then 2 cups of mix you had set aside on top of filling
Bake 350* 25-30 min
remove from oven let cool 15 min and sprinkle with powder sugar if you wish
