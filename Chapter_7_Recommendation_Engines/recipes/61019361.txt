Title:
Baked Rice & Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white rice
1 cup water
1 cup milk
1/4 cup milk
1 egg
4 apples, peeled & sliced
Cinnamon
Brown Sugar

Directions:
Boil rice in 1 cup milk and 1 cup water until done.
Line bottom of greased casserole dish with rice. Add alternate layers of apple slices topped with sugar and cinnamon and layers of rice.
Beat the egg with 1/4 cup milk, and pour over rice and apple mixture to moisten.
Cover and bake at 350F for 30-40 minutes, until apples are soft.

Submitter: 
Jenny Deveau
Description: 
Homemade rice & apple recipe. Serve with a vanilla sauce.
