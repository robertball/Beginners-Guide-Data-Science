Title:
Boston Cream Pie Donuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dough:
3/4 C Milk
2 1/2 Tbsp Shortening
1 Pkg Instant Yeast
3 Tbsp Warm Water
1 Large Egg, beaten
2 Tbsp Sugar
3/4 Tsp Salt
2 1/4 C All-Purpose Flour
.
Pastry Cream:
1 1/2 C Half & Half
1/2 Tsp Vanilla Extract
1/2 C Sugar
2 Large Eggs
1 Large Egg Yolk
2 Tbsp All-Purpose Flour
.
Chocolate Glaze:
2 C Confectioners' Sugar (sifted)
1/2 C Unsalted Butter
1/4 C Whole Milk, warmed
1 Tbsp Light Corn Syrup
1 1/2 Tsp Vanilla Extract
4 Oz Dark Chocolate, chopped

Directions:
Dough:
Heat the milk in sauce pan . Be careful not to singe.
Place the shortening in medium bowl
Add the warmed milk to shortening and whisk until melted. Set aside, cool to lukewarm.
In a large bowl, combine yeast, water, and shortening mixture.
Add the eggs, sugar, salt, and half of the flour. Combine and mix until well combined.
Add the remaining flour and mix until dough pulls away from the bowl.
Turn dough out onto floured surface
Knead until smooth and elastic.
Transfer to a well-oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
On a well-floured surface, gently press out dough to 3/8-inch thick.
Cut out dough using a 2 1/2-inch doughnut cutter or pastry ring.
Set on floured baking sheet, cover lightly with a towel, and let rise for 30 minutes.
Preheat the oil in a deep fryer or Dutch oven to 365 degrees F. Gently place the doughnuts into oil.
Fry until golden brown on both sides
Transfer to a cooling rack
Allow to cool for 15 to 20 minutes prior to glazing.
.
Cream:
In a heavy saucepan, scald half & half with vanilla. Remove from heat.
In a medium bowl whisk sugar, eggs, egg yolk and flour
Whisk a small amount of the hot half & half into the egg mixture to prevent the eggs from cooking.
Add the tempered eggs into the half & half and whisk.
Return saucepan to stovetop
On medium heat, SLOWLY bring mixture to a boil
Remove from heat. Transfer to a glass bowl and cover w plastic wrap
Refrigerate to set
.
Glaze:
Using a fine-mesh sieve, sift icing sugar into a small bowl; set aside
Combine butter, milk, corn syrup, and vanilla in a medium saucepan and heat over medium heat until butter melts
Decrease the heat to low, add the chocolate, and whisk
Turn off heat, add confectionersâ€™ sugar, whisk until smooth
