Title:
Fat Free Balsamic Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1055352.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup white vinegar (regular or rice wine)
1/4 cup water
1 Tbsp garlic powder
1 Tbsp onion powder
1/4 tsp dried thyme
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Whisk all ingredients together. Pour over your choice of meat or produce. Refrigerate for 1-4 hours.

Submitter: 
Lee Crowell
Description: 
A quick and easy marinade that packs a lot of flavor, perfect if you love balsamic vinegar. Great with chicken, steak, shrimp, and tofu, or even try your favorite vegetables. You'll never miss the oil!
