Title:
Simple cornish hens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1078647.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cornish hens
1 cup of brown sugar
1/4 cup of garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon of turmeric

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.  Mix dry ingredients in bowl.  Rub each hen inside and out with mixture.  Bake breast side up for 1 to 1/2 hours basting with pan drippings every 30 minutes. (internal temp needs to be 165 for safe consumption)

Submitter: 
Shonna
Description: 
Cornish hen recipe using only 3 ingredients.  Great base recipe.
