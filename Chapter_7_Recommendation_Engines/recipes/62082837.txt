Title:
coconut french toast with tropical fruit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf French bread, sliced in 1-inch slices
1 1/4 cups light coconut milk
1 1/4 cups Egg Beaters
1/2 cup sugar + 3 tbsp sugar
1 tbsp vanilla extract
1 cup flaked coconut
2 tbsp lime juice
3 mangoes
1 papaya

Directions:
1.  Combine coconut milk, Egg Beaters, sugar, vanilla and coconut in a bowl.
2.  Dip each slice of bread in mixture and place in a single layer in a 13 x 9 inch baking dish or jelly roll pan (coat with cooking spray first).  Cover and refrigerate for 8 hours or overnight.
3.  Mix mango, papaya, 3 tbsp sugar and lime juice in a bowl, cover and refrigerate for 8 hours or overnight.
4.  Preheat oven to 350.
5.  Remove bread from refrigerator and uncover.  Let stand at room temperature for 15 minutes.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
6.  Serve warm with tropical fruit mixture.

Submitter: 
michelle o'c
Description: 
Prepare this the day before you plan to eat it, let it set up in the fridge overnight and you have a tasty breakfast treat!
