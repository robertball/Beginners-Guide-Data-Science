Title:
Masala Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large potatoes
1/4 C. vegetable oil
2 tsp. mustard seeds
1 tsp. ginger root, finely chopped
4 oz. diced green chilies
1/2 tsp ground coriander
1/4 tsp turmeric
1 T. curry powder
2 tsp. salt
1 T. lemon juice
2 T. cilantro, minced
1 C. plain yogurt

Directions:
Place potatoes in a large sauce pan. Cover with boiling water. Cover pot.
Simmer for 30 minutes or until potatoes are tender. Remove from pan. Cool slightly. Peel and cut into 1 inch cubes. Set aside. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet. Add mustard seeds. Cook over low heat until they start to pop. Add chilies, coriander, ginger, tumeric, curry powder and salt. Cook over low heat stirring for 1 minute. Add potatoes and toss well to coat. Sprinkle with lemon juice and cilantro.
Stir yogurt into potatoes.
