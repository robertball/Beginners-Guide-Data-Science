Title:
Savory Couscous Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup dry couscous
1 1/4 cup chicken stock or water
1 Tbsp olive oil
1/8 tsp salt
1 clove minced garlic
1 large plum tomato, chopped
1/4 cup fresh chopped oregano
1/4 cup fresh grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Bring stock, olive oil, salt, garlic and chopped tomato to a boil. Stir in couscous, cover and turn off heat. Let stand for 5 minutes. Add cheese and fluff with fork.

Submitter: 
EAKE
Description: 
Such a lovely and quick alternative to rice or potatoes. Can be served hot or at room temperature.
