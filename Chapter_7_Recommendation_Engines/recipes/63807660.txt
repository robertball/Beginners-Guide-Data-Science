Title:
Chicken Zoodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1056297.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil or butter
3 minced cloves of garlic
1 cup diced onions
1 cup diced celery
1 cup sliced carrots
5 (14.5 ounce) cans of low sodium chicken broth
3/4 pound cooked chicken breast cut into bite sized pieces
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1 pinch dried thyme (optional)
3 medium sized zucchini cut into zoodles (see footnotes)

Directions:
In a large stock pot heat olive oil.  Cook onion and celery in olive oil until just tender, approximately 5 minutes. Pour in chicken broth and stir in chicken, carrots, basil, oregano, thyme, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes before serving. Place desired amount of zoodles in a soup bowl. Ladle hot soup over the zoodles and serve immediately.

Description: 
With the winter months approaching fast my niece and I co-conspired to come up with this warm and comforting soup using zoodles that's easy on the waistline.
