Title:
Caryn's Best Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3434061.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c sugar
1 c brown sugar
2 c shortening
2 eggs (beaten)
2 tbsp cream of tarter (up to 4 tbsp for crispy outside)
4 tbsp baking soda
2 tsp vanilla
1 tsp salt
2 c semi sweet chocolate chips
4 1/2 c flour

Directions:
In a large bowl, cream sugars and shortening together.
Press this mixture to the side of the bowl.
In the empty side, add eggs, cream of tarter, baking soda, vanilla, and salt and blend this mixture til frothy. Then blend with sugar and shortening mixture.
Add chocolate chips.
Add flour, and blend in flour about a cup at a time.
Bake sheets of cookies at 325 degrees for 13-15 minutes.
