Title:
Granny Blue Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Granny Smith apples, cored and chopped
3-5 c thinly shredded green cabbage
4 ounces blue cheese, crumbled
1 c sour cream
1/4 c grated cheddar cheese
4 T finely chopped green onion
1 1/2 T fresh lemon juice
1 1/4 T red wine vinegar
1/2 t salt
1/2 t black pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large salad bowl.
Toss well until cabbage and apple are thoroughly coated with the dressing.
Cover and chill 4 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Cat Lady Cyndi
Description: 
This salad is a nice alternative from the basic coleslaw. The tartness of the apple and boldness of the blue cheese really make this stand out. This is especially tasty served along side shredded barbeque or Buffalo chicken sandwiches.
