Title:
Pork Tenderloin with Creamy Mustard Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb pork tenderloin
salt and pepper
1t vegetable oil
1/2 c evaporated milk
2T dijon mustard
2-3 green onions, sliced

Directions:
Cut pork into 1" thick slices.
Place pork between two pieces of plastic wrap.
Flatten using mallet and season with salt and pepper.
Heat oil in large skillet.
Add half of pork and cook on each side for 2 minutes.
Remove and set aside, keep warm.
Repeat with remaining pork.
Reduce heat to low.
Add milk, and stir to loosen brown bits.
Stir in mustard and onions.
Return meat to skillet.
Cook 1-2 minutes until sauce is slightly thickened.
