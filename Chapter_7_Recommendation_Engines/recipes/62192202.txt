Title:
Parma rosa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 quarts water
2 boxes angel hair
2 tsp. garlic salt
1/4 cp. butter
1 6oz. can tomato paste
1 tsp. garlic salt
3/4 cp. cream
1/2 cp. Parmesan

Directions:
Put the eight quarts of water in a large pot.turn eye to high
When boiling add angel hair pasta and garlic salt.
Boil for 5 minutes then drain.
Put pasta back in pot and add remaining ingredients and stir to combine.

Submitter:

Description: 
My mom found this recipe years ago and tweaked it. When we make this we don't make side dishes and we still can't finish the pot!
