Title:
easy cheesy potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package hash browns
16oz shredded sharp cheddar cheese
8oz sour cream
8oz Velveeta cheese cubed
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1/2 stick butter cubed

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
In 13x9 cake pan, mix sour cream and cream soup.
Stir in 8oz shredded cheese, cubed velveeta cheese, cubed butter.
Stir in hash browns.
After everything is mixed together smooth out and cover with remaining 8oz shredded cheese.
Bake uncovered 45min

Submitter: 
Dominic Trapp
Description: 
Made these for a potluck with friends.  Went over wonderful! The pan was empty!
