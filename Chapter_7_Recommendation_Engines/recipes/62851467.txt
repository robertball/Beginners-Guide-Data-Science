Title:
Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 cup diced onion
2 tsp minced garlic
2 - 28oz cans tomato sauce
1 - 12oz can tomato paste
6 oz water
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
2 tsp italian seasoning
2 tsp oregano
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2 Tbl sugar

Directions:
Brown ground beef and onion in pot until beef is no longer pink, add garlic and cook for 1 minute.  Drain fat.   Add tomato sauce and paste.  Fill tomato paste can half full with water, run water into each tomato can to rinse, pour water into pot, stir.  Add cheese and additional seasonings, bring to boil.  Turn pot down to low and simmer covered for one hour, sirring occasionally.
