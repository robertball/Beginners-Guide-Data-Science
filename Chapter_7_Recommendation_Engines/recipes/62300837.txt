Title:
Spicy Shrimp Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds large shrimp, peeled and deveined
1/2 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
4 green onions chopped
1/2 cup white wine vinegar
i cup oil
4 teaspoons red pepper flakes
2 teaspoons salt
Pepper to taste
1 Tablespoon lemon juice
8 Tablespoon Dijon mistard

Directions:
Cook shrimp in boiling water until pink. Drain and transfer to large bowl. Combine remaining ingredients and pour over warm shrimp. Toss well so thst every shrinp is coated.  Cover and refrigerate over night
