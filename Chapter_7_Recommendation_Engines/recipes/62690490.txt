Title:
What the ELLE...Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium tart apples, peeled and sliced
2 tablespoons water
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons sugar
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 tablespoons apple sauce

Directions:
Place apples in two greased 10-oz. custard cups; sprinkle with water. In a bowl, combine the flour, sugar and cinnamon. cut in applesauce until mixture is crumbly. Sprinkle over apples. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 30-35 minutes or until tender. Serve warm.
