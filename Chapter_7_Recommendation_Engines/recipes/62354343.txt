Title:
Green Mango Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 green mangoes (firm and unripened, each about fist-sized)
1 medium red onion
1 medium red bell pepper
1/4 cup mint, chopped
1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
1 thai chili pepper (also called bird's eye chili)
2 tablespoon fish sauce
1 teaspoon sugar
1/2 cup dry roasted peanuts

Directions:
Remove skin from mangoes, remove pith and cut flesh into matchsticks.
Thinly slice red onion and red bell pepper.  Cut open thai chili pepper lengthwise, scrape seeds off and cut into strips.
In bowl, mix together mangoes, red onion, red bell pepper, thai chili pepper, mint and cilantro.
In small bowl, stir together sugar and fish sauce until dissolved, then pour over salad.  Marinate in refrigerator, at least 2 hours.
Top with peanuts and a squirt of lime juice just before serving.
