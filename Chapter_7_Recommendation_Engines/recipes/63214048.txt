Title:
Kim's New England Clam Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bacon, diced
1 1/2 cups chopped onion
1 1/2 cups water
4 cups peeled and cubed potatoes
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
ground black pepper to taste
3 cups light cream
1 stick of butter
2 or 3 (10 ounce) cans minced clams

Directions:
Place diced bacon in large pot over medium-high heat. Cook until almost crisp; add onions, and cook 5 minutes.
Stir in water and potatoes, and season with salt and pepper.
Bring to a boil, and cook uncovered for 15 minutes, or until potatoes are fork tender.
Pour in light cream, and add butter.
Drain clams, reserving clam liquid; stir clams and 1/2 of the clam liquid into the soup.
Cook for about 60 minutes. Do not allow to boil.
