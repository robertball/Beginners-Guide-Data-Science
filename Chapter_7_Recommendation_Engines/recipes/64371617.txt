Title:
Spring Herb Hummus Vegetable Garden Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans chickpeas
1/2 cup tahini
1/2 tsp salt
zest and juice from 1 lemon
1 garlic clove, peeled
1 cup fresh assorted herbs (basil, tarragon, parsley, chives, mint, etc.)
1/2 cup water
assorted vegetables such as; carrots, sugar snap peas, asparagus, radish, celery, broccoli, etc.

Directions:
1. Combine the chickpeas, tahini, salt, zest and lemon juice, garlic, herbs and water in a blender.
2. Blend until smooth. Taste and add more salt or lemon juice if youâ€™d like.
3. Spread the hummus in a baking dish that is at least 2-inches deep.
4. Arrange the vegetables in the dip in neat rows to resemble a garden.
This dish can be made up to 1 day in advance. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Ashley Baron Rodriguez
Description: 
Vegetable platters need not look all look the same. Here, fresh Spring vegetables sit in an herb-laced hummus to resemble a Spring garden in full bloom.
