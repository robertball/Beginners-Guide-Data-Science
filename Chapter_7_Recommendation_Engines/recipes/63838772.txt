Title:
Pancit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1066612.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package thin rice noodles
1/2 cup soy sauce
pepper to taste
1/2 medium head cabbage, shredded
2 carrots, shredded
2 green onions, chopped into 1 inch pieces
1 1/2 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined

Directions:
Place the rice noodles in a large bowl, and cover with warm water.
When soft, cut into 4 inch lengths, drain, and set aside.
Saute the cabbage and carrots until tender.
Season with soy sauce and pepper.
Stir in the noodles, green onions and shrimp.
Cook until shrimp is pink about 5 to 10 minutes.
