Title:
Mom's Lentil Soup Recipe

1 carrot sliced thin
2 stalks celery
1 clove garlic
onion
ham bone
salt
pepper
chili powder
and cayenne pepper to taste
1 can 15 oz. can diced tomatoes

Directions:
Put all in a stock pot and cover with water. Boil until beggies are tender.
