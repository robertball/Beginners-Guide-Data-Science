Title:
Banana Pudding Crunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/603051.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cold milk
2 (3.4 ounce) packages instant vanilla pudding mix
1/2 cup sour cream
2 medium firm bananas. sliced
1 cup sugar
1 cup chopped pecans
1 egg, beaten

Directions:
In a bowl, combine milk, pudding mix and sour cream; whisk until mixture begins to thicken, about 1 minute. Fold in bananas. Pour into a 1-1/2-qt. serving bowl. Cover and refrigerate.
For topping, combine sugar, pecans and egg; spoon onto a greased 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees F for 20 minutes or until browned and crunchy. Cool. Using a spatula, loosen pecan mixture from pan and break into a small pieces. Sprinkle over pudding just before serving.

Submitter: 
Joanie  Ward
Description: 
Field editor Joanie Ward of Brownsburg, Indiana gives a twist to a traditional comfort food by adding a crunchy meringue-nut topping. It's a dressed-up dessert that's so easy to prepare.
