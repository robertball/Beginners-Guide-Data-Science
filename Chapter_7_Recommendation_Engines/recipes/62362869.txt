Title:
Grilled Asparagus with Lemon Caper Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1# fresh asparagus, trimmed
1T olive oil vinaigrette dressing (recipe below)
2T pinenuts, toasted (roast in dry frypan until golden)
1/4C shaved Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Brush asparagus with olive oil, and season with salt.
Grill over med.-high heat, turning often, until asparagus are just tender, about 8-10 minutes
Transfer asparagus to a warm plate. Drizzle vinaigrette over asparagus
Sprinkle with pinenuts and top with Parmesan cheese
Vinaigrette Dressing
2T finely chopped shallots
2T lemon zest
2T fresh lemon juice
1T drained capers
1T choppd flat-leaf parsley
3T EVOO
salt and pepper
Mix shallots, lemon zest and juice, capers, and parsley in small bowl.
Whisk in olive oil and season to taste with salt and pepper. Set aside
