Title:
Spinach and Garbanzo Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/597117.jpg
Ingredients:
5 Tbsp cooking oil (preferably olive)
4 cloves garlic, peeled and finely sliced
3 medium-sized ripe tomatoes, chopped
1 Tbsp paprika
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1 qt water
1 double cube vegetable bouillon, crumbled
1 bunch fresh spinach, washed and chopped
4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch chunks
2 cans garbanzos (chick peas), drained
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. In a large pot, over med-low heat, warm the garlic in the oil, stirring until fragrant, a few seconds. Add tomatoes, paprika, and cumin. Saut√© until tomato is tender, about ten minutes.
2. Add water, bouillon, spinach, potatoes, and garbanzos, covering and increasing heat. Once the boiling point is reached, lower the heat to simmer. Let the stew simmer 10 minutes, or until potatoes are tender. Serve hot.
