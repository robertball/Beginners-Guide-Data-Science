Title:
Rolled Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 asparagus spears
1/8 inch slice prosciutto
1/8 inch slice Fontina cheese, no skin
1/2 lemon
1/2 stick of butter
salt and pepper

Directions:
In a small microwave safe oval baker, Layer Fontina cheese ontop of Prosciutto.  Put asparagus spears ontop and roll up.  Put 1/2 stick of butter ontop and squeeze lemon juice.  Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cover with Saran Wrap and  microwave for 4-5 minutes until asparagus is cooked.  Garnish with parmesan cheese ontop, and serve with a loaf of Italian Bread...

Submitter: 
GOZMURPH
Description: 
Asparagus rolled in prosciutto and fontina cheese.  Excellent appetizer!  Serve with your favorite Italian Bread.
