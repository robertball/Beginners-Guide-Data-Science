Title:
A+ Vegan Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2c spelt flour
2c oat flour
2T baking powder
4T ground flaxseed meal
1/2t salt
3 1/2c lowfat vanilla soy milk
4T applesauce
2T honey or agave nectar
1 1/2T vanilla extract
2c blueberries (fresh or frozen)

Directions:
1. whisk flour, baking powder, flaxseed meal and salt.
2. combine wet ingredients in a separate bowl
3. form a well in the center of the dry ingredients and add wet ingredients. Stir the batter until moistened.
4. let batter rest 15 minutes.
5. spoon batter on hot griddle and sprinkle blueberries onto wet batter. When bubbles form, flip pancake.
You can freeze and pop in toaster. YUM!
