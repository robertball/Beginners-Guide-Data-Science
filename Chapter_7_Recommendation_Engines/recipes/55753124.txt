Title:
Easy Refrigerator Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups thick sliced cucumber or cut tip and poke holes with fork
2 cups thinly sliced onions
1 1/2 cups sugar Omit the sugar
1 1/2 cups cider vinegar maybe white vinegar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon mustard seed
1/2 teaspoon celery seed
1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric Omit
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves Omit
Red Pepper Flakes 1/2 teaspoon
Fresh Dill Sprig in each jar
Use Gallon Freezer bag

Directions:
Place cucumbers and onions in a large bowl; set aside. Combine remaining ingredients in a saucepan; bring to a boil. Cook and stir just until the sugar is dissolved. Pour over cucumber mixture; cool.  Cover tightly and refrigerate for at least 24 hours before serving.
