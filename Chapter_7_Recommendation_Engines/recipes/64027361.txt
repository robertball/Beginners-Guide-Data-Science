Title:
Big Ray's Broccoli Chop Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1118968.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 can chick peas, drained and rinsed (14 oz)
5 cups broccoli finely chopped
1/2 tsp salt
2 Tbsp water
1/4 tsp pepper
grated zest 1 lemons
1 Tbsp fresh tarragon, chopped

Directions:
In large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat and add garlic.  When garlic starts to sizzle, add chic peas and stir to coat.  Add broccoli and salt and cook until broccoli turns bright green (about 2 min).  Stir in water and turn down heat to medium low, cover and cook an additional 5 min.
After 5 min, check on broccoli and if dry add an additional Tbsp of water.  Return cover, cook an additional 2 min until broccoli is tender.  Stir in pepper, lemon zest and tarragon.  Can be served hot or room temperature.
