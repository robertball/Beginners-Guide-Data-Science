Title:
Blackened Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs salmon
2 tsps garlic salt (or 1 1/2 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp garlic)
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp thyme

Directions:
Heat cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat and spray with Pam. Season skinless side of salmon. Put salmon in pan skin-side down and sear. Flip; peel off skin and season other side. Cook 6-8 minutes per side or until fish flakes easily with fork.
