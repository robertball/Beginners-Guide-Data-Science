Title:
Pistachio Pecan Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3.4 oz package of pistachio instant pudding mix
20 oz can crushed pineapple in juice
8 oz container of cool whip
1 cup mini marshmallows
1/2 cup coarsely chopped pecans

Directions:
combine pineapple and pudding mix until well blended.  add cool whip, marshmallows and pecans.  refrigerate and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Rebecca Cleary
Description: 
If you like pecans and pineapple, you will love this pudding!
