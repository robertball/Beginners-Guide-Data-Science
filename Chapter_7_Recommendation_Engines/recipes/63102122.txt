Title:
Scot's Hamburganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 lg. onion, sliced
3 cups beef stock
2 tablespoons flour
1/4 cup cold water

Directions:
1.Cook ground beef + onions in frying pan, breaking ground beef up into small crumbles. 2.Place beef + onion mixture in paper towel lined bowl. 3.Combine flour and water. SHAKE WELL! 4.Whisk in flour mixture, stirring until thickened. 6.Add beef and onion to gravy. 7.Serve over rice or potatoes.
