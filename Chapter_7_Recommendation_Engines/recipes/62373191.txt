Title:
Vegan Cream of Broccoli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion, minced
salt
curry powder
ground black pepper
4 cloves garlic, minced
6 cups broccoli florets, chopped
1 quart box veg broth
Bragg's Liquid Aminos
1/4 cup almond butter
paprika

Directions:
In large skillet, saute onion, salt, curry powder, pepper until onion is slightly soft, add garlic and continue to saute 2 more minutes.  Add  an inch of broth, bring to boil; add broccoli and steam until bright green.  Add remaining broth and cook until broccoli is tender.
In batches, transfer veggies to blender. Blend at low speed until evenly chopped. Add almond butter, paprika, and Liquid Aminos to taste. Blend until smooth. Serve.

Description: 
Rich and creamy broccoli soup.  You won't even miss the cheese.
