Title:
Tastes-Like-Beef Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/575399.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
2 tablespoons steak sauce
cooking spray
4 hamburger buns

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together ground turkey, onion soup mix and steak sauce. Shape into 4 patties.
Heat a large skillet over medium heat, and lightly coat with cooking spray. Cook burgers 4 minutes on each side. Serve on hamburger buns.

Submitter: 
KEESHABOOKER
Description: 
This recipe is for those who want to cut beef out of their diet, but still love a good burger! Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.
