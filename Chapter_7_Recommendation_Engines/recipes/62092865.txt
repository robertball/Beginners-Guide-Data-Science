Title:
Honey Rosemary Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 4 large carrots, peeled and sliced into 1/4" diagonal slices
- 1/4 cup water
- 2 tbsp. butter
- 1/4 cup honey
- 1 spring fresh rosemary leaves (stripped from stem)

Directions:
Place carrots, water and butter in a 2 quart saucepan.  Bring to a boil and simmer for 10 minutes.  Add honey and rosemary leaves and simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
