Title:
Hard Steamed Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/833739.jpg
Ingredients:
12 large eggs
Large pot with steamer insert
Enough water to steam eggs
Bowl of ice water for chilling the eggs

Directions:
Set the eggs out long enough to take the chill off of them.
In a pot for steaming add enough water to hard steam the eggs for 15 minutes.
Turn pot of water on high heat until water comes to a boil then turn heat down to medium. Insert steam basket in pot, cover and steam eggs for 15 minutes.
Immediately place cooked eggs in ice water to stop the cooking process. When the eggs are cool enough to handle make a small crack on the large end of the egg and place back in the ice water for approximately 20 minutes.
Peel and serve

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
Look no further for a method to make hard cooked eggs, fresh or old, that are easy to peel. This works for 1 to 12 eggs.
