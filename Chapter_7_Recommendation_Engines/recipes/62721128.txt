Title:
Oven Ham and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
¦1/2 C. butter, room temperature
¦2 Tbs. Dijon mustard
¦1 1/2 Tbs. poppy seeds
¦1 1/4 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
¦1/2 med. onion, finely grated
¦8 cracked pepper sandwich rolls
¦1 lb. shaved boiled ham
¦8 slices Swiss cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Mix together butter, mustard, poppy seeds, Worcestershire sauce and onion. Split rolls and spread mixture thinly on both sides. Divide ham between rolls, top with 1 slice of Swiss cheese, and replace sandwich tops.
Wrap sandwiches in aluminum foil and warm at 400 degrees for 10 minutes or until cheese melts.
