Title:
NALYSNYKY [MELESNIKI] Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/941894.jpg
Ingredients:
FILLING:
large container dry cottage cheese
1 Tbsp.fresh dill,chopped
2 green onions,chopped
salt and pepper
3 egg yolks
CREPES:
3 eggs
1/2 tsp.salt
1 cup milk
1 cup flour
CREAM SAUCE:
1 Tbsp.marg.
2 green onions,chopped
1 Tbsp.fresh dill,chopped
1 pint whipping cream

Directions:
FILLING:
Mix together the cottage cheese with 1 Tbsp.dill,2 chopped green onions,3 egg yolks,salt and pepper.
CREPES:
mix together the eggs and milk,gradually add the flour and salt.
Fry crepes and set aside till all mixture is done.
In center of crepe add 3-4 Tbsp.of cheese mixture,roll and set in long dish seam side down.
CREAM SAUCE:
In sauce pan melt marg over medium heat,add 2 green onions,cook till soft.[don't brown]
Add cream and bring to a slow gentle boil,add dill,salt and pepper.Pour over malesteke.
Bake covered at 350 for 1 hour.SERVE IMMEDIATELY.

Description: 
Ukrainian side dish.Crepe filled with a cottage cheese and dill mixture,baked in cream.Just like my baba use to make.
