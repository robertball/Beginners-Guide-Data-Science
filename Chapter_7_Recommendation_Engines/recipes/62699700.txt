Title:
Coconut Ginger Soup with Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (400ml) coconut milk
4 cups chicken broth
4 cups Asian veggies cut into strips (carrots, red pepper, mushrooms, bamboo shoots, bok choy)
1 cup water
2 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
4 tsp fish sauce
1 tsp garlic powder
1 piece ginger, sliced and bruise
1 lime leave (optional)
12 oz chicken breast, sliced (marinade with salt, white pepper, water, cornstarch and sesame oil)
juice from 1/2 lemon
juice from 1/2 lime
2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)

Directions:
combine first 9 ingredients in a large pot over medium heat
simmer 7-10 minutes
add chicken, lemon juice, lime juice and turn off heat
let sit for 10-15 minutes until chicken is cooked
stir in cilantro

tangy coconut soup with lots of flavour
