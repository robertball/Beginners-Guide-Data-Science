Title:
Oreo Ice Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chocolate Graham crackers, 6 sheets, crumbled, divided
Oreo cookies, about 10, crushed in big chunks
Vanilla Ice Cream, 1 qt, softened
Cookies'n Cream Ice Cream, 1 qt, softened
Hot fudge, 1 cup
Butter or margarine, 1/4 cup, melted

Directions:
Mix the crumbled Chocolate Grahams and butter and press about 3/4 of it into the bottom of 9" Springform pan cover with hot fudge topping and freeze for about 15 minutes.
Spread vanilla ice cream over crust and sprinkle remaining Chocolate Grahams and freeze for about 30 minutes.
Spread cookies'n cream ice cream over vanilla ice cream layer.
Arrange Oreo cookie pieces around the border of the cake and freeze 4-6 hours, but preferrably overnight.
