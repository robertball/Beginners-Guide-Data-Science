Title:
Avocado breakfast hole in one Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 avocado per person
Salt
Pepper
1 egg per person
Pico de Gallo
Tortilla chips

Directions:
Cut avocado in 1/2 and scoop out 1 Talb flesh set aside
Season with salt n pepper
Place avocado on top of cupcake ban to balance it...crack med egg in center season salt n pepper ... bake 425 until White of egg is set about 16-18 min
Top with pico de Gallo and crushed tortilla chips

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
Bake these 1/2 avocado eggs right in the avacado
