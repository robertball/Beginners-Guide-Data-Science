Title:
Yankee Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/911295.jpg
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts
1/2 cup of ham, cubed
1 cup of shrimp
6 oz (2 links) of andouille sausage, sliced into rounds
1 large onion, diced
2 red peppers, diced
5 stalks of celery
5 cloves of garlic, minced
2 teaspoons of olive oil
2 cans (16 oz) diced tomatoes
2 tablespoons Cajun spice
2 tablespoons hot sauce
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
5 bay leaves
1 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
2 cup chicken broth
2 cup of rice, uncooked
1 cup of water

Directions:
1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.  Saute chicken and sausage until lightly browned.  Stir in onion, bell pepper, celery, and garlic.  Cook for 5 minutes.
2. Add shrimp, ham, chicken broth, Worcestershire sauce, bay leaves, black pepper, hot sauce, red pepper flakes, and Cajun seasoning. Cook for 5 minutes.
3. Add diced tomatoes, and rice.  Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 min, stirring occasionally. Add additional water if needed.
