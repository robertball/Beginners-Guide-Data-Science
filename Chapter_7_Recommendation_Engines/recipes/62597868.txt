Title:
Delicious Hummus Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Whole grain tortillas
6 tbs cream cheese
1 cup Hummus
1/2 cup shredded carrots
1/2 cup thinly sliced bell pepper
1/4 cup thinly sliced or chopped onion
1 Roma tomato, sliced

Directions:
Spread a thin layer - 1tbs of Cream Cheese on each tortilla, then add a layer of Hummus as much or little as you want according to your own taste. Add the carrots, bell peppers, onion, and tomatoes to each tortilla. Roll the tortillas, and serve.

Submitter: 
mammawiddison
Description: 
These incorporate whole grains, delicious hummus and fresh veggies to create a delicious and healthy snack or light meal.
