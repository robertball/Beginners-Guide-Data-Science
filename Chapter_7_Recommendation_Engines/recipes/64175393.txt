Title:
Lemon Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter
3 cups powdered sugar
4 tsp milk
4 tsp lemon juice

Directions:
Cream butter and 1 cup powdered sugar.  Stir in milk and lemon juice.  Then add remaining 2 cups powdered sugar until light and fluffy.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
Delicious on Poppy Seed Cake.
