Title:
Glenda's Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb shirmp
8 oz cream cheese
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 t. lemon juice
3 chopped green onions
1 packet of unflavored gelatin
1 cup mayonnaise

Directions:
Boil, peel and finely chop shrimp.  Melt cream cheese and soup over low heat stirring until creamy.  Remove from heat.  Mix cream cheese and soup mixture, lemon juice, gelatin, onions, mayonnaise and shrimp in medium bowl.  Chill and enjoy with Ritz crackers!

Submitter: 
Joanie
Description: 
This is a wonderful, creamy shrimp dip made with mushroom soup.  It disappears quickly every time I make it.  Great for holiday get togethers!
