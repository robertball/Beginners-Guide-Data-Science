Title:
Sweet Acorn Fritters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6367825.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Acorn Flour
1 Cup All Purpose Flour
1 teaspoon Baking Powder
1/4 C Sugar
Pinch of Salt
1 Egg, Beaten
3/4 Cup Milk
Oil for Frying
Powdered Sugar
Cinnamon and Sugar

Directions:
Heat a high heat oil in a cast iron skillet or deep fryer
Mix the ingredients together in a large bowl
Use a teaspoon to drop rounded spoonfuls of batter into the hot oil and fry until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes, turning to make sure each side is golden brown
Remove from oil and place on a plate lined with paper towels to absorb extra oil
Fill a small bowl with powdered sugar and another small bowl with the cinnamon and sugar.
Roll half of the fritters in one type of sugar and the other half in the other type of sugar
Serve hot

Submitter: 
Shanda
Description: 
Delicious treat using acorns!  Not healthy because of the frying and sugar but definitely tasty!!
