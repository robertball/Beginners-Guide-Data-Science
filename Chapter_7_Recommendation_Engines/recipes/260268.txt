Title:
Gluten-Free Pizza Crust with Cassava Flour Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cassava flour
1/2 cup coconut flour
1/2 cup rice flour
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
1 tablespoon olive oil
water, or as needed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Line a pizza pan with parchment paper.
Combine cassava flour, coconut flour, rice flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a large bowl. Whisk in eggs and olive oil. Add 1/2 cup water; mix until batter is the consistency of mashed potatoes, adding more water as needed.
Spread batter on the lined pizza pan with a spatula.
Bake in the preheated oven until dry, about 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
J G
Description: 
Basically what the title says. I put this together because I wanted a gluten-free crust that had the right texture. So many of the gluten-free recipes have a weird texture, different from gluten flour. I love making my own dough traditionally with regular flour...but gluten doesn't like me.
