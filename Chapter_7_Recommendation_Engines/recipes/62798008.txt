Title:
Summer ChampGria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/654952.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz Mason Jar
1/2 Lemon, freshly squeezed
1/2 Lime, freshly squeezed
1 stemmed Cherry
Red Sangria
Champagne, Brut

Directions:
Fill Mason Jar with crushed ice cubes fill 1/3 of the glass full with Sangria. Squeeze fresh lemon & lime juice over Sangria. Top off 1-2 inches with Champagne. Garnish with lime wedge & cherry.
