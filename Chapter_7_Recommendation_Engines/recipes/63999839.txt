Title:
Big Ray's Pineapple Curry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1109954.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans (8 ounces each) unsweetened pineapple chunks, undrained
6 bone-in chicken breast halves, skin removed
1 can (15 ounces) chickpeas, rinsed and drained
1 large onion, cut into 1-inch pieces
3 medium carrots, julienned
1 medium sweet red pepper, cut into strips
1/2 cup coconut milk
2 Tbsp cornstarch
2 tsp sugar
1/4 cup curry powder
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 tsp ginger powder
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp salt
fresh basil
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp lime juice
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes

Directions:
Drain pineapple, reserving 1/2 cup juice. Place the chicken, beans, vegetables and pineapple in a 6-qt. slow cooker. In a small bowl, combine coconut milk and cornstarch until smooth. Stir in the sugar, curry powder, garlic, ginger, salt, pepper, lime juice, pepper flakes and reserved juice; pour over chicken.
Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or until chicken is tender. Serve with rice and top with fresh basil.
