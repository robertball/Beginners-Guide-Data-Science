Title:
White Bean, Tomato  and Green Chili Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (14-oz) cans Vegetable Broth
2 (16 oz.) cans White Beans, drained and rinsed
2 (4oz.) can chopped Green Chilis
1 onion, cut in quarters
2 pints fresh grape tomatoes
2 t. chili powder
2 t. ground cumin
1/2 c. chopped fresh cilantro
4 Tbl. fresh Lime juice
2 Tbl. Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 t. salt

Directions:
Combine one half of the broth, chili powder, cumin and beans over medium heat.
Combine remaining broth, green chilis, and onion in a food processor and pulse until onions are coarsely chopped.
Add onion mixture to pan.
Add tomatoes and cilantro to food processer and process until coarsely chopped.
Add tomato mixture to pan and bring to a boil.
Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 15 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
Remove from heat; add in lime juice, olive oil and salt.
