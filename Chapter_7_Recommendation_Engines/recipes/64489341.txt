Title:
Roasted Garlic Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds red potatoes (six potatoes, approx 5.5 oz each / 2.5" to 3" dia)
2 Tbsp olive oil
6 cloves fresh garlic (about 3 Tbsp finely chopped)
1/2 tsp kosher salt (coarse/large grain)
1/2 Tbsp coarse black pepper
1.5 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, minced (optional)

Directions:
1. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.
2. Wash potatoes, cut potatoes into quarters (wedges) and place in baking tray.
3. Finely chop fresh garlic and add to roasting pan.
4. Add Olive oil to pan.
5. Add salt and pepper.
6. Mix thoroughly with hands
7. Turn potatoes so one sliced side is facing down on pan (skin side up)
8. Roast in oven for 45 minutes. Optional: at 30 minutes, flip other sliced side of potatoes down to brown on the pan
9. Remove from oven, sprinkle with fresh parsley and serve
