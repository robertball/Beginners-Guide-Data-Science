Title:
Crustless Vegetable Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 dozen eggs
Â½ bag fresh baby spinach
5-7 crimini mushrooms, sliced
1 small package sun-dried tomatoes
1 orange or yellow bell pepper, diced
2-4 green onions, diced
1 tablespoon fresh oregano
8 ounces of shredded raw jack cheese (or your favorite cheese)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
Crack eggs into bowl. Whisk until blended.
Prepare all vegetables and blend into eggs.
Shred cheese and add to egg mixture. Reserving a little for the top.
Grease 9x13 baking dish. Pour egg mixture into baking dish. Bake for 45 minutes or until no longer giggly in middle.
Cool for 10 minutes. Serve.
