Title:
Chinese Pearl Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup glutinous rice
2/3 pound lean ground pork (may also use ground turkey or beef)
2 T. water
1 T. cooking wine
1 T. cornstarch
1 T. soy sauce
1 small onion chopped
1/2 tsp. grated ginger
1/2 tsp garlic or 1 clove minced
1/2 tsp salt						dash of pepper
Chinese cabbage for lining bamboo steamer.

Directions:
Rinse, then soak rice for 2 hours, remove and drain.  Set aside.
In medium bowl, stir together remaining ingredients.
Divide and roll meat into 1Â½-2 inch balls.
Roll each ball in rice to completely coat.
Line bamboo steamer with Chinese cabbage.
Place meatballs on top of cabbage and cover with lid.
Place bamboo steamer over pot of boiling water or over water-filled wok.
Steam  over high heat 30 minutes.
Remove and serve with steamed rice and vegetables.
Optional: In a small bowl whisk together 1/4 c soy sauce, 1T. sesame oil and garlic to taste. Sprinkle lightly over meatballs, rice and steamed vegetables.

Submitter: 
Mom2MMJ
Description: 
These tasty meatballs are easy to make.  Wonderful for your Chinese New Year celebration or as an addition to a holiday buffet.  Some folks dye the glutinous rice with red or green for a holiday touch.
