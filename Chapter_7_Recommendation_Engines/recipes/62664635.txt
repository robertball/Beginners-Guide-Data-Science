Title:
Cheddar Cheese Rolled Flank Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/827135.jpg
Ingredients:
Marinade:
1 cup chopped onions.
2 cups pineapple juice.
1 tsp. salt.
1 tsp. thyme.
1/2 tsp. pepper.
1/2 tsp. rosemary leaves, crushed.
2 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce.
1-2 lbs. beef flank steak.
Stuffing:
1 1/2 cups. fresh bread crumbs.
1 1/2 cups. shredded chedar cheese.
1/4 cup. chopped onions.
1/4 cup. chopped parsley.
1/2 cup. minced garlic.

Directions:
In a medium bowl stir together all marinade ingredients except steak. With mallet, pound steak to 1/4 in. thickness. Place steak in plastic food bag, pour marinade into bag; seal tightly. Refrigerate 24 hours, turning twice. Heat oven to 400F.
Remove steak from marinade; reserve marinade.
In medium bowl combine all stuffing ingredients.
Place stuffing mixture over entire surface of steak; pressing slightly. Tightly roll up steak, jelly roll fashion. Tie with string to secure filling inside roll. Line a 13x9&quot; baking pan with heavy duty aluminum foil. Place steak in prepared pan; baste with reserved marinade. Bake, basting with marinade every 15 minutes; for 50 to 60 minutes or until meat thermometer reaches 160F.
Just before serving baste with marinade.

Submitter: 
Jeannette Miller-Neill
Description: 
Slice into this tender, marinated flank steak and discover a moist, flavorful stuffing.
