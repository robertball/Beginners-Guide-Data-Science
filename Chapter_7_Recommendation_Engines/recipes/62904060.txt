Title:
Worlds Best Italian Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/705884.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb.ground beef
1 lb. ground veal
1 lb ground pork
3 eggs
1 large bunch of parsley
1 cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano
2 cups seasoned breadcrumbs
1 loaf of Italian bread
1/2 cup of olive oil

Directions:
Rip Italian bread into pieces and place into a bowl of water. Squeeze out excess water from bread thourghly and place in bowl with beef, veal and pork. Add parsley, cheese, breadcrumbs and mix together with hands to form the meatballs.Place olive oil in pan and fry the meatballs until lightly browned.Take fried meatballs and put in a pot of gravy ( marinara) for 1 1/2 hours or longer.
Serve over pasta or enjoy with a salad.

Description: 
These meatballs are from an old Italian recipe that people go crazy over! They are delicious! I live in Chicago so I use D'Amatos Italian bread but you can use any kind of Italian bread
