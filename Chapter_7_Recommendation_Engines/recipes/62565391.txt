Title:
Mix 'n' match chocolate bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups     semi-sweet chocolate chips
2 tbsp     butter
4 cups     miniature marshmallows
11/4 cups  candy-coated chocolate pieces, such as M&M
1 cup      pretzel sticks chopped (1/2 inch pieces)
1/2 up     chopped peanuts

Directions:
In a saucepan over low heat, melt chocolate chips and butter, stirring constantly until smooth.  Remove from the heat, let cool until just slightly warm.
In a large bowl, combine marshmallows, candies, pretzels and peanuts.  Stir in chocolate mixture, mixing well.  Press evenly into 13x9 inch pan lined with waxed paper.  Chill until firm, about 30 minutes.  Cut into bar.
