Title:
Asian Sesame Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp Toasted sesame seed
1 tsp Minced garlic dried spice
1 tsp Minced onions dried spice
1/4 tsp ground ginger spice
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper (optional)
1/3 cup honey
1/3 cup thinly sliced green onions
2 TBSP soy sauce
2 lbs. salmon

Directions:
1. mix honey, green onions, soy sauce and all of the spices except Red Pepper in small bowl until well blended.  Sir in Red pepper to taste, if desired.  Place salmon in 13 X 9 inch baking dish.  Spoon honey mixture evenly over salmon.
2.  Roast salmon in preheated 375F oven 20 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork, basting occasionally with honey mixture.
