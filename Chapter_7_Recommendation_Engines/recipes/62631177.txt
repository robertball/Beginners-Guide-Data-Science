Title:
Super Simple Pasta Fagioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/577154.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. Minced garlic
1 Tbsp. olive oil
1 28 oz. can crushed tomatoes with basil
2 15 oz. cans small white beans, rinsed and drained
1/2 c. ditalini (or other small noodle pasta)
1 c. frozen spinach
4 c. Water
Parmesan Cheese (optional)
2 Beef bullion cubes
1/2 tsp. dried oregano
1/2 tsp. dried basil
1 1/2 tsp. salt
pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large pot.  When oil is hot, add minced garlic and cook just until fragrant(about 45 sec.) Immediately add tomatoes and stir.  Add water, bullion cubes, oregano, basil, salt and pepper and bring to a boil.  Once base is boiling, add ditalini and cook for 7 min.  Add beans and spinach and cook until heated through.  Cook and serve with optional parmesan cheese.
