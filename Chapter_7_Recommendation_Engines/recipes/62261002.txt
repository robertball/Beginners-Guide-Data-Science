Title:
Mama Long's Goulash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. of stew beef
1/2 chopped onion
1 tbl. olive oil
2 tsp. chopped garlic
4 cups water
2 beef bouillon cubes
1 8oz can tomato sauce
1 Tbls.vinegar
1 Tbls. worchestire sauce
1 bay leaf
3 Tbls. Paprika
1 tsp. dry mustard
1/2 tsp. lemon juice
2-3 potatoes
bag of wide egg noodles

Directions:
Brown stew meat with chopped onion and olive oil over low/med heat in a big pot.
Add garlic, water, beef bouillon cubes, tomato sauce, vinegar, worchestire, bay leaf, paprika, dry mustard and lemon juice.
Cook over low heat, COVERED for 2-3 hours.
Peel, and dice 2-3 potatoes, add to soup. Cook for 1/2 hour uncovered for thicker soup, covered for thinner soup.(carrots and celery are good options too)
Meanwhile, boil 8 oz. of wide egg noddles
Poor stew over noodles, serve warm.

Submitter: 
Lara
Description: 
My mother received this recipe while briefly living in Germany many years ago. A hearty stew that can easily be modified by adding veggies of your choice! I can eat this stew all year long!
