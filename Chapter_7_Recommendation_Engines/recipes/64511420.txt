Title:
Dark Delicious Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2745670.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup oil
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup molasses
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1 1/2 cup bran
1 cup flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp soda
3/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Beat together oil, sugar, molasses, and eggs. Add milk and  bran. Let batter sit to soften the bran.  In a separate bowl mix dry ingredients together. Add dry ingredients to batter and stir until just blended. Place in a greased muffin tin. Makes 12. Bake at 400Â°F for 15-20.

Submitter: 
Melissa Walker Hudson
Description: 
I don't eat any other bran muffin. These are dark with molasses, and very moist. Add fruit (either pieces or puree) if desired.
