Title:
KRETSCHMAR® French Bistro Turkey Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984752.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
3 tablespoons mayonnaise
2 tablespoons honey
8 slices French bread
4 ounces KRETSCHMAR® Colby Jack Cheese
8 slices KRETSCHMAR® Turkey Off The Bone
1 apple, cored and thinly sliced

Directions:
Combine mustard, mayonnaise and honey in a small bowl.  Spread mixture on four slices of bread.  Add cheese, turkey, apple and second slice of bread.  Cut sandwich in half.

Submitter: 
Kretschmar®
Description: 
A new twist on a classic turkey sandwich. A deliciously sweet and tangy spread on new KRETSCHMAR® Turkey Off The Bone makes it seem like Thanksgiving leftovers any day of the week!
