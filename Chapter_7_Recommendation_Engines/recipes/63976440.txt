Title:
Chocolate Sheetcake (from the long-haired blonde at Walmart) with chocolate frosting. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Combine in bowl:  2 c sugar
2 c flour
Combine in saucepan:
1 c (2 sticks) butter
4 T cocoa
1 c wat
Stir and bring to a boil.  Pour over the sugar/flour mixture.
Add:               2 eggs
1/2 c buttermilk ( may sub 1 1/2 tsp lemon juice or vinegar, add milk to equal 1/2 cup and let set 5 minutes)
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp vanilla
Mix well. Pour into a greased and floured pan (I use Baker's Joy).  Bake at 350F for approximately 20 minutes.
Frosting:
Combine in saucepan:   1 c sugar
5 T butter
1/3 c milk
6 oz milk chocolate chips
Bring sugar, butter and milk to a boil, stirring constantly.  Cook one minute; remove from heat.  Stir in 6 oz milk chocolate until melted.
