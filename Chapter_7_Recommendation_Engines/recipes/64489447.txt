Title:
Chicken tortilla soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 onions, diced
2 cloves of garlic, minced
2-3 T oil
2 lg cans refried beans
2 15-oz cans petite diced tomatoes
2 15-oz cans petite diced tomatoes with chiles and Mexican flavors
1 large can or box of chicken stock
3-4 bags of prepared/cooked chicken (Mexican flavor, if possible)
4 cans of corn
2 packages of medium sized tortillas
Garnish:
sharp cheddar cheese
sour cream
tortilla strips
sliced avocados, if desired

Directions:
Saute onions in oil until translucent.  Add garlic partway through.
Add refried beans to onions and mix well.  Add tomatoes, then add chicken stock gradually until desired thickness.
Let simmer for 30 minutes.
Add corn and chicken. Add some hot sauce , if desired.
While soup is simmering, heat oven to 450 degrees. Slice tortillas into 1/2" x 4-5 in strips, and bake on greased cookie sheet until slightly browned.
Ladle soup into bowls and top with garnish
