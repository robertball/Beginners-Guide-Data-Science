Title:
Simple Stuffed Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Thick Cut Pork Chops
1 Box Stuffing Mix (use your favorite)
1/4 c Chopped Onions
1/4 c Chopped Celery
Salt
Pepper
4 Dollops Butter/Margerine
2 tbsp Oil

Directions:
Heat oven to 350
Cut each pork chop in a "butterfly" style
Salt & Pepper all sides of pork chops to personal taste Heat oil in frying pan and quickly sear all sides of each pork chop
Prepare stuffing mix according to pkg directions and add onions and celery
Place pork chops (closed) into casserole dish
Add stuffing to casserole dish around, inside, and atop pork chops leaving center of each uncovered
Place dollop of butter on each pork chop
Cover and bake in 350 oven for 30 to 35 mins

Submitter: 
Carrie Bell
Description: 
Quick and easy, this recipe makes it look and taste like you spent all day in the kitchen.
