Title:
V's 3 Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/920769.jpg
Ingredients:
1 24oz jar of tomato sauce (your choice)
1 small bottle of bbq sauce (also your choice, I chose sweet)
2 1/2 lbs ground beef
1 can dark red kidney beans
1 can light red kidney beans
1 can white kidney (cannellini) beans
1 sweet onion chopped
2 tbsp chili powder
2 tbsp minced garlic
salt n pepper (optional)

Directions:
Use a big tall pot for the tomato and bbq sauce, set on medium/low heat
Mix in chili powder
Drain beans, add to sauce
Brown ground beef, add to sauce
Brown chopped onion, add to sauce
Lightly brown garlic, add to sauce
Let simmer on low heat for an hour stirring often
