Title:
Fresh Fruit Tart Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRUST:
3/4 c. butter or margarine, melted
1 1/2 c. spoon-sifted all-purpose flour
3 Tbs. granulated sugar
FILLING:
11 oz. cream cheese (softened) or nuefchatel cheese
7 Tbs. granulated sugar
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
TOPPINGS:  (Select any combination of 4 from the following)
1 pint strawberries, hulled and cut in half
2 kiwis, peeled and sliced
1 pint blueberries
1-2 c. seedless grapes, red or white
1-2 c. cherries, pitted and stemmed
2-3 peaches, peeled and sliced
Glaze:
1/2 c. orange marmalade
or 1/2 c. apple jelly
or 1/2 c. strawberry jam
1 T. water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Combine crust ingredients; mix well,  Use fingers to spread on a 12- inch pizza pan (DO NOT USE A SMALLER PAN).  Bake for 12 to 15 minutes or until light golden brown.  Cool.  Combine filling ingredients; spread on cooled crust to within 1/2 inch of edge.  Chill thoroughly.  Arrange fruit on top of filling with sliced berries cut side down.  Chill until ready to serve.
*Best served the day it is assembled.  The crust and filling can be prepared one day ahead.

Submitter: 
Virginia Mom
Description: 
The basis of this simple recipe comes from a local cookbook.  Choose the ripest, sweetest fruit available if you prefer to skip the glaze.  I've tried all of the fruit listed but our preference is berries.
