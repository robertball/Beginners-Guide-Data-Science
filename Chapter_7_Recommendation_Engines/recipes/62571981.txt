Title:
Easy Glazed Cornish Hens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C apple jelly
1/2 t cinnamon
2 Rock Cornish game hens, halved
salt

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees. In small saucepan combine apple jelly and cinnamon. Heat, stirring until jelly is melted. Set aside. Place hen halves, skin side up, on rack in shallow roasting pan. Sprinkle with salt. Roast 20 minutes.
Brush with jelly-cinnamon mixture. Roast 25-30 minutes longer or until browned and tender.

Description: 
Moist succulent game hen with a sweet apple-cinnamon glaze.  Our Christmas Eve family meal!
