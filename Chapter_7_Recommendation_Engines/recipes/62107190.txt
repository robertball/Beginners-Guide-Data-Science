Title:
Fiesta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 green pepper
1/2 red onion
1 can corn (rinsed, drained)
1 can black beans (rinsed, drained)
1/2 cup salsa
2 tsp chili powder

Directions:
Dice pepper and onion
Mix corn, beans, pepper, onion, and salsa. Stir in chili powder
Refrigerate at least 1 hr before serving
