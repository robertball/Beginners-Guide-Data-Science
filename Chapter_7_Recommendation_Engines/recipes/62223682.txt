Title:
Cornbread Stuffed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Stuffing:
2 slices bacon
1/4 cup onion, diced
2 T celery, diced
2 T bell pepper, diced
2 corn muffins, crumbled
1/2 t salt
1/4 t cayenne pepper
1/2 cup chicken stock
Chicken:
1 pound chicken breast, boneless skinless
1 T olive oil
1 t salt
Glaze:
1/2 cup chicken stock
1/2 cup orange apricot marmalade

Directions:
**USE A CAST IRON SKILLET TO MAKE** = PREHEAT OVEN TO 400
STUFFING MIXTURE:
1)  Cook bacon and drain
2)  Saute onions, bell pepper and celery 4-5" in remaining grease
3) Add cornbread and mix well;  Season to taste then add stock
4) Reduce 2-3", cool, then fold in bacon bits
PREPARATION OF DISH:
1) Flatten chicken breasts, then season with salt/pepper
2) Place 1/2 cup of stuffing mixture in lower part of breast;  Roll up and tie with twine.
3) Heat oil in pan and saute breasts on each side until golden brown
4) Place pan in oven
COMPLETION:
1) While chicken bakes, mix stock and marmalade in medium saucepan.  Bring to boil and let reduce 5 to 7";
Bake chicken 20" minutes then pour glaze over all
Bake additional 5-7" longer
Remove from oven and let sit 5"
Cut into 1/2" slices and serve

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Boneless chicken breasts stuffed with a lovely cornbread dressing and topped with an orange/marmalade glaze.
