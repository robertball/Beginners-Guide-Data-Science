Title:
Grandms's famous Matzo ball soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup matzo mean
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 tablespoons margarine
2 eggs
3 or 4 quarts or boiling water
1/2 cup cold water
chicken broth
any noodles or vegetables you enjoy in your chicken soup (optional)

Directions:
1. Mix matzo meal and salt and set aside.
2.Melt margerine (no not allow to bubble) and set aside.
3. Beat the eggs lightly and set in seprate bowl.
4. Add a little of the 1/2 cup water to the eggs and stir.
5.Add everything together and mix lightly untill it makes a firm dough. Make sure to mix lightly, do not press.
6. Let mixture sit in the refrigerator.
7. Boil 3 or 4 quarts or water on the stove.
8. When water is at a full boil, take mixture out or the refrigerator.
9. Lightly roll (do not press) dough into 2-3 inch balls and drop them into water.
10. While matzo balls are boiling, boil your chicken broth and other soup ingrediants in a souce pan.
11.In 15 minutes, turn off your stove, and remove balls from water with a large spoon, and add to the chicken broth. Serve and enjoy.
