Title:
No Bake Peanut Butter Nutella Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/773396.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons Butter
1/2 Cup Sugar
1/4 Cup Milk
1 Tablespoon Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
1/4 Cup Peanut Butter (Chunky or Creamy)
3/4 Cup Nutella
2 Cups Rolled Oats

Directions:
Melt 3 tablespoons butter in a large saucepan on medium-low heat.
Stir in 1/2 cup sugar, 1/4 cup milk, and 1 tablespoon cocoa powder until combined.
Add 1/4 cup peanut butter, and 3/4 cup Nutella. Stir on medium-low heat until all ingredients have melted together.
Remove from heat and mix in 2 cups rolled oats.
Quickly drop tablespoon sized balls onto wax paper, and refrigerate for 1-2 hours until set.
