Title:
Spinach Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c yellow corn meal
1 c flour
1 TB baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
2 TB sugar
1 TB olive oil
1 egg
1 cup skim milk
1 pkg frozen spinach, thawed, drained
1 small onion, chopped
1 cup low fat cottage cheese

Directions:
Combine corn meal, flour, baking powder, salt, sugar, oil, egg, milk, spinach, onion and cottage cheese; mix well.  Pour mixture into an 8 x 8 inch sprayed baking dish.
Bake in 350 degree onion for 35 minutes, until browned lightly and set in the middle.

Healthy corn bread, great with tomato soup.
