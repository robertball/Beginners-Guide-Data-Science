Title:
Jewish Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
3 tsp baking powder
2 cups sugar
1 cup vegitable oil
4 eggs
1/3 cup orange juice
2 tsp vanilla
1 tsp almond extract
3 apples, peeled, cored and sliced thin
3 tbs cinnamon
3 tbs sugar

Directions:
Toss apple slices in cinamon and sugar.
Mix/beat all other ingredients.
Pour 1/3 batter into bundt pan.
Add apple mixture to remaining batter.
Pour remainder of batter into pan.
Bake at 350 for 1 hour and 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
ltlmsmfft
Description: 
This was one of my favorite cakes growing up. This type of cake should not have any icing and is more like a pound cake with a twist.
