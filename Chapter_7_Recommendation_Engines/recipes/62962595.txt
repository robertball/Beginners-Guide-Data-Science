Title:
Pumpkin bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 cup oil (can sub for unsweetened applesause)
2 teaspoons cinnamon
15 oz can pumpkin
1 2/3 cup sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt (optional)
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups flour
Frosting
1/2 cup butter (softned)
3oz cream cheese
2 cups powered sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Beat: eggs, sugar, oil/applesauce & pumpkin until light and fluffy
Sift flour, powder, cinnamon, salt, soda then add to pumpkin mixture
spread in ungreased pan and bake @ 350 for 30 minutes
Frosting
Cream: butter, cream cheese, vanilla add powdered sugar then beat till smooth

The perfect pumpkin bar, from my mother in-law Carolyn.
