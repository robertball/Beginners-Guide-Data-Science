Title:
Fabius' Shepherd Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 med onion chopped
1 can of tomato soup
1 large bag of frozen peas and carrots
1 microwave container of mashed potatoes (time saver)
dashes of dried Italian herbs
dashes of Worchestershire sauce
dashes (opt) of tobasco sauce
Shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown beef and chopped onion
Add frozen peas and carrots
Add 1 can of tomato soup
Add herbs and Worchestershire sauce to taste
Simmer 15 minutes + so everyone gets to know each other!
Place in greased casserole dish (eyeball the size needed)
Add microwaved mashed potatoes on top
Sprinkle with shredded cheddar cheese
Place in warm oven (275?) till cheese melts

Submitter:
