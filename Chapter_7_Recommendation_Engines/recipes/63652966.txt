Title:
Phyllo Dough Pizza Triangle Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package thawed phyllo dough
1 cup tomato sauce
1 cup mozzarella cheese
1 cup parmesan cheese
3 tablespoons garlic powder
1/4 cup chopped basil
1 cup olive oil
1 cup of pizza toppings of you choice (optional)

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350
Unwrap the phyllo dough and lay it on a clean flat surface
Take 1 sheet and brush olive oil on it
Lay another sheet of phyllo dough on top
Cut the dough into 1 inch strips
Brush sauce on to the bottom of each strip
Put a pinch of garlic on
Sprinkle some cheese on to the sauce and garlic
Add the toppings you would like
Finish with a little of the basil
Fold up the corner of the dough to make a triangle.  Then fold the triangle again, and keep going up until the strip is all rolled up.
Repeat with the rest of the dough.
Put the triangle rolls on a baking sheet and brush with more olive oil.
bake for 17 minutes or until golden brown.
