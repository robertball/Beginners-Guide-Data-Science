Title:
Creamy Sweet Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-4-5 medium-sized orange sweet potatoes, boiled and mashed
-250ml heavy cream
-1 medium white onion, chopped
-5 cloves of garlic, chopped
-2 cups coconut milk
-salt and pepper (to taste)
-1/2 tsp hot sauce(optional)
-herbs and spices (any will do, this is optional)
-olive oil

Directions:
1. Saut√© onions and garlic in olive oil until golden brown.
2. Add the sweet potato, stir.
3. Pour in the coconut milk and all purpose cream, stir.
4. Add the herbs and spices, salt and pepper.
5. Let it cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes.
