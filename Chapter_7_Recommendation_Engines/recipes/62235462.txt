Title:
Caramel Fudge Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 oz.) Bag Caramels
1 box German Chocolate Cake mix
2/3 cup evaporated milk (divided)
3/4 cup softened butter
1 (12oz) bag chocolate chips
1 cup walnuts or pecans (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Melt 1/3 cup evaporated milk and caramels in a saucepan.  Set aside.  Combine cake mix, 1/3 cup evaporated milk, 6 oz chocolate chips, butter and nuts.  Mix well.  Press 1/2 of dough into the bottom of a 13x9" greased pan.  Bake 10 mins.  Remove and sprinkle bake crust with rest of chocolate chips. Pour caramel sauce over the chips and then remaining dough on top.  Bake 20 mins.  Let cool.
