Title:
Gruel-less Gazpacho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 red pepper
1-2 cloves garlic
1 cup chicken stock
splash of red wine (optional)
1/4 of a hothouse English cucumber
sprig of fresh chopped parsley
6 oz. frozen, pre-cooked shrimp (optional)

Directions:
Add all of the ingredients, except the shrimp, in order to the Tall Cup and twist on Cross Blade.
Pulse until the consistency is smooth, but has some texture.
Thaw the shrimp.
Place shrimp in a single layer in two bowls. Top with gazpacho. Serve cold.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
Best served cold. For a fancy appetizer, place chilled, cooked baby shrimp in the bottom of cups and pour gazpacho over the top. This can also be used as a tasty chip dip.
