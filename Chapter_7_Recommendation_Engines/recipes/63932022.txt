Title:
Walnut Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 (12 oz) pkg frozen winter squash, thawed
1/4 cup plus 2 Tbl brown sugar (subi splender brown sugar)
2 eggs
5 Tbl softened butter
3 Tbl flour
1/4 cup low-fat milk
1 tsp salt
1tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup walnuts or pecans

Directions:
preheat oven to 350. coat large baking dish w/cooking spray
Beat together squash, 1/4 cup brown sugar & eggs
in saucepan, melt 3 Tbl butter. Add flour & cook, stirring for 1 min. Whisk in milk, salt & cinnamon
Beat milk into squash; transfer to baking dish
bake 45min. Stir 2Tbl butter, 2 Tbl brown sugar & nuts. Sprinkle on squash. Bake another 15 min
