Title:
Rosemary blue cheese garlic bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 long lean loaf of sourdough or french bread
8 T salted butter
1/4 lb blue cheese sliced
2 T dried rosemary
4 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp onion salt

Directions:
Mix the herbs together
soften...don't melt...the butter and mix in herbs
cut loaf of bread in half and spread mix
place sliced blue cheese on loaf and broil for 4 minutes or until cheese is slightly melted
sprinkle with any remaining rosemary

Submitter: 
Mrs. Falvo
Description: 
I just wanted to put my favorite flavors together! I normally suggest fresh herbs but with this please use already dried.
