Title:
Snickers Apple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Granny Smith Apples, unpeeled but cored
6 King Size Snickers candy bars
12 oz. Cool Whip

Directions:
Cut up apples into bite-size chunks.  Also cut up Snickers bars into similar bite-size chunks. Put both in a serving bowl bowl and add the Cool Whip.  Mix all 3 ingredients together.  Keep refridgerated until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Barb Andrews
Description: 
This recipe always raises eyebrows as to what's in it, but once people try it, it gets gobbled up in minutes!  I'm asked to bring it to every party now!
