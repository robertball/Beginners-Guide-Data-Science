Title:
Mummy Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of refigerated breadstick dough
1 pack hot dogs
mustard and poppy seeds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.  Using 1 dough strip for each, wrap hot dogs to look like mummies, leaving opening for eyes.  Place on ungreased baking sheet.
Bake 12-15 min or until light golden brown.
Place dots of mustard and poppy seeds for eyes.
