Title:
Carla's Chili Mac Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. elbow macaroni
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 onion (red or yellow), chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 can sliced tomatoes
1 can 8 oz. can tomato sauce
1 8 oz. can water
4 Tbsp. chili powder
3 cloves of garlic, pressed
2 tsp. cumin
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. garlic salt (optional)
1/2 tsp. hot pepper sauce (optional)
Cheddar cheese (optional)

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to package directions.
While macaroni is cooking, brown meat in large sauce pan with onion, bell pepper, and garlic (1 tsp. garlic powder can be substituted for the garlic cloves, if desired).  Drain.
Add all remaining ingredients, except pasta, to meat mixture.   Bring to boil.
Drain cooked pasta.  Add to meat mixture.  Mix well.
Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 8-10 minutes.
Add shredded cheddar when served if desired.  Also great with sliced cheddar.

Submitter: 
Carla Woodard
Description: 
This chili mac recipe is quick, easy, and great for those crave chili mac, but do not like beans!
