Title:
Robert Rounds Famous Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1 package Hiddle Valley Ranch Dressing ( powder pkg)
2. 1 package chili seasoning
3. 2 large cans tomatoes- whole or diced
4. 2 cans rotelle( with chilies)
5. 2 cans chili or kidney beans
6. 1 can corn
7. 1 can green chilies
8. 1 medium onion diced
9. 1 lb ground beef
garnishes
10. sour cream
11. tortilla chips
12. grated cheese ( Cheddar or Monterrey or Mexican mix)
13. lime juice or a fresh lime
14. fresh chopped cilantro
15. hot sauce

Directions:
In a frying pan cook ground beef and onions till hamburg cooked thoroughly
In large pot or crock-pot add 1. 2. 3. 4. 5. 6. 7. juice and liquid from the cans as well
stir in the cooked hamburg and onions
simmer for at least 1 hr...may use crock pot and put on low all day
serve in bowls and garnish with 10. 11. 12. 13. 14.as per your taste

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
this recipe is easy...may use the crock-pot...it is the perfect seasoning with the Hidden Valley dry salad dressing and pkg of chili mix ( I use mild)
Robert was an associate at the Wal-mart I worked at and he brought this to a few potluck lunches
My family and friends have loved this
 serves 8
