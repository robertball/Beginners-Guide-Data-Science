Title:
Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts
Small knob of fresh ginger - discard outer layer
Pinch of salt
1 stalk celery - finely chopped
1/2 red pepper - finely chopped
1/4 cup of mayonaise

Directions:
Bring pot of water to boil.  Add chicken breasts and ginger and cook until chicken is done.
Drain and discard ginger.  Cool.  Shred chicken.
Add celery, red pepper, dash of black pepper and mayonaise to taste.
Mix and refrigerate for 1 + hours.  Serve on store bought crescent rolls.
