Title:
Beef Kabobs with Parmesan Orzo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/496913.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Lb boneless beef top sirloin steak, Cut 1" thick
2 Red or Yellow bell peppers, Cut into 1" pieces
1 Tbsp chopped fresh basil or 1 tsp dried basil
1 Tbsp Italian dressing
2 Large cloves garlic
Parmesan Orzo:
1 Cup uncooked orzo Pasta, Cooked
2 to 3 Tbsp Chopped fresh basil or parsley
2 Tbsp shredded Parmesan Cheese
2 tsp Olive Oil

Directions:
1. Soak eight 8" bamboo skewers in water 10 minutes
2. Cut beef steak into 1" Thick pieces. Toss beef and bell peppers with 1 tbsp basil, dressing
3. Mix orzo ingredients in bowl, Keep warm
4. Place kabobs on grill over medium, ash-covered coals. Grill, Uncovered, about 9 to 11 minutes for medium rare to medium, turning occasionally. Serve with orzo.
*To Broil: Place kabobs on rack in broiler pan so surface of beef is 3" to 4" from heat. Broil about 9 to 12 minutes for medium rare to medium doneness, turning once.
