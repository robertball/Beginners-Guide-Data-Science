Title:
Kid-approved Mock Eggs Benidict Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Eggs
1 can of Campbells Cream of Celery soup
3/4 can of milk
8 slices of American Cheese
4 English muffins, split in half

Directions:
Scramble the eggs in a skillet. In another pot,combine the can of soup with the milk and heat through. Toast the muffins. Next, add a slice of cheese on each half of muffin, and melt in microwave. After that, add a scoop of the eggs, and top with a scoop of the soup mixture. Enjoy!

Description: 
Yummy and quick breakfast or dinner when short on time. My mom was the one who changed the ingredients and came up with this recipe.
