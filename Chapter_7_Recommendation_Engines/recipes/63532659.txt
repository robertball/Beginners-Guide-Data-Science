Title:
Mom's Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/964807.jpg
Ingredients:
1 shallot, finely chopped
1 bag fresh baby spinach
1/4 cup bacon, cooked and crumbled (reserve 1/2 tsp fat)
1 cup strawberries, sliced
1/2 cup chopped walnuts, toasted
1/2 cup Sargento Parmesan Cheese
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1/4 cup plus 1/2 teaspoon olive oil
1 Tbsp real maple syrup
1/2 tsp fresh thyme, finely chopped
sea salt and cracked black pepper, to taste

Directions:
Sautee shallot in bacon fat and 1/2 tsp olive oil.  In a large serving bowl, layer spinach and strawberries.  Whisk together vinegar, oil, maple syrup, rosemary, thyme salt, and pepper and let flavors meld for five minutes.  Toss salad with dressing, then top with walnuts and cheese.  Finish with fresh cracked pepper.

Submitter: 
Angela Sackett  Superhotmama
Description: 
Showcasing Sargento's shredded parmesan cheese, this salad is perfect for all year round (but especially when you can get your hands on fresh, ripe strawberries).
