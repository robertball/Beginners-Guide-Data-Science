Title:
Meatloaf with Sticky Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
500grams sausage mince
500grams mince steak
1 cup bread crumbs
1 desertspoon curry powder
2 tbsn chopped parsley
2 onions chopped finely
1 egg
1/2 cup water
1/2 milk
Sauce:
1/2 cup water
1/4 cup worchestshire sauce
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 tomato ketchup
1 tbsn butter
2 tbsp malt vinegar
1 tsp instant coffe granules

Directions:
Preheat oven 180c
Combine all ingredients for meatloaf.
Mix well.
Form into loaf.
Place into roasting dish.
Bake 30 mins.
In meantime combine sauce ingredients in saucepan.
Brin to boil.
Simmer 5 mins
Pour sauce over meatloaf.
Bake further 45 mins.
Basting every 15 mins.
