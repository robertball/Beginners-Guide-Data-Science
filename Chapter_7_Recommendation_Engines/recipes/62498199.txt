Title:
Buckeyes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123143.jpg
Ingredients:
1 stick butter, softened
18 oz. peanut butter
1# powdered sugar
1 T. vanilla
12 oz. chocolate chips
12 rectangle paraffin

Directions:
Mix butter, peanut butter, sugar and vanilla.
Roll into 1 in. balls and refrigerate at least 1 hr.
Melt chocolate chips and paraffin in microwave.
Use toothpick to dip balls into chocolate.
