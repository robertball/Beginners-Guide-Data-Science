Title:
Mah's Jerk Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2222881.jpg
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
1/4 teaspoon curry powder
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/8 teaspoon ground thyme
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/8 teaspoon ground ginger

Directions:
Whisk red pepper flakes, allspice, curry powder, black pepper, thyme, cayenne pepper, and ginger together in a bowl.

Submitter: 
Brian Jenkins
Description: 
Easy jerk rub to put on your meat of choice.
