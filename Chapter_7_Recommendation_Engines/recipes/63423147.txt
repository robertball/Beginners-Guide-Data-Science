Title:
annies stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg sage sausage
giblets from 1 turkey, diced
2 med onion, chopped
1 cup celrey, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 pkg sliced mushrooms, chopped
1 pkg bread cubes and seasoning
2 eggs
1 - 2 cans chicken broth

Directions:
night before:
cook sausage and giblets on stovetop. Add onion, celrey, mushrooms and garlic. let cool then refrigerate overnight.
Mix bread cubes, seasonig packet, eggs, sauasage mix, moisten with chicken broth. bake 1 hour at 325 in coved cassserole dish.
