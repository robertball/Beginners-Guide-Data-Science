Title:
Pegu Club Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5049285.jpg
Ingredients:
ice
2 fluid ounces gin
3/4 fluid ounce orange-flavored liqueur (such as Cointreau®)
1/2 fluid ounce fresh lime juice
2 dashes aromatic bitters (such as Angostura®)
lime

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice; add gin, Cointreau(R), and lime juice. Shake in bitters. Shake well and strain into a chilled glass. Garnish with a slice of lime.

Submitter:

Description: 
One delicious gin cocktail, with Cointreau, lime juice, and bitters. It's practically a gin margarita.
