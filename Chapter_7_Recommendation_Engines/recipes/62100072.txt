Title:
Chewy Gingersnaps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1/2 cup molasses
2 1/2 cup flour
2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp salt
extra sugar for rolling cookies in

Directions:
Cream together butter and sugar. Add egg and beat until fluffy.
Mix in molasses.In a separate bowl, sift together dry ingredients.
Slowly stir dry ingredients into butter mixture, a little at a time.
Roll into golf ball size balls and roll in sugar. Place on lightly greased cookie sheet. DO NOT press down.
Bake at 350 for 8 to 10 minutes. Cookies will start to appear cracked. Do not overbake.
Let cool on cookie sheet for a few minutes before moving to rack to finish cooling.

Submitter: 
jowolf2
Description: 
These are the best gingersnaps. The secret is not to overbake them so they stay nice and chewy.
