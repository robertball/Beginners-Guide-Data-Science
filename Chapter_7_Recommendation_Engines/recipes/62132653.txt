Title:
Zip-Tang Italian Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C Red Wine Vinegar
1/4 C Apple Cider Vinegar
2 Cloves minced garlic
2 Tbsp Italian Herb mix
1 Tbsp finely grated parmesean cheese
1 tsp dijon mustard
1/2 C Extra Vigin Olive Oil
Salt and Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Pour all ingredients into a narrow necked jar with a tight fitting lid. If necessary use a funnel.
Screw the lid on the jar and shake to mix.
Chill in the fridge and shake before each use.

Submitter: 
AZ
Description: 
I like a dressing with zing and flavor, so I mix this up about once a week. It also makes a great marinade for chicken, fish and vegetables.
