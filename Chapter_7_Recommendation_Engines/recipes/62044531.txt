Title:
Aunt Rosie's Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 or 2 lbs of Chicken pieces (with bones and skin)
1 cup of milk
1 cup of flour
about 4 eggs (beaten)
2 cups of seasoned bread crumbs
1 Tablespoon of Oregano
1 Tablespoon of Grated Cheese
1/2 Cup of Olive Oil

Directions:
Put the Milk, Flour, eggs, and bread crumbs in seperate bowls. Put the Cheese and Oregano and mix it with the bread crumbs. Dip the chicken in the milk then the flour and then the eggs and then the bread crumbs. Do this to all the chicken pieces and then put the chicken in the 1/2 cup of hot oil in a pan until brown. Then put on a oven pan and put in the oven for 350 degrees for about 1 hour.

Submitter: 
StevenRN
Description: 
My grandmother's older sister Rosie used to make this chicken ALL the time. Everyone loved it. Its kind of bland but it just falls off the bone and its a good comfort food.
