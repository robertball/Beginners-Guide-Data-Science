Title:
Shrimp Scampi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
I Love Shrimp!  I love Garlic! I love Garlic!
God Bless the genius who came up with this recipe!
1.5 sticks of butter
2 lb. shrimp (with tails)
¼ c. shallots
3 Tbsp. chopped garlic
1 tsp. salt
½ tsp. pepper
2 Tbsp. lemon Juice
¼ c. fresh parsley (chopped)

Directions:
Clan and de-vien shrimp, but leave tails on.  Melt butter and saute shrimp, onions and garlic 5-8 minutes.  Turn shrimp once. Do not overcook.  Transfer shrimp to hot platter and leave juice in pan.  Add salt pepper and lemon juice to butter mixture.  Heat and pour onto shrimp.  Sprinkle with parsley.
