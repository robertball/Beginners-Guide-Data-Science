Title:
Crockpot Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds Ground pork
1 whole Onion, Medium, Finely Chopped
3 stalks Celery Finely Chopped
¾ cups Hoisin Sauce, Divided
¾ cups low salt Soy Sauce
¼ cups Water
3 cloves Garlic, Minced
1 Tablespoon Fresh Ginger, Grated
2 Tablespoons Brown Sugar, Packed
1 Tablespoon Sesame Oil
1 Tablespoon Hot Chili Oil
5 ounces, weight Can Bamboo Shoots, Drained, Finely Chopped
8 ounces, weight Can Water Chestnuts, Drained, Finely Chopped
Bean Sprouts
chopped peanuts
¼ cups Fresh Cilantro, Snipped
12 whole Iceberg Lettuce Leaves, Cold

Directions:
Brown ground pork with chopped onion. Drain off excess liquid.
In a large crock pot, combine cooked turkey and onion with celery, ½ cup hoisin sauce, soy sauce, water, garlic, ginger, brown sugar, sesame oil, chili oil, bamboo shoots and water chestnuts. Cover and cook on low for 6 hours.
Before serving, mix in the bean sprouts and cilantro. Serve by scooping a spoonful in the lettuce, sprinkle on peanuts, drizzle with the leftover (¼ cup) hoisin sauce.
