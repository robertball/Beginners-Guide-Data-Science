Title:
Chicken-Stuffing Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margerine
1 cup water
enough seasoned stuffing mix for 6 servings
1 can cream of chicken or mushroom soup
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup milk
2 cups cooked chicken, diced
1 cup frozen mixed vegetables, cooked and drained
1/2 tsp. poultry seasoning
1 cup shredded cheese (Cheddar, Swiss, or Monterey Jack)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
In small saucepan over high heat, melt butter in water; heat to boiling. In large bowl, pour hot water mixture over stuffing mix; toss to mix well.  Pat 1/2 of the stuffing mixture into 12" x 8" baking dish.
In medium bowl, stir together condensed soup, mayonnaise, milk, chicken, mixed vegetables,  and poultry seasoning until well mixed.  Spoon evenly over stuffing mixture in baking dish; top with remaining stuffing mixture.
Bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes or until heated through.  Sprinkle with shredded cheese.  Bake 5 minutes more or until cheese is melted
