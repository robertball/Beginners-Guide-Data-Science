Title:
Top Secret Pit Master's Texas style rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup raw sugar (Turbinado)
1/4 cup salt (I use plain)
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tbs garlic powder
2 tbs granulated onion
2 tbs paprika
2 tbs chili powder
2 tbs black pepper
1 tbs cayenne pepper
1 tbs cumin
1 tsp nutmeg

Directions:
mix well, rub generously onto meat and allow to soak in for an hour or so before cooking.
