Title:
Sweet Potato-Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4460114.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small banana
1/2 small cooked sweet potato
1/2 cup cottage cheese
1/2 cup low-fat milk
5 cubes ice, or as needed
1 tablespoon cocoa powder
1 tablespoon molasses
1 tablespoon honey
1/8 teaspoon almond extract

Directions:
Blend banana, sweet potato, cottage cheese, milk, ice, cocoa powder, molasses, honey, and almond extract together in a high-power blender (such as Ninja(R)) until smooth.

Rachel Lee
