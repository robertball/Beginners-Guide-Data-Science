Title:
Spinach Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb small curd cottage cheese
1 lb shredded mozzarella cheese
1 egg
1 10oz pkg frozen chopped spinach
1 tsp salt
3/4 tsp oregano
1/8 tsp pepper
1 jar spaghetti sauce
1/2 pkg lasagna noodles
1 cup water

Directions:
Hold some cheese to sprinkle on top!
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Thaw spinach and drain well.  In a large bowl mix cottage cheese, remaining mozzarella, egg, spinach, salt, oregano, and pepper.
In a greased 9x13 baking pan layer 1/2 cup sauce, noodles, 1/2 cheese mixture.  Repeat. Top with remaining noodles and sauce.  Sprinkle with remaining cheese.  Pour water around edges.  Cover with foil.  Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour and 15 minutes.

Description: 
For those who think that Lasagna is too time consuming and difficult, think again!  This lasagna is quick and easy and way better than the frozen stuff you get at the grocery store.
