Title:
Mom's Meaty-Cheesy Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground beef
1 medium onion, diced
2 cloves garlic,  minced or 1/8 tsp. garlic powder
1 Tbsp. Olive oil
2-28oz. Cans chopped tomatoes
2-8 oz. cans tomato sauce
3/4 C water
1 Tbsp. Balsamic Vinegar (optional)
1 Tbsp. Parsley flakes
2 tsp. sugar
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. oregano
1/4 tsp. black pepper
?9 cooked Lasagne noodles
12 oz. Mozzarella cheese
16 oz. large curd cottage cheese
1 C. Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat the oil and brown the meat with the onions.? Add remaining sauce ingredients and bring to a boil, then simmer, lightly covered (to keep from spattering) for 30-90 minutes. Cook the noodles and remove from  water. Scoop 1 cup of sauce and spread in Lasagna pan.? Lay 3 cooked noodles over sauce.? Add another cup of sauce followed by 1/3 of the cottage cheese (use a soup spoon to distribute over sauce).? Sprinkle 1/3 of the mozzarella over all.? Sprinkle 1/3 of the parmesan over all.? Repeat in layers, finishing all ingredients. Bake in oven 60 minutes at 350 degrees. Remove from oven and allow to set for 10 minutes before serving. Even better reheated!
