Title:
Cucumber and Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups think sliced cucumbers
2 cups thin sliced green peppers
2 cups thin sliced onions
1 Tbl salt
1 cup wine vinegar
2 cups sugar
2 tsp mustard seed
1 tsp celery seed

Directions:
Put cucumbers, green peppers,onions, and salt in a clean GLASS pickle jar
Boil vinegar, sugar,celery seed, and  mustard seed let cool 2 hrs and pour into jar
Refrigerate for 1 day.

Submitter: 
Kirkham Kevin
Description: 
nice salad to snack on at night. when the season is right we try to keep it in the frig all the time.
