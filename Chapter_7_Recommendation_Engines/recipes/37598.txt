Title:
Valentine Heart Breadsticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983127.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (11 ounce) can refrigerated soft breadsticks
2 tablespoons KRAFT 100% Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme leaves, crushed

Directions:
Twist 2 pieces breadstick dough together; form into heart shape. Place on cookie sheet. Repeat with remaining breadstick dough.
Sprinkle hearts with cheese and thyme.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 15 to 18 minutes or until lightly browned.
