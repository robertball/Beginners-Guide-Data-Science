Title:
The Ultimate Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/835217.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons sesame seeds
1 bunch green onions, chopped
8 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons tahini
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Place the sesame seeds in a dry skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir for 5 minutes, or until golden brown and fragrant.
In a medium bowl, mix toasted sesame seeds, green onions, garlic, tahini, soy sauce, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, red wine vinegar, and pepper. Place desired meat in the mixture, cover, and marinate as long as you like, but overnight is best. Grill as desired.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
Always a crowd-pleaser, this marinade with tahini, soy sauce, sliced green onions and sesame seeds works great with chicken or steak. Go ahead, try some of each!
