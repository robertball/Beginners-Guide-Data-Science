Title:
Turkey & Broccoli Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 box linguine noodles;
1 cup broccoli florets;
1 can cream of mushroom condensed soup;
1/2 cup milk;
1/2 cup grated parmesan;
1/4 tsp pepper;
2 cups cooked turkey, cubed

Directions:
Cook linguine according to directions. Add broccoli for last 4 minutes. Drain. In large skillet, heat soup, milk, cheese and pepper. Add turkey, noodles and broccoli and heat through. Serve and top with additional parmesan.
