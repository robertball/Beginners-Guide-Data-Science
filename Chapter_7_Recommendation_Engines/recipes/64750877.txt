Title:
Keto Instant Pot Whole Chicken Recipe with Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Season
3 tbsp oil divided
1 Cup Chicken stock
4lbs whole chicken giblets removed, washed and patted dry
6 Cups of Green Beans

Directions:
Green Beans
1. Preheat oven to 400 and bake for 25 minutes
Whole Chicken
1. Put the chicken in a bowl and rub the olive oils an seasoning all over including it
2. Select the saut√© setting of the Instant Pot, add in the oil and when hot brown the whole chicken on both sides (about 4 mins on either side).
3. Remove the chicken from the Instant Pot, and set aside on a plate.
4. Add the remaining chicken stock into the instant pot, and scrape any bits stuck to the bottom of the pot from frying with a silicon or wooden spoon.
5. Place the trivet inside before adding the chicken on top of the trivet.
6. Cover your Instant Pot and set the valve to the SEALING position.
7. Select the manual setting or pressure cook, make sure the pressure is set to high and set the timer to 25 mins.
8. Cook till done then let the steam release naturally about 10-15 mins before opening.
9. Let the chicken rest for 10 mins before serving.
