Title:
Banana Chocolate Walnut Frozen Yogurt (higher protein, lower fat) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C Fat Free Greek Yogurt
1/3 C Truvia
1/2 t Vanilla
1 scoop vanilla protein shake
24 g dark chocolate, chopped
28 g walnuts, chopped
1 medium banana

Directions:
Slice banana and put on cookie sheet.  Place in freezer 15-30 min.  Put yogurt, Truvia, vanilla, protein shake in ice cream maker and follow the machines instructions.  When close to consistency you want add the chocolate, walnuts and banana and let the machine run 2-5 minutes.
