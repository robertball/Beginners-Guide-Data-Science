Title:
Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 8oz cream cheese softened
1 - 8 oz cracker barrel sharp cheese grated (or you can cheat and use pre-shredded!)
1/4 cup mircle whip
1/4 cup chopped onions
5 oz chopped walnuts

Directions:
Mix first four ingredients together.  Make two balls.  Wrap in plastic wrap and chill two hours or overnight.  Roll in chopped walnuts.

Submitter: 
Dog Fish Head aka Fabius
Description: 
Very tasty cheese spread.  Can be formed into a ball or a log!
