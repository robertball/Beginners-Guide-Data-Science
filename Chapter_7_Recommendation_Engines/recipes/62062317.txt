Title:
Easy Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 med head of cabbage, shredded
2 lb. uncooked skinless, oneless       chicken breast
2 14.5 oz. cans diced tomatoes w/ garlic and onion
1 16 oz. can Navy beans
3 qts. low sodium chicken broth

Directions:
1.Put all ingredients in a large stockpot and bring to a boil.
2.Reduce heat and simmer at least 2 hrs.  The longer it simmers the better the flavor!

Submitter: 
tball
Description: 
Easy and delicious chicken soup, especially if you like cabbage.  Good on a cool autumn night with a crusty loaf of bread, although my kids enjoy this year round.
