Title:
MiMi's Dumplings for Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour, sifted
1 tsp. salt
4 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 egg , beaten
3 tbsp. butter, melted
2/3 cups milk

Directions:
Sift dry ingredients and make a well in center
Beat egg in milk, add butter
Dump milk mixture into dry ingredients, stirring quickly
Drop by TBL into boiling broth
Cover and simmer 18 min DO NOT LIFT LID UNTIL DONE!!!!
can add:
1/8 tsp rosemary or
2 T parsley      or
1 T horseradish

Submitter: 
Kim Snyder
Description: 
This recipe was handed down from my Great Granma Mimi Petrauske-Funk
