Title:
Honey Wheat 'n Oat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg, beaten
1 cup warm water
2 tbsp honey
2 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
1/2 cup oats (we love Roger's porridge oats with oat bran, wheat bran & flax seed)
1 1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 1/2 tsp bread machine yeast
2 tbsp butter or margarine

Directions:
1. Add ingredients to machine according to manufacturer's directions.
2. Select Basic or Whole Wheat cycle and press start.

Submitter: 
ambikins
Description: 
This recipe gets made daily around our house! Soft, flakey crust and light, airy middle makes this bread totally irresistable!
