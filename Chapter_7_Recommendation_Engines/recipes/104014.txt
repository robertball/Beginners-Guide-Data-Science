Title:
Lemonade by the Pitcher! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984261.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups water
1 cup fresh lemon juice
1 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
Ice cubes for serving
Fresh mint sprigs and lemon slices for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Stir water, lemon juice, and SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener in a large pitcher until the SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener dissolves.
Serve over ice, garnished with the mint and lemon, if desired.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Add a touch of elegance by garnishing with mint sprigs and thin slices of lemon.
