Title:
Latin Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground chuck
2 TBL adobo seasoning
2 cups jasmin rice, cooked
2 limes
1 bunch cilantro, divided
2 heads Bibb lettuce, washed, separated, drained
salsa

Directions:
Season ground beef with adobo seasoning; brown beef and drain. Season cooked rice with finely chopped zest of two limes, juice of one lime and 2 TBL chopped cilantro.
Serve beef, rice lettuce and salsa in separate dishes so guests can build their own wraps.
