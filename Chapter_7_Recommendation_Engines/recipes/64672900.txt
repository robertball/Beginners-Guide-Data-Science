Title:
Udon Noodle Salad with Fresh Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz udon noodles
1 bunch green onions, chopped
1 small yellow pepper, diced
1 small green pepper, diced
1 c shelled edamame
1 c cherry tomatoes, halved
2 c spring mix lettuce
Dressing...
3 garlic cloves, minced
2 tbsp hoisin sauce
3 tbsp rice vinegar
2 tsp sesame oil
2 tsp soy sauce
1 tsp sriracha
1 tsp wasabi paste
1 tbsp agave syrup
1 tbsp lemon juice

Directions:
Cook noodles according to package directions. Drain, rinse, and set aside.
In a large bowl, combine all veggies. Add noodles.
In a small bowl, combine all dressing ingredients, and whisk well or emulsify.
Add dressing to salad mixture and toss well.
