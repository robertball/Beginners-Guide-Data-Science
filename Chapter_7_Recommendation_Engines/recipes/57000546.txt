Title:
Salmon with Garlic, Lemon and Dill Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound salmon fillets
1/4 cup Olive oil
3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
5 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon dried dill weed
1 tablespoon fresh pressed garlic
sea salt to taste
freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a medium baking dish.
Place salmon in the baking dish. Mix the oil, garlic, dill, 3 tablespoons Dijon mustard, sea salt, pepper and lemon juice in a small bowl, and sprad over the salmon.
Bake 25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until salmon is easily flaked with a fork.
