Title:
Easy Spinach Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990878.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1 cup shredded Italian cheese blend
1/2 cup mayonnaise
8 ounces Classico® Creamy Alfredo Sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a small casserole dish, mix the spinach, artichoke hearts, cheese, mayonnaise, and Alfredo sauce.
Bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until lightly bubbly and lightly browned.
