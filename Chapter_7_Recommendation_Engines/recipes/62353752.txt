Title:
Mexican Manicotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 16oz. can refried beans
2 1/2 tsp chili powder
1 1/2 tsp oregano
1 8oz. package manicotti shells
2 1/2 c. water
1 16oz jar picante sauce
16 oz. (2 c.) sour cream
4 oz. (1 c.) shredded mexican cheese
Sliced olives (optional)

Directions:
In a bowl, combine uncooked ground beef, beans, chili powder and oregano.  Spoon into UNCOOKED manicotti shells and arrange in a 9x13 pan.  Combine water and picante sauce, pour over shells.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.
Remove from fridge 30 minutes before baking.  Cover and bake at 350 for one hour.  Uncover and spoon cream cheese, mexican cheese and olives on the top.  Bake 5-10 minutes longer until the cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Traci Caha Myers
Description: 
Can be used without ground beef for a vegetarian meal too!
