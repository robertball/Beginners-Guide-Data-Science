Title:
Stuffed Peppers for Two Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Green Papper
1 Red Pepper
1 Yellow Pepper
1/2 lb of ground chuck
1/4 lb Chorizo
1/3 cup finely crushed Chez-it's
2 T milk
1 bunch Green Onions chopped
1 egg
1/4 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
Boil a sauce pan of water.  Cut the peppers from the stem downward and seed.  Drop peppers in boiling water.  Boil for about 3-4 minutes.
Meanwhile, mix the hamburger and chorizo, add the rest of the ingredients, just until mixed.
Remove the peppers from the water and drain.  Arrange in an oven proof skillet or 8x8 dish or pan.
Stuff peppers with the mixture and sprinkle with parmesean cheese.
Bake at 375 for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
MARYFLOWERS
Description: 
This is an easy quick and delicious meal for two.  The chorizo is not overpowering and is so flavorful that you don't need to salt or pepper.  Add a salad and mac and cheese and you've got a fast way to get out of the kitchen on a weeknight with great reviews.
