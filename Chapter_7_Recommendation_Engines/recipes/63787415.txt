Title:
Italian Style Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/950042.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1/2 lb. ground pork
1/3 c. seasoned Italian breadcrumbs
1 egg
1 T Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp. dried parsley
1 tsp. Sun of Italy Italian Spice Mix
1 tsp. onion powder
1 "squirt" prepared yellow mustard
cracked black pepper and sea salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl.  Mix gently with hands until combined (do not overwork the meat).
Roll into 10-12 meatballs.
Brown in skillet over medium high heat, turning to brown all sides.  Add to spaghetti sauce to finish cooking OR place in one layer in rectangular pan and bake in oven, 400 degrees for 20 minutes.  Add to sauce.
