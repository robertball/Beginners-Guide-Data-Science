Title:
Awesome Rib Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•1/2 cup sugar
•1/4 cup paprika
•3 tbs. season salt
•2 tbs. chili powder
•2 tbs. ground black pepper
•1 tbs. celery salt
•1 tbs. onion powder
•1 tbs. garlic powder
•2 tsp. ground sage
•1 tsp. dry mustard

Directions:
1.Mix everything together in a big bowl. Generously rub ribs with seasoning. Store the rest of the rub in an airtight container for later uses.
2.Slab your ribs with your favorite BBQ sauce and throw them on the grill!!!
3.(or in the oven). Enjoy!
4.**This recipe makes approximately 1 1/2 cups rub so you WILL have a good amount left over.
