Title:
Ragu alla bolognese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
EXtra virgin olive oil
1/2 onion, finely chopped
1 carrot, finely chopped
1 celery stalk, finely chopped
1/2 lb ground beef
2oz finely minced prosciutto crudo or bacon
1/3 cup red wine
1 can cirio pelati (400 grams)
salt and pepper
1 lb spaghetti or tagliatelli

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Saute onion, celery, and carrots until soft.
Add beef and prosciutto or bacon. Pour in the red wine and cook until evaporated.
Add cirio pelati, season with salt and pepper and simmer for 2 hours.
In the meantime, cook the pasta. Stir finished sauce into cooked pasta.
