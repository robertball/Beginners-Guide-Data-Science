Title:
Simple Salmon with Balsamic Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/322284.jpg
Ingredients:
4 (4 ounce) boneless, skinless salmon fillets, patted dry
1/2 cup Smart Balance buttery spread or butter, melted
1 lemon, cut in half
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
Balsamic Sauce:
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1/8 cup soy sauce
1/8 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon minced fresh garlic

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Dip salmon fillets into melted butter, covering both sides  and arrange on baking dish.
Squeeze lemon, drizzling juice evenly over fillets.
Next, combine Balsamic Sauce ingredients and dab it on generously over top of each fillet.
Sprinkle fillets evenly with parsley.
Drizzle any remaining butter over fillets.
Bake uncovered at 375 degrees for 30 minutes.
Cover with foil and let stand 3 minutes before serving.
