Title:
No Mushroom Beef Tips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1091321.jpg
Ingredients:
Marinade:
1 lb stew meat or tips, cut into bite size pieces
1/8 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp kosher salt
1 tbs red wine vinegar
2tsp corn starch
small diced onion
Sauce:
2cups water
2 heaping tablespoons of minced garlic
2 boilon cubes
1/4 worschire sauce
1/4 soy sauce
corn starch (approx. 2tablespoons)

Directions:
Marinaide meat for at least 20mins. Add it and diced onion to an oiled pan and brown and sautee onions.
While browning, combine water, cubes, soy sauce, woreshire sauce. boil. make sure cubes are disolved. Disolve corn starch in a seperate bowl with broth and add to pot. When broth is thickened add the beef, onions and all the juices from the pan.
Serve over noodles.
