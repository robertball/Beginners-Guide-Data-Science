Title:
Shrimpy Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8-ounce block of cream cheese, softened
1 8-ounce container sour cream
2 tsp. Old Bay seasoning
1 tsp. dried toasted minced onion
1 4.25-ounce can tiny shrimp, drained (and an optional step, rinsed)

Directions:
Place cream cheese in a medium bowl and beat with an electric mixer until smooth.
Add sour cream to the bowl and again, beat until smooth.
Begin adding the Old Bay seasoning, 1 teaspoon at a time and beat until incorporated. Taste after each addition and stop when the flavor tastes good to you.
Add the minced onion and beat until smooth.
Mix in half of the can of shrimp with a rubber spatula and stir until the shrimp is thoroughly mixed into the dip.
Taste. Add more shrimp if desired.

Submitter: 
Kat G
Description: 
Creamy and delicious, this recipe lends itself to tweaking to make it perfect for your tastes.We use 2 tsp. of the Old Bay seasoning and the whole can of shrimp but this recipe is imminently suited to making it precisely to your own tastes.
