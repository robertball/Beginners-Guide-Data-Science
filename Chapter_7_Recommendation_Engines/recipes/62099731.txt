Title:
Delicious Doubletree Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup rolled oats
2 1/4 cups flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1 cup softened butter
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
3/4 cup sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
2 eggs
3 cups semisweet chocolate chips
1 1/2 cups chopped walnuts

Directions:
Grind oats in a blender until fine.
Combine with flour, soda, salt, and cinnamon in a medium bowl.
Cream together butter, sugars, vanilla, and lemon juice in a large mixing bowl.  Add eggs and mix until smooth.
Blend in dry mixture.
Add chips and walnuts by hand.
Chill overnight.
Place large spoonfuls on an ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 16-18 min.
Cool on cooling rack and store in a tightly sealed container to keep the cookies moist.

Submitter: 
Steph T.
Description: 
These cookies taste just like the yummy cookies that you get when you stay at a Doubletree.
