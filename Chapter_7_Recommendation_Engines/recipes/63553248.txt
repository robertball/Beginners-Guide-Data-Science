Title:
Donut Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dough:
1/2 cup white sugar
1/4 cup butter, melted
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup all purpose flour
Topping:
1/4 cup melted butter
1/2 cup white sugar
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients for dough and mix until smooth.
Place batter in a greased muffin tin. This recipe will make 6 muffins.
In a small bowl, combine sugar, brown sugar, and cinnamon for topping. Place melted butter in a seperate bowl.
Place in oven and cook for 15 minutes.
Once muffins are done cooking, remove from muffin tin, dip in butter, then dip in sugar mixture to coat the whole muffin. Repeat for all muffins.
Enjoy!
