Title:
Blended Banana Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4428218.jpg
Ingredients:
4 banana, sliced

Directions:
Place banana slices in a resealable freezer bag. Freeze until firm, about 1 hour.
Empty banana slices into a blender; blend until creamy and the consistency of ice cream. Scoop into a bowl.

Submitter: 
Katie McElwee
Description: 
Quick, fun, and kids love it! Goes well with chocolate syrup and sprinkles!
