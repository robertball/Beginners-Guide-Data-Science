Title:
Zippy Orange BBQ Chicken Packets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993514.jpg
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon butter
2 cups shredded deli rotisserie chicken
1/2 cup barbecue sauce
3 tablespoons frozen (thawed) orange juice concentrate
1 tablespoon honey
2 medium green onions, chopped
1 (8 ounce) can PillsburyÂ® refrigerated crescent dinner rolls
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
In 1-quart saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in chicken, barbecue sauce, orange juice concentrate and honey. Remove from the heat; stir in green onions. Set aside.
Separate dough into 4 rectangles; press perforations to seal. Spoon 1/2 cup chicken mixture onto half of each rectangle; sprinkle with cheese. Fold dough over filling; press edges with fork to seal.
Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 375 degrees F 15 to 20 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Margaret Noble
Description: 
Tangy and sweet barbecue chicken is wrapped in flaky crescent dough and baked until golden brown for this fun, portable dinner.
