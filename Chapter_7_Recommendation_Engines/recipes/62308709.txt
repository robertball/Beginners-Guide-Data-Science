Title:
Awesome Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/370490.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup rice
2 cups water
1 cube Caldo De Pollo (Chicken Bouillon Cube)
1/3 fresh tomato, diced
4 oz tomato sauce
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tbsp diced onion
1 tbsp oil

Directions:
First heat a fry pan with oil. When oil is hot, add rice and cook until lightly browned. Then add onion and saute for 1 minute while stirring frequently. Add garlic and saute for 2 minutes stirring frequently. Now add the tomato sauce and cook for 1 minute. Add water and fresh tomato and let simmer for 20 minutes. Fluff with fork. Enjoy.
