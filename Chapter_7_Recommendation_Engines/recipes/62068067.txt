Title:
Soy spare ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4lbs bone in, Pork spare ribs
1 15oz bottle soy sauce
1 1/2cups sugar

Directions:
Cut spare ribs and line them up in a 9x13 inch glass dish.  Mix soy and sugar together in microwave safe bowl and heat until sugar is melted.  Pour sauce over ribs.  Bake at 350 in oven for 1 hour.  Baste twice throughout baking.

Submitter: 
crazymama76
Description: 
So easy to make.  Started making them for parties and now no one can get enough.  You can have the ribs cut in half and serve also as an appetizer.
