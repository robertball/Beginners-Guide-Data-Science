Title:
Peanut Butter Cookie Dough Bites {trim healthy mama} Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2615246.jpg
Ingredients:
¤ 1/4 cup almond flour {ground fine}
¤1/4 cup protein powder
¤1/4 cup natural peanut butter
¤ water {as needed}
¤1/2 oz. dark chocolate {chopped}

Directions:
Combine almond flour, protein powder, and  natural peanut butter in a bowl until well combined.
Mixture  will be crumbly. Add enough water to form a cookie dough consistency ---> the dough should hold together when you squeeze it. stir in chopped chocolate. Form into balls --->about 2 tsp. of dough per ball---> and refrigerate until ready to serve........add a bit of sweetener in needed.
