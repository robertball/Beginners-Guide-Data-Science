Title:
Apple Smothered Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065117.jpg
Ingredients:
6 pork loin chops
1/2 teaspoon ground sage
salt
3 tart apples, peeled and sliced
3 tablespoons molasses
3 tablespoons flour
2 cups hot chicken stock
1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
1/3 cup raisins

Directions:
Sprinkle chops with salt and sage. Brown slowly in hot skillet.
Place chops in 9 x 13 baking dish. Top with apple slices and molasses.
Add flour to fat in skillet and cook one minute.
Add stock and bring to boil; add vinegar, raisins and salt to taste.
Pour liquid over chops.
Cover and bake at 350 for one hour.
