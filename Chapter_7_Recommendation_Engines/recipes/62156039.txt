Title:
Low fat grilled cheese and onion sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices of low fat bread
1 - 2 slices of fat free cheese
1 thin slice of onion
1 tsp mayo
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat frying pan on medium-low heat. Use a tableknife to thinly spread mayo on both slices of bread. Place first slice of bread mayo side down in frying pan, top with 1 slice of cheese, onion slice, 2nd slice of cheese and top piece of bread (mayo side up).  Sprinkle with salt and pepper and cook until desired darkness (flipping at least once).

Submitter: 
Tamaralynn
Description: 
A classical grilled cheese sandwich with a low fat and savory twist.  My mother in law turned me onto these. The Mayonnaise is a great way to save calories on your favorite comfort food.
