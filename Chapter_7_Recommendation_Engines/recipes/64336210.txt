Title:
Healthy Mango Lasso for One Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ cup Milk
½ cup Yogurt
1 Mango, peeled and diced
1 Tbsp Sugar OR Honey
¼ tsp Ground Cardamom

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender.
Blend until smooth.
