Title:
Lemon Ginger London Broil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs flank steaks
1/2 cup fresh squeezed lemon juice
2 tablespoons lemon zest
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons fresh grated ginger
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
1. Combine ingredients and marinate steak 12-24 hours in a resealable plastic bag. Turn bag occasionally.
2. Grill over medium high heat until desired degree of doneness. Let stand for 5 minutes before slicing thinly across the grain.
