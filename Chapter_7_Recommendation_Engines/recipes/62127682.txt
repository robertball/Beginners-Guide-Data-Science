Title:
Rock Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp mixed spice
3 oz sugar
3 oz raisins
1 oz candied peel
pinch salt
3 oz butter
pinch of nutmeg
1 egg
A Little milk

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 215*C
Sieve the flour,salt and baking powder into a bowl.
Rub in the butter. Add sugar, chopped peel, mixed spice, nutmeg and raisins. Mix well.
Mix to a fairly stiff consistency with the beaten egg, using a little milk if required.
Take a piece of the mixture between 2 forks and pile on a greased cookie sheet.
Continue till all the mixture is used.
Bake in oven for 20 mins.
Remove from oven and sprinkle sugar onto hot buns.

Submitter: 
Trishie
Description: 
This is a slightly spicy fruit bun and is a family favourite! My mother used to make these all the time.
