Title:
Quaker Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/466460.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box Quaker Oat Squares
1 box Quaker Oat Oh's
1/2 box Corn Chex
6 Tbsp butter
2 tsp seasoning salt
3 Tbsp worchestershire
3 cans mixed nuts
4 bags of fish crackers (mix varieties)
1-2 bags sea salt bagel chips (opt)

Directions:
Melt and mix the butter, seasoning salt and worchestershire and pour over first 3 ingredients listed. Bake at 250` for 1 1/2 hrs stirring occasionally.
Add the rest of the ingredients and mix together.
