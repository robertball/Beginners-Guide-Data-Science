Title:
Norma's Game day Chii Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
2 pounds of ground chuck
1 medium onion, chopped
3 to 4 garlic cloves, minced
2 tablespoons of chili powder
2 teaspoons of ground cumin
1 to 2 teaspoons of ground red pepper
1 teaspoon of paprika
1 (6 oz) can of tomato paste
1 (14.5 oz) can of beef broth
1 (12 oz) bottle of dark beer
3 (8 oz) cans of tomato sauce
2 (15 oz) cans of pinto beans, drained and rinsed
1 (4.5 oz) can of chopped green chiles, undrained
1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Cook first 3 ingredients in a 5 to 6 quart pot over medium heat, stirring occasionally for 8 to 10 minutes or until meat crumbles and is no longer pink. Drain well, and return to pot. Add chili powder and next 3 ingredients; cook for 1 minute. Add tomato paste and cook for 1 minute. Add remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil. Cover; reduce heat to low and simmer for 2 hours.
