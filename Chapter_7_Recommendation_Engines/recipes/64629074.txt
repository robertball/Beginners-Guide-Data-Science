Title:
Sausage Hominy Skillet Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4473319.jpg
Ingredients:
1 link any kind of sausage - cut bite size
1 can hominy - drained and rinsed
2 TBSP Real Butter
1 small onion - chopped
1 small handful cilantro - chopped
2-3 jalapeños - seeded and diagonally sliced
Fresh cracked pepper
Lemon pepper season salt

Directions:
Melt butter in skillet.
Add cracked pepper to butter to infuse flavor.
Add onions, jalapeños and hominy. Mix and cover with lid undesturbed for 3-4 minutes on med high heat.
Add sausage, cilantro, lemon pepper. Toss all and cover again 3-4 mins.
Remove from heat and let sit COVERED 3-4 mins.
Stir all together before serving. The hominy starch thickens liquid in pan and creates an awesome glaze over everything!

Submitter: 
MitchJGray
Description: 
Mama made this often as we grew up. Today I know it worked well because the hominy cuts the richness of sausage, and onions and peppers add crunch!
