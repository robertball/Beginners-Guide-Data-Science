Title:
ColePorter Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) bag coleslaw mix
3/4 cup mayo
1 tbsp dijon mustard
1/4 cup light brown sugar
3 tbsp cider vinegar
3 tbsp buttermilk
1/8 tsp salt, or to taste
1/4 cup onion, diced (optional)
1 tsp parsley

Directions:
In medium bowl combine cider vinegar and buttermilk with mayo.
Add lght brown sugar and whisk to dissolve sugar.
Add dijon mustard, salt, parsley to mixture.
Add onion and coleslaw to bowl and combine with mayo mix.
Chill before serving to marry the flavors.
