Title:
Texas Tea I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/970305.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 fluid ounce vodka
1/2 fluid ounce rum
1 fluid ounce tequila
1/2 lemon, juiced
1 tablespoon cola-flavored carbonated beverage

Directions:
In a cocktail mixer, combine vodka, rum, tequila, lemon juice and cola. Stir, and pour into glass filled with ice.

Submitter: 
Jan Fietz
Description: 
It's the same thing as a Long Island Ice Tea, except you omit the gin, and double the tequila.
