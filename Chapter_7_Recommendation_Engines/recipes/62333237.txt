Title:
Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 lb sausage
l cup fresh bread crumbs
1 onion chopped
1 1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 small can tomato sauce
Sauce:
1 1/4 can tomato sauce
3 T mustard
4 T brown Sugar
3 1/2 T Vinegar
1 Cup Water

Directions:
Lightly mix ingredients and form as loaf in shannow dish.  place in moderate oven (350).  Base with sauce as it cooks.
