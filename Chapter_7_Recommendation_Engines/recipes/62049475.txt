Title:
Bea's beer cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/486302.jpg
Ingredients:
4 oz. cream cheese
8 oz. sharp cheddar cheese, grated
1 1/2 TBSP. green onions
1 1/2 tsp. fresh parsley, chopped
1/2 tsp. dry mustard
1/4 cup beer
1/2 tsp. celery seed
4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
salt to taste

Directions:
Bring cheeses to room temperature.
Mix all ingredients, except salt, in a food processor until light and fluffy.
Taste to check seasoning  Chill to blend flavors, at least two hours, but preferably overnight.  Remove from refigerator 30 minutes before serving.  Serve with hearty crackers.

Submitter: 
cookin'mama
Description: 
This potted cheese was my mother in law's favorite.  No matter where it started out, it always ended up directly in front of her!  It keeps for a long time in the fridge and the flavor just keeps getting better.
