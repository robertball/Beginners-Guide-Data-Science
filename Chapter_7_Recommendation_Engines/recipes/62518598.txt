Title:
Rice and Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/511276.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup  Onions (Chopped)
1 Cup  Celery (Chopped)
4 Tablespoons Oil
6 Cups  Rice (Cooked)
2 Packages Broccoli (Frozen)
2 Cans Cream of Mushroom Soup
2 Cans Cream of Chicken Soup
1 Pound Velveeta

Directions:
Saute onion and celery in oil until soft.
Cook rice and broccoli separately according to package directions.
Mix all ingredients together in a two-quart baking dish. Bake at 375Â° for 30 to 45 minutes.
This may be prepared in advance and stored in the refrigerator.
