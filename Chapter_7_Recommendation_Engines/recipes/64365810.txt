Title:
Laura's Ultimate Veggie Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c. ground, boiled seitan (simmered in veg broth)
1 c. cooked beans, black or kidney
1 c. finely chopped carrot
1/2 c. chopped hazelnuts
1 c cooked wild rice, slightly undercooked (opt)
1 1/2 c faro or wheat groats, slightly under-cooked in water seasoned with Worcestershire sauce
1/4 c nutritional yeast
4 oz mushrooms, finely chopped
3/4 c rolled oats, coarsely chopped in processor
1 c cooked rice
4 tsp garlic powder
4 tsp onion powder
1/2 c dry mushroom powder (opt)
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
2 T tomato paste
1/4 tsp liquid smoke (opt)
1 T Spike seasoning
3 T red miso
3 T tahini
2 T molasses
6 T wheat gluten

Directions:
In a food processor, finely chop seitan and put into a large bowl.
Process beans til nearly smooth, add to bowl. Process carrot, hazelnuts, mushrooms, oats until finely chopped, add to bowl.
Add rest of ingredients to bowl and mix very well, using hands to knead the mixture to develop the gluten.
Cook right away or set in refrigerator to develop flavor. Make into thin patties and cook in a non-stick skillet over medium low heat about 3 min each side or bake in 350 oven about 20 min.
