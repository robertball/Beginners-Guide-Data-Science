Title:
Tropical Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cans Mixed Tropical Fruit
8 oz Macadamia Nuts
1/4 Cup Sweetened Flake Coconut
1/4 Cup Mini Marshmallows
1 Bannana (sliced)
1 tbls of Lemon Juice
1 tsp Sugar

Directions:
Place bannana slices in a mixing bowl and sprinkle with the lemon juice and sugar. Drain canned fruit and reserve juice. Add coconut to the juice and set aside. Add fruit nuts and marshmallows to the bannanas and toss. Pour coconut mixture over fruit and toss well. Chill.
