Title:
Fruit Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick Butter
3/4 cup Sugar
1 tsp Baking Powder
pinch of Salt
12 Plums or 8 Peaches
Lemon Juice
Sugar
Cinnamon

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs then combine with dry ingredients.
Pour into baking dish and place halved plums or peaches skin up.
Sprinkle with cinammon, sugar, and lemon juice.
Bake 1 hour at 350F

Submitter: 
prime47
Description: 
It's called fruit cobbler because you can make it with either plums or peaches, maybe even both!
