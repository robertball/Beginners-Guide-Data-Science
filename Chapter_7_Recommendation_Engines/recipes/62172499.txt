Title:
French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 thick slices 1- or 2-day-old french bread
4 medium eggs or 3 large eggs
1 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Directions:
Beat together egg, milk, nutmeg and vanilla.
Heat a griddle or skillet coated with cooking spray over medium-high heat. Wait for it to fully heat up.
Dunk each slice of bread in egg mixture, soaking both sides.  Place in pan, and cook on both sides until golden.  Serve hot.
