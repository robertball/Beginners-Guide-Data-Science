Title:
Sticky Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 cups popped corn
1/2 cup butter
10 large marshmallows
1/2 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Pop the corn in an air popper.  Melt the next three ingredients in the microwave in a glass bowl.  Pour over popcorn and stir until well coated.

Submitter: 
Stacey
Description: 
This came from a friend and is now the most requested family night treat from my children.  Like caramel corn only MUCH faster and fun to eat!
