Title:
Chicken Marsala Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
4 chicken breasts
1/2 C flour
2 T chopped garlic
1/2 # sliced mushrooms
1 C chicken broth
1/2 C tomato sauce
1 T basil
1 T oregano
1/2 C canned peas
1 C Marsala wine
3 lg. potatoes, cooked and cubed

Directions:
Melt butter in pan. Cut chicken into 1 inch strips, coat with flour and brown in butter. Add garlic and onion and cook 5 min. Remove everything from pan and set aside. Turn heat to high, add wine to deglaze pan. Add mushrooms, chicken and onions, broth, sauce, basil and oregano, potatoes and peas. Simmer 15 min. May be served immed. or is excellent when reheated.
