Title:
Amaretto and Mushroom Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/707999.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Chicken breasts
2-3 C. Sliced mushrooms
2 Tbsp. Margarine or Butter
3/4 C. Amaretto
1/2 C. Slivered almonds
S & P to taste

Directions:
Add sliced mushrooms and margarine to large skillet over medium heat.  S & P mushrooms and cook until softened.  Set aside.
Butterfly chicken breasts, S & P each side, then pan fry in skillet that was used for mushrooms until just cooked (you may need to add a bit more margarine or some olive oil to the pan).
Add slivered almonds and stir until toasted.
Add amaretto and cooked mushrooms & cook until alcohol has burned off and sauce is thickened to a light syrup.
Serve each chicken breast topped with mushrooms and almonds and dribbled with sauce.
