Title:
Tomatillo Cream Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small chicken broiled breast
5-6 lb. fresh tomatillos, husked, washed, caps removed
1/2 tsp salt
16 oz salt-free chicken broth
3 oz cream cheese
1 clove garlic, finely chopped
1/2 tsp. mixed herbs such as McCormick Parsley Patch
2 tsp. green jalapeno salsa (hot)
small red pepper
sour cream
cilantro

Directions:
Par boil the tomatillos in water with salt.
Remove from heat and drain as soon as they begin to soften, and rinse with cold water.
Cut the tomatillos into quarters
Return to pan, add chicken broth, and bring to a slow simmer
Add cream cheese, garlic, herbs and salsa
Puree with hand blender and continue to simmer slowly for at least 1/2 hour, stirring frequently
Add more chicken broth and/or salsa to desired consistency and flavor; DO NOT ADD SALT
Dice chicken and red pepper to small pieces and mix together in small bowl
Ladle soup into serving bowls, top with 1 tsp. sour cream and some cilantro leaves.  Arrange small amounts of chicken/red pepper mixture around the sour cream.

Submitter: 
Fred Guevara
Description: 
A hearty, flavorful and colorful soup to compliment your Mexican meal
