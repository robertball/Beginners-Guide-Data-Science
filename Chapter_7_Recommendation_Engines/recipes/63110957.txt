Title:
Creamy Chicken Over Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts
1 qt chicken stock
1 T canola oil
1 medium onion, chopped
4 cloves of garlic, chopped (more or less to taste)
1/2 yellow bell pepper, diced
1 C frozen peas and carrots blend
1/2 C milk
4 oz cream cheese, cut into cubes
1 tsp dried parsley
2 T flour
salt and white pepper, to taste
2 C cooked brown rice

Directions:
Poach chicken in the stock.  When cooked, chop chicken into 3/4 inch cubes. Reserve 1/2 cup of strained stock and save the rest for another recipe.
While chicken is poaching, warm oil in a skillet.  Saute the onion, garlic and bell peppers until the onion is translucent.
Add peas and carrots and cook till heated through, a few minutes.
Add the 1/2 cup reserved stock and the parsley.  Bring to a simmer then stir in the milk and cream cheese cubes.
Cook till smooth, then add the chicken and the flour.  Cook a few minutes longer till thickened.  Season with salt and white pepper to taste.  Spoon over cooked brown rice and serve.

Submitter: 
*~Lissa~*
Description: 
Cubed chicken and vegetables come together in a cream cheese accented sauce served over hearty brown rice.
