Title:
Couscous Salad with Tomato and Basil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided
2 Garlic cloves, minced
1 (15.75-ounce)can fat-free, less-sodium chicken broth
1-1/2 cups uncooked couscous
2 cups chopped tomato
1/3 cup thinly sliced fresh basil
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 cup (1 ounce) crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
Combine 1 tablespoon olive oil and garlic in a large microwave-safe bowl, and microwave at high for 45 seconds. Add the broth; microwave at high 4 minutes or until mixture simmers. Gradually stir in couscous; cover and let stand 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork. Stir in the tomato, basil, vinegar, salt and pepper. Drizzle with 1 tablespoon oil, and sprinkle with cheese.

Submitter: 
cpoko
Description: 
This is a super easy, great tasting salad that can be served hot or cold. The balsamic vinegar and feta add great flavor. You can also add chopped chicken breasts to make it a complete meal.
