Title:
One Pan Mexican Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1004989.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large potatoes, washed, peeled and sliced into 1/4" thick wedges
1 bell pepper,(color of your choice)seeded and sliced like potatoes
1 large onion, chopped
1 1/2 tbls. olive oil
1 1/2 tsp. kosher salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1/2 cup Herdez  Red Guajillo Chile Cooking Sauce
1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
2 green onions, sliced thin

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
In a 13"x9" casserole dish, toss together the potatoes, olive oil, salt, pepper, onion and bell pepper. Mix well.
Bake uncovered on middle rack for 20 minutes. Then mix well again and bake for another 10 minutes.
Then mix in the Herdez cooking sauce, top with shredded cheddar and bake for 5 more minutes.
Serve warm, topped with green onions.

Submitter: 
Lillian
Description: 
A one pan side dish that is mild in spice but big in flavor thanks to the Herdez cooking sauce! Ready in 35 minutes!
