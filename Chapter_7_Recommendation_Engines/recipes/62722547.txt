Title:
Carmel Ice Cream Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C Heavy Whipping cream, divided
3/4 C Sugar
1/2 light corn syrup
1/4 C butter
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract

Directions:
In a heavy saucepan, combine 1/2 C cream, sugar, corn syrup, butter and salt. Bring to a boil over medium heat until a candy thermometer reads 234 deg. (soft ball stage), stirring constantly. Cool to 220 deg. stir in remaining cream. Bring to a boil. Remove from the heat; stir in vanilla.
