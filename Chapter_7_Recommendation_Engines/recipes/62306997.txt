Title:
New Hampshire White Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Water
2 1/2 Tbsp Butter or Margarine
2 1/2 Tbsp Sugar
1 tsp Salt
3 C Bread Flour
1 1/2 tsp Active Dry Yeast

Directions:
Place ingredients in breadmaker in order listed.  Select 1 1/2 lb loaf, white bread cycle.
