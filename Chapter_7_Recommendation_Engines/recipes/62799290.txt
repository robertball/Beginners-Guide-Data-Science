Title:
Stovetop Chicken Divan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chicken, cooked, cubed
Half package egg noodles (or 6-8 oz noodles of your choice)
2 tbsp butter
1/4 c flour
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1 1/2 tsp curry powder
2 c milk
1/2 c grated parmesan
1/2 c sour cream
1/2 c shredded mozzarella
1-2 heads broccoli florettes, chopped

Directions:
Cook noodles according to package directions.
Make white sauce: melt butter in pan, whisk in flour, mustard and curry. Slowly add milk and stir constantly until it thickens. Reduce heat and stir in parmesan, sour cream and then mozzarella.
Blanch broccoli: bring small pan of water to boil, add broccoli for 4 minutes, remove with slotted spoon.
Combine all in bowl and serve.

Submitter: 
charlotte221
Description: 
I wanted a healthier version of chicken divan with less cheese and no condensed soups that I could make on the stovetop. This is an admittedly basic recipe but my husband and toddlers loved it! Custom versions and adjustment are welcome -- and appreciated!
