Title:
Restaurant style spicy ranch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp hidden valley ranch salad dressing(buttermilk recipe),
1 cup mayonnaise,
1/2 cup buttermilk,
1/2 tsp coarse ground black pepper,
1/2 tsp garlic powder,
1/2 tsp paprika,
1/4-1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl.  Mix well.  Cover bowl and chill dressing for at least 30 minutes before serving.
