Title:
Savory Roast Ham with Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Shank Ham Roast
Rub:
4 tsp chopped garlic
1 1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
2 tsp sage
1 tsp ground mustard
1 tsp parsley
1/4 c olive oil
Glaze:
1 c honey
1/4 tsp ground mustard
1/4 tsp ginger
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 475. Mix Rub ingredients. Rub ham roast on all sides with Rub. Place on rack in roasting pan. Insert oven proof meat thermometer in thickest part near the bone. Place in oven for 20 minutes. Reduce oven temperature to 325. Brush ham with glaze, and every 25 minutes thereafter until ham reads 140 on meat thermometer nearest the bone. Tent with foil for 15 minutes, then carve.
