Title:
Tomato Consomme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups chicken broth
16 oz can of crushed tomatoes
2 tsp tomato paste
1/2 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp basil
1/2 tsp allspice
2 tsp lemon juice
2 Tbsp dry sherry
2 Tbsp parsley finely chopped

Directions:
Place all ingredients except sherry and parsley in a saucepan.
Simmer for 15 minutes.
Puree in a blender and return to saucepan.
Add the wine and garnish with parsley.
Bring to simmer.
Serve hot or cold.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
Serve before roast beef or steak.
