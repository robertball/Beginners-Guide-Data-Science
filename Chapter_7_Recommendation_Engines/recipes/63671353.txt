Title:
Mediterranean Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz Penne or your favorite shape pasta
1/3 cup Pesto (homemade is best)
2/3 cup quartered cherry tomatoes
1/2 cup quartered kalamata olives
2 Tbls minced red onion
3/4 cup canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained
1 clove minced garlic (optional)
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions.  While pasta is cooking, quarter tomatoes and olives, mince onions and add all of it to a large bowl.  Rinse and drain chickpeas before adding to other vegetables. If your pesto is very garlicy, you may not need the additional garlic.  Add salt and pepper to taste then mix all of the ingredients together and let set until pasta is done. Drain pasta, rinse under cool water. Let drip until most of the water is dripped out, 5 min.  Mix pasta with the other ingredients spoon on top of a bed of salad greens and serve.

Submitter: 
Mary P.
Description: 
This pasta salad is a quick, fresh and healthy meal.  Double or triple for a crowd pleaser.
