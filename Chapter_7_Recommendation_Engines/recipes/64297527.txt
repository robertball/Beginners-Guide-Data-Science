Title:
Rice Breakfast Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup brown rice
1/2 cup water
1 cup garbanzo bean flour
1/2 cup rolled oats
1/2 cup dried currants
1/4 cup unsweetened choppped coconut
2 T sunflower seeds
2 T chia seeds
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/3 cup peanut butter
1/3 cup unsweetened applesauce
1/4 cup honey
1 egg

Directions:
Heat oven to 375.  Spray 8" pie pan.
Bring rice and water to boil in small pan.  Turn to simmer, cook until water is absorbed, 20-25 minutes
Combine peanut butter, applesauce, honey and egg.
Add cooked rice, garbanzo bean flour, oats, currants, coconut, baking powder and cinnamon.
Spread evenly in pie pan.  Bake 20 minutes, until edges are golden brown.
Cut in wedges.
