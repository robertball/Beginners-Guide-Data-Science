Title:
Delicious Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 1/2 pounds bottom round
2 carrots
2 celery stalks
1 medium onion or 2-3 shallots
2-3 medium potatoes
3-4 garlic cloves chopped
Enough water to cover
Salt to taste

Directions:
Chop veggies into 1&quot; chunks.
Coursely chop garlic.
Place pot roast in slow cooker.
Add veggies.
Add enough water to cover.
Add salt.
Cook on High setting for 3 to 4 hours, or on Low setting for 8 to 9 hours.

Submitter: 
Mary Mock
Description: 
Perfect comfort food! Leftover's great for quick meals.  Makes great sandwiches on your favorite roll.
