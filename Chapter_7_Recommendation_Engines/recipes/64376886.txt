Title:
Stracciatella Soup with Spinach and Tortellini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3958381.jpg
Ingredients:
3   32oz  cartons of Natures Promise Organic Chicken Broth
1   package (20 oz) of Three Cheese Tortellini (such as Buitoni)
1   package of baby spinach
2   eggs
Fresh Basil  ¼ (+) cup chopped
Nutmeg  ½ (+) teaspoon, or (to taste)
Salt (to taste)
Pepper (to taste)
Parm Cheese

Directions:
In a large pot empty the chicken broth, add the basil, nutmeg, salt, and pepper and bring to a soft boil, let boil for about 5- 10 min to get the spices well blended.
In the meantime whisk the two eggs together in a small bowl.
After spices are blended to the right aroma, bring pot up to high heat and through in the tortellini.
Slowly pour in the egg, whisking quickly at the same time.
Add in about 1-2 cups of spinach (to your liking).
When tortellini is done (5 -7 min) remove from heat and serve; place parm cheese on the table for ppl to sprinkle in their bowl?
