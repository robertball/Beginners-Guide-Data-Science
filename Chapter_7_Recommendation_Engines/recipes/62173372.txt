Title:
Chocolate Banana Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/285633.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3.5 oz.) box instant chocolate pudding
2 cups milk
3 Medium Bananas
1 Graham Cracker crust
1 cup heavy whipping cream
2 Tbsp. powdered sugar

Directions:
Prepare pudding according to package with two cups milk.
Let the mixture firm.
Slice bananas and layer them on the bottom of the crust.
Pour pudding mixture over the banans and spread until theyre covered.
Let the filling set up at least half an hour in the fridge.
In a bowl, whisk the whipping cream with the powdered sugar until you get stiff peaks.
Spread whipped cream over the top of the set pie.

My variation on a delicious classic dessert but that is very simple to make.
