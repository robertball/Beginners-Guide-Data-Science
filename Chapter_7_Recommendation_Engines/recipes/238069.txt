Title:
Summery Afternoon Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5397804.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1/3 mango - peeled, seeded, and diced
4 strawberries, hulled
1/4 cup low-fat peach yogurt
3 teaspoons honey

Directions:
Blend milk, mango, strawberries, yogurt, and honey together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Leardi Family
Description: 
Whenever I need a quick refreshing cool-down, this smoothie is my summer favorite!
