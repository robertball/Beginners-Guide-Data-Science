Title:
Homemade Dishwasher Soap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/702123.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup 20 Mule Team Borax
1 cup Arm & Hammer Washing Soda
1/4 cup Kosher salt
2 pkgs. unsweetened lemonade ~or~
1/4 c. citric acid (i.e. LemiShineÂ®)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a plastic container or zip-lock bag.  Use 1 tablespoon each time you run the dishwasher.  For an added sparkle to your glasses/dishes add white vinegar to the Rinse Aid compartment.
***I have city water and a water softener.
