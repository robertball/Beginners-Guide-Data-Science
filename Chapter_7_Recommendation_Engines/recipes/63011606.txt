Title:
Stevie Lee's Easy Glogg Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 individual packet hot apple cider powder
8 oz. red wine
8 oz. water
1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp. ground cloves
2 T. brown sugar
1 slice orange or lemon

Directions:
Heat hot apple cider packet and water in small saucepan on the stove over low heat. Add red wine, spices, sugar and citrus slice. Let simmer about 5 minutes. Taste and add additional sugar or spices if desired (sweetness will depend on what you like and the type of wine used). Pour into two mugs and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Stevie Lee
Description: 
This is something I came up with when I really wanted glogg but didn't have all of the ingredients. Perfect on a cold night in front of the fire!
