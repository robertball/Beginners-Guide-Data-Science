Title:
Grated Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups sugar
3 tbsp. flour
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
2 eggs - lightly beaten
1/2 cup butter - melted
1 tsp. vanilla
3 cups coarsely grated apples (about 4 apples)
2 unbaked pie crusts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Place one crust in pie plate.
Combine flour, sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg in a large bowl.
Add eggs, butter and vanilla, then fold in apples.
Pour mixture into pie crust and then place other crust on top.
Crimp edges and cut a slit to vent steam.
Bake for 10 minutes, then reduce temperature to 350 ad bake for an additional 60-65 minutes or until the top is golden brown.
Cool slightly before serving.
