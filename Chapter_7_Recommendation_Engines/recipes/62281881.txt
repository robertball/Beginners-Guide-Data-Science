Title:
Mango Pineapple Berry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/351805.jpg
Ingredients:
1 mango, peeled, seeded and cut into large pieces
1 cup frozen strawberries
1 cup low-fat vanilla yogurt
6 oz. pineapple juice
1/2 cup frozen blueberries

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
A refreshing tropical smoothie.  Full of flavor and antioxidants!
