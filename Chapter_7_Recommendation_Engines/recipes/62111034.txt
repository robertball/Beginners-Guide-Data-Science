Title:
Macadamia Nut Crusted Talapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Talapia fillets
10oz bag of macadamia nuts
1 tbs soy sauce
2 cloves of garlic
1 ts of hot sauce
1 tbs brown sugar
1 tbs olive oil
1 ts pepper

Directions:
Pre-heat the oven to broil
Wash fish, set aside
Combine the remaining ingredients into a food processor
Place a thick layer of the macadamia paste on the bottom of a standard pan, not a broiler pan, place fish on top. Cook for about 15 minutes

Submitter: 
Chrissy Mc
Description: 
Broiled white fish coated with a chopped macadamia nut mixture
