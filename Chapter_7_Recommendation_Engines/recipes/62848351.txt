Title:
Nellie's Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. hamburger meat
1/2 lb. bacon or sausage
1 chopped onion
1/3 c. brown sugar
1/3 c. sugar
1/4 c. catsup
1 T mustard
2 T molasses
1/2 t. chili powder
1 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
15 oz. kidney beans
15 oz. pork n beans
15 oz. butter beans

Directions:
Cook the hamburger meat with onion with the bacon (chopped) or sausage. Rinse the beans and add. Add the rest and simmer.
