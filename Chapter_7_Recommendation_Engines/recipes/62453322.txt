Title:
Mrs. Fields Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups margarine
2 cups sugar
2 cups brown sugar
4 eggs
2 tsps vanilla
5 cups blened oats (finely blended)
4 cups floour
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
2 tsp baking powder
2 bags semi sweet chocolate chips (24 oz. total)
1 8 oz Hershey bar (shaved) (sweet chocolate)
1 cup chopped nuts (macadamia or hazel nut)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Cream together the margarine, sugar, brown sugar.
Add the eggs and vanilla to the creamed mixture
In a large bowl mix the oats, flour, baking soda, salt and baking powder, chocolates and nutes
Add the creamed mix to the dry mix.
After thoroughly mixed, form dough into golf ball sized balls and place on greased cookie sheet
Bake at 350 degrees for 10 -12 minutes
