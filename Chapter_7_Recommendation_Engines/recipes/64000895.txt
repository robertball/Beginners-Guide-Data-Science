Title:
Grandma's Fudge Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 2 cups sugar
* 3 Tablespoons all-purpose flour or cornstarch
* 3 Tablespoons cocoa
* dash of salt
* 2 cups of milk
* 5 egg yolks, beaten
* 1/2 stick of butter
* 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
* 2 baked pie crust
* 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
* 2 teaspoons sugar
Meringue:
* 5 egg whites
* 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
* 2 teaspoons sugar

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Mix the first four ingredients in a saucepan. Gradually stir in milk. Cook over moderate heat until mixture thickens and boils. Boil for 1 minute. Stir half of the mixture into egg yolks, then blend egg mixture into the hot mixture in the saucepan. Add butter and vanilla and boil until very thick. Pour into baked pie crusts. Beat egg whites until stiff. Fold in sugar and cream of tartar. Place half of the meringue on each pie and bake until brown.
