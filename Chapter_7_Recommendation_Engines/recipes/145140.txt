Title:
Limegasm Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/970326.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can frozen limeade
3 fluid ounces tequila
3 fluid ounces triple sec (orange-flavored liqueur)
2 cups crushed ice
kosher salt

Directions:
Combine the limeade, tequila, and triple sec in a blender; fill with ice; blend until smooth.
Wet the rims of six glasses and dip in kosher salt. Pour mixture into glasses to serve.

Submitter: 
Julianna
Description: 
These are close enough to margaritas for a working-through-college budget.
