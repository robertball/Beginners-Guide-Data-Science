Title:
Clean eating snickerdoodle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1130693.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups whole wheat flour
1/2 tsp. cream of tartar
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. sea salt
1/2 cup coconut oil
1 cup coconut sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla extract
2 Tbsp. Coconut sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon - heaping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Line cookie sheets with parchment and spray with cooking spray
Sift the flour, cream of tarter, baking soda,1 tsp. cinnamon, sea salt in a bowl and set aside
Combine coconut oil, 2 eggs, vanilla extract, and 1 cup coconut sugar in a food processor until well mixed
Add the dry mixture and process until combined
Combine 2 Tbls. coconut sugar and heaping tsp of cinnamon in a bowl
Roll cookie dough into a ball and then roll in the sugar mixture
Flatten slightly and place on the lined cookie sheet
Bake for 8 to 10 minutes

Submitter: 
Melissa Lindsey Gibson
Description: 
This is not your typical snickerdoodle, but they are so good you won't feel guilty eating them.
