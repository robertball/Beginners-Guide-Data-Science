Title:
Scalloped Potatoes and Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 large potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
1 large chopped onion
3 tablespoons butter or margarine
3 cloves of garlic; minced
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 3/4 cups vegtable broth
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
3/4 cup shredded cheddar
Paprika

Directions:
In a greased 2-1/2-qt. baking dish, layer potatoes and onion.
In a saucepan, melt the butter and garlic; stir in flour until smooth. Gradually add broth, mayonnaise, salt and pepper and cheese; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thick and bubbly. Pour over potatoes. Sprinkle with paprika.
Cover and bake at 325 degrees for 2 hours or until tender.
