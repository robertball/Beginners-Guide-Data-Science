Title:
Purple Beet, Carrot and Onion Medly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 beets
3 long purple carrots
3 small purple onions (apx. 1 cup)
3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
Coarse salt to taste
Ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Slice beets, carrots and onions into a casserole dish.  Splash with 3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar.  Sprinkle with salt and freshly ground pepper to taste.  Cover and bake for 40 minutes at 400 degrees.

Submitter: 
Jennifer
Description: 
A delicious combination of purple veggies.  They each retain their unique flavor while complimenting eachother nicely.
