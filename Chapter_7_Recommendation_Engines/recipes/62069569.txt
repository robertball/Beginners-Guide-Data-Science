Title:
Great-Great Gramma Crane's No Fail Chocolate Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups powdered sugar
3 TBS butter
1 tsp vanilla
5-6 TBS cocoa powder
5 TBS milk
dash of salt
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (OPTIONAL)

Directions:
1.  Melt the butter in a medium size saucepan and add in the cocoa.  Once it is blended, pour in the milk and stir.
2.  Add in the butter, vanilla, salt and powdered sugar.

Submitter: 
PREGOCOOK
Description: 
This is a family recipe that always comes in handy in a pinch.  It is super easy to make and tastes fabulous!  I like to use it with Nancy's Perfect Chocolate Cake
