Title:
Orange and Grape Tortellini Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980182.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 ounce) package BUITONI® Refrigerated Three Cheese Tortellini, prepared according to package directions, chilled
1 cup red seedless grapes
1 cup green seedless grapes
1 (11 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained
1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
1/4 cup chopped walnuts, toasted
1 cup honey Dijon salad dressing

Directions:
COMBINE pasta, grapes, oranges, onion and nuts in a medium bowl. Add dressing; toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Buitoni®
Description: 
Mandarin oranges and grapes pair perfectly with cheese tortellini in this cool and refreshing pasta salad. Serve with crunchy breadsticks and tall glasses of Nestea with a twist of lemon.
