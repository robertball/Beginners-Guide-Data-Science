Title:
Cream Cheese Garlic Breadsticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup  (1/2 of 8-oz. tub) PHILADELPHIA 1/3 Less Fat than Cream Cheese
1 tsp.  garlic powder (I like to use minced fresh garlic to taste)
1 can  (11 oz.) refrigerated soft breadsticks

Directions:
HEAT oven to 350Â°F.
MIX cream cheese and garlic powder until blended.
UNROLL breadstick dough into 12 pieces; spread with cream cheese mixture. Fold each piece lengthwise in half, then twist slightly. Place on baking sheet.
BAKE 15 min. or until golden brown. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
FunwithFood
Description: 
This is a different twist on garlic breadsticks. It is a lower calorie, lower fat option without sacrificing on taste. I love the creamy garlicky filling in these soft breadsticks. Great with Italian dishes, as well as soup or salad. Recipe is from Kraft Philadelphia cream cheese.
