Title:
Orange Glazed chicken wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh OJ (2 oranages)
2 TBSPS Grated orange zest (3 oranges)
6 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup soy sauce
1 tbsp brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
4 pounds chicken wings

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. In a large bowl, combine oj with orange zest, garlic, soy sauce, brown sugar, salt, and pepper. Add the chicken wings and toss to coat.
One 2 large baking sheets, arrange the wings in a single layer. Reserve 1/4 cup of the oj mixture over the wings. Bake for 20 minutes.
