Title:
Fun Shepherds Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1 pouch of Costco mashed potatoes
2. 1-1 1/2 pds raw, lean hamburger
3. 3 sliced large mushrooms
4. 10 oz bag frozen green beans
5. 1 can cream of mushroom soup
6. 1 tsp garlic power, basil, and paprika
7. 1 c. shredded sharp cheddar cheese
8. 1/3 c. each chopped red and yellow peppers

Directions:
1. Put peppers, raw hamburger, green beans, and soup in a 9x13 baking pan.
2. Mix thoroughly with hands.
3. Put cheese over top of all.
4. Make mashed potatoes and add spices.
5. Put potatoes over mixture.
6. Bake at 350 for 30 min.
