Title:
Nutty Monkey Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/563660.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 medium bananas, mashed
3/4 cup sugar
1 egg, lightly beaten
1/3 cup canola oil
3/4 cup mini chocolate chips
3/4 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease 18 muffin cups, or line with muffin papers.
In a large bowl, mix together 1 1/2 cups flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt.
In a separate bowl, beat together bananas, sugar, egg and canola oil.
Stir the banana mixture into the flour mixture just until moistened.
Fold in chocolate chips and pecans.
Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups.
Bake in preheated oven for 16 to 20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Pam Ziegler Lutz
Description: 
Crunchy pecans add to the texture of these delicious muffins that will keep everyone asking for more!
