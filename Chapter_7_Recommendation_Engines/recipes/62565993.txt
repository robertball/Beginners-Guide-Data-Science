Title:
** Chewy Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c. butter or margarine, softened
2 c. sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla
2 c. unsifted AP flour
3/4 c. Hershey's cocoa
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1 c. finely chopped nuts (opt.)

Directions:
Cream butter or margarine & suger in lg. mixing bowl. Add eggs & vanilla; blend well.
Combine dry ingredients & blend into creamed mixture. Stir in nuts, if desired.
Drop by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 350 degrees for 7 min. (Do not overbake; cookies will be soft. They will puff during baking, flatten upon cooling.)
Cool on cookie sheet until set, about 1 min.; remove to wire rack to cool completely.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
From Hershey's easy-does-it recipe #6. Used to make these when the kids were little. These cookies are great for making ice cream sandwiches.
