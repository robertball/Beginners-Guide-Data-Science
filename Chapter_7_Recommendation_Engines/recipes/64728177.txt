Title:
Collard Green AWESOMENESS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6170678.jpg
Ingredients:
2 bunches Collard Greens
1 Onion
2 Slices Hickory Smoked Bacon
1/4 cup water x 2
1 can Cream of Chicken soup
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
OK: SUPER EASY
Slice up bacon and onions and brown in pot.
Take 1/4 cup salted/peppered water and deglaze bottom.
Rinse greens and chop & drop into pot - stems and all.
Cover and bring to medium high heat until greens turn bright.
Stir gently until all greens begin wilting down. Re-cover.
Mix can of soup with another 1/4 cup water. It will be thick.
Lift lid off greens pot and pour soup in circle all over the top.
Use a spoon to “ice” the green tops with soup mix like a cake icing. Re-cover.
Heat goes down to low and let it all simmer 10 mins.
Lift lid and mix throughly. Check for seasonings and add salt/pepper as needed. Re-cover.
Simmer an additional 10 mins, stir again, cover, remove from heat.
Let stand 10 mins before final stir and serving.
