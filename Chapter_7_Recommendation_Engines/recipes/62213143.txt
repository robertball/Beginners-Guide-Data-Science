Title:
Chuck's  Chunky Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
8 oz. brown sugar
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter
4 1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
16 oz. peanut butter
1 Tblsp. cinnamon
1 Tblsp. vanilla
2 tsp. baking soda
3/4 bag of chocolate chips or mini Hershey kisses
1/4 pkg. M&M's
raisins (optional)

Directions:
In mixing bowl combine eggs and sugars.  Add butter; mix well.  Add oats.  Add peanut butter, cinnamon, vanilla and baking soda; mix well.  Stir in chocolate chips, M&M's and raisins.  Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls.  Flatten slightly if desired.  Bake 350 deg. for 12-14 minutes or until set.
NOTE: These cookies do not contain flour.
