Title:
Braised Lentils with Chard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. diced yellow onion
1 diced celery stalk
1 diced carrot
3 minced garlic cloves
1 1/2 c. green lentils
3 c. veggie broth
1/2 tsp. salt
2 c. packed chard leaved, sliced
1 T. olive oil

Directions:
Heat 1 T. oil and add onion; saut√© 5 minutes. Add celery, carrot, and garlic and cook 3 minutes. Add lentils and stir to coat.
Add broth, cover and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and cook till lentils are tender, about1 1/2 hours (or presoak or use pressure cooker! Original recipe said 40 minutes)
Stir in salt, and top lentils with chard. Cover and cook till wilted, about 5 minutes. Stir in olive oil and serve.
