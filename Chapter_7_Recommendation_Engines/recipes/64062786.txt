Title:
Corn Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Bag Frozen Corn
8 oz Block Cream Cheese( Only use 3/4 of it )
1/2 Stick Butter
5 slices Jalapeno Pepper (optional)

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a microwable dish, cover and micro for 5 min... stir..Micro in 1 min increments until all mixed and warm
