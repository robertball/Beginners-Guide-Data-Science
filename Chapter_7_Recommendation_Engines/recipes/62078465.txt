Title:
Sally Lunn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter
1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup milk
2 cups flour
3 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Cream the butter and sugar together. Add the beaten eggs. Add the milk alternately with the sifted dry ingredients.
Pour into 2 greased loaf pans or 2 greased pie pans.
Bake 20-25 min at 450(F) deg.
Use toothpick to test readiness.

Submitter: 
Jane T.
Description: 
Light as a feather and fit for a king!Baked in oven with basic ingredients. Mmm good when served with real butter.
