Title:
Incredibly Delicious Italian Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup buttermilk
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup butter
2 cups white sugar
5 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla,or vanilla butternut vanilla.or almond extract
1 cup flaked coconut
1 teaspoon baking powder
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
8 ounces cream cheese
1/2 cup butter
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 cups confectioners' sugar
2 tablespoons light cream
1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
1 cup sweetened flaked coconut

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease three 9 inch round cake pans or 13 X 9.  In a small bowl, dissolve the baking soda in the buttermilk; set aside.
In a large bowl, cream together 1 cup butter, egg yolks and white sugar until light and fluffy. Mix in the buttermilk mixture, 1 teaspoon vanilla, 1 cup coconut, baking powder and flour alternating. Stir until just combined. Fold in beaten egg whites Pour batter into the prepared pans.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.
To Make Frosting:  In a medium bowl, combine cream cheese, 1/2 cup butter, 1 teaspoon vanilla and confectioners' sugar. Beat until light and fluffy.  Mix in a small amount of cream to attain the desired consistency. Stir in chopped nuts and remaining flaked coconut. Spread between layers and on top and sides of cooled cake. Set for 1/2 to 1 day in refrigerator.
