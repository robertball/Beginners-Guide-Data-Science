Title:
Traditional Chile con Queso Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart roasted diced green chile
2 T. butter
1/2 med. onion diced
1 clove garlic finely chopped
1 (14.5 oz.)can diced tomatoes
1 T. Caldo de Pollo or chicken bullion
1/2 can evaporated milk
2-3 cups mozarella cheese

Directions:
Sautee onion and garlic in butter on medium heat until transparent.  Add green chile and heat through.  Add tomatoes and bring to a soft boil.  Add canned milk and chicken bullion, bring to boil again.  Remove from heat and add cheese one cup at a time until desired thickness is reached.
Excellent with chips or tortillas.

Submitter: 
CHILIMEX5
Description: 
This is an authentic traditional recipe full of flavor.  It is creamy and rich and a wonderful use of roasted green chile.  Everyone I make it for asks for the recipe.
