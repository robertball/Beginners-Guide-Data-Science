Title:
Orange Chocolate Breakfast Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/772096.jpg
Ingredients:
1 orange - peeled, quartered, and cut into 1/2 inch slices
2 tablespoons chocolate syrup
1 cup cold milk
1 cup crushed ice

Directions:
Combine orange pieces, chocolate syrup, milk, and ice in a blender. Blend until smooth. Pour into a tall glass and enjoy.

Submitter: 
c4soccermix
Description: 
This drink is a great-tasting breakfast treat that only takes about 10 minutes to make. Why not try a breakfast drink with chocolate? Drink up and enjoy!
