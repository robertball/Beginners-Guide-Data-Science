Title:
Harvest Vegetable Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. fall vegetable (any kind you can mash)
1/4 c. water
1/2 c. onion, finely chopped
1/4 c. flour
1/2 c. French or Catalina dressing
1 egg
1 heaping Tablespoon brown sugar
Breadcrumbs to top

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cube vegetable and put in microwavable dish with water. Microwave on high 15 minutes or until easy to mash.
Stir vegetable with all other ingredients in casserole dish. Top with breadcrumbs. Bake 30-35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Tina Cochrane de Monroy
Description: 
A versatile recipe that lends itself to any vegetable you have in abundance. If you have a bumper-crop in your garden, this is the perfect side dish.
