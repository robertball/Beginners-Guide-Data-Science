Title:
Snoodge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
2 cans Del Monte Fresh Cut Whole Kernel Corn
2 cans Green Giant Kitchen Sliced Green Beans
Two Pouches Betty Crocker Homestyle Creamy Butter Mashed Potatoes
1 1/3 cup milk
4 tablespoons butter
Salt to taste

Directions:
Brown ground beef in a large pot.
After beef is fully cooked, add in both cans of both corn and green beans and stir together.
In separate saucepan, cook potatoes according to package instructions.
Once potatoes are ready, fold the potatoes into beef/bean/corn mixture.  Heat through and add salt to taste.
