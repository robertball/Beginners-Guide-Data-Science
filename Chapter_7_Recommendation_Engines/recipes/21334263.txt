Title:
Diana's Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 LB hamberger
diced onion to taste
salt/pepper/garlic powder
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1/2 can water

Directions:
Brown the hamberger and onions. Season to taste and drain. Mix in soup and Water. serve over egg noodles.
