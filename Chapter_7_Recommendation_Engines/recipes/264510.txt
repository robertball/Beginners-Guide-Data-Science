Title:
Simple Vegan Tzatziki Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6203335.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) package soft tofu, chilled
1 cucumber, peeled and quartered
1/4 teaspoon orange zest, or to taste

Directions:
Puree tofu, cucumber, and orange zest in a food processor until smooth.

Submitter: 
stronglive1
Description: 
A mild and vegan version of tzatziki sauce, a great refreshing, cooling sauce or dip.
