Title:
Fun Santa Vegetable Tray Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4573365.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower, separated into florets
2 red bell peppers, cut into strips
1/2 cucumber, sliced
1 cup cherry tomatoes
2 black olives

Directions:
Create a Santa face out of all the vegetables: cauliflower for the beard, red bell peppers for hat and mouth, cucumber slices for the face, cherry tomatoes for nose and shoulders, and olives for the eyes.

Submitter: 
Francesca
Description: 
If you're looking for healthy and fun ideas for Christmas, try this veggie tray shaped like Santa. Serve with hummus or any dip.
