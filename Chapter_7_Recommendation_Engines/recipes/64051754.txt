Title:
Potato Spuds topped with Low fat Southwestern Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1126590.jpg
Ingredients:
4 potatoes, 1lb of ground turkey, 1 can  organic diced tomatoes, 1 cup frozen organic corn, 3 organic carrots grated, 1 small onion chopped, 1/2 green pepper chopped, 1 can of organic kidney beans rinsed, 2 Tbl chili powder, 1 Tbl garlic powder, 1 Tbl Italian seasoning, celery salt, cheese (optional), sour cream (optional), chives (optional), jalapenos (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Poke then rub oil on potatoes and sprinkle with celery salt.  Put directly on center oven rack and place a cookie sheet underneath on other rack to catch drippings; cook 1 hr or until cooked.  Add ground turkey, onion, carrots, chili & garlic powder, Italian seasoning and a drizzle of oil in a large pot.  Cook on medium/ high heat while stirring for 5-7 min.  Once turkey is cooked stir in; tomatoes, green peppers, corn, kidney beans and 16oz water.  Reduce heat to simmer uncovered for 45 min.  Once thick add celery salt to taste.  Fork open  a potato, add huge spoonful of chili then top with cheese, sour cream, chives and jalapenos!

Submitter: 
Smokin Poston
Description: 
This is a great low fat Southwestern turkey chili recipe that is best served over potatoes.  I make enough to make several leftover dishes for  the weeks lunches.
