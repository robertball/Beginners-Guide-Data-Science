Title:
Nanny 129's  roasted potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3475696.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Small potatoes, quartered
1 Tbs or more, Dry ranch or Caesar dressing mix
Pam

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
Peel and quarter potatoes, rinse and pat dry
Spray with pam
Sprinkle with ranch mix, mix well
Place on baking pan lined with parchment paper and place in oven.
Stir after 20 mins and cook until crisp. The time depends on the size of the potato wedges.
