Title:
Caprese Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 Toothpics
20 Grape Tomatoes
10 oz Mozzarella Cheese
2 Tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2 Tablespoons Chopped Basil
Pinch Salt
Pinch Pepper

Directions:
Cut Cheese into pieces smaller than Tomatoes.
Toss all ingredients until Tomatoes and Cheese well coated.
Put 1 Tomato and 1 piece of Cheese on each Toothpick.
