Title:
Chicken Quesadillas and Fiesta Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980311.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast, cubed
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's® Condensed Cheddar Cheese Soup
1/2 cup Pace® Chunky Salsa or Pace® Picante Sauce
10 flour tortillas (8-inch)
Fiesta Rice:
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's® Condensed Chicken Broth
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup Pace® Chunky Salsa
2 cups uncooked instant white rice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
Cook chicken in nonstick skillet until done  and juices evaporate, stirring often. Add soup and salsa and heat through.
Spread about 1/3 cup soup mixture on half of each tortilla to within 1/2-inch of edge. Moisten edge with water. Fold over and seal. Place on 2 baking sheets.
Bake 5 minutes or until hot. Serve with Fiesta Rice.
For Fiesta Rice: Heat Campbell's(R) Condensed Chicken Broth, 1/2 cup water and 1/2 cup Pace(R) Chunky Salsa in saucepan to a boil. Stir in 2 cups uncooked instant white rice. Cover and remove from heat. Let stand 5 min. Fluff with fork.
Serve with Pace(R) salsa. For dessert serve orange wedges.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
A spicy, creamy chicken mixture fills crispy-baked tortillas, cut into wedges and served with Mexican-flavored rice.
