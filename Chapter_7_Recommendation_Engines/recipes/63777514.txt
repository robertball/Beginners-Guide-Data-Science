Title:
Garlic-Thyme Flat Iron Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1046878.jpg
Ingredients:
2-3 pound flat iron steak
6 cloves garlic, minced
3 Tbsp fresh Thyme leaves
2 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 tsp sea salt
1 tsp fresh cracked black pepper
1/2 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
salt & pepper, to taste

Directions:
In a small bowl mix together the garlic, thyme, salt, pepper, paprika, red pepper flakes and olive oil.
Spread marinade evenly over both sides of steak and gently press in. Double wrap in plastic and place in refrigerator for 4-8 hours.
Preheat a gas grill on high. Unwrap steak and sear on grill for 4 minutes per side. Reduce grill heat to low and continue to cook about 8 minutes or until desired doneness is achieved.
Cut into thin slices and serve.

Description: 
This is a simple and flavorful marinade for flat iron steak. You can't go wrong with garlic!
