Title:
Ranch Chicken Mac Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1(17.5oz) Box Macaroni and Cheese
1(10oz) Can of chicken drained
2tbs. Ranch salad dressing
1/2c. Chopped tomato
3tbs Sliced green onion

Directions:
Prepare macaroni and cheese according to packaging instructions.
Add chicken, salad dressing, tomato and onion. Stir gently and heat until warmed through.
