Title:
Chicken Florentine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless bone-in chicken thighs
1/4 cup butter
3 teaspoons minced garlic
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup half-and-half
1 package frozen spinach, drained & thawed
1 package sliced mushrooms
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Melt 1 Tbsp butter in pan.  Add mushrooms.  Sautee until cooked through.  Add spinach and garlic and a little salt.  Cook 2 more minutes.
Put spinach & mushrooms into bottom of square pyrex pan.
Melt the rest of the butter in the pan that you cooked the spinach & mushrooms in over medium heat. Stirring constantly, mix in the garlic, lemon juice, cream of mushroom soup, pepper, and half-and-half.
Place chicken on top of spinach & mushrooms.  Pour sauce over chicken.
Bake for 30 minutes.
Sprinkle with cheese.  Cook for 10 more minutes.
Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Description: 
Chicken Florentine casserole, which can also be adapted with either fish or shrimp, lies on a bed of spinach leaves and mushrooms, has a creamy white sauce mixed with garlic, Parmesan cheese, and Italian seasonings, and is topped with baked mozzarella.Modified from: Chicken Florentine Casserole
