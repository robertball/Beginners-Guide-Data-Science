Title:
Magic Cookie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup( 1 stick) butter
1 1/2 cup grahm cracker crumbs
1-14 oz. can sweetened condensed milk
2 cups (12 oz.) semi sweet choclate chips
1 1/3 cup flaked cocunut
1 cup finely chopped walnuts

Directions:
1) Preheat oven 350 degrees (325 for a glass dish) In 13x9 inch pan melt butter in oven
2)Sprinkle grahm cracker crumbs evenly over butter; pour condensed milk evenly over crumbs top with remaining ingredients; press down firmly with fork.
3) Bake 25-30 minutes or until lightly browned
cool completly then cut into bars
Enjoy...Happy Baking

Isabella Riche

A soft warm cookie bar great for parties.
