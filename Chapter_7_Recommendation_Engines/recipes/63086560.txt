Title:
30-Minute Chili Italiano Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/781118.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb Hot Ground Italian Sausage
1 lb 96% extra lean ground beef
2 (16 oz) cans red kidney beans, drained
1 (24 oz) jar spaghetti sauce
2 (10-1/2) cans diced italian style tomatoes (with basil, garlic, and oregano)
1 (16 oz) can fat free reduced sodium beef broth
1 (16 ounce) jar peperoncini peppers, drained and roughly chopped
1 sweet onion, diced
1 clove garlic, minced
2 tablespoons chili powder
2 teaspoons cumin
1 teaspoon italian seasoning
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper (optional)

Directions:
In a dutch oven pan brown the sausage and ground beef.  Drain off excess fat.  Stir in the remaining ingredients and simmer for about 25 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
For a unique, delicious, quick and easy chili with an italian twist, try this 30-Minute Chili Italiano featuring Hot Ground Italian Sausage with ground beef, kidney beans, spaghetti sauce, italian style diced tomatoes, peperoncini sweet italian peppers, with italian and chili spices.  It's ready in 30 minutes and it's molto delizioso!
