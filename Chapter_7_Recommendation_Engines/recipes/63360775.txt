Title:
al fresco Teriyaki Meatball Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/895143.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package al fresco Teriyaki Ginger chicken meatballs - halved or quartered
1 red pepper - chopped irregular sized pieces
1 yellow pepper - chopped irregular sized pieces
2 carrots - sliced into diagonal coins
1/2lb snow peas - trimmed & cut on bias
1 can Baby Corn - chopped
1 can (8oz) pineapple chunks, drained
2tbs peanut oil
2tbs Ponzu Sauce
Lemon Pepper to taste

Directions:
Chop vegetables & set aside
Slice meatballs & set aside
Heat 2tbs of peanut oil in a hot wok or large saute pan
when hot (first bubbles on edge of oil) - add Ponzu & sliced pineapple - stir fry until pineapple has started to absorb the ponzu
Add in vegetables and stir fry to coat with ponzu & oil - 2 to 3 minutes
Add meatballs & combine & stir fry - 3 to 5 minutes
Add Lemon Pepper to taste
Serve with white rice

Submitter: 
Doug Matthews
Description: 
Sliced al fresco teriyaki ginger chicken meatballs stir fried with a bevy of vegetables & some ponzu
