Title:
Pesto Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/365006.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large or 2 medium boneless, skinless chicken breast, cubed
1 small jar pesto
1 tsp tomato paste
1 small can mushroom caps, or stems and pieces
1/2 lb egg noodles.
Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
Bring water to a boil and cook pasta according to package directions.  Set aside.  (These can cook while the chicken simmers.)
Stir tomato paste into pesto, in a skillet over medium heat, mixing thoroughly.
Stir mushrooms and cubed chicken into pesto mixture.
Cover and simmer 15 minutes, or until chicken is thoroughly cooked and juices run clear.
Gently stir Chicken Peston into hot noodles and serve.
Sprinkle with grated parmesan, if desired.

Description: 
A quick and simple dish for 1 or 2 people, full of flavor.
