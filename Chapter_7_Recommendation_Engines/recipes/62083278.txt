Title:
Crab Roll-ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Flour Tortilla shells, burrito size
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 pkg imitation crab flakes
1 small can sliced black olives (opt)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Gradually mix crab flakes with cream cheese until well blended.  Add olives, salt and pepper.
Spread thin layter of crab mixture evenly across tortilla, roll up (jelly roll style).  Slice into 1" sections. Layer on plate. Repeat until all of the crab mixture has been used.
Cover with plastic wrap or place in covered tupperware. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
