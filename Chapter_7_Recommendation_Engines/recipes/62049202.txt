Title:
Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds chicken thighs
2 medium onions (chopped)
2 cloves garlic (choppped)
2 cups tomatoes (chopped)
2 tablespoons flour
2 tablespoons curry powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup water
2 lemons
cooking oil or butter
Optional:
4 carrots (diced)
3 pototoes (diced)

Directions:
Remove skin and fat from chicken.
Coat chicken with flour and fry in cooking oil or butter until golden.
Add chopped onion and saute briefly.
Combine curry powder with salt and sprinkle over chicken and onions.
Mix and fry together for 1-2 minutes.
Add chopped tomatoes and water. Potatoes and carrots if preferred.)
Simmer, stirring occasionally for 1 hour.
Squeeze lemons over curry before serving.
