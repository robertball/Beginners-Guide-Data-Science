Title:
Gluten Free Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 loaves GF bread, cubed and toasted
2 ribs celery, diced
1 yellow onion, diced
2 T. olive oil
2 T garlic, chopped
1 T each,chopped rosemary, sage, and thyme
1 c. chicken stock
1 egg yolk
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
Saute the onion and celery in olive oil on medium low heat until they are translucent.  You will be able to smell the onions. Add garlic and herbs and cook until you smell the herbs, 1 to 2 minutes and remove from heat.
Bring chicken stock to a boil on high heat.  Place egg yolk in medium sized bowl and carefully ladle 2 to 3 ounces of the chicken stock to the egg yolk, slowly, while whisking the mixture. Add the rest of the chicken stock to the egg mixture.
Add the cooled celery mixture into the stock and egg mixture.  Toss the bread cubes into this mixture and stir to coat the bread.  Add the salt and pepper and toss again.  Place all into a greased casserole dish (3 qt.) and cover with aluminum foil.
Bake for 20 minutes at 425ยบ, then remove the foil and bake for another 10 minutes.  It is done when toothpick comes out clean.
