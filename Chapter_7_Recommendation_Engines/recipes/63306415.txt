Title:
Grandma Parker's Oatmeal Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup softened Margarine (Parkay sticks)
1 cup Brown Sugar
1 cup white Sugar
2 Eggs
1 tsp. Vanilla
2.5 cups Flour
1/2 tsp. Salt
1 tsp. Baking Soda
3 cups Quick Oats
1/2 cup Raisins
1/2 cup Coconut
1/2 cup Walnuts (optional)
Chocolate chips for variation (optional)

Directions:
In bowl, mix the flour, salt & baking soda together then set aside. In a mixing bowl, add the butter, sugars, eggs & vanilla and mix well with mixer.  Mix in the dry ingredients.  After all is mixed well together, add the raisins, coconut and walnuts(and chocolate chips if desired).  Place on ungreased cookie sheet with cookie scoop.  Bake @ 350 degrees for 8-10 minutes.  Enjoy!
