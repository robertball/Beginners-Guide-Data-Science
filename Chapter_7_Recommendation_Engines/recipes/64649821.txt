Title:
Palachentas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 c milk
1/2 c sugar
2 c flour

Directions:
1.  Beat the eggs.
2.  Then add the sugar and milk in the milk.
3.  Slowly add flour beating until smooth.
4.  Use a good nonstick skillet on medium heat.  Pour 1/3 c batter into the pan and swirl around until it makes a flat circle.
5.  Brown on each side and then remove.
6.  Serve with your favorite jelly or cottage cheese spread over the palachenta and roll up.

Submitter: 
Renee Heston
Description: 
A recipe passed down from my Grandma Dolfen.  Everyone in the family loves this classic.
