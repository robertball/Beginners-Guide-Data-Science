Title:
Cucumber Ranch Seafood Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peeled, seeded, diced cucumber
1 minced garlic clove
1/2 cup low fat mayo
1/4 cup low fat buttermilk
3 Tbs chopped fresh chives
1 juiced lemon
1 tsp dijon mustard
1/2 tsp celery salt
1/2 lb farfalle pasta
1 pkg imitation crab chunks
1/4 lb shrimp cut in halves
2 stalks celery, diced
1/4 sweet onion, diced
3 sliced green onions
1/2 cup chopped seeded cucumber
1 cup frozen peas, thawed
1 cup cherry (or sundried) tomatoes, halved

Directions:
Use blender or food processor to blend first eight ingredients until smooth (through celery salt)
Cook pasta according to package directions, drain, rinse with cool water and transfer to bowl
Stir dressing onto cooled pasta and mix in vegetables (reserve fresh tomatoes until right before serving)
