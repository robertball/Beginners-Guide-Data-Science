Title:
Grammie's Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large eggs
2 cups sugar
1 cup vegetable oil
2 cups grated zucchini
3 tsp vanilla
3 cups flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp baking powder
3 tsp cinnamon
chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Prepare bread pan with cooking spray or cut parchment paper.
Sift together all dry ingredients except nuts and set aside.
Mix eggs in a separate bowl. Add vanilla and vegetable oil. Stir in shredded zucchini.
Add dry ingredients and stir until moist.
Fold in nuts if desired.
Pour batter into prepared pan and cook for 1 hour to 1 hour and 30 minutes or until knife inserted in center is pulled out clean.

Submitter: 
Sweet_CarolineS
Description: 
My grandmother made this bread every Christmas. It is moist and delicious. You can make it ahead and freeze for several months.
