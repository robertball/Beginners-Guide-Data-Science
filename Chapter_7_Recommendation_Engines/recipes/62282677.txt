Title:
The Old-Fashioned Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/352328.jpg
Ingredients:
2 oz. Bourbon or Rye
3 Dashes Angostura Bitters
Sugar Cube (1/2 tsp)
1/2 Orange Wheel
Brandied Cherries

Directions:
Muddle bourbon/rye, sugar, bitters,  until sugar dissolved.
Stir with ice, poor over ice in lowball glass
Garnish with 1/2 orange wheel and brandied cherries.
