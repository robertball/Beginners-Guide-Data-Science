Title:
Luna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T diced garlic
2 T dijon mustard
1 t. salt
1 t. pepper
2 T. lemon juice
1/2 c. white wine vinegar
1 c. olive oil

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except oil. Mix well, then whisk in oil until thickened. Serve at room temp. Makes lots- store excess in refrig.
Salad: Romaine, feta cheese, craisins, candied walnuts (sugar, water, vanilla in pan to dissolve then add walnuts and toast lightly).
