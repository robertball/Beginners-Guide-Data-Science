Title:
Minute速 Rice Porcupine Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983637.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter or margarine
1 small onion, chopped
1 pound lean ground beef*
1 cup Minute速 White Rice, uncooked
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 (1.25 ounce) packet meatloaf seasoning
1/4 cup water
1 (15.5 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce

Directions:
Melt butter in small skillet over medium high heat. Add onions; cook and stir until tender.
Place onions, meat, rice, egg and seasoning in large bowl. Add water; mix until well blended. Shape into medium-sized meatballs.
Pour spaghetti sauce into skillet. Bring to boil; add meatballs. Return to boil. Reduce heat to low; cover. Simmer 15 minutes or until meatballs are cooked through.

Submitter: 
Minute速 Rice
Description: 
These meatballs made with ground beef, onion, Minute速 Rice, egg and meatloaf seasoning look like miniature porcupines once they've been simmered in spaghetti sauce.
