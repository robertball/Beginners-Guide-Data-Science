Title:
Bacon Wrapped Little Loaves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/799530.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs ground beef
1 large egg
¼ cup dry bread crumbs
¼ cup chopped onion
¼ cup lemon juice
¼ cup chopped green bell peppers (optional)
½ water
½ tablespoon Instant Beef Bouillon
6 slices of bacon cut in half (12 pieces)
1 cup Shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
1. Heat oven to 350 degrees
2. Mix all the ingredients, except bacon
3. Shape the mixture into 6 loaves
4. Crisscross 2 of the half slices of bacon across   each loaf, tucking the ends under each loaf
5  Place theloaves on a rack in a shallow baking pan, sprinkle the cheese on top of each and bake uncovered for 50 minutes
6  Let stand for 5 to 10 minutes before serving.
