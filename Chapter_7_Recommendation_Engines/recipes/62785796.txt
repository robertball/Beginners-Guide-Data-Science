Title:
Chicken Fiesta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 (1.27 ounce) packet dry fajita seasoning, divided
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (11 ounce) can Mexican-style corn
1/2 cup salsa
1 (10 ounce) package mixed salad greens
3 green onions, chopped
1 tomato, cut into wedges
Ranch dressing
1 lime wedged
avocado
cheese
sour cream

Directions:
Rub chicken evenly with 1/2 the fajita seasoning. Grill chicken and cut into strips.
In a large saucepan, mix beans, corn, salsa and other 1/2 of fajita seasoning. Heat over medium heat until warm.
Prepare the salad by tossing the greens, green onion and tomato. Top salad with chicken and dress with the bean and corn mixture, ranch dressing, avocado, cheese and sour cream.  Squeeze lime as desired.

Submitter: 
KESSIANNE
Description: 
Salad greens, onions and tomatoes are topped with Mexican flavored black beans, corn, and grilled chicken breasts. This is an attractive and zesty all in one dish.Modified from: Chicken Fiesta Salad
