Title:
TexMex Fideo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 5 oz. pkg. fideo vermicelli (or spaghetti broken into thirds)
2 T. extra virgin olive oil, divided
1 lb ground beef
1/2 Texas Sweet Onion,
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1 can chicken broth (14 oz.)
1/3 c. water
sm can chopped green chiles
2 to 3 T Chili powder (to taste)
1/4 tsp. salt (or to taste)
pinch ground black pepper
1/4 tsp. ground cumin
1/8 tsp. ground cayenne
1/2 tsp. Mexican oregano
1 c. shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
In skillet, saute vermicelli in 1 T olive oil until golden brown, stirring constantly. Set aside.
In large skillet, cook half the onion and meat until the meat is brown and onion is translucent. Save the rest of the onion for garnish.
Add chicken broth, water, green chiles, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, pepper, cumin, cayenne pepper, and oregano to browned meat in skillet. Add pasta. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low.
Cover and simmer on low heat for 15 minutes or until liquid is absorbed.
Sprinkle individual servings with shredded cheddar cheese and reserved chopped onion. (or parmesan cheese if desired.)

Description: 
This is a delicious mexican spaghetti prepared South Texas style. Everyone has their own fideo recipe, all a little different, just like our salsas... this is my youngest son's favorite and most requested dish.
