Title:
coconut ice cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cans coconut milk
1 c. cocoa powder OR 3 c. chopped fruit
1/2 c. agave syrup
1/2 T. vanilla

Directions:
Whisk ingredients, pour into ice cream maker.  For fruit, add in last five minutes.
