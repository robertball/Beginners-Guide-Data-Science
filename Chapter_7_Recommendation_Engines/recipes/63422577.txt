Title:
Roasted Autumn Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 T. butter or margarine
1 T. chopped fresh or 1 tsp. dried sage leaves
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1/2 lb. Brussels sprouts, cut in half (2 cups)
1/2 lb. parsnips, peeled, cut lengthwise in quarters, then cut into 2-inch pieces (2 cups)
1/2 c. small butternut squash (about 1 lb.) peeled, seeded and cut into 1-inch pieces (2 cups)
8 oz. bag ready-to-eat baby-cut carrots (2 cups)

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees.  In 1-quart saucepan, melt butter.  Stir in sage and garlic.  In ungreased 9x13-inch pan, place remaining ingredients.  Pour butter mixture over vegetables; stir to coat.  Cover with foil; bake 45 minutes.  Remove foil; bake about 15 minutes longer or until vegetables are tender.
