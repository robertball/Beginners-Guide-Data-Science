Title:
Chunky Apple Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Topping:
1/4 cup butter, melted
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
6 Tbsp. flour
1 tsp. cinnamon
Batter:
1-1/2 cups firmly packed brown sugar
2/3 cup cooking oil
1 egg
1 cup buttermilk
1 tsp. soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla
2-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
2 cups peeled apples, cut in 3/4 inch chunks

Directions:
Prepare topping by combining butter, brown sugar, flour and cinnamon in small bowl. Set aside. Peel and chop apples. Set aside. In mixing bowl, combine brown sugar, oil and egg. In a large measuring cup, combine buttermilk, soda, salt and vanilla. Blend flour into sugar and oil mixture alternately with buttermilk mixture. Stir just until combined. Fold in apples.
Spoon batter into greased muffin tins. Sprinkle with topping. Bake at 325 for 30 minutes. Cool for 10 minutes, then serve.

Submitter: 
AUNT MAMIE
Description: 
Soft and tender, these muffins will melt in your mouth. The slow baking softens them, and the streusel topping adds just the right crunch. Our family's favorite!
