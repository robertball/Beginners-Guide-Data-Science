Title:
Peach Pie, made with sour cream, peaches. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
5 tlbs flour
1 cup sour cream
1/4 tsp salt
2 cups peaches (fresh peaches are great)
2 tlbs sugar
1/4 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Mix sugar, flour, sour cream, salt and peaches together.  Pour ingredients into an unbaked pie crust.  Cut top pie crust into 3/4 inch wide strips and criss-cross strips on top of filling.  Sprinkle with sugar/cinnamon mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes to 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Judy Kwiatkowski
Description: 
This is an easy recipe that tastes great! With the criss-cross top, it looks professionally made.  Wonderful way to use peaches when in season. I found this recipe in a monthly booklet from Nebraska Public Power, submitted by Cheryl Thramer, Ewing NE.
