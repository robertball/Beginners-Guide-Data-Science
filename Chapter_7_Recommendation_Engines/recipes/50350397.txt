Title:
Pepperoncini Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 pound) beef chuck roast
4 cloves garlic, sliced
1 (16 ounce) jar pepperoncini
1 medium onion
1 8oz package sliced mushrooms
1 package provolone cheese

Directions:
Make small cuts in roast, and insert garlic slices in cuts. Place roast in the slow cooker, and pour the entire contents of the jar of pepperoncini, including liquid, over meat. Add onion for extra taste
Cover, and cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours. Serve on italian roll with provolone cheese, onions and mushrooms
Cook on high for 3 hours
Cook on low for 6 hours

Submitter: 
hank grein
Description: 
Roast beef cooked in a slow cooker with garlic and pepperoncini makes a delicious and simple filling for gyro sandwiches. Serve on hoagie rolls with provolone or mozzarella cheese, and your choice of condiments. When making the sandwiches, place meat in rolls, cover with cheese, and zap in a microwave for a few seconds. Don't forget to use the pepperoncini in the sandwiches.Modified from: Pepperoncini Beef
