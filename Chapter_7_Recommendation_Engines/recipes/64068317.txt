Title:
Carrots and Lentils Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons evoo
1 medium onion, thinly sliced
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tablespoon tomato paste
1/2 teaspoon ground chili pepper
1 pound carrots, cut into thin half-rounds
1/4 teaspoon sea salt
3 cups water (or vegetable broth)
1 cup uncooked dried lentils
1/4 cup Greek yogurt
salt and fresh ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
1 - Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat.  Add onion and cook until lightly browned, stirring occasionally.  Add garlic and cook 2 minutes.  Stir in tomato paste, ground chili pepper, carrots and sea salt.  Cook 2 minutes.  Remove from heat.
2 - Combine water (or broth) and lentils. Bring to a boil.  Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 30 minutes.  Uncover, increase heat to medium-high, and stir in onion mixture.  Cook 2 minutes or until liquid almost evaporates.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.  Cool to room temperature.  Serve with a dollop of Greek yogurt.

Submitter: 
Ellen Janet Nestler
Description: 
This is a great, protein rich, vegetarian dish of Turkish origin.
