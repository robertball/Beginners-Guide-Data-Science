Title:
Garden Fresh Breakfast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/465176.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 cup chopped zucchini
1/2 cup chopped green pepper
1/4 cup sliced green onions
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
1 medium tomato, seeded and diced
4 eggs
2 tablespoons water
2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
Paprika

Directions:
In a large skillet, saute mushrooms, zucchini, green pepper and onions in butter for 5 minutes or until tender. Drain off juices. Stir in tomato. In a bowl, beat the eggs, water, mustard, salt and pepper. Pour over vegetables. Cover and cook over medium heat for 5-10 minutes or until eggs are set. Sprinkle with cheese and paprika. Cut into wedges to serve.

Submitter: 
Donna King
Description: 
Here in northwest Missouri, we have an abundance of zucchini, peppers, onions and tomatoes. So I make this attractive garden recipe a lot, and that pleases my hungry family!
