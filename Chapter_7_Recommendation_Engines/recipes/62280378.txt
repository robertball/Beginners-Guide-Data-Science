Title:
M&M;'s Christmas Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
29 plain silver cupcake liners
1 box vanilla or chocolate cake mix
2 bags of M&M;'s Brand Milk Chocolate Candies for the Holidays
Green food coloring
1 16 oz. container chocolate frosting

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place the plain cupcake liners in cupcake pans.
2. Prepare cake batter according to the package directions.
3. Spoon 1/2 cup of the batter into each cupcake pan. Bake for 19-20 minutes. Cool completely.
4. Frost the cupcakes.
5. Decorate 29 cupcakes with red and green M&M's Chocolate Candies.
