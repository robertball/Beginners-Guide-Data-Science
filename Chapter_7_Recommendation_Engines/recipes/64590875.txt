Title:
Summertime Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 lb box wagon wheel pasta
1 box frozen peas
1 box frozen corn
1 large bottle ranch dressing
salt
pepper

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions on box.   Drain and cool in refridgerator.
Mix pasta and remaining ingredients together and enjoy.
