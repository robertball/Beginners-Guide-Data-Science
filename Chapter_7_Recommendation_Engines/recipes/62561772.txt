Title:
Jennifer's Baked Pork Chops and Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/543166.jpg
Ingredients:
2 thick cut pork chops
1 box pork breadcrumbs
1 egg, or just use water
3/4 tablespoon garlic powder
2 tablespoon vegetable oil
3/4 cup milk
1 can cream of mushroom soup
-- or use pork gravy (store-bought)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425. Grease pan. Dip pork chops in egg or water, then cover chops with breadcrumbs. Lay  pork chops on pan and bake. Bake for about 40 minutes.
For gravy, heat soup and milk (or store-bought).
Pour gravy over pork chops. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
jennifer_russell
Description: 
Crispy, crunchy pork chops with a creamy gravy spilling over with mashed potatoes and a salad! Yum!
