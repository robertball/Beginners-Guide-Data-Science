Title:
Sauced Tortellini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sour cream
1 tbsp. Italian seasoning
1/3 cup olive oil (could sub avocado - I'm going to try it)
1/3 cup parmesan cheese
salt & pepper
garlic, to taste
tortellini of your choice (4 servings)

Directions:
Mix ingredients.
Warm in microwave before adding to fresh cooked tortellini.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
I tried to replicate a creamy-sauced pasta dish we enjoyed on the west coast. I think I came pretty close :)
