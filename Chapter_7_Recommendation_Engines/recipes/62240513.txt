Title:
Creamy Cheesy Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz bag frozen cauliflower
2 Tbsp cream cheese
2 Tbsp sour cream
1 Tbsp butter
Salt and pepper to taste
1 - 2 Tbsp milk (optional)

Directions:
Prepare cauliflower per package instructions until tender.
Drain cauliflower and place in a medium size bowl.  Add the other ingredients and mash with a potato masher.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
If the mixture is too dry, add milk 1 tbsp at a time until you reach the desired consistency.

Submitter: 
DASKDS
Description: 
Mashed cauliflower mixed with cream cheese, butter and sour cream.  Wonderful!
I usually make this with onion or garlic seasoned cream cheese for extra flavor.  You can use light or lowfat cheese and sour cream to make it healthier.
