Title:
Crockpot Kielbasa and Sauerkraut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds sauerkraut, rinsed and drained
1 tablespoon caraway seeds (optional)
2 Tbsp. Splenda brown sugar blend
1/2 cup apple juice
1/2 pound bacon, cut into 1-inch pieces
1 large onion, chopped
1 1/2 pounds kielbasa sausage or smoked sausage, cut into 1-inch thick slices

Directions:
Place the bacon into a large skillet and cook until partially browned.  Add the chopped onion and cook until the bacon is browned and the onion is beginning to brown.  Pour off the fat.
Place a liner in a crockpot and pour all the ingredients into the crockpot.  Stir together well. Cover and cook on low for 3-4 hours.
