Title:
Edible Cake Jewels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/563843.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup light corn syrup
Candy food coloring, any color (omit if you want white jewels)
A few drops orange oil, if desired (or any other flavoring oil)

Directions:
Lightly spray candy mold and funnel with cooking spray, set aside until ready to use.
Thoroughly mix sugar and corn syrup in a 4-cup microwave-safe glass measure. Cover with plastic wrap.
Microwave on HIGH for 3 minutes and 15 seconds.
Remove from the microwave and carefully remove plastic wrap.
Quickly stir the sugar mixture and then cover with a NEW sheet of plastic wrap.
Microwave on high for 3 minutes and 15 seconds.
Remove from microwave, carefully remove plastic wrap and stir with a clean spoon.
After boiling has ceased, stir in coloring and flavoring.
Pour syrup quickly and carefully using funnel with a rubber stopper or candy funnel (controls the flow of the syrup) into prepared molds.
Wait until cooled and they should pop out easily.
You can repeat as often as desired.

Submitter: 
Mollie
Description: 
A microwave recipe for hard candy jewels. Beautiful, realistic cake jewels that are completely edible. A stunning addition to cakes, cupcakes or cookies. Imagine how pretty your cakes will look adorned with these!
