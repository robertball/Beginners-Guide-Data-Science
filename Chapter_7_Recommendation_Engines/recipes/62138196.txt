Title:
Slow Cooker Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 carrots, thinly sliced
5 stalks celery, sliced
4 potatoes, cubed
1 9 oz. package frozen green beans
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup diced canned tomatoes
4 cups tomato juice
1/2 cup water
1 tbs. dried parsley
3/4 tsp. dried oregano
1 tsp. herb seasoning
salt to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in crock pot insert. Cook on low for 8-10 hours or on high for 6 hours. Season with salt to taste. Serve hot.

Submitter: 
larkspur
Description: 
This soup is fat free, but it is so delicious that you would never know! To speed up preparation on a busy morning, you can assemble it in the pot and store it overnight in the fridge. !
