Title:
Caramel Apple Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese, softened
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Blend together all ingredients until smooth and creamy.  Serve with fresh apple slices or assorted fruit.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
A nice treat with apple slices.
