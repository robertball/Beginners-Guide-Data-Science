Title:
Pork Loin Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb pork loin roast
Garlic, slivered
Salt
Ground oregano/cumin mixture (3 to 1 ratio)
Pepper
Fresh lime juice
White wine

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350*.
Cut slits in roast.
Stuff garlic slivers rolled in salt into slits.
Roll roast in oregano/cumin mixture coating all sides.
Pepper to taste.
Brown roast in vegetable oil on all sides.
Brush lime juice onto all sides of the roast.
Place in a covered baking dish and add wine until it is half way up the side of the roast.
Roast covered for 3-1/2 to 4 hours or until tender.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
Pork Roast extraordinaire.  Serve with red beans and rice.
