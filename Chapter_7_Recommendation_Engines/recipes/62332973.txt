Title:
Easy Slow Cooker London Broil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds flank steak
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed tomato soup
1/2 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
2 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 thinly-sliced onion
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1 lb. bag of baby carrots

Directions:
Layer meat, onions, mushrooms and carrots in the slow cooker. In a medium bowl, mix together mushroom and tomato soups; stir in Worcestershire sauce. Pour mixture over beef. Sprinkle dry onion soup mix over top.
Cover, and cook on Low for 8 to 10 hours.
Serve with mashed potatoes or rice.

Submitter: 
CookinChris24x7
Description: 
This is a VERY quick prep recipe using simple ingredients.  You can use these same ingredients for any type of meat you happen to have on hand.  Works great for boneless chicken breasts and pork chops as well.Modified from: Slow Cooker London Broil
