Title:
Meringue Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 egg whites
1/4 tsp. cream of tartar
dash salt
1/2 cup sugar
whipped topping prepared without sugar as meringue is sweet enough.
fresh fruit, or other toppings

Directions:
Place egg white in mixing bowl; let stand at room temp. x 30 minutes.
Add cream of tartar & salt; beat on med. until soft peaks form.
Gradually beat in sugar, 1 tbsp. @ a time, on high until still peaks form and sugar is dissolved.
Line baking sheet with parchment or waxed paper.
Drop 6 mounds on sheet, shaping into cups with back of spoon.
Bake @ 225 x 1 1/2 hours or until set and dry.
Turn oven off; leave meringues in oven x 1 1/2 hours.
Store in airtight container.
Just before serving, top cups with whipping cream and fruit or other topping.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
These light, sweet, crunchy meringue cups can be filled with whatever you'd like to showcase - fresh fruit and whipped cream are esp. good. These can be made well in advance, frozen and filled when needed.
