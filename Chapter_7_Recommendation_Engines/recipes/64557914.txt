Title:
Blueberry Coconut Cake-Cookie Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. powdered (confectioner's) sugar
2 tbsp. milk
1/4 tsp. vanilla extract.

Directions:
In a small bowl, slowly mix 1 tbsp. milk and a drop or two of extract. Continue adding small amounts of milk and extract; stir until smooth. Serve chilled for best flavor.

Submitter: 
The Writing Baker
Description: 
You can add all kinds of extracts to change the flavor to suit whatever you put the glaze on, but I did vanilla because it is pretty basic.
