Title:
Pasta with Spinach Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. pasta
salt and pepper
1 10oz. pkg. frozen spinach - thawed
1/3 cup grated Parmesan + some for garnish
2 tbsp. pine nuts
1 garlic clove
1 tsp. lemon zest
2 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup cold water

Directions:
Cook pasta to al dente and reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water.
While pasta is cooking, place spinach, Parmesan, pine nuts, garlic, lemon zest and juice in food processor and process until a paste forms.
With processor running, add oil and cold water and process until smooth and creamy.
Season to taste with salt and pepper then add to pasta.
Toss and add reserved pasta water a little at a time until sauce is thinned slightly and coats pasta.
Serve sprinkled with Parmesan and pine nuts if desired.
