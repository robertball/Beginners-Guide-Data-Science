Title:
Quick and Easy Carrot  and Chickpea Salad. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp.olive oil
2 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1 clove of garlic chopped very fine
i tsp. ground cumin
1/4 tsp. salt
fresh ground pepper
2 cups shredded carrots
1 small can of chickpeas drained and rinsed
2 tbsp. fresh chopped parsley
2 tbsp. chopped white onion

Directions:
In a medium size bowl add oil,lemon juice, garlic, cumin and the salt and pepper. Whisk together until well mixed.Add the carrots ,chickpeas, parsley and onions, toss gently until all is mixed. *See notes*

Submitter:

Description: 
A refreshing and different spin on a carrot salad given to me by a neighbour many years ago.
