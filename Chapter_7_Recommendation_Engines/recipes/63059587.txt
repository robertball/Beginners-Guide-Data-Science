Title:
Low Carb Upside-Down Crustless Pepperoni Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/770043.jpg
Ingredients:
Basic ingredients: 5-7 thin slices of slicing pepperoni
3-4 oz of shredded mozzerella cheese
oregano, salt, pepper, red pepper (to taste)

Directions:
In an ungreased 8x8 inch dark baking pan (square or round) line pepperoni evenly along the bottom. Cover with cheese, sprinkle oregano, add other desired toppings.
Place in 450F degree oven for 10-15 min until cheese is slightly browned.
Remove, allow 2 minutes before cutting.
Yeild 2 servings
* Note if making larger size, time may need be adjusted.
