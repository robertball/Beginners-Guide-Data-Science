Title:
Beef, Tators, and Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
seasoned salt
pepper
1/4 cup minced onions
1 egg
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
2 cans sliced potatoes
2 cans sliced carrots
salt and pepper
minced onions

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425. Grease 9x13 pan and set aside. In a large bowl, mix together beef, seasoned salt, pepper, egg, onions, and sauce. Form into 6 patties. Top with canned vegetables and sprinkle onions, salt, and pepper as desired. Bake for 30 minutes or until patties are cooked through. Serve and enjoy!
