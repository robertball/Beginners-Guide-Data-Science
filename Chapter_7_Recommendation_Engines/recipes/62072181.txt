Title:
Ranch Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz bar low-fat cream cheese, softened
1/2 packet Hidden Valley Ranch seasoning, or to taste
seasoned pepper

Directions:
1. Mix softened cream cheese and ranch with mixer until blended.
2. Refridgerate for 5-10 minutes.
3. Use waxed paper and form into a ball. Sprinkle seasoned pepper until it coats ball.  Refridgerate until ready and serve with Fritos or Wheat Thins.

Submitter: 
paynes
Description: 
This is a simple and delicious appetizer.  It is always a big hit at get togethers and is great with Fritos or Wheat Thins.
