Title:
chipotle squash and black-bean burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 delicata squash (about 1 pound)
2 T olive oil
1/4 tsp. salt
1 can black beans, well drained
1/2 c. chipotle salsa
4 large flour tortillas
1/2 c. jack cheese
1/2 c. chopped cilantro
lime wedges for serving

Directions:
Trim both ends from the squash and then cut in half lengthwise to remove seeds. Quarter each half lengthwise.  Cut the strips into 1/2-inch dice. (Total = 3 cups)
Heat oil over medium-high heat.  Add squash and salt and cook, stirring often, till browned and tender. Add the beans and salsa; stir. Lower the heat and cook about 2 minutes more, till all is coated with salsa.
Heat over to 350. Put one-dquarter of filling in each tortilla and top with cheese and cilantro.  Roll up and wrap in foil. Bake for 15 minutes.
