Title:
Cucumbers for Freezing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 c. thinly (3mm) sliced cucumbers
3 med. onions, sliced
1 green pepper, chopped
2 c. sugar
1 c. vinegar
1/2 tsp. celery seed
1 Tbsp. salt

Directions:
Mix all ingredient in large bowl.
Refrigerate for 24 hours
Put in containers and freeze.
Just defrost to serve.

Submitter: 
Sue
Description: 
Great way to enjoy the summer's cucumbers during the winter.
