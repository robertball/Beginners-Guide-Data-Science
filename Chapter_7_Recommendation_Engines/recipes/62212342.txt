Title:
Buckeyes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 oz. peanut butter
1.5 lb powdered sugar (around 6 cups)
0.5 lb melted butter
12 oz semi-sweet chocolate
2 Tbs Crisco

Directions:
Mix and roll into balls the peanut butter, powdered sugar and butter. Place balls on cookie sheet covered with a kitchen towel and waxed paper and freeze.
Melt chocolate and Crisco in a double boiler. Dip frozen balls (use a toothpick, then smooth over hole after dipping) into chocolate.
Chill balls again to set chocolate.
