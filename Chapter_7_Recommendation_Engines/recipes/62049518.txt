Title:
Carol's Best Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2- pounds ground chuck
1- med. onion finely diced
2- Med carrot, peeled and shredded
3- large eggs
4- slices of white bread (cheap doughy type) soaked in 1/2 cup milk
1 T. salt
2 t. pepper
2 t. garlic powder
Topping:
1 cup favorite BBQ sauce
2 T. prepared mustard
4 T. Brown sugar
1/4 cup ketchup

Directions:
Mix beef, onions, eggs, carrots, spices, and lightly squeezed out bread
together, don't over mix.  Form in loaf.  Bake covered at 375 degrees for 20 minutes, pour sauce over meatloaf and continue to bake for another 35 45 minutes uncovered until juices run clear.  Form a longer thinner loaf so bake time is not extended.
