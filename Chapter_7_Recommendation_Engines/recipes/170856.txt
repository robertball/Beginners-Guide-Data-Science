Title:
Tomato Basil Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982179.jpg
Ingredients:
1 sourdough or baguette French bread, cut into 1-inch slices
1 (14.5 ounce) can RED GOLD® PETITE DICED TOMATOES or REDPACK® PETITE DICED TOMATOES, drained
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
3 tablespoons fresh chopped basil
Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Brush bread slices with olive oil and broil on each side until toasted.  In a small bowl combine the remaining ingredients.
Spoon tomato mixture onto the toasted bread and place under the broiler for 1 to 2 minutes to melt the cheese.  Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Red Gold®, Inc.
Description: 
Tomatoes, cheese and basil piled high onto toasted French bread and broiled creates an appetizer that everyone will love.
