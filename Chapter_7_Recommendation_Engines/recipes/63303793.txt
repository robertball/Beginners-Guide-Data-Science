Title:
Explosive tomato finger sammies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/872568.jpg
Ingredients:
1 loaf of French bread, sliced
2-3 tomatoes, sliced
1 c grated cheese
Black olives rings
Sriracha garlic chili sauce concentrate
2 sl bacon, cooked and crumbled
Hot pickled carrots (I use ones from pickled jalapenoâ€™s)
1 pickled jalapeno
Olive oil

Directions:
Cook 1-2 slices bacon and crumble. In same pan and bacon drippings, cook tomato slices until tender. Remove and brown French bread on each side (brush with olive oil first if desired.)
Grate cheese. Using homemade or store bought pickled jalapenos and carrots, dice one large jalapeno and as many carrots as you like.
In a small bowl mix together grated cheese, jalapeno, carrots and black olives (use as many black olives as desired) crumbled bacon and 2 Tbs. of Chili Garlic Sauce.
Top toast bread with fried tomato and cheese mix. Micro for 2 min. or till cheese is melted. Enjoy this delicious HOT appetizer.

Submitter: 
Lynns Kitchen
Description: 
This is not for the faint of heart.  It's a mouth and tummy fire explosion of pure flavor!  The French bread can be eliminated and this could be served as a side.
