Title:
Potato Leek Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ozs. bacon, cooked and crumbled
4 leeks, thinly sliced, white and some of green
1 # potatoes, scrubbed and cubed
1 qt. chicken broth
2 bay leaves
dill to taste
sea salt and white pepper to taste
sour cream for topping (opt)

Directions:
Cook leeks in oil until softened.
Add broth and potatoes;cover.
Cook over med-low heat until potatoes are softened, about 30 min.
Remove from heat, cool slightly.
Add milk and dill.
Season with salt and pepper.
Serve with sprinkle of bacon over soup.
