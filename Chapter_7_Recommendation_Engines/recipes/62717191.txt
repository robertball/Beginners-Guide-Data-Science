Title:
Simple Tahini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4c Roasted Sesame Seeds
1/4 c Olive Oil
Seal in airtight container, keeps for up to a month.

Directions:
Place roasted sesame seeds and olive oil in a blender or food processor.
Blend on high until smooth.

Submitter: 
badbabybeth
Description: 
Tahini is a paste made from sesame seeds.  It is an essential ingredient in hummus.
