Title:
Banana Banana Strawberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/57018.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana, broken into chunks
1 teaspoon banana extract
3/4 cup milk
1 (8 ounce) container strawberry yogurt
2 teaspoons white sugar

Directions:
Place banana, banana extract, milk, yogurt, and sugar into a blender. Blend until smooth and serve.

Submitter: 
Jennifer V
Description: 
Banana extract provides this smoothie with a double dose of fruit flavor!
