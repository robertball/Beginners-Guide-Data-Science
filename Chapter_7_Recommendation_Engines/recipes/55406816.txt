Title:
Italian Dressing Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoon garlic salt
3 tablespoon onion powder
3 tablespoon white sugar
6 tablespoons dried oregano
3/4 teaspoon dried thyme
3 teaspoon dried basil
3 tablespoon dried parsley
3/4 teaspoon celery salt
3 tablespoons dried red & green bell pepper (you can find in most spice isles in store)
3 teaspoons red pepper flakes
3 teaspoons lemon pepper

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix together the garlic salt, onion powder, sugar, oregano, red & green dried pepper,red pepper flakes, thyme, basil, parsley, celery salt and lemon pepper.  Store remaining dry ingredients in a tightly sealed container.
To prepare dressing, whisk together 1/4 white or balsamic vinegar, 2/3 cup canola or olive oil, 2 tablespoons water and 2 tablespoons of the dry mix. Taste best if mixed together at least 3-4 hours before using.
