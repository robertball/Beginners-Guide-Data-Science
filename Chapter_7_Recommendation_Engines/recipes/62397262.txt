Title:
A to Z Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c flour
2 c sugar
1 t salt
1 t soda
1/2 t baking powder
3 t cinnamon
3 eggs
1 c oil or apple sauce
3 t vanilla
2 c fruit ( anything from apples to zuccini)
1 c nuts - optional

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and pour into 2 well greased bread pans. I use pam spray it works great.
bake until knife comes out clean.
