Title:
Chicken Mirabella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons dried parsley
1/2 cup dry white wine (not sweet)
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1/2 cup green olives, sliced
1/2 cup canned prune, chopped
1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
2 teaspoons dried oregano
4 teaspoons garlic, chopped
1/4 cup capers
3 bay leaves
Dash of salt (optional)
6 boneless skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and place in a freezer bag.
When ready to serve, defrost and bake covered at 350 degrees for approx 45 min.

Submitter: 
Paula DiBacco
Description: 
Prunes & Capers mixed together with wine....great sweet & sour dish.  Make ahead of time, and freeze.  Then just defrost, bake and serve.
