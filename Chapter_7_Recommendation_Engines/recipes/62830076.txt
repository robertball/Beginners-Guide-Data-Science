Title:
Scrambled Tofu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 cup onions, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 cup red or green peppers, chopped
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1 cup diced seeded tomatoes
1 (12 ounce) package firm tofu, pressed and crumbled
1/2 tsp ground turmeric
1/2 tsp curry powder
1/2 tsp bried basil
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a medium skillet over medium heat, and saute onions, garlic and peppers until tender. Stir in tomatoes and tofu. Season with salt, pepper, turmeric, curry powder and basil. Reduce heat, and simmer until heated through. Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese to serve.
Serve as is or in a tortilla, burrito-style.
