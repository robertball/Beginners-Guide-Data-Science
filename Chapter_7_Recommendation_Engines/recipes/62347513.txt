Title:
Garlic-Buttered Green Beans with Mushrooms and Walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh or frozen green beans
1/2 cup walnuts
1/2 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
6 tablespoons butter or margarine
2 teaspoons onion powder
1 teaspoon garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook green beans in water to cover until crisp-tender or steamer. Meanwhile, in a skillet, saute mushrooms and walnuts in butter until tender. Add onion powder and garlic powder. Drain beans; add to skillet and toss. Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Charles Millwood Jr.
Description: 
These dressed-up beans are simple to make but look and taste special. They're a perfect side dish for nearly any meal.Modified from: Garlic-Buttered Green Beans
