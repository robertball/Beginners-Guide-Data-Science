Title:
Creamy Granita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups brewed strong SEATTLES BEST COFFEE® (any flavor), cooled
1/4 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1 cup thawed sugar free whipped topping, divided
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Pour coffee into large bowl. Add SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener; stir until SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener is completely dissolved. Add 1/2 cup of the whipped topping; stir with wire whisk until well blended. Pour into 8-inch square pan.
Freeze 4 hours or until firm.
Draw fork repeatedly through whipped topping mixture just before serving; spoon evenly into six dessert dishes. Top with the remaining 1/2 cup whipped topping; sprinkle with cinnamon. Store leftover dessert in freezer.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
A great treat to compliment your after dinner coffee.
