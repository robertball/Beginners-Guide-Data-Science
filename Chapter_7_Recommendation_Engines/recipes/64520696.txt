Title:
Breakfast egg, sausage, and bacon muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of Bisquick
3 eggs
1 cup shredded cheese (whichever is your favorite)
1 lbs cooked breakfast sausage
8 slices of cooked bacon

Directions:
In a mixing bowl add eggs and Bisquick
stir with whisk
add cook sausage and mix
add 4 slices of bacon crumbled
mix thoroughly
add mixture to nonstick muffin pan
*note muffins will rise so don't add too much mixture*
top with rest of bacon crumbled
bake at 350 degrees for 15 mins or until browned enough
