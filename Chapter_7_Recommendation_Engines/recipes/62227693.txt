Title:
Rosie's Sour Cream Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
2 cups Sugar
2/3 cup Butter/Margarine
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup sour cream
2 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
Topping:
5 tbs brown sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Mix together all cake ingredients in order they come.
Mix topping in seperate bowl.
Grease and flour bundt pan. Spoon in half the batter and top with half the topping. Repeat layers with remaining ingredients. Bake at 350 for one hour.  Finely chopped nuts or raisins may be added if desired.
