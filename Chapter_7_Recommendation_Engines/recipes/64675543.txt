Title:
Sweet and Sour Glazed Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds chicken wings, split
Salt and pepper
4 cups pomegranate juice
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 teaspoon coarse salt
2 teaspoons chili powder
1/4 teaspoon cayenne

Directions:
Preheat oven t 450 degrees
Place wings on baking sheet and sprinkle with salt and pepper
Bake until deep golden brown, crisp, and cooked through, 45-50 minutes
In a sauce pan, combine pomegranate juice, brown sugar, salt and bring to a boil over high heat
Reduce by three-quarters, it should coat the back of a spoon, 35-40 minutes
Stir in spices
Toss hot wings with mixture.  Serve warm
