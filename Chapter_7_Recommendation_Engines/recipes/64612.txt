Title:
Balsamic Glazed Pearl Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987150.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
2 teaspoons HERB-OX® Beef Flavored Bouillon Granules
1 (16 ounce) package frozen pearl onions, thawed and drained
1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
1 tablespoon sugar
2 sprigs fresh thyme

Directions:
Heat the butter and beef granules in a large saute pan over medium heat and add the onions. Saute until tender, about 5 minutes. Add balsamic vinegar, sugar and thyme and heat until onions are coated and browned.

Submitter: 
HERB-OX® Bouillon
Description: 
The rich flavors in these onions makes an elegant, fast and simple side dish.
