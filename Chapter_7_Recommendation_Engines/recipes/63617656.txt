Title:
Lazy Daisy Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
scant 1 cup sugar
1 cup flour
1 tsp. baking powder.
pinch of salt
2 tablespoons butter
1/2 cup milk
TOPPING
1 cup unsweetened medium coconut
3 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons milk
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease and flour,or spray baking Pam in a 9x9 baking pan
Put butter and milk in a pot over low heat until butter melts. In a medium sized bowl beat the eggs, add the sugar, beat till glossy. Add the rest of the ingredients and stir till combined. Pour into baking pan. Bake for 20- 25 minutes
While the squares are baking, put the ingredients for the topping in a bowl, and stir till combined. It will be a little stiff, but it will spread.
When the squares are done spread topping over squares while hot  and put under the broiler for 2 minutes, till lightly browned, watch closely.
