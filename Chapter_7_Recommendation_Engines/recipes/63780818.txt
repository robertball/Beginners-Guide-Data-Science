Title:
Healthy Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12.5 oz. can Chicken Breast
1/4 cup low fat mayonnaise or Greek yogurt
1/4 tsp paprika
1/2 cup chopped celery
2 green onions, chopped
1/4 cup green bell pepper, minced
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
In medium bowl, mix together mayonnaise with paprika.  Blend in celery, onions and bell pepper.  Add drained chicken and gently blend.  Season with salt and perp pepper to taste.  Chill at least 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Cindi Fortmann
Description: 
This is a very easy, yet delicious chicken salad.  Sometimes, I like to add some garlic and/or curry powder to the recipes to spice it up.
