Title:
Toll House Walnut Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3919716.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 inch) pie shell
2 eggs
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup butter, melted
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
In a large mixing bowl, beat eggs until foamy. Beat in white sugar and brown sugar until ingredients are thoroughly combined, then add flour. Stir well. Blend in melted butter.
Stir chocolate chips and walnuts into mixture. Pour mixture into pie shell.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour. Serve warm with whipped cream or ice cream, if desired.

Submitter: 
Betty
Description: 
This version of the popular pie contains lots of crunchy walnuts. At its best when served warm with whipped cream or ice cream.
