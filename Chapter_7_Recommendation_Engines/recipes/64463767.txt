Title:
Sloppy Toms with Turkey Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
2 lbs ground turkey
1 package turkey dogs (6-8 turkey dogs), cut into bite-sized slices
1 onion, chopped (or use about 1 cup frozen chopped onion)
1 15oz can tomato sauce
3 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
3 tbsp dark brown sugar
1 tbsp chili powder
1 tbsp steak seasoning (such as Montreal Steak Seasoning)
dash of white vinegar (optional)
8 hot dog or hamburger buns

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.  Add turkey and cook for 3 minutes, stirring regularly and breaking up chunks into smaller crumbles.
Add sliced hot dogs and cook for another 2-3 minutes.  Add onions and cook for another 5-6 minutes.
In a medium bowl, mix together tomato sauce, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, chili powder, steak seasoning, and optional vinegar.  Pour over turkey and onions after the meat and onions are finished cooking.  Mix well and simmer for another few minutes.
