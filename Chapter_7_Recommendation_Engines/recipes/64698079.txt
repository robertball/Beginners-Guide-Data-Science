Title:
Vegan Cabbage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Head of Cabbage
3 cups Water
1 can Diced Tomatoes, 14.5oz
1 can Spicy V8, 11.5oz
1/2 cup white sugar
2 tbsp Rice Vinegar
1 1/2 tsp Salt
1 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
1 medium Onion
4 Garlic Cloves
3 stalks Celery
1 Leek
1/2 cup Vegetable Oil
Optional: Carrots

Directions:
1. Shred the cabbage and put in the crockpot
2. Add water, spicy V8, diced tomatoes, sugar, vinegar, salt, and pepper to the crockpot, turn on high
3. Dice onion, leek, and celery
4. Heat vegetable oil. Add onion, leek, celery, and garlic cloves, stir to coat vegetables in oil, cover over medium heat until onion, leek, and celery have softened
5. Blend the softened mixture (it will be green) and add it to the crockpot (soup base will turn reddish brown)
6. Heat in crockpot until cabbage reaches your preferred level of softness versus crunchiness
Optional: Add carrots to crockpot
Note: Delicious over rice

Submitter: 
OneSpunCookie
Description: 
An easy soup one can make with a blender and in the crockpot, AKA slow cooker.
