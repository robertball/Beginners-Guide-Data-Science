Title:
Banana Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large box vailla pudding (instant)
4 Bananas sliced
3 cups milk
1 can EagleBrand sweetened condensed milk
1 small tun Whipped Cream
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 box of Vanilla Waffers

Directions:
Slice bananas in 1/4" slices set aside. Combine milk and sweetened condensed milk in mixing bowl. Add vanilla and pudding mix to milk. Fold in whipped cream. Do not over mix! Add a layer of cookies and a layer of bananas to the bottom of a large bowl. Cover with pudding. Repeat steps until full. Top with cookies and refrigerate for 30mins.
