Title:
Joan's Mexican Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Fritolay bean dip (or refried beans if you can't get the bean dip)
1 c. sour cream (I use reduced fat)
1/2 c. mayonnaise
i can sliced olives
1 package of taco seasoning
3 avocados, mashed with 1 t. lemon juice
3 chopped tomatoes
3 scallions, chopped
Grated cheddar cheese -- Mexican combination works well too

Directions:
Spread bean dip in a flat serving dish
Mix sour cream, mayonnaise, and  +/- 1/2 package (to taste) of taco seasoning and spread in dish
Layer avocados in the dish
Mix tomatoes, olives and scallions and layer in dish
Top with a layer of cheese.
Serve with scoop sized Fritos or tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Sudie B
Description: 
This is my sister-in-law Joan's recipe.  Our kids, now adult. have requested it for years.
