Title:
Easiest Pudding Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 20-oz can crushed pineapple
1 16-oz container fat-free whipped topping
1 4-oz box pistachio instant pudding mix

Directions:
Use refrigerated topping or thaw a short time on counter for easiest mixing.  Whisk pineapple (undrained) and pudding mix together for two full minutes.  Fold in whipped topping.  Refrigerate for at least 5 minutes to set.

Submitter: 
Nonei
Description: 
Quick and easy, and not nearly as sweet as most pistachio pudding salads!
