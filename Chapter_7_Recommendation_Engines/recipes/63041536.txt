Title:
Chicken and Bow Ties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz bow tie pasta
2 TBL olive oil
1 tsp dried basil, crushed
1/8 tsp crushed red pepper
1/2 cup oil-packed dried tomatoes, drained & cut into thin strips
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 lb. skinless, bonesless chicken breast halves, cut into thin bite-size strips
1 can (14 oz) chicken broth
1/4 cup dry white wine
1/2 pint whipping cream

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions; drain.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet, cook the garlic in hot oil over medium heat for 30 seconds. Add chicken, basil and crushed red pepper. Cook and stir for 4 minutes or until browned. Add chicken browht, dried tomates and white wine. Bring to boiling; reduce heat. Simmer, uncovered, about 10 minutes or until chicken is tender and no longer pink. Stir in whipping cream and the 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese; simmer 2 minutes more. Stir pasta into chicken mixture. Heat thru. Pass additional Parmesan cheese, if desired.
