Title:
White Chocolate Sand Dollars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz white chocolate chips
1 cup chopped nuts
1 cup dried cranberries, chopped
1/2 cup shredded coconut, toasted

Directions:
Heat white chocolate in microwave safe bowl, in one minute intervals, until melted, stirring after each minute.
When melted, add remaining ingredients and stir until combined.
Spoon by tablespoonful onto wax paper or Silpat.
Allow chocolate to harden.

Submitter: 
Jaay
Description: 
These delicious candies make a quick and easy snack for any chocolate lover!  They received their name when I quickly looked at them and proclaimed they resemble the lovely little ocean creature.
