Title:
Angel Hair with Roasted Red Pepper Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. angel hair pasta
2 tbs olive oil
1 cup finely chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, sliced
1 12 oz jar roasted red peppers
1/2 cup half and half
pinch cayenne pepper

Directions:
Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Stir in pasta. Reduce heat and cook pasta until al dente, about 4 minutes. Drain pasta. Return pasta to the pot, toss in olive oil and cover pot to keep pasta warm. Heat remaining tbs olive oil in a large skillet. Add onion and garlic, cook until onions soften, about 3 minutes. Place onion and garlic mixture, red peppers, half and half and cayenne in blender and puree until smooth. Spoon over pasta and coat well. Serve at once.
