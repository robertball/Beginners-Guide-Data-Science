Title:
Banana Nog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 banana
1 c milk
1 T sugar
1/4 t vanilla
5-7 ice cubes

Directions:
Combine ingredients in blender at slow speed to break up ice.  Blend for 1 minute at high speed.
