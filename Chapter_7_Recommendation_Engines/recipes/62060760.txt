Title:
CHEESE  FILLED  LOAF Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. chipped cream cheese
1 (crock refill size) Cheddar or wine
Cheddar or Port cheese
1/2 c. beer
Salt, pepper, garlic powder
1 med. size loaf pumpernickel bread

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.  Hollow pumpernickel loaf.  Fill with cheese mixture. Cut pumpernickel bread into cubes for dipping.
