Title:
Old-time Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups thinly sliced, pared onions
2/3 cup minced onions
2 tbsp flour
1 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
2 tbsp butter or margarine
1 Â½ cups scalded milk
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
4 slices provolone cheese
1/2 cup grated Parmesan or Romano cheese
paprika

Directions:
Start heating oven to 375 degrees
Arrange layer of potatoes in greased 2-qt casserole
Cover with some of onions
Sprinkle with some of combined flour, salt & pepper
Dot with some of butter
Repeat layers until all are used, ending with butter
Pour milk over all
Sprinkle with paprika
Bake, covered 45 min
Uncover
Bake 15 mins longer or until tender
