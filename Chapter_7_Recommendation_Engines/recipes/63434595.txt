Title:
Salmon Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz. sliced pieces of raw salmon
1/2 package of cream cheese
1/4 cup of dried tomatoes, sliced into small pieces
diced garlic
parmesan
pepper
dried paprika
1 premade crust
optional minced green onion or dried green onion

Directions:
Preheat the oven to about 375 degrees fahrenheit. Spreak the cream cheese over the bottom of the fork-poked crust. Sprinkle this with the salmon, dried tomato pieces and parmesan. Bake for about 7-8 minutes until the salmon changes color. Remove from the oven and top with pepper, dried ground paprika and/or green onion.

Submitter: 
Bonny Buckley
Description: 
Very quick, delicious and simple. It tastes like something out of a gourmet cafe.
