Title:
Pumpkin-Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups	unfitted all-purpose flour
2 cups	quick or old fashioned oats, 	uncooked
2 tsp. 	baking soda
2 tsp. 	ground cinnamon
1 tsp. 	salt
1 1/2 cups 	butter or margarine, softened
2 cups 	firmly packed brown sugar
1 cup 	granulated sugar
1 	egg
1 tsp. 	vanilla
1 can 16 oz. Libby's Solid Pack Pumpkin
1 cup 	semi-sweet chocolate 	morsels  (or raisins)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350o.
Combine flour, oats, soda, cinnamon and salt; set aside.
Cream butter; gradually add sugars, beating until light and fluffy.
Add egg and vanilla; mix well.  Alternate additions of dry ingredients and pumpkin, mixing well.  Stir in morsels.  Drop by heaping tablewspoon onto lightly greased sheet.  Spread into pumpkin shape.  Bake 20-25 min. until cookies are slightly-firm and lightly browned.  Do not overbake.  Remove and cool on rack.  Yield 32 large cookies or 64 medium cookies.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Miskiewicz
Description: 
The pumpkin in these cookies make them extra chewy and soft.  Nuts or raisins may be added also. I've been making them for about 20 years. They are wonderful for the fall.
