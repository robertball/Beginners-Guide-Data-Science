Title:
Minty Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
0.5 Cups Butter
0.5 Cups Brown Sugar
0.5 Cups White Sugar
2 Eggs
1 tsp. Vanilla
0.5 tsp. Instant Coffee
0.25 Cups Pecans
2 Cups Flour
0.25 tsp. Salt
0.5 tsp. Baking Soda
0.5 Cups Andes Creme de Menthe Baking Chips
0.5 Cups Semi Sweet Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
Cream butter and sugar.
Beat in eggs, vanilla and instant coffee.
Grind pecans in a coffee grinder.
Mix in ground pecans.
In a separate bowl, mix together flour, salt and baking soda.
Next, add flour mixture to egg mixture.
Combine until all flour is incorporated.
Add Andes Baking Chips and Chocolate Chips, mix well.
Spoon onto a greased baking sheet.
Bake for 8 to 11 minutes or until cookies are golden brown.
