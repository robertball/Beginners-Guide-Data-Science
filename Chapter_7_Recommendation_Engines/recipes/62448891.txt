Title:
baked trout fillets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 trout fillets
2 TB butter or marg.
2 TB chopped scallions
1 TB chopped fresh parsley
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
4 small artichoke hearts, halved (canned in water)
1/2 TB Lemon juice
1/3 cup white wine
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon cayenne
1/2 c. chopped cherry tomatoes

Directions:
Preheat oven 325
Melt butter and saute onion parsley and garlic for 1-2 minutes. Add mushrooms for 1-2 minutes. Add lemon, salt and cayenne. Remove from heat and gently stir in artichoke hearts.  Pour over fish and bake for 20 min.  Sprinkle with tomatoes and place under broiler for 1-2 minutes to brown top of fish.
