Title:
Herbed Quinoa Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup qinoa
2 cups chicken stock
1/2 cup cucumber, diced
1/2 cup tomato, diced
1/4 cup red onion, diced
2 green onions, diced
2tbs cilantro, chopped
1/2 tsp ancho chili powder
3 tbs extra virgin olive oil
1 tbs lemon juice

Directions:
Add quinoa to stock in a pot and bring to a boil
Reduce heat and simmer for 10 - 15 minutes, or until liquid is absorbed. Remove from heat and let cool completely.
In a large bowl combine all the rest of the ingrediants to the quinoa and toss well.
Chill and serve.
