Title:
Balken Brei I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Pounds Pork
2 Pounds Beef
4 C. Craklings
5 C. Flour
Salt and Pepper to taste
Nutmeg
Allspice
Cloves
Crushed Anise Seed, optional

Directions:
Cook pork and beef in 1 Gal. of water until done.  Save broth and let cool.
Grind meats.
Add meats and cracklings to the broth, bring to boil, add spices, enough to suite your own taste.
Sift in flour, while stiring the whole time.  The mixture must be very thick.
When well cooked, pour into greased loaf pans.
When cold, slice and fry very crisp, and serve hot with syrup.
