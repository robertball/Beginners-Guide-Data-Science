Title:
Raspberry Citrus Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1cup butter softened
3/4cup sifted powdered sugar
2cups all-purpose flour
4 eggs
1 1/2cups granulated sugar
1/3cup lemon juice
2tbl finely shredded orange peel
1/4cup all-purpose flour
1tsp baking powder
1 1/2cups fresh raspberries and/or blueberries
Powdered Sugar

Directions:
Crust:
Beat butter with an electric mixer on medium speed for 30 seconds to soften.
Add 3/4cup powdered sugar. Beat till combined.
Press mixture onto bottom of greased 13x9x2" pan.
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes, or until golden brown.
Filling:
While crust is baking mix eggs, granulated sugar, lemon juice, orange peel, the 1/4cup of flour, and baking powder in a large bowl.Beat 2 minutes with electric mixer till combined.
Sprinkle berries over crust after it has finished baking.
Pour filling over berries arranging berries evenly with a spoon.
Bake dish at 350 for 30-35 minutes or until light brown and filling is set.
Cool thoroughly in pan on wire rack.
Cut into triangles or bars as desired.
Just before serving sprinkle with powdered sugar.
