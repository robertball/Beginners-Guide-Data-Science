Title:
Platino 96  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984384.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Jose Cuervo Platino® Tequila
1/4 ounce simple syrup
1 dash Angostura bitters
3 mint leaves
2 ounces Champagne

Directions:
Combine simple syrup and 2 mint leaves in mixing glass and muddle.  Add Jose Cuervo Platino and Angostura Bitters, ice and shake.  Strain into a chilled flute or coupe glass.
Top with champagne and garnish with a mint leaf.

Submitter: 
Cuervo®
Description: 
When creating the archetypical tequila drink, get authentic flavor with Jose Cuervo Platino® tequila.
