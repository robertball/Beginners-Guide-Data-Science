Title:
Mushroom Lentil Barley Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1965050.jpg
Ingredients:
2 quarts vegetable broth
2 cups sliced fresh button mushrooms
1 oz dried shiitake mushrooms, torn into pieces opt
6 strips smoky maple bacon "Tofurky" chopped Opt
3/4 cup uncooked pearl barley
3/4 cup dry lentils
1 small onion diced
1 red bell pepper diced opt
1 stalk celery and leaves, diced opt
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley opt
6 cloves minced garlic, divided
2 teaspoons dried summer savory
3 bay leaves
1 teaspoon dried basil
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a slow cooker, mix the broth, button mushrooms, shiitake mushrooms, barley, lentils, onion, bell pepper, celery, parsley, 1/2 the garlic, savory, bay leaves, basil, pepper, and salt.
Cover, and cook 4 to 6 hours on High or 10 to 12 hours on Low. Or cook on stove top for about 2 hours. Add the other half of garlic at end. Remove bay leaves before serving.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
This crock pot recipe is easy to assemble and needs no attention while it cooks. The flavors blend nicely to give it a wholesome earthy flavor that is unbelievably vegan. Serve with garlic bread or corn bread.Modified from: Mushroom Lentil Barley Stew
