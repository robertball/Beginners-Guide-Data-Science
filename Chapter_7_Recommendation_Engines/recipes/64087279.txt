Title:
Chocolate Fudge Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
4 oz. unsweetened chocolate
2 cups flour
1/4 cup corn starch
2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup butter
2 1/4 cups (1 pound) brown sugar
3 eggs
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 cup sour cream
1 cup boiling water
frosting:
4 oz. unsweetened chocolate
1/2 cup butter
1 pound powdered sugar
1/2 cup milk
2 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place chocolate and butter in microwavable bowl. Microwave 1 minute, stir. Microwave 1 more minute. Make sure all chocolate is melted. Set aside.
Cream sugar, eggs and chocolate mixture until fluffy. Add vanilla, baking soda and salt, then add flour and cornstarch alternately with sour cream. Mix well. Stir in boiling water. Pour into greased and floured 9 inch layer pans. Bake at 350 degrees for approximately 20 minutes or until it tests done. Cool in pans on wire rack 10 minutes
Frosting:
Place chocolate and butter in microwavable bowl, microwave until melted, see above. Combine sugar, milk, vanilla and chocolate mixture in medium sized bowl. Set bowl in pan of ice water and beat with a wooden spoon until frosting is thick enough to spread.
