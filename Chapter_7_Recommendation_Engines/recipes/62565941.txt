Title:
** Chuck's Oatmeal Krispies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. shortening
1 c. brown sugar
1 c. white sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 c. flour
3 c. regular oatmeal
1 tsp. salt
1 tst. baking soda
1/2 c. nuts or raisins (opt)

Directions:
Cream together shortening, brown sugar & white sugar. Add eggs & vanilla.
Stir in dry ingredients.
Divide into 2 rolls. Wrap rolls in wax paper/plastic wrap & chill in refrigerator until firm (or 1 hr. in freezer).
Preheat oven 350 degrees.
Cut into 1/4" slices. Bake on cookie sheet for 10 min.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
From good friend Carolyn back in the 1980's. I remember her kids sneaking warm cookies off the cooling rack. They're still great cookies.
