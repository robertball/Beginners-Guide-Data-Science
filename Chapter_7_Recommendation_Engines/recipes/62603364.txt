Title:
Cookie Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/561759.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup sifted confectioner's sugar
2-4 tsp. milk
2 tsp. light corn syrup
assorted food coloring

Directions:
Place sugar and milk in bowl. Stir until mix thoroughly.
Add corn syrup and mix well.
Add food coloring, if desired.
If too stiff add couple more drops of milk.

Submitter: 
Mollie
Description: 
This is a basic cookie icing that dries to a shiny, hard finish. Great for decorating sugar cookies, making outlines and fill-in.
