Title:
Easy Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter (smooth or crunchy)
1 cup white sugar
1 egg

Directions:
Mix together peanut butter, sugar and egg.
Roll into balls and flatten with a fork on an ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake at 325F for 12 minutes.

Submitter: 
Lynda McLean Falloon
Description: 
The easiest peanut butter cookie recipe I've come across and they are soft and full of yummy peanut butter flavour!
