Title:
Fish and Shrimp Marinara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 onion, thinly sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup dry white wine
2 (14.5 ounce) cans seasoned or unseasoned diced tomatoes
2 T Italian or Tuscan Seasoning (I use Penzey's)
1 pound white fish fillets
1 dozen shrimp, peeled and deveined
Chopped fresh flat leaf parsley
Capers
Salt and pepper to taste
Cooked rice, polenta, or couscous

Directions:
In a large frying pan, heat oil over medium heat.  Saute onions and garlic in olive oil until softened.
Stir in wine and cook down for a minute.  Then add tomatoes and Italian Seasoning.  Simmer for 15 minutes. Season with salt and pepper
Place fillets and shrimp in sauce.  Simmer for about 5 more minutes, or until fish and shrimp turn white.
Sprinkle with capers and parsley. Serve with or or over rice, polenta, or couscous

Submitter: 
ItalianFoodie
Description: 
Any white fish fillets work well for this dish.  The shrimp add an extra dimension. For family or company:  superb tasting fish dish!  Serve over rice, polenta, or couscous.Modified from: Fish Fillets Italiano
