Title:
Best-Ever Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
2 cups flour
1/4 cup cocoa
1 stick butter
1 cup water
1/2 cup oil
2 eggs
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 1/2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. vanilla
1 box (16 oz.) powdered sugar
1/4 cup cocoa
1 stick butter
1/3 cup buttermilk
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Combine first 3 ingredients in a large bowl.  Melt butter with water and oil in a small pan over medium heat and bring to a boil.  Pour over dry ingredients and mix well.  Add eggs, 1/2 cup buttermilk, baking soda & 1 tsp. vanilla; mix well.  Pour into a 15x11" baking pan.  Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until done.  Meanwhile, combine powdered sugar and remaining cocoa in mixing bowl.
Remove cake fromm oven.  Melt remaining butter in small pan over medium heat.  Add sugar mixture with remaining buttermilk and vanilla; mix well.  Immediately pour over cake, spreading evenly to all sides.
Let cake cool completely before cutting.
