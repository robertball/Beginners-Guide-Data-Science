Title:
EASY Pumpkin-Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small can pumpkin
1 large over-ripe banana
1 cake mix (spice is best, or carrot or chocolate)
chocolate chips, raisins, nuts, optional

Directions:
Mix all together and bake in 4 small loaf pans for 25-30 minutes at 350 degrees.  Cool, wrap in clear plastic and foil.  Can be frozen.

Submitter: 
Karen Monson

Super fast when you need something nice and simple!
