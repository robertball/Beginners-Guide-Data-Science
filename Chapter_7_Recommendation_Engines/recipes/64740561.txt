Title:
Zaatar Buns Keto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup almond flour
1/4 cup of fresh zaatar or thyme leaves
3 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp butter
3 eggs
2 tbsp yogurt
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix all the ingredients and place in buttered muffin pan using an icecream scoop.
Bake for 30 minutes.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Mama & her Girls
Description: 
Satisfying Middle Eastern bun best served with olive oil to dip, olives, cucumbers and nabulsi or halloum cheese.
