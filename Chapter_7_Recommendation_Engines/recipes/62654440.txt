Title:
Chicken or Turkey Tetrazzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 ounces spaghetti
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup chopped onion
2 clove garlic, minced
1 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
2 cups shredded American cheese
3 cups cooked and cubed chicken
1 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1 tablespoons diced red bell pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Cook spaghetti according to package directions, add salt to cooking water. Drain.
In large saucepan or Dutch oven over medium heat, melt butter. Add onion and garlic. Cook, stirring occasionally, 2 minutes. Add soup, broth, Cajun seasoning, bell pepper, parsley and 1 cup of the cheese. Cook and stir until cheese melts and mixture is smooth. Add spaghetti and chicken or turkey. Mix well. Transfer to 2-quart baking dish. Cover with foil.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
Remove foil. Sprinkle with remaining 1 1/2 cups. cheese. Continue baking 2 minutes or until cheese melts.

Description: 
Warm up a cold night with a satisfyingly creamy bake of chicken or turkey in cream of mushroom soup and American cheese with spaghetti noodles. A sprinkling of chopped parsley and bell peppers adds colorful crunch.Modified from: Chicken or Turkey Tetrazzini
