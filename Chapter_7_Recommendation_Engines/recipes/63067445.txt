Title:
Cheddar Tators Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123176.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can cr. chicken soup
1 can (12 oz.) evaporated milk
1 c. sour cream
1/2 c. butter, melted
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. onion powder
1 pkg. (32 oz.) frozen tator tots
1 1/2 c. (6 oz.) shredded cheddar cheese
1 c. crushed potato chips

Directions:
Combine first 6 ingredients.
Gently stir in tator tots.
Transfer to greased 13x9 pan.
Sprinkle on cheese and chips.
Bake @350 degrees for 30-35 min.
