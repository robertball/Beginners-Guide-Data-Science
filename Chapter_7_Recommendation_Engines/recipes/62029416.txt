Title:
One-Pan Tuna and Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/679732.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped green pepper
1 clove minced garlic
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
2 (5 ounce) cans tuna, drained
3 cups (6 ounces) dry egg noodles
1 (10-1/2 ounce) can cream of celery or cream of mushroom soup
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
3 cups milk
1 (3 ounce) can chopped mushrooms, undrained (optional)
1 (15 ounce) can peas, drained OR 1-1/2 cup (202 grams) frozen peas, thawed

Directions:
In a deep skillet, saute' onion, celery, green pepper and garlic in butter until crisp-tender. Add tuna in a layer; sprinkle noodles over top.
Combine soup, salt, pepper, milk and mushrooms. Pour over noodles, making certain all of the noodles are moistened. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer gently until noodles are just tender (about 10 minutes), stirring occasionally. Add peas and let sit for a few minutes to thicken.
