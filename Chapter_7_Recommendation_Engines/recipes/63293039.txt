Title:
Pork Rites (Taverns) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb ground pork
2 Tbsp vinegar
1/2 cup water
1 cup ketchup
1 Tbsp brown sugar
1 tsp dry mustard
1 tsp salt
12 hamburger buns

Directions:
Brown pork and drain.
Combine remaining ingredients.  Add to pork.
Simmer 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
jodygreiner
Description: 
Similar to a sloppy joe but the pork gives it an unique flavor.
