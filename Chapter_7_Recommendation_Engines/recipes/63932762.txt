Title:
Chicken Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts cut into strips or cubes
1 tablespoon olive oil
4 slices bacon
8 oz. fettuccine
½ cup butter
½ cup cream or half and half
1 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 tablespoon parsley flakes
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook bacon until crispy
Remove from pan and drain on paper towels.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta, and cook for 8 to 10 minutes, or until al dente;  drain.
While pasta is cooking, saute chicken in olive oil until juices run clear.
Remove chicken from pan and set aside.
Heat butter and cream in pan over low heat until butter is melted.
Add cheese, parsley, salt & pepper and stir until cheese is melted.
Serve cheese sauce over fettuccine with chicken. Crumble bacon on top. Top with more grated parmesan cheese if desired.

This is not figure friendly, but if I want to splurge this is my go to recipe. Serve with a nice salad and some crusty Italian bread.
