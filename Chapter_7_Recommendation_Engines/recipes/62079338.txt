Title:
Catalina Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons vegetable oil
6 boneless skinless chicken breast halves. (If the breasts are thick I pound them to make them thinner)
1 cup Catalina Dressing
I cup apricot jam
1 envelope dry onion soup mix

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 350 degrees. Heat oil in large skillet on medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook 4 minutes on each side or until browned. Remove chicken from skillet and place in baking dish.
MIX dressing, jam and soup mix in small bowl then pour over chicken.
BAKE 45 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
Serve with rice and a tossed green salad to complete the meal.

Submitter: 
Oprah
Description: 
Combining Catalina dressing with apricot jam and onion soup mix creates a delicious flavor that is both spicy and sweet. My family and friends love it.
