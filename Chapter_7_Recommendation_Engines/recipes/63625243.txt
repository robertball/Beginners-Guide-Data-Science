Title:
Asian Teriyaki Ginger Meatball Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 oz) package al fresco Teriyaki Ginger Chicken Meatballs, placed on a sheet pan, coated with cooking spray
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 tbsp ginger root, fresh, finely grated
1 tsp garlic cloves, fresh, minced
2 tbsp sherry wine
1 (32 oz) can chicken broth, low fat, unsalted
1/2 tsp red chili pepper, crushed flakes
(3 oz) angel hair pasta, whole wheat, dry
1/2 cup scallions, fresh, chopped
1/2 cup red bell pepper, sliced
(3 oz) snow peas, fresh blanched about 1 cup, sliced into thirds
1 tsp sesame oil (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350&deg; f.
Heat the al fresco Teriyaki Ginger Meatballs for 15 minutes.
Place a medium size sauce pot, coat bottom with olive oil over medium heat, and gently saute the ginger and garlic, stir vigorously for 2 minutes. Add the sherry, after 1 minute, add the chicken broth. Bring to a boil and add the chili pepper flakes.
Meanwhile, in a separate sauce pot, cook the angel hair pasta according to package instructions. Drain and add to the soup pot when done.
Add the scallions, red peppers and snow peas to the soup pot, simmer for 2 minutes.
Season with the sesame oil.
