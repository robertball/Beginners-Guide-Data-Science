Title:
Orzo Antipasto Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c uncooked orzo pasta
1 c frozen peas
1 can chopped baby corn
1 sm jar marinated artichoke hearts, drained
1 jar marinated mushrooms, drained
1 jar marinated asparagus spears, chopped & drained
1 c kalamata olives, pitted and drained
4 slices string cheese, chopped
1 Tbsp garlic, minced
1/2 c flat leaf parsley, chopped
zest of 1 lemon
juice of 1-2 lemons
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook orzo pasta according to directions until just done.
Drain and add peas.
Rinse under cool water until all pasta is completely cold.
Add remaining ingredients, stir, and chill at least.
Serve.

Submitter: 
MERRIPAN
Description: 
I love antipasto, but don't like how difficult it is to transport.  This was a great way to combine two things I love, with a fresh, clean taste to it that everyone can enjoy.  Perfect for summer camping and picnics, and easy to prepare and travel with!
