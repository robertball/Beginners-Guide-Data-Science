Title:
Grilled Fillets and Vegetable Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984232.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Gorton's® Grilled Fillets (any flavor)
1 cup chicken broth
1 tablespoon vinegar
2 teaspoons cornstarch
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 (16 ounce) package Vegetable Mixture: Broccoli, Red Pepper, Onion and Mushroom, or mix of your choice
Cooked rice

Directions:
Cook Gorton's Grilled Fillets according to package directions.
Meanwhile, in 12-inch skillet, bring broth, vinegar, cornstarch and black pepper to a boil over high heat. Continue boiling, stirring frequently, 1 minute or until slightly thickened. Stir in vegetables and return to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer covered, stirring occasionally, 7 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
To serve, arrange hot rice on serving plates. Evenly top with vegetable mixture, then hot Grilled Fillets.

Submitter: 
Gorton's®
Description: 
In the mood for a lighter seafood meal? This recipe will delight your tastebuds and provide you with a plate full of protein and veggies. Just mix Gorton's low fat grilled fillets with tender vegetables and place on top of your favorite rice for a delicious, well-balanced meal.
