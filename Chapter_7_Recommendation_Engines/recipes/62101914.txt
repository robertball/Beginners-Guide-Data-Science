Title:
Fantastic "Fried" Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups poultry stuffing mix, any kind
2 Tbsps. grated Parmesan
1 clove of garlic, crushed
1/2 stick melted butter
6 meaty chicken pieces with bones
(I always use thighs, skinned)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 deg.
Pour stuffing mix in blender/food proc.Pulse till they are crumb-like.
Stir in 2 Tbsps. Parmesan cheese.
Melt butter in saucepan and add crushed garlic while butter is melting.
Dip chicken pieces in melted butter/garlic,then dip them in crumb mix.
Place breaded chicken pieces on a greased cookie sheet.
Bake at 350 deg. for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
SheasLounge
Description: 
Easy and tasty baked chicken pieces that are great for summer picnics or outdoor
gatherings - just double or triple recipe to adjust servings.
I always figure 2 pcs. for each person.
