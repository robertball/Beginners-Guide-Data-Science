Title:
Zucchini Pineapple Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
2 cups sugar
1 cup oil
1 1/4 tsp. vanilla
2 cups grated zucchini
3 cups flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
1 cup crushed pineapple, drained
1/2 cup raisins (optional)
1 cup nut meats
add all spice or pumplin pie spice

Directions:
Beat together oil, eggs, sugar and vanilla.  Add zucchini and dry ingredients.  Stir in pineapple, raisins and nuts.  Pour into 2 greased and floured loaf pans.  Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees.
