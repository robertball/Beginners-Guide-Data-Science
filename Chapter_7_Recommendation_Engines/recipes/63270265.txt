Title:
Mediterranean Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
pinch of sea salt & pepper
2 tbs extra virgin olive oil
1 small red onion, sliced
2 tbs garlic, minced
2 C grape tomatoes, diced
2 tbs fresh basil, chiffonaded
1/4 C kalamata olives, diced
4 C baby spinach

Directions:
Rinse the shrimp and pat dry.
Season with the salt & pepper and set aside.
In a large saute pan, heat the olive oil on medium heat.
Add the onions and garlic, saute for 2-3 minutes until the onions lightly crisp.
Add the tomatoes and saute another minute.
Add the shrimp and sear 1 minute on each side.
Add the basil and olives, stirring everything together gently.
Add the spinach to wilt, toss gently and serve.
