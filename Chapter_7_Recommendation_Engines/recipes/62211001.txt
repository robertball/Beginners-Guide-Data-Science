Title:
Puffed Wheat Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2cup butter
1/2 cup corn syrup
1cup brown sugar
8 cups puffed wheat cereal
6 Tbsp. cocoa
4 handful mini marshmallows
cooking spray

Directions:
over low heat in med. saucepan, stir butter, sugar and cornsyrup til mixed. Add marshmallows, stir til melted. In a large bowl, mix together puffed wheat and sauce. then press into a pan sprayed with cooking spray. chill, cut into squares.

Submitter: 
Eirene
Description: 
The only good way to eat Puffed Wheat, lol! This is like rice crispie squares, except better!
