Title:
Chili Powder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c ground coriander
1T ground ginger
2 T telecherry black pepper
1 1/2 T ground jalepno pepper
1 1/2 T ground cayenne
1/4 c ground cumin
1/4 c hungarian paprika

Directions:
Sift ingredients together, store in airtight containers.
Taste:  3 T moderate,  4T warm,   6T very hot
