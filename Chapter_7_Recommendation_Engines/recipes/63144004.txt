Title:
Grandma Rau's Nut Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
3 Tbsp sugar
1 stick butter
1 cup sour cream
3 egg yolks
1 packet yeast
Filling:
1 1/4 cup sugar
1 stick butter
1/2 cup milk
1 pound walnuts, ground fine
1 tsp vanilla
1 egg

Directions:
Dough:
Mix together flour, salt, sugar, and butter until crumbly.
Combine sour cream, egg yolks, and yeast. Add to the dry mixture and blend well.
Refrigerate overnight or at least 8 hours. Cut into 4 pieces.
Filling:
Heat sugar, butter, & milk until sugar dissolves. Stir in ground walnuts & vanilla. Add milk if the filling is too thick.
Roll each piece of dough out to 1/4 inch. Add filling and roll up. Place on a well greased baking sheet (or sheet covered in parchment paper) Brush with an egg wash (1 egg mixed with 1 Tbsp water).
Allow to rise around 1/2 - 1 hour. Bake 30 minutes at 375 degrees.

Submitter:

Description: 
Hungarian Nut roll with ground walnut filling... could make it into a poppy seed roll instead!
