Title:
Caribbean Breeze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1379039.jpg
Ingredients:
ice
1 fluid ounce orange vodka
1 fluid ounce raspberry vodka
1/2 fluid ounce coconut-flavored rum
2 fluid ounces orange juice
2 fluid ounces pineapple juice
1 dash grenadine
1 orange wedge, for garnish

Directions:
Fill a tall glass with ice. Pour the orange vodka, raspberry vodka, rum, orange juice, pineapple juice, and grenadine over the ice; stir well. Garnish with an orange wedge.

Description: 
This is a refreshing, fruity cocktail.
