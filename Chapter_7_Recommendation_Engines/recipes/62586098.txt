Title:
Ginger Crisp Cookies (WW-2 Pts) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp ground cinamon
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp sea salt
1/8 tsp ground all spice
3/4 cup sugar
4 tbsp unsalted butter, room temp.
1 large egg
2 tbsp molasses

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350.
In med. bowl whisk, flour, ginger, cinamon, baking soda, salt & allspice.
Reserve 1/4 cup of the sugar in a small bowl.
In a large bowl, with electric mixer on med. speed, cream the remaining 1/2 cup sugar and the butter.  Mix in the egg molasses, continuing to beat until combine until combined.  Mix in the dry ingredients.
Drop dough onto greased cookie sheet by the tablespoon full, leaving 2-3 inches between cookies.  Spray the surface of a flat surface bottom container (measuring cup) na ddip into remaining sugar and flatten the mounds of dough.
Bake until cookies are firm to the touch and lightly colored around the edges, 10-12 minutes.
Cool on the baking sheet 10 minutes then remove the cookies from baking sheet and cool completely on rack.

Description: 
To boost spiciness, replace up to 3 tablespoons of the 1/2 cup granulated sugar used in the dookie dough with dark brown sugar, this will also broduce a slightly chewier cookie.
