Title:
Tortellini Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3205963.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg tortellini (any kind)
2 stalks celery, chopped
1 cup chopped onion
1/2 jar spaghetti sauce
1/2 pkg vegetarian ground beef
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Bring a pot of water to a boil, add tortellini and cook as package recommends. Cook veggie ground as recommended on package. Mix celery and onion together with sauce in a small pot, heat on low.  Once everything is done, mix it all together in a casserole dish.  Bake at 375 for 15 minutes, the broil for another 10-15 mins. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Shyanne Eileen Marchand
Description: 
With tortellini, spaghetti sauce, your choice of veggies and a vegetarian ground beef.
