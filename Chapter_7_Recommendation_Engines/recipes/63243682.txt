Title:
Country STRAWBERRY Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups of strawberries
1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated crescent roll dough
half a stick of butter
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
6 oz of Mountain Dew â„¢
Vanilla Ice Cream - optional

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Separate the crescent roll dough into four rectangles. Make a pocket with each crescent roll and roll a handful of strawberries into each rectangle. Pinch to seal and place in the baking dish.
Melt butter in a small saucepan and stir in the sugar and cinnamon. Pour over the strawberry dumplings. Pour Mountain Dew(TM) over the dumplings.
Bake for 35 to 45 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown.
Serve with vanilla ice cream!
