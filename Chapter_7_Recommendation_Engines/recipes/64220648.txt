Title:
Green Beans and Arugula Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb Green Beans, trimmed
2 Tbl Olive Oil
3 Garlic cloves, thinly sliced lengthwise
1/2 lb Arugula, chopped with stems removed
1 tsp fresh Lemon zest
3/4 tsp Salt
1/2 tsp Black Pepper

Directions:
Cook beans for 4-6 minutes. Drain. Heat oil in skillet over high heat. Saut√© garlic, stirring, for 1 minute. Add beans, arugula, zest, salt and pepper for about 2 minutes.
