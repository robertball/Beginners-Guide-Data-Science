Title:
Macadamia Nut Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup shredded coconut
1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch single crust pie
1 c. Karo syrup
3/4 cup white sugar
1 cube butter, melted
3 eggs
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 cups coarsely chopped raw macadamia nuts
1 cup heavy whipping cream
3 tablespoons cream of coconut

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.  Press coconut into bottom and sides of unbaked pie shell.
In a large bowl, mix together the corn syrup and sugar,then add butter. Mix in eggs and vanilla and salt until well blended. Let stand 1 hour. Fold in macadamia nuts.  Pour into pie shell.
Bake for 50 minutes or longer in the preheated oven until top is brown, and filling is set. Let pie cool.
To make Topping:  In a chilled bowl, whip cream to soft peaks. Gradually pour in cream of coconut while whipping just until cream of coconut is fully incorporated. Slice pie into wedges and serve, passing coconut cream separately.

Submitter: 
Cathy Heinemann Tucker
Description: 
Shredded coconut is pressed into an unbaked pie shell and baked. The macadamia nut filling is stirred up, poured into the crust and topped with a luscious coconut whipped cream topping.Modified from: Marshall Islands Macadamia Nut Pie
