Title:
Pickled Cowcumbers ~ Delights for Ladies by Sir Hugh Platt, 1609. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Gal Water
4 pints Verjuice
2 C Kosher Salt
1 big handfull chopped Dill

Directions:
Wash and slice cucumbers.
Put all ingredients except cucumbers into a pot. Make pickle and allow to cool completely.
Place cucumbers into a jar and pour in liquid. Keep refrigerated.
