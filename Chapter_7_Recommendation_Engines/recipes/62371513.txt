Title:
Jackie's Crab and Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
1 tomato, chopped
1 onion, chopped
2 tablespoons of Big Daddy's Chia Roasted Garlic Rub
2 (6 ounce) cans crabmeat, drained and flaked
1 (4.5 ounce) can tiny shrimp, drained
1/2 cup banana pepper rings, chopped

Directions:
Melt the cream cheese in a fondue pot. Gently fold in tomato, onion, garlic, crab, shrimp, and peppers.

Submitter: 
BIG DADDY'S
Description: 
This dip is gets demolished every time I make it. Simple ingredients give unbelievable flavor when mixed together -- cream cheese, crab, shrimp, onion, tomato, garlic, and banana peppers served with tortillas chips. You won't regret serving this!Modified from: Jackie's Crab and Shrimp Dip
