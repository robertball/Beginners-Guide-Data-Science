Title:
Cooked Oatmeal Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. cooked oatmeal
3/4 c. honey
3/4 c. oil
4 T. yeast
1 T. sugar
1 1/2 c. warm water
3 c. warm milk
5 t. salt
12 c. white flour
3 c. more white flour

Directions:
Soak yeast & sugar in warm water.  Mix cereal, honey & oil in mixer.  Add yeast mixture (after it begins to grow) & enough flour to make a batter like pancake batter.  Add milk and salt.  Gradually add flour.  Mix for 10 min.  Let rise 20 min. in bread pans. Bake 375 degrees for 45 min.

Submitter: 
Miriam
Description: 
This is a yeast oatmeal bread that uses up leftover oatmeal.
