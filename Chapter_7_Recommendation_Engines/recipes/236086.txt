Title:
Microwave Chocolate Kahlua Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1089858.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package angel food cake mix
1 (18.25 ounce) package chocolate fudge cake mix
2 tablespoons water
1 tablespoon Kahlua Pumpkin Spice (or regular Kahlua)
Chocolate topping
Whipped cream
Other toppings: frosting, strawberry sauce

Directions:
Mix cake mixes together. (Mixture makes enough for about 24 Kahlua cakes).
Spoon 3 tablespoons of mixture into a lightly oiled coffee mug. Add water and Kahlua. Stir well and microwave until cooked through, 1 minute 20 seconds.*
Let the cake stand for a few minutes before adding topping of your choice.
