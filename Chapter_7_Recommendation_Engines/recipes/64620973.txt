Title:
Strawberries & Cream Sipper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz. Bolthouse Farms Strawberry Banana Smoothie
3 oz. Bolthouse Farms Vanilla Chai Smoothie
2-4 oz. water

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and pour over crushed ice.

Description: 
Recipe from the Gastroparesis-Friendly Kitchen. This is a very tasty little pick-me-up with a good amount of vitamins B6, B12 and C. It has a quite a bit of sugar though, so enjoy in moderation.
