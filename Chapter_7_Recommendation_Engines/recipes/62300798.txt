Title:
Parmesan Crusted Baby Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb baby carrots
2 tsp butter or maragine
1/4 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Boil baby carrots until tender (approx. 15 minutes). Drain.
2. Melt butter or margarine in large pan. Add carrots and simmer for 1-2 minutes, stirring frequently.
3. Sprinkle parmesan cheese on top. Wait about 1/2 a minute before stirring. Simmer for about 2 minutes, stirring a couple more times.

Submitter: 
Becky Taylor
Description: 
A simple and delicous way to create a carrot side-dish.
