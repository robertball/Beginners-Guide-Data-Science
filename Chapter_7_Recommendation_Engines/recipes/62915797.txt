Title:
Shrimp Bisque and Grits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the Bisque:
1/2 stick unsalted butter
1/4 cup diced yellow onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
5 Tab. all purpose flour
3 cups reduced sodium chicken broth
2 cups heavy cream
1 cup corn
1 1/2 cup fresh or drained canned tomatoes
3 links andouille sausage, halved lengthwise and cut into 1/2 inch slices
1 Lb. medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
2 tea. crab boil seasoning
1/4 tea. salt
1/8 tea. pepper
For the Grits:
3/4 tea. salt (divided)
1 1/2 cup quick cooking grits
4 Tab. unsalted butter
1 cup grated parmesan
2 Tab. heavy cream
1/8 tea. pepper
chopped scallions for garnish

Directions:
To make Bisque: Melt butter in large saucepan over med. heat. Add oniion and cook til translucent, 3-4 min. Add garlic and cook til fragrant, about 2 min. Stir in flour and cook 1 min. Whisking constantly, gradually add the broth, then the cream. Add corn and tomato. (If using fresh, dont add til very end or they will fall apart)Cook over med. heat for 15 min. to meld flavors. Cook sausage, drain and add to bisque. Toss the shrimp in the crab boil seasoning, and add to bisque. Cook until shrimp are opaque throughout, about 8 min. Remove bisque from heat, cover, and set aside.
Grits:
Bring 5 cups water to boil in med. saucepan. Add 1/4 tea. of the salt. Stir in grits. Reduce heat to low and cook 5 min. Stir in butter, parmesan, cream, pepper, and remaining 1/2 tea. salt. cook for 5 min. to melt cheese.
To serve, spoon the grits into shallow bowls and top w/ bisque. Grnish w. scallions.
