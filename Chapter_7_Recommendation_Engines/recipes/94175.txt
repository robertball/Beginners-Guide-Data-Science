Title:
Cheese Fritters with Chutney Chili Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons butter or margarine
3 tablespoons flour
1 cup half-and-half
1/4 teaspoon ground red pepper
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 egg
1 tablespoon water
1 1/2 cups fine dry bread crumbs
oil
1 (15 ounce) can HORMEL® Chili No Beans
1 (4.25 ounce) can CHI-CHI'S® Diced Green Chilies, drained
1/3 cup PATAK'S® Sweet Mango Chutney

Directions:
In saucepan over low heat, melt butter. Add flour and cook 1 minute, stirring constantly. Gradually add half-and-half; cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until thick and bubbly. Remove from heat; stir in pepper and cheese until melted. Cool completely, until firm.
Combine egg and water, beating well. Set aside. Pour oil to a depth of 2 to 3 inches into a Dutch oven; heat to 375 degrees F. Roll 1 tablespoon cheese mixture in bread crumbs; dip in egg mixture and roll again in bread crumbs.
Fry fritters in hot oil, until golden brown; drain. In saucepan, combine chili, green chilies, and chutney. Cook over low heat until thoroughly heated. To serve, spoon chili onto serving plates; arrange fritters over chili.

Submitter: 
HORMEL® NATURAL CHOICE® Deli Sandwich Meats
Description: 
Balls of cheese are breaded and deep-fried, then served with a chili-chutney sauce.
