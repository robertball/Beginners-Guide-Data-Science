Title:
Hollywood Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bars German Chocolate (broken up)
12 oz pkg chocolate chips
1 pt marshmallow cream
4 1/2 cups sugar (superfine)
1 13 oz evap milk
1 stick butter
1 T vanilla
1-2 cups chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Butter 9x13 pan (I use this and 1 or 2 pie plates)
Combine chocolate bars, chips, and marshmallow cream in large bowl.
Combine next 3 ingredients in pan--boil at medium heat 8-10 min. Stir constantly.
Remove from heat and add vanilla--mix well.
Pour quickly over chocolate and marshmallow mixture.  Stir vigorously.  Melt all ingredients.
Add nuts.  Pour in pans.  Cool 24 hours.  Makes about 5 lbs.
