Title:
Zucchini Parmesan with Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
4 zucchini - peeled, sliced lengthwise, and sliced
1 whole shallot, very thinly sliced
2 teaspoons minced garlic
1 teaspoons dried oregano
1 teaspoons dried basil
1 1/2 cups spaghetti sauce
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat; saute shallots and garlic until fragrant and softened.  Add zucchini and saute until softened, 5 to 10 minutes. Season zucchini with oregano and basil. Add spaghetti sauce; cook and stir until heated through, about 5 minutes. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over zucchini mixture.
