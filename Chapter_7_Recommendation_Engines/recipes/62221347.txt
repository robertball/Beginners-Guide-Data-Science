Title:
Oriental Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag coleslaw mix
1 bunch green onions
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup vinegar
1 Tbsp. soy sauce
1/2 cup butter
1 pkg. ramen noodles (seasoning discarded)
1/2 cup sesame seeds
4 oz. slivered almonds

Directions:
In a small pan, melt butter and toast ramen noodles, almonds and sesame seeds until golden brown.
Whisk together sugar, vinegar, oil and soy sauce.
Chop green onions and toss with coleslaw mix.
Add ramen noodle mixture.
Just before serving add dressing and toss to coat.

Submitter: 
Jessica
Description: 
My mom would make this for every get together we had and it was always the hit of the party.
