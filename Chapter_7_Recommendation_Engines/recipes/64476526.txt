Title:
Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 TBLs butter
1 TBLs finely chopped onion
1/4 C shredded Cheddar cheese
salt and pepper to taste
5 carrots, chopped
1 C. peas
1 TBLs vegetable oil
1 onion, chopped
1/2 lbs lean ground Beef
1/2 lbs lean ground Lamb
2 TBLs tomato paste
3/4 C beef broth
1/4 C shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.  Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain and mash. Mix in butter and 1/4 cup shredded cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste; set aside.
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.  Add carrots and peas and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain and set aside. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C.)
Heat oil in a large frying pan. Add onion and cook until clear. Mix beef and lamb well and cook until browned. Add diced onion and cook until onions are translucent. Pour off excess fat, then stir in flour and cook 1 minute. Add tomato paste and beef broth. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
Spread the ground meat in an even layer on the bottom of a 2 quart casserole dish. Next, spread a layer of carrots and peas. Top with the mashed potato mixture and sprinkle with remaining shredded cheese.
Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Jack
Description: 
This easy shepherd's pie is a layered casserole of ground beef & lamb and veggies in a homemade gravy. It's topped with mashed potatoes and Cheddar cheese.Modified from: Shepherd's Pie VI
