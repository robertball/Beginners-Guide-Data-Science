Title:
Baked Pepperoni Pasta Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989964.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces rotini pasta
1 pound lean ground beef
1 small onion, diced
28 ounces Classico® Tomato and Basil Sauce
4 ounces sliced pepperoni sausage
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
In a medium skillet over medium-high heat, cook beef with onion until beef is brown.  Drain.  Combine beef mixture with sauce, pepperoni and cooked pasta and pour into a 9x13 inch baking dish.  Top with mozzarella.
Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, until cheese is melted and golden.
