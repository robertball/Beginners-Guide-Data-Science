Title:
Mince Meat Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup shortening
1 1/2 cup sugar
3 eggs
3 cups flour unsifted
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/3 cup None Such Mince Meat

Directions:
Cream shortening and sugar, add eggs, beat.
Combine dry ingredients and add to the above.  Mix.
Stir in the mince meat.
Drop by spoonfuls 2 inches apart on greased cookie sheet.
Bake at 375 degrees 8 to 10 minutes or until brown.
