Title:
Vanilla Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup sugar
2 Tbsp cornstarch
1/8 tsp salt
2 cups whole milk (or 2%)
2 egg yolks, slightly beaten
2 Tbsp butter, softened
2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Blend sugar, cornstarch and salt in 2 qt saucepan.  Combine milk and egg yolks; gradually stir into sugar mixture.
Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until mixture thickens and boils.  Boil and stir 1 minute.  Remove from heat; stir in butter and vanilla.  Pour into dessert dishes and cool.
