Title:
Slow Cooker Beef Stroganoff I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds cubed beef stew meat
4 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed GOLDEN mushroom soup
2 chopped onions
2 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
Salt
Pepper
Garlic
2 Beef Bullion Cubes
1 packet Onion Soup Mix
1 cup water
fresh mushrooms
8 ounces cream cheese
Sour Cream

Directions:
In a slow cooker, combine the meat, soup, onion, Worcestershire sauce, salt, pepper, garlic, bullion cube and water.
Cook on Low setting for 5 hours. Saute mushrooms and add about 30 minutes before serving with cream cheese. Stir in 2 dollups of sour cream just before serving.
