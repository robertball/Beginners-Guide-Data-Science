Title:
Blackberry vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz blackberry preserves or no sugar just fruit
1/2 cup red wine vinegar
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1.5 cups vegetable oil

Directions:
Blend all ingredients with immersion blender.  Refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Claudia
Description: 
This reminds me of a blackberry balsamic vinaigrette.  I like to use a brand of preserves that is a no sugar added, just fruit option.
