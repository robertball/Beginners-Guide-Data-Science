Title:
Roman Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans Albacore tuna, drained and flaked
1 can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped in large pieces
1 medium red pepper, diced
1 small can black olives, sliced in rings
1 medium red onion, chopped
1 bunch parsley, minced
4 or 5 basil leaves, rolled up tight and sliced  thin
1 garlic clove, minced
Mayonnaise
Juice of one lemon

Directions:
Put all the ingredients except the mayonnaise and lemon juice in a large bowl, toss gently to mix.
Add the  enough mayonnaise to lightly blend the salad  and add the juice of one lemon and toss again. You may prefer to add more mayonnaise after tasting.
Chill and serve with delicious fresh hard roll. Place tuna salad on the bed of Bib or Romaine  lettuce.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
The addition of artichokes, fresh basil and garlic creates a tuna salad fit for a feast. Has great eye appeal and flavor to top any tuna salad out there.
