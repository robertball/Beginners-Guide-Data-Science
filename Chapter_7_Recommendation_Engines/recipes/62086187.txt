Title:
Shirley's Oven Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless chicken breasts or 1 fryer, skin removed;
3 tablespoons vegetable or olive oil; 1/2 cup fine dry breadcrumbs; 2 tablespoons dry parsley flakes crushed; 1/2 teaspoon salt; 1/2 teaspoon paprika; 1/4 teaspoon onion or garlic powder; 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Brush chicken parts with oil.  Combine remaining ingredients in a medium bowl. Roll chicken pieces in crumb mixture, pressing crumbs evenly onto chicken. Arrange chicken pieces in ungreased baking pan. Bake at 350degrees until chicken is fork tender, about 1 hour.
