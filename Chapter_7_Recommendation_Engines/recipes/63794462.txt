Title:
Vegetarian Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoon EVOO
1 large onion diced
4 cloves garlic minced
4 stalks celery diced
3 large carrots diced
1/3 pound frozen cut green beans
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried basil
salt and pepper to taste
1 28 oz can diced tomatoes
1 14 oz can crushed tomatoes
6 cups vegetable broth
1 15 oz can kidney beans rinsed
1 15 oz can cannellini beans rinsed
1 cup elbow pasta or mini rigatoni
1/3 c parmesan cheese grated
2 Tablespoons Fresh basil (optional)

Directions:
Heat EVOO in large pot over Medium heat.
Add onion and cook until soft about 3 minutes.
Add Garlic and stir
Add celery and carrot cook til soft 5-6 minutes
Add green beans, dried spices cook for about 5 minutes
Add tomatoes and broth bring to a boil. If it looks too thick add a bit of water.
Reduce heat to low and simmer 20 minutes. Stir in beans and pasta cook until pasta is tender 7-10 minutes.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Serve into bowls and Garnish with Parmesan and Fresh Basil.

Submitter: 
Shannon
Description: 
A simple and hearty soup for those cold days. Serve with a Caeser Salad and Garlic toast and you have a simple, nutritious meal.
