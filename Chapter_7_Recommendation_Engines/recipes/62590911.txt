Title:
Blueberry Graham Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup graham cracker crumbs
1/4 cup chopped walnuts
2 tbsp. sugar
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
2 tbsp. butter - melted
1 - 3oz. pkg. cream cheese - softened
1/2 cup ricotta cheese
1/3 cup confectioner's sugar
2 tsp. lemon juice
4 cups fresh blueberries
Whipped cream - optional

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine cracker crumbs, walnuts, sugar and cinnamon.
Stir in melted butter and set aside.
In mixing bowl, combine cream cheese and confectioner's sugar until smooth.
Add ricotta cheese and lemon juice and mix well.
Place 1/2 of blueberries on bottom of serving bowl, top with cream cheese mixture, crumbs and remaining bluerries.
Garnish with whipped cream if desired.
Refrigerate until serving.
