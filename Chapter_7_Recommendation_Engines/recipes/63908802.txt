Title:
Ziti and bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ziti or other pasta
10 slices bacon
1 large yellow onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 tsp crushed chili pepper
1/2 tsp basil
1/4 tsp pepper
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook bacon in frying pan until crisp. Add onion until tender. Add garlic and cook one minute longer.
Add pepper, basil, simmer uncovered for 2-3 minutes.
Toss pasta with ingredients and sprinkle with cheese.
