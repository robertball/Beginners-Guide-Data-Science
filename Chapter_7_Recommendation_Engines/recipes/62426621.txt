Title:
Easy Breezy Cream Cheese Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/448125.jpg
Ingredients:
8oz cream cheese
1/2 cup confectioner's sugar
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
In a medium mixing bowl, soften cream cheese in microwave for about 10 seconds or until slighly soft.
Add confectioner's sugar, vanilla and cinnamon to cream cheese.
Beat with a hand mixer for 30 seconds to a minute, until ingredients are fully blended and mixture is smooth.
Use as desired!

Submitter: 
sparrow
Description: 
This sweet cream cheese topping is so versatile, the possibilities when making or using this really are endless. Great on crepes, french toast, pumpkin pie, for dipping fruit, and so much more!
