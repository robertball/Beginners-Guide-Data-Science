Title:
Orange Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/821296.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
2 teaspoons grated orange peel
18 thin slices white bread

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine butter, sugar and orange peel; mix well.  Spread on both sides of bread; cut in half diagonally.  Place on ungreased baking sheets.  Bake at 350 degrees F for 8-10 minutes.  Turn slices over; bake 7 minutes longer (watch carefully).

Submitter: 
Helen  Applequist
Description: 
'A friend gave me the recipe for this sweet snack,' informs Helen Applequist of St. Louis Park, Minnesota. 'I make it with thinly sliced bread from Pepperidge Farm. It's lovely with tea.'
