Title:
Asparagus Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. butter
1-1/2 small onion, diced
1 T. tomato paste
1 c. water
1 t. salt
1/2 t. pepper
1lb. asparagus, cut into 1" pieces
4 c. chicken (or vegetable) broth or boullion
3/4 c. abborrio rice or acini de pepe (which I prefer)
grated cheese, to garnish

Directions:
Saute onion in butter. Dissolve tomato paste in 1 c. water and add to
onion, with salt and pepper. Add asparagus, and simmer until
crisp/tender
(about 3-4 minutes). Set aside. Bring broth to a boil, add rice or
pasta,
and simmer until al dente (15 min or less). Add asparagus mixture to
broth.
Serve with garnish of grated cheese if desired.
