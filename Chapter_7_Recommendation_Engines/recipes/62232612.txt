Ingredients:
3/4 Cup orange juice
3/4 Cup white sugar
1 Cinnamon Stick
1 orange thinly sliced
1 lemon thinly sliced
10 whole cloves
1 750mL bottle of wine (Red/White or Fruit)
1 bay leaf
1 star anise
2 cardamom pods
1 pinch of allspice
1 pinch of mace

Directions:
Simmer orange juice
Add sugar and cinnamon bring to a boil
If in a large pot add all the other ingredients and heat gently, do not boil
If using a crock-pot put all ingredients in and add the spiced sugar juice to it put on low
Let simmer several hours
Serve warm, you may want to strain it as you pour it into cups
can be made in a double or triple batch
