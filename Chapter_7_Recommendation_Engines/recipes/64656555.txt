Title:
Dave's instant pot Lentil soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12oz boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 tbsp chicken better than bouillon
3 bouillon cubes
1 tbsp chopped cilantro
1 small onion, diced
1 tbsp minced garlic
1 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp Oregano
1 tomato, diced
1/4 tsp Tajin
1 tbsp cayenne pepper
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp salt
7 cups water
1 tbsp baking soda (optional)

Directions:
Soak lentils overnight with at least 8 cups of water mixed with 1 tbsp of baking soda if desired. This can help to reduce gas.
Add all ingredients to instant pot. Cook on soup setting for 30 minutes and quick release pressure. remove chicken and shred, then return to pot and serve.  Chicken should come apart easily when shredded.

Submitter: 
Dave Lehmer
Description: 
This is a lentil soup that I make regularly. It's spicy and very filling.
