Title:
Goofballz Dinomite Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2465580.jpg
Ingredients:
Butter, marshmallows, rice crispy cereal, peanut butter, chocolate chips

Directions:
1. Melt 3 tbls of butter in large saucepan over low heat.
2. Add 10 oz of marshmallows (1 small bag or about 40 marshmallows.
3. Stir until marshmallows and butter are combined and smooth.
4. Pour in three cups of rice crispy cereal and stir until well distributed.
5. With a buttered spoon, make heaping piles of mixture on wax paper and smooth into dino head shapes.
6. Let dino heads cool and spread peanut butter over mixture (be sure to do inside of mouth and back of head and neck).
7. Use chocolate chips to make teeth, eyes, and spikes along the back.
8. Chill and serve!
