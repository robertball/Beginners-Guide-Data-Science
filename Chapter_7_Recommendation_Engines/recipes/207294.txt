Title:
Kahlua Frozen Mudslide Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce Kahlua
1 fluid ounce Kahlua Coffee Cream
1 fluid ounce vodka
1 large scoop vanilla ice cream
Ice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients together and blend.

Submitter: 
Kahlua
Description: 
Here's an icy-cold, sweet cocktail made from Kahlua, vodka, and a big scoop of vanilla ice cream.
