Title:
Avo-Berry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6864534.jpg
Ingredients:
2 scoops Emergen-C® Protein Fuel and Super Foods Mixed Berry
1 cup frozen blueberries
1/2 cup orange juice
1/2 ripe avocado, peeled and pitted

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender. Blend until smooth. Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Emergen-C®
Description: 
Avocado is the secret behind this creamy, dairy-free smoothie. Emergen-C® Protein Fuel & Superfoods Mixed Berry, blueberries, and orange juice round out this flavorful snack.
