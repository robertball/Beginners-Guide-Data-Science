Title:
Ultimate Party Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can Ocean Spray® Jellied Cranberry Sauce
1 (12 ounce) bottle Heinz® Chili Sauce
1 (2 pound) bag frozen, pre-cooked, cocktail-size meatballs NOT ITALIAN
ADD A LITTLE LEMON JUICE AND SUGAR

Directions:
Combine sauces in a large saucepan.  Cook over medium-low heat, stirring until smooth.  Add meatballs.  Cover and cook for 15 minutes or until meatballs are heated through, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Arlyne Zager Sackstein Patters
Description: 
A classic party appetizer, these easy meatballs in cranberry-chili sauce are ready in minutes to serve your guests.Modified from: Ultimate Party Meatballs
