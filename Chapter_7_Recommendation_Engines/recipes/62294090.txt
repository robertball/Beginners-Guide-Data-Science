Title:
Parmesan Crusted Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ea 5-6 oz Tilapia Fillets
Non-stick Cooking Spray
Perel Basil Sundried Tomato Olive oil
Parmesan Crust:
½ cup Panko Bread Crumbs* Parmesan Herb Style
½ cup Parmesan Cheese, pre-shredded
1 tsp Land O Lakes Parmesan & herb seasoning
Parmesan Base:
½ cup Parmesan Cheese, pre-shredded
1 Tbsp. Parsley, dried or fresh
3/4 cup Refrigerated Alfredo Sauce
1 tsp Land O Lakes Parmesan & herb seasoning

Directions:
Preparation:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Spray Non-stick Cooking Spray onto a wire rack that will raise your fish above a baking pan.
In a small bowl, mix the Panko bread crumbs and ½ cup Parmesan cheese and the 1 tsp of Land O Lakes seasoning  to make the crust for the topping.
To assemble the Parmesan Base, place ½ cup Parmesan cheese, 1 Tbsp. parsley and the prepared Alfredo sauce, and 1 tsp of Land O Lakes seasoning in a medium bowl and stir well.
Coat fish with Perel Olive oil, Coat bottom and sides of fish with Parmesan crust mixture place on the oiled rack, lightly glaze top with the Parmesan base, Evenly sprinkle the Parmesan Crust on top of the fish. Bake on the top shelf of the oven for 15-20 minutes or until fish reaches 140-150 degrees and the crust is golden brown.
