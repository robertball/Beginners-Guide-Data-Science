Title:
Quick Mediterranean Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (6 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, chopped
salt and pepper to taste
Greek seasoning to taste
3 Idaho potatoes, washed and chopped
1 (10 ounce) can black olives, drained
1/2 c chopped feta, plus more for garnish
3 Tbsp Lemon Juice
1/2-1 cup Greek-style vinaigrette with feta

Directions:
Place the frozen chicken, potatoes, 1/2 c feta and olives in a 2 quart microwave-safe dish, and season with salt, pepper, and Greek seasoning. Stir in lemon juice. Cover dish tightly with plastic wrap.  Cook 8 minutes or so on high until the chicken is fully cooked.  Add the vinaigrette to taste.  Stir to coat, sprinkle with additional feta, and serve.

Submitter: 
ChefMom
Description:
