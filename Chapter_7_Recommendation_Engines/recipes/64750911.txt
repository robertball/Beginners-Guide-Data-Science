Title:
Keto Buffalo Chicken Tenders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb chicken tenders
1 egg
1 cup almond flour
Buffalo sauce
1 TBSP heavy whipping cream
Salt/pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In a bowl, whisk an egg with the heavy whipping cream.
In a separate bowl, combine salt, pepper, and almond flour.
Dip the chicken tenders into the egg then into the almond flour. Coat all over with a fork.
Grease a baking sheet and place the tenders on it.
Bake for 25-30 minutes.
Broil for 1-2 minutes on high to get them crispier.
Toss with buffalo sauce and enjoy
