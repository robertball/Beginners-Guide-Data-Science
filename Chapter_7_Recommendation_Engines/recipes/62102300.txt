Title:
dark chocolate coffee bean fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme
* 1 1/2 cups white sugar
* 2/3 cup evaporated milk
* 1/4 cup butter
* 1/4 teaspoon salt
* 2 cups dark chocolate chips
* 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
* 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
* 1/2 cup whole coffee beans

Directions:
1. Line an 9x13 inch pan with aluminum foil. Set aside.
2. In a large saucepan over medium heat, combine marshmallow cream, sugar, evaporated milk, butter and salt. Bring to a full boil, and cook for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
3. Remove from heat and pour in semisweet chocolate chips and dark chocolate chips. Stir until chocolate is melted and mixture is smooth.
4. Stir in coffee beans and vanilla. Pour into prepared pan. Chill in refrigerator for 2 hours, or until firm.
