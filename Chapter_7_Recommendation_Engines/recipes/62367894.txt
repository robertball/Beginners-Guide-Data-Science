Title:
Tri-Color Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/409003.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12oz) Tri-Color Pasta
1 (8oz) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
1 (2.25oz) can of sliced black olives, drained
5oz (half block)extra sharp cheddar cheese, diced

Directions:
In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta according to directions, rinse under cold water and drain. Once pasta is cool, add olives, cheese, and dressing. Mix will, cover and refrigerate for 2-4 hours. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Krissy16
Description: 
Tri-color pasta, olives & sharp cheddar cheese tossed with a zesty dressing make this easy summer salad a great side dish at any BBQ!
