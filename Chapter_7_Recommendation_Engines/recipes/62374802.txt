Title:
Asian Fusion Vermicelli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/413658.jpg
Ingredients:
CHICKEN
2-3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (depending on size), in thin strips
sweet thai chili sauce
garlic seasoning, salt and pepper to taste
SALAD
1 carrot, peeled and julienned
1 red pepper, julienned
3 green onions, diced
1/2 a long english cucumber, julienned
1 large red chili pepper, seeded and diced
125g rice vermicelli noodles
70g mixed greens (1/2 regular box)
DRESSING
3 cloves fresh garlic, crushed
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
3 tbsp pure sesame oil
2 tbsp lime juice (or juice of two medium limes)
2 tbsp rice vinegar
2 tbsp fish sauce
2 tbsp low sodium soy sauce
GARNISH
sriracha sauce
sesame seeds

Directions:
Brown chicken in thai chili sauce on medium heat. Season to taste with garlic seasoning, salt and pepper.
While chicken is cooking, add vermicelli to boiling water.  Remove from heat and let soak 4 minutes. Drain.
Mix dressing ingredients in small bowl.
Add salad ingredients (except lettuce) to a large bowl and toss with dressing.
Plate the mixed greens and top with dressed salad. Layer on chili chicken and sesame seeds.
Serve with sriracha sauce for those who like things extra spicy.
