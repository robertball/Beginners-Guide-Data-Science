Title:
Salmon Zucchini Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/287611.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small can (100 grams) salmon (skin, cartilage removed)
1 T. mayonnaise
1 cup shredded zucchini
1/2 to 1 cup crushed, seasoned crackers
1 egg
olive oil or olive oil spray

Directions:
Combine ingredients, mixing well.
Shape into patties.
Fry in small amount of olive oil on non-stick pan until golden crust forms.

Submitter: 
Hezzy_tant_Cook
Description: 
These patties are light with a crisp outer crust. You can feel good about the veggie combo goodness. This is nice served with cold edamame (soybeans), and canned yams, heated through. Quick summer meal.
