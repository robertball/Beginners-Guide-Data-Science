Title:
Italian Style Turkey Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon Italian-style seasoning
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
3/4 teaspoon crushed anise seeds
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon dried minced onion
2 pounds ground turkey
2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the ground black pepper, parsley, Italian-style seasoning, garlic powder, crushed red pepper flakes, anise, paprika, minced onion flakes and salt; mix well.
Place ground turkey in a separate large bowl and add the spice mix to it. Mix this thoroughly with your hands. set in fridge for two days.
In a large skillet over medium high heat, saute the seasoned turkey for 10 minutes, or until well browned and crumbly.
