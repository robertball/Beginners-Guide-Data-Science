Title:
Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c sugar
1/4 tsp salt
3 squares chocolate
1 c milk
2 tbsp light corn syrup
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 c peanut butter
1 c peanuts

Directions:
Combine sugar, salt, chocolate, milk and corn syrup in 3 quart heavy saucepan.  Boil over medium heat stirring until sugar dissolves.  Cook until soft ball stage (234*F) on candy thermometer.
Remove from heat.
Do NOT stir.
Let cool until the bottom of the pan feels lukewarm to touch (110*F).
Add vanilla and peanut butter and beat with wooden spoon until candy begins to lose its shine.
Add nuts and pour quickly into buttered 8" square pan.
Cover & chill till firm.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
A melt-in-your mouth confection and a favorite for those who love Reece's Pieces. I made this every fall and winter, my father-in-law's and husband's favorite homemade candy.
