Title:
Veggie Cream Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 ounces softened cream cheese (can be low or no fat)
1/4 cup finely chopped walnuts
2 small sweet bell peppers, finely diced (or about 1/4 c finely diced bell pepper)
2 tablespoons sour cream
1/2 t garlic powder
1 T dried minced onion
Dash hot sauce

Directions:
Mix together all ingredients and let sit about 1 hour before spreading on crackers, bagels or stuffing in celery.

Submitter: 
It's A New Day
Description: 
I used to love garden vegetable cream cheese and tried making it at home.  This is pretty close.  Great on bagels, crackers or stuffed in celery.Modified from: Savory Stuffed Celery Bites
