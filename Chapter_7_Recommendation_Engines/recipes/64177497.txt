Title:
Salad Dressing for  Mexican Chopped Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons water for cooking the corn starch
1 tsp corn starch or arrowroot
¼ cup fresh lime juice (1 large lime)
2 tablespoons honey
½ teaspoon cumin
1 clove garlic
½ teaspoon salt
freshly ground black pepper
½ cup finely chopped cilantro
taste and add salt if needed

Directions:
Mix starch into cold water, put in pan and cook until clear and thick (or use microwave). Put all in food processor or blender and mix until garlic is well chopped and incorporated.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
Yummy no fat dressing, with just the right hint of spice. So good, who needs store bought?
