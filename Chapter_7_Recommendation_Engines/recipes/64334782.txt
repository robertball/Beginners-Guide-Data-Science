Title:
Jennie's Heavenly Slow Cooker Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
1 (1.34 ounce) onion dip mix
2 (6.5 ounce) cans mushroom stems & pieces
1 (8 ounce) container cream cheese
2 1/2 c chicken broth
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
2 Tlbs chives

Directions:
Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat, and stir in the salad dressing mix, mushrooms, cream cheese, and chicken broth until the sauce mixture is hot, smooth and well combined. Place the chicken breasts into the bottom of a slow cooker, and pour the sauce mixture over the chicken. Cover and cook on Low setting until chicken is tender, about 4 hours.

Submitter: 
ID Sparks
Description: 
Chicken breasts cook gently in a creamy sauce of cream cheese, golden mushroom soup, Italian dressing mix, and white wine in this easy slow cooker recipe.Modified from: Jennie's Heavenly Slow Cooker Chicken
