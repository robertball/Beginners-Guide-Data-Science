Title:
Italian Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Italian sausage (half regular, half spicy)
1 onion, finely diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup sliced carrots
1 potato, diced
2 (14 ounce) cans beef broth or stock
1 can Italian-style stewed tomatoes, diced
1 (14.5 ounce) can great Northern beans, undrained
2 small zucchini, cubed
2 cups spinach - packed, rinsed and torn
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt
worcestershire and red wine vinegar to taste

Directions:
In a stockpot brown sausage with onion and garlic.  Add carrots and potatoes and cook 3 minutes.  Add  broth,tomatoes and zucchini.  Season with salt and pepper, etc. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 15 minutes.  Stir in beans with liquid and spinach. Cover, and simmer until done, about 5 minutes.
