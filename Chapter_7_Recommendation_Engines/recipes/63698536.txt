Title:
Tracy Ann's Pineapple Upside-Down Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1028383.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Duncan Hines "Butter Recipe" Golden Cake Mix
1/2 cup (1 stick) butter
1 1/2 cups  packed brown sugar
1 can (20 oz.) pineapple slices (drained)
Maraschino cherry halves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Melt butter in a 13 x 9 pan. Sprinkle brown sugar evenly in pan. Arrange pineapple slices on sugar. Put cherry halves, round side down, in the center of each slice. Evenly spread coconut  next.
Prepare cake as directed on package. Pour batter over fruit and spread evenly. Bake for 45 - 55 minutes, until tests done with a toothpick. Let stand 5 minutes to set topping. Loosen cake from pan and turn upside down on a platter.

Submitter: 
Tracy Ann
Description: 
This is a quick, easy way to make a wonderful old-fashioned cake. The recipe was given to me by a lady I used to work with and I fixed it for many of our pitch-in dinners.
