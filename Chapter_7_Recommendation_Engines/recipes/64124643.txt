Title:
Reese's Peanut Butter Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c butter, melted
2 c graham cracker crumbs
1 3/4 c powdered sugar
1/4 c brown sugar
1 c peanut butter
1/2 tsp vanilla
1 (11 oz) bag milk chocolate chips

Directions:
In large bowl, combine all ingredients except chocolate chips. Stir until smooth and creamy. Pour mixture into a 9x13 inch pan.
Melt chocolate chips and spread over peanut butter mixture.
Chill of at least one hour. Let stand at room temperature 10 minutes before cutting,
