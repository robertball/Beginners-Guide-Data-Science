Title:
**Secret** Ingredient Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. lean ground beef
3 tbsp olive oil, divided
1 med. onion, diced
1 lg. green pepper, chopped
1 sm. red pepper, chopped
14 lg. mushrooms, thick sliced
1 sm. zuchini, chopped
1 18oz can diced tomatoes, with juice
1 lg. can red kidney beans, drained but not rinsed
1 can lentils, drained and rinsed
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 can baked beans in tomato sauce, with sauce
1 6oz can tomato paste
1/3 C chili sauce
3 tbsp balsamic vinegar ** (secret ingredient) **
3/4 C water
1/4 C loose packed brown sugar
1 tbsp beef bouillion powder
4 tbsp chili powder
2 tbsp tex-mex seasoning
1 pkg. taco seasoning
2 tsp cilantro paste, or 1 tbsp fresh cilantro chopped (optional)

Directions:
Heat 1 tbsp oil in lg. frying pan.
Add beef, cooking until well browned and broken up.
Remove from heat.
In lg. heavy bottomed dutch oven pot, heat remaining 2 tbsp oil on med. high heat.
Add onion, peppers, mushroom and zuchini. Saute, stirring, for 7-8 minutes until veggies start to soften. Add diced tomatoes. Continue cooking for another 7-8 mins, stirring, until mixture starts to boil. Reduce heat to medium.
Add all the beans and stir to blend in.
Add cooked ground beef and remaining ingredients.
Stir to blend well. Bring to a soft boil.
Reduce heat to low, cover and cook for 1 1/2 hours. Stir from time to time, checking that chili is not sticking to the bottom of the pot.
Serve with hot cooked rice.
Awesome the next day. Serve in bowls, with grated cheese layered on top and taco chips for dipping. :)
