Title:
Tartar Sauce III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/13992.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup sweet pickle relish
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium bowl combine the mayonnaise, onion, relish and season with salt and pepper to taste. Mix well and let stand for at least 10 minutes. Store in a closed container in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
LPOLCIN
Description: 
Quick and easy! You can make a fresh version of this classic condiment that tastes better than what you would buy in the store. Serve with your favorite fish.
