Title:
Frito Corn Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/733839.jpg
Ingredients:
2 -16 ounces whole kernel corn
1 red peppers
1 yellow peppers
1 green peppers
1 cup green onions
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 cup cheese
9 3/4 ounces Fritos chili cheese corn chips

Directions:
Drain canned corn. Dice peppers and green onion. Put in a large bowl and add mayonnaise , you could increase this to 1 cup and cheese. Just before serving add the Fritos.
