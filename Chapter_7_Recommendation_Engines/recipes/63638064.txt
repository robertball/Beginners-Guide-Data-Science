Title:
Strawberry Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1014333.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
2 cups all purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 cup water
2 1/2 tablespoons strawberry preserves
1 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
12 large strawberries (optional)
Your favorite frosting!

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease 12 muffin cups or line with paper baking cups.
In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, and baking soda. Then, make a well in the center and pour in the water, strawberry preserves, mayonnaise, and the vanilla extract. Mix until just blended.
Place cupcake batter in prepared muffin tin and push one large strawberry in the center (optional). Bake cupcakes for 20 to 25 minutes or until tops spring up when pressed.
