Title:
Grandma Opal's Spiced Pecans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. pecans
1 egg white
1 TBSP water
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
dash salt

Directions:
Beat egg white and water until frothy.  Mix in pecans until all are coated.  Mix other ingredients and stir in pecans until all are coated.  Bake on cookie sheet at 225 degrees for 45 minutes.  Stir every 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
My grandma used to make these and I loved them!  The house would smell like cinnamon and nutmeg when they were cooking and I used to eat them hot right out of the oven.  You'll love these...so simple and good!
