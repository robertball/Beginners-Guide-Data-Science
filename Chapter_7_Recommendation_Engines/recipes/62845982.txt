Title:
Simple Salmon Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (5 oz) cans boneless, skinless salmon, drained
1 egg, beaten
2 tablespoons finely chopped yellow onion
3/4 cup finely crushed soda crackers
Pinch of salt and pepper
1/2 cup vegetable oil, for frying

Directions:
Over medium heat, heat oil in skillet.  Mix together salmon, egg, chopped onion, and 1/4 cup cracker crumbs.  Form into patties and dust with additional cracker crumbs and salt and pepper.  Fry until golden brown, or about 2 minutes on each side.
