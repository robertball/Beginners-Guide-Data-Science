Title:
Avocado and Mango Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1/4 cup water
1/3 cup lime juice
1/2 cup chili garlic sauce
2 medium mangoes - peeled, seeded and diced
2 avocados - peeled, pitted and diced
1 (10 ounce) package spring lettuce mix

Directions:
In a saucepan over medium-high heat, stir together the brown sugar and water. Bring to a boil, then pour into a medium bowl. Stir in the garlic chili sauce and lime juice. Set the dressing aside.
In a large bowl, toss together the mangoes and avocados. Arrange the spring salad mix on serving plates, then top with dressing.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
This is a colorful and very tasty mix of mangoes and avocados in a spicy lime dressing.Modified from: Chicken, Avocado and Mango Salad
