Title:
Irresistible Granola Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/344716.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup diced walnuts
1/3 cup diced or sliced almonds
1/3 cup sliced pecans
1/3 cup lightly packed brown sugar
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
2 cup oats
1/2 cup honey

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients then mix in the wet, will be crumbly when mixed.
Grease 11x13 pan. Press dough firmly in pan with knuckles (it hurts a little because of the nuts).
Bake 325F for 20-23 minutes or until edges are browned.
Let cool completely. Loosen edges and cut into ten bars.
Take them out of pan and put into a airtight container.

Submitter: 
Nicole
Description: 
Yummy granola bars that you will bake a again and again. Simple, fast, filling, healthy, and they taste way better than any granola bar your going to buy at the store.
