Title:
German Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
3 eggs
1/2 cup oil
5 cups sliced apples
1 tsp baking soda
2 tsp cinnamon
2 cups sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup walnts

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients
Mix wet ingredients
Mix both together
Fold in apples & walnuts
Use 13x9x2 pan
Bake 1 hr (glass pan at 325 - metal pan 350)

A chocolate, apple & walnut scratch cake.
