Title:
Layered Chicken Enchilada Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979509.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 medium onion, chopped
2 1/2 cups cooked, shredded chicken breast meat
1 (7 ounce) can ORTEGA速 Diced Green Chiles
1 (1.25 ounce) package ORTEGA速 Taco Seasoning mix
8 corn tortillas
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
1 (16 ounce) jar ORTEGA速 Salsa Prima Homestyle Mild

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 350 degrees F. Grease 13x9x2-inch baking dish.
HEAT oil in large skillet. Cook onion, stirring occasionally, until tender. Remove from heat. Stir in chicken, chiles and seasoning mix.
LAYER half of tortillas in prepared baking dish. Top with chicken mixture, beans and 1 cup cheese. Layer with remaining tortillas. Top with salsa and remaining cheese.
BAKE for 30 to 35 minutes or until heated through and cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Ortega速
Description: 
Layers of mouth-watering tortillas, tender bits of shredded chicken and kidney beans make a healthy and satisfying dish, especially when served with a crunchy green salad and tall glasses of chilled Libby Kerns Nectar.
