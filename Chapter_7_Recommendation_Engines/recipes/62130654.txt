Title:
Basic Marinade for Beef and Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Basic Marinade
INGREDIENTS:
1/4 c Red wine vinegar
1/4 t Onion salt
2 T Vegetable oil
1/4 t Garlic powder
2 T Soy sauce
Freshly ground black pepper
2 T Ketchup

Directions:
DIRECTIONS:
Combine and use with beef, pork, or chicken. Marinate for 24 - 48 hours,
in fridge.
Dispose of leftover marinade.
Bake or grill meat.

Submitter: 
Andrea F
Description: 
This marinade is delicious used on 2 Top Sirloin Steak. Grill Steak to medium rare and cut against the grain. DELICIOUS!!!!
