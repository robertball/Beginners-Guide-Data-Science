Title:
Biscuit Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984370.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package refrigerated buttermilk biscuits
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon onion powder

Directions:
Cut each biscuit into thirds; place on a greased baking sheet. Combine Parmesan cheese and onion powder; sprinkle over biscuits. Bake at 400 degrees F for 7-8 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Joy  Beck
Description: 
Biscuit Bites couldn't be easier. Convenient refrigerated biscuits are simply sprinkled with Parmesan cheese and onion powder, then baked. 'These savory mini biscuits are wonderful with soup, a main dish or even as a snack,' writes Joy beck of Cincinnati, Ohio. 'We like to munch on them instead of popcorn while watching television.'
