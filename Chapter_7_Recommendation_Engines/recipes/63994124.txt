Title:
Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 pint heavy cream
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp garlic, or to taste
1/8 tsp white pepper to taste
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 (16 ounce) package dry fettuccine pasta
1/8 tsp nutmeg

Directions:
Place pasta in a large pot of lightly salted boiling water. Cook until tender, about 8 minutes. Drain.
Combine the butter, salt, white pepper, garlic, and 1-1/2 cup heavy cream in a saute pan. Bring to a low boil over medium-low heat. Whisk in Parmesan cheese slowly. Reduce, and add remaining heavy cream to thin. Reduce, stirring, until thickened slightly. Toss in 12 ounces of pasta. sprinkle nutmeg over top of pasta.
