Title:
Big Ray's Savory Onion Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2993728.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup all-purpose flour, divided
3-4 lb skinless chicken thighs
2 Tbl olive oil
1 envelope onion soup mix
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 bottle (12 ounces) beer

Directions:
Place 2 tablespoons flour in a large resealable plastic bag. Add chicken, a few pieces at a time, and shake to coat. In a large skillet, brown chicken in oil on all sides. Remove and keep warm.
Add soup mix, garlic and remaining flour, stirring to loosen browned bits from pan. Gradually whisk in beer. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.
