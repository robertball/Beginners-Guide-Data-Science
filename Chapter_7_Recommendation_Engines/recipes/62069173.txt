Title:
Spicy Skillet Sausage & Potatos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 hot Italian Sausage links
8-10 small red potatoes, cubed
1 yellow onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
2 Tbs. olive oil
1 Tbs. parsley
2 tsp. garlic pepper
2 tsp. salt
2 tsp. paprika
2 tsp. oregano

Directions:
Heat olive oil in electic skillet. Add cubed potatoes.  Season with salt, garlic pepper, parsley, paprika & oregano.  Cut sausage links into bite size pieces.  Add to skillet along with chopped onion & bell pepper. Cover and cook at 250 until sausage is done and potatoes are tender.

Submitter: 
Amy
Description: 
This is a quick and easy dinner to throw together in the evening.  
We like our food with alot of flavor in our house, this is one of the favorites.  Serve this in a bowl with a nice dinner salad on the side...couldn't be easier!
