Title:
BUSH'S® Spicy Mandarin Chicken and Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981330.jpg
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 teaspoon Chinese 5-spice
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 (28 ounce) can BUSH'S® Bold & Spicy Baked Beans
1 (11 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained
1/2 cup thinly sliced celery

Directions:
Sprinkle Chinese 5-spice evenly over both sides of chicken breasts. In skillet, brown chicken on both sides in hot oil; drain. Combine remaining ingredients. Pour over chicken. Bring mixture to a boil. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes or until chicken is done.

Submitter: 
BUSH'S® Beans
Description: 
Bold and Spicy Baked Beans mixed with the mandarin oranges and Chinese 5-spice give this chicken dish a zesty kick.
