Title:
Mom's Awesome Sausage Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Pizza Dough
1 lb Italian Sweet Sausage
1 lb Italian Hot Sausage
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzerella cheese
black pepper (to taste)
garlic powder

Directions:
Fry the sausage in frying pan until cooked through. Set aside to cool.
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
Flour the counter a little and your hand and rolling pin.
Roll out the dough, try to make a circle (as best as you can).
Sprinkle the meat on the dough leaving a 1" edge.
Sprinkle lightly with black pepper and garlic pepper.
Sprinkle the mozzarella over all.
Roll up into a log & shape into a horseshoe.
Place on baking pan lined with foil, seam side down.
Pinch the ends tight to seal.
Bake for 20 mins til a nice golden brown color.
