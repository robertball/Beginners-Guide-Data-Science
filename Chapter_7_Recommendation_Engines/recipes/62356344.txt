Title:
Absolutely Boston Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Absolutely Almond Pound Cake
2 c. water, divided
1 c. melted butter, divided
3 oz. instant vanilla pudding
Sweet and Simple fudge mix
3 c. milk divided

Directions:
Prepare cake with 1 c. water and 1 stick butter.  Pour mixture into round cake pan and bake as directed.  Cool.  Cut cake in half horizontally.  Prepare instant pudding as directed with 2 cups milk.  After pudding has set, spread between layers of pound cake.  (You will have pudding left over).  Prepare fudge mix with 1 stick butter and 1 cup milk.  All mixture to cool slowly before drizzling over the top of the cake.  Cool Completely.
