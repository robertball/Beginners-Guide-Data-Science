Title:
Balsamic Marinade for Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/8 cup balsamic vinegar
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
3 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 small sprig fresh rosemary, or 1/8 teaspoon dried ground rosemary

Directions:
Whisk all ingredients together until well blended.
Marinate chicken for at least 1 and up to 8 hour.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
strong flavors with no fat!  Great marinade and doesn't take long to impart a lot of flavor.
