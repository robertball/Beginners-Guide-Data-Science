Title:
Chicken and Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 4-5# chicken or chicken parts
2 Qts. Water
1 Bay Leaf
4 Cloves Garlic (peeled and left whole)
1 Clove (whole)
1 Stick Butter
3 Carrots(1/2" slice)
3 Celery Stalks(1/2" slice)
1 Lg Onion (1/2" dice)
2 tsp. Salt
1/2 Tsp. White Pepper
1/2 Tsp. Red Pepper(cayenne)
1/2 Tsp. Black Pepper
1 Tsp. Paprika
1 Tsp. Herbs de Provence(dried)
1 Tsp. Tarragon(dried)
1 Tsp. Rosemary(dried)
1/2 C. Flour
1/2 Milk or Cream
1 Pkg. Buttermilk Bisciuts(8 count)

Directions:
Put chicken in a large dutch oven(6-9 qt.) Add water, garlic,clove and bay leaf. Bring to simmer and cook for 30 min. Remove chicken and save broth.
Saute carrot,celery, and onion in butter in same pot that you cooked chicken. Add Herb,spices and flour: cook for a couple of min. Add 1/2 cup broth and stir. Now add rest of broth and milk and cook until broth thickens.Add diced chicken and put bisciuts on top or broth mixture and bake at 400F. for 15min. Cover and bake for an additional 10min.
