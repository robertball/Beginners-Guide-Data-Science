Title:
Simply White Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 1/2 cups sugar
3 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup milk
1/2 cup margarine
1 tsp. vanilla or 1/2 tsp. almond extract
5 egg whites
(can add 1 cup coconut for a fun alternative)

Directions:
Blend all but the vanilla and egg whites.
Beat to blend and then beat 2 minutes on medium.
Add vanilla and egg whites.
Beat 2 minutes.
Bake @ 350 x 25 - 30 minutes in 9" x 13" pan or less time for cupcakes, till test done with toothpick.
Bake approx. 15 min. for cupcakes.
