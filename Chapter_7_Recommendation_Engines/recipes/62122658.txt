Title:
Pumpkin Pie Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 32 oz large can Pumpkin Pie Mix
1 can evaporated milk
2 eggs
1 box yellow cake mix
1 block butter
1 cup  chopped walnuts
butter/flour for pan

Directions:
Mix pumpkin, milk and eggs with beaters.  Pour mixture into buttered/floured 9 x 13 pan.  Sprinkle with dry yellow cake mix.  Top cake mix with cube of sliced butter.  Finish with chopped walnuts. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes

Submitter: 
Lisa
Description: 
The creaminess of pumpkin pie without the crust topped with walnuts.
