Title:
Honey Walnut Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup toasted walnuts
1 Tbsp. oil
2 Tbsp. Honey
Pinch of salt

Directions:
Put walnuts in a food processor and start grinding them.
When they get into small uniform pieces, add the oil, salt and honey.
Continue to grind it until it makes a paste.

Submitter: 
Jessica
Description: 
I made this recipe up when I was trying to find something other than peanut butter...what I got was a delicious slightly sweet homemade walnut butter perfect for toast in the morning.
