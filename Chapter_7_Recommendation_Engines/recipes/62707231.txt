Title:
Apple Glazed Chicken with Butter Pecan Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1	 bag	Success® White Rice
2	 tsps	 unsalted butter, divided
4		 boneless skinless chicken breasts (about 1 lb.)
1/2	 Tbsp	 salt-free seasoning
1/2	 cup	 apple cider
1	 tsp	 Dijon mustard
1/4	 cup	 pecan pieces
1/2	 tsp	 salt
2	 Tbsps	 fresh parsley, chopped

Directions:
Prepare rice according to package directions.
In large skillet over medium heat, melt 1 teaspoon butter.  Season chicken breasts with salt-free seasoning.  Sauté chicken 4 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.  Remove chicken from skillet and keep warm.  Add cider and Dijon mustard to skillet and simmer 3 minutes.  Return chicken to pan and coat with sauce; remove from heat.
In medium saucepan melt remaining butter.  Add pecans and stir until lightly toasted.  Add cooked rice and salt and toss to combine.
Serve rice topped with chicken and sauce.  Garnish with parsley.
