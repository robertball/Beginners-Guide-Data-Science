Pot Roast Recipe

1teas pepper
3 tbs olive oil
2 cups beef stock
7 whole cloves
Parsley, chopped
2 large onions, chopped
8 carrots, peeled and chunked
8 med potatoes quartered
1 pound can whole tomates
1 cup celery sliced

Directions:
Rub beef with pepper and brown in olive oil within large pot
Pour in stock, remaining pepper, cloves onions carrots parsley potatoes tomatoes and celery
Add more liquid
Crockpot it optional
