Title:
Frosted Peanut Butter Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 1/2 cups packed brown sugar
1 cup sugar
2 1/2 cups creamy peanut butter, divided
1 egg
1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
2 1/2 cups quick cooking oats
2 cups all purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
CHOCOLATE FROSTING
6 tbls butter or margarine, divided
4 cups confectioners sugar
1/2 cup baking cocoa
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
6 to 8 tbls milk

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugars. Add 1 cup peanut butter, egg and vanilla, mix well. Combine Oats, Flour, Baking Soda, and salt; add to the creamed mixture. Spread into a greased baking pan.
Bake at 350 for 13-17 minutes or until golden brown. Cool slightly on a wire rack, about 12 minutes. Spread with remaining peanut butter. Cool completely.
In a mixing bowl, combine the butter, confectioners sugar, cocoa, vanilla, and enough milk to achieve spreadable consistency. Spoon over peanut butter layer, then spread. Cut into bars
