Title:
Liam's Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1 15 oz can Chili, No Beans

Directions:
In microwave-safe bowl, heat chili, no beans 1-2 minutes, till hot. [Cover to prevent splatter mess in microwave (=]
Stir in softened cream cheese.
Serve with chips of choice.
