Title:
Jewel's Apple Cider Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7072968.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup apple cider
1/2 cup butter, melted
2/3 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups flour
2 tsp cinnamon
2 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
4 oz cream cheese, softened
4 Tbsp butter, softened
1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Line a muffin pan with paper liners.
In a stand mixer, mix together the melted butter with the sugars. Add eggs and mix well.
Add flour, salt, baking soda, cinnamon, vanilla, and apple cider. Beat on high speed for 2 minutes.
Divide batter evenly into muffin cups. Bake for 20 minutes.
prepare frosting while cupcakes are cooling.
In a stand mixer, beat cream cheese and butter together. Add in powdered sugar and vanilla and beat until creamy.
When cupcakes are completely cooled, top with the frosting.
