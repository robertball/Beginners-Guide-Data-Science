Title:
Church Lady Macaroni and Cheese in a Casserole Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz elbow pasta
16 oz cheddar cheese, reduced fat, shredded
8 oz mozzarella, shredded
8 oz mexican cheese blend, reduced fat, shredded
4 eggs, beaten
24 oz fat free cottage cheese
salt and pepper generously, at least Â½ tbsp of each

Directions:
Cook and drain pasta.  In a big(huge) bowl, mix the eggs, cottage cheese, and salt and pepper. Stir in 3/4 of each shredded cheese, mixing well.  Fold in pasta thoroughly but gently.  Slop into a 9 x 13 greased casserole and sprinkle all remaining cheese on top.  Bake at 350 F in a pre-heated oven for 30 minutes or until cheese is browned and bubbly.

Submitter: 
rjkuns
Description: 
This recipe came about by blending mac&cheese; recipes from 3 different little old ladies at church.  It's easy, fast, and tasty.  Cottage cheese keeps it from drying out, and using reduced fat cheese really helps cut down the overall fat in the recipe.
