Title:
Polish "Lazy Pigs" Casserole (Golabki) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 med. head cabbage
2 large onions - diced & divided
1 tablespoon minced/chopped garlic
1 10 3/4 oz.can condensed tomato soup
1 46 oz. can tomato juice or V-8
1# ground beef or turkey
1 1/2 cup COOKED rice
1 tablespoon beef base
salt and pepper to taste
1 tablespoon butter or margarine

Directions:
Spray crockpot with non stick spray.
Slice cabbage into thick strips.
Combine tomato juice and soup, set aside.
Brown the onions in butter/margarine and set aside.
Mix ground beef, 1/2 of the onions, minced garlic, cooked rice, beef base, salt & pepper together and form into thick patties.
Brown the patties in frying pan.
Mix sliced cabbage with reserved onions.
Starting with the cabbage/onions layer the meat patties into the crockpot.
Pour the tomato soup/juice mixture over the top of layered casserole.
Turn crockpot to high and cook for 6 - 7 hours.

Submitter:

Description: 
My family loves Pigs-n-Blankets (Golabki) or stuffed cabbage rolls but...they are so labor intensive that I developed this quick and easy version for weekday meals. Throw it in the crockpot before you go out and come home to the delicious aroma of dinner just waiting to be served!
