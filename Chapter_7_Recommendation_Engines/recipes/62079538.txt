Title:
Great Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c olive oil
2 1/2 TBSP chopped garlic
1 TBSP fresh basil
8 oz mozzarella cheese
1 loaf Italian bread

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Combine 2 TBSP olive oil, garlic and basil using a mortar and pestle.  Mash into a paste.  Add remaining olive oil and mix well.
Cut bread into thick slices.  Lightly toast.  Remove from oven and spoon a portion of the garlic mixture over each slice.  Top with mozzarella cheese.  Cook until cheese is melted and slightly browned.

Submitter: 
BONF
Description: 
This recipe is meant to be played around with.  Use any fresh Italian herbs you have on hand or try Asiago or Parmesan cheese instead of Mozzarella.  You could also use melted butter instead of olive oil.  Use more or less depending on how dry you like your garlic bread.
