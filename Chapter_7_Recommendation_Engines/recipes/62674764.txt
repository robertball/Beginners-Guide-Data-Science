Title:
My favorite Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 package martha gooch lasagna noodles, uncooked
1 container ricotta cheese
1 cup parmesan cheese, grated
2 eggs
2 jars hunts pasta sauce
1 lbd hamburger
2 cups yellow cheese
2 cups mozzarella cheese
1 tsp italian seasoning
1 tbs garlic
8 oz baby bella mushrooms

Directions:
preheat oven to 350. In medium bowl, combine ricotta, parmesan and eggs; mix well. In a separate bowl combine garlic, hunts pasta sauce,mushrooms and italian seasonings.
in a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread one cup pasta sauce. Layer with half each of the uncooked noodles, ricotta mixture, hamburger, remaing pasta sauce and cheese. Repeat layering. top with parsley. cover tightly with aluminum foil; bake covered for 1 hour. uncover and bake an additional 15 minutes. let stand 10 minutes before serving.
