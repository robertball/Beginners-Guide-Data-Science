Title:
Layered Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound bacon
1 1/4 c. mayonaise
1 10 oz. package frozen peas (thawed)
4 green onions (chopped)
1 small red onion (chopped)
1 head iceberg lettuce (shredded small)
2 tbs. granulated sugar

Directions:
Cook and drain bacon. Crumble and set aside.
Place shredded lettuce in the bottom of a clear 9x13 baking dish (you don't have to use clear but it's prettier that way)
Place chopped red onion on top of lettuce.
Place thawed peas over red onion
Mix mayonaise and sugar togather in a bowl. Spread over peas.
Place crumbled bacon over mayonaise.
Place green onions over bacon.
Simple!

Submitter: 
Emily H.
Description: 
My mom used to make this for every potluck and it was always a hit and super simple to make!
