Title:
Lemony Tuna Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 12oz can of chunk light tuna, well drained
1 cup of cracker or bread crumbs
1/4 cup of chopped onion
2 T. chopped bell pepper
1 egg- beaten
3 T. lemon juice
1/2 tsp dillweed
cooking spray or oil for browning

Directions:
Combine tuna, 1/2 cup cracker crumbs, onion, green pepper, egg, lemon juice and dillweed in a bowl. Mix well.
Shape into 6 patties.
Coat with 1/2 cup cracker crumbs.
Brown on both sides  in large skillet.

Submitter: 
Jean Zimmerman
Description: 
Easy and economical. Can be saved for a leftover sandwich if you like to brown-bag your lunch.
