Title:
Ultimate Grilled Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices of bread
1 banana
1/2 cup of peanut butter
1/4 cup of jelly
2 tablespoons of honey
2 tablespoons of butter

Directions:
Warm skillet and coat with cooking spray.
Mix banana, peanut butter, jelly, and honey in a medium sized bowl and mash until it has a paste texture.
Apply butter to one side of each piece of bread.  Apply peanut butter mixture between 2 slices of bread, butter side out and grill both sides until browned.

Submitter: 
Brent Berkeypile
Description: 
I created this recipe to make 2 sandwiches because you won't be able to stop with just one.
