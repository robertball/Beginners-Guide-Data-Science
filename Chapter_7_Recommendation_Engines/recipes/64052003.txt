Title:
Simple Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb lean ground beef
1 whole chopped onion
1 garlic clove, finely chopped
2 (14 1/2 ounce) cans diced tomatoes, undrained
2 (8 ounce) cans tomato sauce
1 can creamed corn
2 +/- tablespoons chili powder
1 teaspoon ground cumin
salt t taste
pepper to taste
red pepper to taste
2 (15-16 ounce) cans kidney beans, rinsed and drained

Directions:
1. cook beef, onion, and garlic in saucepan until  beef is brown
2. stir in remaining ingredients, EXCEPT beans
3. heat to boil, reduce heat to low
4. cover and simmer 1 hour, stirring occasionally
5. stir in beans
6. heat to boiling, reduce heat to low
7. simmer uncovered about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until desired thickness
