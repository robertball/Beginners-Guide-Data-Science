Title:
No Bake Candy Apple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4581502.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) can crushed pineapple
4 Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored, and cut into bite-size pieces
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®), thawed
3 chocolate-coated caramel-peanut nougat candy bars (such as Snickers®), cut into bite-size pieces
3/4 cup peanuts

Directions:
Mix crushed pineapple, Granny Smith apples, whipped topping, candy bars, and peanuts in a bowl.

Submitter: 
PURPLE68
Description: 
This is a unique treat with no baking required. It tastes just like a caramel apple and is great in the summer or the fall.
