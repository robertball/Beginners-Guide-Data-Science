Title:
Scalloped Apple Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987574.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup sugar
2 teaspoons grated orange peel
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 1/2 cups Pepperidge FarmÂ® Corn Bread Stuffing
1/2 cup pecan halves, coarsely chopped
1 (16 ounce) can whole berry cranberry sauce
1/3 cup orange juice or water
4 large cooking apples, peeled and thinly sliced

Directions:
Stir the butter, sugar, orange peel, cinnamon, stuffing and pecans in a medium bowl and set aside.
Stir the cranberry sauce, orange juice and apples in a large bowl. Add half of the stuffing mixture and stir lightly to coat. Spoon the apple mixture into an 8-inch square baking dish. Sprinkle the remaining stuffing mixture evenly over the apple mixture.
Bake at 375 degrees F. for 40 minutes or until the apples are tender.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Take a walk on the sweet side with this flavorful, fruit-packed stuffing bake that is good with most any meat, but especially good with pork dishes.
