Title:
Aglio Olio - Pasta with Olive Oil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/866337.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
2 teaspoons garlic, minced
pinch of red pepper flakes
2 ounces chicken stock or cooking water from the pasta
2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
1/2 pound angel hair pasta
fresh grated romano or parmesan – to taste
salt and freshly ground black pepper – to taste

Directions:
Boil pasta as directed on package.  While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a large saute pan over medium heat.  Add garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook until garlic starts to soften and lightly brown.  Add chicken stock (or cooking water) and parsley.  Drain pasta and add to saute pan.  Toss the angel hair with the olive oil – season to taste with salt and pepper.  Serve immediately and top with cheese.  Enjoy!
