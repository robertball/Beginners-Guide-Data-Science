Title:
Orange Party Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12oz) Can Frozen Orange Juice Concentrate, Thawed
2 Liters Lemon-Lime Soda, Chilled
1 (46oz) can Pineapple juice, chilled
1 Pint Orange or Lime Sherbert (Pineapple works best if you can find it)

Directions:
Prepare Orange Juice according to package directions
Pour into a Large Punch bowl
Stir in Soda and Pineapple Juice
Top with 2 scoops of Sherbert (Or more if desired for more tangy, creamy taste)and just let them float around and melt slowly. It'll keep the punch cold.
Serve Immediately, makes approx. 5 1/2 quarts
