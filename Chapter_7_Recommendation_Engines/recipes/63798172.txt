Title:
Kay's Hawaiian Tiramisu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 egg yolks
1 1/8 cup white sugar
1 cup milk
3 cups heavy cream
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 pound mascarpone cheese
4 Del Monte 4 oz cups diced mango, drained
1 20 oz can crushed pineapple, drained
1 15 oz can mandarin oranges, drained
Maraschino Cherries, for garnish
Smucker's Orange Cream Ice Cream Shell, for garnish
3-4 (3 ounce) packages ladyfinger cookies

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until well blended.  Whisk in milk and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture boils.  Boil gently for 1 minute, remove from heat and allow to cool slightly.  Cover tightly and chill in refrigerator 1 hour.
In a medium bowl, beat cream with vanilla until stiff peaks form.  Whisk mascarpone into yolk mixture until smooth.
Split ladyfingers in half lengthwise,place in bottom of a deep 9x13 pan.  Spread half of the fruits over the ladyfingers. Spread half of mascarpone mixture over ladyfingers, then half of whipped cream over that.  Repeat layers.  Top with cherries and drizzle WELL SHAKEN Orange Ice Cream Topping for looks. Cover and refrigerate 4 to 6 hours, until set.
