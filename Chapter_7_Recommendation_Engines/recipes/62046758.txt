Title:
Quick and Easy Texas Chili Tackle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3lbs Lean Ground Beef
3 cans Diced Tomatoes with green chiles
3-4 cans Chili beans (Mild or Extra Spicy)
1 tablespoon garlic powder
Dash of kosher or  table salt
Dash of pepper
2 Cups of Shredded Cheese
1/2 Cup of diced red onion (for garnish)
1 cup chopped cilantro(for garnish)
Corn Chips or Cornbread are great sides. Add the corn chips to the bottom of your bowl for a Frito Pie!

Directions:
1) Brown the ground beef over high heat. As meat browns season with garlic salt, salt, and pepper.
2) Drain.
3) Return meat to pot and add tomatoes and beans. Stir and bring to a slight boil over medium to medium high heat.
4) Reduce heat and simmer 25 minutes to 1 hour.
Once the chili simmers to your liking serve into bowls and garnish with cheese, onion, and cilantro. Add picante sauce or pico de gallo for and extra kick.
Enjoy.
*Refrigerate in an airtight container or bowl covered with foil and saran wrap. Reheat over medium/medium high heat.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is hit with both my family and my boyfriends family. It's an easy and simple chili recipe that can be spiced up with chicken or beef sirloin. Make it mild or hot and spicy. This is a great starter for those looking for a great chili recipe. As with most chili recipes, this is great the next day!
