Title:
BAKED SALMON Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound salmon fillet with skin on
1/2 cup ground almonds
4 cups spinach, steamed (optional)
1/2 tsp ground fennel
1/2 tsp ground chili powder
1 tbsp lemon juice
salt and pepper to taste
coconut oil
3 tbsp cilantro

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
Mix together ground almonds, chili powder, and fennel.
Season salmon with salt and pepper.
Pour lemon juice over salmon.
Coat both sides of fillet with almond mixture.
Lightly coat the broiler pan with coconut oil.
Place fillets skin side down bake for 15 minutes or until salmon peels
easily off the skin.
Serve over a beat of steamed spinach if desired and top with fresh
chopped cilantro.
