Title:
Chinese orange beef with star anise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
ORANGE SAUCE
1 tbsp vegtable oil
2 shallots cut in half and sliced thin
2 star anise, whole
1 thai chili slit lengthwise (seeded) keep in 1 piece
2 cups orange juice (store bought)
2 fresh oranges juiced and zested
1 tbsp ginger ( scored then smashed)
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp soy sauce
2 whole star anise ground up fine
1 tbsp butter
sea salt
Flank Steak
1 flank steak (2lbs) cut into 1/4 slices across the grain
3/4 cup cornstarch or arrowroot flour
2 tbsps garlic powder
1/2 tsp cayenne
1 tsp ground black pepper
2 star anise ground
salt

Directions:
FOR SAUCE
heat oil in saute pan over medium to high heat
saute shallots, tossing then add whole anise and chili
Deglaze with orange juice (store bought and juice of fresh)
add ginger and sugar, stir
reduce sauce over med. heat until syrup consistency.
Strain into smaller saucepan, return to stove
add soy sauce, ground star anise and orange zest
over low heat cook until zest is tender
remove from heat and butter and let it melt into sauce.
season with salt
FLANK STEAK
In a deep fryer or large wide based pot heat oil to 365.
Prepare a tray with paper towels of newspaper
Place cornstarch (or arrowroot), garlic powder, cayenne, salt, black pepper, and half of the groun anise in a bowl.
lightly dredge flank strips in mixture.
fry in batches
remove from oil and place on lined tray
fry all meat until finished
Place flank steak in a bowl and toss in Orange Sauce.
Season with the other half og ground anise and salt.

Submitter: 
Debra Rose
Description: 
The beef is succulent, crispy and tender.  The sauce just punching out the flavour perfectly.
