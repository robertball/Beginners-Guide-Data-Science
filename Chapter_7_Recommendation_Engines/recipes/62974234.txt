Title:
Fire and Brimstone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ground chuck,2 pounds
Spicy V8 Juice, 1 46 ounce bottle
Crushed tomatoes, 1 28 ounce can
Tomato puree, 1 28 ounce can
Chili peppers and Habernero peppers (Rotel), 2 10 ounce cans
Kidney beans, 2 15 ounce cans
Black beans, 1 15 ounce can
Cayenne Pepper, ground, 1 teaspoon
Chili powder, ground, 1 teaspoon
Cinnamon, ground, 1 teaspoon

Directions:
Brown the ground chuck using care to prevent it from overcooking. Drain grease and pat dry with a paper towel. Remove as much of the grease and oil as possible.
Combine remaining ingredients in a large slow cooker set on high stirring thoroughly.
When ground meat is ready stir it into the mix.
Cover and cook on high for 1 hour. Stir often. After one hour reduce to low. Serve from the slow cooker with your choice of garnishments.

Submitter: 
Bryant Evans
Description: 
A rich,meaty and spicy chili straight from the devil's backyard.
