Title:
Paleo Pecan/Maple Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2376983.jpg
Ingredients:
-4 salmon fillets (4 oz)
-Salt and pepper
-1/2 cup pecans
-1 tsp smoked paprika
-1/2 tsp chipotle powder
-1/2 tsp onion powder
-3 tbs pure maple syrup
-1 tbs apple cider vinegar

Directions:
1. Sprinkle your salmon fillets with salt and pepper
2. Combine all dry ingredients into food processor and pulse until it becomes a crumb like texture.
3. Place spoonfuls of mixture onto the fillets and spread all over the top surface of salmon.
4. Place in refrigerator uncovered for 2 to 3 hours
5. Preheat oven to 425 degrees and cook salmon for 12-14 minutes.
