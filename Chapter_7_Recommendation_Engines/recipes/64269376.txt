Title:
Crock Pot Meatballs Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
Sam's club prepared meatballs
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup ketchup
1/8 cup soy sauce

Directions:
Place meatballs in crockpot on low
Make enough sauce to cover the meatballs
Heat until ready to serve
