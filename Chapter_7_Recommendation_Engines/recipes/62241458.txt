Title:
Baked ziti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar (2 #) spagetti sauce (w/meat, optional)
1 1/2 c. water
15 oz. ricotta
8 oz. shredded mozzarella, divided
1/4 c. grated Parmesan
8 oz ziti or rigatoni pasta (uncooked)
Veggies like spinach, mushrooms, peppers, etc., optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.  In large bowl, combine sauce, water, ricotta 1 c. mozza, and parmesan, then ziti and veggies.
In 13 x 9 dish, evenly spoon ziti mixture.  Cover tightly with foil and bake 55 minutes.
Remove foil and sprinkle with remaining mozza.  Bake uncovered 5 minutes.  Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
