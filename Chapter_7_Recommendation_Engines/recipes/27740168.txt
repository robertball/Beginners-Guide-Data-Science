Title:
Simple Peach Sorbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (15-1/4oz.) Del Monte Peaches in heavy syrup
1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Place unopened can of fruit in freezer; freeze until soild, approximately 24 hours.  Submerge unopened frozen can in very hot tap water for 1 minute.  Open can and pour any thawed syrup into food processor bowl.  Remove frozen fruit from can.
Cut fruit into 8 chunks.  Place frozen chunks in fruit processor, add vanilla.  Process until smooth, scraping blade as needed.  Serve immediately or spoon into freezer safe container and freeze until desired firmness.
