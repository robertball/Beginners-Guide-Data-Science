Title:
Big Ray's Broccoli-Chicken Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1208856.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups (8 ounces) shredded cheddar cheese, divided
20 Ritz crackers, crushed
1 cup cubed cooked chicken
2 tubes (10 ounces each) refrigerated biscuits
1 can (10-3/4 ounces) condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
3 cups frozen chopped broccoli, cooked and drained

Directions:
Place biscuits in greased jumbo sized muffin cups, pressing dough onto the bottom and up the sides. Add 1 tablespoon cheese and crackers to each cup.
In a large bowl, combine the chicken, soup and broccoli; spoon into each muffin cup. Bake at 375Â° for 20-25 minutes or until bubbly. Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
