Title:
Twisted Veggie-Infused Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/354749.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground pork
1 pound ground chicken
1 8.5oz jar sun-dried tomatoes w/oil
1 8oz container Kalamata olives, pits removed
3 oz portabella mushrooms
1 med-large green bell pepper
1 1/2 cup chopped napa cabbage
1 10oz frozen spinach, thawed, drained and pressed to remove any liquid
2 cups seasoned bread crumbs
1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp honey
1 tbsp lemon juice
1/2 tbsp brown sugar
1/2 tsp fresh tarragon
1 large fresh basil leaf
1/2 fresh rosemary
1 tbsp garlic

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Spray non-stick cooking oil into large loaf pan. Set aside. In a large food processor, combine sun-dried tomatoes w/oil, pitted olives, mushrooms, and bell pepper, blend and chop together well. Add ground pork and ground chicken. Blend together well. Add remaining ingredients. Pulse and combine well. Pour mixture into greased loaf pan. Bake 30 - 45 minutes or until internal temp is 155-160 degrees.
