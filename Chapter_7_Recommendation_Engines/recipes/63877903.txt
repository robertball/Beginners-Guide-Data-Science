Title:
Gluten Free Key Lime Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust
2 cup gluten free graham cracker crumbs
4 Tbsp granulated sugar
1 stick unsalted butter, melted
Filling
3 egg yolks
14oz sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup key lime juice (such as Nellie & Joes)
Decoration
1/2 cup whipped cream
1 tbsp granulated sugar
Key limes, thinly sliced

Directions:
Crust
Preheat oven to 350
Crush graham crackers
Mix together the crumbs, sugar & butter until combined
Press crust into bottom of 9" pie pan
Bake for 10 min
Remove from oven (but don't turn off) and allow to cool
Filling
In large bowl, whisk egg yolks with sweetened condensed milk
Stir in the key lime juice, not stirring too much and creating bubbles
Pour into cooled pie crust
Bake at 350 for 15 min
Remove from oven and allow to cool completely
Refrigerate until ready to decorate and serve
Decorations
Just before serving, vigorously whisk the whipped cream with the sugar until the cream forms medium stiff peaks
Place dollop on top
Garnish with thinly sliced lime
