Title:
Praline Pecan Crunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz box Quaker Oat Squares
2 cups Pecan Pieces
1/2 cup Light Corn Syrup
1/2 cup Firmly packed Brown Sugar
1/4 cup Margarine or Butter
1 tsp Vanilla
1/2 tsp Baking Soda

Directions:
Heat oven to 250 degrees.
Combine Cereal and Pecans.
Combine Corn Syrup, Brown Sugar, and Margarine in a large bowl.
Microwave high 1-1/2 Minutes and stir.
Continue Microwaving for 1 to 1-1/2 minutes more or until boiling.
Stir in Vanilla and Baking Soda.
Pour over Cereal.
Stir to coat evenly.
Bake 1 hour one cookie sheet, stirring every 20 minutes.
Break into pieces
