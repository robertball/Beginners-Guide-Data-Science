Title:
Citrus Peanut Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984921.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head Savoy Cabbage, shredded
2 medium Oranges
1 1/4 cups Golden Raisins
1/2 teaspoon Ground Cumin
3/4 cup Roasted Peanuts
1 cup Marzetti® Slaw Dressing

Directions:
Peel oranges and cut into small pieces.  Combine with remaining ingredients in large bowl.  Toss gently.  Cover and chill before serving.

Submitter: 
Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Description: 
Juicy orange pieces, peanuts, golden raisins add just the right counterpoint to this delicious slaw.
