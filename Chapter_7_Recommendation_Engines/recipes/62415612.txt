Title:
Peach Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Small Container of Egg Beaters
1 3/4 cups Splenda
5 fresh peaches, pitted and chopped
2 cups skim milk
1 cup fat free half-and-half cream
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 & 1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In large bowl, mix together egg beaters and splenda until smooth; puree peaches in blender or food processor and stir 2 1/2 cups of puree into egg mixture. Stir in milk, half-and-half, vanilla and salt and mix well.
Pour mixture into freezer canister of ice cream maker and freeze according to manufacturer's instructions.
