Title:
Air Fryer Elote Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5528028.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp. lime juice
2 tsp. cotija cheese
1/4 tsp. chili powder
2 ears of corn, shucked and cut in half
cilantro for garnish *optional*

Directions:
1. Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees.
2. Mix mayonnaise, lime juice, cotija, and chili powder in a flat shallow dish.
3. Roll each piece of corn in the mayonnaise mixture until all sides are covered.
4. Place all 4 pieces of the corn in the basket of the air fryer and  cook for 8 minutes. Garnish with cilantro if desired.

Submitter: 
Soup Loving Nicole
Description: 
All of the flavors you love in street corn without having to fire up the grill. Got a craving in the middle of the winter when it is too cold to grill? No problem!
