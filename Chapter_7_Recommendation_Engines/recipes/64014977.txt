Title:
7 up Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
fruit filling / fresh fruit
Cake Mix
Can of 7-up
Butter slices
Brown sugar
Oatmeal / Granola

Directions:
Put fruit in sprayed 9x13 baking pan and cover with dry cake mix (DO NOT STIR!).
Pour 7Up over whole pan (again, do not stir).
Cover and bake for 20 minutes at 350Â°F.
(Optional) Melt some butter and brown sugar together, mix in oats/granola, spread on top of cake.
Cook additional 20-30 min uncovered.
Makes 12 servings.
Can also be cut in half.
I used a 9x9 in pan and a box of Jiffy cake mix, small skinny blue and white box, and one cup of diet 7-up.
