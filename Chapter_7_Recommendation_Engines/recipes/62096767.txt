Title:
Chicken Long Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless skinless chicken thighs
8 cups water
1 tbsp. salt
1 1/2 tbsp. minced bottled ginger
1 large onion, julienned
5 small chicken bouillon cubes
8 oz. long rice or mung bean thread

Directions:
Put chicken in large pot.
Add 6 cups of water, the salt, and ginger.
Bring to a boil.
Skim the soup scum.
Cover pot and simmer 40 min.
Remove chicken and shred meat.
Add 2 cups water, bouillons, onion, shredded chicken to broth.
Add long rice, cover and cook 5 minutes.
Turn heat off and let it stand for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Renee from Hawaii
Description: 
A standard dish at any luau - actually of asian origins. Hawaiian chicken noodle soup!
