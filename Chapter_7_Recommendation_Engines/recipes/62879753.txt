Title:
Orange Balsamic Glazed Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/693618.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large 1" thick boneless pork loin chops
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1 TBL olive oil
Glaze:
1/2 cup no sugar added orange marmalade
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup fat free reduced sodium chicken broth
1 tsp poultry seasoning
1 tsp dried rosemary
1 tsp chives
1/2 black pepper
1/2 garlic powder
1 tsp crushed red pepper
2 TBL brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Sprinkle the pork chops with black pepper, garlic powder and cayenne pepper.
Saute the pork chops in a skillet with 1 tablespoon olive olive and brown for about 2 or 3 minutes on each side.
Transfer pork to a baking dish and bake about 20 minutes in a preheated oven.
Meanwhile in a saucepan on the stove over low heat, add the marmalade, balsamic vinegar, chicken broth, poultry seasoning, rosemary, chives, pepper, garlic powder, crushed red pepper and brown sugar and stir until thickened.
Brush on the glaze onto the pork chops and bake for 10 more minutes or until juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
A delicious sweet and slightly spicy glazed pork chop dish with an orange balsamic glaze.  It goes well with roasted asparagus and pecan rice.
