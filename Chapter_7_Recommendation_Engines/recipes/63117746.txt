Title:
Cheesy Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (10.5 oz) condensed cream of chicken soup
2 cups sour cream
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
2 1/8 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese
1/3 cup chopped yellow onion
1 (30 oz. or 6 cups) package shredded (Ore Ida Country Style) Hash Browns, thawed
Optional: 2 cups crushed corn flake cereal & 1/4 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
Coat 13x9 baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.
In a large bowl, mix together soup, sour cream, salt, and pepper.  Stir in 2 cups cheese, onion, and Hash Browns.
Spoon evenly into baking dish.
OPTIONAL: In medium bowl, mix together cereal and butter.  Sprinkle evenly onto top of Hash Brown mixture.
Bake uncovered for about 45 minutes, or until hot & bubbly.  Sprinkle remaining 1/8 cup cheese on top of hot casserole.  Let rest for 5 minutes.
