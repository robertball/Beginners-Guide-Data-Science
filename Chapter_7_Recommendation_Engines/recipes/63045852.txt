Title:
Breakfast Strata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground sausage
6-7 eggs, slightly beaten
6 slices white bread, cubed
2 cups milk, 2%
1 tsp. dry mustard
1 tsp. paprika
1 cup grated sharp (or extra sharp) cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown the ground sausage and then drain off fat.  Let cool.
Cube bread into 1" squares.
Mix cooled sausage, bread cubes, mustard powder and paprika in large bowl.
Beat eggs and milk separately.
Prepare a 9" x 13" baking dish by spraying lightly with non stick spray.
Pour bread cube/cheese/seasoning mix into baking dish.
Add egg/milk mix.
Cover and let sit in fridge for at least 12 hours.
Remove from fridge, uncover and bake @ 350Â° F for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
MR. YO
Description: 
Our Christmas morning traditional breakfast.  First thing I do when I come downstairs is preheat the oven and then pop the casserole in when we are an hour away from breakfast.  And, it's satisfying enough to hold everyones appetite in check until dinner.
