Title:
Broccoli hicken Cheddar Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6347804.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Skinless Boneless Chicken Breast
2-4 Broccoli heads
12 oz Velvetta Cheese
1 gram shredded Sharp Cheddar
2 tblspn Cream Cheese
1/2 cup Milk (Whole milk if possible)
.25 cup Heavy cream
.5 cup Chicke broth (I make my own)
1/3 cup Butter
4 tbspn Flour
Half  White Onion
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Wash and cut up chicken into cubes - or you can boil it whole and shred it. Depends on your liking (Chunky Chicken/Shredded Chicken)
2. Boil water; Toss in a half of an onion and pinch of salt. Once water is boiling, put the chicken to boil for 10-15 minutes on medium heat.
3. While your chicken is boiling start another pot of boiling water and toss in your broccoli for 3-7 minutes.
4. Once the chicken is done, strain it - you can use the broth or dump it if youâ€™re opting for the canned or boxed. Put chicken aside.
5. Strain the broccoli, chop in fine pieces, salt and put aside.
6. In a medium pan, melt butter on LOW heat. Once butter is butter is melted add flour 1 tbls at  a time while whisking. 2-3 minutes. (ITS IMPORTANT YOU COOK YOUR FLOUR)
7. Put all wet ingredients in once flour has been cooked (Broth, Milk, Heavy cream)
8. Chop your velvetta and add into the wet ingredients. Add sharp cheddar. Add cream cheese. Stir consistently.
9. Once all the cheese have melted, add in the chopped broccoli and stir for 1 minutes. Put the lid on and leave it for 5 minutes.
10. Stir. Add in chicken breast.  Lid back on for 5 minutes.
ENJOY!
