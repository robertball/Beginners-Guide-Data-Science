Title:
Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/891146.jpg
Ingredients:
32oz bag frozen tater tots
1 pound ground sausage
1/2 large onion
2 cloves minced garlic
2 tablespoons chili powder
1 tablespoon dried basil
12 eggs
3/4 cup heavy cream
1 teaspoon pepper (to taste)
2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese

Directions:
Pre-heat oven for tater tots per manufacturers directions.Cook tater tots per the directions on bag, but only 1/2 to 3/4 of the recommended time.
Adjust oven temp to 375 degrees.
Place onion, garlic, sausage, chili powder, and basil in a medium skillet and brown over med/high heat until sausage is cooked through and onions are soft.
Beat eggs and cream in a bowl until well blended and season with pepper.
Spray 13x9 glass baking dish with cooking spray. Line bottom with tater tots. Sprinkle with cheese. Spread meat and onions. Pour egg mix over all.
Place in 375 degree oven for 30-40 minutes.
Remove from oven and let sit for 5 minutes. Cut and serve!
