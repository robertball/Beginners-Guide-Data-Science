Title:
Vegetable Salad NO Cook Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz. can White Shoe Peg Corn
1 15 oz. can Whole Kernel Corn
1 15 oz. can French Style Green Beans
1 15 oz. can Very Small Green Peas
1 Extra Large Sweet Onion
1 Green Bell Pepper
1 Red Bell Pepper
1 Yellow or Orange Bell Pepper
1 Whole Bunch Celery
Marinate Ingredients
3/4 cup white vinegar
1 t. salt
1 t. pepper
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Chop all vegetables in large bowl.
Add all canned vegetables
Mix all your marinate ingredients together and bring to a boil in a sauce pan. (this is the only thing you need to cook)
When it is to the boil stage, pour it over your vegetables and mix thoroughly
Refrigerate for at least 3 hrs prior to serving, even better over night.

Beautiful and Easy Cold Vegetable Salad with almost no cooking required.
