Title:
I Love These Monster Cookies!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup white, granulated sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs, whisked
5 T. room temperature butter
1 T. white corn syrup
2 t. vanilla extract
1 1/4 t. baking soda
1 cup peanut butter
3 cups quick oats
3/4 cup m & m's
1/2 - 2/3 cup chocolate chips
1/2 - 2/3 cup coconut
Extras: raisins, other chips, nuts, (not to exceed 3 T)
nuts, raisins,

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F. In a large bowl, combine the first 9 ingredients. Fold in oats, m & m's, chocolate chips coconut and any extras.
Drop cookies on a cookie sheet and bake 8-11 minutes depending on how large you make your cookies - smaller cookies cook faster, of course! Cool cookies on the cookie sheet so they firm up before removing them.
