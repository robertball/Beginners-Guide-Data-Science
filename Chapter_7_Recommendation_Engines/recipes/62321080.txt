Title:
Mama's Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
1 lb. ground beef
1 cup chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 cans (10 oz. ea.) condensed tomato soup
1/2 cup water
2 Tbsp. parsley
2 Tbsp. sugar
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. oregano
1 tsp. basil
2 Tbsp. vinegar
1 tsp. Rosemary (optional)
2 pints small curd cottage cheese
1 lb. mozzarella cheese, shredded
grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In a medium saucepan brown ground beef, onion and garlic over medium heat until beef is no longer pink.  Drain if necessary.
Stir in soup, water, parsley, sugar, salt, oregano, basil, vinegar, and rosemary (opt.)  Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Place 3 noodles in a 9x13 pan.
Spread 1/3 of the cottage cheese over the noodles.
Spread 1/3 of the sauce mixture over the cottage cheese.
Sprinkle 1/3 of the shredded mozzerella cheese over the sauce mixture.
Repeat steps 5-8 two more times.
Sprinkle top with parmesan cheese.
Bake uncovered for 30 minutes.
Let lasagna stand 5-10 minutes before serving.
