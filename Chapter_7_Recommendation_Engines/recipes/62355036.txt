Title:
Cowboy Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup All-Purpose Flour
1 Tbsp Garlic Powder
1/2 tsp Fresh Ground Pepper
2 Egg Whites
1 1/2 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
4 (1/4-pound) thin cut beef round sirlion steaks
1/4 tsp Salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 475.
Combine flour, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a shallow plate.
lightly beat egg whites to a slight foam with the Worcestershire sauce in a shallow bowl.
Pound each steak with a mallet.
Dredge with flour, shake off excess, then dip into egg mixture, and finally dredge in flour mixture again.
Spray a nonstick baking sheet with cooking spray.  Set steak on sheet, then spray the steaks.  Bake for 10 to 15 minutes until browned.
