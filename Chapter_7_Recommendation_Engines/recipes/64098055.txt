Title:
Green Tea Frappuccino Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1236090.jpg
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons of matcha green tea powder
4 teaspoons of boiling water
12 milk ice cubes
1/4 cup of milk
2 1/2 tablespoons of Vanilla syrup
1/4 cup of whipped cream
Topping:
Whipped Cream

Directions:
Add milk into ice cube trays and let freeze completely.
In a small cup or bowl, add the matcha green tea powder in along with boiling water. Mix until the powder dissolves. Place in refrigerator for about 30 minutes, or until completely cooled.
With a blender, add the frozen milk cubes, Chilled Matcha green tea mixture, Chilled vanilla syrup, and whipped cream. Blend until the everything is combined, and lump free.
Pour in cup how ever way you prefer and top with whipped cream.

Submitter: 
shellyMwu
Description: 
I've had the Starbucks's green tea frappuccino a few times and always was intrigued with the flavors. I decided to experiment myself, coming up with something very similar. Love this drink a lot!
