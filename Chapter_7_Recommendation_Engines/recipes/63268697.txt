Title:
Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs, beaten well
1 c. oil
2 c. sugar
1 T. imitation vanilla extract
3 c. flour
1 t. soda
1 T. cinnamon
1/2 t. baking powder
1 t. salt
1/2 t. ginger
1/2 t. cloves
2 c. grated zucchini (add last)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together well (adding zucchini last) and pour into two greased loaf pans.
Bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees.
