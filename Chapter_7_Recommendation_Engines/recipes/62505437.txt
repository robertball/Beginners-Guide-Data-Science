Title:
FESTIVE CRANBERRY PINEAPPLE SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 20 oz. can crushed Pineapple, undrained
2 small boxes Rasberry Jello
1- 16 oz. can whole berry Cranberry Sauce
1 medium Apple, chopped
2/3 cup chopped Walnuts

Directions:
Drain pineapple, reserving all liquid.
Add enough cold water to pineapple juice to measure 3 cups.
Bring to a boil; remove from heat.
Add gelatin, stirring at least 2 minutes.
Add Cranberry Sauce, stirring until well blended.
Pour into large bowl.
Refrigerate 1-1/2 hours. Stir in pineapple, apple & walnuts...Stir gently.
Topping
2-1/2 pints Whipping Cream
1- 8 oz. Cream Cheese
1- 10 oz. pkg. Mini-Marshmallows
Mix together until desired smoothness
