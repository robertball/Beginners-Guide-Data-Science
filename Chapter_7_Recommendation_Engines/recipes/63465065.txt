Title:
Butternut Squash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5795374.jpg
Ingredients:
1 butternut squash, peeled, cut up, and cooked until soft
1/2 medium onion, cut up
3 tbsp butter
3 c chicken broth
1/4 tsp pepper
4 oz cream cheese, or to taste

Directions:
In large stock pot, sauté onions in butter.
Add all other ingredients, except cream cheese.
Bring to boil and simmer 20 minutes.
Allow to cool, then purée mixture in blender and return to stock pot.
Add cream cheese and reheat, but not to boiling.
Serve.
