Title:
Italian Macaroni Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked elbow mararoni
1 pound ground beef
2 carrots, shredded
1 large onion, chopped
2 celery ribs, chopped
1 can (28 oz.) diced tomatoes, undrained
2 cloves garlic, minced or 1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. dried oregano
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to package directions until tender.  Drain.
In a large skillet, brown the beef along with the onions, carrots, and celery until meat is no longer pink; drain.
Add garlic, salt, black pepper and oregano and the tomatoes.
Bring to a boil; cover and reduce heat and simmer for approximately 20 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
Add macaroni to meat mixture and mix thoroughly.
Pour into greased 9x13 baking dish.
Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top.
Cover with foil and bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for approximately 25 minutes or until bubbly.

Submitter:

Description: 
I came up with this recipe after cleaning out my vegetables in the refrigerator and boiling some macaroni.  I decided to brown some hamburger and wow this came out delicious!  I served it with a green salad and some garlic bread for an easy, delicioius Italian meal.
