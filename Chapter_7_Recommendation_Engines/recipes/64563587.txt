Title:
Cheesey Mashed Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium potatoes, peeled and quartered
1 TBL salt + 1 TSP, divided usage
1/4 cup melted butter
1 cup Sour Cream
1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
2 TBL chopped fresh chives
1/2 cup French fried onions
2 TBL bacon pieces

Directions:
Heat oven to 325F. Butter a 1 1/2 quart casserole. Place potatoes in 3 quart saucepan; cover with cold water. Add 1 TBL salt. Bring to a boil. Cover; reduce heat. Cook 20 minutes or fork tender. Drain. Put pan back on burner for 1 minute, shaking the pan until potatoes are dry. Mash potatoes with potato masher or mixer. Add butter, sour cream, cheese, chives and 1/2 tsp salt. Mash or stir until mixed. Spoon the mixer into casserole. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until hot. Sprinkle with onions and bacon. Bake an additional 5 minutes.
