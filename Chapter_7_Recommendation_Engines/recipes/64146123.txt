Title:
Asian Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1382607.jpg
Ingredients:
Julienned cucumbers
Bell Peppers (green or red)
Onions
Cabbage (optional for crunch)
3 parts rice wine vinegar
2 parts olive oil
1 part dark or light sesame oil
1 part soy sauce or shoyu or tamari
crushed garlic
minced ginger
few pinches sugar

Directions:
Combine cucumbers, bell peppers, onions, and cabbage.
Whisk together rice wine vinegar, olive oil, sesame oil, soy sauce, crushed garlic, minced ginger, and sugar. Add to vegetables.
Toss and serve.

Submitter: 
Sophie Pipilas
Description: 
This is a great light and refreshing salad.
