Title:
Red and White Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 lasagna noodles
16 oz Ricotta cheese
8oz spinach
1 egg
1/4 C Fresh Parmesan cheese
1/2 C Mozzarella cheese
1/2 tabs parsley
Red sauce:
8 oz Italian ground turkey or sausage
8 oz prosciutto diced
28 oz marinara sauce
1 Tabs Olive oil
2 cloves whole peeled Garlic
1 medium zucchini diced
1 medium yellow squash diced
1 medium onion diced
1/2 tsp sea salt
White Sauce:
2 tabs Butter
2 tabs Flour
1 tabs chicken bullion
2 Cups Heavy cream
1 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese
1 tsp oregano

Directions:
Boil noodles in water until pliable but firm about 5 minutes. Meanwhile: Combine Ricotta, spinach, egg, Parmesan cheese, mozzarella and parsley in bowl and set aside.
For red sauce:
Sauté ground turkey and prosciutto drain and add marinara sauce. Heat olive oil in skillet over medium heat add garlic cloves and sauté until garlic is browned. Remove garlic and add zucchini, yellow squash and onion, sauté 5 minutes or until tender. Season with salt and combine with marinara sauce mix.
For white sauce:
In heavy bottom pot melt butter over med heat, mix in flour and chicken bouillon. Slowly add heavy cream while whisking to form a roux. Continue to whisk under med heat about 5 minutes until thick.
Spread 1/2 cup white sauce in the bottom of a 9 x 12 pan. Layer 4 lasagna noodles top with 1/2 ricotta mixture, 1/3 white sauce and 1/3 red sauce, repeat layers. For top layer add the last of the white sauce followed by red sauce, top with mozzarella and oregano. Cover with foil and bake for 35 minutes at 350, remove foil and cook an additional 10 minutes.
