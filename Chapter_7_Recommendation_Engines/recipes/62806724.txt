Title:
Easy Hot Corn Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20oz frozen corn
1- small onion chopped
2 Cups Milk
2 eggs, beaten
1 - 8.5 oz corn muffin mix
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325. Combine corn and onion in food processor and process using pulse until corn is broken, not pureed. Add milk and eggs and pulse until blended. Add muffin mix and a dash of salt; pulse until mixed.
Pour mixture into greased 11 ¾ x 7 ½ inch baking pan and bake for 45 to 50 minutes. Sprinkle pudding with cheese and place under broiler 3 to 4 inches from top and broil until cheese is melted and golden.
