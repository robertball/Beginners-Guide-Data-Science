Title:
Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups dry, small whole grain elbow macaroni, cooked and drained
3 tablespoons cornstarch
1 tsp salt
1 tsp dry mustard
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
1 (12 fluid ounce) can lowfat Evaporated Milk
1 cup water
3 tablespoons butter or margarine
2 cups shredded sharp reduced-fat cheddar cheese, divided
1 cup bread crumbs (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Grease 2-quart casserole dish.
Combine cornstarch, salt, mustard,garlic powder and pepper in medium saucepan. Stir in evaporated milk, water, and butter. Cook over medium-heat, stirring constantly, until mixture comes to a boil. Boil for 1 minute. Remove from heat. Stir in 2 cups cheese until melted. Add macaroni; mix well. Pour into prepared casserole dish. Top with remaining cheese.
Bake for 25 to 30 minutes or until cheese is melted and light brown.
