Title:
Gourmet Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
28 ounce can of crushed tomatoes
6 ounce can of tomato paste with basil & garlic (Contadina has an Italian tomato paste with Italian seasonings if you can find it.)
2 cups of chicken or vegetable broth, low salt/sodium
12 ounce can low sodium V-8 or tomato juice
5 Tablespoons sugar, level/not heaping
4 Tablespoons butter
2 cloves of garlic, minced
black pepper to taste
2 cups of heavy cream

Directions:
In a large pot combine all ingredients EXCEPT the heavy cream.  Simmer for 20 minutes.
Let cool.
Whisk in the cream and reheat.
Serve.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
This makes a large pot of tomato soup but it won't last long, everyone keeps going back for just a little more.
It's easy to make having almost everything on hand when neeeded.

You simmer it for 20 minutes, let it cool then reheat after adding in the heavy cream.
