Title:
Italian Stromboli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tube refrigerated pizza crust
2 cups (8 ounces) shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese
3 slices peppered deli ham
6 slices hard salami
4 slices sandwich pepperoni
1 tablespoon oregeno
1 teaspoon garlic powder
Spray olive oil
Italian Seasoning Mix

Directions:
Pat dough into a 14-in. x 12-in. sprayed rectangle pan. Top with a single layer of ham, pepperoni, and hard salami. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over dough to within 1/2 in. of edges. Roll up tightly from a long side; pinch seam to seal. Place seam side down on an greased baking sheet. Spray top of loaf with olive oil.  Sprinkle with Italian Seasoning Mix.
Bake at 375Â° for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown. Cut with a serrated knife.
