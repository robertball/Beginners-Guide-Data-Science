Title:
holiday sweet potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large sweet potatoes, peeled and sliced
1 Tbsp. flour
2 cups heavy cream
4 cloves garlic, minced
3 Tbsp. unsalted butter
1/4 cup dried bread crumbs
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a saucepan melt the butter. Add flour and garlic and stir until color deepens a bit.  Add cream and simmer until the mixture thickens slightly.
Layer sweet potatoes in a buttered casserole dish.
Pour sauce over potatoes and bake at 325 degrees for 1 hour.
Remove from oven and sprinkle with bread crumbs and cheese and bake for 10-15 more minutes.

Submitter: 
cookin'mama
Description: 
When I tasted this at a neighbor's house, I knew I just had to have the recipe. Despite the name, this unique sweet potato casserole is not just for holidays.  It is as delicious with pork as it is with turkey. If you dare to be different, you will be rewarded!
