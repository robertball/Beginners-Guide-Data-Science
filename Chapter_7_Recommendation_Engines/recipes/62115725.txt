Title:
Eggs Saratoga Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 tbs cream cheese
1 tsp chopped chives
1 small garlic clove, minced

Directions:
In a small bowl gently scrammble eggs with garlic. Cook eggs on medium heat, stirring frequently until cooked.
Put scrambled eggs in a bowl with the cream cheese and chives. Stir until the cream cheese and chives are evenly mixed through the eggs.

Submitter: 
Ellie
Description: 
These creamy scrambled eggs are best served along side, or inside of a croisant.
