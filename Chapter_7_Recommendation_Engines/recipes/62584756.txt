Title:
Easy Chili That Stretches! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons Chili powder
2 teaspoons Onion powder
2 teaspoons Salt
1 teaspoon Paprika
1/4 teaspoon Garlic powder
1 teaspoon Cumin
1/4 teaspoon Ground cayenne pepper
1-2 lbs Lean ground beef
1 46 fluid oz. can tomato juice
1 15 oz. Can kidney beans
1 15 oz. Can chili beans
1 15 oz. Can black beans
1 Onion, chopped
1 can Rotel
1 can Diced tomatoes
Saltines or Oyster Crackers
Shredded Cheese

Directions:
Combine chili powder, onion powder, salt, paprika, garlic powder, cumin and cayenne pepper in small bowl and set aside.
Brown ground beef with chopped onion and 1 tablespoon of the seasoning mix.
Drain excess grease.
In large pot, add ground beef mixture and all remaining ingredients.
Heat until warmed through.
