Title:
WAFFLES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2711101.jpg
Ingredients:
Bread Flour 120g
Cake Flour 40g
Salt 1/2 tsp
Sugar 30g
Yeast 1/4 tsp
Egg 1/2
Milk 45ml
Powdered sugar 30g
Butter 30g (room temperature)

Directions:
1. Mix with flours, sugar and yeast
2. Add in egg and milk then stir well, and add butter and powdered sugar.
3. Knead the dough for about 10 mins, cover with food wrap and let it seat for 40 mins.
4. Divide each dough for about 50g, let it seat for 20 mins.
5. Bake with waffle machine, spread some powdered sugar on the top of dough.
