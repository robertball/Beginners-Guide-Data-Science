Title:
Olive Garden Pasta e Fagioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/772310.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 small onion, diced (1 cup)
1 large carrot, julienned (1 cup)
3 stalks celery, chopped (1 cup)
3 cloves of garlic, minced
2 14.5 oz cans diced tomatoes
1 15 oz. can great northern beans with liquid
1 15 oz. can red kidney beans with liquid
1 15 oz. can tomato sauce
2 12 oz. can V-8 juice
1 T. white vinegar
1 1/2 t. salt
1 t. oregano
1 t. basil
1/2 t. pepper
1/2 t. thyme
1/2 lb. pasta

Directions:
Brown ground beef in a large saucepan or pot over medium heat.  Drain off most of the fat.
Add onion, carrot, celery and garlic and saute for 10 minutes.  Add remaining ingredients except pasta and simmer for one hour.
About 50 minutes into simmer time, cook the pasta in 1 1/2 to 2 quarts of boiling water over high heat.  Cook 10 minutes for just until pasta is al dente or slightly tough.  Drain.
Add the pasta to the large pot of soup.  Simmer for 5-10 minutes and serve.
