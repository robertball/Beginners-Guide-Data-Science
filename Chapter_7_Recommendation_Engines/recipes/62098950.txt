Title:
Em's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb lean ground beef
1/2lb ground sage sausage
1c. seasoned bread cubes
1 small white onion; minced
2 eggs
4 tbls ketchup
1 tsp soy sauce
4 tbls dark brown sugar
1 tsp ground pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp freshly grated ginger

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375
Mix ground beef, sausage, bread cubes, and eggs together in a large bowl.
Add minced onion
Add 1 tbls ketchup, 1tsp black pepper and 1/2 tsp salt
Mix all ingredients together and place in a meatloaf pan.
Mix remaining ketchup, brown sugar, soy sauce, and ginger in a small bowl and wisk together
Brush half of the glaze over meatloaf
Bake on center rack for 35 minutes
Remove from oven and brush remaining glaze onto meatloaf
Continue cooking for another 20 minutes

Submitter: 
Emily H.
Description: 
Enjoy this savory meatloaf with a sweet and tangy glaze.
