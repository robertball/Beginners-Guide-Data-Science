Title:
Sunshine's Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups broccoli
½ cup red onion
1 Cup frozen peas
½ cup raisins
½ cup seed/nuts optional mix in just before serving
¾ cup mayo
¼ cup sugar
2 tbsp white or cider vinegar

Directions:
Mix all together and chill at least one hour before serving

Description: 
Standard mayo-based broccoli salad, but yummy with peas.
