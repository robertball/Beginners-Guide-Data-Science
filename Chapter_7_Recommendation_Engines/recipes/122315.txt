Title:
Crab Quiche Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975711.jpg
Ingredients:
8 eggs, beaten
2 cups half-and-half cream
1 large sweet red pepper, chopped
1 (8 ounce) package imitation crabmeat, chopped
1 cup soft bread crumbs
1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup chopped green onions
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
In a bowl, combine all ingredients. Transfer to a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 30-35 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean. Let stand 10 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
Nancy  Robaidek
Description: 
'On a farm, a hearty breakfast is a given,' comments Nancy Robaidek of Krakow, Wisconsin. Loaded with cheese, this satisfying crab casserole is an easy-to-assemble addition to a brunch buffet.
