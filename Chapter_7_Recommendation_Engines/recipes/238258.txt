Title:
Summertime Limeade Fizzle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2577298.jpg
Ingredients:
20 small limes, halved
1 liter club soda
1 cup white sugar
1 pinch salt
water as needed

Directions:
Squeeze lime juice from limes into a pitcher. Add 5 of the lime halves into the lime juice. Stir club soda, sugar, and salt into lime juice until sugar dissolves. Fill the rest of the pitcher with water and stir again.

Submitter: 
Karen Janice Francis
Description: 
I made this fizzle limeade one hot summer day in July. It really hits the spot over traditional limeade. The club soda and fresh squeezed limes make all the difference! Pour over ice and serve.
