Title:
Bread & Butter Pickle Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 hard-boiled eggs
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1-2 Tbsp. honey mustard
2 Tbsp. sliced bread and butter pickles
1/4 tsp. pepper
Paprika (optional)

Directions:
Hard-boil, cool and peel eggs.
Cut in half lengthwise; carefully remove yolks and place in a small separate bowl.  Mash with fork.
Add mayonnaise, honey mustard, pickle and pepper.  Blend well until smooth.
Scoop yolk mixture into egg halves; garnish with paprika, if desired.
Refridgerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Kimberly
Description: 
This is a deviled egg recipe that uses bread and butter pickles and honey mustard.
