Title:
Creamy White Tea and Peach Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2140241.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups chilled, brewed white tea
2 cups frozen sliced peaches or mango
1 cup Nordica 1% or Fat Free Cottage Cheese
2 tablespoons honey
1 tablespoon lime or lemon juice

Directions:
Combine tea, peaches, cottage cheese, honey and lime juice in a blender. Blend on high speed for 2 minutes or until very smooth. (Adjust sweetness to taste.) Serve immediately.
