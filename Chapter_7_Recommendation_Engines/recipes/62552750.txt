Title:
Crockpot Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*1 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes
*1 16 oz can pinto beans
*1 16 oz can white beans
*1 16 oz can butter beans
*1 27 oz can chili beans
*4 celery stalks chopped
*1/2-1 red onion chopped
*1/2-1 sweet onion chopped
*1 lb ground turkey
*1 package chili powder mix
Optional toppers:
*Shredded cheese
*Plain yogurt

Directions:
Place thawed ground turkey in crockpot on high. Takes about 2 hrs to cook.
Break meat up when it is done cooking.
Add the beans, tomatoes, onion, celery, & chili powder and stir.
Stir occasionally over next 2-3 hrs.
Serve warm and top with a dollup of the yogurt.
