Title:
Best Ever Sesame Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Large bunch of broccoli
2 Tbsp Olive Oil (or substitute 1 tbsp sesame oil)
2 Tbsp White Vinegar
2 Tbsp Lite Soya Sauce
3 Tsp sugar
2 Tbsp sesame seeds

Directions:
STEAM broccoli until tender crisp
WISK together oil, vinegar, soya sauce, sugar and sesame seeds in saucepan over low heat until sugar is dissolved.
PLACE steamed broccoli in bowl and pour sauce over top.
SERVE with slotted spoon.

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
The BEST broccoli I've ever eaten.  My kids cheer when this goes on the table!!
