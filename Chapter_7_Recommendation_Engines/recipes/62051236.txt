Title:
Creamy Green Chilie Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 dozen corn tortillas
1/4 cup butter or margerine
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 - 16 oz. carton sour cream
1 - 16 oz. carton small curd cottage cheese
2 - 8 oz. cans whole green chilies, cut into strips (divided use)
1 lb. Monterey Jack cheese-cut into strips
2 cups shredded Monterey Jack or Mexican Blend cheese
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. onion powder
1 tsp. chili powder
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix together sour cream and cottage cheese; season with garlic, onion, and chili powders, and salt and pepper.  Set aside about 1 cup of sauce for topping enchiladas.  Melt margerine/butter in medium fry pan with oil. Fry tortillas one at a time in oil mixture until softened-about 15 seconds each side.  Spread 2 tablespoons sour cream mixture down the middle of softened tortilla.  Place several chilie and cheese strips on top of sour cream mixture.  Roll tortilla into an enchilada.  Continue with remaining tortillas placing enchiladas seam side down in a greased baking dish.  Top with reserved sour cream mixture and chili strips. Sprinkle grated cheese over top.  Cover dish with foil.  Bake at 350 for 20 min. removing foil for last 5 min of baking.

Submitter: 
SweetBasil
Description: 
Like a Chile Relleno inside an enchilada. The unusual white sauce and filling for these savory meatless enchiladas uses both sour cream and cottage cheese. Adding shredded chicken to the filling is another delicious option.
