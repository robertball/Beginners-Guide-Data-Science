Title:
Pizza sauce- no added sugar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 TB olive oil
1/2 TB  minced garlic
1/2 tsp. dried oregano
14.5 oz. can undrained, diced tomatoes (Contandina organic)
6 oz. can tomato paste
1/4-1/2 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. fresh ground pepper

Directions:
Heat olive oil on medium in small saucepan.
Add garlic and oregano-stir and heat for one minute.
Add remaining ingredients.
Bring to boil then reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes, stirring often, before spreading over prepared pizza crust.

Submitter: 
Amy Bean
Description: 
Make your own flavorful pizza sauce to avoid added sugars!
