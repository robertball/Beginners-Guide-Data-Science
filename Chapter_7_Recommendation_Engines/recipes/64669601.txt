Title:
BBQ Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 slab of baby back pork ribs
seasoned salt or any rub you want
Bottled BBQ sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 275 degrees.
Season ribs with seasoned salt or preferred rub. Place in ovenproof pan which has been sprayed with Pam or use aluminum foil. Add 1/4 inch water and cover with foil.
Cook for 6 hours.
Remove from pan,  pour out water and return to pan, let stand for 30 to 60 minutes.
Cover with BBQ sauce and return to oven on broil. Broil about 5-10 minutes until the ribs are warmed through and the sauce is slightly browned.
