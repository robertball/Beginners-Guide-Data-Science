Title:
Special Friend Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked long-grain rice
2 TBS. peanut oil
2 medium eggs, beaten
1 TBS. soy sauce
1 TBS. ginger
2 tsp. minced garlic
1/2 small onion chopped
1 green chili, seeded and finely chopped
3/4 cup green peas
4 oz. raw shrimp peeled
1/2 cup corn kernels, fresh, canned or frozen
4 scallions, trimmed and chopped

Directions:
Prepare veggies and rice as listed for each ingredient.
Heat wok and add 1 TBS. of peanut oil.
Beat the eggs with soy sauce and chill. pour mixture into the wok and cook until set. Remove the eggs from the wok. Cool, then cut into shreds. Set aside.
Add the remaining TBS of peanut oil to the wok. Add garlic, ginger and onion. Cook until the onion is soft and slightly transparent.
Add peas, shrimp and corn - stir-fry for 3 minutes. Stir in the scallions and the shredded eggs. Heat for 2 minutes or until hot.
