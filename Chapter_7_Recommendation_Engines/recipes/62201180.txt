Title:
Janis Dohmann's Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs, slightly beaten
1 cup light corn syrup
1/4 cup sugar
2 tablespoons flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 1/4 cups broken pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Spread pecans in an unbaked deep dish pie shell
Mix remaining ingredients and pour over pecans.
Bake at 375 degrees for 40-50 minutes or until filling is set.
