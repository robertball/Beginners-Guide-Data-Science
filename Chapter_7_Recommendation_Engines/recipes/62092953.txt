Title:
Teriyaki Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup brown sugar packed
1/8 cup brown mustard
1 tbs minced garlic

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Marinade your choice of meat in marinade for a couple hours. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Cherry83
Description: 
Very simple, but VERY tasy teriyaki marinade. Great on chicken, pork, steak, whatever you prefer!
