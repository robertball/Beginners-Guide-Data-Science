Title:
French Onion Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 (1 oz. ) french bread slices
2 tea. butter
1 large onion, very thinly sliced
1 1/2 Tab. brown sugar
1 cip shredded swiss

Directions:
Arrange bread on baking sheet. Bake at 375 for 8 min. or until lightly toasted. Remove from oven...leave bread on sheet.
While bread toasts, melt butter in pan..add onions and cook 3 min. or until onions are tender. add sugar and cook 5 min more or until onion is tender and browned. spoon mixture over bread, top w/ cheese.. broil about 2 min. until cheese melted
