Title:
Peanut Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1759430.jpg
Ingredients:
Layer 1:
2/3 c roasted peanuts, chopped
1/2 c butter
1 c flour
Layer 2:
1/3 c peanut butter
1 8 oz pkg of cream cheese
1 c powdered sugar
1 4 1/2 oz size Cool Whip Frozen Topping
Layer 3:
1 small pkg instant vanilla pudding
1 small pkg instant chocolate pudding
2 3/4 c milk
Layer 4:
1 9oz container Cool Whip Frozen Topping
1 oz semi-sweet chocolate, grated or shaved
1/3 c. chopped peanuts

Directions:
Layer 1:  Throughly blend flour and margarine.  Add peanuts, pat evenly into 2 9" pie tins.  Bake 20 minutes at 350 degrees.  Cool completely.
Layer 2:  Cream peanut butter and cream cheese together.  Add powdered sugar and mix well.  Fold in frozen whipped topping and spread over cooled Layer #1.  Refrigerate while you are making layer #3.
Layer 3:  With electric mixer blend pudding mixes with milk.  Spread over Layer #2.  Allow to chill so it firms up before adding Layer #4.
Layer 4:  Top other layers with frozen topping and sprinkle chocolate and peanuts over top. Chill 2-3 hours.
