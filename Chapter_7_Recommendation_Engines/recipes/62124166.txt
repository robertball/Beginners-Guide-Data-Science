Title:
Broiled Asparagus with Lemon Tarragon Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch (about a pound) of fresh asparagus,trimmed
5 t of olive oil (1 t reserved for dressing)
Kosher salt and fresh black pepper to taste
3 t lemon juice
1 shallot, minced
1 t dried tarragon
1/4 t dijon style mustard

Directions:
Arrange the asparagus on a rimmed cookie sheet and coat the asparagus spears with 4 teaspoons of olive oil. Season with kosher salt and fresh ground pepper.
Broil in preheated oven about 4 inches from the broiler
for about 8 - 19 minutes, or until lightly browned and tender. Turn the asparagus halfway through the cooking process.
In a bowl, wisk lemon juice, shallot, tarragon, dijon mustard and reserved olive oil. Drizzle dressing over the cooked asparagus, adjust seasonings with more kosher salt and pepper if needed and serve hot.

Description: 
Asparagus broiled with a dijon/lemon/tarragon dressing. The dressing really brightens the taste. Quick to assemble and make.
