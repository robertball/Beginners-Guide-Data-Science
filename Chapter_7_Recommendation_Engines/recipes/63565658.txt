Title:
Ruby Red Grapefruit Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz. Ruby Red grapefruit juice
1 oz. vodka
1 oz. triple sec or cointreau
1 Tbsp. of sugar or sugar in the raw, for coating rim of glass
ice

Directions:
Place 1 Tbsp of sugar on a small plate.
Wet glass with cold water.
Dip drinking rim of glass in sugar and coat rim.
Pour all liquid ingredients into a shaker with ice and shake.
Strain and pour into martini glass.
Serve immediately, with lemon or grapefruit rind garnish if preferred.
