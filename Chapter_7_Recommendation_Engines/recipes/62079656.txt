Title:
Heavenly Lemon Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mixture:
250 g butter (softened)
250 g sugar
250 g flour
Grated rind of 3 lemons
1 tsp baking powder
3 eggs
Syrup:
Juice of 3 lemons
1/2 cup of sugar

Directions:
1. Cream butter and sugar together.
2. Mix in eggs one at a time
3. Add flour and baking powder.
4. Line round cake tin and pour in mixture.
5. Bake for 45-55 mins at 180 degrees Celsius
6. When slightly cool, poke all over surface with a knitting needle.
7. Pour syrup very slowly over surface.
8. Allow to cool slightly before cutting.
Syrup instrustions:
1. Put sugar and lemon juice into a saucepan.
2. Heat until sugar has dissolved.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
Minimal and inexpensive ingredients, minimal preparation time, easy, and tastes divine... it has to be heaven!  Delicious moist lemon cake, perfect served hot with cream for desert, or cold with coffee!
