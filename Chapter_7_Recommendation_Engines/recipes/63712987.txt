Title:
Baked Sweet Garlic Brie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Non-stick Cooking spray
Brie
Jar minced garlic
Brown sugar
Water-About a tablespoon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Spray glass baking dish with non-stick cooking spray. Place brie in baking dish. Cover top of brie with a thin, solid layer of minced garlic. Top garlic layer with an equal amount of brown sugar. Pat brown sugar down lightly. Add equal amounts of additional garlic and brown sugar next to brie in baking dish. Add water to bottom of pan to keep from burning. Bake until cheese is bubbly. Best served with a dense bread to soak up the delicious juice.
