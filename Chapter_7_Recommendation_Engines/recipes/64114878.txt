Title:
Big Ray's Zucchini Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1281859.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups shredded unpeeled zucchini
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 cups (8 ounces) shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
1 cup (4 ounces) shredded cheddar cheese, divided
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 can (15 ounces) Italian tomato sauce (or pizza sauce or spaghetti sauce)
1 medium green pepper, chopped
1 cup chopped spinach

Directions:
Place zucchini in strainer; sprinkle with salt. Let stand for 20 minutes. Squeeze out moisture.
Combine zucchini with the eggs, Parmesan and half of the mozzarella and cheddar cheeses. Press into greased 13-in. x 9-in. baking dish.
Bake, uncovered, at 400Â° for 25 minutes until edges start to brown. Add tomato sauce; spoon over zucchini mixture.
Sprinkle with remaining cheeses; add green pepper. Bake 20 minutes longer or until heated through.
