Title:
Chili With the Works Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1 (1.25 ounce) package McCormick® Chili Seasoning
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (15 ounce) can black beans
8 (10 inch) flour tortillas, warmed
1 cup sour cream
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
2 cups shredded lettuce
sliced green onion, if desired

Directions:
Brown ground beef in a large skillet; drain fat. Stir in seasoning mix, tomatoes and beans. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes.
Spread 1/2 cup chili onto center of each tortilla; top each with 2 tablespoons sour cream, 1/4 cup cheese, and 1/4 cup lettuce.  Sprinkle with sliced green onion, if desired.  Fold bottom of each tortilla over chili; fold sides over, overlapping fillings.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
Cheer on your favorite sports team with our Chili With the Works Wraps! They are a creative, hand-held alternative to regular chili and fun to enjoy with friends.
