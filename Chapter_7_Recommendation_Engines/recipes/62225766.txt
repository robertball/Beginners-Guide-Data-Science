Title:
Southern Charm Cornbread Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/932735.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Tbs Butter
3 Onions, chopped
2 cups celery with leaves
12 large Cornbread muffins cubed
Handful Sage leaves, finely chopped
2 teaspoons dried thyme
2 Egg
1/2 C. Heavy Cream
1/2 C. Chicken Stock
Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
Melt the butter in a medium skillet over medium heat. Add the onions and celery for about 10 minutes, or until soft. Add sage, thyme stirring for 1-2 min then set aside.
Add cornbread pieces in a large pan, season well with salt and pepper.
In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg, cream, and stock, and pour that over the cornbread. Stir till well combined and spoon it into a buttered baking dish. Bake until hot and top is slightly crusty, about 30 minutes.
