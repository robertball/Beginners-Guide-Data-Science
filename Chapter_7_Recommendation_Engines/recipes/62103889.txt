Title:
Pasta Salad Pronto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box tricolor spiral noodles
1/2 bottle italian salad dressing
1/2 cup sliced black olives

Directions:
Cook noodles according to package directions. Toss with salad dressing and olives. Serve chilled.
