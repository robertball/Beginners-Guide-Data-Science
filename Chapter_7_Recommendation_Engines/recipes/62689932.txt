Title:
Strawberry Rhubarb Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Unbaked pastry for 9" double crust pie
2 cups diced fresh rhubarb
2 1/2 cups whole hulled strawberries
1 cup sugar
1/4 cup flour
3 tablespoons butter, cut into bits
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. In a medium bowl, mix together the rhubarb, strawberries, sugar, flour butter, and nutmeg. Pour into unbaked pie shell. Finish with a lattice top. Bake pie in at 450 degrees for 10 minutes. Remove pie from oven and reduce heat to 375 degrees. Sprinkle the top of the pie with a mixture of 1 tablespoon sugar and 1 teaspoon cinnamon. Continue baking for 30 minutes until filling is bubbly and pastry is golden.
