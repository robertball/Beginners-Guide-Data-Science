Title:
Chipotle beef fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 or 2 Zucchini
1 or 2 summer squash
1 lb hamburger meat
1lb (by volume) of spinach frozen or fresh  [thaw if frozen]
1 15 ounce can of diced tomatoes
½ cup Chipotle mango chile sauce
and
2 cups of cheese   [any flavor cheddar is best]
for the Chipotle sauce:
3 mangoes peeled, pitted, and chopped
1 ¾ c. fresh cilantro
3 Chipotle chile peppers in adobo sauce
3 TB unflavored vinegar [I used ACV]
4 cloves of garlic
3 TB fresh lemon juice
1 c. of nut, coconut, or canola oil     [NOT PEANUT OIL]

Directions:
for the sauce
put the mangoes, cilantro,chiles,vinegar, garlic, lemon juice, oil and 1tsp each of salt and pepper in a food processor and puree until smooth, adjust the seasonings too taste no cooking necessary.
for the fry:
on medium high saute zucchini and squash together in cast iron fry pan with enough butter to cover the bottom of the pan move to a bowl
add the meat to the pan stir until no more pink is visible add Chipotle mango chile sauce
add the spinach to the meat and stir two minuets before adding the zucchini into the meat mix
add tomatoes and top with cheese
put a cover on it and cook for 10 minuets
