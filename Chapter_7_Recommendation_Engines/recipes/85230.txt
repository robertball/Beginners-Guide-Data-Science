Title:
Vanilla Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup olive oil
3 tablespoons white wine vinegar
1 teaspoon McCormick® Pure Vanilla Extract
1 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Tarragon Leaves
1/4 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Coarse Grind Black Pepper
1/2 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients. Mix well.
Chill before tossing with salad.

Submitter: 
McCormick® Gourmet Collection®
Description: 
Perfect as a dressing for a salad of baby greens with strawberries or grape tomatoes. Add toasted walnuts, if desired.
