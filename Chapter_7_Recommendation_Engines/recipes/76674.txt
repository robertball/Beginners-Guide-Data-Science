Title:
Cinnamon Apple Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985024.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups sifted all-purpose enriched flour
2 tablespoons sugar
4 teaspoons double-acting baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup milk
1/2 cup CRISCO® Oil
2 cups chopped, pared apples
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup CRISCO® Oil
1/2 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
Combine flour, 2 tablespoons sugar, baking powder and salt. Add milk and 1/2 cup CRISCO(R) Oil; stir just enough to hold dough together.
Place on lightly floured surface and knead 10-12 strokes. Roll to 1/4-inch thick rectangle. Cover dough with combined apples, 1/2 cup brown sugar and cinnamon.
Roll up jelly roll fashion. Cut into 1-inch thick pieces.
Combine 1 cup brown sugar, 1/2 cup CRISCO(R) Oil and nuts in bottom of 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan. Place rolls in pan
Bake at 425 degrees F for 15-20 minutes, until desired brownness. Immediately turn upside down onto large platter or cookie sheet. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Crisco® Olive Oil
Description: 
Warm apples and cinnamon combine to make this a perfect cold-weather dessert that's sure to leave a sweet aroma.
