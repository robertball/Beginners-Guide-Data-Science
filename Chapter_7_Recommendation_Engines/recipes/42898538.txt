Title:
Pop Overs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/922114.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup sifted flour
1 cup milk
1/2 tsp salt
2 eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425
Beat incredients together just til smooth
Pour into well greased cup cake pan filling each cup 1/2 way
Bake 40-45 min.
Serve at once.
