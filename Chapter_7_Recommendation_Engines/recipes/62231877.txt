Title:
Baked Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. Potatoes (~3 medium) - cooked, peeled, and cubed
1 1/2 c. Chicken broth
1 Tbl. Butter
1 Tbl. Flour
1/4 tsp. Dill Weed
1/8 tsp. Salt
Dash of Pepper
1 c. Milk
3/4 c. Broccoli Florets - cooked
2 slices Bacon - cooked and crumbled
1/2 c. Sour cream
1/2 c. Cheddar cheese - shredded
1 Green onion - chopped

Directions:
In a bowl combine 1/2 potatoes and 1/2 chicken broth.  Mash or blend until almost smooth.  Add remaining potatoes and broth, set aside.
In a saucepan over medium heat melt butter.  Stir in flour, dill weed, salt and pepper.  Add milk.  Cook and stir until slightly thickened and bubbly.  Cook 1 minute more.
Stir in potato mixture, broccoli, bacon and green onion.  Cook until heated through.  Add sour cream and cheese, serve once cheese is melted.
Garnish with additional sour cream, cheese, and green onion.
