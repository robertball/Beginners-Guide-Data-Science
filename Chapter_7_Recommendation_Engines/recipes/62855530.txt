Title:
Neopolitan Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup shredded Monterrey jack cheese
3 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 cup minced fresh basil
1/4 cup minced fresh parsley
2 teaspoons minced fresh oregano
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon pepper
2 tablespoons olive oil or vegetable oil, divided
6 cups thinly sliced red potatoes

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first eight ingredients; set aside. Grease a shallow 2-1/2-qt. baking dish with 1 tablespoon oil. Place one third of the potatoes in a single layer in dish. Sprinkle with a third of the cheese mixture. Repeat layers twice. Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 55-65 minutes or until the potatoes are tender.
