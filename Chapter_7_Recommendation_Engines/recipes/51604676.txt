Title:
Linda’s White Chocolate and Cranberry Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. flour
1 T. baking powder
1/2 t. salt
1/4 c. sugar
3/4 c. mix-ins (mixture of craisins and white chocolate chips)
1 1/2 c. heavy cream

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients.
Add mix-ins.
Pour cream in and stir until mixture forms a soft, sticky dough.
Pour onto a lightly floured board.
Knead 8-10 times.
Pat into a circle 1" thick.
Cut into 8 wedges.
Put on ungreased cookie sheet or parchment paper 1" apart.
Brush with cream and sprinkle with sugar.
Bake at 425° for 15 minutes or until springy when touched.
