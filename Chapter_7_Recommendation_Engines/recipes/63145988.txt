Title:
Feather Light muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c shortening
1/2 c sugar
1 1/2 c flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
1/2 c milk
Topping:
1/2 c sugar
1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
1.  In a mixing bowl, cream shortening, sugar, and egg.
2.  Combine dry ingred: add to creamed mixture alternately with milk.
3.  Fill greased muffin tins 2/3 full.
4. Bake at 325 for 20-25 min. or until golden.
5. Let cool for 3-4 min.  Meanwhile, combine sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl.  Roll muffins in melted butter, then in sugar mixture.
