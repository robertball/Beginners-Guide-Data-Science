Title:
chorizo burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Trader Joe's vegetarian chorizo, crumbled
2 onions, chopped
2 bell peppers, chopped
green chile chopped, to taste
3 small red potatoes, cooked al dente, cooled
6-8 tortillas

Directions:
Saute onions and peppers till soft.  Add chorizo and green chile.  Heat thoroughly.
Cut potatoes in bite-sized pieces.  Gently fold in to mixture.
Serve in tortillas.
