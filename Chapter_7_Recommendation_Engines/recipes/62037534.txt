Title:
Company Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6/7 medium RED potatoes, 8-oz. Philly cream cheese, 1 stick melted butter(not oleo),1 cup sour cream, 1 t salt, 1/2 t pepper, 1/4 t garlic chips, buttler slices to put on very top.

Directions:
Cook and peel potatoes, drain. Mash potatoes using NO milk just the melted butter. Beat in cream cheese sour cream and seasonings. Put in greased caserole (9 x 13), dot with thin butter slices and bake 350 degrees for 45 minutes. May freeze, but needs to be room temp. before baking.
