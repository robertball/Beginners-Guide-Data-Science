Title:
Curried Tofu Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package EXTRA FIRM tofu
1 large chopped green onion
1 large grated carrot
1 chopped red bell pepper
1/4 cup peanuts
1/4 cup dates or raisins
1 chopped garlic clove
SAUCE:
1 tsp curry powder
1 tbs soy sauce
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
1 tbs vinegar (white or rice)
1 tbs lemon juice
2 tbs mayonnaise

Directions:
Slice tofu into small cubes
Place tofu onto lightly oiled cookie sheet
Place in preheated oven (350 degrees) for 25 minutes, stirring half way through cooking time.
Remove from oven and cool tofu.
Mix curry, lemon juice,mayonaise, vinegar, red pepper and soy sauce in small bowl.
Set aside.
In large bowl combine tofu, carrot, red pepper, peanuts, dates and green onion.
Top with sauce and mix thoroughly.
You can chill, if you like, for an hour before serving.
Serve on lettuce, with crackers, in pita, on toast or in a wrap.
Pairs well with couscous, quinoa, or rice pilaf and a red cabbage slaw.

Description: 
A great vegetarian alternative to tuna or chicken salad. Sweet and spicy!
