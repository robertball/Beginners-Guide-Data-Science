Title:
Pineapple Teriyaki Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1C dry quinoa (rinsed and dry pan toasted)
Mix the following:
2 C veg broth
1/2 tsp poultry seasoning
3 1/2 T low sodium soy sauce
1 heaping tsp minced ginger (from a jar)
1 T raw sugar
1/2 tsp sesame oil (optional, but adds a distinctive Asian flavor)
Pour into pan with the toasted quinoa.
Add:
1 C frozen peas
1 C carrots matchsticks (I buy these packaged in produce area)
Cilantro to taste
1/2- 1 tsp salt
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp black pepper
You could add red pepper flakes or sriracha for spice
Heat to good boil. Cover. Reduce heat to medium low simmer. Cook 20 minutes or until liquid is absorbed and quinoa is able to be fluffed.
Add:
1 sweet red bell pepper, small dice
4-5 green onions small dice
1/2 C drained tidbit pineapple pieces
Stir. Cover. Allow to sit off heat 5 minutes. Plate and squeeze fresh lime juice over quinoa to serve.
