Title:
Tater Tot Hotdish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup (Regular or 98% Fat Free)
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's ® Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup (Regular or 98% Fat Free)
1/2 Can Milk or Water
2 Cups Frozen Peas or Mixed Vegetables
3 cups frozen Tater Tots

Directions:
Cook beef and onion in 10-inch skillet over medium-high heat until beef is well browned, stirring to break up meat. Pour off fat.
In a separate bowl, or in the same pan; mix soup, milk, and vegetables with hamburger mixture.
Spoon mixture into 12x8-inch shallow baking dish. Arrange potatoes on top of casserole.
Bake at 425 degrees F for 30 minutes or until potatoes are done.
