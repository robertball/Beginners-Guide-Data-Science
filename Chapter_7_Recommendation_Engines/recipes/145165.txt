Title:
Icy Lemonade Slush Recipe

Ingredients:
1 large lemon, peeled and seeded
1/2 cup SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
3 cups cold water
6 ice cubes

Directions:
Place the lemon, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, cold water, and ice cubes into the container of a blender. Blend until smooth, and serve immediately.

Description: 
With the taste of old fashioned lemonade and the icy texture of a slush, this drink is perfect for a hot summer day. Adjust the quantities to your taste.
