Title:
Microwave Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 sliced apple
2 Tbsp rolled oats
1 Tbsp brown sugar
2 tsp butter
1/8 tsp cinnamon
2 Tbsp chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Place apple in bowl.  Mix remaining ingredients together and sprinkle on apple.  Microwave on high for 4-5 minutes or until tender.  Let stand one minute.
