Title:
Butter Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
.33 Cup Butter
3-4 Tablespoon Milk
16 oz. Powdered Sugar
1 Teaspoon Vanilla

Directions:
Soften butter.  Add milk alternately with sugar stirring until smooth.  Add vanilla and any more milk to bring to desired consistency
