Title:
Budget Oven Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/265236.jpg
Ingredients:
1/8 cup olive oil
1/2  teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1 teaspoon all purpose seasoning
4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into wedges

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F
On a baking sheet place potato wedges, vegetable oil, and sprinkle garlic powder, salt and all purpose seasoning.  Using hands toss all ingredients together and spread potatoes in a single layer.
Roast for 20 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven, turning occasionally to brown on all sides.

Submitter: 
CookingOnABudget
Description: 
Potatoes bought in 5 or 10 lb bulk bags bake up into crispy wedges that work perfect for a side dish for any meal, breakfast or dinner.  If you have fresh herbs available in your garden, chop some up and garnish the potatoes before serving for an added special touch!Modified from: Oven Roasted Potatoes
