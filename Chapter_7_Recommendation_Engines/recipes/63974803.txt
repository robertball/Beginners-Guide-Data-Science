Title:
Grilled Spicy Skirt Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 - 1 lbs Skirt Steak, Trimmed
1 TBL Mexene Chili Powder
1 tsp ground Cumin
1 lg clove of Garlic, crushed
2 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
1 tsp Sugar
3/4 tsp freshly ground Black Pepper
1/4 tsp ground Allspice
1 TBL vegetable Oil

Directions:
Trim excess fat from Skirt Steak
Mix all dry ingredients and crushed Garlic.
Rub on both sides of steak, drizzle on Worcestershire Sauce, put in zip-lock bag and allow to rest in the refrigerator for 1-4 hrs.
Remove from frig.
Coat Steak with vegetable oil and allow to sit for 45 mins.
Grill to MR on a VERY Hot grill.
Remove, Cover with foil and REST steak for 15 mins.
Cut steak ACROSS THE GRAIN into 1/4" strips and serve on warmed Tortillas with sorted assorted "fixin's" . . .
Grated Cheese, Chopped Tomatoes, Sliced Blk Olives
Diced Onion, Salsa, etc.
