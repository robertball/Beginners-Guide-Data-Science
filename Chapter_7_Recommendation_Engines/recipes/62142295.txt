Title:
No Bean Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbs olive oil
1 pound lean ground turkey meat
1 tbs minced garlic
1/4 cup chopped onion
2 tbs chili powder
1 tbs cumin
1 can mexicorn
2 cans stewed tomatoes, any variety
1 4 oz can diced jalapenos
shredded cheese for topping

Directions:
Heat Olive Oil in large sauce pan.
Add turkey meat, garlic, onion, chili powder and cumin.  Do not drain excess fat.
Once meat is browned, add Mexicorn, both cans of stewed tomatoes and half can of jalapenos.
Heat thoroughly and taste test.  If needed, add additional chili powder, cumin or jalapenos.
Serve hot with shredded cheese on top!
