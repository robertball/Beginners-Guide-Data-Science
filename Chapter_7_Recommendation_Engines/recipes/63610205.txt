Title:
Easy Cheese and Ham Scalloped Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 - 8 oval butter crackers
2 tablespoons butter
5 Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
1 small onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 quart heavy cream (35% + butterfat)
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup flour plus 2 tablespoons (reserved)
1 - 2 tablespoons chicken broth or stock (if needed)
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder (if desired)
1/4 teaspoon onion powder (if desired)
1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1 1/2 cups cubed fully cooked ham

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Create butter topping (if desired) by melting butter in a small saucepan.
Chop potatoes, onions and garlic (as needed) and set aside.
Melt butter until melted but not brown. Using flour create a thick roux. Add more flour as needed.
Slowly pour cream into the mixture until a paste emerges. Add cheese a a tablespoon at a time and stir in until well incorporated.
Create a layer of potatoes, onion, garlic and ham in 9 X 13 baking dish.
Create a second layer of potatoes, onion, garlic and ham.
On the top place a layer of sauce, slather well.
At the half hour cooking point add the buttery cracker mixture.
Bake in the preheated oven until the potatoes are tender, about 1 hour.
