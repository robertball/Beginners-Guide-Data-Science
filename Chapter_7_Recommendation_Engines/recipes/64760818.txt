Title:
Mustard Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 qts cucumbers (peeled & seeded)
2 qts onions
2 qts cabbage
1 red pepper
1 green pepper
12 cups sugar
2 qts vinegar
4 tsp. celery seed
4 tbsp. salt
6 tsp. tumeric
8 tbsp. dry mustard
1 1/2 cups flour
1 tbsp. sugar

Directions:
Wash and grind (fine chop in food processor) all the veggies. Measure after grinding.
Cook all ingredients together, EXCEPT flour & second amount of sugar . Let them cook down (reduce), approx. 45 min.
Mix flour and second amount of sugar into a paste.
Mix flour mixture into hot veggies. Cook a little longer, till sauce is clear and thick.
Put into jars.
Makes 8 quarts

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
An all-time favorite relish recipe that I haven't been able to find at farmer's markets anywhere
