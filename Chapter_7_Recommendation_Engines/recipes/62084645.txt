Title:
Thai Beef-Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 each 1-lb. Flank steak
Olive oil, salt and pepper to taste
Salad:
1/4 cup red onion, sliced thinly
2 or 3 whole tomatoes, cut in wedges
1/2 cup sliced cucumber
2 whole Thai Chiles, sliced into
rounds (seeded if you prefer it
milder)
*if you cannot find Thai chiles, you may use Jalapeno or Habanero peppers
DRESSING:
1/4 cup Thai Fish Sauce
1/4 cup  fresh Lime Juice
1 T. soy sauce
2 cloves minced garlic
3 T. fresh Ginger, minced
3 T. fresh Cilantro, chopped (remove
stems before chopping)
5 scallions, chopped
1/4 cup Red Onion, peeled/chopped
1 Tbl. Asian Sesame Oil
1 Tbl Asian Chile Oil or Chile sauce
Extra lime juice

Directions:
Rub steak with olive oil, salt and pepper.  Grill or broil until done as you desire.  Let cool, then slice into thin strips.
Arrange veggies on a platter or shallow bowl.  Lay steak strips over veggies.  Mix all dressing ingredients together and pour over beef.  Sprinkle additional lime juice over all!  Eat and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Sherbg
Description: 
A very refreshing salad-- you can change the ingredients to suit your own taste, and alter the amount of heat or spice you add.  A nice main course dish or potluck contribution.
