Title:
Mint Melt Aways Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
A - ¾ cup butter
A - 1 ½ cups brown sugar
A  2 Tbsp. crème de menthe liqueur
B  12 oz. bag chocolate chips
C  2 eggs
C  2 ¾ cups flour
C  ½ tsp. salt
C  1 ¼ tsp. baking soda
24 Andes Mints

Directions:
In sauce pan melt A’s stirring occasionally. Add B’s stir until melted – let stand 10 minutes. Add C’s and mix to form soft dough. Wrap dough and chill at least 1 hour.
Roll into 1-inch balls – place on lightly greased cookie sheet. Leave ample space. Bake at 350F for 8-9 minutes.
Remove cookies. Place ½ Andes Mint on top of cookie and let melt some and use back of spoon to swirl.
