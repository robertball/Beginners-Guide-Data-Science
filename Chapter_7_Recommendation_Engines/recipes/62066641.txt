Title:
Hot Spinach Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup light (not fat free) mayonnaise
1/2 cup light sour cream
1/2 cup light cream cheese
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
2 cups shredded Monteray Jack cheese
1 teaspoon onion salt
1 teaspoon garlic powder
2 cans artichoke hearts drained and chopped
1 package frozen chopped spinach thawed and drained well
2 whole roasted red peppers diced

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2) Combine mayonnaise, sour cream, cream cheese.  Mix well.
3) Add Cheddar cheese, Monterary Jack cheese, onion salt, garlic powder, artichoke hearts, spinach, and roasted red peppers.  Mix well and pour into a 2 liter baking dish.
4) Bake uncovered for 30-35 minutes until lightly browned on top and cheese is bubbling.  Serve warm with pita chips, Triscuits, or tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
TISHUBA
Description: 
A lightened version of this favorite with roasted red peppers for added taste and extra color.  Perfect for Christmas time when you are hosting a big crowd!
