Title:
Crazy Cook's Gluten Free Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Boneless Chicken breasts
1/8 pound thinly sliced pieces of prosciutto
3 cloves of garlic (minced)
1 tablespoon dijon mustard
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1/2 cup extra vergin olive oil
6 oz gluten free spaghetti
dash of salt
dash of ground pepper

Directions:
Put mustard, salt, pepper, and minced garlic in a medium sized bowl. Then add olive oil and red wine vinegar to the bowl. Mix well.
Rinse off chicken and put in a bowl or plasic bag. Add half of the olive oil mixure to the chicken.
Preheat the stove to medium-high heat. When hot, add chicken.
Flip chicken as needed until cooked.
While cooking the chicken, cook the prosciutto in a small pan on meduim heat. Cook until brown. Once done, place on a plate to cool.
While cooking the prosciutto and chicken, bring water to a boil.
Add spaghetti. Cook for the amount of time on the package. Drain well.
Chop up the chicken and prosciutto into bite size pieces.
Add the chicken and prosciutto to spaghetti. Stir well.
When plating, drizzle the remaining olive oil mixure over the spaghetti.
ENJOY!
