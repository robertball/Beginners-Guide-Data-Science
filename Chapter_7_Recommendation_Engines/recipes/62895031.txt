Title:
Spicy Thai Tuna dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/700268.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small can of tuna in water
palm full of finely diced onion
1/3 cup of diced cucumber
1/3 cup of canned corn
palm full of chopped fresh cilantro
juice from half a lime
1/4 tsp chopped garlic
1/4 tsp chopped ginger
1/4 tsp Dijon mustard
1 Tbs of hoisin sauce
1 Tbs of tamari sauce
1 tsp (or more depending on how spicy you like it) of srirachi sauce
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients thoroughly, eat with chips

Submitter: 
Diania Merriam
Description: 
I discovered this one night while throwing together various leftover ingredients from other meals.  It's based on a single serving of canned tuna.  When increasing the serving size, be conservative with the spices and flavorings. It's easy to overwhelm when combining bold flavors.
