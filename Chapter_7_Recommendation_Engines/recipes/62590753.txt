Title:
Chipotle Shredded Chicken for Wraps/Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 Jalapeno chopped, seeds removed
1/2 - 7oz jar of Chipotle Peppers in Adobo Sauce (you may freeze the other half for a later time)
1 tablespoon vinegar
2 tablespoons hot sauce (EX: Valentinas)
1 - 8oz block cream cheese, cut into squares
1 teaspoon mexican oregano
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/4 teaspoon coriander
1/4 teaspoon white pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Place chipotle peppers and adobo with vinegar into blender and blend until smooth. Set a side.
Spray slow cooker with cooking spray.  Place all
ingredients into slow cooker. About half way through cooking time, stir together.  Cook on low for 6 hours. Shred chicken after 6 hours.
Use meat for sandwiches or wraps.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This recipe is quick and easy to throw into the crockpot.  Makes great sandwiches and wraps.
