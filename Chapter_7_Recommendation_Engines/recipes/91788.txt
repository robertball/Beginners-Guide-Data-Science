Title:
Low Fat Moist Chocolate Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/21189.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Low Fat Sweetened Condensed Milk (NOT evaporated milk)
1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
4 egg whites
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Spray 13x9-inch baking pan with non-stick cooking spray.
In saucepan, over low heat, melt chocolate chips with EAGLE BRAND(R) and cocoa.  In large bowl, combine chocolate mixture and remaining ingredients.  Pour into prepared pan.
Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until center is set.  Cool.  Store tightly covered at room temperature.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
Give up fat not flavor with these chocolate brownies -- they have 25 percent less fat than regular brownies.
