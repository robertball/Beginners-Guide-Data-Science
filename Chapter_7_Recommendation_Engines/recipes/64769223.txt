Title:
Cream Cheese Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz butter
4 oz cream cheese
2 cups powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Melt butter and cream cheese.
Add vanilla.
Stir in powdered sugar.

Submitter: 
Therese Aronica
Description: 
For red velvet cupcakes, banana bread, etc.
