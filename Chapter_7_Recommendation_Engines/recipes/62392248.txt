Title:
Baklava Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/426075.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 c. almonds
1/4 c.. walnuts
1/4 c. pistachios
1/2 lemon, zested
1 T. butter
1 1/2 T. sugar
1/2 t. cinnamon
1/8 t. vanilla
1/4 c. sugar
1/4 c. honey
1/2 c. water

Directions:
Toast the nuts in a 350 degree oven for 8-10 minutes until browned and fragrant.  Chop the nuts, zest, 1 1/2  teaspoon sugar, butter, cinnamon and vanilla in the blender.  Put cups in mini muffin tins. Spread filling equally between filo cups (approximately 1 teaspoon per cup).  Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes.  Meanwhile, mix remaining sugar, honey and water in saucepan.  Cook until thickened.  Spoon 1 teaspoon per cup, then repeat with remaining liquid.  Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or the cups will be hard.

Submitter: 
KramerGirls
Description: 
This is an easy and quick dessert that has the taste of traditional baklava without the sticky mess.  It is an adaptation of Sunny's recipe.  There were a few suggestions made by those who tried the recipe.
