Title:
Toffee Thumbprint Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cookies:
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 1/3 cups butter, softened
250 g cream cheese
2 eggs
4 cups flour
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 pkg. skor toffee bits
Filling:
1/4 cup whipping cream
3/4 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 tbsp. corn syrup

Directions:
Beat sugar, butter and cream cheese till light and fluffy.
Beat in eggs.
Mix flour, baking powder and salt; add to butter mixture; mixing well.
Stir in toffee bits.
Chill x 1 hr.
Roll dough into 1" balls; place 2" apart on baking sheets. Make an indentation in center of each ball for filling.
Bake @ 350 x 10 min. or until edges are golden.
Remove from the oven and reinforce the indents again.
Cool on baking sheet x 5 minutes; move to wire rack to cool completely.
Filling:
Meanwhile heat the whipping cream until steaming.
Pour over chocolate chips; whisk until melted and smooth.
Stir in corn syrup.
Spoon about 1/2 tsp. into each cooled cookie.
Let stand until set (ok, you can sample them before the filling cools!).
Makes about 6 doz.
