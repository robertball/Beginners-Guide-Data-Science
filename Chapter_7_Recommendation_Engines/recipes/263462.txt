Title:
Easy Swiss Chard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5264210.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
1 bunch red Swiss chard, stems and center ribs cut out and chopped together, leaves coarsely chopped separately
salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
1 pinch ground nutmeg, or to taste

Directions:
Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat and cook chard stems until softened, about 4 minutes. Add leaves and cook until wilted, about 5 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.

Submitter: 
Lena
Description: 
This is a simple way to prepare Swiss chard or rainbow chard which makes a great side for meat or fish dishes.
