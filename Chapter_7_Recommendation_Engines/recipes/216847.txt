Title:
Chicken with Creamy Roasted Poblanos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988661.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3 pound) broiler-fryer chicken, cut up
1 (10 ounce) tub PHILADELPHIA Original Cooking Creme
2 tablespoons milk
4 large poblano chiles, roasted, peeled, seeded and cut into strips
4 slices Oscar Mayer Baked Cooked Ham, chopped
1 tomato, seeded, chopped

Directions:
Cook chicken in large skillet on medium heat 10 min. or until browned on both sides, turning occasionally. Cover; cook 10 min. or until done (165 degrees F.) Drain any liquid from skillet.
Stir in cooking cream, milk, chiles and ham; cook, covered, on medium-low heat 2 to 3 min. or until heated through, stirring occasionally.
Top with tomatoes just before serving.

Submitter: 
PHILADELPHIA Cooking Creme
Description: 
PHILADELPHIA Original Cooking Creme complements mild poblano peppers in this flavorful chicken dish.
