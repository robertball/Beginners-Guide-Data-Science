Title:
Hula's Oatmeal Peanut Butter Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cookie Part
1/2 cup soft butter
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 egg
3 1/4 cup flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup quick oats
Filling Stuff
3 TBSP soft butter
1 cup powder sugar
1/2 cup peanut butter
3 TBSP heavy whipping cream

Directions:
Set oven to 350
For the cookie part
Cream 1/2 cup butter + 1/2 cup peanut butter + 1/2 cup sugar + 1/2 cup brown sugar + 1 tsp vanilla
Add 1 egg and beat well, like show it you mean business
Combine 3 1/4 cup flour + 1/2 tsp baking soda + 1/4 tsp baking powder + 1/2 tsp salt then mix into wet mixture
Stir/blend in 1 cup quick oats
Weigh out 108gm (3.8oz) cookie ball then squish a bit into cookie form
You are NOT done yet, need to make the filling for the sammich cookie.
Filling
Beat 3 TBSP soft butter + 1 cup powdered sugar + 1/2 cup peanut butter
Add 3 TBSP heavy whipping cream and keep beating till light and fluffy like a cloud
