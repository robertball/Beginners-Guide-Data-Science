Title:
Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box lasagna noodles
1 qt. Spaghetti sauce
1 lb. Hamburger, browned.
3 C. Mozzarella Cheese
1/2 C. parmesan cheese
1 container of cottage cheese
2 cans reg. Tomato sauce.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°
Spray Pan w/ cooking spray
Layer: 1: Noodles, 1/3 sauce, 1/2 meat, moz. Cheese, parm. cheese 2: Noodles, cottage cheese, parm. Cheese. 3: Noodles, 1/3 sauce, 1/2 meat, moz. Cheese, parm. Cheese 4: Noodles, 1/3 sauce, moz. Cheese.
Cover with foil. Bake 1 hr.
Uncover. Bake 15 min. (cheese will become golden brown)
Let stand 15 min. before cutting.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
This is a great recipe because you do not have to cook the noodles before hand. They cook with the sauces and moisture.
