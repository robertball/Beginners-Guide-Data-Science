Title:
Buttermilk Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 x all purpose flour
3 t baking powder
1/2 t salt
1/4 c shortening
3/4 c milk

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 425'.  In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
2.  Blend in shortening until mixture looks like coarse crumbs.
3.  Stir in the buttermilk quickly.
4.  Sprinkle area with flour.  Turn dough out onto area and knead lightly 20-25 x's.  Pat out to 1/2 inch thick.
5.  Cut out biscuits with a glass and arrange on a ungreased baking sheet an inch apart.
6.  Bake for 10 - 12 minutes or until light brown and puffy.
7.  Serve warm.
