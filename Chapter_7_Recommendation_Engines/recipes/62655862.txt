Title:
Easy Cheesy Chicken Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (6 oz.) Stove Top Stuffing Mix for Chicken
1 1/2 lb. boneless skinless chicken breast, but into 1 inch pieces.
1 bag (14 oz.) frozen boccoli florets, thawed, drained.
1 can (10 3/4 oz.) condensed cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup milk
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
Prepare stuffing mix as directed on package, set aside.
Mix chicken and broccoli in 13x9 baking dish.
Stir in soup, milk, and cheese.  Top with prepared stuffing mix.
Bake 30 minutes, or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Lance Hinkle
Description: 
This is a quick and easy chicken bake, using Stove Top Stuffing.
