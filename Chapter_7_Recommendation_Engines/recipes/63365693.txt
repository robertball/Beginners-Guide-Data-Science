Title:
Baked Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz cream cheese
1 tsp garlic salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/2 C sour cream
2.5-3 pounds potatoes
2 T butter
paprika

Directions:
Boil the potatoes. Mash them up with everything except the paprika. Spread the mash into a 9x13 pan, sprinkle with paprika. Bake at 400degF covered for 25 minutes, and then uncovered for 10 minutes more.
