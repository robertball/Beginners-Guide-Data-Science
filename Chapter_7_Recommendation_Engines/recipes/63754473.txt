Title:
Stuffed Baked Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1041729.jpg
Ingredients:
4 large baking apples
1/3 cup dried fruit (I used cranberries, but dates, raisins, or any fruit should work)
1/3 cup sliced, toasted almonds (or any nut you prefer)
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/4 cup melted butter
1 1/2 tablespoons honey
1/4 cup water (or apple juice)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*
Core apples and peel a strip from the top of each
Mix together all the remaining ingredients except water
Fill each cavity with mixture and place in baking dish
Add the water to leftover filling and pour around the apples.
Bake for 50-60 minutes, basting twice, or until apples are done.
Serve warm with ice cream or half&half if desired.
Spoon the sauce from the baking dish over the ice cream.
