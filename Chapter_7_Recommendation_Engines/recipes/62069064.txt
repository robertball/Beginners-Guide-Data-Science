Title:
Lee's Special Rice Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chicken stock
2 tablespoons olive oil
Â½ large onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
1 cup long-grain white rice; preferably basmati or jasmine
1 teaspoon curry
1/4 cup golden raisins
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup pine nuts
1 large plum tomato, chopped
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
1. In a medium saucepan, simmer chicken stock. In a large pot heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; cook until softened.
2. Add rice and curry to the pot with the onion and garlic. Stir until rice is lightly browned and coated. Add warm broth, raisins and salt. Boil over high heat, stir, reduce to simmer. Cook, covered, until liquid evaporates, 15-20 minutes.
3. In a small frying pan over medium-high heat toast the pine nuts for 2-3 minutes, shaking and tossing. Remove immediately.
4. Stir the pine nuts, tomato, parsley, and cinnamon into the cooked rice. Adjust seasoning. Add pepper to taste.
