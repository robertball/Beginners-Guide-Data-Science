Title:
SunButter® Apple Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4518110.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons SunButter®, any variety
1 large apple, cored and sliced into 6 rings
6 tablespoons granola
Chocolate chips or raisins (optional)

Directions:
Spread SunButter(R) on apple slice, sprinkle with granola and chocolate chips or raisins if desired. Top with another apple slice. Repeat using remaining apple slices.

Submitter: 
SunButter®
Description: 
Quick, easy, and peanut-free! Serve this delicious SunButter Sunflower Butter snack to your kids after school, or make them for yourself!
