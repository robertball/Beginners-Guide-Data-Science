Title:
Garlic Mashed Potatoes Secret Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds unpeeled red potatoes, quartered
1/2 pounds butter, room temperature
3 oz Romano cheese, grated
3 cloves chopped garlic (roasted or saut√©ed)
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 tbsp. dried dill

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.  Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm,  about 45 minutes; drain. Meanwhile, drizzle peeled garlic with olive oil, wrap in foil and roast in 375-degree oven for 35 minutes. Stir in butter, cheese, garlic, salt and pepper. Mash with a potato masher until lumpy. Be careful not to overmix.
