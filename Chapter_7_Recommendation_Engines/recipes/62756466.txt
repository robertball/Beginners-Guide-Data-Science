Title:
Andhra-style Curry powder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c whole coriander seeds
15 whole dry red chilies
1/2 T cumin seed
1/2 T mustard seed
1/2 T fenugreek seed
1/2 T black peppercorns
15-20 curry leaves
3 T turmeric powder

Directions:
Dry the curry leaves by dry frying in an ungreased pan over low heat, about 4 to 5 mins.
Mix whole spices and the dried curry leaves in an electric coffee grinder and grind to a fine powder.  It may take more than one batch, depending on the size of your grinder.
Mix in the turmeric
Store in an airtight jar.

Submitter: 
KitchenBarbarian
Description: 
A curry powder mix much like the 777 brand curry powder that was a staple in my mother-in-law's house.
