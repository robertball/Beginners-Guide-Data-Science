Title:
Cheesy Broccoli Pasta Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2lb medium pasta (penne, ziti, etc.)
8c fresh broccoli florets
1T olive oil
1 small onion chopped
2T flour
1t dry mustard
1-2/3c milk
1/2t salt
1c shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 and spray a 9x13 dish with nonstick spray.
Cook pasta about 5 minutes.
Add broccoli and cook 3-4 additional minutes.
Drain and return to pot.
While pasta and broccoli are cooking, heat oil in a large saucepan.
Cook onion until tender.
Add flour and mustard; cook about 1 minute.
Whisk in milk and salt gradually.
Increase heat and cook until sauce boils and thickens.
Add sauce to pasta/broccoli and stir.
Place in baking dish and top with cheese.
Bake 25 minutes.
