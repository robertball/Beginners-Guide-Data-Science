Title:
JELL-O Fruit and Cookie Dream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989129.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (6 ounce) containers strawberry flavored yogurt
1 (4 serving size) package JELL-O Strawberry Flavor Sugar Free Gelatin
3/4 cup thawed COOL WHIP LITE Whipped Topping
1 cup blueberries
1 cup raspberries
1 cup sliced fresh strawberries
6 OREO Chocolate Sandwich Cookies, crushed

Directions:
Mix yogurt and dry gelatin mix in medium bowl until well blended.
Add whipped topping; stir with wire whisk until well blended.
Spoon layers of yogurt mixture, berries and chopped cookies alternately into each of six dessert dishes. Store leftovers in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
JELL-O
Description: 
Whipped strawberry yogurt is layered with fresh berries and chopped chocolate cookies for this dreamy dessert.
