Title:
Blueberry Crumb Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup sugar
1 egg
2 cups flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup milk
2 teaspoons baking powder
2 cups blueberries
Topping:
1/2 cup sugar
1/3 cup flour
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 cup butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Cream butter and sugar until smooth
Add egg and milk
Mix together flour, baking aoda and salt and add to creamed ingredients
Carefully fold in blueberries
Spread in a greased 9" square pan
Mix topping ingredients to form pea size crumbs
Sprinkle cake with topping
Bake 45-50 minutes until done
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
LAMBR
Description: 
A wonderful easy cake made with fresh blueberries. We make this every summer when we pick fresh berries.
