Title:
Brookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (21 1/2  ounce) package fudge brownie mix
1/4  cup all-purpose flour
1/4  cup vegetable oil
3  tablespoons water
2  eggs
3/4  cup Hershey's Kissables

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
In a medium bowl combine mix, flour, oil, water, and eggs. Stir.
Fold in the Kissables.
Place teaspoonfuls of batter onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake for 12 minutes or until set. Be careful not to bake too long, or they'll be dry.
Let cool on sheet for 3 minutes, and then place on rack.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Hugatunity
Description: 
These simple, delicious cookies are made with Valentines' colored Kissables for extra sweet romance! Enjoy.
