Title:
Easy vegetarian red beans lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the lasagna:
4 oven-ready lasagna noodles
4 oz grated gruyere cheese, or swiss
1 tbsp olive oil
1 little onion, chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
1 (15 oz) can red beans
1/2 red pepper, cubed
1 (15 oz) can diced tomatoes
1 tsp dried basil
1 tsp dried oregano
Salt and pepper
For the bechamel sauce:
3 tbsp butter
3 tbsp all-purpose flour
1.5 cups milk
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Fry the diced onions in olive oil, approximatly 5 minutes. Add the garlic, salt to taste, and cook for 5 more minutes.
Add the beans, drained tomatoes, red pepper, and the herbs. Mix well and simmer 10 minutes on medium-low to medium heat.
In the meanwhile, prepare the bechamel.
Melt the butter, and add the flour. Mix well for a few minutes, then add the cold milk very slowly.
Add the parmesan and cook the sauce on low heat until slightly thick.
In a 9x9 squared dish, spread half the bean mixture, top with 2 lasagna noodles, add the remaining beans, and finally the 2 last noodles.
Cover with the bechamel sauce, and top with grated cheese.
Cook on 350F for 20 mn, or until the cheese is golden.
