Title:
Beef Tenderloin in Funghi Porcini Creamy Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb of beef tenderloin medallions
1 small red onion, finely chopped
1 teaspoon of minced garlic
2 tablespoons of butter
2 tablespoons of olive oil
1 cube of beef bouillon
20 grs of dried Funghi Porcini mushrooms
1 cup of hot water
1/2 cup of whipping cream
Salt and pepper
2 lb of baby potatoes, with skin
Olive Oil
Thyme

Directions:
Funghi Porcini Sauce:
Saute√© onion and garlic in olive oil and butter in large pan. Simmer until onion is tender and transparent. Add cube of beef bouillon and stir. Soak Funghi Porcini mushrooms in hot water and add mushrooms and water to sauce when soft. Season with salt and pepper. Add cream and simmer for 5 minutes. Reserve hot.
Beef medallions:
Season beef with salt and pepper. Fry in wok with olive oil at very high heat. Brown on both sides, leaving center pink. Place in the middle of a cooking pan. Preheat oven at 400F.
Sauteed Baby Potatoes:
Cook baby potatoes with skin in hot water with salt. Cut them in halves and sautee potatoes in olive oil until golden. Season with salt and thyme.
Cover beef medallions with funghi porcini sauce. Put potatoes around medallions. Cover with tin foil and cook in 400F oven for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Cynthia Piedra De Russell
Description: 
This is a very hearty dish, ideal for a winter dinner. It can be served just with the potatoes that the recipe calls for as a side dish, or adding your favorite veggies: they will taste great with the dried mushroom sauce!
