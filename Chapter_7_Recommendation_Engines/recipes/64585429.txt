Title:
Workout Recovery Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz chopped peeled sweet potato (2 cups)
4 oz peeled orange
3 oz coarsely chopped carrot (2 small carrots)

Directions:
Juice in electric juicer. Serve immediately for best quality or within 24 hours.
Stir bright, citrusy pulp into zucchini or banana bread batter.
Cals 245; Fat 0.5g; Pro 5g; Fiber 2g; Sugars 23g; Iron 2mg; Sodium 185 mg

Submitter:

Description: 
We were amazed at how delectably creamy the sweet potato becomes in the juicer, plus it's a bit higher in calories and loaded with potassium.
