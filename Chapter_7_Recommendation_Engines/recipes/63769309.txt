Title:
Pho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1044770.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Star Anise
1 tsp Fennel Seeds
1 Stick of Cinnamon
6 Whole Cloves
1 tsp Fried Garlic
1 tbs Cilantro
1/4 Sliced White Onion
1/8 cup Diced Green Onion
4 Leaves of Basil
1/2 Uncooked Thinly Sliced Steak
Hand full of Rice noodle
2 tbs Pho Beef Paste
1 condensed Pho cube
1/4 Lime
1 tsp Coriander
2 tsp Cardamon
Hang full of Bean Sprouts
3 cups Water

Directions:
1.Bring 3 cups of water to boil in sauce pan. 2.Prepare green onions, white onions, cilantro, and lime then set them aside for later. 3.Combine all dry herbs and place inside cheese cloth or you may place inside of water as is but you will need to strain hot liquid to get all the herbs out before serving. 4.Put beef paste and Pho cube into boiling water and stir until cube is dissolved. 5.Once dissolved place herbs into pan. 6.Allow to boil for as long as you feel the longer the better. Cook for a minimum of 15 minutes. 7.Once satisfied with flavor remove the herbs. 8.In separate pan put just enough water to cover bottom of pan. Place rice noodle in for exactly 10 seconds and place in a bowl. Best to put small amount in at a time. 9.Once you have noodles cooked place uncooked steak on top of noodles try to use a fairly large bowl so you can spread the steak out over noodles. 10.Now pour boiling broth over steak and noodles. The broth must be boiling to cook the steak in bowl. 11.Then you can place all the ingredients you set aside earlier, make sure to squeeze lime juice in bowl and place leaves of basil in also. Pho is ready to eat. Enjoy!
