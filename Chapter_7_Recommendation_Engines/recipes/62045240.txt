Title:
Banana Coconut Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Banana
1 cup of milk
3 oz. of coconut cream(not milk)
Ice Cubes

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth and thick.  If more milk, ice and/or coconut cream is needed, then add more.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Food Lover
Description: 
This is a great smoothie that I had while on vacation in a Carribbean island.  Makes you think of a beach when you drink it!
