Title:
Cajun Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/648829.jpg
Ingredients:
26 ounces free flowing salt (43 tbls)
1 1/2 ounces black pepper ground (2 1/2 tbls)
2 ounces red pepper ground (3 tbls + 1 tsp)
1 ounce garlic powder pure (1 1/2 tbls)
1 ounce chili powder (1 1/2 tbls)
1 ounce MSG (1 1/2 tbls)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients well. Use seasoning as you would salt. When it's salty enough, it's perfectly spiced.
