Title:
Double Chocolate Ice Cream Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup shortening
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup HERSHEY®'S Cocoa Powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup HERSHEY®'S MINI CHIPS™ Semisweet Chocolate
15 scoops chocolate ice cream, softened

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
Beat shortening, granulated sugar, brown sugar, eggs and vanilla in large bowl until creamy. Stir together flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt; add to egg mixture.  Stir in chocolate chips.  Drop by heaping tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheet.  Flatten each with palm of hand or bottom of glass into a 3-inch circle about 1/4-inch thick.  (Add 2 tablespoons flour if dough is too soft to handle.)
Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until almost set.  Cool 1 minute; remove from cookie sheet to wire rack.  Cool completely.
Place scoop of slightly softened ice cream on flat side of one cookie; spread evenly.  Top with second cookie, pressing lightly.  Wrap in foil; immediately place in freezer.  Freeze until firm.

Chocolate, chocolate chip cookies with ice cream sandwiched between -- a fabulous, frozen treat.
