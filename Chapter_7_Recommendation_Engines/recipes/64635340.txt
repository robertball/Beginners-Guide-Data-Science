Title:
Cornbread Stuffed Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4491720.jpg
Ingredients:
Stuffing:
2 C  crumbled corn bread or muffins
1/2 LB crumbled or chopped bacon
1 red bell pepper diced
1 TB diced jalapeno
2 TB minced garlic
2 TB dried parsley
salt & pepper
1 egg beaten
Meatloaf:
3 TB EVOO
1 C diced red onion
1 TB diced jalapeno
2 TB minced garlic
2 LB ground beef
1LB pork
2tsp sea salt
2 tsp black pepper
2TB dried parsley
1TB dried tyme
1tsp mustard
1/4C ketchup
1TB Worcestershire
2 eggs
4 slices of cheese (your preference)

Directions:
toast bread crumbs
brown the bacon, drain and in the same skillet add the red pepper jalapeno and garlic, cook until soft.
in a bowl combine the cornbread crumbs, bacon, and veggie mixture add the parsley, salt and pepper to taste and the egg.  Mix together
Meatloaf:
med skillet add oil and cook red onions, jalapeno, and garlic until soft then cool
in a bowl combine the meat, S&P, parsley, thyme, mustard, ketchup, Worcestershire, eggs  & mix
divide meat mix in half in bread pan, creating a canoe, loosely fill the stuffing then cover with the other 1/2 of the meat mixture.  put it in a preheated oven on 350 uncovered for 1 1/2 hrs.  the last 5 min place the cheese on top and place back in the oven for the remaining 5 min.
