Title:
Emily's apple dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 peeled and sliced apples
1/2 stick butter
half of a box of white cake mix
1 t cinnamon
1 t granulated sugar
cooking spray

Directions:
preheat oven to 350, spray cake pan, spread apples in the bottom, spread cake mix over the apples, sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar, pour butter over it, bake for 30 minutes at 350

Submitter: 
Laura Leh
Description: 
My daughter loves to make this...very simple and tasty.  All of my kids love to eat it.
