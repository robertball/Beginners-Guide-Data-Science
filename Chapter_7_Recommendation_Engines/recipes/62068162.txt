Title:
better then spinach dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package frozen turnip greens
a pachage hidden valley FIESTA dip mix
1 8 oz container sour cream
1 1/2 cups of shredded cheese
1 tsp olive oil
3 Tablespoons mayo
1/2 small onion
1 tablespoon chopped garlic
salt / pepper to taste

Directions:
mix the FIESTA dip and the sour cream
put about 3 tablespoons aside and save the rest for a veggie or chip dip
steam the turnip greens and drain off excess juices
in a small pan brown the onion and garlic
add the steamed turnip greens
add the mayo, cheese, sour cream salt and pepper
place in a 8x8 baking dish and heat thoroughly.

Submitter: 
Renee' Levins
Description: 
I had planned to make spinich dip but no frozen spinach.  I had to substitute for a last minute party. I only had turnip greens on hand and to my suprise it turned out even yummier than original spinach dip recipe and my house guests loved it.
