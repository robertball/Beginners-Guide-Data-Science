Title:
Zucchini banana bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup vegetable oil
2 cups sugar
4 eggs
1 1/2 cups grated zucchini
4  medium bananas, mashed
1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Grease and flour 2 10" loaf pans. Preheat oven to 300f. In a large bowl, combine oil and sugar. Blend well. Add eggs. Blend well. Blend in zucchini and bananas.
In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
Gradually blend the flour mixture into the large bowl. Stir in nuts, if desired.
Pour into prepared pans. Bake for 1 hour or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
