Title:
Gluten Free Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup margarine
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 well beaten eggs
1 1/4 cup rice flour
1 tbsp xanthan gum
1 tsp baking powder (gluten free)
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ginger
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp allspice
1/4 tsp salt
3 cups rolled oats
1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Lightly grease cookie sheet
Melt margarine over medium heat
Remove from heat and stir in sugars
Cool 5 minutes
Add eggs one at a time, beating by hand until smooth
Stir in flour, xanthan gum, baking powder and baking soda Stir in salt, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg and allspice
Add oats, stir until combined (batter will be thick)
Stir in chocolate chips
Drop onto cookies sheets
Bake for 7-9 minutes at 375

Submitter: 
Irene
Description: 
Delicious Cookies, made in one saucepan.
Make sure your oats and baking powder are gluten free
