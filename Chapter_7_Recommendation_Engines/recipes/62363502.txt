Title:
Peanut Butter Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup creamy peanut butter
4 cups sifted powdered sugar
1/4 cup milk
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
In a bowl beat peanut butter until fluffy.
Gradually add 2 cups of the powdered sugar, beating well.
Slowly beat in the milk and vanilla.
Slowly beat in more sugar (and additional milk, if necessary) till of spreading consistency. (You may not need the full 4 cups of sugar).

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
This is the perfect frosting for chocolate or banana cakes!
