Title:
Versatile Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (1 pound) loaf white bread, torn into small pieces
1 quart hot milk
3 eggs, beaten
2 cups SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
2 tablespoons vanilla extract
1 cup golden raisins
3 tablespoons margarine

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large mixing bowl, pour hot milk over bread. Blend eggs, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, and vanilla. Stir egg mixture into the bread mixture and blend well. Add raisins. Mix well.
Melt the margarine and pour into a 9x13-inch pan. Pour bread mixture over margarine. Bake for 40 minutes, or until firm and golden brown. Serve hot or cold.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
Try substituting a good-quality eggnog for the milk (or part of it) if you are making this for Christmas. Otherwise, enjoy this just as it is!
