Title:
Bok Choy Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 baby bok choy
1 T of olive oil
1 to 2 cloves of minced garlic
1 t of minced ginger or 1/4 t of powdered
2 T of water

Directions:
seperate bok choy and clean. set aside
add oil to hot wok or large fry pan
add garlic and ginger to hot oil, mix in
add bok choy and stir fry to coat
add water and cover for a 2 minutes or till desired texture
Serve promptly

Submitter: 
fred
Description: 
goes nicely with any meat, quick and easy.
