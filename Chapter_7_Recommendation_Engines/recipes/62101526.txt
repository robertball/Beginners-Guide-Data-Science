Title:
"Italian" [French] Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 thin slices of Italian bread
3/4 c melted butter
1/2 tbsp cinnamon (optional)
Honey (for dipping)

Directions:
Heat large griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
Brush each slice of Italian bread with melted butter. Sprinkle with cinnamon, if preferred.
Place in pan and cook, 1-2 minutes each side.
Cut each slice into smaller pieces for easy dipping. Serve hot with honey on the side. Warm honey in the microwave for a few seconds if desired.

Submitter: 
even_vegan
Description: 
A lovely spin on your traditional French Toast using just Italian bread, butter & honey. Cinnamon can also be added if preferred.
