Title:
chili (chile) con queso dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of Velveeta cheese (1 lb.)
1 can of Rotel
3/4 cups milk

Directions:
Cut up the velveeta into small cubes.
In a medium saucepan over low heat, combine the velveeta and milk stir continuosly until melted.
Once cheese is melted stir in the can of Rotel.
Serve with tostadas(tortilla chips) and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Melony1976
Description: 
This is a cheese dip that you eat with tostadas. Here I made it with Rotel which is mild and good on flavor. If you want it spicy you can add in your favorite mexican salsa. You can use any processed cheese but if you want the best results and taste use Velveeta, trust me.
