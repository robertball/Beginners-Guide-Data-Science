Title:
Egg Nog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984138.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Baileys Irish Cream
1/4 ounce Bushmills Original Irish Whiskey
1 whole egg
2 cups milk
1 pinch nutmeg

Directions:
Blend all ingredients (except milk) until smooth and pour into a heat-proof mug.
Add boiling milk, sprinkle with nutmeg and serve.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Baileys Irish Cream and a little Bushmills Whiskey make this smooth and traditional eggnog taste special.
