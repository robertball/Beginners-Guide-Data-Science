Title:
Fried Onions and Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/789313.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium onions
1 cup of mushrooms
2 TBSP olive oil
dash salt
dash pepper

Directions:
Peel and slice onions to desired thickness
Wash, and cut off ends of mushrooms.
Slice mushrooms to desired thickness.
Drizzle olive oil in frying pan or griddle
Lay prepped onions and mushrooms over olive oil
Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Cook over med-high heat and occasionally stir to brown all sides of vegetables.

Submitter:

Description: 
A good topping for burgers, steak, sandwiches. The basic way to cook these two ingredients.
