Title:
FAVORITE CHOCOLATE COOKIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
1&1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs
2 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups all purpose flour
2/3 cup baking cocoa
3/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 (12 oz.) package semi-sweet chocolate chips
3/4 cup chopped pecans, optional

Directions:
Cream together butter and sugar.  Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.  Beat in vanilla.  Combine flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt.  Gradually add dry ingredients to creamed mixture.  Stir in chips and pecans.  Drop by Tablespoon 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes or until set.  Remove to wire racks.

Submitter: 
Graden
Description: 
If you love chocolate, you will love this cookie!  You can substitute white or vanilla chips in place of the chocolate chips if desired.
