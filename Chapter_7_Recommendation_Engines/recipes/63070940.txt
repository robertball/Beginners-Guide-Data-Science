Title:
Penne with Balsamic Broiled Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked penne pasta
2 small zucchini
1 medium red bell pepper
1/2 cup Ken's Lite Balsamic Vinaigrette
1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
2 TBL fresh mint, chopped

Directions:
Cut zucchini and red bell pepper into 2 x 1/2" strips. Toss to coat with 3 TBL Ken's Lite Balsamic Vinaigrette. Place vegetables on a pan and sprinkle with pepper. Broil for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are just tender.
Mix vegetables with pasta, remaining vinaigrette, cheese and mint.
