Title:
Cheesey dinomite's fluffy bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1t yeast
1/4 cup warm water ( think bath water)
2t unsalted butter
2T milk
2t sugar
Pinch of salt
1 to 1.5 cups flour
4 mozzarella cheese sticks

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except  cheese
Allow to rise in warm place one hour
After an hour, roll dough on parchment paper or non stick  surface
Cut circles using a cookie it biscuit cutter, should be about sixteen
Cut cheese sticks in half place in middle of one circle
Paint outer edge off circle with melted butter (you may need more)
Add another dough circle and pinch circles down with fingers or fork
Optional sprinkle  top with basil.
Bake at 375 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes
