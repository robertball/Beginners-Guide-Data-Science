Title:
Slow Cooker Barbecue Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds pork baby back ribs
salt and pepper to taste
Bottle of BBQ sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Season ribs with salt and pepper. Place in a shallow baking pan. Brown in oven 15 minutes. Turn over, and brown another 15 minutes; drain fat.. Place ribs in slow cooker.. Pour sauce over ribs, and turn to coat.
Cover, and cook on Low 6 to 8 hours, or until ribs are tender.
When finished put back in oven to Carmalize and maybe even broil to darken...add more BBQ sauce as needed

Submitter: 
Liz VanHuss Bateman
Description: 
An easy and delicious way to prepare tender barbecued ribs without the barbecue!Modified from: Slow-Cooker Barbecue Ribs
