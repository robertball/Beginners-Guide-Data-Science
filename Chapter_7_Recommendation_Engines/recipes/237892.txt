Title:
Hilton Head Iced Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1931858.jpg
Ingredients:
ice, as needed
4 fluid ounces beer (such as Miller® High Life®)
3 fluid ounces orange juice
1/2 fluid ounce vodka
1/2 fluid ounce gin
1/2 fluid ounce white rum
1/2 fluid ounce silver tequila
1/2 fluid ounce triple sec
3 fluid ounces cola-flavored carbonated beverage (such as Coca-Cola®), or more as needed

Directions:
Fill a pitcher with ice. Add beer, orange juice, vodka, gin, rum, tequila, and triple sec; stir gently. Fill tall glasses with ice. Pour drink mixture into each glass. Top each drink off with cola.

Submitter: 
csappe
Description: 
Similar to Long Island iced tea, but with a mule kick. Great for hot and humid days.
 If you are the cook, save yours for after the biscuits come out of the oven or the steaks off the grill.
