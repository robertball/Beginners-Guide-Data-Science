Title:
Jan's Sausage Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1123164.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups finely diced red skin potatoes, skins on. (about 1/4 inch cubes)
1 (12 ounce) package of Johnsonville sausage patties
1 large onion, diced
1 tablespoon fresh, chopped parsley
salt and pepper to taste
6 over easy eggs (optional)

Directions:
Dice potatoes and set aside.
Heat sausage in a large frying pan over a medium heat Break up patties into small pieces as you go but do not crumble completely.
Once sausage is broken down add potatoes, parsley and onion. Turn heat to medium high. Cook until sausage is cooked through and nicely brown. Potatoes should be golden brown and to test for completion you should be able to smash one with a spoon when finished cooking. Onion should be translucent and a little brown. This should take about 15 - 20 minutes.
Cook eggs over easy keeping yolk from cooking through.  Gently place on top of hash.
Season with salt and pepper to taste
Serve immediately
