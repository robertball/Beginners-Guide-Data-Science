Title:
Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981794.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Cream of Shrimp Soup
1/2 teaspoon Louisiana-style hot sauce
1/4 cup finely chopped celery
1 tablespoon finely chopped onion

Directions:
Stir cream cheese until smooth. Stir in soup, hot sauce, celery and onion. Refrigerate at least 2 hours.
Serve with crackers, chips or fresh vegetables. Makes 2 1/4 cups.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
A creamy dip made with shrimp soup, cream cheese and hot sauce is perfect for scooping with veggies or crackers.
