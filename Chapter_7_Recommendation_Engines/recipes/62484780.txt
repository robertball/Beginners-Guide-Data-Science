Title:
Oven Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/488266.jpg
Ingredients:
-	2 cups Ritz crackers crumbs
-	¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
-	¼ cup chopped parsley
-	1 or 2 teaspoon of salt
-	1 clove garlic, minced
-	1/8 teaspoon pepper
-	8 chicken breast halves
-	½  cup butter, melted

Directions:
Combine cracker crumbs, cheese, parsley, garlic, salt and pepper.  Dip chicken pieces in butter and roll in crumb mixture.  Arrange chicken in shallow baking dish.  Pour remaining butter over chicken.  Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 1 hour.  Do not turn chicken.  TIP: Freeze remaining crumbs mixture for use another day.
