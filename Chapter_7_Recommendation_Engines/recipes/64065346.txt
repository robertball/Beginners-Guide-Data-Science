Title:
Jr's Grilled Baked Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large baking potatoes
Vidalia onion slices
Butter slices
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Slice potatoes from the short side almost to bottom.
Pull apart slices and salt and pepper.
Add sliced onion pieces between potato slices.
Add butter slices between potato slices.
Wrap in heavy duty foil and seal well.
Place on pre-heated grill.
Baked for 1 hour or until potatoes are soft when pressed, turning occasionally.
Remove from grill and let sit for 10 minutes before unwrapping.

Submitter: 
DebbyJean
Description: 
The onions and butter just melt into the potatoes, leaving a great, tender, moist and flavorful potato.
