Tropical Snowball Recipe

1/2 cup BACARDI速 Rum
4 fluid ounces Minute Maid速 Orange Juice
1 fluid ounce grenadine
5 cups ice

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Fuzzy Navel mix, BACARDI(R) Rum, orange juice and grenadine.
