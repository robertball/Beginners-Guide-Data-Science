Title:
Mexican Avocado Dressing (Aderezo de Aguacate) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7665056.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large avocado, peeled and pitted
1/2 cup Mexican crema
1/2 cup whole milk
1 1/2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice, or more to taste
1/2 teaspoon salt, or more to taste
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Place avocado, crema, milk, lime juice, salt, and pepper in a blender; blend until dressing is smooth.

Submitter: 
Yoly
Description: 
Avocado gives a natural creaminess to this dressing. The Mexican crema makes it more authentic and the lime juice makes it pop!
