Title:
Hominy Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbs butter
1 zucchini sliced
1 sliced tomato
1 bell pepper diced
2 Tbs flour
1Tbs chili powder
1 tsp salt
1 green pepper sliced
1 can yellow hominy drained
1 can garbanzo beans drained
1 C shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Layer ingredients
Butter deep dish. . Add sliced zucchini . Sliced tomatoes. sliced onions.
Sprinkle Mix and add as next layer:   flour, chili powder and salt
Place layers of sliced peppers, hominy, and garbanzo beans.
Top with cheddar cheese.
If casserole dish is too large, repeat layers.
Cover and bake 350 for 40 min
