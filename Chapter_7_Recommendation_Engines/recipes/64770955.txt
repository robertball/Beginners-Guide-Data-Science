Title:
Bavarian Cream Chocolate Covered Doughnut Holes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7486458.jpg
Ingredients:
Almond Bark (1 package)
Instant Vanilla Pudding (2 boxes)
Vanilla Extract (1 TBS)
Heavy Cream (2 Cups)
Whole Milk (1 Cup)
Doughnut Holes (2 packages or 24 balls)

Directions:
For the Bavarian Cream:
Mix pudding, Vanilla Extract, Heavy Cream and Whole Milk.  Set Aside
Almond Bark
Melt in a double boiler until creamy.
Using a chopstick, press a hole through your doughnut hole.  Fill with Bavarian Cream mixtures.  Dip the  cream filled doughnut into the almond bard, place on wax paper to completely dry.

Submitter: 
Michelle Hughes
Description: 
A great new holiday treat that is sure to make every child (and child at heart) happy!  Delicious pre-made doughnut holes, filled with Bavarian cream, and covered with almond bark!
