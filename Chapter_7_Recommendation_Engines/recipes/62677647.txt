Title:
Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Combine main ingredients
2 Eggs {beaten}
3/4 cup milk.
1/2 cup Bread crumbs
1/4 cup of chopped onion
1tsp Salt and 1tsp pepper
1/2 tsp sage
1 1/2 lbs of ground beef.
{TOPPING}
1/2 CUP Ketchup.
2 TBLSP Brown Sugar
1tsp dry mustard.

Directions:
Combine main ingredients mix well and cook in Loaf pan for 50 mins at 350 degrees.
while the loaf is baking, in a small dish mix together the Topping INgredients. after the 50 mins of bake time is up take the Loaf out of the Oven. Spread the topping over the loaf and Bake for 10 mins more at 350.
