Title:
KC Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. hamburger
1 can pork and beans
1 can chili beans
1 can butter beans
1/2 c. sugar
1/2 c. onions, chopped
3/4 lb. bacon
2 T. molasses
1/2 c. brown sugar

Directions:
Brown hamburger and bacon. Pour off excess grease. Break up bacon in bite sized pieces.
Add the rest of the ingredients.
Bake in oven 1 1/2 hours at 350 or in crock pot on low for 5 hours.
