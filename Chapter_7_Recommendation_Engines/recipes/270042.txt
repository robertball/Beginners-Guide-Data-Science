Title:
5-Minute Guacamole  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2281726.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ripe avocados, finely chopped
8 cherry tomatoes, finely chopped
1/4 onion, finely chopped
1 teaspoon minced garlic
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine chopped avocado, cherry tomatoes, onion, and garlic in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Mix to combine.

Submitter: 
Alex
Description: 
This is one of my family's absolute favorite snacks, and it's way better than the guacamole you buy at the store! This recipe makes enough for about 4 people, as an appetizer or poolside snack (served with tortilla chips).
