Title:
Cherry Berry Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cherry pie filling
1 bag frozen blueberries
A lite sprinkle of cinnamon all over the fruit.
1 white cake mix
1 can sweetened  condensed milk, caramelized,"see note"
1/2 can evaporated milk
Garnish with Cool Whip or vanilla ice cream.

Directions:
In a 9 x 13 in. pan, spray with Pam, Dump cherries and berries into pan, add the cinnamon, top with cake mix and pour on the caramel sauce and the evap milk. Bake at 350 degrees for 45-50 mins. Serve warm with your choice of toppings.
