Title:
Quick Peanut Blossoms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1051478.jpg
Ingredients:
1 roll Pillsbury® refrigerated peanut butter cookies
3 tablespoons sugar
36 Hershey®'s Kisses® milk chocolates, unwrapped

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Shape dough into 1-inch balls; roll in sugar. On ungreased cookie sheets, place balls 2 inches apart.
Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until golden brown. Immediately top each cookie with 1 milk chocolate candy, pressing down firmly so cookie cracks around edge. Remove from cookie sheets to cooling racks. Cool completely before storing.

Submitter: 
Pillsbury® Cookies
Description: 
A holiday classic, these cookies are quick and simple, thanks to Pillsbury® refrigerated peanut butter cookies.
