Title:
Sweet Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1031116.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can canned chickpeas (garbanzo beans), drained with liquid reserved
5 pitted prunes, or more to taste
1/4 cup olive oil
1 tablespoon tahini
1 tablespoon stevia sugar substitute (such as TruviaÂ®)
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Combine chickpeas, 1/2 the reserved liquid, prunes, olive oil, tahini, stevia, and salt in the bowl of a food processor. Process until smooth.

Submitter: 
Thomas Michael Hyland
Description: 
This is so simple and easy, I can't believe I didn't make it sooner. Smooth and sweet, it goes great with pita chips or dipping vegetables.
