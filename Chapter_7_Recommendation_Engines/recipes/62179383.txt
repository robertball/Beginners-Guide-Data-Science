Title:
Snowballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289896.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups butter, softened
1 cup white sugar
2 tablespoons vanilla extract
4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoons salt
2 cups chopped pecans
1-1/2 cups sifted confectioners' sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
Cream the butter, sugar, and vanilla until light and fluffy.
Mix together the flour and salt; mix into the butter mixture. Stir in the pecans.
roll the dough into 1 inch balls.
Place 2 inches apart on the baking sheets and bake for 12-15 minutes or just until brown. Put on racks to cool for 15 minutes, then roll in the confectioners sugar.
