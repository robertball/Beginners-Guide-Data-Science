Title:
Raspberry Chocolate Supremes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 cup confectioners' sugar
1/2 cup butter
.
1/2 to 1 cup raspberry jam
.
3 ounces cream cheese, softened
2 tablespoons milk
1 cup white chocolate chips
.
4(1 ounce) squares semisweet chocolate, chopped
3.25 oz heavy cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375ยบ and line a 9" pan with foil and spray with Pam for baking.
Lightly spoon flour into a measuring cup. Level off.
In a bowl combine the flour and powered sugar.
Cut in butter with a fork and mix well.
Press mixture into the prepared pan.
Bake 15 to 17 minutes until lightly brown; remove from oven and score into bars while still warm.
Spread jam evenly over baked crust.
Freeze for 30 minutes or until jam is hard.
In a small bowl beat cream cheese and milk until smooth.
Melt white chocolate chips over low heat and add to mixture; beat until smooth.
Drop cream cheese mixture by teaspoons evenly over jam; spread carefully and refrigerate.
Cut chocolate squares into small pieces and melt with cream over low heat, stirring constantly.
Drizzle over white chocolate layer.  Cool completely.
Cut into bars and store in refrigerator.
