Title:
Southwestern Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/411177.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups water
4 cans (8 ounces each) tomato sauce
2 cans (16 ounces each) kidney beans, rinsed and drained
2 cans (14-1/2 ounces each) chicken broth
2 cups frozen corn
2 cups salsa
2 teaspoons dried minced onion
1 to 2 teaspoons dried oregano
1 to 2 teaspoons dried basil
2 cups (8 ounces) shredded cheddar cheese
Tortilla chips, optional

Directions:
In a slow cooker, combine the first nine ingredients.
Cook on low 6-8 hours or on high 4-6 hours.
Sprinkle individual servings with cheese.
Serve with tortilla chips and sour cream, if desired.

Description: 
Original recipe from tasteofhome.com; A Southwestern kick adds zip and zest to this tomato basil-based soup. With corn, salsa and beans, this recipe is guaranteed to spice up a cool fall night. Jean Ecos, Hartland, Wisconsin.
