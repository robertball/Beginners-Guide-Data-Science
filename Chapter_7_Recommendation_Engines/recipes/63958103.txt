Title:
Frank's RedHot Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8oz.)pkg. Cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup Frank's Original Cayenne Pepper Sauce or Frank's Buffalo Wings Sauce
1/2 cup Bleu cheese or ranch dressing
2 cups Shredded cooked chicken
1/2 cup Crumbled bleu cheese or your favorite shredded cheese

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a 1-quart baking dish.
Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
Serve with crackers and/or vegetables.
