Title:
Victoria's Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/490901.jpg
Ingredients:
4 large cucumbers, thinly sliced
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon ground black pepper
1 pinch garlic powder
1/2 cup mayonnaise

Directions:
Place the cucumber slices into a large bowl. Season with salt, pepper and garlic powder. Stir in mayonnaise until coated. Refrigerate for 20 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
SECRETV2
Description: 
This easy cucumber salad lets you turn your cucumbers into an instant side dish! A light seasoning of salt pepper and garlic powder and some mayonnaise is all you need.
