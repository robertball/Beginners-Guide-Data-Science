Title:
Biscuit and Gravy Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 pound sausage
- 1 1/2 oz pork gravy mix or 1 package Pioneer Brand Peppered Sausage Gravy Mix
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 6 eggs
- 1/2 cup of milk
- salt & pepper to taste
- 1 can "Pillsbury Grands Biscuits"

Directions:
-Preheat oven to 350 degrees
-Grease a 9x13 pan with Pam or whatever you like
-line the bottom of the pan with biscuits
-brown the sausage and scatter over biscuits
-sprinkle with cheese
-whisk eggs, milk, salt & pepper and pour over cheese
-make gravy mix per package directions and pour over egg mixture
- add more cheese to top and bake for 30 minutes or until egg is fluffy and cheese is bubbly

Submitter: 
Angela
Description: 
I got this recipe at a church potluck. All the flavors blend really well. I've made it for breakfast, lunch and dinner. So far every time I've served it, I haven't had any left overs. =D Enjoy!!!
