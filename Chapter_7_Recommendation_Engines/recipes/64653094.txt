Title:
Spagetti Meat Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4543590.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds Hamburg Ground
24 ounces Tomato Sauce
28 ounces Tomato Crushed
28 ounces Tomato Whole
6 ounces Tomato Paste
14.5 ounces Tomato Sliced Stewed (Optional)
Teaspoon Olive Oil
Green Pepper Diced
Onion Diced
6.5 ounces Mushrooms Pieces And Stems
2 Tablespoon Sugar White
2 Tablespoon Red Cooking Wine
2 Tablespoon Italian Seasoning
1 Bay leaf
3 Gloves Garlic Minced
1 Teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce (Optional)
1 Teaspoon Salt
1 Teaspoon Ground Black Pepper
5 Leaves Basil Fresh (Optional)

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion, green pepper, and garlic; cook and stir until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
Add tomatoes, mushrooms, sugar, wine, basil, Italian seasoning, bay leaf, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer
In a frying pan brown ground beef; strain and add to pot; let sauce simmer for 2 to 3 hours.

Description: 
I love Spaghetti Sauce over a spaghetti noodle or ravioli. It can  start with a good marinara sauce but has to include meat. When tomatoes are in season I like using fresh crushed tomato.
