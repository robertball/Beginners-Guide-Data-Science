Title:
Dottie's Chocolate Chip Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C. Milk
1/3 C. Sugar
2 Pkgs. dry yeast
2 eggs beaten
1/2 C. Chocolate chips
1/2 C. Margerine or butter
1 Tsp. salt
1/4 C. very warm water
3 1/2 C. sifted flour (I didn't sift and it came out ok...
Topping:
1/2 Cup Sifted flour
1/3 C. Sugar
1/3 C. Chopped nuts
1/2 C. Chocolate chips
1/4 C. butter or margerine
1 1/2 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Scald milk; stir in butter, sugar, and salt. Cool until luke-warm.
Sprinkle yeast into very warm water in large bowl or mixer. Stir to dissolve. Add milk mixture, eggs and 2 1/2 cups of flour.
Beat at medium speed until smooth (15 seconds or so). With spoon, blend in 1 cup flour and morsels.
Turn into well greased (I used Pam) 10" angel cake pan.
Sprinke with topping.
Cover; Let rise in warm place, free from draft, until doubled in bulk about 1 hour.
Bake in hot oven (400 f.) for 35 minutes.
Turn out of pan immediately; let cool on wire rack.
