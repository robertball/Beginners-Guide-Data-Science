Title:
Cappuccino Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975909.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cold fat-free milk
2 teaspoons MAXWELL HOUSE Instant Coffee
1 pkg. (4 serving size)  JELL-O Vanilla Flavor Fat Free Sugar Free Instant Reduced Calorie Pudding & Pie Filling
1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 cup thawed COOL WHIP LITE Whipped Topping

Directions:
Pour milk and instant coffee into medium bowl. Add dry pudding mix. Beat with wire whisk 2 minutes or until well blended. Pour into five dessert dishes.
Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.
Add cinnamon to whipped topping; stir with wire whisk until well blended. Spread over pudding.

Submitter: 
JELL-O
Description: 
You'll think you're sipping a rich cappuccino when you taste this creamy dessert.
