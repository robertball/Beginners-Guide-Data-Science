Title:
Creamy Cilantro Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1032148.jpg
Ingredients:
Creamy Cilantro Salad Dressing
2 medium Anaheim chilies, roasted, peeled and seeded
1/3 cup roasted pepitas (pumpkin seeds)
2 garlic cloves, peeled and crushed
¾ cup canola or peanut oil
¼ cup red wine vinegar
¼ cup grated Cotija or Parmesan cheese
1-1/2 cups real mayonnaise
¼ cup distilled water
2 bunches fresh cilantro, rinsed and stemmed
¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
½ teaspoon salt

Directions:
Place all of the ingredients except the cilantro in a food processor and blend until well blended, approximately 30 – 60 seconds.  Add the cilantro in batches and blend until well incorporated, another 30—60 seconds.  Refrigerate for an hour or more before using.
