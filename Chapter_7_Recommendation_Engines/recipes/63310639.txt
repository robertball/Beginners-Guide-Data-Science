Title:
Easy Layered Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/875278.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg refrigerated cookie dough
Peanut Butter Cups or Chocolate Sandwich Cookies
Prepared Brownie Mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
smoosh 1.5 squares of break-apart refrigerated cookie dough into the bottom of each well of a non-stick muffin pan.
Place Reese cup upside down on top of cookie dough (or an Oreo!).
Top with prepared box brownie mix, filling 3/4 full.
Bake for 18 minutes
