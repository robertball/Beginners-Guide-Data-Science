Title:
peach cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1cup of sugar
1cup of flour
1cup of milk
1stick of butter melted
1 large can of sliced peaches
Tip: also berries work great too!

Directions:
In a mixing bowl mix the flour, sugar and milk together until well blended. set aside.
Pour melted butter into the bottom of your pan. Pour the peaches and the juice in the pan. Pour the flour,sugar and milk mixture over the peaches, do not stir it. Bake on 350â€¢ for approx 40 minutes. Enjoy.
