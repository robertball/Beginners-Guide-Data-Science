Title:
How to stuff a wild zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/893189.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large zucchini, halved, seeded
Olive Oil
1 box chicken flavored rice mix
2 cloves garlic, chopped fine
2-4 tbsp. onions, chopped fine
2 links al fresco Italian Sausage, peeled, chopped
7 oz. mushrooms, coarse chopped (optional)
1/2 c. shredded Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Brush cut edges of zucchini with olive oil to coat and place in baking pan. Bake until knife inserted in side of zucchini is tender about 25-40 min. While zucchini is baking make rice mix according to pack with the following changes. After adding water and seasoning packet to mixture, add garlic and onion. Cook for approx. 10 min. or until  half of water is absorbed. Then add the sausages, mushrooms and continue to cook 7-10 min. or until water is absorbed. Fill zucchini halves with mixture and top each with Parmesan Cheese. Place in oven and cook until cheese is melted. Serve stuffing with a bit of the zucchini.

Submitter: 
Deborah Norris
Description: 
A sausage and rice stuffing for zucchini or just as a casserole.
