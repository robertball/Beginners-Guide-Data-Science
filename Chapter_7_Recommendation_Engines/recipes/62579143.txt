Title:
Pickle Wrap Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz block of Philadelphia cream cheese softened
2 pckgs of Carl Budding Ham or Corned beef-chopped
1-2 cups chopped dill pickles(depending on your preference)
1-3 tbsp of juice from pickles

Directions:
Mix all together and serve with wheat thins.
