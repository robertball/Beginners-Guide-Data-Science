Title:
Bugogi (Korean Roast Beef) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. beef tenderloin or pot roast
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 Tbs sugar
1 Tbs sesame oil
black pepper, good sprinkling
roasted sesame seeds, good sprinkling
1 green onion, finely chopped
2 Tbs soy sauce
3 Tbs water

Directions:
Put everything in a slow cooker.
Cook on low 10-12 hours.
Serve with rice and broccoli.

Description: 
Tasty, tender roast beef, serve over rice with broccoli.
