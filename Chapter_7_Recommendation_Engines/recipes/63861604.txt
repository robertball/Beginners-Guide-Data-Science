Title:
Basic Buttermilk Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. cold butter
2 c. self-rising White Lily flour
3/4 c. buttermilk
1/4 c. melted butter for brushing tops of biscuits

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Grease or spray a baking pan.
Cut butter into flour.
Gradually stir in buttermilk.
Turn dough out onto lightly floured pastry cloth.
Roll or pat dough into a rectangular shape.
Cut biscuits with biscuit cutter and place touching in a greased baking pan.
Bake for 12-14 minutes or until golden brown.
Brush with melted butter.

Submitter: 
Shearone
Description: 
A good basic buttermilk biscuit that I have been making for years.  Excellent with jams and jellies - hot out of the oven.
