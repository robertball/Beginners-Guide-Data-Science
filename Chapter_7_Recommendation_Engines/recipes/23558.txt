Title:
Sex On Miami Beach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1141284.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger vodka
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger melon liqueur
1 fluid ounce raspberry flavored liqueur
4 fluid ounces pineapple juice

Directions:
In a tall glass full of ice, pour vodka, melon liqueur and raspberry liqueur. Fill glass with pineapple juice; stir.

Submitter:

Description: 
A wonderfully fruity, cool and refreshing (albeit somewhat on the sweet side) drink that also carries quite a punch (no pun intended.)
