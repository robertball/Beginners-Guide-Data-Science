Title:
Scalloped Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork chops
1 medium onion, sliced
3 medium potatoes, sliced
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 soup can of milk
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown chops in a little oil; remove from pan.
Lay chops in an oblong baking dish.  Top with onions.
Layer potatoes over onions.
Combine soup and milk; mix and pour over chops.
Cover with foil. Bake at 400 for 50 minutes.
Remove foil, top with cheese, and continue baking until cheese is melted.

Submitter:

Description: 
Pork chops and potatoes baked in a simple sauce - a complete meal.
