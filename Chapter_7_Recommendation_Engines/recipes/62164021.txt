Title:
Chile Rellenos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can roasted mild green chiles
1 lb. Monterrey Jack cheese (grated)
1 lb. Cheddar cheese (grated)
6 eggs
5 tbsp. flour
1 8 oz. can tomato sauce

Directions:
In a 9x13 casserole dish (glass pyrex works as well), place a layer of the chiles (spread open, seeds removed) along the bottom of pan.
Combine the cheeses together and cover the layer of chiles with the combined grated cheese.
Repeat with a layer of chiles and another of cheese, reserving about 1/4 c. of cheese for later.
Separate the eggs.
Beat the egg whites until light and fluffy.
Add the flour to the egg yolks and combine.
Combine the egg whites with the yolk and flour mixture and combine until smooth.
Pour the mixture over the top of the casserole.
Bake for 35 minutes at 350 degrees.
Remove from oven and spread the tomato sauce over the top and sprinkle with the remaining cheese.
Bake an additional 15 minutes and serve.

Submitter: 
Angela Wolery-Garcia
Description: 
Chile Rellenos have always been one of my favorite dishes, but I have a large family and have found a way to savor that fluffy buttery texture much easier buy creating a casserole.
