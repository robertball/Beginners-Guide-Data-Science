Title:
Chilaquiles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Oil
1 large onion, chopped
6 cloves garlic, chopped
2 to 4 chipotle chiles packed in adobo sauce
8 Roma Tomatoes, seeded and chopped
S/P
6 Stale corn tortillas
8 eggs
1 cup Queso Fresco
1/2 cup cilantro

Directions:
Heat 1 tablespoon oil over medium heat in a large skillet.  Add onion and cook until softened, stirring frequently, about 10 min.  Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 2 mins.  Add chipotles and tomatoes and cook until tomatoes are softened and rather mushy, 10 mins. You may need to add a little water or chicken broth to puree smoothly.
Puree in blender till smooth. Season with salt and pepper. Return to pan to keep warm.
Heat about 1/2 inch of oil over medium high heat. Cut tortilla into 4 triangles and fry till golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
Fry 2 eggs to desired doneness.
Arrange tortilla chips on a plate, top with 2 eggs, cheese and cilantro.
