Title:
The Midtown Village Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/352364.jpg
Ingredients:
2 oz rye whiskey
1 oz sweet vermouth
3/4 oz ruby port
1/4 oz Meletti amaro
2 dashes Angostura bitters
2 dashes Orange bitters
1 orange twist, flamed, for garnish
3 brandied cherries skewered on a pick, for garnish

Directions:
Fill a pint glass with ice.
Add the rye whiskey, sweet vermouth, ruby port, amaro and old-fashioned and orange bitters and stir well.
Strain into a chilled coupe and garnish with the flamed orange twist and skewered cherries.
