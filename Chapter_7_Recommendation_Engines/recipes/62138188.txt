Title:
No-fail Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup margarine
2/3 cup evaporated milk
3 cups white sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1 package (300 grams) Hershey's Peanut Butter Chipits
2 cups minimarshmallows
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Melt margarine in a heavy pot over medium heat. Add evaporated milk, sugar, and salt, stirring constantly. Bring to boil, stirring to prevent burning. Boil for 5 minutes, remove from heat, and add the chipits. Stir until melted, and then add vanilla and marshmallows. Stir until completely blended. Pour into a 9x9 inch pan, cool, and enjoy!

A VERY rich and creamy fudge with a great peanut butter flavour!
