Title:
Banana & flax seed muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1544128.jpg
Ingredients:
6 large banana's
2 egg's
3/4 cup melted butter (salted)
11/2 cup all purpose flour
1 1/4 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 cup lightly ground flax seed
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp baking powder

Directions:
Mash banana's. Add melted butter, egg's and both sugars. Lightly beat or mix. Add remaining dry Ingredients and stir until combined. Scoop into lined muffin tins. Baked at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
