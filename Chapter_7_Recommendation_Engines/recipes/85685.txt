Title:
BACARDI® Mixers Bellini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984258.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 fluid ounce) can frozen BACARDI® Mixers Fuzzy Navel mix
1/2 cup champagne
5 cups ice
Peaches for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Fuzzy Navel mix, champagne and ice for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Garnish with peaches.

Submitter: 
BACARDI® Mixers
Description: 
Your guests will feel special when you prepare these delightful blended Bellinis for them. Garnish with fresh peach slices on a skewer, and soak up the summer sun.
