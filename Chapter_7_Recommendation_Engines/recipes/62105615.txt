Title:
Mexican Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 (6 in.) corn tortillas
4 small chicken breasts, cooked and cubed
1 20 oz can of enchilada sauce
1 package of taco seasoning mix
1 tablespoon of ground black pepper
1 4 oz can of diced green chiles
1 onion, diced
1 15 oz can of black beans
1 can of corn
2 cups of white rice (cooked will take less time to prepare casserole)
16 oz of shredded cheddar cheese
8 oz of shredded pepper jack cheese
1/2 cup crushed white corn tortilla chips

Directions:
1. Ensure your chicken is precooked and cubed. In large 5 qt skillet add cubed chicken and diced onion and saute with olive oil. Sprinkle with black pepper.
2. Add can of enchilada sauce, green chiles, black beans, corn, and rice to chicken. Cook for about 10 mins in skillet. Then add a handful of shredded cheese to melt into chicken mixture.
3. Spray 9x13 casserole dish with nonstick cooking spray and then line bottom of pan with 6 tortillas. Do not dip tortillas in mixture before adding to pan.
3. Spread chicken mixture over tortillas and sprinkle with shredded cheese. Top with 6 more tortillas then add more chicken mix and cheese, add third layer in same way. Leave top open (no tortilla topping) and add extra cheese. Sprinkle with crushed tortilla chips.
4. Bake in oven for approx. 10 mins or until cheese is bubbly. Let sit at least 5 mins before serving.
