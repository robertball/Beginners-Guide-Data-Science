Title:
Easy Thai Peanut Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1106387.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 ounce) pouch Progresso™ Recipe Starters™ creamy roasted garlic with chicken stock cooking sauce
1/3 cup creamy peanut butter
2 tablespoons soy sauce
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
2 tablespoons chopped dry-roasted peanuts

Directions:
In medium bowl, mix cooking sauce, peanut butter and soy sauce with whisk until smooth; set aside.
2. In 10-inch nonstick skillet, heat 1 tablespoon oil over medium heat. Cook chicken in oil about 8 minutes, until browned, turning once. Add sauce mixture to skillet; heat to boiling. Reduce heat; simmer covered 5 minutes; stirring occasionally or until the thickest part of the chicken breast reads at least 165 degrees F.
Lower heat to medium low; cook for 15 minutes, uncovered. Sprinkle with peanuts. Serve immediately.
