Title:
Cheddar Bacon Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1pkg 8oz cream cheese softened
1C sour cream
5 green onions thinly sliced
4 medium tomatoes chopped
1 large green pepper chopped
1 jar (16oz) taco sauce
2C shreded cheddar cheese
1lb sliced bacon, cooked and crumbled
Tortilla chips or Taco chips

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, beat cream chees and sour cream . Spread in an ungreased 13x9 dish.  Combine onions, tomatoes and green peppers; sprinkle over the cream cheese layer. Pour taco sauce over the vegetables. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese. Refrigerate. Just before serving sprinkle with bacon. Serve with chips.
