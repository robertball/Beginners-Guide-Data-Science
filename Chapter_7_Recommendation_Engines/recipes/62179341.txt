Title:
Creamy corn muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 package "JIFFY" Corn Muffin Mix
2 large eggs
1 can (12 oz) Cream style corn
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/2 tsp. lemon juice
3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375Â°, grease muffin pans or use paper liners.
Mix all ingredients together. Pour into prepared muffin pans, filling about 1/2 full. Bake 22-28 minutes. Let cool before removing from pan.
