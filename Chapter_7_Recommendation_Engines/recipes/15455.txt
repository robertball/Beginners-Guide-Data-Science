Title:
Pineapple Walnut Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1008998.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
4 ounces cream cheese
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
1 cup walnuts
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
Blend together the pineapple, cream cheese and vanilla. Stir in nuts, fold in whipped topping and chill before serving.

Submitter: 
June Barbour
Description: 
This light salad is nice and sweet and can be prepared in 5 minutes.
