Title:
Thimble Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. Butter
1/4 C. White Sugar
1 Egg - separated
1 Tsp. Vanilla
1 C. sifted flour
Coconut or nuts
Jelly or Jam

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cream butter.  Add sugar gradually.  Mix well.  Add well beaten egg yolk, vanilla and flour.  Shape into small balls and dip into unbeaten egg white.  Dip round in coconut or nuts.  Dint centre with thimble.  Please on greased baking sheet and bake for 5 minutes.  Re-dent cookies and further bake 12-15 minutes.  Fill with jam while still hot.
