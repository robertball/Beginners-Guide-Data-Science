Title:
Big Ray's Couscous And Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3428888.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup couscous
4 Tbsp olive oil,divided plus more for brushing
2 pints ripe cherry tomatoes on the vine (such as Sun Gold, Black Cherry, or teardrop;
about 24 oz.)
½ head of red cabbage
¼ small red onion, thinly sliced
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
Crumbled Feta cheese, chopped toasted almonds, fresh flat-leaf parsley leaves, and fresh oregano leaves (for serving)

Directions:
Cook  couscous according to package directions; toss with 2 Tbsp. oil.
Toss tomatoes with 2 Tbsp oil; season with salt and pepper. Grill over high heat, turning occasionally, until tomatoes are charred and blistered, about 3 minutes. Transfer to a plate to cool.
Brush cabbage with oil; grill over high heat, turning occasionally, until lightly charred, 5–8 minutes. Let cool; coarsely chop. Toss with tomatoes, onion, and couscous; season with salt and pepper. Serve topped with Feta, almonds, and parsley and oregano leaves.
