Title:
Quick Zingy Penne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 onion, chopped
3 garlic cloves, chopped
1 pound hot italian sausage
1 green onion, chopped
1 28 oz can diced tomatoes
2 teaspoons basil or italian seasoning
3/4 teaspoon dried fennel
8 oz can tomato paste
8 oz can tomato sauce
1/3 cup chicken broth
penne pasta

Directions:
Over medium heat, add oil and saute onion until it starts to soften.  Add garlic and cook 1 or 2 minutes longer.  Add sausage and brown, breaking up into small pieces.
Add green pepper, fennel, and italian seasonings.  Cook for a few minutes and mix in tomatoes, sauce and paste and broth.  Cook, covered, on low heat for about 20 minutes.
When sauce is done, pour over cooked pasta, mix carefully and serve.  Top with parmesan cheese
