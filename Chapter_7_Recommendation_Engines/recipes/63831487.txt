Title:
Sloppy Joe Sandwich -- Jersey Style! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Lb. Turkey Breast, thinly sliced
1/2 lb. Roast Beef, thinly sliced
1/4 lb. Swiss Cheese
1/2 cup prepared Cole Slaw
1/3 cup Russian Dressing
6 slices of good Deli Rye

Directions:
Line up the bread slices vertically.
On the first slice spread a generous amount of the Russian dressing; place the 1/2 of the turkey breast over the dressing.
One the second slice of bread place 1/2 of the roast beef, 2 slices of the Swiss cheese, 1/2 of the coleslaw and a small amount of additional Russian dressing. Place the third slice of bread on top, then place those two slices on the first.
Slice into halves or thirds, depending on the size of the bread slices.

Submitter: 
Valerie Kasper
Description: 
The BEST Sandwich I've ever eaten!  Found it in a small deli in Summit, NJ several years ago.  This is a meal unto itself.
