Title:
Slow Cooker Meatball Barley Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 egg
3/4 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1 onion, minced
4 carrots, minced
3 celery ribs, thinly sliced
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 cup barley
1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
2 3/8 cups water
3 (10 ounce) cans beef broth
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed tomato soup
1 bay leaf
1 tablespoon parsley
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
ground black pepper, to taste

Directions:
In a large skillet sautee the onions, carrots and celery in the olive oil  unti soft.   In a large bowl mix together the ground beef, bread crumbs, egg, and 3/8 cup of water. Combine in your slow cooker the diced tomatoes, remaining  water, broth, onions, celery, carrots and tomato soup. Season with bay leaf, parsley, garlic, thyme, and pepper.  Make mini meatballs out of the ground beef mixture and put into the slow cooker.  Cook for 10 hrs on low or 6hrs on high.
