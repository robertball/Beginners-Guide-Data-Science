Title:
Overnight Cinnamon Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. pecan pieces
1/2 C. raisins (optional)
1 pkg. (25 ounce) frozen white dinner rolls
2/3 C. packed brown sugar
1 pkg. (3 oz.) vanilla or butterscotch pudding and pie filling mix NOT INSTANT
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 C. butter, cut in small pieces or melted

Directions:
Sprinkle nuts and raisins in bottom of a greased 9 x 13 inch baking pan,angel food cake or bundt pan. Arrange the frozen rolls over nuts and raisins.  Srinkle with brown sugar. In a small bowl, combine pudding mix, cinnamon and nutmeg; sprinkle over rolls.  Top with butter pieces or pour melted butter over. Cover with plastic wrap (greased. Let sit on the counter overnight. Be sure plastic wrap is secured and no air gets in pan.
Next morning, preheat oven 350 and bake 25 min or until golden brown.   Allow to cool 2-3 minutes then turn pan out onto waxed paper or serving dish.
Make maple icing,
powdered sugar, sifted
2 tbsp. butter
milk
maple flavor or your choice of flavorings
Melt butter in small saucepan, add powdered sugar and enough milk or cream to make a pourable icing. About 2-3 tbsp. milk. If too thin add more sugar. Drizzle over top of rolls before serving.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Easy to make using frozen dinner rolls from the freezer section in the grocery store. Use only Butterschotch or Vanilla COOKED pudding mix.
