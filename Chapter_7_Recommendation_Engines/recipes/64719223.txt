Title:
Best Leftover Cranberry Sauce Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5861670.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 cups flour
1 cup oatmeal
1/3 -1/2 cup brown sugar (depending on how sweet your sauce is)
1 tbs baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1.5 cups cranberry sauce
1/2 cup milk
1/3 cup olive oil
1 egg
pecans to go on top

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 C.
2. Prepare muffin tin with paper or silicone liners, this recipe yields 12 muffins.
3. Mix wet ingredients together with an electric mixer, add salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, sugar, baking soda and powder then gradually add the flour and oats.
4.  Pour mixture into muffin liners and sprinkle with chopped pecans.
5. Bake for 21 minutes then let them cool for about 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Marie Wishart
Description: 
These muffins are so soft and fluffy and so delicious with the roasted pecans on top!
