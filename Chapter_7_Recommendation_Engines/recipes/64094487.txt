Title:
Banana Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups flour
1 cup sugar
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp nutmeg
3/4 tsp cinnamon
3/4 cup shortening (or 1/2 cup oil)
1 egg
1/2 cup walnuts
1-3/4 cup rolled oats
1 cup mashed bananas (2 or 3)

Directions:
In large bowl, mix flour with sugar, soda, salt, nutmeg and cinnamon.  Cut in shortening.
Add the egg, nuts, oats and bananas, blending to make a smooth dough.
Drop by tsp onto an ungreased baking sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees F. for 8-10 minutes or until light golden brown.
