Title:
Electric Screwdriver Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979866.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 ounce Jose Cuervo Especial® Tequila
3/4 ounce Smirnoff No. 21™ Vodka
2 ounces orange juice
1 ounce energy drink
1 slice orange

Directions:
Pour Jose Cuervo Especial(R) Tequila, Smirnoff No. 21(TM) Vodka, orange juice, and energy drink into a glass half filled with ice.
Stir well and garnish with orange slice.

Submitter: 
Smirnoff
Description: 
When creating the archetypical tequila drink, get authentic flavor with the gold standard in tequila, Jose Cuervo Especial® tequila.
