Title:
Easy Mu Shu Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 ounce coleslaw mix, divided
10 ounce shredded carrots, divided
6 ounces shiitake mushrooms, sliced
3 cloves garlic, minced
3/4 cup Hoisin sauce, divided
3 tablespoon reduced sodium soy sauce
3/4 pound pork loin roast
12 (6-inch) flour tortilla
1 bunch green onion, chopped (optional)
2 tablespoon sesame oil (optional)

Directions:
1. Place half of the coleslaw mix and carrots in slow cooker. Add mushrooms; toss to combine. Stir in garlic. Add 1/2 cup hoisin sauce and soy sauce stir to combine. Place pork on top of vegetables. Cover; cook on low 4 to 5 hours or until pork is fork tender.
2. Shredded pork with two forks. Add remaining half of coleslaw mix and carrots and remaining 1/4 cup of Hoisin sauce; stir to combine.
3. Warm tortillas according to package directions. Divide pork mixture among tortillas. Top with green onions and sesame oil if desired.
