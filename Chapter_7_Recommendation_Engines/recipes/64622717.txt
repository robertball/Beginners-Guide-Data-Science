Title:
Peanut Butter Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
1 egg
1/3 cup peanut butter
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup flour
1 cup rolled oats (quick)
1/4 teaspoon salt
Topping
1 cup chocolate chips
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1/4 cup peanut butter
2 to 4 tablespoons milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
Cream butter and sugars.
Blend in remaining ingredients.
Spread in 9 x 13 pan and bake for 20-30 minutes.
Sprinkle with chocolate chips and let stand for 5 minutes.
Combine powdered sugar, peanut butter, and milk and mix well.
Spread the melted chocolate chips over the crust evenly and drizzle with the peanut butter/powdered sugar mixture.
Cool and cut into bars. Enjoy!
