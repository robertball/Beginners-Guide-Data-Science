Title:
Helen's  Mollasses Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup oil
1 cup of brown sugar
1/4 cup bare rabbit light mollasses
1 egg
1/2 t. salt
1/2 t. soda
2 cups of plain flour
1 t. cinniman

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
In a large bowl mix sugar, egg, oil, togeather, then add mollasses.  When that is mixed well you then add the salt, soda & flower into the mix.
Put tsp. full of mix on cookie sheet.  Use fork to make a "X" design on the top of cokie. Sprinkle a little sugar on top.  Bake until brown.  About 8-10 minutes.  ENJOY THEM WARM OR COOL!

Submitter: 
Kathy Van Pelt
Description: 
These cookies are the best! My mother was Helen.  She passed away two years ago and left this recipe to me. As you can tell by this recipe my mother was a wounderful cook!
