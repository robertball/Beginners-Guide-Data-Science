Title:
Dark Chocolate Pie - Gluten Free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Container Udi's Gluten Free Snickerdoodle Cookies
1/4-1/2 cup almond flour
1/2 stick butter melted
2 1/4 C Lactose Free Skim Milk
1 box (6 serving size)chocolate pudding & pie mix - not instant
1 1/2 C Gluten Free/Lactose Free Dark Chocolate Chips

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375. Crush Udi's Gluten Free Snickerdoodle Cookies
2. Place crushed cookies in a bowl. Add almond flour. Add melted butter. Mix together and then press into pie plate.
3. Bake at 375degrees for 8-12 minutes. Watch carefully. Remove and then let completely cool.
4.In 2 Qt sauce pan, cook milk and pudding mix over medium heat 5 to 8 min oruntil mixture comes to a full b oil, stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Add chips; stir until chips are melted and smooth. Pourinto medium bowl; cover surfact of pudding with plastic wrap. Refrigerate about 2 hours or until cold.
5. Spoon and spread pudding evenly into crust. Refrigerate at least 4 more hours until set.
