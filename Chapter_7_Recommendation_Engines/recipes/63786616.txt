Title:
Oven Roasted Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2qt halved medium-small tomatoes
3 sm green bell peppers, quartered
8 cloves garlic, peeled & halved
24 leaves fresh basil, minced
2T corn starch
olive oil
paprika
smoked salt
dried parsley

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
Place the tomatoes, peppers, and garlic in a large casserole dish.  Toss with olive oil.
Place in the 400-degree oven.  Bake for 20 minutes.
Remove from oven and pour into a large pot.  Cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
Pour into strainer over a large mixing bowl.  Remove and set aside the peppers.  Press the rest through the strainer until all liquid and pulp is in the bowland all that's left in the strainer are skins and seeds.
Finely dice the peppers and replace into the bowl.  Also add the basil.  Season with paprika, smoked salt, and parsley, to taste.  Pour back into the pot.
Bring the uncovered pot to a boil for 20 minutes.  Pour back into the mixing bowl.
Stirring vigorously, slowly add the cornstarch.
Let set for 10 minutes, then serve.
