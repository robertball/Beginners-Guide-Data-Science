Title:
Mom's Comforting Chicken Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup boneless chicken
1/4 cup butter
1-2 cloves garlic, minced
1 green onion, chopped
10 mushrooms, sliced
2 medium potatoes, diced and boiled
1/2 cup green peas, boiled
2 carrots, diced and boiled
1 tablespoon flour
1 cup chicken stock
1/2 cup cream
1/2 tsp mustard powder
salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoons cilantro, chopped

Directions:
Heat butter in heavy skillet
Add garlic and chicken pieces
Saute until chicken is lightly browned
Add onion and mushrooms
Cook for two minutes, stirring often
Add flour and cook for 1 minute
Slowly mix in chicken stock, cream and seasonings
Cook until thickened
Add potatoes, carrots and peas and heat until warmed through
Remove from heat and garnish with chopped cilantro

Submitter: 
valerie160
Description: 
This is a delicious, rich, creamy,and satisfying Chicken Stew that my mom makes when the weather is nippy out.  Use more or less veggies to your liking if desired. I usually bake a homemade loaf of bread to dig in. This is comfort food at it's best!!
