Title:
Southern Texas Style Chuckwagon Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/760868.jpg
Ingredients:
2 bunches Collard, Mustard, Turnip or Kale greens
4 slices bacon
1 med onion
1/2 cup strong coffee
1/4 cup non-thick balsamic vinegar
Salt
Pepper
Garlic Powder

Directions:
Cook onions and bacon till onions translucent in large skillet on medium heat with lid.
Wash and cut FRESH greens and place in skillet.
May need to wilt down handful by handful - but they will all fit.
Once all wilted and in cookware, add in the coffee, vinegar, garlic powder, salt & pepper.
TASTE the liquid to ensure seasoned enough for you.  Put lid back on and turn to LOW to MED LOW HEAT and simmer for 30 minutes or until tender.
Last few minutes remove lid and let liquid reduce and coat greens.
