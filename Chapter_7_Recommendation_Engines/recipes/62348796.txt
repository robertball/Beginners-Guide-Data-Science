Title:
Shari's Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1.5 liter bottles Livingston Red Rose wine
3/4 cup Brandy
3/4 cup + 2 Tablespoons Triple Sec or Cointreau
1/4 cup apple juice
1/4 cup orange juice
3/4 cup sugar
ginger ale or 7up

Directions:
Mix together.  Add cut-up strawberries, grapes, apples.
Make at least a few hours in advance for best flavor.
Drink straight, or add 7up or ginger ale to taste.
