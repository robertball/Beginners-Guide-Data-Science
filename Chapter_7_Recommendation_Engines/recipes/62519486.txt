Title:
Cardamom Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup milk, scalded
1 tsp cardamom
1/3 cup sugar
3 1/2 cups flour
1 egg, beaten
1 tsp salt
2 tsp yeast

Directions:
Combine butter, sugar and salt in a large bowl.
Pour milk over it.
Add 1 cup flour, yeast and cardamom; mix well.
Add rest of flour and egg, mix.
Add flour until it forms a soft dough.
Knead and put in greased bowl.  Cover and let rise to double, about 1 hour.
Punch down and let rise again, about 1 hour.
Divide dough into 6 equal portions.  Roll into thick strips, 10-12" long.
Braid 3 strips on a greased baking sheet, tucket ends under.  Repeat with other three strips.
Cover and let rise 30 minutes
Brush egg wash on top and bake at 350Â° for 25 minuts.
