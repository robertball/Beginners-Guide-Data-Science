Title:
Three-Bean Vegetable Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup bulgur wheat
1 cup orange juice
3 Tbl. olive oil
1 medium onion, finely copped
4 medium cloves garlic, minced
2 medium red bell peppers, finely chopped
1 28 oz. canned tomotoes, undrained
1 14.5 oz. can Mexican stewed tomatoes, undrained
3 Tbl. chili powder
1 tsp. ground cumin
1 tsp. dried oregano, crushed
1/4 tsp. ground cinnamon
1 14.5 oz. can vegetable broth
1 15.5 oz. can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 15.5 oz. can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 15.5 oz. can pinto beans, drained and rinsed
1 cup frozen corn (or 1 can, rinsed and drained)

Directions:
Combine the bulgur and orange juice.  Set aside until the juice has been absorbed.
In a large pan or Dutc oven heat the olive oil over medium heat.  Add the onion and garlic; saute 5 minutes.  Add the bell peppers and saute 5 minutes.
Break up the tomatoes into smaller pieces.  Add with the liquid to the vegetables; add stewed tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, oregano, cinnamon and broth.  Bring to a boil, reduce the heat and simmer 20 minutes.
Add the bulgur, kidnew, black and pinto beans.  Simmer 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Stir in the corn and simmer 5 minutes.
