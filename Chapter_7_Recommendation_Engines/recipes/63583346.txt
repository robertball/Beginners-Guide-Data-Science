Title:
Sesame Garlic Chicken with Lo Mein Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless chicken breasts cut in diagonal slices
2 T cornstarch
1 T water
1 box frozen lo mein noodles
3 T oil
2 cloves garlic minced
1 pkg. Asian Slaw
Salt and Pepper
1 c. Wegmans sesame garlic sauce

Directions:
Mix chicken, cornstarch and 1 T water
Heat 2T  oil in pan on high and stir fry chicken 4-5 minutes.  Add remaining oil, garlic, slaw and stir fry about 3 min.  Blanch noodles for about 2 minutes drain well.
Return chicken to pan, add noodles, sauce  and heat through.
