Title:
Tacoritas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871680.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cans	cream of chicken soup
2 cans	water
1 tsp	oregano
1 tsp	sage
1 tsp	cumin
3 lb	hamburger
1 large	onion, chopped
3 Tbsp	chili powder
3 tsp	garlic salt
20	flour tortillas - 8-10 inch

Directions:
Combine soup, water, oregano, sage & cumin. Bring to a boil and simmer 1/2 hour, stirring occasionally. Brown hamburger with onion. Drain and add chili powder and garlic salt.. Mix 1 1/2 cups sauce with meat mixture. Put meat mixture on a tortilla and roll up. Repeat with remaining meat mixture and tortillas. Place in 2 9x13 pans that have been coated with cooking spray. Pour rest of sauce over tortillas. Bake at 350 for 15 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
I don't know where the name came from - but this is a really good enchilada like dish. It makes a lot! I freeze half of them.
