Title:
Danielle's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz mild Italian ground sausage
16 oz mild pinto chili beans, undrained
16 oz kidney chili beans, undrained
1 c chopped onion
1 c chopped green bell pepper
14 oz diced tomatoes
1/4 t black pepper
1 T chili powder
2 T oregano
2 cloves garlic, chopped
Optional Garnish: chopped green onion & sour cream

Directions:
1.  In a large pan, brown sausage over medium-high heat.  Add onion and peppers and saute until they are translucent.
2.  Stir in remaining ingredients (except green onion and sour cream).  Bring to a boil.  Cover and reduce heat.  Simmer for 20 minutes.
3.  Garnish with green onion and sour cream if desired.
