Title:
Maple-Balsamic Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3 inch Piece Rosemary
1/2 cup Apple Cider Vinegar
1/4 cup maple syrup
2 coddled egg yolks
1/3 Cup Crumbled Bacon Pieces
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup extra-virgin olive oil

Directions:
Place Rosemary in Vinegar and soak overnight, then remove.
Place vinegar, maple syrup, egg yolks, salt, and pepper into a blender. Pulse to combine, then add the olive oil in a steady stream with the motor running.

Submitter:

Description: 
This dressing is good on any salad, especially salads containing chicken or shrimp!Modified from: Maple-Balsamic Vinaigrette
