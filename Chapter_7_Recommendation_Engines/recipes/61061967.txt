Title:
Broccoli Buffet Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups broccoli florets
1/2 cup chopped red onion
1/4 cup pistachio nuts- shelled
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
3/4 cup Hellman's mayonnaise thinned with lemon juice
2 tablespoons Splenda
1 tablespoon lemon juice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a measuring cup, mix together mayonnaise, splenda, and lemon juice.
In a salad bowl, stir together broccoli, red onion, pistachios, and crumbled feta cheese. Toss with yogurt dressing, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Cover, and refrigerate for 2 hours.
