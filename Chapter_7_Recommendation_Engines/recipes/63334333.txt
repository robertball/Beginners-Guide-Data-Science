Title:
White Bean Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp lemon zest
1/4 tsp dried, crushed rosemary leaves, or scant 1 tsp minced fresh rosemary
1 15 oz can cannellini beans, drained, liquid reserved
2 Tbsp chopped parsley
1/2 tsp brown sugar

Directions:
Cook garlic, rosemary, and lemon zest in oil for 1-2 minutes.  Stir in beans.  Mash, adding in bean liquid by the tablespoon until it's the desired consistency.  Stir in brown sugar and parsley.  Salt and pepper to taste.
