Title:
Sticky Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/798684.jpg
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 tablespoons corn syrup
1/2 cup pecans
20 each frozen bread rolls
1 package regular butterscotch pudding (not instant)

Directions:
Start Later in the Evening
Grease a large bundt pan.
Layer nuts in the bottom of the pan and arrange rolls on top of nuts.
Sprinkle dry pudding on rolls.
Mix butter, brown sugar, cinnamon, vanilla and corn syrup over medium heat. Pour over rolls.
Let set on counter over night.
7.	Bake in morning at 350° for approx. 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
It's impossible to eat just one of these soft, yummy sticky bunsthey have wonderful old fashioned goodness.
