Title:
White Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sour cream
1 can cream mushroom soup
1 cup favorite italian dressing (optional)
1/2 bag frozen premade meatballs

Directions:
place meatballs in pan or crockpot
cover with sauce and stir
cook until meatballs heated to temp recommended on bag
crockpot: low 4-8 hours, high 2-4 hrs
serve over egg noodles, rice, or potatoes
