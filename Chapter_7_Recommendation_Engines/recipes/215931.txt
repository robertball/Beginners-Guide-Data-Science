Title:
Creamed Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987996.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 (250 g) package Philadelphia Brick Cream Cheese, cubed
2 tablespoons milk
1 (14 ounce) can cream-style corn
3 cups frozen corn, thawed
1/2 cup Kraft Double Cheddar Shredded Cheese
1/3 cup sliced green onions

Directions:
Cook cream cheese and milk in medium saucepan on medium heat until cream cheese is melted, stirring frequently.
Add cream-style and thawed corn; stir. Cook 4 min. or until heated through, stirring occasionally.
Spoon into serving dish; sprinkle with Cheddar cheese and green onions.

Submitter: 
Philadelphia Cream Cheese
Description: 
This quick, creamy side dish combines both cream-style corn and corn kernels and is topped with Cheddar cheese and sliced green onions.
