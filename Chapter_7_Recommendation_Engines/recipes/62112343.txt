Title:
Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/284892.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 c. butter
3/4 c. granulated sugar
3/4 c. packed brown sugar
2 tsp vanilla
2 c. flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (180 degrees C
In a medium bowl, combine butter with both sugars. Add vanilla.
Add eggs and mix until combined, scraping the sides of the bowl often.
Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt.
Gradually add to creamed mixture & mix in the chocolate morsels.
Drop 1/4 c. fulls onto non greased cookie sheet(s).
Bake for 10-15 minutes or until just browned around the edges.
