Title:
Homemade Dark Chocolate Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces good quality semi-sweet dark chocolate, roughly chopped
3 tablespoons unsalted butter
1/3 cup milk or soy milk

Directions:
Heat a medium size saucepan on low heat.  Melt the butter, using a silicone spatula to stir frequently.  Add the chocolate, stirring constantly, and then slowly add the milk.  Keep stirring until all ingredients are melted and incorporated.  Pour or spoon over cake or ice cream.

Submitter: 
StunnaShades
Description: 
Delicious, smooth, and if you can believe it, no added sugar. Just 3 ingredients create a perfectly delicious chocolate glaze for cakes, quick breads, or ice cream.
