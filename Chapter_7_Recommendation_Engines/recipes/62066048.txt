Title:
No-pasta Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef, lean, browned and drained
1/2 cup carrots, grated
4 ounces mushroom pieces
1 medium onion, chopped
1/2 teaspoon thyme
1 tablespoon basil
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 pound tomatoes, canned
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1 teaspoon sweetener
1 teaspoon salt
1 head cabbage
24 ounces cottage cheese, lowfat
1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
1 tablespoon parsley
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon oregano
2 cups mozzarella cheese, part skim milk, grated
1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Brown hamburger, with onion and garlic, drain; add carrots and mushrooms.  Add canned tomatoes and break up with a fork.  Add basil, thyme, nutmeg, salt and sweetener. Simmer for 1/2 hour.
2. While meat sauce is simmering, Immerse cabbage  in kettle of boiling water, breaking off cabbage leaves.  Cook leaves for about 3 minutes or until limp.  Remove and drain.
3. Mix cottage cheese, 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, 1 tablespoon parsley, 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, 1 teaspoon oregano in a small bowl and set aside.
4. Spray 13x9x2" pan with non-stick cooking spray.  Reserve 1/4 cup of the meat sauce.  Make a layer of cabbage leaves flattened in the bottom of pan.  On top of that layer 1/2 of the meat sauce.  Next layer 1/2 of the cottage cheese mixture and then 1/2 of the mozzerella cheese.  Layer one more time with cabbage, meat sauce, cottage cheese mixture, and mozzerella cheese. Sprinkle top with 1/4 cup of the reserved meat sauce and sprinkle with the remaining parmesan cheese.
5. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and bake 45 min.  Remove from oven and let sit for 15 minutes before slicing.  Can be made ahead and refrigerated.  Allow an additional 10 to 15  min. if refrigerated.

Submitter: 
Ginn
Description: 
A very filling low carb lasagne. Uses cabbage leaves in place of lasagne noodles. We find it every bit as good if not better than lasagne with pasta.
