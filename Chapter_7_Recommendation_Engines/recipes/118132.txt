Title:
Quick Coconut Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
2/3 cup water
3 eggs
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
1 cup flaked coconut
1 cup chopped pecans
1 teaspoon rum extract
1 teaspoon coconut extract

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, beat the cake mix and butter. Add the remaining ingredients. Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups half full. BAke at 350 degrees F for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool for 5 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks.
