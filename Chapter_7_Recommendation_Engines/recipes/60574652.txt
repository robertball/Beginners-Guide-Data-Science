Title:
Baked Teriyaki Skinless Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 tablespoon cold water
1/2 cup brown Splenda sugar
1/2 cup lite soy sauce
1/4 cup cider vinegar
2 clove garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger or 1/4 tsp. fresh)
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
6 skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
In a small saucepan over low heat, combine the cornstarch, cold water, sugar, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, ginger and ground black pepper. Let simmer, stirring frequently, until sauce thickens and bubbles.
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Place chicken pieces in a foil lined 9x13 inch baking dish. Brush chicken with the sauce. Turn pieces over, and brush again.Cover with foil.
Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Turn pieces over, and bake for another 10 minutes, or until no longer pink and juices run clear. Brush with sauce every 10 minutes during cooking.
