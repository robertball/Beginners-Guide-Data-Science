Title:
Cookies and Cream Peppermint Bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 10 oz. bags white chocolate chips
14 peppermint flavored chocolate sandwich cookies
1/4 tsp. peppermint extract
2 full size candy canes, crushed

Directions:
Line cookie sheet with wax or parchment paper. Melt white chips in microwave on medium heat for 2 min. stirring after one minute. Crush sandwich cookies in large ziploc bag.Mix half of crushed cookies into melted chocolate. Stir in peppermint extract.
Spread chocolate mixture evenly on cookie sheet. Sprinkle remaining cookies and crushed candy canes on top of melted chocolate.
Let stand until bark has hardened, approx. 2 hours. Break into random pieces with hands. Makes 3 dozen pieces.

Submitter: 
AMPBRUIN
Description: 
I came up with this recipe while shopping at Trader Joe's. They didn't have the ingredients for traditional peppermint bark so I improvised. My husband loved this new recipe even more than my old one! It's a great one for getting the kids involved, too!
