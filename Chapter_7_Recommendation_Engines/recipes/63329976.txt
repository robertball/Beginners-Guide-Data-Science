Title:
Cilantro Lime Pico de Gallo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large tomatoes chopped
1/2 chopped white onion
2 cloves of garlic chopped
1/8 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1 lime, juiced
1/8 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
*Optional-chopped jalapeno's to taste

Directions:
Chop tomato's, onions and garlic and mix together.  Add the juice from one lime, salt and pepper and mix well.  If your family and friends like a bit a bit of a kick add some chopped jalapeno's to taste.  Allow the dish to marinated in the fridge for a few hours before serving. If you are serving this as a salsa it will only serve 2-4.  If you are serving this as a compliment to a dish, it will serve 6-8

Submitter: 
ltlmsmfft
Description: 
This is a simple recipe that can serve as a compliment to a dish or as a type of salsa for an appetizer.  We like to eat it with our taco's.
