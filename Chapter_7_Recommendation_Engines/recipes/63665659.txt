Title:
Eddie's Cucumber Slices Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 to 4 large cucumbers, sliced
2 medium onions, thinly sliced
3 tbsp minced, fresh dill OR 1 tbsp dried dill weed
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup vinegar
1/2 cup water
1 tsp salt

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine the sugar, vinegar, water and salt. Bring to a boil, allow to cool slightly. In a large bowl, combine the cucumber slices, sliced onion and dill. Pour liquid mixture over cucumbers. Cover and refrigerate at least 3 hours, or overnight.

Submitter: 
Julie Rehagen
Description: 
These are my son's favorite sliced cucumbers. So quick and easy!
