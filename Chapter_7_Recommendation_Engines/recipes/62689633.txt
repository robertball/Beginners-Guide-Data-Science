Title:
Karebear's Cranberry-Pear Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 or 5 pears
2 (10 ounce) bags fresh cranberries
1 1/2 cups sugar
1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
Grated rind of 1 orange
1/2 cup fresh orange juice
1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Rinse and sort cranberries. Peel and core pears and cut into 1-inch pieces. Combine all ingredients in a heavy saucepan. Cover and bring to a boil. Simmer for 15 minutes, stirring twice. Ladle into sterilized jars or allow to cool and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
GoodGoneGirl
Description: 
So simple to make, you will never go back to cranberry sauce from a can! This recipe makes quite a lot. I freeze or can what I don't use for Thanksgiving and give it as gifts or use it during the holiday season.
