Title:
TGI Friday's Jack Daniels Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 t minced garlic
3 T minced onion
1/4 t cayenne
2/3 cup water
1 cup pineapple juice
1/4 cup Teriyaki sauce
1 T low sodium soy sauce
1 1/3 cup brown sugar
3 T apple cider vinegar
1 T Jack Daniels Whiskey
1 T crushed pineapple

Directions:
Place onion, garlic, and cayenne pepper in mortar & pestle and mash into a thick paste.
Place above and all other ingredients in saucepan, bring to boil, lower heat, and let reduce 50 minutes to an hour or until thick and sticky.
When grilling, use towards end as sauce will burn due to high sugar content...
