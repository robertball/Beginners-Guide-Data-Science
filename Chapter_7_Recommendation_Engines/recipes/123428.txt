Title:
Tender Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/210868.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3 pound) boneless pork roast
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
3/4 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup sugar
2 teaspoons ground mustard

Directions:
Cut roast in half; place in a 5-qt. slow cooker. Combine remaining ingredients; pour over roast. Cover and cook on low for 8-9 hours or until a meat thermometer reads 145 degrees (63 degrees C). Remove roast to a serving platter and keep warm. If desired, skim fat from pan juices and thicken for gravy.

Submitter: 
LaVerne  Peterson
Description: 
LuVerne Peterson of Minneapolis, Minnesota shares this melt-in-your mouth fall-apart-tender pork roast. 'It's wonderful to serve to company because it never fails to please,' she promises.
