Title:
Creamy & Hearty Bolognese Meat Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2lbs Ground MEATLOAF MIX (veal, pork, beef)
1/2 cup finely chopped CARROTS
1/2 cup finely chopped CELERY
1/2 cup finely chopped RED ONION
1/2 tsp SALT
1/2 tsp PEPPER
1/2 pt  HEAVY CREAM
1 cup   Salted BUTTER
42 oz   CRUSHED TOMATOES (1 1/2 cans)
4 cups  BURGUNDY RED WINE (more or less to your liking)

Directions:
•CHOP Carrots, Celery & Red Onion, INTO TINY PIECES & COOK VEGETABLES IN 3 TBSP BUTTER UNTIL TRANSPARENT (about 13 mins)
•BROWN CHOP MEAT, SEASON IT WITH SALT AND PEPPER, DRAIN FAT
•ADD MEAT AND VEGGIES TOGETHER and get really hot (high flame for 5 mins)
•ADD WINE AND COOK UNCOVERED for APPROX. 15 MINUTES until wine is reduced to approximately one cup.
•ADD CRUSHED TOMATOES AND SIMMER FOR 2 1/2 HOURS  (low heat)
•ADD ENTIRE STICK OF BUTTER and STIR
*ADD HEAVY CREAM, A LITTLE AT A TIME UNTIL YOU GET DESIRED RESULTS.
HEAT FOR AN ADDITIONAL 5-10 MINUTES.

Submitter: 
Gina Ferraro Brown
Description: 
A creamy, hearty Italian meat sauce that will make your home smell like an upscale Italian restaurant. 

Bursting with flavor, thick and delicious.

Serve with Large Rigatoni and lots of crusty Italian Bread!
