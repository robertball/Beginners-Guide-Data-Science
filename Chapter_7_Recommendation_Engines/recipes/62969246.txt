Title:
Campbell's Cheesy Chicken & Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (10 3/4 oz.) Campbell's Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup
1 1/3 cups water
3/4 cup uncooked regular long-grain white rice
1/2 tsp. onion powder
1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
2 cups frozen mixed vegetables
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts halves
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Stir soup, water, rice, onion powder, black pepper and vegetables in 2-qt. shallow baking dish.
Top with chicken. Season chicken as desired. Cover.
Bake at 375 for 50 min. or until chicken and rice are done. Top with cheese. Let casserole stand for 10 min. Stir rice before serving.
