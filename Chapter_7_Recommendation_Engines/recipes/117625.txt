Title:
Cinnamon Apple Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/30083.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups whole wheat flour
4 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 eggs, lightly beaten
2 cups fat-free milk
2 tablespoons honey
1 tablespoon canola oil
1 medium apple - peeled, cored, and chopped

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, cinnamon and salt. Combine the eggs, milk, honey and oil; stir into dry ingredients just until moistened. Add apple.
Pour batter by 1/3 cupfuls onto a hot nonstick skillet coated with nonstick cooking spray. Turn when bubbles form on top; cook until second side is golden brown.

Submitter: 
Kim McConnell
Description: 
'My family loves the apple and cinnamon flavor in these light yet hearty pancakes,' Kim McConnell shares from Tulsa, Oklahoma. 'And best of all, they're sweet enough to eat without syrup.
