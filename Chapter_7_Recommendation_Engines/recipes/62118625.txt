Title:
The BEST Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of lettuce
1 lb ground beef
1 package taco seasoning
1 bag doritoz (smashed)
1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 bottle Catalina dressing (or to taste)

Directions:
Fry hamburger until browned, drain grease.
Mix in Taco seasoning and prepared as directed on seasoning packet.
Chop lettuce and put into a large bowl
Smash doritoz until fine and mix them with the lettuce. (Most of the time all you need is 3/4 of the bag)
Stir in the cheese and hamburger meat as well.
Once mixed together, stir in the Catalina dressing. Depending on your taste, you may need more or less then written above.

Submitter: 
Becky Mader Wisniewski
Description: 
This family favorite made with Catalina dressing, doritoz, cheese, and seasoned hamburger is sure to please everyone at your next get together!!!!
