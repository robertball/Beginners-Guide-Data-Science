Title:
Trishie's Mushroom Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30g dried mushrooms
450 ml pint of boiling water
30g butter
1 medium onion peeled and cut into thin wedges
125g risotto rice
1 (295g) can of condensed mushroom soup
salt
freshly ground black pepper
parmesan cheese grated

Directions:
Place the mushrooms in a bowl and pour 3/4 pint of boiling water over them
Leave to saok for 30 minutes
Melt the butter in a saucepan and add the onion, then cook over a medium heat for about 5-7 minutes until it starts to soften
Put the rice into the pan and cook the onion and rice together for about 1 minute stirring constantly
Pour in the soup and the soaked mushrroms along with any remaining liquid in the bowl
Reduce the heat and simmer for about 15 minutes stirring occasionally until thickened and the rice is tender
(If its too dry add a little boiling water, if its too runny boil rapidly to evaporate excess liquid)
Season to taste with the salt & Pepper
Spoon onto warm plates and sprinkle with the parmesan cheese
