Title:
Brownie Fruit Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box brownie mix
1 (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese, softened
1 tub (8 oz.) Cool Whip, softened
Your choice fresh fruits: Quartered strawberries, mandarin oranges, sliced bananas, pineapple chunks, blueberries, grapes, etc.

Directions:
1. Bake brownie mix according to its instructions in a lightly greased 9x13 pan. DO NOT OVERBAKE! Cool on wire rack.
2. Mix cream cheese and cool whip until smooth. Spread over cooled brownies.
3. Randomly place fruit on top of brownies in a fun, colorful pattern.
4. If not serving immediately, chill until serving.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is one of the best desserts I've served, and it's so easy to make! Use your favorite seasonal fruits to customize this recipe according to your preferences!
