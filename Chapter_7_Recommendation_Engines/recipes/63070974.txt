Title:
Schnitzel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887213.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds pork steak
1 cup flour
4 eggs
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
salt and pepper to taste
4 cups bread crumbs
1/8 cup oil for frying

Directions:
Cut the pork into steaks, about as thick as your finger.
Dredge in flour.
In a shallow dish, beat the eggs with 1 tablespoon oil, salt and pepper. Coat the pork steak with egg mixture, then with bread crumbs.
Heat 1/4 cup oil in a heavy skillet over medium heat. Fry pork steak until golden brown, about 5 minutes on each side.
