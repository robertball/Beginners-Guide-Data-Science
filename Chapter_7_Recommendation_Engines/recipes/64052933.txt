Title:
Italian chicken & rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2270901.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz skinless boneless chicken breast
1/2 cup onion
1 can (14.5 oz) chicken broth
1 can (14.5 oz)!french style green beans, drained
1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes (I use basil, oregano & garlic), undrained
1 1/2 cup instant rice
Grated parmigiana (optional)

Directions:
Cook chicken and onions with non stick cooking spray in saut√© pan.
2. Once chicken is no longer pink and cooked stir in chicken broth and diced tomatoes. Bring to a boil.
3.Stir in rice and beans.
4.Reduce heat and simmer covered for 5 minutes.
5.Remove from heat. Let stand 3 minutes, covered.
6.Serve with grated parmigiana and bread/butter.
