Title:
Apple Cake (revised) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
3/4 cup apple sauce or yogurt
1/2 cup canola oil or butter
3/4 cup white sugar (turbino sugar)
1/4 cup broun sugar
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
3 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 cups diced apple with peel
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1/2 cup chopped nuts
TOPPING
1/2 cup melted butter
1/2 cup ground flax seed
1/4 cup broun sugar
1/4 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease and flour one 9x13 inch cake pan. Or use a 10-12 cup bunt pan 350 degrees for 55-65 minutes (you have to pack the dough into the bunt pan with wet hands) Check for doneness 2&#34; from edge.
Beat vegetable oil and eggs until foamy. Add the sugar, flour, ground cinnamon, baking, soda, salt, and vanilla and mix well. Stir in the diced apples. Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 to 40 minutes.  Cool cake in pan for 10 minutes. Cake needs no frosting.

Submitter: 
Mywemans
Description: 
This is a tasty cake that stores well and is quite easy to make. Also good with a light drizzle of Buttermilk SauceModified from: Apple Cake I
