Title:
Vegan Protein Chocolate Overnight Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5606575.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana
1 1/4 cups rolled oats
2 tablespoons pea protein powder
1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
1 cup soy milk
1/3 cup water
1 1/2 tablespoons flax seeds
1/2 cup fresh mixed berries

Directions:
Place banana in a bowl and mash with a fork until pureed. Add oats, protein powder, cocoa powder, soy milk, water, flax seeds, and berries; stir well and transfer to a mason jar.
Cover and refrigerate overnight.
