Title:
Cinnamon Streusel Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1/2 c milk
1 t vanilla
1/4 c vegetable oil
1 1/2 c all purpose flour
1/2 c sugar
2 t baking powder
1/2 t salt
Streusel:
2 T flour
1/4 c sugar
2 t cinnamon
2 T cold butter

Directions:
Heat oven to 400
Grease bottom of 8x8 or 9x9 pan
In bottom of mixing bowl beat egg lightly with a fork
Stir in milk, oil and vanilla
Add flour, sugar, baking powder and salt all at once
Mix just until mixture is combined, it will be lumpy, do not overmix
Spread evenly into bottom of pan
For streusel:
Mix flour, sugar, cinnamon then cut in butter until mixture is crumbly
Sprinkle evenly over batter
Bake 20-25 minutes be careful not to over bake!

Submitter: 
Audrey Noland
Description: 
Sweet and cinnamony with a hint of vanilla. Quick and easy to make!
