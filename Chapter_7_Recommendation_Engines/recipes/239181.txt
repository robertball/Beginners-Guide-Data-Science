Title:
Creamy Pumpkin Pie Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4976836.jpg
Ingredients:
1 frozen banana
3/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
1/2 cup almond milk
1/2 cup ice, or as desired
1/4 cup pumpkin puree
1/2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
Blend banana, yogurt, almond milk, ice, pumpkin puree, and pumpkin pie spice together in a blender until smooth.
