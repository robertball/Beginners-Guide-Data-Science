Title:
Blueberry Grapefruit Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3245505.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 large pink grapefruit, peeled and membranes removed
1 cup blueberries
2 tablespoons minced red onion
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 jalapeno pepper, chopped
1 teaspoon honey

Directions:
Dice grapefruit into blueberry-size pieces and transfer to a large bowl. Add blueberries, onion, cilantro, lime juice, jalapeno pepper, and honey; mix well.

Submitter: 
Lori North
Description: 
This salsa goes great with barbecued salmon.
