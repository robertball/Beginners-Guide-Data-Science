Title:
Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cup pureed pumpkin
1 1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup butter
3 eggs
3-4 cups flour
2 tablespoons baking powder
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
2 cups of chopped walnuts or chocolate chips (or half of each)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Mix pumpkin, brown sugar, butter, eggs. Mix dry ingredients together and slowly combine with the pumpkin mix. Slowly stir in the nuts and chocolate chips and bake.
muffins: 20-25 min.
miniature loaf pans: 25-30 min.
Large loaf pan: 50-60 min.

Submitter: 
Danielle Kroll
Description: 
This is really good pumpkin bread. It can be made with either fresh or canned pumpkin puree. It is best from fresh pumpkins!
