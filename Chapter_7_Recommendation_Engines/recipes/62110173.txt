Title:
Jello Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup of boiling water
1 box of jello gelatin (WE LIKE JELLO BERRY FUSHION AND TROPICAL FUSHION, THAT WAY THERES 5 FLAVORS!)
1/2 cup cold water
1 cup of ice
1 (8oz) container of thawed cool whip
1 (6oz) graham pie crust

Directions:
Stir boiling water into gelatin in large bowl at least two minutes until completely dissolved.
Mix cold water and ice. Add to gelatin, stirring until ice is melted.
Stir in whip topping with wire whisk untill smooth. Refridgerate 20 minutes or to mixture is thick, Spoon into crust.
Refrigerate  4 hours or overnight (overnight works better!)
DON'T USE FREEZER
