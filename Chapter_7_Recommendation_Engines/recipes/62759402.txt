Title:
Fried Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 ounces whole fresh mushrooms
1 cup Flour
½ cup Cornstarch
¾ teaspoon Baking Powder
¼ teaspoon Garlic Salt
1 cup Water
2 cups Panko Breadcrumbs

Directions:
1: In a large bowl mix flour, baking powder and salt. Add water and mix until a smooth batter has formed. Dip mushrooms into the batter and coat evenly. Allow excess batter to drip off.
2: Roll the mushrooms in breadcrumbs until coated thoroughly. Repeat until all mushrooms are coated.
3. Heat deep fryer to 365 degrees. Deep fry mushrooms in batches until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.

Submitter: 
drcook
Description: 
I grew up eating these and they always bring up great memories of growing up in Zimbabwe.  Here is my mom's recipe, it makes a great snack!  The recipe is very versatile, you can use plain breadcrumbs instead of panko or plain salt instead of the garlic salt.
