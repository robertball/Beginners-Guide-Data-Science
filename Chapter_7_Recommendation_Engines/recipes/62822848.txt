Title:
Crab Cakes, the best! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 3 (16oz.) cans lump crab meat, drain
1 cup dry bread crumbs, divided
1 egg, beaten
1/4 cup green onions, chopped fine
1/4 cup red bell pepper chopped
2 - 3 tablespoons mayonnaise  (add a little at a time as to not use too much mayonnaise)
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
Place crab meat in in a bowl, add 1/3 of the bread crumbs, egg, onion, red pepper, mayonnaise, lemon juice, garlic powder and cayenne.  Mix well.
Divide into 8 parts.
Make 2-inch balls by rolling in your hand.
Roll balls in the remaining bread crumbs.
Melt butter in skillet.  Place balls in skillet, LIGHTLY flatten balls into patties.  Do not smash the patties, keep patties thick.  Cook on each side for 3 to 4 minutes until done.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
I've finally found a great and simple recipe for canned crab cakes.  See if you agree.  The trick is to keep them thick; do-not press down on them!
