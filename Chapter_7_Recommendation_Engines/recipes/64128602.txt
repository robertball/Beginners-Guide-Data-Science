Title:
Cheese Steak Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1327063.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pizza crust, prepared
1 large bell pepper, sliced
1/2 large onion, sliced
1 C. white mushrooms, sliced
1 clove garlic, minced
2 T. sunflower oil
1 1/2 C. roast beef, shredded
1 1/2 C. beef gravy
1 1/2 C. processed cheese food, shredded
1 1/2 C. provolone cheese, shredded
1 C. cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Heat baking stone in the oven to 435 degrees.
While stone is heating, sautee all vegetables in sunflower oil on medium-low heat until slightly tender.
Once stone has heated, remove from oven and brush with olive oil.  Arrange pizza crust on baking stone.
Spead processed cheese food evenly on pizza crust, then spread gravy on top the processed cheese food. Distribute on top evenly the veggies and roast beef. Top with cheeses.
Bake at 435 degrees for about 15 minutes or until cheese has melted and crust has started to brown.

Description: 
The classic philly cheese steak turned into a pizza. Home made beef gravy will make all the difference.
