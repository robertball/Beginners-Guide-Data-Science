Title:
Easiest Crock Pot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
2 12 oz jars of chicken gravy
salt & pepper is optional

Directions:
Salt and Pepper chicken breasts if desired.
Place chicken breats and gravy into crock pot.
Stir so chicken is equally covered.
Cook on high 4-6 hours or low (recommended)for 8 hours.
Serve with mashed potatoes or another comfort food side dish.

Submitter: 
Jen Camilleri
Description: 
Never has a meal been so easy to make! 2 ingredients is all that is needed for the juiciest chicken the whole family will love.
