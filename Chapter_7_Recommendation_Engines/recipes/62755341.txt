Title:
perfect white cake! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
2 cups sugar
3 eggs plus 2 egg whites
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 1/2 cups cake flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup milk
1 cup sour cream

Directions:
preheat oven to 350. grease and flour 2 9-inch cake pans. in a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. beat in eggs, one at a time. add vanilla; mix well. combine dry ingredients; add to creamed mixture alternately with milk and sour cream (batter will be thick). mix for about 1 minute with stand mixer. DO NOT OVER-MIX or you will get large air pockets in your cake.
pour into pans and bake for 25-30 minutes.
allow to cool in pans for 15 minutes, loosen edges with butter knife and turn out onto wire rack to cool completely.
