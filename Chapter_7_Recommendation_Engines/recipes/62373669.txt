Title:
Coconut Pistachio Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/412975.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup Shortening
3 tablespoons Butter (softened)
1/2 cup Sugar
1/2 cup Brown Sugar
1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
1 teaspoon Almond Extract
1 Egg
1/2 teaspoon Baking Soda
1/4 teaspoon Salt
2 cups Flour
1/2 - 1 cup Chopped Pistachios (personal preference)
1/2 - 1 cup Coconut Flakes (personal preference)

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, beat together shortening, butter, sugar, brown sugar, vanilla extract, almond extract and egg.
In a seperate bowl sift together dry ingredients; flour, salt and baking soda.
Slowly beat sifted dry ingredients into the large bowl of previously mixed ingredients until thoroughly combined.
Add in chopped pistachios and coconut and mix into the dough with a large spoon and/or your hands until evenly dispersed.
Spoon heaping spoonfuls onto a cookie sheet.
Bake at 325 degrees for 8-10 minutes, until the bottoms are light golden brown.

Submitter: 
Ashley Bohacz
Description: 
I altered my mom's famous chocolate chip cookie recipe to accomodate two of my favorite flavors, coconut and pistachio. These crunchy on the outside, chewy on the inside cookies have been a huge hit ever since and one of my favorite to make. Hope you enjoy!
