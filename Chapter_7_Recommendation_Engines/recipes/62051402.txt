Title:
Easy Szechwan Chicken w/ Cashews Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 to 2-1/2 lbs. boneless chicken, cut into bite sized pieces
1 large onion, quartered or sliced OR 1 bunch green onions, sliced
1 large green pepper, sliced
½ cup whole cashews
½ cup soy sauce
½ cup cooking sherry or white cooking wine
2 Tb. Cornstarch
¾ tsp. sugar
¾ tsp. ground ginger
¾ tsp. crushed red pepper
½ tsp. garlic powder
Cooking Oil

Directions:
Mix soy sauce, sherry and spices together in a medium bowl.  Add chicken pieces and marinate for 1-2 hours, stirring occasionally (marinate longer for spicier chicken).
In large skillet or wok, heat cooking oil over medium heat and sauté onions and green peppers until al dente.  Remove from skillet and set aside.
Add chicken to skillet.  Discard marinade.  Cook chicken approx. 10-15 minutes or until cooked through.  Add cashews, cook for 1-2 minutes longer.  Stir in peppers and onions.  Serve over white rice.

Submitter: 
cheri
Description: 
This is a very easy and very tasty Chinese recipe that is also kid-friendly!  It goes great with white rice and egg rolls!
