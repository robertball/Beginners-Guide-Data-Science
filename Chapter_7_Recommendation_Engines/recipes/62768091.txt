Title:
Thai Fried Bananas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/641652.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
4 fresh, firm bananas, peeled and cut into 1 to 2-inch pieces
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon black sesame seeds
1 lime, juiced

Directions:
Heat a wok over high heat and add 2 tablespoons of butter. Once melted, toss in bananas and add the 1/4 cup of sugar and 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon.
Cook down sugar and add the sesame seeds and the lime juice. Stir together and serve over vanilla ice cream.
