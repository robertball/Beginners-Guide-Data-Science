Title:
Mushroom & Sausage Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground pork sausage
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
1 teaspoon salt (divided)
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
4 tablespoons butter, melted
8 oz sliced white mushrooms
4 eggs, beaten
1 cup milk
3 russet potatoes, shredded
1 yellow onion, shredded
8 ounces mild Cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Crumble sausage into skillet and cook over medium heat until evenly brown; drain on paper towel.
Toss shredded potatoes and onions with ½ ts salt, pepper & garlic.
In a medium bowl, mix together mustard, ½ ts salt, eggs and milk.  Add sausage, mushrooms and half of the cheese, mix to combine.
Grease a deep baking dish (9x13) layer with potato mix and then drizzle with butter.  Next layer sausage, mushrooms, cheese and egg mixture into a greased 9x13 inch baking dish.  Sprinkle with remaining cheese.  Cover, and chill in the refrigerator for 8 hours, or overnight (or bake right away)
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Cover, and bake 45 to 60 minutes. Uncover, and reduce temperature to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Bake for an additional 30 minutes, or until set.

Submitter: 
TheCattlemansWife
Description: 
Threw this together with remaining ingredients during a camping trip - easy and everyone loved it.  I add green chili and different peppers depending upon the crowd.  Is better if left overnight, but can be baking right away.
