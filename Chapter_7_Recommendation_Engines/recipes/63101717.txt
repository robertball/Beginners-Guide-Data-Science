Title:
Chicken Spinach Feta Pockets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices bacon (cut into bite sized pcs)
2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (cut into 1" cubes)
1 small onion, diced
2 tsp minced garlic
1 tsp dried parsley
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp fresh lemon juice
10 oz pkg fresh spinach
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
1 pkg puff pastry sheets

Directions:
preheat oven to 400 degrees.  place chicken in a bowl with lemon, pepper, parsley and garlic.  set aside.
in medium saute pan cook bacon. once crisp drain on paper towels and reserve bacon grease.
saute chicken in bacon grease until no longer pink on outside, add onion and cook until onion is translucent.
add spinach to pan a handful at a time until it begins to wilt.  remove from heat.
cut each pastry sheet in half. (you will have 4 pcs).  on floured surface, spread out pastry & layer feta, chicken and bacon.  fold pastry over stuffing and crimp edges with fingers.
place pockets with seam side down on a parchment lined baking sheet.  bake 30 min, or until golden brown.
