Title:
Layered Greek Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/438614.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8 oz. Pkg. Cream Cheese, Softened
1 TBSP. Lemon Juice
1 tsp. dried Italian Seasoning
3 Cloves of garlic, minced (or 1 tsp powdered)
1 1/2 cups prepared hummus
1 Cup chopped Cucumber
1 Cup chopped Tomato
1/2 Cup chopped pitted Kalamata Olives
1/2 Cup crumbled feta Cheese
1/3 Cup Sliced Green onions

Directions:
Beat cream cheese, lemon juice, Italian seasoning and garlic with mixer until smooth.
Spread cream cheese into deep 9" pie plate.
Evenly spread hummus on cream cheese layer.
Top with cucumber, tomato, olives, feta cheese, and green onions.
Cover and refrigerate 2 to 24 hours.  Serve with pita chips, veggies, or any crackers you like!

Submitter: 
SavedByGrace
Description: 
My aunt got this recipe out of a Better Homes & Gardens Magazine years ago and every time it's made someone asks for the recipe. Cream cheese with spices topped with hummus which is then topped with veggies and feta cheese makes a dip that people just can't stop eating!
