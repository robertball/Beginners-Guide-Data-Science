Title:
Snickerdoodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups Flour
2 tsp Baking Powder
1 1/3 cups sugar
1 cup butter
1/4 tsp salt
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
3 tbs sugar
2 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. Cream the butter, eggs, vanilla & sugar - mix well
3. Add baking powder, salt & flour - mix well
4. In small bowl or dish, mix sugar & cinnamon
5. Make one inch balls of dough & roll in cinnamon/sugar mixture
6. Place on non-stick cookie sheet with a little press to flatten about one inch apart
7. Bake 10-12 minutes until lightly browned
