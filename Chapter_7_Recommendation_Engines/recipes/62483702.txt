Title:
Jim's Easy Cheesy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
salt and pepper to taste
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (11 ounce) can condensed cream of Cheddar cheese soup

Directions:
Rinse chicken, and pat dry.  Sprinkle with salt, pepper and garlic powder.  Place in slow cooker.
In a medium bowl, mix together cream of chicken soup, cream of mushroom soup and cream of Cheddar cheese soup.
Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
Another slow cooker hit combines three varieties of canned soups for a creamy sauce that could not be easier to make.Modified from: Easy Cheesy Chicken I
