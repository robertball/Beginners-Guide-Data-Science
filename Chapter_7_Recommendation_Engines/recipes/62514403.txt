Title:
Fried Green Tomato Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/508858.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box Jiffy Corn Muffin mix, or your favorite cornbread recipe.
4-5 golfball size green tomatoes (or 2 nice medium) choppped.
2-3 tsp. butter melted, 1/2 tsp. salt (more or less to taste)
1 TBLsp. sugar.

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees
Add chopped green tomatoes to melted butter in skillet & sautee' until soft, set aside. Make Jiffy Corn Muffin mix according to package directions, stir in sautee'd green tomatoes. Pour mixture into greased 8x8 pan & bake until lightly browned & toothpick inserted in center comes out clean, about 15 min. Serve warm with butter or enjoy it plain ;)
