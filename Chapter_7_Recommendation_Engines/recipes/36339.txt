Title:
Old Bay® Crab Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986444.jpg
Ingredients:
2 slices dried bread, crusts removed
2 tablespoons milk
1 tablespoon mayonnaise
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon McCormick® Parsley Flakes
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 teaspoon Old Bay® Seasoning
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 egg, beaten
1 pound fresh lump crabmeat

Directions:
Break bread into small pieces and moisten with milk. Add remaining ingredients and combine well.
Shape mixture into patties. Broil or fry until golden-brown on both sides.

Submitter: 
Old Bay®
Description: 
If you like Maryland crab cakes, you will love this classic Old Bay® recipe featuring fresh lump crab meat that is sensationally seasoned with Old Bay® seasoning.
