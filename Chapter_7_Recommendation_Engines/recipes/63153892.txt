Title:
Classic Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 medium green bell peppers slice in half
3 egg, lightly beaten
1/2 Gal. marinara sauce, divided
2 cups uncooked instant rice
1 finely chopped onion
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
Dash pepper
3 pound uncooked lean ground beef

Directions:
Cut peppers in half lenthwise and remove seeds. Place in a large peferated pan and steam for 10 minutes.Let drain In a bowl, combine the egg, 2 cups of the marinara sauce, rice, onion, salt, Worcestershire sauce and pepper. Crumble beef over the mixture and mix well. Stuff into peppers. Place in an ungreased long pan. Pour the remaining sauce over peppers. Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 50-55 minutes or until a meat thermometer reads 160 degrees F.
