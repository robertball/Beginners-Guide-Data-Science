Title:
Kirsten's Hamburger Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. hamburger
2 tbsp. minced onion
1/2 tsp. minced garlic
1/4 tsp. celery salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 1/2 cups hot water
5 tsp. or cubes of beef bullion
1 4 oz. can mushrooms
3 tbls. cornstarch (mixed with 1/2 c water)

Directions:
1. cook in large saucepan (at least 12") hamburger, minced onion, minced garlic, celery salt, and pepper.  Brown and drain.
2. add 5 beef bullion cubes and mushrooms cook over med heat until boiling, simmer for 10 mins.
3. Then add cornstarch (with the 1/2 cup water mixed in first!) simmer until thickend up, should only take a few minutes, add more cornstarch if necessary.

Submitter: 
KIRSTENLIV
Description: 
A hamburger gravy mixture that makes a main dish when added over mashed potatoes and corn. A delicious favorite with kids! My mom always made it for us growing up, it was something that was made at school for lunch that we all just loved so she made her own recipe at home.
