Title:
Healthier Cheeseburger Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked whole wheat macaroni
1 1/2 cup Ragu Double Cheese sauce
6oz 15% fat ground sirloin
Salt to taste
Ground black pepper to taste
Garlic powder to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta to desired tenderness,  per box directions.  Form burgers into patties and season as desired. Cook burgers to desired doneness.
Drain pasta, put back in pot. Pour in cheese sauce and mix together. Chop up burger patties and mix in to pasta.
YOU ARE READ| TO EAT.

Description: 
This was a spu of the moment idea, since I've never had Hamburger Helper and I want a recipe that is simple,  somewhat quick, and possibly healthier than other recipes out there.
