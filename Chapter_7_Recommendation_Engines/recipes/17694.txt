Title:
Caesar Salad Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977279.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package torn romaine lettuce
2 cups SARGENTO® ChefStyle Shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese, divided
1 ripe avocado, diced (optional)
1 (4 ounce) can ORTEGA® Diced Green Chiles
1/2 cup canned black beans, drained and rinsed
1/4 cup frozen whole-kernel corn, thawed
1/3 cup Caesar salad dressing
1/4 cup ORTEGA® Thick & Smooth Taco Sauce
1 package (12) ORTEGA® Taco Shells, warmed
Diced tomatoes

Directions:
COMBINE lettuce, 1 cup cheese, avocado, chiles, beans and corn in large bowl. Combine dressing and taco sauce in small bowl; mix well. Pour over lettuce mixture; toss well to coat.
FILL each taco shell with salad mixture; top with remaining cheese and tomatoes.
