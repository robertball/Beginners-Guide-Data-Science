Title:
Easy Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lbs frozen hash browns with peppers and onions (approx 3 c.)
3/4 c. shredded pepper-jack cheese
1 c. (6-8 ounces) cooked ham, diced
5 eggs, beaten
1 (12 ounce) can evaporated milk
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 2 quart baking dish.
Spread hash browns evenly in bottom of baking dish. Sprinkle with pepper jack cheese and ham.
In a medium bowl, mix eggs, evaporated milk, salt and pepper. Pour over potato mixture. (you may cover and refrigerate over night at this point)
Bake for 55 minutes in preheated oven or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.

This can be made ahead of time and refrigerated overnight.
