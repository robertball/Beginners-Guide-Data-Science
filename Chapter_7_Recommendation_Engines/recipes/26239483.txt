Title:
Crock Pot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One chocolate cake mix (Carla uses Betty Crocker Chocolate fudge), dry
4 eggs
3/4 c veg oil
1 c water
6 oz chocolate chips (Carla uses 12 oz)
1 c pecans
One 160oz carton sour cream
One pkg dry chocolate pudding mix

Directions:
Pour dry cake mix into crock pot.  Add eggs, veg oil, water, chocolate chips, pecans, sour cream and dry chocolate pudding mix.  Mix throroughly in crock pot.  Set on "low" and cook for 6 hours.  Serve warm with ice cream or cool whip.
