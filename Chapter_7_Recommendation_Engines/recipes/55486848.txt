Title:
Basil, Tomato and Mozzarella Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 fairly large slices thick Italian (or sourdough) bread
6-8 fresh basil leaves, chopped
1-2 tomatoes, sliced
2-3 ounces mozzarella cheese
Optional additions:
Roasted garlic
Chopped roasted red and/or green pepper slices
Pesto
Small amount of red pepper flakes
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Layer the tomato, cheese and basil  on one side of each sandwich.  Lightly brush each outer side of sandwich with olive oil and close.  Put into panini grill until cheese is melted.
In a small dish, stir together the balsamic vinegar and red pepper flakes. Use as a dipping sauce.

Submitter: 
Diane
Description: 
This is a quick and refreshing no cook vegetarian meal. Basil, mozzarella and tomato on Italian bread.  Great for those hot summer evenings when you don't feel like cooking.Modified from: Basil, Tomato and Mozzarella Sandwich
