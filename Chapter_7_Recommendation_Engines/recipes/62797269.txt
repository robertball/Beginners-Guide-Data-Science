Title:
Better DOUBLE GARBANZOS CON CHORIZO Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz can organic garbanzo beans, undrained
1 package chorizo, well browned in casings, then cut in 1/2 and sliced
2 tbs olive oil
2 large onions chopped
6 cloves garlic, minced
1 red pepper, chopped
2 Roma tomatoes, peeled and chopped
2 tsp oregano
8 servings cooked basmati rice
Pickled jalapenos

Directions:
In a large pan saute the onion and garlic in the olive oil until the onion is just translucent.  Add the chorizo and garbanzos with the liquid, the diced peppers, oregano and tomato.  Cover and reduce heat to low, cook about 25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Serve over hot cooked rice, garnish each plate with the jalapeno rings.
