Title:
Chipolte Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1442581.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Natural Blend Oil (Crisco Olive/Canola Oil)
1 Cup of Parmesan Cheese
5 TableSpoons of Minced Garlic
Chipotle Seasoning (To Taste)
Seasoned Salt (To Taste)

Directions:
Mix oil and garlic, let stand for 15 minutes.
Mix chipotle and season salt, mix well.
Add Parmesan Cheese and mix to texture.

Submitter: 
Casey McLaughlin
Description: 
This chipotle pesto sauce is perfect for flat breads, single serving breads, and much more! I use this recipe with my Chipotle Flat Bread recipe!
