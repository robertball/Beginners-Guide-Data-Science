Title:
Mango Agua Fresca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/402637.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Mangoes
1 Cup Sugar
1 pinch ground cinnamon
Water

Directions:
Peel mangoes.  Remove mango fruit from core, discarding core.  Place mango and 1 cup of water into a pan.  Bring to boil for 10 mintues.  Let cool about 10 minutes.
After cooling place liquid and mango into blender add 1 cup cold water and cinnamon also.  Blend until smooth.  Strain through a sieve into an empty pitcher.  Add sugar to pitcher and fill with cold water to 2 quarts.  Stir to disolve sugar.  Place into fridge until cold and serve.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This is a fresh mango water served througout Mexico.  Make sure you have ripe mangoe's or it will taste bad.
