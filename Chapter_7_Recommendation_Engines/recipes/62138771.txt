Title:
Red Velvet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 oz.) yellow cake mix
1/2 a (3.5 oz.) vanilla instant pudding mix
1 cup water
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 (1 oz.) bottle red food coloring
1 tbsp. cocoa
1 tbsp. distilled white vinegar
4 eggs
1 stick butter,softened
FROSTING:
1 (16 oz.) box confectionerâ€™s sugar
1 (8 oz.) package cream cheese, softened
1 stick butter, softened
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups walnuts or pecans, finely chopped

Directions:
With electric mixer, blend all ingredients for the cake mixture until moistened.
Beat at medium speed 4 minutes.
Pour batter into 4 greased 9-inch cake pans.(Wax paper liner in bottom of pans is helpful)
Bake in a 350 degree oven 15 minutes.
Let layers cool.
Beat the frosting ingredients in mixer, saving 1 cup of nuts to sprinkle between layers and on top.
Note:
Tear strips of waxpaper to place around the bottom layer of the cake prior to icing.
After icing is applied, remove for a professional, and clean platter around the cakes edges.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
