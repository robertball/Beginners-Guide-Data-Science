Title:
Autumn Chicken Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 medium sized thawed chicken breasts
Two medium sweet potatos peeled
Three small sweet red apples
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 tablespoon black pepper
1/2 tablespoon salt
1 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 cup melted butter

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees and put a pot of water onto boil.
Wash sweet potatos and cut into 1/4 inch round slices.
Boil sweet potato rounds until tender. Let dry on paper towels.
While sweet potatos are boiling, whisk melted butter, brown sugar, salt, pepper, and onion powder in a small mixing bowl.
Take tawed chicken breast and, with a basting brush, coat  with the marinade. Put extra brown sugar mixture aside.
Core sweet red apples and cut into 1/4 inch rounds.
Take a 9x13 baking dish and coat with non-stick cooking spray.
Put down a layter of sweet potatos first, then with basting brush, coat sweet potatos with brown sugar mixture.
Alternate layers with apples and sweet potatos, coating each layer with sugar mixture.
Set coated chicken on top and place in oven. Cook until chicken is golden brown and completely cooked.

Submitter: 
Miss Blurton
Description: 
This is an easy and delicious dinner. A hearty dish for autumn and winter dinner parties. A family favorite and sure to please!
