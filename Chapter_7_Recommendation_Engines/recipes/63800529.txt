Title:
Ghoul-ash Spooktacular Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs ground beef (94% lean) (may substitute turkey if desired)
3 garlic cloves, roughly chopped
2 diced onions
2 green bell peppers, diced
8 oz can sliced olives
12 oz can corn
1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
1/2 tablespoon pepper
1 tablespoon seasoned salt
2 tablespoons Italian seasoning
1 tablespoon oregano
1 tablespoon basil
2 dried bay leaves (or 3 fresh)
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 32oz can tomato sauce
2 15oz cans diced tomatos
3 cups water
2 cups elbow macaroni

Directions:
Put 4 tablespoons olive oil to large dutch oven and heat on medium heat.
Add ground beef/turkey and brown.  Drain fat (or leave about half for more flavor).  Remove and set aside.
Add diced onions and garlic, heat until onions turn.
Add salt, pepper, garlic powder, seasoned salt, and Italian seasoning.  Heat and stir until thoroughly mixed.
Add bay leaves and Worcestershire sauce.  Mix and heat.
Add tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, bell peppers, olives, corn, and water.  Bring to boil, then turn heat to low and let simmer for 20 - 25 minutes.
Add macaroni and let simmer until macaroni is soft.

Submitter: 
markfernandez
Description: 
This is a pretty standard goulash, but I like to make it a bit different for Halloween.  Serving the ghoul-ash in mini-pumpkin bowls with gummy spiders and bugs is a big hit with the kids (and some adults!).
