Title:
Vegetarian Yellow Split Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup dried yellow split peas, rinsed
4 cups water
1/2 cup vegetarian sausage style recipe crumbles
2 chicken or vegetarian bullion cubes
1/2 cup chopped yellow onion
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a soup pan, heat split peas, water, bullion cubes, salt and pepper to boiling.
Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes covered with the lid of pan tilted.
Fry onions in a pan until slightly browned and add to soup.
Add the vegetarian sausage crumbles to soup.
Simmer for another 15-20 minutes until thickened, stirring occasionally.
Add more salt and pepper to taste.
Serve.

Submitter: 
Heidi Habeck-Johnson
Description: 
An easy, hearty, healthy split pea soup with vegetarian sausage recipe crumbles. The seasoning from the crumbles give this soup a good flavor.
