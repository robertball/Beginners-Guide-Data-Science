Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2448467.jpg
Ingredients:
SIX GOBFULS OF MARSHUMALLOWS
SIX SPACKLINGS OF MINT CHIPS
SIX DOLLOPS OF MARMALADE
ALL OF THE SPRINKLES
KALE TO GARNISH

Directions:
PREHEAT OVEN TO 1 BILLION FIFTY (LOWER ELEVATIONS SET TO BROIL)
IN HUMONGOUS BOWL MIX MARSHUMALLOWS, MINT CHIPS AND LOVE.  MIX BY HAND UNTIL MALLUMS IS EVENLY DISPERSED.
SPOON MASMURLOWS MIXTURE INTO MUFFINS CUPS.  ADD ONE SINGLUAR DOLLOP OF MARMALADE TO EACH CUP.  GARNISH FESTIIOUSLY WITH KALE.
BAKE IN HELLFIRE FOR 3 MINUTES OR UNTIL MASSUMALLOWS IS GOLDEN BROWN AND VERY FLUFFY.  DEPLOY SPRINKLES TO TASTE.
OPTIONAL: SERVE WITH SIDE OF FLAMES
