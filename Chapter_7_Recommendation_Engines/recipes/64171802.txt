Title:
Chicken and Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Boneless Chicken Breasts, 3/4" cubes
3 Bell Peppers (Red, Green and Yellow), Diced
3 8 Oz cans of Tomato sauce
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp Garlic Powder
1 tsp Onion Powder
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Salt Chicken, let sit.  Pat dry and wipe away excess salt.
Sprinkle chicken with Garlic and Onion powder and brown in pan with olive oil
Add peppers, tomato sauce.  Salt and Pepper to taste.
Cover and simmer on low heat for 20 minutes.
Serve with rice.

Submitter: 
Fred Perry
Description: 
Simple chicken recipe for quick dinner on a budget.
