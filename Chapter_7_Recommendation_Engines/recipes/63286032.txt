Title:
Peaches and Pudding Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2- 3 cups of canned peaches, drained, juice reserved
1 tbsp dry, butterscotch, cook and serve,  pudding mix
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup of reserved peach liquid
Topping:
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1 package, four serving size, cook and serve, butterscotch pudding mix
1/2 cup chopped pecans, optional
1/2 cup butter, melted
1/4 cup sugar
2/3 cup flour
1/2 cup quick cooking oats

Directions:
In an ungreased 1 1/2 quart casserole, combine the peaches, 1 tbsp pudding mix, brown sugar and fruit syrup. Set aside.
In a medium bowl, combine topping ingredients until well blended and crumbly. Sprinkle over fruit mixture. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 to 50 minutes, until deep golden brown and bubbly. Serve warm with ice cream or whipped cream.
