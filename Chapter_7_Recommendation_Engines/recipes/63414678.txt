Title:
TWO-POTATO GRATIN Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/921670.jpg
Ingredients:
1 LR RED ONION
1 TBSP OLIVE OIL
1 1/2 LBS BAKING POTATOES (ABT 3 MEDIUM)
12 OZ SWEET POTATOES (ABT 1 MEDIUM)
5 OZ gRUYERE CHEESE, SHREDDED (1 1/2 CUPS)
1 CUP HALF-AND-HALF
2 TSP CORNSTARCH
1 TBSP BUTTER

Directions:
PREHEAT OVEN TO 400.
TRIM, PEEL AND CUT ONION IN THIN WEDGES.
IN 12" SKILLET, HEAT OIL ON MEDUIM.
ADD ONION IN SINGLE LAYER AND SPRINKLE WITH 1/8 TSP SALT.
COOK 6 MIN OR UNTIL GOLDEN, TURNING OFTEN; COOL SLIGHTLY.
MEANWHILE, PEEL POTATOES; CUT INTO 1/8" THICK ROUNDS.
ARRANGE HALF OF POTATOES AND HALF OF ONION IN SHALLOW 2-QUART BAKING DISH IN SINGLE LAYER, OVERLAPPING SLICES.
SPRINKLE WITH HALF OF THE CHEESE.
ARRANGE REMAINING POTATOES AND ONION ON TOP.
IN 2-QUART SAUCEPAN, WHICK HALF-AND-HALF INTO CORNSTARCH TO DISSOLVE.
ADD BUTTER AND 1/4 EACH OF PEPPER AND SALT.
HEAT ON MEDIUM-HIGH UNTIL BUBBLES BEGIN TO FORM AROUND EDGE, WHICHING.
POUR OVER POTATO MIXTURE.
COVER WITH FOIL; BAKE 30-MINUTES.
UNCOVER, SPRINKLE WITH REMAINING CHEESE AND BAKE 25 MINUTES OR UNTIL GOLDEN AND BUBBLING.
