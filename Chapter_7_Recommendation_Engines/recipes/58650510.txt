Title:
Fall Harvest Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c sugar
3 c chopped apples
2 1/4 c flour
1 1/2 tsp soda
1 tsp salt
2 tsp cinnamon
3/4 c oil
2 eggs
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
1/2 c chopped nuts

Directions:
After chopping apples mix sugar in well and set aside.
Mix all dry ingredients, and add to apples. Mix wet ingredients in BY HAND, no mixer, adding nuts last.
Pour into a  greased 8x10 or 9x9 glass cake pan. Bake 40 to 45 min at 350.
Glaze with a simple powdered sugar or brown sugar glaze.
