Title:
Scalloped Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup finely chopped onions
1/4 cup chopped green or red bell peppers
2 tablespoons butter
1 beaten egg
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup coarsely crushed Ritz crackers
1 8 oz. can creamed corn
1 7 3/4 oz. can whole kernel corn

Directions:
Cook onion and sweet pepper in 1 tablespoon of the butter till tender.
Combine egg, milk, 1/3 cup of the crushed crackers, and a dash pepper.
Stir in onion mixture and corn.  Pour into a greased 1-qt casserole.
Melt remaining butter; toss with remaining crumbs.
Sprinkle crumb mixture atop corn mixture.
Bake in 350F oven about 35 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Hanson
Description: 
For the holidays, when plain corn just won't do!
