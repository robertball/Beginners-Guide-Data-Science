Title:
Choco Peanut Butter Rice Crispies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups rice crispy cereal
1 cup cocco pebble cereal
4 cups mini marshmallows
2 tbsp butter
2 tbsp natural peanut butter

Directions:
Melt butter in large pan on low heat. Add marshmallows and peanut butter and stir over low heat until melted. Remove from heat and stir in cereals. Press into greased 9 by 13 pan. Let cool. Serve same day.

Submitter:

Description: 
Chocolate cereal and peanut butter make these crispy treats special.
