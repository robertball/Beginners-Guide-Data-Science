Title:
Cranberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Fresh Cranberries-Ground
1 Navel Orange-Ground
2 Cups Sugar
1 Cup Globe Grapes-Seeded
1 Cup Pecans-Ground or Broken
1/2 lb. Miniature Marshmallows
1 Small Container of Cool Whip

Directions:
Grind, 1 package of cranberries and a navel orange.
Mix together with 2 cups of sugar.
Cover and leave in fridge 3 hours to overnight.
Drain and mix in 1 cup globe grapes, 1 cup ground or chopped pecans, 1/2 lb miniature marshmallows & 1 small container of Cool Whip.
Mix Well.

Jasmine Gonzalez
Description: 
Sweet and Tart! One of my favorites!
