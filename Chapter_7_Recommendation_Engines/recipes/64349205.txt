Title:
Low Carb Turkey Quinoa Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2110165.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Eggplants, medium to large size
1 lb. ground turkey
1 cup Quinoa, uncooked
1 24 oz.can spaghetti sauce (I like Hunt's)
1 lb. shredded Mazzorella cheese
1 lb. shredded Cheddar Jack cheese
1 small yellow onion, chopped
3 cloves of garlic, minced
Salt and pepper
Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
1. Peel eggplants and sliced lengthwise, 1/8" thick.
2. Heat olive oil in a pan. Use non-stick pan and spray eggplant before putting them in a pan, if you want less oily lasagna.
3. Fry until soft and color has changed.
4. Set aside the eggplant.
5. Put a little olive oil to saute garlic then once fragrant add onions and turkey, stir to break.
6. Add the Quinoa, stir.
7. Season with salt and pepper.
8. Add spaghetti sauce and cook until bubbly.
9. Spread some sauce on a 13"x 9" pan and arrange eggplant then shredded Cheddar Jack cheese.
10. Repeat until you run out of eggplant with sauce on the top.
11. Spread the Mazzorella cheese evenly on top of sauce.
12. Cover with foil and put in a pre-heated 350 degree oven for 45 minutes.
13. Uncover and cook for another 15 minutes to make it lightly brown.
14. Serve hot with Parmesan cheese. (optional)

Submitter: 
Daniel Tan
Description: 
I made this because I need a low carb meal. This is delicious and satisfying. 
The spaghetti sauce has 10 grams of carbs and 6 grams sugar for 1/2 cup.
