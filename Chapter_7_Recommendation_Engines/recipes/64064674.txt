Title:
Burrito sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1130470.jpg
Ingredients:
1can tomato purée
1can water
1 can tomato sauce
1 stick oleo or butter no salt
1/4 cup flour
1 pkg taco seasoning
2 TBSP beef base

Directions:
Take the 1 can tomato purée, sauce, water, taco seasoning and beef base bring to a small boil. In a separate pan melt butter/ oleo and wisk in flour, when combine whip into the sauce until smooth bring back to simmer.
