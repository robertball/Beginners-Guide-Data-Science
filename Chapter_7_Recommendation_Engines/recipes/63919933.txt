Title:
A Good Sub For EAGLE BRAND MILK Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup powered milk
2/3 cup granulated white sugar
1/3 cup boiling water
3 tablespoons melted butter

Directions:
Combine all in a bowl and blend together. Store in fridge, will last about 5-6 days.
in the fridge.
