Title:
Spicy Indian Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991008.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Corn Oil
2 teaspoons Spice Islands® Mustard Seed
1 1/2 teaspoons Spice Islands® Ground Turmeric
1 teaspoon Spice Islands® Ground Ginger
1 medium onion, sliced
1 head cauliflower, cut into flowerets
1 1/2 teaspoons Spice Islands® Sea Salt
1/2 teaspoon Spice Islands® Crushed Red Pepper
2 tablespoons lemon juice
2 tablespoons chopped, fresh cilantro

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet. When hot, add mustard seeds and cover. When seeds stop spattering, add turmeric, ginger and onions. Fry for 3 minutes.
Stir in cauliflower, salt, crushed red pepper and lemon juice. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes or until tender. Sprinkle with cilantro and serve.

Submitter: 
Spice Islands®
Description: 
Spice up your cauliflower with this Indian inspired side dish.
