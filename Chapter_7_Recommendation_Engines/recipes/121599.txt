Title:
Cocoa Munch Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984674.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups CheeriosÂ® cereal
4 cups Chex cereal (any variety)
1 cup slivered almonds
2 tablespoons baking cocoa
2 tablespoons sugar
1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted
1 cup raisins
1 (12 ounce) package vanilla or white chips

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the cereals and almonds. In a small bowl, combine cocoa, sugar and butter. Pour over cereal mixture and toss to coat. Pour into a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking pan. Bake at 250 degrees F for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes. Cool completely. Stir in raisins and chips.
