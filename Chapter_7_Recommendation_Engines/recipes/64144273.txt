Title:
Baked Soy Lemon Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup soy sauce
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon vegetable oil
4 pork chops

Directions:
In a shallow dish, combine soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, lemon juice, pepper and oil. Add pork chops, and turn to coat evenly. Cover, and refrigerate for at least 1/2 hour.
Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
Place marinated pork chops in a roasting pan, and bake in  preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes, basting meat often with marinade.

Submitter: 
Jacque
Description: 
Pork chops are marinated in a soy, lemon, and garlic sauce before baking. Serve with a fresh garden salad and mashed potatoes for a terrific meal. Bake in marinade.Modified from: Baked Soy Lemon Chops
