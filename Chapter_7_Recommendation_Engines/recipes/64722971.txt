Title:
Emergency Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Unpeeled raw potatoes cubed
1 onion finely chopped or 1/2 C. dried onion
2 stalks celery diced
32 oz. chicken broth
2-3 chicken bouillon cubes
1 pint half and half
1# bacon cooked and crumbled or 3 oz.  Real Bacon Bits
1 10.5 oz. can Cream of Mushroom soup
2 C. shredded cheddar cheese
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
1.  In a large stock pot combine celery, chicken stock and bouillon cubes.
Bring to a boil and boil for 15 minutes until potatoes are almost done. Add water if needed to cover potatoes.
2.  Add half and half, bacon, and Cream of Mushroom soup. Stir until creamy. Simmer for approx. 15 min. and potatoes are done.
3.  Serve with cheese as a garnish.

Submitter: 
Jim
Description: 
An easy and delicious comfort soup. When you're short on time but want the real thing, this is it.
