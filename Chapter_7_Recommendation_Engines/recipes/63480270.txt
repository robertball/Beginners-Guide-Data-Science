Title:
Salad with Honey-Mustard Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123202.jpg
Ingredients:
Dressing:
1 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup vinegar
1/4 cup honey
2 Tbsp. lemon juice
1 tsp. onion powder
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. celery seed
1 tsp. ground mustard
1 tsp. paprika
Salad:
1/2 cup almonds
3 tbsp. sugar
9 cups torn romaine
1 cup (4 oz.) shredded cheddar
2 hard cooked eggs

Directions:
In saucepan combine oil, sugar, vinegar, honey, lemon juice and seasonings.  Cook until sugar disolves.  Cool.
In a skillet over low heat, cook 1/2 cup slivered almonds in 3 tbsp. sugar until nuts are glazed.  Cool.
Top romaine with cheese, eggs and glazed almonds.
Drizzle with dressing.
