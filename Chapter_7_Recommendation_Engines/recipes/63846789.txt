Title:
Easy Seasoned Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068444.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large flour tortillas
2 Tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
2 Tablespoons olive oil
Seasoned salt
Freshly ground black pepper
Seasonings and seeds of your choice, such as:
Sesame seeds
Fennel seeds
Crushed Rosemary

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Put tortilla on baking sheet. Brush each tortilla lightly with butter; brush on thin layer of olive oil. Sprinkle lightly with seasoned salt and pepper. Sprinkle the seeds and herbs to your taste.  Using a pizza cutter, slice the tortilla into eight wedges. Bake at 350 for 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Marcella
Description: 
Delicious, crispy and easy. The possibilities are endless as far as seasonings.
