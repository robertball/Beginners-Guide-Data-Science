Title:
Marie's Healthy Breakfast Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 to 1 cup Spinach
1 Frozen Banana
1/2 Small Avocado
1/4 cup Almond Milk
1/2 tsp Vanilla
1/4 tsp Cinnamon

Directions:
Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
fordmarie
Description: 
The avocado makes this smoothie almost more rich in texture than a milkshake. What a healthy and satisfying breakfast!
