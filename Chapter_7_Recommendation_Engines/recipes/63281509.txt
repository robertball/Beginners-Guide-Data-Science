Title:
Slow Cooker Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Chocolate Cake Mix any brand
3.9 oz. instant chocolate pudding mix
4 eggs
1 c. water
3/4 c oil
2 c. sour cream or yogurt
1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Spray a 4 qt slow cooker with cooking spray
In a large bowl add the cake mix, pudding mix, eggs, water, oil, sour cream/yogurt,  Beat well.  Stir in chocolate chips.
Pour mixture intot he slow cooker and cook on LOW for 4 hours.  Remove crock from cooker when done so it will not continur to cook.
Serve with Ice Cream

Super moist cake mad in 4 hours.
