Title:
Katies Pumpkin Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups white sugar
1 1/4 cups oil  (or 3/4 cup applesauce & 1/2 cup oil)
2 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups canned pumpkin
4 eggs
2 cups all-purpose flour
3 teaspoons baking powder
2 teaspoons baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp. ground cloves
1/4 tsp. ground ginger
1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
Very, very moist and delicious. No Frosting Needed! As a bundt cake, bake for 45 minutes, For muffins, approximately 28 3/4 full), baked for 18-20 minutes.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease & flour a 12x18 inch pan.
Sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and cinnamon. Set aside.
In a large bowl combine sugar and oil.
Blend in vanilla and pumpkin.
Beat in eggs one at a time.
Gradually beat in flour mixture.
Stir in nuts.
Spread batter into prepared 12x18 inch pan.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.
Allow to cool, and Ice with your favorite cream cheese iceing.
