Title:
Jalapeno Brie Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz brie (use a good quality one - triple cream)
1 jar jalapeno pepper jelly
1 pkg (15 count) phyllo pastry shells

Directions:
Set cups on a cookie sheet.  Fill each with a tsp of softened brie; top with 1/2 tsp pepper jelly and bake in a 400 degree oven 4-5 minutes or til melted.

Submitter: 
Terry Wyatt
Description: 
Received this from a friend for a nice quick holiday appetizer that is delicious. After experimenting with different bries I found that Belletoile was the best. It is a triple cream brie with a buttery flavor.
