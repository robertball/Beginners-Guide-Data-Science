Title:
Banana, Orange, and Ginger Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2829120.jpg
Ingredients:
1 orange, peeled
1/2 banana
3 ice cubes
2 teaspoons honey
1/2 teaspoon grated fresh ginger root, or to taste
1/2 cup plain yogurt

Directions:
Layer orange, banana, ice cubes, honey, and ginger in the blender; top with yogurt. Blend until smooth.
