Title:
Caramel Apple Pie Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 graham cracker pie crust
2 boxes of cream cheese
1 21oz can of apple pie filling
1/2 cup of sugar
1/2 tsp of vanilla
1/2 cup caramel topping (I used Smuckers)
2 eggs

Directions:
*Prehead oven to 350*
1. Put 1/4 of the pie filling in the bottom of the pie crust.
2. Combine cream cheese, sugar and vanilla... blend until smooth. Add 2 eggs. Pour into pie crust. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
3. While cheesecake cools, mix remaining 3/4 of apple pie filling and 1/2 cup of caramel in sauce pan until it is warm and mixes together easily. Spread caramel/apple mixture over cheesecake.
