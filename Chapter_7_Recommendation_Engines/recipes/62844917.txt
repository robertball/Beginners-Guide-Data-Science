Title:
Saffron Couscous Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Israeli couscous
3 cups chicken or vegetable) broth
1 pinch Saffron threads soaked in 1/2 cup dry white wine for 10 minutes?
1 1/2 tsp salt
2 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
In 4-qt pot, heat with olive oil over medium heat. When oil is shimmering, add couscous and toast for 3 minutes.
Carefully stir in broth, saffron + white wine, and salt. Bring to a boil over high heat.
Once it starts to boil, immediately cover and turn to low heat.
Let couscous cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes. Add more broth if couscous starts to stick to the bottom and cooking is not yet complete.
Turn off the heat and let it sit for another 5 minutes.

Description: 
Easy fragrant side dish. Great with grilled meat or fish!
