Title:
Honeydew Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/996243.jpg
Ingredients:
1 honeydew melon - halved, seeded, and peeled
1 tablespoon honey
1/4 teaspoon white sugar

Directions:
Blend honeydew, honey, and sugar in a blender on low speed until smooth, about 1 1/2 minutes.

Submitter: 
Junoir Chef
Description: 
A refreshing summer drink. Easier than a piece of cake!
