Title:
"Maple Sugar" Pancake Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/910830.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1 cup brown sugar (7.5 oz)
1/2 cup maple sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 tsp vanilla
1 TBSP butter

Directions:
Bring the water and all the sugars to a boil over medium/high heat.  When it starts to boil, whisk it until it boils regardless of whisking.  Turn heat down to low and whisk in butter and vanilla
