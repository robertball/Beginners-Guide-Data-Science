Title:
Cheddar Bay Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups Bisquick
4 tablespoons COLD butter
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
3/4 cup buttermilk or 3/4 cup milk and 1 1/2 tablespoons white vinegar, let sit 20 minutes
2/3 cups sharp finely shredded cheddar cheese
Garlic herb butter:
1/3 cup butter, melted
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon dried parsley flakes
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Line a large cookie sheet with parchment paper. Preheat oven to 425. In medium mixing bowl combine Bisquick and garlic powder; cut the cold butter into Bisquick mixture. Add buttermilk or sour milk, stirring just enough to mix dry into wet. Stir in the cheddar cheese. Place 1/4 cup spoonfulls 2 inches apart onto prepared cookie sheet. You will probably get 9 to 10 biscuits. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, until golden brown. to the melted butter stir in the onion and garlic powders, alt and parsley. Using small spoon or pastry brush spoon or brush butter mixture ovre the biscuits as soon as they come out of the oven.
