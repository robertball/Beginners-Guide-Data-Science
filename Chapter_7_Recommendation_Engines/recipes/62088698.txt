Title:
red pepper soup with hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T olive oil
1 large onion, chopped
2 red peppers, chopped
3 T hummus with roasted red peppers, store bought
2 cups (fat-free) chicken broth
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup (fat-free) half and half

Directions:
Saute peppers and onions in oil, about 5 minutes.
Add chicken broth and salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, lower heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
Allow to cool somewhat and puree in food processsor or blender. Add hummus and half and half and puree until smooth. Allow flavors to meld. Serve hot. Serves 2.

Submitter: 
simone stevin
Description: 
This red pepper soup uses store bought hummus to zip up the taste. So easy to make and so tasty.
