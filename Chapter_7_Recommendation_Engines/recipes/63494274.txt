Title:
Fit Protein Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1004124.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup milk
3/4 cup crushed ice
1 banana
1 serving of whey protein powder
2 Splenda packets

Directions:
Place all ingredients into blender and set on smoothie setting. Blend and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Misty
Description: 
Cut back on banana and throw in strawberries for some change. This is a great smoothie for diabetics, weight lifters, and low-carbers.
