Title:
Sausage and Shrimp Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1182590.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound whole wheat pasta
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 pound Italian sausage
1 medium onion, sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 container cherry tomatoes
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 pound  large shrimp, peeled and deveined (tails removed)
1 cup frozen peas
1 teaspoon sea salt
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground pepper

Directions:
Cook the pasta according to package (less 3 minutes) in a large salted pot.
Meanwhile, heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
Add the sausage and onions and cook, breaking the sausage up with a spoon and stirring the onions occasionally, for 10 minutes. Add in garlic and stir around.
Add the tomatoes, chicken broth, shrimp, peas, salt and pepper.
Simmer, stirring occasionally, until the shrimp are opaque throughout and the sauce has slightly thickened, 3 to 5 minutes.
When pasta is done, drain (reserve 1 cup of water) and add pasta in pot.  Let continue cooking until your doneness.  If the sauce it too think either add in more chicken broth or pasta water.
Top with freshly grated parmesan cheese then serve!
