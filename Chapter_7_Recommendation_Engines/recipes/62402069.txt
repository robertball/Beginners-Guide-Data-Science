Title:
Mama's Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/431845.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Stick Butter
1 Cup Brown Sugar
1 Cup Flour
1 Cup Oatmeal
1-1/2 Teaspoons Cinnamon
6-8 Red Apples

Directions:
Peel and dice apples into slices.  Place apples into 9 x 13 baking dish.
Melt butter.  Mix brown sugar, flour, cinnamon and oatmeal together in a seperate bowl.  Add melted butter to bowl, mix through.
Spread mixture over apples.  Bake in oven at 350 degrees for about 45-50 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
An old family recipe.  I like to eat mine fresh out of the oven with caramel drizzeled over it and a scoop of vanilla ice cream if I have it!
