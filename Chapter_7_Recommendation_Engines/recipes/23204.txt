Title:
French Herb Wine Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup LAND O LAKESÂ® Butter, softened*
2 tablespoons dry white wine or sherry
1 tablespoon finely chopped shallots
2 teaspoons chopped fresh parsley*
3/4 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme leaves**
1/2 teaspoon finely chopped fresh garlic

Directions:
Beat butter in small bowl at medium speed until creamy (1 to 2 minutes).  Reduce speed to low.  Add all remaining ingredients; continue beating until well mixed (1 to 2 minutes).
Cover; refrigerate at least 1 hour to enhance flavors.

Submitter: 
Land O'Lakes, Inc.
Description: 
This rich herbed butter can accent any meat entree.
