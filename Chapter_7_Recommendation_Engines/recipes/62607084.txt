Title:
Angelic Cake and Berries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/563384.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup lemon sorbet
2 teaspoons lemon zest
1/2 pint blackberries
1/2 pint strawberries, sliced
1/2 pint raspberries
1 store-bought angel food cake

Directions:
Melt sorbet over low heat with zest in a small sauce pot. Stir in berries to coat. Cut cake and pour berries and sauce over top.
