Title:
Aunt Debbi's Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Chicken Breasts (Breast Halves)
6 Slices of Baby Swiss Cheese
2 10 oz. Cans of Cream of Chicken Soup
3-4 oz by weight of Chicken Flavored Stove Top Stuffing (Dry)
8 TBS. Butter Sliced Thin

Directions:
Grease a 9x13 glass baking dish with non stick cooking spray.
Place Chicken in the dish and top with the sliced swiss cheese.
Spread cream of chicken soup evenly over all six pieces.
Sprinkle Stove Top stuffing mix evenly over entire casserole.
Place thinly sliced butter evenly over entire Casserole.
Cover in Foil. Bake for 50 Min @ 350 then uncover and bake for an additional 20 min. or until internal temp. of chicken registers 165 degrees.
