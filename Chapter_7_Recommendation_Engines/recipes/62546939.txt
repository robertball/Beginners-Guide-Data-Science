Title:
Guava Jelly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups guava juice
4 1/2 cups sugar
1/4 cup lime juice
1 package liquid pectin

Directions:
Place juice and pectin in a large stainless steel pot and bring to a boil.
Add sugar.
Bring to a boil again.  Boil for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat, skim, pour into sterilized jars, and seal.
Process in a boiling water bath for 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
Delicious on toast or as a glaze for meats.
