Title:
Quick and Easy Fruit Salsa with Whole Wheat Cinnamon Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 cups of frozen field berries and/or strawberries, thawed
1 large granny smith apple, cored (leave skin on)
1 tsp. of lemon juice
1 package of 10" whole wheat tortillas
1 table spoon cinnamon
3 table spoons white sugar
2 table spoons olive oil

Directions:
Cut the whole wheat cinnamon tortillas into bite sized chips, place in a large ziploc bag.
Add the olive oil. Seal the bag and shake until coated.
Add the cinnamon and white sugar to the bag. Seal and shake once again until coated.
Spread the chips on a cookie sheet and bake for about 7-10 minutes at 350 until crispy. Do not over bake!
In a blender, add the berries, apple and lemon juice. Blend for approximately 5-10 seconds until coarsly blended.
Pour the salsa into a bowl and serve with the cinnamon chips.
