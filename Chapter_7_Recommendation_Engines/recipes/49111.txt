Title:
Patriotic Pork Mesquite BBQ Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds pork baby back ribs
1 (1.06 ounce) package McCormick® Grill Mates® Mesquite Marinade Mix
1/2 cup ketchup
1/3 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup orange juice

Directions:
Preheat grill to medium heat. Place ribs on grill over indirect heat. Grill until ribs are done and tender, 1 to 1 1/2 hours; turn occasionally.
Meanwhile, in small bowl, stir together remaining ingredients. Baste ribs with BBQ sauce during last 20 minutes of grilling time, turning as necessary to coat all surfaces.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
Grill Mates Mesquite Marinade Mix makes these succulent baby back ribs the hit of any summer barbecue.
