Title:
Racquel’s Homemade Lumpias Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/423194.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Turkey
1 Carrot (diced)
½ head Cabbage (chopped)
1 can Corn
2 tbs. Soy Sauce
1 tsp. Ground Pepper
1 cup Cheese
2 tbs. Grapeseed Oil
5 cloves Garlic
1 tsp. Sea Salt
25 pieces Lumpia Wrappers

Directions:
Heat large pan to medium heat add Grapeseed oil
Add minced garlic and ground turkey, cook until browned Add diced carrots, corn and cabbage Add ground pepper, sea salt and soy sauce
Drain 5 minutes
Add cheese of choice/mix well
Wrap lumpias
Deep fry and enjoy!

Submitter: 
pinay lumpia winner
Description: 
Racquel Magana won the Good Day Sacramento 4th Annual Lumpia Contest on November 25, 2009!

These measurements make 25 lumpias. Adjust accordingly.
