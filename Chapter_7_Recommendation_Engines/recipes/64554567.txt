Title:
Gumbo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c beef broth
14 oz can tomatoes, undrained
1 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp dried thyme
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (more or less to taste)
1-2 TBS olive oil
4 cloves garlic, chopped
1 onion, chopped
1/2 c celery, chopped
1 green pepper, chopped
2/3 lb. shrimp, peeled and deveined
2 boneless chicken breasts, cooked (grilled) cubed
1 lb. andouille sausage, sliced
Hot cooked rice

Directions:
Saute celery, onion, green pepper till crisp tender in olive oil.  Add garlic and cook a bit more.  Add beef broth, tomatoes, sugar, thyme, chili powder, pepper, and cayenne pepper.  Simmer 10 minutes.  Add shrimp, cooked chicken and sausage and simmer until shrimp are pink.
