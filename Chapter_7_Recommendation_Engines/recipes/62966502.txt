Title:
Morrocan Chicken Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkgs. (20 oz. each) boneless Chicken Thighs, sliced
2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed.
5 carrots, peeled and sliced.
1 medium sweet yellow onion, diced.
2 cloves garlic, minced.
2 cans (4 oz. each) diced mild green chilis
1 can (8 oz) tomato sauce
1 can (15 oz) coconut milk
1 cup chicken broth
1 can (15 oz.) garbanzo beans undrained
1 can (15 oz.) sweet peas undrained
1 cup yellow raisins
2 Tbsp Curry powder
1 1/3 Tbsp fish sauce
1 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
salt and pepper to taste
opt. fresh sage to taste

Directions:
In a 7 quart crockpot, on high temperature:
Combine diced sweet potatoes, carrots, onion, garlic, salt, pepper, sage, and chicken broth.
Add in chicken. Sprinkle curry powder over chicken, stir to coat.
Add in cans of green chili, tomato sauce, coconut milk, garbanzo beans, peas, and fish sauce.
Lastly add the raisins, and cinnamon. Stir thoroughly.
Leave in crockpot, on high, stirring occasionally, for about 6 hours, or until chicken is cooked through and stew is hot and bubbly.

Description: 
A delicious and easy fix-it-and-forget it recipe. A mild curry with sweet raisins makes a great comfort food. Serve over rice!
