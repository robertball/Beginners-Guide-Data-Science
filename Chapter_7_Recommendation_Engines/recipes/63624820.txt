Title:
amaranth(red leaves) with onion&garlic (south indian) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large bunch of fresh amaranth,
15 cloves of garlic,
1 large onion,
3 table spoons vegetable oil ,
1 cube of butter/2 tea spoons of ghee,
5 teaspoon of red chili powder,
1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
water.
2 tamarind
for seasoning..
1 tablespoon vegetable oil,
1 teaspoon mustard seeds,
1 teaspoon blakgram,
2 red chillies,
2 cloves garlic,
2 curry leaves stem.

Directions:
wash amaranth leaves and cut them into small pieces including stem.Take a non stick vessel, switch on your stove and keep the vessel and add 3 tablespoons of oil.
add all the leaves and stem.now make the paste of onion and cloves and add  it on the top of the leaves.
now add chillipowder, salt, turmeric, tamarind and pour half cup of water to it
let it cook till the water goes and add butter.
for seasoning
add oil in a sauce pan and heat it
add mustard seeds,red chillies,black gram,garlic and curry leaves let it pop and add it in the amaranth.
and you can eat it with rice,dosa or idlli.
it tastes like heaven.
