Title:
Breakfast Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/647161.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large or 3 medium potatoes
OIl
1/4 cup chopped onions
1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
5 eggs
1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup shredded Cheddar Jack
Salsa
Sour Cream

Directions:
Wash and cut potatoes in rough wedges.
Boil in salted water until almost done.
Fry potatoes, onions, and green peppers in skillet.
Remove potato mixture and keep warm.
Whisk eggs and milk together until well combined.
Scramble eggs in skillet used for potatoes until almost done; remove from heat and cover with skillet to lid to finish cooking.
Put potato mixture on serving plate.
Top with scrambled eggs.
Sprinkle with cheese.
Top with salsa and sour cream, if desired.
