Title:
Creamy Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lb potatoes - cooked and mashed
1 - 8oz. cream cheese
1 c. half and half
1/2 c. margarine
1 t. salt
1 t. onion salt
1/4 t. pepper
1 t. seasoned salt

Directions:
Mix all together the day before you plan to serve. Refrigerate over night. Bake 1/2 hour at 350 degrees. Or just bake and eat.
