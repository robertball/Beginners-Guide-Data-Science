Title:
Grilled cheese with a kick! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/746153.jpg
Ingredients:
2 slices of bread
2 slices of sharp cheddar
2 tbsp of garlic cream cheese
1 1/2 tbsp of hot banana peppers
2 slices of hot genoa salami
1 tbsp of garlic butter

Directions:
Heat the garlic butter in a small skillet set to medium heat.
Spread cream cheese onto both bread slices. Lay out slices of cheese on either side. Place banana peppers & salami on one side and put the 2 slices together
Cook for a 2-3 minutes on each side, pressing down with a spatula to make sure the cheese melts.

Submitter: 
Raquel Teixeira
Description: 
A simple way to change a classic into a modern kicked up version!
