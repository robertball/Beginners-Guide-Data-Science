Title:
Cream Chicken with Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast
black pepper
paprika
cumin
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 cup milk
1/2 cup mushrooms (optional)
1/2 cup onion (optional)

Directions:
Season chicken with pepper, paprika and cumin to taste.
Brown chicken on both sides in electric skillet.
Mix milk with soup and pour over chicken.
Add mushrooms and onion and cover.
Cook, rotating chicken periodically for 20-30 minutes until cooked.
Serve with rice.
