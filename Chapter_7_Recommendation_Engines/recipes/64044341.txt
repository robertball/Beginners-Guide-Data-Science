Title:
Favorite Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 each chicken breast
1 cup breadcrumbs
1 tablespoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
2 eggs
1/4 cup olive oil

Directions:
Fillet the chicken breast into eight pieces and tenderize.
Mix the breadcrumbs, parsley, garlic powder, salt and pepper in a shallow dish
Beat the eggs in another shallow dish.
Heat the olive oil in a non-stick skillet.
Dip the chicken fillets into the egg first and then coat on both sides with the breadcrum mixture.
Fry each side of the chicken fillets in the olive oil until golden brown and cooked all of the way through.
