Title:
Queso con Carne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Velveta block
1/2 c Milk
2 lb Ground Beef
1 pkg Taco Seasoning
4-5 cans Rotel

Directions:
Cut Velveta into 1" cubes and place in crock pot on high with milk.  Allow Velveta to begin melting.  In a large skillet brown ground beef and drain.  Add taco seasoning and Rotel to the ground beef.  Stir and bring to a boil.  Pour ground beef mixture into crock pot and heat until Velveta is melted.  Dip will thicken in the crock pot.  Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Patti
Description: 
This queso dip will not last at any party or gathering, and all of your guests will be coming back for more.
