Title:
Taco Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup dried onion flakes
3 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
3 T ground cumin
2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Place in dry, covered container; shake well to mix.  To season, use 2-5 T  of mix with 1/2 c water for each pound of meat or beans.
