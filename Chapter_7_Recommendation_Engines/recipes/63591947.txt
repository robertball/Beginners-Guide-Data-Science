Title:
Easy Spicy Chinese Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz whole wheat pasta
2 tbsp chunky peanutbutter
1.5tsp low sodium soy sauce
1.5tsp rice vinegar
dash cayenne
1tbsp scallion, finely chopped
(Add chicken broth or veg stock if sauce comes out too thick

Directions:
Cook whole wheat pasta over medium heat until done, drain
In saucepan, combine chunky peanut butter, soy sauce, rice vinegar and cayenne over medium heat until it become a sauce.  If it is too thick, add chick or veggie broth or a little water to thin it out a little
Once sauce in ready, add pasta and toss till fully coated with sauce.  Sprinkle scallion on top and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Casey McMahon
Description: 
I like this because its flavorful, but very quick and easy and uses ingredients that you can normally find around the house easily.  Nice for a quick lunch
