Title:
Melon Heaven Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 large ripe cantaloupe
2 quarts cold water
1 large honeydew melon

Directions:
Grate cantaloupe and place in a 2 quart pitcher.  Fill the pitcher will water and place in the refrigerator overnight.
Use a balling spoon to make little balls out of the honeydew melon.  Add the honeydew melon balls to the cantaloupe mixture just before serving.

Submitter: 
ECKO
Description: 
My mom is Filipino and this is a drink that she makes often during the summer.  It is very delicious and a great way to cool off in the summertime.
