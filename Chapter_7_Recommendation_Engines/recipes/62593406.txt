Title:
Jell-o Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg jello
1/2 c. Warm Water
1 1/2 c. mini marshmallows

Directions:
Spray 8" square pan with non-stick spray.
Use paper towel to spread on bottom & sides.
Mix jell-o and water and microwave on high 1 1/2 minutes.
Stir to dissolve completely
Add marshmallows to gelatin.  Microwave 1 minute (OR
LESS--watch VERY carefully) or until marshmallows are
almost melted.  Stir until completely melted and mixture
is smooth (creamy layer will float to top.
