Title:
Cheddar Broccoli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups chopped broccoli
½ cup chopped celery
½ cup chopped onion
2- (10 oz) cans of chicken broth
Salt and pepper to taste
¼ cup flour
3 cups milk
1 cup grated cheese

Directions:
Place broccoli, onion, broth, celery, butter and salt in a large sauce pan.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat and cover. Simmer for 10 minutes or until veggies are tender.
Smoothly combine flour and milk, and stir into veggie mixture.
Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture comes to a boil and thickens.
Add cheese and stir until melted.
Ladle into bowls and garnish with more cheddar cheese.
