Title:
Great Grandma Hoch's Green Tomato Mince Meat Pie Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6# Green Tomatoes
6# Apples
2# Dark Brown Sugar
4# Raisins
4T Salt
2C Suet
2C Apple Cider Vinegar
1  Lemon
1  Orange
4T Cinnamon
4t Cloves
2t Nutmeg

Directions:
Easy. Chop all above and mix together. Cook down on low/med. heat or in a crock pot till thickened. Process in quart canning jars and seal. Let it sit for as long as you want (weeks, months, years?). The longer the better. Prepare two pie crusts and make a pie. Can also be used in any recipe calling for mincemeat. Enjoy.
