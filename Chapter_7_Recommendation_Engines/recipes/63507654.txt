Title:
Summer Garden Crustless Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/958220.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium yellow onion minced
3 cloves of garlic minced
1 TBS olive oil plus a little more to oil pie plate.
5 cups kale, ribs removed and chopped
1 cup diced tomato
1/2 cup shredded carrots
1/4 tsp. kosher salt
2 grinds fresh pepper or to taste
5 eggs
3/4 whole milk
1 7.5 ounce bag Sargento 4 States Cheddar Cheese
1/4 cup fresh Italian/flat leaf parsley minced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Heat oil over medium heat.
Cook onion and garlic until fragrant.
Add chopped kale, salt and pepper cook stirring occasionally for 5 minutes.
Add tomatoes and carrots, cook an additional 5 minutes.
Set pan aside to cool while preparing the rest of the ingredients.
Whisk eggs and milk together in a medium sized bowl. Stir in Sargento 4 States cheddar.
To egg mixture add Kale mixture.
Add parsley.
Stir to combine.
Poor into oiled pie dish.
Bake for 50 minutes.
Let cool a few minutes before slicing.

Submitter: 
Singer6
Description: 
This is one of my favorite recipes to make in the summer when the vegetable garden is offering plenty of fresh vegetables.  You might want to use less kale if you prefer a more fluffy quiche.
