Title:
Fresh-Squeezed Salty Dog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/92948.jpg
Ingredients:
1 grapefruit, juiced
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger gin
salt to taste

Directions:
Shake together the grapefruit juice, gin, and a generous amount of salt in a cocktail shaker.  Pour into a glass over ice.
