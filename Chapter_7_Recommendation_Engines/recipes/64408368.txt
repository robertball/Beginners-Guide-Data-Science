An Awesome Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

1 cup cooked buckwheat groats
2 large avocados, diced
1 fresh jalapeno, or to taste
1 small fresh red onion, minced
Cilantro
1 lime
EVOO, to taste

Directions:
Mix together and eat.
