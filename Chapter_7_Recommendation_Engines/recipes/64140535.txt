Title:
Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp unsalted butter
1/2 cup onion, minced finely
1 cup mushrooms, finely chopped
2 lb lean ground beef
1/4 cup parsley
1 large egg, beaten
2 tbsp lemon zest
1/2 tsp dried oregano
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup tomato sauce
2 tbsp red wine
1 small clove of garlic, minced
1/4 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Melt butter in skillet over medium heat. Saute shallots until tender.
Transfer to a large bowl and add mushrooms, beef, parsley, egg, and lemon zest.
Add oregano, salt and pepper to taste, then let stand in refrigerator 1 hour.
Form into balls about the size of ping pong balls and then set aside.
Heat olive oil in skillet over medium high heat. Cook meatballs until they brown evenly on all sides.
Mix tomato sauce, wine, garlic and cinnamon into skillet. Cook until well blended. Season with pepper. Serve with meatballs.

Submitter: 
Sarah Pryor
Description: 
Meatballs with red wine tomato sauce work as an appetizer or side dish (or in a sandwich!)
