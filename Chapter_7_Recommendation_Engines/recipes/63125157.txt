Title:
Cajun Chicken Pasta on the Lighter Side Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/805628.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz uncooked linguinie
1 pound chicken breast strips
1-2 tsp cajun seasoning
1 tbs olive oil
1 medium red bell pepper, thinly sliced
1 medium yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
8 oz fresh mushrooms, sliced
1/2 red onion, sliced
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 medium tomatoes, diced
1 cup fat free low sodium chicken broth
1/3 cup skim milk
1 tbs flour
3 tbs light cream cheese
fresh cracked pepper
2 scallions, chopped
salt to taste
cooking spray

Directions:
Prep all vegetables.  In a small blender make a slurry by combining milk, flour and cream cheese.  Set aside.  Season chicken generously with cajun seasoning, garlic powder and salt.
Prepare pasta in salted water.
Heat a large heavy nonstick skillet over medium high heat; spray with cooking spray and add half of the chicken.  Saute 5-6 minutes or until done, set aside on a plate and repeat with the remaining chicken.  Set aside.
Add olive oil to the skillet and reduce to medium heat; add bell peppers, onions and garlic to skillet, saute 3-4 minutes.  Add mushrooms and tomatoes and saute 3-4 more minutes or until vegetables are tender.  Season with 1/4 tsp salt, garlic powder and cracked pepper to taste.  Reduce heat to medium low add chicken broth and pour in slurry stirring about 2 minutes.  Return chicken to skillet; adjust salt and cajun seasoning to taste, cook another minute or two until hot, then add linguine and toss well to coat.  Top with chopped scallions and serve.
