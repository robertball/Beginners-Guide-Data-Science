Title:
Mexican Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb to 1 lb chorizo (depending on mood)
1/2 large onion, chopped
2 cups rice
1 to 2 tablespoons canola oil
1 large jar Pace Mild salsa
1 carton low sodium chicken broth
1 to 2 cans diced green mild chilis, undrained
2 cubes powdered cilantro
1 to 1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder
1 to 1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 teaspoon Goya light seasoning
juice of 1 lime

Directions:
Remove chorizo from casing and place in large dutch oven or frying pan (with lid).  Cook over medium heat breaking up with the back of a spoon for 2 to 3 minutes.
Add chopped onion and rice.  Cook until chorizo is cooked and rice is starting to brown (about 8 to 10 minutes).  Add 1 to 2 tablespoons canola oil if needed.
Add salsa, chicken broth, chilis, cilantro, chili powder, garlic powder, Goya seasoning and lime juice.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and cook until rice is tender.  If needed, add additional water 1/4 to 1/2 cup at a time.
