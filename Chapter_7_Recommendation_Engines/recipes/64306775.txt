Title:
*Overnight* Sausage Egg Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 eggs
2-1/4 cups milk
1-1/2 teaspoons ground mustard
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 pound Pork Sausage Roll, cooked and drained
2 cups cubed white bread
1-1/2 cups (6 ounces) shredded cheddar cheese
Read more:

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk the eggs, milk, mustard and salt. Stir in the sausage, bread cubes and cheese. Pour into a greased 13-in. x 9-in. baking dish. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
Remove from the refrigerator 30 minutes before baking. Bake, uncovered, at 350Â° for 30-40 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
