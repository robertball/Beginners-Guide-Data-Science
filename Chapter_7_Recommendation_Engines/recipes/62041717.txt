Title:
Mixican Egg Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 corn tortillas
12 eggs
1/2 cup milk
4 oz. (1 cup) shredded Cheddar cheese
4 oz. (1 cup) shredded Montery jack cheese
1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
4-oz. can diced green chilles, drained
1 tablespoon oil
1 1/4 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
1/2 medium green bell pepper, cut into 1 -inch long strips
10-oz. can mild enchilada sauce.

Directions:
Heat oven to 350. Generously grease 13x9-inch baking dish.  Warm tortillas as directed on package; overlapping edges of tortillas.
In large bowl, beast eggs and milk until well blended.  Stir in cheeses, red pepper and chiles.  Pour egg mixture over tortillas.  Bake at  350 for 25 to 35 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out cleaqn.  Meanwhile, heat oil in small saucepan over medium heat.  Add mushrooms and green pepper;cook and stir until tender.  Add enchilada sauce; heat thoroughly.  Serve over baked egg mixture.
