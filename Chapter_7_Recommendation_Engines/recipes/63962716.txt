Title:
Almond Cheese Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup almonds, slivered
1 7/8 ounces sesame seeds
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
2 tablespoons parmesan cheese, grated
2 tablespoons shallot, minced
1/4 teaspoon salt
dash white pepper

Directions:
Arrange almonds on baking pan, bake at 350 for 5 minutes, cool.
Pour sesame seeds into a 10-inch skillet and stir over medium heat until golden, cool.  Beat cream cheese, parmesan cheese, shallots, salt & pepper until fluffy.  Stir in sesame seeds.  Refrigerate until its firm enough to handle (about 1 hour).  Shape into 40 balls *, spread nuts on waxed paper lined baking sheets, roll sides and bottoms of balls in almonds.  Chill several hours or overnight.
*  Or just make one big ball.  Can also add 1 tsp garlic for flavor.
