Title:
Quick Strawberry Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
1 heaping tbsp strawberry jam

Directions:
Combine ingredients and whisk or blend in food processor until  smooth.
Toss with salad greens.

Submitter: 
Ms. Robinson
Description: 
I whipped this up on the fly to toss with a salad of field greens and sliced peaches. Easy,light and fruity. One batch makes enough for one big salad bowl.
