Title:
SLOW COOKER CHERRY COBBLER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (21oz.) cans cherry pie filling
1 (18.25 oz.) pkg. yellow cake mix
4 Tbsp. butter, softened
1/2 cup toasted pecans, chopped
vanilla ice cream

Directions:
Lightly coat the liner of 5 quart slow cooker with nonstick cooking spray. Evenly spread the cans of cherry pie filling into the bottom of the slow cooker.
In a bowl, combine the cake mix and butter until crumbly. Sprinkle the mix over the pie filling. Top with the chopped nuts.
Cover and cook on LOW for 3-4 hours. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Submitter:

Description: 
This recipe is over twenty years old. I got it when a slow cooker was known as a crock pot and the liners were not removable.
