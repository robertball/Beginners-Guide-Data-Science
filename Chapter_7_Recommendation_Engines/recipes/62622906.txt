Title:
Aunt Mel's Double Decker Fudge II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 7 or 9 oz jar marshmallow creme
4 1/2 cups sugar
1 12 oz can evaporated milk
dash of salt
1 cup flavored chocolate baking chips or 1/2 tsp baking flavor plus 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
1 cup white chocolate chips
1 cup miniature chocolate chips

Directions:
Butter a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Stirring constantly, over medium heat combine butter and marshmallow creme until melted.  Gradually add sugar until incorporated.  Add milk and salt.
Stirring constantly, cook over medium heat to a low boil.  Reduce heat slightly and boil for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat.  Pour half of mixture into a lowl. Add to the bowl flavored chocolate baking chips or baking flavor plus semi-sweet chips and chopped nuts.  Pour into baking dish.  Chill for 5 minutes.
To remaining half, stir in white chocolate chips and stir until blended.  Allow the mixture to cool just slightly. Add mini chocolate chips, only stir until mixed in. Pour over chocolate mixture in baking dish.
Chill in fridge 4 hours to over night.

Submitter: 
Noel Keen
Description: 
This is our family favorite fudge for the holidays.  Aunt Mel always made it for friends and family.  She had lots of yummy combinations.Modified from: Aunt Mel's Double Decker Fudge
