Title:
Chicken-Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 10 oz. pkgs. broccoli spears or
1 	large head fresh broccoli
6-8 	chicken breasts (deboned & cooked)
2 cans 	cream of chicken soup
1 cup 	mayonnaise
1 tsp. 	lemon juice
1/4 cup 	white cooking wine
1/2 tsp. 	curry powder
1/2 cup 	bread crumbs
1/2 cup 	shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Cook broccoli and arrange on bottom of casserole dish.  Place cooked breasts over broccoli.  Combine next 5 ingredients.  Pour over chicken.  Sprinkle cheddar cheese and bread crumbs over top.  Bake at 350o for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Miskiewicz
Description: 
I have been making this recipe for years and my family and friends just love it.  I normally serve it with rice and biscuits. My husband loves to combine it all together.  Yummy!
