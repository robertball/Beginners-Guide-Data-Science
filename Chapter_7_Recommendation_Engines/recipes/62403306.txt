Title:
Ramon Noodle Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1pkg Chicken ramon noodles
1pkg oriental ramon noodles
1 bag coleslaw mix
1/2C sugar
1/3C canola oil
1/4C red wine vinegar
sunflower seeds to taste
shaved almonds

Directions:
Mix Vinegar, oil, sugar, and the spices from the noodles.
Mix the coleslaw, crunch ramon noodles, Mis with sunflower seeds,and shaved almonds.
Pour the liqid ingredients together just before serving. Enjoy!
