Title:
No Bake Chocolate Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/889236.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup agave or 1/3 cup xylitol (sugar sub)
5 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 cup natural peanut or almond butter
1 cup rolled oats
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a saucepan over medium heat combine the maple syrup, cocoa and cinnamon. Boil for three minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and stir in the peanut butter, rolled oats and vanilla until well blended. Drop by heaping spoonfuls onto waxed paper and chill to set, about 30 minutes.
