Title:
Hazelnut Fudgy Gelato by Judy Matthews Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. heavy cream
2 c. whole milk
3/4 c. sugar, divided
4 egg yolks
1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
1/2 jar Nutella

Directions:
1. Combine cream, milk, & 1/2 c. sugar & cook over medium heat until sugar dissolves. (5-6 minutes)
2. In medium bowl, whip 1/4 c. sugar into the yolks & beat with electric mixer until eggs are thick & pale yellow. (4-5 minutes) Pour 1/2 c. of the warm milk/cream mixture into the egg mixture & stir. Add this mixture back into saucepan. Cook over very low heat, stirring constantly, until mixture is thick enough to coat the back of a wooden spoon. (~10 minutes)
3. Place a strainer over a medium bowl & strain warm custard. Discard egg bits. Add vanilla and Nutella to custard, stirring until dissolved.
4. Completely cool mixture before pouring into ice cream maker. (~12 hours refrigeration)
5. Follow manufacturer's instructions for ice cream making & freezing.
