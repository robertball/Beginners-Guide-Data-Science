Title:
Orange Beef-Style Tofu Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 carrots, sliced
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1/2 cup orange juice
1/4 cup warm water
1 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon chili paste
3 teaspoon cornstarch
1 (16 ounce) package firm tofu, drained and cut into strips
2 green onions, sliced opt
2 tsp sesame seeds opt

Directions:
Saute carrots until just tender, using water if needed. Mix the orange juice, soy sauce, sugar, chili paste, water in a small bowl.
Form a well in the center of the carrots, and pour in the sauce. Bring sauce to a boil. Mix tofu into the wok, and continue cooking until coated with the sauce. Garnish with onion and sesame seeds

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
When I get a craving for orange beef, I make up this tofu stir-fry! Pieces of firm tofu in a mildly spicy orange sauce. Serve over rice noodles, and use any vegetables you like!Modified from: Orange Beef-Style Tofu Stir-Fry
