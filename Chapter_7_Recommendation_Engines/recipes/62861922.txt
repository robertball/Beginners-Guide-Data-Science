Title:
Baked Egg and Potato Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 egg whites or 1/2 cups egg beaters
1/2 cup skim milk
3 1/2 cups frozen hash brown potatoes, thawed
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup onion, chopped
4 TBSP bacon, crumbled
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp chili powder
4 green onions, chopped

Directions:
Whisk together the eggs and milk in a large bowl.
Stir in hash browns, 3/4 cop of the cheese, onion, 2 TBSP of the bacon, and the spices.
Coat an 8x8 baking dish lightly with cooking spray and add egg mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees for about 45 minutes or until a knife comes out of the center clean.
Sprinkle the top with the remaining cheese and bacon and the green onions.
Let it stand for several minutes before cutting.
Cut into 6 equal pieces.
