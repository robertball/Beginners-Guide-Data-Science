Title:
Cheesecake tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package gram crackers
2 tbsps. white sugar
1/4 cup butter
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1/4 cup  white sugar
1/4 cup sour cream
1/4 cup heavy cream
1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 egg
1 cup whipping cream
3/4 cup blue berries
1/2 cup white sugar
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
First whirl gram cracker in a blender until their crushed. Then in a medium size bowl combine 2 tbsps. sugar and butter. Place small amounts of gram cracker mixture into a medium cup cake holder. flatten so that it covers bottom.
Place in the oven and cook for 5 min. or less.
Meanwhile combine cream cheese,sour cream, white sugar, heavy cream, flour, vanilla, and egg until smooth.
Pour mixture into cup cake holder. About 2 inches thick. Place in oven and cook for 7 min.
Meanwhile, blend whipping cream, blue berries, 1 tsp. vanilla, 1/2 cup white sugar. When berries are shooth remove mixture from blender and beat until cream forms peaks (not stiff).
When tarts are finished cooking(fairly solid, but no cracks) chill them for at least two hours. Serve tarts crust side down, topping can be hot or cold.
