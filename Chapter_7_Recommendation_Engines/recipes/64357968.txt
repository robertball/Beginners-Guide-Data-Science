Title:
Crock Pot Roast Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb chuck roast
Flour
Garlic powder
Rosemary
Oil
8 carrots, sliced
1 onion, sliced
2 stalks celery, sliced
8 medium potatoes, chunks
2 cans mushroom soup
1/2 package dry onion soup mix
1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup red wine
1 cup beef broth

Directions:
In a large plastic bag, mix flour, garlic powder, salt, and rosemary.  Place roast in bag and shake to coat.
Heat oil in Dutch oven on stovetop.  Remove roast from bag and sear in oil until browned.
Place roast in crock pot.  Top with carrots, onion, celery and potatoes.
In a large bowl, mix together remaining ingredients and pour over roast and vegetables.
Cook on high for 4 hours and low for 2 hours.  Thicken gravy with flour before serving (if desired).

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
Very tasty gravy in this easy to make ahead pot roast.
