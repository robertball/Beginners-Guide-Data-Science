Title:
Do Ahead Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4694737.jpg
Ingredients:
9 medium potatoes (3 pounds)
3/4 c milk
1/2 c whipping (heavy) cream
1/2 c butter
1 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper

Directions:
Peel and cut potatoes. Bring to boil in salted water, reduce heat and cove. Cook until tender. Drain.
Meanwhile, melt butter in microwave and add cream, milk, salt and pepper. Measure out 1/4 c and set aside.
When potatoes have drained, put pieces though a ricer. If not fuller tender, out riced potatoes through the ricer a second time.
Add milk and butter mixture to riced potatoes a little at a time,  mixing well between adding more.
Butter a two quart baking dish and spoon potatoes into the dish. Bake immediately or cover and refrigerate up to 24 hours.
To bake, heat oven to 350. Pour remaining 1/4 of milk mixture over the potatoes. Bake 40-45 minutes. Stir just before serving.
