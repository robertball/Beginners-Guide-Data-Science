Title:
O-O-Omega Spelt & Rye Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/962665.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons Chia Seeds
3 Tablespoons Flax Seeds
5 Tablespoons Lecithin
2 Cups boiling water
2 Tablespoons Honey
1 Tablespoon Molasses
1 cup Almond Milk
3 1/2  teaspoons baking powder
1/2 cup sesame seeds
1/2 cup flax seeds
6 cups spelt flour
2 cups rye flour

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 F.
Soak Chia, flax lecithin in 2 cups boiling water for 5 minutes
Blend mixture in high speed blender.
Add honey, molasses, almond milk.
Sift flour, salt and baking powder together.
Add Sesame Seeds and Flax Seeds
Combine wet mixture to dry mixture.
Work in together, until fully blended
Divide into two loaves and place in oil pans.

Submitter: 
ChrissyintheKitchen
Description: 
Spelt & Rye Bread with Flax and Chia seeds, a delicious bread without the wait of yeast. This is a dense bread, doesn't rise like regular breads, can be used like a cracker if you like, slice thin and toast!
