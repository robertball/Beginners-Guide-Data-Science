Title:
Magical Disappearing Clam Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cans Minced Clams Drained
3 Cans Chopped Clams Drained
4 Cups Sour Cream
4 oz Cream Cheese Room Temp
1/4 Teaspoon Worsteshire
1 Teaspoon Fresh Lemon Juice
1 Tablespoon Fresh Minced Garlic
1/2 Teaspoon salt
1/4 Teaspoon Black Pepper

Directions:
Cream together the cream cheese and sour cream with a hand mixer. Add the clams, garlic, lemon juice, worsteshire salt and pepper and mix well with a large spoon. Cover and refridgerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
Missy Kitty
Description: 
This garlicky dip goes so fast at all my parties. Excellent served with thick potato chips and cut up vegetables.
