Title:
Simple Cajun Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 and 1/2 tablespoons salt
1 tablespoon oregano
1 tablespoon paprika
1 tablespoon cayenne pepper
1 tablespoon black pepper

Directions:
Simply mix the ingredients together in a plastic back and shake it all up.

Submitter:

Description: 
Here is a simple way using normal kitchen spices to make cajun seasoning.
