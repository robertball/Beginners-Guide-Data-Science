Title:
Cherry Almond Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (1 pound 1.5 ounce) pouch Betty CrockerÂ® sugar cookie mix
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
1/2 tablespoon almond extract
1 egg
1 cup chopped dried cherries
1/2 to 3/4 cup of chopped allmonds (add what looks good to you

Directions:
Mix cookie mix, butter, orange extract and egg in a bowl until soft dough forms.
Stir in cherries; blend.
Drop dough by rounded teaspoonfuls 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake at 375 degrees F for 8 to 10 minutes. Cool 3 minutes; remove from cookie sheet to paper towels. Cool completely, about 15 minutes.
