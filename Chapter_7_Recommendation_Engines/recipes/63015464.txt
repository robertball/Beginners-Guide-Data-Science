Title:
Bugle Santa Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/754341.jpg
Ingredients:
1. Bag of Plain Bugles ( found in the chip or snack isle
2. 1lb white almond bark ( one pkg ...not sure of weight)
3. bag M&M red and green colored ones

Directions:
line cookie sheet or plates with wax paper
melt 2. as per directions on pkg
dip the bottom of a Bugle in the melted white chocolate
place one colored M&M on the chocolate ....place on wax paper and cool so chocolate hardeneds

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
Cute...look like a Santa hat...fast no bake treat to make for your children
