Title:
Jane's Famous Breadsticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C. warm water
1 tbspn yeast
2 tbspn sugar
1 tspn salt
4 C. flour
Topping
1/4 cup soft butter
1/4 cup mayo
1/4 cup of parmesan
1/2 tspn garlic powder
1/2 tspn parsley

Directions:
In a large bowl combine warm water, yeast and sugar and let sit for ten minutes to proof.
Add salt and 3 1/2 C. of flour and mix until a soft dough is formed. Add remaining flour until the dough starts to clean the sides of the bowl.
Knead for 3 minutes
Cover bowl with a cloth and let the dough rest for ten minutes.
Coat a cookie sheet with butter or PAM.
Take dough and pull across sheet until you have a flat rectangle.
Take a sharp knife and score the dough into bread sticks.
Brush topping over dough.
Set oven to 350.
Let rise for 25 minutes until dough is about double.
Bake 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
