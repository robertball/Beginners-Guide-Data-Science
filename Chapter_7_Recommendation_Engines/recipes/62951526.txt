Title:
Ice Cream Kolachky Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound butter (no substitutes),softened
1 pint vanilla ice cream, softened
4 cups flour
Fruit  filling
Confectioners sugar

Directions:
Cream softened butter with softened ice cream. Blend in flour. Pinch off about 1 tablespoon of dough and shape into ball. Place on ungreased cookie sheet about 1 1/2 inches apart. Make an indentation in center of ball and add about 1/2 teaspoon fruit filling. Bake at 350 degrees for about 22-25 minutes. Cool on rack. Sprinkle with confectioners sugar when cool.

Submitter: 
Sandy Wall- Krusinski
Description: 
This a very easy kolachky recipe. Everyone will be amazed that they taste so good and are so easy.
