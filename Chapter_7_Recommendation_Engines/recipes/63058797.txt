Title:
Hot Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter
2-8 oz. cream cheese
1/2 pkg. Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
8 tsp. lemon juice
1/2 c. chopped onion
14 oz. can chicken

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a sauce pan and heat.
Put in small crock pot and serve with Ritz Chips.
