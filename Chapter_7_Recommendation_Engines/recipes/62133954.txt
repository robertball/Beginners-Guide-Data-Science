Title:
So Easy Chicken Tetrazzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 7oz pkg thin wheat spaghetti cooked
1/4 lb sliced fresh mushrooms
1/4 C melted butter
2 C Chopped cooked chicken (rotisserie or boiled)
1 can 98% Fat Free Cream of Chicken
1 can 98% Fat Free Cream of Mushroom
1 16oz container reduced fat sour cream
1 C shredded Reduced fat cheddar divided
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
Badia Complete Seasoning to taste
(Optional: 3 chopped scallions)

Directions:
Boil pasta in salted water
While the pasta is cooking, combine all the remaining ingredients
Add drained pasta
Pour mixture into a 9 x 13 glass baking dish
Microwave for 6 minutes on high
Stir, microwave for another 6 minutes on high
Top with reserved cheddar cheese and microwave for another 2 minutes
Let stand covered for 2 minutes and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Priscilla
Description: 
This a recipe that is packed with flavor and you can cook the entire dish in the microwave! I find this is a healthier version than most and it reheats well, if you have any left over!
