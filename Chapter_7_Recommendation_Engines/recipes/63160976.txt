Title:
Cinnamon Espresso Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/810926.jpg
Ingredients:
½ Cup Butter, softened
2/3 Cup Brown Sugar
½ Cup Granulated Sugar
1 Egg
1/3 Cup Espresso
½ Teaspoon Pure Vanilla Extract
½ Teaspoon Baking Soda
½ Teaspoon Baking Powder
½ Teaspoon Salt
3 Cups All Purpose Flour
1 ½ Teaspoons Cinnamon
1 ½ Cup Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Directions:
1.)	Cream butter and sugars together. Mix in egg, espresso, and vanilla.
2.)	Sift baking soda, baking powder, salt, and flour together. Begin adding ½ cup at a time until well incorporated.
3.)	Mix in cinnamon.
4.)	Stir in chocolate chips
Baking:
1.)	Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
2.)	Drop cookie dough the size of 2 Tablespoons onto baking sheets.
3.)	Bake for minutes

Submitter: 
House of Aqua
Description: 
These flavors meld together well creating something that isn't your usual chocolate chip cookie.
