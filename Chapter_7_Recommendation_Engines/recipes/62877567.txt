Title:
All-Purpose Garlic Herb Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon minced fresh garlic
1/2 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/8 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1/4 teaspoon dried rosemary
2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix the kosher salt, garlic powder, fresh garlic, black pepper, onion powder, cayenne pepper, oregano, thyme, rosemary and olive oil. Allow the mixture to sit about 15 minutes.
Rub on meat, cook to safe temperature.
