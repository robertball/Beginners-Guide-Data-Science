Title:
Homemade Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breast
2 cups dry bowtie pasta
1 can cream of celery soup
1 can chicken stock
4 cups of water
1 onion
1 stalk of celery
1 carrot
1 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Boil chicken breast until fully cooked @ 15 to 20 mins, and set aside to cool.  While the chicken in cooking go ahead and prepare your bowtie pasta, cook it a minute or two less than the package suggest. Cut celery, onion and carrot in to small pieces all about the same size.
In a large pot bring chicken stock to a slow boil and add carrots, celery, and onion, cover and let simmer for about 10 mins or until carrots have softend.  Cut cooled chicken breasts in to small bite size pieces and put in pot.  Add cream of celery soup and pepper stir until mixed well and add the water.  Bring back to a simmer and add pasta continue to simmer soup until pasta is fully cooked and somewhat soft.

Submitter: 
Robin Lee Starnes
Description: 
This is a easy chicken noodle soup recipe that is great on a dreary day the perfect comfort food.
