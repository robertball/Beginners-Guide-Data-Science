Title:
chocolate chip cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1cup marg.(2blocks), softened.
11/2cups Brown sugar
2 large eggs
1tsp. vanilla extract
2cups flour
1/4 cup cornstarch
1 tsp.baking soda
3/4 tsp. salt
2 cups chocolate chips

Directions:
cream marg. and sugar. add eggs, 1 at a time, beating well after each. Add vanilla. beat until smooth. Combine next 4 things.Add to marg. mixture in two additions. mix well. Add chips. mix well. drop onto geased cookie sheet about 2 inches apart. bake at 350 for about 10min. until golden.
