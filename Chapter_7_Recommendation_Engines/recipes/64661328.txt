Title:
3 Packet Roast in the Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4563979.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lb roast
1 envelope Italian Seasoning
1 envelope Ranch Dressing
1 envelope Brown Gravy Mix
2 c beer or water

Directions:
In a bowl combine the three envelopes and 2 c. beer, mix well.
Sear the roast on all sides
Add the roast to the slow cooker
Pour the beer seasoning mix over the roast
Cook on high 4 hours or low 8 hours.

Submitter: 
Jen Cooks For Fun
Description: 
An easy week night meal that cooks while you are at work and even makes it's own gravy!
