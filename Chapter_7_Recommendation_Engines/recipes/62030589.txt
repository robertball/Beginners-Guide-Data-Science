Title:
No-Tomato Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. extra lean ground beef
2 tsp. minced garlic
3 tsp. ground cumin
1/2 c. chili powder
1/4 tsp. black pepper
3 T. flour
3 c. water
1 1/2 lbs. processed cheese (such as Velveta), cubed

Directions:
Brown ground beef with garlic. Do not drain.  Add remaining ingredients, except cheese.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer 20 minutes.  Add cheese and stir until melted.

Submitter: 
MINIVANMOMOF3
Description: 
My husband and I perfected this chili for those who don't like tomatoes.  It is also great on hot dogs and makes wonderful Frito Chili Pie!
