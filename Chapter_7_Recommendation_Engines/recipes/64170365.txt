Title:
Chicken Rice-A-Daddy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1466223.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup white rice
3/4 cup vermicelli
4 Tbsp butter
3 cups chicken stock/broth
1/4 - 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
1/4 - 1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp Salt, or to taste

Directions:
Break vermicelli into small (1/2 inch to 1 inch long) segments.
Put butter in pan. When melted, add rice and vermicelli and cook at medium heat until the pasta is browned, stirring frequently.
Add chicken stock and seasonings, stir, and heat to a boil.
Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until liquid absorbed, approximately 15-20 minutes.
Serve hot and enjoy.

Description: 
This Rice-A-Roni clone was a big hit with my daughter when she was growing up; it was her who chose the name. This recipe is still a staple in our household: all the flavor of Rice-A-Roni, none of the additives!
