Title:
Rhonda's Big Batch of No Bake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups white sugar
1/2cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1cup butter
1cup milk
1/4teas salt
1 18oz. box of quick cooking oats
1cup peanut butter
2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a saucepan bring sugar, cocoa, margarine, milk, and salt to a full rapid boil for 1 minute.
Add quick cooking oats, peanut butter, and vanilla; mix well.
Pour into greased pan. Score when still warm into desired size squares!

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
Easy to make and just throw in a greased 9x13 cake pan and score while warm! This is definitely a crowd pleaser!Modified from: No Bake Cookies III
