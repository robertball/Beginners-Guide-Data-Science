Title:
Chicken Taco Soup by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cubed, cooked chicken (2 large breasts)
2 cans diced tomatoes with chilis (Rotel)
1 10oz can green enchilada sauc
1 small can chopped green chilis (4oz)
1/2 medium onion, chopped
2 cups chicken broth (up to 4 cups)
1 can black beans
3/4-1 package frozen corn, fiesta is awesome, ok to use canned
1 tbsp cumin
1 tbsp chili powder
1 tbsp garlic powder (or 2 cloves, minced)
1 tbsp taco seasoning
1 tsp black pepper
2 tsp salt and/or garlic salt
Optional toppings: shredded cheese, cilantro, tortilla chips, sour cream

Directions:
Dump all in crockpot
Cook on low 4-5 hours.
Easy to increase quantities and make a bigger batch.

Submitter: 
Tammy Doerr
Description: 
So easy! Just dump it all n the crockpot at lunch time and you'll have a delicious soup for supper!
