Title:
Alexander's Special Cinnamon Caramel Sauce Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 Ounces Caramels
24 Ounces Chocolate Chips
24 Ounces Cinnamon Sugar

Directions:
1. Pour all the ingredients into different bowls.
2. Melt all ingredients in their own bowls.
3. Mix all the melted ingredients into an extra large bowl.
4. Pour the sauce onto any dessert that you wish.
5. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Alexander Petrie
Description: 
This recipe makes Cinnamon Caramel Sauce that makes every type of food taste better than ever!
