Title:
Creamy Clam Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/568964.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6.5 ounce) can minced clams, drained with juice reserved
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 1/2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon minced garlic (one clove)
2 teaspoons lemon juice
1 teaspoon onion juice (optional)
Salt to taste
dash Pepper to taste
1/4 cup clam broth

Directions:
Rub mixing bowl with cut garlic clove.  Mix together the clams, cream cheese, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, lemon juice, and onion juice until evenly combined. Thin the mixture to your desired consistency by stirring in the clam juice.
