Title:
Supper Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871681.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound	ground beef
1/2 cup	onion, chopped
1 teaspoon	seasoned salt - optional
1/2 teaspoon	cumin
2 cans	refried beans
1 package	taco seasoning mix
2 cups	Monterey Jack cheese - grated
1/4 cup	green chiles - diced
1 cup	grated cheddar cheese
2/3 cup	salsa
Tortilla chips
Guacamole - optional
Sour cream - optional
Chopped green onions - optional
Sliced olives - optional

Directions:
Heat skillet at medium-high temperature; brown meat and onions. Drain
well. Return meat and onions to skillet; add cumin and mix well. In a
large mixing bowl, combine refried beans and taco seasoning mix;
blend well. Add grated cheese to bean mixture; mix together.
Spread beans in shallow 9X13 pan. Cover over bean mixture with
browned meat and onions. Sprinkle chiles evenly over meat. Top with
grated cheddar cheese. Pour salsa over cheese.
Bake uncovered at 400 for 20-25 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
Place tortilla chips around edge of meat/beans dish and garnish as
desired.
