Title:
Tuscan Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
T. olive oil
1 large onion
2 - 3 cloves garlic
1 pound Italian sausage
8 cups chicken broth
1 large can diced tomatoes
2 large sweet potatoes
1 can cannellini beans
salt to taste
pepper to taste
thyme
rosemary
oregano
1 bunch kale, chopped
parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a large pot on medium heat sauté onion and Italian sausage until onion is translucent and sausage is browned.  Add garlic and sauté 1 minute more.  Add chicken broth, tomatoes and sweet potatoes and bring to a boil.  Lower heat, add seasonings and simmer for 20 minutes or until sweet potato is tender.  Add beans and kale and simmer until kale wilts and is tender.  Serve with a sprinkle of parmesan.

Submitter: 
java junkie
Description: 
A  healthy, hearty soup for all seasons.  If you happen to have a parmigiano-reggiano rind, toss it in when you add the broth for a richer flavor.
