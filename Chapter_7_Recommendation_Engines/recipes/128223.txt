Title:
Wild Rice Medley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 (14 ounce) cans reduced-fat chicken broth
1/3 cup wild rice, rinsed several times and drained
1 cup white rice
1 cup shredded carrots
1/4 cup chopped scallions
1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
In a saucepan over high heat, bring broth to a boil. Stir in wild rice. Reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer for 25 minutes.
Stir in white rice. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes. Stir in carrots, scallions and pepper. Cover and simmer until rice is tender, about 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Rodale
Description: 
Wild rice imparts a subtle nutty flavor to this exceptionally low-fat and easy-to-prepare side dish.
