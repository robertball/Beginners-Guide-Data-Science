Title:
Pumpkin Black Bean Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons Vegetable Oil
1 Cup Chopped Onion
1 Cup Chopped Yellow Bell Pepper
3 Cloves Garlic, minced
1 1/2 teaspoons Cumin
1 1/2 teaspoons Chili Powder
2 (15-oz.) cans Black Beans, rinsed and drained
2 1/2 Cups chopped cooked Turkey
1 (16-oz.) can Pumpkin
1 (14 1/2-oz.) can diced Tomatoes, undrained
3 Cups Chicken Broth
1/2 Cup Cream Sherry

Directions:
Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion, yellow bell pepper and garlic; saute until soft. Stir in oregano, cumin and chili powder. Cook 1 minute longer. Stir in black beans, turkey pumpkin, tomatoes, chicken broth, and sherry. Bring to boil, stirryng occasionally.

Submitter: 
Debbie Lopez
Description: 
Do not be afraid of the pumpkin you will be pleasantly surprised. The cream sherry gives this a nice taste. This is a good and healthy way to use left over turkey.
