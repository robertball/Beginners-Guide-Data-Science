Title:
Quick Berry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984286.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup raspberries
2 tablespoons lemon juice
2 tablespoons sugar, or to taste
1 cup blueberries
1 cup diced strawberries

Directions:
In large bowl, combine raspberries, lemon juice and sugar.  Using large fork or potato masher, smash the berries to make a puree.  Fold in blueberries and strawberries.  Store covered in refrigerator.
