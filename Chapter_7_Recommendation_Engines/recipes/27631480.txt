Title:
Frozen Peach Shortcake Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tub (8oz.) Cool whip thawed
1pt. (2 cups) vanilla ice cream softened
1 pkg. (4 serving size) Jello Brand peach flavor Gelatin (unprepared)
4 cups pound cake cubes
1/2 cup rasberry preserves
12 small peach slices
12 rasberries

Directions:
Stir whipped topping, ice cream and dry gelatin (unprepared) in large bowl until well blended. Stir in cake cubes. Spoon into 8" square pan.
Freeze 3 hours or until firm.
Drizzle with raspberry preserves. Cut into squares. Top each square with 1 peach slice and 1 raspberry. Store any leftover dessert in freezer.
