Title:
Caipirissima Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977779.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Oronoco™ rum
5 wedges lime
1 teaspoon sugar, or to taste
3 ounces freshly squeezed lime juice
Crushed ice

Directions:
In a cocktail shaker, muddle 4 lime wedges and sugar.
Add Oronoco(TM), lime juice, and ice; shake vigorously.  Strain into an ice-filled glass to serve.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Using luxury rum like Oronoco™ Rum adds a subtle sweetness, smooth taste and underlying complexity to this drink recipe.
