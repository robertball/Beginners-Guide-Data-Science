Title:
Jim's Sangria Ole! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/423429.jpg
Ingredients:
Ingredients
1 lemon, sliced
1 orange, sliced
1 ( 12 oz can ) Sliced Pineapple and juice
1 (12 oz can) mandarin oranges and juice
1 ( 6 oz pkg)  fresh raspberries
3 tablespoons brown sugar
2 oz vodka
2 oz Triple Sec ( or Cointreau)
1 oz Gin
1/4 cup orange soda
1/4 cup lemon-lime soda ( such as Sprite or 7-up )
1- cup pineapple juice
3/4 cup orange juice
1 - bottle dry red wine  (Cabernet, Pinot Noir or Spanish)
Ice

Directions:
Combine all and Enjoy! It's best if you let it sit in the refrigerator for a few hours to let the flavors combine.
