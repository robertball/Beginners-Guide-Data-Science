Title:
Apple Pie with Oatmeal Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup quick oats
3/4 cup butter, melted
1 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. salt
1-1/4 cups water
2/3 cup sugar
3 TB cornstarch
2 cups apples, diced
1/4 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 cup walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Mix together flour, quick oats, melted butter, brown sugar and salt.
Reserve 1 cup for topping
Pat remaining in a 9-inch pie plate for the crust
For the filling, bring water and sugar to a boil
Slowly add cornstarch, salt, vanilla and cinnamon
Cook for 1 minute.
When thick, remove from heat and add apples.
Pour into crust and top with reserved crumbs
Preheat oven to 350 and bake for 45-50 minutes
Garnish with chopped walnuts

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
This pie is similar to an apple crumb pie but is baked in a sweet crust made of oatmeal.  Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
