Title:
Garlic Herb Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large baking potatoes
1 pkg dry italian seasoning
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
1/2 tsp dried thyme
1/4 tsp crushed dried rosemary
1 tsp chopped fresh garlic
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Scrub potatoes.
Cut in half than cut halves into medium half moons.
Place potatoes in an 8x8 oiled or sprayed baking dish.
Drizzle olive oil on potatoes.
Sprinkle italian seasoning,thyme, rosemary and garlic over potatoes.
Add parmesan cheese and pepper to taste.
Mix well and bake uncovered in preheated oven for 35 mins or until tender and golden brown.

Description: 
A quick and easy side dish that can be prepared in a matter of minutes. Great with poultry, beef, pork or lamb. Not just for adults,kids love these as well.
