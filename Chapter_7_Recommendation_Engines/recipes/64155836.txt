Title:
Imitation Crab Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb imitation crab
1 large bell pepper
4 stalks celery
1/3 c mayo
1/3 c ranch dressing
1/4 t. fresh cracked pepper
4-5 green onion

Directions:
Chop and break crab to make flaky in large bowl.
Chop bell pepper, celery and slice green onion into small pieces and add to crab.
Stir in mayo and ranch dressing to mix well.
Top with cracked pepper. Let sit in fridge for at least 2 hours or overnight. Serve

Submitter: 
Wendy Meyer
Description: 
Yummy crab salad that makes a lot but you wont throw any out. My family eats if for dinner then lunch the next day and sometimes as a night snack.
