Title:
Olya's Russian Beet Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 beets
4-5 potatoes
4-5 carrots
1/2 c. frozen peas
1/2 c. onion, finely chopped
2 T. apple cider vinegar
1/2 t. sea salt, to taste
1 T. dill
1/4 t. freshly ground pepper, to taste
1 T. non-GMO sunflower oil

Directions:
Bring root vegetables to boil, cover and turn off heat. Let them sit on the hot burner until they cool down (1-2 hours).
Meanwhile, cover onion with vinegar & set aside.
Peel & chop root vegetables.
Add remaining ingredients and stir well.
Refrigerate several hours or overnight.
Serve with sour cream, if desired.

A beautiful garnet color, with root vegetables from late summer.
