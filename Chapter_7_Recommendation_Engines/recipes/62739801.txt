Title:
Creamy Macaroni and Feta Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/628783.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound box of pasta (your choice)
2.5 cups milk
1/3 cups flour
4 tablespoons butter
6 ounces cream cheese (room temp)
1 cup Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup mozzarella cheese (shredded)
8 ounces feta cheese (crumbled)
1-2 cups spinach
garlic powder and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta then drain and set aside
In a medium saucepan mix milk and flour until no clumps remain. Add butter and heat to melt.
Toss in spinach and cream cheese. Mix well until cheese is melted in.
Add the rest of the cheeses and stir continuously til melted. Feta will remain in chunks.
Add cooked pasta and enjoy!
