Title:
hamburger ala foil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 onion, diced
2 large carrots, sliced thin
2 large potatoes, peeled and sliced thin
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Form ground beef into four patties.  Add vegetables on top and season with salt and pepper.  Wrap tightly in aluminum foil. Throw directly into hot ashes and cook approximately 50 minutes.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
My husband who was in the Boy Scouts when he was little had these on the open fire all the time.  I fix them at home on the grill or even throw them in the oven directly on the rack.
