Title:
Energy Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One slice of whole wheat bread
One banana
1-2 TB peanut butter

Directions:
Spread the peanut butter on bread. Place the banana in the center of the bread and fold up the sides so it resembles a hot dog. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
KATIES8422
Description: 
A quick, tasty and portable meal that's good for you. You can use low calorie whole wheat bread if you want to cut down the calories. Make sure the first ingredient in the bread is whole wheat, otherwise it's not the good stuff!
