Title:
Peachy Keen Mocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/569475.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup peach-mango nectar
1.5 cups Seven Up or lemon lime soda
1 cup sliced peaches
9 cubes of ice

Directions:
Put about 1/3 of a cup of sliced peaches in each glass. (Divided amongst three glasses)
Add 1/3 cup peach mango nectar to each glass.
Add 1/2 cup Seven Up to each glass.
Put about 3 ice cubes in each glass.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Ivon
Description: 
A peachy drink for anyone who is not of age or doesn't drink.
