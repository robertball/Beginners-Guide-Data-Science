Title:
Orange Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2lb Pork Chops
8 oz Frozen concentrate orange juice
1 cup Ketchup
2 Tablespoon oil for browning
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Salt and pepper pork chops as desired.  Place oil in skillet on medium-high heat.  Brown pork chops on both sides.  Mix frozen concentrate orange juice with ketchup in bowl.  Place chop in baking dish.  Pour orange juice and ketchup mixture over pork chops. Cover with foil and bake at 350 degree for 30 minutes then uncover and bake remaining 30 minutes.
