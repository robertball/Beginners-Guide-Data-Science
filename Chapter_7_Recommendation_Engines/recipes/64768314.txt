Title:
Kaisers " Pointless mess"    /  Kaiser "Schmarn" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz of Flour
14 oz Eggs or 8ea
24 oz Milk.
1 oz Sugar
6 tbsp.  Butter
5 oz Raisins.
6 oz Chopped Apples.
2 oz. Powdered Sugar

Directions:
slowly mix the Flour Gradually with the Milk to avoid Lumps and add the Sugar; next scramble the Eggs and mix into the Flour/Milk mixture.   in a Small  roasting Pan or  Large  Iron Skillet, (the deepness of the Batter should be about an 3/4 Inch ).  make it Hot and add Half the Butter; when Preheat  your Oven to High - Med. Heat .  Starting on the Range; when the Butter is lightly Brown, Pour the Batter into the Pan and begin Cooking  the Batter over Med Heat.  Stir Slightly,  when it sears, add the Raisins and Apples, or just Raisins, cover with a lid ajar and wait for a Bulge to appear.  at this point;  Pierce it;  cut into Very Large Squares ( use Two Spatulas if Necessary) and Turn over.  Now Move it to the oven. Keep covered . Bake about  7  minutes. ( do not let it Burn);  Remove back to the Range top and using two Forks Quickly Tear into Smaller pieces.  add the rest of the Butter.  Mix with a Spatula.   Arrange on Serving Plates and sprinkle with the rest of the Powdered Sugar.

Submitter: 
Alois
Description: 
Sweet Egg like souffle of Eggs, Milk. Flour,  Raisins and Powdered Sugar.
