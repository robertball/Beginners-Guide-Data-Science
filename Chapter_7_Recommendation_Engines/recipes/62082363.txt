Title:
Fudge Surprise Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. flour
1/2 c. finely chpped pecans
12 oz. high quality semiweet choc chips
1/4 c. unsalted butter (no subtitutes)
14 oz. sweetened condensed milk
1 t. vanilla
13 oz. milk choc. kisses (or your favorite mini choc candy)
2 oz. white baking choc.
1 t. veg. oil

Directions:
Combine flour and pecans; set aside.  In double boiler, melt choc chips and butter, stir smooth.  Stir in milk and vanilla until blended.  Add to flour mixture; mix well.  Shape 1 tbs of dough around each candy, roll in palms to seal.  Place 1" apart on parchment paper lined or ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 7-9 min. or until tops begin to crack.  Cool on wire racks.  Melt white choc and oil (add food color as desired), drizzle over cookies.

Submitter: 
BARBARALB
Description: 
These are delicious melt in your mouth dough with a surprise chocolate candy center.  You can use any type of mini candy you like, mix them up in the same recipe and use different color drizzle to tell them apart.
