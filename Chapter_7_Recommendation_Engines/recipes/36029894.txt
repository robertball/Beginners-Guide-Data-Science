Title:
Spicy and Delicious Kale Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbl. coconut oil
1/2 pound spicy sausage sliced into 1/2 inch pieces
4 cups chicken stock
4-6 garlic cloves, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
6 medium carrots, sliced into 1/2 inch pieces
1 red pepper, chopped
10-12 baby red potatoes, cut into bite size pieces
1 large bunch kale, stemmed and shredded
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Heat oil in a large Dutch oven and saute sausage until brown (5-8 minutes).  Drain and reserve.
Deglaze pan with a little chicken stock.  Add garlic, onions, carrots and red pepper, and cook 5 minutes, until they begin to soften.
Add remaining chicken stock and potatoes, bring to a boil then reduce to low heat and cover.  Cook for 10-15 minutes until carrots and potatoes are done.
Add sausage and kale, bring back to a boil, then simmer 6-10 minutes until kale is tender.
Season and serve with a toasted baguette.
