Title:
Stacie's Chicken&Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pack of boneless chicken
2 or 3 cans of cresent rolls
1 small can of chicken broth
1 can of cream of celery
1 cup of flour
Salt
Pepper
Oregano
garlic salt
onion flakes

Directions:
Boil your chicken in plenty of water to be used later for boiling dumplings.
Drain(keep broth) and cool chicken.
Bring broth back to boil.
Add your seasings to taste.
Pour a cup of flour on a plate.
Open your cresent rolls and 1 by one coat with flour and place in boiling broth.
Now add your chicken broth and cream of celery soup to broth.
Stir, but not to much. Stiring to much will break up your dumplings. Now break up your chicken into bite size pieces and add to broth.
Cook until dumplings are tender.
