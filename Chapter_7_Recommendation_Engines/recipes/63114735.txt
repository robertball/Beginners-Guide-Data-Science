Title:
pizza  crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. flour
1 1/4 c warm water
1pkg yeast
1t salt
1t honey
1/4c milk

Directions:
mix flour and salt in a bowl, make well.
In a sep bowl, add honey, yeast and warm water.  gently stir and let sit 10 min .
Add water/yeast mixture, 2T olive oil to flour.  knead until mixed and smooth.  roll into a ball, cover, let rise approx 1hr.
pat onto greased pan, add toppings...425 degrees, approx 20 min.  put under broiler at the end of baking to further brown the top
