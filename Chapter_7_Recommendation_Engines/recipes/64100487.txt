Title:
Lemon Lush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1229241.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. Flour
1 stick soften butter
1/2 c crushed nuts
8 oz cream cheese softened
1 c confectionery sugar
3 c cool whip
2 pkgs instant lemon pudding
3 c milk
Lemon zest

Directions:
Layer 1. Combine flour, butter and nuts.  Spread in bottom of baking dish.  Bake 15 min at 350.  Let cool
Layer 2.  Blend cream cheese, confectionery sugar and 1 c cool whip
Layer 3.  Combine lemon pudding, milk
Top with 2 c cool whip and lemon zest
Refrigerate and serve
