Title:
Strawberry and Melon Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982624.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (.7 ounce) envelope GOOD SEASONS Italian Salad Dressing Mix
1/4 cup orange juice
1/2 cup oil
2 tablespoons water
1 (10 ounce) package salad greens
3 cups melon chunks
1 cup sliced fresh strawberries
3 tablespoons sunflower seeds

Directions:
Mix salad dressing mix, juice, oil and water until well blended.
Pour over greens, melon and strawberries in large bowl; toss lightly. Sprinkle with sunflower seeds.
