Title:
Heavenly Chocolate Lava Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 tbsp butter (72g)
3.5 oz dark chocolate (one standard chocolate bar, 98g)
2 eggs
1 egg yolk
3 tbsp sugar
3 tbsp flour
2 tsp cocoa powder
pinch of salt
powdered sugar (optional)
1 scoop vanilla ice cream (optional)

Directions:
Melt the butter and chocolate together over a double-boiler.
Stir to combine. Whisk together the eggs and sugar until the mixture is light yellow in color, and the sugar is dissolved. About 3 minutes.
Stir the warm chocolate mixture into the egg mixture and whisk until combined.
Sift in the flour, cocoa, and salt. Fold in with a spatula until combined.
Sprinkle Cocoa powder in individual ramekins. (You can also butter individual ramekins) Spoon into 4 buttered 5-oz ramekins, and tap on the table to settle any air bubbles. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Place ramekins in a baking dish and bake for 13-15 minutes.
Dust with powdered sugar if desire. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream if desired
