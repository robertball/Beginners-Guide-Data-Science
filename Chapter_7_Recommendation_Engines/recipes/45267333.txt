Title:
Green Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can green beans, drained - or try frozen
1 (15 ounce) can peas, drained - frozen
1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained - frozen
1 (4 ounce) jar pimentos - optional
1 onion, chopped
4 stalks celery, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped - + red & yellow
1/2 cup vegetable oil - or olive oil
1 cup distilled white vinegar
1 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons water

Directions:
Combine vegetables in a large bowl.
In a small bowl, mix together oil, vinegar, sugar, salt, and water.  Pour over vegetables, and stir to coat.  Refrigerate, covered, for 4 hours or overnight.
