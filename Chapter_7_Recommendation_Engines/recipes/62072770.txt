Title:
Party Crunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Rice Krispies cereal
2 cups broken pretzel sticks
1 (20-25 ounce) package Almond Bark
2 cups Captain Crunch cereal
2 cups dry roasted peanuts

Directions:
Mix cereals, pretzel sticks, and peanuts together in a large bowl.  Melt almond bark in microwave as directed on package.  Pour almond bark over mixture in bowl; mix well.  Spread in a thin layer on wax paper to set.  Break into pieces.

Submitter: 
UCMOM
Description: 
A lady I work with brought this to work one Christmas.  It is so good and unbelievably easy to make.  If you like sweet/salty combinations, you will love this.
