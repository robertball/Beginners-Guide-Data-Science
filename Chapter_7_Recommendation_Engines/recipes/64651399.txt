Title:
Mayonnaise Dressing for Fruit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup plain or vanilla yogurt
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon honey
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a small bowl and blend.
Mix with fruit and serve.

Submitter: 
Lorraine Friberg
Description: 
A different taste for a fruit salad.
