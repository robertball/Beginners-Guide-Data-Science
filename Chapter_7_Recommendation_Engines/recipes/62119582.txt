Title:
Kahlua Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1/2 pkg. chocolate wafers, crushed.  approx. 1 1/4 C.
6 T. butter, melted
1/2 C. finely chopped nuts
1 T. sugar
Filling:
1 Qt. coffee ice cream
1 pt. whipping cream
1 tsp. vanilla
1/3 C. Kahlua
4 Heath bars, crushed

Directions:
Crust:
Crush chocolate wafers, then add butter, nuts and sugar.  Pat into a greased pie plate and bake at 350 for 10 minutes.  Let cool.
Filling:
Soften ice cream.  Whip the cream, then add vanilla and Kahlua.  Gently fold crushed heath bars into whipped cream mixture.  Gently combine the whipped cream mixture with the softened ice cream.  Pour into pie crust and freeze.  Minimum 4 hours or overnight.  Remove from freezer 15 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
jeanneferg
Description: 
A frozen pie with coffee ice cream and Kahlua.  A nice festive dessert.
