Title:
Super Good Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 frozen Container Limeaide- any size
2 - cans of  water
1 - can of tequila
1 - can triple sec  or Grand Gala
1/2 - can Amaretto (Optional)

Directions:
Mix in a pitcher the can of limeaide - use the juice can and add rest of ingredients
Pour into salt rimmed glasses. Some perfer to use sugar; what every your preference.

Submitter: 
Debbie Cash
Description: 
This is so good and simple to make too.  Its nice cause you can use either the large can or small can of limeaide depending on how much you want.  Ypu can also adjust the Tequila/Triple sec according to your taste. YUMMY
