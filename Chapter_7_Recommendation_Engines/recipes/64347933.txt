Title:
No Powdered Sugar Peanut Butter  frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2106477.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cream cheese
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/4 cup honey
1 cup cool whip

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, peanut butter, and honey together until smooth.  Fold in cool whip.

Submitter: 
Bobbie Susan
Description: 
This was inspired by my son who doesn't like overly sweet frosting but loves peanut butter.
