Title:
Oreo Truffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 18oz. pkg. Oreos, crushed and divided
8 oz. pkg. cream cheese, softened
2 8 oz. pkg. semi-sweet chocolate, melted

Directions:
Line a baking sheet with waxed paper.
Mix 3 c. of the cookie crumbs and the cream cheese in a food processor, until blended.
Shape into 42 one inch balls.
Dip balls into melted chocolate.
Sprinkle with remaining chocolate crumbs.
Refrigerate one hour or until firm.

Submitter: 
Gwenn Weiss
Description: 
This recipe was in the free Cosco cookbook that they hand out every few months.  These truffles always get RAVE reviews, and no one will believe how easy they are to make!
