Title:
BUTTERNUT SQUASH CASSEROLE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CASSEROLE:
1/2C. Sugar
1/2C. Light Brown Sugar
1C. Milk
1/2C. Butter or Margarine, melted
1Tsp. ground, Cinnamon
1/2Tsp. ground, Allspice
1/4Tsp. ground, Nutmeg
1 1/4Tsps. pure Vanilla Extract
3 Eggs
TOPPING:
1 1/4Cs. Light Brown Sugar
3/4C. Flour
1C. Old Fashioned Quacker Oats
1/2C. Butter or Margarine, melted
1Tsp. ground, Cinnamon
1 1/2Cs. Peacans or Walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Wash and peel butternut squash, chop and cook until tender. Drain well and place squash into food processor and peree!
Mix first 10 ingredients together and place into a well greased 10X10X2-inch casserole dish.
Mix topping ingredients, mix well and sprinkle onto top of casserole. Place into a 350 degree F. oven and bake for 35 to 45 minutes or until golden brown.
Serve hot!!
