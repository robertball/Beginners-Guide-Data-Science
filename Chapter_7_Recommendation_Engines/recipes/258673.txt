Title:
Empanadas de Jamon y Roquefort (Ham and Roquefort Empanadas) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4540639.jpg
Ingredients:
cooking spray
12 empanada pastry discs
2 cups cubed Roquefort cheese
2 cups cubed ham
1 egg, beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease 2 baking sheets with cooking spray.
Arrange pastry discs on a flat work surface. Dab a little water around the rims. Place some ham and Roquefort cubes in the center; fold pastry over and press with a fork to seal.
Transfer empanadas to the prepared baking trays. Brush beaten egg over the tops.
Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Fioa
Description: 
Quick and versatile empanadas made with ham and tasty Roquefort. Only 4 ingredients used for this gourmet finger food that your guests will love. Serve warm or cold.
