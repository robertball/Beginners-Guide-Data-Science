Title:
Mongolian Beef - Paleo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs beef flank stead, cut into stir fry-sized strips
2 tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp ginger, minced
2 cloves garlic, minced
3/4 cup coconut aminos
3/4 cup water
1/2 cup honey or agave
1/4 cup arrowroot powder
1/2 cup carrots, shredded
3 green onions, chopped separate green and white parts

Directions:
Coat flank steak stripes in arrowroot powder. Add remainder of ingredients except green parts of onions to crockpot and stir well to combine. Place flank steak into the crockpot and cook on high for 1 hour then on low for 5-6 hours. Top with green onions to serve.
