Title:
Tuna Rice Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-bag of uncooked rice (quick cooking)
1-can of spring water tuna
2 cups of crispy chow main noodles
1 red bell pepper (or any other veggies!)
2 cups of soy sauce

Directions:
1. prepare rice according to package
2. drain tuna, break into small pieces and empty into bowl.
3. dice red bell pepper and add to tuna
4. add prepared rice to tuna and bell pepper and mix thoroughly.
5. top with chow mein noodle
6. add soy sauce for flavoring as needed

Submitter: 
Jen Camilleri
Description: 
A light, healthy twist on tuna which is not only tasty but simple to make!  For added nutrition add as many vegetables as desired!
