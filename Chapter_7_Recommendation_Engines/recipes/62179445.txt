Title:
Dutch Meatballs (Gehakt ballen) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb meat loaf mix or 11/2 lb port and 1/2 lb hamburger meat
4 slices white bread
1/3 cup milk
1 egg
1 small onion finely chopped
1 TBS freshly grated nutmeg
1/2 tsp cloves
salt and pepper
1/2 cup butter
1 can diced tomatoes
1 can beef broth
1 pack onion soup mix
2 cups of water

Directions:
crumble bread into a mixing bowl - add milk and egg and mix well add cloves and nutmeg salt and pepper and onion and meet mix well and form into 6 balls.
Melt butter in a large dutch oven, when butter is hot add meat balls and brown on all sides add tomatoes, broth, water and onion mix bring to a boil and then simmer for about 1 hour
Serve with potatoes
