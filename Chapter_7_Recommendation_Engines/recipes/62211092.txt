Title:
Shrimp Diablos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package frozen cooked shrimp thawed
1 bell pepper chopped
2 jalapenos chopped without the seeds
1/2 onion chopped
1 can tomato soup
tabasco sauce

Directions:
sautee the jalapenos, bell pepper and onion for 3 to 5 minutes add shrimp for about 5 more minutes add tomato soup and about 3 dashes tabasco sauce give or take how hot you want it let cook for 5 more minutes then serve.....
