Title:
Pasta Fagioli with Sausage Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. olive oil
4 thin slices of pancetta - chopped
1 medium onion - chopped
1 carrot - peeled and chopped
2 stalks celery - chopped
1 bay leaf
3 cloves garlic - minced
3 tbsp. tomato paste
4 cups chicken stock
2 cups water
1-15oz. can cannelini beans - drained
3/4 lb. ground pork
1 egg
1/4 cup breadcrumbs
1/4 cup grated Parmigiano Reggiano
1 tsp. fennel seed
1 cup ditalini pasta

Directions:
In large pot, add olive oil and pancetta. Cook pancetta until crispy.
Add onion, carrot, bay leaf and garlic to pot with some salt and pepper and cook until tender - about 5 min.
Add tomato paste and cook until brick red in color, about 30 sec.
Add stock, water and beans to pot and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to medium.
While soup is heating, mix pork, egg, breadcrumbs, cheese, fennel seed, salt and pepper.
Mix to combine and form into 1" balls.
Drop meatballs into soup and simmer about 8 min. or until cooked through.
Turn heat to medium high and add ditalini to pot.
Cook to al dente.
