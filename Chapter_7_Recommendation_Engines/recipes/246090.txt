Title:
"OOO-MAMI" Sweet and Spicy Dip and Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3240430.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 tablespoons prepared salsa verde
2 tablespoons white sugar
2 tablespoons lime juice
2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh cilantro
2 tablespoons water
1 tablespoon sriracha hot sauce
1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
1 tablespoon rice vinegar

Directions:
Shake soy sauce, salsa verde, sugar, lime juice, cilantro, water, sriracha, ginger, and rice vinegar together in a container with a tight-fitting lid until sugar is dissolved. Refrigerate to allow flavors to blend, about 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Jose Napoleon Cuasay Artiaga I
Description: 
A top secret ingredient (salsa verde) gives this fusion sauce a different element that will have you saying, OOO-MAMI! Serve as a dipping sauce for pot stickers, egg rolls, or fried chicken. Use as a marinade for grilled meats and seafood; fantastic with chicken, pork, and shrimp.
