Title:
Spicy Korean Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1 cup soy sauce
1 cup water
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 tablespoon lemon juice (optional)
4 teaspoons hot chile paste (optional)

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over high heat, whisk together the SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, soy sauce, water, onion powder, and ground ginger. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, and simmer 5 minutes.
Remove the mixture from heat, cool, and whisk in lemon juice and hot chile paste. Place chicken in the mixture. Cover, and marinate in the refrigerator at least 4 hours before preparing chicken as desired.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
Use as a marinade for chicken, or to baste chicken while grilling. Make it as spicy as you like by adding more hot chile paste.
