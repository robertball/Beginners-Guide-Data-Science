Title:
Banana Pudding Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535581.jpg
Ingredients:
1 full sheet low fat graham cracker-crushed
1/2 banana sliced
1 sugar free vanilla pudding cup

Directions:
In a small serving bowl, place banana slices in the bottom, top with pudding, and sprinkle with the graham cracker crumbs. THATS IT! :)
