Title:
Pina Colada Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package vanilla or french vanilla cake mix,
1 3.5 oz vanilla pudding mix
1 1/3 cups water
4 eggs
1/4 cup vegetable or peanut oil
3 cups flaked coconut
1 1/2 cups crushed pineapple, drained
1/2 cup chopped pecans
2 tablespoons butter
2 teaspoons milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 8 ounce package cream cheese
3 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 9x13 inch cake pan.
Combine cake mix, pudding mix. water, eggs, and oil. Blend until smooth. Stir in 1 cup of the coconut, 1 cup crushed pineapple, and the nuts.
Bake at 350 degrees F for about 30 minutes (or until thouroughly baked).
Pina Colada Frosting
Place 1 cup coconut on an ungreased cookie sheet. Toast coconut until brown. Set aside.
Cream butter and cream cheese. Alternately add sugar and milk. Stir in last cup of coconut (untoasted) and remaining 1/2 cup pineapple drained.
Spread icing on cooled cake. Sprinkle with toasted coconut.
