Title:
Party Apple Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups light sour cream
1/4 cup light brown sugar, packed
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
6 apples - a mix of red, green & yellow
1/2 cup lemon lime soda

Directions:
To make the dip, in a small bowl, combine sour cream, brown sugar & cinnamon. Mix well, cover & chill 30 minutes.
Wash apples & pat dry. Cut each apple into 8 wedges & remove core. Place all wedges in a large bowl. Pour lemon lime soda over the apples & toss well. Drain well.
Stir dip again. Using 8 dollar store clear plastic tumblers, divide dip between each. Stand 6 assorted apple wedges in each cup. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
HappyGrandma
Description: 
An easy recipe and easy way to make individual snack cups for a children's party or play group!
