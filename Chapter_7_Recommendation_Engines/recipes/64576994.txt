Title:
Great Pumpkin Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15oz. can pumpkin
1 12oz can evaporated milk
3 eggs
1 cup sugar
1/2 tsp. cinnamon (or more, to taste)
1/4 tsp. nutmeg (or more, to taste)
1/8 tsp. ginger (or more, to taste)
1/8 tsp. cloves
1 pkg. white or yellow cake mix
3/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Mix first 5 ingredients together well.
Put in greased 9" x 13" pan.
Sprinkle with dry cake mix.
Drizzle with butter.
Top with walnuts.
Bake @ 350 x 1hr or till knife inserted near center comes out clean.
Serve with ice cream or whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
An easy dessert that features pumpkin, spice and nuts. Makes a large pan.
