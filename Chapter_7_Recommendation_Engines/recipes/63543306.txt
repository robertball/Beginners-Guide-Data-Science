Title:
Ira's Magical Secret Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Equal Parts:
Salt
Sugar
Ground Cumin
Mild Chile Powder
1/2 Parts:
Ground Mustard
Paprika
Garlic Powder
Onion Powder
For Heat:
Chipotle Powder
Cayenne

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
Rub onto chicken, beef, pork or fish.
Barbeque to taste.
Excess rub should be stored in the refrigerator in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
This is a recipe given to me by Ira Moss, one of the truly great bar-b-que'rs of all time.
