Title:
Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 bell peppers
1 cup rice
2 cups water
1 lb ground turkey
2 1/2 cup spaghetti sauce
4 cloves garlic
Red pepper
Oregano
Parsley
Mozzarella or Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 375
Combine rice and water in a small pot. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, simmer 12-15 minutes or until water is absorbed
Brown turkey. Stir in sauce, minced garlic, red pepper, oregano, and parsley. Simmer on low while rice finishes
Cut peppers in half lengthwise, de seed, and lay in a sprayed 9x13 pan
Add cooked rice to meat sauce
Spoon rice/sauce mixture into peppers. To with mozzarella or Parmesan cheese
Bake 20 minutes
