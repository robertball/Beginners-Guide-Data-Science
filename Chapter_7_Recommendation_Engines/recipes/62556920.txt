Title:
Carrot Puff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1    Pound Carrots
1/2  Cup Melted Margarine
3    Eggs
1/2  Cup Sugar
3    Tablespoons Flour
1    Teaspoon Baking Powder
1    Teaspoon Vanilla

Directions:
Peel and cut the carrots into one inch slices.
Cook the carrots in salted water.  Bring to a boil and then let the carrots simmer for 20 minutes or until the carrots are tender.
Drain the carrots.
In a blender combine the margarine, eggs, sugar, flour, baking powder and vanilla.
Add the carrots to the blender a little at a time and puree.
Pour into a medium size baking dish prepared with a non-stick spray.
Bake at 350 for 45 minutes until firm.
Let stand for 5 minutes.
Enjoy!

Description: 
This is a great side dish for any dinner.  Very light and delicious.  Always disappears quickly and you will make this one again and again!
