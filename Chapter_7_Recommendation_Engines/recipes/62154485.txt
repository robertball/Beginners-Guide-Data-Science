Title:
Lime Pineapple Dream Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/335054.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can lemon-lime soda
1 cup mini-marshmallows
1 small box lime jel-lo
1 pkg. cream cheese
14 oz. can crushed pineapple with juice
1 cup whipping cream
1/2 cup whipped dressing (mayo)

Directions:
Heat lemon-lime soda & marshmallows in saucepan on medium heat until marshmallows are melted (5-10 min), stirring frequently.
Stir in box of lime jel-lo powder, mix until dissolved.
Cut up cream cheese and add to lime mixture, (still on med. heat), stir frequently until melted & mixed thoroughly.
Remove from heat & stir in can of crushed pineapple with juices until mixed.
Put in fridge until partially thickened (1-2 hours).
Blend whipping cream until stiff.  Mix in whipped dressing.
Fold mixture into lime jel-lo bowl.
Pour into serving bowl.
Chill in fridge at least 4 hours.
