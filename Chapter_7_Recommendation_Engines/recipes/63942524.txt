Title:
Beth's Raspberry Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dough
8 oz package cream cheese
1/2 cup butter
4 c Bisquick mix
2/3 cup milk
1/2 cup raspberry jam (each cake)
Cream Cheese Mixture
1 pkg cream cheese
1/4 cup confectioner sugar
1 tsp vanilla
Icing
2 cup confectioner sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla
3 tbsp milk

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 425 degrees. Cut the cream cheese and butter into the Bisquick mix. Then add the milk to the mix as well and form the dough into two balls. Roll out onto a well floured surface. Fold and transfer the dough to a rectangular cookie sheet. Cut 2" strips down the sides. Spread the cream cheese mixture then the jam over the top of cream cheese mixture. Fold strips over the filling braiding as you go down. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until lightly brown. Let cool then drizzle with icing.

Submitter: 
Kevin Gorton
Description: 
A gal from our church Beth made these one time for a women's retreat brunch. Its a delicious pastry that makes people believe like you slaved for hours to make it but you really didn't.
