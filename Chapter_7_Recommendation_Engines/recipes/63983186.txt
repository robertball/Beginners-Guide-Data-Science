Title:
Taco Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 teaspoon all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon cornstarch
1 onion sliced
1/2 beef bouillon cube
1 teaspoon garlic
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/4 teaspoon granulated sugar

Directions:
1. Wash 1 lb ground beef until most of the blood has drained from the meat. Let excess water drain out.
2. Brown beef with onions in skillet; drain fat.
3. Add seasoning mix, all purpose flour, cornstarch and 3/4 cup of water.
4. Bring to a boil then reduce heat.
5. Simmer covered, stirring occasionally, until very little liquid remains, but meat should not be bone dry either.

Submitter: 
Marzia Razavi
Description: 
My version of a famous restraunt's seasoned ground beef recipe. This beef is perfect for taco's, mexican pizza, taquitos, or just about anything that calls for Mexican flavored ground beef.
