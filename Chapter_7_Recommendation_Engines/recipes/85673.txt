Title:
Five-Minute Chocolate Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987268.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package semi-sweet chocolate
2/3 cup heavy cream
3 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 tablespoons BACARDI® Rum
Fruits or cakes, diced

Directions:
Combine chocolate, cream, vanilla and BACARDI(R) Rum in saucepan over low heat for five minutes or until smooth.
Dip strawberries, brownies, marshmallows, bananas or pound cake into chocolate.

Submitter: 
BACARDI® Mixers
Description: 
Try this enticing chocolate fondue at your next gathering. Elegant enough for company, but easy enough for just snacking while watching a movie.
