Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2451262.jpg
Ingredients:
1 12-oz package white chips or morsels
2 teaspoons vegetable oil
1 10-oz bag fruit flavoured miniature marshmallows

Directions:
Line a 13x9 baking pan with wax paper; set aside.
Pour marshmallows into a large bowl; set aside.
Add white chips and oil to saucepan then heat and stir over medium-low heat until chips are completely melted and mixture is smooth.
Pour melted chips over marshmallows and gently stir until all marshmallows are evenly coated.
Spread mixture into prepared pan then refrigerate until set (about 2-3 hours). Once set, lift from pan by pulling up on wax paper. Cut up into small squares. Store in an airtight container at room temperature.
