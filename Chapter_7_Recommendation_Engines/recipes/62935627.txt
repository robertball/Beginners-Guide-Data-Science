Title:
Roasted Garlic & Parmesan Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1 teaspoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon basil
1 teaspoon black pepper, coarsely ground
1 whole garlic head, roasted and mashed
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 cup EVOO

Directions:
Whisk together vinegar, mustard and spices until well-combined. Whisk in garlic and cheese. Slowly drizzle in EVOO while whisking constantly. Chill at least one hour and serve. Store remainder in tightly sealed container in fridge.

Submitter: 
lucylove
Description: 
Whole roasted garlic plus freshly grated Parmesan cheese in a light vinaigrette.
