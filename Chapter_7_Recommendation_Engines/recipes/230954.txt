Title:
Salted Caramel and Chocolate Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/959702.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) tub PHILADELPHIA INDULGENCE Dulce de Leche Caramel, softened
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 ounce BAKER'S Bittersweet Chocolate, melted

Directions:
Spread INDULGENCE onto bottom of shallow dish.
Drizzle with chocolate; sprinkle with salt.
Refrigerate 15 min.

Submitter: 
PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese
Description: 
Decadent! A sweet and salty dip perfect for parties. It takes no time to put together and will wow your guests. Serve with apple slices, pretzels, or simple cookies.
