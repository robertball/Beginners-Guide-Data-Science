Title:
Toaster Oven Chicken Enchiladas II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/270252.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cooked chicken meat
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
1/3 cup finely chopped green onions plus estra for garnish
2 teaspoons Mexican seasoning or taco mix
1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles
1/3 cup plus two tablespoons favorite enchilada sauce, divided
2/3 cup sour cream, divided
6 (10 or 12 inch) corn or flour tortillas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Place dinner plates on top of toaster oven to warm.
In a medium large bowl, combine the chicken, 2/3 cup cheese, green onions,  mexican seasoning, green chiles, 1/3 cup sour cream and 1/8 cup enchlada sauce. Mix well and taste for seasoning.
Lay 4 tortillas flat on the counter. Divide the filling evenly between the tortillas.  Roll up and place seam side down in a 9x9 glass baking dish.
In a small bowl combine the reserved 1/3 cup enchillada sauce and the 1/3 cup sour cream.  Pour over the enchiladas and bake for 25 minutes.  Lightly tent if it gets too golden.
