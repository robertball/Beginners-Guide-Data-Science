Title:
Taco Lasagna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/644044.jpg
Ingredients:
1lb hamburg (lean so you don't have to drain)
1/4 large vidalia onion
1/2 large green bell pepper
1 Packet taco season
1 can Campbell cheddar cheese (10oz)
1 jar taco sauce (16oz)
1 can diced tomatoes w/ chile peppers (14oz)
12oz of cheddar cheese
6 flour tortillas (9 inch round)
Sour cream,chopped lettuce, crushed tortilla chips optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Cook 1st four ingredients in skillet on stove. While cooking spread thin layer of taco sauce in bottom of round cake pans then place first tortilla over it. Divide 1/3 can of cheddar cheese for first layer of both lasagnas then add some taco sauce for next layer. Once hamburg is cooked spread on lasagna then spread layer of diced tomatoes w/ chiles over it. Top the layers by sprinkling on some cheddar cheese. Place 2nd tortilla on top and follow pattern. Place 3rd tortilla and top w/ remaining cheddar cheese and taco sauce and shredded cheddar then place both lasagnas in oven for 20min. or until cheese and tortilla edges are golden. Serve w/ shredded lettuce and sour cream on the side. Can also top w/ crush tortilla chips for added texture.
