Title:
Georges Tuna Dump Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-12oz jar Marinated Artichokes(do not drain)
2-5oz cans tuna in water(drained)
2-18oz cans Progresso creamy mushroom soup
12oz egg noodles
8-16oz shredded cheddar cheese (personal preferrence)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 deg.
Spray a deep casserole dish with pam
Chop the artichokes roughly and put in bowl with their juice. Add everything else except the cheese and mix. Place in a casserole dish and put a thick layer of cheese on top. (this is critical to hold in the moisture) Bake 1 hour till top is bubbly and brown.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
Cousin George had me over for dinner one day and served this casserole. He says he is famous for it and after eating it I see why! The best part about it is the prep takes less time than it does for the oven to preheat!!!!
