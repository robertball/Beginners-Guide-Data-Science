Title:
World's Best Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 liters red wine
1 6 oz. can frozen lemonade concentrate
1/3 cup orange-flavored liqueur
5 cups lemon-lime soft drink, chilled
Lemon and orange slices as garnish

Directions:
Combine wine, lemonade concentrate, and liqueur in a large container.  Cover and chill until ready to serve.
To serve, combine wine mixture and soft drink.  Serve with fruit slices.

Submitter: 
CSANDST1
Description: 
This Sangria is fantastic!!!  I've given this recipe to a number of friends.  For the red wine, I use 2 750ml bottles of Lambrusco.  You won't be able to stop drinking this!
