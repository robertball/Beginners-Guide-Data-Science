Title:
Cookies-and-cream milk shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 chocolate sandwich cookies (such as Oreos)
2 cups vanilla ice cream, slightly softened
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
Place cookies in a zip lock bag and crush cookies with a rolling pin.
in a blender combine ice cream and milk.  Blend until smooth, add crushed cookies and pulse a few times just to blend.
Pour into two tall glasses and garnish if desired with a dollop of whipped cream and drizzle with chocolate syrup.
