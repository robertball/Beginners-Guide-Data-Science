Title:
Pineapple Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb deli ham (thinly sliced)
1 8 oz tub of Philadelphia Pineapple Flavored Cream Cheese
3 12' flour tortilla
1 bunch green onions

Directions:
Evenly spread the cream cheese on the flour tortilla to cover.
Layer the ham on top of the cream cheese to cover.
Lay on green onion stem on one end of the tortilla.
Starting at the end with the green onion, roll the tortilla into a log.
Cut the ends off to square up the tortilla.  Cut the log into 1/4-1/2 inch pinwheels.
The logs can be made ahead of time and refrigerated overnight.  Cut when ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Michele
Description: 
These little appetizers are quick and very easy.  What goes better together better than pineapple and ham?  They can be made 'seasonal' by using different flavored tortilla with different coloring.
