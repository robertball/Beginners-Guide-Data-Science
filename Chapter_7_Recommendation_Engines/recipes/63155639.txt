Title:
Ham Roll-Up Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/808892.jpg
Ingredients:
1lb of ham
1 block or 1.5 cups of cream cheese
3 whole pickles
2 tablespoons of pickle juice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into a food processor.
Mix until well blended but not runny.
Chill and serve with crackers or bread.

Submitter: 
kristinamarie
Description: 
A dip/spread that goes awesome with crackers or on toasted bread.
