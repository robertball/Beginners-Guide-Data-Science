Title:
Summer Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless Chicken Breasts or 1 whole chicken cut put with skin removed
1 c BBQ Sauce of your choice
1 c Apricot Preserves
1 pkg Dry Onion Soup Mix

Directions:
Heat BBQ Sauce, Preserves and Soup Mix to boiling. Pour over chicken. Bake at 350 1 to 1 1/2 hours.

Submitter: 
Julia
Description: 
Boiling the sause before you bake, make this easy Chicken dinner delicous. Just what you need in the summer!
