Title:
Sour Cream Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/942911.jpg
Ingredients:
COOKIES:
1 cup butter, softened
1 1/2 cups sugar
3 eggs
1 cup (8 ounces) sour cream
2 tsp vanilla extract
3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
FROSTING:
1/3 cup butter, softened
2 cups confectioners' sugar
2-3 tbls milk
1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
In mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Beat in eggs.  Add sour cream and vanilla; mix well. Combine flour, baking powder and baking soda; add to the creamed mixture and mix well. Chill doug at least 2 hours or overnight. Roll on a heavily floured board to 1/4 in. thickness.  Cut with a 3-in. cutter.  Place on lightly greased cookie sheets.  Bake at 350 for 10-12 minutes or until cookie springs back when lightly touched.  Cool. Mix frosting ingredients until smooth; spread over cookies.
