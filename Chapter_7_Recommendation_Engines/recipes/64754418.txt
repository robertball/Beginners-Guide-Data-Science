Title:
Jewel's Roasted Corn Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6886213.jpg
Ingredients:
3 ears of fresh sweet corn, roasted on the grill and then chilled
2 jalapeno peppers, diced
1/2 red onion, diced
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves, roughly chopped
the juice of one fresh lime, about 3 Tbsp
salt, to taste

Directions:
Cut the corn kernels from the cobs and place into a medium sized bowl.
Add in the jalapeno, red onion, garlic, cilantro, lime juice and salt. Mix well, gently breaking up any large corn chunks with the spoon.
Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.

Submitter: 
RainbowJewels
Description: 
This dish is inspired by my favorite salsa at Chipotle. 
You can use it to top  tacos or burritos, or just scoop it up with tortilla chips.
