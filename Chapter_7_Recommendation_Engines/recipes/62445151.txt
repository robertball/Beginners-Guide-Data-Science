Title:
Vanilla Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups water
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 tbs. Vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a sauce pan, bring to a boil over high heat, allow to boil for thirty seconds than cool and use. Store airtight in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Caleb1993
Description: 
vanilla flavored simple syrup for vanilla iced coffee like the one from a certain chain that has Golden Arches as a trademark and starts with an M.
