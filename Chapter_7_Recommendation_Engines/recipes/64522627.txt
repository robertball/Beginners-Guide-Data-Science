Title:
Salted Apple Pear Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Fresh crisp Apples diced
2 Fresh Firm Pears diced
2 Celery Stalks chopped
2 Limes zested & juiced
1 tsp Kosher Salt
1 tsp FRESH Cracked Pepper

Directions:
Chop & Drop everything in one bowl.
Mix well so lime juice coats all fruit to prevent browning.
Refrigerate at least 15 mins for flavors to develop.
Serve and enjoy.
