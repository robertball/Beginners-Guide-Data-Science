Title:
Hallelujah Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup chicken (or vegetable) stock
14 oz. can chickpeas, drained
3 cloves garlic
2 Tbs. lemon juice
3 Tbs. tahini (sesame paste)
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Add all ingredients into Tall Cup.
Twist on the Cross Blade and blend until smooth.
Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
Best served cold with triangles of pita bread.
