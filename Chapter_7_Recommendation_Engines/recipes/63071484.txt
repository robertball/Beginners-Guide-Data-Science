Title:
Kartoffelsuppe - (Potato Soup) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886784.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups potatoes, cubed
3 stalks celery, diced
1 cup onion, chopped
2 cups carrots, diced
3 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth, divided
4 cups half-and-half cream
6 tablespoons butter, melted
6 tablespoons flour
4 cubes  chicken bouillon
1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
In a large pot, bring potatoes, celery and onion to a boil in 2 cups of broth.  Cook until potatoes are tender, 15 minutes.  Drain and reserve liquid.
Combine reserved broth and half-and-half in pot.
In a bowl, combine melted butter and flour.  Stir into half-and-half  mixture over medium heat.  Stir until thickened.  Stir in reserved vegetables, remaining broth, bouillon and pepper.
Heat through and serve.
