Title:
Roasted Garlic Parmesan Zoodles (Zucchini Noodles) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small/medium zucchinis, spiralized (or a large package of zucchini noodles)
2 minced garlic cloves (or 2 frozen garlic cubes, defrosted)
Olive oil
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp dried parsley (optional)
Salt and Fresh Ground Pepper to taste
Grated Parmesan to taste
Fresh chopped parsley for garnish
Lemon juice, for garnish

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
In a large bowl, combine zoodles, garlic, olive oil, paprika, dried parsley, and salt.  Spread evenly over a large baking tray.  Bake for 10 minutes.  Remove from oven, stir, and bake for another 5 minutes.
After removing from oven, sprinkle with grated parmesan.  Garnish with fresh chopped parley and lemon juice.
