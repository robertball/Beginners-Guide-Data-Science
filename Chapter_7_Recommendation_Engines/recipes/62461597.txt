Title:
Fluffy Pumpkin Cheesecake Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages (8oz each) cream cheese, softened
2/3 cup sugar
2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
1/2 cup plain yogurt
1 can (15 oz) pumpkin
1 Keebler ready crust, Extra serving size pie crust

Directions:
1. In large mising bowl beat cream cheese on med speed of electric mixer until fluffy.  Add sugar and spice.  Beat until combined.
2. Add yogurt, mixing until just combined and then stir in pumpkin.
3. Pour into crust.  Bake @ 350 for 35 to 40 minutes or until center is almost set. Cool for 1 hour on wire rack  Refrigerate at least 3 hours.
4. Garnish as desired.  Store in refrigerator.
