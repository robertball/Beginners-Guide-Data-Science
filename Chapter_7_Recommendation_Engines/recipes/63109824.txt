Title:
Avocado and Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 avocados mashed
a pint of grape tomatoes cut in half
one lime, juiced
1/8 cup Hellman's mayo made with olive oil
2 cups fresh spinach
one hard boiled egg. diced
1 tsp. salt

Directions:
mash the avocados and add the mayo. Mix until the mixture is creamy and add the juice of one small lime. Add the salt to the mixture and the tomatoes. Mix well and add the spinach. I generally tear it into pieces. Top with the diced egg

Submitter: 
Frugalmommy
Description: 
A mix of avocados and mayo with a hint of lime make this salad refreshing
