Title:
Cheesy Baconator Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/625423.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups potatoes, peeled and cubed
1 cup celery, chopped
1 cup onion, chopped
2 cubes beef bouillon
2 cups water
2 tablespoon minced garlic
2 teaspoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
3 cups half and half
3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
2 3oz packages real bacon bits

Directions:
1. In a large sauce pan, add potatoes, celery, onions, beef bouillon, water, garlic and parsley.  Season with salt and pepper and simmer until vegetables are tender.
2. In a separate bowl, mix flour and half-and-half.  Once it is well blended, slowly add to soup and cook until soup thickens.
3. Stir in cheese and bacon and simmer until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
AbbiCarp
Description: 
This recipe was inspired by a potato soup that my son makes.  I was looking for something thick and hearty that would comfort me on a cold night.  I served it with cornbread and it ended up being so good that there was not a drop left!
