Title:
Cheesy Fried Hot Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 hot dogs
1 tablespoon of margarine
1 slice of cheese, cut into 4 pieces
4 hot dog buns
ketchup

Directions:
Melt the butter in a pan on medium heat.  Place the hot dogs in the pan.  When the first side gets browned turn them over and cut them lengthwise but not all the way through.  Place one of the cheese slices in the cut and continue to cook until browned.  Remove from the pan and add your favorite toppings.

Submitter: 
hungrydad
Description: 
My grandmother used to make these for us when we were kids.  Now , I like to have them on a bun with hot sauce and ketchup.
