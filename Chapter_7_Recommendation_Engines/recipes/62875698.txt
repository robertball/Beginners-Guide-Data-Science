Title:
Fried Ravioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of frozen ravioli
1 egg
Splash of milk
Breadcrumbs
Oil

Directions:
Take bag of ravioli and rinse briefly in warm water until barely sticky but still cold. Mix egg and milk in a small bowl. Then add bread crumbs to another bowl/plate.
Dip ravioli in egg then bread crumbs. When finished, heat oil in large pan until it bubbles when wooden spoon is placed in pan.
Fry ravioli until golden brown. Serve with ranch or warm marinara sauce.
