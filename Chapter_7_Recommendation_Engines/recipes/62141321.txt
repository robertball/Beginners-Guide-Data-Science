Title:
Mama's Punkin' Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 C flour
1 tsp baking powder
2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp cinnamon
3 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1 can (15 oz) solid pack pumpkin
3 C granulated sugar
1 C canola oil
1/2 C water
4 eggs
1 walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ÂºF. Grease 2 regular sized loaf pans or 4 small loaf pans.
Mix together & set aside: flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, & pumpkin pie spice.
In a large mixing bowl blend: can of pumpkin, sugar, canola oil, water, & eggs.
Add the flour mixture a little at a time, blend with mixer 3 minutes or so until well blended. Stir in nuts if using.
Pour pumpkin mixture into prepared pans.
Bake approximatly 50 minutes for small loaves and 1 hr 15-30 minutes for regular sized loaves. Use toothpick to test for doneness. Cool 15 minutes and remove from pans to cooling racks.
