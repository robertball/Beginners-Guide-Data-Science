Title:
Pepper Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. chuck steak, cut in chunks
4 large red and/or green peppers cut into 1 inch pieces
1 large onion sliced
1 8 oz. can tomato sauce
1 8 oz. can of water
3 tbsp. garlic powder
salt to taste

Directions:
Season the meat with salt and 1 tbsp. garlic powder. In a large pot or dutch oven put in the tomato sauce and can of water.  Season with the salt and 2 tbsp. garlic powder.  Add the onions to the pot and then add the meat.  Cook covered on low heat for about one hour until the meat is done.

Submitter: 
SANDI149
Description: 
This is a takeoff on the old standby pepper steak.  Filled with delcious peppers, chunks of beef, garlic and onions.  Serve over rice, if desired. So delicious.
