Title:
The best strawberry sorbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups frozen strawberrys
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/8 cup sugar

Directions:
First put the  strawberries  in your food processor for about 30 seconds or until smooth. Next add your lemon juice and sugar and bled for 10 seconds.  Lastly put the sorbet in the freezer for about and hour.
