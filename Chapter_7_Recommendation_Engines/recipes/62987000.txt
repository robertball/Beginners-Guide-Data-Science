Title:
Cream Cheese Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups all purpose flour, sifted
1/4 tsp salt
1-1/2 cups sweet butter, softened
1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese
3 cups sugar
6 large eggs
1-1/2 tsp. pure vanilla extract

Directions:
Do not preheat oven.
Grease and flour 12 cup tube pan.
sift together flour and salt, set aside
cream the butter, cream cheese and sugar on low speed for 10 min. or until light and fluffy.
Beat in eggs alternately with the dry ingredients, mixing well with each addition.
Add the vanilla.
Pour into prepared pan. Set the temp to 325 degrees and bake for 1 1/2 hours.  cool cake in the pan.

Submitter: 
Terry Goff Tripp
Description: 
If you can imagine a pound cake that tastes like cheesecake, you will have a good idea of just how rich this pound cake really is. With its velvety-smooth texture and delicate cream cheese flavor, this cake is perfect by itself.
