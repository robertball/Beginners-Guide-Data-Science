Title:
Colorful Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bags/boxes tri-color rotini
1 large red onion, minced
1 large red or yellow pepper, diced
1 head broccoli, in small florets
1 bottle fat free Italian salad dressing
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
salt and pepper.

Directions:
Cook tri-color rotini in a pot of boiling salted water till slightly undercooked, about 6 minutes for large rotini, 4 minutes for small.
Meanwhile, dice the veg and add to a large bowl.  Rinse the pasta immediately in cold water, drain.  Add the pasta to the veg, pour 3/4 of the bottle of dressing on top, stir.
Chill for an hour in the fridge, stir, and add the rest of the dressing if needed, then top with parmesan cheese when about to serve.  Enjoy!

Description:
