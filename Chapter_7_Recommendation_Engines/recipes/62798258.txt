Title:
Cilantro Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/655097.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups chopped cilantro
2 stalks celery, finely chopped
1/2 onion, chopped
2 tbsp shredded unsweetend coconut
1/2 cup Silk coconut milk
1/4 Silk almond milk
2 tbsp lemon juice
2 tsp coriander
1/4 tsp mustard seed
1 tsp chili powder
1 1/2 tbsp minced garlic
1/2 tbsp minced ginger
1 tsp cumin seeds
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Add water as needed to blend into a paste. Serve cold.
About 18 calories a tbsp.
