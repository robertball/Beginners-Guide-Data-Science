Title:
Laurie's Cream Cheese Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2(8)ounce packages of cream cheese
1 3/4 cups of milk
2 teaspoons dry mustard powder
Dash of ground pepper
Dash of nutmeg
1 tablespoon of cooking sherry
1 teaspoon of garlic salt

Directions:
Cube and let cream cheese soften in sauce pan.
Add 1 3/4 cups of milk
Heat until cream cheese is almost melted.
Add 2 teaspoons of dry mustard powder.
Add dash of ground pepper.
Add dash of nutmeg.
Add 1 tablespoon of cooking sherry.
Add 1 teaspoon of garlic salt.
Heat until cream cheese is completly melted.
(Milk will vary if you use non-fat)
