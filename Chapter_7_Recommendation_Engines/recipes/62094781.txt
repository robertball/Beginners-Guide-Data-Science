Title:
New Mexico Green Chile Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. hamburger
2-Big Jim chiles, roasted, peeled and diced OR:
1-6.5 oz can green chile, diced
About 15 crackers, crushed
3/4-cup onion, chopped
1-cup tomatoes, peeled and chopped OR:
1 small can diced tomatoes
2-eggs
1/2-cup milk (or enough to make soft)
1/2 to 1-tsp. salt
1/2-tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Mix ingredients together and put in a greased loaf pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 1hr and 10 min.
Let set for 10 min. and slip out of pan onto serving dish.

Submitter: 
BARBARA1940
Description: 
Growing up in New Mexico, we had access to the Hatch green chile (the best!). I add green chile to a lot of my recipes and it rather sets this meatloaf off. It's very good cold, made into sandwiches the second day.
