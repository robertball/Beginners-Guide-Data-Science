Title:
12th Man-Hattan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1981481.jpg
Ingredients:
2oz Dry Fly Washington Wheat Whiskey
1/2 oz sweet vermouth
3 dashes Angostura bitters
mandarin orange peel
2 maraschino cherries
ice

Directions:
Add ice to a cocktail shaker
To the shaker add 2oz of the whiskey, and 1/2 oz of the sweet vermouth
Top that with three dashes of the bitters
using a long bar spoon, gently stir the drink in the cocktail shaker
Strain the drink into your cocktail glass
Run the mandarin peel along the rim of the glass, and drop the peel into the drink
Drop the maraschino cherries into the glass, and enjoy!

Submitter: 
The (not so) Swedish Chef
Description: 
This is the perfect drink to serve to the Seahawks fan in your life! When a cocktail just isn't enough, try a Hawktail!
