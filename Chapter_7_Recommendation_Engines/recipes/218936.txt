Title:
McCormick速 Pecan Cookie Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992229.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
2 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar, divided
2 teaspoons McCormick速 Pure Vanilla Extract
1/2 teaspoon McCormick速 Ground Nutmeg
1/8 teaspoon salt
2 cups flour
2 cups finely chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Beat butter in large bowl with electric mixer on medium speed until light and fluffy. Add 1/2 cup of the sugar, vanilla, nutmeg and salt; beat until well blended. Stir in flour and pecans.
Shape into 1-inch balls. Place 1 inch apart on ungreased baking sheets.
Bake 15 minutes. Remove from baking sheets. Immediately roll hot cookies in remaining 2 cups sugar. Place sugared cookies on wire racks to cool. When cool, roll again in sugar.
