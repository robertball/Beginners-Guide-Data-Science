Title:
Big Ray's One Skillet Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4506931.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 can (14-1/2 ounces) diced tomatoes with basil, oregano and garlic, undrained
2 jars (14 ounces each) spaghetti sauce
1 can condensed cream of onion soup, undiluted
2 large eggs, lightly beaten
1 cups  cottage cheese
3/4 tsp Italian seasoning
9 no-cook lasagna noodles
1 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, divided

Directions:
In a large skillet, cook beef and garlic over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain. Stir in tomatoes and spaghetti sauce; heat through. Transfer to a large bowl.
In a small bowl, combine the soup, eggs, cottage cheese, 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese and Italian seasoning.  Return 1 cup meat sauce to the skillet; spread evenly. Layer with 1/2 cottage cheese mixture, 1/2 remaining meat sauce and half of the noodles, breaking to fit. Repeat layers of cottage cheese mixture, meat sauce and noodles. Top with remaining meat sauce. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 15-17 minutes or until noodles are tender
Remove from the heat. Sprinkle with remaining cheese; cover and let stand for 2 minutes or until melted.
