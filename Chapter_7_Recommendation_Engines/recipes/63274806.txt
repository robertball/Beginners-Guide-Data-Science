Title:
Simple, Healthy, Fast and Delicious Eggplant Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Medium - large eggplant, sliced, 1/8 inch thickness
2 eggwhites, beaten
Flour
Seasoned breadcrumbs.
olive oil ( Spray or Pam)

Directions:
Preheat the oven 350 degrees. Rack in the middle of the oven
Spray a cookie sheet with olive oil spray or pam.
Dip each slice of eggplant in flour to dredge.
Dip in the egg white to coat.
Dip in seasoned breadcrumbs and place on cookie sheet
Bake 10 minutes then flip each slice over and bake 10 more minutes.
Sprinkle  on Parmesan cheese before serving or use a think slice of Mozzarella Cheese and allow to melt before removing  from oven.
Serve with your favorite tomato sauce.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
I didn't feel like frying the breaded eggplant for Eggplant Parmesan and decided to use my oven instead.
The results were really terrific and saved my a lot time and clean up was easier.
