Title:
Baked Apples stuffed with Skor and Snickers candy bar mixture Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 nice size apples
1/2 cup coarsly chopped Toffee candy bar (such as
Skor and Heath)
1/2 cup coarsly chopped Snickers candy bar
2 TBS butter or oleo
3/4 cup apple cider or apple juice
1 TBS sugar

Directions:
Cut a small slice off the top of the apple. Less than 1/2 inch.
Being careful not to go all the way through the bottom of the apple, clean out apple core and seeds.
Place apples in a 9" baking dish.
Put the chopped Snickers bar and the Chopped toffee bar into a small bowl and blend well.
Fill center of apple half way with the candy bar mixture. Add 1 1/2 tsp butter or oleo on top of chopped candy bar. Finish filling apple with chopped candy bar and top with tsp. butter or oleo.
Mix apple juice or cider with 1 TBS sugar and blend will until sugar is disolved. Pour into dish with apples.
Bake apples at 350* until tender, around 1 to 1 1/2 hours.

Description: 
These baked apples remind me of the delicious chocolate covered apples that are so beautiful. Warm and yummy. I like to add a scoop of ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream.
