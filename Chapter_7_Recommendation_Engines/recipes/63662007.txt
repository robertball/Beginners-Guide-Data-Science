Title:
Roasted Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1017466.jpg
Ingredients:
4 sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1" cubes
6 parsnips, peeled and cut into strips
2 green peppers, cut into strips
6 mini sweet peppers, quartered
1/2 red onion, cut into large chunks
3 Tbsp. olive oil
2 Tbsp. balsamic vinegar
1 Tbsp. lemon juice, freshly squeezed
1/2 tsp. sea salt (or more to taste)

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
Combine vegetables in a medium sized bowl and pour olive oil, balsamic vinegar and lemon juice over the top.
Stir together than sprinkle salt over the top and combine.
Pour into a roasting pan and cook in the oven for 20 minutes.
Give the veggies a stir then cook for an additional 15 minutes.
Stir veggies and roast for 10 minutes more.
Add more salt to taste if needed.

Submitter: 
halfnotes
Description: 
This was a big hit in my household and can be made with a wide variety of vegetables.  Herbs and other spices could be added but I wanted to start with a simple base.  Have fun with this one!
