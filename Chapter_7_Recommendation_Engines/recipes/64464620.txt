Title:
Cheesy Broccoli and Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2433462.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup milk
1 tbsp chicken soup base
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
3 tablespoons butter, divided
2 cooked and seasoned chicken breasts cubed
4 cups vegetables such as broccoli florets and red bell peppers
2 1/4 cups shredded Cheddar or Mont-Jack cheese divided
1 tablespoon onion powder
1/4 cup panko bread crumbs
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in large nonstick skillet and saute broccoli and red bell pepper until crisp tender. Turn off heat. Whisk milk and flour and add to veggies. Add chicken, cooked vegetables, 2 cups cheese, and onion powder; toss to mix. Pour into 8-inch baking dish.
Combine panko crumbs, remaining 1 tablespoon butter and remaining 1/4 cup cheese in small cup. Sprinkle over top of casserole.
Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until crumbs are golden and mixture is bubbly around edges.
