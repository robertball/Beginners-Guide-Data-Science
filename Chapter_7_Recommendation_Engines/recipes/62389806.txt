Title:
Hamburger Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 slices bacon, diced
2 cups of thinly sliced mushrooms
1/2 C chopped onion
1 T olive oil if needed
1 1/2 lb ground beef
1 1/2 T flour
3/4 t salt
1/4 t paprika
dash pepper
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 can milk
1 C sour cream

Directions:
Cook bacon in large heavy skillet. Remove bacon to waiting dish, saut√© the mushrooms in the bacon grease. Add to waiting bacon. Cook the chopped onions, adding small amount of olive oil if necessary. When transparent, add to bacon and mushrooms. Brown beef in the same pan. Spoon off excess fat.
Return cooked bacon, mushrooms and onion to cooking pan. Sprinkle flour, salt paprika and pepper into meat. Stir in mushroom soup. Add 1/2 can of milk. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
Stir in sour cream, heat through but do not boil.
Serve over rice or noodles.
