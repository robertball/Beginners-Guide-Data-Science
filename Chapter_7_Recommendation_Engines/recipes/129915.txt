Title:
Blueberry Cheesecake Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979155.jpg
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons butter, melted
2 cups HONEY MAID Graham Cracker Crumbs
2 (8 ounce) packages PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened
3/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 (10 ounce) jar blueberry jam or preserves
1 cup blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Mix butter and graham crumbs in 13x9-inch baking pan. Press firmly onto bottom of pan. Refrigerate until ready to use.
Beat cream cheese in large bowl with electric mixer on medium until creamy. Add sugar, eggs and vanilla; beat until well blended. Set aside. Stir jam in jar until softened; spread evenly onto crust. Sprinkle with blueberries; top evenly with the cream cheese mixture.
Bake 30 min. or until slightly puffed. Cool completely in pan. Cut into 24 bars to serve. Store any leftover bars in tightly covered container in refrigerator up to 3 days.

Submitter: 
Philadelphia Cream Cheese
Description: 
Blueberry preserves and fresh blueberries are the middle layer of these sweet treats.
