Title:
Citrus Gelatin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 3oz packages of lemon gelatin
1 cup boiling water
1 quart vanilla ice cream, softened
1 11oz can mandarin oranges
1 8oz can crushed pineapple
1 can cooking spray

Directions:
In a large bowl, dissolve the gelatin into the boiling water. Whisk in the pre-softened ice cream until fully melted.
Drain the oranges and pineapple of any excess juice. Stir into the mixture.
Take a 6-cup mold or bowl and spray with cooking spray. Refrigerate for 2 hours or until firm. Put the molded mix onto a serving dish, or keep in the bowl to serve.

Submitter: 
Kristi Laudert
Description: 
An easy to make dessert that is great for holiday gatherings.
