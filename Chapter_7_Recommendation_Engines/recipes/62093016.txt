Title:
Easy Summer Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups dry elbow macaroni
1 Cup salad dressing, (Miracle Whip)
1/4 Cup sugar
1 small to medium size cucumber
1 medium tomato
1/4 Cup onion

Directions:
Cook the macaroni according to package directions for 9-10 minutes. Drain, and rinse in cold water.
Mix the salad dressing and sugar together in medium size bowl.
Chop the vegetables in small pieces and add to the dressing.
Gently stir in the macaroni and serve right away.

Submitter: 
Gina
Description: 
Simply delicious and easy. Best served right away so can make it up last minute. This recipe came from a school kitchen and was always a huge hit with teachers and students alike.
