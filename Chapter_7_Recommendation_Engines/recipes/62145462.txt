Title:
Cabbage, Banana, Peanut Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large head of green cabbage
2 firm bananas (not too ripe)
1/4 tsp. salt
1/3 cup sugar
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup crushed peanuts

Directions:
Cut cabbage into 1 inch cubes and place a single layer at a time into food processer, pulse several times, to get a fine consistantcy. Repeat until all cabbage has been finely chopped.
Coarsely crush peanuts with rolling pin.
Peel and dice bananas(do this at the last minute to prevent discoloring).
Just before serving mix all ingredients well.
Serve imediatly.

Submitter: 
Mae Long
Description: 
This sweet slaw will tempt even the most picky eaters to try it. My grandfather invented this slaw 45 years ago to get us kids to eat cabbage.  It's now a family favorite.
