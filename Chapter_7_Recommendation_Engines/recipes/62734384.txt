Title:
Cool and Crunchy French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/627068.jpg
Ingredients:
5 crunchy toast crackers, plain or wheat (usually found in a grocery store's cracker aisle)
1 tablespoon of butter
2 teaspoons of powdered cinnamon
1/4 cup of maple syrup

Directions:
Soften the butter on a plate or in a bowl by placing in microwave for a few seconds. Spread the butter on the toast, then sprinkle a good pinch of cinnamon on each. To top it off, drizzle the top of each with syrup.

Submitter: 
fiestyturtle
Description: 
You don't need a diner to capture the essence of French toast; this sweet, creamy, crunchy combination is ready in 1 minute!
