Title:
Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups margarine, softened
2 cups sugar
2 cups brown sugar
4 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla
12 oz. pkg. chocolate chips
3 1/2 cups flour
1 1/2 tsp. salt
2 tsp. baking soda
4 cups rolled oats

Directions:
Mix margarine and sugars together well.
Stir in eggs and vanilla.
Add dry ingredients and chocolate chips.
Mix well.
Drop by tablespoonfuls onto cookie sheet.
Bake @ 350 x 10 - 15 minutes (depending on size of cookies & individual oven temps)

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A family favorite. Freezes well. This makes a LOT of cookies, but then you'll always have some on hand to send home with loved ones ;)
