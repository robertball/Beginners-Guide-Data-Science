Chocolate Walnut Crumb Bars by NestleÂ® Recipe

Ingredients:
1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
2 cups (12 oz. pkg. semi-sweet chocolate morsels), divided
1 1/4 cups (14 oz. can) sweetened condensed milk
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup chopped walnuts
Cooking spray

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.  Spray a 9" X 13 " pan with cooking spray.
In large mixing bowl, beat butter until creamy.  Beat in flour, sugar, and salt until crumbly.  With floured fingers, press 2 cups crumb mixture into the bottom of pan.  Reserve remaining mixture. Bake for 10-12 min. or until edges are golden brown.
In a small saucepan over low heat, warm 1 1/2 cups of chocolate morsels and sweetened condensed milk, stirring until smooth.  Stir in vanilla.  Spread over hot crust.
Stir walnuts and remaining morsels into the reserved crumb mixture.  Sprinkle over chocolate filling.  Bake for 25-30 min. or until center is set.
Cool in pan on wire rack.
