Title:
Applesauce Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c Corn meal
1 c Whole wheat or Whole spelt flour
1/3 c Honey
3t Baking powder
1/2t Salt
1 c Applesauce
1/2 c water
1 egg
2 T melted butter

Directions:
Mix dry. Mix wet. Combine. Pour in 8 x 8" greased pan. Bake at 425 for 20 min.

Submitter:

Description: 
Moist corn bread. Serve with chili beans.
