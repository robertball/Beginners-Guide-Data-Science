Title:
Slow Cooker Potatoes with Bacon and Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1015358.jpg
Ingredients:
4 medium potatoes
4 slices raw bacon trimmed of excess fat and cut into 1 inch pieces
1 medium onion, sliced
2 cloves garlic, sliced
salt and pepper to taste
3 pats of butter
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Spray heavy duty foil with Pam.
Peel and largely dice potatoes and place on foil.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Add onion, garlic and bacon.
Top with butter pats.
Bundle top of foil together and place in crock pot.
Cook on high for 5 hours or low for 8 hours.
Remove bundle from slow cooker and place potato mixture in large skillet.
Fry until browned.
Sprinkle 1/4 cup of cheese over each serving.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
Frying potatoes takes so long to get them crispy.  I like this because you can prepare it in the morning and have it ready to finish in the skillet right  before eating.
