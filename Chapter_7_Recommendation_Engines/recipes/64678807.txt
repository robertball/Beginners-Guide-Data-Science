Title:
Green Chile Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp vegetable oil
1 1/2 lbs pork butt, cut in 1-inch cubes
1 1/2 cups diced onion
1 tbsp minced garlic
6 cups chicken broth
1 lb red potatoes, cut in 1/2 inch cubes
3 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups roasted, peeled, chopped green chiles, hot
1 1/2 cups roasted, peeled, chopped green chiles, mild
3 tbsp diced red bell pepper
2 tbsp chopped cilantro

Directions:
Heat the oil in a 6-quart pot over high heat and brown the meat in batches.  Set aside.
In the same oil, sauce the onions until brown.
Add the garlic and sauce 1 minute.
Return meat to the pan along with any juices that have accumulated.  Add the broth, potatoes, salt and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour, until the potatoes are tender.
Add the green chiles and the red bell pepper, cook 15 to 20 minutes more.
Add the cilantro, stir and serve.
