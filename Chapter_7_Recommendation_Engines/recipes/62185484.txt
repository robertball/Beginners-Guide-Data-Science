Title:
Mango Coconut Creme Brulee' (American Measurements) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons(25 grams) of sugar
6 egg yolks
1/3 cup(100 ml) coconut cream
2 cups (400 ml) Cream
2 mangoes chopped up
Hand full of toasted coconut

Directions:
1.  heat cream and coconut cream
2.  mix yolks and sugar
3.  Pour cream mixture over sugar mixture
4.  In ramekins, put mango and coconut
5.  Pour strained mix over
6.  Bake in water bath at 160 degrees centigrade

Submitter: 
Carole Anne Stanton Hochstetle
Description:

This simple to make recipe came to me from a chef at a restaurant who took time to write the recipe for me.
