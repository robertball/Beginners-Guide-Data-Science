Title:
Shrimp Cocktail Sauce or Ketchup - Sugar Free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2289860.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup xylitol
1/4 cup hot water
1 6-ounce can Tomato Paste
1/2 cup Apple Cider Vinegar - with the 'mother'
2 scoop Kal Pure Stevia Extract (equivalent to 1 tbsp Sugar)
1 teaspoon Sea Salt
1/4 teaspoon Onion Powder
1/8 teaspoon Garlic Powder
5 tbsp (75 g) Horseradish - less if you don't like it spicy

Directions:
Combine xylitol and hot water in a 1 quart glass storage container
Heat for 15 seconds on high in microwave
Stir until xylitol is dissolved
Add the rest of the ingredients, Except the Horseradish
Stir until smooth
Heat in microwave on high for 30 second intervals until it boils, stir between each interval, reduce to 10 second intervals once it starts bubbling
Then heat at 30% power for 10 minutes, stirring often
Let cool
Chill and store in a covered container
Once chilled add horseradish

Submitter: 
Zan
Description: 
Simple sugar free shrimp cocktail sauce that was created from the Heinz Ketchup copycat recipe.
