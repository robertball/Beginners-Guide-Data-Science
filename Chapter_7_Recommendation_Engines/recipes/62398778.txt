Title:
Lemon Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c butter
1 c sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1 1/2 c flour
1 t baking powder
1 t salt
1/2 c milk
1 lemon rind, grated (2T)
1/2 c nuts (optional)
3 T lemon extract
Lemon Glaze
1/4 C lemon juice
1/2 c sugar

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and pour into a large loaf pan. Bake 50 minutes at 350. (2 small loaf pans 30-40 min). Top with lemon glaze while hot. Cool in pan.
