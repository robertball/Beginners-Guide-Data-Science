Title:
Elvis Watermelon Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5449551.jpg
Ingredients:
1 slice center-cut slice of watermelon, 1 to 2 inches thick
2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter
1/2 banana
1 teaspoon honey, or to taste
1 tablespoon chopped peanuts

Directions:
Cut watermelon slice into 8 wedges, but keep them together for a nice presentation. Pat dry with a paper towel or clean dishcloth.
Spread peanut butter evenly over the watermelon, stopping at the rind. Top with sliced banana. Drizzle with honey and top with peanuts. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Katherine Martinelli
Description: 
Elvis was famous for his love of banana and peanut butter sandwiches, and it turns out the combination works amazingly well as a watermelon pizza. Topped with crunchy chopped peanuts, this watermelon pizza dessert or snack has it all going on: refreshing watermelon, creamy protein, sweet bananas, and crunchy nuts.
