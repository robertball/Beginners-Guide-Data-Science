Title:
Dorothy's Cocktail Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C ketchup
1/2 C. Heinz Chili Sauce
Zest and juice of l lemon
Juce of 1/2 small lime
1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
1 t. puree chipotle in adobo
2 T. vodka
pinch of garlic salt

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix all ingredients and refrgerate.
Lasts 2 weeks in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Dorothy Clark
Description: 
To cook shrimp:
Boil a big pot of salted water.  
Add fresh lemons.
Add shrimp.  Cover pot.
Turn off heat.  Take off heat after 3-4 minutes.
Drop into cold water to stop cooking.Modified from: Craig's Cocktail Sauce
