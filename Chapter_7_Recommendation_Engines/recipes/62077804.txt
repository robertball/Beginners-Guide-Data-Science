Title:
Lauras Asian Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 chopped red onion
1 bag cole slaw
4 Tablespoons rice wine vinegar
1 can mushrooms
soy sauce
garlic oil
pepper

Directions:
First off, heat a large skillet over med heat on the stove top. Add about 3 tablesppons garlic oil and let heat. Add in chopped onion and let simmer for 5 min. Add in bag of slaw. Dash in vinegar, and add more if you like a stronger taste. Add in everything else, and let simmer together for 15 min or until limp and slaw is transparent.
Serve warm or cold, and enjoy!
