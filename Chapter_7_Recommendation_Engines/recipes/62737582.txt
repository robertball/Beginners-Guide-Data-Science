Title:
Sour Cream Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. flour
2 tbsp. butter
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. sugar
1 tsp. salt
1 c. sour cream, not low fat
1/4 tsp. nutmeg, not looks likes a lot, but adds a lot of flavor, do not omit

Directions:
Melt butter and add to sour cream. Sift dry ingredients and add to sour cream slowly. Mix well. Cut into shapes and bake 12-15 mins.

Submitter: 
Deborah Norris
Description: 
Old fashioned biscuit recipe. Don't skimp on ingredients, no low fat here.
