Title:
Mini Sausage Jalapeno Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent rolls
1 (16 ounce) package ground sausage
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese with chive and onion, softened
1 jalapeno chile pepper, seeds removed and finely diced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Place ground sausage in a large skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown.
While sausage is cooking, de-seed and finely dice jalapeno chile pepper.
Roll out crescent rolls onto a large cutting board. Flatten to form a single rectangular shape, pressing seams together.
Cut crescent rolls into 24 equal squares (6x4).
Press each sqaure into ungreased 1 1/2 inch muffin cups (mini-muffin size) to make a shallow shell.
Drain sausage and place in a medium bowl. Mix with the cream cheese and diced jalapeno chile pepper.
Place 1 tablespoon of sausage mixture into each cup.
Bake in the preheated oven 12 minutes, or until the crescent rolls are golden brown.
Serve while warm.

Submitter: 
Kristi Goulette Lozensky
Description: 
These yummy little tarts are great to serve at parties. Sausage, cream cheese and jalapeno give it a little kick. Adjust the amount of jalapenos to your liking.
