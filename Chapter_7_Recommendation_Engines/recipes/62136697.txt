Title:
Maple Raisin Layer Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2  pkgs  Cream cheese, at room temp. (8 oz pkgs)
1  cup  marg. softened
1  cup  brown sugar (packed)
3/4  cup  pure maple syrup,divided
1 1/2  tsp  maple extract,divided
1  cup   raisins
1  pkg  white cake mix(double layer size)
1  cup  sour cream
3  lg  eggs
1/4  cup  cup oil
1 1/2  tsp  cinnamon

Directions:
(icing)At medium speed beat cream cheese with marg. until smooth;add sugar. Beat until light, 1-2 min. Beat in 1/2 cup syrup and 1 tsp. extract. Refrigerate while baking cake.
Preheat oven to 350*F. Coat 2(9") round cake pans with cooking spray. Toss raisins with 1 Tbs.cake mix. At low speed beat next 4 ingr. and remaining mix,maple syrup and extract 1 min..
Increase speed to medium: beat 2 minutes. Fold in raisins. Divide batter between pans. Bake 30 mins. or until toothpick comes out clean. Cool 10 mins.
Remove from pans.
Let cool then ice.
