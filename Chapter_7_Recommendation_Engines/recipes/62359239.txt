Title:
Sarah's Bolognese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/681134.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound Hot Italian sausage
1 pound Ground Beef
1 10 oz can of Evaporated Milk
1 lbs of cubbed ham
1 large onion - chopped
4 cloves garlic - crushed
1 Green Pepper - diced
1 lb mushrooms - sliced
1 carrot - finely shredded
1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
2 cups white wine
1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1 tablespoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Brown sausage and beef. Add evaporated milk and reduce by 1/2.  Remove from skillet and saut√© onion, garlic, green pepper.  Add mushrooms, ham, carrot, tomatoes, wine and seasonings. Simmer for 1.5 hours or in pressure cooker at high for 10 minutes.
