Title:
Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/3 plus 1 Tbsp shortening
2 to 3 Tbsp cold water

Directions:
Measure flour and salt in bowl.  Cut in shortening thoroughly.  Sprinkle in water, 1 Tbsp at a time, mixing with fork until all flour is moistened and dough almost cleans side of bowl.
Place dough on floured cloth-covered surface.  With floured stockinet-covered rolling pin, roll dough 2 inches larger than inverted pie plate.  Fold pastry into half, unfold and ease into pan.  Trim.
For baked pie shell, prick bottom and side with fork.  Bake at 475 degrees F for 8 to 10 minutes.
For other pies, bake as directed in your recipe.
