Title:
Peanut Butter Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks margarine
10 whole graham crackers
2 cups peanut butter
1 lb powdered sugar
12 oz. melted chocolate chips

Directions:
Crush graham crackers
Melt margarine and mix in graham cracker's and peanut butter. When well combined, add powdered sugar.
Press mixture on the bottom of a cookie sheet 9x15(ish). Pour melted chocolate chips on top and chill for 1 1/2 hours.
