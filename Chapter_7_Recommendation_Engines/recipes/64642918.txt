Title:
Chicken Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless Chicken Tenders Cut1 Tbs Coconut Oil Tsp pepper1 Tsp onion Salt1 c. Heavy Cream or milk1 c. Milk2 c. Water5 garlic cloves3 tbs margarine box fettuccine noodles or other pasta c grated parmesan

Directions:
Cook oil, Chicken, Cream, milk, water, salt, pepper, margarine, garlic, parmesan in large sauce pan. Cook on high bring to boil.Break noodles in , add to pan and cover.Decrease heat to medium, cook until al dente.Cool & Serve, top with lemon juice and parsley. 3 servings, Best eaten same day.

Submitter: 
Eva Maria
Description: 
Delicious, creamy and flavorful. Best if enjoyed the same day!
