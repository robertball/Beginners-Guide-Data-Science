Title:
Diabetic Taco Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4539545.jpg
Ingredients:
3lbs Ground Turkey
1lb Low fat Sharp Shredded Cheese
20 Whole Wheat Tortillas
Diced fresh tomato (for garnish)
Chopped Lettuce (for garnish)
Taco Seasoning:
4 TBSP Chili Powder
1 Tsp Garlic Powder
1 Tsp Onion Powder
4 Tsp Black Pepper
2 Tsp Oregano
2 Tsp Paprika
2 Tsp Cumin

Directions:
Brown turkey until cooked
Add all the ingredient for taco mix stir in with 1/2 cup water allow to simmer 10 minutes
Spoon meat mixture into heated wheat tortillas top with cheese and lettuce and tomato (if desired)

Submitter: 
Michelle Hughes
Description: 
Who says you can't have a great taco if you're diabetic?  This recipe comes with homemade taco seasoning that even my kids fell in love with!
