Title:
Honey Baked Chicken II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 lbs. chicken breasts
1/4 cup butter, melted
1/2 cup honey
1/4 cup prepared mustard
1 teaspoon salt
1-1/2 teaspoon curry powder
1 garlic clove minced
1/4 tsp. cayenne

Directions:
Place chicken pieces in a shallow baking pan, skin side up. Combine the melted butter or margarine, honey, mustard, salt, garlic, cayenne and curry powder and pour the mixture over the chicken. Cover and marinate in refrigerator 1-2 hrs.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Cover chicken w/foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.  Remove foil and baste with pan drippings, until the chicken is nicely browned and tender and the juices run clear, approx. 20-30 min.
