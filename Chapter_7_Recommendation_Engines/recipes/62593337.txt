Title:
Simplicity Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. boneless beef, cubed
1 env. dried onion soup mix
2 cans cream of celery soup
2 soup cans of water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 275 degrees.  Combine soups and water in large, oven safe pot with lid.  Add beef.  Cover and bake for 4-5 hours.
Serve over hot cooked rice, noodles or mashed potatoes.
