Title:
Breakfast Sausage Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb bulk sausage, any flavor
3 eggs, lightly beaten
1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
2 packages crescent rolls

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
1.  Brown sausage and drain. This may be done the night before and stored in the refrigerator.
2.  Mix sausage with eggs and cheese.
3.  Spread one roll of crescent rolls in the bottom of a 9x13" baking dish, pinching seams together to seal.
4.  Top crescent rolls with sausage mixture, leaving 1/2" on all sides.
5.  Unroll the second roll of crescent rolls.  Pinch seams together to seal and place on top of sausage mixture.  Fold edges of bottom layer of crescent rolls over edges of top layer and pinch to seal.
6.  Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes.  Let stand 10 minutes.  Cut into bars.

Submitter: 
ASHBETH
Description: 
This is a delicious alternative to the old-fashioned breakfast sausage casserole.  It's also portable which is great for mornings when you're running really late, just make sure that you let your serving sit for about 10 minutes before taking it in the car!
