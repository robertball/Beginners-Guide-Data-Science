Title:
Mom's Cocktail Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground beef vs 50+ premade meatballs (1/2 OZ EACH)
1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
1 egg beaten
1 onion, minced
1/2 tbs worchestershire sauce
pepper to taste
12 oz bottle chili sauce
10 oz  jar grape jelly

Directions:
mix the first 6 ingredients.  shape into one inch balls.  brown meatballs in shortening. remove and clean skillet.  heat chili sauce and grape jelly in skillet.  add meat balls and simmer uncovered 30 mins.  serve with cocktail picks.
