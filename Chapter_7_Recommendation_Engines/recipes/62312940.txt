Title:
Cheesy Greenbeans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/373320.jpg
Ingredients:
1 14.5 Oz Can of Cut Green Beans
2 Slices of Bacon, diced
1 Tbsp Cajun Seasoning
1 Tbsp Dried Chopped Onions
1 Tbsp Garlic Mushroom Seasoning (or sub 1 Tsp Garlic Powder)
1/2 Cup Grated Sharp Cheddar
1/3 Cup Half and Half
1 Tsp Pepper
Splash of Cajun Hot Sauce

Directions:
In saucepan, fry chopped bacon until done
Add un-drained can of green beans to the bacon
Add all seasonings
Boil uncovered until beans are tender
Add Half and Half
Add Cheese
Simmer until juice is reduced to a sauce consistency
Serve

Submitter: 
Jesse Cody
Description: 
This is a simple and delicious variation of a cheesy green bean casserole.
