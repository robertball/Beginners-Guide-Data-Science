Title:
Restaurant-Style Coleslaw I plus a little zip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package coleslaw mix
2 tablespoons minced onion
1/4 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup buttermilk
1 1/2 tablespoons apple cider or white wine vinegar
2 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/8 tsp celery seed
1/8 tsp dry mustard
1/8 tsp onion powder

Directions:
Combine the sugar, salt, pepper, milk, mayonnaise, buttermilk, vinegar and lemon juice, celery seed, mustard, and onion powder; mix until smooth. Pour mixture over the coleslaw; stir well and chill for at least 1 hour.
