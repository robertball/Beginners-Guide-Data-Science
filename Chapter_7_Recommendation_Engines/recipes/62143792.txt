Title:
Ally's Crockpot Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz can diced tomatoes
1 28 oz can whole tomatoes
3 Tbsp Kalamata olive oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup finely chopped red onion
1/4 tsp each of rosemary, thyme, oregano, parsley, basil, and tarragon
1/2 yellow bell pepper, chopped
1/2 green bell pepper, chopped
1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
4 chicken sausages, diced
dash each of ground black pepper, ground white pepper, and sea salt
1 tsp brown sugar
4 oz tomato paste
4 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup shredded romano cheese
1/4 cup shredded parmesan cheese

Directions:
Combine tomatoes, tomato paste, and cheeses in a crock pot set to high heat.
In a skillet, heat 1 Tbsp olive oil and sautee garlic, onion, chicken sausage, peppers and spices. When onions are translucent and chicken sausage is thoroughly cooked, transfer to crock pot and stir.
Add balsamic vinegar, brown sugar, and the remaining olive oil. Stir and leave for two hours.
Set heat to low and simmer for at least an hour more, or until you get home from work ;)

Submitter: 
ally-gator
Description: 
With zesty chicken sausage and two varieties of cheese, this spaghetti sauce is a heart-warming rendition of a favorite classic.
