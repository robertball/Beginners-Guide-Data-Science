Title:
The Best Asparagus ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-lbs asparagus
2-Tbs butter
2-tsp soy (regular or low sodium)
1-tsp balsamic vinegar. , toss well to coat
1-lemon for zesting
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400. Arrange 2-lbs asparagus in a single layer on baking sheet. Coat w/ cooking spray & sprinkle w/ salt & pepper. Bake for 12 minutes. Melt 2-T butter in a small skillet over medium heat; cook 3 mins or until lightly browned, shaking pan occassionally. Remove from heat & stir in 2-t low-sodium soy or regular soy sauce  & 1-t balsamic vinegar. Drizzle over asparagus, toss well to coat. Garnish with cracked black pepper and grated lemon zest.
