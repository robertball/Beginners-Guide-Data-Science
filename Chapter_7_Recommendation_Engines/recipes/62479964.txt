Title:
Hot Caramel Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup corn syrup
1 cup brown sugar
1 14oz can sweetened condensed milk
apple slices

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except apple slices in a suate pan. Bring to boil stirring frequently over medium heat.
Cool and serve or place in  a crockpot on warm and serve hot.
Left overs can be kept in airtight container in the refigerator.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
Serve hot or cool. Very delicious!
