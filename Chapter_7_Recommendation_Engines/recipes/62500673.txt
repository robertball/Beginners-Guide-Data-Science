Title:
Maple Balsamic Vinagrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 Cup Balsamic Vinegar
1/4 Cup Olive Oil
1/4 Cup Apple Juice
3 Tbsp Maple Syrup
2 Tbsp Lemon Juice
1 Tbsp Dijon Mustard
1 Tbsp Dill (fresh) or 1-1/2 tsp dried Dill
1 tsp minced Garlic
1/4tsp Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Mix all in salad dressing bottle; good in fridge for a few weeks

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
This is such an easy and super delicous salad dressing...and the best part is that there is no preservatives or junk in it.  Stays good in the fridge for a number of weeks.
