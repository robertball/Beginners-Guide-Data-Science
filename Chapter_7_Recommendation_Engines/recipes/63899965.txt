Title:
Chocolate Pumpkin Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1082060.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box chocolate cake mix*
4 eggs
3/4 cups sugar**
1/2 cup oil
1 cup pumpkin
1/4 cup water
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Mix all ingredients until blended
Pour into greased and floured bundt pan
Bake for 35 - 45 minutes***

Submitter: 
Dianna Jacobs-Fresh
Description: 
It's so simple to make and oh so good the longer it sits the better it gets.
