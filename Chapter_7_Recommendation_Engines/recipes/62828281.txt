Title:
Taco Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp oil
1 C diced frozen onion, thawed
1 lb ground turkey
1 packet taco seasoning
1 C water
1 can black beans, drained & rinsed
1 C whole frozen corn, thawed
2 TBS salsa
for topping (optional):
shredded cheddar cheese
salsa
sour cream
tortilla chips

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet, sautee onions. Add ground turkey and brown and drain. Meanwhile mix taco seasoning and water. Add seasoning, beans, and corn to skillet. Cook over medium heat until most of the liquid evaporates. Serve with optional toppings over crushed tortilla chips.
