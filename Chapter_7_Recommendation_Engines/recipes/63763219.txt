Title:
Teriyaki chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup low sodium soy sauce
1 cup dry white or red wine
3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
4 chicken breast

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and pour over chicken.
Let chicken marinate for at least an hour and up to 24 hours in refrigerator.
Pre-heat oven to 400F.
Bake/roast at 400F for 50 minutes, basting occasionally, or until internal temperature is 165F.
