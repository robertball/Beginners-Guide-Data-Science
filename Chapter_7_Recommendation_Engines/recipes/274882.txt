Title:
Berry-Banana Instant Soft Serve Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6864529.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups coarsely chopped fresh strawberries
2 scoops Emergen-C® Protein Fuel and Super Foods Mixed Berry
3 large ripe bananas, sliced and frozen

Directions:
Place strawberries and Emergen-C(R) Protein Fuel & Super Foods Mixed Berry in a blender or food processor. Process until pureed.
Add bananas and pulse until finely chopped. Blend until smooth. Serve immediately for soft-serve texture, or freeze for a few hours for a firmer consistency.

Submitter: 
Emergen-C®
Description: 
This fruit-based, dairy-free soft serve has just three ingredients, making it a breeze to whip up. With a frozen banana base, Emergen-C® Protein Fuel & Superfoods Mixed Berry, and strawberries, this cool treat is packed with protein and flavor.
