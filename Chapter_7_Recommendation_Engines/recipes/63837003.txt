Title:
Blackberry Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1066167.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking Powder
1 tsp. Baking Soda
1/2 C. Premium dry cocoa
1 C. Granulated sugar
3/4 C. Light cream
3 Eggs
1/2 C. Blackberry jam
Icing:
1 C. Premium chocolate, grated
1/2 C. Granulated sugar
1/4 C. Light cream
2 Tbsp. Cornstarch

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
Grease and flour a 13" by 9" by 2" cake rectangular cake pan. Set aside
Blend flour, b. powder, b. soda, cocoa and granulated sugar together in a small bowl. Set aside
Mix light cream, eggs and blackberry jam together in a large bowl with a wire whisk until light and frothy
Stir in dry ingredients and blend until mixture becomes silky and smooth
Pour batter into prepared cake pan and bake for 30 minutes until cake has risen in the middle sufficiently
Remove from oven and allow to cool before icing the cake
Icing:
In a small saucepan combine grated chocolate and granulated sugar over medium heat until chocolate has melted
Add light cream and cornstarch to icing and stir until icing is of spreadable consistency
When cake has cooled apply icing and store in refrigerator for longer storage time.

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A chocolate lovers cake with a premium chocolate icing that makes this cake very light and moist to enjoy for a luncheon or a nice dessert for a dinner party with friends
