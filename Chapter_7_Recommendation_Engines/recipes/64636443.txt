Title:
Seven or more layered Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 loose cups of Romaine, bout two hearts
1 cup chopped cauliflower or mixed red peppers and english cucumbers
10 oz package baby frozen peas
1 pd of crispy bacon crumbled
2 cups grape tomatoes or 4 roma tomatoes
1/2 red onion
3 hard cooked eggs
10 oz Sharp cheddar cheese shredded
Dressing:
1 1/4 cups mayonaise
2 Tablespoons white sugar
2/3 cups grated parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 dried dill
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In bowl or pan layer
lettuce, onion, peas, cauliflower, dressing, bacon, tomatoes, cheese and hard boiled eggs.
Cover and chill in fridge a couple of hours.
