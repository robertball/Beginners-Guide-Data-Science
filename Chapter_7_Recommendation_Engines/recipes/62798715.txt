Title:
Cheesy Chicken with Ranch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup Ranch Dressing
1 cup crushed cheese flavored snack crackers
1/4 teaspoon Lawry's Seasoning
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast cut into chuncks.
1/8 cup olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.  Coat the bottom of 9 x 13 pan with olive oil.  Crush cheese crackers to make 1 cup.  Stir in Lawry's seasoning.  Dip chicken chuncks into ranch dressing and then coat with crackers.  Place in pan.  Cook at 400 for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Cynthia A. Blair
Description: 
I played with this recipe because I didn't have sour cream in the fridge. My kids really loved my version.  It's something they could make themselves too.Modified from: Cheesy Chicken
