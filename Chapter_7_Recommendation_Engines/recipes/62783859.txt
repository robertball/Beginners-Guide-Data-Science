Title:
Cinnamon Swirl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup almonds
1/3 cup cashews
1/3 cup walnuts
4 tbsp raisins
10 dates
pinch of cinnamon, allspice and nutmeg

Directions:
Heat up the dates in themicrowave for 30 seconds or so.
Blend all the ingredients in a food processor.
Place themixture on a tray covered with baking paper. Tightly pack everything and place in the fridge to cool.
When cool, remove from the fridge and cut into bar shapes.
