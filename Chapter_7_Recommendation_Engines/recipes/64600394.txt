Title:
Crazy Good, and Crazy Healthy Buttermilk Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. White Flour
3/4 C. Bob Mills Oat Flour
1/4 C. Bob Mills Teff Flour
2 Scoops Z Natural Foods Protein Powder
1 tsp. Celtic Salt
1 tsp. Baking Soda
2 tsp. Baking Powder
2 tsp. Raw Sugar
4 tbls. Shortening [melted] (Try Coconut Shortening!)
2 Eggs
1 pint Buttermilk
Pam Coconut Oil Spray

Directions:
Measure and melt your shortening.
Mix all wet ingredients along with salt.
Mix the rest of the dry ingredients.
Slowly combine [I use a hand mixer to really mix wet ingredients].
Whip out your pan and start frying!

Submitter: 
Jill Johnson
Description: 
For years my grandmother has made the best buttermilk pancakes of my life.. After decades of manipulating and cultivating the recipe for a healthier and just as amazing version of my favorite pancake, I give you my famous Buttermilk Pancakes.
This is a double recipe. I meal prep them for the week.
