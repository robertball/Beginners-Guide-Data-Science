Title:
CREAMED SPINACH Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2Tbsp. Water
10ozs. chopped frozen Spinach, thawed
1/2c. Cream Cesses, softened
1 to 2Tbsps. Milk
1/4tsp. Seasoned Salt
2Tbsps. cooked Bacon pieces crispy

Directions:
Heat water to a boil in medium saucepan. Add spinach; cover and cook for 2 to 3 minutes or until wilted. Drain well; set aside.
Add cream cheese, milk and salt to same saucepan; cook and stir over low heat until smooth. Stir in cooked spinach; heat through. Trandfer to a large bowl.
Serve hot and sprinkle with crispy bacon.
