Title:
Coconut Mocha Frappe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4553002.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup coconut milk (such as Silk)
1/2 cup cold coffee
1/4 cup shredded coconut, toasted
2 tbs chocolate syrup
1 cup coffee ice cubes
1 pkt choice sweetener, such as Truvia, optional

Directions:
Brew coffee, freeze 1 cup in ice cube tray
Place coconut in pan and toast, stirring constantly until lightly browned, 3-4 minutes
Add all ingredients in blender cup and blend until icy and frothy
Top with fat free whip cream, 1/2 tsp toasted coconut and chocolate syrup
Note: Any nut milk can be used

Submitter: 
Yoly
Description: 
Coconut, chocolate and cold coffee make a delicious cool me down drink. What more could you ask for in an ice cold coffee drink?
