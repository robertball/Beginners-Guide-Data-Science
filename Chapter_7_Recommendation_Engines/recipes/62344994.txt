Title:
Party Size Asian Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/665436.jpg
Ingredients:
2 12oz bags prepared, prewashed salad greens
1 6oz can sliced water chestnuts, drained
1 6oz can sliced bamboo shoots, drained
1 6 oz package fresh bean sprouts
8 oz shitake mushrooms (or any mushroom you prefer), sliced
1 red pepper, cut in strips
1 green pepper, cut in strips
1 yellow pepper, cut in strips
1 14 oz can mandarin oranges
1 14 oz can rice noodles (such as La Choy brand)
1 bottle prepared asian sesame dressing

Directions:
Add the ingredients to the bowl as listed for a pretty presentation.  Spinkle rice noodles and add dressing before tossing.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
This is one of my most requested salads at picnics, pot lucks, and holidays. Find your biggest salad bowl, it serves a crowd as a side.  Make it the night before if you like, but add the dressing and rice noodles just before serving.
