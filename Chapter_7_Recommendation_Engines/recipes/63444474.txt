Title:
Edmond's Green Goddess Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup chopped green onions- green and white parts (about 7)
1 cup fresh basil leaves
1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (about 2)
2 teaspoons chopped garlic (about 2 cloves)
2 teaspoons anchovy paste
2 teaspoons kosher salt
1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 cup sour cream

Directions:
Place all ingredients EXCEPT for sour cream in blender or food processor.   Blend until smooth.
Add the sour cream and process until just blended.

Submitter: 
Claudia
Description: 
This was my father's favorite dressing.  It is always a hit -- even for those who hate anchovies.  

Good as a dip to vegetables as well.
