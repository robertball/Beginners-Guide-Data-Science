Title:
Individual Frittatas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 eggs
1/2 lb. cooked and crumbled bacon (ham, sausage, etc.)
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (change to your preferred variety)
1 cup finely chopped cooked broccoli (tomatoes, mushrooms, etc.)
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Grease muffin cups with cooking spray.
Preheat oven to 400°.
Crack eggs into a bowl and beat with a whisk.  Salt and pepper to taste.
Layer bacon and broccoli into the muffin tin cups, filling each about 1/3 of the way full.
Pour egg over the top of each, leaving a bit of space at the top.
Sprinkle a pinch of shredded cheese on top of each cup.
Bake in the oven at 400° for approximately 15-20 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean or nearly clean.

Submitter: 
Amy Hendrix Borland
Description: 
Baked individual frittatas.  Feel free to change the ingredients according to your own preferences.
