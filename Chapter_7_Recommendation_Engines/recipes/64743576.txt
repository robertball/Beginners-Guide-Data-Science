Title:
BEST BUTTERCREAM FROSTING Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 CUP HALF & HALF
1 TSP VANILLA
3/4 CUP BUTTER (SOFT)
6 CUPS POWDERED SUGAR
1/8 TSP SALT
2 TBSP LIGHT CORN SYRUP

Directions:
BEAT BUTTER AT MEDIUM SPEED UNTIL CREAM. GRADUALLY ADD POWDERED SUGAR & SALT ALTERNATELY WITH HALF & HALF AND VANILLA. STIR IN CORN SYRUP1
