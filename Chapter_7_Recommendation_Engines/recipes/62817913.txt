Title:
Fajitas like Chili's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/696708.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup lime juice
9 TBSP Olive Oil
12 Cloves Crushed Garlic
3 TBSP Soy Sauce
1 TBSP Salt
1 1/2 tsp liquid smoke
1 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
3/4 tsp black pepper
3 lb. boneless skinless chicken breast or skirt steak
6 TBSP water
1 1/2 tsp lime juice
3 dash salt
3 dash black pepper
3 large Spanish onion, sliced thin
1 1/2 green bell pepper, seeded, sliced thin
1 1/2 red bell pepper, seeded, sliced thin
1 1/2 yellow bell pepper, seeded, sliced thin

Directions:
-Combine first 8 ingredients in a sealable plastic container, add chicken/steak to container, cover, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight (preferred)
-Combine water, 1/12 tsp lime juice and dash of salt and pepper in bowl, set aside.
-Grill meat over medium high flame fro 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
-Cut meat into thin strips; set aside and keep warm.
-In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook onion and peppers in olive oil until brown; remove from heat; pour reserved liquid mixture over onions and peppers.
-Toss together meat, onions, and peppers.
