Title:
Secret Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground beef
1/2 lb italian sausage
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 large onion, quartered
4 carrots, peeled & quartered
1 T olive oil
2 T dried oregano
1 T dried basil
1 t pepper
1 t cinnamon
2 T brown sugar
1 30 oz can crushed tomatoes
1 30 oz can tomato sauce

Directions:
Scramble fry ground beef and Italian sausage; drain excess fat.
Meanwhile, chop onion and carrots in food processor.
Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat.
Cook veggies with garlic until onion is transparent, about 5 minutes.
Add meat and remaining ingredients to saucepan.
Simmer 1/2 hour, stirring occasionally.
Add salt to taste.

Submitter: 
Lisa H
Description: 
This spaghetti sauce is secret because it has loads of onions and carrots hidden in it.  My husband and kids love it - I quadruple the recipe every time and freeze the extra in zip-lock bags.
