Title:
Bison Short Ribs Braised in Cabernet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 bison short ribs (3-4 x 2" bones per person.
1 cup canola oil
2 Tbsp salt
2 Tbsp chile powder
2 Tbsp cumin
1 Tbsp black pepper
2 large onion, coarse chopped
3 red peppers (or 9 stalks celery), coarse chopped
9 medium carrots, coarse chopped
3 cloves garlic
9 ripe tomatoes, coarsely chopped (or 2 cans tomato paste)
2 bottles cabernet sauvignon wine
6 bay leaves

Directions:
In a medium pan, slowly boil tomatoes to reduce liquid and simulate tomato paste
Combine spices, seasonings, oil and ribs
In a large oven-proof pot, sear the ribs on all sides.  Do this in batches to ensure browning of the meat.
Remove meat from pot.
Add onions, peppers/celery, garlic and carrots.  Cook for 5 minutes to partially deglaze bottom of pot.
Add tomatoes/paste
If pot is not deglazed, add a small amount of wine to complete deglazing.
Add ribs back to the pot.
Add sufficient red wine to almost cover ribs.
Add bay leaves
Cover pot and put in 175F oven for at least 8 hours or overnight.
20 minutes before serving, strain off 4 cups of liquid and place in a sauce pan
Simmer the strained liquid until reduced by half for sauce.
Plate ribs and pour about 1/8 cup sauce over ribs.
