Title:
Easy Butternut Squash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large or 2 small butternut squash, peeled, seeded and chopped into 2 inch squares
1 large onion, chopped
1 1/2 cup chopped carrots
1/4 cup olive oil
6 chicken bouillon cubes
6 cups water
1 t. Salt
1 t. Pepper

Directions:
Add the squash, onion, carrots and olive oil to a pot and stir over medium high heat for 5 minutes, or until the veggies start to tenderize.
Add the water, boullion cubes, salt and pepper and simmer for 40 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Blend with an immersion blender or in batches in a traditional blender.  Season with salt to taste.

Submitter: 
Kel Kel
Description: 
This recipe is a healthy and easy alternative to the delicious autumn favorite.  It makes a large batch which freezes well, and spices can be adjusted to taste.
