Title:
V's Apple Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/323109.jpg
Ingredients:
3/8 cup wheat flour
3/8 cup all-purpose white flour
1 cup bran flake cereal
1/2 cup white sugar
2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon table salt of your choice (I use fine-ground sea salt)
1 egg, beaten
1/4 cup vegetable or canola oil
1 cup applesauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Lightly grease or line 12 muffin cups.
Combine dry ingredients. In a seperate bowl, combine wet ingredients. Mix together until just moistened.
Fill muffin cups 2/3 full. Bake at 400 F for 15 minutes.
Cool 5 minutes on wire rack & serve warm.

Submitter: 
V's Kitchen
Description: 
These bran muffins use applesauce and wheat flour to create a wonderful healthy muffin. Easy to customize to anyone's flavor favorites!
