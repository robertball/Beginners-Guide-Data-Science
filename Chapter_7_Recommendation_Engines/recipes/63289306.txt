Title:
Tami's Mexican Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 boneless, skinless chicken breast
3 C water (to cover chicken)
2 Tbl garlic powder
2 Tbl chili
1 Tbl onion powder
2 tsp cumin powder
2 tsp smoked paprika
2 tsp ground chipotle pepper
2 tsp salt
6 small new potatoes, cut in quarters
2 C fresh green or yellow beans, cut in halves
2 green peppers, seeded, chopped
1 1/2 C tomato sauce
2 green onions, diced, white parts separated
2 avocado, diced and mixed with 1 Tbl lime/lemon juice
1/2 C shredded tex-mex cheese
1/2 c sour cream

Directions:
In a large pot add chicken, water to cover and all spices from garlic to salt inclusive. Boil chicken on med-high heat for about 25 minutes, adding more water if it gets low.
Add potatoes, beans, peppers, white parts of green onions and then tomato sauce. Cook for an additional 10 minutes or until potatoes and beans are tender. Take chicken out and shred with fork. Add chicken back to pot. Ladle in to bowls. Sprinkle each bowl with cheese, avocado, dallop of sour cream and green onion.
