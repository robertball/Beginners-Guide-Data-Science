Title:
Veggie Puree Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon vegetable oil
2 carrots, peeled and chopped
2 sliced leeks, whites only
2 potatoes, peeled and chopped
2 parsnips, peeled and chopped
boiling water
4 oz baby food green beans, optional

Directions:
Heat oil in a deep saucepan. Add carrots and leeks and saute until softened, about 6-7 minutes.
Add potatoes and parsnips. Pour in boiling water until it just covers the veggies. Cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until veggies are very tender.
Puree using immersion blender, food processor, or blender and stir in baby food green beans, if desired.
