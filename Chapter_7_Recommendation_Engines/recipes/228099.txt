Title:
Paleo Friendly Banana Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4579869.jpg
Ingredients:
2 bananas, peeled and frozen
1 teaspoon vanilla extract, or more to taste
1/4 cup coconut milk, or more as needed

Directions:
Puree bananas and vanilla extract in a blender until smooth. Add coconut milk gradually until desired consistency is reached.

Submitter: 
Michelle Cooley Davis
Description: 
No sugar, all natural, and yummy! Add pecans, walnuts, almonds, mint leaves, or cocoa powder if you'd like!
