Title:
Philly Cheesesteak Sandwich with Garlic Mayo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 cups mayonnaise
2 tablespoons garlic powder
1 Cup vegetable oil
1 case Philly steak
37 green bell peppers, cut into 1/4 inch strips
37 onions, sliced into rings
75 Hotdog buns
1 Bag shredded mozzarella cheese
1 container dried oregano

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine mayonnaise and garlic. Cover, and refrigerate. Preheat oven to 500 degrees F (260 degrees C).
Heat oil and grill on flattop over medium heat. Saute beef until lightly browned. Stir in green pepper and onion, and season with salt salt free seasoning to taste. Saute until vegetables are tender, and remove from heat.
Spread each bun generously with garlic mayonnaise. Divide beef mixture into the buns. Top with shredded cheese, and sprinkle with oregano. Place sandwiches on a baking pan.
Heat sandwiches in preheated oven, until cheese is melted or slightly browned.
