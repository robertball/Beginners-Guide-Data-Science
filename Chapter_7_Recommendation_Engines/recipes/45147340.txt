Title:
Chicken and Broccoli Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 (16 ounce) package linguine
1 cup fresh or frozen broccoli flowerets
2 tablespoons butter
1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into cubes
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell's Cream of Chicken
1/2 cup mozzerella
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup grated Mozzerella cheese
Garlic and Italian seasoning to taste
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Cook linguine according to package directions. Add broccoli for last 4 minutes of cooking time. Drain.
Heat butter in skillet. Cook chicken until browned, stirring often.
Add soup, milk, cheeses, spices to linguine mixture and heat through. Serve with additional Parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Sheila Marino
Description: 
Linguini is mixed with pieces of tender chicken and broccoli flowerets and coated with a rich, satiny Alfredo sauce featuring Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup to make a quick and fabulous dish.Modified from: Chicken and Broccoli Alfredo
