Title:
Italian Onion Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cucumbers, cubed
1 red onion, diced
1 green bell pepper, diced
2 roma tomatoes, cubed
1 stalk celery, diced
1 tablespoon sea salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 (8 ounce) bottle zesty Italian-style salad dressing

Directions:
Combine cucumbers, red onion, bell pepper, tomatoes and celery in a large bowl; season with sea salt and black pepper. Pour dressing over cucumber mixture.
Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 1 hour.

Submitter:

Description: 
Use prepared zesty Italian-style salad dressing to marinate a simple salad of cucumber, onion, celery, roma tomatoes and bell pepper.Modified from: Italian Onion Cucumber Salad
