Title:
Fiesta Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1345967.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed nacho cheese soup (such as Campbell's® Fiesta Nacho Cheese)
1 (10 ounce) can refried beans
1 cup sour cream
1 (8 ounce) jar chunky-style salsa (such as Pace®)

Directions:
Stir nacho cheese soup, refried beans, sour cream, and salsa together in a small saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir until smooth and warm, about 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Alewis
Description: 
Warm and kickin' bean dip. Great for parties. Can be served in a slow cooker or dish. Serve with chips. Also makes a great topping for burritos.
