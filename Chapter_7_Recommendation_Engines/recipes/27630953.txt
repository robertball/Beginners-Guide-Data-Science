Title:
Grilled Lemon Garlic Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lb. red potatoes halves
1/4 cup olive oil, divided
1 TBS. grated lemon rind
1/4 cup lemon juice
2 garlic cloves, pressed
1-1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. coarsley gorund pepper
1/3 cup chopped fresh parsley
Veggie cooking spray

Directions:
Bring potatoes and water to cover to a boil in a large Dutch oven over medium heat; cook 20-25 minutes or until tender. Drain and toss with 2TBS. olive oil.
Stir together lemon rind, next 5 ingredients, and remaining 2TBS. olive oil.
Coat a cold cooking grate with cooking spray, and place on grill over medium-high heat (350-400). Place potatoes on cooking grate, and grill, covered with grill lid, 5 minutes, turning occasionally. Gegntly tosshot potatoes with lemon mixture. Serve immediately or at room temperature.
