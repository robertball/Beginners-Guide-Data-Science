Title:
Sunday Dinner Sponge Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup white sugar plus sugar for dusting pan
4 eggs
1 1/3 cup milk
2 tablespoons butter
2 teaspoons baking powder
2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease tube pan and dust with sugar. Sift together flour and baking powder. Heat milk and butter in a small saucepan until just boiling, then remove from heat and set aside.
Beat eggs until foamy, then add sugar. Continue beating on medium-high speed until thick and lemon colored. Add vanilla, reduce mixer speed to medium and add hot milk/butter in a steady stream. Quickly beat in flour mixture.
Pour into a 10 inch tube pan. Bake in preheated oven 45 minutes, or until top springs back when lightly tapped.
