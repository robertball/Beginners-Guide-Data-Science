Title:
Lemony Sauteed Shrimp with Broccoli and Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tbsp. butter
4 scallions, white and green parts separated - thinly sliced
1 tbsp. lemon zest
5-6 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1 lb. broccoli cut into small florets with stalks peeled and sliced
1 lb. large shrimp
1 10oz. pkg. frozen peas

Directions:
In a large skillet, melt 2 tbsp. butter.
Add white part of scallions, zest, broccoli and 1/2 cup water.
Season with salt and pepper.
Cover and cook until broccoli is bright green and just beginning to soften - about 3 or 4 min.
Add shrimp and peas.
Cover and cook, tossing occasionally, until shrimp are opaque and peas are tender, about 4-6 minutes.
Add lemon juice, green part of scallions and remaining butter.
Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Toss gently and serve.
