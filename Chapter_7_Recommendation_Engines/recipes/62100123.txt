Title:
Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/332614.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Olive Oil
2 thinly sliced garlic cloves.
1 small onion chopped
2 tbs. italian seasoning
1/4 cup Fresh Parsley
1/4 cup Green Pepper (optional)
1/4 cup Red Pepper (optional)
1/2 cup chopped Baby Bella mushrooms (optional)
1/2 cup chopped Sundried Tomatoes.
1 large can (about 30-45oz.) Tomato Sauce
1 large can (about 30-45oz.) diced Tomatoes
1 empty large can filled with water.
1/2-1 small can of Tomato Paste
1 ts. salt
1 ts. Pepper
1 Bag of your favorite Pasta

Directions:
Use a Large Pot
Heat olive oil
Mix in garlic,onion,italian seasoning,Parsley,green pepper,red pepper,baby bella mushrooms and sundried tomatoes.
Let ingredients start to heat up..aprox. 5 min.
Then add in tomato sauce, diced tomatoes,tomato paste, and 1 large can of water (use empty tomato sauce can)
Let come to a boil
Reduce heat and let sauce cook slowly while stirring occasionally.
Heat should be alittle more than simmer.
You'll know when sauce is done due to it's thickness. add salt and pepper to taste.

Description: 
A beautiful thick red italian gravy just bursting with flavor. The fresh ingredients compliment eachother sending you for seconds. Only takes 20 minutes to prepare...just let the sauce cook itself...In the mean time sit back and enjoy a nice bottle of your favorite wine while your home is filled with this authentic Italian aroma
