Title:
Chicken and green chile crock pot soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 chicken breasts cooked and cubed
2 onions
1 green pepper
3 jalepenos ( optional)
2 cans of chopped green chile
1 can of green enchilada sauce
1 can of chick peas
1 can of red kidney beans
1 can of sliced green beans
1 can of rotel tomatoes
1 carton of chicken stock
1 bunch of fresh cilantro chopped
2 tsp ground cumin
2 cloves of garlic
salt and pepper

Directions:
1. Saute the onions and garlic until soft and starting to brown, transfer to the crockpot
2. Add all the other ingredients to the crock pot
3.  on High for 1 hour and then reduce to low for 3-4 hours to let the flavors develope
4. Serve garnished with cheese, sour cream and tortilla chips

Submitter: 
Sarah Deaver
Description: 
Warming and filling soup spiced with cumin and cilantro. Very easy to make. Great served with grated cheese, sour cream and tortilla chips. to minimize salt use beans/ stock that is reduced sodium
