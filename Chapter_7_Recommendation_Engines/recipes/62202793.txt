Title:
Chicken and Corn Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/308351.jpg
Ingredients:
2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 can diced tomatoes
1 can tomatoe sauce
1 can green chiles
1 small onion chopped
1 package McCormicks chili mix
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon chili powder
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
1/2 package frozen corn
1 (15 ounce) can black beans

Directions:
Season chicken with garlic salt and sear in pan - place chicken in crock pot with remaining ingredients. Cook 6 to 8 hours on Low setting.
About 3 to 4 hours before you want to eat, shred the chicken with 2 forks. Return the meat to the pot, and continue cooking.
Stir the corn and the black beans into the slow cooker. Simmer until ready to serve.

Description: 
Use a slow cooker to make this chili with chicken, pinto beans, and corn, flavored with salsa, cumin, and chili powder.Modified from: Chicken and Corn Chili
