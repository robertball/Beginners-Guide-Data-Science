Title:
Chicken Parmesan Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons Olive Oil
3 cloves of Garlic
Red pepper Flakes
2 pounds chicken tenders
3 to 4 cups marinara sauce
1/3 to 1/2 cup cup red wine
8 to 10 ounces mozzarella cheese
4 to 6 ounces grated Parmesan cheese
5 to 6 ounces of partially crushed garlic croutons

Directions:
Add olive oil and garlic to 9 x 13 casserole dish and sprinkle with crushed red pepper. Place chicken tenders on top. Mix the sauce with the red wine and pour over chicken. Add 1/2 the mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top of sauce. Top with croutons and add remaining mozzarella and Parmesan on top. Bake at 350 degrees covered with foil for 30 minutes, uncover and bake for another 30 minutes. Serve over angle hair pasta.

Submitter: 
Brenda Brown
Description: 
This is a tasty low fat recipe for a Casserole style Chicken Parmesan. The best part is that it is fast and easy to prepare. Serve over your favorite pasta!
