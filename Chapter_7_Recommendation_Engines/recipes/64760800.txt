Title:
Crab Apple Liquor Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
crab apples
sugar
vodka

Directions:
Fill 2 qt jar with quartered crab apples
Fill 1st half with apples, then 1 cup sugar
Fill rest of jar with apples, then add another cup sugar
Fill with vodka
Seal
Turn for 90 days
