Title:
Best Lemon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust
1 3/4 c flour
2 sticks unsalted butter, cold
1/2 c sifted powdered sugar
Filling
1 3/4 c sugar
4 eggs
6 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
4 Tbsp flour
Topping
1/2 c powdered sugar sifted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Crust:  Using pastry cutter, blend flour and butter to pea size lumps.  Add powdered sugar and blend.
Crust: Press firmly into a 9x13 glass pan.  Bake for 15 minutes until edges are very light golden.
Filling: Mix sugar and eggs with a fork.  Do not overmix.  Add lemon juice and flour, mix well with fork.
Pour filling over the hot crust and return to oven for 25 to 30 minutes.
Sift powdered sugar over lemon bars while still hot.
Cool ten minutes, then cut into small squares with a sharp knife.
