Title:
Egg nog cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Yellow cake mix
2 eggs
1/4 cup melted butter
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp. rum extract
Filling:
1 package instant vanilla pudding
2 cups egg nog
1/2 tsp. rum extract
Topping:
2 cups whipped cream
1 1/4 cups sugar
dash of salt
1/2 tsp rum extract
1 T. unflovored gelatin
2 T. cold water
Or
3-4 cups cool whip
1/2 tsp. rum extract

Directions:
Mix cake mix, eggs, 1 1/2-2 cups egg nog, butter, nutmeg, and 1/2 tsp. rum extract together for four minutes.  Pour into 2 round cake pans or one 9 by 13 pan(use a deeper one if you have one).  Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees.
Make filling:  Mix pudding, 2 cups egg nog and 1/2 tsp. rum extract.  Put in refrigerator to firm up.
Make topping:
Dissolve gelatin in water, whip cream and add other ingredients as you whip the cream.  Or, you can use mix the cool whip and rum extract together.  Put in refrigerator till ready to assemble cake.
Assemble the cake:
Cover 1st layer of cake with  1/2 of filling.  Top with second layer and cover with the rest of the filling.  Cover all with whipped cream mixture or cool whip mixture.  If you are using a 9 by 13 pan, cover whole cake with filling and then cover with whipped cream mixture.  Sprinkle with nutmeg or red/green sugar mixture if you desire.  Keep in refrigerator.
