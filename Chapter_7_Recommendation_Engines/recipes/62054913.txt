Title:
best banana cake ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
1 yellow cake mix
1 c. sugar
3/4 c oil
3 bananas, mashed
1 c. chopped pecans
4 eggs

Directions:
Directions
Mix all ingredients together. Bake in 13 x 9 greased cake pan for about 50 minutes to 1 hour at 350 degrees.
After cake cools, frost with cream cheese frosting (store bought works well)
