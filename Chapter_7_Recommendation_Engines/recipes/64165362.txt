Title:
Cake Mix Upsizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C AP Flour
1 C Sugar
2 tsp Baking Powder
1/4 tsp Baking Soda

Directions:
Whisk all and store in mason jar. Whisk before using and level with a knife.
15 1/4 oz cake mix-8 Tbsp + 1 tsp extender(or 3 oz).
16 1/2 oz cake mix -5 Tbsp extender(or 1 3/4 oz).

Submitter:

Description: 
Mix to extend the smaller cake mix sizes to 18.25 oz
