Title:
Slow cooker chicken chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/797421.jpg
Ingredients:
3-4 Medium boneless chicken breast
2 14oz. cans diced tomatoes
1 10oz. can tomatoes and green chilies
1 7oz. can diced green chilies
1 15oz. can tomato sauce
1 can red kidney beans un-drained
1 can black beans un-drained
1 green bell pepper chopped
1 yellow or red bell pepper chopped
1 poblano or Anaheim pepper chopped
1-2 jalape√±o peppers chopped
3-5 cloves of garlic chopped
1 lg.onion chopped
2 stalks celery diced
1 12 oz. can favorite beer
2 teaspoons of  ground cumin
2 tablespoons ground chili pepper
2 teaspoons dried parsley or 2 tablespoons of fresh parsley chopped
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon cocoa powder
2 teaspoons of paprika
2 teaspoons of ground black pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients into a slow cooker except chicken raw chicken breast and beer. Add enough beer to reach the thickness you desire, remember it will get soupier as vegetables cook. Press chicken breast into the chili and turn on low for 8 hours, take chicken out and shred with forks and place back in for at least another hour.
