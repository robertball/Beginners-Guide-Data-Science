Title:
Big Ray's Rhubarb Relish Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1016599.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups finely chopped fresh or frozen rhubarb
2 cups strawberries (or whatever berries you have on hand)
2 1/4 cups packed brown sugar
1/2 cup vinegar
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground allspice
1/4 tsp ground cloves
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp cornstarch(optional)

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine all ingredients and cook over medium heat for 30 minutes or until thickened, stirring occasionally.  To thicken quicker, add the cornstarch.  Cool and store in refrigerator.
