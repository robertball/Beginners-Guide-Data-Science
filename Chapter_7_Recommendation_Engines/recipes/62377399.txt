Title:
Hamburger Hotdish!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/416629.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound hamburger
1 Quart Canned Tomatoes or 1 Large Can of Hunt's Stewed Tomatoes
1 med. onion (finely chopped)
1/8 to 1/4 cup water
1/2 to 3/4 cup ketchup
5 Tbsp. butter
1/2 tsp. Worchestire Sauce
salt & pepper to taste
1/2 tsp. oregano
Egg noodles or elbow macaroni

Directions:
Brown 1 pound hamburger with the finely chopped onion. Drain. Add meat mixture to large saucepan. Add tomatoes, water, ketchup, butter, worchestire, salt & pepper, and oregano. Heat on Medium for 10 minutes - (while noodles cook).
In seperate pan cook your egg noodles or elbow macaroni. Follow label directions. Drain noodles.
Mix cooked noodles into sauce mixture and gently fold together. Serve immediately.

Description: 
This is my mother's recipe that she gave to me and I am sharing it with you!! It is fabulous! This is very easy to make and is easily modified by the cook! Quick 25 minute recipe and dinner is served!!
