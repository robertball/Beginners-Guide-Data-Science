Title:
Savory Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 lb. chuck roast
1 med. onion
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/4 can water
1/4 c. brown sugar
1/4 c. vinegar
1 T. Worcestershire sauce
1 T. mustard
salt and pepper

Directions:
Pre-heat oven at 300F
Combine soup with water, brown sugar, vinegar, worcestershire sauce, mustard salt and pepper. Whisk together in a bowl and set aside.
Salt and pepper roast.
In a large skillet sear roast until browned on both sides. About five minutes.
place roast in a large roasting pan.Add your favorite vegetables if you wish
Pour soup mixture over roast and vegetables. I found that this makes amazing mashed potatoes.
Bake for 4 hours. You may also make this in the Crock-Pot
