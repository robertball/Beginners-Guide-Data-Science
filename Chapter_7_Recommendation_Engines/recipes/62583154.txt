Title:
Texas Caviar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup cider vinegar
1/2 cup oil
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1 Tbsp. water
1 can white shoe peg corn (drained)
1 can black beans (drained & rinsed)
1 can black eyed peas (drained & rinsed)
1 can pinto beans (drained & rinsed)
1 4oz. can green chilies
1 4oz. jar chopped pimentos (drained)
1 small onion (chopped)
1 cup celery (chopped)
2 cup red and green peppers (chopped)

Directions:
In a saucepan, stir together the first 6 ingredients and bring to a boil.  Remove from heat and let cool.
While cooling, mix together the last 9 ingredients in a large bowl.
Add cooled liquid mixture into the bowl of beans and veggies.  Stir and refrigerate overnight.  Drain before serving. Serve with tortilla scoops. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jessica Kathleen
Description: 
A simple version of the classic spicy Texas Caviar.  Sure to be a hit at any get-together!  Bonus: safe for all tastebuds that can't handle heat :-)
