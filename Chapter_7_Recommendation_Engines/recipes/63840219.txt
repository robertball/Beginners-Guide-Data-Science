Title:
Taco Surprise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound lean ground beef
4 ounces macaroni
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 cup salsa
1 cup diced tomatoes
1/2 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
2 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese
2 ounces shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1/2 cup crushed tortilla chips
1/4 cup sour cream (optional)
1/8 cup chopped green onions

Directions:
Day 1:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water until al dente.  Drain.
In a large skillet, cook and stir ground beef and chopped onion over medium heat until brown.
Mix in tomato soup, diced tomatoes, and taco seasoning mix.   Stir in pasta.
Spoon beef mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Bake for about 20 minutes
Day 2:
Sprinkle crumbled taco chips and grated cheese on top.
Bake for 15-20 minutes, until the cheese is melted.
Serve with chopped green onions and sour cream, if desired.

Submitter: 
Sandra Moore Dixon
Description: 
For a fun and flavorful baked fiesta, cook ground beef and chopped onions with tomato soup, diced tomatoes and taco seasoning mix. Add cooked macaroni and bake with a scrumptious topping of corn chips, Cheddar and Monterey Jack cheese!Modified from: Taco Casserole
