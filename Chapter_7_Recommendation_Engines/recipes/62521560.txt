Title:
Garlic & Rosemary Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4-1/2 oz sliced prosciutto, coarsely chopped
1-2 garlic cloves, peeled and minced or pressed
1 1/2 ts. chopped fresh or ½ tsp dried rosemary leaves (set aside 4-6 sprigs for garnish)
1 lb slender green beans, ends removed
salt and pepper

Directions:
1.   In 10-12" pan, sauté prosciutto over med-high heat until crisp, about 1 min. Remove from pan with slotted spoon & set aside.
2. Add garlic, chopped rosemary and 2 tbsp water to pan. Sauté until garlic is fragrant, about 30 sec; do not scorch.
3. Add beans and 1/3 c water & sprinkle with salt. Cover & cook just until beans are tender to bite, about 3 min. Uncover & sauté until liquid has evaporated.
4. Arrange beans on platter, sprinkle with prosciutto & garnish with rosemary sprigs. Season to taste with salt & pepper.
