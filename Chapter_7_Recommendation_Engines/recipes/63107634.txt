Title:
Vitamix Crustless Spinach Quiche with meat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Spray dish w/olive oil (or reserved drippings;)
1 lb. spicy pork sausage or breakfast sausage
8 jumbo eggs or 10-12 smaller
1 small onion
1/2 red pepper
1/2 green pepper
4 c. fresh spinach
1.5 c. (+ more for sprinkling on top if you like) sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 ripe Roma tomatoes, sliced and seeded

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease any oven safe, glass or ceramic casserole or pie dish.
In VitaMix pitcher, crack eggs, and mix them on high for 30sec .   Add salt and pepper, onion and colored peppers. Mix on variable 1/2 speed for 20 secs or so, until chopped fine.   Add spinach and cheese and mix variable 1/2 speed 10 secs or so, until chopped to your liking. Pour into prepared pie dish. Top w/browned sausage and it will sink to even distribution, hopefully.  Top w/ sliced roma tomatoes and extra cheese or garnish you like.
bake 350 for 20-30 mins or so, until puffed, browned and eggs have set. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Jamie Goebel Eavou

Throw it all in VitaMix, after sausage is browned.  An easy prep spinach quiche with meat, eggs, onion, peppers and cheese.Modified from: Crustless Spinach Quiche
