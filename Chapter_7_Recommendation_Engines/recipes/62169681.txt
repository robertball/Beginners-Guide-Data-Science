Title:
MIXED VEGETABLE SAAGU Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ kg mixed vegetables ( you can use potatoes, french beans, green peas, carrots)
½ teaspoon Turmeric (haldi ) powder
1 teaspoon mustard seeds
2 dry red chillis
1 pinch asafoetida powder (hing)
1 sprig curry leaves (kadipatta)
1 teaspoon Tamarind paste
Salt to taste
To grind:
1 cinnamon stick (dalcini)
1 teaspoon cumin seeds (jeera)
1 teaspoon black peppercorns
3 cloves (lavang)
1 teaspoon coriander seeds (dhania)
2 tablespoons Dalia (Chutney Ka Chana)
3 tablespoons dessicated coconut
1 tablespoon Poppyseeds (Khus Khus)

Directions:
Cut vegetables into even size dice. Boil in water with salt and turmeric powder. Use as little water as you can since we will be using this water.
Take all the ingredients for grinding and grind into a fine paste using a little water.
Add 2 tablespoons oil in a frying pan. Add mustard seeds, red chillis, asafoetida, and curry leaves. Now add the cooked vegetables along with the cooking liquid. Add the ground masala paste. Add tamarind and more salt if needed. Cook the saagu on low heat for 5 minutes. Serve hot.

Submitter: 
SUSMITA
Description: 
I learned this recipe from my mother-in-law. I love the kick from the fresh ground spices. Saagu is a classic Kannada preparation and is eaten with poori, dosa, chapati, and sometimes idli too. I like it best with poori.
