Title:
Raw Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 organic Celery root
1/4 cups raw organic pecans
1 small clove garlic, minced
2 tsp organic black pepper
1 Tbs Nama Shoyu or Braggs aminos
1 Tbs extra virgin olive oil
1 head organic broccoli, chopped
Raw sesame seeds and sea salt to taste

Directions:
Wash, peel, and grate the celery root.
In a food processer, process pecans and garlic until ground.
Add nut mixture in a bowl with grated celery root.
Mix in the remaining ingredients.

Submitter: 
Raw_Angel
Description: 
This dish can be heated in a dehydrator if you want warmer 'un-cooked' fried rice.
