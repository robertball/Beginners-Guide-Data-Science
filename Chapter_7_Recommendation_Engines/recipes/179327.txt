Title:
Hurricane Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982606.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 ounce Captain Morgan Original Spiced Rum
3/4 ounce dark rum
3 ounces passion fruit
3 ounces mango juice
2 ounces sweet and sour mix
1 wheel lime

Directions:
Add Captain Morgan Original Spiced Rum, dark rum, passion fruit or mango juice, and sweet and sour mix.
Shake and pour into crushed ice-filled hurricane glass or other specialty glass.
Garnish with lime wheel.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
The Hurricane uses a mix of Captain Morgan Original Spiced Rum with dark rum, and adds passion fruit and mango juices for extra tropical flavor.
