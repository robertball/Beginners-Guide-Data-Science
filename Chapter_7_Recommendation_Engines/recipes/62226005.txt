Title:
Great-Grandma's Chocolate Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C sugar
1 tbs flour
2 tbs cocoa
1/2 C milk
1/4 C butter
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Put all ingredients into a saucepan, cook on med and stir until boiling; reduce heat and simmer 15-20 min stirring very frequently.
