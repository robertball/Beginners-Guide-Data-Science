Title:
Double Chocolate Gooey Butter Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chocolate cake mix
3 eggs
1 c butter
1 8-oz pkg cream cheese
3-4 tbsp cocoa powder
1 16-oz pkg powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 c. chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.  Butter a 9X13 pan.
Combine cake mix, 1 egg, 1/2 c of melted butter.
Stir until blended.
Pat into pan and set aside.
Beat chream cheese until smooth, add remaining 2 eggs and cocoa pwd and beat.
Reduce speed to low and add sugar till well combined.
Slowly add remaining butter and vanilla.
Beat until smooth.
Spread over batter in pan.
Bake 40-50 minutes.  Do not overcook - the center should be a little gooey.
Let cool partially before slicing and serve with whipped cream or ice cream.
