Title:
Praline Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 15 oz can pumpkin
3 eggs slightly beaten
1/2 cup brown sugar
2/3 cup maple/praline syrup
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ginger
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1&1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1&1/4 cup evaporated milk
1 - 9 inch pie shell

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Add ingredients to large bowl in the order listed stirring well after each addition.  Pour filling into pie shell and bake for 1 hour or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Graden
Description: 
It's the Maple/Praline syrup that makes this pie stand out from the others.  After trying a dozen recipes, this one surpasses all the others in flavor.
