Title:
Chocolate Toffee Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Devil's food cake mix w/ pudding in the mix
3 (3 oz.each) boxes instant pudding
6 Heath bars or equivilent amount toffee chips
1 quart whipping cream
3/4 cups Kahula
2 1/2 cups + 2 Tablespoons milk
1 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
Prepare cake according to package.  Bake in a 9x13 pan.  Cool.
Prepare chocolate puddings with milk and Kahlua
Place Heath bars in plastic, seal and crush with the side of a hammer.
Cut cool cake into 1 1/2 inch cubes.
Whip cream, adding powdered sugar before stiff peaks form.
In a trifle bowl or a large clear bowl, layer 1/2 of the cake, 1/2 of the pudding, 1/2 of the whipped cream and 1/2 of the toffee chips.
Repeat with remaining ingredients, ending with toffee chips.
Chill at lest 4 hours before serving.
