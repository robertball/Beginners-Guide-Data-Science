Title:
Summer Salad on a Stick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1328409.jpg
Ingredients:
8 honeydew melon balls
16 salami
24 baby arugula leaves
8 bamboo toothpicks

Directions:
Thread 1 melon ball, 2 slices salami, and 3 arugula leaves, respectively, onto a toothpick. Repeat with remaining toothpicks.
