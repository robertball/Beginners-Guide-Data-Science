Title:
POTATO LASAGNA Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/810375.jpg
Ingredients:
6-8  Raw potatoes,thinly sliced
1 onion,chopped
15 oz.can tomato sauce
1 cup mozzarella cheese,grated
2 lbs.ground beef
1 can cheddar soup
sprinkle of oregano

Directions:
Line 9x13 pan with 2 layers of potatoes.
Brown hamberger with onion and drain.Add soup and mix in,pour over potatoes.
Spread tomato sauce over mixture and sprinkle with oregano.
Cover with foil.Bake at 350 for 1 1/4 hr.or untill done.
Remove foil.Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and bake for 5 more minutes.

Submitter: 
koko
Description: 
Layers of thinly sliced potatoes with ground beef and cheddar and mozzarella cheeses.Topped with tomato sauce.
