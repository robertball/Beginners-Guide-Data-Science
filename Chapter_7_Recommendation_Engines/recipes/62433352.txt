Title:
Mrs. Brooks' Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. yeast
1 cup warm water
1 tsp. sugar
5 eggs
3/4 cup oil
2 tsp. salt
1 cup sugar
2 cups boiling water
2 cups cold milk
Enough flour until dough is not too sticky (about 11 1/2 cups)

Directions:
Combine 2 Tbsp. yeast, 1 cup warm water and 1 tsp. sugar in a small bowl (let sit for 10 minutes until frothy).
In mixer combine: eggs, oil, salt, sugar, boiling water, and cold milk.
Add yeast mixture and mix together.
Continue mixing and add enough flour until dough is not too sticky (do not exceed 11 1/2 cups).
Knead for a few minutes, then let rise until double in a greased bowl (1 1/2 hours).
Punch down and let double again (1 1/2 hours).
Make into buns and let rise (1 hour).
Bake at 325-350 for 20-25 minutes or until brown.
