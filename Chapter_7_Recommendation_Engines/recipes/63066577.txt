Title:
Easy Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup plain yogurt
1 TBL honey
1 TBL lemon juice
1 cup seedless grapes
1 can (11 oz) mandarin orange segments
1 can (8 1/4 oz) pineapple chunks in syrup
1 red apple, sliced
1 cup bite size salad greens

Directions:
Drain mandarin oranges and pineapple. Mix yogurt, honey and lemon juice in medium bowl. Stir in remaining ingredients.
