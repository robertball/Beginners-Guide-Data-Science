Title:
Irish Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb bacon, cut into 1 inch pieces
1 medium onion, chopped
1 cup celery, chopped
5-7 lbs potatoes, cleaned and cubed, peel is optional
2 cans evaporated milk
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large saucepan, cook bacon til crispy.  Set aside(hide it in the microwave, to take away tempation!).In left over grease, cook onion and celery, til translucent.  Pour off extra grease, leaving vegetables in the pan.  Add potatoes, then enough water to cover all but 1 inch of potatoes.  Boil for about 10 minutes, stirring often, til potatoes are very soft.  Add canned milk, and warm through.  Add bacon just before serving.

Submitter: 
Firewife42
Description: 
My Irish Great Grandmother fixed this regularly.  My kids know they will have it every Halloween, then all winter long.
