Title:
Easy Holiday Eggnog Shake  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4337752.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large banana
1/2 cup vanilla frozen yogurt
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup low-fat eggnog
4 large ice cubes
1 teaspoon malted milk powder (optional)

Directions:
Combine banana, frozen yogurt, milk, eggnog, ice cubes, and malted milk powder; blend until smooth.

Submitter:

Description: 
A twist on classic holiday eggnog that everyone will love, especially the kids!
