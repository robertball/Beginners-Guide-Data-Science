Title:
Complete Pancake Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/29425.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons baking powder
1 tablespoon salt
1 cup dry milk powder
1 cup shortening

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together flour, baking powder, salt and dry milk. Cut in shortening until mixture resembles coarse cornmeal.

Submitter: 
Kay King
Description: 
Here is a homemade dry pancake mix that can be used for many pancake recipes. It should be stored in the refrigerator in an airtight container. Combine 1 1/3 cup of dry mix with 3/4 cup water and 1 egg.
