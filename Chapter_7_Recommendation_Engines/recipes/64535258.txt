Title:
Shoo-Fly Cake aka Molasses Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups flour
¾ cup vegetable oil
2 cups light brown sugar packed
Save 1 cup of mixture for crumb topping
1 cup molasses
2 cups boiling water
1 tsp baking soda
¼ tsp salt

Directions:
Mix first three ingredients
Add molasses, boiling water, baking soda, and salt.
Pour mixture into prepared baking pan.
Sprinkle the cup of saved mixture for topping.
