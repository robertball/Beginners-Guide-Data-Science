Title:
Herb Roasted Chicken Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978663.jpg
Ingredients:
4 TysonÂ® Fresh Chicken Leg Quarters
4 small yellow potatoes, cut into halves
1 tablespoon butter, melted
2 teaspoons rosemary leaves, finely crushed
3/4 teaspoon thyme leaves
1/2 teaspoon garlic pepper seasoning
1/2 teaspoon oregano leaves

Directions:
. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Line broiler pan with foil. Spray broiler pan rack with nonstick cooking spray. Wash hands. Place chicken and potatoes on rack. Wash hands.
In small bowl, mix butter, rosemary, thyme, garlic pepper and oregano. Rub or brush mixture evenly on chicken and potatoes. Wash hands.
Roast chicken and potatoes 35 to 40 minutes or until potatoes are tender and chicken is done (internal temp 180 degrees F).

Tyson Foods
Description:
