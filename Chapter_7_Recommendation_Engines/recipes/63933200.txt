Title:
Big Ray's Crock Pot hot German Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1090451.jpg
Ingredients:
10-12  medium red potatoes, cut into 1/4 inch slices (peeled or unpeeled, which ever you prefer)
1  large onion, chopped
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
4 Tbsp all-purpose flour
4 Tbsp sugar
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp celery seed
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 lb cooked bacon, crumbled

Directions:
Mix potatoes and onion in crock pot; mix remaining ingredients except bacon; pour into crock pot.
Cover and cook on low setting 4 to 5 hrs or until potatoes are tender(watch carefully, the newer crock pots run hotter and depending on how thin you sliced the spuds it may be done sooner).
Stir in bacon.
