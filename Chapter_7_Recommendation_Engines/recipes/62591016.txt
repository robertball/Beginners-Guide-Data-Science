Title:
Chicken chimichangas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c. shredded cooked chicken
2/3 c. salsa
1/3 c. green onions
1 tsp. cumin
1 tsp. oregano
1/2  tsp. salt
1 pkg. tortillas (about 8)
butter
1 c. jack cheese
guacamole (can be made by smashing avocados and mixing with salsa and garlic salt; add fresh tomatoes if available)

Directions:
Combine chicken, salsa, onion, cumin, oregano and salt in a saucepan.  Simmer 5 minutes or until most of liquid has evaporated.
Brush one side of tortillas with butter.  Spoon about 1/3 c. chicken mixture onto center of unbuttered sides and top with 2 T. cheese.  Fold two sides over filling and fold ends down.
Place seam side down in 13 x 9-inch baking dish.  Bake in preheated oven at 475 for 13 minutes or until crisp and golden brown.  Top with guacamole and additional sauce to serve.
