Title:
General Tso's Chicken    Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup cornstarch
2 Tablespoons water
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 teaspoon minced ginger
1/3 cup sugar
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 Tablespoons white vinegar
1/4 cup sherry
1/2 teaspoon monosodium glutamate
2/3 cup chicken broth -- hot
1 1/2 pounds chicken thighs without skin -- boned and cubed
2 Tablespoons soy sauce
1/2 teaspoon white pepper
1 egg -- beaten
1/2 cup cornstarch
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 cup scallions -- diced
6 small dried chili pepper -- chopped

Directions:
-Mix cornstarch and water.
-Add garlic, ginger, soy sauce, vinegar, sherry, M.S.G., and chicken broth. Mix well.
-Refrigerate until needed.
-Mix chicken, soy sauce, white pepper, and egg.
-Add cornstarch until chicken is coated evenly.
-Add oil to help separate chicken pieces.
-Deep fry chicken in 350ยบ oil until crispy. Drain on paper towels.
-Place small amount of oil in wok and heat until wok is hot.
-Add scallions and peppers and stir-fry briefly.
-Pour refrigerated mixture and chicken into wok.
-Simmer and stir until sauce is thick.

Submitter: 
Robbie Rice
Description: 
Broccoli is a perfect accompaniment to this spicy Oriental dish.
