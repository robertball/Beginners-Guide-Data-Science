Title:
Not Another Chicken Casserole! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/563648.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups diced cooked chicken
2 cups cold cooked long grain rice
1/2 cup diced red pepper
1 can sliced mushrooms, drained
1 tsp chopped fresh rosemary
1 TBSP chopped fresh parsley
1 cup fresh broccoli florets, blanched and quick-cooled
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup sour cream
1 cup milk
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup shredded swiss cheese

Directions:
Preheat over to 350 degrees.
Combine first 7 ingredients in a 2.5 qt casserole dish.
In a medium bowl, combine soup, sour cream, and milk. Add salt and pepper to taste. Stir into ingredients in casserole. Stir in cheese.
Cover and bake for 30 minutes. Uncover and bake 20 minutes longer.

Submitter: 
Pam Ziegler Lutz
Description: 
I'd planned on using my cooked chicken in another recipe, but was missing a key ingredient. In a pinch, I threw together some ingredients I had on hand, added some leftover rice, and ended up with a tasty dinner!
