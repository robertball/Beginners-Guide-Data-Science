Title:
Spud Soup Extraordinaire Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 slices bacon
6 c milk
2/3 c margarine
2/3 c flour ( do NOT pack)
1 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp salt
4 green onions (thinly sliced)
4 large bakers potatos or 10 red potatos (cooked w/ skins)
1 c shredded cheese
sour cream (optional topping)

Directions:
Brown, drain & crumble bacon - set aside.
Melt margarine over low heat, whisk in flour until smooth. Gradually stir in milk, whisking until mixture becomes thick.
Cut cooked potato into cubes leaving skins on.
Stir in potato & onion. Bring to a boil, stir frequently. Reduce heat to a simmer.
Mix in 2/3 crumbled bacon, cheese, salt & pepper - keep on a low simmer for 20 min, stirring occasionaly.
Top with remaining bacon and a dolop of sour cream (optional)
Enjoy!
