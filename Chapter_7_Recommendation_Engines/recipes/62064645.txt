Title:
Enchalida Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg flour tortillas
1 lb lean ground beef
1 can refried beans
3 C shredded cheddar cheese
small onion finely chopped
chopped garlic clove
1/2 c chopped black olives
1 c sour cream
1/2 c salsa
1 pkg taco seasoning
1 sm can tomatoe paste

Directions:
brown ground beef and onion/garlic - fold in beans, olives - add seasoning and tomatoe paste and remove from heat
spray lasagna pan with cooking spray
line bottom with tortillas
spread with 1/3 meat mixture
top with 1/3 cheese
repeat tortillas, meat mixture and cheese until gone with cheese as top layer
bake 20 minutes at 350-degrees
cut like lasagna and serve wtih a spoonful of salsa and a dolup of sour cream

Submitter: 
Pam Rosterman
Description: 
This is a taste of mexico baked to bubbly perfection.  My husband and son can finish the whole thing off with no help!
