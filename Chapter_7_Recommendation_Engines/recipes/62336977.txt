Title:
Low Carb Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef, cooked and drained
1 onion diced
1-2 tomatoes diced or sliced per preference
1 package taco seasoning mix
pork rind crushed up
cheddar cheese shredded
Lettuce of choice (about 2 cups per serving)
ranch or other preferred dressing

Directions:
cook ground beef adding onion and taco seasoning mix and small amount of water as needed for desired consistency
to assemble per serving:
2 cups lettuce
2 oz meat mixture
1 oz shredded cheese
desired amt of onion / tomato
1 pork rind crused
2 tbs of desired dressing

Submitter: 
Rosemary Vandello
Description: 
taco salad using pork rind instead of Doritos
