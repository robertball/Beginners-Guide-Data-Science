Title:
Chocolate Martini Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3712826.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 fluid ounces chocolate liqueur
1 1/2 fluid ounces creme de cacao
1/2 fluid ounce vodka
2 1/2 fluid ounces half-and-half
1 cup ice

Directions:
Combine chocolate liqueur, creme de cacao, vodka, half-and-half, and ice in a cocktail shaker. Cover and shake until chilled. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

Submitter: 
Allrecipes
Description: 
This liquid dessert combines chocolate-flavored liqueurs with vodka and half-and-half for a boozy and decadent drink.
