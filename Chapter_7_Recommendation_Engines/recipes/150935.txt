Title:
Twisted Island Breeze (Parrot Bay Pineapple Wave Runner) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976110.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 ounces Captain Morgan Parrot Bay™ pineapple rum
2 ounces grapefruit juice
1 tablespoon cranberry juice
2 ounces pineapple juice
1 slice pineapple

Directions:
Pour Captain Morgan Parrot Bay(TM) pineapple, grapefruit juice, cranberry juice, and pineapple juice into an ice-filled highball glass and stir.
Garnish the cocktail with a slice of pineapple.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Add fruit flavor to your drinks without all the slicing by using Captain Morgan Parrot Bay™in Coconut, Mango, Pineapple or Passion Fruit.
