Title:
Greek-style pasta salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package bowtie pasta, cooked per package directions
1 can artichoke hearts, drained, cut into bit size pieces
1 can black olives, drained, slightly chopped
3-4 peperoncini peppers, chopped
3 roma tomatoes, seeded and cut into bite size pieces
1 cucumber, seeded and cut into bite size pieces
Crumbled feta cheese
2 tbs red wine vinegar
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp dried oregano
salt and pepper

Directions:
Cook pasta per package directions, rinse and cool.
Combine tomatoes, cucumbers, artichoke hearts, olives and peperoncini. Toss with pasta
In a separate bowl, combine vinegar, oil, garlic, oregano, salt and pepper.
Pour over pasta and vegetable mixture
Sprinkle with feta cheese
Chill and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Diamondgirl
Description: 
This is a light and delicious pasta salad that is a breeze to make. It makes a cool summer meal all by itself.
