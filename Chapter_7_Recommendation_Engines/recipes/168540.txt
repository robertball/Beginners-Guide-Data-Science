Title:
Frosty Strawberry Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982458.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk
8 strawberries (fresh or frozen)
1 scoop vanilla California ice cream

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender or food processor and blend until thick and smooth. Pour into tall glass and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
California Milk Advisory Board
Description: 
Milk, strawberries, and California ice cream are blended together for this quintessential summertime treat. Refreshing and fruity, this shake recipe can be made for one or increased for a crowd.
