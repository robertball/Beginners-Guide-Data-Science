Title:
Slow Cooker Indian Butter Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 lbs Boneless-skinless Chicken Breast
1 Onion, Diced
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 teaspoons curry powder
1 tablespoon curry paste
2 teaspoons tandoori masala
1 teaspoon garam masala
1 teaspoon cayenne powder
2 teaspoons chili powder
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1 tsp ground cardamom
1 cup low-fat plain yogurt
1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
salt to taste

Directions:
Dice 1 onion
Mince 4 cloves of garlic
Heat 2 tbsp butter and 2 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
Add garlic and onion to skillet
Cook and stir until the onion has softened and turned translucent, about 10 minutes.
Add chicken.
Stir in 1 tsp ground cardamom, 2 tsp curry powder, 1 tbsp curry paste, 2 tsp tandoori masala, 1 tsp garam masala, 1 tsp cayenne powder, 2 tsp chili powder and 6oz can of tomato paste.
Shake 1 can of coconut milk thoroughly and stir into skillet
Add 1 cup plain yogurt and stir to combine
Pour into a slow cooker, and salt to taste
Cook on High 4 to 6 hours, or on Low 6 to 8 hours until the chicken is tender and the sauce has reduced to your desired consistency.

Submitter: 
Ben Inouye
Description: 
Delicious and easy butter chicken in a crock pot.  Adjust cayenne to suit taste.
