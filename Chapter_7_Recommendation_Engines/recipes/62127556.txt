Title:
Great Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 4 medium Hass avocados, peeled, pitted and diced
* 1/2 Chopped Blanched Onion
* 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
* Kosher salt to taste
* 2 tbls. fresh lime juice
* 2 tbls. chopped cilantro

Directions:
1. Blanch whole onion for two minutes in boiling water. Remove and let drain and cool.
2. Coarsely mash avocados with a wooden spoon until the avocados are creamy but still very chunky.
3. Add the tomato, onion, and salt to taste, and stir together. Sprinkle with lime juice and cilantro, then stir and taste once more. Add more onion, salt, lime juice, or cilantro as desired.

Submitter:
