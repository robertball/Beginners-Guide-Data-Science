Title:
My Hearty Bean Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean groung beef
1 medium chopped onion
1/2 lb cooked bacon(crumbled)
1 can(28 oz) baked beans in mollasses sauce(undrained)1 can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 large can waxed yellow beans(drained)
1 large can green beans(drained)
Sauce:
1/2 brown sugar
1/2 cup ketchup
3 tbsp honey
2 tbsp cider vinegar

Directions:
in frying pan cook the ground beef and onion, add the cooked crumbled bacon, tranfer to to a 2-2 1/2 quart baking dish(I use my large roaster pan)
Stir in all the beans until mixed
In a small bowl whisk together the ingrediants for the sauce pour over the bean mixture.
Bake uncovered.

Submitter: 
Bernadene
Description: 
I got this hearty recipe from browsing through a magazine some years ago, i have made it several times and everyone loves it, especially the husband its a hearty meal for all to enjoy!
