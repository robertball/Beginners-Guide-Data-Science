Title:
Cashew Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (5-6 oz.) chicken breasts
all-purp flour for dredging
2 Tab. olive oil
1/2 cup roasted cashews
4 scallions cut into 1 inch pcs.
1/2 cup soy sauce
1 lemon
1/2 cup chicken stock, or more as needed

Directions:
Cut chicken into strips the size of a baby's index finger. Dredge the chicken in the flour and shake off the excess. Fry the chicken in the olive oil until cooked on both sides, then throw in the scallion and cashews. Add the soy and juice of the lemon. Add the stock to cover and cook another 1-2 min. to thicken. Serve w/ rice.
