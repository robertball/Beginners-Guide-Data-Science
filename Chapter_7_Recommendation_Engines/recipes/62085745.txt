Title:
Herb Garlic Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 to 8 boneless skinless chicken breasts halves (about 2 lbs.)
1 packet Shake'N Bake seasoned coating mix for chicken, Herb & Garlic or Savory Roast

Directions:
Moisten chicken with water; shake off excess.
Shake chicken, 1 to 2 pieces at a time, in shaker bag with mix.  Discard any remaining mix and bag.  Place chicken in 15 x 10 x 1-inch baking pan lined with foil or sprayed with cooking spray.
Bake at 425 degrees F for 20 minutes or until cooked through.

Submitter: 
Darci
Description: 
Great with Velveeta shells and cheese dinner, green beans and a tossed salad with your favorite salad dressing.
