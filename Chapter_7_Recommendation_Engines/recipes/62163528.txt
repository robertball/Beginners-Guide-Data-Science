Title:
Corny Chocolate Chip Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Jiffy cornbread mix
1 (17.5 oz.) bag of prepared chocolate chip cookie mix
2 eggs
1/2 cup unsweetened apple sauce
1/3 cup milk
1/2 cup chopped nuts optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Beat eggs, apple sauce, and milk to combine. Dump in both mixtes and stir away.
Prepare a bundt cake pan by buttering/non-stick cooking spray and flour. Pour batter into your prepared pan. Bake for 35-40 minutes.

Submitter: 
dani
Description: 
A crazy experiment gone good! A mixture of cornbread mix and prepared chocolate chip cookie mix turned into a cake. Sounds weird but tastes surprisingly good.
