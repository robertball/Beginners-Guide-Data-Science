Title:
Big Ray's Farmer's Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6058507.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups frozen hash browns
3/4 cup shredded monterey jack pepper cheese
1 cup cubed cooked ham
1/4 cup green onion, well chopped
1/4 cup bell pepper, well chopped
4 well beaten eggs
1 (12 ounce) can evaporated milk
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Grease 7" x 11" rectangular baking dish.
Arrange potatoes evenly on bottom of baking dish, potatoes will thaw overnight in the fridge.
Sprinkle with cheese, ham, onions.
Combine milk and eggs and seasonings.
Pour over potatoes and cheese.
Refrigerate over night.
Bake at 350 for 40-50 minutes until set.
Let rest 5 minute.

Submitter:

Description: 
There are so many ways to make it your own. you can use chopped cooked bacon in place of ham, or crumbled sausage, add mushrooms or any other veggie that floats your boat!
