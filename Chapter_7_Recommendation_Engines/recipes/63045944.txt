Title:
Curried Ground Beef, Rice and Red Lentils Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 oz ground beef
1 onion
1 tbl curry
3/4 tsp cinnamon
4 cups reduced sodium beef broth
1 cup white rice
1 cup red lentils

Directions:
Brown ground beef and onions in large pan (I used a wok), until onions are browned and beef is no longer pink. Add curry and cinnamon, mix well, add beef broth. Bring to a boil, add rice and lentils, reduce heat and cover, simmering for 20 minutes or until all liquid is absorbed. Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Tisha Goldsmith
Description: 
Simple, one pot meal, very tasty on a chilly evening.
