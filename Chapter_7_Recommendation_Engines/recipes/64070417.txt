Title:
Fresh Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake
4 cups finely diced apples
1 cup raisins
1 cup pecans
3 cups flour
2 cups sugar
1 1/4 cup vegetable oil
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
2 tsp. soda
1/2 tsp. salt
2 tsp. cinnamon
Icing
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup butter
1 tsp. vanilla
1 heaping TBSP flour

Directions:
Cake
Preheat oven to 300 degrees. Prepare bundt pan or 9x13 inch cake pan.
Mix sugar, oil, eggs and vanilla until fluffy. Add remaining ingredients, mix well. Pour into cake pan.
Icing:
Combine all ingredients in a sauce pan, cook until thick. Cool and spread on cake.
