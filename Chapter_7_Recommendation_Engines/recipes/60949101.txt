Title:
Easy Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/277217.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1 egg
1 cup milk
1 cup dried bread crumbs
salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 tablespoons prepared mustard
1/3 cup ketchup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine the beef, egg, onion, milk and bread OR cracker crumbs.  Using cookie cutter, shape the meat mixture into men. Season with salt and pepper to taste and place in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
In a separate small bowl, combine the brown sugar, mustard and ketchup. Mix well and pour over the meatloaf men.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 35 minutes.

Description: 
This recipe is really easy and my kids love it when I make it meatloaf men. I use a gingerbread man cookie cutter and shape the meat into little men. It is cute and the kids giggle every time that they cut the man's head off.Modified from: Easy Meatloaf
