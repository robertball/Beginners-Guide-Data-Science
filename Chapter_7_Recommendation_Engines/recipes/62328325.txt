Title:
Strawberry Dream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 3 oz. pkg. Strawberry Jello
1- 3 1/4 oz. pkg. Vanilla Pudding(NOT INSTANT)
2 cups water
1 1/2 cups Cool Whip, thawed
9 inch graham pie crust
1 cup fresh, sliced strawberries

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine jello, pudding, and water. Stir over med-high heat until it comes to a full boil. Pour into a bowl.
Chill until slightly thickened.
With an electric mixer on low speed, mix Cool Whip into jello mixture.
Stir in strawberries and then pour into pie crust.
Chill until firm.
