Title:
Golden State Mushroom Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983398.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 medium onion, chopped
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 1/2 cups milk
1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
1 teaspoon chicken bouillon granules
1 cup sour cream
Minced fresh parsley

Directions:
In a large saucepan, saute mushrooms and onion in butter until tender. Sprinkle with flour, salt and pepper; mix well. Gradually stir in milk, broth and bouillon; bring to a boil. Cook and stir for 2 minutes. Reduce heat. Stir in sour cream; heat through (do not boil). Garnish with parsley if desired.

Submitter: 
David Patton
Description: 
Mushrooms have become very popular in my area, but they've long been a favorite of mine. After years of searching for a good mushroom soup recipe, I gave up and created my own.
