Title:
Granny Weeks' Maryland Crab Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. crab meat
3 Tablespoons self-rising flour
4 shakes worcesteshire sauce
1 egg
1 teaspoon parsley flakes
1 teaspoon mustard
2 Tablespoons mayonnaise
1 teaspoon Old Bay Seasoning

Directions:
Mix all ingredients until just blended; do not overmix.  Shape into small patties, pan fry in 1/2 inch of hot oil until crispy on the outside.  Drain on paper towels. Serve hot with Saltine Crackers.

Submitter: 
ELLENMEL
Description: 
Tried and true recipe from the heart of Maryland.  Not a lot of fillers so you can really taste the goodness of the crab meat.
