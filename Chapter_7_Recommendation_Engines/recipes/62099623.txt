Title:
Easy No Bake Peanut Butter Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - ready to bake pie crust
1 - package 3.4 oz. instant vanilla pudding
1/2 C. Creamy peanut butter
1 1/2 C. Confections Sugar
4 oz. whipped topping-softened

Directions:
Cook pie crust according package directions, set aside to cool. Mix peanut butter and confections sugar with a fork until it resembles small crumbles, set aside. Make the vanilla pudding for a pie recipe according to the package directions. Add 1/2 peanut butter crumbles to the bottom of the cooled pie crust. Then add prepared pudding and then the softened whipped topping. Top with rest of peanut butter crumbles. Chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Amanda F.
Description: 
We call this Easy Peanut butter pie, It is easy, quick to make and so good! Even if you are not a big peanut butter fan, you will love this pie!
