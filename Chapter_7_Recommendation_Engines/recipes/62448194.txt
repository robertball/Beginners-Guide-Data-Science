Title:
Teriyaki Roast Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup soya sauce
½ cup cooking oil
¼ cup light molasses
1 Tbsp. ground ginger
1 Tbsp. dry mustard
8 cloves garlic
6 lb. boneless beef roast

Directions:
Poke holes in roast with a fork.
Refrigerate roast in marinade over night.
Cook for 1.5 hours at 325 F.
Baste with left-over marinade.
