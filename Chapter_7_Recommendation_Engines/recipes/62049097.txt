Title:
Leftover Ham Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cooked Ham (with ham bone if available)
Carrots
Potatoes
Onions
Garlic powder
parsley
pepper
water

Directions:
Cut up ham into bite size pieces. If using the ham bone cut meat off the bone but be sure to leave some meat on the bone.
Cut up carrots in quarters.
Peel and cut potatoes into quarters.
Slice onions, do not chop them up, just use slices.
Put ham bone in crockpot (if you have it) and all cut ingredients. Fill the crockpot about half way with water.  The amount of each ingredient is to your preference.
Season with parsley, garlic powder and pepper.  Cook on low for 8 hours, serve hot with bread rolls and butter.

Submitter: 
Melissa
Description: 
Ham cooked in the crockpot in a delicious stew with carrots, potatoes and onions... it melts in your mouth! Perfect for using leftover Christmas or Easter Ham.
