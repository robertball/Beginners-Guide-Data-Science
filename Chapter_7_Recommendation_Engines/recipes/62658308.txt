Title:
Pork Chops in Puttanesca Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork loin centre chops (boneless or bone-in)
2 tbsp vegetable oil
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp hot pepper flakes
1/4 tsp salt
1 can (19 oz) diced tomatoes
1/4 cup tomato paste
1/2 cup chopped pitted kalamata olives
1 tbsp drained capers
1/4 cup minced fresh basil or parsley

Directions:
In large skillet, heat 1 tbsp of the oil over medium-high heat and brown pork chops. Transfer to a plate.
Drain fat from pan, then add remaining oil. Fry onion, garlic, pepper, hot pepper flakes and salt over medium heat until softened (about 4 minutes).
Add tomatoes, tomato paste, olives and capers.  Bring to boil making sure to stir well (including any bits from the bottom of the pan).
Reduce heat and simmer about 10 minutes.
Return the pork chops and any juices to the pan then add basil.
Cover and simmer about 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Leanne Cooke
Description: 
A flavourful way to perk up your pork chops!
