Title:
Easy Bacon and Cheese Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices bacon cooked & crumbled
2 Tbls flour
1/2 cup chopped onion
5 ounces shredded Swiss cheese
3 ounces grated Parmesan cheese
1 (9 inch) deep dish frozen pie crust
4 eggs, lightly beaten
1 cup half-and-half or heavy cream
1 medium tomato thinly sliced
3/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (200 degrees C).
In a medium bowl, mix the bacon, onions, and both cheeses.  Place this mixture in the unthawed pie crust.
Mix the eggs and half and half,flour, salt and pepper in a bowl.  Pour the egg mixture over the cheese mixture.top with tomatoes
Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 325 degrees F (175 degrees C) and bake for an additional 45 minutes, until top of quiche begins to turn brown. Remove from oven and let it sit for 15 minutes before serving.  A salad goes great with this.
