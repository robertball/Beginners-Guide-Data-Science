Title:
Peach Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3 oz package lemon jello
3/4 cup hot peach filling (recipe to follow)
1 cup orange juice (can't remember if I used)
1 cup whipping cream, whipped
3 oz cream cheese
1/2 cup powdered sugar
Peach Pie Filling
1 bag frozen peaches
3/4 cup sugar (1/2 cup brown sugar)
3 T flour
1/2 teaspoon almond extract
1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Make the peach pie filling first.
Mix 3/4 cup boiling peaches to jello.
Add OJ
In a separate bowl mix cream cheese, whipped cream and sugar.
