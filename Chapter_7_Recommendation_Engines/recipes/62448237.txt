Title:
Peanut Butter Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flours
1 ¼ cups sugar
1 tsp. salt
2 sticks margarine
1 cup water
3 Tbsp. cocoa
½ cup buttermilk
1 tsp. baking soda
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
Frosting:
1 stick of butter
3 Tbsp. milk
3 Tbsp. cocoa
2 ½ cups powdered sugar

Directions:
Combine first 3 ingredients in a large bowl.
In saucepan, bring margarine, water and cocoa to a boil.
Slowly pour into dry ingredients. With mixer, and add buttermilk, soda, eggs and vanilla.
Bake in a cookie sheet at 350 for 20-25 minutes.
Spread with peanut butter, then frosting.
