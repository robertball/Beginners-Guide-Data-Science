Title:
CARROT SOUFFLE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb fresh carrots, boiled until soft
6 eggs
2/3 cup sugar
6 Tbl matzo meal
2 tsp vanilla
2 sticks butter or marg, melted
Topping:
6 Tbl brown sugar
4 Tbl marg, melted
1 C chopped walnuts

Directions:
Place boiled carrots & eggs into food processor & puree. Add next 5 ingredients & process until smooth.
Bake in 9x13 panb at 350 for 40min. Add topping & bake 5-10min more.
Can be made day in advance
