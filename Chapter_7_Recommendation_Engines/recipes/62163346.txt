Title:
Top of the stove cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup milk
2 T. peanut butter
3 cups oatmeal
2 T. cocoa
1 t. vanilla

Directions:
Boil sugar, buter, milk, and peanut butter for 1 minute.
Add oatmeal, cocoa, and vanilla.
Mix well.
Drop small spoonfuls onto wax paper.
Let sit at room temperature for 20min.
Enjoy!
