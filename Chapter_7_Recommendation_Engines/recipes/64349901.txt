Title:
Chili coke roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-5 lb. pork or beef roast
1 can of stag chili or manwich
1 pkg. of onion soup mix
1 can of coke
Large hamburger buns

Directions:
Place roast into slow cooker
pour coke over the roast int the slow cooker
Pour the can of coke over the roast
pour the can of chili/manwich over the whole roast
Spread onion soup mix over roast
