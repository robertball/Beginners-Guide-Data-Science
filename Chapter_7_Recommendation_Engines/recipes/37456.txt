Title:
Cheesy Quesadilla Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986283.jpg
Ingredients:
1 TACO BELL HOME ORIGINALS Flour Tortilla
2 KRAFT Singles
1 (16 ounce) jar TACO BELL HOME ORIGINALS Thick 'N Chunky Salsa
1 (16 ounce) container BREAKSTONE'S or KNUDSEN Sour Cream

Directions:
Place tortilla on microwavable plate. Place Singles on half of tortilla. Fold tortilla in half; cover with plastic wrap.
Microwave on HIGH 25 to 40 seconds or until Singles begin to melt. Let stand, covered, 1 minute or until cool enough to eat. Fold in half again. Serve with salsa and sour cream.
