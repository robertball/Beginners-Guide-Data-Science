Title:
Spinach and Lemon Zest Stuffed Chicken Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen chopped spinach (thawed)
1 tsp lemon zest, grated
2 tsp butter, softened
2 Tbsp parsley minced
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/2 cup italian breadcrumbs
8 toothpicks

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Press liquid from thawed spinach. Combine with lemon zest, butter, and parsley.
Place chicken breasts between sheets of wax paper. Use mallet to flatten.
Spread spinach mixture over half of each chicken breast.
Roll each breast starting at the side with the spinach mixture and secure with toothpicks.
Roll each breast in the breadcrumbs.
Place the chicken in a shallow pan. Bake for 35-40 minutes. Toothpicks should remove easily when chicken is done.
