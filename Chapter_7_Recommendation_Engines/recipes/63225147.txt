Title:
Tom Kha Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons cooking oil
1 Tablespoon red curry paste
2 teaspoons finely chopped lemon grass
1 inch galangal root, thinly sliced
2 keffir lime leaves
12 ounces tofu of meat of your choice, in small pieces
16 ounces coconut milk
2 cups chicken stock
1 teaspoon lime juice
1 teaspoon fish sauce (or to taste)
1 large fresh tomato, diced
1 12 ounce can baby corn, drained and sliced
1/2 cup sliced fresh mushroom
1/4 cup chopped cilantro

Directions:
In a large pan, combine cooking oil, red curry paste, lemon grass, galangal and tofu or meat over medium heat.  Stir for 3-4 minutes until the spices become aromatic.
Add coconut milk, chicken stock, fish sauce, keffir lime leaves, tomato, corn, mushroom and lime juice.  Reduce heat and cover. Simmer on low for 15-20 minutes.
Remove from heat.  Add cilantro and additional fish sauce to taste.
Serve over warm rice.

Submitter:

Description: 
The first time I tried this classic Thai soup, I fell in love with it.  A savory mix of coconut milk,  popular Thai spices and veggies, plus tofu or the meat of your choice make for a soup worth eating frequently!  Serve it over rice to extend the pleasure.
