Title:
Peas with Butter and Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup frozen peas
Water
Salt
Fresh ground Pepper
Butter
Freshly shaved parmesan

Directions:
Place peas in small saucepan with lid.  Fill with water until peas are just covered.  Cook on medium heat for 4 minutes with cover on, stirring occasionally.  Test a pea to make sure it's cooked to your liking.
Drain peas in a colander and place in a bowl.  Season with salt, freshly ground pepper, butter, and Parmesan.

Submitter: 
HDN
Description: 
Quick and simple peas.  Makes a good side dish, but I actually like to eat it as a snack or for breakfast!
