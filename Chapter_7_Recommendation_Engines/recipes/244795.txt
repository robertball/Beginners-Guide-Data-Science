Title:
Lavender Tom Collins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2552345.jpg
Ingredients:
12 lavender flowers
3 cups boiling water
1 cup ice cubes
6 fluid ounces Collins mix
2 fluid ounces gin
2 maraschino cherries

Directions:
Place lavender flowers in an empty ice cube tray; pour boiling water over flowers. Place ice cube tray in the refrigerator to cool, about 15 minutes. Transfer to freezer and freeze until solid, about 3 hours.
Fill a glass with ice. Pour Collins mix and gin over ice; stir. Top drink with a lavender ice cube and garnish with maraschino cherries.

Submitter: 
Heather
Description: 
Lavender brings some yummy sophistication to the classic Tom Collins. You can make your own Collins mix with soda and lavender simple syrup, or try this quick work-around. The flavor will slowly melt, giving the perfect hint of lavender!
