Title:
Delicious Blueberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/444498.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup apple juice
1 tablespoon instant iced tea powder
1/2 cup frozen blueberries
1 frozen banana
1 tablespoon lemon juice (optional)

Directions:
Place the apple juice, iced tea powder, blueberries, banana, and lemon juice into a blender pitcher. Blend on high until smooth.

Description: 
This is a fruity, delicious, and easy-to-make smoothie.
