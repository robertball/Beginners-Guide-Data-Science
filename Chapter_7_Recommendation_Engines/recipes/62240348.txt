Title:
Ranch Chicken in Crockpot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. baby carrots
4 boned and skinned chicken breasts
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 envelope Ranch Dip mix
1/4 cup sour cream

Directions:
Place carrots in the bottom of the crock pot.
Mix soup and dip mix and pour 1/2 over the carrots.
Put chicken on top of carrots.
Pour rest of soup and dip mix on top of chicken.
Cook on high 3-4 hours.
Scrape sauce off chicken into crock pot and and remove chicken.
Stir in the sour cream right before serving and allow to warm a bit.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
This recipe is so quick to prepare.  Just put it in the crockpot and come back a few hours later to a wonderful meal.
