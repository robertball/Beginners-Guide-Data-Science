Title:
Mom's Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium apples, peeled and thinly sliced
3/4 cup rolled oats
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup flour
3 tablespoons butter
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
dash of cinnamon

Directions:
grease an 8 inch gratin dish.  Place apples in dish.
Mix flour, sugar and oats.  Cut butter in small pieces and add to flour/sugar/oat mixture.  Add cinnamon and vanilla.  Sprinkle over apples and bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes.

Submitter: 
HARRY WETZEL
Description: 
I got this recipe from my mom.  It is one of the easiest crisp recipes I have seen, and it tastes great.  If you're looking for a quick dessert and have a bunch of apples on hand I highly recommend it.
