Title:
Quick and Easy Tater Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of ground beef
1 can Campbell's cream of chicken and herb soup (mom used cream of mushroom)(I found it at Walmart)
1/2 pound of shredded cheese (sharp chedder is great)
1-2 pounds of frozen tater tots
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Brown 1 pound of ground beef and drain.
Put meet into square glass casserole dish and add can of soup. Stir to mix well and smooth level in the dish.
Cover with 1/2 pound of shredded cheese, spread evenly.
Cover with frozen tater tots evenly to cover cheese entirely.
Cook uncovered on cookie sheet on middle rack until tater tots are golden brown and cheese is bubbly.  Approximately 30-45 minutes.
Serve hot and salt and pepper to taste.
Add a salad and you have the four food groups.

Submitter: 
Chris Wilkie
Description: 
This is a casserole I have cooked for years. My mom got it from a co-worker at the hospital.  It is a simple meal, can be prepared ahead of time and refrigerated overnight until ready to cook or even frozen until needed later.
