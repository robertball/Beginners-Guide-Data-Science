Title:
Rockin's Bacon Ranch Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz package rotini pasta cooked according to directions and drained.
1 lb bacon cut in small pieces and browned in skillet. Drain on paper towel.
1 1/3 cup mayonnaise
3 tablespoons dry ranch salad dressing mix(recipe by BEC on allrecipes
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon pepper
2 medium tomatoes, chopped
2/3 cup milk
1 (4.25 ounce) can sliced black olives
1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix mayonnaise, ranch dressing mix, garlic powder, and pepper.
Stir in milk until smooth.
Place rotini, bacon, tomato, black olives and cheese in bowl and toss to coat with dressing.
Cover and chill at least 1 hour in the refrigerator. Toss with additional milk if the salad seems a little dry.

Submitter:
