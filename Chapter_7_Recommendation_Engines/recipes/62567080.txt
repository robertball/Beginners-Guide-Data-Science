Title:
Mother Teal's Divinity Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sugar
1 cup Karo syrup
1/2 cup water
2 egg whites
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Beat egg whites until they stand in stiff peaks.  Put together sugar, syrup and water in saucepan and bring to boil (stirring constantly).  Pour 1/3 of syrup into eggwhites beating constantly (with mixer).  Put remainder of syrup and bring to boil again, pour 1/2 of mixture in, bring to boil again, pour remainder in mixture, add vanilla and nuts.  Let cool and spoon onto wax paper.

Submitter: 
Exhibits
Description: 
This is a good recipe, but you must be patient!  If you don't cook to soft ball stage, you will have a sticky mess!
