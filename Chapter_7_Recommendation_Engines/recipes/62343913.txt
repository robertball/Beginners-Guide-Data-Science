Title:
Chicken Broth in a Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/393108.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 pounds bone-in chicken pieces
6 cups water
2 stalks celery, chopped
2 carrots, chopped
1 onion, quartered
1 tsp dried basil
3-4 whole cloves of garlic
2 bay leaves
1 Tbs of Montreal Chicken seasoning

Directions:
Place all of the ingrediatens in your slow cooker.
Cook on Low setting for 8 to 10 hours.
Strain before using, and discard vegetables.
Chicken may be removed from the bones, and used in soup.
Leave the stock in the fridge for the day.
Skim the remaining fat from the top.

Submitter: 
MarqueH
Description: 
This easy slow cook method needs to simmer for 8 hours to yield approximately one quart of rich chicken stock to use in soups, sauces and other recipes.Modified from: Chicken Broth in a Slow Cooker
