Title:
Instant Pot® Cashew Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6498779.jpg
Ingredients:
14 ounces chicken breast, cut into 1” cubes
2 Tbsp corn starch
1 Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp mirin
2 Tbsp soy sauce
2 Tbsp mirin
2 Tbsp hoisin sauce
2 tsp sesame oil
3/4 cup water
1 tsp peanut oil
3/4 cup raw cashews
1 Tbsp white sesame seeds
1 Tbsp black sesame seeds
1 Tbsp peanut oil
2 cups bell pepper, in 1” slices
1 medium onion, cut into eighths
1 garlic clove, minced
8 oz can baby corn, drained and cut into bite-sized pieces
8 oz can sliced water chestnuts, drained

Directions:
In a medium size mixing bowl, sprinkle the corn starch over the chicken and mix well until each piece of chicken is well coated. Add one tablespoon soy sauce and one tablespoon mirin. Stir well. Set aside.
In a small bowl, mix together 2 tbsp soy, 2 tbsp mirin, 2 tbsp hoisin sauce, 2 tsp sesame oil, and 3/4 cup water. Whisk together, set aside.
Turn on a multi-functional pressure cooker (such as Instant pot®) and select Sauté function. Add 1 tsp peanut oil, two types of sesame seeds. Stir to toast evenly. About 3 minutes. Add remaining 1 tbsp peanut oil. Add garlic, onion, and bell pepper and sauté until fragrant, about 5 minutes. Add chicken mixture. Stir well. Add soy sauce mixture, baby corn, and water chestnuts. Stir to combine.
Close lid. Set manual mode for 3 minutes. Carefully release pressure using quick release method. Stir and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
A classic take-out favorite made at home in your Instant Pot. This recipe uses chicken breast to keep it on the lean side, but the chicken is tenderized to maintain a velvety texture. Serve over white rice.
