Title:
Pumpkin Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 29 ounce can pure pumpkin
2. 12 ounce  can evaporated milk
3. 3 eggs
4. 1 cup sugar
5. 1 teaspoon salt
6. 3 teaspoons cinnamon
7. 1 box yellow cake mix ( could use spice cake and omit cinnamon)
8. 1 cup chopped nuts ( walnuts or pecans ok)
9. 3/4 cup melted margarine

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*
Mix 1. 2. 3. 4. 5. 6. until blended...pour batter into 9x12" Greased and floured pan
Sprinkle 7. on top evenly...cover with 8.
Pour melted 9. over the top
Bake 50 min
may wish to serve with whip cream
