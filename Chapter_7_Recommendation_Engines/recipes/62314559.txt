Title:
Danielle's Chicken Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package tri-color spiral pasta
1 lb. bonless, skinless chicken breasts, small cubes
2 medium red bell peppers, chopped
1 medium yellow bell pepper, chopped
2 medium cucumbers, seeded and chopped
2 cups grape tomatoes, halved
4 cups broccoli, chopped
8 ounces feta cheese
1 bottle balsamic vinaigrette salad dressing
2 tbl. butter
1 tab. balsamic vinegar
3 tsp. garlic powder
2 tsp. ground black pepper

Directions:
Melt butter on medium high heat in large pan.  Add chicken and cook for about 5 minutes.  Add balasamic vinegar, garlic powder and black pepper to chicken.  Cook until chicken is done.  Be sure to turn chicken cubes during cooking time.
In the meantime, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.  Cook pasta for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
In a large serving bowl add pasta, chicken, vegetables and feta cheese.  Carefully mix everything together taking care not to break tomatoes.  Add dressing and carefully mix again until everything is evenly coated.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Danielle B.
Description: 
Zesty, crunchy and fresh.  Perfect for a summer get together!
