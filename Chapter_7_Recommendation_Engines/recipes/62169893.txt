Title:
Finger Lick'n Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs. Chicken wings or breast/thigh/drumstick
1 cup soy sauce
1 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp salt
1/4 cup vegetable oil (or canola)
1/4 cup frozen pineapple concentrate (thawed)

Directions:
Cut wings (if using wings) in 3 small parts. (Do not use the tip of the wings) Place chicken in 9" x 13" pan. Mix all above ingredients together, wisk well, and pour over chicken. Marinate overnight. (Marinating for 2 days is even better!) Bake this in same pan for 1 1/2 hours at 350* F. After this cools it jells. When ready to serve, just heat.

Submitter: 
Cooking Mama
Description: 
A delicious, sweet and salty chicken wing! So tender, the meat falls of the bone! You can use chicken wings, or breasts/thigh/drumsticks. A must try! Great for potlucks, or a good game! I often sub regular soy sauce for light soy sauce.
