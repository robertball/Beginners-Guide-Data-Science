Title:
Easy Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box (1 lb or 4 C.) powdered sugar
1/2 C. cocoa
1/4 tsp. salt
6 T. butter
4 T. milk*
1 T. vanilla
1 C. chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except nuts in top of double boiler
Place over hot water and stir until smooth
Add nuts
Spread in buttered pan
Cool and cut into squares
*Note: milk amount may vary according to type of milk you use -- just put in enough so that when everything is mixed, it is nice and smooth, not runny.

Submitter: 
JOLLYGREEN23
Description: 
If you can use a double boiler, you can make this delicious fudge!
