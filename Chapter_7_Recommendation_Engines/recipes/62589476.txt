Title:
Balsamic Pork with Shallots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 tsp. olive oil
1 lb. pork tenderloin, cut into 1/2" slices
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
2 1/4 cups thinly slices shallots (about 8)
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 cup water
2 tbsp. balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Heat 1 tsp. oil in large skillet. Sprinkle pork with 1/4 tsp. salt and pepper.  Add pork to pan and cook 3 minutes on each side or until done.  Remove from pan and keep warm.
Add remaining oil to pan and reduce heat to medium.  Add shallots and cook 10 min. or until tender.  Add garlic and cook 2 min.  Stir in 1/2 cup water and vinegar and simmer 6 min.  Add remaining salt and pepper, spoon over pork and serve immediately.
