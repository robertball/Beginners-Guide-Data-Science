Title:
Creamy Spinach Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz box lasagna noodles
3 jars of alfredo noodle sauce (Classico garlic, sundried tomato & four cheese)
3 16oz bags frozen chopped spinach
2 15oz containers cottage cheese
1 16oz bag of Mozzarella cheese
2 teaspoons Lawry's Seasoning Salt
2 tablespoons Italian Seasonings
1 tablespoon Garlic granules or powder
1 tablespoon Rosmary Garlic granules or season to your desired taste

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2.  In a large bowl thoroughly mix all ingredients with a large wooden spoon.  Set aside.
3.  Boil lasagna noodles according to box directions.
4.  Add some of the mixture to the bottom of a deep dish lasagna pan or large roasting pan.
5.  Layer in lasagna noodles (fill-in corners with the noodles), then the mixture, then sprinkle with the Mozzarella cheese.
6.  Top the final layer with remaining Mozzarella cheese, cover with lid and bake for 45 minutes or until edges are lightly browned.
7.  If using a pan that doesn't have a lid, cover with aluminum foil, cook for 35 minutes, then uncover for the remaining 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Tonia's Tastings
Description: 
This is a great alternative to the basic tomato sauce based lasagna.  Even people who hate spinach, love this lasagna!  Feel free to add a pound of your favorite meat, herbs & spices and fresh vegetables to this recipe...It's your kitchen so have fun!  Bon Appetite!!!
