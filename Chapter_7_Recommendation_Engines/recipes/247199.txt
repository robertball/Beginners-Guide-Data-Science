Title:
Easy and Delicious Avocado Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3668183.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 cup sour cream

Directions:
Mash the avocados in a deep bowl. Mix in garlic, salt, and black pepper. Stir in sour cream until well-blended.
