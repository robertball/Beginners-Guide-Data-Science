Title:
Almond chocolate cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/399028.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
1 cup all purpose flour
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 egg
1/2 cup of milk
1/2  (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup chopped almonds
1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder and salt. Add the cream cheese (at room temperature), eggs, milk, oil, cinnamon and vanilla, mix for 5 minutes. Stir in the almonds and chocolate chips.
Fill cups 2/3 full.
Bake for 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
