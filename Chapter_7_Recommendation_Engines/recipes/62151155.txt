Title:
Cabbage Roll Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground beef
1 tbsp oil
1 chopped onion
1 cup ketchup
2 tbsp brown sugar
2 tbsp vinegar
2 tbsp lemon juice
1/2 cup water
1 can tomato soup
5 tbsp rice
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
4-5 cups chopped cabbage

Directions:
Brown ground beef in oil. Add onion and cook until soft.
Stir in rest of ingredients except cabbage.
Put chopped cabbage in 9x13 casserole dish.
Pour ground beef mixture over top.
Cover and bake about 1 1/2 hours.

Submitter: 
Melissa
Description: 
Taste just like Grandma's cabbage rolls without all the hassle!
