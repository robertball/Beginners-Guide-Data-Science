Title:
Roast beef dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pounds lean chuck roast
2 cans french onion soup
2 pounds carrots, peeled and quartered
3 pounds potatoes, peeled and quartered
2 onions, quartered

Directions:
Put all ingredients in large oven pan. Add enough water to cover to vegetables. Cover and seal with foil. Put into 300 degree oven in the morning and with be done at dinner time.

Description: 
This is such an easy recipe especially for busy people who do not want to fuss with dinner.
