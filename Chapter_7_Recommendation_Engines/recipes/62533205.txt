Title:
Mamaw Bea's Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 c yellow squash, sliced
1/4 c onion
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 c sour cream
1 c sharp cheddar, shredded
1 (8oz) bag herb-seasoned stuffing mix
1/2 c butter or margarine, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Boil squash & onion in salted water for 7 minutes; drain.
Combine soup, sour cream, cheese; fold into squash mixture
Combine stuffig & butter; spread 1/2 into bottom of baking dish.  Spread veggies over.  Spoon remaining stuffing on top.
Bake for 25 - 30 min
