Title:
Pumpkin Triffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of vanilla pudding (1.5 oz)
1 bag of ginger snaps
1 container of cool whip
1 can of pumpkin pie filling
cinnamon
milk (to make pudding)

Directions:
Prepare pudding according to package directions.
Mix pudding, cinnamon to taste and pumpkin pie filling.
Line a dish with ginger snaps, layer next with pumpkin mixture, then cool whip.
Repeat in a lasagna style fashion.
Chill for several hours.
Top with crumbled ginger snaps.

Submitter: 
Fredda O.
Description: 
A fluffy pumpkin flavored twist on a traditional banana pudding dish.
