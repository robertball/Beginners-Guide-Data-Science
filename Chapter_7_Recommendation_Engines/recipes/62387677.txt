Title:
Chinese Chicken Broth Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/422681.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lbs Chicken backs or necks cut to 1" pieces
5 slices of ginger
2 green onions (whites only)
4 whole black peppercorns
2 cloves garlic smashed (skin intact)

Directions:
1. Boil 10 cups of water on high heat.
2. Add chicken and stir bring back to boil and allow to boil for 3 minutes.
3. Drain chicken and rinse well with cold water, making sure any scum or blood is rinsed off.
4. In a clean pot or slow cooker, cover chicken with 2" of water. Water should be 2" above chicken.
5. Add smashed garlic, ginger, peppercorns, and green onion whites. Do Not stir!!
6. Bring water to simmer, just under boiling point if using stove or on let cook on Low in a slow cooker for 8 hours.
7. If using stove top method allow to simmer for 3 hours, making sure your stock never boils.
8. Allow to cool without stirring, then refrigerate.
9 When cold skim off fat and save for other recipes.
