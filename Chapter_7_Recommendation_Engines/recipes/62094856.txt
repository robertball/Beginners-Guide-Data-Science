Title:
Firm Gruyere Polenta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup vegetable broth
2 cups water
1 cup coarse-cut cornmeal
shreaded gruyere cheese
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil water and vegetable broth.
Whisk in corn meal.
Reduce to a simmer.
Mix/whisk for 20-25 minutes, until firmer.
Move cornmeal to pyrex pie pan.  At this time you can add 1/2 c parmesean cheese, any seasonings you like, salt & pepper, or even sauteed garlic & rosemary.
Refrigerate 1 hour, up to overnight.
When ready to serve, heat in 375 degree oven for 10 minutes, then top with shreaded gruyere.  Heat again until cheese is melted.  Serve with salad & vinegarette dressing.

Description: 
A firm coarse ground polenta topped with gruyere cheese.
