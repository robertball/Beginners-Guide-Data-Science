Title:
Yakiudon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
oil
1/2 small shallot, sliced
1/2 cup sliced meat of your choice if desired
1/2 carrot, shredded
1 brick udon noodles
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp dashi powder (basically instant soup stock)
1 tsp sugar
freshly ground black pepper to taste
1 green onion, sliced
dried bonito flakes, to garnish

Directions:
Prepare udon noodles according to package, drain and set aside
Heat up a small bit of oil in pan over medium heat
Add shallots, meat, carrots.
cook until carrots and shallots are soft but not overly brown. About 2-3 minutes
Add drained udon noodles, soy sauce, dashi powder, and sugar.
Toss to coat noodles and turn heat to medium-high to reduce sauce
Turn heat off, taste, and season with pepper to taste
Garnish with green onions and bonito flakes.
Enjoy hot!
