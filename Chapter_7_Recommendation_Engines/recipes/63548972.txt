Title:
Salisbury Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/969417.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large onion, chopped
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
1 egg
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp black pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
1/4 cup ketchup
1 TBSP Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp mustard powder
1 pkg brown gravy mix
1 cup chopped mushrooms
1 TBSP dry parsley

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together 1/3 cup chopped onion with ground beef, bread crumbs, egg, salt, black pepper and garlic powder.
Shape into 6 patties.
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown both sides of patties.
Pour off excess fat and add remaining onion and mushrooms to skillet.
In a small bowl, blend gravy mix with appropriate amount of water (see back of gravy package) until smooth.
Mix in ketchup, Worcestershire sauce and mustard powder.
Pour over meat in skillet.
Cover, and cook for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
