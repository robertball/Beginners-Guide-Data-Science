Title:
Guacamole de Sola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 small avacados
1/2 tbsp. lemon juice
1/3 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. cayenne pepper
1/8 tsp. cumin

Directions:
Scoop avacado meat from shell and place in a medium bowl. Add lemon juice, salt, garlic powder, cayenne pepper and cumin.
Mash mixture with a fork until it is at desired consistency.

Submitter: 
Sola
Description: 
Whipped this up to go with a seven-layer dip and it has become my standard recipe that I use anytime it is called for! Can also be made in a food processor.
