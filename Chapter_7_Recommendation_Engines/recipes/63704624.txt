Title:
Tex-Mex Egg Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz spicy pork sausage
1 (32 oz) bag frozen southern-style hash brown potatoes
1 (4.5 oz) can chopped green chiles, undrained
3 cups Colby-Monterey Jack cheese, shredded (12oz)
6 eggs
1 1/2 cups milk
1 cup salsa

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease 13x9 pan.
Cook sausage and drain.
Spread frozen potatoes in baking dish.
Sprinkle with sausage, green chiles, and half of cheese.
In medium bowl, beat eggs and milk until well blended and pour over potatoes.
Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
Bake uncovered 50-60 minutes.
Let stand 10 minutes.
Serve with salsa.

Description: 
Spice up your breakfast menus by serving an egg casserole featuring spicy sausage, green chiles and salsa.
