Title:
Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Avocados
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp season salt
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 small onion
1 cup chopped tomatoes
2 tbs chopped jalapeno

Directions:
Half, pit and mash avocados, combine remaining ingredients and serve with chips or on side for Mexican dishes
