Title:
Magic Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/503801.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup creamy salad dressing like Miracle Whip
1/2 cup of French dressing (regular or low calorie/fat)
2/3 cup of very low calorie Raspberry Walnut vinaigrette

Directions:
In a medium sized bowl with a wire whisk combine the creamy salad dressing with the French. Stir well. Slowly whisk in the Raspberry Walnut vinaigrette. Store in a covered container in the refrigerator and use to dip meat, raw vegetables, bacon rind snack food or foods of your choice.
