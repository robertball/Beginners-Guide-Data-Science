Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken and Wild Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless split chicken breast halves
1 can Cream of Chicken
1 can Cream of Mushroom
2 packages rice pilaf with seasoning packet from 1 box
2-3 cups water
1 tsp rosemary
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
Mix together soups, rice, seasoning packet, and 1 cup water in slow cooker. Sprinkle breasts with pepper and rosemary and place in slow cooker. Cook on low for 4-5 hours adding water and stirring as needed.
