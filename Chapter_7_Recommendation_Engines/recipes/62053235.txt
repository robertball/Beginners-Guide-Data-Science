Title:
Kim's Pork and Vegetable Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sliced cauliflower
2 cups sliced broccoli
1/4 cup vegetable oil, divided
2 carrots, sliced
1 can (15 oz.) whole baby corn, rinsed and drained
1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed
1 pound boneless pork (I used tenderloin) cut into thin strips
2 green onions, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 TB fresh ginger, minced or 1 tsp. ground ginger
1/2 tsp. chili powder
1 cup water
1/2 cup soy sauce
4 tsp. honey
2 TB cornstarch
2 TB cold water
1/2 cup salted peanuts, diced

Directions:
In a skillet or wok, stir fry cauliflower and broccoli in 3 TB oil for 5 minutes.  Add carrots and stir fry for another 5 minutes.  Add corn and peas and stir fry until vegetables are crisp and tender.  Remove and keep warm.  Stir fry pork in remaining oil until no longer pink.  Add onions, garlic, ginger and chili powder.  Remove and keep warm.  Combine water, soy sauce and honey.  Combine cornstarch and cold water and gradually add this to wok, stirring constantly until thickened.  Return vegetables and pork back to wok and heat through.  Stir in peanuts.  Serve over rice or noodles.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
Any vegetables could be used for this stir fry.  Served over rice with a salad and have a complete meal in no time.
