Title:
Easy Mexican Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/385668.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef (or small package quorn)
taco seasoning packet
1-2 cups salsa
1 can black beans
2 cups sour cream
1 (2 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained
1/2 cup chopped green onion
1/2 cup chopped fresh tomato
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook ground beef until no longer pink.  Add taco seasoning packet with some water, then stir in salsa and beans, and heat until liquid has dissipated.
Oil a 9x13 baking dishy.  Spoon beef mixture over chips. Spread sour cream over beef, and sprinkle olives, green onion, and tomato over the sour cream. Top with Cheddar cheese.
Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until hot and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Kathy Higgins
Description: 
Ground beef, salsa and black beans are layered with tortilla chips and sour cream and topped with melted cheese in this tasty dish.Modified from: Easy Mexican Casserole
