Title:
Green lentil and zucchini soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1336219.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups cooked and drained green lentils( not canned)
4 TBL butter or  2 TBL sunflower oil
1 medium onions minced
1 TBL of garlic powder
1 TBL Italian seasoning
4 Carrots chopped
4 cups chopped zucchini chopped
1 28 Can Tomatoes(no salt added)
2 TBL beef bullion or 2 cubes
2-3 cups small pasta (Diatlini or elbow)
6 cups of water

Directions:
Chop all vegatables and soak in large bowl of water.  Helps reduce starch from squash.  Cook and prepare lentils as stated on package.  While they cook add to large stock pot butter, onions, garlic powder, carrots and italian seasoning; cook on high about 7 min.  After onions turn clear add beef bullion and the can of tomatoes; continue to cook on high for 7 more minutes.  Drain the lentils, add to stock pot along with cups of water and 2-3 cups of pasta depending on size of pasta.  Reduce heat and simmer for 20-30 minutes or until pasta is tender.  Enjoy

Submitter: 
Smokin Poston
Description: 
I love lentils and always am looking for new ways to have them.  With a garden full of zucchinis this recipe was a no-brainer.  One of our favorite staples.  Even good served cold on hot days.
