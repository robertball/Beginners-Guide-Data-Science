Title:
Easy Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Beef Round Tip Steaks, cut 1/8-1/4" thick
4 c. uncooked egg noodles
4 tsp vegetable oil
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1 can sliced mushrooms
1 pkg brown gravy mix
1 c. cold water
1/4 c. sour cream

Directions:
Cook egg noodles, keep warm.  Stack beef steaks, cut lengthwise in half and then crosswise into 1" wide strips. In large skillet heat 1/2 tsp oil over med. heat. Add beef and garlic, stirfry 1 minute or until no longer pink. (DO NOT OVERCOOK!) Remove from skillet.
In same skillet, cook mushrooms. Remove skillet from heat. Add gravy mix and water. Blend well. Return to heat, bring to boil. Simmer 1 minute or until sauce is thickened. Return beef to skillet and heat through. Serve over warm noodles, with sour cream.

Submitter: 
Debbie B.
Description: 
Excellent and very easy. Uses brown gravy instead of cream sauce!
