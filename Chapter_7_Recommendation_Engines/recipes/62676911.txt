Title:
Vegetarian Stromboli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10oz refridgerated pizza dough
1- 10 oz package chopped spinach
1 medium red pepper. sliced
1 cup chopped broccoli
3 cloves chopped garlic
1/2 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
1 green onion sliced
2T olive oil
8oz shredded low moisture mozzarella cheese
1tsp salt
1tsp black pepper
1tsp italian seasoning
1tsp garic salt

Directions:
Bring small saucepan of water to boil add spinach boil 3-4 mins until wilted. drain and set aside
In a medium pan heat oil and add peppers, broccoli, garlic, mushrooms and green onion.
Saute' 7-10 mins on low heat add spices(except garlic salt) and mix well.
Roll out pizza dough on lightly floured surface.
Spred cheese over dough leaving a 1&quot; border around edges.
Top with vegetable mixture and roll like a jellyroll.
Sprinkle garlic salt over the stromboli.
Preheat oven @ 375 and bake 30 - 35 mins until golden brown.
Let stand 10-15 mins , slice and serve.
**** Be sure to have the vegetable mixture as dry as possible when you add it to the dough..

Submitter: 
CNM CATERING
Description: 
Quick and easy vegetarian stromboli! Unlike a classic stromboli that uses a mixture of meats and cheese this stromboli uses only vegetables.
