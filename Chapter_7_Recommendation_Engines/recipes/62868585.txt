Title:
Quick 'n' Easy Beer Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can beer
3 c. self-rising flour
3 T. sugar

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.
Pour into a greased loaf pan.
Bake at 350 degrees until done, about 45 minutes.
NOTE: If you don't have self-rising flour, make your own! For each cup of all-purpose flour, add 1 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder and 1/4 teaspoon of salt.
