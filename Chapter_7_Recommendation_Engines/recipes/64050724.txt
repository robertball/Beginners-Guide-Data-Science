Title:
Mom's Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1130651.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 C  rice
1     qt Milk 2 plus 2
1/2  Cup Raisins
1/3   Cup Butter
3      Eggs
1/2   Cup Sugar
1      tsp vanilla
1/4   tsp salt
cinnamon, nutmeg

Directions:
Mix rice with 2 cups milk in top of double boiler. Cook till rice is tender. Add raisins and butter. Let cool until warm so as not to cook the custard mixture.
Combine eggs sugar, vanilla, salt and 2 cups milk stir into rice mixture. Pour into a greased 1 1/2 qt dish.
Set in pan 1/2 full of warm water. Bake in low oven, 350 degrees,  for 45 min. until set.
