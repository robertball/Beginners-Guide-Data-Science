Title:
No Bake PNut Grahams Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup chunky peanut butter
1/4 cup honey
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/3 cup nonfat dry milk powder
1/3 cup quick-cooking oats
2 tablespoons graham cracker crumbs

Directions:
1/3 cup chunky peanut butter 1/4 cup honey 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/3 cup nonfat dry milk powder 1/3 cup quick-cooking oats 2 tablespoons graham cracker crumbs Directions In a small bowl, combine the peanut butter, honey and vanilla. Stir in the milk powder, oats and graham cracker crumbs. Shape into 1-in. balls. Cover and refrigerate until serving. Yield: 15 treats.
