Title:
Minute Minestrone Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup onion, diced
1 T olive oil
1 can Italian style diced tomatoes
1 can diced tomatoes with green   peppers, onions and garlic
1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 1/2 cups tomato juice
1 T italian seasoning
1 t garlic powder
1 cup cooked pasta, any small variety
S & P to taste

Directions:
- Saute onion in olive oil in a medium sauce pan until onions carmelize
-While onions cook, Prepare pasta following directions on package, drain and set aside
-When onions are cooked, add both cans of tomatoes, beans, tomato juice and seasonings, stir.
-Cook on med-low heat til bubbly, stiring occocianally
-Stir in cooked pasta, continue to cook until pasta is heated
-Serve hot with a sprinkle of paramesen cheese

Submitter: 
Beth Maples
Description: 
This is the perfect soup for a cold winters night when you need warming up, but don't have time for a complicated recipe.
