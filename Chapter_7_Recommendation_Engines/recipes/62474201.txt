Title:
Banana Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c all-purpose flour
1/2 c milled flax seed
2 c All Bran cereal, soaked in 1 1/4 c orange juice
1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
2 teaspoons baking powder
2 teaspoons baking soda
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon allspice
1/2 t salt
3 egg whites
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 cup mashed ripe bananas (3 large)**
***(this might also work well with canned pumpkin)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 for 15 min. In a small bowl, combine the cereal w orange juice and stir; set aside. In a large bowl, combine flour, flax, spices, b powder, b soda, salt - stir thoroughly.  Add the soaked cereal to the bowl.  In the small bowl, mix the egg whites, sugar, oil, and bananas.  Add to larger bowl and gently mix to combine all.  Add any preferences for nuts, dried fruits, blueberries, etc. Sprinkle tops with sugar, if desired.
Fill greased or paper-lined mini-muffin cups to the top, but not overflowing.  Bake at 350 degrees F for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool in pans 10 min. before removing.
