Title:
Aunt Carol's BBQ Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/759649.jpg
Ingredients:
Meatballs:
1-1/2 lbs. ground beef
6.5 oz. canned milk
1 C. quick oats
1 tsp. chili powder
1 egg
1/2 C. chopped onions
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. garlic salt
pepper
BBQ Sauce:
1 Tbsp. liquid smoke
1 C. catsup
1/2 C. brown sugar
1/4 tsp. garlic salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/4 C. chopped onions

Directions:
Meatballs:
Combine all ingredients, and roll into balls. Cook on a cookie sheet in oven 25 minutes at 350. Make sauce while they are baking.
Sauce:
Cook sauce until sugar dissolves. Remove meat balls from cookie sheet and place in baking dish. Pour sauce over them and return to oven and bring to boil.

Submitter: 
akasten06
Description: 
This is a recipe from my great Aunt Carol.  These meatballs are delicious, both adults and children love this dish.  These are great as an appetizer or a main dish.
