Title:
Stuffed Mirlitons, (Chayote Squash), Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 medium mirlitons
1 lb.small shrimp
2 6oz. cans crab meat
3 medium onions, chopped
1 large bell pepper, chopped
3 large sticks celery, chopped,(1/3 cup)
2 cloves garlic, chopped
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 tsp. paprika
1 cup bread crumbs

Directions:
boil mirlitons till tender.Scoop out the meat a save the shells. Saute this with the peppers,onions,celery,garlic and the shrimp for 15-20 minutes. Mix the crab meat,parsley,bread crumbs and paprika.Salt and pepper to taste. Mix all ingredients and stuff the shells and bake 30 minutes in preheated 325 degrees oven.
Note: 1 pound of fresh pork sausage can be substituted for the seafood.

Submitter: 
Marty Adams
Description: 
This is a New Orleans style casserole. It is similar to stuffed greenpeppers. It is mixed with crab meat, shrimp, and seasonings of your choise.
