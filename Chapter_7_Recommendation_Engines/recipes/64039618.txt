Title:
Avocado BLT with Basil Pesto Mayo on Ciabatta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1122907.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Ciabatta Rolls
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup prepared Basil Pesto
1 large Avocado from Mexico, sliced, skin and pit removed
1 large ripe tomato, sliced
2 large green leaf lettuce leaves
4 slices thick bacon, cooked

Directions:
Slice each Ciabatta Roll in half horizontally and place on individual plates.
Whisk together the mayonnaise and Basil Pesto until well combined.
Spread the mayonnaise mixture evenly on the inside halves of each roll.
Layer the bacon, lettuce, tomato, and avocado on the bottom half of each roll.
Place remaining half of roll on top to close sandwich.
Serve Basil Pesto on the side, if desired.

Submitter: 
CookinCyn
Description: 
The classic BLT sandwich only gets better prepared on Ciabatta rolls topped with thick slices of avocado and a savory blend of mayo and Basil Pesto.
