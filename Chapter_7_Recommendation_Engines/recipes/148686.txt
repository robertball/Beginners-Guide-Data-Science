Title:
Rubber Soul Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/827224.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cubes ice
1 fluid ounce pear vodka
4 fluid ounces cranberry juice

Directions:
Place ice cubes into a rocks glass. Pour in vodka and cranberry juice. Stir and serve.

Submitter: 
Mary
Description: 
I am crazy for the fragrance and the taste of pears. When I saw pear vodka, I had to try it!  I was not sure what to mix it with but finally found the perfect combination!
