Title:
Cornbread Biscotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 9x13 pan of cornbread, cooled or day old
1/2 cup melted butter
1/4 to 1/2 tsp spices &/or herbs of choice, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Line 2 large baking sheets with parchment paper. Add spices &/or herbs to warm melted butter, if using.
Turn cornbread out on a cutting board. Thinly slice crust from each of the short ends. Cut cornbread in half lengthwise. Cut each half in 1/2 x 4 1/2 inch slices. Use a pastry brush to brush both sides of each slice lightly with plain or seasoned melted butter. Arrange slices on lined baking sheets.
Bake 8 to 12 minutes, turning once, until golden & crispy on each side.

Submitter: 
HappyGrandma
Description: 
A fun & yummy side for chili, soups or stews. Can be an appetizer by cutting in shorter pieces & serve with a dip, spread, or salsa.
