Title:
Thanksgiving Day Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups water
1 (6 ounce) package strawberry flavored Jell-OÂ® mix
1 (10 ounce) package frozen strawberries, thawed
1 (16 ounce) can jellied cranberry sauce
1/2 cup chopped pecans
8 leaves lettuce
1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup sour cream
1/3 cup white sugar
1 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions:
Boil the water and combine with the strawberry flavored gelatin mix in a medium bowl. Mix in the strawberries, cranberry sauce and pecans. Transfer to a 2 quart mold. Chill in the refrigerator 6 to 8 hours, or overnight.
Place the molded gelatin mixture on a serving plate and surround the base with lettuce leaves.
In a medium bowl, mix the cream cheese, sugar and lemon juice with an electric mixer until fluffy. Fold in the sour cream.  Use this mixture as a topping for each serving of salad.
