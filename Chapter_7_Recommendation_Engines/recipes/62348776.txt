Title:
Shrimp Rangoon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz softened cream cheese
1/4 pound of cooked shrimp
1/2tsp. garlic powder
1/2tsp. cracked black pepper
2T. minced white onion
12 wonton wrappers
oil to fry

Directions:
Heat oil to 350.  Rough chop the shrimp.  Mix together cream cheese, onion, garlic powder, black pepper, and shrimp.  Place a spoonful of cream cheese mixture onto the wonton.  Dampen the edges of the wonton with water.  Fold wonton over cream cheese mixture and press together.  Fry until golden brown, about 3 minutes.
