Title:
St. Patrick's Day yum yum cookies:) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups unsifted flour
1 t. baking soda
1 cup softed butter of margarine
1 t. salt
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 brown sugar
1 t. vanilla extract
2 eggs
1/2 to 1 bag Green M&Ms

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375'F. Mix together ingredients. Shape into 1 inch balls. Put into oven for 8 minutes.
(Recomended - Set timer for 4 minutes, then swap cookies- from shelves)
