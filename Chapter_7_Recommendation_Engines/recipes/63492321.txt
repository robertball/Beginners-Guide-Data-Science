Title:
Avocado Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole avocado, peeled & pitted
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/3 cup sour cream
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon tabasco sauce
1/4 cup cold water

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth and creamy.

Submitter: 
goodeats
Description: 
Fantastic topping for turkey burgers or any other burger, for that matter.
