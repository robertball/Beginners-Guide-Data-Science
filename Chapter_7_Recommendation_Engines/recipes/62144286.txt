Title:
Easy Holiday Eggnog Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of low fat eggnog
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup of vanilla forzen yogurt or frozen ice cream
1 large banana (peeled)
4 large ice cubes
1 teaspoon of malt powder (optional)

Directions:
Add egg nog, milk, frozen yogurt, banana, ice cubes, and malt into a blender.
Blend until smooth.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
MensaJenn
Description: 
A twist on classic holiday eggnog that everyone will love, especially the kids!
