Title:
Beef with sour cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbs shortening, I used butter
2 Med Onions, sliced thin
1/2 cup beef broth or water
3 cups frozen beef cubes
1/4 cup shredded sharp cheese
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1/2 cup dairy sour cream

Directions:
Melt shortening in a skillet. add onions; saute' until soft and clear. Add broth, beef, seasoning. Cover and simmer gently until beef is cooked through, stirring occasionally. Add cheese, stir until melted. Leave covered until ready to serve then stir in sour cream.
Serve over rice or noodles.
