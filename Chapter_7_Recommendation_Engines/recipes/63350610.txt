Title:
Peanut Brittle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/890920.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Sugar
1 cup Light corn syrup
1 cup Water
2 cups Spanish peanuts Unroasted (raw)
1/4 tsp Salt
1 tsp Butter
1/4 tsp Baking soda

Directions:
Combine sugar, corn syrup and water in heavy skillet. Cook slowly (medium-medium high heat), stirring till sugar dissolves. Cook to soft -ball stage 234-240 degrees (use a candy thermometer)
Add peanuts and salt. Cook slowly (medium high heat) to hard-crack stage 300-310 degrees (watch temp closely)
Add butter and soda stir to blend (mixture will raise and bubble make sure your pan can accomidate)
Pour onto "foil covered - buttered" cookie sheet pull to edges with fork until thin.

Submitter: 
Ruth Jones
Description: 
I've used this same recipe for many years! Always is a hit, crunchy, not chewy.
