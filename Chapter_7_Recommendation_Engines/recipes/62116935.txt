Title:
Mom's Hot Layered Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Can chili or refried beans
1 lb. ground beef
8 oz. salsa
2 8 oz. bricks cream cheese (you can use light), softened
4 cups cheese
1/2 C diced green onions (garnish)
1/2 C diced olives (garnish)

Directions:
Cook ground beef until browned.  In 9x13 pan, layer beans or chili.  Spread ground beef over beans.  Spread salsa over beef.  Spread cream cheese over salsa.  Sprinkle cheese over cream cheese.  Garnish with onions and olives.  Bake at 350F for about 30 minutes or until cheese is bubbly.  Serve with taco chips.

Submitter: 
Kris Frame Bethune
Description: 
I have to give credit to mom, she has been cooking this up for years.  Baked cream cheese gives it that special taste.
