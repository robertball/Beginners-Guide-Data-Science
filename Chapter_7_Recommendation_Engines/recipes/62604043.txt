Title:
Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup vegan margarine, softened
3 TBS vanilla flavored soy milk
1 1/4 cup sugar (vegan brand)
1 TBS Molasses
2 Tsp Vanilla Extract
2 1/2 cups flour (vegan brand)
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups chocolate chips (vegan brand)
or enough chocolate chips to your liking :)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Cream the first 4 ingredients together with mixer.
Then slowly add the remaining dry ingredients.
After dough is well mixed, fold in chocolate chips.
Drop by spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake 8-10 minutes
Cool on rack then enjoy :)

Submitter: 
Jena LaBree
Description: 
After trying several different vegan recipes that I came across, I think this combination I created from them works the best. I have been making these over and over again and they always turn out fabulous! Everyone loves it and they can't tell its vegan!
