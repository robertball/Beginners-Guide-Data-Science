Title:
Banana Pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Bananas (cut in half)
1 whole bag of chocolate chips(melted)
Sprinkles
Nuts

Directions:
Cut the bananas in half, melt the chocolate in a chocolate melter, dip the bananas in the chocolate, after dipped in chocolate roll in sprinkles or nuts,and place on parchment or wax paper, put in freezer for 5 minutes.
