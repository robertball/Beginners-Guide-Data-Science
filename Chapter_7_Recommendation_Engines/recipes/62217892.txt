Title:
Oven-fried Zucchini Sticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Canola oil cooking spray
1/2C whole-wheat flour
1/2C all-purpose flour
2T cornmeal
1t. salt
1/2t. pepper
1 1/2# zucchini (about 3 medium, cut into 1/2" x 3" sticks)
2 egg whites, lightly beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 475 degrees. Coat large baking sheet w/cooking spray. Combine flours, cornmeal, salt and pepper in large, sealable plastic bag. Dip zucchini in egg whites and shake in bag to coat. Arrange, not touching, on baking sheet. Coat all exposed sides w/spray. Bake in center rack for 7 minutes. Turn, spray any flour spots w/spray. Continue baking until golden and just tender, about 5 minutes more. Serve hot.
