Title:
Quick Homemade Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbs. Wesson Oil
1/2 clove garlic minced
1 Lb. ground beef
1/2 C. chopped onions
2 (8oz) cans tomato sauce
1 tsp. salt
1 1/2 tsp. chili powder
1/2 med. green pepper, chopped
1(15oz) can kidney beans, undrained

Directions:
Cook garlic and meat in large skillet until meat is cooked through. Add beans with liquid, onions and green pepper. Pour in Tomato sauce. Add salt and chili powder and stir. Simmer over low heat. stirring occasionally until chile is of desired thickness (about 45 minutes.)
