Title:
Potato Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small to medium potato
water
2  3 pounds powdered sugar
Peanut butter approx. 1 cup(enough to cover once potato mixture is rolled out)

Directions:
Peel potato and throw peelings away.
Wash potato and dice into small pieces and put into small pan.
Pour water into pan to cover potatoes.
Bring to a rumbling boil and set on cool burner, cover with lid till cooled (about 2 hours)
Pour water off potatoes and place into large bowl.
Mash the potatoes.
Add in powdered sugar only 4 – 5 tablespoons at a time till thick enough to knead.
Knead 4 to 5 times adding powdered sugar as needed to form a dough.
Turn out onto surface (wax paper is ideal) dusted with powdered sugar and roll out ¼ to ½ inch thick in a 18 x 12 inch rectangle.  Spread peanut butter evenly over top (add peanut butter to your taste).
Roll up long ways with powdered sugar hands into a long roll and transfer onto long pan and refrigerate over night.
Cut into ¼ to ½ slices and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Dawn Raegene Mendenhall Scott
Description: 
My Mom made this for us when we were growing up.  It is easy and fun to make.  The best part is it only calls for 3 ingredients.  I made it for my kids and they love it.  I will give this 1 warning:  Extremely sweet and very hard to say no once you have had some.
