Title:
asian potstickers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package wonton skins
3/4 lb ground pork
1 bunch scallions
1 can water chestnuts
1 1inch piece ginger, peeled
1 can chicken broth
1 teaspoon sesame oil
2 cloves minced garlic
2 tablespoons cilantro, chopped
1 cup soy sauce
2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
1 teaspoon sesame seeds
1/2 teaspoon salt and pepper
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1/2 cup water
3 tablespoons sugar
1 egg, beaten

Directions:
combine soy sauce, water, hoisin sauce, sesame seeds, sesame oil, ginger, red pepper flakes, sugar, and half of the garlic in a spill proof container. shake well and set aside. chop scallions and water chestnuts finely. add meat, salt and pepper and 4 tablespoons of sauce mixture.
take wonton skins and add small amount of meat to center of each wonton. seal with egg on all sides. in a frying pan with a lid, heat 1 tablespoon of oil on medium high heat. place wontons in oil, in a circle. cook for 2 minutes. do not move around. add some of the broth and quickly cover with lid. cook for 2 minutes and remove. repeat until all the wontons are used. serve with sauce

Submitter: 
merri rosenthal
Description: 
this is my most requested appetizer to bring to any party. you can make them the traditional way or deep fry them. they are tasty however you make them.
