Title:
Apple/Nut Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c diced apples
1/4 c butter
1/2 c chpt nuts
1 c sugar
1/4 c milk
1 egg
1 c flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
cream sugar and butter, add egg and beat well
add apples
sift flour, salt, spices and baking soda together and add to mixture. add milk, nuts and mix well
bake in shallow pan @325 for 45 min.
top with whip cream   ice cream   or frosting
