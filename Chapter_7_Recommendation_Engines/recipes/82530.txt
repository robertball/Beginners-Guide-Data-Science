Title:
Cheese and Veggie Open-Faced Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 medium tomato, diced
1 stalk celery, chopped
1 green bell pepper, finely chopped
1 cup shredded reduced-fat Cheddar cheese
1/4 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
4 slices SARA LEE® Soft & Smooth™ Whole Grain White Bread

Directions:
Preheat your oven's broiler.
In a medium bowl, stir together the onion, tomato, celery, bell pepper, cheese, chili powder, and salt. Arrange slices of bread on a baking sheet or broiling pan. Top each one with 1/4 of the mixture, spreading out to the edges.
Broil for 4 to 5 minutes, or until cheese melts. Watch closely to prevent burning. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Sara Lee Soft & Smooth™
Description: 
Garden fresh veggies -- tomatoes, peppers, celery -- top these easy open-faced cheese sandwiches. Great for after school snacks.
