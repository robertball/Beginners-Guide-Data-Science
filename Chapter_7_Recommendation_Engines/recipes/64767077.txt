Title:
Slow cooker northern bean and ham soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups or 1 small package northern beans
I large onion chopped
2 cups chopped ham cubed
2 large  carrots cubed
4 stalks celery  chopped
3 small potatoes cubed
1 tablespoon garlic powder or granular  garlic
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon  ground black pepper
2 tablespoons ham soup base L.B.   Jamesons

Directions:
Rinse beans
Chop onion
Add beans and onion to crock pot
Boil 8 cups water
Add water to crock pot
Add 1 teaspoon salt 1/2 teaspoon black ground pepper
Cover and cook on high for 2 hours
Lower heat to low continue cooking for about 6 more hours
Cover carrots , celery , and potatoes  with boiling water for 5 minutes
Drain water add carrots, celery,and potatoes to crock pot
Add 1 tablespoon  garlic powder or granular  garlic
Add 2 tablespoons ham soup base
Cover and cook on low until vegetables are tender
Serve with bread
