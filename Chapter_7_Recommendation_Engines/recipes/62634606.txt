Title:
Chocolate Sheet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Stick Oleo
3 1/2  Tbs Cocoa
1/2  Cup Crisco
1   Cup Water
Dash Cinnimon
Bring these ingredients to a boil and pour over
2  Cups Flour
2   Cups sugar
Beat in mixer, let mixture cool a few min while mixing.
Add 2 eggs
1 tsp Vanilla
Beat Well
Add 1/2  Cup Butter Milk mixed with 1 tsp Baking Soda
Can also sub Reg milk with 1 tsp vinegar for Buttermilk.

Directions:
Follow Directions as listed above.
Pour on a greased and floured baking pan.
Bake 20 min in preheated 400 Deg oven
