Title:
Mary's Slice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Base:
1 cup margarine, melted
1 cup brown sugar
2 cups flour
1 tsp. vanilla
Topping:
chocolate chips, about 1 cup (could substitute for other flavored chips)
chopped walnuts, pecans, almonds, coconut, colored sprinkles, etc. or combo.

Directions:
Base:
Mix base ingredients. Pat into greased 9" x 13" pan.
Bake @ 350 x 11-14 minutes. Check often. Take out when puffy - not overbaked.
Topping:
Sprinkle enough chocolate chips over hot base to almost cover it.
Wait a few minutes until melted. Spread with the back of a spoon.
Sprinkle walnuts, pecans, almonds, coconut or colored sprinkles over chocolate while still melted.
Cool.
Can be doubled & baked in 10" x 15" cookie sheet.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A potluck favorite. Chocolate sprinkled with nuts, etc. over a caramelly brown sugar shortbread base.
