Title:
Graham Cracker torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 graham crackers(finley ground)
1/41b melted butter
1lb marshmellows
1C orange jucie
1pkg knox gelatine
2Tbsp water
1 pt. cream (whipped)

Directions:
Make the crust, mix the crackers with the butter and pat into a 13x9in pan.(save 3Tbsp of crumbs for the top) Bake at 350 for 8min.
warm the jucie in a large saucepan and add the marshmellows, then let sit 15min. Stirring occasionally. Disolove the geletin in the water and then add to the marshmellows. Fold in the whipped cream until jucie disappers. Pour into the baked curst. Then top with  the remaining crumbs. Chill 2 hours.
