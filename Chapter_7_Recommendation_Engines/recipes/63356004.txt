Title:
Oyster Mushroom Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T. oil
2 shallots, thinly sliced
2 tsp. minced garlic
5 oz. oyster mushrooms, sliced or coarsely chopped
1 c. grated mozza
8 fresh oregano or basil leave, roughly chopped
1/2 c. parmesan, grated
1 large slightly pre-cooked pizza crust (5 minutes) (or two small Boboli)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Heat oil over med. high heat. Saute shallots and garlic for 1 minute. add mushrooms and cook just to soften, stirring frequently, 1 to 2 minutes.
Sprinkle pizza crust with mozza, then spread mushroom mix over it. Scatter basil or oregano on top, sprinkle with Parm, and bake 8 to 10 minutes till cheese melts.
