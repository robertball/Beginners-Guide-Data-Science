Title:
Easy Honey Mustard Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter, melted
1/3 cup honey
1/4 cup prepared mustard
4 teaspoons curry powder
1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts

Directions:
Combine the melted butter/margarine, honey, mustard, curry powder and cayenne powder. Mix well.
Place chicken breasts in a 9x13 inch baking dish and pour
honey/mustard mixture over chicken.
Cover with tin foil, and bake in 350 degree oven until chicken is cooked, about 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
LMLSCA
Description: 
This is already and easy recipe, but you can also make the sauce, pour over the chicken in a freezer bag and freeze. To prepare, thaw and cook as called for in the recipe. This makes this recipe even easier.
