Title:
Easy Cake Mix Cookies- Basic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg cake mix, 2 layer size
1/2 c soft shortening or butter
1 tbsp water
2 eggs
Optional: 1 c chopped nuts, flaky coconut, or chocolate or butterscotch bits.

Directions:
Empty half of cake mix into bowl and add remaining ingredients.  Blend well. Add balance of cake mix and beat until smooth. Blen in optional ingredients. Drop teaspoonfuls on greased cooke sheets.
