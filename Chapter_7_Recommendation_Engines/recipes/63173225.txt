Title:
Arancini(rice balls) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound uncooked rice
1 pound ground veal browned and drained
3-4 cups tomato sauce
1 cup of cooked peas
salt and pepper
1/2 cup grated reggianno parmasaen cheese
2 eggs well beaten
1 cup Italian seasoned breadcrumbs
2 cups canola oil for frying

Directions:
Cook rice in unsalted water till grains crush between fingers.Rice should be mushy when squeezed.
combine tomatoe sauce, browned veal and peas in saucepan. Heat til boiled and simmer 15 min.Sauce should be thick and not runny.Cool.
Add parmesan cheese, salt and pepper to rice and mix well
Place 2 tablespoons cooked rice mixture in the palm of your hand.
Make a well and place 1 tablespoon of meat /sauce mixture onto rice.Mold rice around meat mixture to completeley enclose.
Dip rice balls in beaten eggs and roll in seasoned breadcrumbs.Place on wax paper lined bake sheet.
Heat oil in deep wide saucepan til at least 375F and fry rice balls til golden brown on all sides about 2-3 minutes. Drain on paper towel lined plate.
