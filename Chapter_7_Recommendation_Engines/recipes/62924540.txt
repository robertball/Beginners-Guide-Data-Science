Title:
The Best Little Beef Sates In Singapore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/749086.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs.ribeye steak
3 Tbsp.light brown sugar
2 Tbsp.ground corriander
1 Tbsp. turmeric
1 1/2 tsp.ground cumin
1 1/2 tsp.freshly ground pepper
3 Tbsp.asian fish sauce
3 Tbsp.oil

Directions:
Cut steak into 1" cubes and place into non-metal bowl.Stir in all ingredients.Let marinate in fridge,covered,for at least 2 hours.
Soak wooden skewers in water.
Drain steak,discard marinade.Thread steak onto skewers.Keep in fridge till ready for use.
Heat grill to high.Brush and oil grate.
Grill 1-2 minutes per side for medium rare.
Serve with Singapore peanut sauce.
