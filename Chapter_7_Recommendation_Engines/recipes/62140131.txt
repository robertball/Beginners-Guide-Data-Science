Title:
Sunset Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Bottle (750 ML) Rose or any blush wine
1/2 C. Vodka
1/4 C. Triple Sec
1 C. Pineapple juice
1/2 C. Simple syrup (recipes follows)
1 Fresh orange sliced into rounds
1 Fresh lemon sliced into rounds
1 Fresh lime  sliced into rounds
1 package fresh raspberries
Basic Simple Syrup:
1 C. White Sugar
1 C. Water

Directions:
Combine all the liquid ingredients into large pitcher, and chill for at least 1 hour.
Add fruit at least 30 minutes before serving, the longer the fruit marinates the better.
Serve over ice and enjoy!
Basic Simple Syrup Directions:
In a small saucepan, bring sugar and water to a boil; simmer until the sugar is dissolved, appx. 3 minutes. Remove from the heat and let cool completely.
The syrup can be refrigerated in a glass jar for up to 1 month.

Submitter: 
Celeste
Description: 
This tropical sangria is great at any party, or on a hot day!
