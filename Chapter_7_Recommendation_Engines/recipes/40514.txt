Title:
Mac & Cheese Stuffed Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986380.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package STOUFFER'S® frozen Macaroni and Cheese, cooked according to package directions
4 large fresh tomatoes
4 slices bacon, fully cooked, drained and crumbled
1/4 cup chopped green onions
1/4 cup fresh, coarse breadcrumbs
2 tablespoons shredded Cheddar cheese
2 teaspoons butter or margarine, melted

Directions:
HOLLOW out tomatoes, leaving a 1/4-inch wall around sides and bottom. Place in 9-inch-round cake pan to prevent tipping.
COMBINE bread crumbs, cheese and butter in small bowl; sprinkle mixture evenly on top of each tomato.
COMBINE hot Macaroni and Cheese, bacon and onion in medium bowl; fill tomatoes with mixture.
BAKE in preheated 350 degrees F. oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Stouffer's®
Description: 
These stuffed tomatoes are a great idea for family gatherings or a party.
