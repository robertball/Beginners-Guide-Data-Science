Title:
Cherry Cheesecake Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4575220.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow cream (such as Marshmallow Fluff®)
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®), thawed
1 (12 ounce) can cherry pie filling
1 individual package graham crackers

Directions:
Mix cream cheese and marshmallow cream until smooth. Fold in whipped topping. Spread into a serving dish. Cover with cherry pie filling. Break graham crackers into sticks and arrange them around the dip.

Submitter:

Description: 
Whip up this creamy cherry cheesecake dip with marshmallow cream and cherry pie filling for a quick and easy dessert that'll sweeten any potluck.
