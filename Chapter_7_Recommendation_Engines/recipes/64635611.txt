Title:
Fettuccine Scallop Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs scallop (medium sized)
2 jars of Catelli Alfredo Sauce (garlic)
1 cup mushroom (chopped)
1 tbsp garlic (minced)
3 green onions
2 tbsp sundried tomatoes
2 tbsp butter
2 tbsp parsley
1 tsp basil
1.5 boxes fettuccine noodles

Directions:
1. Boil water and noodles
2. Melt butter in hot skillet/tall pan
3. Brown garlic, tomatoes, basil, parsley
4. Add scallops to pan (2-3 minutes each side)
5. Remove from pan
6. Saute mushrooms and onions
7. Lower heat and add Alfredo sauce
8. Stir often
9. When sauce is hot, add the scallops until they have warmed up
10. Serve over noodles
