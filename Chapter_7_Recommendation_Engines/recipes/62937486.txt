Title:
Caramel Latte Milk Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups coffee Ice cream, slightly softened
1/3 cup milk
3 Tbsp Caramel Sauce
1/4 cup whipped cream

Directions:
Combine milk, ice cream and caramel sauce in a blender and blend until smooth.
Pour in to 2 glasses, top each glass with whipped cream and drizzle with caramel sauce.
