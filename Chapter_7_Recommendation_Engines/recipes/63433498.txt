Title:
Kiwi & Tangerine Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 - 4 Kiwis, peeled and diced
4 small or 2 medium tangerines, peeled and diced
1/2 cup red bell pepper
1/4 cup chopped cilantro
1/4 teaspoon salt
poppyseed dressing, drizzled

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine all the ingredients. Chill briefly before serving.

Submitter: 
Vincent Appel
Description: 
A fruit salsa that goes well on tortilla chips.
