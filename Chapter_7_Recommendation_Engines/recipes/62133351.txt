Title:
Apple Cinnamon French Toast Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices cinnamon bread, with or without raisins
4 eggs, lightly whisked
1 cup half and half
1/2 cup milk
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1 tbsp sugar
1 tsp vanilla
2 Golden Delicious apples, peeled and cored, chopped or sliced
Topping:
1 stick butter, room temp
1/2 cup light brown sugar, packed
1/2 cup pecans, chopped
2 tbsp maple syrup
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg

Directions:
Spray 8x8 baking dish to coat.
Layer 4 slices of bread on the bottom of baking dish; sprinkle with half the apples, and repeat one time.
Combine eggs, half and half, milk, cinnamon, nutmeg, and vanilla in a bowl. Mix well. Pour over the bread. Cover with foil and set in fridge overnight.
The next day, pull out of fridge 1 hour before baking.
Make topping by mixing all the ingredients into a bowl. Spread over the top layer of apples.
Bake at 400 degrees uncovered 30-40 minutes, until done, puffed and golden.

Submitter: 
KristenB
Description: 
This is a great brunch dish. Comfort food at its best. This does not need any additional syrup and is always a big hit.
