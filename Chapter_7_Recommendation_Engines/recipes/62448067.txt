Title:
Indonesian Ginger Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c soy
1/4 c canola oil
1/4 c lemon juice (1 lemon)
2 t lemon zest
2 t dijon mustard
1/4 c ginger minced
4 cloves garlic minced

Directions:
mix all; marinate fish/chicken for couple hours (don't over-marinate fish to avoid it falling apart);
grill
