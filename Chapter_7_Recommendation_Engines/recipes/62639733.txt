Title:
Creamy Potato, Ham and Broccoli Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/605781.jpg
Ingredients:
1 15 oz. jar Creamy Alfredo sauce
1 teaspoon dried Italian seasonings
1/2 cup milk
3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
4 russet potatoes,peeled and sliced to 1/8"
1 1/2 - 2 cups diced ham
1 1/2 cup green beans OR
1 1/2 cups fresh broccoli OR
1 1/2 frozen peas
2 cups (+/-) shredded mozzarella OR Swiss cheese
TOPPING
1 cup Italian seasoned breadcrumbs
1 Tablespoon butter
1 Tablespoon grated parmesan
OR French fried onion rings

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
Lightly grease a 9x9 inch baking dish.
In a small bowl, whisk together the Alfredo sauce,Italian seasonings, salt, pepper, Parm and milk.
In a large bowl, combine ham, vegetable and shredded
cheese; pour Alfredo mixture over all and toss to mix well.
Arrange evenly in baking dish.
Cover and bake at 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 1 hour
While casserole is baking, melt butter in a small skillet, add the breadcrumbs and parmesan and brown. Uncover, top with toasted bread crumbs and bake at 350 degrees F (175 degree C) for additional 30 minutes, or until potatoes are tender.
OPTION:  Top with french onion rings for the last 15 minutes in place of the toasted bread crumbs.
Let stand 10 to 15 minutes before serving.
