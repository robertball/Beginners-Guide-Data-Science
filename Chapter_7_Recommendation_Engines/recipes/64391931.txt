Title:
Grandma's Taco Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound ground beef
1 small onion
12oz tomato paste
1 can corn, drained
1 pkg taco seasoning mix
8oz tortilla chips
2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Brown beef and onion.  Stir in tomato paste and 3 cans of water.  Add taco seasoning and corn.  Bring to a boil.  Layer beef mixture, corn chips, and 1 cup cheese in 9 x 13 casserole dish. Put the rest of the cheese on top.
Bake 10 minutes at 350 degrees.
Top with salsa and/or sour cream, fresh avocado slices, olives... the possibilities are endless.
