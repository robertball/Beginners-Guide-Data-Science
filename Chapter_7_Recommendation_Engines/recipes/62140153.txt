Title:
Spaghetti Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cooked spaghetti
1 pound of cooked shredded chicken
1/4 cuup diced onion
1/4 cup diced green pepper
2 jars of your favorite spaghetti sauce
1/2 tsp. basil
1/2 tsp. oregano
1 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. pepper
salt too taste
RESERVE following ingredients for topping.
1 Tablespoon each grated Ramono & parmasean cheese
3/4 cup each shredded cheddar and mozzarella cheese

Directions:
RESERVE ramono and parmesean cheeses, cheddar and mozzarella cheeses for topping just before baking.
Boil one pound spahgetti acording too package directions drain well, After removing sphegetti from pot, mix all other ingredients toghether in same pot while sphagetti is draining.  Heat too a simmer, then add drained sphaghetti.  stir well.  Pour into 9x13 glass baking dish,  sprinkle top with ramono and parmesean cheese, then top with cheddar and mozzarela shredded cheese.  Bake 350 degrees for 30 minutes until heated through and cheese is melted.

Description: 
a twist on traditional spaghetti easy to prepare then bake. Use chicken for a change in flavor, I usually have these ingredients on hand. Serve with garlic bread and a salad. yummy!
