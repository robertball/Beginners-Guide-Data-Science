Title:
Lemon Pie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1023670.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups flour
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
1 cup butter, softened
4 eggs
1 1/2 cups sugar
1/2 cup lemon juice
1 tablespoon lemon zest

Directions:
Mix 2 cups of flour and confectioner's sugar together.
Cut in the butter or margarine.
Mix well until the dough resembles pie dough consistency.
Press the dough into a 9x13 inch baking pan.
Bake at 350Â° for 15 to 20 minutes or until golden brown.
Beat together eggs, sugar, 4 tablespoons flour, lemon juice and lemon rind for at least 1 minute.
Pour the mixture over the baked crust.
Bake the bars another 20 minutes, or until the lemon topping has set.
Sprinkle with confectioner's sugar when cooled.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Tangy lemon filling on a buttery short bread crust. This is a really easy recipe that my mom made before the lemon bar 'rage' was on!Modified from: Lemon Pie Bars
