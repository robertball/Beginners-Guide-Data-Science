Title:
CLAM FRITTERS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:

Directions:
My stepmother got this recipe from an 80-year-old woman back in the 1940's.
1 egg, beaten
1 cup minced clams
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup flour
1/4 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
1-2 Tbs. chopped onion
Chopped green pepper (optional)
Beat egg and add flour gradually. Slowly add milk and remaining ingredients. Make into fritters (patties) of desired size; pan-fry in oil; drain well.
