Title:
David's Pancake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c flour
1 c milk
4 eggs
dash of nutmeg
2 tbsp mixed cinnamon and sugar

Directions:
Beat ingredients until smooth.  Heat oven to 400 degrees.
2.  Put 1 stick of margarine in a 9x13 pan and melt it in the oven.
3.  Pour the prepared batter into the melted butter in the cake pan, but do not stir!
4.  Bake for 15 min.  Remove from oven. Top with apple pie filling and cinnamon and sugar, or bananas, walnuts, and cinnamon/sugar.
5.  Return to oven for 5 more minutes.
