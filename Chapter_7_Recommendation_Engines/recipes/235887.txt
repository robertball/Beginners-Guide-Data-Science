Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces Irish cream liqueur (such as BaileysÂ®)
2 fluid ounces vanilla-flavored vodka
2 fluid ounces cinnamon schnapps
2 fluid ounces butterscotch schnapps
1/2 cup crushed ice, or as needed

Directions:
Pour Irish cream liqueur, vanilla-flavored vodka, cinnamon schnapps, and butterscotch schnapps into a cocktail shaker; add ice. Cover shaker, shake, and strain drink into a martini glass.
