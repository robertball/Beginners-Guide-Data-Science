Title:
Baked Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. brown sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 c. butter (I use about 2/3 of a stick)
3 c. oatmeal
1 c. milk
2 tsp baking pwd
1 tsp salt
vanilla, cinnamon and other spices as desired

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.  Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.  Serve with milk.
