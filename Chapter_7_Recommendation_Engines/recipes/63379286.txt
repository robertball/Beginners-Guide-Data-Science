Title:
Cream of Chicken Soup with Almonds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 chicken breast (skin on)
broth from chicken or 3 or 4 cans of chicken broth
1 small box of frozen peas & carrots
1 large jar diced pimento
1 small bag of sliced almonds
1 T.Chicken Seasoning (not poultry seasoning but chicken)
2 large chopped onions
4 ribs of chopped celery
2 sticks of margarine
1 quart Half & Half
2 pints Heavy Whipping Cream
1 1/2 T. chopped garlic
1 t. salt
1 t. pepper
1 T. garlic powder
2 T. flour

Directions:
Boil chicken breasts. Take out, skin & chop in chunks, set aside
On medium heat, in a large heavy duty soup pot, bring to boil: chicken broth, milk & seasoning.
Add peas & carrots
While vegetables cook, saute onions, celery, chopped garlic, in one stick of margarine.  Add to soup when done.
Drain pimento, add to soup
Make a rue by melting 1 stick of margarine on medium heat, add flour as if you were making pan gravy. Stir until it turns a golden color, stiring constantly, do not burn your rue.
Add slowly to simmering soup, while continuing to stir. Cook until soup thickens. If it does not thicken enough, use some corn starch as a thickener.
Add chopped chicken the last 5 minutes.
Ladel soup in bowls and sprinkle almonds on top, do not add to soup pot itself, but in each individual soup bowl right before served.
