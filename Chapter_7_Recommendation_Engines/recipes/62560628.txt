Title:
Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/536151.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey breast, cooked
1 3/4 c water
1/2 c onions, chopped
6 ozs no-salt-added tomato paste
1/2 c bell peppers, chopped
1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
1/4 c celery, chopped
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp olive oil
1/4 tsp salt
15 ozs canned dark red kidney beans, undrained
1/4 tsp garlic powder
14 1/2 ozs canned crushed tomatoes

Directions:
Place turkey, onions, bell peppers, and celery in a large saucepan with oil.
Cook over medium heat for ten minutes, stirring and separating turkey as it
cooks. Add beans, tomatoes, water, tomato paste, worcestershire sauce, chili
powder, salt, and garlic powder. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer for 30
minutes.
