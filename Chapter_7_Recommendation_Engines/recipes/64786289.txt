Title:
Dad's Homemade Pull Apart Rolls - Bread Machine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7.5 oz water
3 cups bread flour
2 TBL dry milk
3.5 TBL sugar
1 tea salt
1 TBL wheat glutin
3 TBL butter for dough
3 TBL butter for basting
2 tea active yeast

Directions:
1. Combine ingredients and let sit for 10-15 minutes on floored countertop.
2.  Apply non-stick spray into muffin tins.   Form dough into walnut size balls and combine 2-3 in muffin tin.
3.  Melt butter and brush on top.   Put non-stick wrap over the top and let rise until 2x in size (45 minutes to 1 hour).
4.  Bake on 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes (golden brown on top)
