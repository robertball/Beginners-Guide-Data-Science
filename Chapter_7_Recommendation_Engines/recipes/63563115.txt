Title:
Pear and Cheese Tartlet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbs. butter
1 large onion, small dice
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. white sugar
1 clove garlic, minced
1 Tbs. mango chutney
1 tsp. aged balsamic vinegar
1 pear, small dice
3 oz. crumbled blue cheese
3 oz. crumbled goat cheese
4 oz. cream cheese, softened
2/3 cup chopped walnuts
12 tartlet shells, frozen*

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium high heat, melt the butter, and then add the onions, salt and sugar.
Cook until golden brown, stirring often.
Add the garlic and reduce the heat to medium. Cook for 2 minutes.
Mix in the chutney and the vinegar and remove from the heat.
Preheat the oven to 375 F.
Remove tartlet shells from freezer and place on a parchment lined baking sheet.
Combine the onion mixture and the blue, goat and cream cheeses.
Spoon the filling into shells.
Bake at 375 F for 15 to 20 minutes, or until the filling is melted and slightly browned.
Can be served warm or at room temperature.

Submitter: 
Doc Simonson
Description: 
These savory tarts of pear and cheese are complimented by the caramelized onions, chutney and balsamic vinegar. Sweet, rich and luscious, these decadent appetizers will become a favorite!
