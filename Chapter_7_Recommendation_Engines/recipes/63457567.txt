Title:
Fudge Drop Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ounces semisweet (or bittersweet) chocolate pieces
6 tablespoons butter
14oz can sweetened condensed milk
1 cup all-purpose
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
On top of double boiler, over hot water, melt chocolate and butter.
Stir in sweetened condensed milk.
Blend in flour vanilla and nuts.
Mix well.
Chill in refrigerator until firm enough to scoopt onto baking sheets.
Bake for 10 minutes.
Yield 5 1/2 dozen.

Submitter: 
AllieGeekPi
Description: 
If you love fudge, you'll love these cookies!  Wonderful addition to any Christmas cookie platter.  These fudgy cookies are simple to prepare using few ingredients.  They are a decadent treat.
