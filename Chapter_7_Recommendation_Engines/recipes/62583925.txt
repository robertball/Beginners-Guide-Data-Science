Title:
Carol's Chinese Cabbage Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cabbage, sliced
2 packages dry ramen
8 Tablespoons slivered almonds, toasted
8 Tablespoons sesame seeds, toasted
1 bundle green onions, chopped
Dressing:
1 cup salad oil
6 Tablespoons rice vinegar
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons Accent
4 Tablespoons sugar
1 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Mix salad ingredients. Add dressing immediately before serving and toss.
