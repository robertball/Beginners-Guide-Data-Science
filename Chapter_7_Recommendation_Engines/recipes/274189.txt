Title:
Chocolate Banana Sorbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6711641.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ripe bananas, peeled and frozen
1/4 cup cocoa powder
3 tablespoons powdered sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon instant coffee granules (optional)

Directions:
Combine bananas, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and instant coffee in a powerful blender such as a Vitamix(R). Using the tamper, blend until all ingredients are incorporated. Serve immediately or freeze in a freezer-safe container for 3 hours for a firmer texture.

Submitter: 
Yoly
Description: 
Satisfy your sweet tooth with this quick, easy, and delicious sorbet. Best of all, you can make it with only 4 ingredients and it's ready to eat immediately or place in your freezer for later.
