Title:
Big Ray's Rhubarb Oat Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1132041.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups chopped fresh or frozen rhubarb
2 cup packed brown sugar, divided
1/2 cup water, divided
2 tsp lemon juice
8 tsp cornstarch
2 cup old-fashioned oats
1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup flaked coconut
1 egg
1 cup of chopped pecans
1 tsp salt
2/3 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large saucepan, combine the rhubarb, 1 cup brown sugar, 1/4 cup water and lemon juice. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium; cook and stir for 4-5 minutes or until rhubarb is tender.
Combine the cornstarch and remaining water until smooth; gradually stir into rhubarb mixture. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened. Remove from the heat; set aside.
In a large bowl, combine the oats, flour, coconut, salt and remaining brown sugar. Stir in butter until mixture is crumbly. Divide the mixture in half. Add egg to one of the mixture and mix well.  Press that half into a greased 9x13 in baking dish.  Add chopped pecans to other half and Spread with rhubarb mixture.  Add chopped pecans to other half and sprinkle the remaining half of the oat mixture on top and press down lightly.
Bake at 350Â° for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown. Cool on a wire rack. Cut into squares
