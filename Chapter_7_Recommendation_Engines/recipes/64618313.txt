Title:
Prime Rib Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Left over prime rib, cut into bite sized pieces
2 left over baked potatoes, sliced
Fresh mushrooms, sliced
Green pepper, cut into strips
One saut√©ed onion
One cup sour cream
Horseradish
One pizza crust
2 cups of cheese, any kind (swiss, mozzarella provolone)

Directions:
Prepare crust as directed. Mix sour cream with a tablespoon of horseradish. Use more or less to your own taste. Top with sliced potatoes. Sprinkle one cup of cheese. Next layer of prime rib, mushrooms, peppers and onions. Top with remaining cheese. Bake at 400 degrees until cheese is melted and crust is done.

Submitter: 
Lisa K
Description: 
This delicious pizza is an old favorite created by my brother in law. Tastes like the whole dinner in a beautiful slice!!
