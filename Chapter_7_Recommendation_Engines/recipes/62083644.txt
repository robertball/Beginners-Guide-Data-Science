Title:
Andrea's Pumpkin Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can pumpkin
2 cups sugar
2 eggs, beaten
2 TBS milk
2 tsp vanilla
1 cup oil
4 cups flour
2 tsp baking soda
4 tsp baking powder
2 tsp cinnamon
1 pkg mini choc chips
1 cup nuts (optional)

Directions:
Measure dry ingredients first and set aside.  In seperate bowl add all wet ingredients and mix well.  Gradually add four mixture until well blended.  Add mini chips (and nuts) and drop by tablespoons on to ungreased cookie sheet. Cook for 9 to 11 minutes

Submitter: 
Shann
Description: 
The easiest and best tasting pumpkin cookies i've ever made.  Got the recipe from a co-worker along with her permission to post it here.  Makes a large volume of cookies for school bake sales and they freeze very well also.
