Title:
Cranberry Orange Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. fresh cranberries
1 c. sugar
Juice of 1 navel orange
Processed orange peel
Water to make 1 cup liquid

Directions:
1. Squeeze the orange and measure the juice.  Add water to bring to 1 cup.
2. Whirl the peel in a food processor.  Mix cranberries, sugar, juice, peel and water in a large saucepan.
3. Cook and stir over medium high heat until the cranberries pop and the sauce thickens.
4. Pour into serving dish and cover with plastic wrap.
5. Cool to room temperature then chill if desired.
6. May be served at room temperature or cold.
7. May also be poured into sterile sealer jars and sealed with sterile new lids and stored for future use or to give as home canned gifts.

Submitter: 
SEAMSTERRIFIC
Description: 
This is my adaptation of a very old English recipe.  It adds a really different touch to the holiday dinner table and it's also wonderful used as a low sugar jam on toast.
