Title:
Horseradish Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983555.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound processed cheese, cubed
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup horseradish
Assorted crackers

Directions:
Melt cheese in the top of a double boiler. Remove the heat. Stir in mayonnaise and horseradish. Pour into a small crock or ceramic bowl. Chill. Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Margie  Wampler
Description: 
Here's a recipe that's simple, but oh-so-good! This tangy cheese spread has a wonderful combination of flavors that taste even better when served with crisp rye crackers. It's a fine appetizer or snack!
