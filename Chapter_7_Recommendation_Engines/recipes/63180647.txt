Title:
Mom's Southern Pineapple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pretzel Topping:
1 C. pretzels, crushed (not too fine)
1/2 C. brown sugar
3/4 stick butter
Salad:
8 oz. cream cheese
1/2 C. sugar
tall can drained crushed pineapple
8 oz. whipped topping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix pretzels, brown sugar, and butter in 13x9 pan.  Bake for 7 minutes.  Stir well immediately after removing from oven.  Stir a few more times as they cool.  Set aside.
Cream together cream cheese and sugar.  Stir in drained pineapple and whipped topping.  Chill in serving bowl.  Just before serving, stir in pretzels, reserving some for topping.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
Creamy pineapple dessert with candied pretzels.
