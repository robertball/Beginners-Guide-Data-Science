Title:
Aphrodite's Dream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Vinaigrette:
1/3 cup olive oil
3 tbsp white wine vinegar
salt
black pepper
sage cayenne pepper
orange juice
salad:
green salad leaves
1 pomegranate
1 orange

Directions:
In a large bowl mix all ingredients for vinagrette. Season with spices and herbs to taste.
wash salad leaves, peel pomegranate and orange. Slice orange in bitesize pieces.
Mix salad greens, orangeand pomegranate with the vinagrette.
