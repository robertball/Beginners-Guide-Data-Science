Title:
Sicilian Style Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Two good cuts of beef steak.
(Fillet,T-Bone or Porterhouse)
1 C. Italian style bread crumbs
1 C. Grated Parmesan cheese
1 Tsp.Seasoning salt
1 Tb. Garlic powder
1/2 C. Olive oil
Place olive oil in a shallow bowl or pie plate.
Combine bread crumbs,cheese seasoning salt and garlic powder in another bowl or pie plate.Mix with your fingers or a fork to disperse evenly.
Dredge the steak through the olive oil on both sides. Drain off excess.
Coat steaks with the dry mixture.
Bake on the center rack of the oven @ 350 degrees F. for 15 min for medium, 20 min for well.

Directions:
