Title:
grandma doris fruit cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter
2 cups of sugar (divided)
1 cup flour
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 egg
1 cup of milk
1 pinch of salt
1 quart of either blueberries, blackberries, peaches,apples, or rubarb (little more or less depending on how many beeries you like)

Directions:
preheat oven to 375F.
place the stick of butter in an 8 by 11.5 by 2in glass dish and melt.
when butter has melted, pour the fruit to cover the bottom of the pan.
pour one cup of the sugar over the fruit (note: if u are using caned fruit it may take less sugar, if using rubarb then add an extra 1/2 cup of sugar to pour over the rubarb).
in a bowl mix the 1 cup of sugar, 1 cup of flour, pinch of salt, 1 teaspoon of vanilla, and the 1 egg, stir well then gradually add in the cup of milk and stir until all blended well. pour the batter over the fruit in the baking pan.
bake in the preheated oven for at least 30 min or until brown on top and firm.
