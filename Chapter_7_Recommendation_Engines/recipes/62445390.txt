Title:
Cucumber Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup plain low-fat yogurt
1/3 cup finely chopped cucumber (about 1/3 of a regular garden cuke)
1/2 tablespoon chopped dill (fresh or freeze dried)
1 clove of garlic, minced
1 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl.  Allow at least 20 minutes for flavors to blend.  May be made an entire day in advance.

Description: 
I like this sauce on falafel.
