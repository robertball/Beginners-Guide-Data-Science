Title:
Hot Strawberry Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983487.jpg
Ingredients:
8 cups apple cider or apple juice
1 (10 ounce) package frozen sliced strawberries
4 inches stick cinnamon
1 teaspoon whole cloves
cinnamon sticks (optional)
Fresh strawberry or apple slices (optional)

Directions:
Combine cider or juice, frozen sliced strawberries, the 4 inches stick cinnamon, and cloves in a large saucepan. Bring to boiling and reduce heat. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes. Strain through a sieve lined with 100-percent-cotton cheesecloth.
To serve, pour cider mixture into eight heatproof glasses or cups. Garnish each serving with a fresh strawberry slice, if desired.

Submitter: 
Better Homes and Gardens
Description: 
If you're looking to warm up after a few trips down the sledding hill with the kids, serve up some of this warm spiced apple-berry beverage.
