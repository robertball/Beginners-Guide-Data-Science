Title:
Raspberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. fresh or frozen raspberries
2-3 T. sugar
1-2 oz. raspberry liqueur
2 t. cornstarch

Directions:
Wash raspberries.  Place in small saucepan with sugar and cook over low heat until it begins to boil.  Remove a small amount of the raspberry liquid into a small bowl and mix with cornstarch until smooth.  Return cornstarch mixture to the raspberries and cook on low heat until the sauce thickens.  Serve while still warm over good quality vanilla bean ice cream.

Submitter: 
Linda Smith
Description: 
This recipe is beautiful, easy and so very delicious as an ice cream or pound cake topping.
