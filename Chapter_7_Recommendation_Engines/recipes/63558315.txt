Title:
Pine Nut Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 med onion chopped
2 talb olive oil
1 cup long grain or basamati rice
1/2 tea salt
1- 14 oz can chicken broth
1/4 cup water
1/2 cup dried apricots chopped- Great Lake Brand
1/3 cup pine nuts toasted dry fry pan (Great Lakes Brand)
1 tablespoon dried basil
1/2 tea pepper
1/2 tea ground cumin

Directions:
Cook onion in oil over low heat.Add rice, salt, broth and water...bring to a boil. Cover turn the heat down to simmer for 15 min to 18 min- till rice is tender and liquid absorbed, remove from heat. add appricots...cover let stand 5 min....stir in nutsbasil and cumin...pepper to taste...
