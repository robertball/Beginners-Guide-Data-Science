Title:
Herbed Lemon Orzo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups orzo
6 Tbsp. chopped fresh parsley
1 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
2 tsp. freshly grated lemon zest
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large pot of boiling water, cook orzo until al dente(about 7 to 9 minutes). Drain well and transfer to a serving bowl. Stir in parsley, oil, and lemon zest. Season with salt and pepper.
