Title:
Bourbon Glazed Chicken w/ Pineapple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of garlic
1 tbsp olive oil
2/3 cup water
1 cup pineapple juice
1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 1/3 cups dark brown sugar
3 tbsp lemon juice
3 tbsp minced onion
1 tbsp jack daniels whiskey
1 tbsp crushed pineapple
4 chicken breasts, split, skinless, flattened
1 pineapple, peeled, cored, sliced

Directions:
Combine all sauce ingredients and stir occasionally until mixture boils, then reduce heat to simmering for 40-50 minutes.
Place sliced pineapple in oven safe pan.  Place chicken breasts on top of pineapple, and bake for 15 minutes.
Glaze chicken with syrup mixture, coating generously.
Cook until internal temp reaches 180 degrees.

Submitter: 
Brenda Laughlin Sumner
Description: 
A recipe that is similar to the popular restaurant chain.  Chicken is baked with pineapple and then glazed with a rich syrup.
