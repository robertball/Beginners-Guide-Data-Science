Title:
Nachos del Sol Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984048.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) container reduced fat sour cream
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning
3/4 pound lean ground beef, browned and cooked
1 1/2 cups Mexican blend cheese
8 ounces shredded lettuce
1 small tomato, diced
1 (12 ounce) package triangle-shaped tortilla chips

Directions:
Mix sour cream and taco seasoning.
Spread mixture on a large plate, then sprinkle with beef, cheese, lettuce and tomatoes.
Place chips with bottom of triangle shape 1/4 inch into the taco mixture to form a sunshine shape around the plate. Serve immediately.
