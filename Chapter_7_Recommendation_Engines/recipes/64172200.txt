Title:
Wild Rice Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds ground beef
2 eggs
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup panko
2 cups cooked wild rice
1 small onion, diced small
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 teaspoons pepper
2 teaspoons dried parsley
1 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
2 bullion cubes, crushed
2 tablespoons nutritional yeast (optional)

Directions:
Beat eggs in a small bowl.  Add remaining ingredients to a large bowl, then add in eggs. Mix with your hands, just until combined.  Scoop into a 9 x 13 pan (or pan of your choice).  Bake at 350° for one hour to one hour and 15 minutes.  A meat thermometer should read 160°.  Time may vary based on the pan size.

Submitter: 
superfox12082
Description: 
We have an abundance of wild rice and this is a pretty good way to use some of it.  Enjoy!
