Title:
NO DOUGH PIZZA Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1030845.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust
(1/2)1 (8 oz) package of regular cream cheese, room temperature
(1)2 eggs
(1/8)1/4 tsp ground black pepper
(1/2)1 tsp garlic powder
(1/8)1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
Topping
(1/4)1/2 cup pizza sauce
(3/4)1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
toppings - pepperoni, ham, sausage, mushrooms, peppers, onions, olives or whatever you want
Garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Lightly spray a 9Ã—13 (7x9) baking dish with cooking spray.
Mix cream cheese, eggs, pepper, garlic powder and parmesan cheese in the Nutri Bullet until combined.
Spread into baking dish. Bake for 12-15 minutes, our until golden brown.
Allow crust to cool for 10 minutes.
Spread pizza sauce on crust.
Top with cheese and toppings.
Sprinkle pizza with garlic powder.
Bake 8-10 minutes, until cheese is melted.
