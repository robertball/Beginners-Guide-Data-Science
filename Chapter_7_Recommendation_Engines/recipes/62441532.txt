Title:
Caramel Pull Apart (Monkey Bread) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/458098.jpg
Ingredients:
24 Frozen Dinner Rolls, thawed but still a little cold
1 Cup Sugar
2 tsp Cinnamon
1/2 Cup Butter, melted
1/2 Cup Caramel Ice Cream Topping

Directions:
Cut Rolls in Half.
In a gallon size plastic bag shake together sugar & cinnamon.
Dip roll pieces in butter and then drop in the bag and shake well to coat the pieces.
Place the pieces in a well greased/sprayed bundt pan.
Sprinkle the remaining sugar/cinnamon mix over the top of the rolls.
Drizzle the caramel topping over the rolls.
Cover with plastic wrap and let rise, until it is about an inch below the top of the bundt pan.
Remove plastic wrap and bake at 350 for 30-35 min.
Remove from oven and invert onto a serving platter.
