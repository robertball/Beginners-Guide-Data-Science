Title:
Shredded Beef for the Instant Pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 pounds flank steak cut into 8 pieces. Any tough, lean cut of beef will work.
2 tbs avocado or olive oil
1 med. onion, finely chopped
8 oz cremini mushrooms, roughly chopped
2 lg cloves garlic, about 1 tbs. Micro planed or finely chopped
1/4 cup dry red wine
1 1/2 cups beef broth
1 tsp dry thyme
3/4 tsp salt, or to taste
freshly ground pepper, to taste
1 tbs Worcestershire Sauce
1/4 cup water
3 tbs flour

Directions:
Press the saute function and adjust to high.
Saute the steak, working in batches, until brown. Set aside.
Add the onions and mushrooms and saute until the onions are translucent, about 5 minutes.
Add the garlic and thyme and saute until fragrant.
Add the wine to the pot and cook until it's almost evaporated.
Add the broth and Worcestershire sauce to the pot and bring to a simmer.
Add the beef and turn to coat.
Press the meat option and adjust time to 20 minutes.
Allow a natural release for at least 20 minutes.
Remove the meat and set aside.
Make a slurry by combining the water and flour, stir well.
Press the saute option and add the slurry slowly.
While the liquid is thicken, use two forks to pull the meat apart.
Add the meat to the liquid and stir.

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
A great way to made a tough cut of beef tender and flavourful. Serve it over crusty buns or mashed potatoes, either way enjoy!
