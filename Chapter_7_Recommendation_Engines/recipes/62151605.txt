Title:
YouWillEatMoreSalad Balsamic Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/828381.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbs White Vinegar
4 Tbs Balsamic Vinegar
4 Tbs Sugar
1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 tsp Pepper
1/2 C Oil (Vegetable or Canola)
1 tsp TOASTED Sesame Oil

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in jar and shake.
The toaster sesame oil is optional, but it adds a whole other layer of flavor. BUT it must be the toasted kind!
Great with any of these; dried cranberries, goat cheese, feta, toasted pinenuts, or sesame seeds.
