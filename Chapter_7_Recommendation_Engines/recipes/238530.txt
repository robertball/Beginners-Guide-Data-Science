Title:
Easy-Breezy Coconut and Banana Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1367564.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana, broken into chunks
3/4 cup milk
1/2 cup Greek yogurt
1/4 cup oats
1/4 cup coconut milk
1/4 cup shredded coconut
2 tablespoons ground flax seed
1 tablespoon white sugar

Directions:
Blend banana, milk, yogurt, oats, coconut milk, shredded coconut, flax seed, and sugar together in a blender until smooth.
