Title:
Calico Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb hamburger
1/2 lb of bacon (diced)
1 can of lima beans
1 can of pork and beans
1 can of butter beans
1 can kindey beans
2 tsp vinegar
3/4 c brown sugar
1/2 c catsup
1 tsp salt
1 tsp mustard

Directions:
Brown the hamburger and diced bacon and drain. Add  all the other ingredients. Back at 350 for an hour or put into a crockpot for 4 hours.
