Title:
Roast Beef (1.5 kgs) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 kg roast (sirloin tip or eye of round)
Butter
Salt and pepper to taste
Merlot reduction sauce

Directions:
Put roast in roaster with blob of butter on top (may use oven bag watch roasting times may be quicker)
Add cracked pepper and salt to taste
Cook at 300-325 for 2 1/2 hours, if pan gets dry toward the end, add 1/4 water or merlot
Heat drippings, and a little flour and potato water, stir until smooth
Add a little pepper and salt to taste, soya sauce can give a little cooler but watch the salt content
