Title:
Toad in the Hole with Turkey Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 slice whole wheat bread
1 egg
3 turkey sausage links, ready-made
Dash of salt & pepper
Dash of Paprika (optional)

Directions:
Use a round cookie cutter to cut out the center of the bread.
Spray nonstick cooking spray on the wheat toast and place on a skillet.
Crack egg and drop it into the hole in the bread. Cook for 1 minute and flip the wheat-egg toast and cook for another minute. For a runny yolk, don't flip.
Sprinkle wheat-egg toast with Paprika, if desired.
Microwave ready-made turkey sausage links according to directions on package.
