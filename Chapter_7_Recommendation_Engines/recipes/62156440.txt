Title:
mexican corn in a cup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans whole kernel corn
1 Tbs mayonnaise
1-2 tsp Tajin fruit seasoning w/lime
1 Tbs parmesan cheese
1-2 limes

Directions:
Heat the corn over medium heat until the corn is steaming.
Remove from heat and drain water.
Fill several cups or mugs halfway with heaping spoonfuls of corn.
Add 1 Tbs mayonnaise, 1-2 tsp of tajin, and parmesan cheese to each cup.
Squeeze lime juice on top of corn. enjoy
mayo and seasoning is not exact for everyone, may have to add more or less to taste.

Submitter: 
Kelsie
Description: 
I grew up just outside of a mexican border town and at least twice month my grandmother would take me across the border do to some shopping. I remember the food stands found at every corner street and almost all of them sold elote. It was one of my favorite vendor foods. everytime i make it, i am reminded of my childhood.
