Title:
Herb Fryer Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990174.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup lemon juice
1/4 cup olive or vegetable oil
1/4 cup minced fresh parsley
2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 tablespoon grated lemon peel
1 teaspoon minced fresh thyme
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 (3 pound) broiler-fryer chicken, cut up

Directions:
In a large resealable plastic bag, combine the lemon juice, oil, parsley, onion, garlic, lemon peel, thyme, salt and pepper; add chicken. Seal bag and turn to coat; refrigerate overnight, turning occasionally. Drain and discard marinade. Grill chicken, covered, over medium heat for 35-40 minutes or until juices run clear, turning every 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Charlene  Sylvia
Description: 
'We use our grill all year long,' says Charlene Sylvia of Sandy, Utah. 'Our boys love this chicken, and it's a hit with company, too.' To really bring out the lemon flavor, Charlene suggests piercing the chicken skin before marinating.
