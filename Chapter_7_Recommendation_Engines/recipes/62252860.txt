Title:
B & B Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup shredded cheddar (or other) cheese
3/4 cup chopped pepper
1/4 cup chopped onion
or
1/2 cup chopped broccoli
or
3/4 cup chopped shitake mushrooms (sauteed)
or
Other vegetables
Ham, bacon,  or other meat chopped
1 3/4 cup milk
5 eggs
3/4 cup Bisquick

Directions:
Heat oven 375 degrees
Butter a 10-inch quiche dish
Chop and very lightly saute vegetables (or steam broccoli)
Put vegetables and any meat into the dish
Sprinkle cheese over vegetables
In blender, mix milk, eggs and Bisquick and pour over vegetables.
Bake for 45-50 minutes.

Submitter: 
food-dancer
Description: 
This versatile crustless quiche goes together quickly with a variety of additions for flavor.  Bisquick is an ingredient with the egg-milk topping.  Several variations are suggested.
