Title:
Peruvian Aji Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Head Romaine Lettuce
2 jalapeno chilies (seeds and stem removed)
5 green onions
1 clove garlic
1/2 bunch cilantro leaves
1/4 cup mayonnaise
dash of salt
pinch of black pepper

Directions:
Remove stems from the lettuce and cilantro.  Put all ingredients into a blender or food processor and process until smooth.

Submitter: 
Dorothy
Description: 
I got this recipe from a restaurant in California 10 years ago.  It's been a staple in my refrigerator ever since.  It's excellent with Rotisserie Chicken.
