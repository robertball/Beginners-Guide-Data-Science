Title:
Sambal Ulek Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
25 fresh red chilis
vinegar
2 teaspoons salt

Directions:
Put chilis, seeds and all, in blender and add enough vinegar to keep mass moving.  Blend to a paste and add salt.

Submitter: 
mikekey
Description: 
Indonesian chili paste to use in stir-frys and for seasoning sauces.  Very spicy so a little goes a long way.  Store in a jar with lid in refrigerator.
