Title:
MEL'S CHOCOLATE STRAWBERRY PIE   (*/+) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/659459.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package (12 ounces) milk chocolate pieces
2 packages (8 ounces each) cream cheese, softened
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 prepared 8 or 9-inch crumb pie crust
1 package (16 ounces) frozen sliced strawberries in syrup, thawed
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 tablespoon cornstarch
sweetened whipped cream (optional)

Directions:
Melt chocolate in double boiler over hot (not boiling) water; mix until smooth. (i used a pan with water in it and then put a metal bowl in that with the chocolate. worked great!)
Cool to room temperature.
Beat cream cheese until fluffy; beat in chocolate and vanilla until smooth and thoroughly blended. Pour into pie crust; smooth top.
Chill until firm.
Meanwhile, heat strawberries in small saucepan.
Mix lemon juice and cornstarch and add to strawberries.
Cook and stir over medium heat until clear and thickened, about 3 to 4 minutes. Cool to room temperature.
Pour over pie; spread to cover chocolate filling.
Chill.
Garnish with whipped cream if desired.
