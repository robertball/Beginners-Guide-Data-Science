Title:
Fire Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 oz bag oyster crackers
5 tbsp canola or vegetable oil
2.5 tbsp ranch dressing mix
1/2 tbsp garlic salt
3/4 tbsp red pepper flakes

Directions:
Mix all together, let sit for at least 30minutes, tossing frequently.
