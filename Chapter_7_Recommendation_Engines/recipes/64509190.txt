Title:
Honey Roasted Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pds sweet potatoes; peeled and cut into cubes
olive oil
honey
fresh lemon juice
salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F
Place cubed sweet potatoes into a bowl.
Mix the following ingredients and pour over the sweet potatoes:
2 Tbsp olive oil
2 Tbsp honey
1 tsp fresh lemon juice
1/2 tsp salt
Place in 9"x13" pan and bake 1 hour until tender and stir occasionally.

Submitter: 
Valerie
Description: 
This is great Fall comfort food. Sweet potatoes never disappoint.
