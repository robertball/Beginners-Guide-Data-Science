Title:
Beef Stew VI Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds cubed beef stew meat
1 cup flour
1 tbls onion powder
1 tbls garlic powder
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
4 cubes beef bouillon, crumbled
1 cup red wine
5 cups water
1 teaspoon dried rosemary
1 teaspoon dried parsley
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
5 carrots, cut into 1 inch pieces
4 stalks celery, cut into 1 inch pieces
2 large onions, chopped
2 teaspoons cornstarch
2 teaspoons cold water

Directions:
add flour, onion powder, and garlic powder to a zip lock bag and shake well.  Add beef and shake well to coat
In a large pot or dutch oven, cook beef in oil over medium heat until brown. Dissolve bouillon in water and pour into pot. Stir in rosemary, parsley and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover and simmer 1 hour.
Stir potatoes, carrots, celery, and onion into the pot. Dissolve cornstarch in 2 teaspoons cold water and stir into stew. Cover and simmer 1 hour more.

Submitter: 
Trey
Description: 
Beef, carrots, potatoes and celery are seasoned with rosemary and parsley in this simple, stovetop stew.Modified from: Beef Stew VI
