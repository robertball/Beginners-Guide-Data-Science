Title:
Sweet & Spicy Shrimp & Chourico Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/452863.jpg
Ingredients:
40 medium sized uncooked shrimp already peeled
1 cured Chourico sausage, cut into 1/2 inch slices
1/2 cup of Sweet Thai Chili sauce
1/4 cup of soy sauce
5 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
1-2 tsp of chili paste (sriracha sauce)
1/2 tbsp of freshly ground pepper
8 skewers

Directions:
Mix the sweet thai chili sauce, soy sauce, garlic, pepper and sriracha sauce together in a bowl. Marinate the shrimp in this for at least 4 hours to overnight.
Preheat a grill to medium high heat.
Skewer 5 shrimp and 2 pieces of chourico on each.
Cook on grill until shrimp is pink and slightly charred.

Submitter: 
Raquel Teixeira
Description: 
This is an awesome BBQ treat in the summer that requires little prep but delivers huge on flavour. The shrimp even stands alone if you can't find this Portuguese sausage.
