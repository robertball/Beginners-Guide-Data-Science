Title:
Confetti Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1# box acini de pepi (tiny round pasta)
4 eggs
1-20 oz. can crushed pinapple
2- 11 oz. cans mandarin oranges
1 c. sugar
1 tsp. salt
4 Tbsp. flour
8 oz. cool whip

Directions:
Cook and drain macaroni. Drain fruit and set aside juice. Mix flour, sugar and salt. Add to juice, with eggs. Cook over medium heat. Stir constantly until thick; like medium pudding. Add to macaroni. Add fruit and stir. Set in refrigerator overnight, covered. Add cool whip in morning.

Submitter: 
NightStars
Description: 
This large salad is great for picnics. Many people think it's tapioca but it's really acini de pepi
