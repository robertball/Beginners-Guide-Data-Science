Title:
CALICO BEANS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Brown 1 # hamburger & 1/2 # bacon
Drain off grease.  Then Add:
1 med browned onion
1 t. salt
1 t. mustard
1/2 c. catsup
3/4 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. white sugar
3 t. vinegar
1 - 2# can pork & beans
1 can (small) red kidney beans  (opt.)
1 pkg. frozen lima beans (cooked)  (opt.)

Directions:
Mix all together and pour into a casserole.
Bake for one hour at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Marjorie Taylor
Description: 
I take these beans to a lot of pot luck dinners and they are always all gone.
