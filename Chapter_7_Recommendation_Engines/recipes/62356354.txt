Title:
Almond Bark Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. almond bark
1 c. peanut butter
2 c. peanuts
2 c. mini marshmallows
3 c. rice krispies

Directions:
Melt almond bark in microwave on half power until melted.  Stir in peanut butter.  Add rest of ingredients and mix well.  Drop by teaspoons on waxed paper.

Submitter: 
Traci Caha Myers
Description: 
Great for the cookie exchanges around the holidays!
