Title:
Nonna Assunta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
2 cloves garlic
1/2 c. butter
1 c. white wine
4 Tbl.flour
1 can artichokes
8oz.mushrooms

Directions:
First coat all the chicken with flour and place in skillet on med with butter.  Cook 3 or 4 min on each side.  Add garlic, artichokes and mushrooms with white wine.  Let everything reduce. Let sauce thicken.
Serve with pasta or potato.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
Succulent chicken breats served in a white wine sauce with artichokes and mushrooms.
