Ingredients:
6 slice Bacon, cut in 1/2
1 tablespoon Brown Sugar
1 Bun
1 tablespoon Butter, softened
Lettuce
1 Tomato Slices

Directions:
1. Prepare the grill to direct heat.
2. Place the bacon slices on the grill and brown them. Place a cast iron skillet over the bacon slices to keep them flat. Flip and cook on the other side as well.
3. Sprinkle brown sugar on both sides of the bacon and allow to caramelize.
4. Toast the bun and spread butter on both halves.
5. Place a lettuce leaf on the bottom half of the bun, followed by tomato slices and bacon. Cover with the top half of the bun.
6. Serve hot.
