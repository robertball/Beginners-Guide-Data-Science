Title:
Mama's Huevos Rancheros Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mama's Huveos Rancheros
Sauce
1 onion chopped fine
1/3 stick butter
1 Can Chicken Broth
1 Can Tomatoes with Green Chilies (diced)
1 Can Chopped Green Chilies
1 Can Green Enchilada Sauce
1/4 tsp. cumin
1/4 tsp. oregano
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper
Corn tortillas
Eggs
Grated cheese (cheddar or Mexican Quesadilla cheese)

Directions:
Saute chopped fine onion in butter or oleo, add chicken broth, diced tomatoes and green chilies, green chilies, green enchilada sauce, the dry spices and a little water. Let simmer for 1 hour or so.
Slightly warm tortillas in hot oil -- not crisp just soften. Put 2 tortillas on oven proof plate and cover with a little sauce. Place 2 half cooked fried eggs on top and cover well with more sauce. Top with grated cheese. Bake in 375 oven for about 15 - 20 minutes or until eggs are done to your liking and piping hot!

Submitter: 
Paula Taylor
Description: 
This is a very old recipe that my Mom used to make for special breakfast or brunch! They are delicious. Great served with a side of refried beans!
