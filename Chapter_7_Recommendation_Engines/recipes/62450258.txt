Title:
Vegetarian Chili Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Vegetarian Hormel Chili
2 cups cubed Velveeta Cheese

Directions:
1.In large saucepan, combine chili and cheese. Heat over low heat, stirring frequently, until cheese is melted and chili mixture is hot and bubbly. Serve with corn chips.
