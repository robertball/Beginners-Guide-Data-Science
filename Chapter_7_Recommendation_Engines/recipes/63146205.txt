Title:
Brownie Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks butter
4 squares of Baker's semi-sweet chocolate
1 cup flour
1 3/4 cup sugar
4 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees. In a medium saucepan, melt the chocolate with the butter. Allow to cool slightly. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, eggs and vanilla. Mix thoroughly. Add the butter and chocolate mixture, mix well. Fill paper lined baking cups about 3/4 full. Bake at 325 degrees about 40 minutes.   Enjoy!
