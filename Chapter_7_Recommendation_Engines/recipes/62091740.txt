Title:
Creole rice and kidney beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 cup of cooked rice
- 1/2 cup canned kidney beans
- 1 1/2 cup of water
- 1/2 cup coconut milk
- 1 tsp thyme
- 1 small onion with 3 cloves stuck in it
- salt

Directions:
1. In a pot, put rice, kidney beans, water, coconut milk, thyme, onion and a pinch of salt
2. Bring to a boil or until coconut milk thickens
3. Cover pot with lid and let simmer 20 minutes
4. Take off lid and let cook 5 more minutes to reduce remaining liquid
5. Take off from stove

Submitter: 
Mel
Description: 
A really good rice recipe coming from Haiti.  Easy and quick!
