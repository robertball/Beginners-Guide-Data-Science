Title:
Easy Baked Chicken Teriyaki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 chicken legs
2 cup teriyaki sauce
1/2 cup lemon juice
2 teaspoons minced fresh garlic
2 teaspoons sesame oil

Directions:
Place chicken, teriyaki sauce, lemon juice, garlic, and sesame oil in a large resealable plastic bag. Seal bag, and shake to coat. Place in refrigerator for 24 hours, turning every so often.
Place marinated chicken legs in baking pan and roast for 1 hour at 350.  Pierce chicken with fork to see if cooked and if not adjust cooking time.  Remove from oven and add fresh vegetables and rice or potatoes to make casserole.  Debone chicken and discard bones.

Submitter: 
Chefetta
Description: 
Chicken legs marinated in teriyaki sauce, lemon, garlic, and sesame oil, then grilled to a tasty finish. Very easy and great for a hot summer's evening. Leftovers are great on a green salad or sandwich. Be sure to grill very hot and very fast!!Modified from: Easy Grilled Chicken Teriyaki
