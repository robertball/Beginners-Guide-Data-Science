Title:
Sour Cream Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 flour tortillas
8oz canned or fresh roasted green chilies chopped
1 pint sour cream
2 10oz cans condensed cream of chicken soup
1 1/2 lb Monterey jack cheese, shredded
1 1/2 lb sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
4 green onions, chopped
2 stewed chickens

Directions:
Remove chicken meat from bones and chop.  Mix soup, sour cream, half the cheese, and green chilies until blended.  Fill each tortilla with a generous scoop of chicken mixture, roll and fill a 9x13 greased pan.  Spread remaining mixture over the top.  Sprinkle the rest of the cheese over the top.
Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes.
