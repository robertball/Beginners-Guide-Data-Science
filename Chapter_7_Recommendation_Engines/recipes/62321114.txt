Title:
Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 Tbsp. vinegar
1/4 cup sugar
2 heads broccoli, cut into bite size pieces
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
bacon bits

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix mayo, vinegar and sugar together.
Add broccoli, onion and cheese to mixture and stir.
Garnish with bacon bits.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.
