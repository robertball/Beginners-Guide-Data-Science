Title:
Split Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb or 2 1/3 cup peas (yellow is best)
1 1/2-2 quarts water or chicken stock (4 cans) vs 7 cups hot water
ham pieces or bone (add 2 cups died ham)
2 med onions, chopped
2 cups carrots ( 3 carrots)
1 cup celery ( 2 ribs)
salt and pepper
1-2 cloves garlic, minced
2 bay leaves
1 tsp thyme
red pepper
oregano optional
paprika
garnish with parsley

Directions:
rinse and soak peas overnight. saute onion in bacon fat.  combine all-cover-bring to boil. reduce heat and simmer gently for 3 hrs.  may puree in blender.
Can use crock pot instead.  No need to soak peas. Layer ingredients in slow cooker in the order given; pour in water. Do not stir ingredients. Cover and cook on HIGH 4 to 5 hours or on low 8 to 10 hours until peas are very soft and ham falls off bone. Remove bones and bay leaf. Mash peas to thicken more, if desired. Freezes well.
