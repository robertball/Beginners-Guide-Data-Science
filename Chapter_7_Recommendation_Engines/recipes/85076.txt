Title:
Divine Crab Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 egg, beaten
2 slices bread, crusts removed and broken into crumbs
1 tablespoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Seafood Seasoning
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon mayonnaise
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 pound fresh crabmeat

Directions:
Combine egg, bread crumbs, seafood seasoning, Worcestershire sauce, mayonnaise and baking powder in a large bowl. Stir in crabmeat. Mix well; shape mixture into 8 one-half inch thick patties.
Broil for 10 minutes, without turning.

Submitter: 
McCormick® Gourmet Collection®
Description: 
McCormick's® Gourmet Collection® Seafood Seasoning gives this Divine Crab Cake recipe an intriguing spice flavor and a hint of spice heat, but doesn't mask the delicate flavor of the crabmeat. Serve on crackers for Maryland style crab cakes.
