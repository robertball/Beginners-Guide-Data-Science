Title:
Pancake Topped Taco Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/596413.jpg
Ingredients:
Filling:
•	1/2 lb. ground turkey
•	1/2 cup red bell pepper, chopped
•	1 (15 oz) can black beans, drained
•	1 can (15.25 oz.) whole kernel corn, drained
•	1 cup salsa
•	1 package (1.25 oz.) taco seasoning mix
•	1 cup shredded reduced fat cheddar cheese
•	Topping:
•	1/2 cup pancake mix
•	2/3 cup 1% milk
•	1 egg, slightly beaten
•	2 tsp. vegetable oil

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350° F.
2. Brown meat and drain.
3. Add peppers, beans, and corn, cook 2 minutes over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
4.Add salsa and taco seasoning; cook 2-3 minutes or until heated through.
5.Pour hot filling mixture into greased 9&quot; x 9&quot; square baking dish. Sprinkle with cheese; set aside.
6.For topping, combine pancake mix, milk, egg and oil; stir with wire whisk until large lumps disappear. Pour over filling mixture; spread evenly.
7.Bake 30-35 minutes or until topping is golden brown.
8.Sprinkle top with additional cheese, if desired.

Description:
