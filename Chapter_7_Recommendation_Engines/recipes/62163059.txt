Title:
Light and Fluffy Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can crushed pineapple, drained
2 (14 oz.) cans mandarin oranges, drained
2 apples, peeled and chopped
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1/2 cup sweetened flaked coconut
8 oz. whipped topping
Optional ingredients: maraschino cherries, fresh grapes

Directions:
Drain pineapple and mandarin oranges well.  Add to bowl with chopped apples, pecans, coconut and whipped topping.  Mix gently so mandarin oranges will stay intact.  Top fruit salad with extra coconut on top for decoration.  Chill for a few hours and serve.

Submitter: 
Heather C
Description: 
This fruit salad is light and refreshing and is great during the summer for a light dessert.  I make this and take it to family reunions and get togethers and it is an unusual hit.
