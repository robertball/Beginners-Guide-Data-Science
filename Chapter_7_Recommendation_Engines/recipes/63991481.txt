Title:
Jewish Applecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 apples
4 Tbs. sugar
2 tsp. cinnamon
4 eggs
2 cups sugar
1 cup oil
3 cups flour
3 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup orange juice
2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Oven - 350 degrees
Slice apples and chop making about 1 inch pieces. pieces. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon mixture and set aside.
In separate bowl...Beat 4 eggs in 2 cups sugar.  Beat til fluffy. Add oil and beat again.
Sift flour, baking powder, and salt. (I do not sift)
Add to beaten mixture, alternating with 1/2 cup orange juice.  (can use juice from an orange)
Beat in vanilla
Pour 1/2 batter in tubed pan, then 1/2 apples, batter and other half apples.

Submitter: 
Mara
Description: 
This cake is not only delicious as a dessert but wonderful in the morning with a cup of coffee.
