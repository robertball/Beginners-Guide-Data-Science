Title:
Robby's Hot Dog Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. ground beef
1- 1.25 package French's Chili-O seasoning
1/2 cup chili sauce
Water as needed

Directions:
Saute ground beef in skillet until browned
Remove from heat and lightly drain ground beef, leaving some of the meat juice
Finely mince ground beef - I use a food processor
Place ground beef into sauce pan
Add chili sauce and French's Chili-O seasoning
Heat through, adding water for desired consistency
Serve as topping for hot dogs and hamburgers

Submitter: 
Chef Robby
Description: 
A delicious and easy-to-make meat sauce topping for hot dogs.  Great on burgers too!!!!
