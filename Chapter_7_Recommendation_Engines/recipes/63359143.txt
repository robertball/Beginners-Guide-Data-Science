Title:
Broccoli Cheese Soup, the best Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 carrots
1 onion
1 stick of butter
1 box frozen chopped broccoli
1 box frozen broccoli hunks
2 cans of chicken broth, unsalted *(be sure it’s unsalted or low salted because if not the soup will be too salty)
2 cans cream of potato soup
16 oz. Velveeta Cheese, grated or cut up
small carton of sour cream

Directions:
Slice carrot and onion and sauté in butter.  Let it cool off, then put into a food processor and blend until finely chopped.  Remove and put into a stockpot.
Add chicken broth to vegetables in pot and add broccoli.  Simmer 30 minutes.
Add 2 cans of cream of potato soup and the 16 oz. of cheese.  Simmer 10 minutes over very low heat.
Just before serving add a small carton of sour cream.
