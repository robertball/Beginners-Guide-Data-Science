Title:
Spicy Shrimp Quesadilla Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15-20 Medium sized shrimp
2 8 Inch Tortillas
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 small roma tomato
Sriracha to taste
Sour cream, optional

Directions:
Remove tails from shrimp, and give them a rough chop if desired.
Cut the tomato into four slices.
Preheat skillet to medium-low.
Spread 1/8 cup of the cheese on half of one tortilla.
Add half of the shrimp, then top with 2 tomato slices and then another 1/8 cup of the cheese.
Add sriracha to taste.
Fold in half and place in heated skillet.  Cook for approximately 1-2 minutes per side.
Repeat for the second quesadilla.
