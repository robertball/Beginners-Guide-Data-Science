Title:
Baked Southwest Omelet Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1 cup milk
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 cup chopped green pepper, sautéed
1 cup chopped onion, sautéed
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup bacon bits or chopped ham

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
In a blender, combine eggs, milk, flour, salt and pepper; cover and process until smooth. Pour into mixing bowl.  Sauté chopped green peppers and onions in 2 tablespoons of butter.  Add sautéed chopped green peppers and onions to egg mixture.
Pour into prepared baking pan.  Sprinkle half the cheese mixture over top of eggs, etc.
Bake in preheated oven until set, about 20 minutes.
Carefully loosen edges of omelet from pan. Starting from the short edge of the pan.  Sprinkle cheeses and bacon bits or chopped ham on omelet. (Reserve some for topping the omelet roll.)
Carefully roll up omelet. Place omelet seam side down on a serving plate, sprinkle with reserved topping and cut into 6 equal sized pieces.

Description: 
This omelet is so easy and delicious.  It bakes in the oven, so there is no watching the stove.  My son loves to make it for us.Modified from: Baked Omelet Roll
