Title:
Feta Cheese and Shrimp Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion, minced
4 garlic cloves, minced
1 1/2 lbs large shrimp, peeled and devained
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 - 28 oz. can chopped tomatoes
2/3 cup Crumbled Feta Cheese
Pepper, to taste
2 tbsp fresh oregano, chopped
2 cups spaghetti noodles, cooked

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Heat olive oil in a large oven-proof skillet over medium heat. Add minced onion and cook for two minutes, stirring often. Add garlic and cook an additional minute. Add tomatoes and their juice and bring to a boil. Cook until tomato juices thicken, about 5 minutes. Season with pepper to taste.
Remove tomato sauce from heat and stir in shrimp. Sprinkle with Feta cheese.
Bake until shrimp are cooked, about 10 minutes. Sprinkle chopped oregano and serve hot, over cooked pasta.
