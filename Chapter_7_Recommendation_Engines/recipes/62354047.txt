Title:
Grandma Bachman's Bunny Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box yellow cake mix
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/8 cup poppy seeds
4 eggs
1 Cup watm water
1 box butterscotch instant pudding

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Grease 2 loaf pans. Dump all ingredients in bowl. Beat with mixer for 3 minutes or until smooth. (To mix by hand, take at least 5 minutes.) Pour into two greased loaf pans. Bake for about 1 hour. Remove from oven.Tip pans on sides until cool. Tip pans over to remove bread.
