Title:
Carolina Crab Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg, lightly beaten
1/2 cup soft bread crumbs
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp. grated onion
1/2 tsp. minced fresh parsley
1/2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/8 tsp. seafood seasoning
1/8 tsp. ground mustard
dash pepper
dash hot pepper sauce
1 can (6 oz.) crab meat, drained, flaked and cartilage removed
1 Tbsp. canola oil
Mustard Sauce:
2 Tbsp. mayonnaise
2 Tbsp. sour cream
1 Tbsp. Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp. lemon juice
1/2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the first 10 ingredients.  Fold in crab.  Refrigerate for 30 minutes.  Meanwhile, combine sauce ingredients.
In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat.  Drop crab mixture by 1/4 cupfulls into the pan; cook for 3-5 minutes on each side or until golden brown.  Serve with sauce.

Submitter: 
Kinza
Description: 
These crab cakes are spiced just right, and the sauce is the perfect accent.
