Title:
Chicken Salad Croissants Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/448719.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked chicken breast, cut up
1/4 cup diced celery
1/4 cup raisins
1/4 cup dried cranberries
1/4 cup sliced almonds
3/4 cup mayonnaise
2 Tablespoons chopped red onion
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
5 Croissants

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients except for the croissants. Mix well.
Spoon about 1/2 cup into each croissant.

Submitter: 
Dawn
Description: 
This chicken salad is our family's favorite.  It's easy and can be made ahead.  I make this often as it's always requested.
