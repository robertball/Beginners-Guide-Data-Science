Title:
Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/734655.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 med onion, finely chopped
1 clove garlic, finely chopped or (1/2 tsp garlic salt)
3 8 oz cans tomato sauce
3 cups water
1/2 tsp sugar, optional(to take the tang out)
1 tsp salt, or (to taste)
1 tsp ground black pepper, or (to taste)
24 oz dry spaghetti

Directions:
Brown ground beef, garlic, and onion, in skillet on med heat,stirring occasionallly, until evenly browned through. Drain.
Return beef to skillet, and add tomato sauce, water, sugar, salt, and pepper. Simmer on med heat for 20 mins or so.
While sauce is simmering, in a lg pot boil 6 quarts of water, (add 1 tsp of salt to water, Optional)
Add spaghetti and return to a boil.
Cook uncovered about 10 min, or until tender, stirring occasionallly. Avoid over cooking.
Remove from heat and drain. "Do NOT rinse spaghetti!"
Return spaghetti to pot, add sauce and stir. Cover and let marinate at least 10 min before serving.
