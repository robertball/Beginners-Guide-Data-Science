Title:
Ma Poa tofu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3915501.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 Cup chicken broth
4 TBS Hoisin Sauce
4 chopped chili pods
1/2 ground pork
2 TBS Chinese Hot oil
1 pound firm tofu, cubed
1/3 cup corn starch mixed with 3/4 cup water

Directions:
1.Brown pork and drain most, but not all of the fat. Add the chili pods and reduce heat to low.
2.Poach tofu in boiling water for 5 minutes, then drain. This is necessary to open the pores on the tofu an all the oils to bond to it.
3. Add broth and hot oil to the pork mixture and increase heat to medium.
4. Add the tofu and stir.
5. Add starch mixture and bring to a boil.
6. Keep stirring and allow to boil until reaching desired thickness. (about 4 minutes)
Serve with white rice.
