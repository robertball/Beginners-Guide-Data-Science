Title:
White Chocolate Chunk Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup shortening
1/2 cup white sugar
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 egg
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
10 ounces chocolate chips

Directions:
In a large bowl, cream butter and shortening; gradually add sugars, beating well at medium speed with an electric mixer. Beat in egg and vanilla. Combine flour, soda, and salt; stir into creamed mixture. Stir in chocolate chips. Chill dough for 1 hour.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Lightly grease cookie sheets. Drop dough by heaping tablespoonfuls 3 inches apart onto prepared cookie sheets.
Bake for 12 to 14 minutes in preheated oven. Cookies will be soft.  Cool slightly on cookie sheets; transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
