Title:
Garlic kale quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068664.jpg
Ingredients:
1 clove of minced garlic
1 cup chopped kale
1/3 cup uncooked quinoa
1/4 tsp sesame oil
1 tbsp olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook quinoa according to package instructions.
Meanwhile, sauté garlic and kale in olive oil on medium heat until kale has wilted.
Season with salt and pepper.
Add cooked quinoa to garlic and kale and continue to sauté.
Add sesame oil.
Add a splash of liquid (water or white cooking wine) to keep from sticking.
