Title:
Curry Powder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/844160.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Tablespoons Turmeric powder
2 Tablespoons Ground Cumin
1 Tsp Ground Cloves
2 Tablespoons Paprika
1 Tablespoon Ground black pepper
1 Tablespoon Cinnamon
1 Tablespoon Salt
3 Tablespoons Cayenne Pepper
3 Tablespoons Red Indian chile powder XXX Optional
2 Tablespoons of Brown Sugar
2 Tablespoon ground Coriander

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients in a bowl be sure to see that the brown sugar is completely broken down. (NO lumps) Store in a jar.

Submitter: 
Buon Appetito
Description: 
If you don't like your curry powder hot decrease or eliminate the Indian pepper.
