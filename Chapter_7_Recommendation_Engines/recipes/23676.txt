Title:
Blonde Mini Kiss Square Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter, softened
1 cup packed light brown sugar
2 eggs
2 tablespoons milk
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 3/4 cups HERSHEY®'S MINI KISSES™ Semisweet Baking Pieces
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease 13x9x2-inch baking pan.
In large bowl, beat butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs, milk and vanilla; beat well. Stir together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt; gradually add to butter mixture, beating until well blended. Stir in MINI KISSES Chocolate. Spread batter evenly in prepared pan. Sprinkle nuts over top; press gently into batter.
Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until golden brown. Cool completely in pan on wire rack. Cut into bars.
