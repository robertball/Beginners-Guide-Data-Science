Title:
Pie crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/3 c plus 2 tab butter flavored crisco
3 tab water
.

Directions:
Combine flour n salt in mixing bowl
With pastry blender or 2 knives cut shortening into flour mixture till pea sized balls form
Sprinkle water into flour 1 tab at a time, mix lightly with fork
Gather dough together with fingers so it cleans the bowl
Press firmly into a ball (divide in 2 if for 2 crust pie)
Put down a white dish towel with 2 tab flour sprinkled on it
Put a knee high nylon over a rolling pin
Put dough on floured toe and roll out
Pinch broken edges immediately
Do not over roll it will make it tough
Turn pie pan upside down n measure pie crust to be one in larger
With a butter knife cut off extra pieces and put them where u have missing area
Re roll
Put pie pan on a cookie sheet
Carefully fold in half and place in pie pan
Fold extra edges under
For one crust pinch for two crust fold under and wait for top then pinch (you can use a fork on the edges also
Put filling in
Fold aluminum foil over and add filling
