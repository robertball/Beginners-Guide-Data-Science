Title:
Kelly's Scalloped Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans corn (drained)
2 cans creamed corn
3/4 box Jiffy Corn Bread Mix
1 egg
1/2 stick butter (melted)
1 cup sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Mix everything in a casserole dish.  Bake uncovered until golden brown on top.

Submitter: 
LUSYRSGIRL
Description: 
This is a requested favorite during the holidays.  I have been ordered to bring this dish to every family event. It takes only moments to mix and leaves you time to get ready for the occasion while it bakes.
