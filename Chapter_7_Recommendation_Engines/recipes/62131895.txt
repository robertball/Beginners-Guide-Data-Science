Title:
Fluffy No-Bake Cherry Cheesecake Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
About 80 Nilla (Vanilla) Wafers, finely crushed
1/3 cup of butter
1/2 cup of brown sugar
16 ounces of Cool Whip
2-3.4 ounce packages of instant cheesecake flavored pudding
2-20 ounce cans of cherry pie filling

Directions:
Mix crushed wafers, butter, and brown sugar until it sticks together (preferably in a food processor).
Press wafer crust down into bottom of 13X9 pan.
Gradually add pudding to Cool Whip until it's a consistent mixture.
Evenly spread Cool Whip mixture over Nilla crust.
Evenly spread pie filling on top the Cool Whip mixture.
Cover and refrigerate before serving or overnight.

Submitter: 
Louann Rinken
Description: 
This quick and easy no-bake recipe makes a delicious treat that receives rave reviews!  It uses easy to find ingredients like Jell-o Pudding, Nilla Wafers, and Cool Whip, making this cheesecake lover's dream dessert a breeze to make.
