Title:
Hot - n - Spicy Honey Glaze Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2530732.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pack boneless skinless chicken thighs
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
2 tbsp soy sauce
½ cup olive oil
1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
¼ cup honey
¼ cup salt
1 ½ tbsp black pepper (course)
2 tbsp ground red pepper
1 tbsp granulated garlic powder
1 tbsp chili powder

Directions:
For the marinade / basting sauce combine 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce, 2 tbsp soy sauce, ½ cup olive oil, 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar,  ¼ cup honey, in a container of your choice and mix until honey is well blended.
Add chicken and 3/4 of the marinade in a ziploc bag and put in fridge over night.
Save some extra marinade for basting and don't use the same marinade the chicken was in.
Make the dry rub by combining ¼ cup salt 1 ½ tbsp black pepper, 2 tbsp ground red pepper,1 tbsp granulated garlic powder,1 tbsp chili powder and mix.
Grill chicken over direct heat and baste both sides.
Shake the dry rub like salt and pepper on both sides of the chicken half way through the grilling process. Use amount to your liking.
Grill until you have a glazed and crusty appearance.
Move to indirect heat until done.
Chicken is done with an internal temp of 165F deg.

Description: 
Chicken with a nice kick. This is a great recipe for the grill! If you like hot -n- spicy that is full of flavor, you have got to try this one!
