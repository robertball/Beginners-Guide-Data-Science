Title:
No Stress Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/2 TBS cajun seasoning

Directions:
Cut fat from chicken breasts
Sprinkle each side of both breasts with cajun seasoning and place on a broiling pan
Broil chicken for 10-15 minutes, or until juices run clear, turning over half way through cooking

Submitter: 
annersmarie
Description: 
It's none too flashy, but this is the recipe that I tend to fall back on after a long day at work.  It takes almost no preparation, and goes great with rice and mixed vegetables.  I also like to use barbeque sauce for dipping.
