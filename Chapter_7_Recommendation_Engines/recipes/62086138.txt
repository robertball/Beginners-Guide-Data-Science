Title:
Perfect Pierogi Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 Lasagna Noodles
----------------------------------
Cottage Cheese Layer
1 - 24 oz Container Cottage Cheese
1 tsp Onion Salt
1/8 tsp black pepper
2 Large Eggs
------------------------------
Mashed Potato Layer
3 Cups Leftover Mashed Potatoes (or mashed potatoes from grocers dairy case.  DO NOT use instant.)
1 1/2 Cups Sharp Cheddar Cheese
1 1/2 tsp Dried Dill Weed
1 tsp Onion Salt
1/8 tsp black pepper
2 Large Eggs
-------------------------------------
Topping
1/2 Lb Bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 stick Butter
1 Large Yellow Onion, diced
1 Bunch Green Onions, diced
Sour Cream (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Boil Lasagna Noodles until barely soft (approx 8 minutes) and drain.
Spray the bottom of a 9 x 13 baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.  Layer 4 noodles in the bottom.
Combine Cottage Cheese, 1 tsp onion salt, 1/8 tsp black pepper and 2 eggs.  Spread over lasagna noodles.  Top with 4 more lasagna noodles.
Combine mashed potatoes, cheddar cheese, dill weed, 1 tsp onion salt, 1/8 tsp black pepper and two eggs.  Spread over lasagna noodles.  Top with 4 more lasagna noodles.
Melt 1 stick butter in pan, sautee yellow and green onions until soft and slightly browned.  Spoon over top of casserole and add crumbled bacon.
Cover with foil and bake 45 minutes.  Remove foil and continue baking for 10 additional minutes.  Remove from oven and let stand 8 - 10 minutes.
Serve with sour cream, if desired.

Submitter:

Description: 
My husband LOVES the home made pierogi, his mother makes on holidays.  As a working woman, I ask you...who has the time?  So, I created this cheater recipe that incorporates all the flavors of traditional pierogi, with out all the muss and fuss.  My husband said it was even better than his mother's recipe!
