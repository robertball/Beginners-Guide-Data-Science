Title:
Brandy's Peanut Butter Sheet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
2 c flour
2 c sugar
1 tsp baking soda
½ tsp salt
2 sticks butter
1 cup water
½ cup peanut butter
½ cup buttermilk
2 eggs, beaten
1 tsp vanilla
Icing:
1 stick butter
½ cup peanut butter
½ tsp vanilla
4 cups powdered sugar
1/3 cup milk

Directions:
Mix flour, sugar, soda, and salt, set aside.  In sauce pan combine butter, peanut butter, and water, bring to boil.  Add buttermilk, eggs, vanilla. Mix well with dry ingred.  Pour into a greased and floured sheet cake pan. Bake at 350 for approx 20 - 30 min.
Icing: melt butter and peanut butter, add vanilla. Mix with powdered sugar and milk.  Beat until smooth. Pour over warm cake.

Submitter: 
Brandy
Description: 
This Peanut Butter Cake is great for a large crowd. It is always the first dessert gone at family gatherings.
