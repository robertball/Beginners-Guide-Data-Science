Title:
Crystal's Raspberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad -
Spring mix of salad greens
Small can of mandarin oranges
1/2 red onion sliced into small rings
3/4 cup of cranraisins
3/4 cup of glazed candied walnuts
Ground fresh pepper to taste
Shredded Parmesan cheese to taste
Dressing -
3 tbsp raspberry vinaigrette
1 tbsp cooking oil
1 tbsp sesame oil
1 tbsp honey

Directions:
Salad -
Toss salad greens, mandarin oranges and cranraisins with raspberry dressing.
Divide tossed salad onto plates and layer with two rings of red onion on top.
Sprinkle walnuts and parmesan cheese on top and serve.
Add black pepper if desired.
Dressing -
Blend ingredients until emulsified.
Pour over salad and toss well to mix.
Finish with candied walnuts and parmesan cheese

Submitter: 
Crystal S
Description: 
Perfect for al fresco dining.  Serve with Andrew's favorite grilled pork chops from this site.  Enjoy!
