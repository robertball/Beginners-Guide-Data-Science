Title:
Bellini Slushie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3669467.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups frozen peach chunks
1 cup peach schnapps
1 (750 milliliter) bottle Champagne, chilled

Directions:
Combine peach chunks and peach schnapps in a blender; blend until smooth.
Fill 8 champagne flutes halfway with blended peach mixture. Fill flutes to the top with Champagne.

Submitter: 
Elizabeth
Description: 
Frozen peaches blended with peach schnapps and stirred with champagne make a slushie that's a delightful summer cocktail. Garnish with mint.
