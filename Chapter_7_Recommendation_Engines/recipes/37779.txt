Title:
Easy Banana Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977571.jpg
Ingredients:
1 egg
3/4 cup milk
2 tablespoons oil
1 cup pancake and waffle mix
1 cup POST SELECTS BANANA NUT CRUNCH Cereal

Directions:
Beat egg in large bowl; stir in milk and oil.  Add pancake and waffle mix; stir just until moistened.  Stir in cereal.  Let stand 5 minutes.
Pour 1/4 cup batter onto hot griddle for each pancake; cook until bubbles form on top.  Turn to brown other side.  Garnish with fresh bananas and strawberries and sprinkle with powdered sugar.
