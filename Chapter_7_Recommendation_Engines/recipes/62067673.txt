Title:
Mandarin Orange Jello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large box Orange Jello, dry
1 small Cottage Cheese - small curd
1 package Dream Whip, unprepared
1 small can Mandarin Oranges, drained
1 small can crushed pineapple, well drained (optional)

Directions:
In medium bowl, whip together dry Jello mix, cottage cheese, and dry Dream Whip until well blended.  Fold in manadarin oranges and crushed pineapple (optional).  Chill 1 hour or until set.  Enjoy.

Submitter: 
anmlnrs
Description: 
My mom used to make this for every holiday or special event.  It's a one-dish recipe, and can even be served as a light desert.
