Title:
Pot Roast with Ginger Snap Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 to 3 lb. Roast beef or chuck
1/2 box ginger snap cookies
Allspice
1 onion
6 Potatoes (small)
salt
pepper
8 to 12 oz. egg noodles

Directions:
Spray bottom of dutch oven with pam or melt small about of butter to sear. Brown the meat on all sides. Add enough water to cover the meat. Lower flame. Add allspice, about half the can. Crush the ginger snaps and add.add more to make thicker. Add peeled, cut onion. Add peeled potatoes, Sliced or quartered. Continue to cook on medium heat approximately 1 to 2 hours. Gravy will thicken. Stir to prevent sticking. Add more water if too thick. serve over boiled noodles. (may also add carrots about half way through)
