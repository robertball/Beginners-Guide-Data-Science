Title:
Breakfast Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 eggs
3 c. milk
2 slices bread - cubed
1-1/2 lbs cheddar chees - shredded
1-1/2 lbs Jimmy Dean pork sausage
1 t. dry mustard

Directions:
Brown sausage and add remaining ingredients
Refrigerate in covered bowl overnight
Bake in greased 9 x 13 pan - 350 degrees for about an hour.

Submitter: 
Mary Johnston
Description:

Easy breakfast for a group.
