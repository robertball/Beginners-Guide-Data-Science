Title:
Spinach and Artichoke Crescent Roll Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4579398.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz) container of whipped cream cheese
1 (14 oz) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
3 cups spinach leaves chopped (or may use thawed frozen chopped spinach will have to adjust amount though)
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
Salt and Pepper to taste (prefer Kosher and fresh ground pepper)
3 (8 oz) tubes of buttery crescent rolls
1/2 cup Italian style Bread Crumbs
Optional: dip into a mix of Ranch/Frank's Hot sauce for extra kick

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
Line baking sheet with parchment paper (or silicone mat)
In large bowl mix together cream cheese, chopped artichoke hearts, spinach, garlic, mayonnaise, feta and Parmesan
Season with salt and pepper to taste
Remove rolls from can and separating into eight triangles (per can). Top each triangle with a heaping  tablespoon (or so) with the spinach/cream cheese mixture on the wide end.
Starting with the wide end, roll up each triangle, folding the ends underneath and then dredge in bread crumbs to coat
Place on baking sheet seam-side down and bake until golden brown approximately 10-12 minutes.
Serve hot.

Submitter:

Description: 
Flaky, buttery crescent rolls stuffed with favorite spinach and artichoke dip and oozing melted feta and whipped yummy cream cheese.
