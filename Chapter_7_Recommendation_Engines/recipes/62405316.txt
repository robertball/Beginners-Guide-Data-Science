Title:
Peanut Butter Chocolate Milk Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 rounded scoops of vanilla ice cream
2 Tbs chocolate syrup
1/2 cup milk
3 Tbs peanut butter, creamy (can substitute with chunky, if preferred)

Directions:
In a blender, combine ice cream, chocolate syrup, and milk. Blend until just incorporated. Add peanut butter and blend until smooth and well incorporated. Serve and enjoy! Makes 1 serving.

Submitter: 
Angie Glant
Description: 
Thick and creamy PB and chocolate milk shake for any time of the year.
