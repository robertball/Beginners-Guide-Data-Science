Title:
Hawaiian Coconut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
3 C. flour
1 t. baking powder
1 t. soda
1 t. salt
1 C. shortening
1 C. sugar
1 C. brown sugar
2 eggs
2 T. water
2 t. vanilla
3 C. oats
2 C. coconut

Directions:
Cream shortening and sugars. Add eggs, water and vanilla. Add flour, baking powder, soda and salt. Beat until smooth. Fold in rolled oaas and coconut. Shape dough into small balls and place on greased cookie sheets. Bake at 350 degrees for 12-15 minutes.
