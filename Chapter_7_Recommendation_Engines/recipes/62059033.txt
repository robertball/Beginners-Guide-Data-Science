Title:
Heidi's Comfort  Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cooked, diced chicken
1 cup cooked, diced ham
2 cups broccoli
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 tbsp mayo
2 tbsp milk
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1.5 cups chicken flavored stuffing mix

Directions:
In a round casserole dish, add chicken, ham and broccoli. In a seperate bowl, mix soup, mayo and milk. Add to casserole dish. Add shredded cheese and cover with stuffing mix.

Submitter: 
morgens_aunt
Description: 
I came up with this recipe as I wanted a good comfort meal. A great and simple casserold that can be used with left over ingrediants.
