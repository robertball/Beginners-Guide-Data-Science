Title:
Dreamy Hot Cocoa for One Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/556820.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tsp unsweetened cocoa
2 tsp non-fat dry milk
2 tbsp Splenda
pinch of salt (optional)
8 oz. skim milk
1-2 tbsp Torani Sugar-Free Chocolate Syrup

Directions:
Mix dry ingredient in mug.
In a separate mug, microwave skim milk to desired warmth.
Add milk to dry ingredients, stirring until fully incorporated.
Add chocolate syrup and stir.
Enjoy!!

Submitter: 
WickedCreations
Description: 
A true dieter's delite that absolutely creamy, dreamy, sweet and guilt-free-you would not know it!! Very yummie!! Easy to make on a very cold winter's night!
