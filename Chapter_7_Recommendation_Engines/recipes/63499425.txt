Title:
Caroline's Coconut Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/955404.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup jasmine rice
2/3 cup light coconut milk + 1 1/3 cup water
1.5 - 2 lb chicken breast or thigh, cut in thin 1 in. strips
1 small onion, diced
3 small or 2 large garlic cloves, minced
butter/olive oil for sauteeing
7oz. chopped green chilies
8 oz water chestnuts
1/2 can Rotel
1 T. chopped cilantro
Juice from 1/2 lime
1/2 cup half and half
1/2 cup sour cream
1 cup shredded jack cheese

Directions:
Bring water/coconut milk mixture to a boil, add rice, stir over high heat for one minute.
Turn burner to low and cover saucepan. Set timer for 25 minutes
Cook chicken in large saute pan with olive oil. When almost done, add onion, garlic, and saute in 1 T. butter until onions are soft.
Add chilies, water chestnuts, Rotel, cilantro, and lime juice.
Take off heat and mix in half & half and sour cream.
Stir in cheese and put lid on pan until ready to serve over coconut rice.

Submitter: 
Lisa Eagen
Description: 
This is a favorite from my sister-in-law who modified a different recipe so her youngest daughter, Caroline, loves it!
