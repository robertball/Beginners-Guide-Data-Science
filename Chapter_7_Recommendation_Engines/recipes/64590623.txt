Title:
Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Red potatoes - 5 or 6
1 onion, chopped fine
3-4 celery stalks with leaves, chopped fine
1/4 cup relish
1 Tab. dried parsley
1 Tab. sugar
2 Tab. Gulden's Mustard
5-6 shakes of the Regina Red Wine Vinegar
Miracle Whip, 3/4 Cup (to start with)
Paprika to garnish

Directions:
Boil potatoes until  done.  Meanwhile, chop celery and onion fine and place in the bowl.  Once potatoes are tender, rinse under cold water and start slicing into chunks over top the onion and celery.  Add rest of ingredients and slowly mix together .  May need to add more Miracle Whip.  Garnish with paprika!  Voila! :)  Enjoy.  P.S.  We like taking bites of it while warm!  :)

Submitter: 
Virginia
Description: 
Creamy potato salad with a touch of German flavor - thanks to my Mama (from Nurnberg, Germany).
