Title:
Chicken Potato Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989684.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3 pound) broiler-fryer chicken, cut up
1 pound red potatoes, cut into chunks
1/2 cup prepared Italian dressing
1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Place chicken in a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish. Arrange potatoes around chicken. Drizzle with dressing; sprinkle with Italian seasoning and Parmesan cheese. Cover and bake at 400 degrees F for 20 minutes. Uncover; bake 20-30 minutes longer or until potatoes are tender and chicken juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Debbi  Mullins
Description: 
'On evenings I'm busy helping our two kids with homework and don't have time to spend in the kitchen, I rely on this easy recipe,' relates Debbi Mullins of Canoga Park, California. Italian dressing gives fast flavor to the juicy chicken and tender potatoes in this satisfying supper.
