Title:
Grandma's Banana Macadamia Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/454456.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1 Tbsp grated orange peel
1 tsp. vanilla
2 med. mashed bananas
1 cup flaked coconut
3/4 cup chopped macadamia or other nuts
1/4 cup orange juice

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Combine 1st 8 ingredients; beat at low speed 2-3 minutes.
Add bananas and juice.  Beat 1 minute.
Stir in coconut and nuts.
Spread into greased breadpan.
Bake 60-65 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
A fun tropical banana bread recipe from my grandma.  Grandpa recommends serving it with peanut butter.
