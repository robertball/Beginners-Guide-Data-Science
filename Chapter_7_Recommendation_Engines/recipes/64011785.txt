Title:
Whole Wheat Pancake Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups whole wheat flour
3 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
1/2 cup white sugar
2 1/2 tablespoons baking powder
4 teaspoons baking soda
4 teaspoons salt

Directions:
Whisk whole wheat flour, unbleached flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl; store in an airtight container.          To make pancakes, combine 1 cup of mix, 1 cup milk, 1 egg and 2 tablespoons oil.  Mix well and ladle 1/4 cupfuls onto medium hot skillet.

Submitter: 
Ellen Janet Nestler
Description: 
Keep a container of this handy homemade baking mix in your pantry for quick pancakes or waffles anytime you get a craving.Modified from: Whole Wheat Pancake Mix
