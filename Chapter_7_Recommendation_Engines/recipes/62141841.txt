Title:
couscous for breakfast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 oz) box couscous
2 cups milk
1/3 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup maple syrup
2 tbls butter
1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
combine all ingredients, except couscous, in a pot and bring to a boil.
take off heat, stir in couscous, cover, let stand 5 min.
fluff with fork, (if you want stir in some dried cranberries or raisins) and serve.

Submitter: 
Annie McCaffrey
Description: 
Couscous is so easy to make. i make this dish for breakfast and my family just loves it. It's great on a cold day.
