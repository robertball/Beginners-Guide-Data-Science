Title:
Easy Buffalo Potato Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1096259.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Large Potatoes
Olive Oil
Salt/Pepper to taste
1 cup Frank's Red Hot
Butter/Margarine
Ranch Dressing

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 400 Degrees.
Cut potatoes into wedges (The smaller the wedges the crispier they will be).
Spray baking sheet(s) with cooking spray and place potato wedges on sheet approx 1 in. apart.  Sprinkle/drizzle with olive oil (no measuring required!) Salt/Pepper to taste.
Bake 30 minutes.
Add enough melted butter to hot sauce to obtain desired level of "hotness".  Place in large bowl with lid.  Add baked potato wedges, replace lid, and toss around until all wedges are covered with hot sauce.
Place on plate and drizzle with ranch.  ENJOY!
