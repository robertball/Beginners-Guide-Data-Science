Title:
Oven Baked Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. Mahatma Jasmine rice
2 1/4 cup water
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. white vinegar
4 Tablespoons butter

Directions:
Butter a 2 1/2 qt. Pyrex dish.
Put rice in dish.
In a saucepan bring the water, vinegar, salt and butter to a full boil.
Pour this water mixture over the rice.
Stir and COVER.
Bake at 325 for 20 - 25 minutes.
