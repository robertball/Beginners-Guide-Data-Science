Title:
Brownie In A Mug Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c flour
1/4 c sugar
2 Tbsp cocoa
1 pinch of salt
2 Tbsp Vegetable oil
3 Tbsp water

Directions:
In a medium sized bowl combine flour, sugar, cocoa, and salt.
Next add Vegetable oil and water mix well.
pour in mug, cook in microwave on high for  1 minute 45 seconds.
