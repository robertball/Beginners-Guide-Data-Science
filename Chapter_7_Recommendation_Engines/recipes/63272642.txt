Title:
Cinnamon Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/955027.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lb bag of apples (8-9 medium sized apples)
1/4 C. Red Hots or Cinnamon Imperials
1 tsp. Lemon Juice

Directions:
Use a peeler, corer, slicer and then cut apple into fourths.
Put all ingredients in microwave-safe bowl.
Microwave on high for 15 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes.
Put apples into wide-mouth 1 qt. canning jar and seal while still hot.
Refrigerate after sealed / cool.

Submitter: 
havit-please
Description: 
Red-hot flavored apples stored in a 1 qt. canning jar, so you can make ahead of time.
