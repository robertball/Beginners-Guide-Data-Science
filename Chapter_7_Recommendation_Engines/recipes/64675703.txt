Title:
Chocolate Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups sugar
1/4 lb butter
1 can of Pet evaporated milk
1 large package chocolate chips
1 jar of marshmallow fluff
Chopped pecans

Directions:
Mix sugar, butter and milk and cook to soft ball stage
Stir in chocolate chips and marshmallow fluff
Add chopped pecans
Pour into buttered pan
Chill
