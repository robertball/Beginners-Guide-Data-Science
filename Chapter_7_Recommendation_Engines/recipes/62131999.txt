Title:
Creamy-Spicy Shrimp Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-4 cups cooked pasta (depending on family size)
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
1/2 cup chopped onion
4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 can evaporated milk
3/4 cup water
3 tablespoons tomato paste
1 lb fresh peeled/deveined shrimp
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
3/4 teaspoon OLD BAY Seasoning
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Directions:
Melt butter in medium saucepan over medium heat. Add celery, green pepper, onion, and garlic; cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes or until vegetables are tender. Stir in flour. Gradually stir in evaporated milk, water and tomato paste. Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture just comes to a boil. Cook an additional 1 to 2 minutes or until mixture slightly thickens. Stir in shrimp, parsley, seasoning, salt and pepper flakes sauce. Cook until heated through.
SERVE over pasta
NOTE:If a spicier dish is desired, substitute Creole seasoning for the OLD BAY Seasoning.

Submitter: 
Vanessa Moore
Description: 
This is the best sauce for any pasta dish ever... use it with shrimp, or chicken, even beef will taste great in this dish or add milk for a creamy styled soup.
