Title:
BOB'S Grilled Corn 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
INGREDIENTS:
1.4 Corns on the Cob
2.1/4 teaspoon Salt each cob
3.1/4 teaspoon Pepper each cob
4.4 tablespoon of Butter each cob (4)
5.1 Tablespoon of Sugar.
6.1 Teaspoon of Paprika for color (if desired)
7.1 Tablespoon of Parmasan crated (if Desired)
8.Heavy Duty Foil

Directions:
DIRECTIONS:
1.SOAK the Corn With Husks on in Cold water 30 Minutes. This allows the corn to steam on the grill
2.STRIP the Hush. “Do not remove the husk completely,”
3.REMOVE the SILK.
4.SPREAD the BUTTER with a brush on the corn,
5.ADD the SALT and PEPPER
6.ADD  SUGAR,  lightly on the cobs.
PREPARING & GRILLING
Wrap the cobs with the Husks.
Wrap each ear of corn in a double thickness of heavy-duty foil.
Grill, covered, rotate over medium heat 15 minutes.
Or until corn is tender, turning occasionally.
