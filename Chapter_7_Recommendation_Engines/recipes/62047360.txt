Title:
Cherry Twinkie Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 Twinkies
2 (3 oz) pkg cherry jello
1 cup hot water
1 cup cold water
2 (3 oz) pkg vanilla instant pudding
2 cups cold milk
12 oz. Cool Whip, thawed
1 (10 oz.) jar maraschino cherries, drained and chopped
1/2 cup flaked coconut

Directions:
Lay twinkies in the bottom of a 9x13 pan. Mix hot water and jello until dissolved; mix in cold water. Slowly drizzle jello over twinkies. Sprinkle with half of chopped cherries. Mix pudding and milk ... mixture will be thick. Carefully spread pudding over twinkies. Spread Cool Whip over the top, then sprinkle with coconut and remaining chopped cherries. Cover and refrigerate for at least 5 hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
Sue S.
Description: 
One of the easiest, most addictive dessert recipes ever! Layers of cake, pudding, whipped cream and coconut. Even if you don't like Twinkies, try it. Trust me
