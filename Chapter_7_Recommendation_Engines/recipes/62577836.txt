Title:
Cinnamon White Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup shortening
3/4 cup white sugar
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 egg
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
10 ounces white chocolate, chopped
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
1.In a large bowl, cream butter and shortening; gradually add sugars, beating well at medium speed with an electric mixer. Beat in egg and vanilla. Combine flour, soda, and salt; stir into creamed mixture. Stir in white chocolate and cinnamon. Chill dough for 1 hour.
2.Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease cookie sheets lightly. Drop dough by heaping tablespoonfuls 3 inches apart onto prepared cookie sheets.
3.Bake for 12 to 14 minutes in preheated oven. Cookies will be soft. Cool until nice and firm.
