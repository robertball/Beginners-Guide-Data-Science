Title:
Carmel Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4C popped corn
1/2C almonds
1/2C pecans
1/2C butter
1/4C corn syrup
2/3C sugar
1/4-1/2tsp vanilla

Directions:
Spread corn and nuts on greased baking sheet. Melt butter, stir in corn syrup,and sugar. Bring to a boil over medim heat. Stir. Boil for 10-15min. When mixture turns light carmel color, remove from heat and stir in vanilla. Pour over corn mixture, mix till coated. Spread out to dry or form into balls and wrap! Enjoy!
