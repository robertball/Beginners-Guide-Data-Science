Title:
Monica's Ultimate Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lbs of ground beef
1/2 of an onion
1/3 of a bell pepper
1 tbsp of seasoning salt
1 tbsp of Italtian seasoning
pinch of parsley
1 handfull of whole corn
1 jalapeno diced
1 can of tomato sauce
Parmesean Cheese
2 eggs
10 crumbled crackers crush very fine.
Sauce:
1 Can of tomato sauce
1tsp of seasoning salt
pinch of parsley or to your liking
1 tsp of sugar
1tsp of garlic salt very light
1 tbsp of italian seasoning
sprinkle powdered parmeasan cheese

Directions:
Take ground beef put in mixing bowel add onion, bellpepper, eggs crumbled saltine crackers, jalapenos, corn, seasoning salt, parsley, italian seasoning. Mix everything by hand and add to baking dish. Boil tomato sauce for about 4 minutes and also add 1 parsley, sugar, garlic salt, italian seasoning and garlic salt. Once it simmers, poke holes in the meatloaf with a wooden spoon and pour sauce over meatloaf and cook at 350 degrees for 45 min. Once meatloaf is done sprinkle with parmesan cheese on top let meatloaf cool for 10 min before serving.

Submitter: 
Monica Fuller
Description: 
I came up with this recipe because I wanted to spice up my old meatloaf. I've seen meatloaf with whole corn and I wanted to give it a try at home.
