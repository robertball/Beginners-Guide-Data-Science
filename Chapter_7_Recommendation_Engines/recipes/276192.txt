Title:
Autumn Apple Pie Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7153770.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce vanilla-flavored vodka
1 fluid ounce cinnamon whiskey (such as Fireball®)
1/4 fluid ounce lemon juice
ice cubes
1/2 cup chilled sparkling apple cider
1 dash cardamom bitters

Directions:
Combine vodka, whiskey, and lemon juice in a cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake.
Strain mixture into a glass and add cider and bitters; stir. Serve.

Submitter: 
voraciousgirl
Description: 
All the flavors of fall in a glass! Fireball® whiskey, vanilla vodka, and sparkling apple cider make this a very refreshing drink. You can add sliced apples as a garnish or pomegranate seeds for a festive look! Perfect for Thanksgiving.
