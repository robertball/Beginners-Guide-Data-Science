Title:
Donair Meat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds of lean hamburger (triple ground)
3/4 cup of bread crumbs
2 tsp peper
1-2 tsp cayenne red pepper
1 1/2 tsp oregano
3 tsp Paprika
2tsp onion powder
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2tsp salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Knead it for about 20 minutes. make 2 tight loaves. Bake on broiler pan for 2 to 2 1/2 hour at 300F. Let loaf cool down cut in slices
