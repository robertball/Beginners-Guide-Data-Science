Title:
Crunchy Walnut-Crusted Salmon Fillets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup walnuts
3 tablespoons dry breadcrumbs
3 tablespoons lemon rind, finely grated
1 1/2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
1 1/2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
Coarse salt and pepper to taste
6 (4 or 5 oz.) salmon fillets, skin on
Dijon mustard to taste
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

Directions:
Coarsely chop walnuts in food processer.
Add breadcrumbs, lemon rind, olive oil and dill, salt and pepper,  pulse until crumbly. Mix should stick together, set aside.
Arrange salmon fillets skin side down on parchment paper lined rimmed baking sheet.
Brush tops of fillets with Dijon mustard. Spoon 1/3 cup of walnut crumb mix over each fillet: gently press mixture into the surface of the fillets.
Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for up to 2 hours.
Oven 350 degrees.
Bake fillets 15 to 20 minutes or until salmon fillets are opaque throughout.
Just before serving sprinkle with lemon juice.
