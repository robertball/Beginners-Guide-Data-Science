Title:
Easy Spicey Chicken Ramen Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5290817.jpg
Ingredients:
6 c chicken stock (from rotisserie chicken)
2 c meat (from rotisserie chicken)
2 cloves garlic, grated
2 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbsp sriracha sauce
1 tbsp rice vinegar
2 handfuls fresh spinach
12 oz ramen noodles
Toppings:
Soft boiled egg
Sriracha sauce

Directions:
Place all ingredients except noodles and toppings in soup kettle and bring to simmer for ten minutes.
Meanwhile, bring water to boil in separate saucepan. Boil ramen noodles three minutes and strain.
Ladle noodles in bowl, ladle soup on top, top with toppings as desired, serve.

Submitter: 
KKraft
Description: 
Start with chicken broth from leftover rotisserie chicken for this easy and tasty soup.
