Title:
Rhubarb Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123255.jpg
Ingredients:
mini marshmallows
yellow cake mix
3 cups rhubarb
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup water
1 small pkg. strawberry jello

Directions:
Grease a 9x13 cake pan.
Place marshmallows on bottom of pan in a single layer.
Mix cake mix as directed on pkg and put on top of marshmallows.
Cook the rhubarb, sugar and water until hot.
Add the jello and stir until dissolved.
Pour over top and bake @350 degrees for 35-40 minutes.
