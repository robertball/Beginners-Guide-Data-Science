Title:
Cranberry Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cooked, boneless chicken breast halves, diced
1/3 cup  dried cherries
1/3 cup diced celery
1/3 cup toasted, chopped pecans
1/3 cup low-fat mayonnaise
1 tablespoon buttermilk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/3 cup cubed apples (optional)
Romaine

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the chicken, dried cranberries, celery, nuts, mayonnaise, milk, salt and pepper and apple if desired. Toss together well and refrigerate until chilled. Serve on toasted cracked wheat bread or croissants or over romaine.
