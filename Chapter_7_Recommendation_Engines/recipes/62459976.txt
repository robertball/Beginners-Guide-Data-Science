Title:
Garlic Kebobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine
1 head of garlic (cloves peeled and pureed in processor)
2 tbsp. brown sugar
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. pepper
2 fresh chicken breast (cubed)
2 steaks (your choice, nothing too fatty or marbled, cubed)
1 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
1 1/2 cup white pearl mushrooms
1 cup pearl onions (peeled)
1 zucchini
1 zucchini squash

Directions:
Light grill to start warming at medium temperature
Melt butter or margarine in microwave
Add pureed garlic, brown sugar, salt and pepper and integrate fully with brush
Cube chicken, steak and zucchinis
Assemble kebobs alternating whole mushrooms, tomatoes and pearl onions with the cubes of chicken, steak and zucchinis
Place kebobs on grill and brush with plenty of the garlic-butter mix
Close the grill and allow to cook for 5-7 minutes
Once the chicken is close to being done, flip once and brush on another layer of the garlic-butter mix
Leave grill open until chicken is done
Remove and let stand 5 minutes before eating (tomatoes and mushrooms stay hot the longest so beware)

Submitter: 
Sara Perry
Description: 
Absolutely finger-licking! Always make more than you expect to eat... trust me, it won't last. If you are one of those people that always adds more garlic to a recipe, DON'T DO IT, try the original first, then tweek it.
