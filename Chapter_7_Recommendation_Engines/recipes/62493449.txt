Title:
Creamy Slow Cooker Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz frozen corn
8 oz. cream cheese
4 TAB butter
2 TAB sugar
6 TAB water

Directions:
Combine ingredients in slow cooker. Cook on low for 4 hrs. Stir ever hr.
