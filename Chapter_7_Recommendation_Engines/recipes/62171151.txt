Title:
Moroccan Chicken-Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. cut-up chicken, skin & fat removed
2 large red onions, chopped
6 carrots, peeled & sliced
6 garlic cloves, minced (yes - all of it!)
3 bay leaves
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. turmeric
1 tsp. cinnamon
2 tsp. cumin
4 L. chicken stock
1/3 cup couscous
1 cup sliced green onions

Directions:
Place chicken pieces in large pot with onions, carrots, garlic, bay leaves, seasonings and broth.  Bring to a boil and reduce to low.  Simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.  Remove chicken pieces from soup and remove bones.  Cut chicken into bite sized pieced and return to pot.  Add couscous and green onions and simmer until heated through.

Submitter: 
Here's To Good Friends
Description: 
This could be a 'meal in itself soup' as it's quite filling.
