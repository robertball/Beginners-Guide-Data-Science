Title:
Rice with Pigeon Peas and Sweet Italian Chicken Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/894071.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp vegetable oil
1 cup long grain rice
1 1/2 cup water
1 tsp salt
2 Tbsp sofrito
1 envelope sazon
2 al fresco sweet italian chicken sausages, chopped
1 can (15 oz) pigeon peas

Directions:
In a non stick skillet, cook and stir sausages to brown. Set aside.
In a saucepan, heat oil and add rice. Cook and stir until it sizzles. Add sofrito, sazon, salt and stir. Add water, sausage and pigeon peas. Bring to a boil. Stir once. Reduce heat to low and simmer covered for 15-20 minutes until rice is tender and water has been absorbed.

Submitter: 
LatinaCook
Description: 
This is a twist on a Puerto Rican favorite, Arroz con Gandules. Traditionally some of us like to add pieces of pork to the rice. Al Fresco Chicken Sausage is a healthier alternative that yields delicious results.
