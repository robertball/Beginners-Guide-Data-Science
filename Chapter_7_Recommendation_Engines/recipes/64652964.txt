Title:
Chocolate Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs, lightly beaten
1 cup corn syrup
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
2 Tbsp butter, melted
1 tsp pure vanilla extract (real stuff, not just flavored)
2  pre-made pie crusts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  In a large bowl, combine eggs, corn syrup, sugar, chocolate chips, butter and vanilla.  Stir until well blended.  Add pecans.
Place each pie crust in a small pie pan.  Crimp edges of crust in a decorative pattern.  Pour half of liquid mixture into each pan.
Bake for 50-55 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean.  Cool on  a wire rack.  Store in refrigerator.
