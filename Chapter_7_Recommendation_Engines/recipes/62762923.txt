Title:
Dilly Crab Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/639397.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz. package medium pasta of your choice
8 oz. imitation crab meat, flaked
1c. sour cream
1 c. mayonnaise
1/2 c. greek yogurt
1 tsp. minced garlic
1 tablespoon dill weed
2 tsp. Old Bay seasoning
1 tsp. dried celery
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions; rinse in cold water and drain. Place in a large bowl. Add flaked crab.
Combine sour cream, mayonnaise, yogurt and all seasoning/spices. add to pasta mixture and mix well. Chill until serving.
