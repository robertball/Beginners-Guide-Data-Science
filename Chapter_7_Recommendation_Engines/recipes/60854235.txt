Title:
Turkey Tetrazini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can condensed can cream of mushroom soup
1 can condensed can cream of chicken soup
1 cup canned turkey or chicken broth
2 cups grated processed cheese
6 cups cooked spaghetti (12oz uncooked)
4 cups diced cooked turkey
1/2 cup mushrooms
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
dash of paprika

Directions:
Blend soups and broth.
Stir in processed cheese; mix with spaghetti, turkey and mushrooms.  Turn into greased 11-1/2 x 7-3/8 baking dish.
Sprinkle top with parmesan cheese and paprika.
Bake in moderate oven (350) about 30 minutes, until bubbly and browned.
