Title:
KC’s  Smoked Beef Ribeye Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6048581.jpg
Ingredients:
-Ribeye roast(whatever size you choose).
-Fiesta Brand “Uncle Chris’s Steak Seasoning”.
-Montreal Steak Seasoning.
-Olive oil.

Directions:
-Coat roast with olive oil
-Sprinkle with Uncle Chris’s and then Montreal Seasoning.
-Once smoker is up to 275 degrees, throw roast on.
-Smoke until internal temp hits 130 degrees.
-Remove from smoker and let rest at least 30 minutes.
-Enjoy!!!!!!!!
