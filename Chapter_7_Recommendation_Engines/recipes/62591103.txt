Title:
Low Fat Ranch Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Daisy Light Sour Cream
1/8 cup Dukes Light Mayo
6 tbs nonfat buttermilk
1/2 tsp onion powder
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp dill weed
1 tsp chives
1 tsp parsley
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Wisk all ingredients together.  Thin with buttermilk if necessary.  Refridgerate for at least one hour.
