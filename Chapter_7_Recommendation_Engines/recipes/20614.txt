Title:
Cedarwood Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/789147.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce lemon juice
2 fluid ounces vodka
4 fluid ounces cranberry juice
3 1/2 fluid ounces ginger ale

Directions:
In a tall glass over ice combine lemon juice, vodka, cranberry juice and ginger ale. Stir and serve.

Description: 
A cool summer punch with cranberry juice, vodka and ginger ale over ice.
