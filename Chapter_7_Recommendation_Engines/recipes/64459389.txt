Title:
French Market Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs squash, cubed
8 oz sour cream
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 onion, chopped
6 oz herb-flavored stuffing mix
1/2 cup butter
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place squash in a saucepan and cover with water, simmer over medium heat until nearly tender. Drain. Mix squash, sour cream, soup, onion and half of stuffing mix, butter, salt & pepper. Spoon into greased 13x9 baking dish. Sprinkle with remaining stuffing mix. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes.
