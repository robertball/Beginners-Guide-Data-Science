Title:
Easy Carmalized Walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pound of walnut halves
1 Stick of butter
1/2 Pound of brown sugar
2 Teaspoon of cardamom

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Melt butter. Add brown sugar and cardamom and mix well. Stir in walnut halves. Spread evenly on baking sheet. Bake 10 minutes, stir and bake another 5 minutes. Remove from oven and cool 10 minutes. Stir in the pan and cool another 45 minutes. Make sure completely cool before storing.
