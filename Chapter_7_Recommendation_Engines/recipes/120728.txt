Title:
Spaghetti 'n' Meat Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987762.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound ground beef
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 medium tomato, seeded and chopped
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1/4 teaspoon pepper
Hot cooked spaghetti

Directions:
In a skillet, cook beef and onion over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain. Add the tomato sauce, tomato, Worcestershire sauce, salt, Italian seasoning and pepper. bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 10 minutes or until heated through. Serve over spaghetti.

Submitter:
