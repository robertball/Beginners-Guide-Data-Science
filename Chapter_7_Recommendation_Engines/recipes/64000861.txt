Title:
Spicy Skillet Chicken Fiesta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1114131.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp Tajin salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 boneless skinless chicken breast
2 T vegetable oil
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 can Mexican style corn
1/2 cup salsa
2 cups cooked rice

Directions:
Coat both sides of chicken by sprinkling with chili powder, salt, and pepper.
Cook 2 cups of minute white rice while chicken is cooking
mix together, beans, corn, and salsa in a bowl
add mixture to chicken and simmer 5 minutes
serve on bed of rice
