Title:
Christmas Wassail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cranberry juice
3 1/4 cups hot water
1/3 cup sugar
6 oz. can frozen lemonade concentrate
1 stick cinnamon
5 whole cloves
2 oranges, cut into thin slices

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except oranges in slow cooker.  Stir until sugar is dissolved.
Cover.  Cook on high 1 hour.  Strain out spices.
Serve hot with an orange slice floating in each cup.
