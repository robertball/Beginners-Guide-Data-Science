Swamp Frog Punch Recipe

Ingredients:
1 (750 milliliter) bottle vodka
1 (750 milliliter) bottle gin
2 (2 liter) bottles lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
2 (0.13 ounce) packages unsweetened lemon-lime flavored drink mix (such as KOOL-AIDÂ®)

Directions:
Mix vodka, gin, lemon-lime soda, and drink mix together in a very large punch bowl until thoroughly combined and drink mix has dissolved.
Stir in ice and allow to chill for about 5 minutes.
