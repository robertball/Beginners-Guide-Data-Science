Title:
Sweettreats Crusting Butter Cream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/806073.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs C&H; powdered sugar
1 1/2 cups Icing shortening (sold at cake supply stores) (No Crisco)
1/2 cup salted butter, softened
2 tablespoons clear vanilla
1/3 cup water or non dairy coffee creamer

Directions:
In a mixing bowl cream together the shortening and butter. Add the powdered sugar, vanilla and water and mix until light and fluffy.

Submitter: 
Angela Welch
Description: 
I have been decorating cakes for twelve years and this is my favorite crusting butter cream recipe. I have tried other recipes and always go back to my old faithful recipe. It has never failed me.
