Title:
Spicy Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 Boneless, skinless chicken thighs
12 cups chicken broth
1 oz can tomato sauce
1 cup V8
1 small onion
4 cloves garlic
1-3 jalapenos seeded and deveined
1 16 oz can diced tomatoes (DRAIN)
1 10 oz can diced tomatoes with green chiles (DRAIN)
1-2 tbsp cumin
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
In skillet, cook chicken, then cool and shred.
While cooking chicken, bring to boil chicken broth.
While heating broth, puree onion, jalapenos, cumin and garlic.
When broth is boiling, add puree, tomatoes and tomatoes with green chiles along with tomato sauce and V8.
Simmer for 1 hour.
Add shredded chicken.
Serve with crackers, crispy tortilla strips, mozzarella cheese or bread
