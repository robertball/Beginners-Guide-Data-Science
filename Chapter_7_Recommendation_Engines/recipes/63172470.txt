Title:
Baked Macaroni & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. rotini pasta, cooked and drained
1 stick butter
1/2 c. flour
2 c. milk
2 bay leaves
1 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp. ground nutmeg
1/2 tsp. cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp. dry mustard
1/2 c. grated white cheddar cheese
1/2 c. grated Monterey Jack cheese
1/2 c. grated Asiago cheese
1 c. finely chopped ham
2 Tbsp. chili powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
In medium saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter. Add the flour, stirring & cooking until the flour is fully incorporated. Add the milk & bring to a simmer, stirring often. Add the bay leaf, Worcestershire sauce and spices; stir well. Add the cheeses; continue cooking & stirring until the cheese is all melted & blended into the sauce. Stir in ham.
Pour the sauce over the pasta in a large bowl; toss to coat well. Pour mixture into a large buttered casserole & spread out evenly. Sprinkle with chili powder. Bake for 35 minutes or until hot & bubbly.
