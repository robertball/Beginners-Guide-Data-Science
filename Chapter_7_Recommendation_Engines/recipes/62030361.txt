Title:
ZESTY MANHATTAN CLAM CHOWDER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2C. Thick Sliced, Hicory Smoked Bacon, finely diced
1/2C. Onion, finely minced
2Tbsps. Garlic, finely minced
3/4C. Celery, diced
2Cs. Clams, drain, reserve liquid
4Cs. Potatoes, peeled and diced into 1/2-inch cubes
2Cs. Chicken or Beef Broth
2 cans {14 1/2oz. each} Del Monte, Diced Tomatoes
Salt and Pepper to taste
1/2Tsp. Thyme Leaves, crumbled

Directions:
In a large saucepan, cook bacon, onion, garlic and celery, until bacon is a golden brown and crisp, and vegetables are tender crisp.
Stir in reserved clam liquid, potatoes and broth. Cook uncovered until tender, about 15 minutes.
Add clams, diced tomatoes and seasoning.  Heat to boiling. stir occasionally!!
Serve hot with Oyster Crackers or Texas Herb or Garlic Toast!!

Submitter: 
Corwynn Darkholme
Description: 
This is a very easy and zesty chowder to make!! Goes well with any kind of dinner party!!
