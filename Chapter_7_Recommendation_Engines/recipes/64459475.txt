Title:
Cheesy Pretzel Knots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can refrigerated pizza crust
8 oz. cheddar cheese
8 oz bacon, crispy cooked and crumbled
1 tbs olive oil
1 tbs course sea salt

Directions:
Heat oven to 350. Line cookie sheet with cooking parchment paper. Unroll dough onto cutting board. Cut 10-15 strips. Place cheese cube in center on strip and sprinkle with bacon. Wrap dough around cheese to resemble a knot. Place on cookie sheet. Brush with olive oil. Sprinkle tops with salt. Bake about 15 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.
