Title:
Skillet Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices of bacon, cooked & chopped
2 1lb cans diced tomatoes
1/2 cup chopped green pepper
1tbsp sugar
1/4 tsp pepper
1 cup chopped onions
1 lb ground beef
5oz elbow macaroni
1 tsp salt
8 oz shredded cheese

Directions:
cook bacon, pull out bacon chop it and put it back in the skillet. Add onions and peppers, cook until tender. Add ground beef, brown. Drain the mixture and return it to the skillet. Stir in tomatoes, macaroni, and seasonings. Cover and simmer 20min or until macaroni is tender. Add cheese. heat until cheese it melted. Serve.
