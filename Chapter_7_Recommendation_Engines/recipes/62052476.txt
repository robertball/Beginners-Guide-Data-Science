Title:
Party 'Shrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkgs mushrooms
3 green onions - chopped
1/2 cup fried bacon or bacon bits
1 pkg cream cheese - softened
1/2 stick butter or margarine

Directions:
Remove stems from mushrooms.  Chop stems and add onion, bacon, and cream cheese.  Stuff mushroom heads with cream cheese mixture.  Place stuffed mushrooms heads in glass cake pan.  Cut butter into thin slices and place one slice over each stuffed mushroom.  Broil in oven until warm and browned.
