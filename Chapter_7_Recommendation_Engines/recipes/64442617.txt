Title:
Kathy Bridges Barbeque Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-2 1/2 lbs chuck roast
1 large chopped onion
2 TB each crisco oil, vinegar, lemon juice, brown sugar
1 c ketchup
3 TB worcestershire sauce
1 TB mustard
2 tsp chili powder
1/2 c each water and diced celery
1 tsp salt
1 dash pepper

Directions:
mix all ingredients, pour over chuck roast. Bake and check for water as needed. mash meat to shred
