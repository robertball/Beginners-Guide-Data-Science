Title:
Snickerdoodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 eggs
2 1/2 cups flour
2 tsp. cream of tartar
1 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
3 tbsp. sugar
1 1/2 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Mix margarine and first amount of sugar together well.
Beat in eggs.
Mix in flour, cream of tartar, baking soda and salt.
Shape into 1" balls.
Stir remaining sugar and cinnamon together in small deep bowl.
Roll balls of cookie dough in sugar/cinnamon mixture to coat.
Bake on cookie sheets @ 400 x 12 minutes.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A classic. Often requested. From my favorite cookbook series. This calls for cream of tartar - which I've found most reasonably prices @ either Nutters or Bulk Barn.
