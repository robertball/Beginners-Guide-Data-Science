Title:
The Real White Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 premade pizza crust(Boboli or MamaMary's)
1 Garlic bulb
1 stick butter
16 oz. Mozzarella Cheese (slices)
16 oz. Feta Cheese
16 oz. Ricotta Cheese
2 tbsp Fresh Basil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Roast garlic bulb in foil for 20 mins.
When roasted peel off foil and skin.
Put garlic in blender with butter.
Turn oven temp. down to 375 degrees.
Spread garlic butter on pizza crust.
Add basil next, followed by cheeses.
Bake for 15-20 mins. or until bubbly.

Submitter:

Description: 
Great for those who can't eat tomatoes or tomato sauce, but love pizza!
