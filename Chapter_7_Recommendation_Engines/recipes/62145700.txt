Title:
Chorizo Sweet Potato Breakfast Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. chorizo
1 c. sweet potato, diced into 1/4 inch cubes
1/2 c. onion, diced
1 red bell pepper
1 green bell pepper
1 c. Mexican cheese, shredded
tortillas
6 eggs
olive oil
salt & pepper, to taste
Siracha sauce for added heat, if desired

Directions:
Drizzle sweet potatoes with olive oil and season with salt & pepper.
Wrap in foil and bake at 375 approximately 35 minutes or until cooked through. (This can be done the day before).
In large skillet add peppers and onion, saute until soft.
Add chorizo to skillet along with peppers and onions, brown.
Beat eggs and add to skillet, mix until scrambled.
Assemble sweet potatoes, chorizo & egg mixture, shredded cheese and Siracha sauce (if desired) to tortilla.
Roll into burrito and enjoy!
