Title:
Scot-Tini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1109046.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes
1/2 cup good quality vodka
1 tablespoon Scotch whiskey

Directions:
Fill a rocks glass with ice cubes. Pour in vodka and top off with just a splash of Scotch whiskey. Stir gently before serving.

Submitter: 
Dr.Owen L Falk
Description: 
This is a cocktail for vodka drinkers that also like a flavor of scotch
