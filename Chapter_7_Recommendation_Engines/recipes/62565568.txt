Title:
French Oven Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-2.5 pounds beef (top or bottom round)in 1" chunks
-4 large carrots quartered crosswise
-3 large ribs celery cut in 1 inch pieces
-6 oz small fresh mushrooms
-1.5 cups tomato juice
-1/2 cup water
-1/4 cup quick cooking tapioca
-1 T sugar
-2t salt
-2 small bay leaves
-1t dried basil
-1/4 t pepper
-1 cup frozen green beans
-12 oz frozen smile white onions

Directions:
Heat oven to 300 degrees. Mix all ingredients except, green beans and onions, in a heavy oven proof pot. Cover and cook 3 hours stirring 3 times during cooking. 1 hour before done add green beans and onions. Remove bay leaves before serving.

Submitter: 
Dawn Foster
Description: 
Easy to make stew thickened with tapioca. There is no gluten in this recipe!
