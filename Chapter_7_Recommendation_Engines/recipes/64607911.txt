Title:
paleo sausage meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 apple chopped
1 onion chopped
2 Tbsp fresh sage
2 Tbsp fresh rosemary
1/2 tsp sea salt
1 lb ground sausage
2 Tbsp olive oil

Directions:
Put first 5 ingredients in a food processor and mix well. Add sausage and pulse 3 or 4 times until well mixed. Roll into balls and fry in hot pan with olive oil covered for 6-8 minutes flipping once.
