Title:
Caramel Corn - Old Dutch Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/546983.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Bag of Old Dutch Puffcorn (8 oz)
1 C butter (not margarine)
1 1/4 C brown sugar
2/3 C light corn syrup
1 t baking soda

Directions:
* Preheat oven to 250 degrees.
* Combine butter, brown sugar and corn syrup in a 2 qt saucepan.
* Cook on medium heat until mixture has melted.
* Once melted, add baking soda (this will cause the mixture to foam.)
* Place puffcorn in a large roaster pan; pour caramel mixture over the puffcorn and stir until mixed.
* Place in oven for 45 minutes, stirring at least every 10-15 miutes.
* Remove from oven, pour on wax paper and break apart.
* Let cool and ENJOY!!

Submitter:

Description: 
This is always a crowd pleaser, easy to make, keeps well when set out for an evening - except it disappears pretty fast!
The recipe is on the back of the Old Dutch Puffcorn bag, we usually double the amount so that it lasts a LITTLE longer.
