Title:
Oatmeal Cookie Shot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 oz Bailey's Irish Cream
3/4 oz Butterscotch Schnapps
splash Jagermeister
splash Goldschlager

Directions:
Make sure that all ingredients are cold (I put them in the freezer).  Combine in large shot glass.  You know the rest!
