Title:
Brownie Chocolate Chip Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (19.8 ounce) package Duncan Hines dark chocolate fudge brownie mix
3 large eggs
1/2 cup vegetable or canola oil
1/4 cup water
3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRANDÂ® Sweetened Condensed Milk
3 large eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease bottom only of (4) pie tins. Prepare brownie mix as package directs for chewy brownies. Spread evenly in prepared pans. Bake 10-15 minutes or until set.
In large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy. Gradually beat in sweetened condensed milk. Add eggs and vanilla; mix well. Stir in chocolate chips. Pour into prepared pans.
Reduce oven temperature to 300 degrees F. Bake 25-30 minutes or until set. Cool. Chill thoroughly. Garnish as desired. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator or seal then freeze.
