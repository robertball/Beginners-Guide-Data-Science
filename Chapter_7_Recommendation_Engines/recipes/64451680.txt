Title:
Coeur de la Creme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5780488.jpg
Ingredients:
1    cup  heavy whipping cream
1/2  cup  confectioners sugar
1    8 oz pkg  creme cheese, room temperature
1/2  teaspoon  vanilla, optional
Lingonberries or dark chocolate sauce, for garnish

Directions:
Prepare 2 days before serving:  Line your mold with white paper towels.  Whip the cream to stiff peaks.  In a separate bowl, cream the sugar and cream cheese to well blended.  Fold the whipped cream into the sugar mix.  Drop into mold.  Cover with more paper towels. Refrigerate.  Every 8 hours, turn upside-down and replace the paper towels, refrigerate.  Serve cold and refrigerate any leftovers.
