Title:
Spring salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of mixed salad (lettuce, shredded carrots, purple lettuce etc.)
Kraft Rasberry vinegrette
12 large strawberries
1/2 cup sugared pecans (buy from store or make your own)
1 small package of goat cheese (more if you like)

Directions:
1.  Slice strawberries
2.  Toss salad with sliced strawberries
3.  Add rasberry vinegrette and toss (do not add too much as salad will get soggy)
4.  Crumble goat cheese on top
5.  Sprinkle sugared pecans on top
6.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
CHOCO4EVER
Description: 
This is a wonderful salad for the spring and summer.  Easy, beautiful, and tasty!
