Title:
Side Green Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head green leaf lettuce, chopped
2 Roma Tomatoes, chopped
1 cup salad dressing, your choice

Directions:
Wash lettuce and place in bowl.  Toss in tomatoes.  Pour in salad dressing and toss.  Place in refrigerator until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Ursula Blakely
Description: 
Easy green leaf salad, sure to please.
