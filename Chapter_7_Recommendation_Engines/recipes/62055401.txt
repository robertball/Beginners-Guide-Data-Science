Title:
SLOP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2310143.jpg
Ingredients:
1 LB GROUND BEEF
2 CANS CAMPBELLS FRENCH ONION SOUP
1 PACKET LIPTON DRY BEEF ONION SOUP
1 1/2 CUPS ELBOW MACARONI
1 1/2 CUPS OF WATER

Directions:
BROWN GROUND BEEF WITH LIPTON DRY ONION SOUP MIX. WHILE BROWNING THE BEEF COOK ELBOW MACARONI FOR 10 MINS. AFTER BEEF IS BROWNED ADD 2 CANS FRENCH ONION SOUP,WATER AND ELBOW MACARONI. SIMMER ON LOW UNCOVERED 1 HOUR STIRRING FREQUENTLY. LET STAND 10-15 MINS AND ENJOY!
