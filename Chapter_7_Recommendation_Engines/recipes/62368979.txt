Title:
eggs baked in seasoned potato skins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/409804.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large baked potatoes
2 eggs
2 tbs butter
salt,pepper,parsley
1 slice bacon, crumbled
1/3 c shredded cheese

Directions:
Slice the top off the potatoes and scoop out the insides to form a deep "bowl". Add 1/2 tbs butter, salt,pepper,and any other seasoning. Crack an egg in each potato and top with othe 1/2 tbs butter, bacon and cheese. Bake @ 350F for 15-20 minutes, sprinkle with parsley before serving.
