Title:
Easy "As Pie" Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground Beef
1 Medium onion chopped
1 15 oz. can diced tomatoes
1 15 oz. cam tomato sauce
2 15 oz. cans kidney beans/undrained
1 4 oz. can diced green chiles hot or mild
1 Tbs. minced fresh garlic
1 packet of chili seasoning mix
1 Tbs. garlic salt
1/2 can beer
1 cup water
Shredded cheese of choice (optional)
Sour cream (optional)

Directions:
In large pot brown meat and onion till browned, approx. 5-10 min.
Drain off excess fat.
Over medium heat, add all canned items.
Add garlic, seasoning mix, salt, beer and water.
Mix well and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
Serve with cheese and sour cream if desired.

Submitter: 
Brigette Schieberl
Description: 
This is as easy as it gets. Make as spicy as you like. Made with beer gives a extra flavor of yum! Serve with cheese and sour cream on top with cornbread on the side. Your family will be asking for more!
