Title:
Sandy's Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
crust:
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
3 tbsp sugar
1/3 cup melted butter
filling:
16 oz cream cheese, softened
2 eggs
3/4 cup sugar
2 tsp vanilla
1 tsp lemon juice
topping:
1 cup sour cream
2 tbsp sugar
1 tbsp vanilla

Directions:
crust: mix sugar and graham cracker crumbs together. add melted butter and mix well with a fork. put into a greased 9‚Äù pie plate
filling: on low combine sugar and eggs. add softened cream cheese and beat until smooth. stir in vanilla and lemon juice. pour over graham cracker crust. bake in 350 degree oven for about 30 minutes. let cool completely.
topping: combine ingredients and spoon over cooled cheesecake. refrigerate.

Melissa Butas
