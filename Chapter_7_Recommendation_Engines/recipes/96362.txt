Title:
JENNIE-O's Cheesy Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) package JENNIE-O TURKEY STORE® Ground Turkey
3/4 cup bread crumbs or oatmeal
1/2 cup milk
1 egg
1/2 cup American cheese, grated
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Lightly mix all ingredients together. Place in a well greased loaf pan. Place in oven and bake at 350 degrees F for one hour.

Submitter: 
JENNIE-O TURKEY STORE®
Description: 
A great meatloaf made with ground turkey and creamy American cheese is mixed in. Top it off with more cheese for an extremely cheesy meatloaf.
