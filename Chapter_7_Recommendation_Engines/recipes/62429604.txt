Title:
Easy Mango Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 ripe mango
1 18.25 oz box yellow cake mix
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup water
3 eggs
2 tbsp. fresh lime zest
Glaze:
1 tbsp. butter, melted
Juice from 1/2 lime
1-2 tbsp. mango juice (reserved from cake ingredients)
1 tbsp. golden rum (optional)
2 cups confectioner's sugar

Directions:
Cake
1. Preheat oven to 350 degress and grease bundt pan.
2. Peel mango with veggie peeler or paring knife.  Cut pieces from pit and squeeze pit for any remaining juice.
3. Smash mango into bits with potato masher or fork. You can also puree in blender or food processor.
4. Combine remaining cake ingredients and add 1 cup of smashed mango (reserve remainder for glaze). Beat by hand for 2 minutes.
5. Pour into greased pan and bake for 40-50 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
6. Cool 5 minutes in pan, then invert onto wire rack and cool completely.
Glaze
1. Combine melted butter, lime juice, mango juice and rum.
2. Add confectioner's sugar and stir until you reach a desired consistency.
3. Spoon over cooled cake.

Submitter: 
SIOBANM
Description: 
A box cake modified to include fresh mango, topped with a homemade glaze.  Serve with whipped cream for an easy, summery dessert!
