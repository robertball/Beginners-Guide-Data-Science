Title:
Instantpot Chipotle black beans (Copycat) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6691686.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbsps olive oil
2 medium yellow onions, chopped
6 cloves garlic, minced
1 pound dried black beans, rinsed and sorted
2 tsps adobo sauce
2 whole chipotle chiles
2 bay leaves
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp dried oregano
6 cups water, or more as needed
2 tbsp or juice of 1 lemon, to taste
2 tbsp or juice of 1 lime, to taste
sprig of cilantro
Salt to taste

Directions:
Add oil, and press 'Saute' button  on Instantpot
Add onions and saute until softened ( about 5 min)
Add garlic and stir until fragrant (about 30 sec)
Add beans, adobo sauce, chiles, bay leaves, cumin, oregano, and water cover
Stir to combine
Secure lid, close valve, and press 'chili|beans' button
Once cycle is complete, add lemon and or lime juice to taste
Serve with cilantro garnish
