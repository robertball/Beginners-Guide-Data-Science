Title:
Awesome Sausage, Apple and Cranberry Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 cups cubed whole wheat bread & cubed white bread
1 pound ground Sage Pork sausage
2 cup chopped onion
2 cup chopped celery
2 1/2 teaspoons dried sage
1 1/2 teaspoons dried rosemary
1 teaspoon dried thyme
2 Jonagold apples, cored and chopped
1 cup dried cranberries
1/3 cup minced fresh parsley
2 1/2 cups turkey stock
4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
2 eggs, slightly beaten

Directions:
Two days prior to cooking cubed bread and leave covered with dish cloth to dry. Preheat oven to 375 degree F.
In a large skillet, cook the sausage, onions and celery over medium heat, stirring and breaking up the lumps until evenly browned. Add the sage, rosemary, and thyme; cook, stirring, for 2 minutes to blend flavors.
Pour sausage mixture over bread in bowl. Mix in chopped apples, dried cranberries. Drizzle with turkey stock and melted butter, and mix lightly.  Spoon into 10 X 16 baking dish.
Cover with foil. Bake 30 mins. Uncover, bake another 30 mins.

Submitter: 
Kat
Description: 
Freshly toasted bread cubes, turkey sausage, chopped apples and dried cranberry combine to create a tasty fruited stuffing for a 10-pound turkey.Modified from: Awesome Sausage, Apple and Cranberry Stuffing
