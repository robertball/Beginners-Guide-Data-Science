Title:
Panini with Turkey and Roasted Red Pepper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989419.jpg
Ingredients:
2 thick slices country-style bread
1 tablespoon CARAPELLI® mild olive oil
2 teaspoons mayonnaise
2 slices provolone cheese
4 slices HORMEL® NATURAL CHOICE® Oven Roasted Deli Turkey
2 tablespoons thinly sliced PELOPONNESE® roasted red peppers

Directions:
Preheat a Panini maker or stove-top Panini grill.*
Brush one side of each bread slice with olive oil. Place, oiled side down, on a clean surface. Spread mayonnaise on each slice. Top one bread slice with 1 slice of cheese, turkey, red peppers, and 1 slice of cheese. Cover with remaining bread slice, oiled side up. Cook in panini maker 3 to 5 minutes or until the bread is browned and cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
HORMEL® NATURAL CHOICE® Deli Sandwich Meats
Description: 
Roasted red peppers add a great Mediterranean twist to these grilled cheese and turkey paninis.
