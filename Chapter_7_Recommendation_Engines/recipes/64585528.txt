Title:
Mona's Five Bean Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28 oz can Bush's original baked beans
1 16 oz can Bush's baby butter beans (drained)
1 15 oz can Van Camps pork & beans
1 15 oz can great northern beans (drained)
1 15 oz can Lima beans (drained)
1/2 c catsup
1/3 c BBQ sauce
3 rounds tbsp brown sugar
1/2 lb cut up cooked bacon

Directions:
Place all ingredients in slow cooker. Cook on low for 5-6 hours.
