Title:
KELLOGG'S® CRISPIX® French Onions 'N Chives Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985901.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 ounce) package dry French onion dip mix
3 tablespoons dry cultured buttermilk powder
2 tablespoons dried chives
8 cups KELLOGG'S® CRISPIX® cereal
2 cups pretzel sticks
3 tablespoons vegetable oil

Directions:
Combine French onion dip mix, powdered buttermilk and chives. Set aside.
In 2-gallon zipper-type storage bag, combine KELLOGG'S(R) CRISPIX(R) cereal and pretzels. Pour oil over cereal mixture. Close bag and gently toss cereal mixture until well coated. Add French onion dip mixture and close bag. Gently toss cereal mixture until well coated. Store in airtight container.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S® CRISPIX®
Description: 
KELLOGG'S® CRISPIX® mixes are an easy way to add a little zest to the appetizer table, or to a night in front of the TV. Onion flavors liven up this version quite nicely!
