Title:
Light Cream of Corn Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp light olive oil
1 medium chopped onion
1 cup chicken broth
2 cans of corn kernels with juice
2 medium potatoes cubed
½ tsp salt
¼ tsp white pepper
¼ tsp Cayenne pepper
4 cups of skim milk
Chopped parsley for garnish

Directions:
Sauté the onion in the olive oil until translucent for approximately 3 - 5 minutes.
Add the chicken broth, the corn with the juices and the cubed pealed potatoes. Get the mixture to a boil and then reduce to low/medium heat and let simmer for 20 minutes.
Add the salt, white and cayenne pepper in the last 5 minutes.
Using a stick blender, puree the mixture then add the milk.
Allow the soup to simmer for 20 minutes where it should thicken.
Serve warm and garnish with the parsley.

Submitter: 
Mama & her Girls
Description: 
No one will ever know that this soup is made with skim milk. Using the chicken broth will give it the rich taste without the added fat.
