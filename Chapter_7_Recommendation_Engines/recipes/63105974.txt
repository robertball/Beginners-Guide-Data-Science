Title:
No Bake Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 med. green peppers
1 tsp. sugar
1/2 tsp. basil
1 (15 oz.) can tomato sauce
3/4 lb. lean ground beef
1 sm. onion, chopped
2-1/4 c. Minute rice or UNCLE BEN'SÂ® quick rice
1 tsp. salt
1/2 c. grated 2% cheddar cheese
2 c. water
2 tsp. butter (optional)

Directions:
Cut peppers in half lengthwise. Remove and discard stem ends and seeds. Parboil in boiling salted water for 10 minutes; drain.
Add sugar and basil to can of tomato sauce; set aside.
Brown ground beef and onion well in a large skillet. Stir in 1/2 cup of the tomato sauce mixture, 1/4 cup of rice, and 1/2 teaspoon of the salt. Cover and simmer 5 minutes; stir in cheese.
Spoon meat mixture into peppers; place in the skillet. Pour remaining sauce around peppers; cover and simmer 5 minutes.
Meanwhile bring water, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and the butter to a boil. Stir in remaining rice. Cover; remove from heat. Let stand 5 minutes.
Serve peppers with rice; top with sauce and additional grated cheese, if desired. Makes 5 to 6 servings.
