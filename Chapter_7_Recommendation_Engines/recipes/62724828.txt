Title:
Hot Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups extra sharp cheddar, grated
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
7 ounce can diced green chiles
4 ounces black olives, sliced or chopped

Directions:
1.  Mix together all of the ingredients.
2.  Heat 3-4 minutes in microwave on high.
3.  Stir mixture after it is heated.  Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
kirklandcook
Description: 
This is a very quick to prepare appetizer that disappears quickly wherever I take it.  This is delicious served with tortilla chips.
