Title:
Balsamic Cane Reduction Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups real mayo
2 Tbsp. soy sauce
2 Tbsp. balsamic vinegar
2 Tbsp. dark brown sugar (melted in a Tbsp. of water)
1 Tsp. garlic Powder
1 Tbsp. parsley

Directions:
Combine all ingredients until smooth.

Submitter: 
Melissa Vincent
Description: 
Makes a fantastic fruit and vegetable dip and a great dressing for salads.
