Title:
Key Lime Sauce for Seafood & Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/611549.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup reduced-sodium chicken or vegetable broth
2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
2 tbsp fresh lime juice
3 tbsp finely chopped sweet onion
2 tsp canola oil
1 tsp finely grated lime zest
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 tsp ground black pepper

Directions:
Quick saute onion and garlic in oil in non-stick pan.  Add broth and simmer briefly to combine and reduce slightly.  Add to remaining ingredients in a medium bowl and mix well to combine. Refrigerate until ready to use.
WW PointsPlus value per serving:  1
(Serving size:  1/4 cup)
