Title:
Neverland Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
1 1/2 cups peanut butter
1 cup margarine
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup brown sugar, firmly packed
2 eggs, slightly beaten
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1 cup white chocolate morsels
1 cup of coarsely chppped macadamia nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Combine flour, baking powder, and baking soda in a small bowl; set aside.
Beat peanut butter, margarine and both sugars in a large bowl with elecric mixer on medium-high until creamy. Add eggs, one at a time, blending after each addition. Add vanilla. Gradually add flour mixture beating on low speed after each addition. Stir in morsels and nuts.
Drop batter by rounded tablespoons onto an ungreased cookie sheet, (if you own a stone, that works even better!) 1-1/2 inches apart. Bake for 15-16 minutes. Let them cool for 5 minutes. Remove to wire rack. Cool completely.
