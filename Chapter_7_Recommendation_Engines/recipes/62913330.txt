Title:
African Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Large Cloves Garlic
1 Bunch Cilantro (leaves and stems)
1/2 cup Peanut Butter
2 teaspoons cumin
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon cayenne
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup chicken broth
6 to 8 boneless and skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1" pieces
1 can (28 oz) diced tomatoes with juice
2 lbs sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
1 can garbanzo beans, drained
2 cups fresh green beans, ends snapped, cut into 1" lengths
2 Cups Whole Wheat Couscous

Directions:
Puree Garlic, Cilantro, Peanut Butter, Spices, Salt, and Chicken Broth in blender.
Spray crock with non-stick spray.
Layer Chicken, Garbanzo Beans and Sweet Potatoes in crock.  Cover with sauce.  Cook on Low for 5 to 6 hours, until chicken and potatoes are very tender.
Gently stir Green Beans into stew.  Cover and let cook on Low for 15 minutes.
Serve over Whole Wheat Cous Cous.
