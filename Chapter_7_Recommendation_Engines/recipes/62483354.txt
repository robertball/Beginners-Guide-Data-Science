Title:
Cinnamon Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/487315.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups apples peeled and sliced
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup butter
1 cup all-purpose flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place apples in large bowl.  Sprinkle with 1 teaspoon of cinnamon and 1/4 cup sugar.  Add water and mix until all apples coated.
In a separate bowl, cream the remaining sugar and butter.  Blend in flour and remaining cinnamon.  Sprinkle mixture evenly over apples.
Bake in preheated oven 35 minutes or until mixture is bubbling. Broil under low for 2 minutes or until topping is golden brown.
