Title:
Morning After Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cups all-purpose flour, sifted
3 tea baking powder
1/3 tea salt
2 TBL sugar
1 1/4 cup whole milk, brought to room temp
1 egg, room temp
4 tea melted butter
1/2 tea vanilla

Directions:
1.  In large bowl, mix all dry ingredients and sift.
2.  In medium bowl, mix egg then add milk and butter.
3.  Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and stir lightly until barely incorporated.
4.  Heat griddle or frying pan to med-hi heat.   Add butter or oil.   Scoop 1/2 cup of batter onto griddle.   Brown on both sides and serve with hot with maple syrup.
