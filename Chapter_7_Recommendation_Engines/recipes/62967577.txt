Title:
BAKED ONION RINGS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 egg whites
3 Tbsp.milk
big pinch cayenne pepper
2 med.yellow onions
1/2 cup flour
salt and pepper to taste
2-3 cups panko[japanese bread crumbs]

Directions:
Mix egg,milk and cayenne together.
Pour panko into shallow pan.
Cut onion into 1/4 in. rings,separate
With fork coat rings in flour batter,let excess drip off.With another fork coat well in panko crumbs.
Transfer to greased cookie sheet.Spray lightly with oil.
Bake 12-15 min. at 400.
