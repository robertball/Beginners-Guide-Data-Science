Title:
Pickle Wheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Dried beef
3 oz. Cream cheese
Whole dill pickles (smaller - medium size works best)

Directions:
Dry pickles well with paper towel.
Spread cream cheese on 2 slices of beef.
Roll around a pickle. Slice in 1/2 inch slices.
Continue until the beef and cheese are all used.

Submitter: 
Jeniflower
Description: 
We demanded these from my friend for every party!  They are always a hit and also make a great low-carb snack.
