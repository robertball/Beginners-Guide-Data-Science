Title:
Banana Chocolate Walnut Frozen Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Cups Greek Yogurt
1 Cup Sugar
3 large ripe frozen bananas
3/4 Cup Mini Chocolate Chips
3/4 Cup Chopped Walnuts

Directions:
In a blender, mix sugar, yogurt and bananas until well blended.  Pour into ice cream maker for time according to your machine.   Add nuts and chips while mixture is still soft, about 15 minutes into processing time.  Serve or freeze for later.
As an alternative, mix in nuts and chips after blending and put directly into freezer.
Tip:  When bananas are too ripe to eat, peel them and keep in a freezer bag for smoothies and ice cream.
