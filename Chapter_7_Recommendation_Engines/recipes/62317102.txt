Title:
Sunshine cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 yellow cake mix
4 eggs
1/4 cup applesauce
1 (11oz) can mandarin oranges with juice
1 (8oz) pkg whipped cream
1 lrg box instant vanilla pudding
1 (15 oz) can crushed pineapple

Directions:
Mix cake mix, eggs, oranges with juice and applesauce together. Pour into greased 9x13 inch pan. Bake at 325 for 35-40 minutes.
For frosting mix pudding with pineapple. Fold in whipped cream until well blended. Cover and refigerate until ready to serve.
After cake has cooled, refrigerate until ready to serve. Spread icing on top just before serving.
