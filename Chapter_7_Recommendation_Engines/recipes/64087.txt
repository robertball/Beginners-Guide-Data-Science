Title:
Red Pepper Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) jar roasted red peppers
1 cup cilantro fresh
6 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
6 tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon Savory Marketâ„¢ Oven Roasted Garlic Flavor
1 teaspoon mustard, ground
1 teaspoon coriander, ground
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 cup pine nuts, chopped

Directions:
Puree first nine ingredients in processor until smooth. Add nuts until chopped, but not ground. Season with salt and pepper. The pesto can be made two days ahead. Cover and chill. Let stand one hour at room temperature before serving.
Serve over pasta with grated parmesan.

Submitter: 
Savory Market
Description: 
This is really one of my favorites. Easy to make and who can resist the wonderful flavor of pine nuts and roasted red peppers?
