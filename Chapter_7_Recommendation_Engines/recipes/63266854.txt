Title:
Foolish Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 avocados, skinned and pitted
1 roma tomato, diced
1/2 red onion, diced
1/2 jalapeno, minced
1 tbsp. finely chopped cilantro
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. cumin
1/2 tsp. white pepper
1 tbsp. lime juice
2 shakes of hot sauce
salt and pepper

Directions:
Mash the avocados and mix in all other ingredients.
