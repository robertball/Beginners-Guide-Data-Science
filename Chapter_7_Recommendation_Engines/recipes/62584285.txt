Title:
Stuffed & Wrapped Veal Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 6oz veal tenderloins - butterflied
6 strips of bacon
2 tbsp of butter
2 shallots, diced
1 cup of chopped mushrooms
2 tbsp red wine
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Heat the 2 tablespoons of butter in a small saucepan, add the shallots and mushrooms. Saute for about 5 minutes. Add the wine and let cook through another 5 minutes.
Place a tablespoon of the shallot mixture in the center of the butterflied tenderloin. Close and wrap a piece of bacon around to close. Repeat with the remaining tenderloins.
Top the tenderloins with the remaining mixture and salt and pepper to taste. Bake at 350 for about 30 minutes.
