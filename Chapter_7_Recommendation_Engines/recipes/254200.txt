Title:
Fruit Punch Wine Slushie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4458830.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 (750 milliliter) bottle red wine
1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen concentrated fruit punch
ice cubes

Directions:
Combine wine and fruit punch concentrate in a blender. Fill the rest of blender with ice; blend until mixture is the consistency of a slushie.

Submitter: 
cheryl pd
Description: 
These are so popular at fairs, but I never knew how to make one until now. So simple and great for a hot day! You could use whatever combination of wine and frozen concentrate juice you would like.
