Title:
Provencal grilled chicken breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup diced onion
1 T chopped fresh oregano
1 t chopped fresh thyme
1 t chopped rosemary
1 t chopped fresh savoury
1 t fresh lavendar
2 T olive oil
1 T lemon juice
4 - 6 oz frenched skin-on boneless chicken breasts

Directions:
preheat grill to medium
puree onion, herbs, 2 tbsp oil, lemon juice and a bit of salt and pepper in a food processor
spread onion mixture over chicken and grill about 15 minutes on each side, rotating 90 degress on each side halfway through
