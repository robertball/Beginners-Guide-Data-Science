Title:
Soft Chocolate Chip Cookies--r and r bean Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter (2 STICKS)
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
Put butter, eggs, sugar and vanilla into the mixer.
Beat until it looks smooth.
Add salt and baking soda and mix some more.
Add the flour and mix some more.
Last add the chocolate chips.
Mix together until chocolate chips are well mixed into the dough.
Put walnut sized balls on cookie sheet and bake for about ten minutes.
