Title:
Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871678.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbsp	butter
1	small onion - diced small
4	stalks celery - sliced thin
3	carrots - diced
4	cloves garlic - minced
8 cups	Homemade Chicken Stock
1 tsp	fresh thyme - chopped
diced meat from one chicken
egg noodles

Directions:
Melt butter in a large pot, add onions and saute until transparent.
Add celery, carrots, and garlic and sweat vegetables a little. Add
the chicken stock and bring to a boil, then lower heat to simmer for
15 minutes until carrots are tender. Add the chicken and bring back
to a roiling boil. Add noodles and boil until tender, about 15
minutes.. Lower heat and season to taste. Simmer 15 minutes longer,
then serve.
