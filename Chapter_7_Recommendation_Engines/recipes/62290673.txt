Title:
Sausage and Zucchini Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound bulk Sausage
2 medium Zucchinis
1 Tbs. Vegetable Oil
1 Pound Ricotta Cheese
1 Egg White
8 oz. Mozzarella Cheese
3/4 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
2 cups Spaghetti Sauce
6-9 No Boil Lasahna Noodles

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 Farenheit
Quater and slice zucchini.
In a skillet over medium heat cook sausage and zucchini until sausage is no longer pink and zucchini is tender.
Combine Ricotta cheese, egg white, 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese, and mozzarella cheese in a bowl.
Prepare a 9*13 casserole dish by lightly covering the bottom with spaghetti sauce, add lasagna sheets on top of sauce layering as needed to fit. Next add half of the sausage mixture follwed by half of the cheese mixture. Top with 1 cup of spaghetti sauce. Continue to layer all ingredients.
On top of the final sauce layer sprinkle the remaing parmesan cheese over  the top.
Bake for 45 minutes or until top is bubbly and browned.
