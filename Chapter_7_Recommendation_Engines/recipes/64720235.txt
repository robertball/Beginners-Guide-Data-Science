Title:
Posole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cloves garlic, diced
1 small onion, diced
1 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp salt
1 can (14.5 oz.) tomatoes
1 cup chicken stock
1 pound boneless county-style pork ribs, cut into 1-inch cubes
1 can (14.5 oz.) hominy, rinsed and drained

Directions:
Place all ingredients except hominy in heavy pot. Bring to a boil, reduce to simmer, cover and cook 1 hour.  Add hominy; simmer 30 mins or until pork reaches a safe internal temperature of 145 degrees.
Serve with Chopped cilantro, thinly sliced radishes, crumbled queso fresco, warmed corn tortillas and lime wedges. I SKIP ALL THESE AND SERVE WITH  CHOPPED AVOCADO.1
