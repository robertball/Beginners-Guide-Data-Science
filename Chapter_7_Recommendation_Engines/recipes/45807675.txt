Title:
Roasted Potato Medley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 russet potato, cubed small
1 red potato, cubed small
1 sweet potato, peeled and cubed small
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
2 teaspoons minced fresh thyme, or 1 tsp. dried
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup low fat, low sodium chicken broth

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
In a mixing bowl, combine the russet potato, red potato, sweet potato, olive oil, vinegar, thyme, garlic and chicken broth. Toss well to coat.
Place into casserole dish and roast uncovered for 40-50 minutes, stirring at 15 and 30 minutes, or until potatoes are tender.
