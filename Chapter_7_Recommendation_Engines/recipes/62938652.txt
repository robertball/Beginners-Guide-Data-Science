Title:
One Pot Lazy Cabbage "Rolls" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. ground chuck
2 tsp. olive oil
1 onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 med. head cabbage, diced
2 c. long grain white rice
4 1/2 c. water
2 cans condensed tomato soup
1 can water
1 can tomato sauce
1 envelope of dried onion soup mix
Salt and pepper, to taste.

Directions:
Brown ground chuck, drain and set aside.
Sautee onion, garlic, and cabbage in olive oil until slightly softened.  Set aside.
Prepare rice with 4 1/2 c. water in a large pot.
Turn burner off.
Add ground chuck, cabbage mixture, soups, tomato sauce, onion soup mix.
Mix thoroughly.
Place lid on pot as it continues to "steam" the cabbage mixture, about 10 minutes.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Serve in bowls with a dash of hot sauce.

Submitter: 
TARAWVU
Description: 
I came up with this one evening when I wanted cabbage rolls but didn't feel like the hassle of actually rolling the meat mixture in the cabbage and turning on the oven.
