Title:
Vitamin Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup grated cabbage
2 chopped pimentoes
1/2 cup shredded raw carrots
1 package lemon jello
1 3/4 cups boiling water
1 shredded apple
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 tablespoons lemon juice or vinegar
Mayonnaise

Directions:
Pour boiling water over jello. Allow to stand until cool and slightly thickened. Fold in remaining ingredients. Chill and serve with mayonnaise dressing.
