Title:
SUPER EASY PUMPKIN SPICE CAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/760464.jpg
Ingredients:
1 BOX SPICE CAKE
1 LARGE CAN PUMPKIN PIE MIX (30 0Z)
COOL WHIP OR FROSTING

Directions:
COMBINE 2 INGREDIENTS AND COOK ACCORDING TO SPICE CAKE DIRECTION.
COOL AND TOP WITH COOL WHIP.
