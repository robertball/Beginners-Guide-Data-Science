Title:
Fresh Fig Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/293468.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups fresh figs (peeled, cut in half)
1 tbsp. cornstarch
7 tbsp. sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon or apple spice
6 tbsp. butter
Juice of 1/2 lemon
pie crust, unbaked

Directions:
Select figs that are ripe and firm.
Peel figs and cut in half.
Place figs in an unbaked 10 inch pastry shell.
Combine cornstarch, sugar and spice; sprinkle over figs. Add lemon juice and dot with butter.
Form lattice top with strips of pastry.
Bake in a 425 degree oven for 30 to 40 minutes or until brown.

Submitter: 
Francesca de Oliveira
Description: 
A simple but delicious pie. Puts good use to fresh figs and is a surprising family favorite!
