Title:
Almond Butter and Blueberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1321874.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup almond milk
1 cup blueberries
4 ice cubes, or more to taste
1 scoop vanilla protein powder
1 tablespoon almond butter, or more to taste
1 tablespoon chia seeds, or more to taste

Directions:
Blend almond milk, blueberries, ice cubes, vanilla protein powder, almond butter, and chia seeds in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Cindy Anschutz Barbieri
Description: 
My almond butter and jelly smoothie is a spin-off of 'peanut butter and jelly' and it is my perfect mid-afternoon snack or sometimes even my lunch!
