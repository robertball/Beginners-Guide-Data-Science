Title:
Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Haas avacados (Fresh and not too ripe!)
1/2 Tbl. white onion
2 Tbl. Sour Cream
1 Tbl. Salsa
2 or more Teaspoons Salt
1 Teaspoon Pepper

Directions:
Chop the onion fine
Smoosh the avacados and add all the rest!
Enjoy!

Submitter:

Description:
