Title:
Sausage, Egg and Cheese Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1848679.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cans of regular biscuits
8 eggs
1/4 cup milk
1 tsp ground mustard
Salt and pepper to tast
1 lb of breakfast sausage
1 4oz pkg of shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown the sausage in a skillet and drain well.
Beat the eggs into a bowl and add the 1/4 cup of milk, ground mustard, and salt and pepper to taste.
Slice the biscuits into quarters. In batches, dip the biscuits into the egg mixture and place in a Bundt pan.
Create 2-3 layers of biscuits, sausage, cheese. Pour the remaining eggs over the layers.
Bake at 350 for about 35-40 minutes or until the top is golden brown.
Serve immediately

Description: 
Rich breakfast bread that you can slice or pull apart with your hands.
