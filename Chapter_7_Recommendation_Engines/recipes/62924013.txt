Title:
7 bean crock pot soup (Henry's) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups dry beans (or 1 quart jar) 7-bean soup from Henry's markets. You will find the 7-bean combo in a big bin of dried goods.
12 cups water or 3 quart jars of water
3 tbsp chicken boullion granules
3 tbsps minced onion, dry
1 tbsp pepper
2 tsps salt

Directions:
Soak beans in a big pan or pot of water overnight
Rinse beans thoroughly prior to putting into crock pot
Put in crock pot
Cover with 3 quarts of water (or 12 cups). Setting high on crock pot.
When water is just getting warm (in crock pot) add all remaining spices.
Cook on high approx 6 hrs, may reduce to low and cook additional 2 hrs. as needed. Beans should be soft.

Submitter: 
Dede
Description: 
This pot of bean soup is excellent on a cold winter day. Cook in crock pot all day. 
Any left over beans, after broth is gone, can be addede to a warm four tortilla.
