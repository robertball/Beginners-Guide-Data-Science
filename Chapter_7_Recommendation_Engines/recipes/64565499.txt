Title:
Egg roll Stirfry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey, beef or pork
1/2 onion, diced
1 small head cabbage, shredded
3 carrots, shredded
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp alpine touch seasoning
2 tablespoons sesame oil
1 tablespoon canola oil
1/4 cup soy sauce

Directions:
Brown meat and onion in large skillet, do not drain
Add cabbage and carrots, cook medium high 3-4 minutes
Mix oils, garlic, ginger, soy sauce and seasoning in bowl
Reduce heat to medium, add soy sauce mixture to skillet and continue to cook 5-10 minutes until cabbage is tender.
