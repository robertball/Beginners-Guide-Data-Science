Title:
Jimmy Dean Hearty Holiday Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983667.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (16 ounce) packages Regular Flavor Jimmy Dean Pork Sausage
2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed minestrone soup
2 (15 ounce) cans pinto beans
1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes and green chiles
1 cup water or stock
1 tablespoon chili powder (optional)

Directions:
In large skillet, cook sausage over medium high heat, stirring frequently until thoroughly cooked and no longer pink. Add remaining ingredients; bring to a boil. Cover and simmer on low heat for 45 minutes.

Perfect for warming up on a cold day, this hearty chili comes together with very little effort.
