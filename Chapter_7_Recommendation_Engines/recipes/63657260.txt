Title:
Cliantro Lime Cauliflower Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Head of cauliflower “riced” either had grate the cauliflower or run through the food processor
3/4 of a cup of freshly squeezed lime juice
1 tsp salt
3 tablespoons cilantro, chopped

Directions:
In a skillet sautee riced cauliflower until tender.
Stir in lime juice, salt and cilantro.
