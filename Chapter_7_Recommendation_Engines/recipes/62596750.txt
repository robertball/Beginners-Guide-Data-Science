Title:
French Toast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups bread raison cinnamon bread cut in cubes
4 eggs
1 1/2 cups milk
1/4 cup white sugar, divided
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 tablespoons butter, softened
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly butter an 8x8 inch baking pan.
Line bottom of pan with bread cubes. In a large bowl, beat together eggs, milk, salt and vanilla. pour egg mixture over bread. Dot with butter; let stand for 10 minutes. Sprinkle cinnamon over the top.  Bake in preheated oven about 45 to 50 minutes, until top is golden.
