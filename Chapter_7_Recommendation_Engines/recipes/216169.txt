Title:
Creamy Spinach Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991232.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter, melted
1/2 cup chopped onions
1 clove garlic, minced
1 (8 ounce) package PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened
1 (8 ounce) container BREAKSTONE'S Sour Cream
3 eggs
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed, well drained
1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Directions:
Heat oven to 325 degrees F.
Melt butter in small skillet on medium heat. Add onions and garlic; cook and stir 5 minute or until crisp-tender. Beat next 5 ingredients in large bowl with mixer until well blended. Add onion mixture and spinach; mix just until blended.
Spoon into 9-inch square pan sprayed with cooking spray. Sprinkle with nutmeg; cover.
Bake 45 minute or until heated through and lightly browned, uncovering after 30 minute.

Submitter: 
BREAKSTONE'S
Description: 
Spinach is baked in a creamy base of BREAKSTONE'S Sour Cream, cream cheese and eggs in this easy family-pleasing favorite side dish.
