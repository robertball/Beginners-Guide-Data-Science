Title:
Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Egg Whites
2 - 3 pounds sweet potatos
1 cup mini marshmellows
1/2 cup sugar free maple syrup or honey
1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk or milk of choice
1 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
1 tsp salt
3 - 6 packets Stevia or 1 T sweetner of choice
1 tsp Vanilla extract
Topping:
3/4 cup chopped pecans
1/4 cup sugar free maple syrup or honey
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp salt
1-2 packets of Stevia or a tsp of sweetner of choice
Optionl: 1/2 cup old fashioned oats

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees. Pierce sweet potato several times with the times of a fork or knife (this lets steam out while baking)Place the sweet potatos on a large baking sheet lined with foil. Bake until tender, about 40-45 minutes. Turn oven down to 375 degrees, and spray a large (9x13 inch) casserole dish with cooking spray.
Gather ingredients.
Once sweet potatoes are cooled enough to handle, cut sweet potato in half lengthwise and remove the skin.
Place the sweet potato in a blender or food processor with other ingredients and pulse until smooth, Pour into prepared casserole dish.
Meanwhil, to make the topping, mix together pecans, syrup and cinnamon in a small bowl, (and oats if desired). Toss pecans in syrup util well coated. Sprinkle over sweet potato mixture.
Bake for 40-45 minutes or until sweet potatoes are 'set' and/or no longer jiggle in the middle.
remove from oven and top w/ mini marshmellows and put back in over at 400 and just bake until marshmellow puff and brown slightly about 2-4 minutes
Serve warm

This is a lighter version to regular sweet potato casserole.
