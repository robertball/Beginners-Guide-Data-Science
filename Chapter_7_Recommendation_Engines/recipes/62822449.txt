Title:
Rosemary Pork Skewers with mango cream, roasted fingerlings and mango shallot salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/666828.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/4 pounds boneless pork ribs country style cut into 1 1/2 inch cubes
5 rosemary sprigs
Salt and pepper
1/4 teaspoon granulated garlic
1/3 cup pork fat drippings
1/4 cup olive oil
1 cup instant potato flakes
24 ounces fingerling potatoes
2 tablespoons chopped rosemary
3 tablespoons olive oil
3 garlic cloves minced
5 shallots halved
3 big pinches kosher salt
10 twists of the pepper mill
3 shallots diced
1 garlic clove sliced
1 tablespoon olive oil
3/4 cup mango puree
1 1/2 cups water
1/2 lime juiced
2 teaspoons chile paste
1/2 cup heavy cream
2 1/2 peeled halved and thinly sliced
4 large shallots thinly sliced
1 lime juiced
6 teapoons olive oil
3 teaspoons mango puree
1 pinch of salt

Directions:
Insert a boning knife to make a whole in each pork cube.  Thread 6 pieces of pork onto each of the 5 rosemary sprigs.  Seaon with salt and pepper and the granulated garlic.
Cut the large fingerlings in half and put in a large bowl. Add the chopped rosemary, garlic, halved shallots, 3 tablespoons olive oil, kosher salt and pepper and toss to combine.  Place on a greased sheet pan and cook at 425 degrees for 25 minutes turning once.
Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a pan over medium heat and add the 3 minced shallots and 2 sliced garlic cloves and sautee for 5 minutes.  Add the 3/4 cup mango puree, water, 1/2 lime juiced, chile paste and cook for 15 minutes.  Strain and return the liquid to the pan.  Reduce the liquid for about 7 minutes until nearly a syrup.  Add the cream and cook for 8 minutes until thickened over medium heat.
Add the potato flakes to a pie pan and coat the pork in them.  Add the pork fat drippings and the 1/4 cup olive oil to a fry pan over medium heat.  Fry for about two minutes on all sides and drain on paper towels.
Whisk together the juice of 1 lime, 3 teaspoons mango puree, 6 teaspoons olive oil and a pinch of salt.  Dress the sliced shallots with a little dressing.  Serve with 1/2 sliced mango and the dressed shallots on top.  The fingerlings and then the skewer with the mango cream on top.
