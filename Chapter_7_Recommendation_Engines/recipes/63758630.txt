Title:
Big Ray's Mini Breakfast Quiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1042521.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups broccoli florets
6 strips bacon
12 eggs
1/2 cup milk
1 medium onion
1 bell pepper
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground pepper

Directions:
In a medium skillet, cook bacon until slightly crisp (8 to 10 minutes).  Dice and set aside to cool.
Dice onion and bell pepper.  In same skillet with bacon grease, cook onion and pepper until slightly brown.
Take broccoli and stem about 3 minutes.  Chop and set aside to cool.
In a medium bowl, whisk eggs and milk.  Stir in bacon, onions, pepper, cheese , salt and pepper.
Coat a 12 cup nonstick muffin tin with cooking spray. Fill cups with mixture.  Bake for 20 to 25 minutes until cooked through at 375 degrees.
Cool slightly.  Remove quiches.  Refrigerate remaining quiches
