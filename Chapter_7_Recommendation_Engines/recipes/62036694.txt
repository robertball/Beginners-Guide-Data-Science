Title:
Hot Pesto Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayo
1 pkg pesto sauce mix (Knorr makes one)
1/2 chopped toasted walnuts
1/4 cup sour cream
1/4 grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
In microwavable bowl combine mayonaise, sauce mix, walnuts, sour cream and parmesan.  Microwave on high 1 1/2 minutes, stirring once.  Microwave 30 seconds longer or until heated through. Stir.  Serve with melba rounds, crackers or crusty bread.

Submitter: 
jmacsaunders
Description: 
Hot and creamy.  Very Easy and Microwavable.  Serve with crackers or crusty bread.  Guaranteed party favorite.
