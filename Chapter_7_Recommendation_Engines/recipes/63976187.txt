Title:
Chocolate Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jiggers Chocolate Liqour
1 jigger Cream de Cocoa
1 jigger Vanilla Vodka
2 jiggers Vanilla Almond Milk or Coffee Creamer
Chocolate Syrup

Directions:
Combine first 4 ingredients into a cocktail shaker with ice and shake until container is cold. Squirt chocolate syrup around top of martini glasses and strain cocktail into glass.
