Title:
Ultimate Alexander Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup cold brewed coffee
2 pts. coffee ice cream
1/2 cup brandy
1/2 cup chocolate syrup
Garnish: sweetened whipped cream, chocolate curls, and milk chocolate sticks

Directions:
Process cold brewed coffee, ice cream, brandy, and chocolate syrup in a blender until smooth, stopping to scrape down sides. Pour mixture into glasses, and garnish, if desired. Serve immediately.
