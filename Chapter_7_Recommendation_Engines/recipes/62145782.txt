Title:
Grape Jelly Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground chuck
1-2 eggs
1 onion
2 Tbsp parsley
1 tsp oregano
1 clove garlic minced
1/2 tsp basil
1 tsp salt
1/3 cup cracker meal
1 medium bottle ketchup
1 small jar of grape jelly

Directions:
Saute chopped onions in a little butter or oil.  Add to cground chuck plus all other ingredients except ketchup and jelly.  Roll into small meatballs.  Brown on all sides turning frequently and carefully over medium heat.  Pour ketchup into large pot and melt over medium low heat.  Add jelly and then meatballs.  Simmer until meatballs are fully cooked (about 45 minutes) Refrigerate overnight.  Heat before serving.
