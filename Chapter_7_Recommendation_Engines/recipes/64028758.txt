Title:
Pea, Brocoli and Feta Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. penne pasta
1 head of broccoli, cut into small florets
4 tsp. red wine vinegar
1 tsp. Dijon mustard
1 tsp. honey
3 tbsp. olive oil
1/3 cup frozen peas, thawed
1/4 tsp. red pepper flakes
1/4 cup fresh mint - chopped
1.4 cup flat-leaf parsley - chopped
1 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
Cook penne in boiling salted water and add broccoli during last 3 minutes of cooking time.
Combine mustard, honey and olive oil in a small jar with a pinch of salt and shake well.
Toss hot pasta and broccoli with half of the dressing, stir in peas and red pepper flakes and cool.
Once pasta is cool, add chopped herbs and feta cheese.
Toss with remaining dressing and add salt and pepper to taste.
