Title:
Gluten-free Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/969918.jpg
Ingredients:
* 1 3/4 cups gluten-free all-purpose flour
* 4-5 mashed medium ripe bananas
* 3 eggs
* 1 cup sugar (100% sugar cane)
* 1/3 cup coconut oil
* 1/2 cup unsweetened apple sauce
* 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
* 1 teaspoon baking soda
* 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
* 1 teaspoon organic cinnamon
* 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
No Mixer needed!
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
In large bowl, combine flour, baking soda, sugar, salt, and cinnamon. Mix together with a large spoon.
In a small bowl, combine mashed bananas, eggs, apple sauce, vanilla and coconut oil. Mix together with a large spoon.
Combine small bowl of liquid ingredients into the large bowl of dry ingredients. Mix together for several minutes.
Coat a 9x5 bread pan with cooking spray. Pour batter into pan and top with walnuts.
Bake at 350Â° for 50-60 minutes, or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.
Cool for at least 10 minutes before removing bread from pan(s).
