Title:
Chocolate Cookie Pretzel Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
2 cups crushed pretzels
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter - melted
Pie:
1 cup butter - softened
3/4 cup light brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
2 1/4 cups flour
2 cups milk chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Crust:
Combine crushed pretzels, melted butter and sugar in a bowl and mix well.
Press firmly into bottom and up the sides of a 10" pie pan.
Bake for 8 minutes.
Filling:
Cream butter and sugars until fluffy.
Add eggs and vanilla and mix until smooth.
Add baking soda, flour and salt and mix until well combined.
Stir in chocolate chips and press mixture into crust.
Bake for 30-35 minutes then remove from oven, cover to prevent burning and bake another 15-20 minutes.
Let pie cool COMPLETELY before serving - if you do not, the inside will be mushy.
