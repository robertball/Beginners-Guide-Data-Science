Title:
ratatouille tart Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-2 12 c ratatouille (from long recipe version)
3 eggs 1/2 c milk, as needed
1/4 teaspoon salt
fresh ground black pepper
1 oz gruyere cheese, grated (1/2 c tightly packed)
1 oz parmesan cheese, grated (1/4 c)
pie crust

Directions:
mix everything together, including the parmesan. prebake a pie crust for 7 mins, allow to cool, then fill with the ratatouille mixture. bake for 30-40 mins, until the top is nicely browned
