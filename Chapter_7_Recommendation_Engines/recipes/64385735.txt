Title:
Ally's Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Racks of Pork Spareribs
1 1/2 Cup of Regular BBQ Sauce
1 Teaspoon of Apple cider vintages
1 Tablespoon of Dijon Mustard
2 Tablespoons of honey
1 Teaspoon of Red Pepper Flakes
1 Tablespoons of granulated Garlic

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees
In a large pot, boil ribs for 2-3 hours (or until tender)
Place cooked ribs on broiler pan
Sprinkle with Garlic Powder
In a medium bowl, mix together BBQ sauce, Apple cider vinegar, mustard, and honey
Apply sauce to ribs
Heat in preheated oven for 1 hour

Submitter: 
RiceLover
Description: 
These ribs have just the right mixture of tangy sweetness and a little bit of fire! Boil them until tender, heat slowly in the oven, and enjoy!!!
