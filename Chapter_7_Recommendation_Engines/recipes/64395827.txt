Title:
Pistachio Crusted Salmon Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c salmon
1/2 c bread crumbs
1 T dijon
1 t grated lime zest
S&P;
1 lrg Egg
1 c ground pistachios
3 T vege oil
Lime

Directions:
combine salmon, bread crumbs, mustard, zest & S&P. Add egg.
Form into patties, coat with pistachios.
Refrigerate for  30 min up to 12 hours
Cook the cakes in oil.
Serve with lime wedges.
