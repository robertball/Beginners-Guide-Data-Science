Title:
German Chocolate Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup evaporated milk
1 cup sugar
3 egg yolks, beaten
1/2 cup butter
1 tsp vanilla
1-1/3 cups coconut
1 cup pecans, chopped

Directions:
Mix milk, sugar, egg yolks and butter in saucepan.  Stir and cook until thickened.
Remove and add vanilla, coconut and pecans.  Cool and spread on cake.
