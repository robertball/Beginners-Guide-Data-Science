Title:
Berry Overnight Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4473977.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Quaker® Oats (Quick or Old Fashioned, uncooked)
1/2 cup non-fat milk
1/2 cup non-fat plain Greek yogurt
1 teaspoon chia seeds (optional)
1 cup fresh mixed berries and fruit

Directions:
Add Quaker(R) Oats to your container of choice and pour in milk.
Layer Greek yogurt, chia seeds and mixed fruit and berries.
Refrigerate overnight and enjoy in the morning!

Submitter: 
Quaker®
Description: 
Prep these fruity, creamy oats the night before and in the morning you've got a quick, fresh breakfast.
