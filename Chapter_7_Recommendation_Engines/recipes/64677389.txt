Title:
Rosted Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups russet or red potatoes, skin on, diced to bite sized pieces
4 slices of bacon, crip cooked, drained and crumbled
2 T. minced sweet onion
2 hard cooked eggs, chopped
3/4 cup Miracle Whip salad dressing
Salt and  pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 F.
Line a baking sheet with foil, spray with olive oil. Spread out diced potatoes.
Bake about 30 minutes till potatoes are golden and tender, stirring once halfway thru.
Mix all other ingredients in large bowl.
Gently stir in potatoes.
Serve warm or chill, to your preference.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
A different twist on traditional potato salad. The roasted potatoes and bacon create a really nice savor flavor, balanced by the sweetness of Miracle Whip.
