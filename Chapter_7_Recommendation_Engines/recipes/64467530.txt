Title:
Kicked up Rice Krispie Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2463877.jpg
Ingredients:
3 TBS  butter, melted and browned
10 oz  Marshmallows
6 cups Rice Krispies
1 tin of sweetened condensed milk
2 cups  semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 cup butterscotch chips
chocolate sprinkles (optional)

Directions:
Line a  11x15 in jellyroll pan with parchment paper. if any of the edges of the pan are exposed, make sure you spray with cooking spray or coat with butter.
in a large pot melt butter until it starts to brown add marslmallows at once  and stir until until completely melted. Remove from heat. Add Rice Krispies and stir until coated.
Turn mixture onto your prepared pan and lightly press to spread and fill the pan. you may need to lightly butter your hands for this step. Set aside.
Make the fudge by emptying the condensed milk and chocolate chips into a heat proof bowl over a pot of simmering water. stir until chocolate chips are melted. Pour this mixture and spread over your pan of rice krispies.  Add the butterscotch chips to the same heat proof bowl and melt over the pot of simmering water. once melted, put dollops on top of the fudge layer and with a knife or silicone spatula spread evenly over fudge layer. With a fork, drag it on top of butterscotch layer to make textured lines from one end to the other all the way across until the whole pan is textured. Sprinkle with sprinkles. Cut into 2in.x1in. squares.
