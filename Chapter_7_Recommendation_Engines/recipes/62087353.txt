Title:
Apple Tortillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans apple pie filling
12 flour tortillas
2 teaspoon cinnamon
2/3 cup butter
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Divide fruit evenly on the 12 tortillas.
Sprinkle each with cinnamon.
Roll up and place seam-side down in a lightly greased baking pan.
Combine butter, the two sugars and water to a boil;
reduce heat and simmer, stirring constantly, 3 minutes.
Pour sauce over the tortillas and sprinkle with more cinnamon, if desired.
Bake 20 minutes.
