Title:
All-Star AvoDog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980371.jpg
Ingredients:
8 hot dogs
8 hot dog buns
2 medium ripe tomatoes, seeded, chopped in 1/4 inch pieces
2 large ripe Chilean Hass avocados, halved, pitted, peeled, chopped and mashed
2 limes, juiced
Salt, to taste
grilled or raw onions (optional)

Directions:
Grill hot dogs until lightly browned.
Open hot dog buns and grill flat.
Mix avocados, diced tomatoes and lime juice in a small bowl.
Place hot dogs on each dressed bun and top with avocado mixture.
Add grilled or raw onions if desired.  Sprinkle with a little salt.  Serve

Submitter: 
Fresh Chilean Hass Avocados
Description: 
These delicious grilled hot dogs are garnished with creamy avocado, ripe tomatoes and grilled onions.
