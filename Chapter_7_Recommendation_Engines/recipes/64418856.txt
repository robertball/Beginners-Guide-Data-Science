Title:
taco pasta salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4cups elbow pasta cooked and cooled
2tbsp taco seasoning
1tbsp lime juice
half bunch cilantro sm diced
1tbsp salt kosher
1c mayo
jalapenos 2 small diced
white onion small, diced small
1 tbsp feta cheese
1c chicken diced

Directions:
add to a big bowl mix it up
place in frig for 2hrs
place in a clean bowl
add cilantro leaves
