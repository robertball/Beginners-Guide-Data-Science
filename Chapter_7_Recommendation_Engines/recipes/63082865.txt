Title:
Ramen Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups vegetable broth
3 1/2 cups water
2 (3.5 ounce) package ramen noodles with dried vegetables
2 teaspoons soy sauce
1 teaspoon chili oil
1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
3 green onions, sliced
1 package frozen vegetables
Dash of seasame oil

Directions:
In a medium saucepan combine broth, water, noodles and vegatables.  Cover and bring to a boil over high heat; stir to break up noodles.  Reduce heat to medium and add soy sauce, chili oil and ginger.  Simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes.  Stir and garnish with green onions and seasame oil.

Julie Hansen Ostler
