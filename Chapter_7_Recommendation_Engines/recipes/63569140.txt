Title:
Tami's Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons honey
3 tablespoons oil
1 egg
1 1/2 cups milk
2 tsp vanilla
Oil for frying

Directions:
Melt oil and honey in a small microwavable container for 20 seconds.
Sift flour and baking powder into a large bowl,stir in salt. Make a well in the centre of dry mixture and add in melted oil and honey, egg, vanilla and milk; whisk until combined.
Let batter rest for 5 minutes. Preheat a large skillet over medium heat.
Add 1 tsp of oil to the pan. Pour batter into the hot skillet, about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake. Add more oil as required. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes, until bubbles appear on the sides and center of each pancake and its golden brown. Flip and cook other side until golden, about 1 to 2 minutes.
