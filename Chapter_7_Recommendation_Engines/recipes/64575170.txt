Title:
3 Minute Marinara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 to 1/2 onion (peeled and diced)
6 cloves garlic (peeled and chopped)
1 tablespoon thyme leaves (Fresh)
1 1/2 cups whole, peeled, San Marzano tomatoes
1 small bunch basil leaves (torn)

Directions:
Heat a 12-14 inch saute pan with olive oil over medium-high heat.
Add garlic saute until caramelized.
Add onion and thyme.
Meanwhile place the tomatoes in the carafe of a blender and puree until smooth.
Remove to the saute pan, season with Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper and allow to simmer for 1-3 minutes.
Add basil and stir to combine.
