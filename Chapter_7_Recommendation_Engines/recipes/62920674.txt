Title:
Spinach and bacon and cheese Stuffed Chicken Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of spinach leaves
4 thin chicken breast
8 slices of bacon
4 cloves of garlic minced
1/2 cup minced white onion
salt and pepper
1/2 cup of sredded cheese of choice
4 slices of cream cheese
SAUCE
1/2 cup heavy cream
1 cup chicken broth
2 tbs flour
olive oil
garlic minced
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook your bacon to real crispy and then chop very small.  Lay your chicken breat out on a board and place spinach leaves on first, then sprink a small amount of minced garlic, sprinkle chopped bacon on next, place crean cheese slice and your choice of cheese slice.  Fold chicken over and poke with tooth pick or roll and poke with tooth pick.
Place olive oil in skillet and brown chicken on all sides then place in baking dish and back on 350 until chicken is done.
Meanwhile - Use your skillet of olive oil and cook your onion and garlic minced in pan then add flour and brown alittle.  Once browned add chicken stock and slowly add cream let simmer and thicken.
When chicken is done serve over noodles or rice and pour sauce over both.
