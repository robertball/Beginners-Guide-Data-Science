Title:
Baked Chicken Tenders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts (or tenders)
1 to 1-1/2 cups Panko Bread Crumbs
1 Egg beaten or buttermilk (light)
(if using buttermilk marinate in fridge for one hour)
I can't believe its not butter spray or similair
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp kosher salt
1 tsp pepper
2 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees
Use foil or wax paper on a cookie sheet and spray with Pam
Cut chicken into 1/2 inch strips lengthwise
In large ziploc freezer bag add bread crumbs and spices mix well
Dip chicken strips in egg or remove from milk
Add two at a time into bread crumb mixture and shake to cover
Put each piece on cookie sheet
Repeat this process until all the chicken has been used
Spray each peice with Butter spray 2 times
Bake about 7 minutes on each side
For extra crunch turn on broiler on low for two additional minutes

Submitter: 
Tarryn
Description: 
A healthy twist on the classic favorite chicken tenders....I personally would prefer these over fried any day!
