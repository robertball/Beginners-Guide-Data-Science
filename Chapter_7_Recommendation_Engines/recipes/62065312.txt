Title:
Dilly Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1075128.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Cream Cheese - room temperature
1 1/2 C. Grated Sharp Cheddar Cheese
1 C. coarsely chopped Dill Pickles
1/4 C. Chopped Green Onions
2 Tbsp. Mayonnaise
1 Tsp. Worchestershire Sauce
Parsley (optional)

Directions:
Combine first 6 ingredients.  Beat until smooth. Form into a ball and adorn with fresh parsley sprigs. Cover and chill until firm.  Serve with assorted crackers.

Submitter: 
PTRICK
Description: 
This appetizer has been a Christmas tradition in my family for years.  It can also be a hit any time of the year. The crunch of the dill and cheese combination will not disappoint. It keeps well for days in the fridge and can be made ahead.
