Title:
Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
3/4 onion
1/4 cup carrot (finely chopped)
1/4 cup celery (finely chopped)
1/4 green pepper (finely chopped)
Chili powder
1 can stewed or diced tomatoes
32 oz can pinto beans (could add more if you want)
8 oz can tomato sauce
1 Tblsp white sugar
1 Tblsp cocoa powder (or slightly less)

Directions:
Fry up greese with vegetables.
Add chili powder and cocoa powder to taste.
Add can stewed/diced tomatoes.
Add pinto beans and tomato sauce.
Add sugar and salt (if needed).
Simmer 30 minutes.
