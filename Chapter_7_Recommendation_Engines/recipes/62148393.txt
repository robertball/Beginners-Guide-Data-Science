Title:
Injera (Ethiopian Flat Bread) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups Warm water
2 1/2 Cups Self rising flour
3 Tablespoons club soda

Directions:
Place all ingredients in bowl, using hand blender combine ingredients throughly
Using electric skillet
Spray with "Pam"
scoop 1/3 cup of batter onto preheated skillet or electric skillet
Cook until no longer mushy but has consistency of a crepe

Submitter: 
Shanda
Description: 
Very quick recipe!  Easy to make.  Not exactly the same as traditional Injera but very good and no wait!
