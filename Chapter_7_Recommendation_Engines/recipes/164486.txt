Title:
Kamikaze Shots for a Crowd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/713264.jpg
Ingredients:
6 fluid ounces vodka
3 fluid ounces lime juice
3 fluid ounces triple sec

Directions:
Pour the vodka, lime juice, and triple sec into a cocktail shaker over ice. Cover, and shake until the outside of the shaker has frosted. Strain into a chilled shot glasses to serve.
