Title:
Linda's Wicked Witch's Brew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 Liter Bottle Sprite
2-120z Cans of Plain Seltzer Water
1-750 of Malibu Rum (optional)
2-64oz Ctn. Dole Pineapple,Orange,Banana Blend
1-Small bag of Gummy Worms
2-1 Pound Bags of Frozen Melon Balled Fruit (varies in store)
1-Large Plastic Cauldron (any party store)
1-Glass Bowl that will fit inside Cauldron
Ice Cube Tray
Plastic Eyeballs (from any party store, wash first)
DRY ICE CHUNKS(optional for effect) BE CAREFUL NOT TO COME IN CONTACT ON INGEST THE DRY ICE, CAN BE BURNED FROM EXTREME COLD.

Directions:
Fill ice cube trays and add one gummie worm to each cube freeze until party time.
Prepare your bowls, place glass bowl inside cauldron set aside.
In large bowl or juice mixing container add Sprite, Seltzer, Dole Juice, Malibu Rum.
Fill up glass bowl inside cauldron with brew, add ice cubes with gummie worms, add bags of frozen fruit (as much as you want) some fruit maybe saved for left over punch mixture,add cleaned plastic eyeballs,add optional dry ice.
Make sure whoever serves brew does not get dry ice in drinks!
Garnish with leftover gummie worms!
Enjoy! Happy Halloween
