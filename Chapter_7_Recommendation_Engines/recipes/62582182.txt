Title:
Sparkling Apple Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 qts chilled apple cider
1 C lemon-lime soda or lemon juice
1/2 C sugar if using lemon juice
cinnamon schnapps to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingred in pitcher. Can add club soda or lemon soda just before serving over ice.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Made with cinnamon schnapps, apple cider and lemon juice or lemon-lime soda. Great for the holidays
