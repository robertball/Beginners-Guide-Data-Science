Title:
Really Cranberry Orange Yummy Gummy Pudding Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/404188.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups flour
3 cups white sugar
4 teaspoons baking powder
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
2 teaspoons salt
3 tablespoons orange zest (dried)
3/4 cup finely chopped pecans
1 1/2 cups butter softened
3/4 cup orange juice concentrate
1 1/2 cups water
3 teaspoons orange extract
2 bags fresh or frozen cranberries (340 grams each)

Directions:
Mix first 6 ingredients and set aside.
Put butter in a bowl and allow to soften.
Mix orange juice concentrate, water, and orange extract in a separate bowl.
Rinse cranberries and remove stems as needed.
Cut butter into dry mixture.  Add liquid mixture and stir just enough to thoroughly combine. Fold in cranberries.
Put in 10x14 greased pan (large lasagna pan) and even out.
Bake at 350 degrees F about 50 minutes until top is medium brown.  Reduce heat to 325 degrees F and cover with foil.  Bake 20-30 more minutes or until knife comes out clean.

Description:
