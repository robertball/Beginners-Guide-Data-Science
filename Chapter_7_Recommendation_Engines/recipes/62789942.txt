Title:
Vanilla Bean Birthday Cupcake Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/651386.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 sticks unsalted butter, softened
4 oz cream cheese, softened
3 cups powdered sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla bean paste (no substitutions)

Directions:
Mix the softened butter and cream cheese together on low speed for 2 minutes, or until blended and smooth.
Add the powdered sugar, 1 cup at a time, mixing well for 1-2 minutes or until smooth each time.
Add the vanilla bean paste, mix for 2 more minutes until frosting is smooth and creamy.
Refrigerate for at least 20 minutes.
Pipe onto your favorite cupcakes.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Baker
Description: 
Buttercream was too sweet and fluffy and my daughter doesn't care for pure cream cheese frosting, so I came up with this to frost her birthday cupcakes.  It was a BIG hit, and tastes just like birthday frosting should.  Plus, it has vanilla bean flecks throughout.
