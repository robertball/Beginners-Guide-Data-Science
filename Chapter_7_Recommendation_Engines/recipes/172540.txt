Title:
Chocolate Chip Heart Cookies (Cookie Mix) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985598.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 pound 1.5 ounce) pouch Betty Crocker® chocolate chip cookie mix
1/4 cup Gold Medal® all-purpose flour
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 egg
3-inch open heart-shaped cookie cutter

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees F. In medium bowl, stir cookie mix, flour, butter and egg until well blended. Roll into 1 1/4-inch balls.
Spray cookie cutter with cooking spray; place on ungreased cookie sheet. Press dough ball evenly into cutter. Remove cutter; reshape dough if necessary. Continue making heart-shaped cookies, placing 2 inches apart on cookie sheet. Spray cookie cutter as needed with cooking spray.
Bake 6 to 8 minutes or until light golden brown. Cool 2 minutes; remove from cookie sheet.

Submitter: 
Betty Crocker®
Description: 
No rolling pin is needed! A cookie cutter and balls of cookie dough are the secret to lovely cookies.
