Title:
Slow-Cooked Short Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
4 pounds boneless beef short ribs
1/4 cup butter
1 large onion, chopped
1 1/2 cups beef broth
3/4 cup red wine vinegar or raspberry vinegar
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup chili sauce or barbecue sauce
1/3 cup ketchup
1/3 cup Worcestershire sauce
5 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder

Directions:
Transfer to a 6-qt. slow cooker. In skillet, combine the remaining ingredients. Cook and stir until mixture comes to a boil; pour over ribs. Cover and cook on low for 9-10 hours or until meat is tender.

Submitter: 
MsChristy
Description: 
Smothered in a mouth-watering barbecue sauce, these meaty ribs are a popular entree wherever I serve them. The recipe is great for a busy cook - after everything is combined, the slow cooker does all the work.Modified from: Slow-Cooked Short Ribs
