Title:
Oven fried vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup instant potato flakes
1/3 cup parmesan cheese
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 cup butter or margarine melted and cooled
2 eggs
4 cups of raw vegetables cut in strips, zucchini, and mushrooms.

Directions:
Combine potato flakes, parmesan, garlic powder, and melted butter in a small bowl. In another bowl beat eggs. Dip vegetables one at a time into egg, then the potato mixture, coat well. Place on an ungreased baking sheet. Bake at 400 degrees for 20-25 minutes until lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Dave McIntyre
Description: 
This recipe is a lighter alternative to
pan fried zucchini. If you have big zucchini remove seeds.
