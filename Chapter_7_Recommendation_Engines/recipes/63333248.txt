Title:
Chopped Steak, Mushroom Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
1/2 c minced onion (red excellent)
2 T minced garlic
1 T salt
1 T pepper
2 T peanut oil
1/4 red wine (or brandy)
1 c beef boiullion
2 c mushrooms (canned are great)
1T fresh thyme leaves, minced
1/2 T fresh rosemary leaves, minced
generous pinch of nutmeg
3 T butter, room temp preferred

Directions:
1. Preheat oven 400 degrees.
2. Blend first 5 ingredients in a bowl.
3. Make patties
4. Heat large saucepan with oil, medium heat.
5. Cook patties 3 min. per side and then put in the oven for 3-4 min.
6. Keep pan warm, add wine/brandy and after 1 min add the remaining ingredients for 3-4 min.
7. Remove from heat and whisk in butter.

Submitter: 
Christine
Description: 
Chef Irvine recipe from The Trails in San Diego. Burgers with a thin sauce. Excellent for a quick dinner with ground beef. The herbs really come through if fresh.

Goes well with instant mashed potatoes, rice or quinoa.
