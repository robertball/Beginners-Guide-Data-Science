Title:
Tequila Mojito (Cuervo Mojito) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976519.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 ounce Jose Cuervo Especial® Tequila
8 leaves fresh mint
1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoon lime juice
1/3 ounce chilled club soda
Fresh mint leaves (optional)

Directions:
Fill a glass halfway with ice.
Crush mint leaves using back of a spoon; add crushed mint to the glass with sugar and lime juice.
Pour Jose Cuervo Especial(R) Tequila into the glass; top with chilled club soda.
Stir well and garnish with mint leaves.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
When creating the archetypical tequila drink, get authentic flavor with the gold standard in tequila, Jose Cuervo Especial® Tequila.
