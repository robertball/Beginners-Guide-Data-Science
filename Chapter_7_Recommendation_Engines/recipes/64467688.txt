Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2449065.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons butter
4 cups Miniature Marshmallows
6 cups Rice Krispies cereal
Blue food coloring
Shredded coconut
White cake frosting (can be store bought or homemade)

Directions:
In large saucepan melt butter over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted.
Add approximately 10 drops of blue food coloring and mix well.
Remove from heat.
Add Rice Krispies cereal. Stir until well coated.
Cover hands with butter and shape into Dinomite egg shapes with hands. Be careful as it will be hot.
Dip the bottom of each Dinomite Fluffy Bites into frosting to coat and roll in coconut until "fluffy".
Enjoy :)
