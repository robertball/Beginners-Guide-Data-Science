Title:
Perfect Plum Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/709338.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 in) refrigerated pie crust
Approx. 12 Italian Prune Plums
1 tbsp. lemon juice
1/2 cup white sugar
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Pit and slice plums
Lay out the bottom piece of pie dough in your pan
Combine the dry ingredients and the lemon in a large bowl, add plum slices and gently mix to coat
Pour plum mixture into the crust and spread evenly
Place top layer of crust on as desired (lattice, whole with slits for steam to escape, etc.)
Bake for about 50 minutes or until golden brown and bubbly. Check about 30 minutes in-- may need to cover with foil to prevent over-browning.
