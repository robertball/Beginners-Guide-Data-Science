Title:
Earth Worm Delights Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984876.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
6 cups Kellogg's速 Cocoa Krispies速
1 (2.75 ounce) package gummy worms
6 chocolate sandwich cookies, finely crushed

Directions:
In large saucepan melt butter over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat.
Add KELLOGG'S COCOA KRISPIES cereal. Stir until well coated.
Using buttered spatula or wax paper spread mixture into 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan coated with cooking spray, leaving uneven surface. (Do not press firmly.) Arrange gummy worms on top. Sprinkle with cookie crumbs, lightly pressing into cereal mixture. Cool. Cut into 2-inch squares. Best if served the same day.
