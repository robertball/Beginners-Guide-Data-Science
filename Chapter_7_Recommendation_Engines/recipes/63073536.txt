Title:
Coconut Pecan Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887275.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup evaporated milk
1 cup sugar
3 egg yolks
1/2 cup butter
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups flaked coconut
1 cup pecans, chopped

Directions:
In a saucepan over medium heat, combine milk, sugar, egg yolks, butter and vanilla.
Cook, stirring frequently, until thickened.
Remove from heat and stir in coconut and pecans.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
This frosting is great on many cakes, not the least of which is German Chocolate Cake.Modified from: Coconut Pecan Frosting II
