Title:
STRAWBERRY COFFEE CAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 OZ cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup butter, softened
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup milk
2 eggs, well beaten
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
18 oz jar strawberry preserves
1 Tbl lemon juice
1/4 cup light brown sugar
1/2 pecans, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 (325 for glass pan)
Combine cream cheese, butter & sugar. Beat until light & fluffy. Stir in milk, eggs & vanilla. Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda & salt. Add to cheese mixture. mix until smooth (batter will be stiff)
Spread half batter in greased & floured 9 x 13 pan. Blend preserves with lemon juice & spread evenly over batter. Dot remaining batter evenly over preserves. Mix nuts & brown sugar; sprinkle over cake.
Bake 40min. serve warm
