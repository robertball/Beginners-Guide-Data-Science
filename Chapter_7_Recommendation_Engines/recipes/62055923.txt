Title:
Hot Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz. jar chunky applesauce
1 can cherry pie filling
1 can  sliced peaches
1 can mandarin oranges
1 can pineapple tidbits
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 t. cinnamon

Directions:
drain all fruit.  Combine all ingredients in a crockpot and cook on low for 90 minutes.  May be baked in a 350 degree oven for 90 minutes when using a 9x13 pan.

Submitter: 
dutchkidzx2
Description: 
This is a great hot salad to serve on a cold night.  This tastes great with any dish and can be changed according to your tastes.
