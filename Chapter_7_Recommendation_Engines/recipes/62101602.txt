Title:
Spicy Cheese Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Phyllo tart shells
1/4 lb Harvarti Cheese, in small cubes
Jalapeno jelly

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350.
Place tart shells on cookie sheet
Place cheese cube in shell and top with a small amount of jelly on the top of the cheese.
Bake in oven about 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
ALISSASMOM
Description: 
A savory, quick starter that can be served either hot or at room temp
