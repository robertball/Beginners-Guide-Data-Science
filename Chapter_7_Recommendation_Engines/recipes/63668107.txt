Title:
St. Louis style 3 cheese spinach Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1018847.jpg
Ingredients:
Spinach mixture
1 - 12 oz box Oven Ready/No boil lasagna
1 - 12 oz bag frozen spinach
1 cup plain yogurt
2 eggs
2 tsp salt
2 tsp pepper
1 cup water
Burger Mix
1lb ground beef
1 tbsp pesto alla genovese
2 tbsp cottage cheese
2 - 26.5 oz cans Delmonte traditional pasta sauce
2 cups shredded Mozzerla
2 cups shredded provel cheese
1/2 cup shredded mild cheddar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees
In one pan prepare spinach mixture, in another pan prepare burger mix simultaneously.
a. Add frozen spinach, yogurt, salt & pepper until evenly mixed in about (5-7 minutes).Remove from heat and mix in eggs.
Add water, cover pan and return to medium heat for 15 minutes. Stir half way through.
b.In another prepare burger mix.
Add burger, pesto and cottage cheese. cook until beef is brown. About 5 minutes (do not over cook)
Heat pasta sauce on low-medium heat about 3 minutes.
Layer
In a 9x13 baking dish:
Spread 1/2 sauce
4 noodles horizontal, 1 vertical fit to pan
1 cup sauce
1 cup spinach mix
1/2 cup mozzerela, 1/2 cup provel
1/2 cup sauce
Noodles
1 cup sauce
1 cup burger mix
1/2 cup spinach mix
1/2 cup mozzerela, 1/2 cup provel, 1/2 cup mild cheddar
1 cup sauce
1 cup mozzerela, 1 cup provel
Cover & Bake for 45 minutes
Let cool about 10 minutes before cutting

Eva Maria
Description: 
This is a great tasting hearty Lasagna with flavor in every layer!
