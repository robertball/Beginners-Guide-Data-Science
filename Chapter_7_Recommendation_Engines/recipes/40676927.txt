Title:
Lemon Meringue Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
1 T. butter
3 T. cold water
1 t. lemon rind, grated
2 T. milk
3 egg whites
1 t. lemon juice
1 1/4 c. water
1/2 c. cornstarch
6 T. lemon juice
3 egg yolks
1 - 8" pie crust
6 T. sugar

Directions:
Combine 1 c. sugar, 1 1/4 c. water and butter; heat until sugar dissolves.  Add cornstarch blended with remaining cold water; cook slowly until clear, about 8 minutes.  Add 6 T. lemon juice and rind; cook 2 minutes.  Slowly add egg yolks, beaten with milk; bring to boiling. Cool.  Pour into baked shell.  Spread with meringue made of egg whites, 6 T. sugar and 1 t. lemon juice, whipped til stiff peaks form.  Bake 350 for 12-15 minutes, or until meringue starts to lightly brown on tips.
