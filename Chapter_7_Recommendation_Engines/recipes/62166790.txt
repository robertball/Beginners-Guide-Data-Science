Title:
Asian Style BBQ Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
I package (or 8) 1" thick pork chops
2 Tbs. Corn Starch
4 Tbs. Honey
4 Tbs. Soy Sauce
3 Tbs. Brown Sugar
1-2 Tbs. Red Pepper Flakes (or to desired taste)
1 Tsp. Lime Juice
4 cloves of crushed garlic or 1 tsp. garlic powder
1 Tsp. chopped ginger or 1/2 tsp. powdered ginger
Sesame oil if pan frying.

Directions:
Sprinkle pork chops with corn starch on both sides and place in a deep dish.
Mix honey, soy sauce, brown sugar, red pepper flakes, lime juice, garlic, and ginger.
Pour half of the sauce over the pork chops, turn them over and pour the other half.  Marinade in refrigerator for at least 5 hours turning every so often.
Take pork out of refrigerator 30 minutes prior to cooking.
If pan frying, pour about 1/4" of sesame oil in skillet and heat.
Place pork chops in hot skillet and cook, turning once, until they are golden on the outside and thoroughly cooked.
If Barbecuing Cook 10-12 minutes per side or until thoroughly cooked.

Submitter: 
Liv
Description: 
Quick and Easy Asian Style Pork Chops that can be barbecued or pan fried. Wonderful alongside rice or vegetables, and leftovers can be turned into delicious fried rice! Use fresh ingredients instead of dried or powdered if possible. Serve with Sweet Chile Sauce or Hot Mustard for dipping.
