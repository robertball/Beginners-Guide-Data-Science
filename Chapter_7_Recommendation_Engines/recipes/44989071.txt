Title:
Slow Cooker Quick-n- Easy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 (8 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 cup chicken broth
4 oz cream cheese (try with 8 oz when I have it)
1/2 teaspoon dried parsley  (try with basil when I have it)
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
2 cloves of garlic

Directions:
Place chicken in the slow cooker
In a medium bowl:  mix the Italian-style dressing, soup, broth, parsley, thyme, salt, and pepper. Pour over the chicken in the slow cooker.
Cover, and cook on Low for 7 hours
Stir in the cream cheese
Continue cooking on low for 1 hour

Submitter: 
Linda T.
Description: 
You can start with frozen chicken breasts without having to adjust the cooking time.Modified from: Easy and Delicious Chicken
