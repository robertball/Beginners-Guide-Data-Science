Title:
Chocolate Chip Kahlua Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients for cake
1 box devil's food cake mix
1 4 oz box instant chocolate pudding
4 eggs
1/2 cup kahlua
1/2 cup veg. oil
3/4 cup of water
1 12oz bag of semi-sweet chocolate MINUS 1/2 cup(reserve for glaze)
1 dollop sour cream
Ingredients for glaze
1-1/2 cups powdered sugar
1/2 stick of butter
reserved chocolate chips(1/2 cup)
1/2 cup of water (or more as needed)

Directions:
Combine all cake ingredients except for the chocolate chips
and mix for a FULL FIVE MINUTES(batter will be thick).
Stir in chocolate chips.
Spray or grease a 10 or 12 inch bundt pan and pour batter into pan.
Bake for 40 or 50 minutes.
While cake is baking make glaze and let cool.
Place all glaze ingredients in sauce pan.
Melt over low heat.
When cake is done invert onto a DEEP RIMMED platter so sauce won't run off.
Wait to glaze the cake until right before serving.
