Title:
Red Velvet cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/780455.jpg
Ingredients:
•1 box white cake mix (2-layer size)
•1 box (4-serving size) instant vanilla pudding mix
•4 large eggs
•2 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
•1 tablespoon white vinegar
•1 cup milk
•1/2 cup vegetable oil
•2 ounces red food coloring
FROSTING
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 cup powder sugar
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1  cups heavy whipping cream

Directions:
Combine the cake mix, pudding mix, oil, eggs, cocoa, oil,and food coloring.mix vinegar with the milk let sit a minute then add to cake mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
*Whipped cream frosting *
whip the cream first put in frig.mix the rest and fold into whip cream.
