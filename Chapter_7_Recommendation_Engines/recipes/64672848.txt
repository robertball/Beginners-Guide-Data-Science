Title:
Apple Cream Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4588448.jpg
Ingredients:
3 or 4 tender sweet apples ( Cameo, Fuji, or Gala)
3 eggs at room temp
1 c sugar
3/4 c whipping cream
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 c Flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
3/4 tsp salt
Powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325°. Butter and generously flour a 9” springform pan.
Peel, core and slice apples into 1/4” rings, and set aside.
Beat eggs and sugar on high speed until thickened, about 2 minutes. Reduce speed and add cream and vanilla.  Beat 30 seconds more. Add flour, baking powder, and salt and blend on low speed to blend.
Add apples to batter and stir gently to separate and coat pieces. Pour into prepared pan and arrange apples flat.
Bake until golden brown and toothpick inserted comes out clean, @ 60-75 minutes.
Let cake cool @ 20 minutes. Run knife around edge before removing pan.
Let cool about 10 minutes more. Dust with powdered sugar, top with whipped cream and serve warm.
