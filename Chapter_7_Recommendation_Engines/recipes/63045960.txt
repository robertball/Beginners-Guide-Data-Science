Title:
Chocolate Crossiant Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 frozen crossiant
1 small bar of chocolate

Directions:
Heat the frozen crossiant in a toaster oven for 1-2 minutes.
Place the chocolate on top of the crossiant and toast for 1-2 minutes. Make sure the chocolate has only melted and is not burned.
Take the crossiant out and enjoy!

Description:
