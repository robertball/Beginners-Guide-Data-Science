Title:
Healthy Berry and Spinach Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1937442.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups frozen berries
1 cup plain yogurt
1/2 cup orange juice
1/4 cup fresh spinach, or to taste
5 strawberries

Directions:
Blend berries, yogurt, orange juice, spinach, and strawberries together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
animalluver
Description: 
This is an easy way to get kids to eat their veggies. If you want a more fruity taste, exclude the yogurt.
