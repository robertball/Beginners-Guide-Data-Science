Title:
Classic Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large rip Avocados (halved seeded and mashed)
2 tbsp freshly squeezed lemon or lime juice
1/2 cup freshly minced red onion
1/2 cup diced tomatoes
2 tbsp minced fresh Cilantro
1 Jalapeno (seeded and minced, a couple shakes of hot sauce instead)
2 tsp minced garlic
1/4 tsp salt
pinch of black pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl
Cover and refrigerate until serving
Guacamole will keep for about 3 days in the fridge if covered well

Submitter: 
mkinshella
Description: 
Serve with baked, whole grain tortilla chips, or a burger topper or as an accompaniment to grilled fish
