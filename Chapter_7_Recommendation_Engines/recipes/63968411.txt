Title:
Cindi's Crock Pot Italian Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1110109.jpg
Ingredients:
5 lbs rump roast, pot roast, or chuck roast
3 cups water
3 beef bouillon cubes
1/2 stick margarine
1 envelope Good Seasons Italian dressing
3/4 tsp. oregano
Pepper (to taste)
Salt (to taste)
4 green peppers, sliced
6 crusty hero rolls
Fresh mozzarella (optional)
Frank's Red Hot sauce (optional)

Directions:
Put meat, water, bouillon cubes, margarine, italian dressing packet, oregano, and pepper into a crock pot.  Cook on high for 8 hours.
Pull meat apart and remove all fat.  Put meat back into juice and add green peppers.  Continue cooking for another 30-60 minutes, until green peppers become soft.
Add hot sauce to taste (or allow each person to add to their own sandwich).
Serve on warm rolls.  Top with melted mozzarella, if desired.

Submitter: 
HDN
Description: 
Juicy shredded beef and peppers make a great sandwich - especially with fresh mozzarella!
