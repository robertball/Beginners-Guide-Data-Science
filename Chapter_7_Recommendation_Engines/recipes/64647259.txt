Title:
Chicken Salad Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4530665.jpg
Ingredients:
2 whole chicken breast cooked, ground using food processor
2 large stalks celery, minced or ground using food processor
½ red onion, minced or ground using food processor
¼ cup mayonnaise, or to taste
¼ cup sour cream (Optional)
2 tablespoon sweetened dried cranberries
2 tablespoon walnuts, crushed (Optional)
½ teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Cut chicken into chucks to cook and ground in food processor. Boil cut chicken in water until thoroughly cooked.
In a bowl add celery, red onion, mayo, sour cream, cranberries, and walnuts.
Cool chicken and ground in food processor. Add chicken to bowl and stir. Salt and pepper to your taste.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
When I buy chicken in bulk I usually plan to use some to make a sandwich spread. Goes great on a sandwich with lettuce and tomato or on a bed of lettuce. Easy to make and stays well in refrigerator for a few days.
