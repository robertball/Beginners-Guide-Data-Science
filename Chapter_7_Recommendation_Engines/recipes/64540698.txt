Title:
Cajun Honey Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:
8 chicken thighs
Salt and pepper to taste
2/3 cup honey
3 TBSP melted honey
3 TBSP Cajun Seasoning
Green onions, for garnish

Directions:
Season the chicken with salt and pepper to taste.
In a small bowl, combine honey, butter and Cajun seasoning.
Coat chicken on all sides with mixture.
