Title:
Crispy Parmesan Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/927223.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 Cup Parmesan cheese
1/2 Cup Panko bread crumbs
1/4 Cup Seasoned bread crumbs
1/4 tsp each paprika, salt, pepper
3 Tbsp melted butter
1 tsp Dijon mustard
2 tsp white wine (opt)
1 clove garlic, crushed
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, pounded to even thickness.

Directions:
Heat oven to 400.
Line a baking sheet with foil and spray with cooking spray
Mix cheese, bread crumbs and seasonings in a shallow dish. Melt butter in another dish and then whisk in Dijon, garlic and wine.
Dip each chicken breast in the melted butter mixture and then dip into crumb mixture, evenly coating. Lay in a single layer on prepared baking sheet. if there are left over crumbs gently pat on top of chicken.
Bake 20 minutes or until chicken is done.
