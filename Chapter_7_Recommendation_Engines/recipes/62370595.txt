Title:
Lime Squeeze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/413389.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 teaspoon grated lime zest
2 limes, juiced
1 (2 liter) bottle carbonated water, chilled
Splenda to taste

Directions:
In a pitcher, combine lime zest, Splenda, lime juice and carbonated water. Pour into tall glasses over ice.

Submitter:

Description: 
This limey drink is enough to tantalize any palate. The combination of fresh lime and carbonated water delights the senses!Modified from: Lime Squeeze
