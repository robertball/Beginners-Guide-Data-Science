Title:
Devil's Food Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter {no substitutions}
2 cups sugar
1 ts vanilla
1 ts almond extract
2 eggs
2-1/2 cups cake flour
1 cup cocoa
2 ts baking soda
1/2 ts salt
2-1/4 cups buttermilk

Directions:
Measure flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt into a large measuring cup.
Cream butter and sugar, add flavorings and eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
Add flour mixture and buttermilk alternately to the sugar-butter mixture mixing until all ingredients are well incorporated. Finish by mixing on medium for a minute.
Pour into 3 greased and floured 9 inch cake pans and bake for 30-35 minutes at 350F or until it tests done with a toothpick
Let cool and frost with Buttermilk Chocolate Buttercream Frosting{also submitted by me}

Submitter: 
melis
Description: 
A deep dark chocolate cake that is unbelievably moist. {And unlike most chocolate cakes, this one is even better the next day}

Use Buttermilk Chocolate Buttercream (submitted by me as well)to frost
