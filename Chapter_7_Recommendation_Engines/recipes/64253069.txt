Title:
Joan's quick chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. canola oil
1/2 clove of garlic
1 lb. ground beef
1/2 c. onion, chopped
15 oz can of tomato sauce
1 t. salt
2 t chili powder
1/2 green pepper, chopped
1 15 oz can of dark red kidney beans, undrained

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat oil; cook and stir  beef and garlic until meat loses its red color. Add beans with their liquid, onion and green pepper. Pour in tomato sauce. Add salt and chili powder and stir well. Simmer over low heat , stirring occasionally, until chili is of desired thickness--about 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sudie B
Description: 
A family favorite.  Great on a busy, cold night. Serve with cheese and sour cream.
