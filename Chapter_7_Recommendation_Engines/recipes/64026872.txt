Title:
Potato Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1118862.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium potatoes
1 tespoon salt
1/3 cup olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 180°C.
Line a muffin tin with paper liners.
Using a mandolino slicer, slice the potatoes with the peel on.
Add one slice to each liner brushing it with oil afterwards.
Continue this until each liner has approx. 8 potato slices in it.
Top with oil and salt.
Bake at 180°C until crispy brown outside. Approx. 12 minutes.
