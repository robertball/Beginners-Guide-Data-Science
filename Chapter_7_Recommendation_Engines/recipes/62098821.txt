Title:
Winter Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package taco seasoning
1 package Ranch dressing mix
1 small can chopped green chilis
1 can diced tomatoes
2 cans Ranch Style Beans
2 cans Whole Kernal Corn
2 cans Rotel tomatoes and green chilis
1 chopped onion
2 lbs. ground beef

Directions:
Cook ground beef and onion until no longer pink.  season with salt and pepper if desired.
Add the green chilis, rotel, tomatoes, beans and corn, all UNdrained.
Add the taco seasoning and the Ranch dressing mix. Stir well.
Let simmer for 30 minutes or until warmed through, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
SweetPea
Description: 
This is such a wonderful and hearty soup that is served great with cornbread and salad on a chilly night.
