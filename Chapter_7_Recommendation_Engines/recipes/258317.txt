Title:
Very Berry Smoothie from Almond Breeze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4520605.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup strawberries, hulled and chopped
1/2 orange, peeled and chopped
3/4 cup Almond Breeze Almondmilk Unsweetened Vanilla

Directions:
Place all ingredients into blender and blend on high until smooth.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
Toss some berries into the blender to make the best – and easiest – berry almondmilk smoothie.
