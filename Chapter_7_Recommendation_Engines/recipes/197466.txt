Title:
Easy Watermelon Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/668637.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 small watermelon, cut into bite-sized chunks
1 (4 ounce) container crumbled feta cheese
1/2 cup salted sunflower seeds

Directions:
Combine the watermelon, feta cheese, and sunflower kernels in a large bowl; toss. Serve immediately.
