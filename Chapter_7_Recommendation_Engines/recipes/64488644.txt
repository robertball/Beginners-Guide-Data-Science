Title:
Frozen Meatball and Fries Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon butter
1 (16-ounce) package frozen precooked meatballs
1 (15-ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (16-ounce) jar Alfredo sauce
2 tablespoons milk
2 tablespoons ketchup
2  tablespoons mustard
1 1/2 cups shredded Colby cheese
3 cups frozen French fries
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
In medium saucepan, combine onion, bell pepper, garlic and butter; cook until onion is tender, stirring frequently.
Add Frozen meatballs, ketchup and mustard;  cook until meatballs are thawed.
Add undrained tomatoes and bring to a boil.
Add Alfredo sauce and bring to a simmer.
Add milk to Alfredo sauce jar, close jar, and shake; add this to skillet.  Simmer 2 minutes.
Remove from heat and add cheese.  Cook and stir until melted.
Pour mixture into a 2 1/2 quart to 3 quart casserole dish.
Top with frozen French fries and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
Bake, uncovered, for 45 - 55 minutes or until casserole is bubbly and French fries are golden and crisp.

Submitter: 
Kim Harder Olson
Description: 
A main dish recipe that uses Frozen precooked meatballs, jarred Alfredo sauce and Frozen French Fries.  A favourite dish for families!
