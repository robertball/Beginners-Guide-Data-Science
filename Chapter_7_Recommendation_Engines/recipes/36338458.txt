Title:
Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 C flour
1 tsp cream of tartar
1/2 tsp soda
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp salt
1 egg
1 1/2 C powdered sugar
1 C butter

Directions:
In large mixing bowl beat butter, powdered sugar until fluffy. Beat in egg and vanilla. Scrape sides of bowl often. Mix in cream of tartar, soda and salt, until mixed well. Add flour 1/2 C at a time, beat at low speed for 30-40 seconds until well mixed. Roll out cookie dough on a well floured surface and transfer to a cookie sheet. Bake at 350 deg. for 10-12 minutes or until lightly browned. Remove from baking sheets. Cool on baking rack.
