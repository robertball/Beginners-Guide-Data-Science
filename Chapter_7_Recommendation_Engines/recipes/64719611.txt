Title:
Cashew Nut Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 C Coarsely Chopped Cashew Nuts
2 C (1 can) Vegie-burger
2 T Butter
1 Large Chopped Onion
2 Eggs, Beaten
3/4 C Cracker Crumbs
1 C Evaporated Milk
1/4 C Milk
1 1/2 t Savorex or Marmite
1 t paprika
1 Envelope Golden George Washington Seasoning
1/2 t Accent
1 t Sage
2 T Chopped Parsley
1/2 t Celery Salt
1 T Nutritional Yeast
1 t Garlic Powder

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and mix well.
Pout into greased or sprayed loaf pan.
Bake 45-60 minutes at 350 degrees F.
Serve in any variety of way you would meatloaf.
