Title:
Julie's Sensational Smoky Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBSP olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 small can fire-roasted diced green chilies (with juice)
1 teaspoon smoked paprika
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
2 10 oz packages frozen corn, thawed
2 cans small white potatoes, rinsed, drained, and diced
4 cups vegetable broth
1 cup half and half
___________________
Sliced green onions, optional
Grated cheddar, optional
Sour cream, optional
Tortilla chips, optional

Directions:
In a stockpot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and cook, stirring occasionally, until onion is tender but not browned. Add chilies, garlic, paprika and red pepper flakes. Cook, stirring, for 2 minutes. Add corn, potatoes, broth, and half and half. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, simmer for 15 minutes. Using an immersion blender, puree soup to desired consistency, leaving some chunks of potato and corn. Serve with optional toppings as desired.

Description: 
This hearty chowder uses pantry staples to come together quickly. It is spicy, smoky, creamy, and out-of-this-world delicious! A perfect meal for a chilly evening. Try it tonight!
