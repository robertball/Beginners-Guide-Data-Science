Title:
Tuscan Chicken Rolls with Pork Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds chicken breasts
1/2 pound ground pork
1 onion, finely chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 egg, beaten
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground savory
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 tablespoons butter, melted
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup dry white wine
1/2 cup cold water
2 teaspoons cornstarch
1/2 teaspoon chicken boullion

Directions:
Place chicken between 2 pieces of plastic wrap and pound out thin. Cook pork, onion and garlic over medium heat until well browned. Drain. Stir in eggs, bread crumbs, 1/2 teaspoon salt, savory and pepper. Split up pork mixture between pieces of chicken. Poll up and secure with toothpicks. Place in greased baking dish. Brush with butter, pour over the top any remaining. Sprinkle with 1/2 teaspoon salt. Add wine and cook uncovered for 40 minutes. Remove chicken to platter. Pour juices into a pan and place over medium heat. Combine cold water and cornstach and mix well. Add to juices along with chicken boullion. Heat to a boiling, stirring constantly. Boil and stir 1 minute. Pour gravy over chicken and serve.
