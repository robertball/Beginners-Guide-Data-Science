Ingredients:
2 cups water
2 cups elbow macaroni
3/4 pound VELVEETA Pasteurized Prepared Cheese Product, cut up
1 (16 ounce) package frozen vegetable blend, thawed, drained
1 (6 ounce) can tuna, drained, flaked
2 tablespoons milk

Directions:
Bring water to boil in saucepan. Stir in macaroni. Reduce heat to medium-low; cover. Simmer 8 to 10 minutes or until macaroni is tender.
Add VELVEETA, vegetables, tuna and milk. Stir until VELVEETA is melted and mixture is thoroughly heated.
