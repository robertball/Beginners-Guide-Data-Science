Title:
Bacon-Wrapped Pork Fillets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pork tenderloins (1-1 1/2 lbs each)
12 strips of thin-sliced bacon
2 cups fresh parsely leaves, packed
4 T garlic, chopped
1 t. ground pepper
1 t. red pepper flakes
salt to taste
1/2 cup olive oil
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
2 T water
Skewers

Directions:
Trim silverskin and fat from pork then cut into 2" thick filets.
Wrap bacon strips around each filet overlapping ends by 1".  Secure with toothpick.
Thread 3-4 filets on eack skewer; insert a second skewer for stability.
For marinade: mince parsley, garlic and seasonings in a food processor.  Add oil, vinegar and water and process until blended.
Brush pork with marinade and let sit for 1 hour.
Grill pork skewers, covered, for 4 mintutes per side (including bacon sides), basting each before turning.

Submitter: 
Joanie
Description: 
If you love garlic, this recipe is for you!  It's simple to make and so flavorful! I recently made it for a family BBQ and everyone wanted the recipe.
