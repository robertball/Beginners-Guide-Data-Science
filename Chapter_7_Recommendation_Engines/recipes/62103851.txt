Title:
Moms Classic Egg Salad Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large eggs
1/2 cup mayo
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
8 slices white bread

Directions:
Put eggs in a saucepan. Cover with water and boil for 10 minutes.
Rinse in cold water, and peel. In a mixing bowl, mash eggs with a fork. Blend in mayo, salt and pepper. Spread on slices of bread and arrange sandwhiches.

Submitter: 
CUPYCAKESMURF
Description: 
This is a simple egg salad sandwhich, just like mom used to make! No fancy twists on this already great recipe! It's the ultimate quick comfort food.
