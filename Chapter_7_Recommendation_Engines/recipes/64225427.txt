Title:
chicken pot pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 C margarine
1/3 C chopped onions
1/3 C flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1.5 C Chix broth
2/3 C milk
3 C shredded cooked chic / turker
2 C frozen mixed vegys

Directions:
Heat oven to 425
In med sauce pan, melt margarine.
Add onion & cook for 2 min
stir in flour, salt & pepper until well blended
Stir in broth & milk, stir constantly until it thickens
Add chix & mixed vegys
Spoon mixture into prepared pie crust
top with 2nd pie crust
Bake at 425 for 30 - 40 min
let stand for 5 min before serving
