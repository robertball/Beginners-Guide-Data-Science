Title:
Six Can Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
1 (10 ounce) can chunk chicken
1 (15 ounce) can black beans (rinse well)
1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers, drained
Cumin and Chili Powder to taste
Add sauteed carrots, onion and celery also.
Serve with Avocado and a squeeze of lime.

Directions:
Open the cans of corn, chicken broth, chunk chicken, black beans, and diced tomatoes with green chilies. Pour everything into a large saucepan or stock pot. Simmer over medium heat until chicken is heated through.

Submitter: 
Jilly~Bean
Description: 
Delicious and EASY zesty soup recipe that uses only 6 canned ingredients! Serve over tortilla chips, and top with shredded Cheddar cheese. Throw away the cans and no one will know that it is not from scratch!Modified from: Six Can Chicken Tortilla Soup
