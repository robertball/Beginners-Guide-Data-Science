Title:
Creamy Herb Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tbsp. butter
2 sm. onion
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup dry white wine
1/2 cup heavy cream
8 oz. cream cheese
1 tsp tarragon
1 tsp chicken spice
4 uncooked chicken breast.

Directions:
Preheat to 350
Grease oven proof dish with butter
Place skillet over medium heat and add 2tbsp butter
Saute onion, garlic and tarragon in butter until soft
Remove and set aside
Add 2 more tbsp. butter to skillet over low heat add wine and cheese.
Stir until well mixed then add cream and spices
Pour chicken broth into dish and add chicken breasts
Spoon over onion mixture followed by cheesy cream
Bake for 50 min till cooked through and bubbly.
