Title:
Mom Lapierre's Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz pasta;
2 tbs butter;
4 tbs flour;
1 tsp salt;
1 tsp dry mustard;
2 1/2 cups milk;
2 cups shredded cheese;
1/4 cup buttered bread crumbs

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions; drain. Prehear oven to 375. In a medium saucepan, melt butter, add flour, salt and mustard and sitr until well blended on medium heat. Slowly add milk and heat, stirring constantly, until thickens. Add 1/2 cup cheese and cook until melted. Mix noodles and sauce and transfer to casserole dish. Top with bread crumbs and remaining cheese. Sprinkle parsley on top. Cook 20-25 minutes, until top is bubbly.

This is my mom's baked macaroni and cheese recipe. Turns out it's pretty standard, but I don't want to take any chances that something's different!
