Title:
Garlic Parmesan Orzo Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c uncooked orzo pasta
3 t minced garlic
1/2 c butter, cubed
1/2 c grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 c milk
2 T minced fresh parsley
1 t salt
1/4 t pepper

Directions:
1.  Cook orzo according to package directions; drain.
2.  In a large skillet, saute garlic in butter until tender.
3.  Add the Parmesan cheese, milk, parsley, salt, and pepper.
4.  Once blended, add the orzo pasta.  Cook and stir until heated throughout.

Submitter: 
Renee Heston
Description: 
This is a great side dish with chicken!
