Title:
Chocolate lava cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package chocolate cake mix
1 small pack of instant chocolate pudding mix
1 12 oz bag of chocolate chips

Directions:
Prepare pudding and set aside
Prepare chocolate cake and pour into greased slow cooker
Pour pudding mix into batter
Sprinkle chocolate chips
Cook on high for 3 and 1/2 to 4 hours
