Title:
Slow Cooker Navy Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/393631.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package dried navy beans
6 cups water
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (14.5 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 tablespoon cajun seasoning
1 onion, chopped
2 clove garlic, minced
1 pound chopped ham
2 cubes chicken bouillon
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon Tabasco sauce
1 tablespoon dried parsley
3 teaspoons garlic powder
1 bay leaf
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Combine beans, water, tomatoes,tomato sauce, onion, garlic, ham, bouillon, Worcestershire sauce, parsley, garlic,cajun seasoning and bay leaf in a slow cooker; set on low for 12 hours.
Season with salt and pepper. Discard bay leaf.

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
A great soup to simmer all afternoon, full of tomatoes, onions, ham, and spices.  This recipe makes eight to ten servings so make sure you have a large crock pot on hand.Modified from: Navy Bean Soup I
