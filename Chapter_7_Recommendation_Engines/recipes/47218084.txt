Title:
Brownie Swirled Peanut Butter Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C granulated sugar
1/4 C butter, softened
1 T water
3/4 C semisweet chocolate chips
1 large egg
1/2 t. vanilla
1 C flour
1/4 t baking soda
1 2/3 C (10 oz) milk chocolate & peanut butter chips, divided
3/4 C creamy peanut butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 12 muffin cups
Combine sugar, butter and water in med. microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on high for 1 min. or until butter is melted. Add semisweet chips;stir until melted. Add egg and vanilla and stir well. Add flour and baking soda and stir until blended. Allow to cool to room temperature. Stir in 1 cup swirled chips. Spoon a heaping tablespoon batter into each prepared muffin cup.
Bake for 13-15 min. or until top is set and wooden pick inserted center comes out still slightly wet. Place muffin pan on wire rack.Centers of brownies will fall wpon cooling. If some do not, with back of teaspoon, tap center to make a hole.
Place peanut butter in small, microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on high for 45 sec.; stir. While brownies are still hot, spoon a scant tablespoon peanut butter into center of each brownie. Top with remaining swirled chips. Cool completely in pan on wire rack. Run a knife around edge and carefully remove from pan.
