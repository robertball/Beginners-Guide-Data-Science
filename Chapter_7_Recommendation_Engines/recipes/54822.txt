Title:
Tropical Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup marshmallow creme
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 tablespoon orange juice concentrate

Directions:
In a bowl, stir together mayonnaise, marshmallow creme, ginger, and orange juice concentrate until well blended. Let rest for a few minutes, then stir again.

Submitter: 
CAROLYN JOHNSON5
Description: 
An easy fruit dip that is also good as a dressing for a fruit salad.
