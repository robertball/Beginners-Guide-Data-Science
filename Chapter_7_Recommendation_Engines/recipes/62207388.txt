Title:
Sugar-free Deamsicle Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large box sugar-free orange gelatin
1 cup boiling water
1 cup cold water
1 large box sugar-free vanilla pudding
2 small cans mandarin oranges, drained
1 large sugar-free whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
Combine gelatin and boiling water.
Stir to dissolve thoroughly.
Stir in cold water and let sit 5 minutes.
Add pudding mix and stir until blended.
Add well-drained orange segments.
Fold in whipped topping.
Spoon into goblets and chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
An easy, tasty dessert good for those w/ or w/o sugar issues.
