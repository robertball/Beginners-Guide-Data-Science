Title:
Grama Jama's Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cans regular cut green beans
1 3/4 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
2 TBS melted butter
1/2 cup crumbled Ritz crackers
4 oz sour cream
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 container french fried onions
1 tea salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix in a large bowl: 4 oz sour cream, 1 can cream of mushroom soup, 1 3/4 cups shredded sharp cheddar, 1/2 cup crushed Ritz crackers & salt. Add 4 cans regular cut green beans.
Pour over it all, 2 TBS melted butter and mix gently.
Mix together and put in a buttered casserole dish.
Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.
Add 1/2 container French fried onions. Bake for 10 more minutes.
