Title:
Savory Herb Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1084687.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c. medium or long grain rice
1 T dried onion
1 tsp. dried parsley
1/2 tsp. dried basil
1/2 tsp. dried thyme
2 cloves garlic, minced
cracked black pepper and salt
2 c. chicken broth
2 c. water
1 tsp. chicken base or 1 chicken bouillon cube

Directions:
Drizzle frying pan with olive oil. Add rice, onion, herbs and garlic.  Saute over medium high heat until fragrant and garlic is lightly toasted.
Add broth and water and chicken base.  Bring to boil, cover and reduce heat to low.  Simmer until rice is done, about 20 minutes.

Description: 
Yummy, fragrant rice, a perfect accompaniment to roast chicken.
