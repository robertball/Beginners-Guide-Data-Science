Title:
BBQ'd potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium russet potatoes
1tsp seasoned salt
1/2tsp Black pepper
1tsp Oregano
1/2tsp Chilli powder
2tbs olive oil

Directions:
cut potatoes in half length wise & slice into thich fries.
Mix all ingredients & place into a tin foil pan, covered with tin foil.
Puncture tin foil top with a skewer or knife to let steam escape.
Place in your bbq for 15-20 min
Enjoy
