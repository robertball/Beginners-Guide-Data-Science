Title:
Apple Pudding Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c      sugar
1/2c     shortening
1        egg
2c       chopped apples
1c       flour
1 tspn   baking soda
1/4 tspn nutmeg
1/2 tspn salt
LEMON SAUCE
1 1/2Tbspn  corn starch
3/4c        sugar
1/2 tspn salt
1/2          lemon sliced
2 Tbspn      Butter

Directions:
Heat the oven to 350 degrees
Cream the sugar and shortening
Add the egg and apples
Stir in the dry ingredients
Pour into a greased 8 inch pan
Bake for 35 minutes
TO MAKE THE LEMON SAUCE
Mix the dry ingredients with 1/2 c cold water.
Add 1 c Hot water and cook for 10 minutes
Add sliced lemon and butter
Serve hot
