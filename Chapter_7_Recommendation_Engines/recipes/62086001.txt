Title:
Nicfries Peanut Butter Butter Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 slice of bread
1 tbsp peanut butter
1 tbsp brown sugar (light or dark will work)
1 tsp butter or margarine

Directions:
Evenly spread butter over the slice of bread.
Follow with evenly spreading the peanut butter on top of the butter.
Evenly sprinkle the brown sugar on top of the peanut butter.
Place is toaster over and toast on medium for approx 2 minutes.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Nicole Evans
Description: 
This is an easy to make warm and sweet treat that makes a great dessert or midnight snack. Great for kids or those with a sweet tooth! My previous Pastor and his daughter used to make these all the time!
