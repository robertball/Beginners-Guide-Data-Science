Title:
Simply Succulent Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886899.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup chicken broth
1 pound tiger prawns, peeled and deveined
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1/2 cup butter
3 cloves garlic, crushed

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium heat, bring chicken broth to a simmer and add prawns; cook until pink.
Add red pepper flakes to taste.
In a microwave-safe bowl, combine butter and garlic; heat until completely melted.
Place prawns on a platter with separate cups of garlic butter for dipping; serve.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Tasty prawns are lightly simmered in chicken broth, spiced up with a sprinkling of red pepper flakes, and served with garlic-infused butter for dipping.Modified from: Simply Succulent Shrimp
