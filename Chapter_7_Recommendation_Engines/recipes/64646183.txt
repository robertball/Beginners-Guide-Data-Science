Title:
Sam's Super Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
28 ounce can of crushed tomatoes
4 ounce can of Green Chiles
1 bunch finely chopped green onions
1 large bell pepper chopped
2 tablespoons white vinegar
1 1/2 tablespoons of sugar (I omit)
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
Optional:
2 tablespoons of diced Cilantro
1/s tablespoon jalapeno pepper

Directions:
mix ingredients together in a bowl, store in refrigerator overnight before eating for best results but can be eaten right away.

Submitter: 
Mary Rose
Description: 
Great salsa with mixed of canned and fresh ingredients that I found on a note card in my husband's cabinet when we met. It is flavorful but not spicy.
