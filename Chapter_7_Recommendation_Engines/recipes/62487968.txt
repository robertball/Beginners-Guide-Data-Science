Title:
Bubba's Stuft Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/495498.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. ground turkey
1/2 lb. ground round
4 slices of bread, toasted and crushed
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 bell pepper, diced finely
1 small onion, diced finely
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 lb. turkey bacon, cooked and crumbled
3 c. mozzarella cheese
1 bunch torn fresh basil
6 oz. frozen spinach, thawed, rinsed, drained and dried
3 roma tomatoes, diced with liquid removed
garlic powder to taste

Directions:
1. Combine meats, bread crumbs, garlic, pepper onion and salt and pepper.
2. Spread in a 1/2 inch layer on waxed paper on counter.
3. Place remaining ingredients in layers on meat layer.
4. Using wax paper, roll meat and layer into a loaf and transfer to shallow baking dish.
Bake 1 hour on 375 degrees.

Submitter: 
Teressa DeJong Drenth
Description: 
On a recent vacation to California to visit my sister and her kids she and I developed this recipe with what she had in the kitchen.  It was absolutely delicious!
