Title:
Bev's Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp oil
3 oz bacon, diced
1 cup carrot, diced
3/4 cup onion, diced
3/4 cup celery, diced
1 cup potatoes
3/4 cup flour
11 cups water
1 cup lentils
2 tsp salt
1 tsp thyme
2 Tbsp beef base (bouillon cubes)
Pinch of nutmeg
Pinch of black pepper

Directions:
In large soup pot, heat oil and saute bacon, carrots, onion and celery until vegetables are soft.
Add flour, stirring constantly.
Slowly add water.  Then add remaining ingredients and simmer for 3 hours.

Barbara Bartz

A flavorful thick soup.
