Title:
Egg Free Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
2 cups white sugar
1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup Milk
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup Sour Cream
1/4 cup Apple Sauce
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, stir together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder and salt.
Pour in milk, vegetable oil, Sour Cream, Apple Sauce and vanilla; mix until well blended. Spread half of the batter into a 9x13 inch baking pan. Sprinkle the Chocolate Chips on top.  Spread the other half of the batter.
Bake for 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven or until they are done to your liking. Let cool for about 10 minutes before slicing.

Description: 
A fudgy brownies great for people with an egg allergy, or those who simply want to make brownies and are out of eggs.
