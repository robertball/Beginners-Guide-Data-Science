Title:
Chicken-chile Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg burrito-size flour tortillas
2 cups shredded Mexican-cheese blend
2 cups shredded chicken
1 can green chiles
1/4 cup sliced green onion
2 Tbsp butter, melted
Salsa and sour cream for dipping

Directions:
Top one half of each tortilla with cheese, chicken, chiles and onions. Fold over and press down with spatula. Heat skillet to medium. Brush tortillas with melted butter. Cook 4 minutes or until golden brown, turning once. Cut into wedges, Serve with salsa and sour cream.
