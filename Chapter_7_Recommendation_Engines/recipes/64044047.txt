Title:
Oatmeal Chia Seed Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1124048.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups rolled oats
2/3 cup whole wheat flour
1 cup brown sugar
2 TBS chia seeds
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking powder
2/3 cups apple sauce
3 TBS coconut oil
1 cup dried cranberries
1/4 cup shredded unsweetened coconut (optional)
1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line cookie sheet with parchment paper.
Combine dry ingredients.
Add apple sauce and coconut oil; mix well.
Fold in cranberries, shredded coconut and chocolate chips.
Spoon onto lined cookie sheet, bake 10-15 minutes.
Cool on rack.

Submitter: 
Island Girl
Description: 
An easy way to incorporate chia seeds, these delicious cookies contain no eggs or butter and are easy to make.
