Title:
Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871607.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 pkg	taco seasoning
2/3 cup	water
lettuce, shredded
tomato, chopped
shredded Cheddar cheese
Salsa
Taco Shells
Refried Beans

Directions:
Cook beef in 10-inch skillet over medium heat 8 to 10 minutes,
stirring occasionally, until brown; drain.
Add taco seasoning and water. Cook 10 minutes.
Heat refried beans.
