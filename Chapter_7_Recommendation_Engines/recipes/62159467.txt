Title:
Summer Strawberry Buckle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c butter, softened
3/4 c sugar
1 egg, beaten
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/2 c whole milk
2 c cake flour, sifted
2 tsps. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
2 c fresh strawberries, sliced
Topping
1/4 c butter, softened
1/2 c brown sugar
1/3 c flour, sifted
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. nutmeg

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl cream sugar and butter till light & fluffy.
Blend in eggs and vanilla, then milk.
In a seperate bowl, sift together dry ingredients, and stir in to liquid mixture.
Fold in berries, and spread in a greased and lightly floured 9x9 inch pan.
Make the crumb topping:
Cream butter and sugar.
Blend in flour, cinnamon and nutmeg.
Sprinkle over batter.
Bake in 375 degrees oven 30-35 minutes.
Serve warm with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream.

Submitter: 
Cat Lady Cyndi
Description: 
This is a delicious strawberry dessert that is served warm with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream. The topping is a sweet concoction with brown sugar, cinnamon & nutmeg. Enjoy!
