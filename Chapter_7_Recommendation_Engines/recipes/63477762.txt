Title:
The Quintessential Basil Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
75g/2½oz fresh Basil Leaves
120ml/8tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2 cloves Garlic - finely chopped
2 Shallots - finely chopped
150ml/5fl oz Rice Wine Vinegar
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Put all the ingredients, apart salt and pepper, in a bowl and mix well;
With a blender, “Blitz” everything to a smooth dressing;
Season to taste and serve

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
A dressing to cover any eventuality. Full flavoured, the combination of the basil with the wine vinegar is a delight to the tastebuds
