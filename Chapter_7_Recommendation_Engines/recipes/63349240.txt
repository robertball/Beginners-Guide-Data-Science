Title:
Corn and Black Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/955750.jpg
Ingredients:
1 15oz can black beans, drained
1 11oz can Mexicorn (corn, red peppers, green peppers), drained
1/2 tsp olive oil
1/16-1/8 cup white wine vinegar
1/4 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp cumin
Juice of 1/2 lime (optional)
1 tbsp cilantro, finely chopped (optional)
1/4 tsp Red chili pepper flakes (optional)
Salt & Pepper to taste (optional)

Directions:
Pour beans and corn in colander.  Rinse well and sit for a few minutes to drain.  Pat dry with a paper towel if desired.
In a medium bowl, mix beans, corn, olive oil, vinegar, chili powder, and cumin.  Add additional ingredients if desired.
Serve chilled.

Submitter:

Description: 
A quick, healthy side dish that can be made with ingredients in the cupboard.  One of my husband's favorite side dishes!
