Title:
Calypso Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the pork chops:
4 boneless pork chops
2 tbsp. brown sugar
2 tbsp. curry powder
1 tbsp. cumin
1/4 tsp. salt
For the Salsa:
8 oz. canned crushed pineapple drained (reserve juice)
1/4 c. diced red onion
1 tbsp. minced jalapeno (or to taste)
1 small bunch cilantro - chopped
For the rice:
2 c. instant brown rice
1 small bunch cilantro - chopped
2 tbsp. butter
1/8 tsp. salt

Directions:
Put pork chops in a square bake dish. Combine sugar, curry, cumin and salt and pour over pork chops. Rub into the meat until it is well covered. Let stand at room temperature 30 minutes. Bake covered in 350 degree oven for 30 minutes or grill.
Combine all salsa ingredients and chill for 1 hour.
Cook instant rice on stove top according to instructions. Replace part of the water with the reserved juice. Cook until done. Stir in butter, cilantro and salt.
To serve: Slice pork chops into thin slices on the bias. Fan out over the cilantro rice and top with the salsa.

Description: 
Spiced pork chops with a refreshing fruit salsa and cilantro rice. Quick and easy to make, but special enough to serve to company.
