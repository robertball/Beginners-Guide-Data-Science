Title:
Big Ray's Smoky Zucchini Baba Ghanoush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3738853.jpg
Ingredients:
6 medium zucchini
2 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup tahini
3 Tbsp lemon juice, divided
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1 Tbsp toasted sesame seeds
1/2 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 500 degrees.
Halve zucchini length wise and drizzle with 2 Tbsp olive oil.  Bake on rimmed sheet until charred and soft, about 45 minutes.
Remove from oven and cool. Scoop out the flesh and combine with remaining ingredients.
Serve with pita chips or veggies strips.
