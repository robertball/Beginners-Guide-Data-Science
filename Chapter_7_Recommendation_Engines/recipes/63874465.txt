Title:
Cauliflower curry soup with roasted  crunchy garbanzo beans, cauliflower, kale and brown rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1075222.jpg
Ingredients:
RICE
1 T coconut oil
1 c. brown rice (or mix of brown rice and quinoa)
2 c. water
(make more rice if desired)
SOUP
1-2 T coconut oil
5-8 garlic cloves, chopped
1 onion, chopped
1 apple, peeled, chopped
1 T curry powder
1/2 T cumin
1 tsp tumeric
1 head cauliflower (used 3/4 for soup and 1/4 for roasting)
4 c chicken stock
salt and pepper to taste
ROASTED GARBANZO BEANS AND CAULIFLOWER
1 15 oz can garbanzo beans
1/4 to 1/2 head cauliflower
1 T coconut oil (melted)
sprinkle of cumin
salt
1/2 bunch head kale, stems removed, sliced thinly
1 T coconut oil
salt to taste

Directions:
RICE
melt coconut oil and brown rice medium heat
add water, bring to boil
reduce heat and simmer 30 min or until water absorbed and rice done
SOUP
add coconut oil to large pot
saute garlic, onion until soft
add apple and let soften
add spices, until fragrant
add cauliflower
add stock
bring to boil, simmer until cauliflower and all veggies very soft
process in food processor
return to pot
salt/pepper to taste and adjust seasoning
BEANS AND CAULIFLOWER
preheat oven to 400
rinse and drain beans
dry beans with paper towel
put beans and cauliflower on cookie sheet
toss with coconut oil
sprinkle with little cumin (or curry powder or combination to taste)
add salt
roast approx 30 minutes, stirring couple times
KALE
remove stems, slice thinly
dry with paper towels
toss with coconut oil
salt to taste
roast at 400 for 10-15 minutes (i started on lower rack with beans/cauliflower, then moved to top to finish)

Excellent cauliflower curry soup with roasted crunchy beans, cauliflower and crunchy kale.
