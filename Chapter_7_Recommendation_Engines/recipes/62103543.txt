Title:
Mocha Pudding Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 5 oz package of Jell-O cook and serve chocolate pudding
3 cups skim milk
3 TBS Hills Bros. Cappuccino double mocha mix.
6 TBS Cool Whip Lite

Directions:
Stir pudding mix , cappuccino and milk in a saucepan over medium heat, using a wire wisk until it boils, remove from heat. Separate into 6 ramekins or bowls, chill - 2-3 hours, top each with tbs of cool whip.
