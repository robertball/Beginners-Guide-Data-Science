Title:
Chicken and Rice Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 chicken thighs (bone in)
1/4 cup flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 cup butter or margerine
2 cups boiling water
3 chicken bouillon cubes
1 teaspoon instant minced onion
1 cup regular rice (uncooked)
1 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
Wash chicken and pat dry.
In a ziploc bag, mix together flour, salt and pepper.
Melt butter or margarine in a 13x9 inch dish.
Shake chicken pieces one at a time in the bag with flour mixture and place skin side down in pan.
Bake 20 minutes at 400 degrees.
Remove chicken and pour boiling water into pan with remaining ingredients except paprika.  Mix well and arrange chicken skin side up on top.  Place back in oven and cook for 40 more minutes.  When done, sprinkle with paprika.

Submitter: 
Nancy Toth
Description: 
This chicken recipe is so easy and so delicious! If you love chicken and rice with a buttery taste, you'll love this recipe!
