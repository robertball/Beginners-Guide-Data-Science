Title:
SHREDDED HASH BROWN CASSEROLE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 BAG SHREDDED HASH BROWN POTATOES
1 CAN OF CREAM OF MUSHROOM
1 CUP SOUR CREAM
1/2 CUP MELTED BUTTER
2 CUPS SHREDDED CHEDDAR CHEESE
1 CAN FRENCH FRIED ONIONS
PARMESEAN CHEESE
1/2 CUP BUTTER MELTED

Directions:
MIX THE 1ST 5 INGREDIENTS AND THEN PUT ON TOP THE FRENCH FRIED ONIONS, SPRINKLE PARMESEAN CHEESE AND POUR THE MELTED BUTTER OVER THE TOP
BAKE AT 350 FOR 50 MINUTES
