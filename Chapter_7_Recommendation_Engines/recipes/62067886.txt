Title:
Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon butter
1 Celery stalk, diced
1/2 Onion, diced
1/2 Teaspoon dried basil
1/2 Teaspoon celery seed
1 Teaspoon fresh parsley, chopped
1 carrot, diced
1 garlic clove, crushed or minced
1 cup lentils
6 cups hot chicken or vegetable stock

Directions:
Heat butter in pot over medium. Add celery and onion; cook 2 minutes. Add carrots, spices and garlic; cook 2 minutes. Add lentils and stock; bring to boil. Cook partially covered for 1 1/2 hours over low heat. Serve hot.

Submitter: 
PIPPYMOE
Description: 
The celery seed really complements the lentils nicely. A thick and hearty soup.
Meat lovers will not even miss the meat!
