Title:
Sweet and Spicy Shrimp Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb uncooked shrimp
1/4 & 1/3 cup sweet chili sauce (thai)
Juice from 1/2 a lemon
4 cloves minced garlic
1 tbs Tequila (optional)
1/4 tsp sea salt (or to taste)
1/4 tsp freshly ground pepper
red pepper flakes for added heat(optional)
6 bamboo (soaked in hot water for 30 minutes) or metal skewers

Directions:
In a medium sized sealable container, combine 1/3 cup chili sauce, juice from half a lemon, garlic, tequila, salt, pepper and red pepper flakes.  Mix, add shrimp, cover container and shake. Let marinate for 30 - 60 minutes in fridge. Fire up barbeque to a medium high heat. Slide shrimp onto skewers.  Place on hot grill.  Using remaining 1/4 cup of sweet chili sauce,  flip and baste shrimp every minute until shrimp is cooked through. - Do not overcook!
