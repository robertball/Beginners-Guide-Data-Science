Title:
Aunt Tammy's Philly  Cheesesteak Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup onions, chopped
1/2 cup green pepper, chopped
1/2 cup Italian dressing
1/4 lb. Provolone cheese, cut into pieces
1 block of cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup of mayo
1/2 lb. sliced roast beef, cut into pieces
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute onions and green peppers in Italian dressing until soft.
Add Provolone, cream cheese, salt, pepper and mayo until melted and combined.
Then stir in roast beef.
Pour into an oven proof dish and top with parmesan.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 25 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
Serve with toasted cut up hoagie buns or your favorite crackers.

Submitter: 
xraygirl411
Description: 
Everyone in my family loves cheesesteaks.  I decided to turn that into a dip for football season.  As soon as I served it I knew it was a hit.  It's best served with toasted hoagie buns but even crackers work well too.
