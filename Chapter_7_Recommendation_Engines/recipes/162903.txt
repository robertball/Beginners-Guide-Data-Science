Title:
Irish Spritzer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/799646.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces club soda
ice
1 fluid ounce amaretto liqueur
1 fluid ounce Irish cream liqueur

Directions:
Pour the club soda into a short glass over the ice cubes. Add the amaretto and Irish cream liqueurs and stir.

Submitter: 
MomZilla (Evin)
Description: 
They served this before I saw Lord of the Dance in Las Vegas. It's still my favorite drink!
