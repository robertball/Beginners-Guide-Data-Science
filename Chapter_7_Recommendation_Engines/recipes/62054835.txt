Aunt Dee's Strawberry* Jell-OÂ® Pretzel Salad Recipe

Ingredients:
2 cups thin pretzels, crushed
3 Tbsp.sugar
3/4 Cup butter or oleo, melted
1 large package strawberry Jell-O
2 cups boiling water
2 10 oz. pkg. frozen strawberries, chopped in pieces
1 small can, crushed pineapple (tuna size), drained
1 8 oz. cream cheese
1 cup powder sugar
1 8 oz Cool Whip
Mini marshmallows to decorate

Directions:
STEP 1  Mix 2 cups crushed pretzels, sugar, melted butter or oleo. Spread in a 13x9 glass pan. Bake to make base crust for 10 minutes @ 400 degrees - Let cool 1 hour
STEP 2  Dissolve package of strawberry Jell-O with boiling water. Add frozen strawberries, drained crushed pineable. Chill until almost jelled/set - approximately 1 hour in fridge.
STEP 3 - Blend together cream cheese, Cool Whip , and powdered sugar.
ASSEMBLE:  Once crust is completely cooled, spread cream cheese mixture to form second layer. Finish with almost set Jell-O mixture.  Dot with mini marshmallows as desired.
Note:
this can be made the day before!
may substitute raspberry, cherry or any combination of fruity flavors desired!

Description: 
At some point during the holiday...we MUST eat this side dish (or it's simply not a complete holiday at our house)!  With its fruity gelatin, sweet cream center and salty crust...kids of all ages can never get enough.  Best enjoyed with dear old and cherished new friends and family, just like Aunt Dee!
