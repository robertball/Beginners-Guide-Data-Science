Title:
How to Make Sweetened Condensed Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2738985.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup white sugar
1/2 cup water
1 1/8 cups powdered milk

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine all the ingredients. Heat to boiling. Reduce heat and cook until thick, about 15 to 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jeannie
Description: 
Make the sweetened condensed milk at home instead of buying it at the store. This recipe makes enough to equal the standard can size of 14 ounces.
