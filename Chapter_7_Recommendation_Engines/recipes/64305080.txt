Title:
Get-Up-and-Go Muffins with Greek yogurt, Oatmeal and Blueberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1966876.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups plus 1 Teaspoon Flour
1 Cup quick Oats
1/3 Cup Brown sugar
1/2 Teaspoon salt
1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
1 Teaspoon Baking Powder
1.5 Tablespoon Vanilla
1/2 Cup unsweetened applesauce
2 Eggs
1 1/4 Greek Yogurt
2 Tablespoons of Water of Milk
1 1/4 Cup Blueberries

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350
Spray muffin tin or line with muffin cups
In a medium sized bowl whisk Flour, Oats, salt, baking powder, an baking soda and set aside
In a large bowl whisk eggs, brown sugar, applesauce, greek yogurt, vanilla, and water or milk
wash and dry blueberries and toss with 1 teaspoon of flour (this will prevent them sinking to the bottom)
Fold Dry ingredients into Wet mixture, then fold in blueberries. DO NOT OVER MIX.
Fill muffin cups 3/4 way full
Bake for 20 mins
Enjoy fresh from the oven or cool completely and store in air tight container for 2 days or pack away and freeze.

Submitter: 
Chef V
Description: 
No OIL, No BUTTER...this muffing makes me feel not guilty about having more than just one. They are great to Grab and GO for a quick breakfast. The Oats, and Greek Yogurt will keep you feeling Full.
