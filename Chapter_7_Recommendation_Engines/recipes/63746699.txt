Title:
Chorizo Autentico de Carlsbad v. 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pork, shouder, ground	2 lbs
Fat pork	                        2 oz
Salt, table	                        6 gram
Pure red chili powder	5 Tbsp
Paprika	                        2 Tbsp
Garlic, powder	                2 Tbsp
Oregano, dry leaf	        2 Tbsp
Cumin seed, crushed	2  tsp
Coriander seed, crushed	 1/4	tsp
pepper, black	                1 tsp
Sugar, granulated	  1 1/2 tsp
vinegar, cider or wine	4 Tbsp
Water	                        2 1/2 Tbls
Red pepper Flakes            2 Tbls

Directions:
Add ground pork and ground pork fat into a bowl and mix with your hands. Add ingredients and thoroughly mix all of the spices and wet ingredients.
After covering you can age the mixture for a few days in a refrigerator. This well let your spices blend together. You can keep in the refrigerator for a couple of weeks.
You can use the mixture immediately and you probably will after you breath-in this spicy sausage. Wrap in small packages for freezing or you can stuff casings as well.
You may want to experiment with the different chili powders to find the one or combination of chills that meets your needs.
