Title:
Big Ray's Italian Baked Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/997825.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbl olive oil
1 small onion, chopped
3 clove garlic, chopped
1 can 28 oz crushed tomatoes
1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp fresh ground pepper
5 eggs
4 oz slized mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees. In a 12 cast iron skillet(or other oven safe nonstick skillet), heat olive oil over medium heat and cook onions untill soft, about 3-5 minutes.  Stir in garlic and cook an addtional minute.  Pour in tomatoes, 1/4 cup of basil, and spices.  Reduce heat to medium and simmer for 5 minutes.
Remove skillet from heat and crack eggs into sauce, spacing evenly apart.  Distribute cheese around skillet and bake in oven on top rack untill egg whites are set, about 8 minutes.
Carefully remove skillet from oven and scatter remaining basil on top.  Serve immediatly with crusty bread.
