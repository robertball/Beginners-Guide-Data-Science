Title:
Shrimp pad thai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz. rice-stick noodles
1/4 cup tomato-based chili sauce
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
3 TBS. soy sauce
2 TBS. light-brown sugar
1 TBS. anchovy paster
4 TBS. vegetable oil
4 garlic cloves, minced
1 lb. peeled and deveined medium shrimp
3 cups bean sprouts, plus more for garnish
8 scallions, trimmed, halved lengthwise, and cut crosswise into 2" pieces
1 large egg, lightly beaten
Assorted garnishes such as red pepper flakes, cilantro, lime wedges, chopped dry-roasted peanuts, and bean sprouts

Directions:
Bring a large pot of water to boil; remove from heat.  Stir in noodles; let soak until softened (3 minutes).  Drain; rinse under cold water until cool.
In a small bowl, whisk together chili sauce, lime juice, soy sauce, and anchovy paster.  In a large skillet, heat 2 TBS. oil over medium0high heat.  Add garlic, and cook until fragrant.  Add shrimp; cook tossing often, until just opaque throughout, about 3 minutes.  Transfer shrimp to plate.
Return skillet to medium-high heat.  Add remaining 2TBS. oil along with the noodles and chili-sauce mixture; cook, tossing, until combined, about 1 minute.  Add bean sprouts, scallions and shrimp.  pour in egg; toss until noodles are coated and cooked through, about 2 minutes.  Serve topped with garnishes.
