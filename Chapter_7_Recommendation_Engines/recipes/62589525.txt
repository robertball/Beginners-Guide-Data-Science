Title:
Baked Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium baking potatoes
4 slices bacon
1/2 c chopped celery
1/2 c green onions
1 can chicken broth
1.5 c milk
1 garlic clove
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 c sour cream
shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Bake potatoes and coarsely mash.
Combine broth, milk, potatoes, celery, onion, garlic, salt and pepper, bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer 10-15 minutes.
Stir in sour cream and bacon and heat for 5 minutes.
