Title:
Homemade Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1/4 c. chopped onion (or dried onion flakes)
1 sq. (1 oz.) semi-sweet baking chocolate
1/4 c. brown sugar, packed
1/2 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. salt
3 tbsp. chili powder
2 tbsp. flour
2 cans (6 oz. ea.) tomato paste
2 cans (approx. 15 oz. ea.) chili, pinto, and/or kidney beans
2 c. beef broth
1/2 c. beer

Directions:
Saute ground beef and onions in a large kettle over high heat. Lower heat to medium. Add chocolate and stir until melted. Add brown sugar, pepper, salt, chili powder, and flour; blend well. Stir in tomato paste, beans, broth, and beer. Heat to boiling, stirring almost constantly. Reduce heat to medium and let summer 15-20 minutes to blend flavors.
