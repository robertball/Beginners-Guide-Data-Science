Title:
Caramels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123137.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. butter
1 lb. light brown sugar
1 c. light corn syrup
1 can (14 oz.) sweetened condensed milk
1 t. vanilla
1 c. chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Melt butter in a heavy saucepan.
Stir in brown sugar and mix well.
Add corn syrup and condensed milk.
Cook and stir over medium heat until mixture reaches soft ball stage or 238 degrees on a candy thermometer.
Remove from heat and add vanilla.
Pour into a well-greased 9x13 inch pan.
Sprinkle nuts on top.
Refrigerate to cool thoroughly.
Cut into 1/2 inch squares and wrap in waxed paper.
