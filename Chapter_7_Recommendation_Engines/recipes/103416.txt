Title:
Apricot and Mustard Glazed Pork Loin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982884.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (2 pound) pork loin
salt
pepper
1 cup water
3 tablespoons coarse grain mustard
3 tablespoons apricot preserves
1 tablespoon Market Pantry™ butter, melted
1 teaspoon Market Pantry™ minced garlic

Directions:
Heat oven to 450 degrees F.  Season roast with salt and pepper.
Place in shallow uncovered roasting pan.  Roast 15 minutes.
Meanwhile, stir together remaining ingredients.  Spoon glaze over roast, spreading to cover.  Reduce temperature to 350 degrees F.  Return to oven and roast 45 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 145 degrees F.
Place roast on a warm platter and cover for 10 minutes before slicing.

Description: 
This combination of flavors has a little something for everyone. The mix of salty and sweet, savory and spicy is incredibly satisfying.
