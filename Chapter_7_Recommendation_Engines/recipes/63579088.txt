Title:
Italian Provalone Hamburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, finely chopped
1 small green pepper, finely chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 egg, lightly beaten
3 teaspoons dried basil
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 LBS ground beef
1 LBS Italian sausage, casings removed
8 slices of provolone cheese
8 Keiser rolls

Directions:
In food processor combine sausage and hamburger.
Add onion, green pepper, onion, basil, oregano, salt and pepper and combine.
Grill or cook over medium-high heat 5-7 minutes on each side or until meat reaches 160 degrees.
Top each burger patty with a cheese slice 1 minute longer or until cheese melts.
Grill rolls cut side down for 1-2 minutes or until toasted.
