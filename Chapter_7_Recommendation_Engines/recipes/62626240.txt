Title:
Fruit Smash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sifted flour
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup chopped nuts, such as pecans, almonds or walnuts
1/2 cup butter (1 stick), softened
3 ounces cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 envelope Dream Whip whip topping mix
1 can fruit pie filling (blueberry, raspberry, strawberry, etc.)
Cool Whip, thawed, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400. In bowl mix together flour, brown sugar, and nuts. Cut in the butter until well incorporated and forms soft dough. Using fingers press dough evenly into the bottom of an 8 x 8 baking pan. Bake at 400 for 10 to 15 minutes or until just beginning to brown; cool completely. In a mixing bowl cream together the creamcheese, powdered sugar and vanilla; set aside. In another small bowl mix Dream Whip according to box directions. Blend into the cream cheese on lowest speed. Spread mixture over cooled crust. Spoon and spread fruit pie filling over cream cheese mixture, using as much or as little (to your taste). If desired top with Cool Whip. Chill several hours before cutting and serving.

Submitter: 
FunwithFood
Description: 
I received this recipe from my sister-in-law a few summers ago. The toasted nut crust compliments the light creamy layer and fresh fruit topping. You may use whatever fruit topping you like which makes this a very versatile dessert.
