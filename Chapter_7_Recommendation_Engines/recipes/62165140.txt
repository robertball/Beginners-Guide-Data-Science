Title:
Mom's Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion, chopped fine
1 celery stalk, chopped fine
1 carrot, chopped fine
3 garlic cloves, minced
3 slices uncooked bacon, diced
1 pound lean ground beef, pork or veal (or a mixture of all three)
2 28 ounce cans crushed tomatoes
2 tbs. tomato paste
2 tbs. olive oil
2 tbs. balsamic vinegar
2 tbs. chopped fresh basil
2 tsp. sugar
1 tsp. dried oregano
Salt
Pepper
Cooked Spaghetti
Freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Warm 2 tbs. of olive oil in a sauce pot over medium heat.
Add bacon to pot and saute until just crispy.
Add onion, carrot, celery and oregano and saute mixture for 8-10 minutes.
Add minced garlic and cook for 2 minutes.
Add ground beef and cook until browned.
Add balsamic vinegar and stir until evaporated.
Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste and sugar.
Once sauce is bubbly season to taste with salt and pepper.
Just before serving add fresh basil.
Serve over cooked spaghetti with freshly grated parmesan cheese.
