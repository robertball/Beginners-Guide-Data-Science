Title:
Dole® Whip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3398172.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (20 ounce) cans crushed pineapple, drained
1/3 cup white sugar
2 tablespoons lemon juice
2 tablespoons lime juice
1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream

Directions:
Blend pineapple, sugar, lemon juice, and lime juice together in a blender until mostly smooth; freeze until slushy consistency, 1 to 2 hours. Stir cream into pineapple mixture until evenly incorporated.

Submitter: 
Corinne
Description: 
My mom and I had this at the Magic Kingdom® in Disney® and it is to die for! Excellent on a hot day as a smoothie and even better when frozen as a sorbet.
