Title:
Passover Mandel Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 c sugar
3/4 cup oil
1 1/2 cup cake meal
1 c chopped nuts
dash salt

Directions:
Mix all together & refrigerate for 30min. Then, roll into logs---use wax paper or other non-stick paper. Then, bake at 350 for 30min.
Let cool. Slice & return to oven to finish baking. About 15min---but watch them!!!
