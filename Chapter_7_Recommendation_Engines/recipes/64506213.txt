Title:
Hoison Chicken Udon Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz dry udon noodles, prepared according to package
2 chicken breasts, cut into small pieces
1 tbsp sesame seed oil
1 tbsp olive oil
1/3 cup soy sauce, low sodium
1 tbsp sriracha sauce
1/4 cup hoisin sauce
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp freshly grated ginger
mushrooms sliced
1/2 cup chicken broth
3 green onions, chopped
black pepper to taste

Directions:
Prepare noodles according to package, drain well and immediately rinse with cold water. set aside
heat olive oil and sesame seed oil in a  large skillet or a pot and add chicken pieces and cook until chicken is no longer pink and starts to brown. Add ginger, garlic, mushrooms and cook for a couple more minutes until mushrooms are starting to brown.
Add soy sauce, hoisin sauce, sriracha, and chicken broth,, stir and bring to a boil. Add noodles to the pot and cook until noodles are heated through. Garnish with green onions and serve hot.
