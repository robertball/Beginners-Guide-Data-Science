Title:
Southwest Ranch Sauce - Buffalo Wild Wings Copycat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4534608.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of milk
3/4 cup of mayonnaise
1 1/2 tablespoons of Hidden Valley Ranch DRESSING mix, not dip.
1 teaspoon of ketchup
1 teaspoon of sriracha
1 teaspoon of McCormick Gourmet Collection Southwest Seasoning blend
1 tablespoon of taco seasoning
2 teaspoons of Goya Adobo seasoning with pepper
2 teaspoons of pepper
2 teaspoons of chili powder
2 teaspoons of smoked paprika
1/4 teaspoon of cumin
1/4 teaspoon of cayenne (or to taste)

Directions:
Mix mayonnaise, milk and ranch dressing mix together to make ranch dressing (You can also use a cup of Ranch dressing and 1/4 cup of mayonnaise  to make the base).
Next, add ketchup and sriracha, and stir.
Then, mix in southwest seasoning, taco seasoning, chili powder, smoked paprika, cumin, adobo, and pepper.
Finish off with adding cayenne to taste, depending on whether you want it more mild or hot.
Refrigerate until serving
