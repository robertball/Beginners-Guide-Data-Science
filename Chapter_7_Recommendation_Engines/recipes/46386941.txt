Title:
Vegetable Stir-fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 medium onion, sliced
3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
Ginger, powdered or fresh
1 cup water
1/3 cup tablespoons rice wine vinegar
3 tablespoons honey
3 tablespoons soy sauce
3 teaspoons cornstarch dissolved in
3 tablespoons water
Any vegetables on hand (broccoli, celery, zucchini, cauliflower...)
1 carrot, peeled and sliced thin
1 green/red/yellow bell pepper, seeded and cut into strips
2 cups cabbage, chopped
1 1/4 cups bean sprouts (optional)
1 can bamboo shoots, drained and chopped (optional)
1 can water chestnuts
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
2 medium green onions, thinly sliced diagonally (optional)

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat. Stir in onions and cook for one minute. Stir in garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
Stir in carrots, cabbage (+ any veggies needing longer cooking time) for 2+ minutes. Stir in remaining vegetables, ginger and crushed red pepper, and heat through. Remove from heat.
In a small saucepan, combine water, rice wine vinegar, honey, and soy sauce, and bring to a simmer. Cook for two minutes, then stir in cornstarch and water mixture. Simmer until sauce thickens. Pour sauce over vegetables and rice. Garnish with scallions.
