Title:
Colorful potato salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups potatoe
3 hard cooked eggs, chopped
1/2 cup diced celery
1/2 cup chopped green onion or chives
1/2 cup of fresh or frozen peas
1/2 cup grated carrot
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/2 cup chopped fresh spinach
chopped radishes (opt.)
1 cup mayonnaise or to taste
1/4 cup dill pickle juice
1 tsp. mustard
S & P

Directions:
Cook potatoes, then mash or cube, add remaining ingredients when potatoes have cooled

Description: 
A very pretty and tasty salad for the summer, not to mention nutritional.
