Title:
Retro Stuffed Bell  Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 small or 4 large green peppers
1 strip bacon
1 onion
1 pound hamburg
1/2 cup cooked rice
1/2 cup corn (frozen or fresh)
2 tsp chili powder
Salt and pepper to taste
Topping:
1 can tomato soup
1/4 cup ketchup
Grated cheddar cheese

Directions:
Blanch pepper halves in boiling water for 1 - 2 minutes; plunge in ice water after removing from boiling water. Set aside to drain and cool.
Cook bacon and onion until onion starts to turn golden. Add hamburg to hot skillet; let it sear then break up until cooked thoroughly. Drain. Add corn, chili powder, salt and pepper; stir in rice. Let cool.
Pack meat mixture into pepper halves arrange in 13 X 9 baking dish. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix soup and ketchup; drizzle over peppers; top with cheddar cheese. Bake 30 to 45 minutes.
