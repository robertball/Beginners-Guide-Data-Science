Title:
Espetadas (Portuguese Beef Shish Kabobs) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/422785.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lbs of sirloin steak (or tenderloin/eye round, cubed
6 bay leaves, roughly chopped
8 cloves of garlic
3/4 cup of red wine
2 tbsp of coarse salt
freshly ground pepper to taste
skewers

Directions:
Put the cubed beef, bay leaves, garlic, wine, salt and pepper into a bowl and mix until the beef is coated all over.
Marinate overnight.
Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
Skewer the beef onto wooden sticks that have been soaked in water.
Cook a few minutes on each sides of the cubes until the meat is medium rare.

Submitter: 
Raquel Teixeira
Description: 
This is a Portuguese classic dish that originates from the island of Madeira. It involves simple ingredients but delivers amazing flavours. This is a regular staple in the summer and the first meal I have when I travel to my home country, Madeira.
