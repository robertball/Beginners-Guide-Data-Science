Title:
Chicken Saute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16oz chicken, cut into 1 inch pieces
2 cups dark greens
1 cup chopped bok choy
1 cup mushrooms
can water chestnuts
1 cup bean sprouts
1 cup broth
1/2 teaspoon ginger
dash of cayenne

Directions:
Saute chicken in broth until cooked through.  Add veggies and spices. Saute until crisp tender.
