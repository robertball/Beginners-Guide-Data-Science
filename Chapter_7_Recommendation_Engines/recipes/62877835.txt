Title:
Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
about 1 lb green beans
4 tbls butter
1/4 c olive oil
1/2 onion, diced
Tony Chach's
Fresh Ground Pepper
1 1/4 c chicken stock
1 clove garlic, minced

Directions:
Boil the beans in salted water and cook about 8 mins; drain well.
Melt the butter in a pan; add the oil.
Add the onions, cook 3-4 mins or until translucent.
Add the beans, Tony Chach's, pepper, garlic. Add the chicken stock and stir.
Cover and cook until tender - about 15 mins on med high heat.
