Title:
Tropical Breeze Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 boxed white cake mix
3 egg whites
1 1/4 cup water
1/3 cup oil
8 drops Pina Colada candy oil
6 drops Coconut candy oil
6 drop banana cream candy oil
Filling:
2 cups whipping cream
1 small box instant pudding banana cream or coconut cream

Directions:
In a mixing bowl add the water, oil, egg whites, and candy oil flavorings. Add dry ingredients and mix for two minutes on medium speed. Bake at 325 for 35 to 40 minutes or until done. In a chilled bowl add the whipping cream and pudding flavor of your choice. Whip until peaks form.

Submitter: 
Angela Welch
Description: 
I was asked to come up with a tropical flavor cake for a bridal shower and this was the end result. Light fluffy white cake with a slight taste of the tropics.
