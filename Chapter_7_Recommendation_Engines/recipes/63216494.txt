Title:
Emeril's Best Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c all purpose flour
1/2 tsp fine sea salt
1/4 tsp baking powder
2 sticks cold unsalted butter cut into cubes
2 T Sugar
about 1/3 c ice cold water

Directions:
Whisk flour, sale, baking powder and sugar. Add butter all at once and mix until resembles coarse crumbs. Add water 1 tablespoon at a time until dough just comes together. Don't overmix! Turn out onto plastic wrap- there will be crumbs- form into two balls, flatten slightly, and wrap in the plastic wrap. Refrigerate for 30 minutes. Roll out and proceed with your favorite pie recipe.
