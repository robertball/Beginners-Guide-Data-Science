Title:
Low Country Boil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 gallons water
1 cup old bay
2 oranges, cut in half and squished over the pot
2 onions cut up
1-3 beers as you wish (I think 1 is good)
5 lbs of little potatoes
at least 3-16 oz of andouillle sausage (use smoked sausage)
8 ears of corn husked, and broken in 1/2
4 pounds of shrimp
scallops
crab leg
other hard shell fish if ya want

Directions:
First, get the water boiling-with the seasonings, oranges,onions, beer, and add sausage now.
Add potatoes and corn first and let them cook for 15 min add crab, then shrimp, then scallops in that order for correct cooking times.
take off the heat.
