Title:
Congo Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/472852.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. box of brown sugar
2/3 cup corn oil
3 eggs
2-3/4 cups sifted all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
2-1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 tsp vanilla
1 c. chopped nuts
1 c. chocolate chips

Directions:
Cream brown sugar, oil and eggs, beating well after each egg addition. Sift together flour, salt and baking powder. Combine with creamed mixture. Add vanilla, nuts and choclate chips. The dough will be very heavy. Spread in greased 13x9 dish. Bake at 350 for 35 minutes. Cut into squares when slightly cool.
