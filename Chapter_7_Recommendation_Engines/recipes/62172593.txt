Title:
Chocolate Cherry Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/308335.jpg
Ingredients:
Bars:
1 box dark chocolate cake mix
1 21 oz can cherry pie filling
1-2 tsp almond extract
2 eggs beaten
Frosting:
1 cup sugar
5 TB butter
1/3 cup milk
1 6oz pkg (1 cup) semi sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease & flour a 15x10 inch jelly roll or 13x9 inch pan.
In large bowl, combine the bar ingredients.  By hand, stir until well mixed.  Spread into prepared pan.  Bake jelly roll pan 20-30 minutes; 13 x 9 pan 25-30 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.  While bars cool, prepare frosting.
In small saucepan, combine sugar, butter & milk.  Bring to hard boil and boil, stirring constantly for 1 minute.  Remove from heat; stir in chocolate chips until smooth.  Pour over partially cooled bars.  Let cool completely.  About 36 bars.
