Title:
Shrimp Asopao Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3T olive oil
1/2 green pepper diced and 1/2 red pepper diced
1 small tomato diced seeded or 1 can diced tomatoes drained well
1 small onion diced
1 lb medium to large fresh shrimp
1 bay leaf
3cloves of garlic minced or 3 teas of minced jar garlic
1T tomato paste
1/2 teas salt and pepper
1T adobe seasoning
1 1/2 c short grain rice
7 cups of water
optional red pepper flakes season to taste
3/4 canned or frozen peas

Directions:
Sprinkle the shrimp with salt,pepper, adobe and optional red pepper flakes and set aside.
In a large deep pot, heat the olive oil and add the peppers, tomato, onion cook til soft.
Add tomato paste and garlic and stir to coat the mixture.
Then add the uncooked rice, stir to coat the rice.
Add 7 cups of water and 1 bay leaf, bring to a light boil and reduce the heat, cover at a simmer for 20 minutes, then add the shrimp and peas and cook 5 minutes more or until shrimp is pink. Remove from heat or this will thicken up.
Serves 6-8 serve with a nice crusty cuban bread or pan cubano bread

Judith Hornlein

A cuban recipe latino style shrimp soup hearty like a beef stew would be
