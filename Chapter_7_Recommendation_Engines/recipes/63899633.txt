Title:
Sausage Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 white bread slices, cut into cubes
1 lb. pork sausage, cooked, crumbled, and drained
1 1/2 c. grated sharp cheddar
10 large eggs
2 c. milk
2 tsp. dry mustard
1 tsp. salt
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Grease 9x13 casserole.  Place bread cubes in prepared dish.  Sprinkle sausage and cheese evenly over the bread cubes.  Beat together eggs, milk, mustard, salt, & pepper.  Pour over sausage mixture.  Let stand at least 1 hour or overnight to soak bread cubes.  Bake at 350 until center is set, about 50 minutes.
