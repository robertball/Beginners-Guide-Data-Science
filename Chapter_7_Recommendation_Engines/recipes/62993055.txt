Title:
Day Before Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cubed
6 ounces cream cheese
1 cup sour cream
2 teaspoons onion powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 tablespoons butter
Moisten prior to reheating using the following
1/2 stick of butter
1/2 cup of milk

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Drop in potatoes, and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes.
Transfer potatoes to a large bowl, and mash until smooth. Mix in the cream cheese, sour cream, onion powder, salt, pepper and butter. Cover, and refrigerate 8 hours, or overnight.
2 1/2 hours prior to eating, add the butter and milk to mixture and place into greased crock pot, and reheat.

Submitter: 
Tricia McTamaney
Description: 
This recipe helps you plan ahead by allowing you to make your mashed potatoes in advance!Modified from: Day Before Mashed Potatoes
