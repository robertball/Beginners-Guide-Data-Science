Title:
Steal Cut Oats and Quinoa Breakfast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1080384.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup steal cut oats
1/2 cup quinoa
3 cups water
2 tbs ground almonds
2 tbs ground flax seed
1 tbs cinnamon

Directions:
Boil 3 cups of water
Add steal cut oats and quinoa to water and simmer stirring frequently to avoid burning
Once water has been absorbed, stir in ground almonds, flax seed
Pour into glass container and sprinkle with cinnamon
Refrigerate once cool for up to a week.

Submitter: 
AnneP
Description: 
The best make ahead breakfast! Full of fiber and protein will keep you full all morning. Keeps in the fridge for a week.
