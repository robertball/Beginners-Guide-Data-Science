Title:
Creamy Chicken and Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sliced carrots
1 1/2 cups chopped onions
1 cup sliced celery
2 tbsp. snipped fresh parsley
bay leaf
3 medium sized chicken legs and thighs, skin removed
2-10 3/4 oz. cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup water
1 tsp. dried thyme
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 cup peas

Directions:
Place carrots, onions, celery, parsley and bay leaf in slow cooker.
Arrange chicken on top of vegetables.
Combine soup, water, thyme, salt and pepper in a bowl.   Pour over chicken and vegetables.
Cover.  Cook on low 8-9 hours or high 4-4/12 hours.
Remove chicken from slow cooker.   Cool slightly.
Debone chicken, cut into bite sized pieces or shred and return to slow cooker.
Remove and discard bay leaf.
Stir peas into mixture in slow cooker.  Cook 5-10 minutes until peas are heated through.

Submitter: 
Natasha Sellner
Description: 
Pour over 12 ounces of noodles.  Toss gently to combine and serve with a nice crusty bread and a salad.
