Title:
Cream of Salsa Something Yummy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of Healthy Request Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup
1 Jar of salsa
1 1/2 lbs of chicken

Directions:
Dump all of the ingredients in a crock pot and cook on high for 4 hours.  When done, serve over white rice and top with shredded cheddar cheese.
