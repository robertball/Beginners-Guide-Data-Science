Title:
Mom's Tea Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups sifted Monarch flour
7 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp nutmeg
6 Tbsp sugar
2/3 cups chilled shortening
2 eggs, well beaten
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
Sift into bowl first 5 ingredients. Cut in shortening finely. Combine beaten eggs and milk.  Make a well iin flour mixture, and add liquid all at once. Mix lightly with a spatula. Kneed for 10 seconds and roll out to 3/4 inches thick.
Arrange on tray and cut with cookie cutter. Brush tops with milk. Bake at 375 degrees for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
KerriJ
Description: 
Delicious family recipe for tea biscuits that was given to my mom back in the 1950s by her grandmother.  My mom always served these with her homemade chili.
