Title:
Baked garlic Bundles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz phyllo
3/4c butter
3 large heads of garlic
1/2c finely chopped walnuts
1c Italian seasoning

Directions:
Cut phyllo into 2" strips, remember to keep damp by covering with a moist towel.
Take one strip and brush with butter. Place one garlic clove and 1t walnuts over length of strip.
Roll strip and tuck sides in as you go.
Brush with butter, roll in bread crumbs.
Repeat with remaining phyllo. Place on a cookie sheet.
Make 350* for 20 min.
