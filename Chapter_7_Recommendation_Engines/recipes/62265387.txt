Title:
Sausage and Bean Slow Cooker Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/341259.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon oil
1 lb of good quality sausage
1 medium chopped onion (8 ounces)
12 fluid ounces beef stock
4-5 carrots cut into chunks
14 ounces tomatoes
14 ounces chickpeas, drained and rinsed
14 ounces red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 tablespoon Tabasco® or Sriracha® sauce (optional)
2 tablespoons tomato puree

Directions:
Heat oil in a large frying pan, add sausage and brown. Add onion and brown briefly. Remove sausage and cut into 1.5 to 2 inch pieces.
Place carrots into slow cooker, add chick peas and beans then top with sausage.
Put stock, tomaotes, tomato puree and hot sauce into the frying pan and warm, stirring to loosen pieces from the bottom of the pan.  Pour into the slow cooker.
Cook on low for 4 to 6 hours or more.

Description: 
This hearty slow cooker dinner will fill your kitchen with a delicious aroma and isn't that same old chicken and cream-of-something slow cooker meal. Serve it with a crisp green salad and a loaf of crunchy bread for a stick-to-your-ribs dinner.
