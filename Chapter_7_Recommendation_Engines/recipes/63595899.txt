Title:
Roasted Three Potato Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1000294.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large sweet potatoes peeled and cut
8 medium white potatoes peeled and cut
12 medium red potatoes peeled and cut
1/4 cup of olive oil
1/4 tsp of cumin
2 tsp. garlic salt
1 tsp of black pepper
Sprinkle of thyme and rosemary

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400*. Peel and cut all potatoes into 3/4" pieces (or cubed to your liking). Place oil on the bottom of a roasting pan and add the sweet potatoes and cumin. Cook in oven for 15 min., stirring occasionally. Then add the white and red potatoes and the garlic salt, herbs and black pepper. Finish cooking all together for another 45 minutes, stirring occasionally until potatoes are soft and browned.

Submitter: 
AuntE
Description: 
I love being able to mix up potatoes. The color makes a good presentation but the taste is wonderful. I add parsnips most of the time and nobody knows they're getting an extra vegetable!
