Title:
My Moms No Boil Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/943632.jpg
Ingredients:
Box lasagna noodles
2 Eggs
32oz  Ricotta
16oz  Mozzarella Cheese
1/2C  Parmesan Cheese
45oz Spaghetti Sauce
1 lb Hamburger or Italian Sausage(optional)
1 Onion (optional)
1 C Mushrooms(optional)

Directions:
Mix Ricotta, eggs, Parmesan cheese, half the mozzarella cheese into a bowl.
In a 9x13 lasagna dish start by spooning sauce into the bottom of the dish then add a layer or noodles.
Cover top of noodles with layer of sauce then add half of the ricotta mix, spreading evenly.
Add another layer of sauce then layer of noodles.
Add another layer of sauce over noodles then the other half of the ricotta mix.  Add layer of sauce then another layer of noodles.
Cover noodles with a layer of sauce then the remaining half of the cheese.
If you want to add hamburger, sausage, mushrooms, onions or whatever you can.  Cook the meats before then add to the sauce.
Bake at 375 covered with foil for 45 min then remove foil and bake an additional 15 min or until cheese is golden brown.

Submitter: 
ChrisB
Description: 
This is my moms no boil recipe, it is easy to customize to your taste. I cook the sauce ahead of time and add spices.  I also brown some beef with garlic, mushrooms and onions then add to the sauce.
