Title:
17 day diet Chicken Gyros Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg bonless skinless chicken
1 tsp of Olive Oil.
Tzatziki sauce:
2 containers of Greek Plain Non Fat Yogurt.
Garlic Powder
Cucumber, cut and de-seeded (1)
Splash of Lemon juice
Salt and pepper
Fixin's:
Lettuce
Tomato

Directions:
Cube the chicken breasts and and put them in a frying pan with 1 tsp of Olive Oil, cook em!
Once those are cooked remove from pan and let cool.
Tzatziki sauce:
Mix 2 containers of Greek Plain Non Fat Yogurt.  Garlic Powder, finely diced Cucumber, cut and de-seeded, a splash of lemon juice, and some salt and pepper.
Wrap in lettuce, add fixins and enjoy!
