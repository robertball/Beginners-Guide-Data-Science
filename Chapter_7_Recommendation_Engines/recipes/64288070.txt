Title:
Potato Pancakes (Katharine V. Smith's) Recipe

Ingredients:
2 eggs
4 tbsp Bread Crumbs
1 sm. onion
1 apple (optional)

Directions:
Mix eggs, bread crumbs. Mix 1/2 cup potatoes at a time till all potato are in.  Don't cook too fast.
