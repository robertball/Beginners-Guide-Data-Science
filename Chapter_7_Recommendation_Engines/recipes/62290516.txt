Title:
Chili Coke Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3-4 pound) beef chuck roast
1 (12-oz) can cola beverage (like Coca ColaÂ®)
1 package onion soup mix
1 (12-oz) bottle chili sauce
8 cups mashed potatoes (prepared with butter and milk)

Directions:
Place all ingredients except mashed potatoes in crock-pot and cook on low for 7-10 hours.
Shred meat and mix back into the juices in the crock pot.  Let cook for another 30 minutes.
Serve meat and sauce over hot mashed potatoes.

Submitter: 
Keri
Description: 
Beef roast slow cooked in a chili/onion/cola liquid, then shredded and served over mashed potatoes.
