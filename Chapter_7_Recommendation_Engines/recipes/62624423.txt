Title:
Cranberry Chicken Catalina Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
1 can cranberry sauce
1 cup Catalina salad dressing
1 tsp rosemary garlic seasoning
1 tsp onion powder
1 cube chicken bouillon
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes

Directions:
Place chicken breasts in a slowcooker sprinkle with rosemary garlic seasoning and onion powder.
Top with salad dressing and cranberry sauce.  Sprinkle red pepper flakes on top.
Add bouillon cube.
Cook on low 8 hours or high for 5.  Stirring occasionally.
