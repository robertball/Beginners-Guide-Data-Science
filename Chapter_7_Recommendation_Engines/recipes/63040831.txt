Title:
French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Eggs
1 Cup Milk
1/4 tsp. vanilla
1/8 tsp. cinnamon
8-10 slices of bread

Directions:
In a shallow bowl, beat together first 4 ingredients. Dip bread into batter and cook on griddle.
