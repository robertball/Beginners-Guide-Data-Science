Title:
Jalapeno Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2363391.jpg
Ingredients:
2 bananas, broken into chunks
2 cups baby spinach
1 cup frozen mango chunks
1/2 teaspoon chopped jalapeno pepper, or to taste
1 cup water, or as desired

Directions:
Layer banana, spinach, mango, and jalapeno pepper in a blender; add water and blend until smooth, adding more water for a thinner smoothie.

Submitter: 
Alli Shircliff
Description: 
Spice up your morning by adding jalapeno pepper to your green smoothie for a refreshing on-the-go breakfast.
