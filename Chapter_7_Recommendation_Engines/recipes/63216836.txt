Title:
Easiest Black Beans (Frijoles Negros) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons canola oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
1/2 cup chicken broth
salt to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in a small sauce pan, on medium heat, add garlic and stir until fragrant. Then add beans and broth. Cook for 5 minutes. Salt if needed.

Submitter: 
moaa
Description: 
Very easy and delicious. Great as a side dish or topping/filling. Add a dash of cayenne for some heat. Use this as a base and add onions, jalapenos, bacon (omit oil if using bacon), etc., saute it along with the garlic.
