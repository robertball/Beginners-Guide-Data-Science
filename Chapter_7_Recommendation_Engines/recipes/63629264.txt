Title:
Tomato, Cucumber and Lentil Salad - Vegan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1009353.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked lentils
2 tomatoes
1 english cucumber
1/2 red onion
1/2 cup fresh basil
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Cook lentils as directed on package (1 lb. dried lentils to 8 cups water, cook for 25 minutes or until tender but not mushy).  You can also use the pre-cooked lentils from Trader Joes.
Peel the cucumber and chop the tomato and cucumber into bite size pieces.  Finely chop the red onion and basil.  Stir all the ingredients in to the lentils and dress with balsamic vinegar to taste.
This is delicious served hot or cold and is a very satisfying vegan main dish.

Submitter: 
yumnjam
Description: 
This is one of my favorite fresh summer salads.  The lentils add a nice smoky flavor to the spicy onion and basil and the sweet balsamic vinegar.  This makes a great vegan main dish.
