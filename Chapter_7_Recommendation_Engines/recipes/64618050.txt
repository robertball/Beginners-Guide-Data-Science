Title:
Baked French toast casserole,  Paula Deen style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf French bread
8 large eggs
2 cups half and half
1 cup milk
1 tsp. vanilla extract
2  tbsp. Sugar
1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
Dash salt
Maple syrup
Praline topping:
1/2 lbs. butter ( 2 sticks)
1 C. Packed light brown sugar
1 C. Chopped pecans
2 tbsp. Light corn syrup
1/2 tsp. Ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg

Directions:
Slice French toast into 1" slices
Arrange slices in a generously buttered 9 x13 baking dish in two rows
In a large bowl, combine eggs,half &half, milk, sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.
Beat with a rotary beater or whisk until blended but not too bubbly
Pour mixture over bread until all are covered evenly.
Spoon some mixture between slices.
Cover with foil overnight
The next day:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Spread praline topping evenly over bread.
Cook for 40 minutes or until puffed and lightly golden

Sandy Scott

Yummy, delectable breakfast casserole.  Great for a brunch party!
