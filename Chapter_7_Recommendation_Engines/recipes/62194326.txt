Title:
Hot Mulled Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. brown sugar (cut in 1/2 so it's not too sweet)
1/2 tsp. whole all-spice
1 tsp. whole cloves
1 cinnamon stick
1/4 tsp. salt
1 pinch ground nutmeg
1 large orange, quartered
2 qts apple cider

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.
Simmer 10 minutes & serve
