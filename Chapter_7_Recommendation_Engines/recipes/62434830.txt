Title:
Garden Chicken Stew And Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 whole chicken breasts
6 small red potatoes, whole
2 stalks of celery, chopped
1/4 cup fresh green onion
1/4 cup fresh parsley
1/4 cup fresh onion
1 fresh garlic clove, minced
2 tsp salt
1 cube organic vegetable bouillon
1 cup chicken stock or 1 cube chicken bouillon
2 cups water
1 cup milk
2 tbsp butter
1 cup chickpeas
1 cup rice
1 package potato gnocchi

Directions:
Put all ingredients into crock pot except for gnocchi. Set crock pot to low setting, and cook for 6-8 hrs. Add gnocchi during last 1/2 hour of cooking. Add pepper to taste, and serve.

Submitter: 
A. Piatt
Description: 
A little chicken, seasoning, and veggies from your own garden come together in your crock pot for a home cooked meal sure to please.
