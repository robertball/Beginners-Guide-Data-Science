Title:
Garbanzo Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 carton  vegetable broth (48 oz if available or 32 oz)
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 (28 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes, chopped, juice reserved
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Bring vegetable broth to a boil in a large pot.
Meanwhile, heat oil in a small skillet over medium heat.  Saute garlic.  Add garbanzo beans, tomatoes, basil, thyme, salt and pepper.  Heat through and serve.

Submitter: 
Claudia
Description: 
A VERY easy vegetarian meal in a bowl.  The combination of garbanzo beans (chick peas) with pasta creates the complex proteins a vegetarian needs. Very palatable for the garlic and tomato lover.Modified from: Garbanzo Tomato Pasta Soup
