Title:
White Bread III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/158757.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast
3 1/4 cups bread flour
2 tablespoons dry milk powder
2 teaspoons salt
2 tablespoons white sugar
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/4 cup instant mashed potato flakes
1 3/8 cups water

Directions:
Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select White Bread setting; press Start.
