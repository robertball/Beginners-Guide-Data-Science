Title:
Snicker Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ounces Cool Whip
3 Snickers candy bars, chopped
8 ounces cream cheese
3 granny smith apples, chopped
lemon-lime soda

Directions:
Place chopped apples in a bowl and cover them with lemon lime-soda.  This will keep the apples from turning brown. Mix cool whip and cream cheese until smooth.  Add candy bars.  Drain soda from apples and mix them with the other ingredients. Chill.

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
This tastes very much like the big carmel, chocolate covered fuji apples, but is much easier to make.
