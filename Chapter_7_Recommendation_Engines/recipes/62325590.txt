Title:
Potato Salad Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 large eggs
2 teaspoons of pickle relish
2 teaspoon of mustard
(amount of mustard and relish used can be altered to your taste)
4 teaspoons miracle whip
1 large or 2 small potatoes
pepper
salt
paprika

Directions:
Boil eggs for 30 minutes. Then cool eggs under cold water.
Cut potato up into small cubes and boil until tender.
Slice eggs lengthwise and scoop out yolk.
In a separate bowl, mix yolk with relish, mustard, miracle whip, a pinch of salt and some pepper.
Smash potatoes coarsely and mix in with other ingredients.
Stuff eggs with a generous amount of salad and sprinkle with paprika.
Cover with clear wrap and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Sumrall Butler
Description: 
Sliced eggs stuffed with potato salad. Makes a great appetizer or brunch.
