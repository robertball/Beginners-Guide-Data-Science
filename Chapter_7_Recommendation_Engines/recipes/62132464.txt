Title:
BLT Appetizer Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2(8 ounce) cans refrigerated crescent rolls
1 lb Bacon fried
1 cup mayonnaise
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
3 Green onions, chopped
2 Large tomatoes, chopped and deseeded

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a cookie sheet pan with non-stick cooking spray.
Unroll and pat crescent roll in to pan and stand for 5 min, poke with fork
Bake  crescent rolls for 10 min, and cool
Cream the mayo, cream cheese, and garlic salt together.  Spread onto cooled crescent rolls.
Chop the bacon, sprinkle on top of the cream spread.
Remove seeds from tomatoes, and chop. Sprinkle on top of cresent.
Sprinkle chopped green onion, and store in fridge until serving.
