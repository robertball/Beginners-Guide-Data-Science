Title:
Shrimp Penne in a vodka cream sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c diced onion
2 oz. vodka
1/2 c pregeau tomato sauce
small container of half and half
1 tbsp of becel butter flavor margarine
1 small can of sliced black olives(Drained)
1/2 cup of sliced zucchini
2 tbsp of fresh basil minced
2 cloves of garlic minced
1 cup of fresh spinach leaves rinsed
2 cups of cooked jumbo shrimp
1 box of whole wheat penne pasta
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup of sliced mushrooms

Directions:
bring a pot of slightly salted water to a boil.  Cook pasta according to directions
when cooked, rince and set aside
melt butter in frying pan and lightly saute onion, mushroom, shrimp, zucchini and garlic
when onion and mushroom start to turn a little brown add vodka and simmer on low until almost evaporated
add spinach and basil.cook until slightly wilted
add container of half and half
cook over medium heat until just starting to boil stirring continuously
add tomato sauce and olives
continue cooking until heated thoroughly
add cooked penne and continue cooking until pasta is reheated
add salt and pepper to taste
serve with a sprinkle of feta cheese
