Title:
Rice Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 cup margarine
1 (10 oz.) pkg frozen broccoli, chopped
1 can cream of mushroom
8 oz. velveeta
1 cup cooked rice

Directions:
Brown onion in margarine. Add broccoli and stir until thawed. Add soup, then cheese, and stir until melted. Add rice and place in a 2 quart baking dish. 350 for 20-30 min.
