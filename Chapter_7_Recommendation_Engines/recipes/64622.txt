Title:
Sweet & Spicy Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982514.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups pretzel sticks
3 cups square-shaped cheese flavored crackers
3 cups wheat cereal squares
1 1/2 cups mixed nuts
1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted
3 teaspoons HERB-OX® Chicken Flavored Bouillon Granules
3 tablespoons firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon ground red pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 250 degrees F. Combine pretzels, crackers, cereal and nuts. Spread mixture onto a 15 x 10 x 1-inch jelly roll pan. In bowl, combine butter, bouillon, brown sugar and ground red pepper. Pour over cereal mixture; stirring to coat.  Bake for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes. Remove from oven and cool. Store mixture in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
HERB-OX® Bouillon
Description: 
This irresistible snack mix has a fantastic blend of flavors and textures to enjoy. Mixed nuts, pretzels, cheesy crackers, and crispy cereal are blended with sugar and spice for a snack that is sure to keep you munching.
