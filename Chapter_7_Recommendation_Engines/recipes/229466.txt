Ingredients:
Salad Ingredients:
1 bunch romaine lettuce, torn
1/2 small red onion, thinly sliced
2 cups fresh strawberries, halved
1/4 cup slivered almonds
Creamy Poppy Seed Dressing:
3/4 cup light mayonnaise (not salad dressing)
1/4 cup skim milk
2 tablespoons Truvia速 natural sweetener, plus
1 teaspoon Truvia速 natural sweetener*
1 teaspoon vinegar
1 teaspoon poppy seeds

Directions:
Combine romaine, onion and strawberries in a large bowl.
In a separate small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, milk, Truvia(R) natural sweetener, vinegar and poppy seeds.
Drizzle dressing over salad and toss.  Sprinkle with almonds.

This colorful salad tastes as good as it looks. The dressing is sweet and creamy and poppy seeds add a peppery punch.
