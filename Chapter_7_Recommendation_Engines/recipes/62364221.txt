Title:
Pork Back Ribs, Tender and Simple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Slab of Pork Back Ribs
2 tsp Kosher Salt
2 tsp Ground Garlic
1 tsp Freshly Ground Black Pepper
1/2 cup BBQ Sauce
Cooking Spray

Directions:
Shake salt, pepper and garlic on the ribs to taste. Cut into individual servings. Place ribs on a foil covered baking sheet, spray with cooking spray. Cover the ribs with foil, creating a tent. Bake at 250 degrees for 2 hours. 15 minutes before they're done, remove cover and baste ribs with BBQ Sauce. Continue to cook uncovered.

Submitter: 
JPsMommie
Description: 
You don't need a barbecue to make delicious, moist, fall off the bone ribs.
