Title:
Baked Pork Steak with apples and onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pork steak
Apples (I like Gala), cored and sliced
Onions, sliced
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
Worchestershire sauce (opt)

Directions:
Cover a baking sheet with foil; grease the foil. Place sliced apples on foil (to be under the steaks).
Rub Worchestershire sauce into steaks (if desired).  Season steaks with salt and pepper.  Place over sliced apples.  Cover steaks with sliced onions.  Bake at 375F for 45 minutes.
