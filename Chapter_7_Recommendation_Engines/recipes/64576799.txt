Title:
Potato and Sausage Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. vegetable oil
2 carrots, cut into chunks
2 stalks celery, sliced
1/2 medium onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
3/4 lb smoked sausage, cut into 1/2" chunks
2 tsp. paprika
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
6 medium potatoes, peeled & cubed
2 1/2 cups chicken broth

Directions:
In large saucepan or Dutch oven, heat the oil over med. heat.
Add carrot, celery, onion and garlic and cook, stirring, for about 5 minutes or until onion is beginning to soften.
Add the sausage, paprika, salt and pepper and cook for about 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
Add potatoes and broth and bring to boil over med-high heat.
Cover, lower heat to med-low and let simmer about 20 minutes or till potatoes are tender.
