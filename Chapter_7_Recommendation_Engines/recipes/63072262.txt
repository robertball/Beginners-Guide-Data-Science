Title:
Spiced Almond Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
4 cups all purpose flour
2 tsp. ground cinnamon
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. ground cloves
1 tsp. allspice
1 cup slivered almonds

Directions:
In a bowl, cream butter, shortening and sugars until light and fluffy.
Add eggs and beat well.  Combine dry ingredients; stir into creamed mixture along with nuts.  Shape into three 9 inch x 1 1/2 inch rolls; wrap in plastic wrap.
Refrigerate for 2-3 days for spices to blend.
Unwrap and cut into 1/4 inch slices.  Place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets.
Bake @ 350 degrees for 12-14 minutes or until set.
Remove to wire rack to cool
