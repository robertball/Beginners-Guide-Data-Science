Title:
Cuervo Golden Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980673.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lime
Salt
1 cup ice
1 ounce Jose Cuervo Especial® Tequila
1/2 ounce Grand Marnier liqueur
3 ounces Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix

Directions:
Rub the rim of a chilled margarita glass with half a lime; dip the rim into salt to coat.
Pour Jose Cuervo Especial(R) Tequila,  Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix, and Grand Marnier liqueur into a shaker with ice.
Shake the cocktail and strain into the prepared glass filled with ice.

Submitter: 
Cuervo®
Description: 
When creating the archetypical tequila drink, get authentic flavor with the gold standard in tequila, Jose Cuervo Especial® tequila.
