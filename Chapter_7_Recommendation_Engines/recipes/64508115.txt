Title:
Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 hard-boiled eggs
1/3 cup Miracle Whip
1 to 1-1/2 tsp white vinegar
1 to 1-1/2 tsp sugar
dash salt
dash pepper

Directions:
Remove shell from hard boiled eggs.
Slice in half.
Aside yolks into mixing bowl.
Place egg whites on plate or serving dish.
Mix yolks, Miracle Whip, vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper using hand mixer until desired consistency .
Fill egg whites with yolk mix.
Chill in refrigerator for 2 hours minimum or overnight.
Enjoy.
