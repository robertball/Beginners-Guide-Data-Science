Title:
Tuna Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups macaroni, cooked and rinsed in cold water.
1 can tuna, rinsed
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
3 tablespoons Catalina dressing
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup chopped dill pickles
1 cup diced celery
1 tablespoon celery seed

Directions:
Combine and chill.

Submitter: 
Rosebud
Description: 
Don't skip the celery seed. It makes the dish. Can be served with a green salad or used as sandwich filling.
