Title:
Yumm-o Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oz sweetened lime juice
6 oz sour mix
3 oz your favorite tequila
1 oz triple sec
orange slices
Ice

Directions:
In a glass with ice combine the lime juice, sour mix, tequila, and triple sec. Garnish with orange slices.
