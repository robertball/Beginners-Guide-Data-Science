Title:
Robust Marinara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lbs. Italian sausage (sweet or hot)
1 sweet onion, diced
1 head of garlic, minced
2 Tbs Worcestershire sauce
2 15 oz cans italian stewed tomatoes, diced
1-2 Jars tomato & basil marinara (1 jar for extra thick marinara)
1-1/2 cups of red wine
2 bay leaves
1/2 Tbs dry italian seasoning
1/2 tsp salt
2 Tbs butter or olive oil

Directions:
Add Iitalian sausage and Worcestershire sauce to a  large pan over medium heat.
Brown sausage and cook through, about 5-7 minutes
Remove from pan and put in colander to drain fat.
Put cooked sausage in food processor and blend for 5 seconds
In pan melt butter and add onions and garlic.
Saut√© for 2-3 minutes until tender and translucent.
Turn heat down to medium low.
Add red wine and bay leaves: let reduce for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add italian stewed tomatoes, Italian seasoning and salt.
Let simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Reduce heat to low.
Add jar of tomato basil marinara and italian sausage.
Cover and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Remove bay leaves and serve

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
This is a hearty marinara, packed with incredible flavors! Allowing this marinara to take the time to reduce and simmer is the key to such robust and aromatic flavors.
