Title:
Jalapeno Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
6 small jalapeno peppers, seeded and chopped
2 cups sour cream
salt to taste

Directions:
Measure lime juice and jalapenos into a blender; blend until smooth. Add sour cream; mix on low speed until just blended; season to taste with salt; transfer to a serving bowl.

Submitter: 
Jimmy Dean
Description: 
This simple sauce is made with sour cream, fresh jalapenos, and lime juice.  Serve cold with spicy deep-fried chimichangas.
