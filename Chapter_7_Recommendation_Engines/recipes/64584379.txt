Title:
Big Ray's Pickle Juic Cabbage Slaw Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3738392.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
6Tbsp pickle juice from your favorite jar of pickles
2Tbsp Dijoin mustard
2 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
1-2  tsp hot sauce (to taste)
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
2 Tbsp sugar

Directions:
In a medium bowl, whisk all ingredients together until well blended.
Store refrigerated for up to 2 weeks or use immediately.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Whether you use the coleslaw mix from the store or chop up your own cabbage an carrots, this dressing will really top it off.
