Title:
Microwave Fruit Crumble (Pie Fill) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 540 ml fruit pie fill, your choice
3/4 cup flour
3/4 cup rolled oats
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup butter or margarine, softened
1 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Empty can of pie fill into 8" x 8" glass pan.
Mix remaining ingredients till crumbly.
Sprinkle over fruit.
Heat on high in microwave x 8 min.
Serve warm with ice cream, milk or whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Choose your favorite fruit pie filling to flavor this last-minute-what-do-I-make-for-dessert solution!
