Title:
Charleston BLT Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4465958.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound bacon strips, cooked and crumbled
1 cup sour cream
1 cup mayonnaise
2 medium tomatoes, seeded and finely chopped
1 (0.53 ounce) package McCormick® French Onion Dip Seasoning Mix

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in large bowl until well blended. Cover.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
McCormick Spice
Description: 
How do you better a BLT? Turn it into a dip. With five easy ingredients--bacon, mayo, sour cream, tomatoes and McCormick® French Onion Dip Seasoning Mix--you can whip up this dip before the big game starts. Load it on to toasted bread or crackers and discover for yourself why Charleston is so beloved for bacon.
