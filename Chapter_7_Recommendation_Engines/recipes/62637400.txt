Title:
Paleo Coconut Dark Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/580396.jpg
Ingredients:
Dry Ingredients:
2 cups almond flour
2/3 cup shredded unsweetened coconut
1/3 cup coconut flour
3/4 cup dark chocolate chips or cocoa nibs
Wet ingredients:
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla extract
1.5 tsp coconut oil
1/2 cup almond butter (softened)
1/4 cup honey or agave extract (I use both)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix together almond flour, coconut flakes and coconut flour in bowl.
In a separate bowl, beat the eggs.
Add vanilla extract, coconut oil (in liquid state), almond butter, and honey/agave. Mix well.
Incorporate wet ingredients into dry.
Using a scoop, form solid cookie balls.
Place 1" apart on parchment paper-lined cookie sheet
Bake for 9-10 minutes.

Submitter: 
MelissaMC
Description: 
These cookies are made using almond flour, coconut flour, coconut oil and dark chocolate nibs.   Not technically Paleo, but a good treat if you are MOSTLY Paleo :)
Gluten-free, dairy-free, sugar-free.
