Title:
Stuffed Tomatoes   how many your call Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- Grape tomatoes
- Pastry bag filled with herb Gournay cheese
- Chopped chives
- Balsamic reduction

Directions:
Cut a thin slice off the bottom of each tomato to create a flat surface for the tomatoes to stand up.
Slice off the top of each tomato and scoop out and discard the pulp.
Pipe herb Gournay cheese into each tomato.
Sprinkle chives over the tomatoes.
Drizzle balsamic reduction over the tomatoes.
