Title:
Cheesy Turkey Crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crepes:
1 c. Bisquick
1 egg
1 c. milk
Sauce and Filling:
1/4 c. butter
1/4 c. Bisquick
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
2 c. milk
2 c. (16 oz.) Velveeta, cubed
7 - 8 oz. cooked chicken or turkey, chopped

Directions:
Crepes:  Combine all ingredients and mix until smooth.
Heat medium skillet to med-high and spray with cooking spray.
Add batter to pan in small amounts, swirling hot pan immediately to completely and thinly coat the surface.  Crepes should be very thin.
Flip to brown on both sides.
Stack up and set aside until ready to fill.  No need to keep warm.
Sauce and Filling:  Melt butter in saucepan.
Blend in baking mix, salt, & pepper.
Cook over low heat, stirring, until smooth and bubbly.
Remove from heat and slowly stir in milk.  Return to heat and bring to a boil, stirring constantly.
Boil and stir 1 minute.
Add cubed cheese and stir until melted.
Put chopped meat in a small mixing bowl.
Stir in a small amount of cheese sauce, just enough to moisten.
Spoon meat mixture into prepared crepes, roll up, and place seam side down in a 9x13 casserole.
Pour cheese sauce over crepes, reserving a small amount for serving.
Bake uncovered at 350 for 15 - 20 min or until bubbly and slightly browned.
