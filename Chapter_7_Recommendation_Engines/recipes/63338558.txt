Title:
Spinach Cheese Twirls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 tsp. water
1/2 cup shredded Muenster (or cheese of your choice) cheese
1/4 cup grated Parmesan
2 tbsp. green onion, chopped
1/8 tsp. garlic powder
all purpose flour
1 pkg. frozen chopped spinach, thawed and very well drained
1/2 of 17.3 oz. pkg. puff pastry dough - thawed

Directions:
Heat oven to 400.
Combine cheeses, green onion and garlic powder in a medium bowl.
Whisk together egg and water.
Sprinkle flour on work surface and unfold pastry dough.
Brush pastry with egg mixture.
Top dough with cheese mixture and spinach.
Starting at short side, roll up like a jelly roll.
Cut into about 20 (1/2") slices.
Place onto baking sheets and brush with egg mixture.
Bake for 15 minutes or until golden brown.
