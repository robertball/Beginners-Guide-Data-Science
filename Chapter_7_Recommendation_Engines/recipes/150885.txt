Title:
Don Julio Margarita Legendario Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976108.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Don Julio® Blanco tequila
1/2 ounce Grand Marnier liqueur
1/2 ounce chilled lime juice
1 wedge lime

Directions:
Salt the rim of a rocks glass; fill glass with ice.
Pour Don Julio(R) Blanco Tequila, Grand Marnier liqueur, and fresh lime juice into a shaker.
Shake and strain over ice; garnish with lime wedge.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
The true Margarita is made with fresh juice and the light, clean taste of Don Julio® Blanco tequila for a flavor that is crisp and not overly sweet.
