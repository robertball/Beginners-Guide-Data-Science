Title:
Taco Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 tsp garlic powder
1 small can green chilis
1 can diced tomatoes
1 pkg taco seasoning
bag of chips (corn chips, cheddar/sour cream potato chips, etc.)
16 oz sour cream
16 oz shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown ground beef with garlic powder, drain.
Add green chilis, tomatoes and taco seasoning, simmer 10 min.
Layer crushed chips, meat sauce, sour cream, cheese & top with chips.
Bake at 350 degrees for 1/2 hour.
Remove and sprinkle with more cheese.
Return to oven until cheese melts.
Serve with taco sauce, salsa, lettuce, tomatoes, etc.

Submitter: 
ChefJoe
Description: 
My daughter requests this dish at least once a week.  We try different kinds of chips to change it up a little bit.
