Title:
Sinful S'more Clusters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of grahmm crackers
1 bad of marshmallows (mini or large)
2 bags of milk chocolate chips
optional: candy pieces like M&Ms;, Snickers, Twix, etc.

Directions:
In a double boiler melt chocolate chips. In a large bowl smash crackers until small pieces (do not crush!). Mix in marshmallows. When chocolate is melted add to cracker and marshmallow mix. With buttered spoons or hands (careful mixture will be hot). When well mixed place onto waxed paper in clusters. Cover when done with waxed paper. Let harden for three hours or overnight (overnight is best). ENJOY!

Submitter: 
Jackie Fernandez-Gonzalez
Description: 
Sinfully delish easy to make snacks. Perfect for bake sales or pot lucks. A new spin on an oldtime camp fire favorite!
