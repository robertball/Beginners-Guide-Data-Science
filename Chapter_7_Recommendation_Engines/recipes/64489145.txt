Title:
Onion Chip Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz sour cream
1 tbsp dried minced onion
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp chives
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Mix it all together and let sit overnight. Enjoy the next day with potato chips.
