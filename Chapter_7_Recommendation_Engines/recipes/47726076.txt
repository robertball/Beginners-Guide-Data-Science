Title:
Homemade Nutella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 CUP hazelnuts
5 TABLESPOONS agave nectar
1/4 CUP cocoa powder
1 TABLESPOON vanilla
1 tablespoon HAZELNUT OIL
PINCH of sea salt
1 1/2CUPS CONFECTIONERS SUGAR

Directions:
(1) Roast nuts 8-10 minutes til turning dark golden color. Put in colander, roll around (pressing down) till skins come off, discard skins. (2) Grind nuts to smooth butter in processor or blender. (3) Add all other ingredients and blend til smooth.
