Title:
Chili Cheese Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can corn (drained)
1 can chili
8 oz. shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 package corn chips

Directions:
Mix soup, corn, and chili in a casserole dish.  Microwave on high for 5 minutes.  Stir.  Add cheese and microwave 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.  Add corn chips and serve.
