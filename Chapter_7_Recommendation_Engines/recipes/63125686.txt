Title:
Martha Washington Cherries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/796590.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Packages Powdered Sugar
3/4 Stick Margarine
1 Can Eagle Brand Milk
1 Teaspoon Vanilla
1 Cup Pecans, Chopped
6 Jars Maraschino Cherries

Directions:
Drain cherries and set aside.
Melt butter and remove from heat.
Stir in Eagle Brand Milk and vanilla.
Beat in sugar in three or four additions.
Blend in nuts.
Divide dough in small amounts and wrap each cherry seperately.
Chill candy wrapped cherries well.

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
Use as a delightful holiday gift idea or with a holiday buffet.
