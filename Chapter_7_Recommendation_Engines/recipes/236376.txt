Title:
Red Velvet Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4568983.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup chocolate ice cream
1/2 cup milk
3 tablespoons red velvet cake mix
1 tablespoon cream cheese frosting (optional)
2 tablespoons whipped cream, or to taste

Directions:
Blend ice cream, milk, cake mix, and frosting together in a blender until smooth; pour into a glass and top with whipped cream.

Submitter: 
HTR
Description: 
Red velvet cake mix transforms the traditional milkshake recipe into a red velvet lover's dream. This indulgent and brilliantly hued recipe is perfect for Valentine's Day, the holidays, or any day of the week. Add additional ice cream for a thicker milkshake.
