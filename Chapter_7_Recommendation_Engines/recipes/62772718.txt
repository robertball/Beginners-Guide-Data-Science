Title:
Creme de Menthe Cake III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package devil's food cake mix
3 tablespoons creme de menthe liqueur
1 (16 ounce) can chocolate fudge frosting
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
2 tablespoons creme de menthe liqueur

Directions:
Prepare 1 box cake mix as directed, except replace 3 tablespoons Creme de Menthe liqueur for 3 tablespoons water.
Pour into 13x9 inch pan and bake according to package directions.
Remove from oven and cool completely. Frost with chocolate fudge frosting. Fold 2 tablespoons creme de menthe into whipped topping. Spread topping on the cake. Refrigerate.
