Title:
Sauteed Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
4 large tart apples - peeled, cored and thinly sliced
2 teaspoons cornstarch
3/4 cup cold water
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
In a large skillet or saucepan, melt butter over medium heat; add apples. Cook, stirring constantly, until apples are almost tender, about 8 to 9 minutes.
Dissolve cornstarch in water; add to skillet. Stir in brown sugar and cinnamon. Boil for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and serve warm.

Submitter: 
Kimberly
Description: 
Sweet, sauteed apples are great for breakfast, but you can serve them at any meal. The syrup from the apples is delicious on homemade pancakes or waffles.Modified from: Sauteed Apples
