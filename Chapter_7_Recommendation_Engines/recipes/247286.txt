Title:
Gourmet Wasabi Grits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3713740.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 ounce) packet instant grits (such as Quaker®)
1/2 cup boiling water
1 teaspoon wasabi sauce (such as S&B® Wasabi)
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix grits and boiling water together in a bowl; stir in wasabi sauce, salt, and pepper.

Submitter: 
TaMater
Description: 
My budding gourmet chef husband invented this one morning as a perfect side to his wonderful Lump Crab Meat Swiss Cheese Omelets!
 Can't wait to serve it to dinner guests with a nice piece of Marinated Steel Head Salmon and steamed Asparagus!
