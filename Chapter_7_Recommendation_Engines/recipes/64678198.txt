Title:
Grandma's Cherry Cheese Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups - whipped topping
8 oz. - cream cheese
1/2 Cup - powdered sugar
21 oz. - fruit pie filling (standard size)
1 graham-cracker crumb pie crust

Directions:
1) Set your cream cheese out to warm just a little so that it's easier to mix.
2) In a medium-sized bowl mix the cream cheese, whipped topping, and powdered sugar together at med-high speed until smooth and creamy.
3) When mixed thoroughly and smooth, pour into pie crust and spread evenly being careful not to crack or break the pie crust.
4) Top with fruit topping of choice and cover.
5) Place in refrigerator for 3 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Jeanine Miller
Description: 
Fluffy and light, this cheesecake is sure to delight!  :)  Not as heavy as a traditional cheesecake and much sweeter.  So if you're looking for something familiar, but sweet and creamy this cheesecake is the ticket.
