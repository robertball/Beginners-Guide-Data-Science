Title:
Mom's Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 cup vegetable oil
2 cups granulated sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla
2 cups grated zucchini
8 1/2 oz can of crushed pineapple
3 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
3/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup raisins or dates

Directions:
Beat eggs, oil, sugar and vanilla until thick.  Stir in zucchini and pineapple.
Combine dry ingredients and add to liquid mixture.  Stir in nuts and raisins/dates.
Bake in greased loaf pans at 350 degrees for 1 hour.  Let cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans.

Description: 
A great way to use up that extra zucchini from the garden.  This recipe is sure to please!
