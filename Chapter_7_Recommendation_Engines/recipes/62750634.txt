Title:
Shrimp with Mushrooms and Spinach in a Creamy Herb and White Wine Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/633906.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound sliced fresh mushrooms
1 clove garlic, minced
1 Tbsp. butter
1 Tbsp. olive oil
salt and pepper to taste
1/3 cup dry white wine
1 pound peeled and deveined shrimp
1 10 oz. container Kraft Philadelphia Italian Cheese & Herb Cooking Creme
1/2 cup chopped frozen spinach (or 1 cup fresh)

Directions:
Melt butter and olive oil over medium heat in a large skillet. Add mushrooms and garlic and season lightly with salt and fresh ground black pepper. Cook and stir until mushrooms are tender, about 7 - 8 minutes.
Pour in white wine and add shrimp. Cook until shrimp are opaque, about 4 minutes.
Add cooking creme and spinach. Bring to a simmer and cook 2 - 3 minutes. Serve over rice or pasta.
