Title:
Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Deviled Eggs
12 Large Eggs
6 Tablespoons Mayonnaise
2 Teaspoons Mustard
Salt & Black Pepper
Paprika for dusting

Directions:
Boil eggs
Peel eggs - halve lengthwise
Scoop yolks into medium bowl
Reserve egg whites
Mash yolks with Mayo,Mustard,Cayenne
Scoop or pipe mixture into egg halves, dust with paprika
Variations: Smoked salmon
Prepare deviled egg yolk mixture using 3 tbsp sour cream in place of Mayo, stir in half cup of finely chopped smoked salmon & 3 tbsps of fresh chives
Fill egg white halves and topped with chopped capers
Variation: Curried Crab
Prepared deviled egg mixture
Add an extra 2 tbsp of mayo
Stir in 3/4 cup finely chopped crabmeat, 2 tsp ketchup and 1/4 tsp curried powder
Fill egg white halves
