Title:
Pumpkin Peanut Butter Refrigerator Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/826691.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup uncooked old fashioned oats
1/3 cup skim milk
1/4 cup low-fat Greek yogurt
1-1/2 teaspoons dried chia seeds
½ cup pumpkin puree
2 tablespoons PB2
2 tablespoons pure maple syrup
¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
In a half pint (1 cup) jar, add oats, milk, yogurt, chia seeds, pumpkin puree, PB2, maple syrup and cinnamon. Put lid on jar and shake until well combined. Refrigerate overnight or as long as 2-3 days. Eat chilled.
