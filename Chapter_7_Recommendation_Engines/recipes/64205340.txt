Title:
Oven-Fried Bananas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cooking spray
1/4 cup crushed graham crackers, vanilla wafers, ginger snaps, or other crushed cookies or cereal, like cocoa puffs
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1 pinch salt
1 large banana, cut into slices

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and spray with cooking spray.
Combine crumbs, cinnamon, ginger, and salt in a bowl.
Spray banana slices on both sides with the cooking spray.
Roll banana slices in the bread crumb mixture to coat, and arrange slices on the prepared baking sheet.
Lightly spray the banana slices again.
Bake in the preheated oven until crisp, 10 to 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Cori
Description: 
These little banana treats are coated in crumbs and baked to a delicious, crispy texture. Serve it with fat-free ice cream for a guilt-free dessert.Modified from: Oven-Fried Bananas
