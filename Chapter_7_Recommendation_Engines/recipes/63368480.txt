Title:
Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
2 cups hot sauce
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1/3 cup blue cheese dressing
1 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
Tortilla Chips for dipping

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 9x9 pan or pie plate. Add the chicken to the pan and mix in the hot sauce. In a separate bowl combine the cream cheese and blue cheese dressing. Spread this mixture evenly over the chicken mixture. Cover the top with shredded mozzarella and bake in the oven for 30 minutes.
