Title:
Spicy Molasses Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup molasses
2 tbsp tabasco sauce
1 tbsp yellow mustard
1 tbsp white rice vinegar
1 tsp minced fresh ginger
1 tsp minced garlic
1 tsp toasted sesame oil
2 lb party wings (wingettes and drummettes)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat boiler with rack 6 inches from element.  Line a baking sheet with foil.
Season wings to taste with sale and pepper.
Combine molasses, tabasco, mustard, vinegar, ginger, garlic, and oil.  Reserve 2/3 cup sauce.
Arrange wings on baking sheet and broil 10 minutes.  Flip wings, baste with molasses mixture, and broil 6 minutes more.  Flip wings and baste uncoated side.  Broil until they begin to carmelize and an instant read thermometer inserted into the thickest part of the meat reads 165 F, about 3 minutes more.
Toss wings with reserved sauce.  Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
Tame the spicy heat of these wings with cool blue cheese or ranch dressing.
