Title:
Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans cream style corn
1 can whole kernel corn
1 9 oz package Mexican style corn bread mix
3 eggs
2 cups cheddar cheese
3/4 cup oil

Directions:
Stir together all of the ingredients.  Bake @ 325 degrees in a 9x11 baking dish for 30 minutes

Submitter: 
agadoelleon
Description: 
Mexican corn bread mix and cheese with corn makes a fantastic and popular casserole.  Everyone will be wanting more and the recipe.
