Title:
Vegetable Vermacelli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 oz Q&Q; Vermacelli
1 tbspn olive oil
1 cup of water
2 zuchinni -sliced
2 squash -sliced
1 box mushroom -sliced
1 green bell pepper -sliced
1 red bell pepper -sliced
1 yellow bell pepperm -sliced
2 diced tomatoes
1 crown broccoli (cut)
2 diced garlic cloves
1 tspn cumin
1 tblspn italian seasoning
4 oz can of tomato sauce
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup chopped cilantro

Directions:
1. Brown vermacelli in olive oil at medium heat stirring occasionally as not to burn vermacelli.
2. Add garlic halfway of browning vermacelli
3. When browned, add 1/2 cup of water and tomato sauce.
4. Add spices, salt & pepper and vegetables
5. Cover and simmer for 15 - 20 minutes at medium heat or until all veggies are soft and done.
For extra zip, add tspn of ground cayenne pepper or 2 tblspn chili powder.

Submitter: 
Gina M. Ford
Description: 
Vermacelli with a mix of vegetables and a quick, easy dinner too - 30 to 45 minutes.
