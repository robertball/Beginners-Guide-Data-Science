Title:
Beef and Wild Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb. Ground Beef
1/2 Small Onion, Chopped
1 (6.2 oz) Package fast cooking long grain and wild rice mix
1 (10 3/4 oz) Can Tomato Soup
1/4 Cup Milk
1/4 Tsp. Pepper
1 Cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese and an additional handfull to spread on top

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350'. Spray 2 Qt. baking dish with cooking spray.
Brown ground Beef with Onions and drain
Prepare Rice Mix according to Pckg. Directions (Including the Seasoning packet it comes with) But Omit Butter.
Stir all ingredients together and spread evenly in baking dish.
Sprinkle more cheese over top and bake 25-30 minutes or until heated through and cheese is melted and bubbly.

Description: 
This is an extremely simple recipe that has alot of flavor. It's not award winning but it is simple and uses minimal ingredients and it's quick.
