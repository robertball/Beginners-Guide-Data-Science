Title:
Easter Traditions Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dressing:
2 cups mayonnaise
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
1/4 cup fresh dill
2 tablespoons water
1 tablespoon minced garlic
Salt & pepper to taste
Salad:
2 pounds asparagus, trimmed to 2 inch pieces
2 bags triple washed salad greens ( such as leaf, Boston, and butter lettuce)
1 pound bag cooked peeled & deveined shrimp, thawed
2 pints grape or cherry tomatoes, halved
2 avocado, diced

Directions:
Dressing:
Add dressing ingredients to medim bowl and wisk until well blended.  Season with salt and pepper.  Refrigerate for several hours or overnight to meld flavors.
Salad:
Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and add asparagus.  Cook until just tender, about three minutes.  Drain and rinse with cold water.
Add all salad ingredients to large serving bowl.  Toss with desired amount of dressing and serve.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
This salad is an Easter tradition in our family.  The ingredients and flavors say spring, but it is delicious any time of year!  Skip the appetizers, the abundance of shrimp make this an appetizer and salad in one.  Enjoy!
