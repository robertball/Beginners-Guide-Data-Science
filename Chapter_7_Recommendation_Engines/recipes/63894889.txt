Title:
Sweet Hawaiian Crockpot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can pineapple chunks
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/3 cup light soy sauce
1 Tbl cornstarch
2 pounds chicken breast tenderloins.

Directions:
Separate pineapple juice, and mix juice with sugar, cornstarch & soy sauce. Add sauce, pineapple and chicken to crockpot and cook on low 6-8 hrs and chicken should just fall apart.
