Title:
July - Lite Apron - BBQ Spiced Salmon and Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz uncooked skinless salmon fillet(s)
2 clove(s) (medium) garlic clove(s)
1 item(s) lemon(s)
1/2 lb uncooked string beans
2 Tbsp Dijon Mustard
1 item(s) uncooked shallot(s)
1 serving(s) BBQ Spice blend
1 serving(s) BBQ Spice blend, smoked sweet paprika, sweet paprika, grnd corriand
2 Tbsp olive oil
1/4 cup(s) fat free skim milk
4 medium uncooked scallion(s)
1 1/3 cup flakes Instant Mashed Potatoes

Directions:
Heat a medium pot of salted water on high.
trim green beans
mince garlic and smash
thinly slice scallions
quarter and de-seed lemon
peel and mince shallot to get  2 Tbsp do not use remainder of shallot
place in bowl with juice of all four lemon wedges.
Fill a bowl with ice water
Add the green beans to pot of boiling water.
Cook 2-3 minutes then transfer to bowl of ice water. once cool drain and dry with paper towel add the garlic paste and mustard to the shallots and lemon.
season with salt and pepper.
Whisk in 1 T of olive oil and whisk until combined.
Make the potatoes using butter buds and fat free milk per instructions on instant potato box. pat the salmon filet's dry and season on both sides with salt, pepper and BBQ rub.
Heat 1 T oil on med. high until hot.
cook salmon 3-5 minutes per side.
Dress the beans with the vinaigrette.
top potatoes with scallions and serve.
