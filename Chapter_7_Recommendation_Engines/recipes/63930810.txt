Title:
Pumpkin Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 load French Bread
1/2 - 1 cup Raisins (depending on taste )
1 tsp. Maple Flavoring
1 tsp. Rum Flavoring
approx. 1/4 cup Water to soak/plump Raisins
1 can Eagle Brand Sweetened Milk
1 can Pumpkin Puree
1/3 cup Brown Sugar
3 cups Milk
2 tsp. Vanilla
3 large Eggs
1/3 cup Butter - melted
2 tbsp. Molasses
2 tsp. Pumpkin Pie Spice
2 tsp. Cinnamon
1 tsp. Allspice
1/2 tsp. Ginger

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 9" X 13" Baking Dish. Cube Bread and spread evenly in Baking dish. In a microwavable dish combine Raisins, Maple and Rum Flavoring, and enough water to almost cover. Microwave for approx. 1&1/2 minutes to plump raisins. Set aside and let cool slightly.
In large bowl combine Sweetened Milk, Milk, Eggs, melted Butter, Brown Sugar, Molasses, Pumpkin Puree, Vanilla, Spices and Raisin mixture. Pour over Bread and let sit for at least 1 hour to be absorbed. Bake 45 - 50 minutes until knife inserted comes out clean. Great served warm or cold and can be complimented with Whipped Cream or Milk.

Submitter: 
Mary
Description: 
Combination of 2 classic recipes - Bread Pudding and Pumpkin Pie. Put this together on a whim and loved it. Taste is more comforting than sweet.
