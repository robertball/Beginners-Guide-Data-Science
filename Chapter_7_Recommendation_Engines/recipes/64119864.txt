Title:
The World's Best Tuna Fish Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1298364.jpg
Ingredients:
2 5 oz. cans white tuna fish, drained
2 5 oz. cans light tuna fish, drained
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon sweet relish
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
2 tablespoons chopped celery
1 tablespoon minced onion
Kosher Salt
Black Pepper
8 slices whole wheat bread
Lettuce leaves (optional)
Sliced Tomato (optional)

Directions:
Place the tuna, mayonnaise, relish, mustard, celery, and onion in a medium-size bowl and blend well.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Evenly spread the tuna salad over 4 slices of bread.  Top the tuna salad with lettuce and/or tomato, if desired, and then top each sandwich with another slice of bread

Submitter: 
Jennifer Hanson
Description: 
At the risk of falling prey to hyperbole, I have to agree with the author that THIS is The World's BEST Tuna Fish Sandwich. (taken from The Mom 100 Cookbook by Katie Workman)
