Title:
Monkey Bread Made Easy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/385347.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tubes refrigerator biscuits (7.5 oz each)
¾ cup white sugar
1 TBS cinnamon
1 stick of butter or margarine
½ cup brown sugar
1 TBS cinnamon
cooking spray

Directions:
Pre-heat your oven to 400 degrees.
Spray a muffin tin with cooking spray.
Remove biscuits from packing and cut into quarters.
Mix together white sugar and 1 TBS cinnamon.
Dredge biscuit nuggets in mixture and place into muffin tins until each muffin cup is full.
In a microwave safe container, melt butter with brown sugar and 1 TBS cinnamon.
Spoon melted mixture evenly over the filled muffin cups.
Bake for 20 minutes, until tops are crispy and brown.
Allow to cool for a few minutes before removing from the pan.
