Title:
Homemade Doodle Chow Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups brown rice, white rice, or oats
4 cups water
2 cups chopped veggies (no onions or garlic)
6 eggs
lb shredded or ground meat, poultry or fish
1 lb cottage cheese
2 T oil

Directions:
Mix water, rice and veggies, and ground or finely chopped meat, and cook about 30 minutes.  Add eggs and oil and cottage cheese, and mix until well combined.  Cook on low heat until eggs are set.
For best nutrition, rotate grains, veggies and protein.
I have a large dog and I freeze this in one cup portions, and I combine this with dry dog food.

Submitter: 
Anisa
Description: 
good to have on hand as a supplement to dry dog food or as a treat!
