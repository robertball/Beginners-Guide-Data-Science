Title:
Prairie Barbecue Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons canola oil
1/4 cup beer
2 tablespoons liquid honey
1/4 cup Dijon mustard
1 teaspoon coarse black pepper
4 boneless pork loin chops

Directions:
Combine canola oil, beer, honey, mustard and pepper in a sealable plastic bag.
Remove 2 tablespoons of marinade; set aside.
Add pork to remaining marinade and squeeze bag to coat pork with marinade; seal bag.
Let stand 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
Remove chops from marinade and pat dry; discard marinade.
Grill chops over low heat for 30 minutes or until done, basting occasionally with reserved marinade.

Submitter: 
The Canola Info Virtual Kitchen
Description: 
Canola oil's high smoke point makes it a great oil for grilling!  Brush it on the grill to prevent sticking.
