Title:
Apple Cranberry tea cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Cranberry Apple Zinger tea bags
2 Orange Peoke tea bags
8 cups water
1 cup sugar
1 cinnamon stick
6 cloves
dash of all spice
2 aples, peeled, cored and chopped into small pieces

Directions:
In a large pot bring water to a boil.  Take off heat and add tea bags, cover and allow to steep for about half an hour.
Take tea bags out and simmer.  Add apples, cinnamon stick, cloves sugar and all spice.  Stir.
Allow to cook for 30 mins or until apples are slightly softened.
*note:  if you add apple juice cut down on water and sugar.

Submitter: 
babybluangel
Description: 
I made this cider one cold night when i didnt have apple juice.  You could add apple juice and it would be even better.  I added chunks of apple but chunks of pear tastes delicious too!
