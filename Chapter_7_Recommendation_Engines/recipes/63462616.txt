Title:
Macarooni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c butter
1/2 c flour
2 c milk
16 oz velveeta
4 cups elbow macarooni
1 cup cheddar chees
12 ritz crackers (about 1/2 cup)

Directions:
Heat oven 350. Melt 3 tbl butter in saucepan.  Whisk in flour and cook 2 min.  stirring constantly.  Gradually stir in milk and bring to boil.  cook and stir 3 to 5 min or until thickened.  Add velveeta; cook 3 minuter or until melted, stirring frequently.  Stir in macaroni.
Spoon into casserole spayed with cooking spay: sprinkle with cheddar.  Melt remaining butter; toss with cracker crumbs and sprinkle over casserole.
