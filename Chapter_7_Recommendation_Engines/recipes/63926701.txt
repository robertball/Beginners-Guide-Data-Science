Title:
Bill's Favorite Burger Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. 85/15 hamburger
1/2 tsp. salt, or to taste
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/4 tsp. garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat gas grill to 400*F.
Mix all ingredients.
Form three large hamburger patties.
Spray patties with PAM or other vegetable based oil to prevent sticking to grill.
Grill to desired level of doneness.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
These burgers are simple yet very tasty.
