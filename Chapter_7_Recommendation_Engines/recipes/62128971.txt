Title:
Huachinango A La Plancha (Grilled Red Snapper) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ea. 	6oz. portions fresh red snapper
4 oz. 	Mayonnaise
2 oz.  Fresh lime juice
3/4	Tbsp. Salt
1/4     teaspoon freshly ground pepper

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the mayonnaise, lime juice, salt and pepper. Rub the fish with this mixture and marinate in refrigeration for 40 minutes.
Preheat a grill or griddle and lightly grease.
Place the snapper portion, bone side up, on cooking surface for approx. 30 seconds until caramelized. Turn over and continue to cook until opaque and cooked through.

Submitter: 
Chef Rick
Description: 
Fish grilled in this manner is a specialty in the palm roofed beach restaurants of Barra Vieja, on the outskirts of Acapulco. It is cooked on a wood fired grill or plancha (griddle).
