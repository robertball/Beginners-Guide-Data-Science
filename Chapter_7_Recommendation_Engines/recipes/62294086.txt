Title:
Salmon/Potatoe Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 envelopes Salmon
1/3 cup bread crumbs
3/4 cup instant potatoes
1/4 cup chopped red/green peppers
salt/pepper/red pepper
1 egg
4 tbl spoons salad dressing whip
3 tbl spoons parsley
2 tbl spoons lemon juice

Directions:
Mix all ingrdients, form into patties, roll in bread crumbs and fry in small amount of oil untill well browned.
