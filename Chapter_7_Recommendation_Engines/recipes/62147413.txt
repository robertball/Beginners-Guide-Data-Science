Title:
Patriotic Fruit Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/353612.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1/2 cup margarine
1 1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs
2 3/4 cup flour
1 tsp cream of tartar
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
package of strawberries - sliced
package of blue berries
3 to 4 bananas - sliced
lemon juice
Filling
2 - 8 ounce packages of cream cheese
1 cup sugar
2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Beat shortening, margarine, sugar, and eggs.
Add dry ingredients.
Spread cookie dough in a rectange on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake at 350 for 8-10 minutes.
While baking, mix cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until smooth.  Use mixture for filling.
When cookie is cool, spread on cream cheese filling.
Arrage fruit on cookie pizza.
Put blue berries in left top corner for stars on flag.
Line strawberries and bananas for stripes on flag.
Be sure to dip bananas in lemon juice before putting on pizza, this will help bananas to stay fresh.
Serve!

Submitter: 
Marcy R.
Description: 
This is a fun recipe for the 4th of July holiday!  Fresh fruit is a hit on desserts during the summer months! Making this dessert is as fun as eating it! This recipe feeds a lot, but the leftovers are only good a day or two.
