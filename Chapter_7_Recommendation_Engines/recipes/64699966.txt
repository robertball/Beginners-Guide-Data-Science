Title:
Hula's Fudge - Salted Caramel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
395g can Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 C Brown Sugar
2 Tbsp Glucose Syrup
1/4 C Golden Syrup
125g Butter chopped
180g White Chocolate, finely chopped
1 tsp Sea Salt Flakes

Directions:
Grease the Fudge pan.
Place SCM, sugar, syrups and butter in a pan over low heat.
Cook, stirring without boiling for 10 minutes or until glossy and sugar is dissolved.
Increase heat to med/low and stir constantly.
Bring to a simmer and cook for 6-8 minutes until mixture thickens and comes away from the side of the pan.
Remove from heat and stir in chocolate until combined and melted.
Spoon into fudge pan.  Smooth the top and sprinkle over the salt pressing it down gently with the back of a spoon.
Set aside for 30 mins then cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 6 hours before serving.
