Ingredients:
2  cans tomato sauce - 29oz
1 can chick peas
1 can cannellini beans
1 can peas
1 can corn
2 carrots chopped
1 zucchini chopped
1 yellow squash chopped
1 head of escarole chopped
4 garlic cloves whole but pressed
Extra virgin olive oil
Red pepper flakes - optional
Parmesan rind
Half pound of short pasta

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large sauce pot.
Soften carrots, zucchini & squash in oil - season with salt, pepper & red pepper - do not brown unless you like that.
Add both cans of tomato sauce.
Fill each can with water and add water to pot.
Drain and rinse all the canned veggies and add to pot.
Add garlic and Parmesan rind to pot.
Add escarole to pot. Season with salt & pepper.
Bring soup to a boil stirring occasionally and gently.
Add pasta to boiling soup and cook until pasta is cooked.
Serve with Parmesan on top.
