Title:
Elsy's Blueberry Breakfast Special Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup frozen blueberries
2 Tablespoons orange juice
1 Teaspoon maple syrup
1 Teaspoon lemon juice
1/3 Teaspoon cinamon powder
1 Banana
1 Tablespoon chunky peanut butter
1 handfull corn flakes

Directions:
Put the blueberries and orange juice in a saucepan over medium heat, when they come to a bubble, lower the heat and add the maple syrup and lemon juice. Stir lightly and add the cinamon.  Stir again. Slice the banana over the mixture. Leave for 1 minute and turn off heat.
Put the blueberry/banana mixture in a small bowl. Serve topped with peanut butter and corn flakes.
