Title:
Thai Red Curry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/588674.jpg
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons olive oil
1/2 teaspoon peanut butter
1 tablespoon sesame oil
1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 tablespoon Thai red curry paste
1 cup sliced halved zucchini
1 red bell pepper, seeded and sliced into strips
1 cup sliced carrots
1 onion, cut into small pieces
1 tablespoon cornstarch
400g can light coconut milk
3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
500 g rice

Directions:
1) Saute the chicken in  sesame oil. Cut into thin square pieces.
2) Cook the rice.
3) Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces; cook and stir for about 3 minutes. Mix in the curry paste, zucchini, bell pepper, carrot and onion. Cook and stir for a 5 minutes.
4) Dissolve the cornstarch in the coconut milk, then pour into the skillet. Bring to a boil, add peanut butter, then simmer over medium heat for 2 minutes.
5) Right before serving, stir in the cilantro. Serve with rice.
