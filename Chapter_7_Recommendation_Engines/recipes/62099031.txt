Title:
Shrimp Mold Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tsp. Knox gelatin
1 can tomato soup
1/2 can water
1  8 oz. cream cheese
1 C. mayonnaise (Hellman's)
1 1/2 C.celery, finely chopped
1 small onion, finely chopped
1/2 tsp. salt
8 ounces frozen tiny shrimp

Directions:
Disolve the gelatin in the cold water.
Cook the shrimp and chill over ice until ready to use. Drain on paper towel to remove excess moisture.
Bring to undiluted soup to boil and add cream cheese.  Stir until blended and cream cheese is melted.
Remove from heat and add gelatin mixutre and blend with wooden spoon.
Add mayonnaise, celery, onion, salt, and shrimp.
Pour into an oiled mold and chill until set.
Carefully dip the mold in warm water to release.
Garnish with leaf lettuce, sliced green and or black olives and pimento. Serve with crackers.
