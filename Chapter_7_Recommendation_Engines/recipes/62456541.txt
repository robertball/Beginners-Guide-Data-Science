Title:
Fabulous Corned Beef Brisket in slow cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lb corned beef brisket
3 cups water,
4 medium potatoes- cut in quarters (*keep large)
2 onions cut in quarterss
1 or 2 shallot, cut into pieces (adds great flavor)
4 large carrots, cut into small slices
whole garlic, cut into slices
SPICES:
garlic powder
onion powder
chives
parsley

Directions:
Trim off the fat on brisket, Mix all the spices together,roll or press brisket in all the spices,
make slits on brisket, place garlic inside the slits
Quickly saute or brown the brisket, to solidify the spices on or inside the meat.
put carrot, onion, shallot and potatoes in the bottom of crockpot,
set brisket on top
add water to crockpot
set on low for 8 hours
VOILA!!
