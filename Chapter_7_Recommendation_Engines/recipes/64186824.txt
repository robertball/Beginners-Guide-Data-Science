Title:
Filipino Chicken Adobo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs chicken thigh/ drumsticks
4 tablespoons of dark soy sauce
2 tablespoons white cane vinegar
2 tsp minced garlic
1/2 tsp brown sugar
1 tsp fish sauce
1 tsp peppercorn
1 cup water

Directions:
1.Mix soy sauce, vinegar and garlic with chicken in a glass bowl.
2. Marinade the chicken for at least 3 hours.
3. pan fry the chicken until all sides are brown
4. pour the leftover marinade into the pot and add water and peppercorn.  Let it simmer until chicken is tender.
5. Mix in brown sugar and fish sauce
Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Sy Simbulan
Description: 
This is a Filipino favorite.  It is a staple food back in the Philippines and is pretty popular among other ethnicities
