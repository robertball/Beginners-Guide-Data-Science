Title:
Scalloped Spuds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. butter or margarine
2 tbsp. flour
1 1/4 cups milk
1/4 cup onion, chopped
1/4 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
3 - 4 medium potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced (about 3 cups)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Grease 1 qt. casserole dish.
Melt butter in saucepan.
Stir in flour and milk.
Cook over medium high heat, stirring, until thick and bubbly.
Remove from heat & stir in 3/4 cup cheese.
Stir to melt.
Place 1/2 potatoes in casserole dish.
Cover with 1/2 of the sauce.
Repeat with rest of potatoes and sauce.
Top with remaining cheese.
Bake, covered x 45 minutes.
Uncover and continue to bake another 30 minutes, or until potatoes are tender.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Especially good paired with ham. Plan ahead - I allow myself 1 1/2 hours from start to serving.
