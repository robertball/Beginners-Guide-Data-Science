Title:
Neil's Favorite Key Lime Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/612514.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust: 1 1/2 cup pretzels finely chopped
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup butter melted
Filling: 1/4 cup water
1 pkg of sugar free lime gelatin (4oz)
2 containers of key lime yogurt
1 tub (8oz) of frozen whipped topping

Directions:
Crust: Combine all ingredients and press into 9in pie pan bake at 400 degrees for 5 mins.
Filling: Microwave water for 45 mins on high and whisk in gelatin then add yogurt and mix.
Fold in whipped topping. Place filling into crust.
Refridge for 4 hours

Submitter: 
Donna Fary Rice Bobchin
Description: 
Fluffy non custard Key Lime Pie with pretzel crust. My husbands favorite Key Lime Pie.
