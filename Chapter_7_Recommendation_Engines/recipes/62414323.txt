Title:
Lamb and Coconut Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lamb, lean.
1 tin coconut milk
1 cup of water
3 pearl onions diced
1 cup mixed vegetables (preferably carrot, peas and beans)
1 tlb diced ginger
4 whole cardamoms
1 2 inch stick of cinnamon
3 cloves
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp whole peppercorn
2 tlbs oil, vegetable, canola or sunflower

Directions:
Heat oil in pan
Add cardamom, cloves, cinnamon and peppercorn till bubbles appear
Add diced onions and ginger, wait till translucent.
Add lamb cut into 1 inch cubes
Stir fry on low to medium heat
Once lamb softens, ddd coconut milk, vegetables and water.
Simmer on low-medium heat for 20 mins
Once lamb is tender, serve with rice and lentil pancakes or  Sri Lankan"hoppers"

Submitter: 
sugarFiend
Description: 
Lamb and Coconut Stew is one of the most flavourful of all South Indian dishes.
Easy to prepare and make, it is best had with rice and lentil pancakes, commonly known as dosas. Freshly ground paste is best but requires some expertise. You can get instant dosa mix in most Indian grocery stores.
