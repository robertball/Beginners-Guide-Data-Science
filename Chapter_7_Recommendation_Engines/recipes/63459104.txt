Title:
Shari's Christmas Tree Press-Outs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or marg. softened
3 oz package cream cheese
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups sifted flour

Directions:
Cream butter and cream cheese together
Add sugar and mix well.
Add vanilla
Refrigerate dough for about 1/2 hour or longer as desired.
Fill press 1/2 full, form cookies on ungreased sheets.
Bake 8 to 10 minutes at 375.

Submitter: 
SIXMITTS
Description: 
A simple dough for press-outs. Cookies keep or freeze well. Use a little food color as desired.
