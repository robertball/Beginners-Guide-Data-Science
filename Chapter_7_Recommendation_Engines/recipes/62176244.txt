Title:
Rich chocolate cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1027476.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
2 cups sugar
6 tablespoons cocoa
2 teaspoons soda
2 teaspoons salt
12 tablespoons (3/4 cup) cooking oil
2 tablespoons vinegar
2 teaspoons vanilla
2 cups cold water

Directions:
mix all dry ingredients together
make a well in dry mixture and add wet ingredients
mix with a fork(or spoon) until well blended
pour in 9x12 pan(may grease pan or not)
bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes
