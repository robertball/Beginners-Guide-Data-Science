Title:
Candy Coated Pecans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg white
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 dash vanilla extract
4 cups pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 275 degrees F (135 degrees C). Line a cookie sheet with non-stick foil.  Spray the foil with cooking spray.
Beat egg white until stiff. Add brown sugar and vanilla.  Stir until smooth. Mix in pecans and stir until coated. Pour the nuts onto the prepared cookie sheet.
Bake until browned, stirring and turning after 15 minutes, then continue baking for 15 more minutes (a total of 30 minutes).
