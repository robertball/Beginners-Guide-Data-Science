Title:
Serena's Strawberry Lassi  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4537180.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups plain whole-milk yogurt
2 cups strawberries
1 cup ice
1/2 cup white sugar

Directions:
Combine yogurt, strawberries, ice, and sugar in a blender. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Serena
Description: 
This is an absolutely delicious and refreshing Indian yogurt drink. It is so tasty that it is almost like a dessert. Your family and friends will love it!
