Title:
Crab Stuffed Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup packaged herb seasoned stuffing mix
1/2 cup celery, diced
1/2 cup chopped green onion
1 (5 oz.) can crab meat, drained
3 tbsp. butter
3 tbsp. dry white wine
paprika
6 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
SAUCE:
1/3 cup shredded Swiss cheese
1/3 cup dry white wine
3 tbsp. butter
3 tbsp. flour
1 pint half & half

Directions:
Prepare stuffing mix according to directions.
Melt butter in microwave
In skillet, cook celery and onion in melted butter for 2 minutes.
Add wine, stuffing mix, crab meat and mix well. Set aside.
Pound chicken breasts between two pieces of waxed paper until thin. Season with salt and pepper.
Divide stuffing mix between chicken breasts, roll up and secure with toothpicks. Sprinkle with paprika.
Place on baking sheet and bake for 45 minutes.
Sauce:
Melt butter in saucepan and stir in flour. Stir until a paste is formed. Gradually add wine and then the cream. Cook until a smooth sauce is beginning to form then add the cheese. Stir until the cheese is melted.
Serve chicken topped with sauce.
