Title:
Country Breakfast Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 (8-inch round) flour tortillas
4 eggs
1/4 cup milk
Salt and pepper to taste
1 Tbs butter
18 frozen tator tot potatoes
Vegetable oil for frying
1 cup plus 2 tablespoons shredded cheddar cheese
1 packet (2.5 ounce) country gravy mix
Water and/or milk as needed to prepare gravy according to package directions

Directions:
Fry the tator tot potatoes in 1/2 inch of oil at 375 degrees for 3 minutes, or until golden brown.  Remove to paper towel lined plate to drain.
Lightly beat the eggs with 1/4 cup of milk.  Add salt and pepper to taste.
Melt butter in a large skillet.  Scramble cook the eggs in the butter until fluffy and cooked through.
While eggs are cooking, prepare gravy mix according to package directions.
Warm the tortillas in the microwave on high power for 30-45 seconds.
Assemble the burritos:  Divide the eggs between the tortillas.  Top each one with three tator tots and approximately 3 Tbs of shredded cheese.  Divide the country gravy between the burritos.  Fold one side of the tortilla over the filling, then fold the bottom up and fold the other side over.

Submitter: 
Keri
Description: 
These are my version of a delicious breakfast burrito that used to be served at a local restaurant.  The restaurant discontinued the item, so I re-created it at home.
