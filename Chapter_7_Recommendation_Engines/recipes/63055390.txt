Title:
PF Changs  Knock Off - Asian Stir Fry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T hoisin sauce
1 T soy sauce
1 T sherry
2 T oyster sauce
2 T water
1 tsp sesame oil
1 tsp sugar
2 tsp cornstarch

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and store in the refrigerator.
Use minced garlic and ginger in your stir fry vegetables or add about 2 cloves minced garlic and 1/2" of minced fresh ginger, to the sauce.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
A knock off recipe of PF Changs stir fry sauce.  Very easy to mix up and have on hand in the refrigerator.  It can also be used as a marinade.  Make extra!
