Title:
Belzile's Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs lean ground beef
2 onions (chopped)
3 garlic cloves (chopped)
2 cans stewed tomatoes
2 cans tomato sauce
2 cans tomato paste
2 cans tomato soup
1 large can tomato juice
1/4 cup ketchup
2 tbsp HP sauce
2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
2 tbsp sugar
1 tbsp salt
1 tbsp pepper
2 tbsp oregano
1 tbsp cayenne pepper
4 bay leaves
1 green pepper (chopped)
1 tray sliced mushrooms

Directions:
1. In large pot brown ground beef, onions & garlic
2. Add remaining ingredients (tomato cans) except half a tomato juice can (assess for thickness)
3. Simmer for 3-4 hours, stir every 30 minutes
