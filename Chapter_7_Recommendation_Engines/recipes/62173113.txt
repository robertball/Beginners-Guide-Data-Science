Title:
Bar Cookies from Cake Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/3 cup water + 1 TBLSP.
2 egg yolks
1 (18.25 ounce) package cake mix
1 cup chopped pecans
1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
following ingredients Optional: can be in place of above or along with.
1/2 cup coconut
1 cup peanut butter chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Mix in water and egg, then stir in cake mix. Finally, mix in nuts and chocolate chips. Spread mixture into pan.
Bake for 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven. Cool completely before cutting into bars.
NOTE: Bars will be thick to spread.
