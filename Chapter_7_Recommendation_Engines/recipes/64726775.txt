Title:
Almond Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp almond extract
3 1/2 cup sifted powdered sugar
3 Tbsp milk or light cream
2-3 drops food coloring (optional)

Directions:
Beat together butter, vanilla , and almond extract until smooth. Add, alternating,  powdered sugar with milk or light cream . Then add food coloring!
