Title:
Eggplant Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 eggplant (1/2 inch slices), peel removed
1 huge, ugly, flavorful tomato, slices
fresh basil
fresh thyme
fresh parsley
thin sliced mozzarella
chopped canadian bacon

Directions:
Preheat broiler.
Place eggplant sliced on cookie sheet and broil 5-8 minutes.
Add Canadian bacon, spices, tomato, and cheese.
Bake 5-8 minutes

This is a fun and healthy pizza.
