Title:
Chinese salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 oz.) bag Cole slaw
1 (4 oz.) bag shredded carrots
2 pks. Ramon Chicken soup, crushed
1 C. sunflower seeds
3/4 C. almonds
Dressing
3/4 C. olive oil
1/2 C. vinegar
1/2 C. sugar
1 or 2 dressing mix packets from Ramon Chicken soup package

Directions:
Mix all the salad ingredients in a bowl.. DO NOT pour dressing over salad until 1 hour prior to serving
