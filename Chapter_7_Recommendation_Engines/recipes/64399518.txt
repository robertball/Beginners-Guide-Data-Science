Title:
BBQ Dry Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cups white sugar
1/3 cups brown sugar
1 Tbs salt
1 Tbs freshly ground black pepper
1 Tbs smoked paprika
1 Tbs onion powder
1 Tbs garlic powder

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together all the ingredients. Allow the meat to come to room temperature and pat dry with a paper towel. Rub onto pork 10 minutes prior to grilling. Store any leftover rub in a sealed container.
