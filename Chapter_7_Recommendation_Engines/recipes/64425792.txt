Title:
Vegan Unstuffed Cabbage Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cabbage, sliced into strips
1 onion, chopped
1/2 head garlic, diced
2 tbsp chia seeds
2 cans diced tomatoes
1 can tomato paste
1 can chick peas, drained and rinsed
1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
2 cups uncooked quinoa
2 tbsp smoked paprika
1 tsp basil
1 tsp oregano
1 tbsp black pepper
1/2 cup water

Directions:
Add all ingredients into a large slow cooker.
Cook on low for 8 hours.

Submitter: 
Adam Venn
Description: 
An easy, vegan version of a staple in most households. With the combination of traditional favors and textures, everyone - even meat-eaters - will love it!
