Title:
Chicken Fried Steak ( hamburger meat) style and White Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6708762.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs. hamburger meat, NOT Rolled or Frozen Patties
1/2 cup all purpose flour, divided
1/2 cup self-rising flour
1 Tablespoon Meat Tenderizer
1 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
1 Teaspoon Salt
1 Teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 eggs
1/3 cup milk
Vegetable oil
Gravy
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 1/2 to 2 cups whole milk
1 teaspoon ground pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
1. Get your Frozen Hamburger patties out of the freezer
2. Place 1/2 cup all purpose  flour, 1/2 cup self rising flour, 1/2 cup all purpose flour, divided
1/2 cup self-rising flour, 1 Tablespoon Meat Tenderizer , 1 Teaspoon Garlic Powder , 1
Teaspoon Salt, 1 Teaspoon pepper, 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper  in a Gal plastic baggie.
3. Whisk together eggs and 1/3 cup milk in a  shallow bowl.
4. Dredge frozen meat in flour, dip in egg mixture.
5. Heat about 1/2-inch oil in a large pan  over medium heat.
6. Turn to low heat and Cook meat for 25 minuets Without turning, Turn patties over and cook
another 25 on low heat. Now turn heat to Med- high and cook until medium brown and crisp
on both sides, turning once.
7. Remove steak to a paper towel-lined plate.
8. using oil left in pan, Measure out 1/4  cup flour, 1 teaspoon ground pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt, Add flour to pan and cook over medium heat for 1 minute. Gradually whisk
in milk and cook until thick.
