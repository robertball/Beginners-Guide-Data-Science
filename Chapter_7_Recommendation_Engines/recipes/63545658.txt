Title:
Peasant Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3c water
1c quick-cooking barley
5 md carrots, peeled & sliced
5 sm/md cloves garlic, peeled & sliced
2 scallions, minced
1T pignoli
1 16oz can cannellini beans, drained
10oz frozen brussels sprouts
4 frozen or blanched leaves collard greens, chopped
salt
dill weed
marjoram
thyme
celery salt
savory
carrot greens (dried)
rosemary (conservatively)

Directions:
Combine carrots, beans, garlic, scallions, and 1c water in a pot.  Cover and bring to a boil.
Then add the barley and the rest of the water.  Add all seasonings to taste.  Cover and bring back to a boil.
Once the barley is cooked, bring down to a simmer and add pignoli, brussels sprouts, and collards.  Re-season, to taste.
Let simmer for at least 5-10 minutes, or until the brussels sprouts are hot.  You can let it continue to simmer to let the flavors meld, adding more water if desired or needed, though the consistency should be thick.

A surprisingly delicious stew made with simple winter vegetables and seasoned with a half-dozen herbs.
