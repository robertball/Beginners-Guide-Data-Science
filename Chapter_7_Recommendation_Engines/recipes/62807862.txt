Title:
Zucchini with Cashews Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 julienned zucchini
1 teaspoon olive oil
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
Dash pepper
1 small bag toasted, chopped cashews

Directions:
In a skillet, saute zucchini in oil for 5 minutes or until crisp-tender. Sprinkle with garlic powder, pepper and cashews. Serve immediately.
