Title:
Mango-Curry Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup(s) mango nectar
1 tsp honey
1 tsp dijon mustard
1/2 tsp curry powder*
1 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
1 tsp grapeseed or canola oil
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp sea salt
*There are many kinds of curry powder - I used Maharajah, which has a wonderful balance of flavors.

Directions:
Whisk or shake all ingredients together.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
I had to sub some ingredients for a Weight Watchers recipe - Chicken, Mango and Spinach Salad - and ended up with an awesome new dressing.  Sweetness from the mango, exotic spice from the curry powder and tanginess from the vinegar.  Stands up to spinch very well!
