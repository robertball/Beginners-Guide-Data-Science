Title:
Cheesy Baked Hash Brown Potatoes with Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 ounce container sour cream
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
Cooked bacon (about 8-10 pieces)
Package Ranch Dip Mix
Frozen has brown potatoes (or you can thinly slice russet potatoes)

Directions:
Combine first four ingredients. Mix in has browns or if using russet potatoes then arrange them overlapping in pan and pour mixture over). Spread in baking dish and bake at 375 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes
