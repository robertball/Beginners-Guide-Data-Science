Title:
Creamy Basil Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744742.jpg
Ingredients:
4 garlic cloves, unpeeled/ 1/2 tsp garlic powder
3 cups fresh basic (stems/buds discarded)
1 oz Parmesan, grated (approx 1/2 cup)
1/4 cup part-skim ricotta
1 shallot, minces (approx 2 TBS)
2 TBS olive oil
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Toast garlic in skillet in medium-high heat (shaking pan occasionally) until color deepens slightly (about 7 minutes). Transfer to plate to cool then peel and chop
Place basil in gallon bad, pound with meat mallet until bruised
Process garlic and basil, Parmesan, ricotta, shallot, oil, and 1/2 tsp salt in food processor until smooth (30 seconds). Scrape bowl as necessary.
Transfer to bowl, season with salt and pepper to taste; toss with pasta.
