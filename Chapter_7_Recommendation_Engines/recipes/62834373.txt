Title:
Strawberry-Lemon Persecco Granitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pints cleaned and stemmed strawberries
2 tablespoons honey
1 (14 oz) pint of lemon sorbet
1 bottle of persecco

Directions:
1) Place strawberries and honey in a food processor and pulse until berries are crushed.
2) Add lemon sorbet and pulse.
3) transfer to a shallow baking dish (pyrex) and add persecco
4) place in freezer and every 30 min. (for 2 hours), take a fork and mix/shave ice crystals.
5) let freeze over night until ready to scoop out into glasses.
