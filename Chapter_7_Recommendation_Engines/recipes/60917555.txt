Title:
Frito Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz. Fritos - slightly crushed, divided
1 c. peanuts
1 c. light corn syrup
1 c. sugar
1 c. peanut butter
1/2 c. (or more) chocolate chips, melted

Directions:
Grease 9 x 13. place 1/2 Fritos and 1/2 peanuts in pan.  In a saucepan, bring corn syrup and sugar to a boil.  Add peanut butter till blended. Drizzle half over stuff in 9x13.
Add remaining Fritos and peanuts to syrup mixture and mix.
Spoon over mixture in pan and press down.  Drizzle with melted chocolate.
