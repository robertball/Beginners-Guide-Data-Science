Title:
Gluten Free Delicious Soft Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
3/4 cup white sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs or egg replacer
1 teaspoon GF vanilla extract
1/2 C. corn starch
1/2 C. sorghum flour
1/2 C. sweet rice flour
1/2 C. brown rice flour
1/2 C. almond meal
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
3 cups thick cut rolled oats

Directions:
In a medium bowl, cream together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla. Combine flours, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon; stir into the creamed mixture. Mix in oats. Drop dough (into about a 2 inch ball if you rolled it)onto a parchment lined cookie sheet.
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease cookie sheets.
Bake for 10 minutes in preheated oven. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
Pour a big glass of milk and enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
These oatmeal cookies are very moist with a good flavor. Add a cup of raisins, chocolate chips, or cinnamon chips if you desire.Modified from: Soft Oatmeal Cookies
