Title:
Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup shortening
2 eggs
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 cup sugar
1 tbsp vanilla
3/4 cup flour
1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
splash of espresso

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Spray 8x8 pan and dust with cocoa powder.
Melt shortening and butter. Beat in eggs one at a time. Mix in espresso, vanilla and salt. Stir in flour and cocoa.
Spread into pan.
