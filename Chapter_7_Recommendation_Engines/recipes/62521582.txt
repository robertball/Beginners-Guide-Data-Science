Title:
Fajita Steak and Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 steaks or chicken breasts
marinade:
1 pkg taco or fajita seasoning mix
1 bottle italian dressing
1 can sprite
water to cover

Directions:
mix marinade, cover meat and marinade in fridge overnight
drain
grill, broil, or bake meat until desire doneness
slice
serve with tortillas, cheese, sour cream, lettuce, tomatoes, sauteed onions and peppers
