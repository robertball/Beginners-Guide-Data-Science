Title:
Single Cup Hot Buttered Rum Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1/2 tablespoon powdered sugar
1 shot of good dark Jamaician rum
2 tablespoons Creme Brulee ice cream or French Vanilla ice cream
Dash of cinnamon
Simmering water
Dash of nutmeg

Directions:
In a large coffee mug combine butter and sugars and smash it together with a fork. Add rum and add ice cream. Add a dash of cinnamon. Top the mug with simmering water. Mix all together with a fork until butter and ice cream have melted and it's frothy on the top. Add a dash of nutmeg and enjoy!

Submitter: 
witchywoman
Description: 
All I could find were recipes for a quart of hot buttered rum mix...I don't have the room to store that much so I fiddled around with the measurements to do a single cup and presto!
