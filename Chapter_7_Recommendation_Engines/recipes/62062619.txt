Title:
Vanilla Flan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C Evaporated Milk
5 Egg yolks
1 Egg white
3/4 C white sugar
1/4 Tsp. Vanilla extract
For caramel crust:
1 C white sugar

Directions:
1. In a saucepan, melt white sugar until brown and completely dissolved.
2. Pour the melted sugar in an 8-inch round pan (or any preferred mold) and spread evenly. Set aside.
3. In a large mixing bowl, mix together all ingredients until sugar is dissolved.
4. Pour inside pan and cook inside a large steamer or double boiler for 30 minutes or until firm to the touch.
5. Remove from steamer then let cool for a few minutes.
6. Put decorative plate larger than the pan over it and invert the flan.
7. The caramelized sugar should now be a delicious syrup to eat with the flan.

Submitter: 
Janis P.
Description: 
This is the usual dessert my family and I ate ever since I was a kid and we never get sick of it! It's very light and the texture is smooth.  Plus, you can substitute orange or lemond rind for the vanilla to give a different flavor.
