Title:
5 Minute Microwave Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box confectioners sugar
1/2 jar chunky peanut butter
1/2 stick butter
1/4 cup milk
1 tsp. vanilla
1 bag chocolate chips

Directions:
Place wax paper in the bottom of a 8X8 dish.
In a 3 quart microwavable bowl, combine the sugar, butter and milk.
Stir to combine and microwave on high for 1 minute.
Remove, stir in the peanut butter and cook for 1 minute.
Remove and stir, cook for 1 minute.
Add the vanilla, stir and cook 1 minute.
Remove, add chocolate chips, and stir till completely disolved.
Pour mixture into the prepared dish, and chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
Quick and easy, one bowl microwavable fudge. So delicious you won't be able to keep this one to yourself.
