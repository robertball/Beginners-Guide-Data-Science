Title:
Sausage Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of sausage
3-4 chicken bouillon cubes
2 chopped green onions
1/3 cup flour
2 cups water
1-2 cups of milk
Cooked, sliced potatoes
sliced swiss cheese

Directions:
Brown sausage with bouillon cubes and green onions in a large skillet.  Mix flour with water and pour over sausage. Simmer for about 5 minutes or until thick. Add milk and simmer briefly. Pour over cooked sliced potatoes. Pour into casserole dish and place sliced swiss or any other white cheese on top.  Cover and bake in microwave or 375 degree oven until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
ACHOCOCAT
Description: 
A hearty, cheese-topped, potato and sausage casserole with green onions.  Very tasty!
