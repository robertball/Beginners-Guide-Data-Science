Title:
Apple Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups + 4 tablespoons cake flour
2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup applesauce
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
4 eggs
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 tablespoon warm water
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 teaspoon apple liqueur/extract
4 medium granny smith apples - peeled, shredded
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Whisk together flour, spices, and salt. Set aside.
Cream together butter, applesauce and sugars.  Mix in eggs, apple liqueur/extract and vanilla extract.  Stir together baking soda and 1 tablespoon warm water, and mix into the sugar mixture. Stir in flour mixture and apples until well blended.  Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake for approximately 1 hour, or until a tester comes out clean.  Cool in pan.  Once cool, shake pan to loosen cake.  Turn onto plate, and dust with confectioners' sugar.
