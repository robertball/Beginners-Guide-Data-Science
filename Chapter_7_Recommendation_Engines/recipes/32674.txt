Title:
Italian Stallion Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1658852.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 ounce) envelope instant cocoa
1 cup boiling water
1 fluid ounce hazelnut liqueur
1 fluid ounce coffee flavored liqueur
1 fluid ounce brandy

Directions:
In a 12 ounce coffee mug, stir together instant cocoa and boiling water. Stir in hazelnut liqueur, coffee liqueur and brandy.
