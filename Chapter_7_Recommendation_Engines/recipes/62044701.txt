Title:
Southern Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. Pasta (any style)
1/2 cup diced Hot -or- Mild Banana Pepper
1 package Vegetable Soup Base Mix (Knorr's used for this recipe)
2 cups cubed Ham
1 1/2 cups Mayonaise
1/2 cup Bacon Bits
1 packet of Italian Dressing Powder Mix (can be found in the dressing aisle of most grocery stores)
1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
Black Pepper to taste

Directions:
In a saucepan bring to a rolling boil: 4 quarts water. Combine: Pasta and Vegetable Soup Base Mix. Reduce heat by half and allow to boil 15-20 mins -or- to desired tenderness.  In a larger serving bowl mix Mayonnaise, Italian Dressing Mix, Bacon Bits and Pepper. Stir until well mixed. Add diced Banana Peppers and cubed Ham. When pasta is cooked remove from heat and drain. Immediately place under cold running water to stop cooking process and cool the mixture. Drain. Place into serving bowl and stir to coat all ingredients well. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Place covered in the refrigerator to remain cold until served. Stir one last time right before serving. Upon standing salad may get dry. 1-Tbs. milk may be added to return salad to a creamy consistency. This recipe can be altered in a variety of ways for any occasion. Experiment with different vegetables, meats or toppings.
