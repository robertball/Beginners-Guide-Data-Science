Title:
Jan's, 5 gallon pail of Laundry Detergant Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3- 3lb 7 oz boxes of Super Washing Soda
3- 4 lb 12 oz boxes of Borax
4- 14 oz bars of Zote Soap (or Fells Naptha)
3- 1.3 lb boxs of Oxy-Clean
2- 28 oz. bottles Purex Crystals (or Downey Unstoppables)

Directions:
Shred the bars of Zote using a hand greater or cut into small chunks and run through a food processor with the blade attachment.
Mix all ingredients together.
Use 2 - 3 tablespoons per load.

Submitter: 
What's for dinner, mom?
Description: 
Great cleaning power and fresh scent that wont break the bank!  Safe for HE washers.  This is a big batch that will lasts for several months.
