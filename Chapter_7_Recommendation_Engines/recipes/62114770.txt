Title:
Pecan Chicken with Honey Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Marinade:
1/2 cup olive oil
2 tsp fresh thyme, minced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
Honey Cream Sauce
2 Tbsp butter
1 Tbsp shallots, minced
1 cup orange juice
1/4 cup whiskey
1 cup whipping cream
2 Tbsp honey
1 Tbsp cider vinegar
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
Coating:
1/2 cup pecans, chopped
1/4 cup flour

Directions:
Mix marinade ingredients. Place chicken and marinade in Freezer bag.
Sauce: Melt butter in medium saucepan over low heat. Saute shallots in butter until tender. Add orange juice and whiskey over medium heat, and continue cooking until liquid is reduced by one half. Add cream. Continue cooking until slightly thickened. Add honey, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Cool. Freeze separately using freezer bag.
Place chopped pecans and flour in freezer bag together.
Serving Day:
Thaw. Dredge chicken breasts in flour mixture. Heat a small amount of olive oil in large skillet. Pan fry chicken breasts until golden brown and cooked through. Heat sauce over low until warm; do not boil. To serve, place chicken on individual serving dishes and pour cream sauce over chicken breasts.
