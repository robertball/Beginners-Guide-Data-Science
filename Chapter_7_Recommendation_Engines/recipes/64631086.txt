Title:
Low Carb Keto Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4477301.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup melted butter
1/4 cup coconut oil “liquefied”
3 table spoons grapeseed oil
4 large eggs
4 egg whites
2 teaspoons baking powder
2/3 cups of almond flour
1/3 cup coconut flour
½ teaspoon xanthan gum
½ teaspoon fine salt

Directions:
1. Add all your dry ingredients to a large bowl and mix well.
2. Break whole eggs and egg whites together into a food processor and pulse well until well beaten.
3 . Add all dry ingredients into eggs into the processor ; along with coconut oil (liquefied) , melted butter, and grape seed oil
4. Pulse or mix until smooth.
5. Spread bread batter into a pre-greased 8X4 “ bread loaf pan. I personally use organic olive oil spray
6. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes or until loaf is brown; let cool inside bread pan then transfer onto cooling rack . It should pop out nice and easy.

Submitter: 
Girly Hobbies
Description: 
Only 2 net carbs a slice, this recipe is lower in cholesterol than most low carb breads; using only 4 whole eggs. It's keto, paleo,  wheat free, gluten free, celiac, and diabetes friendly. The best part is that it's less eggy than most low carb breads.
