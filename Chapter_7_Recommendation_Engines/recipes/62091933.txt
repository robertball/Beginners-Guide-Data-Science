Title:
Round steak and gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup flour
2 tsp. paprika
2 tsp.onion powder
3/4 tsp. garlic powder
2 pounds beef round steak (cut into serving size pieces)
3 TB oil
1/2 cup diced onion
1 cup water
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1 cup beef broth
1 can (10 3/4 oz) cream of chicken soup (undiluted)

Directions:
Combine first four ingredients in a resealable plastic bag and shake.
Add beef a few pieces at a time and shake to coat.
In a dutch oven over medium high heat, brown steak in oil on both sides until browned.
Remove and keep warm.
In the drippings, saute onion until tender.
Stir in water, broth, soup and pepper.
Bring to a boil.
Add meat and gravy in oven-proof dish.
Cover and bake at 325 degrees for 2 hours or until tender or place in pressure cooker and cook for 50 minutes.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
The meat is so tender and moist and the gravy is so flavorable.  Instead of baking it in the oven, I use my pressure cooker.  This is wonderful with mashed potatoes, rice or noodles.
