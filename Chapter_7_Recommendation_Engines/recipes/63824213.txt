Title:
White Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1060916.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast cooked and diced
1 can mexican corn
1 can navey beans
1 can northern beans
1 can chicken broth
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 1/2 cups sour cream
1/2 jar queso cheese dip
4 tbls taco seasoning, or to taste

Directions:
Cook chicken breast in the chicken broth till done then cut up into bite size pieces, I used an electric knife.
Place all the ingrendients (including the broth from the cooked chicken) in a large pot or crock pot and let simmer for an hour while stirring occationaly.

Submitter: 
Nichole
Description: 
I searched online looking for a thicker white chili recipe and couldn't find one I liked so I created this one! It is delicious!
