Title:
Curry Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6364596.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup red lentils
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion flakes
1 tsp curry powder
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp turmeric
1/2 tsp cumin
1 boullion cube
Add on :
1 -2tsp olive oil
2 chopped carrots
1 head of cauliflower chopped into small florets
1 bunch of kale leaves (remove veins and stems) rip into smaller pieces
2 cups of boiling water to add to boullion cube
1 can half fat coconut milk

Directions:
1.  In a large pot, heat olive oil over med-high heat. Add carrots and cauliflower , saut√© for 7-8 minutes until veggies are softened.
In a bowl, Add 2 cups boiling water to bouillon cube f and stir until dissolved. Add broth and coconut milk to veggies bringing to a boil.
2.  Add lentils and dry ingredients and cover and simmer for 20 minutes until lentils are fully cooked.  stir in kale and let sit for 5 mins on low heat.  Remove from heat, Serve and enjoy!
