Title:
M's Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2354095.jpg
Ingredients:
4	avocados
½	cup diced red onion
1	medium tomato, seeded and diced
Juice of 1 lime
1/3	cup chopped fresh cilantro
1/2  teaspoon garlic powder
1	jalapeno, seeded and diced (optional)
¼	to ½ cup sour cream
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut avocados in half and remove pits.  Slice avocado in skins into small cubes.  Spoon cubes into bowl.  Add the lime juice and toss to coat.  Mash just a bit of the avocado with a fork.  Add the remaining ingredients, and mix well.
