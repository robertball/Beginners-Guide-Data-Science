Title:
Garlic Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1299684.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons unsalted butter
1 tablespoon olive oil
4 teaspoons finely minced garlic
2 pounds extra-large (21 to 25 count per pound)shrimp, peeled and deveined
4 scallions, both white and light green parts, sliced
Big pinch of red pepper flakes (optional)
1/4 cup dry white wine
1 cup chicken broth
3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice (from about 2 lemons)
2 tablespoons minced fresh Italian parsley
Kosher salt
Freshly Ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Heat the butter and olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.  Add the garlic and cook until softened (do not let it brown), about 2 minutes.
Increase the heat to medium-high, add the shrimp, scallions and red pepper flakes, if using and cook,, stirring frequently, until the shrimp just turn pink, about 4 minutes.
Add the white wine and cook until the wine is almost evaporated 1 to 2 minutes.  Add the chicken broth and let come to a simmer.  Ad the lemon juice and parsley, and season the shrimp with salt and pepper to taste.  Toss well and you're done; serve it up!
