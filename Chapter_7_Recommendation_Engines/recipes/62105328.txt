Title:
Chicken & Bean Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion chopped
1 small red pepper chopped
3 small tomatoes chopped (or substitute 2 cans of chopped tomatoes)
1 small bag frozen corn
1 can black eyed peas (canned is best)
16 oz bag lentil beans (sorted and clean)
1 can green diced chilis
1 32 oz box chicken broth
1 cube chicken boullion
1 packet Taco seasoning
2-4 cups of water (to taste)
4-5 corn tortilla cut into small strips
grilled chicken breasts chopped very small
Shredded cheddar cheese for topping
Corn tortilla chips for topping
Cilantro for garnish (optional)
Lemon

Directions:
In large soup pot use 1 tbs oil & brown the strips of corn tortilla until crispy.
Set aside on plate lined with paper towels.
Add 1 ½ tbs canola or olive oil to same soup pot and sauté onions until translucent.
Add chopped tomatoes & peppers and sauté.
Pour in water and broth and bring to a boil.
Add frozen corn.
Add black-eyed peas and lentils
Add bullion and taco seasoning (3/4 to full package to taste)
Add the browned tortilla strips.
Cook on med/low for about 1 hour until lentils and peas are tender.
Add grilled chicken the last 15 minutes of cooking.
Serve in bowls with crushed tortilla chips and shredded cheese on top.
Garnish with Cilantro and a quick squeeze of fresh lemon juice.
