Title:
Easy Whole Wheat Pancakeks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
3/4 cup whole wheat
1/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
1/2 cube butter, or 1/4 cup oil
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Blend milk and wheat in blender for 4 minutes on high speed.
Add rest of ingredients and blend at high speed for 3 minutes.  Pour on hot griddle.
