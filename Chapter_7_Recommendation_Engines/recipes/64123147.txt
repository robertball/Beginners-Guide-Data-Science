Title:
Onion Panade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf French or Italian bread, cut into 1/2 inch slices
2 C. coarsely grated Gruyere cheese or a mixture of Swiss and Parmesan
1 envelope onion soup mix prepared as instructed

Directions:
Heat oven to 425. Arrange bread slices on 2 baking sheets and bake 10 minutes, turning once, until slices are dry and golden. Turn oven down to 350. Butter a 2 qt.casserole dish. Arrange half the bread slices on the bottom of casserole dish. Sprinkle with half the cheese and top with remaining bread slices. Pour soup over bread and sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake 25-30 minutes, until bread has absorbed most of the soup and cheese is golden.
