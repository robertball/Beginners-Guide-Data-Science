Title:
Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/718324.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 c. finely chopped onion
3 T. butter, melted
1 t. salt
2 T. flour
1/3 c. water
1 Â– 8oz jar cheese whiz
2 eggs, beaten
2- 10oz packages of chopped broccoli, thawed and drained
1 c. saltines, crushed
6 T. butter, melted

Directions:
In a bowl, mix the broccoli and onion.
Melt the butter in a small saucepan. Add the flour and salt, mix to make a roux. Add water until it thickens.
Add cheese whiz, making a thick mixture. Set aside.
Add eggs to broccoli and onion. Add cheese sauce. Mix.
Put into a greased casserole dish.
Mix saltines and melted butter together and cover top of casserole.
Bake at 350 for 45 minutes. Cover for first 30 minutes. After that, uncover.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
Delicious mixture of cheese, broccoli, and crunchy saltine crackers make a tasty treat.
