Title:
Spicy Bacon and Cheese Devilled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
2 slices bacon
1/4 cup cheddar
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 1/2 tablespoons sour cream
1 teaspoon cayenne

Directions:
Boil eggs to preferred doneness.  Peel them, slice them in half and scoop out the yolks into a bowl.  Arrange egg whites on a plate and set aside.
Fry bacon til crispy.  Drain fat.  Crumble 1 1/2 slices into bowl with the yolks and mix them together.  Crumble remaining bacon and set it aside for the topping.
Mix cheese, salt and sour cream into yolk mixture. When well mixed, scoop spoonfuls into egg white halves.  Sprinkle with cayenne pepper to your taste.  Top with remaining bacon.  This recipe can be stored in the fridge or served right away.

Submitter: 
linda
Description: 
Traditional devilled eggs are spiced up with some cayenne pepper, bacon, cheddar and sour cream.
