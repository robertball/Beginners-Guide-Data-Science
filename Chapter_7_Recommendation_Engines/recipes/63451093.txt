Title:
Overnight Blueberry French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 slices day-old bread, cut into 1-inch cubes
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, cut into 1 inch cubes
** cinnamon
**1/4 C powdered suger
**tsp. vanilla extract
1 cup fresh blueberries
12 eggs, beaten
2 cups milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/3 cup maple syrup
1 cup white sugar
2 tablespoons cornstarch
1 cup water
**splash of lemon juice
1 cup fresh blueberries
1 tablespoon butter

Directions:
Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange half the bread cubes in the dish, sprinkle with cinnamon and top with cream cheese blended with powdered sugar and vanilla (thin with milk to make spreading easier), add fruite and top with remaining bread cubes.
In a large bowl, mix the eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and syrup. Pour over the bread cubes. Cover, and refrigerate overnight.
Remove the bread cube mixture from the refrigerator about 30 minutes before baking. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Cover, and bake 30 minutes. Uncover, and continue baking 25 to 30 minutes, until center is firm and surface is lightly browned.
In a medium saucepan, mix the sugar, cornstarch, and water. Bring to a boil. Stirring constantly, cook 3 to 4 minutes. Mix in the remaining 1 cup blueberries and lemon juice. Reduce heat, and simmer 10 minutes, until the blueberries burst. Stir in the butter, and pour over the baked French toast

This is a very unique breakfast dish. Good for any holiday breakfast or brunch, it's filled with the fresh taste of blueberries, and covered with a rich blueberry sauce to make it a one of a kind.Modified from: Overnight Blueberry French Toast
