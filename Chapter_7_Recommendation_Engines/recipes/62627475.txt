Title:
Yummy chicken pot pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans cream of chicken
2 cans cream of celeri
3 cans cream of mushroom
1 tsp sage
3 tbsp parmesean & Herbs (club house)
2 tbsp parsley
2 tsp pepper
2-3 cups chopped chicken
4 cloves garlic
1/2 cup parmesean
3 tbsp chicken bouillon concentrate with 1/2 cup water

Directions:
Mix soups, sage, parsley, pepper together in mixing bowl. cook chicken, garlic, parmesean, chicken bouillon concentrate until most water is gone. Mix all of it in together and pour into pie shells. Cook at 375F for 45 minutes. Makes 3 pies
