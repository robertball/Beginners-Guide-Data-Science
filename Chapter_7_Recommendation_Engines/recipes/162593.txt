Title:
Mixed Greens and Pear Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982804.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup PLANTERS Pecan Halves
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon sugar
5 cups torn mixed salad greens
1 large pear, thinly sliced
1/2 cup KRAFT Natural 2% Milk Reduced Fat Colby & Monterey Jack Cheese Crumbles
1/4 cup dried cranberries
1/3 cup KRAFT Light Zesty Italian Reduced Fat Dressing

Directions:
Place pecans in microwaveable pie plate sprayed with cooking spray. Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar; toss to coat. Spread evenly onto bottom of pie plate. Microwave on HIGH 1 to 1-1/2 min. or until toasted, stirring every 30 sec.
Meanwhile, toss greens with pears, cheese and cranberries in large bowl.
Add pecans and dressing just before serving; mix lightly.

Submitter: 
KRAFT Salad Dressing
Description: 
Sweet pecans and cranberries add to the color and textures of this mixed-greens salad tossed with sliced pears and the kick of KRAFT Light Zesty Italian dressing.
