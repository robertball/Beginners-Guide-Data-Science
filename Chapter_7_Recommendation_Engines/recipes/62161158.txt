Title:
Mixed Berry Yogurt Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c flour
1/3 c sugar
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1 egg
1/4 c orange juice
1 tsp vanilla extract
8 oz vanilla yogurt
1 cup mixed berries

Directions:
Combine all dry ingredients. Add egg and remaining wet ingredients, all except berries. Mix until all dry ingredients are moist. Add in berries. Pour batter in a lightly greased muffin tin, sprinkle sugar on tops of muffins. Bake 400 degrees for 20 mins.

Submitter: 
CortyPie
Description: 
Quick and easy homemade muffins that stay really moist for days! They are always a hit for breakfast.
