Title:
Jimmy Dean Bacon Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 (2.2 ounce) packages Jimmy Dean Fully Cooked Premium Precooked Bacon, heated according to package directions and finely chopped
1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and finely chopped
1/2 cup thinly sliced green onions
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup sour cream
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
salt and pepper to taste
crackers or pita crisps

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine first six ingredients, reserving 1/3 cup bacon for garnish. Stir well; season to taste with salt and pepper. Prepare and serve one of two ways (hot or cold): A) Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Transfer ingredients to a lightly greased 2-quart casserole and bake uncovered 25 minutes, or until lightly browned, -or- B) Cover and chill for at least 2 hours or overnight. Garnish with remaining bacon. Serve with crackers or pita crisps.

Submitter: 
Jimmy Dean
Description: 
Perfect for a party, the addition of chopped bacon makes this artichoke dip a true crowd-pleaser.
