Title:
Stuffed French Toast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Casserole Layers
18 slices of raisin bread
16 oz. low fat cream cheese (room temperature)
1/4 cup Splenda sugar substitute
1/2 cup egg substitute
1 tsp vanilla extract
Topping
Remaining contents of Egg substitute carton
3 cups milk
2 tsp vanilla extract
Dash of cinnamon
Dash of nutmeg

Directions:
Combine cream cheese, egg substitute, Splenda, and vanilla extract from "Casserole Layer" portion of recipe in mixer.
Place 6 pieces of bread in 9x12 baking dish.
Spread 1/3 cheese mixture over bread.
Repeat for 2 more layers.
Combine topping portion of recipe and pour over prepared casserole.
Bake in convection oven at 375 degrees for 40 minutes on center rack.
Reduce heat to 365 degrees and bake for an additional 10 minutes.
