Title:
Ambers nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/440689.jpg
Ingredients:
*1/2 bag tortilla chips
*1 bag shredded sharp cheddar cheese
*2 slices velveeta cheese
*4 tbsp. salsa
*6 chopped seeded black olives
*5 slices jalenos

Directions:
cover a cookie sheet with chips
cover the chips in shredded cheese
rip up pieces of velveeta and lay them in sepperated place
lay olives all over the chips evenly
evenly drip salsa on chips
put jalepenos in different places on the chips.
bake on 350 for 10 min.
