Title:
Pumpkin Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 scoop vanilla ice cream
1 to 2 Tbsp canned pumpkin
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 C milk

Directions:
Combine in a blender until smooth. Sprinkle top with cinnamon to serve.
