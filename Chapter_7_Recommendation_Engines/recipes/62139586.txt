Title:
Terry's True Texas Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil
1 large onion - chopped
4 cloves garlic - minced
5 anaheim chiles - chopped
4 jalepenos - seeded and chopped
1 bell pepper - chopped
1 pkg ground turkey breast
1/2 lb mild italian sausage - casing removed
1 1/2 lb fresh plum tomatoes, roasted
1 beer
2 tbsp cumin
2 tbsp chili powder
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp sea salt

Directions:
Roast tomatoes in 425 degree oven for 20 minutes
chop onions and peppers, saute in olive oil
brown ground turkey and sausage in olive oil
Add all of above in large dutch oven, including beer and spices and simmer 1 to 2 hours.
