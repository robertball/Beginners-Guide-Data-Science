Title:
Braised Asian Kale Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups low salt chicken broth
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon minced lemongrass
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1 tablespoon soy sauce
6 cups tightly packed kale leaves, rinsed and stemmed
1 - 2 tablespoons rice vinegar

Directions:
In your slow cooker, combine chicken broth, garlic, lemongrass, soy sauce, and ginger. Heat on high for about 30 minutes to combine flavors.
Add kale and stir to coat with the broth mixture.
Reduce heat to low and cook for approximately 2 hours. If desired, finish with a splash of rice vinegar before serving.

Submitter: 
SJRJA
Description: 
What can be healthier than kale - I combined my favorite Asian seasonings to give it a different and delicious twist! I make this in the slow cooker, but you could easily do it on the stove top, as well.
