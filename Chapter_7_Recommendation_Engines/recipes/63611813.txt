Title:
Loaded Mashed Potato Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices bacon, diced
1 small onion, chopped
1 stalk celery, chopped
1/4 tsp., minced garlic
1 1/4 cups mashed potatoes
1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 egg, slightly beaten
1/4 tsp. yellow mustard
1/8 tsp. black pepper
2 dashs hot sauce
vegetable oil

Directions:
Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat until crisp and browned, about 10 minutes. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain the bacon on paper towels, reserving bacon grease in skillet.
In the reserved bacon grease over medium heat; cook and stir onion, celery, and garlic until tender and lightly browned, about 15 minutes.
Transfer vegetables and bacon to large bowl; stir in mashed potatoes, cheese, flour, egg, mustard, pepper, and hot sauce.
Heat vegetable oil in a clean skillet over medium heat. Drop spoonfuls of mashed potato mixture, flattening slightly, and fry patties until golden brown on both sides, about 5 to 7 minutes.
Serve with sour cream.
