Title:
Crispy Fried Chicken Wingss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 chicken wings
1/2 cup flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 can (5.3 ounces) evaporated milk
1/2 cup olive oil
Paprika

Directions:
1. Mix the first 5 ingredients (flour, salt, paprika, garlic powder and pepper) in a plastic bag.
2. Dip the chicken into the evaporated milk.
3. Add a few pieces at a time to the spice mix in the bag and shake to coat.
4. Heat the oil in a large skillet and add the chicken. Brown on all sides over medium-high heat. Cook for 25 min.
5. Sprinkle with paprika.
