Title:
parmesan chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6039959.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup all purpose fl
2 large eggs
1 12 cups panko breadcrumbs
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1tbs mustard powder
salt and pepper
4 small skinless chicken breast
8 tbs olive oil

Directions:
pound chicken breast til flat.
place flour in bowl, beat eggs in another bowl, combine cheese and mustard and panko.
Season chicken with salt and pepper.
dredge in flour then eggs, then panko. (can be frozen at this point up to 3months) brown in oil .
