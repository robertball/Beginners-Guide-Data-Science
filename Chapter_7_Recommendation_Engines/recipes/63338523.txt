Title:
Grilled Chicken Caesar Penne Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
1/4 cup grated Parmesan
2 tbsp. olive oil
1 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1 1/4 tsp. anchovy paste
1 tsp. Dijon mustard
1 clove minced garlic
1 clove garlic - halved
2 romaine hearts - halved
4 oz. bread - cut into slices
1/2 lb. penne pasta
1/4 cup sliced radicchio

Directions:
Whisk first 6 ingredients and minced garlic in a bowl and season to taste with salt and pepper.
Grill chicken, romaine and bread.
Grill bread until toasted.
Grill romaine until well marked, about 3 minutes.
Cook chicken until done.
Rub toasted bread with halved garlic and tear into pieces.
Slice chicken.
Cook pasta.
Drain pasta and toss with all ingredients except bread.
When all ingredients are well-coated, top with toasted bread and serve.
