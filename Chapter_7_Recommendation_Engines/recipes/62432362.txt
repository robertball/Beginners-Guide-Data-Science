Title:
Extremely Easy Chicken Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb uncooked linguine
2 boneless chicken breast halves (cooked and cubed)
1 (4.5 ounce) can sliced mushrooms
1 (16 ounce) jar Alfredo sauce
1 (10 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables
1/3 cup milk

Directions:
Boil linguine.
In a large saucepan, (medium heat) add chicken, mushrooms, Alfredo sauce, frozen vegetables, and milk. Cook until heated through.
Serve warm Alfredo sauce mixture over cooked linguine. Enjoy!
