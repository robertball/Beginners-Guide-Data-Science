Title:
Fourth of July Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2390717.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup blueberries
1 cup sliced strawberries
1 cup chopped watermelon
1 cup red grapes
1 cup shredded coconut

Directions:
Combine blueberries, strawberries, watermelon, and grapes in a bowl; add coconut.

Submitter: 
Maggie
Description: 
This is the best holiday salad, hands-down. Great wholesome healthy taste! Great for the fireworks, picnics, or a family dinner side. Add a hint of love and enjoy!
