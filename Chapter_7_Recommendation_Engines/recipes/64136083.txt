Title:
QUAKR OATS APPLE CRISP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. cooking apples
1 tbsp. lemon juice
1/3 c. flour
1 c. oatmeal, uncooked
1/2 c. brown sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. salt
1/3 c. melted butter

Directions:
Peel apples, slice into 13x9 buttered pan. Sprinkle lemon juice over apples. Combine dry ingredients. Mix in melted butter. Sprinkle over apples.  350 for 30 min.
