Title:
MAPLE-FRIED CORN Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups fresh corn, cut from cobs
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon salt
3 tablespoons reduced calorie maple syrup
3 tablespoons skim milk

Directions:
Coat a large nonstick skillet with cooking spray; place over medium-high heat until hot. Add corn and onion, and saute 5 minutes.
Combine cinnamon and remaining ingredients. Add to corn in skillet, and cook over medium heat 20 minutes, stirring frequently.
