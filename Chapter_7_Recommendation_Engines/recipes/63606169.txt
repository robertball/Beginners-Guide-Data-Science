Title:
Tidwell Tuna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package egg noodles
1 onion diced
1 tsp minced garlic
1 tbs butter for sauteeing
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup shredded  cheese
2 cups frozen green peas and carrots
2 (6 ounce) cans tuna,including the water
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of mushroom soup or cream of chicken or cream of mushroom
2 tsp dill
salt and pepper to taste.
1/2 cup italian bread crumbs
1 cup crushed potato chips

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook pasta in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes, or until al dente; drain.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (220 degrees C).
saute onion in butter add garlic.
add cans of soup.  add 2 cans of water.  mix heat to hot but not boiling.  add the peas and carrots.  remove from heat add the cheese.  mix in the noodles and spices.  Add more garlic, onion powder  as desired
Transfer to a 9x13or large square covered glass baking dish.  and top with bread crumbs and  potato chip crumbs and remaining 1 cup cheese.
Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until cheese is bubbly.
