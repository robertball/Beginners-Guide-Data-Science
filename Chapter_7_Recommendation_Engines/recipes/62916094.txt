Title:
Peanut Butter Swirl Chocolate Brownines Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Nonstick cooking spray
1/4 C butter
3/4 C sugar or sugar substitute blend
1/3 C cold water
3/4 C refrigerated or frozen egg product, thawed or 3 eggs, lightly beaten
1 1/4 C all-purpose flour**
1 t baking powder
1/4 C creamy peanut butter
1 t vanilla
1/4 C canola oil
1/2 C unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 C miniature semisweet chocolate pieces

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Line a 9x9x2-in baking pan with foil, extending foil up over the edges of the pan. Lightly coat foil with nonstick spray. Set aside
In med. saucepan, melt butter over low heat; remove from heat. Whisk in sugar and water. Whisk in egg, oil, and vanilla until combined. Stir in 1 C flour and the baking powder until combined. (Batter will be thin at this point.)Place peanut butter in a sm. bowl; gradually whisk in 1/2 C batter until smooth. Set aside. In another bowl, combine remaining 1/4 C flour and the cocoa powder. Stir into the plain batter; stir in the chocolate pieces. Pour chocolate bater into prepared pan.
Drop peanut butter batter in sm. mounds over choc. batter in pan. Using a thin metal spatula, swirl batters together. Bake for 20-25 min. or until top springs back when lightly touched and a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool completely in pan on a wild rack. Cut into bars.
If using splenda sugar blend for baking, decease baking time 15-18 min

Submitter:

Description: 
Can use a number of flour substitutes-Whole wheat pastry flour, whole wheat flour, gluten free baking mix, corn, oat or white bean flours. Around 140 calories and 13 carbs.
