Title:
Cornflake Drops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c sugar
1 c peanut butter
6 c corn flakes
1 c corn syrup

Directions:
Heat the sugar and syrup until it comes to a boil, and sugar melts completely.
Remove from heat and stir in the peanut butter.
Add the cornflakes, and stir in.
Drop by spoonfuls onto wax paper and let cool for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
Sweet and very simple to make. A real crowd pleaser.
