Title:
Tortilla Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2535332.jpg
Ingredients:
2- Fajita style soft tortilla shells (any variety)
1/2 cup prepared pasta sauce
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella
Season with dried basil, oregano and garlic to taste.
Add ons:
Diced tomatoes, diced onions,  diced peppers, sliced olives, steamed broccoli, et cetera

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Place tortilla on cookie sheet. Top tortilla with half of the pasta sauce, mozzarella and other topping if desired. Sprinkle with dried seasonings.
Repeat with other tortilla.
Bake in oven for 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.
Slice with pizza cutter.
