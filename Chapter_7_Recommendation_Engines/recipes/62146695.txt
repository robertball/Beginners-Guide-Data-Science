Title:
Mock Maple Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup honey (you can substitute brown sugar)
1 1/2 cups cold water
2 tablespoons Corn Starch
1 tsp imitation maple flavoring (also try vanilla, almond extract, or even imitation banana for a fun twist)

Directions:
Add honey to sauce pan on medium heat.
Wisk corn starch in cold water until smooth; add to pan.
Stirring constantly with a wire whisk, bring to a boil while over medium heat and boil 1 minute.
Remove pan from heat and add flavoring, wisking to combine.
Serve hot on pancakes or waffles.  Yumm!

Submitter: 
Namaste Mama
Description: 
Grandma's recipe from the Great Depression is perfect on hot pancakes.  Try slathering your pancakes in peanut butter and pouring this over top for a yummy breakfast treat.
