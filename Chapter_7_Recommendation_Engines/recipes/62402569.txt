Title:
GRANDMA'S FUDGE SAUCE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
4 (1 oz) unsweetened choc. squares
3 cups sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla
1 can (1 2/3 cup) evaporated milk

Directions:
Melt slowly in heavy kettle &  STIR constantly.
Gradually add sugar, salt, vanilla evaporated milk.
Careful not to scorch.
Bring just to a boil.
