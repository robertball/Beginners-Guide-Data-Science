Title:
Big Ray's Slow-Cooker Bacon-Ranch Chicken and Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3608344.jpg
Ingredients:
8  boneless, skinless chicken thighs
6 slices bacon, cooked and diced
2 to 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 package (1 oz) ranch dressing and seasoning mix
1 can (10.75 oz) condensed cream of chicken soup
1 cup sour cream
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 cup water
8 oz pasta, cooked

Directions:
Spray  slow cooker with cooking spray; place chicken thighs in cooker.
In medium bowl, mix remaining ingredients except pasta. Pour over top of chicken.
Cover; cook on Low heat setting 6 hours or on High heat setting 3 to 4 hours.
When about 15 minutes are left, cook and drain pasta as directed on package.
Just before serving, shred chicken with 2 forks, and toss creamy chicken mixture with cooked spaghetti.
