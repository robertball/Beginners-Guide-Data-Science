Title:
Low Fat Spicy Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/909177.jpg
Ingredients:
One can of Hormel Spicy Chili. One can of refried beans.  One can of Tyson* premium chunk chicken. One can of Spicy Rotel Tomatoes.

Directions:
Combine ingredients into a sauce pan on medium heat. Bring to a boil for three minutes while stirring.

Submitter: 
Jenna Witten
Description: 
The easiest and fastest chili you're ever gonna make! Spicy in a slow-burn sort of way.  You won't be able to eat just one bowl!  I like to eat it with tortilla chips crumbled on top with a tall glass of milk.
