Title:
Bacon and Cheddar Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990719.jpg
Ingredients:
14 hard-cooked eggs
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1 1/2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/3 cup crumbled cooked bacon
1/4 cup finely shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives or green onion tops

Directions:
Cut eggs lengthwise in half. Remove yolks to medium bowl. Reserve 24 white halves. Finely chop remaining 4 white halves.
Mash yolks with fork. Add mayonnaise, sour cream, mustard, lemon juice and pepper; mix well. Add chopped egg whites, bacon, cheese and chives; mix well.
Spoon 1 heaping Tbsp. yolk mixture into each reserved egg white half.
Refrigerate, covered, to blend flavors.

Submitter: 
American Egg Board
Description: 
Deviled eggs are always popular, but add bacon and Cheddar cheese, along with some tangy Dijon mustard and lemon juice, and they'll be the first appetizer to disappear.
