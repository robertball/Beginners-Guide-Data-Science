Title:
GRANDMA ZELDA'S SWEET POTATO CASSEROLE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-40 oz can Gold Yams drained
1 c sugar
1/2 melted butter
1 tsp. vanilla
1/3 c milk
1/2 c brown sugar
1/4 c butter
2 1/2 T flour
1/2 c chopped pecans or walnuts

Directions:
Mix all of the first 5  ingredients until well blended;  Pour in a 3 qt. casserole
With the rest of the next ingredients cut butter into flour and brown sugar to make coarse crumbs.  Stir in nuts.  Sprinkle over sweet potato mixture.  Bake at 350 for 40 minutes to 1 hour or until browned and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Grace Carolyn Bowers-Reimondo
Description: 
A wonderful Thanksgiving Sweet Potato Casserole that is a hit with the Reimondo, Harding and Paulsen Families.
