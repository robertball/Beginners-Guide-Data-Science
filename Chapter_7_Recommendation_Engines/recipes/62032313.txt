Title:
Italian-Style Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound medium to large pasta shells
1 large cucumber
1 large tomato
1 green pepper
1 rib of celery
16 oz bottle of ZESTY Italian salad dressing
1/2 jar of Salad Supreme (or similar) salad spice
Optional:  Diced cheese (hard, such as cheddar or Asiago)and/or diced pepperoni.

Directions:
1.  Cook pasta according to package directions.  Do not overcook!  Drain, then rinse with cold water.
2.  While pasta is cooking, dice cucumber (peeled and seeded), tomato, green pepper and celery into very small pieces.  Add cheese and pepperoni, if using.  Mix together in large bowl.
3.  Add cooled pasta, entire bottle of salad dressing and half jar of Salad Supreme.  Mix well.
4.  Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Judy in Delaware
Description: 
A quick and delicious treat for a barbeque or buffet-type dinner party. The tiny dice of the vegetables nestle into the shell pasta. Everyone loves this one.
