Title:
Broccoli And Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) box of frozen broccoli, thawed out and thoroughly drained
2 Tablespoons of chopped onions
14 ounce can of cream corn
1/2 teaspoon of salt
dash of pepper
1 egg, slightly beaten
1/4 cup of bread or cracker crumbs
2 Tablespoons of butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix the first 6 ingredients together and put in a 1 or 1 1/2 quart baking dish.
On the top add the bread or cracker crumbs and pour the melted butter over it.
bake for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Linda Grein
