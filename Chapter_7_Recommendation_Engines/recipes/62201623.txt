Title:
"Philly" 15 minute Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRUST
1 cup graham cracker crumbs
3 tbl sugar
3 tbl butter / margarine (melted)
FILLING
3 8oz pkgs of Philly Creamcheese (softened)
3/4 sugar
3 eggs
1 cup mini simi-sweet chocolate pieces(optional)
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
CRUST
Combine crumbs, sugar and butter; press onto bottom of 9 inch springform pan.
FILLING
Combine cream cheese & sugar (mixing on medium).
Add eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each.
Blend in chocolate pieces & vanilla.
Pour over crust
Bake at 450 for 10 minutes
Reduce oven to 250 and continue to bake for 35 minutes.
Loosen cake from rim of pan; cool before removing rim of pan.  Chill
