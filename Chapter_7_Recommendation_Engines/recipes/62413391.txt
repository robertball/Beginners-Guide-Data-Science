Title:
Lori's Favorite Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless chicken breasts cut up
1-2 cups Pepperidge Farm Cubed Herb Seasoned Stuffing Mix
1- 10 3/4 oz can cream of chicken soup
1/3 cup of water
4-6 slices of Swiss cheese(or 2 per breast)
1/4 cup (4 Tablespoons) butter melted

Directions:
Put cut up chicken in a 13Ã—9 pan.
Place Swiss cheese over chicken. In a bowl mix soup and 1/3 cup of water. Spread over chicken.
Top chicken with stuffing mix and drizzle with melted butter
Cover with foil and bake 350 for 30 minutes. Uncover and bake for another 20 minutes or until cooked all the way through.

Submitter: 
Lori Davis
Description: 
This is one of my families favorite casserole recipes. It is so easy to make! You can either use whole chicken breasts or cut the chicken into pieces.
