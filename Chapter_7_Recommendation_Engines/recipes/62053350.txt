Title:
Pork Chop Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium potatoes, sliced(with or without skins, your choice,
6 pork chops,
1 cup sour cream,
1 can cream of mushroom soup,
1/2 cup milk,
2 TBSP chives, chopped,
1 cup grated cheddar cheese,
salt and pepper to taste.

Directions:
In a 4 quart casserole dish, place potatoes and arrange on bottom of dish evenly.  In a medium bowl, combine sour cream, soup, milk, chives, and cheese.  Pour mixture over potatoes, reserving about 1/2 cup.  Arrange pork chops on top of potatoes, and pour remaining mixture ontop.  Bake in a 350 degree oven for 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until potatoes are done.  Salt and pepper to taste.
