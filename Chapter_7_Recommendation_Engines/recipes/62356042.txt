Title:
Scrumptious Shrimp Scampi Linguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/401098.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1lb box linguine
1 red pepper, to equal 1/2 c. chopped
1 yellow pepper, to equal 1/2 c. chopped
4 Tbsp butter, divided
3 Tbsp EVOO, divided
1/2 Tbsp crushed red pepper
2 Tbsp heavy whipping cream
6 cloves garlic, sliced
3 Tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
1/2 c. Dry white wine (e.g. Chardonnay)
1 Tsp Sea salt
45-60 large shrimp (peeled, deviened, tail removed)
1 tsp lemon juice
1/4 c. Clam juice

Directions:
In 12" skillet, simmer 2 Tbsp butter, 1 1/2 Tbsp oil,
garlic, red and yellow peppers.
Simmer on medium heat until garlic is clear & peppers are tender for approx 10 min.
Add shrimp, cook until pink, then remove shrimp
from mixture & set aside.
Add wine, lemon juice, and clam juice. When
mix boils, add remaining butter and remaining
oil.
When butter melts, return shrimp, add heavy cream, crushed red pepper and fresh parsley. Add 1 tsp sea salt.
After pasta boils, reserve 1 c. of water pasta was
cooked in to add to mixture.
Combine pasta and shrimp mixture, mix
thoroughly for 5 min allowing pasta to absorb
sauce and serve.
