Title:
Easy Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 ounces of cream cheese
1/2 cup butter
2 teaspoons vanilla
2 cups powdered sugar

Directions:
Put butter in a microwave for 7 seconds.
Beat together cream cheese, butter, and vanilla in a small mixing bowl till it's almost whipped.
Slowly mix in powdered sugar.
Continue mixing until the frosting looks smooth.
Store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
live_love_cook
Description: 
This is a simple recipe for delicious cream cheese frosting. It has less powdered sugar than most recipes... that's one reason it tastes so good.
