Title:
Whole 30 Slow Cooker Asian Short Ribs By Angela Sackett, EverydayWelcome.com Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6231351.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons coconut oil
2-3 pounds short ribs
Sea salt and cracked black pepper
2 large sweet onions, quartered
1 stalk celery, chopped
4 carrots, cut into bite sized pieces
1 cinnamon stick
1 teaspoon dried powdered ginger
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 teaspoon sea salt
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 cup beef broth
Â¼ cup coconut aminos
1 tablespoon fish sauce
2 tablespoons arrowroot powder
3 green onions, chopped

Directions:
Season short ribs liberally on all sides with salt and cracked black pepper.
In a large, deep skillet, brown short ribs and onion quarters in coconut oil, on all sides.
Place ribs and onion in slow cooker with celery, carrots and cinnamon stick.
Stir together ginger, garlic, salt and pepper, broth, coconut aminos, and fish sauce.
Pour over ingredients in slow cooker and cook on low for 6-8 hours.
If desired before serving, raise heat to high and stir in arrowroot powder.
Allow to thicken slightly and serve, if desired, with cauliflower rice, topped with chopped green onion.
