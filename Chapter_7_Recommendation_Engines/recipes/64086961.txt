Title:
Lemon Mustard Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast halves, boneless & skinless
2 Tbsp margarine
1-1/2 Tbsp Dijon mustard
1-1/2 Tbsp lemon juice
1/2 tsp tarragon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Place chicken in greased shallow baking pan or casserole.
Melt margarine, then stir in mustard, lemon juice and tarragon.  Pour over chicken and bake for 30-45 minutes until done.
