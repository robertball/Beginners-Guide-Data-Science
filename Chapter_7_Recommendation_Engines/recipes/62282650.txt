Title:
Agave Basil Gimlet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/352312.jpg
Ingredients:
5 fresh basil leaves
2 ounces Plymouth Gin
1/2 ounce agave nectar
3/4 ounce fresh lime juice

Directions:
In a pint glass, muddle fresh basil, agave nectar, and lime juice.
Add ice and gin.
Shake vigorously and strain into stemmed glass.
Garnish with fresh basil leaf.
