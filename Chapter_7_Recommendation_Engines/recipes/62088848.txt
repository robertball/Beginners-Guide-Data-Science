Title:
Kathy's Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 hard boiled eggs
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1.5 tsp. creamed horseradish
1.5 tsp. Dijon mustard
1.5 tsp. Bacos bacon bits, crushed
1 tbsp. ranch salad dressing
1/2 tsp. black pepper
Paprika

Directions:
Peel hard-boiled eggs.  Cut in half and scoop yolks into small bowl.  Reserve whites.  Mash yolks with a fork.  Add all remaining ingredients except paprika.  Mix until creamy.  Fill whites with mixture.  Sprinkle paprika on top.

Submitter: 
KATHYP100
Description: 
For those who like their deviled eggs a little more savory and not so tart like the recipes that include pickle relish.  These eggs are very flavorful with a little bit of a kick.
