Title:
corn and mashed potato bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
about 3-4 cups leftover mashed potatoes
1 can creamstyle corn
4 tblsp. melted butter
1/4 cup italian style bread crumbs
garlic salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees.
spray casserole dish with nonsitck cooking spray.
in casserole dish, combine potatoes and corn.
add salt and peper to taste.
after all is combined sprinkle with bread crumbs
drizzle with butter.
bake for 25-30 minutes or untill top is goden brown and potatoes are heated through.

Submitter: 
mistycolleen
Description: 
this satisfying side dish is a favorite in our house. using leftover mashed potatoes and canned cream corn creates this comforting casserole.
