Title:
Charred Habanero Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/793769.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Whole Orange Habaneros (De-seeded)
1 Whole Serrano pepper (De-seeded)
1/2 Large Onion -- sliced 1/4" thick
3 Large Cloves Garlic -unpeeled
1/4 teaspoon Sea Salt
1/4 teaspoon Mexican oregano
1/4 Pound Tomatillos -- Husk removed
1/4 Pound Tomatos
1/2 Lime -- juiced

Directions:
Blacken chiles and tomatillos over gas stove burner, barbecue, or oven broiler. (use broiler as a last resort).
Heat heavy cast iron skillet and cook onion slices and garlic till slightly blackened about 4 min a side.
Add all ingredients to blender or food processer and buzz till well mixed but still slightly chunky.

Description: 
Hot but tasty salsa, got the recipe from a friend of the family many years ago, and her memory can still live on through the making and remaking of her salsa....
