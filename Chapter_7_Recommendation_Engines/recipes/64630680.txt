Title:
Cheesy Pork Balls with Onion Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
250 grams thinly sliced pork scraps
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 teaspoon cornstarch
shredded cheese
1/4 yellow onion, grated
1 clove garlic, grated
1 Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp vinegar
1/2 Tbsp sugar
1 Tbsp sake
1/2 Tbsp mirin
mizuna

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the meat, salt, pepper and cornstarch. Mix well then divide the pork into 8-10 portions.
For each portion of pork, add cheese at the center then form it into a ball-like shape enclosing the cheese. Put the pork balls on a microwave-safe dish.
Prepare the onion sauce by mixing the grated onion and garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, sake and mirin. Pour the sauce on top of the pork balls.
Cover then put in the microwave and cook for 5 minutes using 600W.
Cut the mizuna into 5-cm widths then put on a serving plate.
Put the pork balls on top of the mizuna then bring back the sauce into microwave. Cook for 2 more minutes using 600W then pour on top of the pork balls.
Serve and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
A simple recipe making use of pork scraps formed into balls then cooked in a microwave. It doesn't take much time to prepare and the cooking time is also fast. A lot of flavors in one dish, in a good way.
