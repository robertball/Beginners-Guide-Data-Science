Title:
Pigs in a Blanket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 or 2 pounds sausage and beef hamburger
1 small diced onion
1 clove garlic (diced)
1 egg
1 cup rice
salt and pepper to your taste
1 head cabbage
1/2 cup water
1 can tomato sauce

Directions:
Mix in throughly partially cooked rice and seasoning with the meat, onion, salt, pepper, egg, and garlic.  Cut heart out of par boiled cabbage.  Place head of cabbage in boiling water until leaves losen from head.  Drain, cut vein from cabbage.  PLace about 1 tablespoon of meat mixture on cabbage leaves.  Roll and fasten by tucking sides in.  Line bottom of pan with small or odds and ends of the cabbage and place close together in pan or baking dish.  Pour 1 can of tomato sauce and 1/2 cup water over cabbage rolls.  Cover and bake in oven at 350 degrees about 1 1/2 hours or until done.
