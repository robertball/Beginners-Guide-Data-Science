Title:
Chocolate Melting Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/585103.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. Chocolate chips
1 lb Unsalted Butter
2 ½ cups Pasteurized Egg (or 10 fresh eggs)
2/3 cup flour
½ cup sugar

Directions:
Melt chocolate and butter in microwave at 50% power (Defrost)
Beat eggs, add sugar, sift flour into mix
Fold in melted chocolate and butter mix
Pour into Glass Ramekins
Bake at 300 for 12 - 15 minutes in serving dish. Dessert should be firm around edges and wiggly in the center.
Place stencil on top of dessert and sift powdered sugar.
Serve Warm
