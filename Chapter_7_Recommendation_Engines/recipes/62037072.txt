Title:
Baby Onions with Spinach and Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. frozen baby onions
3 Tbsp. unsalted butter
1 clove garlic, minced
10 oz. pkg chopped, frozen spinach, thawed and drained
3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/4 cup heavy cream
Salt and pepper to taste
3 Tbsp. dry bread crumbs

Directions:
Cook onions according to package directions; drain well. Melt 2 Tbsp. butter in same saucepan over medium heat. Add garlic and cook 1-2 minutes. Add spinach and cook, stirring frequently, until liquid evaporates, about 5 minutes. Stir in 1/2 cup parmesan and the cream. Add onions and combine. Transfer to shallow 1-qt. baking dish.
Combine remaining parmesan and the bread crumbs and sprinkle over onion mixture. Dot with remaining 1 Tbsp. butter.
Bake at 400Â° until top is browned, about 20 minutes. Serve hot.  8-10 servings

Submitter: 
Sue S.
Description: 
Two holiday vegetables in one great dish! Tiny baby onions and spinach in a rich cream sauce - very yummy and it looks great on a holiday buffet table
