Title:
Pork Albondigas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meatballs
12 oz. lean ground pork
1/2 cup. grated unpeeled zucchini
1/2 cup unsalted tortilla chips
1/4 cup sliced scallions
2 Tbs. chopped cilantro
1 lg. egg
1 1/2 tsp. minced garlic
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. ground cumin
1/2 tsp. dried oregano
1 can (10 oz.) mild enchilada sauce.

Directions:
1. Meatballs: In large bowl, using hands or a wooden spoon, mix all ingredients until blended.  With moistened hands, roll into 20 1 1/2 in. meatballs.
2.Coat a large nonstick skillet with a nonstick spray.  Heat over medium heat, add meatballs, increase heat to medium high and cook turning 5 min. or until brown on all sides.
3. Add enchilada sauch and 1/2 c. water to skillet.  Bring to boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer, stirring occasionally, 5 min. or until meatballs ae cooked through.
