Title:
Alexander's Perfection On Steak Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Steak Rolls
9 Cans Filled With Tomato Sauce
12 Bags Of Mozzarella Cheese
2 Pounds Of Sliced Steak

Directions:
1. Take the twelve steak rolls and heat them up in the microwave.
2. Then take out the sliced steak and place it into a metal frying pan over medium heat until the meat is fully cooked.
3. Remove the twelve steak rolls from the microwave and pour a small amount of the tomato sauce into each of the steak rolls.
4. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
5. Put some of the sliced steak onto the cheese.
6. Heat them until they are evenly combined.
7. Eat up!
