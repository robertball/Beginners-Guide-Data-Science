Title:
Chicken Alouette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (17 1/4  ounce) package frozen puff pastry sheets thawed.
1 (4 ounce) container garlic and spice flavored Alouette cheese (can sub homemade boursin here)
6 skinned and boned chicken breast halves
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 egg beaten
1 tablespoon water

Directions:
Unfold pastry sheets and roll each sheet into a 14x12 inch rectangle on a lightly floured surface.
Cut one sheet into four 7x6 inch rectangles.
Cut second sheet into two 7x6 inch rectangles and one 12x6 inch rectangle.
Set large rectangle aside
Shape each small rectangle into an oval by trimming off corners.
Spread pastry ovals evenly with cheese.
Sprinkle chicken breast halves with salt and pepper.
Place one in center of each pastry oval.
Lightly moisten pastry edges with water.
Fold ends over chicken and press to seal.
Place each bundle seam side down on a lightly greased baking sheet.
Now to get fancy!
Cut remaining large pastry rectangle into 12 1/4 inch strips.
Braid 2 strips together and place crosswise over chicken bundles, trimming and reserving excess braid.
Braid 2 additional strips and place lengthwise over bundle, trimming and tucking ends under.
Repeat procedure with remaining strips.
Cover and refrigerate up to 2 hours, if desired.
Combine egg and 1 tablespoon of water; brush over pastry bundles.
Bake at 400 degrees on lower oven rack 25 minutes or until bundles are golden brown.
Garnish if desired.

Description: 
If you want to wow your guests then this is your go to recipe!  Can be made ahead which is a good idea as there is some finger work required  but it is worth it.
