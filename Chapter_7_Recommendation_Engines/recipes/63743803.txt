Title:
Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg yol beaten
3 tbsp milk
1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Stir together flour, baking powder, and salt
Cream butter, add sugar, gradually beating between additions
Add egg yolk, mix well
Add dry ingredients alternately with milk and add vanilla
Chill dough thoroughly
Roll 1/4 " thick on lightly floured board
Cut and place on ungreased cookie sheet 8-10 minutes
