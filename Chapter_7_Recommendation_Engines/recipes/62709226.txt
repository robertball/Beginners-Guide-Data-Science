Title:
Slow Cooker Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 potatoes, peeled and cubed
2 onions, chopped
2 cups diced carrots
3 stalks celery, chopped
2 teaspoons minced garlic
1 (16 ounce) bag frozen whole kernel corn, thawed
1 (16 ounce) tube of cream style corn, thawed
2 tablespoons butter
1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
2 cups cubed ham or smoked sausage
2 cubes  chicken bouillon
1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
1-2 teaspoons cornstarch for thickening

Directions:
In a slow cooker, place all ingredients except ham and evaporated milk.
Cook on low setting for 6 hours and then stir in the ham.  After another hour, stir in evaporated milk which has been mixed with cornstarch. Cook for 30 more minutes.

Submitter: 
Libby
Description: 
A can of evaporated milk finishes this recipe for corn chowder with ham, onion, celery,carrots and potato in a chicken bouillon soup base.  Allow 8 hours cooking time.Modified from: Slow Cooker Corn Chowder
