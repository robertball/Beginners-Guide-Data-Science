Title:
Sausage, Egg, Cheese Biscuit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 eggs
8 slices american cheese
8 frozen sausage patties
8 biscuits (canned or frozen)

Directions:
Scramble and fry eggs, bake biscuits, place eggs on 1/2 biscuit heat sausage patties and place on egg, top with cheese and the other 1/2 of biscuit. serve hot
