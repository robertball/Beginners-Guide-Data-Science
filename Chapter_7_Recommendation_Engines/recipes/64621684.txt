Title:
Aunt Rocky's Salted Caramel Pecan OMM (lchf, gluten free, grain free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4439957.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbsp (414g)  melted butter (or coconut oil for dairy free)
1 large (50g)  egg
1/2 tsp vanilla
2 tbsp Walden Farms Caramel Dip (zero carbs, zero calories)
2 tbsp (13g)  Bob's Red Mill golden flaxseed meal
2 tbsp (14g)  Honeyville Blanched Almond Flour
1 tbsp (5.38g) Lifesource Oat Fiber
1 tbsp (12g) Just Like Sugar Brown
1/2 tsp (2g) baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1 Tbsp. pecans, chopped

Directions:
Melt butter in 4" ramekin or wide soup mug.
Beat the egg, vanilla, and caramel dip in with the melted butter until creamy.
Add all dry ingredients and mix thoroughly.
Microwave on high for 1 minute, 15 seconds.  The muffin should be completely set in the center.  If not, add another 10 seconds of cook time.
Cool for 5 minutes to fully set.  Enjoy!
