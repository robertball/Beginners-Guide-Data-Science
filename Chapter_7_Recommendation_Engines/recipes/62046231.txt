Title:
Potato & Ham Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium potatoes, peel and sliced thin
1/2 cup green onion, finely chopped
1 cup ham leftovers, cubed
1 cup chicken broth
1 pint whipping cream
1 cup parmesan cheese, finely grated
White pepper, to taste

Directions:
Peel and slice potatoes into thin slices.
In greased 8x8 baking dish, spread 1/2 of the potatoes evenly.
Sprinkle 1/2 of the ham, onions an parmesan cheese evenly.
Add a dash of white pepper.
Repeat for second layer.
Pour chicken broth evenly over the potatoen, then whipping cream. Sprinkle with additional parmesan cheese, if desired.
Bake for 45 minutes at 375F, or until crust is golden brown. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
FoodieWife
Description: 
This casserole is really easy and costs very little to make. It's a great main dish on a chilly day, but it's also a delicious side recipe. If you like to use a mandolin, it makes slicing the potatoes even quicker and easier.
