Title:
Lemon Sheet Cake with Lemon Pie Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983022.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package lemon cake mix
4 eggs
1 (15.75 ounce) can lemon pie filling
1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
2 cups confectioners' sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, beat the cake mix and eggs until well blended. Fold in pie filling. Spread into a greased 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees F for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool on a wire rack.
In a small mixing bowl, beat cream cheese, butter and confectioners' sugar until smooth. Stir in vanilla. Spread over cake. Store in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Alyce  Dubisar
Description: 
Lemon pie filling lends a splash of citrus flavor to convenient cake mix, and a rich cream cheese frosting gives it sweetness. My family likes this cake cold, so I cut it into squares and freeze before serving. -Alyce Dubisar, Coos Bay, Oregon
