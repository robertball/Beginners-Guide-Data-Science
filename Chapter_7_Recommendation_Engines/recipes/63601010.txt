Title:
Sweet and Spicy Bacon Wrapped Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts cut length-wise into tenders
Bacon slices to equal the number of tenders
1 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp Pepper
1 tsp granulated garlic
1/2 tsp chili powder
1 cup brown sugar packed

Directions:
pre-heat oven to 400
1. cut breasts into slices or buy tenders from your local supermarket. (always buy local!) Mix the spices into a flat pan and roll the tenders in the spices.
2. Discard remaining spices and put the brown sugar in the pan, fluff it up and roll the bacon wrapped tenders in the brown sugar and place in a well oiled pyrex pan to the pieces do not touch.
3. Bake for 30-40 minutes until brown and crispy.

Submitter: 
Robert Loughrey
Description: 
Caramelized Bacon over spicy chicken, it's delicious with rice pilaf or mashed potatoes. cut into bite sized pieces after cooking and put on toothpicks, this makes a great hot appetizer.
