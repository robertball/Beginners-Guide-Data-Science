Title:
Rosemary Sea Salt Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
3 tablespoons olive oil + 2 tbsp reserved for topping
1 1/2 teaspoons honey
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 tablespoon dried rosemary + 1 tsp crushed for topping
2 1/2 cups bread flour
1 1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast
Sprinkling of coarse Sea Salt

Directions:
Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select Dough cycle; press Start.
Remove from breadmaker, w/sharp knife cut into 16 equal pieces. Form into equal-sized rolls. Place on greased cookie sheet. Brush tops w/olive oil, sprinkle w/crushed rosemary & coarse sea salt.
Cover and let rise in warm place for 45 minutes or until doubled in size.
Bake in oven at 375 for 17 - 20 minutes, w/9 x 13 pan of warm water on bottom rack of oven for crusty tops.

Submitter: 
Julie Holden
Description: 
Wonderfully generous with the rosemary, with support from a little black pepper and Italian seasoning, this bread comes out light and fragrant.Modified from: Jo's Rosemary Bread
