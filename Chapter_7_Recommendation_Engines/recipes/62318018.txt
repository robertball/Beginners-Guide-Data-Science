Title:
Grilled Turkey Burgers with Cranberry Horseradish Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
BURGERS
1 1/2 lbs lean ground turkey
1/2 c. celery
1/2 c. yellow onion
1 tbsp. thyme (fresh if you can)
1 tbsp. poultry seasoning
1 tbsp. parsley (fresh if you can)
salt and pepper
1 c. bread crumbs
1 egg
CRANBERRY DRESSING
1 can of whole cranberries
2 tbsp. prepared horseradish
juice from fresh squeezed lemon
salt and pepper
pinch of cumin

Directions:
Mix all the burger ingredients in a bowl and form into patties, place on grill and grill until juices run clear.
For Dressing: Mix all ingredients together and let sit for 15 min. Place on burger and enjoy!
