Title:
Crab Salad Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For Cups:
Cooking spray
18 wonton wrappers
2 tsp. canola oil
1/4 tsp. salt
For Dressing:
1 tsp. lemon zest
2 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1/4 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. red pepper flakes
2 tbsp. olive oil
For Salad:
1/2 lb. lump crab meat
1 stalk celery, finely diced - about 1/4 cup
1/2 cup finely diced mango
1/4 cup thinly sliced scallions
2 tbsp. fresh cilantro - chopped

Directions:
Heat oven to 375.
Brush both sides of wonton with canola oil and gently press into mini muffin tin to form cup shape.
Sprinkle with salt and bake until brown and crisp - about 8-10 minutes.
Cool cups before removing from pan.
For dressing, whisk together all ingredients until well combined.
For salad, toss together all ingredients then add dressing and toss to coat.
Fill each cup with crab mixture and serve immediately.
