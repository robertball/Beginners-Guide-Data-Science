Title:
Spicy Pear & Cranberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 1/2 cups white sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon ground allspice
1 pear - peeled, cored and diced
1/4 chopped dried cranberries
1/4 cup honey
2/3 cup vanilla yogurt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In large bowl, beat margarine and sugar untill smooth.
Beat in the rest of the wet ingredients.
Stir in the dry ingredients-- add the flour one half cup at a time.
Stir in chopped pears and cranberries.
Bake 15 minutes. Remove to wire racks to cool.
