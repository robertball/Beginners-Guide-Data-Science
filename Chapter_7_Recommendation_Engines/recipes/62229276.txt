Title:
Cranberry Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup buttermilk or plain yogurt
1 egg
2 3/4 cups flour
4 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1 cup coarsely chopped cranberries (fresh or frozen)
1/2 cup sugar
Rind of 1 orange (optional)
1 tbsp. butter, melted
1/4 cup icing sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 345F.
Beat buttermilk and egg in small bowl and set aside.
In large obwl, measure flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
Cut in butter until mixture resembles small peas.
Mix in cranberries, sugar and orange rind.
Add buttermilk mixture and stir until soft dough forms.  Using your hands, form dough into a large ball and place on floured surface.
Pat out to 1" thickness.  But in 4" rounds.
Place on ungreased cookie sheet and bake scones for 15 - 20 minutes.
While still warm, brush with butter and sprinkle with icing sugar.
