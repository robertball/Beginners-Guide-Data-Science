Title:
Cinnameg Chocolate Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/718398.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups Chocolate Ice Cream
¼ Cup Milk
1 Tablespoon Dutch Pressed Cocoa Powder
1 Teaspoon Vanilla
½ Teaspoon Ground Nutmeg
½ Teaspoon Ground Cinnamon

Directions:
In a blender combine chocolate ice cream, milk, cocoa powder, vanilla, nutmeg, and cinnamon. Blend together and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
House of Aqua
Description: 
A thick milkshake that blends fall spices and chocolate together.
