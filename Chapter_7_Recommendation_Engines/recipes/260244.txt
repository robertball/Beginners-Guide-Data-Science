Title:
Honey-Kissed Smoothie Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4557520.jpg
Ingredients:
Smoothie:
1 frozen banana
1 cup almond, soy, or dairy milk
3 tablespoons almond butter or natural peanut butter
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 tablespoons Aunt Sue’s® Raw & Unfiltered Honey
1/4 cup coconut milk
1/4 cup dark chocolate chips
1/2 cup ice
Toppings:
Assorted berries
Shredded coconut
Prepared granola
Assorted chopped nuts

Directions:
Combine all smoothie ingredients into a blender. Blend until smooth.
Remove and pour into a bowl.
Garnish with assorted toppings. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Aunt Sue’s® Raw Honey
Description: 
Full of all-natural ingredients like fruit, nuts and honey, this smoothie bowl will help you start your day on a healthy note.
