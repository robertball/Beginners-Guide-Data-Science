Title:
Pan Seared Salmon II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon butter
4 (4 ounce) fillets salmon
2 teaspoons vegetable oil
1/2 red bell pepper, diced
1/2 yellow bell pepper, diced
2 teaspoons garlic, finely chopped
1 lemon

Directions:
) I would use the large skillet to cook the red and yellow peppers, then add the garlic until just aromatic.  Remove and set aside then add butter to skillet and salt and pepper the salmon, cook salmon fillets about 3 to four minutes per side. Drizzle with lemon juice.  Serve with a wedge of lemon
