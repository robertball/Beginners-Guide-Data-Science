Title:
Pepperoni Cavatini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. Hamburger
1/2 lb. Sausage
1/2 lb. Pepperoni
2 1/2 Cups Macaroni (cooked)
1 lg. Jar Spaghetti Sauce
1/2 can Italian Stewed Tomatoes
1/2 cup Green Peppers (optional)
1/2 cup Mushrooms (optional)

Directions:
Mix together in a 13x9 baking dish. Tpo with 1/2 cup mozorella and 1/2 cup cheddar. Bake at 350 at 30 minutes.
