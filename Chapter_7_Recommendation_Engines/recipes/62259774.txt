Title:
Easy BBQ Pork for Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can beef broth
4 pounds boneless pork butt
1 (18 ounce) bottle barbecue sauce

Directions:
Pour can of beef broth into slow cooker, and add boneless pork butt. Cook on low heat for 10 hours.
Remove meat and shred with two forks. It will seem that it's not working right away, but it will.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Stir in barbecue sauce. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
