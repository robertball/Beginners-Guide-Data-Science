Title:
Bavarian pork chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 loin pork chops
1 large clove garlic halved
1/2 tsp caraway seed
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 Tbsp flour
2 Tbsp vegetable oil
12 oz beer or ale
1 Tbsp prepared mustard
1 c canned beef broth or brown stock

Directions:
Rub chops on both sides w/garlic.  Sprinkle w/caraway seeds, salt and pepper, and rub into chops.  Dredge in flour.  Brown on both sides in oil in skillet.  Remove chops and drain off all fat, leaving brown bits in the pan.  Add beer, bring to boil over high heat, stirring and scraping skillet to loosen brown bits.  Simmer liquid until reduced to 1/2 cup.
Stir in mustard and broth.  Return chops to skillet and simmer for 45 minutes, Covered turning once.
