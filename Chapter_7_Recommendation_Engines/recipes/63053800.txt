Title:
Evelyn's Perfect Pastry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 tsp. salt
1 cup shortening (Tender flake)
2 tbsps. cold water
1 egg
1tbsp. white vinegar

Directions:
Blend flour and salt together.  Cut in shortening until it looks like coarse meal. Mix together water,egg and vinegar. Add quickly to flour mixture. Do not handle too much. Divide dough into two balls and refrigerate for 10 minutes before rolling out. When pie is ready to bake, wash the top with a beaten egg and a sprinkle of coarse sugar.
