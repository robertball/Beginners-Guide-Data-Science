Title:
Fall Pudding Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1059593.jpg
Ingredients:
1 5.1 oz. box chocolate instant pudding
1 5.1 oz. box vanilla instant pudding
1 12 oz. can 2% evaporated milk
1 15 oz. can pumpkin (NOT pumpkin pie mix!)
3 1/4 c. 2% milk
3 c. heavy whipping cream
6 T. powdered sugar
1 t. pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
Whisk chocolate instant pudding and 3 c. 2% milk for 1-2 minutes (or follow directions on package if they differ). Refrigerate to set.
Whisk vanilla instant pudding and can of 2% evaporated milk. Refrigerate for 5 minutes.
Mix pumpkin and pumpkin pie spice into vanilla pudding. Thin with up to 1/4 c. 2% milk if needed to layer smoothly.
Whip heavy whipping cream until thickened. Add powdered sugar to taste 1 T. at a time until stiff.
Layer 1 oz. each pudding into 4 oz. ramekins (or larger containers if desired), top with whipped cream. Chill until ready to serve.

Description: 
A great, easy fall dessert with pumpkin, chocolate and whipped cream. A favorite at large gatherings!
