Title:
Comida China Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cauliflower florets
1 cup broccoli florets
1 zucchini
1 carrot
1 celery
1/2 small onion
1 minced garlic clove
1 boneless skinless chicken breast halves
2 tablespoons of Flour
Salt and Pepper
2 tablespoons of Soy sauce
2 tablespoons of Oyster sauce
Olive Oil (optional)

Directions:
Cut the chicken in bite pieces then salt and pepper chicken pieces to taste, then roll in flour to coat. Fry pan the chicken in oil until brown and reserve.
In a wok fry add the oil it can be the oil of your preference. Cook the garlic for a few seconds, then add the vegetables, onion, carrots, celery, zucchini, cauliflower and broccoli cook for a few minutes
Add the chicken, add soy sauce and oyster sauce. Salt and Pepper to taste and finish cooking for 5 minutes or until desired tenderness of the vegetables.
Serve over white rice.

Submitter: 
LittleOne
Description: 
My mom use to make this dish and served it over white rice. This is how we call it even if their ingredients are not Chinese the taste resembles to the Chinese cuisine in Mexico.
