Title:
Greek Feta Orzo Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans white cannelini beans, drained and rinsed
1 cup orzo
1 cup crumbled feta cheese
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 lemon, juiced
1/2 cup toasted pine nuts
2 cloves garlic, pressed or minced extra fine
2 Tbsp fresh chives, chopped
1 Tbsp red onion, minced extra fine
1 Tbsp fresh oregano chopped
2 tsp sugar
1 tsp black pepper
1/8 tsp dried thyme
salt to taste.

Directions:
Boil orzo to al dente (approx. 10 minutes) and allow to cool while preparing other ingredients.
In a salad cruet or jar, mix lemon juice, garlic, red onion, oregano, sugar, black pepper, and thyme and shake to emulsify. Salt to taste.
In a large mixing bowl, combine cooked orzo, cannelini beans, feta cheese, toasted pine nuts and chopped chives. Toss with dressing. Chill before serving.

Submitter: 
NUNPUNCH
Description: 
A light, refreshing, new take on classic Greek flavors. Great as a side or a simple summer evening meal.
