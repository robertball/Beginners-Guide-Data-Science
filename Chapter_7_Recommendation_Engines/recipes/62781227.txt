Title:
Bill's Curried Chicken Stew With Coconut Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds chicken thighs, bone in, skin on
1 medium yellow onion, chopped or sliced
1 large jalapeno pepper, finely diced
1 tablespoon ground cumin
1 tablespoon curry powder
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1 cup peeled potatoes, cut in 1-inch chunks
1 cup halved baby carrots
2 cups white beans
12 ounce can diced tomatoes
1/2 cup canned coconut milk
1 cup chicken broth
Salt and pepper to taste
Cilantro, chopped peanuts and unsweetened shredded coconut for garnish

Directions:
Pat dry chicken thighs.  In a dutch oven, brown both sides in small batches in canola oil.  Remove and set aside.
Saute onions and jalapeno pepper in dutch oven until translucent.
Add cumin, curry, and cayenne pepper to onion mixture.
Lower heat and gently roast spice/onion mixture for 2 minutes.
Add potatoes, carrots, white beans,coconut milk, canned tomatoes, chicken thighs and chicken broth.
Simmer on low heat with out top for 45 minutes.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Serve with cilantro, coconut and peanut garnish.
