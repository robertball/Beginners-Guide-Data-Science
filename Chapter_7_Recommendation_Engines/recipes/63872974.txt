Title:
Amazing Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Potato, Diced
1 Can Sweet Peas
1 Cup Onions, diced
1 Cup Celery, Diced
1 Cup Carrots, Diced
1/3 Cup Melted Margarine/Butter
1/2 Cup All-Purpose Flour
2 Cups Chicken Broth
1 Cup Half-and-half
1 Teaspoon Salt
1/4 Teaspoon pepper
4 Cups Chicken, Cooked and Chopped
2 Pillsbury Refrigerated Pie Crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Sauté Onion, Celery, Carrots, and Potatoes in margarine/butter for 10 minutes
Add flour to sautéed mixture, stirring well, cook one minute stirring constantly
Combine broth and half-and-half and gradually stir into vegetable mixture
Cook over medium heat stirring constantly until thickened and bubbly
Stir in salt and pepper add chicken and stir well
Pour mixture into a shallow two quart casserole dish and top with pie shells
Cut slits to allow steam to escape
Bake for 40-50 minutes or until crust is a golden brown and filling is bubbly and cooked through

Submitter: 
Aubrie Maegan Puffenbarger
Description: 
This dish is great for any time of year but especially on cold snowy days. It is so simple and easy to prepare and is always a great crowed pleaser
