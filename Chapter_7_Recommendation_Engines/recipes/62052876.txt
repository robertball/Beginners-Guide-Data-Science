Title:
Russian Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup French Dressing
1 cup Miracle Whip
1 Teaspoon Hot Sauce

Directions:
Add all ingredients into bowl and mix. Pretty simple :)

Submitter: 
Amanda Johnson-Lindsey
Description: 
This is a terrific dressing tangy, but sweet. I make it alot for parties or dinners. Just keep making it until you get the amount desired. The below recipe is for a small amount for 4 people or so.
