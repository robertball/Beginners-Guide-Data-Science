Title:
SWEET POTATO FRIES Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
Fry Time (oven baked, that is)
Prep time: 15 mins Cook time: 25 mins Total time: 40 mins
Serves: 6
A spicy, smoky oven baked sweet potato fry everyone will love!
Ingredients
5 medium sweet potatoes – peeled and cut into ½ inch wide strips
2 Tbsp olive oil
2 Tbsp pepper
2 Tbsp garlic powder
1 Tbsp salt
1 Tbsp chili powder

Directions:
Prepare a large rimmed baking sheet with foil and non-stick spray
Wash and peel the sweet potatoes
Cut each potato into quarters then into ½ inch wide strips and put in large bowl
Pour olive oil over potatoes and toss to coat
In a small bowl combine pepper, garlic powder, salt & chili powder
Toss spice mixture with potatoes
Spread potatoes onto baking sheet in a single layer
bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown
