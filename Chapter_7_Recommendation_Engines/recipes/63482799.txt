Title:
Seafood Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
pasta
immitation /lobster
veggies
mayo
s/p
parsley

Directions:
Early in the day cook pasta about 8-12oz. Drain and add italian dressing just enough to prevent pasta from sticking together.  refrigerate.
later--add 1lb imitation crab/lobster
chop--veggies-any kind you have on hand-- about 2 cups of onion, celery, carrots, broccoli, green bell pepper or red, cucumbers, nuts or seeds, olives black or green,  peas, mandarine oranges, sweet relish pickle
Dressing: thin down mayo with small amount of water or milk for dressing. This can be served on the side and everyone uses the amount they want or pour it on the salad.
