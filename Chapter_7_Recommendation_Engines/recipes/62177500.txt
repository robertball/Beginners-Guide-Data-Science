Title:
Slow Cooker Pork Chops II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
2 teaspoons minced garlic
1 tablespoon paprika
1 tablespoon onion powder
1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried basil
4 thick cut boneless pork chops
Kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk together the olive oil, chicken broth, garlic, paprika, onion powder, Italian seasoning and basil. Pour into the slow cooker.
Cut small slits in each pork chop with the tip of a knife, and season lightly with salt and ground black pepper. Place pork chops into the slow cooker, cover, and cook on "LOW" for 6 hours or until chops are very tender.
Baste periodically with the sauce; turning chops at least 1 time.
