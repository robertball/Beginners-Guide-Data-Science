Title:
Strawberry & Pineapple Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Angel food cake (store bought or make ahead)
2 Packages instant vanilla pudding
4 Cups of milk
1 8oz can pineapple tidbits; drained
1 Quart strawberries, tops removed and sliced
1 8oz tub of whipped topping

Directions:
Cut cake into 1" squares, and place in the bottom of trifle bowl.
Mix 2 boxes of instant pudding with 4 cups of milk for 2 minutes or until well blended.
Pour prepared pudding over top of cake layer.
Sprinkle sliced strawberries and pineapple over top of pudding layer.
Top with the tub of whipped topping.
Chill for 2 hours and serve.
