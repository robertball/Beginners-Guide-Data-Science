Title:
Coq au Vin alla Italiana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 pounds dark meat chicken pieces
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
5 cloves crushed garlic
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon poultry seasoning
5 (4 ounce) links of sweet or hot (or mixture of both) Italian sausage, sliced
1 cup chopped onion
3 carrots, sliced
1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary, crushed
1 cup red or white wine
2 (14.5 ounce) cans diced Italian flavored tomatoes
salt and pepper to taste
water/cornstartch

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat oil. Add 1/2 of the garlic. Season flour with poultry seasoning. Dredge chicken parts in flour, then brown in the skillet for 4 or 5 minutes. Add the sausage, and saute for a few minutes. Add the onion, carrots, mushrooms, rosemary and the remaining garlic. Stir all together.
Add the wine and tomatoes; stir. Cover and let simmer over low heat for 25 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste and let simmer for another 10 minutes. Thicken with water/cornstartch mixture.
Let cool covered for 10 minutes, then serve.

Description: 
Take the usual coq au vin ingredients of chicken, vegetables and red wine, toss in loads of garlic, tomatoes and sweet Italian sausage, and you have yourself a feast.Modified from: Coq au Vin alla Italiana
