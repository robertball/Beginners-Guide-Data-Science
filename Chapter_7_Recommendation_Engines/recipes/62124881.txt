Title:
Lasagna Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2lbs ground sirloin
4lb 3oz jar of spaghetti sauce
1 Tbsp minced garlic
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp italian seasoning
1 lb package ricotta cheese
2 Tbsp sugar
2 cups mozzarella cheese
1 egg, beaten
1 cup red wine
1 cup grated paremsan cheese
16oz package of radiatore (or similar) pasta, cooked & drained
2 Tbsp dried parsley

Directions:
Brown meat over medium heat, adding spices.
Drain meat.
Add meat to Prego, plus red wine and sugar.  Simmer for ½ hour.
Meantime, beat ricotta cheese with egg and parsley; set aside.
Place pasta with ½ of the sauce and Ricotta cheese mixture in a large bowl and stir to blend.
Add ½ of the remaining sauce plus ½ of the mozzarella cheese and parmesan cheese and blend.
Pour the remaining sauce into the bottom of a treated 9x13 baking dish.
Add the pasta mixture and top with remaining cheese.
Bake covered with foil at 375 F for 30 minutes.
Uncover and bake an additional 15 minutes.
Let sit 15 minutes prior to serving.

Description: 
All the flavor of lasagna without the work of layering.
