Title:
Unbaked Fruit  Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of unflavored gelatin
1 package of vanilla instant pudding (dry)
1 cup sour cream or buttermilk
1 20 oz can of crushed pineapple
1 small jar of maraschino cherries
2 large fresh bananas
or any other fresh fruit you have

Directions:
In a small bowl dissolve gelatin in water, according to directions or use juice from the pineapple.
Mix dry pudding with sour cream or buttermilk, then add gelatin and the remainder of the ingredients.
Pour into a prepared pie shell of your choice and chill several hours until set.
Serve with whipped cream
