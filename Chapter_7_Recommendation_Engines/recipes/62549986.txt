Title:
Mexican Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/531836.jpg
Ingredients:
5 ounces bittersweet chocolate (coarsely chopped)
3/4 cup flour
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
Dash of black pepper
Dash of chili pepper flakes
1 1/4 cups sugar
1/4 cup butter, softened
1 large egg
1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350
2. Place chocolate in a small glass bowl melt in microwave, stirring occasionally. (2 min)
3. Add flour and next 5 ingredients into bowl. Combine with whisk.
4. Combine sugar and butter and blend with mixer until well blended.  Add egg; beat well. Add cooled chocolate and vanilla; beat just until blended.
5. Drop leveled tablespoon of dough on baking sheets lined with parchment paper.
6. Bake at 350 for 10 minutes.  Remove from oven, cool in pans for 2 minutes.  Remove from pan to wire rack.
