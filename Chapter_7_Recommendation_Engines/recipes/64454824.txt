Title:
heart shapped potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
potatoes
oil

Directions:
slice potatoes into 3/4 slices
pre-cook for 10 minutes
cut into heart shapes using cookie cutter
pre-heat oven to 400
spray foil and place on cookie sheet
toss potatoes in oil
cook for 15 minutes
