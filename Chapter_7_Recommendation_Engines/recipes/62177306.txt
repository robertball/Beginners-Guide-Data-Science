Title:
Neopolitan Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground pork, veal or beef
1/2 cup  chopped onion
1/2 Cup grated Swiss cheese
1/2 Cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 Egg, beaten
1/4 Tsp Tabasco sauce
1 1/2 Tsp salt
2 TBsp chopped parsley
BISCUIT DOUGH
2 cUPS AP flour
3 Tsp baking powder
1 Tsp salt
1/3 cup salad oil
2/3 cup milk
1 egg yolk, slightly beaten

Directions:
Cook meat and onion over low heat until meat is no longer pink.  Do not overcook.  Stir with a fork while cooking to break  up meat.  Remove from heat and cool.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Mix in cheeses , egg, tabasco sauce , salt and parsley.     Place 1 square (1/2) of biscuit dough, spread out, in the bottom of a 9x9-inch greased pan.  Spread the meat mixture over the dough/ cover with the other square (1/2) of dough.  Brush the top with egg yolk.  Bake at 400 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.
BISCUIT DOUGH
Sift 2 cups AP flour, 3 Tsp baking powder and 1 Tsp salt into a mixing bowl.  Pour 1/3 cup salad oil, and 2/3 cup milk into a measuring cup, but do not mix.  Add all at once to the flour mixture.  Stir with a fork until mixture cleans the sides of the bowl and rounds into a ball.  Smoooth by kneading 10 times without additional flour. Divide in half.  With dough on waxed paper, pat each half into a 9x9-inch square,or roll between waxed paper sheets.
