Title:
Overnight Chicken Fruit Salad II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cubed cooked chicken
1 pound seedless red grapes, halved
1 cup sliced celery
1 (8 ounce) can sliced water chestnuts, drained
1 large can pineapple chunks, drained
1 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon curry powder
1 cup slivered almonds, toasted
Papaya, sliced
Avocado Sliced

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine chicken, grapes, water chestnuts and pineapple. Combine mayonnaise, soy sauce, lemon juice and curry powder. Pour over chicken mixture; toss lightly until coated. Cover and chill 8 hours or overnight. Stir in almonds just before serving.  Garnish with papaya and avocado.
