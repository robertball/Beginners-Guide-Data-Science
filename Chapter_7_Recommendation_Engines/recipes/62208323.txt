Title:
Spicy Puttanesca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of whole or diced tomatoes
1/2 can of tomato paste
3-5 garlic cloves, minced
1 tin of anchovies
1/2 yellow or red onion, chopped
Olive Oil for cooking
Dash of dried parsley and basil
2 palmfuls of capers
Splash of red wine vinegar
Splash of lemon juice
Crushed Red Pepper flakes
10-20 kalamata olives , chopped with a little juice
10 green pimento olives, chopped with a little juice
2-3 spoonfuls of ricotta (optional)
8 oz. of pasta

Directions:
Heat olive oil in deep skillet.
Brown garlic in pan, and add anchovies until they start to break up. Add red pepper flakes right before you...
Add tomotoes, paste, olives, onions, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, capers, parsley and basil.
Stir and simmer for 10-15 minutes while you cook the pasta al dente.
At last minute, add in ricotta to make a creamier sauce.

Submitter: 
LaurenSyd
Description: 
Tangy puttanesca sauce, richened with anchovies and kalamata olives.
