Title:
Peach Bowl Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/4 cup all-purpose flour
6 TBL sugar
1/4 tsp salt
9 TBL cold butter, cut into small cubes
1 package (8 oz) cream cheese
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
1/4 tsp almond extract
1 cup whipped topping
4 medium peaches, peeled and diced

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar and salt. Cut in butter until crumbly. Press onto an ungreased 12" pizza pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 15-18 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool on a wire rack.
In a small bowl, beat the cream cheese, confectioners' sugar and extract until smooth; fold in whipped topping. Spread over crust. Arrange the peaches on top. Refrigerate leftovers.
