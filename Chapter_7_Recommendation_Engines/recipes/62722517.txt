Title:
Tex Mex Black Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 teaspoon vegetable oil
1 chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 can diced tomatoes, undrained
1/2 cup spicy picante sauce
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon chili powder
red pepper sauce to taste
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack or cheddar cheese
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro (1 tsp dried)
1 tablespoon fresh lime juice (skipped)
1/2 block light cream cheese

Directions:
In a medium size frying pan, heat oil over a medium heat. Saute onion until soft; add garlic and saute 30 sec.  Stir in tomatoes, beans, picante sauce, cumin, chili powder, pepper sauce, and cilantro into the frying pan.  Stir and simmer until bubbling.  Add cream cheese and cheddar; reduce heat to low and stir until melted through.
Serve with light sour cream and tortilla chips.
