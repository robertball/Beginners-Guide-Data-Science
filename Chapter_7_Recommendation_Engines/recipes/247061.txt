Title:
Coconut Minis Vanilla Sandwich Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3547832.jpg
Ingredients:
12 So Delicious® Coconut Milk Minis Vanilla Sandwiches
1 (9 ounce) tub So Delicious® Dairy Free CocoWhip
1/2 cup berries (raspberries, blackberries, strawberries or cherries)
Drizzle of Jen's Zen vegan chocolate sauce

Directions:
Start by assembling 6 sandwiches side by side.
Top with CocoWhip and layer with another 6 sandwiches: repeat. Top with raspberries, blackberries, strawberries or cherries.
Finish with a drizzle of Jen's Zen vegan chocolate sauce.

Submitter: 
So Delicious® Dairy Free
Description: 
Life doesn't always go as planned. Sometimes, fun has to happen on short notice. This gorgeous dairy-free dessert tastes astoundingly good and comes together in a matter of minutes. Your kids can even help you make it! How fun is that?
