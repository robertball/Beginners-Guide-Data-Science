Title:
Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks butter
2 1/4 c bread flour
1t salt
1t baking soda
1/4c sugar
1 1/4 c brown sugar
1 egg
1 egg yolk
2T milk
1 1/2t vanilla
2c chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Melt butter in saucepan over low heat.
Sift together flour, salt, and soda.
Cream sugar and melted butter.
Add eggs, vanilla, milk and mix.
Add dry mix and chips
Bake 14 minutes at 375.
