Title:
Baileys B52 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980678.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ounces Baileys® Irish Cream
1/4 ounce Godiva® Cappuccino Liqueur
1/4 ounce Grand Marnier liqueur

Directions:
Layer Baileys(R) Irish Cream, Godiva(R) Cappuccino Liqueur, and Grand Marnier liqueur into a shot glass to serve.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Adding a touch of richness to any drink, Baileys® Irish cream liqueur mixes fine Irish whiskey, fresh dairy cream and a hint of chocolate flavors.
