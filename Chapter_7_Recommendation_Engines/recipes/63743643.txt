Title:
Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb bag of hash brown potatoes
1/2 cup melted butter
1/4 tsp pepper
1 tsp salt
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 can cream soup
1 cup milk
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup grated cheese

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and bake at 350 degrees for about 60 minutes (covered)
Remove cover and bake an additional 10 minutes
