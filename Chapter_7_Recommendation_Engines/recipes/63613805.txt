Title:
Red Guajillo Chile Popcorn with Spicy Dark Chocolate Drizzle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1005186.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 C popcorn kernels
1 C salted peanuts (optional)
1 C packed brown sugar
1/2 C vegetable oil
1/2 C corn syrup
1/2 C Herdez Red Guajillo Chile Mexican Cooking Sauce
1/2 tsp ground cayenne pepper
1 tsp hot sauce, such as Valentina
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 C dark chocolate chips
1 tsp chili powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees.
Pop popcorn and spread in a greased 9x13 cake pan.  Spread peanuts over popcorn.
Combine brown sugar, oil, corn syrup, cooking sauce, cayenne pepper, and hot sauce in a medium saucepan.  Heat to boiling.  Cook additional 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Remove pan from heat, stir in baking soda.  Pour sauce mixture over popcorn and toss to coat.
Place popcorn mixture in oven.  Bake for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.
Spread popcorn on wax paper and allow to cool.
Place chocolate chips and chili powder in a microwave-safe bowl.  Heat for 30 seconds at 50% power.  Stir.  Continue to microwave at 50% power in 15 second intervals until mixture is melted and combined.
Drizzle mixture over cooled popcorn.  Allow chocolate to harden before storing or serving.
