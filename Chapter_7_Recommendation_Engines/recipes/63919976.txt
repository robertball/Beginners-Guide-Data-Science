Title:
Taco Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 eggs
1 cup sweet onion, diced
1 cup yellow bell pepper, diced
1 Tbsp. butter
1 tsp. taco seasoning
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt butter in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat.
Add onions and peppers with a pinch of salt and cook until softened.
Meanwhile, whisk together 12 eggs and slowly sprinkle in taco seasoning.
Pour eggs into pan and combine with onions and peppers.
Cook until eggs begin to set on the bottom of the pan.
Lift the edges of the omelet with a spatula and let the uncooked part of the eggs flow toward the edges. Continue cooking (lifting edges as needed)until the center of the omelet looks done.
Cut into pie slices and serve.
