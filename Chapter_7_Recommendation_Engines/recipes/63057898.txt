Title:
Cafe Mocha Chip-Filled Amish Friendship Bread Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/769250.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Amish Friendship Bread Starter
3 eggs
1 cup oil
1/2 cup milk
1 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
4 tablespoons International Cafe Mocha Mix
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups flour
2 small (4 ounce) boxes instant cream cheese pudding
1 cup mini chocolate chips
1 container of whipped chocolate frosting

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325° F (165° C).
In a large mixing bowl, add ingredients as listed.
Grease muffin tins or line with cupcake wrappers.
Pour the batter evenly into muffin tins.
Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center of the cupcake comes out clean.
Let cool.
Use the “cone method” to fill the cupcakes with frosting (see Kitchen Notes below).
Use same frosting to top the cupcakes.
ENJOY!
