Title:
Italian Crock-Pot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Chicken breast (boneless/skinless)
1 8oz. Cream Cheese (softened)
1 Can, Cream of Chicken
1 Can, Cream of Celery
1 Envelope of Italian dressing seasoning
1 Cup of water

Directions:
Lay chicken breast in bottom of Crock-Pot. In a bowl mix, Cream of Chicken, Cream of Celery, Italian dressing packet, and cup of water. Mix well. Pour over chicken breast in Crock-Pot.
Cook on low for 8 hours.
At the 7th hour, add cream cheese.
Cook one more hour.
Stir after 30 minutes and before serving.
Serves well over rice, pasta, etc.
