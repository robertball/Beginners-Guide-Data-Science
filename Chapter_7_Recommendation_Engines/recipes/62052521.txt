Title:
Grandmama Fuller's Red Velvet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cake:
2 1/2 cups self-rising flour
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp vinegar
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp cocoa
1 cup buttermilk
1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
2 eggs
1 ounce red food coloring
Frosting:
1 stick butter
1 box confectioners sugar
1 cup chopped nuts(your preference, I use pecans)
8 ounces cream cheese
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Cake:
Blend all ingredients except food coloring and vanilla for about 3 minutes.  Gradually beat in food coloring and vanilla.  Bake in 2 layers at 350 degrees for 35 minutes or til toothpick inserted in middle comes out clean.
Frosting:
Cream, cream cheese and sugar.  Add butter slowly, and beat well after each addition.  When well blended, add vanilla and nuts.  Spread on cold cake.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
I don't like red velvet cake, and I will eat this.  I have never had a problem with it turning out and I am not the greatest cook.  But people are always offering to pay me to cook this one.
