Title:
Cappuccino Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRUST
2 c. graham cracker crumbs
1/3 c. sugar
1 T. coffee liquor (Kahlua)
1/2 tsp. instant coffee
6 T. butter, melted
FILLING
4 pkg. cream cheese, softened
1 c. sugar
2 T. flour
2 1/2 tsp. vanilla
4 eggs
3 T + 1 tsp. coffee liquor (Kahlua)
1 T instant coffee
GARNISH
21 whole toasted almonds
1 square Baker's semi-sweet chocolate
1/4 tsp. coffee liquor (Kahlua)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325
For Crust:
Mix coffee liquor and instant coffee into melted butter then add in crumbs
Press onto bottom and up sides of 9" springform pan.
For filling:
Combine instant coffee and liquor and set aside
Beat cream cheese, sugar, flour and vanilla together (do not overmix this will make cheesecake dry)
Add eggs one at a time making sure to scrape down the bowl until blended
Add in coffee mixture and blend well
Pour into crust
Bake 60 to 70 minutes or until center is just barely set, Cool 2 hours on counter and then refrigerate minimum of 6 hours overnight is better
Garnish as directed
