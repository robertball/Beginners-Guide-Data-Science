Title:
creole beef with rice (or macaroni) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground or chopped beef
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 c celery, chopped
1 tbsp flour
1 c canned tomatoes without juices
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
1/2 c water
2 c rice or macaroni, cooked

Directions:
Brown meat.  Add onion and celery.  Brown lightly.  Sprinkle flour over meat.  Then add tomatoes, seasonings and water. Stir and simmer for 10 minutes.
Serve over rice or macaroni.

Submitter: 
MICHELLE_F
Description: 
This is a favorite of ours when we're looking for a quick but tasty dinner after work.  It is such a recipe that you can make slight variations depending on ingredients on hand.
