Title:
Skinny Girl No-Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/954140.jpg
Ingredients:
7oz. lean ground beef (at least 90/10 or better)
2 cups diced tomatoes
4 tablespoons minced onion (fresh or dried)
2 teaspoon minced garlic
1 teaspoon chili powder
Tad of garlic powder
Tad of onion powder
Salt and pepper to taste
*Optional: Hot sauce of choice

Directions:
In a stock pot, lightly brown the ground beef, breaking it up as it cooks.
Add garlic and mix well.
Then add seasoning mixture (chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder) mixing well.
Add tomatoes and water once ground beef is just cooked.
Mix well and bring to a light boil.
Stir and reduce to a simmer for 1-15 minutes.
Turn off heat and let sit for 1-15 minutes allowing the spices to penetrate the ground beef.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
*Spice it up with your choice of hot sauce if preferred.

Submitter: 
Sadie Melgoza
Description: 
No-Bean Skinny Girl Chili is a healthy low-cal/low-carb dieting alternative to regular chili omitting the beans.
