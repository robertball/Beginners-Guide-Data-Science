Title:
Italian Herb Dipping Oil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Filippo Berio Extra Virgin Olive Oil
10 grinds McCormick® Italian Herb Seasoning Grinder
1 1/2 teaspoons grated Parmesan cheese
McCormick® Sea Salt Grinder

Directions:
Mix all ingredients. Serve on small plates or bowls with your favorite crusty bread.

Submitter: 
Filippo Berio Olive Oil
Description: 
Dip crusty bread into a delicious dip of olive oil flavored with Italian herb seasonings, Parmesan, and sea salt.
