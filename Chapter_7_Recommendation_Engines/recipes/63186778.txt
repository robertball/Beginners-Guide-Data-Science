Title:
Shrimp Portobello Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/820633.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 Small Onion thinly sliced
1 Portobello Mushroom diced
1 lb Small raw shrimp, tails & shell removed
1 15oz. Jar Alfredo sauce
1 24oz. Jar Marinara sauce
1/2 cup Ricotta cheese
24 oz. Mozzarella cheese shredded
9 oz Package flat no boil lasagna noodles

Directions:
Saute onions & mushrooms in olive oil on medium high, about 5 minutes.
Add shrimp to onions and cook until pink, about 1-2 minutes. Set aside.
In bowl combine alfredo and marinara sauces, mix well.
Pour 1 cup of sauce mix into bottom of a 9"x13" lasagna pan.
Layer ingredients as follows: noodles, ricotta cheese, shrimp mix, matzarella cheese.
Repeat layering 2 more times, using up all of the ingredients evenly.
Bake at 350 for 45 minutes, let rest 10 minutes.
Serve with garlic bread and a green salad.
