Title:
Green Beans, Carrots and More Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1" fresh minced ginger
4  minced green onions
1 1/2 Tbsp. butter
2 strips of cooked crumbled bacon -OPTIONAL
1 Tbsp. light Agave or honey
1 leaf shredded kale with rib removed - about 1 cup
1 cup carrots cut as circles
1 cup fresh green beans cut in 1" pieces
2 Tbsp. ground almonds
salt to taste

Directions:
Place green beans and carrots in pot and bring to a boil.  Boil for 15 minutes. Drain.
While green beans and carrots are boiling, saute onions and ginger in butter until tender. About 10 minutes.
Stir in bacon, agave, kale, and almonds into sauteed onions then remove from heat.
Add the kale/onion mixture to green beans and carrots.  Stir.  Salt to taste.
Serves 4-5 as a side dish.

Description: 
Great way to get kale into your diet without the bitter taste.
