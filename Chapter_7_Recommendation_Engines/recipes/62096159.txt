Title:
Easy Cheesy Broccoli with Olive Oil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Bunch (1 Lb.) Fresh Broccoli
1 1/2 Tbsp Olive Oil
1 1/2 Cloves Garlic, Minced
1/2 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
1 1/2 Tbsp. Fresh Lemon Juice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Wash Broccoli, removing tough ends and split boccoli into seperate flowerets
Cut pieces so they are no more than 3 inches long
Put broccoli into steamer and steam approx. 10 minutes or until it's bright green and tender-crisp
Transfer to serving dish
Heat olive oil with garlic and pour heated oil and garlic over broccoli
Sprinkle with parmesan cheese, lemon juice and salt and pepper
Serve Immediately

Description: 
A Quick and flavorful way to eat freshly steamed broccoli.
