Title:
Better than Applebees Spinach & Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/816986.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) box frozen chopped spinach , thawed
1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts , drained and roughly chopped
1 cup shredded Asiago cheese
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
10 ounces prepared alfredo sauce
1 teaspoon minced garlic
4 ounces softened cream cheese
pepper (optional)
For an extra creamy dip - use a larger jar of Alfredo Sauce - for an extra kick use a garlic flavored Alfredo Sauce.

Directions:
1) Thaw Spinach (Do not pay dry)
2) Soften & Blend Cheeses
3) Mix in Alfredo Sauce
4) Combine Ingredients into an 8x8 baking dish
5) Bake 25 - 30 Minutes or until cheese bubbles
6) Turn on Broil for 2 - 3 minutes for golden texture

Submitter: 
Fireman Greg
Description: 
If your a fan of hot dips, and love the famous Spinach & Artichoke Dip from Applebees - we have managed (after several trial & error sessions) to master and improve the famous Applebees Spinach & Artichoke dip.   This is a REAL HIT, is quick, easy, and an affordable dip for you next party or gathering.
