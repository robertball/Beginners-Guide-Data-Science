Title:
Pinto Beans with Chorizo and Pico deGallo over Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Red beans
1 pound dried red pinto beans
1 poind Chorizo sausage
1 large onion (chopped)
2 cloves garlic (chopped)
1 jalepeno pepper (1/2 seeds removed and chopped)
9 cups water
2 vegetable bullion cubes
1 Tbsp. chili powder
1/2 Tbsp. cumin
1 Tbsp. Cajun seasoning
1 tsp salt
Rice
1 1/2 cups brown rice
3 cups water
Pico de Gallo
3 Roma tomaotes (seeded and chopped)
1 sweet onion (chopped)
1 clove garlic (chopped)
1 jalepeno (1/2 seeded and chopped)
juice of 1 lime
1 Tbsp fresh cilantro (chopped)

Directions:
Red Beans
Sort and rinse beans, place in crock pot. Add water, bullion cubes, onion, garlic, jalepeno, and seasonings. Brown Chorizo, drain on paper towels, and add to pot. Cook on low 8-10 hours.
Rice
Place rice and water in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer 35 minutes. Remove from heat and let stand 10 minutes. Fluff with a fork.
Pico De Gallo
Combine all ingredients.
Serve beans over rice. Top beans with pico de gallo.

Submitter: 
cookbookangie
Description: 
Slow-cooked pinto beans and sausage are topped with pico de gallo and served over rice for a comforting dish.
