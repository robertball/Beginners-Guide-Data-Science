Title:
Low-Carb Chocolate Coconut Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4576983.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter, softened
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder, sifted
1/4 cup low-calorie natural sweetener (such as SwerveÂ®)
1 teaspoon sugar-free vanilla extract
5 tablespoons unsweetened coconut cream, or as needed

Directions:
Whisk butter, cocoa powder, sweetener, and vanilla extract in a bowl with an electric mixer until well combined. Beat in coconut cream, 1 tablespoon at a time, until desired frosting consistency is reached.
