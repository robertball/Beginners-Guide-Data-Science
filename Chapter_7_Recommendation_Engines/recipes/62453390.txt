Title:
Mexican Rice - Restaurant Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/587340.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups long grain white rice, rinsed
1/4 cup canola oil
1 yellow onion cut into 1 inch chunks
3 cloves of garlic, minced
3/4 cup tomato puree
2 tsp sea salt
2 3/4 cups of chicken broth
1 bell pepper cut into 1 inch chunks *optional
1 carrot, diced *optional
1 tomato, diced *optional

Directions:
In a large frying pan add the oil and put heat on medium high. Add the rice and saute until golden brown approximately 10 minutes.
Add the onion and bell pepper and saute another 5 minutes, making sure the rice is toasted to a nice golden brown.
Add the minced garlic. Continue to saute for an additional 3 minutes.
Add the tomato puree, chicken broth, carrots, tomato, and salt. Stir and turn heat up to high. Bring to a full boil while stirring. Continue to boil for 2 minutes.
Reduce heat to simmer, cover and let simmer for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
ANGELSTAR
Description: 
This is a very flavorful easy rice, made from scratch. It tastes just like your local Mexican Restaurant. Add tomotoes, bell pepper and carrots if you like.
