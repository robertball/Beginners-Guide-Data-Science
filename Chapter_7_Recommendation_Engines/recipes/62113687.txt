Title:
Potatoe Crunchy Tenders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. of chicken tenders
1 package of instant garlic potatoes
1 1/2 cups of milk
1 egg
2 tsps salt
2 tsps pepper
1/2 cup oil

Directions:
1. In large fry pan preheat oil.
2. In a mixing bowl combine milk and egg wisk together.
3. Combine instant potatoes and salt and pepper.
4. Soak chicken in milk mixture.
5. Dip into potatoe mixture.
6. Fry in oil for about 7-10 min.
7. Place on paper towels to drain off exess oil.
