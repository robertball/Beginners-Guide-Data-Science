Title:
Salsa Verde Chicken Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cup shredded rotisserie chicken
1 cup green salsa
1/4 cup minced cilantro
Juice of 1 lime
8 taco shells

Directions:
In a saucepan over med-low heat, combine the chicken, salsa, cilantro and lime. Bring to a low simmer. Serve in taco shells with desired toppings.
