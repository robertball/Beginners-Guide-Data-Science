Title:
Greek Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
500 gr. frozen fyllo pastry
150 gr. fetta cheese
150 gr. yellow cheese (edam)
200 gr. greek-type yoghurt
oreganon
1 egg beaten

Directions:
Defrost the fyllo pastry and grate the cheese. Mix the fetta with the grated cheese and the yoghurt. Add oreganon as preferred. Place one fyllo pastry in a well-oiled baking tray and pour in the cheese mixture.  Cover with the second fyllo pastry and close the edges carefully.  Make holes over the pies with a fork and brush with the beaten egg. Bake for about 30-40 min.  Once cool cut into square pieces.

Submitter: 
GATOULA
Description: 
Easy to make, devine to eat! Ideal for tea parties, picnics or as an accompaniment to a meal.
