Title:
Cola Pot Roast By deb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes
1 cup cola-flavored carbonated beverage
2 tsp Italian seasoning
1 6 oz can tomato paste
3 tsp minced onion
3/4 cup chopped celery
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
1 tsp frahm seasoning
3 pounds beef chuck roast or tenderized round steak
32 oz box beef broth

Directions:
In a large bowl, break up tomatoes in their juice.  Stir in cola, broth, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, onion, celery, salt, and garlic salt. Stir until sauce mix is dissolved.  Place meat in slow cooker, laying flat.   Pour tomato mixture over meat. Cook on high for 2 hrs  And then reduce heat to low. Simmer slowly for about 2 1/2 hours, or until meat is tender.
