Title:
Breakfast Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 15.25oz cans tropical fruit salad (or fruit cocktail)
2 large bananas
3 6oz containers Orange Cream Yogurt

Directions:
Drain fruit cans thorougly; place fruit in bowl
Slice bananas and add to bowl
Stir in all 3 containers yogurt
Chill in covered container at least 1 hour
Serve

Submitter: 
Candice
Description: 
Light and fruity, I usually serve along side big Brunch meals as the fruit dish. Also great with wholewheat toast, Melba toast or english muffin and a cup of coffe for a lighter, nutritional and filling breakfast. Great as a snack and kids love it!
