Title:
Dutch Macaroni, Ham and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked macaroni
2 cups diced ham
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup shredded Gouda cheese
1 cup shredded farmers cheese
1 cup cubed velvetta cheese
3 tablespoons parmesan cheese
1 finely diced onion
2 tablespoons flour
1/2 stick of butter
salt and pepper to taste
a few dashes of Maggi seasoning
Topping -
1 stick of melted butter
1 1/2 cup Italian bread crumbs

Directions:
Place the butter in a pot along with the onions.  Cook until the onions are soft.  Add the flour and cook about a minute.  Add the milk and cook until the flour mixture is incorporated.  Add all the cheeses, (I use a food processor to help me shred the cheese) ham, salt, pepper and Maggi and cook over medium to low heat until the cheeses are melted.  Place the cooked macaroni and the cheese mixture in a casserol dish stirring well to meld the mixture together with the macaroni.
Combine the melted butter and bread crumbs together and crumble over the top of the casserole.
Place in a 400 degree oven for about 15 minutes or the top is golden brown.
ENJOY!!!!!!!

Submitter: 
Linda Davis Womble
Description: 
This recipe was given to me by my Dutch Mother.  It is best when you use the leftover Christmas ham.  I tweeked it a little to US ingredients.
