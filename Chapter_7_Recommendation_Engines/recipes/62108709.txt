Title:
BBQ Pork or Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
MEAT:
5 pound pork shoulder roast OR beef brisket
1 large onion, quartered,
3 teaspoons garlic salt
1 teaspoon pepper
BBQ Sauce:
1 large bottle of "Montgomery Inn" BBQ sauce
2/3 cup of ketchup
34 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup dried minced onions
1/2 teaspoon of pepper
2 tablespoons freshly minced garlic
5 Tablespoons worcestershire sauce
Salt to taste

Directions:
MEAT:
Place meat in a large, heavy sauce pan or crockpot with garlic salt, pepper, and quartered onion.  Simmer until the meat falls off the bone. (3 to 4 hours on stovetop or 8 to 9 hours in a crockpot.)
Drain meat and allow it to cool.
Remove the fat and pull all the meat off into shreds.
SAUCE:
In a heavy saucepan on medium heat, mix all the sauce ingredients together.  Stir until butter is melted.
Reduce heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add desired amount of sauce to the meat and simmer for at least 30 minutes.
ENJOY!

Submitter: 
Linda B. II
Description: 
This recipe works great with beef or pork.  I make up extra sauce to use on my ribs and chicken, too.  It all freezes well.  Enjoy it at picnics, BBQ's, potlucks, or parties.
