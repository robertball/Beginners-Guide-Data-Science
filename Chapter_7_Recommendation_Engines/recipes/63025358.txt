Title:
Rum Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6 oz Bag of semisweet chocolate chips
3 tabs light corn syrup
1/2 cup dark rum or bourbon
2.5 cups vanilla-wafer crumbs crushed
1/2 cup confectioner's sugar
1 cup finely chopped walnuts

Directions:
Melt the chocolate in a sausepan over low heat.  Add the corn syrup and liquior and stir.  Take the pan off the heat and add the rest of the ingredients.  Mix everything together and let it sit for about 30 minutes until all things have firmed up a bit.  Form the mixture into balls and roll them through the sugar.  Make them a few days in advance to let them season.
