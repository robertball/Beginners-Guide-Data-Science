Title:
Fish Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 5oz can drained tuna
1 cup corn
4 tablespoons sour cream
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
2 teaspoons garlic salt
2 teaspoons pepper
5 flour tortillas
1 diced tomato

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
Combine tuna, corn sour cream, cheese, salt and pepper in bowl and set aside
Set tortillas in oven for 2 minutes on each side
Spread tuna mixture onto tortillas and sprinkle with tomatoes
Fold tortillas in half (taco style)
Heat in microwave on HIGH for 3o seconds
STUFF YOUR FISH TACO EATIN FACE
