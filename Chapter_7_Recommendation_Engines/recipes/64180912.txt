Title:
Fall Harvest Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1503489.jpg
Ingredients:
All ingredients are Organic
1lb of organic ground turkey
1 organic onion chopped
28 oz organic fire roasted diced tomatoes
6 oz can of organic tomato paste
15 oz can of Organic Great Northern Beans (drained)
15 oz can of Organic Kidney Beans (drained)
15 oz can of Organic Black Beans
15 oz can of Organic Butternut squash
15 oz can of Organic Pumpkin
1 tsp organic garlic salt
1 tsp organic garlic powder
1/2 tsp organic cinnamon
2 tsp of organic chili powder
1/2 tsp of organic pepper

Directions:
In a dutch oven (or pot)
cook meat and onion
add garlic salt, garlic powder, chili powder, and pepper
add tomato paste, pumpkin and butternut squash
stir till blended
add diced tomatoes
add kidney beans
add great northern beans
add black beans
Heat and Serve
(you may want to add more season to taste)
