Title:
steel cut oats with apples and pecans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup almond milk
1 cup quick cooking steel cut oats
2 apples cored and chopped
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
4 1/2 tsp honey, agave nectar or organic maple syrup
1/3 Cup chopped pecans or any nuts (toasted)

Directions:
combine the almond milk, 1 cup water and oats. Bring to a boil and then reduce heat to low and cover. Simmer until most of the liquid is absorbed 10-12 minutes. Let stand for 5 minutes.
While oatmeal is cooking place the apples, cinnamon and 1/2 tsp of maple syrup and 1 tbsp water. Cook until apples are soft 3-5 minutes. stir in nuts and pour over oats.
