Title:
Iowa Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb ground beef
1 package taco seasoning
1 medium onion, chopped
8oz chopped green chilies
16oz sour cream
2 cans cream of chicken soup
12 flour tortillas
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 small tomato, chopped
1 1/2 cup shredde lettuce
1/4 cup black olives, sliced

Directions:
1. Brown and drain ground beef, taco seasoning, onion and half of green chilies. Cook for 6 to 8 minutes until beef is browned.
2. In a medium pan warm together sour cream, soup and remaining green chilies.
3. Pour 1/2 of sour cream mixture into a 9 x 13 inch pan.
4. Fill tortillas with meat mixture. Roll up and place close together in pan. Pour remaining cream mixture over top. Top with cheddar cheese.
5. Bake in 350 degree oven for 35-45 minutes until hot and bubbly.
6. Top with tomato, lettuce and black olives.

Submitter: 
farmerbrown
Description: 
Easy ground beef enchiladas that can please a crowd. They travel to potlucks well and also freeze well. My family love these!
