Title:
Chicken with a Twist Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell's® Condensed Cheddar Cheese Soup
1 cup Pace® Picante Sauce
1/4 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 (8.75 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1 1/2 cups cubed cooked chicken or turkey
4 cups hot cooked corkscrew-shaped pasta

Directions:
Mix soup, picante sauce, milk, garlic, corn, chicken and pasta in saucepot. Heat through.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Cheddar cheese soup and Pace® Picante Sauce make a convenient flavorful sauce that clings to the corkscrew pasta. Add cooked chicken or turkey--and it's a hearty quick-fix meal.
