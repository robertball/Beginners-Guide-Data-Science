Title:
Korean Spiced Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2300068.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbl sesame oil
1 lb ground beef
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 tsp ground ginger
1/2 - 1 tsp crushed red pepper
salt & pepper
chopped green onions (optional)

Directions:
Heat a large skillet over medium heat.  Add sesame oil and when hot, brown ground beef with garlic. Drain.
Stir in brown sugar, soy sauce ginger and red pepper (amount depending on how spicy you want it).  Simmer to blend.
Serve over rice with green onions as garnish.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Looking for a way to break the up the monotony of the same old, same old?  Here is a fast and simple way to use up a pound of ground beef.
