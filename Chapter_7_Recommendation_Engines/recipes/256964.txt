Title:
Creamy Shrimp Dip  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4488526.jpg
Ingredients:
6 ounces cream cheese, softened
1 (4 ounce) can small deveined shrimp
1/3 cup mayonnaise
3 tablespoons chile sauce
1 tablespoon finely chopped onion
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon lemon juice

Directions:
Combine cream cheese, shrimp, mayonnaise, chile sauce, onion, Worcestershire sauce, and lemon juice in a bowl; beat with an electric mixer until well mixed.
