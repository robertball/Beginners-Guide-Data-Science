Title:
Roasted Butternut Squash & Apple Bisque Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2697305.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Lb. Butternut Squash peeled and chopped
2 Tsp Olive Oil
2 Granny Smith Apple, Peeled & Chopped
1/2 Medium Yellow Onion, Chopped
2 Tb Minced Garlic
1/2 Tb Sage
1/2 Tsp. Dried Thyme
1 1/2 C. Chicken Broth
1 1/2 C. Water
1/4 C. Half and half cream
Salt & Pepper
Sour cream
Bacon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425
1.  Bake squash on foil for 45 minutes
2.  While squash bakes, saute onion, sage, thyme, garlic and apple in oil for 5-7 minutes  in dutch oven
3.  Add roasted squash, broth, water, salt and pepper to onion mixture and bring to boil.  Simmer for 10 minutes
4.  Remove from heat.  Add cream and blend using immession blender until smooth.
5.  Serve with a dollop of sour cream and bacon pieces
