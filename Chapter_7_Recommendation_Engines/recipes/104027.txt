Title:
Ruby Red Chiller Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
3 cups Ruby red grapefruit juice
1 cup water
2 Ruby red grapefruits, peeled, sectioned, and chopped
2 cups mineral water, chilled
Optional Garnishes: fresh mint sprigs and strips of lemon peel

Directions:
Combine first 4 ingredients, stirring until SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener dissolves. Add grapefruit, cover and chill.
Add mineral water just before serving. Garnish, if desired. Serve immediately.
