Title:
Balsamic Vinaigrette Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
2 teaspoons dark brown sugar, optional*
1 tablespoon chopped garlic
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
3/4 cup olive oil

Directions:
Put all ingredients in screw top jar and shake well. Refrigerate at least 30 minutes prior to use for best flavor.

Submitter: 
Kerleygirl
Description: 
This is Emeril's recipe for balsamic vinaigrette.  It's good! I deviate from his recipe by using raspberry balsamic and I don't add the sugar.
