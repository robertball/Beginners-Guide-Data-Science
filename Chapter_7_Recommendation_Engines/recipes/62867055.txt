Title:
Salsa, Sour Cream & Cream Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. cream cheese (softened)
1 tub sour cream
1 bottle Thick & Chunky salsa
1 ½ cups shredded cheddar and/or mozzarella cheese
2 tomatoes, seeded, finely chopped
1 small iceburg lettuce, broken into bite-sized pieces
¼ cup green onion, chopped (optional)

Directions:
Mix together softened cream cheese and sour cream. Spread evenly in small casserole dish. Pour salsa over top, spreading evenly. Sprinkle with cheese. Sprinkle chopped tomato, lettuce and green onion on top.
Chill. Serve with nacho chips on the side.
