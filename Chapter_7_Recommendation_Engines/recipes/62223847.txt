Title:
Kaili's Hearty Slow Cooker Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 lb. mild italian sausage
1 can tomato soup
1 6 oz can tomato paste
1 28 oz. can diced tomatoes
3/4 c. frozen corn
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1/2 medium onion, diced
1 large can bean medley
2 cloves garlic
1.5 teaspoon oregano
1.5 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 c. chili powder

Directions:
Brown ground beef on medium-high heat. Add the 2 cloves of garlic.
While ground beef is cooking, chop sausage into bite size pieces. Add to slow cooker.
Add corn, onion, red pepper, tomato soup, and tomato paste onto sausage and stir together.
Add ground beef to the slow cooker. Stir in the diced tomatoes, oregano, black pepper, salt, and chili powder.
Cook on low for 8 hours.

Description: 
A yummy beginner chili. Easy to make and you can tweak it to your own tastes.
