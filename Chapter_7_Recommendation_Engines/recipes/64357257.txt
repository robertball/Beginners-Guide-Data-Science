Title:
BBQ Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pork ribs
1 medium onion, chopped
Salt & pepper to taste
1 bottle BBQ sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Place pork ribs in roaster.  Season with chopped onion, salt and pepper.
Bake , covered, for 1 hour.
Drain fat.  Add BBQ sauce and bake for 1-1/2 hours or until ribs are tender, stirring occasionally.
