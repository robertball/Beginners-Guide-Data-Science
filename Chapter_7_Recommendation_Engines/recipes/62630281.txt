Title:
Perfect No-Boil Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1032187.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 - 2 pound ground beef
1 large chopped onion
8 cloves garlic, crushed
2 15oz can tomato sauce
2 14.5 oz cans crushed tomatoes
1 10.75 oz condensed tomato soup
1/2 cup water
2 tsp dried oregano
1 1/2 tsp dried basil leaves
2 Tbsp white sugar
2 tsp Italian seasoning
1 1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
3 Tbsp dried parsley, divided
15 lasagna noodles
3 15 oz containers ricotta cheese
3 eggs
3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided

Directions:
In a large skillet, cook ground beef, sausage, onion and garlic until browned.  Move into a 6-8 quart kettle.
Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato soup, tomato sauce and water.   Add in sugar, oregano, Italian seasoning, salt and 2 Tbsp parsley, basil and pepper.   Simmer, covered, for 1 1/2 hour, stirring occasionally.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
In a large bowl, combine ricotta cheese, 2 cups mozzarella cheese, 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, eggs and remaining parsley.
Spoon 1 3/4 cups of the meat sauce in the bottom of a large lasagna tray.
Add 4 noodles lenthwise and one crosswise over the meat sauce.
Spoon another 1 3/4 cups of meat sauce over the noodles.
Spread 1/2 of the cheese mixture over the meat sauce.
Repeat layers:   1  3/4 cups sauce, noodles, 1 3/4 cups sauce, remaining 1/2 of cheese mixture.
Repeat layers again, 1 3/4 cups sauce, noodles and 1 3/4 cups sauce.
Cover with tightly with foil and bake in a preheated oven for 1 hour 20 minutes.
Remove foil and sprinkle on remaining mozzarella cheese and top that with remaining Parmesan cheese.
Bake an additional 10 minutes, uncovered.  Let stand 15 minutes before serving.
