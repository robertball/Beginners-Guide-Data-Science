Title:
Applewood-Smoked Candied Bacon with Honey Habanero Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3791989.jpg
Ingredients:
16 slices applewood-smoked peppered bacon
dark brown sugar
4 habanero peppers, chopped and seeded
1 cup fresh chunked pineapple
½ cup honey
3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 clove garlic, minced
¼ teaspoon ginger powder

Directions:
Place bacon on a cookie sheet.
Bake at 350 degrees until it is just before crisp.
Pour off the grease and sprinkle dark brown sugar evenly on each slice of bacon.
Return to the oven until the bacon is crisp and the sugar has glazed.
Set aside to cool on aluminum foil.
In a pan over low, heat the pineapple, garlic, ginger powder, and lemon juice, stirring frequently until the pineapple softens.
Add the honey, habaneros and apple cider vinegar and continue to heat until the honey is dissolved.
Remove from heat and place in a blender and pulse until thin.
