Title:
Asian Inspired Broccoli & Slaw Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cloves minced garlic
1/2 cup mayo
1 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp Sriracha
1.5 lbs chopped broccoli
3.5 tbsp tamari
5 tbsp olive oil
1/3 cup crushed peanuts
8 tortillas (taco size)
1/2 lime
2 stalks sliced green onions
10 oz coleslaw mix (or shredded carrots & cabbage)
1.5 tbsp rice vinegar
2 tsp brown sugar

Directions:
Heat oven to 425(F) degrees.
Toss broccoli with 2 cloves garlic, 2 tbsp tamari, 3 tbsp olive oil, salt and pepper to taste. Roast in the oven for 20 minutes, shaking the pan after 10 minutes. Top with peanuts for the last 5 minutes.
While broccoli is cooking, dress coleslaw mix with green onions, 1.5 tbsp tamari, rice vinegar, brown sugar, & 2 tbsp olive oil.
Combine 1 clove garlic, mayo, lemon juice, and Sriracha.
Warm tortillas.
Slice lime into 4 wedges.
Enjoy broccoli tacos topped with slaw, lime juice, and Sriracha mayo.

Description: 
Sriracha Mayo, cole slaw, and roasted peanuts & broccoli come together for some great tasting tacos.
