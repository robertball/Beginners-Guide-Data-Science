Title:
Southwest Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 large onion, chopped
2 red bell peppers, chopped
4 stalks celery, chopped
28 ounce can tomatoes
8 oz can fire-roasted green chiles
1 pound dry black beans, cooked (or 4 standard cans)
4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
2 tsp cumin
2-3 Tbsp dry sherry
1/2 cup sour cream
salt to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in large pot. Saute onion, bell peppers. and celery until softened.  Add all other ingredients except sour cream.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat, simmer 30 minutes.  Using and immersion blender, blen to desired consistency.  Remove from heat and whisk in sour cream.  Adjust with salt to taste.
