Title:
Green Tomato Mincemeat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 qts. tomatoes (chopped and drained)
1 c cider vinegar
2 lbs. brown sugar
1 T lemon juice
1 t salt
1 lb raisins
1 lb currants
1 t cinnamon
1 t cloves
1 t allspice
1 t nutmeg

Directions:
Put in kettle and cover with water. Boil 1/2 hour,drain. Add cider vinegar, brown sugar, raisins and currants.
Cook until thickened. When cold add cinnamon, cloves, allspice, and nutmeg.
