Title:
Harissa Egg Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5615023.jpg
Ingredients:
•	1 ripe avocado
•	1 tsp lime juice
•	3 large eggs, boiled and peeled
•	1 tbsp harissa pasta
•	1 Tbsp mayonnaise
•	2 tsp spicy brown mustard
•	10 cornichons, chopped
•	1 spring onion, chopped
•	3 large radishes, diced
•	1 tbsp fresh coriander, minced
•	Salt and pepper, to taste
•	1 tbsp smoked paprika

Directions:
1.	In a bowl, add half the avocado and lime juice. Mash with a fork.
2.	Cut the eggs in half. Scoop out the yolks and add them to the bowl. Add the harissa paste, mayonnaise, and mustard. Mix well until creamy. Salt and pepper to taste.
3.	Add the chopped cornichons, onion, and radishes. Mix to coat the vegetables.
4.	Coarsely chop the remaining avocado half and all the egg whites. Fold into the creamy avocado mixture taking care to leave some chunks of avocado.
5.	Top with fresh coriander and smoked paprika before serving

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Boiled eggs, avocado, and harissa are stars in this spicy twist of a classic. Serve on toast or try in lettuce leaves for a refreshing touch
