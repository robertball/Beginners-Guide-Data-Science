Title:
Italian Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (19 ounces) chick peas
4 cups mixed salad greens, such as Bibb, Boston, Arrugula, and Radicchio
1 cup sliced cucumbers
1/2 cup red onion rings
1/2 cup yellow or sweet red pepper strips
1 jar (6 ounces) marinated artichoke hearts
Garlic Vinaigrette Dressing:
3/4 cup olive oil
1 1/2 teaspoons Italian seasonings, crushed
1 1/2 teaspoons minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 cup wine vinegar

Directions:
Drain chick peas. Place peas in a salad bowl along with salad greens, cucumbers, onions, yellow pepper, and artichokes. Toss lightly with Garlic Vinegrette Dressing.
Dressing:
In a sealing container, combine olive oil, Italian seasonings, garlic, salt, and pepper. Let stand 1 hour.  Add wine vinegar. Shake well. Serve over salad greens. Refrigerate any remaining salad dressing.
