Title:
Elise Jean's Orzo stuffed mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2139423.jpg
Ingredients:
large portobello mushrooms
orzo
feta
oil
red onion
garlic
sundried tomatos
spinach

Directions:
preheat oven to 425
infuse garlic with oil
scrape gills from mushrooms, coat with oil
bake in oven for 20 mins
cook orzo al dente
chop spinach, tomatos, onion
drain orzo place in large bowl. Add spinach and cover for 10 minutes.
add other veggies and oil mixture.
spice with rosemary and sea salt to taste.
place orzo mixture in mushroom.
Serve
