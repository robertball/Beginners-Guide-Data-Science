Title:
Shredded Vegetable Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/386003.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup DannonÂ® Plain Yogurt
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 tablespoon honey
1 tablespoon sesame oil
1 tablespoon chopped ginger
1 dash black pepper
1 (16 ounce) bag shredded vegetable slaw mix

Directions:
In a bowl, whisk together yogurt, lemon juice, soy, honey, oil, ginger and pepper. Add vegetable slaw mixture and toss to coat. Let slaw stand at least 20 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
DANNON
Description: 
This tangy and savory Asian-inspired slaw is made with yogurt, ginger, and sesame oil.
