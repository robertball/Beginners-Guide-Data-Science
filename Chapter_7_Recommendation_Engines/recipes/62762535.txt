Title:
Mixed Bean Rarebit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices bacon, cooked and chopped
1 onion, chopped
1 lb can red beans
1 lb can small lima beans
1 lb can pork & beans
1/4 lb Velvetta, cubed
1/2 c brown sugar
1/3 c ketchup
2 T Worchestershire

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in greased casserole dish.
Bake at 350F uncovered for 30-60 minutes until bubbly.
