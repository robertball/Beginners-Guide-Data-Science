Title:
Mushroom Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup each of finely diced red and yellow peppers
1/4 cup finely diced red onion
1 glove garlic
2 cups each sliced shiitake, oyster and brown mushrooms
1/8 teaspoon kosher salt
18 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
cayenne pepper to taste (optional)
1 spring of freshly chopped thyme
1/4 cup red wine
1/2 cup chicken jus

Directions:
spray a large saute pan with non stick spray and place over medium heat.  When hot, lightly saute peppers, onions and garlic for about 30 seconds.  Add mushrooms, stirring constantly unit tender, about 7 minutes.  Season with salt, pepper, cayenne and thyme.  Remove mixture from pan and set aside.  Deglaze pan with red wine and reduce until almost dry, about 3 minutes. Reduce heat and add chicken jus and mushroom mixture.  Bring to a boil and serve
