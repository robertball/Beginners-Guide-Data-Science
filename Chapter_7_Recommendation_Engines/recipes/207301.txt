Title:
Kahlua Cocoa Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
Balls:
1/2 cup Kahlua
1/4 cup light corn syrup
1/3 cup candied cherries, chopped
1/3 cup golden raisins, chopped
2 1/2 cups fine vanilla wafer crumbs
1 cup powdered sugar, sifted
1 cup pecans, finely chopped
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
Coatings:
Cocoa powder OR powdered sugar OR flaked coconut OR chopped or ground nuts

Directions:
In a bowl, combine Kahlua, corn syrup, cherries and raisins.  Add remaining ingredients and blend well.
Shape into small balls and roll in desired coating.
Cover and store in cool, dark place.

Submitter: 
Kahlua
Description: 
Fruity, nutty truffles, flavored with chocolate, have a secret ingredient--Kahlua. Vary the coatings for a pretty candy dish.
