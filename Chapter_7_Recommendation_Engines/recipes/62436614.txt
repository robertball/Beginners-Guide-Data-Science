Title:
Baked Potatoes On The Grill Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Potatoes (# as needed)
Olive Oil
Salt & Pepper
Pam Spray (prefer Pam Grilling Spray)
Sour Cream, Butter, Bacon Bits, etc. for potato toppings
**Need Tin-Foil**

Directions:
Rinse/scrub each potato with water, then pat dry. Use a fork to poke each potato about 8x.
Spray Only one side of tin foil with Pam Spray.
Lightly rub each potato with olive oil to coat and place onto tin foil.
Sprinkle potato with salt and pepper and wrap.
Place potatoes on pre-heated grill and cook for approximately 1 hour - turning every 10 minutes until potato can be easily pierced with a fork.
Serve with butter, sour cream, bacon bits, etc.

Submitter: 
Sarah Simons
Description: 
This is an easy way to make baked potatoes on the grill - delicious everytime!
