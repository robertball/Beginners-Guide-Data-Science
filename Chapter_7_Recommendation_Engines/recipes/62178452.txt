Title:
Chicken Salad to die for Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 4lb rotisserie chicken,pack of lipton dry veggie dip, 1 celery stalk,1/2 small onion chopped, 1 gala apple finely chopped, 15 seedless grapes cut in halves, 3 1/2 tbls of dukes mayo, 3 1/2 tbls. of sour cream, salt and pepper to taste. opt. pecans.

Directions:
break down chicken mix all ingrediants in large bowl, let stand in fridge for at least an hour. enjoy!!
