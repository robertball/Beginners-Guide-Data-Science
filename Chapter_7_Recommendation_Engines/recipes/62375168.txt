Title:
Chicken Cordon Bleu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 chicken breast halves.
6 slices ham
6 slices swiss cheese
1 1/2 C. crushed corn flakes or bread crumbs
2 T. parsley
1/2 t. paprika
1/4 C. Buttermilk

Directions:
Heat oven to 425Â°
Pound chicken breasts until thin.
Stuff with cheese and ham and fold in half.
Mix dry ingredients in one MB and pour buttermilk in the other.
Dip stuffed chicken in buttermilk.
Coat with crumb mixture.
Bake for 35 min.
