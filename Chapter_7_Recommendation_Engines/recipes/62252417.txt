Title:
Too Cool For School Pumpkin Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Box Yellow Cake Mix
1 Can (14.5 OZ) solid packed pumpkin
3/4 cup light brown sugar
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 tsp. allspice
4 eggs
For Topping:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup light brown sugar
1/2 to 3/4 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.Combine Cake mix, pumpkin, sugar, oil, cinnamon, nutmeg, and allspice. Beat for 1 minute. Add eggs. Beat 2 more minutes. Pour into greased Bundt pan.
For Topping: Melt 1/2 cup butter with 1/2 cup of sugar. Add nuts. Pour half of mixture on top of cake batter. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
Cool in pan for 15-20 minutes, then invert onto plate. When completely cool, drizzle remaining topping on cake.
