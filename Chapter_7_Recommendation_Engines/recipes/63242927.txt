Title:
Meatballs for Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup coarse fresh bread crumbs
1/4 cup milk
1/2 lb pork sausage
3/4 lb ground beef
1 egg
1/2 cup ricotta cheese
3/4 cup grated Parmigiano-Regiano cheese
1 large shallot, minced
Pinch of freshly grated nutmeg
pinch of ground cloves
Fresh marjoram leaves, chopped
2 cloves of garlic, minced
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
To make the meatballs, in a a large bowl, combine the bread crumbs and milk and mix well.  Add the sausage, beef, egg, ricotta,Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese, the shallot, nutmeg, cloves, a good sprinkling of the marjoram and garlic cloves, minced.  Mix breifly with your hands just until the ingredients are well distributed.  Season well with salt and pepper.  From the mixture nto small balls that are about one inch in diameter.
