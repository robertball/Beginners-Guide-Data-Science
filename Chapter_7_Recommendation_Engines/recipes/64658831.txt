Title:
Mexican Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean hamburger
1 tbsp olive oil
1/2 small yellow onion, finely chopped
2 tsp smoked paprika
1 tsp cumin
1 tbsp chili powder
1 tsp kosher salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
1 can (15 oz) black beans, rinsed
1 1/2 cup sweet corn
1 can (4 oz) green chillies
1 jar (10 oz) enchilada sauce
12 8" flour tortillas
12 oz Mexican cheese blend
1 can (6 oz) olives, sliced and drained
2 scallions, finely chopped
2 tbsp chopped cilantro

Directions:
In a skillet over medium heat add beef & onion brown the beef, about 5 - 7 minutes. Drain the fat.
Add paprika, cumin, chilli powder, salt, pepper, tomatoes, beans and corn, stir to combine.  Allow to cook while preparing the pan.
Smooth about 1/3 of the enchilada sauce over the bottom of a 9 x 13 pan.
Lay tortillas on the bottom of the pan, slightly overlapping until the pan is covered (about 4 tortillas).
Spoon half of the beef mixture over the tortillas, spread evenly. Layer the tortillas on the beef mixture.  Smooth about 1/3 of the enchilada sauce over the tortillas.  Sprinkle 1/2of the cheese on top of the enchilada sauce. Scatter the green chilies on the cheese.  Add the remaining beef mixture & spread evenly.  Layer tortillas on top of the beef mixture.  Spoon remaining enchilada sauce over the tortillas.  Sprinkle remaining cheese on top of the tortillas.  garnish top with olives and scallions.
Bake for 10-15 minutes, until cheese is melted and lasagne is warmed through.
Remove from oven and sprinkle with cilantro.
