Title:
Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Chicken Breasts cut into 3/4 inch pieces
Skinless Smoked Turkey Sausage (Kielbasa)
8-14 oz cooked peeled deveined shrimp
sliced celery, 1 chopped onion, 1/2 chunked green pepper
1 can diced tomatoes with basil, oregano, garlic
1/2 (8 oz) can of tomato paste
1 can chicken broth
1 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 cup instant rice
Cajun Seasoning: 1/4 tspn peper, 1/4 tspn garlic powder, 1/4 tspn onion powder, 1/8 tspn ground red pepper, 1/4 tspn paprika

Directions:
Put all ingredients in slow cooker except shrimp, rice and green pepper. Cook covered on low for 5-6 hours or hight for 2.5-3 hours.  Turn slow cooker on high and stir in shrimp, rice and green pepper. Cook 30 minutes more or until shrimp and rice are tender.

Homemade slow cooker jambalaya with chicken, turkey sausage and shrimp.
