Title:
Hot Cherry Pie drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3624182.jpg
Ingredients:
100% real cranberry juice.
1 shot of amaretto
can of whipped cream (optional)

Directions:
warm the cranberry juice on the stove or in a microwave.  ladle 8-10 ounces of juice into a coffee mug.  add a shot of amaretto.  stir.  top with whipped cream, if desired.  serve and enjoy.

Submitter: 
wynnkin
Description: 
just warm up the cranberry juice and add a shot of amaretto.  top with whipped cream if you like.  even a cherry.  this tastes more like hot cherry pie than hot cherry pie does.  8^)
