Title:
Quick Black Beans and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 onion, chopped
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, undrained
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 teaspoon cumin
1tsp chili powder
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 1/2 cups uncooked instant brown rice

Directions:
In large saucepan, heat oil over medium-high.  Add onion, cook and stir until tender.  Add beans, tomatoes, spices and garlic powder.  Bring to a boil; stir in rice.  Cover; reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes.  Remove from heat; let stand 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
sugarbean
Description: 
This ones pretty straight forward. Brown rice, black beans, stewed tomatoes, a bit of onion and oil, and in about fifteen minutes you have a hearty, delicious meal. Serves four.Modified from: Quick Black Beans and Rice
