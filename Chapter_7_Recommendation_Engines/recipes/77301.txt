Title:
Chilled Cafe Latte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons Folger’s Instant Coffee Crystals
3/4 cup warm water
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Fat Free or Original Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 cups ice cubes

Directions:
Dissolve coffee in water in small bowl.
Combine dissolved coffee, sweetened condensed milk, and vanilla in blender container; blend well. Gradually add ice, blending until smooth.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
Serve this sweet, smooth drink as a morning pick-me-up or after dinner - it is great any time.
