Title:
Spicy Oven-Roasted Chickpeas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123111.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (15 oz.)cans chickpeas (garbanzo beans) thoroughly drained and rinsed (about 3 cups)
2 T. olive oil
1 t. ground cumin or garlic powder
1 t. chili powder
1/2 t. cayenne pepper
1/2 t. sea salt

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees and arrange a rack in the middle.
Place the chickpeas in a large bowl and toss with the remaining ingredients until evenly coated.
Spread the chickpeas in an even layer on a rimmed baking sheet and bake until crisp, about 30 to 40 minutes.
