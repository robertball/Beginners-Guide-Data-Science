Title:
Carmela's Chicken Rigatoni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbl butter and 1/4 cup olive oil
6 oz. mushrooms
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked and chopped
salt and pepper
2 teaspoons basil
4 oz. dry marsala
14 oz. heavy cream
16 oz. rigatoni pasta (pre cooked)
2 oz. grated parmesan cheese
Caramelized onions (recipe below)

Directions:
Directions:
To make caramelized onions:
2 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
1 large yellow onion, sliced thinly
salt, to taste
pepper, to taste
1 teaspoon sugar
Over medium heat, melt the butter in a medium skillet.  Add the olive oil and continue heating until until incorporated.
Add salt, pepper and onions stirring constantly, cooking until onions start to soften (about 5 minutes).
Mix in the sugar and continue cooking until onions are golden brown, frequenly scraping the browning bits off the bottom of the pan (approximately 20 minutes).
In hot saute pan add butter, olive oil, mushrooms, chicken, basil, caramelized onions, salt and pepper and saute for approximately 60 to 90 seconds.
Add the wine and saute for 60 seconds.  Add the heavy cream and bring to a boil over high heat.  Add pre-cooked pasta and saute until sauce coats the pasta.  Add parmesan cheese and continue to toss until cheese is completely incorporated.

Grilled chicken, mushrooms, caramelized onions, fresh basil and Parmesan cheese tossed with imported rigatoni pasta and Marsala cream sauce
