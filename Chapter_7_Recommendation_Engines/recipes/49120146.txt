Title:
BOUTIQUE SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup 		grape juice
2 tsp.		red wine vinegar
1 		garlic clove, minced
1/4 tsp.		each salt and pepper
1/4 cup 	extra virgin olive oil
1 pkg.		Spinach leaves
1 		diced roasted red pepper
to taste	sliced almonds, toasted

Directions:
Bring grape juice to boil in a saucepan.  Reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes or until reduced and syrupy.  Remove from heat and whisk in vinegar, garlic, salt and pepper.  Drizzle in oil, whisking.
Place spinach leaves on salad plate.  Sprinkle peppers and almonds over spinach.  Drizzle warm salad dressing over it.
