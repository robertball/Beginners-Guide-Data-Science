Title:
Coconut Cream Pie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/698894.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups of graham cracker crumbs
2 sticks (1 cup) butter
4 packages instant vanilla pudding
2 8oz containers of Cool Whip
4 cups of milk
2 cups coconut
1/8 cup toasted coconut

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Melt butter and mix into the graham vracker crumbs.
Press into a 9x13 pan and bake for 20 minutes.
Remove from oven and cool completely before making filling.
For filling beat the milk and the pudding until smooth.
Fold in one container of Cool Whip and then fold in the 2 cups of coconut.
Pour over crust and smooth evenly.
Smooth 2nd container of Cool Whip over the filling and sprinkle to toasted coconut on top.
Refrigerate 2 hours before serving.

Description: 
My dad swears that this is the best coconut cream pie he has ever had. This version is super easy to make, tastes fabulous, and makes a big pan, so it's perfect for family get togethers or if you just love leftover pie.
