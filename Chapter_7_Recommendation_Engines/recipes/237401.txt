Title:
Healthy Strawberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4471699.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup almond milk
1 banana
1/2 cup frozen strawberries
1/3 cup plain fat-free Greek yogurt
3 tablespoons rolled oats
1 tablespoon slivered almonds

Directions:
Blend almond milk, banana, strawberries, yogurt, oats, and almonds together in a NutriBullet(R) or blender until smooth.
