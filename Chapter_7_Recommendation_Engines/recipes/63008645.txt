Title:
Andes Creme de Menthe Chunk Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. salted butter sofened
3/4 c. dark brown sugar
1/2 c. white granulated sugar
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. baking powder
2 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
1 pkg. Andes Creame de Menthe Baking Chips
2 2/3 c sifted flour

Directions:
Blend butter, sugars, baking soda, baking powder, vanilla and eggs until mixed.  Stir in baking chips and then flour.  Chill approximately one hour in refrigerator.  Measure out approximately 1 oz. of dough.  Form ball and slightly flatten.  Raise oven rack one level above middle and bake on non stick baking pans.  Bake at 350 degrees for approximately 10 minutes.  Cool on pan for 2 minutes before removing.  Cool completely on cookie rack
