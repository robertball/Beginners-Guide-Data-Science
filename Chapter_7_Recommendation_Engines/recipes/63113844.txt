Title:
Sweet & Spicy Bacon Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/791702.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts cut in thirds (12 pieces)
12 Bacon slices
salt and pepper to taste
garlic powder
chili powder
1/3 c. brown sugar

Directions:
Cut each breast into 3 equal sized pieces, season all the chicken with the spices, then wrap a bacon slice around each piece, roll in brown sugar and place into an oven proof dish LINED WITH FOIL....make sure you have some olive oil in the dish too, like 2 or 3 tablespoons is fine.
Bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and bacon is nice and brown and crispy.
