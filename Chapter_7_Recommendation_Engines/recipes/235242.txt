Title:
Whipped Pumpkin Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1526481.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup pumpkin puree
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping (such as Cool WhipÂ®), thawed

Directions:
Beat cream cheese in a bowl with an electric mixer until smooth; mix in pumpkin puree until smooth. Stir confectioners' sugar and pumpkin pie spice into mixture until evenly mixed; fold in whipped topping. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for flavors to blend, about 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Cynthia Moinet
Description: 
Fluffy and creamy pumpkin dip that goes great with graham crackers and ginger snaps.
