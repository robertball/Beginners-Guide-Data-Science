Title:
Brunswick Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2 lbs) Chix
1 Lbs Country Ham (Cubed)
1 Lbs Edward's Surry VA  Sausage
1 1/2 C Chopped Onion (4)
3 (16 oz.) cans of Crushed/Cubed/Diced Tomatoes
RO*TEL Tomatoes
1 (8 oz.) Tomato Paste
1/4 C Sugar
1/4 C White Vinegar
2 TBLS Worchestershire Sauce
2 TBLS All-Purpose Flour
1 LBS Peeled Potatoes, CUBED
1 Lbs Corn
1 Lbs Lima Beans
1 Lbs Okra
1 Lbs Green Beans
1 Lbs Black-eyed Peas
3 Stalks Celery, Chopped
4 C Chix Stock
4 C Beef Stock
Hot Sauce to taste

Directions:
Saute Fresh Vegetables in Ham Fat/Salt pork.
De-Bone Chix.
Add to Chix Stock, including ALL the pork/chix fat.
Add spices & Potatoes, simmer 10-15 mins
Add Corn, Lima Beans, Okra, Green Beans, Black-eyed Peas, Celery & canned Tomatoes. and cook until just tender.
Cool.
Refrigerate for 2 days. Reheat and serve.
