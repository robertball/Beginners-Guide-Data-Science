Title:
Easy Cheesy Ham & Apple Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/505732.jpg
Ingredients:
8 large eggs
1/2 cup sour cream
2 tablespoons milk
1/2 cup scallions, chopped
2 teaspoons all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 cup fully cooked ham, finely diced
1 cup gruyer cheese, finely grated
1 large apple, peeled, cored & thinly sliced
1/4 cup butter
2 tablespoons brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease (4) 10oz souffle/ramekin dishes well with butter (Pam will leave brown speckles on dish).
In a blender or with a handheld emulsion blender, blend eggs, sour cream, milk, scallions, flour, salt and pepper and for 30 seconds.
Divide ham and cheese between the 4 dishes. Pour egg mixture over the ham & cheese.
Bake 25-30 minutes or until a knife inserted comes out clean.
While souffles bake, saute apples in butter, add cinnamon and brown sugar. Saute until apples are slightly soft and sugar has melted. Spoon apples into the center of each souffle and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
mickdee
Description: 
Given the ingredients from the the Gang Up Challenge, this is an easy cheese & scallion souffle with an unexpected apple garnish. Adapts well to light ingredients. It can be easily halved for 2 servings.
