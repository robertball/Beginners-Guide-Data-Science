Title:
cheap veggie beans and rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups instant white or brown rice
2 Tb. olive oil
1 small onion (chopped)
1 clove garlic (chopped)
2 or 3 carrots peeled and sliced very thin
1 can yellow corn (drained)
1 can black beans with liquid
1 can pinto beans with liquid
1 tsp. lime juice
1 Tb. cumin
1 tsp. chili powder
salt and pepper to taste
fresh or dried cilantro (garnish optional)
1 cup shredded colby or chedder cheese

Directions:
Prepare rice according to package directions and set aside.
In a large skillet, saute onion, garlic, and carrots in olive oil over medium high heat until soft.
Add all other ingredients and bring to a soft boil for 2 min.
Reduce heat to medium low and simmer, stirring occasionally for 15 minutes covered.
Serve bean mixture over rice.
Top with shredded cheese and cilantro.

Submitter: 
momof3
Description: 
This is a simple yummy recipe I made for my husband and 2 year old. Neither one cares for tomatoes, but they could easily be added to the recipe. This recipe is mild for the kids but can be kicked up with more spice. It's also good for families on a budget!
