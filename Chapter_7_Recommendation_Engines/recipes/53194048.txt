Title:
Chocolate Scotcheroos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
1 cup light corn syrup
1 cup peanut butter
6 cups crispy rice cereal or special K
12 oz bag semisweet chocolate chips
12 oz bag  butterscotch chips
1 cup toasted pecans

Directions:
Butter a 9x13 inch baking pan.
In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the sugar and corn syrup, and bring to a rolling boil. Remove from heat immediately, and stir in peanut butter. Mix in the rice cereal until evenly coated. Press and smooth out the mixture into the prepared pan.  Sprinkle toasted pecans evenly over top of cereal layer.
In a glass bowl in the microwave, melt the chocolate and butterscotch chips, stirring occasionally until smooth and well blended. Spread over the top of the bars. Chill until set, then cut into bars.
