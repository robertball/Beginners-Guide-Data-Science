Title:
Oatmeal Sourdough Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sourdough
½ cup warm water
3 tablespoons honey
1 tablespoon molasses
1 egg
½ cup margarine
½ cup quick oats
3 cups all purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoons yeast

Directions:
Place ingredients in bread machine in the order listed by the manufacturer. Select dough cycle and start. When cycle is completed, shape dough into rolls, cover with clean towel and let rise until doubled. Bake at 400 degrees F for 10-12 minutes or until browned.

Submitter: 
Sally
Description: 
Oatmeal gives these rolls a lovely texture, while the sourdough adds a distinct flavor.
