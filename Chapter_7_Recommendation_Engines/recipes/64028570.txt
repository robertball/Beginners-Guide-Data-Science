Title:
Cherry Cream Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Graham cracker crust
8 oz. package of cream cheese - softened
1/2 cup powdered sugar
2 cups Cool Whip
21 oz. can of cherry pie filling

Directions:
Beat together cream cheese and sugar until light and fluffy.
Fold in whipped topping and blend until smooth.
Spread mixture into crust and spoon pie filling over the top.
Cover pie and chill at least 2 hours before serving.
