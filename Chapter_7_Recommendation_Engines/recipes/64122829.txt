Title:
Beer Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C. flour
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1 1/2 T. baking powder
2 T. sugar
1 12oz. can room temp beer

Directions:
Mix flour, salt and baking powder. Blend in sugar and beer, mix until it forms a ball. Place in well greased loaf pan. Let stand for 15 minutes. Bake at 350 for 40-45 minutes, until top is golden brown. Remove from pan and let cool.
