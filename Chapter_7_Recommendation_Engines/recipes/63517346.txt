Title:
Chicken Ditalini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. olive oil
1 small onion, finely chopped
2 stalks celery, finely chopped
1/2 pound of baby carrots, thinly sliced
2 tsp. dried parsley, or 2-3 T. chopped fresh flat leaf parsley
1 tsp. dried thyme
1-2 large chicken bouillon cubes
cracked black pepper and salt
2 cups chicken broth
12 cups water, divided
1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/2 c. pearl barley
1 c. split peas
1 lb. potatoes, peeled and cubed
1/4 c. freshly shredded parmesan cheese
1/2 c. heavy cream
1 lb box of ditalini pasta

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large pot, add onion, celery, parsley, thyme, salt and pepper.  Cook and stir until onions are translucent.
Add broth, 2 cups water and bouillon, bring to boil.  Add chicken breasts, cover and reduce heat and simmer until chicken is done. Remove chicken to platter to cool. Cut into small pieces.
Add rest of the water and bring back to boil.  Add barley and cover.  Cook on low boil for 20 minutes.
Add split peas.  Cook 20 minutes more.
Return cubed chicken to pot and add potatoes and cook 10 minutes more. Cook ditalini pasta separately according to package directions.
Add parmesan cheese and stir well.  Place cream in a large measuring cup or bowl.  Ladle some of the hot broth into the bowl to temper the cream.  Add all back into the pot.
Taste and adjust seasonings.  Place desired amount of cooked ditalini pasta in a bowl and ladle soup over it.  Sprinkle with additional parmesan if desire.
