Title:
Juicy Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can pineapple chunks with juice
1 apple - peeled, cored and diced
1 can mandarin oranges
1 banana, sliced
1 cup seedless green grapes, halved
1 cup strawberries
1 cup blueberries and raspberries
1 small box instant vanilla pudding

Directions:
In a large bowl, toss together the pineapple, apple, mandarin oranges, banana, strawberries, blueberries and raspberries. Then sprinkle a little vanilla pudding over the ingredients.  Add the juice from the pineapple and orange and let chill until serving.

Submitter: 
Judy Smith
Description: 
Pineapple, apple, orange, banana and grapes unite in this juicy and delicious fruit salad!Modified from: Juicy Fruit Salad
