Title:
Sour Cream Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken on the bone
1 envelope onion lipton soup mix
1/2 carton light sour cream
4-6 new potatoes
butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*.  Remove skin from chicken and place in a baking dish.  Place a generous tablespoon of butter over each peice of chicken.  Quarter and arrange potatoes around chicken.  Sprinkle onion lipton soup mix over everything.  Cover with foil.  Bake for 30 min.  Turn heat down to 250* cook another 2 hours for meat to fall off the bone.
Once done, pour (about) 3-5 tablespoons of butter drippings into sour cream in a bowl.  Mix until you have the consistensy of thin gravy.  Serve over chicken and potatoes.  YUMMY!
