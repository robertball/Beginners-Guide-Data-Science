Title:
Big Ray's Fiesta Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1208881.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can (16.3 oz) refrigerated buttermilk biscuits
1/2 cup salsa
1 1/2 cups cubed cooked chicken
1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese (6 oz)
1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
1/2 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1/2 cup frozen corn, thawed
1/4 cup sliced ripe olives, drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cut each biscuit into 8 pieces; toss with salsa to coat.
Spread in sprayed 8-inch square (2-quart) glass baking dish and bake 15 minutes
Mix remaining ingredients and top biscuits.  Bake additional 20 minutes or until edges are deep golden brown and center biscuit pieces are no longer doughy. Let stand 15 minutes. Serve with additional salsa and sour cream if desired.
