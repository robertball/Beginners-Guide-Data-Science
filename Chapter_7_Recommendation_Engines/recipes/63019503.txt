Title:
Microwave Peanut Butter Chocolate Swirl Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/755894.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg.White Almond Bark
1 small jar 12-14 oz peanut butter
3 regular size Hershey Bars

Directions:
Break Almond Bark in half. Put one half in a bowl and melt in microwave. Add the whole jar of peanut butter and mix well. Add the second half of the almond bark and push to the bottom of the mixture and microwave for about 1 minute and mix, breaking up pieces of the bark. You may need to microwave an additional 30 seconds and mix again. When the bark is totally melted, pour into an 8x8 pan lined with wax paper or sprayed with pam. Melt the 3 Hershey bars in a small bowl and drizzle over the fudge mixture. Take knife and cut through the chocolate to swirl into the peanut butter mixture. Refrigerate for about an hour. Cut into small pieces.
