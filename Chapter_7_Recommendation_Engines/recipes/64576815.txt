Title:
Cream Cheese Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
250 g cream cheese, softened
4 cups icing sugar
1/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
2 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Put all ingredients in mixing bowl.
Beat slowly until combined, then at med. speed until light and fluffy.
Adjust icing sugar for desired consistency.
Makes about 3 1/2 cups.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Classic covering for carrot or spice cakes.
