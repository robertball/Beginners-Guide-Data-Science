Title:
Big Daddy's Carolina Coastal Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 large frozen strawberries
2 scoops of Mila
2 (8 ounce) container lemon yogurt
2/3 cup orange juice

Directions:
In a blender, combine frozen strawberries, Mila, yogurt and orange juice. Blend until smooth. Pour into a glass and serve.
