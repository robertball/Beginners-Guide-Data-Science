Title:
Kahlua No Bake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/294515.jpg
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup Kahlua
2 cups firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
4 cups quick oats

Directions:
Heat butter, sour cream, Kahlua, and brown sugar in 2 qt. saucepan over low heat, stirring frequently.
When it comes to a full rolling boil, time for 5 minutes.
Remove from heat.  Stir in flour and oats.
Drop by spoonfuls onto parchment papered cookie sheets.
When set, store in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
