Title:
Chicken, Bacon & Avocado Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken boneless, skinless chicken breasts
Johnny's Garlic Spread/Seasoning (found at Costco)
2 T. Olive Oil
sea salt & pepper
6 slices bacon, cooked crisp
2 sliced tomatoes
6 red lettuce leaves
1 red onion, sliced
2 avocado's, mashed with 1/2 t. (or to taste) garlic powder & a pinch of sea salt
mayo
6 crusty buns

Directions:
Pound the chicken breasts until about 1" thick.  Cut in half.
Season with sea salt & pepper, and Johnny's Garlic Seasoning or Garlic Powder.
Saute in olive oil and turn half way thru, until completely cooked.  About 5-8 min. on each side.
Slice bun and spread each side with mayo.  Top bun with a chicken cutlet, 2 pcs. of bacon, lettuce, tomato, a slice of red onion and spoonful of avocado.

Submitter: 
tizzylizz
Description: 
One of my husbands favorites.  Make sure you use a hearty, crusty bun or bread--something that will hold all the items without causing a big mess while trying to eat it.
