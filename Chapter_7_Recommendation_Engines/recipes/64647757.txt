Title:
Big Ray's Chili Sauce Grilled Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4531809.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bottle (12 oz) chili sauce
1/3 cup white wine
1/4 cup olive oil
10 to 12 garlic cloves, minced
4 tsp dried basil
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
8 bone-in chicken thighs

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk the first seven ingredients until blended. Pour 1-1/2 cups marinade into a large resealable plastic bag. Add chicken; seal bag and turn to coat. Refrigerate 8 hours or overnight. Cover and refrigerate remaining marinade.
Drain chicken, discarding marinade in bag. Place chicken on a greased grill rack, skin side down. Grill, covered, over medium heat or broil 4 in. from heat 15 minutes. Turn; grill 10-15 minutes longer or until a thermometer reads 170°-175°, basting occasionally with reserved marinade.
To Bake: Place on greased baking sheet and cook in preheated oven at 350 for 30 minutes.
Freeze option: Freeze chicken with 1-1/2 cups marinade in a resealable plastic freezer bag.
Freeze reserved marinade in a freezer container. To use, thaw chicken with marinade and the reserved marinade in refrigerator overnight. Drain chicken, discarding marinade in bag. Grill as directed, basting with reserved marinade

Description: 
This BBQ sauce will add flavor to these moist chicken thighs. You can enjoy the tender grilled chicken not just in summertime, but can bake as well to enjoy throughout the year.
