Title:
tomato cream chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small boneless, skinless chicken breast
salt and pepper
1 tsp olive oil
2 Tbs tomato paste
1 tsp dried tarragon leaves
1/2 cup chicken broth
2-3 Tbs balsamic vinegar
2 Tbs half and half

Directions:
brown the chicken in olive oil, add salt and pepper as desired.
Remove chicken and add tomato paste and tarragon... mix well until blended.
add remaining ingredients and mix well
slice chicken breast into small strips and cover with sauce. Serve
