Title:
White Chocolate Christmas Crunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Cheerios
2 cups Stick Pretzels
4 cups Crispix
2 cups Peanuts
1 bag M&M;'s
1 bag White Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Mix the Cheerios, Pretzels, Crispix, Peanuts, and M&M's together in a large bowl.  Melt the White Chocolate Chips in the microwave.  Pour melted chocolate over the mix and stir until the chocolate is covering the mix.  Pour onto a sheet of wax paper to dry.
