Title:
Hamberger Skillet Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/586404.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 small onion diced
8 oz egg noodles
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1 1/2 tsp Mrs. Dash seasoning
1 1/2 c. shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown ground beef in skillet, add diced onions and sautee till tender.  Meanwhile cook noodles in boiling water in a seperate pan.  Add Mushroom soup to the mean and simmer till noodles are done.   Add Mrs. Dash and Noodles and stir well. Top with cheese, cover skillet and allow to simmer for 15 minutes or till cheese is melted.
