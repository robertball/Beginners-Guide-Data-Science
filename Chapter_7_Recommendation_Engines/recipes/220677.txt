Title:
Chicken with Creamy Tomato Basil Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989850.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
4 small (4 oz.) boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 clove garlic, minced
1 (10 ounce) tub Philadelphia Tomato Basil Cooking Creme Cream Cheese Product
2 tablespoons water

Directions:
Heat oil in large nonstick skillet on medium-high heat. Add chicken; cook 6 to 7 min. on each side or until done (170 degrees F). Transfer to plate; cover to keep warm.
Add garlic to skillet; cook and stir 1 min. Add Cooking Creme and water; cook and stir 2 to 3 min. or until heated through.
Spoon sauce over chicken.

Submitter: 
Philadelphia Cooking Creme
Description: 
Chicken breasts are quickly pan-fried, then topped with a creamy tomato-basil sauce--and it's ready to serve in less than half an hour.
