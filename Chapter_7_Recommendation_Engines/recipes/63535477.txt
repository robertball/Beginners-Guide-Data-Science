Title:
Tom Kha Gai ~ Thai Chicken and Coconut Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/965671.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Tbs. minced peeled fresh ginger
1 Tbs. garlic cloves, minced
1 (3-inch) stalk lemongrass or 2 Tbs. minced lemongrass (from tube)
1 fresh red chili, sliced
3 cups chicken stock
1 15 oz. can unsweetened coconut milk
2 Tbs. soy sauce
1 Tbs. sugar
1 cup sliced mushrooms
3 Tbs. Lime juice
2 cups shredded cooked chicken
3 Tbs. chopped fresh cilantro
2 Tbs. fresh lime juice

Directions:
In a large saucepan, bring the broth, ginger, garlic, lemongrass, and red chili to a simmer.
Add the coconut milk, soy sauce sugar, half the cilantro, lime juice and mushrooms.  Cook for 5 minutes.  Add the cooked chicken and cook for another 5 minutes.  Right before serving, add additional lime juice and the remaining fresh cilantro.  Serve with hot Jasmin rice.  Aroi mak mak!

Submitter: 
yumnjam
Description: 
This is a savory soup that my kids ask me to make on rainy or cold days.  They love the lemon-y flavor.  My husband says it's even better the next day!
