Title:
Minestrone Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz can of red kidney beans- drained
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1 clove of garlic
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 T olive oil
1/4 cup of chopped parsley
1 zucchini cubed
2 ribs of celery, chopped
1 diced carrot
1 chopped onion
3 T butter or margarine
1 11 oz can of stewed tomatoes
2 1/2 cups of beef broth
1/4 cup of uncooked elbow macaroni
1 8 oz can of tomato sauce
grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
In large saucepan, place 1/2 of the kidney beans and mash them.
Place the remaining kidney beans in the pot.
Place all ingredients in the pot except macaroni and cheese.
Cover and cook on low heat for 1 hour stirring occasionally.
Add macaroni into the pot and cook for 10-15 minutes or until tender.
Garnish soup with cheese after it is put into bowls.
