Title:
Cofer's Hot Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1123267.jpg
Ingredients:
4 lb. chicken wings, tips discarded & separated at the joint
1/4 cup butter
1 Tb. white vinegar
5 Tb. wing sauce

Directions:
Melt butter in large pan.  Add vinegar & wing sauce, mix well over low heat.  Turn off heat.
Deep fry wings in small batches for 10 minutes in 375 degree oil. When 2 minutes left to cook, turn low heat on under sauce & mix again.  Remove wings from oil & place in sauce, turning wings to coat well.  Leave in sauce 2 to 3 minutes.  Remove to dish.  Continue with next batch.

Submitter: 
ccofer
Description: 
These wings & a stack of napkins is all you'll need.  Our nod to Hooters Wings.
