Title:
Fresh Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 jalapeno chile peppers
1 serrano pepper
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1/2 red onion, finely chopped
1 tablespoon white sugar
1/8 teaspoon seasoned salt
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin (or more if you like more)
1 tablespoon Lime juice
1/8 teaspoon or less Cayenne Pepper
1/8 teaspoon or less Ginger (not too much)
1/4 cup cilantro (or more if you like)
2 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chiles
2 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Place serrano and jalapeno chile peppers on a medium baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven 15 minutes, or until roasted. Remove from heat and chop off stems.
3. Place serrano and jalapeno chile peppers (and seeds), garlic, onion, white sugar, salt, ground cumin, cayenne pepper, cilantro, ginger, and one can of diced tomatoes in a blender or food processor. Chop using the pulse setting for a few seconds.
4. Mix in remaining tomatoes and diced tomatoes with green chilies. Transfer to a medium bowl. Cover and chill in the refrigerator until serving.

Submitter: 
Staci Booth
Description: 
I try to make this salsa every summer. It can be used when recipes call for salsa or diced tomatoes with green chiles to add more flavor.
