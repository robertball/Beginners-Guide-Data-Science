Title:
Creamed Peas and Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-4 red potatos, peeled and cut
Frozen peas
Milk or cream
1/2-3/4 C. flour
Water

Directions:
Cook potatos until almost done (15-20 min.)
Add frozen peas cook about 5 min.
Put flour in separate pot
Add water until it is creamy and there are no bubbles.
Drain potatos and peas.
Add milk or cream to cover Pot. and peas.
Bring almost to boil- add flour mixture.
Stir all the time until desired thickness

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
This is one of my favorite recipes from my Grandma Jones. It is especially taste with fresh peas from her garden, but frozen work, too.
