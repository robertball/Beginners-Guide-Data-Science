Title:
Healthier Chocolate Chip Zucchini Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3121597.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup grated zucchini
1 1/2 cups GF oat flour
3/4 tsp sea salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/3 cup Truvia baking blend
1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
1/3 cup canola oil
1 tsp apple cider vinegar
1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees
Prepare a muffin tin by spraying with nonstick cooking spray
In a large bowl, mix together dry ingredients except chocolate chips
In a food processor, mix together zucchini and wet ingredients
Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients until combined well
Add chocolate chips & mix into batter
Add batter to muffin tin
Bake 20 minutes or until a toothpick can be slid out of the muffin clean
Allow to cool 10 minutes in the muffin tin before removing & enjoying
