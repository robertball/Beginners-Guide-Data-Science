Title:
Ok Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sift together 1 1/2 c flour
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
2 squares unsweetened chocolate
1/2 lb. butter, softened
3/4 c granulated sugar
3/4 lb. dark brown sugar, tightly packed
2 large eggs
2 Tblsps. Kahlua
1 Tblsp. vanilla
1/4 c instant coffee
6 oz. semi-sweet chocolate morsels
3/4 c walnut pieces
3/4 c pecan pieces
Store-bought name brand dark chocolate frosting

Directions:
Sift together 1 1/2 c flour
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
Set aside
Melt 2 squares unsweetened chocolate in double boiler
Cream butter w/mixer
Mix granulated sugar into butter mixture
Mix dark brown sugar into mixture
Mix eggs into butter mixture
Mix 2 Tblsps. Kahlua into butter mixture
Mix 1 Tblsps. vanilla into butter mixture
Mix melted chocolate squares into butter mixture
Mix coffee into butter mixture
Mix sifted ingredients into butter mixture
Stir chocolate morsels into butter mixture, thoroughly
Stir nut pieces into butter mixture, thoroughly
Bake in a 9"x12" pan at 375 degrees for 25 minutes
When completely cool, add frosting on top
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Sharpshooter
Description: 
These brownies are the moisest, most chocolaty and best brownies I have ever tasted, and I've tasted a lot. It takes time to prepare (and clean-up), but well worth it. You may even want to prepare a pan for yourself! Just double the recipe.
