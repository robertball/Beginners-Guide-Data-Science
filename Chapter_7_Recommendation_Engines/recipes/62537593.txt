Title:
Southern Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Large Sweet Potatoes Baked
1/2 cup white sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 tablespoons butter, softened
1/2 cup evaporated milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon ground cloves
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
3 tablespoons butter, softened
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Bake Potatoes at 375 F degrees for 1 1/2 hours. Peel skin from warm potatoes and mash. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. In a large bowl, mix together the sweet potatoes, sugar, eggs, salt, butter, milk, cinnamon, cloves and vanilla. Mix until smooth. Transfer to a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In medium bowl, mix the sugar and flour. Cut in the butter until the mixture is coarse. Stir in the pecans. Sprinkle the mixture over the sweet potato mixture.
Bake in the preheated oven 30 minutes, or until the topping is lightly brown.
