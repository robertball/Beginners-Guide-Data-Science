Title:
German Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
6 eggs
1 cup (all-purpose) flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla
1/4 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Blend first five ingredients in a blender. Be sure to smooth out any flour lumps. -Blend in butter a little at a time. -Grease muffin tins well and fill slightly less than half-full. -Bake for 15 minutes, or until puffy and golden on top. The crater will form on its own. -Add your favorite toppings. And a dusting of powdered sugar

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
made in muffin tins. So easy, the crater forms on its own!
