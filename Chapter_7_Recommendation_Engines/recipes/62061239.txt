Title:
Grilled Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 bell peppers
Shredded or sliced mozzarella- enough to cover the peppers.
sliced jalepeno peppers- enough to place on the bell peppers.
oregano to taste for variation

Directions:
1. Cut bell peppers into large chunks. Maybe an inch or so square.
2 Grill peppers inside down on grill until slightly charred. Turn over and add jalepeno peppers to each pepper. Sprinkle with shredded mozzarella and add oregano to taste. Grill to preference and serve.

Submitter: 
Deedle Gee
Description: 
A Friend made this the other day and it was great, so I had to share. The more colors you use the more colorful it is.
Our friend just sprinkled oregano. We added the jalepeno for added heat.
