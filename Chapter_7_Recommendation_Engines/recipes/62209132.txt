Title:
Cabbage and cheddar pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Basic pie crust
oil
1 large onion
7-8 c. thinly sliced green cabbage
1/4 c. sour cream
1 tsp. Dijon mustard
1 c. grated sharp Cheddar
1/2 c. dry breadcrumbs

Directions:
Prepare pie crust.
Saute onion in oil over medium heat for  2-3 minutes.
Add cabbage, reduce heat to medium low, and cook, partially covered and stirring often, for 15-25 minutes, or until very oft, adding salt and pepper to taste.  Add water as needed.
Preheat oven to 375.
Remove from heat; mix in sour cream and mustard.
Add cheese, stirring gently.
Spoon into pie crust and sprinkle with crumbs.
Cool 5 minutes before serving
