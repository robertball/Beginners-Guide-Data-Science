Title:
Eggless Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/551908.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 and 1/2 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 cup boiling water
1 tsp baking soda
2 cups quick cooking oats
7 oz chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees, beat butter, sugars, and vanilla, add flour and salt, and mix well. Dissolve baking soda in boiling water, and to mix and stir well to combine. Stir in oats and chocolate chips. Roll into small balls and flatten on cookie sheets. Bake 10-12 minutes.
