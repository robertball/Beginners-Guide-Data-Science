Title:
Best Spinach Dip Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
1 (1.8 ounce) package Vegetable soup mix or dry leek soup mix
1 (4 ounce) can water chestnuts, drained and chopped
1/2 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
3 sprigs of green onions chopped
1 (1 pound) loaf round sourdough bread

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together mayonnaise, sour cream, dry leek soup mix, water chestnuts and chopped spinach. Chill in the refrigerator 6 hours, or overnight.
Remove top and interior of sourdough bread. Fill with mayonnaise mixture. Tear removed bread chunks into pieces for dipping.

Submitter: 
Marilyn Sevigny
Description: 
A flavorful spinach dip with water chestnuts fills a tasty bread bowl. The perfect recipe for entertaining.Modified from: Best Spinach Dip Ever
