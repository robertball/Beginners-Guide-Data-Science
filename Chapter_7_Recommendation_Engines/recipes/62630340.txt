Title:
Spaghetti Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6# Spaghetti, cook and drain
3 cups butter
18 eggs
3 1/2 cups parmesan cheese
3 1/2 # Ricotta Cheese
5 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup Italian Seasoning
1 10# can Spaghetti Sauce

Directions:
Combine butter, eggs and parmesan & ricotta cheese. Mix with prepared spaghetti.  Combine garlic, italian seasoning and spaghetti sauce.  Pour over spaghetti mixture.  Bake at 350* for approx 30 minutes.
