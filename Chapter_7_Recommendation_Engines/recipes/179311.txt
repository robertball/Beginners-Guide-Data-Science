Title:
Cuervo Especial Pink Sunrise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984506.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Jose Cuervo Especial
2 ounces cranberry juice
2 ounces apple juice
1 splash club soda
1 piece orange slice

Directions:
In a highball or rocks glass filled with ice, combine Jose Cuervo Especial with cranberry and apple juice.
Top off with club soda or seltzer.
Garnish with orange slice.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Jose Cuervo Especial tequila, cranberry juice, apple juice and an orange slice make a pretty sunrise cocktail.
