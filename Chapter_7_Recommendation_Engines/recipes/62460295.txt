Title:
Linguine with clam sauce and peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box (8-9 oz.) linguine
3 T. butter
1 tsp. finely chopped garlic
1/4 c. chopped fresh parsley
2 cans (6 1/2 oz.) chopped clams, drained
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
3/4 c. sour cream
1/3 c. milk
1/3 c. roasted red peppers, coarsely chopped

Directions:
Cook linguine.
Meanwhile, in skillet, melt butter till sizzling; add garlic.  Cook over medium heat 1 minute.  Ad parsley, clams, and W-sauce.  Cook, stirring, one minute.  Reduce heat to low; stir in sour cream and milk.  Stir constantly till heated through.  Stir in roasted red peppers.
Serve over linguine
