Title:
Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/271251.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1 cup heavy cream
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium low heat. Add cream and simmer for 5 minutes, then add garlic and cheese and whisk quickly, heating through. Stir in food coloring and serve.

Submitter: 
Cooks4Kids
Description: 
My kids only want to eat pasta that comes from a box so when I want to eat something like this, I have to be sneaky. I eliminate the parsley and use food coloring to make it neon orange. With some cute noodles like letters or characters, everyone is happy.Modified from: Alfredo Sauce
