Title:
Ham, Broccoli and Potato casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 to 1 lb of cooked diced ham
32 ounce package of frozen cubed Hash browns thawed
16 ounces frozen broccoli cooked
1 can condensed cream of broccoli soup
1 can condensed cheddar cheese soup
1 cup or so of milk
1 1/2 cups of french fried onions divided
1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon of pepper
6 to 8 ounces cheddar cheese

Directions:
cook thawed potatoes in 400 degree oven for 15 to 20 minutes in 9 x 13 dish. Take out of oven and add cooked broccoli on top of potatoes and diced ham on top of broccoli. Mix soup ,milk, garlic, pepper, 3/4 cup of onions and half of cheese in bowl and spread on top of the potatoes, broccoli and ham. Bake in oven covered for 45 minutes. Spread remaining cheese and onions on top and bake until brown, aprox 5 to 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Brenda Brown
Description: 
Great comfort food on a cold day. Very tasty and goes together easy.
