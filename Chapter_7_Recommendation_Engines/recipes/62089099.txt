Title:
Tarragon Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup mayonnaise
3 Tbsp. snipped fresh parsley
2 Tbsp. fresh chives
1 to 2 Tbsp fresh tarragon or 2 tsp. dried tarragon, crushed
1 tsp. finely shredded lemon peel
2 tsp. lemon juice
1 tsp. Dijon-style mustard
1/8 tsp. ground black pepper
1 12-oz. can solid white tuna in water, drained and flaked
8 slices of your favorite bread (I usually use oat nut bread)
8 - 12 thin tomato slices (optional)
Lettuce (optional)
4 slices Provologne cheese (optional)

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine mayonnaise, parsley, chives, tarragon, lemon peel, lemon juice, mustard and pepper; stir until wel combined.  Stir in tuna, breaking up any large pieces with a fork.  Refrigerate for 30 minutes, or until chilled.
Place 4 slices of bread on work surface and top with tomato, lettuce and cheese.  Spread tuna over prepared bread and top with 2nd slice of bread.

Submitter: 
MARIA BUJAK
Description: 
This is a recipe for a tuna sandwich you would expect from a high-end sandwich shop.  It is light, fresh and full of flavor.  You must try this!
