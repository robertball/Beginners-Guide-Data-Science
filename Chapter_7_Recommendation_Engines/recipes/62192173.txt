Title:
Caramel Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/354209.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package caramel~wrappers removed
1 5oz can evaporated milk, divided
1/2 cup butter melted
1 package german chocolate cake mix
1 package chocolate chips

Directions:
In double boiler combine caramel and 1/3 cups evaporated milk, and melt until smooth.  Pre-heat oven to 350.  Meanwhile, take the melted butter and combine with cake mix and the remaining milk and mix.  Spead half of the mixture on bottom of 13 x 9 cake pan.  Bake about 6 minutes.  Take out and add the bag of chocolate chips and then pour caramel over the mixture.  Crumble the rest of the mixture over the top.  Return to oven and bake another 10-12 minutes.  Let cool and cut into squares.  Enjoy!
