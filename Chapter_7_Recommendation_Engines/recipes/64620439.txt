Title:
Leftover Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 extra large fried chicken breast
10 oz can yellow corn
10 oz can diced tomatoes with green chilis
14.5 oz can reduced sodium black beans
14 oz can reduced sodium chicken broth
1/8 tsp cumin
1/8 tsp chili powder
1/8 tsp garlic powder
2 tbsp salsa

Directions:
1. Remove skin and bones from chicken breast and shred all meat.
2. Add all ingredients to a saucepan and heat to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes, or up to an hour for all ingredients to blend well.
3. Add your favorite toppings.  Try sour cream, guacamole, tortilla strips.

Submitter: 
Kate Leonard
Description: 
A combination of things I always have around make a quick, yummy lunch or dinner.
