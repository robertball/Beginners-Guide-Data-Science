Title:
lime shrimps with mango salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/417104.jpg
Ingredients:
For the shrimps
1 bag of mini cooked shrimps
2 cups of coriander
3 tbs of lime
3 garlic cloves
Salt ( to ur liking)
1/4 cup tequila (optional)
Salsa
1 mango diced
Half a green pepper
2 tomatoes
1 avocado
3 tablespoons lime
1 small green chili
1 tsp  of sugar
Tortillas (best ones are the mexican corn tortillas)

Directions:
Star with marinating the shrimps with garlic,a bit of olive oil, and salt.
You can keep it in a tupperware until you cut the rest of the ingredients for the salsa.
Once you have diced all the ingredients for the salsa, set aside.
Depending if you want to keep this healthy or not,you can use some butter or nothing at all.
Cook the shrimps on medium heat until all the water comes out.
Once all the water is gone throw in the coriander and the tequila and some lime.
After all is done, spoon some shrimps and the salsa into the torilla and enjoy!
A very healthy crunchy and favorful summer recipe.
