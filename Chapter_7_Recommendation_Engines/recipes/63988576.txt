Title:
Zucchini cinnamon cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1106771.jpg
Ingredients:
Cobbler mix:
8 cups zucchini
1 cup sugar
3 Tbsp flour
dash of salt
1 tsp apple pie spice
3/4 tsp cream of tartar
1 Tbsp lemon juice
.
Cake Mix: ( you can use 1 box of white cake mix instead)
4 oz cake flour
3 oz sugar
pinch of salt
2 1/2tsp baking powder
2 oz sugar
1/2 cup butter (Melted)

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 350*F
Peel & Cut zucchini lengthwise, strip out seeds. Cut in 1/2" chunks. Cook in bowling water until tender.
Drain, then cool in cold water for about 5 min.
Drain and add sugar, flour, salt, apple pie spice, cream of tarter, & Lemon Juice
Stir well (Do not Beat)
Pour into a creased baking dish (13x9")
In a small bowl mix together cake mix: cake flower, sugar, salt, baking powder, sugar. (Or You can substitute for 1 box of white cake mix)
Spread dry cake mix over top of Cobbler mix
Drizzle melted butter over cake mix
Bake in oven for 60min
