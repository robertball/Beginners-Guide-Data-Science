Title:
Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon sesame seeds
1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
1 English cucumber, peeled, seeded, sliced into slivers
Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Combine rice wine vinegar, soy sauce, sugar, sesame seeds and sesame oil. Toss cucumbers in this sesame mixture. Season with salt and pepper. Reserve for plating of spring roll.
