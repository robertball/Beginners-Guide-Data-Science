Title:
Pear Manhattan Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5357282.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces rye whiskey
2 fluid ounces pear vodka
1 3/4 fluid ounces sweet vermouth
ice
1 maraschino cherry
1 slice pear

Directions:
Pour whiskey, vodka, sweet vermouth, and ice into a cocktail shaker. Cover and shake until chilled.
Strain into a chilled martini glass and garnish with maraschino cherry and pear slice.

Submitter: 
Allrecipes
Description: 
This is one of the most popular cocktails at St. Felix Bar, in Hollywood CA.
