Title:
Walnut & Roasted Ham Linguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package fresh whole wheat linguine
4 - 5 cloves garlic, minced
2 shallots, chopped
1/3 cup olive oil
1 tsp hot pepper flakes
1/2 cup walnuts, chopped
1/2 pound fully cooked ham, sliced
1/3 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet, saute garlic, shallots and hot pepper flakes in oil for two to five minutes.
Add walnuts and saute for two minutes. Stir in ham slices. Cook until heated through, about five minutes.
Drain pasta and toss with ham mixture. Sprinkle with parmesan and parsley.
