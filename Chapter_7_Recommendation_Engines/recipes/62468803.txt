Title:
120 Calorie Sugar-Free Hot Cocoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T unsweetened Dutch-process cocoa powder
1T + 2t Splenda
2T hot water
1C 1% milk

Directions:
Put cocoa and sweetener in microwaveable mug. Add hot water and stir until smooth. Pour in milk and stir again. Microwave on high for 1 1/2 min. or until hot (don't boil).

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Quick drink made in microwave. Less calories if using fat-free milk. Can add coffee or cinnamon for something different.
