Title:
Big Ray's No Bake Chocolate Pretzel Peanut Butter Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5170790.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 sticks butter, melted
2 cups pretzel rods, crushed into crumbs
1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
1 1/4  cups peanut butter, divided
1 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
In a medium bowl, add the melted butter, pretzel crumbs, confectioners' sugar and 1 cup of peanut butter and stir together until well combined.  Press mixture evenly into the bottom of a 9" x 13" baking dish.
Combine together 1/4 cup peanut butter  with chocolate chips in a microwave save bowl. Microwave in 30 second intervals, stirring in between, until chocolate and Peanut butter are melted and smooth.  Mix to blend, then spread over peanut butter-pretzel layer.  Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before cutting into squares.
