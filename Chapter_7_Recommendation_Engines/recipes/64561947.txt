Title:
Big Ray's Crock  Pot Blueberry Dump Cake Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3477130.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box White Cake Mix {16.25 oz.}
2 cans Blueberry Pie Filling {21 oz. each}
1 stick Butter, melted

Directions:
Spray inside of crock pot with non-stick cooking spray
Empty cans of blueberry pie filling into bottom of crock pot and spread out evenly.
In medium mixing bowl, combine dry cake mix and melted butter, and stir until crumbly. {break up any large chunks into small crumbles with spoon}.
Pour cake/butter crumble mixture over blueberry mixture in crock pot, and spread out evenly, and cover crock pot with lid.
Cook on HIGH for 2 hours, or LOW for 4 hours. Serve with Ice Cream or Whip Cream.
