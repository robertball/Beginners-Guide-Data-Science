Title:
Seasoning Substitute for Chex Mix Packets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1116067.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tsp Paprika
1 tsp Kosher Salt
1 tsp Dried Celery
1 tsp Dill
1 tsp Oregano
1/2 tsp Black Pepper
1/2 tsp Cayenne Pepper
1/2 tsp Rosemary
1/2 tsp Cumin
1/2 tsp Marjoram
1/2 tsp Parsley
1/2 tsp Thyme
1/4 tsp Turmeric
1/4 tsp Caraway
1/4 tsp Coriander

Directions:
Mix together in a blender/food processor until all spices are thoroughly mixed, especially if using  whole spices.  Store in an air tight container.
For Chex Mix follow the directions on the cereal box for the party mix, then add 3 tsp Seasoned Salt to 6 tsp melted butter, 5 tsp Worcestershire, 3/4 tsp garlic powder and 1/2 tsp onion powder.  Mix well and pour over party mix; stirring until evenly coated.  Bake party mix 1 hour at 250 degrees, stirring every 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Amanda Elaine
Description: 
Seasoned salt works great as a substitute for the Chex Mix Seasoning Packet.  This seasoning can be used anywhere a recipe calls for seasoned salt.
