Title:
Date Nut Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package dates
1 cup pecans
1 cup sugar (white sugar will do)
1/2 cup butter (1 stick)
1 heaping cup of flour
3 egg yolks, reserve whites
1 teaspoon baking powder
Beaten egg whites (see above)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Cream butter and sugar
Cut and dredge dates in four with nuts
Beat egg yolks into butter mixture
Add floured dates and nuts
Lastly, add stiffly beaten whites of eggs
Bake about 45 minutes
Cool, slice and roll slices in powdered sugar
