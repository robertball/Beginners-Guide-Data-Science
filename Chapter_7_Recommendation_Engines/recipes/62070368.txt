Title:
Slowcooker BBQ Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 roast
1 packet of onion soup mix
1 bottle of your favorite BBQ sauce

Directions:
Place roast in a slowcooker, sprinkle onion soup mix over top, and then pour the BBQ sauce over the roast. Cook on low for 8 hours.

Submitter: 
LISALC98
Description: 
An amazing BBQ roast that is so easy to prepare! I always get great compliments with this roast and the leftovers are great on sandwiches.
