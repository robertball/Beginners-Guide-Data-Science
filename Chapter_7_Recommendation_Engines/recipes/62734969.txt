Title:
Broccoli Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium red potatoes, diced and boiled
1 hard boiled egg, chopped
2 green onions, diced
1/3 c. diced orange pepper
1/2 c. chopped raw broccoli
1/2 c. peas
1/2 c. chopped spinach
3 slices cooked bacon, chopped
3/4 c. mayo
3/4 c. sour cream
2 tbs. Hidden Valley Ranch seasoning

Directions:
Combine all veggies and bacon in a large bowl.
Top with mayo, sour cream, and ranch seasoning.
Mix until well blended.  Chill for an hour.

Submitter: 
TARAWVU
Description: 
I came up with this one evening when I wanted a different spin on a traditional potato salad.  It's packed with veggies making me feel a little less guilty for eating it.
