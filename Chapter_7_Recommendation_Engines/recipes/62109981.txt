Title:
Italian Sausage & Penne Pasta Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Italian Sausage
1 cup diced red or sweet onion
1 cup diced red or green bell pepper
1 cup thinly sliced mushrooms
8 oz. Uncooked penne pasta
2 cups beef broth
1 cup evaporated milk
1 tsp. Minced garlic or ½ tsp garlic powder
1 tsp. Italian seasoning
½ tsp. Salt
1/4 tsp. Black Pepper
3/4 c Cheddar Cheese
1/4 c Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Remove casings from sausage and crumble into skillet.  Add onion, bell pepper and mushrooms.  Cook until sausage is brown and vegetables wilt.  Drain any excess grease.  Add pasta and broth.  Cover and reduce heat.  Cook for 15 minutes or until pasta is tender (may need to add 1/2 C more water).  Add milk, garlic, italian seasoning, salt and pepper.  Cook until thoroughly heated, but do not boil.  Remove from heat and stir in cheddar cheese.  Sprinkle with parmesan.
