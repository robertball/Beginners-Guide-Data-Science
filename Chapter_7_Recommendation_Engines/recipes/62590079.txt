Title:
Wegman's Butternut Squash and Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz cleaned and cut butternut squash (1 pkg from Wegmans)
1/4 red onion
Wegman's Basting Oil (or EVOO)
3-4 oz Fresh Baby Spinach
Handful dried cranberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cut butternut squash into 1 inch cubes and julienne the onion.
Line baking dish with non-stick foil, put squash and onion on the dish, drizzle with oil and salt and mix.
Roast 55 minutes.
Toss with spinach and cranberries.
