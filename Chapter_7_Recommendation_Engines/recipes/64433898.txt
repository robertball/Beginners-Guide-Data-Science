Title:
Butter Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5280670.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
2 tbsp sugar
1 tsp salt
2 1/2 sticks (1 1/4 cup unsalted butter)
6 tbsp cold water

Directions:
Cut chilled butter in 1/4 inch squares
Put ingredients in food processor & pulse until it is the consistency of corn meal.  Add the cold water.
Form into balls the size you need for a crust and refrigerate over night.
Take out and and roll out and put in pie shell
