Title:
Easy Italian Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/402716.jpg
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken (cut up)
3 garlic cloves minced
2 medium onions
3 medium tomatoes
3 Tablespoons of Olive Oil
2 Teaspoons of dried basil

Directions:
Cut the onions into rings. (They can be large or small it doesn't really matter). Place half of the onions on the bottom of a large oven proof pan. Take one tomatoes and squeeze it over the onions (in the pan) and then break up the tomatoes and place on to p of the chicken.
Place your chicken on top of the onions and tomatoes. Drizzle the Olive Oil over the chicken, then sprinkle the basil and garlic over the chicken. Place the remaining onions on top. Squeeze the remaining two tomatoes over the chicken and tear the rest of the tomatoes up and leave on top. Cover the dish and cook at 450 for 50 minutes and then remove the cover for the remaining 5-10 minutes.

Submitter: 
de'Monfort
Description: 
This is a very easy chicken to throw together and cook. As well, it is very tasty and goes with most anything mash potatoes, baked potatoes, sweet potatoes, rice, green beans, peas, carrots or even wilted spinach.
