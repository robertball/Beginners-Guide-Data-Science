Title:
Cinnamon-Apple Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter (softened)
3/4 cup granulated sugar
3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 cups flour
2 packets apple-cinnamon oatmeal
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1 1/2 cup golden raisins

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375.
Cream butter, sugars, eggs and vanilla in a large mixing bowl.
Sift flour, oatmeal, salt, baking soda,and cinnamon into a separate bowl. Add dry mixture a cup at a time until fully incorporated.
Drop spoonfuls onto non-greased cookie sheet. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Emily H.
Description: 
An easy, and delicious cookie recipe made with instant oatmeal and golden raisins.
