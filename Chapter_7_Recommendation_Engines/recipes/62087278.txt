Title:
Praline-Topped Butternut Squash Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 12 ounce packages frozen squash OR 1 large fresh butternut squash (cooked)
4 tablespoons butter
1 teaspoon salt
2 eggs (slightly beaten)
Topping:
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
3 tablespoons soft butter
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, combine:  cooked squash, butter and salt.  Heat until hot and butter has melted (stirring frequently).  Remove from heat.
Add eggs to squash, mixing very well.
Pour squash mixture into a 1 quart casserole dish.
Combine topping ingredients in a small bowl.  Sprinkle mixture over top of the squash.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Eileen Fuore
Description: 
This butternut squash souffle is an easy, yet elegant recipe.  Your friends and family will ask for this dish on all holidays, or as a side dish for a week day meal.  The eggs give the squash a souffle-like texture that is sure to please even the pickiest eaters!
