Title:
Blue Hawaiian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984700.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Smirnoff® Orange Vodka
1 ounce blue curacao liqueur
2 ounces orange juice
1 ounce pineapple juice
1 slice orange

Directions:
Pour Smirnoff(R) Orange Vodka, blue curacao liqueur, orange juice, and pineapple juice into a glass filled with ice.
Stir well and garnish with orange slice to serve.

Description:
