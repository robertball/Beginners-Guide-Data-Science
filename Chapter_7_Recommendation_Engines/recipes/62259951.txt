Title:
Curry chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion
2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
8 ounces of cubed chicken
3-4 tablespoons of curry paste concentrate
1/2 cup of diced tomatoes
3/4 cup of water
1/3 cup of heavy cream
Cooked rice to serve with the chicken

Directions:
Saute the onions in the oil then add the chicken. Cook the chicken until lightly browned. Add in curry paste, water, tomatoes, and cream. Cook until thickened. Make sure to stir frequently.
