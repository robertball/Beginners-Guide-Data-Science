Title:
Tomato Rotini Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/805863.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Beef, cooked and crumbled
1 can Progresso Tomato Rotini Soup
1 can whole kernel corn, drained
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
Generous dash of Seasoned Salt

Directions:
Cook beef in a large skillet until no longer pink. Add tomato rotini soup (do not drain) and allow to come to a boil. Stir in drained corn and black beans. Bring to a simmer. Stir in a generous dash (abt 1 tsp) of Seasoned Salt. Serve.

Submitter: 
Megan Huntley
Description: 
A throw together dish using canned soup, corn, black beans and hamburger. Great for when you're in a rush.
