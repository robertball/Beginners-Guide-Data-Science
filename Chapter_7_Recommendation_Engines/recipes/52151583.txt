Title:
Pan-Seared Chicken Breasts with Shallots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded thin
salt and pepper to taste
2 shallots, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1/3 cup dry white wine
1/2 cup chicken broth
8 oz mushrooms sliced
1 Tbls. Cornstarch mixed w/ water.

Directions:
Melt 1 tablespoon butter and heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Season chicken with salt and pepper, and brown on both sides in the skillet. Cover, and continue cooking 10 minutes, or until chicken juices run clear. Set aside, and keep warm.
Mix shallots and garlic into skillet over medium heat, and cook until tender. Add mushrooms & cook till just right. Stir in wine, and cook until heated through.Stir in broth & cornstarch, and continue cooking 5 minutes, or until reduced and slightly thickened. Mix in the remaining butter until melted. Add chicken to just warn it, then Serve the sauce over the chicken.
