Title:
Quick Tuna Salad jodys way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can solid white tuna packed in water, drained
1/3 cup LITE MAYO
2 tablespoon sweet pickle relish, or to taste
2 egg whites chopped
1/2 teaspoon mrs. dash

Directions:
Mix aall tuna together with salad dressing and relish, eggs in a small bowl with a fork. Serve.

Submitter: 
Jody Comerford
Description: 
This three ingredient tuna salad is great when you are in a hurry; very quick and easy to make. It tastes great every time!Modified from: Quick Tuna Salad
