Title:
Sauteed Radishes and Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 zucchini squash, sliced
3 tablespoons of butter
1 bunch of radishes, trimmed and cut in half
vegetable oil
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon thyme
salt and pepper to taste
grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Melt butter over medium heat in medium skillet or frying pan
Add radishes and zucchini to the pan, making sure the radishes are flat side down
Cook about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally
Season with salt, pepper, onion powder, garlic powder,  and thyme
Cook another 10 minutes or so, or until vegetables are softened. If there is not enough butter in the pan to cook the vegetables, add a tablespoon or so of vegetable oil
Serve and top with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Halley Jarrett
Description: 
I had a lot of radishes from my garden, and I had never cooked with them before. This recipe is the result of experimentation.
