Title:
Anna's Authentic Italian Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Olive oil or butter
12 oz – Salt Pork (rinse off)
1-1/2 Lbs – Ground Pork
1-1/2 Lbs – Ground chuck
3 Stalks – Celery
3 large – Carrots
1 extra large Onion
2 cans of diced Tomatoes
3 large cans of puree
1 small can tomato paste
2 Tbls. – Vegeta
4 cloves – garlic minced
1 cup – white wine
1 can – chicken broth
1/2 cup cream
1 stick butter
Optional: add 4-5 leaves of fresh basil – remove when done cooking
Parsley – hand full of chopped fresh or frozen

Directions:
In meat grinder or food processor grind up salt pork (or have the butcher do it for you  – Add olive oil or butter to a large pot – cook salt pork until golden brown.
In meat grinder or food processor grind celery and carrots –add to pot;
grind onion and garlic – add to pot – cook until soft
Add ground pork and ground beef – cook until brown – drain any excess fat
Add Vegeta
Simmer until meat and vegetables are cooked
Add wine and continue simmering until wine is reduced.
Add tomato paste – mix well – add puree mix well – add diced tomatoes
Add chicken broth, butter and cream
Bring to a boil; simmer 1 hour.  Sauce is done when oil from meat pools on top.
Recipe makes approx. 8 quarts

Submitter: 
CATHYRAE
Description: 
My co-workers' 84 year old Italian friend created this authentic homemade Spaghetti Sauce.  I've tried many sauce recipes and never have I found one as delicious as Anna's.  I know you will agree.
