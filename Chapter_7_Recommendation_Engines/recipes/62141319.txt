Title:
My Husband's Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sweet Potato Casserole
3 cups mashed sweet potatoes (canned)
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 eggs
1/2 stick butter
1/2 cup milk
1 tablespoon vanilla
Topping:
1/2 stick butter
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup pecans
1 cup coconut
1/2 cup self rising flour

Directions:
Mix first 7 ingredients (from potatoes to vanilla) and put in 11 x 7 inch baking dish that has been sprayed with PAM. For topping, melt 1/2 stick butter and stir in brown sugar. Mix in pecans, coconut and flour. Pour on top of potatoes, bake at 350 for 35 minutes.
