Title:
Spinach Caesar Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/602426.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. ground turkey
1/4 c. grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 c. seasoned Italian bread crumbs
2 tbsp. creamy Caesar dressing
1 egg, beaten
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. dried basil
1 tsp. black pepper
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 c. frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed.

Directions:
In a large bowl, break up ground turkey. Add remaining ingredients, and gently mix until well combined. Careful to to over mix. Press into patties. Grill or cook until fully cooked.
