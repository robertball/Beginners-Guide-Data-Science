Title:
Easy & Delicious 3-Bean Crockpot Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.25 pounds ground turkey
1 large onion, chopped
1 tbl minced garlic
3 cans kidney beans, drained
3 cans stewed tomatoes, undrained
1 can corn, drained
2 cans butter beans, drained
2 cans pinto beans, drained
2 tbl chili powder
1 tsp cayenne pepper
2 tsp cumin
1 tsp coriander
1/2 tsp red hot chili flakes
1 tbl oregano
2 tsp salt

Directions:
In a large saucepan, brown the onion with the garlic.
Add the turkey meat; brown.
In your crockpot, add ALL the canned items, and the turkey mixture.
Add all the spices.
Stir and set the crockpot on LOW for 4-8 hours.
