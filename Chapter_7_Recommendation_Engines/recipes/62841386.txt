Title:
Christi's Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 bell peppers, cut in half
cooking oil
1 pod garlic
2 lbs ground beef
salt & pepper
1 chopped onion
1 chopped bell pepper
1 chopped celery stick
1/4 c water
2 c cooked rice
1/4 block butter
parsley & shallots

Directions:
Cut peppers in half & clean out.
In a saucepan, heat cooking oil.
Add garlic, beef, salt & pepper
Cook until brown
Add onion, bell pepper,celery.
Cook until soft.
Add 1/4 c water.
Stir well, Cover & cook for 15 mins.
Add rice, butter, parsley & shllots
Mix well. If too moist add small amount of bread crumbs.
Fill bell pepper halves.
Sprinkle with bread crumbs. Dot with butter. Place peppers in baking dish. Add 1/2 c water in bottom of dish and bake at 350 degrees until top is brown
