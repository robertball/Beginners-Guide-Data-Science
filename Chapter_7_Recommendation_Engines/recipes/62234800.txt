Title:
Classic Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 tbsp butter
6 carrots, chopped
3 celery stalks, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 onion, chopped
4 cups chicken broth
6 cups peeled and diced potatoes
4 cups milk
1/2 cup cubed turkey ham
1/2 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. ground cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp. ground mustard
2 tbsp. cornstarch
1/2 cup warm water

Directions:
In large stock pot melt butter over medium heat.  Add celery, carrots, green pepper and onion.  Cook and stir until tender.
Add potatoes and broth, simmer until tender.
Stir in milk, seasonings, and turkey ham.
Dissolve cornstarch in 1/2 cup water and slowly stir into soup.
Bring to a gentle boil for 1 minute, then turn heat to low - medium low and simmer soup for 15 to 20 minutes.

Description: 
This soup is full of delicious veggies.  Serve with fresh home-made cornbread for a delicious hearty dinner.
