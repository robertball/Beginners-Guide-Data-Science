Title:
Roast Beef and Arugula Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989892.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon low-fat mayonnaise
2 teaspoons horseradish
4 slices Roman Meal Bread
4 slices tomato
4 ounces lean roast beef, very thinly sliced
1 cup arugula or wild greens

Directions:
Spread mayonnaise and horseradish evenly over 2 bread slices.
Layer tomato, beef and arugula between slices of bread creating 2 sandwiches.

Submitter: 
Roman Meal
Description: 
A touch of spicy horseradish makes this quick sandwich a delight.
