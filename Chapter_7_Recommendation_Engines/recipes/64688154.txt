Title:
skillet roasted cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5382753.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower
3 tbsp olive oil
small sweet onion, sliced
4 cloves garlic chopped
1/2 tsp basil
1/2 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp parsley
1/2 c fresh grated parmesan cheese
salt to taste
pepper to taste

Directions:
heat oven to 425 F
heat olive oil in skillet
add onions and salt
saute for 15 min. then add garlic and herbs
add cauliflower and toss together
place skillet in oven, roast for 35 min, stirring half way through roasting time
sprinkle with cheese and serve
