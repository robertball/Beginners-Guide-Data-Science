Title:
Shrimp Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. chili powder
1 tsp. garlic salt
1 Tbsp. olive oil
1 Tbsp. lime juice
1 lb. shrimp, peeled, deveined and tails removed
1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
1 orange bell pepper, thinly sliced
1 red onion, thinly sliced
Fajita-size flour tortillas
Thousand Island dressing
Cheese, cilantro, avocado, sour cream, lettuce, tomatoes, etc.

Directions:
In a medium bowl, whisk together chili powder, garlic salt, oil and lime juice. Add shrimp and toss to coat. Let marinade for 10 minutes.
Heat a large skillet or griddle over med-high heat. Spray with non-stick cooking spray. Add red and orange peppers, onion. Saute until tender, about 7-8 minutes. Transfer to a bowl and cover with foil to keep warm.
In the same skillet, add shrimp with marinade. Cook shrimp about 2 minutes on each side.
Serve shrimp, onion and peppers in flour tortillas and garnish with Thousand Island dressing and desired toppings.
