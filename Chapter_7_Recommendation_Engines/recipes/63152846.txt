Title:
Carne ( Beef) Picada Tortillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1 lb Carne Picada
2. 1 large onion sliced
3.1 large yellow pepper thin slices ( could use red or green...yellow is great for color and sweetness)
4. 1/4 cup olive oil
5. 1 15oz can black beans - drained and rinsed
6. 2 talb white wine vinegar
7. 1 teas pepper ( I used a steak spice seasoning)
8. 1/2 teas thyme
9. 1/2 teas chili pepper powder
10. 1 teas (goya) Adabo all purpose seasoning with pepper
11. 1  1/2 cups cooked rice
12. 1/2 cup cut up tomatoes
13. 1/2 cup shredded cheese - cheddar and monterey mix
14. 1/2 cup sour cream
15. 1/2 cup lettuce shredded
16. 4-6 large flour tortilla( 10")

Directions:
cook rice as directed to make 1 1/2 cups cooked amount ...while the rice is cooking
in lg frying pan -place 4.and 3. and 2....med heat...cook till tender and remove to a bowl
add 1. to the fry pan ( might have to add more oil)
cook med heat 3-5 min till cooked...add 6. 7.8. 9. 10
stir...add 5. and heat for 2-3 min...add cooked rice and stir
Heat up 16. and serve the beef/rice/bean  mixture
in tortilla...garnish with 12.13.14.15.

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
the beef was so tender and the flavor amazing....Carne Picada cut of beef with peppers,onions,rice,beans and more wrapped in a large flour tortilla...I asked my husband what he would call what we ate and he said ...delicious ....smart man!
