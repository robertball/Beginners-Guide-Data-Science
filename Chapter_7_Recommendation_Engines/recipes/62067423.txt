Title:
Buttermilk corn bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c cornmeal
1 c bisquick
1/2 c butter or margarine, melted
1 c buttermilk or sour milk
1 tsp baking soda
2 eggs
1/2 c sugar

Directions:
Heat oven to 425.  Grease 8" square pan or 9" round pan.
Stir all ingredients til smooth.  Pour into pan.  Bake 18-20 min or until golden brown.  Serve warm.

Submitter: 
ESTEPHAN
Description: 
Quick and easy corn bread, just sweet enough.  Uses up your sour milk.
