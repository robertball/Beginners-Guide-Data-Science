Title:
Thai Curry Black Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp canola oil
1/2 - 1 package A Taste of Thai Panang Curry Paste
1 large onion, diced
1 red bell pepper, diced
1 green bell pepper, diced
6 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbsp oregano
1 lb. dry black beans, cooked (or 4 15 oz cans, undrained)
1 28 oz can tomatoes
1/4 tsp salt
chopped cilantro to taste, as garnish

Directions:
Heat oil in large pot over medium heat.  Saute onion and bell pepper until softened.   Add Panang curry paste and whisk until dissolved.  Add garlic and oregano; saute another minute or two.  Stir in black beans and tomatoes, adding water as necessary.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat, simmer 30 minutes.  Serve with cilantro (over cooked rice if desired).
