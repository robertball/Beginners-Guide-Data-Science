Title:
Soupa Avgolemono (Egg-Lemon Soup) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups chicken broth
3/4 cup uncooked white rice or orzo pasta
3 eggs
juice of 1 lemon

Directions:
Bring broth to boil in saucepan.
Add rice or orzo and cover and cook on medium-low heat for 20-25 minutes (rice) or 10 minutes (orzo).
While rice cooks, lightly beat eggs and lemon juice together.
When rice/pasta is done, remove from heat and slowly mix about one cup of hot broth into the egg/lemon mixture.
Gradually add the egg/lemon/broth mixture to the soup, stirring constantly.
Place saucepan back on heat and stir until soup is heated through (do not boil).
