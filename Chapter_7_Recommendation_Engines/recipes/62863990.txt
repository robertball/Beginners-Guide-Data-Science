Title:
Fudgier, Denser Best Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 cup self-rising flour
3 tablespoons butter, softened
3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
2 tablespoons milk
1 tablespoon honey
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup confectioners' sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8 inch square pan.
In a large saucepan, melt 1/2 cup butter. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Stir in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into prepared pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook.
To Make Glaze:  Melt 3 tablespoons butter in small saucepan. Add 3 tablespoons cocoa, 2 tablespoons milk, 1 tablespoon honey, 1 teaspoon vanilla, and 1 cup confectioners' sugar, mix well.  Pour over brownies while they are still warm for a nice glossy glaze finish
