Title:
Rustic Mushroom Herb Cheese bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf of your favorite artisian bread
6 cups sliced mushrooms
2 cloves garlic, minced
4 sprigs fresh Thyme
Chives for garnish
1 tablespoon olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
6 slices of brick sharp Cheddar cheese
6 slices of brick Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
Heat oil in a skillet, and brown mushrooms on medium high heat for 3-5 minutes.
Turn off heat and add garlic to pan, stir.
Preheat oven to 400 and line sheet with tinfoil
Cut your bread in half, lengthwise.
Evenly distribute mushrooms over both halves of bread.
Sprinkle with fresh Thyme
Layer cheese on top alternating between the two. (may need more cheese depending on length of your loaf of bread)
Bake in oven till heated through and cheese is melted about 15-20 minutes (broil to get cheese crispy and browned).
Remove from oven and sprinkle with fresh Chives.
Enjoy

Submitter: 
Chef V
Description: 
I like to serve this with a bowl of soup, but this also makes a great lunch or appetizer on its own. I like to use a sharp Cheddar and Monterey Jack cheese, but can be customized to suit your tastes.
