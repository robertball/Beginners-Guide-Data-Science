Title:
Hula's Key Lime Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 egg yolks - save the whites for something else
2 cans condensed milk
1 cup lime juice preferably FRESH
Lime zest from 3 BIG limes or from 4 not so big fellas

Directions:
Set oven to 375
Note you need to make a graham cracker crust first so look that up under recipes.
Zest and juice limes
Mix everything together and mix really well, BAM you're done, now just bake!
May need to bake longer than 15 minutes just make sure middle isn't wiggly before you take it out.
