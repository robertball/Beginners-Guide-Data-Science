Title:
Strawberry Poppy Seed Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2492808.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup raspberry vinegar
1/4 cup wild flower honey
1/2 tsp minced garlic
1 tsp minced onion
1/2 tsp lavender salt or sea salt
8 cracks of fresh ground pepper
4-5 medium/large strawberries
1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 tsp poppy seeds

Directions:
Add all ingredients except oil and poppy seeds to Magic Bullet or blender. Blend until smooth and there are no chunks of strawberry left. Stop blender and scrape honey from bottom of blender if necessary. Add oil and briefly blend until incorporated. Stir in poppy seeds, but do not blend as to not break up the seeds. Pour into a squeeze bottle and store in fridge.

Submitter: 
Samuela
Description: 
This dressing is great on avocados, salads, or even fresh baked chicken and fish. It's easy and delicious!
