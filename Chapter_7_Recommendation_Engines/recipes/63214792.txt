Title:
Curry Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 boneless chicken breasts (I grill the chicken after pounding it out with kosher salt and ground pepper)cooked and cubed, 1 Â½ cups green grapes (halved), 4 ounce package (1 cup) sliced almonds, 1 cup Granny Smith apples with peel, 1 cup diced celery, 2 cups mayonnaise, 1 tablespoon curry, 1 teaspoon lemon juice, 1 Head of crisp iceberg lettuce.

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine chicken, grapes, apples, celery and almonds. Mix well. In a separate bowl, blend mayonnaise, curry and lemon juice well. Pour over chicken mixture and toss. If it looks dry, add more mayonnaise. Serve over a bed of crisp iceberg lettuce.  Refrigerate unused portions.
