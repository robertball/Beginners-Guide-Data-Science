Title:
Crustless Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. cooked, cubed ham (or any leftover meat)
1 c. sour cream
6 beaten eggs
1/4 c. grated swiss cheese
1/4 c. melted butter
3/4 c. bread crumbs or Panko

Directions:
Mix meat, sour cream, eggs and cheese together until well blended.  Pour into a greased 8 inch square baking dish.  In a separate bowl mix together bread crumbs and melted butter.  Top egg mixture with breadcrumb mixture.  Bake at 375 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes or until a knife comes out clean.

Submitter: 
drjwall
Description: 
I love this recipe when I want to make quiche, but don't have time to make a pie crust.  It's simple, filling, and delicious served with salsa or hot sauce!
