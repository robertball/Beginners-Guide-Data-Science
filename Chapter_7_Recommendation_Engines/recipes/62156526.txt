Title:
Flour-less Oatmeal Pancake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pancake:
non-stick cooking spray
1/2 cup 5-minute oats
2 egg whites
1 tsp. Splenda or another zero calorie sweetener
1/4 tsp. Cream of Tartar
1/2 soft banana
Topping:
1/2 cup frozen berries
1/2 tsp. Splenda or another zero calorie sweetener

Directions:
Pancake:
Spray a small frying pan with non-stick cooking spray and place on stove over medium heat.
Beat remaining pancake ingredients with a fork until well combined and/or slightly frothy.
Once frying pan is hot, pour mixture into pan to make pancake(s).
Brown on one side, then flip to brown the other.
Transfer pancake to plate.
Topping:
Place frozen berries in a microwave-safe bowl and microwave for 1 - 2 minutes, until boiling.
Pour over pancake.
