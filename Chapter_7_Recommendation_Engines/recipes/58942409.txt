Title:
Garden Fresh Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups chopped fresh tomatoes
1 slice onion
4 whole cloves
3 garlic cloves smashed
1 bay leaf
1 teaspoon thyme
2 Tablespoons of chooped basil
2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/2 cup of milk
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons white sugar, or to taste

Directions:
In a stockpot, over medium heat, stir fry the onion, garlic clove and then add  the tomatoes, bay leaf, thyme  and broth. Bring to a boil, and gently boil for about 20 minutes to blend all of the flavors. Remove from heat, let cool. Remove the bay leaf and discard.  Puree the tomato mix with the basil in a blender.
In the now empty stockpot, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour to make a roux, cooking until the roux is a medium brown. Gradually whisk in thye milk, a bit  at a time , so that no lumps form, then stir in the rest of the tomato mixture. Season with sugar and salt, and adjust to taste.
