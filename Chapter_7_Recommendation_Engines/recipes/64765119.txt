Title:
Slow Cooker Italian Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs Italian sausage
3 med chopped onions (2 1/4 C )
2 C sliced fresh mushrooms
6 garlic cloves minced
2 cans (14 oz ea) diced tomatoes undrained
1 can (29 oz) tomato sauce
1 can (12 oz) tomato paste
2 Tbs dries basil leaves
1Tbs dried oregano leaves
1 Tbs sugar
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 pkg of McCormick Italian style spaghetti mix

Directions:
Cook sausage, onions, mushrooms, and garlic in 12 in skillet over med heat about 10 min, stirring, until sausage is no longer pink; drain.
Spoon sausage into a 5 Qt slow cooker. Stir in remaining ingredients.
Cover and cook on low. 8-9 hrs or until veggies are tender
