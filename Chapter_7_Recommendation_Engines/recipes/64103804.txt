Title:
Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup oil
2 cups sugar
4 eggs
2 cups pumpkin (16 oz can)
2/3 cup water
3-1/3 cup flour
2 tsp soda
1-1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp cloves
1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)
2/3 cup raisins (optional)

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F.  Grease two 9x5x3 inch loaf pans or three 8-1/2x4-1/2x2-1/2 inch loaf pans.
In large bowl mix oil and sugar.  Stir in eggs, pumpkin and water.  Blend in flour, soda, salt, baking powder, cinnamon and cloves.  Stir in nuts and raisins.
Pour into pans and bake for 70 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.  Remove from pan and cool.
