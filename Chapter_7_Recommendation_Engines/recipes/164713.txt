Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Chocolate Cereal Bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986938.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1 (12 ounce) bag semisweet chocolate chips
2 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal

Directions:
In a large heavy sauce pan, melt chocolate and butter over low heat, stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Stir in KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal.
Immediately spread hot mixture thinly on foil lined baking sheet coated in cooking spray. Let cool. Refrigerate until hard. Break into bite size pieces. Store in airtight container in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*
Description: 
Thin, crunchy and sweet, these easy bite-sized treats are made with chocolate chips and KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*.
