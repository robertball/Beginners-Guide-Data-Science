Title:
Cabbage Balushka or Cabbage and Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb bg of egg noodles
1 lg onion
1 head of cabbage
8 Tbsp of real butter (Only real butter will do)
Salt and black pepper to taste.

Directions:
Boil egg noodles and drain. Set aside.
Chop onions and cabbage. Melt butter in large skillet or Wok. Brown onions, wilt cabbage, add egg noodles. salt pepper to taste. stir fry. and serve.

Submitter: 
rlecook
Description: 
This Hungarian Favorite is cabbage, onions, and egg noodles, sauteed in butter. salt pepper to taste. So easy, so good.
