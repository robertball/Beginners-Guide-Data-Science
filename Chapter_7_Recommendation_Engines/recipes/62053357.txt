Title:
A Favorite Fudge Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup melted butter,
1 cup sugar,
2 eggs,
1 tsp vanilla extract,
2 squares melted chocolate,
1/4 cup self-rising flour,
1/2 cup chopped pecans.

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except flour, and pecans in a medium saucepan, until well blended over medium-low heat.  Remove from hear; add flour and pecans.  Pour into greased 9 inch pie pan, and bake at 325 degrees for 30 minutes.  Do not over cook, it will taste burned.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
I had a pie similar to this I had bought at Wal-Mart, and I absolutely loved it.  So I had to find out how to make it on my own.  I have a friend who is very good a deciphering what ingredients are in recipes he tastes, so this is what we came up with.
