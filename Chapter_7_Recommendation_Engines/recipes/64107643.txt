Title:
Pea pesto and goat cheese crostini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1255277.jpg
Ingredients:
2 baguette
olive oil
2 garlic cloves
1.5 c edamame
1 c peas
3-4 garlic cloves
Little olive oil
1 lemon juiced
Handful basil
Handful parsley

Directions:
slice baguette in 1/4 in slices
brush both sides with with 1-2 garlic cloves, olive oil and sprinkle with salt
bake 400 for about 5-10 min, until start to crisp
combine peas, edamame, garlic, olive oil, salt, lemon, parsley, basil in blender and puree until blended
spread pesto on bread
add little goat cheese
bake another 5 min or until goat cheese softens and crostini are golden/crisp
serve with chopped green onion, parsley, and/or basil
