Title:
Mexican Indian Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup long rice
2 1/4 cup water
1 large can diced tomatoes
chopped green pepper
chopped onion
dash of cumin
dash of turmeric
dash of curry
dash of garlic powder

Directions:
Mix ingredients.
Bring to boil.
Simmer for 45 minutes (covered)
Let sit for 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
A mix of Mexican and Indian spices for a great international meal
