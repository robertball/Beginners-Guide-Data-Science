Title:
Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 stick butter (softned)
2 eggs
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups wheat flour
3 mashed bananas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees then butter & flour loaf or mini loaf pan.
cream: sugar, butter & eggs
in seperate bowl mix: flour & soda then add to wet
mash 3 ripe bananas and add to mixture
bake 40-45 minutes, until toothpick comes out clean.

A moist and delicious bread the entire family will love.
