Title:
Dorothy's  Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
4 beef bouillon cubes
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 onion, chopped
1/4 cup butter

Directions:
Melt butter in pan. Saute onion until soft.
Combine water, bouillon, and flour, in a small saucepan.  Bring to a boil over medium heat and cook until thickened.

Submitter: 
Dorothy Clark
Description: 
A quick and easy gravy made with beef bouillon, flour, onion and butter.  Serve over mashed potatoes, if desired.Modified from: Simple Beef Flavored Gravy
