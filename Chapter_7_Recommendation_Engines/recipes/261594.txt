Title:
Delicious Pan-Fried Baka Bana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4580203.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 pinch salt
1/2 cup warm water
1 large ripe plantain, sliced
vegetable oil for frying

Directions:
Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl. Add warm water, stirring constantly, until a pancake-like batter forms, about 2 minutes. Dip plantain slices into the batter.
Heat vegetable oil in a deep skillet over medium heat. Cook plantains in batches until golden, about 2 minutes. Flip and cook for 1 minute more. Drain on paper towels.
