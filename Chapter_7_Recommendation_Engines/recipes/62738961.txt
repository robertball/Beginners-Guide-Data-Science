Title:
RORY'S RIBS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/693750.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp.extra-virgin olive oil
3 garlic cloves,crushed
2 1/4 cups ketchup
1 3/4 cup chicken broth,divided
7 Tbsp.soy sauce,divided
6 Tbsp.packed brown sugar
4 1/2 Tbsp.light molasses
3 Tbsp.red wine vinegar
3 slabs baby back ribs
6 Tbsp.chili powder
salt and pepper

Directions:
Heat oil in sauce pan over med. heat.Press garlic,and add to oil,Stir 30 seconds.Add ketchup,3/4 cup of broth,3 Tbsp.soy sauce,brown sugar,molasses and vinegar.Bring sauce to a simmer,reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until reduced to 3 cups.Stir occasionally.Transfer to bowl.Cover and chill.
Sprinkle each side of each rib with salt,pepper and 1 Tbsp.chili powder, cayenne pepper can also be used for extra spicy.Arrange 3 racks of ribs on 2 baking sheets.Postion one oven rack in third top of oven and second oven rack on bottom third level.Preheat oven to 450F.Pour 1/2 cup of remaining broth and 2 Tbsp.remaining soy sauce around ribs on each baking sheet,cover with foil.Bake 30 min.then reverse sheets.Bake until tender,about 45 min.Remove foil and baste with BBQ sauce.Bake another 10-15 minutes.
TO BBQ: Preheat BBQ to medium-high.Cook ribs in large foil roasting pan,with the broth and soy sauce,cover with foil.Baste with the BBQ sauce.
