Title:
BROCCOLI SOUFFLE  */+ Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/686574.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups mashed cooked broccoli
1 cup cooked rice (leftover rice or you can cook it ahead of time)
1 small grated onion
1/2 8 oz carton sour crem
2 cups grated sharp cheddar cheese (i added a little extra)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix all ingredients except for 1 cup of the cheese.
Place in buttered baking dish and cover with remaining cheese.
Bake for 15 minutes or until cheese is nicely melted and browned.  You can also put the broiler to it for about 5 minutes if you like.
