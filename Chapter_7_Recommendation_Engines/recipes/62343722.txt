Title:
World's Best Cold Tuna Noodle Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One 7 oz. package of Creamette Rings
1/2 can (2.5 oz) of Starkist ChunkLight Tuna in water (drained)
1-2 cups MiracleWhip Salad Dressing (based on your liking)
2 Tbsp. white vinegar
1/2 Tbsp. lemon juice
1/8 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup diced onion
1/2 cup unthawed green peas
1 tsp. fresh ground black pepper
1/4 tsp. dried dill weed
2 Tbsp. milk (optional)

Directions:
Cook 7 oz. package of Creamette Rings as directed on package. Drain & rinse with cold water for 2 minutes. Shake well & set aside in large mixing bowl. Add drained tuna, MiracleWhip salad dressing, white vinegar, lemon juice, celery, onion, unthawed green peas,fresh ground pepper, and dried dill weed. Mix well. Chill for 1 hour & serve. **You may add 2 Tbsp. of milk to make it more creamy if you like!**

Submitter: 
Sarah Simons
Description: 
This is a recipe from my grandmother Carol. It is wonderful everytime. Great to bring to a potlock, compliments any grilled/baked meal! Definitely a crowd pleaser! Very easy to make!
