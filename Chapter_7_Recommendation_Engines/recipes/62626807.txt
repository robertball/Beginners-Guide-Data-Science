Title:
Sausage Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. spicy sausage
1 lb. regular sausage
1 cup chopped green chiles
1 cup diced onion
1/2 cup diced bell pepper
1/2 cup diced celery
2 cloves garlic, finely diced
2 tablespoons butter
1 1/2 cups thick salsa
2 cups shredded cheddar
1 large loaf Italian or French bread

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400&#0176;
Brown and drain sausages; place in large bowl.
Melt butter in skillet and saute veggies for 5-10 minutes.
Add salsa and cheese and heat thoroughly.
Combine with sausage.
Cut bread in half lengthwise; carefully remove soft insides of both halves.
Fill hollowed out loaf, rounding to fill top when replaced.
Replace top and wrap tightly in heavy duty foil.
Bake at 400&#0176; for 15 minutes.
Serve with sour cream.
