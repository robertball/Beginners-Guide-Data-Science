Title:
Simple Black Eyed Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans (15 oz each) black eyed peas, rinsed and drained very well
2 jalapenos, seeded and diced
1 small red onion, diced
1/4 cup olive oil
2-3 Tbls red wine vinegar
salt and pepper

Directions:
Combine peas, jalapenos, red onion, olive oil and vinegar in a bowl. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Description:
