Title:
Pork Tenderloin with Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 envelope onion mushroom soup mix
1 pkg frozen spinach, cooked and drained
1/2 lb sweet Italian sausage, browned
1/2 cup Italian bread crumbs
1/2 cup sliced almonds, toasted
2 eggs, slightly beaten
2 tablespoons chopped parsley
2 teaspoons thyme leaves
1 teaspoon finely chopped garlic
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325
Butterfly 2 pork tenderloin fillets
Spread filling on one side of meat; tie into roll
Rub with oil and top with garlic and thyme
Roast 1 1/2 hours
