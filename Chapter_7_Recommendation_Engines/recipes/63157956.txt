Title:
BRAISED TURKEY WINGS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 turkey wings
Paprika
Salt
White pepper
Garlic salt
1 qt. chicken broth
1/2 c. flour
1/2 c. diced green bell pepper
1 c. diced onion
1 c. diced peeled tomatoes

Directions:
Cut turkey wings at joints and discard tips. Arrange meatier wing portions in shallow baking dish and season to taste with paprika, salt, white pepper and garlic salt. Bake at 450 degrees until golden brown, turning as needed to brown evenly, about 20 minutes.
Gradually stir chicken broth into flour until smooth. Add green pepper, onion and tomatoes. Pour sauce over turkey wings and continue baking at 350 degrees for 2 hours, or until tender.
