Title:
Spiced Air Fried Chickpea's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can chickpeas, rinsed and well drained
1 T Nutritional Yeast
1  t smoked paprika
1 t  garlic granules
1 pinch cumin
1/2 t coarse salt
1 T olive oil

Directions:
Rinse and drain the chickpeas and lay them on a double layer of paper towels, cover with another layer and let them dry for 30 minutes.
Set the temp on the air fryer to 180
Add the chickpeas to a medium bowl, when dry, and add the seasonings and oil, toss to coat.
Add the chickpeas to the air fryer and shake every 4 minutes.
Cook until crispy, about 20-22 minutes

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
Great for a quick snack or take them with you to eat along the way.
