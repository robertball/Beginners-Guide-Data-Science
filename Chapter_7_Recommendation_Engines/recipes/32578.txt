Title:
Strawberry Kiwi Milkshakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1010285.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups sliced fresh strawberries
4 cups sliced kiwifruit
4 cups milk
4 cups vanilla ice cream

Directions:
Place the strawberries, kiwi, milk, and ice cream in a blender (in batches, if necessary), and blend until smooth. Pour into tall cups, and enjoy!
