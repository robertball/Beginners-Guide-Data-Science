Title:
Chocolate Silk Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups crushed oreos
1\2 cup melted butter
3 tbl sugar
14 oz cream cheese
1 cup chocolate syrup
2 2/3 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips melted
2 8 oz cartons extra creamy whipped topping thawed
shaved dark chocolate bar opt.
homemade sweet whipped cream opt.

Directions:
In a bowl combine cookie crumbs, butter and sugar.
Press into the bottom and up the sides of a springform pan.
Bake at 375 degrees for 8-10min. Cool completely on wire rack.
In a mixing bowl beat cream cheese and chocolate syrup until well mixed.
Beat in melted chocolate.
Fold in whipped topping.
Pour into cooled crust and refrigerate over night.
Remove sides of pan.
Pipe homemade whipped cream around outside of pie shell style in 3 or 4 rows.
In the middle pile shaved chocalte bar.
Cut with hot, sharp knife.
Cut small pieces it's really rich!
