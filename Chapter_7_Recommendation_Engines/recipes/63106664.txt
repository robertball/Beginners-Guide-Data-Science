Title:
Creamy White Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*1 lb boneless skinless chicken, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
*1 onion, chopped
*1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
*1 Tablespoon vegetable oil
*2 (15-ounce) cans Great Northern beans, rinsed and drained
*1 (15-ounce) can chicken broth
*2 (4-ounce) cans chopped green chiles, drained
*1 teaspoon salt
*1 teaspoon cumin
*1 teaspoon oregano
*1/2 teaspoon black pepper
*1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
*1 cup sour cream
*1/2 cup heavy cream

Directions:
Saute' the chicken, onion and garlic powder in hot oil in a large saucepan over medium heat for 5 to 10 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink. Stir in the next 8 ingredients; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, for 30 minutes. Remove from heat. Stir in the sour cream and heavy cream and serve immediately. Yield: 4 to 6 servings.

Submitter: 
Grannies Rule
Description: 
I got this recipe from a friend of mine at work, and it is one of my family's favorites! It can be made healthier by substituting the sour cream and heavy cream with lowfat milk, and fat-free sour cream. It carries the following credit: Debra Pellett, Xi Beta Phi, Atlantic, Iowa
