Title:
Kickin' Tuna Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (large) Albacore Tuna in water
2 tsp Lemon Pepper
2 tsp Greek Seasoning (Cavendors)
1 tsp Garlic Powder
1/4 cup Mayonnaise
1 small Sweet Onion finely chopped
2 T Sport Peppers in oil finely chopped

Directions:
Combine all ingredients. Season to taste. Put on bead of choice and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Cindy
Description: 
My husband loves food with a bit of heat and this sandwich delivers. You get all the flavor of the tuna with a little kick. This tuna is great for salads or sandwiches.
