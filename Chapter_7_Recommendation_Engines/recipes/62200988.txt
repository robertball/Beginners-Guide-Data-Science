Title:
Old Fashioned Gingerbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/303541.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Splenda
1/2 cup butter
1 egg
1 cup molasses
2 1/2 cups self-rising flour
2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 teaspoon ground ginger
1/2 cup applesauce
1 cup hot water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Greased and flour one 9 inch square pan.
Cream together the sugar and butter or margarine.  Add egg, and beat well.  Mix in the molasses.
Sift together the flour,cinnamon, ginger and clove.  Add dry ingredients to the creamed mixture; add the hot water, and mix well.
Pour batter into pan and bake for one hour. Allow to cool in pan and serve.
