Title:
Breakfast Cookie II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ cup brown sugar
21/2 cups rolled oats
2 cups self rising flour
2 cups wheat flour
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 cup canola oil
1 cup no sugar added applesauce
2 tbsp water
5 egg whites
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
3/4 cup raisins
1/4 cup walnuts (opt)
3/4 cup chopped dates

Directions:
1. preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease cookie sheets.
2. In a large bowl, stir together oats, flours and cinnamon.
3. Make a well in the center and pour in canola oil, applesauce, water, egg whites and vanilla. Mix until well blended.
3. Stir in raisin, nuts and dates.
4. Scoop cookies onto cookie sheet or roll into golf ball sized rolls. Place cookies 2 inches apart and flatten to ½ tall with wet hands. These cookies do not flatten very much while baking.
5. In the preheated oven, bake 8 minutes for chewy cookies or 10-12 minutes for dry cookies.
6. Remove cookies from sheets and cool on wire rack.
