Title:
Fennel and carrot soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 small carrots
2 small fennel bulbs
1 small onion
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/2 teaspoon of caraway seeds
1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
1 teaspoon of ground coriander
1/3 cup shopped parsley
2 chicken breasts
1 bay leaf
1 1/2 cups of water
1 can of coconut milk
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine water, chicken breasts, bay leaf and some salt and cook until chicken is ready.  In the meantime, chop onion, fennel and carrots.  Heat oil in a frying pan, add caraway and cumin seeds and fry them lightly.  Add chopped vegetables and saute them until soft. Ground coriander and add to the vegetables. Remove chicken from the pot, add vegetables and boil until ready.  Process using hand held blender until smooth. Add coconut milk, salt and pepper to taste and re-heat.  Meantime, chop chicken and parsley. Add them to the soup and immediately remove soup from heat. Serve hot.
