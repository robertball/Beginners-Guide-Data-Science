Title:
Greek Roasted Garlic Chicken Sausage Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz package Roasted Garlic Chicken Sausage
2 tbsp olive oil
2 Cloves garlic, minced
1 Medium onion, chopped
1 Red bell pepper, chopped
1 tbsp minced fresh rosemary
4 Italian plum tomatoes, chopped, divided
1/2 cup Chopped green olives with pimiento, divided
1/2 cup Chopped black olives, divided
1 package (8 oz) Feta cheese, crumbled, divided
1 package Pita wraps
Rosemary sprigs

Directions:
In large fry pan over medium high heat, place olive oil.
Add garlic and chicken sausage and cook, stirring about 5 minutes.
Remove chicken and add onion, pepper and minced rosemary
Cook 5 minutes or until vegetables are tender
In large bowl, mix together chicken sausage, saut&eacute;ed vegetables, &frac12; of the tomatoes and olives and 1 cup of the crumbled feta cheese
Divide among 8 wraps, placing mixture on half of each
Roll mixture into crepes jelly-roll style, folding in sides, to contain filling
Place wraps in large glass baking dish and heat, covered, in 350&deg; oven for 15 minutes
Remove wraps to center of large platter.
Sprinkle with remaining feta cheese.
Spoon remaining tomatoes and olives down center of wraps.
Garnish with rosemary sprigs.
