Title:
Homemade Deviled Ham Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz sliced cooked ham (lunch-meat or any other)
1/4 of a medium onion
3 T Miracle Whip light
1 tsp prepared mustard
2 tsp honey
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
Tabasco, few shakes, or to taste
1 tsp dry mustard
1/2 tsp paprika
1/8 tsp salt
1/8 tsp white pepper

Directions:
Slice 1 quarter of a medium onion (about 2 T chopped), and cut ham into chunks.  Chop each very fine in food chopper or processor, and move to a bowl or container which has a lid.  Add all remaining ingredients; stir until very well mixed.  Refrigerate until ready to use.
Make delicious sandwiches on your choice of bread with a thin coat of mayo, sliced bread and butter pickles, and a generous layer of Deviled Ham.

Submitter: 
B L
Description: 
You will never again buy a ham spread product after tasting this recipe.  Use your mini chopper or food processor to whip up a large or small batch. Easily modified for personal tastes, but try it once as written - it is delicious -- AND -- it's low-fat too!  Makes delicious sandwiches with bread and butter pickles, perfect for summer.  Men and kids love these.
