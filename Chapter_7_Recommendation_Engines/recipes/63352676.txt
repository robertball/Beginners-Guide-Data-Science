Title:
Cacio e Pepe Plus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/891651.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb thin spaghetti
1 large chicken breast, cut into bite-sized chunks
2 tbsp butter
2 tbsp olive oil
8 oz fresh baby portobello mushrooms, whole or sliced
4 cloves fresh garlic, minced
1/2 large onion, thinly sliced
3/4 cup of white wine
2 tbsp freshly cracked black pepper or more, to taste
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta al dente according to package directions. Drain, toss with olive oil and set aside in a covered bowl.
In the same pot, melt butter over medium heat. Add onions and saute until translucent.
Add mushrooms, garlic and chicken. Allow to cook, stirring frequently, until chicken is opaque.
Add white wine. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
Add cooked pasta, black pepper and Parmesan cheese. Toss to incorporate. Serve immediately.
