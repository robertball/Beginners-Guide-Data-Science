Title:
Epic Strawberry Chocolate Banana Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1333851.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups 2% milk
1 banana
1 cup strawberry ice cream
2 tablespoons chocolate sauce

Directions:
Blend milk, banana, ice cream, and chocolate sauce together in a blender on high speed until smooth, about 1 minute.
