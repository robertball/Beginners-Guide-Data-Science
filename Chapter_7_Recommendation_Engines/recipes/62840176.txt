Title:
Short Grain Rice Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBSP olive oil
2 TBSP light-colored balsamic vinegar (or red wine vinegar)
1 clove garlic, finely chopped
2 TBSP chopped fresh parsley
salt and pepper to taste
1 small cucumber, chopped
1 1/2 cup cooked short-grain brown rice
2 TBSP dried cranberries
2 TBSP pecans, chopped

Directions:
Combine first 6 ingredients and allow 15 to 30 minutes to marinate. Stir in rice, cranberries and pecans. Chill and serve.

Submitter: 
SIXMITTS
Description: 
I love the nutty flavor of short-grain brown rice, and created this recipe to use the special blood-orange olive oil and cranberry-pear balsamic vinegar from Taste the Olive. The vinegar is a lovely pink color.
