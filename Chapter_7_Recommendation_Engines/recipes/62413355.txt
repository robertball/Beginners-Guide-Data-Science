Title:
Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. Sugar
1 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
1/2 tsp Ground Cloves
1/2 tsp Allspice
1/2 tsp Nutmeg
1/2 tsp ground Ginger
1/2 tsp Salt
1 1/2 C. Pumpkin (NOT "mix")
1 1/4 C. Carnation Evap Milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425F.
Blend sugar, spices, and salt. Add eggs, pumpkin, and Carnation milk.
Pour into unbaked pie shell.
Bake for 15 mins and reduce heat to 350F. Continue baking for 40 mins or until knife comes out clean.
