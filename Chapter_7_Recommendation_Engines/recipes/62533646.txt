Title:
Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/519165.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups sweet potato, cubed
1/2 cup white sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 tablespoons butter, softened
1/c cup milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/3 cup all purpose flour
3 tablespoons butter, softened
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  Put sweet potatoes in a medium saucepan with water to cover.  Cook over medium high heat until tender; drain and mash
In a large bowl, mix together the sweet potatoes, sugar, eggs, salt, butter, milk and vanilla.  Mix until smooth.  Transfer to a 9 X 13 pan.
In a medium bowl, mix the sugar and flour.  Cut in the butter until the mixture is coarse.  Stir in the pecans.  Sprinkle the mixture over the sweet potato mixture.
Bake in preheated oven 30 minutes, or until topping is lightly brown.
