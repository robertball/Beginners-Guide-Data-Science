Title:
Carrot Pineapple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981755.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large Carrot pared and shredded
1 (8 ounce) can crushed Pineapple, drained
1/4 cup Almonds, chopped
1/4 cup Golden Raisins
4 tablespoons Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
1 dash Ground Nutmeg

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl and mix well.   Chill to blend flavors.

Submitter: 
Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Description: 
A delicious medley of shredded carrots, crushed pineapple, almonds, and plump golden raisins get a kick from Marzetti® Slaw Dressing and ground nutmeg.
