Title:
Emeril's Essence Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2112081.jpg
Ingredients:
8 tbs (1/2 cup) paprika
3 tbs cayenne
5 tbs black pepper, freshly ground
6 tbs garlic powder
3 tbs onion powder
6 tbs salt
2 1/2 tbs dried oregano
2 1/2 tbs dried thyme

Directions:
mix all ingredients together and store in air tight container

Submitter: 
Sophie Pipilas
Description: 
I put this spice on just about everything I bake from sweet potatoes to fish
