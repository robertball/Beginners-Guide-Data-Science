Title:
Lemon Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of Lemon Supreme cake mix
1 stick of melted butter
3 eggs
1 box of powdered sugar
1 8 oz package of cream cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Mix cake mix, meled butter, and ONE egg. After mixture becomes well blended, press and flatten the mixture into a 13" x 9" baking dish. Then combine 2 eggs, a box of powdered sugar, and the pkg of cream cheese. Mix well and pour over the bottom layer. Cook in the oven until it is lightly brown. Allow to cool before cuting.
