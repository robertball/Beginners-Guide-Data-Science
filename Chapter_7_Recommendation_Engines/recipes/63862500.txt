Title:
Bill's Kitchen Sink Bread Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lbs or 700 grams of seasoned bread croutons
3 Tbsp olive oil
1/4 cup or 100 grams of butter
2 onions, chopped
4 large cloves of garlic, minced
4 stalks celery, chopped
3 teaspoons poultry seasoning
1 teaspoon pepper
Salt to taste
1 quart orange juice
2 carrots, grated
1 apple, peeled cored and chopped
1/2 cup chopped pecans or 80 grams
1/2 cup raisins or 90 grams
1/2 cup chopped prunes or 90 grams
1/2 cup pepitas or 80 grams
8 oz or 250 grams of mushrooms, sliced (optional)

Directions:
In a large stock pot add olive oil and melt butter over medium heat. Cook onion, garlic, celery and mushrooms until soft.
Season with poultry seasoning, salt, and pepper.
Stir in croutons until evenly coated.
Moisten with orange juice; mix well.
Add the balance of ingredients and continue to mix well.
Chill, and use as a stuffing for turkey, or bake in a buttered casserole dish at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 to 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
A bread stuffing which incorporates a generous amount of chopped celery, onion, seasonings, dried fruit and nuts yielding enough to dress a 20 to 25 pound turkey.Modified from: Bread and Celery Stuffing
