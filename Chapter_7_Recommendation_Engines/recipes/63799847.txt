Title:
Ed's Cabbage Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cabbage              1
Rice
Bacon
Onions
Tomato Juice
Butter (salted)
Pickling Salt
Pickling Vinegar
Pepper
Water

Directions:
1)Cut off bottom inch of cabbage from core & discard a few outer leaves.
2)Boil a stock pot of water place cabbage in core side up, when boiling separate leaves with a butter knife and fork and put in a roaster.
3)Cut off thick part of stem & trim bottom of leaves.
4)Boil new water add 2 tsp. of pickling salt & 2 Tbsp. pickling vinegar put in leaves and turn off heat and let sit until ready to wrap leaves.
5)Cut frozen 1 1/4lb bacon into 1/2" & 1&4" strips & until crisp then add diced onions do not drain grease add 2 Tbsp. of salted butter.
6)Try to roll some leaves, if too stiff re-boil water and place leaves in for a few minutes.  May have to do this 2 or 3 times.
7)Cook rice: 80 leaves for 6 cups of uncooked rice add salt and butter when cooking rice, then wrap rice in leaves.
8)Place layer of leaves on bottom of roaster with tomato juice.
9)Layer cabbage rolls after every layer put tomato juice salt and pepper, top with cabbage leaves & juice.
10)Bake 350F for 1 1/2hrs to 2 hours, check after 1 1/2 hours to see if cabbage is soft.
