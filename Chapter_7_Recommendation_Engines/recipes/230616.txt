Title:
Bhel Puri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, finely chopped
3/4 cup grated fresh coconut
2 tablespoons finely chopped green chile pepper
2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh cilantro
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon coconut oil, melted (optional)
1/2 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
salt to taste
1 tablespoon water
2 cups puffed rice

Directions:
Mix onion, grated coconut, green chile pepper, cilantro, lemon juice, coconut oil, ginger, and salt together in a bowl; stir in water until well incorporated. Fold puffed rice into onion-coconut mixture until coated.
