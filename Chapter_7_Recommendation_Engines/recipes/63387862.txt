Title:
Baked Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/908557.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb. lean hamburger
1 5oz. can evaporated milk
1/2 c. oatmeal rolled or instant
2 eggs
1/4 c. minced onion
1 clove minced garlic
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 pkg. onion soup mix

Directions:
Combine all and mix well.  On a cookie sheet or jelly roll pan sprayed with cooking spray, space out approximately 48 walnut sized meatballs, or 36 more hefty ones.  Bake in oven at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
ttkc
Description: 
Husbands favorite meatballs for pasta dishes (he has told me to stop experimenting).  This makes about 4 dozen to freeze for quick dinners.
