Title:
Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Medium Onion Diced
4 Cloves Garlic Minced
1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
2 Medium Tomatoes Diced
2 Tablespoons Tomato Paste
3 Cups Chicken Broth
1/4 Teaspoon Dried Oregano
1/4 Teaspoon Salt
1/4 Teaspoon Black Pepper
2 Cups Rice

Directions:
Saute onions in olive oil till just starting to get soft. Add garlic and cook until fragrant. Add tomato, chicken broth, tomato paste, oregano, salt and pepper. When boils, cover tightly and simmer on low for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
John Mark
Description: 
Easy to make rice that goes well with any mexican food.
