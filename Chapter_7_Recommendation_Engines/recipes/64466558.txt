Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of chocolate chips (at least a 12 oz bag)
1 package of white marshmallows (tiny ones are best)(at least 10 oz bag)
2 teaspoons vegetable oil (or coconut or olive oil)
1 cup of tiny butterscotch chips (or whatever tiny chips you like - mint chocolate makes a wonderful addition)

Directions:
Line casserole dish or baking pan 12x12 with wax paper
Pour marshmallows into a bowl
Add chocolate chips and oil to saucepan, medium heat, stirring constantly until melted
Pour melted chocolate over the marshmallows and stir gently to coat evenly
Spread mixture into the lined dish/pan and sprinkle with butterscotch chips
Chill for at least one hour and enjoy!
