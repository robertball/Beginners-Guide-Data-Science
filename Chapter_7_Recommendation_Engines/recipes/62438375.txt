Title:
Vegetable Batter Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup flour
3/4 cup cornstarch
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup water
1 egg, lightly beaten

Directions:
Mix the dry ingredients. Add water and egg. Mix well. Use to coat cauliflower, onions, zucchini, green beans, etc. Deep fry to golden brown.

Submitter: 
Jamie Lowe
Description: 
A easy, light batter that you can cover basically any vegetable with.
