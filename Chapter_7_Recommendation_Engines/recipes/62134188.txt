Title:
Buffalo Chicken Dip II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8oz packages cream cheese - softened
2 10oz cans chicken breast meat (can substitute boiled chicken breast meat - shredded)
1 bottle Buffalo Wing sauce (like Sweet Baby Rays)
1/2 cup Blue Cheese dressing
3/4 cup Ranch dressing
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Drain canned chicken and place in a small bowl.
Add wing sauce to chicken and, using a fork, mix gently pulling apart any large chunks.
Set aside to marinate.
In a small bowl combine softened cream cheese and 1/2 cup blue cheese dressing, stirring until smooth.
Spread mixture in the bottom of 2qt (7"x11") glass baking dish.
Pour chicken and sauce mixture over the top of cream cheese mixture, making sure to spread evenly.
Next pour 3/4 cup ranch dressing over chicken, again spreading evenly.
Top with shredded cheddar cheese.
Place in oven for 15-20 minutes, or until cheese is melted and mixture is bubbly at the sides of dish.
Serve warm with tortilla chips or cut up celery.

Description: 
This warm and creamy dip tastes just like a buffalo wing without the mess!
