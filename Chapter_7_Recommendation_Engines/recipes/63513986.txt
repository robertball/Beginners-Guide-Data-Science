Title:
Grandma Evoy's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meatloaf:
3 pounds of ground beef
2 cups of fresh bread crumbs
2 medium onions chopped
1 8 oz can Hunts Tomato Sauce
2 eggs beaten
3 teaspoons salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
____________________
Sauce:
1 8 oz can Hunts Tomato Sauce
2 cans of water
4 tablespoons vinegar
4 tablespoons brown sugar
4 tablespoons of prepared mustard

Directions:
For the meatloaf; Lightly mix all the ingredients and form into a loaf. Place in a shallow pan and bake at 350.
While meatloaf is baking combine all sauce ingredients in a saucepan. Heat and simmer.
Pour over meatloaf baking for 1 1/2 hours longer, basting occasionally.
