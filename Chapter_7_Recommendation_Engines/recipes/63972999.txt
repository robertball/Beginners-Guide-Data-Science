Title:
Mom's Perfect Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices dry bread
2 lb. ground beef
4 eggs
1 c. grated Parmesan
1/4 c. parsley
1 tsp. oregano
2 tsp. salt
dash pepper
milk

Directions:
Pour milk over bread until just soaked.  /let stand 2 - 3 min. until well moistened.
Squeeze out excess milk.
Combine soaked bread with all other ingredients in a large mixing bowl.  Mix thoroughly.
Form into small balls.
Brown in oven at 350.  Drain grease.
Serve immediately or freeze.
