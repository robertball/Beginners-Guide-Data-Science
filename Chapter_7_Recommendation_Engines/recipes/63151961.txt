Title:
Meyer Lemon Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 3/4 oz meyer lemon juice (3-4 lemons)
1 1/4 cup sugar
3 large eggs
1/4 cup melted butter
9 inch pastry shell

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine first 3 ingredients in a blender, and blend for 3 minutes or until smooth.
Add butter and blend for another 30 seconds.
Pour into pastry shell and bake for 30-35 minutes.
Rest pie for at least 15 minutes to set.

Submitter: 
Melissa Cebrian
Description: 
A tangy sweet and sour treat, the meyer lemons make it sweeter than a traditional lemon pie, but still keep that citrus kick.
