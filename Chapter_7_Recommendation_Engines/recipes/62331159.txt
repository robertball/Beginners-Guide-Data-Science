Title:
Maple Dill Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups peeled and sliced carrots
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon chopped dried dill
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Place carrots in a skillet and pour in just enough water to cover. Bring to a boil over medium heat; turn down and simmer until the carrots are tender, about 10 minutes. Stir in butter, brown sugar, dill, salt, and pepper.

Submitter: 
The-Baby-Bow-Lady
Description: 
Melted butter, brown sugar, and dill complement the carrots while bringing out their natural flavor. The kids love these, and so do I!Modified from: Maple Dill Carrots
