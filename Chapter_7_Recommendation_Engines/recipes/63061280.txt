Title:
Boudreaux's Texicajun Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/770714.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small package fresh mushrooms
2 Green Bell Peppers- Finely Chopped
4 tbsp dried granulated garlic
4 tbsp dried onions
1 large yellow onion
2 tbsp cajun seasoning
1 cup finely chopped celery stalks
1/4 cup olive oil
1 1b. package grated cheddar cheese
2 lb. ground meat
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can tomato soup
1 can tomato sauce
1 small jar green olives chopped
8 oz. package wide noodles
Add 1 small can of chiles or rotel for a spicy version

Directions:
Add olive oil to the pan and brown the meat with the cajun seasoning.  Add & Saute all of the vegetables in with the meat until they are clear.  Cook noodles separately  and drain.  After meat and Veggies are ready, combine Meat & Veggies with Soups, Noodles and Green Olives in a large bowl.  Mix Well and pour into casserole dish.  Top with Cheese and cook at 350 until it is bubbling and hot in the middle of the pan.

Submitter: 
Lupe Boudreaux
Description: 
This is a take on a classic country noodle casserole dish.  This is a staple of traditional ranch cooking and I have of course cranked it up for my Texicajun friends. Carpe Diem and God Bless!
