Title:
Grandma Bev's Rhubarb Dessert (Rhubarb Crisp) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/965691.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust:
1c All purpose flour
5T powdered sugar
1/2c butter
Filling:
2 eggs, beaten
1 1/2c sugar
1/4c all purpose flour
3/4t baking powder
1/4t nutmeg
3c cut rhubarb

Directions:
Crust:
Cut butter into flour and powdered sugar until crumbly.
Pat into bottom of 8x8 pan.
Bake at 350F until golden (15-20 min)
Filling:
Beat eggs well.
Add all other dry ingredients and blend together.
Stir in rhubarb.
Pour filling onto crust.
Bake 35 minutes (a little longer with frozen rhubarb)
