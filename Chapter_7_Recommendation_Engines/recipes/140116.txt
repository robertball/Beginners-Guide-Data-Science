Title:
Rocky Mountain Citrus Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup orange vodka
3/4 cup white grapefruit juice
3/4 cup white cranberry juice
3 orange slices for garnish

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker 1/4 full with ice cubes. Pour in vodka, grapefruit juice, and cranberry juice. Cover and shake until the outside of the shaker has frosted. Strain into three chilled martini glasses and garnish with a slice of orange.

Submitter: 
NIEMEIER
Description: 
This is not your typical overly-sweet martini.  It's very refreshing and a favorite at our Colorado home!
