Title:
Ricotta Veggie Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. sliced mushrooms
2-3 large tomatoes, cored, seeded, and chopped
1 large onion, slivered
olive oil
2 T. balsamic vinegar
1 T. each fresh marjoram, basil, oregano
4 oz whole milk ricotta
pizza dough

Directions:
Roll out your favorite purchased pizza dough and set on a 16" pizza pan.  Preheat oven to 400.
Saute mushrooms in oil till golden brown.  Transfer to a bowl and stir in balsamic vinegar; add salt and pepper.
Saute onions in oil over medium-low heat till they turn a rich golden brown.  Add Salt and pepper.
Toss the tomatoes with the herbs and 1 T. olive oil in a small bowl.  Salt and pepper to taste.
Distribute onions, then mushrooms, then strained tomatoes.  Top with ricotta.  Drizzle with 1 to 2 T. olive oil.
Bake 20 minutes.
