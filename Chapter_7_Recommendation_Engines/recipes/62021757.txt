Title:
Braided Italian Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups warm water
2 packages yeast
4 tablespoons sugar
10 cups unbleached flour
4 teaspoons salt
4 eggs
3 tablespoons oil
1 egg for glaze

Directions:
Mix water, yeast and sugar. Let it sit until all ingredients are dissolved. With a wooden spoon, mix flour,salt, eggs and oil with yeast mixture. Mix well then turn out on well floured surface and knead dough until smooth and elastic. Place dough in greased bowl covered, let rise until double in size. Punch down then divide into 3 equal  parts, cut each into 3 parts roll each section into 12" ropes. Braid the 3 sections together and pinch ends to seal, and roll the ends under the loaf. Place loaves on parchment lined cookie sheet, let rise until double in size. Beat the remaining egg then brush each loaf completely. Bake at 375 for 20 - 30 minutes, until golden brown color.
