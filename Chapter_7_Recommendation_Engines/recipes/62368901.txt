Title:
Olive Salad Sandwhich Spread for Muffulettas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/498404.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup chopped stuffed green olives
1/4 cup chopped black olives
1/4 cup chopped green peppers
1/4 cup chopped mushroom
1/4 cup chopped pickles dill or sweet
1/4 cup chopped onions
1/4 cup chopped canned artichoke
1/4 cup chopped cucumber
1/4 cup chopped tomato
1   cup Italian salad dressing

Directions:
chop all ingredients mix in Italian dressing any kind, light or regular and mix put in a zip lock bag or storage container.  It is best to sit for a few days to incorporate the flavors.  In my house it gets eaten right away..

Submitter: 
Angel
Description: 
This spread goes on a muffuletta sandwich or as a side salad.  I hollow out a loaf of Italian bread and add this olive spread to both sides of bread and in the middle add the meats and cheese.  It will feed a family of six.
