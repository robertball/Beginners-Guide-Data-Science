Title:
grands taco melts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
taco seasoning
salsa
shredded cheese

Directions:
HEAT OVEN TO 375 DEGREES
cook ground beef.
add salsa
add taco seasoning
cook til thick
ROLL GRAND BISCUIT TO 6" CIRCLE
FILL 1/2 WITH TACO MEAT
ADD 1 TBLSPOON CHEESE
FOLD BISCUIT OVER FILLING AND SEAL
