Title:
Onion Soup Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 Tbsp. dried minced onion
3 cubes or 1 Tbsp. beef bouillon, crumbled
1 tsp onion powder
1/8 tsp. fresh ground black pepper
1/8 tsp. celery seed
1/8 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. parsley

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.  Use as you would a Lipton Onion Soup Packet.

Submitter: 
Claudia
Description: 
My own version of Lipton Onion Soup Mix.  This is good to make for a gluten-free diet.  Just make sure you use gluten-free bouillon.
