Title:
Heavenly Strawberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box vanilla wafers (crushed)
1/2 lb. butter, unsalted
4 eggs
1 (16 oz) box powdered sugar
1 qt. fresh strawberries
1 pt. (2 cups) whipping cream

Directions:
Finely crumble vanilla wafers and place all but 1/2 to 3/4 cup in a 9x13 glass dish.
In an electric mixer combine 1/2 lb. butter*, eggs, and powdered sugar.  Beat until light and fluffy.  Spread over crumbs.  (Carefully to avoid mixing up crumbs.)
Slice 1 quart fresh* strawberries and place over top of creamed mixture.
Beat whipping cream until stiff.  (DO NOT add sugar!)  Spread over strawberries, being sure to cover them well.
Sprinkle with remaining crumbs.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.
*DO NOT USE SUBSTITUTIONS!!!
