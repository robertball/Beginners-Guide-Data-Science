Title:
Basic Lemon Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (18.25 oz.) lemon cake mix
1 pkg. (3.9 oz.) lemon instant pudding mix
4 large eggs
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup warm water
1/2 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Place rack in middle of the oven.
Lightly spray a 12 inch bundt pan with Pam.
Place cake mix, pudding mix, eggs, sour cream, water and oil into a large mixing bowl. Blend with an electric mixer on low speed for 1 minute. Scrape down sides of bowl and continue to mix at medium speed for 2 minutes. The batter should be thick and well combined. Pour batter into pan and bake for 45 to 50 minutes or until cake bounces back when lightly touched.

Submitter: 
STILLSWEET43
Description: 
This lemon cake grows moister by the day. It's so rich you need a little frosting or glaze, just have a patience to let it rest and cool before you slice it.
