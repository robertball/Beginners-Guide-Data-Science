Title:
Thick and Creamy Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 (12oz) can evaporated milk
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 stick butter
1 bag egg noodles

Directions:
1.  Place chicken in Dutch Oven pot half full of water.  Boil chicken on high heat until done. Remove and chop into small pieces.
2.  Cook egg noodles in the same water chicken was cooked in.  Also add in chopped chicken, soup, butter and milk.
3.  Turn heat down to low and let cook for 20 minutes covered.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
April Butler
Description: 
This recipe was passed on to me from a good friend.  Its delicious!!!!!  Great to eat on a cold day.
