Title:
ravioli skillet lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c tomato based pasta sauce
1/3 c water
1 9oz package refrigerated cheese or meat filled ravioli
1 egg lightly beaten
1 15oz carton ricotta cheese
1/4 c grated Romano or parm cheese
1 10oz package frozen chopped spinach thawed and well drained
grated romano or parm

Directions:
1 preheat broiler.in 10in broilproof skillet combine pasta sauce and water.bring to boiling.stir in ravioli.cover cook over med heat about 5 minor until rav nearly tender,stir once to prevent sticking
2 meanwhile in med bowl combine egg,ricotta and 1/4 c romano.dot ravioli mixture with small amounts of spinach.top spinach w spoonfuls of ricotta mix
3 cover and cook low for about 10 min or just until ravioli are tender and ricotti is set
4 broil 4-5 in from heat for 4-5 min or until top is lightly browned.sprinkle with additional cheese
