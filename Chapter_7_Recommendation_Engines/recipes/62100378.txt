Title:
Split Pea with Ham and Veggies Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Olive Oil
1 medium onion, diced
2 medium baking potatoes, peeled and chopped
3 carrots, peeled and chopped
1 12 oz. package fresh sliced mushrooms
1 lb. pre-cooked diced ham
1 lb. dried split peas
2 cans chicken broth
Water
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
In a large pot, saute onion in olive oil
Remove from heat and add in potatos, carrots, mushrooms, ham and split peas
Pour chicken broth over the veggies and ham
Add enough water to just cover the mixture
Simmer, uncovered for one hour, stirring occassionally
Uncover and simmer for another two hours, allowing peas to soften and blend together as the base of the soup
Should soup become too thick and peas not soften enough, add up to one cup of water and simmer, uncovered, for one more hour
Add salt and pepper to taste

Submitter:

Description: 
An aromatic blend of veggies and ham for a cold weather treat!
