Title:
Banana Saskatoon/Blueberry Muffins (No sugar added) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large ripe bananas
1/3 cup date paste
1 large egg
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/3 cup olive oil
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup quick cooking oats
1 1/2 cup saskatoons/blueberries, fresh or frozen

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375Â°F and line a 12-count muffin pan with paper liners.
Mash bananas in a large mixing bowl.
Add maple syrup, egg, and vanilla extract and stir with a spatula until combined.
Add olive oil.
Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, salt, and oats. Gently stir into wet ingredients. Don't overmix!
Fold in saskatoons.
Divide batter between 12 muffin cups.
Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
