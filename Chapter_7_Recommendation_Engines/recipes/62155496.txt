Title:
Marinated Carrot Julienne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. julienned carrots
2 cups water mixed with 1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup white vinegar, divided in half
1 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon granulated garlic
1 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp rosemary, crushed lightly in mortar & pestle
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp freshly grated black pepper
1/2 tsp mustard seeds, lightly crushed
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Boil 2 cups salted water, plunge carrots into boiling water, and cook about 4 minutes, until crisp tender.  Strain.  Transfer to large bowl.  Toss with 1/4 cup vinegar while carrots are hot.
Combine remaining ingredients and whisk well.  Pour over cooked carrots, and mix well.
Store in strong zip lock bag with marinade.  Toss bag over each time you open the fridge.  Refridgerate several hours or overnight.
Serve alone or over salads, along side cheese and crackers etc.  You can substitute 1 can of green beans for the carrots, or mix them.  Make sure to heat beans completely before tossing with initial vinegar rinse.

Submitter:

Description: 
A refreshing savory summer salad - serve well chilled.  Ideal for picnics or with cocktails too!
