Title:
Cranberry Apple Cinnamon Heaven Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh or frozen cranberrys
1 cup fresh, frozen, or dried apple
1/2 cup of walnuts or pecans
6 tbls of butter
1/2 cup of sugar
1 tbls of vanilla
1 tsp of cinnamon

Directions:
In saucepan warm butter with fresh or frozen cranberrys and apples.
Saute until the fruit is tender approximately 3-5 minutes.
Add walnuts, sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, then saute for another 3-5 minutes.
Once this is reduced and most of liquid is gone, you are ready to enjoy.

Description: 
This is a collection of fresh or frozen cranberry's with apples, sugar, walnuts, vanilla, and butter.
