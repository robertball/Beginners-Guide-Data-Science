Title:
Creamy Tomato Basil Tortellini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
olive oil
2 garlic cloves, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
2 (15 oz.) cans diced fire-roasted tomatoes
2 (10 3/4 oz.) cans Campbell’s tomato soup
2 soup cans milk (fresh or evaporated)
10 fresh basil leaves, chopped, or 1 T. dried basil
1/2 tsp. Italian seasoning
1 (14 1/2 oz.) can chicken broth
3 Tbsp. tomato paste
1/2 cup light cream or 1/2 cup half-and-half
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 (9 oz.) package cheese tortellini

Directions:
Coat soup pan with olive oil. Add onion and garlic and sauté till soft.
Add canned diced tomatoes, basil, Italian seasoning. tomato soup, and two cans milk. Stir till blended.
Add chicken broth and stir in tomato paste till blended.
Simmer for 20 minutes
Add light cream and Parmesan cheese.
Add tortellini and cook till tender (at least 15 minutes).

Christine Denstaedt

Yummy creamy tomato soup would be great with sausage
