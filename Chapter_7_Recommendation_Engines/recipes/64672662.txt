Title:
No Crust Keto Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz can pumpkin puree
1 c organic heavy cream
1/3 c swerve granulated
1/3 c swerve powdered
3 large organic eggs
2 tsp pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
preheat oven to 350
grease 10 inch pie pan with butter
combine all ingredients with handmixer and blend until smooth
pour mixture into greased pan
bake 50-60 min or until center is slightly higher than edges
