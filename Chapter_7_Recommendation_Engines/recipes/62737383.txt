Title:
Forever the Best Golden Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. dry yeast
1/4 c. warm water
3/4 c. sour cream, NOT light or fat free
1 egg, slightly beaten
2 tbsp. butter, NOT margarine, melted
2 c. all-purpose flour, sifted
1/3 c. sugar
1 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. baking soda
butter, melted
Caramel Frosting:
1/4 c. butter
1/4 c. brown sugar
2 tbsp. evaporated milk
1 c. powdered sugar, sifted
Melt butter, add brown sugar and bring to boil stirring constantly. Reduce heat and simmer 2 mins. Add milk, return to boil but when reaches boil take off heat and cool to lukewarm. Add sugar and stir until smooth. Drizzle on cake ring.

Directions:
For cake recipe combine yeast and warm water: stir until yeast is dissolved. Add sour cream, egg and butter blending thoroughly. Sift flour, sugar, salt and baking soda. Add 1 c. of the sifted ingredients to yeast and beat with electric mixer at med. speed for 2-5 mins until mixed. Stir in remaining flour. On a lightly floured board, knead until dough is smooth, about 5 mins. Put in a buttered bowl to rise until doubled. Shape into about a 25 inch long roll and put in a 7 cup or so ring mold that has been coated with the melted butter. Bake 20 mins. or until done; remove to cool. Frost with Caramel Frosting.

Submitter: 
Deborah Norris
Description: 
A simple tasting old fashioned coffee cake that melts in your mouth.
