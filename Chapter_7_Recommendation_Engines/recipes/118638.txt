Title:
Patriot Chipotle Ranch Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/83347.jpg
Ingredients:
1 packet Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Dip Mix
2 1/2 cups sour cream
3 tablespoons fresh lime juice
2 tablespoons pureed chipotle peppers
1 teaspoon ground cumin
2 1/2 tablespoons fresh chopped cilantro

Directions:
In medium bowl, combine all ingredients and blend until smooth.  Refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
This spicy Ranch dip is sure to please your guests.
