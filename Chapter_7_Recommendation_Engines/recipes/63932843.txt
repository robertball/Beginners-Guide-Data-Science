Title:
Raw Coconut Chocolate cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2Cup pure virgin coconut oil
1/2Cup honey
1/2Cup cocoa (or raw cacao)
2 tsp vanilla
1 & 1/2 Cups unsweetened coconut
Optional - zest 2 oranges for chocolate orange flavour or 1 tsp of peppermint extract for chocolate mint flavour.  other options are dried fruit & nuts..this is a very versatile recipe

Directions:
Place first four ingredients in sauce pan on low to melt coconut oil and mix ingredients well.
Remove from heat and add coconut.
If mix is a bit runny, leave sitting for a few mins and it will stiffen up.
Spoon cookies onto wax paper lined pan and place in freezer for 30 mins to set.
Remove and place in airtight container.  Can be kept in fridge or freezer.

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
This is the easiest raw, gluten free, dairy free, sugar free recipe ever!
