Title:
Balsamic Dijon Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
2 T Dijon mustard
2 T mayo
pepper to taste
1 tsp. minced garlic

Directions:
In a small mixing bowl, combine ingredints. Mix well.  Cover, and chill before serving.

Submitter: 
Dawn Norby
Description: 
A delicious balsamic vinegar and honey Dijon mustard sauce. Use for dipping vegetables in or spreading on crackers or as a marinade.Modified from: Balsamic Dijon Dipping Sauce
