Title:
Chop! Chop! Its Salad for Dinner! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head romaine lettuce, chopped into bite size pieces
1 large tomato, seeded and chopped
1 medium cucumber, chopped
1 avocado, chopped
1/2 cup canned black beans, drained and rinsed
1/2 cup whole kernel corn
10 tortilla chips, crushed into bite size pieces
parmesan peppercorn salad dressing, enough to coat

Directions:
Combine lettuce, cucumber, tomato, avocado, black beans, and corn in a large salad bowl.  Add enough salad dressing to coat and toss salad well.  Crush tortilla chips over the salad and toss once more.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Aimee Schwartz Coughlin
Description: 
This is a great dinner-sized salad for when you are in a hurry and looking for something light, but satisfying.  Grilled chicken or shrimp can also be added, but it is hearty on its own.  Serve with garlic bread and enjoy!
