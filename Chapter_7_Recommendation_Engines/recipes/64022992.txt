Title:
Chicken Divan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20oz Frozen Chopped Broccoli
2 cups cooked chicken breasts - cubed
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 cup milk
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp lemon juice
1/2 tsp curry powder
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1 1/2 tablespoons melted butter
2 cups rice (uncooked)
4 cups water

Directions:
Cook rice. Place in bottom of 9x13 pan.
Arrange cooked broccoli and chicken on top of rice.
Combine next 5 ingredients and pour over chicken.
Sprinkle cheese on top.
Mix breadcrumbs and melted butter; sprinkle on top.
Bake in 350 degree oven for 25-30 minutes, uncovered.
