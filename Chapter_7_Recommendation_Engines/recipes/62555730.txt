Title:
Jack Daniels Smokies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Ketchup
3/4 cups Bourbon / Wiskey
3/4 cups Brown Sugar
1/2 cup water
1 TBS chopped onion

Directions:
Mix together, pour over little smokies (4 packages or 3 lbs), cook in crock pot for several hours until hot.
