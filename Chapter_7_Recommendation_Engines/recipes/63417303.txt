Title:
Sweet, Sticky and Spicy Chicken with vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon brown sugar
2 tablespoons honey
1/2 cup soy sauce
2 teaspoons chopped fresh ginger root
3 teaspoons chopped garlic
2 tablespoons siracha
salt and pepper to taste
2 skinless, boneless chicken breast - cut into 1/2 inch strips, dredged in flour
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/2 onion, cut into strips
1 red pepper cut into strips
1/2 cup fresh mushrooms, cut

Directions:
Mix together brown sugar, honey, soy sauce, ginger, garlic and siracha in a small bowl.
Lightly salt and pepper and flour the chicken strips.
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken strips and brown on both sides, about 4 minute per side. Remove from pan.  cook veggies until crisp-tender. Add back chicken Pour sauce over all. Simmer uncovered until the sauce thickens, 8 to 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Muirae Demerse Kenney
Description: 
This delicious dish uses skinless boneless chicken breasts cooked in a gingery, spicy, soy  sauce. It's great as an appetizer or as a main dish served with rice and a veggie.Modified from: Sweet, Sticky and Spicy Chicken
