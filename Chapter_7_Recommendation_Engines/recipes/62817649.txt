Title:
Spiced Drumsticks With An Out Of This World Bbq Sauce!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 drumsticks
Seasoning:
1/4 cup paprika
2 tsp. cayenne
2 Tbs salt
2 tsp. white pepper
2 Tbs black pepper
2 Tbs garlic powder
2 Tbs chili powder
2 Tbs oregano
1/2 cup olive oil
Out of this World BBQ Sauce:
2 Tbs olive oil
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 Tbs chopped garlic
1 tsp. cumin
1 Tbs. chile powder
1/3 cup malt vinegar
1 cup ketchup
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 soy sauce
2 Tbs. worcestershire sauce
2 Tbs Chipotle in adobo (sauce only)
1/4 cup finely chopped cilantro

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix all of the spices and whisk in olive oil. Coat drumsicks in mix and add to a baking sheet. Bake for 45 minutes.
Meanwhile Preheat grill.
To make BBQ sauce: Heat oil over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, cumin and chile powder and saute until the onions are soft. Add malt vinegar and deglaze scraping the bottom of the saucepan. Add the rest of the ingredients and simmer until slightly thickened. Transfer to blender and blend until smooth. Put in a bowl and stir in chopped cilantro. Refrigerate until needed.
When the drumsticks are done in the oven, place them on the hot, oiled grill for 10 to 15 minutes until they have a nice crispy skin. Remove and enjoy! Either drizzle BBQ sauce, dip or even dunk the whole thing in! Its yummy no matter what you do!
