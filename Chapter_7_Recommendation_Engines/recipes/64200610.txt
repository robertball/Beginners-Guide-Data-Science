Title:
Hearty Cassoulet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 onion, finely chopped
4 boneless skinless chicken thighs, chopped
1/4 lb smoked turkey sausage, finely chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp black pepper
4 tbsp tomato paste
2 tbsp water
3 15 oz cans Great Northern beans, rinsed and drained
1/2 cup plain bread crumbs
2 tbsp fresh minced parsley

Directions:
Heat oil in a medium skillet over medium heat.  Add onion, saute for 5 minutes or until tender.  Add chicken, sausage, garlic, thyme, and pepper.  Cook and stir 5 minutes or until meats are browned.
Remove skillet from heat.  Stir in tomato paste and water until blended.  Transfer to a slow cooker and stir in beans.  Cook on low 4 to 4 1/2 hours.
Combine bread crumbs and parsley in a small bowl.  Sprinkle on cassoulet and heat through.
