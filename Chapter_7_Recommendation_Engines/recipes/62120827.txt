Title:
Chicken Vegetable Soup with Barley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups chicken
2/3 cup of barley
8 cups of chicken stock
2 stalks of celery, chopped
1 large carrot, chopped
1 can of diced tomatoes, or one large tomato chopped
3 cloves of garlic, minced
1 TBSP soy sauce
1 tsp basil
1/8 tsp oregano
1/8 tsp thyme
Dash of cayenne pepper
2 TBSP fresh parsley, minced

Directions:
Put all of the ingredients except the parsley into an 8 qt. stock pot.
Bring to a boil, cover and reduce heat.
Simmer the soup until carrots are tender, stirring occasionally.
Adjust seasonings to taste, and add the parsley and serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
Hearty Soup! Great use of leftover chicken.
