Title:
Kamikaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jigger (1.5 oz) vodka
1 pony (1 oz) cointreau (or triple sec, if you're cheap)
1/2 pony (1/2 oz) Rose's lime juice

Directions:
Chill a cocktail glass by filling with ice or stashing in the freezer.
Combine a cup of ice with the vodka, cointreau, and Rose's lime in a shaker.  Shake vigorously.
Dump the ice out of your cocktail glass (or remove from freezer).  Strain the shaken mixture into the glass.
Enjoy!
