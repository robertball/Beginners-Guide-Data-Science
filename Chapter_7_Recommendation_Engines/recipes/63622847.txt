Title:
Sweet Potato Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1007714.jpg
Ingredients:
1/1/2 cups butter milk or plain yogurt
4 large eggs
1/2 cup sugar
2 1/2 cups flour
3/4 TBLS baking powder
3/4 cup cooked sweet potato puree (canned or fresh baked)
3 TBLS melted butter
2 peaches
2 plums
1 cup grapes
1/4 cup pecans

Directions:
Blend the first 7 ingredients well and let rest for 20 minutes.
Make waffles in a waffle maker.
Slice the fresh fruits such as peaches, plums and grapes
Top with fruits and toasted pecans.
Add maple syrup to taste.
