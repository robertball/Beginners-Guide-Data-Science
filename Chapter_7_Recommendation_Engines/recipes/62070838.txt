Title:
Simple and Delicious Dark Chocolate Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups confectionsers' sugar
2 oz. unsweetened chocolate
10 tbsp. butter

Directions:
Put the chocolate on low in a medium saucepan and stir until melted.
In a bowl combine the chocolate, 2 tbsp. melted butter, and 1 cup confectioners' sugar. Stir until smooth, then add another 2 tbsp. butter and powdered sugar. Repeat until all ingreedients are used.
