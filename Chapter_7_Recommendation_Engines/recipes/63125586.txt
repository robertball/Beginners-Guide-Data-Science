Title:
Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/796563.jpg
Ingredients:
7 each Chicken Breasts
1 cup Water
2 chicken bouillon cubes
1 cup Oil
1 teaspoon Salt
All-Purpose Flour

Directions:
Start chicken breasts boiling in a large pot of water with 2 chicken bouillon cubes, no salt necessary at this point.
For the dumplings mix the water, oil, salt, and flour together. Keep adding in the flour until the dough is stiff. Roll out and drop into the water with the cooking chicken breasts. Cook until dumplings are tender.
