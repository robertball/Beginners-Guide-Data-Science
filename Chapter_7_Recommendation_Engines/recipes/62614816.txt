Title:
Turtle Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 unbaked 9 inch deep dish pie crust
12 oz jar caramel ice cream topping
3 eggs
1/3 c. flour
1/2 c. sugar
1/4 c. melted butter (I use unsalted)
1 tsp. vanilla
1/4 tsp. salt (omit salt if you use salted butter)
1 c. semi sweet chocolate chips
1 c. chopped pecans

Directions:
Stir eggs, flour, sugar, butter, vanilla and salt until well blended. Add carmel sauce and blend. Stir in chocolate chips and pecans. Pour into unbaked pie shell. Bake at 375 for 40-50 mins. Cool.
