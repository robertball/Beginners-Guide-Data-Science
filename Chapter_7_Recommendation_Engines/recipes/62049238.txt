Title:
GLORIFIED CROCK POT PORK CHOPS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless pork chops
1 cup dry stove top dressing
1 can 98% fat free Cream of Celery or Mushroom Soup (I like mushroom)
1/2 can water

Directions:
Put pork chops on bottom of crockpot
Next spread evenly DRY Stuffing mix on each shop
Pour soup evenly over chops - pour water
Cook on low all day OR on high 4-5 hours depending on thickness of chops.

Submitter: 
Spokes
Description: 
A simple, very tasty weight watchers recipe for an evening meal which is quick to prepare.   Points per serving appx. 7.  Serve with vegetables and you'll be satisfied and not felt denied of luscious tasting food.
