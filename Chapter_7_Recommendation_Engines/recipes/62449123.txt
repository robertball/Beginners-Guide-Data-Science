Title:
Fruit and Cheese Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 strawberries, halved
1 cantelope, cleaned and cubed
2 pkgs. of cheese shapes, cubed cheeses or string cheese snacks chopped into 1 inch sections
5 kiwi fruits, peeled and cubed
Red grapes
1 cabbage (optional if gift giving)
36 disposable skewers

Directions:
Pierce each skewer with fruit and cheese, each in the same order. Serve or store covered in refrigerator until read to serve.
If gift giving or using for a shower, slice a small section off of a head of cabbage to create a flat surface.  Place the flat surface down and press skewers into the cabbage to form a bouquet.

Submitter: 
Miss Amy
Description: 
Great for a shower, a gift or just to leave in the fridge for kids to grab a healthy snack on the go.  Fruit and cheese choices can be modified according to your preferences and can also be shaped for special occasions.
