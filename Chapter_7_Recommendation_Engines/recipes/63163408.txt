Title:
Slow Cooker Cincinnati Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups low-sodium beef broth
1 8 ounce can tomato sauce
1/4 cup tomato paste
2 tablespoons cider vinegar
2 tablespoons chili powder
2 teaspoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
3 garlic cloves, chopped
1 large yellow onion, diced
2 pounds ground beef
1 pound spaghetti, cooked
Finely shredded cheddar cheese and diced white onions (optional)

Directions:
Whisk together broth, tomato sauce, tomato paste, vinegar, chili powder, cocoa powder, cumin, cinnamon, cloves, allspice, salt and pepper in the base of a slow cooker. Add garlic and yellow onion, and crumble in beef; mix. Cover and cook on HIGH for 6 hours or LOW for 8 hours.
2. Ladle chili over cooked spaghetti. Garnish with finely shredded cheddar cheese and diced white onions, if desired.
