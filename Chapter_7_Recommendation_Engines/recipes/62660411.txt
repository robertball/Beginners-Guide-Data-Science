Title:
Senegalese Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 stick butter
1 medium onion diced
3/4 cup celery chopped
3 cloves garlic chopped
3 tbs curry powder
1 tsp Maggi
1 tsp MSG (optional)
1 dash Tabasco
1/4 cup Wondra flour
2 tbs chicken base
5 cups whole milk
2 cups peeled chopped tomatoes
1 cup dry white wine
1 bay leaf
4 cups cooked chicken shredded

Directions:
In Dutch oven pan, stove top, saute butter, onion, celery, garlic and curry powder. Remove from heat when veggies are translucent.
Off heat add: Maggi,MSG, Tabasco, paprika, Wondra, chicken base, and milk.
Stir all ingredients together and bring to a boil on medium heat.
Remove from heat and add tomatoes, white wine and bay leaf. Simmer 20 minutes then add chicken. Let stand for a few minutes then ladle it over cooked rice.

Submitter: 
RedHotTarheel
Description: 
This dish can be a soup or a stew. I like it as a stew as it makes a great one dish meal. The curry flavor is not overpowering and is subtle. Served over rice it a meal fit for a king. An easy to prepare chicken dish for any cook. Guaranteed to get raves from guests.
