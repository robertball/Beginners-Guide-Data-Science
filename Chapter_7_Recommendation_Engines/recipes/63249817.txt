Title:
famous pound cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1/4 tsp baking powder
2 cups flour
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp salt
1 cup milk
1 stick butter

Directions:
preheat oven to 175 degrees (350 F)
mix the butter in an electric mixer until creamy.
add the eggs one at a time until it is incorporated.
ad the flour and milk to the egg mix.
spread flour around a bunt cake pan to prevent batter from sticking.
pour the batter in and bake for 1 hour.
