Title:
Mexican Style Pasta Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz bow tie pasta
1/2 C chopped onion
1/2 chopped red sweet pepper
3 T butter
1/3 C flour
1 t dried cilantro, crushed
1/2 t ground cumin
3 C milk
6 oz colby cheese,cubed
1 1/2 C shredded Monterey Jack cheese (6 oz)
2/3 C halved pitted green and/or ripe olives
chili powder

Directions:
1. Preheat oven 400. Butter 3 qt. casserole dish, set aside. In 4qt.Dutch oven cook pasta according to package directions. Drain, return to pan. In large saucepan cook onion and sweet pepper in butter over med. heat about 5 min. or until tender. Stir in flour, salt, cilantro and cumin.. Add milk al at once. Cook and stir until mixture is thickened and bubbly. Reduce heat to low. Stir in colby cheese and 1 cup of Monterey Jack cheese; stir until cheese is melted. Pour over drained pasta; stir to combine.
2. Layer 1/2 pasta, all the salsa, and then remaining pasta mixture in dish. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup Mont. Jack cheese and olives. Sprinkle lightly with chili powder. Bake uncovered, 20 min or until bubbly around edges and heated through. Let stand 5 min. before serving
