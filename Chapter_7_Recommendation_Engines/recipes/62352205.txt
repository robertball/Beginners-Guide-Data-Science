Title:
Tropical Triple Treat Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup margarine, softened
1 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1 cup mashed banana
1 8 oz can crushed pineapple
1 cup coconut

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour 12 muffin cups or use paper liners.
Mix together flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
In a large bowl, cream together margarine and sugar until smooth. Beat in eggs, one at a time.
Add bananas and pineapple and mix well.
Add dry ingredients, stirring just until combined.
Fold in coconut.
Pour into prepared muffin tin and bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 18-22 minutes (or until toothpick comes out clean).
