Title:
Tomato & Pesto Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Ready Crust Pizza
1 3 oz pesto sauce
1 4 oz mozzarella cheese
2 Tomatoes (sliced in circles)
1 Ricotta Cheese
Dried Oregano to garnish
Basil leaves to garnish

Directions:
1.  Spread pesto sauce on pizza crust
2.  Sprinkle mozzarella over pesto sauce
3.  Place tomato slices on cheese evenly and top
with ricotta cheese.
4.  Bake according to pizza crust instructions
(usually about 20- 30 minutes).
5.  Garnish with oregano & basil
