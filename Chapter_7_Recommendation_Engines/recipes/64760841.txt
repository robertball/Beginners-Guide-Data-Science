Title:
Big Ray's Super Creamy and Crunchy Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7113232.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tsp salt
1 tsp dry mustard powder
1 tsp granulated garlic
3/4 tsp sweet paprika
1 tsp granulated onion
1Tbsp celery seed
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
3 cups elbow macaroni
1 1/2 cups mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1 (5 ounce) can evaporated milk
2 Tbsp whole grain mustard
1/4 tsp balsamic vinegar
1 medium onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup bread & butter pickles, coarsely chopped
3 Tbsp finely chopped green bell pepper (2 mini peppers)
3 Tbsp finely chopped red bell pepper (2 mini peppers)
2 Tbsp finely chopped yellow bell pepper (2 mini peppers)
3 stalks celery, finely chopped
3 medium carrots, shredded

Directions:
Mix salt, dry mustard, granulated garlic, paprika, granulated onion, black pepper and celery seed  in a small bowl.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes. Drain and rinse macaroni under cold water until cooled.
Whisk mayonnaise, sour cream,  evaporated milk, whole grain mustard and balsamic vinegar in a large salad bowl until dressing is smooth and creamy; stir in seasoning mix until well blended. Mix macaroni, onion, pickles, green, red, and yellow bell pepper, celery, and carrots into the dressing until thoroughly coated. Rest in refrigerator until serving time.

The eye catching combination of colours and the mixture of flavours and textures in this salad will be the hit of the next BBQ . It's really best when allowed to  leave it overnight in the fridge, the consistency will be perfect the next day.
