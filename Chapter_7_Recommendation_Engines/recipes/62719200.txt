Title:
BLT Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 16oz packages wacky macaroni (or other noodles)
1 lbs bacon
8 roma tomatoes, cored and diced
1/4 head lettuce, sliced into small peices
1 green onion, diced
1 - 16oz package shredded cheddar cheese
1 - 16oz bottle ranch dressing
1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:
Cook bacon until done, set a side on towel to drain grease.  Once cool chop into small peices.
Follow directions on pasta to cook.  Drain and rinse with cool water.
Once pasta is cool add bacon, tomatoes, lettuce, green onion.
In bowl mix 1 cup ranch with sour cream.  Add to pasta salad, mix and add remaining bottle of ranch to pasta.  Mix 3/4 of the bag of cheese into pasta.  Top salad with remaining cheese.
Serve or store in fridge until ready to eat.
