Title:
Spicy Tuna Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of Chunk Light tuna in water (drained)
1 cup of chopped avocado
2 tbsp of mexican crema (sour cream)
1/4 cup of salsa picante (see my recipe for salsa picante)
4 flour tortillas
Chopped onions and cilantro
1/2 lime (optional)

Directions:
In a pan or a griddle warm the tortillas until evenly browned around the edges on both sides
In a bowl combine the tuna, salsa, avocado, and crema and mix.
Place in the fridge while you finish warming the tortilas.
Fill the tortillas with the tuna mix and sprinkle with onions and cilantro.
Squeeze a little lime into them.

Submitter: 
Chef Kiki
Description: 
A quick lunch or dinner option with a kick. You can adjust the spicy factor depending on the sauce you use.
