Title:
Sweet and Spicy Crock Pot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. boneless, skinless chicken thighs
Salt and pepper
1/2 cup diced onion
2 cloves of garlic - minced
1 cup honey
1/4 cup ketchup
1/2 cup soy sauce
2 tbsp. olive oil
1/4 tsp. red pepper flakes
4 tsp. cornstarch
1/3 cup water
3 scallions - chopped
1/2 tsp. sesame seeds

Directions:
Place chicken in crock pot and season lightly with salt and pepper.
Combine onion, garlic, honey, soy sauce, ketchup, oil and pepper flakes.
Pour mixture over chicken.
Cook on low for 3-4 hours or on high for 2 hours.
When time is up, remove chicken from crock pot and shred.
Combine water and cornstarch and add to crock pot.
Cook covered on high heat for about 10 minutes or until slightly thickened.
Top chicken with sauce, scallions and sesame seeds.
Serve over rice.
