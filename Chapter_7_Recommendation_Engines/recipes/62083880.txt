Title:
Blitz Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
½ cup butter
½ cup sugar
1 t. vanilla
4 egg yolks  well beaten
1 cup cake flour
1 t. baking powder
1/8 t. salt
5 T. milk
Topping:
3/4 cup sugar
4 egg whites
½ t. cinnamon
1 T. sugar
Custard:
½ cup sugar  3 T. cornstarch
2 egg yolks, beaten
2 cups scalded milk
½ t. vanilla

Directions:
Cake:
Cream butter and sugar thoroughly.
Add vanilla and egg yolks and beat well.
Sift dry ingredients and add alternately with milk.
Pour into two 9 in. cake pans lined with waxed paper.
Topping:
Beat egg whites until stiff & fold in sugar.
Spread over cake batter.
Sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar.
Bake 350º for 20-30 minutes.
When cooled, spread custard filling between layers.
Custard:
Blend sugar and cornstarch in saucepan. Add egg yolks.
Pour in milk and cook stirring constantly until thickened.
Add vanilla and cool.
