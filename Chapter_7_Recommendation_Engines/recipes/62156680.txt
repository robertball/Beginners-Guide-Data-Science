Title:
Asparagus Panzanella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb medium asparagus
Kosher salt and fresh ground pepper to taste
1 seedless cucumber, skinned and cut into wedges
10 cherry tomatoes, cut in half
2 small tomateos, yellow or green, wedged
1 med. red onion, cut into wedges
3/4 cup kalamata olives, pitted
1/4 cup capers,drained
3/4 cup mozzerella balls
3 cups or 1/2 a loaf of dense, crusty artisian bread, cubed
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil for salad
drizzled olive oil for bread
3 cloves diced garlic for bread

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 F.
Place bread in a bowl and drizzle with olive oil just to coat add garlic and salt and pepper, toss.
Transfer to a cookie sheet and toast for about 7 minutes on each side. Let cool.
Cut off and discard tough ends of asparagus.
Bring a med pan of salted water to a boil.
Prepare an ice bath.
Cook asparagus until bright green and just tender, 2-3 minutes. Transfer to ice bath to stop the cooking, cool and help retain the green color. Drain and place on a towel.
Cut the asparagus in thirds.
In a large bowl, combine asparagus, cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, olives, capers, mozzarella, and bread, toss to combine.
In a small bowl whisk together red wine vinegar and olive oil. Saeson with salt and pepper.
Drizzle dressing over over vegetables just to coat. Let sit about 20 minutes. Serve.

Description: 
Asparagus and other vegetables are tossed together with Italian style bread and drizzled with red wine vinegar and olive oil.
