Title:
Loaded hashbrowns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 oz package Southern style hash browns
4 slices of bacon
1/2 cup Cheddar Cheese
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup green onions

Directions:
Cook hash browns according to package directions. Meanwhile, cook bacon in a medium nonstick skillet over medium heat for 6 minutes or until crisp drain and crumble. Top hash browns with cheese; cover and cook until cheese melts. Top hash browns with bacon, sour cream and green onions.
