Title:
Shredded Beef for Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (4 pound) frozen boneless chuck roast
1 cup white wine
2 (7.75 ounce) cans Mexican style hot tomato sauce
4 tablespoons crushed garlic
4 serrano chiles, diced
salt and ground black pepper to taste
1 bunch green onions, chopped
1 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Place the frozen roast in the slow cooker. Pour the wine and Mexican style hot tomato sauce over the top. Season with garlic, chiles, and salt and pepper to taste.
Cover, and cook on Low for 9 hours. When done, the roast should shred easily with a fork. Shred the roast into the juices, and mix in the chopped green onions and cilantro.
