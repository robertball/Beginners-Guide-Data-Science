Title:
Crab salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3lb. of imitation crab meat
1 cup chopped green onion
1 cup chopped celery
1/2 cub chopped green pepper
1/2 cup ranch dressing
1/2 cup heavy mayonnaise
1 teaspoon of sugar
1/2 teaspoon of pepper
1/2 teaspoon of salt
1 teaspoon of salad vinegar

Directions:
separate crab
put together all ingredients and mix well
best when set in the refrigerator over night

Submitter: 
cookingmommy
Description: 
this crab salad taste just like the crab salad from golden corral!
