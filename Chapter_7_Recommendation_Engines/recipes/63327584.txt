Title:
Martha Stewart's Basic Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 sticks salted butter, softened
1 pound confectioner's sugar, sifted
1/2 tsp vanilla extract, as desired
1/4 -1/2 tsp other extracts, as desired
gel food colorings, as desired

Directions:
Beat butter on medium high until pale and creamy, about 2 minutes.  Reduce speed to medium.  Add sugar, 1/2 cup at a time, beating after each addition, about 5 minutes.  (After every two additions, increase speed to high and beat 10 seconds). Add extracts and colorings and beat until smooth.
Use immediately or refrigerate up to three days (bring to room temp before using).
