Title:
Kung Pao Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast cut into chunks
6 tablespoons peanut oil
4 tablespoons rice wine
4 tablespoons soy sauce
4 tablespoons sesame oil, divided
4 tablespoons cornstarch, dissolved in 2 tablespoons water
2 teaspoons distilled white vinegar
4 teaspoons brown sugar
2 tablespoons sesame oil
1 bunch green onions, chopped
3 tablespoons chopped garlic
4 ounces chopped peanuts
1/2 sweet onion sliced thin
Spinach
Broccoli
Zucchini
Snow Peas
Whole red peppers (optional)
2 cups rice, rinsed 3+ times or until it runs clear
3 cups water

Directions:
To Make Marinade: Combine 2 tablespoon rice wine, 2 tablespoon soy sauce, 2 tablespoon oil and 2 tablespoon cornstarch/water mixture and mix together. Place chicken pieces in Ziplock and add marinade for about 30 minutes.
Make rice by bringing water and rice to boil for 2 minutes, reduce heat and cover for 9 minutes, turn heat off, let set for 10 minutes, fluff.
While rice is cooking, make sauce: In a small bowl combine 2 tablespoons wine, 2 tablespoons soy sauce, 2 tablespoons oil, 2 tablespoons cornstarch/water mixture, vinegar and brown sugar. Set aside.
Heat peanut oil in wok until very hot, add green onion, garlic, onions and peanuts for 2-3 minutes. Add chicken with marinade, cook until meat is white, remove from wok.
Heat peanut oil until very hot, add veggies stir fry for 3-4 minutes, add chicken back and add sauce until heated through and sauce thickens, serve over rice.
