Title:
Sweet and Salty Peanut Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of lightly crushed corn ships, divided
2 cups of salted peanuts, divided
1 cup light corn syrup
1 cup sugar
1 cup peanut butter

Directions:
Place half of the corn chips and peanuts in a 9 X 9 pan lined with parchment paper.
In a large saucepan, bring the corn syrup and sugar to a boil. Remove from heat and stir in peanut butter until smooth. Drizzle half of mixture over corn chips and peanuts in the pan.
Add remaining corn chips and peanuts to remaining syrup, stir until combined. Spoon over mixture in pan, press down lightly.
Cool completely before cutting.
