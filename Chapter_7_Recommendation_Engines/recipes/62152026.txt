Title:
Green Banapple Smoothie. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 of a Green Apple
Banana
12 oz Fat Free Skim Milk

Directions:
First, take your banana and cut it into 9 pieces.
Then, Take your apple put a apple chopper or knife and cut it into 3 pieces.
After that, take 12 oz of milk and pour it in a blender along with the fruit.
Put it on "high" or "chop" for about a minute.

Description: 
A great green apple and banana smoothie! Great for serving to kids and it's really healthy!
