Title:
Broccoli Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz broccoli
2 cups cooked chicken pieces (4 chicken breasts)
1 cup mayo
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 Tbsp. lemon juice
1/2 cup grated cheese
2 Tbsp. margarine/butter, melted
2 cups bread crumbs

Directions:
Cook broccoli until tender and place in bottom of 9 x 13 pan.
Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces and saut√©; add to broccoli.
Combine mayo, cream of chicken soup, lemon juice and grated cheese and pour over chicken and broccoli.
Mix melted margarine with bread crumbs and sprinkle over casserole.
Bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes.
