Title:
Italian Hoagies, Veggie-Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBSP olive oil
1 yellow onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 sweet green pepper, chopped
1 12 ounce package frozen soy crumbles (such as Morningstar Farms Meal Starters Grillers Recipe Crumbles)
1 jar prepared pasta sauce
1 TSP fennel seeds
1/4 TSP cayenne pepper, or to taste
6 soft hoagie rolls, split

Directions:
1) In a large skillet, saute onion and pepper over medium-high heat in the olive oil until tender. Add garlic and saute for 30 seconds more.
2) Add frozen crumbles, pasta sauce, fennel, and cayenne.
3) Bring to a boil, then turn heat to low and allow to simmer for 15 minutes.
4) Broil split hoagie rolls until golden brown.
5) Place rolls on plates, top with mixture, and enjoy.

Submitter: 
juliebfleming
Description: 
A delicious, hearty sub that will appeal to vegetarians and meat-eaters alike. Ground beef could be substituted for the soy crumbles for non-vegetarians.
