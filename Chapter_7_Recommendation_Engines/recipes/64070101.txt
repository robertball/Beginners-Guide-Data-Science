Title:
Big Ray's Mediterranean Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1132333.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/4 cup lemon juice
2 Tbsp. dry white wine
2 tsp. dried oregano leaves
1/2 tsp. black pepper
2 cloves garlic, minced
3 lb. chicken pieces (breasts, thighs and/or legs)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except chicken in large bowl. Add chicken; toss to coat. Refrigerate 4-24 hours to marinate.
Heat oven to 350°F. Remove chicken from marinade; discard marinade. Place chicken in single layer in shallow pan.
Bake 50 to 55 min. or until done (165ºF).

Description:
