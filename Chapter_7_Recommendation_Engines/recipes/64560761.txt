Title:
White Whole Wheat #2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Water 1-1/3 cup
Hard Red Wheat 2 cups
Hard white wheat 2 cups
Honey 4 tbsp
Salt 2 tsp
Vital wheat gluten 1 tbsp
Yeast (original) 2 ounces
Half & Half 4 tbsps

Directions:
Add to bread machine in the order listed. Make sure salt does not contact yeast and make use yeast does not contact water.
Set on basic wheat bread setting

Bryant Evans

Made with hard white whole wheat and hard red wheat
