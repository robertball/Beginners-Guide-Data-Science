Title:
Zucchini Stuffing Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. StoveTop Dressing Mix (I usually use the cornbread)
1/4 butter, melted
2 lbs. zucchini or squash, or a mix
1 med. onion
water
1 can cream of chicken soup
8 oz. sour cream (low-fat is fine)
1 cup grated cheddar cheese
salt and pepper

Directions:
1.Mix dry stuffing mix with 1/4 c. melted butter and set aside.
2.Wash and slice zucchini into 1/3 inch round (do not peel) and dice onion. Cook together with about 1/2 c. water until zucchini is crisp-tender. Drain.
3.Mix zuccini-onion mixture with soup and sour cream. Add salt and pepper to taste.
4.Layer 1/2 of stuffing mix into bottom of 9x13" pan. Top with zucchini mixture, then sprinkle 1/2 c. of cheddar cheese, the rest of the stuffing mix, then the remainder of cheese. Cover with foil and bake at 350 for 30 minutes, remove foil and bake and additional 15 minutes.
