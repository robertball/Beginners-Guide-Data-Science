Title:
Hot Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1c mayonnaise
1c parmesan
1t garlic powder
1-1/2t lemon juice

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and heat in oven for 30 minutes at 350.
