Title:
Oven Roasted Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 red sweet potatoes (or yams), peeled and cut into 1 inch cubes
2 Tbl. coconut or light olive oil
1 Tbl. freshly squeezed lemon juice
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. freshly ground nutmeg
zest from one lemon
sea salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Place sweet potatoes into a 9x13 pan.
Place all other ingredients into a bowl and mix together.  Pour over sweet potatoes and make sure they are well coated. *Sprinkle sea salt to taste over the potatoes.
Place into preheated oven and cook uncovered for 30 minutes.
Stir sweet potatoes and return to oven for 15 more minutes.
Remove from oven and let it rest for 5 minutes.
Serve.

Submitter: 
halfnotes
Description: 
My children love sweet potatoes and I decided to roast them with toppings we usually add after steaming them.  The result was fabulous and we all decided it was one to keep around!
