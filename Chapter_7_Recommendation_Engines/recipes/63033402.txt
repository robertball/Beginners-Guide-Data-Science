Title:
Mexican Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoons light olive oil
2 cup uncooked long-grain rice
2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/2 red bell pepper
1 seeded jalapeno
1 can  tomato sauce
3 1/2 cups chicken broth (or water with Better than Bouillion)

Directions:
Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat and add diced onion, bell pepper and jalapeno. Stir constantly until onions are translucent. Add rice, continue to stir frequently until until puffed and golden.  While rice is cooking, sprinkle with salt garlic powder and cumin.
Stir in tomato sauce and chicken broth; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.  Fluff with a fork.
