Title:
Seven Layer Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28-oz. can refried beans
1 small can chopped green chilies
1 packet taco seasoning
1 cup guacamole
16 oz. sour cream
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 6-oz. can sliced black olives, drained
salsa
tortilla chips

Directions:
Mix together refried beans, green chilies, and taco seasoning.  Spread mixture on large platter (or dinner plate if cutting recipe in half).
Spread guacamole on bean mixture, followed by sour cream (careful not to mix the two).  Add cheese, black olives, and salsa.
Cover and refrigerate 30 minutes.  Serve with tortilla chips.
