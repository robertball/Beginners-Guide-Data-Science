Title:
So Good You'll  Wanna Slap the Cook Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups white sugar
3 eggs
1 cup butter, melted
2 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 package of chocolate pudding
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup milk chocolate chips
1 cup of whip cream
3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
1/4 cup of brown sugar
1 egg
1/2 cup of evaporated milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Grease pan.
Beat 2 cups of sugar & 3 eggs together until foamy.
Stir in oil & vanilla.
Combine flour, cocoa, baking powder & salt; stir egg into mixture.
Stir in chocolate chips.
Reserve one cup of batter, spread rest into bottom of pan.
In a medium bowl, beat whip cream, peanut butter, 3/4 cup of brown sugar, 1 egg & 1/2 cup of evaporated milk until smooth & creamy.
Spread peanut batter over layer of chocolate batter.
Mix reserved chocolate batter, into peanut butter layer. Cut through batter w/knife to swirl.
Bake 35 to 40 minutes.
Cool completely before cutting.

Description: 
Delicious does not begin to describe the taste of these brownies. So Good You Will Want To Slap the Cook Brownies, is a delicious peanut butter and chocolate brownie recipe. These brownies make the perfect homemade gift for the sweets lover in your life. Garnish with whip cream and strawberries and your dinner party will definitely be a hit.
