Title:
Black Beans with Tomatoes and Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup(s) beans, black
2 teaspoon oil, olive
1/4 cup(s) onion(s)
1 medium tomato(es)

Directions:
Drain and rinse black beans, thoroughly.
Heat the olive oil in a small skillet over medium heat. Add the onions and saute until soft. Add the tomatoes and black beans and cook until heated through. Serve.
