Title:
Caribbean Chicken Cescent Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb chicken breast, skinned, boned and cut into 1/2 pieces
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
2-3 tsp butter
3 oz. cream cheese
2 tbs mango chutney, chopped
1/4 cup red bell pepper
1/4 cup sliced green onion
1 8 oz can crescent rolls
1 egg white, slightly beaten
1/2 tsp sesame seeds

Directions:
Heat oven to 375. Sprinkle chicken with salt, garlic powder and red pepper. Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken, stir fry 2 minutes. Add cream cheese and chutney, cook and stir until cheese is melted. Remove from heat, stir in bell peppers and onions. Separate dough into 4 rectangles, firmly press perforations to seal. Spoon 1/4 of chicken mixture on half of each rectangle. Fold dough over and press edges together. Seal edges with a fork, place on cookie sheet. Brush tops with egg whites, sprinkle with sesame seeds. Bake at 375 for 15-20 minutes.
