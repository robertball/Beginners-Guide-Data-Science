Title:
Kale & Apple Salad (100 Days of Real Food) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2Tbsp Olive Oil
2tsp Apple Cider Vinegar
1tsp Fresh Squeezed Lemon Jc
1/8tsp Salt
6Cups torn kale leaves (ribs removed)
1/2 an Apple, cut into thin pcs
1/3Cup Walnuts, chopped and lightly toasted
3-4Tbsp Crumbled Blue Cheese

Directions:
1. In a small jar, combine the olive oil, vinegar, lemon juice and salt.  Shake well.
2. Place the kale leaves into a large salad bowl and massage dressing mixture into leaves.
3. Gently toss with apple, walnuts & blue cheese
