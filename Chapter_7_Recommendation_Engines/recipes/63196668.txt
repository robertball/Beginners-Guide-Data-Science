Title:
MY Blue Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Can frozen Limeaide
1/2 Can Tequila
1/2 Can Blue Curacao

Directions:
Fill blender half full with ice; then throw in frozen can of limeaide; add half a can of tequila; half a can of  blue curacao.  Blend throughly.  Enjoy!!

Submitter: 
Debbie Cash
Description: 
Just threw this stuff together last night for a new Maragarita recipe. Tested it out on my husband; and SIL who was visiting from out of town...  it got WONDERFUL reviews from both.
