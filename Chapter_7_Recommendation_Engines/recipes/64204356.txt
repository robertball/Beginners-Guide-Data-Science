Title:
Pumpkin Poke Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Spice Cake mix
(Eggs, water and oil as recommended on box)
1 C Pumpkin puree
1 C Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 C Caramel Sauce (or ice cream topping)
1 box Instant Pumpkin Pudding mix
1/2 C Powdered Sugar
1 C Milk
1 8oz Whipped Topping, thawed
1 C Crushed Gingersnap cookies (opt)

Directions:
Mix cake as directed, adding pumpkin puree. Grease or spray 13x9 baking dish. Bake as directed at 350 until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
Remove cake from oven and poke holes throughout cake, using the handle end of a wooden spoon. Mix sweetened condensed milk and caramel sauce together. Pour over cake, making sure it goes into the holes. Place cake in fridge to cool, approx. 1 hour.
In medium bowl, stir together pumpkin pudding, powdered sugar and milk until thick. Fold in whipped topping. Spread over cooled cake. Top with crushed gingersnap cookies if desired.
Store leftovers in refrigerator.
