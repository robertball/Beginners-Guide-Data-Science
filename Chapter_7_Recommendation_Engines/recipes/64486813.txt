Title:
Eggplant Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 eggplant, sliced and quartered
1 can diced tomatoes, with juice
1/2 package of rigatoni pasta
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp basil

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir garlic until tender, about 2 minutes. Add eggplant; cook and and stir often, until eggplant is softened, about 5 minutes. Add tomato and juice; cook until sauce is slightly reduced, about 20 minutes.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked el dante, about 15 minutes. Drain and set aside.
Pour sauce into pot, mix thoroughly and serve.
