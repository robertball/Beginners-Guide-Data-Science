Title:
Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pound ground beef
1 large onion, chopped
1-2 Tablespoon(s) minced garlic
1 Teaspoon Season All
1-2 (10.75 ounce) cans Condensed Cream of celery Soup (Regular or 98% Fat Free)
2 tablespoon ketchup
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 cup frozen or canned vegetable, corn, green beans, peas and carrots, etc...
1 - 2 Cups mashed potatoes
1/2 to 1 Cup Shredded American or cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Cook beef, onion and Garlic in large skillet over medium-high heat until well browned, stirring to break up meat. Pour off fat.
Stir Season All, soup, ketchup, and pepper into skillet. Spoon beef mixture in rectangular baking dish.  Layer vegetables over meat mixture.  Spoon potatoes over beef mixture.
Top with shredded cheese
Bake at 400 degrees F for 15 minutes or until cheese is melted and potatoes are lightly browned.
