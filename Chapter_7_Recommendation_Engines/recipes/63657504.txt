Title:
My cousin Melissa's Pineapple Cheesecake Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups graham crumbs
1/2 cup butter
2 blocks(500g)softened cream cheese
1 tsp vanilla
3 eggs
1 large tub (500 ml) sour cream
1 tsp vanilla
1 19 oz can pineapple, crushed
1 cup sugar

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F.
Melt butter and add to graham crumbs, mix.
Press 1/2 of graham mixture in 9x13 inch pan, leaving 1/2 cup mixture to sprinkle on top.
Beat cream cheese until smooth.
Add sugar, vanilla and eggs one at a time until smooth.
Pour over graham mixture.
Bake for 30 minutes.
Remove from oven.
Drain pineapple well; add to sour cream and vanilla.
Carefully pour over cheesecake.
Sprinkle remaining crumbs.
Return to oven for 10 minutes.
Completely cool, cut into squares and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Volleyballmom
Description: 
This is not a traditional cheese cake, but a great summertime dessert. It's a bit fluffy and very rich. Handed down by my cousin Melissa years ago. Hope you enjoy!
