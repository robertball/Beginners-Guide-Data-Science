Title:
Jamie's Cranberry Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup almonds, blanched and slivered
1 pound spring mix lettuce
1 cup dried cranberries
2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds
1/4 cup white sugar
2 teaspoons minced onion
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
1/4 cup cider vinegar
1/2 cup olive oil

Directions:
Spread almonds in a single layer on cookie sheet.   Bake at 350 for 10 minutes.  Let cool.
In a large bowl, combine the lettuce with the toasted almonds and cranberries.
In a medium bowl, whisk together the sesame seeds, sugar, onion, paprika, white wine vinegar, cider vinegar, and olive oil. Toss with lettuce just before serving.
