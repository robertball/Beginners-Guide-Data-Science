Title:
Old Time Wheat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1907781.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups White Whole Wheat flour
4 cups unbleached flour
2 tbsp rapid rise yeast
1 tbsp salt
1/2 c. Coconut Palm Sugar or brown sugar]
3 tbsp gluten (optional)
1/3 cup coconut oil or your choice of oil
3 cups HOT tap water (I use as hot as I can get out of the tap)
1/2 -3/4 cup chopped walnuts or sunflower seeds (optional)

Directions:
Mix first 5 ingredients in large bowl or mixer.   Add oil and mix well.   Add water, mix and knead till dough is soft and smooth and doesn't stick to bowl.  Place in a greased bowl and grease top.  Let rise until double.  Punch down and shape into 2 loaves.  Put in greased pans, poke holes with fork over the top.  Cover with a towel and let rise til double.  Bake at 350 degrees F. 30-35 minutes.  Remove from oven and brush top with butter.

My Mom's basic bread recipe.  This is my go to recipe that I make weekly.
