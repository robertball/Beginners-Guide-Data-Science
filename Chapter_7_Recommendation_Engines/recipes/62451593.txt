Title:
Stuffed Cabbage Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cooked brown rice
1 head cabbage
1 pound lean ground beef
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/2 t. garlic
1/2 t. oregano
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce
1 (16 oz) can diced tomatoes
1 (4 oz) can diced green chilis
2 T. brown sugar
2 T. lemon juice
2 t. worchestershire sauce

Directions:
Cook rice according to package directions.
Bring a large, wide saucepan of lightly salted water to a boil. Add head of cabbage and parboil for 5-6 minutes; drain and place in cold water.
In a medium mixing bowl, combine the tomato sauce, green chilis and diced tomatoes.
In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, 1 cup cooked rice, onion, garlic, oregano, egg, salt and pepper, along with 2 tablespoons of tomato mixture. Mix thoroughly.
Line a greased 9 x 13 pan with cabbage leaves and place a small amount of tomato mixture on top of them.
Divide the beef mixture evenly among the cabbage leaves. Roll and lay seam side down in pan.
Pour remaining sauce over rolls.  Add another layer of cabbage leaves on top.  Bake at 350 degrees for about an hour.
