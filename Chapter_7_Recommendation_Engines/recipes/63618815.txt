Title:
Seafood Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1006508.jpg
Ingredients:
1 slice fish fillet
1 slice squid/cuttlefish
1/2 cup cooked ham
2 garlic,crushed
1 shallot,chopped
1/2 tablespoon salt
1/4 tablespoon ground pepper
12 lasagna noodles
10 ounces cream cheese
1 egg
1/2 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
1 small can mushroom soup (250ml)
1/3 cup water
2 tablespoons wheat flour
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
In a saucepan, cook fish fillet,cuttlefish,garlic,shallot over medium heat until well cooked. Add in mushroom soup, milk and water. Season with salt and pepper.  Simmer, covered for 5 minutes, keep stirring.
In a mixing bowl, combine flour with 1/3 cup water. Add into the saucepan and cook for 3 minutes.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes.
Combine egg, cream cheese and grated cheddar cheese in a mixing bowl.
Preheat oven to 190C.
To assemble, speared 1/2 cup of fish and cuttlefish sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.  Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over the sauce. Spread with a layer of cheese mixture.  Repeat layers and top with remaining cheese mixture.
Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes.
