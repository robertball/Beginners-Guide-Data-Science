Title:
Bacon Burger Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb. ground beef
1/3c. Panko bread crumbs
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 small onion, diced
2t. season salt
1/4t. black peper
8 slices of bacon

Directions:
Mix beef, onion, garlic, bread crumbs, and seasonings thouroughly.
Divide into 8 equal portions.
Shape each portion into shape of hot dog.
Wrap each hot dog with bacon and secure with toothpick.(Do not over lap bacon)
Place on foil lined cookie sheet and, place pan so food is 4 to 5 inches from broiler.
Broil 10 minutes on first side, or until bacon is done.(You may want to drain the grease off the cookie sheet to get crisper bacon)
Turn and broil second side for 5 minutes or until bacon is done.
*If you like you can sprinkle with a little chesse and bake at 525 degreese for 1-2 min.
