Title:
Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can crushed pineapple
1 can cherry pie filling
1 box yellow cake mix
2 sticks butter
1 package walnuts

Directions:
In a 9x13 pan pour crushed pineapple, then pour the cherry filling on top. Sprinkle the yellow cake mix on top. Chop walnuts and sprinkle on top of cake mix. Put squares of butter on top of walnuts covering all the nuts.
Bake at 350 for i hour or until golden brown.
