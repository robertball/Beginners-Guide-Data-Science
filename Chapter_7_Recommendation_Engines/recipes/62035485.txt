Title:
Houston's Spinach/Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pks. frozen chopped spinach
1 cup monterey jack cheese, grated
1 cup colby cheese, grated
1 can artichoke hearts(chop & drain)
**1 can chicken broth(enough for consistency only)
1 onion (Chopped & saute in margarine)
Tony Chachere seasoning
salt & pepper
parmesan cheese (enough to top off dish)

Directions:
Cook and drain spinach well.  Add all other ingredients except parmesan cheese
using only enough **chicken broth for the right consistency.  Top with parmesan and pop in oven until cheese is melted and dipo if is warm.  serve with tortilla chips.
