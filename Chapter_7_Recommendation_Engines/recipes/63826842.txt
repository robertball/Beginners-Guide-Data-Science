Title:
Gravy Baked Pork Chops I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (1 1/4 inch thick) pork chops
1 Tbl Butter
Salt and Pepper to taste
Old Bay Seasoning to taste
Garlic Powder to taste
3/4 cup milk
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 Clove Garlic, Minced
1 Small Onion, Chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Season pork chops with salt and pepper, Old Bay Seasoning & Garlic Powder to taste. In a large skillet over medium high heat, saute the Garlic & Onions in butter until translucent. Then remove. Then brown the Pork Chops in remaining butter for about 5 minutes per side.
In a separate medium bowl, combine the milk, garlic, onions and soup. Place the pork chops in a 9x13 inch baking dish and pour the soup mixture over the chops.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes.
