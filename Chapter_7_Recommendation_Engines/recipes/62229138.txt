Title:
Awesomely Easy Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter, melted (1/2 cup originally)
2 eggs, beaten
1 (8.5 ounce) package dry corn bread mix
1-3/4 cup FROZEN CORN KERNELS, THAWED or [1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained]
1 (14.75 ounce) can creamed corn
1 cup LIGHT sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C), and lightly grease a 11x7 (9x9) inch baking dish.
In a medium bowl, combine butter, eggs, corn bread mix, whole and creamed corn and sour cream. Spoon mixture into prepared dish.
Bake for 60 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the top is golden brown.
