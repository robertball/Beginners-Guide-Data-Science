Title:
Indian Butter Chicken, Julie's Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Julie’s Indian Butter Chicken
·	2 tablespoons olive oil
·	1 white onion, chopped
·	3 tablespoons butter
·	2 teaspoons lemon juice
·	1-2 tablespoons minced garlic
·	1-2 tablespoons minced fresh ginger root
·	2 teaspoons garam masala
·	2 teaspoons chili powder
·	2 teaspoon ground cumin
·	1 tsp ground coriander
·	¼ cayenne pepper
·	2 tablespoons sugar
·	1 bay leaf
·	1/4 cup plain yogurt (not low fat)
·	1 cup  heavy cream
·	1 cup tomato puree ( canned tomatoes food processed until smooth)
·	3 T. Tomato paste
·	1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
·	1 pinch salt
·	1 pinch black pepper
·	1 tablespoon peanut oil
·	1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-size pieces
·	1 teaspoon garam masala
·	1 pinch cayenne pepper
·	¼ cup ground cashews
·	Major Greys Chutney for garnish
·	Yogurt (not low fat) for garnish

Directions:
DIRECTIONS
1.	Heat 2 tablespoon oil in a large saucepan over medium high heat. Saute shallot and onion until soft and translucent. Stir in butter, lemon juice, ginger-garlic , cayenne, coriander, garam masala, chili powder, cumin, sugar and bay leaf. Cook, stirring, for 1 minute. Add tomato puree and paste,   cook for 2 minutes, stirring frequently. Stir in heavy cream and yogurt. Reduce heat to low, and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring frequently. Season with salt pepper. Remove from heat and set aside.
2.	Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a large heavy skillet over medium heat. Cook chicken until lightly browned, about 10 minutes. Reduce heat, and season with 1 teaspoon garam masala and cayenne. Stir in a few spoonfuls of sauce, and simmer until liquid has reduced, and chicken is no longer pink. Stir cooked chicken into sauce.  Add ground cashews and cook on low for 10 minutes.  Serve with a tablespoon of Chutney and a dallop of yogurt on the top.
