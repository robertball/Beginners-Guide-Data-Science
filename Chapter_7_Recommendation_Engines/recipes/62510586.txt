Title:
Hotcha Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 (2 inch) frozen mini tart shells
.
2 eggs
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup red bell pepper, seeded and chopped fine
1/2 of a small jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped fine (or more to taste)
1/2 tsp fresh minced garlic
4 teaspoons evaporated milk
1/2 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
.
1/4 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
1/4 cup chopped cilantro

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Place frozen tart shells on a baking sheet.
Beat the eggs until whites are incorporated and no longer ropey.
In a large skillet, saute the red and jalapeno peppers in the butter until they have softened.
Add the garlic to the skillet and cook for about 1 minute until softened but not browned; let cool for a few minutes.
Add about 1/4 cup of the eggs to the cooled skillet, then add skillet mixture to the remaining eggs.
Stir in the evaporated milk.
Stir in the cheese.
Fill the shells with about 1 tablespoon of the egg mixture.
Bake in the preheated oven until the shells are lightly golden brown and the filling is set, about 20 minutes.
Remove from oven and top with the additional cheese.
Cool on wire rack.
Top with chopped cilantro when ready to serve.
