Title:
Ground Turkey Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey (season with dried sage, thyme, garlic, Italian seasoning)
8 cups water
8 beef bouillon packets Herb Ox
2 cups white wine
Chopped celery
1 can Corn, drained
1 cup frozen Peas
2 carrots, sliced
1/4 cup rice

Directions:
Combine browned turkey and seasonings with remaining ingredients.
Simmer one hour.
