Title:
Cauli-tato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5622128.jpg
Ingredients:
4 quarts water
2 tablespoons salt
1 head cauliflower, washed and cut into bite-sized pieces
2 hard cooked eggs, diced
1/2 cup celery, sliced thin
3 tablespoons minced sweet pickles, or sweet pickle relish
4 tablespoons minced onion
3 pieces cooked bacon, crumbled
1 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon spicy mustard, or to taste
1/8 teaspoon turmeric
salt and pepper, to taste
additional crumbled bacon and fresh parsley for garnish, if desired

Directions:
In a large pot or stock pot, combine water and salt and bring to a boil over medium high heat.
When water is boiling, add cauliflower pieces and allow the water to come to a boil again.  Boil for three minutes.
Remove pot from heat and drain.
Spread cauliflower pieces on a metal tray and place in the freezer for 20-25 minutes to cool.
While cauliflower is cooling, prepare other ingredients and place in a large bowl.
Add cooled cauliflower pieces and toss to combine.
Add salt and pepper, to taste, and garnish with additional bacon crumbles and fresh parsley, if desired.

Submitter: 
Bibi
Description: 
Need to cut a few carbs?  This salad substitutes cauliflower bites for potatoes.  All the flavors you love in potato salad are here, minus a few carbs, and plus fiber!
