Title:
Chinese Style Glazed Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Pork Tenderloins (silver skin removed)
**
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup apricot preserves
1/4 cup hoison sauce
1/4 cup dry sherry
2 tablespoons freshly grated ginger
1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
2 cloves minced garlic
1 teaspoon 5-spice powder
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
**
1/4 cup ketchup
1 tablespoon molasses

Directions:
Whisk together all ingredients between astericks.
Measure out 3/4 cup for glaze and put in saucepan (reserve the rest for marinade).  Add ketchup and molasses.  Bring to simmer. Cook about 5 minutes until thichened.  Reserve 1/4 cup.
Butterfly tenderlions, leaving 1/3-1/2" at hinge.  Pound lightly until evenly thick.
Put reserved marinade and tenderloin into a gallon ziplock bag.  Squeeze out excess air and distribute marinade around meat.  Seal and refrigerate 30-minutes to 4-hours.
Heat, clean and season grill.  Reduce heat to medium-high.  Brush tenderlion with vegetable oil.  Cook pork on 1st side for about 2-minutes.  Flip and brush with glaze.  Cook 2 minutes - flip and glaze 3 more times.
Remove meat from grill and brush with reserved glaze (use a clean brush).  Cover with foil and let rest 5-minutes.
Slice thin and serve with rice.
