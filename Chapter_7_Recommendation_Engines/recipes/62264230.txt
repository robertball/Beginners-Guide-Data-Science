Title:
Mom's Salmon Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14.75 ounce) can salmon
2 cups Saltine cracker crumbs ( or a "sleeve" )
2 eggs, beaten
1 tsp dry mustard
1 tsp Worchestershire
Butter for frying as needed

Directions:
Take the salmon, saltines,dry mustard, Worchestershire and eggs...mix them in a bowl. Form them into patties ( I use a 1/3 cup per patty). Fry them in the butter ( olive or vegetable oil can be used too) 4 minutes on each side until golden brown
