Title:
Bibingka Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
3 eggs
1 cup sugar
1-1/4 cups coconut milk
1/4 cup Gouda cheese
2 Tbsp butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Mix flour, baking powder and salt, set aside.
Beat eggs until creamy.  Gradually add sugar, beating well after each addition (about 5 minutes).
Add flour mixture alternately with coconut milk.  Mix well.  Pour mixture into two 8 inch layer pans.  Bake for 15 minutes.
Sprinkle top with grated cheese.  Bake 10-15 minutes more.  Brush top with butter as soon as it comes out of the oven and sprinkle with sugar if desired.  Serve with grated coconut.
