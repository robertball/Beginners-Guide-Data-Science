Title:
Nana's Apple cobbler with a butter crumb topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 small to medium apples (peeled and sliced)
1/8 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 c water
Butter crumb topping
1 stick real butter
1 cup white sugar
I cup white flour
1/8 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp cinnamon
dash of salt

Directions:
Mix apples with spices and water let sit at room temp for one hour.
Sift spices with flour,mix softened butter with sugar till blended, slowly add the flour mixture and blend with a pastry blender till small uniform crumbs appear.
