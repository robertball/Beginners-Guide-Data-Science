Title:
Warm Italiano Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (250 g) package Philadelphia Light Brick Cream Cheese Spread, softened
1/4 cup pesto
1 plum tomato, chopped
1/4 cup KRAFT 4 Cheese Italiano Shredded Cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Place cream cheese spread in a oven safe dish; top with pesto, tomatoes and shredded cheese. cover with foil.
Bake 8 to 10 min. or until shredded cheese is melted and cream cheese is softened, but still holding its shape.
Serve with crackers.
