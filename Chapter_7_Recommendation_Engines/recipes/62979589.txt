Title:
Banana Oat Energy Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups oatmeal
1/2 cup chopped peanuts
1 cup unsweetened applesauce
2 bananas
2 carrots
1 apple

Directions:
1. Mash bananas
2. Shred apple and carrots
3.Mix all together
4. Bake in a 9*13 pan for 20 minutes or until golden brown
