Title:
Crispy Texas Catfish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/635398.jpg
Ingredients:
4 (6-9 oz) filets of catfish
2 cups yellow cornmeal
2 tbsp salt
2 tbsp pepper
1/2 cup vegetable (soybean) oil

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together cornmeal, salt and pepper thoroughly
Heat oil in large skillet over high heat
Dip catfish filets individually in cornmeal crumbs and shake excess off
Lay on hot oil and flip once when cornmeal is golden (about 2 minutes per side)
Serve with lemon wedges

Submitter: 
CatfishCove
Description: 
A recipe inspired by our secret restaurant recipe... this is a great base you can build on. Add spices to make it your own, we recommend paprika, cayenne and lemon pepper.
