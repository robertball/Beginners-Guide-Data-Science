Title:
Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 1.5 lb lean ground beef
1/3 cup chopped onion
1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
2 tbsp. butter
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1.5 cups sour cream, or more, to taste
2 tbsp. flour
1 tsp. seasoning salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. garlic powder, or fresh minced - even better!
1/2 tsp. paprika, optional
1 - 2 tbsp. liquid smoke, to taste (optional)
1 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Melt butter in large fry pan.
Saute onion and mushrooms. Add beef and cook till browned.
Sprinkle flour and seasonings over beef. Stir to mix.
Add liq. smoke, Worcestershire, soup and sour cream, stirring over med. heat until thickened.
Serve over hot noodles or mashed spuds with garlic toast on the side!

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Ground beef makes this a quick, tasty comfort food served over noodles or mashed spuds
