Title:
Italian Ice Box Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (11oz) box vanilla wafers
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 (14oz) can sweetened condensed milk
1 (20oz) can crushed pineapple with juice
1 (8oz) carton whipped topping (Cool Whip)
2 cups shredded coconut
1 cup chopped pecans
1 (10oz) jar of maraschino cherries, drained

Directions:
Place 1 layer of vanilla wafers in the bottom of a 9x13 dish.
In a bowl mix the sweetened condensed milk, lemon juice and pineapple.  Pour over wafers.
Place the rest of wafers on top of the mixture and then top with whipped topping.  Top with coconut, pecans and cherries.
Refrigerate overnight.
