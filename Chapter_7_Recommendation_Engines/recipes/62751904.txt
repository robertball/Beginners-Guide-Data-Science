Title:
Cottage Cheese Loaf III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (24 ounce) container of cottage cheese
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups Pepperidge Farm Bread Stuffing
3 tbsp mayonnaise
2 tbsp food yeast flakes
1 tbsp mckay's chicken seasoning
1 med yellow onion chopped fine
1/2 can of Campbells mushroom soup (family size)
1 cup of water
2 cups of cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix all ingredients except mushroom soup, water, and cheddar cheese together. Put mixture in greased baking dish. Bake @ 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes. Loaf is done when you shake it and its firm in the middle.
Now take 1/2 can of Campbells mushroom soup and 1 cup of water and mix well. Spread mixture on top of casserole. Grate 2 cups of cheddar cheese and sprinkle over the top. Bake until cheese is melted.
