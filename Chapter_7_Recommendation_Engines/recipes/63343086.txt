Title:
Chili Garlic Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. large shrimp(about 32) - peeled and cleaned
1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
1 1/2 tbsp. olive oil
1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper
3 large cloves of garlic - thinly sliced
1 bay leaf
1/2 cup dry white wine
4  1/4" thick lemon slices
2 tbsp. chopped fresh flat leaf parsley
1 tbsp. butter
2 cups cooked brown rice

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat.
Sprinkle salt and pepper on shrimp.
Add shrimp, garlic, red pepper and bay leaf to pan and cook about 3 minutes, stirring frequently.
Add wine and lemon to pan, increase heat to medium high and bring to a simmer.
Cook until liquid is reduced to about 1/2 cup - about 3 minutes.
Remove from heat and discard bay leaf and lemon slices.
Add parsley and butter and stir until butter melts.
Serve over rice with lemon wedges if desired.
