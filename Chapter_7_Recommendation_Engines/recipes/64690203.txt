Title:
Hula's Anzac Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5119293.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups of EACH of quick oats + coconut flakes + flour
3 C brown sugar
3 sticks melted butter
3/4 C golden syrup
3 tsp baking soda
6 TBSP boiling water

Directions:
Set oven to 350
Combine 3 cups EACH of quick oats + coconut flakes + sugar + flour mix 'em up
Melt 3 sticks of butter and 7 TBSP (= .45 cup so not quite 1/2 cup) golden syrup
Once butter is melted put 3 tsp of baking soda in 6 TBS boiling water till dissolved
Now pour baking soda/water mixture into the melted butter and mix well
Finally pour the wet mixture into the dry mixture and stir till well blended
Weight out 108gm (3.8oz) cookie balls, then squish them a bit into form of a cookie
