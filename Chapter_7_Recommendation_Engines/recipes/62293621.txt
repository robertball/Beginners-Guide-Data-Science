Title:
Grandma's Teriyaki Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. Sake
1 1/2 c. Shoyu
1 1/2 c. Sugar
1/2 c. Mirin
1 1/2 T. ginger, fresh ground

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a saucepan and boil. Cool and store in refridgerator.
