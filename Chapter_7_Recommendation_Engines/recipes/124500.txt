Title:
Mom's Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984473.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
4 teaspoons sugar
4 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
2 eggs, beaten
3/4 cup milk
1/4 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Make a well in the center. Combine eggs, milk and oil. Pour into well and mix just until dry ingredients are moistened (do not overmix). Spoon into greased muffin cups. Bake at 400 degrees F for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Jane  Jensen
Description: 
These muffins were always a special treat when I was a child. Now I have lots of muffin recipes, and I make one kind or another several times each week. But none are better than these...and for me, none come out of the oven with so many heartwarming memories attached!
