Title:
Easy Chicken Enchilada Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded chicken
1 cup enchilada sauce
1 cup sour cream
3/4 cup salsa
1 cup corn (optional)
4 oz can diced green chiles
12 white corn tortillas, torn in small pieces
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
Green onion to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. In large bowl, whisk together, enchilada sauce, sour cream and salsa. Ad chicken, corn, green chiles and 3/4 cup cheese stir to combine. Add corn tortillas, stir. Spray 9x13 dish and scoop mixture inside. Sprinkle with remaining cheese and bake 25 minutes or until golden brown and bubbly. Let rest 5 minutes before serving. Top with green onions.
