Title:
Steak Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887611.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup olive oil
1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 tablespoons garlic powder
3 tablespoons basil
1 1/2 tablespoons parsley flakes
1 teaspoon white pepper
1 teaspoon dried minced garlic

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender.
Blend on high speed for 30 seconds until thoroughly mixed.
Pour marinade over desired type of meat.
Cover, and refrigerate for up to 8 hours.
Cook meat as desired.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Prepare succulent steaks with this easy marinade that's a tried and true family favorite.Modified from: Best Steak Marinade in Existence
