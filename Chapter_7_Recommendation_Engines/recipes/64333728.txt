Title:
Savory Zesty Cream of Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2058485.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound chorizo
4 cups russet potatoes
4 stalks of celery
1 - 2 cups baby carrots
1 medium onion
32 0z chicken broth
2 cups heavy whipping cream
2 tbsp flour
1/2 tsp celery salt
2 tbsp olive oil
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown chorizo, drain & set aside. If you can't find ground chorizo you can buy it in links and take it out of it's casing.
Peel & cube potatoes. Place in cold water & set aside.
Rinse carrots and chop into bit sized pieces  (if desired, or keep whole)
Chop celery & onion then saute in olive oil in heavy pot on medium low heat until soft.
Pour in chicken broth then add chorizo, drained potatoes and carrots.
Add celery salt, and then salt & pepper to taste.
Simmer over medium low heat until everything is tender.
Reserve 1/2 cup of heavy cream, pour remaining into pot. Continue cooking over medium low heat.  Check seasoning, add more if needed.
Warm remaining cream slightly and whisk in flour.
Pour milk / flour mixture into soup slowly.
Simmer until thickens.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Michelle Horne
Description: 
This recipe is derived from my grandmother's potato soup recipe.  I've changed it up a bit to give it a little kick. The chorizo is a fantastic addition and makes the soup more of a meal than a side dish. Wonderful for cold & rainy nights. Your whole family will love this.
