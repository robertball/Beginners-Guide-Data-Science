Title:
Chicken Vesuvio Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup all purp flour
1/2 tea. salt
1/2 tea. pepper
1/2 tea. dried thyme
1/2 tea. dried rosemary
1 tea. dried oregano
1 (3 lb.) chicken, rinsed and patted dry, cut into 8 pcs.
olive oil for frying
4 baking potatos, unpeeled, cut into eighths, soaked in ice water
1/3 cup chopped fresh parsely
4-8 cloves minced garlic (to taste)
1 cup dry white wine
1 cup cooked peas

Directions:
Heat about 1/2 inch olive oil in a large, heavy skillet to 360 degrees. Fry the chicken a few pcs. at the time until well browned. dont crowd the pan. Drain chicken on paper towels. Pat the potatoes dry and add to skillet of hot oil. cook until golden brown all over. Remove and drain on paper towels. Pour off all but 1/4 inch of fat from the pan. Return chicken and potatoes to the skillet crowding them in as neccessary. Sprinkle with the chopped parsely and minced garlic- pour the wine over. Bake uncovered for 20-25 minutes. Strew the peas over last minute.
