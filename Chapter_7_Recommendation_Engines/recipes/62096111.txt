Title:
Double Cheesy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cheddar cheese soup
1/2 soup can of milk
1 tbsp. dried parsley
1/2 tsp. paprika
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4  tsp. pepper
4 cups potatoes, sliced
1 onion, diced
1 cup Monterey Jack cheese, grated (Or if you like it spicy use Pepper jack)

Directions:
In a bowl, combine soup, milk, parsley, paprika, salt and pepper.
In a greased or nonstick 9x13 dish layer half the potatoes, half the onions and half the cheese. Repeat.
Pour soup mixture over all.
Bake at 350' until potatoes are done, approx. 30-45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jodster
Description: 
A slightly different way to make a potato side dish.
