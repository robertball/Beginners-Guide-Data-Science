Title:
Rotisserie Chicken in vertical electric rotisserie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3-5 pound) whole chicken
1 pinch salt
1/4 cup butter, melted
1 tablespoon smoked paprika
1-1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Season the inside of the chicken with a pinch of salt. Spray inside of basket with no-stick spray. Place the chicken onto spit and into rotisserie basket, wings down & tucked in, legs up.
Mix together the butter, paprika, salt, garlic powder, and pepper. Baste the chicken with the butter mixture. Close the lid and cook for 1 to 1 1/2 hours (20 min/lb), basting occasionally, until the internal temperature reaches 180 degrees F (83 degrees C) when taken in the thigh with a meat thermometer.
Turn off rotisserie element and let stand for 10 to 15 minutes before cutting into pieces and serving.
