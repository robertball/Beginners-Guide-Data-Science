Title:
Vegan Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 TB Tofutti cream cheese
4 TB Earth balance margarine
2 TB Tofutti sour cream
3 oz Daiya mozzerella cheese
2 TB nutritional yeast
1/4 cup soy milk
dash garlic powder
dash nutmeg

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in blender.  Pour over pasta.
