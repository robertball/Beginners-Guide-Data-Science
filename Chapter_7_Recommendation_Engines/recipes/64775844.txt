Title:
Big Ray's Cranberry Sauerkraut Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7622858.jpg
Ingredients:
2 can (14 ounces) whole-berry cranberry sauce
1 can (14 ounces) sauerkraut, rinsed and well drained
2 bottle (12 ounces) chili sauce
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 package (32 ounces) frozen fully cooked homestyle meatballs
Minced chives

Directions:
In a 4-qt. slow cooker, combine the cranberry sauce, sauerkraut, chili sauce and brown sugar. Stir in meatballs. Cover and cook on low until heated through, 4-5 hours. Top with chives to serve.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
These are super! So easy and fast to prepare the slow cooker, toss it in before work. This is sometimes used  as a dinner meat not just an appetizer. 
 If you don't like sauerkraut, don't let it deter you from trying these, you'll forget sauerkraut is even in there.
