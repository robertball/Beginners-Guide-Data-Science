Title:
Chili Sauce Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 c tomato sauce
1/4 c brown sugar
2 T vinegar
1/4 c cinnamon
Pinch ground cloves
Pinch allspice

Directions:
For 1 cup of chili sauce, combine 1 cup tomato sauce, 1/4 cup brown sugar, 2 tbsp. of vinegar, 1/4 cup cinnamon, a pinch of ground cloves and a pinch of ground allspice.
