Title:
Pecan Pralines Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 CUP Brown Sugar
1/2 CUP Granulated Sugar
1/4 TSP Salt
1/2 CUP Heavy Cream
1/4 TSP Cream Of Tartar
1 CUP Chopped Pecans
2 TBSP Butter
1/2 TBSP Vanilla Extract

Directions:
Combine the brown sugar, granulated sugar, heavy cream, and cream of tartar in a sauce pan.  Stir over low heat until the sugar dissolves, wiping the sugar crystals from the sides of the pan with a rubber spatula or wet brush.
Over medium high heat, cook the mixture for approximately 15 minutes of until the mixture reaches the soft ball stage (234°F - 240°F), on a candy thermometer.  A regular thermometer will work as well to check for the proper temperature.
Add the pecans.  Cook for 2 minutes stirring constantly.  Remove from heat.
Add the butter and vanilla extract.  Beat until the mixture looks creamy around the edges of the pan.
Drop the candy from a spoon onto waxed paper, baking parchment paper, or a well-greased pan.
Allow to cool and get firm.
