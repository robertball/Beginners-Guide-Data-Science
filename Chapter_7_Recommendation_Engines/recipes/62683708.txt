Title:
Lightened Indian Butter Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized chunks
2 Tb butter, divided
1/3 cup minced onion
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 8oz can tomato sauce
1/2 15oz can evaporated fat free milk
1/4 cup plain thick greek yogurt
1 tsp salt
1/8 tsp cayenne powder
1/4 tsp chili flakes
1 1/4 tsp garam masala

Directions:
Melt 1 Tb butter in a skillet over low heat. Stir in onion and garlic, and cook slowly until the onion carmelizes to a dark brown, about 15 minutes.
Add the rest of the ingredients except for the chicken to the pot and simmer for 10 minutes.
Add the cut chicken, cover and cook on low for 25 minutes, turning heat down if it starts boiling.
Serve with basmati rice and Naan or pita.
