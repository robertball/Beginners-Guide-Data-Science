Title:
Pineapple Marmalade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 orange
1 lime
4-1/2 cups crushed pineapple
4 cups sugar
1 cup water
1 package powdered pectin

Directions:
Zest orange and lime.  Put zest in a pot with water.  Bring to a boil and simmer for 20 minutes.  Cut membrane from fruit and chop fruit coarsely.  Add to zest mixture and cook for 15 minutes.  Put crushed pineapple into a large pot and add zest/fruit mixture.  Stir in pectin and bring to a full rolling boil that cannot be stirred down.  Add sugar all at once and return mixture to a full rolling boil.  Boil 1 minute.  Remove from heat and skim off any foam.  Ladle into jars.  Process in a water bath for 10 minutes.
