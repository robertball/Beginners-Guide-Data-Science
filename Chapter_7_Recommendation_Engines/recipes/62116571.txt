Title:
German Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 strips bacon
3 medium potaotes, diced
1 medium onion, diced
2 cans seasoned green beans
1 pound little smokie sausages

Directions:
Fry bacon until crisp, drain and crumble. Saute onion in dripping. Add liquied from beans and diced potatoes. Salt and pepper to taste. Simmer until potatoes are almost done. Add beans and sliced sausages. Sprinkle crumbled bacon on top to serve.
