Title:
Hearty Garden Vegetable Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbs extra virgin olive oil
1/2 small onion, diced
1 tbs garlic, minced
1 tbs tomato paste
1 tsp celery seed
1 tsp cumin seed
2 celery stalks, sliced
1 small carrot, diced
1 small turnip, peeled and diced
2 C kale or collard greens, chopped
1 large tomato, chopped
1/2 tsp sea salt
1 15 oz can pinto beans
1 C fresh or frozen corn
5 C water
2 tbs fresh basil (2 tsp dried)
salt and pepper to taste
splash of balsamic vinegar or lemon juice

Directions:
In a large soup pot, heat the oil over medium heat and saute the onions and garlice until they sweat.
Add the tomato paste and stir to coat the onions.
Add the celery and cumin seeds, then the carrots, celery, and turnips, sauteeing for a few minutes to soften.
Add the greens and tomato, saute 1 minute then add the salt.
Add the pinto beans with liquid, corn, water, and basil and bring to a boil, turn down to low and simmer for 15 minutes.
Add salt & pepper to taste and a splash of vinegar or lemon juice.
