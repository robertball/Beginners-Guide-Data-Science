Title:
Flower Pot Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 unglazed, untreated terra cotta flower pots (wash in hot water before using)
Chocolate cake mix (any flavor works)
Quick Chocolate Frosting (use half)
3 ½ cups icing sugar
1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
12 Tbsp (1 ½ sticks) unsalted butter, softened
½ cup milk, room temp.
2 tsp. pure vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
Brush inside of each pot with oil and line with parchment paper or foil. Brush lining with oil, and lightly dust with cocoa.
Mix batter according to pkg. directions.
Divide batter among prepared pots, filling each 2/3 full.
Transfer to a rimmed baking sheet. Bake, rotating sheet ½ way through.
Let cakes cool completely before decorating.
Sift sugar and cocoa together into a medium bowl.
Wisk in butter, milk, and vanilla until smooth. Use immediately.
Decorate tops of pots with oreo crumbs (dirt) and insert gummy worms, chocolate rock candies or mint sprigs to decorate flower pot.
