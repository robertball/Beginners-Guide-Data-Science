Title:
Maple walnut quick bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 3/4 cup flour
1 Tbsp baking powder
1/3 cup butter
1 cup maple syrup
1 egg, lightly beaten
2/3 cup evaporated fat free milk
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup toasted walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Lightly grease a 9 X 5 loaf pan.
Mix flour and baking powder in large bowl, make well in centre.
Beat butter in medium bowl with wooden spoon until creamy, mix in syrup. Beat in egg and then milk and vanilla. Pour mixture into well of flour and stir until just combined. Will be lumpy. Stir in walnuts.
Scrape batter into loaf pan. Bake until golden brown or a toothpick inserted near the centre comes out clean. About 40 - 50 minutes.
Cool on wire rack 10 minutes. Turn out of pan onto wire rack to finish cooling.
