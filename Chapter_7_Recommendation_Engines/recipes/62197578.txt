Title:
Grilled Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/301452.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Salmon or 4 Salmon Filets
1 Lemon, sliced
1/2 Onion, diced
1/2 cup Lemon Juice (approx.)
Ms. Dash Original Seasoning

Directions:
Preheat grill to med-high.
Rinse Salmon thoughly and place on layer of aluminum foil. Pour lemon juice over Salmon filets or in cavity, do not saturate.
Sprinke Ms. Dash over filets or in cavity. Add lemon slices and onion.
Place second sheet of aluminum foil over salmon and tighly secure. (important to ensure moist salmon)
Cook on upper rack of grill for 15-20 minutes. Flip, being careful not to tear aluminum foil. Cook additional 15-20 minutes.
If you have a good seal on aluminum foil the salmon can cook for another 10 minutes to ensure it's done without worry about being dry.
Remove from grill and serve on platter.
If you have never cooked salmon before: Skin can be removed with a spatula by gentlty pealing off.
For whole salmon: After removing skin slowly run a spatula along spine, gently pull meat away from bones. Remove skeleton buy slowly pulling up the spine from forward end of salmon. Take your time!
Serve with rice and your favorite veggie.
