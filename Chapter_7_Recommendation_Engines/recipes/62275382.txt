Title:
Confetti Turkey Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground turkey
1/3 cup each, minced green and red bell peppers, celery, and red onion.
1/4 tsp. each dried thyme, rosemary & majoram
1/4 tsp. each seasoned salt and pepper
1/4 cup chili sauce
1 cup commercial bread crumbs
1 egg

Directions:
Pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees.
Break apart ground turkey with a fork.
Add the peppers, celery and onion.
Add the seasonings abd chili sauce.
Mix well with a fork.
Add the bread crumbs.
Add the egg and mix all ingredients well with hands.
Lightly spray a loaf pan with Pam.
Pat the turkey mixture into pan, shaping it slightly away from the sides of the pan.
Lightly spread chili sauce over top of loaf.
Bake uncovered for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
Remove from oven and let rest 5 minutes before serving.
