Title:
Cosmopolitan (Cosmo) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981500.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 ounces Smirnoff No. 21™ Vodka
1/4 ounce triple sec
1 ounce cranberry juice
1 twist lemon

Directions:
Pour Smirnoff No. 21(TM) Vodka, triple sec, and cranberry juice into a shaker with ice.
Shake and strain the cocktail into a martini glass; garnish with a twist of lemon.

Submitter: 
Smirnoff
Description: 
Clean and ultrasmooth Smirnoff No. 21™ Vodka gives this cocktail a clear, pure taste unmuddied by impurities or harshness.
