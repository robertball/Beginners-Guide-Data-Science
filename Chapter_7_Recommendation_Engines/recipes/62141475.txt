Title:
Chocolate Candy Cane Bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 - 1/2 cup candy cane pieces
2 cups milk chocolate,semi-sweet or white chocolate chips

Directions:
In a double boiler (or on the lowest setting in a pot) - melt chocolate on low heat, stirring constantly with a rubber spatula.  Stir in candy cane pieces.
Spread on some parchment paper to cool and harden.
Break into servable pieces.
