Title:
Aunt Mirlinda's Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c flour
2 c sugar
2 tsp soda
2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp salt
1 c canola oil
4 eggs
3 c grated carrots

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients.  Add oil and mix.  Add eggs one at a time.  Add carrots.  Bake 30-90 minutes at 350* F.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
This is a favorite family recipe of a very fun Aunt, Mirlinda who loved to laugh and visit over good food - especially desserts!.
