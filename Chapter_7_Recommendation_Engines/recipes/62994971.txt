Title:
Baked Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/745601.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups brown rice
2 1/2 cups water
1 T butter
1/2 t sea salt
1/2 t granulated garlic
1/2 t black pepper
1 t (heaping) of Tones chicken base

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Place the rice in an 8" square baking dish
Bring the water, butter, spices and chicken base to a boil in a covered saucepan. Once it boils, pour it over the rice.
Stir a bit
Cover with heavy-duty aluminum foil. This step is important, cover tightly - you want the steaming effect.
Bake on middle rack for 1 hour.
Fluff and serve.

Submitter: 
Scotdog
Description: 
This is my spin on Alton Brown's recipe. I've made a lot of brown rice and this is the absolute best way to make it.
