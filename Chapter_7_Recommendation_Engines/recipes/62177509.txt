Title:
Liegnitzer bomben Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 oz honey
1 1/4 c sugar
9 tbsp butter
6 tbsp milk
3 eggs
1/2 btl of lemon essence or
1 tsp fresh grated lemon zest
1 pinch cardamom
1 pinch mace
1 pinch cloves
1 pinch cinnamon
4 c + 1 tbsp flour
5 tbsp cocoa
2 1/2 tsp baking powder
3/4 c + 1 tbsp currant
3/4 c + 1 tbsp almonds (chopped)
3/4 c + 3 tbsp citron (diced)
1 1/4 c apricot jam
2 tbsp water
3/4 c powdered sugar
3 tbsp cocoa
4 tbsp hot water

Directions:
Warm the honey, sugar, and butter and milk slowly melting then carefully. Transfer to a mixing bowl and cool.
Mix and sift together the baking powder, cocoa and flour. When the melted ingredient are almost cold, mix
in the eggs, flavoring (or lemon zest) and spices, flour, currants almonds and citron until almost smooth.
Grease little muffin tins, and fill 1/2 full with dough.
Bake in a 350 degree oven for 20-25 min.
Mix the jam with the water and boil up. Brush thinly over the cooled cookies.
For the icing sift the powdered sugar and cocoa and blen with as much water as will give a good coating consistancy. Ice the cakes.
After they have completly dried, pack in airtight containers to keep for a week.
