Title:
Fragrant Summer Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/630477.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 lb Organic Baby Spring Mix
1/2 cup Cheese Garlic Croutons
1/3 cup dried and sugared cranberries
1/2 cup walnuts
2/3 cup grated asagio cheese
1/4 cup of Alessi balsamic vinegar
1/8 cup of honey
1/4 cup of regular olive oil

Directions:
Combine all in a salad bowl and toss well. Microwave salad for approximately 15-20 seconds to warm and wilt. Enjoy.

Submitter: 
Peaceluvandhappiness
Description: 
A salad featuring the tangy flavor of good balsamic vinaigrette mingled with walnuts, dried cranberries, crunchy croutons, and a zip of fresh grated asiago cheese.
