Title:
Saucy Chicken and Asparagus Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2lb fresh asparagus, trimmed
2T vegetable oil
4 boneless chicken breasts
1/2t salt
1/4t pepper
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2c mayonnaise
1t lemon juice
1c shredded cheddar

Directions:
Place asparagus in a sprayed 9x9 dish.
Brown chicken in oil in a skillet and season with salt and pepper.
Arrange chicken over asparagus in dish.
Mix soup, mayo, and lemon juice and pour over chicken.
Cover and bake at 375 for 40 minutes.
Sprinkle with cheese and let sit for 5 minutes to melt.
