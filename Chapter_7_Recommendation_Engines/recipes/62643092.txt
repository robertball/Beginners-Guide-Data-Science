Title:
Pound Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/583434.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
4 oz. cream cheese, softened
2 cups sugar
4 large eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. baking powder
3 cups all purpose flour
8 oz. sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Beat butter and cream cheese until creamy, add sugar and beat until fluffy. Add eggs, 1 at a time beating until blended after each. Stir in vanilla.
In seperate bowl combine flour, baking soda and baking powder.
Gradually add this to butter mixture alternating with sour cream. Beating until blended.
Spoon into muffin pans 2/3 full. Bake @ 350 for 13-15 mins for mini cupcakes and 20-24 mins for regular sized cupcakes. Makes 6 dz. minis and 2 dz. regular.

An easy, yummy pound cake in cupcake form :) I got this recipe out of a Southern Living magazine and modified it alittle. Enjoy!
