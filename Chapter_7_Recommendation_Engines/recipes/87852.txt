Title:
Apple Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2437988.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
4 slices white or whole wheat bread
1 tablespoon cinnamon
1 large apple, cored and thinly sliced

Directions:
Set oven to broil.
Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread. Place apple slices on buttered side of bread. Sprinkle cinnamon on top. Place bread on a baking sheet.
Place in a preheated oven until toasted, about 2 minutes.

Submitter: 
NATALIE12345
Description: 
Apple toast is a great after-school snack for kids who are picky eaters. It doesn't take long to make and is a light snack that will leave you satisfied.
