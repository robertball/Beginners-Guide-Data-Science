Title:
Gail's Double-Duty Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. Hamburger
2 eggs
3/4 cup ketchup
1/2 medium onion finely chopped
1/2 green bell pepper, finely chopped
1 cup dry oatmeal (quick cooking)
1 teaspoon prepared mustard
salt and pepper to taste
Topping:
1 cup ketchup
1 tsp. prepared mustard
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 Tablespoon soy sauce
2 Tablespoon cider vinegar

Directions:
Combine all ingredients until well blended.  Place meat mixture in a loaf pan.  Pour half of the sauce over meatloaf.  Bake at 350 degrees for 60-65 minutes .  Drain.  Let stand 10 minutes before slicing.  Serve with remaining sauce for dipping.
FOR TOPPING:
In a small microwave safe bowl, combine all ingredients and microwave for about 30 seconds or until ingredients are warm enough to dissolve the sugar.

Submitter: 
Gail Cobile
Description: 
This meatloaf is delicious hot out of the oven and  any leftovers are fabulous for next day sandwiches for a lunchtime treat.  Tart, tangy, sweet and savory all wrapped up in one dish!
