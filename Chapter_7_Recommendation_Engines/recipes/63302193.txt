Title:
Sweet Caprese Quinoa Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Quinoa (43g)
1 cup water
1 tbsp Balsamic vinegar
1/2 tbsp honey (10g)
Juice from 1/2 lemon
pinch of salt
Fresh mozarella ball, cubed
~20 basil leaves, chopped
~3 or 4 tomatoes, chopped

Directions:
Boil quinoa and water until liquid is absorbed. Cool slightly, then combine liquid ingredients
