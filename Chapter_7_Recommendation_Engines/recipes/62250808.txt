Title:
A Meat Hearty Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/332577.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups ricotta cheese
1/2 shredded parmesan
4 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp dried parsley
1 tsp dried basil
1/4 tsp black ground pepper
2 eggs
2 pound ground beef
2 (48 ounce) jar PregoÂ® Traditional Italian Sauce
12-16 cooked lasagna noodles
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Mix ricotta cheese, shredded parmesan, 3 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese, salt, parsley, basil, black pepper and eggs. Set aside.
Cook beef in saucepan until browned. Drain off excess fat. Return to pan and stir in pasta sauce.
Spread 1-1 1/2 cups beef mixture in each of 2 2-quart shallow lasagna baking dishes. Top with 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese. Top each with 2-3 lasagna noodles, lining the entire pan and then divide 1/2 ricotta cheese mixture into each pan, spreading evenly. Top with 2-3 lasagna noodles. Repeat layers. Top each with 2-3 lasagna noodles, remaining beef mixture and grated Parmesan cheese.
Bake at 400 degrees F for 30 minutes or until hot.  Let stand 10 minutes.
