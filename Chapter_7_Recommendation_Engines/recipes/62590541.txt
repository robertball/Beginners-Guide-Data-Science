Title:
Bruschetta Stuffed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Italian tomatoes
1-1/4c mozzarella
1/4c chopped basil (fresh)
1 package (6oz) chicken stuffing
8 chicken breasts pounded thing
1/3c Italian dressing

Directions:
Combine tomatoes, 1/2c cheese, basil, and stuffing.
Place stuffing on each chicken breasts, roll, and place in 9x13 dish.
Bake 40 minutes at 350.
Add remaining cheese and bake 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Christine Simon Michener
Description: 
Can also make Mexican variation using Rotel tomatoes, Mexican style cheese, and cilantro
