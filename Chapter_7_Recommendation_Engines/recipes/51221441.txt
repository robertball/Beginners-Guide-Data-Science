Title:
Baked Polenta with Fresh Tomatoes and Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tube polenta
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1tsp basil
salt and ground black pepper to taste
1 tablespoon butter, chilled and cut into pieces
2 tomatoes, sliced
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 fresh basil leaves, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Grease an 8x8 inch baking dish.
Slice polenta layer in bottom of casserole dish, sprinkle with1 cup Parmesan cheese and  basil leaves; season with salt and pepper to taste. Arrange the  chilled pieces of butter over the top.
Bake the polenta in the preheated oven until bubbly and beginning to brown, 15 miniutes.  Remove from the oven.
Decoratively arrange the slices of tomato over the top of the polenta, spread the remaining basil leaves over the tomatoes, and sprinkle with 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese.
Return to the oven and bake until the tomatoes are warm and the Parmesan cheese is bubbly, 5 to 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Diana Ackaret
Description: 
This recipe is a perfect way to highlight and use fresh summer tomatoes.  It makes a great side dish for grilled chicken or pork chops.Modified from: Baked Polenta with Fresh Tomatoes and Parmesan
