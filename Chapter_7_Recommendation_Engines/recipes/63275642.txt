Title:
Baked Cod with Thyme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs cod fillet
1 tbs EVOO
1 tsp fresh thyme
1/2 tsp sea salt
1/4 tsp onion powder

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375*. Cut the cod into 5 oz pieces.
Combine all other ingredients in a small bowl, set aside.
Spray a baking sheet with EVOO or cover with parchment paper.
Place the cod on the baking sheet and spread the herb mixture over each piece evenly.
Bake for approximately 12-15 minutes or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.
