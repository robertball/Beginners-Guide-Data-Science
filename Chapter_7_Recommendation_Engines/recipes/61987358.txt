Title:
Mexican-Style Corn and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion, finely minced
2 tsp. canola oil
1 1/2 cups uncooked white rice
2 cups water
1/2 green pepper, diced
1/2 tsp. salt
dash of garlic powder
1/4 tsp. ground cumin(optional)
1 tsp. chili powder
pinch cayenne pepper
1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen corn
kernels
1(1 pound)can diced tomatoes,
including juice

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over medium heat, cook onion in the oil until soft. Add rice, water, green pepper, salt, garlic powder, chili powder, cumin, and cayenne pepper. Stir in the corn and tomatoes. Cover, lower heat and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes, until the liquid is absorbed and the rice is just tender.
