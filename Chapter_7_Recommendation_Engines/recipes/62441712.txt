Title:
Simple Hamburger Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground chuck
1 onion, chopped
1 (8 oz.) pkg. sliced fresh mushrooms
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 TBLS. flour
1 can beef broth
1 can cream of muchroom soup (with roasted garlic if you have it)
2 TBLS. Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp. black pepper
1/2 tsp. salt
1 c. sour cream
2 tsp. horseradish (optional)
1 (12 oz.) pkg. egg noodles, cooked

Directions:
Cook ground beef, onion, mushrooms, and garlic. Drain. Add flour, and cook 2 minutes while stirring. add broth, soup, Worcestershire, pepper, and salt. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer stirring occasionally for 15 minutes. In a bowl, combine sour cream and horseradish; add to beef mixture. Stir. Cook for 5 minutes or until hot. Serve over hot cooked egg noodles. Garnish with sour cream and fresh parsley.
