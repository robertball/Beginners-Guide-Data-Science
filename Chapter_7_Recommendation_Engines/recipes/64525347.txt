Title:
Spicy Peach Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 20-ounce package frozen mini meatballs
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 onion, chopped
1/4 teaspoon salt
Dash white pepper
3/4 cup chili sauce ( I use Siracha )
1/2 cup peach jam
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Bake meatballs as directed on package. While those are baking, heat olive oil in a saucepan and add onion, salt, and pepper. Cook and stir until onion is starting to turn brown and caramelize. Add chili sauce, peach jam, and water; stir; and bring to a boil. Add the cooked meatballs and stir to coat. Serve in a chafing dish or Crock-Pot.

Submitter: 
Michael Bacon
Description: 
These meatballs are a hit.  Hot and sweet make it a delicious treat.
