Title:
Pancake Mix Mini Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2044897.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup of Hungry Jack extra light & fluffy pancake mix
1/2 cup of water
1 1/2 tablespoons of liquid egg white
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Spray a small muffin tin with PAM; set aside. Mix the pancake mix together with all of the ingredients until lumps are gone.Pour the batter into the muffin tins about 3/4 full. Bake for 10-12 minutes.

Submitter: 
Chef Lizzie
Description: 
This is a quick and easy recipe to freeze and eat in the mornings.
