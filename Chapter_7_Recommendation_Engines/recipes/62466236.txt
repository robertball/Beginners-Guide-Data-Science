Title:
Slow Cooker Teriyaki Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp. ground ginger
3-4 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 1/2 lbs. chicken drumsticks/wings
1/3 cup flour
1 cup water (may need a little more)

Directions:
Combine all but the last two ingredients and place in crockpot.
Cook on low for 4 hours.
Just before serving, whisk water and flour together and pour into teriyaki sauce to thicken it.
Serve over rice.
