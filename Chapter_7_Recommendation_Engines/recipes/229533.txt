Title:
Ham and Cheese Skewers with Crunchy Maille® Cornichons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991745.jpg
Ingredients:
12 Maille® Cornichons
12 cherry tomatoes
4 slices country ham
1 small block of Swiss cheese, cut into cubes
4 wooden skewers

Directions:
Cut each slice of ham in 3, and roll each piece on itself to form a little square.
Slide a Maille(R) cornichon, then a cube of ham, then a cube of cheese, then a tomato on a skewer. Repeat 3 times to fill the skewer.
Repeat for each skewer.

Submitter: 
Maille
Description: 
For a simple and tasty appetizer, simply place cornichons, cherry tomatoes, ham, and cubes of Swiss cheese on wooden skewers--and serve!
