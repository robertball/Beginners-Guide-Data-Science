Title:
Electric Lightning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4522012.jpg
Ingredients:
ice cubes
1/2 fluid ounce vodka
1/2 fluid ounce coconut-flavored rum
1/2 fluid ounce blue Curacao liqueur
1 fluid ounce lemon-lime soda
1 wedge lemon

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice; pour vodka, rum, and blue Curacao over the ice and stir. Pour lemon-lime soda into the shaker; stir gently. Strain beverage into a cocktail glass and garnish with lemon wedge.

Submitter: 
SANDI149
Description: 
This is a perfect drink for summer or anytime of year as it makes a wonderful presentation because of its color. Make a big batch of them for all your friends. It's quite easy to make, too!
