Title:
Cream Cheese Lemon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box lemon cake mix
1/3 cup butter or margarine – softened
1 egg
8 ounces cream cheese – softened
1 cup powdered sugar
1/2 lemon – grated
2 tablespoons lemon juice or 1/2 fresh squeezed lemon
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
Container: 9 x 13 baking pan and a mixing bowl

Directions:
1. In a mixing bowl,blend dry cake mix, butter, and 1 egg.
2. Press into a 9x13 pan ungreased.
3. Gradually add in the power sugar
4. Stir in lemon juice until smooth
5. Reserve 1/2 cup of this mixture and refrigerate it for later use.
6. Beat the remaining two eggs , add the vanilla, and the beat the mixture into the remain cream cheese mixture.
Bake at 350 until set 25 to 30 minutes.
7. Cool completely
8. Spread the reserved cream cheese mixture onto the bars
9. Refrigerate until firm; then cut into squares
