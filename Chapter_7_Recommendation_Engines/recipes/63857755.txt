Title:
Lamb Shepherd's/Cottage Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 medium potatoes, peeled (Yukon Gold)
4 cloves of garlic
5 tbsp butter
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup cheddar cheese, grated
1 medium onion, diced
2 carrots, grated
1.5 lbs lamb, cut into 1/2-1 in. chunks
2 tbsp flour
1/2 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp salt
3 tbsp ketchup
1.5 cups beef broth
1 small can of mushrooms
1 cup petite frozen peas

Directions:
Place potatoes and garlic in a pan with enough water to cover potatoes.
Boil for 20 minutes or until potatoes pricked with fork slides off easily.
Drain potatoes, and add 3 tablespoons of butter, half of the cheese and milk.
Mash potatoes until smooth, and set aside.
Preheat oven to 375 F.
Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in a frying pan on medium high heat.
Add onions and carrots, and saute until onions are translucent (3-4 min)
Add lamb, and continue to saute until meat is browned
Add flour, thyme, salt and pepper, and stir until meat and vegetables are evenly coated.
add ketchup and broth, and stir.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and let simmer for 10 min.
Transfer meat mixture to shallow casserole dish, and spread evenly.
Sprinkle peas evenly over meat mixture
Spoon potatoes on top of peas, and smooth to cover
Sprinkle remaining cheese on top, and bake in oven for 25 minutes.
