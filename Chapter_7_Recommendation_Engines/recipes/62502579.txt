Title:
No-fuss Beef Barley Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 pound) beef chuck roast
4 cups packaged beef stock
1 bay leaf
3 carrots, sliced into long ovals
2 stalks celery, chopped
1 onion, chopped
1/2 tsp. oregano
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. sugar
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
1/2 c. barley
1 (16 ounce) package frozen peas
1 cup packaged beef stock

Directions:
Place 4 cups of beef stock and everything else but the barley and frozen peas into the slow cooker and cook on low all day.  About 1 hour before serving, add barley, peas and remaining cup of beef stock and turn up to high.  Remove bay leaf, correct seasoning, and serve with good bread or rolls.
