Title:
easy pizza dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon yeast
1 cup warm water
1 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoon olive oil
start with two cups flour
pizza toppings

Directions:
1.Dissolve yeast in warm water,then add sugar.
2.Stir remaining ingredients in until well combined.Let dough sit for 5 to 8 minutes.
3.preheat oven to 425 degrees.
4.Spread dough onto a lightly greased pizza tray.Add toppings and bake for 10 to 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
jaydubs
Description: 
Quick and easy ,tastes great and fills you up.Make sure you have it in the oven long enough or dough will be gooey.
