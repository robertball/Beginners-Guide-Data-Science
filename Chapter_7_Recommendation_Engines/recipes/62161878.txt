Title:
Bean enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can refried beans
1/2 cup onion
1/4 cup yogurt
2 cloves of garlic
2 tomatoes
1 teaspoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/2 sour cream
10 tortillas

Directions:
To make filling, mix beans, onion and yogurt.
To make sauce, saute garlic, tomato, oregano, pepper and cumin on medium heat until mixture liquifies (about 10 minutes).
Remove from heat and stir in sour cream.
To assemble, scoop 1/4 cup of filling on each tortilla.
Roll up, and place seam down in shallow baking pan.
Cover with sauce.
Bake uncovered in preheaded 350 degree oven for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
linda
Description: 
This hearty recipe can be made quickly with ingredients you have on hand. Filling can be prepared in advance and stored in the fridge.
