Title:
Crustless Chili Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup fat-free egg substitute
3 tablespoons flour
2 tablespoons + 2 teaspoons tub margarine, melted
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
1/4 teaspoon salt
3-4 drops red pepper sauce
1 cup cottage cheese (small curd, 2 % fat)
1 1/2 ounces Monterey Jack cheese, grated
1/4 cup chopped green chilies
1/4 drained chopped roasted red pepper
1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Spray pie pan
Preheat over to 350, 35 minutes
Combine all ingredients, except parmesan
Beat well; spoon into pie pan
Sprinkle with parmesan
Cut into fourths and serve immediately
Can be kept in refrigerator and warmed in microwave
