Title:
Cabbage Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups chopped cabbage
tsp salt
1/4 cup chopped green pepper
1 lb browned ground beef
4 cups water
2 cans kidney beans (light or dark, your choice)
1 large can diced tomatoes
(or regular size can and tomato paste to your taste and consistency)
tablespoon worcestershire

Directions:
cabbage ... chop as you like it
cover with water in the soup kettle, salt lightly and cook until tender - add chopped green pepper in with the cabbbage.
In a separate pan brown 1 lb ground beef.
Add to cabbage and cover with water, simmer until cabbage is tender.
When beef and cabbbage are ready, reduce heat to low and simmer.
Add kidney beans, tomatoes, and worcestershire.
Let it simmer for a bit, until you can't wait any longer.
Serve yourself.  Enjoy.
