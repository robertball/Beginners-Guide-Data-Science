Title:
Fiber One "Bread" Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/795854.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Pouch Fiber One Original Cereal (3 3/4 cups)
2 diced apples
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup dried cherries
1/3 cup & 2 tbs brown sugar
1 tb butter
4 eggs
2 1/2 cups half & half
1 tb vanilla
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp red sea salt
powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
dice apples & toss with 2tb brown sugar
saute apples, raisins & cherries in butter until apples soften
Mix eggs, rest of brown sugar, half & half, vanilla, cinnamon & sea salt in a bowl
fold in cereal & fruit
let stand at room temperature for 20 minutes
stir every few minutes
pour into buttered pie dish
Bake for 40 minutes or until toothpick is clean
allow pudding to cool & settle for 60 minutes
dust with powdered sugar
serve with dollop of whipped cream
