Title:
Adapted Libby's Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 & 1/4 cups sugar
6 tsp. cinnamon
1 & 1/2 tsp. salt
3 tsp. ginger
1 & 1/2 tsp. cloves
6 eggs
3 cans (45 oz) pumpkin
3 cans (36 oz) evaporated milk

Directions:
MIX sugar, cinnamon, salt, ginger and cloves in small bowl.
BEAT eggs in large bowl.
STIR in pumpkin and sugar-spice mixture.
Gradually stir in evaporated milk.
POUR into pie shell.
BAKE in preheated 425° F oven for 15 minutes.
REDUCE temperature to 350° F; bake for 40 to 50 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean.
Cool on wire rack for 2 hours. Serve immediately or refrigerate. Top with whipped cream before serving.

Submitter: 
katznkidz
Description: 
For 2 deep dish pies, make enough for 3 and double the spices.
