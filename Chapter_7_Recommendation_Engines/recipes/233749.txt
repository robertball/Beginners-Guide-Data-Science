Title:
Blueberry Banana Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3816793.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups vanilla ice cream
1 cup milk
1 1/2 cups blueberries
1 banana
1/4 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Place vanilla ice cream and milk in a blender and blend until smooth. Add blueberries, banana, sugar, and vanilla extract; blend until evenly mixed.

Submitter: 
Lisa Altmiller
Description: 
This is my kid's favorite milkshake. Made with vanilla ice cream and yummy, healthy blueberries and bananas, it's a perfect summertime treat!
