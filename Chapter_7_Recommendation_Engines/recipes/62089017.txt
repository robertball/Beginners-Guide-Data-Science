Title:
Back to School Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts
2 tbsp peanut oil
2 tbsp sherry
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 egg white
tsp red pepper flakes
tbsp corn starch
3 cloves garlic, chopped
3 bunches green onion, chopped
1 can baby corns
1 cup snowpeas
1 tbsp sesame oil
1 cup peanuts
1 cup chicken broth

Directions:
thinly slice chicken
mix peanut oil, sherry, soy sauce, egg white, red pepper flakes, and cornstarch
marinade chicken in mixture 30 min.
stir fry garlic 1 min.
add chicken, stir fry until done
add chicken broth, peanuts, sesame oil,peas, corn, and onions
heat
Serve over rice

Submitter: 
Kristyn
Description: 
My mother-in-law gave me this recipe, and we just love it. She used to make it for my husband and his friends when it was time to go back to school after summer break.
