Title:
Baked Spaghetti Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/764425.jpg
Ingredients:
1 loaf Rhodes Bread Dough, thawed at room temperature
6 oz whole wheat spaghetti, cooked
1 jar spaghetti sauce
8 oz mozzarella cheese, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1egg yoke
Parmesan cheese
1package Sausage or 1 pound hamburger or both
Pepperoni
5 tablespoons of Butter melted
1/2 tsp seasoned salt

Directions:
1) Cook spaghetti, meanwhile, in a large skillet, cook beef or sausage until no longer pink; drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and seasoned salt; set aside.
2) In a large bowl, whisk the eggs, Parmesan cheese and butter, drain spaghetti, add to egg mixture and toss to coat.
3) Spray counter lightly with non stick spray
4) Roll out bread dough flat to 12x16 inch rectangle, cover with wrap and let rest for 15 minutes
5) Remove wrap from dough, place spaghetti in the center in about a 4inch wide strip. I mix the sauce and spaghetti all together first.
6) Place cut up cheese across the top of the spaghetti mix
7) Make cuts about 1-1/2 inches apart from the edge of the filling to the ends of the dough
Begin braiding by folding the dough from the outside while taking turns on each side folding in the filling
7) Place on a baking sheet, brush with egg yoke and sprinkle a generous amount of Parmesan cheese and garlic powder on the top.
8) Bake at 350 for 30-35 minutes or until golden brown. Cool, slice and serve
