Title:
Oriental Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. spaghetti
vegetable or sesame oil
1/4 C. green onions
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
2 T. soy sauce
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook spaghetti. Immediately after draining, mix with enough oil to glisten noodles so it doesn't stick. Mix in other ingredients.

Description:
