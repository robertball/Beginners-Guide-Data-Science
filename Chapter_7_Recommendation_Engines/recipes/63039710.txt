Title:
Bodybuilder pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup oatmeal
1/2 cup fat-free cottage cheese
1 egg
3 egg whites
1 dash cinnamon
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth.
cook as you would regular pancakes, in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.  Flip when bubbles form and set in the top of the pancakes.  Cook for a few minutes more on the bottom of the pancakes

Submitter: 
Mark Adams
Description: 
High protein, high fiber, low fat way to have your morning pancakes!
