Title:
Yogurt Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C Greek yogurt (I used Stonyfield Farms)
I t vanilla extract
1/2 C powered sugar, sifted

Directions:
Whisk all ingredients until they become a bit thick.
Place in the fridge to thicken even more (at least 30 minutes).
Spread on cupcakes

Submitter: 
brasicana
Description: 
This is a quick and yummy glaze to put on top of cupcakes or regular cake!!
