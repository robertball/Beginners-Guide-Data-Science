Title:
Carrot and Almond Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1104896.jpg
Ingredients:
¤ 3 cups grated carrots
¤ 2 cups flour
¤ 1&1/4 cup oil
¤ 2 cups sugar
¤ 1 cup peeled almonds
¤ 4 eggs
¤ 1&1/2 tbsp baking powder
¤ 1 tsp vanilla
¤ 2 tsp cinnamon
¤ 1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Beat the eggs, oil, sugar and vanilla in a bowl. Incorporate the flour, baking powder, salt and cinnamon slowly to the mixture while beating on low speed. Fold in the carrots and almonds.
Pour the mixture in a large circular greased pan and bake for 40 minutes.
Prepare the cream cheese frosting, frost the cake and decorate with almonds and carrots.

Submitter: 
Meri
Description: 
Very rich cake recipe! The combination of carrots and crunchy almonds makes it a tasty and healthy dessert.
