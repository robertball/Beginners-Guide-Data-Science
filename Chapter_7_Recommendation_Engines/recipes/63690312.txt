Title:
Mozzarella and Ham Stromboli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can refrigerated pizza dough
2 c. mozzarella cheese
1 pkg. ham lunchmeat
1 T. butter

Directions:
Roll out pizza dough.
Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and lay out ham slices on one half.
Fold pizza dough over and seal edges.
Brush with butter.
Bake in a 425 degree oven until golden brown.
Serve with pizza sauce.
