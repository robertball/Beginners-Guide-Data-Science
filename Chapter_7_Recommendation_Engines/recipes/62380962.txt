Title:
Jalepeno Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 bunch cilantro
2 jalepenos, seeded
3 cloves garlic
1 cup plain yogurt
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine cilantro, jalepenos and garlic and process in food processor.
Add yogurt salt and pepper and process until desired consistency is reached.
