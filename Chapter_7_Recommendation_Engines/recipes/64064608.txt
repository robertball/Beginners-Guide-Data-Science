Title:
Newport Clam Chowder II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter (1 stick)
1/2# bacon
3 large onions, chopped
1-1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 51oz. can chopped clams
8 boiling potatoes, unpeeled & chopped
1 qt. heavy cream
2 cups 2% milk
salt and pepper to taste
1-1/2 teaspoon chopped fresh dill weed
1 teaspoon Old Bay
1-1/2 teaspoon MSG

Directions:
Melt butter in a large kettle or stock pot over medium heat and add bacon. Add onions and saute until clear. Stir in flour and cook over low heat, stirring frequently, for 2 to 4 minutes. Set aside to cool.
In a separate pot, bring clams and clam juice to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
In a small saucepan, cover peeled potatoes with water. Bring to a boil and cook until potatoes are tender, about 15 minutes. Drain and set aside.
Slowly pour hot clam stock into butter/flour mixture while stirring constantly. Continue stirring and slowly bring to a boil. Reduce heat and add cooked potatoes. Mix in cream, Old Bay, salt and pepper, MSG and chopped dill.  Heat through but do not boil.
