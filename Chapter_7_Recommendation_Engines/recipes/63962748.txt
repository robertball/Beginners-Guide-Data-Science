Title:
Blue Cheese Dip with Garlic and Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 bacon slices, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
8 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
1/4 cup half and half
4 ounces blue cheese, crumbled
2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
3 tablespoons chopped smoked almonds (abt 1 ounce)
crackers or raw vegetables

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook bacon in a heavy, large skillet over medium-high heat until almost crisp; add garlic and cook until bacon is crisp.  Drain the bacon on a paper towel.
Using an electric mixer, beat cream cheese until smooth; add half-and-half and beat until well-combined.  Stir in bacon and garlic, blue cheese and chives.  Transfer to a 2-cup baking dish; cover with foil.  Bake 15 to 20 minutes.  Sprinkle with chopped almonds.  Serve with crackers or vegetables.
