Title:
Cajun Chicken and Fettuccine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5434866.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz. Fettuccine (or Linguine)
4 tablespoons butter
4 tablespoons flour
4 cups milk
1 tablespoons Louisiana Cajun Spice
8 oz Asiago Cheese, grated
1/4 C sherry
1 bunch green onion
5-6 chicken breasts

Directions:
Cook Fettuccine or Linguine according to package directions.
Season chicken breasts with Cajun Seasoning. Grill 5 minutes, until slightly underdone.
Make Roux: Melt butter. Stir in flour. Cook a few minutes. Stir in milk. Heat through. Add 1T cajun spice, cheese, sherry. Chop green onions and chicken and stir in.
Serve over cooked Fettuccine or Linguine
