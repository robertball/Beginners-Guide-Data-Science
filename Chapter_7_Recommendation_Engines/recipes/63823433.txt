Title:
Chicken Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 Chicken Breasts
4 oz sour cream
4 oz cream cheese
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can of cream of mushroom soup
1 stick butter
1 can water chestnuts, sliced
2 stacks of Ritz crackers, crushed
Shredded cheese of your choice (optional)

Directions:
Cook chicken in salted water until tender.  Cut into bite sized pieces and set aside.  Mix the soups, sour cream, and cream cheese and heat slowly, blending well.  Add the broth from the chicken as needed.  Add water chestnuts and chicken.  Mix melted butter with the cracker crumbs and put half into a 9x13 glass casserole.  Add chicken mixture and top with remaining crumbs.  Bake at 325 for 40 min.  Shredded cheese can also be put on top for added flavor.
