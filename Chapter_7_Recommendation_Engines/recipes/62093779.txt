Title:
Crispy Sesame Pork Loins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup dry red wine
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp dried parsley
4 pork loin steaks
3/4 cup sesame seeds
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 tsp ground pepper
1 tsp dried parsley

Directions:
Mix the red wine, worcestershire sauce and dried parsley in a medium bowl and let the pork loins marinade in it for about 20 minutes.
Combine all remaining ingredients in a seperate medium bowl.
Coat each steak in the sesame mixture and place in a baking dish.
Bake at 400 degrees for about 30 minutes or until pork loins are completely cooked.

Submitter: 
Angie
Description: 
This red wine marinade and sesame seed breading will make pork loins melt in your mouth! The red wine gives the meat a rich flavor and the sesame seed provide a nutty coating that is quite unique.
