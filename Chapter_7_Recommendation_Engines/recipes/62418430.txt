Title:
Miracle Baking Powder Pie Crust III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/489786.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
3 teaspoons baking powder
8 tablespoons 2% milk
1 tablespoon powdered sugar (Optional)
1/2 cup white shortening
1/2 cup butter

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine flour, salt, baking powder and powdered sugar.
Place 1/2 cup of flour mixture in a small bowl, and stir in milk until smooth.
Cut shortening and butter into remaining flour mixture until it looks like coarse meal. Blend in the flour-milk mixture.
Wrap dough into a ball and chill in refrigerator until firm in texture.
Cut ball into halfs and roll out between two sheets of wax paper.  Place in pie pan by gently peeling away the wax paper.  Place holes with a fork into the bottom and sides of the pie crust.
For a pre-baked pie crust: Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees and bake 14-18 minutes or until golden brown.
