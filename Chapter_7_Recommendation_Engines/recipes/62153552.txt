Title:
Oven Brown Rice with Carrots and Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup brown rice
1- 14 oz. can beef broth
½ c. cream of chicken soup
½ c. milk
¼ c. butter
4 oz. can sliced mushrooms  drained
1 cup finely diced carrots

Directions:
Preheat over to 350 degrees.
Spray a 2 qt. casserole dish with non-stick cooking spray.
Mix all ingredients together and bake uncovered for 60 min. until liquid is absorbed and rice is tender.
OPTIONAL – add slivered almonds or diced   waterchestnuts.
