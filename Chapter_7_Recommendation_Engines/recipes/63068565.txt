Title:
Dorothy Lynch- Large batch recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 c. oil
12 c. sugar or equivalent of Splenda Sugar Blend
9 c. apple cider or white wine vinegar
15 cans tomato soup
3/4 c. celery seed
1/2 c. dry mustard
1 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/4 c. sea salt or less if using regular table salt
1 1/2 tsp black pepper
2 small cans tomato paste (opt)

Directions:
Using a stand mixer add oil first. Mix the dry mustard with the sugar so mustard doesn't ball up. Add rest of ingredients and mix for 30 minutes.
