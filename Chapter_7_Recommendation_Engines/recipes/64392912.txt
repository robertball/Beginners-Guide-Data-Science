Title:
Chicken casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2230221.jpg
Ingredients:
1-2 cups cooked Chicken (shredded or in pieces, seasoned with garlic powder & pepper)
1 lb cooked small pasta (we like shells)
1 & 3/4 cups dry stuffing mix-divided
1 can cream of chicken soup (I make my own)
2 cans of milk (use the soup can or 22oz)
2 tlbs butter or margarine

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
Place soup, milk & butter in large saucepan or pot
Bring to a boil and lightly simmer until thickened, stirring often
Remove from heat and stir in 3/4 cup of stuffing mix, chicken and noodles.
Pour into 13x9 baking dish and top with remaining stuffing.
Bake 25 minutes or until sauce is bubbling and stuffing is brown.
