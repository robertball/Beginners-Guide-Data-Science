Title:
Fruity Turkey Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/519101.jpg
Ingredients:
½ c. Dried cherries
¼ c. raisins
1 can mandarin oranges
3 apples, peeled & chopped
16 oz. cubed bread stuffing
16 oz. corn bread mix
1 tbsp sage
Ground pepper & salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in large bowl and stuff in bird.

Submitter: 
US92
Description: 
THANKSGIVING MEAL 2010  This is the stuffing that Ron the Food Dude put inside his Whiskey Glazed Turkey (see recipe in US 92 Recipe Box) The Whiskey Glaze gave the stuffing a little bit of a whiskey flavor too.
