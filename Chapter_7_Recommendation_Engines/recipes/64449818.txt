Title:
Chorizo Stuffed Roasted Poblano Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2386864.jpg
Ingredients:
5 Poblano Peppers, fresh
1 9oz tube Chorizo (beef or pork)
1 15oz can corn
2 cups white rice (cooked)
1/2 cup Monterey Jack cheese
Salt & pepper as needed

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°
2. Cook rice until fluffy.
3. Roast poblano chiles over open flame until skin is black and bubbled. Set aside.
4. Cook chorizo until crumbled.
5. Mix canned corn, cooked rice and chorizo. Season as needed.
6. Peel skin and seeds from poblano. Place chiles on greased baking sheet.
7. Fill chiles with chorizo mix.
8. Bake at 350° for 15min.
9. Remove from oven, sprinkle with jack cheese, and bake another 5-7minutes until cheese is melted.
10. Serve with bread and a salad. Use sour cream if it's too spicy.
