Title:
Raspberry Sparkler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 6 oz. cans frozen mixed citrus juice concentrate, (my favorite:  white grape raspberry)
1 Sprite 2 liter
1/2 10 oz. package frozen raspberries

Directions:
Make Citrus drink according to can instructions, substituting Sprite for water.  Place a rounded teaspoon of frozen berries in the bottom of each glass and pour punch mixture over the top!

Jennifer Hanson

Quick and easy sparkling (non-alcoholic) punch
