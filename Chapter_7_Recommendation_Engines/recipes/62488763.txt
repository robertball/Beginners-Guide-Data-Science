Title:
Mother Earth's Tropical Fruit Fluff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (3.4 oz.) boxes of lemon flavored instant pudding mix (unprepared)
2 (20 oz.) cans of undrained crushed pineapple
1/2 c. chopped pecans
1 (8 oz.) tub of extra-creamy coolwhip
1 c. sweetened coconut flakes
2 (15.25 oz.) cans of tropical fruit
1 (15 oz) can of mandarin oranges
1 lb. (16 oz.) fresh strawberries (halved)

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix until dissolved the 2 boxes of dry pudding mix with the 2 cans of crushed pineapple (with juice)
Stir in the chopped nuts
Fold in the coolwhip until well blended
Mix in the coconut
Add the tropical fruit, mandarin oranges and strawberries and stir until well blended.
Chill for 2 hours
Add the sliced bananas before serving.

Submitter: 
Terra
Description: 
Aloha! A tasty twist to the pistachio fluff, this wonderfully sweet salad brings lemon pudding and strawberries to the mix. A delicious side dish for any summer picnic.
