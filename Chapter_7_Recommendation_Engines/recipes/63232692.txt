Title:
Pickled Herring and Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Eurofresh English Seedless Cucumbers
1 Cup Apple Cider Vinegar
1/2 Cup granulated sugar
2 12oz. jars of Vita Herring in Wine Sauce
1 Onion
4 Eurofresh Tomatoes on the Vine

Directions:
1. Using a mandolin thinly slice the Cucumbers and          Onion into a large glass mixing bowl.
2. Dice the Tomatoes and combine with Cucumber and Onion.
3. In a sauce pan over medium heat combine the vinegar and sugar and bring to a boil.
4. Pour the vinegar solution over the Cucumbers, Onion, and Diced Tomatoes and refrigerate for 30 minutes to marinate.
5. Drain an reserve the liquid from the jars of Herring. Cut the Herring into smaller pieces and then take the reserve juice and herring and combine with the rest of the Ingredients. Refrigerate for 1 hr to chill before serving.
