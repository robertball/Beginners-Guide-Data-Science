Title:
Yorkshire Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1090052.jpg
Ingredients:
7/8 C. (1 C. Scant) all-purpose Flour
1/2 tsp. Salt
1/2 C. Milk (ROOM Temp.)
2 Eggs Beaten (ROOM Temp.)
1/2 C. Water (Warm Temp.)
2 TBLS. Oil

Directions:
Blend Flour and Salt together.
Form a well in the center.
Stir in the Milk, beaten Eggs & Water and beat until light and airy.
