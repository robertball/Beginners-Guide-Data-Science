Title:
Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. vegetable oil
2 Tbsp. chopped garlic
1 C white wine
3/4 gallon whole milk
2 C roux
1 Tbsp. chicken base
1 Tbsp. salt
2 Tbsp. seasoning mix
2 C parmesen cheese

Directions:
Heat vegetable oil in a sauce pan on medium heat.  Add garlic and saute until you smell the garlic.  Deglaze with the white wine and reduce by half.
Slowly stir in milk.  When milk is warm, about 80-90 degrees, add roux, chicken base, salt, and seasoning mix.  Stir with whip until roux is dissolved.
When sauce starts to thicken, turn heat to low.  Add parmesen cheese and blend well.

Submitter: 
Chef Hurley Baird
Description: 
A thick and creamy sauce for any pasta!
