Title:
Swiss Round Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Lbs. Round Steak, Cut into serving size Pieces
3 Tbsp. Flour
1/4 Cup Butter
1/4 - 1/3 Cup Low Sodium Beef Broth
1 (14.5 oz) Can Diced Tomatoes, Undrained
1/4 Tsp. Sugar
1/4 Tsp. Salt
1/8 Tsp. Pepper
1/4 Tsp. Dried Basil
2 Cloves of Minced Garlic
1 Green Pepper, Chopped
1/2 Onion, Chopped
1 Cup Shredded Mozzarella Cheese (Optional)

Directions:
Place Flour in a large Ziploc bag
Add beef pieces to bag, seal and shake to coat. You may have to do it in batches
Place butter in a large, deep skillet and melt only, do not brown butter
Add beef pieces and brown beef on all sides
Add Broth, tomatoes, green pepper, onion ,Garlic, salt, basil, sugar and pepper and bring mixture to a boil
Reduce heat to a simmer, cover and simmer meat for 1 1/2 Hours. Stirring occasionally.
Sprinkle with cheese and let it simmer uncovered for a few minutes or until cheese starts to melt. (Optional)
Serve.

Submitter: 
Jodster
Description: 
This is an easy basic recipe that my family and I love. The long cook time ensures that the meat comes out flavorful and very tender. If there is any sauce left over I spoon it over mashed potatoes after dishing up everyones plates. Yumm!
