Title:
Chocolate Coconut Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks softened butter,
2 boxes powdered sugar,
2 cans angel flake coconut,
5 cups chopped pecans,
tootpicks,
1 block parafin,
12 ounces semi-sweet chocolate morsels.

Directions:
Mix first four ingredients in a large bowl.  Roll into balls about 2 inches in diameter.  Stick toothpicks into each ball.  Place on cookie sheet lined with waxed paper.  Place in freezer for about 20 minutes.  Melt parafin and chocolate morseld in double boiler.  Keep on low heat, and dip balls into chocolate and let sit until cool and set up.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
My mom used to make these every Christmas, it was our special treat.
