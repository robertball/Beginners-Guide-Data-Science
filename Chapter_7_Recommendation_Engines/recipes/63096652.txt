Title:
Irish Flag Curry Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pt. (or more if too strong) Mayo
1 med/lg onion grated
5 T ketchup
5 tsp. curry powder
1 T Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp. Tabasco

Directions:
Grate onion in a blender or food processor. Pour into a bowl. Add the remaining ingredients and mix well.

Submitter: 
Sudie B
