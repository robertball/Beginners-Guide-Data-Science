Title:
Pasta with sausage and arugula Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz pasta (used penne, but broken lasagna noodles, rigatoni, or bowties would be great)
.6lb sausage, removed from casings (2 sausages or more to preference)
6 oz arugula
5-8 cloves Garlic
Pasta water
parmesan cheese
Lemon juice
olive oil

Directions:
cook pasta to al dente in boiling salted water
saute garlic in olive oil
add sausage and brown
turn off heat, add arugula while pasta finishes cooking
add pasta to sausage/arugula
add pasta water
add approx 1/2 lemon juice or more to taste
salt
olive oil if needed
parmesan cheese
toss all
