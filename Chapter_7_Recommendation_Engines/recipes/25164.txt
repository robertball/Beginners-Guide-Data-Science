Title:
Lime Fruit Salad in a Mold Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1318484.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3 ounce) package lime flavored Jell-O® mix
1 cup boiling water
1 cup cold water
4 rings canned pineapple, drained
1/2 cup pitted dark sweet cherries, drained
1/4 cup halved seedless red grapes
4 pears - peeled, cored and chopped

Directions:
In a large bowl, dissolve the gelatin in the boiling water. Whisk in the cold water and set aside to cool slightly.
Rinse a 1-quart mold in cold water. Stir the pineapple, cherries, grapes and pears into the gelatin and pour into prepared mold. Refrigerate until set, at least four hours.

Submitter: 
SALLYCOOKS
Description: 
This sparkling lime gelatin salad is embedded with luscious bits of pineapple, cherries, grapes and pears, and takes only minutes to prepare! Allow several hours for Jell-O® to set. I serve this with cream cheese and/or mayonnaise.
