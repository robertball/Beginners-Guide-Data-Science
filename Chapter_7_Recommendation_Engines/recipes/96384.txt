Title:
Grilled Turkey Burger with Roasted Red Pepper Mayonnaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993450.jpg
Ingredients:
TURKEY BURGERS
1 (20 ounce) package JENNIE-O TURKEY STORE® Ground Turkey
2 finely chopped green onions
1/2 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
1/2 cup chopped fresh spinach
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
6 Kaiser rolls, split, toasted if desired
ROASTED RED PEPPER MAYONNAISE
1/3 cup light mayonnaise
2 tablespoons chopped roasted red peppers
1/2 teaspoon grated lemon peel
1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In large bowl combine all turkey burger ingredients except Kaiser rolls. Mix well. Form into 6 (1/2 inch thick) patties.
Heat broiler. Spray broiler pan with nonstick cooking spray. Broil burgers 4 to 6 inches away from heat, turning once, 4 to 6 minutes on each side or until center is no longer pink. Toast buns if desired.
Meanwhile, in small bowl combine all mayonnaise ingredients; mix well. Refrigerate until serving time.
Place each turkey burger inside roll; top with mayonnaise.

Submitter: 
JENNIE-O TURKEY STORE®
Description: 
These are not your average burgers. Ground turkey patties are seasoned and grilled then served on toasted kaiser rolls with a roasted red pepper mayonnaise.
