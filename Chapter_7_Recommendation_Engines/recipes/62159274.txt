Title:
Asian slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Asian Slaw
Ingredients:
2 packages (3 oz. each) Ramen noodle soup mix beef flavored
2 packages (8.5 oz.) coleslaw mix
1 cup sliced almonds, toasted
1 cup sunflower seeds kernels
1 bunch green onions, chopped
1/2 cup sugar
3/4 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup white vinegar

Directions:
Preparation Remove flavor packets from soup mix and set aside, crush uncooked noodles.
Place noodles in a large bowl. Top with coleslaw mix and sprinkle with toasted almonds, sunflower seeds, and green onions.
Whisk together contents from flavor packets, sugar, oil and vinegar. Pour over slaw mix. Cover and chill for 4 hours.
