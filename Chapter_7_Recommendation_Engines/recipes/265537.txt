Title:
Zucchini Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5626766.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup ice cubes, or as needed (optional)
1/2 zucchini, shredded
1/2 frozen banana
1/2 cup orange juice

Directions:
Combine ice cubes, zucchini, banana, and orange juice in a blender. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Isabela14
Description: 
Zucchini blended with orange juice and banana makes a healthier and great-tasting breakfast smoothie. And don't worry, you can't even taste the zucchini in it.
