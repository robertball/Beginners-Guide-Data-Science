Title:
Christmas Tea Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sour cream
1 tsp. baking soda
4 c. flour
1 c. sugar
2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. cream of tarter
1 c. butter
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Lightly grease baking sheet.
Combine sour cream and baking soda and set aside.
In a large bowl mix flour, sugar, baking powder, cream of tarter, and salt.
Cut in butter.  Add sour cream and baking soda mixture.  Add egg.
Turn onto lightly floured surface, kneading briefly.
Roll dough into 3/4" thick round.
Cut into 12 wedges and place them on baking sheet.
Bake 12-15 minutes until brown on bottom.
