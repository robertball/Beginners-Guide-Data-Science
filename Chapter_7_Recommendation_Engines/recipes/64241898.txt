Title:
Quiche Leonard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1748784.jpg
Ingredients:
1 sheet of puff pastry (thawed)
1/3 cup of grated asiago cheese (or whatever you have on hand)
1/4 cup finely diced cooked bacon or ham
4 eggs
1/4 cup half & half
2 slices of large tomato
1/4 cup of mozzarella
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Grease quiche pie dish and line it with the pastry. Dust a little flour on the pastry to make it easier to handle. Bake in the oven at 425 degrees for 20 mins until golden brown but not fully cooked.
Layer grated cheese on the bottom, followed by the bacon, whisk the eggs, add salt and pepper and pour into pie crust. Add a little half and half without letting mixture overflow.  Top quiche with sliced tomatoes and mozzarella.
Back at 425 degrees for 25 mins, reduce heat to 375 degrees and bake for another 20. Checking - remove when golden brown.
