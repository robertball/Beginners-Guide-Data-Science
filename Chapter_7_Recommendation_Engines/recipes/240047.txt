Title:
College Kid Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
NA
Ingredients:
¾ cup coconut oil
¾ cup white sugar
¾ cup firmly packed brown sugar
2  eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
¾ teaspoon ground cinnamon
½ teaspoon salt
2 ¾ cups instant oatmeal
1 cup blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Beat coconut oil, white sugar, and brown sugar together in a bowl until smooth. Add eggs and vanilla; beat until light and fluffy.
Whisk flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt together in another bowl. Gradually beat flour mixture into sugar mixture; stir in oatmeal and blueberries until dough is combined. Drop dough by the teaspoonful 2-inches apart onto baking sheets.
Bake cookies in the preheated oven until golden brown, 8 to 10 minutes. Cool slightly on the baking sheet before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Submitter: 
Lorin Renee
Description: 
A really fast and easy way to make oatmeal if you're a college student and don't have access to a fully-stocked pantry. It's quick, simple, and wonderful for birthdays and dessert nights!
