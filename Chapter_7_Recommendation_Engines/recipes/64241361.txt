Title:
Caramel for Apples & Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Karo Syrup
1 Cup Margarine
2 Cups Brown Sugar
1 Can Eagle Brand Sweeten Milk

Directions:
Add syrup, margarine, & brown sugar and cook to a soft ball stage (225 degrees)
Add 1 can Eagle Brand Sweeten Milk cook at 235 (hard ball)
Dip clean dry apples
Chill
For Caramel Popcorn cook to soft ball stage and pour over 5 Courts of popped popcorn
