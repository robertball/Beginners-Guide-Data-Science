Title:
Fig, Mustard, & Champagne Glazed Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/960750.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbs canola oil
2tbs Fig butter
2 tbs Dijon Mustard
4 Chicken Breasts
1 tbs Champagne Vinaigrette
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F.
Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, to taste.
In a large saute pan over high heat, add the oil. Once heated, place the chicken into the pan. Cook for 3-5min.(depending on how thick chicken is).
Mix fig butter, Dijon Mustard and Champagne vinaigrette in a small bowl.
Flip chicken with tongs and coat top with Fig mix. Put chicken in preheated oven using your oven-safe frying pan. Cook for 15 min.
Take chicken out, test to make sure it's thoroughly cooked and Serve.
