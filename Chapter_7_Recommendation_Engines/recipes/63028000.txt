Title:
Holiday Eggnog Deluxe - Deb's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs, separated
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 teaspooon vanilla
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
3/4 cup crown royal
1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons rum
3 cups whipping cream
2 cups milk
1/4 cup sugar
ground nutmeg

Directions:
Beat egg yolks until thick and lemon colored; gradually add 3/4 cup sugar, vanilla, and 1/4 teaspoo nutmeg, beating well at medium speed of an electric mixer.  Slowly stire in brandy and rum; cover and chill 8 hours.
Place chilled mixture in a punch bowl; gradually stir in cream and milk. Beat egg whites (at room temperature) in a large mixing bowl until soft peaks form.  Gradually add 1/4 cup sugar, and beat until stiff.  Fold whites into chilled mixture. Sprinkle with nutmeg.  Yield: about 3 quarts.
