Title:
Alpine Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4C cooked, deboned and chunked chicken
1/2# Swiss cheese or 6oz slivered
1 onion, diced
2C diced celery or less (to your liking)
2C toasted bread cubes
1C mayonaise or salad dressing
1/2-3/4C milk
1t salt
chopped almonds

Directions:
Mix all ingred. Bake in 2qt. casserole dish that has been sprayed with cooking spray for 30-40 min. at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Can substitute turkey for the chicken. Simple ingredients that are thrown together in casserole dish, baked and enjoyed by even the pickiest eater.
