Title:
Detox Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4547704.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Granny Smith apple
1 1/2 cups fresh spinach
1 cup blueberries
1/2 cup soy milk
4 ice cubes (optional)

Directions:
Combine Granny Smith apple, spinach, blueberries, soy milk, and ice cubes in a blender; blend until smooth.
