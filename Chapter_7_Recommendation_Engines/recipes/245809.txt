Title:
Strawberry Colada Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3288325.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cold coconut water
1 cup diced peaches
2 kiwis, peeled and sliced
1/2 banana
5 strawberries
1 tablespoon honey
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 teaspoon wheat germ

Directions:
Blend coconut water, peaches, kiwis, banana, strawberries, honey, ground ginger, and wheat germ together in a blender on medium speed until smooth, 30 seconds to 1 minute.

Submitter: 
Dr. Maiysha Claiborne
Description: 
Loaded with vitamin C and potassium, this smoothie is great for a quick immune boosting breakfast on the go.
