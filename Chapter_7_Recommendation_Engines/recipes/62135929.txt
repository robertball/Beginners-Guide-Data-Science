Title:
EASY Portobello Blue Cheese Rigatoni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups of Rigatoni or Penne
1/2 cup of olive oil
1/2 cup of Blue Cheese crumbles
1 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 tablespoon of dried parsley
1 teaspoon of garlic salt
2 portobello mushroom, sliced
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Cook pasta as directed
While pasta is cooking saute garlic in olive oil, approx 40 seconds. Add sliced portobello mushrooms, red pepper flakes, and garlic salt.
Saute on medium heat until mushrooms are light brown in color. Add parsley and salt and pepper.
Mix with pasta and add blue cheese crumbles.
