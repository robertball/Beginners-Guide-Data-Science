Title:
Moroccan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tablesoons Olive Oil
8 Chicken Legs
1 Large Onion, halved and thinly sliced
2 teaspoons Turmeric
2 teaspoons Ground Ginger
2 teaspoons Cinnamon
1/12 Cups Raisins

Directions:
In a large pot, heat oil over medium-high. Place chicken legs in pot; brown on all sides, about 10 minutes total. Transfer chicken to plate.
Add onion and 1/4 cup water to pot; stirring to loosen browned bits. Add turmeric, ginger, and cinnamon; cook, stiring occasionally, untill onion has softened, about 5 minutes. Return chicken to pot. Add 2 cups water and raisins, bring to boil. Reduce heat; partially cover, and simmer 45 minutes.
Transfer chicken to plater, cover with aluminum foil to keep warm. Boil sauce on high 10 minuts more to reduce, to about 1/2. Top chicken with sauce and serve.

Submitter: 
Debbie Lopez
Description: 
This chicken has a nice light sauce that is a good balance of sweet and mild spice. that goes great over couscous.
