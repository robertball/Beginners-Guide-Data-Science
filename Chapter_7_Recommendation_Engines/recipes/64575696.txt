Title:
Mediterranean Fish in Parchment Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3598434.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon olive oil
3-4 cloves garlic, smashed
? teaspoon red pepper flakes, crushed
1 cup grape tomatoes, halved
2 Tablespoons capers, drained
1 c mixed olives
handful of parsley and fresh thyme
salt and pepper
2 large filets of fish (used cod, but could use halibut or other wild fish)
1 T butter
2T olive oil
2T white wine
arugula or spinach

Directions:
preheat oven to 425
Season fish with salt and pepper (use olive oil also if concerned about being dry)
Heat 1 T olive oil, add garlic, red pepper flakes and saute until fragrant and softened
Stir in tomatoes, capers, olives, herbs and saute briefly.
Add wine and olive oil, remove from heat
Place handful of arugula (or spinach) in the center of large piece of parchment (can bake individually or all together)
Put fish on top of arugula
Add tomato/olive/caper/herbs mixture
cut up butter and place on top
Fold parchment to create a packet
Place on baking sheet and bake for about 8-10 minutes
Transfer each packet to a plate and use caution when opening â€“ the steam will be very hot!
Serve over fresh arugula or spinach with green onion or other salad toppings
serve with aioli
