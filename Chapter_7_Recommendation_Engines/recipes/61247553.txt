Title:
Spinach Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (9 inch)  pie crusts
2 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
2 eggs beaten
small diced onion, browned
8 ounces ricotta cheese
8 ounces mozzarella cheese
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 cups diced Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Press  pie crusts into 2 glass pie dishes.
In a large bowl, combine the spinach, salt, pepper,eggs, onion, ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan, and Cheddar cheeses. Mix together well and pour into bottom pie crust.  Sprinkle with parmesan on top.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30-35 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes and cut into wedges.
