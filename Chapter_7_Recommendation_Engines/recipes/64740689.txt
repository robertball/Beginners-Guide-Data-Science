Title:
Crockpot Swedish Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounces) can cream of mushroom soup
2 cups beef broth
1 cup fresh sliced mushrooms
1/2 cup onion (diced)
1 teaspoon garlic powder
2 Tablespoons A1 Steak Sauce
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon allspice
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 (28 ounces) bag frozen homestyle meatballs
1 cup sour cream

Directions:
Whisk together cream of mushroom soup and beef broth in your slow cooker.
Add mushrooms, onion, steak sauce, spices, and mix until combined.
Add meatballs and stir in until meatballs are coated in sauce.
Cover and cook on high for 4-6 hours or low for 8-10 hours.
Mix in sour cream the last 30 minutes of cooking.
