Title:
Beef and Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 rounded cup macaroni
1 clove garlic minced
1/2 cup onion chopped
1/4 green bell pepper, chopped
2-8 oz cans tomato sauce
2 tbs Worchestershire sauce
salt, pepper, sugar
basil, oregano, tobasco sauce
parmesan cheese
2 cups water

Directions:
brown meat and veggies in large pot.  add remaining ingredients, cover, and cook 20 mins
