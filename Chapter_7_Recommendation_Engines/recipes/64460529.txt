Title:
Fresh cherry muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2418658.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
3/4 c sugar plus 1 T
2 t baking powder
1/2 t salt
1/3 c oil
1/3 c milk
1 egg
1/2 t almond extract
1 c fresh pitted and quartered sweet cherries

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
Line muffin pan with papers or spray with non stick spray.
Mix flour, baking powder, salt and sugar in a medium sized bowl.  In a small bowl, mix together oil, egg, milk and extract.
Add wet mixture to dry and stir gently until incorporated. Fold in the cherries.
Fill muffin cups 2/3 way full. You will have between 10-12 muffins. Sprinkle muffins with reserved sugar and bake for 20 minutes.
