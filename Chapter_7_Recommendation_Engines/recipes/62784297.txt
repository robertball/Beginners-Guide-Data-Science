Title:
Thai Curry Mussels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs mussels
2 tsp  olive oil
1 garlic clove  minced
3 shallots finely sliced
1/4 cup of white wine
1 stalk of lemon grass bulb smashed and chopped in half
1/2 thai chili finely sliced
1 tsp yellow curry powder or paste
1 leek white part only julienned
5-6 roma tomatoes - seeded
1/2 red bell pepper julienned
1/2 yellow pepper julienned
1 carrot julienned
1/4 cup coriander roughly chopped
1/4 cup green onions finely sliced
1/4 cup picked and torn thai(or regular) basil
1/2 lime
1 tbsp honey
1 can coconut milk

Directions:
Add the oil, garlic and shallots to a wide round pot on med heat sweat until they are soft   then add the curry, lemon grass and half the coriander and thai chili, toast out for about 10 sec.
Add the wine and mussels to the pot and cover with a lid steam for about 4 minutes until the mussels open.
Strain the mussels in a colander and bowl reserving the mussel liquid and lemon grass then cover the mussels in the bowl with cling film to keep warm.
Using the same pot add the coconut milk, mussel liquid and honey and reduce by half
Add the tomato and all the julienned garnish (the peppers, carrot,leek etc the rest of the herbs and green onion)
Add back in the mussels and toss around squeeze in the lime and serve

Description: 
Serve with crusty bread to soak up the yummy sauce. Thsi recipe will serve 6 people as an appetizer or 3 as a meal Enjoy :D
