Title:
Miso Oatmeal Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2353901.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1/2 cup rolled oats
1 teaspoon miso paste, or to taste
1/4 cup fresh spinach, or to taste
10 walnut halves
2 tablespoons chopped roasted seaweed, or to taste (optional)
1/2 avocado - peeled, pitted, and cubed

Directions:
Bring water to a boil in a saucepan; add oats and miso paste. Cook for about 1 minute. Add spinach, walnuts, and seaweed to oat mixture; cook, stirring occasionally, until oatmeal is desired consistency, about 5 minutes.
Pour oatmeal into a bowl and top with avocado.

Submitter: 
Nancy Williamson Farrell
Description: 
A Japanese visitor said miso soup is a classic 
breakfast soup and I thought why not add oatmeal instead of tofu. Now it's a must each morning for me and much more satisfying than a green smoothie or sweet oatmeal!
