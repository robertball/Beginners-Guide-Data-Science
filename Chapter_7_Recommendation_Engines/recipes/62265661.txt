Title:
No Bake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C Sugar
5 Tbsp Cocoa
1/2 C Margarine
1/2 C Milk
3 C Quick cooking oats
1/2 C Peanut butter
1 tsp Vanilla
1 pinch salt

Directions:
In a saucepan bring sugar, cocoa, margarine, milk, to a rolling boil, boil for 1 minute. Remove from heat. Add oats, peanut butter, vanilla, and salt.Mix well. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper, and let cool.
