Title:
Big Mama's Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4459635.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme

Directions:
Blend marshmallow creme and cream cheese until smooth. Cover and refrigerate until chilled, about 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Brittney Tun
Description: 
This sweet and simple dip is fantastic with just about any fruit.
