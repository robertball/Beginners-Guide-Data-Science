Title:
Kahlua® Espresso Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1060421.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 fluid ounces Kahlua
1 fluid ounce Absolut® Vodka
1 fluid ounce fresh brewed espresso, cooled

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Add Kahlua, vodka, and espresso. Shake vigorously, and strain into a chilled martini glass.

Submitter: 
Kahlua®
Description: 
Kahlua and espresso bring a sophisticated mocha note to vodka martinis.
