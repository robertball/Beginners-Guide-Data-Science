Title:
Tarragon Chichen Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/719550.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast,boneless,skinless
2 Tbsp.olive oil
2 Tbsp.butter
6 shallots chopped
2 pared carrots,sliced 1/4 in rounds
1/4 cup brandy
1 cup white wine
1/4 cup chopped fresh tarragon or
2 tsp.dried tarragon leaves
1 1/2 Tbsp.chopped fresh chervil or
1/2 tsp.dried chervil leaves
1 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp.pepper
1 cup light cream
1 egg yolk
1 Tbsp.flour
1/4 lb.fresh mushrooms,thinly sliced
2 Tbsp.butter

Directions:
In 6-quart dutch oven,heat oil and 2 Tbsp.butter.
Add chicken breast 2 at a time,saute turn on other side.Remove chicken as it browns.
To the drippings in dutch oven add shallots and sliced carrot,saute 5 min. untill golden.
Return chicken to dutch oven,heat well.
When hot slightly heat brandy in ladle and ignite.
Add wine,tarragon,chevil,salt and pepper.
Bring to boil,reduce heat and simmer gently,covered
for 30 mins.
Remove chicken,keep warm.
Strain drippings,then return to dutch oven.
In small bowl, combine cream,egg yolk and flour,mix well.Stir into drippings in dutch oven.Bring just to a boil,stirring as it thickens.Add more wine if it's to thick.
Saute mushrooms in hot butter for 5 min.
Spoon sauce over chicken,garnish with mushrooms.
Serve with a good pasta.
