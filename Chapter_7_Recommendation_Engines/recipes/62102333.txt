Title:
Simple Homemade Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 heaping T. cocoa powder
8 heaping T. white sugar
16 oz. milk, divided

Directions:
Mix cocoa and sugar together in a med. saucepan.  Add 1/2 the milk, and whisk thoroughly until all the powder is absorbed, then add remaining milk.
Heat over med/low heat, whisking about every 1 min, scraping the bottom and corners well, until tiny bubbles just break the surface.
Serve in heavy mugs with mini-marshmallows or whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Carrie Causey
Description: 
Have you seen the movie Chocolat? It was Vianne's hot chocolate that inspired this thick creamy drink. Instant hot chocolate won't even taste like chocolate anymore! And it's so simple!!
