Title:
Big Baby Bubbas Back Bacon Barbeque Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/484352.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound thick hickory smoked bacon bacon, chopped
2 medium onions, chopped
4 15 oz cans Bushs baked beans (drained) (15 ounce)
2 cans bush butter beans (drained)
2 cans bushes black beans (drained)
1 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup ketchup
1/4 cup prepared mustard
1/2 cup hickory flavored barbaque sauce

Directions:
Chop up bacon into bits and cook in a skillet over medium heat until browned.
Add the onion and saute until tender.
Drain oil
Stir in the beans, brown sugar, ketchup, mustard and barbeque sauce
Cook on medium, stirring occasionally, until bubbly.

Submitter: 
Tim
Description: 
A big hit for me this summer!
This is a crowd sized for picnics etc.
All ingredients from Aldis is economical.
Try also in your crock pot or dutch oven.
The black and butter beans provide a nice color differential and there is lots of bacon and onions for huge flavor.Modified from: Simple Baked Beans II
