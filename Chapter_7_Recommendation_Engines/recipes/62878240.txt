Title:
Whipped cream mousse frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/692994.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups Cool whip
1 small box instant vanilla or white chocolate pudding 3.4 ounce
1 cup heavy whipping cream
1/4 cup milk
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
In a large bowl add the heavy cream, milk, vanilla and pudding. Whip until peaks start to form. Fold in the Cool Whip. Refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Angela Welch
Description: 
A friend of mine requested a light whipped cream mousse like frosting for her Mothers Day cake. This is what I came up with. It does require refrigeration.
