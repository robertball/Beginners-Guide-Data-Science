Title:
Corn Fritters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 egg yolks
1 2/3 cups cooked whole-kernel corn
½  teaspoon salt, pepper
Dash ground red pepper
¼ cup sifted all-purpose flour
3 egg whites, stiffly beaten
6 tablespoons quality frying oil

Directions:
•	Beat eggs yolks well.
•	Add corn, salt, pepper, flour.
•	Fold in egg whites.
•	Drop by tablespoons into hot oil in skillet.
•	Cook on both sides until brown and done.
•	Serve as a side dish or a main dish with   maple syrup and butter

Submitter: 
Anna Silvani-Morrison
Description: 
Excellent with fresh corn when it's in season!
