Title:
Avocado and tomato salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Cups of Avocado. Medium diced
2 Cups of Grape or Cherry Tomatoes
2 Cups of Cucumber, Medium diced
1 Cup of chopped Red Onion
2 Tablespoons of fresh Lime Juice
1/4 Cup of Olive Oil
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
chop, combine, enjoy.   you can substitute fat free Italian dressing for the oil.

Submitter: 
Shelldesc
Description: 
Quick easy salad or side.  Substitute different color tomatoes for interest.   Also good with a couple of dashes of Tobasco.
