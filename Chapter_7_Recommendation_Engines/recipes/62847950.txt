Title:
Warm rice ,artichoke, spinach and chicken salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/678925.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil
1 cup diced onion
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 cup pine nuts
1 tsp dry Knorr vegetable stock mix
1/2 tsp turmeric
Zest of 1 lemon
2 cups uncooked rice
4 cups water
1 bunch green onions
1/4 cup torn basil leaves
10 small mushrooms (brown or white), thinly-sliced
1 large bunch spinach. rough chop
1 cup marinated artichoke hearts, rough chop
1 cup shredded Parmesan (not grated!)
2 cups diced cooked chicken breast
Black pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Heat oil in pot of medium-sized rice cooker until hot.
2. Add onion and garlic and cook until the onion begins to soften.
3. Add pine nuts and cook until they are slightly brown.
4. Stir in dry stock mix, tumeric and lemon zest and allow to bloom for 1 minute.
5. Add rice and cook for 2-3 minutes.
6. Add water and reset rice cooker for a regular white rice cycle.
7. When rice cooker is finished cooking, fluff the rice then place the green onions and basil on top of rice (do not stir in yet) and let steam for 5 minutes.
8. Stir greens and rice together and add mushrooms to the mix.  Place spinach on top of mixture and steam for another 5 minutes.
9. Stir mixture together while adding the artichoke hearts, cheese and chicken.  Add pepper to taste and serve.

Description:
