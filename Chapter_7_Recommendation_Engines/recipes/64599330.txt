Title:
Cabbage Roll Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meat
1 lb Ground beef, lean
1/2 lb Ground pork, lean
Produce
1 Bay leaf
3 cloves Garlic
1 Head cabbage, medium
1 Onion, large
1 tsp Thyme
1 (28 ounce) can Tomatoes
Canned Goods
4 cups Beef broth
2 tbsp Tomato paste
Condiments
1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
Pasta & Grains
3/4 cup Rice, long grain
Baking & Spices
1 tsp Paprika
1 Salt and pepper
Drinks
1 1/2 cups V8 or other vegetable juice

Directions:
fry the meat onion and garlic ...drain fat add rest of ingredients...when  cabbage  is cooked...add the rice...cook till rice done

Gina Lee Lafreniere
