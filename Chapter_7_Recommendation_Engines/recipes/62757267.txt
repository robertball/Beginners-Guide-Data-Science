Title:
Caramel Toffee Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz) pkg. cream cheese, softened
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. vanilla (you can add more to taste)
Toffee bits (chocolate or regular)

Directions:
Combine cream cheese, brown sugar, and vanilla. Add desired amount of toffee bits.

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
Tart Granny Smith apples are perfect for dipping into this creamy caramel dip.
