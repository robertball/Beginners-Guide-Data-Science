Title:
Pot Luck Layered Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large head iceberg lettuce - rinsed, dried, and chopped
1 red onion, sliced
1/2 cup water chestnuts, sliced
1/2 cup bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 small can sliced black olives
1 bundle cherry or grape tomatoes
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cucumber, sliced
1 1/4 cups mayonnaise
1 pkg dry ranch mix
Dusting of grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Mix ranch mix and mayonnaise and set aside.
Layer in the following order: (this will make two glad oven disposable dishes)
Lettuce
Cucumber
Chestnuts
Mayo mixture (smooth on like icing a cake)
cheese
bacon
onions
black olives
tomatoes
Then dust with parmesan. Cover and let sit overnight for flavors to blend or serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Katy Blaylock
Description: 
My MIL makes something like this, but I dont like the usual suspects that go in a layered salad, so I took out the peas, changed the dressing and Voila! A masterpiece was born!
