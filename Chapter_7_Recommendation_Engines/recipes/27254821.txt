Title:
Crock Pot Fruit Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint of applesauce
1 lg can of slice peaches
1 lg can of slice pears
1 lg can of pineapple chunks
1 lg can of mandarin oranges
1 lg can of tropical fruit cocktail
1 lg can of cherry pie filling
1/3 cup of brown sugar
1 tsp of cinnamon
1/2 cup of walnut halves

Directions:
2-3 hours before serving
layer in a crock pot and heat:
applesauce
peaches(drained)
pears(drained)
pineapple(drained)
fruit cocktail(drained)
cherry pie filling
Mix the brown sugar and cinnamon and sprinkle on top of fruit mixture.
Cover with the walnut halves.

Submitter: 
Cindy Yonkman
Description: 
This is a really different side dish to serve at a brunch- or perhaps with a ham dinner. Everyone will love it! It does make up a large batch. I've also taken it to a pot luck and have always gotten great reviews!
