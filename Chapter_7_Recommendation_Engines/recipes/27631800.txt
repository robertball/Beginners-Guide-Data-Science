Title:
Saliba's Buckeyes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. butter
2 tsp vanilla
1-1/4 cup confectionary sugar
1 cup peanut butter
semi sweet chocolate chips
bar of parafin wax

Directions:
Mix butter, vanilla, sugar and peanut butter. Roll into balls.
Chill or freeze balls.
Melt chocolate and 1/2 block of parafin wax. Insert tooth pick into peanut butter ball. Dip in chocolate and place on wax paper cookie sheet to harden. Refrigerate to keep structure.
