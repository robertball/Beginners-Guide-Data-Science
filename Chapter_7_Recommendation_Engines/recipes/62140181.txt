Title:
Bacon Cheddar White Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans sliced white potatoes
1/8 c. butter
1/2 c. bacon bits
1 c. shredded cheese
1/2 c. sour cream
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Drain potatoes and put in sauce pan with butter.
Add sour cream. Heat until sour cream is creamed.
Add cheese. Heat til completely melted.
Add Bacon bits.. remove from heat and let sit for 3 mins for sauce to thicken.

Submitter: 
Katy Blaylock
Description: 
I had totally forgotten to get some baking potatoes for our steak dinner. I got everything else but those! So I threw this together and it was nearly equal to a baked potato! My daughter, Shelby, loves it!
