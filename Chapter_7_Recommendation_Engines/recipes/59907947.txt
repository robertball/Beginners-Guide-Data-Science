Title:
Budget Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/271111.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 cup Ranch dressing
3/4 cup pepper sauce (such as Frank's Red HotÂ®)
1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
Beat the softened cream cheese until light and fluffy.  Add the ranch dressing, mix until combined.  Add hot sauce and cheddar cheese.  Mix until well combined. Pour into heat proof dish and bake for 15 minutes or until hot and bubbly.

Submitter: 
CookingOnABudget
Description: 
I removed the cans of chicken meat from this recipe,  and baked the dip until bubbly and hot.  Serve this with chips, crackers, or celery and you'll never notice that the chicken is missing from this recipe!Modified from: Buffalo Chicken Dip
