Title:
Zippy Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 ripe avocados, medium size
2 Roma tomatoes, ripe
1 small white onion
1/2 cup sour cream
1/8-1/4 cup fresh lime juice
1/2 tsp. cumin
1 tsp. coarse sea salt
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2-1 tsp. chili powder
10 drops Tabasco

Directions:
Peel and pit avocadoes.
Chop or mix in food processor until mixture holds together, but with small chunks remaining.
Core tomatoes, and chop into small dice.
Peel and chop onion .
Add onion, tomato and sour cream to avocado mash.
Mix by hand. Do not process.
Add remaining ingredients, using spices and lime juice to taste.
Chill before serving to allow flavors to mix.

Submitter: 
Mrs. L
Description: 
A spicy, chunky guacamole. Great with tortilla chips, or as a topping for many Mexican dishes.
