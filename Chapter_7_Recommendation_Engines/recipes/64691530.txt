Title:
curry chicken with rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup basmati rice
3 teaspoons canola oil
6 skinless chicken thighs, trimmed
2 cups finely chopped onion
2 tablespoons garlic, minced
2 tablespoons minced fresh ginger
2 tablespoons curry powder,
1 tablespoon ground coriander
½ teaspoon ground pepper (cayenne),
2 cups reduced-sodium chicken broth
1 1/2 cups  chickpeas,
1 /2 jar of salsa
2 cups frozen peas
Salt & freshly ground pepper to taste
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro,

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400°F.
Soak rice in cold water for 20 minutes then heat for 20 minutes to soften. Drain.
heat 1 teaspoon of the oil in large wok over medium-high heat.
Add chicken and cook until browned on all sides, 6 to 8 minutes. Transfer the chicken to a plate. Reduce the heat to low, add the remaining 2 teaspoons oil and heat until hot. Add onions and cook, stirring, until light golden, 10 to 15 minutes.
Stir in garlic, ginger, curry powder, coriander and ground red pepper; cook, stirring constantly, until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
Increase the heat to medium-high; stir in broth, chickpeas, salsa and the soaked and drained rice; bring to a simmer.
separate meat from bone on the browned chicken pieces  and mix with the rice.
Place in baking dish and cover and bake for 20 to 25 minutes, until the rice is tender and most of the liquid has been absorbed.
sprinkle with cilantro
