Title:
Jill's Heart Healthy Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5125389.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 Onions, chopped
4 cloves Garlic, minced
1 pound Ground Beef
3/4 pound Beef Chuck, cubed
2 (14.5 ounce) can diced Tomatoes with juice
2 (6 ounce) cans Tomato Paste
1 cup strong brewed coffee
1/2 can or bottle dark beer (drink the rest)
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder  (Dutch)
1 tablespoon cumin powder
3 tablespoons chili powder
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon ground coriander
1 teaspoon salt
1 (15 ounce) can kidney or small red beans
1 (15 ounce) can small white beans
1 (15 ounce) can small black beans
Toppings: Green Onions, Grated Cheese, Sour Cream

Directions:
Heat oil in a large Dutch oven over medium heat.
Sautee 1 of the chopped onions, 1/2 the Minced Garlic, Ground Beef and cubed Chuck in oil for 10 minutes, or until the meat is well browned and the onions are tender.
Season with Brown Sugar, Chili Powder, Cumin, Cocoa Powder, Oregano, Cayenne Pepper, Coriander and Salt.
Mix in the 2 cans of diced Tomatoes with juice, Dark Beer, Coffee, Tomato Paste .
Stir in 3 cans of the drained Beans.
Reduce heat to low, and add the other chopped Onion and rest of the minced Garlic.
Simmer for 2 hours.
If possible, let cool or freeze to allow flavors to penetrate.
Serve with toppings. (Green Onions, Grated Cheese, Sour Cream)

Submitter: 
Jill Sander
Description: 
This is my go-to chili recipe. The beer, coffee, chocolate and brown sugar bring out a great flavor!
