Title:
Mama's Creamy Oyster Artichoke Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lbs. frozen artichoke hearts, thawed
3 cloves garlic, chopped
4 cups chicken stock
1 cup scallions, trimmed and chopped
1 tsp. salt (optional), or to taste
1/2 tsp. hot pepper sauce
1 tsp. fresh thyme
1/2 cup unsalted butter
1/2 cup plus 1 Tbsp. all purpose flour
4 cups heavy cream
4 cups shucked oysters
3 Tbsps. fresh parsley, chopped

Directions:
Combine first 7 ingredients in a heavy saucepan. Bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to medium low and simmer 12 minutes, stirring occasionally. Melt butter in a heavy skillet over medium heat. Add flour, stirring constantly. Cook 4-5 minutes or until mixture becomes light brown. Transfer to artichoke mixture and combine thoroughly. Stir in cream and cook 10 minutes, or until just heated through. Do not boil. Add oysters and simmer 5 minutes. Stir in parsley and serve with crusty bread.

Submitter: 
KIKI810
Description: 
My mom makes this stew every chilly Christmas Eve.  I love it so much I make it several times a year.  If you love oysters and artichokes you will love this creamy, warming stew.  Enjoy!
