Title:
Zuppa Arrabiata (Spicy Tomato Soup) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbs olive oil
1 onion chopped
3 cloves of garlic minced
2 lbs of assorted tomatoes chopped
1/2 cup white wine
1/2 cup water
1 tsp salt
3 oz. tomato paste
1 tsp oregano
1 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1/4 C fresh basil leaves
1/4 tsp black pepper
2 tsp white sugar
1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a large stock pot heat oil over medium heat and add onions.  Cook until soft; about 5 minutes and add garlic.
Cook for 2 minutes- do not let garlic brown.
To the pot add tomatoes, white wine, water, tomato paste, salt, basil, red pepper, sugar and black pepper. Cover and bring to a simmer over medium heat. Simmer for 45 minutes stirring occasionally.
Remove from heat and use an immersion belnder to make smooth or blend in batches in a blender.
Return to heat; add parmesan cheese heat an additonal 3 minutes and serve.
