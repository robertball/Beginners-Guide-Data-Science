Title:
Pretzel Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups chopped Rold Gold Pretzels
3 tablespoons + 1 cup confectioners' sugar
3/4 cups melted butter
1 (8 oz.) package cream cheese
1 egg
1 (9 oz.) tub cool whip
2 (3 oz.) packages strawberry jell-o*
2 cups boiling water
3 (10 oz.) package frozen strawberries or fresh strawberries**

Directions:
In a bowl combine together chopped pretzels, confectioners' sugar, and melted butter.  Mix well.  Take mixture and spread out evenly in a 9 x 13-inch baking pan.  Bake at 350 degrees F for 10 to 15 minutes.  Cool for 10 minutes.
In a bowl beat together cream cheese,egg and 1 cup confectioners' sugar.  Beat until soft.  Fold in cool whip.  Spread on crust and chill.
Dissolve strawberry jello in boiling water.  Add frozen strawberries and chill, until it starts to gel.
Pour over top of cream cheese layer. Refrigerate.
Top with more cool whip when serving. (optional)
Variation: *Can be substituted for cherry flavored gelatin.
**Can be substituted for 2 cans of cherry pie filling or fresh cherries.

Submitter: 
Darci
Description: 
A different way to have your strawberries, pretzels, jello and cool whip.  Its been in my family for years.
