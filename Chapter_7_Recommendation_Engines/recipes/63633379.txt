Title:
Dale's Coconut Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box White Cake Mix
1/4 cup oil
3 eggs
8 oz sour cream
8 1/2 oz Cream of Coconut
Icing
1 box Powdered Sugar
1 stick unsalted butter
2 tablespoons milk
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 bag shredded sweetened coconut

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Stir cake ingredients together only until blended.  Pour into prepared cake pans.
Bake until done.  Cool on wire racks.
Mix powdered sugar, butter, milk and vanilla until smooth.  Add a handful of coconut to icing mix and stir.
Ice the cake as desired.  Press loose coconut over the sides and top of cake.  Chill before serving.

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
This was the first recipe I used out of a hand-written cookbook that my wife's hairdresser, Robert, gave to us when we got married.  It has become so popular with my family that it is now referred to as Dale's Coconut Cake. Thanks Robert! It's our secret!
