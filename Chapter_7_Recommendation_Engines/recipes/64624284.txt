Title:
Queso Flameado Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz cooked chorizo (optional)
4 oz can green chiles
3 C grated Monterey Jack cheese
2 oz Tequila

Directions:
In an earthenware dish (or a cast-iron pan), layer the cooked chorizo, the drained chiles and the cheese. Pop into a hot oven until the cheese is just melted. Remove from oven, pour tequila over the top, use a lighter or match to set on fire. Let the flames subside, then serve with tortilla chips for dipping.
