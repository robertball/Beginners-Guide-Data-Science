Title:
Baked Mac and Cheese with Sour Cream and Cottage Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8oz blocks of mild cheddar cheese
1 8oz block sharp cheddar cheese
3/4 cup sour cream
3/4 cup cottage cheese
1 egg
salt to taste
pepper to taste
1 lb elbow macaroni noodles

Directions:
1. Shred all cheese
2. Cook macaroni noodles in well salted water until al dente per box directions
3. Drain pasta, do not cool
4. Reserve about 1/2 cup of cheese then mix all other ingredients in a large bowl until well combined, cheese will not melt completely. Mix egg with cheese and sour cream first to keep from cooking against hot pasta
5. Spray a corningware lg casserole dish or 9x13 pan with nonstick spray then put mixture in dish. Smooth out top and then sprinkle on reserved cheese.
6. Cook in preheated 350 degree oven for 40min or until cheese is all melted.
7. Let sit about 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Carolinagirl
Description: 
Rich and cheesy but not too dry.  My family loves this and we have it for all our big meals.
