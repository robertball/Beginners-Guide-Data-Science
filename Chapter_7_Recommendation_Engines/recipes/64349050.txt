Title:
Asparagus Tempeh Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground ginger, divided
Dash pepper
1 pkg tempeh, thinly sliced
2 tablespoons cornstarch
1 cup veggie broth
3 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon cider vinegar
1 1/2 pounds fresh asparagus, trimmed and cut into 2 inch pieces
1 (8 ounce) can sliced water chestnuts, drained
1/4 cup chopped green onions
4 cups hot cooked rice

Directions:
In a shallow glass container, combine sugar, salt, half of the ginger and pepper. Add tempeh and turn to coat. Let stand for 15 minutes. In a small bowl, combine the cornstarch, broth, soy sauce, vinegar and remaining ginger until blended; set aside.
In a large skillet or wok, brown tempeh in non stick skillet. Remove and keep warm. Add water chestnuts and onions; stir-fry for 1 minute. Stir cornstarch mixture; add to the skillet. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened. Return tempeh to the pan; heat through. Serve over rice.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
Soy sauce and ginger boost the flavor of this savory stir-fry that features strips of tempeh and water chestnuts along with tender asparagus.Modified from: Asparagus Beef Stir-Fry
