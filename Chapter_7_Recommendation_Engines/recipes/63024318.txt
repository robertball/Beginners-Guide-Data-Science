Title:
nanas Pineapple Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1 cup sugar
2. 2 tablespoons flour
3. 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
4. 2 large cans tidbit pineapple with juice
5. 1 sleeve Ritz crackers- crushed
6. 1 stick butter-melted

Directions:
oven 350*
mix 1. 2. 3. 4. mix well
pour into casserole or 9x12
put 5. on top
pour 6. over crackers
bake uncovered 25-30 min

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description:
