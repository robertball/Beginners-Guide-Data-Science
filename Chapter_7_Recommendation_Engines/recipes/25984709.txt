Title:
APPLE-STUFFING MINI MEATLOAVES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 (5.9-ounce) box Pork Helper® Pork Chops and Stuffing (such as Betty Crocker®)
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1 medium peeled and diced Granny Smith apple
1/4 cup finely chopped celery
1/2 cup 1 percent milk
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup hot water
Fresh parsley sprigs

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees. Coat a 10-by-15-inch pan (with sides) with cooking spray.
In large bowl, beat eggs. From box of Pork Helper, set Gravy Mix packet aside.
Add remaining contents of Seasoning and Stuffing packets, ground beef, apple, celery, milk and Worcestershire sauce to eggs; stir until thoroughly combined.
Shape mixture into 6 (3 by 4 inches) loaves; place in coated pan.
Bake 30 to 40 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 160 degrees.
Tent with foil and let stand 5 minutes.
Meanwhile, in a small bowl, mix Gravy Mix and hot water with wire whisk or fork until smooth.
Thin with water to desired consistency.
Place meat loaves on large platter; spoon gravy over loaves.
Garnish with parsley.

Submitter: 
Amy McElroy
Description: 
A good twist to meatloaf that even picky eaters will enjoy.
