Title:
Congo Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 3/4 cup flour
2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2/3 cup shortening, melted
2 1/2 cup brown sugar
3 eggs
1 cup pecans, chopped
6 ounces chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Sift together flour, baking powder and salt
Melt shortening and stir in sugar.  Allow to cool slightly
Beat in (one at a time) the eggs
Add flour mixture, nuts, and chocolate chips
Pour into greased pan (9X13)
Bake 25-30 minutes.  Allow to cool before cutting into squares.
