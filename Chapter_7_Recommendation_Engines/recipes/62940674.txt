Title:
Green Bean, Carrot and Mushroom Medley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound fresh green beans, cut into 1-inch lengths
2 carrots, cut into thick strips
1/4 cup butter
1 onion, sliced
1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 tsp rosemary
1 tsp thyme

Directions:
Place green beans and carrots in 1 inch of boiling water. Cover, and cook until tender but still firm. Drain.
Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute onions and mushrooms until almost tender. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 3 minutes.
Stir in green beans, carrots and remaining seasonings.
Cover, and cook for 5 minutes over medium heat.
