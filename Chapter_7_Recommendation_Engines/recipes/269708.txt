Title:
Maple-Bacon Snap Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons chopped bacon
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1 tablespoon minced shallot
1 teaspoon vegetable oil
2 cups sugar snap peas, strings removed
2 tablespoons maple syrup
1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat a large frying pan over high heat. Add bacon and saute until fat is rendered, 2 to 5 minutes. Stir in red pepper flakes; cook for 1 minute. Add shallot and saute for 1 minute. Pour in oil, heat through, and toss in snap peas. Cook, without stirring, until lightly blackened, 3 to 5 minutes. Drizzle in maple syrup, stir to coat, and saute for 2 minutes. Mix in lemon juice and season with salt and pepper.

Description: 
Sweet, savory, and super quick. Take out the bacon for an even quicker, vegetarian side!
