Title:
Green Goddess Pico de Gallo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/465388.jpg
Ingredients:
6 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
1/2 red onion, minced
3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
1/2 lime, juiced
2 clove garlic, minced
1 Tsp garlic powder
1 Tsp ground cumin
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Stir the tomatoes, onion, cilantro, jalapeno pepper, lime juice, garlic, garlic powder, cumin, salt, and pepper together in a bowl. Refrigerate at least 3 hours before serving.

Submitter:
