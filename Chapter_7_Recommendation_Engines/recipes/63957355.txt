Title:
Broccoli Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T Coconut oil
1 T butter
5-10 garlic cloves
1 onion
1 apple
2+ c broccoli
1.5 c peas
3 bay leaves
4 c chicken stock
Salt
Pepper
1 c (large handful) parsley

Directions:
melt butter and coconut oil
saute garlic, onion,
salt and pepper to taste
add broccoli, apple, peas
add stock
add handful parsley and bay leaves
simmer until vegetables are very soft
blend in blender to desired consistency
return to pot and add lemon/lemon zest, salt to taste
serve with parsley, goat cheese if desired

Submitter: 
seren
Description: 
easy pea/broccoli soup; use vegetable stock and no butter for vegan
