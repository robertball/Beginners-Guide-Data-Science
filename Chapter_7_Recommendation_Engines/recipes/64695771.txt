BEEF TIPS
1.Beef Sirloin

3.onions

7.curry
8.cumin
GRAVY
1.Beef bullion cube

Directions:
first cut up some onions and some other peppers if you want to. Then thaw out some beef sirloin. Get out a skillet, put some oil on it and let it heat up. Put the oven on med/high.When heated put the peppers in the skillet and let them cook.when peppers are done put into a bowl. Get meat out of of the package and put onto a cutting broad. Cut the fat off  and cut the meat into small pieces. Then put the meat into the skillet, add some onion powder, garlic, pepper and if you want curry, and cumin.  when meat is all the way cooked add peppers with the meat.
then get measuring cup and fill it up with 3 cups of water. Then add 1 beef bullion for each cup. So for this recipe add 3. Heat in the microwave until cubes are melted. Then stir and poor into the skillet withe the meat and peppers. Heat everything up until thick and bubbly. Then put into a bowl and serve!!!
