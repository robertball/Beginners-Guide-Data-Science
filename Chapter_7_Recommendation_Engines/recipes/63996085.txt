Title:
German Pancakes by Maria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 - 1/2 cup butter
6 eggs, lightly beaten
2 cups milk
2 tsp vanilla
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/3 cup sugar
2 tsp cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon salt
Dash of nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (175 degrees C). Melt butter in 9x13 baking dish.
In a medium bowl, mix remaining ingredients together.  Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish.
Bake on center rack in the preheated oven for approx. 20 minutes, until golden brown.
Powder with powdered sugar and serve.

Submitter: 
Maria Tedjamulia
Description: 
This quick recipe takes little time and yet tastes like you spent all morning slaving over the perfect breakfast.  The light, golden crepe is fabulous served with maple syrup or your favorite fruit preserves!  It's even great plain.  Enjoy!Modified from: German Pancakes II
