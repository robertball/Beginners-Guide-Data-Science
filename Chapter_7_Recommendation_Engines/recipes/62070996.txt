Title:
Strawberry Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 1/2 cups chocolate graham cracker crumbs
1/4 cup sugar
6 Tb melted butter or margarine
Pie:
8 oz Neufchatel cheese
3/4 cup sugar
1 tsp lime or lemon juice
8 oz Cool Whip, thawed
2 cups sliced strawberries (wash and blot them dry first!)
1 - 9" chocolate graham cracker crust, baked and cooled

Directions:
1) Crust: Combine choc. graham cracker crumbs and 1/4 cup sugar.  Stir in melted butter or margarine with a fork.  Press onto sides and bottom of glass 9" deep dish pie plate.  Bake at 350 degrees for 8 minutes.  Cool on rack.
2)Wash strawberries and BLOT DRY on paper towels.  Do NOT sugar them! (we want them crisp).  Slice to get 2 cups and set aside.
3) For Pie: Beat Neufchatel cheese and sugar.  Beat in lime (or lemon) juice.  Fold in thawed Cool Whip.  GENTLY fold in sliced strawberries (do NOT over-mix).  Spoon into cooled crust.  Chill until firm, about 4 hrs.

Submitter: 
STARFLOWER
Description: 
Yummy cream cheese pie with sliced fresh strawberries in a  chocolate graham-cracker crust that tastes like a chocolate cooky (I love this crust).  Easy to make and good for summer.  Try subbing other fruit for the strawberries, such as sliced banana, mango, or blueberries.
