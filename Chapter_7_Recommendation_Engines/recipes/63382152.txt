Title:
Mom's Green Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups ginger ale
3 cups Sprite (or 7up)
2 cups pineapple juice
4 cups lime sherbet

Directions:
Combine ginger ale, Sprite, and pineapple juice.  Add sherbet and stir to mix.
