Title:
BLT Egg Benedict Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4474272.jpg
Ingredients:
1 whole grain English muffin toasted and slightly buttered
2 poached eggs
1 slice of bacon cooked and cut in half
2 slices of tomato
sliced avocado to your liking (I LOVE avocado so I add a fair amount ;)
1 package of Knorrs Hollandaise sauce (this would make 6 Eggs Benedicts)

Directions:
Layer:
Toasted muffin
bacon
poached egg
tomatoe
avocado
Drizzle hollandaise sauce, pair with breakfast side salad
Can be paired with side salad:
1 cup of spring greens with a light balsamic vinegarette ( 2 tbsp GOOD olive oil, 4 tbsp balsamic vinegar, 1 tsp of a flavored balsamic vinegar, salt/pepper to taste)...lightly dress salad with this
