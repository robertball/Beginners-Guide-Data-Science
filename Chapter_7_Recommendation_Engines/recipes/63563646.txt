Title:
Best Ever Chuck Wagon Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pounds ground beef
1 teaspoon olive oil
1 large white onions, chopped
1 green bell peppers, seeded and chopped
1 (15 ounce) cans kidney beans, drained
1 (15 ounce) hot kidney beans
1 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce
1 large can of diced tomatoes
1 tablespoon chili powder
2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon garlic

Directions:
In a large pot, saute the onions, green pepper and until onions are translucent. Add the hsmburger until brown. Add the kidney beans and tomato sauce to the beef mixture, and season with chili powder, salt, garlic salt and hot pepper sauce. Bring to a simmer, and adjust seasonings to taste if necessary. Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
