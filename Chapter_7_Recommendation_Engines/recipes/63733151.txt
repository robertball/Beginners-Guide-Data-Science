Title:
cocktail meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
1/3 cup finely chopped onion
1/4 cup milk
1 tablespoon snipped parsley
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 egg
1/4 cup shortening
1 bottle (12 oz) chili sauce
1 jar (10 oz) grape jelly

Directions:
1. mix ground beef, bread crumbs, onion, milk, parsley, salt, worcestershire sauce, pepper and egg: shape into 1 inch balls
2. cook meatballs in shortening in 12 inch skillet until brown remove and drain fat
3. heat chili sauce and jelly in skillet, stir constantly until jelly is melted.
4. add meatballs and stir until coated. simmer uncovered for 30 minutes and serve hot
