Title:
Tiramisu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Zabaglione:
15 egg yolks
2 C sugar
1 2/3 C milk
3 1/4 C heavy cream (1)
2 tsp. vanilla extract
2/3 C strong brewed coffee
1/3 C rum
5 3oz. packages lady fingers
cocoa powder
In place of 2 1/2 lbs. mascarpone:
48 oz. cream cheese, room temperature
1 C sour cream
1 1/2 C heavy cream (2)

Directions:
In a sauce pan, whisk eggs and sugar until well blended.  Add milk and whisk together.  Cook on medium heat, stirring constantly until boiling gently for 1 minute.  Let cool in the refrigerator for about 1 hour.
In a medium bowl, beat cream (1), with vanilla until stiff peaks form.  Set aside.
Take cream cheese, sour cream, and heavy cream (2), and mix together until smooth.  Add zabaglione and mix until smooth.  Set aside.
Spray 4" hotel pan.  Place half of the ladyfingers on the bottom of the pan.  Mix coffee and rum together.  Drizzle half of the coffee/rum mixture evenly over the ladyfingers.  Add half of the cream cheese mixture and spread over the ladyfingers.
