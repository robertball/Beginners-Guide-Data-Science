Title:
Beef and Bok Choy stir-fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pre-made containers of cooked rice
2 t rice vinegar
2 T sugar
for the sauce
1/4 c low sodium beef broth
2 T low sodium soy sauce
1 T cornstarch
1 T chili garlic sauce
1 t brown sugar
1 t sesame oil (optional, pete doesn't like it
1/4 t beef base
for the stir fry
1 t oil
1 lb steak, seasoned with salt and pepper
3 t minced ginger - optional
2 t minced fresh garlic
8 cups of chopped fresh bok choy
2 c sliced white onions
sesame seeds
c sauce
1 t

Directions:
for the sauce
whisk together all the ingredients, set aside
for the stir fry
heat oil over high heat, sear steak about 3 minutes per side, transfer to plate, loosely tent with foil, let rest 3-4 minutes
add ginger and garlic to pan and stir-fry for 10-15 seconds, add bok choy and onions, until bok choy wilts, about 2 minutes.
heat in micro, pre-made rice
slice steak into bite size pieces and add it to the plan including all accumulated juices, and sauce mixture
stir fry until the sauce thickens, 1 minute
serve stir-fry with rice and garnish with sesame seeds
