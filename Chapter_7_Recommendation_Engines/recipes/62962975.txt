Title:
Protein Bar Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups oats
1/2 cup all natural p-nut butter
1/2 cup water
4 scoops protein powder (your choice) we use Beverly International

Directions:
Line an 8 x 8 pan with wax paper
Mix all ingredients, press into pan and freeze for 30 minutes then keep in fridge.

Description:
