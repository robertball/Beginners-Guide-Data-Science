Title:
Green Bean Casserole with a Kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. French-cut green beans, thawed and drained
10 3/4 oz. condensed cream of mushroom soup
8 oz. spicy jalapeno melting cheese
1 1/2 c. hot water
1/4 c. butter
6 oz. stuffing mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine beans and soup in 2-quart casserole dish.
Top with spicy cheese.
In medium bowl, add water to butter and stir until melted.
Stir in stuffing mix just until moistened.
Spoon over bean mixture.
Bake 35-40 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
US92
Description: 
From the US 92 Around the World cookbook, Tom Green shared this recipe that gives a little bit of spice to a green bean casserole.
