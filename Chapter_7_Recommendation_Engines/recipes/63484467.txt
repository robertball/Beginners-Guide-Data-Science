Title:
Beef Stroganoff Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon vegetable oil
1 large onion, chopped
8 ounces sliced mushrooms (fresh)
3 cloves garlic
1/4 cup dry white wine
1 can (10 3/4 oz) cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup sour cream
1 Tablespoon Dijon mustard
4 cups cooked egg noodles

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray 9x13 baking dish with cooking spray.
Place beef in a large skillet, sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Brown beef over med-high heat until no longer pink, stirring to separate beef.  Drain fat from skillet, set beef aside.
Heat oil in same skillet over med-high heat until hot. Add mushrooms, onion, garlic.  Cook and stir for about 2 minutes or until onion is tender.  Add wine.  Reduce heat to med-low; stir in soup, sour cream and mustard until well combined. Return beef to skillet.
Place noodles in prepared dish.  Pour beef mixture over noodles and stir until well coated.
Bake uncovered for 30 minutes or until heated through.  Garnish with radish and parsley sprigs if desired.
