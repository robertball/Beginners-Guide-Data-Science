Title:
Pulled Pork Ya'll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068339.jpg
Ingredients:
5 lb. Boneless Pork Shoulder
1 teaspoon- Seasoning Salt
1 teaspoon- Black Pepper
1 teaspoon- Cumin
1 teaspoon- Cayenne Pepper
1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon
1 teaspoon- Garlic Powder
1 teaspoon- Onion Powder
1/4 Cup Packed Brown Sugar
2 Tablespoons- Oil
1 1/2 Cup Water
1/2 Cup Cider Vinegar
18 oz Bottle of Your Favorite BBQ Sauce

Directions:
Pull out your slow cooker, set to low and add water and cider vinegar.
Pull out pork and trim if needed.
Rub the pork with oil and set aside 5 minutes.
Mix seasonings together and rub mixture into the pork.
Place pork into slow cooker and walk away for 6-8 hours.
When pork is done remove from slow cooker and let rest for 10 minutes.
(OPTIONAL) Reserve 1 1/2 Cups of cooking liquid and set aside.
Discard cooking liquid.
Place pork back in slow cooker and shred. (CHOICE)Either add the cooking liquid back into the pork to moisten or add 18 oz. of your favorite BBQ sauce.
