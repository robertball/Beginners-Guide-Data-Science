Title:
Mexican Black Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 garlic cloves minced
1/2 tsp roasted cumin
2 cans black beans, rinsed and drained
3/4 can chicken broth
1/2 onion, finely chopped

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a crock pot and cook on high for 3-4 hours.  When done, mash beans lightly with  a large spoon and serve over rice.

Submitter: 
ltlmsmfft
Description: 
These black beans are a great mild, family friendly version of a Mexican favorite.  They go great over rice or in a burrito.
