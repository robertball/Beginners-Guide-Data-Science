Title:
Crispy batter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Your meat/veggie/fish mix. If you want crab cake, use crab, egg, onions, shallots, etc.
1 cup flour
1 cup pangko mix
1 cup bread crumbs
1/4 salt
1/4 pepper
1 large egg

Directions:
1. Combine Pangko, salt, pepper and bread crumbs in one bowl, place flour in a separate container, beat egg and place in a separate bowl
2.  Ready your meat/fish patty.
3. Lightly coat with flour
4. Dip in egg
5. Coat with pangko mix. Make sure it is covered.
Fry it in a frying pan with half an inch oil about 3 mins on each side. You can also deep fry it for 5 mins.
Place patty cakes on a rack and let oil drip. Serve with any sauce. e.g. ketchup, mayo, etc.
