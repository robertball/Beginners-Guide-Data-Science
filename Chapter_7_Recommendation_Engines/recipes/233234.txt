Title:
Watermelon, Almond, Feta and Mint Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5504560.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Honey Roasted Wonderful Almond Accents
6 cups cubed seedless watermelon
8 ounces crumbled feta cheese
3 tablespoons finely chopped fresh mint
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
2 tablespoons lime juice
Kosher salt
Fresh ground black pepper

Directions:
Combine watermelon, feta, mint, lime juice and olive oil in a medium serving bowl. Stir gently to mix. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Top with Wonderful Almond Accents before serving.
