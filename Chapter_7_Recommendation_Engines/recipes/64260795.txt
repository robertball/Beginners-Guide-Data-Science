Title:
DILL-GLAZED CARROTS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
11/2	pounds carrots, peeled
2	cups water
1	tablespoon unsalted butter
1	tablespoon apple cider vinegar
1/4	teaspoon salt
1/4	teaspoon black pepper
2	tablespoons chopped fresh dill
1/2	teaspoon honey

Directions:
1 Cut carrots diagonally into 1/2-inch-wide pieces.
2 Combine carrots with water, butter, vinegar, salt, and pepper in a lidded skillet. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat; cover and simmer 10 minutes. Uncover and cook, stirring occasionally, until liquid evaporates and carrots are tender and glazed, about 20 minutes.
3 Remove from heat and stir in dill and honey.

Submitter: 
RuthE
Description: 
Fresh dill makes these carrots pop! Serve them with your favorite meatloaf or roast chicken
