Title:
Frito Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ppunds yellow summer squash
1 (10 ounce) can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 stick margerine
1 medium onion, diced
2 Tablespoons brown sugar
1 small package Fritos, slightly crushed
1 cup grated Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Saute squash, onions and sugar in non-stick pan until tender.  Add soup.  Add salt to taste; stir in margarine.  Place half the squash in 9 by 13 inch dish and cover with the Fritos.  Add remaining squash.  Spread cheese over all.  Bake at 400 degrees until cheese is melted.
