Title:
Date Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Batter:
1 cup flour
1 cup sugar
1 cup dates, chopped
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 cup milk
Sauce:
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup boiling water
1 tbsp. butter

Directions:
Mix batter ingredients.
Put into a greased 9" x 9" cake pan.
Mix sauce ingredients.
Pour sauce over batter.
Bake at 350 x 30 minutes.
Tastes best served warm, with either whipped cream, milk or ice cream.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Oven baked in a sauce, this date loaded dessert is so yummy!
