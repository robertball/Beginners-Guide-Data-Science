Title:
Chicken and Potato Surprise Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 lb. frozen seasoned cubed potatoes (such as hash browns, may be onion, ranch, o'brien style)
1 pkg. Stovetop Stuffing mix
1 12-oz. can cream-style corn
1 lb. (16 oz.) sour cream
1 12-oz. can cream of mushroom soup
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
2 cups Durkee French Fried onions

Directions:
Prepare Stovetop Stuffing according to directions, set aside.
Spray 9 x 13" pan with cooking spray.
Layer potatoes on bottom of pan.
Layer stuffing on top of potatoes
Lay chicken breasts on top of stuffing.
Mix together:
Cream-style corn, sour cream and cream of mushroom soup.
Blend well, then stir in shredded cheddar.
Pour over top of chicken breasts.
Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.  Top with Durkee Fried Onions and bake an additional 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
A meal-in-one with a surprise layer of stuffing!  Serve cranberries on the side!  The top layer of crunchy onions puts the flavor of this casserole over the top!
