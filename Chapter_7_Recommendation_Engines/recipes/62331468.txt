Title:
Best Cajun Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon rubbed dried sage leaves
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon onion powder

Directions:
Mix paprika, cumin, black pepper, cayenne pepper, sage, onion powder, and garlic salt.

Submitter: 
hnorton1112
Description: 
This is one of the best blackened/cajun seasonings I have come across!  It has amazing flavor and the perfect amount of spice.Modified from: Cajun Spiced Pork Chops
