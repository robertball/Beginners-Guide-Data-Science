Title:
Slow Cooker Smoked Oxtail and Sweet Potato Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5666600.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs smoked oxtail, cut into segments
14.5 oz can fire roasted diced tomatoes
1 cup beef broth
1 medium onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, chopped
2 medium sweet potatoes, chopped
1 large bell pepper, chopped
2 bay leaves
2 tsps chili powder
1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp ground allspice
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
2 tbsps brown sugar
Fresh parsley (optional)

Directions:
1. In a 6 quart slow cooker, place oxtails, onions, garlic, sweet potatoes, and bell pepper.  Pour tomatoes and beef broth over meat and veggies. Mix in bay leaves, chili powder, cinnamon, allspice, salt, black pepper, and brown sugar.
2. Cover and cook on high for 5-6 hours, or on low 6-8 hours.  Stirring occasionally.
3. Remove oxtails from slow cooker. Shred meat off of bones, and discard bones.
4. Skim fat off the top of the liquid in the slow cooker. Add meat back into slow cooker. Add in additional salt, black pepper, or brown sugar to taste. Stir to combine.
5. Serve warm, topped with fresh parsley if desired.

Submitter: 
Kim
Description: 
Combining smoked oxtail and sweet potatoes in the slow cooker makes for a delicious, and surprisingly easy meal. The sweet potatoes cook down to help thicken the stew, while the oxtail lends a ton of wonderful smokiness. It's great served with cornbread!
