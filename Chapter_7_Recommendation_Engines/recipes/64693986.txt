Title:
Homemade Pancake / Waffle Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups granulated sugar
2 cups brown sugar
2 cups water
32 ounces white Karo syrup
1 to 2 tsp. maple flavoring

Directions:
Combine granulated sugar, brown sugar, and water in a saucepan over medium heat.  Heat just until it comes to a boil.  Remove from heat.  Add Karo syrup.  Add maple flavoring to taste.  Store in refrigerator.

Submitter:

Description: 
My husband's family always makes their own syrup rather than buying a commercial product.
