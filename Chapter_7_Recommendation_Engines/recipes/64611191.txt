Title:
Orange Ginger Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. medium carrots
2 tablespoons sugar
2 teaspoons corn starch
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup orange juice
4 tablespoons butter
Chopped parsley

Directions:
Cut carrots on bias (thick) and cook until tender.
Combine sugar, corn starch, salt and ginger in small saucepan. Add orange juice and cook stirring constantly until mixture thickens and bubbles. Boil 1 minute and then stir in butter.
Pour over hot carrots and toss. Garnish with parsley.
