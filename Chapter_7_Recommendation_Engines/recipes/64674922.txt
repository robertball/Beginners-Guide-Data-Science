Title:
Budget Red Clam Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4621659.jpg
Ingredients:
10.5 ounce Can Red Clam Sauce
(2) 6.5 ounce Can Chopped Clams
14.5 ounce Can Stew Tomatoes
6 ounce Can Tomato Paste

Directions:
On the stove in a 1 quart pot add Red Clam Sauce, Chopped Clams with juice, and Stew Tomatoes with juice. Bring to medium heat and cook for about 10 minutes.
Stir tomatoe paste into sauce and cook another 10 minutes.
Serve over thin linguine pasta.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
This is a simple and cheap clam red sauce over thin linguine. I've been known to doctor it up with fresh herbs (Oregano and Basil), red wine (1/3 cup), and sugar (1 to 2 tablespoons).
