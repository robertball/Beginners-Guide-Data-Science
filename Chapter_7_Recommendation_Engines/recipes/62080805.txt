Title:
JUICIEST TURKEY EVER!!! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10-12 LB TURKEY
2 STICKS OF BUTTER
1 VIDALIA SWEET ONION, QUATERED
1/2 GREEN BELL PEPPER
8 BABY CARROTS
1 STALK OF CELERY, HALVED
SEASONED SALT(PREFERABLY CAJUN)
10 CUPS TURKEY OR CHICKEN BROTH
BRINE:
1 CUP SALT
1 CUP LIGHT BROWN SUGAR
2 LEMONS (QUATERED)
2 NAVEL ORANGES (QUATERED)
2 TABLESPOON POULTRY SEASONING
2 GALLONS WATER

Directions:
WASH TURKEY AND REMOVE GIBLETS. PLACE IN A  CLEAN PLASTIC GARBAGE BAG OR IN A LARGE STOCK POT. MIX THE SALT, SUGAR, POULTRY SEASONING, AND WATER AND POUR SOLUTION OVER THE BIRD. ADD FRUIT & LET IT SOAK 8-24 HOURS IN REFRIGERATOR.(IF IN BAG, PLACE IN A LARGE BOWL OR POT IN CASE OF LEAKS)
WHEN READY TO PREPARE, PREHEAT OVEN TO 350,DISCARD BRINE, EXCEPT 1/2 OF A LEMON AND 1/2 ORANGE. RINSE BIRD WELL AND PAT DRY. RUB ONE STICK OF SOFTEN BUTTER ALL OVER, UNDER AND AROUND THE BIRD. SEASON TO TASTE WITH THE SEASONING SALT. PLACE VEGGIES AND RESERVED ORANGE AND LEMON INSIDE BIRD CAVITY.PLACE BIRD BREAST SIDE DOWN IN ROASTER, POUR 2-3 CUPS OF BROTH OVER TURKEY AND ROAST COVERED FOR ABOUT 1 1/2 HOURS.(MELT REMAIING STICK OF BUTTER IN BROTH AND BASTE BIRD EVERY 30 MINUTES WITH AT LEAST 1/2 CUP OF STOCK)THEN REMOVE COVER AND TURN BIRD BREAST SIDE UP AND COOK UNTIL DONE.

Submitter: 
TIANA1T
Description: 
THIS TURKEY IS SOOOO MOIST, YOU WILL NEVER COOK IT ANY OTHER WAY AGAIN. DON'T LET THE BRINE SCARE YOU, EVEN THOUGH IT HAS A LOT OF SALT IN IT, YOUR BIRD WON'T BE SALTY. FOR AN EVEN MORE FLAVORFUL BIRD, USE AND INJECTOR AND INJECT THE BIRD IN VARIOUS SPOTS WITH BROTH. YUUUUUMMMY!!
