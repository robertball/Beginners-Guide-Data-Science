Title:
Chicken 'n' biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter (4 oz)
3 lbs of chicken in 1/2 inch cubes
1 onion, diced
2 stalks of celery, sliced
3 carrots, sliced
4 ounces of mushrooms, sliced (1/2 package)
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp ground pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp poultry seasoning
1 tsp dried parsley
2 tbsp flour
2 cups of chicken stock
15 oz of canned cut green beans
8 large canned biscuits

Directions:
Preheat oven according to biscuit directions
In a deep skillet, melt butter over medium high heat
Brown chicken in butter, and remove from skillet
Add onions, celery and carrots and saute for a few minutes
Add mushrooms and garlic and continue to saute for a few more minutes
Return browned chicken and add next 5 ingredients, mix thoroughly
Add stock, stir, and let simmer on medium low heat for 15-20 minutes
Place biscuits in oven
Add green beans, and simmer for an additional 5 minutes

Description: 
an easy chicken stew, served over fresh baked biscuits
