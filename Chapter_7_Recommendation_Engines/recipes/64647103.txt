Title:
Zucchini Noodles with White Wine Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 large zucchini, spiralized (set aside in a colander for 15 minutes)
1 large spiralized red onion
2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
2 tablespoons olive oil
4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
1/2 cup dry white wine
1 teaspoon sea salt
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
2 tablespoons unsalted butter
1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
1/2cup fresh parsley, chopped

Directions:
Heat large sauté pan to medium high heat. Add oil and let it get very hot.
Add onion and tomatoes to sauté  for 3-4 minutes. Add garlic and sauté another 30 seconds. Pour in white wine and stir using a wooden spoon.
Add zucchini tossing around using tongs. Cook for about 3 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
Add in butter and let melt then top with parmesan cheese and fresh parsley. Serve to enjoy this simple yet flavorful dish!

Submitter: 
Cindy Anschutz Barbieri
Description: 
This recipe reminds me of a simple dish my Nana and I would make together.  I wish she was still here so I could show her my zucchini recipes.   She and I loved zucchini together and this dish would be a big hit!
