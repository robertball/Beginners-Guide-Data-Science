Title:
Spicy Thai Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1077582.jpg
Ingredients:
20 wings
1/4 c dry sherry
1/4 c oyster sauce
1/4 c honey
3 t chopped fresh cilantro
2 t chili sauce
2 t grated lime peel
4 med. green onions, chopped
3 cloves garlic, finely chopped

Directions:
Cut each wing at joints to make 3 pieces; discard tip. Cut off & discard excess skin.
In large glass bowl, stir remaining ingredients until well mixed. add chicken to marinade. Seal bag; turn to coat. Refrigerate at least 1 hour, turning once.
Heat oven to 375. Place chicken in ungreased 15x10x1" baking pan. Bake uncovered 30 mins, stirring frequently. Bake about 20 mins longer or until juices are clear.
