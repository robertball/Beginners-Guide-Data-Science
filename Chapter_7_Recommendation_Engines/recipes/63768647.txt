Title:
french toast bake girl scouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup corn syrup
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup butter
6 eggs
2 cups milk
1 tsp vanilla
12 slices bread

Directions:
In saucepan over medium heat, bring corn syrup, brown sugar and butter to boil; boil 5 minutes. Pour into greased 9x13 pan.
Arrange bread over the syrup mixture.
Beat eggs, milk, vanilla together and pour over bread.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.
Uncover and bake at 350 degrees for 45 min.  Turn bread over in pan before serving.  At camp it took 60 min to cook. You need to cook until top is well browned
