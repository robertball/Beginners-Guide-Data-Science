Title:
Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lg cans sweet potatoes, drained
1 1/2 c. sugar
2 eggs
1 1/2 t. vanilla
1/2 c. milk
1/2 c. butter, melted
Topping
3/4 c. oats
3 T. flour
3/4 c. brown sugar
1 1/2 t. cinnamon
1/3 c. melted butter
1 c. crushed pecans
mini marshmallows

Directions:
Mix the potato mixture with a mixer.
Prep baking dish with cooking spray, then pour in.
Mix the topping ingredients by hand.  Sprinkle on top.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.
Sprinkle top with mini marshmallows, then bake 5 more minutes.
