Title:
Harvest Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 strips of bacon, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1 can cream-style corn
1 egg, beaten
Black pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat a medium skillet and add bacon pieces.  After they have rendered some fat, add the onion.  Saute until the onions are translucent.  Add the can of cream-style corn and heat until bubbly.  While stirring, add the beaten egg.  Cook until thick.

Submitter: 
cooks4forty
Description: 
I have been eating this since the 70's and my kids love it too.  These five ingredients make a fantastic side dish!
