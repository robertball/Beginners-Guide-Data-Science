Title:
Chili Glazed Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup honey
1 1/2 tbsp. bourbon
2 tbsp. chili powder
1/2 tsp. ground ginger
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. lime zest
1 tsp. lime juice
4 - 6oz. salmon filets

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except salmon in a small bowl.
Preheat broiler.
Place salmon filets on a greased cooking pan, skin side down.
Spoon honey mixture over salmon and broil until salmon is done to your liking.
