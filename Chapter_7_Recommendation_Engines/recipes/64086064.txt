Title:
Chicken Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1172935.jpg
Ingredients:
large flour tortillas (4 to 6 tortillas)
1 can refried beans
1 can Rotel tomatoes (mild, hot, or original)
1 envelope taco seasoning
3 baked chicken breasts, baked and shredded
bag of Mexican style shredded cheese
sour cream
shredded lettuce

Directions:
In a pan heat and stir together refried beans, tomatoes, taco seasoning.
Stir in shredded meat.
Spoon mixture into tortillas, wrap, and place in glass casserole dish, side by side.
Sprinkle bag of cheese on top.
Cover with foil and put in preheated oven at 325.
Bake for about 30 to 40 mins.
Serve with shredded lettuce and sour cream.
