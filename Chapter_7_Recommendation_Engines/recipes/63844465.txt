Title:
Patt Meyer's Lemonade Cookie Hearts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/4 c sifted flour
1 c butter
1 c sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1 tsp soda
4 Tbsp frozen lemonade concentrate
1 tsp vanilla or 1/2 tsp lemon extract

Directions:
Rub together flour, salt, sugar and butter.
Add beaten eggs and soda.  Stir in lemonade concentrate.
Add vaniilla, if desired.
Chill dough in refrigerator for at least  one hour in refrigerator, then roll out and bake 350 degrees for 8 - 10 min.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Valentine Cookie Hearts great with powdered sugar frosting.
