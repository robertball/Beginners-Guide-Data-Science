Title:
Easy Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/1082767.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) jar chunky-style blue cheese salad dressing (such as Marzetti(<sup>&reg;</sup>))
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
3  cooked chicken breasts, shredded
1 (12 ounce) bottle Buffalo wings sauce (such as Frank's(<sup>&reg;</sup>) <sup>&reg;</sup>edhot(<sup>&reg;</sup>))
1 (4 ounce) package blue cheese crumbles

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Stir blue cheese salad dressing and cream cheese together in a mixing bowl. Stir chicken, Buffalo wings sauce, and blue cheese crumbles into the salad dressing mixture; pour sauce into a baking dish.
Bake in preheated oven until bubbling, 15 to 20 minutes.

Description: 
Buffalo wings in a dip can be served with tortilla chips. Add celery if you like.
