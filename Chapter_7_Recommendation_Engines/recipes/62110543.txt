Title:
Italian  BBQ Roasted Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound asparagus(about one bunch)
2-3 T extra-virgin olive oil
1 pinch Kosher Salt
pepper to taste
about 1/4 cup each freshly grated Parmesan and Asiago cheeses

Directions:
Preheat grill to medium high (you can only hold your hand over it for a few seconds at that heat.)
snap end off asparagus spears and place in roasting pan(do not overlap)
Drizzle oil over asparagus and shake pan to evenly coat. Add salt and pepper to your liking
cook for 10-15 minutes depending on how hot your grill is, asparagus should still be firm but have a slight change in colour
Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over asparagus in an even layer, continue roasting asparagus until softened and brighter in colour. Serve

Submitter: 
Kimmi
Description: 
This recipe is a perfect side dish beside a steak and roasted potatoes coated with fresh herbs. Recipe is easy to double or triple to feed a crowd.
