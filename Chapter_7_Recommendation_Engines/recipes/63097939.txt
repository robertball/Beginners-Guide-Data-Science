Title:
Baron's Cocktail Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. cooked meatballs
1 cup grape jelly
1 12 oz bottle chili sauce
2 teaspoons Coleman's mustard powder

Directions:
Combine the jelly, chili sauce and mustard together in a smaller Slow Cooker or Dutch oven and cook over low heat till simmering and well combined.  Thaw and drain and add meatballs to the cooker or Dutch oven.  Cover and simmer on low for 2 hours on low for slow cooker or 1/2 hour for the dutch oven.  Serve hot with toothpicks.

Submitter: 
Baron
Description: 
This is an extremely easy appetizer that keeps them coming back for seconds.Modified from: Cocktail Meatballs IV
