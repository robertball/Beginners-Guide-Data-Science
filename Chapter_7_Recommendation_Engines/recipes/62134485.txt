Title:
Sweet And Sour Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast halves
1/3 cup maraschino cherries
1 can (8 ounce) crushed pineapple, with juice
1 small bell pepper, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1/3 cup honey
1 Tablespoon ground ginger

Directions:
Preheat your oven to 350.
In a 7x11 baking dish or a medium bowl, put chicken breast halves, fruit and vegetables together.  Mix them up well and pour honey on the top.  Sprinkle ginger on tope of the honey.
Bake for 1 hour.
Serve it with rice.

Submitter: 
Linda Grein
