Title:
Hotdog Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of uncooked white rice
3/4 cup of water
1/2 tablespoon butter
1/2 teaspoon of salt (optional)
1 hot dog

Directions:
Put water, butter, and salt in a small saucepan.
Bring water to a boil, making sure butter is melted.
Cut the hot dog into 1/4-1/2 inch slices.
Add rice and hot dog to the saucepan.
Cover and reduce heat to low. Cook for 20 minutes.
After 20 minutes, remove from heat and let stand for 5-10 minutes. Enjoy.

Submitter: 
Grace Yusckat
Description: 
A fairly quick, cheap, tasty snack: sliced hot dog in butter-flavored rice.
