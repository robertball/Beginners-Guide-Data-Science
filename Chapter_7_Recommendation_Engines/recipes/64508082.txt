Title:
Chelsea's Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
GLAZE:
2 cups orange juice
1/2 cup pineapple juice
6 3/4 ounces vanilla pudding mix
SALAD:
1 pound strawberries, sliced
4 whole bananas, sliced
1/2 pound red grapes, halved
1/2 pound green grapes, halved
1/2 pound fresh pineapple chunks
1 cup fresh blueberries

Directions:
Whisk together orange juice, pineapple juice and pudding mix.  Bring to a boil then let cool to thicken.
When glaze is cool, mix all ingredients together and toss to coat with glaze.
If making ahead, don't add bananas until ready to serve.
