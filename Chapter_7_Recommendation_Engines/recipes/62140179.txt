Title:
Guacamole By Shelby Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Haas Avocados
1/4 c. mayo
2 tbsp garlic powder
2 tbsp onion powder
3 tsp salt
2 tsp pepper
1 tbsp celery salt
Lime juice to taste

Directions:
Peel avocados and remove pit
Mash together all ingredients
Might need some more of "this or that" til its to your liking.
Serve immediately or when flavors blend well.

Submitter: 
Katy Blaylock
Description: 
My dad taught Shelby, my 9.5 yr old daughter, to make guacamole. She has tweeked it to mega perfection. If you dont like the cilantro or tomatoes and just like a simple guacamole, this is THE one!
