Title:
Crunchy onions BBQ chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C BBQ sauce
1/2 t pepper
1 1/3 C crushed French-fried Onions
1/4 C parmesan
1 1/2 lbs chicken breast

Directions:
Put bbq sauce in a shallow bowl. In another bowl mix pepper crushed french onions & paremsan.
Dip chicken in bbq sauce and then in onion mixture on one side. Place crumb side up in baking dish and bake at 400 for 20-25 min.
