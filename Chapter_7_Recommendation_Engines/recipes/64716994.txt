Title:
Easy Grilled Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5787899.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ears of corn, husks removed

Directions:
Wrap each ear of corn in a wet paper towel.
Wrap each one again in tin foil.
Place the corn on a preheated grill.
Grill for about 20 minutes, turning every 5 minutes or so.
Remove from the grill and enjoy your delicious grilled corn!
