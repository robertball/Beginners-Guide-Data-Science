Title:
Roasted Garlic with Peppercorn and Thyme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/933968.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large garlic bulb
3 twiggs of fresh thyme
Fresh ground Sea salt (to taste)
1/4 teaspoon fresh ground peppercorn
Olive oil

Directions:
Pre-heat oven at 400 f.
Cut off top of garlic bulb and place in a garlic roaster or small cast iron pan with lid. Coat with olive oil, add thyme, salt to taste and pepper.
Bake with lid on for 30 minutes and an additional 10 minutes without lid.
Cool and remove from skin. Use in required recipe.

Submitter: 
Danielle Girouard
Description: 
I wanted to make potates for dinner but wanted to serve something different so I thought of roasting garlic and adding them to the potatoes.
