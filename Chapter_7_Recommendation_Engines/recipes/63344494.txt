Title:
Low Fat/Low Sodium Alfredo Broccoli Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz Red onion - about 1/6th of a large onion
1 tablespoon Olive oil
1 clove of garlic
1 tablespoon All-purpose flour
1 cup Skim milk
1/2 cup Low sodium chicken broth (Pacific Natural foods)
1/4 tsp Freshly crushed black pepper
1 oz Shredded Parmesan cheese (Kraft)
1 lb Broccoli florets
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1/2 tsp ground mustard
1/4 tbsp garlic powder

Directions:
1. Microwave cook the potatoes. then do the same for the broccoli.
2. In a small pot, warm milk and chicken broth (stir).
3. Whisk Saute' chopped onion in skillet on medium heat with oil for 4 minutes.  One more minute with garlic. turn down heat low if browning quickly. should be thickened
4. Turn heat back to medium & pour milk/broth mixture into skillet slowly while stirring flour/onions. Keep stirring until thickened.
5. Whisk all listed spices...until blended well then add cheese and stir well.
Place potatoes in individual's bowls opened up wide like a baked potato.  Stir up potato starch slightly
6. Place broccoli on potatoes of each plate.
7. Pour Alfredo sauce on potatoes/broccoli.
