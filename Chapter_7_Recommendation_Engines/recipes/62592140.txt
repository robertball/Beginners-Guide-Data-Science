Title:
Nana's Pan Seared Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (4 ounce) fillets tilapia
1/8 teaspoon onion powder
1/8 teaspoon smoked paprika
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
Dash of ground black pepper
Dash of Old Bay seasoning
3 to 4 tablespoons Spicy garlic oil

Directions:
Season fillets with onion powder, paprika, garlic powder, black pepper and Old bay seasoning coating both sides. Pour oil into skillet over medium-high heat. Place fillets into skillet and sear both sides about 4 minutes each. Drain on a paper towel to discard excess oil. Serve warm. Enjoy!
