Title:
World's Greatest Onion Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbs olive oil
4 small yellow onions chopped
1 shallot finely chopped
2 sprigs fresh thyme
2 Tbs balsamic vinegar
2 Tbs worchestershire
1/2 Tsp salt
1/4 Tsp pepper
1 8 oz cream cheese, softened
1/2 Cup sour cream
2 Tbs mayonnaise
pinch of celery salt

Directions:
Heat oil in a heavy pan and add onions and shallot and thyme.  Let cook until the onions have melted about 45 minutes.
Add vinegar and worchestershire; let cook until slightly thickened about 15 minutes.  Season with salt and pepper.  Remove from heat and let cool to room temp.
Meanwhile in a stand mixer combine the cream cheese. sour cream and mayo unitl fluffy.  about 5 minutes.  Add cooled onion mix and celery salt.  Transfer to crock and chill for at least two hours.  Serve with crudite.  Will last for two weeks in hte fridge.
