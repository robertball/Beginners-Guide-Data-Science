Title:
Avocado and Egg Breakfast Burrito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/798445.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large 10 inch flour tortillas
1 Tablespoon of butter
4 medium eggs
1 cup mild cheddar shredded cheese
1 Hass avocado sliced
1 small tomato sliced or chopped
Handful of chopped Cilantro (optional)
dash of hot sauce (optional)

Directions:
Place flour tortillas on a microwave safe plate
place shredded cheese on the flour tortilla in the center towards one end. Microwave on high for 20-30 seconds just to melt cheese while cooking eggs.
Cooks eggs in one tablespoon of butter over med/low heat until softly scrambled.
Put eggs over the cheese, top with sliced avocados and tomatoes and Cilantro. Salt and pepper if desired.
Top with your favorite hot sauce.

Submitter: 
*Sherri*
Description: 
A flour tortilla wrapped around, soft scrambled eggs, with the savory creaminess of avocado, add some tomatoes, shredded cheese, and even some cilantro for a great tasting breakfast burrito that will keep your appetite curbed all day long.
