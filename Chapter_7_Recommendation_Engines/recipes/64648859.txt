Title:
Dave's Mahi Mahi Veracruz Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Mahi Mahi filets
1 cup of grape tomatoes
1/2 cup of white wine
3 Tblsp of capers with a little juice
1/4 cup of sliced green olives
3 cloves of garlic (chopped)
1/2 a small bulb of shallot (chopped)
4 springs of thyme leaves, and rest bundled
3 Tblsp of Ghee (or salted butter)
1 Tblsp of safflower oil
1 Tsp of Salt

Directions:
Sauce:
Add 2 Tblsp of ghee (or salted butter) & 1 Tblsp of oil to heated skillet
Sweat down garlic and shallots till soft (2-3 minutes on Med heat)
Add capers, olives, thyme, and wine. Reduce 3-5 minutes (reduced about 3/4 away)
Add 1 Tblsp of ghee (or salted butter)
Add tomatoes, salt and remaining thyme bundle
Reduce heat to Med-Low and simmer for 10 min.
Serve sauce  in ramekins
Fish:
Add 1 Tblsp of ghee (or slated butter) & 1 tsp of oil to same skillet.
Add salt & peppered filets to hot skillet and cover (approx 2-3 minutes per side)
Serve on plate with light sauce over each mahi mahi filet and allow each person to add extra sauce from their own ramekin as desired.

Submitter: 
David Smith
Description: 
My own personal take on a Mahi Mahi Veracruz dish with tomatoes, capers, thyme and white wine.
