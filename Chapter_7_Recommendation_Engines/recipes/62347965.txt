Title:
Sausage, Peppers, Onions, and Potato Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. Italian sausage, cut into 2-inch chunks
Olive oil
4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into thick slices
2 large green peppers, cut into wedges
2 large red peppers, cut into wedges
3 large onions, cut into wedges
1/2 cup white wine
1/2 cup chicken stock
1 tsp. Italian seasoning blend
Salt and pepper

Directions:
*Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
*Sauté sausage in a little olive oil in a large skillet until well browned.  Transfer the cooked sausage to a large baking dish.
*Add about 1/4-inch of oil to the skillet and sauté the potatoes until lightly browned on both sides.
*Transfer the potatoes to the baking dish with the sausages.
*Add the peppers and onions to the skillet.  Sauté the vegetables about 5 minutes so that they are still firm.
*Transfer the onions and peppers to the baking dish.
*Add the wine, chicken stock, and seasonings to the baking dish.  Gently stir the sausage and vegetables to distribute the seasonings.
*Bake for 20-25 minutes.  Serve.
