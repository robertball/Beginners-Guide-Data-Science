Title:
DRESSINGS:  Blue Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 small clove chopped garlic
1 Tbsp milk
Juice of 1/2 lemon
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a food processor, and pulse until mixed but still slightly chunky.
