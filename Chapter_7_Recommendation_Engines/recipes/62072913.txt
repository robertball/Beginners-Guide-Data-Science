Title:
Rigatoni Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 breasts chicken, cooked & cubed
2-3 green onions, chopped
1 pkg. rigatoni pasta, cooked & rinsed
1/4 cup sunflower seeds
1/2 cup cashew halves
1 can olives
1 cup grape tomatoes
fresh spinach leaves
DRESSING:
2 TB sugar
1 tsp salt
1 tsp accent
1/4 cup olive oil
3 TB rice vinegar

Directions:
Make dressing ahead of time & chill.  Combine remaining ingredients. Pour dressing over & toss.

Submitter: 
Penny Allen
Description: 
Surprisingly sweet and delicious, as well as pretty.  Every time I make this, I get at least 2 or 3 requests for the recipe. I like to marinate the chicken ahead of time.
