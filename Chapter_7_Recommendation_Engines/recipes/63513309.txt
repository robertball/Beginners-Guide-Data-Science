Title:
Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 Cup Shortening
3/4 Cup Sugar
1/2 Tsp. Grated Orange Peel
1/2 Tsp. Vanilla
1 Egg
4 Tsp. Milk
2 Cup Sifted Flour
1 1/2 Tsp. Baking Powder
1/4 Tsp. Salt

Directions:
Thouroughly cream shortening , sugar , orange peel and vanilla . Add egg, beat till light and fluffy . Stir in milk . Sift together dry ingredients , blend into creamed mixture . Divide dough in half . Chill 1(one) hour .On lightly floured surface roll to 1/8 ". Cut into desired shapes . Bake on greased cookie sheet at 375 degree , about 6-8 minutes . Cool slightly , remove from sheet.

Submitter: 
Sandra Davidson
Description: 
This is my favorite sugar cookie recipe that my mom would make at Christmas , then decorate and give to people .
