Title:
Artichoke Heart Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6oz Jars Marinated Artichoke hearts
1 sm onion
1 clove Garlic
4 eggs
1/4 cup dry bread crumbs
1/2 Tsp Salt
1/8 Tsp pepper
1/8 Tsp Oregano Leaves
1/6 Tsp hot liquid pepper (Tobasco)
2 Cups Grated Cheese
2 Tbsp minced parsley

Directions:
Drain liquid from one jar of artichoke hearts into saucepan
Drain other jar for other use
Chop hearts
Heat marinade on medium
Add onion and garlic and cool 'til limp
In a bowl beat eggs
stir in crumbs, salt, pepper, oregano, & hot pepper
Add Cheese, parcely, artichokes, & onion mix
Turn into 7x11 baking pan
Bake uncovered at 325 for 30 min
Cool in pan and cut into squares
