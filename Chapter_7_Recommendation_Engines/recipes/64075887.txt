Title:
Vegan, gluten and dairy free coffee whitener Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1137276.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup premium coconut milk
1 cup unsweetened almond milk
1 tsp vanilla
1 Tbsp coconut oil (optional)
2 Tbsp raw cane sugar
1 or 2 Tbsp brown rice syrup or corn syrup or date syrup

Directions:
Heat the coconut milk, almond milk, oil,syrup and sugar over medium-low heat until the ingredients are well combined and the sugar is completely melted.
Add the vanilla and mix.
Blend with an immersion blender until smooth and creamy.
Allow to cool and then store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
A non dairy, yet rich and flavourful way to lighten coffee.  The syrup gives this a creamy texture like the store bought types but without chemicals or additives.
