Title:
BLT Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. shell pasta, made to package directions
2/3 cup ranch salad dressing
8 slices cooked bacon, crumbled
2 cups sliced grape tomatoes
1/3 cup green onion
1/8 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
1/2 teaspoon worcestershire

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except tomatoes and bacon. Chill for 2-3 hours prior to serving. Toss in tomatoes and bacon right before serving.
