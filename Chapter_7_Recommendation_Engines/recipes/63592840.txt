Title:
Sweet Hawaiian Crockpot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Chicken legs or thighs, no skin
1 cup of Pineapple chunks
1 cup of Brown sugar
1/2 cup of Soy sauce
1 tbls Garlic salt
1 tbls peppercorn pepper
1 tbls of dried minced onions
2 tbls olive oil
1 cup of water

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a crockpot. Cook on low for 5-6 hours. Or cook on high until meat is tender and pulls apart easily.
Serve over Jasmine rice and enjoy!
