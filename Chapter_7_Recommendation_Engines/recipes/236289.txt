Title:
Mango Pineapple Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2302547.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup frozen pineapple chunks
1 cup frozen mango chunks
1 ripe banana, sliced
2/3 cup fresh spinach
1/3 cup orange juice
1 cup ice

Directions:
Place pineapple, mango, banana, spinach, orange juice, and ice, respectively, in a blender and blend until smooth.

Submitter:

Description: 
A yummy, healthy smoothie made with mangoes, pineapple, banana, and spinach (shhhh!) that's sure to be a hit!
