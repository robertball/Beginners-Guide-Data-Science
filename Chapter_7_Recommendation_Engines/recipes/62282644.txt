Title:
La Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/352304.jpg
Ingredients:
1 oz Silver Tequila
1 oz Cointreau (or Triple Sec)
1 oz Fresh Lime Juice
Course Salt

Directions:
Combine with in ice-filled shaker
Shake for 1 minute (not kidding)
Strain, pour into cocktail or coupe glass rimmed with salt (salt is not optional).
Garnish with wedge of lime.
