Title:
Sloppy Joes sans meat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion, diced
2 med. green peppers, diced
1 1/2 c. TVP
1 1/2 c. boiling water
2 1/2 c. tomato sauce (or tomatoes)
1-2 T. chili powder
1 T. soy sauce
2 T. sugar
salt and pepper to taste
cayenne to taste

Directions:
In a large saucepan saute onion and peppers in oil.
Add boiling water, TVP, and remaining ingredients.  Simmer for 20 minutes.
Serve on buns.
