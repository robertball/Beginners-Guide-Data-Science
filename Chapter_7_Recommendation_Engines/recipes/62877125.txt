Title:
Swample Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 blue Gatorade
3/4 bottle of Perrier
1/2 bottle of Rose wine
1 can of Red Bull
1 can of Peach Snapple
1 package of Skittles
2 cups of vodka

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a punch bowl. Garnish with orange, lime, and lemon wheels.

Submitter: 
smerki
Description: 
This delicious, sweet and fizzy drink may not be the prettiest punch, but it is a crowd pleaser and perfect for Halloween parties.
