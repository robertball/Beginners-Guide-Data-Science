Title:
Mojo Mojito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bunch of fresh mint leaves to taste
8 fluid ounce key lime juice or Goya Lime juice
8 fluid ounce brown rum
8 ounce sugar
8 cups lemon lime seltzer water

Directions:
Mash mint leaves
In a glass jar add sugar, lime juice,and rum
Mix well.
Add mint leave and let soak 5 minutes.
Add lemon lime seltzer water and enjoy.  If you are ready to serve right away add ice, but it is better to let the flavors meld together by putting in the fridge for 2 hours. Then pour over ice.

Submitter:

Description: 
Refreshing summer drink that you will want to make over and over. Great for warm summer days or nights.
