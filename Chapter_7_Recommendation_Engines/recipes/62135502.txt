Title:
Zuchhini and summer squash salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1)2 fresh zucchini
2)2 yellow summer squash
3)2 cloves garlic (minced)
4)1 tbsp crushed chilli flakes
5)2 tbsp butter
6)1 tsp lime juice
7)salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1)Slice zucchini and summer squash into thin strips
2)Melt butter in a small pan. Lower heat and add garlic and chilli flakes.Leave for 5 mins.
3)Toss zucchini and summer squash with the butter mixture.
4)Add lime juice, salt and pepper to taste.
5)Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Sunaina
Description: 
A unique way to eat zucchini and summer squash. Great with pasta.
