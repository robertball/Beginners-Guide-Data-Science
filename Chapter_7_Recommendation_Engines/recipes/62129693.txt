Title:
Buddy's & Bubba's Homemade Dog Food Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb. lean ground beef or turkey cooked and drained well
2 chicken leg quarters boiled/simmered with skin on until cooked through, about 45 minutes, remove skin when done and reserve defatted broth
2 cups cooked brown rice, drained of any excess water
2/3 cup dry rolled oats
1 10 oz. pkg. frozen chopped spinach, thawed and thoroughly squeezed and drained
2 cups frozen or fresh broccoli, steamed and chopped
1 15.5 oz can kidney or black beans, drained, thoroughly rinsed and drained again, and mashed with potato masher
2 raw carrots, shredded
1 small clove garlic minced or pressed (**Dog's love the flavor of garlic but too much will make them anemic.  Use sparingly or not at all)
1 cup low fat cottage cheese
1/2 cup olive oil
For variety:
*can add 1/2 cup blueberries.
*can replace carrots with 1/2 can pumpkin
*can use cooked cracked wheat in place of either rice or oats
*can add steamed and finely chopped celery or cauliflower
*can add steamed peas
*can add any shredded cheese, sparingly
*can use mild cooked fish
*can add scrambled cooked egg
*can add fish oil in place of olive oil

Directions:
In a skillet, cook ground beef or ground turkey until done and no longer pink, drain well and add to large mixing bowl.
in a stock pot, bring to a boil two chicken leg quarters, skins on, and then simmer for 40 minutes until done and juices run clear. Cool chicken then remove/discard skins and bones.  Chop chicken into small pieces and add to large mixing bowl.
Reserve defatted chicken broth.
Use defatted broth or water to make brown rice. Drain any excess liquid when done and add rice to bowl.
Add the 2/3 cup dry rolled oats to bowl
Thaw, squeeze and drain the spinach then pull it apart, adding it to bowl.
Steam the brocolli, drain excess liquid, coarsely chop in small pieces and add to bowl.
Add the mashed beans to bowl
Shred two raw average sized carrots and add to bowl
Mince or press 1 small clove garlic into the bowl**
Add the cottage cheese and olive oil and mix all together thoroughly.
Store in airtight Gallon-size Ziploc Freezer bag and freeze for later use or refridgerate for daily use.
Add 1/2 cup to favorite dog kibble (reduce kibble accordingly, depending on size of dog) or use as a full meal replacement by the cup.  Serving sizes are adjustable based on type of use and size and/or numbers of dogs.

Submitter: 
Karen Martell Bishop
Description: 
A healthy addition to, or replacement for, your dog's daily meals that he will happily devour.
