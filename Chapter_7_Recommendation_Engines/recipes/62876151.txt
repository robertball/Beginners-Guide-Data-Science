Title:
Breakfast Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
approximately 2 cups prepared sausage gravy (I use jimmy deans super sausage gravy from this site)
3-4 large egg whites
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup Orginal Bisquick Mix
1 cup milk
2 eggs

Directions:
grease a 9" pie plate. Dump the sausage gravy in the bottom of the pan. add the egg whites. In a seperate bowl mix the Bisquick Mix, 2 whole eggs, cheese and milk stir until just combined. Pour the batter over the sausage mixture bake @ 400 degress F for 45 minutes or until top is golden brown.
