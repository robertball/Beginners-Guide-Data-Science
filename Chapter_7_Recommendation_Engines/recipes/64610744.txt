Title:
Mommy's Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of sausage (mild or sage)
1 1/2 cups of chopped onion
1 1/2 cups of  chopped celery (including leaves)
5 cups bread crumbs
1  9-inch pan of homemade cornbread (about 5 cups)
2 teaspoons of rubbed sage
1/2 cup of chopped green pepper
1 teaspoon of marjoram
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 1/2 cups of Chicken Broth
2 tablespoons butter

Directions:
Cook sausage (crumbled) in a large skillet until it is browned.  Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.  Cook onion and celery in drippings in skillet until tender.  Combine sausage, cooked vegetables, bread cubes, cornbread, sage, marjoram, and pepper in a large mixing bowl; blend well.  Moisten with chicken broth.  Place in a greased 13x9x2 inch backing dish, dot with butter, cover and bake for 20 minutes at 350.  Uncover and bake and additional 10 minutes or until golden brown .  This will make about 12 cups or 10 to 12 servings.
