Title:
French Toast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 eggs, lightly beaten
1 1/2 cups milk
1 cup half-and-half cream
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 loaf of Texas Toast (cut in strips like toast sticks), or loaf of French bread, cut in 1 inch slices
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 cup light brown sugar
2 tablespoons maple syrup
1 cup chopped pecans
1 tablespoon of cinnamon
Powdered sugar

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, cream and vanilla. Dip bread slices into egg mixture and place in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking pan.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a small bowl, combine butter, sugar, maple syrup and pecans. Spoon mixture over bread.
Bake in preheated oven until golden, about 40 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.  Sprinkle with powdered sugar.

Submitter: 
Adina Ryan
Description: 
If you like a crunchy sweet french toast, this is to die for!  It is just fabulous and something you can do ahead of time to make the morning go a bit smoother.  Serve with some sausage links or patties and fruit.Modified from: Make Ahead French Toast
