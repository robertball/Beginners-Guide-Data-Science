Title:
Hula's Lemon Cream Cheese Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lemons
Lemon zest
16 ounces cream cheese
1/2 cup sugar
2 TBSP melted butter
3 TBSP sugar

Directions:
Set oven to 350
Zest and Juice 2 lemons - divide zest as will use 1/2 at different times
Mix really well 1/2  of the lemon zest with 16 ounces cream cheese + 1/2 cup sugar + all of the lemon juice
Pour into pan, probably 8 x 8
Mix 2 TBPS melted butter + other 1/2 lemon zest + 3 TBSP sugar then brush this mixture on top of the stuff in the pan
Now bake
