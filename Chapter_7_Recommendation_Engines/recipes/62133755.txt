Title:
Savory Summer Vegetable Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small eggplant, cut into ½” chunks
1 medium yellow squash, cut into ½” chunks
1 medium zucchini, cut into ½” chunks
1 medium onion, roughly chopped
1 cup plain, dry breadcrumbs
4 tbsp melted butter
1 tsp salt
½ tsp garlic powder (or more to taste)
½ tsp onion powder (or more to taste)
1/3 cup crushed Ritz crackers
¾ cup shredded cheddar
¾ cup chopped tomato

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F.  Mix all veggies together, and add breadcrumbs, salt, and garlic and onion powders.  Pour into 2 quart, greased baking dish.  Drizzle butter evenly over veggies.  Bake for 40 mins or until veggies are tender.  Mix together crackers and cheese and sprinkle evenly over cooked veggies.  Sprinkle tomatoes over cheese mixture.  Bake an additional 12 minutes.

Submitter: 
Dana H.
Description: 
A delicious, healthful mix of garden veggies, held together with breadcrumbs and spices will be sure to leave you satisfied--and with room for dessert!
