Title:
Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp poultry seasoning
1/4 tsp paprika
1 chicken, cut in pieces
1/4 cup oil

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients in a paper bag.  Drop a few chicken pieces at a time into bag and shake bag until pieces are coated.
Pan fry at 300 degrees F for 12-15 minutes until chicken is brown and crisp on both sides.  Reduce heat to 260 degrees and continue frying until tender, turning frequently (about 35 minutes).
