Title:
Pasta & Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871659.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces rotini pasta
1 smoked sausage link
1 small can sliced black olives
1/4 cup Henderson's salad dressing.

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions.
Broil sausage link in oven, or cook on grill until done.
Slice the sausage.
Toss all ingredients together. Serve.

Submitter: 
J Scholfield
Description: 
Simple add-what-you-want-to-it kind of summer recipe. The kids pick out all of the veggies.
