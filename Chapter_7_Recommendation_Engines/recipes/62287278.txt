Title:
Sandy's Sour Cream Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Butter
3 Cups Sugar
6 Eggs, separated
1/2 tsp vanilla or flavor of your choice
1/2 tsp baking soda
3 Cups Flour
1 Cup Sour Cream

Directions:
Heat oven to 300 degrees
Butter and flour a tube pan.
Separate the eggs, set yolks aside.  Whip the egg whites until they form stiff peaks.
In another bowl cream butter and sugar together.  Add egg yolks one at a time.
Add the baking soda to the flour.
Alternate adding flour and sour cream to the butter mixture.
When all the flour and sour cream have been incorporated.  Fold in the egg whites by hand.  Do not use the mixer to fold in the whites.  You should still be able to see some of the egg white throughout the batter.
Evenly spoon the batter into the tube pan.
Bake at 300 degrees for 1 1/2 hours.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
My sister in law Sandy gave me this old family recipe.  It has become our traditional cake to make Christmas trifle.  It is a bit of a bother to make but is so worth the time and effort.
