Title:
Olivia's Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1 C. Rosemaria pasta
1 C. converted rice
Water to cover
1 chicken bullion cube

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, brown the Rosemaria and rice in the butter.
Add enough water to completely cover the mixture and bring to a boil.
Add the chicken bullion, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
This is a very old recipe from a friend who was a good cook and liked to share her treasured recipes for everyone to enjoy.
