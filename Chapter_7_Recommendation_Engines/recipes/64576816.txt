Title:
Glaze for Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup icing sugar
1 tbsp. water
1/4 tsp. flavoring, your choice

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together well.
Adjust water & icing sugar to make a barely pourable consistency.
Drizzle over cake.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A thin glaze to drizzle over any cake, esp. a bundt cake. Use 1 or more for a beautiful presentation and a delicious taste.
