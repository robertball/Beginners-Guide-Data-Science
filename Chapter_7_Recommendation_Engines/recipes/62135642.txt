Title:
Yummy Baked French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf white bread, cut into chunks
1 dozen large eggs
1 8 oz package cream cheese
1/2 cup pure maple syrup
2 tbs pure vanilla extract
1 tbs almond extract
1 cup milk (2% or better)
2 tsp cinnamon
2 tbs nutmeg
pinch of salt
Butter or margarine to grease pan

Directions:
At least 12 hours in advance, mix the eggs, milk, maple syrup, flavorings and seasonings
Grease a 9X12 GLASS casserole pan with butter or margarine
Put the cut bread into the pan and add the egg mixture
Cut the cream cheese into pieces and dot over the mixture
Leave in the fridge overnight to allow the bread to absorb the mixture
In the morning, heat the oven to 350 degrees
Bake for 25-30 minutes until mixture is set
Cut into squares and serve with powdered sugar and additional maple syrup and/or fruit

Submitter: 
Ingalyn
Description: 
You do have to make this the night before, but oh, you'll be so glad that you did.  Super rich and creamy, not a low fat dish, save this for a special occasion.  Don't be freaked out by the ingredients.  This makes enough to feed 12 easily.
