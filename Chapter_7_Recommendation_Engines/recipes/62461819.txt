Title:
Tortellini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cloves garlic
2 cans diced tomatoes
3-4 cups chicken broth
1 cup fresh or frozen spinach
2 cups cheesy tortellini
2 tbsp olive oil
Salt & Pepper to taste
1 tsp hot pepper flakes- OPTIONAL

Directions:
Sizzle the garlic with olive oil until garlic is very light brown. Add in chicken broth and simmer, add tomatoes and spinach and let simmer for 25 minutes and then add in cooked and rinced tortellini. let simmer for 5 minutes and serve hot!
