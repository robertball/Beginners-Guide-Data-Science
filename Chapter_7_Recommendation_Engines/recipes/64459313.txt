Title:
Hungarian Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 stick of butter
3 lbs chicken, cut in bite size pieces
paprika
1 small green cabbage, cored and cut in 1/2 inch slices
salt & pepper
2 red cooking apples, cored and sliced
1 medium onion,chopped
1 tbs grated lemon peel
2 tsp caraway seeds
1 tsp sugar
8 oz Swiss cheese

Directions:
Melt butter in a large skillet. Dust chicken with paprika and brown on all sides. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 375. Place cabbage slices on the bottom of a 13x9 inch baking dish. Sprinkle with salt & pepper. Cover and bake for 20 minutes until cabbage is almost tender. Remove cabbage and arrange apples and onion over the cabbage. Sprinkle with lemon peel, caraway seeds and sugar. Place chicken on top. cover with foil and bake another 25 minutes. uncover, sprinkle with cheese and return to oven until cheese melts, about 5 minutes.
