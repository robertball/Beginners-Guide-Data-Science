Title:
Golden Chicken Coconut Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/466858.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup diced/chopped onion
2 cloves minced/grated garlic
1 1/2 tsp grated/minced fresh ginger
3 tablespoons olive oil
3 1/2 tablespoons curry powder
1/2 teaspoon tumeric powder
1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
1/2-3/4 tsp cayenne pepper (to taste)
salt/pepper to taste
2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breast, cut into 2 inch-ish pieces
1 can coconut milk, shaken
1 can (same size as coconut milk) diced tomato
1/3 cup plain yogurt
1/2 Cup of fresh chopped cilantro, separated(some for garnish)
1 teaspoon garam masala
cornstarch as needed/desired, 1 tablesppon at a time

Directions:
dice/chop onion, grate/mince garlic and ginger.
heat the olive oil in large saucepan over med high heat, add onions/garlic/ginger and saute until onions are translucent.
Add the curry powder, coriander, cayenne, salt/pepper and tumeric; cook in onion/oil mixture, just a few minutes to help bring out the spice flavors, a slight paste will form
add the cut up chicken pieces and cook, stirring frequently to coat with spice mixture, approx 5 minutes or until outside of chicken *just begins* to turn white.
add one can coconut milk and one can diced tomato with juices; stir together and bring to boil.
dec temperature on stove to low, cover and simmer for 40 minutes.
30 minutes into simmer, remove lid and add yogurt, garam masala, and 1/4 cup fresh cilantro; stir until incorporated; additional salt/pepper to taste
continue to simmer, lid off, until sauce thickens. If not thick enough for your taste, add 1 tablespoon corn starch and stir until desired consistency achieved.
Serve hot over basmati or brown whole grain rice, garnish with fresh cilantro

Submitter: 
BarleyJack
Description: 
Tender chicken mixed with Indian spices and coconut, serve over rice for a healthy, warm meal perfect on cool evenings.
