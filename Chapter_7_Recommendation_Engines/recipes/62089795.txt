Title:
Chicken Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cooked rotissere chicken
1 1b. egg noodles
1 green pepper chopped
1 onion chopped
2 cloves garlic minced
1 jar pimentos
1 can sliced black olives
1 large can mushrooms with juice
1 stick butter
1 1/2 lbs. velveeta, cut up into chunks
salt and pepper
1-2 c. chicken broth

Directions:
Debone chicken and put into a large stock pot.
Add all ingredients and heat through to a boil over med heat to allow cheese to fully melt.  You may add more broth if you feel the broth is too thick.
Boil Noodles seperately until cooked.  Serve soup over noodles.  Serve with hot buttery dinner rolls.

A unique but very rich and creamy casserole the whole family will love.  A company pleaser.  A feel good comfy meal.
