Title:
Homemade Cream of Whatever Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. instant dry milk
1 c. ap flour
2 tsp. salt
1 c. butter

Directions:
Combine milk powder, flour and salt and mix well. Cut in butter until  it resembles fine crumbs. Store in refrigerator for 2 months.
WHITE SAUCE:
1/2 c. white sauce mix
1 c. cool water
pepper, herbs and spices, if desired
In small saucepan, combine 1/2 c. mix and water. To thicken or thin, increase water. Cook over low heat until smooth, stirring constantly. Add your own spices and seasonings.
