Title:
Yaya's Apple pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/471178.jpg
Ingredients:
6 medium size apples, peeled and diced
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
3-4 tbsp cinnamon
2 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup flour ( optional)
-
1 cup caramel ( home-made preferably )

Directions:
Mix lemon juice and vanilla with diced apples, then in seperate bowl stir in brown sugar, white sugar, cinnamon and some flour. Pour into apples and mix, then pour into pie shell. Drizzle caramel into the mixture and place pie dough on top.
Cook at 375 for 30 minutes.
