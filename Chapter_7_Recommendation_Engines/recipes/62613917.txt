Title:
Italian Baked Penne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz penne pasta
1 lb ground italian sausage
1c chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1/4t black pepper
1-1/2t Italian seasoning
1 can Italian style diced tomatoes
2 cans crushed tomatoes
3 c spinach, chopped (optional)
8oz mozzarella, cubed
1c Parmesan cheese, divided

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Cook pasta 8 minutes in boiling salted water.
Drain and return to pot.
Cook sausage, onion, and garlic until sausage is cooked through.
Add pepper, Italian seasoning, and tomatoes to pan.
Simmer 8 minutes.
Combine sausage mixture with pasta, spinach, mozzarella, and 1/3c Parmesan.
Pour into 9x13 pan and top with remaining parmesan.
Bake 15 minutes.
