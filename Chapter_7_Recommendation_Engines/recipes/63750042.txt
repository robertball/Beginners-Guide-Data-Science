Title:
Buffalo Chicken Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Chicken breast
2 eggs
1 cup flour
1 cup vegetable oil
2 Tablespoons Cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper
1 tablespoon garlic
1/4 cup hot sauce
1 teaspoon butter

Directions:
Put oil in saucepan and cook on medium heat.
Mix flour, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
Cut the chicken into 1 inch pieces.
Crack the eggs and put in bowl and beat them.
Dip the chicken into the egg and then into the flour mixture, then put in the hot oil and cook each side for about 3 minutes.
Put the butter into the hot sauce and heat it in the microwave for 1 minute.
After all the bites are cooked you can either use the hot sauce as a dipping sauce or pour over top the whole batch.
