Title:
Penne Pasta with Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound penne pasta
1 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 pound asparagus and/or green beans, trimmed and cut into 1 inch pieces
1/2 cup fresh broccoli florets and/or cauliflower
1/2 cup chopped red and/or green bell pepper
1/2 cup chopped zucchini
2 Tbs. butter
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 can diced tomatoes, well drained
- or - 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes, packed in oil
4 ounces grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.  Toss with olive oil.
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, roast vegetables until flecked with dark brown.  Set aside.
In a large skillet over medium heat, melt butter.  Saute garlic, onion (and sun-dried tomatoes if used) until heated through. Stir in tomatoes. Toss with penne, roasted vegetables and Parmesan.  Place in a casserole baking dish.
Bake in preheated oven 30 minutes covered, 10 minutes uncovered.
