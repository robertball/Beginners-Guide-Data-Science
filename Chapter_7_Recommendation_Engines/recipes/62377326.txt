Title:
Grilled Flavord Corn on the Cob Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Cobs of Corn
2 tablespoons margarine (not melted)
2 tablespoons lime juice
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon minced garlic

Directions:
Mix together margarine, lime juice, chili powder, and minced garlic.
Pull husk of corn back and remove silk. Leaving husks pulled back. Do not completely remove husks.
Brush mixture on corn, and pull husk carefully back over the corn to cover.
Grill corn for 30 minutes or until tender.

Submitter: 
kuiper55
Description: 
This is a great corn on the cob receipe that you can thrown on the grill while having a summer cookout. It's flavorful and juice!
