Title:
20 minute White Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped onions
1 clove garlic, minced
1 TBS. vegetable oil
1 lb. ground turkey
1 cup chicken broth
1 (14-1/2 oz.) can stewed tomatoes
1/3 cup grey poupon
1 TBS. chili powder
1/8 - 1/4 tsp. ground red pepper
1 (15 oz.) can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
1 (8oz.) can corn, drained
tortilla chips, shredded cheddar cheese and cilantro optional

Directions:
In 3qt. saucepan. over medium-high heat, saute onions and garlic in oil until tender.
Add turkey; cook until no longer pink, stirring occassionally to break up meat.  Drain.
Stir in chicken broth, tomatoes, mustard, chili powder and pepper.  Heat to a boil; reduce heat.  Simmer for 10 minutes.
Stir in beans and cor; cook for 5 minutes.  Top with tortilla chips, shredded cilantro if desired.
