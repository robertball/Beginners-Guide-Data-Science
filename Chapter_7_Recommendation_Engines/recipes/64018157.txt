Title:
Cinnamon Dainties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup sifted powdered sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 cup applesauce
1 T vanilla
2 1/2 cups sifted flour
1/2 cup granuated sugar
1 1/2 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven  to 325.  Place parchment paper on cookie sheet or just not put anything there at all.
Cream butter.  Add powdered sugar and salt, stirring well.
Add applesauce and vanilla.
Blend in flour gradually, stirring until combined.
Shape scant teaspoons of dough on ungreased cookie sheet.Flatten slightly with glass dipped in sugar (after touching the first cookie, the glass will have anough butter on it to make some sugar stick to the glass.)
Bake at 325 for 15-18 minutes.
Combine sugar and cinnamon.  Roll baked cookies in this mixture.

Submitter: 
sueb
Description: 
This is my great grandmother's recipe from the late 50's or early 60's.  I was so interested in talking with her, that I didn't check the oven and they burned.  She was a great advocate of not wasting food, so she made me eat all that nobody else would!
