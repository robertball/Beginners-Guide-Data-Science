Title:
Warm Sweet Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
4 large potatoes, peeled and diced
2 rashers lean bacon
1 tbsp olive oil
2 green onions, finely chopped
1 red onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup light mayonnaise
1/4 cup dijon mustard
1/3 cup balsamic vinegar
1 tsp turmeric
1 tbsp chopped fresh chives
dash salt and pepper

Directions:
PLace the sweet potato and potato in a pot and boil constantly for 20 minutes or until soft but not mushy.
In the meantime, fry the bacon in a skillet with the oil then chop into bite sized pieces when cool.
In a small bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, turmeric, chives, salt and pepper. Transfer to a large bowl and mix in bacon bits, spring onion and red onion.
Let the potato and sweet potato cool slighty, then add to the mayonnaise mixture.
Garnish with extra chives and spring onion if desired.

Submitter: 
Chicken
Description: 
Sick of all the too sweet sweet potato recipes? This salad is hearty enough for a main dish but is not loaded with sugar or butter.
