Title:
Kristi's BBQ Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 slabs of babyback ribs
1 1/2 cups BBQ sauce
1 1/2 cup ketchup
1 tbsp hot sauce
1/2cup brown sugar
2 tbsp. lemon juice
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
2 tbsp. worcestershire sauce
2 tbsp. heinz 57
2 tbsp. A-1
1  clove or tbsp garlic powder
1 tbsp liquid smoke
1/2 cup butter
1 1/2 tbsp. onion powder
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp cavendars greek seasoning
1 tsp cumin
2 tsp paprika
1 tsp oregano
2 tbsp teriyaki sauce
2 tbsp soy sauce

Directions:
Cut ribs between each bone and remove the membrane.
Place the ribs in a large stock pot and add enough water to cover.
Bring stock pot to a boil, and reduce heat to medium for 45 minutes.
Meanwhile, mix the remaining ingredients in a medium saucepan and let  simmer.
Prepare the grill, and spray rack with non-stick spray.
Carefully remove the ribs from the stock pot, and place on the medium/low heated grill, bone side down.
Coat with the prepared sauce.
Let cook for about 10 minutes, turn and coat the other side.
Let cook for an additional 10 minutes, remove and taste a little bit of heaven on earth.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
Ribs slow boiled, then finished up on the grill with a taste that's out of this world.....
