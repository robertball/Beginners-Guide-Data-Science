Title:
Zuppa Toscana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Italian Sausage
2 Large Potatoes, cut in half and sliced 1/4 inch
1 large onion, chopped
1/4 cup bacon bits
2 gloves garlic, crushed
2 cups spinach
16 oz. chicken broth
1 quart water
1 cup cream

Directions:
Slice sausage diagonally, brown in soup pot. Remove any grease. Add chicken broth, onion, garlic, potatoes and water. Cook until done, 10 minutes.
Add bacon, salt and pepper to taste, cook 10 minutes longer. Turn stove to low heat, add spinach and cream, heat through and serve.
For a smaller recipe I use I sausage, crumble it and drain fat with paper towel and then smaller portions of everything else.
