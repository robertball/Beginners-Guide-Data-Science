Title:
Costa Rican Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1049764.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ripe banana, peeled and cut into chunks
6 ice cubes
1 cup coconut milk
1 cup evaporated milk
1/2 cup light rum, or amount to taste
1/3 cup coffee-flavored liqueur (such as KahluaÂ®)

Directions:
Place the banana and ice cubes in a blender, and pour in the coconut milk, evaporated milk, rum, and coffee liqueur. Blend until well mixed. Pour into 2 tall glasses and serve.
