Title:
Broccoli And Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans (10.75 ounces) cream of chicken soup
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 teaspoon curry powder
1 cup milk, or less, used to blend in as needed
20 ounces of frozen broccoli, thawed and chopped
2 or 2 1/2 cups of cooked shredded chicken
1/4 cup mushrooms (optional)*
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1/2 cup of butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Blend together soup, mayonnaise, curry powder and milk.  In a 9x13-inch baking pan, layer with the thawed broccoli that is chopped, on top of that put your cooked chicken and then the soup mixture.  Then sprinkle on the top the bread crumbs and pour melted butter over that.
Bake for 45 minutes.
* Blend in the sliced mushrooms with the soup mixture if desired.
