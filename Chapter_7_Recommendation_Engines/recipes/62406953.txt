Title:
Cheese and Chili Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb cheddar cheese
1/2 lb jack cheese
1 sm can chopped chilies
1 C milk
1 T Flour
1 egg

Directions:
Mix cheeese adn chilies together in a 9x9 baking dish.  Mix milk, flour, egg together and pour over the top. Bake at 350 for 1 hour or until set.
