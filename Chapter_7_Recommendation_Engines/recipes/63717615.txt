Title:
Quick and Easy Vegetable Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1032887.jpg
Ingredients:
1	pound ground beef, or stew meat
1	large onion, chopped about 1 to 1 ½ cups
2	cloves garlic, minced
½	medium sized head green cabbage, chopped
1	cup fresh celery chopped
1       to 2 can's petite diced tomatoes, undrained
1	can no salt added whole kernel corn, drained, or 1 ¼ cups fresh or frozen
1	can no salt added green beans, drained, or 1 ¼ cups fresh or frozen
1	can no salt added sliced carrots, drained, or 1 ¼ cups fresh or frozen
2	fresh russet potatoes, cut into 1 inch cubes, about 1 ½ to 2 cups
2	32 ounce containers low sodium beef broth
2	tablespoons better than bouillon beef base
Salt and pepper to taste
1	tablespoon dried Italian seasoning

Directions:
In large stock pot, brown ground beef or stew meat with onion and garlic.  Season lightly with salt and pepper.  once browned and cooked completely, remove as much fat as possible.
Once Browned; add broth, tomatoes,cabbage, celery, and any fresh vegetables you are using along with beef base and Italian seasoning.  Bring to a simmer; simmer 15 minutes then add potatoes. (If using stew meat let simmer for at least 30 minutes before adding potatoes) Cook until potatoes are just tender.    Let warm through and taste and adjust seasoning.  Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
This is a great way to stretch a pound or so of ground beef.  it freezes wonderfully, and is very comforting to eat.  soak up some broth with crusty bread.
