Title:
Simple Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1260387.jpg
Ingredients:
1 - 8 oz. block cream cheese - softened
1/3 cup white sugar
1 - 8 oz. whipped topping - thawed
1 - graham cracker pie crust

Directions:
Mix with a mixer, the cream cheese and sugar until well blended.  Fold in the whipped topping.  Spread in graham cracker pie crust and chill for a least 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Molly
Description: 
I received this recipe from my brother, many years ago.  When he prepared dinner for a date, this was always his go-to dessert.
