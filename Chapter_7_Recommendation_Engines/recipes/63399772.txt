Title:
Sicilian Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium cauliflower
3/4 cup garlic and herb basting oil
3/4 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
3/4 cup water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cut cauliflower into bite sized florets.
Place in large bowl and add basting oil, tossing until well coated.
Add bread crumbs and cheese and mix well.
Put water into a large casserole dish with a lid.
Put cauliflower in casserole dish and cover.
Bake for about 30-40 minutes or until fork tender.
Leave lid off for last 5-10 minutes of cooking time.
Top with additional grated cheese before serving.
