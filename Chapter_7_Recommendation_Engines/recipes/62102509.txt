Title:
Cheesy Chicken Enchilada casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. butter or oleo
1 white or yellow onion, finely chopped
2 cloves Garlic, minced
1/4 cup milk
2 small (4 oz.) cans green chiles, chopped (1 cup)
1 tsp. ground Cumin
16 oz. sour cream
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1/2 tsp. Salt
1-1/2 - 2 cups Chicken breasts, cooked and shredded  (or can use cooked Turkey)
10-12 corn tortillas
2 cups shredded Cheddar Cheese
Garnish: (optional)-- Avocado slices,
Black olive slices; Slices of Fresh Jalapeno; Dollops of green Salsa

Directions:
Heat butter over medium heat in large fry pan.   Add onion, and stir, cooking until it begins to turn transluscent.  Add garlic and cook 2 minutes more.
Add milk and stir to blend.  Add 2 cans Green chiles, Sour Cream, 2 cans of cream of chicken soup, ground Cumin, salt, and shredded chicken meat.  In a greased casserole dish, put a thin layer of the chicken-sour cream sauce.  Add 5-6 Corn Tortillas to cover (tearing to fit the shape of your dish).  Cover with 1/2 of remaining chicken "sauce'.  Sprinkle 8oz. of grated Cheddar over.  Add 5-6 more Tortillas over this; add rest of chicken sauce and then cover with remaining Cheddar.  Bake at 350 deg. F. for 45 minutes.  If desired, garnish with black olive slices, avocados, or fresh Jalapeno slices.   Serve with cooked rice.

Submitter: 
Sherbg
Description: 
A quick, cheesy, oven casserole dish-- great way to use leftover cooked Chicken or Turkey.  Kids and adults both love it, an easy mid-week meal with ingredients from your pantry!  Serve with rice and a salad.
