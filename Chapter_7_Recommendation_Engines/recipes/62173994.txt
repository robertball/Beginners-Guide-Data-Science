Title:
Tilapia with Parmesan/Sour Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs Tilapia/Orange Roughy or Flounder
8 oz. sour cream
1/4 cup shredded parmesan
1/2 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. basil
1/4 tsp. oregano
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
2 Tbsp. Italian bread crumbs
2 Tbsp. melted butter

Directions:
Place fish fillets in greased 13x9 inch dish.
Combine sour cream, parmesan & seasonings and spread over fish.
Sprinkle bread crumbs on top and drizzle with melted butter.
Bake @350 degrees for 20-25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sonja Oswalt
Description: 
This is so good!  I made the full recipe of sauce, used only what I needed for one piece of fish and refrigerated the rest to use later.
