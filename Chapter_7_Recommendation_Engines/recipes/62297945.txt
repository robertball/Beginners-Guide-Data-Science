Title:
Avocado Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Avocado
3 Tablespoons Sour Cream
1/2 Lime Juiced
1 Tspn Cumin
1 Tspn Garlic Powder
1/4 Cup Cilantro
1/3 Cup Water
1 Tablespoon Tomatoes Sauce

Directions:
Place everything in blender and blend until smooth.  Serve with your favorite tacos.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This goes great on any tacos.  I came up with this recipe after trying something similar at a local restaurant.
