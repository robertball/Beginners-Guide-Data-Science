Title:
sugar free glazed strawberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package, 4 serving size, sugar free strawberry jello
1 1/2 cups boiling water
1 1/2 cups cold water
3 pounds fresh strawberries

Directions:
dissolve jello in boiling water.
stir in cold water.
refridgerate about 1 hour.
cut berries into bite size peices.
stir into glaze.
serve with angel food or pound cake.

Submitter: 
pennycandy
Description: 
i make this for my diabetic mother. it's a dessert she can have and enjoy with out guilt. serve over slices of angel food cake and top with whipped cream for a spring dessert.
