Title:
Nanny's Chocolate Chess Pie(Nanny=Helen Van Hoozer=my nanny) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 unbaked pie shell(9 inch)
1 stick melted oleo(margarine) 1/4 pound
1 1/2 cups sugar
3 1/2 tablespoons cocoa
2 large eggs (beaten)
1 teaspoon vanilla
6 oz. evaporated milk

Directions:
mix well together-pour in unbaked pie shell bake in preheated 325 degree oven for 50 minutes- best served with whipped cream

rich and creamy old fashioned chess pie- in chocolate form.
