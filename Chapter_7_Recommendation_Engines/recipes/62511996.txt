Title:
panzanella salad (bread salad) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/507440.jpg
Ingredients:
1 loaf (pound) day old italian bread cubed
4 vine ripened tomatoes or plum tomatoes, seeded and cut into medium cubes
1/2 cup Kalamata olives chopped
1/2 red onion sliced thin
1 cucumber, peeled and diced
1/2 cup fontina cheese cubed (I use fresh shredded parmesean cheese if I don't have fontina on hand)
2 tablespoons fresh basil, coarsley chopped
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except bread and let sit for 10 minutes.
Place cubed bread in a dish.
Pour dressing/veggie mix over bread and toss to coat.
Salad should be served within 2 hours.
