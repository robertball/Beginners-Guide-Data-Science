Title:
Impossibly Easy Coconut Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983738.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Original Bisquick® mix
2 cups milk
1 cup flaked or shredded coconut
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
4 eggs

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease 9-inch pie plate.
Stir all ingredients until blended. Pour into pie plate.
Bake 50 to 55 minutes or until golden brown and knife inserted in the center comes out clean. Cover and refrigerate any remaining pie.

Submitter: 
Bisquick®
Description: 
Enjoy a scrumptious coconut pie that’s impossibly easy to make because there’s no crust to roll.
