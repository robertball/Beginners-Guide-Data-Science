Title:
BUSH'S® Granny's Country Style Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979595.jpg
Ingredients:
9 large Granny Smith apples
1 (28 ounce) can BUSH'S® Country Style Baked Beans
1 pound smoked kielbasa sausage, diced
1 cup onions, diced
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Slice off tops of 8 apples and scoop out inside with melon-baller.  Reserve apple tops and discard apple insides.  Peel and dice remaining apple, set aside.
Mix together Bush's Country Style Baked Beans, diced apple, sausage and onions.  Season with salt and pepper.
Stuff apples with baked bean mixture.  Wrap with aluminum foil and place on side of grill.  Grill for 30 minutes until heated through.  Allow to cool for 4 minutes, remove foil, place tops on apples and serve.

Submitter: 
BUSH'S® Beans
Description: 
In this brilliant combination of ingredients, apples are stuffed with a spicy, sweet mixture of baked beans and sausage, and heated on the grill.
