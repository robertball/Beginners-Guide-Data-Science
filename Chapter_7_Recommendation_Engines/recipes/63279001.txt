Title:
Shredded Barbecue Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds skinned and boned chicken thighs
1 T olive oil
1 cup ketchup
1/4 cup dark brown sugar
1 T Worcestershire sauce
1 T cider vineger
1 T yellow mustard
1 t ground red pepper (optional)
1/2 t garlic salt

Directions:
Brown chicken 4 minutes on each side if desired, or place directly in crock pot.
Combine ketchup and next 6 ingredients.  Pour over the chicken.
Cover and cook on HIGH for 1 hour.
Reduce heat to LOW and cook 5 to 6 hours additional hours.
Remove chicken from sauce, shred with a fork.
Stir the shredded chicken into the sauce and heat an additional 30 minutes.
Serve with hamburger buns or over rice.
