Title:
Gefilte Fish jello mold Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6042638.jpg
Ingredients:
2 3oz pkg Lemon jello
2 cups borscht liquid
1 small jar horseradish (6oz)
Dash of lemon juice
1 large jar Gefilte Fish ( I use pike)

Directions:
Heat jello and borscht together to dissolve the jello. Add entire jar of horseradish (including the liquid) and lemon juice. Let cool. Oil mold and pour 1/2 jello mixture into the mold. Let slightly gel. Lie fish in mold as close together as possible. Cover with rest of jello mixture. Fills a 6 cup mold. Use a ring mold. Refrigerate
until hard.
