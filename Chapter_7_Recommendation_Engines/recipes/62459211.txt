Title:
yummy easy fish recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pieces White fish of your choice i use tilapia
4 tbs soy sauce
2 tbs butter
1-2 tsp fresh grated ginger root

Directions:
Place thawed fish in oven safe baking dish.  Add soy Sauce butter and ginger.  Bake at 350 for 20 minutes or until fish is cooked through.  Serve over Basmati or Jasmine Rice.
