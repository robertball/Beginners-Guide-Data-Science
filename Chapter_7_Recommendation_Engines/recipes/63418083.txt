Title:
Chicken Ditalini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/923141.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups chicken stock
7 oz skinless-boneless chicken
2 TBSP butter
2 stalks celery, diced
1 medium carrot, diced
1 small onion, diced
1 clove garlic
Salt and pepper to taste
4 oz Ditalini pasta
1 TBSP chopped Italian parsley

Directions:
In soup pot over medium heat add stock and chicken for 15 minutes until chicken is cooked.
Remove chicken from pot and cut into bite sized pieces.
Add chicken back to pot.
In small skillet saute chopped garlic, onion, celery and carrot in butter until onion is translucent.
Add veggies to soup pot.
Add ditalini and cook until tender.
Add parsley, salt and pepper.
