Title:
Super Easy Egg Halwa - Anday ka Halwa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Vegetable Oil
1 Cup Powdered Milk
1 Cup White Sugar
3-4 Cardamoms
1 Tablespoon Chickpea Flour
4 Eggs
2 Tablespoons Chopped Almonds

Directions:
Heat the oil in a heavy bottom pan and add the crushed cardamoms.
Add the chickpea flour and saute till you can smell the roasted aroma of the flour.
Add milk to the oil and continue cooking till the mixture is golden brown in color.
Combine the eggs and sugar and beat well.
Add the egg mixture to the golden milk, and continue stirring till the oil separates.
Serve in a deep bowl and garnish with the almonds. You can also cut it into pieces and serve each piece separately.

Submitter: 
Celestial
Description: 
A family favorite! Everyone loves the soft and sweet texture of this amazingly simple way of making egg halwa. The traditional halwa usually takes up to 3 hours for cooking; this recipe reduces it to 15 minutes without compromising on the divine taste! Enjoy!
