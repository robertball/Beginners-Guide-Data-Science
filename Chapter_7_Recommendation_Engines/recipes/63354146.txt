Title:
Chipped Beef Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1801366.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8-oz package cream cheese, softened
2 Tablespoons milk
1 Tablespoon finely diced onion
dash garlic salt
dash salt
dash pepper
1/2 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
1 3-oz package finely sliced chipped beef (like Budding brand), shredded
1/2 cup finely chopped pecans, optional

Directions:
Mix all ingredients, except the nuts, in a bowl.  Shape into a ball or loaf as desired.  Roll in pecans to cover.  Chill until firm, about 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Lucky Noodles
Description: 
My mother's recipe we have at nearly every family gathering.  A hit at parties too.  Serve with assorted crackers.  Use skim milk, low fat cream cheese and low sodium worcestershire sauce to reduce the calories and sodium.
