Title:
Easy Cream Cheese & Sausage Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground pork sausage
8 oz. cream cheese
2 cans of crescent rolls

Directions:
Brown the sausage and drain fat.  Add cream cheese and cook over med. heat until it is well blended and the cheese is completely melted.  Spray a pyrex pan with cooking spray.  Unfold the crescent roll dough and make a layer to fit the bottom of the pan, covering it completely.  Next pour your sausage and cheese mixture over the dough. Top this off with another layer of the crescent dough.  Bake according to the directions on the can of the crescent rolls.
