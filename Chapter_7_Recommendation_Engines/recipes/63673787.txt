Title:
Jim's Seasoned Grilled Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1020364.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Chicken breasts
1-Tablespoon Sazon complete
1- Tablespoon Tones citrus grill
1- Tablespoon lemon pepper seasoning
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat the grill. Take Sazon, Citrus grill,lemon pepper and mix together. sprinkle the mixture over the chicken breasts. Grill the chicken until it's moist and tender about 20 minutes.
