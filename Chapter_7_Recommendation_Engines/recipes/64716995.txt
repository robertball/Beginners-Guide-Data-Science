Title:
Sautéed Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5782792.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb green beans
1 tbsp unsalted butter
Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
Trim or snap off the tops of the green beans.
Heat a large skillet over medium heat and melt the butter.
Toss the green beans into the pan.
Sauté the green beans for 5-10 minutes.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Remove from heat, serve, and enjoy!
