Title:
Cranberry Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c cream cheese (soy cream cheese can be subtituted)
3 T cranberry sauce
2 tsp confectioners sugar

Directions:
Mix the cream cheese and confectioners sugar together.
Fold in the cranberry sauce.

Submitter: 
Mari
Description: 
A simple cream cheese frosting that can be used with your Thanksgiving left overs.
