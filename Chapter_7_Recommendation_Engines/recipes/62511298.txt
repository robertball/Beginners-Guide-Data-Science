Title:
Power Protein Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3.5c All natural nut Butter (i.e. Peanut or Almond butter, no sugar added!)
.25c Honey or Agave Juice
1c Powdered Milk or Powdered Milk Substitute (i.e. Soy milk powder)
1c Oat Bran OR Flax Meal OR Wheat Bran
1c Rolled Oats
.5c Milk or Milk Substitute (i.e. Soymilk)
.5c Almond Paste (optional)
2 scoops Protein powder
.5c Ground nuts OR nut meal (i.e. pecans)
.75c Unsweetened, finely ground coconut

Directions:
1. Mix honey, peanut butter, and powdered milk together until smooth (this 'batter' will be somewhat stiff). Fold in oats, nuts, protein powder, and bran until combined.
2. If using the almond paste, mix in separate bowl with milk until combined. Stir milk (or milk and almond paste mixture)into the nut butter mixture until combined. Knead with hands until well combined and malleable.
3. With bare hands or mellonballer, roll mixture into 1-1.5 inch balls. Roll in the finely ground coconut.
4. Store in airtight container. Refrigerate if 'room temp' exceeds 70F.

Submitter: 
The Viking Bunny
Description: 
These little balls make a great after workout snack that is high in protein and LOW in sugar (but not low in flavor!!)! They are also airport safe and travel well! 

This recipe is very elastic and easily adaptable to everyone's dietary needs.
