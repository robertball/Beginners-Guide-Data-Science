Title:
GF Oatmeal Gingersnaps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening (or Butter)
1/4 cup molasses
1 egg (2 egg whites)
1 1/2 cups all-purpose gluten free flour(Bob's Redmill)
1 1/2 tsp Xamtham gum
1 cup sugar or 1/4 cup brown sugar + 1/2 cup Aguave
3/4 cup quick-cooking oats
3/4 teaspoon baking soda, reduced for high altitude
2 teaspoon ground ginger
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1/4 teaspoon salt
Additional cinamon/sugar for rolling

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream shortening, molasses and egg. Combine dry ingredients; stir into creamed mixture. Roll into 1-in. balls; roll in sugar. Place on greased baking sheets. Flatten slightly. Bake at 350 degrees F for 9 minutes (do not overbake), let cool on sheet 1 min then cool on rack.
