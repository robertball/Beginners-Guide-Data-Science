Title:
Candy Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups sliced peeled tart apples
2 tablespoons lime juice
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup all purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon salt
Pastry for double crust pie (9 inch)
TOPPING:
3 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 tablespoon heavy whipping cream
1/4 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
In a large bowl, toss apples with lime juice. Combine the sugar, flour, cinnamon and salt; add apples and toss lightly.
Line a 9-inch pie plate with bottom crust and trim even with edge; fill with apple mixture. Dot with butter. Roll out remaining pastry to fit top of pie. Place over filling. Trim, seal and flute edges; cut slits in top pastry.
Bake pie at 400 degrees for 40 to 45 minutes or until golden brown and the apples are tender.
For topping, melt butter in a small saucepan. Stir in brown sugar and cream; bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Remove from the heat and stir in pecans.
Pour over top curst. Bake the pie 3-4 minutes longer or until bubbly. Place on a wire rack. Serve warm.
