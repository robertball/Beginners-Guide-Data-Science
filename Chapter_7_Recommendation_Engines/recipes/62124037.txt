Title:
Anne's Green Chili Breakfast Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Shredded Cheddar Cheese
2 pkgs frozen Southern Style Hashbrowns
1 lb Hamburger
1 lb Pork Breakfast Sausage
4-6 4.5 oz cans Green Chilies Drained
2 15 oz cans Stokes Green Chili
4 Whole Fresh Tomatoes Diced
18 eggs
1 can Diced Jalapenos (optional)
30 Tortillas
Aluminum Foil cut into 30 squares

Directions:
Brown & drain hamburger and sausage in pan.
At same time brown hashbrowns in a separate pan.
Pour both into large mixing bowl or very large pot.
Add cheddar, green chilies, Stokes green chili and Tomatoes.
Scramble and cook eggs.
Add eggs to mixture.
Mix well.
Add about 1 ½ mixing spoonfuls to each tortilla center.
Fold ends over then roll.
Roll foil over burrito and place in freezer.
For spicier burritos add jalapeños to mixture after eggs are added.
If you are in a hurry you can replace the Fresh Tomatoes, Jalapenos and Green Chilies with 3 cans of any flavor of Rotell.

Submitter: 
Anne
Description: 
Combination of Egg, Sausage, Potatoes, Tomatoes, Green Chili and Cheese.  These are great for a big gathering or just freezing for breakfast here and there.  This is always a favorite in my house and my parents.
