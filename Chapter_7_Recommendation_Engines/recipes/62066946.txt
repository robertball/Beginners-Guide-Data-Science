Title:
Sour Cream Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken mixture:
1 lb. shredded chicken, cooked
1 small onion
2 T. butter
1/4 cup hot picante sauce
3 oz. cream cheese
1 tsp. ground cumin
Enchiladas:
8 flour tortillas (soft taco size)*
2 cups shredded mexican style cheese
Sour cream sauce:
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup milk
8 oz. sour cream
*8 corn tortillas may be substituted (fry in oil before stuffing)

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350.  Cook onion in melted butter until tender in large skillet.  Stir in cooked chicken, picante sauce, cream cheese, and cumin.  Heat and mix until just blended.  Immediately take off heat before mixture separates.  Set aside.
2.  Combine soup and milk in small saucepan.   Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until bubbly.  Remove from heat and add sour cream.  Mix until blended.  Set aside.
3.  Fill each tortilla with chicken mixture and sprinkle with cheese.  (Reserve 1 cup for topping)  Roll up and place seam side down in 12x8 baking dish.  Continue until baking dish is full.  Pour sour cream sauce over tortillas.
4. Cover and bake at 350 for 25 minutes.  Sprinkle with remaining cheese and bake uncovered for an additional 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.
*Beef mixture can be substited instead of chicken.  Cook 1 1b. of beef, saute onion in butter, and combine with taco seasoning.  Continue recipe at step 2.

Description: 
This is an amazing recipe for enchiladas.  The sour cream and cream of mushroom soup make a delicious creamy sauce.  You can either fill with beef or chicken mixuture to make different variations.
