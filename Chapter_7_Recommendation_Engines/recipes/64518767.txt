Title:
Blackberry Jam/blackberry on a cloud Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups strained blackberries  ( 1 cup juice with seeds 4 cups without seeds, 1 cup water) and keep the strained seeds for a dessert
4 1/2 cups sugar
1 box certo light

Directions:
Put blackberry juice in large sauce pan.
Combine certo with  1/4 cup sugar  then add to blackberries.
Bring  to a boil over high heat.
Add rest of sugar (4 1/4 cup)
Return to a Hard boil for 1 minute
Pour into jars screw on lids  and process  in hot water  bath 10 mins
Use the strained seeds for an easy dessert
Per serving
1 tbsp seeds mixed with some sugar (maybe 1 tsp or to taste)
2 Tbsp sour cream mixed with 1 tsp instant vanilla pudding (or instant cheesecake flavoured instant pudding)
Put mixed pudding in a small dish and top with the seeds mixed with sugar and serve

Description: 
Scrumptious with peanut butter on toast for breakfast
and leftover strained seeds make a perfect dessert
