Title:
Sweet Red Wine Chopped Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 fresh mushrooms (sliced)
1 Tomato (chopped)
1/2 cup Black Olives (halved)
1 cup cooked Ditalini pasta
1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese (or gorganzola)
3 slices of bacon (cooked & crumbled)
1/4 cup green onions (chopped)
3 cups Romaine lettuce (chopped)
3 Tablespoons Red Wine Vinegar
2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
1/2 Tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon onion powder
salt
pepper
Garlic Croutons

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine olive oil, red wine vinegar, sugar, garlic powder and onion powder, refrigerate dressing for 1 hour (or as long as you have).
Chop lettuce into bite size pieces (about 2 inches), and combine with mushrooms, tomato, black olives, bacon, green onions, pasta, and blue cheese.
Toss with prepared red wine vinagrette and top with a dash of salt, pepper, and croutons.
