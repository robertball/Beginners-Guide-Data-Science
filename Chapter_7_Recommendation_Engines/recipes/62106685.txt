Title:
Greek Cucumber Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup feta cheese
1 block softened cream cheese
3/4 cup sour cream
1/2 cucumber seeded and finely diced,divided
1 tsp ground black pepper

Directions:
Combine all but half of diced cucumber in food processor.  Process until smooth.
Hand mix in remaining cucumber.
If dip is too thick add cucumber juice or a splash of milk to thin out.

Submitter: 
CookBrooke
Description: 
I call this my 10 year Tzatziki dip because it has taken me that long to perfect it.  It is not traditional by any means but very good - like the sauce you may get on a gyro at the fair.  If you have fresh dill add some!
