Title:
Veggie Soup Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 head cabbage, coarsely chopped
2 onions, diced
1 green bell pepper, diced
4 carrots, peeled and diced
5 ribs celery, chopped
1 14.5 oz cut green beans, drained
1 14.5 oz diced fireroasted tomatoes
46 oz tomato juice
1 cup beef broth
1 envelope onion soup mix
water to cover vegetables
Salt and pepper to taste
4-6 dashes tabasco sauce
1 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a dutch oven. Cover and simmer for 30-40 minutes over medium heat, stirring often.
