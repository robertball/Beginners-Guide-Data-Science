Title:
Enchilada Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. cooking oil
2 T. all-purpose flour
2 T. chili powder
1 tsp. ground cumin
1 can (14 oz. chicken broth)
1 can tomato sauce (8 oz.)
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. garlic powder

Directions:
Heat oil in large saucepan; stir in flour & chili powder.
Cook for 1 minute then all remaining ingredients.
Bring to boil & simmer for 10 min.
Refrigerate extra.
