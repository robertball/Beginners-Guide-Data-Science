Title:
Easy Chop Suey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/822781.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs of Beef Stew meat cut in cubes
2 stalks of celery chopped
2 small onions chopped
2 tbls of Flour
pepper to taste
2 beef boullion cubes
2 teaspoons of Bean molasses or Brown Gravy Sauce
soy sauce(to taste)
1 can of sliced water chesnuts (drained)
1 can of bean sprouts(draiined)
1 can of sliced mushrooms(drained)
Olive Oil
Garnish with crispy noodles(optional)

Directions:
Take a stew pot and coat the bottom well with oil.
Take the meat and coat it in the flour and pepper mixture and fry it until brown and cooked all the way through.(don't worry if it appears gummy - it will help thicken the sauce)
Add the celery,onions, 2 beef boullion and the molasses, cover with water.
Add soy Sauce to taste and cover with a lid and simmer for about 20 - 30 minutes until beef and vegatables are tender.
Add chop suey vegatables and heat thoroughly.
(If sauce is not thick enough dissolve 1 teaspoon of corn starch in cold water add and simmer until it thickens).
Serve over rice and top with crispy noodles if desired
