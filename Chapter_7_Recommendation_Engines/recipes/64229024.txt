Title:
Luisa's Chile Verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of pork stew meat
1 poblano pepper
I small jalapeno
1 onion quartered
5 tomatillos - husks removed
1/4 cup tomatillo cooking sauce (Herdez)
salt and pepper to taste
cumin and garlic powder to taste
small amount of oregano
10 sprigs cilantro

Directions:
Pre Heat the oven to 400 degrees.  Roast the poblano pepper, onion, tomatillos and jalapeno until brown - about 15 minutes.
Put the poblano pepper in a bowl and cover with saran wrap for 10 minutes.
Peel the skin and add to a blender along with the jalapeno, tomatillos and onions.
Add 1/4 of cooking sauce.  Puree until smooth.  If needed add more cooking sauce.  The mixture should be on the thick side.
Salt and Pepper the pork and cook in a small amount of oil over medium flame in a SS pan until brown.
Drain pork on paper towels
Pace pork in a small crockpot and add the sauce, cumin,garlic powder and oregano
Cook for 6-7 hours on low checking occasionally and if to thick add a small amount of water
When cooked remove from heat and add chopped cilantro

Submitter: 
Luisa
Description: 
I serve this with Mexican White Rice and Chili Beans.
You can also use as a filing for burritos or tacos

The recipe can easily be doubled or tripled for larger families
