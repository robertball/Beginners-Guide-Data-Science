Title:
Virgin Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons coarse salt
1/2 lime, cut into 4 wedges
1 6 ounce can frozen limeade concentrate, thawed
1/4 cup orange juice
4 cups ice cubes

Directions:
1.	Place the salt in a shallow dish.  Rub the rim of each of 4 margarita glasses with a lime wedge and dip the rims into the salt; set the lime wedges aside.
2.	In a blender, blend the limeade, orange juice, and ice cubes on high speed for 1 to 2 minutes, or until well blended and ice cubes are crushed.
3.	Pour evenly into the glasses and garnish with the lime wedges.  Serve immediately.
