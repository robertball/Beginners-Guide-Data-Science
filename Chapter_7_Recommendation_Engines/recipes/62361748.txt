Title:
Hoisin Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/951150.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can water chestnuts
1 tbsp corn starch
1 tbsp rice vinegar
1 tsp crushed red pepper
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tbsp sesame oil
3 tsp sugar
3 tbsp hoisin sauce
1/4 c chicken broth
1 lb boneless chicken breast or pork chop
1 carrot, sliced
2 tsp ginger
1/2 small onion, sliced
1 green peppers, sliced

Directions:
Slice meat and in a medium bowl combine with 1tbsp of hoisin sauce. Mix and set aside. Slice carrots, onion, and bell pepper.
In a small bowl mix the remaining amount of hoisin sauce, broth, corn starch, rice vinegar, sugar, and crushed red pepper. Set aside.
Heat sesame oil in a wok. Stir fry meat. When meat is almost cooked through, add garlic and ginger and stir fry until fragrant (about 30 seconds)
Add carrot, bell pepper, onion,and water chestnuts and stir fry until desired crispness. Add hoisin sauce mixture. Coat well and stir fry for about 3 minutes.
Serve over your desired type of rice.
