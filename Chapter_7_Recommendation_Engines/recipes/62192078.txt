Title:
Buttered Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/298624.jpg
Ingredients:
6-8 carrots, peeled and sliced into 1/2 inch slices
water
1 tablespoon butter or Molly McButter sprinkles
2 tablespoons fresh parsley, minced
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Place sliced carrots in a pot and cover with just enough water to cover.  Bring to a boil and simmer until just fork tender, about 10 minutes.
Pour off all the water and add the butter and parsley.  Add salt & pepper.

Submitter: 
Cindy in Pensacola
Description: 
This is one of my favorite vegetable side dishes.  Easy and healthy.
