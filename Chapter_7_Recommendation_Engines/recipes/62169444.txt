Title:
Luscious Autumn Pumpkin Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 cups canned pumpkin
2 eggs
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ginger
1/2 cup quick-cooking oatmeal
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 large can evaporated milk
3/4 cup sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1/2 cup brown sugar, firmly packed
2 Tablespoons butter, melted

Directions:
Combine flour, oatmeal and brown sugar together in a bowl; cut in 1/2 c butter. Press into the bottom of an ungreased 9x13 inch baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.
Combine pumpkin, evaporated milk, eggs, sugar, salt and spices. Pour into baked crust and continue baking for 20 minutes.
Prepare topping of chopped pecans, remaining brown sugar and melted butter. Sprinkle over filling and continue baking 15-20 minutes longer until pumpkin mixture is set.
Cool in pan and cut into squares. Serve with whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Cat Lady Cyndi
Description: 
This is a delicious, easy pumpkin dessert with a sweet crunchy praline topping. To serve, cut into squares and add a dollop of fresh whipped cream. This a wonderful alternative to the classic pumpkin pie.
