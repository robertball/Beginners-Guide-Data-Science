Title:
Las Vegas Galbi (Korean-style beef ribs) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4558404.jpg
Ingredients:
2. 5lb beef short ribs, flanken style
5 cloves of garlic
1 white onion
1/2 large asian pear (cored and cubed)
1 kiwi (skinned and cubed)
1 cup soy sauce
1 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup sesame oil
1/4 tsp black pepper
sesame seeds (optional)
1 dog (optional, not for eating)

Directions:
place beef short ribs (flanken style) in a large bowl of water for an hour or so
in a food processor, combine garlic, onion, asian pear, kiwi and puree
transfer to a bowl and stir in soy sauce, brown sugar, honey, sesame oil, and black pepper
marinate for about 4 hours on each side, or over night.
before grilling, strain the galbi strips and discard excess marinade. pour sesame seeds on top if each piece before grilling, if desired.
Serve with rice, kimchi, garlic, etc

Submitter: 
TheElusivePastry
Description: 
So this is basically LA Galbi with my own personal touches.  It has received praise from many Koreans; however, I'm not sure if they were just being nice or not.  You don't need a portable/tabletop  stove for this (you can use a stove top instead) but its more fun this way.
