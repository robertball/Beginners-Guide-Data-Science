Title:
Amazing Buffalo Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces package cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup bleu cheese dressing
1/2 cup of Franks hot sauce
1/2 cup of crumbled bleu cheese
2 cups diced cooked chicken (boiled)
Ranch dressing to taste, appr. 1/4 cup

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together into a slow cooker and cook until heated; serve.

Submitter: 
CrissyCK
Description: 
I've had so many different buffalo dips.  I got this one from a coworker because it is AMAZING.  I will never switch now.
