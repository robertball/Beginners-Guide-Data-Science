Title:
Garden Fresh Veggie Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Sour Cream
1 Cup Mayonaise
1 Tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
1 Tablespoon chopped fresh dill
I Tablespoon finely minced green onion (only green part, not white)
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
4-5 dashes worcestershire sauce
3-4 dashes tobasco sauce
pinch salt
Optional: extra fresh dill and green onion for garnish

Directions:
Chop/mince all fresh ingredients and add to a small mixing bowl.  Measure in garlic and onion powders.  Add sour cream and mayonnaise. Combine.  Add worcestershire sauce and tobacco sauce along with pinch of salt.  Combine.  Chill in fridge overnight for best flavor.  Garnish with fresh herbs or chopped green onions as desired.

Submitter: 
kherder330
Description: 
This dip disappears very quickly at parties! I created the recipe because I wanted an alternative to the packet ranch dip that is so popular. This dip is fresh, zingy and a cinch to throw together!
