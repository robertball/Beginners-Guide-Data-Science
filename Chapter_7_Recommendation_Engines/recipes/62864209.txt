Title:
AVOCADO CHICKEN & JACK   */+ Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/658103.jpg
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 teaspoon olive oil
1 clove garlic, grated or minced finely
juice from one or two limes
1/2 teaspoon onion flakes
1 pinch salt
1 pinch ground black pepper
1 avocado - peeled, pitted and sliced thinly
2 ripe tomatoes, sliced
1 (8 ounce) package shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Marinate chicken in olive oil, garlic, lime juice, onion, salt, and pepper.  You can marinate this for a few hours if you like, really makes the flavors better.
Cook chicken in marinade on the stove for 15 minutes or until chicken is browned and just about done.
Place chicken on cookie sheet and top each breast with 1 to 2 slices of tomato and some shredded cheese.  Place your slices of avocado on top of that. Place in oven for 10 to 15 minutes, until cheese melts. Serve immediately.
