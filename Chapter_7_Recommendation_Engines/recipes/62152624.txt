Title:
Whole Wheat Macaroni and Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz enriched whole wheat elbow macaroni with wheatgerm
1/4 cup butter or safflower oil
1/4 cup stoneground 100% whole wheat flour
2 cups milk
seasoned salt
seasoned pepper
1 cup unsalted raw cashews or other nuts, coarsely chopped
2 cups swiss cheese, grated
4 slices swiss cheese
1 precooked garlic sausage, sliced
1/2 TB soft whole wheat bread crumbs
1 TB poppy seeds

Directions:
Preheat oven at 450 degrees
Cook macaroni in salted water
After cooked, add a little milk to macaroni to moisten and unstick
In a separate pan, melt 1/4 cup butter and blend in flour
Gradually add milk, stirring until thickened
Season to taste with salt and pepper
Alternate macaroni, nuts, cheese and sausage in shallow 1-1/2-2 quart baking dish
Pour sauce over
Place strips of cheese on top
Brown bread crumbs in 1 TB butter and place on top of cheese
Sprinkle poppy seeds over
Bake 450 degrees for 20-25 minutes

Submitter: 
Geroniho
Description: 
This is a totally different type of macaroni and cheese casserole which has a lot of interesting flavors in it from whole wheat pasta to swiss cheese to raw cashews to poppy seeds to garlic sausage. It's good.
