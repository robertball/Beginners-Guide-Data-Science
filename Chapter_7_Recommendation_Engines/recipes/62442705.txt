Title:
Roasted Tomatillo Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  1/2" slices of yellow onion
5  medium/large tomatillos
1  ear fresh corn, husked
1  medium pobalano chile - 3 - 4" long
2 TB  olive oil
2 TB brown sugar
1/4 cup tightly packed cilantro

Directions:
Brush onion, tomatillos, corn & chile w/olive oil.
Grill veggies over medium heat until onions are lightly charred and tomatillos soften and begin to collapse, corn is tender and chile is softened.
Place chile in bowl, cover with plastic wrap and steam 10 minutes.
Peel chile, remove stem and seeds.
Place veggies (except corn) and cilantro, brown sugar and 1/4 tsp salt into food processor.  Puree.
Add corn that has been stripped from the cob.
Taste, add more salt or sugar to taste.
