Title:
mammasues chicken tacqitoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30ct. corn tortillas,one whole chicken,2cans salsa,veg oil,salt,pepper,garlic.

Directions:
boil whole chicken til falls off bone,cool,debone,remove skin,cut into small pieces.mix in bowl w/salsa,salt,pepper,garlic.in fry pan heat oil 1in.place tortillas in hot oil turn & remove,place on cookie sheet.put cicken in tort then roll up,place in hot oil &brown both sides eat &enjoy
