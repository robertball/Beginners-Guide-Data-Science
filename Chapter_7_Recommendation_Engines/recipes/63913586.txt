Title:
Black Beans and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon olive oil
1 red onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup uncooked white rice
1 1/2 cups low sodium, low fat vegetable broth
2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
2 cans black beans, drained and rinsed
1 can original Rotel
1 can corn, drained
splash of lime

Directions:
In a stockpot over medium-high heat, heat the oil. Add the onion and garlic and saute for 4 minutes. Add the rice and saute for 2 minutes or until rice turns white.
Add the vegetable broth and rotel.  Bring to a boil, cover and lower the heat and cook for 20 minutes. Add the spices, black beans, corn and splash of lime

Submitter: 
Amanda L.
Description: 
White rice, garlic and onion is cooked in a nice vegetable stock for about 20 minutes. Black beans, cayenne and cumin are added to the pot, given a stir ...and that's it. Black Beans and Rice for six.Modified from: Black Beans and Rice
