Title:
Rocket and Parma Ham Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/998789.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (7 ounce) bag arugula
7 ounces Parma ham, torn into thin strips
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Arrange arugula on a large, flat platter.
Top arugula with Parma ham.
Drizzle olive oil and balsamic vinegar over the salad.

Submitter: 
Vikil
Description: 
This is a very simple and tasty recipe for a beautiful-looking salad which can be an elegant dinner party starter or a mid-day snack.
