Title:
Quick Pork Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ounces cooked tenderloin, cut in small bite-size pieces (aprox 1 cup)
1 Tbsp oil
3 Tbs soy sauce
1 cup frozen peas & carrots, cooked and cooled
2 cups cooked white rice
1 green onion chopped
1/8 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp garlic powder
pinched crushed red pepper (optional)
1 egg

Directions:
Combine soy sauce, ground ginger, garlic powder, and red pepper; set aside.
Heat oil in skillet; stir fry pork and green onion just until green onion tender. Stir in rice, peas & carrots, and soy sauce mixture.  Stir together until combined and heated through.
Push rice mixture to one side of skillet; beat egg quickly then stir into cleared side of pan, scrambling.  Combine with rice mixture when egg thoroughly cooked.
Serve with additional soy sauce if desired.

Submitter: 
Robin Whitworth
Description: 
Leftover pork tenderloin and leftover rice make a quick meal.
