Title:
My Favorite Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6743324.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head green cabbage, finely shredded
1/2 head red cabbage, finely shredded
2 large carrots, grated
1 medium red onion, quartered and thinly sliced
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
1 tablespoon kosher salt
1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 teaspoon celery seeds
2 teaspoon dry mustard

Directions:
Combine the green cabbage, red cabbage, carrots, and red onion in a large bowl.
Combine the sugar and vinegar in a small saucepan and bring to a simmer over medium heat, stirring until the sugar dissolves.
Allow the mixture to cool, then whisk in the mayonnaise, lemon juice, salt, pepper, celery seeds, and mustard.
Pour the dressing over the cabbage and toss to coat before serving.
If you prefer your coleslaw cold refrigerate before serving. Enjoy!
