Title:
Sweet & Sour Kielbasa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-lb. kielbasa
2 cp. ketchup
1/2 cp. brown sugar
2 T. Worcestershire Sauce
6 T. butter or margarine
1/2 cp. finely chopped onion
4 T. lemon juice
1/2 cp. water
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Add all ingredients into a saucepan and simmer on low heat until onion is cooked.
