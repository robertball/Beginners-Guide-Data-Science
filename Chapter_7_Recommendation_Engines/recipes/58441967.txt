Title:
Macaroni and Cheese Bake - My Way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (10.75 oz) condensed cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup milk
8 oz. elbow macaroni
8 oz. colby cheese
8 oz. monterrey jack cheese
1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp curry
1/2 tsp cumin
3/4 tsp garlic powder
1 1/2 tsp butter
1/2 tsp basil
1 cup traditional-style bread crumbs

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.  Cut colby and jack cheeses into small cubes; set aside.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  In a medium saucepan, combine soup, milk, salt, pepper, cumin, curry, garlic powder, butter, basil and parmesan cheese and cook over medium heat until simmering.  Remove from heat and stir in macaroni.
Layer the casserole dish with 3/4 cup of bread crumbs.  Pour macaroni mixture into casserole dish and spread evenly.  Add cheese cubes and mix uniformly throughout.  Bake for 25 minutes or until cheese is golden and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Dave Baker
Description: 
This is a pretty simple recipe using cream of chicken soup that can be used when you have a large group to feed.  I got it from my dad who feeds 8 people, and this does the job.Modified from: Macaroni and Cheese Bake
