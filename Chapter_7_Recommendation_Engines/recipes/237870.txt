Title:
Tomato Basil Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1186990.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh spinach leaves
1/2 large tomato, cut into chunks
1/2 cup frozen mango chunks
1/2 cup ice cubes
2 large fresh basil leaves

Directions:
Blend spinach, tomato, mango, ice, and basil leaves together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Amber
Description: 
I absolutely love this recipe! The tomato and basil go so well together and the bright green color is so beautiful! Plus, how can you go wrong when it's good for you, too?
