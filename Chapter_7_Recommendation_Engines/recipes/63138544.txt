Title:
Banana Split Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 C. Grahm Cracker Crumbs
1/4 C. Sugar
1/3 C. Butter, melted
1 C. Butter, softened
2 Eggs
1 tsp. Vanilla
2 C. Powdered Sugar
2 20 oz. cans crushed Pineapple, drained
3-4 Bananas
16 oz. whipped topping (Dream Whip works best!)
Nuts, chopped (optional)

Directions:
Mix the grahm cracker crumbs, 1/4 sugar, 1/3 c. melted butter.  Using the back of a large spoon, press crumbs in bottom of a 13" x 9" in. glass baking dish. Chill for 10 min.  Beat 2 sticks of softened butter, the eggs, vanilla, and powdered sugar for 10 min. or until fluffy. Spread over crust, slice and layer Bananas, spread Pineapple over bananas.  Spread with whipped topping, sprinkle with chopped pecans or walnuts (optional). Allow to chill for at least an hour.  If you can wait that long!
YUMMY! I hope you all enjoy this dessert!
