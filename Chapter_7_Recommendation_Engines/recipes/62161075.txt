Title:
Cheesy Chili Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 c popped popcorn
2 T butter - melted
1 t chili powder
1 T garlic powder
2 T grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
POP all your popcorn and place in a bowl (I prefer one with a lid) melt your butter and then pour it over and shake or toss till coated..then add the rest of spices and cheese and toss again . store in air tight container at room temp for about 3 days at the most .

Submitter: 
jessmomof3
Description: 
Great snacking popcorn ...cheese flavor with a burst of garlic .
