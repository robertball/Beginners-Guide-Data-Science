Title:
Easy, Cheesy Chicken Skillet Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz applewood-smoked bacon
1/4 cup unsalted butter
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 1/2 cups chicken broth
2 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese, 10 oz
1-2 tbsp hot sauce
4 cups steamed white rice
2 cups shredded cooked chicken

Directions:
Preheat broiler. In cast iron or ovenproof skillet, cook bacon until crisp. Drain on paper towels; when cool, crumble.
Reserve 2 TBSP drippings in skillet. In pot, melt butter over medium heat. Whisk in flour, salt and pepper until smooth and bubbly. Slowly whisk in broth; over high heat, cook, whisking until thickened. Reduce heat to low; stir in 1 1/2 cups cheese and hot sauce until melted and smooth.
Remove from heat. Stir rice into drippings in skillet. Stir in chicken and cheese sauce. Top with remaining cheese, then bacon. Broil 3-5 minutes or until cheese is melted. Serves 4-6
