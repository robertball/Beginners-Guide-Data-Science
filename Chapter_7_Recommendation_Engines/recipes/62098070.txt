Title:
Crustless Greek Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 onion, chopped
1 (10 oz) package frozen, chopped spinach, thawed and drained
1 tsp minced garlic
6 eggs, beaten
1 cup crumbled feta
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 cup milk
1/8 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp lemon pepper
1 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp dried mint
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup sliced black olives (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Heat oil in an 9 inch iron skillet over med-high heat.  Add onions and cook, stirring occassionally, until soft.  Add spinach and garlic and cook until excess moisture has evaporated.
In a large bowl, combine eggs, cheese, milk, nutmeg, lemon pepper, oregano, mint, salt and pepper, and olives.  Mix well.
Pour egg mixture into skillet, and stir until blended.
Bake in preheated oven 30 minutes.  Let cool 10-15 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Dana H.
Description: 
This is a standard quiche with Greek inspired flavors.  This is one of our family favorites, served breakfast, lunch or dinner.
