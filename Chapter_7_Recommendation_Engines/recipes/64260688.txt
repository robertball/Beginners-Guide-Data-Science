Title:
Glazed Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups Brussels sprouts, washed and halved
2 T olive oil
2 T minced garlic
Sea Salt
Black Pepper
1/4 c balsamic glaze

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Mix Brussels sprouts with olive oil and garlic in large bowl to coat then place on cookie sheet with cut sides down
Sprinkle sea salt and pepper to taste
Bake for 20-30 minutes or until desired softness
Remove from oven and place in serving bowl
Drizzle with balsamic glaze and toss lightly

Submitter: 
orangechickie
Description: 
The key to making Brussels sprouts interesting is balsamic glaze.  Drizzle a little or a lot!
