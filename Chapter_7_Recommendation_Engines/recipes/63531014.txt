Title:
OMG chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 split chicken breasts
1/2 cup sour cream, mayo or yogurt
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
italian bread crumbs

Directions:
Spread sour cream with parmesan cheese over chicken breasts, in a baking dish. Sprinkle italian bread crumbs on top. Bake at 400 degrees for 20-30 min.
