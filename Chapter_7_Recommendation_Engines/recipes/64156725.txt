Title:
Teriyaki Sauce Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1/2 cup Vegetable Oil
1/4 cup sesame oil
1/4 cup soya sauce
2 tbsp lemon juice
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 tbsp green onions, chopped
2 tbsp ginger root, minced

Directions:
In a small bowl combine the oils and lemon juice.
Add minced garlic, ginger root & green onion.
