Title:
New Orleans BBQ Shrimp ala JIM Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/431126.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds peeled and deveined shrimp
2 tablespoons chili powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 stick butter
1 onion diced small
3 cloves minced garlic
1 tablespoon chili powder
1/2 cup ketchup
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
3 tablespoons light brown sugar
1/2 cup Worchester
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 bay leaf
1 teaspooon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
Several dashes louisiana hot sauce
6 iceberg lettuce leaves
2 tablespoons chives

Directions:
1) Toss the shrimp , 2 tablespoons chili powder and salt. miz well. Refrigerate while preparing the sauce
2) Heat a saucepan over med heat and add butter. sautee the onions until soft ( 4 mins). Add the garlic and 1 tablespoon chili powder.
3) Add the ketchup , olive oil, brown sugar, worcestershire,lemon juice, bay leaf, thyme and cayenne pepper to the pan. bring sauce to a boil then simmer uncovered for 20 mins until thick
4) Discard the bay leaf. Add the salt and hot sauce
5) Toss the marinated shrimpinto the sauce and simmer for 2-3 minsuntil the shrimp are just cooked through.
6) Arrange the shrimp over the lettuce and garnish with chives.
ENJOY!
