Title:
Creamy Ricotta Spread Recipe

Ingredients:
2 tablespoons ricotta cheese
1 1/2 teaspoons orange juice
1 tablespoon granular no-calorie sucralose sweetener (such as SplendaÂ®)
1/2 teaspoon honey
1 tablespoon nonfat dry milk powder

Directions:
Mix together the ricotta cheese, orange juice, sweetener, honey, and dry milk powder in a small bowl until the mixture forms a spreadable, even texture. Refrigerate leftovers.
