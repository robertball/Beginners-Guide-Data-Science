Title:
Pecan Honey Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 tsp soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 egg
3/4 cup oil
1/4 cup honey
1 cup sugar
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Mix flour, soda and salt together. Add rest of ingredients and mix.  Don't need a mixer,just mix with spoon.  This will be quite thick.  put into greased 11 x 13 inch pan and pat to even out.  Bake 20-25 minutes in pre-heated oven.  Let cool before cutting into serving pieces.

Submitter: 
grannygigi
Description: 
This is an old recipe that was in the Government Surplus box with a jar of honey many years ago.  Very good and rich.  Hope you like!!
