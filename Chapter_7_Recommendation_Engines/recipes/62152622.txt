Title:
Holiday Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large cans crushed pineapple
2 standard size cans mandarin oranges
3 pounds green and/or red grapes
6 bananas
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1/4 cup sugar
2 TBS flour
16 large marshmellows
1 pint whipping cream

Directions:
The night before: drain fruit and make custard sauce
- Drain pineapple and oranges overnight outside refrigator (inside refrigerator in summertime)
- Halve grapes, remove any seeds and place in plastic container in refrigerator
- Make custard sauce (let sit to cool outside refrigerator in winter; inside refrigerator in summer)
To Make Custard:
- Beat eggs well
- Place eggs in small pan
- Mix in milk and stir until WARM ONLY.  Do not let mixture get hot.
- Mix sugar and flour separately in small bowl and add a little egg-milk mixture and blend.
- Add sugar/flour mixture to egg-milk mixture SLOWLY.
- After mixture has thickened, add marshmellows and stir until dissolved.
- Let cool.
Next day:
- Place pineapple and oranges in large bowl.
- Add grapes and 6 sliced bananas.
- Whip cream in medium-sized bowl
- Add custard sauce to whipped cream, blend lightly and add to fruit gently
- Retain bowl in refrigerator until just before serving.
