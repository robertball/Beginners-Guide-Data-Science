Title:
Mom's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 sliced white bread torn into small pieces.
1/2 cup milk
1 med. onion minced
2 cloves garlic pressed
1/4 Cup packed minced parsley
1 lb. ground beef
1/4 grated parmesan cheese
1 egg
1 tsp. salt
pepper to taste

Directions:
Soak bread in milk add rest of ingredients.
Bake 350 for 1 hour
Cover top of loaf with glaze of choice if desired during last 20 min. of cooking.
