Title:
Mom Sawyer's Cranberry Ice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1750660.jpg
Ingredients:
4 c cranberries
2 1/2 c sugar
3 1/2 c boiling water
1/2 c orange juice
1/4 c lemon juice
2 c Cool-Whip

Directions:
Cook first three ingredients until berries pop open, then put through a collander and mash/press. Keep the juices from the collander, throw away the "mash" unless you want to use it for something else!
Add additional juices and freeze until mushy.
Stir and add whipped cream and mix well.
Stir occasionally till well-frozen.

Submitter: 
Buck Sommerkamp
Description: 
A classic for Thanksgiving dinner or any time! This recipe has been in the family since 1938.
