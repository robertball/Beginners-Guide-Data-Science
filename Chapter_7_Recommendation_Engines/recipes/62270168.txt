Title:
Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups diced peeled potatoes
1 3/4 cups sliced carrots
2/3 cup chopped onion
2/3 cup butter or margarine
2/3 cup flour
1 3/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon poultry seasoning or thyme
3/4 teaspoon pepper
3 cups chicken broth
1 1/2 cups milk
4 cups cubed cooked chicken
1 cup frozen peas
1 cup frozen corn
Pastry for 2 double pie crusts

Directions:
Cook potatoes and carrots in microwave until tender/crisp
In skillet, saute oinions in butter until tender.
Stir in flout, salt, poultry seasoning and pepper until blended.
Gradually stir in vroth and milk,
Bring to a boil, cook and stir 2 minutes until thickened.
Add chicken, peas, corn, potatoes and carrots.
Remove from heat
Place in pie crust
Bake at 425 for 35-40 minutes, or until crust is golden brown.
Let stand 15 minutes before cutting.
