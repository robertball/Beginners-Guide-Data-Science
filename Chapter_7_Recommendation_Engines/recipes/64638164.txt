Title:
Turkey Breast with Creamy Herb Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Butterball Roasters
4 Tbsp butter, melted
2 envelopes (0.7 oz) Italian dressing mix
1 tsp salt
2 tsp pepper
10.75 oz can cream of chicken soup
1/4 c chicken broth
4 oz cream cheese, cut into cubes

Directions:
Brush turkeys with butter.
Combine Italian dressing mix, salt, and pepper.
Sprinkle over turkey, and place in slow cooker.
Cover and cook on low for 4 hours or until meat thermometer inserted into thickest portion registers 165F.
Remove turkeys, cover, and keep warm.
Add soup, broth, and cream cheese to drippings in slow cooker.
Cook 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally until smooth and heated.
Slice turkey, and serve with gravy.
