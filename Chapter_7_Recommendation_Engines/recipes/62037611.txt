Title:
Snickers Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small boxes of French Vanilla Pudding mix
2 cups milk
2 small containers of Cool Whip, thawed
8 Granny Smith Apples, diced with or without peel
6 frozen Snickers Bars, Chopped

Directions:
Mix pudding and milk together.  Let stand a minute.  Add Cool Whip, apples and Snickers Bars.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.
Can be easily cut in half.
Hint:  Use a rolling pin to roll over the frozen Snickers bars and they break easily.

Submitter: 
Kathy W.
Description: 
Great salad/sidedish for a summer barbeque.  However we have been knowb to use it all winter too!!
