Title:
Vegan Cream of Carrot and Ginger Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds organic carrots, cleaned and roughly cut
2 garlic cloves, chopped
2 medium onions, chopped
2 T olive oil
2 cups broth
1 1/3 cups coconut milk
1/3 cup soy milk (or any other non-dairy milk)
2 tsp grated fresh ginger
1/2 tsp sea salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2 T dried parsley (as garnish)

Directions:
Steam the carrots until soft.
While the carrots are steaming, saute the garlic and onions in the olive oil until they are softened and slightly brown in color.
Combine steamed carrots, cooked garlic and onions, and all remaining ingredients in a blender. Blend on puree setting.
Heat the blended soup in a large saucepan, and serve garnished with dried parsley.
