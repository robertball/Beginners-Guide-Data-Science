Title:
Starlit Summer Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/49016.jpg
Ingredients:
12 red seedless grapes
1/2 cup milk
1 (6 ounce) container peach yogurt
2 tablespoons white sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup ice

Directions:
In a blender, mix the grapes, milk, peach yogurt, sugar, vanilla, and ice. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Chef W
Description: 
This simple and unique smoothie is perfect for breakfast or snacking. Has a smooth, uniform flavor that makes it light and delicious.
