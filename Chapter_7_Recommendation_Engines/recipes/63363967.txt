Title:
Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 rotisserie chicken, cup up
1 jar (16oz) salsa
1 cup frozen corn, thawed
1/4 cup barbeque sauce
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 cup butter, melted
8 flour tortillas (10 in)
1 jar (15.5 oz) salsa conqueso dip, warmed
4 cups (16oz) ground mexican cheese blend
2 2/3 cup crushed nacho cheese tortilla chips
1/2 c sour cream

Directions:
In large sauce pan, combine the first five ingredients; heat through.
Brush butter over one side of each tortilla. Place one tortilla, buttered side down, in a large skillet. SPread with 1 cup chicken; top with another tortilla, buttered side up. Cooke over medium heat for 1-2 minutes or until bottom is lightly browned. Flip.
Spread 1/2 cup queso dip over top of each quesadilla. Sprinkle cheese around outside edge. Cover and cook for 1-2 minutes or until cheese begins to melt. Remove to a cutting board. Sprinkle chips over middle of quessadilla. Spoon a dollop of sour cream into the center. Cut into 6 wedges. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
