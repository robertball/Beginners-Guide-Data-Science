Title:
Paleo Barbecue Sauce With Some Kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2181417.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup beef stock
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1/4 cup shallot, minced
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
1 tablespoon avocado oil
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon prepared horseradish
1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Mix beef stock, tomato paste, shallot, Dijon mustard, garlic, apple cider vinegar, avocado oil, cumin, cayenne pepper, black pepper, horseradish, red pepper flakes, and salt in a saucepan over medium heat. Bring to a simmer, stirring until mixture is well combined. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until slightly thickened, 15 to 20 minutes, stirring frequently.

Submitter:

Description: 
Paleo style. You can put this on anything from chicken to steak to eggs. Feel free to substitute as needed if you need less kick.
