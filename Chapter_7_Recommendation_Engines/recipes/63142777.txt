Title:
Joan's Asian Noddle Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ CUP SLICED ALMONDS
8 TSP SESAME SEEDS
7 TBSP RICE VINEGAR
4 TBSP SUGAR
1 TBSP SALT
1 TBSP PEPPER
1/2 CUP CANOLA OIL
1  16 OZ BAG COLESLAW
2 PKGS RAMEN NOODLES
(BROKEN UP  THROW SEASONING AWAY)

Directions:
1.  In a skillet over medium low to medium heat,
Toast almonds and sesame seeds until light brown.
2.  In a small bowl, combine vinegar, sugar, and pepper.  Whisk in oil.
3.  In a large bowl, combine coleslaw mix and ramen noodles. Add contents of small bowl and mix well.  Toss in the almondsand seeds just before serving.
