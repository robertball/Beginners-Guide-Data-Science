Title:
Chicken Tommyzzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/931621.jpg
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken roasted or boiled (if you want to do a shortcut, just use a rotisserie chicken)
1 box of spaghetti noodles
1 vidalia onion
1 green bell pepper
1 family size can cream of mushroom soup (or 2 regular sized cans)
4 cups (2 bags) of shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 can chicken broth
butter

Directions:
Dice onion and pepper then saute in butter. Boil spaghetti noodles in water and chicken broth, then drain. Pinch chicken into bite sized pieces. Butter the bottom of a 9X11 pan, then layer spaghetti noodes, chicken, 1/2 cream of muchroom soup, 1/2 of the onions and peppers and 1 bag of cheese. Then layer everything again. Cook on 350 for about 35-40 minutes, until cheese bubbles.
