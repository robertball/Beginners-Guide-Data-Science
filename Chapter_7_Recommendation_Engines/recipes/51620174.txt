Title:
Turkey Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cubed cooked turkey
3/4 cup turkey gravy
1 cup shredded carrots
2 cups prepared stuffing
1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
2 cups warm mashed potatoes
1 cup of shredded chedder cheese

Directions:
In a greased 2-qt. baking dish, layer the turkey, gravy, carrots, stuffing and corn. Top with potatoes. Add cheese at the last 20 minutes or so of baking. Bake, uncovered, at 325 degrees for 45-50 minutes or until edges of potatoes are browned.
