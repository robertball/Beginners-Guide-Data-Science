Title:
Letty's Albondigas Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1031199.jpg
Ingredients:
Meatballs
1/2 lb lean ground beef
1/2 lb ground pork
1   egg, beaten
2   garlic cloves, minced
1/2 cup cooked rice
1/2 cup Mint leaf, chopped
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1  teaspoon ground cumin
Soup
6  cups chicken broth Homemade or canned
1/2 cup onion, chopped
3  stalks celery, cut into small pieces
1  large fresh diced tomato with seeds and juice
2 Tbs. canned Adobe sauce
1 Tbs. 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1  teaspoon oregano
1  large baking potato cubed
1  Large carrot diced
1  large zucchini, sliced
salt
pepper
Garnish
1/2 cup cilantro chopped
1/2 cup chopped raw onion
diced avocado
1 fresh lime cut in 6 wedges

Directions:
Make the meatballs first: Combine everything and mix thoroughly. Form meatballs and roll tightly between the palms of your hands You should be able to make around 20 to 24 meatballs.
Combine chicken broth, onion, celery, tomatoes and their liquid, adobe sauce, cumin, oregano in a large pot.
Bring to broil, and reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes. Drop meatballs in the soup which is slightly boiling. Return to simmer and cook another 10 minutes. Add cubed potato and carrots cook 10 minutes Add zucchini and cook another 10 minutes.
Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
Serve in bowls and top each serving with chopped cilantro, raw onion and diced avocado and a squeeze of fresh lime juice to brighten all the flavors.

Submitter: 
Terry E.
Description: 
My co-working Letty made this amazing soup for one of our Soup Friday's.... LOVED IT! The fresh mint makes it authentic and the splash of lime at the end adds another layer that can only be described as genus!
