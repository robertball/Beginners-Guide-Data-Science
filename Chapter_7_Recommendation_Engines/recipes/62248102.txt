Title:
Melody's Corn Macque Choux Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon bacon grease
3 tablespoons butter
1/2 cup beef broth
1 cup chopped onions
1/2 cup diced celery
1/2 cup diced green bell pepper
1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
5 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon minced fresh thyme
1 (20 ounce) package frozen corn
2 strips crisp cooked bacon, crumbled
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 cup minced fresh parsley
Creole seasoning to taste

Directions:
In a two quart saucepan, combine bacon grease, butter, onions, celery, both kinds bell peppers, salt, pepper, parsley, Creole seasoning and thyme.  Saute on a medium high heat until onions are translucent.  Add garlic and saute another two minutes.  Add in the rest of the ingredients, bring to a boil stirring to combine all ingredients well, cover, reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally while cooking other foods for your meal.

Submitter: 
Melody Dumplin
Description: 
A common Creole dish that can be served as a side dish to any combination of meat and potatoes, pasta or rice dishes.  A flavorful compliment.
