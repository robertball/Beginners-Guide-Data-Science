Title:
Buttermilk Frozen Custard Base Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/820527.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup Sugar
1/8 tsp Salt
1 3/4 cup Whole Milk
2 tbsp Unsalted Sweet Cream Butter
2 Large Eggs (beaten)
1 1/2 cup Buttermilk
1 tsp Vanilla Extract

Directions:
In saucepan, combine sugar, salt, whole milk, and butter.
Cook over medium heat until mixture starts to steam.
In a medium bowl beat eggs then slowly whisk half the hot mixture into the eggs, then pour back into the saucepan.
Reduce heat to medium-low and cook until mixture becomes slightly thick. (About 3 minutes)
Remove from heat and refrigerate until chilled.
After mixture has chilled add the buttermilk and vanilla extract and pour into ice cream mixer.
Churn until done or add fruits a few minutes before custard is done.

Submitter: 
Jerimy Wallace
Description: 
A great semi-tangy frozen custard base flavored with buttermilk. It tastes very much like cheesecake but uses no cream cheese. Would be great with tart fruits such as berries or lemons,limes, ect. and garnished with a sugar cookie or vanilla wafer.
