Title:
Juicy Pecan-Crusted Fish LGLS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 fish fillets
1 cup pecans, freshly toasted
1/4 cup evoo
2 tbsp lemon juice
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp dried marjoram
1/4 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp sea salt.

Directions:
Preheat 400 F
Combine all except nuts, and pour over fish
Blend 1/2 cup pecans to flour consistency, Chop rest coarsely
Dip each filet into ground pecans.
Lay in baking dish and top with chopped nuts
Bake for 20 minutes
Serve with green beans (page 90-91)

Submitter: 
Mary Landis
Description: 
Pecan Crusted Fish--- Salmon, Flounder, Char Croaker, Haddock or Tilapia with toasted pecans
