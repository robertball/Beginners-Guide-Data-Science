Title:
Lacy French Fried Onion Rings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1 egg
1 cup milk
1 tbsp vegetable oil

Directions:
Stir dry ingredients together.
Beat egg until thick and lemon colored, add milk and oil. Stir together.
Stir in dry ingredients and beat until smooth.
Dip onion rings or shrimp into batter and fry in hot (375 degrees) oil.
