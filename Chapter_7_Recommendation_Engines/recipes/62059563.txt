Title:
Hot Asparagus Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 cans cut asparagus
8oz lite cream cheese
4oz bleu cheese
1 large egg
2 loaves sandwich style white bread
butter flavored nonstick spray

Directions:
Spray baking sheet with spray. In a food processor combine cheeses and egg til well blended. Cut off crusts of bread and roll flat with rolling pin. Spread with cheese mixture, then place 2 rows of asparagus on top. Roll up and pinch seam.Cut in half. Place on baking sheet seam side down.  When the sheet is full spray all the rolls. Freeze til firm, remove from baking sheet and place in freezer baggies til ready to use. At that time take out as many as you need and bake in a preheated 400 degree oven for 12-15 minutes. The rolls should be slightly browned.

Submitter: 
DECAR48
Description: 
Make these up when you have the time.  They stay wonderfully frozen for 2 months!
Pull out as many as you need at a time.
