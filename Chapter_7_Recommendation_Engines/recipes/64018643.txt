Title:
Karen's Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. graham cracker crumbs
1 c. plus 3 T. sugar, divided
3 T. butter, melted
40 oz. (5 packages) full fat cream cheese
3 T. flour
1 T. vanilla
3 eggs
1 c. sour cream

Directions:
Bring all ingredients to room temperature before beginning.
Place a rimmed cookie sheet on the bottom rack of the oven and position the other rack in the center of the oven.
Pour hot water in the cookie sheet.
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine graham cracker crumbs, 3 T. sugar, and melted butter.
Press mixture into the bottom of a springform pan.
Bake at 350 for 10 min.
Beat together cream cheese, 1 c. sugar, flour, and vanilla with electric mixer on med. speed until well blended.
Add eggs, one at a time, mixing each just until blended.
Blend in sour cream.
Pour over crust.
Bake at 350 for 65 min or until center is almost set.  Do not open oven until done.
Remove from oven and run a knife around the rim of the pan to loosen.
Cool on wire rack 1 hour.
Refrigerate at least 4 hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
Dense, delicious, creamy cheesecake.  A much requested favorite.
