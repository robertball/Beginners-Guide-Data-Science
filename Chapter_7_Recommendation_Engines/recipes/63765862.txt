Title:
No Guilt Ramekin Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1044093.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz. low fat cream cheese
1/2 cup 2% milk
2 eggs
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp. cornstarch
1/2 t. vanilla extract
cinnamon to taste
1/2 cup fruit topping (fresh fruit or pie filling)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Place cream cheese, milk, eggs, sugar, cornstarch, and vanilla extract, in a blender and blend until smooth.
Pour into the 4 (5 ounce) ramekins filling to the top, and bake at 350 degrees for approximately 30 minutes or until the center has set.
Remove from oven and place on cooling rack.
Lightly sprinkle with cinnamon.
Garnish with your favorite fruit or pie filling and serve when cooled.

Submitter: 
SHORECOOK
Description: 
I was looking to make a dessert that was low in fat, quick to make, and easy to serve.  This is it, my No Guilt Cheesecake!
