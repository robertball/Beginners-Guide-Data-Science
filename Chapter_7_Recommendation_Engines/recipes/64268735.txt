Title:
Ellen's Baked Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup enchilada sauce
2 eggs
1 tablespoon finely shredded cheddar or monterrey jack cheese
2 tablespoons heavy cream or crème fraiche

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Spoon enchilada sauce into the bottom of 2 ramekins. Crack an egg into each ramekin sprinkle with cheese and top with cream or crème fraiche.
Bake in the preheated oven until yolks are just set, 10 to 12 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
Eggs are baked in a spicy enchilada sauce and topped with cream and cheese, for a rich and creamy brunch dish.Modified from: Chef John's Baked Eggs
