Title:
Pumpkin Spice Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Spice cake mix
1/2 C applesauce or 1 tub of snack applesauce
1 pkg. cinnamon chips
1 can pumpkin (not pie mix)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Combine all ingredients and stir until well blended.
Fill cupcake liners with batter.
Bake for 20 min.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Quick and delicious.  Only 4 ingredients.  So moist.
