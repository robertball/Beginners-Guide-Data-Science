Title:
Loretta's Mexican Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground beef, cooked
2 cup tortilla chips, crushed a litlle
1 can cream chicken soup
1 can cream of mushrooma soup
1 (14 oz) enchilada sauce
1 can chopped green chilies
1 can rotel, drained
1 (6 oz) can evaporated milk
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (or cheddar jack)

Directions:
Layer bottom of 13x9 pan with tortilla chips.  Mix all remaining ingredients, except cheese.  Pour over chips.  Sprinkle cheese over top.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes or until hot.
