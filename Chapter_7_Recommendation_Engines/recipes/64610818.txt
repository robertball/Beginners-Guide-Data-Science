Title:
Ang's Honey Bun Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4163151.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box yellow cake mix
2/3 cup vegetable oil
1/3 cup water
4 eggs
1 cup sour cream
1 Tbsp. cinnamon
1 cup light brown sugar
1 cup chopped pecans or walnuts (optional)
2 cups powdered sugar
2 tsp. vanilla extract
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
Mix cake mix, oil, water, eggs, and sour cream. Pour 1/2 of the batter in greased and floured 9 X 13" glass dish. Mix cinnamon, brown sugar, and nuts. Pour 1/2 of the sugar mixture, then pour the rest of batter and sprinkle the rest of sugar mixture over the top of cake. Swirl the mixture with a knife. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 - 35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
With a knife, poke several slits into cake. Mix powdered sugar, vanilla, and milk, then drizzle over cake while still warm.

Submitter: 
AlabamaAng
Description: 
This is my version of a ginormous and simply delicious honey bun! It is best served with a big scoop of vanilla ice cream!
