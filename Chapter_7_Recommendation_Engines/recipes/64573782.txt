Title:
Chocolate Avocado Pudding with Greek Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3575368.jpg
Ingredients:
2 small avocados
1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
1/8 cup raw honey
2 tbsp nonfat Greek yogurt
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 tsp sea salt

Directions:
Place all the ingredients into a blender and blender on high until smooth and creamy.
Remove from blender and transfer to a ramekin.

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description:
