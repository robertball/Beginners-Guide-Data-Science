Title:
Broccoli Chicken Fettucini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups broccoli, fresh or frozen
1 cup mushrooms, fresh sliced
4 oz fettucini, uncooked
2 Tbsp butter
1/2 lb chicken breasts, cut in strips
1 small onion, sliced
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup, condensed
1 cup chicken broth
1 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 cup parmesan cheese, shredded

Directions:
Cook fettucine, drain and set aside.
Steam fresh broccoli and saute fresh mushrooms and set aside.
In butter, saute chicken, onion and garlic until no longer pink.
Stir in soup, broth, onion powder, pepper, broccoliand  mushrooms.  Bring to a boil.
Add drained fettuccine to chicken mixture.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 5 minutes or until heated through.  Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
