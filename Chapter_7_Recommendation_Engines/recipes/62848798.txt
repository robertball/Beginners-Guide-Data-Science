Title:
Mediterranean Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/679285.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tbsp. olive oil
1 tbsp. prepared mustard
1/2 cup lemon juice
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp. fresh oregano

Directions:
Combine and pour over thin boneless pork slices. Marinate in refrigerator 1-8 hours. Serve on grilled or roasted meat.

Submitter: 
Jill Sander
Description: 
If you've been searching high and low for a marinade that will dress up your meat, this is it. From chicken to beef to fish, it's versatile enough to fit just about any bill.
