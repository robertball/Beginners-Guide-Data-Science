Title:
Double Chocolate Brownie Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package dark chocolate cake mix
2 (3.9 ounce) packages instant sugar free chocolate pudding mix
4 eggs
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup water or coffee
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10 inch Bundt pan. Have all ingredients at room temperature.
In a large bowl, stir together cake mix and pudding mix. Make a well in the center and pour in eggs, sour cream, oil and water. Beat on low speed until blended. Scrape bowl, and beat 4 minutes on medium speed. Stir in chocolate chips. Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake in the preheated oven for 50 to 60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.
Glaze with chocolate  glaze.
Chocolate Glaze:
1 cup heavy cream
8oz chocolate, chopped or 1 1/2 cups mini chocolate chips
2Tbs butter, cut into small pieces.
Heat cream till it reaches boiling point. Remove from heat. Pour over chocolate. Stir gently till chocolate is melted. Stir in butter. Use immediately.
