Title:
Vodka marinated olives Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 13 ounce jar green olives
Vodka

Directions:
Drain olives from jar and give them a good rinse; or not, if you want if dirtier
Add olives back into the jar and replace the brine with vodka
Let olives marinate overnight
Snack on these yum-yums but be careful!!

Submitter: 
LINDA MCLEAN
Description: 
My mother-in-law showed me this tastey little trick years ago. If you love vodka and love olives, you're in for a treat!
