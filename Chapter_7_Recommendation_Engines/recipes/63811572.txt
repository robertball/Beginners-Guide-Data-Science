Title:
Simple Carrot Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1056890.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups carrots, chopped in small chunks
1/2 onion, sliced
4 cloves garlic
1 tsp dried thyme
1/2 tsp dried tarragon
3 cups fat free chicken broth
3 cups water
2 Tbsp heavy cream (optional)

Directions:
Combine the carrots, onion and chicken broth in a large pot along with 3 cups of water, or enough to cover the ingredients.
Smash the garlic cloves; add to the pot, along with the thyme and tarragon. Bring to a boil over high heat.
Reduce heat to medium low. Cover and simmer for 40-50 minutes, or until the carrots are very tender.
Remove from heat; let mixture cool for 10 minutes.Puree with a hand held wand, or mash with a potato masher and whisk until smooth.
Reheat soup to desired temperature. If desired, stir in heavy cream before serving.

Submitter: 
Lee Crowell
Description: 
This soup is low calorie, high flavor comfort food - a favorite in my house on a chilly autumn evening.
