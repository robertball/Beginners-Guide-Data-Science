Title:
Mini Cinnamon Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/741144.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans of crescent rolls
1/2 stick of butter, softened
1/4 cup sugar
1 t cinnamon (I used a bit more)
chopped nuts (optional)
Glaze:
1 cup powdered sugar
2 T milk

Directions:
Separate rolls and spread with butter.
Combine sugar, cinnamon and nuts (could also add raisins) and sprinkle over rolls.
Roll up from short end and cut into 3 slices.
Place in 10" pie plate (or deep dish pampered chef baking dish) cut side down.
Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown in a 350 degree oven.
Let cool slightly.
Mix the powdered sugar & milk together.
Drizzle over rolls.
