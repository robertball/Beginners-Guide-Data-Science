Title:
Blueberry Cornmeal Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups flour
2/3 cup yellow cornmeal
2 tsp. baking powder
2 tsp. baking soda
6 tbsp. vegetable oil
2 large eggs
1 tbsp. vanilla extract
1 tsp. honey
10 tbsp. (1 1/4 sticks) butter, softened
3/4 cup plus 3 tbsp. sugar, divided
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup ricotta cheese
1/3 cup plain yogurt
3 cups fresh blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.
Spray 10" springform pan with cooking spray.
Whisk flour, cornmeal, baking powder and baking soda in a bowl.
In another bowl, whisk together eggs, oil, vanilla and honey.
In another bowl, with an electric mixer, mix together butter, 3/4 cup plus 2 tbsp. sugar and 1 1/2 tsp. salt until creamy.
With mixer running, gradually add egg mixture to butter mixture.
Mix in flour mixture just to blend.
Add ricotta and yogurt and beat on low speed just to blend.
Pour half of batter in pan and scatter half of blueberries over batter.
Spoon remaining batter in dollops and spread to cover blueberries.
Scatter remaining blueberries over top and sprinkle with remaining 1 tbsp. of sugar.
Bake until cake is golden brown and tester/toothpick comes out clean in center - about 1 hour and 15 minutes.
Cool completely in pan on rack.
