Title:
Banana Popsicles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/298206.jpg
Ingredients:
6 bananas
2 lbs yogurt (the big container, easier for dipping)
1 cup granola
12 popsicle sticks

Directions:
Cut peeled banana in half and stick on a popsicle stick.  Dip the banana in yogurt and sprinkle granola all over it.  Place on tin foil, wax paper, etc and freeze for a few hours until hard.
