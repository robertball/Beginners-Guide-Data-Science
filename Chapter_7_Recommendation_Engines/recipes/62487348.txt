Title:
Best Ever Dinner Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tsp instant dry yeast
1 1/2 cup warm water
4 cups unbleached flour
1/2 cup sugar
1 1/2 tsp sea salt
1/3 cup Crisco, softened
1 Egg

Directions:
Mix yeast in warm water.  Pour water in bowl of KitchenAid mixer. Add remaining ingredients.  Using dough hook attachment, mix for 1-2 minutes.Turn dough into greased bowl, cover with damp towel, and let rise until double (about 1 hour). Roll dough into 16 golf ball sized portions.  Place in greased 9x13 inch pan.  Cover and let rise again until doubled in size.  (Before baking you can make a wash of 1 egg mixed with 1 tablespoon water. Brush over the tops of rolls to make a golden shiny glaze if desired.) Bake at 350 degrees until brown, about 18 minutes. Remove from oven. Place on wire rack to cool.
