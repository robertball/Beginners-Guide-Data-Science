Title:
Hot Vegetable Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups hot peppers
4 cups green tomatoes
4 cups onion
4 cups cabbage (optional)
2 tablespoons mustard seed
1 tablespoon celery seed
4 cups vinegar
2 cups water
6 cups sugar
1/2 cup course salt

Directions:
Mix the course salt with the vegetables and let stand overnight.
Rinse thoroughly.
Add vinegar solution.
Cook 15 minutes
Process 10 minutes
