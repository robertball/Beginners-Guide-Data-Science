Title:
Bacon Wrapped Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 chicken breast
2 8oz. packages cream cheese
2 bunches green onion
2 lbs. bacon

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees. Pound your chicken breasts flat. In a mixing bowl mix cream cheese, chives, salt and pepper. Spread about 1/4 cup of cream cheese mixture onto each chickent breast.  Roll chicken breast starting at small end then wrap in bacon (about 4 pieces bacon per breast). Place breasts on a wrack in side of baking dish and bake for 1 hour.
