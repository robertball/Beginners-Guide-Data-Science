Title:
Stuffed Pepper Soup I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 onion, chopped
1 tsp garlic, minced
1 (29 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 (29 ounce) can diced tomatoes
2 cubes beef bouillon cube
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 can beef broth
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 tablespoon soy sauce
2 tablespoons worcestershire
1/4 ketchup
1 cup instant white rice

Directions:
Cook rice in 1 cup beef broth.  Reserve remainder.
In a Dutch oven brown beef over medium high heat. Drain off any fat.
Add the peppers, onion and garlic to the browned meat and saute for 3 minutes.
Stir in the tomato sauce, diced tomatoes with juice, bouillon cubes, brown sugar, ketchup, worcestershire, pepper and soy sauce. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 30 to 45 minutes. Stir in rice and heat through.
