Title:
Baked Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3c cooked, chopped chicken
3 medium celery stalks, diced
1/2 c mayo
1/3 c sour cream
1/3 c toasted almonds, optional
1 chopped scallion
salt and pepper
1/8 t vinegar
1 c shredded cheddar

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in 2qt dish and top with cheese.
Bake 30 minutes at 350.

Submitter: 
Christine Simon Michener
Description: 
Great on it's own or on a sandwich or crackers
