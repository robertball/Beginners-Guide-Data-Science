Title:
Decorator Colored Cream Dough for Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter, softened
1/4 cup granulated sugar
6 tablespoons light cream
1 cup all-purpose flour
Paste or Gel food coloring

Directions:
1. Beat softened butter in a small bowl on medium speed for 30 seconds. Add granulated sugar and beat until combined. Beat in light cream. Add all-purpose flour and beat until smooth. Divide dough into 2 to 3 portions. Color each portion with the food coloring.
2. Place each color in a decorating bag fitted with a writing, rose, star tip etc. Pipe colors on all the unbaked cookies on the cookie sheet. Bake as cookie recipe directs. Once baked, the piped-on designs are durable and the cookies store and freeze well.

Submitter: 
Cat Lady Cyndi
Description: 
This dough allows you to pipe decorative details on sugar cookies before baking. Use any tip you wish, any colors and a pastry bag, and the possibilities are endless.
