Title:
Coconut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flaked, sweetened, Coconut
3/4 cup of Sugar
1 tsp Vanilla
1/4 cup Flour
2 eggs, beaten

Directions:
Mix eggs and Vanilla in a mixing bowl. Add sugar and flour and mix well. Stir in Coconut until well mixed.
Drop by teaspoon full onto a well greased cookie sheet and bake at 350 degrees for 17 minutes. Edges should be brown.
Use a strong, thin bladed spatula to remove from baking sheet. Re-oil the cookie sheet for subsequent trays if making more than one batch.

Submitter: 
kcryss
Description: 
Moist, chewy, delicious cookies that disappear as soon as they leave the oven. I get raves from everyone that has ever tried them. Quick and easy these cookies can be whipped up in a matter of minutes and because they do not spread, you can cook an entire batch on one cookie sheet.
