Title:
Amish Friendship Bread Starter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/570233.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (0.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
1/4 cup warm water (110Â° F)
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup white sugar
1 cup milk

Directions:
In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let stand 10 minutes.
In a 2-quart glass, plastic or ceramic container, mix flour and sugar until well combined.
Slowly stir in milk and dissolved yeast mixture.
Cover loosely with plastic wrap or a dishtowel. Let stand at room temperature until bubbly. This is Day 1 of the 10-day cycle.
For the next 10 days handle starter according to the instructions for Amish Friendship Bread (see footnotes).

Submitter: 
Friendship Bread Kitchen
Description: 
This is the basis of all the wonderful Amish Friendship Bread recipes out there! If you haven't received a bag of Amish Friendship Bread starter but would like to make the bread, this is the recipe for starting your starter.
