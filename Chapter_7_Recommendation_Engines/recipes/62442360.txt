Title:
Na-Na's mini applesauce muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon allspice
1/4 cloves
1/2 cup of oil
1 cup applesauce

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients until blended. Then add oil and applesauce. Mix until blended. Bake in mini muffin pans for approximately 14 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is my great grandmas recipe, that's been an overwhelming favorite for 3 generations now. Its been loved, and unchanged for a 100 years! So easy to make and the kids go crazy!
