Title:
Cornmeal Pancakes Gluten Free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup gluten free cornmeal
1 cup boiling water
1 tablespoon blue agave syrup
1/2 cup all purpose gluten free flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 cup milk
1 egg
1 tablespoon sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Combine cornmeal and boiling water. Let it sit a minute while you preheat your griddle and gather the other ingredients. Add all the other ingredients and whisk them together. Bake on preheated griddle turning when you see permanent bubbles all the way around the edges. Serve with your favorite pancake toppings.

Submitter:

Description: 
Slightly crisp with a soft, almost creamy interior, this is the ultimate gluten free pancake recipe.
