Title:
Jim's Chicken Wings With a Kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds chicken wings
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup Italian-style salad dressing
2 tablespoons Louisiana hot sauce
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Mix the soy sauce, italian dressing, hot sauce, garlic powder,cayenne pepper, red pepper flakes and blsck pepper in a bowl. Reserve half of the mixture.
Place the wings in a zip lock bag and marinate at least 4-6 hours, preferably all night.
Heat grill and cook the wings about 5 minutes on each side. Do not pat the wings dry before you grill them
Take the reserved mixture and use as a dipping sauce

Description: 
This is an old family recipe for people who love wings and want some bite!
