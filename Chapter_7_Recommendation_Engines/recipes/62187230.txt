Title:
FRITO PIE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 FAMILY SIZE BAG OF FRITOS CORN CHIPS
2 16oz CANS NO BEAN, HORMEL CHILI
1 16oz CAN LARGE BLACK,PITTED OLIVES, SLICED
1 CUP WATER
1 32oz BAG SHREDDED CHEDDAR CHEESE

Directions:
IN 13" X 9" GLASS BAKING DISH, POUR ENTIRE BAG OF FRITOS.SPRINKLE OLIVES EVENLY OVER TOP.MIX CHILI WITH WATER AND POUR OVER MIXTURE MAKING SURE TO COAT ALL OF THE FRITOS.EVENLY SPREAD CHEESE OVER TOP OF DISH. BAKE IN 350* OVEN FOR APX. 20 MIN. OR UNTIL CHEESE IS MELTED. SERVES 8, OR 4 IF YOU'RE REALLY HUNGRY.(:
