Title:
Carnival Puffs(Carnevale Sfinge) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
Pinch of salt
3 tsps bakng powder
1/4 cup of sugar
1/2 cup of water
2 cups vegetable oil or canola oil for frying.
1 cup granulated sugar.

Directions:
sift flour, salt, baking powder and sugar together,
Add water to dry ingredients(batter will be sticky)
Heat oil in medium pot to 375F
Dip a tablespoon into a glass of wam water.Take 1 heaping tablespoon of batter and using a rubber scraper, slide it off spoon into the hot oil.
Fry 2 or 3 puffs at a time until golden brown, about 3 minutes. Drain on paper towels, and roll in granulated sugar while still hot. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
BramptonMommyof2
Description: 
These are traditional Italian fried dough treats,made on St.Joseph's Day and Shrove Tuesday. I remember my mom and dad making these in their bakery and at home .I still make these today.Hope you love them as much as we do. Beware ,they are so addictive.
