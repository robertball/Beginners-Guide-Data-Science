Title:
Bourbon Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips
½ C. sugar
3 T. corn syrup
½ C. Bourbon
2 ½ C. crushed vanilla wafers
1 C. finely chopped nuts…I use Walnuts
Extra sugar

Directions:
Melt chocolate and corn syrup in microwave (slowly so that it doesn’t curdle.)
Add sugar.
Blend in bourbon
Combine crushed cookies, chocolate mixture and nuts.
Form into 1” balls and roll in sugar.
Let them sit a few hours before storing.
Store in tin with waxed paper between layers.
