Title:
Onion Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2115486.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tbs extra-virgin olive oil
1/2 large red onion, thinly sliced
Kosher salt
1 cup dry red wine
2 tbs red wine vinegar
2 tsp honey
1 thyme sprig
Freshly ground pepper

Directions:
In a skillet, heat the oil. Add the onion and a pinch of salt and cook over moderately low heat, stirring, until softened, about 10 minutes
Add the red wine, vinegar, honey and thyme and simmer, stirring, until the liquid has evaporated, 15 minutes
Season the onion jam with salt and pepper; discard the thyme.

Submitter: 
Sophie Pipilas
Description: 
An excellent gourmet topping for your favourite burger or sandwich
