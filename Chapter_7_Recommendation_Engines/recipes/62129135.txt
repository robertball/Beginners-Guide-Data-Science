Title:
Worlds Best Steak Sandwhich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tomato, sliced
1 avacado, sliced
2 sandwhich rolls
1 tsp garlic powder
margerine or softened butter
1/4 cup fresh spinach leaves
1 tbls vegetable oil
4 very thin cut steaks
8 pieces fresh cut italian mozerella
2 tsp balsalmic vinegar

Directions:
preheat broiler
slice open rolls
spread with margerine or butter
sprinkle with garlic powder
broil until edges are slightly burnt, set aside
heat frying pan on high
add oil
flash brown meat
lower temp., continue cooking until desired doneness
on one side of the rolls layer spinach, avacado, tomato
sprinkle with balsalmic
on the other side of the rolls layer cheese and steak
close sandwhich, bon appetit!

Submitter: 
Jackie B.
Description: 
It is very easy to make, and oh-so good. I've eaten it two days in a row, but you may have a hard time keeping it together, or fitting it in your mouth!
