Title:
Wild Rocket (Arugula) & Parmesan Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/848830.jpg
Ingredients:
2 bags of wild rocket/arugula (enough to serve 4 people)
1/4 cup roughly chopped cilantro
1 tsp fresh lemon juice
1 tsp Olive Oil
1 tsp Balsamic Vinegar
1 tsp Red Pepper Flakes OR fresh finely chopped red chili pepper
pinch of Black Pepper
1/4 cup shaved parmesan

Directions:
In a large salad bowl, mix arugula and roughly chopped cilantro.
Drizzle with lemon juice, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar.  Add red pepper flakes and pinch of black pepper.  Toss salad and increase seasonings as desired (cilantro, lemon juice, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, red pepper flakes, pepper).
After you are happy with the seasoning, add freshly grated parmesan flakes and lightly toss.

Description: 
Quick, easy, healthy, looks like the salad dishes you'd find in an upscale restaurant.  You can serve it as a salad before the meal, or on the plate with the meal.
