Title:
Cheesy Mushroom Bundles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 button mushrooms, one inch in diameter
1 puff pastry sheet, thawed
1/4 cup chive and onion cream cheese
1 egg, slightly beaten

Directions:
Heat oven to 400. Remove stems from mushrooms. Fill each mushroom with 1/2 tsp cream cheese.
On lightly floured surface, roll puff pastry to 15x10 inch rectangle. Cut into 24 (2 1/2 inch) squares.
For each bundle, place 1 filled mushroom in center of 1 pastry square. Draw up 4 corners to form a small bundle; pinch firmly to form small knob. Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Brush with egg.
Bake at 400 for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown. Cool 5 minutes before serving
