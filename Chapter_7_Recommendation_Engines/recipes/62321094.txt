Title:
Oven Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 pounds beef roast, cut into 1" pieces
2-4 medium potatoes, chopped
at least 4 carrots, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1-2 stalks celery, chopped
2 cups tomato juice
2 Tbsp. sugar
2 tsp. salt
3 Tbsp. minute tapioca

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees F.
Put beef, potatoes, carrots, onions and celery in a 9x13 pan.
Mix remaining ingredients in a small bowl.  Pour over meat and vegetables.
Cover tightly with foil.
Bake 5 hours.

An easy hearty stew that's great on a cold night!
