Title:
Summer Squash Kebabs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup cubed yellow squash
1 tsp olive oil
rosemary
1/2 cup steamed quinoa
lemon zest

Directions:
Arrange squash wedges onto skewers. Brush with olive oil, sprinkle with rosemary, and grill until done.
Serve with quinoa and grated lemon zest to taste.
