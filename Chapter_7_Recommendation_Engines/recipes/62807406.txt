Title:
Everclear Grandma's Apple Pie "Ala Mode" Moonshine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/659501.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Gallon Apple Cider
1/2 Gallon Apple juice
4 Cinnamon Sticks
1 Clove
1 Cup Granulated Sugar
1 Cup Brown Sugar
3 cups Everclear
1 Pint Vanilla Vodka

Directions:
bring to boil cider, juice, cinnamon, clove, and sugars. Reduce heat and simmer low 20 minutes. remove from heat and cool completely. stir in booze. bottle and refrigerate
