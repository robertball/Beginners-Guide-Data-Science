Title:
Hot Pepper Jelly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 c. - whole pickled jalapeno peppers, drained, seeded, and finely chopped (1 jar 10/14 oz peppers)
2 1/2 c. - bottled apple juice
1/4 c. - apple cider vinegar
5 1/2 c. - sugar
1 box powdered sure-jel

Directions:
Chop peppers in processor. Put in heavy saucepan, mix in 1 box of powdered sure-jel (no lumps). Heat on stove until can not stir down the boil. Add sugar all at once. Bring to a full boil again and boil for 1 minute, stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Fill canning jars to 1 inch from top. Screw down tightly. Cover with towel and let cool completely. Do not move jar until set.

Submitter: 
Brad Miller
Description: 
I have only tried this with Jalapenos peppers so far but I think it would be good with any kind of hot peppers.
