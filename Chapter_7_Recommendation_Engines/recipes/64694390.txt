Title:
Simple Crab and Shrimp Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5218354.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb.  flake style imitation crab meat
1/2 lb. cooked shrimp
3 hard boiled eggs
2 green onions
1 tbsp chopped dill
1/4  cup petite white corn
1/4 cup petite green peas
1/4 cup sour cream
1/4 cup mayonnaise
6 single shrimp/tail on for garnish
fresh chopped dill for garnish
cherry tomatoes for garnish
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Chop crab and shrimp into small bite size.
Chop hard boiled eggs, green onions and fresh dill.
In large mixing bowl add the chopped crab, chopped shrimp, eggs, green onion, dill corn and peas.
In separate  bowl, combine sour cream and mayonnaise.
Add mayonnaise/sour cream mixture to bowl of vegetables and seafood and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
Sahara B
Description: 
Simple crab and shrimp salad that will fulfill your needs. Light and right on carb levels....Enjoy this simple dish..use light mayo and light sour cream to cut even more.
