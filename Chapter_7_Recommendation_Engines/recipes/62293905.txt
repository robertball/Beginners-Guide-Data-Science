Title:
Taco Terrific Layered Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/400622.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground meat (You can use chicken, turkey, pork or beef)
3 c. shredded cheese (Colby is always good)
1 can refried beans (use the one with jalapenos for a little kick)
16 oz sour cream
16 oz guacamole (your own or the premade at the store)
1 5oz can sliced black olives (mostly for decoration)
1/4 c. taco sauce
1 pkg taco mix

Directions:
Brown meat. Rinse off grease. Put back in skillet and add taco mix.
Add refried beans and mix thoroughly.
In a 9x13 casserole dish, line bottom with all meat mix.
Layer as follows:
1 c. cheese
Guacamole
1 c. cheese
Sour Cream
Taco sauce dripped on Sour Cream
1 c. Cheese
Sprinkle with black olives
Serve with tortilla chips, Fritos or even just tortillas.

Submitter: 
Katy Blaylock
Description: 
I use this for parties! Also great if you want to serve it with warm flour tortillas to make tacos if you have left overs!
