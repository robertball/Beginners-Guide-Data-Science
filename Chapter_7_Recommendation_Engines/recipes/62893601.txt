Title:
Spicy Stir Fry Broccoli And Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/699730.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon of Gochujang Paste (fermented Korean red pepper paste) Or 1\4 tsp red pepper
1 1/2 cups Broccoli florets
1 1/2 cups Cauliflower florets
1/2 lb chicken cut into small cubes
2 green onions (1 inch long pieces)
1 tablespoon of garlic
1 tablespoon of ginger
3 mushrooms
Finishing sauce
1/4 cup of Chicken stock
1 tablespoon of soy sauce
1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
1 tablespoon of hoisin sauce
1 tablespoon of dry sherry (optional)
1 1/2 teaspoons of corn starch
1 tablespoon of sesame seed oil

Directions:
Cook Chicken, Set Chicken A Side
In A Fry Pan With Small Amount Of Vegetable Oil
Add Carrots
Add Garlic, And Ginger
Add Green Onions
Add Broccoli
Add Cauliflower
Add Mushrooms
Add Cooked Chicken
Add sauce, And allow to thicken
