Title:
Deadly Dippers and Swamp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package(s) 8oz. softened reduced fat cream cheese
3/4 cup(s) shredded pepperjack cheese
3 scallions
1/2 cup(s) fresh cilantro leaves
1 tablespoon(s) lime juice
1/2 teaspoon(s) garlic salt

Directions:
Put all items in a food processor until smooth. Scrape into a bowl; cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour, or up to 1 day.
Heat oven to 375 degrees. Using Halloween cookie cutters, cut shapes from various flavored flour tortillas. Plae on baking sheet and bake 8-10 minutes until crisp. Cool; store in an airtight container at room tempature. Serve with dip.
