Title:
Fayes Chocolate Mayonnaise Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 cup sugar
2 teaspoons baking soda
½ teaspoon salt
½ cup powdered cocoa
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup water
2 teaspoons vanilla

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients together. Add remaining ingredients. Bake ate 350 for approximately 35 minutes. Frosting when cooled.

Submitter:

Description: 
My mom use to make this when I was growing up. It is a very moist chocolate cake. We use to eat it warm with peaches.
