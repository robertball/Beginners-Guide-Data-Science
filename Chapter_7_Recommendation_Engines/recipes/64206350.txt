Title:
Autumn Applesauce Spice Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1597095.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups of all purpose flour
1 teaspoon of salt
4 teaspoons of baking powder
2 tablespoons of white sugar
1 teaspoon of cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon of ginger
1/4 teaspoon of ground cloves or allspice
2 eggs, beaten
1 1/2 cups of warm milk
2 tablespoons of melted butter
3/4 cup of Mott's Applesauce
1 teaspoon of vanilla
TOPPING:
1 can of apple or cherry pie filling
1 cup of water

Directions:
Heat waffle iron according to directions.
In a large bowl, mix all the dry ingredients, flour salt, baking powder, sugar, and spices.
In a separate bowl, beat eggs,then stir in milk, melted butter, applesauce, and vanilla.
Batter will be thick.
Brush waffle iron generously with oil or melted butter, or spray with an oil spray.
Ladle batter into the preheated oiled waffle iron. Cook til crispy, generally 4-6 minutes.
Add heated topping (can of pie filling plus 1 cup water to thin) and finished with a dollop of fresh whip cream.
