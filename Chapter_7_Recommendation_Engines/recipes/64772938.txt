Title:
Red Beans & Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag dried red kidney beans, soaked overnight
2 large onions
6 cups water
1 pound Polska Kielbasa Sausage, cut into bite size pieces
3 tablespoons bacon drippings
3 cloves garlic
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flake

Directions:
1. Drain and rinse the beans.
2. In a large pot, heat bacon grease and sear meat over medium heat unitl thoroughly heated.
3. Add onions and cook almost on high, stiring until the onions are tender.
4. Add beans, water, and spices. You can use real garlic or garlic powder.
5. Reduce the heat to medium or medium low . Beans should gently bubble.
6. Without a pressure cooker, cook 1-2 hours, watching the liquid level, and without a top, until you have the consistancy you want.
