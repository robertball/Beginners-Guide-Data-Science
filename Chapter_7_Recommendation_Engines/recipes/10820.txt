Title:
Ranger Cookies III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7814667.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups butter
1 1/2 cups white sugar
1 1/2 cups packed brown sugar
3 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
3/4 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 cups all-purpose flour
3 cups rolled oats
2 cups raisins
1 cup flaked coconut
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Cream butter with sugars; beat in eggs and vanilla.  Stir in remaining ingredients.
Bake on ungreased cookie sheet for 8-10 minutes at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

Submitter: 
Rhonda
Description: 
Drop cookies with raisins, coconut, nuts and oatmeal.  Delicious!
