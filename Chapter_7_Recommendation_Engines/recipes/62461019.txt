Title:
Sweet potato, Herb, and Chicken soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Leftover rotisserie chicken simmer to fall off bone
Diced sweet potato
1 qt organic chicken broth
Chopped green onions  ¼ c
Sweet green pepper  ½ c chopped
Baby Bella Mushrooms sliced 1 cup
Celery leaves ¼ cup
Herbs
Marjoram  tsp dried
Basil 3 large leaves
Rosemary 1 sprig
Sage  5 leaves
Sea salt ½ tsp
Pepper ¼ tsp
Parsley Tbsp dried
Curry powder ½ tsp
Oregano small handful fresh leaves
Paprika ½ tsp

Directions:
Cook on med/low heat until sweet potatoes are tender.
That simple!

Submitter: 
Lynda
Description: 
I always have leftover rotisserie chicken after the company leaves.  I am on a very strict diet for inflammation, so using the items I can have I tossed this together and was rather tasty!
