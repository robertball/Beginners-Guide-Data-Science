Title:
Baklava, THE BEST YOU HAVE EVER HAD! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1120065.jpg
Ingredients:
Filling:
2 pounds of walnuts,ground fine in  the food processor
2 pounds of Phyllo dough,defrosted
1 cup white sugar
1 1/2  teaspoons cinnamon
5 sticks melted butter
Honey Syrup:
1 cup white sugar
1 cup light Karo  syrup
1/4 cup honey
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 cup water

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 300 degrees
Dough Directions:
Mix ground walnuts,white sugar and cinnamon in a large mixing bowl
Butter the sides and bottom of an 11x16 baking pan
Place 1 sheet of phyllo in the pan,and using a soft brush coat the sheet with melted butter
Do this to 4 sheets of phyllo to create the base of your Baklava
Apply a layer of ground walnuts
Take 3 sheets of phyllo and butter each one and place on top of one another
Every sheet of phyllo must be buttered before the next sheet is placed on top
Apply a layer of ground walnuts
Repeat 3 sheets of buttered Phyllo
Apply a layer of walnuts
Use 4 sheets of buttered Phyllo dough to make the top of the Baklava
Cut from corner to corner to create diagonal pieces of baklava
Cut multiple rows approximately 1 inch wide
Use care when cutting as the dough will stick to the knife
Place in the oven at 300 degree for 60-90 minutes, until golden brown and flaky
When finished baking remove from the oven and immediately pour the honey syrup over the entire tray
Let stand overnight to absorb the syrup
Honey Syrup directions:
Combine all Honey syrup ingredients  in a sauce pan and bring to a boil
Let cool
Pour over hot Baklava when  it is removed from the oven
