Title:
Semi-Indulgent No-Bake Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz fat-free frozen whipped topping, thawed
8 oz low-fat cream cheese, room temperature
low-sugar chocolate syrup (to taste)
1 tablespoon vanilla
1 chocolate cookie crumb crust

Directions:
On low speed, mix vanilla and cream cheese together until fluffy. Begin adding chocolate syrup to taste (until the mixture acquires a good brown color). Fold in whipped topping. Scoop mixture into cookie crumb crust, and chill for at least 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
JARRIE
Description: 
This no bake cheesecake is the simplest I have ever come across, bar none, and it's delicious too!
