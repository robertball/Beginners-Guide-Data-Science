Title:
Peach Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978877.jpg
Ingredients:
6 ripe peaches, peeled and sliced
1 (9 inch) unbaked deep-dish pastry shell
1/2 cup sugar
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup whipping cream

Directions:
Arrange peaches in the pastry shell. In a bowl, combine sugar, flour and salt; stir in cream until smooth. Pour over peaches. Bake at 400 degrees F for 40-45 minutes or until filling is almost set. Serve warm or cold. Refrigerate leftovers.

Submitter: 
Karen  Odom
Description: 
My family loves this dessert and asks for it often. It's a breeze to make and is delicious served warm or cold.
