Title:
Chicken w/Apricot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Bonless skinless chicken breast(about 1 1/4 Lbs)
3/4 tsp salt divided
1/4 tsp ground pepper
1/4 tsp cheyenne pepper
1/4 C all purpose flour
1 Tbs canola oil
3/4 C white wine
1 med. shallot minced
4 fresh apricots, pitted and chopped ( can sub dried if needed)
2 Tbs apricot preserves
1/2 tsp dried tarragon

Directions:
Place chicken breasts between 2 pieces of plastic wrap. Pound thin using a rollin pin,mallet,or heavy pan. Until about 1/2 in thickness. Sprinkle with 1/4tsp salt and pepper. Place flour in a shallow dish dredge chicken in flour, shaking off excess.
Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chicken and cook until brown.(3-5min per side)Transfer to plate and keep warm.
Off the heat add wine and shallots to the pan. Over medium heat cook,scraping up any browned bits.Let slightly reduce(about 3 min).
Add apricots and cook until the fruit begins to break down.Stir in preserves,tarragon,cheyenne pepper
and remaining salt. Place chicken back into pan can cook until heat through. Serve with Sauce.
