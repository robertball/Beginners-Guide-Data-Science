Title:
Blueberry Pudding Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2. c fresh or frozen blueberries
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp lemon juice
1 c flour
3/4 c sugar
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 c milk
3 tbsp butter, melted
--
3/4 c sugar
1 tbsp cornstarch
1 c boiling water

Directions:
Toss blueberries with cinnamon and lemon juice; place in greased 8" baking dish.
In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, and baking powder; stir in milk and butter.
Spoon over berries.
Combine sugar and cornstarch; sprinkle over batter.
Slowly pour boiling water over all.
Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes, until cake tests done.
