Title:
Rosemary Lime Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 1/4 cups graham cracker crumbs
3/4 cup finely chopped pecans
1/4 cup sugar
1 T. finely chopped rosemary
1/3 cup butter, melted
Filling:
3 (8 oz.) pkgs. Neufchatel cheese
1 cup sugar
3 large eggs
2 T. lime juice
1 T. grated lime peel
1 T. finely chopped rosemary
3 T. all purpose flour
Topping:
1 1/2 cups sour cream
1/4 cup sugar
1 T. grated lime peel

Directions:
In bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, pecans, sugar, and rosemary; stir in butter.  Press into a 9" springform pan, set aside.
In a mixing bowl, combine, Neufchatel cheese, and sugar; beat until smooth.  Add eggs, beat until combined.  Add lime juice, lime peel, rosemary, and flour, beat until blended.  Pour into crust.
Bake at 350 for 55 minutes, or until crust is almost set.  Remove from oven, let cool 5 minutes.  Combine topping ingredients, spread over cheesecake.  Return to oven for 5 minutes.  Cool on wire rack for 10 minutes.
Carefully run knife around the sides of the pan to loosen, cool at least 1 hour.  Refrigerate overnight.  Remove sides of pan.  Let sit 1/2 hour before serving.

Description: 
Go green with the tastes of rosemary and lime in this wonderful cheesecake.  The tastes compliment each other.
