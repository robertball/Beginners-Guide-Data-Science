Title:
Sugar Cookie Frosting peanut butter flavored Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups confectioners' sugar
4 tablespoons creamy peanut butter
5 tablespoons milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon butter
1 tablespoon sweetex

Directions:
In a large bowl, cream together the confectioners' sugar and butter, shortening until smooth. Add peanut butter.  Gradually mix in the milk and vanilla with an electric mixer until smooth and stiff, about 5 minutes. You may need more or less milk.
