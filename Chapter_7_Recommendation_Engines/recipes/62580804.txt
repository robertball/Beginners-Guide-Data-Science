Title:
Macaroni & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
small onion, chopped
2 tablespoons flour
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1 cups milk
1 1/2 cups extra sharp cheddar cheese, grated
1/4 teaspoon salt
dash of ground pepper
7 ounce package macaroni
1 tablespoon butter (smart balance)
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

Directions:
1. Cook macaroni as directed on the box.
2. While macaroni is cooking, heat butter in saucepan over low heat. Saute onion.
3. When onion is translucent, stir in flour, salt, and mustard. cook over low heat,stirring constantly until butter is absorbed.
4. Remove from heat and gradually stir in milk.
5. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil and stir for one minute.
6. Stir cheese until melted. Add macaroni, stirring u.ntil heated through.
