Title:
Pumpkin Butter Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp cloves
1 egg beaten
1/4 cup butter melted
1 1/2 cup pumpkin butter, divided into two 3/4 cups portions
1/2 cup apple juice or cider
1/2 cup raisins (optional)
1/2 cup toasted walnuts (optional)

Directions:
1.  Combine all the dry ingredients (flour, sugar, salt, soda, powder, and spices) in a large bowl and whisk together.
2.  Combine 3/4 cup pumpkin butter, apple juice or cider, beaten egg, melted butter and whisk together.
3. Grease a loaf pan and pour in half the batter.  Then gently spoon the remaining 3/4 cup pumpkin butter over the batter.  Gently add the rest of the batter.
4.  Cook in a 350 degree oven for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
5.  Be sure to cook on a middle rack!

Submitter: 
khannon
Description: 
I had some extra pumpkin butter and came up with this recipe.  This delicious, moist bread is flavorful and fragrant; the smell when cooking reminds me of Thanksgiving!
