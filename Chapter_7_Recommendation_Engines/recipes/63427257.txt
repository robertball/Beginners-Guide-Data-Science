Title:
Raspberry Wonder Danish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Phyllo dough / puff pastry
Raspberries
Sugar
Cornstarch
lemon juice

Directions:
Heat raspberries, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice until boiling and thick.  Lightly puree mixture with fork or immersion blender.
Fill phyllo dough
Bake according to phyllo dough package directions.
