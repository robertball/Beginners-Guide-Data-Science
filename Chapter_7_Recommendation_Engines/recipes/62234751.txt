Title:
Tangy Pork Steaks. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Pork Steaks
1/4 cup Heinz 57
3 Tb Brown sugar
5 tsp White Vinegar
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 medium onion chopped thinly

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except Pork Steaks and onions in a bowl. Layer onions on pork. Brush liberally with sauce. Let sit one hour. Bake at 350 for a half hour or until pork comes to temprature.

I needed a tangy pork chop recipe for a chinese craving. They aren't chinese but still very good.
