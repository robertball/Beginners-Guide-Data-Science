Title:
Marlene's Chicken Waldorf Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1032070.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups cubed, cooked chicken breast
1 (20 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained
1 Red Delicious apple, chopped
1 Golden Delicious apple, chopped
1 cup sliced celery
1/2 cup seedless red grapes
1/2 cup seedless green grapes
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
3/4 cup milk
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1 (1 ounce) packet ranch dressing mix
6 leaves lettuce, or as needed

Directions:
Combine chicken, pineapple, Red Delicious apple, Golden Delicious apple, celery, red grapes, green grapes, and walnuts in a large bowl.
Whisk milk, mayonnaise, and ranch dressing mix together in a separate bowl until well blended.
Pour ranch dressing over chicken mixture; toss gently to coat.
Serve salad on a bed of lettuce leaves.

Submitter: 
Jersey Tomato
Description: 
A delicious version of a classic recipe! Make sure the apples and grapes are sweet. Halve the grapes if they are very large. This salad recipe is much requested by those to whom I have served it!
