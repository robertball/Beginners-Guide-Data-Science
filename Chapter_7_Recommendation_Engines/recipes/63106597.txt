Title:
Chicken Tortilla Snacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 13 oz. can chicken breast
1/2 cup refried beans
1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
2 tablespoons sour cream
3/4 cup guacamole
1/4 cup salsa
6 large tortillas
suggested toppings: chopped tomatoes, onions, green peppers, jalapenos, salsa, sour cream, guacamole

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
In a bowl, mix the cream cheese and sour cream. Mix in the salsa and shredded chicken.
Place two tortillas on a cookie sheet. Spread 1/4 cup refried beans on each tortilla. On top of the beans sprinkle 1/2 cup cheese for each tortilla. Place another tortilla on top of each stack and spread half the chicken mixture onto each.
On the final two tortillas spread half the guacamole on each. Then place the guacamole side down on the chicken mixture of the stacks. Sprinkle 1/2 cup cheese on each stack.
Bake for 10 minutes at 425.
Cut stacks into wedges and serve with suggested toppings.
