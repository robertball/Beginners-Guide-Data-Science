Title:
Cut-Out Almond Cookies with Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-Cookies-
1-1/2 bups butter softened
2 cups white sugar
4 eggs beaten
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 tsp almond extract
5 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
-Icing-
4 cups confectioners sugar
1/2 cup crisco
5 tbsp milk
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 tsp almond extract
Mix all ingredients and add the food coloring of your choice.
*this icing sets up very nice

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar together until well mixed.
Beat in eggs, vanilla and almond extract.  Stir in flour, salt, baking powder.  Cover and chill 1 hour or overnight.
Bake at 400 for 6-8 minutes.  Cool on wire rack and ice.
