Title:
Salsa Fresca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1093033.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large can tomatoes, drained and chopped, or several fresh tomatoes, chopped
2 small cans green chilies, chopped
1/2 medium onion, chopped
2 tablespoons white vinegar
1/2 tablespoon dried parsley or 1 tablespoon fresh
1 or 2 tablespoons hot sauce

Directions:
Mix all together. Chill.
