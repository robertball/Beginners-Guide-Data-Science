Title:
Mexican Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb skinless, boneless chicken breast (cubed)
1 tsp cooking oil
1/2 cup onions (diced)
1/2 cup green pepper (diced)
1 cup chicken broth
1 cup salsa
1 small can corn (drained)
1 cup minute rice
3/4 cup cheddar cheese

Directions:
in large skillet, saute onions, peppers, and chicken in oil till chicken is done.  mix with salsa, chicken broth, and corn in a medium pot and bring to a boil.  reduce heat and add rice.  cover 5 minutes till rice is tender, and add cheese on top.  cover till cheese is melted, and enjoy! add salt and pepper to taste.
