Title:
Country Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
1 can (14oz) diced tomatoes with garlic and onion
1 can (14 oz) chicken broth
1 can (15 oz) sweet corn, drained (or frozen corn)
1 can (8 oz) peas, drained (or frozen peas)
1 cup uncooked instant rice

Directions:
Cook chicken in 1 tbsp oil in skillet, 5-7 minutes.
Stir in undrained tomatoes, broth and 1/2 cup water.
Bring to a boil.
Stir in corn, peas and rice.
Cover and remove from heat. Let stand 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Christal Kuchera
Description: 
A reasonably healthy meal that is easy to make in a short amount of time.
