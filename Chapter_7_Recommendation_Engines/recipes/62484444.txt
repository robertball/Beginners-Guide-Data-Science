Title:
Family Enchiladas with Fried egg Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/488013.jpg
Ingredients:
8 Corn tortillas
1 C onion chopped
1 1/2 C red enchilada sauce
2 bay leaves
1 Tbls,garlic salt
2 C sharp cheddar cheese shredded
4 eggs fried,salt and pepper to taste
oil for frying
Garnish  with > green onion,sour cream, cilantro, tomato,black olives

Directions:
Simmer enchilada sauce with bay leave and garlic salt,reduce a few minutes till a little thicker. Remove bay leave. Fry the tortillas, dip in the sauce and fry again (with the sauce on)for a few minutes. Lay on a plate and add chopped onion and cheese. While you are frying the egg, pop enchiladas in the nuker and melt cheese. Top with egg. Garnish with your choice of toppings.
