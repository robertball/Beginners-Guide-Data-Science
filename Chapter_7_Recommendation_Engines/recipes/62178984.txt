Title:
Cathy H's French Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289583.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups hot water
1 TBL yeast
1 TBL sugar
proof the yeast...
1 tsp salt
4 cups flour

Directions:
put yeast, sugar and water in mixer bowl, and let sit 5 min to proof
Add 1 tsp salt, & 3 cups flour
Mix and slowly add 1 more cup flour.
Knead and add flour as needed.
Rise approx 1 hour in greased, covered bowl.
Shape into loaves and let rise, covered with cloth about 1/2 hr in french bread rack
Bake 20 min @ 400

Submitter: 
Kim Snyder
Description: 
A hearty French bread recipe that is great for dipping in soups, chili or making into bread chips for dipModified from: Cathy Hensler's French Bread
