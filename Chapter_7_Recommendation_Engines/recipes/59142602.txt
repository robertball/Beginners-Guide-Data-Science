Title:
Gluten Free German Potato Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 tablespoons matzoh meal
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
6 medium potatoes, peeled and grated
1/2 cup very finely chopped onion
1/4 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
In a large bowl, beat together eggs,  matzoh meal , baking powder, salt, and pepper. Mix in potatoes and onion.
1 egg per 2 cups of shredded potatoes
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. In batches, drop heaping tablespoonfuls of the potato mixture into the skillet. Press to flatten. Cook about 3 minutes on each side, until browned and crisp. Drain on paper towels.

Submitter: 
Sharon
Description: 
These are a nice change from regular pancakes. They make a great dinner meal when served with bratwurst sausage. I spread mine with cranberry sauce and top with maple syrup.Modified from: German Potato Pancakes
