Title:
Stuffed Peppers with Tuna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium red peppers
salt
1 1/2 cups cooked rice
2 cans tuna, drained and flaked
1/2 cup chopped celery
2 Tbsp finely chopped onion
dash pepper
3/4 cup mayonnaise
Panko crumbs

Directions:
Cut peppers in half lengthwise; remove tops and seeds. Cook peppers in a small amount of boiling salted water for about 5 minutes; drain.
Place peppers in a shallow baking dish. In a bowl, combine rice with tuna, celery, onion and dash pepper. Stir in mayonnaise until well blended.
Spoon tuna rice mixture into pepper shells. Pour about 1/2 cup of water into the baking dish around stuffed peppers. Sprinkle Panko crumbs over top of each stuffed pepper. Bake stuffed peppers at 350Â° for 30 minutes.

Delicious tuna spin on stuffed peppers.  Even kids love this dish!
