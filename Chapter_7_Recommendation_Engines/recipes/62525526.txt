Title:
Mexican Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/514979.jpg
Ingredients:
4-6 Potatoes, boiled and diced
1 cup celery, chopped
1 onion, diced
2 Tablespoons butter
1 can cream of celery soup
3 cups chicken stock
1 4 oz. jar pimentos
1 cup Velveeta, diced
salt and pepper

Directions:
On medium heat, melt butter in 5 quart pan.  Add celery and onion and saute about five minutes.  Add one cup stock and stir.  Then mix in cream of celery soup.  Add potatoes, pimentos, and rest of the stock.  Simmer for about 15-20 minutes. Add Velveeta,and salt and pepper to taste.  Enjoy!
