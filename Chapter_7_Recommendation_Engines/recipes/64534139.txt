Title:
Cornbread Pepper Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3081330.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2c all purpose flour (I use gf)
1/2c cornmeal
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4c grated Parmigiano Reggiano
1 tsp smoked paprika
1 tsp crushed chipotle
2Tbsp + 2tsp vegetable oil
1 egg
1c buttermilk
1/4c red bell pepper, chopped
1 jalapeno, chopped
oil for waffle iron

Directions:
Preheat waffle iron.
Mix together: flour, cornmeal, baking powder, baking soda, cheese, smoked paprika, and chipotle.
Mix together: oil, egg, and buttermilk.
Mix the two bowls together.
Fold in the bell pepper and jalape√±o.
Add the batter to the waffle iron according to the manufacturer's instructions.
