Title:
cheesy chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 chicken breast boiled and shredded
1 cup of sour cream
1 can of condensed cream of chicken soup
2 cups of cheddar cheese
1 sleeve of Ritz crackers
1/2 cup butter

Directions:
1. boil and shred chicken
2. mix soup and sour cream
3. add cheese and chicken, mix
4. place in 9 x 13 pan
5. crumble Ritz over the top
6. melt butter and pour over Ritz
7. Cover and bake at 350 for 45 minutes or until bubbling
