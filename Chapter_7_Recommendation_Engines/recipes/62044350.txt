Title:
Chocolate No Bake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk
4 tbsp. cocoa
1 stick butter
2 cups white sugar
2 1/4 cups oatmeal
1/2 cup peanut butter
2 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Bring to a rolling boil:
1/2 cup milk
4 tbsp. cocoa
1 stick butter
2 cups white sugar -
Boil 1 1/2  mins. Remove from heat and add:
2 1/4 cups oatmeal
1/2 cup peanut butter
2 tsp. vanilla
Mix well and stir until it starts to thicken. Drop by spoonfulls on wax paper to cool.
