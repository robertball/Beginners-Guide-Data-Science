Title:
Incredible Ham Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint Guiness Beer
1 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoons prepared mustard

Directions:
Mix all 3 ingredients with a mixer or whisk. Baste the ham all over generously with all the glaze. As the ham bakes baste every 1/2 hour with the juices in the bottom of the pan. As the ham bakes and  the juice mixes with the glaze...Delicious!

Submitter: 
JimChicago52
Description: 
This will surprise everyone! Dare your guests to guess what is in this glaze!
