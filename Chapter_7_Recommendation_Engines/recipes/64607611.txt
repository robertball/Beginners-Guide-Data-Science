Title:
Roasted Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb crimini (Baby Bella) mushrooms, stemmed
2 Tbsp olive oil
3/4 tsp crushed rosemary
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground pepper
3 garlic cloves, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450, coat a baking sheet with cooking spray
Halve or quarter the mushrooms depending on the size.
In a bowl, toss mushrooms with olive oil, rosemary, salt and pepper. Transfer to prepared baking sheet.
Roast the mushrooms for 10 minutes. Add the garlic and stir to combine.
Roast until the mushrooms are tender and starting to brown, 7-10 minutes. Serve.
