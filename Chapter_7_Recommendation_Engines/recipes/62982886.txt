Title:
Frozen lemonade pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1110586.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups heavy cream
1 14-ounce can sweetened condensed milk (chilled)
1 6-ounce can frozen lemonade
1 9-inch graham cracker crust

Directions:
In a cold medium bowl, whip the cream until stiff peaks form.
In a separate bowl, mix together the condensed milk and frozen lemonade.
Fold the lemonade mixture into the whipped cream.
Pour the filling into the crust and freeze overnight.
Let thaw about 10 minutes before serving.

Description: 
A refreshing, cool, lemony treat.
