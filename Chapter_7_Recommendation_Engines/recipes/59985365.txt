Title:
Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds chuck roast
Montreal Steak seasoning
olive oil
garlic
garlic salt/pepper
salt and pepper to taste
1 packet dry onion soup mix
1 cup beef broth
3 carrots, chopped
1 onion, chopped
5 potatoes, peeled and quartered (or use red potatoes)

Directions:
Take the chuck roast and season with steak seasoning, rub with garlic, salt and pepper to taste. Brown on all sides in olive oil in a large skillet over high heat.
Place in the slow cooker and add the soup mix, broth, carrots, onion, potatoes and 1 more clove minced garlic.
Cover and cook on low setting for 8 to 10 hours. Add garlic salt, reg salt, etc to taste as cooking. If have time, add 1/2 veggies at beginning and then rest halfway through.
For gravy, mix remaining broth with some flour and garlic salt.
