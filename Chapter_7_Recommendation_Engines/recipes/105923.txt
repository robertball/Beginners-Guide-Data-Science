Title:
Bratwurst with Tomato Pepper Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978350.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (19 ounce) package Bob Evans® Bratwurst
1 (10 ounce) basket cherry tomatoes, thinly sliced
1/4 cup diced onion
1/4 cup diced green pepper
1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
2 tablespoons Italian dressing

Directions:
Simmer bratwurst in 1/2-cup water for 15 minutes, turning occasionally and adding more water if necessary.  Remove from skillet.  Preheat grill to medium heat.  Grill bratwurst for 5 to 10 minutes, or until internal temperature registers 160 degrees F.  In small bowl, combine tomatoes, onion, green pepper, thyme and Italian dressing.  Serve alongside bratwurst.

Submitter: 
Bob Evans®
Description: 
Brats served with a beautiful relish made with fresh tomatoes, onions, and peppers; the perfect recipe for summer!
