Title:
Strawberry Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boxes Hostess Twinkies (you need at least 12 Twinkies)
1 - 10oz. carton frozen sliced strawberrires, thawed
12 oz. Cool Whip

Directions:
Line a 9x13 dish with Twinkies.  Spread strawberries over Twinkies.  Cover with Cool Whip.
Refrigerate overnight.
