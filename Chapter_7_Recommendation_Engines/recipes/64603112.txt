Title:
Pita bread with bread machine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 oz water
1 1/2 Tbsp Olive Oil
1 1/2 tsp salt
2 1/4 tsp sugar
4 1/3 cup flour
2 1/4 tsp yeast

Directions:
Put everything into bread machine to make dough
When dough ready, roll out to 12 individual rounds
Bake on wire rack 4-5 mins at 450 degrees
When puffed up, remove from oven and place on cooling rack with a slightly wet kitchen towel over top while cooling
