Title:
Slow Cooker Turkey and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/523431.jpg
Ingredients:
Just wanted to let you guys know a GREAT way to use up that leftover turkey. Turkey and Dumplings in a slow cooker.
Use any left over Turkey(however amount you want)
2 TBS of butter
2 Cans of Cream of Chicken Soup
1/2 onion Dash of garlic
Dash of poultry seasoning
1 can of chicken broth
1/2 package of Grande Biscuit dough
Any vegetables you like(potatoes,carrots etc)

Directions:
Place everything in crockpot BESIDES the biscuit dough. Give it a stir. cook for about 4 hours on high. The last hour, roll out the biscuit dough and cut into squares. Place on the top of the turkey and liquid. Let cook for an hour. DONE! Delicious comfort food while using up that leftover turkey.
