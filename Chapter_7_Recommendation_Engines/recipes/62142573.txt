Title:
Cinnamon Orange Coffee Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C Sifted Flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/8 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 C Sugar
1 tsp Cinnamon
1/2 tsp Nutmeg
1/3 C shortening
2 Tbs. grated orange rind
1/3 C chopped nuts
1 egg; beaten
1/2 C milk

Directions:
Sift Flour, measure and sift again with baking powder, baking soda, salt, sugar and spices into mixing bowl. Cut in shortening until it resembles fine crumbs.  Add orange rind and nuts (if you make ahead, cover bowl tightly and store in refrigerator).
Combine egg and milk.  Add to mixture. Mix in enough to moisten flour.  Spread batter evenly in a grased 9 x 9 pan.  Sprinkle with this topping: 1/2 C sugar, 2 Tbs flour, 2 Tbs butter, and 1/2 Tbs Cinnamon.
Bake at 375 for 25 min.
