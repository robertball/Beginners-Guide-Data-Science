Title:
Bacon Salmon Swirls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/569399.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pieces of bacon, cooked
6 grape tomatoes chopped
2 oz smoked salmon, flaked
1 clove of garlic, minced
2 oz of mayonnaise
3-4 sprigs of chopped chives
salt and pepper to taste
1 sheet of puff pastry

Directions:
Make an aioli of the mayonnaise, minced garlic, chives, salt & pepper. Set aside. Roll out the puff pastry to approx. 12" square. Working quickly, spread the aioli onto the pastry, top with the bacon, tomatoes, and salmon. Roll up jelly roll style and cut into 12 equal pieces. Lay on a parchment lined baking sheet and chill for approx 30 minutes. Bake for 18 minutes in a hot 450 oven.
