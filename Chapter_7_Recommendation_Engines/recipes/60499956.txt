Title:
Big M's Spicy Lime Grilled Prawns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 large tiger prawns, peeled and deveined
2 limes, zested and juiced
2 green chile peppers, seeded and chopped
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1/2 teaspoon honey
pinch of sea salt & fresh ground pepper
1 (1inch) piece fresh ginger root, chopped
1/2 medium onion, coarsely chopped
skewers

Directions:
Place the prawns and lime zest in a large, non-metallic bowl. Place the lime juice, chile pepper, garlic, ginger, onion, honey,salt & pepper in a food processor or blender, and process until smooth. You may need to add a little oil to facilitate blending. Pour over the bowl of prawns, and stir to coat. Cover, and refrigerate for 1/2 to 1 hour.No longer or the marinate will cook the shrimp and they will be tough.
Preheat grill for medium-high heat. Thread prawns onto skewers, piercing each first through the tail, and then the head.
Brush grill grate with oil. Cook prawns for 5 minutes, turning once, or until opaque.

Description: 
Succulent and moist grilled prawns. Serve with salad, potatoes, and bread. Enjoy!Modified from: Big M's Spicy Lime Grilled Prawns
