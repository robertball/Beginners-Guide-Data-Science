Title:
Crunchy Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 celery ribs, diced
1 yellow pepper, diced
1 medium apple, diced
1 small red onion, diced
1 (15 oz) can organic black beans, drained
1 (15 oz) can organic kidney beans, drained
3 tbsp balsamic vinegar
2 tbsp olive oil
2 tsp agave nectar
1/2 tsp salt
Freshly ground pepper
1/4 cup chopped cilantro leaves

Directions:
In a large bowl combine the celery, yellow pepper, apple and beans. Toss with the vinegar, olive oil agave nectar, salt and pepper. Top with the chopped cilantro.

Submitter: 
Kinza
Description: 
Dr. Oz...Beans are a super-lean protein that are high in both fiber and folate. This salad mixes beans with crisp ingredients for a texture twist that is sure to tickle your taste buds.
