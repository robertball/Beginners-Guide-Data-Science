Title:
Strawberry Juice w/ Cucumber Lime Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 limes
Filtered Water
1 cucumber
10 strawberries

Directions:
Juice limes and pout into picture .  Add water (enough to dilute the lime juice) as if making lime-aid.  Juice/blend cucumber and then add to pitcher using strainer.  Juice/blen strawberries and then add to the pitcher.  Add swetener if desired. (Splenda, Agave nectar, honey, Stevia, Truvia or Raw Sugar)

Submitter: 
Shannon Campbell
Description: 
Delicious and light.  Great summertime sipping.
