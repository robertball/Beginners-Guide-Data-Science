Title:
Root Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 rutabaga (diced)
1 potato (diced)
1 onion (diced)
3 large carrots (diced)
1 large chicken breast (diced)
chicken broth
spices to taste: salt, pepper, oregano, thyme, basil

Directions:
Place ingredients in the crockpot. Cover ingredients with chicken broth. Cook in crockpot on high for 5 hours.
