Title:
Sweet & Sour Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 Cups White Rice
Fruity Sweet and Sour Sauce, doubled (allrecipes.com)
Oil
Onion, diced
Carrots, sliced
2 Bell Peppers, diced
pineapple tidbits (juice used in sauce)
2 Bags Frozen Tempura Chicken

Directions:
Steam rice.  Bake chicken as directed by package.  Make Fruity Sweet and Sour Sauce.
Heat oil in skillet.  Saute onion, carrots, peppers.
Add pineapple and thickened sauce, simmer 10 minutes.
Add chicken and stir.  Serve over rice.
