Title:
Fall Harvest Crockpot Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 frozen boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 cup chicken broth
2 medium apples, cored and sliced
5 pearl onions, peeled and halved
1 snack size box of raisins
1/4 tsp cloves

Directions:
1. Pour broth into the bottom of your slow cooker.
2. Add the sliced apples, onions and raisins to the broth.
3. Place frozen chicken breasts over the top of the apple/broth combo.
4. Sprinkle the chicken with the cloves.
5. Cover and cook.  5 hours on high or 8 hours on low.

Submitter: 
BRIMAT143
Description: 
This chicken not only tastes wonderful on a fall day but it makes your home smell heavenly.  Cloves add a little kick!
