Title:
Traditional Party Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. butter
1 1/4 t. seasoned salt
4 1/2 T Worcestershire sauce
3 C. each:  corn, rice, wheat Chex
1 C. mixed peanuts

Directions:
Combine butter, salt and sauce and heat in microwave until butter is melted.
Stir well to dissolve seasoned salt.
Pour butter mixture over cereals and mix well.
Dump into shallow pans for quicker toasting.
Bake for at least 1 hour at 325Â° stirring every 15 minutes.
