Title:
Basic Crepes for 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886194.jpg
Ingredients:
! eggs
1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup water
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, whisk the egg.
Add the milk and water and whisk to combine
Add the flour, one tablespoon at a time, and whisk until combined and smooth. Add the salt and butter; beat until smooth.
Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.
Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each crepe.
Tilt the pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface evenly.
Cook the crepe for about 2 minutes, until the bottom is light brown. Loosen with a spatula, turn and cook the other side.
Serve hot.
