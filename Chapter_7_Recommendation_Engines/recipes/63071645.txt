Title:
Amy's Maple Balsamic Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup canola oil
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1 T pure maple syrup
1/8 tsp salt
1/8 tsp black pepper
dash white pepper.

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a container and wish to combine, cool in the fridge for an hour prior to serving to meld flavors.

Submitter: 
ALD0014
Description: 
Sweet and tangy great on a bed of greens with goat or blue cheese.  Simple and delicious.
