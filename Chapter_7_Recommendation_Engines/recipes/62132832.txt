Title:
Hot Corn Meal Breakfast Cereal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups milk
1/2 cup fine corn meal
1 tbs. butter
2 1/2 tbs. sugar
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
1/8 tsp. salt
cinnamon to taste

Directions:
In a medium saucepan add the milk, butter, sugar, cinnamon and salt.
Cook over medium heat for about 3-4 minutes or until butter is completely melted and milk starts to steam.
Slowly whisk in the corn meal till well mixed, making sure there are no lumps.
Stir the cereal till slightly thickened then take it off the heat (as it will continue to thicken as it cools).
Serve in deep bowls and sprinkle cinnamon over cereal to taste.

Submitter: 
VETTY430
Description: 
If you like polenta, you will like this lightly sweetened, creamy cereal.  It's great on cold mornings, but you can enjoy this any time of the year.
