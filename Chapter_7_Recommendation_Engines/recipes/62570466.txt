Title:
Marshmellow Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sugar
3/4 cup margarine
1 cup evaporated milk
12 oz. chocolate chips
2 tsp. vanilla
7 oz. jar of puffed marshmellow
Nuts

Directions:
Boil sugar, margarine and milk for 5 minutes.
Add remaining ingredients.
Pour into a buttered 9X13" pan, cool and cut.
