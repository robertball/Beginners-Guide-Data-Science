Title:
White Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992563.jpg
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon vegetable oil
1 onion, chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
1 cup shredded cooked chicken (from an Archer Farms™ oven roasted chicken)
2 teaspoons Archer Farms™ cumin
2 (15 ounce) cans Great Northern beans, undrained
1 (4.5 ounce) can diced green chiles
1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:
Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat.  Add onion and garlic.  Cook and stir 3 to 5 minutes or until onion is tender.
Stir in chicken, cumin, beans and chiles.  Reduce heat to low.  Cover and cook an additional 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Stir in sour cream.
Ladle into bowls.  Serve chili with a sprinkle of your favorite toppings, such as chopped cilantro, chopped tomatoes, crumbled tortilla chips and shredded Mexican cheese blend.
