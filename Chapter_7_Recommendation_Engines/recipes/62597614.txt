Title:
Bourbon Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz OJ concentrate
12oz lemonade concentrate
2c brewed tea (steep 3 teabags in 2c water for 3 min)
1c sugar
1-1/2 c bourbon
6c water
7-up or other lemon-lime soda

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except soda.
Combine and freeze for at least 36 hours before serving.
Mix as desired with 7-up, more or less depending on strength.
