Title:
Mustard Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4443095.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons Chinese hot prepared mustard
1 3/4 cups sour cream
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1/2 teaspoon dried minced onion flakes
1/4 cup chopped green onions
1/2 teaspoon paprika

Directions:
In a medium bowl, blend Chinese hot prepared mustard, sour cream, mayonnaise, dried minced onion flakes, green onions and paprika. Chill until serving.

Submitter: 
sal
Description: 
A blast of hot Chinese mustard will always liven up the party! This mustard dip packs a wallop.
