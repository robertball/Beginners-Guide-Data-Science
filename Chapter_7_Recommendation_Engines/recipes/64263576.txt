Title:
Caramel Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 qt popped corn
Salt
2 sticks butter
2 c. brown sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 c light corn syrup
1/2 tsp soda

Directions:
Salt popcorn as usual and keep warm in oven. Grease a very large roasting pan.  Bring butter, sugar, vanilla, and syrup for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat and add soda, stir well.  Pour over popcorn and mix well.  Bake at 200 for 1 hour stirring every 15 minutes.
