Title:
Festive Whole Berry Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068476.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
2 cups water
2 12-ounce package Fresh or Frozen Cranberries
Cinnamon & nutmeg

Directions:
Combine water & sugar in a medium saucepan. Bring to boil; add cranberries and spices to taste, return to boil. Reduce heat and boil gently for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Cover and cool completely at room temperature. Refrigerate until serving time.

Description: 
This is a whole berry cranberry sauce with a hint of holiday spices!
