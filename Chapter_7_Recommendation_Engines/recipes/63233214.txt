Title:
Fantastic and Healthy Apple Muffin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup wheat flour
1/4 cup rolled oatmeal
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
1/8 tsp ground cloves
1/8 tsp nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup applesauce
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 1/4 teaspoons vanilla
3 large apples

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Grease or line a 12 cup muffin pan.
3.  In a medium bowl, mix flour, oatmeal, wheat flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves and salt.
4. In a large bowl, beat together apple sauce, sugar and eggs until smooth. Mix in vanilla.
5. Shred 2-1/2 apples with cheese grater.  Chop remaining 1/2 apple and set aside.
6.  Stir in shredded apples, and gradually blend in the flour mixture.  Incorporate chopped apples.
7.  Spoon the mixture into the prepared muffin pan.
8.  Bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a toothpick inserted in the center of a muffin comes out clean. Allow to sit 5 minutes before removing muffins from pan. Cool on a wire rack.

Submitter: 
Chrissi Guarnieri
Description: 
After making a few different apple muffin recipes, I made one that tastes fantastic and is good for you too!  Our kids love them!
