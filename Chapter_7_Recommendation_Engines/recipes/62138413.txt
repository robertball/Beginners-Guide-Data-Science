Title:
Sausage-stuffed Butternut Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 lb. butternut squash
8 oz. pork sausage
2 cloves garlic
1/4 c. onion, chopped
1/2 tsp. parsley
1/4 tsp. red pepper
1/2 tsp. oregano
1/2 c. soft bread crumbs
1/3 c. parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cut squash in half, scoop out seeds.
Roast squash in a shallow pan with water at 375 degrees till tender.
Let squash cool partially.
Meanwhile, fry sausage till brown.  Do not drain fat.
Add onions and garlic to sausage, cook till softened.
Remove flesh from squash, leaving 1/3 inch flesh in the shell to keep it sturdy.
Mix together: squash, sausage mixture, parsley, pepper, oregano, bread crumbs and cheese.
Stuff shell with squash mixture.
Bake squash at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.
