Title:
BBQ Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Rack Baby Back Ribs
Cajun Seaon
Garlic Salt
Black Pepper
Worchester Sauce
Sweet Baby Rays
2 Cups Water

Directions:
Cover tightly with foil.
Bake 4 Hours at 325*.
Remove foil and baste with Sweet Baby Rays on both sides.
Finish on grill or in oven.
