Title:
Lacy French-Fried Onion Rings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 white onions, cut into rings
1 cup flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1 eggs
1 cup milk
1 TBSP vegetable oil

Directions:
Heat deep fat fryer to 375 degrees
Combine flour, baking powder and salt together. Beat egg in a deep bowl until thick and lemon-colored. Add milk, oil and stir in dry ingredients. Mix well. Dip onions in batter and fry until brown.
