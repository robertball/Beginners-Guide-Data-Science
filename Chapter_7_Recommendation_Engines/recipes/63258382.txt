Title:
Creamscicle Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 to 5 peeled, organic oranges
2 to 3 organic pears or apples
1 sweet potato
2 cups ice, optional

Directions:
Process through a juicer one item at a time, then mix juices. To thicken, if desired, put in blender with the ice and blend until smooth.
