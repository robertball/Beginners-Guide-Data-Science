Title:
Nate's Breakfast Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5620555.jpg
Ingredients:
4 small Russet potatoes or 3 cups of Oâ€™Brien Potatoes
3 Tbsp. of butter for potatoes
8 tortillas
4 Tbsp. of butter for tortillas
1 lb. of sausage
1 dozen eggs
1 lb. of bacon
cheese to taste
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Dice potatoes (if using raw potatoes)
Melt 3 Tbsp. of butter in a pan over medium heat
Add potatoes to pan when butter is melted
Add salt and pepper to taste
When done, add to large bowl
Brown and drain sausage
When done, add to large bowl with potatoes
Fry bacon and pat grease off with paper towel
Dice bacon and add to large bowl with potatoes and sausage
Scramble eggs and add to large bowl with potatoes, sausage, and bacon
Stir ingredients in large bowl
Melt 4 Tbsp butter
Brush both sides of tortilla with melted butter
Fry Side 1 for 1-2 minutes. Flip.
Fill with ingredients from large bowl
Add cheese if desired
Serve with salsas and sour cream if desired
Leftovers can be wrapped in tin foil or saran wrap and frozen for an easy to-go breakfast

Submitter: 
Gabudell
Description: 
With bacon, sausage, eggs, and potatoes, this burrito is a hearty meal for breakfast or breakfast at dinner!
