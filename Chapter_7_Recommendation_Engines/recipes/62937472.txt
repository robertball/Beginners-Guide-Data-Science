Title:
Strawberry Cheesecake Milk Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 graham cracker squares
2 cups strawberry-cheesecake ice cream
1/3 cup milk
1 cup fresh sliced strawberries
Whipped cream for topping (optional)
2 Strawberries for topping (Optional)

Directions:
Place the graham cracker crumbs in a ziplock bag and crush using a rolling pin.
Blend ice cream, milk, sliced strawberries and half of graham cracker crumbs in the blender, blend until smooth.
Pour in to two glasses and top each one with a dollop of whipped cream, left over crackers crumbs and a strawberry.
