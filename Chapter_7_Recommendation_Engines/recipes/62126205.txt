Title:
Versatile Spicy Italian Eggplant Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large eggplant, peeled and cut into chunks
4oz plain canned tomato sauce
2 Tbsp Italian seasoning
1 tsp (or to taste) cayenne pepper
2 Tbsp garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a shallow baking pan, combine cayenne pepper, Italian seasoning, salt and pepper, garlic powder and eggplant chunks. Cover with foil. Bake in 400 degree oven for 40 minutes.
Remove foil. Turn oven to 300 degrees. Add tomato sauce. Bake for 15 minutes.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
LovelyRitaMetermaid
Description: 
Roasted eggplant with spicy seasonings baked in sauce.  Simple instructions, very few ingredients. Can be used as a side dish or as pasta sauce!
