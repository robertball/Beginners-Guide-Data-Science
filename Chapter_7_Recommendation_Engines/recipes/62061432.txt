Title:
Waikiki Banana Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C. Shortening
1 Cup Packed Firmly Packed Brown Sugar
1 tsp Vanilla
1/2 t. Lemon extract
1 1 /2 c. All purpose flour
1 1 /2 tsp Baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 Cut chopped nuts
1/3 C. Powdered Sugar
1 Tsp Cinnamon

Directions:
Combine shortening, sugar, flavorings, and bananas in mixing bowl; beat
hard. Sift flour with baking powder and salt; add to first mixture. Mix
well. Stir in nuts. Bake in greased 11x7" pan at 350 degrees for 30 to 35
minutes, or until done (nicely brown. While still warm, cut into bars and
remove from pan. Mix powdered sugar and cinnamon; gently roll the warm bars
in the sugar-cinnamon mixture. Makes 20-24 bars. NO EGGS NEEDED! VERY
MOIST! (I tripled this recipe into a 17x10: Pan or doubled in a 9x13 pan.)
Enjoy!
