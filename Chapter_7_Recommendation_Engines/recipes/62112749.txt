Title:
FRIED CABBAGE AND NOODLES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 LB BACON
1 MEDIUM ONION
1 HEAD OF CABBAGE
1 BAG OF KLUSKI NOODLES
FRANKS RED HOT SAUCE

Directions:
FRY THE BACON AND CHOP IT UP
THROUGH BACK INTO THE GREASE
CUT UP CABBABE AND ONION AND ADD TO GREASE AND SAUTEE
HAVE NOODLES ALREADY COOKED AND ADD
TOP WITH FRANKS RED HOT SAUCE
