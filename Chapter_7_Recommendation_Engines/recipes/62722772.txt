Title:
Oven-Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 T butter or margarine ( ? 1/2 evoo)
1/4 t thyme
1 t paprika
1 t salt
1/4- 1/2 t pepper
1/2 C dry breadcrumbs
1 broiler-fryer chicken or 3 # skinless chicken pieces

Directions:
Melt butter in 9 x 13 in baking pan
Combine bread crumbs and seasonings in bowl or plastic bag
Swish chicken pieces in butter, dredge in crumb mixture, and lay in baking pan.
Bake in hot oven (400 F) until browned, about 30 min.
Reduce heat to 350 F, turn chicken and bake 30 min longer or until tender.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Uses dry breadcrumbs instead of flour.
