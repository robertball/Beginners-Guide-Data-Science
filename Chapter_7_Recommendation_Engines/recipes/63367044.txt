Title:
Mexican Sunset Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup water (70 to 80 degrees)
1/2 cup sour cream
3 Tbsp. chunky salsa
2 Tbsp. plus 1-1/2 tsp. taco seasoning
4-1/2 tsp. sugar
1-1/2 tsp. dried parsley flakes
1 tsp. salt
3-1/3 cups bread flour
1-1/2 tsp. active dry yeast

Directions:
In bread machine pan, place all ingredients in order suggested by manufacturer.  Select basic bread setting.  Choose crust color and loaf size if available.  Bake according to bread machine directions (check dough after 5 minutes of mixing; add 1 to 2 tablespoons of water or flour if needed). Yield: 1 loaf (about 2 pounds).  Editor's Note: Use of the timer feature is not recommended for this recipe.
