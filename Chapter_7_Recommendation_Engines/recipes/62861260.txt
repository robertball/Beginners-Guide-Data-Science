Title:
Chicken Cacciatore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small can pizza sauce
1/4 cup port wine
1/2 tsp salt
2 TBSP dried onion
2 TBSP dried green pepper
2 TBSP Worcestershire sauce
2 TBSP dried parsley
1/8 tsp dried, minced garlic
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
1 bay leaf
5 - 7 boneless chicken breast halves

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except chicken. Cover bottom of crock with a bit of sauce. Layer chicken and sauce, ending with sauce. Cook on low 3 to 5 hours.
