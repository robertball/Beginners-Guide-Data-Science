Title:
Grilled Chicken Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1/2 cup olive oil
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
4 cloves garlic
fresh basil, about 8 leaves chopped
paprika, salt, and pepper
goat cheese (optional)

Directions:
Cut chicken into chunks for skewers. Place in dish to marinate and sprinkle with salt, pepper, and paprika.
In a small bowl, combine red wine vinegar, lemon juice, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, and basil. Whisk together.
Slowly mix in olive oil until combined.
Pour marinade to cover chicken. Marinate 8 hours or overnight.
Put chunks of chicken onto skewers for grilling. Grill over med-high heat for about 8 minutes on each side until chicken is no longer pink.
Top with crumbled goat cheese when serving, if desired.

Submitter: 
AppleChef
Description: 
A terrific summer dish for the grill that the whole family will enjoy! Marinating this chicken over night will give it a wonderful flavor that will have all your guests requesting the recipe.
