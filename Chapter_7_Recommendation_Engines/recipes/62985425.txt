Title:
Blueberry Lemon Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For crust:
1 cup butter - softened
1 cup powdered sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups flour
For filling:
1/2 cup sugar
3 tbsp. flour
Juice and zest of 1 lemon
1/8 tsp. salt
3 eggs
2 cups fresh or frozen blueberries

Directions:
Heat oven to 400.
To make crust, grease 9 inch square baking dish.
In a medium bowl, cream together butter, powdered sugar and vanilla until fluffy.
Add flour and mix just until mixture forms small crumbs.
Press about 2/3 of the dough into baking pan and bake for 12-15 minutes or until lightly browned.
When crust is done, remove from oven and reduce temperature to 350.
In a medium bowl, mix sugar, salt, flour, and zest.
Add eggs and lemon juice and whisk until smooth.
Spread blueberries in even layer over crust and then pour egg mixture over the blueberries.
Sprinkle reserved crumb mixture on top and bake for 30-40 minutes or until crumb topping is golden and filling is puffed.
Cool completely before cutting.
