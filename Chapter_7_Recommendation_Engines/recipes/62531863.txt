Title:
Poppy Seed Cake by Ron Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package yellow cake mix
1 small package lemon pudding
1/4 cup poppy seeds
3/4 cup oil
1 cup water
4 eggs

Directions:
Mix ingredients together and pour into greased bundt pan.
Bake 45 to 60 minutes at 350 degrees.
Glaze topping or serve with sliced fruit such as strawberries, kiwi, grapes.
