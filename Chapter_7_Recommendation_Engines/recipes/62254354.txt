Title:
O. Henry Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 cup white corn syrup (Karo - Regular)
1.25 cups peanut butter
5-6 cups Special K Cereal
6 oz bag chocolate chips

Directions:
In large saucepan, bring sugar and syrup to a boil.
Remove from heat and stir in peanut butter till smooth.
Stir in Special K cereal until coated.
Press in a parchment-paper lined pan (or use glass pan, well greased).
Pour bag of semi-sweet chocolate chips on top.
Put in a very hot (400 degree) oven for about 1 minute - just till the chocolate is soft.
Remove from oven and spread the chocolate.
Cut IMMEDIATELY into bars.
Cool totally and cover with saran wrap.  Beast when left to set overnight.
