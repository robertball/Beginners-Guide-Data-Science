Title:
Almond Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs, well-beaten
1/2 cup butter, melted
3/4 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. almond extract
3 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 tsp. salt
1 Tbsp. baking powder
1/2 cup coarsely chopped almonds, toasted

Directions:
In large bowl, combine flour, salt and baking powder. Set aside.  In mixing bowl, combine eggs and butter, beating well.  Gradually add sugar, beating well. Stir in vanilla and almond extracts.
Gradually add flour mixture to creamed mixture; stir in toasted almonds.
Divide dough into 6 pieces. Shape each into a 4-inch long loaf.Place on non-stick cookie sheet.  Press each loaf to slightly flatten. Bake at 325 for 30 minutes.  Remove from oven; after 5 minutes carefully move loaves to wire rack.  Cool for 30 minutes.  With serrated knife, cut each loaf into 1/2-inch slices.  Place slices, cut side down, on cookie sheets.  Bake an additional 20 minutes at 325. Remove to rack to cool completely.

Submitter: 
AUNT MAMIE
Description: 
These mini-biscotti-type cookies are crunchy, not too sweet, and may be fancied up by dipping them in white or dark chocolate.
