Title:
Sausage in Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box cornbread mix
milk, to prepare cornbread mix
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
3 green onions(with tops), chopped
(about 1/4 cup)
1 lb. precooked sausage links
..........................
SALSA-SOUR CREAM:
2 cups prepared salsa
1/2 cup sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees; place cast iron skillet in oven to preheat. Mix cornbread as directed on box; stir in cheese and green onions. Add to hot skillet. Place sausages in spoke-fashion on top of cornbread.
Bake about 20 minutes, or until cornbread is golden brown.
While cornbread is baking, prepare salsa-sour cream:mix salsa and sour cream in 1-quart saucepan. Heat over medium heat until hot(do not boil).
Serve cornbread in wedges with salsa-sour cream.
