Title:
Basil Strawberry Mango Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4488476.jpg
Ingredients:
4 leaves basil
1 cup frozen mango pieces
5 hulled strawberries
1 cup water
1/4 cup white sugar, or to taste
3 cubes ice

Directions:
Blend the basil, mango, strawberries, and water in a blender. Add sugar and ice cubes; blend again until smooth.

Submitter: 
squeakers215
Description: 
This smoothie makes for a great not too sweet cool summer beverage.
