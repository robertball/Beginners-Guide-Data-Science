Title:
"The Big Sandwich" By Vikki Welch :updated By Rachael Welch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2049548.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large Sourdough round
1 red bell pepper
1 green bell pepper
1 onion, sliced
1 packet au jus mix
1/4 cup water or beef broth
2 TBSP BBQ sauce
black pepper
1 lb. roast beef or pastrami
any cheese
butter or olive oil (garlic butter)

Directions:
Preheat oven 425 degrees F.
Saute peppers and onion until soft, add meat, au jus, and water.... bring to boil, then simmer.
Cut top off and pull filling out of round. Rub inside wall of round with butter or oil (as well as the cap of round). Set on cookie sheet, the place in oven for 7-8 minutes.
Take out of oven and lower temp to 350 degrees F. Fill the round with Veggies and meat mixture. Top with cheese and place cap of bread on top of round.
Wrap in foil and place back on cookie sheet, then back in the oven for 20 minutes.
Take out of oven and remove foil. Slice with large knife into wedges... enjoy!

Submitter: 
Rachael Welch
Description: 
This is my husband's birthday sandwich. Very versatile and something the whole family loves.
