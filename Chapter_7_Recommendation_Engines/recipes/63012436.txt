Title:
Chocolate Covered Toffee Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1 1/2 cups sugar
2. 2 sticks butter
3. 3 tbsp water
4. 1Tbsp light corn syrup
5. 1 tsp vanilla
6. 2 cups ( 12 oz) semisweet choc chips
7. 1 cup chopped almonds or walnuts or pecans
( better if you toast nuts on cookie sheet in oven before)

Directions:
lightly grease 13x9" pan
In med sauce pan med heat bring 1.2.3.4. to a boil without stirring till candy thermometer is at 300* to 310*( cold water drop makes brittle mass snaps easily)
remove from heat and add 5.
Pour int pan spread evenly...wait 2 min then spread 6. over evenly press gently 7. fridge till cool then break into pieces
