Title:
Lime-Shrimp Avocado Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
2 pounds shrimp, peeled, deveined, & chopped
¼ cup lime juice
1 pound cellentani (corkscrew) pasta, cooked
4 avocados, peeled, pitted and chopped
1 pint grape tomatoes, quartered
1 large red onion, chopped
½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
Dressing:
1/3 cup olive oil
1/4 cup lime juice
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp. dry mustard
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine shrimp and 1st ¼ cup of lime juice and marinate in refrigerator overnight.
Drain shrimp.
Cook shrimp in microwave approximately 6 minutes,stirring halfway through, until pink.
Let shrimp cool.
Combine shrimp with the, pasta, avocados, tomatoes, red onion, and cilantro in a bowl and toss.
For the dressing combine olive oil, 2nd ¼ cup lime juice, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, dry mustard and salt and pepper in a cruet and shake well.
Pour dressing over salad and toss.
Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
MANDYMARTIN
Description: 
A fresh, spring-time pasta salad perfect for a Cinco de Mayo celebration.
