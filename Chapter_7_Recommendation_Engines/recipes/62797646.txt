Title:
HOT AND SPICY GRILLED SHRIMP ON A STICK Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 clove minced garlic
1/2 tsp.cayenne pepper
2 Tbsp.olive oil
1 tsp.coarse salt
1 tsp.paprika
2 tsp.fresh lemon juice
1 1/2-2 lbs.shrimp[rinsed & dried]
lemon wedges

Directions:
Soak sticks in water.In bowl mix together all the spices with the oil and lemon juice,add shrimp and let marinate.Start grill or preheat broiler.Arrange rack closest to top.Thread shrimp onto sticks.
Grill/broil for 2-3 mins.per side.Turn once.
Serve with lemon wedges.
