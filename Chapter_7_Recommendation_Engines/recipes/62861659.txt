Title:
Toffee Chocolate Chip Toonies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup unsalted butter, softened
¾ cup packed brown sugar
¼ cup granulated sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
1 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
½ tsp. baking soda
¼ tsp. salt
½ cup toffee bits
½ cup mini semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
In large bowl, beat butter with brown and granulated sugars until fluffy; beat in egg and vanilla.
Stir together flour, baking soda and salt; stir into butter mixture until combined. Stir in toffee bits and chocolate chips.
Drop by rounded 1 tsp., about 2 inches apart, onto parchment paper-lined baking sheets. Bake in 350F oven until golden, 10-12 minutes. Let cool on pan on rack for 5 minutes. Transfer to rack; let cool completely.
