Title:
Baked Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/288336.jpg
Ingredients:
Cheddar Cheese (and/or)
Muenster Cheese
Monterey Jack Cheese
1 1/2 Cups Half and Half
1/2 Small Package of Velveta Cheese
2 Eggs Beaten
6 Tablespoons Butter
Italian Bread Crumbs
Elbow Macaroni

Directions:
In a pot, boil a cup and a half of half and half, the cheese from the deli and also mix in 1/2 small package velvetta cheese with 2 eggs beaten.  In another pot, cook your noodles to el dente, once done strain and add 6 tablespoons melted butter with it.  Mix everything together, bake for 30 minutes at 350 or until hot and bubbly, then sprinkle the top with italian bread crumbs and broil for a couple minutes to make it crunchy.  Enjoy!
