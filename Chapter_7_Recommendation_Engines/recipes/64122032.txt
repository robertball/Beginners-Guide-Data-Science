Title:
Tortellini Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Frozen Tortellini
Green Pepper chopped
Tomato chopped
Cubed Chicken
Shredded Cheese
Italian Dressing
Bake at 350 for approx. 30 mins.

Directions:
Cook tortellini according to package directions.
Mix green peppers, tomatoes, chicken, cheese and salad dressing with drained tortellini.
Put in casserole dish and bake at 350 until hot and golden brown on top. Approx. 30 mins.
