Title:
Butter Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup crushed buttery round cracker crumbs
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. onion powder
1/4 tsp. paprika
salt & ground black pepper to taste
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/4 cup (1/2 stick) butter, cut into pieces

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
Place eggs and cracker crumbs in two separate shallow bowls. Mix cracker crumbs with garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper. Dip chicken in the eggs, then dredge in the crumb mixture to coat.
Spray 9x13 (no larger!) baking dish with Pam and arrange coated chicken. Place pieces of butter around the chicken.
Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes, basting with pan juices twice during the process, or until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear.

Submitter: 
ANYAFIRE
Description: 
Chicken breasts are dipped in beaten eggs and cracker crumbs, then baked with butter. These chicken breast are really tender and moist. Excellent flavor! I never have leftovers.Modified from: Famous Butter Chicken
