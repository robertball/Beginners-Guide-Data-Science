Title:
Frozen Sherbet Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/669669.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package frozen raspberries
1/2 gallon pineapple sherbet
2 firm bananas. sliced

Directions:
Place frozen raspberries in hot water for 5 minutes to thaw slightly.   Empty sherbet into a large bowl.  Stir in raspberries and bananas.  Place bowl, covered,  in freezer until firm.

A great and refreshing dessert for a hot summer day!
