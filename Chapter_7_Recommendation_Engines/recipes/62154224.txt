Title:
Fruit salad with dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Fruit:
2 Apples chopped
2 Oranges chopped
1 Bananna quartered and sliced
15-20 Grapes halved
10-15 Strawberries halved and sliced
Dressing:
2 Tbsp Mayo
1 tsp lemon juice
Sugar or Spenda
Optional Fruit Additions:
2 Kiwis chopped
Blueberries whole

Directions:
Cut up fruit to desired size
Mix fruit in large bowl
Add mayo and lemon juice and mix
Add sugar or Splenda to taste (I usually use about 5 packets of Splenda)

Submitter: 
Holyflower93
Description: 
Light, sweet and creamy fruit salad. Lots of room to change things around and make it the way you like.
