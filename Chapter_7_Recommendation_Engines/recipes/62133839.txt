Title:
Dessert Blues Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, melted
2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1/4 sugar
1/2 cup chopped nuts
8 oz large marshmallows (300 gr bag)
1/2 cup milk
1 1/2 cups whipped topping
19 oz Blueberry pie filling
1/4 cup sugar
2 tsp lemon juice

Directions:
Mix together butter, graham crumbs, sugar and nuts together and press into 9 X 13 pan. Reserve 1/2 cup for topping.
Melt together, over low heat, marshmallows and milk. Cool to room temperature. Fold in the whipped topping. Spread over crumbs. Chill for about 1 hour.
Mix together blueberry pie filling, sugar and lemon juice. Spoon over marshmallow mixture. Sprinkle with reserved crumbs. Chill overnight or at least for a few hours.

Submitter: 
jowolf2
Description: 
A very pretty dessert. Change it up by using different pie fillings as toppings. Only takes 15 minutes to make, a great no-bake dessert! Chill for a few hours, or overnight, and serve.
