Title:
Italian Chicken Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Whole Chicken
24 oz of Chicken Stock (not broth)
1 tsp cilantro
1 tsp Parsley Flakes
1 tsp fresh Garlic
1/2 Onion - chopped
Sliced Carrots
1 16 oz can tomato paste
1 16 oz can diced tomatoes
1 box rigatoni's

Directions:
Place whole chicken in a soup pot.
Boil chicken in water for about 30 minutes.
Drain water and rinse chicken in warm water (removing excess chicken and fat) leave what skin you can.
Place whole chicken back in soup pot.
Add tomato paste, diced tomatoes, chicken stock, onion, carrots and spices.
Simmer for about 4 hours
Remove chicken - let cool and pull apart
Add chicken back into broth, thicken if desired and serve over cooked Rigatoni's
