Title:
Spinach Mushroom Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pastry for 9-inch one crust pie
1 10 ounce package frozen spinach, thawed and drained
1/2 cup thinly sliced mushrooms
1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
1/3 cup sliced green onion
1 cup plain yogurt
1 cup milk or evaporated skim milk
salt and pepper to taste.

Directions:
1.  Heat oven to 425 degrees.  Place pasty in 9-inch pie plate and flute edges.  Arrange spinach, mushrooms, cheese and green onion in pastry-lined pie plate.
2.  Beat yogurt slightly; beat in remaining ingredients.  Pour into pie plate.  Bake uncovered 15 minutes.
3.  Reduce oven temperature to 300 degrees.  Bake until knife inserted in center comes out clean, about 30 minutes longer.   Let is stand 10 minutes before cutting.
