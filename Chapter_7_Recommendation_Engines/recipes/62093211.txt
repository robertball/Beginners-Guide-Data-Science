Title:
Independance Day Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Butter Flavor shortening
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
4 cups all purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
12 ounces white chocolate chips
12 ounces dried cranberries
12 ounces dried blueberries

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
Cream the shortening, sugar and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
Lightly beat the eggs, and beat into the creamed mixture.
Mix the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt together; then mix into the shortening/sugars mixture.
Stir in the white chocolate chips, dried cranberries and dried blueberries.
Form into balls about the size of a golf ball.
Place balls on ungreased cookie sheet, and flatten to about 1/2" thick.
Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the 350 degree F oven.
Transfer to wire rack to cool completely.
