Title:
Chicken Pasta Parmigiana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979105.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.5 ounce) package TysonÂ® Frozen, Fully Cooked Breaded Chicken Tenderloins
1 (26 ounce) jar pasta sauce
8 ounces pasta, cooked
1 cup grated reduced fat mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
COOK: CLEAN: Wash hands. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Heat pasta sauce on stove top or in microwave. Toss pasta with three-fourths of sauce to coat thoroughly; spread evenly in a 1 1/2-quart shallow baking dish. Place chicken patties on pasta and drizzle with remaining sauce. Sprinkle with grated cheeses to cover entire dish. Bake 15 minutes.
SERVE: Serve with a tossed salad and garlic bread, if desired.
CHILL: Refrigerate leftovers immediately.

Tyson Foods

A baked dish with breaded chicken tenderloins, pasta and sauce covered with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
