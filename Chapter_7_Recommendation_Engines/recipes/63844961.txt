Title:
Herb and Garlic Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068046.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 tablespoons vinegar
2 teaspoons white sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon parsley
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 teaspoon basil
1/4 teaspoon celery seed
1/4 teaspoon marjoram
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Whisk vegetable oil, vinegar, sugar, salt, parsley, garlic, basil, celery seed, marjoram, and black pepper together in a bowl.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
You can make a quick and tasty salad dressing with oil, vinegar, garlic and an assortment of herbs like marjoram, celery seed, and basil.Modified from: Herb and Garlic Dressing
