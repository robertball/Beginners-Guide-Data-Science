Title:
Creamy Chicken and Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb Breast Tenderloins
3 cans of Healthy Request Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 can of Cream of Mushroom with Roasted Garlic Soup
1 large can of French's fried onions
1 lb frozen green beans

Directions:
Place a crockpot liner in the crockpot
Place chicken and soup in liner.
Stir well so that chicken is completely covered.
Cook on low for 8 hrs or 4 hrs on high.
Stir in frozen green beans.
Cook until desired tenderness. About 1 hr.
(You can substitute can green beans for a quicker meal)
Stir in half of French's fried onions and spread other half on top as garnish.
Cook additional 15 minutes.
Gravy is great on Mash Potatoes.
