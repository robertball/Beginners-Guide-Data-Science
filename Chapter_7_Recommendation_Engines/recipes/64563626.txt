Title:
Triple-Citrus Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lemonade and 1 orange Crystal Light On the Go Packets
3 1/2 cups water
1/2 cup tequila
2 TBL lime juice
ice

Directions:
Pour 1 lemonade and 1 orange Crystal Light On the Go Packets into a pitcher; stir in water. Stir in tequila and lime juice. Pour over ice cubes in glasses.
