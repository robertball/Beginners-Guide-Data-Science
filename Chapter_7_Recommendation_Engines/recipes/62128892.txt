Title:
easy one dish chicken vegetable habenero soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cooked rotisseerie chicken, diced
48 oz chicken broth
2 boullion cubes
2 cups of water
1 large sweet onion, diced
5 large carrots, diced
1 stalk celery, diced
3 tbs vegetable oil
1 cup long grain rice or wild rice
1 can diced tomatoes
4 fresh habaneros, tops cut off

Directions:
soak rice in water overnight, drain
Heat large saucepan with oil on high
sautee onion, carrots, and celery until soft
add water, and whole habeneros
add boullion cubes and boil
add chicken and chicken broth
reduce heat and cover
add rice and tomatoes with broth
serve once rice is cooked

Submitter: 
Jackie B.
Description: 
This recipe is NOT for people who don't like their food spicy, and it makes a perfect cold remedy.
