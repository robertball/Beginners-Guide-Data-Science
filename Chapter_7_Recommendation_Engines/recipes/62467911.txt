Title:
Baked Ziti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1	pound ground beef
Salt and pepper to taste
Garlic powder to taste
½	cup onion, chopped (about 1 cup)
1 ½	to 2 cups Prego® Fresh Mushroom Italian Sauce (your favorite pasta sauce)
8	ounces tube-shaped pasta (ziti) (about 3 cups), cooked and drained
2	cups shredded mozzarella cheese (about 12 ounces)
1	cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook and season the beef and onion in an 8-quart saucepot over medium high heat until the beef is well browned, stirring often to separate meat. Pour off any fat.
2.	Stir the sauce, ziti and 1 ¼  cups mozzarella and ½ cup parmesan cheese in the saucepot. Spoon the beef mixture into  an 8x8 casserole dish. Top with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
3.	Bake at 350°F. for 30 minutes or until the beef mixture is hot and the cheese is melted.

Description: 
pasta and beef dish that is rich and filling.  Freezes well.
