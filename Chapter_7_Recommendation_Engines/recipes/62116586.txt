Title:
Shrimp Boil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Â½ - 2 lb uncooked shrimp
1 lb crab legs (optional)
2 quarts water
5 ears corn (halved)
5 small red potatoes (halved)
1 onion (cut into 4 large pieces)
2 lemons  (cut in half and juiced)
1 pkg shrimp boil spice bag

Directions:
Bring water to a boil in a large stock pot
Add spice bag, onions, potatoes, corn, and lemons
When adding lemons squeeze the juice into the water
Turn heat to med-high and boil for approx. 20-30 minutes Add the shrimp and crab and boil for another 6-8 minutes   Serve this authentic shrimp boil on newspaper
let your guests grab what they want with their hands!
