Title:
Kelsey's Favorite Stuffed Green Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked white rice
garlic powder
3 large green bell peppers, halved and seeded
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1 onion, diced
chopped fresh mushrooms
1 T diced garlic
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
italian seasoning
basil
15 oz (1/2 jar) spaghetti sauce
15 oz can diced tomatoes (flavored)
shredded mozzarella cheese
shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, bring water and garlic powder to a boil. Add rice and stir. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Blanch peppers - boil water, add peppers, let sit for 3 minutes in a strong boil then put in cold water right away. Remove peppers from the water and set aside in a 9x13 inch baking dish sprayed with olive oil.
In a large saucepan over medium heat, brown the ground beef - 1/2 way through add the chopped onions, garlic and mushrooms and continue cooking -  drain. Return to heat and mix in cooked rice, and salt, pepper, italian seasoning and basil. Pour in tomato sauce diced tomatoes(with liquid included) and mix thoroughly. Let simmer for about 10 minutes. Remove from heat.
Spoon the meat mixture onto each half of the green peppers. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes or until mixture begins to turn golden brown.
Sprinkle mozzarella and cheddar cheeses over the top of each stuffed pepper. Return to the oven and bake until cheese is lightly browned, about 5 to 10 minutes.
