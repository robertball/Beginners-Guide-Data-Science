Title:
Apple Glazed Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4lb pork roast
salt and pepper
4-6 apples, peeled, cored, sliced
1/4 apple juice
3 tablespoons brown sugar
1 teaspoon ground ginger

Directions:
salt and pepper roast
combine all remaining ingredients in crock pot and cook for 6-8 hours
Double or triple sauce for larger roasts
