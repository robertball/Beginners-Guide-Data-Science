Title:
Toscana Soup II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1012857.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs Graziano Bros spicy Italian sausage
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
3/4 cup diced onion
1 1/4 teaspoons minced garlic
4 cups chicken stock
4 potatoes, halved and sliced
4 cups sliced kale
1/3 cup heavy cream
1 tsp Red pepper flakes

Directions:
Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.  Saute Sausage drain and add sauteed onions; add garlic and pepper flakes cook 1 minute.
Stir in broth and potatoes; simmer 15 minutes.
Reduce heat to low and add kale and cream; simmer until heated through and serve.

Submitter: 
Cucina di papa
Description: 
This soup is sure to warm your bones on a chilly night and won't leave you hungry.  Potatoes, sausage and kale are simmered in chicken broth and cream, with onions and garlic.Modified from: Toscana Soup
