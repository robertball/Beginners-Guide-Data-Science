Title:
Quick Black Forest Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/635055.jpg
Ingredients:
CAKE:
1 (18.25 ounce) package devil's food cake mix
2 eggs
1 tsp almond extract
1 tsp vanilla
1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
GLAZE:
1 Cup white chocolate chips (melted)
1 8 oz. carton of soft cream cheese
1 1/2 cup confectioners sugar
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Mix together: cake mix, beaten eggs, extracts, cherry pie filling and 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips. Stir until just combined.  Pour batter into a greased 9x13 inch pan. Batter will be thick
Bake in a 350 degree F (175 degree C) oven for 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.  Remove cake from oven and let cool.
To Make Glaze: Beat cream cheese till smooth, add confectioners sugar and melted chips
Spread Glaze over cooled cake and sprinkle with nuts
