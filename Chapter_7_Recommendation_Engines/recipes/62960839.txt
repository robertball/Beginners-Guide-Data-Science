Title:
GINGER SNAP PUMPKIN PIE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C GINGER SNAPS
16 oz PUMPKIN (CANNED, ORGANIC IF POSSIBLE)
1/2 t EACH: CINNAMON, GINGER, CLOVES, NUTMEG
1/2 C EGG WHITES (APPROX 4)
1/2 C SUGAR OR HONEY
12 oz CAN EVAPORATED SKIM MILK

Directions:
1.  PREHEAT OVEN TO 350 DEGREES FAHRENHEIT
2.  GRIND COOKIES IN A FOOD PROCESSOR.
3.  USE BUTTER, COOKING SPRAY OR VEGETABLE OIL AND LIGHTLY COAT THE INSIDE OF A 9" PIE PAN.  PAT CRUMBS ON PAN EVENLY.
4.  MIX REST OF THE INGREDIENTS IN A MIXING BOWL AND POUR INTO CRUST.
5.  BAKE UNTIL KNIFE INSERTED IN CENTER COMES OUT CLEAN (APPROX 45 MIN.)
6.  COOL ON RACK OR IN REFRIGERATOR PREFERABLY OVER NIGHT BUT AT LEAST 3 HOURS.
