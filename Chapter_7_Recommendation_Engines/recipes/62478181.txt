Title:
Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Sponge Cake - break into pieces and dry
Sliced fresh strawberries (drain juice and save)
2 cans mandarin oranges (save juice)
1 jar home canned peaches (save juice)
3 sliced bananas
4 cup pkg strawberry jello - use juice instead of water - pour over trifle and set
1 pkg French vanilla custard - cool and pour over set jello
whipping cream on top
Chocolate shavings for garnish

Directions:
Layer Cake and fruit.
Pour jello over and let set.
Pour vanilla custard over when jello is set
Whipping cream and chocolate shavings on top
