Title:
Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, diced
6 oz tomato paste
2 cups tomato sauce
1/2 cup water
1/2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp Italian herb seasoning
1 tsp basil
1 bay leaf
1 tsp sugar
1/4 tsp salt
2 dashes pepper
1/8 tsp rosemary
1/4 tsp fennel
1 Tbsp cooking wine

Directions:
In 3 qt saucepan, brown ground beef with onion and garlic.  Add remaining ingredients and bring to boil.  Simmer on low heat for 30 minutes.  Serve with spaghetti noodles.
