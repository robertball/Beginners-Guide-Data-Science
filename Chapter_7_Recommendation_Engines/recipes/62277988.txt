Title:
Dianne's Beef Barley Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/349105.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. stew meat
1 bay leaf
2 tablespoons oil
3 carrots, chopped
3 stalks celery, chopped
1 onion, chopped
1 (32 ounce) carton Pacific beef broth
1 (28 ounce) can Muir Glen diced tomatoes
1 tablespoon white sugar
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon dried, crushed rosemary
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup barley

Directions:
Put all ingredients, except for the barley, in a slow cooker and cook on low for 4 hours, then on high for 4 hours.  Add barley during the last 1/2 hour of cooking.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
Stew meat is slow cooked in broth and tomatoes, with veggies and seasonings.  Barley is added at the end.  One of the best soups I have made!Modified from: Beef Barley Vegetable Soup
