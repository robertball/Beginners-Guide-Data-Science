Title:
Slam Dunk Butterflied Coconut Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3227060.jpg
Ingredients:
24 shrimp
2 cups flour
2 eggs
2T milk
16 oz.  flaked sweetened coconut
1 qt. oil

Directions:
Peel, and devein the shrimp leaving the tails on.  To butterfly, use a sharp knife and cut down the back of the shrimp, NOT all the way through, and press open flat.
Using three medium bowls, beat eggs and milk in one, place flour in the second one, and coconut in the third.
Dredge the shrimp in the flour, then dip in the egg/milk mixture  and repeat.  Roll the coated shrimp in coconut gently pressing it into the battered shrimp.
In a deep fryer (or skillet), preheat the oil to 350 degrees.
Deep fry the coconut shrimp in the preheated oil until golden brown.   Drain on paper towels.
For a tropical flavor, serve with "Slam Dunk Coconut Shrimp Dipping Sauce" from this site.

Submitter: 
SHORECOOK
Description: 
This recipe I created when I started making coconut shrimp.  I wanted more coconut flavor, so I butterflied the shrimp to give them more surface to hold the coconut.  They are best served with Slam dunk coconut shrimp dipping sauce from this site.
