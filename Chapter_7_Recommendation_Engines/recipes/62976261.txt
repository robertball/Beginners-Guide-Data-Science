Title:
Never-Fail Meringue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cold egg whites
1 Tbsp.cold water
3 Tbsp.sugar
1 1/2 tsp.cornstarch
1/4 tsp.and a pinch of baking powder

Directions:
Use a cold metal bowl and beat egg whites with cold water untill stiff.
Whisk together sugar,cornstarch and baking powder in small bowl and gradually beat into egg whites.Continue beating untill mixture is stiff and glossy.
Spread over pie taking care to touch the pie shell to seal the pie.
Brown under broiler for a few minutes till golden[watch carefully!]
