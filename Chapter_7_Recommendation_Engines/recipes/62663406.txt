Title:
Taco Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 to 2 pounds ground beef
2 pounds Velveeta (1 brick)
6-8 jalapeno peppers
1/4 cup milk
10-12 ounce jar picante salsa chunky style
2 packages taco seasoning

Directions:
Brown ground beef in covered pot with cut up peppers for 20-30 minutes.
Drain off fat and add taco seasoning.
Cut brick of Velveeta cheese into about 1&quot; thick slices. Place in 2 quart microwavable bowl.
Add milk and Picante salsa.
Cook at medium or low (depends on microwave power) for 10 minutes. Stir. Continue this process until creamy.
Add meat. Stir and cook at medium or low another 10 minutes and serve hot with nacho chips or corn chips.
