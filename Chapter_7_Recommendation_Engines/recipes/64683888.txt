Title:
Cream Of Pea Ground Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4977841.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 can 10.5 ounce cream of mushroom soup
1 can 4 ounce mushrooms
1/2 cup milk
1 tablespoon butter
1/2 onion minced
1 tablespoon flour
1 1/2 cup frozen peas

Directions:
In a small frying pan melt butter and the add onion. Cook onion for about 5 mintues until translucent. Stir in flour to make a rue.
In a large frying pan cook ground beef until it browns and drain off fat.
Stir into to large frying pan mushrooms, mushroom soup, and milk. Cook on simmer for 20 minutes.
Add peas and rue to large frying pan and simmer for another 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
My mother used to make it like this at home and pour it over toasted white bread. Quick and easy recipe over a shingle as I would say.
