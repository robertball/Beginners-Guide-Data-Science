Title:
Aromatic Chicken Braise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chickien drumsticks
4 chicken thighs
3 T peanut oil
1 T gingerroot
2 garlic cloves
1/4 c green onion chopped
orange zest
1 cinnamon stick
1/2 t pepper flakes
1/2 t pepper corns
1 1/2 c chicken broth
3 T soy
3 T rice wine
2 t sugar
4 c rice

Directions:
Remove excess fat and skin, season with salt and pepper.
Heat oil in wok, add ginger, garlic, 2 T green onion, 1/2 orange zest, cinnamon, pepper flakes, and peppercorns. Stirfry 2 min.
Add chicken, brown on all sides 4-5 min. Add 1/2 c broth, soy, wine, and sugar. Simmer and cover for 25 min.
Remove chicken, add remaining broth, boil till reduced.
Return chicken for 2 min. Remove cinnamon stick.
Serve over rice with remaning onion and zest.
