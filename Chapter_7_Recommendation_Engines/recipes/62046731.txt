Title:
Sausage Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf frozen bread dough
1 pound sausage, cooked and drained
2 eggs, beaten
1 1/2 cup shredded cheese of your choice (my favorite is swiss and cheddar)

Directions:
Roll thawed bread dough into a rectangle.  Sprinkle with sausage and cheese, alternatig sausage and cheese.  Drizzle with half of egg mixture.  Roll up like a jelly roll.  Spread remaining egg over top of bread.  Place on greased cookie sheet with the seam on bottom.  Cook at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
A great idea for brunch.  This is a delicious change from the ever popular sausage ball recipe.
