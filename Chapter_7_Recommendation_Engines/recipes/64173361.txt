Title:
Indian Lentils and Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 white onion, halved and sliced into 1/2 rings
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup lentils
1 can (2 cups) chicken broth
1/2 cup water
1 (10 ounce) package frozen spinach
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon cardamom
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1 bay leaf
1/4 teaspoon clove
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander

Directions:
Heat oil in a heavy pan over medium heat. Sauté onion for 10 minutes or so, until it begins to turn golden. Add minced garlic and sauté for another minute or so.
Add lentils, chicken broth, water, and seasonings to the saucepan. Bring mixture to a boil. Cover, lower heat, and simmer about 35 minutes, until lentils are soft.
Meanwhile cook the spinach in microwave according to package directions. Add spinach to the saucepan. Cover and simmer until all is heated, about ten minutes.
