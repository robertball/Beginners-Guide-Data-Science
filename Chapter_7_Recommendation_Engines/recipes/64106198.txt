Title:
Frankfurter Crown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices bacon
1/2 cup chopped onion
1can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup water
dash salt and pepper
3 cups sliced, cooked potatoes
1cup cooked, cut green beans
1/2 pound franks, split and cut in half

Directions:
Cook bacon. Remove and crumble.
Cook onion in bacon drippings.
Stir in soup, water, salt, pepper. Mix well.
Add potatoes and beans. Stir.
Pour into 1 1 /2 quart casserole dish.
Stand franks around edge.
Bake 350 degrees for half hour.
Top with crumbled bacon.

Submitter: 
aunt star
Description: 
I believe this originally came from the Campbell Soup kitchen. I usually simply dice the franks and use a can of green beans. When in a hurry, top with imitation bacon chips. As well, omit the potatoes and spoon directly from stovetop onto microwave baked potatoes. Top with cheese.
