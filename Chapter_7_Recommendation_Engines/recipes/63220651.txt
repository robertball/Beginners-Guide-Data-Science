Title:
Susan's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. hamburger
1 cup dry breadcrumbs
1 cup milk
1 egg
1 sm. onion (chopped)
1 TBSP Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp ground sage

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.  Spread in ungreased loaf pan, 9x5x3.  Spread ketchup over top and cook uncovered in 350 oven until done, about 1 1/2 hrs.
