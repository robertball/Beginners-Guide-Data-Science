Title:
HOLUBTSI  [cabbage rolls] Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/738391.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups long grain rice
3 1/2 cups water,boiled
1 cup chopped bacon
1 cup chopped onion
1/2 tsp.salt
1/2 tsp.pepper
2 heads cabbage
SAUCE:
2 Tbsp.oil
1/2 cup chopped onions
3 cans tomato soup

Directions:
Wash rice in a strainer,tranfer to large pot,add boiled water.
Fry bacon and onion together till bacon is slightly browned,pour bacon and onion into the rice[yes grease and all-that adds flavour.]
Cook rice.Fluff up with a fork and cool in the fridge.[the cold bacon grease helps the rice stay together.]
Bring water to boil in a large pasta pot with a strainer.Core cabbage,Steam and peel leaves and remove as they soften,keep top and broken leaves to line bottom of casserole and for top of cabbage rolls.Care must be taken to not over cook the cabbage.It should be soft enough to crinkle in your hand.Trim off middle vien on cabbage leaves.
Start with the wide soft part of the cabbage add about 2 Tbsp. or so to the end start to roll bringing the sides to the middle.[like making a egg roll]
Line casserole dish with leaves,add cabbage rolls until casserole is full.Make sure they're nice and tight against each other.
Heat oil in frying pan.Add onions and cook till soft.Add the tomato soup[without water]to the frying pan.Cook until bubbling.Pour over the cabbage rolls.Top with remaining leaves.Cover with foil.
Bake at 325 for 3 hours or until fork goes through cabbage easily.
