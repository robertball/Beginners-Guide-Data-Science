Title:
Spanish Rice w/Ground Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz ground beef, browned and drained
1 T olive oil
1/4 cup onion, diced
1/8 cup bell pepper, diced
1 can rotel
1/2 T garlic, minced
1 beef bouillon cube
1 3/4 cup water/liquid
1 cup rice
2 oz cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Heat olive oil in skillet.
Saute onion 2 or 3 minutes, then add bell pepper, garlic, and rice and saute another minute or so or until lightly toasted.
Add bouillon cube, water, rotel, and ground beef.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 15 to 20 minutes.
Sprinkle cheese on top before serving.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Spicy rice and ground beef mix.  I like to use left over taco meat for this recipe and/or add a bit of taco seasoning to meat for added flavor.
