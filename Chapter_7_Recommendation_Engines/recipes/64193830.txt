Title:
Vegan & Gluten-Free Pumpkin-Coconut Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 heaping scoop vegan non-soy protein powder
1 cup gluten-free baking mix
2 chia (or flax) “eggs”
½ cup organic pumpkin
2 tsp. vanilla
¾ cup unsweetened almond milk or coconut milk
2 tsp. cinnamon
1/3 cup unsweetened coconut
1/3 cup dry roasted or raw almonds, chopped

Directions:
Follow directions to prepare chia/flax eggs.
In large bowl, combine chia/flax eggs with remaining liquid ingredients (except coconut oil), then slowly add dry ingredients, mixing well.
Heat coconut oil in iron skillet; pour ¼ cup batter per pancake. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and finish cooking.
Serve topped with your favorite nut butter.
