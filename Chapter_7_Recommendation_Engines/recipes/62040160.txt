Title:
Cabbage Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 ounces sauerkraut -- drained
2 pounds ground beef, lean
1 pound ground pork
1 each medium onions -- chopped
1 1/4 cups rice -- un-cooked
16 ounces tomato sauce -- canned
1/2 cup milk -- lowfat okay
1 1/2 Tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
2 heads cabbage -- boiled and cooled
2 cans tomato soup, condensed -- prepared as directed
1/2 pound bacon -- un-cooked

Directions:
-Spread sauerkraut in bottom of 13"x9"x2" pan.
-Brown beef, pork, and onion in large saucepan.
-Add rice, tomato sauce, milk, Worcestershire sauce, and garlic powder to saucepan. Mix thoroughly.
-Wrap Â½ C. meat mixture in each leaf of cabbage.
-Secure with toothpicks.
-Place rolls onto sauerkraut.
-Pour tomato soup on top of cabbage rolls.
-Lay strips of bacon over rolls.
-Bake, covered in 350 degree oven for 2 hours.

Robbie Rice
