Title:
Deep-fried Onion Rings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large Bermuda onions
1 cup sifted flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 egg, beaten
1 c buttermilk
Corn oil for deep frying

Directions:
Slice the onions into slices 1/3 inch thick or to personal preference.  Soak in ice water for one hour.  Separate into rings.  Drain and pat dry with paper towels.
Beat together flour, soda, salt,egg, and buttermilk until smooth.
Heat oil to approx 375F, or if using a deep fryer, to temp recommended by manufacturer.
Dip onion rings in batter and deep-fry in hot oil for about five minutes, being careful not to crowd the onion rings. Drain.
Can be reheated in a warm oven to serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
Bermuda onions covered in a buttermilk batter and deep-fried.
