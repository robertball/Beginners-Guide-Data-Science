Title:
Italian Salami Appetizer Kebabs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1059520.jpg
Ingredients:
12 fully cooked frozen meatballs (1/2-oz. each), thawed
12 large fresh basil leaves
12 cherry tomatoes
12 small fresh mozzarella cheese balls*
6 slices deli-sliced Margherita® Genoa or Hard Salami, halved
12 (4 inch) bamboo skewers or cocktail sticks

Directions:
In skillet over medium heat cook meatballs 5 to 6 minutes or until heated through; turn frequently to brown.
To assemble kebabs, wrap 1 basil leaf around 1 cherry tomato; thread onto skewer. Thread 1 heated meatball. Wrap 1 mozzarella ball with a halved salami slice; thread onto skewer. Repeat with remaining ingredients to form 12 skewers.

Submitter: 
Margherita Meats
Description: 
Fresh basil leaves curled around cherry tomatoes, savory meatballs and Margherita® Hard Salami-wrapped mozzarella balls, all skewered one-by-one to make one beautiful appetizer.
