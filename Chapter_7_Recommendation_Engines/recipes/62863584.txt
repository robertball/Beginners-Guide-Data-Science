Title:
Beer Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Strawberries
1/4 Cup Blueberries
1/4 Cup Sugar
2 Tbs Grenadine
2 Tbs Lime Juice

Directions:
Mix ingredients together. Chill for 1 hour
Fill a rock glass 1/2 full of beer ad 1/2 full of punch mixture. Enjoy.
