Title:
Grilled Sesame Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp dry sherry
3 Tbsp reduced sodium soy sauce
3 Tbsp rice wine vinegar
2 Tbsp hoisin sauce
1/2 tsp grated fresh ginger
2 Tbsp green onion, chopped
1 Tbsp sugar
1 Tbsp Oriental dark-roasted sesame oil
1 1/2 lbs flank steak or your choice of steak

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a clean jar with a tight lid.  Shake to mix well.  Place your choice of steak in a large ziplock bag and pour marinade over.  Press the air out of the bag and zip shut.  Marinate in the fridge for anywhere from 4-8 hours, turning occasionally.
Prepare grill to medium heat, and grill steak for 7-10 minutes each side, depending on thickness, to desired doneness.

Submitter: 
LINDA W.
Description: 
This sweet and spicy marinade is a great way to tenderize a flank steak or london broil cut.  Serve with a side salad with mandarin oranges and Oriental noodles to complete the Asian theme!
