Title:
Creamy Chicken Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 Chicken Breasts
6-8 Slices of Swiss Cheese
1/4 cup white wine
1 cup Pepperidge farm dressing (crumbs)
1/4 cup melted butter
1 can cream of chicken soup

Directions:
9x13 Pan spray with Pam
arrange chicken in pan and top each piece with cheese
mix soup and wine together in a bowl and spoon over chicken and cheese
mix dressing with melted butter and sprinkle over chicken
pre-heat oven to 350 bake for 55 minutes to an hour
Serve with Alfredo or Parmesan noodles
