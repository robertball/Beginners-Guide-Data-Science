Title:
Chocolate Peppermint Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/525089.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup dry milk powder
1/2 cup crushed hard peppermint candies (about 20 pieces)
1/2 cup crushed walnuts
Coating:
1/4 cup crushed hard peppermint candies (about 10 pieces)
1/4 cup crushed walnuts

Directions:
In a medium non-stick saucepan, melt the chocolate chips over medium-low heat, stirring constantly until smooth.  Remove from heat and stir in dry milk powder, the 1/2 cup crushed peppermints and 1/2 cup crushed walnuts.  Stir until evenly combined.
For the coating, combine the 1/4 cup crushed peppermints and 1/4 cup crushed walnuts on a plate.  With clean hands, roll chocolate mixture into 1-1/2‚Äù balls, and then roll in coating.  Place on a sheet of waxed paper to cool.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
This recipe is a family holiday tradition.  The chocolate-peppermint flavor combination has always been one of my favorites.  They are very easy to make, albeit a little messy, and a beautiful addition to any holiday cookie platter.
