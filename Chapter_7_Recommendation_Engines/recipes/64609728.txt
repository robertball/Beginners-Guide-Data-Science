Title:
Big Ray's Graham Strusel Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4133198.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
3/4 cup chopped pecans
1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 package yellow cake mix (regular size)
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
1 Tbsp milk

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the cracker crumbs, brown sugar, pecans and cinnamon. Stir in butter; set aside. Prepare cake mix according to package directions.
Pour half of the batter into a greased 13" x 9" baking pan. Sprinkle with half of the graham cracker mixture. Carefully spoon the remaining batter on top. Sprinkle with the remaining graham cracker mixture.
Bake at 350Â° for 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool on a wire rack. Combine confectioners' sugar and milk; drizzle over coffee cake.
