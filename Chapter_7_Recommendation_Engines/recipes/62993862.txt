Title:
Mostaccioli with Vodka Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T butter or margarine
1 T olive oil
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 small onion, chopped (1/4 cup)
1/4 C chopped full cooked ham (about 2 ounces)
2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (about 1/2 lb) cut into 1" pieces
1/2 C vodka or chicken broth
1/2 C shipping (heavy) cream
1/2 C sliced pitted olives (optional)
1 T chopped fresh parsley
1/2 tsp pepper
1 pkg (16 oz mostaccioli or penne rigate pasta
1/4 C freshly grated or shredded parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat butter & oil in 10-inch skillet over medium-high heat. Cook garlic and onlion in butter mixture about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally until onion is translucent.
Stir in ham and chicken. Cook about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally until chicken is brown, Sir in vodka. Cook uncovered until liquid has evaporated.
Stir in whipping cream, olives, parsley and pepper. Heat to boiling; reduce heat. Simmer uncovered about 30 minutes, stirring frequently until thickened.
While sauce is simmering, cook and drain pasta as directed on package. Add pasta to sauce, toss gently until pasta is evenly coated. Sprinkle with cheese.

Submitter: 
Tricia McTamaney
Description: 
This is a modern pasta recipe popular in northern italy. Cooks looking for different ways of cooking short-cut pastas found vodka to be an innovative addition to the repertoire.
