Title:
Salmon Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Cups Uncooked Pasta Shells (Medium Size)
2/3 Cup Catalina Dressing
15 Ounce Can Pink Salmon (drained, deboned, descaled and flaked)
1 Cup Diced Celery
4 Tablespoons Sweet Pickle Relish
4 Hard Boiled Eggs, diced
1/4 Cup Green Onion
3 Tablespoons Dried Parsley
1 and 1/3 Cups Mayonnaise
1/2 Teaspoon Salt
1/2 Teaspoon Garlic Salt
1/4 Teaspoon Black Pepper
1 Tablespoon Dijon Mustard

Directions:
Boil Pasta Shells according to package directions.
Drain, put into a Large Bowl, add Catalina Dressing and chill the mixture.
In a Medium Bowl, mix Salmon, Celery, Relish, Eggs, Green Onion, Parsley, Salt, Garlic Salt, Pepper and Mayonnaise.
Mix the Pasta and Salmon Mixtures together in the Large Bowl, and chill for about 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Lindsey Harbison Neill
Description: 
This delicious, tangy pasta salad is great for picnics and gatherings!  Even the kids love it!
