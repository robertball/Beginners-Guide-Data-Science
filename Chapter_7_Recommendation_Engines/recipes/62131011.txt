Title:
Black Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (15 oz.) black beans, drained/rinsed
1 ripe avocado, peeled/diced
1 ripe tomato, diced
1 tsp dried cilantro
1 tsp dried parsley
1/2 ounce fresh lime juice (about half a lime)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients. Serve over salad greens, or with pita bread or tortilla chips. Good with salsa and sour cream on the side. Delicious with or without finely-chopped onion on top.

Submitter: 
Eula Thompson
Description: 
Quick Mexican-inspired salad, a good use for an extra avocado.
