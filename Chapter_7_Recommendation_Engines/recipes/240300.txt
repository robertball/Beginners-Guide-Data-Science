Title:
Spicy Buffalo Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1856042.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Greek yogurt
2 tablespoons hot sauce
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 pinch garlic powder
1 pinch salt

Directions:
Mix Greek yogurt, hot sauce, sugar, lemon juice, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, and salt together in a bowl.

Submitter: 
Michael Dixon
Description: 
Warning: Keep away from children. This dip is very spicy! This is my first recipe. I hope you like it! Constructive criticism is greatly appreciated. Serve with chips, crackers, vegetables, etc.
