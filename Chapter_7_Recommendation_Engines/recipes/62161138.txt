Title:
Greek Seven Layer Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. tub Cream Cheese Spread - Chive & Onion
7 oz. tub plain Hummus
1 medium cucumber, finely diced
3 Roma tomatoes, diced
1/4 cup minced purple onion
4 oz. crumbled Feta cheese
5 oz. pitted Kalamata olives, sliced
Pita Chips

Directions:
In a pie plate, or medium sized serving dish, spread cream cheese to cover the bottom.
Spread hummus over the cream cheese to cover.
Top with cucumbers and tomatoes.
Sprinkle onion evenly over top.
Sprinkle Feta cheese over onion layer.
Top with kalamata olive slices.
Serve with pita chips.

Submitter: 
Anne Z.
Description: 
This tastes like you're eating a Greek salad. It is so fresh and light. A new twist on the Mexican Seven Layer Dip.
