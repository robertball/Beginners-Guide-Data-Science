Title:
Oven Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups brown rice
1 tsp salt
2 Tbsp butter
3 cups boiling water

Directions:
Place rice, salt, and butter in a casserole dish that has a cover.
Pour boiling water over rice.  Stir.
Cover and place in 400 degree F oven for 1 hour.
Remove from oven, fluff with fork, and serve!
