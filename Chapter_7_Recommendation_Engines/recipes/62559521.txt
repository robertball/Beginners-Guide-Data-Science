Title:
Nach-Oh Oh! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/535442.jpg
Ingredients:
2 corn tortillas**
2 ounces lean turkey taco meat
1 ounce fat free shredded cheddar cheese
1 tablespoon chopped green onion
2 tablespoons diced tomatoes
1 tablespoon light sour cream

Directions:
** To make tortilla chips, Lightly spray each side of the tortillas with cooking spray. Cut each tortilla, using a pizza cutter, vertical into 4 strips, then horizontal to cut strips in half. Arrange on a baking sheet and bake on 350 degrees for 10 minutes. Watch carefully as not to burn them.
Brown and drain turkey meat.
Layer ingredients onto a plate, beginning with tortilla chips, then turkey meat, cheese, green onions, tomatoes, and sour cream.
