Title:
Jan's Pancake Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/994964.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1/2 cup white sugar
1 and 1/2 cups dark brown sugar
1 teaspoon  maple extract
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon extract
1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Boil water in a large sauce pan.
Add sugars and bring to a LOW boil (more than a simmer) stirring constantly. Stir until all sugar is absorbed. About 10 minutes.
Add extract and salt.  Cook another minute.
Remove from heat and let thicken.  You can place in a clean jar once completely cooled.
Stores in the refrigerator for several weeks.

Submitter: 
What's for dinner, mom?
Description: 
Simple and easy pancake syrup without preservatives or artificial thickeners.  You can easily create your own variety by simply changing the extracts used.
