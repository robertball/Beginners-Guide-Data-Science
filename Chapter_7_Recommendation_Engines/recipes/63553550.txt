Title:
Strawberry Cream Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 shots vanilla vodka
2 shots heavy cream
1/2 shot strawberry liqueur
1/2 shot strawberry nectar
Strawberry, for garnish
Read more at:

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a pitcher filled with ice. Stir and strain into chilled martini glasses.
Garnish with a strawberry.
