Title:
Baked Pork Chops & Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 center cut pork chops
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup sour cream
1 tsp dried, minced onions
salt & pepper to taste
2.8 oz can French fried onions
16 oz can sliced potatoes, drained

Directions:
Arrange pork chops in a lightly greased baking dish. Combine soup, sour cream, minced onion, salt & pepper. Spread on chops. Top with fried onions. Cover and bake at 325 for 2 hours, or until chops are tender. Arrange potato slices over chops, reduce heat to 300 and bake an additional 45 minutes.
