Title:
Ginger Veggie Stir-Fry with Oyster Flavored Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1/8 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup dry sherry
2 tbsp vegetable oil
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 teaspoons chopped fresh ginger root
1 chopped onion
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 small head broccoli, cut into florets
1/2 cup green beans, cut in 1" pieces and then cut lengthwise into halves
2 roma tomatoes, quartered and then halved
3/4 cup julienned carrots
1 cup mushrooms, quartered
1/2 cup snow peas (optional)
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/4 cup spicy oyster flavored sauce
1/8 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, sherry and cornstarch, set aside.
In a wok or large fry pan heat oil.
Add chopped ginger and garlic. Saute for 1 minute.
Add onion and red bell pepper.  Saute for 3 minutes.
Add broccoli and green beans.  Saute for 4 minutes.
Add mushrooms, carrots and tomatoes. Heat through.
Add chicken broth. Simmer for 1 minute.
Add oyster flavored sauce.
Add sherry, soy sauce and cornstarch mixture, heat till thickened.
Serve immediately over rice.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
This recipe calls for broccoli, snow peas, carrots, and green beans, but you can use any of your favorites. The vegetables are stir fried with garlic, ginger, and soy sauce. Serve over your favorite rice.Modified from: Ginger Veggie Stir-Fry
