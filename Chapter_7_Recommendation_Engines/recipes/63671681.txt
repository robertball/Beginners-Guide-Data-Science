Title:
Garlic Shrimp Fettuccini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 oz frozen shrimp
1 lb fettuccini noodles (more or less, depending on family size)
minced garlic to taste
1/4 cup butter
olive oil (3 drizzles)
italian seasoning
fresh ground pepper
red pepper flakes
salt
parsley
4 TBS Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Boil pasta to al dente. put shrimp in water for 1-2 minutes or put shrimp in strainer and strain pasta over shrimp to warm. Save 1/4-1/2 cup pasta water. In pot, melt butter, add oil, spices and garlic. Let garlic brown a bit to flavor the oil/butter. Add pasta, shrimp and Parmesan cheese into pot and stir to coat. If needed, add a bit of the pasta water to help coat.
Served with Homemade Cheddar Bay Biscuits.
