Title:
Pretzel Candies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3808362.jpg
Ingredients:
Chocolate Pretzel Rings:
mini pretzels or rings
chocolate kisses (hugs & kisses work too - they soften a bit faster & add some fun stripes)
M&M; candies
Pecan Caramel Candies:
mini pretzels
Rolo candies
pecan halves

Directions:
Chocolate Pretzel Rings:
Place pretzel rings or mini pretzels on greased baking sheets.
Place a chocolate kiss in the center of each pretzel.
Bake @ 275 x 2-3 min. or until chocolate is softened.
Remove from oven.
Place an M&M on each, pressing down so chocolate fills pretzel.
Refrigerate x 10 min. or until chocolate is firm.
Pecan Caramel Candies:
Place mini pretzels on foil-lined baking sheets.
Place a Rolo candy in center of each pretzel.
Bake @ 250 x 4 min. or until candies are softened. They will retain their shape.
Place a pecan half on each, pressing down so chocolate fills the pretzel.
Cool slightly.
Refrigerate x 10 min. or until set.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
2 variations of fun salty-sweet treats with pretzel bases.
