Title:
Tilapia with peppers and corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6  Tilapia fillets
1  diced small red onion
1  sliced green bell pepper
1  sliced red bell pepper
1  can diced tomatoes
1  4oz can tomato sauce
1  can corn drained
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
1-2 teaspoons lemon pepper
2 tbs. olive oil

Directions:
Heat 1 tbs. olive oil in pan and add onion.
Cook onion until transparent.
Add both green and red bell pepper and cook for approx 3-5 min. stir occasionallly.
Add tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, corn and garlic salt, mix well and cook for 1-2 min.
If sauce appears to thick add water to adjust thickness to preference.
Take pan off heat.
In large saute pan add 1 tbs olive oil and heat.
Season Tilapia filets with lemon pepper.
Place filets in heated pan and cover with tomato, pepper, and corn mixture.
Cover pan and cook for 10 min.
Serve fish with pepper mixture on top.

Submitter: 
REBECCAROD
Description: 
Tilapia cooked in healthy tomato based sauce of peppers and corn. It is great served over white or brown rice. Can also use any fresh light whitefish like cod, sole etc. I use tilapia since it is the only fresh I can find in these parts.
