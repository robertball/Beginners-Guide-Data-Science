Title:
Peppered Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982108.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 pound) pork tenderloin
2 teaspoons lemon pepper
1 1/2 teaspoons cayenne (red pepper) OR pepper blend seasoning

Directions:
Rub tenderloin with both peppers seasonings, covering entire surface. Place in shallow roasting pan and roast in 425 degree F. oven for 15-20 minutes, until internal temperature (measured with a meat thermometer) reads 155-160 degrees F. Let roast rest for 5 minutes before slicing to serve.

Submitter: 
National Pork Board
Description: 
Simple seasonings let the pork tenderloin go with just about anything from a simple side of pasta and buttered green beans to creamy garlic mashed potatoes and a casserole of mixed vegetables.
