Title:
Mashed Turnip & Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large turnips, peeled and cubed
4 potatoes, peeled and cubed
1/2c. skim milk
dash of salt & pepper
water (to cover)

Directions:
Boil water in large pot. Add turnip and potatoes and reduce heat to medium.
Cook, stirring frequently, for 30 mins.
Drain.
Add milk gradually while mashing with potato masher. Salt & pepper to taste.
