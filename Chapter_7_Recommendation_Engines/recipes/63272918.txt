Title:
Key Lime White Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c. margarine or butter
3/4 c. packed brown sugar
2 T. sugar
1 egg
1 1/2. t. lime juice or extract
2 c. bisquick
6 oz. white chocolate chips

Directions:
Beat margarine, sugar, egg, and vanilla until well mixed. Stir in remaining ingredients.
Drop onto cookie sheet and bake at 350 degrees for 8-10 minutes.
