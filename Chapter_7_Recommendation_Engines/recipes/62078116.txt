Title:
Frosted Walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups sugar
1/2 cup sour cream
1 t. vanilla
1 lb. walnuts (not chopped) spread these out onto a coo

Directions:
Cook first three ingredients 5 minutes over med flame stirring so it won't stick.
Spread walnuts out on a cookie sheet.
Pour cooked ingredients over walnuts mixing thoroughly until all are covered.
Let them dry for about 1/2 hour.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
MOLLYSPITCHER
Description: 
This is a wonderful recipe for frosted walnuts. They have a delicate sugar coating. You can't eat just one!
