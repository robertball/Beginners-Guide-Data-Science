Title:
Buffet Salmon Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz can salmon
6-8 slices bread (6 is enough)
1/2 cup mayonnaise (or less)
1 large green pepper, finely chopped (optional)
3/4 cup chopped celery
1/2 tsp salt
1 medium onion, chopped
3 cups milk
4 eggs, slightly beaten
10 oz can mushroon soup
1/2 cup grated old chedar cheese (or more if desired)

Directions:
Flake fish and mash, including bones.  Cube 1/2 of bread and place in bottom of larged, greased baking dish.  Mix together salmon, mayonnaise, pepper, celery, onion and salt.  Spread over bread.  Trim crusts from remaining bread and place on top of salmon mixture.  Mix eggs and milk together and pour over bread and salmon.  Refrigerate several hours.  Bake at 325 for 15 minutes.  Remove from oven and spoon undiluted mushroom soup over top.  Sprinkle with grated cheese and return to oven to bake for 1 hour.
