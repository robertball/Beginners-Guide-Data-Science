Title:
Gator Smoothies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4484482.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups ice
2 cups grape flavored sports drink
2 scoops vanilla ice cream

Directions:
In a blender, combine ice, sports drink and ice cream. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Description: 
This is a really good smoothie. You can use your favorite flavor of sports drink.
