Title:
Turkey Barley Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Stock
1 turkey carcass
5 qt. water
10-12 peppercorns
1 1/2 c. coarsely chopped onions
1/2 c. chopped carrots
Thyme - to taste
3 stalk of celery
1 bay leaf
Soup
6 stalks celery, cut into 1/2 inch slices
1 1/2 lbs. carrots, chop in 1 inch chunks
2 med. onions, diced
2 bay leaves
1/2 c. chopped mushrooms
1 cup barley
2 tsp. salt
Â½ tsp. pepper
1 tsp. marjoram
pinch thyme

Directions:
Bring turkey carcass to boil in large pot (6 quart) of water. Add broth ingredients. Add peppercorn and bay leaves. Simmer for 2-1/2 hours skimming the scum that floats to the top. Add lost water. Remove carcass, cool and shred meat and save. Strain stock; return to stock pot.
Add soup ingredients, bring to boil than simmer for 1 1/2 hours. Stir occasionally; add turkey meat in the last 10 minutes.
