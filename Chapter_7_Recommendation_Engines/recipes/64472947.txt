Title:
DIY Blueberry Applesauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2483364.jpg
Ingredients:
5 apples, cored and sliced
4 cups fresh blueberries
cinnamon, optional

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a crockpot and place on low heat for at least 3 hours, or until sauce-like.
Place in a large container and store for up to two weeks.

Submitter: 
Athletic Avocado
Description: 
Stop buying applesauce and make your own!  Its a breeze to make, just throw a few simple ingredients in the crockpot and let it simmer for a few hours!  Your kids will love it!
