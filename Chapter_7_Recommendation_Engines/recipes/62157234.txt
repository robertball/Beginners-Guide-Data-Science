Title:
It's Fall Y'all Easy Chocolate Popcorn Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. of white chocolate or chocolate (I use bark)
4 cups of popcorn (already popped)

Directions:
Shave or chop chocolate, using double boiler method melt chocolate stirring frequently.
Place popped popcorn in large mixing bowl. Once the chocolate is completely melted, pour it in the bowl with the popcorn and mix, coating the popcorn.
When cool enough to handle shape popcorn balls and place on wax paper until completely cooled.

Submitter: 
LisaT
Description: 
These quick and easy popcorn balls will become a family favorite. I love to make and give them to neighbors in Christmas tins.
