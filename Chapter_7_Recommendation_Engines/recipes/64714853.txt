Title:
Pork Roast Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
pork roast 2.5-3 lbs.
Sprinkled to taste:
season salt
cumin
garlic powder
dried onion flakes
cayenne
1 lime- skin grated on pork. Juice over everything when ready to cook.  More if needed (not enough juice)
cilantro- roughly chopped bunch
fresh garlic 3-4 finely chopped
tomato 1 roughly chopped
jalapenos 4 cored and quartered.
Water-just a little to match lime juice.
Corn totillas
Toppings of your choice.
Suggested-Fresh salsa, goat cheese.

Directions:
To prepare, season up roast with Johnny's season salt, garlic powder, cumin, and cayenne.
Place in crock. Grate lime peel over meat. Sprinkle generously with onion flakes.
Add chopped garlic to roast.
Cover generously with roughly chopped cilantro.
Place quartered jalapenos evenly over top of that.  Add tomato. Or not...
Squeeze juice of one juicy lime over all roast, add a little water 1/4 C. at most to side for moisture.
cook on low until done.
When roast is done remove from crock, shred or chop for tacos.
With some leftover vege's and juice, blend and add to meat to keep moist. At your discretion.

Submitter: 
Tammy H.
Description: 
Slow cooked pork roast. Cilantro, lime, garlic,  jalapenos, onion, all in one pot. Chop for taco's, Delicious!
