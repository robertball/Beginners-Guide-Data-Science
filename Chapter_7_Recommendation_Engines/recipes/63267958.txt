Title:
Our Favorite Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/857343.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/3 c shortening
1-1/3 c margarine
2 c sugar
2 c brown sugar
4 eggs
4 tsp vanilla
6 c flour
2 tsp soda
2 tsp salt
Chopped nuts (opt)
12 o pkg semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees (F).  Mix together well the shortening, margarine, sugars, eggs and vanilla. Blend in remaining ingredients.  (I like to combine all my dry ingredients before adding to creamed mixture).  Drop y spoonfuls on cookie sheet.  Bake for 8-10 minutes.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
...during my children's formative years these were the 'go to' cookies and ones made for every bake sale.
