Title:
French Silk Chocolate Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cups butter
1 cups white sugar
3 oz squares unsweetened baking chocolate
1 teaspoons vanilla extract
4 eggs OR 1 cup Egg Beaters = 4 whole eggs
1 (9 inch) pie shell, baked
rassberries for garnish

Directions:
1 Melt chocolate in microwave until it is melted and smooth. Allow to cool to lukewarm.
2 In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar until pale yellow and very fluffy. Blend in the melted chocolate and vanilla. With an electric mixer on medium speed, beat in the eggs, one at a time, taking 2 to 3 minutes minutes for each egg. If you use egg beaters add 1/4 cup for each egg. Pour filling into baked pie shell. Refrigerate 4 hours before serving.
Shave semi-sweet chocolate over pie and place fresh rassberries around the top  if desired.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a pie I ahve made for over 25 year. It is a big hit every place I have brought it! The egg beater work well as a replacement for the raw egges if you will be serving it to preg women, the elderly, small children or people with comprised immune system.
