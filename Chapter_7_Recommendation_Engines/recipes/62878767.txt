Title:
Slow Cooker Cilantro Lime Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 16 oz jar salsa (or your favorite homemade equivalent)
1 packet taco seasoning
1 lime (juice from one lime)
3 tbs fresh cilantro chopped

Directions:
Add all ingredients in slow cooker and cook for 4 hours on high or 6-8 hours on low.  When done, shred the chicken meat then serve in soft or hard taco shells with your favorite toppings.

Submitter: 
ltlmsmfft
Description: 
This is a nice change from plain chicken taco's.  The cilantro really adds a burst of flavor to this recipe.
