Title:
BBQ Hamburgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1077329.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb. ground beef
1 tsp salt
dash of pepper
1 can tomato soup
1/2 c sweet pickle relish
1 t brown sugar
1 t vinegar
1 t worcestershire

Directions:
Mix beef, salt & pepper. Form into 8 patties. Brown in a skillet, you may need a little shortening; pour off fat.
Combine remaining ingredients, pour over burgers.
Simmer 20 mins or until done; stirring now & then.
