Title:
June Summer Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Juice of one lime
2 T. olive oil
2 T. white wine vinegar
salt and pepper to taste
2 tomatoes, chopped
1 avocado, chopped
1 cucumber, halved, seeded, and chopped
1/2 cup corn (canned or frozen - I use Trader Joe's frozen roasted sweet corn)
2 bunches scallions, chopped
1/4 cup chopped cilantro

Directions:
In a bowl, lightly whisk lime juice, olive oil, vinegar, and salt and pepper.  Add remaining ingredients and toss to coat. Refrigerate and serve.

Submitter: 
Marian
Description: 
While barbecuing up a bunch of chicken, I wanted something light on the side that tasted fresh, cool, and refreshing.  This easy to make salad hits the mark every time.
