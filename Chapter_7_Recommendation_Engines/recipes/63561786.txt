Title:
Peanut Butter Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks softened butter
1 cup creamy peanut butter
1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
3 cups powdered sugar

Directions:
In a large bowl, using a hand mixer, cream the butter till light & fluffy.
Add peanut butter, heavy whipping cream, vanilla extract and 1/2 cup powdered sugar.  Cream together till well blended.
Add sugar, 1/2 cup at a time, beating to completely incorporate each time.
Once all sugar is added, whip one minute on medium high speed.

Submitter: 
carrol cofer
Description: 
This recipe is a slightly modified version of one offered by Betty Crocker for their Chocolate Banana Peanut Butter Delight layer cake.
