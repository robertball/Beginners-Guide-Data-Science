Title:
Turkey Sausage Barley Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion, diced
1 tbs. olive oil
.75 pounds of turkey sausage
1 clove garlic, crushed
32 oz. chicken stock
6 oz. frozen chopped spinach
2 tsp. kosher salt
black pepper to taste
1/3 c. barley

Directions:
In a large pot cook onion in olive oil until soft. Add the turkey, break apart and brown. Add garlic. Cook a few minutes more then add broth and spinach, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil and reduce to a simmer. Cook 20 minutes. Add barley and simmer 60 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Juli Carvi
Description: 
Turkey sausage with spinach and barley is the perfect soup.
