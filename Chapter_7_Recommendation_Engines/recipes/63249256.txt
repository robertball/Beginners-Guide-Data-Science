Title:
Spiced Maple Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1cup butter softened
1/4 - 1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 - 1/2 cup maple syrup
Cinnamon
Nutmeg
Ginger. (All spices to taste)

Directions:
Mix together and chill for 30 minutes.  Can be formed into a roll or just put into sealed container.

Submitter: 
Karen Zastrow
Description: 
We were given a small container of this for Christmas, along with the recipe.  My family LOVED it, especially on sweet breads like banana, zucchini or gingerbread.
