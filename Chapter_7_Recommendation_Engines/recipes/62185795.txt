Title:
Powdered Sugar Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Soft Butter
2 Cups Powdered Sugar
2 large eggs
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
3 Cups Flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 Cup ground almonds (opt)
dash of salt

Directions:
Cream butter and powdered sugar till fluffy.
Add eggs and vanilla.
Mix flour, baking powder, salt and almonds (all dry ingredients).
Add dry ingredients to butter and sugar mixture.
Refrigerate 2 to 4 hours.
With extra powdered sugar knead slightly and cut with cookie cutters.
Bake at 350 degrees till light brown
