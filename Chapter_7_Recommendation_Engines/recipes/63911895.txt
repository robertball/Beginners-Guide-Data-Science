Title:
Quinoa Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1084942.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cooked quinoa
3 large eggs
1/4 cup feta crumbles
1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
1/4 cup chopped onions
1/2 tsp thyme
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Spray muffin pan with Pam or line with muffin cups.
Cook quinoa according to directions(Can be made ahead). In a large bowl whisk eggs. Add all other ingredients.
Fill muffin pan 1/2 way to top. Bake at 400 for 20-30 minutes or until edges turn brown and muffins are firm to the touch.
