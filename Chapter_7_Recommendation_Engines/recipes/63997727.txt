Title:
Easy-to-Eat Pumpkin Walnut Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1109303.jpg
Ingredients:
Muffins:
2 cups Baking Mix
1/2 tsp salt
1-1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
2 beaten eggs
1 can of pumpkin puree
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup of canola oil
1/4 cup chopped walnuts
-
Optional Streusel Topping:
1/4 cup brown sugar
3 Tbs flour
2 Tbs butter
1/4 cup finely chopped walnuts

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl measure out the baking mix, salt and pumpkin pie spice. Mix to blend.
In a medium sized mixing bowl add the 2 beaten eggs, can of pumpkin puree, sugar, and oil. Mix well.
Add the wet ingredients to the large bowl of dry ingredients and hand stir until ALMOST blended.
Add the walnuts and stir until blended. Do not over mix. Less is more.
Line(2)cupcake baking tins with cupcake liners and spoon 1/3 cup of mixture into each cupcake liner. You should have approximately 18 muffins.
Blend the ingredients for the streusel topping with your fingers and sprinkle on top of muffins before putting in the oven.
Bake in 350 degree oven for 17 to 20 minutes until the muffins are golden brown and firm to the touch.
Remove the muffins tins from the oven and set on baking racks to cool thoroughly. Approximately 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Fancy Nancy
Description: 
This is an easy and simple recipe and produces a wonderful, not-too-crumbly muffin. The texture is less crumbly if you let the muffins cool completely. Eat with a smear of butter for an awesome treat. 
This recipe makes 18 muffins so give some away to friends and neighbors or take some to the office. Enjoy.
