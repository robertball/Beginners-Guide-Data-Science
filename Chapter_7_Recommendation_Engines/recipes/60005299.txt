Title:
Chicken Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion, diced
1/4 cup butter
3 (10 ounce) cans chicken chunks, or chunk or shredded chicken breast
1 pint sour cream
1 (10.75 ounce) can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of celery or cream of mushroom soup
3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (8 ounce) package uncooked egg noodles
4 ounces buttery round crackers, crushed
small can diced chilies or jalapenos, optional
small can peas, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  In medium skillet, fry onion in butter until softened.  Pour into 9x13 baking dish.
In large bowl, combine chicken chunks, sour cream and soup.  Stir in 2 cups of shredded cheddar.  Stir in uncooked egg noodles.  Pour mixture over onions in baking dish.  Top with crumbled crackers and remaining cheddar.
Bake 30 minutes, or until top is golden and noodles are soft.
