Title:
Lela's Protein Mango Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2384098.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 mango, chopped, or more to taste
1/2 cup low-fat vanilla yogurt
1/2 cup almond milk
1/2 cup ice
1 scoop vanilla whey protein powder
1 teaspoon honey, or to taste (optional)

Directions:
Blend mango, yogurt, almond milk, ice, protein powder, and honey together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Lela
Description: 
Whip up a refreshing protein vanilla mango shake.
