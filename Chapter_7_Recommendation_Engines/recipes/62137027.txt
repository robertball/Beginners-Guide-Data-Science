Title:
Mozzarella Mini Quiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon vegetable oil
1 clove garlic
1/4 onion, chopped
2 oz Chipotle sausage
3 cups spinach, chopped
1 cup mushrooms, chopped
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 cup green onion, chopped
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
1.   Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2.  Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.  Add onions and garlic and cook, stirring occasionally, until onions are soft and transparent.  Chop sausage into small pieces and cook until brown.  Add in mushrooms and stir in spinach and continue to cook until excess moisture has evaporated.
3.  In a bowl, combine eggs, cheese, green onion, salt and pepper.  Stir in the cooked spinach mixture until ingredients have been evenly distributed.  Spoon into mini muffin pan about 4/5 full.
4.  Bake in preheated oven for 8 minutes, or until inserted toothpick comes out clean.  Let cool for 5 minutes before serving.

A small serving of mini quiches made with mozzarella cheese, sausages, and a variety of good-for-you vegetables.
