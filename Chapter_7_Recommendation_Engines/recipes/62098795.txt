Title:
Beef Tenderloin Steaks Topped with Horseradish and Dijon Mustard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBSP Dijon mustard
1/2 TBSP prepared horseradish
1/4 tsp dried basil
1/4 tsp dried thyme
1/4 tsp dried tarragon
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 lb beef tenderloin steaks(about 2-4 steaks)
Salt to taste

Directions:
Combine first 6 ingredients in a small bowl (Mustard through pepper).
Spread mixture evenly over top and sides of steaks.
Wrap steaks individually with plastic wrap and refrigerate 1 to 24hrs.
Preheat oven to 400 or prepare grill.
Remove steaks from plastic wrap and sprinkle with salt.
Place steaks on a roasting rack in a baking dish lined with tin foil and bake 30min for medium-rare up to an hour for well done.  (Grill until desired temp.)

Submitter: 
B.J.
Description: 
Taste buds rejoice!  Tender steaks of beef tenderlion get the royal treatment with herbs and a horseradish and dijon mustard coating.  Prep this entree for marinating a day ahead and then just bake it in the oven.
