Title:
Gluten Free Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. bag broccoli florets
12 oz. box Pacific Organic Cream of Mushroom soup
1/2 cup Hellman's mayonnaise
Small finely chopped onion
4 oz. grated cheddar cheese
2 eggs, beaten
Glutino Crackers

Directions:
Steam broccoli in bag for 4 minutes.
Combine in bowl: broccoli, soup, mayonnaise, onion, cheese and eggs.
Pour into buttered 2 1/2 qt Pyrex dish.
Optional: top with crushed and buttered Glutino Crackers
Bake at 400 for approximately 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
SCLadyA
Description: 
This traditional broccoli casserole recipe is still delicious with gluten free products.
