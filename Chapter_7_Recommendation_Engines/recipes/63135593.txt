Title:
Automatic Dishwasher Detergent Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32oz Lidded plastic Container
2 Cups washing soda
2 cups Borax
25 drops grapefruit essential oil.

Directions:
Put on a pair of rubber gloves.  Add washing soda, borax and oil to the bowl.  Stirring with your hands to mix and eliminate any lumps.
Transfer powder to your plastic storage container.
TO USE: Use 2 Tbsp detergent per wash.  You can also add 1 tsp vinegar if desired to the rinse cycle to prevent spots on silverware and glasses.

Submitter: 
TheBritishBaker
Description: 
Dishes, silverware and glassware come out shiny and clean thanks to this simple three ingredient powder containing antibacterial grapefruit essential oil.  Approx cost to make is $2.45 (enough for 32 Loads)
