Title:
Blueberry Spinach Protein Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3788729.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pint fresh blueberries
2 cups fresh spinach
1 scoop chocolate-flavored protein powder

Directions:
Combine blueberries, spinach, and protein powder in a blender; blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Deborah J Bell
Description: 
Fresh blueberries, spinach and protein powder. Easy, quick, and delicious. Make a couple hours ahead and stick it in the freezer - it's yummy that way!
