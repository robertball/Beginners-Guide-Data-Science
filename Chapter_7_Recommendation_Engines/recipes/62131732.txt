Title:
Easy and Delicious Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground beef
1/4 C. fine bread crumbs
1 egg
1/2 - 3/4 tsp. seasoned salt
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1 TBS. (heaping) dried onion flakes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and mix well.
Roll into 1 inch balls.
Bake on a cookie sheet approx. 20 minutes, turning once.

Submitter: 
CaraMeg
Description: 
I like to make these and freeze them...they go well with any sauce.  I prefer the taste of these over any packaged meatballs.
