Title:
Three Meat Ragu for Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ cup Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
½ tsp. Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
1 large Onion, chopped
4 (28-oz.) cans Whole Peeled Tomatoes
6 cloves Garlic, minced
¼ cup Italian Tomato Paste
1 Tbsp. Sugar
3 tsp. Kosher Salt, plus additional for seaoning
Fresh Ground Black Pepper
4 oz. Ground Beef Chuck
4 oz. Ground Pork
4 oz. Ground Veal
3 tsp. Dried Rosemary or Savory, crumbled
2 tsp. Dried Thyme
2 tsp. Dried Oregno
2 Bay Leaves
1 (3-inch) piece Parmigiano-Reggiao Cheese Rind (optional)
1 cup Freshly Grated Parmigiano-Reggio Cheese

Directions:
Heat Olive Oil in a Dutch oven over medium heat.  Stir in Red Pepper Flakes and cook for 15 seconds.
Add Onion and cook, stirring occasionaly, until onion is golden brown, 10 to 15 minutes.
While onion cooks, pour tomatoes and their juickes into a large bowl and crush the tomatoes with your hands.
Stir Garlic into Onion and cook until fragrant, 1 minute.  Stir in Tomato Paste and cook until brick red, 1 minute.
Add the Crushed Tomatoes, Sugar, 1 tsp. Salt, and Black Pepper.  Set the lid slightly ajar and reduce heat.
Heat large skillet over medium-high heat.  Add meats and herbs.  Season with the remaining 2 tsp. Salt and some pepper.
Break up meat with a wooden spoon and cook until it loses its rosy color, about 5 minutes.  Add to sauce with Bay Leaves and Cheese Rind, if using.  Simmer, stirring occasionally, for 1½ hours.  (If sauce gets too thick, add water).  Season with Salt and Pepper.
Just before serving, remove bay leaves and cheese ring, if used)  Stir in Grated Cheese
