Title:
Summer time Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ripe avocados, cut into bite size pieces
1/2 finely chopped sweet onion
3-4 ripe roma tomatoes, cut into bite size pieces
1T Balsamic vinegar
1T Lemon juice
2T olive oil (or any healthy fat oil)
1/4c Fresh Cilantro, chopped roughly

Directions:
In a medium size bowl, place avocados, onions, and tomatoes. Add vinegar, lemon juice, olive oil and cilantro. Gently toss to coat. EAT!

Submitter: 
Lori Rowley-Sipple
Description: 
This is a quick and healthy salad to whip up anytime!
