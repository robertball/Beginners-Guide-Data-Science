Title:
Garlic Blue Crab with potatoes, eggs sausage and corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2366862.jpg
Ingredients:
blue grabs, eggs, potatoes sausage, corn, minced garlic, garlic powder, garlic salt, old bay butter and parsley.

Directions:
Clean your blue crabs and break them in half. Boil your eggs until done. (crack them once they have cooled off). Also boil you potatoes, sausage, and corn. (All at one time, or each one alone). In a nice size pot (BIG) In another nice size pot (big size) brings about to cups of water to a boil. Add the crabs and season with all ingredients but the minced garlic and parsley. Let the crabs steamed for about 6-10 minutes while flipping them and adding more season if needed. Once crabs or done, place them in an aluminum pan. Add the potatoes, eggs, sausage and corn to the pan after the crabs. In a small sauce pan, add the about 3 stick of butter, minced garlic parsley and all other ingredients on the stove on LOW HEAT and lets everything melt while steering  it all up. Once it has all dissolved, pour it over your crabs and other sides.
