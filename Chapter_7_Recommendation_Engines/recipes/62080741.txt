Title:
Southwestern cheese dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 block light cream cheese softened
Light mayo-1 cup
2 cups light sour cream
1/2 cup your favorite salsa
1/2 a cup chopped black olives
1/2 a cup chopped green olives
1/2 a cup chopped bell pepper
1/2 a cup chopped onion
1/2 a cup chopped tomato
1/2 a cup chopped canned jalapenos
2 cloves chopped garlic
1/2 a cup chopped fresh cilantro
1/2 cup sliced green onion
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
dash worchestire sauce
Dash or two hot pepper sauce

Directions:
blend all ingredients in large mixing bowl. Serve with sliced breads or crackers or as a dip for chips.
