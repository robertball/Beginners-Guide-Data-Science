Title:
Hula's Melting Moments Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5119290.jpg
Ingredients:
Cookie Part
3 sticks soft butter
1/2 cup powdered sugar
3 cups flour
2/3 cup custard powder
Filling
3 cups powdered sugar
1/4 cup of butter
6 to 8 TBSP hot water

Directions:
Set oven to 350
Cookie Part
Cream 3 sticks soft butter and 1/2 cup powder sugar
Sift 3 cups flour + 2/3 cup custard powder, then mix into butter mixture till doughy
Roll into 40gram cookie balls, then squish into little cookies.
Bake
Now make the frosting filling.
Mix 3 cups powdered sugar + 1/4 cup soft butter
Gradually add 6 to 8 TBSP hot water.  NOTE frosting needs to be very thick so add water a little bit at a time.
If you want to change things up add 1 to 2 TBSP lemon juice or any fruit juice or jam in place of 1 to 2 TBSP of hot water.
Once the cookies are done baking put a healthy amount of frosting on one cookie and squish another cookie on top to make a sammich.
