Title:
BOCA® Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/4 medium onion, finely chopped
1 teaspoon oil
1 (1 cup) pouch frozen BOCA Meatless Ground Burger
1/2 cup barbeque sauce
1 tablespoon sweet pickle relish
2 hamburger buns, split

Directions:
COOK and stir onion in oil in small nonstick skillet on medium heat 3 minutes or until tender
ADD ground burger, barbecue sauce and relish; mix well. Cook 3 minutes or until heated through, stirring occasionally.
SERVE on buns.

Submitter: 
BOCA®
Description: 
With no greasy ground beef to brown off, relish these sweet and tangy Sloppy Joe sandwiches.
