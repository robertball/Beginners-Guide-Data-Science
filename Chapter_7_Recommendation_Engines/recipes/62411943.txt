Title:
Quinoa Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked quinoa
4 celery stalks, chopped
1 pint cherry tomatoes, cut in half
1 medium zucchini, chopped
1/2 cup parsley, chopped
1 can great northern beans, rinsed and drained
3/4 cup italian dressing
1 cup feta cheese
2 tbs parsley, chopped

Directions:
Cook quinoa according to package directions. Transfer to large bowl. Mix in the celery, cherry tomatoes, zucchini, parsley and beans. Toss with italian dressing. Add 1/2 cup feta cheese and mix well. Top with remaining feta and parsley. Chill 2 hours. For best results, chill overnight.
