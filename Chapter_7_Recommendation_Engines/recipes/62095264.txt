Title:
Avocado and Bean Burrito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-8" Whole Wheat Tortilla Shell
1/2 Large, Ripe Avocado, mashed
1/4 Cup Vegetarian Refried Beans
Sprinkle Mexican Blend or other cheese
Salsa or Hot Sauce to taste

Directions:
Spread refried beans over tortilla shell, followed by avocado, cheese, and salsa or hot sauce.
Roll into a burrito and cook for 1 minute in the microwave (can also be eaten cold)
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Lindsay McDermott
Description: 
This is a low-fat, quick, easy, and tasty meal that can be eaten for lunch or dinner, hot or cold!
