Title:
Roasted Potatoes & Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz turkey sausage, sliced
4 cups potatoes, cubed
3 cups bell pepper, chunked
1 large onion, chunked
2 Tbs rosemary (fresh preferred)
1/3 cup olive oil
1 heaping Tbs Dijon mustard
2 Tbs parmesan (fresh preferred)
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp Italian seasoning
3/4 tsp sea salt
pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Put the sausage, potatoes, bell pepper, onion and rosemary into a large mixing bowl.
Mix together the olive oil, mustard, parmesan, garlic, italian season, salt and pepper. (whisk it together.)
Pour over vegetables, and blend well.
Put onto a cookie sheet.
Bake for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Cyndi Martin McAlpine
Description: 
Found this while looking for a way to quickly cook and use a bunch of potatoes.  It was quite tasty and will be a keeper for us to use again.
