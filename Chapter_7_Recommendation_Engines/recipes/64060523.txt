Title:
Slow Cooker Santa Fe Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained
1 (14 ounce) can whole kernel corn
1 jar salsa
4 -5 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, cut into cubes
8 tortillas

Directions:
Combine the black beans, corn, and 1/2 jar salsa in a slow cooker. Lay chicken breast atop the black bean mixture; top with another 1/2 jar salsa.
Cook on High for 3-5  hours.
Add cream cheese to chicken mixture; cook another 30 minutes.
Spoon chicken mixture into middle of tortillas; roll tortillas around filling.

Submitter: 
Cindi Fortmann
Description: 
Canned black beans and corn mingle with prepared salsa and cream cheese with chicken in the slow cooker for a tangy filling for tortillas in this easy meal idea.Modified from: Slow Cooker Santa Fe Chicken
