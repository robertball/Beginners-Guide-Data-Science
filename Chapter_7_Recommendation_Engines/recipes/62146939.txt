Title:
Cranberry Pecan Tossed Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Packages Italian Blend Salad
1 8oz package Pecans
1/2 Container Feta Cheese Crumbles
3 Carrots - shredded
1 Cup dried cranberries
1 Small Red Onion - cut into thing rings
1 Cup of your favorite croutons
1 Bottle Balsamic Vinaigrette Dressing

Directions:
In a small bowl combine the dried cranberries, pecans and 1/2 cup of dressing, toss to coat.
In a salad bowl combine, lettuce mix, shredded carrots, red onion and croutons, toss together. Sprinkle the feta cheese over the salad and then top with pecan/cranberry mixture. Serve with additional dressing on the side.

Description: 
This is a quick and easy salad perfect for a family dinner or a larger party.
