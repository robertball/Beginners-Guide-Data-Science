Title:
Easy, Fast Hangover Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups of water
1 head of garlic, peeled and minced
1 small onion, chopped
1 chicken bullion cube

Directions:
Put water, garlic and onion in medium sauce pan. Bring to boil. Add chicken bullion cube, lower heat and cover. Simmer for 20 minutes. Sip slowly from coffee cup. You may eat the onion and garlic.

Submitter: 
ellie may
Description: 
This recipe came from Emma in Three Points, Arizona. She recommended it to me, and it saved the day! Couldn't be much easier, and works amazingly well when you have too much fun the night before.
