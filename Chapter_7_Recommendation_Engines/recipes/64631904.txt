Title:
Steamed Beef with Tofu and Scallions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
250 grams tofu
1/2 scallions (green part)
50 grams caulifloret
50 grams shimeji mushrooms
150 grams beef (thinly sliced or use beef scraps)
1 1/2 tablespoon soy sauce
1 1/2 tablespoon sake
1 tablespoon sugar
15 grams mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Pat dry the tofu with the use of kitchen paper. Then, slice it into bite-size pieces. Slice the green part of a scallion thinly. Slice the caulifloret into bite-sized pieces. Cut the roots of the himeji mushrooms then separate the pieces individually.
Combine the soy sauce, sake and sugar then toss it to the beef.
Add the tofu, scallions, caulifloret and shimeji mushrooms to the beef then carefully mix.
Cover then cook in the microwave for 6 minutes using 600W.
Take out then carefully mix the ingredients. Add the mozzarella cheese on top then put back the cover. Keep covered until the cheese melts then you can serve.
