Title:
Garlic Lime Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Boneless Chicken Breast Halves
1/3 Cup Olive Oil
1/4 Cup Lime Juice
1 Tsp. Garlic Powder
1 Tsp. Cilantro leaves
1/4 Tsp. Ground Cummin
1/4 Tsp. Ground Red Pepper
1/4 Tsp. Season Salt

Directions:
Put all ingredients into a zip lock bag and mix well.
Refrigerate 30 Minutes.
Grill or broil on low tempurature for 10 minutes per side or until chicken juices run clear.
