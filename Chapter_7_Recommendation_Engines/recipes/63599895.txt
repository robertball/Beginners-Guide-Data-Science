Title:
Simple Vegan Tzatziki Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14oz soft tofu, chilled
1 cucumber, peeled and quartered
Orange rind, to taste

Directions:
Combine all items in a food processor and puree until smooth.

Description:
