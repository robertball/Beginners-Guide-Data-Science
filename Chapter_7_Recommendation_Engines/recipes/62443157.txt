Title:
Sweet & Simple Strawberry Vanilla Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/635816.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can evaporated milk
1 cup vanilla yogurt
1 small banana
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups frozen sliced strawberries
liquid honey as needed

Directions:
Place milk, yogurt, banana, vanilla, and strawberries in the order listed in blender. Blend until smooth. Add sweetener to taste.

Description: 
A naturally sweet strawberry smoothie.
