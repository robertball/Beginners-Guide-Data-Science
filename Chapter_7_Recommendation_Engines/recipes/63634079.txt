Title:
Cornbread Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7oz cornbread, cubed into 1"-2" pieces
1pt grape tomatoes
fresh parsley to taste, chopped
olive oil
rice vinegar
chili powder, to taste

Directions:
Place the cornbread cubes into an aluminum tray and combine with enough of equal parts olive oil and vinegar to dampen them but not enough to make them soggy.
Place on a grill on medium heat for 5-10 minutes or until browned.  Meanwhile, also grill the tomatoes, in their own tray or on a grill topper, until hot.
Combine the cornbread, tomatoes, parsley and chili powder.  Serve.
