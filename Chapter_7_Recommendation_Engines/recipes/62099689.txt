Title:
Cranberry Citrus Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups orange juice
2 cups sugar
2 - 12 oz. bags fresh cranberries
zest of 2 oranges
1 cup walnuts, chopped

Directions:
In a large pot combine the orange juice, sugar, cranberries, and zest.  Bring to a boil and reduce heat to simmer.  Cook 10 minutes.  Remove from heat and let cool.  Stir in walnuts and refrigerate overnight before serving.

Submitter: 
Marian
Description: 
A wonderfully easy cranberry sauce that once you try it, you will never open a can of cranberry sauce ever again! Not too tart, not too sweet, and an unexpected crunch with the walnuts adds to the great taste.
