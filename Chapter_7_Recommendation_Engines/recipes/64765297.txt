Title:
Shrimp and Grits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups lower-sodium chicken broth
3 cups water
1 1/2 cups uncooked grits
1 Tbsp. canola oil
1 cup chopped yellow onion
1 cup chopped poblano chile
2 tsp. minced garlic
2 cups unsalted fire-roasted diced tomatoes
1 tsp. Worchesershire sauce
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1 tsp. kosher salt, divided
1/4 cup unsalted butter, divided
1 lb. raw shrimp
2 oz. Parmesan cheese, grated
2 1/2 Tbsp. half/half
3 Tbsp. sliced scallions
1/2 tsp. hot sauce

Directions:
Bring broth and water to a boil over high.
Whisk in grits; reduce to mediium heat.  Cover and cook for 20-23 minutes
Heat oil in large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
Add onion and poblano; cook 8-9 minutes
Add garlic; cook 1 minute
Stir in diced tomatoes, Worchestershire, pepper and 1/4 tsp. salt
Add 1 Tbsp. butter. cook, stirring often until melted
Add shrimp and cook 3-4 minutes
Add 6 Tbsp. Parmesan, half/half, remaining 3 Tbsp. butter and remaining 3/4 tsp. salt to grits
Divide in bowls; sprinkle with scallions and remaining Parmesan.
