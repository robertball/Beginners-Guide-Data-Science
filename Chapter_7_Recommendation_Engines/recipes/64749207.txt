Title:
Green Jello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6oz pkg of lime jello
2 c boiling water
8 oz cream cheese
1 small can crushed pineapple, drained
1 small jar chopped pimentos, drained
1 c finely chopped celery
1 c finely chopped nuts (pecans or walnuts)
8 oz cool whip, thawed

Directions:
mix lime jello with boiling water until dissolved.
pour hot jello over 8oz of cream cheese. stir & set aside to cool.
mix together pineapple, pimentos, celery, and nuts.
fold into jello mixture & chill until it starts to set.
mix in thawed cool whip, pour into mold or container with lid.
refrigerate covered for 4 hours.
