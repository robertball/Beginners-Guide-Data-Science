Title:
Creamy Pesto Penne with Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Knorr Creamy Pesto Sauce Mix
1 c milk
1/4 c olive oil
1/2 lb ground sausage
16 oz box penne pasta

Directions:
Boil the water for pasta and prepare according to package directions.
Cook sausage in skillet.
Prepare sauce according to package directions.
Mix all ingredients and serve.
