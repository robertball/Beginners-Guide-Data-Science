Title:
Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1088760.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lb. ground beef
2 eggs
1 beefy onion soup mix pack
1 c bread crumbs
2 c ketchup
3 oz. Worcestershire
Salt & pepper

Directions:
Mix together & form. Place in oven on 350 for 1 1/2 hours.
Cover with foil while cooking for 1st hour; then unwrap to cook additional 30 mins.

Submitter: 
laady_jr
Description: 
This meatloaf goes great with mashed potatoes, french-style green beans & cheesy baked mac 'n cheese!
