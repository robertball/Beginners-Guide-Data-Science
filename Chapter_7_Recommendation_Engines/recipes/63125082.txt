Title:
TBC Hobo Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ginger Ale
smoked kielbasa, chuncked
red potatoes, chunked
brown potatoes, chunked
cabbage, chunked
rutabaga, peeled and chunked
baby carrots
onion, chunked
smoked ham, chunked
regular ham, chunked
frozen corn or fresh corn off the cob

Directions:
Ginger ale, water and both ham chunks in large pot. Boil for an hour.
Add rutabaga, onions, carrots and the kielbasa.  Boil for an hour.
Add corn and potatoes. Boil for an hour.
Add cabbage, salt and pepper. Boil for 1/2 hour.
Serve.
