Title:
chili dog sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 onion, chopped fine
3 TBS pickle relish
3 TBS dry HOT mustard
2 cans spicy V-8 juice
1 1lb can diced tomatoes, with juice
3 TBS chili powder
1 cube beef boullion
salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
Brown beef and onion in pot, crumbling the meat into very fine portions.  Add remaining ingredients, stir.  Simmer for 1 1/2 hours, stirring occassionally,  or until it reaches desired thickness.  At intervals, taste and add more seasonings, if necessary.
