Title:
choco banana bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
~ 1 cup whole wheat flour
~ 1/4 cup all purpose flour
~ 1/3 cup splenda
~ 3 tbsp. brown sugar(not packed)
~ 1 tsp. baking powder
~ 1/2 tsp. baking soda
~ 1/4 tsp. salt
~ 1/4 cup fat free liquid egg subsitute
~ 2 tbsp. light wipped butter or light buttery spread
~ 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
~ 3/4 cup mased very ripe banana
~ 1/4 cup mini semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
1. preheat oven to 350. spray a 9x5 in. pan wit nonstick spray.
2. In a large bowl, combine both types of flour, splenda, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix well.
3. In a medium bowl, combine egg substitute, butter, and vanilla extract. Whisk until uniform. Stir in mashed banana. Add mixture to the large bowl, and stir until uniform.(Batter will be thick)
4. Stir chocolate chips into the batter. Transfer to the loaf pan, and smooth out te top.
5. Bake until a toothpick inserted in the corner comes out clean.
6. Let cool completely, about 10 minutes in the pan and 45 minutes out of the pan on a cooling rack. EAT UP AND ENJOY!!!
