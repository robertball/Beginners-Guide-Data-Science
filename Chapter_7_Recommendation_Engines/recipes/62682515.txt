Title:
red curry paste-Nam Prik Gaeng Ped Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T coriander seeds
1 t cumin seeds
5 dried red chilies, slit lengthwise, deseeded and soaked for 15 min in hot water
3 T sliced green onions
8 garlic cloves, smashed
2-3 thin slices of galangal (ginger)
2 T lemongrass, inside thickest part of stem, sliced
2 t lime zest (kaffir)
1 T chopped coriander root (cilantro)
10 black peppercorns
1 t shrimp paste (dry-fry)

Directions:
Add oil to pan and dry coriander and cumin for 5 min over low heat.
Remove and grind to powder adding remaining ingredients (except shrimp paste) and grind well.
Add shrimp paste and grind again.
