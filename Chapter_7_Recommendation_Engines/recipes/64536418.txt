Title:
Raspberry Pecan Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup fresh raspberries
1/2 tablespoon red wine vinegar
1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
2 1/2  tablespoons pure maple syrup or agave nectar
1/2 cup cold water
1/2 cup toasted pecans
(1/2 cup of additional water to thin if desired.)

Directions:
Using a standing blender or food processor, puree all the ingredients, except the pecans and water, and blend until fully smooth and creamy.
Add the pecans, and pulse until well incorporated but still maintaining some texture.
Add the other 1/2 cup of water, if need to thin.

Submitter: 
John Murphy
Description: 
Got tired of buying Ken's Steak House Raspberry Pecan dressing. So here is a healthier, cheaper recipe.
