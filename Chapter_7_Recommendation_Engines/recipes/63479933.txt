Title:
Berry Bliss Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup cold milk
1 pkg. instant vanilla pudding (4 serving size)
1 1/2 cups Cool Whip - divided
1 frozen pound cake (10.75 oz.)
1/4 cup orange juice
2 cups mixed rasperries and strawberries
1/4 cup black berries

Directions:
Place pudding mix in a bowl, add milk and whisk until well blended.
Stir in 1 cup of Cool Whip.
Cut cake horizontally into three layers.
Brush layers with orange juice.
Top bottom layer with 3/4 cup of mixed berries and half of pudding mixture.
Place next layer on top and repeat.
Cover with top cake layer.
Top with remaining 1/2 cup of Cool Whip and remaining berries.
Cover loosely with foil and refrigerate at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.
