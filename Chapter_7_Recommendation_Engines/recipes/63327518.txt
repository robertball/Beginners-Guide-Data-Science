Title:
Middle Eastern Vegetable and Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp olive oil
1 large onion, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp ground cumin
4 cups vegetable broth
2 cups water
1/2 pound (1.25 cups) lentils, picked over and rinsed
1 small eggplant, diced
2 medium zucchini, diced
1 medium tomato, chopped
zest and juice of 1 lemon
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.  Add the onion and celery.  Cook until lightly browned, stirring occasionally.  Add garlic and cook another minute or two, stirring constantly.  Add broth and lentils; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover, and simmer about 15 minutes.  Add eggplant and return to boil.  Reduce heat, cover, and simmer another 15 minutes.  Stir in zucchini and tomato and return to boil.  Reduce heat, cover, and simmer another 3-5 minutes, until zucchini are tender, but still bright.  Remove from heat and stir in lemon zest and juice.  Taste and adjust with salt snd pepper as needed.

Submitter: 
Melissa Gardner
Description: 
A delicious, flavorful, and healthy variation of vegetable soup.
