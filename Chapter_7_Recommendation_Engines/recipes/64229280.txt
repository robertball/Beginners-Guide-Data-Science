Title:
Curried Chicken with Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp oil
1 lb chicken breasts, cut in strips
1 small onion, sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp curry powder
2 carrots, sliced
1 red pepper, sliced
90 gm snow peas
1 can (375 ml) evaporated milk
2 tsp chicken bouillon
1 Tbsp cornstarch
1/3 cup water

Directions:
In an electric frypan saute chicken in oil.  Add onion and garlic and saute 2 minutes.  Add curry powder and saute 1 minute.  Add carrots, red pepper and snow peas.   Saute for a couple of minutes.  Then cove, lower heat and simmer until vegetables are cooked, but slightly crisp.
Gradually stir in evaporated milk and chicken bouillon cube.  Bring to a boil, stirring.  Reduce heat and simmer 1 minute.  Mix cornstarch with water and stir into milk.  Return to boil.
Serve with rice.
