Title:
Grands Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Grands biscuits, cut into pieces
1 lb sausage - browned, drained & cooled
2 c shredded cheese of choice
6 eggs, beaten
1/2 c milk
salt, pepper, parsley, to taste

Directions:
Heat oven to 425.  Spray 13x9 pan.  Place biscuit pieces evenly.  Top with sausage & cheese.  Mix eggs with milk, salt & pepper.  Pour over all.  Bake 15 min or until biscuits done.
