Title:
Gran's Hot Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked chicken breast, diced (3 breasts)
1 can cream of chicken soup
3/4 cup Hellman's mayo
1 cup diced celery
1 tbs lemon juice
3 hard boiled eggs, sliced
1 tsp onion juice
1/2 tsp salt
1 can water chestnuts
1/2 cup slivered almonds or potato chips

Directions:
mix above togehter and put in 2 quart greased casserole dish and top with either almonds or potato chips. Bake at 350 for 25 min(you can add chix stock if too dry)
