Title:
White Chocolate Raspberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 egg
1/3 cup milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/3 cup white chocolate chips
1 cup raspberries

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Grease muffin cups or line with muffin liners.
2.  Combine 1 1/2 cups flour, 3/4 cup sugar, salt and baking powder.  Add oil, egg, milk, and vanilla.  Mix.  Fold in white chocolate chips and raspeberries.
3.  Put mixture into muffin tins.  Sprinkle with white sugar to make a crispy top.
4.  Bake for 20 to 25 minutes in the oven, or until done.
