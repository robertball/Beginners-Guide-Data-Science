Title:
Brian's Lower Fat Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c brown sugar
2 egg whites
1/2 cup white sugar
1 cup mashed bananas
1/2 tablespoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 cup applesauce
3/8 cup fat free sour cream
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Mix and bake at 350 F for 1 hour. Lightly cover top of bread with foil last 15 minutes. Remove and cool when insert butter knife comes out clean.

Submitter: 
BRSMITH63
Description: 
Egg whites and no butter make this a lower fat option that takes like the fatter version
