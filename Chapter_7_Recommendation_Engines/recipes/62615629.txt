Title:
CoCo Peango MockTail Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 cup Peach Nectar
1 cup Mango Nectar
2 tsp cherry juice
3 tsp geradine
1/2 tsp coconut extract
1 12 ounce can lemon lime soda

Directions:
Mix all ingredients above thoroughly. Pour over Ice and serve.
