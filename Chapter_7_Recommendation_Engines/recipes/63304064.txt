Title:
Gobble-Good Turkey Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of celery soup
1/4 cup onion, finely chopped
1 cup rice, white long-grain
1 1/2 cups milk (1 1/4 cup if using instant rice)
2-3 cups leftover turkey (may substitute chicken)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large casserole dish.  Bake covered 45 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
This recipe is simple but good and a great way to use leftover turkey.
