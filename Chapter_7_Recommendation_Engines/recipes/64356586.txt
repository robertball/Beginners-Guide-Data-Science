Title:
Parmesan Crusted Chicken with veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken Breasts
Shredded Parmesan Cheese
Flavor God Garlic Seasoning
Cooking spray or olive oil
Veggies for steaming

Directions:
Heat pan with cooking spray or tiny bit of olive oil
Coat the chicken breast with Flavor God Garlic seasoning and shredded Parmesan cheese.
place chicken in pan and cook until done
Steam veggies
