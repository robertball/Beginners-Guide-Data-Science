Title:
Basic Arroz con Gandules Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
4-4 1/2 cups water
2 cups rice
1 packet sazon
a 15 oz can of gandules
1/2 cup sofrito
(A lot of times you will find chopped ham thrown in too)

Directions:
Saute the sofrito with the olive oil in a pot (6 or 7 quart pot)
Add water, rice, gandules, & sazon
Boil for 3 or 4 minutes
Lower your heat to simmer & let cook for about 35 minutes.  Cover your pot, but not with a lid that lets out the steam.
Stir it very well before serving.
