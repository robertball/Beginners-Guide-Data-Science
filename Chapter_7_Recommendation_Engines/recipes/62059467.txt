Title:
chicken and pasta casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless chicken breast
1 can of cream of chicken
1 can of cream or mushroom or celery soup
2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
2 cups of mayo
1 package of egg noodles
2 cups of crushed plain potato chips
1/4 cup of green pepper (diced)

Directions:
can of cream of mushroom soup,peppers,mayo,cream of chicken soup, and mix together. Set aside bowl..After boiling chicken, cut into bite size pieces and add to bowl of above mixture. Boil noodles only til half done, remove and mix all of the above ingredients together. place in 13x9 baking dish..top with cheese,,then place chips on top...cover with foil and bake at 350 for 45mins...

Description: 
This dish is sure to please even the most picky eaters. My children love it and they enjoy helping to prepare it. Great for those pot luck dinners too.
