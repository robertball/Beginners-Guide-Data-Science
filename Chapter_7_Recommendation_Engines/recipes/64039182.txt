Title:
Rosemary Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-½ lb small red potatoes, thinly sliced
4 large cloves garlic, minced or thinly sliced
2 green onions thinly sliced (optional)
4 T olive oil
¼ t salt
¼ t pepper
1 T white vinegar
2 t Dijon style mustard
1-4 sprigs fresh rosemary, depending on taste

Directions:
Mix the potatoes, onion, and garlic (if using sliced) in a coated pan.
Combine the olive oil, salt, pepper, vinegar, mustard and garlic (if using minced).
Pour the dressing over the potatoes and stir to thoroughly coat.
Place the rosemary sprig(s) on top of the potatoes.
Cover the pan with foil and bake at 350 degrees for 45-60 minutes, until the potatoes are tender.
Remove the rosemary sprigs and serve.
