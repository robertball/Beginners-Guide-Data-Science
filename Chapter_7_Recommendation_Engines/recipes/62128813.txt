Title:
Iced Tea Cooler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. orange juice (not concentrate)
1 Tbsp. lime juice
1 c. strawberries, quartered
1 c. raspberry flavored white tea
1 c. citrus flavored green tea
3 c. sweet tea*
Ice cubes to make 2 qts.
*Sweet Tea
1-1/2 qts. water
3 lg. tea bags
1/c. sugar
Ice cubes

Directions:
In blender, combine first five ingredients, blend until stawberries are pureed.
Add sweet tea and ice cubes, pulse until slushy.
Store in refrigerator and re-pulse, as needed, when serving.
Sweet Tea:
In 2 qt. pot, add water and tea bags.
Heat over med-high heat until boiling.
Turn off heat and allow tea to steep for about 2-3 minutes.
Remove tea bags and add sugar, stir until sugar is dissolved.
Add ice cubes, and stir, to make 2 qts.
Serve over ice or cool in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Teri Carrillo-Fox
Description: 
A mixture of sweet tea, flavored green teas and fruit, this iced tea cooler hits the spot on a hot day!
