Title:
Sugar-Free Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large package sugar-free instant pudding mix
1 1/2 cups milk
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese (reduced fat)
1 (8 ounce) container lite sugar free frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine pudding mix and milk. Mix well and let stand until thickened.
In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Add pudding and mix well. Finally, fold in whipped topping.

Submitter: 
katieleee
Description: 
Versatile cream cheese frosting flavored with sugar-free instant pudding. It will frost one 9x13 or one 2 layer cake. Use any flavor of pudding mix.Modified from: Sugar-Free Frosting
