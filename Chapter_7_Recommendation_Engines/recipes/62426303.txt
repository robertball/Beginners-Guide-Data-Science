Title:
Coleslaw Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/447988.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 large head green cabbage(about 6 cups)
2 C red cabbage
1/4 C onion
1 carrot
Dressing:
1/2 C cider vinegar
2 TBL vegetable oil
1 1/2 TBL sugar
1/4 tsp dry mustard powder
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/8 tsp celery seed

Directions:
Finely chop the first three ingredients and combine in a large bowl. Shred carrot and add to bowl.
Combine all dressing ingredients in a seal-able container and shake to mix. Pour over vegetables, mix well, cover and refrigerate.
