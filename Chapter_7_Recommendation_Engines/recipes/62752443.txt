Title:
Traditional Haroset Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
A handful of raisins
A couple of pitted dates
A few dried apricots
A bit of sweet red wine
A chopped-up apple
A handful of chopped walnuts

Directions:
Put all the ingredients in the food processor and pulse until it becomes a lumpy paste.

Submitter: 
Meir Hoberman
Description: 
This is an approximation of the haroset recipe my family made for several years.  All the quantities are approximate: please adjust them as fits the tastes of your family.
