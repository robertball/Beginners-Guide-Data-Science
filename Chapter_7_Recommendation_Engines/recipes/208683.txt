Title:
Avocado Ginger Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992136.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Chilean Hass avocado
2 oranges
1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
1/4 cup radishes, chopped
1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Cut avocado in half, remove pit, and peel. Cut into cubes and place in a medium bowl. Peel and section oranges over avocado in bowl so juice can drip into bowl. Cut orange sections into bite-sized pieces. Add radishes, ginger and salt and toss gently to mix. Cover surface with plastic wrap and chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Chileanavocados.org
Description: 
Avocado is a perfect salsa ingredient. It adds richness and flavor and partners well with almost any other ingredient. You can use radishes for a spark of color. Or use jicama or green or red pepper if you wish. Try this salsa with fish, poultry or pork.
