Title:
Sautéed Baby Bok Choy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 baby bok choy
4 tablespoons minced garlic
2 tablespoons butter
2 cans chicken broth
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Sauté the butter and garlic together over medium heat, in a sauce pot, until lightly browned. Add the chicken broth and baby bok choy and simmer until fork tender, approximately 6 minutes, giver or take. Serve.

Submitter: 
Sarah Johnson
Description: 
Baby Bok Choy have a milder taste than the full grown deal and are also more tender. I just sauté them in some chicken broth and garlic and serve them with rice and fish. My family enjoys the taste of garlic so we use quite a bit. Feel free to adjust accordingly.
