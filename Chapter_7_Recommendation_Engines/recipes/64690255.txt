Title:
Beef Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Lime Juice
5 cloves Garlic
2 medium Onions
1.52 large Peppers, sweet, red, fresh,
6 oz Beef, top sirloin,
4 Flour Tortillas, low fat
1 tbsp Canola Oil
1 tsp Salt

Directions:
Slice Beef, peppers and onions, into thin strips
Marinate with lime, garlic (mashed with salt) and other seasonings to taste
Add oil and heat.
When sizzling, cook just the beef first, making sure not to overload the pan or else the beef will steam not fry.
Add peppers and onions and cook until meat is done and vegetables are slightly wilted but still a little crisp.
Warm tortillas and serve.
