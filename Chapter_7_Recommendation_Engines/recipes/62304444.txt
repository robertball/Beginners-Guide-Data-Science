Title:
Fast Grilled Chicken Breast Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Kikkoman Soy Sauce (can use reduced sodium)
1/4 cup Clover Honey
1/2 cup Sweet Japanese plum wine
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 bunch green onion (more or less depending on preference), finely chopped
Cracked Pepper
3-4 Boneless/Skinless chicken breasts, rinsed and drained

Directions:
1) In a plastic Ziploc freezer bag, combine soy sauce, wine, honey, garlic, green onion, and pepper, and zip tightly closed releasing most of the air.
2) Massage bag thoroughly to mix all ingredients well.
3) Using a fork or meat tenderizer, score chicken breast lightly, do not pound flat.
4) Add chicken breasts to bag, massaging generously into the marinade sauce.
5) Allow to marinade at least 20 minutes before cooking.  Can be grilled or pan seared and baked.
