Title:
Spicey Chicken Marsala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 8 serving package of pasta, your choice
1 12oz can of fire roasted, diced tomatoes
1 cup marsala wine
1/2 cup half and half
2 cups parmessian cheese
4oz chopped bacon
4 tbl minced garlic, or two cloves
2 tsp fresh rosemary, not chopped
8 green onions, chopped
2 shallots, minced
4 chicken boullion cubes

Directions:
Heat iron skillet on medium for about 20 minutes.
Add breasts to iron skillet with 1 tbl of oil over medium heat.
Let them cook for about 10 minutes then turn and cook for another 10 minutes.
Add bacon,scallions and green onions and increas heat to saute...about 10 minutes should do.  Add garlic.
Add rotelle, tomatoes and wine and cook for one hour hour on low heat.  If your skillet is shallow, you may want to switch to a larger non-stick pan.
Cook the pasta according to directions on package.
Add creame and cheeses and increase to medium heat and cook for 10 minutes or until cheeses blend.
Remove from heat and let stand for 10 minutes.
Serve and enjoy!
