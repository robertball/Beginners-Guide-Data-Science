Title:
Simple Roast Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 4-5 pound whole chicken- rinsed and patted dry
1 onion- quartered
4 stalks of celery- washed and cut in half width wise
red wine
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp ground black pepper
1 tsp garlic salt
1 tsp dried parsley
1 tsp marjoram

Directions:
Place paprika, ground black pepper, garlic salt, parsley, and marjoram in a small bowl. Mix thoroughly.
Pack the cavity of the chicken with the onion quarters and celery stalks and place in a roasting pan- breast side up.
Rub the spice mixture over the chicken.
Pour just enough red wine into the roasting pan to cover the bottom; being careful to not pour over the chicken.
Cover and cook in a 250 degree oven for 4-5 hours or an internal temperature of 190 degrees.
