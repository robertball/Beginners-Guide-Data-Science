Title:
BUSH'S® Bold and Spicy Bean and Cheese Tostadas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979966.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (28 ounce) can BUSH'S® Bold & Spicy Baked Beans
1 cup chopped onion
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1 tablespoon olive oil
4 (8 inch) corn tortillas
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
1/2 cup chopped fresh tomato
1/4 cup chopped ripe olives
3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro (optional)

Directions:
In skillet, cook onion, chili powder and cumin in hot oil until onion is tender.
Stir in BUSH'S(R) Bold & Spicy Baked Beans; cook over low heat until hot. Set aside and keep warm. Combine cheese, tomato, ripe olives and cilantro, if desired. Set aside.
Heat tortillas according to package directions. Spoon bean mixture over tortillas. Top each with cheese mixture.
