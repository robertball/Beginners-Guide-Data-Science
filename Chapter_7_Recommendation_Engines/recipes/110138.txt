Title:
Mississippi Iced Tea Recipe

Ingredients:
8 ice cubes
2 1/2 fluid ounces amaretto liqueur (such as DisaronnoÂ®)
1/2 fluid ounce rum
1/2 fluid ounce triple sec
2 fluid ounces cola-flavored carbonated beverage
2 fluid ounces energy drink (such as Monster Energy)

Directions:
Fill a large glass with ice. Pour in the amaretto, rum, and triple sec. Stir, and pour in the cola beverage and energy drink at the same time to fill the glass.
