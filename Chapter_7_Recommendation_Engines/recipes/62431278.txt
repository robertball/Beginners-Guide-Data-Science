Title:
Mexican Lasagna I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground bison or grass fed beef
1/2 large onion, diced
3 garlic cloves, pressed
1 (16 ounce) can refried beans
1 cup fresh or frozen corn kernels
2 teaspoons dried oregano
1 teaspoon ground cumin
12 corn tortillas
2 1/2 cups picante sauce
1/2 cup water
2 cups Greek yogurt
2 (2 ounce) cans sliced black olives
2 cups Mexican cheese mix (cheddar/jack)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Brown bison or beef with onions and garlic.
In a large bowl, combine beef, beans, corn, oregano, cumin.
Cut 4 tortillas to fit bottom of a 9x13 inch pan. Spread half of beef mixture over tortillas. Top with 4 more tortillas. Spread with remaining mixture, and top with remaining tortillas.
In a medium bowl, mix water and picante sauce. Pour evenly over layers. Cover tightly with foil.
Bake 1 hour.
Blend Greek yogurt and olives in a medium bowl. Spoon over lasagna, and top with cheese. Bake uncovered until cheese melts, about 5 to 10 minutes.
