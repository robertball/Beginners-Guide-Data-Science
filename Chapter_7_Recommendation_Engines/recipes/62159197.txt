Title:
Peanut Butter & Honey Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices bread
2 T peanut butter
1 t honey
1/2 t softened butter or margarine

Directions:
In a bowl mix peanut butter, honey, and butter until a smooth consistency.
Smooth onto bread and enjoy.

Denise McCabe
