Title:
BBQ Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon Market Pantry™ vegetable oil
1/2 (3.5 ounce) package Fresh Cuts diced red onion
1 pound ground beef
2 tablespoons Archer Farms™ Las Cruces BBQ Seasoning
1 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups Market Pantry™ ketchup
1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
3 tablespoons brown sugar

Directions:
Heat oil in large saucepan over medium-high heat.
Add onion and saute until translucent.
Add ground beef and brown.
Add BBQ seasoning and salt. Cook until meat is completely brown.
Stir in ketchup, vinegar, sugar, 1/2 cup water and reduce heat to simmer until thickened.
Serve on toasted hamburger buns.

Submitter: 
SuperTarget
Description: 
Ground beef is sauteed with onion and seasoned with barbeque seasoning and sauce before being served on buns. A great quick meal everyone will enjoy.
