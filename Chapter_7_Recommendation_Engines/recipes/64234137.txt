Title:
Grandmother's Buttermilk Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
2/3 cup white sugar [Reduced to 1/3 sugar]
2 eggs
1 cup buttermilk [powder with water]
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup cornmeal
1 cup all-purpose flour [1/2 white, 1/2 wheat]
1/2 teaspoon salt
2/3 cup corn

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8 inch square pan. OR [grease cast iron skillet with veg oil and place in over while preheating]
Melt butter in large skillet. Remove from heat and stir in sugar. Quickly add eggs and beat until well blended. Combine buttermilk with baking soda and stir into mixture in pan. Add corn. Stir in cornmeal, flour, and salt until well blended and few lumps remain. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes OR [15- 20 min for skillet], or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
