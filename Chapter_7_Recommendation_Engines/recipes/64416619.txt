Title:
Fish Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-10 fish filets (tilapia, cod, halibut)
flour tortillas
sliced cabbage
Sauce
1/2 cub mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream or 1/2 cup Greek yogurt, plain
1 lime, juiced
1 Tbsp capers, minced to taste
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp dried dill weed
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper, to taste (optional)

Directions:
Prepare and cook fish to your preferred method.
Sauce:
Blend mayo and sour cream (or yogurt) together; blend in lime juice, then capers, cumin, oregano, dill, and cayenne; it can be served immediately or can be made ahead.
Assemble tacos by putting 1-2 fish fingers (depending on size or tortilla), add a sprinkle of cabbage, a drizzle of sauce, then roll up.
