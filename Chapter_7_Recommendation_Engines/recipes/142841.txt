Title:
Almond Mocha Ice Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/603946.jpg
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons instant coffee granules, regular or decaffeinated
4 tablespoons boiling water
1 3/4 cups chocolate low fat milk
1/2 teaspoon almond extract
Whipped nondairy dessert topping (optional)

Directions:
Dissolve instant coffee into boiling water. Add in LACTAID(R) Chocolate Lowfat Milk, and almond extract and stir.
Serve over ice.
