Title:
Venison Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground venison
1/2 onion-chopped
1 carrot-halved and sliced thinly
1 stalk celery-sliced thinly
1/2 cup frozen corn
2 cups beef broth
3/4 cup red wine
2 cups potatoes- cubed
2 cloves garlic
1 tsp marjoram
1 tsp thyme
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup mushrooms-sliced (may used canned)
2 TBSP cornstarch
2-ready made pie crusts

Directions:
In large skillet brown venison, onion and garlic.  As this is browning in a separate saucepan, boil broth, potatoes, carrots, and celery to soften.  Once meat is browned, add broth and vegetable mixture to skillet. Stir to mix. Add rest of ingredients. Ladle out small amount of broth, mix with cornstarch and re-add to skillet.  Cook until mixture thickens.  Place 1 room temperature pie crust into deep dish pie pan.  Once mixture is thickened pour into pie pan, place 2nd pie crust on top. Pinch crusts together.  Cut a few vent whole in top with knife or fork.  Bake @ 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Mendy
Description: 
a very savory venison pie that my whole family loved.  It was my first time cooking with venison, and I was quite pleased with the outcome.
