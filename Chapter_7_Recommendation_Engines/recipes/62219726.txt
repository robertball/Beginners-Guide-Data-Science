Title:
Prosciutto Pesto Chicken Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded to 1/4 inch thickness
1 cup prepared basil pesto
chopped sun dried tomatoes,added to pesto
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
sliced prosciutto,wrapped around each breast
season with salt,pepper, garlic

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Spread 2 to 3 tablespoons of the pesto sauce onto each flattened chicken breast. Place one slice of cheese over the pesto. Roll up tightly, and secure with toothpicks. Place in a lightly greased baking dish.
Bake covered for 20 mins,uncovered last 15 mins. in the preheated oven, until chicken is nicely browned and juices run clear.
