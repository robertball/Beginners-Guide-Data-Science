Title:
Carrot Spoon Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c finely grated carrots
1.5 c flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
3/4 tsp salt
4 eggs
1 c brown sugar
1/2 c butter

Directions:
preheat oven 350.  Microwave carrots and 3 tbl water until tender about 4 minutes, then drain.  In a bowl mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, eggs, brown sugar, and butter.  Fold in carrots.  Bake 50-60 minutes.
