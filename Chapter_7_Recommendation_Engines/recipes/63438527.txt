Title:
CORN CASSEROLE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can whole kernel corn
1 can cream corn
8 oz. carton sour cream
2 eggs (beaten)
1 box Jiffy corn muffin mix
1 stick butter or oleo

Directions:
Mix all together, spray a 2 qt. casserole with Pam. Pour mixture in pan.
Bake @ 350 degrees for 50 - 55 minutes till golden brown.

Submitter: 
Marjorie Taylor
Description: 
A great casserole for something fast for covered dish dinners.
