Title:
Cheeseburger Rotini Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1178026.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box Rotini
1 pound ground beef
1 small onion chopped
1 (10 oz.) can Ro-Tel tomatoes and green chilis
1 (10 oz.) can Tomato soup
1 packet dry onion soup mix
1 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon minced garlic
salt and pepper to taste
2-4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Prepare rotini according to package.  While rotini is cooking, saute the ground beef and onion until browned; drain excess fat.  Add tomatos, tomato soup, onion soup mix, sour cream, garlic, 1/2 cup cheese, and salt and pepper
Spray a 9x13 baking dish with pam. Put half of the cooked rotini in the bottom of the dish; then half of the meat mix; then 1 cup cheese, and repeat.
Bake for 30 minutes.
