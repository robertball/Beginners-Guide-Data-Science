Title:
Cranberry Apple Sauce II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1061294.jpg
Ingredients:
12 ounces fresh or frozen cranberries
6 ounces unsweetened apple juice concentrate

Directions:
In a sauce pan, combine the cranberries and apple juice. Cook over medium-low heat until berries burst. Additional sugar may be added if desired. Mix well, chill and serve.

Submitter: 
Mary Beth
Description: 
Good low sugar recipe.  Extra sugar is not necessary with the apple juice concentrate.
