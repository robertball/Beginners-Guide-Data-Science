Title:
Old Fashioned Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 lb. salt pork diced
2 medium onions diced
1 clove garlic minced
1/2 c. sliced celery
1/2 c. sliced carrots
1/4 c. chopped parslely
1 C. chopped cabbage
1 1/2 c. chick peas
1 16 oz. can diced tomatoes
1 guart chicken broth
1/2 t. basil
1/4 t. thyme
1/2 c. broken spaghetti
1 c. chopped spinach or escarole
salt, pepper to taste
1 c grated parmesan cheese (fresh is best)

Directions:
Brown salt pork & onion
Add garlic, celery, carrots, parsley & cabbage
Add chicken broth and 4 cups water .
Add remaining ingredients except for the last five.
Bring to boil.  Cover simmer 30 minutes.
Add escarole or spinach & spaghetti.
Cover simmer 10 minutes-season with
salt & pepper.
Serve with fresh grated parmesan over soup in bowl.
Serve with crisp Italien bread & butter.

Submitter: 
MOLLYSPITCHER
Description: 
This recipe is authentic Italien Minestrone Soup.  It's from an old recipe my aunt gave.  It's over 60 years old.
