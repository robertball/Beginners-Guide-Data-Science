Title:
Pomegranitini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/25471.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fluid ounces vodka
2 fluid ounces orange liqueur
2 fluid ounces pomegranate juice
1 cup crushed ice
1 twist lemon zest

Directions:
Pour vodka, orange liqueur, and pomegranate juice in a shaker, and add crushed ice. Shake vigorously, and strain into glass. Garnish with twist of lemon zest.
