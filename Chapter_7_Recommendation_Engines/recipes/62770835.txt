Title:
Stir Fry Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb flank steak
1 T soy sauce
1.5 t cornstarch
1/4 t sugar
1 T oil
Vegetables: carrots, green beans, celery, garlic, onion, ginger root, peppers
Cooked rice.

Directions:
Combine all but the vegetables in a bowl or ziplock and refrigerate up to 1 day.
Cook the rice if you haven't already.
Cook each vegetable separately by stir frying in about 1 T of oil adding a splash of water and dash of salt with the vegetable. Remove to serving dish.
Stir fry beef in a bit more oil. Add back vegetable juices and additional water as needed to make a juicy sauce. Add back vegetables and heat through.
Serve over cooked rice.
