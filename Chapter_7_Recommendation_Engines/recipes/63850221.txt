Title:
Prize Winning Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
2 c. sugar
1 & 1/2 C. Oil
4 Eggs
2 c. all purpose flour
3 c. grated raw carrot
3 tsp cinnamon
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
2 tsp vanilla
Frosting:
1 stick butter, softened
1 8-oz pkg cream cheese, softened
1 box powdered sugar
1 c. chopped pecans

Directions:
for Cake: Cream together sugar, oil and eggs.  Add dry ingredients and vanilla and mix well.  Finally, stir in grated carrots.  Grease and flour 2 9" round baking pans.  Bake at 350 for 35-45 minutes.  Let cool before icing.
For Frosting:
Cream butter and cheese.  Gradually add pwd sugar until smooth.  Ice between layers, sprinkle nuts between layers and on top of cake.
