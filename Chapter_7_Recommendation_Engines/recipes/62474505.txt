Title:
Baked Cucumbers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cucumbers
2T wine vinegar
1 1/2 t salt
1/8 t sugar
3 T melted butter
1/2 t dill
3 T green onion
1/8 t pepper

Directions:
Peel, scoop out seeds, and slice into 3?8" strips-2" long
Toss with next 3 ingredients, let stand 30 min and drain.
Mix remaining and bake 1 hour at 375*. Tossing 2-3 times during baking.
