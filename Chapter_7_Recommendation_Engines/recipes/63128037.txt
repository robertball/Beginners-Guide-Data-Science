Title:
MaMaw's Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/797522.jpg
Ingredients:
4 quarts Tomatoes
3 Whole Bell Pepper
3 Medium Onions
1 Cup Vinegar
5 Whole Jalapeno Peppers
Salt
Pepper
Garlic

Directions:
Dip tomatoes in hot water until skins slip off easily.
Chop tomatoes in food processor. Add peppers, onions, salt, pepper, and garlic to taste.
Cook until thick.

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
This is a cooked salsa that Mamaw (Eva Noble) made every summer.
