Title:
Sous Vide Flank Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5lbs Jim Beam marinated flank steak (from Town Meats)
Optional: Toasted buttered french bread or garlic bread, fresh mozzarella, arugula, horseradish

Directions:
Take meat pieces out of bag and place in a ziploc bag (without the liquid)
Cook using sous vide method at 134 degrees for 2 hours
Remove bag from hot water and place in a bowl of ice water for 30 minutes
Bring back to room temperature with moderate temperature water
Sear for 1 min on each side in a hot cast iron skillet
Slice against the grain into thin strips
Serve on its own or on a sandwich with the optional ingredients
