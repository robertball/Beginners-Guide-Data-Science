Title:
Mixed bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can green beans
1 can kidney beans
1 can yellow beans
1 can garbanzo beans
1 cup celery
1 red onion cut in fine rings
1 green pepper, diced fine
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup vinegar
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 tsp salt
ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Drain all beans and rinse with cold water
Add celery, red onion and green pepper
Combine sugar, vinegar and oil and shake well
Mix all together
Add spices to taste
Refrigerate several hours
