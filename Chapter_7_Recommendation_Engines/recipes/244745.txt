Title:
Parmesan Peppercorn Wing Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2588283.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ranch salad dressing (such as Hidden Valley® Original Ranch®)
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon fresh cracked black pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Combine ranch dressing, Parmesan cheese, butter, black pepper, and garlic powder in a saucepan over medium-low heat; cook and stir until sauce is smooth and thickened, 10 to 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Meghan Hess
Description: 
Serve on or on the side of hot wings!
