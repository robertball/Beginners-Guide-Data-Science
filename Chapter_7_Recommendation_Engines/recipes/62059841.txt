Title:
Lemon Chess Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar         1 Tbsp. flour
1 Tbsp. corn meal    1 cup milk
4 beaten eggs    1/4 cup melted butter
4 Tbsp. grated lemon peel (tiny grate)
1/4 cup lemon juice
One unbaked pie shell

Directions:
Toss sugar, flour and corn meal with fork.  Add to remaining ingregients and mix together thoroughly.  Pour into unbaked pie shell and bake 35-45 minutes at 350 degrees.
cool and serve.
