Title:
Eggplant Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1061707.jpg
Ingredients:
Sea salt, as needed
2 large eggplants, thinly sliced (1/4 - 1/3")
4 eggs, beaten
3 cups bread crumbs
2 Tbsp Parmesan cheese, grated
2 Tbsp salt-free garlic herb seasoning
Olive oil mister, as needed
1 (28 oz)jar spaghetti sauce, divided
1 (16 oz) pkg mozzarella cheese, shredded & divided
1 (5 oz)ctr Parmesan cheese, shredded & divided
1/2 teaspoon dried basil

Directions:
Slice eggplant and sprinkle both sides with sea salt. Place in colander and let drain for 30 minutes.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Rinse slices and pat dry. Dip in egg, then in bread crumbs. Place in a single layer on a cookie sheet. Spritz with olive oil. Bake in preheated oven for 10-12 minutes on each side.
In a 9x13 inch baking dish spread spaghetti sauce to cover the bottom.  Place a layer of eggplant slices in the sauce.  Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.  Repeat with remaining ingredients, ending with the cheeses.  Sprinkle basil on top.
Bake in preheated oven for 35 minutes, or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Jessica Paige
Description: 
Salted eggplant before breading it and then misted it with olive oil before baking to a golden brown. Made my own seasoned bread crumbs to lessen sodium, also added grated Parmesan cheese to the bread crumb mixture to also crisp it up as well.Modified from: Eggplant Parmesan II
