Title:
Chocolate Caramel Raspberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984637.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
25 caramels, unwrapped
4 (1 ounce) squares semi-sweet chocolate
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
1/4 cup Smucker's® Seedless Red Raspberry Jam
1/2 cup whipping cream
1 pound cake, sliced

Directions:
In heavy saucepan, over low heat, melt sweetened condensed milk, caramels and chocolate; stir until smooth.  Stir in butter and jam until smooth.
Serve warm over pound cake. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
Caramel and raspberries flavor this decadent creamy sauce. Serve with pound cake, or use as a dip for fruit.
