Title:
Marilyn's Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups flour
1 1/4 cup white sugar
1 1/4 cup brown sugar
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 tablespoon cinnamon
4 large eggs
2/3 cup vegetable oil
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 cups mashed bananas

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients.
Combine eggs, vegetable oil, vanilla, and mashed bananas, mix well.  Add to dry ingredients and mix well
pour into 3 regular greased loaf pans.
Bake at 325 degrees for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
