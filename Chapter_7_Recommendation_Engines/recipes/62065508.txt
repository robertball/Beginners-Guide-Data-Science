Title:
Italian Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean beef
1 Tbsp butter or margarine
1 Tbsp olive oil
1/2 lb sliced fresh mushrooms
1/2 large onion, sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup crushed tomoatoes in puree
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/2 tsp dried oregano
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup red wine
1/2 cup beed broth
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Cut meat into 1-inch cubes. Brown in butter and oil over medium-high heat. Remove.
2. Saute mushrooms for 3-4 minutes. Remove. Saute onion and garlic for about 3 minutes. Add tomatoes, parsley, oregano, salt and pepper. Simmer for 5-10 minutes.
3. Add wine, broth and cheese, simmer 5 minutes longer. Stir in meat and mushrooms.
4. Cover and cook for about an hour. Add a little water or broth if needed.

Submitter: 
Michelle Wilcox
Description: 
Nice cold weather meal. Serve with rice or noodles.
