Title:
Narwhal's Favorite Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2469995.jpg
Ingredients:
25 Marshmallows
25 Chocolate kisses or chocolate bar nibs
Powdered sugar
Cocoa powder
Kale (optional)

Directions:
1. Press the chocolate kiss into the marshmallow.
2. Mix the cocoa powder and sugar until blended.
3. Roll the marshmallow in the cocoa powder/sugar blend until coated. You may need to very lightly moisten the marshmallow first.
4. Serve on a bed of kale, like a dinosaur would eat!
