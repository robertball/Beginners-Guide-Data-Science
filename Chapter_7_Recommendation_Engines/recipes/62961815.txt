Title:
Whole Wheat Banana Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/730761.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
4 teaspoons baking powder
3 egg
2 cups (almond) milk, plus more if necessary
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp almond extract
1 tsp vanillia
3 tablespoon cane sugar or artificial sweetner
2 chopped bananas
1 tbls brown sugar
coursely chopped white chips

Directions:
Sift together flour and baking powder, set aside.  Beat together the egg, milk, salt and sugars, vanillia, almond extract in a bowl. Stir in flour until just moistened, add bananas & chips, and stir to incorporate.
Preheat a heavy-bottomed skillet over medium heat, and spray with cooking spray.  Pour approximately 1/4 cup of the batter into the pan for each pancake.  Cook until bubbly, about 1 1/2 minutes.  Turn, and continue cooking until golden brown.

Description: 
The Banana's in these whole wheat pancakes are so sweet and moist that they don't even need butter when eaten while hot!  This is our Saturday breakfast, healthy for me; yummy for husband and kids.Modified from: Whole Wheat Blueberry Pancakes
