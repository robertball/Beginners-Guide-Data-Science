Title:
Homemade Grapenuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/918790.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups whole wheat flour
1/4 cup brown sugar
3/4 t baking soda
1/2 t salt
1/4 cup barley syrup or molasses
1 cup buttermilk
1/4 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Whisk dry ingredients together.
Add molasses, buttermilk, then butter stirring well to incorporate.
Place in a greased 8x8" pan and bake @ 350 for 30" or until toothpick comes out clean.
Let cool completely, then cut cake into large pieces and crumble onto cookie sheets (I needed 2).
Bake at 275, stirring every 15 minutes, for apx 60 to 75 minutes depending on how large your chunks are.
