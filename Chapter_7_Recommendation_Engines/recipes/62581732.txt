Title:
Tex Mex Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can chicken broth
1 can diced tomatoes
1 can black beans, rinsed & drained
1 can corn, drained
1 can cream style corn
2 c. cooked meat (optional)

Directions:
Mix all in a saucepan & bring to a boil. Reduce heat & simmer uncovered for 5 min. Serve with sour cream & shredded cheddar cheese.
