Title:
Grilled Garlic and Rosemary Lamb Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lamb loin chops and 2 lamb chop ribs
1/2 c olive oil
1/4 c red wine vinegar
1/2 to 1 lemon, juiced
1/2-1 tsp dijon mustard
5-7 garlic cloves, minced
rosemary sprigs (2-3 T), minced
salt

Directions:
combine marinade
marinade meat for 2-4 hours or over night
grill to desired doneness
served with garlic rosemary aioli

Submitter: 
seren
Description: 
Grilled lamb chops marinated in garlic and rosemary
