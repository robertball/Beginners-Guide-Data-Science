Ingredients:
12 oz browned sausage
3 slices bread torn
1/2 cup monterey jack cheese
6 eggs
1 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1/2 cup cheddar cheese

Directions:
Beat eggs lightly, add milk, salt and pepper, and mix well.  In 2 qt dish sprayed with pam, layer bread, then sausage then jack cheese, then egg mixture, then cheddar.  refrigerate overnight.  Allow to sit 15 minutes at room temp before baking.  Bake at 325 for about 45 minutes.
