Title:
Twice Baked Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/954952.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs red potatoes, baked
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 lb sliced bacon
3 cups (24 oz) Sour Cream
2 cups (8 oz) Shredded mozzarella cheese
2 cups (8 oz) Shredded cheddar cheese
2 sliced green onions

Directions:
Microwave potatoes for about 4-5 minutes in the microwave
Cook bacon in frying pan and let cool. Then crumble.
Cut baked potatoes in 1 inch cubes and place half in a 9 x 13 greased baking dish. Sprinkle half of the salt, pepper, bacon, sour cream, and cheeses. Repeat Layer.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
Sprinkle with green onions.
