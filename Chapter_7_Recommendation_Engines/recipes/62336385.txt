Title:
Awesome Fettuccine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz Fettuccine or Anglehair pasta
2T. Olive Oil
1 T. chopped Garlic
1c sliced fresh Mushrooms
1/4 c. White Wine
1/4 c. Chicken Broth
1 lg Tomato, peeled and diced
Chopped fresh Basil, 7 leaves
2 T. Butter
Salt/Pepper
Parm. Cheese for Garnish

Directions:
Add Pasta to boiling salted water.
As the pasta cooks, heat the olive oil in a 12" saute pan.
Add the garlic, saute 30 seconds.
Add the mushrooms, saute approx 2 min.
Add the white wine, reduce by 1/2, then add the chicken broth, reduce by 1/2.
Add the tomato, basil and chicken/shrimp or whatever you desire (great without meat also).
Saute for 1 minute, then add the butter to the sauce.
Mix with the cooked, drained pasta.  Salt/Pepper to taste.  Top with Parm Chz and Enjoy !

Submitter: 
Cathy Brown-Bergmann
Description: 
I wanted a fettuccine recipe that wasn't heavy, rich and full of unwanted calories.  I found this and it is absolutely fantastic.  Add grilled chicken, cooked shrimp or whatever does it for you.
