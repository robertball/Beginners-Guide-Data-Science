Title:
Healthy Heart Breakfast Parfait Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/360353.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup rasins
1 tsp. finely shredded orange peel (set aside
2 Tbsp. orange juice
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 an oz. pkg of reduced fat cream cheese, softened
1 Tbsp. sugar
2 cups fresh raspberries, blueberries, sliced strawberries, and/or cut up peaches
1/2 cup low fat granola
Honey (optional)
Shredded orange peel (optional)

Directions:
1. In a small microwave-safe bowl combine raisins and orange juice. Cover and microwave on high power for 30 to 45 seconds; let stand 1 minute to plump raisins. Stir in vanilla, set aside.
2. In a medium bowl combine cream cheese and sugar; beat with an electric mixer on low to medium speed until smooth. Stir in raisin mixture and orange peel.
3. In four tall glasses layer half the cream cheese mixture, half the fruit, and half the granola. Repeat layers, drizzle with honey and top with additional orange peel.

Submitter: 
Hillari Brenza
Description: 
Fruit is a powerful ally against heart disease, and fresh ingredients make a delicious difference in a heart healthy diet.
