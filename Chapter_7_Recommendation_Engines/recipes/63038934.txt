Title:
Breaded Porkchops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/763028.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pork chops, 1/2 inch thick
2 Tbsp flour
1 egg, stirred
1 cup bread crumbs
1 tsp rosemary
1/2 tsp salt
pepper
2 Tbsp melted butter

Directions:
Combine bread crumbs, rosemary, salt and pepper in a shallow pan.
Put flour in a plate or shallow pan.
Put stirred egg in a shallow bowl or pan.
Coat the chops with the flour.
Dip each chop in the egg, then in the bread crumbs, turning until it is covered.
Brown the chops in butter or vegetable oil until browned.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes, turning once. Uncover and bake about 14 minutes longer.
