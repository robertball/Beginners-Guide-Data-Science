Title:
Clams Oreganata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cans clams with juice
1 cup bread crumbs
1/4 cup melted butter.
Oregano, parsley, garlic, salt and pepper to taste.
24 Clam shells
1/2 cup parmesean
4 oz mozzarella

Directions:
Mix clams, bread crumbs, butter, and spices in a bowl.
Fill clam shells with mixture.
Sprinkle with parmesean and mozzarella cheese over the clams.
Bake at 350* for 15-30 minutes until top is slightly golden.
