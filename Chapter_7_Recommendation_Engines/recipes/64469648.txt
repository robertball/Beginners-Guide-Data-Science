Title:
Karen's Dinoriffic Dinomite Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Pillsbury crescent rolls
1/2 - 1 lb lean ground beef
2-3 tbs. sweet barbecue sauce
8 oz. bag shredded cheddar cheese
prepared mashed potatoes
1 whole egg
1 tbs. water
Salt and pepper

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 2. cook meat in skillet 3. when meat is mostly cooked, add salt, pepper, and barbecue sauce 4.Separate crescent triangles and place flat on baking sheet 5.Sprinkle teaspoon of cheese onto center of each triangle 6. Spoon teaspoon full of meat mixture over cheese 7. Spoon teaspoon of mashed potatoes over the meat mixture in each triangle 8. Fold each corner toward center of triangle cover the filling and overlapping so there are no gaps 9.Mix egg and water. Brush egg wash over tops of puffs 10. Bake for 15 minutes in 375 oven 10.Let cool and enjoy!
