Title:
Famous Hot Dog  pear Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 4 Bartlett Pears
2. 2 lg onions
3. 2 green sweet peppers
4. 1 Red sweet pepper
5. 1 Jalapeno pepper
6. 1 and 1/2  Cup Sugar
7. 2.25 teas mustard seed
8. 3/4 tea allspice
9. 1/4 tea ground cinnamon
10. 3/4 tea turmeric
11. 1 tab. salt
12. 1 and 1/2  cups white vinegar
13. 1/2 cup water

Directions:
Chop 1-5 ingred...place in colander..over sink pour boiling water over veg...drain thoroughly and place drained veg in large stockpot.
In med bowl combine 6 7 8 9 10 & 11....add to stock pot and mix well
add 12 and 13 to pot and bring to a boil ...simmer 20-30 min set aside to cool...can be used immed or can store glass jars in fridge or can hot bath can it
