Title:
Rum Runner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985621.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 ounce Captain Morgan Original Spiced Rum
1/4 ounce blackberry liqueur
1/4 ounce creme de banana liqueur
2 ounces orange juice
1/2 ounce grenadine
8 ounces crushed ice

Directions:
Add Captain Morgan Original Spiced Rum, blackberry liqueur, creme de banana liqueur, orange juice, grenadine, and crushed ice in a blender.
Blend until slushy and pour into glass.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
A tropical paradise of Captain Morgan Original Spiced Rum with blackberry, banana, orange and grenadine flavors is served blended and ice cold.
