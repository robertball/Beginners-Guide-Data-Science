Title:
Mediterranean Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/858557.jpg
Ingredients:
Salad:
1/2 box of pasta (about 4 ounces total)
1/2 bag baby spinach (about 4 ounces, cut into bite sized pieces)
1 cup red pepper (diced)
1/2 cup red onion (diced)
1 can/jar of small artichoke hearts (about 6 ounces, quartered)
1/3 cup feta cheese crumbles
Salt/pepper to taste
Dressing:
1/4 cup olive oil
1/8 cup red wine vinegar
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 tsp fresh basil (or ½ tsp dried basil)
1 1/2 tsp fresh oregano (or ½ tsp dried oregano)
¼ tsp ground mustard seed
1/8 tsp salt
1/8 tsp ground black pepper

Directions:
Cook pasta as directed,rinse in cold water, and then set aside.
Wash spinach and set aside to dry.
Chop the pepper and onion into small, even pieces.
Drain artichokes and cut into quarters or bite-sized chunks.
Cut spinach into bite-sized pieces.
Mix pasta with all vegetables in a medium to large bowl.
Add feta cheese to bowl.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
In a seperate bowl whisk the olive oil, vinegar, and all the herbs/seasonings together.
Toss with dressing.
Chill in refrigerator for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld.

Submitter: 
Melissa
Description: 
A colorful salad that is both sweet and savory.  Perfect for a picnic or hot summer days.
