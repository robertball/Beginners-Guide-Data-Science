Title:
no bake reeses peanut butter squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12/ c graham cracker crumbs
1 box powdered sugar
1/12 c peanut butter
1 cup butter, melted
1 bag choco chips

Directions:
use wax paper in bottom of pan
melt chips in microwave
mmix crumbs, sugar, p. butter
blend in butter
LAYER, 1st 1/2 of choco chips..
NEXT crumb mixture
THIRD p/butter mix
top w/rest of choco
CUT WHILE WARM
