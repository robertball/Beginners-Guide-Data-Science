Title:
Fruit Salad in Seconds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/422238.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pint fresh strawberries, sliced
1 pound seedless green grapes, halved
3 bananas, peeled and sliced
1 (8 ounce) container strawberry yogurt

Directions:
In a large bowl, toss together strawberries, grapes, bananas, and strawberry yogurt. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
CATHY BYRON
Description: 
Caught in a pinch for a salad when unexpected company showed up at mealtime, I improvised with this combination. Instantly it became one of my family's favorites. Best of all, it is quick and easy. You can use lemon yogurt instead of strawberry, if you wish.
