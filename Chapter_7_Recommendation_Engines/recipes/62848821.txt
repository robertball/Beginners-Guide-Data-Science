Title:
Luncheon Seafood Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb small cooked shrimp
1 lb cooked crab
4 T lemon juice
1/2 C Mayo
1/2 yogurt or sour cream
1/4 c parsley
3 T chopped chives
1 T minced onion
1 t Lowrey's salt
1/2 t thyme
1/4 t white pepper
1 C sliced black olives
1 C sliced celery
1 large avacado
1 med tomato
lettuce

Directions:
Combine shrimp and crabmeatand toss with 2 T lemon juice. Chill. Mix mayo and yogurt with parsley, chives, onion, Lowrey's, thyme and pepper. Add remaining 2 T lemon juice. Add seafood mixture along with the olives and celery and mix well. For each serving, arrange lettuce on serving plate and top with a mound of the seafood mixture. Slice the avacado and tomato and arrange around the salad.

Submitter: 
Genevieve Johns Seivert
Description: 
This a the perfect combination of seafood and crunch! Great summer salad.
