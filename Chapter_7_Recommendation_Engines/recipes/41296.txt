Title:
Hot Ham Hoagies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989083.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup barbecue sauce
3/4 teaspoon ground mustard
3/4 teaspoon garlic salt
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1 pound thinly sliced fully cooked ham
lettuce leaves and sliced tomato, onion and Swiss cheese
6 hoagie or submarine buns, split

Directions:
In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the first five ingredients; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 15 minutes.
Place lettuce, tomato, onion and cheese on buns; top with ham mixture.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home
Description: 
These satisfying sandwiches have a delicious hearty filling. They're simple to prepare and great for a gathering, too.
