Title:
Mitzie's Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 TB butter
1 onion, diced
2 tsp minced garlic
3 TB flour
8 oz beef broth
3/4 tsp prepared mustard
2 tsp Worcestershire
1/4 cup light sour cream
1/4 cup light cream cheese

Directions:
Brown ground beef with salt & pepper. Drain.
Saute onion and garlic in butter 3-5 minutes.
Add flour; brown 1 minute.
Add meat back to pan. Stir.
Add beef broth & bring to a boil. Add mustard & Worcestershire. Cover & simmer for up to 1 hour (less is ok).
Just before serving, add cream cheese & sour cream. Serve over egg noodles.

Submitter: 
JARRIE
Description: 
I couldn't find a beef stroganoff recipe using ground beef that I liked anywhere, so I asked on Facebook, and one of my friends messaged me her personal recipe, which I tried and loved! Serve over egg noodles.
