Title:
Brunch Cassarole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 slices stale white bread (sandwich-cut), cubed
3/4 pound cheddar cheese, grated
1 large can mushrooms
2 pounds Swiss Brown and Serve sausage, or Original Brown and Serve
2 1/4 cups whole milk (not 2%)
3/4 tsp dry mustard
4 extra large eggs
SAUCE
1 can cream of mushroon soup
1/3 can whole milk

Directions:
Brown the sausage in the oven and then cut the sausage into bite size pieces.
Grate cheese and cube bread.
Layer into buttered cassarole in the following order:
A. - Bread
B. - Cheese
C. - Sausage
D. - Mushrooms
Beat eggs, milk and mustard together.  Pour over the cassarole, cover and refigerate overnight.
FOLLOWING MORNING
Remove in the morning and return the cassarole to room temperature.
Mix the can of mushroom soup and 1/3 of soup can of milk.
Pour over cassarole.
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Lorraine Friberg
Description: 
Great for a crowd.  Made the day before.  Very tasty.
