Title:
Whiskey Sidecar Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3669871.jpg
Ingredients:
3 fluid ounces whiskey
1 1/2 fluid ounces orange liqueur
1 fluid ounce lemon juice
3/4 fluid ounce simple syrup
2 cups lightly crushed ice

Directions:
Pour whisky, orange liqueur, lemon juice, and simple syrup in a blender; add ice, cover, and blend until slushy consistency.

Submitter: 
Cally
Description: 
This frozen sidecar is great to cool off in the summer heat.
