Title:
Air Fryer Baked Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  Large Russet potatoes, about 8 oz each
1/2 Teaspoon coarse sea salt
1 Tablespoon peanut oil
Snipped chives, optional
Sour cream, optional
Butter, optional

Directions:
Preheat the fryer to 200
Scrub the potatoes and dry well
Brush the potatoes with the oil and sprinkle with salt
Place them in the basket and place the basket in the air fryer.
When done, slice the potatoes, lengthwise, and serve with your favourite toppings
Note: The potatoes do not need to be pricked with a fork before cooking or turned while cooking.
Test  for doneness by piercing with a fork.

Submitter: 
Bren
Description: 
Beautifully crusty on the outside and light and fluffy on the inside, just like a great baked potato should be!
