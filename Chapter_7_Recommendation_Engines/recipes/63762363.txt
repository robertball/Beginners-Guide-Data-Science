Title:
Nearest Thing To Mom's Eggplant Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 eggplants
1/2 C melted butter
1/2 C chopped onions
S & P to taste
2-3 eggs
1 C milk
1 C cracker crumbs

Directions:
Cook eggplants in salted boiling water until soft and easily pierced with fork. Drain and mash.
Add butter, onions and salt and pepper; blend well
Beat eggs w/milk and stir into eggplant mixture
Stir in cracker crumbs
Bake in 2 1/2 qt. dish that has been sprayed w/butter or oil cooking spray.
350 degree oven for 45 min.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
I think this is the eggplant recipe that Mom made.  Has all the ingredients that I remember Mom using.
