Title:
Avocado Banana Walnut Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6350151.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium avocado
2 small bananas
2 large eggs
0.50 cup milk
2 cups Whole Wheat Flour
0.50 cup Light Brown Sugar
0.25 cup white Sugar
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp Salt
0.50 cup walnuts, ground or chopped
1 tsp Cinnamon

Directions:
In stand mixer, cream bananas and avocado together.
Add eggs and milk and mix together.
In separate bowl, whisk together all dry ingredients except walnuts.
Mixing on low, slowly add dry ingredients and continue until incorporated.
Add in walnuts and mix 1 more minute.
Heat oven to 350*F.
Divide batter in 12 paper lined cups in muffin tin.
Bake 20 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
