Title:
Peanut Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 C. peanut butter
3 T dry roasted peanuts
2 T brown sugar
2 T fresh orange or lime juice
3 T lite soy sauce
1/4 tsp five spice powder
1/4 C dry sherry or port wine
**

Directions:
Place all of the ingredients into a glass jar.  Heat in the microwave 30 seconds on high.  Seal tightly and shake vigorously.  Remove the lid and heat for an additional 30 seconds then replace the lid and shake again.  Pour over sit fried vegetables or seasoned and COMPLETELY COOKED chicken, short ribs or pork. ** The dish may be garnished with additional peanuts or sliced green onions.

Submitter: 
Sally Anne
Description: 
A quick peanut sauce to add to cooked vegetables, pork or chicken.
