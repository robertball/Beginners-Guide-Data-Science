Title:
BUTTERBALL COOKIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 CUPS BUTTER
1 CUP POWDERED SUGAR
2 TSP VANILLA
4 1/2 CUPS FLOUR
1/2 TSP SALT
1 1/2 CHOPPED WALNUTS
CHILL
BEAT BUTTER THEN ADD POWDERED SUGAR & VANILLA
SIFT TOGETHER SALT & FLOUR AND ADD TO ABOVE THEN ADD
CHOP NUTS

Directions:
BAKE 10-12 MINUTES AT 350
