Title:
Pinwheel Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 (1 ounce) packet Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix
2 green onions, minced
4 (12 inch) flour tortillas, warmed
1 (4 ounce) can diced green chilies, rinsed and drained
1 (2.5 ounce) can sliced black olives, rinsed and drained

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, Ranch and onions until blended.  Spread evenly on tortillas.  Blot dry chilies and olives on paper towels.  Sprinkle equal amounts of chilies and olives over cream cheese mixture.  Roll tortillas tightly and cover with Glad(r) Cling Wrap.
Chill at least 2 hours.  Unwrap and cut rolls into 1-inch pieces.  Discard ends. Serve with spirals facing up.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
Tortillas filled with a creamy Ranch spread are rolled and cut to resemble pinwheels.  Great for children or a party.
