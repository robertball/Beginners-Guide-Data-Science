Title:
Creamy Cucumber Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/957437.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup plain yogurt
1/2 cucumber, peeled and finely chopped
1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground white pepper

Directions:
In a blender, combine the yogurt, cucumber, lemon juice, garlic, salt and pepper. Blend until smooth and refrigerate until chilled.

Submitter: 
Michele O'Sullivan
Description: 
Use this delicious dressing on a fresh green salad.
