Title:
Healthier (but still awesome) Awesome Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/277378.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (10.75 ounce) cans low-fat, low-sodium condensed cream of mushroom soup
3 pounds chuck roast
1 large onion, chopped
2 cups sliced carrots
1 lb small red potatoes
1/2 lb string beans
1/4 cup sjopped parsely

Directions:
In a slow cooker, mix cream of mushroom soup, chopped onion and pot roast.
Cook on High setting for 3 to 4 hours, or on Low setting for 8 to 9 hours. Add vegetables 1.5 hours before it's done.

Submitter: 
MakeItHealthy
Description: 
This is a very easy recipe for a delicious pot roast, I make it healthier by omitting the onion soup mix, using low-fat and low sodium cream of mushroom soup, and adding vegetables!Modified from: Awesome Slow Cooker Pot Roast
