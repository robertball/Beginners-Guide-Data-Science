Title:
Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 to 1 1/2 lbs ground beef
Taco Seasoning
1 head of lettuce
2 medium tomatoes, sliced small
1 lb shredded cheddar cheese
1 large bottle catalina dressing
1 large bag tortilla chips

Directions:
Brown ground beef at medium heat. Season as desired with taco seasoning. Drain and cool. Toss chopped lettuce, tomato and cheese in a large bowl. Add dressing and ground beef, toss well. Crush tortilla chips and add to salad. Add dressing and mix well. Keep refrigerated until serving.
