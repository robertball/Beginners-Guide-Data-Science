Title:
Bisquick Garlic Cheese Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Bisquick Mix
2/3 Cups Milk
1/2 Cup (2oz) Chedder, shredded
2 Tbsp Butter
1/8 tsp Garlic Powder

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 450
2.  Stir bisquick, milk and cheese until soft dough forms.
3.  Drop dough by 9 spoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheet.
4.  Bake 10 mins.
5.  Stir melted butter and garlic powder together and brush on top of warm biscuits.
