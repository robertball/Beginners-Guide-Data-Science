Title:
Maple Mocha Brownie Layer Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
brownie batter for a 9"x13" pan
2 cups whipping cream
2 tsp. instant coffee granules
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp. maple flavoring
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Prepare 9" x 13" brownie batter.
Bake in two 9" round pans x 20 min. @ 350.
Cool for 10 min. before removing from pans to cool on wire racks.
Beat whipping cream with coffee granules until stiff peaks form.
Gradually beat in sugar and flavorings.
Frost the cooled cake as a layer cake with the whipped cream frosting.
Store in fridge.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Two layers of brownies frosted with maple mocha whipped cream frosting!
