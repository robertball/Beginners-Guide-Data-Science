Title:
apple,bacon,and cheddar panini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices of bread,lightly buttered
1 slice of bread plain
1/4 medium sized apple,peeled,and sliced very thin
4 peices of bacon,cooked
and 1/4 cup of shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
preheat a panini press
place one of the buttered pieces of bread,butter side down on the panini press
place the 1/4 cup of cheese on the slice of bread
put the plain peice of bread on top of that
place your bacon and apples on top of that and cover with the other peice of bread butter side out
close your panini press and cook until well browned

Submitter: 
Karen Steinke Reilly
Description: 
three slices of bread with cheddar cheese,bacon and sliced apples all heated through on a panini press until browned on the outside this will send your taste buds to another world...enjoy
