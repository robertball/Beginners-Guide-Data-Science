Title:
Tex Mex Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2418158.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cp Jasmine Rice
3/4 White Onion, Medium, Diced
1 med Jalape√±o - Seeded, Diced
1 can (10 oz.)Rotel Diced Tomatoes & Green Chilies - UNDRAINED
1 tsp. Salt
10oz. Water
1 stick of butter
1/2 cup Kraft Shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
In a medium sauce pan, melt better over medium-high heat.
Add Onions & Jalapeno stir in and cook until onions are translucent (about 4min).
Add can of Tomatoes and Green Chilies - KEEP the can - fill with water about 3/4. Add to pan.
Stir in rice.
Bring mixture to a boil, reduce heat to simmer about 20 min.
Top mixture with shredded cheese, allow to melt.
Stir in cheese and serve.
Serve on Tacos or as a side. Top with additional shredded cheese.
