Title:
CRUSTLESS COCONUT CUSTARD PIE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/946889.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 c. all purpose or whole wheat flour
1/2 c. sugar
12 oz can of evaporated milk
4 oz. milk
4 eggs
1/4 c. butter, melted
1 tbsp. vanilla
1/4 tsp. salt
1 1/2 c. flaked coconut

Directions:
Pour evaporated milk into a measure cup and fill with milk to 16 oz. Then combine first 8 ingredients in container of electric blender and process for 10 seconds. Stir in coconut. Pour into a buttered 9" deep dish pie plate. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes or until set. (Pie will puff, then fall slightly.) Cool. Chill thoroughly.
