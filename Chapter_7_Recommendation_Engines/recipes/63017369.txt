Title:
Larson Cheesy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. frozen hashbrowns
2 cans cream of chicken soup
8 oz. sour cream
2 cups grated cheddar cheese
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 c. melted butter
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and place into a 9x13 baking dish.
Top with 1/4 c. melted butter and 2 c. crushed corn flakes or potato chips.
Bake for 50 minutes at 350 or until bubbling and tint of brown on top.
