Title:
Chicken with Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 4 oz whole chicken legs (or 4 parts legs, thighs or drumsticks)
1/2 cup broccoli, chopped
1/2 cup red bell pepper, chopped
1 medium-large carrot, chopped
1 large celery stalk, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1 cups Basmati rice, uncooked
1 cup italipasta small bow ties, uncooked (or 1/2 cup medium bow ties)
3 tbsp Extra virgin olive oil
3 tbsp Chefmaster 52% oil margarine
3 tsp low sodium chicken bouillon powder
2 tsp black pepper
1.5 tsp rosemary
1.5 tsp thyme
1 tsp sage leaves
3 tsp dried parsley
3.5 cups water

Directions:
saute all veggies with rice in large skillet or wok, until rice is browned
add pasta, spices, chicken bouillon and water, stir to mix
lay the chicken parts into liquid as it comes to a boil
cover and cook for 25-30 minutes or until liquid is absorbed and chicken is cooked
