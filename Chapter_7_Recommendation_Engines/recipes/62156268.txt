Title:
Super Party Spinach Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter
8 oz sour cream
8 oz cream cheese, cut into small squares
1 small jar marinated artichoke hearts, chopped (reserve juice to place in crockpot along with artichoke pieces)
1 1/2 pkgs. (16 oz) frozen spinach cooked according to package directions
1 c. grated parmesan cheese
8 oz shredded Monterrey Jack or Mozzarella or Four Cheese Italian blend cheese (can also do a combination of the three types)
1/2 to 1 large onion, diced
1 to 2 t. garlic

Directions:
Spray crock pot with non-stick cooking spray.  Place all ingredients in crock pot and stir to combine.
Cover and cook on low 1 1/2 to 2 hours or until cheese is melted, stirring occasionally.
Serve with tortilla chips, bagel or pita chips.

Submitter: 
Daisy K
Description: 
This is a super easy variation of the restaurant style dip.  Very creamy and has a good flavor!  The crockpot makes it easy to throw it all in and then work on other things in the kitchen!  Perfect for game day parties or any get together with family and friends!
