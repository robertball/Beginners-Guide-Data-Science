Title:
Team Four Tuna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2782839.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons minced red onion
2 pepperoncini peppers, minced
1 tablespoon mayonnaise
1 tablespoon rice vinegar
2 imitation crab legs, chopped
2 (5 ounce) cans tuna, drained

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, stir together red onion, peppers, mayonnaise, vinegar, imitation crab, and tuna. Chill if desired.

Submitter: 
Scuba Rich
Description: 
Not your ordinary tuna fish sandwich! It has a bite but very tasty!  This is a favorite on my team. Serve on your favorite bread.
