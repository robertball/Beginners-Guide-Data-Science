Title:
Baked 4 Cheese Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/494752.jpg
Ingredients:
250 g pkg. cream cheese
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 tbsp grated romano cheese
1/2 cup mayonnaise or miracle whip
3/4 tsp garlic powder
2 tbsp finely chopped red pepper
2-3 green onions finely chopped
1/2 pkg frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
pinch of cayenne pepper
1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Mix first 5 ingredients with hand or stand mixer until well mixed.
Add next 2 ingredients and mix until just incorporated.
Add spinach and mix until just incorporated.
Transfer mixture to small ovenproof dish.
Sprinkle with cayenne pepper to taste.
Top with cheddar cheese.
Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
Remove for oven and allow to sit for 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Kelli F
Description: 
This dip is great served with pita chips, tortilla chips, bread or vegetables.
