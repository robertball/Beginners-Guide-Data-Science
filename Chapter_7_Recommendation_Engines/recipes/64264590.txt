Title:
Slow Cooker Barbecue Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon creole seasoning
3 pounds chuck roast
15 ounces tomato sauce
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup diced green pepper
1/3 cup brown sugar
2 cubes beef bouillon
1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder
1 clove garlic, minced
1 teaspoon mustard powder
Hot sauce, to taste

Directions:
Combine flour and creole seasoning, rub the into the roast. Brown roast in a large skillet. Place roast in bottom of slow cooker. Pour in the tomato sauce, onion, green pepper, brown sugar, bouillon, chili powder, garlic and mustard powder. Mix well.
Cover slow cooker and cook on high setting for 8 hours OR on low setting for 14 to 16 hours.
