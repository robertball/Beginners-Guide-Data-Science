Title:
Big Ray's Foil-Wrapped Fish with Creamy Parmesan Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3771867.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tilapia fillets (1 lb.), thawed if frozen
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 Tbsp dried chopped onion
1 tsp granulated garlic
1/8 tsp. ground red pepper (cayenne)
2 zucchini, sliced
1/2 red bell pepper, cut into strips

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray 4 large sheets of heavy-duty foil with cooking spray. Dry fillets and place 1 fish fillet on center of each sheet; in small bowl mix mayonnaise, parmesan cheese, onions, garlic and red peppers.  Spread with mayonnaise mixture.
Top with remaining ingredients; fold to make 4 packets.
Bake 10 to 12 min. or until fish flakes easily with fork.
Note: To grill, heat grill to medium-high heat and heat for same time.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Tender fish fillets are topped with mayonnaise, Parmesan and crisp vegetables, then wrapped in foil packets for easy baking/grilling.
