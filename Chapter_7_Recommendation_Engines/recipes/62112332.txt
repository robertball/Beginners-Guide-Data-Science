Title:
Italian Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
medium potatoes
3/4 C. butter divided
1/2 C. Parmesan cheese
1 C. Italian bread crumbs
1/2 C. chopped onion
Cayenne Pepper, Black Pepper and Salt to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.  Peel and dice potatoes.  Melt 1/2 C. butter.  Cool, pour into plastic bag.  Add potatoes, bread crumbs and onion.  Shake until potatoes are evenly coated.  Line a 10x14 pan with foil.  Pour potatoes evenly into pan.  Dot with remaining 1/4 C. butter over potatoes.  Sprinkle with parmesan cheese, salt and pepper.  Bake, uncovered until potatoes are soft.

Submitter: 
Doreen P
Description: 
A great way to bake potatoes with seasoning!
