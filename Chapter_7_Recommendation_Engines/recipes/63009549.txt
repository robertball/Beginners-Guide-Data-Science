Title:
Tark's Tragic Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter
1 1/2 c. graham cracker crumbs (one cello package in box)
1 14 oz can sweetened condensed milk
8 oz semi sweet chocolate chips
1 1/2 c. coconut flakes
1 c. chopped nuts

Directions:
Melt butter in 9 X 13 pyrex baking dish.  Spread graham cracker crumbs over evenly.  Pour can of sweetened condensed milk over top.
Add chips, coconut, and nuts. Press down lightly.
Bake 25-30 minutes at 350 degrees.
