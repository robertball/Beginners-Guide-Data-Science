Title:
Spicy Seasoned Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1tsp chili powder
1/2tsp salt
1/4t garlic powder
1/4t onion powder
1/4t white pepper
1/8t cayenne pepper
3/4c flour, divided
Water
Vegetable Oil
1 Medium Potato

Directions:
Slice potato with skin on into match stick slices.  Soak in water so they won't turn brown.
Combine chili powder, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, white pepper, cayenne pepper and 1/2 cup flour in shallow dish.  Add just enough water to make a batter the consistancy of thick pancake batter.
Pour 1 inch of oil into large skillet and heat on medium high heat.
Dip potatoes into 1/4c flour then add to batter one at a time and add to skillet.  Fry about 5 minutes until golden
brown.
Remove from skillet and drain on paper towels.

Description: 
These crisp, spicy fries go great with burgers!
