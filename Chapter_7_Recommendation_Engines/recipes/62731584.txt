Title:
Goolash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 oz Hunt's Tomatoe Sauce (Italian)
14.5 oz Del-Monte chunked chili-style stewed tomatoes
1 lb ground beef
1 lb macaroni noodles

Directions:
Cook noodles al dente.
Brown beef.
Mix in tomatoes and tomatoe sauce.
Cook until warm.
Add noodles right before serving.
