Title:
Hawaiian Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 ounces pineapple chunks
2 1/2 cups ham strips, cooked
1/2 cup green pepper
2 tablespoons butter
2-4 tablespoons vinegar
2 tablespoons brown sugar, packed
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoons corn starch
1 1/3 cup water
2 tablespoons green onion chopped
1 1/3 cups instant rice

Directions:
Drain pineapple, reserving juice; add enough water to juice to make 1 1/2 cups liquid.  Saute ham and green pepper in butter.  Combine pineapple liquid, vinegar, brown sugar, mustard, salt, pepper and corn starch; mix well.  Add to ham; cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until sauce is thickened and transparent.  Stir in pineapple.  Combine water, salt, pepper and green onions in a medium saucepan; bring to a boil.  Stir in rice; cover and remove from heat.  let stand 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Nannette Turner

Simple and fast and it is going to satisfy even the most finicky eaters.
