Title:
Rendezvous Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c white vinegar
4 c water
1/3 cup Rendezvous seasoning
Rendezvous seasoning
1/2 c salt
1/4 c pepper
1T garlic powder
1T oregano
1T celery seed
1T papricka
1T chili powder

Directions:
The top  is the basting sauce.
Cook meat over grill 325-350 degrees
Start ribs bone side down until golden brown, baste 2x with sauce then flip.
Baste every 10 min for an hour; 30min per side.
