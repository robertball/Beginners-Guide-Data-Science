Title:
Pork Chops to Live For Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons oil
4 pork chops
1 large onion, sliced
4 cloves garlic
1 package mushrooms
1 can each cans cream of mushroom/chicken soup
2 cups milk

Directions:
Brown chops in hot skillet
saute onion slices mushrooms and garlic
Place pork chops into a slow cooker, and arrange sliced onions/garlic/mushroom over meat. Mix soup and milk and pour over the meat and onions.
Cover, and cook on High for 4 to 5 hours, or on Low for 8 to 10 hours.
