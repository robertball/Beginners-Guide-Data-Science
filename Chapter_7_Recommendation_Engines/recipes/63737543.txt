Title:
Vegetable Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1037620.jpg
Ingredients:
1	tablespoon unsalted butter or margarine
1	cup white or brown rice
¼	cup minced onion
1/8	teaspoon each salt, pepper, and garlic powder
½	to ¾ cup minced vegetables, such as broccoli and carrots
2	cups low sodium chicken broth

Directions:
1.	In a saucepan over medium heat melt butter and cook onion until transluscent.
2.	Add rice and stir to coat, add vegetables and stir.  Let warm for about 20 seconds stirring frequently.  Turn up the heat and add the seasonings and broth.  Stir; cover and bring to a boil then turn down to a simmer.  Simmer for 20 minutes then remove from the heat and let set covered for another 20 minutes.  Then Serve

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
easy rice side dish that will use up tid bits of vegetables or leftover cooked vegetables.  Any veggies will work.  so use your favorites.
