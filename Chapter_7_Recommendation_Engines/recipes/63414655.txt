Title:
ROASTED POTATOES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/921662.jpg
Ingredients:
1 PKG (1 OZ) RANCH DRESSING MIX
2 LBS SMALL RED POTATOES, QUARTERED
1/4 CUP EVOO

Directions:
PLACE POTATOES IN A BAGGIE OR BOWL & ADD OIL, TOSS TO COAT.
ADD DRESSING MIX TOSSING AGAIN TO COAT.
BAKE ON AN UNGREASED BAKING PAN @ 450 DEGREES.
