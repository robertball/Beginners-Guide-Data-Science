Title:
No Soak Cuban / Guatamalan Style Pinto Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb dry pinto beans washed
1 bunch green onions chopped
1 jalepeno pepper chopped
1 poblano pepper chopped
10-12 sprigs cilantro
4 slices bacon
2 tbsp chopped garlic
1 regular can diced tomatoes
1 bay leaf
1 tsp dried oregano
1 tbsp ground cumin
10 cups beef broth

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
In an oven proof dutch oven, finely slice bacon and cook till crisp.
Add chopped jalepeno, poblano, green onion, cilantro.
Stir and cover for 2-3 minutes for moisture to form.
Uncover and stir to get up all bits of brown at bottom of pan.
Add rinsed dry pintos, garlic, can of diced tomatoes, broth, cumin, oregano, bay leaf.
Bring to heavy boil for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
Tightly cover dutch oven and place in oven for 3 to 4 hours.

Description: 
A favorite eatery of ours cooks authentic Cuban / Guatamalan style beans. Have landed on their secret for terrific beans - it's in the Sofrito. No presoak, but begin this 3-5 hours before eating time.
