Title:
Heathy Potato Leek soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Leeks chopped
4 Medium Potatoes (2 lb) Cubed
32 oz Vegetable Both
1 3/4 cup water
4 TBSP olive oil
3/8 tsp of Cayenne powder
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Clean and Chop Leeks, heat 2 TBSP olive oil in stock pot, add chopped leeks.  Cook for 10min stirring frequently, add remaining Olive Oil intermittently.
Add Vegetable Both, water and cubed potatoes.  Bring to boil, then reduce heat and cook for 25 minuets stir occasionally.
Use submersible hand blender to blend until smooth or transfer in batches to blender and puree.
stir in cayenne powder and salt
