Title:
Bacon Spinach Artichoke Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3885027.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cloves Garlic (If you don’t have fresh garlic, use jarred diced garlic)
1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
¾ Cup 2% Reduced-Fat Milk
1 Tablespoons Whole Wheat Flour
1 Teaspoon Fresh Cracked Pepper
2 Tablespoons Shredded Parmesan Cheese
14 oz Pre-made Whole Wheat Pizza Crust (I use Boboli)
1 Cup Baby Spinach
1 Cup Shredded Reduced Fat Mozzarella Cheese
½ Cup Diced Chicken Breast
2 Slices cooked Bacon, crumbled
½ Cup Jarred Artichoke Hearts, drained
½ Cup Crumbled Reduced Fat Feta Cheese
1 Teaspoon Red Pepper Flakes, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
To make the garlic sauce-
Heat a skillet on medium heat and add olive oil. Add diced garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
Add flour and pepper and cook for 1 minutes, whisk constantly.
Slowly add milk and whisk constantly to make a roux.
Cook for 3 minutes.
Remove from heat and stir in parmesan cheese until melted.
To make the pizza-
Lay pizza crust on parchment paper.
Spread garlic sauce evenly over pizza.
Place spinach over sauce in an even layer.
Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over spinach in an even layer.
Evenly distribute diced chicken, bacon, artichoke hearts, and feta cheese on top.
Place pizza directly on oven rack and bake for 12 minutes.

Guilt free Bacon Spinach Artichoke Pizza with homemade garlic cream sauce!  Easy to make, low fat, and cooks in 12 minutes!
