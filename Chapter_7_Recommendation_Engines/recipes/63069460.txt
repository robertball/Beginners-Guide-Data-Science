Title:
HERB ROASTED BRINED TURKEY Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 cups water
1/2 cup kosher salt
1/2 cup sugar
3 cloves garlic,crushed
2 bay leaves
several fresh sage leaves

Directions:
Mix ingredients in large stock pot.Add turkey and marinate no longer than 6 hours.Rinse turkey before roasting.

Submitter: 
koko
Description: 
A brine that adds moisture to your turkey.
