Title:
Sausage Pepper Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/517250.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb regular ground sausage
1 can Grands (8)biscuits
1 med/lg bell pepper or 1/2 pkg frozen bell pepper
1 white onion or 1/2 pkg frozen white onions
2 cups shredded cheddar

Directions:
Chop peppers and onions. Cook sausage, onion, and pepper mixture til sausage is done. Drain very well. Cut biscuits into quarter slices. In a greased, non fluted bundt pan place one half of biscuit slices, then a layer of sausage, pepper mixture and 1 cup shredded cheddar. Repeat Layering in same order. Bake at 350 for 30 to 40 minutes. I check this w a chopstick for doneness. Let bread cool in pan before removing. Serve warm. Freeze leftovers.

Submitter: 
d newman
Description: 
Wonderful as a breakfast bread. Delicious w soups. Can be sliced, individual slices frozen, and reheated.
