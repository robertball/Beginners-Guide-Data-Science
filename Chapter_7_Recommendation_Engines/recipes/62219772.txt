Title:
Cabbage Soup with Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz V-8
6-8 celery ribs, diced
2 cans diced tomatoes (used fire roasted)
32 oz beef broth
1 c. water
2 ham steaks, diced
2 smoked ham hocks
1 onion, diced
1 1b shredded cabbage
1 c. shredded carrot
1 envelope onion soup mix
1 t. dried parsley
pepper to taste

Directions:
Add all ingredients to 6 qt pot, as listed.  Bring to a boil.  Cover and simmer for atleast 2 1/2 hours until cabbage is tender.  Remove ham hocks.  Stir and serve.

Submitter: 
Amy Hague Miller
