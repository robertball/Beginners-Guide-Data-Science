Title:
Montreal Peppered Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976268.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive oil
1/4 cup soy sauce
4 teaspoons McCormick® Grill Mates® Montreal Steak Seasoning
2 pounds sirloin or strip steak

Directions:
Combine olive oil, soy sauce and Grill Mates(R) Montreal Steak Seasoning in a large self-closing plastic bag or glass dish.  Add steak and seal bag or cover.  Refrigerate 30 minutes or longer for extra flavor.
Remove steak from marinade; discard marinade.  Preheat grill or broiler.  Grill or broil steak 8-10 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
Montreal Peppered Steak is an easy way to give steak a restaurant quality flavor.
