Title:
Avocado, Tomato, and Feta Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/669003.jpg
Ingredients:
2-3 cups elbow macaroni cooked al dente
8 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
3 ? jalapeno peppers, seeded and diced
4 ounces crumbled feta cheese
4 avocados - peeled, pitted and diced
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
to taste: fresh parsley [or cilantro] chopped
to taste: salt and ground black pepper
to taste: choice of garden fresh herbs:  thai basil, lemon thyme, oregano etc
to taste: choice of garden fresh greens:  spinach, beet greens, arugula etc, cut into thin strips with scissors.

Directions:
Cook and drain pasta, cool under running water and let drain again.  Toss into a large bowl with  a bit of oil.
Combine the tomatoes, jalapeno peppers, herbs, sliced greens, chopped parsley [or cilantro] and feta cheese in a large bowl; toss together. Add the diced avocado; drizzle the lemon juice over the avocado to keep it from turning brown. Mix into the cooked and cooled pasta. Season with salt and pepper. Mix well and serve.
The oil and herbs serve to coat the pasta; no dressing has been added.

Submitter: 
Gerri Anne
Description: 
This fresh salad uses tomatoes, accompanied by  jalapeno peppers, avocado, and feta cheese. Use garden fresh herbs and greens to make it supremely tasty and healthy.Modified from: Cilantro, Avocado, Tomato, and Feta Salad
