Title:
Hot Jezebel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar Pineapple Preserves
1 jar Apricot Preserves
3 Teas. Horseradish
Black Pepper to taste.
1 brick Cream Cheese
Crackers

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a bowl and mix on high for 1 minute or until it has a nice smooth consistency.  Pour over cream cheese(you may not need all of the mixture, just put in a jar and save in fridge for up to a week)
Scoop onto crackers and ENJOY!

Description: 
A yummy and spicy cracker topping.
