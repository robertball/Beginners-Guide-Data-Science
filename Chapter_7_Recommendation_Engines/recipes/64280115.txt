Title:
Chunky Vegetable Chili- slow cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Red Beans
4 ribs celery
1 small head cabbage
4 chopped carrots
1 can tomato sauce
1 can stewed tomatoes
6 cloves garlic
2 tbs chili powder
4 tsp dried oregano
2 tsp salt
2 cup water

Directions:
In slow cooker, combine all ingridients. Cover, coon on low 5 1/2 to 6 hours.
