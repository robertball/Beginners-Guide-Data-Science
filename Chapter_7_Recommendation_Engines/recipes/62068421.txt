Title:
Orange Sherbet Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 gallon orange sherbet (softened)
2 gallons Sunny Delight
1 liter 7-up
1 liter orange soda
The recipe makes 2 punch bowls, so 1/2 all ingredients if necessary.

Directions:
1. Place 1/2 of the orange sherbet in a punch bowl
2. Poor 1/2 of the remaining ingredients over the orange sherbet and serve imediately
3. Repeat directions for the 2nd punch bowl

Submitter: 
Sarah V
Description: 
I have served this creamy orange punch recipe at many showers and it's always a hit!
