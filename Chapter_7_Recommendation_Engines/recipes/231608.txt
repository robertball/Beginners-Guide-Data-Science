Title:
Banana Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/994339.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups milk
2 large ripe bananas
2 tablespoons slivered shelled pistachios
1 tablespoon honey

Directions:
Blend milk, bananas, pistachios, and honey in a blender until smooth and frothy, about 2 minutes.

Submitter: 
Autumn Leaves
Description: 
My husband taught me this recipe. It's yummy. Add a scoop of vanilla ice cream if you like (not to the blender, just on top).
