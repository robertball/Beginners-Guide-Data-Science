Title:
Cranberry Ginger Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz Fresh Cranberries
1 apple, cored and diced
1 C Sugar
1/2 C crystalized ginger, chopped
1 small yellow onion, chopped
1/3 C Raisins
3 T lemon Juice
2 t minced garlic
1 t dijon mustard
1/4 lemon peel, chopped
1 t ground cinnamon
1/2 t salt
1/4 t nutmeg

Directions:
rinse and pick over cranberries.  Combine all ingredients in large saucepan and bring to a boil, stirring often.  Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered for 10-15 minutes, until berries have burst.  Stir occasionally.  Simmer until thick, about 45 more minutes.
Yield 3.5 cups
