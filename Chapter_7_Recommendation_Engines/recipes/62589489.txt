Title:
Curried Chicken with Rice and Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 pounds cut up chicken pieces
4 tablespoons olive oil
4 tablespoons curry powder
2 small white potatoes,cut into wedges
1 onion chopped
1 scallion chopped
salt to taste
pepper to taste

Directions:
Rinse chicken and pat dry. 2. Season chicken with salt, pepper and curry powder. 3. In a large skillet heat oil until almost hot, add onion and scallion.  4. Add chicken, skin side down. 5. Add potatoes (scatter around). 6. Brown chicken until lightly browned on both sides. 7. Simmer 30 minutes over medium heat. Serve.

Submitter: 
Nanasnthakchen
Description: 
White potatoes are used as thickening agent in this recipe and they taste great after absorbing the curry sauce!. My husband would pick out the potatoes and not eat the rice.Modified from: Curried Chicken with Rice
