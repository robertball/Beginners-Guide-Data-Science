Title:
Fruit Pizza I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 1/2 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoon cream of tartar
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
8 ounce cool whip

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until smooth.  Mix in eggs. combine the flour, cream of tartar, baking soda and salt; stir into the creamed mixture until just blended. Press dough into an ungreased pizza pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 12 to 15 minutes, or until lightly browned. Cool.
In a large bowl, gently fold cream cheese with cool whip until light. Spread on cooled crust.
Arrange desired fruit on top of filling, and chill.

Submitter: 
Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
Description: 
This is a delicious, attractive dessert, perfect for a children's birthday party. The crust is made from cookie dough pressed into a pizza pan. After the crust is baked and cooled, it's frosted with sweetened cream cheese and decorated with slices of fresh fruit. Chill well before serving.Modified from: Fruit Pizza I
