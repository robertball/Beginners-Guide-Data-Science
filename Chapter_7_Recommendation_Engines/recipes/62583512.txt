Title:
Broccoli Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 eggs, beaten
1 small onion, chopped
1 package frozen chopped broccoli
1 1/2 Tablespoon butter, melted
2 cups cottage cheese
2 packages Jiffy cornbread mix
2 Tablespooons sugar

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients together and mix well.  Pour into a greased 9 X 13 inch baking pan.  Bake in a 350 degrees oven for about 35 minutes or until done.

Submitter: 
Lorraine Friberg
Description: 
Very good and easy to make.  Makes a large 9 X 13 pan.
