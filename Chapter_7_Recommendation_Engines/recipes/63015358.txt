Title:
CHICKEN PARMIGIANA CASSEROLE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/756314.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup oil
1 onion,chopped
1 carrot,diced
3 cloves garlic,minced
1 tsp.dried oregano
1/2 tsp.each salt & pepper
1-28oz.can tomatoes
1/4 cup tomato paste
1-14oz.can artichoke hearts,drained,halved
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
6 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/2 cup flour
1 egg
1/4 cup bread crumbs
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/2 tsp.dried oregano
1/4 tsp.cayenne
pinch of salt

Directions:
In saucepan,heat 1 Tbsp.of the oil over medium heat;cook onion,carrot,garlic,oregano,salt and pepper untill soft,about 5 minutes.Add tomatoes,breaking up with a spoon;add tomato paste.Bring to a boil;reduce heat and simmer untill thick enough to mound on a spoon,20 minutes.
Meanwhile,between two sheets of plastic wrap,pound chicken to 1/4 inch thickness;cut in half crosswise.Place flour in shallow dish.In separate shallow dish,whisk egg with 2 Tbsp.water.In third dish,stir together bread crumbs,parmesan cheese,oregano,cayenne and salt.Dip chicken into flour,then egg mixture,then bread crumbs.
In large skillet.heat remaining oil over medium heat,cook chicken in batches,turning once,untill golden brown and no longer pink,4-6 minutes.
Spread 1 cup of the sauce in a 13x9 inch baking dish.
Arrange chicken,overlapping,over the sauce.Pour remaining sauce over top.
Add the artichokes,then the mozzarella cheese.Bake at 375 for 20 minutes.
