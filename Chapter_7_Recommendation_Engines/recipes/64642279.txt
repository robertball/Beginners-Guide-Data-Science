Title:
Avocado Chocolate Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ripe avocados1 banana (just ripe, no spots)5 Tbsp. raw cacao powder2 Tbsp. raw honey C almond or coconut milk.

Directions:
Blend everything together in a food processor until the consistency is smooth and velvety. Adjust sweetness as needed. Serve immediately.
