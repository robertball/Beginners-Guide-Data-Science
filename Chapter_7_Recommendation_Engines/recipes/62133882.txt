Title:
Crispy Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1) 1 large cauliflower
2)2 tbsp grated fresh ginger
3) 2 finely chopped green chillies
4) 1 tsp turmeric
5) 1 tsp chilli powder
6) .5 tsp garam masala
7) 1 tsp coriander powder
8) 1 tsp cumin seeds
9) 1 tsbp vegetable oil
10) Cilantro for garnish

Directions:
1) Chop cauliflower into bite sized pieces.Wash and dry well.
2) Heat oil in a non-stick pan and add cumin, ginger, chillies,turmeric and coriander powder. Cook for 2-3 mins.
3) Add cauliflower and toss well in spices.
4) Continue cooking on high heat.
5) Add salt, garam masala and red chilli powder.
6) Cook on high heat till cauliflower is cripy.
7) Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.

Submitter: 
Sunaina
Description: 
A simple yet tastefull cauliflower dish. Can be eaten with Basmati rice, roti or as a side.
