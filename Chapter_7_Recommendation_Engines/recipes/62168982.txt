Title:
D's Taco Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/778538.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground beef
1 clove garlic
1/2 package taco seasoning
1/3 cup water
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 cup regular or fat free refried beans
1 can (16 oz) diced tomatoes with garlic and onion
2 T. fresh cilantro
1 cup salsa
2 cups shredded lettuce
2 medium tomatoes, chopped
1 small can (2.25 oz) sliced black olives
2 cups shredded Mexican Four cheese
1/4 cup green onions, chopped
1 (12 inch) ready-made pizza crust

Directions:
Brown ground beef with onion and garlic in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Drain fat.
Stir in taco seasoning and water to cooked ground beef.  Bring to boil.
Reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
To make "pizza salsa" sauce, in a blender or food processor, blend can of diced tomatoes with onion and garlic, cilantro and salsa until desired consistency.
Spread refried beans onto pizza crust, leaving 1/2 inch border.
Spread 1 cup of "pizza salsa" sauce on top of refried beans.
Sprinkle ground beef mixture on to pizza.
Add 1 1/2 cups of shredded cheese over entire pizza.
Place crust on baking sheet or on oven rack for crisper crust.
Bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 until cheese is bubbly and crust is golden brown.
Remove from oven and let cool 5 minutes.
Top with shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, black olives, green onions and remaining shredded cheese.
Serve with remaining "pizza salsa" sauce, sour cream and additional salsa if desired.

Submitter: 
Daisy K
Description: 
We love to order taco pizza from the local pizza parlor but it got too expensive.  So I came up with this variation of it that my family loves!  It can be suited to match anyone's tastes and the ready-made crust makes it a snap on busy days.
