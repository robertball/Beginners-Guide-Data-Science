Title:
Patatas Alioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. small red potatoes
1 C. mayonnaise
1/2 C. sour cream
1 t. yellow mustard
2 T. minced fresh garlic
1 t. ground white pepper
2 T. chopped fresh parsley
salt to taste
slivers of red bell pepper for garnish
sprigs of parsley for garnish

Directions:
Boil potatoes until tender, then cut each into large bite size pieces chunks; cool. WHIP together mayonnaise, sour cream, mustard, garlic, white pepper, parsley and salt. Combine potatoes with the whipped mixture and chill. Garnish with slivers of red pepper and parsley.
