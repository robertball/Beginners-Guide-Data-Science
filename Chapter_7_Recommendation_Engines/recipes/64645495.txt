Title:
Big Ray's Horseradish Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4526242.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can chic peas, drained & rinsed(15 oz)
1/2 tahini
1 tsp olive oil
3 Tbsp lemon juice
2 Tbsp creamy horseradish
1 tsp salt
water as needed

Directions:
Process all ingredients in a food processor until smooth, if too thick add water to desired consistency. Top with additional olive oil before serving.
Serve with pita chips and sliced veggies.
