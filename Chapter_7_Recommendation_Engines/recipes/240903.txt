Title:
Clean Green Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 Granny Smith apples, halved and seeds removed
5 stalks celery, leaves removed
1 cucumber, roughly chopped
3 romaine lettuce leaves
3 leaves kale
5 sprigs fresh parsley
1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
1 tablespoon water, or as needed

Directions:
Place apples, celery, cucumber, romaine lettuce leaves, kale, parsley, lemon juice, and water in a blender or juicer; process until desired consistency is reached, adding more water as needed.

Submitter: 
Brittany Curtis
Description: 
Use as part of a juice cleanse or as a clean meal replacement.
