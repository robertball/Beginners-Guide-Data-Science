Title:
Crockpot Rotissiere Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken
Pam
Lawry's seasoning salt
aluminum foil

Directions:
Clean chicken inside and out. spray with olive oil spray.
Sprinkle inside and out with Lawreys season salt, heavier the better!!!!!
Spray inside of crock with cooking spray. NOTE: Do not put any water in the crockpot.
Roll some wads of aluminum foil into balls and put them in bottom of crock. the chicken is going to sit on these.
Put chicken back side down in crock on top of aluminum balls.
Cook on high (wil not come out the same on low) 4-6 hours
