Title:
Sun-Dried Tomato and Basil Vegetarian Chickpea Sandwich Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (19 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
1/3 cup red onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
2 tbsp. minced sun-dried tomatoes
2 tbsp. minced fresh basil
1 to 2 tablespoon mayonnaise (or plain Greek yogurt)
1 tablespoon lemon juice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Drain and rinse chickpeas. Pour chickpeas into a medium size mixing bowl and mash with a fork. Mix in celery, onion, mayonnaise (to taste), lemon juice, dill, salt and pepper to taste.

Description: 
Serve this tasty sandwich spread on crusty whole grain rolls or pita bread, with lettuce and tomato.Modified from: Vegetarian Chickpea Sandwich Filling
