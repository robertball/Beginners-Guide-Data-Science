Title:
Vegetarian Panini Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8x8 ciabatta bread, cut in 4 sections then sliced, or 8 slices sourdough bread
1/4 c goat cheese
1/4 c prepared basil pesto
8 slices eggplant, 1/4 inch thick
1/4 small red onion, sliced thin
1/2 medium red or green bell pepper, thinly sliced
1/4 c sliced white mushrooms
4 tomato slices
1/4 c fresh baby spinach leaves
6 Tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
In 2 T olive oil, saute onion, bell pepper, and eggplant 2-3 minutes or until tender. Add sliced mushrooms and saute 1 minute more.
Brush the outsides of the bread slices liberally with the remaining olive oil. Assemble sandwiches by layering  the goat cheese, sauteed vegetables, tomato slices, spinach leaves, and pesto.
Grill 2-3 minutes on each side in heated cast iron skillet, or in panini press until crispy.

Submitter: 
ELLENMEL
Description: 
Fresh summer vegetables and goat cheese melt together in this awesome panini. If you don't have a panini press, you can use a cast iron skillet and a brick wrapped in foil to smash the panini.
