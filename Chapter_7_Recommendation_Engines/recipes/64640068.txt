Title:
Memere Hamburger Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4511927.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound hamburger, browned and drained
1 large onion, chopped
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 clove garlic, crushed
½ teaspoon Worestershire
2 stalks of celery, chopped
2 carrots (Optional), chopped and blanch
2 beef bouillon cubes
½ (12 ounce) package frozen corn kernels
2 cans (11.5 Ounce) V8 Vegetable Juice, Low Sodium
1 cup elbow macaroni, cooked
1 can (28 ounce) crushed tomatoes
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and simmer

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
This was a recipe that was passed down from my wife's grandmother. The only alteration I made was to optionally add carrots and doubling the crushed tomatoes. You can add whatever veggies you want like zucchini, green beans, even mushrooms. It’s hearty and flavorful and satisfying.
