Title:
Extremely Moist German Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/893672.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg German Chocolate (baker's chocolate)
1/2 cup boiling water
1 cup margarine
2 cups sugar
4 egg yolks
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups All-Purpose Flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup buttermilk
4 egg whites
1 1/2 cups evap milk
1 1/2 cups sugar
4 egg yolks beaten stiff
3/4 cup margarine
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
2 cups shredded coconut
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
The cake:
Melt chocolate in pan, by sitting it larger pan with boiling water. Once melted, take off heat.
Cream butter & sugar with blender until fluffy. Add yolks, one at a time. Blend in vanilla & melted chocolate.
Then sift in flour with baking soda & salt; add alternately with buttermilk to chocolate mixture, beating well after each addition.
Beat 4 egg whites until fluffy, and then FOLD into mixture till moderately mixed, not stirring. Pour mixture into 3, 9inch pans lined with wax paper.
Bake at 350 degrees; 30-35 mins.
Cool on rack.
The icing:
Mix all ingredients into pan, minus the coconut and pecans. Cook over medium heat for 12 minutes to simmering. Add coconut and pecans, stirring frequently till reaches moderate consistency to cool as icing.
(if when cooled, is not good for icing, can heat again on stove till desired consistency is reached)
