Title:
Mexican Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1130823.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 envelope taco seasoning mix
1 can Rotel
1 cup shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 1/4 cup milk
3/4 cup Bisquick
2 eggs
Dash of red pepper sauce

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees. Lightly grease deep dish pie plate. Cook and stir meat and onions in 10 inch skillet until beef is brown.  Stir in seasoning pack and Rotel.  Spread in dish. Sprinkle cheese over top.  Beat remaining ingredients until smooth. Pour over meat mixture. Bake until golden brown or until toothpick comes out clean aprrx. 25 to 30 minutes.  Let stand 5 minutes. Garnish with peppers and cheese if desired.
