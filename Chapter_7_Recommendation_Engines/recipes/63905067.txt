Title:
Spicy Garlic Lime Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
2 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/3 cup chicken broth (better than bouillion)
Juice and zest from half of a fresh lime
Sprinkle with a bit of garlic powder

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix together salt, black pepper, cayenne, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, thyme and parsley. Sprinkle spice mixture generously on both sides of chicken breasts.
Heat butter and olive oil in a large heavy skillet over medium heat. Saute chicken until golden brown, about 6 minutes on each side, add chicken broth. Lightly sprinkle with garlic powder and lime zest and juice. Cook 5 minutes, stirring frequently to coat evenly with sauce.
