Title:
Ranch Dressing II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/959739.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 teaspoon dried chives
1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
1/2 teaspoon dried dill weed
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, sour cream, chives, parsley, dill, garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
DAWNIA
Description: 
This ranch dressing is great because it doesn't have all the chemicals and preservatives that many do.  You can also use buttermilk in place of the sour cream, if you like.
