Title:
Tomatillo Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 pound chopped tomatillos tomatoes
1/2 pound chopped tomatoes
2 jalapeno peppers, seeded and minced
4 cups water
Dry chicken powder to taste
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro or 2 teaspoons dried
1/4 cup sour cream (optional)
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil chicken in water until well cooked.
Remove from water/broth.
Add onions, garlic, tomatillos, tomatoes, jalapeno peppers, cilantro and broth.
Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover the pot, and simmer for about 15 minutes.
Blend vegetables in pot with immercible  blender.
At this point taste the soup; if not flavourful enough, add dry chicken powder.
Slice the chicken into thin slices, and  then shred.  Stir into soup.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.
When ready to serve, stir in the minced cilantro and ladle into bowls.  Serve with sour cream.

Submitter: 
TJ
Description: 
This is a Mexican-style chicken soup with tomatillos and chicken breast meat cooked in a stock seasoned with fresh jalapenos.  Serve with a dollop of sour cream and sprig of cilantro.Modified from: Tomatillo Soup
