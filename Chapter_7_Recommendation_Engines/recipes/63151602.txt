Title:
Bailey's Chocolate Cheesecake~~from AR/UK Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/807938.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/8 cup chocolate graham cracker crumbs
1/2 cup confectioner's sugar
5 tbsp cocoa powder
1/3 cup butter, melted
3 (8oz) pkg cream cheese, softened
1 1/4 cup granulated sugar
4 tbsp cocoa powder
3 tbsp all purpose flour
3 eggs
1/2 cup sour cream OR mascapone cheese OR plain greek yogurt
4 tbsp Bailey's Irish Cream Liqueur

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
In a large bowl, sift together the graham cracker crumbs, confectioner's sugar and 5 tbsp cocoa. Add melted butter and stir until well mixed. Pat into the bottom of a 9-inch springform cake pan. Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes; set aside to cool. Increase oven temperature to 450 degrees.
In a large bowl, combine cream cheese, sugar, 4 tbsp cocoa and flour. Beat at medium speed until well blended and smooth. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Blend in the sour cream and Bailey's liqueur; mixing on low speed. Pour filing over crust base.
Bake at 450 degrees for 10 minutes. Reduce oven temperature to 250 degrees and continue baking for 1 hour. Remove from oven.
With a knife, oosen cake from sides of pan. Allow to cool slightly, then push up base of the time to remove cheesecake. Chill before serving.
