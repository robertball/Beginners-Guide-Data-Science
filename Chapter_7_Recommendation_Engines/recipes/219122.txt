Becel速 Sweet Spiced Squash Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 medium acorn squash
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1 tablespoon golden brown sugar
1 tablespoon Becel速 Buttery Taste margarine

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Cut squash in half using a large sharp knife, and scoop out seeds. In a small bowl combine cinnamon, nutmeg, brown sugar and Becel(R) Buttery Taste margarine. Rub spice and margarine mixture over cut side of squash.
Place on a baking dish and bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes or until squash is very tender.
