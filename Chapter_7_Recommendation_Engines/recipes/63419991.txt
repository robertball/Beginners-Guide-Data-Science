Title:
Maple Pecan Sweet Potato Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/923998.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust
1 cup cooked and mashed sweet potatoes or yams
2 eggs, beaten
½ cup light brown sugar
½ tsp. ground ginger
½ tsp. ground cinnamon
? tsp. freshly grated nutmeg
½ tsp. salt
¼ cup maple syrup
1 cup evaporated milk
¾ cup pecan halves
2 Tbsp. maple syrup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Prepare dough for one 9 inch pie. Refrigerate until ready to bake.
Blend together the yams, eggs, brown sugar, ginger, cinnamon, nutmeg, vanilla, salt, corn syrup and evaporated milk. Pour filling into crust.
Bake pie for 20 minutes.  Remove pie from oven.
Toss pecan halves in 2 Tblsp. maple syrup to coat.  Arrange coated pecan halves on top of partially cooked pie.
Bake pie another 20 – 25 minutes, or until the pie mounds in center.
Cool completely before serving either plain or with whipped cream.

Submitter: 
SEAMSTERRIFIC
Description: 
A Canadian twist on an old favourite, this classic sweet potato pie is sweetened with brown sugar and maple syrup, lightly spiced with ginger, cinnamon and nutmeg and topped with maple drenched pecan halves.
