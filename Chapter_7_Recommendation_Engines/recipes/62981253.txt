Title:
Thanksgiving Make Ahead healthier mashed potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes,
4 cups water
2 tsp sea salt
1/2 cup light butter
2 (3 ounce) packages reduced fat cream cheese
1-1/2 cup fat free half and half
1 teaspoons sea salt
ground black pepper to taste
light butter
paprika (optional)
dried chives (optional)

Directions:
Peel and quarter potatoes. Heat water & 2 tsp of sea salt in 4 quart pan or dutch oven over high heat until boiling.  Add potatoes.  Cover & cook til returns to boil.  Reduce heat to low & cook about 20 minutes, or til potatoes are fork tender.  Drain & return potatoes to pan.
Mash potatoes til smooth.  Mix in butter, cream cheese & the last 1 tsp of sea salt.  Gently warm the 1/2 & 1/2 in microwave, and add gradually until the potatoes are creamy and fluffy, to your taste.n  You may not use all of the half & half, depending on your preference.
Cool; refrigerate in tightly covered container.  These can be made several days before Thanksgiving.
Day of serving:  Spread 1/2 potatoes in buttered 8" square glass baking dish.  Dot with some butter, paprika & chives.  Repeat with second 8" pan, if needed.  Bake at 350 for about 40 minutes.

Description:
