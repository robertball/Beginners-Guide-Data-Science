Title:
Eagle Brand Double Delicious Cookie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c margarine or butter
1 1/2 c graham cracker crumbs
1 (14 oz) can Eagle Brand Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 (12 oz) package semi sweet cholate chips
1 c peanut butter chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350' (325' for glass dish).  In 13 x 9 inch baking pan, melt margarine in oven.  Sprinkle crumbs evenly over margarine; pour eagle brand evenly over crumbs.  Top with chips;press down firmly.  Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until lightly browned.  Cool.  Cut into bars.  Store loosely covered at room temp.
