Title:
stuffed hot dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg hot dogs
2 cup instant mashed potatos (prepared)
4 slices american cheese

Directions:
slice hot dogs almost all the way through
place in boiling water 5min
remove from water pat dry
place on cookie sheet and fill with potatos
cut cheese in half and place one on each dog
place in 300 drg oven for 25 min
remove and top with condments
