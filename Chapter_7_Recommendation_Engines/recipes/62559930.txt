Title:
Chorizo Latke's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Russet potatoes, peeled and shredded
1 package of beef Chorizo (16oz)
One yellow onion diced
2 eggs lightly beaten
Vegetable oil for cooking
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Shred potatoes and wring out excess water in dish towel.
Heat 1 tsp of oil in pan, add chorizo, cook thoroughly, drain and set aside. reserving one tsp oil in pan, add onion and saute until translucent about 4 minutes.
Mean while mix eggs and potatoes with salt and pepper to taste.
Add Chorizo and onions fold into potatoes until well mixed.
In a clean pan heat approx 1 TBSP oil until almost smoking.
Add a heaping TBSP of potatoes and smash down into pan, cook in batches approx 2 minutes per side or until golden brown. Drain on paper towels. Makes Approx 18-25 depending on size of potatoes.
Serve while warm with sour cream and choice of hot sauce
