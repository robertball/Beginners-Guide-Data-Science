Title:
Cherry S'More Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/442926.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10-ounce) jar of maraschino cherries without stems (may be replaced with pineapple slices), 1 (19-ounce) package of fudge brownie mix (for 13x9 pan), 1/2 cup oil, 1/4 cup water, 2 eggs, 1/4 cup butter (melted), 8 whole graham crackers, 1/2 cup milk chocolate (or dark chocolate if preferred) chips, 1/2 cup chopped pecans (optional), 1 cup mini marshmallows

Directions:
Drain cherries (or pineapple) into a cup and place on a paper towel. Set aside.
Prepare brownie mix according to package directions.
Spread melted butter in a 13x9x12 inch pan.
Cover bottom of pan with graham crackers breaking them if necessary. Spread brownie batter over crackers.
Bake in a preheated oven 5 minutes less than directed on the brownie mix package. Sprinkle chocolate chips, pecans, cherries (pineapple) and marshmallows over the top. Return to oven; bake an additional 5-8 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out mostly clean. Let cool. Cut into squares. Enjoy.

Description: 
These brownies are a good project to work on with children as they can have fun adding toppings. Cherries add a nice fruity twist but can be left out if you don't like them. They may also be replaced with pineapple.
