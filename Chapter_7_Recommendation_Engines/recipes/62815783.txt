Title:
Val's Bandito Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/664764.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/2 medium onion, chopped
3/4 cup medium Pace® Picante Sauce
1/4 cup molasses
1 tablespoon yellow mustard
1 tsp. chili powder
1 (28 ounce) can pork and beans
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans, rinsed and drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until it's tender.
Stir the picante sauce, molasses, mustard, chili powder, pork and beans, black beans, and pinto beans in the saucepan and heat to a boil. Reduce the heat to low. Cook till heated through.
Transfer mixture to baking dish and bake for 20-30 minutes till hot and bubbling.

Submitter: 
Valerie Brunmeier
Description: 
Here's a great side dish for your next barbecue, open house or buffet.  Picante sauce is the ingredient that makes these baked beans especially good....give them a try.Modified from: Bandito Baked Beans
