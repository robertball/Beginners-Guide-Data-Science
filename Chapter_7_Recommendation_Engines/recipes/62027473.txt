Title:
Spaghetti Amore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground beef
1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 can Tomato Soup
3/4 soup can of water
1 1/2 tsp garlic salt
1 1/2 cups grated cheddar cheese
1 can sliced mushrooms (or pieces)
8 oz spaghetti

Directions:
In large skillet brown ground beef.  Drain grease and return to fire.  Stir in soups, water, garlic salt, mushroom, and 1/2 c of cheese.  Mix well and simmer on low.  While beef mixure simmers, cook spaghetti and drain water.  Combine beef mixture and spaghetti in 2 qt casserole coated with non-stick spray.  Top with remaining cheese and bake at 350 for 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
lonwolf32
Description: 
This is a great, easy casserole.  I've never served it to anyone who didn't like it.  I usually serve it with a salad and garlic bread.  It's perfect for a make-ahead dish and freezes beautifully!
