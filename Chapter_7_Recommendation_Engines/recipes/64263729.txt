Title:
Spiced Pretzels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup oil
1 pkg dry Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing Mix
2 tsps fresh pressed garlic
1 - 1 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1 16 oz. bag thin pretzel sticks
1 16 ox. bag small pretzel twists

Directions:
Preheat oven to 200F.
Combine first 4 ingredients in large measuring cup.
Mix pretzels in a LARGE bowl (I use Mom's bread pan.)  Pour the oil mixture over the pretzels and stir/toss until well coated.
Spread pretzels onto 2 cookie sheets.
Bake 1 hour stirring every 15 minutes.
Store in airtight containers.
