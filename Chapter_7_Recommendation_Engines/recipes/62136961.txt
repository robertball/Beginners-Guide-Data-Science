Title:
GA. Style Low Counrty Boil, A.K.A. Frogmore Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 gallons of water
1/2 cup lemon juice
1 medium onion, cut in half
2 tbsp. garlic powder
1 tbsp. salt
1 3oz. box Zatarain's crab boil in a bag
4 tbsp. Old Bay seasoning
3 lb. can of whole potato's
10 frozen mini ear, corn-on-cob
2 lb. peeled & deveined shrimp
2 lb. crawfish
2 lb. crab legs
2 lb. smoked pork sausage,cut in 1/2" pieces
1 stick of butter

Directions:
Add your water to a big pot and bring it to a boil.
Add the lemon juice, onion, garlic powder, salt, Old Bay, stick of butter, and the boil in a bag.
Let it boil for about 5 minutes.
Add the corn, and let it return to a boil.
Add your sausage, and let it return to a boil.
Seperate your crab legs, and add them, too.
If you like crawfish, now would be the time to stick them in.
After it starts boiling again, add the potatoes.
Let it return to a boil, and add the shrimp.
Stir it up real good, and put a lid on it.
Let it keep boiling for about 15 more minutes.
Drain it,pour it out on a counter, and dig in.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
A delicious combination of seafood and vegetables, with some out of this world seasoning. Boiled in a big pot, and shared with many friends. Many times duplicated, but this is by far the best you will ever taste. And you will have this requested many times over.
