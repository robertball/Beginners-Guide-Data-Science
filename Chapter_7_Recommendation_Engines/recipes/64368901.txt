Title:
Oven Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Russett Potatoes
Slice in 1/2 inch strips
1/2 tsp. sea salt
2 T. canola oil

Directions:
Peel and slice potatoes in 1/2 inch strips.
Place in a large pot with water to rinse off some of the starch.
RInse potatoes. Then, place in a large pot filled with cold water. Bring to a boil. Boil for 3 minutes.
Drain in colander. Make sure potatoes are not wet otherwise the oil will not stick.
Place in a bowl. Add 2 T. canola oil and coat potatoes.
Sprinkle with 1/2 tsp. sea salt.
Bake in 450ยบ oven for 20-35 minutes or until golden brown.

Description: 
Oven fries that are not soggy.
