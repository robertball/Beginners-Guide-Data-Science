Title:
Epic Lunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3592024.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs chicken breast
32 oz salsa
32 oz corn
32 oz black beans
32 oz chicken broth
2 cups cooked brown rice
8 oz shredded cheddar cheese
1 tbsp cayenne pepper
1 tbsp garlic powder
1 tbsp onion powder

Directions:
1) Pre-cook the rice
2) Cut the chicken breast into small cubes
3) Mix all ingredients in a large baking dish
4) Cover and bake for 90 minutes at 375 degrees

Submitter: 
Will Lee
Description: 
I make this so I can have an epic lunch all week.  I split it up evenly into 5 servings and bring one to work every day.  Very choice.
