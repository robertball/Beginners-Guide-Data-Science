Title:
Tender Chicken Breast With Creamy Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breast
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon paprika
1/2 cup chicken broth
1/3 cup bacon drippings
1/2 cup flour
2 cups milk
salt & pepepr to taste

Directions:
Season chicken breast with chili powder, garlic powder & paprika.
Pour chicken broth into the bottom of a casserole dish and place seasoned chicken on top.
Cover tightly with foil and bake at 375 for 1 hour til done.
Remove chicken from pan and place on a platter.
Reserve the chicken broth from pan and set aside.
Pour the bacon drippings into a skillet. Turn heat up to med-high.
When the grease is hot, add the flour and stir continuously for 1 minute. Add salt and pepper and keep stirring.
Turn heat down to medium temp.
Slowly, a little at a time, add the chicken broth, stirring continuously.
Slowly add milk and continue stirring.
Return heat to med-high. Bring to a boil, stirring, until you achieve desired consistancy.
Spoon gravy over chicken and serve.

Submitter: 
Janine
Description: 
This is my family's favorite chicken dish with some of the best gravy I have ever tasted. I like to serve this dish with mashed potatoes. I hope you and your family enjoy it also.
