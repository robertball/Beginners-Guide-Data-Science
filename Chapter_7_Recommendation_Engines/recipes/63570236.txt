Title:
mac and cheese veggie chicken casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tbsp. country crock spread, melted, divided,
1 cup skim milk
8 oz. cooked rotini pasta
7 cups frozen vegetables, thawed
2 1/4 cups sredded cheddar cheese
1/4 cup dry bread crumbs
1 tbsp. grated parmasean cheese

Directions:
1. preheat oven to 400*. Combine 3 tbsp. butter with the milk, pasta, chicken, veggies, and chedder
cheese in an 8-inch baking pan.
2. Combine bread crumbs, Parmesean and remaining Country Crock in a small bowl; sprinkle on top. Bake 30 minutes or until heated through.
