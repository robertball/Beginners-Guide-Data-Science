Title:
Orange Poppy Seed Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1½ cups all-purpose flour
1½ teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup butter, softened
½ cup white sugar
2 eggs
1/3 cup orange juice
1 pineapple yogurt
1 tablespoon orange zest
1 tablespoon poppy seeds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
Beat in eggs, one at a time.
Combine flour, baking powder and salt, set aside.
Mix orange juice and yogurt together and add to creamed mixture alternately with the flour mixture.
Stir in orange zest and poppy seeds.
Pour into a greased 8-in. x 4-in. x 2-in. loaf pan.
Bake at 325 degrees F for 40minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean.
