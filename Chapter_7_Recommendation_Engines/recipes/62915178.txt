Title:
Ronald's Spinach Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/709807.jpg
Ingredients:
1 deep dish pie crusts
1 1/2 cups swiss cheese, grated
8 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
3 eggs
1 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup milk
1/2 tsp salt
dash cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1 package frozen spinach, thawed and drained

Directions:
Sprinkle cheese and bacon into uncooked pie crust.
Beat remaining ingredients together and pour over cheese and bacon.  Bake in pre-heated oven at 375 degrees for 1 hour or until firm and browned.

Submitter: 
sixgermans
Description: 
Spinach can be replaced with asparagus, crab, etc.
