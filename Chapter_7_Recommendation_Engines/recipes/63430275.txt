Title:
Flatbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/936643.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c flour
2 c quick-cooking oats
2 c Total cereal, crushed after measured
1 tsp salt
1/2 c sugar
1 1/2 sticks butter, softened
3/4 c warm water
1 tsp baking soda

Directions:
Combine first six ingredients and mix til slightly crumbly.
Combine water and soda, pour over dough and mix as you would a pie crust.
Divide into @ 12 balls slightly smaller than tennis balls.
Using a well-floured pastry cloth and pin, roll out circles.
Place circles on baking sheet, score bread, and bake at 400 degrees for 7-10 minutes, until a nice golden color.
