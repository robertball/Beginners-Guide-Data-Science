Title:
Cheese-lovers' Healthy Vegetable, Bacon, and Egg Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/488475.jpg
Ingredients:
8 slices turkey bacon
1 cup frozen cauliflower
1 (6.5 ounce) can mushroom pieces & stems
2 green onions, finely chopped
1 medium red bell pepper, chopped
1 tbsp unsalted butter
1 cup liquid egg whites
1/4 cup water
1/2 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp garlic powder
8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 medium tomato, chopped

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350.
2. Cook bacon per microwave package directions, until bacon is crisp. Break into bite-size pieces.
3. In large skillet, saute cauliflower, green onion, and bell pepper in butter until tender. Remove from heat. Set aside.
4. In a mixing bowl, beat together egg whites, water, oregano, pepper, and garlic powder until foamy.
5. Stir in bacon, cheeses, tomatoes, mushrooms, and cauliflower mixture. Pour in greased square metal baking dish. Bake for 40 minutes.
