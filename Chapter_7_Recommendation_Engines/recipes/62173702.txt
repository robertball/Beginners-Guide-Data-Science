Title:
Favorite Old Fashioned Gingerbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white sugar
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup applesauce
1 egg
1 cup molasses
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1 cup hot water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Greased and flour one 9 inch square pan.
Cream together the sugar and butter or margarine.  Add applesauce than add egg, and beat well.  Mix in the molasses.
Sift together the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger and clove.  Add dry ingredients to the creamed mixture; add the hot water, and mix well.
Pour batter into pan and bake for 35 min. Allow to cool in pan and serve.
