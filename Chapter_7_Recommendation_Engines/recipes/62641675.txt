Title:
Baked Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, lightly drained
1 (14.75 ounce) can cream-style corn with liquid
1/2 cup sour cream
1 cup butter, melted
2 eggs, beaten
1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
1 (12 ounce) package corn muffin mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ยบ.
In a large bowl, combine the whole-kernel corn, cream-style corn, sour cream, melted butter, beaten eggs, 1 cup of the cheese and the corn muffin mix.
Mix well and pour into a 9x13 inch baking pan.
Bake for 45-55 minutes.
Remove from oven and sprinkle with 1/2 cup Cheddar.
