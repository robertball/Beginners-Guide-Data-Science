Title:
Big Ray's Kielbasa Cabbage Skillet for a Crowd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1130352.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb Polish Kielbasa Sausage or Polish sausage, cut into 1/2-inch slices
4 Tbl butter, divided
1 large head cabbage (4 pounds), coarsely chopped
2 medium onions, chopped
2 cans (15 oz) diced tomatos
1/4 cup sugar
1 Tbl paprika
3 to 4 large potatoes, cubed

Directions:
Cook the potatoes 8 minutes in the microwave.  In a Dutch oven, brown the onions and sausage in 2 tablespoons butter; remove and set aside. In the same pan, saute cabbage in remaining butter until tender.
In a small bowl, combine the diced tomatos, sugar and paprika; pour over cabbage mixture. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; add the potatoes and reserved sausage. Cover and simmer for 40 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
