Title:
Eggplant Rounds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup cornmeal
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
salt to taste
Container of Eggbeaters
1 eggplant, sliced into 1/2 inch rounds

Directions:
In a shallow dish, combine corn meal, chili powder, oregano and salt. Dredge each eggplant slice in egg beaters and then in the cornmeal mixture until coated on both the sides. Shake off excess. Layer slices on parchment lined baking pan. Bake at 30-40 minutes at 400 turning once

Submitter: 
Sue Houser Winant
Description: 
Savory grilled eggplant or brinjal. If you're having a barbeque we recommend grilling these rounds rather than frying. They are delicious either fried or grilled!Modified from: Eggplant Rounds
