Title:
Fastest, Cheapest Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can chicken and rice soup
1 can diced tomatoes with green chilies
1 can corn niblets, drained
tortilla chips
sour cream (optional)

Directions:
In a large saucepan, combine the soups, UNDRAINED tomatoes and drained corn.  Stir to combine and heat through.
Top with crushed tortilla chips and sour cream, if desired.
