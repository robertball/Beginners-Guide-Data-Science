Title:
the 5 minute side salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 leaves fresh basil
1 avocado
1 tomato
1 handful of fresh mozzarella (pearl size)
1 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
sea salt to taste

Directions:
Start by roughly chopping the basil and adding olive oil and sea salt to it
Dice the avocado and tomato and toss with the basil
add pearl sized mozzarella right before you serve

Submitter: 
Sara Perry
Description: 
This salad I threw together one night in a starving hurry (I am 5 months pregnant) because I was so busy cooking our steaks that I completely forgot to make a side dish! The entire dinner my husband made yummy noises after each salad bite... It only took 5 minutes to make!
