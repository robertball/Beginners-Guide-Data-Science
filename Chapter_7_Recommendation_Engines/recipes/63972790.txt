Title:
Veloute Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. butter or margarine
1/4 c. flour
1/8 tsp. pepper
1 c. chicken broth
3/4 c. water
1 tsp. lemon juice

Directions:
Melt butter in a small saucepan.  Add flour and cook until bubbly.  Cook, stirring constantly. about 3 min. to cook off flour taste.  Slowly add 1 c. chicken broth, stirring constantly.  Add 3/4 c. water slowly, stopping when desired consistency is reached.  Add 1 tsp. lemon juice.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
Silky sauce, use for topping chicken and salmon patties.
