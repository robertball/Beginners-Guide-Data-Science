Title:
Mexican Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb cooked shredded chicken breast
1 lb cooked ground beef
1 can diced Rotel diced tomatoes
1 can corn
1 black beans
1 can pinto beans
1 can kidney beans
1 packet taco seasoning mix
1 packet ranch dressing mix
2 cups water

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a crockpot.  Do not drain cans.  Cook on low for 7-8 hours or high for 4-5 hours.

Submitter: 
java junkie
Description: 
Very easy, crowd-pleasing soup.  Delicious topped with corn chips, shredded Mexican cheese and sour cream.
