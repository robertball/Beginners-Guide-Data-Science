Title:
Cherry Cordial Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983881.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
2 cups half-and-half
2 cups whipping cream, unwhipped
1 (10 ounce) jar maraschino cherries (without stems), well drained and chopped
3/4 cup semi-sweet mini chocolate chips
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon almond extract

Directions:
In ice cream freezer container, combine all ingredients; mix well.
Freeze according to manufacturer's instructions.  Freeze leftovers.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
Home made vanilla ice cream is enhanced with chocolate chips and pieces of maraschino cherries in this delicious treat.
