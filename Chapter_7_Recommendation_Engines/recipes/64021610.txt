Title:
Jello Strawberry Nut Mold Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 3oz. package of strawberry Jello
1 small can of crushed pineapple
1/2 cup crushed nuts
1 - 10oz. package of frozen strawberries - thawed
1 cup boiling water
1-2 sliced bananas
1 cup sour cream

Directions:
Prepare Jello as directed then add fruit.
Pour half in a dish or mold and refrigerate until firm.
Beat cream cheese and sour cream together until smooth, then add nuts.
Pour over thickened Jello and refrigerate to thicken.
Pour remaining Jello over cream cheese mixture and refrigerate until firm.
