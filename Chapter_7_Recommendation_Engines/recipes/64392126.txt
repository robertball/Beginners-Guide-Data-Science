Title:
Vegan Pumpkin Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15oz can pumpkin puree
1 14.5 oz can diced fire roasted tomatoes
1 16 oz can Kidney beans
1 15 oz Black beans
1 15 oz can Garbanzo beans
1 2.25 oz can sliced olives
1 4 oz can diced green chilies
1 Table spoons tomato paste
8 oz can tomato sauce
3 red bell peppers (diced)
1 onion (diced)
5 cloves garlic (minced)
1 5 oz bag baby spinach
2 cups veg broth
1/4 cup orange juice
1 Tablespoon oil oil
1 Tablespoon dark chocolate ground cocoa powder
1 Tablespoon brown sugar
2 teaspoons Chipotle Chili powder
2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon Mexican oregano
1/2 teaspoon coriander

Directions:
Place all ingredients in slow cooker. Stir to combine. Stir occasionally.  Cook on low 8 hours or high for 5.
You can saute the onions, peppers and garlic for a better flavor. Not necessary.
