Title:
Buttermilk ColeSlaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/466432.jpg
Ingredients:
1 head of Nappa Cabbage, chopped into thin strips, no core
1/2 cup buttermilk
2 Tbsp mayonnaise
2 Tsbp sour cream
1 tsp sugar
3 green onions, chopped finely
1/2 cup shredded carrots,
1/2 bunch of cilantro, chopped finely

Directions:
Add buttermilk, mayonnaise, sour cream, sugar, cilantro and green onions to a large mixing bowl.
Add the cabbage and carrots and mix well.
Chill for 1 hour before serving
