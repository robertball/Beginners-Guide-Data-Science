Title:
Sally Lunn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6099469.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 C Milk
1/2 T Water
8 T Butter
3 Eggs
1 1/2 t salt
1/4 C Sugar
3 C Bread Flour
2 t yeast
Egg Wash
2 Tablespoons Water + 1 Egg
Cornmeal

Directions:
Proof the yeast in lukewarm water and sugar.
Melt butter.
In a large bowl combine the rest of the dry ingredients.
Make a tunnel in the middle and add the butter mixing in lightly, remake tunnel and add eggs one at a time slowly combing then slowly add milk and yeast-water mixture.
Kneed dough until when you press thumb into bread dough the indentation pops right back up.
Place damp cloth over bowl and allow to rise for 1 hour.
Punch back down and form into a loaf placing loaf onto greased and dusted with cornmeal bread stone.
Cover with damp cloth and allow to rise 2nd time for 30 minutes.
Brush with egg wash.
Place in pre heated 350 degree F oven.
Bake for 30-40 minutes and until when knocking on loaf it sounds hollow.
