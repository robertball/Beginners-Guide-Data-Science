Title:
Coconut Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup coconut oil, solid or melted
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups sifted confectioners' sugar

Directions:
Beat cream cheese and coconut oil in a bowl with an electric mixer until creamy. Mix in vanilla extract. Stir in confectioners' sugar, about 1/2 cup at a time, until frosting is smooth and creamy.

Submitter: 
CRUNCHM
Description: 
This simple recipe gives the flavor of coconut without the flakes. It is great on brownies or a pineapple cake. Brings the taste of the tropics. Store in the refrigerator once made.
