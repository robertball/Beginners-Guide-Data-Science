Title:
Cranberry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz Catalina dressing
1 pkg. dry onion soup mix
1-1lb can whole-berry cranberry sauce
4 chicken breasts

Directions:
Mix together first 3 ingredients. Add chicken. Place in freezer bag.
Serving Day:
Thaw. Place chicken and sauce in greased baking dish and baked uncovered at 350 degrees until done (30-40 min).
