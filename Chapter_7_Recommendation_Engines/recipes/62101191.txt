Title:
Decadent Rum Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 squares of semi-sweet baking chocolate
1/4 cup corn syrup
1/4 cup dark rum
2 cups of oreo baking crumbs
Coatings such as chocolate sprinkles, ground almonds and/or flaked coconut

Directions:
Place chocolate in medium microwaveable bowl. Microwave on MEDIUM 2 min. or until chocolate is almost melted; stir until completely melted. Add corn syrup, rum, and crumbs; mix well.
Cover and refrigerate 1 hour or until firm enough to handle.
Shape into 24 balls. Roll in coatings.
Allow flovours to mellow by storing in covered container in refrigerator for 2 days.

Submitter: 
Mirelle B
Description: 
I make this every Christmas and usually give them as gifts.  They are easy to make (no bake) and taste wonderful! Substitute orange juice for the non-alcoholic version of this.
