Title:
Crunchy Chicken and Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989653.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Pepperidge Farm® Herb Seasoned Stuffing, crushed
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 egg
2 tablespoons butter, melted
1 (12 ounce) jar Campbell's® Slow Roast Chicken Gravy

Directions:
Stir the stuffing and cheese on a plate. Beat the egg in a shallow dish with a fork or whisk. Dip the chicken into the egg. Coat the chicken with the stuffing mixture. Place the chicken onto a baking sheet. Drizzle with the butter.
Bake at 400 degrees F for 20 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
Heat the gravy in a 1-quart saucepan over medium heat until hot and bubbling. Serve the gravy with the chicken.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Crushed dry stuffing mix makes makes this chicken really crunchy and flavorful...and the entire dish is on the table in less than 45 minutes.
