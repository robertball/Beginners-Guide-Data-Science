Title:
Fruity Steel Cut Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups water
1 cup steel cut oats
2 apples, peeled and finely diced
1/4 cup raisins
1/4 cup dried, sweetened cranberries
1 tablespoon flaxseed meal
2 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Bring water to a boil in medium saucepan. Stir in oats, return to a boil, and then reduce heat to simmer.
After simmering about 10 minutes, stir in the remaining ingredients.
Continue simmering, covered, for about 20 minutes or until all the water is absorbed.
Serve with brown sugar and milk.

Submitter: 
SJRJA
Description: 
Apples, raisins and cranberries add pizzazz to the healthy combination of steelcut oats and flaxseed meal. A breakfast that is healthy and delicious!
