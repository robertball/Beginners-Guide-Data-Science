Title:
Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz fettuccine
1/4 cup butter, cubed
1 1/2 cups Parmesan cheese, grated
Pepper, salt to taste

Directions:
Heat oven to 200 degrees.  Place a large oven-safe bowl in the oven to warm.
Bring a large saucepan of salted water to a boil.  Add pasta and cook according to pkg. directions.  When pasta is finished, reserve Â¾ cup of the cooking water, then drain the pasta.
A  bit at a time, add the cheese and the reserved pasta cooking water, continuing to toss until all of the cheese is added and melted.  Season with pepper.
