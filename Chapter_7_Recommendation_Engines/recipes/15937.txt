Title:
Easy to Remember Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/418978.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup shortening
1/4 cup cold water

Directions:
Place flour in a medium bowl. Cut in shortening until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Add water and mix just until dough comes together. Allow to rest in refrigerator before rolling out.

Submitter: 
Deirdre Dee
Description: 
This is not a recipe I would recommend to a novice baker. But if you know how to make a pie crust, it is super easy to remember! Half as much shortening as flour, and half as much cold water as shortening!
