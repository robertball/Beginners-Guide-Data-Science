Title:
Maple-Mustard Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds green beans, ends trimmed
2 tablespoons coarse-grained Dijon mustard
3 tablespoons Safeway SELECT Verdi Balsamic Vinegar
1 1/4 tablespoons Safeway SELECT 100% Pure Maple Syrup
2 teaspoons Safeway SELECT Verdi Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
2 tablespoons chopped green onion (including green tops)
Salt and fresh-ground pepper

Directions:
In a 4- to 5-quart pan, bring 2 quarts water to a boil over high heat. Add beans and cook, uncovered, until tender-crisp, 5 to 8 minutes; drain and pour into a bowl.
Meanwhile, mix mustard with vinegar, maple syrup, and oil. Pour over hot beans and mix to coat well; sprinkle with onion. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
