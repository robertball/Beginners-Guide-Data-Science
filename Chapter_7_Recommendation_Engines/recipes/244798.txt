Title:
Green Tea Pear Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2760826.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup almond milk
1 scoop vanilla protein powder
1 cup fresh spinach
1 pear, cored
2 tablespoons chopped cashews
1/2 teaspoon green tea powder (matcha)

Directions:
Mix almond milk and protein powder together in a cup; pour into a blender. Add spinach, pear, cashews, and green tea powder; blend until smooth.
