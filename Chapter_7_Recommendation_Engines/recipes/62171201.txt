Title:
Fantastic Pumpkin Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 2/3 cup sugar
1 cup canola oil
2 cup shredded carrots
1 cup crushed pineapple
1 15oz. can pumpkin
2 cups flour
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. pumpkin pie spice
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
CREAM CHEESE FROSTING:
1 8 oz package cream cheese
3/4 stick softened butter
1 tsp. vanilla
2 1/2 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
1. In a mixing bowl combine all ingredients in order listed above; taking time to mix each ingredient well. Pour into greesed 15x10 baking dish (I use a 9x13 and and 8x8 2/3 of batter in 9x13 and 1/3 batter in 8x8). Bake at 350 degrees for 25-30 min. Cool completelt and frost with cream cheese frosting.
2. For frosting, combine room temperature cream cheese and butter, add vanilla and sugar. Whip till smooth. Spread on cool bars.
Store bars in fridge

Submitter: 
Mindy
Description: 
I developed this recipe to liven up the traditional pumpkin bar. My famiy says it's the best pumpkin bar they've ever had. This bar is dense and moist.
