Title:
Chicken Pot Pie Soup by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6575804.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 c flour
2 tsp cornstarch
2 c water
2 1/2 c  skim milk
2 celery stalks, chopped
2 carrots, chopped
1/2 medium onion, chopped
2 tsp chicken bouillon powder
fresh ground pepper
1/2 tsp thyme
1 tbsp garlic powder
2 potatoes, peeled and cubed small
2 large chicken breast, cooked,  diced small
1/4 c frozen peas
1/4 c frozen corn
Pinch nutmeg
salt

Directions:
Create a slurry by combining 1/2 cup of the cold water with flour and cornstarch  in a medium bowl and whisk until well blended. Set aside.
Spritz olive oil Pour remaining water and milk into a large pot and slowly bring to a boil.
Spritz bottom of large Dutch oven with olive oil, medium high heat. Add carrots, celery and onion. Sauté 3-4 minute.
Add remaining water, milk, bullion, thyme, fresh pepper and bring to a boil.
Partially cover and simmer on low until vegetables are soft, about 20 minutes.
Add potatoes and cook until soft, about 5 minutes.
Add chicken and slowly whisk in slurry, stirring well. Add frozen peas and carrots.
Cook another 2-3 minutes, until soup thickens and everything is hot.
Adjust salt and pepper to taste (do this AFTER it’s all cooked)

Submitter: 
Tammy Doerr
Description: 
Hearty and healthy soup/stew. Served with Skinnytaste garlic knots (but just made them into drop biscuits)
