Title:
Nancy's 'Served Forever' Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1, 8oz pkg cream cheese
2 tsp  lemon juice
1 - 1.5 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 clove garlic, minced
Salt & Pepper to taste
1, 6oz can crab (or 1 c. Flaked crab meat)

Directions:
Whip cream cheese.
Gradually add lemon juice.
Beat until smooth.
Add  Worcestershire, garlic, salt and pepper.
Add crumbled crab.
Chill.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
A family favorite especially at Christmas and New Year's time when we have gatherings.  Great with cracers,chips or raw vegetables. Its a family favorite and tradition.
