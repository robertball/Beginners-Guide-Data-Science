Title:
LOU’S TEXAS CAVIAR WITH A TWIST FROM MEME Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 CAN EACH OF THE FOLLOWING:
BLACKEYED PES
PINTO BEANS
BLACK BEANS
GREAT NORTHERN BEANS
YELLOW CORN
WHITE OR SHOEPEG CORN
BLACK OLIVES – CHOPPED
3 CANS OF ROTEL TOMATOES
1 TSP MINCED GARLIC
SMALL BUNCH OF GREEN ONIONS – CHOPPED
1 16OZ ITALIAN DRESSING
A LITTLE CILANTRO – CHOPPED
SQUIRT OF LIME JUICE

Directions:
DRAIN ALL THE BEANS AND CORN, RINSE UNDER COLD WATER, LET DRAIN, THEN MIX ALL INGREDIENTS TOGETHER.  REFRIGERATE A COUPLE OF HOURS TO LET THE FLAVORS MIX … SERVE WITH TORTILLA CHIPS.  ENJOY!

Wonderful party dip or vegetable side dish ... very different from the usual.
