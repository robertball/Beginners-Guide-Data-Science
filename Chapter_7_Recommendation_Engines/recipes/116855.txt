Title:
Festive Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/217829.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups reduced-fat vanilla yogurt
4 large firm bananas, sliced
2 (11 ounce) cans mandarin oranges, drained
1 (20 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained
1 cup miniature marshmallows
1/4 cup flaked coconut, toasted

Directions:
Line a strainer with 4 layers of cheesecloth and place over a bowl. Place yogurt in prepared strainer; cover yogurt with edges of cheesecloth. Refrigerate for 8 hours or overnight. Remove yogurt from cheesecloth and discard liquid from bowl.
Combine the bananas, oranges, pineapple, marshmallows and coconut. Add yogurt; stir gently to combine. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Ashley Braswell
Description: 
In Huntsville, Alabama, Ashley Braswell dresses up her fruit salad with yogurt cheese--the solids remaining after the whey has been drained from yogurt.
