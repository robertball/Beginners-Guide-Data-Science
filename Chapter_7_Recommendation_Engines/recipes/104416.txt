Title:
Raspberry Party Shots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/786279.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) package raspberry flavored Jell-O® mix
2 cups boiling water
3/4 cup ice water
1 1/4 cups raspberry vodka

Directions:
Stir the gelatin into the boiling water until dissolved.  Pour in the ice water and vodka; mix well.  Pour into shot glasses or devices specifically made for 'Party Shots.'  Chill in the refrigerator for 1 to 2 hours, until set.

Submitter: 
Stephanie
Description: 
A raspberry vodka gelatin shot that is wonderful for parties!
