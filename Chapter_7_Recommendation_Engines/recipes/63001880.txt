Title:
Danny Wood's Turkey Empanada's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/748691.jpg
Ingredients:
20 El Sembrador frozen empanada discs (Or any brand you find in frozen food section)
2 lbs ground turkey meat
2 lbs ground turkey breast
4 tablespoons olive oil
1 large diced onion
8 cloves of minced garlic
½ cup of fresh chopped cilantro
Half of a diced green pepper
Half of a diced yellow pepper
Half of a diced red pepper
2-14oz bag of low-fat Mexican cheese
Olive oil cooking spray
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Set oven to bake at 400 degrees.
In a large non-stick pan on medium/high heat, sauté onion, garlic, and all the peppers till tender.
Add all of the ground turkey, salt and pepper to taste and remove from heat when the meat is slightly under cooked.
Place the turkey meat in a strainer to drain excess liquid.
In a large bowl, mix turkey meat, cheese, and cilantro.
With a large spoon, place a generous amount of mixture onto one of the empanada discs.
Fold disc over and close with a fork.
Place all of the empanadas on a non-stick cookie tray and spray the pan and empanadas with olive oil spray.
Bake in the oven for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
