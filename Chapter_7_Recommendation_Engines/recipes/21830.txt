Title:
Pork Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/835269.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup dry mustard
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
3/4 cup chile sauce
3/4 cup pineapple juice
2 teaspoons white sugar

Directions:
Mix together dry mustard, brown sugar, chili sauce, pineapple juice and sugar and chill.

Submitter: 
sal
Description: 
A sweetened mustard, chile and pineapple flavored marinade for your pork recipes.
