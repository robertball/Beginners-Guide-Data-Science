Title:
Galen's Easy Chicken Tika Masala with Cauliflower and Chickpeas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Jar Tika Masala sauce
3 Chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
1/2 head cauliflower chopped to bite size pieces or bag of pre cut
1 can chickpeas ( garbanzo beans) drained and rinsed

Directions:
Empty jar of sauce into a  pan.  Fill jar 1/3 with water and shake getting every last bit of sauce and pour into pan.
Add cauliflower and chickpeas and simmer on medium heat until cauliflower is cooked to your prefered texture.
While simmering,  Sear the chicken on a grill on high then turn down heat and finish grilling till fully done or after searing cut into bite size chunks and finish cooking in the sauce mixture.
Serve with nan bread, rice, thick rustic homemade tortillias or any crusty bread.
Can substitute lentils for chickpeas

Submitter:

Description: 
Fast easy Indian inspired meal.  One pot dish with only 4 ingredients so clean up is a breeze.  Perfect for a hard day when you're craving hearty but healthy comfort food. Even better the next day.
