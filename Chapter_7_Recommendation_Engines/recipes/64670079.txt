Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Chicken Breasts (Boneless)
Adobo peppers in sauce,  minced
1 onion, minced
2-3 cloves garlic, minced
3-4 small red, yellow, orange peppers, minced
1-2 tbs. Taco sauce
Chile Lime Peruvian Seasoning
1 pkg. Flour or corn tortillas
1 small can sliced Black olives
2 c. Shredded Cheddar Cheese
8 oz. Tomato Sauce
1 pkg. powdered Enchilada Sauce Mix
1 bunch Green Onions

Directions:
Mince garlic, onions, peppers, adobo included.  Add to crock pot.  Season chicken with Peruvian Lime Seasoning on both sides.  Try to cover chicken with vegetable mixture.  Cook on high for 4 hours.
Shred chicken.
Add 1-2 tbs. taco sauce, mix together.
Prepare enchilada sauce as stated on mix packet (8 oz. tomato sauce, enchilada sauce mix, 1 1/2 c. water).  Simmer until thickened.
Place  1/4 c.-1/3 c. shredded chicken in flour tortilla and roll up.
Place in 9x13 baking dish.
Continue until all chicken filling is used up.
Top with enchilada sauce and 2 c. shredded cheddar cheese.
Bake at 350 for 10-15 min. until cheese is melted.
Remove from oven.  Top with diced green onions and black olives.
Serve with sour cream.

Submitter: 
TARAWVU
Description: 
A super-easy and delicious enchilada recipe.  I couldn't find a recipe our family liked so I browsed a bunch of different ones and came up with this.  I know traditional enchiladas use corn tortillas but our family prefers flour.
