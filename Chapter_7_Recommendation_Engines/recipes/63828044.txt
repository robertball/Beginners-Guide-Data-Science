Title:
Crab Sandwiches, ala Dave Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can crab meat
5 to 7 pieces of white bread, crumbles (no crusts)
14 cup (or so) of melted butter
1/4 cup minced onion
1/4 cup minced celery
1/3 cup olives, cut in half
1/2 Tablespoon dried basil
1/2 Tablespoon dried thyme
Salt and pepper, to taste
2 hard boiled eggs, minced
Horseradish, to taste
1 1/2 cups (or about) mayonnaise
6 slices of cheese
6 English muffins, split and buttered

Directions:
Mix first 2 ingredients and pour melted butter over them.  Prepare the rest of the ingredients except cheese and muffins, and add to the crab and bread mixture.
Put the mixture atop the split muffins
Put in a low oven 250 degree or about, to warm
When warmed, put cheese slices atop each muffin and mixture and put under the broiler until melted and slightly brown.

Submitter: 
Lorraine Friberg
Description: 
This is a recipe from a neighbor friend who gives only rather indefinite amounts and times, so again you will need to adjust to your taste.  The result is really great!!!!!
