Title:
Mom's Western Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup vinegar
2/3 cup ketchup

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a blender and store in a tightly sealed bottle in the fridge.

Submitter:

Description:
