Title:
Epicure's Fiesta Taco Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground beef
2 Tbsp.Epicure's Pueblo Bean Dip Mix
2 cups prepared Epicure's Poco Picante Salsa, divided
1-8 oz. pkg cream cheese
2 cups sour cream
2 Tbsp. Epicure's seasoning for nacho cheese dip
2 cups grated cheddar or marble cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
Cook beef and Pueblo bean dip mix until lightly browned. Drain excess fat and stir in 1/2 cup salsa. Set aside.
Soften cream cheese in microwave on high for 15-30 seconds. Stir in sour cream and seasoning for nacho cheese dip.
Spread cheese mixture on bottom of 2 Epicure dip bakers. Top with beef mixture.
Thinly spread with remaining salsa and top with cheddar.
Bake for 15 minutes, or until mixture is bubbling. Serve hot with taco chips.
