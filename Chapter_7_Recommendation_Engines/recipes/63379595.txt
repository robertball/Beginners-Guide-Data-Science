Title:
Easy Beef Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
1 pkg Spanish-style rice
1 can diced green chiles
2 cans enchilada sauce
1 1/2 Tbsp taco seasoning
1 (28 oz) can refried beans
2 cups cheddar cheese, divided
1 pkg burrito-size flour tortillas
Sour cream and guacamole

Directions:
Brown ground beef; drain. Add taco seasoning, green chiles and 1 can enchilada sauce; simmer over low heat. Prepare rice according to package directions. Heat beans in small saucepan, stir in half the cheese. Spread 2 Tbsp beans in tortilla; top with 2 Tbsp rice and 1/2 cup meat. Roll up; top with remaining cheese and sauce. Heat 1 or 2 at a time in microwave for one minute. Garnish with guacamole and sour cream.
