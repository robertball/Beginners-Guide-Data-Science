Title:
Slow cooker breakfast casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 oz. frozen shredded hash browns
16 oz. breakfast sausage
16 oz. cubed ham
6 green onions
12 oz. sharp cheddar cheese shredded
12 eggs
1/4 cup milk
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper

Directions:
1 Grease the insert of 6 qt. slow cooker
2 layer 1/2 hash browns, season with salt and pepper
top with 1/2 sausage,  green onions cheddar cheese
repeat layers 2 more times ending with cheese.
3 in a large bowl whisk last 5 ingredients together.
Pour over top of layers.
4 cook on low for 6-8 hours
