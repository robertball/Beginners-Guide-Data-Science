Title:
Creamy Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup uncooked short or medium grained rice
2 cups milk, divided
1/2 cup white sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 egg, beaten
2/3 cup raisins
1 tablespoon butter
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2-1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Bring 1 1/2  cups salted  water to a boil in a saucepan
Stir rice into boiling water. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
Combine 1 1/2 cups cooked rice, 1 cup milk, 1/2 C. sugar.
Cook over medium heat until thick and creamy, 15 to 20 min'
Put some of the warm mixture into the egg and milk.  After about 3 minutes, mix them together, slowly. Cook 2 minutes more, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and stir in butter, vanilla, and cinnamon.
Stir in raisins.

Submitter: 
Dorothy Clark
Description: 
Cooked rice is combined with milk, sugar, egg, and golden raisins in this quick stove-top rice pudding.Modified from: Creamy Rice Pudding
