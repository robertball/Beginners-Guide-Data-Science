Title:
Easy Chocolate Caramel Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg Choc. cake mix. ( I use German but choc. cake with pudding works well also).
Mix per instructions on box.
Melt 16 oz pkg of Kraft carmels  or 1 bottle of carmel topping and mix
1 can ofEagle Brand milk. ( If using carmel topping, you don't have to melt and it will be thinner than Kraft
carmels.)
frosting
1 small pkg of instant vanilla pudding and mix using 1 cup of milk until thick. Fold
1 8oz cool whip into pudding.

Directions:
1 pkg Choc. cake mix. ( I use German but choc. cake with pudding works well also).
Mix per instructions on box.  Pour half of batter in greased 9 x 13 pan.  Bake 350 for 15 min.
Melt 16 oz pkg of Kraft carmels  or 1 bottle of carmel topping and mix with 1 can of
Eagle Brand milk. ( If using carmel topping, you don't have to melt and it will be thinner than Kraft
carmels.).Pour carmel over baked choc. cake.  Top with remaining batter.  Bake 15 min
or until done.  Cool
Frosting:  1 small pkg of instant vanilla pudding and mix using 1 cup of milk until thick. Fold
1 8oz cool whip into pudding.  Frost.
