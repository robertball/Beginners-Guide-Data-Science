Title:
Healthier Southern-Style Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/457909.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds fresh green beans, lightly steamed
1 tablespoon unsalted butter
1 ounce bacon, chopped (we use the thin sliced Ready Crisp)
1/2 cup onion, chopped

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, add bacon and butter and render bacon until cooked, but not yet crispy. Add onion and continue to cook until just tender. Add beans and saut√© until the beans are tender and heated through.

Submitter: 
Scott Koeneman
Description: 
These beans had all the wonderful bacon and onion flavor we were after, retained all their nutrition and one serving was only 90 calories with 4 grams of fat and 5 of fiber. Yum!
