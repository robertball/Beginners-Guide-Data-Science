Title:
Dawn's Corny Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package corn bread muffin mix
1/3 cup milk
1 egg, lightly beaten
1 (15 ounce) can canned cream corn
1 15 oz can corn, drained
1/4 cup diced onion, optional
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9 inch square baking dish.
Place the muffin mix a large mixing bowl. Stir in the milk and egg. Mix in the cream corn, corn and onion. Pour batter into prepared pan. Sprinkle cheese on top.
Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until a knife inserted into the center of pan comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Dawn Marie Downour
Description: 
Great casserole that uses ingredients found on hand and bakes at 350 with the rest of your meal.Modified from: Camp Cornbread
