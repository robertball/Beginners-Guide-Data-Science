Title:
Fresh Margherita Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pizza crust (freshly made dough, either homemade or purchased
2 cups thinly sliced or chopped fresh tomatoes, de-seeded
1/4 cup olive oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
1 large ball fresh mozzarella
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella
1/4 cup shredded romano or parmesan
dash salt

Directions:
lightly saute garlic in olive oil.  Pour over tomatoes, stir and allow to marinate 10-15 minutes.
Spread pizza crust on 11x14 pan.  Let rest.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Brush 2 TBSP marinade on pizza crust
Thinly slice fresh mozzarella and add to crust.
Place tomatoes(with garlic and rest of marinade) on top of cheese and sprinkle with basil.
Sprinkle shredded mozzarella and romano on top.
Bake in 400 degree oven for 15 minutes or until crust is browned and cheese melted.

Submitter: 
tidewrinkle
Description: 
Pizza with fresh tomatoes, fresh mozzarella and fresh basil (as well as fresh garlic) baked to perfection in a hot oven.
