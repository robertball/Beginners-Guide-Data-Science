Title:
Hearty Beef and Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb ground beef
1/4C green peppers, chopped
1 can kidney beans, undrained
1Tbsp onion soup mix
1 jar of spagetti sauce
1 can of mushrooms, drained
1/4tsp garlic powder
10oz can of refridgerated biscuits
10 1/2in cubes of cheddar cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 400.
Brown ground beef and peppers. Drain. Add beans, sauce, mushrooms, soup mix, garlic powder. Heat through until bubbling.
Separate biscuits, Wrap cheese in biscuits. Place biscuts on top of meat mixture and bake 18-23 minutes until biscuts are golden brown.
