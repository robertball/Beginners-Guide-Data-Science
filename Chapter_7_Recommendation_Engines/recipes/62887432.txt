Title:
Not A Secret Anymore Steak Marinade. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/697506.jpg
Ingredients:
1 - 1 1/4lb steak ( i used london broil)
1 large tomato
1 small onion
1/4 cup water
1 beef bouillon cube
2 cloves garlic smashed
1/2 t black pepper
1/2t old bay seasoning

Directions:
Dissolve the bouillon in the water and set aside.
In a handi chopper chop tomato and onion to desired size
Combine tomato, onion , garlic , pepper and old bay in a small bowl.
pour the water mixture onto steak
top with tomato mixture
refriderate 8 hrs
grill to desired wellness
