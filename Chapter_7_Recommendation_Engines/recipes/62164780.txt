Title:
Cheesy Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1/3 c. milk
2 slices American cheese

Directions:
Beat together the eggs and milk.
Melt a pat of margarine in a skillet and add the egg mixture.  Place cheese, in quarters, on top.
Cook on medium heat, stirring with a spatula, until no longer runny.
