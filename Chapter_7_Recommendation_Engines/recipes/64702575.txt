Title:
Big Ray's Vegetarian Linguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5400180.jpg
Ingredients:
6 ounces uncooked linguine
2 Tbsp butter
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 medium zucchini, thinly sliced
1 medium yellow squash, thinly sliced
1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 large tomato, chopped
2 green onions, chopped
2 garlic clove, minced
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 cup provolone cheese, shredded
3 Tbsp grated Parmesan
2 tsp minced fresh basil

Directions:
Cook linguine according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, heat butter and oil over medium heat. Add zucchini and mushrooms; saute 3-5 minutes. Add tomato, onions, garlic and seasonings. Reduce heat; simmer, covered, about 3 minutes. Drain linguine; add to vegetable mixture. Sprinkle with cheese and basil. Toss to coat

Submitter:

Description: 
A tasty alternative to meat-and-potatoes meals is this vegetarian linguine.  The  colors in this  pasta dish help make it a stick-to-the-ribs supper that takes advantage of fresh mushrooms, zucchini and other vegetables as well as basil and grated Parmesan cheese.
