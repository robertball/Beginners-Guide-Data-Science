Title:
Firestick Potato Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 potato
2 t olive oil
1/4 t cayenne pepper
1/4 t salt
1/4 t garlic, granulated
1/2 t paprika
1/4 t chili powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 and place cooking tray in pan to heat up prior to adding potatoes.
Cut the potatoes into 8 wedges and coat in the olive oil.
Combine all spices together.
Coat the oiled potatoes with the spice mixture until they are evenly covered.
Bake the potatoes in a 350F oven until the center of the potatoes has reached 180F and the outsides are crispy, about 25 minutes or so.
