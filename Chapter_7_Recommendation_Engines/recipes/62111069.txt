Title:
John Wayne Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-4 cans chopped green chilies
1 lb. Monterry Jack cheese
1 lb. Chedder cheese
4 egg whites
4 egg yolks
2/3 cup evaporated milk
1 Tbls. flour
1/2 tsp each salt & pepper

Directions:
Grate cheese and combine with green chilies. Pour into buttered 9x13" casserole dish. Beat egg yolks, add flour, salt, pepper and milk. Beat egg whites till stiff peaks form. Fold into egg yolk mixture. Pour over cheese mixture. Bake at 350 degrees for approx. 30 min.

Submitter: 
Alice Mayo Edelhauser
Description: 
This was given to me by a dear friend many years ago. I have served this at many brunches and have always been asked for the recipe. It is great served with fruit salad, biscuits, breakfast pastries, bacon, sausage, or whatever you like. Enjoy!
