Title:
MARTI'S TABBOULEH Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1114013.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c. bulgur wheat
3 c. curly parsley, finely chopped
1/2 c. fresh mint, finely chopped
8 green onions, including tops, finely sliced
1 pint grape tomatoes, quartered
1 European cucumber, chopped
1/4 c. olive oil, lemon flavored, if available
1/4 c. fresh lemon juice
1 t. salt
1/2 t. pepper
1/4 t. ground cumin
Garnish:
6 grape tomatoes, halved
6 slices cucumber
1 sprig parsley
several sprigs fresh dill

Directions:
Place bulgur in a large bowl.
Pour 3 cups very hot water over. Let stand for 1 hour.  Drain and return to bowl.
Stir in parsley, mint, green onions, tomatoes and cucumbers.
Mix olive oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper and cumin in a cup.  Pour over the tabbouleh and mix well.  Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
Before serving mix again well and arrange garnishes as desired.

Submitter: 
Marti
Description: 
I have developed my own version of this wonderfully refreshing Mediterranean side dish. We especially like it served with grilled lemon pepper chicken breasts.
