Title:
Iced Green Tea with Mint and Honey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
4 green tea bags
2 mint tea bags
1/3 cup honey
5 cups hot water
4-6 sprigs of fresh mint

Directions:
Boil water and add tea bags. Let steep for 5 minutes.
Discard tea bags. Add honey and stir.
Cool in the refrigerator for 30-60 minutes.
Serve over ice and garnish with fresh mint leaves.

Submitter: 
Natalie Tibbs
Description: 
What could be more refreshing on a warm summer day? And the beauty of this drink is that you'll boost your immune system with each delicious sip. Andrea Beaman
