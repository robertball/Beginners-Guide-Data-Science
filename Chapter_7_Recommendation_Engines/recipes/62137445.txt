Title:
Val's Stupid Easy Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lg can diced tomatoes
1 large can tomato sauce
1 can Chicken broth
1 can water
1  Dry onion soup mix
1 lg 16 oz bag of frozen mixed vegetables

Directions:
Pour tomatoes, tomato sauce, broth,water and onion soup mix into a large pot and mix well. Add frozen vegetables and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer 2 hours. Serve with a green salad and warm french bread
