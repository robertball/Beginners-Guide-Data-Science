Title:
El Chico Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c Miracle Whip
1/2 c. Milk
1/2 t Paprika
1/2 t Garlic Powder
1/2 t White Pepper
1T White Sugar

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Chill for at least 2 hours

Submitter: 
Kevin G
Description: 
This is the great pink El Chico dressing.  Goes great on any Mexican food.
