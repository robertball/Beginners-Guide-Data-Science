Title:
Spinach Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg (10oz) thawed drained chopped spinach
1 (14oz) jar marinated artichokes, drained and chopped
6oz cream cheese softened
1/4 cup sour cream
1/4 cup mayo
1/3 cup grated parmesean
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and heat in oven 350 for abt 8-10 min stirring every few min. Serve hot. With crostinis

Delicious quick artichoke dip served hot.
