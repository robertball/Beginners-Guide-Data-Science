Title:
Cranberry Banana Oat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/526923.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c. all-purpose flour
1 c. quick oats
1 T. baking powder
1/2 t. salt
1 1/4 c. mashed ripe banana (about 3 bananas)
2 large eggs
1/3 c. melted butter
1/2 c. sugar
3/4 c. dried cranberries
1/2 t. lemon juice
2 T. sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Lightly grease an 8 1/2x 2 1/2 inch loaf pan with a little bit of butter.
Whisk together flour, oats, baking powder and salt and in large bowl. Set aside.
Whisk bananas, eggs, butter, and sour cream together in a separate bowl. Add sugar, cranberries, and lemon juice; mix until blended. Gently add the dry ingredients to the banana mixture. Mix until combined but do not over mix.
Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and bake until lightly browned, about 45-50 minutes. Allow the bread to cool in the pan before removing it, about 1 hour.

Description: 
A fun twist on banana bread. The cranberries add a sweet and tart flavor that really kicks up the flavor of this bread. Super moist and super flavorful, it's a great addition to brunch!!
