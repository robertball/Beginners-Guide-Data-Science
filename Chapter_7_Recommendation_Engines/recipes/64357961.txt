Title:
Bev's Minestrone Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground beef
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 cup onion, chopped
6 cups water
1 lb, 13 oz can chopped tomatoes (with juice)
2 beef bouillon cubes
1 16 oz can kidney beans, drained
1-1/2 cups rigatonni
2 cups zuchinni, diced
1 cup celery sliced
2 Tbsp fresh parsley, chopped (or 1 Tbsp dry)
2 tsp oregano
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
Parmesan cheese, grated

Directions:
In large soup pot, brown ground beef, garlic and onion.
Add water, tomatoes and bouillon cubes.  Bring to boil.
Add remaining ingredients (except for cheese).  Cook 15 minutes.
Serve with cheese sprinkled on top of bowl.
