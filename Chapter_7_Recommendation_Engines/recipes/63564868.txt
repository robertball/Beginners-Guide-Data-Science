Title:
Corn Beef & Cabbage To Die For Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 oz. beef stock or broth
24 oz. chicken stock
2-3lb. brisket or round cut (corn beef)
4 tbls. of pickling spice
3-4 bay leafs
1 tbls. dryed garlic
1 head cabbage

Directions:
Trim any excess fat from the brisket
Place the brisket in a slow cooker Set to low
Pour in the just the beef stock.
Place all the spices in a cheese cloth bag, seal and place in the slow cooker.
Cook the brisket for about 8 hrs.
Remove the brisket, let the stock cool. Strain the stock to remove anything that is not liquid.
Add the chicken stock to the beef stock.
Cut the cabbage in quarters, then half the quarters.
Cut out the heart of the cabbage before placing in the stock.
As the cabbage is cooking, cut the corned beef into small spoon size bites and add to the slow cooker.
The cabbage should be ready in about 21/2 hrs
