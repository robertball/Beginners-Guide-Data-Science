Title:
Fat-Free Creamy Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups of peeled, diced potatoes
8 oz fat-free chicken broth
salt, to taste
garlic salt, to taste
black pepper, to taste

Directions:
Boil potatoes until tender. Drain well.
Mash or put through potato ricer, adding broth until desired thinness and consistency is reached.
Season to taste.
