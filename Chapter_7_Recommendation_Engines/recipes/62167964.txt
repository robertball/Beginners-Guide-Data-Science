Title:
Easy Cheesy Creamed Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Packages Frozen Spinach
1/2 Cup Pamesan Cheese
4T Half and Half
Salt and Pepper to taste
Dash of Nutmeg

Directions:
Thaw spinach in a bowl in a microwave reserving the liquid.
Place the spinach in a medium size saucepan and cover with reserved liquid.  Be sure to cover the top of the spinach and add more water if needed.  Bring to a boil then reduce heat to low; simmer covered for 20 minutes.
Drain the liquid; stir in Parmesan cheese, half and half, salt and pepper and a dash of nutmeg.
