Title:
Cucumber Sandwich Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 C. soft cream cheese with herbs and garlic (from 8 oz tub)
12 slices cocktail bread
12 thin slices of cucumber

Directions:
Spread about 1 1/2 tsp. cream cheese on to each slice of bread.  Top each with cucumber slice.
Serve immediately, or cover loosely and refrigerate until serving time.
