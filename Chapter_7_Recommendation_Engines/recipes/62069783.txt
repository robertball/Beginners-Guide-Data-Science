Title:
HOT CREAMY CRAB DIP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15oz) can yellow corn, drained
2 1/2 cups immatation crab meat (chopped up in bite size pieces)
1/4 Cup HOT salsa
1 (8oz) package cream cheese (softened and cut in small cubes for easy mixing)
1 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 Cup shredded taco cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.  In 9 x 9 baking dish mix all ingredients except shredded taco cheese. (Make sure to blend cream cheese well with mix.) Sprinkle shredded taco cheese on top and cover with foil.  Bake for 40 minutes or until hot.  Enjoy!!!  Your tastebuds will thank you!!

Submitter: 
Hawn
Description: 
I've been making this crab dip for over 10 years and everyone loves it! Even won first place in our families bake off 2 years ago!! All the ingredients baked together gives this dip an AWESOME flavor!!
