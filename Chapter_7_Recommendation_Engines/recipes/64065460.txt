Title:
Sweet Popcorn Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 quarts popped corn
1/4 cup Splenda
2 TBSP water
1/4 cup dark corn syrup
2 TBSP butter
1/2 cup peanuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 200 degrees. Spread popped corn and peanuts in a jelly roll pan. Place in warm oven.
Combine splenda, water, corn syrup and butter in a heavy saucepan; cook stirring constantly, over medium-high heat, until splenda dissolves. Bring mixture to a rolling boil; boil 3 minutes. Pour hot syrup over corn and peanuts, stirring well with a wooden spoon.
