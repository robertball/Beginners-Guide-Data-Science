Title:
Kitchen Sink Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Margerine, softened
1/2 Cup Applesauce
1 Cup packed Dark Brown Sugar
1/2 Cup White Sugar
2 Eggs
2 Tbsp. Vanilla Extract
1 1/2 Cups Flour
1 tsp Baking Soda
1/2 tsp Salt
3 Cups Rolled Oats
1 Cup Reese's Peanut Butter Chips
1 Cup Milk Chocolate Chips or Semi Sweet Chocolate Chips
3/4 Cup Shredded Sweetened Coconut

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
Cream butter, applesauce, brown sugar and white sugar in mixing bowl until smooth
Beat eggs in one at a time, then vanilla
Mixing with a spoon add flour, baking soda and salt until just blended. Then add oatmeal, chocolate chips, peanut butter chips and coconut until evenly ditributed.
No need to chill dough.
Drop by heaping spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake for 12-14 minutes or until golden brown at edges.
Remove from cookie sheet after 5 minutes to cool on wire rack.

Submitter: 
GourmetSoy
Description: 
These are crunchy on the outside and chewy on the inside! These are great and a little healthier than conventional cookies because applesauce is substituted for some of the butter:)You'll never know and it keeps them nice and moist.
