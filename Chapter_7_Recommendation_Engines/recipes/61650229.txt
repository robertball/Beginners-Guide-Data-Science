Title:
Healthier Too Much Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/281266.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (17.1 oz) natural chocolate cake mix
1 (4.5 oz) package instant chocolate pudding mix
1 cup low-fat sour cream
1/2 cup vegetable oil
4 eggs
1/2 cup warm water
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
confectioners' sugar for dusting on top of the cooled cake (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, mix together the cake and pudding mixes, sour cream, oil, beaten eggs and water.  Stir in the chocolate chips and pour batter into a well greased 12 cup bundt pan.
Bake for 50 to 55 minutes, or until top is springy to the touch and a wooden toothpick inserted comes out clean. Cool cake thoroughly in pan at least an hour and a half before inverting onto a plate  If desired, dust the cake with confectioners' sugar before serving.
