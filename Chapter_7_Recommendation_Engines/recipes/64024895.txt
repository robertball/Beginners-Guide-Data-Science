Title:
ComeBack My Way Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup good mayo
3/4 cup chili sauce
1/4 cup canola oil
3 tbsp dijon mustard
2 tbsp cider vinegar
2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
3 tbsp finely grated onion with juice
1 tbsp minced garlic
1 tsp paprika
3/4 tsp celery seeds
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp fresh ground black pepper
5 shakes hot sauce, or to taste

Directions:
Whisk all ingredients together. Store in a covered jar in the refrigerator for 2 weeks - if it lasts that long!

Submitter: 
HappyGrandma
Description: 
A jar of comeback sauce is a must-have in Southern refrigerators. A dollop on fried green tomatoes or crab cakes, a dipping sauce for everything from shrimp to chicken nuggets, fried pickles or zucchini, French fries & onion rings, grilled okra or as a great spread for sandwiches or burgers.
