Title:
Mexican Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. ground beef
1 can (16 oz) refried beans
1 can (4 oz) chopped green chilies
1 pkg. (4 Tbsp+) taco seasoning
1/2 cup water
2 Tbsp. hot salsa
4 cups shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese, divided
12 oz uncooked oven-ready lasagna noodles
1 jar (16 oz) mild salsa
2 cups water
2 cups (16 oz) sour cream
1 can (2.25 oz) sliced ripe olives, drained
3 green onions, chopped
1 medium tomato, chopped, optional

Directions:
Cook beef in frying pan. Once cooked, add beans, chilies, taco seasoning, water and hot salsa.
In greased 9 x 13 pan, layer 1/3 noodles and 1/3 meat mixture. Sprinkle with 1 cup cheese. Repeat layers twice more.
Combine mild salsa and water, pour over top.
Cover and bake at 350 F for 1 hr or until heated through.
Top with sour cream, olives, onions, tomatoes, if desired, and remaining cheese. Bake, uncovered, 5 minutes longer.
Let stand 10-15 minutes before cutting
