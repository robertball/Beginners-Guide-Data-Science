Title:
Picante Cream Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987390.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (16 ounce) jar PaceÂ® Picante Sauce
Cut-up vegetables or tortilla chips

Directions:
Place cream cheese in medium bowl and stir until smooth. Add picante sauce and mix well. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve. Serve with cut-up vegetables or chips.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
It couldn't be easier to make this zesty dip...it combines cream cheese and picante sauce to ensure you've got a tasty appetizer ready in a snap.
