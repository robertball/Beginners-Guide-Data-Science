Title:
Crockpot Chicken Cacciatore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/487335.jpg
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 green bell pepper, seeded and diced
8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 onion, finely diced
2 carrots,peeled and diced
2 tablespoons minced garlic
1 (28 ounce) can of crushed tomato
1 teaspoon of italian seasoning
1/2 teaspoon of sugar
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place all ingredients in slow cooker. Stir to make sure liquid evenly distributed.
Cover, and cook on Low for 7 to 9 hours.

Description:
