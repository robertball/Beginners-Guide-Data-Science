Title:
Cucumber Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 medium cucumbers
1 small clove garlic
3 Tablespoons white vinegar
3 cups chicken broth
2 and 1/2 cups sour cream
2 Tablespoons Lowrey's Seasoned Salt
Almonds, Tomatoes, Onions, Parsley

Directions:
Peel, seed and coarsely chop cucumbers
Peel and chop garlic
Puree in food processor with about 1 cup broth
Add vinegar, sour cream, salt and rest of chicken broth
Puree until smooth.
Chill until icy cold
Garnish with any or all of the following
chopped tomatoes, onions, parsley or slivered almonds.

Submitter: 
Jane G
Description: 
This soup has been in our family for 4 generations. Grandmother added the garlic, mom added Lowrey’s Seasoned Salt and I made it a puree. It's light, refreshing on a hot summer’s day and decidedly Cucumber!
