Title:
Low Carb Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Low carb flour tortillas
Water
Salt to taste

Directions:
Dampen tortilla on both sides with water. Salt tortillas.
Cook in microwave on high for 3 minutes. Cut tortillas into wedges. Flip over and cook for additional 2 minutes or until crisp depending on microwave and thickness of tortillas.
