Title:
Chicken Supreme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces sour cream
1 tsp garlic powder
2 TBSP lemon juice
1 tsp sea salt
1 tsp salt (optional)
1 tsp paprika
dash of tabasco
8 chicken breasts
2 cups corn bread stuffing mix
1/2 cup butter.

Directions:
Thoroughly mix together all ingredients except chicken. Pound chicken slightly if they are too thick. Add to mixture and marinate overnight.
Spread stuffing mix in pan, crush slightly. Coat chicken breasts with stuffing and roll up, place them in a 9x13 pan.
Melt butter and pour over chicken.
Bake at 350 degrees for one hour or until internal temperature reaches 160 degrees.
