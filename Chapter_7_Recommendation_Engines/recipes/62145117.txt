Title:
Banana nut bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tabl. Baking powder
3 1/2 c. Flour
1 tea. Baking soda
1 tea. Salt (optional)
2 c. Mashed bananas (4-6)
3/4 C softened butter
3 lg eggs
3/4 c. milk
3/4 c. Chopped walnuts
1 1/2 c. sugar

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350.
Grease 2 Loaf pans.
Mix dry ingredients together
Then combine both together.
Pour 1/2 of the mixture into each pre-greased loaf pan.
Bake 55 min, or until a tooth pick comes out clean.
I usually have to bake an additional 10-15 min. It just depends!
