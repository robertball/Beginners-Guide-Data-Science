Title:
Enchilada Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 11.5 oz package flour tortillas for burritos
2 cups cubed cooked chicken
1/2 cup uncooked instant white rice
8 oz shredded monterey jack cheese
1 15 oz can black beans, drained
1 19 oz can red enchilada sauce
1 cup cooked corn
1 cup chunky salsa
2 tbsp thinly sliced green onions

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Grease a 9 inch round baking or casserole dish.
Cut 5 of the tortillas in half, and cut remaining tortillas into 2 1/2 inch wide strips. In a large bowl, combine chicken, rice, 1 cup of the cheese, beans, and 1 cup of the enchilada sauce; mix well.
Layer 4 of the tortilla halves in the bottom of the baking dish. Top with 1/4 cup enchilada sauce and half of the chicken mixture. Top with 2 tortilla halves; fill in empty spaces with 3 tortilla strips. Spoon corn over tortillas. Spread salsa over corn. Layer with 2 tortilla halves and 3 strips. Top with remaining half of the chicken mixture. Continue layering with remaining 2 tortilla halves and strips, enchilada sauce, cheese, and 2 tablespoons green onions.
Bake for 35 to 45 minutes or until mixture is throughly heated and cheese is melted. Let cool for 5 minutes.
