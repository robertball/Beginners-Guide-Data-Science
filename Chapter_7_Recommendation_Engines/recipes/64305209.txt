Title:
Marathi mirch achaar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Green Chilli  or Adrak - 2 cups ( Use any variety)
Red wine vinegar or Rice vinegar --1/2 cup
Oil - 4 tblspn
For Grinding:
Coriander Seeds / Thania - 3 tblspn
Fennel Seeds / Sombu / Saunf - 1 tblspn
Mustard Seeds / Kaduku - 1 tsp
Fenugreek Seeds / Vendayam - 1 tsp
Asafoetida / Hing / Kaya Podi - 1/2 tsp
Turmeric Powder / Manjal Pod - 1tsp
Salt - 1 tblspn

Directions:
ash green chillies and wipe them dry. Now chop them into small pieces.
Take all the ingredients given in the list in a blender. Make into a powder.
Add the powder into a bowl and vinegar  mix well.
Now heat the oil till it smokes. Pour the smoking hot oil over the spices and mix well.Now add in the chopped chilli and toss well with the masala.
Take a clean, dry, air tight jar and pour the chillies  into them.
Cover and store them for 2 days before consuming.
