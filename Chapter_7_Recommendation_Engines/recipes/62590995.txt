Title:
chicken pasta toss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz bow tie pasta or less
3 c. broccoli flowerets
2 boneless chicken breasts (about 1 1/4 #) cooked, cut into strips
1 cup sun-dried tomatoes in oil
1/4 c. oil from tomatoes
1 1/2 cups cheese:  romano, mozza,etc.

Directions:
Cook pasta, adding broccoli during last 3 minutes of cooking time.  Drain.
Toss pasta and broccoli with remaining ingredients, seasoning with salt and pepper.
