Title:
Turkey/Chicken Slider Delight (kid-prepared) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5150839.jpg
Ingredients:
32 Ritz crackers
4 oz. cream cheese
8 tsp. ranch dressing
4 slices of turkey (or chicken) breast
16 grape tomatoes
4 tsp sriracha

Directions:
Place 16 Ritz crackers on a plate (the others will be used as the tops of the sliders).
Put a quarter-sized piece of cream cheese on each cracker.
Pour 1/2 tsp of ranch on top of the cream cheese.
Place a 1 by 1 inch piece of turkey/chicken on the cracker.
Cut tomatoes into 4 slices (but only use the middle two) and put on to the turkey slice. Set the sliders aside.
Take the other 16 crackers and spread 1/4 tsp sriracha onto each one.
Place the sriracha crackers onto the turkey crackers and put onto plate.
Optional-Serve with a glass of milk.

An easy to make snack/appetizer thats spicy and cool.
