Title:
Mediterranean Chicken II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ½ to 3 lb meaty chicken pieces
2 T cooking oil
1/4 cup dry white wine (optional)
1-14½ oz can tomatoes, cut up
¼ tsp ground cinnamon
1 6 oz jar marinated artichoke hearts,drained & quartered
1/2 envelope onion soup mix
1/2 cup pitted ripe olives, halved

Directions:
Skin and rinse chicken, pat dry.  In a
12 in skillet brown chicken in hot oil.
Drain fat.  Stir together the undrained
tomatoes and soup mix.  Add to chicken
in skillet.  Bring to boiling, reduce
heat.  Simmer, covered for 30-35
minutes.  Add artichokes, olives, wine,
and cinnamon.  Simmer, covered, 10
minutes or till chicken is no longer
pink.
Note:  Can also use boneless, skinless chicken breasts instead of chicken pieces.
