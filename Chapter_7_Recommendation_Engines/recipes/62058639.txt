Title:
BLUEBERRY SNACK CAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups self-rising flour
1  cups sugar
cup cold butter or marg
1 cup milk
2 eggs separated
2 cups fresh or frozen blueberries (do not thaw before adding to batter)

Directions:
Combine flour and sugar.  Cut in butter until crumbly.  Set aside  cup for topping.
Add baking powder, milk, egg yolks to remaining mixture and mix well.
Beat egg whites until soft peaks form and fold into batter.
Pour into greased 13 X 9 pan.  Sprinkle with blueberries and reserved crumb topping.
Bake at 350 for 30-35 minutes or until golden brown and inserted toothpick comes out clear.  Enjoy!
