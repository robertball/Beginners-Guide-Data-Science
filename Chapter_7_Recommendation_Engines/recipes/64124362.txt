Title:
Herb Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium zucchini
1.5 tbsp fresh rosemary, minced
1.5 tbsp fresh thyme, minced
cold pressed EVOO
salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F or grill to medium.
Slice the zucchini lengthwise into halves.
Lightly brush both sides with olive oil.
If baking, place on a cookie sheet.
Sprinkle rosemary and thyme over the zucchini. Salt and pepper to taste.
Cook for 15 minutes and serve warm.

Submitter: 
CleanHealthandFitness
Description: 
This is a quick and easy side dish that you can add to the grill or throw in the oven with very little prep.
