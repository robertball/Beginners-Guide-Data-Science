Title:
Easy Bacon and Chicken Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1343359.jpg
Ingredients:
10 Slices Bacon
1 small carrot, finely diced
1 stalk celery, diced
1 small onion, finely diced
1 clove garlic, minced
2 Diced Jalapenos
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
1 1/2 cups white corn kernels
1 1/2 cups yellow corn kernels
2 Cans Sweet Creamed Corn
4 russet potatoes, diced
2 cooked rotisserie chicken breast halves, shredded
4 cups chicken stock, divided
4 cups chicken broth
4 cups fat free half-and-half
Fresh ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Chop and add bacon into large frying pan and add carrot, celery, onion, garlic and jalapenos into frying pan and cook thouroughly. Stir flour into mixture to make a paste; cook until lightly browned and flour gives off a slightly toasted smell, about 5 minutes. Watch carefully, flour burns easily. Remove saucepan from heat and set aside to cool, about 15 minutes.
While waiting for the bacon veggie mix to cook add white and yellow corn, creamed corn, potatoes, chicken, and chicken stock into crock pot. Stir  the bacon veggie mixture into ingredients in the crock pot. Cook on low for 5-7 hours
Stir half-and-half, and black pepper into chowder the last 30-60 minutes of cooking.

Description: 
A rich corn chowder built on a base of chicken stock and half-and-half has yellow and white corn, potatoes, and shredded chicken for a meal in a bowl.Modified from: Easy Chicken and Corn Chowder
