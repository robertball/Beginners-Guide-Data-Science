Title:
Pork Chops Ala Jasmine All-In-1 Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/749594.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon olive oil or vegetable oil
4 bone-in pork chops, 3/4-inch thick
*will work with boneless*
1 tablespoon onion powder
1 cup uncooked regular long-grain jasmine rice
2 chicken bouillon cubes dissolved as per instruction on package (10.5 oz)
1 cup Simply Orange orange with mango juice
3 tablespoons fresh thyme
4 orange slices

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet.  Add chops and cook until browned.
Add onion powder and rice and cook until rice is browned.  Stir in broth, orange juice and 2 tablespoons thyme.  Heat to a boil.  Cover and cook over low heat 20 minutes or until done.
Top with orange slices and sprinkle with remaining thyme.
