Title:
Warm Trail Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c rolled oats
1/4 c raisins
1/4 c chopped walnuts
1/4 c sunflower seeds (raw)
2 T grape juice
1 T maple syrup

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, mixing thoroughly, in a microwave-safe bowl.
Microwave for 30 seconds.
