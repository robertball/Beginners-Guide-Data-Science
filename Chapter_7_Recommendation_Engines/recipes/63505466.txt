Title:
No Crust Egg Custard Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine - Softened
4 eggs
3/4 cup white granulated sugar
1/2 cup all purpose flour
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 cups milk
dash salt
Sprinkle of cinnamon and nutmeg (for top of pie)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Liberally butter a 9-inch pie plate.
Put all ingredients (except cinnamon and nutmeg) in a blender and blend until well mixed.  (About 30 seconds)
Pour into a well buttered 9-inch pie plate and sprinkle with cinnamon and nutmeg.
Bake at 350 degrees for 45-50 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean and bottom of pie is lightly brown.
Let cool and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Dorothy
Description: 
No crust?  Well actually, the flour will settle to the bottom of the pie when it is cooking and make it's own crust.
