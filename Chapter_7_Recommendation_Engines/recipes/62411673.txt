Title:
Cilantro Lime Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb boneless chicken thighs, cut into strips
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp onion powder
2 cloves roasted garlic
1 bunch Cilantro, chopped fine
2 limes
1/4 C chicken stock
salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 450 degrees.  Wrap garlic cloves in aluminum foil, and place in oven to roast for 15 minutes.
Heat saute pan to medium heat, add olive oil.  Season chicken thighs with salt, pepper, and onion powder, then add to hot pan.  Brown chicken on each side for approximately 8 minutes.
When chicken is brown, take garlic out of the oven and add into the pan.  Stir around to mash up the garlic clove.  Add in cilantro, the juice of two limes, and chicken stock.  Coat chicken with sauce and turn heat down to low.  Simmer for 5 minutes.
Serve with brown rice.
