Title:
Cheesy Bacon and Mushroom Baked Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1 lb Box macaroni - cooked and drained
1 tbsp butter
1 package sliced mushrooms
5 slices of bacon - cooked and crumbled (reserve fat)
1/4 cup chopped white onion
1 tsp garlic powder
6 ounces tomato paste
8 ounces sour cream
1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar or colby jack cheese
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
In a skillet, heat butter and saute mushrooms.  In reserved bacon fat cook onion until soft.  In a bowl combine mushrooms, bacon, onion and macaroni.  In a separate bowl combine sour cream and tomato paste. Add to macaroni mixture. Add 1 cup cheese to mixture.  Season with salt, pepper and garlic powder. Mix well.  Coat a casserole dish with non-stick spray or butter. Pour mixture into dish and sprinkle remaining cheese on top.  Bake at 350 for 20 minutes or until hot.
