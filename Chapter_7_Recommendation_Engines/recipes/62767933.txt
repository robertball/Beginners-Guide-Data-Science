Title:
Fusilli with Tomatoes, Peas, Garlic, Feta and Mint Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ounces fusilli pasta
2 T olive oil
2 8-ounce cans diced tomatoes
5 cloves garlic, minced
1 c baby peas, fresh or frozen
3 T capers, rinsed
1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
salt and pepper to taste
1/3 c crumbled feta

Directions:
Cook pasta, drain well. Return to pot and toss with 1 T olive oil.
Meanwhile, drain tomatoes, reserving 1/3 c of the juice. Set aside.
In a large skillet over medium-low heat, saute the garlic in the remaining olive oil until fragrant but not brown.
Add the peas, capers, tomatoes and reserved juice. Increase heat to medium-high and cook until the sauce is simmering.
Stir in the mint, salt, and pepper.
Serve the pasta with the sauce spooned on top and sprinkled with the feta.
