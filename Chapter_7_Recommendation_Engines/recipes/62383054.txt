Title:
Big Daddy's Peach Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can sliced peaches, drained
4 scoops vanilla ice cream
2 cups skim milk
1/4 cup orange juice
2 scoops of Mila

Directions:
In a blender, combine peaches, ice cream, skim milk, Mila and orange juice. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.
