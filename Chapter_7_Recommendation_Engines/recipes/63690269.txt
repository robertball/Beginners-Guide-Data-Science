Title:
Chicken Pasta Summer Italian Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1025928.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box of multi color Rotini Pasta (cooked and cooled)
1 diced red bell pepper (diced)
2 lbs chicken breast (cooked and diced)
1 garlic clove (minced)
1 can of Garbanzo beans
1 can of black olives
2 cups Broccoli (bite site lightly steamed and cooled)
1 bottle of Wishbone House Italian dressing

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and chill for at least 1hr for best taste!

Diona Thompson
Description: 
This is a summertime family favorite salad.
