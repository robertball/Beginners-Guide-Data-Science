Title:
Dark Chocolate Toblerone Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065485.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Tolerone Dark Chocholate Bars (3.52 Oz, 100 Grams each), broken into large pieces
2 Eggs
2 Tbls Confectioner's Sugar
1/2 C & 2 Tbls Heavy Cream
1 Dash Cream of Tartar

Directions:
Place Toblerone in large bowl and cover with boiling H2O.
Allow Chocolate to soften, but NOT melt. Chocolate should NOT lose shape.
Pour off H2O.
In a separate bowl, beat eggs sugar until light.
Add the softened Chocolate and blend well.
Beat the Cream to stiff peaks and add Cream of Tartar.
Carefully, fold beaten cream into the chocolate mixture.
Chill 2-3 Hrs
