Title:
White Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts
24 oz chicken broth
42 oz can great northern beans
1 small jar green chilies
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon oregano
2 Tablespoons oilive oil
1 medium onion, chopped

Directions:
Cook chicken 15 minutes and dice
Saute onion in oil for 2 minutes
Add chicken broth and spices, chilies, onion; cook 30 minutes
Add drained and rinsed beans and chicken; cook 15 minutes
Top servings with shredded cheese
