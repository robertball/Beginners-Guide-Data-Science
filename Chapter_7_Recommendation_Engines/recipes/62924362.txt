Title:
** Chuck's No-Boil Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. hamburger - browned & drained
1 jar (30 oz.) favorite red pasta sauce
1 (12 oz.) carton low-fat cottage cheese
1 lb. low-fat mozzarella cheese - shredded
1/2 box (8 oz.) regular lasagne noodles (uncooked)
1 c. water
1/2 c. parmesan cheese - grated

Directions:
Mix cooked hamburger, pasta sauce & cottage cheese together in medium bowl.
Layer uncooked noodles, sauce & shredded mozzarella cheese in baking dish until all ingredients are used.
Top with parmesan cheese.
Pour water into pan around the edges.
Cover pan & bake at 350 degrees for 1 hr.
Uncover & bake an additional 15 min.
Remove from oven & let stand 15 min. before serving.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
Got this from good friend Carolyn Williams Robinson in the 1980's, before no-cook noodles were even invented.  It's an easy, basic semi-homemade lasagne.  I remember my friend and smile every time I serve this.
