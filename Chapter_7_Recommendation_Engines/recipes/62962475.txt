Title:
Basil Pesto a la Duke Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup chopped basil
1 Tbsp pine nuts
2 Tbsp parmesan cheese
1 clove garlic, minced
3 Tbsp Olive oil

Directions:
Combine the ingredients (except oil) in a mini chopper or food processor.
Add the oil after everything else is well chopped and give it a final few pulses to combine.
This should make about 10-12 Tbsp pesto.
Save the leftover pesto in the fridge up to a week or in the freezer up to 6 months.
