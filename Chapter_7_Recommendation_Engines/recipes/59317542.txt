Title:
Aunt Mary's Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the meatballs:
3 lbs. ground beef
1 C. onion, finely diced
3 C. bread crumbs (Use unseasoned if making to use in this sauce, or Italian seasoned if making for a spaghetti type sauce)
2 C. milk
2 eggs
2 teas. salt
For the sauce:
1 12 oz. jar of grape jelly
1 12 oz. jar of chile sauce
3 Tblsp. lemon juice

Directions:
Combine meat and onions and set aside.
Combine the remaining meatball ingredients and form onto medium size balls.
Bake on parchment paper for 15-20 minutes at 350 &#0176;
Drain on paper towels.
IMeanwhile, in a large pot, mix the jelly and chile sauce w/ the lemon juice.
Heat slowly until the jelly is dissolved.
Add the meatballs and simmer for 1 1/2 - 2 hours.
