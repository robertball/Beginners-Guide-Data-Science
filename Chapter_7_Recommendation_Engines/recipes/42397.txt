Title:
Olive Cheese Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/386044.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 (2.25 ounce) can sliced ripe olives, drained
2 green onions, chopped
1 1/2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 (1 pound) loaf unsliced French bread

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first five ingredients.
Slice bread in half widthwise and lengthwise; place on a baking sheet. Spread cheese mixture over cut sides of bread.
Bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes or until the cheese is melted.

Submitter:

Description: 
This rich, cheesy bread is a perfect complement to seafood, Italian food or barbecue fare.
