Poppy Seed Dressing III Recipe

1/2 cup whole milk
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
2/3 cup white sugar

Directions:
Stir the mayonnaise, milk, vinegar, sugar, and poppy seeds together until the sugar has dissolved.

Description:
