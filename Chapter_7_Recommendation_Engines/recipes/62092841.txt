Title:
Crunchy Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages ramen noodles (any flavor)
1 cup sunflower seeds (shelled)
1 bunch of green onions
1 cup slivered almonds
1# bag of coleslaw mix w/ carrots
1/2 cup vinegar

Directions:
Put coleslaw mix into a large bowl
Add dry ramen noodles(crush,but dont make into powder)
Add onions,nuts and seeds
Mix together.
Put 1/2 cup vinegar into a jar and add both packets of the ramen noodle seasonings
Shake up and add to coleslaw mix 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
SloppyJo
Description: 
This is a great side dish or salad item. I like to use spicy ramen noodles, for a more zesty flavor!! This recipe is even better the next day!!!
