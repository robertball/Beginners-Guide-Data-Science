Title:
Italian Dipping Sauce - The Real Deal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/836762.jpg
Ingredients:
NOTE: Any of the dried herbs below can be replaced by double the amount of fresh herbs.
1 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup aged balsamic vinegar or 2 Tablespoons lemon juice (or combination)
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 tablespoons dried basil
1 tablespoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1/4 teaspoon dried rosemary
1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 Tablespoon parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a covered bottle. Refrigerate 8 hours. Shake and serve. Keep refrigerated. Optionally cut additional fresh herb leaves for garnish.
