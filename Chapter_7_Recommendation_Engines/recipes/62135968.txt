Title:
Savory Low-Carb Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Meatloaf Mix (Or any ground beef/meat)
1 Egg, Beaten
1 Small Yellow Onion, Chopped
1 tsp (or 1 clove) minced Garlic
1 Tbs Worcestershire Sauce
1 tsp Salt
1/2 tsp Ground Black Pepper
1/2 Cup Crushed Low Carb Cheese Biscuits (J & M Foods Low Carb Cheese Biscuits) or other low-carb cracker
Low Carb Ketchup (Heinz One Carb Ketchup)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°.  Combine all ingredients in bowl.  Transfer to loaf pan and form into loaf shape.  Top with low carb ketchup and bake one hour.
