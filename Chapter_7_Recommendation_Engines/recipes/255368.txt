Title:
Vinagrete (Brazilian Tomato Slaw) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4005969.jpg
Ingredients:
5 tomatoes, diced
1/2 English cucumber, quartered and thinly sliced
1 red bell pepper, seeded and diced
1/2 cup diced onion
1/2 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
1/4 cup lime juice
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup diced green onions
1/4 cup finely chopped fresh cilantro
2 tablespoons cider vinegar
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, onion, parsley, lime juice, olive oil, green onions, cilantro, vinegar, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Let stand at room temperature until flavors combine, about 3 hours.

Submitter: 
Kevin Marcelle
Description: 
A refreshing side dish to pair with chicken, pork, or beef.
