Title:
Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cans Grands biscuits
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 T. cinnamon
1 stick butter
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Take the biscuits and slice each one into three pieces. Combine 1/2 c. sugar, brown sugar, and cinnamon. Roll the biscuit thirds into the mixture and arrange in layers in a greased bundt can. Use all of the sugar mixture. Next, melt the butter and add 1/2 c. sugar and cinnamon. Pour over the biscuits. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 min. Cool 20 to 15 min. and invert onto a serving plate.
