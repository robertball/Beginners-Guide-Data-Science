Title:
Roasted Turkey Enchilada Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993432.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups chopped leftover roasted turkey
2 cups salsa
1 (19 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 cup frozen corn
2 tablespoons chili powder
4 large flour tortillas
3/4 cup sour cream
1 1/2 cups KRAFT Habanero Heat Shredded Cheese

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees F.
Combine first 5 ingredients.
Cover bottom of 13x9-inch baking dish sprayed with cooking spray with 2 tortillas; top with layers of half each of the turkey mixture, sour cream and cheese. Repeat layers; cover.
Bake 40 min. or until casserole is heated through and cheese is melted, uncovering after 30 min.

Submitter: 
KRAFT Shredded Cheese
Description: 
Leftover turkey is transformed into an easy family dish with a few Mexican-inspired ingredients.
