Ingredients:
8 Jalapeno peppers
4 Chicken breast
1 block cream cheese
1/2 cup cheddar cheese
onion powder to taste
garlic powder to taste
paprika to taste
salt to taste
pepper to taste
8 slices of bacon

Directions:
Butterfly chicken breast,  I normally combine 1tsp each of paprika, pepper, onion, and 2tsp of garlic powder and salt.
combine cream cheese, cheddar cheese and minced jalapenos ( i remove seeds and membranes )
Spread mixture evenly amongst the chicken breasts.
Wrap two strips around each chicken breast secure with a toothpick
Normally we grill the chicken breast or I bake in the oven with a couple tablespoons of grass-fed butter (add fat for keto diet) at 350 for about 25 mins.
We normally serve with grilled zucchini and a side salad.

Description: 
Simple recipe. I am sure other people have made this recipe before.  We like to grill this during the summer and winter months.
