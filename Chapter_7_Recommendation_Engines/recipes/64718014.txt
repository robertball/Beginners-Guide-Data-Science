Title:
Triple Cheese Gluten-Free Jalapeno Poppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5819983.jpg
Ingredients:
•	4 red jalapenos
•	4 green jalapenos
•	250g soft goat cheese
•	3/4 c finely shredded Cheddar cheese
•	1/2 c finely shredded Monterey Jack cheese
•	1 tsp chili powder
•	1 tsp ground cumin
•	1/4 tsp ground coriander
•	1/4 c gluten free bread crumbs
•	1 tbsp Mexican oregano
•	2 tbsp cotija cheese
•	2 tbsp fresh thyme

Directions:
1.	Preheat the oven to 200°C and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2.	Cut the jalapenos in half, lengthwise. Using gloves, remove the seeds and the membranes.
3.	In a mixing bowl, mix the goat cheese, cheddar cheese, Monterey Jack cheese, chili powder, cumin, and coriander until well combined. Set aside.
4.	In a separate bowl, mix together the bread crumbs, oregano, and cotija cheese.
5.	Fill each jalapeno half with equal amounts of the goat cheese mixture. Gently roll the tops of the filled peppers in the bread crumbs. Place them onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes or until the bread crumbs have browned.
6.	Top with fresh thyme.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Three kinds of cheese and gluten free bread crumbs make a delicious appetizer the whole family will love. The gluten free bread crumbs crisp up just like regular ones thanks to adding cotija cheese before baking.
