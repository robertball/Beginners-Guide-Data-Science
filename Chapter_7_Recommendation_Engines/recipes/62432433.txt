Title:
Mom's Sweet & Sour Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 ounces Tomato Sauce
1/2 cup Brown Sugar
1/4 cup Vinegar
2 teaspoons Mustard
1 1/4 pound Ground Chuck
1 Egg, beaten
1/8 cup Minced Onion
1/4 cup Bread Crumbs
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1/2 teaspoon Pepper

Directions:
Mix tomato sauce, brown sugar, vinegar and mustard.
Mix remaining ingredients with 1/2 cup of tomato mixture.
Make into loaf and place on cookie sheet or in pan. Pour 1/4 cup of tomato mix over top and bake at 375 for 50 minutes to 1 hour.
Heat remaining sauce to serve. Great with mashed potatoes.
Double recipe for two loafs.
