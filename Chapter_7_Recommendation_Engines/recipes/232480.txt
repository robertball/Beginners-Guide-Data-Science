Title:
Paleo Tortillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1019138.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup almond flour
5 eggs
2 teaspoons honey
1 pinch salt
2 teaspoons butter, or as needed

Directions:
Mix almond flour, eggs, honey, and salt together in a bowl; form mixture into 6 balls. Press each ball into a tortilla shape.
Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat; cook each tortilla in the hot skillet until lightly browned, 1 to 2 minutes per side.

Submitter: 
Mouretsu
Description: 
Great paleo recipe for crepes or tortillas. Season with herbs of your liking.
