Title:
Beefy Biscuit Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1078064.jpg
Ingredients:
#1 ground beef
1 small onion, diced
2  4-oz. cans green chilis
1/2 tsp. cayenne pepper
salt to taste
1 egg
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
8 oz. tomato sauce
1 tube (10) biscuits
1 1/2 C. cheddar cheese, shredded
1/2 C. sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Spray 8" square pan with vegetable spray.
Brown beef, onion, green chiles. Drain.
Add cayenne, garlic, and tomato sauce, simmer while preparing dough.
Separate dough into 10 biscuits, then each biscuit into 2 equal rounds.
Press 10 (of the 1/2 biscuits) onto bottom of the pan.
Combine 1/2 C. cheese, egg, and sour cream.
Remove meat mixture from heat, and add sour cream mix. Add salt to taste.
Spoon mixture over dough layer. Arrange remaining biscuits over meat and sprinkle with remaining cheese.
Bake for 25-30 minutes until deep golden brown.
