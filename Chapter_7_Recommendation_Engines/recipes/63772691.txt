Title:
Breakfast Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1098117.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Pie Crust
4 Eggs
1/2 cup Milk
1/2 cup cubed pre-cooked Potatoes
1/2 cup cubed Ham
1/2 Shredded Cheese
Dash of pepper

Directions:
1) Preheat oven 375 degrees 2) Spray pie pan and place pie crust & cut off any excess dough off the edges 3) Mix the eggs, milk & pepper, set aside 4) Spread the potatoes, ham and cheese over pie then pour the egg mixture over the stuffing. 5) Sprinkle extra cheese on top (optional) 6) Cook in oven for 40-45 min or until egg is firm and slightly golden brown
