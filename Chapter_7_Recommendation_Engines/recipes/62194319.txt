Title:
Triple-Chocolate Coffee Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 beaten egg
1 21 1/2-oz. package fudge brownie mix
1/4 cup cooking oil
1/4 cup coffee liqueur or strong coffee
1/4 cup strong coffee or water
3/4 cup milk chocolate pieces
3/4 cup white baking pieces with cocoa butter (optional)
1/2 cup semisweet chocolate pieces
1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans

Directions:
In large mixing bowl combine egg, brownie mix, cooking oil, coffee liqueur, or coffee, coffee or water, milk chocolate pieces, white baking pieces, semisweet chocolate pieces and the nuts. Stir just until combined.
Spread in a greased 13x9x2-in. baking pan. Bake in a 350 degree oven for 30 minutes.
Cool completely in pan on a wire rack. Cut into bars.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Made with brownie mix, coffee, milk chocolate, white chocolate and semisweet pieces, and nuts, they take just minutes to stir together and pop in oven. Ths recipe was a winner in a Better Homes and Gardens contest in Feb. 1991. Still a winner to me.
