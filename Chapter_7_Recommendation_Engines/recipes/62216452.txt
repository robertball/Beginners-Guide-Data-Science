Title:
Richter's Ricotta Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/312647.jpg
Ingredients:
Cookies:
2 sticks of butter
2 cups of sugar
1 lb of ricotta cheese
3 eggs
1 tsp of vanilla
4 cups of flower
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp of baking soda
Icing:
8 oz. of cream cheese (room temperature)
1 tsp of vanilla
2 cups of powdered sugar

Directions:
Cookies:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cream butter and sugar with a hand mixer.
Add eggs, mix.
Sift together flour/salt/soda in seperate bowl.
Slowly add to creamed butter mixture, drop by tablespoon on ungreased cookie sheet and bake 12-15 minutes in oven.
Icing:
Mix ingredients and spread on semi-cooled cookies.
