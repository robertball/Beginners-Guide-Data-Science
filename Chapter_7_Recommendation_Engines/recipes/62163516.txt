Title:
Southern Country Fried Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2380492.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
¼ Garlic power
¼ Onion power
4.5-ounce beef cubed steaks
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1.cup beef broth

Directions:
Combine the flour, pepper, salt, garlic power, onion power in a zip lock bag.( you will use to flour the meat) Coat the steaks on both sides with flour mixture.  Make sure to coat them well, pressing the flour mixture into the meat.
Heat 3 tablespoons of vegetable oil in the large skillet over medium heat.
Fry the steaks in the pan until they're browned on both sides.  You can add a little more oil (1 to 2 tablespoons) if needed.
Add the broth, stirring well.  Use enough broth to make the amount of gravy you want.
Cover the skillet and let simmer on low heat 30 minutes, or until the meat is tender and the gravy is thickened.
Taste the gravy and add salt and pepper, if desired.
