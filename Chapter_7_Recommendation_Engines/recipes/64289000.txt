Title:
Miso Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T olive oil
1 T sesame oil
1-2 T fresh chopped ginger
6-8 garlic cloves chopped
14 oz Laksa Noodles
14 oz firm tofu, chopped into 1/2 in cubes
2-3 c shitake mushrooms
6-8 leaves kale
2 tsp Thai red curry paste
6 c chicken or vegetable or mushroom broth (use mushroom)
2-4 T tamari
1/2 c or more miso paste
squeeze lime
4-6 green onions, chopped
handful cilantro
handful basil
siracha sauce

Directions:
preheat oven 400
if desired, drizzle 1-2 T sesame oil and 1-2 T tamari over tofu and bake at 400 until crispy
put garlic and ginger in olive oil on low-medium heat to soften
add chopped mushrooms and kale just to soften
add tamari to taste
cook noodles as directed and drain, set aside
heat broth
add miso and tamari and red curry paste or siracha to taste
heat to boil for bit
add mushroom/kale/garlic/ginger mixture, noodles and tofu to broth and heat through
best if sits for while
add green onions, cilantro, basil and lime as desired

Submitter: 
seren
Description: 
Warm miso soup; many variations.  Use mushroom broth next time.
