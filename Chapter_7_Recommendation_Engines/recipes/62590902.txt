Title:
Pineapple Almond Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup all purpose flour
3/4 cup quick cooking oats
1/3 cup brown sugar
5 tbsp. butter
1/2 tsp. almond extract
3 tbsp. sliced almonds
1 cup pineapple preserves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
In food processor combine flour, oats and brown sugar.
Process until blended.
Add butter and process until crumbly.
Remove 1/2 cup of mixture, add sliced almonds and reserve.
Press remaining crumb mixture into greased 9" square pan.
Spread preserves over crust and sprinkle with reserved crumb/almond mixture.
Bake for 25-30 min. or until golden.
Press remaining
