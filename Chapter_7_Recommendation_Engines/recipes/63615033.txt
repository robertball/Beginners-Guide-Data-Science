Title:
Tropical Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1005490.jpg
Ingredients:
1 part Vodka
1 part Mango Puree
1 part Natural Pineapple Juice
1 part Passion Fruit Juice
splash Grenadine

Directions:
Pour all ingredients minus grenadine into martini shaker with ice.
Shake for 20-30 seconds.
Pour into a martini glass and add a splash of grenadine which sinks to the bottom of your glass.(..and looks amazing!!)
Garnishes welcome ex: Whole strawberry on the side of glass, chunk of fresh pineapple etc...

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
This is a dessert in a glass! The recipe is explained in parts so you can make it in little or large quantities. The grenadine looks beautiful plus it balances out the fruit juices to give it a wonderful full body flavor so don't skip this step!
