Title:
Vanner Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
5 tablespoons sweetened tropical punch flavored drink mix powder
6 cups water
12 fluid ounces 190 proof grain alcohol
1 1/2 cups sloe gin
2 cubes ice

Directions:
Measure drink mix into a large pitcher. Pour in water, grain alcohol and sloe gin. Toss in ice, mix well and serve.

Submitter: 
MRSMAYNARD
Description: 
This is for adults only. A high-alcohol punch-flavored drink. Its toxicity comes in the form of 190 proof grain alcohol and sloe gin.
