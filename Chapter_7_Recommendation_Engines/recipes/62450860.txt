Title:
Crystal's Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz cooked tri colored pasta
1/4 lb cubed provalone cheese
1/4 lb cubed hard salami
1/4 lb pepperoni
1 C onions chopped
1 C green and black olives (optional)
Sauce:
1 Tbsp Oregano
1 Tbsp Salt
1 1/2 Tbsp sugar
1/2 cup oil
1/2 cup red wine vinegar
1/4 C parsley to taste, chopped

Directions:
Mix sauce together.
Combine ingredients and stir.
If its a little dry just add some more red wine vinegar.
