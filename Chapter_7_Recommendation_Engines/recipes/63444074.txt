Title:
Marvelous Millet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/934810.jpg
Ingredients:
1 onion
2 carrots
3 tablespoons Olive Oil
1 cup millet
2 1/2 cups water
1 veggie bouillon (or un-chicken bouillon)
1/2 teaspoon Tumeric (This is a super antioxidant!)

Directions:
Chop onion.
Peel and dice carrots.
Saute the onion in olive oil in sauce pan until onion is soft and clear.
Add millet and saut√©, stirring constantly, cover millet with oil and toast. Millet should be slightly brown.
Add water, bouillon, turmeric and bring to a boil.
Simmer cook for 15 minutes.

ChrissyintheKitchen
Description: 
A tasty side dish that compliments meat or vegetarian entrees. Millet is easy to digest and gluten-free.
