Title:
Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can pumpkin
3 c. sugar
1 1/2 t. salt
1 t. cinnamon
1 t. nutmeg
3 1/2 c. flour
1 t. vanilla
2 t. baking soda
2/3 c. water
1 c. vegetable oil
1 c. chopped nuts

Directions:
Mix 1 1/2 c. flour with the oil. Set aside.
Mix remaining ingredients in order until well combined.
Add oil/flour mixture, mix well.
Pour into 2 greased loaf pans and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
