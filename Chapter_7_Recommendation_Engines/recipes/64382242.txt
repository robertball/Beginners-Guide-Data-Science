Title:
Basic Lemon Dijion Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ shallot
1 garlic clove
1 T white vinegar
1 T apple cider vinegar
2 T lemon juice
½ tsp salt
½ tsp dijion mustard
¼ c olive oil
¼ c water (too much)

Directions:
combine all except water and olive oil in bowl
whisk
put in blender and blend
slowly add olive oil and water
