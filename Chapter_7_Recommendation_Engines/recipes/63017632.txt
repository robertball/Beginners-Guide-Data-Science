Title:
Breaded Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/755096.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds pork tenderloin
2 eggs
1/2 cup Italian-style dried bread crumbs
1 Tablespoon garlic salt
salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoon Canola oil for frying

Directions:
Trim and pound the pork tenderloin into 3/8" thickness. Dip the pork into the eggs to coat then dip in the breading of Italian bread crumbs and garlic salt mixture. Fry in canola oil until golden brown on each side.
