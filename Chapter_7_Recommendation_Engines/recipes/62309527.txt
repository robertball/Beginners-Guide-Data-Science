Title:
Chopped Herring Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
jar herring
2 apples, chopped--not too fine
matzah--1/2
2 boiled eggs

Directions:
chop apples, add some matzah, chop a little more---not too mushy. add about 1/2 jar herring, drained. chop again. add a little sugar. top with grated egg.
