Title:
BBQ Onion Soup Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs extra lean ground beef
1 finely chopped onion
1 tsp each: ground coriander, paprika, steak spice
1 Tbs each: ketchup, barbeque sauce
1 egg, lightly beaten
1/2 cup breadcrumbs
1 package onion soup mix dissolved in 1/2 cup water

Directions:
Mix all ingredients
Form into 8 patties
Barbeque until cooked and dress to taste

Submitter: 
AnneP
Description: 
A simple, inexpensive summer favourite! The dry soup mix and BBQ sauce make these burgers extra juicy and full of flavour!
