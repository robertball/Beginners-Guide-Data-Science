Title:
Easy Homemade Flavored Spreads Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Strawberry:
1/2 cup Land O Lakes Fresh Buttery Taste Spread or Land O Lakes Spread with Sweet Cream
1/4 cup powdered sugar
1/4 cup strawberry or your favorite flavor preserves (not jam)
Apple Cinnamon:
1/2 cup Land O Lakes Fresh Buttery Taste Spread or Land O Lakes Spread with Sweet Cream
2 TBL firmly packed brown sugar
2 TBL apple jelly
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon

Directions:
Combine all ingredients for desired flavored spread in small mixer bowl. Beat at medium speed until fluffy and well mixed (1 minute).
Serve spread on toast, muffins, waffles, pancakes, etc. Store refrigerated in airtight container up to 2 weeks.
