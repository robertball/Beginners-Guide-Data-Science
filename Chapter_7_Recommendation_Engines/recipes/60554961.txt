Title:
Jackie's Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb cubed beef
1 TBS olive oil
salt and pepper to taste
1 16 oz can diced tomatoes
1 8 oz can tomato sauce
1 TBS soup base disolved in 1/2 cup water.
1 chopped onion
2 sticks chopped celery
1 bag frozen peas
1 lb carrots cut into rounds
4 potatoes cut into 2 inch chunks
2 cups water

Directions:
Brown salted and peppered beef cubes in olive oil.
Transfer beef cubes to crock pot.
Add rest of ingredients and stir to mix.
Cook on low for 10 hours.
