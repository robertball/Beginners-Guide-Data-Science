Title:
Quick Chicken with Fruity Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
Butter flavored non stick cooking spray
1 teaspoon powdered chicken bouillon
Ground black pepper
1 can mexicorn, drained
1 small 8 oz. can crushed pineapple, drained
1 small red sweet bell pepper, diced
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
2 tablespoons fresh jalepeno pepper, seeded and minced
1 tablespoon honey
1 teaspoon lemon or lime juice

Directions:
CHICKEN: Rinse and pat dry chicken breasts with paper towel.
Spray each breast front and back with butter spray.
Sprinkle chicken lightly with bouillon & black pepper.
Broil chicken about 4 inches from heat for 10 to 12 minutes or until cooked through, turning once.
SALSA: Stir together remaining ingredients and chill until ready to serve.
Serve atop broiled chicken breasts.

Submitter: 
Alta Nesbitt Rees Sieker
Description: 
Broiled juicy chicken breasts are served with a fruity salsa made with corn, pineapple, sweet red peppers and cilantro. Light and refreshing. I like to serve this dish along side a mushroom and onion couscous and a fresh garden salad.
