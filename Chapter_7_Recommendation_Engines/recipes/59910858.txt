Title:
Quick and Easy Hot Artichoke and Spinach Dip II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/270925.jpg
Ingredients:
15 ounce jar of spinach dip (I use Tostistos brand)
1 14 ounce can of chopped artichoke hearts
1 cup of pre-shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Lightly grease a small baking dish.
In a medium bowl, mix together spinach dip, chopped artichoke hearts, and half of the shredded mozzarella cheese
Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese. Heat in the microwave on high for 3 - 5 minutes, or until cheese begins to melt and the dip is heated through.
