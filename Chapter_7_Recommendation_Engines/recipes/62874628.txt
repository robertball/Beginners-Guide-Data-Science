Title:
Sesame Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. tilapia
2 Tbsp. olive oil
2 Tbsp. rice vinegar
2 Tbsp. lite soy sauce
1 tsp. chopped garlic
2 Tbsp. sesame seeds

Directions:
Spray broiler pan with vegetable oil spray. Place tilapia. Whisk together olive oil, rice vinegar, soy sauce and garlic until well blended. Pour over fish. Sprinkle sesame seeds over fish. Broil at medium heat about 8 inches from heat for about 20 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
