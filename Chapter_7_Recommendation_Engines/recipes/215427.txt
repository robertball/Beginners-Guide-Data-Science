Title:
Crescent Chicken Potpie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992059.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables
2 cups cubed, cooked chicken
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed reduced-fat reduced-sodium cream of chicken soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed reduced-fat reduced-sodium cream of mushroom soup
1 (8 ounce) can PillsburyÂ® Refrigerated Crescent Dinner Rolls

Directions:
In large bowl, mix vegetables, chicken and soups. Pour into greased or sprayed 13x9-inch (3-quart) glass baking dish.
Unroll dough into 2 long rectangles; press perforations to seal. Place rectangles over chicken mixture; pinch center edges to seal. Press outside edges of dough to edges of baking dish.
Bake at 350 degrees F 25 to 30 minutes or until top is golden brown.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Houck Spink
Description: 
Mix up a simple pot pie filling with mixed vegetables and cooked chicken and top with flaky crescent dough, for an quick and delicious comfort food dinner.
