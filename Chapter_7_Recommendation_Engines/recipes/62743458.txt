Title:
Chicken Marsala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/630658.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup all-purpose flour for coating
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded 1/4 inch thick
garlic to taste
4 tablespoons butter
4 tablespoons olive oil
3 cup sliced mushrooms
1 cup Marsala wine
1/2 cup cooking sherry
a little slurry (cornstarch water mix)
splash of chicken broth
splash of heavy cream

Directions:
In a ziplock bag, mix together the flour, salt, pepper and oregano. Put chicken pieces in flour mixture bag and shake to coat.
In a large skillet, melt butter in oil over medium heat. Place chicken and garlic in the pan, and lightly brown.  Turn over chicken pieces, and add mushrooms. Pour in wine and sherry.  Cover skillet; simmer chicken 10 minutes, turning once, until no longer pink and juices run clear.
Thicken with slurry, add chicken broth and heavy cream.
