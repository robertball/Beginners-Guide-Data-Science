Title:
Salmon with Herb Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/9242.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 pounds fresh salmon fillets
1 1/4 teaspoons OLD BAY® Seasoning, divided
1 cup dry bread crumbs
1 tablespoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Parsley Flakes
1 tablespoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Basil Leaves
1/4 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Garlic Powder
1/4 cup unsalted butter
1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
1 tablespoon grated lime peel
Herb Sauce:
1/3 cup heavy cream
1/3 cup sour cream
2 teaspoons McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Chopped Chives
2 teaspoons McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Basil Leaves
1 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Parsley Flakes
1/4 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Coarse Grind Black Pepper
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Spray a baking sheet with nonstick cooking spray. Place salmon on the sheet and sprinkle with 1/4 teaspoon OLD BAY(R).
Melt butter in a large skillet. Combine bread crumbs, parsley, basil and garlic in a medium bowl. Add crumb mixture to skillet along with lime juice, lime peel, and remaining 1 teaspoon OLD BAY. Saute until crunchy; about 6-8 minutes.
Coat salmon fillets with bread crumb mixture. Bake in a preheated 400 degrees F oven 15 minutes, or until fish flakes with a fork.
Combine sauce ingredients and serve with salmon.
