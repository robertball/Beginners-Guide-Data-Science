Title:
Taco Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz. can refried beans
16 oz. sour cream
1 pkg. taco seasoning (my preference is the Taco Bell brand)
3 T. salsa
1 4.25 oz. can diced green chiles
16 oz. package finely shredded cheddar cheese
1 4.25 oz. can sliced black olives
1 tomato, seeded and diced
2 green onion tops, sliced thinly on the bias
10" disposable pizza pan

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together refried beans and salsa.
Using a spatula or big serving spoon, spread the bean mixture out evenly in the pizza pan.
Mix together sour cream, diced chiles, and taco seasoning.
Spread sour cream mixture evenly over the bean mixture.
Sprinkle shredded cheese evenly over the sour cream, then apply olives, then tomatoes, then green onion.
Serve with tortilla chips
