Title:
White Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2oz. White Almond Bark
1/4 cup White sugar
1 tsp. vanilla or peppermint extract
4 cups 2% milk
6 large marshmallows
green and red sugar sprinkles if desired.

Directions:
In medium pot on stove add white almond bark,
extract, sugar, 1/2 cup of milk and 2 large marshmallows
place on medium heat until boiling, stirring often
lower heat and add the last 3 1/2 cups of milk.
add remaining  marshmallows
heat to desired temperature.
Pour into four 6oz. mugs. One marshmallow for each mug.
sprinkle sugar sprinkles into each mug

Submitter: 
cocoa Queen
Description: 
A creamy smooth white chocolate drink that'll keep you nice a warm during those cold winter days
