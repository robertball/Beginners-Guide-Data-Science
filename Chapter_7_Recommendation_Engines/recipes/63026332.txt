Title:
Kim's Granola Breakfast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups oats
?1/3 cup brown sugar
?1/3 cup coconut
1/3 cup of wheat germ, wheat bran, and oat bran
1/3 cup of nuts
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. vanilla
1/3 cup oil
1/4 cup honey

Directions:
Cook oats in microwave, covered, for 2 mins.
Mix in sugar, coconut, germ and brans, nuts, cinnamon and vanilla.  Add oil and honey.
Cook for 5 mins, stirring every 2 mins.
Let cool then add sunflower seeds, chocolate chips, whatever you want.
