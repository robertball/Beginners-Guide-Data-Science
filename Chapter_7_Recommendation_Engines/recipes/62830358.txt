Title:
Jana's Oatmeal Raisin Cookies/bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup firmly packed Brown sugar
1/2 Cup Sugar
2 Eggs
1 Teaspoon Vanilla
1-1/2 Cups Flour
1 Teaspoon Soda
1 Teaspoon Cinnamon
1/2 Teaspoon Salt
3 Cups Quick cooking Oatmeal
1 Cup Raisins

Directions:
Beat toether butter and sugars until creamy
Add eggs and vanilla beat well
add flour, soda, cinnamon, salt and mix well
Stir in Oatmeal and raisins and mix well.
drop by rounded Tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet
Bake 10-12 mins or until golden
cool 1 min on cookies sheet before removing.  For making a bar cookie Bake 30-35 mins in ungreased 13x9 baking pan.  Makes about 4 dz.

Submitter: 
Nancy Edenfield
Description: 
These were given to me by my sister in law.  They are so moist and good.
