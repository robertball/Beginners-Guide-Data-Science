Title:
Super Easy Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6 oz. can tomatoe paste
6 oz. water
1 tsp. oregano leaves, crushed
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Combine ingredients into bowl and mix well.
I normally use 1 tbl. fresh oregano.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Miskiewicz
Description: 
Unlike most pizza sauce recipes, this one only has 5 ingredients which makrs it is easy and quick.  My family actually loves it.
