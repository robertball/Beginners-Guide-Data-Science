Title:
Italian Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1 lb+ ground beef
1 6oz can tomato paste
2 eggs (I use egg beaters)
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1/4 cup finely chopped green pepper
s/p to taste
Inner part
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1 and 1/2 cups cottage cheese
1 3oz (smallest can you can find) chopped mushrooms, drained (I also use stems and pieces)
1 T dried parsely
1/4 t dried oregano

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Mix the ground beef and top ingredients together
Pat half the mixture into bottom of 8x8x2 inch baking pan (I use my meat loaf pan)
Spread cottage cheese mixture evenly over meat in pan
Top with remaining meat mixture
Bake for one hour
Let meat loaf stand 10 minutes before serving
