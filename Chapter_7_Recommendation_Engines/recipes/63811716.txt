Title:
Easy Salisbury Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
1 (10.75 ounce) can cream of onion soup
1/2 pkg Lipton onion soup mix
1 4.5 ounce pkg roast beef gravy mix
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1/2 cup dry panko bread crumbs
1 egg
1/2 cup water
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 tablespoons ketchup
1/2 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon dry mustard powder

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together 1/3 cup cream of onion with ground beef,  1/4th pkg lipton soup mix, panko bread crumbs, egg, salt and black pepper. Shape into 6 oval patties.
In an electric skillet set temp to 225, brown both sides of patties. Pour off excess fat.
In a small saucepan prepare brown gravy mix according to package directions. In a small bowl combine ketchup, water, Worcestershire sauce remaining lipton mix, cream of onion soup and mustard powder. Add mixture to finished brown gravy and mix well. Pour over meat in skillet. Cover, and simmer for 40 minutes, stirring occasionally.
