Title:
Peanut Butter Hummus from JIF® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977291.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Crisco® Puritan Canola Oil with Omega-3 DHA or Crisco® 100% Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1/2 cup JIF® Omega-3 Creamy Peanut Butter
1 (16 ounce) can chickpeas, rinsed and drained
3 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon dark sesame oil
1 clove garlic
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
Pita chips

Directions:
Place canola or olive oil, peanut butter, chickpeas, lemon juice, sesame oil, garlic and salt in food processor. Blend until smooth. Stir in parsley.
Serve with additional olive oil and pita chips.

Submitter: 
Jif®
Description: 
Homemade hummus is ready to serve in 15 minutes for a delicious, classic dip.
