Title:
chopped salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
SALAD
6 c. lettuce/spinach, chopped
2 scallions thinly sliced
1 c. radishes, sliced
1 c. bok choy stems, diced (optional)
Handful of mint leaves, roughly chopped
Handful of basil leaves, roughly chopped
1/3 c. dry-roasted peanuts
PEANUT DRESSING
4 T. smooth peanut butter
2 T. boiling water
2 T. lime juice
1 T. peanut oil
1/4 tsp. red pepper flakes
1 tsp. grated ginger
2 tsp. soy sauce

Directions:
Toss salad ingredients together.
Stir all dressing ingredients till smooth, adding more water if necessary. Add 3-4 T. to salad, mixing well. (You will have leftover.)
