Title:
Peppermint Stick Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter
1 cup sugar
1 egg
2 tsp mint extract
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 3/4 cup AP flour
1/2 tsp salt
red food colouring
Green food colouring
1 1/2 cups white baking chips
crushed peppermint candies

Directions:
-In  a large bowl; cream butter & sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in egg and extracts. Combine flour and salt and gradually add to cream mixture. Mix well
-Set aside half of the dough. Add green food colouring to half of dough. Add Red colouring to other half of dough. Wrap dough separately in plastic wrap and place in fridge for 1-2 hours or until easy to handle.
- Preheat oven to 350 Degrees. Divide green and red dough into 48 portions each. Roll each into a 4 inch rope and press one of each colour together and twist.
-Bake 10-12 mins or until set.
-Melt choco chips when cookies are cooled. Dip end of cookie into choco chips and then dip into crushed candies.
