Title:
Best stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large bell peppers (red, green or orange)
1/2 pound ground turkey breast
1/2 cup brown rice.
1/2 onion chopped finely
2 cloves of garlic minced
1 TBS extra virgin olive oil
1 med. zucchini chopped finely
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
salt and pepper to taste
1 TBS fresh parsley
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese (or more to taste)
2 TBS tomato sauce
2 TBS bread crumbs

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 400 degrees.
Cook brown rice according to directions on package.  Set aside for later use
Cut tops off peppers and remove seeds and inside. So they look like cups.
Put peppers in a microwave safe dish, with a small amount of water in the bottom and cover with plastic wrap. Microwave for 4min to steam peppers  (varies depending on microwave)
In a large pan, saute onion and garlic in olive oil over medium heat until onion is translucent and garlic is fragrant.
Add turkey to oinion and garlic.  Then season with cayenne, salt and pepper.
When the turkey is just about finished add the chopped zucchini and cook until zucchini is soft. Remove from heat.
Add cooked brown rice to the meat mixture and stir in parmesan cheese and fresh parsley.
Stuff each pepper with 1/2 of the mixture.  Top each with 1TBS tomato sauce then 1TBS bread crumbs.
Put into oven for 15 min or until bread crumbs have browned slightly.

Submitter:

Description: 
The ingredients as listed stuffed 2 very large green peppers. I sometimes add other veggies to the stuffing as well (spinach, beans, jalepenos...).  Its very versatile so make it to your liking!
