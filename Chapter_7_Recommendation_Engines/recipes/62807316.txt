Title:
Royal Icing - Wilton Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/659467.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons Wilton Marinque Powder
1 lb sifted confectioners suggar
6 tablespoons warm water

Directions:
Beat all ingredients until icing forms peaks (7-10 minutes at low speed with a heavy-duty mixer, 10-12 minutes with a hand-held mixer)
Makes 3 cups.
*for stiffer icing, use 1 tablespoon less water.
