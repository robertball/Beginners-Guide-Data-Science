Title:
Easy Chicken & Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Cream of Mushroom (or Cream of Chicken) Soup
1 cup Water
3/4 cup Rice
1/4 tsp. Salt
1/4 tsp. Pepper
4 Chicken Breasts
*** Note:  4 Pork Chops can be substituted for Chicken Breasts ***

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375°
Mix soup, water, rice, salt, and pepper together in a casserole dish.  Top with chicken breasts. Cover with foil.
Bake at 375° 45 minutes to 1 hour.
