Title:
Chicken Enchilada Bake - Healthy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2	tbsp Margarine or butter
½	c. Chopped onion
1	 Garlic clove, minced
½	c. Sliced ripe olives
½	c. Dairy Sour cream
1	4 ounce Can diced green chilies, drained
1	10 ¾ ounce Can cream of chicken soup
1 ½	c. Cubed, cooked chicken or turkey
1	c. (4 ounces) shredded Cheddar Cheese
8 Corn or Flour Tortillas
¼	c. Milk

Directions:
1.	Heat oven to 350.
2.	In medium saucepan melt margarine; sauté onion and garlic in margarine until tender.
3.	Stir in ¼ c. ripe olives, green chilies, sour cream and soup; mix well.
4.	Reserve ¾ cup sauce; set aside.
5.	Fold in chicken or turkey and ½ c. of the cheese to remaining sauce.
6.	Warm tortillas as directed on package.
7.	Fill tortillas with chicken or turkey mixture; roll up.
8.	Place seam side down in ungreased 12 x 8 inch baking dish.
9.	Combine reserved ¾ c. sauce and milk. Spoon over tortillas.
10.	Bake @ 350 for 30-35 minutes or until bubbly.
11.	To serve, sprinkle with remaining cheese and olives.
