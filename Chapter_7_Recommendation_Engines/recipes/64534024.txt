Title:
Neapolitan Breakfast Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3080002.jpg
Ingredients:
1 frozen medium to large banana
1 cup frozen strawberries
1/2 cup vanilla yogurt (full fat or Greek)
1 scoop vanilla protein powder (about a 1/4 cup)
3 tablespoons Nestle's Quik Chocolate Powder
15 ounces of milk (or almond milk)

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth. Serve immediately or take with you in a to-go cup
*Experiment with optional ingredients: These are optional ingredients I have added to spice things up throughout the week: 1 tablespoon smooth peanut butter Or 1/4 avocado and 1/8 teaspoon nutmeg  Or 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon Or 1/4 teaspoon peppermint extract and 1 teaspoon cocoa powder

Submitter: 
rquill67
Description: 
You can't go wrong with vanilla, strawberries and chocolate. Fast and easy when you're on the go in the morning.
