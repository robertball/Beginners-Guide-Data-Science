Title:
Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/454493.jpg
Ingredients:
3 boneless chicken breasts
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of celery soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
salt, pepper and other other seasoning to taste
2 (7.5 ounce) small tubes refrigerated biscuit dough
1 (12 ounce) bag frozen mixed vegetables

Directions:
In a large pot over high heat, combine the chicken with enough water to cover, and boil for 15 to 20 minutes. Reserve remaining water in the pot. Remove chicken and allow it to cool, then pull it apart into bite size pieces; return to pot. Reduce heat to medium and add the cans of condensed soup and mixed vegetables. Season with salt and pepper. Pull the biscuit dough into pieces, and add to the soup. Simmer over medium heat for 10 minutes, or until the dough is cooked through.

Submitter: 
Leo
Description: 
Boneless skinless chicken breasts are cooked in water. A can of cream of celery soup, a can of cream of chicken soup, some mixed veggies and refrigerated biscuits for dumplings are added in this stew.Modified from: Chicken and Dumplings III
