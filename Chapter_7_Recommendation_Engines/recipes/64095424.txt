Title:
Big Ray's Rosemary Sweet Potato Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1208566.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Tbl olive oil
1 Tbl minced fresh rosemary
1 garlic clove, minced
1 tsp cornstarch
1/8 tsp pepper
3 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/4-inch julienned strips (about 2-1/4 pounds
3/4 tsp salt

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the first five ingredients. Add sweet potatoes; mix to coat.
Arrange in a single layer on two 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pans coated with cooking spray. Bake, uncovered, at 425Â° for 30-35 minutes or until tender and lightly browned, turning occasionally.
Sprinkle with salt and serve.
