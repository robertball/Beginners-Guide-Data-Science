Title:
Katt's Kasserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
500 grams Casserole Meat (diced)
1 Potato (Sliced)
2 Carrots (Sliced)
1 Onion (Sliced)
1 Stick Celery (Sliced)
1 Cup Water
5 Teaspoons Gravy Powder

Directions:
Coat Meat in Gravy Powder.
Place Meat in layers in Casserole Dish, alternating with the other ingredients, finishing with a layer of potato.
Pour water over the top.
Cover dish and cook in a moderate oven for 90 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kattjovi
Description: 
I found this to be brilliant when I was nursing my children.  I could make it while they napped, and still dish up a nutritious dinner.
