Title:
Manella's WHIPPED SCOTCH COOKIES GF version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2050936.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound butter (room temp)
1 cup icing sugar( also called confectioner's sugar)
1 and 1/4 cup corn starch
1/4 cup arrowroot or sweet rice flour
1 and 1/2 cups white rice flour
1 and 1/2 cups sorghum flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
Cherries, (the kind you get in a jar) optional

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 325.
Cream the butter, add all the rest and beat until fluffy. It does come together just takes a moment.
Drop from teaspoon on greased cookie sheet, or parchment paper.
Press a bit of red or green cherry on top.
Bake in  325 oven for 25 minutes,or until
lightly brown.If your using cherries drain them on a paper or tea towel so they are not so wet.
