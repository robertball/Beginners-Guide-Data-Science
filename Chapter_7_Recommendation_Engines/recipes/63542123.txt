Title:
Sweet and Tangy Chicken Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
8 Chicken thighs/breasts
1 package of dry soup onion mix
8 oz of Russian dressing
1 cup of apricot preserves. (jelly)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients, except chicken, in a bowl.
Place chicken pieces in a baking pan. Sprinkle with pepper.
Pour sauce over chicken and make sure itâ€™s coated well.
Roast uncovered at 400 degrees for 1 hour to 1 hour and 15 minutes until chicken is done. Baste several times during cooking. This is the way the chicken will look when done.
