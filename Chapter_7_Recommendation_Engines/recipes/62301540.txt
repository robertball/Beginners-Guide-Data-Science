Title:
Blackened fish canadian style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 8-10 oz fish fillets
2 cups melted butter divided
2 tbsp paprika
1 tbsp granulated garlic
1/2 tsp dried thyme
1/2 tsp oregano
1 tbsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp cayenne pepper

Directions:
Preheat grill.  Place 1 cup melted butter in a shallow bowl.  Dip fillets in butter, lay them on a platter and set aside.  In a bowl combine all seasonings, sprinkle on both sides of fillets and pat seasonings into fillet. Add wood chips and fillets to grill and cook 3-4 min per side until fish is flaky.  Top with 1 tbsp butter and serve.
