Title:
Italian Biscotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound butter, softened
4 cups white sugar
8 eggs
1 Tbsp baking powder
2 tsp vanilla
4 Tbsp anise seed
6 oz almonds, blanched and chopped
8 cups all-purpose flour
6 oz dried cranberries
1 cup pistachios, shelled

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F
Mix butter and sugar until crumbly and light.  Add eggs, baking powder, vanilla, anise, and almonds.
Stir in flour gradually until smooth.
Mix in cranberries and pistachios.  The dough should be slightly sticky.
Divide dough into 4 parts.  On a jelly roll pan, line with aluminum foil and shape each section into a loaf about 15 inches long and 4 inches wide.
Bake loaves for 45 minutes.  It the dough beings to spread while baking, reshape with a spatula.  Remove from oven, cool, and cut into 1 inch slices.
Place slices back onto baking sheet and bake for 10-15 minutes, until golden.
