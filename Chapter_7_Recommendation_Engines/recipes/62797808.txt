Title:
OLD COUNTRY BAKED BEANS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/723632.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg.navy beans or white beans
6-8 cups water
1 onion diced
1/2 lb.side pork,
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup ketchup
1/2 cup molasses
1 tsp.dry mustard
1 tsp.salt
dash cloves

Directions:
Cover beans with water and let soak overnight in a pot.In the morning bring to boil and simmer gently untill skin burst[1 1/2-2 hours.]Drain and save all liquid.
Combine molasses,ketchup,brown sugar salt,mustard and cloves together.Add 2 cups of saved liquid.
Arrange half of pork and onion on bottom of crock pot.Add beans and stir in molasses mixture.Add more of the saved liquid if necessary to cover beans.Place remaining side pork on top.
Cover and bake 5 hours.Stir every hour and add more liquid if needed.Keep beans moist while baking to keep them from drying out.
