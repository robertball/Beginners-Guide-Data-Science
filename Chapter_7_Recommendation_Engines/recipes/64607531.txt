Title:
Meat Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 kg lean hamburger
2 eggs
1 1/2 cup rolled oats
2 tsp garlic powder
2 tbsp parsley flakes
2-3 tsp onion flakes
2 tsp dry mustard
2 tsp worcestershire
salt and pepper
1/2 cup milk
Jelly balls
3/4 cup apple or grape jelly
3/4 cup chilli sauce
1/4 cup soya sauce
Sweet and Sour
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup vinegar
1 cup brown sugar
4 tsp corn starch
Polynesian
1 cup apricot jam
3 tsp apple cider vinegar
1/4 tsp paprika

Directions:
In a large bowl combine all ingredients and mix well
Roll into small balls (3/4-1 inch size)
Bake on a cookie sheet in oven for 15 minutes@ 400 degrees
Use whatever sauce you want - Jelly, Sweet and Sour or Polynesian
Jelly balls
Microwave ingredients for 2 1/2 minutes pour over meatballs and microwave again for 6 minutes
Sweet and Sour/Polynesian
Mix all together, heat and stir until thick, pour over meatballs
