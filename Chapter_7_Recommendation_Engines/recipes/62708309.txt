Title:
Spaghetti, Tuna and capers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
500 g good quality spaghetti
1 (185 G) can tuna in olive oil
1 (440G) can peeled roma tomatoes
1 small white onion diced
1 Tbsp butter
sea salt
crushed black peppercorns
capers
grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Drain oil from tuna
Add butter to a heavy based frypan
Add diced onion and saute until soft and clear
Cook spaghetti
Dice tomato and add to pan
stir over low heat
Add salt and peppercorns to taste
Add tuna and stir
Add capers
Serve over spaghetti on warm plates, with parmesan and garnished with basil leaves
