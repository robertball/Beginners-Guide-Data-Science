Title:
Lower Fat Chicken Fettuccini Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 skinless, boneless chicken breast - cut into cubes
2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
4 cloves garlic, minced, divided
1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
1 pound whole wheat fettuccini pasta
1 onion, diced
1 - 12 ounce package sliced mushrooms
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon salt
3/4 teaspoon ground white pepper
3 cups skim milk
1 cup Fat Free half-and-half
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
8 ounces shredded Reduced Fat Colby-Monterey Jack cheese
4 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
1/2 cup Reduced Fat sour cream

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium heat combine chicken, 1 tablespoon olive oil, garlic and Italian seasoning.  Cook until chicken is no longer pink inside.  Remove from skillet and set aside.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.  Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Meanwhile, add 1 tablespoon olive oil to the skillet.  Saute onion, 2 tablespoons garlic and mushrooms until onions are transparent.  Stir in flour, salt and pepper; cook 2 minutes.  Slowly add milk and half-and-half, stirring until smooth and creamy.  Stir in Parmesan and Colby-Monterey  Jack cheeses; stir until cheese is melted.  Stir in  chicken mixture, tomatoes and sour cream.  Serve over cooked fettuccini.
For the butter flavor, if desired, Add 1 tablespoon of butter to finished chicken mixture.
