Title:
Chicken Cobb Breakfast Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1042142.jpg
Ingredients:
• 12 large eggs
• 1/2 cup plain yogurt
• 1/2 cup milk or half and half
• 4 ounces grape tomatoes, halved
• 1 teaspoon dried parsley
• 8 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
• 1 cup chicken, cooked and chopped
• 2 avocados, diced
• 1 cup monterey jack cheese, shredded
• 2 green onions, sliced
• 1 teaspoon sea salt
• 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
• Butter or Cooking Spray for coating pan

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 350°F. Coat a Le Creuset Pan or 9×13-inch baking dish with butter or cooking spray.
In a large bowl whisk eggs. Then add in remaining ingredients and combine well
Bake until eggs are cooked through, puffy, and beginning to brown. About 25 minutes

Submitter: 
Cindy Anschutz Barbieri
Description: 
Try my baked chicken cobb breakfast bake for your next brunch using your Le Creuset Pan! 

I am sure everyone will just love it. I used montery jack cheese but you can certainly use blue cheese if you prefer. Either way … Enjoy!
