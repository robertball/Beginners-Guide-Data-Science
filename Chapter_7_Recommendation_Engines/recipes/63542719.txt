Title:
Cherry Crumble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
21 1/2 ounce Cherry Pie Filling
Yellow Cake Mix (18.25 ounces)
1 teaspoon Almond Extract
1 teaspoon Cinnamon
1/2 Cup Butter (melted)
1 Cup Chopped Walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Lightly spray a 12 x 7 1/2 inch baking dish.
Place pie filling, almond extract and cinnamon in baking dish and stir to incorporate; then spread evenly.
In a mixing bowl, place dry cake mix and chopped walnuts; mixing to coat.
Add melted butter to this mixture and mix with a fork until large crumbs form and the dry cake mix is all moistened.
Sprinkle crumb mixture evenly over the top of the prepared cherries.
Bake in preheated 350 degree F. oven for 30 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Randy
Description: 
Quick, easy and delicious is what this recipe is.  Mix it up and pop it in the oven just before you sit down to eat and when you are finished eating you have a hot dessert that is fantastic with a scoop of Vanilla Bean Ice Cream on it.
