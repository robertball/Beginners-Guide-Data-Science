Title:
Pesto Bow Tie Chicken Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 C Chicken, chopped
20 oz. cream of chicken soup
16 oz. bow tie pasta, cooked and drained
12 oz. marinated artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1 C. parmesan cheese, grated
7 oz pesto sauce
4 oz. diced pimentoes
3/4 C. oil packed sun dried tomatoes, chopped

Directions:
Combine the chicken, soup, pasta, artichoke hearts, cheese, pesto, pimentos and sun dried tomatoes in bowl.
Spoon into lightly greased 3 1/2 quart baking dish.
Sprinkle with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.
Bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes.

Genevieve Johns Seivert

Creamy and delicious and nice enough to serve to guests.
