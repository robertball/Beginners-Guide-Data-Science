Title:
Pineapple BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans pineapple chunks, drained and blended (makes 3 cups)
1 ½ TBSP ginger
1 TBSP garlic powder
½ teaspoon chili powder
3 TBSP apple cider vinegar
1½ cups ketchup
3 TBSP light brown sugar
1 TBSP molasses
1 TBSP teriyaki sauce
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
1.  In a medium saucepan, combine pineapple, ginger, garlic, and chili powder. Bring to a low boil, stirring occasionally. Allow to simmer for 5 minutes.
2.  Add vinegar, ketchup, molasses, brown sugar, and teriyaki sauce, stirring to combine. Reduce heat to low and allow to simmer for 20 minutes, stirring as needed. Allow to cool before using. Keep refrigerated.
