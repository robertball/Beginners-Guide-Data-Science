Title:
Chocolate Covered Cherries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 60 maraschino cherries
* 3 Tablespoons corn syrup
* 2 cups sifted confectioner's sugar
* 1 pound chocolate confectioner's coating

Directions:
1) Drain cherries and set on paper towels to dry.
2) In a medium bowl, combine butter and corn syrup until smooth. Stir in confectioner's sugar and knead to form a dough. Chill to stiffen if necessary, wrap each cherry in about 1 teaspoon of dough. Chill until firm.
3) Melt confectioners coating in a heavy saucepan over low heat. Dip each cherry in and place on waxed paper lined sheets. Chill until completely set. Store in an airtight container  in a cool place. Best after 1 or 2 weeks.
