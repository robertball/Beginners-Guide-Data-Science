Title:
Garden Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup vegt. oil
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
2 tsp chopped fresh parsley leaves
2 green onions, finely sliced (including white)
1 garlic clove minced
1 tsp of salt
1 tsp finely chopped fresh basil
1 tsp finely chopped fresh dill
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
4 medium tomatoes, core removed and 1/2 inch thick slices (variety of colors)

Directions:
In a medium bowl, use one that has a lid, combine all ingred. except for tomatoes and stir. Add tomatoes and secure lid. Toss gently and chill for 2 hours or over night. Just before serving stir gently.

Submitter: 
Cheryl's Cooking Now!
Description: 
A client of mine shared this recipe with me. This recipe comes from P. Allen Smith, 2010 Clarkson edition. The dressing has a wonderful flavor. It's even better as it sits. Best to make it the day before serving.
