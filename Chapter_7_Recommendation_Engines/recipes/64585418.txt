Title:
Cucumber Water Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
22 oz sparkling or filtered water
5 cucumber slices
2 thyme sprigs
4 tangerine slices

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a 32-oz mason jar or pitcher.
Stir gently, cover, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
Store in refrigerator up to 3 days.
