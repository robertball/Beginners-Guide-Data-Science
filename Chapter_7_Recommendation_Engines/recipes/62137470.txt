Title:
Best Ever Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup flour
1/2tsp baking powder
1/4tsp salt
1 stick unsalted butter (softenened)
2 eggs
3 packets of Nestle's Choco Bake Pre-Melted unsweetened chocolate
1/2tsp vanilla
1 cup sugar
Powdered sugar

Directions:
Cream the butter and sugar with mixer
Beat in eggs and chocolate packets
Sift together flour, salt, baking powder in separate bowl.
Beat in dry mixture gradually into wet mixture, about 1/4 cup at a time.
Stir in vanilla.
Spray 8x8 pan with cooking spray
Bake at 350 for 25 min or until toothpick comes out semi-wet.
Cool in pan for about 5min.
Cool on wire rack.
Sift powdered sugar on top.

Submitter:

Description: 
My cousin and I have been making these brownies since 1994. I have yet to find something better. Beware you will eat the whole pan.
