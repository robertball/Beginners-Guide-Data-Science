Title:
Ultimate Frozen Strawberry Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 fluid ounces rum
2 fluid ounces triple sec
1 1/2 cups fresh strawberries
2 tbsp strawberry syrup (breakfast type)
12 fluid ounces frozen lemonade concentrate

Directions:
Put on your favorite summer music, loud.  Put strawberries and rum in blender; blend until strawberries are pulp.  Add the rest of the stuff!   Blend for 30 seconds or until smooth. Throw in a few ice cubes.  Serve in margarita glasses with the rims dipped in powdered sugar.  Drink and have fun!!!
