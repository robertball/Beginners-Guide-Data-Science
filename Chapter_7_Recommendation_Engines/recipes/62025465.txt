Title:
No- Egg Egg Nog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gallon whole milk
2-3 packages French Vanilla instant pudding
1 1/2 C sugar
1 T vanilla extract (I prefer Mexican vanilla)
1 12-oz container Cool Whip
1 bottle rum extract
nutmeg to taste

Directions:
Mix milk, pudding mix and sugar well.  Add vanilla and rum extracts.  Fold in Cool Whip. Chill and sprinkle nutmeg on top when ready to serve.

Submitter: 
ILA8030
Description: 
This is a delicious egg nog without the eggs or alcohol.  You can even make it lower fat if you use lowfat milk and lowfat Cool Whip.
