Title:
Banana Cacao Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bananas (if you want it sweeter than use RIPE bananas)
1/4 cup of cacao powder
2 eggs
sprinkle of chia seeds (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F
Prepare a muffin tin with cupcake liners
Mash the bananas in a medium sized bowl.  Add the eggs to the mashed bananas and combine, then add the 1/4 cup of cacao powder and mix well.
Put about one ice cream scoop of mixture in each cupcake liner
Sprinkle a pinch of chia seeds on top of each muffin (optional)
Put in the oven and bake for about 11-13 minutes
Then take out and let cool on cooling rack for about 2 minutes
Enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
this recipe is super simple and super healthy but it tastes delicious and is great for breakfast and snacks on the go!
