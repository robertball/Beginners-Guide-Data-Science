Title:
Glazed Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium carrots
1/3 cup water
1 Tbsp butter
1/4 tsp beef bouillon
Dash black pepper
Parsley flakes (or fresh snipped)

Directions:
Cut carrots into strips.
In medium saucepan, mix water, butter, bouillon and pepper.  Heat to melt butter.  Add carrots and cook until carrots are tender (10-15 minutes).
Remove carrots.  Boil liquid until reduced to glaze consistency.  Add carrots and coat with glaze.  Transfer to a bowl and sprinkle with parsley.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
This savory glaze makes a great vegetable dish.
