Title:
Dinner Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup milk
1 egg
1/3 cup shortening
1 tsp salt
3 cups all-purpose flour
3 TBS. sugar
1-1/2 tsp. active dry yeast

Directions:
Place all ingredients in bread machine.  Program dough cycle setting; press start.  Grease 13x9 pan; set aside.
When cycle is complete, remove dough to lightly floured surface.  If necessary, knead in additional all-purpose flour to make dough easy to handle.  Divide into 18 equal pieces.  Shape each dough piece into smooth ball.  Place in prepared pan.  Cover with clean towel; let rise in warm, draft-free place for 45 minutes or until double in size.
Preheat oven to 375 bake 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.  Remove from pan and cool on wire rack.
