Title:
Thanksgiving in a Pan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. stuffing mix
2 1/2 cups cubed cooked turkey
2 cups frozen mixed veggies, thawed
1 1/2 cups turkey gravy

Directions:
Prepare stuffing mix according to directions on pkg.
Put stuffing into a greased 11" x 17" x 2" baking dish (or equivalent).
Top with turkey, veggies, and gravy.
Cover & bake @ 350 x 30 - 35 min. or till heated through.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A delicious way to use leftovers from a roast turkey (or chicken) dinner. All one pan :)
