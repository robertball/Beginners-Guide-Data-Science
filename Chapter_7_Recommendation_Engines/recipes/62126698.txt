Title:
Pasta salad with asparagus, cucumber, and celery Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound small shell pasta, or whatever small pasta you have.
1 handfull of asparagus spears, more or less, cut into pieces.
1 large cucumber, seeded and cut into bite sized pieces.
2-3 stalks celery, cut into bite sized pieces.
Your favorite vinegrette or Itailian dressing.
Salt and pepper to taste.

Directions:
Cook the pasta accoring to package directions.
In the last 3 or 4 minutes of cooking, toss in the asparagus.
Drain the pasta and asparagus.
Immediately chill pasta and asparagus with cold water.
Shake the pasta and asparagus dry and put into a bowl.
Add the cucumber and celery.
Add dressing to coat well.
Salt and pepper to taste.
