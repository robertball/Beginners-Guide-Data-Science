Title:
Bacon Wrapped Chicken with Trifecta Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
4 Slices of Bacon (each slich cut in half)
1 Can of Swanson Beef Broth
1/2 teaspoon of salt
1/2 teaspoon of Pepper (1/4 reserved for sauce)
1 teaspoon of Galic Powder
1 tablespoon of cornstarch
1 tablespoon of water

Directions:
1. Preheat Oven to 350 Degrees
2. Season Chicken Breasts with Salt, Pepper & Garlic Powder on Both Sides and Place Two Pices of Bacon on Top of each Chicken Breast to Cover.
2. Set Chicken breast in a Casserole Dish, Pour Beef Both Over Top and bake for 25 Minutes.  Then turn on the broil for a minute or so to crisp up the bacon a bit.
3. Remove Chicken From Casserole Dish and Set Aside.  Move Broth to Stove-Top Pan and bring to a Simmer.
4. Mix Together Cornstarch and Water and add to Broth While Using a Whisk.  Add Rest of Pepper and Whisk for another 2 Minutes.
5.  Pour Sauce over Chicken and Serve.

Submitter: 
Jess
Description: 
Chicken wrapped in bacon and cooked in beef broth creates a gravy that has the best of all three worlds.
