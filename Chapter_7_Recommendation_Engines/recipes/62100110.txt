Title:
Holiday Peanut Butter Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Crunchy Peanut Butter (prefer Jif)
1 cup Smooth Peanut Butter (prefer Jif)
2 1/2 cups powered sugar
1/2 cup softened butter
1 t vanilla
3 cups of Rice Krispies (crushed)
1 package of chocolate almond bark
1 12oz bag of semi sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Crush 3 cups of Rice Krispies (e.g.,using food processor)
Mix crunchy peanut butter, smooth peanut butter and butter together
Add 2 1/2 cups of powdered sugar - until combined
Add 1t of vanilla
Add Rice Krispies
Dough will be a little sticky.  Form into small balls (1 tsp) - can do by hand or use small cookie dough scoop.  Yields about 60 balls.
Freeze peanut butter balls overnight.  When ready to finish, melt almond bark and chocolate chips together (on stovetop in double boiler or in the microwave per package directions).
Dip frozen balls into warm chocolate until fully covered.  Place on cookie sheet. Store chocolate covered peanut butter balls in fridge.
