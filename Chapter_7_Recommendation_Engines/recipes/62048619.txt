Title:
Spoon cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Jiffy corn muffin mix
1 can creamed corn
1 can regular corn, drained
1 cup buttermilk
1/2 stick butter
optionals:  shredded cheddar cheese, chopped jalapenos, chopped red pepper)

Directions:
Heat oven to 350. In a large, deep frying pan or saucepan, melt the butter.  Add the remaining ingredients and stir well.  Place into a buttered casserole dish and bake for approx. 25 minutes.  This is not a traditional cornbread that you slice !  You will need a spoon to serve this dish while hot.  The next day you will be able to slice and serve.

Submitter: 
Christine R.
Description: 
A ridiculously easy and memorable stouthern-style cornbread that you spoon onto a plate fresh from the oven.
