Title:
The Rum Rickey Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/971326.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 fluid ounce lime juice
1 1/2 fluid ounces rum
1 cup ice
4 fluid ounces club soda
1 lime wedge

Directions:
Pour lime juice and rum into ice-filled highball glass and fill with club soda. Stir. Add a wedge of lime.

Submitter: 
Allrecipes
Description: 
This classic long drink is as refreshing as a summer day is long. 
The rum Rickey combines lime juice and rum with ice and club soda for a fizzy, refreshing cocktail for a hot summer day.
