Title:
Mom's Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/346617.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked macaroni
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
milk

Directions:
In casserole mix macaroni, cheese.
Pour in milk until it just covers the top of the macaroni mixture.
Bake at 350 F for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Kelli F
Description: 
This is macaroni and cheese the way my Mom makes it - and it is still my favorite.
