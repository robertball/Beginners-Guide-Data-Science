Title:
Creamy Home Made Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 oz. container Greek yogurt
10 packets splenda
1 egg white

Directions:
place egg whites in a medium bowl and beat until they form soft peaks. Once egg whites are whipped add the Greek yogurt, and the splenda. Stir until well combined. Chill.

Submitter: 
PURPLEMAGGOT
Description: 
This is a great low-calorie dip for fruit desserts.
