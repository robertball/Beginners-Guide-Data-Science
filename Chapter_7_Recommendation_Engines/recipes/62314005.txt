Title:
Rotisserie Style Slow Cooker Chicken Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 whole chicken (4-5 lbs), skinned (i’m wierd that way)
2t kosher or sea salt
1t paprika
1t onion powder
½t dried thyme
1t italian seasoning (or oregano, basil and garlic powder totalling 1t)
½t cayenne pepper  more if you like it hot
½ black pepper
optional to stuff 4 garlic cloves and 1 quartered onion into chicken cavity before cooking.

Rub spices all over chicken.
Either put onion in bottom of slow cooker or inside chicken with garlic.
