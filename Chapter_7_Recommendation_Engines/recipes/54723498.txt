Title:
Baked Teriyaki Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoon cornstarch
3 tablespoon cold water
1 1/2 cup brown sugar
1 1/2 cup soy sauce
3/4 cup cider vinegar( a little light on measurement)
3 clove garlic, minced
1 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
3/4 teaspoons ground black pepper
(If need be may add more cornstarch to sauce if not thick enough)
12 good sized chicken thighs

Directions:
In a small saucepan over low heat, combine the cornstarch, cold water, sugar, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, ginger and ground black pepper. Let simmer, stirring frequently, until sauce thickens and bubbles.Let cool and marinate overnight in zip lock bag
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Place chicken pieces skin side up in a foil lined  9x13 inch baking dish. Brush chicken with the sauce.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Turn pieces over,baste with sauce and bake for another 30 minutes, until no longer pink and juices run clear.
