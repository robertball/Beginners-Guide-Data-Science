Title:
Corn Bread Extraordinaire Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup cornmeal
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons honey
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 tsp zesty pepper medley (Bulk Barn)
1/4 cup cooked corn kernels
1 egg
1/2 cup milk
2 tablespoons butter, melted
1/2 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1 finely chopped green onion

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine cornmeal, flour, zesty pepper medley and baking powder. In another bowl, whisk the egg, milk, honey and butter. Stir into dry ingredients just until moistened. Gently fold in cheese, corn and onions.
Pour into a greased 8x8" baking pan. Bake at 400 degrees F for 11 minutes or until toothpick comes out almost clean. Cut into 9 peices. Can be served warm.
