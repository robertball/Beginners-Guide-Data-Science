Title:
Mock Sour Cream II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1963468.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup low-fat cottage cheese
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/4 cup skim milk

Directions:
Mix cottage cheese, lemon juice and milk in blender until smooth. Keeps in refrigerator for a week.

Submitter: 
Brenda Ward
Description: 
A substitute for sour cream that is low in cholesterol.
