Title:
Valhalla Light Breakfast Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz light crescent rolls
1 lb turkey sausage
1 cup frozen hash browns potatoes, thawed and drained
1 cup fat free cheddar cheese = shredded
lcup egg beaters
1/4 cup skim milk
1/8 tspn black pepper
salt to taste
2 TBS Grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
25pre heat oven to 375
9 x 13 pyrex plate
Pat crescent rolls into bottom of casserole dish
Top with sausage, hash brown and cheese
beat together egg, milk, salt and pepper
Gently pour over mixture in pyrex
sprinkle with Parmesan
Cook 20 - 25 minutes

LostinSouthBama
Description: 
I had this at an extraordinary bed and breakfast and held on to the recipe.  Now i'm creating the lighter version.  sits well in frige for a day at least, will confirm freezing.
