Title:
Harvest Three Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/894789.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans (16 oz) black beans, drained
2 cans (16 oz) kidney beans, drained
2 cans (16 oz) pinto beans, drained
1 bag (12 oz) frozen corn
2 lbs. lean ground beef
1 small yellow onion, chopped
1 small green pepper, chopped
1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
1 can (4 oz) diced green chiles, drained
3 tsp. chili powder
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp ground pepper
1/2 tsp cumin
pinch of cinnamon
1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
sour cream (garnish)

Directions:
In large pan, cook beef, onion and green pepper until meat is browned. Drain excess grease. Once meat is drained, add remaining ingredients, except sour cream. Bring to boil. Cover, reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes. Serve and garnish with sour cream.

Submitter: 
MariaTheSoaper
Description: 
A nice hearty chili with Kidney, Pinto & Black beans and corn. This recipe can be made in a slow cooker or on the stove top, depending on how much time you have. Its a family favorite that you can use almost any meat to make instead of just beef.
