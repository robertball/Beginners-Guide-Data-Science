Title:
Admiral's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds coarse ground beef
3 pounds cubed beef stew meat (small cubes)
1/3 cup oil
1 large red bell pepper, diced
1 large green bell pepper, diced
2 med onion, diced
2 cup mushrooms, diced
1 bulb minced garlic
4 dried chiles, seeded
125 ml diced pickled jalapeno peppers
5 tablespoons standard chili powder
1 tablespoon cocoa powder
2 tablespoons cumin
1 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 tablespoon oregano
1 tablespoon onion powder
1 tablespoon dried cilantro
1 tablespoon smoked paprika
2 cups dark beer
1 can (28 ounce size) chopped tomatoes
1 can (369 ml size) tomato paste
1 can, (28 ounce size) black beans, rinsed

Directions:
Saute meat till browned in olive oil, drain the meat, , set aside. Saute mushrooms, peppers onions and garlic for 10 minutes. Add spices, saute for 1 minute or until spices become fragrant. Add tomatoes, paste, beer and beans. Heat on medium-high heat until bubbling.
Add everything, including meat, to a large slow-cooker. Cook on low heat for 6-8 hours.
