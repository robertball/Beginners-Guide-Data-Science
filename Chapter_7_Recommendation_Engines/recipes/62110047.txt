Title:
Cactus Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup Nopales, rinsed and dried
4 Eggs
2 TBS El Pato Salsa de Chile Fresco
1/3 cup Cobby Jack Cheese, grated
2 Tortillas

Directions:
In large frying pan scramble Nopales, eggs, and Chile Fresco together. When eggs are set place in tortillas and top with cheese.

Submitter: 
Debbie Lopez
Description: 
Many years ago an elderly Mexican neighbor would have me over for breakfast and serve these burritos. As she spoke only broken English and I spoke no Spanish I learned to make these by watching her cook.
