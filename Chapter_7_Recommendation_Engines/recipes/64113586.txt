Title:
Great Grandma Marry's Pierogi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dough:
2 cups flour
1 egg
1 egg white
6 Tablespoons water
1 dash of salt
Filling:
1 pound farmers cheese
1 egg
1 egg yolk
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 Tablespoon chopped green onion
1 dash of pepper

Directions:
add flour on board form into a roll and cut slices. Roll into a size of a saucer. Place cheese in the center of the dough, wet the edges with water, seal all around and form into a dumpling shape. Slow boil water for five minutes then flip to the other side for five more minutes. DO NOT cover the pot. DO not let stick to the bottom of the pot.Fry on frying pan with oil until the sides are light brown.

Submitter: 
Danielle Kroll
Description: 
These are pirogi's made by my great grandmother. They take a lot of work and patience, but are worth it.
