Title:
Authentic Chicken Chalupas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
corn tortillas, shredded chicken, red tomato sauce, green tomatillo sauce, chopped onions

Directions:
lay out your corn tortillas on a table or some sort of surface. with a medium sized spoon, spoon out 6 chalupas with red sauce and six with green sauce , next add a small amount of shredded chicken about 2 pinches next add a small amount of onions to each chalupa. After everything has been added to the chalupas on a sauce pan put about 1/3 cup of oi  heat it up and put the chalupa in once the tortilla is browned and somewhat crispy take off the pan
