Title:
Delicious Apple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/455511.jpg
Ingredients:
10 Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and chopped
2 (8 ounce) cans pineapple chunks
2 cups raisins
1 cup chopped pecans
20 ounces sour cream
1 teaspoon granulated sugar

Directions:
Combine the apples, undrained pineapple, raisins, pecans and sour cream. Mix well and add sugar to taste. Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
Carmen Cuevas
Description: 
This salad is great after dinner.  It's become a classic in my house. Everyone enjoys it, and you'll love it too!
