Title:
Meat Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs hamburger, 1/2 cup club crackers or oatmeal ( I prefer club crackers) 1 onion minced, 1 teaspoon poultry seasoning, 1 teaspoon salt,pepper to taste, 1 beaten egg, 1 cup milk.  Sauce:  1 can cream of mushroom soup, 1 cup water, 1 Tablespoon white sweet whine.

Directions:
Combine all ingredients. Roll into ball and roll in bisquick. Brown in vegtable oil. remove from pan and deglaze with 1 cup of water, scraping all the bits off the bottom. Add one can cream of mushroom soup, sweet white whine ( I use  Chateau La Salle Blanc). Add meatballs back into pan with the sauce, cover and bake for 1 hour at 1/2 hours at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Bethany Bambenek
Description: 
This meat ball recipe is a family favorite passed down for generations. the meatballs are simmered in the oven in cream of mushroom soup and a little added personal touch of my own. Takes some time to make but well worth it.
