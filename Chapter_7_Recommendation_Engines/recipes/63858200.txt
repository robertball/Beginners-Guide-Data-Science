Title:
Tracy Ann's French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1070833.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pieces of bread
1 egg, beaten
1/4 cup Half & Half
1/4 cup milk
1 tsp. sugar
1 Tbs. butter
1 tsp. Virgin Coconut Oil

Directions:
1). Mix egg, Half & Half, milk and sugar in a medium bowl.
2).  Melt butter and Virgin Coconut Oil in a frying pan on medium-low heat.
3). Dip the bread in the egg mixture, covering both sides.
4). Fry on medium to medium-low heat until golden brown on both sides of bread.
5). Top French Toast with powdered sugar, a dab of butter and maple syrup.
6). Serve immediately. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Tracy Ann
Description: 
An easy to fix family favorite, and quick way to make your family members feel very special and loved. The Virgin Coconut Oil adds a little extra healthy sweetness. Enjoy!
