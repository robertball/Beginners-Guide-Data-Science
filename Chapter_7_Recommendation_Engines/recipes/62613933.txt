Title:
Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large sweet potatoes
1/2c brown sugar
1/4c sugar
1T vanilla
2 eggs
1/2 stick melted butter
1/2 can evaporated milk
1c brown sugar
3/4c flour
1-1/2c pecans, chopped
1 stick butter, softened

Directions:
Bake sweet potatoes and mash with all ingredients up to evaporated milk.
Grease 9x13 pan and bake for 30 minutes at 325.
Combine topping ingredients and top potatoes.
Bake another 10 minutes.
