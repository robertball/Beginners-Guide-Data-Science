Title:
My White Clam Sauce with Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large can (51oz.) Chopped Sea Clams Drained  (save theDrained liquid)
1 Large can (48oz.) Chicken Broth
1/2 Stick Butter
3/4 C. Olive Oil
1 1/4 Tsp Pepper (or to taste)
2 1/2 Tsp. Oregano
5 Tbsp. Basil
7 1/2 (Tbsp.) Cloves Crushed Garlic
2 Tbsp. Minced Onion
1 1/2 Tsp. Salt
7 1/2 Tbsp. Parsley
Parmesan Chese grated
Your choice of pasta 2#

Directions:
While your pasta is cooking, drain the Clams. Add the olive oil to a pot, heat and cook clams till they "pop". Add the butter. When butter melts add the drained clam sauce and chicken broth. Bring to a boil and add all spices and garlic. Boil for 20 minutes. Drain the pasta and "wet" with olive oil. Serve in a bowl and top with Parmesan cheese if desired.
