Title:
French Onion stewed chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 chicken leg quarters
1 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/2 cup olive oil, divided
6 yellow onions, thinly sliced
1 cup fruity red wine, such as Merlot
4 thyme sprigs
2 bay leaves
6 cups chicken broth
1/2 cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Sprinkle chicken
with salt and pepper. Brown half of chicken in 2
tablespoons oil in a large Dutch oven; set aside.
Brown remaining chicken in 2 tablespoons oil,
and set aside. Add remaining 1/4 cup oil and
onions to pan; cook 10 minutes or until onions
begin to soften. Stir in wine, thyme, and bay
leaves; bring to a boil, and cook until wine
evaporates. Return chicken to pan, and add
broth. Cover and cook at 325 degrees 1 hour or
until chicken is done; sprinkle with parsley.
