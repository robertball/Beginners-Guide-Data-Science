Title:
Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Cream of mushroom soup
1/2 C. milk
4 C. cooked cut green beans
2 C. French fried onions
Pepper

Directions:
Stir soup, milk, pepper, beans and 1 cup of onions in dish.
Bake at 350 for 25 min. or until hot.  Stir.
Top with remaining onions.  Bake for 5 min. more.
