Title:
Crab Stuffed Flounder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds flounder fillets
1 cup crabmeat - drained, flaked and cartilage removed
1 tablespoon finely chopped green bell pepper
1/2 teaspoon ground dry mustard
1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon salt
ground white pepper, to taste
3 crushed buttery rounds
1 egg white
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 egg yolk
5 tablespoons mayonnaise
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1 tablespoon dried parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Rinse the fillets and pat dry with paper towels.
Combine crab meat, green pepper, mustard powder, Worcestershire sauce, salt, white pepper and the crushed saltines. Beat egg white and Stir this into the crab meat mixture.
Brush the flounder fillets with olive oil. Place in a lightly greased, shallow baking dish. Spoon the crab mixture over the fillets and drizzle with any remaining olive oil.
Bake the fillets at 400 degrees for 15 minutes.
Non-Medifast Section
------------------------------
While the fish is baking, lightly beat the egg yolk in a small bowl.  Stir in 5 tablespoons of mayonnaise. Remove fish from oven and spread this mixture over the stuffing of Non-medifast meals; sprinkle with paprika and parsley, over all.
Increase oven temperature to 450 degrees and bake until golden and bubbly, about 6 minutes.
