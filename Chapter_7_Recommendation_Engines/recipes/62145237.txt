Title:
Sausage, Sage and granny smith apple browned butter sauce over cheese ravioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
11 tbs butter/ divided
3 granny smith apples
Lime juice
1 pkg sweet italian sausage
1 pkg frozen cheese ravioli
1 1/2 tsp dried sage

Directions:
Pull casings off sausage and brown in pan...set aside to drain in a colander.
chop apples in 1/4 sized squares and sprinkle with lime juice to prevent oxidation. Saute apples till soft and slightl;y caramelized in 3 tbs butter. set aside.
Put remaining butter in heavy skillet on med high till it gets foamiy and the milk solids start to brown DO NOT BURN It'll have a nutty scent.
add sage ( it will foam up....that's ok) generously salt and pepper to taste.
Add sausage and apples and heat.
Cook ravioli according to package directions and top with sauce
If desired top with fresh grated parmesan
