Title:
Hula's Double Layer Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3oz Cream Cheese (softened)
1 Tblspn Half n Half
1 Tblspn Sugar
1 1/2 cups thawed Cool Whip
Graham cracker Crust
1 cup cold Half n Half
2 packages (4oz size) Jello Vanilla Instant Pudding
1 can (16oz) Pumpkin
1 tsp Cinnamon
1/2 tsp Ginger
1/4 tsp Cloves

Directions:
Bottom layer.
Mix cream cheese, 1 tblspn Half n Half and sugar until smooth.
Gently stir in Whipped topping.
Pour 1 cup Half n Half into mixing bowl.  Add pudding mix.  Beat with whisk until well blended,  1-2 minutes.  Let stand 5 mins to thicken.
Mix in Pumpkin and spices.  Spread over cream cheese layer.
