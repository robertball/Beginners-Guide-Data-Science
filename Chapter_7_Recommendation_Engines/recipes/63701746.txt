Title:
Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of spaghetti
1 lb ground turkey
1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
1 teaspoon of italian seasoning
2 16-oz cans of tomato sauce

Directions:
Boil a pot of water.
Once, water is boiling, add noodles and cook according to package.
Brown ground turkey and remove any excess grease.
Pour tomato sauce into a separate pot.
Add meat, onion powder, garlic powder, and italian seasoning to sauce.
Wait for sauce to boil and simmer for 10 minutes.
Once sauce and noodles are complete, drain noodles.
Mix sauce and noodles together.
