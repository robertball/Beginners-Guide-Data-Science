Title:
Ed's Streusal Cheesecake II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4            Philadelphia cream cheese packages (8oz) each softened
Sugar
Flour
1 Tbsp   Vanilla extract
1 Cup     Sour Cream
4            Eggs large
2 Cups   Blueberries
0.5 tsp   Cinnamon
3/4        Cup Butter

Directions:
Heat to 325F
Mix 1.5 cups of flour 1/3 cups of sugar and 0.5 tsp of cinnamon in a medium bowel.  Cut in 3/4 cup of butter and mix until resembles course crumbs.
Set 1/2 cup of crumbs aside.
Press rest firmly in bottom of a 9 inch springform pan.
Bake crust for 25 minutes or tip a light brown edge.
Beat cream cheese with 1 cup of sugar and 3 tbsp flour and 1 tbsp vanilla extract with a mixer until just blended
Mix one cup of sour cream until just blended.
Mix 4 eggs one at time, pour over crust.
Crown with two cups of blueberries then sprinkle with crumb mixture.
Bake for 85 to 90 minutes or until centre is just set.  Run a spatula around edge of cake to loosen.  Place in fridge for at least 4 hours.
