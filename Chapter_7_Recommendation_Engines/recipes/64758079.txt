Title:
Easy Parmesan Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
5 cups chicken broth , divided
1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup dry white wine
3 tablespoons unsalted butter
2 teaspoons  Kosher salt
1 teaspoon  fresh ground black  pepper
1 cup frozen peas

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
Place the rice and 4 cups of chicken broth in a Dutch oven
Bake for 45 minutes, until most of the liquid is absorbed and the rice I’d al dente.
Remove from the oven
Add the remaining cup of chickrn stock, the Parmesan, wine, butter, salt, pepper, and peas.
Stir vigorously for 2-4 minutes until the rice is thick and creamy and the peas are heated through
Swerve hot.
NB:  i’ve substituted other vegetable for the peas.  Butternut squash is very good.
