Title:
Oven-roasted Corn Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups fresh corn
1 Tablespoon olive oil, divided
2 Tablespoons white balsamic vinegar
1 Tablespoon Country Dijon mustard
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 cup chopped and seeded tomato
1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
1/2 cup chopped green onion, whites and greens

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Combine corn and 1 teaspoon oil on a baking sheet coated with spray.  Bake for 20 minutes or until browned, stirring occasionally.
Combine remaining 2 teaspoons oil, vinegar, mustard, salt and pepper in a medium bowl.
Add corn mixture, stirring well.
Stir in tomato, bell pepper and onion.
