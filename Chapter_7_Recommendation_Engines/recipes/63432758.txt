Title:
Hamburger Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb 94% lean ground beef
1 large onion, diced
3 large celery stalks, diced
1 can roasted garlic diced tomatoes
2 cans water
3 beef bouillon cubes
1/2 tsp chopped garlic
1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
1/2 bag peeled mini carrots, sliced 1/4 in thick
3 medium peeled and diced white potatoes
1/2 - 1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 bay leaf
1/2 tsp dried basil, crushed
1/2 tsp dried marjoram, crushed
1/4 tsp dried thyme, crushed
1-2 T worcestershire

Directions:
Spray a large nonstick dutch-oven sized pan (that has a lid) with cooking spray. Brown beef well over med heat.  Add onions and celery and cook 3-5 min.
Add tomatoes, water, bouillon, garlic, vegetables, carrots, potatoes, salt, pepper, spices, and worcestershire. Stir well and taste for seasoning.  Add additional salt and pepper to taste.
Bring to a boil, cover, reduce heat and simmer 30 minutes or longer.  Serve with buttered rolls or cornbread.

Submitter: 
B L
Description: 
Ground beef with vegetables and spices cook up quickly to make a healthy, tasty, and delicious from scratch soup.  Family favorite.
