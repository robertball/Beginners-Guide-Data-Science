Title:
homemade Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 ripe avocados
1 small white onion, minced
1 15 oz an Ro-Tel tomatoes with green chili, drained
1/2 lemon, juiced
1 lime, juiced
1 clove garlic minced
1/4 c fresh cilantro, chopped
salt & pepper, to taste
1/2 Tsp chili powder
1/2 Tsp cumin

Directions:
Mash the avocados together with the remaining ingredients.  Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.
