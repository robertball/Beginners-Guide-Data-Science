Title:
Baked Spaghetti Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs (1 cup egg substitute)
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
1 lb. mozzarella cheese, grated
8 oz. (1/2 container) Classico â„¢ Four Cheese Alfredo
1 tsp. garlic powder/minced garlic
1 tsp. basil
1 tsp. oregano
2 tsp. Italian seasoning
16 oz. spaghetti, cooked and drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Mix eggs, milk, cheeses, Alfredo sauce, and spices in mixing bowl. Pour mixture onto cooked and drained spaghetti.
Put entire mixture into a greased 8x8 baking dish (or 9x13 for more servings). Make sure pasta mix is packed into the dish tightly.
Bake for 30-35 min. Let stand for 5 min. Top each serving with 1/4 cup spaghetti sauce, Alfredo sauce, marinara, white wine sauce, or mushroom sauce.
