Title:
Jeanette's Crunchy Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups oats
2/3 cup flour (white or wheat)
2/3 cup white sugar
2/3 cup brown sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 cup oil
2/3 cup water
cinnamon to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees F.
In large bowl, mix all ingredients thoroughly.
Spread mixture in two ungreased baking pans.
Bake for 1 1/2 hours, stirring once after about 45 minutes to break up the clumps.
Let cool, then store in tightly sealed container.

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
I got this recipe from my friend Jeanette, who I knew in Nabire, Indonesia.  We made it all the time there because cereal wasn't available in the stores.  I still make it all the time now because I think it's a lot better than store-bought granola!
