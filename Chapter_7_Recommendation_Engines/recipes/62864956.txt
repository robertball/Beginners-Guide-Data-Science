Title:
Split Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups dried split peas (small bag)
2 quarts chicken broth
1 1/2 pounds ham bone
2 onions, chopped
3 cloves garlic
3 stalks celery, chopped
3 carrots, chopped
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 T dried marjoram
1 T dried thyme
2 bay leafs

Directions:
Boil ham bone in chicken broth for 1 1/5 hours.   Remove bone; cut off meat, dice and return meat to broth. Add bay leaf and peas. Saute onion and garlic and sautee with spices. Add in celery and carrots. Saute for 2-3 minutes. Add to soup and cook slowly, uncovered for 45-60 minutes, or until vegetables are tender.

Submitter: 
Genevieve Johns Seivert
Description: 
This is a hearty pea soup in which dried split peas and a ham bone are cooked with carrots, potatoes, onions and a pinch of marjoram.Modified from: Split Pea Soup
