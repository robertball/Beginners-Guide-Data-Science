Title:
Maple-Cranberry Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup maple syrup
1/3 cup cranberry juice
1/4 cup butter
1 teaspoon lemon juice

Directions:
Combine syrup, cranberry juice, butter, and lemon juice in a small saucepan; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium and simmer until glaze reduces to about 1/3 cup, about 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Bodagato
Description: 
This is a delicious glaze we use each year for Thanksgiving. If you use the pan drippings for your gravy, the glaze gives it a nice flavor that complements the meat well.
