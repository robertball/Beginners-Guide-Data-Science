Title:
Quick and Healthy Strawberry Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup plain yogurt
2/3 cup powdered sugar
1 cup cool whip
2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
4 slices angel food cake

Directions:
Whisk together yogurt and powdered sugar.  Fold in cool whip.  Stir in strawberries.  Serve over sliced angel food cake.  Garnish with extra cool whip and straberries if desired.

Submitter: 
Sara Wahnsiedler Perry
Description: 
I was looking for a dessert using strawberries that took little effort and used ingredients I had around.  Some yogurt, cool whip, and a store bought angel food cake and this is what I got!
