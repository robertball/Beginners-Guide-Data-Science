Title:
Curried Sweet Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Three Sweet Potatoes
4-5 cloves of garlic
2 Tab olive oil (Divided)
1 onion diced
1 yellow or orange pepper  diced
1 jalapeno pepper  diced fine
4 stalks of celery  diced
4 carrots sliced
4 cups of veggie or chicken broth
1 cup Greek Yogurt
¼ cup chopped cilantro
1 tea sweet curry
1 tea ginger
1 tea cumin
½ tea cinnamon
½ tea turmeric
¼ tea nutmeg
1 tea kosher salt

Directions:
Cut potatoes in half and roast skin side down in olive oil along with garlic cloves until soft  400 oven for about 30 minutes.  Allow to cool to touch
Heat olive oil in a soup kettle – sauté onion and peppers until soft – about 2 minutes.  Add celery and carrots and cook another 5 minutes on medium heat.
Combine spices in a bowl – add to vegetable mix and stir together so vegetables are coated with spices.  Add broth and cook until carrots and celery are tender 30-45 minutes.
Scoop flesh of sweet potatoes out of the skins and add to soup.  Mash garlic and add to soup.  Use immersion blender to blend.  Allow flavors to meld together on low heat for ten minutes.  Turn heat off add Greek yogurt and blend again.  Stir the cilantro into the soup.  Serve immediately.
