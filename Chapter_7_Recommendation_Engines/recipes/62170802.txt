Title:
Grandmas Iron Skillet Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cornmeal
1/2 cup flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 cup oil
1 1/2 cups buttermilk
2 eggs

Directions:
Heat oven to 450 degrees. Mix all ingredients; beat vigorously for 30 seconds. Pour into greased cast iron skillet. Bake until golden brown, 20 - 25 minutes. Serve warm, (with butter of course!!).
