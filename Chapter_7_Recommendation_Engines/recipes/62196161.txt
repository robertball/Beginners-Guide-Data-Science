Title:
Mexican Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1/4 cup cream
salt and pepper to taste
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
1 small 4 oz can green chilis
1/4 cup fresh salsa
1 10 oz can black beans
2 cups fresh spinach, wilted on stovetop
1 tbs olive oil
1 cup Mexican blend shredded cheese
Jalapenos for topping
Sour cream or Greek yogurt on the side

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease quiche dish or 9x9 round pan with cooking spray.  Cook fresh spinach over medium heat on stovetop with 1 tbs olive oil until wilted but still bright green, 2-3 minutes.  Meanwhile, whisk eggs, cream, salt, pepper, and cayenne in small bowl.  Spread green chilis, salsa, black beans, and cooked spinach evenly on the bottom of the quiche dish.  Pour in egg mixture.  Top with cheese and jalapenos.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30-45 minutes, until egg is set and top is golden brown.  Serve with sour cream or Greek yogurt on the side (optional).

Submitter: 
Katie D
Description: 
A flavorful healthy alternative to traditional quiche with black beans, salsa, green chilis, cheese, spinach, and jalapenos.
