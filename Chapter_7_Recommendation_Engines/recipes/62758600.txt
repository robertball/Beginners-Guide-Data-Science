Title:
Quick Roasted Red Pepper Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/637652.jpg
Ingredients:
28 oz can crushed tomatoes
1 12 oz jars roasted red bell peppers
1 t dried basil
1 t dried oregano
1 t onion powder
1 t garlic powder
1/2 t ground black pepper

Directions:
Pour tomatoes and peppers (with juices) into pot.  Use immersion blender to puree.
Add spices and simmer 5-10 minutes.  Add salt to taste.
Serve over whole wheat rotini or cheese tortellini.

Submitter: 
The Bunny Chef
Description: 
This sauce is very easy, very yummy, very healthy and very red!  Leftovers taste even better.  It can be prepared from start to finish in the time it takes pasta to cook.  You can put the peppers in a regular blender before adding to the pot if you don't have an immersion blender.
