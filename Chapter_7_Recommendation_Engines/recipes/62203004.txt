Title:
Vanille Kipferl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/304867.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup butter (room temperature, no substitutes!)
3/4 cup sugar
3 egg yolks
2 tsp vanilla
2 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup ground almonds
1/2 tsp baking powder (no more!)
Icing sugar

Directions:
Mix butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla until well blended.
Mix in flour and ground almonds until thoroughly blended.
Form crescent shapes or rounds on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake at 325F for 10-12 minutes, do not brown.
Sprinkle tops with icing sugar when cool.

Submitter: 
Jenny Deveau
Description: 
Passed down home recipe for almond cookies, or Vanille Kipferl, always made for Christmas. Perfect for rounds or crescents, very light with rich buttery almond flavour.
