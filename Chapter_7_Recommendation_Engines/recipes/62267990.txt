Title:
Janets Mexican Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. cooked hamburger meat
Tortilla chips
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 small jar picante sauce
American cheese slices

Directions:
Mix cooked meat with soups.  Start with a layer of chips followed by the meat mixture and some of the picante sauce.  Continue with additional layers of meat, chips and picante sauce.  Top with cheese slices and finish with a layer of chips.

Submitter: 
memalorie
Description: 
Hamburger, cream of chicken soup, cream of mushroom soup, salsa, chips and cheese make a good quick supper
