Title:
Triple Chunky Fudgy Fudge Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of vegtable oil
2 tablespoons of water
2 normal sized eggs
1 package of Betty Crocker mix, it can be the normal mix or the extra mix.
1 package of chocolate chips
Hershey's chocolate syrup

Directions:
~Preheat oven to 350
Into a bowl, put the mix.
Measure out the water, eggs, and vegtable oil.
Mix the water, eggs, and vegtable oil in a seperate bowl for best results.
Then, add it to the brownie mix in the bigger bowl.
Add as many chcocolate chips as you would like.
Take the Hershey's chocolate syrup and keep mixing it into the brownie mix untill the mix has turned dark.
Lastly, put it into a 13x9 sized pan.
Cook for 25 minutes.
Let it cool for about an hour so cutting is easier.
