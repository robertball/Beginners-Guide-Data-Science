Title:
Mom's King Ranch Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb Ready Made Rotisserie Chicken
1 Green Bell Pepper finely chopped
3/4 Red Bell Pepper finely chopped
1/4 Cup Yellow Onion finely chopped
10 oz Campbell's Cream of Mushroom Soup
10 oz Campbell's Cream of Chicken Soup
10 oz Can Chicken Broth
10 oz Can Rotel with Green Chiles
24 oz of Velveeta, Shredded, Divided into 3
15 Corn Tortillas cut in half

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Debone chicken and pull into bite size pieces, save any broth, set aside.
Lightly sauté Onion, Green Bell Pepper & Red Bell Pepper until Onion is slightly transparent. Leaving some crunch. Drain any liquid.
In a large bowl, mix Cream of Mushroom, Cream of Chicken, Rotel, 5oz of Chicken Broth, Sautéed Onion and Bell Pepper. You should have a little over 6 cups of soup mix.
Lightly grease a deep 9x13'' cake pan.
Dip Tortilla halves in remaining 5oz of Chicken Broth along with any Broth you had left over from the Rotisserie Chicken.
Line bottom of pan with 10 of the tortilla halves, followed by 1/3 of the Chicken, 2 cups of Soup Mix, 8oz of Velveeta. Repeat Tortillas, Chicken, Soup & Velveeta twice more, ending with Velveeta.
Bake at 425 for 25-30 minutes.

Description: 
This is a recipe I learned from my Mom. It's so yummy and excellent comfort food.
