Title:
Somewhere Over the Rainbow Recipe

Ingredients:
1 pint rainbow sherbet
2 liters lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage

Directions:
Place the sherbet in a punch bowl. Pour in the carbonated beverage. Stir gently.
