Title:
Hootenany Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1 cup milk
5 eggs
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Blend together all ingredients, except butter.
Transfer mixture into a greased 9x13 baking dish. Pour melted butter over top. Do not mix.
Bake for 25 minutes and drizzle with maple syrup.
