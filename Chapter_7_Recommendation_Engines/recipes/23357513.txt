Title:
Quinoa Briyani Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup quinoa
1 T. vegetable oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 t. fresh ginger, minced
1 t. turmeric
2 sticks cinnamon
3 whole cloves
3 cups boiling water or vegetable broth
1 cup carrots, diced
1 cup green beans, cut into 1" pieces
1 cup potato, peeled and diced
1 cup each brocolli & cauliflower floretes
1/2 cup peas, fresh or frozen
1 T. margarine, butter or ghee
1 medium onion, sliced in rings
2 T. cashew pieces
1 cardamom pod or 1/8 t. ground
1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped

Directions:
Place quinoa in a fine mesh sieve and rinse throroughly. Shake out as much water as possible.
In a heavy 12" skillet, heat oil over medium heat.
Add quinoa and stir briefly.
Stir in garlic, ginger root, turmeric, cinnamon sticks & whole cloves.
Cook until spices are fragrant, about 2 minues.
Add water or broth.
Bring mixture to a boil.
Add potatoes, carrots and cauliflower.
Cover and reduce heat to low.
Simmer for ten minutes.
Add broccoli and green beans, cover and simmer another ten minutes.
Add green peas and simmer for five more minutes, until all liquid is absorbed and vegetables are tender.
Remove whole cinnamon sticks and whole cloves.
In a 10" skillet, heat butter over low heat.
Add onion rings and saute until golden.
Add cashew pieces and stir constantly until cashews are lightly toasted.
Transfer quinoa-vegetable mixture to serving platter.
Top with onion-cashew mixture.
Sprinkle with fresh cilantro.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Lynn Gunderson
Description: 
A delicious and healthy take on the traditional Indian Briyani.
