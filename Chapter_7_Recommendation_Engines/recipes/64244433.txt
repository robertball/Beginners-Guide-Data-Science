Title:
Sweet Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5(14.5-ounce) cans whole green beans
1/2 stick butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
10 slices of cooked bacon, crumbled
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Drain green beans, and pour into a 9 x 13-inch baking dish.  Melt butter, and stir in brown sugar.
Pour butter and sugar mixture over green beans.  Cover with foil, and bake for 30 minutes. Sprinkle with crumbled bacon.

Submitter: 
pepper
Description: 
Quick and easy.  Great alternative to traditional green bean casserole.
