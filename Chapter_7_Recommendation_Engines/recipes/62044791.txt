Title:
Cheesy Chili Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground hamburger or turkey
2 onions, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 cans Bush's Chili Magic (any variety)
2 cans diced tomatoes with green chilies(undrained)
1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce with chili seasoning
chili powder to taste (optional)
1 pound Velveeta cheese, cubed

Directions:
Brown hamburger or turkey along with the chopped onion and garlic until meat is no longer pink; drain. Add the cans of Chili Magic, diced tomatoes with green chili, tomato sauce and chili powder (if using).  Add cubed Velveeta and cook until heated through and cheese is melted.
