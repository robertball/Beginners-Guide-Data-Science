Title:
My Rosy Watermelon Milk Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2287679.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups chopped seedless watermelon, chilled
3/4 cup cold water
5 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk, chilled
1/2 teaspoon vanilla powder
1/2 teaspoon raspberry extract (optional)

Directions:
Blend watermelon, water, condensed milk, vanilla powder, and raspberry extract together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Reem
Description: 
A very simple and delicious watermelon milk shake. No sugar is added. You'll love it! For best results, chill the watermelon and condensed milk in the freezer before making this shake.
