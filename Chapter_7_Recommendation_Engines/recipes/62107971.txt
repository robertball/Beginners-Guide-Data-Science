Title:
Strawberry Shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Shortbread Crust/Topping:
2 cups whole wheat flour
1 cup butter or margarine
1/2 t. salt
1/2 cup Splenda or white sugar
Strawberry Filling:
4 - 6 cups fresh strawberries, cleaned & sliced
1 cup Splenda or white sugar
2 T. whole wheat or all purpose flour
1/4 cup half & half

Directions:
Preheat over to 375 degrees.
Prepare the shortbread crust/topping by combining dry ingredients with cold butter which has been cut into small pieces to make mixing easier.  Use two butter knives, a pastry blender, or food processor to mix will until mixture resembles a coarse meal.
Press 2/3 of the mixture into the bottom of an ungreased 9 x 13 baking dish or cake pan.
Prepare the filling by combining strawberries, sugar, cream and flour until well blended.  Pour over top of crust.
Sprinkle remaining crust/topping over top of strawberries.
Bake in preheated oven 30 - 40 minutes until toping is golden brown and strawberries are bubbly.
Serve warm with whipped topping or ice cream.

Submitter: 
* Jennifer *
Description: 
I was looking for a recipe for strawberry shortcake that was all-in-one, but couldn't find one, so I created this one. I use whole wheat flour and Splenda, but you can substitute all purpose flour and/or white sugar as well.
