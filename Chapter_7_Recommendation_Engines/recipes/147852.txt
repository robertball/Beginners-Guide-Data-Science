Title:
Our Best Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup sugar
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups All-Bran® Original or All-Bran® Bran Buds®
1 cup milk
1 egg
1/3 cup shortening

Directions:
Stir together flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Set aside.
In large mixing bowl, combine Kellogg's(R) All-Bran(R) cereal and milk. Let stand  about 2 minutes or until cereal softens. Add egg and shortening. Beat well. Add flour mixture, stirring only until combined. Portion evenly into twelve 2 1/2-inch muffin-pan cups coated with cooking spray.
Bake at 400 degrees F about 25 minutes or until lightly browned. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Kellogg's® All-Bran®
Description: 
These versatile muffins are great as is, or with molasses instead of sugar. Spread them with butter and cinnamon sugar, then broil, for a spicy warm treat.
