Title:
Corn Salsa Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- Regular Sized Can of Corn
1- 2 cup bag of shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup - Sour Cream
1 cup- Mayo
1 jar of salsa (any variety, prefer regular sized mild pace salsa)

Directions:
Mix together all ingredients into a medium sized bowl.  Refrigerate until serving.  Serve the dip with tortilla chips (usually takes two bags).
