Title:
beef and pasta florentine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb.(500g) Ground beef
1small onion,diced
1 can(540ml)Italian seasoned tomatoes
1 can(796ml)crushed tomatoes
1tsp each   dried oregano,basil,salt,garlic powder
3 cups(300g) shell pasta(or any pasta you have on hand)
1 box frozen chopped spinach,thawed and squeezed dry
1can(290ml)sliced mushrooms
1cup(250ml) cottage cheese
3 cups mozza/cheddar blend shredded cheese

Directions:
cook beef and onion over med-high heat until browned, drain if necessary.
Stir in italian tomatoes, crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, garlic powder and 2 cups(500ml)water. Bring to boil.
Add pasta reduce heat, cover and simmer for 25 min. or until pasta is done.
Stir in spinach, cottage cheese, and mushrooms.
Pour everything into a large casserole dish, and top with the cheese.
Bake at 350 for 15 min, then broil until cheese is bubbly and golden, about 5 min.
