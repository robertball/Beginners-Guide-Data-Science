Title:
Balsalmic Grilled Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-4oz Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breast
1/2 cup Olive Oil
4 tbsp balsamic vinegar
4 tsp dried onion flakes
3 tsp Italian seasoning
3 tsp ground mustard
2 tsp thyme
2 tsp salt
2 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well in a large resealable plastic bag.
Allow chicken to marinate for at least 30 minutes.
Grill chicken on both sides until juices run clear or at an internal temp of 180

Description: 
An easy clean chicken recipe that tastes like it takes much longer to cook that it does.
