Title:
Easy Barbecue Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978254.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) can DOLEÂ® Pineapple Chunks
1/3 cup barbeque sauce
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cubed
1 DOLE Red or Green Bell Pepper, cut into cubes
1 zucchini, sliced

Directions:
Drain pineapple; reserve 2 tablespoons juice.  Soak wooden skewers in water.
Stir together reserved juice and barbecue sauce in small bowl; set aside.
Thread pineapple chunks, chicken, bell pepper and zucchini onto skewers.
Grill or broil 10 to 15 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink, turning and brushing occasionally with barbecue sauce. Heat remaining barbecue sauce in saucepan or microwave until hot.  Serve barbecue sauce with kabobs.   Serve with coleslaw and green beans, if desired.

Submitter: 
Dole Food Company
Description: 
Barbecue doesn't get much easier than this. For extra color and flavor, add sliced mushrooms and red onion wedges to your skewers.
