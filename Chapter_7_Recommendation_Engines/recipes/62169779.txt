Title:
Pumpkin Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups cubed or torn french bread
1 1/2 cups whole milk
1 egg
1/2 cup sugar
2 egg yolks
3/4 cup pumpkin
2 tsp. Pumpkin pie spice
1/2 cup melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Toss melted butter with cubed bread and lay in 8x8 baking dish.
Whisk together cream, pumpkin, sugar, PP spice, egg and egg yolks until well mixed.
Pour over bread and let soak in for 5-10 minutes.
Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
