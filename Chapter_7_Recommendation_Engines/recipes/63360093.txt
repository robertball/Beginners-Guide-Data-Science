Title:
Sam's Tunnel of Fudge Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
2 1/4 C flour
3/4 C Cocoa
1 3/4 C sugar
1 3/4 C softened butter
6 eggs
2 C powdered sugar
2 C walnuts
Glazes:
3/4 C powdered sugar
1/4 C cocoa
2-3 Tbls milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Grease 12 C bundt pan with butter and dust with flour.  Combine flour and cocoa and set aside.  Cream butter and sugar until fluffy, add eggs gradually.  Add powdered sugar and mix thoroughly.  Stir in flour/cocoa mixture by hand until blended.  Gently stir in nuts.  Stir into prepared pan.
Bake for 45 to 50 minutes (until top is set and edge pulls away from the side of the pan. Cool on a wire rack for 1 1/2 hours to allow fudge to set.  Turn on to serving plate and cool completely.
Prepare the glaze, combining powdered sugar, cocoa and 2 tbls milk.  mix thoroughly.  Add enough milk to create a smooth pourable glaze.  Pour over cake allowing to run down the sides.
