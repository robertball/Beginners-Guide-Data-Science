Title:
Chicken Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb chicken, cut up and no skin
8 cups water
2 Tbsp oil
2 medium stalks celery, chopped
2 large carrots, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
3 Tbsp flour
3 chicken bouillon cubes
3/4 cup uncooked rice
1 Tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp salt (optional)
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp thyme
1 bunch spinach leaves

Directions:
Boil chicken in water until done.  Remove chicken, cool and remove meat from bones.  Skim off fat from broth and set aside.
Saute celery, carrots and onion in oil until tender.  Stir in flour until blended.  Cook 1 minute, stirring constantly.
Gradually add 1 cup reserved broth to vegetables.  Add remaining broth, bouillon cubes, rice, lemon juice, salt, pepper and thyme.  Heat to boiling.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer 20 minutes or until rice is tender.
Add chicken and spinach and heat through.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
The spinach adds a nice twist to this chicken soup.
