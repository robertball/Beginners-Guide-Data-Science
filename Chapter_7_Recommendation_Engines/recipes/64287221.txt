Title:
Spicy Cocoa Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2049589.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 - 2 lbs. ground beef
1/2 onion, chopped
1/2 green bell pepper, chopped
4 cloves garlic (8tsp. minced)
2 Tbsp chili powder
2 Tbsp cocoa chili powder
2 tsp salt
2 tsp italian seasoning
1 tsp cumin (to taste)
1/2 tsp paprika (to taste)
2 cans Rotel
1 can kidney beans
1 can red bean
1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
dash red pepper flakes
dash red pepper Season All

Directions:
Brown beef, add onion and bell pepper until soft.
Add to crockpot
Add the rest of the ingredients to crockpot and mix well.
Cook LOW 8-10 hours or HI 3-5 hours (cook LOW for best results
