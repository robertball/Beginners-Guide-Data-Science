Title:
Chana Masala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/841134.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
1 chopped medium onion
1 minced clove of garlic
1/4 teaspoon of red (cayenne) pepper
1 1/2 teaspoons of ground ginger
1 tablespoon of ground coriander
2 teaspoons of paprika
1 teaspoon of turmeric
1/8 teaspoon of cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
1 large bay leaf
1 15 ounce can of diced tomatoes with juice
3/4 cup of water
2 15 ounce cans of chickpeas (rinsed)
1 16 ounce can of coconut milk

Directions:
Heat oil in a Dutch oven (6 qrts). Saute onion until translucent. Add garlic and saute about 5 minutes more. Add red pepper, ginger, coriander, paprika, turmeric, cinnamon, pepper, and 1/4 cup of water. Saute for another few minutes. Add tomatoes, chickpeas, the remaining water and the bay leaf. Simmer uncovered on low for 10 minutes. Stir in coconut milk and simmer a few minutes more. Add more water if too thick. Add salt to taste. Serve with jasmine rice or couscous.

Submitter: 
Alison Bruce
Description: 
Delicious blend of Indian spices with Chickpeas and Tomatoes.
