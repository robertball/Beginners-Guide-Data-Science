Title:
Best and Easyest  Key Lime Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 inch) prepared graham cracker pie crust
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup key lime juice
1 (8 ounce) package frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together condensed milk and lime juice. Let mixture stand for 15 minutes to thicken slightly. Fold in whipped topping and mix until smooth. Pour mixture into graham cracker crust. Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
Carol A
Description: 
Very easy, very good, light Key lime taste that still makes you pucker. Took me a while to perfect this one. No baking, no fuss - takes me 10 or 15 minutes to make.Modified from: World's Best Key Lime Pie
