Title:
Apricot Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, halved
2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
1 cup apricot preserves
1 (2 oz.) package dry onion soup mix
1 cup crispy fried onions
1/4 cup ketchup
1/4 cup granulated sugar
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup canola or flavorless oil
1 tsp kosher salt
1/2 tsp fresh cracked black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375F
Grease a 9X13 inch casserole dish with cooking spray
Whisk ketchup, sugar, vinegar, paprika, garlic powder, Worcestershire, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper in a bowl.  Slowly add in canola oil, whisking constantly to combine.
Whisk in dry onion soup mix.
Add apricot preserves to dressing and whisk again to combine.
Place chicken into the casserole dish and cover with apricot mixture.
Bake for 40 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
Remove from oven and garnish with crispy fried onions to serve.
Recipe adapted from Favorite Family Recipes and 12 Tomatoes.
