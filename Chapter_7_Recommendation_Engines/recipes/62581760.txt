Title:
Lemon Garlic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c butter
6 chicken breasts
4 garlic cloves
1/2 c chicken broth
1/2 c white wine
3 tbsp lemon juice
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper

Directions:
Melt butter in a large skillet over med heat. Add chicken & garlic & cook 3-4 min per side. Add broth & next 4 ingredients & bring to a boil. Cover & reduce heat to a simmer for 20 min. Serve over pasta or rice.
