Title:
Easy, Slow cooker Beef Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 lbs. beef stew meat or chuck roast, cut into several big chunks (any tougher cut of beef you have, will work)
1 can coconut milk or 2 cups homemade coconut milk
3 tablespoons red Thai curry paste

Directions:
Add beef, coconut milk and curry paste to the slow cooker. Use a fork to mix in the curry paste. Cook on low for 6-8 hours. Use 2 forks to pull apart the beef into smaller chunks. Use a slotted spoon to remove the beef from the liquid. Serve and enjoy!
