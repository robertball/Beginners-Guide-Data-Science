Title:
Stephanie's Corn Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8.5 oz) boxes Jiffy Corn Muffin Mix
2 (150z) cans whole kernel corn with liquid
2 (15oz) cans creamed corn
1 pint (2 cups) sour cream
1 cup (2 sticks) salted butter, melted
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*.  In a large bowl mix all ingredients until combined.  Pur into a 13 x 9 baking dish. (We use a 2 1/5inch deep half chafer foil pan for Turkey Circus.)  Bake for 45 - 50 minutes in preheated oven until light brown.

Submitter: 
Woodbine United Methodist
Description: 
easy recipe made with Jiffy Corn Muffin Mix.  This is the one we use for the Turkey Circus and have many requests.
