Title:
Chicken Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 tablespoon water
1 1/2 tablespoon butter
1 1/2 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 onions (chopped)
3 cups cooked white rice, cold
3 tablespoons soy sauce
2 teaspoons ground black pepper
2 1/2 cups cooked chicken (chopped)

Directions:
In a small bowl, beat eggs with water. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium low heat. Add eggs and leave flat for 1 to 2 minutes. Remove from skillet and cut into shreds.
Heat oil in same skillet; add onion and saute until soft. Then add rice, soy sauce, pepper and chicken. Stir fry together for about 5 minutes, then stir in eggs. Serve hot.
