Title:
Budget Italian Spaghetti Sauce with Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/275216.jpg
Ingredients:
MEATBALLS
1 pound lean ground beef
3/4 cup Italian bread crumbs bread crumbs
2 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1 egg, beaten
SAUCE
3/4 cup chopped onion
5 cloves garlic, minced
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 (24 ounce) jar of tomato pasta sauce

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine ground beef, bread crumbs, parsley, Parmesan, garlic powder and beaten egg.  Mix well and form into 12 balls.  Store, covered, in refrigerator until needed.
In a large saucepan over medium heat, saute onion and garlic in olive oil until onion is translucent.  Stir in pasta sauce, bring to a simmer, add meatballs and continue to simmer over low heat  for 30 minutes    Serve over pasta.
