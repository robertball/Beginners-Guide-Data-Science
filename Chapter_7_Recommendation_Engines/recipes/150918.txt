Title:
Simple Sangrita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976109.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Don Julio® Reposado tequila
2 cups tomato juice
2 ounces orange juice
2 ounces grapefruit juice
2 ounces lemon juice
2 ounces lime juice
6 dashes hot sauce
1 wedge lime

Directions:
Pour tomato juice, orange juice, grapefruit juice, lemon juice, lime juice, and hot sauce into a shaker with ice; shake. Pour a portion into a Tequila shot glass.
Add Don Julio(R) Reposado Tequila to another Tequila shot glass; garnish with lime wedge to serve.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
In Mexico, tequila is drunk neat, with a small glass of Sangrita on the side. Nothing pairs better with homemade Sangrita than Don Julio® Reposado tequila.
