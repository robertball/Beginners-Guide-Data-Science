Title:
Cheesy Chicken Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground or turkey chicken
1-2 eggs, lightly beaten
1/4 cup roasted garlic light cream cheese
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 tablespoon dry bread crumbs, a little more if using 2 eggs
1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
1 tablespoon garlic powder (less)
1 1/2 tablespoons vegetable or olive oil
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Line a rimmed baking sheet with aluminum foil, and spray with cooking spray.
Combine the chicken, eggs, cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, bread crumbs, red pepper flakes, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, vegetable oil, salt, and pepper in a large bowl; mix well. Form mixture into 20 meatballs; place on prepared pan.
Bake in center of preheated oven until juices run clear, 17 to 18 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).

Submitter:

Description: 
Garlic cream cheese adds great flavor to these zesty chicken meatballs, while ensuring that they'll be light, moist, and delicious.

Variations:  Add finely chopped spinach; substitute feta for parmesan; sun-dried tomatoes finely chopped; pine nuts, toasted.

Use cookie scoop to measure meatballs, or form with hands that have been lightly rubbed with cooking oil or sprayed with PAMModified from: Cheesy Chicken Meatballs
