Title:
Stuffed Cornish Hens Burgundy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Rock Cornish hens (about 1 1/2 pounds each)
Salt and pepper to taste
4 Tbsp butter
1/2 cup slivered almonds
6 Tbsp sliced green onions
2/3 cup long grain rice
2 cups water
2 chicken bouillion cubes
2 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp salt
6 Tbsp snipped parsley
Burgundy Glaze
1/2 cup Burgundy
1/2 cup currant jelly
2 Tbsp butter
1 Tbsp lemon juice
2 tsp cornstarch
2 tsp Worchestershire sauce
1/2 tsp ground allspice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Lightly season hens with salt and pepper.
In a small saucepan over low heat, heat butter almonds, green onions and rice.  Cook 10 minutes stirring constantly.
Add water, bouillion, lemon juice and salt, bring to boil.
Reduce heat and cover. Cook slowly until liquid is absorbed
Toss rice mixture with parsley and stuff lightly into hens.
Truss hens, place breast side up in shallow pan.
Cover hens loosely by tenting them with aluminum foil, roast for 30 minutes.
Uncover hens, brush with Burgundy glaze. Continue roasting uncovered for 1 hour, brushing frequently with glaze.
Drumsticks will move easily in sockets when done.  Remove to hot platter and serve with remaining glaze.
Burgundy Glaze:
Add all glaze ingredients to small saucepan over medium heat.
Stir constantly until mix thickens, remove from heat.
