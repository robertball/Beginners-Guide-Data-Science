Title:
Bacon and Potato Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986340.jpg
Ingredients:
6 slices bacon, cut into small pieces
1 cup chopped carrots
1 (20 ounce) package Simply Potatoes® Diced Potatoes with Onion
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
1/2 cup water
1 cup milk

Directions:
Cook bacon in 3-quart saucepan over medium heat until crisp; drain grease. Add carrots. Continue cooking 2 minutes or until crisp tender.  Stir in Simply Potatoes(R) and flour.
Slowly stir in broth and water. Continue cooking, stirring constantly, until mixture comes to a boil and starts to thicken.  Reduce heat to medium-low, cook 15 to 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, or until potatoes are tender. Stir in milk. Continue cooking 2 to 3 minutes or until thoroughly heated (do not boil). Season with salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Simply Potatoes®
Description: 
A steaming cup of rich, hearty chowder warms the soul on a cold, rainy day.  Ready in 30 minutes.
