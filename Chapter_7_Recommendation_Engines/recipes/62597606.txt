Title:
S%^t on a Shingle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb velveeta
1lb pork sausage
1t worcestershire
1/4c ketchup
2t dried minced onion
garlic salt
2t oregano
1 loaf party rye

Directions:
Brown and drain sausage.
Melt velveeta into sausage.
Stir at low heat.
Add other ingredients and spread on party rye.
Bake on a sheet at 450 for 4-10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Christine Simon Michener
Description: 
AKA Hanky Pankys, SOS, etc.  May be frozen before baking.
