Title:
White Chocolate Raspberry Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg.(1.5 oz.) instant white chocolate fat-free, sugar-free pudding mix
2 cups skim milk
1 cup fat free Cool Whip
2 cups raspberries

Directions:
Combine pudding mix and milk according to package directions.
Once pudding is set, gently fold in Cool Whip, then raspberries.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Andrea Patton
Description: 
A quick, easy, refreshing and light dessert!
