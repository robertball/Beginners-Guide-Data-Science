Title:
Curry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 Kgs Chicken
1/3 cup butter
1/3 cup honey
1/4 cup dijon mustard
4 tsp curry
1/8 tsp cayenne
2 White onions chopped coarsely
12 carrots quartered
6 Parsnips quartered
1 cup frozen peas - optional

Directions:
Combine butter, honey, mustard, curry and cayenne over medium heat, once mixed well pour over chicken in a medium size roaster.
Add onions, carrots and parsnips
Bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes, durning the last 15 minutes add peas if desired
Serve with rice, I usually double the sauce ingredients to have some for the rice
