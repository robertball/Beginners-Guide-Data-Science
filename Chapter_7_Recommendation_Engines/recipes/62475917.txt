Title:
Nonnie's Italian Wedding Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cookie:
4 c. flour
1/2 c. butter
6 eggs
1 tsp. anise extract
1 c. sugar
1/8 tsp. salt.
Icing:
1 c. powdered sugar
2 t. milk
Round Candy Sprinkles

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl cream butter and sugar.  In a separate bowl mix dry ingredients.
Alternately add a portion of the dry ingredients and 2 of the eggs until both are gone. Mix well after each addition.
Form into 1" balls or shapes.  Usually roll a 1" ball into a snake and twist to form a "love knot."  can braid, pinwheel, etc.  Use your imagination, but only 1 to 1 1/2" ball of dough at time.
Bake on an ungreased cookie sheet in a 350 degree oven for 10 min.  Cool on cooling rack.
Mix together the pwd. sugar and milk for desired consitancy of pouring/drizzling.  Drizzle cookies with icing and immediately sprinkle with candy sprinkles.

Submitter: 
J. Lynn
Description: 
Handed down through generations, these delightful shaped cookies look as wonderful as they taste.  Not too sweet, perfect with coffee.  Great activity cookie for kids to help make!
