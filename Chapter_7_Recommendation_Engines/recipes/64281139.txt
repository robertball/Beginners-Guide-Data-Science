Title:
Gabe's Buffalo Ranch Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1903728.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups crispy rice cereal squares (such as Rice Chex速)
3 cups crispy corn cereal squares (such as Corn Chex速)
3 cups hot and spicy flavored cheese crackers (such as Hot ans Spicy Cheez-Its速)
2 cups buffalo flavored pretzel pieces
6 tablespoon butter, no substitutes, melted
3 tablespoons hot sauce (such as Franks速 Red Hot)
1 packet dry ranch dressing mix
3/4 teaspoon celery seed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees. Line a large cookie sheet with foil and spray with non stick cooking spray; set aside.
In large bowl, combine cereals, crackers and pretzels, set aside.
In a small bowl, mix melted butter, hot sauce, ranch dressing mix and celery seed, until smooth and well combined.
Pour over cereal mixture and stir until evenly coated.
Pour onto baking sheet.
Bake 25-30 minutes, stirring every 10 minutes, until crisp.
Remove pan from oven and allow to cool completely.
Store in an airtight container.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
My son, Gabe, wanted to try something different than the usual snack mix. He LOVES anything buffalo, so this was his creation. This is not super spicy, but a really nice balance of flavors, enjoy!
