Title:
Easy Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef -OR-
1 lb ground turkey / chicken -OR-
1 lb steak, cut into thin strips
2 tbsp garlic powder -OR-
3 cloves garlic, minced
pepper to taste
1 small can mushrooms
2 cups water or chicken broth
2/3 cup ketchup
1 cup sour cream
1 tbsp corn starch
1/2 cup cold water

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown meat, garlic and mushrooms.  Add pepper to taste as it's frying.  Once finished, drain grease from pan and add 2 cups water or broth and ketchup.  Blend to mix completely and set to medium-low.
Meanwhile, in a large pot, boil enough water to cook egg noodles in.  Follow cooking directions for al dente noodles.
In the skillet, add the sour cream and blend until smooth.  This could take a few minutes, depending on how cold the sour cream is.  Let simmer for about 4-7 minutes.
In a seperate small bowl, mix corn starch and 1/2 cup cold water.  Turn up heat to boiling under skillet, and add corn starch mixture.  Stir for about 1 minute, then turn heat down to simmer, and let sit.
Drain noodles and add to skillet.  Mix completely.  Add salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
MERRIPAN
Description: 
This is a quickie recipe that tastes like you've worked all day on it.  It's great with either ground beef, turkey or steak, and can be made with chunks of vegetables to make it vegetarian.
