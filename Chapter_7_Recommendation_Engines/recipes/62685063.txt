Title:
Bulk Cuban Ropa Vieja for Freezer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon vegetable oil
4 pounds beef flank steak
1 cup water
2 teaspoons beef bouillon
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 small onions, sliced
2 green bell pepper, seeded and sliced into strips
8 cloves garlic, chopped
2 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
2 teaspoon ground cumin
2 teaspoon chopped fresh cilantro
2 tablespoon olive oil
2 tablespoon white vinegar

Directions:
Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Brown the flank steak on each side, about 4 minutes per side.
Transfer beef to a slow cooker. Pour in the water, bouillon, and tomato sauce, then add the onion, bell pepper, garlic, tomato paste, cumin, cilantro, olive oil and vinegar. Stir until well blended. Cover, and cook on High for 4 hours, or on Low for up to 10 hours. When ready to serve, shred meat and serve with tortillas or rice.

Description: 
A large crockpot full of yummy beef, with lots of extra for freezing. This is great shredded beef served on tortillas or over rice. Add sour cream, cheese, and fresh cilantro on the side.Modified from: Cuban Ropa Vieja
