Title:
Melissa's Turkey Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey breast
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 box lasagna noodles
1 jar Barilla Marinara spaghetti sauce (28 oz)
4 cups (2 lbs) ricotta cheese
2 cups mozzarella cheese (shredded)
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 eggs
1 Tbsp fresh parsley (chopped)
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground black pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, 1 & 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, parsley, salt, and pepper. Set aside.
Brown turkey meat in oil over medium heat. Stir in sauce and simmer.
Preheat oven to 350*
Cook pasta according to package, drain.
Lay noodles flat on wax paper to cook.
Spread around 1/3 cup of meat sauce on bottom of 13 x 9 x 2 inch pan. Arrange 4 pasta noodles over sauce overlapping the edges. Spread 1/3 of the cheese mixture over pasta. Spread with 3/4 cup of meat sauce.
Repeat layers twice, beginning and ending with pasta.
Top with remaining meat sauce, sprinkle with 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese and additional Parmesan cheese.
Cover with foil.
Bake for 45 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
Remove foil and continue to bake for about 10-15 minutes to brown top.
Let stand for 10-15 minutes before cutting.
