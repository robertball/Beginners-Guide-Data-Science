Title:
banana pepper egg casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3044778.jpg
Ingredients:
16 oz shredded co-jack cheese
1 12 oz jar hot or mild pepper rings  (I use mild)
5 eggs
1/4 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon garlic

Directions:
Spray  an 8x8 casserole dish with PAM.
Spread half the shredded cheese on the bottom
Layer pepper rings on top of the cheese
Whisk  the 5 eggs, milk & garlic and pour over the cheese
Sprinkle the rest of the cheese on top
Bake @ 350 for 40-45 min

Submitter: 
Jane
Description: 
Easy to make casserole that has just the right amount of tang.
