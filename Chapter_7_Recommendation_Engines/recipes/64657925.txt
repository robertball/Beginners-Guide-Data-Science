Title:
Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs, chopped into cubes
1 1/2  c carrots, peeled and sliced
1 1/2  c celery, sliced
1 lemon
3 Tbsp minced garlic
2 Tbsp rubbed sage
2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp salt
3 Tbsp corn starch
4 Tbsp olive oil
4 Tbsp chicken soup base
8 c water

Directions:
Mix corn starch into 1 cup cold water and set aside. Heat oil and garlic in large soup pot, add carrots, celery, salt and saute for 5-10 minutes. Add chicken to the pot and more oil if needed. Cook and stir until no longer pink. Add 7 cups water and soup base. Coax anything sticking to bottom of pot off with spatula as you bring everything to a boil.  Boil 20 minutes, add pepper, sage and corn starch water. Continue to boil for 5 minutes longer. Take off heat and add juice of lemon.  Cooked egg noodles, rice or pasta make a nice addition to this soup.

Submitter: 
Robyn Stone
Description: 
Classic chicken soup with a punch of lemon and garlic
