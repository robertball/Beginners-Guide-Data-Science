Title:
watermelon soup with blackberries and jicama Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pounds seedless watermelon on the rind
3 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
3 teaspoons fresh lime juice
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1 cup fresh blackberries
1 cup peeled and sliced jicama, in 1-inch matchsticks
2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint

Directions:
Cut the watermelon off the rind.  Cut 1 cup into 1/4-in dice and set aside.  Cut the rest of the watermeon into cubes.
In the work bowl of a food processor, placed the cubed watermelon and process until smooth.  Transfer the puree to a medium bowl and stir in 2tsp of the olive oil, 2tsp of the lime juice and the chili powder.  Let stand at room temperature for 1 hour.
Meanwhile, in a small bowl, placed the reserved diced watermelon, the blackberries, jicama, and remaining 1tsp each olive oil and lime juice.  Stir gently and set aside.
Just before serving, stir the mint into the blackberries and jicama.  Divide the soup among 4-6 bowls.  Place a large spoonful of the blackberries and jicama on top, dividing the mixture evenly among the bowls.  Serve.
