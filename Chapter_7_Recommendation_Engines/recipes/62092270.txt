Title:
Cristi's Lemon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the Shortbread:
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1-1/2 cups all purpose flour
1/8 tsp. salt
3/4 cup chilled butter, chopped
For the topping:
4 eggs
1-1/2 cups granulated sugar
1 tsp lemon rind
1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
3/4 cup whipping cream
powdered sugar for dusting (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
Spray 13 x 9 inch cake pan with non-stick spray
Place powdered sugar, flour, salt and chopped butter in food processor.
Process until mixture resembles coarse cornmeal.
Press mixture into cake pan and bake in 325 oven for 20 minutes.
Beat eggs and sugar until blended.
Add lemon juice and lemon peel and mix.
Whip cream lightly and fold into egg/sugar/lemon mixture.
Pour onto warm crust, return to the oven and bake for 40 minutes.
Cool completely.
Dust with powdered sugar(optional)

Submitter: 
Cristi Hursh
Description: 
I bake these bars on Sunday evening and take them to my co-workers on Monday morning.  Always a hit!!!
