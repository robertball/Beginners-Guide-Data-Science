Title:
take the nite off(crockpot) pineapple chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds bone-in,skin-on chicken breasts
1 can pineapple chunks(with juice)
3 tablespoons honey
1/2 cup brown sugar(i prefer dark)
1 tablespoon soy sauce
2 teaspoons powdered ginger

Directions:
place chicken in bottom of crockpot- add pineapple(juice too)- drizzel with honey- sprinkle with brown sugar-drizzel with soy sauce-sprinkle with powdered ginger- cook on high 1 hour- and on medium 6 hours(or til 180 degrees) comes out moist -tender- nice mild dish -

Submitter: 
nanny129
Description: 
served with brown rice and steamed broccoli- makes a nice asian style meal.
