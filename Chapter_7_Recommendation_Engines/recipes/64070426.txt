Title:
Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cup butter
1 1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp. grated orange peel
1 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
8 tsp. milk
4 cups flour
3 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar together, add orange peel, vanilla, then eggs Beat until fluffy.
Stir in milk, add dry ingredients, blend.
Divide dough in half, chill 1 hour.
Roll, cut and bake
