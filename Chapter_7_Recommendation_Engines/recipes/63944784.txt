Title:
Cheryl's Chicken Marsala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp flour
2 tbsp butter
2 tbsp olive oile
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, pounded to 1/4" thickness
2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
2/3 cup Marsala wine
salt, pepper, garlic powder
2/3 cup heavy cream
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
Add salt, pepper and garlic powder to flour. Dredge chicken and set aside.
Saute mushrooms in butter until softened. Remove and set aside.
Add olive oil and chicken to pan. Cook until cooked through, about 10 minutes per side. Remove from pan and keep warm. Add mushrooms and wine to pan, stirring with the bits left in the pan. Boil for 2 to 4 minutes, seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
Stir in cream and milk and simmer until heated through, about 5 minutes. Return chicken to pan and heat through.

Chicken breasts are sauteed, then braised in Marsala wine and cream with mushrooms.Modified from: Easy Chicken Marsala
