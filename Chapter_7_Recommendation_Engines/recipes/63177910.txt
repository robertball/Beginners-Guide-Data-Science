Title:
Brussel Sprouts ala Angela Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/816800.jpg
Ingredients:
1-2 Lb.Package fresh Brussel sprouts
1- medium onion diced
1- Tablespoon diced garlic
6- Slices bacon diced
1- C chicken or beef stock

Directions:
Take the Brussel sprouts and cut off the ends then cut them in half lengthwise. Take the diced bacon,diced onion,garlic and saute them together in a pan until caramelized. Pour in the chicken or beef broth to deglaze the pan.Combine the Brussel sprouts as soon as you add the chicken or beef broth. Stir everything all together well then put on the lid so the Brussel sprouts steam. Simmer for about 15 minutes until the sprouts are cooked but still firm.Great side dish with our Easter ham.  ENJOY!
