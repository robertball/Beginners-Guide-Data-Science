Title:
Grandma Kramer's Peanut Brittle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1 cup light corn syrup
1 cup water
3 cups shelled raw peanuts
2 Tbsp vanilla
2 Tbsp butter
2 Tbsp baking soda

Directions:
Butter large shallow 15x1-x1-inch pan.
Blend sugar, salt, corn syrup and water in large heavy saucepan.
Bring to a boil.
Cook on high to 234 degrees.
Add peanuts slowly, stirring constantly, keeping mixture bubbling.
Continue to cook on high, stirring constantly for 8 minutes.
Stir in butter and continue to cook 2 minutes or to 300 degrees.
Remove from heat and stir in vanilla.
Sprinkle soda over surface and stir vigorously until mixture is light and puffy.
Pour into greased pan.
Cool and store in air tight container.

Submitter: 
KramerGirls
Description: 
This recipe originally came from Aunt Rita Lutz (Uncle Billy) and was called Puffy Peanut Taffy Brittle.
