Title:
Awesome Creamy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-3 lbs. potatoes, cooked, peeled and cut up. (appx. 6 cups)
-1 10 oz. can of reduced sodium cream of celery (can substitute w/ cream of mushroom or chicken)
-1/2 cup sour cream
-1 3oz. package cream cheese, softened
-2 tablespoon butter, melted
-3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
-1/2 cup sliced green onion or chives
-1/4 cup milk
-1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
-1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
1.Cook potatoes until just tender and cut into bite size pieces.
2.Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a bowl, stir together the soup, sour cream, cream cheese and butter.  Then stir in 1/4 cup. of cheddar cheese, 1/2 of green onions, milk, salt and pepper.  Then gently add the potatoes.  Transfer to baking dish.
3.Bake for 25-30 min or until heated through. Sprinkle w/remaining cheese and green oinion and bake until cheese is melted or bubbly.  Let stand for 5 min. before serving.

Submitter: 
MommyFromSeattle
Description: 
A family recipe using potatoes, cream of celery, cream cheese and shredded cheddar for the perfect creamy potatoes.  A great Sunday night or potluck dish.
