Title:
Faye's Apple Crisp Version 2 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
APPLE FILLING:
8-10 large apples
4 tbsp lemon juice
4 tbsp granulated sugar (or more depending on tartness of apples)
1 tbsp (or to taste) cinnamon
1/2 tsp. nutmeg (or to taste; optional ingredient; if used instead of cinnamon-then increase to 1 tsp)
1/4 c water
TOPPING:
1 c  butter
1 c brown sugar
1 1/2 c flour
1 oatmeal
1tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Mix together topping ingrdients and refrigerate. (Cold topping = crisper topping).
Combine lemon juice, sugar, and cinnamon in 9"x13" pan.
Peel, core and slice apples into lemon juice mixture, stirring to coat apple slices evenly.
Spread topping over apples.
Bake at 375 degrees F for 60 minutes or until apples are tender and topping is golden crisp.
Cool.
Serve warm with vanilla bean ice cream.
