Title:
Hannah Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups frozen corn OR 2 cans canned corn
2 t. olive oil
2 med. zucchinis (about 2 cups cubed)
2 t. Italian seasoning
1 large onion (about 1 cup chopped)
Garlic to taste
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Rinse frozen corn in colander in sink to partially thaw (if you chose frozen corn).  Drain.
In 12 inch skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
Peel and coarsely chop onion, adding to skillet as you chop.
Cut and discard the ends of the zucchinis and cut into bite-size pieces.  Add zucchini to skillet.
Cook, stirring occasionally, until onion is translucent, about 3 minutes.
Add corn and herbs to veggies and stir to mix well.
Cover and reduce heat to medium and steam until heated through, about 3 minutes.
Freeze amount you don't use.  Will keep in refrigerator for up to three days.

Submitter: 
Miss Moneypenny
Description: 
A tasty mixture of corn and zucchini that goes well with many cooked meats.
