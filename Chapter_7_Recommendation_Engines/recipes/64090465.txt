Title:
Pork with Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb pork tenderloin, thinly sliced
2 tsp cornstarch
1/2 tsp sugar
2 tsp soy sauce
2 Tbsp oil
1 Tbsp ginger root, strips
1-1/2 cups chicken stock
1/2 lb cabbage, sliced
1 Tbsp cornstarch, dissolved in 2 Tbsp water
1/2 tsp sugar
2 Tbsp soy sauce

Directions:
Combine pork slices, 2 tsp cornstarch, 1/2 tsp sugar, and 2 tsp soy sauce and mix well.  Let stand for 30 minutes.
Heat oil in large saucepan and saute ginger. Add pork and stir fry for 5 minutes or until nearly done.  Add chicken stock and bring to boil.
Continue cooking until liquid is reduced to about half, stirring constantly.  Add cabbage and cook for 5 minutes.  Mix 1 tbsp cornstarch dissolved in 2 Tbsp water, 1/2 tsp sugar and 2 Tbsp soy sauce.  Add to pork mixture and stir until smooth and thick.  Serve with rice.
