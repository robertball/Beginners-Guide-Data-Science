Title:
Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small cabbage, grated
1/2 small onion, grated
2 carrots, grated
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 Tbsp oil
2 Tbsp vinegar
1/3 cup sugar
Dash salt
Dash celery salt

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix cabbage, onion and carrots.
In a medium bowl mix mayonnaise and sugar with a mixer.  Add oil, vinegar, salt and celery salt.  Mix well.  Add to vegetables and mix well.  Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
