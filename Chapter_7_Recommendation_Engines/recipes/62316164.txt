Title:
No Bake Pineapple Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/376363.jpg
Ingredients:
2 TBS Plain Gelatin
1 Can Pineapple Cubes
1 Cup Pineapple Reserve Juice
125 ml Heavy Cream
5 Egg Whites
8  TBS Sugar
1/2 Cup Whipping Cream

Directions:
Strain the pineapple pieces and reserve the juice.
Dissolve gelatin in 2 TBS of warm water and set aside.
Heat the pineapple juice in a sauce pan and add the gelatin. Stir till dissolved. Set aside to cool.
In a separate bowl, beat the egg whites adding little sugar in intervals till the egg whites are stiff and soft shiny peaks form.
Fold in the cream lightly in the egg mixture.
Mix in the pineapple gelatin mixture and pineapple pieces, fold till fully incorporated. Transfer the mixture in a serving dish.
Referigerate for 5 hours before serving or until completely set. Top with whipping cream and more pinapple pieces.
