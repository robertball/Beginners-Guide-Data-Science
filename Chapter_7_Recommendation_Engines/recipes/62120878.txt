Title:
Rockin New Year Rollups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkg smoked beef
1 small jar restaurant style dill pickle slices
1 8 oz pkg cream cheese
1 tsp each dried onion, chives, garlic, basil, oregano
1/8 tsp crushed red pepper
1-2 stalks green onion (discard white bulb)chopped

Directions:
In small bowl, combine softened cream cheese, dried onion, chives, garlic, basil, oregano, and red pepper until well mixed.
Spread 1/2 tbs of cream cheese mixture on two slices of beef that are kept together.
Srinkle chopped green onion on top of cream cheese mixture
Arrange pickle slices on one side of the beef in single line from end to end
Roll pickles and meat together longwise.
Cut into 1 inch bites, serve chilled
