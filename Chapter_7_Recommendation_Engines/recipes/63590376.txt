Title:
Banana Pineapple Sorbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ripe bananas
3 oz pineapple juice
3 oz frozen pineapple juice
2 Tbs shredded coconut

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a food processor or blender. Puree until smooth. Serve immediately

Ed G II
Description: 
A cool recipe with a tropical flavor to make simple desert good for vegans, vegetarians, gluten free, in a matter f minutes
