Title:
Oven-BBQ Beef Brisket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Teas. Coarse Gr. Pepper
2 Tbls. Brown Sugar
1 Teas. Celery Seeds
1/8 Teas. Garlic Powder
1/8 Teas. Paprika
1/8 Teas. Onion Powder
1/2 Teas. Salt
1 Teas. Liquid Smoke(optional)
3 1/2 to 4 Lbs. Fresh Beef Brisket
1 1/4 Cup BBQ Sauce

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine first 8 ingredients and rub on to both sides of brisket in a roasting pan. Cover with foil and refrigerate 10-12 hours. Bake covered at 300F. for 45 min. per pound of brisket. Chill for aleast 1 hour to let fat congeal.
When ready to serve remove beef from pan;remove fat with slotted spoon and discard reserving juices. Cut beef into thin slices across the grain and place in pan your are going to use to serve. In a small bowl combine 1 cup juice and bbq sauce; pour over sliced beef. Bake at 325F. for 45 Min.

Submitter: 
Mad Butcher
Description: 
It is best to put sliced brisket into a serving dish that is onev proof. Because the beff is do tender that it is  hard to transfer from a pan after heating with the sauce. It is that tender.
