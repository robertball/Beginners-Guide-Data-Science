Title:
Filippino Lechon Kawali Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2lbs        Pork belly
3     	        Garlic cloves, crushed
2     	        Bay leaves ,whole
1/2 teaspoon    Black pepper
1/2 teaspoon  	Salt
Water for boiling
Oil for frying

Directions:
Cut the pork belly into serving pieces then combine with the garlic, pepper,bay leaves, salt and water in a pan.
Bring to a boil and simmer for 35-45 minutes or until skin is tender.
Drain, cool and air dry.
Deep-fry liempo pieces in batches until golden brown.
Mix all the sauce ingredients and serve lechon kawali while itâ€™s hot.
