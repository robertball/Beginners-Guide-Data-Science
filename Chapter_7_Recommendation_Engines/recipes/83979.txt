Title:
Baja Chipotle Fish Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979598.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Lawry's® Baja Chipotle Marinade With Lime Juice, divided
1 pound firm, boneless white fish fillets
8 soft taco size corn tortillas, warmed
2 cups shredded green cabbage
For Chipotle Sauce:
1/2 cup Hellmann's® or Best Foods® Real Mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup Lawry's® Baja Chipotle Marinade With Lime Juice

Directions:
In Ziploc(R) bag, combine fish and 3/4 cup Marinade.  Marinate in refrigerator for 1 hour, turning several times.
Mix Chipotle Sauce ingredients and chill.
Remove fish from marinade, discarding used marinade.  Grill fish, turning and brushing with reserved 1/4 cup Marinade.
To serve, divide fish among tortillas (use two tortillas for each taco). Top with cabbage and drizzle with Chipotle Sauce.

Submitter: 
LAWRY'S®
Description: 
Grilled fish and a creamy smoked pepper sauce will take your tastebuds to the white sandy beaches of Mexico!
