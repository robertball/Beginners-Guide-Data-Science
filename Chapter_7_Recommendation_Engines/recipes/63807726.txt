Title:
World's Best Honey Garlic Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1055975.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup ketchup
1/6 cup honey - local is best
1/8 cup low salt soy sauce
2 garlic cloves crushed
1.5 -2 lbs inch thick pork chops

Directions:
In bowl kill all the honey,ketchup, soy sauce, garlic. This is the glaze.
Over a hot grill, sear both side of the pork chops. Once that is done lightly brush on glaze on both sides until pork chops are done. Internal temp of around 140-150

Submitter: 
John Chandler
Description: 
A quick and simple grilled pork chop that everyone will love.
