Title:
Fat-Free Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
splash of fat-free chicken (or vegetable) broth
drained 14 oz. can chickpeas
2 Tbs. lemon juice
2 cloves garlic
paprika
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Add all ingredients, except paprika, to Tall Cup.
Twist on Cross Blade and blend until smooth.
Salt and pepper to taste, and sprinkle with paprika.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
Best served cold with warm triangles of pita bread.
