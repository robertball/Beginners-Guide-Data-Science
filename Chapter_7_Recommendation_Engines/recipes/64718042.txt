Title:
Pumpkin, Arugula and Quinoa Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 sugar pumpkin, peeled and cubed( about 4 cups.
1/4 cup olive oil, divided.
1/2 teaspoon each salt and pepper.
1 cup Gluten free Quinoa, uncooked and rinsed.
2 cups home made chicken broth( 35% or no salt added if using boxed broth)
1/2 red onion sliced paper thin.
4 cups baby arugula, washed and dried.
1/2 cup plain goat cheese ( the creamed kind)
1/2 cup roasted pumpkin seeds.
1 teaspoon pepper to taste.

Directions:
Preheat oven  to 400 degrees.
On a rimmed baking sheet toss pumpkin with 1 tablespoon olive oil,
salt and pepper.
Roast,stirring occasionally until pumpkin is golden  and tender
when pierced with a fork, about 15 minutes. Set aside.
In a medium saucepan combine Quinoa and broth and reduce heat to simmer.
Cover and cook for 15 minutes or until most of  the liquid is absorbed.
Remove from heat and keep covered for 15 minutes, then fluff with a fork.
In a large shallow serving bowl combine quinoa with roasted pumpkin
and any juices from the baking sheet.
Drizzle with remaining oil.
Toss with onion, arugula, and crumbled goat cheese.
Sprinkle with pumpkin seeds, and the extra pepper if desired.
You can use sunflower seeds in place pf pumpkin.
Butternut squash is you can't find sugar pumpkin.
