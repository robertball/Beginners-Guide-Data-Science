Title:
Lou's Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2  lb. Italian Sausage
1/2  lb. Hamburger
2    lb. Jar Prego (Chunky with Mushrooms)
1    Cup Water
15 oz. Grated Mozzarella Cheese
1/2 Teaspoon Oregano
2    Tablespoons Chives
1    Egg
15 oz. Ricotta Cheese
8 oz. Lasagna Noodles
1/2 Cup Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
In a skillet brown and drain the italian sausage and hamburger.
Add the Prego and water and let simmer for 5 minutes.
In a medium bowl, mix the mozzerella cheese, oregano, chives and egg.
Do not cook the Lasagna noodles.
In a 9x13 pan layer:
1 1/2 cups of the meat sauce
1/2 of the noodles
1/2 of the ricotta cheese
1/2 of the mozzarella cheese mixture
Repeat the above layer.
Top with the remaining meat sauce and the parmesan cheese.
Cover and refrigerate overnight.
Bake at 350 degrees for 50 - 60 minutes.
Enjoy!

An easy and delicious lasagna recipe.  Very tasty and always disappears quickly!
