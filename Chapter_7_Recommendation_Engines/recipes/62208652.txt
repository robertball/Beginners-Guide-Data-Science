Title:
Ann's Fantastic Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large navel orange, peeled, segmented and chopped
1 ruby red grapefruit, peeled, segmented and chopped
2 granny smith apples, peeled, cored and diced
2 bananas, sliced
1 (15 ounce) can chunk pineapple, drained...reserve juice
1 (10 ounce) jar maraschino cherries, drained and halved
1 cup red or green grapes, halved
1/2 cup sweetened flaked coconut
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
Sugar to taste or if preferred, use honey to taste

Directions:
Combine all fruits in a large bowl, except bananas. Mash slightly with the back of a wooden spoon to release juices. Add coconut, walnuts and bananas. Cover and refrigerate for at least an hour for flavors to blend.

Submitter: 
witchywoman
Description: 
This is a fruit salad my mother-in-law makes and it is just wonderful and probably the best I have ever had. If you don't like a particular fruit, just substitute it with one that you like!
