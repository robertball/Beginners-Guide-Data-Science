Title:
Quick and Easy Bacon and Egg Breakfast Quesadilla Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
No-Stick Cooking Spray
1 (7 inch) flour tortilla
2 eggs
1 Tablespoons milk
2 slices  bacon
4 tablespoons cheddar cheese
salsa (optional)

Directions:
Cook bacon until crisp. Break into small pieces and set aside.
In a bowl, whisk the eggs and milk until combined. Spray small skillet with non-stick spray and heat until hot. Add egg mixture; cook and stir over medium-low heat until eggs are completely set.
Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Spray one side of tortilla and skillet with cooking spray; place in pan sprayed side down. Cover half of the tortilla with eggs, bacon and cheese. Fold in half and cook until lightly golden brown and then flip and cook the other side.  Serve warm with salsa.
