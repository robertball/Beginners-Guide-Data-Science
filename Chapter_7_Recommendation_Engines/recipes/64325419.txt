Title:
Cheesecake Swirled 5 Bite Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2031523.jpg
Ingredients:
Brownie:
1 cup melted butter
1 1/2 cup white sugar
4 eggs
1 1/2 Tablespoon of vanilla
3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 cup flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon of salt
cheesecake filling:
8 oz cream cheese room temp
1 egg
1/4 cup white sugar
3 tablespoons of flour
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 and grease cupcake pan
Brownies:
In a Large bowl whisk by hand the butter, white sugar, and vanilla till well blended.
Add eggs one at a time and whisk well between each egg
Add cocoa powder, and mix well
In a small bowl combine flour, baking powder, and salt.
Add flour mixture to wet batter and mix well.
Cheesecake filling:
Blend cream cheese till smooth, then add in sugar vanilla and flour, blend till incorporated and lastly add egg, and mix well.
Spoon brownie batter into greased cupcake cups, filling about 3/4 way full
Add about 1 tablespoon of cheesecake mixture on top of brownie batter and swirl together with a toothpick.
Bake in oven for 18-20 mins
Remove from oven and remove from pan promptly onto a cooling rack..you don't want you brownies to over cook.
This recipe will make 18-24 cupcake sized brownies, depending on how full you fill your pan.
