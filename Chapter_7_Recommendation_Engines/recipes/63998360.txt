Title:
Parsley Red Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* Red potatoes (how much ever you want to use)
* parsley flakes
* butter melted
* tad of Italian seasoning
* garlic powder
* salt

Directions:
Clean red potatoes well. Cut up the potatoes and put in a pan (if using red potatoes you can leave the skin on, if using other type of potatoes then you must peel them first).
Pour butter over potatoes and add desired seasoning amount to potatoes.
Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven until tender, stir occasionally to coat with butter.
