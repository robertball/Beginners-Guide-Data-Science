Title:
Cheese and Bacon Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 medium size potatoes.
250g unsmoked bacon.
1 packet cheddar cheese sauce mix (or make your own).

Directions:
Peel potatoes and chop in half.
Parboil potatoes for 10 minutes in lightly salted water and drain.
While potatoes are cooling, chop bacon roughly into bite-size pieces.
Once potatoes are cool to the touch, chop into slices and layer potatoes and bacon alternately in a casserole dish.
Make cheese sauce and pour into the casserole dish, covering potatoes and bacon.
Cover and bake at 170C for 45 minutes.
Remove cover for final 15 minutes of cooking.
Serve!

Submitter: 
Patches
Description: 
A scrummy dinner for a cold day! Alternating layers of potatoes and bacon are gently cooked in a cheddar cheese sauce. Serve with peas, broccoli and some crusty bread.
