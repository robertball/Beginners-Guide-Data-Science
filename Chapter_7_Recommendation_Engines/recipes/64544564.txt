Title:
Spaghetti Pie Recipe

Ingredients:
6 oz spaghetti noodles
2 T butter
1/3 c parmesan cheese
1 lb ground beef or italian sausage
2 c (or 1 jar) of prepared spaghetti sauce
1 c cottage or ricotta cheese
2 oz shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Cook spaghetti noodles. Stir in butter until melted. Add parmesan cheese and eggs. Form into a buttered 10 inch pie plate.
Cook ground beef, then add pasta sauce to it. Spread cottage or ricotta cheese on "crust", then fill with the meat/sauce mixture. Bake at 350Â°F for about 20 minutes. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese, and bake for 5 more minutes.
