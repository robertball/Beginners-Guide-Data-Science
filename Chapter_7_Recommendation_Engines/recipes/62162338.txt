Title:
Honey Milk Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/336307.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 c. honey
1/2 c. peanut butter
1 c. dry milk solids
1 c. uncooked rolled oats
wheat germ, optional

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
Mix well, then knead by hand until blended
Shape into small balls.
Enjoy! No refrigeration necessary.
