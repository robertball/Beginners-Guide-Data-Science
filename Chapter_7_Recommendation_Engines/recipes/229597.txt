Title:
Pepper Vodka Cherry Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2229573.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pint ripe cherry tomatoes
1/2 cup pepper-flavored vodka
3 tablespoons sea salt
1 tablespoon lemon pepper

Directions:
Poke 5 or 6 holes with a wooden pick or skewer in each tomato and put in a shallow bowl. Pour vodka over tomatoes. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and marinate until the flavors blend, 1 to 2 hours.
Mix sea salt and lemon pepper in a small bowl; serve with the marinated tomatoes for dipping.

Submitter: 
Cpeltz
Description: 
Very easy and delicious appetizer, like a bite-size bloody mary!
