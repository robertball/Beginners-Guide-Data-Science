Title:
Grandma B's Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup peanut butter (use a good one for better flavor)
1/2 cup shortning
1   egg
1 1/4 cup flour
1/2 teas baking powder
1   teas baking soda
1/4 teas salt
1 teas vanilla

Directions:
Mix shortning, sugars and peanut butter. Mix in remaining ingredients. Roll into balls, place on ungressed cookie sheet. Flaten with a fork. Bake at 375 degrees for 10 - 12 minutes.
