Title:
Baked Chicken Lasagna Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups part skim Ricotta cheese
1-1/4 cup part skim Mozzarella cheese, shredded
1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
1 egg
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
2 cups shredded baked chicken breasts
8 cooked lasagna noodles
1-1/2 cup tomato sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Mix first 8 ingredients, reserving 1/4 cup mozzarella cheese for baking, set aside.
Lay a large piece of plastic wrap on counter. Lay noodles flat, top each noodle with a layer of ricotta mixture and 1/4 cup chicken.
Roll one noodle at a time.
Fill bottom of baking dish with 1/2 cup tomato sauce.
Lay rolls seam side down, cover with remaining sauce and cheese.
Bake at 375 for 20 minutes.
