Title:
Chili Rubbed Tilapia with Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/951803.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fillets of Tilapia, or other firm white fish
1-2 bunches of Asparagus, ends trimmed (depending on how much you like to each)
1/2 Teaspoon of Garlic Powder
1/4 Teaspoon of Sea Salt
2 Tablespoons of Chili Powder
Olive Oil Spray (natural)
Two lemons halved

Directions:
ASPARAGUS:
Trim the ends of the Asparagus, wash and pat dry
Lightly glaze a Pyrex or similiar dish with olive oil
Place Asparagus in pan and lightly glaze with olive oil then sprinkle with sea salt
Set aside
Pre-heat oven to 350
SPICE MIXTURE:
Blend the chili powder, garlic powder and salt in a small bowl and spread mixture onto a plate, spreading evenly
FISH:
Spray Olive Oil Spray on each side of the fillets
Dredge the fillets on each side onto the spice mixture and coat
Heat a pan on medium and spray with olive oil
Place reserved asparagus in the oven and bake for approx. 8-15 mins. depending on the stalks thickness.
At the same time, add fish and cook until mid section appears to be lightly opaque
Gently turn fillets and and cook for approx. 3 more minutes
Serve immediately with asparagus and squeeze a little lemon onto the fillet and asparagus.

Submitter: 
Sadie Melgoza
Description: 
Tiliapia, often perceived as a dull fish can be made delicious with a simple chili spice rub mixture. I am all about less is more, living clean and feeling lean.
