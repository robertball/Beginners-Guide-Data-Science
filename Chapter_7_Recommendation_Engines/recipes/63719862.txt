Title:
V-8 Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Have a recipe for u:
V-8 Soup:
2 big cans V-8
Large package of stew meat from Kroger
2 large cans Veg-Al
Ms. Dash
Lemon pepper
Oregano
Hot sauce if you want
Cheez-Its

Directions:
Rinse off meat & combine with as much spices as you want. Boil until meat is done. Drain meat & add to drained veg-al & V-8 in a big pot. Add hot sauce to taste and simmer for 30 minutes. Best soup ever!
Garnish with cheez-its!
