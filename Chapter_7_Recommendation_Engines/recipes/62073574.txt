Title:
Deviled Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium Idaho potatoes
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 cup diced celery
1/2 cup chopped hamburger dill pickles
1/2 cup chopped pimento-stuffed green
olives
1/2 tsp. dill weed
Salt and Pepper to taste
1/4 cup chopped parsley (optional)
2 hard boiled eggs, chopped
1 cup (or more to taste) Miracle Whip
Salad Dressing (has to be
Miracle Whip for best flavor)
3 Tablespoons prepared mustard

Directions:
Boil potatoes with skin on for about 20 minutes or until done.  Peel off skin and dice into a large bowl.  Add the rest of the ingredients except for Miracle Whip and mustard.  Mix gently.  Stir together Miracle Whip and mustard and pour over ingredients.  Mix gently, refrigerate.  Even better if made day before and refrigerated so flavors blend.
Miracle Whip and mustard amounts may be adjusted to taste.

Submitter: 
Barb Wahnsiedler Klein
Description: 
This is a very tasty potato salad that everyone enjoys.  My family thinks it is the best ever!
