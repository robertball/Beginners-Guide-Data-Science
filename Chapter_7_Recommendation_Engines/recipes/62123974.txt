Title:
Tart Cranberry Lemon Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1 T. lemon zest
1/4 cup butter, softened
1 cup sugar
1 egg, lightly beaten
3/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries, coarsely chopped
1/2 cups chopped walnuts or pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease and flour a 9 x 5" loaf pan.
Whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
In separate bowl, cream butter and sugar.
Add egg to creamed mixture.
Add lemon juice and zest.
Mix well until combined.
Add dry ingredients.
Mix well.
Fold in chopped cranberries and nuts.
Batter will be very stiff.
Put into prepared pan.
Bake for 50 minutes to 1 hour.
Let cool before slicing.

Submitter:

Description: 
Sweet & tart bread full of fresh cranberries.  No one will wonder what kind of bread this is...loaded with tart flavor.  I combined several favorite recipes and came up with this yummy bread.
