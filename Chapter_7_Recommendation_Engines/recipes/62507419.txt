Title:
Creamy Jalapeño Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-5 lb. pork roast
Salt, pepper and favorite seasoning
Equal parts sour cream and jalapeño jelly

Directions:
Season roast and cook in a rotisserie (or regular) oven for 2-3 hours.
Mix sour cream and jalapeño jelly in saucepan, and heat thoroughly.
Slice roast and serve with sauce on the side (in a gravy boat).
