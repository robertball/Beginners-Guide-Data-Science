Title:
Flax Muffin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 to 1 1/2 T of coconut oil, melted
Pinch of salt
1 egg, beaten
1/4 t baking powder
4 T flax mill (ground flax seed)
1/2 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Mix ingredients together in a microwave-safe bowl, then let sit for a minute.
Microwave for 1 minute.
Leave in microwave 1 minute.
Cut and toast.
Top with butter, peanut butter, or jelly.
