Title:
Italian Muffuletta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbls olive oil
2 Tbls white wine vinegar
2 tsp fresh snipped oregano
1 tsp Dijon-style mustard
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
12 slices crusty sourdough bread
2 medium tomatoes, sliced
6 oz thinly sliced provolone cheese
2 cups loosely packed basil leaves
5 pepperoncini salad peppers, seeded and thinly sliced

Directions:
In a screw-top jar combine olive oil, vinegar, oregano, mustard, and crushed red pepper. Set aside.
On six of the bread slices, layer tomatoes, cheese, basil and pepperoncini salad peppers.
Shake oil mixture. Drizzle oil over peppers. Top with remaining bread slices. Wrap tightly. Chill up to 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Jessica
Description: 
Not a traditional muffuletta with olive salad, but the pepperoncini's compliment well.
