Title:
Pumpkin Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/913592.jpg
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 c. sugar
2/3 c. pumpkin
3/4 c. flour
1 t. baking powder
2 t. pumpkin pie spice
1 c. crushed walnuts (opt.)
powdered sugar
8 oz. cream cheese
1 c. powdered sugar
4 T. butter
1 t. vanilla

Directions:
Beat the eggs; mix in sugar and pumpkin.
Add dry ingredients and mix well. Pour onto greased pan and sprinkle on the optional walnuts.
Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes. Upon removal of oven, immediately invert pan over a pillowcase sprinkled generously with powdered sugar.
Roll up tightly starting at the narrow end; let cool.
Meanwhile, mix together cream cheese, powdered sugar, butter, and vanilla.
When cake cools, carefull unroll and spread cream cheese mixture on the inside, keeping about 1/2" of space on each side. Roll back up (without the pillowcase) and sprinkle with powdered sugar.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
A family favorite for years and years. The secret? a 15x10x1 pan to cook the batter in.
