Title:
Keto King Ranch Chicken Casserole (Notilla Chicken Casserole) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c chopped/shredded chicken (approx 1 lb)
2 T ghee (or butter or olive oil)
1 onion, chopped
4-5 garlic cloves, chopped
1.5 T cumin
1 T chili powder
2 tsp smoked paprika
2 tsp oregano
2 4oz cans mild hatch green chiles
3/4 c mild salsa
2 c sour cream
juice from 1/4 to 1/2 lime
salt to taste
16 oz jack or cheddar or combination cheese, shredded

Directions:
preheat oven to 400
grease 9x13 inch baking pan with olive oil
melt ghee in large skillet
add onions to soften, add garlic, stir until softened and fragrant
add spices and salt, saute until fragrant
add green chiles and salsa
add sour cream
add chicken
season to taste with lime juice and salt
layer 1/3 of chicken, 1/3 of cheese, alternate, top with cheese
bake 400 degrees for 30 min or until bubbling and cheese starting to brown
