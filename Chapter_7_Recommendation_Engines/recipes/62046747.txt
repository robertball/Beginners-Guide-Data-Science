Title:
Creamy Cherry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz package cream cheese, softened
1 8oz carton whipped topping
1 21oz can cherry pie filling
2 11oz cans mandarin oranges, drained

Directions:
Combine cream cheese and whipped topping.  Stir in pie filling.  Fold oranges into cream cheese mixture. Chill.  If you prefer it frozen, place it in a loaf pan and freeze it at least 8 hours.

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
This creamy salad tastes wonderful, but not too sweet.  I like it chilled, but my husband likes it frozen.  It is sure to be a winner served either way!
