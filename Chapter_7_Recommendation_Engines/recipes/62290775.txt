Title:
All-in-One Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast
3 zucchinis
2 sweet potatoes
2 large red potatoes
2 8oz sliced fresh mushrooms
1/2 cup seasoned breadcrumbs
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/4 cup and 2 tablespoons olive oil
salt
pepper
garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Cut chicken in to small cubes and cook in large skillet with mushrooms and 2 tablespoons olive oil until all juices are gone and mushrooms and chicken are browned. Spinkle with salt, pepper and garlic powder.
Meanwhile cut up zucchini, sweet potatoes and red potatoes in to dime size cubes.
In a large bowl combine all ingredients with 1/2 cup breadcrumbs, 1/2 cup grated parmesan and 1/4 cup olive oil. Mix well and transfer to lightly greased casserole dish.
Bake for 1 hour stirring every 15 minutes.
