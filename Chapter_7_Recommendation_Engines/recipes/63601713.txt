Title:
French Toast Apple Strata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 to 8 slices of french toast
2 granny smith apples, cored peeled and sliced thinly
2 eggs
1 cup light cream
1 tsp. cardamom
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup brown sugar + 2 tbsp.
lemon juice to taste

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
Beat the eggs. Stir in the cream, cardamom, vanilla, and half-cup of brown sugar. Set aside.
Cut the french toast into strips.
Toss the french toast and apple pieces together in a loaf pan, packing them loosely. Pour the egg/cream mixture over top.
Sprinkle with lemon juice and the rest of the brown sugar.
Bake for 20 to 30 minutes until a knife comes out clean.

Description: 
This is my recipe for leftover french toast, and/or what I make with any artisan/bakery bread that will go bad before we can use it up.
