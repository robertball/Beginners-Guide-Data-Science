Title:
Slow Cooker Creamy Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 onion, finely chopped
4 (10.5 ounce) cans condensed chicken broth
2 cups water
7 large potatoes, diced
3/2 teaspoon celery salt
1/2 teaspoon dried dill weed
1 1/2 teaspoon ground white pepper (or course black)
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
3 cups half-and-half cream
1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
granulated garlic to your taste
1/2 cup potatoe flakes

Directions:
Place  onion in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until onions are soft. Drain off excess grease.
Transfer the  onion to a slow cooker, and stir in chicken broth, water, potatoes, salt, dill weed, and white pepper and garlic. Cover, and cook on Low 6 to 7 hours, stirring occasionally.
In a small bowl, whisk together the flour and half-and-half. Stir into the soup along with the evaporated milk,and 1/2 cup of potatoe flakes. Cover, and cook another 30 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Jack Pleugers
Description: 
This is a very rich and creamy soup.  A great family favorite. It may be cooked on the stove or in a slow cooker. Garnish with chives, if desired.Modified from: Slow Cooker Creamy Potato Soup
