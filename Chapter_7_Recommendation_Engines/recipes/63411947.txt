Title:
Ratatouille II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/920478.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
1/2 onion, finely chopped
2 garlic cloves, very thinly sliced
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 small zucchini, chopped
1 small yellow squash, chopped
1 small eggplant, chopped
1 cup tomato, chopped
Few sprigs fresh thyme
Salt and pepper
Few tablespoons soft goat cheese

Directions:
See notes section before starting.
In a non-stick skillet add 1 TBSP of Olive Oil, add onion and sweat them for a few minutes.
Add garlic and cook for about 30 second.
Add bell pepper and cook until it just starts to soften.
Add zucchini and yellow squash and cook until they just start to soften.
Add the other TBSP of olive oil and eggplant; cOOK until softened.
Add tomatoes and thyme cook until just softened.
Take a presentation ring (or a tuna can with both ends cut out) put some of the Ratatouille in the bottom, a little bit of goat cheese, a little bit more of the Ratatouille and press it down gently to set it. Slowly lift the ring off and add a small crumble of goat cheese on top.

Submitter: 
Dianna Jacobs-Fresh
Description: 
Closer version to the French Ratatouille.
