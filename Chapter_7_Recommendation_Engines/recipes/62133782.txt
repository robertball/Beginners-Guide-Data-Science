Title:
Easy Caesar dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head romaine lettuce
4 slices crisp bacon crumbled
1 cup croutons
1 wooden salad bowl
Dressing:
1 egg yolk
2 cloves garlic
1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/2 cup grate parmesan cheese, fresh or from a can
2 tsp. anchovy paste
2 tsp. dijon mustard
1 tsp. lemon juice
1 tsp. red wine vinigar
1 tsp. worcestershire

Directions:
Rub inside of wooden bowl with gralic
Chop remaining garlic and place in bowl
Whisk all dressing ingredients together in wooden bowl
Add chopped lettuce, bacon and croutons in bowl, mix and serve.
Don't have a wooden salad bowl, just skip rubbing the bowl with the garlic.
Add a grilled chicken breast for a complete meal.

Submitter: 
Renee Manz
Description: 
Awesome, super easy, home made caesar salad dressing.  All done in one bowl in just a few minutes.  My husband and I just love it.
