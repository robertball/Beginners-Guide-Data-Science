Title:
Katies' Frybread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
COMBINE IN LARGE BOWL;
.    4 cups all-purpose flour
.    1 teaspoon salt
.    1 tablespoon baking powder
.
STIR IN;
.     1 1/2 cups warm water (110 degrees F)

Directions:
Knead until soft & not sticky. Do not to over knead as it will become tough.
Shape dough into 3 inch balls.
Flatten into 1/2 inch thick patties & make a small hole in the center of each patty.
Heat 1 inch shortening in large pan, or use fryer.
Fry one at a time on both sides.
Drain on brown paper, or paper towels.
Sprinkle with powdered sugar, cinnamon sugar, or use as base for tacos, top with cheese & cooked meat, broil & serve with soup or salad
