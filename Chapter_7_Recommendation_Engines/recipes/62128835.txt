Title:
Skillet Pork Chops, Squash and Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 to 6 boneless pork chops
6 yellow squash
2 cups thinly sliced cabbage
1 small onion chopped
1tbsp olive oil
3 tsp soy sauce
2 garlic gloves minced
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/2 cup herb seasoned dried stuffing mix or dried bread crumbs

Directions:
In large skillet heat oil on medium heat
Brown pork chops 8 to 10 minutes on each side
Add garlic, squash, cabbage, onion, salt, pepper and soy sauce
Cover, reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes.
Stir vegetables occasionally leaving the chops on the bottom.
Remove from heat and sprinkle bread crumbs on top, let stand covered for 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Nita Rockwell Minto
Description: 
Boneless porks chops, yellw squash and cabbage make up this tasty all in one skillet dinner.
