Title:
Pan Peanut Butter Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/943941.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter
1 (16 ounce) package graham crackers, crushed
4 cups confectioners' sugar
2 cups butter, melted
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Combine peanut butter, graham crackers, powdered sugar and butter. Press into 9 x 13 inch pan.
Melt chocolate chips and spread over peanut butter mixture. Allow to harden.  (Cut while still soft or layers will separate.)

Submitter: 
Vicki Monte
Description: 
I got this recipe from my sister-in-law and they do taste a lot like the real thing.
