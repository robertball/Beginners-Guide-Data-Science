Title:
Meatballs & Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box beef Rice-a-Roni
1 egg
1 pound ground beef
2 packets brown gravy mix
4 cups water

Directions:
Mix dry rice-a-roni, egg, and ground beef.  Shape into 18 balls.  Brown in skillet, or bake at 350 for 20 minutes.  Drain grease.
Combine water, seasoning packet from rice, and gravy mix.  Bring to boil, add meatballs.  Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer 20-30 minutes.

Submitter: 
redamber
Description: 
Meatballs simmered in a rich brown gravy.  Great served with mashed potatoes.
