Title:
Blueberry Upside Down Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 TSP. butter or margarine-softened, divided
1/4 C. packed brown sugar
2 C. frozen blueberries -you can use fresh
3/4 C. sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 C. milk
1-1/4 cake flour
1-1/2 tsp baking power

Directions:
Melt 2 TBSP. butter, stir in brown sugar. Spread into an ungreased 8" baking dish. Arrange blueberries in a single layer over brown sugar mixture, set aside. In a large mixing bowl cream remaining butter, beat in sugar. Add egg and vanilla, mix well. Combine flour & baking powder, add to creamed mixture alternately with milk. Carefully pour over blueberries. Bake at 350 40-45 min. or until toothpick comes out clean. Invert onto serving platter, cool.
