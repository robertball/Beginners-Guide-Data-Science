Title:
Big Ray's Chipolte Rub Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1016406.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Tbl brown sugar
6 Tbl ground dried chipotle pepper
3 Tbl paprika
3 Tbl dry mustard powder
3 Tbl ground cumin
2 Tbl season salt

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix all ingredients. Store in an air tight container in a dark, dry place.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
Love to grill, love to rub. This rub brings out the best in anything on the grill from beef, chicken, pork, fish and even veggies.
