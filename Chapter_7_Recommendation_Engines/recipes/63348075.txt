Title:
Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/889904.jpg
Ingredients:
6 bell peppers - red, orange, yellow, or green
1 lb. ground beef
onion powder
garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt
3 cups cooked rice
1 can (16 oz) tomato sauce

Directions:
Cook rice.
Cook and stir ground beef; drain fat and add onion powder, garlic powder, salt, rice, and half of the tomato sauce.
Cut off tops and core peppers, cook in boiling water for 2-3 minutes. Drain.
Stuff peppers with rice/beef mixture. Place in deep casserole dish.
Pour remaining tomato sauce over peppers. Replace pepper tops.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees 30-45 minutes.
Uncover and bake 15 min. longer
