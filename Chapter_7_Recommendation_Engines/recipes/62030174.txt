Title:
Cream Of Broccoli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups chicken broth -- lowfat okay
3 cups chopped fresh broccoli flowerets
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 each bay leaf
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 cup flour
1/3 cup cooked ham -- diced
1/2 cup heavy cream

Directions:
-Combine chicken broth, broccoli, onion, bay leaf, salt, and pepper in large saucepan.
-Bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
-Puree half of soup and flour in blender. Return to saucepan.
-Simmer until thick, stirring constantly.
-Add ham and cream to saucepan.
-Stir and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
