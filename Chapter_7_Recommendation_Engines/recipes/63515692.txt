Title:
Crispy Caesar Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breast, fileted
3/4 cup plain or Italian style bread crumbs
1/8 cup parmesan cheese
1 tsp. dried parsley
1 tsp. Sun of Italy Italian Seasoning
1 tsp. onion powder
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/4 tsp. seasoned salt
cracked black pepper and salt
1/2 - 3/4 cup Caesar dressing

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Filet chicken breasts horizontally. Set aside.
Mix dry ingredients in small bowl. Place dressing in another small bowl.
Dip each piece of chicken in dressing to coat, then dip in crumbs.
Place on baking sheet, or stone, spray each breast lightly with cooking spray, and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until meat thermometer place in center of chicken registers 180 degrees. Turn chicken halfway through, if desired.

Flavorful chicken, crispy on the outside, juicy on the inside.
