Title:
Soy Ginger Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup soy sauce
1 teaspoon finely grated fresh ginger
1 tea spoon sugar free honey
1 teaspoon Dijion mustard
2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
1. In A small sauce pan combine the soy sauce and ginger and bring to a simmer.
2. Remove from heat and stir honey and mustard.
