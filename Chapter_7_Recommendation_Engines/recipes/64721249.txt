Title:
Instant pot spaghetti and meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5935014.jpg
Ingredients:
10 ounces spaghetti, broken in half
4 cups water
12 count (30 ounces) fresh pork meatballs
28 ounces crushed tomatoes
2 garlic cloves, minced
3/4 tsp garlic powder
3/4 tsp Italian seasoning
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp sugar
1/4 tsp oregano

Directions:
Pour water into instant pot. Add spaghetti noodles - mix around to insure noodles are separate. Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
Quick release pressure and drain water from noodles. Place noodles in a covered dish. Keep warm.
Add crushed tomatoes, minced garlic  and meatballs to the instant pot, stir to insure meatballs are covered by the sauce.
Cook on high pressure for 10 minutes. Quick release pressure.
Add remaining seasonings to the sauce. Stir to combine.
Place noodles in a bowl and serve with meatballs and sauce.

Submitter: 
thedailygourmet
Description: 
This is a fast to make spaghetti and meatball recipe- for convenience sake store bought fresh meatballs are being used, although homemade meatballs can be used too.
