Title:
Parmesan Baked Pork Chope Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4509773.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless pork chops 1/2" thick
1 Tbs olive oil
1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese grated
1/2 cup Italian Bread Crumbs
1 tsp black pepper
2 tsp garlic power

Directions:
1. On a plate combine the last four ingredients
2. Rub the pork chops with olive oil
3. Press the pork chops into the cheese mixture
4. Line a cookie tray with aluminum foil and spray with Pam
5. Place the pork chops on cookie sheet and bake at 350 for 35-40 min
