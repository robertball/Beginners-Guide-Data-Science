Title:
Orange Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup shortening
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon grated lemon rind or orange rind
3 cups powdered sugar
1 tablespoon lemon or orange juice
3 tablespoons water

Directions:
Combine shortening, salt, peel, and 1 cup sugar and food coloring.
Add lemon juice, water and remaining sugar alternately.
Mix until smooth and creamy.
