Title:
Teriyaki marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup low sodium soy sauce
1 cup dry white or red wine
3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Mix all ingredients pour over what you want to marinate.
Let meat, fish or poultry you want to marinade sit for at least an hour and up to 24 hours.
