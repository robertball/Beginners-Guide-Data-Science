Title:
Zesty Orange Rice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2215698.jpg
Ingredients:
•	1.7 L milk
•	1 stick of vanilla
•	200g of sugar
•	300g of risotto rice
•	3 shavings of lemon peel
•	5 eggs
•	50 ml of orange liqueur
•	40g of raisins
•	zest of 1 orange

Directions:
1.Simmer 1.7 L of milk.
2.Put the stick of vanilla in the simmering milk, and stir.
3.Add the 200g of sugar.
4.Add the 300g of rice, and stir.
5.Add the 3 shavings/slices of lemon peel.
6.When it starts to boil, turn down the heat.
7.Keep stirring on low heat for 20-25 min., or until the rice is cooked.
8.Separate the yolks and whites of 5 eggs.
9.Add the 50ml of orange liqueur to the egg yolks, and stir.
10.Beat the whites until stiff peaks form.
11.Let the rice cool in the fridge.
12.When cooled, remove vanilla stick, lemon peel, and add 40g of raisins, and the zest of 1 orange.
13.Add the egg yolk mixture to the rice, and stir.
14.SOFTLY  fold in the stiff egg whites.
15.Pour slowly into a 24cm cake pan, greased, or lined with baking paper.
16.Bake for 60 min. in the oven at 180°/350°F.
17.Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
18.Enjoy!
