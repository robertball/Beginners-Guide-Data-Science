Title:
CARROT AMBROSIA SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6Cs. Carrots, peeled and shredded
1/4C. fresh squeesed Lemon Juice
1 1/2Cs. Mandarian Orange Slices, drained
2Cs. Dark Raisins
1 {8 1/4oz. can} Crushed Pineapple, drained
1/2C. Coconut Flakes
1 1/2Cs. Miniature Marshmallows
4Tbsps. Honey {I love Orange Blossom or Clover}
3/4C. Sour Cream
1/2C. Mayonnaise or Miracle Whip

Directions:
Combine first 7 ingredients in a large bowl, tossing lightly.
Combine honey, sour cream and mayonnaise; mix well and pour over carrot mixture. Toss lightly to coat, chill about 8 hours before serving.
Serve on lettuce leaves or in salad bowles.

Submitter: 
Corwynn Darkholme
Description: 
Quick, easy and very yummy! This a great salad for any summertime picnic or dinner party!!
