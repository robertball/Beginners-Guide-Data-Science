Title:
Moist and Tender Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb.extra lean ground beef
1 lb. regular ground beef
4 slices white bread,crusts removed
1/2 cup milk
1/2 onion,finely diced
1/2 green bell pepper,finely diced
1 1/2 tsp.garlic powder
1 tablespoon dried thyme
1 tablespoon dried oregano
1 tsp. salt
2 tsp. black pepper
1/4 cup fresh parsley,chopped
1/2 cup unseasoned bread crumbs
2 eggs,beaten
1 cup grated parmesean cheese

Directions:
In small bowl,tear bread slices into small pieces and add milk;allow to soak while combining other ingredients.In large bowl,place both meats and add all other ingredients.Add soaked bread.Mix well,however do not overmix as this will cause meatballs to be tough.Pinch off small bit of mixture and roll into ball,about 2 inches in diameter.Place on ungreased baking sheet,leaving space between each meatball.Bake at 400 F. for 45 mins.Allow to cool for 5 mins. before adding to sauce.

Submitter: 
RockingItCountryStyle
Description: 
I played with different ingredients until I created this recipe...Great in spaghetti!Try them on hoagies with sliced or grated mozzerella cheese and marina sauce for terrific subs!!I make up a large batch and divide into smaller portions and freeze for quick lunches and suppers.
