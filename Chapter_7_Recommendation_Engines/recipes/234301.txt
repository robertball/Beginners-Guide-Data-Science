Title:
Quick Cantaloupe Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2328228.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cantaloupe - peeled, seeded, and cubed
3/4 cup milk
1 tablespoon white sugar (optional)

Directions:
Place cantaloupe in a blender and top with milk and sugar. Cover and blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
JayKay
Description: 
Must be wondering...cantaloupe juice? Hmm, well guess what, it's a perfect juice to refresh your day! Only 3 ingredients and you are ready to go! One fruit equals thousands of benefits. Eat the other half of the cantaloupe.
