Title:
Chicken Zucchini Enchiladas with Hatch® Green Chiles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5566563.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
1 medium onion, diced
2 cloves garlic minced
2 teaspoons ground cumin
2 teaspoons chili powder
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
3 cups shredded chicken
1 4 oz. can Hatch® Chopped Green Chiles, drained
1 1/3 cup green enchilada sauce; divided use
4 large zucchini, halved lengthwise
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheddar cheese blend
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro, for garnish

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350º Fahrenheit.
In a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Add onion and cook until soft, 5 minutes, then add garlic, cumin oregano and chili powder;  stir until combined.
Add shredded chicken, green chiles and 1 cup enchilada sauce. Stir until well combined.
Using a vegetable peeler or a mandolin make thin slices of zucchini.
Lay out three zucchini slices, slightly overlapping, and place a spoonful of chicken mixture on top. Roll up and transfer to a baking dish. Repeat with remaining zucchini and chicken mixture.
Spread remaining 1/3 cup enchilada sauce over zucchini enchiladas, sprinkle with cheese and bake until cheese has melted, 20 minutes.
Garnish with chopped fresh cilantro.
Cooks note: Use red enchilada sauce if you prefer it.
