Title:
Pumpkin Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 slices white bread, cubed
1 (15oz.) can pumpkin puree
2/3 cup sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. ginger
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1 tsp. vanilla
1/8 tsp. salt
6 eggs, beaten
1 (5 oz.) can evaporated milk
1 cup milk
1/2 cup chopped pecans (optional)

Directions:
Spray a 13"x9" pan with cooking spray and place the cubed bread in the pan.  Combine the remaining ingredients and pour the mixture over the bread cubes.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.  In the morning, uncover and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
cooks4forty
Description: 
This bread pudding-style casserole is great for a holiday breakfast for overnight guests or any weekend you want a treat.  It's assembled the night before and baked in the morning.
