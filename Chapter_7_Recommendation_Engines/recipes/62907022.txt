Title:
Less sweet, more spice & cheese= Perfect! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard
1/2 tablespoon jalapeño mustard
1/2 cup Ketchup
1/2 cup barbecue sauce
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
cayenne papper to taste (1/2 teaspoon)
6 Buns
12 slices Extra sharp cheddar cheese or gruyere cheese

Directions:
In a medium skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef, onion, and green pepper; drain off liquids.
Stir in the garlic powder, mustard, jalapeño mustard, ketchup, barbecue sauce, cayenne pepper; mix thoroughly. Reduce heat, and simmer for 30 minutes. Season with salt, pepper and cayenne.
Place buns in the oven with cheese- (either extra sharp or gruyere cheese, or both) on both buns until buns are brown and cheese is melted. Spoon onto bun.

Submitter: 
Mdaggett
Description: 
Ground beef, onion, green pepper, ketchup and barbecue sauce are seasoned with garlic powder and cayenne pepper to make this hearty meat filling. Serve on hamburger buns, cheesy buns work too!Modified from: Sloppy Joes II
