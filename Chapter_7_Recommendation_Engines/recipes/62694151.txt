Title:
Chicken-Artichoke Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 to 3 lbs. boneless, skinless chicken breasts
14-1/2 oz. can chicken broth
14-oz. can artichoke hearts, drained and quartered
1/4 c. sliced mushrooms
2 T. butter, sliced
3/4 c. half-and-half
1/2 c. grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 t. dried rosemary
1/4 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
1/4 c. all-purpose flour

Directions:
Simmer over medium heat until juices run clear; drain.
Place chicken in a lightly greased 13"x9" baking pan.
Top with artichokes and mushrooms; set aside.
.
Combine remaining ingredients except flour in a saucepan; bring to a boil.
Whisk in flour until smooth; pour over chicken.
Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
Serves 6 to 8.
