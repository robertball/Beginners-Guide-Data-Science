Title:
Simply Delicious Meat Loaf and Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978418.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1/2 cup Italian-seasoned dry bread crumbs
1 egg, beaten
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Golden Mushroom Soup
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Mix thoroughly beef, bread crumbs and egg.  Shape firmly into 8x4-inch loaf in baking pan.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes.  Spread 1/2 can soup over top of meat loaf.  Bake 30 minutes or until done.
Mix 2 tablespoons drippings, remaining soup and water in saucepan.  Heat through and serve with meat loaf.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
The name says it all - this family-pleasing meat loaf is made special and scrumptious with golden mushroom soup.
