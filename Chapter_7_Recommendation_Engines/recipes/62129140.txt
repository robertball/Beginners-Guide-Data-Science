Title:
Sweet Baked Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1lb bag of frozen baby carrots
1/3 c. butter
1/2 c. sugar
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/3 c. boiling water

Directions:
Place frozen carrots in a 8 X 8 shallow baking pan. Combine remaining ingredients and pour over carrots. Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hrs.
*Recipe can be doubled.

Submitter: 
Monica
Description: 
Sweetened carrots are a perfect vegetable addition to any meal. Kids love them!!
