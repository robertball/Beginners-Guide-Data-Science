Title:
Basic Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5348159.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
8 ounces cream cheese
4 cups confectioners' sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
Beat softened butter and cream cheese until well blended.
Add powdered sugar and vanilla. Beat until creamy.

Submitter: 
JJ
Description: 
This recipe delivers a basic cream cheese frosting using just butter, cream cheese, confectioners' sugar, and vanilla.
