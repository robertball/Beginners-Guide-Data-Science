Title:
Broccoli-Tomato Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 box Penne, or other medium sized pasta
1 1/2 tbsp. of olive oil
2 tbsp. margarine
2-3 cloves crushed garlic
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
2 cups of chopped broccoli florets
1 (14.5 oz) can of diced tomatoes, undrained
1 tbsp. of dried Italian seasoning
Grated Paremesan cheese to preference

Directions:
1.Cook pasta according to box directions. Chop broccoli and add to boiling water during the last minute or 2 of pasta cooking time.
2.Heat olive oil and butter over medium heat in a large skillet. Add garlic. Cook for 1-2 minutes. Add cayenne pepper.
3.Toss in the pasta, broccoli and tomatoes. Add Italian seasoning Saute everything for a few minutes, making sure the oil and butter coats everything. Add Parmesan cheese and serve.

Submitter: 
Sola
Description: 
This is quick and very tasty. It's a nice alternative to a traditional pasta with sauce.
