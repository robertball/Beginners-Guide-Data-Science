Title:
Ginger Carrot Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbl. olive or vegetable oil
1/2 Cup minced onion
1/4 Cup grated fresh ginger or ginger puree
2 Cloves Garlic, minced
2 Tsp. Curry Powder
32 Oz. Vegetable Broth or Stock (I used Swanson's Broth)
4 Cups sliced, peeled carrots
Most of a 14.5 oz can of Coconut Milk
1/2 Tsp.  Cumin
1/2 Tsp. Ground Ginger or to taste

Directions:
Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat.  Add onion and cook till translucent, about 8 minutes.  Add garlic and ginger and cook for a minute or two.  Add stock and carrots.  Cover and simmer until carrots are tender, 30 minutes or more.
Let cool and puree in blender or food processor.  (Or leave in pan and use immersion blender to puree.)  Return to pan and add Coconut Milk and spices.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.  Enjoy!
