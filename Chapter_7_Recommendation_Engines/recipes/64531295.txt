Title:
Cheese waffers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbs. Graded cheese
1 tsp. Softened butter
1 tbs. Flour
1 tbs. Whole wheat flour
A pinch if poppy seeds (optional)

Directions:
Put the butter, flour and cheese in a bowl
Mix until blended
Roll out 4-6 balls of dough
Put them on a cookie sheet and push them down gently
Sprinkle on poppy seeds
Bake at 375 degrees

Submitter: 
Izzy Fiona
Description: 
These bread-like balls of yummyness are perfect for a Sunday morning breakfast with eggs and bacon. They are also great with jam.
