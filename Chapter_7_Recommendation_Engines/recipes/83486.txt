Deviled Eggs Marzetti速 Style Recipe

Ingredients:
12 eggs, hard boiled and shelled
1/2 cup Marzetti速 Slaw Dressing
3 tablespoons classic yellow mustard
Salt and pepper to taste
Paprika, parsley or ground pepper

Directions:
Cool eggs and slice in half.  Remove yolks and put in bowl; set whites aside.  Add remaining ingredients to yolks.  Mix until smooth.  Salt and pepper to taste.
Fill egg white shells with yolk mixture.  Sprinkle with paprika, parsley or ground pepper.
