Title:
Made up Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
3 large eggs
6 pieces bread
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup dale's sauce (or approximate sub.)
1/2 cup dried onions
ketchup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Combine beef, eggs, soy sauce, worcestershire sauce, dale's sauce and onions in large bowl.  Mix with hands until very well blended. There so not be large bread clumps in the mix.  Divide mixture into 6 evenly sized loaves.  Place on foil covered baking sheet.  Cook for 30 minutes. Remove from oven and put ketchup on top of each loaf then place back in oven for an additional 15 minutes.  Serve with mashed potatoes for an old fashioned family favorite.

Submitter: 
CHRISTIE612
Description: 
A savory homestyle meatloaf.  The individual servings make it easy to serve and reheat.
