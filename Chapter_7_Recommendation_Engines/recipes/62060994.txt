Title:
Party Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
1 2-1/2 oz. pkg. dried beef
1 can of cream of mushroom soup
1 cup sour cream

Directions:
Preheat over to 350.  Grease a 9x13 baking dish.  Line the bottom with the dried beef.  Place chicken on top of dried beef.  Mix sour cream with soup and pour over chicken.  Cover and cook for approx. 1-1/2 hours.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
The saltiness of the dried beef and the richness of the sour cream gives the chicken an added punch.  This is great served over rice.
