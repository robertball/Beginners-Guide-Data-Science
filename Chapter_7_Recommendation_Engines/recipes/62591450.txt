Title:
Quick & Easy Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871604.jpg
Ingredients:
2 frozen pie crusts
16 oz egg beaters
16 oz 2% milk
2 cups shredded cheese
1 pound cooked sausage

Directions:
Cook sausage. In small bowl, combine egg beaters and milk. Layer shredded cheese and sausage in pie crust. Top with egg beaters combo.
Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
