Title:
Scrambled Peanut Butter Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
2 tablespoons peanut butter
2 teaspoons fresh garlic, minced
2 teaspoons fresh ginger, minced
1 teaspoon Maggi sauce or soy sauce
Sriracha sauce to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in small bowl.
Put mixture in medium skillet.
Scramble on medium heat until eggs are firm.,
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Sue Forster
Description: 
My husband grew up in West Africa where peanut butter stew was popular, so he  started wondering if peanut butter would go with eggs.  The result was this recipe.  It was a hit!
