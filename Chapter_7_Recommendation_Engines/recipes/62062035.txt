Title:
South by Southwest Black-eyed Pea Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large cucumbers, peeled, seeded and diced.
3 green onions, thinly sliced
1 (15 oz.)can of black-eyed peas, drain and rinse
1 (11 oz.)can Green Giant Mexicorn,drained
1 1/4 cups Chunky Salsa, your choice of spiceness
If using for a salad:
1 head of Iceberg Lettuce, torn into bite-size pieces
2 medium diced tomatoes
1/3 cup shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
In mixing bowl, combine all ingredients. Toss well. Store in refrigerator until ready to serve. To make into a salad: In salad bowl, combine lettuce bits, tomato, and black-eyed pea mixture; toss and sprinkle with cheese.

Submitter: 
CAROLINECOOKS
Description: 
Terrific as chunky dip or as a salad with Mexican food. Lots of color as well. Enjoy!
