Title:
Chicken Dumpling Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. of chopped chicken
1 cup of chopped carrots
1 cup of peas
1 cup of celery
1/3 cup butter
2 Tbs dried onions
1/3 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp celery seed
1 3/4 cups chicken broth
2/3 cup milk
1 roll of biscuits

Directions:
Combine veggies and cubed chicken in a pan and saute until veggies are tender and the chicken is cooked through.
Transfer mixture to a casserole dish.
Melt the butter in the same pan.
Stir in flour, salt, pepper, celery seed, and dried onion
Slowly add milk and chicken broth and let thicken.
Once liquid mixture is thick, add to the casserole dish until the liquid is almost as high as the chicken and vegetables.
Cut up biscuits into 1 inch pieces and cover the veggies
Bake for 30 minutes at 350 Degrees until biscuits have raised and browned.

Submitter: 
Lindsey
Description: 
This is more like a chicken dumpling casserole.  My mother used to make something very similar for me when I was a kid.
