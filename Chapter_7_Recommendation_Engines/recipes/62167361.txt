Title:
Enchilda Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 8 oz. sour cream
1 can refried beans
1 tsp. hot sauce
1 envelope Enchilada seasoning
4 oz. Monterry Jack cheese, split
4 oz. cheddar cheese, split
1 small can green chilies (optional)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well.  Pour into large pie pan or two small pans.  Top with half of cheeses.
Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes or until bubbly.
Serve with your favorite tortilla chips.
Options:  More cheese can be added either to dip or on top.  Jalapenos are also a great addition.  Non-fat or low-fat ingredients can also be used.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Miskiewicz
Description: 
I have been making this recipe for about 20 years and it is a big hit whenever I serve it.  It is my daughter's favorite appitizer.  The recipe is so easy to make and very very versitle.  It can be made using all low-fat to non-fat ingredients with great results.
