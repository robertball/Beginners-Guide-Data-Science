Title:
Baileys Almande Dairy-free Almondiest Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6049896.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup almond milk
1 tablespoon cocoa powder
2 fluid ounces Baileys Almande
Optional: dairy-free whipped topping or slivered almonds

Directions:
Heat 1 cup of almond milk on the stove or in the microwave until steaming. Mix cocoa powder into hot almond milk.
Pour mixture into mug before adding 2 ounces of Baileys Almande.
Garnish with dairy-free whipped topping or slivered almonds if desired.

Submitter: 
Baileys
Description: 
The ultimate dairy-free almond hot chocolate made with light-tasting, dairy-free Baileys Almande.
