Title:
Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb potatoes, peeled and quartered
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
1 tbsp Johnny's seasoning salt
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup milk more or less to consistency
1-1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 pound lean ground beef
2 cloves garlic, minced
15 ounce frozen or 2 cans mixed vegetables
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of celery soup
1 tsp johnny's
2-3 cups sharp cheddar cheese
Paprika to garnish
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (175 degrees C). Coat a 9x13 baking dish with cooking spray.
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook potatoes in boiling water until tender, about 15 minutes. Drain, Mash potatoes by themselves until they are smooth add heavy whipping cream, butter, johnnys and garlic salt. Add Milk to consistency
In a large skillet, add garlic, cook ground beef until brown over medium-high heat. Drain fat from pan. Stir mixed vegetables, mushroom soup, celery soup, and salt and pepper; heat through. Pour into prepared baking dish, cover with mashed potatoes, and sprinkle on top. Dash paprika on the very top
Bake in preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until cheese is golden.
