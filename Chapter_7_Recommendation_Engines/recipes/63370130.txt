Title:
Slow Cooker Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 eggs
1 diced onion
1 diced red pepper
1/2 pound sliced mushrooms
4 medium potatoes
1 cup cheddar cheese (or your favorite cheese)
salt and pepper

Directions:
Spray slow cooker with baking spray.  Place sliced potatoes on bottom of slow cooker. Whisk the eggs and add the onion, red peppers, mushrooms, and salt and pepper to taste. Pour the egg mixture over the potatoes.  Sprinkle the cheese over the mix.  Cook on high for 3 to 4 hours on high.

Submitter: 
groucho
Description: 
This is a delicious casserole that does not take long to bake in the slow cooker. It is great for a weekend morning. You can add cooked bacon or ham if you love meat.
