Title:
Blast-Off Blood Mary Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
4 cups chilled tomato juice
1 1/3 cups vodka
3 lemons, juiced
1 1/2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 tablespoons prepared horseradish, drained
3/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce (such as TabascoÂ®)
1/2 teaspoon celery salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Stir tomato juice, vodka, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, horseradish, hot pepper sauce, celery salt, and black pepper together in a pitcher.

Description: 
This is the ultimate bloody mary! After several tries and a few not-so-good-next-mornings, I perfected this yummy concoction! Serve chilled with big green olives and/or celery stalks and a smile! Try pepper-infused vodka for an even spicier concoction.
