Title:
Crusty European Bread! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6034449.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Cups all purpose flour
1 T salt
1 T yeast
4 Cups Water

Directions:
Place all dry ingredients in bowl, stir.
Add room temperature water,  stir.
Consistency is looser then traditional Bread dough.
Cover (I cover with "press and seal plastic wrap"), and set on counter overnight.
Next morning
Using either a dutch oven or a large crockpot with lid, place it in oven with lid on.
Pre-heat oven and crock pot or dutch oven too 400 degrees fahrenheit.
Carefully remove hot cooking vessel from oven
Remove lid
Pour in bread dough.  (It sometimes has thick soup consistency but thats ok)
Place lid on cooking vessel.
Put cooking vessel back into the oven with lid on.
Bake for 30 minutes, at 400 degrees fahrenheit.
Remove lid, bake another 15 minutes, at 400 degrees fahrenheit, until golden brown.
Remove from oven and cooking dish to let cool.
