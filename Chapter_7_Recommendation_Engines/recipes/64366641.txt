Title:
Primal Cravings Shrimp Pad Thai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2164652.jpg
Ingredients:
butter (or coconut oil) for the pan
1-1.5 lb of Shrimp
1 lb zucchini (or more if you like lots of noodles)
1 onion, diced
4 garlic cloves minced
1 TB fresh minced ginger
1 TB apple cider vinegar
3 tp almond butter
1 tp Sriracha or chili garlic sauce
1 tp fish sauce
2 TB lime juice
salt to taste

Directions:
1 with a mandoline (or julienne peeler), slice the zucchinis lengthwide into think plank/noodles.
2 Over medium heat, melt a TB of butter (oil) and add shrimp and saute til pink. Remove and set aside.
3 in same pan, add more butter/oil if needed and saute the onion, garlic and ginger until soft.
4 Add vinegar, almond butter, chili sauce, fish sauce, lime juice and salt. Stir to combine.
5 Add zucchini noodles to saute pan. Stir until sauce is fully integrated. Cook for about 5 min to heat and soften noodles.
6 Add shrimp back and stir to combine.
7 Serve

Description: 
The recipe is from the PALEO cookbook PRIMAL CRAVINGS. A great no grain / no peanut Pad Thai alternative. Not spicy.. if want spicier.. add more chili
