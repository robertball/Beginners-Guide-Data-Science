Title:
Peppermint-Kissed Fudge Mallow Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/747398.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup reduced-fat plain yogurt
5 TBS butter, melted
3/4 tsp peppermint extract
1 cup all-purpose flour
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup baking cocoa
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking soda
12 large marshmallows, cut in half lengthwise
Glaze:
2 TBS semisweet chocolate chips
3/4 cups confectioners' sugar
3 TBS baking cocoa
3 TBS fat-free milk
1/4 tsp peppermint extract
1/4 cup crushed peppermint candies

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
In a large bowl, beat yogurt, butter, and extract until well blended. In separate bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa, salt, and baking soda; gradually add to yogurt mixture and mix well.
Drop by tablespoonfuls onto baking sheets coated with cooking spray. Bake for 8 minutes.
Place a marshmallow half on each cookie; bake 1-2 minutes longer or until marshmallow is puffed.
Cool for 2 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks to cool completely.
For glaze, in a microwave, melt chocolate chips; stir until smooth. Stir in confectioners' sugar, cocoa, milk, and extract until smooth. Drizzle over marshmallows; sprinkle with candies.
