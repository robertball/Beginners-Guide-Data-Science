Title:
Mexican Manicotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound very lean ground beef
1 16 oz. can refried beans
3 tsp. chile powder
1 1/2 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp garlic powder
1 8 ounce pkg. manicotti shells
2 1/2 cups water
2 1/2 cups Pace picante sauce
2 cups sour creme
2 cups shredded cheddar, jack or mexican blend cheese
sliced black olives

Directions:
Mix the water and Pace picante sauce together and pour 2 cups in the bottom of a 13x9 inch pan.
Mix the uncooked beef with the refried beans and all spices until thoroughly mixed and spoon into uncooked manicotti shells.  (kids can help with this) Layer into pan.  Pour remaining Pace and water mixture over top and refrigerate overnight or at least 8 hours.  Remove from the refrigeraator 30 minutes before baking.  Cover with foil and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.  Uncover, spoon sour creme on top, cover with grated cheese and olives. Bake another 5 minutes to melt the cheese.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Vicky Gunkel
Description: 
Prepare the night before, this is easy enough for kids to help put together and so very good.  All you have to do is serve along side rice and salad and you have a complete delicious meal. Its perfect for entertaining or pot luck dinners.  This dish will become a regular at your table.  All 7 of my children give it a thumbs up.  My husband wants the leftovers if there is any.
