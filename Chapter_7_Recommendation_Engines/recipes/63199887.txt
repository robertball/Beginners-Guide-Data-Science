Title:
Seared tuna with oregano crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lb Tuna steaks (1-1.5) cm thick
4-5 sprigs fresh oregano
2 cloves garlic
Teaspoon sea salt
10 pepper corns
1 T olive oil

Directions:
Smash up oregano, garlic, salt and pepper to a paste, and then add oil. Stir to mix.
Coat tuna on both sides.
Place on to well oiled, super hot grill.
Cook 1 minute per side.
Eat!
