Title:
Big Ray's Grilled Huli Huli Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3594204.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
3 Tbsp ketchup
3 Tbsp cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
2 Tbsp cup sherry or chicken broth
3/4 tsp minced fresh gingerroot
1/2 tsp minced garlic
8 boneless skinless chicken thighs (about 5 pounds)

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix the first six ingredients. Reserve 1/2 cup for basting; cover and refrigerate. Divide remaining marinade large resealable plastic bags. Add  chicken thighs; seal bags and turn to coat. Refrigerate for 8 hours or overnight.
Drain and discard marinade from chicken. Moisten a paper towel with cooking oil; using long-handled tongs, lightly coat the grill rack.
Grill chicken, covered, over medium heat for 6-8 minutes on each side or until no longer pink; baste occasionally with reserved marinade during the last 5 minutes. Yield

Description: 
I got this grilled chicken recipe from a friend that always to be on the cutting edge when it comes to grilling chicken. It sizzles with the flavors of brown sugar, ginger and soy sauce. Huli means turn in Hawaiian.
