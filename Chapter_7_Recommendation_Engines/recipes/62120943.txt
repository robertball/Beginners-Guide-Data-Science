Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Enchilada Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 package flour tortillas
1 lb. ground chicken or shredded chicken
1 jar salsa
1 can diced tomatoes w/ green chilies
1 can enchilada sauce
1 can tomato paste
1 can black beans
1 can whole kernel corn
1 cup cheddar cheese
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp chili powder
Sprinkle cayenne pepper (to taste)
Sour cream (optional)

Directions:
In a large pot sautee chicken and black beans until meat is no longer pink and beans are slightly tender.
Add salsa, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, beans, corn, and spices and mix well.
Cover the bottom of the crock pot with tomato and meat mixture.  Then cover the layer with 1-1 1/2 tortillas depending on crock pot.  Continue alternating meat mixture with tortillas until crock pot reaches 3/4 of the way full.  End with meat and tomato mixture on top.
Cook on low heat for 6 hours.  When serving, garnish with cheddar cheese and sour cream.
*NOTE: Be sure not to overfill the crock pot as the enchildas will expand during cooking.

Submitter: 
Lee Ann Girard
Description: 
This is a easy dish that can be assembled in the morning and left to simmer all day long.
