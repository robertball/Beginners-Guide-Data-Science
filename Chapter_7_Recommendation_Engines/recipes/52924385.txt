Title:
Amaretto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
2 cups white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 1/2 cups vodka
2 tablespoons almond extract
2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine water, white sugar and brown sugar in a saucepan over medium heat. Heat until boiling and all sugar is dissolved. Continue to boil 5 minutes to thicken. Remove from heat and let mixture cool for about 10 minutes. Pour in vodka, almond extract and vanilla. Mix well and store in a sealed bottle.
