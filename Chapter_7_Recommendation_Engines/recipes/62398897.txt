Title:
Mexican Spoon Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16oz can cream corn
3/4 cup milk
1/3 c vegetable oil
2 eggs
1 c cornmeal
1/2 t baking soda
1 t salt
1 4 oz can chopped green chili
1 c shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Beat all together until well mixed. Pour mixture into a casserole dish or iron skillet. Top with 1 cup cheese. Bake at 400 for 45 minutes or until knife comes clean.
