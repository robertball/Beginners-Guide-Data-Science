Title:
Ham Cheddar & Hashbrown Hotdish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4501004.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 lb. ham diced
1 30 oz. package frozen shredded hashbrowns
1/2 c. melted butter
2 c. shredded cheddar cheese, reserve 1/2 c. for topping
8 oz. sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray 9 x 13 pan with cooking spray.
Stir all ingredients together, place in baking pan.
Sprinkle with remaining cheddar cheese.
Cover with foil.
Bake for 1 hour.
Remove foil for last 10 minutes of baking.

Submitter: 
Jen Cooks For Fun
Description: 
This is a quick and easy hotdish that is ooy gooy cheesy delicious! I make it in two 9x9 in. pans and freeze one for later.
