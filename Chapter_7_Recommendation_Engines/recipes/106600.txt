Title:
Backyard Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977832.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 pound) package Bob Evans® Original Recipe Sausage Roll
1 pound ground beef
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/3 teaspoon ground pepper
8 hamburger buns
8 lettuce leaves (optional)
8 tomato slices (optional)
8 onion slices (optional)

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine first five ingredients. Form into 8 patties. Over medium heat on grill, cook burgers on each side until done (no longer pink), about 10 to 15 minutes. Serve with buns and favorite toppings.

Submitter: 
Bob Evans®
Description: 
Big, juicy sausage and beef burgers with Parmesan cheese are the greatest! Serve with your favorite toppings.
