Title:
Four Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef (browned and drained)
1 chopped onion
1.5 Tblspn Chili powder
1 tsp cumin
1.5 tsp oregano
1 tsp cocoa powder
1 15 oz can Hunt's tomato sauce
1 15 oz can kidney beans
1 15 oz can black beans
1 15 oz can great northern beans
1 15 oz can pinto beans

Directions:
brown and drain ground beef in pan. Add onion and saut√© till slightly soft then put in spices and cook about a minute. Add tomato sauce and beans. Do not drain the beans before adding. Simmer all together for 15 minutes to half hour. Serve on rice. The seasonings come from Chile Con Carne recipe in Betty Crocker cookbook.
