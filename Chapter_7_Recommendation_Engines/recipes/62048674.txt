Title:
Light Blue Cheese Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz soft tofu, mashed
1 Tbs white wine vinegar
1 Tbs lemon juice
1 Tbs olive oil (optional)
1/4 cup blue cheese crumbles
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
Dash cayenne pepper (more if desired)

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a bowl and mix until smooth or use a hand blender.  I enjoyed it on a salad of sprouts, mixed greens, and green olives.

Submitter: 
bawkbagawk
Description: 
This is one of the best versions I've had and no I'm not a big low cal-low fat eater but this was a nice surprise.  This is practically healthy!
