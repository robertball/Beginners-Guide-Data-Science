Title:
Chocolate Chip Pecan Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, melted
2 cups sugar
1 tablespoon instant coffee
6 ripe bananas, mashed into large and small pieces
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon cinnamon
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking powder
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
Â½ cup chopped pecans
2 tablespoons melted butter
2/3 cup brown sugar
4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Dissolve the coffee in the 1 cup of melted butter as best you can before adding the sugar.
Stir the butter, coffee, and sugar mixture together with the bananas, eggs, 1 teaspoon of cinnamon, and vanilla extract.
In a separate bowl, combine the 3 cups of flour, salt, baking powder, baking soda, and chocolate chips.
Combine the wet and dry ingredients, without over-mixing. Fold in the pecans.
Spoon mixture into 30 paper-lined muffin cups.
In a small bowl, mix together the 2 tablespoons of melted butter, brown sugar, 4 tablespoons of flour, and 1 teaspoons of cinnamon.
Sprinkle brown sugar toppings over muffins.
Bake 25 minutes at 350 degrees.
