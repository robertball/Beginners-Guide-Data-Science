Title:
Rockland Gazpacho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lb. tomatoes, skinned & chopped
1 cucumber, peeled and chopped
1/3 carrot
1/2 cup basil
1 bunch chives
1/4 cup cilantro
3 stalks celery
5 stalks green onions, chopped
2 tbsp. regular vinegar
3 tbsp. balsamic vinegar
3 tbsp. olive oil
1 tbsp. hot pepper paste
salt and pepper to taste
(bonus: 1/4 red bell pepper, seeded and chopped)

Directions:
Combine the vegetables in a blender; blend until smooth. Add the vinegars, hot pepper paste and olive oil; blend again until well integrated. Season with salt and pepper to serve.
