Title:
Bacon Cheeseburger Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988395.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 3/4 cups water
2/3 cup barbecue sauce
1 tablespoon prepared mustard
2 teaspoons dried minced onion
1/2 teaspoon pepper
2 cups uncooked instant rice
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/3 cup chopped dill pickle
5 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled

Directions:
In a large saucepan over medium heat, cook the beef until no longer pink; drain. Add water, barbecue sauce, mustard, onion and pepper. Bring to a boil; stir in the rice. Sprinkle with cheese. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 5 minutes. Sprinkle with pickles and bacon.

Submitter: 
Joyce  Whipps
Description: 
'My husband and I thought the original skillet dish lacked pizzazz, so we created this tastier version,' reports Joyce Whipps of West Des Moines, Iowa. 'I've had teenage nieces and nephews request the recipe after their first bite.'
