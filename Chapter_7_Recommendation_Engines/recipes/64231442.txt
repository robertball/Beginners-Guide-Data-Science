Title:
Flat snacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1691657.jpg
Ingredients:
1 corn tortilla, hand full of baby kale, mustard, black pepper, a slice of deli ham or turkey

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees, slice corn tortilla and place each on a non stick baking sheet or aluminum pan drizzled with olive oil.  Cut ham or turkey slice in half and place each on top of tortillas, then top each evenly with kale.  drizzle mustard on each half, then sprinkle black pepper.  Place in the oven for 5-7 minutes, or until kale looks crispy.  Let cool for a few minutes and enjoy!
