Title:
Banana Macadamia Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup buttermilk complete pancake mix
1 ripe banana
water
2 T Macadamia Nuts, Toasted
2 teaspoons butter
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1/4 cup pancake syrup
whipped cream, optional

Directions:
Preheat nonstick skillet over medium heat.  Mash 1/2 of banana until completely smooth.  Slice the other half of banana and set aside.
Measure out pancake mix in bowl.  Add banana and just enough water to make desired pancake consistency.
In glass measuring cup add butter, brown sugar and syrup.  Microwave up to a minute until syrup bubbles and brown sugar is melted.  Stir well and set aside.
Pour 1/2 of batter into skillet.  Sprinkle top with 1/2 of macadamia nuts.  Cook until bubbles form on top and begin to pop.  Flip and continue cooking until done.  Repeat with remaining batter.
Top pancakes with sliced bananas, whipped cream if desired and syrup.
Serve immediately.
