Title:
Savory Hamburger Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb of ground beef
4 carrots, sliced
1 small green pepper, diced
1 med. onion, diced
4 celery stalks, sliced
1/2 a large green cabbage, coarse chopped
7 cups beef broth
4 cups spicy V8 juice
1 T sloppy joe seasoning
1 t coarse salt
1 t ground black pepper
1 14.5 oz can petite diced tomatoes
1 11 oz can corn
1 pkg cooked pasta, any shape

Directions:
Brown the ground beef. Sprinkle with the salt and black pepper while cooking.  Set aside.  In large stock pot combine carrots, celery, onion, green pepper, cabbage, and sloppy joe seasoning.  Pour 4 cups of the beef broth and all the V8 juice over the vegetables.  Bring to a boil then cook on med low for 45 minutes.  Add can tomatoes, corn, the ground beef, and additional 3 cups of beef broth.   Simmer on low for 15 minutes.  Serve in bowls with 1/2 to 1c cooked pasta.

Submitter: 
Audrey Ansell
Description: 
This is a hearty cold weather soup with just enough spice to warm you on a cold night.  Perfect by itself or with a nice crusty roll.  Comfort food at its best!
