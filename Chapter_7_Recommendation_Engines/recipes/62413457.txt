Title:
Slow Cooker Pork Chop Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/439433.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs boneless pork loin chops
1/2 cup favorite chunky salsa
1 White onion sliced
2 tablespoon siracha sauce
Juice of 1 lime
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Place all ingredients into slow cooker on low for 8-9 hours.
Shred meat when done and serve with tortillas.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
Came up with this recipe because I got sick of plain old pork chops.  Its great topped with nopales.
