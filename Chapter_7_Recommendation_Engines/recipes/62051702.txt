Title:
cucumbers in sour cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 large cucumbers (sliced as thin as possible) Tip: use a mandolin to slice paper thin
4 slices sweet onion (thinly sliced)
1 tablespoon salt
1 cup sour cream (reduced fat is fine)
1/3 cup white vinegar
1 heaping tablespoon sugar
1 heaping tablespoon dill weed

Directions:
Place sliced cucumbers, onion and salt in large bowl, mix together and set aside. In small bowl mix together sour cream, vinegar, sugar and dill. Squeeze out all liquid from cucumber mix and blot dry with paper towel. Add sour cream mixture to cucumbers and chill.

Submitter: 
MISTIDAWN
Description: 
This is my grandmothers recipe and as authentic as the polish recipe you can get! Very easy and quick too. Always a hit with anyone who loves cucumbers and sour cream!
