Title:
Cherry Blast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/654728.jpg
Ingredients:
17 fresh cherries, pitted
6 pineapple rings

Directions:
Place the cherries and pineapple rings in a blender. Puree until smooth; pour into 2 glasses filled with ice.

Submitter: 
Elizabeth
Description: 
An accidental discovery of cherry and pineapple combination. On even hotter days, try freezing in popsicle molds for cold, fruity treats.
