Title:
Peanut Butter and Honey Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/472180.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons peanut butter
1 teaspoon honey
1 teaspoon butter, softened
2 slices bread toasted
2 Tablespoons of trail mix

Directions:
Stir the peanut butter, honey, and butter together in a bowl until smooth; spread on the toast slices and sprinkle each with 1 tablespoon of trail mix.  Enjoy this as a snack or as breakfast.

Submitter: 
Mrs. CJR
Description: 
Honey, peanut butter, and butter are mixed together to spread on toast.  Add some trail mix on top and enjoy this high energy snack.Modified from: Peanut Butter and Honey Sandwich
