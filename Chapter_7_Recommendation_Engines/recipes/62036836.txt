Title:
Jelly Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. LEAN ground beef
1 egg beaten
1/2 cup. fine bread crumbs
3 tbsp chopped parsley
1/2 cup onion chopped
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. worchestershire sauce
1/4 tsp. pepper
SAUCE
1 - 12 oz. bottle chili sauce
1 - 10 oz. jar grape jelly
1 tsp. lemon juuice
2 tbsp brown sugar
1 tbsp. soya sauce

Directions:
Mix first 8 ingredients.
Roll into 1 inch balls.
Heat chili sauce, jelly, lemon juice, brown sugar and soysauce in a parge pot.  Bring to a boil and add uncooked meatballs,
Simmer meat balls in sauce for 30 minutes.  Serve hot in chafing dish.  Makes about 40 balls.  This freezes well.

Delicious.  A sweet bar-b-que flavor.  Always a hit.  Can be an appetizer or a main dish.  Kids love them.
