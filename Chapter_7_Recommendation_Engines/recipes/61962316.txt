Title:
Beef Florentine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups medium egg noodles
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1 tsp. oregano
1/2 tsp. salt
ground black pepper
2(8 oz.)cans tomato sauce
1/2 cup water
1(10 oz.)frozen chopped spinach,
thawed and drained
1(8 oz.)container cottage cheese
1 small chopped onion
8 oz. shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add the noodles, and cook for 7 to 9 minutes, or until not quite al dente. Drain. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef; drain. Season with oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir in the tomato sauce and water. Remove from heat, and stir in the cooked noodles.
In a medium bowl, combine spinach, cottage cheese, onion, and Parmesan cheese. Spread half of noodle mixture in a 13x9x2-inch casserole dish. Layer with all of the spinach mixture, then cover with the remaining noodle mixture.
Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes. Cover with the mozzarella and continue cooking for 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted.
