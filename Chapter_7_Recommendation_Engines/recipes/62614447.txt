Title:
Brown Sugar Bacon Wrapped Weenies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Weenies
1 lb. bacon
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 stick butter

Directions:
Cut weenies into 1 inch long segments
Cut bacon slices into 4 equal lengths (will be easier if firmed up in the freezer for about 20-30 minutes ahead of time)
Wrap each weenie segment with the bacon and insert a toothpick to hold together
Broil on a cookie sheet, turning once or twice, until bacon is crisp.
Melt butter and brown sugar together in a sauce pot
Pull weenies out of broiler and pour on the brown sugar butter mixture.......ENJOY.
