Title:
Tropical Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1080692.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups pineapple-orange-banana juice
2 scoops vanilla ice cream

Directions:
In a blender, combine pineapple-orange-banana juice and ice cream. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
DOGREN
Description: 
A refreshing summer treat. Pineapple-orange-banana juice is blended with vanilla ice cream in this simple creamy concoction.
