Title:
Vegetable Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs, hard-boiled
1 bunch green onions, minced, white and some green parts
1 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp. Dijon mustard
3 tbs. extra virgin olive oil
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp. fresh parsley, minced
dash of tabasco
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. fresh ground pepper
paprika for topping

Directions:
In a sauce pan, cover the eggs halfway in cold water, then boil them for 2 minutes. Cover and remove from the stove. After 10 minutes, run the eggs under cold water, remove the shells, and finely chop them.
Mince the onion and parsley and combine with the rest of the ingredients.
Serve room temperature over hot vegetables, sprinkling a little paprika over the sauce.

Description: 
This is a great, easy to prepare  sauce for green vegetables and cauliflower.
