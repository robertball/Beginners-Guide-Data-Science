Title:
Best Ever, Easy Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups cottage cheese
1 1b. boneless, skinless, cubed chicken breast
1 onion, chopped
2 tbls. butter or margarine
1 package 8 inch flour tortillas
1 small can of tomato sauce
1 can enchilada sauce
1 can rotel tomatoes (mexican style is best)
3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
1. Saute onion and chicken in 2 tbls. of butter or margarine.  Remove from heat.
2.  Mix cottage cheese with sauteed onion and chicken.  Set aside.
3.  In a medium size bowl, combine enchilada sauce, tomato sauce, and rotel tomatoes.  Stir until blended.
4.  Begin assembling enchiladas by placing 2-3 tbls. chicken mixture in the center of one tortilla.  Sprinkle 2 tbls. mozzarella cheese on chicken, and drizzle 2-3 tbls. enchilada sauce over cheese.
5.  Roll up tortilla.  Place it seam side down in a buttered 9X13 pan.
6.  Repeat process until you run out of chicken mixture.
7.  Pour remaining sauce over enchiladas in pan.
8.  Top with remaining mozzarella cheese.
9.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
