Title:
Peach Glazed Babybacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slabs babyback ribs
1 small jar peach preserves
2 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
1 tsp. Dijon mustard
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Salt and pepper ribs and place in roaster with about one inch of water.
Cover and bake for two hours.
At this point ribs could be refrigerated until ready to grill.
In saucepan, melt together preserves, lemon juice, mustard, cinnamon and salt and pepper.
Remove from heat.
Heat grill and place ribs on grill, brush with peach glaze.
Heat about 15 minutes, turning and adding more glaze till heated through.
Remove from grill and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Cathy H.
Description: 
Different and absolutely delicious. Pre-baked in oven- then a few minutes on the grill to heat them up and glaze!
