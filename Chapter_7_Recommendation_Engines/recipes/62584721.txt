Title:
Jen's Easy Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Bacon
8 Eggs
1 cup Cream
1/8 tsp White Pepper
3/4 tsp Salt
1/3 cup Green onions
1/2 cup Swiss cheese
1/2 cup small curd cottage cheese
1/2 cup frozen chopped spinach (thawed)
1 9 inch uncooked pastry shell
Dried parsley
Paprika

Directions:
Cook bacon and chop finely. While bacon is cooking chop the green onion.
Lightly beat eggs, cream, pepper and salt. Add cheese, bacon and onion and mix.
Fill the pastry shell mixture. Sprinkle dried parsley and paprika on the top of mixture.
Bake at 425 for 15min. Then at 300 for 30min.

Submitter: 
Jeniflower
Description: 
I typically make my own pastry crust for the yummy treat, but a store bought crust works just as well.
