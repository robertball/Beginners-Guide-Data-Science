Title:
Pizza Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. pork sausage
3/4 box of spaghetti noodles
1 can of mushrooms
1 white onion
1 green pepper
2 cups mozzarella cheese
2 cans of pizza sauce
(add anything else that you like on your pizza)

Directions:
Cook the spaghetti noodles. Drain and set aside.
Cook the sausage. Drain and then put it back on the stove. Add in the onion & green pepper. Let them get soft then add in the pizza sauce, noodles and all the other pizza toppings. Mix all these ingrediants together.
Put this mixture into a glass baking dish and top with mozzarella cheese. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes on 350, or until the cheese is melted.
Enjoy!
