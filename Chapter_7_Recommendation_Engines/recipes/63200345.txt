Title:
Baked Spaghetti (Custom) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/827283.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti
2 pound ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
1 pepper (red, green, orange or yellow)
1 (26 ounce) jar meatless spaghetti sauce
1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt
2 eggs
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
5 tablespoons butter, melted
2 cups sour cream
4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, cook beef, pepper and onion over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain. Stir in the spaghetti sauce and seasoned salt; set aside.
In a large bowl, whisk the eggs, Parmesan cheese and butter. Drain spaghetti; add to egg mixture and toss to coat.
Place half of the spaghetti mixture in a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish. Top with sour creeme, meat sauce and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers. Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 40 minutes. Uncover; bake 20-25 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.
