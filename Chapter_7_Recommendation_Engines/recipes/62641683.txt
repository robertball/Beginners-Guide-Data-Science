Title:
Crispy Blackened Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
1/8 teaspoon ground white pepper
1/8 teaspoon onion powder
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1 skinless, boneless chicken breast
2 Tbsp vegetable oil

Directions:
Heat a cast iron skillet to medium high
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Combine all the spices into a small bowl
Coat chicken in a thin layer of vegetable oil and sprinkle with spice mixture on both sides
Add oil to the skillet and heat until just smokey, then add chicken
Cook on each side for one minute, then transfer to the oven and bake for 5-10 minutes (depending on thickness of breast)
