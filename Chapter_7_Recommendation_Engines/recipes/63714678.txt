Title:
Paleo Pizza crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup almond flour
3/4 cup arrowroot powder
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp salt
1 tsp oregano
1/8 tsp black pepper
2 eggs
1/3 cup unsweetened almond milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees and grease a cookie sheet or pizza pan. Combine dry ingredients in a large bowl, whisking to combine.
Add eggs and almond milk to dry ingredients. Mix well.
Use spatula to spread batter onto greased pan and bake in preheated oven for 8-12 minutes.
Remove crust from oven and top with sauce and desired toppings. Bake for an additional 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Nichoel Casey
Description: 
This is a dairy free, gluten free recipe for a pizza crust. It always comes out light and flaky. This dough has also worked to make meat pies and tarts.
