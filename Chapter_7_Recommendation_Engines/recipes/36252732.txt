Title:
Classic Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups medium egg noodles
16 oz can of albacore tuna, drained and flaked
1 cup frozen peas
3/4 cup onion finely chopped
10-1/2 oz can Cream of Mushroom Soup
10-1/2 oz can Cream of Celery Soup
1/3 cup milk
1.2 tsp black pepper
1/4 lb. Saltine crackers (1 sleeve) crushed
1 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Cook the egg noodles according to package directions.
Drain the noodles and place in a large bowl.  Add the tuna, peas and onion and toss to combine.
In a medium bowl, whisk together the mushroom and celery soups, milk and pepper.
Pour over the noodle texture, carefully mixing all the liquid throughout the noodles.
Spread the mixture evenly into a lightly buttered shallow 2 qt. casserole.
Sprinkle the crumbled saltines and parmesan over the top.
Bake until the casserole is piping hot throughout and the top is golden, about 45 minutes.  Let cool slightly before serving.
