Title:
Raspberry Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter (no substitutes), softened
3 - 4 cups confectioners' sugar
1 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/2 fresh raspberries

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and raspberries. Beat in vanilla. Add sugar 1 cup at a time and beat well after each addition until desired consistency is achieved.

Submitter:
