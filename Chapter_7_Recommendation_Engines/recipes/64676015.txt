Title:
Drunken Mussels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
4 cloves of garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon red pepper
1 lemon, zested
2 cups white wine
2 pounds of mussels, cleaned
1 cup chopped parsley

Directions:
Melt butter, add garlic
Add red pepper and zest
Pour in wine
Bring to boil
Stir in mussels and cover
Shake and boil 3 minutes
Stir in parsley
*******Discard unopened mussels
