Title:
Party Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14.5 to 16 oz.) can stewed tomatoes, well drained
1 medium onion, quartered
10 large garlic cloves
3 jalapeno peppers, stemmed and seeded
1 bunch cilantro, cleaned & stems trimmed, divided
4 large tomatoes, seeded and quartered
1 (4 oz.) can diced green chilies
6 large green onions, chopped
1 tablespoon cumin
3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
4 teaspoons fresh lime juice

Directions:
Process stewed tomatoes, onion, garlic cloves, jalapenos and half of the cilantro in a food processor to a chunky puree.
Transfer mixture to a large serving bowl.
Place tomatoes, diced green chilies and remaining cilantro in food processor and blend until tomatoes are finely chopped.   Add to mixture in large bowl.
Mix in by hand the green onions, cumin, and lemon and lime juices.
Season salsa to taste with salt and pepper.
Cover and chill until cold at least 1 hour and up to 4 hours.
