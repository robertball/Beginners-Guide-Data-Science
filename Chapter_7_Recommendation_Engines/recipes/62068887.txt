Title:
Spiced Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
3/4 cup brown suger
1 egg
1 1/2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
3 bananas, mashed
1/4 cup plain yogurt
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cream butter and sugar. With electric mixer beat in egg. Set aside. Sift together flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon and cloves. Combine bananas and yogurt. Add to butter mixture alternating with dry ingredients. Pour into greased loaf pan and bake for 1 hour.
