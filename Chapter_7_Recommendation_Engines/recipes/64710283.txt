Title:
Slow Cooker Spicy Cream Corn Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5567738.jpg
Ingredients:
1lb whole kernel corn frozen
1 8 oz. package cream cheese; cubed
1 8 oz. package sharp cheddar cheese; shredded
2 large jalapenos; seeded and finely diced
1/4 tsp. onion powder
1/4 tsp. garlic; fresh minced (or powder can be used)
1/2 tsp. kosher salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper
Optional: Chili pepper for topping or thinly sliced seeded Habanero (for extra heat)
Optional: 1 cup cooked chopped bacon for topping (since everything is better with bacon)
Tortilla chips to serve with

Directions:
Place whole corn and diced/chopped jalapeno in he bottom of slow cooker (I use a liner for ease of cleaning).
Add rest of seasoning ingredients.
Spread cheese on top of corn mixture, then top with cubed cream cheese;mix well.
Cover and slow cook on high for 2 hours or on low for 4 hours.
Add chopped bacon just before finished cooking.
Stir together until mixture melted and serve hot with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Sahara B
Description: 
This slow cooker spicy cream corn dip has always been a favorite of my friends. We add extra heat so you may wish to adjust heat index to your taste.  I also add bacon since everything is better with bacon. Simple, easy and never a boring dip to serve.
