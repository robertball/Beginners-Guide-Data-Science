Title:
Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 cup chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup chopped carrot
1/4 cup chopped celery
2 (28 ounce) cans crushed tomatoes
3 1/2 cups vegetable broth
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 drops hot pepper sauce

Directions:
Heat oil in a large Dutch oven over medium-high heat.  Saute onion and garlic until onion is tender.
Add carrot and celery; cook 7 to 9 minutes until tender, stirring frequently. Stir in tomatoes, broth, Worcestershire sauce, salt, basil, pepper and hot pepper sauce. Reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer 20 minutes, stirring frequently.
