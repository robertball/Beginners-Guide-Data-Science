Title:
Italian Sausage from Zio Isidoro Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For every kilo of meat:
20 grams of fine ground sea salt (add 5 grams if the meat is very lean)*
3 grams course ground pepper
2 tsp anise seeds** (see notes)
2 tsp fennel seeds **(see notes)
200 ml dry white wine
1 tsp ground cayenne pepper (optional)

Directions:
Grind the meat twice in a large blade grinder.
Grind one more time with the smaller blade.
Add the meat to a large mixing bowl. Add all the ingredients and mix well. Cover and put in a cool area without direct sunlight. (Refrigerate)
Remix the mixture twice or even three times over an 8 hour period.
After 8 hours the mixture is ready to be cooked or put into casings.
