Title:
Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Cream Corn
1 can Whole Corn, drained
1 pkg. Jiffy Corn Muffin Mix
1/2 C Oil or Margarine
1 C Sour Cream
1 C Grated Cheddar Cheese
2 Eggs

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees
Mix together all ingredients except half a cup of cheese and spread in a 13x9x2 in. pan.
Bake for 35-40 minutes.
Top with the remaining half cup of grated cheese and bake for ten minutes more.

Submitter: 
Lainie Vansant
Description: 
This recipe is both easy and delicious and is a favorite at all of our family get-togethers.
