Title:
Peanut Butter Cheesecake Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/615212.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ozs. softened cream cheese
1/2 cup sugar
2 tsp. butter
1 tsp. vanilla
3 cups whipped heavy cream or cool whip
1 1/4 cups creamy peanut butter
1 Oreo cookie pie crust
Crumbled Oreo cookies (topping)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients (except pie crust) until light and fluffy.
Spoon into pie crust.
Top with more topping and some crumbled oreo cookies.
