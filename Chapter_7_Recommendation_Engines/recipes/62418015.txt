Title:
Mixed Green Salad with Avocado and Kalamata Olives Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (8 oz) of mixed greens
2 ripe Avocadoes, chopped into small cubes
12-15 Kalamata Olives, quartered
3 Tbsp Olive oil
1 tsp dehydrated garlic
Salt and black pepper to taste
2-3 shakes of crushed red pepper (optional)

Directions:
Add all ingredients to large salad bowl; mix and enjoy!!

Submitter: 
Alyse Ellienne
Description: 
Light summer salad with olive oil, avocado, and kalamata olives
