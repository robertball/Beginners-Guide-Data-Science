Title:
Easter Egg Dye Bath Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/463319.jpg
Ingredients:
6 small jars professional bakers' gel food coloring
6 cups white vinegar
6 plastic disposable 12 oz.cups

Directions:
Fill each plastic cup with 1 cup of vinegar.
Mix 1/4 teaspoon food coloring in each cup until dissolved (for deeper colors, add more food coloring).

Submitter: 
Alley
Description: 
This Easter egg dye bath will make beautiful, vibrant Easter eggs by using professional bakers' gel food coloring. The violets, yellows, baby blues, oranges and pinks make beautiful Easter eggs - far better than the traditional box decorating kits!
