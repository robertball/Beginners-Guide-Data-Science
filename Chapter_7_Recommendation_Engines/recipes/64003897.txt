Title:
Spareribs and Saurkraut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Lbs spare ribs
2 large yellow onions
2 jars Silver Floss Saurkraut
Whole black pepper
tyme
dill
pepper
greek seasoning
chicken broth

Directions:
Boil ribs in salted water for 30 min.  Let cool and remove from fat.  Slice onions, put in deep pot.  add 2 cans of chicken broth put ribs in the pot.  Add the sauerkraut and simmer for several hours.  Add seasoning as you like.
Delicious
