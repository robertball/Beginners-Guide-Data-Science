Title:
Cincinnati Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
1/2 tsp cumin
1 tsp cinnamon
1 lg onion
2 dashes of Worcestershire sauce
1 toe garlic
2 tbsp chili powder
1/2 tbsp black pepper
1 tbsp salt
1/2 tsp red pepper
1 (6 oz) can of tomato paste
1 1/2 tbsp of vinegar
1 qt water
1/2 tbsp allspice
3 whole bay leaves

Directions:
Crumble meat into the water. Add all other ingredients. Bring to a boil and then simmer 3 hours with cover on the pan. Serve over spaghetti noodles with grated sharp cheddar cheese on top and oyster crackers.
