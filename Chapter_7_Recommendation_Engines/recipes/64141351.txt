Title:
Hamburger Cheese Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 16 oz. can tomato sauce
1 teas. sugar
1 teas. salt
1 teas. garlic powder
1 teas. black pepper
4 cups cooked medium noodles
1 cup creamy cottage cheese
1 8oz. package softened cream cheese
1/4 cup sour cream or Greek yoghourt
1/2 cup chopped green onion
1/2 cup chopped green pepper
1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Brown meat and onion in a large skillet till meat is done and onion is tender. Stir in tomato sauce, sugar, salt and black pepper. Set aside.
Combine cottage cheese, cream cheese,sour cream, green onions and green pepper.
Spread half the noodles in a 11x7x11/2 inch baking pan, top with some meat sauce, then spread cheese mixture on top. Add remaining noodles, and meat sauce. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and pop into oven.
