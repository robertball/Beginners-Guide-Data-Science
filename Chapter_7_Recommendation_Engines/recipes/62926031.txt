Title:
Spicy Baked BBQ Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/930535.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb 96% extra lean ground beef
1/2 cup bread crumbs (can use whole wheat)
1 egg (or 1/4 cup egg substitute)
1 TBL minced garlic
6 green onions, chopped
1 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper (or adjust to taste)
2 TBL dijon or course ground mustard
1  tsp honey
2 TBL barbecue sauce
1 TBL worcestershire sauce
2 tsp italian seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Spray a baking sheet with cooking spray.
In a large bowl mix all ingredients together until it is thoroughly blended.
Form into 1-1/2 inch meatballs and place on the baking sheet.
Bake for 20 to 25 minutes until cooked through.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
These delicious barbecue meatballs are spicy sweet and lower in fat than the typical meatballs since they are baked with an extra lean ground beef.  They are one of our family's favorite main dishes.  Serve with your favorite barbecue sauce or pasta.  One serving of these meatballs has 5 Weight Watchers PPTS.
