Title:
Mocha Fudge Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups milk
1 cup hot strong-brewed coffee
1/4 cup dark brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
Dash of salt
2 large egg whites
8 cups 1" cubed white bread
1 1/4 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine milk, hot coffee and brown sugar in a saucepan over medium heat and bring to a simmer, stirring until sugar dissolves.
Cool to room temperature.
Add eggs, egg whites, vanilla and salt, stirring with a whisk.
Combine bread and egg mixture in a large bowl, tossing gently to coat and let stand 30 minutes.
Spoon half of bread mixture into an 8" square baking dish coated with cooking spray.
Sprinkle evenly with chocolate chips and top with remaining bread mixture.
Bake for 45 minutes or until center is set.
Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
