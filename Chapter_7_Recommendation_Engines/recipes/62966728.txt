Title:
BEST YORKSHIRE PUDDING Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
olive oil, butter,or drippings
2 eggs[must be room temperature]
2 cups milk
2 cups flour
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Pour oil, butter or drippings from roast into muffin tins[ceramic ramekins give great results.]Heat in oven.
Lightly beat eggs,add milk and lightly beat in flour,salt and pepper.
Pour into hot containers and bake at 375 for about 30 minutes.Yorkshires should puff up and are ready when golden brown.
