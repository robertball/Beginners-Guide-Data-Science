Title:
Crunchy Pesto Chicken Thighs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs
3 Tbsp prepared pesto
1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
1/2 Tbsp olive oil
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450. Line a baking sheet with parchment.
In a small bowl combine panko, parmesan, olive oil, salt and pepper.
Place chicken thighs on baking sheet and rub each side with pesto. Then press panko mixture onto chicken.
Bake for 20 minutes.
