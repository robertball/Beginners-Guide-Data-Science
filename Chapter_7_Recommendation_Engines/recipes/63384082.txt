Title:
Slow Cooker Hash with gingerbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/906896.jpg
Ingredients:
500 gr. of beefsteak, cut into cubes of 1 cm.
100 gr. butter
4 large onions, cut into big pieces
2 cloves of garlic, chopped
50 gr. of flower
2 large chunks of gingerbread
2 cloves
2 bay leafs
1/2 teaspoon of basil
Pepper
1 tablespoon of ketchup
1 tablespoon of red wine
1 tablespoon of vinegar
1 liter of Beef bouillon

Directions:
Season the meat with a little salt and pepper
Heat the butter in the frying pan. Fry the onions and garlic, until the onions are glazed. Add the meat, and brown it gently. Add the flower to the frying pan and let it turn brown under constant stirring.
Transfer the meat mixture to the slow cooker. Add the cubes of gingerbread, cloves, bay leafs, basil, cumminseed and pepper to and stir thoroughly.
Add the ketchup, wine, vinegar and broth (as much as need to just submerge the meat) and let the slow cooking begin.
Great served with mashed potatoes and red cabbage!
