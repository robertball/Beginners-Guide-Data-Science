Title:
Simple Slow Cooker Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7176881.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed golden mushroom soup
1 (1 ounce) package onion soup mix
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup water
1 onion, sliced
1 (12 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
4 potatoes, halved
4 pork chops

Directions:
Mix together cream of mushroom soup, golden mushroom soup, onion soup mix, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, and water in a slow cooker.
Stir in onion, mushrooms, and potatoes.
Place pork chops in the mushroom mixture, turning to coat both sides.
Cover and cook on Low for 6 hours.
Spoon the mushroom sauce over the pork chops and potatoes for serving.

Submitter: 
CARRIE98
Description: 
Mushrooms, potatoes, and tender pork chops are slow cooked in a creamy, oniony sauce in this comforting one-pot meal.
