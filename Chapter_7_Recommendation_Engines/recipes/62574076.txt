Title:
Fresh Zucchini Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c grated zucchini
1/2 c low fat buttermilk
1 large egg
1 large egg white
1 t vanilla
1 c flour
1/2 c wheat flour
2/3 c Splenda
2 T brown sugar
1 t baking soda
2 t baking powder
1 1/2 t ground cinnamon
3/4 t nutmeg
1/4 c shortening

Directions:
Preheat to 375. Spray muffin cups. Stir together first 5 ingredients. Combine next 8 ingredients. Cut shortening into flour. Add zucchini. Stir together. Spoon into muffin tins. Bake 12-15 minutes. Cool 5 minutes.
