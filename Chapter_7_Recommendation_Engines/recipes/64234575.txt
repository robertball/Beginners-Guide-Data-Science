Title:
Chicken Bisquits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz cream cheese
2 C cooked, cubed chicken (2 9oz cans cooked chicken breast)
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
2 T milk
1 T onion, chopped
8 oz can cresent rolls
1/2 tsp melted butter
seasoned bread crumbs

Directions:
Mix all ingredients down to the cresent rolls.
Separate the crescent rolls into 4 rectangles, seal the perforations.
Spoon 1/4 of mixture into center of each rectangle; pull 4 corners to center.
Brush tops with 1/2 tsp butter and sprinkle seasoned bread crumbs heavily over the top.
Bake on ungreased cookie sheet 20 - 25 min or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Michelle Wasmund
Description: 
This is a favorite recipe. Can make the bisquits ahead of time and hold in the refrigerator then bake just before you eat!
