Title:
Peanut Butter Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c sugar
1 c Karo
1 1/2 c peanut butter
1 tsp vanilla
6 c Special K cereal

Directions:
In a saucepan combine sugar & Karo & boil. Turn heat off & add peanut butter & 1 T vanilla. Mix well. In a large bowl pour the peanut butter mixture over the cereal & stir well. Let cool to the touch & roll into small balls
