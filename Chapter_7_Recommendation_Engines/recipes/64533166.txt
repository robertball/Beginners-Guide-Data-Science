Title:
Muesli Banana Hemp Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3071538.jpg
Ingredients:
3 ripe bananas
1/4 cup hemp hearts
1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
1/4 cup maple syrup
1 tbsp vanilla extract
2 1/3 cups Gluten Free Muesli
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp sea salt

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 degrees
In a food processor, add all ingredients except hemp hearts & muesli
Blend until combined well
Add hemp hearts & muesli and combine with batter by hand
Place in a 8x8 prepared baking sheet
Bake 30-35 minutes until golden on top
Allow to cool in the pan before cutting & removing
*Tip - cut with a very sharp knife
Keep in the refrigerator or freezer

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Homemade baked Muesli Banana Hemp Bars made with bananas, applesauce, hemp and muesli granola. A soon to be staple in your weekly meal prep, these easy snack bars are ones the whole family will love!
