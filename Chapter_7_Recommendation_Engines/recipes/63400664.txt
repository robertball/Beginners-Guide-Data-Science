Title:
Best Baked Potato Soup for the Crock Pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 Potatoes
9 Strips of Bacon
1 Onion Diced Small
3 Cups Broth (veggie or chicken)
1 tsp. Garlic Powder
2 Cups Half & Half
3 Tbsp. Flour
3 1/4 Cups Shredded Cheese (Cheddar Monterey Jack Mix)
Chives Finely chopped For Garnish (optional)

Directions:
Bake, peel, and cube potatoes.
In skillet, cook bacon until crisp, reserve drippings. Crumble 6 strips of bacon, saving 3 for garnish (optional).
Cook onion in bacon drippings until soft.
Transfer potatoes, crumbled bacon, onions, and garlic powder to crock pot.
Pour 3 cups of broth over potatoes.
Set on low for 6 hours.
Whisk together half and half and flour. Add to crock pot using the whisk to mask up some of the potatoes.
Stir in 3 cups of cheese, saving 1/4 cup for garnish if you wish. Mix until cheese is melted and fully incorporated.
Optional: before serving top with shredded cheese, crumbled bacon, and chives.
