Title:
Cauliflower Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1C panko
3oz white cheddar cheese, finely shredded (3/4C)
2T snipped fresh parsley
2t snipped fresh thyme
1 1/2t orange zest
1 lg. head cauliflower, cut into sm. florets (abt6C)
2T Dijon-style mustard
2T EVOO
1/4-1/2t salt
2T butter, melted
1/4C chopped, roasted almonds

Directions:
In a sm. bowl, combine panko, cheese, parsley, thyme, and orange zest;set aside
In 4-qt pot, cook cauliflower in lightly salted boiling water for 4 min. or until tender but still firm. Drain well.
In lg. bowl, combine EVOO, mustard and salt. Add cauliflower and stir gently to coat. Transfer to a 1 1/2-2-qt. au gratin or rectangular baking dish. Sprinkle w/panko mixture. Drizzle w/melted butter.
Bake in 425 oven on top rack of oven for 15 min. or until heated through and lightly browned. Top w/almonds.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Good dish to serve on Thanksgiving or anytime.
