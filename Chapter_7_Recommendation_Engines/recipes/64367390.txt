Title:
Doc Severinsen's Favorite Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
2 pods garlic, chopped fine
1 teaspoon fresh basil, chopped
2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
1/4 cup olive oil
3 tomatoes, peeled, seeded & chopped
2 pounds fresh raw shrimp, peeled
red pepper, to taste

Directions:
Combine all fresh ingredients (except shrimp) in large saucepan with oil.
Heat and stir until onions are almost clear.  Season to taste, then add shrimp.   Cook only until shrimp are pink, about 3-4 minutes.  Don't overcook!
Serve with pasta or rice.
