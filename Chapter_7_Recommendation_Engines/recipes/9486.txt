Title:
Vegan Pumpkin Nog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3182919.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (29 ounce) can pumpkin puree
4 cups vanilla rice milk
1 cup vanilla flavored non-dairy frozen dessert
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon ground mace

Directions:
Combine the pumpkin, rice milk, rice milk ice cream, cinnamon, nutmeg and mace in a blender. Puree until smooth. Add additional rice milk to thin, if desired.

Submitter: 
Lalena
Description: 
Think of this as a spicy pumpkin flavored riceshake. It's a perfect drink for kids and adults alike!
