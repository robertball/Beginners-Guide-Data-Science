Title:
Parrot Bay Key Lime Mojito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987501.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 ounces Captain Morgan Parrot Bay Key Lime
2 1/2 ounces club soda
2 sprigs mint
1 wedge lime

Directions:
Muddle mint.
Fill glass with ice.
Add Parrot Bay Key Lime and club soda.
Garnish with a lime wedge.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Use Captain Morgan Parrot Bay Key Lime Rum to make this easy and authentic-tasting mojito.
