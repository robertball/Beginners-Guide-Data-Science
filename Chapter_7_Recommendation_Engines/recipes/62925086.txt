Title:
Chicken Noodle Casserole II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
6 ounces egg noodles
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 small can of sliced mushroms
1 cup sour cream
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
1 cup crumbled buttery round crackers
1/4 cup butter
1 small can of mushrooms slices

Directions:
The day before poach chicken in a large pot of simmering water. Cook until no longer pink in center, about 12 minutes. Chill over night.  The next day remove chicken and set aside. Bring Chicken cooking water to a boil and cook pasta in it. Drain. Cut chicken into small pieces, and mix with noodles.
In a separate bowl, mix together mushroom soup, chicken soup, mushrooms and sour cream.  Season with salt and pepper. Gently stir together cream soup mixture with the chicken mixture. Place in a 2 quart baking dish.
Melt butter in a small saucepan, and remove from heat. Stir in crumbled crackers. Top casserole with the buttery crackers.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 40 minutes, until heated through and browned on top.

Submitter: 
Grace Carolyn Bowers-Reimondo
Description: 
This is a great comfort food recipe that fixes up fast on a work evening.  Serve with a vegetable or salad.Modified from: Chicken Noodle Casserole I
