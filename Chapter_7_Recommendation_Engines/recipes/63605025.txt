Title:
Tortilla Soup - Salsa Influenced Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1003033.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small onion diced
1 small yellow bell pepper diced
2tbs butter or oil
2 cups chicken broth
1tbs Cumin
1 tsp Salt
1 tsp garlic powder
2 Cups cooked Chicken Breast, shredded
2 Cups Salsa (tomato based)
1 Can Yellow Corn
Optional:
Avocado
Shredded Cheese
Sour Cream

Directions:
Dice 1 small onion and 1 small sweet yellow bell pepper.
Put 2 tbs of butter in a large pot and begin to melt on medium heat. Then saut√© the diced onion and pepper until onion turns clear.
Add 2 Cups of chicken broth and simmer for about 5 minutes
Add 1 tbs of Cumin, 1tsp of salt, 1 tsp of garlic powder, 2 cups of pre-cooked shredded chicken to the pot. Let simmer another 5 minutes.
Add 2 cups of your favorite red salsa (tomato based kind.) Then add 1 whole can of corn, water and everything to the pot.
Heat for another 10 minutes, then serve warm with sour cream, avocado slices, and shredded cheese.
