Title:
Crockpot Mexican Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. round steak
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. salt
1 Tbsp. chili powder
1 Tbsp. prepared mustard
1 medium onion, chopped
1 beef bouillon cube, crushed
1(16-oz.)can diced tomatoes
1(16-oz.)can kidney beans, rinsed
and drained

Directions:
Spread meat with a mixture of garlic, pepper, salt, chili powder, and mustard. Cut into strips; place in crockpot. Cover with onion, bouillon cube, and tomatoes. Cover and cook on Low for 6-8 hours. Turn control to High. Add beans and cook for 30 minutes more.
