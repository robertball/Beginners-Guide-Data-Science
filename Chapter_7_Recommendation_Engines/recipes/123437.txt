Title:
Quick Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups frozen peas, thawed
1 1/4 cups milk, divided
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Place the peas and 1/4 cup of milk in a blender; cover and process until pureed. Pour into a saucepan; add salt if desired, pepper and remaining milk. Cook and stir for 5 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Paula Zsiray
Description: 
This brightly colored, fresh-tasting soup is one of our daughter's favorites,' writes Paula Zsiray of Logan, Utah. 'She purees it in the blender in just seconds, then 'zaps' a mugful in the microwave until heated through.'
