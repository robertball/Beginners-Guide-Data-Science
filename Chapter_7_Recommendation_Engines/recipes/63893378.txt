Title:
Big Ray's West Coast Chicken and Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1080459.jpg
Ingredients:
4 small  boneless skinless chicken breasts (1 lb.)
3 cups  frozen mixed vegetables (broccoli, red peppers and carrots), thawed, drained
1/4 cup  pesto
1/4 cup  chicken broth
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 cup Shredded Mozzarella Cheese

Directions:
Heat large skillet sprayed with cooking spray on medium-high heat. Add chicken; cover. Cook 5 min. on each side or until lightly browned on both sides.
Add vegetables, pesto and water. Bring to boil. Cook on medium heat 6 to 8 min. or until chicken is done (165Â°F), stirring occasionally.
Sprinkle with cheese; cover. Remove from heat; let stand 5 min.
