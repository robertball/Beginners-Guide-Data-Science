Title:
Strawberry Bavarian Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg frozen sliced strawberries, thawed (10 oz)
1 cup boiling water
1 pkg strawberry gelatin (3 oz)
2 cups Cool Whip (or whipped cream)

Directions:
Drain strawberries, reserving syrup.  Pour boiling water over gelatin in a bowl; stirring until gelatin is dissolved.  Add enough cold water to reserved syrup to measure 1 cup; stir into dissolved gelatin.  Chill until almost set.
When almost set, beat gelatin until foamy.  Fold gelatin and strawberries into Cool Whip.  Pour into bowl and chill until firm.
