Title:
Keto Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5744787.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups almond meal
1 cup pecans, chopped
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 cup heavy whipping cream, at room temperature
3 eggs, at room temperature
2 tablespoons ghee, at room temperature
1 teaspoon vanilla bean paste

Directions:
Mix almond meal, pecans, and baking powder together in a large bowl. Whisk heavy cream, eggs, ghee, and vanilla paste together in a separate bowl.
Heat a waffle iron to medium-high according to manufacturer's instructions.
Mix almond meal mixture and cream mixture together. Let batter rest until thickened, about 5 minutes.
Stir batter and cook in the preheated iron according to manufacturer's instructions.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Waffles made with almond meal, heavy cream, and ghee will satisfy your craving for your breakfast favorite while meeting your keto diet requirements.
