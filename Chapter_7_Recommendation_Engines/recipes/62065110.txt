Title:
Danish Rye Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Danish Rye Bead (Smørbrøds Brød)
Start the sourdough in the morning the day before baking. Combine
1 cup   Warm Water
1 tbsp Yeast
1 tbsp	Sugar
1 cup   Rye flower   dark or light
Cover set in a draft free place
In the evening add.
1 tsp    Yeast
1 tsp    Sugar
½ cup	Warm Water
1 cup   Rye flower
Next Day add to the sourdough
1 tbsp  Sugar
1 tbsp   Salt   Kosher or Sea
1 cup    Warm Water
2 cups  Rye flower
1 cup	 A/P flower, for quick rising, optional
Bake @ 375 for about 45 min.

Directions:
Prepare as any other bread recipe
