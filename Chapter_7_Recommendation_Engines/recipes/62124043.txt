Title:
Pecan Noodle Kugel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound broad noodles
4 Tablespoons melted butter (separated)
4 eggs
1 cup brown sugar (separated)
Pecans (or walnuts)

Directions:
1.  Boil noodles in salted water until tender.  Wash in colander with cold water.  Spread noodles on wax paper until dry.
2.  Beats eggs in large bowl.  Add 2 Tablespoons melted butter.  Add 1/2 cup brown sugar and pinch of salt.  Mix noodles in this mixture and stir well.
3.  Spread bottom of bundt pan or deep casserole baking dish with 2 Tablespoons melted butter.  Sprinkle 1/2 cup brown sugar on bottom and pat onto sides.  Spread nuts over brown sugar.  Carefully pour noodle mixture over this.
4.  Bake at 325 degree preheated oven for 1 hour and 15 minutes.  Remove from oven and invert on serving platter so the brown sugar-nut topping is now on top.  Slice into wedges like a cake!

Submitter: 
ROYSAPER
Description: 
Noodle kugel coated with a brown sugar/pecan mixture hot out of the oven is sweet enough it could also work as a dessert.  This is an old family recipe guaranteed to be loved by all!
