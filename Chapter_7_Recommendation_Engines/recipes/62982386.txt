Title:
Easy Onion Garlic Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz) package cream cheese, softened
1 package Lipton golden onion dry soup mix
3 tbsp. minced garlic
1.5-2 tbsp. granulated garlic
1 tbsp. sour cream (optional)

Directions:
1) Add cream cheese, soup mix, minced garlic, and 1 tbsp. granulated garlic to a small bowl.  Stir until thoroughly mixed.
2) Add more granulated garlic to taste.
3) Add sour cream if you want a thinner spread.
4) Refrigerate until ready to use.

Submitter: 
lkruth
Description: 
4 ingredients, 5 minutes, and you have a delicious appetizer.  Spread on your favorite bread (I recommend whole wheat beer bread) or cracker.  Great for Thanksgiving, the Superbowl, or any time you have guests and don't want too much fuss.
