Title:
Quick cassoulet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can creamy onion soup
1 can tomato soup
1 cup water
1 16oz can small white beans, rinsed and drained
1 lb kielbasa, cut into 1/2" slices
4 small potatoes (340g) peeled and quartered
3 medium carrot, cut into 1/2" pieces
1/2 tsp dried thyme
1/8 tsp pepper
1 bay leaf

Directions:
In 4-5 quart Dutch oven, combine soups and water. Stir in beans, kielbasa, potatoes, carrots, thyme, pepper and bay leaf. Over medium heat, bring to a boil, stirring occasionally.
Reduce heat to low. Cover; cook 25 minutes or until vegetables are tender, stirring occasionally. Remove bay leaf.
