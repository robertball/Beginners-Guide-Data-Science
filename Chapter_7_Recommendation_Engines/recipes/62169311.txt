Title:
Sausage Melts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pound Ground Pork Sausage
1 Onion, Sliced
2 Tbps. Canola Oil
4 slices Provolone Cheese
8 Slices Thick Italian Bread

Directions:
Over Medium Heat add 1 Tbsp. oil to a 10 inch skillet.
Add onions and cook until tender and translucent.
Meanwhile form sausage into four 1/4 pound patties.
Remove onions from the skillet and add remaing oil to the skillet and cook sausage patties until thoroughly cooked through, about 5 minutes per side.
On four slices of bread add a sice of cheese, cooked onions and sausage patty top with another slice of bread.
Cook over medium heat until browned on both sides.
