Title:
Kansas City BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*1/3 cup olive oil
*1 onion, finely chopped
*1/2 bell pepper, finely chopped
*2 tspns. minced garlic
*1 16 oz. bottle catsup
*1/2 cup molasses
*2 tspns hot pepper sauce
*1/4 cup yellow mustard
*2 Tblspns. cider vinegar
*1/2 cup dark brown sugar, packed
*4 Tblspn. Worcestershire sauce
*1 tspn. liquid smoke flavor
*1/4 cup fresh lemon juice

Directions:
Place oil in a large non-aluminum saucepan, then saute' onion, green pepper and garlic until soft (about 10 minutes). Add remaining ingredients and continue to simmer slowly for 20 to 30 minutes. Stir frequently to prevent sauce from burning.
Let rest for at least 1 hour to allow flavors to meld.

Submitter: 
luv2cook
Description: 
Kansas City is famous for their thick, sweet sauce. The liquid smoke flavoring re-creates the flavor of meat slowly smoked in the barbecue pits. Catsup, molasses and yellow mustard combine to lay the foundation for this thick all-American sauce.
Enjoy!
