Title:
Barbeque Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
1 pound hamburger
2 cans kidney beans
2 cans B&M; beans
1 pound bacon, fried & broken up
1/2 cup ketchup
1 tablespoon yellow mustard
2 tablespoons vinegar
1/2 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Brown hamburger and onion together; drain.  In a casserole, combine hamburger mixture with beans and bacon.  Make a sauce by mixing the remaining ingredients in a small bowl, and mix sauce into bean mixture.  Bake 1/2 hour uncovered and 1/2 hour covered at 350 degrees.
