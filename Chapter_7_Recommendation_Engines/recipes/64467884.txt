Title:
Dinomite's Downunder Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2450351.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk
1 egg lightly beaten
2 cup cheese grated
1 cup self-raising flour
3 bacon rashers diced
1 onion small diced
1 handful Kale, chopped (optional, but highly recommended!)

Directions:
STEP 1 Preheat oven to 200C.
STEP 2 Combine egg and milk in a large bowl.
STEP 3 Add remaining ingredients and mix well.
STEP 4 Line a baking tray with baking paper and drop large teaspoons of the mixture onto the tray.
STEP 5 Place into oven and bake for about 20 minutes or until slightly golden on top.

Description: 
These are not popovers and have nothing to do with bread. These are very quick snacks that you can whip up during commercial breaks when you are watching TV and you feel like something cheesy. Brought to you by Dinomite  of GISHWHES.2015
