Title:
Smokey Tomato and Bacon Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1191528.jpg
Ingredients:
24 oz Jar of Classico Tomato and Basil Pasta Sauce
1 lemon, juiced
4 slices Thick Cut smoked bacon

Directions:
Fry bacon in a large skillet until crispy.  Drain on paper towel-lined plate.  Meanwhile, in a medium sauce pan, bring the pasta sauce and lemon juice to a simmer and stir occasionally for 45 minutes while you let it reduce to half. Once it has reached the consistency of tomato paste, crumble your bacon and stir into the mixture.

Tracey Ferrari Posner
