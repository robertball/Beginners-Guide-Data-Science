Title:
Famous Barr French Onion Soup Instant Pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5428888.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lbs onions
1/3 cup butter
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp paprika
1 bay leaf
1/4 cup dry, white wine
1/4 cup sherry
7 cups beef broth, divided
3 ounces flour
French baguette
Sliced gruyere cheese

Directions:
Peel onions and slice in long, thin slices, about 1/8 inch or so
Add onions, butter, salt, pepper, and paprika into Instant Pot.  Set to sauté mode and sauté approximately 4 minutes.
Add white wine and close lid. Set to cook on High Pressure for 20 minutes. Release pressure to vent immediately after finished.
Remove the lid and add sherry. Sauté in sauté mode 3 minutes, scraping up any browned bits.
Add 6 cups of broth and bay leaf. Cover and seal Instant Pot again and cook on High Pressure for 6 minutes. Release pressure to vent immediately.
While cooking mix flour and remaining broth in a bowl.
After venting remove lid and set Instant Pot to sauté again, remove bay leaf, add broth/flour mixture and sauté till thickened, approximately 10 minutes.
Taste and adjust salt/pepper seasonings.
Laden into oven safe bowls and top with slices of baguette and cheese. Broil for 3-5 minutes or until bread is toasted and cheese is melted.
