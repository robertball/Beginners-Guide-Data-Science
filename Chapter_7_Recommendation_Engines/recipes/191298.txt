Title:
Citrus Salad with Pomegranate Yogurt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/205359.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups Dannon® Light 'n Fit® Cherry Vanilla or Strawberry Nonfat Yogurt
2 tablespoons lemon juice
Zest of 1 orange
1/2 cup pomegranate seeds
3 grapefruits
3 navel oranges

Directions:
In a bowl combine Dannon(R) Light 'n Fit(R) Cherry Vanilla or Strawberry Nonfat Yogurt, lemon juice, orange zest and pomegranate seeds gently stir to blend.
Remove all peel and pith from the grapefruits and oranges. Slice each into 5-6 slices. Arrange fruit slices on a platter in a single layer. Pour sauce over fruit and serve.

Submitter: 
DANNON
Description: 
Cherry vanilla yogurt, lemon juice, orange zest, and pomegranate seeds make a tangy and sweet dressing for a simple citrus salad.
