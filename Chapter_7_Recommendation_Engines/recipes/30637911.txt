Title:
Sweet Spiced Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium acorn squash
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1 tbsp golden brown sugar
1 tbsp butter of margarine

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
Cut squash in half and scoop out seeds.
In a small bowl combine cinnamon, nutmeg, brown sugar and butter.
Rub mixture over cut side of squash.
Place on a baking dish and bake for 30 minutes of until squash is tender
