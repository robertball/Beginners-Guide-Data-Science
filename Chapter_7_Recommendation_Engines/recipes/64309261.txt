Title:
Cheeseburger Cups (from Simple & Delicious 2009) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/2 cup ketchup
2 TBLS brown sugar
1 Tbls prepared mustard
1-1/2 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
1 tube (12 oz) refrigerated buttermilk biscuits
1/2 cup cubed processed cheese (Velveeta)

Directions:
Cook beef.
Stir in ketchup, brown sugar, mustard, and Worcestershire Sauce.  Remove from heat; set aside.
Press biscuit into bottom and side of greased muffin cup. Spoon beef mixture into cups. Top with cheese.
Bake at 400 for 14-16 min.
