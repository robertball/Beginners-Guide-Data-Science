Title:
Hot Dog Heaven Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 hot dogs
8 hot dog buns
16 pickle slices, halved
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 onion, chopped small
1/2 pound bacon, cooked and crumbled
4 teaspoons prepared yellow mustard or barbecue sauce, or to taste

Directions:
Cook hot dogs according to your preference; place one hot dog into each bun.
Place 4 pickle halves along one side of each hot dog (between the dog and the bun).
Top each with equal amounts of cheddar cheese, onions and bacon.
Top with mustard or barbecue sauce.
