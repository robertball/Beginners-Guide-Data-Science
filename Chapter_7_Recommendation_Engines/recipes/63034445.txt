Title:
Honey Citrus Turkey Brine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Water
32oz Orange Juice
32oz Honey
1 cup Brown Sugar
1tbs of Red Pepper
3tbs Black Pepper
1 Bay Leaf
2 Cloves of Garlic
2 Oranges Sliced
1 Lemon Sliced
2 Bags of Ice
1 5 Gallon Bucket.

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except ice in a very large pot and bring to a boil for 10 minutes. Remove from heat and add ice to start a chilldown. Pour Brine into bucket and add more ice to finish chilldown. Add your Bird and cover with more ice for 24 hours. Remove Bird and rinse thoroughly before cook prep.
