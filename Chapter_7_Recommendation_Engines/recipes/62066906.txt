Title:
Ivonne's Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups elbow noodles, uncooked
1 cup chopped onions
1/2 cup chopped celery
1 tablespoon butter or margarine
4 ounces canned mushrooms, bits and pieces
1 can (10 3/4 ounces) condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 cup (4 ounces) shredded reduced-fat Monterey Jack-cheddar cheese mix or cheddar cheese
8 ounces yogurt
1 can (14 oz.) of peas and carrots
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 can (6 ounces) water-packed chunk light tuna, rinsed, drained, and flaked
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Preheat the oven to 350ยบ F. Coat a 2 1/2 quart or 13" x 9" baking dish with nonstick spray. Set aside.
2. In a large covered pot, bring water to a boil over high heat. Add the noodles and cook according to the package directions. Set aside.
3. Meanwhile, melt the butter or margarine in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onions and celery and cook, stirring frequently, until tender.
4. Add the soup, cheese, yogurt, canned vegetables and black pepper. Mix well. Add the canned vegetables, tuna and noodles. Fold to coat with the sauce. Spoon into the prepared baking dish.
4. In a small bowl, combine the bread crumbs and Parmesan. Sprinkle evenly over the noodle mixture. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until bubbly and lightly browned.
