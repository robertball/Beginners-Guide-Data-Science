Title:
Mom's Awesome Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1/4 cup chopped onion
2 (15 oz.) cans kidney beans, undrained
2 (15 oz.) cans pinto beans, undrained
2 (15 oz.) cans Ranch style beans, undrained
2 (15.25 oz.) cans whole kernel corn, undrained
1 (1 oz.) package taco seasoning mix
1 (1 oz.) package ranch salad dressing mix
1 (4 oz.) can diced green chiles, optional

Directions:
In a large saucepan over medium heat, cook ground beef and onion until beef is no longer pink; drain.  Stir in remaining ingredients.  Cover and simmer for 15 minutes.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jenny
Description: 
My Mom has been making this for years, and I've eaten it at a lot of family get-togethers.  It is so easy to make and absolutely delicious!  Serve with sour cream, shredded cheddar cheese, cornbread, and/or tortilla chips.
