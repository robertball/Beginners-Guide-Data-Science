Title:
Big Ray's Caribbean Grilled Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1129443.jpg
Ingredients:
3 sweet potatoes, unpeeled
Kosher salt
2 teaspoons finely grated lime zest
Pinch of cayenne pepper
1/4 cup canola oil
Freshly ground pepper
1/4 cup finely chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Parcook the potatoes: microwave potatoes for 4 minutes and place in a refridgerator until cool. Slice each potato lengthwise into eighths.
Preheat a grill to medium or place a cast-iron grill pan over medium heat. Mix 1 tablespoon salt, the lime zest, oil, pepper and cayenne in a medium bowl.
Add the potato wedges to the bowl a mix gently with rubber spatula. Grill until golden brown on all sides (including the skin) and just cooked through, about 2 1/2 minutes per side. Transfer to a platter; immediately sprinkle with cilantro.
