Title:
tomato and eggplant vegetarian vermachelli bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 eggplant (cubed)
1 jar classico's brushetta preparation (or 2 cups of your favorite brushetta tomatoe mixture)
8 cups of cooked vermachelli (or the pasta of your choice
1 pint of mushrooms
1 cup of motzarella
2 cups of cheddar
1/2 cup of parmasan
1 tablespoon of olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Take your mushrooms and eggplant and plance in the microwave to partially cook for 5 minutes. Add brushetta mixture and parmasan and place in an oven safe casserole dish. Plave mixture in the oven for 25-30 minutes until cooked through and reduced. In the meantime prepare your pasta boiling 8 cups of water and 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Let pasta cook for 5-7 minutes at medium temperature. Drain water from pasta and let stand in strainer for 1 minute to make sure all the water has completely evaporated. Remove eggplant mixture from oven and mix with the pasta. Cover the mixture with the remaining motzarella and cheddar and put back in the oven for 20 minutes at 350 (cover if the cheese begins to brown)
for garlic lovers feel free to add a colve of minced garlic to the brusetta mixture.
