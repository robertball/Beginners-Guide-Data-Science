Title:
Healing Cabbage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon butter
2 onions, chopped
6 cloves garlic, chopped
1 large carrot, finely diced
2 stalks of celery, finely diced
2 tetra packs of chicken broth instead of water(8 cups)
2 teaspoons chicken boullion
1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
1/2 teaspoon black pepper, or to taste
1/2 head cabbage, cored and coarsely chopped
1 (14.5 ounce) can Italian-style stewed tomatoes, drained and diced

Directions:
In a large stockpot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Stir in onion , garlic, celery, carrots; cook until onion is transparent, about 5 minutes.
Stir in chicken broth,bouillon , salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then stir in cabbage. Simmer until cabbage wilts, about 10 minutes.
Stir in tomatoes. Return to a boil, then simmer 15 to 30 minutes, stirring often.

Comfort food on a cold winter's night, cabbage simmered in chicken broth is also an age-old folk remedy for curing the common cold.Modified from: Healing Cabbage Soup
