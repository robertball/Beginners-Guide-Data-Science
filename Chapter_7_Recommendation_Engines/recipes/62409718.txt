Title:
Breakfast Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/436754.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp- margarine
1 small onion, chopped
1 garlic clove, chopped
6 eggs, beaten
1/4 tsp- ground cumin
1/2 Cup- red chile sauce or salsa picante
1 Cup (4 ounces)- shredded medium cheddar cheese
6 corn tortillas, softened

Directions:
Heat margarine in a skillet over medium head and saute onion and garlic until tender. Add eggs, cumin, and one quarter cup salsa. Scramble egg mixture until set.
Stir in cheese. Spoon eggs into tortillas, fold and serve. Best topped with more salsa.
