Title:
My Favorite Blue Cheese Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup + 1 tablespoon buttermilk
1/2 cup + 1 tablespoon sour cream
1/4 cup + 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
3/4 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
1 clove garlic, smashed
Few splashes Tabasco
1 8-oz container crumbled blue cheese
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients by hand.  Let flavors meld for several hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Marianne
Description: 
My family loves this dressing!  It's a mixture of different recipes that I've collected, and for us this is the perfect combination.
