Title:
Pepperoni Penne Pasta VI Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box penne pasta
1 can Spaghetti Sauce
1/2 bag pepperoni
1 onion
1 green and/or red, yellow pepper
Sprinkles of Cheese
Parmesan (optional)
Garlic Salt

Directions:
In a pot, heat stove to "high" bring to boil. Add penne pasta, meanwhile, preheat oven to "350 F"  Take saucepan, put sauce in. Cut pepperoni (on cutting board) into 1/2. Once, pasta done; mix in with spaghetti sauce. Add pepperoni, cheese, garlic salt, onion and parmesan.
