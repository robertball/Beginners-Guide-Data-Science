Title:
Kelly's Crack Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb. Sausage
8 oz Cream Cheese
1 can Rotel

Directions:
Cook sausage until no longer pink.Drain
In a sauce pan, add Rotel and cream cheese, over medium heat, stir unitl cream cheese is melted and mixed.  Add to Sauage and Mix Well. Put in a serving bowl and serve with Frito Scoops!
