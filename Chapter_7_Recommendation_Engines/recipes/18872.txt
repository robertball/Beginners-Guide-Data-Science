Title:
Slow Cooker Wieners in Wiener BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/779103.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds hot dogs
1 (18 ounce) jar grape jelly
1 (8 ounce) jar prepared mustard
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

Directions:
Place the wieners in a slow cooker. In a medium bowl, combine the grape jelly, mustard, brown sugar and cider vinegar. Mix well and pour over the wieners.
Cook on low setting for at least 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
CLAYCO
Description: 
This makes a great appetizer for parties, or a main dish for the hot dog lovers in your family. Serve with French fries, all the fixings, and a cold, crisp salad.
