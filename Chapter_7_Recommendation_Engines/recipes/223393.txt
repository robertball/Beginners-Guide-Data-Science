Title:
Pomegranate Basil Champagne Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 leaves fresh basil
1 tablespoon pomegranate juice, or more to taste
4 fluid ounces champagne

Directions:
Place basil leaves in the bottom of champagne flute; pour in pomegranate juice. Lightly muddle the leaves to release flavor. Top with champagne.

Submitter: 
Matt.velure@me.com
Description: 
A very tasty summer cocktail using fresh ingredients.
