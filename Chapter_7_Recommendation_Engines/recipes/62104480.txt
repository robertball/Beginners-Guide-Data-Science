Title:
Hearty Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 carrots  sliced
3 stalks of celery  sliced
2 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp minced garlic
1 c bacon or ham (optional) (I use imitation bacon)
1 qt chicken broth
1 15oz can tomato sauce
4-6 c water
1 ½ c lentils
½ lb farfalle (bowtie pasta)

Directions:
Heat large pan over med/low heat.  Pour in olive oil, garlic & bacon/ham. Cook long enough to heat up garlic and bacon/ham.  Pour in chicken broth, tomato sauce, 3-4c of the water, lentils, carrots & celery.  Bring to a boil & simmer for about 30 min.  Add farfalle and more water as needed.  Cook until farfalle is tender.
Ladle into bowls and serve.  Top with cheese if desired.

Submitter: 
Alisha Bowers
Description: 
This is a wonderful soup that is hearty enough to eat as a meal or just a meal starter.  It's a great vegetarian dish as well.  Even my very picky 3yr old likes this soup!
