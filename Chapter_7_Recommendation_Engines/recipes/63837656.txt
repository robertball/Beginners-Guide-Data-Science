Title:
Easy Key Lime Pie I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 egg yolks, beaten
2 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup key lime juice
1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Combine the egg yolks, sweetened condensed milk and lime juice. Mix well. Pour into unbaked graham cracker shell.
Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes. Allow to cool. Top with whipped topping and garnish with lime slices if desired.

Submitter: 
Dawnby
Description: 
To make this tart and creamy blue ribbon pie, egg yolks, lime juice and condensed milk are stirred together, poured into a graham cracker crust, and baked.Modified from: Easy Key Lime Pie I
