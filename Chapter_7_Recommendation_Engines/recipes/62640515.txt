Title:
Essanaye's Sausage & Spinach Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/582097.jpg
Ingredients:
1 LB Sweet Italian Sausage
3/4 LB Pasta
1 TBS Olive Oil
1 med Sweet Onion, chopped
4-5 cloves fresh minced garlic
1 15 oz can diced tomatoes with juice
6-8 fresh basil leaves, chopped (adjust to taste)
1 cup chicken broth
2 roma tomatoes, diced
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
1-2 cups chopped fresh spinach (suggest 2)

Directions:
Cook sausage in large skillet, dutch oven or wok.  When the sausage is almost done, add the onion & cook 2-3 minutes, add canned diced tomatoes, garlic, basil, and broth, simmer 5 mins to reduce the liquid.  Add spinach and pasta, and cook additional 2-3 minutes.  Remove from heat, add parmesan cheese and garnish with chopped roma tomatoes.  Serve immediately.
