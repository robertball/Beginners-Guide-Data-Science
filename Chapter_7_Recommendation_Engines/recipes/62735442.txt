Title:
Walnut Triangles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the dough:
2 1/3 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup sugar
2 packets vanilla sugar (equiv: 1 teaspoon vanilla extract)
2 eggs
9 Tablespoons butter
For the rest:
4 Tablespoons apricot marmalade
14 Tablespoons butter
1.5 cups sugar
4 Tablespoons water
1 1/3 cup ground walnuts (or other nuts)
1 2/3 cup chopped walnuts
2/3 cup chocolate

Directions:
Mix dough ingredients and refrigerate for 15 mins
While waiting for dough:
Melt 14 Tablespoons butter, sugar, water, and vanilla sugar
Bring to boil and add chopped & ground nuts
Turn off and cool
Roll dough on baking sheet
Spread 4 T marmalade
Pour/spread mixture on dough and marmalade
Bake 30 minutes at 180 degree Celsius (350 F)
Cut in 3 inch squares, then cut squares diagonally
Melt 2/3 cup chocolate cooking squares and dip both edges of triangle
Drizzle remaining chocolate on all pieces

Walnut triangle goodness with marmalade, nuts, and chocolate.
