Title:
Butternut Squash & Roasted Garlic Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 tbsp. Butter
1 1/4 lbs (20 oz.) Pre-cut Butternut Squash
1 Medium Onion, finely chopped
3 c. Chicken Broth
2 tsp. Ground Ginger
2 tsp. black pepper
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 c. plain non-fat yogurt

Directions:
In a large sauce pan over med-high heat, heat olive oil & melt butter. Add squash & sautee, stirring frequently until lightly browned, about 8 minutes. Add chopped onion & continue cooking for 3 minutes, or until onions are sweat. Stir in 2 cups of the chicken broth, ginger, black pepper, salt, & cinnamon. Once squash is soft, turn off heat & transfer the mixture into a blender or food processor. Set the sauce pan aside. Then, add the yogurt. Blend the ingredients by pulsing until mixture is smooth. Transfer back to the sauce pan on medium heat. Add remaining broth & stir until combined. Once soup is heated through, it is ready to be served!
