Title:
Quickie Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices bacon, diced
3/4 cup diced onion
1 2lb bag frozen hash brown potatoes (southern style)
1/2 cup instant mashed potato flakes
6 cups chicken broth
1 cup half and half
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute bacon and onion together till browned, set aside.
Over medium heat, in a large sauce pan, bring chicken broth to boil.
Stir in bacon, onion, frozen potatoes, instant potatoes and turn down heat to a simmer.
Simmer for 30 minutes, then blend in half and half.
Season with salt and pepper, and serve

Submitter: 
Mare
Description: 
This is a quick and easy potato soup, that can be tweaked to your own personal preferences...add veggies or cheese, top with some sour cream...and you've got a hearty cold weather soup!
