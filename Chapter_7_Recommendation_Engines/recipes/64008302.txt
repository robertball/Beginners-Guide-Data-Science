Title:
Lemon-Parmesan Chicken Rice Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 2 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breast(1-inch cubes)
8 oz Bag of frozen broccoli
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 cup uncooked rice
zest and juice of 1 large lemon
1 large onion (medium dice)
1/2 tbsp garlic powder
1/2 tbsp onion powder
salt & pepper to taste
5tbsp oil
2 cups of hot water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 F degrees.
Add 2 tbsp of oil to hot pan, along with uncooked rice. Stir and coat the rice with oil.
Add 2 cups of hot water and bring to a boil. Cover and put in the oven for 12 -15 minutes.
Heat up remaining oil in a large saute pan.
Season chicken with garlic & onion powder.
Saute until brown on both sides.
Steam or boil broccoli until crisp and bright green.
Add diced onion to chicken
Continue cooking until onions are soft and translucent.
Remove rice from oven and spoon into saute pan
Cook rice 3 minutes in saute pan
drain water and season broccoli salt and pepper (butter optional)
Add broccoli to saute pan
Add lemon juice and lemon zest, stir.
Add parmesan cheese, stir and enjoy

Submitter: 
Teyonna Mason
Description: 
A quick and delicious meal that is healthy and the family will love it. Cheesy, lemony, crunchy!
