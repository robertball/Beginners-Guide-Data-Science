Title:
Easy Mexican Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 flour tortillas, burrito size
oil for frying
1 15 oz can refried beans
10 oz. frozen corn, thawed
8 oz. can tomato sauce
small can diced green chiles
1/2 tsp. cumin
sour cream (opt.)
chopped lettuce (opt.)
chopped fresh tomatoes (opt.)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 F.
Heat a large skillet over med. hi heat until hot but not smoking.  Lightly drizzle pan with oil (about 1/2 tsp. per tortilla) and fry each tortilla, turning over once, until lightly browned on both sides.  Set aside.
In a med. bowl, combine beans, corn, sauce, chiles, and cumin.
Place one tortilla in center of a lg. baking sheet.  Spread about 1 1/2 c. mixture evenly on top of tortilla.  Repeat layering with remaining tortillas and mixture, ending with a tortilla on top, and gently pressind tortillas together. Bake for 12 min. or until heated through.  Serve with sour cream, lettuce and tomatoes on top, if desired.

Submitter: 
CARRIELOVESKEITH
Description: 
This recipe is a quick, inexpensive, meatless meal that my family loves.  My two-year-old won't stop eating when I serve this for dinner.  You can also add shredded cheese or chopped fresh cilantro.  Or, substitute black beans for refried beans.
