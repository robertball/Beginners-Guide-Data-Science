Title:
Candy Martini---You will need a shot glass to make this drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 shot New Amsterdam Red Berry flavored Vodka
1/2 shot Triple Sec
1 shot Peach Schnapps
3 shots Cranberry Juice

Directions:
Combine New Amsterdam Red Berry flavored Vodka, Triple Sec, Peach Schnapps and Cranberry Juice in an ice-half filled cocktail shaker. Shake vigorously and strain into a martini glass. Enjoy
