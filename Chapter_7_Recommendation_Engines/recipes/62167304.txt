Title:
Cold Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. sour cream
1 pkg. vegetable soup mix
6 green onions--thin sliced
2 bell peppers chopped (I use red and orange)
10 oz box chopped frozen spinach--thawed

Directions:
In a large bowl mix sour cream and soup mix
Squeeze any excess water from the spinach
Add peppersd, green onions and spinach to the bowl
Stir and chill before serving
