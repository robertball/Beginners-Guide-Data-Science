Title:
Gina's Amazing Pasta & Shrimp Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb dry pasta ( I used bow ties)
1 lb frozen cooked small shrimp
1 small yellow or red pepper - chopped fine
2- stalks celery - chopped fine
1/2 red onion - chopped fine ( can use sweet white instead)
1 cup mayo with lime ( can use regular mayo and add lime juice)
10 grape tomatoes - cut in half
salt & pepper
Fresh Basil/ tarragon/ cilantro - chopped fine
Greek seasoning
Tuscany seasoning

Directions:
cook the pasta in salted water till done and drain
while pasta cooking take shrimp out and begin to thaw
in large bowl mix pasta and shrimp-add spices and chopped vegetables...add mayo and stir well
chill in fridge
