Title:
Ram Tough Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lb hamburger
16 oz tomato sauce
2 onions diced
2 t. garlic powder
1/2 c. chili powder
4 t. cumin
1 T. red pepper sauce
1 1/2 t. salt
1 t. oregano
1 can beer

Directions:
Brown meat. Add rest of ingredients and pressure cook for 15 minutes. Cool. Serve the next day.
