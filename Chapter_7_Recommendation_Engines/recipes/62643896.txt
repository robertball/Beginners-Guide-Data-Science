Title:
Salmon with Brown Sugar/Soy Sauce Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
2 tablespoons Soy Sauce
4 (6 ounce) boneless salmon fillets
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat the oven's broiler and set the oven rack at about 6 inches from the heat source; prepare the rack of a broiler pan with cooking spray.
Season the salmon with salt and pepper and arrange onto the prepared broiler pan. Whisk together the brown sugar and soy sauce in a small bowl; spoon mixture evenly onto top of salmon fillets.
Cook under the preheated broiler until the fish flakes easily with a fork, 10 to 15 minutes.
