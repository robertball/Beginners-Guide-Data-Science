Title:
Ranch Cucumbers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Packet Ranch Dressing mix, or 3 tablespoons of homemade powdered ranch mix
1 Cup Sour Cream
1 Cup Mayo
3-6 Cucumbers, depending on size
1 Medium Onion, sliced, more or less to taste

Directions:
Mix ranch dressing mix with sour cream and mayo.
Slice cucumbers to desired thickness.
Slice onion into thin slices.
Add cucumbers and onion to dressing mix.
Refrigerate for one hour to allow flavors to meld.
Will become watery after a day.
I like to add more cucumbers after a couple of days.

My mom has made this for years.  It's great in the summer at a BBQ!
