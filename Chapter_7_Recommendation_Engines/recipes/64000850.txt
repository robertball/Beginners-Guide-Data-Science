Title:
Green Power Mojito Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1110324.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups of ice cubes (adjust to your preference)
1/2 cup water (adjust to your preference)
Juice of one lemon
Juice of one lime
One orange or tangelo, peeled and sectioned
One banana
7 oz. can crushed pineapple in 100% juice
6 good sized handfuls raw baby spinach or "power greens"
10-12 large mint leaves

Directions:
Squeeze your lemon and lime into the blender, straining the seeds from the lemon. Place all other ingredients in blender and blend until smooth, opening top to push ingredients down as needed.

Submitter: 
Patricia Cooper
Description: 
This tart, satisfying and delicious smoothie disguises healthy greens with a taste similar to a mojito!
