Title:
Carmel Apple Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Granny Smith Apples Chopped
6 Frozen snickers chopped
1 tub Cool whip topping
1 Package vanilla pudding

Directions:
Chop the six apples and place in a bowl.  Chop the six snickers and place in the bowl.  Add tub of cool whip and package of powdered pudding mix.  Mix all together and let it sit in the refrigerator for a couple of hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
Laura Dobbins Corbett
Description: 
A chopped apple salad that looks more like a chicken salad but the most delicious salad/dessert you will eat.  It was gone it about 20 minutes.
