Title:
Viva Las Elvis French Toast Has Left the Building Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/797695.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Fiber One crumbs
1 tablespoon toasted sliced almonds
3 eggs
1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon of Salt
8 slices low carb bread
2 mashed banana
1/4 cup peanut butter
1/4 cup Fiber One

Directions:
Heat griddle over medium-high heat. Combine Fiber One crumbs and almonds in a bowl. Combine egg, vanilla extract and cinnamon in another bowl. Combine Banana, Peanut Butter and Fiber One. Spread mashed banana mixture on 1 side of 2 pieces of bread; top with the remaining bread slice to make a sandwich. Make 4 Sandwiches in this manner. Cook sandwiches on griddle until golden brown, 2 minutes per side. Then bake in 350 degrees oven for 20 minutes. Cut in half and serve with fresh fruit or sugar free syrup if desired.
