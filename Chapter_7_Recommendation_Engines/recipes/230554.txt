Title:
Feta and Almond Cheese Ball  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, at room temperature
5 ounces crumbled feta cheese
1/3 cup butter, at room temperature
20 black olives, drained and sliced
3 green onions, chopped
1 (3 ounce) package sliced almonds

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, feta cheese, and butter together in a bowl using your hands; add black olives and green onions. Form mixture into a ball; sprinkle with almonds. Lightly press almonds into cheese ball. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Sabryna Cornish
Description: 
This is a decadent cheese ball that showcases the flavors of feta cheese. It always gets rave reviews when I bring it to parties.
