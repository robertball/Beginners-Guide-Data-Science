Title:
Stuffed Red Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large red peppers - seeded & cut in half lengthwise
2 cups cooked brown rice
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1 14oz can diced tomatoes - drained
1 cup tomato sauce
1 cup shredded mozzarella
1/4 cup grated parmesan
1 tsp chopped garlic

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Brown ground beef and drain. Return beef to pan and add garlic & chopped tomatoes and heat together on medium for 3-5 minutes.
Transfer Meat mix to large mixing bowl. Add cooked rice, 1/2 cup of the tomato sauce, grated parmesan & 1/2 cup of mozzarella.
In a 9x13 pan add  2 tbl spoons of tomato sauce to lightly coat bottom and place halved peppers, skin side down. Fill each pepper with filling, spoon remaining sauce on top of each pepper and tightly cover with foil.
Bake for 30 minutes, then remove foil, sprinkle with remaining mozzarella and bake or broil until cheese is bubbly.
