Title:
Tex-Mex Smothered Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless boneless chicken breasts
1 egg + 2 Tbsp water
4 tbsp canola oil
1 cup flour
1 tbsp seasoning salt
2 tbsp chili powder
2 tbsp cumin
1/2 - 1 tsp cayenne
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp white pepper
1/2 cup shredded cheese (old cheddar or monterey jack)
1/2 cup chopped green onion
1/2 cup diced tomato
3 tbsp chopped cilantro

Directions:
1.  Rinse chicken and pat dry with paper towels.
2.  In a large plastic bag, combine all dry ingredients and shake.
3.  In a bowl, beat egg and water.
4.  Dip chicken pieces in egg, then place in bag and shake to coat in flour mixture.
5.  On top of stove. fry pieces in oil 5-6 minutes per side until dark golden brown.
6.  Place pieces of chicken in a large casserole dish and bake in a 400 degree oven additional 20 minutes until cooked through.
7.  For the last 5 minutes of cooking top with cilantro, cheese, green onion and tomato.
Enjoy
Great served with beans and rice and grilled corn on the cob.

Submitter: 
MomWhoCooks
Description: 
Spicy (but not too hot), Crispy Fried chicken breasts smothered with melted cheese, green onion and tomato.  Serve with sour cream if you like.
