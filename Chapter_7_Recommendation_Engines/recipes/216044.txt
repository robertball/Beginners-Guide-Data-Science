Title:
California Halibut Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993331.jpg
Ingredients:
6 (4 ounce) halibut fillets, 2 inches thick
1/2 cup KNUDSEN Sour Cream
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup KRAFT Grated Parmesan Cheese
1 tablespoon lemon pepper seasoning
1 dash ground red pepper (cayenne)
3/4 cup Japanese-style bread crumbs (panko)

Directions:
Heat oven to 425 degrees F. Place fish in 15x10x1-inch pan.
Mix all remaining ingredients except bread crumbs; spoon over fish. Sprinkle with crumbs.
Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until top is golden brown and fish flakes easily with fork.

Submitter: 
KNUDSEN
Description: 
Tender halibut fillets cook up flaky and tender in a savory sour cream and Parmesan sauce beneath a crisp brown panko crust.
