Title:
Shrimp Pasta with Clam Sauce and White Wine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16oz package) spaghetti
3 tablespoons Olive Oil
5 cloves garlic minced
2 dashes crushed red pepper flakes
1 pound med shrimp 100ct or larger
1/4 cup white clam juice
1/4 cup dry white wine
3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1 tablespoon chopped fresh sweet basil
sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
1 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese.

Directions:
In a large pot with boiling water cook spaghetti until al dente.
Meanwhile, in a medium sized saucepan over medium heat, cook olive oil, minced garlic and crushed red pepper flakes until garlic is soft and translucent.
Add shrimp, clam sauce, wine, chopped parsley and basil, salt and pepper to taste, and bring to a boil.
lower heat and simmer for 3 to 5 minutes, reducing sauce a bit.
Toss pasta with simmered shrimp and sauce., add freshly grated parmesan cheese and serve.
