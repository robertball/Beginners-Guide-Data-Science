Title:
Zumba's Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 avacados
1.5 oz of pico de gallo
lemon juice to taste
1/8 cup cilantro, fresh, chopped
1/4 tsp. garlic
1/4 tsp. kosher salt

Directions:
Remove stem, cut, pit and scoop avacados.
discard stem, skins.
Mash pulp.
Add remaining ingredients.
Taste, adjust seasonings if necessary.
Refrigerate.
