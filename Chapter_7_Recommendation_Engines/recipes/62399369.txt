Title:
Sammys stuffed bell peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/471781.jpg
Ingredients:
2- lb. ground beef
4- large bell peppers seeded
1- small onion choped
3-green onions choped
3-cloves garlic choped
1/2-cup cooked rice
1- can mixed veggies
1- egg
15.2oz.cans 0f tomato sauce
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
chop a small onion,3-green onion and 3-cloves of garlic, put in large mixing bowl,add egg,can of mixed veggies half cup of cooked rice, cooled,. and a 15.2oz.can of tomato sauce,add ground beef to mixture in bowl,salt and pepper to taste.Cut tops off bell peppers and remove the seeds.Take a spoon fill peppers.Put in a large baking dish and pour the remaining cans of tomato sauce over stuffed peppers.Cover with foil and put in oven.Bake at 350% for 1hr.and a 1/2. Remove and surve.
