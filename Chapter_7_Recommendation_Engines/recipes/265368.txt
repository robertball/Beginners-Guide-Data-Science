Title:
Quick-and-Easy BLT Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/479374.jpg
Ingredients:
6 slices thick-cut bacon
2 romaine hearts, chopped
1 large tomato, chopped
1 large avocado, chopped
Dressing:
1 cup mayonnaise (such as Best FoodsÂ®)
1/3 cup red wine vinegar
1 pinch salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper, or more to taste

Directions:
Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain bacon slices on paper towels.
While bacon is cooking, combine romaine, tomato, and avocado in a bowl.
Combine mayonnaise and vinegar in a bowl; add salt and black pepper.
Pour dressing over salad and toss to combine.

If you love BLTs, here is the salad for you! I like to have this quick and easy salad on a hot night with a glass of chardonnay!
