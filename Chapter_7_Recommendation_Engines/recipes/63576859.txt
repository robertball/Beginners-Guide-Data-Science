Title:
BANANA DROP COOKIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c sugar
3/4 c shortening
1 c mashed bananas
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
1 3/4 c flour
1/2 tsp soda
1 tsp  baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp nutmeg
3/4 tsp cinnamon
1 c oatmeal (I like old fashioned rolled oats)
1/2 c. milk

Directions:
Blend sugar and shortening.
Add bananas , egg and vanilla.
Sift dry ingredients (I don't do this if in a hurry - doesn't seem to make a difference)
Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients.
Blend in milk and oatmeal in alternately.  Do not overbeat.
Add nuts last and mix until they are just folded in.
Drop from tsp.
Bake 375*F for 12 min.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Hearty moist healthy cookies.  May opt to use wheat PASTRY flour for half or all of flour for an even healthier cookie.
