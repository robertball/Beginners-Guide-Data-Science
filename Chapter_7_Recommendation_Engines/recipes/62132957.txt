Title:
Garlic Sesame Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups green beans, whole, fresh or frozen
2 Tablespoon oil
1/4 teaspoon sesame oil
3 garlic cloves, crushed
2 Tablespoon grated fresh ginger
splash of soy sauce
1/2 to 1 teaspoon honey
Kosher salt to taste

Directions:
If using frozen beans, thaw first.  Put oils in a saute pan and add the beans.  Mix in the garlic and ginger, add a splash of soy sauce, honey and salt.  Toss and saute until tender.

Submitter: 
SHECOOKS2
Description: 
This quick and easy recipe for green beans are infused with the delicate flavor of garlic, ginger and sesame.  This is a recipe I created with some of my favorite flavors.
