Title:
Tuscan Chicken w/ white beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large fennel Bulbs
3tsp. olive oil
24 Boneless Chicken cut into 3/4 inch pieces
3tsp. dried Rosemary (crushed)
1 1/2 tsp. blk ppepper
3 can nosalt added Stewed Tomatoes, undrained
3 can reduced sodium Chicken Broth
3 can Cannellini Beans, rinsed and drained

Directions:
Cut off and reserve 3/4 C. fennel (feathery tops)
Chop Bulbs into 1/2" pieces
Heat Oil in Lrg sauce pan- medium Heat
add bulb
cook 5 minutes stirring occasionally
Sprinkle Chicken w/ rosemary and pepper,
Add to sauce pan
cook and stir 2 minutes.
Add tomatoes and Broth, Bring to Boil
Cover and simmer 10 minutes
Stir in Beans,
Simmer uncovered 15 minutes till sauce thickens
Laddle in Bowls, tow with reserved fennel tops.

Submitter: 
Tammy A Farnham
Description: 
Hearty chicken soup with a powerfully robust flavor.  Great winter warm up.
