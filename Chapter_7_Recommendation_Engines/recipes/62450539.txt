Title:
Sausage Skillet Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons vegetable oil
1 24-ounce package frozen hash brown potatoes
½ teaspoon dried oregano leaves
½ teaspoon pepper
2 cups broccoli flowerets
1 ring sausage, slashed
3 1-ounce slices cheddar cheese, cut diagonally in half

Directions:
Heat oil in 10-inch skillet until hot.
Add potatoes, oregano and pepper.
Cover and cook 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until potatoes are light brown.
Add broccoli and sausage.
Cover and cook about 15 minutes or until sausage is hot.
Top with cheese.
Cover and heat until cheese is melted.
