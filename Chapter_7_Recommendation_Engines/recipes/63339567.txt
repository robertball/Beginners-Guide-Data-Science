Title:
Lemon Crab Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. olive oil
1 lb. cooked crab meat
1/4 cup white wine
1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
salt and pepper
1 lb. pasta
1/4 cup grated Parmesan
1/2 cup chopped flat leaf parsley
reserved pasta water

Directions:
Cook pasta al dente, drain, and reserve about 1/2 to 1 cup of the pasta water.
Heat olive oil in a saute pan, add crab and heat just to warm through.
Add wine to crab, bring just to a boil, reduce heat to low, add lemon juice and salt and pepper to taste.
Add cooked pasta to skillet and add Parmesan and parsley.
If pasta seems dry, add some of reserved pasta water.
Serve garnished with Parmesan.
