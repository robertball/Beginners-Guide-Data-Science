Title:
Victor's Flan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Caramel Mixture:
4 cups sugar
1 cup water
Flan:
12 eggs
12 egg yolks
3 tbsp vanilla
5 cans evaporated milk
5 cans condensed milk
1 1/2 cans whole milk (use evaporated can)

Directions:
Caramel Mixture:
Cook until caramel color. Pour in bottom of 4 pie pans
Flan:
Cook at 350' - check after 1 hour
When done:
*top should be brown
*wiggly but not runny

Submitter: 
Chef Hurley Baird
