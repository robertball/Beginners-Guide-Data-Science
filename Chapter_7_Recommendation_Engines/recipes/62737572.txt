Title:
Famous Restaurant Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
8 oz cream cheese
1 cup half-and-half
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
1 (16 ounce) package dry fettuccine pasta

Directions:
Place pasta in a large pot of lightly salted boiling water. Cook until tender, about 8 minutes. Drain.
Combine the butter, cream cheese, and half and half in a saucepan. Season with garlic and salt and pepper to taste. Bring to a low boil over medium-low heat. Cook, stirring, until thickened slightly. Serve over 12 ounces of pasta.
