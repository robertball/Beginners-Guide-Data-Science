Title:
Pasta Fagioli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb ground beef
2 hot or mild italian sausages
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp chili flakes
4 cups chicken broth
2 cups canned chopped tomatoes (with juice)
1 cup dried macaroni
1 can mixed beans (garbanzo, kidney, white etc.) drained.
2 cups chopped fresh spinach
Bocconcini Cheese (fresh Mozzarella balls) for garnish (optional).
Grated Asiago or Parmesan Cheese for garnish (optional).

Directions:
Squeeze Italian sausage meat from the casings and brown with ground beef in a large stock pot.
Drain excess fat from meat if necessary.
Add chopped onion and garlic and saute 5-7 minutes.
Add oregano, chili powder and chili flakes.
Add chicken broth and tomatoes (with juice) and bring to a boil.
Add macaroni and boil until desired tenderness according to package instructions.
5 minutes before serving stir chopped spinach and canned beans through soup.
Serve piping hot and garnished with Bocconcini, Asiago or Parmesan cheeses if desired!

Submitter: 
Kristen Mumtaz
Description: 
Delicious and easy soup is a hearty meal on it's own.  A great one pot dinner.  Tasty when garnished with grated Asiago cheese.
