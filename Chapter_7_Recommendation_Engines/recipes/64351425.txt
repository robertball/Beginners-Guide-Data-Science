Title:
pressure cooker taco pulled pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 lb pork shoulder/butt (boneless) room temperature
2 T canola oil
1 pkg (1.25 oz,) taco seasoning
kosher salt
2 c. chicken broth

Directions:
1. remove excess fat (cap) from meat
2. divide meat into 4 equal portions. rub a little salt into pieces
3. brown meat in wide pan a little at a time in oil at med-hi heat till slight crust forms on both sides (about 8 min per side)
4. Remove pieces to board and rub taco season into each side.
5. pour broth into pressure cooker with rack and place meat on rack
6. lock lid and bring to pressure according to your cooker.  maintain low pressure for 45 min.  use natural release.  check meat.  it should be tender and falling apart. pull apart with 2 forks
7. use in tacos or other applications.

Submitter: 
skewtour
Description: 
The technique remains the same:  tweak this basic technique and add you own twist--bar-b-que, asian, etc. the meat turns out juicy, tender and delicious.
