Title:
Pineapple Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tbsp melted butter
4 Tbsp flour
4 Eggs (well beaten)
1 Cup of Sugar (can reduce to 1/2 cup less sweet ...still good)
1 20 oz can of crushed pineapple with juice.
Cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Mix first four ingredients well. Add crushed pineapple.  Stir well. Sprinkle top with cinnamon. Bake for 1 hour.  Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Santa Maria Cook
Description: 
A mixture of pineapple, flour, butter eggs  and cinnamon.  Excellent side dish with ham or pork. My In-laws are from Pennsylvania.  This recipe is from of the Aunts that live there. A Christmas dinner must-have.
