Title:
Breaded Ranch Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup crushed cornflakes
3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 envelope ranch salad dressing mix
1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted
8 boneless skinless chicken breast halves (2 pounds)

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the cornflakes, parmesan cheese and salad dressing mix.
Dip chicken in butter, then roll in cornflake mixture to coat.
Place in a greased 13" x 9" baking dish.
Bake uncovered at 350F for 45 minutes or until chicken juices run clear.
