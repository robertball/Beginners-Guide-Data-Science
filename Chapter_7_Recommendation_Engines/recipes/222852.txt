Title:
Todd and Jenny's Favorite Summertime Fluff Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2391568.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (6 ounce) containers lemon yogurt
1 (8 ounce) container extra-creamy whipped dessert topping
1 cup blueberries
1 cup raspberries
1 cup blackberries (optional)
1 (9 inch) prepared deep dish graham cracker pie crust

Directions:
Mix lemon yogurt and whipped dessert topping together in a large bowl. Fold blueberries, raspberries, and blackberries into the yogurt mixture; pour into prepared pie crust.
Refrigerate until the mixture sets, about 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Kathy F.
Description: 
This is a light and fluffy perfect summertime dessert. It is perfect on those hot summer nights.
