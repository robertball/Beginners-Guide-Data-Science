Title:
Barbecue and Coleslaw Pretzel Crisps® Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3743025.jpg
Ingredients:
20 Original Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®
1/2 pound shredded, fully cooked pork shoulder (or other favorite barbecued meat)
1 pint prepared coleslaw (homemade or from the deli)
Favorite barbecue sauce (optional)

Directions:
Top each Pretzel Crisp(R) with 1 teaspoon or so of barbecue. Top the barbecue with a bit of coleslaw.
Arrange on a serving platter. Serve with barbecue sauce to drizzle on top.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
Everything you'd expect to see at a Southern barbecue joint--smoked barbecue, creamy coleslaw, and sweet sauce--all in an easy appetizer quickly made with store bought items.
