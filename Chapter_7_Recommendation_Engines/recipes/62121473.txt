Title:
Mike's Mexican Madness Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can red beans
1 can refried beans
1 can corn niblets
1 seven ounce jar salsa
1 pound chicken, diced and sauteed with minced garlic
3 cups cooked rice
1 cup shredded cheese
2 8.5 ounce boxes corn bread mix
eggs
milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400.
Cook rice.
Dice chicken and sautee with minced garlic.
In baking dish, layer refried beans, corn niblets, red beans, chicken, cooked rice, salsa, and cheese.
Mix corn bread according to package and spread a thin layer on top of the cheese.
Bake for 30 minutes. Check center to make sure cornbread is baked. Return to oven until cornbread is done.
Serve with slotted spoon.

Submitter: 
KATIES8422
Description: 
My boyfriend's love for Mexican food inspired this creation. It's a casserole that includes beans, refried beans, corn, rice, salsa, chicken, cheese, and corn bread.
