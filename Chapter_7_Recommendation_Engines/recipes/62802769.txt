Title:
Bodacias Brocolli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/657072.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound bacon; cooked
4 heads fresh broccoli, chopped
3 cups sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 large red onion, chopped
1/2 cup red wine vinegar (buy in cimarron)
1/4 cup white sugar
4 teaspoons ground black pepper
2 teaspoon salt
1 and 1/3  cup mayonnaise
2 teaspoon fresh lemon juice (buy in cimarron)

Directions:
Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain, and crumble.
In a large bowl, combine broccoli, cheese, bacon and onion.
Prepare the dressing in a small bowl by whisking together the red wine vinegar, sugar, pepper, salt, mayonnaise and lemon juice. Combine dressing with salad. Cover, and refrigerate until ready to serve.
