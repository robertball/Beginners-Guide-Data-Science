Title:
Mashed Cauliflower Au Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large head cauliflower, broken into florets
3/4 c. shredded Parmesan Cheese
1/2 c. shredded Corby-Monterey Jack cheese
3 T butter, cubed
3/4 tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp Montreal Steak seasoning
Topping:
1 c. Panko bread crumbs
1 tsp. Italian Seasoning
1 tsp. Garlic Salt
1 T Parmesan Cheese
1/4 c butter melted.

Directions:
1. Placed cauliflower in a stock pot; add water to cover.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer uncovered until very tender.  Drain, transfer to a large bowl.  Mash cauliflower, stir in cheeses, butter and seasonings.  Transfer to a greased baking dish.
2.  In a small bowl, mix bread crumbs and melted butter until evenly coasted;  sprinkle over cauliflower mixture.  Bake, uncovered at 350 degrees for about 40 minutes or until topping is golden brown.

Submitter: 
Grace Carolyn Bowers-Reimondo
Description: 
This recipe transforms Caulflower into something more like
mashed potatoes but with more flavor.
