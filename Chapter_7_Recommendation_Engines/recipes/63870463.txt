Title:
Ham & Cheese Croissant Strata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large croissants, split
6 slices swiss cheese
2 c. chopped cooked ham
8 eggs
1 1/2 c. milk
2 t. Dijon mustard
1/4 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
1 c. shredded Gruyere cheese (4 oz)
chopped fresh chives

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Spray a 9 x 13 casserole.
In baking dish, arrange croissant bottoms, cut side up, in single layer.  Top each with a slice of Swiss cheese, sprinkle with ham. Cover with croissant tops, cut sides down.
In large bowl, beat eggs, milk, salt and pepper.  Pour over croissants.  Sprinkle with Gruyere cheese.
Bake 35 minutes until brown and set.
