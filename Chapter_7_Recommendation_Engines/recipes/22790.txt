Title:
Sweetened Condensed Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7219660.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup boiling water
6 tablespoons butter
1 cup white sugar
2 2/3 cups nonfat dry milk powder

Directions:
Combine water, butter, sugar and milk powder in a blender and blend until thickened, 2 minutes.  Store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Sharon Sincavage
Description: 
Great substitute for commercial condensed milk for use in COOKED recipes.
