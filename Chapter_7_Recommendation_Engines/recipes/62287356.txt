Title:
Japanese Cucumber Salad - Sunomono Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/355415.jpg
Ingredients:
5 Cucumbers
2 teaspoons salt
1/2 cup lemon juice
1/2 cup sugar
1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
Toasted sesame seeds

Directions:
Peel cucumbers lengthwise leaving thin stripes. Then cut in half lengthwise and scoop out seeds with a spoon. Slice the cucumber thinly into a bowl.
Add salt to cucumber and mix with hand until limp. Set aside for 45 minutes.
Transfer cucumbers to a strainer and squeeze as much liquid out as possible and discard the salt water.
In a bowl mix the lemon juice, sugar & vinegar then add the cucumber.
Dish into smaller bowls and sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve.

Submitter: 
Celsia
Description: 
A delightfully tart and sweet salad that's great anytime.
