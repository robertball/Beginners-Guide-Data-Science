Title:
Air Fryer Roasted Okra Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5666041.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound (approximately 8) okra pods, sliced and ends discarded
1 teaspoon olive oil
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground pepper

Directions:
1. Set the air fryer to 350 degrees.
2. Place okra slices, oil, salt and pepper in a mixing bowl. Stir gently to combine.
3. Place okra in a single layer in the basket of the air fryer.
4. Cook for 5 minutes. Toss. Cook an additional 5 minutes. Toss.
5. Cook the final 2 minutes and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Soup Loving Nicole
Description: 
Love the flavor or okra but dislike the slimy texture? Air fry the slime out of it!
