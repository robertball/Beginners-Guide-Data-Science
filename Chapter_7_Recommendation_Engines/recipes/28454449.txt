Title:
Meatloaf with "Snappy Sauce" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/364830.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
10 crackers, crushed
1 package dry onion soup mix
1 egg
1 carrot, shredded
1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. worchestershire sauce

Directions:
Combine the first five ingredients. Mix well.
Shape into a loaf and place in greased baking pan.
Combine remaining ingredients. Spread the sauce over the loaf.
Bake at 350 degrees for 60 minutes.

Submitter: 
Kelli F
Description: 
My husband who didn't like meatloaf now requests this one. It is a combination of my mom's meatloaf recipe, my aunt's sauce, and the addition of carrots for my husband.
