Title:
Peach-Tomato Chutney or BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1242882.jpg
Ingredients:
olive oil
4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
1 onion, chopped
3 peaches (grill/broil then rough chop)
3-4 whole canned tomatoes and little juice (approx 1 c)
3 oz tomato paste
1/4 c apple cider vinegar
1 T tamari
2 T agave nectar
2 T BBQ sauce (optional)
Juice 1/2 lemon
1.5 tsp cumin
1 tsp chili powder
3/4 tsp paprika
Pinch powdered ginger
1 tsp dried oregano
Little Dijon mustard (or dry mustard)
BBQ seasoning salt
salt to taste

Directions:
grill or broil peaches until charred or soft (brush with olive oil, season with salt/pepper; approx 3-5 min each side if broil)
meanwhile, add olive oil to large saute pan, low-medium heat
saute garlic, onion for 5-6 min with salt
add remaining ingredients (except cilantro)
reduce heat to low and simmer for 25-30 min
puree to desired consistency or leave whole/chunky for chutney
season to taste
