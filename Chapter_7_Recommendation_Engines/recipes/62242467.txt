Title:
Upside-Down Date Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C boiling water
1 C cut up dates
=====================================================
1/2 C brown sugar (packed)
1/2 C granulated sugar
1 egg
2 tbsp melted butter
1-1/3 C flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 C chopped nuts (walnuts or pecans)
=====================================================
1-1/2 C boiling water
1-1/2 C brown sugar
2 tsp butter
=====================================================
Top with- whipped cream, cool whip or vanilla pudding

Directions:
Microwave or cook on stove - dates & boiling water
(I cook about 1 minute in microwave)
Cool completely (I like to set outside in the snow)
Cream- sugars, egg, melted butter until light
Add - dry ingredients - mix well
Stir in - nuts & cooled date mixture
Pour into well greased 9 inch square pan
Combine boiling water, brown sugar & butter
Stir well - Pour over top of cake batter
Bake - at 375 degrees for 40-45 minutes - test center of cake
Can be served warm or cold (I like it better cold)
Serve with whipped cream, cool whip or instant vanilla pudding

Renee Laufenberg

I got this recipe from an amish home after eating dinner and going on a sleigh ride with friends.
Delicious - Rich Dessert - One of my favorites
