Title:
Aub's no bakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups sugar
1 tbsp salt
1/2 cup cocoa
1 stick butter
1 cup milk
4 cups uncooked instant oatmeal
1 cup peanut butter

Directions:
Mix sugar, salt, and cocoa
add butter and milk
bring to boil for 1 minute
add all over ingredients and mix well
drop on wax paper
enjoy
