Title:
Cheesy Bratwurst stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 brats, skinned and chunked up or can use already cooked ones sliced in 1/2" or so
4 potatoes cubed, skin or don't...up to you
1 can drained green beans or use fresh if you have them.
1 c. grated cheddar or American
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 c. H2O

Directions:
brown bratwurst and put it all in the large skillet, cover and simmer 30min. or till potatoes are done.
