Title:
Turkey Oriental Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3C butter
1/2tsp ginger
2C celery
1 medium onion chopped
1C green pepper cut in strips
2Tbsp cornstarch
1tsp salt
1Can chicken broth
1Can mushrooms
1pkg chinese vegetables
1 pkg pea pods
1/2C water chestnuts
3C Shredded Turkey

Directions:
Add all the ingredients except cornstarch and broth. Saute all the other ingredients and  then add the turkey. Dissolve cornstarch in broth and salt to chiken broth. Add to the mix to the turkey and vegetables. Serve over rice.
