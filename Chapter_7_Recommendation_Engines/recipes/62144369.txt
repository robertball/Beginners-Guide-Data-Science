Title:
Fried Panfish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
About 14 De-boned and washed Crappie or Panfish fillets.
2 cups of dry bread crumbs ( I like crushed Saltines)
1 egg
Vegetable Oil
Season Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Cover bottom of pan with Vegetable Oil
Beat egg lightly and add fillets (Incorporate egg with fish)
Heat Skillet on Med./ High
In large bowl add bread crumbs and toss fillets in bread crumbs until coated thoroughly.
Add fillets to hot oil and fry until golden brown
Flip fillets once so both sides fry evenly.
Drain on paper towel and season immediately.
Repeat Process with clean oil for larger quantities

Submitter: 
Big Perm
Description: 
This is my favorite way to fry freshwater lake fish. I've never seen a recipe for it, so here's how I do my thing:)
