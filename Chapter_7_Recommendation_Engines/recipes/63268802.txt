Title:
Molasses Pecan Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. flour
3/4 t. baking soda
3/4 c. sugar
1/3 c. molasses
1/2 t. salt
1/2 c. margarine
1 egg
3/4 c. chopped pecans

Directions:
Sift together dry ingredients.
Cream margarine, sugar, and eggs. Stir in molasses.
Add dry ingredients. Mix in pecans.
Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.
