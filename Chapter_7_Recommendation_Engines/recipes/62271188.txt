Title:
Mexican Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups milk
1 vanilla bean
1 stick true cinnamon
1 tsp. urfa biber
8 ounces bittersweet chocolate
2 Tbsp. sugar
Whipped cream
Tea infuser or spice bag

Directions:
Using a sharp knife, split the vanilla bean.
Place biber into tea infuser or spice bag.
In a pot, combine milk, biber, cinnamon, and vanilla. Heat on medium until it is just below the boiling point (small bubbles will begin to appear). Remove biber and reduce heat to low.
Add milk and sugar. Gently stir until all chocolate is melted.
Remove cinnamon stick and vanilla bean. Pour into cups and top with whipped cream. Garnish with bittersweet chocolate shavings.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
Hot chocolate spiced with vanilla, cinnamon, and a hint of chili pepper. The perfect drink to warm you up on a cold winter night!
