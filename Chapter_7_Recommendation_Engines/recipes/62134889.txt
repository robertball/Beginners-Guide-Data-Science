Title:
Gluten Free Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Moist ingredients:
1 cup diced zucchini (washed but not peeled)
2 eggs
½ cup canola oil
1 teaspoon gluten-free vanilla
Dry ingredients:
½ cup white rice flour
½ cup sweet rice flour
¼ cup corn starch
2 Tablespoons tapioca starch
½ teaspoon xanthan gum
1 cup sugar
¾ teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
½ teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
Glaze:
1 Tablespoon powdered sugar
1 teaspoon lemon juice

Directions:
Combine the moist ingredients in a blender and mix until it resembles a milkshake. Set aside.
Combine the dry ingredients by mixing together well in a large bowl.
Add moist ingredient mix to dry ingredient mix. Stir until blended well.  Grease a large loaf pan and put mixture in pan. Bake 1 hour at 325 °. Check center for doneness with a toothpick. Remove from oven and let cool for a few minutes.
Make glaze by mixing sugar and juice together to form a runny glaze. Smooth over top of bread and let bread cool.

Submitter: 
Gluten Free Cook
Description: 
Moist and delicious bread made with gluten-free flour.
