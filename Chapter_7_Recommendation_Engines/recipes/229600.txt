Title:
Blended Strawberry Daiquiri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1016679.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice
5 strawberries
2 fluid ounces white rum
1 fluid ounce lime juice
1/2 fluid ounce triple sec
1/2 teaspoon confectioners' sugar

Directions:
Blend ice, strawberries, rum, lime juice, triple sec, and confectioners' sugar in a blender at high speed until smooth, about 30 seconds.

Submitter: 
Kathi Richards Smith
Description: 
Decorate this blended strawberry daiquiri with a fresh strawberry for an extra presentation touch. Serve in a martini glass with a straw.
