Title:
Stove top Beef and Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1005153.jpg
Ingredients:
1-2 lbs. of beef stew meat
1-2 Tablespoons sesame oil
1 20oz can beef broth
1 beef bouillon cube
1 teaspoon instant coffee granules
0.5 cup soy sauce
1 teaspoon ground ginger
4 crushed garlic cloves
0.5 cup brown sugar
0.25 cup corn starch
0.25 cup cold water
1 large chopped onion
2 lbs. fresh broccoli cleaned and cut into small florets

Directions:
In a heavy pan Saut√© Meat in oil till browned on all sides.
Combine broth, bouillon cube, coffee granules, soy sauce, ginger, garlic and brown sugar in a bowl.
Pour the mixture over the beef and allow to simmer on low till the beef easily pulls apart (approximately 2 hours).
Once the beef is tender add in the onions and broccoli and simmer for 5- 10 minutes till vegetables are cooked to your taste.
Combine the cornstarch with the cold water and stir till smooth. Add to the beef and vegetables and stir till thickened. Enjoy!!! p.s. great on hot rice =)

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
This is delicious served over hot rice. It is also very nutritious! Who needs to order out? If you like a little heat had your favorite spice.
