Title:
Everyone's Favorite Key Lime Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pie:
3 egg yolks
1 14 oz can sweet condensed milk
1/2 cup key lime juice
1/3 cup sifted powdered sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons grated lime rind
1 9 oz graham cracker crust
whipped cream:
1 cup whipping cream
1/3 cup sifted powdered sugar

Directions:
Beat first five ingredients in a bowl at medium speed for one minute. Spoon into crust. Bake at 325 degrees for 30 minutes. Chill for at least 2 hours. When pie is cool, beat whipping cream at medium speed until foamy. Gradually add powdered sugar, beating until soft peaks form - Don't give up too soon! Spread onto pie. Enjoy!
