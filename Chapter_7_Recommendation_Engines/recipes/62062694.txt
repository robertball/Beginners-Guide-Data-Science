Title:
Penne wih Vodka Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb box penne pasta, cooked
3 tbsp olive oil or butter
2 tbsp minced garlic
1 6oz can tomato paste
1 28 oz can italian tomato sauce
1 tbsp crushed red pepper
1 tbsp dried basil flakes
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
1 cup heavy cream

Directions:
In a large saucepan, saute garlic and olive oil over medium heat. Add tomato paste and saute 2 minutes, stirring to combine. Add tomato sauce, red pepper and basil. Add vodka. Simmer over medium heat, stirring constantly. Slowly add parmesan and heavy cream. Reduce heat and simmer until thoroughly combined and sauce starts to bubble. Toss with penne pasta and serve.

Submitter: 
rebeccalovestocook
Description: 
You can adjust the spiciness of this zesty dish by adding or subtracting red pepper. It is very easy to make and is also good with chicken!
