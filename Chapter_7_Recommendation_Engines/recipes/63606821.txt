Title:
Grandma Iona's Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 dozen eggs, hard boiled and diced
5 lbs of potatoes, peeled, boiled and sliced
2 large onions, diced
3 cups Miracle Whip
1 can evaporated milk
3 Tablespoons lemon juice
1/4 cup prepared mustard
1/4 cup white sugar
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
In a 8 quart bowl, combine dressing ingredients in a  and mix well.   Add in potatoes, eggs and onions.   Chill and serve.
Dressing alone will keep for 6 to 8 weeks in the fridge.

Submitter: 
duboo
Description: 
Creamy, sweet and tangy potato salad that my Mom perfected over time.   I've converted some of this and some of that into this recipe.
