Title:
Cabbage Rolls In A Crock Pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cabbage
2 large eggs
2 med. onions, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 t salt
2 t paprika
1 # ground beef
1 # sausage
1 C uncooked rice
Sauce:
1 # sauerkraut, drained
16 oz can tomatoes, cut up and drained
1 can tomato soup
1 T sugar
2 sm. onions, chopped
1 T butter (opt.)
16 oz can cut up tomatoes, undrained (diced tomatoes)

Directions:
Cook cabbage leaves until about half cooked
While cabbage is cooking, in a large bowl combine eggs, 1/2 C onion, garlic 1 t. salt 1 t. paprika and 1/2 t pepper
Mix meat and rice
Mix meat mixture with egg mixture.
Fill cabbage leaves and ford edges.
Place in crock pot with seams down.
Chop remaining cabbage.
Combine chopped cabbage, sauerkraut, tomatoes in juice, tomato soup, sugar, and remaining onion, salt paprika, and pepper.
Pour over cabbage rolls
Cook on low for 6-8 hrs depending on how fast your crock pot cooks.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Cabbage rolls are cooked in tomatoes and tomato soup with sauerkraut and onions
