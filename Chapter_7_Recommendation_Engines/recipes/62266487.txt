Title:
FROGHOPPER'S Fajita Seasoning Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
3 TBLS cornstarch
2 TBLS chili powder
1 tsp salt
1 tsp paprika
2 1/2 tsp sugar
1 1/2 tsp onion powder
1 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1/8 tsp chrushed red pepper flakes
1/2 tsp cumin
1 1/2 tsp chicken bouillon

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and store in air tight container - this recipe equals about 3 packets.
