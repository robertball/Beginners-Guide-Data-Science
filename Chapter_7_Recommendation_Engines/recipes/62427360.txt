Title:
Peanut Butter Bacon Cupcake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/448665.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup crunchy peanut butter
1/4 cup bacon fat
1/4 cup butter, softened
4 eggs
1 (18.25 ounce) package butter cake mix
2/3 cup water
1 pound bacon, fried crispy and chopped and split 2/3 and 1/3.
22 whole in-shell peanuts
Peanut Butter Cream Cheese Frosting
2 8 oz. bricks cream cheese, room temperature
2 cups Creamy Peanut Butter
4 tbs. whipping cream
5 cups powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Line cupcake pans with liners of choice
Combine peanut butter, bacon fat and butter. Cream until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at time, mixing well after each one. Add cake mix alternately with the water. Stir until combined. Mix in 2/3's of bacon. Scoop batter into liners, 2/3 full.
Bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes. Allow cupcakes to cool complete before frosting.
To Make Peanut Butter Frosting: Combine 2 cup peanut butter, and 2 cup cream cheese together until light and fluffy. Add the confectioner's sugar 1 cup at a time. Mix in enough cream to make the frosting the appropriate spreading consistency.
Apply to cool cupcakes. Garnish with chopped bacon and whole peanut.
