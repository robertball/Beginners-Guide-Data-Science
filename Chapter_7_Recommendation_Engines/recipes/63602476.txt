Title:
Homemade Pimento Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (3 oz.) cream cheese, softened
1 cup sharp cheddar cheese, grated
1 cup MontereyJack cheese, grated
1/2 cup mayonnaise
3 Tablespoons pimentos, smashed with fork
Dash of salt
Dash of Texas Pete Hot Sauce
Dash of cayenne pepper
Dash of worcestershire sauce

Directions:
In mixing bowl, add pimentos to grated cheese. Add salt, Texas Pete, worcestershire and cayenne pepper. Add mayonnaise and mix.  If mixture is too thick, more mayonnaise may be added.
Put in airtight container and refrigerate.  Best to let "set" overnight in refrigerator before serving in order for the flavors to meld.
