Title:
7up Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
(1) can cherry pie filling (1) large box cherry jello (1/2) cup sugar (1) cup water (1) cup 7up

Directions:
Mix together water, sugar and pie filling. Bring to a boil for 4 min. Remove from heat and add box of jello stir well until jello is desolved. Cool at room temp. about 10 min. then add 7up slowly when bubbles stop spoon them off, mix well and chill 1 hour befor serving. Do not use any other cola than 7up or diet 7up.

Submitter: 
Connie A. janes
Description: 
Instead of the can of cranberry sauce with your next Turkey and Dressing dinner try this mouth watering delight. Once made it will set up just like cranberry sauce, so good we make it for desert.
