Title:
Oatmeal Cups 6 Variety Lemon/Blueberry/ Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups old fashioned oats, gluten free certified if necessary1 teaspoon ground cinnamon1 teaspoon baking powder teaspoon salt2 cup unsweetened almond milk cup coconut sugar or brown sugar2 teaspoons vanilla extract cup pure maple syrupVARIATIONS (PICK ONE)Blueberry Almond1 cup blueberries, fresh or frozen cup sliced almondsLemon Poppyseed2 teaspoons lemon zest2 teaspoons lemon juice2 teaspoons poppyseedsOrange Cranberry1 cup dried cranberries2 teaspoons orange zest2 teaspoons orange juice cup chopped walnuts, optionalSweet Potato Carrot1 cup sweet potato puree cup grated carrots teaspoon grated nutmeg teaspoon ground clovesPeanut Butter Banana2 large bananas, mashed cup peanut butter, smooth or crunchyApple Cinnamon1 cup small diced apple, I used granny smithI

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees F. Add all ingredients to a large bowl and mix well until everything is combined.Spray a muffin tin with cooking spray and scoop oatmeal mixture in using a large ice cream scoop or a  measuring cup. The mixture will be a little watery, so it's important to mix well before each scoop to make sure you get an even combination of milk and oats.Bake oatmeal cups for 20 minutes, or until cooked through.Thank you for this awesome recipe Shelikesfood!#tfmtrecipes
