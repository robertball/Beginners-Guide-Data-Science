Title:
Stuffed Tomatoes Recipe

Ingredients:
Filling Ideas:
Tuna
Ground Sausage & Cheddar Cheese
Diced Ham & Swiss Cheese
Chicken, Marinara & Parmesan Cheese
Veggie-lover's: squash, mushroom, mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Slice off top 1/2" or so of red ripe tomato; scoop out seeds; fill with one of above suggested fillings; top with shredded cheese of choice and bake at 350-degrees for about 30 minutes or until done!
