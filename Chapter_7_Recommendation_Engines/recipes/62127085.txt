Title:
super simple seafood alfredo with variations Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. cream cheese, cubed(8oz.)
3/4 cup parmesan cheese
1/2 cup butter plus 2 tblsps
1/2 cup milk
salt & pepper to taste
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 cup bay scallops
about 12-15 shrimp

Directions:
saute garlic in tw tablesppons garlic, add shrimp and scallops untill cooked thru, set aside. add remaining ingredients and whisk together over medium heat untill smooth and creamy. if a thinner consistency is desired add more milk. return shrimp and scallops. serve over fettucine.
* with this very basic alfreo sauce the possibilities are endless, swap out the shrimp and scallops for...
VARIATIONS:
Chicken and broccoli
mushroom and onion
ham and mushrooms
chicken and ham
even bacon and peas
