Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2479974.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margarine
40 marshmallows (one small package)
4 cups crisp rice cereal
2 cups chocolate crisp rice cereal
1-2 cups shredded coconut
Caramel sauce (or other sauce of your choice)

Directions:
Melt butter or margarine in a 3-quart saucepan over low heat
Add marshmallows and stir constantly
Stir until marshmallows are melted and mixture is syrupy
Remove from heat
Immediately mix in crisp rice cereal and chocolate crisp rice cereal
Stir until all pieces appear evenly coated
Allow mixture to cool until it can be handled (15-20 min)
Pour shredded coconut into a shallow bowl
Once mixture is cool, roll into a ball in the shredded coconut
Place balls to cool for about 30 min
Drizzle with caramel or desired topping

A soft chewy treat that's also crispy! A rice krispy treat covered in coconut and drizzled with caramel.
