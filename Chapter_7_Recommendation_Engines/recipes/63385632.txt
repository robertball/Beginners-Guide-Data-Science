Title:
Rolled Maple Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 t Ener-G Egg Replacer
3 T rice or oat milk
3/4 c oat flour, plus more for rolling
3/4 c  barley flour
1/4 t salt
1/2 t baking powder
1/2 c vegetable shortening
3/4 c maple sugar
1/2 t pure vanilla extract
currants, or raisins, for decorating (ootional)
Dairy free chocolate chips, for decorating (optional)
Unsweetened coconut flakes, for decorating (optional)

Directions:
1.  In a small bowl, whisk together egg replacer with 2 T rice milk; set aside.  In a medium bowl, whisk together oat and barley flours, salt and baking powder, set aside.  In the bowl of an electric mixer fitted with a paddle attachment, cream shortening and sugar until blended.  Add egg substitute mixtrure, vanilla extract and remaining 1 T rice milk, mix until incorporated.  Add dry ingredients, mix until just combined, scraping dow the sides of the bowl with a rubber spatula if necessary.
