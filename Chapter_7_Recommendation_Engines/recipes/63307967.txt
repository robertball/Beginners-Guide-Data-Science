Title:
Chocolate-y Iced Mocha Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup cold coffee
1 packet diet hot cocoa mix
ice cubes
1 cup cold coffee
1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
2-3 Tablespoons of sugar free chocolate syrup ( such as Torani)

Directions:
Heat the 1/4 cup of cold coffee in microwave for 30 seconds
Mix in the hot cocoa mix
Fil large glass with ice cubes
Pour in the 1 cup of cold coffee, the almond milk, the sugar free chocolate syrup and the hot cocoa mixture.
Stir and enjoy
