Title:
Hearty Breakfast Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup oatmeal
1 cup soy milk (or non fat milk)
1 cup wheat flour
1/2 cup brown sugar (or honey)
1 Tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup ground flax
1/3 cup oat bran
1/3 cup substitue egg (or 1 egg)
1/2 cup applesauce
2-4 ripe bananas - mashed
3/4 cup raisins
3/4 walnuts
3/4 chocolate chips

Directions:
Combine oatmeal and milk in large bowl, set aside.
In separate bowl, stir together flours, sugar, baking powder and salt.
In another bowl combine egg, applesauce and mashed potatoes.
Add to oatmeal mixture.
Stir in flour mixture, add raisins, nuts and chips.
Fill in sprayed cupcake pans.
Bake 425 until done.
I live in a high altitude and have to bake it for 15-17 minutes.  Maybe 12-15?
Yield:  18 muffins.
