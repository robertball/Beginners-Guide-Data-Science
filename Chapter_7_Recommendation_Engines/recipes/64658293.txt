Title:
Alexander's Tropical Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Kiwis
6 Grapefruits
6 Granny Smith apples
6 Oranges
6oz Lemon Juice
6 cans Pineapple Chunks
6 Mango
6 Plums
6 Dragon Fruits

Directions:
1. Slice all the fruit.
2. Put an equal amount slices on each of the 8 platters.
3. The kiwi slices are supposed to be on the outside of the platters
4. Then place the grapefruit slices are supposed to be closer to the center of platters.
5. Continue with the rest of the fruit.
6. When all the fruit is on all the platters, squirt the lemon juice on everyone's fruit filled bowls.
7. Enjoy!
