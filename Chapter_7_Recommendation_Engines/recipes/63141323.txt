Title:
Oreo Cookie Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/803139.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 of 3.5oz package Vanilla pudding mix (u can use other 1/2 to "flour" a cake pan!)
1 cup milk
1 1/2 cups whipping/heavy cream
1/2 (20oz) package chocolate sandwich cookies
1/4 cup butter
1 1/2 Tablespoons powdered/ confectioner's sugar
1 (4oz) package cream cheese, room temp
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar

Directions:
Prepare pudding with the milk according to package directions. Refrigerate.
Pour whipping/heavy cream into medium mixing bowl add mixer beaters and put into the freezer.
Set aside 5 cookies.  Crush or put in a food processor, the rest of the cookies.  Melt butter and mix with crushed/processed cookies.  Press into 8x8 pan.  Put in freezer for 5 minutes.
Remove whipping cream and beaters from freezer.  Add the 1 1/2T powdered sugar and whip to stiff peaks.
Blend 1/2 of the whipped cream, all of the cream cheese and the 1/2c confectioners' sugar. Spread over crust.  Cover and place cake back in freezer 10 - 15 minutes.
Spread pudding over top of cake. Spread the remaining whipped topping on top of the pudding then sprinkle with the remaining cookies (cut into bite size pieces). Keep cake covered and refrigerated.

Oreo cookies in a smooth, creamy (but not too rich) dessert! Modified: 1/2 the cream cheese, using whipped cream & this is for an 8x8 pan!Modified from: Oreo Cookie Cake
