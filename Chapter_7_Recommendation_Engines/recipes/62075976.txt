Title:
Striped Delight Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 graham cracker pie crust
1 large box instant pudding, any flavor
2 cups milk
1 4 oz. pkg cream cheese, softened
1/4 white sugar (to taste)
3 Tbs milk
8 oz. tub whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, sugar, milk together.  Fold in half the container of whipped topping. (Place remaining topping in refrigerator for later). Blend til smooth. Spread into graham cracker crust.
Mix instant pudding according to pudding directions on box.  Spread on top of cream cheese layer.
Chill.  Spread top with remaining whipped topping just before serving.  Store leftovers (if any) covered in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
anmlnrs
Description: 
A ready made graham cracker pie crust, and instant pudding, whipped topping, and cream cheese are the basic ingredients for this delicious pie.
