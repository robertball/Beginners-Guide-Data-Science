Title:
Oatmeal Strawberry Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups of flour
2 cups of granulated sugar
3 teaspoons of cinnamon
2 teaspoon of baking powder
1 teaspoon of salt
1 1/2 cups of oatmeal (old fashion)
1 1/2 cups of vegetable oil
4 large eggs
1 1/4 pounds of strawberries, fresh or thawed (unsweetened)
4 tablespoons of oatmeal (old fashion)

Directions:
Mix flour, granulated sugar, cinnamon, baking powder, salt and oats in a large bowl. Add oil, eggs, strawberries and beat until just moist. Pour into two greased bread pan. Sprinkle with 4 teaspoons of oats. Bake uncovered for 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let cool for 5 minutes then transfer to plate (wire rack, cookiesheet, etc) to finish cooling.
