Title:
Maple Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/477508.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter (no substitutes), softened
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon maple flavoring

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream butter, sugar, vanilla and maple flavoring until smooth. Spread over toast, waffles and pancakes. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Kathy  Scott
Description: 
It's easy to add maple flavor to the breakfast table with this rich spread. Slather the butter on piping-hot pancakes, waffles and toast-- your family will love it!
