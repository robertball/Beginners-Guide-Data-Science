Title:
Vegan Cashew Cheese 2 Ways Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4564325.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups raw cashew nuts
1 tablespoon nutritional yeast
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 cup water
1 tablespoon sriracha sauce

Directions:
Place cashews in the bowl of a food processor; pulse into a fine powder. Add nutritional yeast, cumin, and garlic powder. Blend, pouring water into the feed tube while the processor is running, until smooth and creamy. Remove 1/2 the cheese from the food processor. Add sriracha sauce to the food processor; pulse until well combined.

Submitter: 
The Tortilla Channel
Description: 
Today I am sharing with you how to make vegan cashew cheese in 2 different ways. It's amazing how great this tastes.
