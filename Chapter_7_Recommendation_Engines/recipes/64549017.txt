Title:
Santa Fe Grilled Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T olive oil
1 t cumin, or to taste
1 t paprika, or to taste
1 t chili powder, or to taste
1 t black pepper, or to taste
1 t salt, or to taste
3 chicken breast halves
1 heart of Romaine lettuce, torn
4 Roma tomatoes, cubed
2 cucumbers, sliced
1 c mini carrots
1 bell pepper, any color, sliced
2 medium avocados, peeled, cored and sliced
1 c cheddar cheese, shredded
1 c toasted tortilla shreds (optional)
Ranch dressing or dressing of your choice

Directions:
Slice chicken into strips and coat with spices.
Cook chicken in olive oil over medium heat until cooked through. Set aside.
Combine vegetables into salad bowls. Toss with dressing.
Top with warm chicken, and sprinkle shredded cheese over the top.
Serve with crusty garlic bread.

Submitter: 
WILKBROWN
Description: 
Simple, delicious salad that's great with grilled chicken, chicken sauteed with a little honey or chicken baked with Mexican spices.
