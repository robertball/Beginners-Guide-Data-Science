Title:
Perogie Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/810876.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag frozen perogies
1 red pepper
Â½ onion
2 cloves garlic
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup chopped ham/bacon
1 cup shredded cheese

Directions:
Place perogies in bottom of 9 x 13 pan. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Saute peppers, onion and garlic.
Mix together soup and can of water, onions, pepper, and garlic.
Dump soup mixture over perogies.
Place ham/bacon on top and sprinkle with cheese.
Bake at 350 for 35-45 minutes.
