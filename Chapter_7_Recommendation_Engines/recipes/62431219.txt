Title:
Santa Fe Turkey Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices whole grain bread, toasted
1 tablespoon Kraft Sandwich Shop Garlic & Herb Mayonnaise
6 slices thinly sliced turkey breast
2 slices American cheese
1/4 avocado, thinly sliced
2 pieces leaf lettace
2 slices red onion

Directions:
1. Spread each slice toasted bread with mayonnaise on one side.
2. Fill with remaining ingredients.
