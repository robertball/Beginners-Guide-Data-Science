Title:
Eileen's Pineapple Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz can, crushed pinapple; drained
4 eggs
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter
5 slices bread; cubed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cream butter and sugar.
Add eggs and beat well.
Fold in pineapple and bread.
Pour into buttered 1.5 - 2 quart casserole dish.
Bake for 45 minutes to 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Samantha Kristina
Description: 
The one thing I crave at Thanksgiving almost as much as turkey!  It's easy to make and tastes wonderful.
