Title:
Roasted Red Pepper Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3+ large roasted red bell peppers (1 1/2 lb.)
3 medium Yukon Gold potatoes
1 large onion
olive oil to cook onion
4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
salt to taste

Directions:
coarsely chop peppers
peel and coarsely chop potatoes
cook onion in oil until lightly browned
add potatoes, peppers and broth
bring to boil and simmer covered until potato is tender
puree with immersible blender until smooth
thin with more broth if needed
season with salt
