Title:
Granola Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1092738.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 cups applesauce unsweetened
1 mashed banana
1.5 tsp stevia
5 cups old fashioned rolled oats
1/4 cup flaxseed meal
1 tbs ground cinnamon
3 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
2 3/4 cup milk
Top with raisins, walnuts, chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Mix eggs, vanilla, applesauce, banana and stevia in a bowl.
Add oats, flax, cinnamon, baking powder, salt and mix well with wet ingredients.
Finally pour in milk and combine.
Spray 18 cup muffin tin with cooking spray of cupcake liners.
Add toppings.
Bake for 30 minutes until toothpick comes out clean
