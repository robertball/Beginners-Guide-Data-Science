Title:
Picnic Style Lemon Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ White Cabbage - finely sliced and finely chopped or roughly grated
1 medium Carrot - finely sliced and finely chopped or roughly grated
½ Red Onion - finely sliced and finely chopped or roughly grated
3 Radishes - finely sliced and chopped
2 to 3tbsp Good Quality Mayonnaise
½ Lemon - zest and juice
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Put the cabbage, carrot, onion, lemon zest and radishes into a large mixing bowl;
Add the mayonnaise, lemon juice, salt and pepper, all to taste, and stir well to fully combine together all the ingredients;
Chill in the fridge until ready to serve.

A simple but delicious lemony addition to any salad or BBQ
