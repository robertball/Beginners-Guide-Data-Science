Title:
Cheese Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Graham Cracker crumbs
Butter
1 Lb and 2 oz cream cheese
3 eggs
1/2 tsp vanilla
3/4 cup sugar
1 tsp lemon juice
1 pt sour cream
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup (or to your taste)sugar

Directions:
Mix graham cracker crumbs with enough melted butter so it can be pressed into a springform pan to form crust.  Blend cream cheese, eggs, sugar, vanilla and lemon juice till smooth.  Pour into graham cracker crust and bake at 300* for 45 minutes.  IMPORTANT: cOOL thoroughly overnight is best.  Next day mix sour cream and 1/2 cup sugar, vanilla and pour over cooled cake.  Bake hot oven at 450* for about 7 minutes.  Chill. Can be topped with canned pie mix such as cherry or blueberry Chill again
