Title:
Babka Piaskowa (Sand Cake) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup plus 1 tbs butter or margarine
6 eggs
1/3 cup granulated sugar
1 tsp vanilla or lemon extract
1 2/3 cup flour
1 tbs baking powder
sifted powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Melt butter or margarine in a small saucepan and cool.
With electric mixer, beat eggs and sugar on high until pale and creamy, at least 10 minutes.
Blend in melted butter or margarine.
Add vanilla or lemon extract.
Combine dry ingredients in a small bowl and fold into egg mixture until smooth.
Pour into pan.
Bake 50-55 minutes or until pick inserted into the center of cake comes out clean.
Cool completely on rack.
Sprinkle with sifted powdered sugar.

Submitter: 
Carole
Description: 
Traditional Polish holiday cake.  The recipe was provided by a friend after an international choral festival.  This version is slightly Americanized as it does not use potato starch.
