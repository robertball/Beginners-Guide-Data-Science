Title:
Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup honey
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 cup vegetable oil
3 cups pumpkin puree
1 cup chopped dates
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 tsp. sea salt
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. ground cloves
4 tsp. baking soda
4 1/2 cups wheat flour
1/2 cup wheat germ

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees. Combine honey, sugar, oil, dates, pumpkin, walnuts, salt, cinnamon, cloves, and baking soda and stir well.  Stir in flour and wheat germ, and stir until well blended. Pour into 3 well-oiled 8 1/2 x 4 1/2 pans. Bake 45 minutes to 1 hour. Cool 20 minutes before turning onto a cooling rack.

Submitter: 
Rebecca Hennes
Description: 
This is a great fall classic that is a healthy breakfast or snack alternative, and since it makes 3 loaves you can either freeze some or give it away!
