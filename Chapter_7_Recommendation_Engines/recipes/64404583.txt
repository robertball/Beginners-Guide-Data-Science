Title:
No Knead Rustic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2259141.jpg
Ingredients:
3 1/2 c bread flour
2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp instant yeast
14 oz cool water

Directions:
Whisk together the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
Add the water, stir with the handle of a wood or plastic spoon starting at the sides until it forms a ball and starts to stick.
Leave covered with plastic wrap on counter for 24 hours.
Flour work surface and hands and turn out the dough, flatten, fold sides in using scraper, put in 8 1/2 x 4 1/2 pan, cover with towel, proof 1 to 2 hours.
After 1 hr 15 min, preheat oven 400 degrees.
Bake 45 min.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
Tasty rustic bread, almost sour dough.
