Title:
Chocolate Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs, beaten
1 cup sugar
1 cup corn syrup
2 T butter, unsalted
1 tea. vanilla
1 1/4 cups pecan halves
1 cup chocolate chips, semi-sweet
1 unbaked pie shell
Cool Whip

Directions:
Take the chocolate chips pressing them into the unbaked pie crust.  Next make a circular pattern starting from the center of the pie crust and layer the pecan halves as if making a swirl & set aside.
Mix the beaten eggs with the sugar, corn syrup, butter & vanilla.  Preheat oven to 350.  Pour over pastry shell already layered with the chocolate chips & pecans.  Bake 50 minutes or until set & then cool.
Serve with cool whip.

Submitter: 
Virginia
Description: 
My daughter, Melissa, makes this EVERY year for the holidays!  We aren't ones that enjoy traditional pecan pie but with the chocolate....oooooh yuuuuummmm!!!!!
