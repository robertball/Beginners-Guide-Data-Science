Title:
Janie's amazing smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unsweetened orange juice
1 heaping cup raw, cubed zucchini
2 tablespoons splenda
3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
5 ice cubes

Directions:
Put all ingredients into a blender and process on high for 1 minute or until smooth.

Submitter: 
Janie
Description: 
This is a low calorie, high fiber smoothie that tastes great! It helps keep you full, so it's great for dieters and it's lactose free!
