Title:
Creamy Dreamy Scrambled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 eggs
1/4 cup water
1 tsp seasoned salt
3/4 tsp sweet basil
1/4 rap coarse ground black pepper
2 TBL butter
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 3 oz pkg cream cheese, cubed
1 medium tomato, chopped

Directions:
In large bowl, combine eggs, water, seasoned salt, basil and pepper. Mix until well blended. Set aside.
In large skillet, heat butter over medium-high until hot; cook and stir onion 2 minutes or until tender. Pour egg mixture over onion; cook and stir 2 to 3 minutes or until eggs are almost set. Stir in cream cheese; cook 1 minute or until cheese is melted, stirring constantly. Top with tomato.
