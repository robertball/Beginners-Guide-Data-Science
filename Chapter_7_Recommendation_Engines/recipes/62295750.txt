Title:
Cherry Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (9 inch)  graham cracker crumb crust
1 (8 oz) package of cream cheese, softened
1 (14 oz) can of Eagle condensed milk
1.3 c. lemon juice concentrate
1 tsp. vanilla
1 (21 oz) can cherry pie filling (chilled)

Directions:
In a large bowl, beat cheese until fluffy. Gradually beat in condensed milk until smooth. Stir in lemon juice and vanilla. Pour into crust. Chill 3 hours or until set. Top with pie filling.
