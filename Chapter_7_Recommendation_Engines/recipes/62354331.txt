Title:
Greek fettucine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb spinach fettucine
1-1/2 cup chopped fresh tomatoes
3/4 cup cubed or crumbled feta cheese
(1 tbsp chopped mint)
(1/4 c. chopped black olives)
1/4 tsp ground pepper

Directions:
Cook spinach fettucine according to package directions.
Warm tomatoes (and chopped mint or black olives)in a small saucepan.  When warm, add chopped feta just until it begins to melt.
Mix with drained pasta.  Add salt if desired, but especially pepper, to taste.

Description: 
Spinach fettucine served with a quick sauce of tomatoes and feta cheese.
