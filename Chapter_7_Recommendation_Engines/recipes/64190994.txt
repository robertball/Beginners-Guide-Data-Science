Title:
Quick and Easy Shepherds Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
1 bag mixed veggies
4 cups brown gravy
2 tsp salt
2 tsp fresh ground black pepper
2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp taragon
6 cups mashed potatoes
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown beef in pan and drain.  Return to pan and mix in spices.  Add beef and veggies to 9x13 pan, pour on gravy to just before beef and veggies float.  Top with mashed potatoes and cover with cheese.  Bake 375 for 20 minutes until cheese is browning and ingredients are hot.
