Title:
STOVE TOP Easy Chicken Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (6 oz.) STOVE TOP stuffing mix for chicken.
1-1/2 lb. bonelss skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-size pieces.
1 can (10-3/4 oz.) condensed cream of chicken soup
1/3 cup sour cream
1 package (16 oz.) frozen mixed vegetables, thawed, drained.

Directions:
Prepare stuffing mix as directed.
Mix chicken, soup, sour cream and vegetables in 13x9 baking dish; top with stuffing.
Bake at 400F for 30 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
