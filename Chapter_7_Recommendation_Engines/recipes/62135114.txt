Title:
Low-Cal Layered Cheesy Alfredo Vegetable Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium zucchini, sliced thin
1 tpsp dried Italian seasoning
1 cup torn spinach leaves
2 tbsp chopped red onion
1 wedge Laughing Cow garlic & herb spreadable cheese
1 medium summer squash, sliced thin
1 cup chopped broccoli
1/2 cup chopped mushrooms
1 cup cottage cheese
2 tbsp light alfredo sauce
1 mini Babybel Light cheese round
2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray a 9 x 9 baking dish with cooking spray.
Line bottom of dish with slices from one zucchini.
Sprinkle layer with Italian seasoning.
Cover with spinach leaves and red onion.
Break Laughing Cow wedge into chunks and scatter over spinach and onion.
Cover with slices of summer squash.
Add layer of broccoli and mushrooms.
Combine cottage cheese (you may wish to drain first) and alfredo sauce in small bowl.  Pour mixture over broccoli and mushroom layer.
Cover with slices of second zucchini.
Grate mini Babybel over zucchini and sprinkle with parmesan.  Add salt and pepper as desired.
Cover with foil and bake for 30-40 minutes.
