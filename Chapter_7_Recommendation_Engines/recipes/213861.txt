Title:
KNUDSEN Creamy Layered BLT Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990105.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) container KNUDSEN Sour Cream
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
6 slices OSCAR MAYER Fully Cooked Bacon
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
2 tomatoes, chopped, divided
1 cup shredded lettuce
WHEAT THINS Original Crackers

Directions:
Mix sour cream and onion powder; spread onto bottom of 9-inch pie plate.
Heat bacon as directed on package; cut into small pieces. Sprinkle over sour cream mixture. Top with cheese, 3/4 cup tomatoes and lettuce; sprinkle with remaining tomatoes.
Serve with WHEAT THINS crackers.

Submitter: 
KNUDSEN
Description: 
Creamy sour cream is mixed with onion seasoning and layered with crisp bacon, Cheddar cheese and fresh tomatoes and lettuce for this tasty BLT dip.
