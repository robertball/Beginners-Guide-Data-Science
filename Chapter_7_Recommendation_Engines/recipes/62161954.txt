Title:
Mom's Tuna Macaroni salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/661446.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12) oz box salad-roni
1 large cans tuna (12.5 oz size)
4 med ripe tomatoes diced
1 small can chopped black olives
1 med red onion diced
2 hard boiled eggs chopped
2 T dried parsley flakes
1 T salad supreme seasoning
1 tsp salt
1 cup mayonnaise
1 T prepared mustard
1 tsp garlic finely diced

Directions:
Prepare macaroni according to package, drain and cool.  Drain tuna and add to pasta along with olives, tomato, onion, eggs, parsley, salad seasoning, salt, mustard and garlic.  Add mayonnaise and stir until combined.  Cover and refrigerate at least 4 hours.

Submitter: 
Karen Dean Covington
Description: 
This is a wonderful mayonnaise based tuna salad that goes great with any meal, or even by itself.  This salad taste even better if made a day in advance.
