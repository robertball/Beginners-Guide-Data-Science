Title:
Easy Bisquick Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups quick oatmeal
1 1/2 cups bisquick
1 cup raisins
1 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 cup milk

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375F
2. Mix all dry ingredients well
3. Add vegetable oil and milk and mix well until mixture is well moistened
4. Use tablespoon to measure out cookie mixture
4. Place drops of cookie mixture on ungreased cookie sheet 1 inch apart
5. Bake for 12-15mins at 375F

Submitter: 
drcook
Description: 
An easy and convenient way to use up whats left of that bisquick in your pantry!!  You can replace raisins with chocolate chips or Mn M's.  Have fun with this easy cookie recipe!!
