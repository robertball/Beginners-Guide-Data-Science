Title:
creole marmalade sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup orange marmalade
2 tsp Grey Poupon mustard
1 tsp  white horseradish sauce
1/2 tsp Tabasco sauce
Salt, to taste

Directions:
1.Combine all the ingredients in a small bowl.
2.Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
lwilliams001
Description: 
This is a glaze that goes well on pork or chicken it also serves as a dipping sauce for pork chicken or seafood
