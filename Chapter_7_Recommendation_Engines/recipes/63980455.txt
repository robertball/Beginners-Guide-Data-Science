Title:
Coconut Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp flour
4 teaspoons curry powder
1/4 tsp ground red pepper
2 1/2 lbs steak or chicken cut in 1 inch cuges
1 envelope onion soup mix
1 1/2 c baby carrots
1 large potato, cut into 1 inch chunks
1 can coconut milk
1 cup frozen green peas
rice or noodles

Directions:
1.  In large bowl, combine flour, curry powder and red pepper.  Add chicken; toss to coat.
2.  Brown chicken.  Put in crock pot.
3.  Add remaining ingredients
cook, covered on low 8 to 10 hours until meat is tender.  Stir in peas.  Let stand 5 minutes.  Serve with hot rice or noodles.
