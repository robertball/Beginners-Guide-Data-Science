Title:
Bettie's Cajun Dirty Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3350975.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can black beans undrained
1 lb ground beef
1/4 cup Pico de gallo
1 can 14.5 ounce chicken broth
1 cup white rice uncooked
1 to 2 tbsp Cajun seasoning
2 tbsp chopped green onion for garnish
Minced garlic to taste
Salt to taste

Directions:
In a medium saucepan mix rice, chicken broth, and beans. Bring to a boil and cook according to rice package directions.
Meanwhile, brown hamburger in garlic and Pico de gallo. Cook until no longer pink, drain.
Return meat to pan.
When rice is finished stir into meat mixture. Add salt and Cajun seasoning.
Garnish with green onions.

Submitter: 
Bettie Geese
Description: 
I got bored one evening and decided I wanted some dirty rice. Couldn't find a recipe I liked, so I made my own!
