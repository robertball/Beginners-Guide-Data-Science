Title:
Grandma's Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1899767.jpg
Ingredients:
6 C. water
½ pkg. lentils
6 chicken bouillon cubes or 6 t. powdered bouillon
1 can carrots, drained
1 T. dried minced onions
1 turkey kielbasa, cut in slices
1 T. butter
1/8 t. garlic powder
1 t. salt
Sprinkle of pepper

Directions:
Put all ingredients into large pot and bring to boil.  Lower temperature to a simmer and cook for ½ hour.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Cari Wensley
Description: 
I get lots of recipe requests for this easy soup! This is a family recipe, handed down from my Grandmother.  I have fond memories of this soup from my childhood.
