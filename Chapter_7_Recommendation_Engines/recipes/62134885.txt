Title:
Easy Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground beef
1 small onion, roughly chopped
1 package sliced mushrooms
1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup-low sodium or low fat
1/2 cup fat free half-half or lowfat milk
1 cup low fat sour cream
16oz package egg noddles
Extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
-Cook noddles according to package directions.
-In large skillet, brown ground beef. Drain. Set aside.
-Cook onions and mushrooms using just enough olive oil to coat pan, cook until tender.
-Add beef, soup and half-half. Cook for about 3-5 minutes.
-Turn off heat and stir in sour cream.
-Serve hot with egg noddles.

Submitter: 
Jackie Fernandez-Gonzalez
Description: 
A yummy stroganoff that's easy to make and easy on the wasit line using lean meats, low sodium, low fat products and fresh produce.

It's also easy on the wallet, meal cost under $13
