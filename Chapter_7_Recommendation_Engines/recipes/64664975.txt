Title:
Grandma Clark's Oatmeal Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup oatmeal
1 1/4 cups boiling water
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 cube butter
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
For Topping:
1 cup brown sugar
3 TB butter
3 ounces cream
1 cup walnuts, chopped
1 cup coconut

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Pour boiling water over oatmeal and let stand while mixing cake.
Cream together the sugars, butter and eggs.
Sift together the flour, soda, cinnamon and salt.
Mix flour mixture into creamed ingredients.
Add softened oatmeal and vanilla.
Pour into greased 9" x 13" pan.
Bake 30-40 minutes until toothpick inserted into center of cake comes out dry.
While the cake is baking, mix the topping ingredients together.
After removing the cake from the oven, turn the oven on Broil.
Spread the topping over the hot cake, and place it under the broiler until it begins to bubble and turns slightly brown.
Remove cake from the oven and cool prior to serving.

Submitter: 
Yomama
Description: 
A very moist, dense spice cake, with a wonderful walnut/coconut broiled topping.
