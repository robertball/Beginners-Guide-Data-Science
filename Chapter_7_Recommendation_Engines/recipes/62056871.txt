Title:
CHICKEN, BROCCOLI, RICE & CHEESE CASSEROLE IV Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 CUP OF LONG GRAIN RICE (UNCOOKED)
1 10 OZ CAN CHICKEN BROTH
1 10 OZ. CAN CREAM OF CELERY SOUP (OR MUSHROOM)
1/2 LB. CHICKEN BREAST (CUT IN CHUNKS)
1 HEAD OF BROCCOLI CUT IN FLOWERETTES
1 CUP OF GRATED CHEDDAR CHEESE

Directions:
IN AN 8" SQUARE PAN (OR CASSEROLE DISH) SPREAD UNCOOKED RICE.  POUR CAN OF CHICKEN BROTH OVER TOP.  ADD CUT UP CHICKEN, THEN LAYER BROCCOLI FLOWERETTES OVER CHICKEN.  SPOON CREAM OF CELERY SOUP OVER TOP, SPREADING OVER THE BROCCOLI.  SPRINKLE TOP OF CASSEROLE WITH CHEDDAR CHEESE.  COVER WITH FOIL AND BAKE 350 DEG. FOR ONE HR.
THIS RECIPE CAN BE DOUBLED USING A 9 X 13 PAN.

Submitter: 
BETTYCOOK
Description: 
THIS YUMMY CHICKEN, BROCCOLI, RICE & CHEESE CASSEROLE IS QUICK AND EASY TO MAKE.  EVERYTHING IS THROWN INTO ONE PAN UNCOOKED.
