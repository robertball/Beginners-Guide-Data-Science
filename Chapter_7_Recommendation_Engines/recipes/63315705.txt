Title:
"OG" Zuppa Toscana Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/877341.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Italian Sausage
1 1/2 tsp Crushed Red Peppers
1 Large Diced White Onion
4 strips Bacon diced
2 tsp Garlic Puree
10 Cup Water
5 Cubes Chicken Bouillon
1 Cup Heavy Cream
1 lb diced Russet Potatoes
1/4 of a bunch of Kale chopped

Directions:
1. Saute Italian Sausage and Crushed Red Peppers in a Skillet. Drain off the excess fat and set aside in a bowl.
2. In same skillet saute bacon, onion and garlic over medium low heat for 10-15 min, or until onions are soft.
3. Add chicken bouillon and water to a large pot and bring to a boil.
4. Add potatoes to pot and cool till soft.
5. Add Heavy Cream and cook till heated.
6. Stir in sausage, onions and the kale and cook for 10 min and serve.
