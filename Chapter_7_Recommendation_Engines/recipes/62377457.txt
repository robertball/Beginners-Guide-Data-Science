Title:
watermelon rind-chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small watermelon about 4 lbls
1 1/2 cups rice vinegar (not seasoned)
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 cup water
3 tablespoons minced peeled fresh ginger
2 tsp whole black peppercorns
2 thai chiles, thinly sliced crosswise
1 tsp coarse salt

Directions:
cut and scrape flesh from wm..cut away outer green skin. cut rind into 1 inch pieces (about 9 cups)
Bring rind, vinegar, sugar, cwater,giner, peppercorns, chiles and salt to a boil in lg saucepan. Reduce heat and simmer until rind is transluscent and tender adn liquid is syrupy,about 55 minutes. Divide mixture among 2 pint size and one  1/2 pint size sterilized glass jars; refrigerate. (can refrigerate for up to 3 weeks, to preserve it longer use white-wine vinegar at least 5% acidity) and can it in water bath.
