Title:
Molly's Ricotta Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2070176.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lbs ricotta
2 cups powdered sugar
1 teaspoon lemon juice
2-3 oz citron - cut up fine
¼ teaspoon cinnamon
3 cups flour
1 ½ teaspoon baking powder
1  4 oz stick of butter (softened)
1 teaspoon vanilla
4 egg - separated
¼ cup of water
1 tsp. lemon rind

Directions:
In large bowl add 3 lbs ricotta, 2 cups powdered sugar and 1 teaspoon lemon juice. Put in 2-3 oz citron, cut up fine, and ¼ teaspoon cinnamon.
Beat 4 egg whites and grated lemon rind until real stiff. Fold egg whites into ricotta mixture.
Set aside.
For the crust mix 3 cups flour,1 ½ teaspoon baking powder, 1 stick of butter (softened), 1 teaspoon vanilla, 4 egg yolks, ¼ cup of water. Blend with a fork, then knead. Make a big ball and cover with a towel for 15 minutes. Grease pie dish,cut dough in half, roll out one half and place in bottom of pie dish. Add ricotta mixture. Roll out other half of dough for the top.
Bake at 300 for 45-50 minutes. Turn oven off and leave in for 10 more minutes. Take out, cool completely, and serve.
