Title:
Pimento Shrimp Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups rice
3 cups water
1/2 stick of butter
1 lb shrimp, peeled & deveined
1/2 cup onions
1/2 bell pepper
6 chicken bouillon cubes
1 jar diced pimentos

Directions:
Add rice and water to slow cooker.
Melt butter in pan, saute veggies.
When solft, mix with rice in slow cooker.
Add shrimp & stir.
Turn rice cooker on.
Let steam 20 mins.
