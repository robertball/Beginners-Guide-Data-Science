Title:
Pumpkin Streusel Cheesecake Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cookie Base:
1 pouch (1 lb, 1.5 oz) oatmeal cookie mix
1/2 c crushed gingersnaps
1/2 c finely chopped pecans
1/2 c cold butter
Filling:
2 pkgs cream cheese, softened
1 c sugar
1 c canned pumpkin
2 tbsp flour
1 tbsp pumpkin pie spice
2 tbsp whipping cream
2 eggs
Topping:
1/3 c chocolate ice cream topping
1/3 c caramel ice cream topping

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.  Stir together cookie mix, cookies and pecans.  Cut in butter and mix until crumbly.  Reserve 1 cup.  Press remaining mixture into bottom of 9x13 pan.  Bake 10 minutes, cool 10 minutes.
Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth.  Add remaining ingredients, beat until well blended.  Pour over warm cookie base.  Sprinkle with reserved crumbs.
Bake 35-40 minutes or until center is set. Cool 30 minutes. Refrigerate at least 2 hours.
Before serving, drizzle with toppings.  Cut bars into 6 rows by 4 rows. Store in fridge.
