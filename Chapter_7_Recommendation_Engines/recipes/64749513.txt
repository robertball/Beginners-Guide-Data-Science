Title:
Chicken Salad Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6700631.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Chicken breast (you can use frozen or fresh)
1 14.5 oz. Can of Chicken broth
1/2 Teaspoon Poultry Seasoning
1/2 Teaspoon Celery Salt
1/2 Teaspoon Onion power
1/2 Teaspoon Garlic power
1  Teaspoon sugar
1/2 cup  Celery, Diced small
1/2 cup green onion, sliced thin
5 hard-boiled eggs, smashed
2 cups Mayonnaise or Miracle Whip or 1 cup of each. up to you
1 Tablespoon of Yellow Mustard
1/2-1/4 cup sweet Relish ( depends on how you like it)

Directions:
1.Boil Chicken in Pan with Chicken broth and 1 cup water. drain and  cool. Set aside.
2. Cook boil eggs to make hard-boiled eggs, Once cool ( peal) set aside
3. In a large bowl, add Onions, Celery and smashed eggs, Mayonnaise, sweet Relish and the Seasonings.
4. Pull cooled chicken apart and place in food processor, on chop, pulse until fine. ( note: if you have a small processor, place 1 or 1/2 chicken in food processor at a time, if all didn't chop up fine, remove large parts and place back into the food processor with the remaining breast).
5. Dump chicken into bowl with the mix and stir until nicely blended.
6. Cover and refrigerate for 1-2 hours, or overnight.
Serve on bread as a sandwich, or par it with a Potato Salad
