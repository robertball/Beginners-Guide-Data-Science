Title:
Szechuan Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tbsp soy sauce
2 Tbsp bottled oyster sauce
2 Tbsp rice wine or sherry
2 tsp white pepper
1 Tbsp sugar
2 Tbsp cornstarch
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Combine first five ingredients. Stir fry your choice of entree. Add sauce and toss to coat. Mix cornstarch and water together. Immediately add it to the pan to thicken. Serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
A spicy sauce that is great with vegetables, chicken or beef
