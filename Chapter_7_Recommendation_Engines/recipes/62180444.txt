Title:
Spanish Gazpacho Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ripe tomatoes
4 green onions finely chopped
½ cup green pepper
½ cucumber chopped
1 stick celery
1 clove garlic
1 cup bouillon water
2 cups tomato or Very Veggie Juice
2  T horseradish
1 T vinegar
1 T olive oil
½ cup croutons
sea salt and cayenne to taste

Directions:
Blenderize the vegetables (save some of the green pepper, cucumber and green onion) and tomatoes using the bouillon water and juice to liquefy.  Add alt and cayenne to taste.  Add the horseradish, vinegar and olive oil to the mixture.
Best when refrigerated for a few hours.
Serve each bowl of soup with croutons and chopped veggies.
