Title:
Moms homemade pasta sauce and meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cans of Italian tomatoes
1 Small can of Italian tomato paste
4 Cloves of garlic
Vegetable oil (enough to cover bottom of pan)
2lb ground beef (sirloin 80
% lean)
1lb of ground pork
6 pieces of white bread-crusts removed
1/2 c. milk
2 eggs
3 Tbs. parmesan cheese
pork chop (optional)
sausage (optional)
1 tbs of basil
1tbs pepper
2 Tbs. parsley
2 cloves of garlic

Directions:
1. Cover bottom of sauce pan with oil and garlic (4 cloves). Add any meat that you wish to add to your sauce. (e.g pork, sausage)
2. Brown all meat and set aside. Scrape bottom to loosen and leave brownings in pan.
3. Strain whole tomatoes using only the juice. (I usually put of few pieces of tomato as well. You can smash them using a bowl and pressing them through the strainer catching the juice in another bowl.
4. Add tomato paste to squeezed whole tomatoe juice. Pour sauce in pan and return meat.
5. Sprinkle 1Tbs of black pepper and 1Tbs. of basil into sauce. Cover and simmer for 5-6 hours.
Meatballs:
1. Remove crust from bread and discard.
2. Pour milk over bread, let absorb then squeeze out excess milk.
3. Mix bread, pork, and beef together.
Add 2 Tbs. of parsley, 1 Tbs. salt,2 eggs,and 3Tbs. parmesan cheese.
4. Mix all and form into meatballs. Fry in oil and 2 cloves of garlic until browned on all sides. Add to sauce 1 hour before eating.
