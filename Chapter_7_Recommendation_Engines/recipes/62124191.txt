Title:
Trishie's Chocoholics Mississippi Mud Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/422477.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz Graham Crackers
4 oz Melted Margarine
12 oz Marshmallows
6 Tbs Milk
6 oz Dark Chocolate
3 Tbs Powdered Chocolate (for making chocolate milk!)
1/2 pint of Double Cream

Directions:
Crush Graham Crackers and add melted margarine and mix till the mixture is fully combined.
Put into 8" greased spring-form cake tin and press down. Leave to cool in refrigerator.
Put marhsmallows in a pot with milk and heat on low until melted and foaming.
Whip the cream till thick.
Melt the chocolate.
Remove the marshmallow from the heat and add the melted chocolate and whipped cream folding altogether using a metal spoon.
Pour the mixture into the prepared cake tin.
Leave in the refrigerator to set for 1 - 2 hours.
Carefully open the spring-form sides on the cake tin and serve with whipped cream!!
