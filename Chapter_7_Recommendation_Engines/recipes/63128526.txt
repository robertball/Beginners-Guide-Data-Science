Title:
Salmon Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/797819.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Large Can Salmon (boneless), Drain
2 Whole Eggs
2 Tablespoons Flour
2 Tablespoons Corn Meal
1 Small Onion (chopped)

Directions:
Mix in large bowl (may add cream if desired).
Form into patties and roll in crushed saltine crackers.
Fry in large skillet.
