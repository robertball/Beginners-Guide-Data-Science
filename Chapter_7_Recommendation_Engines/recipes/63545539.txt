Title:
Sauteed Fennel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large fennel bulb, thinly sliced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 t butter
1 T olive oil
1 t Better than Broth beef concentrate
1/4 c Pernod
1/4 c heavy cream
salt
pepper

Directions:
Saute garlic in butter and oil.
Add fennel and beef concentrate and saute until tender.
Add Pernod and saute briefly.
Remove from heat. Stir in cream, salt, and pepper.
