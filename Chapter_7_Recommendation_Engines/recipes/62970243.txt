Title:
Cake Mixes from Scratch-Vanilla, Chocolate & Strawberry Mini-Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon baking powder
3/4 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups white sugar
1/2 cup butter-flavored shortening
3 eggs
1 1/2 cup milk (or more)
3 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 tbsp cocoa
2 tsp strawberry flavored gelatin powder

Directions:
For a Basic Cake Mix: Sift together flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. Cut in shortening until fine crumbs are formed.
Split Cake Mix three ways (about 1 1/2 cups each)
For Vanilla Cake: add 1 egg, about 1/2 cup milk (more if necessary) and 1 tsp vanilla to dry mix; blend until smooth and pourable.  Pour into paper-lined mini-muffin pans to just over 3/4 full.
For Chocolate Cake:  add 2 Tbsp. unsweetened cocoa powder to dry mix.  Add 1 egg, 1 tsp vanilla and about 1/2 cup milk (more if necessary).  Blend until smooth and pourable.  Pour into paper-lined mini-muffin pans to just over 3/4 full.
For Strawberry Cake:  add 2 tsp strawberry gelatin (about 1 tsp if using sugar-free) to dry mix; add 1 egg, 1 tsp vanilla and about 1/2 cup milk (more if necessary).  Blend until smooth and pourable.  Pour into paper-lined mini-muffin pans to just over 3/4 full.
Bake in preheated 350 degree F oven (175 degrees C) for 12 minutes, or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
