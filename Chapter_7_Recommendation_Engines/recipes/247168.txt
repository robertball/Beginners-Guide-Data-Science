Title:
Tomato Bruschetta from Target Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3566050.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package sweet cocktail tomatoes, cut into quarters
1/4 cup thinly sliced fresh basil leaves
1 (8 ounce) ball or log fresh mozzarella cheese, roughly chopped or torn
2 tablespoons olive oil plus additional for brushing
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 (14 ounce) bakery baguette

Directions:
Combine tomatoes, basil, mozzarella, 2 tablespoons olive oil and 1/4 teaspoon salt in large bowl. Stir gently.
Heat grill. Cut baguette into 3/4-inch thick slices. Brush with olive oil. Grill bread slices 2 to 3 minutes, turning as needed, until grill marks appear. Serve with tomato-basil mixture for topping. Season with salt and freshly cracked black pepper.

Submitter: 
Target Test Kitchen
Description: 
Grill baguette slices until warm and lightly crisp. Top with creamy mozzarella, fresh basil and sweet cocktail tomatoes. Enjoy the flavors of summer.
