Title:
Waldorf Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 celery stalks, finely chopped
¼ cup raisins
1 lb apples, chopped into ¼ inch pieces
¼ cup walnuts, chopped
1 Tablespoon minced parsley
2 Tablespoons mayonnaise
3 Tablespoons sour cream or Greek yogurt
1 Tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon sugar

Directions:
Whisk dressing together in a bowl.
Toss with ingredients and serve chilled.

Description: 
A crunchy and satisfyingly sweet accompaniment to your next family barbecue.
