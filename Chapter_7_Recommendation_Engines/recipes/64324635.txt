Title:
Stir Fry Snow Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Peanut Oil
Toasted Sesame Oil
1lbs Snow Peas
Soy Sauce
Ginger (optional)

Directions:
Heat peanut oil over medium heat in a deep frying pan or wok.
Add snow peas - turn heat higher.  Sautee over high heat for about 5 minutes, stirring constantly, until peas start to show brown spots.
Remove from heat - drizzle with sesame oil and soy sauce and ginger (optional).
