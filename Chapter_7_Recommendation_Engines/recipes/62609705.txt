Title:
Sesame Wasabi Cream Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Package (3 oz) cream cheese
2 Tablespoons Kikkoman Soy Sauce
2 to 3 Tablespoons Wasabi Paste
2 Tablespoons toasted Sesame Seeds
Assorted Crackers (Rice, Water, Etc.)

Directions:
Place cream cheese in shallow dish.  Cut cream cheese horizontally down the middle.  Spread Wasabi paste all over, then place cream cheese back on top. If not serving immediately, cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate.   Just prior to serving, pour Soy Sauce over cream cheese and sprinkle with toasted Sesame Seeds. Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
bryciesmom
Description: 
This is a wonderful, spicy spread for crackers that is a great dish for parties.  I couldn't stop eating it!
