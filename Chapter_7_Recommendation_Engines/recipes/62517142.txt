Title:
Tortellini Soup (Best of Bridge) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
3 carrots, sliced thin
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 Tbsp olive oil
28oz + 19oz cans chopped tomatoes
6 Cups water
3 cubes chicken boullion
1 1/2 tsp sugar
2 Tbsp parsley
1 1/2 Tbsp basil
10 oz tortillini
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
1.  Heat oil, saute onion, celery, carrots and garlic until soft.
2.  Add rest of ingredients and bring to boil.
3.  Reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour
4.  In seperate pot, cook tortillini.
5.  Add tortillini to each soup bowl and pour in soup.
serve
