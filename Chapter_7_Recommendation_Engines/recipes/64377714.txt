Title:
Greek Yogurt Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6oz greek yogurt
egg or egg white
1/2 cup flour
1tsp baking soda

Directions:
wisk yogurt until creamy.  Add egg/egg white.  Mix flour and baking soda in separate bowl, add slowly.
Cook on griddle/pan.  Flip when pancake top start to bubble.
