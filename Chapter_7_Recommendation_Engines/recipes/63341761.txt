Title:
Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
2 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 green pepper, chopped
1 lb sausage
1/2 teaspoon oregano
16 ounces stewed tomatoes
8 ounce can tomato sauce
1 med onion, chopped
1 stalk celery, chopped
dash pepper
1 jalapeno, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon cumin
15 ounce can kidnew beans undrained

Directions:
In large skillet brown ground beef and sausage.
Add onion, celery and jalapeno.
Drain excess fat.
Stir in remaining ingredients except beans.
Simmer covered about 30-45 min or until flavors are blended.
Stir in beans.
Heat through
