Title:
Sweet Restaurant Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) bag coleslaw mix
2 tablespoons diced onion (optional)
2/3 cup mayonnaise or Miracle Whip
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/2 cup white sugar or Splenda White Sugar Blend
1 tablespoon white vinegar or rice wine vinegar
1/4 teaspoon Kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon celery seeds
1/2 teaspoon ground mustard

Directions:
Combine the coleslaw mix and onion in a large bowl.
Whisk together the mayonnaise, vegetable oil, sugar, vinegar, salt, mustard, and celery seeds in a medium bowl; blend thoroughly. Pour dressing mixture over coleslaw mix and toss to coat. Chill at least 2 hours before serving.
