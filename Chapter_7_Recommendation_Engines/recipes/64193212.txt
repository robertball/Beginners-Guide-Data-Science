Title:
Ruth's Cranberry Orange Relish (original) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups of ocean spray cranberries
2 oranges
2 cups sugar

Directions:
Put through a food processor the fresh cranberries, 2 oranges quartered and seeded
Add 2 cups sugar
Mix well and chill in refrigerator
