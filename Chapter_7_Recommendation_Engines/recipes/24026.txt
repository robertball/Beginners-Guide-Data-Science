Title:
Easy Chocolate Kisses Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
48 HERSHEY®'S KISSES® with Almonds Chocolates, divided
1 cup heavy cream
6 single serve graham crusts
3 tablespoons chopped toasted almonds

Directions:
Remove wrappers from chocolate pieces. Place 42 pieces in medium microwave-safe bowl. Microwave at HIGH (100%) 1 minute; stir. Microwave at HIGH 45 seconds; stir until melted. Cool to room temperature.
Beat whipping cream in small bowl until stiff; stir into cooled chocolate, stirring until well blended. Fill crusts.  Refrigerate about 2 to 3 hours. Garnish with chopped almonds and chocolate pieces.

Description: 
Individual tarts made with a quick and easy chocolate-almond mousse.
