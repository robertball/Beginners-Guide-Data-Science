Title:
Asian Plum Sauce - Canning recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 pounds plums, chopped (pits removed, but peel left intact)
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp Five Spice
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a non-reactive saucepan and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer approximately 25 minutes until the plums have softened and the sauce has thickened a little.
Puree in a food processor or blender and return to the pot.
Bring back to a boil.
Ladle into hot, sterilized 8 oz jars leaving 1/4 inch headspace.
Remove air bubbles, adjust headspace, and wipe the rims with a clean towel.
Place sterlized lids and rings on jars and process 10 minutes in a boiling water canner.
Let jars rest in canner 5 minutes after processing time has ended.
Remove jars to a towel to rest 24 hours. Check seals, label, and store.
