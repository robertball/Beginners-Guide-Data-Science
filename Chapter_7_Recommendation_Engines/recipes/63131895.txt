Title:
Creole Shrimp and Chicken Gumbo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 pounds medium shrimp, peeled and de-veined
1 pounds chicken breast, cut up into cubes
1/2 pound andouille sausage
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
3 cups chopped okra
2 onions, chopped
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
2 quarts water
1 bay leaf
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon salt
1 red bell pepper, chopped
4 tbsp Tony Chacheres Original Creole Seasoning
2 tbsp Louisiana hot sauce
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large skillet over high heat, add 2 tablespoons oil and flour to the pan, and whisk together quickly. Continue cooking and stirring until a dark roux  forms. Stir in shrimp. Cook and stir for a few minutes, until the shrimp turns pink. Set aside.
In another pan, heat 2 tablespoons oil over medium heat.  Stir in okra and onions; cook until okra is tender.  Mix in tomatoes.  Combine water, bay leaf, garlic, salt, red bell pepper, shrimp, chicken, creole seasoning, and Louisiana hot sauce in a large soup pot.  Cover, and cook slowly for 30 minutes or more.

Submitter: 
Misty
Description: 
A dark roux begins this recipe for thick shrimp gumbo featuring okra, onions, bell pepper and diced tomatoes.  Serve this stew over long-grain white rice.Modified from: Savory Shrimp Gumbo
