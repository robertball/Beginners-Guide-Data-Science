Title:
Cheesy Chicken Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. pizza dough, refrigerated
1 lb. Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breasts
2 cups Mozzarella Cheese
2 cups Cheddar Cheese
1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
1/4 cup Onions, chopped
1 cup Tomatoes, diced
2 cups Mushrooms
1 Garlic Clove
1/2 teaspoon Basil
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1 Tablespoon Olive Oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Roll dough onto pizza pan & bake for 8 minutes.  Fry chicken breasts in large skillet.  Cool & cut into thin strips.  Heat olive oil in skillet over medium heat & add onions & garlic.  Cook until onions are transparent & garlic is lightly browned.  Add in the tomatoes, salt & basil.  Cook until tomatoes are lightly browned & softened.  Spread tomato mix onto pizza crust & top with chicken strips.  Sprinkle the cheddar cheese & mozzarella cheese on top of the chicken.  Place mushrooms on top of cheese & sprinkle with parmesan cheese.  Bake for 15 minutes.
