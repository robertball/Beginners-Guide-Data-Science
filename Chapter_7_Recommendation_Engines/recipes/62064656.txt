Title:
healthy fruit smoothy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c frozen strawberries
1/2 c frozen  blue berries
1/2 c frozen peaches
2/3 cup NESTLE® CARNATION® Nonfat Dry Milk OR 2/3 c soy mile
1/4 c flax seed ground
pineapple juice to texture
1/2 cup ice cubes
2 tablespoons granulated sugar (optional)
2 pineapple chunks

Directions:
in blender mix frozen fruit, dry and 1/2 juice and blend
add milk powder and flax seed
add remaining juice
mix until desired texture
garnish with pineapple chunks

Submitter: 
Pam Rosterman
Description: 
We love this for breakfast or for a soak in the hot tub
