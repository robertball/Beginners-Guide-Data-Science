Title:
Zippy Mexican English Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1018813.jpg
Ingredients:
6 hard boiled eggs, diced
1/4 cup low fat mayonaisse
1/4 cup salsa
1 T canola oil
1/4 cup diced onion
1/4 cup diced red pepper
1T chili powder
1 1/2t. cumin
pinch cayenne pepper
salt & pepper, to taste
4 split and toasted english muffins
4 slices of Canadian Bacon
1/2 cup of Mexican cheese blend (Cheddar,  Montery Jack, Asadero and Queso Blanco)
chopped cilantro (optional)
Extra salsa

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the eggs, mayonaisse and salsa.
In small pan, saute diced onion and red pepper in canola oil until tender.. 3-5 minutes. Add to egg mixture. Add chili powder, cumin, cayenne pepper and salt & pepper.
Add Canadian Bacon slices to pan and saute until brown around edges..3-4 minutes.
Place English muffins cut side up on an ungreased baking sheet. Top each with a slice of Canadian bacon, 1/8 of egg mixture and mexican cheese blend. Bake at 350 degrees F for 8-9 minutes minutes or until cheese is melted. Top with chopped cilantro , if desired and provide extra salsa on the side
