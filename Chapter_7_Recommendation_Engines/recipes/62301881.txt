Title:
Brown Sugar Poptarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/365301.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups all-purpose flour
1 3/4 cups butter
1 tablespoon sugar
2 teaspoons salt
1 egg
1/2 cup very cold water
3 tablespoons butter
1/3 cup sugar,
1/3 cup brown sugar
1 tablespoon cinnamon
Glaze:
1/2 cup sifted powdered sugar
1/4 teaspoon vanilla
1-2 teaspoons milk
1/4 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400*
Add flour, butter, sugar, and salt to a large bowl. Mix flour and shortening with a pastry cutter until it looks nice and crumbly.
In a small bowl, whisk together the egg and water. Add to the flour mixture, stirring in till well-moistened.
Stick your hands in there and shape it into a ball. (Donâ€™t overhandle.)
Divide dough into equal halves.
Wrap each half with plastic and refrigerate for at least 15 minutes before using.
After dough is chilled, roll out in two long strips six inches wide.
Cut into three-inch sections.
Roll into 8 sections, Or however big you want your pop tarts.
Melt the 3 tablespoons butter and combine 1/3 cup sugar, 1/3 cup brown sugar, and 1 tablespoon cinnamon in a small bowl. Brush melted butter on each piece then sprinkle with the brown sugar mixture.
Top with the other strip of pastry and pinch the edges closed.
Prick the centers with a fork to let steam out.
Transfer to cookie sheet and bake for 15 minutes, or until light golden brown.
Let cool slightly and drizzle glaze over top.
