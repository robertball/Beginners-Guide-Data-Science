Title:
Granny's Chocolate Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Sticks Margarine (Short Sticks)
½ Cup Crisco
3 Cups Sugar
5 Large Eggs
3 Cups All Purpose Flour
1 Tsp. Baking Powder
3 Tbs. Cocoa (Heaping)
1 ¼ Cup Milk

Directions:
In large bowl beat the margarine, Crisco and sugar.
Sift the cocoa and flour together; pour in sugar mixture alternating with the flour mixture (Start with Flour and finish with flour)
Add Baking power and eggs.
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour and 15 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
Southern cooking isnt complete without dessert. My Granny used to make the best poundcake known to man and here is how she did it.
