Title:
Sweet Iced Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/613711.jpg
Ingredients:
6 black tea bags
1/2 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1 gallon boiling water
1 (6 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate

Directions:
Place the tea bags and SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener into a 1 gallon glass jar. Fill with boiling water. Let steep for 2 hours at room temperature. Remove and discard tea bags. Stir in lemonade concentrate. Refrigerate until cool.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
Cool and refreshing summer drink made with SPLENDA® Granulated Sweetener instead of sugar. Serve in a tall glass over ice.
