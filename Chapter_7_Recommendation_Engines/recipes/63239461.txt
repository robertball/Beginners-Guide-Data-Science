Title:
Carol's Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
28 oz. can diced tomatoes
10 oz. can Orginal Rotel tomatoes
1/2 onion
3 cloves garlic, peeled
1 jalapeno (remove seeds to make milder)
1 tsp. honey
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1/4 tsp. cumin
handful of fresh cilantro, washed.
juice of lime

Directions:
In a food processor process onion and garlic for a few seconds.
Add all the other ingredients and process until the salsa is desired consistency.  Adjust seasonings.  Serve with whole grain chips.

Submitter: 
Grace Carolyn Bowers-Reimondo
Description: 
This is quick and easy.  I like it hot and it is!
