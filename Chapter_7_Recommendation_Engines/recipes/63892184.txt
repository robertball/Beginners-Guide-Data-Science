Title:
Roast Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds red potatoes, cut into quarters
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary, crushed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (250 degrees C).
cut potatoes and pat until very dry (so they crisp up)  Place potatoes in a large bag and toss with oil, salt, pepper, and rosemary until evenly coated. Spread out potatoes in a single layer(but not the excess oil)face down. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Diane Manzella
