Title:
Devo's Oatmeal Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup packed brown sugar
3/4 cup white sugar
1 cup peanut butter
2 eggs
1 3/4 cup flour
2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup quick oats
1 cup milk chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cream butter, sugars, and peanut butter until smooth.
Beat in eggs one at a time.
Combine flour, baking soda and salt. Stir into creamed mixture.
Mix in oats and chocolate chips until just combined.
Drop onto ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake for 8-10 minutes (or until base of cookie is light brown).
