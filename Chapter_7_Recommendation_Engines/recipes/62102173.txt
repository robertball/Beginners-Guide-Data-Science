Title:
Poppy Seed Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30 ml  Poppy seeds
265 ml  Milk
63 ml  Hard margarine(or butter)
95 ml  Granulated sugar
1     Large egg
2.5 ml  Lemon juice
250 ml  All-purpose flour
5 ml  Baking powder
1 ml  Salt
63 ml  Maraschino cherries, well drained and halved

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 F
Add poppy seeds to milk in a small bowl and let sit for 30 minutes.
Combine Margarine, sugar and egg in medium bowl. Beat until smooth. Stir in poppy seed mixture.
Measure flour, Baking powder, salt, and cherries (if you choose to have them) into small bowl. Mix well. Stir into batter just until moist. Pour into greased pan.
Bake for 1 hour. Cool in pan for 10 minutes and then take out of pan and let cool completely.
