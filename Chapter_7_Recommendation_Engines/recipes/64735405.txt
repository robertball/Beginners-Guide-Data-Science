Title:
Magic Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter (1 stick)
1 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
1 14 oz can sweetened condensed milk
1 cup chocolate chips
1 cup butterscotch or peanut butter chips
1 1/3 cup coconut
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Bake at 350 degrees
Line 13 x 9 inch pan with foil
Place butter in pan and melt in the oven
Sprinkle crumbs over butter
Pour condensed milk over crumbs evenly
Top with ingredients in order
Press down firmly and cook until lightly browned
