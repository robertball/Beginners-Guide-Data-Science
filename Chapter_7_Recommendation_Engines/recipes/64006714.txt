Title:
Ham with Honey and Brown Sugar Glaze - MINE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (5 pound) fully cooked sliced ham
1/4 cup whole cloves
1 cup pineapple juice
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup honey
2 oranges, juiced

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place the ham in a roasting pan and dot with cloves. In a saucepan combine the pineapple juice, brown sugar, honey and orange juice. Stir and simmer over medium-low heat until thickened, about 10 minutes. Pour the glaze over the ham.
Bake the ham uncovered for 1 hour in the preheated oven.

Submitter: 
Andrea
Description: 
A pre-sliced cooked ham is studded with cloves and coated with a tangy glaze before baking. Great for dinner, and the leftovers make wonderful sandwiches.Modified from: Ham with Honey and Brown Sugar Glaze
