Title:
Crock-Pot Chicken Sausage Gumbo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb smoked sausage sliced into thin slices
2 large boneless skinless chicken breasts, cooked and chopped
1 medium yellow onion, chopped
1 medium green bell pepper, chopped
2 cloves of garlic, minced
2-14.5 ounce cans petite diced tomatoes, drained slightly
1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
¼ teaspoon salt
¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
¼ teaspoon hot sauce, you can add more if you want it hotter
1-10 ounce package frozen okra
cooked white or brown rice for serving

Directions:
Slightly brown the sausage and add to slow cooker.
Add all the other items to slow cooker except okra and tabasco.
Stir to combine.
Cover and cook on LOW for 8 hours stirring often.
Stir in okra and hot sauce.
Cook 15-20 minutes longer.
Serve over cooked rice.
