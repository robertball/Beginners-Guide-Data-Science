Title:
Mango Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large Mango
1 jalapeno (remove ribs and seeds if you desire)
½ medium red onion
½ red bell pepper
2 Tbspn chopped parsley or cilantro
1 large lime, juiced
salt
pepper
Tabasco

Directions:
Chop mango, jalapeno, red onion, bell pepper, and herbs
Mix in medium size bowl
Add lime juice, salt, pepper and Tabasco to taste

Submitter: 
EAMJM
Description: 
Serve over grilled meat or seafood OR with chips for dipping.  This will pair well with a nice crisp white wine.
