Title:
Pork loin chops with gravy by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5175523.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless pork loin chops
2  Tbl olive oil
1  white onion, sliced
1  cup chicken broth
2  Tbl corn starch
Lowrys seasoning
Pepper

Directions:
Select SAUTE on the Instant Pot and then ADJUST until it is on the hottest setting.  Allow it to heat up until it says HOT on the display. Seasoning both sides of the chops. When the pot is HOT add  2 Tbl of olive oil into the pan and allow it to heat.  Brown the chops on both sides until lightly brown.  Remove chops from the pan and pour in enough of the chicken broth to deglaze the pan.
Add onions and stir until they are tender.  Pour remaining broth into the pot to deglaze  and add the chops back, cover.
Set on  MANUAL pressure for 4 minutes.  When pressure cycle is complete, allow pressure to natural release (about 8 minutes).  Remove chops from pan.
Take 2 Tbl of the broth from the pot and stir it in the corn starch.  Add mixture to the pot.
Use SAUTE setting on low until gravy thickens, stirring constantly.
Serve gravy and onions on the chops.
