Title:
kids favorite pizza spagetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb. spaggetti, broken in half
4-213ml/7.5oz cans pizza sauce
1 Tbsp. olive oil
1lb. fresh mushrooms, sliced
1-398ml/14oz can pineapple tidbits, drained
1-175g. package of pepperoni, diced
1-380g bag mozza/cheddar grated cheese
1/4 cup fresh parmesan
salt to taste
cooking spray

Directions:
Boil spaggetti acording to package.
While spagetti is cooking, fry mushrooms in oil.
Drain spagetti. Return to pot, add pizza sauce, mushrooms, pineapple, pepperoni, 1/2 the bag of mozza/cheddar, and the salt.
Preheat oven to 350.
Spray a 4.8qt./4.5L lasagna casserole dish with cooking spray.
Pour spagetti mixture into dish, topping with remaining cheeses.
Bake for 25 min., then broil for 5 min, or until cheese is golden.

Submitter: 
Eirene
Description: 
Two of kids favorites, one dish. Any pizza topping can be added or taken out to your own kids likings.
