Title:
Vegetarian Split Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2T olive oil,
1C chopped onion,
1C chopped carrot,
4 cloves minced garlic,
1/4t red pepper flakes,
1t salt,
1 bay leaf,
16oz green split peas; rinsed,
64oz vegetable stock

Directions:
Heat oil in large pot over medium heat.
Sauté onion and carrot until tender (about 5 minutes).
Stir in garlic, pepper flakes, 1t salt, and bay leaf. Sauté 2 minutes.
Stir in peas and stock.
Bring to a boil. reduce heat and simmer uncovered, stirring occasionally, until peas are tender (about 45 minutes).
Cool for a few minutes. Discard bay leaf.
Blend in blender or with immersion blender until smooth. Season to taste.

Description: 
This is an easy soup to make and is very creamy tasting without having to add cream.
