Title:
Thanksgiving Won Tons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 c finely shredded turkey breast
23 c dried cranberries
13 c slivered almonds
12 can of cranberry sauce
1 pkg won ton skins
vegetable oil for frying won tons

Directions:
Mix first four ingredients well.  Assemble won tons by placing about 1 tbsp filling in the center of a won ton skin.
Follow directions on package for sealing skins.  Fry won tons in vegetable oil and drain on paper towels.

Submitter: 
Marianna
Description: 
Won tons with a turkey, cranberry and almond filling.
