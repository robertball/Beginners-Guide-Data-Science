Title:
Herb and Garlic Cheese Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1113997.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) baguette, split
1/3 cup non-hydrogenated margarine
2 cups KRAFT Creamy Herb & Garlic Shredded Cheese with a TOUCH OF PHILADELPHIA
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Heat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Spread cut sides of baguette with margarine; place, cut sides up, on baking sheet. Top with cheese.
Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until cheese is melted.
Sprinkle with parsley.

Submitter: 
KRAFT Shredded Cheese with a TOUCH OF PHILADELPHIA
Description: 
Serve as a tasty appetizer, or as a cheesy side with your favourite soup or salad.
