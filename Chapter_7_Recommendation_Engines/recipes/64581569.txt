Title:
Baked Beef Chiles Rellenos Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3654389.jpg
Ingredients:
6 large poblano peppers, halved & seeded
1-1/2 to 2 lbs lean ground beef, turkey or chicken
1 large onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 14-1/2 oz cans of diced tomatoes with chiles
1/2 to 1 tsp salt (to taste)
1/4 to 1/2 tsp black pepper (to taste)
1 Tbs chile powder
1 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (or to taste)
1/4 tsp chipotle pepper (or to taste)
1/4 tsp paprika (or to taste)
3 to 4  c shredded low-fat "Mexican Cheese Blend"
For Serving:
Low fat sour cream
Pico de Gallo or Salsa of choice

Directions:
Preheat broiler.
Place poblanos cut side down on cookie sheets and put under broiler til skins are blackened and bubbling.
Place pepper in ziplock bag, covered container or paper bag and put aside.
Place ground meat into large cooking pan, add all seasonings including garlic, and cook til no longer pink.
Add onion and cook til onion is slightly softened.
Drain extra fat from pan.
Add tomatoes with chiles to meat mixture and cook til meat is cooked through and onions are completely softened/translucent.
Adjust seasoning to taste and allow meat mixture to cool.
Under running water, remove skin from poblanos - it should come off easily.
Dry poblanos on paper towels.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Spray  casserole dish with non-stick spray.
Place a layer of poblanos (they are easily arranged when sliced in half) on bottom of dish.
Place 1/3 of meat mixture on top of poblanos.
Top meat mixture with 1 c (or more if desired) shredded cheese.
Repeat layers twice more in the same order.
Bake, uncovered, 35 minutes or until golden brown on top.
Serve with dollops of low-fat sour cream and pico de gallo or salsa.
