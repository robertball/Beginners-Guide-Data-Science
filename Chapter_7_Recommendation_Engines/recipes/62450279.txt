Title:
Bloody Mary Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 quarts tomatoes-quartered
1 celery stick, chopped
1/4 c Worcestershire sauce
1/4 c pickling salt
1/4 c lemon juice
1/4 c sugar
1/8 c celery salt
1/8 c A1 sauce
12 drops Tabasco (less if you don't want it as hot)

Directions:
Cook down tomatoes and celery and run through sieve then add the remaining ingrediants.
Put in jars and hot water bath for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Barb Ross
Description: 
Enjoy with or without Vodka!  I've even added it to my chili!
