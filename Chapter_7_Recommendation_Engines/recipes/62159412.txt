Title:
The Beer Budget Bellini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bottle champagne chilled
Peach Schnappes
2 cans lemon-lime soda chilled (can use diet soda)
4 Champagne flutes or white wine glasses
Fresh peach slices for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Add a 1/2 jigger of peach schnappes to each glass.
Fill each glass  1/2 way with champagne.
Top of with lemon-lime soda.
Drop a slice of fresh peach into the champagne flute or place as a garnish one the rim of the wine glass.
