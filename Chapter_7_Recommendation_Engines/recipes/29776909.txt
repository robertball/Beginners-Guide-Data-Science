Title:
Heavenly Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz. package of noodles
1lb. sausage
1 finely cut onion
1 cn. corn
2 cn. vegetable soup (Campbell's)
5 crackers

Directions:
Cook noodles according to package directions.  Fry sausage until browned, stirring constantly.  Combine all ingredients except crackers crumbs:  Place in buttered dish.  Top with crackers crumbs.  Bake 325 degrees about 30 mins.
