Title:
Filthy Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991061.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 fluid ounce Stirrings Dirty Martini
1 1/2 fluid ounces Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka
1 teaspoon dried coriander spice
1 piece gherkin stuffed olive

Directions:
Rim martini glass with dried coriander spice.
In a cocktail shaker combine Stirrings Dirty Martini and Smirnoff No.21 Vodka with plenty of ice.
Cover and shake vigorously.
Strain into the rimmed martini glass.
Garnish with gherkin stuffed olive.

Submitter: 
Smirnoff
Description: 
Garnish your dirty martini with some warm, spicy ground coriander and take your cocktail to the next level.
