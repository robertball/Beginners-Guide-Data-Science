Title:
the perfect Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 avocado(cubed)
1 tsp olive oil
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
take the half avocado. leave it in the skin.
with a pearing knife, make slits in the avocado.
use a spoon to scoop it out.
in a small bowl, mix together the salt, pepper, and olive oil.
add the avocado and mash.
put the mixture back into the skin.
serve cold. enjoy.

Submitter: 
Elle Mcgrath
Description: 
this is a great, simple guacamole. my cat, Zorp even likes it. i actualy use the avocado skin to serve it in.
