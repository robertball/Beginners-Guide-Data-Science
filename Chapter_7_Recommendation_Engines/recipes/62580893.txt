Title:
Rochelle's Broccoli & Grape Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bunches of broccoli
1 bag red grapes
12 slices of cooked bacon, crumbled
1/2 c sunflower seeds
1/2 c diced red onion
Dressing
2 T red wine vinegar
1 c mayo
1/3 c sugar

Directions:
mix salad ingredients together & combine dressing ingredients. Toss together & serve
