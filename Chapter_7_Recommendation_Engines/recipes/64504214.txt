Title:
Amazing Turkey Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lbs Ground Turkey
2 tbls butter
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup red and green chopped bell peppers
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1 egg
2 teaspoons of soy sauce
1 cup of ketchup (divided into 1/4 and 3/4)
1/2 teaspoon of salt
1/2 pepper
1 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Melt butter in skillet. Season the onions and bell peppers with the garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Sautee the vegetables and seasonings in the butter until they are almost done. Roughly, 5 minutes.
Mix the ground turkey, onion/bell pepper mixture, bread crumbs, eggs, soy sauce, and the 1/4 cup of ketchup in a large bowl.
Place mixture in loaf pan and bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees. After 30 minutes, top with the remaining ketchup and continue baking for another 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Christy Hall
Description: 
Delicious, healthy meat loaf with yummy veggies and minimal seasoning.
