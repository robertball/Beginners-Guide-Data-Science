Title:
Hearty Vegetable Beef Barley Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large (46 oz.) can Tomato Juice
4 cups beef broth
1 soup bone (optional)
2 lb. beef (stewing, sirloin, or chuck)
1 cup sliced carrots
1 cup diced potatoes
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup celery
3/4 cup barley
Couple dashes of Worchestersire sauce.
Salt and pepper to taste.
**Can substitute Tomato Juice with V8 for a more spicy taste.

Directions:
Cut beef into small cubes and brown.  In a large soup kettle, combine juice, beef broth, soup bone, Worchestershire sauce and beef.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to low and simmer for 1 hour.  Add other ingredients and simmer for 2 more hours.  Remove soup bone before serving.  Makes 12 servings.

Submitter: 
HollyG
Description: 
Easy to make hearty beef barley vegetable soup that is great during those cold winter months!  A good starter recipe where you can add in pretty much whatever you desire for more flavor or thickness!
