Title:
Grilled Chicken with Cucumber-Peach Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped cucumber
1/3 cup peach preserves
1 Tablespoon chopped fresh or 1 teaspoon dried mint leaves
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 Tablespoons chopped red onion
1 peach/nectarine, peeled, chopped (3/4 cup)
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
Heat grill. In a small bowl, mix cucumber, 2 Tbs preserves, mint, salt, onion and peach; set aside.
Carefully brush oil on grill rack. Place chicken on grill over medium heat. Cover grill; cook 10-15 minutes, turning and brushing 2-3 times with remaining preserves, until juice of chicken is clear when thickest part is cut. Discard remaining preserves. Serve chicken with salsa.
