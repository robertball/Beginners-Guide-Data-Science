Title:
Creamy Danish Blue Cheese Dressing Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1/2 cup crumbled Danish blue cheese
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon pressed garlic
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Gently combine all ingredients
