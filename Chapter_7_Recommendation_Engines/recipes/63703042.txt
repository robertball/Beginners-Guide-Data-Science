Title:
Two Loaves of Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter softened not melted
1 1/2 cups sugar
6 bananas, mashed
4 eggs, beaten
2 1/2 cups flour
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 cup nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°F.  Grease and lightly flour bottom of two loaf pans.
In a bowl, mix flour, salt, and soda together.
In another bowl, cream together butter and sugar.  Stir in mashed bananas and beaten eggs.  Blend in the above flour mixture.  Don’t over mix.  Stir in nuts.
Pour into prepared loaf pans.
Bake:  55 to 60 minutes.  Cool in pans for about 10 minutes.  Take knife and loosen sides of loaf, slide bread out of pan and place right side up on rack.  Cool bread on rack for a couple of hours.
