Title:
Keto Cheese Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz low moisture mozzarella cheese, shredded
4 T coconut flour
1 tsp garlic powder or other seasoning (cinnamon for sweet)
salt

Directions:
preheat oven to 350
melt mozzarella in 30 second increments in microwave safe bowl
add coconut flour
stir to combine
kneed as necessary
roll out to 1/16 inch
cut into shapes
place on parchment lined cookie sheet, separating so don't stick
bake 10 min at 350 or until golden/puffed; bake at 325 for longer for more crispy
