Title:
Lemony green Bean With Feta and Pecans. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds fresh green beans
1/4 cup olive
1 tablespoon dijon mustard
3/4 teaspoon salt
ground black pepper to taste
1 dash Worcestershire sauce
4 ounces feta cheese, crumbled
1 cup of sauteed whole pecans
1/4 fresh sqeezed Lemon Juice

Directions:
Prepare just before serving.  Leave beans whole and just snap off that ugly end (not the pointy one) Add the cleaned green beans to a large pot of boiling water and boil until crisp tender (about 4 minutes).  When the beans are done, drain the water into the colander.  Immediately rinse all in cold water to stop the cooking process.
In a seperate bowl whisk together oil, lemon wrcestershire, mustard and ground salt and pepper to taste.  Dressing will get frothy.
For the dressing:  In a large bowl, whisk together the garlic, rice wine vinegar, cider vinegar, oil, sugar, salt, pepper, hot sauce and Worcestershire sauce.
Just before serving Add beans, cheese and pecans - toss and serve.
Serve at room temperature.
Wash your hands with some nice handmade soap and Enjoy!
