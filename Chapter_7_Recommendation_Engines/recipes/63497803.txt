Title:
Broccoli Cheese Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/954710.jpg
Ingredients:
4 eggs
2 cups diced raw broccoli
1 cup yogurt
1/2 large onion, diced
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 scant cup yellow cornmeal
1 scant cup flour
1 Tablespoon baking powder
1 scant teaspoon salt
1/2 tsp chili powder
4-8 oz crumbled sausage or bacon (whatever is leftover)
2 small handfuls of shredded cheese.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease an 11x7 inch baking pan.
In a large mixing bowl, combine dry ingredients. In a separate bowl, mix together eggs and yogurt. Steam or microwave steam broccoli 2 min. saute onion in butter, add broccoli. Onion should be soft,  broccoli should be al dente. Stir the sausage into the egg mixture until warm, add one handful of cheese then mix into the flour mixture.  Pour batter into prepared pan. Top with the other handful of cheese or to visual satisfaction.
Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the pan comes out clean.

Description: 
Great use of leftover meats  and veggies with things you always have on hand in the pantry.  I would change up the spices depending on the other foods served with it.  Doesn't take long to cook in the 11x7 pan.Modified from: Broccoli Cheese Cornbread
