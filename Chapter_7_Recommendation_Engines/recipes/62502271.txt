Title:
Blueberry Oat Flax Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c. Oatmeal Plus Oat Bran (instant)
1/2 c. wheat flour
3/4 c. all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 egg
1 c. milk
4 T flax seed
1/3 c. vegetable oil
1 tsp vanilla
2/3 c. sugar
1 1/4 c. frozen blueberries

Directions:
1. Mix dry ingredients (through salt)and set aside
2. Mix wet ingredients (through sugar).
3. Slowly add dry ingredients to wet.
4. Stir in blueberries.
Set-up 12-muffin baking pan with paper liners and spray with vegetable oil. Fill each cup full (these will not rise very much).
Bake at 375 for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
SALLY G
Description: 
These super-heart healthy muffins are great for breakfast or mid-afternoon snacking! Flax seed and blueberries provide vitamins and phyto-nutrients, while oats and oatbran provide fiber to keep you going.
