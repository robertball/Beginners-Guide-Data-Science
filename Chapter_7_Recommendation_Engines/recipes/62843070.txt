Title:
Popcorn-and-Oat Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/676607.jpg
Ingredients:
Nonstick cooking spray
1 cup air-popped popcorn
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 cup old-fashioned oats
1/2 tablespoon soy milk
1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
Pinch of salt
1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Coat a baking sheet with cooking spray.
Combine all ingredients in a mixer or food processor. Mix until the popcorn and oats break up, about 2 to 3 minutes.
Dump dough onto baking sheet. Separate it into mounds. Wet palms and press to flatten.
Bake until pale golden brown, about 12 to 15 minutes. Cool on baking sheet.

Submitter: 
Linda T
Description: 
These unusual but delicious cookies are from The Best Life Guide to Managing Diabetes by Bob Greene. The recipe also is gluten-free.
