Title:
Mamaw Bea's Brown Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c chicken broth
4 T butter
4 T flour
black pepper

Directions:
1. Melt butter over medium heat.
2. Whisk in flour; continue whisking 2-3 min until slightly brown.
3. Whisking constantly, stir in broth and whisk until smooth.  Bring gravy to a boil, reduce to medium and add pepper, to taste.  Simmer until thickened.
