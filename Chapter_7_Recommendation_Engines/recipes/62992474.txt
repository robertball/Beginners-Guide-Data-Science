Title:
Snickerdoodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744559.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup shortening
1 1/2 cups white sugar
2 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons cream of tartar
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons white sugar
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
No cream of tartar??  Sub 3 tsp of baking powder for baking soda and cream of tartar

Directions:
Preheat CONVECTION oven to 325 degrees F. (IF BAKING LOTS OF DIFFERENT COOKIES, OTHERWISE BAKE AT 350 OR 375 AND REDUCE TIME)
Cream together butter, shortening, 1 1/2 cups sugar, the eggs and the vanilla. Blend in the flour, cream of tartar, soda and salt. Shape dough by rounded spoonfuls into balls (SIZE OF PING PONG BALL)
Mix the 2 tablespoons sugar and the cinnamon. Roll balls of dough in mixture. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets.
Bake 9 to 11 minutes, or until set but not too hard. Remove immediately from baking sheets.
DOUBLE BATCH MAKES EXACTLY 7 DOZEN

Submitter: 
Catherine Parnell-Proulx
Description: 
These are soft, sweet, and delicious! careful to not make them too big or they will not fully cook in the middle.Modified from: Mrs. Sigg's Snickerdoodles
