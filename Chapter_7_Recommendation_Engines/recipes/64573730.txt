Title:
Coleslaw Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C. Miracle Whip
1/4 -1/3 Cup Sicilian Lemon white balsamic vinegar (to taste)
1/2 tsp. celery seeds
Dash salt and pepper

Directions:
Mix all the above ingredients and pour over the coleslaw.  Enjoy

Submitter: 
Phyllis Trinke Funk
Description: 
Simple, easy and delicious. Not everyone will be able to find Miracle Whip, so feel free to use Mayonnaise and just increase or decrease the balsamic vinegar.  But please, do use a good quality vinegar.  Enjoy.
