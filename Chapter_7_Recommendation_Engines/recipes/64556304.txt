Title:
Pistachio Pudding Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
40 Ritz crackers, crushed
1/2 c butter, melted
2 qt vanilla ice cream, softened
1 1/2 c milk
2 pkg instant pistachio pudding
1 8oz container cool whip
1/2 c Heath bar chips

Directions:
Mix crackers and butter snd pat in a 9" X 13" pan.
Meanwhile, blend ice cream, milk and pudding, and pour over crust. Freeze til hard.
Top with cool whip and sprinkle on Heath bar chips. Freeze.
Let thaw @ 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
