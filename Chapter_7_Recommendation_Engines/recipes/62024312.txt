Title:
Best Grilled Chicken Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups water
1/4 cup Soy Sauce
2 Tbs Honey Mustard
1/4 cup Brown Sugar
2 dashes of Liquid Smoke
1 pkg Seasoning Italian (salad dressing mix)
1 Tablespoon Orange Peel
1 Teaspoon Ground Ginger
2 Teaspoon Paprika
2 Teaspoon Fines Herbs (Spice Islands)
Fresh Rosemary sprigs
1/3 cup Rice Wine
1/3 Sesame Oil
1 package of whole cut up Chicken

Directions:
Mix the above ingredients (excluding the chicken  and rosemary)in a sauce pan. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer coverd for 5 mintes. Allow marinade to cool. Remove skin from whole cut up chicken and place in a plastic marinade pan or bowl. Pour marinade over chicken and refrigerate over night.Disguard marinade Cook on bbq grill adding rosemary sprigs to the coals to add a unique smoked flavor to chicken. Cook chicken till juices run clear. Serve with Pasta salad.

Mouth watering Sweet and Tangy Herbed Chicken with a hint of Smoke flavor.
