Title:
Cheesy Ham and Hash Brown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (32 ounce) package frozen hash brown potatoes
8 ounces cooked, diced ham
1 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of potatoe soup
2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In a large bowl, mix hash browns, ham, cream of potato soup,and Cheddar cheese. Spread evenly into prepared dish. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
Bake 1 hour in the preheated oven, or until bubbly and lightly brown. Serve immediately.

Description: 
This family-friendly breakfast casserole is made with hash brown potatoes and ham in a creamy sauce for a yummy lazy day breakfast.Modified from: Cheesy Ham and Hash Brown Casserole
