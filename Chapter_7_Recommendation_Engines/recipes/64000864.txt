Title:
Black & White Brownie Cheesecake Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1110330.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box dark chocolate brownie mix
1/4 cup warm water with 1 tsp espresso
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 eggs
1-1/2 tsp vanilla
1 cup chocolate chips
8 oz cream cheese
1 egg
1/3 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
mix brownie ingredients together
line muffin tin with liners and spray lightly with cooking spray
spoon batter into cups 2/3 full
mix cream cheese ingredients together with mixer
place 1 T cream cheese batter on top of batter
bake at 350 for 30 minutes
