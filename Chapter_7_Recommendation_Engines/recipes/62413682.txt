Title:
Red Raspberry Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
1/2 cup seasoned rice vinegar
1 (10 ounce) jar seedless raspberry jam
or 6 oz of fresh raspberrys

Directions:
Combine the olive oil, rice vinegar and raspberry jam in the container of a blender. Process until smooth. Store in a jar in the refrigerator.
If you are using fresh raspberrys, there is no need to add sugar as the flavor is good witout the added sweetness.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
A very easy, flavor-filled dressing that goes with any kind of salad. Only three ingredients! I like to use it with romaine, feta, toasted pecans, bacon, and red onion.Modified from: Red Raspberry Vinaigrette
