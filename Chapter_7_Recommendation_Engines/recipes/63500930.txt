Title:
Glenn's Jalapeño Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz. container of sour cream
2 fresh jalapeño peppers chopped and minus seeds and core
2 tbsp. chopped cilantro
2 green onions, chopped
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. black pepper
2 tbsp. Cojito cheese
2 tbsp.  Mayonnaise
2 tbsp. Cream cheese
1 med avocado

Directions:
Mix all ingredients into a blender and purée.  Use as a hot sauce for tacos, enchiladas, chili rellanos, or tamales

Submitter: 
Surfdad
Description: 
This is a very flavorful hot sauce for you Mexican meal!
