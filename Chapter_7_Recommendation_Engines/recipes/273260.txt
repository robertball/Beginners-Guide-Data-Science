Title:
Keto Chocolate-Coconut Fat Bombs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup shredded unsweetened coconut
2 scoops pure stevia powder
3 tablespoons butter, softened
3 tablespoons coconut oil, softened
2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder, or more to taste
1 tablespoon all-purpose blend stevia sweetener (such as Pyure®)
5 drops liquid sweetener (such as SucraDrops®)
20 roasted almonds

Directions:
Line a small baking sheet with parchment paper.
Combine coconut, stevia powder, butter, coconut oil, cocoa powder, stevia blend, and liquid sweetener in a bowl; mix well. Spread out on the prepared baking sheet. Scatter almonds on top and press in. Refrigerate until set, about 30 minutes.
Remove from fridge and cut into pieces.

Submitter:

Description: 
A delicious snack and the whole batch has only 5 carbs. Keep refrigerated.
