Title:
Spicy Chicken Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups chopped cooked chicken breast
1/3 cup peanut sauce(House of Tsang Bangkok Padang)
2 tbsp fresh cilantro
1 tbsp chopped peanuts
1/8 tsp crushed red pepper
7 lg iceberg lettuce leaves

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients in a bowl: stir well. Spoon chicken mixture into lettuce leaves. Eat.
