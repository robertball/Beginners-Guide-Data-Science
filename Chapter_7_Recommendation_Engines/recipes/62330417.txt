Title:
Super Sensational Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/384360.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. 80% or higher ground beef
1 lb. Italian sausage (mild), casings removed
1 jar (32 oz.) Ragu spaghetti sauce (Mama's Garden  Combination)
1 large green pepper cut into bite size pieces
1 medium onion coarsely chopped
1 packet French's dry Spaghetti-O mix, any variety
1 (14 oz. can) diced tomatoes,  Italian style
1/4 cup evaporated milk
Salt, Pepper, Garlic Powder to taste
Pasta of choice cooked per package directions.

Directions:
In a large, deep skillet, combine both meats, breaking up ground  beef, and cook over medium-high heat until beef is no longer pink. Add onion, green pepper, spaghetti seasoning packet, salt, pepper and garlic powder.  Cook until onion is just translucent.  Add tomatoes, Ragu sauce and milk.  Stir to combine.  Reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, for about 20 minutes.  Serve over favorite pasta.

Submitter: 
Gail Cobile
Description: 
This recipe occurred naturally when I tried to improvise using some readymade ingredients and making it my own. My family thinks I did a bang-up job. The resulting homemade and old world flavor has them asking for more and asking often!
