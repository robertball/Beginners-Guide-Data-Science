Title:
Kiwi-Melon Coolers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chopped honeydew melon, frozen
1 1/4 cups apple juice, chilled
1 kiwi - peeled, chopped, and frozen

Directions:
Combine honeydew melon, apple juice, and kiwi in a blender; blend until smooth.
