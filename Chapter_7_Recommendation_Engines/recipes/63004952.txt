Title:
Slim Sweet Green Bean Bundles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 (14.5 ounce) cans whole green beans, drained
1 pound turkey bacon, cut in half
1/4 cup butter, melted
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Wrap 7 green beans with turkey bacon and place in prepared dish.  Repeat, using all the green beans and turkey bacon.
Combine butter with brown sugar.  Pour over green bean bundles and sprinkle with garlic salt.
Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Eat Cake!
Description: 
Green beans are wrapped in turkey bacon and baked with butter, brown sugar and just a hint of garlic salt.  The amount of butter and brown sugar were reduced to make this a healthier option.Modified from: Sweet Green Bean Bundles
