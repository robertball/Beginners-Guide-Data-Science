Title:
Grandpa's Guacamole Recipe

Ingredients:
2 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
1/2 cup sour cream
2 tablespoons dry Ranch-style dressing mix

Directions:
Stir together the avocado, sour cream, and dressing mix in a bowl.
