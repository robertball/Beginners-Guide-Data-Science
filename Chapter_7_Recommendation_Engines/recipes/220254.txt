Title:
Original Ranch Roasted Potatoes - Duplicate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990451.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds small red potatoes, quartered
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 (1 ounce) packet Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix

Directions:
Place potatoes in a gallon-size Glad(R) Food Storage Bag and add oil; seal bag. Toss to coat. Add salad dressing mix and toss again until coated. Bake in ungreased baking pan at 450 degrees F for 30 to 35 minutes or until potatoes are brown and crisp.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley®
Description: 
So easy and so delicious, red potato quarters are tossed with Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning and roasted until brown and crisp.
