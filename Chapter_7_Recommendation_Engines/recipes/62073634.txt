Title:
Fudge Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
2 cups brown sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla
2 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
3 cups oatmeal
FILLING:
1 pkg. chocolate chips
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine butter, brown sugar, eggs & mix well.  Add vanilla.  Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt. Slowly add rolled oats.  Press 3/4 dough into lightly greased cookie sheet.
Combine filling ingredients in microwavable glass bowl.  Heat until melted, stirring once or twice.
Pour filling over presed dough, sprinkle with remaining dough (if desired).
Cook 18 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Penny Allen
Description: 
My mom has made these since I was a little girl.  More oatmeal based, than fudge based, but that's what she calls them.  Delicious!  Great for parties.
