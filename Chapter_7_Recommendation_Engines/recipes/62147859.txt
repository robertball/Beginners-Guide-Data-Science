Title:
Crock Pot Ground Meat & Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pound lean ground beef
1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. dried thyme
4 to 7 medium potatoes
1 large onions
1 can (10 3/4 oz.) condensed cream of potato soup
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Brown ground beef in skillet. Stir in pepper and thyme. Peel & slice the potatoes and onions,  butter the crock pot then place half of the onions and potatoes at the bottom of the crock pot. Add ground beef, then top with remaining potatoes and onions. Mix soup with water and pour over top. Cover and cook on low 8-10 hours or high 3-4 hours.
