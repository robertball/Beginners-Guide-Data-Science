Title:
Horchata de Arroz (Rice Drink) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Long or Medium Grain Rice (uncooked)
1 Cup cold water
1 (12 oz) can evaporated milk
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
The day before, place the rice in a bowl with enough water to cover it and let it soak overnight.
The next day, strain the rice and throw away the water.
Dilute the can of evaporated milk with enough water to make 4 cups of liquid.
Place the rice, 1/2 cup cold water and 2 cups of diluted evaporated milk in a blender.
Blend for about 30 seconds.
When all the rice is ground up, add the sugar, vanilla, and cinnamon.
Blend well.
Add the remaning two cups of diluted milk, the remaining 1/2 cup of cold water and blend well.
Strain through thick layered cheesecloth. It may be necessary to strain more than once but avoid squeezing the cloth.
Serve over ice.

Submitter: 
LatinaCook
Description: 
Refreshing rice drink with a hint of cinnamon. This version does not need to be boiled. Make a slush,if desired, by adding crushed ice. Enjoy!
