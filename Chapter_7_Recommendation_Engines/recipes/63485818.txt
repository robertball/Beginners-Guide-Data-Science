Title:
Tiramisu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 eggs, yolks and whites separated
1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
1 lb marscarpone cheese
2 cups expresso coffee
1/4 cup rum
40-50 savoiardi (lady fingers)
1/4 cup unsweeted cocoa powder

Directions:
beat the egg yolks and granulated sugar together.  Gradually incorporate the marcapone cheese into the egg yolk mixture.  In a separate bowl beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form.  Gently fold the egg whites into the marscarpone mixture.  Combine the expresso and rum  Line the bottom of a 9x13" pan with savoiardi that have been briefly dunked into the expresso mixture.   Cover this layer with half of the marscarpone mixture.  Top this with another layer of savoiardi dunked into the expresso mixture and spread this with the remaining mascarpone mixture.  Dust the top with the cocoa powder using a fine mesh strainer.  Refrigerate for 8 hours before serving.
