Title:
Vegan Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup coconut oil
1 cup self-rising flour
1 cup organic sugar
1 cup nut, rice or soymilk
1 (16 ounce) can sliced peaches in syrup
1 t cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place coconut oil in an 9x13 glass baking dish. Place dish in oven until oil is melted.
In a medium bowl, combine flour and sugar. Mix well, then stir in nutmilk. Spoon mixture into baking dish, on top of melted coconut oil. Pour peaches over flour mixture.
Bake in preheated oven for 50 to 60 minutes, until peaches are bubbly and crust is lightly browned.
