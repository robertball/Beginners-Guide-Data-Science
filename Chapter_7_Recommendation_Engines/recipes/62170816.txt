Title:
Caramel Dream Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/357684.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
1/3 cup margarine, softened
2 eggs
1 (14 oz.) can sweetened condensed milk
1 tsp. vanilla
1 cup pecans, chopped
1 (8 oz.) package toffee bits
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease a 13x9 inch pan.
Combine cake mix, margarine and 1 egg and mix until crumbly.  Pat into the prepared pan.
Combine the remaining egg, sweetened condensed milk, vanilla, pecans, toffee bits and chocolate chips and pour over the crust.  Bake for 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
My husband's grandma always made tons of treats for the holidays, but these were always my favorite.  So rich and delicious, you will enjoy these all throughout the year!
