Title:
Stuffed Grilled Chicken Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breast halves
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
5 tablespoons butter, chopped
3/4 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1 teaspoon lemon peel

Directions:
In a small skillet, saute onion and garlic in 3 tablespoons butter for 2-3 minutes or until tender.  Remove from heat; stir in bread crumbs.  Carefully cut a pocket in each chicken breast half.  Fill with bread crumb mixture; secure with metal or soaked wooden skewers.
In a small microwave-safe bowl, melt remaining butter; stir in lemon peel.  Grill chicken, covered, over medium heatfor 6-8 minutes per side or until juices runclear, basting frequently with lemon butter.
