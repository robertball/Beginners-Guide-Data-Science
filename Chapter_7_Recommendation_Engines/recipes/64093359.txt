Title:
ChipotleSalsa (Chevy's) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium tomatoes
2-4 small jalapenos (red is best)
1/4 medium Spanish onion
2 cloves garlic
2 tablespoons fresh cilantro (chopped)
2 tablespoons white vinegar
2 teaspoons salt
1 1/2 teaspoons liquid smoke (mesquite flavored)

Directions:
Preheat barbecue grill to high temperature.
Remove any stems from the tomatoes, then rub some oil over each tomato.
Rub oil on jalapenos and onion.
Place all on grill and cook until tomato skins split, and jalapenos are charred.
Remove all to a plate and allow to cool.
Add all ingredients to food processor.  Toss out the liquid that remains on the plate.
Chill several hours or overnight.
