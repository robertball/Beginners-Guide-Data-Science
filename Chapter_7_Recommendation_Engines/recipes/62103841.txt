Title:
5 minute Fiesta Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can tuna in water, drained
1/4 cup mayonaise
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon Louisiana hot sauce
1/2 cup Fiesta style cheeze crackers, crushed
4 large leaves of lettuce

Directions:
Mix all ingedients together. Serve over a bed of lettuce. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
CUPYCAKESMURF
Description: 
A creamy blend of tuna, hot suace, and fiesta style cheese crackers give this cold tuna salad a great kick! This is a quick and easy power lunch ready in under 5 minutes!
