Title:
Orange breakfast shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 C frozen orange juice
1/2 sliced banana
1 C plain lowfat yoghurt
1 C skim milk
4 ice cubes
2 wide strips of orange peel, garnish

Directions:
Pour all ingredients (except orange peel garnish) in blender, blend until smooth. Pour, garnish and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Lynn Pennec
Description: 
A quick way to enjoy a healthy lowfat breakfast.
