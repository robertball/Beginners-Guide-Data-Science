Title:
Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 1/2 cup crunchy peanut butter
1 cup white sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda

Directions:
Cream together butter, peanut butter and sugars. Beat in eggs.
In a separate bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Stir into batter. Put batter in refrigerator for 1 hour.
Roll into 1 inch balls and put on baking sheets.  Flatten each ball with a fork, making a cris-cross pattern.  Bake in a preheated 350 degrees F oven for about 10 minutes or until cookies begin to brown. Do not over-bake.
