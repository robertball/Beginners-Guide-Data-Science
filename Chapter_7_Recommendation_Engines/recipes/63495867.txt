Title:
No Yeast pizza crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2c Flour
2 3/4tsp Baking powder
1tsp Salt
1tbsp Oil
3/4c water

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients then add 3/4 cup water and oil. Stir until it forms a ball. The dough should be soft, add a little flour if too sticky. Knead on a floured surface for 3-4 minutes.
Bake at 400Â°F for 15-25 minutes.
