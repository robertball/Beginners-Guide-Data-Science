Title:
Sorta Smores Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 reduced-fat graham cracker squares (2 squares per sheet)
4 Tbs dark chocolate ice cream topping, refrigerated so that it's slightly thickened
2 Tbs dry roasted peanuts, coarsely chopped or crushed
4 Tbs light whipped topping

Directions:
Spread each cracker with the chocolate topping, sprinkle with the peanuts, and top with the whipped topping.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
THERESA_M
Description: 
This is a fast, no-bake treat that tastes sorta like smores -- without the hassle and mess.  Very easy to make and is the perfect treat paired with a with a tall glass of milk!
