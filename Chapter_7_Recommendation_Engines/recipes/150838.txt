Title:
Baileys Caramel Appletini (Baileys Appletini) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978588.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ounces Baileys® Caramel Irish Cream
1/2 ounce Smirnoff® Green Apple vodka
1 slice apple
Caramel sauce

Directions:
Shake Baileys(R) Caramel Irish Cream and Smirnoff(R) Green Apple vodka with ice and strain.
Garnish with apple slice and caramel drizzle.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Adding a touch of richness to any drink, Baileys® Irish cream liqueur mixes fine Irish whiskey, fresh dairy cream and a hint of chocolate flavors.
