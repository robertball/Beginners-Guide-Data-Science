Title:
Cheesy Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Phillips Crab Meat (any grade)
(2) 8 oz. pkgs. cream cheese
8 oz. sour cream
2 Tbsp. lemon juice
2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 Tbsp. fresh or dried parsley
1 tsp. dry mustard
1 1/2 tsp. Phillips Seafood Seasoning
8 oz. shredded cheddar cheese
4 oz. mozzarella cheese

Directions:
1. Allow cream cheese to come to room temperature and soften.
2. In a large mixing bowl, combine softened cream cheese, sour cream, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, 1 tsp. parsley, dry mustard and seafood seasoning. Stir in 7 ½ ounces cheddar cheese and 4 ounces mozzarella cheese. Mix well.
3. Gently fold in crab meat by hand until evenly distributed throughout mixture. Be careful not to break apart the meat.
4. Pour mixture into 9-inch lightly greased pie or quiche pan. Sprinkle with remaining cheddar cheese and parsley.
5. Bake at 350°F for 20-25 minutes or until top is golden brown and bubbling.
