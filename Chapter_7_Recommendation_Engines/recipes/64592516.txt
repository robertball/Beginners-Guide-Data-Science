Title:
Pico de Gallo Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3807522.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Medium Tomatoes (seeds removed)
¼ Cup Cilantro
¼ Cup White Onion
½ Jalapeno
½ Serrano (seeds removed)
2 Tablespoons Lime Juice (1 Fresh Squeezed Lime)
1 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
Half Zest of a Lime
1 Teaspoon Kosher Salt (or fresh cracked salt)
1 Tablespoon Green Onion

Directions:
To remove seeds from the tomatoes, lay tomato on a cutting board so the stem faces the side. Cut the tomato in half. Using a spoon, scoop out the seeds and liquid.
Chop tomatoes, onions, jalapeno, serrano, and green onion into small pieces.
Combine ingredients with lime juice, garlic powder, lime zest, and salt.
Store in an airtight container for at least 1 hour before serving.
Tip: Add 2 teaspoons of white vinegar to ensure the pico de gallo stays fresh longer.
