Title:
Lara's Ultimate Creamy Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
1/2 cup butter, cubed
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1-1/2 cups milk
1 cup sour cream
1 cup grated mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 tespoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground mustard
1/2 teaspoon pepper
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 small can diced green chilies(optional)

Directions:
Cook macaroni according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a large saucepan, melt butter. Whisk in flour until smooth. Gradually add the milk. Bring mixture to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes. Reduce heat; stir in the sour cream, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, salt, mustard and pepper until cheese is melted and smooth. Drain macaroni; toss with cheddar cheese. Transfer to a greased 3-qt. baking dish. Add cream sauce and mx well. Bake, uncovered, at 350 for 35-40 minutes or until golden brown.
