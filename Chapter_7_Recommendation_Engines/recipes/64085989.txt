Title:
Leftover Moroccan Meat Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1172483.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup water
1/4 med yellow onion chopped
3 garlic cloves minced
1 pkg brown gravy mix that makes 1 cup gravy
1/2 tsp of each of these spices:__cumin,__cinnamon, __curry, __ground ginger, __black pepper, __turmeric, and __salt
1 sweet potato diced and cooked
1/2 cup peas
1/2 cup frozen corn
1 cup left over pot roast shredded or other meat
1 frozen deep dish pie crust
1/2 cup feta cheese crumbles

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
In pan add water, garlic and onion.  Bring to a boil, cook five minutes over low heat
Add gravy packet and spices. Stir until blended.
Add potatoes, peas, corn, and pot roast. Stir occationally until heated through.
Pour into frozen pie crust. Top with Feta cheese crumbles.
Bake for about 25 minutes until crust is brown.
