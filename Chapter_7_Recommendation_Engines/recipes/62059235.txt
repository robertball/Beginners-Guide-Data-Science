Title:
Sara Jane's Oatmeal Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Set oven at 375
1 cup Oats (Not instant)
1 Cup Sugar
1 Cup Brown sugar
1/2 cup shortening
1 1/2 Cup flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1 1/4 Cuo boiling water
2 eggs
For icing:
6 tbsp melted butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 tbsp cream or milk
2/3 cup coconut
1/2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Cake: Soak oats in boiling water at least 20 minutes.
Mix both sugars with shortening.
Add eggs and beat well. Sift in flour, salt, baking soda and nutmeg. Add oats and mix well. Bake in an 8"x8" pan for 35-40 minutes.
Topping: Mis all ingredients and pour over cake. Place under broiler until lightly browned.
