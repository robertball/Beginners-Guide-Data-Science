Title:
Turkey Breast with Walnut Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Breast of Turkey;  2 lbs.
3.5 oz of minced Walnuts
3 tblsp. Butter
3 Onions
3 clove of Garlic
1/2-1 cup of Sour Cream
Parsley Green and Root

Directions:
Salt the Turkey and Fry in the Butter.  When half done, add the Sour-cream and
saute until tender.  separately;  Saute the Chopped Onions. Chopped Parsnip and Garlic.
5 minute before completion add the onion mixture;  the Walnuts and chopped
Parsley Greens.

Savory Country style Turkey in Sour-cream and Walnuts
