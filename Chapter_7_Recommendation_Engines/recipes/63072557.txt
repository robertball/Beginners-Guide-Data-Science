Title:
Cake in a hurry! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/774825.jpg
Ingredients:
1 18 oz angel food cake mix
1 18 oz cake mix of your choosing - any flavor
3 TBSP water
1 mug

Directions:
Mix both bags together in a gallon bag.
Grease a mug.
Place 1/3 cup of the mix, with 3 TBSP water in mug.
Mix well.
Place in microwave for 1 and 1/2 minutes   (ovens may vary)
Frost.
Devour!
