Title:
Loaded Bacon Ranch (Chili) Dog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4542984.jpg
Ingredients:
8 hot dogs
8 hot dog buns
1 10 oz. can hot dog chili sauce, such as Castleberry'sÂ®, warmed according to directions on the can, optional
8 slices bacon, cooked crisp
1/2 cup onion, chopped
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup ranch dressing, or to taste
1-2 fresh jalapeno peppers, sliced into rings

Directions:
Cook hot dogs to your liking.
Place 1 hot dog on each bun.
Place 1 slice of bacon along one side of the hot dog.
Top with chili, optional
Layer with cheese, onions, ranch dressing and jalapeno slices.
Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
This is soooo good! My guys love theirs with chili added on top, but I enjoy mine without. Use a good ranch dressing for this (I prefer homemade).
