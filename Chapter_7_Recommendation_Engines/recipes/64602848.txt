Title:
Hamburger/Hot Dog buns with Bread Machine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup milk ( or 1 !/4 cup water and 2 Tbsp milk powder)
1 beaten egg
1/4 cup white sugar
3 3/4 cups flour
1 1/4 tsp yeast

Directions:
Put everything into bread machine and make let the machine make the dough
When dough ready, cut in half and roll out to circles 1 inch thick and cut into 3 1/2 inch rounds or long hot dog shapes.
Place on greased baking sheet and brush with melted butter. Let rise 1 hour then bake at 350 degrees for about 9 mins.
