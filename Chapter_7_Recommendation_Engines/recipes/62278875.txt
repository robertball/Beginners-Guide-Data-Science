Title:
Southwestern Bean Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons vegetable oil
1 onion, chopped
3 garlic cloves, minced
3 fresh jalapenos, sliced
2 (15 ounce) cans great northern beans, drained and rinsed OR 4 cups cooked dried great northern beans, drained and rinsed
1 can creamed corn
1/2 cup carrot, grated
1/2 cup celery, diced
1 1/2 cup vegetable broth (from bullion is OK)
3 teaspoons ground cumin
2 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon ground red pepper
1 cup milk
8 oz. pkg cream cheese or neufechtel cheese

Directions:
1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat and saute the onion, garlic, and jalapenos until onion is softened.
2.  Add half of the beans and mash roughly.
3.  Stir in remaining beans, corn, carrots, celery, and broth.
4.  Bring soup to a simmer and stir in the cumin, chili powder, and cayenne pepper.
5.  Stir the milk and cream cheese into the soup, bringing it gradually to a simmer.
6.  Reduce heat and allow soup to simmer until veggies are tender, about 5 minutes. Stir frequently.

Submitter: 
Denise McCabe
Description: 
A creamy, slightly sweet, spicy combination of beans and veggies.
