Title:
Creamy Corn with Pimento Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans (14 oz) corn, drained
1 tbs butter
4 oz cream cheese
2 oz pimentos, drained

Directions:
In a high sided pot melt butter over medium heat. As butter starts to brown add corn; mix well. Heat for about 5 minutes. Add cream cheese and mix/heat through about 2 minutes. When cream cheese is incorporated fold in pimento. Heat an additional 3 minutes to thicken. Serve hot.
