Title:
Shredded Barbecue Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb chuck roast
1 large onion
1 stalk celery, chopped
1 large green pepper, chopped
1 1/2 quarts of water
Sauce:
2 cloves garlic
1 tsp salt
1 bottle catsup
2 tb brown sugar
2 tb white vinegar
1 tsp allspice
1 tsp dry mustard
1/2 tsp chili powder
2-3 drops Tabasco sauce
1 bay leaf
1/4 tsp garlic salt
1/4 tsp paprika

Directions:
Place roast and vegetables in roaster (or large pot); cover with water. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer covered for 4 hours. Remove meat, saving broth; cool and shred. Return meat to 1 1/2 cups of broth and add sauce ingredients. Slowly boil uncovered for 1 hour adding additional water if needed.
