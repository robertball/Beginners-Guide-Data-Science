Title:
Slovak Jumbalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. polish smoked sausage, cut into 1" pieces
1 red or yellow bell pepper, cored and cut into 1" pieces
1 small onion, sliced
2 ribs of celery, sliced into 1" pieces
3 C. coarsely chopped red or green cabbage
16 oz. can fire-roasted diced tomatoes
16 oz. can light or dark kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 tsp. chili powder
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. black pepper
salt to taste
Whole grain rice, cooked

Directions:
Add about a tablespoon of olive oil to a large dutch oven and lightly brown the sausage, for a few minutes.  Add all of the remaining vegetables except the canned tomatoes and beans.  Turn and saut√©' until the vegetables are just tender.  Add the tomatoes, beans and spices.  Stir, cover and simmer lightly for 20 minutes.  Serve over hot rice.

Submitter: 
Sally Anne
Description: 
I developed this quick recipe one evening when I originally planned on preparing Slovak cabbage and noodles for dinner.  I decided to add the other ingredients to make it a spicier and heartier meal.  It was a big hit.
