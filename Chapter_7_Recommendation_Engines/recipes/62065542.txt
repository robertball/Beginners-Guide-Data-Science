Title:
quacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Whole Avocado's pick the ones with the shiniest skin those are usually the best
half a gallon of 2% milk
2 tbsp onion powder
1 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp garlic powder

Directions:
1. add olive oil garlic powder and onion powder to large bowl
2. Carve out avocado inside
3. Add 1 cup of milk, consistency should be creamy and light green if not of that consistency add one more cup
4. smush everything you've added and it's ready to be served!
