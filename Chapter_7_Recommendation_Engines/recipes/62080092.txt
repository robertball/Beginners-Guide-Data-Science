Title:
Sauteed Squash with onions. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large squash, diced
4 large tomatoes, diced
1 onion, diced
salt and pepper to taste
olive oil to sautee

Directions:
sautee onions in olive oil until tender.
add squash and sautee for 3 min while mixing.
add tomatoes, salt and pepper as desired.
cover and cook until squash is cooked, about 25 min.

Submitter: 
IMANKAY
Description: 
This simple arabic dish enjoys the flavors of squash sauteed with onions in a tomato sauce.  Great to dip wth pita bread, but can work with rice.

have warm pita bread ready and dip into this healthy easy dish.
