Title:
Tropical Strawberry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups frozen or fresh strawberries
1 cup frozen or fresh pineapple chunks
1/2 cup milk
1 1/2 cups frozen yogurt

Directions:
In a blender, blend the strawberries, pineapple, milk, yogurt, until smooth.

Submitter: 
Teresa Haider
Description: 
This strawberry and pineapple blast of flavor is a great way to wake up in the morning, or cool off in the afternoon.Modified from: Tropical Strawberry
