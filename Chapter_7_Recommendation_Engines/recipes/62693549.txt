Title:
Shamrock Icebox Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of Sweetened Condensed Milk (fat-free works fine too)
3 egg yolks
4-6 T. fresh squeezed lime juice and pith
green gel food coloring
1 graham cracker pie crust
Cool Whip for topping (reduced-fat works fine too)

Directions:
Whisk together Sweetened Condensed Milk, egg yolks, and lime juice.
Add green gel food coloring until desired color is achieved.
Pour into graham cracker crust.
Cover and refrigerate several hours.
Top pie with Cool Whip
Refrigerate another hour.
Keep refrigerated until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
I wanted to carry out the Irish theme for my St. Patrick's Day Dinner, so I came up with this variation of my lemon icebox pie.
