Title:
Popovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 vanilla
dabs of butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Spray Pam & dab of butter in each six 6-ounce custard cups.
In a medium bowl beat eggs slightly, Beat in flour, milk & salt until just smooth; being careful not to overbeat. Fill custard cups 1/2 full.
Bake at 450 degrees F (230 degrees C) for 20 minutes. Decrease oven temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) & bake for 20 minutes more. Immediately remove from cups and serve piping hot.

Submitter: 
Sush Maven
Description: 
The secret to light and airy popovers is a very light touch in mixing and a very hot oven for baking.  These are best served hot from the oven.Modified from: Popovers
