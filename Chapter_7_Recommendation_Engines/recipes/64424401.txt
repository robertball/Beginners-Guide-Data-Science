Title:
Chicken for won ton salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup sesame seed oil
Canola oil (1/2, 1/2, or 3/4 cup)
3/4 cup orange juice
I" nub fresh ginger grated
1 tsp salt
1tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
5-6 lb chicken breasts

Directions:
Trim chicken
Mix all ingredients in a zip lock
Squish bag to distribute marinade over chicken
Place bag in fridge and marinate for 1 hour turning once
Cook Patrick utility chicken style
