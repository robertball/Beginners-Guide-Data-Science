Title:
Chocolate Hemp Protein Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1907772.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups water
1 banana, cut into chunks
1/2 cup raw hemp seeds
3 chopped pitted dates, or more to taste
1 tablespoon cocoa powder, or more to taste

Directions:
Blend water, banana, hemp seeds, dates, and cocoa powder together in a blender until smooth. Refrigerate until flavors combine, 3 hours to overnight.

Submitter: 
Amy
Description: 
This shake tastes great and is packed with good protein; the perfect snack after your workout.
