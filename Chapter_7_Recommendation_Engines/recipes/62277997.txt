Title:
Chile Rellenos Casserole with Ranchero Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 egg whites
1/3 cup all purpose flour
1 tsp salt
1 lb Monterey Jack cheese, shreaded
1 Cup evaporated milk
1 Large can whole green chilies (or 3 small ones)
For the Sauce:
2 T oil
1 white onion, diced small
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cumin
2-3 T California Chili powder
1 med red or green bell pepper, bite size pieces
1 T Flour
1 can chicken broth

Directions:
For the casserole:
Heat oven to 350 degrees
Spray a large pyrex dish with cooking spray.
In a medium bowl beat egg whites until stiff.  Fold in milk and flour and salt into the egg whites.
Cover the bottom of the casserole dish with half of the egg mixture.
Open the chilies flat and make a layer of chilies over the eggs. Using about half of the chilies.
Sprinkle the Jack cheese over the chilies.  Top with another layer of green chilies and the remaining egg mixture.
Bake for 30 minutes at 350 until the egg mixture is set.
Let stand for 10 minutes before slicing.  Serve with the sauce.
While the casserole bakes prepare the sauce.
In a medium pan saute the onion, garlic in the oil.
Add the salt, cumin and California Chili Powder.
Add the bell peppers and cook until soft.
Add the flour and stir to form a roux.
Slowly add the chicken broth and stir to avoid getting lumps.
Bring to a boil and then turn to low and simmer.  Taste and adjust the salt and chili powder if needed.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
This is a great way to serve up Chile Rellenos without having to fry them.
