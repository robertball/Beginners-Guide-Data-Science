Title:
Mexican Margaritavon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bottle of Coors Light or Miller beer
1 shot of tequila
1 glass of 7 up

Directions:
place 1 ounce of tequila into a 2 ounce glass
fill with 7 up
place hand over the glass and bang the glass on the table
as it fizzes, down the shot
chase with beer

Submitter: 
recipes need gluten
Description: 
This subtle drink must be enjoyed with a cigar and your closest peeps. It will knock your socks off-be careful, there is an Irish Lassie waiting to scoop your socks.
