Title:
Israeli Salad with Pickles and Mint Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. Persian or English cucumbers, peeled and diced
2 lbs. ripe tomatoes, seeded and diced
3 large dill pickles, diced
1 bunch chopped fresh mint
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
2 fresh lemons, juiced (or more to taste)
1/2 tsp salt (or more to taste)
1 cup mixed spring greens (optional)

Directions:
Put vegetables in bowl.
Whisk together oil, juice, and salt.
Toss dressing with vegetables.
Serve alone or on bed of greens.
