Title:
Dale's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/780434.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound ground beef
1 cup onion, chopped
1/2 cup green pepper, chopped
1 teaspoon ground oregano
1 teaspoon basil leaves
16 ounces whole tomatoes, petite diced, with juice
32 ounces tomato sauce
16 ounces canned kidney beans, rinsed
16 ounces canned black beans, rinsed
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1 bay leaf
1 tablespoon chili powder
dash ground cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon

Directions:
Brown beef with the onions, peppers, basil and oregano.  Drain the grease.
Add the remaining ingredients and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
(Also makes well in a crock pot.)

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
This slightly-adapted recipe comes from the Nantahalia Outdoor Center in Tennessee.  Truly the best chili I've ever had.  I had it served over an open-faced cheeseburger- DIVINE!
