Title:
Garlic Shrimp and Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. butter
1-1/2 oz. chablis
1/2 oz. brandy
1/4 t. salt
1/2 t. white pepper
1 T. worcestershire sauce
2 cloves garlic, minced
1-1/2 oz. shallots, minced
1 c. mushrooms
1 lb. shrimp, peeled and deveined
1-1/2 oz. parsley
2 c. wild rice, cooked

Directions:
Melt butter in skillet over medium heat.
Saute garlic and shallots in butter mixture for one minute; add chablis and brandy, salt, pepper and worcestershire sauce. Add mushrooms and cook one minute more. Remove from heat.
Arrange shrimp in a single layer in shallow baking dish. Pour butter/mushroom mixture over shrimp and broil 10 minutes. Garnish with parsley and serve over rice.
