Title:
Tuna Dip II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/565315.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
3 tablespoons salsa

Directions:
In a small bowl, blend the tuna, cream cheese and salsa. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
SJ_TULL
Description: 
This creamy, colorful dip is a great recipe for unexpected guests or any type of gathering. Use low fat cream cheese, if desired. Adjust the quantity and heat level of salsa to taste. Serve with crackers or mini bagel chips.
