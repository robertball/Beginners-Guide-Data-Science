Title:
BBQ Broccoli Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons cider vinegar
1 tablespoon lime juice
2 tablespoons white sugar
1 tablespoon dried onion flakes
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon smoked chipotle chile powder
1 teaspoon mesquite seasoning
1 teaspoon ground white pepper
1/2 teaspoon celery salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
1 (12 ounce) package broccoli slaw mix

Directions:
Whisk together mayonnaise, vinegar, and lime juice in a large bowl. Add sugar; whisk until dissolved. Mix in dried onion, cumin, paprika, chipotle chile powder, mesquite seasoning, white pepper, celery salt, and garlic salt. Add broccoli slaw and toss until well coated. Marinate in the refrigerator for 1 hour before serving.
