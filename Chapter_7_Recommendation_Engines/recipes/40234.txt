Title:
Cinnamon Chile Crusted Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978764.jpg
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Ancho Chile Pepper
1 1/2 teaspoons McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Ground Saigon Cinnamon
1 1/2 teaspoons McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Oregano Leaves
1 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Chipotle Chile Pepper
1 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Parsley Leaves
1/2 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Ground Cumin
3/4 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Coarse Grind Black Pepper
1 1/4 teaspoons salt
4 New York strip steaks, cut 1-inch thick

Directions:
Combine spices and salt in a small bowl. Spread over a large plate. Roll steaks in spices to coat evenly.
Grill steaks over medium-high heat 8-10 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.

Submitter: 
McCormick® Gourmet Collection®
Description: 
The taste of the Southwest is so easy to make at home with a spice rub blended with ancho and chipotle chile peppers. Just rub the spices over the meat and grill right away for instant flavor.
