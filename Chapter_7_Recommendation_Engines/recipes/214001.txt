Title:
Apricot and Honey Ham Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4488610.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup apricot jam
3 tablespoons honey
1 tablespoon hot English mustard
2 tablespoons orange marmalade

Directions:
Stir together the apricot jam, honey, hot English mustard, and orange marmalade in a bowl until the mixture is well combined.

Submitter: 
Cheryl Gausdal
Description: 
I found this recipe years ago and use this glaze whenever I make a ham now.
