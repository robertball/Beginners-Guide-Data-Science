Title:
Grilled Vegetable Tortellini Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8 oz packages dry cheese or cheese and spinach tortellini
2 or 3 Medium size zucchini
2 or 3 Medium size yellow squash
2 Medium red onions
Olive oil for brushing
Salt & pepper
Dressing ingredients:
1 pkg (.7 oz) Good Seasons Italian dressing dry mix
2 tbl dry italian seasoning
1/4 c balsamic vinegar
3/4 c olive oil
3 tablespoons water

Directions:
Mix dressing ingredients together and set aside.
Cook the tortellini in a large pot of salted boiling water unil al dente. Drain, and cool under cold water.
Slice off the ends of zucchini and sqash and slice in half lengthwiwse. Slice red onions into thick rounds.
Brush vegetables with olive oil, sprinkle with salt and pepper. Grill on medium/high until brown and soft. You don't want to over cook the squash.
Let the vegetables rest for few minutes after you take them off the grill, they will be hot!
Chop vegetables into medium size pieces. In a large bowl, mix together pasta & vegetables. Add dressing, salt, and pepper to taste.
Serve warm or cold the next day.

Submitter: 
carrieanddave
Description: 
This recipe is a great way to use up summer squash and zucchini. Tastes great warm, or you can make it ahead and serve cold.
