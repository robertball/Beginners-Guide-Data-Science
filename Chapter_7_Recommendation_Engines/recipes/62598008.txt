Title:
Ranch Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lb red potatoes
8oz ranch dressing
1c shredded cheddar
1 pkg (2.8oz) bacon bits
1/4t pepper
1/4t garlic powder

Directions:
Boil potatoes 20-25 minutes.
Combine all other ingredients.
Toss potatoes and dressing, mix, and refrigerate 2 hours.
