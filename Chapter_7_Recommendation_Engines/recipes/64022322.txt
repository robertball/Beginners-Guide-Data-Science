Title:
Creamy Swiss and Tomato Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. pasta
1/2 cup shredded Swiss
10 oz. whole leaf spinach
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 - 28oz. can plum tomatoes
1 tbsp. butter
1 tbsp. flour
3/4 tsp. salt
2 cups milk
1 tbsp. Dijon mustard

Directions:
Chop tomatoes into bite sized pieces and let drain.
Cook pasta and just before it's done, add spinach to quickly wilt.
Drain pasta then add tomatoes and toss.
Heat butter in a sauce pan, then whisk in flour, salt and pepper.
Slowly whisk in milk, bring to a boil, then reduce heat and cook about 5 minutes, stirring often, until thickened.
Remove from heat and stir in cheese and mustard then pour over pasta and toss to coat.
Serve immediately.
