Title:
Havanas and Bananas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 ounce rum
3/4 ounce creme de banane
1 1/2 ounces Bailey's irish cream
4 ounces pina colada non-alcholic drink mix
2 cups crushed ice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth and slushy.
Pour into 16 ounce glass and garnish with an orange wedge (optional).

Submitter: 
Kelli F
Description: 
This is my version of a great drink I had at Margaritaville in Las Vegas.
