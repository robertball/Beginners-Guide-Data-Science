Title:
Pickled Mostaccioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb box of mostaccioli pasta
1 1/2 cup vinegar
1 1/2 cup sugar
2 Tbsp prepard yellow mustard
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp dried parsley
1 med onion peeled, quartered
1 med cumcumber with peel. Cut in chunks

Directions:
Prepare pasta according to box instructions
drain and cool, place in large bowl.  Add all remaining ingredients to a blender or food processor. (might need to blend in half batches to avoid overflow)  Blend till all ingredients are well blended into a sauce. (about 3 minutes)
Pour over the pasta, mix well.  Must refrigerate at least 3 days before serving. Stir once a day for first 3 days.  Can be made weeks ahead.

Submitter: 
Santa Maria Cook
Description: 
Marinated pasta in a sweet and sour sauce.  Can be made ahead and will last up to three weeks.  Our family loves this recipes. Great for BBQ parties or any family event.
