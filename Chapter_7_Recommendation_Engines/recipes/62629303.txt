Title:
Chicken Chilaquiles Verdes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large chicken breasts cooked and shredded
1 19oz can green enchilada sauce
1 4oz can diced green chiles, pureed
1/4 cup diced onion
1/4 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp Mexican oregano
3 cups Mexican blend cheese
8 corn tortillas heated on a cast iron griddle
6 cups home made re-fried beans
Chopped fresh cilantro and onion
Optional for breakfast to add to the above:
Eggs cooked to preference
Diced and fried potatoes

Directions:
In a 5 cup slow cooker (or in a pan on the stove-top) place chicken, enchilada sauce, green chiles, onions, cumin and oregano on low for 4 hours. Place beans on warmed tortilla and top with chicken mixture, cheese and cilantro/onion.

Submitter:

Description: 
Chicken Chilaquiles Verdes that are easy to make and serve for breakfast, lunch or dinner. This can also be made on the stove-top.
