Title:
Copycat Famous Dave's Drunken Rum Apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/773736.jpg
Ingredients:
7-8 Granny Smith Apples, Cored and Sliced
1/4 Cup Spiced Rum
2 Cups Sugar
2 Tablespoons Cinnamon
1 Tablespoon Nutmeg

Directions:
Place 7-8 sliced and cored Granny Smith apples into a large saucepan.
Stir in 2 cups sugar, 2 tablespoons cinnamon, and 1 tablespoon nutmeg.
Add 1/4 cup spiced Rum, and stir until the apples are coated, and until ingredients have combined into a thick sauce.
Turn the heat to medium. Bring to a boil on medium heat.
Boil on medium heat for 20 minutes, stirring frequently.
Let cool for 5-10 minutes.

My all time favorite side dish! A very simple copy of Famous Dave's delicious Drunken Apples!
