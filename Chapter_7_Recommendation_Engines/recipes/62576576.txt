Title:
Pasta with Creamy Wild Mushroom Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. uncooked pasta
1 tbsp. butter
12 oz. exotic mushroom blend (fresh - not dried)
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/3 cup finely chopped shallots
1 tbsp. minced garlic
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/4 cup dry white wine
2/3 cup whipping cream
1/2 cup (2 oz.) grated Parmigiano cheese
2 tbsp. fresh parsley

Directions:
Cook pasta to al dente.
In large skillet, melt butter and add mushrooms, onions, shallots, garlic, 1 tsp. salt and pepper.  Cook 12 minutes or until liquid evaporates.  Add wine and cook 2 more minutes.
Add cooked pasta, cream, cheese and parsley.  Toss to coat and add remaining salt if necessary.
