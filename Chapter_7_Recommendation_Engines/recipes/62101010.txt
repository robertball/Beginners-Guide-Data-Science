Title:
Amazing Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb dry lasagna noodles
1 26 oz Jar of Italian Spaghetti sauce
1/2 lb ground beef
1/2 lb italian sausage
1 16 oz small curd cottage cheese
1 8 oz package of cream cheese
1 8 oz sour cream
1 tablespoon oregano
1 tablespoon parsley
1 lb mozzarella cheese (shredded)

Directions:
Soak Lasagna Noodles in Hot water for 30min
Pre-Heat oven to 350.
Cook Ground beef and Italian Sausage until well browned
Drain Grease and add Spaghetti Sauce to meat and let simmer
While Sauce is heating in a large bowl mix cottage cheese, cream cheese and sour cream until well blended.
Mix Parsley and Oregano into Cream cheese mixture.
In a 9x13 dish arrange 3 noodles on the bottom and add 1/2 the spaghetti sauce.
Arrange three more noodles and add the whole bowl of cream cheese mixture.
Arrange 3 more noodles and add the other half of the sauce.
Cook in pre-heated oven for 40 minutes.
Remove from oven and sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
Place back in oven and continue cooking for 10 minutes untill cheese is melted.
Cool and serve.
