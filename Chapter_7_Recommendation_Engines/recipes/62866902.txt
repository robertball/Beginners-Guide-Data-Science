Title:
Pineapple-Bacon Wraps (microwave) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz.) can pineapple chunks (juice packed)
2 tbsp. soy sauce
1/2 tsp. ground ginger
Dash of garlic powder
6 slices of bacon, cut into thirds

Directions:
Reserve 3 tbsp. of pineapple juice from can. Combine juice, soy sauce, ginger, and garlic powder in a small bowl. Add pineapple and let stand 1 to two hours. Drain but save this liquid. Wrap a piece of bacon around each pineapple chunk; secure with a toothpick (wooden). Line plate with several layers of paper towels. arrange around outside edge of plate with 1/4 inch of space between. Microwave at high until bacon is lightly browned, 5 to 9 mins. If using non-rotating microwave dish; rotate and turn several times. You can use the saved liquid to spoon over the appetizer as it cooks
