Title:
Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989533.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (14.5 ounce) cans COLLEGE INN® Chicken Broth
1 (14.5 ounce) can DEL MONTE® Stewed Tomatoes, Mexican Recipe, coarsely chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
2 cups cooked chicken or turkey, diced
1 1/2 cups crushed baked tortilla chips
Cilantro
1/4 cup Shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
Combine tomatoes with juice, broth, onion, garlic and cumin in large pot; simmer 10 minutes. Add chicken or turkey; simmer 3 to 5 minutes. Top individual servings with tortilla chips, cilantro and cheese.

Submitter: 
COLLEGE INN® Broth
Description: 
This tangy tomato-based soup kicks up your taste buds. Top it off with a handful of crunchy tortilla chips and you've got a fiesta on your dinner table.
