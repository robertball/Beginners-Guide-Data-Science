Title:
Red Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 med. tomatoes
2 small red bell peppers
4 sprigs parsley
3 carrots

Directions:
Trim base of bell pepper & remove seeds.
Process tomatoes, bell pepper, parsley, & carrots through Juice Fountain™ using speeds 1, 4, & 5. Serve Immediately
