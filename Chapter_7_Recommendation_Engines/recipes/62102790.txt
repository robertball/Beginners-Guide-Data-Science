Title:
Pesto chicken with roasted vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts (skin off)
1 small jar of red pesto
1 red bell pepper
1 yellow or green bell pepper
1 large red onion
1 large corgette
extra virgin olive oil
1/2 teaspoon dry basil
seasoning (salt/garlic salt & Pepper)
freshly grated parmesan

Directions:
slice peppers and corgettes 1/4 inch thickness and slice onion slightly thinner.
arrange vegetables in a shallow oven dish
coat liberally with olive oil, add seaoning and basil and mix.
with a sharp knife score (slice) each breast 3 or 4 times without cutting right through and place beside the vegetables.
spread the pesto evenly onto each breast and finally sprinkle with a little more olive oil.
cook in pre-heated oven at 180c for aprox 45 minutes.
arrange vegetables with chicken on top, spoon over any remaining pan juices and finish off with grated parmesan.
serve with pasta or garlic bread

Submitter: 
CharlieB
Description: 
This, 'all in one dish' is so easy to prepare and cook and is colourful, tasty and good enough for any special occasion.
