Title:
Panko Parmesan Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/649355.jpg
Ingredients:
4 - 4 oz. salmon fillets
1 1/2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup Panko
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
1 teaspoon Italian herb seasoning
1/8 teaspoon garlc powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400°F.
Lightly grease a 13”x9” baking pan. Place salmon in pan and evenly sprinkle with lemon juice. Season salmon with salt and pepper.
In a medium bowl combine the Panko, Parmesan cheese, olive oil, Italian seasoning and garlic powder. Sprinkle mixture evenly over the salmon.
Bake for 15 to 20 minutes, until topping is lightly browned and salmon easily flakes with a fork.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
Of all the recipes I make, this one is my husband's favorite for salmon. It's made with Panko, or Japanese bread crumbs, which are very light and crunchy. It's baked in the oven until the Panko is nice and toasted.
