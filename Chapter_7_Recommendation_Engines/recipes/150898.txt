Title:
Black Label Manhattan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977355.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Johnnie Walker® Black Label® blended Scotch whisky
1/2 ounce sweet vermouth

Directions:
Add Johnnie Walker(R) Black Label(R) blended Scotch whisky and sweet vermouth to a cocktail shaker filled with ice.
Mix; strain and serve in martini glass.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
One of the blends, Johnnie Walker® blended Scotch whisky is still the first and only choice for blending your own premium cocktail.
