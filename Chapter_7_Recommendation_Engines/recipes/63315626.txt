Title:
Spaghetti Salad, the best! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12-16 oz. Vermicelli (can use thin spaghetti)
1/4 cup diced sweet onion,
2 cucumbers, chop/dice
2 small bell peppers, dice
2 jars McCormick Salad Seasoning (dry spice) *for amount used see note*
1 large bottle Italian Salad Dressing *see note
2 cans chopped black olives

Directions:
Break vermicelli/spaghetti in half, boil until cooked al dente'.  (salt the boiling water if desired)  Do not over cook, drain and rinse with cold tap water, drain well.
Place in large bowl; add remaining ingredeints and mix well.  Cover & refrigerate overnight.
*note ->For the McCormick Salad Seasoning amount If you’re using 12 oz. vermicelli use 1 jar of the Salad Seasoning.   If you’re using 16 oz. of vermicelli, use 1 1/2 jars of the Salad Seasoning. (2 jars will be too much) Don’t use the enitire bottle of Italian salad dressing, you can add more as you think it needs it as you stir in the ingredients.  Make at least one day in advance.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
This is a great summer salad to serve a large group or for any outdoor event.  Make at least one day in advance.
