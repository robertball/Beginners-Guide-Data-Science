Title:
Tender Onion Ring Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breasts (each cut into 6-8 pieces)
1 large onion sliced into thin even rings (1/4")
1/2 cup milk
1 cup flour
1 tsp. paprika, garlic powder, and salt,
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/8 cup worcestershire sauce
3/4 cup water
Pinch of salt
Vegetable oil (to pan sear chicken)

Directions:
Clean and cut 4 chicken breasts into 6-8 pieces each.
Mix 5 dry ingredients in a large bowl.
Slice onion into thin even rings (1/4").
Dip chicken into milk then coat with dry ingredients.
Fill 9x13 pan with mixed water, worcestershire sauce, and pinch of salt.
Add one even layer of onion rings in water.
Preheat thin layer of oil in large frypan, add oil as needed.
Brown each side of chicken pieces in 3 batches (do not crowd).
As chicken batches finish, place chicken in a single layer over onions.
Add another layer of onions over chicken.  Continue with another layer of chicken, then finish with a final layer of onions.
Cover tightly with aluminum foil and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes to 1 hour.  You will know it is finished when the onions are tender and wilted.
Serve with your favorite potato or rice dish.
