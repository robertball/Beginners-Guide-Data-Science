Title:
"Fi-yah" Cajun Boiled Peanuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5603204.jpg
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs raw shelled peanuts
1/4 cup kosher salt
2 oz. concentrated liquid Zatarain's shrimp and crab boil
2 oz. canned sliced jalapeno peppers with liquid (approximately)
.75 oz "Ass Kickin" Cajun hot sauce

Directions:
Place raw peanuts in slow cooker.
Add crab boil, salt, jalapenos and hot sauce and mix together.
Add water to fill and cover .
Cover slow cooker and cook on low for at least 8-10 hours.
Drain and serve immediately or let stand to dry and serve in brown bag cones.

Submitter: 
Sahara B
Description: 
Boiled peanuts  or country caviar is certainly a Southern thang, and even amongst us Southerners, you either love them or you hate them. Well, I love them and so should you!
