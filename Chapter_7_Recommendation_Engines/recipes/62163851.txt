Title:
Strawberry & Banana Peanut Butter Protein Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 Cup of Non-Fat Milk
1 Large Banana ( I freeze my Banana with out the peel the night before)
4-5 Good Size Fresh Strawberries (or you can use Frozen)
1 Tsp Cinnamon
2 TBSP Reduced Fat Peanut Butter
2 Scoops of Vanilla Protein Powder (Or flavor of your choice)

Directions:
Put Milk, Frozen Banana, Strawberries, Peanut Butter, and Cinnamon in the Blender or Magic Bullet and Blend till all is smooth. Then place your Protein Powder in there and give it a few pulses' till all mixed together..Garnish with a strawberry or 2 and enjoy
