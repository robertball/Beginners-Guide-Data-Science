Title:
Cider Snap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 qts. apple cider or juice
4 tbsp. red cinnamon candies
16 apple slices
16 cinnamon sticks

Directions:
Combine cider and cinnamon candies in slow cooker.
Cover.  Cook on high 2 hours or until candies dissolve and cider is hot.
Ladle into mugs and service with apple-slice floaters and cinnamon stick stirrers.

Submitter: 
Natasha Sellner
Description: 
This is a cold winter night luxury.  Make it in the morning and keep it on low throughout the day sot hat it's good fragrance can fill the house. 

--Use cran-apple juice instead of apple cider or juice for a slight variation.
