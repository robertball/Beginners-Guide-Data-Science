Title:
Banana Strawberry  Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 chopped frozen banana
1 cup SOO GOOD strawberry beverage
1/2 cup strawberry non fat yogurt
1 tbs honey (optional)
1 tbs wheat germ (optional)

Directions:
blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth.

Submitter:

Description: 
I love this recipe because it has all the great strawberry taste without the annoying seeds.
