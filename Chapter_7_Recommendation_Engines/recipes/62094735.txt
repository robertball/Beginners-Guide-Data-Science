Title:
Apple Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pkgs of refrigerated buttermilk biscuit dough
1 can apple pie filling
cinnamon and sugar
vegetable oil (for frying)

Directions:
1. Spread biscuit dough out flat and spoon one tablespoon of apple pie filling into center. Flatten second biscuit and place on top and seal around edges.
2. Heat oil med/low for frying in skillet. Place biscuit apple combo into hot oil and fry to golden on each side.
3. Cool on paper towel to catch any excess oil then sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar.

Submitter: 
Malina
Description: 
This is a recipe of my mother's, she used to make these when we were children for a snack, breakfast or dessert. They are super easy and pretty quick. They remind me of fried dough with apples in the middle.
