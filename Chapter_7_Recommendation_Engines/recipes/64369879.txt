Title:
Grandma's Corn Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 T butter, dotted on top
2 T sugar
1 cup milk
2 tablespoons flour
1 (14.75 ounce) cans cream-style corn
salt/pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 Degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a 2 quart casserole dish.
In a large bowl, lightly beat eggs. Add sugar, and milk. Whisk in flour. Stir in creamed corn. Blend well.  Pour mixture into prepared casserole dish.
Bake for 1 hour.
