Title:
Slow Cooker Scalloped Potatoes and Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/366936.jpg
Ingredients:
5 lbs red potatoes, peeled and sliced
3/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup butter stick, cut into pats
4 cups cubed ham
1/2 cup water
1 pint whipping cream

Directions:
Layer in this order:
Sliced potatoes on the bottom.
Spread onions on top of potatoes evenly.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper evenly.
Pour whipping cream evenly.
Place butter pats evenly.
Layer with cubed ham.
Pour water over top evenly.
Cover and cook on high for 5 hours or until potatoes are tender.   After about 4 hours, stir and recover for the last hour.
