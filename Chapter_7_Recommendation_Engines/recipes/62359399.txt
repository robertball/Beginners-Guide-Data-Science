Title:
Fudge Truffle Cheesecake with Teddy Graham Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups finely ground chocolate Teddy Grahams
5 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
1/3 cup sugar
1/8 teaspoon salt
2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
4 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
In a large mixing bowl, mix together ground chocolate Teddy Grahams, melted butter, sugar and salt by hand. Press ingredients into a 9 inch springform pan.
In a microwave, melt the chocolate chips.  Stir to make sure they are very smooth.
In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy with an electric mixer. Gradually beat in condensed milk until smooth. Mix in melted chocolate, eggs, and vanilla. Beat with electric mixer on low speed until the ingredients are thoroughly blended. Pour the filling into the prepared crust.
Bake at 300 degrees F (150 degrees C) for 55 minutes.

Description: 
I changed the crust to use fewer ingredients and blend more easily.  I also melt the chocolate chips in the microwave instead of a double boiler, and included some helpful tips.Modified from: Fudge Truffle Cheesecake
