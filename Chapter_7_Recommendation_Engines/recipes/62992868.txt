Title:
Big Bad Biker Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744731.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 Cups Flour
2 Tsp Baking Powder
1/4 Tsp Baking Soda
1/2 Tsp Salt
2 Eggs
1/2 Cup Oil
2/3 Cup White Sugar
4 Ripe Bananas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 320 degrees.
Pummel 4 ripe bananas (aprox 2 cups)
In a mixer add and combine the following ingedients:
eggs, oil, sugar and pummeled bananas
In a separate bowl mix together thoroughly:
flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
Add dry ingedients to wet ingredients, mix thoroughly.
Grease a loaf pan and add mixture.
Cook 50-70 minutes checking loaf every five minutes making sure not to over cook.
Remove from oven and place on a cooling rack right away.
