Title:
Party Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups candy coated pieces
3 cups thin pretzel sticks, broken in half
3 cups bite size cheddar cheese crackers
1 1/2 cups of raisins

Directions:
Put all ingredients into a large bowl.
Mix together and enjoy!
TIP: You can use a 1 cup measuring cup and scoop out 1 cup of party mix and put in zip lock baggies to share!

Submitter: 
chloepuglover11
Description: 
This easy party mix is easy and quick to put together for a party! You can also add in Popcorn and Dried Fruit and nuts!
