Title:
Chilled Vegetable Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 med ripe tomatos
1 large zucchini
1 large yellow squash
1/2 sweet green pepper
1/2 sweet red onion
4 tablespoons Olive Oil
4 tablespoons Red Wine Vinegar
3 tablespoons water
2 cloves garlic -  minced
1 teaspoon basil
Salt & pepper to taste  about 1/2 teaspoon each.

Directions:
Cut tomatoes, zucchini and squash into good bite-sized chunks, dice bell pepper, slice onion,crush garlic, combine all in a large bowl.Sprinkle with basil and salt. Add olive oil, water, and red wine vinegar. Toss to coat. Refrigerate for about 1 hour before serving.

Submitter: 
LREYNAL
Description: 
This is a simple salad using raw Zucchini and Yellow squash that goes wonderfully with a good ole Texas backyard bar-b-que.
