Title:
Lemon Herb Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups chicken stock
3 cups water
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1 cup finely chopped onion
2 Tbsp. chopped fresh thyme
4 garlic cloves, minced
1 1/2 cup Arborio rice
1/2 cup white wine
2 cups haricots verts or asparagus
1 tsp. kosher salt
16 oz medium raw shrimp
4 tsp. grated lemon rind
3 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice
2 Tbsp Lite cream cheese
1/4 Tsp white pepper

Directions:
Combine stock and water in large glass measuring cup; microwave on high for 2 minutes or more
Heat oil in large sauce pan
Add onion, thyme and garlic.  Saute
Add rice; cook 1 minute, stirring constantly
Add wine; cook until liquid evaporates
Add 1 cup of stock; cook 4 minutes until liquid is absorbed.
Add 2 cups stock, about 1/2 cup at a time, stirring often until each portion is absorbed
Stir in 1/4 cup stock, beans, and salt
Stir in shrimp; cook 4 minutes until shrimp is done
Stir in remaining stock, rind and all other ingredients

Delicious; use either green beans or asparagus
