Title:
Quick and Easy Corn Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ears of corn still in their husk
1 small red bell pepper cut in half with seeds removed
2 green onions diced
1 small bunch cilantro chopped
2 serrano chiles with seeds removed finely diced
1 garlic clove minced through a garlic press
Juice from 1 lime
1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin

Directions:
Microwave the corn and the bell pepper halves for 1 minute.  Remove the bell pepper and continue micrwaving the corn for another 2 minutes.  Finely dice the bell pepper and add to a bowl for the salsa.  Remove the husk and silk from the corn and cut the kernals off the cob.  Add them to the bowl along w/ the remaining ingredients.  Stir to combine.

Submitter: 
Ken from CA
Description: 
This is a great alternative to tomato salsa and can be made in about 10 minutes.  It's fantastic on tacos, served over grilled chicken or just eaten with a spoon.
