Title:
Chocolate Mousse Keto (Low Carb) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
227g Philadelphia cream cheese original
2 cups whipping cream
4 tbsp sweatner such as stevia
2 tbsp cocoa powder
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Using an electric beater, whisk the whipping cream until it forms peaks
In a different bowl using an electric beater mix the remainder of the ingredients
Fold in a tbsp of the whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture
Fold in the remainder of the whipping cream until incorporated
Place in individual serving bowls in the fridge
Serve with berries

Most satisfying dessert thus far while on keto.
