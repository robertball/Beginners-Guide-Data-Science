Title:
Corn Meat Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/462071.jpg
Ingredients:
1. 4 Ears of fresh corns
2. 1 Lb of ground pork.
3. 1 Cups of flour.
4. 1/2 Cup of Constarch or Tapioca Starch.
5. 1 Tbsp of Salt.
6. 2 Tbsp of garlic powder
7. 1 Tspn of Black pepper
8. 1 Tbsp of sugar
9. 3 Tbsp fried shollet

Directions:
Mix all ingredients, use 2 spoon to scoop and drop the meat ball in frying pan.  Make sure the oil is hot at medium heat.
