Title:
Slow-Cooker Salsa Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 skinless Chicken breasts
diced bell pepper
diced onion
1 jar Salsa,mild or hot as desired
1 can black beans,drained
1 can Mexi-corn,drained
1/2 package taco seasoning
Choice of instant rice
Sour Cream

Directions:
Spray inside Slow-Cooker with Pam or other vegetable spray.Combine beans,mexi-corn,salsa and taco seasoning.Pour 1/3 salsa mixture into slow- cooker.Place Chicken breasts into cooker and pour remainder of salsa mixture over chicken.Cook on low 6 hrs or high 3-4 hours.Add diced peppers and onions last hour.Serve over brown or yellow(saffron)rice with sour cream.
Cube leftover chicken and heat chicken and leftover salsa mixture in microwave.Wrap in tortilla(whole wheat is healthiest!)and serve with sour cream and low-fat shredded cheese!Enjoy!
