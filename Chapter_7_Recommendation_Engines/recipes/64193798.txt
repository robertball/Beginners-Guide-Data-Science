Title:
Beef Barley Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2650323.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lb Chuck Roast-cut into 2 inch chunks, 2 tbs olive oil, 4-5 carrots diced, 4 celery ribs diced, 1 large onion diced, 1/2 cup uncooked barley, 1/4 cup chopped parsley, 6 cups beef broth, 2 tsp. Salt, 1 tsp dried basil, 1 bay leaf, 2 tsp. ketchup

Directions:
1. Brown beef in olive oil over medium high heat. Place beef in crockpot.
2. Add diced onions and celery to the pan and saut√© until soft before adding to the crock pot.
3. Add carrots, barley, parsley, beef broth, salt, basil, bay leaf, and ketchup.
4. Cook on low for 8 hours or high for 4-6 hours.

Andrea Luhman
Description: 
My favorite hearty soup when cold weather sets in.
