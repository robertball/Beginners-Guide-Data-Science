Title:
Beer Bread II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
4 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
2 tablespoons white sugar
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle beer
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/4 cup canned green chili peppers, chopped
6 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
In a large bowl, stir together flour, sugar, beer, cheese and chilies. Spoon batter into prepared loaf pan; spread batter to evenly fill pan. Pour melted butter on top of loaf.
Bake in preheated oven for 50 to 60 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of loaf comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Zhora Autumn
Description: 
Add Cheddar cheese and chilies to self-rising flour and beer, and you have yourself a zippy bread that goes great with salads, barbecue and chili.Modified from: Beer Bread II
