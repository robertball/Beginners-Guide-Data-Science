Title:
Egg Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 TBsp. milk
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
In a microwave safe bowl, put the 2 eggs and the milk.  Beat with a fork until yolks are broken and slightly mixed.  Place in a microwave and cook at full power for 30 seconds.  Remove from microwave and stir.  Replace and cook for another 30 seconds, Again, remove, stir and cook for another 30 seconds.  Remove from microwave and place on bread or roll.
