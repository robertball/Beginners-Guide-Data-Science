Title:
"Chicken" and Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups diced vegetarian chicken
1/2 cup chopped white onion
2 celery stalks, chopped
1/3 cup peas
1 carrot, chopped
2 cups brown rice
1/4 cup dry white wine
2 1/2 cups vegetable broth

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in crock pot. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours

Submitter: 
Paige McCarver
Description: 
A vegetarian chicken and rice recipe for the crock pot.
