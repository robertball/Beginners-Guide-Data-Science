Title:
Meatballs Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meatballs:
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1 cup soft bread crumbs
1/4 chopped onion
1 tsp celery salt
1/2 tsp marjoram
1/4 tsp garlic salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 1/2-2 lbs ground beef
1/4 cup flour
2 Tbsp vegetable oil
Sauce:
8 oz chopped mushrooms
1 tsp minced garlic
2 Tbsp butter
2 cups beef broth
1 cup sour cream
1 can cream of mushroom soup
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In large bowl, combine meatball ingredients. Shape into 1 1/2 inch balls. Place flour in shallow bowl, dredge meatballs. In large skillet, brown meatballs in oil. Drain on paper towels. Now melt butter and saute mushrooms and garlic. Add flour, stir. Add beef broth, stir to remove lumps. Bring to boil 2-3 minutes until slightly thickened. Stir in sour cream, soup and pepper. Add meatballs and heat until hot. Serve over egg noodles.
