Title:
Chez Walter Famous Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound carrots, peeled and sliced 1/2 inch thick
1 tsp salt
1 - 2 TBSP brown sugar
1 tsp chicken base or bouillon or to taste
Fresh dill or parsley

Directions:
Place carrots in pot, cover with water, add salt. Cook on medium heat until carrots are slightly soft but still crisp, about 10 minutes. Drain half the water. To the water and carrots in the pot, add sugar and bouillon. Bring to boil and boil about 1 minute  until bouillon is dissolved. Place carrots in dish, with some of the broth and sprinkle with dill or parsley.
