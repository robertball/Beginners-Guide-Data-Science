Title:
Guinness® Corned Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds corned beef brisket
1 cup brown sugar
2 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle Irish stout beer (e.g. Guinness®
nutmeg
cloves
allspice
onions
potatoes
carrots
caggage

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C). Rinse the beef completely and pat dry. (can be made in crock pot)
Place the brisket on onions made into a rack in a roasting pan or Dutch oven. Rub the brown sugar and spices on the corned beef to coat entire beef, including the bottom. Pour the bottle of stout beer around, and gently over the beef to wet the sugar.
Cover, and place in preheated oven. Bake for 1 1/2 hours. Place in veggies and bake for 1 hour more. Allow to rest 5 minutes before slicing.

Submitter: 
Zhora Autumn
Description: 
I discovered this tasty St. Patrick's day treat when I attended an Irish Rovers Concert. My family and friend insist that it is a staple at get togethers any time of the year. Roast this Corned Beef slowly at a low setting for a melt in your mouth delight. The aroma is fantastic!Modified from: Guinness® Corned Beef
