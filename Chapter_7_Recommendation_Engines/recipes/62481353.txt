Title:
Teriyaki Salmon II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Sesame oil (or olive)
1/4 cup lemon juice
1/4 cup soy sauce
1 teaspoon ground mustard
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1 Tablespoon sesame seeds
2 large tablespoons brown sugar
4 (6 ounce) salmon steaks

Directions:
In a small saucepan, combine the first six ingredients; cook over low heat until sugar is dissolved. Set aside 1/2 cup for basting and transfer salmon and remaining marinade in a large resealable plastic bag.  Refrigerate for 1-2 hours, turning once. Drain and discard marinade. Place the salmon on a broiler pan. Broil 3-4 in. from the heat for 5 minutes. Brush with reserved marinade; turn and broil for 5 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork. Brush with marinade.
