Title:
Grecian Cherries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3799645.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cherries, pitted and coarsely chopped
1 cup whole-milk ricotta cheese
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
4 teaspoons ground coriander
4 teaspoons white sugar
1 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions:
Combine chopped cherries with accumulated juices, ricotta cheese, feta cheese, coriander, sugar, and lemon juice in a large bowl. Chill before serving.

Submitter: 
msngutah
Description: 
Savory ricotta and feta cheese combined with sweet, fresh cherries and a dash of coriander - delish! This goes exceptionally well with a whole wheat flatbread or pita.
