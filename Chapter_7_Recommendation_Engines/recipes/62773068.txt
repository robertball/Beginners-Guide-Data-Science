Title:
Rhubarb Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups diced rhubarb
3/4 cup diced red apple
1/2 cup dried cranberries
1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
1/4 cup water
1/4 cup honey
1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
2 teaspoons red wine vinegar
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan.  Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer until until rhubarb is tender, 15 to 20 minutes.  Uncover and simmer, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 5 minutes more. Serve warm or cold.

Submitter: 
Becky Cleveland
Description: 
An interesting blend of sweet and tart.  It is great served with chicken or pork.  It is also good on a turkey or chicken sandwich with cream cheese.
