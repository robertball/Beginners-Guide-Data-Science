Title:
Mediterranean Chickpea Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup evoo
1 large purple onion
2 cloves garlic
1 small bunch Italian parsley
1 small carrot
1 small eggplant
2 cups cooked, sodium free, chickpeas drained
1 cup cherry tomatoes, sliced in half
3/4 cup vegetable broth, divided
1 tbsp oregano
1 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper flakes
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a Dutch oven, add the olive oil and warm it over medium heat. Slice the onions, add to the warm oil. Mince the garlic and parsley then add them to the pan. Stir to sauté. Shred the carrot on the large teeth of a grater and add to the pan. Stir often. Cut the eggplant into one inch cubes. Add and sauté.
Add the chickpeas and tomatoes. Add 1/2 cup broth, oregano, thyme, and cayenne pepper flakes. Mix and bring to a boil. Turn down the heat, simmer until thickened and the vegetables are soft. Add more broth if necessary. Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Chickpeas, eggplant, zucchini, onions, tomatoes, and lots of olive oil stew together to create a traditional Mediterranean meal for your family. Serve with couscous, rice, pasta or pita bread.
