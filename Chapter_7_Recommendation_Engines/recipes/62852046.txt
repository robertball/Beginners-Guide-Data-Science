Title:
Holiday Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cubed, cooked chicken meat
1 cup spicy sweet mayo (from kickin turkey burger recipe from this site)
1 teaspoon paprika
1 cup dried cranberries
1/2 cup crushed pineapple
1 cup chopped celery
2 green onions, chopped
1/2 cup minced green bell pepper
1 cup chopped pecans
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix together mayonnaise with paprika and seasoned salt.  Blend in dried cranberries, celery, bell pepper, onion, and nuts.  Add chopped chicken, and mix well.  Season with black pepper to taste.  Chill 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
Dried cranberries add a sweet tartness to chicken and vegetable salad.  Make a delightful sandwich filling, too.Modified from: Holiday Chicken Salad
