Title:
Kim's Shredded Turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•	13-14 pound turkey
•	½ c water
•	1 can of beef broth
•	½ tsp garlic salt
•	½ jar of McCormicks broiled steak seasoning salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large baking bag in 9 x 13 pan or roaster.
Bake at 350 for about 4-5 hours till falling apart.
Reserve all the liquid. Shred turkey and remove all bones and fat.
Put back turkey into freezer bags flattened and distribute the reserved liquid. Can keep in freezer for months. Takes about 10 minutes to thaw in microwave.

Submitter: 
THELBERG
Description: 
Awesome shredded turkey for sandwiches - perfect for a graduation party.  You can use turkey breasts instead of a whole turkey if you like.
