Title:
Chris's Honey Glazed Ham Steaks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ham steaks
1 large can of pineapple rings, sliced
honey

Directions:
Heat grill to high.
Cook steaks for 3 minutes on one side.
Turn heat to low.
Cover with pineapple slices.
Fill pineapple slices with honey.
Turn off heat and let stand on the grill for 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sandy Schoon
Description: 
My hubby is a grill master!  Here is one of the easiest and quickest of his arsenal.
