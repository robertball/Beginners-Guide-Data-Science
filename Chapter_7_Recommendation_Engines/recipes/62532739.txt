Title:
Hawaiian Haystack Roll ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked rice
2 cups cooked diced chicken
1 can pineapple tidbits
1 can cream of chicken soup
6 flour tortillas
optional ingredients:
chow mein noodles
slivered almonds
grated cheese
coconut
chopped celery
diced ham

Directions:
combine can of soup with juice of pineapple
simmer until heated
layer in large tortilla, cover with sauce and roll
or serve as a rice bowl, layer and cover with sauce
