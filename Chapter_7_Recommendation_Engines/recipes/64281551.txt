Title:
Cinnamon Roll Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf thin sliced white sandwich bread
Milk
1 package cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup melted butter
3/4 cup sugar mixed with 1 tea. cinnamon
sour cream

Directions:
Remoce crusts from bread. Roll each slice flat with a rolling pin. Blend just enough milk with softened cream cheese to make it spreadable. Spread on bread, and roll up. Then put the rolls in melted butter, then in sugar mix. Cut each roll into 2 pieces. Bake on a cookie sheet at 400 degrees for 12-15 min. serve warm w/ sour cream for dipping.
