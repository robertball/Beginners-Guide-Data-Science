Title:
Incredibly Cheesy Turkey Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pounds ground turkey
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
1 egg
1/4 to 1/2 onion, minced
3 cloves garlic, minced
couple dashes Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
6 ounces Colby cheese, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1/4 cup ketchup
2 Tbsp brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
Soak breadcrumbs in milk while mixing other ingredients.
Mix turkey, egg, onion, garlic, worsterschire, salt and pepper.
Once mixed add breadcrumb mixture and mix in. Fold in cheese cubes.
Mix ketchup and brown sugar and pour over top of meatloaf.
Bake for 40 to 45 minutes.
