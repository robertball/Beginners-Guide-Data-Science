Title:
Spicy Lime Grilled Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large clove garlic
1 teaspoon coarse salt
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon OLD BAY Brand seasoning
1/3 cup olive oil
Juice of 3 limes
1/3 cup of dry white wine
2 pounds large shrimp, peeled and deveined
8 wedges lemon, for garnish

Directions:
Preheat grill for medium heat.
In a small bowl, crush the garlic with the salt. Mix in cayenne pepper, OLD BAY Brand seasoning, and paprika, and then stir in olive oil, lime juice, and wine to form a marinade. In a large bowl, pour the marinade on the shrimp until evenly coated and refrigerate for 2 hours.
Lightly oil grill grate. Cook shrimp for 2 to 3 minutes per side, or until opaque. Baste frequently with leftover marinade. Transfer to a serving dish, garnish with lemon wedges, and serve.
