Title:
German Plum Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
1/2 c. sugar
2 eggs
3 c. flour
2 tsp. baking powder
dash salt
3/4 c. milk
5 lbs. Italien prune plums, cut in 1/2, no pit.
Topping:
2 c. flour
1 c. sugar
dash cinnamon
1 pkg. vanilla sugar
1 stick butter, melted

Directions:
In large bowl cream butter, sugar, and eggs.  Add flour, baking powder, salt, and milk.  With wet hands press the dough in the bottom of a large baking pan (15x11" or broiler pan works well).  You can also use 2 9x12" pans.  Press plums standing up in rows in the dough.  In another bowl mix topping ingredients until smooth.  Sprinkle over plums in pan.  Bake at 325 degrees 80 min.

Submitter: 
MISS ALIX
Description: 
My mom makes this Pflaumenkuchen (Plum Cake) every autumn when the Italian prune plums are in season.  You can substitute other fruits like cherries or apples.  It's delicious served with fresh whipped cream!
