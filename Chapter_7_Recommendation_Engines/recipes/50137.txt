Title:
French Vanilla Kiss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984613.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup hot brewed coffee or prepared instant coffee
1/2 cup French Vanilla NESTLE® CARNATION® COFFEE-MATE® Liquid Coffee Creamer
1 tablespoon NESTLE® TOLL HOUSE® Baking Cocoa
Whipped cream (optional)

Directions:
COMBINE coffee, Coffee-mate and cocoa in pitcher; stir. Pour into 2 large mugs. Top with whipped cream. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Nestle® Carnation® Coffee-mate®
Description: 
Rich French vanilla creamer is blended into a chocolate flavored coffee. Top with whipped cream for the ultimate indulgence.
