Title:
Gyros Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package ground turkey
1/2 onion, grated
2 cloves garlic, pressed
1 slice bread, toasted and crumbled
3 Tablespoons Greek Seasoning
1 Red Pepper
1/2 Onion

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in food processor, and mix thoroughly. Saute in olive oil on medium heat, until thoroughly cooked.
