Title:
Big Ray's Jalapeno Popper Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1119585.jpg
Ingredients:
2 packages Neufchâtel cheese softened, (8 ounces each)
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 cup canned chopped green chilies
1/2 cup diced jalapeno peppers
1 cup  Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup  bread crumbs

Directions:
In a large bowl, beat the first five ingredients until blended; spread into an ungreased 2 quart casserole dish. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese; top with bread crumbs. Bake at 400° for 25-30 minutes or until lightly browned. Serve with veggies, crackers and chips.
