Title:
Strawberry-Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 ice cubes
6 large frozen strawberries
1 banana
1 cup non-fat vanilla yogurt

Directions:
Place ingredients in a blender in the order listed.  Blend until smooth. Pour into a large glass & enjoy!

Submitter: 
Dulce
Description: 
Great for breakfast.  We make them for dessert a lot too!
