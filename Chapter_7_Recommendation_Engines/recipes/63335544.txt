Title:
Turnip Greens and Tofu Lasagne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T olive oil
3 cloves garlic
1 pound turnip greens
2 jars Spaggetti sauce
16 oz firm tofu
3 eggs
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
15 oz Riccotta cheese
black pepper to taste
1/2 bunch of fresh Italian parsley
2 pounds shredded mozzerrella cheese
1 box/20 whole wheat lasagne noodles

Directions:
Use food processor to chop the turnip greens really small. In wok brown chopped garlic in olive oil, add greens and saute till wilted and add 1 jar of spaggetti sauce cook about 10 minutes then turn off heat while preparing rest. In large bowl break up the tofu and mix in the ricotta, eggs, parmesan, black pepper and chopped parsley. Cook lasagne noodles about 5 minutes in boiling salted water if desired.
Assemble lasagne. Spray extra large pan with cooking spray. Put two cups of the greens sauce in bottom of pan. Cover with 4 noodles lengthwise. Spread thinly with cheese tofu mixture, sprinkle mozzerella cheese on top of that, pour plain sauce from jar on top thinly and then put another layer of noodles, spread with cheese mixture then sprinkle with mozzerella and then turnip green sauce, put 4 noodles on top same as first layer with plain sauce then repeat another layer with turnip green sauce same as second layer. put noodles spread with sauce and then cover with aluminum foil and bake at 350 for 60 minutes. remove aluminum foil add more mozzerrella cheese. Cook for another 15 minutes or so till cheese melts.
