Title:
Vegan Tofu Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp butter (or smart balance)
1 cup of cubed sweet potatoes
3 cups of vegetable broth
1 cup of dried tofu (break up if cubed)
1 tbsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp dried basil
1/4 tsp thyme
1/4 tsp rosemary

Directions:
1. Put butter and sweet potatoes in a pan over medium heat for 10 minutes stirring occasionally.
2. Raise the heat to high and pour in the chicken broth. Then add the tofu. Wait for it to come to a boil. Once it starts boiling, immediately lower to a medium-low heat.
3. Add the garlic powder, basil, thyme and rosemary.
4.  Cover pot and lower to a low heat. Let simmer for 25 minutes.
5. Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Val N.
Description:
