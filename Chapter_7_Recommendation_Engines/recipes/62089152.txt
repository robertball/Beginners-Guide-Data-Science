Title:
The Great Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 Cups All-Purpose Flour
1 Cup Brown Sugar
2 Tsp Baking Soda
1 Tsp Baking Powder
3/4 Tsp Salt
1  Tsp Cinnamon
1/2 Tsp Ground Cloves
1 Cup Egg
1 16 oz Can Whole Cranberry Sauce
1 15 oz Can Pumpkin
1/3 Cup OIl (or applesauce for lower fat)
1 Tbsp Orange Zest
2 Tbsp Walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease two 8.5 x 4.5 inch loaf pans.
Combine sugar, soda, powder, salt, and spices in large bowl.
In small seperate bowl, mix egg, cranberry, pumpkin, oil, and orange.
Add to flour mixture.
Mix just until moistened
Pour into prepared pans.
Sprinkle with walnuts.
Bake at 350 degrees for 55-60 minutes.
Let cool 10  minutes in pan before removing.

Submitter: 
B. Painter
Description: 
Worth waiting in the patch for this bread!  With  cranberries, orange, walnuts, cloves, and cinnamon, this bread is good enough to make even Charlie Brown happy!
