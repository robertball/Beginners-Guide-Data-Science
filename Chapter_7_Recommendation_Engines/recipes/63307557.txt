Title:
Linguine with Clam Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/897629.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, finely chopped
2 TBSP butter
2 TBSP olive oil
2 (6.5 oz) cans chopped clams, drained (reserve 2/3 cup liquid)
2 TBSP lemon juice
1/2 tsp cracked black pepper, fresh
1 bay leaf
1 TBSP chopped fresh parsley
1/2 lb. linguine, cooked
1 TBSP fresh grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
In medium skillet, cook onion and garlic in butter until golden.
Add reserved clam liquid, lemon juice, pepper, and bay leaf.  Bring to boil; simmer uncovered 5 minutes.
Stir in clams and parsley; heat through.  Remove bay leaf; serve with hot linguine, cheese, and additional pepper.
