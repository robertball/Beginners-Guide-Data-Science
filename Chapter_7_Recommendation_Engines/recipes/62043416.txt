Title:
Lime Cream Cheese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (6 0z) pkgs lime jello
12 oz of cream cheese room temp
2/3 cup mayonnaise
6 cups water
1 (20 oz) can crushed pineapple in its juice
1 1/2 cups chopped pecans

Directions:
Beat cream cheese in mixer until fluffy. Add mayo and mix. Dissolve jello in 4 cups hot water. Add to mayo and cream cheese mixture, stirring to dissolve. Drain pineapple and add enough water to the juice to make 2 cups. Stir the 2 cups of liquid into the cream cheese mixture.  Pour into large pyrex dish. Refrigerate until partially congealed. Stir in pineapple and nuts. Refrigerate to completely set.
