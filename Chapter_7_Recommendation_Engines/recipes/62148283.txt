Title:
Whole Wheat Bananna Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Large mashed banannas
1/2 Cup Honey
2 Cups Whole Wheat Flour
1 Teaspoon baking soda
2 eggs
1/2 Cup softened butter
1/4 Teaspoon salt
1 Teaspoon cinnamon
1/4-1/2 Teaspoon nutmeg
1/4 Teaspoon cloves
1/2 Teaspoon vanilla
1/2 Teaspoon maple flavoring (optional)

Directions:
Mix butter, honey, eggs and banannas in a large bowl.
Add salt, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves to flour in a seperate bowl, and mix well.
Add flour mixture to the bananna mixture, and mix well.
Add vanilla and maple flavoring to the rest and mix in completely.
Pour into 1 regular or 2 small greased loaf pans.
Bake for 1 hour at 325 degrees.
Cool and enjoy!
