Title:
White Cake Frosting II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/957378.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 tablespoons warm water
1 (16 ounce) package confectioners' sugar

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine shortening, salt, vanilla, water and confectioners' sugar. Beat until light and fluffy.

White frosting that can be used for all your cake decorating needs.
