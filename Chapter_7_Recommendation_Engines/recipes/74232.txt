Title:
Brocco Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984426.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Mann's Broccoli Cole Slaw
3/4 cup Mann's Broccoli Wokly®, blanched and chopped
5 ounces tortilla chips
1/2 cup refried beans
1/2 cup red bell pepper, julienned
1/4 cup corn kernels
1 cup pasteurized processed cheese spread, (may substitute bottled cheese)
2 tablespoons sour cream
2 tablespoons guacamole
2 tablespoons cilantro, minced
1/4 cup salsa

Directions:
Spread out tortilla chips on 12-inch ovenproof plate or pan. Portion dabs of refried beans over chips. Sprinkle evenly with Mann's Broccoli Cole Slaw, Mann's Broccoli Wokly(R), bell pepper and corn.
Drizzle melted cheese over. Bake at 425 degrees F for 10 minutes or until heated through and cheese is bubbly. Dollop with sour cream, salsa and guacamole as desired. Sprinkle with cilantro.

Submitter: 
Mann's Fresh Vegetables
Description: 
Liven up your standard nachos with additional layers of Mann's Broccoli Wokly® and Mann's Broccoli Cole Slaw for an uncommonly delicious take on the original.
