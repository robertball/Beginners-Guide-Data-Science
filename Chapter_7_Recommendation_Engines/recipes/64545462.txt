Title:
slow cooked braised short ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6042674.jpg
Ingredients:
6 short ribs(5 to 7 lbs)
2 sprigs of rosemary
6 sprigs of thyme
1 bay leaf
1 celery stalk
3 tsp kosher salt
2 tsp coarsely ground pepper
3 tbs veg. oil
3 med carrots cut into 1 in pieces
1 med onion roughly chopped
4 shallots peeled and sliced 1/4 inch thick
5 garlic cloves, peeled and halved
3 tbs all purpose flour
3 tbs tomato paste
3 cups beef broth
1 cup full bodied red wine
1tsp cornstarch

Directions:
Generously salt and pepper the short ribs in a large skillet over a high heat . Add 2 tbs canola oil and brown the ribs on all sides. In bottom of a slow cooker, whisk together flour and tomato paste until there are no lumps.  Add broth and wine and continue whisking until smooth.  Place all vegetables  and herbs in slow cooker, top with short ribs.  The ribs should be covered by an inch of liquid.  If you need to add more broth or wine feel free to do so.
Cook on low for 8 hrs.
Once the ribs very tender remove them and set aside. Pour liquids and vegetables through a fine mesh sieve and into medium saucepan. Using the back of spoon, press against the solids to release all of the juices into saucepan and discard remaining solids.  Skim as much fat from the top as possible.
Remove 1 cup of liquid, place in separate bowl, and whisk in cornstarch. After cornstarch is added return to pan along with short ribs and warm on med heat until heated through.
