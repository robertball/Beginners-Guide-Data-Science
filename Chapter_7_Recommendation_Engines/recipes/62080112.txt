Title:
Mexican Vegetable Meledy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pack of carrots(peeled and diced in 11/2 inch chunks)
3 cucambers(peeled and sliced in thin slices)
2 Zuccinies(peeled and sliced in thin slices)
1 small onion(cleaned and sliced)
1tbs. knorr suiza chicken bouliion
salt and peper to taste
2-3 stems cilantro chopped

Directions:
1.)sautte carrotts till soft, add in all other ingredients. Cook till all soft and cooked throught. Stirring occasionally so not to stick and allow all vegetables to cook throughly.
Serve with any dish or by itself.

Description: 
Serve this instead of rice or beans with your favorite mexican meat recipe. In my house this is eaten up faster then the meat dishes.
