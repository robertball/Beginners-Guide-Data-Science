Title:
Meat Loaf (Grandma Bland's) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
3/4 cup milk
2/3 cup finely cruched saltines
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 teaspoon salt
Dash of pepper
1 1/2 lb. ground turkey
1 cup ketchup
1/2 cup brown sugar

Directions:
In large bowl, beat eggs. Add milk, saltines, onion, salt, sage, and pepper. Add turkey, mix well. Shape into loaf. Put into ungreased baking dish or pan. Combine reamining ingredients. Spread 3/4 cup over the meat loaf. Bake at 350 degrees for 60 65 minutes. DO not slice for about 10 minutes. Serve with remaining sauce.
