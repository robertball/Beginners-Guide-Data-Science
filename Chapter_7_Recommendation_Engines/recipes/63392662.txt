Title:
Gingy's Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
4 oz unsweetened chocolate
1 1/4 cup sugar
4 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
2/3 cup flour
1/4 tsp salt
1 cup walnuts

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar
Add eggs, salt, vanilla and melted chocolate. Beat til fluffy
Add flour and nuts
Bake in buttered 9" pan at 325 for 25 min or until pick inserted at the corner comes out clean. Will be very moist.
