Title:
Sheet pan shrimp fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound jumbo shrimp no shell, no tail, deveined
1 tsp lemon pepper seasoning
2 onions cut into strips
2 bell pepper cut into strips
2 TBS oil
1 1oz fajita seasoning packet
Fajita flour tortillas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In bowl mix together the shrimp and lemon pepper.
In a large bowl hand toss the pepper and onion strips with olive oil and fajita mix until throughly coated.
Spray a large sheet pan with cooking oil and arrange shrimp on one side and vegetables on the other.  Bake for 30 minutes or until shrimp are pink and opaque.
Stack tortillas on a plate and cover with a wet paper towel. Heat in the microwave for 45 seconds.
