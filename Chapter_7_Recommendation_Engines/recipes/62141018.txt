Title:
Pumpkin French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
4 Tablespoons canned pumpkin puree
1 tsp cinnamon
pinch of nutmeg
8 slices of whole wheat bread
1 Tsp butter for frying pan
maple syrup
icing (powdered) sugar

Directions:
Beat eggs, pumpkin puree, cinnamon and nutmeg in a medium sized bowl.
Dip bread in egg mixture and cover both sides well.
Fry bread slices in a lightly buttered pan over medium high heat.
Place french toast on plates and drizzle maple syrup over top.
Sprinkle small amount of powdered sugar for fun!

Submitter: 
CARI
Description: 
On a quest to get my kids to eat more veggies and not just at dinner time, my pumpkin french toast has now become a family favourite!
