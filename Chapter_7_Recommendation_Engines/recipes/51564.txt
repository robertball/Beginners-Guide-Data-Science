Title:
Stouffer's® Chicken Alfredo Over Baked Potatoes Recipe

Ingredients:
1 (25 ounce) package STOUFFER'S® Skillet Sensations Chicken Alfredo, prepared according to package directions

Directions:
POUR chicken alfredo over baked potatoes.

For a hearty meal that's quick and easy top two steaming hot baked potatoes with our Chicken Alfredo. Add a prepackaged salad and your favorite dressing, and dinner's ready in a flash.
