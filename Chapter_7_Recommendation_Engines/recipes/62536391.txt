Title:
Easy Sausage Gravy and Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) can refrigerated jumbo buttermilk biscuits
1 (9.6 ounce) package Jimmy DeanÂ® Original Hearty Pork Sausage Crumbles
3 heaping table spoons flour
4 cups milk
Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Bake biscuits according to package directions.
Meanwhile, cook sausage in large skillet over medium heat 5-6 minutes or until thoroughly heated, stirring frequently. Drain all grease except two table spoons. Stir in flour. Gradually add milk; cook until mixture comes to a boil and thickens, stirring constantly. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Split biscuits in half.  Place 2 halves on each of 8 plates; top with about 1/3 cup gravy.
