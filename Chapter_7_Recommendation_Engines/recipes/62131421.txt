Title:
aebleskiver Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup packaged biscuit mix
1/4 cup milk
2 egg yolks
2 tablespoons sugar
1/8 teaspoon ground cardamom
2 stiffly beaten egg whites
butter or margerine
powered sugar

Directions:
in mixer bowl combine first 5 ingredients and beat at low speed with electric mixer till mixed.
fold in egg whites.
Grease cups in aebleskiver pan with butter.
Heat pan over medium heat.
Fill cups 2/3 full of batter.
Cook till bubbly, 2 to 3 minutes.
Gently turn with 2 wooden picks.
Cook till done, about 2 more minutes.
Sprinkle with powered sugar.
Makes 12 to 14 aebleskiver.
