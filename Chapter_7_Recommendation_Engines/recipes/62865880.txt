Title:
Crab and Shrimp Melt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices bread
1 pound of shrimp
2 cans of lump crab meat, drained
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sweet pickle relish
1/4 cup ketchup
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
12 slices of muenster cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
In a saucepan, heat 1 teaspoon of oil.  Add shrimp and cook until opaque.  Drain any juices.  Add two cans of crab meat.  Heat thoroughly.
In a separate bowl, mix together mayonnaise, pickle relish, ketchup, salt and pepper.
Toss shrimp and crab meat with a portion of the sauce just until it is wet.
Place bread slices on a baking sheet.  Spoon shrimp and crab mixture on to the bread slice.   Top each with 2 Tablespoons of remaining sauce mixture.  Top mixture with 2 slices of cheese to create an open face sandwich.
Bake for about 7 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.  Serve immediately.
