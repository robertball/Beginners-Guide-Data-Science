Title:
Connie's Brocolli & Cheese and Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups water
2 cups uncooked white or brown rice
4 boneless and skinless chicken breasts cut into bite size pieces
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1/4 cup butter
1 cup milk
1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli
1 small white onion, chopped
3 cups or mild or sharp shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium saucepan, bring the water and 2 tbsp of butter and rice to a boil. Cover, and remove from heat. Let stand 10 minutes.
In a skillet put onion and diced chicken with spray cooking oil. Cover and cook until juices run clear.
In a 9x13 inch baking dish, mix the prepared rice, chicken, cream of mushroom soup, cream of chicken soup, butter, milk, broccoli, onion, and shredded cheese.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes. Stir halfway through cooking to help cheese melt evenly.

Submitter: 
Connie Clutter Laurence
Description: 
A meal-in-one casserole with chicken, rice, broccoli, cheese, onion, and creamy soups all baked into one dish. May be made ahead and frozen.Modified from: Broccoli, Rice, Cheese, and Chicken Casserole
