Title:
Easy Crustless Zucchini Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups zucchini, shredded
4 eggs, well beaten
1/8 cup fresh parsley, chopped
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup oil
1 small onion, minced
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
1 tablespoon grated parmesan cheese
1 cup bisquik mix
2 tablespoons plain bread crumbs (to coat pie plate)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray a 9" pie plate with cooking spray and coat with breadcrumbs. Mix all remaining ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Pour into prepared pie plate. Bake for 45-60 minutes until set and top is golden brown. Remove from oven and allow to cook for 10 minutes. Serve and enjoy.
Variations:
1. You can substitue any shredded cheese to your liking.
2. For a hearter quiche, substitue one cup of the zucchini for 1 cup of shredded potato (squeeze out excess water)
