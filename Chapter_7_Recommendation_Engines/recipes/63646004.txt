Title:
Healthier Carrot Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup coconut oil (or butter)
1 cup honey
2 eggs
2 cups grated carrots
1 cup all purpose flour (or 1/2 whole wheat 1/2 white flour)
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
3 cups quick oats
1 cup raisins or other dried fruit
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375ยบ F.
2. Cream the butter and the honey. Then add the eggs and the carrots and mix well.
3. In a large bowl sift together the flours, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix.
5. Add the oats and the raisins, mixing until well distributed.
6. Drop batter by large spoonful onto a parchment lined cookie sheet and flatten/shape with a fork if desired.
7. Bake at 375ยบF for 15 minutes or until tops are a golden color.

Submitter: 
CAMILLEELISE
Description: 
This makes a relatively healthy, cakey cookie that is a bit like a mix between carrot cake and oatmeal cookies.  I adjusted a few different recipes to use less sugar and more oats and carrots so that I don't feel too bad eating these for breakfast (which I can't resist)!
