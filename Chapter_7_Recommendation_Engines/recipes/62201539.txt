Title:
Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cake mix (yellow, butter brickle or butter pecan)
1 20 oz can crush pineapple
1 21 oz can cherry pie filling
1 cup chopped pecans
1 stick butter (cut into thin slices)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Grease a 13x9x2 pan
Dump undrained pineapple in pan
Dump cherry pie filling in pan and spread evenly
Dump (dry) cake mix into cherry layer
Sprinkle nuts and top with butter slices
Bake for 48-53 minutes

Submitter: 
stitchers
Description: 
A quick n easy cake - great for pot lucks.
