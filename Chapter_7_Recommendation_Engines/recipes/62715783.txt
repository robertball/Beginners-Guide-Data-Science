Title:
Ed's Favorite Beer Can Chicken Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Parts:
Brown Sugar
Dry Mustard
Granualted Onion
Paprika
Kosher Salt
1 Part:
Garlic Powder
Ground Corriander
Ground Cumin
Black Pepper
Cayenne Pepper

Directions:
Mix the above ingriendents according to the 2:1 Ratio indicated above.   To cover a typical 5 lb  Chicken I normally measured the spices in Tablespoons.   However, feel free to mix a larger batch to keep some finished product on hand.

Submitter: 
rharejr
Description: 
This recipe is a tribute to my Uncle who is by far one the best Pit masters I have ever known.   Feel free to put this on chicken, pork, or just about anything else you can think of.  One thing's for sure you will be the hit of the party!
