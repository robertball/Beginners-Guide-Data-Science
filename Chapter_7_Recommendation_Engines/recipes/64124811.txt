Title:
Arrion's Mexican Corn Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cans  Mexicali Corn -- drained
1 small bunch  cilantro (leaves only) -- chopped
1 bunch  green onions -- sliced
8 ounces  sharp cheddar cheese -- grated
1 cup  Hellman's mayonnaise
16 ounces sour cream
cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon  ground cumin

Directions:
In medium mixing bowl, combine corn, cilantro, green onions and cheese.
Add mayonnaise, sour cream, cayenne pepper and cumin and thoroughly
combine.
Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
This dip was a hit at the family reunion.
