Title:
Spicy Garlic Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter or margarine
10-12 cloves garlic peeled and bruised
2 lbs jumbo shrimp
2 TBSP hot sauce (I use Giovanni's Hot and Spicy Sauce from North Shore Hawaii)

Directions:
Melt butter or margarine in a sauce pan and saute garlic until fragrant and tender. Put shrimp and butter/garlic mixture in the bottom portion of a broiler pan and broil for 8-10 minutes...until shrimp are cooked. Toss with hot sauce...serve with white rice...
