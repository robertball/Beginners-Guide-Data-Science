Title:
Chicken with feta cheese salsa topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb chicken breastas
2 plum/roma tomatoes
1/2 cup greek or italian salad dressing
1 full scallion
2 TSP feta cheese

Directions:
Marinate the chicken breasts for 4 to 24 hours in greek or italian salad dressing.  Chop scallion and tomato, add feta cheese to mixture.
Pound the breasts out to about 1/2 inch in thickness, coat a shallow baking dish in remaining dressing.  Preheat over to 350 f
Place chicken breasts in baking dish.  Bake at 350 for 20 mins placing feta scallion topping on for the last 10 mins bringing total baking time to 30 mins.

Submitter: 
JennCD
Description: 
My son is 11 years old and we started cooking together when he was 8.  It turns out he has a real knack for making things up himself and this one is a surefire favorite.  We serve them with a side of egg noddles and steamed vegetables such as broccoli
