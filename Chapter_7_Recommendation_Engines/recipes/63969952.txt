Title:
Poppy Seed Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups sugar
1 1/3 cups oil
3 eggs
3 1/2 cups flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 tablespoon water
1 1/2 teaspoons butter flavoring
2 tablespoons poppy seeds
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1 1/2 cups milk
1 1/2 teaspoons almond flavoring
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
GLAZE:
1/4 cup frozen orange juice concentrate
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 teaspoon almond flavoring
1/2 teaspoon butter flavoring

Directions:
Mix sugar and oil.  Beat in the rest of the ingredients.  Pour mixture in a loaf pan or bundt pan that is greased and floured.  Bake at 350 degrees for about 60 minutes.
Remove bread from pan and poke holes in top of bread with a fork and pour on glaze.
For Glaze:
Mix all ingredients and pour on bread while bread is hot.
