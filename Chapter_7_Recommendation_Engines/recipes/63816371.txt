Title:
Big Ray's Iced Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1058638.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups double brewed coffee
1/2 cup sugar
7 oz sweetened condensed milk (half a can)
1 package instant vanilla pudding mix
2 cups milk

Directions:
Brew 6 cups of coffee with the amount of coffee(double brew) or use 12 Tbs instant coffee with 6 cups water.
In a small bowl, combine sugar and dry pudding mix. Stir in milk and coffee.

Submitter:

Description: 
If you enjoy flavored coffee but hate paying out the nose for it, give this a try.  Drink it hot or chilled.
