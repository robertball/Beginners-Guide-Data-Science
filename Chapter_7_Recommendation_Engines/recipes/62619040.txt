Title:
Sour Cream Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 eggs, well beaten
1 cup very ripe mashed bananas
1/2 cup sour cream
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups flour
about a 1/2 cup of fresh or frozen blueberries (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Beat shortening and sugar until smooth and creamy.  Add salt, eggs, bananas, sour cream, and baking soda; stir in flour.  Turn batter into greased loaf pan; add blueberries if desired.  Bake at 350 for 45 minutes to 1 hour.

Submitter: 
jodee
Description: 
By far the best banana bread I have ever had! The sour cream keeps the bread so moist. I always add a handful of frozen blueberries to mine!
