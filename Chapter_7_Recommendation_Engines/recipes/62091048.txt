Title:
Yummy Chicken Salad Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups leftover cooked chicken, chopped
1/2 cup Miracle Whip Light dressing
1 medium stalk celery, diced
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1 clove garlic, minced (optional)
1 tbsp. green onions, minced
2 tsp. Dijon mustard

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together.
Spread on your favorite bread.
Enjoy!
* I like to spread it on my home made baked bread, or pumpernickle.
* This filling is even better if left to sit in the fridge for 1/2 hour!

Submitter: 
Miss Ashley
Description: 
This is an excellent way to use up some leftover chicken!  Everyone will beg for another one of these delicious sandwiches!
