Title:
Special Cheese Manicotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-8 oz	Package Manicotti shells
5 Cups	Shredded mozzarella cheese
1 Cup	Grated parmesan cheese
3 TBS 	Fresh parsley, minced
½ tsp	Salt
½ tsp 	Pepper
¼ tsp 	Nutmeg
2	Eggs, lightly beaten
1½ Cups Sour Cream
2/3 Cup	Italian dressing
1 Cup 	Zucchini, shredded
1	Large Jar Spaghetti Sauce

Directions:
Grease 9x13” pan.  Cook manicotti, rinse.  Combine 4 cups mozzarella, parmesan, parsley, salt, pepper and nutmeg.  Stir in eggs, sour cream, Italian dressing and zucchini.  Stuff manicotti.  Pour sauce on top.  Bake 35 minutes to 1 hour at 350.  Sprinkle with remaining mozzarella and bake 5 minutes longer.
