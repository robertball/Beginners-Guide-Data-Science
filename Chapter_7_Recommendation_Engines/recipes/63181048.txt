Title:
Asparagus Chicken LGLS-173 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound chicken bnls,sknls-2 inch strips
3 cups Asparagus spears-1 inch pieces
1 white or yellow onion chopped
2 cloves garlic minced
1 tsp evoo
1 cup sliced mushrooms
juice and zest of 1 lemon
1/2 c water
1/2 tsp sea salt
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
3 tbs Italian flat-leaf parsley
TGS Sauce 77
10 cloves garlic peeled
10 shallots, peeled
Olive oil, EVOO

Directions:
1. Saute onion, garlic in oil for 3 minutes
2. Add asparagus, mushrooms and saute 3 more minutes
3. Remove veggies from pan
4. Add chicken, lightly brown for 3 minutes
5. Return veggies and add lemon juice and zest, salt and pepper
6.  Add water, stir simmer 10 minutes cooking chicken.
Sprinkle with parsley and add Terrific Garlic-Shallot Sauce
Recipe TGS Sauce:
10 cloves garlic peeled
10 shallots, peeled
Olive oil, EVOO
Blend ingredients into fine chop, transfer and cover with oil keeps for 7 days refrigerated.
