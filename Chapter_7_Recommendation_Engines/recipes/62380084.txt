Title:
Lemon Pudding-Microwave Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C. Sugar
3 T flour
3 T. cornstarch
2 egg yolks, beaten slightly
2 T. butter
1 t. finely shredded lemon peel
1/2 C. lemon juice

Directions:
In 2 qt. Casserole, combine sugar, flour, and cornstarch.
Stir in water.
Cook uncovered on high for 4-6 min. stirring every 2 min. until it starts to bubble.
Cook for 2-4 more min. stirring every min. until thick and bubble over entire surface
Cook for 1 more min.
Gradually stir 1 C. of hot mixture into egg yolks.
Return all to casserole dish
Cook uncovered on high for 2-3 min. or until bubbly stirring once.
Add butter, stirring just until melted.
Gently stir in lemon peel and juice.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
An easy, from-scratch recipe for lemon pudding in the microwave.
