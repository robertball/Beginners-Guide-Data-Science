Title:
Mom's BBQ Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
2 eggs
1 onion, chopped
2 cups crackers
salt and pepper to taste
2 T worcester sauce
3/4 cup milk
1 1/2 tsp dry mustard
1/4 cup  vinegar
8 ounces tomato sauce
1 cup brown sugar
dash garlic
1 tsp rosemary

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine beef, egg, onion, bread crumbs, salt and pepper. Roll into meatballs about 1 to 1 1/2 inches in size.
In a large skillet over medium heat, saute the meatballs until browned on all sides.
In a separate medium bowl, mix together the rest of ingredients and cook.
