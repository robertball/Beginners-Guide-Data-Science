Title:
Energy Spheres (revitalized) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sunbutter
1 cup honey
1/2 cup coco powder
1/8 tsp salt
4 tsp vanilla extract
2 cups gluten free oats (quick or old fashioned work)
3/4 cup coconut flakes (unsweetened)
6 Tbsp protein powder (sunwarrior)
4 Tbsp chia seeds
1 cup mix ins (1/2 cup roasted sunflower seeds, 1/2 cup craisens)

Directions:
In a 3.5 qt. pot over medium low heat, stir the sunbutter and honey until combined and barely warmed.
stir in the cocoa powder, vanilla & salt. Once incorporated add the remaining ingredients.
Form the dough into .5 oz balls (15g) and set on a cookie sheet. Eat immediately or chill in fridge. Store in an airtight container at room temp.

Submitter: 
Apron Appeal
Description: 
Need a quick boost to get you through the late afternoon? These shperes combine just the right amount of sweet with chocolate and crunch with a protein punch that will give you what you need to get through the end of your day or anything else you need a little extra energy for.
