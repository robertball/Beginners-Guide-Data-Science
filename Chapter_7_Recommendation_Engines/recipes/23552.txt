Title:
Perfect Party Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1260562.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (64 fluid ounce) bottle fruit punch
2 (15 ounce) cans pineapple chunks
1 pint strawberries, hulled and sliced
2 bananas, sliced
2 pints fruit flavored sherbet
1 (2 liter) bottle lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage

Directions:
In a large punch bowl, pour fruit punch. Add pineapple chunks, strawberries and bananas. Slide in the sherbet and slowly pour in the lemon-lime soda.

Submitter: 
Sher
Description: 
This punch is made at all our family gatherings. You can add vodka to taste for an adult party.
