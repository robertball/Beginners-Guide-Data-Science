Title:
Cajun Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/764213.jpg
Ingredients:
3 chicken breast thawed
Cut them into bite size pieces In a bowl combine...
1 tbsp of flour
1 tsp poultry seasoning
3/4 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp paprika
1/4 black pepper
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
2 tbsp margarine
1 cup/ 1/2 cup of mixed nuts (depend on how much nuts you like)
1 package of spanish rice

Directions:
Place the 2 tbsp into a skillet
Start your spanish rice per directions on package.
Mix remaining ingredients and cover the chicken pieces w/ it. Place into the skillet w/ the margarine.Right before  the chicken is all the way cooked add the mixed nuts. Continue cooking the chicken and mixed nuts until chicken is throughly cooked. Afer the spanish rice and your chicken is done cooking. divide the rice and place on the plates then put the chicken and mixed nuts on top of the rice and serve.
