Title:
Coating for restaurant style Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/397221.jpg
Ingredients:
5 lbs all purpose flour
1/4 cup salt
1/2 cup accent (or "no salt")
3 tbsp black pepper
1 tbsp white pepper
3 tbsp dried oregano
1 tbsp garlic salt
1 tbsp thyme
1 tbsp paprika
1 tbsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tbsp cumin
3-6 pcs. chicken (white or dark meat)
1- 2 eggs

Directions:
Mix all ingredients very well in a large bowl.  Put in air tight container to store.
1. Dip chicken parts in beaten eggs.
2. Roll in flour mixture.
3. Cook in oil at 300F until crispy and brown (6-10 minutes depending on the size of chicken parts). For baked chicken, dip in beaten eggs, roll in flour mixture and place on baking sheet, bake in 350F oven for 23-45 mins. depending on size of chicken parts.
Enjoy

Submitter: 
Angele Huard
Description: 
This is the flour, and seasoning, coating for fried chicken that tastes like it's take out from your favorite chicken place!  You can make this bulk recipe and have it on hand whenever you have a craving for yummy fried chicken, or even baked chicken!  This recipe has been in our family for years, I hope you enjoy it as much as I do!
