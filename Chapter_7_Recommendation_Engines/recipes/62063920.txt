Title:
Company Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups broccoli, steamed and drained
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 cup milk
1 can cream of celery soup
2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 tbsp. minced onion
salt and pepper to taste
Topping: 1 stack pack Ritz crackers (about 36 crackers), 1/4 cup butter or 1 stick margarine melted

Directions:
1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.  Pour into a greased 13X9 pan.
2. Crush Ritz crackers and mix with the melted butter.
3. Cover casserole with crumb mixture and bake at 350 for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
TABLESPOON
Description: 
Easy to make, this casserole's flavours blend well and make it a company pleaser.  Even those who don't love broccoli enjoy this casserole.
