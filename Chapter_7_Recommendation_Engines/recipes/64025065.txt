Title:
Sriracha Roasted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup dijon mustard
3/4 cup teriyaki sauce
2 teaspoons crushed red pepper flakes
1 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 cup light brown sugar
3/4 cup sriracha(asia chili sauce)
1(3 pound) whole chicken
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 teaspoons dried oregano

Directions:
Combine dijon mustard,teriyaki sauce,crushed red pepper flakes,onion powder,light brown sugar and sriracha in a
medium bowl;whisk well.
Rinse the chicken throughly,and remove the giblets.Sprinkle with 2 1/2 teaspoons spice mixture
inside the chicken,rub the remaining spice mixture on the outside the chicken.
Place the chicken in a large ziplock bag,pour the
sauce onto the chicken,place it on a shallow dish and
marinade for 4 hours before baking.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Remove chicken from the bag and place in a 9x13 inch
roasting pan,discarding bag with marinade.
Bake in the preheated oven for 1 1/2 hours,basting
several times with remaining sauce.
