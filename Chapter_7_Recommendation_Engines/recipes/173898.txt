Title:
Chicken Salsa Verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983713.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) package Simply Potatoes® Southwest Style Hash Browns
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs*
1 1/2 teaspoons ground mesquite or chipotle seasoning
2/3 cup salsa verde (green salsa)**
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
Chopped avocado
Chopped green onions

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees F.  Spray 13x9 inch glass baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.  Place Simply Potatoes(R) and sour cream in baking dish; stir together.  Spread Simply Potatoes(R) evenly over bottom of baking dish.  Rub both sides of each chicken thigh with mesquite seasoning.  Place on top of potatoes.  Cover; bake 40 to 45 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
Uncover; top chicken with salsa verde.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Continue baking, uncovered, 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.  Serve chicken and potatoes with avocado and green onions.

The best of the Southwest  roasted chicken with green garden salsa and bold mesquite seasonings.
