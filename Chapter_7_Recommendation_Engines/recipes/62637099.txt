Title:
Vodka Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 ½ c flour
1 t salt
2T sugar
12 T cold unsalted butter-cut in the ¼“ slices
½  c. Crisco cut into 4 pieces
¼ c cold vodka
¼ c cold Water

Directions:
¼ c cold Water
Process 1 ½ c flour, salt & sugar/.add Crisco and butter until it starts to collect in uneven clumps. Will look like cottage cheese, Redistribute a round bowl. Add remaining cup of flour until evenly distributed around the bowl.
Sprinkle water and vodka over mixture with plastic spatula fold to mix until dough is slightly tacky & sticks together. Divide into 2 even balls and flatten into 4” disc. Wrap in plastic and refrigerate at least 2 hours.
Roll on generously floured top until 1/8 ´thick
