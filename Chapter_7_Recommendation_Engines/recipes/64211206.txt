Title:
Elbow Macaroni & 3 Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. Uncooked elbows
1/2 Cup Butter
1/2 Cup AP Flour
2 t. dry ground mustard
1-1/2 t. Lowries Seasoned Salt
5 Cups Milk 2%
1# Cabot Seriously Sharp Cheddar
8 oz. Yellow mild Cheddar
5 oz. Frigo Shredded Parmesan

Directions:
Cook paste to el dante according to package direction.
Preheat oven to 375 deg. F.
In a large saucepan, over medium heat,   melt the butter and stir in the flour, mustard, and seasoned salt. Slowly stir in the milk and stir till mixture starts to thicken. Remove from heat and stir in half the cheese till melted. Combine the elbows and cheese sauce mixture and then transfer to a 13 x 9 baking dish. Top with the rest of the cheese and some bread crumbs or crushed potato chips. Sprinkle with a little paprika and bake for 40 minutes. Let cool about 10 minutes before serving.
