Title:
Cumin Black Beans and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. canola oil
1 tsp. minced garlic
1 tsp. cumin seeds
1 cup long grain rice
1 can(14 1/2 -oz.)chicken broth,
plus enough water to equal 3
cups
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 can(15-oz.)black beans, drained
and rinsed

Directions:
Heat oil in large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add garlic and cumin; cook for 30 seconds. Stir in rice. Add chicken broth and water, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer, covered, for 20 minutes.
Stir in beans. Cover and heat until beans are hot, about 2 minutes more.
