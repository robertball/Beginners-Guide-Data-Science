Title:
Pork Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBSP butter
12 ounces boneless pork loin
1/2 cup chopped carrots
1/2 cup chopped broccoli
2 green onions chopped
1/2 cup frozen peas
2 eggs, beaten
2 cups cold cooked rice
3 TBSP soy sauce
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp ground ginger

Directions:
Melt butter in a large non-stick skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir pork, carrot, broccoli and onion in melted butter until pork is cooked through, 7 to 10 minutes. Remove pork mixture to bowl and return skillet to medium heat.
Scramble eggs in the skillet until completely set. Return the pork mixture to the skillet. Stir rice, soy sauce, garlic powder and ground ginger into the port mixture, cook and stir until heated through, 7 to 10 minutes.
