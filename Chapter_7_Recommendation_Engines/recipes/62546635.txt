Title:
HCG Albondigas Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz Skinless Chicken Breast cubed
3 cups Water
1 cube no-sodium Chicken Broth
1 Lemon juiced
1 Tbls Taco Sauce
1/4c Greenbeans 2 inch pieces
1/4c frozen Peas
6 cherry Tomatoes
1 clove Garlic chopped
1 Tbls Onion chopped
1 Tbls Green Pepper chopped
2 sprigs fresh Cilantro or Parsley chopped
1 Tbls Oregano
1/2 Tbls Thyme
4 shakes Paprika
4 shakes Chili powder
2 shakes Cumin
1 Matzo Cracker Plain

Directions:
Take first 6 ingredients (minus 1 cup of water) and put in saucepan on Hi heat till boil, then turn down to Med-Hi. In small skillet cook rest of ingredients till chicken is almost done and onions are glassy. Let the bottom of pan get crusty and dark. Pour a little broth into pan and deglase the pan. Place lid on pan for 5 min to finish off chicken. Pour into stock mixture plus the last cup of water. Let simmer for flavors to mingle for 10min on med. Crumble 1 plain matzo cracker and serve in a large bowl.
