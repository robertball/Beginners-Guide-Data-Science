Title:
Lo Mein Noodles with Tofu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 quarts water
1 (16 ounce) package thin spaghetti
1 tablespoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
1 tablespoon reduced sodium teriyaki sauce
1 1/2 teaspoons Chinese five-spice powder
1/8 teaspoon white pepper
3 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 tablespoon sesame oil
1 (16 oz) pkg extrqa firm tofu
1 (16 oz) pkg frozen stir-fry vegetables

Directions:
In a Dutch oven, bring water and bouillon to a boil. Add spaghetti. Return to a boil; cook, uncovered, for 6 minutes or until almost tender. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup cooking liquid. Add soy sauce, teriyaki sauce, five-spice powder and pepper to reserved liquid; set aside. Set spaghetti aside.
Press tofu between paper towels to remove some of hte water; cut into bite size cubes. Heat oil, add tofu and fry untill golden brown, about 15 min. Add the stir fy vegetables stirring untill vegetables are tender.
Stir in reserved spaghetti. Add reserved soy sauce mixture to pan. Simmer, uncovered, for 3-4 minutes or until liquid has evaporated. Sprinkle with green onions.
