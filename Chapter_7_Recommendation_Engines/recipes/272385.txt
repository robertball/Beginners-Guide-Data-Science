Title:
Cherry Berry Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
12 sweet cherries, pitted
1 cup strawberries, quartered
1/2 cup blueberries
1 orange, zested and juiced
1/2 tablespoon lime zest
1 (750 milliliter) bottle red wine
1/4 cup bourbon (such as Maker's Mark Â®)
2 tablespoons mint leaves, or as needed (optional)

Directions:
Combine cherries, strawberries, and blueberries in a grill pan over medium heat. Cook and stir until fruit softens and juices are released, 3 to 5 minutes.
Place berry mixture, orange zest, and lime zest in the bottom of a carafe and muddle. Add orange juice, wine, and bourbon. Add mint leaves; gently stir. Chill for 2 hours before serving.

Description: 
This sangria has the perfect amount of sweetness and complements the sun-ripened berries of summer--and there is enough alcohol to knock you on your butt without noticing!
