Title:
Candy Jo's Chocolate Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/758637.jpg
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
2-5oz cans milnot
1 teaspoon salt
5 cups sugar
1-16oz jar Marshmallow cream
2-12oz bags butterscotch chips
1 teaspoon Vanilla extract
*Any flaver backing chips will due

Directions:
Heat first five ingreadients over low heat, until melted. Increase heat stiring constantly. Bring to a boil. Stir!! Set timer for five minutes, remove from heat. Stir in vanilla and baking chips. Pour into buttered or wax paper lined pan. Let cure for at least four hours. Cut into 1in X 1in pieces. Serve

Submitter: 
Mandi Whitaker
Description: 
Very tasty treat for the holiday's or just if your in the mood for fudge!
