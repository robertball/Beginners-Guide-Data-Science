Title:
Reuben Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package puff pastry sheets (1 box includes 2 sheets)
1 lb. sliced deli pastrami
1 lb. swiss cheese
1 jar mustard (dijon or grainy mustards are best)
1 egg beaten
caraway seeds

Directions:
Thaw pastry sheets according to package directions.
On a floured surface, roll out thawed dough to about 15" X 10".
Spread thin layer of mustard over whole area.
Cover whole surface of dough with one layer of pastrami.
Cover whole surface of pastrami with the swiss cheese.
Roll up tightly in a jelly roll fashion.
If you are serving within 24 hours, wrap in plastic and place in refrigerator for 1 hour(you can also store in the freezer for up to a month).
When ready to serve, slice 1/4" thick slices and lay on cookie sheet.
Brush tops with egg and sprinkle caraway seeds on top.
Place in 350 degree oven for about 12 minutes, or until lightly browned.
Let sit for a minute before removing from cookie sheet.
Serve with thousand island dressing (or prepared mustard dip).

Submitter: 
Laura Pitaniello
Description: 
Delicious, pretty appetizer. Perfect for any party. They usually go very quickly too!
