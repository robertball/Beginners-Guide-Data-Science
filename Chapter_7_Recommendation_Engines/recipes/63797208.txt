Title:
Broccoli and Cauliflower Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup fresh Broc. Florets
2 cup fresh cauliflower Florets
2 eggs
1/2 cup Mayo
1 med onion chopped fine
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup sharp cheddar shredded
1/2 cup butter melted
1 pkg seasoned croutons crushed

Directions:
Day before --Boil water and blanche florets for 3-4min. Drain and rinse with cool water.  In lightly butter casserole dish distribute florets evenly.  Beat eggs, mix with Mayo, onion, soup and 1/2 cup cheese.  pour evenly over florets. Sprinkle rest of cheese over top and level with spoon. -- next day or before baking -- Pour melted butter over entire top, distribute croutons over top.  Bake @ 350 for 40 min (bubbling)
