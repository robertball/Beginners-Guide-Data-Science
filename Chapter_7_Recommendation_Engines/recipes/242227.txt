Title:
Watermelon-Cilantro Salsa Tropical Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2776893.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups chopped seedless watermelon
1 cup chopped scallions
1 cup chopped fresh pineapple
1 cup chopped fresh mango
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
4 teaspoons fresh lime juice
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Toss watermelon, scallions, pineapple, mango, cilantro, and lime juice together in a bowl; season with salt and pepper.
