Title:
Gourmet Hot Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/511202.jpg
Ingredients:
All beef hot dogs
Garlic powder
Potato buns
Bacon
Provolone cheese
Shredded lettuce
Diced tomato
Chopped onion
Celery salt
BBQ sauce
Mustard

Directions:
Cook bacon. Cook hot dogs in pan on stove top sprinkled with garlic powder. Toast buns with cheese. Throw it all together and season with celery salt.
