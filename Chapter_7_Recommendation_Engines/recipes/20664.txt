Title:
Easy Nacho Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4575411.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
8 ounces chive and onion cream cheese
1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup chopped tomatoes
2 chopped green onions

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the sour cream, cream cheese and taco seasoning. Spread the mixture into a pie dish. Sprinkle the Cheddar cheese over the mixture in an even layer. Top the cheese with the tomatoes and green onions.

Submitter: 
DOXIEMAN
Description: 
A quick and easy dip that everyone loves. Doubling the recipe yields enough for a party. It is great with plain chips or nachos. Sliced black olives may be used as an additional topping.
