Title:
Pork with Lemon Thyme Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. olive oil
2 - 4 oz. boneless pork chops
1/2 cup chicken broth
2 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
2 tsp. fresh thyme - chopped
1 clove of garlic - minced
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Pound pork into thin cutlets and season with salt and pepper.
Heat oil in skillet over medium-high heat.
Place pork in skillet and sear until golden brown on each side - about 5 minutes.
Remove pork from pan then add broth, lemon juice, thyme and garlic.
Simmer until sauce reduces slightly - about 2-3 minutes.
Add pork to sauce and simmer until it it cooked  through - about 3 minutes.
