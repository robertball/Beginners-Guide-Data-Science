Title:
Meatloaf Harrell Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
6 T Worcestershire sauce
3 T steak sauce
1 1/4 T mustard
1 tsp ground black pepper
1/4 c Dr Pepper
1 tsp rosemary
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 T dill relish
2 T sweet relish
1 egg
1 c self-rising flour
1 can (8 oz) tomato sauce
1 onion, chopped
2 pkg Saltine crackers

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Beat egg. Crush crackers. Mix together all ingredients, minus half pack of crackers, in large bowl. Form into loaf in a 9"x13" baking dish. Top with remaining crackers. Bake 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Mrs Harrell
Description: 
Savory, easy-to-make meatloaf. Great main course for cold nights or served as sandwiches!
