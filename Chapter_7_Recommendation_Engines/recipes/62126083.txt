Title:
Deli Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz. (1/2 of 8 oz. pkg) Neufchatel or cream cheese
1 stick margarine
1/2 cup* squeezed out and finely chopped sauerkraut
1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
1 tsp. caraway seeds
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 cup rye flour
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1 cup crisp rice cereal
3 strips turkey bacon, cooked and crumbled
*Approx. 1 cup sauerkraut when squeezed out and chopped will yield the correct amount.

Directions:
Cream Neufchatel cheese and margarine.
Add sauerkraut, Swiss cheese, caraway seeds, and garlic powder.
Stir in flours.
Fold in cereal and bacon bits.
Shape into 3 dozen balls. Place on parchment papered baking sheets, then flatten.
Bake at 350 for 10 minutes.
Best eaten when hot!  Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator.  May be frozen.

Submitter: 
sueb
Description: 
These Swiss cheese, caraway, rye and sauerkraut appetizers are easily made. People enjoy them!
