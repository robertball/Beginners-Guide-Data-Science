Title:
Sausage Cheese Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. (32 oz) bulk sausage, uncooked
1-1/2 c. all-purpose baking/biscuit mix
16 oz. (4 c.) sharp shredded cheese
1/2 c. finely chopped onion
1/2 c. finely chopped celery
1/2 tsp. garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven 375 degrees. Mix ingredients and form into balls (about 1 inch in diameter). Bake 15 minutes on ungreased sheet. (NOTE: Can be frozen uncooked).
