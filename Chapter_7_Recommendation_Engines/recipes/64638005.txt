Title:
Healthy Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups rolled oats (large flake)
1/2 c unsweetened coconut
1/2 c nuts (i use walnuts)
1/4 cup seeds (i use sunflower)
3 tbsp pure maple syrup
2 tbsp coconut oil, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 deg.
Line a large or two small cookie sheets with parchment paper.
In a mixing bowl, combine oats, coconut, nuts, seeds and mix thoroughly. Add maple syrup and coconut oil and mix til everything is moistened. Pour onto parchment lined cookie sheets and spread in an even layer.
Bake in oven for 15 mins. Remove from oven, stir it up trying to get the outside edges into the middle and vice versa. Put back into the oven for another 15 mins. Remove from oven, let cool on pan. Once cooled store in mason jars or or airtight containers in your fridge.

Submitter: 
debbie eckstein
Description: 
This granola is moistened and sweetened with coconut oil and pure maple syrup. Great sprinkled on yogurt, fresh fruit, with milk, or just a couple handfuls as a snack!
