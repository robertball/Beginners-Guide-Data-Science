Title:
Kahlua Chocolate Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs, separated
3/4 c. granulated sugar
1/2 c. butter
1 c. brown sugar
2 1/4 c. flour
1/2 c. unsweetened cocoa
1 1/2 t. baking soda
3/4 c. strong cold coffee
3/4 c. Kahlua

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
In a medium bowl, or in the bowl of a stand mixer, beat egg whites until frothy; then beat in granulated sugar until stiff peaks form.  Set aside.
Cream butter and brown sugar until fluffy.  Beat  in egg yolks, one at a time.
Sift flour, cocoa, and baking soda together.  Add to creamed mixture alternately with coffee and Kahlua; blend well.
Fold egg whites into batter.
Fill prepared cupcake cups to 2/3 full.
Bake 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.  Remove cupcakes from tins and cool on rack completely before frosting.
