Title:
Potato and Ham Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4898690.jpg
Ingredients:
half stick butter
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped onion
3 cups chicken broth
6 cups peeled and cubed potatoes
1 TBS dried parsley
3 TBS  all-purpose flour
4 cups milk
3 cups shredded American cheese
2 cups chopped ham
2-3 tsp salt
1 tsp. black pepper
1/2 tsp turmeric
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp. paprika

Directions:
1.	In a large stock pot melt butter then add celery and onion til slighty tender.
2.	Add chicken broth and parsley flakes and bring to a boil.
3.	Add potatoes and salt, pepper, turmeric, garlic powder, paprika and simmer until vegetables become tender.
4.	In a separate bowl mix flour and milk. Once it is well blended, add to soup mixture and cook until soup becomes thick.
5.	Stir in cheese, cooked ham and simmer until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Nancy Keely
Description: 
This rich golden potato soup is the perfect anytime soup.
