Title:
Slow Cooker French Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1023581.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pounds rump roast
1 (10.5 ounce) can beef broth
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed French onion soup
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle beer
6 French rolls
2 tablespoons butter

Directions:
Trim excess fat from the rump roast, and place in a slow cooker.
Add the beef broth, onion soup and beer.
Cook on Low setting for 10 to 12 hours.
Split French rolls, and spread with butter. Bake at 350Â° for 10 minutes, or until heated through.
Shred the meat with two forks.
Serve with provolone cheese, sauteed onions, pepperoncini peppers, and the sauce for dipping.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
An economical version of an old-favorite sandwich. It is made of rump roast simmered for 7 hours with beef broth, onion soup and beer. The reduced cooking liquid makes plenty of sauce for dipping.Modified from: Easy Slow Cooker French Dip
