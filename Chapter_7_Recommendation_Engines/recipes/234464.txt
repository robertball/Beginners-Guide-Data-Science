Title:
Creepy Crawly JIGGLERS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1043891.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups boiling water
1 (3 ounce) package JELL-O Brand Grape Flavor Gelatin
3 tablespoons JELL-O Brand Lime Flavor Gelatin
16 wooden skewers or coffee stirrers
16 blackberries
128 (1 1/2 inch) pieces black string licorice
8 red miniature candy-coated chocolate pieces, halved

Directions:
Spray wells of plastic ice cube trays or mini muffin pans with cooking spray, set aside. Add boiling water to combined gelatin mixes in medium bowl; stir 2 min. until completely dissolved. Pour into prepared ice cube trays or mini muffin pan cups. Refrigerate 3 hours or until firm.
Place one blackberry "head" and one gelatin "body" on each skewer. Decorate with licorice and candy.

Submitter:

Description: 
Kids will have as much fun making these Halloween 'spiders' as they will eating them.
