Title:
Curry-Celery Pot Roast (Slow Cooker Style) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 pound) beef roast
3 Tbls olive oil
1 clove garlic
Finely ground black pepper
1/4 cup burgandy wine
1/2 cup water
2 beef boullion cubes
1 can condensed cream of celery soup
1/4 tsp celery seed
1/2 tsp curry
1 bay leaf
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1 onion coarsly chopped

Directions:
Place oil in a dutch oven or heavy high sided pan and place over medium heat.
Finely chop garlic or push through a garlic press.  Add to oil.
While garlic is simmering rinse roast and pat dry.  Sprinkle finely ground black pepper over all sides of the roast.
Place roast in oil and brown all sides (about 4 minutes a side.
Once browned, remove from pan and set aside.
Deglaze pan with burgandy wine, making sure to scrape the pan of all the browned bits; bring to a gentle boil.
Add water and beef boullion cubes simmer until cubes dissolve completely about 3 minutes.
In the crock pot add celery soup, celery seed, curry, bay leaf and garlic powder. Mix to incorporate ingredients.
Add wine and boullion mixture to crock pot.
Mix thoroughly.
Place roast and chopped onion into crock pot.
Cook on high for 5 hours or low for 8 stirring gravy at least once.

Description: 
Basic ingedients make a hearty home cooked meal with ready made gravy. Curry and black pepper add a slight zip while canned soup and wine add just the right touch of home cooked goodness. Serve with egg noodles, mashed potatoes, roasted potatoes or polenta to accent the gravy this super easy dish makes.
