Title:
Chinese Chilli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-lbs pork, cubed
1-lb  steak, cubed(round steak)
2-green peppers, lg. chopped
2-onions, lg. chopped
2-celery, sm.stocks, diced
4-cps. broth, beef
1-8oz cn. bamboo shoots
1-8oz cn. water chestnuts, sliced
1-tsp. pepper
2-tbsp. chili powder
4 oz soy sauce
3 tbsp corn starch
8-mushrooms, sliced
1-sm.pkg. bean sprouts

Directions:
1)Brown pork through & add beef & brown with peppers, onions & celery.
2)Cover & simmer 20min until onions are transparent. Stir frequently.
3)Add beef broth, bamboo shoots, water chestnuts, pepper, & chilli powder. Blend.
4)Combine soy sauce with cornstarch until smooth.
5)Pour slowly into meat mixture while stirring until thickened.
6)Add remaining ingredients & cover stiring frequently & simmer 2hrs or until meat is tender.
