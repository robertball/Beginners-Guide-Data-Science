Title:
Quick & Easy Honey Mustard Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 tablespoons brown mustard (more or less as desired)
5 tablespoons honey (more or less as desired)
cooking spray or any kind of oil
4 boneless pork chops

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Mix mustard and honey together in a small bowl.
On four 10-inch pieces of foil (one for each chop) spray cooking spray or rub with oil.  In the center of the foil, place a small amount of honey mustard.  Lay pork chop on top of honey mustard, then coat top of pork chop with honey mustard (be generous!) Wrap chop in foil, making sure there are no openings that will allow steam to escape.  Place the four packages on a baking sheet and bake for 20 - 25 minutes.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
MARIACOZ
Description: 
This is a really easy and different way to serve boneless pork chops.  The sauce that the chops creates in the foil can be poured over rice or vegetables to add extra flavor.
