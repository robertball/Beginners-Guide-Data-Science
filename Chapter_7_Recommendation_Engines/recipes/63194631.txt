Title:
Rainbow Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. cooked brown rice
3 T. frozen orange juice concentrate
2 T. hoisin sauce
2 T. soy sauce
2-4 T. chopped green chile (to taste)
2 tsp. toasted sesame oil
1/2 # green beans, halved crosswise
1 C. thinly sliced purple cabbage
2 medium carrots, matchstick cut
1 small red bell pepper, sliced (1 c.)
1 c. frozen shelled edamame
2 garlic cloves, minced or pressed
1 8-0z can sliced water chestnuts, drained
4 green onions, thinly sliced

Directions:
Cook brown rice.
Whisk together orange juice, hoisin, soy sauce, and chile in a small bowl.
Heat sesame oil in large skilled over high heat. Add green beans and stir fry 3 minutes. Add 3 T. water and stir-fry 3 minutes more.
Add cabbage, carrots, bell pepper, edamame, garlic, and water chestnuts; stir fry 4 minutes.  Stir in green onions and sauce mix; cook one minute more.
Serve over brown rice.
