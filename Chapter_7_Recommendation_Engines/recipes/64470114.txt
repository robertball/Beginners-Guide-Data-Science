Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2463658.jpg
Ingredients:
Balls:
1 lb Idaho Potatoes cooked until tender and mashed
2 c Sauerkraut Well drained
1 c small diced ham
2 egg yolks
1/4 c whole grain mustard
2 Tb chopped green onion
Salt and Pepper
Breading:
Flour
2 well beaten eggs
12 c milk
bread crumbs
olive oil

Directions:
1: Mix first 7 ingredients in a large bowl
2: Shape mixture into small balls
3: Freeze balls for 30 minutes
Breading:
4: Remove frozen balls from freezer
5: Mix eggs and milk in medium sized bowl
6: Roll each ball individually in flour
7: Dip each floured ball into egg/milk mixture then into breadcrumbs.
8: Fry until golden in olive oil

Submitter: 
FireHeadGirl
Description: 
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites are scrumptious sauerkraut balls that are easy to make and share.  Be careful though, they disappear really fast, and not just because Dinomite eats them all.
