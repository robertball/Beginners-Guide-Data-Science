Title:
French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1/2 cup milk
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/8 tsp salt
1/4 tsp nutmeg
2 Tbsp butter
4 slices dry white bread (or French bread)

Directions:
Beat together eggs, milk, vanilla, salt and nutmeg until blended.  Melt butter at 290 degrees in electric frypan.  Dip bread slices into egg mixture and place in frypan.  Brown on each side (about 5 minutes).  Serve with maple syrup.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
The nutmeg makes these very tasty.
