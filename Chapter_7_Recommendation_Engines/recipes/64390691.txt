Title:
Yummy Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups sweet potato, cubed
1/4 cup white sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 tablespoons butter, softened
1/2 cup milk (or condensed milk better)
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 t. cinnamon
2 t. vanilla
TOPPING:
1 cup packed brown sugar
2/3 cup all-purpose flour
6 tablespoons butter, softened
2/3 cup chopped pecans
(use mini marshmallows for one for kids)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
(skip if using canned) Put sweet potatoes in a medium saucepan  with water to cover. Cook over medium high heat until tender; drain and mash.
In a large bowl, mix together the sweet potatoes, white sugar, eggs, salt, butter, milk and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth. Transfer to a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In medium bowl, mix the brown sugar and flour. Cut in the butter until the mixture is coarse. Stir in the pecans. Sprinkle the mixture over the sweet potato mixture.
Bake in the preheated oven 30 minutes, or until the topping is lightly brown.
