Title:
Mexican Wontons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound hamburger, browned and drained
1/4 cup onions, chopped
2 tablespoons green pepper, chopped
1 can refried beans, 15 ounce
1/4 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
1 tablespoon catsup
1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1 package won-ton wrappers, 4 dozen

Directions:
Brown hamburger with green pepper and onion.  Drain.  Add remaining ingredients (except won-ton wrappers).
Spoon teaspoon of filling into center of won-ton wrapper. Wet corners of wrapper with milk, fold sides over and press to seal.
Fry in hot oil until golden brown.  Drain on paper towels.  Serve warm with salsa.
Reheat in oven of 350 degrees for 10 minutes.
