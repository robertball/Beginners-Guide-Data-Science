Title:
Instant Hot Cocoa Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/890263.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1 cup powdered non-dairy creamer
1 cup dry milk powder
3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

Directions:
In a blender or food processor, combine SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, powdered creamer, milk powder and cocoa powder. Mix well, and store in an airtight container.
To serve, put 2 to 3 tablespoons of powder in a mug, fill with hot water, and stir.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
This easy to make creamy hot chocolate mix makes a great gift! You simply mix together cocoa, SPLENDA®, powdered creamer and powdered milk.
