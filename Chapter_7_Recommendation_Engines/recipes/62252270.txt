Title:
Sourdough pumpkin strata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 (1 1/4 inch) slices sourdough bread (about 8 oz.)
6 oz. sharp cheddar, divided
1/2 c. chopped onion, divided
1 can or about 5 oz. (or more, to taste) chopped green chiles, divided
1 2/3 c. milk
1/2 tsp. dried thyme
1/2 tsp. dried sage
1/4 tsp each salt and pepper
1 can (15-oz) pumpkin
2 large eggs
2 T. pumpkin seeds (opt.)

Directions:
Arrange 2 bread slices in a single layer in a 3-qt. dish coated with cooking spray.  Sprinkle with 1/2 c. cheese, one-third onion and one-third chiles.  Repeat layers twice.
Combine milk and next 6 ingredients (milk through eggs) in a blender, and process till smooth.  Pour over bread layers.
Cover and refrigerate 8 hours.
Preheat oven to 350.  Uncover strata and sprinkle with pumpkin seeds.  Bake for and hour or till a knife inserted in center comes out clean.  Let stand 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Susie
Description: 
Assemble this strata the night before, or make first thing in the morning, as it has to set 8 hours.
