Title:
Grammy's Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c Crisco oil
2 c packed brown sugar
1 c white sugar
2 eggs, beaten well
1/2 c cold water
2 tsp real vanilla extract
1 tsp salt
1/2 c crunchy peanut butter
6 c oatmeal (quick-cooking is best)
2 (12 oz) packages chocolate chips
1 package English walnuts or pecans, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
Combine first eight ingredients, mix well. Stir in oats, chips, and nuts.
Drop by spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets. Space well, they will spread while baking. Bake until golden. 12-15 minutes depending on size.

Submitter: 
zeebee
Description: 
These cookies were a family favorite when I was growing up. Peanut butter-oatmeal-chocolate chip makes perfect combo for a fantastic cookie.
