Title:
Mom's Zucchini Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small zucchini, grated (about 2 cups)
1 small onion, chopped fine
1 c. grated cheddar cheese
2 eggs, beaten
3/4 stick butter
3/4 c. cracker crumbs
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. garlic powder

Directions:
Drain zucchini.
In a large bowl, combine zucchini, onion, and most of the cheese (reserve some for topping)
Mix well and add eggs.
In a small bowl, melt butter and mix with cracker crumbs.
Add most of crumb mixture to zucchini mixture, reserving some for topping.
Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
Pour into a buttered 9x9 dish.
Top with reserved buttered crumbs and cheese.
Bake at 350 for 45 min or until brown and cooked through.
