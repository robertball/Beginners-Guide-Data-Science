Title:
Potato Chip Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c milk
1 egg
1-1/4 c potato chips, finely crushed
1/4 t onion powder
1/4 t black pepper
3 boneless, skinless chicken breast
2 T melted butter

Directions:
preheat oven to 400 - coat a rimmed baking pan with non stick cooking spray
1 a small bowl, beat together, milk and egg with a fork until well mixed,  in a shallow dish combine crushed chips, onion powder and pepper
dip chicken pieces in egg mixture then roll them in potato chip mixture, repeat until all chicken is coated
arrange coated pieces in a single layer on baking pan, let sit at room temp 15 minuted - drizzle chicken with melted butter
bake 20-25 minutes or until firm and golden
