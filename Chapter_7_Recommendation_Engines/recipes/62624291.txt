Title:
Swiss Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2T olive oil
1 yellow onion, chopped
2t flour
1/4c dry white wine
6oz low sodium chicken broth
1-1/4lb ground turkey
1lb swiss cheese, diced
1t poultry seasoning
1/4t red pepper
4 English muffins

Directions:
Heat olive oil in skillet over medium-high heat.
Add onions and season with salt and pepper
Cover and cook for 5-10 minutes.
Sprinkle with flour.
Cook 1 minute, add wine and stock, and cook until thickened.
Combine turkey, cheese, salt and pepper, poultry seasoning, and red pepper in a medium bowl.
Separate into 4 parts and patty, no more than 1" thick.
Cook patties in a sprayed skillet over medium-high heat for 5-6 minutes on each side until done.
Serve on English muffins, smothered with onions.
