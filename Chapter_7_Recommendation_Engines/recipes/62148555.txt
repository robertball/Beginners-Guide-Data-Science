Title:
Banana Cranberry Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289146.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 cup softened butter
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 1/3 cups mashed very ripe bananas
1 1/4 cups cranberries,coarsely chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. (175 degrees C.)Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
In a large bowl combine flour,baking soda,salt.cinnamon,and nutmeg In another medium bowl cream butter and brown sugar .Stir in eggs,vanilla,and mashed bananas until well blended.Stir banana mixture into flour mixture, stir just enough to blend wet and dry ingredients, then fold in chopped cranberries. ( the less you stir the better the texture)
Bake in preheated oven for 65 -70 minutes,or until a toothpick inserted in center of loaf comes out clean.Let bread cool in pan about 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
