Title:
Coconut Rice (in Rice Cooker) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/634349.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked jasmine rice
1/2 cup coconut milk (not "lite")
1 cup water
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 tablespoons dry shredded coconut, unsweetened or sweetened (baking type)
1 teaspoon finely chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Mix together all ingredients in a rice cooker. Cover and cook.
When cooker switches from 'Cook' to 'Warm' setting, let steam for another 10 minutes to achieve sticky consistency. Serve.

Submitter: 
Becca Owens
Description: 
Tasty Asian rice that is great served with fish or shrimp.
