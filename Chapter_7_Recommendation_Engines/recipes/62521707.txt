Title:
Adobe Chicken Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1603812.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ½	cups chopped cooked chicken
¼	to ½ cup leftover rice (if you have any)
¼	cup green onions, chopped, Optional
½	cup salsa
1	can (15 oz.) black beans, rinsed and drained
1	can (7 oz.) whole kernel corn, drained
6	spinach or flour tortillas
3/4     cup shredded cheddar or cheese of your choice
1/3	cup sour cream
Additional salsa, if desired

Directions:
1.	Mix chicken, ½ cup salsa, green onions, beans, rice and corn.  Divide chicken mixture among tortillas, spreading to within 2 inches of bottom of each tortilla.  Top each with sour cream
2.	Fold one end of the tortilla up about 1 inch over filling; fold right and left sides over folded end, overlapping.  Fold remaining end down.  Serve with additional salsa.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
This quick easy wrap is Great for leftover bits.  A little rice, cooked chicken,beans, and salsa a little shredded cheese and sour cream, and you have a really tasty wrap sandwich.
