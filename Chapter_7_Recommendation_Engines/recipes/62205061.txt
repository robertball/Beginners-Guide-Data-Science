Title:
Butternut /apple soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T butter
2 T oil
4 c onion
2 T curry
5 lbs butternut squash, peeled and diced
1 1/2 lb sweet apple, peeled and diced
2 t kosher salt
1/2 t pepper
2 c apple cider

Directions:
Mix first 5 and cook 20 min.
Put squash chunks and apple in water and simmer 40 min.
Min all together. Can process is needed
