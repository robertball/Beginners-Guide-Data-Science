Title:
Pineapple and Cranberry Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15.25 ounce) can of crushed Pineapple
1 1/2 Tablespoons fresh ginger root, shredded
2 cloves of garlic, minced
1 1/2 teaspoons orange peel, shredded
1 teaspoon allspice
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper, or to your taste
1 (8 ounce) can whole-berry cranberry sauce
1/3 cup sliced green onions, optional
2 teaspoons fresh lime juice

Directions:
Drain pineapple, reserving the juice.
Pour the pineapple juice into a saucepan and add ginger root, garlic, orange peel, allspice and crushed red pepper.
Cook over medium-high heat for 8 minutes.  Turn off heat.
Add the pineapple, cranberry sauce, onions and lime juice.
Mix well and serve warm over sliced chicken or turkey.

Submitter: 
Linda Grein
Description: 
My sister used to make this salsa recipe every Thanksgiving and also when she had Chicken.  I'm glad that I have the recipe now.  I hope it taste's as good for you as it does for me.
