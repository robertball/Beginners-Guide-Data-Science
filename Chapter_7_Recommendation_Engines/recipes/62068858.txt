Title:
Stuffed Italian Sausage Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb- Italian bread or pizza dough
4 tbls- butter
Â½- tsp garlic powder
1 lb- loose Italian sausage browned and drained on paper towels
4- tbls grated Parmesan cheese
12 oz- grated mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Roll out bread dough to 1/4" rectangle. Melt butter and brush over dough, sprinkle garlic powder and parmesan cheese over entire area then add sausage and cheese. Tightly roll the long side folding in the ends, seal with a little of the butter, transfer to parchment lined cookie sheet then brush the entire loaf with the remaining butter. Let rise in warm place for 1 hour then bake in preheated 350* oven for 20-30 minutes. Remove from oven and let cool for 15 minutes, cut into slices and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Vince Jones
Description: 
This stuffed bread recipe is one that I make for special occasions. I am often asked to make it as a pass around for a pot luck dinner type settings.
