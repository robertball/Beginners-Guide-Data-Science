Title:
Slow Cooker White Bean and Hamhock Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. dry cannelini beans
16 oz. can seasoned diced tomatoes
1 large or two smaller smoked hamhocks
1 cup onion, diced
1 cup celery, sliced
1 cup carrots, sliced
2 cloves of garlic, diced (or 1 tsp. garlic powder)
6 cups chicken broth*
1/4 cup dry parsley
black pepper, to taste
salt, to taste

Directions:
Rinse the beans and put them into a medium sized bowl.  Pour boiling water over the beans and allow them to sit for about an hour.  In the mean time prepare the vegetables and spray the liner of the slow cooker with PAM non-stick spray.  After and hour, pour off the water and place the beans into the crock.  Add the vegetables, broth and seasonings and gently stir the mixtures.  Place the hamhock into the broth.  Cover and cook on low for 8 hours.  Before serving, remove the hamhock and remove the bone and pull the meat apart.  Return the meat to the soup, stir and serve.

Submitter: 
Sally Anne
Description: 
Hearty soup that is especially good in the winter months.
