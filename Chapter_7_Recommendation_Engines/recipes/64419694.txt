Title:
Big Ray's Garlic Cheesy Twists Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2300112.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can (8 oz.) refrigerated crescent dinner rolls
2 Tbsp butter, melted, divided
1 tsp Italian seasoning
1 tsp garlic powder
2 Tbs.Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/4 cup mozzarella cheese, finely shredded

Directions:
Heat oven to 375ÂºF.
Unroll dough on cutting board, pinching cuts together.  In a small bowl, mix 1 Tbsp butter and all other ingredients.  Spread mixture on dough.  Fold over 1/3 dough to one side , then other 1/3 dough to cover.  Cut into 8 strips
Place strips, 1 inch apart, on parchment-covered rimmed baking sheet. Hold both ends of 1 strip, then turn ends in opposite directions to form twist. Repeat with remaining dough strips. Brush with butter.
Bake 9 to 10 min. or until golden brown.
