Title:
Carrillo's Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Avocados
1 tomato chopped
2 slices red onion chopped
2 jalapenos chopped
light cream
salsa
garlic
pepper
salt

Directions:
Mash avocado, add tomatoes, onion, chilies, to avocado mixture.  Thin with light cream.  Add salsa, garlic, pepper and salt to taste.
Chill for an hour to combine flavors.
Serve with fresh tortilla chips.
