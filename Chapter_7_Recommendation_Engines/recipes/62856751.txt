Title:
chopped chickpeas peppers olives lemon confit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2C MARINATED CHICKPEAS
3c dried chickpeas, or 4c canned
2T+1c EVOO
1 onion, quartered
1 celery+1c finely diced
2 medium carrots, peeled and halved
4 sprigs rosemary
2 sprigs fresh thyme
salt and pepper
1c red onion
1c red wine vinegar
2 cloves garlic
1/2 t chopped peperoncini
1T oregano
1c parsley
zest and juice from 1 lemon
8 slices country bread
1/2C ROASTED PEPPERS
2 red peppers
1t garlic
1t rosemary
8 basil leaves
2T EVOO
salt and pepper
4T pitted and chopped Nicoise olives
12 SLICES LEMON CONFIT
5 shallots
6 garlic
2/3c kosher salt
1/3c sugar
4c EVOO
1c flat leaf parsley
pepper

Directions:
Soak chickpeas over night. Heat 2T oil and cook onion, celery, carrots for 15 min. Tie 2 sprigs or rosemary and thyme in a cheesecloth and add to veggies. Add the chickpeas and enough water to cover by 2". Bring to gentle simmer and season with salt and pepper-cook 1 1/2 hrs. Cool completely, drain and discard veggies and herbs.
In a bowl combine red onion and vinegar set aside for 15 min. Drain and add the rest for at least 1 hr.
Char the peppers, remove skin, mix the rest and chill.
Plunge lemons in a pot of boiling water. Drain rinse, wipe, and dry. Slice them very thin, discard the ends and remove the seeds. Combine shallots and garlic, in  another bowl mix salt and sugar.
Arrange a layer of lemon, sprinkle with shallot and then with salt sugar. Repeat and refrigerate for 3 days, flipping 1/2 way through-allowing to cure evenly. Drain and add oil to cover all lemons. Keep for a month.
Coarsely chop chickpeas until spreadable but chunky. Spread over 4 breads and remaining ingredients, season with salt and pepper and remaining bread Cut into halves.
