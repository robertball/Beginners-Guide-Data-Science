Title:
Easy Brined Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Pecies of Chicken
BRINE
1/4 Cup Non Idoized Salt
1 Quart Water
CHICKEN RUB
2 Cups Flour
1 1/2 Tbs Paperika
1 1/2 Tbs Season Salt
1/2 Tbs Pepper
1/2 Tbs Garlic Flakes / 1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 Tbs Onion Flakes

Directions:
Mix Brine solution, place chicken in solution 4hrs to overnight.
Mix Rub ingredents in gallon zip lock bag, coat 2-3 pieces of chicken at a time. placen on oil sprayed backing pan. Bake @ 350 for 30 min turn chicken and bake another 30 min or until juices turn clear.

Submitter: 
Rick Falsetta
Description: 
An easy way way to brine Chicken, to hold moisture and give chicken that salty taste with every bite.
