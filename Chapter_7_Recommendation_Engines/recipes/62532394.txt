Title:
Gail's Butter cream Icing & Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup vegetable shortening
1 tsp. vanilla
1/8 tsp. salt
4 cups powdered sugar
2-3 Tbsp. milk
1 Tbsp. meringue powder

Directions:
Cream butter and shortening together. Add vanilla and salt. Beat in sugar, one cup at a time (scraping sides and bottom of bowl often).
Add milk; beat at high speed until light and fluffy. Keep icing covered in fridge when not in use.
