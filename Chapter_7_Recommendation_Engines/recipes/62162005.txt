Title:
Russian potato and beef salad "Olivier" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 russet potato
3 carrots
3-4 eggs
1 lb bologna
1/3 cup onion
3 medium dill pickles
1(15 oz)can of green peas
2 cups mayo
fresh dill
salt and pepper

Directions:
Put the potato,carrot and eggs in a pot,put enough water just to cover.In about 15 min take out carrots,in 5 more min eggs,put in ice bath.
After the potato is cooked and cooled,dice it,add diced carrots,pickles and onions
Add green peas,season with salt,pepper and fresh dill
Dress with mayo
