Title:
Monster-Eye Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4563155.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
1 (1 ounce) packet taco seasoning mix
8 hard taco shells
1 cup shredded lettuce
1/2 cup chopped fresh tomatoes
1/2 cup finely shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup sour cream
16 slices pitted black olives

Directions:
In a large nonstick skillet, brown turkey over medium high heat. Add taco seasoning mix and water, as directed on taco seasoning packet. Meanwhile, heat taco shells in the oven according to package directions.
Spoon some of the taco meat into each of the taco shells. Top with some of the lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese. Put two dollops of sour cream on each taco to mimic eyeballs. Top each dollop of sour cream with an olive slice to make eyes.
