Title:
Creamy Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 med potatoes
1 med onion
2 stalks celery
1 bay leaf
3 cups boiling water (enough to cover)
1/2 tsp salt
2 tbsp butter
2 tbsp flour
1/4 tsp pepper
2 cups milk

Directions:
Wash, peel and dice potatoes, onions and celery
Put in pot and cover with boiling water
Add salt...do not drain
meanwhile in separate pan......melt butter
add flour using wooden spoon and stir until smooth
Stir in 1/2 cup milk and keep cooking stirring until thickens slightly
Combine white sause with cooked potato mixture and serve while hot

Submitter: 
Margaret Byrne
Description: 
Easy potato soup.....can add bacon and cheese and melt under broiler
