Title:
"No Guilt" Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
3/4 cup flour (used whole wheat white)
1/2 cup cocoa
2 tsp corn starch
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp vital wheat gluten
3/4 cup non-fat yogurt
1 tsp vanilla (or omit and use vanilla flavored yogurt)

Directions:
Spray 8x8 or 9x9 pan with cooking spray.  Preheat oven to 350
Sift/whisk together dry ingredients.
Stir in yogurt until dry ingredients are completely moistened (takes a while)
Scrape batter into pan
Bake for 30 minutes - let cool completely before cutting

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
My attempt at replicating No Pudge brownie mix, where you just add yogurt and bake. A work in progress....
