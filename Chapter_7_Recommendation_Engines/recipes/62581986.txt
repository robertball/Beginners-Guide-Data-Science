Title:
Cranberry Coffeecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c flour
1 c sugar
1 tsp salt
3 tsp baking powder
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
1 tsp lemon juice
1 milk
1/2 c oil
16 oz can jellied cranberry sauce
3-5 tsp cinnamon sugar

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients together & let stand. Beat eggs well. Add lemon, vanilla, milk & oil. Mix thoroughly. Add dry ingredients, mixing well. Slice cranberry sauce into small cubes & fold into batter. Pour into a prepared 9" by 13" pan. Sprinkle top w/ cinnamon & sugar. Bake 25-30 min @ 350 till center tests for doneness.
