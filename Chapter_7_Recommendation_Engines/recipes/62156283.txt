Title:
Slow Cooker Beef Tips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs beef stew meat
2(10.5 oz)cans of cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1(1 oz)envelope of onion soup mix
1 medium sized onion, minced
2 cloves of garlic, minced
1 8oz package of sliced mushrooms (I use baby bella's)
1 cup of ginger ale

Directions:
Place beef in slow cooker
Mix together the cream of mushroom soup, onion soup mix, onion, garlic, mushrooms and ginger ale together and pour over meat.
Cover and cook on high for one hour and then turn it down to low and cook for another 5 hours.
Serve over rice
