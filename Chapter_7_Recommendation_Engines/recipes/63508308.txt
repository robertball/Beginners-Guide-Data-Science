Title:
Farm House Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup skim milk
1 egg
2 tablespoons margarine
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
3 cups bread flour
2 tablespoons sesame seeds
2 teaspoons yeast

Directions:
Place in the pan of your bread machine in the order recommended by your manufacturer.
Either bake to completion or take out of bread machine at the end of the dough cycle and form in to a loaf.
If forming yourself, allow to rise and bake at 350 until 190 degrees or hollow when tapped.

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
One of my first breads to make in my first bread machine.  I still use the recipe when I'm in a hurry, although now I form it myself and use it however I wish (buns, rolls, etc).
