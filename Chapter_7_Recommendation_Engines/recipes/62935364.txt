Title:
Egg, Potatoes, Bacon and Cheddar Breakfast Casserole Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/719190.jpg
Ingredients:
3-4 Russet Potatoes, peeled and shredded
Salt
Pepper
3 tbsp Butter
5 tbsp Milk
5 Large Eggs
3/4 cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
5 Slices Bacon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large skillet melt butter on medium-high heat.  Add potatoes and season with salt/pepper to taste while mixing to coat in butter and seasoning.  Stir occasionally until evenly a light golden brown but still soft(About10-15 min).  Set aside after cooked.
While potatoes are cooking, pan fry bacon until crunchy.  Set aside on paper towels to remove excess grease.
Whisk eggs in bowl until well scrambled.  Add salt and pepper to taste.  Mix in milk and shredded cheese.
Empty skillet of potatoes into 9"x9" glass baking dish.  Crumble bacon into small pieces scattered around on top of potatoes.(remove any excess chunks of fat off of bacon) Pour egg and cheese mixture over potatoes.  Mix slightly to get the eggs and cheese all around and inbetween the potatoes.  Spread evenly.
Bake uncovered for 30 min or until set.  Let cool for 10 min before serving
