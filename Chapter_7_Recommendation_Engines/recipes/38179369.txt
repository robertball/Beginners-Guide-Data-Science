Title:
Apple Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 apples (different varieties) peeled, cored and chopped
1/2 c. water
1/2 c. white sugar
cinnamon to taste (opt)

Directions:
In a large pot, combine all the ingredients.
Cover and cook over medium heat for 15-20 minutes or until apples are soft.
Stir occasionally.  Apples will mash easily with a wooden spoon while stirring.
Cool completely, fill quart sized freezer bags leaving 1/2 inch headspace.
Makes approx 4-5 bags.

Submitter: 
KELLY BENSON
Description: 
Yummy applesauce made from fresh apples. Can be easily frozen too.
