Title:
Best Traditional Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Frozen Pie crust (regular,not deep dish)
3 eggs
1 c sugar
1/2 c corn syrup
1/4 c melted margarine or butter
1 c pecans - use a mixture of coarsely chopped & halves

Directions:
Preheat over to 375. Beat eggs slightly. Stir in sugar, corn syrup & melted butter. stir in pecans. Pour into unbaked pie shell. Bake 35-40 minutes, or until filling is slightly firm. Note for deep dish pecan pie shells: increase ingredients as follows: 5 1/2 T butter, 4 eggs, 1/2c plus 3T corn syrup, 1 1/3 c sugar. Increase baking time to 40-45 minutes.

Description: 
This is my favorite pecan pie recipe. It has been at family gatherings since the 60s and never fails. This uses the small pie crusts, but I have also adapted it to use the deep dish crusts.
