Title:
White Sauce for Fish Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
Juice from 1 medium Lime
2 tsp. Tapatio (or more to taste)
1 Tbsp Marie Ploy Chile Sauce
Chopped Cilantro

Directions:
Mix up the above ingredients in a small bowl. Reserve for your Taco's.  Firm (steamed) white fish is best (should have large chunks of fish in each taco).  Finely slice white cabbage or bok choy.  Fine slice 1 avocado and red onion.  Layer fish, and cabbage/onion avocado mixture onto a warm 4" corn tortilla.  Add a big dollop of the white sauce.

Submitter: 
Keep it Tasty
Description: 
This is a very authentic version of the white fish taco.  You may also add thinly sliced red radish.
