Title:
Cantaloupe Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
You decide the quanity by how big you want your salad to be.
salad greens, mostly spinach
cantaloupe balls
crumbled crisp bacon
cashew halves
1/4 c. honey
1/4 red wine vinegar
1/4 salad oil

Directions:
Mix salad greens with cantaloupe, bacon and cashews.
Mix together honey, vinegar, salad oil.  Toss over salad greens.
Serve immediately.
