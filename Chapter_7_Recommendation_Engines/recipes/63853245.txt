Title:
Shrimp Ranchero Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white & wild rice
1/2 lb shrimp, shelled & deveined
1/2 onion, chopped
3 jalapeno peppers, chopped
1 tblsp cilantro, chopped
1/2 fresh tomato, chopped
1 lime, sliced

Directions:
Saute onion in butter and olive oil.
Add shrimp and jalapeno peppers and saute until shrimp are just pink.
Spritz with lime slices, add cilantro and saute till soft.
Add tomato and heat thru.
Serve over rice.
