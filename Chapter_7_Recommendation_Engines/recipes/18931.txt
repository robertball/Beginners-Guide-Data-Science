Title:
Orange Cream Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/631758.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup orange juice
1/2 cup apple juice
3 tablespoons honey
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 tablespoons nonfat dry milk powder
1 banana, sliced
2 cubes ice

Directions:
In a blender combine orange juice, apple juice, honey, vanilla, milk powder, banana and ice cubes. Blend on high speed for 30 seconds. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Ann
Description: 
This is a great easy recipe which could be a delicious refresher or a breakfast meal!
