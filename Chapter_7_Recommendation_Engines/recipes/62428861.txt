Title:
Fresh Veggie Panini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices of mulitgrain bread
a small bunch of basil leafs
2 slices of cucumber
2 slices of tomatoe
1/4 of a chopped yellow onion
1-2 slices of provolone cheese
EVOO
Light Mayo
Sea Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Begin by adding a drizzle of EVOO to a frying pan and add in the basil leafs. Mix in the sea salt and pepper at let cook on medium heat for 3-5 minutes. While the basil is cooking start to put together the sandwich. Lightly drizzle EVOO to one side of the 2 slices of bread. Begin then to layer with the cucumbers, tomatoes, onions, basil leafs and cheese. Add a the light mayo and sea salt and pepper. Cook on a panini maker or frying pan for 3-5 minutes and ENJOY!
