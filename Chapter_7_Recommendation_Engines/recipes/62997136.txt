Title:
Hamburger veg-all Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/789778.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
2 cups chicken broth
2 cups water
1 (1 ounce) packages dry onion soup mix
1/2 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 (15 ounce) can veg-all
1/2 cup elbow macaroni

Directions:
In a large stock pot, brown ground beef, over medium heat, drain grease.  Add to pot broth, water, onion soup mix,and tomato sauce use emulsifier to blend in onion soup mix. Add vegetables and macaroni.  Bring soup to a boil, simmer until macaroni is done and serve.
