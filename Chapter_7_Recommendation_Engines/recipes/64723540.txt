Title:
Sweet Potatoe Cassarole Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6028962.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 -3 cups cooked mashed sweet potatoes.
1/4 cup butter; melted
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup granulated white sugar
1/4 tsp. vanilla extract
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 cup butter
1/2 pecans; coarse chopped
1 tbsp finely chopped green onion/chives for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Grease a 9x13 baking dish.
In a large bowl, mix potatoes, 1/4 cup melted butter, milk, white sugar, vanilla extract and egg.
In a small bowl, mix together brown sugar and flour. Cut in 1/4 cup butter until mixture is crumbly; the add chopped pecans.
Spread potatoe mixture into the greased baking dish; then top with brown sugar, pecan mixture.
Bake for approximately 25 mins or until golden brown.
Remove and allow to set 5 mins then cut into squares.
Note: I cut this traditional Cassarole in half because of all the other food served during the holiday day dishes....moderation respected!
