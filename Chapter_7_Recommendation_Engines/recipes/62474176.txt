Title:
Roasted Red Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/481052.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound red potatoes, quartered
2 tablespoons diced onion (can be fresh or dried)
2 tablespoons diced garlic (can be fresh or dried)
1 pinch ground black pepper
Chopped fresh basil, parsley, cilantro, thyme or herb(s) of your choice, can be dried as well
Olive oil or spray PAM, etc.

Directions:
Place everything in a pan with enough water to cover potatoes, and boil with medium heat until potatoes are fork tender.
Drain with strainer with small holes so as to keep potatoes along with added spices and herbs.
Empty strainer onto baking tray, arranging potatoes skin side down. Spray with a bit of olive oil, PAM or other coating of your choice, and broil for 5-8 minutes or until browned.
