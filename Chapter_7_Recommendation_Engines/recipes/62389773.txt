Title:
Cheese puff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 slices white bread
3 eggs
1 1/2 C milk
1 t dried parsley
1/2 t dry mustard
1/2 t salt
1 C shredded cheddar
1 t dried minced onion

Directions:
Heat oven to 325Â°.
Cut bread into cubes (remove crusts id not desired).
Beat eggs, milk and seasonings.
Stir in bread cubes, cheese and onion.
Pour into ungreased square Pyrex dish.
Bake uncovered 1 hour.
