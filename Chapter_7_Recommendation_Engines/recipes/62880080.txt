Title:
Spicy Herb Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbs Spanish Paprika
1/2 C plus 3Tbs Champagne vinegar
1 1/2 C extra virgin olive oil
1/2 C chopped flat-leaf parsley
1/4 C finely chopped oregano
2 Tbs minced chives
1 Tsp red pepper flakes
Kosher salt
Freshly ground black pepper.

Directions:
Using a blender, food process,or hand/stick blender, combine the paprika and vinegar. While machine is running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until blended smooth. Transfer to a bowl and whisk in the herbs and red pepper flakes. Season generously with salt and pepper to taste.
