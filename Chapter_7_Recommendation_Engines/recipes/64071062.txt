Title:
Snickerdoodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cookies
1 cup butter, softened
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 eggs
2 3/4 cups flour
tsp. cream of tartar
1 tsp. soda
1/4 tsp. salt
Topping
2 TBSP sugar
2 TBSP cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Cream together butter, sugar and eggs. Add dry ingredients, mix thoroughly. Mix topping ingredients together.
Roll in small balls the size of walnut. Roll these in a cinnamon, sugar mixture. Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake.
