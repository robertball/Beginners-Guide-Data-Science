Title:
Candied Yams with Pineapple and Mandarin Oranges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2895466.jpg
Ingredients:
2     40 oz. Cans Yams (Sweet Potatoes)
1    20 oz. Can Crushed Pineapple in Syrup
1   12 oz. Can Mandarin Oranges in Syrup
1/2 Cup Butter (Salted)
1/2 Cup Golden Brown Sugar
1 Can Orange Juice Concentrate (Thawed)
1 Bag Mini Marshmallows
Ground Cinnamon
Nutmeg

Directions:
Drain yams. In a large baking dish, layer yams to cover bottom of dish. Sprinkle generously (to taste) with ground cinnamon and nutmeg. Cut pats of butter and layer over yams. Spoon half of your pineapple chunks and mandarin orange pieces onto yams then pour half of orange juice over everything. Top with brown sugar. Create another layer of yams and repeat process, topping with brown sugar. Bake at 350 degrees for approximately 30 minutes or until bubbling. Top with marshmallows and allow them to golden. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Shannon Cameron
Description: 
A decadent holiday tradition of candied sweet potatoes with pineapple and oranges, often considered a dessert and wonderful served warm with vanilla ice cream.
