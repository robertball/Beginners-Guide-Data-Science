Title:
Michelle’s Blue Ribbon Banana Nut  Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups All - Purpose Flour (Sifted)
1 Teaspoon  Sea Salt
2 Teaspoons Baking Soda
1 Cup White Sugar
1 Cup Light Brown Sugar
1/8 Teaspoon Nutmeg
1 Teaspoon Baking Powder
1 Teaspoons All-Spice
1 Teaspoon Cinnamon
1 Cup Of Butter Melted
6 Large Over-Ripe Mashed Bananas
4 Eggs
2 teaspoons Vanilla
1 Cup Chopped Pecans
½ Cup Chopped Pecans (For Topping)

Directions:
Preheat Oven To 350 Degrees. Grease Two Loaf Pans.  Whisk All Of The Dry Ingredients Together EXCEPT SUGAR! Crème Butter And Sugar Together Until Smooth. Using A Small Handheld Electric Mixer On Low Incorporate Bananas And Then  Eggs 1 At A Time, Pour In Vanilla. Slowly Dump The Dry Mixture Into The Wet Ingredients In Several Small Steps Mixing Thoroughly. Stop Mixing And Fold In 1 Cup Of The Pecans.   Pour Batter Into The Two Loaf Pans. Dump Remaining Pecans On Top Of Loaves. Bake For 50 Minutes . Let Loaves Cool About 15 Minutes. Serve With Butter. Wrap Leftovers  In Saran Wrap. (If There Are Any) Or Store In Airtight Container In Fridge
