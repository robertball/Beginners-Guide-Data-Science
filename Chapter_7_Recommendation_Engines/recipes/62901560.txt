Title:
Fish Tacos Ultimo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/817894.jpg
Ingredients:
FISH
2 tablespoons lime juice, divided
2 pounds tilapia fillets, raw
2 teaspoons table salt
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon paprika powder
cooking spray
.
CILANTRO-LIME SAUCE
1/2 cup plain fat-free yogurt
1/2 tablespoon canned chipotle peppers
1 1/2 tablespoons cilantro, chopped
2 tablespoons lime juice
.
TOPPINGS
2 medium scallions, chopped
1 medium tomato, chopped
1 medium avocado, peeled/pitted and sliced
1/2 cup salsa
2 cups shredded cabbage
1 cup monterey jack or cheddar cheese, shredded
15 small corn tortillas

Directions:
Rub lime juice into tilapia fillets.  Season fillets with salt, pepper, garlic powder and paprika.  Spray both sides of each fillet with cooking spray and set aside.
Start bbq grill.  As grill warms up make the cilantro lime sauce by combining the yogurt, chipotle peppers, cilantro and lime juice in a blender.  Pulse until well blended.
Chop green onions and tomato.  Peel, pit and slice avocado.  Set prepared veggies, cilantro lime sauce, salsa, shredded cabbage and shredded cheese out to make a taco assembly line.
Grill fish on BBQ.  Warm tortillas in hot skillet.
Have your guests make their own wonderful fish tacos.
