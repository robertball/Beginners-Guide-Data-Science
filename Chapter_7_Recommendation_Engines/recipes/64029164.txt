Title:
Walnut & Blackberry Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
2/3 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup finely ground toasted walnuts
1/2 cup seedless blackberry preserves
Mix together;
1 cup  sugar
1 cup toasted walnuts, finely ground

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium bowl, cream together butter and white sugar until smooth. Mix in 1/2 teaspoon vanila extract.  Mix in flour until dough comes together. Roll dough into 1 1/2 inch balls, roll in sugar, walnut mixture and place on ungreased cookie sheets. Make a small hole in the center of each ball, using your finger, and fill the hole with preserves.
Bake for 11 to 12 minutes in preheated oven, or until lightly browned. Let cool 1 minute on the cookie sheet.
