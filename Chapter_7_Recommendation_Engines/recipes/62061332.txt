Title:
Raisin Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups sifted all-purpose flour
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup sugar
2 teaspoons double-acting baking powder
2 eggs
1/4 cup melted butter
1 cup milk
3 cups raisins

Directions:
1. Prehaet oven to 400 degrees F.
2. Sift together: flour, salt, sugar and baking powder.
3. beat in a seperate bowl: eggs, butter and milk.
4. Combine liquid and dry ingredients.
5. fold in raisins.
6. Fill well greased muffin tin and bake for 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Graca Jeitada
Description: 
Very good muffins with LOTS of raisins.
