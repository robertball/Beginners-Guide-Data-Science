Title:
Tammy's Beanie Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1.3 lbs. of Ground chuck
1 small to medium onion finely chopped
1  15.5 oz. can diced tomatoes
1  15.5 oz.  Dark Red Kidney Beans
1   6 oz can tomato past.
3  15.5 oz. cans of H20
1.5 tsp salt
1 tsp garlic powder
Fresh ground black pepper to taste.
1.5-2 Tabs dried oregano
4-5 handfuls of pasta  (rotini or similar)

Directions:
Brown meat in 4 quart pot.
As meat turns gray but before it starts to brown add the onion and continue to brown meat stirring frequently. When browned to your liking drain grease if needed.
Add  tomatoes, beans, tomato paste,  water,  garlic powder, oregano,  salt and pepper
Stir frequently and bring to a boil and add pasta.
Turn to simmer, cover  and cook 12-15 minutes checking frequently and adding water as needed.
If still to watery, after 12 minutes, leave cover off to thicken.
Adjust seasonings if needed and serve.
