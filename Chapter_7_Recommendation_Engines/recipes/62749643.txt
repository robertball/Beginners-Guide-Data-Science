Title:
Memorable Mai Tai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/633448.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C sugar
3 C water
1 can frozen limeade
1/2 C Bacardi white rum
1/2 C Bacardi Gold dark rum
1/2 C Amaretto
1/4 C Grand Marnier

Directions:
Dissolve sugar in water over low heat.  Remove from heat.
Add limeade and all liquor.  Mix well.  Pour into glass pitcher and refrigerate for 2-3 hours, or pour into bowl and freeze.
Serve over ice, or scoop frozen concoction, into cocktail glasses.

Submitter: 
la rosita resort and spa
Description: 
Living in Hawaii at Ko O'lina for a decade, my Mai Tai recipe is as authentic as it is tasty. 'Ono-liciuos!
