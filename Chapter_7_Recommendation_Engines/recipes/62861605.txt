Title:
Cherry Tomatos Egg Salad Tapas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/685348.jpg
Ingredients:
•20 small tomatoes,
•8 hard-boiled eggs, cooled and peeled
•6 tablespoons mayonnaise with Lime
pepper
•1 tablespoon parsley, chopped
1 tablespoon capers

Directions:
Preparation
Skin the tomatoes, first by cutting out the core with a sharp knife and making a '+' incision on the other end of the tomato. Then place in a pan of boiling water for 10 seconds, remove and plunge into a bowl of iced or very cold water.
Mash the eggs with the mayonnaise, capers, pepper and parsley. 1/4 the tomatoes but don't cut all the way through. Stuff the tomatoes, firmly pressing the filling down. Sprinkle with chopped parsley. Keep refrigerated until ready to serve.
