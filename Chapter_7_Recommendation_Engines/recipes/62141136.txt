Title:
Turkey and Yam Spicey Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb. ground turkey, browned
1 tbsp olive oil
sea salt and ground pepper to season
1/2 cup chopped sweet onion
1-2 cloves garlic, minced
4 med jalapenos, seeded and finely chopped with membrane removed
1 medium yam, pre-cooked and cubed
1 tbsp chili powder
1 tsp cummin
1/2 cup green salsa (made with tomatillos)
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cajun spice
water for saute
1/2 cup loose chopped cilantro (more if desired)
Flour tortillas

Directions:
Brown turkey in 1/2 tbsp olive oil, salt and pepper to taste, set aside
In same pan saute onion, jalapenos and garlic in remaining olive oil until onions begin to carmelize using med. heat.
Add spices, cubed yam and green chili salsa.  Allow to heat through until extra liquid is evaporated.
Warm tortillas and fill with mixture and serve.
Garnish with sliced advacado or sour cream.

Submitter: 
Fooddude
Description: 
Spicey tacos with low fat turkey that have sweet-hot flavor, using yams to give it a surprising twist.
