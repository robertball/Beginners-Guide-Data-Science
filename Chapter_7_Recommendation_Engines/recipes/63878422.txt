Title:
Sandies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1/3 cup sugar
2 tsp. vanilla
2 cups flour
1 cup chopped pecans (optional)
1 cup powdered sugar

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar.  Add water and vanilla. Blend in flour and nuts.  Shape into balls.  Bake at 325 degrees.
Remove from oven and cool.  Roll cookies in powdered sugar.  For the holidays put some green food coloring in your dough to make the cookies festive for the season!

Submitter: 
Melissa Vincent
Description: 
These cookies are very light and airy and not too sweet.  Great with a cup of coffee!
