Title:
Brigadeiro Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa
1 tablespoon butter
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over medium heat, combine cocoa, butter and condensed milk.  Cook, stirring, until thickened, about 10 minutes.  Cook until you boiling and caramel-like.  The batter should pull away from the pan when you tilt it.  Stir continuously. when done cooking, pour it into a lightly buttereddish and cool until it can be handled.  roll into balls then roll in chocolate sprinkles, superfine sugar, coco powder, coconut, crushed nuts, whatever floats your boat.

Submitter: 
Kristi Francis
Description: 
Cocoa, butter and condensed milk are cooked, allowed to cool and formed into little balls for easy eating.Modified from: Brigadeiro
