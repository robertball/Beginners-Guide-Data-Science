Title:
Coconut Jam Tarts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 (2 inch) frozen mini tart shells
.
2 eggs
1/2 cup melted butter
1/3 cup Splenda
4 teaspoons evaporated milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups flaked coconut
4 tablespoons raspberry jam
.
1 cup heavy cream, whipped with sugar and vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Place frozen mini tart shells on a baking sheet.
Beat the eggs until whites are incorporated and are no longer ropey.
Beat in the butter, Splenda, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract, mixing until fully combined.
Stir in the coconut.
Place 1/2 teaspoon of jam into each mini tart shell, and fill the shells with about 1 tablespoon of the coconut mixture.
Bake in the preheated oven until the shells and topping are lightly golden brown, about 20 minutes.
Cool on wire rack.
