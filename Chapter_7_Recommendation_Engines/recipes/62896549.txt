Title:
Butternut Squash Cajun Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and sliced into thick style french fries
1 tsp salt (or to taste)
1/4 tsp pepper (or to taste)
1/2 tsp cajun seasoning (or to taste)
Cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Blot out any moisture in the fries with a paper towl.
Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and cajun seasoning.
Bake for 15 to 20 minutes on a baking sheet sprayed with cooking spray, turning once.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
You'll love these yummy, salty, spicy, slightly sweet healthy alternative to deep fried french fries.  Serve with ketchup or your favorite dipping sauce.
