Title:
Thanksgiving leftover stuffing mushroom caps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp butter
1 8 oz baby Portabello or white mushrooms, cleaned and stems removed
leftover stuffing
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat broiler
heat oil and butter in a  large skillet until foamy. Add msuhrooms and gently saute until they begin to soften and brown about 5 minutes.Remove from heat.
Place mushrooms top side down in a signle layer in a baking pan or cookie sheet. Mound leftover dressing in teh center of each cap. Sprinkle with parmesan chseese and broil until hot and bubbly about 2 minutes.
