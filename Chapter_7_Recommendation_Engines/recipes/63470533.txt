Title:
Frosted Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup saltine cracker crumbs
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
2 eggs lightly beaten
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup minced onion
1/2 teaspoon ground sage
FROSTING
3 tablespoon brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 cup ketchup
1 teaspoon prepared mustard

Directions:
In large bowl combine cracker crumbs, ground beef, eggs, pepper, salt, milk, onion and sage. Mix well. Turn into a 8x4 loaf pan.
Mix brown sugar, nutmeg, ketchup and mustard. Spread frosting over meat loaf. Bake at 350 for 1 1/4 hours.
