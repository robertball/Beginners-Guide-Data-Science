Title:
Fried Cinnamon Strips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983117.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
10 (8 inch) flour tortillas
vegetable oil

Directions:
In a large resealable plastic bag, combine sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg; set aside.
Cut tortillas into 3-in. x 2-in. strips. Heat 1 in. of oil in a skillet or electric fry pan to 375 degrees F; fry 4-5 strips at a time for 30 seconds on each side or until golden brown.  Drain on paper towels.
While still warm, place strips in bag with sugar mixture; shake gently to coat. Serve immediately or store in an airtight container.
