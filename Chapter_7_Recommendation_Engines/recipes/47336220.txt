Title:
Bacon Popover Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/388474.jpg
Ingredients:
2 large eggs
1 cup milk
1 Tbs bacon grease
1 cup shredded American cheese
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 tsp salt
5 strips bacon, cooked and crumbled

Directions:
Place a pan (large enough to hold the loaf pan) in a 400 F oven, half filled HOT with water.
Coat the inside of a loaf pan lightly with oil, then flour the inside of the oiled pan.  Set aside.
Whisk eggs, milk and grease together.
Stir in shredded cheese
Mix in flour just until blended. DO NOT OVERMIX.
Pour batter into loaf pan.
Sprinke crumbled bacon over the batter evenly.
Place loaf pan into the pan with water, in the oven.
Bake at 400 F for 25-30 minutes, until gold brown.
Cool slightly and slice to serve.

Submitter: 
TLW
Description: 
A nice golden loaf for breakfast, light and moist, with cheese and bacon!
