Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2473657.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
5 large marshmallows
2 tablespoons whipped cream cheese
18-20 small pretzels
1/4 cup melted chocolate

Directions:
1. Melt butter in a microwaveable bowl.  2. Add marshmallows, and make sure they are completely coated in butter. 3. Microwave marshmallows and butter for 30 seconds, or until marshmallows are completely melted. 4. Add cream cheese to marshmallow mixture, still until smooth. 5. Refrigerate mixture until firm. 6. Scoop out small dollops of the cream cheese/marshmallow mixture, and sandwich between two pretzels. 7. Dip sandwiches in melted chocolate, and return to the fridge until chocolate has hardened.
