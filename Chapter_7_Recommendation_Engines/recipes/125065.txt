Title:
Sausage Cheese Bites Snack Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pound mild pork sausage
4 cups buttermilk biscuit dry mix
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 cup water

Directions:
Crumble sausage into a skillet; cook over medium heat until no longer pink. Drain.
In a large bowl, combine biscuit mix and cheese. Add the sausage and stir until well blended. Stir in water just until mixed. Shape into 1-1/2-in. balls.
Place on greased baking sheets. Bake at 375 degrees F for about 15 minutes or until golden.

Submitter: 
Nancy  Reichert
Description: 
'These savory little bites are such a tasty snack to serve for a get-together or as an evening surprise for the family,' reports Nancy Reichert of Thomasville, Georgia. 'They also make a nice breakfast treat with a thermos of hot coffee if you're on your way early in the morning.'
