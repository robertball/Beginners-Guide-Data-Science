Title:
The Perfect Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. lean ground beef
3/4 cup ketchup
1/2 teaspoon each, salt and pepper
1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
1 egg, beaten
3/4 cup dry oatmeal
2 teaspoons prepared mustard
TOPPING:
1/2 cup Tomato ketchup
2 teaspoons prepared mustard
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 tablespoons cider vinegar

Directions:
Combine all meatloaf ingredients. Mix thoroughly. Press mixture into loaf pan.  Smooth topping over meatloaf.  B ake at 400 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes. Drain off fat.  Let stand at least 5 minutes before slicing.
TOPPING:
In a small microwave safe bowl, combine all ingredients; mix well.  Place in microwave for 15 to 20 seconds, heating just until sugar is dissolved.

Submitter: 
Gail Cobile
