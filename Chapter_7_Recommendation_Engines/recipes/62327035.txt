Title:
Breakfast Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/958853.jpg
Ingredients:
9- 12 Eggs
2 C Meat (ham, bacon, sausage)
3/4 C Onion chopped
2 1/2 C Milk
2 C Cheddar(shredded)
1 tsp dry Mustard
1 tsp Salt
1 tsp Pepper
6 C cubed French or Italian bread

Directions:
Cook bacon, sausage or ham. Beat eggs, add rest of ingredients to a greased 9x13 pan, cover and refrigerate overnight. Bake 375F for 45 minutes or until knife comes out clean
