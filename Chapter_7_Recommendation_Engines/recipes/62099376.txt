Title:
cinnamon toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 tbsp sugar
4 tbsp cinnamon
thickly sliced bread
soft butter

Directions:
while you toast the bread mix cinnamon and sugar in a cup.
spread butter on the hot toast and sprinkle on cinnamon mixture.
eat and save any leftover mixture for next time.

Submitter: 
sasa
Description: 
this is the quickest, easiest snack anyone could possibly want. I make a cup and keep it handy for when ever i am peckish.
