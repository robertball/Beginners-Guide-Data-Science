Title:
Hurricane Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/692967.jpg
Ingredients:
2 oz grenadine
1 ½ oz light (silver) rum
1 ½ oz dark (gold) rum
1 ½ oz orange juice
1 ½ oz pineapple juice
½ oz triple sec
½ oz lime juice

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a cocktail shaker or glass with ice and shake or stir well. Fill a tall glass with crushed ice and pour drink over ice. Garnish with an orange and a maraschino cherry.

Submitter:

Description: 
It's a tropical drink and a bad storm. Live through one and you'll really want some of the other. From the BlogHer blog My Life As A Mrs. Friday, August 26, 2011.
