Title:
Refrigerator Door Marinated Pork Butt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon minced garlic
1 Tablespoon soy sauce
1 cup apple cider
3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup Italian-style salad dressing
1/3 cup distilled white vinegar
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon onion powder
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon Creole seasoning
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1/3 cup brown sugar
8 pounds pork shoulder
enough water to cover Pork

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix soy sauce, apple juice, Worcestershire sauce, Italian-style salad dressing, distilled white vinegar, garlic powder, salt, onion powder, Cajun seasoning, crushed red pepper and brown sugar.
Score pork shoulder 1/2 inch deep. Place in the bowl with the marinade mixture. Marinate at least 4 hours in the refrigerator.
Preheat an outdoor grill for medium high heat and lightly oil grate.
Cook marinated pork shoulder on the prepared grill 3 hours, or until the internal temperature has reach a minimum of 165 degrees F (75 degrees C). Marinate once or twicewith the mixture while grilling.  Reduce any leftover marinade into a glaze to serve with pork.
