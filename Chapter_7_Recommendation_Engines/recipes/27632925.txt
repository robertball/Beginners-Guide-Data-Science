Title:
Corn and Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped red onion
1TBS. Olive Oil
4 cups fresh corn kernels
2 cups cherry tomatoes, cut in half and de-stemmed
1/4 cup slivered fresh basil leaves
3 TBS. Sherry vinegar or red wine vinegar
salt and pepper
2 ounces of fresh chevre (goat cheese) crumbled.

Directions:
Over medium heat stir onion in olive oil until limp.  Add corn and stir often, just until tender to bite.
Pour mixture into a wide serving bowl and stir in tomatoes, basil and vinegar.  Add salt and pepper.
Sprinkle goat cheese over salad- serve warm or at room temperature.
