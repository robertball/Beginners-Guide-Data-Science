Title:
Icing Glue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 c. powdered sugar
1/8 tsp. cream of tartar
1 egg white

Directions:
Stir together powdered sugar and cream of tartar.  Beat in egg white at low speed until well blended.  Increase speed to medium, beat 5 minutes or until icing is thick, creamy and holds stiff peaks.

Submitter: 
Traci Caha Myers
Description: 
This is great for holding together gingerbread houses!
