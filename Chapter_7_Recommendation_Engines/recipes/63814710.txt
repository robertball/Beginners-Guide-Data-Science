Title:
Bill's Marrakesh Vegetable Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 sweet potato, peeled and cubed
1 medium eggplant, cubed
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
2 carrots, quartered and chopped
1 onion, chopped
9 tablespoons olive oil
6 cloves garlic, minced
2 teaspoons ground turmeric
2 tablespoons curry powder
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon sea salt
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
1/2 cup blanched almonds
1 zucchini, quartered and chopped
1/2 cups raisins
1 to 1-1/2 cups orange juice
10 ounces fresh spinach

Directions:
In a large Dutch oven place sweet potato, eggplant, peppers, carrots, onion, and three tablespoons oil. Saute over medium heat for 5 minutes.
In a medium saucepan place 3 tablespoons olive oil, garlic, turmeric, curry powder, cinnamon, salt and pepper and saute over medium heat for 3 minutes.
Pour garlic and spice mixture into the Dutch oven with vegetables in it. Add the garbanzo beans, almonds, zucchini, raisins, and orange juice. Simmer 20 minutes, covered.
Add spinach to pot and cook for 5 more minutes. Serve!

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
This curry is full of veggies: sweet potato, eggplant, spinach, zucchini, red and green bell peppers, carrots, and onion. It's mixed with blanched almonds, chickpeas, raisins, and spices. Serve with brown rice or couscous.Modified from: Marrakesh Vegetable Curry
