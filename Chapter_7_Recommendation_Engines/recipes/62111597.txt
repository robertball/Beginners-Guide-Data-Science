Title:
English Toffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Sugar
1 Cup Butter
1 Tablespoon Vanilla
12 oz Milk Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Melt butter over medium heat. Add sugar and stir until melted.  Add vanilla.  Stirring constantly, bring to a boil over medium-medium-high heat.  Allow to boil for 10 minutes stirring constantly until mixture reaches the hard-ball stage.  Immediately pour into a greased jelly-roll pan and spread.  Top with chocolate chips and spread until melted.  Allow to cool.
