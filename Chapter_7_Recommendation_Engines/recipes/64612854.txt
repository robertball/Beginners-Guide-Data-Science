Title:
Sylvia's Catsup Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6040850.jpg
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken pieces
3 cloves of garlic chopped
1 cup of ketchup
3 large onions chopped

Directions:
Place the chicken pieces in a roasting pan
Saute onions and garlic
cover the chicken with onion and garlic
cover the chicken with  1 1/2 cup of water and
1 cup of ketchup
Bake 325 uncovered 1 1/2 hrs.
