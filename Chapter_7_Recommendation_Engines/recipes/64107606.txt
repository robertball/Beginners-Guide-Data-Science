Title:
Beet Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1255104.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T olive oil
4-6 garlic cloves, chopped
1 yellow onion, chopped
2 beets, chopped/sliced
1 apple, chopped/sliced
3-4 c veg or chicken stock (or more to desired consistency)
Salt
Lemon juice
Ginger (omit next time)
parsley for serving
goat cheese or yogurt for serving (optional)

Directions:
saute onion, garlic in olive oil in large stock/soup pan
add beets, apple, (ginger omit next time), salt, cook until soft
add stock
simmer 20-30 min
blend to desired consistency
add salt, lemon, parsley and/or goat cheese to serve
