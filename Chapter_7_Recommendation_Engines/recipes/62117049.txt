Title:
Berryweiss beer bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 cup sugar
1 1/2 tablespoons double-acting baking powder
2 teaspoons salt
3 large eggs, beaten
1 1/2 cups  leininkugels berryweiss
1/4 cup berry preserves of choice(seedless rasberry recomended)
1/3 cup vegetable oil or applesauce

Directions:
In a large bowl stir together the flour, the sugar, the baking powder and the salt. In a bowl whisk together the eggs, beer, and the oil or applesauce and stir the mixture into the flour mixture, stirring the batter until it is just moistened. Pour the batter into a greased loaf pan, 11 by 4 1/2 by 2 3/4 inches, or 9 by 5 by 3 inches, place in the middle of a preheated 350Â°F oven for 1 hour to 1 hour and 10 minutes,Let the bread cool in the pan on a rack for 15 minutes, then turn it out onto the rack, and let cool completely. Ennjoy.  goes well with pot roast
