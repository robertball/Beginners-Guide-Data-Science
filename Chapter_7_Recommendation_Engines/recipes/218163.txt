Title:
Cranberry Peanut Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/780503.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup JIF® Creamy Peanut Butter & Honey
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
4 cups honey and nut flavor checked cereal
1 cup honey roasted peanuts
1 cup pretzels (such as midgets or snaps)
1/2 cup sunflower seeds
1 (6 ounce) package dried cranberries

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
Combine butter, peanut butter, vanilla and cinnamon in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on HIGH (100% power) 35 to 40 seconds; stir until well blended.
Mix cereal, peanuts, pretzels, sunflower kernels and cranberries in a large bowl. Pour butter mixture over cereal mixture; toss well to coat.
Line sheet pan with foil; spread mixture evenly across pan. Bake 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally; cool. Store in resealable food storage bag.

Submitter: 
Jif®
Description: 
Peanuts, pretzels, sunflower kernels, and dried cranberries with a peanut-honey, cinnamon coating bake up for a crunchy snack-time treat.
