Title:
Cindy's Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/905251.jpg
Ingredients:
5 Tablespoons butter
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of celery soup
2 cups chicken broth
1 envelope dry onion soup mix
1 1/2 cups uncooked rice
1 cut up chicken, bone in

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large microwave safe bowl melt the butter in the microwave.  Mix in the undiluted soups, onion soup mix and chicken broth.  Mix well then add the rice.
Place about 1/3 of the rice/soup mix in a sprayed 9x13 inch casserole dish.  Place chicken pieces into soup mixture and pour over the rest of the rice/soup mix.
Cover completely with foil and bake for aproximately 1 1/2 to 2 hours, until the rice is soft and all the liquid has been absorbed.
