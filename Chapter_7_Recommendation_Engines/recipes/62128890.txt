Title:
Low carb coleslaw dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup olive oil
3/4 cup Splenda
1/2 cup vinegar
1 tbs celery seed
1 tsp salt
1 minced

Directions:
blend in blender all ingredients except celery seed
add celery seed
chill well and let age overnight

Submitter: 
Jackie B.
Description: 
This dressing is a great and healthy replacement for coleslaw dressing, made with Splenda for a low carb option. For regular slaw, replace Splenda with regular sugar
