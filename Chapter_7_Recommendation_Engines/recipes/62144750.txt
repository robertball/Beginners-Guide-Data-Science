Title:
Gluten Free Crustless Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
1/4 tsp ground cloves
1/8 tsp ground ginger
1 1/4 cups evaporated or condensed milk
1 15 oz can pumpkin
pecans (optional)

Directions:
Stir eggs, brown sugar and spices until blended. Add milk and pumpkin. Stir until mixture is smooth and creamy.  Pour into a 9 inch glass pie pan. Sprinkle with whole or chopped pecans (optional). Bake at 400 for 15 minutes.  Reduce temperature to 350 and bake for an additional 30-40 minutes.  Cool at least one hour then enjoy.

Submitter: 
Anita Schoeb
Description: 
Gluten free crustless pumpkin pie.  Very nutritious, delicious and easy to make.
