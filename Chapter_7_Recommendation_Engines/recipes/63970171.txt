Ingredients:
-10 medium russet potatoes
-3 tablespoons of olive oil
-1/4 cup of butter
-1 medium sweet onion
-about 3 tablespoons of szeged paprika (make sure it is szeged or this will not taste right) You might be able to get away with a sweet Spanish paprika).

Directions:
Cut potatoes into even sized cubes and boil until still tender and not mush. Then sweat sweet onion in olive oil and butter; brown if you like for more flavour. After onion is browned sprinkle generous amounts of paprika on it and then mix into cooked potatoes. mix. Add more of the paprika on top and mix more. Add more butter if needed.
