Title:
RyMan's Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped onions
1/2 cup real bacon bits
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup ketchup
2 TBSP Vinegar
1 TBSP Mustard
1 tsp salt
1 can Butter Beans (15 1/2 oz)
2 cans Pork & Beans
1 can Kidney Beans
1 can Black Beans

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in crock pot. Do not drain beans.
Cook 4-6 hours
