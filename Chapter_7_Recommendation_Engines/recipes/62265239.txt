Title:
Italian Chicken & Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/342989.jpg
Ingredients:
Chicken     4 Medium large boneless skinless breasts
Peppers     1/2 cup total (Red, orange, yellow)
Garlic      3 cloves
Asparagus   1 lb (Just the top half cut into bite sized pieces)
Oregano     to taste (fresh) I used about 1/2 Tblsp
Basil       to taste (fresh) I used about 1/2 Tblsp
Parsley     to taste (fresh italian) I used about 1/2 Tblsp
Rosemary    to taste (fresh)  I used about 1/2 Tblsp
Lemon Juice 2 Tblsp
White Wine  1/4 cup
Tomato      1 15 oz can (diced)
Olive oil   6 Tblsp

Directions:
Cut chicken into bite size pieces. Keep ingredients below separate.Dice Peppers, Mince Garlic.  Mince herbs. In a large skillet, add Olive Oil and heat on high until very hot. (Just below smoking).  Cook chicken in batches until light brown.  Reduce heat to medium and add an additional small amount of olive oil, add garlic, Peppers and Asparagus and cook for 3 minutes.  Add browned Chicken and herbs.  Let cook for 4-5 minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink inside.  Add Lemon juice, wine and tomatoes.  Cook for 1 minute.  Serve over Angel Hair pasta.

Description: 
This is my take on a delicious chicken recipe.  I dreamed up this recipe one day when I was tired of doing the same old chicken recipes all the time.  It is light and very flavorful.
