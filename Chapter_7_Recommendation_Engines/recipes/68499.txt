Title:
Fish Benedict Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986842.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.1 ounce) package Gorton's® Original Battered Tenders
1 (6 ounce) package Canadian bacon
1 packet Hollandaise sauce mix
1 (12 ounce) package English muffins

Directions:
Bake Tenders according to the directions on the package.
Prepare Hollandaise sauce as directed. Cook bacon as directed. Toast English muffins.
Place 1 piece of bacon and 2 tenders on English muffin and spoon Hollandaise on top. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Gorton's®
Description: 
Try Gorton's Original Battered Tenders in place of egg on your next Benedict for fantastic dish that is sure to impress!
