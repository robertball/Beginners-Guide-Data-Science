Title:
No Mayo Easy Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz box rotini
1 12 oz box tri-colored rotini
1 cucumber, chopped
6 roma tomatoes, chopped
1/2 cup of purple onion, chopped (optional)
1 16 oz bottle of ranch dressing
1 16 oz bottle of italian dressing

Directions:
Cook pasta and cool
Combine pasta, cucumber, tomatoes, purple onion in a large bowl.
In a separate bowl, mix together ranch and Italian, then spread over and mix into pasta.
Chill until serving.

Submitter: 
mhassler
Description: 
A friend of mine showed me how to make this. It's incredibly EASY and you'll have so many people asking for the recipe! This is also a great pasta salad if you're looking for something without mayo.
