Title:
Fig and Nut Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 c all purpose flour
1 envelope dry yeast
1 tsp fennel seeds
1/4 tsp salt
4 large eggs, seperated
1/2 c plus 2 tbsp sugar
1/4 c unsalted butter, melted, cooled
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 c walnuts, toasted, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F. Butter and flour 9-in springform pan. Mix first 4 ingredients.  Using electric mixer, beat egg yolks and 1/2 c sugar in large bowl until thick and pale yellow, about 3 minutes. Gradually beat in melted butter, then vanilla. Stir in flour mixture, then figs and 1/2 c walnuts.
Using clean dry beaters, beat egg whites until soft peaks form. Gradually add remaining 2 tbsp sugar and beat until stiff, but not dry. Stir 1/3 of whites into batter to loosen.  Fold in remaining whites. Transfer to prepared pan (layer will be thin.)
Bake cake until tester inserted into center comes out clean, about 30 min. Cool cake in pan on rack. Release pan sides. Place cake on platter. Garnish cake with additional chopped walnuts.
