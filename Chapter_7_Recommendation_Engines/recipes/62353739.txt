Title:
Grilled Teriyaki Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. chicken breasts
1/3 c. brown sugar
1/3 c. Asian Sesame Oil
1/3 c. soy sauce
1 oz. sesame seeds (optional)

Directions:
In skillet toast sesame seeds until golden, 3 to 4 minutes.  In dish combine oil, sugar and soy sauce, add sesame seeds.  Slice chicken breast in half length wise, dip into soy mixture to coat.  Place in broiler pan, 4 inches from heat for 3-4 minutes per side until no longer pink.  Serve with rice if desired.
