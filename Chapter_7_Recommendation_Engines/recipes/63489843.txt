Title:
Megan's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/952268.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground turkey
1/2 medium onion (finely chopped)
1 celery stalk (finely chopped)
1 cup crushed Ritz crackers
1 1/2 cup Organic Valley mild cheddar cheese (shredded)
1 (1.5 ounce) meatloaf seasoning package
2 eggs
6 oz can tomato paste (basil/oregano/garlic)
2 tsp worcestershire
3 Tbsp honey
2-3 slices bread

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Stir eggs, onion, celery, meatloaf spice packet, 2T honey, 3T tomato paste, 2/3 cup cheddar cheese crushed crackers,  1 tsp worcestershire in a bowl until well blended.  Add the ground turkey to the wet mixture. Line bottom of 9x5 inch pan with bread slices. Add turkey mixture and spread evenly.
Mix remaining tomato paste, 1T honey, 1 tsp worcestershire, italian seasoning, salt to taste. Top meatloaf. add remaining 2/3 C cheese.
Bake in preheated oven until the meatloaf reaches 160 degrees F (71 degrees C) and is no longer pink in the center, 60 minutes.
