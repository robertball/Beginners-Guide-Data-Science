Title:
Dee's Pesto Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
PESTO:
1/2 cup lightly toasted pine nuts
1 cup of Fresh Basil (min stems)
tbs of dry parmesean
4 tbs of fresh parmesean
2 tbs of Feta
approx. 5 tbs of extra virgin olive oil
4 med. garlic cloves
Salt & Pepper to taste
2 Med Cans of artichokes quartered
6 sun-dried tomatoes in oil (quartered)
1/2 cup of lightly toasted pine nuts
5 tbs of feta
3 tbs of fresh parmesean

Directions:
Cook Pasta as package directs.
Mix pesto ingredients in food processer. slowly add in the olive oil. add salt and pepper to taste.
Drizzle olive oil lightly over drained pastat noodles.
Add Pesto, artichoke hearts, sun dried tomatoes, cheeses, & Pine nuts to Pasta.
ENJOY!

Submitter: 
MIZCOOK
Description: 
This Recipe Adds the subtle but rich Flavors of artichoke hearts, Cheeses, & Basil Pesto. Even the pickiest of eaters come back for more! Fast Easy Recipe!
