The Best Dang Hushpuppies (in the style of Hyman's Seafood) Recipe

Ingredients:
2 lb. Hushpuppy mix
8 oz. Coca-Cola
Â½ lb. sugar
1 cup water (more if needed)
1 small onion (diced)
2 tbsp. honey

Directions:
In large bowl, combine hushpuppy mix, water, sugar, honey, and onion by hand
Slowly add beer while mixing ingredients.
Shape ingredients in small balls, a little smaller than a golf ball.
Cook in a deep fryer or a large pot with veg oil at 350 until golden brown.
Finally, place hushpuppies on a plate, covered w/paper towels.
