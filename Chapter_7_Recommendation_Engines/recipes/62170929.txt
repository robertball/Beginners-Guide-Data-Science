Title:
Simple Strawberry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/283560.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen strawberries
1 cup apple juice
1 cup vanilla yogurt

Directions:
Pour frozen strawberries, juice, and yogurt into the blender and blend until smooth. Optional: you can top with whipped cream.

Submitter: 
House of Aqua
Description: 
Fast and simple strawberry smoothie. I have used apple, peach, and grape juice in this recipe before and they are all work great! You can easily use organic ingredients if you prefer.
