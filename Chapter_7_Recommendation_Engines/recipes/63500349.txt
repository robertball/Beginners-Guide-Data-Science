Title:
Panfried Cinnamon Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/955648.jpg
Ingredients:
Cinnamon Sauce
1/2 c Brown Sugar
1tbsp Cinnamon
1 tsp Nutmeg
2tbsp Flour
3/4 c Maple Syrup
Batter Mix
1 1/2 c Flour
3/4 c white sugar
1/2 tsp baking soda
Pinch of salt
2 tsp Vanilla
1 egg
1-1 1/2 c milk

Directions:
Pre-Heat frying pan on medium heat for several minutes.
Butter frying pan.
Pour One ladle full of batter onto pan. Let cook for 10 seconds.
Now take a spoonful of cinnamon sauce and spiral over top of batter.
Let cook on this side until batter is bubbling evenly from edges to centre.
Let bubble for 10-20 seconds, and flip.
Let cook on flipped side for 20 seconds and remove from pan.
Serve saucy side up.
Repeat cooking instructions for each roll.
Makes about three large rolls.
