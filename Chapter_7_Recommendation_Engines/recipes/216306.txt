Title:
Slow Cooker Simple Beef Bourguignonne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978497.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Golden Mushroom Soup
1 cup Burgundy or other dry red wine
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon dried thyme leaves, crushed
6 ounces small whole mushrooms
2 cups fresh or thawed frozen whole baby-cut carrots
1 cup frozen small whole onions, thawed
1 (1 1/2 pound) boneless beef top round steak, 1 1/2 inches thick, cut into 1-inch pieces

Directions:
Stir the soup, wine, garlic, thyme, mushrooms, carrots, onions and beef in a 3 1/2-quart slow cooker.
Cover and cook on LOW for 8 to 9 hours or until the beef is fork-tender.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Pieces of beef top round get fork-tender when they're slow cooked together with red wine, golden mushroom soup and vegetables to make this savory and delicious stew.
