Title:
Rob's Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can beef consume'
1 soup can of water
1 package of onion soup mix
1 stick of butter
1 cup uncooked white rice
1 small can of mushrooms

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Throw all ingredients in a 2 quart buttered dish.  Bake for 1 hour. (Stir
once, half way through baking.)

Submitter: 
MADDIES MOM
Description: 
Tasty and easy throw together side dish that goes best with beef.  We always double the recipe, as it never lasts long!
