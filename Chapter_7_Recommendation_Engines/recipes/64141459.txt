Title:
Summer Fruit Clafoutis Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup blackberries
1 cup chopped rhubarb
3 eggs, room temperature
2 tbsp honey
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup almond flour
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
Lemon zest

Directions:
Wash and drain fruit.
Place fruit at bottom of a buttered pan.
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
Combine all other ingredients except eggs and blend (or beat).
Add in eggs one by one.
Beat till mixture is smooth.
Pour batter over fruit.
Shake out air bubbles.
Bake till clafoutis puffs out, for less than half an hour.
Let deflate, then chill in refrigerator. Cracks are normal.

Submitter: 
Crysteaux Sun
Description: 
Easy, oil-free, mess-free, and gorgeous way to highlight summer fruit.
