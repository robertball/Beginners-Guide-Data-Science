Title:
Cream Cheese Frosting II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup butter, softened
2 cups sifted confectioners' sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/8 tsp salt

Directions:
In a medium bowl, cream together the room temperature cream cheese and butter until creamy. Mix in the vanilla, then gradually stir in the confectioners' sugar. Store in the refrigerator after use.  May add mor3e confectioners sugar.

Submitter: 
Linda Peterson
Description: 
This is a wonderfully creamy frosting that goes well with pumpkin bread, carrot cake, chocolate cake, on cookies, or between cookies. If you want chocolate frosting, add 1/4 to 1/2 cup cocoa, according to how rich you want it.Modified from: Cream Cheese Frosting II
