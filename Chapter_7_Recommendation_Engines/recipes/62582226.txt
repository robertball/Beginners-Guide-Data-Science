Title:
Crock Pot Sweet Potatoes and Cranberries W/Toasted Pecans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 sweet potatoes, uncooked, peel and dice
1 C cranberries, fresh or frozen
1 apple, peeled and diced
1 t cinnamon
1/4 t nutmeg
3/4 C brown sugar
1/3 C orange juice
1/2 C pecans, toasted

Directions:
Combine all ingred, exc. nuts
Toast pecans in oven or dry skillet, set aside, don't burn
Cook 6-8 hrs, until yams are tender. Stir every hr. or so
Put in dish and top w/pecans

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
One more sweet potato dish to try.  Tried an oven baked dish similar to this but without the cranberries which was just ok. Goes with Thanksgiving tradition for another sweet potato dish to try
