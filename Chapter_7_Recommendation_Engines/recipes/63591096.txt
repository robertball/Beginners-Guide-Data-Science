Title:
Avocado Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 ripe avocado, skin and core removed
- 12oz unsweetened almond milk
- 1 tablespoon honey

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into blender and blend until smooth;
Add additional milk if desired.

Submitter: 
Desire For Fit
Description: 
Do not let the ingredients scare you! This antioxidant rich smoothie will brighten your skin, fill you up, and taste like the most delicious smoothie you've ever had!
