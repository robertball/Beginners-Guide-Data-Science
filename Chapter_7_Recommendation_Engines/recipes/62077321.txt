Title:
Rhubarb Pudding Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
1 1/3 Cup - Graham Crumbs
1/3 Cup - Margarine
1/3 Cup - Brown Sugar
Filling:
4 Cups - Sliced Rhubarb
1/2 Cup - Water
1 Cup - Sugar
3 Tbs - Corn Starch
1 Pkg - Instant Vanilla Pudding
1/2 Cup - Whipping Cream

Directions:
Crust:
Mix and reserve 3 Tbs for topping
Press remainder of mixture into buttered 9"x13" pan and cool
Filling:
Cook rhubarb in water until soft, stir in sugar and bring to a boil, thicken with a corn starch and water mixture
Cool mixture and pour over base
Prepare pudding and put over rhubarb mixture
Let it set in the fridge
Spread the whip cream over the pudding and sprinkle with the remaining 3 Tbs of crust mixture and cool

Submitter: 
Freya
Description: 
A great way to use fresh or frozen rhubarb.  This dessert is not too sweet and has a the tangy rhubarb taste.  Adding more/less sugar to the rhubarb pudding will make the tangynes more to your liking.
