Title:
Dark Chocolate and Cinnamon Frozen Custard Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/460036.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 Cups Heavy Whipping Cream
1/2 Cup Milk
3/4 Cup Granulated Sugar
2 Tablespoons Dark Cocoa Powder
1 Ounce 85% Dark Chocolate, finely chopped
5 Egg Yolks
1/2 Tablespoon Cinnamon

Directions:
On medium heat, whisk heavy cream, milk, sugar, and dark cocoa powder until well blended. Add finely chopped dark chocolate and mix until melted. Add egg yolks and continuously mix using a whisk and/or rubber spatula until the mixture is creamy and has thickened slightly. Remove from heat and pour into a tightly covered container to cool in the fridge for at least 6 hours.
Remove cooled mixture from the fridge and mix cinnamon until well blended. Pour cooled mixture into ice cream as per directions on your unit.
Sprinkle extra cinnamon on top of ice cream just before serving. Store in a tightly covered container in the freezer.

Submitter: 
House of Aqua
Description: 
This is a gourmet blend of smooth and creamy dark chocolate frozen custard with cinnamon. Garnish with a an extra sprinkle of cinnamon and a piece of dark chocolate on top.
