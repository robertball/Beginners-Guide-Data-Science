Title:
Cilantro Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/691269.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cilantro leaves
3 cloves garlic
3 tbsp lime juice (may use lemon if out)
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 tsp kosher salt
1/8 to 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

Directions:
Place all ingredients in blender and puree.  Freeze in ice cube and/or mini muffin trays.  Add to soups, sauces, etc.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
A modified recipe from Thyhandhathprovided.com - this is an absolute killer means of preserving cilantro.  Cilantro blended with lime juice, garlic, and olive oil then frozen.
