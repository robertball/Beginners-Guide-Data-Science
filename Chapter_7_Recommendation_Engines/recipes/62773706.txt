Title:
Slice and Bake Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. shortening
2 c. sugar
2 c. brown sugar
2 c. peanut butter, your choice
2 tsp. vanilla
4 eggs
5 c. all-purpose flour
4 tsp. baking soda

Directions:
Prepare 4 14" by 12" pieces of waxed or plastic wrap: set aside. Cream shortening, sugars and peanut butter. Beat in vanilla and eggs until light and fluffy. Combine flour and baking soda and gradually add flour mix into egg mix. Stir until blended. Divide into 4 pieces of 8-10 inch rolls. Wrap in plastic wrap and store in freezer. To cook, Preheat oven to 350F and cut frozen dough into 1-inch thick slices. Slice those into 1/4ths and roll each into a ball. Place on sheet pan and use fork to flatten with a nice criss cross design. Bake for 8-10 min. until lightly brown around edges. Cool on racks.

Submitter: 
Deborah Norris
Description: 
Make ahead and store in freezer for a rainy day treat.
