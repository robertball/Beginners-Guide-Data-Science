Title:
EASY BEEF STROGANOFF Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbl vegetable oil
8 oz mushrooms, sliced
1 cup onions, chopped
1 1/2 lb lean ground beef
3 cups medium egg noodles
3 cups tomato juice
1 Tbl worchestershire sauce
2 tsp salt
1/2 green pepper, chopped
1/3-1 cup sour cream **

Directions:
In large skillet over medium heat, add oil. Saute mushrooms, onion & green pepper until softened. Add meat & cook until meat is no longer pink. Drain excess oil, if necessary
Place noodles over meat. Combine tomato juice, Worchestershire sauce & salt & pour over noodles. Bring to boil, reduce heat, cover & simmer for 10-20min until noodles are tender. Stir in sour cream to desired taste & consistency and cook until heated through.
