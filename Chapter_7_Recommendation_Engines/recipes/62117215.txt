Title:
Fettuccine Carbonara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 9oz. pkgs. fresh fettuccine or linguine, cooked according to pkg. directions.
1lb. thickly sliced Bacon, cut crosswise into strips
1/2C. Green Onions, use white part of onion, sliced
1/4C. Dry White Wine
2Cs. Heavy Cream
1C. Romano or Parmesan Cheese, freshly grated
3 Eggs, lightly beaten
1tsp. Garlic Powder
1/4C. Parsley, chopped
1/4tsp. freshly grated Black Pepper

Directions:
While pasta cooks, saute bacon and onions on medium heat, until lightly brown. Pour off all but 1/4C. of bacon fat and add wine. Simmer 5 minutes.
Add 1 & 1/2Cs. of cream and cheese
and cook, while stirring, until cream starts to bubble and thicken.
Combine remaining cream with eggs, gralic powder, parsley and pepper. Stir into sauce, remove from heat. Drain pasta, and place with sause and toss until pasta is well coated.
Best served with garlic bread.

Creamy and smoky, easy to make.
