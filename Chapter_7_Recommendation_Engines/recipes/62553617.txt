Title:
Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 boiled and shredded chicken breasts
1 block of cream cheese
1 bottle of bleu cheese dressing
1 bottle of Frank's Red Hot
1 bag of shredded cheese, like pepper jack

Directions:
Mix everything together except shredded cheese, sprinkle cheese on top after everything else is mixed. Bake at 350 for a half hour or until it's all bubbly. Serve hot with tortilla chips, carrot, or celery. Also is delicious with lettuce and tomato in a wrap.
