Title:
Toffee Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. butter
1 c.brown sugar
1 egg yolk
2 c. flour
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Mix together, put in cake pan, bake for 20 min. or till brown.
Put about 1/2 to 1 pkg. choc. chips on top, return to oven to melt choc. chips, just a few minutes, spread choc. chips
