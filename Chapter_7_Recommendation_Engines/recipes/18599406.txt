Title:
Deviled Ham Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds smoked boneless ham, diced
6 hard boiled eggs
2 cups Miracle Whip
1 jar sweet pickle relish
1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 Tablespoon mustard
6 green onions, sliced
1 small green bell pepper, diced
3 stalks celery, diced

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir together the Miracle Whip, relish, pepper, mustard, and salt until blended.
Grind the ham, onion, green pepper, eggs and celery using a meat grinder; spooning in the mixture as a lubricant.
Stir well.
Store the salad, covered, in the refrigerator, serve on bread or toast.
