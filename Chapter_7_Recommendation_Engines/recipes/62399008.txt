Title:
Roni's Mostaccioli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb cooked and rinsed mostaccioli pasta
1 1/2 Cups Vinegar ( I use 1 Cup)
1 1/2 Cups White Sugar (I use 1 1/4 Cup)
1 TBS Dried Parsley Flakes (fresh is fine - per your preference)
1 teaspoon Salt
3/4 teaspoon Black Pepper
1 teaspoon Garlic Powder (use minced or fresh if you want - to taste)
2 teaspoons prepared Mustard
1/2 teaspoon Celery Seed
2 large Cucumbers julienned
1 medium to large Onion, diced
1 Green Pepper, diced
2 Tomatoes, cut up
1 1/2 Cups Salad Oil (I use 1 Cup)

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients except the pasta and oil together and pour over the pasta. Mix it together and pour the oil over all, last. Mix it again.
Refridgerate. Best chilled.

Submitter: 
americanwumn
Description: 
Nice pasta salad with mostaccioli and veges. No mayo! Refreshing:) I do alter the amounts of some of the ingredients due to personal preferences.
