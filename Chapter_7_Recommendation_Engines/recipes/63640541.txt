Title:
Oriental Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T Balsamic vinegarette
2 T Lemon juice
2 T Soy sauce
1/2 t Paprika
1/2 t each salt and pepper
1 1/2 t Brown sugar
3/4 t Ginger
1/2 t Red pepper flakes
2 Garlic cloves, minced
2 T Green onion, chopped
1/2 t Sesame oil
1/4 C Peanut oil
4 4 oz. Salmon filets

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine balsamic vinegarette, lemon juice, soy sauce, paprika, salt/pepper, brown sugar, ginger and red pepper flakes until dissolved.  Then add the remainder of the ingredients to complete the marinade.
Add the salmon to the marinade and refrigerate for 2 hours.  Grill on medium high 4 minutes per side.

Submitter: 
Adina Ryan
Description: 
A greatway to incorporate more seafood into your week.  I serve it with a side of oriental noodles or rice and sauteed green beans.
