Title:
Stroka's Steak Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2282738.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup olive oil
1/3 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
1/3 cup Worcestershire sauce
6 cloves garlic, minced
3 tablespoons hot sauce (optional)
3 tablespoons dried basil
1 1/2 tablespoons dried parsley
1 tablespoon red pepper flakes (optional)
2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Blend soy sauce, olive oil, brown sugar, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, hot sauce, basil, parsley, red pepper flakes, and black pepper in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Brian Stroka
Description: 
You can enjoy delicious steaks when you prepare them with this simple, yet mouth-watering, marinade.
