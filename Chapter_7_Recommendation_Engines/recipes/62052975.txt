Title:
Taquitas Con Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Taquitas:
24 3 inch corn totillas
1 1/2 cups cooked meat (beef, pork or chicken)
oil for frying
Guacamole:
2 ripe avocados
1/2 tomato - chopped
2 teas. lime juice
2 green onions
1/2 teas. salt
1/2 teas. minced garlic
1 jalapeno pepper-minced

Directions:
Taquitas:
Soften tortillas by wrapping in foil and heating for 15 minutes in oven.
Working on 1 taquito at a time, place a narrow pile of meat along the center of tortilla; roll tightly and secure with a wooden pick.
Heat about 1 inch of oil over medium heat in a large, heavy skillet.  Fry the taquitas, turning to brown evenly.
Drain well on paper towels and serve with Guacamole.
Guacamole:
combine all ingredients together and mix well.  If you like your guacamole smooth you can do it all in a food processor.
