Title:
Windowsill Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large Fuji apples
1 lb mild sausage
1 lb thick cut bacon
1 cup unsalted butter
1/3 cup flour
1/2 cup regular sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tbs vanilla
2 tsp cinnimon
2 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp red pepper flake
12 oz northern white beans
1 onion

Directions:
Peel and core the apples. Cut 1/3 into large wedges, 1/3 into 1/2 inch cubes and dice the last 1/3rd (going for variation in sizes). Place in crock pot
Melt the butter in a sauce pan on low. Once melted, add the flour. Mix until smooth. Add the vanilla, cinnimon, nutmeg, regular sugar, brown sugar. Mix and keep warm until needed
Rough chop the onion and bacon. Place both in a large pan and sautee until both start to brown, remove and drain grease. Set aside
Cook the sausage in the same pan until browned. Remove and drain grease. Set aside.
Drain the beans.
In the crockpot, add the Bacon/Onion mix, the sausage, the beans, and the red pepper flakes to the apples. Pour the liquid mix over top. Stir.
Cook on low until the apples are soft.
