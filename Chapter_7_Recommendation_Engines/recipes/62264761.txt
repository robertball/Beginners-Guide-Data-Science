Title:
Apple Wine "Apfelwein" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gallon store bought apple juice, no additives/preservatives other than vitamin C
1 Cup White Sugar
1/2 Cup Dextrose Corn Sugar
3 Apples Preferably Granny Smith or Tart boiled and Pureed
1/8- 1/4 tsp Champagne Yeast
1/2 tsp Yeast Nutrient
1/2 tsp Lemon Juice

Directions:
Pitch yeast in warmed juice and 1/8tsp yeast nutrient
Add Puree and juice to carboy
Cool carboy to room temp
Add yeast mixture
Add airlock
Wait!!!!
