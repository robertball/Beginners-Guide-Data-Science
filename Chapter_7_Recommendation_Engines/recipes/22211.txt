Title:
Orange Pineapple Slushie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7142499.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups orange juice
1 (15 ounce) can crushed pineapple
2 teaspoons white sugar
10 cubes ice

Directions:
In a blender, combine orange juice, pineapple, sugar and ice cubes. Blend on low until there are no more chunks of ice.  Blend on high until smooth and frothy.  Pour into 2 glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
CK
Description: 
A very healthy slushie made with orange juice and canned pineapple blended with ice. It is very quick and easy to make.
