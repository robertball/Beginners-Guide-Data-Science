Title:
Coconutty Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980901.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup SPLENDA® Brown Sugar Blend, packed
1 cup flour
2 eggs
1/4 cup SPLENDA® Brown Sugar Blend, packed
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 1/2 cups coconut
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Combine the butter, 1/4 cup SPLENDA(R) Brown Sugar Blend and flour until a dough forms. Press into a 13X9 baking pan.
Bake for 10 minutes.
Blend the eggs, remaining 1/4 cup SPLENDA(R) Brown Sugar Blend, vanilla, flour and baking powder.  Stir in the coconut and the pecans.
Spread the filling onto the par-baked crust.
Return to the oven for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
A rich, buttery crust is topped with a divine coconut-pecan topping to make these reduced-sugar cookie bars.
