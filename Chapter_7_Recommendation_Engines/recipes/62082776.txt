Title:
Magical Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. button mushrooms
¼ c. pesto sauce (store-bought is easiest)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Decapitate mushrooms.
Fill each mushroom cap with approximately ½ t. pesto.
Bake for 10 – 12 minutes.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
CHERRY007
Description: 
Not THOSE kinds of mushrooms. These mushrooms are magical because they are so delicious and yet so easy to make. Even if you are allergic to the nuts in the pesto, these shrooms are well worth the trip to the ER!
