Title:
2Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb lean ground beef
1/3 cup chopped onion
14 oz can red kidney beans, rinsed
2 cooked or smoked sausages, cut in 1/4" slices
2 medium carrots, sliced
10 oz can condensed tomato soup
1/2 cup frozen corn
19 oz can diced tomatoes
1 cup spaghetti sauce
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. chili pepper
cayenne or chipotle powder to taste

Directions:
Brown beef in dutch oven.
Add rest of ingredients.
Stir and bring to boil.
Let simmer, covered x 1 hour or till carrots are tender. Add water if it appears too dry and thick.
OR
After bringing all ingredients to boil, put into slow-cooker. Cook on low approx. 4 hours, or until carrots are tender. It can cook on low several hours & be ready to eat whenever you are. Another option is to cook it on high in slow-cooker for an hour, then on low till done, to speed it up.
Adjust spices before serving or have extra spices available for individuals to adjust their own portions.
Serve with buns or cornbread.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A family favorite, usually made in the slow-cooker. The addition of sausage makes it unique and extra hearty. Spice it to your personal taste!
