Title:
FB Peanut Butter Yogurt Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C fat free milk
1/2 C fat free yogurt
2 T creamy unsalted peanut butter
1/4 very ripe banana
1 T honey
4 ice cubes

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a blender. Process until smooth.
Serve in a tall glass.
