Title:
Pink Dimple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Macadamia Nut Rum - 1 shot
Malibu rum - 1 shot
half-half - 1/2 shot
1 inch of fresh banana
pineapple juice - 1 shot
splash of grenadine

Directions:
blend all ingredients with ice
pour and enjoy!
garnish with pineapple wedge and cherry
