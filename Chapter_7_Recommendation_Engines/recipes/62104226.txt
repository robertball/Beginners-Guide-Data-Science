Title:
Baked chicken tenders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 - boneless skinless chicken thighs cut into quarters
1-1/2 - potato flakes
1 - cup - shredded parmesan cheese
1 - teaspoon garlic pepper
1 - teaspoon salt
1 - teaspoon garlic powder
1 - teaspoon onion powder
1 - cup mayonaise (I use Best Food's)
2 - tablespoon canola oil or vegetable oil

Directions:
In a bowl, mix potato flakes, parmesan cheese and spices.  Mix together well.
In a 13 x 9 pan, spray with Pam and add oil to bottom making sure the oil is spread throughout the bottom.  Roll the chicken nuggets in mayonaise, then roll in potato flake mixture.  Lay each nugget on 13x9 pan.  Bake in oven for 25 minutes or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Tina Andre' Fox
Description: 
These are a wonderful chicken nugget that you can bake.  The flavor is great and they have an nice crunch to them.  We baked them for our church and they were a huge hit.
