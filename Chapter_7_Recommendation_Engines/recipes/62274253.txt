Title:
Spinach soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb (450 gms) Spinach Leaves
1 1/2 pints (850ml) vegetable stock (or neutral chicken stock will do)
1 Tablespoon lemon juice
Salt and pepper to taste
Tabasco sauce (just a few drops needed)
3/4 pint (425 ml) Buttermilk

Directions:
In a saucepan, place the spinach, the stock, the lemon juice and the salt and pepper.
Bring to the boil and then simmer for 10 minutes.
Take off the heat and allow to cool (safety reasons) before pur√©eing in a blender.
Pass the results through a sieve.
Keep until 10 minutes before required.
Reheat the soup gently.
Add a few drops of Tabasco and the Buttermilk. Mix thoroughly.
Serve with Croutons and/or a swirl of double cream.
