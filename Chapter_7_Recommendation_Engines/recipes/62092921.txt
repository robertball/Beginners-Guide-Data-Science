Title:
Mom's Favorite Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large apples - peeled & diced
2 cans mandarin oranges
1 1/2 cups sliced seedless grapes
1 or 2 cans pineapple tidbits (drained)
1 large or 2 small jars maraschino cherries - sliced (Reserve juice)
1 cup chopped pecans
1 12 oz. bag miniature marshmallows
1 8 oz. container sour cream
1 large container Cool Whip

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.  You may add some of the cherry juice to achieve a pretty pink color and garnish top with cherries.
Refrigerate for 4 hours & serve.

Submitter: 
BAYOU GAL
Description: 
Lots of fruit, pecans, & marshmallows blended together with cool whip for a wonderfully different & refreshing fruity treat!
