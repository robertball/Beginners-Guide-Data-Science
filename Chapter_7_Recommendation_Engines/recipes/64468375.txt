Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2454178.jpg
Ingredients:
• 1 lb. of cooked elbow macaroni noodles.
• 3 cups of shredded sharp cheddar cheese.
• 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese.
• 1 cup of shredded havarti cheese.
• 1/2 a cup of butter.
• 1/2 a cup of flour.
• 4 cups of milk.
• 1/2 of a large onion.
• 5 jalapeños.
• Panko bread crumbs.
• A pinch of salt and pepper.
• A teaspoon of ground mustard.
• Wax paper.

Directions:
Step 1.
• Dice and de-vein the jalapeños.
• Dice the onion.
• Now sauté the diced jalapeño and onion in a frying pan with some extra virgin olive oil.
• Throw in the teaspoon of ground mustard and keep sautéing until the onions are translucent.
Step 2.
• Add in with the sautéed Dino bits the 1/2 cup of butter and 1/2 cup of flour.
• Mix until there are no lumps.
• Now add the 4 cups of milk and cook until bubbly.
• Once bubbling (boiling, your choice) add the 3 cups of cheddar, 2 cups of mozzarella, the 1 cup of havarti, and then the pinch of pepper and salt.
• Cook until all of the cheese is melted.
• Add the 1lb. Of cooked elbow macaroni noodles.
• Once stirred together pour the substance into a 9 by 13 baking dish (or whatever you can conjure) and put into the fridge until the macaroni is cold and thick.
Step 3.
• Let macaroni sit until room temperature.
• Lay wax paper down.
• Scoop little balls of the macaroni onto the wax paper.
• Once balls are made, lay out three shallow bowls.
• In one bowl add flour.
• In the next add around 3 beaten eggs.
• In the last add the Panko bread crumbs.
( For these directions you may repeat the amount if necessary based on the amount of fluffy bites you are tending too. )
• Rolls the macaroni balls in the flour, then eggs, and finally the bread crumbs.
• Deep fry the fluffy balls until golden brown.
Final step.
Place all of the fluffy balls onto a platter a share them amongst  your Gishwhes team.
Your team will need something to snack on while you laze about and let PuddingPals stomp you.
You won't regret making Dinomite's Fluffy Bites.
