Title:
Quick & Easy Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups white sugar
1 3/4 cup All Purpose flour
1/3 cup cocoa
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup vegetable oil
5 eggs
1/2 tbsp Vanilla
1 cup Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Grease 9 x 13 pan
Combine dry ingredients in a large bowl.  Add remaining ingredients except chocolate chips and mix well.
Pour into prepared pan.
Sprinkle chips on top.
Bake 30 minutes.
Cool on rack 30 minutes before slicing

Submitter: 
kuisinebykaren
Description: 
These brownies are quick & easy, and you can subsitute any chips or nuts for the topping!  My kids love them!
