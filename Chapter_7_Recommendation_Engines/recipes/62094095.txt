Title:
Brenda V's Sticky Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dough:
1/4oz package of self rising yeast
1/2 warm water
1/2 cup milk
1/3 cup sugar
1 egg
4 cups flour
1/2 cup melted butter
1 tsp salt
Filling:
1/4 cup melted butter
1/4 cup sugar
2/4 cup brown sugar
2 1/2 tbsp cinn
chopped pecans, and raisens...approx 1/4 cup each
Frosting:
2 packages regular cream cheese
2 tbsp butter
1 1/2 cup powered sugar
1 tbsp vanilla flavoring
1 tsp cinn
oven at 350"

Directions:
Disolve yeast in 1/2 cup warm water, set aside
combine milk, egg, salt, sugar. While stiring, add 1/2 cup melted butter.
Add 1/2 flour, mix until smooth.
Add yeast mixture, and remaining flour
When dough becomes "pizza dough like", place on floured surface and kneed for about 5 mins.
Place in oiled bowl, cover for about an hour or until dough rises. (should double in size)
While waiting for dough to rise, prepare filling and Icing.
Place dough on floured surface, and press down or flatten. Roll dough with rolling pin until approx 16 by 9 inch.
coat with 1/4th cup melted butter
sprinkle with filling.
Roll into a log, making sure to pinch the ends.
Slice into desired size servings, place on oiled baking sheet. ( Before I put the slices on the baking sheet, I usually will sprinkle a little brown sugar and cinn on the pan first...this makes sure the bottom of the buns are well coated)
cover, and let rise 30 mins.
Bake for thirty mins.
Ice while still warm, or set icing aside and let guest do it at their leisure.

Submitter: 
Brenda Vandervort
Description: 
this recipe is great for entertaining guest for brunch, or as desert for Sunday dinner. Their easy to make, with lots of flavor. Enjoy!
