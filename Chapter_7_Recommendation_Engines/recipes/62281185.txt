Title:
Mom-Mom's Quick-N-Easy Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/365119.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box chocolate cake mix
1 4oz. chocolate pudding mix that you cook (NOT instant)
1 10 oz bag chocolate chips
2 cups whole milk

Directions:
Cook pudding with the 2 cups milk as directed on package in large saucepan.  Add dry cake mix and mix well. Cake batter will be very thick. Pour into greased 13 x 9 pan.  Sprinkle chocolate chips over batter.  Bake 350* for approximately 25 minutes.

Chocolate-Chocolate-Chocolate!  Easy and oh so good.  You can switch this up with a variety of flavors too. Just right when you need a chocolate cake quick!
