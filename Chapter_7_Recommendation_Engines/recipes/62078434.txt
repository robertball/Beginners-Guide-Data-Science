Title:
Graham Cracker Toffee Crunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15-20 whole graham crackers
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup butter
2 cups chocolate chips
1 cup chopped peanuts

Directions:
1. Lightly spray cookie sheet with cooking spray.  Line with graham crackers.
2. In a saucepan, bring butter and brown sugar to a boil. Boil for three minutes.
3. Pour over graham crackers immediately, making sure all crackers are covered.
4. Sprinkle chocolate chips over crackers.  Allow to melt a bit, then spread with a spatula covering all the crackers.
5. Sprinkle with nuts.
6. Allow to cool, then crack into pieces and serve!

Submitter: 
CHRISPENTINA
Description: 
No oven required!  Fast and easy!  Be prepared, they are gone in minutes! I make these and give them as Christmas presents every year...my neighbors think I slaved in the kitchen! You can experiment with different types of chips and nuts.
