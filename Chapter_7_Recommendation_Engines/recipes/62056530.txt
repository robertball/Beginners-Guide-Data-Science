Title:
Honey Graham Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-8 ounce pkg. softened cream cheese
1-8 ounce can crushed pineapple with juice
1-3.5 ounce box instant golden vanilla pudding **do not prepare as directed on box*

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.  Mix on low speed until well blended.  Ready to serve as dip for graham crackers.  Will need to be stored in covered dish in refridgerator.

Submitter: 
Valerie Cain Cuff
Description: 
Unique sweet craving satisfier made quick and easy.  Have your honey graham crackers ready!
