Title:
Blue Cheese Tagliatelle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30g butter
1 large courgette, sliced
3 cloves garlic, crushed
100ml white wine
100g blue cheese, crumbled
300ml cream
freshly ground black pepper
500g white or green tagliatelle
2 - 3 tablespoons freshly grated Parmesan cheese
chopped fresh parsley, to garnish

Directions:
1. Melt the butter in a frying pan.  Add the courgette and garlic and cook until the courgette is tender.  Stir in the wine, cheese, chream adn pepper.  Simmer for 10 minutes.
2. Meanwhile, add the tagliatelle to a large pan of rapidly boiling water and cook for 6 - 8 minutes, until tender.  Drain, rinse under warm water, drain again.
3. Return the pasta to the pan.  Add the sauce and toss through the pasta for a few minutes over a low heat.  Serve sprinkled with Parmesan and parsley.
