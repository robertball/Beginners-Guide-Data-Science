Title:
Party Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065746.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (64 ounce) can Hawaiian Punch
1 (12 ounce) can frozen orange juice, with 1 can water
1 (2 liter) bottle of Squit Soda, chilled

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in punchbowl to dissolve orange juice completely.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
A light and refreshing party punch that is non-alcoholic.  Simple to make, serve over ice or chilled.Modified from: Party Punch
