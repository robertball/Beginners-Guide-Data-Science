Title:
Bay Scallop Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons unsalted butter
6 large garlic cloves, minced
2 shallots, minced
4 tablespoons parsley, minced
2 tablespoons lemon juice
2 teaspoons kosher salt
1 teaspoon pepper
6 tablespoons olive oil
1 cup panko
6 tablespoons pinot grigio
2 lbs bay scallops

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.  Place 6 gratin dishes on sheet pan.
Place butter, garlic, shallot, lemon juice, salt, and pepper into food processor and combine.  Add olive oil while blending.  Fold in parsley and panko.
Place 1 tablespoon of white wine in to bottom of each dish.  Divde scallops evenly among dishes.  Top with butter mixture.  Bake 10 to 12 minutes.  Serve with toasted crustini.
