Title:
Spice It Up Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons oil
2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1/2 c. salsa, mild, medium or hot
1/2 c. shredded Monterey Jack or Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Heat a frying pan that has a cover.  Add oil.  Cook Chicken about 2 minutes until browned.  Turn over and brown other side.  Top each breast with half the salsa.  Cover, simmer 10 min.  Shut off heat.  Top each breast with shredded cheese.  Cover.  Let sit until cheese melts, about a minute.

Submitter: 
SPICYGIRL
Description: 
Fast and easy stove-top recipe with only four ingredients.  Serve with rice and a salad for a complete meal.
