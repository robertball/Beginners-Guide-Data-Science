Title:
Pears 'n' Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/464038.jpg
Ingredients:
4 15oz cans Pears, cut in chunks
1 1/4 cup shredded Meduim cheddar cheese
1/4 cup real mayonnaise

Directions:
Drain pears thoroughly
Add Mayonnaise, mix well to coat pears
Add shredded cheese and toss
Refrigerate at least 4 hours before serving

Submitter: 
Candice
Description: 
This three ingredient salad is absolutely a Smash Hit with everyone. It's easy, light and delicious! And, it goes with just about anything.
