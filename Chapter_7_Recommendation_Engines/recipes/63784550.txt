Title:
Vitamix: Fruit Sorbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 ounces (180ml) apple juice concentrate, thawed
1 banana peeled
4 baby carrots, about 1 1/4 ounces
3-4 cups ice cubes

Directions:
1. Place all ingredients into the Vitamix in the order listed and secure lid
2. Select Variable 1
3. Turn machine on and slowly increase speed to Variable 10, then to high.  Use the tamper to press the ingredients into the blade
4. In about 30 seconds, the sound of the motor will change and four mounds should form
5. Stop machine.  Do not overmix or melting will occur
