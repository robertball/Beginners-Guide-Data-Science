Title:
Pollo en Mole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Vegetable oil
1/4 kilo of mole concetrate (paste)
1 liter of chicken broth
1 whole chicken cut into pieces. (Cut each whole   breast in half.  Separate legs from thighs).
1 large onion, chopped.
4 large cloves of garlic, minced.
1 large bell pepper, diced.
1/2 buch of cilantro, chopped.
4 stalks of celery, diced.
salt & pepper to taste.
1 jalapeno pepper, minced. (optional)

Directions:
Pat dry the chicken parts, salt and pepper and then brown in vegetable oil.
Using the same pan and vegetable oil, add the onion, garlic, bell pepper, jalapeno and celery.  Saute until the vegetables are just tender.
While the chicken and vegies are sauteeing, mix the mole paste and chicken broth in a large pot and heat on a stove cook top.
Stir contantly while heating the mole/broth mixture.
When mole concentrate is completely dissolved into the broth, add the browned chicken and sauteeed vegetables.
Cook uncovered on stove top on simmer for 1 to 1-1/2 hours or until mole sauce has thickened and chicken is tender.
Serve over steamed rice.
Garnish with cilantro.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
This chicken mole recipe came from our neighbor Margot de Los Angeles in Mazatlan.  We don't make our own mole concentrate.  We buy about a kilo of mole once a year at the Oaxaca Fair that comes to Mazatlan in February.
