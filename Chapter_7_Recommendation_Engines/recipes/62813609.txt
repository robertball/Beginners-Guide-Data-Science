Title:
Floyd Eudy's Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/683533.jpg
Ingredients:
4 granny smith apples
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
2 tbsp flour
1/8 tsp salt
1 tbsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
3 tbsp butter
1 tbsp lemon juice
2 (9 inch) Pillsbury pie crusts

Directions:
pre heat over to 450 degrees.
peel & slice apples into chunks. add lemon juice to apples, toss to coat & prevent browning.
mix dry ingredients & cut in butter with a fork. add dry ingredients to the apples.
fill 9" pie crust then cover with 2nd pie crust. seal and pinch.
brush top crust with room temperature butter & sprinkle with cinnamon & sugar.
bake at 450 for 10 minutes.
THEN BAKE AT 350 for 40 minutes.
