Title:
Mango Cilantro Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large Mango (Chopped)
1 Bunch Cilantro
1/4 Cup White Vinegar
1/4 Cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 tsp White Pepper

Directions:
Remove as many stems from the cilantro as you can.
Combine the chopped mango, cilantro, white vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a blender or food processor and pulse for 20-30 seconds.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Michelle R.
Description: 
A sweet and tangy dressing that goes perfectly with any salad.
