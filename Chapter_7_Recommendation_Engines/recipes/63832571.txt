Title:
crockpot apple crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 cups sliced apples
1 cup oats
1 1/2 cup flour
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup regular sugar
3 teaspoons cinnamon
3/4 cup butter- melted

Directions:
line crockpot or spray with non stick butter
line apples up in the crockpot
mix rest of the ingredients together and pour over the apples
cook low 3-4 hrs

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
hardest part is cutting up the apples...mix and forget
