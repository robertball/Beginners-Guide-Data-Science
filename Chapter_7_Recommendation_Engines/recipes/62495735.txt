Title:
Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. granulated sugar
1/2 c. butter
2 eggs
1 1/2 . mashed bananas
1 Tbl. lemon juice
2 c. flour
3 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Cream together sugar and butter.  Add eggs one at a time, beating hard after each.
Stir in bananas and lemon juice.
Sift together flour, baking powder and salt.
Add and mix quickly.
Pour into greased 9" x 5" x 3" loaf pan.
Bake 1 hr @ 375 deg.
