Title:
Basic, Instant, Chicken and Rice... Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked instant rice (any cooked rice works)
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 can of milk (or water)
salt and pepper to taste (optional)...

Directions:
Mix all ingredients, heat and serve.

Submitter: 
Nauntie
Description: 
This recipe is a great instant year round family meal. Super easy to change up with simple add ins. I have made this dish for almost 40 years. I don't recall any kid that did not like it once they tried it.
