Title:
Cucumber-carrot salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¼ 	c 	Seasoned rice vinegar
1 	ts 	Sugar
½ 	ts 	Vegetable oil
¼ 	ts 	Grated peeled gingerroot
? 	ts 	Salt
1 	c 	Sliced carrot
2 	tb 	Sliced green onions
2 	tb 	Finely chopped red bell pepper
½ 		Cucumber, (1 cup) halved lengthwise, seeded and sliced

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients in a medium bowl; stir with a whisk. Add carrot and remaining ingredients; toss to coat. Cover and chill 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Somer
Description: 
Cool and refreshing asian salad.  Very simple!
