Title:
Fall Oatmeal Breakfest Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c milk
2 tbsp brown sugar
1 tbsp butter
1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1/8 tsp salt
3 tbsp pumpkin puree
2 tbsp ground flax seed (optional)
1 medium baking apple (peeled, cored, and diced)
1 cup rolled oats (NOT quick cooking oats)
3/4 cup raisins
1 tsp vanilla
cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Lightly coat a 1 quart baking dish with oil spray.
Combine the milk, sugar, butter, pumpkin pie spice, salt, pumpkin puree, and ground flax (flax is optional) in a 2 quart saucepan. Cook, stirring constantly, over high heat for 4 minutes to scald the milk. Add the apple, raisins, and the oats. Continue cooking on High for 1 minute. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla.
Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish. Bake for 15 minutes. Remove from the oven, stir thoroughly. Return to the oven and bake for 15 more minutes.
