Title:
Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds hamburger
1 egg
1 cup oats
1 cup milk
1 teaspoon salt
pepper
1/2 teaspoon sage
1/4 cup onion, chopped

Directions:
Combine ingredients and put in greased loaf pan. Cook 50 minutes at 350.
