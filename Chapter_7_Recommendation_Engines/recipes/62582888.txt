Title:
Authentic Russian Salad "Olivye" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/549538.jpg
Ingredients:
-6 medium sized to large potatoes
-4 hard boiled eggs
-1 or 2 carrots
-6 large pickles
-ham (not smoked)
-15 oz can of green peas (not frozen)
-mayo
-parsley or dill (optional)

Directions:
Fill a large pot with water, put on heat and throw in peeled potatoes and peeled carrots. As it starts boiling add in the eggs. Cook for about 20/30 minutes.
Meanwhile chop pickles and ham into cubes, add green peas and mix together in a large salad bowl.
Do the same for potatoes, carrots and eggs.
Mix everything together thoroughly and add dill or parsley if desired.
Finally dress the salad in mayonnaise to taste.

Submitter: 
lovefullofpie
Description: 
This is a recipe my mom and grandmother uses every time there's a family gathering or a special occasion. To make this a vegetarian dish just leave out the ham.
