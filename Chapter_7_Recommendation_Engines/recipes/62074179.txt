Title:
Mexican Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Boboli (thin crust) pizza shell
1/2 pound either ground sirloin or chicken.
1 small container of Philadelhia (light) cream cheese w/ chives
1/2 a jar of your favorite salsa (I prefer Newmans Hot)
1 small green pepper
1 small onion
2 cups of shredded jalapeno chedder
Sprinkle of Garlic Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450
Brown meat, and drain excess grease.
Dice both onion % green pepper, set aside.
Spread a thin layer of cream cheese onto the pizza crust, out to the edges.
Then spread the salsa on top of the cream cheese.
Sprinkle the beef or chicken, then the pepper & onion.
Finish with a layer or shredded chedder.
Sprinkle Garlic Pepper over everything.
Bake at 450 for 12-14 minutes or until cheese starts to slighly brown on top.
Cut & serve.

Submitter: 
Scott in  MA
Description: 
A super-simple to make pizza that should please all who enjoy Mexican tastes.
