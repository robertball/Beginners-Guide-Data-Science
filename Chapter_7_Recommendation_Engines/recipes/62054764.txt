Title:
SKOR Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of German Chocolate cake mix
1 can of sweetened condensed milk
1 container of whipped topping
5 SKOR candy bars, crushed (Heath bars may be used as a substitution)

Directions:
Bake cake per package directions, in a 13X9' pan. When cake has cooled, take a fork and poke holesthroughout the entire cake. Pour (or spoon) the condensed milk evenly over cake. Refrigerate 8 hours or over night. Before serving, spread whipped topping evenly over cake. Crumble candy bar bits on top.
