Title:
Creamy Chipotle Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pound chicken breast
1 teaspoon salt
1 garlic clove
6 cups of water
3 1/2 cups of sour cream
1 teaspoon  salt
1 cup chicken broth
3 canned n seasoned chipotle peppers

Directions:
Boil chicken until tender in the water with salt and garlic. Cut in to desired size pieces, set aside.
Blend sour cream, salt, chicken broth (can use the water where the chicken was boiled) and chipotle peppers (can use 2 if u want it less hot).
Pour the chipotle sauce in a large pan and add the chicken.
Stew at very low heat for 10-15 minutes.
Suggestion: Serve hot with withe rice and vegetables.
