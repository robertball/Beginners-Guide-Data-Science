Title:
Mediterranean Lentil and Feta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
19 oz can lentils or chickpeas, drained and rinsed
2 tbsp. freshly squeezed lemon juice
4 tsp. olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp. ground cumin
1/2 tsp. oregano
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
1  pint grape tomatoes, cut in half or 1 large tomato, coarsely chopped
1/2 cup diced English cucumber
2  roasted red peppers, coarsely chopped
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley, mint or basil
1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
1/2 cup crumbled light feta cheese

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir lemon juice with oil, garlic, cumin, oregano, salt and cayenne.
Add lentils, red peppers, tomatoes, cucumber,  parsley, mint or basil, and onion.
Crumble in feta. Stir to mix. Serve or refrigerate up to 2 days.

Submitter: 
AnnInLondon
Description: 
This easy, inexpensive and nutritious main dish or side salad uses pantry staples and fresh vegetables. It comes together quickly and keeps well for a couple of days of tasty lunches.
