Title:
Three Cheese Baked Ziti with Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ziti
8 oz sausage meat
15 oz of ricotta cheese
8 oz grated mozzarella cheese
8 oz grated parmesan cheese
1 egg
2 jars of spaghetti sauce

Directions:
Cook ziti until just before it is done.  Fry sausage meat until done.  Discard sausage grease.
In a large bowl, combine ricotta, parmesan, about 7 ounces of mozzarella, egg, sausage, and sauce until mixed.  Stir in ziti.
Add mixture to a lightly greased baking dish.  Sprinkle top with the rest of the mozzarella.
Cover with tin foil and bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Tikeren
Description: 
My own spin on my parents' old baked ziti recipe.  I love it for the cheese, but my husband loves it for the sausage!
