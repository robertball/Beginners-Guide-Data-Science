Title:
Potato Scones (using leftover mashed spuds) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 tbsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
3 tbsp. butter or margarine
1 cup mashed spuds, warmed up a bit
1/3 cup milk, or more
1 egg

Directions:
Mix first 3 ingredients.
Cut in butter until crumbly.
Mix spuds, milk and eggs.
Stir spud mixture into flour mixture until soft dough forms. Don't over-mix.
Turn onto a lightly floured surface and knead gently 10 - 12 times until no longer sticky.
Gently pat or roll dough till 3/4" thick.
Cut into 10 - 12 wedges.
Place wedges on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake @ 400 x 15 - 18 minutes, or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A great way to use leftover mashed spuds. Like a basic baking powder biscuit, only better :)
