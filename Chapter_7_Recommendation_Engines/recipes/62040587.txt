Title:
Mom-in-Law's Quick Hearty Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 can Rotel tomatoes
1 can Ranch-style beans
1 can Progresso minestrone soup
1 can mixed veggies

Directions:
Brown & drain beef.  Dump entire contents of all cans.  Simmer for 30 min.  Serve with bread or whatever suits your fancy!
