Title:
Sweet Noodle Kugel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16oz egg noodles
5 eggs
1 1/2 cup sugar
1 20 oz can crushed pineapple
24 oz orange juice
1 1/2 Tablespoon vanilla
butter
cinnamon

Directions:
Cook and drain noodles. In a bowl, add beaten eggs, sugar, OJ, pineapple with juice and vanilla. Add noodles to greased baking dish. Pour liquid over. Dot with butter and sprinkle with cinnamon.
Bake uncovered, 350 degrees for 1 hour.
