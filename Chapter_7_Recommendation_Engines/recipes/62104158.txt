Title:
Poppy Seed Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup oil
1/4 Cup rice vinegar
1 1/2 Tbl. lemon juice
1 1/2 Tbl. honey
2 Tsp. poppy seeds
1/4 Tsp. salt
1/4 Tsp. pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a blender or a small food processor until well blended.  Wonderful over any mixture of fruit, lettuce and nuts.

Submitter: 
KONWAL
Description: 
A light, fresh and flavorfull dressing that is perfect poured on a mixture of lettuce and sliced fruits like oranges, grapefruit and avocados.
