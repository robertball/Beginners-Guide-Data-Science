Title:
Robust Minestrone Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups leftover cooked roast beef, or uncooked stew beef
2 tbsp olive oil or bacon fat
2 onions, chopped
1 large carrot, chopped
3 celery stalks with leaves, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 16 oz can diced tomatoes
6 cups beef broth
2 cups water
1 rind from fresh parmesan cheese, optional
1 tbsp oregano
1 tsp basil
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp celery seed
1 tsp sea salt
1 tsp pepper
1 large dash Worcestershire sauce
1 can red or white kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 can cut green beans, drained
1 cup bowtie pasta

Directions:
In a large soup-pot, heat olive oil or bacon fat over medium heat, then saute onions, carrots, celery, and garlic for about 5 minutes, or until tender but not browned.
Add the roast beef or stew beef, can of tomatoes, the beef broth, water, the parmesan rind, and all of the spices, and stir well.  Bring to a simmer, and skim any foam, then cook, covered, over low heat, for about 40 minutes.
Add the can of kidney beans (rinsed and drained), the can of green beans(drained), and the bowtie (or other) pasta, and simmer a further 10 minutes or so.
Check seasonings, remove parmesan rind, and enjoy!
