Title:
Mashed Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/512945.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 12 oz. can Tomato Soup
1 12 oz. can french style green beans
5-6 Cups Mashed Potatoes
2 Cups shredded mild cheddar cheese
salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Brown hamburger and drain. Stir in tomato soup. Spread meat mixture into the bottom of a 9X11 baking dish. Layer green beans and then the mashed potatoes. Top with shredded cheese. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.Let stand 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Kristen
Description: 
This is a fast, delicious and easy recipe that kids absolutely love! It is a great way to use up left over mashed potatoes and I almost always have all the ingredients on hand!
