Title:
Chicken Chow Mein Hong Kong Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2825281.jpg
Ingredients:
400g Thinly sliced skinless/boneless chicken breast
250g Egg noodles
1 Tablespoon rice wine (sake)
1.5 Tablespoon light soy sauce
0.5 Teaspoon ground white pepper
0.5 Teaspoon ground black pepper
2 Tablespoons oyster sauce
0.5 Cup of chicken broth
2 Teaspoons corn flour (Corn starch in USA)
2 Teaspoons water
1 Teaspoon sesame oil
1 Table spoon cornstarch (for velveting the chicken)

Directions:
Marinade chicken in egg white, sesame oil and corn flour
Boil noodles in water until noodles are soft
Set noodles aside on paper towels to dry out
Cook noodles in oil in wok for about 3-5 minutes on each side
Take noodles out and put on paper towel
Add chicken to hot oil (make sure to break up so it doesn't stick together) cook for around 3 minutes
Take chicken out and set aside (leave a little bit of oil)
Add spring onions, rice wine, light soy sauce, chicken stock, black and white pepper and stir
Add water and corn flour mix to thicken
Add oyster sauce (stir)
Add bean sprouts and chicken
Stir fry for roughly 5 mins to cook the chicken through and serve.
