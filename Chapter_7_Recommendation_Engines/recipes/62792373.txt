Title:
Grandma's Season Salt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (26oz) pkg. salt
2 Tbsp onion salt
2 Tbsp celery salt
3 tsp garlic salt
2 Tbsp paprika
2 1/2 Tbsp black pepper
4 Tbsp sugar
3 tsp. ground mustard
3 tsp turmeric

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.  Mix well and store in air-tight container.

Submitter: 
scarlett
Description: 
This season salt is MUCH better than store bought brands!  Great to make and share!
