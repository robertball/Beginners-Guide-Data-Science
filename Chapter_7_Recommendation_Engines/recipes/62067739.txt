Title:
Crock Pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 steaks of choice
1 can mushrooms,drained
1 onion,sliced
1 bottle Teriyaki sauce
foil

Directions:
Place each steak on a piece of foil.
On top,spread 1/4 of the mushrooms and onions.
pour 1/4 bottle Teriyaki sauce over the top.
Wrap tightly and place in crock pot.
Cook on low in crock pot for 6 to 8 hour`s.
