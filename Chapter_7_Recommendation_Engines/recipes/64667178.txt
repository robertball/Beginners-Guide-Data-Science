Title:
Big Ray's Tuscan Chicken and Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4573970.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into 3/4-inch pieces
2 tsp minced fresh rosemary or 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp coarsely ground pepper
1 cup chicken broth
2 Tbsp sun-dried tomatoes (not packed in oil), chopped
1 large onion, chopped
2 large carrots, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
2 medium zucchini, chopped
1 bunch spinach, chopped
1 can (15-1/2 ounces) Great Northern beans, rinsed and drained

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the chicken, rosemary, salt and pepper. In a large nonstick skillet coated with cooking spray, cook chicken over medium heat until browned.
Stir in broth, onion, celery, zucchini,  carrots, spinach and tomatoes. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 8-10 minutes or until chicken juices run clear and carrots are soft. Add beans; heat through.
