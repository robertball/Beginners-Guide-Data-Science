Title:
Green Chili Stew (custom) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds pork meat
1 tablespoon California style garlic salt
2 tablespoons ground cumin
2 tablespoons chili powder
4 Anaheim chilies, diced OR 2 (7 ounce) cans diced green chiles
1 large sweet onion, diced
2 stalks celery, diced
1 ounces diced tomatoes
2 cans pinto or Pinquitos beans, drained
1 can corn, drained

Directions:
Cut meat into bite sized cubes. Place in bottom of crock pot.
Stir spices into meat.
Top meat with celery, peppers and onion.
Top with tomatoes & juice.
Cook for 5-7 hours.
Add beans & corn. Cook 1 hour more.
Serve with corn bread & salad.

Submitter: 
Joan
Description: 
This is a flavorful, mildly spicy, Southwestern stew.  Serve hot with corn bread.Modified from: Green Chili Stew with Pork
