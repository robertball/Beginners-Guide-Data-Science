Title:
Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream style corn
2 eggs, beaten
2 tablespoons flour
1/3 C. butter, melted (can use a little less)
1/2 C milk
1/2 C. sugar (can use less)
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven 450 degrees F. Butter casserole dish. combine all the ingredients and pour into the casserole dish. Bake at 450 degrees F for about 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Simple to prepare. A great accompaniment for beef, turkey or pork. Guests frequently request a copy of this recipe.
