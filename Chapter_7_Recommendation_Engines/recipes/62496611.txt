Title:
Vegetarian Harvest Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp sesame oil
2 cups maize (any variety: white, yellow, blue etc.)
1 cup onion, chopped
1 t garlic, chopped
2 cups squash (any variety: pumpkin, zucchini, acorn, butternut etc.), cut bite size
1 cup tomatoes,diced
1 mild chili, chopped
2 cups dry beans (any variety: adzuki, pinto, anasazi, navy etc)
2 cups vegetable broth
2 quarts water
1 tsp salt (or to taste)

Directions:
Roast maize in a pan with sesame oil.
Add onion and garlic to maize and saute.
Add remaining vegetables and saute lightly.
Combine vegetables with the beans in the soup pot.
Add broth and then water and salt.
Simmer low for two hours or until beans are tender.

Submitter: 
Jesse Anne

Traditional flavors of corn, beans and squash in a hearty stew.Modified from: Short Ribs and Squash Stew
