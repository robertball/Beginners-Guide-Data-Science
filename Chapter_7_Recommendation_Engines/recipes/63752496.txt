Title:
Banana Pudding Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons of flour
Â½ cup of sugar
4 cups of milk
5 large egg yolks
2 tablespoons of butter
2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
7 Bananas (not overly ripe, no brown spots)
A box Nabisco vanilla wafers

Directions:
Place the flour, sugar, milk, vanilla extract and butter in a large saucepan on high heat. Add each of the egg yolks one at a time into the saucepan and stir until mix well. Bring to a boil. After the ingredients thicken, simmer the ingredients on low heat until the mixture combines into a thick pudding texture. Cool for 2 minutes.
Cut slices of the bananas, place in a bowl and set aside. In a clean baking pan, place a layer of vanilla wafers on the bottom. Then add a layer of sliced bananas on top of the vanilla wafers followed by the pudding mixture on top. Repeat this step until everything are used.
Whipped Cream Topping: Whipped Topping: Whip together 1 cup chilled heavy cream, 1 tablespoonful powdered sugar, and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract. Spread over cooled banana pudding.
