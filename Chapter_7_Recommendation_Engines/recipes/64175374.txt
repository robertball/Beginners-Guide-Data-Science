Title:
Chocolate Pumpkin Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/3 cups brown sugar
1 cup pumpkin
4 eggs
1/4 cup oil
2 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
2 tsp cocoa
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp allspice
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp nutmeg
2/3 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Mix brown sugar, pumpkin, eggs and oil. Add remaining ingredients (except chocolate chips and beat on low speed.  Stir in chocolate chips.
Bake in greased 9x13 inch pan for 30-40 minutes.  Cool and cut in squares.
