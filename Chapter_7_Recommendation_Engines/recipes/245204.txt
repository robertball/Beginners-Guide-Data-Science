Title:
Strawberry Lemonade Pie Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2675653.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
Yellow food coloring (optional)
6 tablespoons butter, melted
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1/4 cup sugar
1 (21 ounce) can LUCKY LEAFÂ® Premium Strawberry Fruit Filling & Topping

Directions:
Mix together the lemonade concentrate and sweetened condensed milk. Add in the whipped topping and yellow food coloring if using. Mix until combined.
Let sit in the fridge 8 hours or overnight.
Mix together the melted butter, sugar and graham cracker crumbs.
Layer the crumb mixture, lemonade mixture and strawberry fruit filling in small glasses. Repeat.
You can also make this into a whole pie. Just press the crumb mixture into a pie plate and top with lemonade mixture. Set in fridge 8 hours or overnight. Top with fruit filling.

Submitter: 
Lucky Leaf Fruit Filling
Description: 
These pretty little parfaits with layers of creamy lemon, strawberry, and sweet graham cracker crumbs make a delightful party dessert.
