Title:
Baked salmon fillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3467632.jpg
Ingredients:
Salmon filet, olive oil, coarsed cracked pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Preheat over 350F
2.  Olive oil to griddle surface
3. Place thawed filet skin side down on the cool griddle
4. A little olive oil over the surface of the fish
5. Cracked pepper over the fish to taste
6. Put in preheated oven lower third.
7. set timer 20 minutes and done

Submitter: 
Jeffrey MacNair
Description: 
Simple clean wild salmon filet cooked on a cast iron griddle
