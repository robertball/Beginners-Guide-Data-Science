Title:
Alabama White Barbecue Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c. light mayonnaise
3 T low-fat buttermilk
2 T cider vinegar
2 t Dijon mustard
1 t sugar
1/2 t coarsely ground pepper
1/2 t salt
2 cloves garlic, minced

Directions:
Whisk together and serve with grilled chicken or pork, on sandwiches, or as a coating for grilled corn.

Submitter: 
fullerla
Description: 
Alabama's contribution to the Southern barbecue tradition is a great alternative to a traditional red barbecue sauce.  This recipe is from cookbook author Meredith Deeds.
