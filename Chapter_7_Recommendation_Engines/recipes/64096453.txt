Title:
Perfect Summer Strawberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 head Romaine Lettuce, torn up washed and spun dry
1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
1/2 box local strawberries(about 12 nice sized ones) washed, hulled and sliced.
1/2 English cucumber, diced.
1/4 cup slivered almonds, toasted.
Poppy seed dressing.Store bought..

Directions:
Combine all except the poppy seed dressing in a large bowl. Add  poppy seed dressing to your taste.
