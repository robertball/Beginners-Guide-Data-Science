Title:
Slow cooker pork tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pork tenderloin (approx. 1lb.)
1 tablespoon taco seasoning
1 cup fresh salsa

Directions:
Cut tenderloin into 4 pieces. Rub each piece with taco seasoning.
Place the prepared meat into a 1-quart slow cooker. Pour salsa over the meat.
Cover and cook on low 4-5 hours, until the pork shreds easily. Toss with the pot juices and serve with your favorite taco fixings.

Submitter: 
lucylove
Description: 
Lean pork tenderloin shreds into a perfectly-seasoned filling for tacos. With only 3 ingredients you can't go wrong.
