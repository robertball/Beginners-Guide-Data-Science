Title:
Jeanine's Decadent Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1 1/2 cups flour
1/4 cup cocoa
1 tsp salt
1 cup oil
4 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Mix all in a large bowl.  Beat for about 3 minutes (batter will be very thick).  Pour batter into lightly greased 9x13 pan.  Bake at 350F for about 30 to 35 minutes (depends on oven).

Submitter: 
Joyel Hidber
Description: 
This brownie recipe is from my mom...I have made many batches of these brownies for my daughter's various school functions, etc.  It's also really good hot out of the oven with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
