Title:
Moms Cinnamon Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Package dry yeast
1/4 Cup Water
1 Teaspoon Sugar
1 cup milk
3 cups flour
1/4 cup butter
3 Tablespoons sugar
1 Egg slightly beaten
1 Teaspoon Salt
Filling
1/4 Cup softened butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 Teaspoon Cinnamon
Icing
1/4 Cup Cream
1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla
1.25 Cups Sifted Confectioners sugar

Directions:
Dissolve yeast in warm water with 1 teaspoon sugar.
Stir in milk and 1 cup flour, beat until smooth.
Let stand until batter is light and bubbly about 1/2 hour.
Cream together butter and remaining sugar.
Add slightly beaten egg, salt .
Add in remaining flour and make into soft dough.
Let rise until doubled in size about an hour.
Roll out into rectangle 12x18 inches.
Spread with softened butter and sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon.
Roll up dough tightly and slice into rolls.
Place rolls in baking pan and let rise until light and fluffy. 9x12 pan.
Bake at 400 for 20-25 minutes.
Make icing and ice while rolls still warm.
Enjoy!
