Ingredients:
3 cups Corn Chex速 cereal

4 cups Wheat Chex速 cereal
1 cup mixed nuts
6 tablespoons butter or margarine
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
2 teaspoons seasoned salt
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon onion powder

Directions:
Combined cooking method! Preheat oven to 250 degrees. In large microwavable bowl, mix cereals, nuts, pretzels and bagel chips; set aside. In small microwavable bowl, microwave butter uncovered on High about 40 seconds or until melted. Stir in seasonings. Pour over cereal mixture; stir until evenly coated.
Microwave uncovered on High 4 minutes, thoroughly stirring every 2 minutes. Transfer mix to large skillet and bake for 30 minutes, stiring every 15 minutes.  Cool and store in air tight container.
