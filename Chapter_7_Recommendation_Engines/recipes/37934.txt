Title:
Oatmeal Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1932078.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 fluid ounce butterscotch schnapps
1/2 fluid ounce Irish cream liqueur
1/2 fluid ounce jagermeister liqueur
1/2 fluid ounce cinnamon schnapps

Directions:
In a shot glass, layer butterscotch schnapps, Irish cream and Jagermeister. Float cinnamon schnapps on top.
