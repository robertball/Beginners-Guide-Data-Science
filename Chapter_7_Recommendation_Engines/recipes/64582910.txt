Title:
Sour Cream Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup flour
2 teaspoons Baking powder
1/8 tsp baking soda
3/4 teaspoon salt
3 Tablespoons shortening
1/3 cup each milk and sour cream

Directions:
Mix together the dry ingredients then cut in shortening then add milk and sour cream. Put on floured board and knead 10 times. Roll out and cut our biscuits and bake 425 about 10 mins
