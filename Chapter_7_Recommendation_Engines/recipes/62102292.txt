Title:
Broccoli Divan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T. butter
3 T. flour
1-1/2 cups chicken broth
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 c. mayonaisse (lower fat if desired)
1/2 c. whipping cream (or non-fat1/2&1/2)
5 T. sherry
2 pkgs. frozen broccoli stems, cooked
1 c. grated Parmesan
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Melt butter in med. saucepan. Whisk in flour. Gradually add chicken broth and stir with whisk till thickened. Stir in nutmeg. Let cool slightly. Blend in mayonaisse,whipping cream or fat-free 1/2 & 1/2,sherry,salt and pepper. Set aside.
Cook broccoli per pkg. directions, drain.
In a 9X13 casserole, arrange broccoli.
Sprinkle with 1/2 the parmesan, and pour sauce over all. Sprinkle with remaining parmesan.
Bake at 325 deg. about 30 minutes or till lightly golden and bubbly.
Can be made day ahead and refrigerated. Let come to room temperature before baking.

Submitter: 
SheasLounge
Description: 
Great broccoli dish in a rich creamy sauce enhanced with sherry (my fav) and topped off with parmesan cheese.
An elegant dish that your guests will love.
Can be adjusted for the fat-conscious and turns out just as great!!
