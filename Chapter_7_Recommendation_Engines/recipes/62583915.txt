Title:
Oven Chicken Tenders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken tenderloin filets
4 Tablespoons butter, melted or 2 egg whites, beaten
1 cup dry breadcrumbs
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon dried chopped parsley
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees.
Combine the breadcrumbs, cheese, parsley, salt, and pepper in a pie plate or wide bowl.
Dip one chicken tender at a time into the melted butter or egg whites, then dip in the breadcrumb mixture, coating well on both sides.
Place breaded chicken pieces on a nonstick baking sheet or greased large baking dish.
Bake for 12-15 minutes, turning once during baking.
