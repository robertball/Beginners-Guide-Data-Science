Title:
Absolutely Delicious Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 64 ounce can of V8 juice
3-4 stalks celery cut up
3 large carrots cut up
2-3 medium potatoes cubed
1 onion cut up
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
pinch of black pepper
1 1/2 pounds of beef cut of your choice, cubed
1 can corn
1 1/2 cups green beans
1 1/2 Tablespoons of quick tapioca

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Mix all ingredients together in a large pot, close lid
cook for 5-8 hours
serve

Submitter: 
StevenRN
Description: 
One of the best tasting beef stews you will ever eat! Also, the little effort required for this recipe is just unbeatable! But really this recipe can be altered any way you like, with whatever veggies you like!
