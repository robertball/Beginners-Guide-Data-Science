Title:
Venison Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/543072.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground deer
1 lb ground pork
1 1/2 c dried herb dressing mix crushed into crumbs
1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1/2 medium onion, finely chopped
1/2 medium bell pepper, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
2 eggs
1tsp Natures Seasoning
1/2 c beef broth
1/2 c ketchup
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
Stir all of the ingredients in a large bowl until well mixed.  Pour into a 9x5 loaf pan and bake at 350 degrees for 50-60 minutes.  Top with your favorite sauce for the last 15 minutes of baking if you like.

Submitter: 
agadoelleon
Description: 
A delicious and lean meatloaf made with deer/pork butt I created myself.  It made a hearty meal served with honey sweet potatoes, turnip greens and homemade sourdough bread.  Hope you enjoy this recipe.
