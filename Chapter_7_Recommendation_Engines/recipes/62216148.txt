Title:
Cicken Tortilla Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large bag 10 inch tortillas
1 1/2 cup sour cream
2 cups chopped cooked  chicken
1 large jar salsa (mild to hot)
1 Large can refried beans
2 tsp chili powder
1 pkg shredded mexican cheddar mix
You can add alot of other things to the chicken mixture but my family cannot eat anything too spicy

Directions:
Spray inside of spring form pan.
Mix together chopped chicken, sour cream , 3/4 cup of shredded cheese and chili powder, save rest of cheese for top of pie ( add your spicy additions here if you want)
This is a layered type pie so start with one tortilla on bottom
Spread 1/4 of refried beans over tortilla
another tortilla over refried beans
Spread 1/4 of the chicken mixture over tortilla
Another tortilla over chicken mixture
Spread 1/4 of salsa over tortilla
Another tortilla over salsa
Repeat layers until you have tortilla on top
Spread remaining cheese over top of tortilla
Cover with foil
Bake at 350 for 45 to 60 minutes
Take out of oven and let  sit for 10 minutes to set.
Unlatch and allow to sit for 2 to 3 minutes
Cut  and serve...

Submitter: 
C.Lynn
Description: 
I made this for many many people and have never had a bad review....make sure you use a springform pan. Serve with sour cream and salsa on the side and a salad....
