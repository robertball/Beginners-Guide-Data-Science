Title:
Summertime Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 onion, diced
1 lb. Italian turkey sausage, sliced
1-2 zucchini or yellow squash, sliced
Water
3-4 fresh tomatoes, diced
1 can black beans
2 cubes chicken bouillon
Rosemary
Sage
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Saute onion in olive oil over medium heat.  Add sliced sausage and brown.
Add squash and continue to cook until softened, adding water as necessary to keep squash from scorching.
Add remaining ingredients and cover with water to make soup.  Let simmer to enhance the flavors.

Submitter: 
Mrs.Morgan
Description: 
I'm always looking for creative ways to use the bountiful zucchini from our garden in the summer. This one was a hit with my family!
