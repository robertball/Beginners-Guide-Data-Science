Title:
Swedish Cabbage Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 large cabbage leaves
1 whole egg white, beaten
1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup onion, finely chopped
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 pound ground beef, browned and drained
1 cup rice, cooked
16 ounces tomato sauce
2 tablespoon brown sugar
2 tablespoon lemon juice
2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Core the cabbage head.  Place the head, core side down, in 1 inch of boiling water to steam the leaves.  Cover and check frequently to peel off the outer leaves as they become soft and pliable.
Combine egg, milk, onions, salt, pepper, beef and rice.
Place about 1/4 cup meat mixture in the center of each cabbage leaf.  Fold in sides and roll ends over meat.  Place in slow cooker.
Combine tomato sauce, brown sugar, lemon juice and Worcestershire sauce.  Pour over cabbage rolls.
Cover.  Cook on low 7-9 hours.
