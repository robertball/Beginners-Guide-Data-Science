Title:
Oaktown Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/395405.jpg
Ingredients:
8 Green or Red Peppers
1 lb ground turkey
Frozen spinach chopped squeezed dry (10oz package)
2 cups cooked brown rice
1 large onion chopped finely
2 large tomatoes chopped finely
6 garlic cloves minced
1 T and 1 t dried dill
Frozen chopped spinach package (10oz) squeezed dry
2 cups cooked brown rice
2 T chopped parsley
1/3 c golden or thompson raisins
1/2 cup pine nuts or chopped pisatchios
juice of 1 lemon
1 cup crumbled feta cheese
1 T Braggs Amino Acids or Soy Sauce
Ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Saute the onions, 1/2 of the garlic and turkey on medium heat until cooked (aprox. 8 minutes)
Mix together remaining ingredients and season with pepper or Braggs to taste.
Stuff peppers with the mixture.
Add 1/3 cup water to bottom of a large casserole dish.
Place stuffed peppers in dish and bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
JoyFullGrl
Description: 
Ground turkey, brown rice, golden raisins, feta, nuts and spinach create a dish where each bite is filled with flavor.
