Title:
puffed wheat squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 heaping cups puffed wheat cereal
1 cup margarine
2/3 cup cocoa powder
2 cups brown sugar
1 cup corn syrup
2 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Scoop puffed wheat into a large bowl and set aside.
Place margarine, cocoa, brown sugar, syrup, vanilla into a pot and stir over med/high heat until combined.
Let mixture boil for 2 minutes. *DO NOT boil for too long or the bars will become hard.
Remove the pot and pour the hot mixture over the cereal.  Toss cereal until combined and press firmly into a greased 9x13 pan
Allow to cool completely before cutting

Submitter: 
Terri Hansinge
Description: 
This is a mixture of many recipes and trial and error to find a square that is chocolate enough and doesn't fall apart
