Title:
cheflucas's double chocolate banana surprise cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887169.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 c.all purpose flour
3/4 c.coca powder
2 c.white sugar
1 1/2 tsp.baking powder
1 tsp.salt
1 1/2 tsp.baking soda
2 eggs
1 c.milk
1/2c.vegetable oil
2 tsp.vanilla
1 c.water, boiling
2 bananas, thickly sliced
3 oz. chocolate chips
6 oz. chocolate, melted
2 oz.white chocolate, melted

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and grease or paper cupcake tin
mix flour,sugar,coca,baking soda,baking powder,and salt in a mixer or large bowl.
mix in eggs, milk, vanilla and oil
add boiling water and mix
fill cupcake tins 1/3 way full
place slice of banana in center of each cupcake and lightly press down
one at a time place dollop of melted chocolate on banana and fill with batter in till the cupcake tin is 2/3ds of the way full.
repeat until all tins are full
sprinkle cupcakes with chocolate chips
bake at 350 degrees for 30 min.
take out to cool and remove from trays
after completely cool drizzle tops of cupcakes with melted white and dark chocolate
enjoy!
