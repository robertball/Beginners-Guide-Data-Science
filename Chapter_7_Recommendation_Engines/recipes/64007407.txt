Title:
Corn Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all purpose flour (10oz)
1 cup cornmeal (5oz)
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp table salt
2 large eggs
3/4 cup sugar (5 1/4oz)
8 T butter (1 stick), melted
3/4 c sour cream
1/2 c milk

Directions:
Oven to 400; rack to middle position.  Spray standard size muffin tins.
Whisk flour, cornmeal, baking powder, baking soda and salt together and set aside.
Whisk eggs in medium bowl.  Add sugar to eggs and whisk until thick (30 sec).  Add melted butter and whisk.
Add half of the sour cream and half of the milk and whisk.  Add remaining half sour cream and milk and whisk once more.
Add wet ingredients to dry and fold with rubber spatula until just combined and evenly moistened.
Using a 1/3c measure, divide batter evenly among cups, dropping the batter to form mounds.  Do not level or flatten surface of the mounds.
Bake until light golden brown, apx 18 minutes, rotating pan halfway through baking period.  Cool in pan for 5 minutes then transfer to wire rack and cool for 10 minutes before serving.
