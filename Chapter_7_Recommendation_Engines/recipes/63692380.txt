Title:
YOGURT COOL WHIP PIE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 oz.) containers greek yogurt
1 (8 oz.) container Extra Creamy Cool Whip, defrosted
3/4 cup mashed fruit of your choice
1 9" prepared graham cracker pie crust

Directions:
In a large bowl, fold together the yogurt, cool whip and mashed fruit. Mound into the pie crust; decorate with fresh fruit if desired. Lightly cover with plastic wrap.
Freeze at least 6 hours or until firm. Thaw 1/2 hour before serving.
