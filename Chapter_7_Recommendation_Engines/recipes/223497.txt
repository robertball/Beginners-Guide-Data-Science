Title:
Watermelon Summertime Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1160837.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes
1/2 cup coconut water (liquid from inside coconut)
1/4 cup cherries, pitted
1 cup cubed, seeded watermelon
1 teaspoon granular sucralose sweetener (such as SplendaÂ®) (optional)

Directions:
Place ice in a blender and pour in coconut water; add cherries, watermelon cubes, and sweetener. Cover and blend until slushy, about 1 minute. Pour into 2 glasses to serve.

Description: 
A crisp and refreshing summertime treat! This drink is a great afternoon pick-me-up.
