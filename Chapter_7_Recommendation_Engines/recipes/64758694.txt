Title:
Biscotti - Plain Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7027716.jpg
Ingredients:
Flour 160g
Sugar 70g
Egg 100g
Almond Flour 40g
BP 4
Almond 60g
Cranberry 40g

Directions:
1. Mix Egg and Sugar
2. Add flour mixture ( flour, BP, Almond powder , sifted)
3. Add Almond and Cranberry
4. Put the cookie dough on flour dusting table, Rolled to a log
5. Bake at 180/160 for 15 min
6. Cut into 3mm slice
7. Bake at 150C for 40 min

Crunching and crispy, full of nutty flavor
