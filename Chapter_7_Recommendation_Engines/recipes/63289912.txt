Title:
Polish Sausage and Kraut on Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 10" Polish Sausage
4 Hoagie Rolls
1 Bag Sauerkraut
1 Green Apple, Sliced
1 Onion, thinly sliced
1 T Brown Sugar
1/2 Cup Light Ale
Brown Mustard

Directions:
Brown Onion and Apple with sugar until golden brown.  Add rinsed and drained kraut and ale.  Cover and cook 10 to 15 minutes until apple and onion are cooked through.
Cook Sausage over fire until brown and crispy.
Serve on buns with mustard and Kraut.
