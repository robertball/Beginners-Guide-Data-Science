Title:
New England Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups dried great northern beans
8 cups water
1 1/4 cup chopped onion
1 cup barbecue sauce
3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 cup molasses
1 tbs yellow mustard
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/8 tsp garlic powder
4 slices bacon, cut into pieces

Directions:
Sort and wash the beans, place in a large dutch oven and cover with water 2 inches above the beans. Bring to a boil and cook 2 minutes. Remove from heat and let stand 1 hour. Drain beans and return to pan. Add 8 cups of water and chopped onion. Bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat and simmer 2 hours. Preheat oven to 350. Drain bean mixture and return to pan. Add barbecue sauce and remaining ingredients and stir well. Turn into a baking dish and bake for 1 hour.
