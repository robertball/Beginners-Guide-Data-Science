Title:
Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C pineapple juice (or 4 thawed cubes)
2 scoops vanilla frozen yogurt
1/2 C frozen fruit
1/2 over ripe banana
1/2 Tbl pasturized, Canadian liquid honey

Directions:
Put all in your blender. Blend continuously for 1 minute. Pulse for 2 minutes, or until it is smooth. Serve.
