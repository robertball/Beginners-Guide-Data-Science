Title:
Tomatillo Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3101823.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Avocado
1 Large Tomatillo
1/2 Medium White Onion
1-2 Garlic Cloves
1-2 Jalapenos
1/2 tsp. Ground Cumin
2 Tbsp. Chopped Fresh Cilantro
1 Lime, juiced
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Roughly chop all ingredients, except the lime, and put into a blender. Add all the juice from the lime.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Taste, season if needed.
Serve with chips, or on your favorite tacos!

Submitter: 
Jill Garcia Schmidt
Description: 
Tangy, spicy, creamy and delicious - this may very well change the way you eat guac forever... or at least it will be your new favorite dip.
