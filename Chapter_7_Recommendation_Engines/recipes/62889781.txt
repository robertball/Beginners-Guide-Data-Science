Title:
cabbage with apples and onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cabbage shredded
1 large onion sliced thin
1 large apple peeled and sliced thin
1tps savory (ground)
1tsp caraway seed
salt and pepper to taste
1/2c butter

Directions:
melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat
add cabbage, apples and onions and seasonings
mix to coat with butter
cover until cabbage wilts then remove cover and cook stirring occasionally until onions and cabbage caramelize.
