Title:
Spicy Tequila-Lime Tomato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
28 ounces canned fire roasted tomatoes
1 can crushed tomatoes
2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
2 jalapeno peppers seeded and chopped
1 green bell pepper seeded and chunked
1 large onion, chunked
4 ribs celery chunked
5 cloves garlic chopped
2 cups vegetable stock
1/4 cup tequila
juice of one lime
3 tablespoons chopped cilantro
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large soup pot, saute the garlic, onions and celery in olive oil until softened. Add jalapeno and bell pepper and saute until just soft.
Add tequila and simmer until slightly reduced - about 3 or 4 minutes.
Add tomatoes, stock and lime juice. Bring to a boil and reduce to simmer for at least 20 minutes.
Stir in cilantro just before serving.

Submitter: 
cloe
Description: 
Simple, inexpensive and hearty, my boyfriend and I love this different take on boring tomato soup. Best of all it is very low in calories. Add shrimp for a protein boost.
