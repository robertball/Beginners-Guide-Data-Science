Title:
Zucchini Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 C oil
2 C sugar
2 T vanilla
3 C Flour
1 T cinnamon
1 1/2 t baking soda
1 t salt
1/4 baking powder
1 c nuts (optional)
3 c grated zucchini

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Dont overmix. Bake at 375 for 20-25 minutes
