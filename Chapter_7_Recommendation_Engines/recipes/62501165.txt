Title:
Man Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/499607.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 pound thick sliced bacon (pork or turkey)
1/2 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed
1/2 (8 ounce) container fat free sour cream
1 (9 inch)  unbaked pie crust (rolled up)
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 onion, finely diced
1/4 pound fresh mushrooms, finely diced
4 ounces Monterey Jack cheese, shredded low fat
4 ounces Cheddar cheese, shredded low fat
2 ounces Parmesan/Reggiano/Asiago cheese mix (DiGorgno)
4 eggs
3/4 cups fat free half-and-half
1 TBSP dried thyme
salt and white pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
Unroll pie crust into 10 inch pie plate, gently stretching to fit, prick dough and bake for 5-10 minutes.
Place bacon in a cookie sheet, and cook in oven 10 minutes or until crispy and set aside.
Thaw spinach in microwave, drain, set aside.
Heat olive oil in skillet over medium heat. Saute onions until soft and translucent.
Stir in mushrooms, and cook for 2 minutes, or until soft.
Stir in cooked bacon. Remove from heat.
In a large bowl, combine spinach, sour cream, salt and pepper. Spread into pie crusts.
Layer with bacon mixture.
Mix together Monterey Jack, Cheddar and Parmesan, and sprinkle over pie.
Whisk together eggs, half-and-half and thyme. Season with salt and pepper, and pour over pie.
Place pie on baking sheet, and bake on middle shelf  in preheated oven for 40 minutes. The top will be puffed and golden brown. Remove from oven, and let stand for 5 to 10 minutes.
