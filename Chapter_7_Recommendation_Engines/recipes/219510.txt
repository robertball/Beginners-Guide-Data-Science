Title:
Fiesta Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982481.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) jar PaceÂ® Picante Sauce
1/4 cup water
2 cups uncooked quick-cooking brown rice
1 cup canned black beans, rinsed and drained
1/2 cup frozen whole kernel corn, thawed

Directions:
Heat picante sauce and 1/4 cup water in a saucepan to a boil.
Stir in rice, beans and corn. Cover and remove from heat. Let stand 5 minutes. Fluff with fork.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Ordinary rice gets extraordinary flavor when it's cooked with picante sauce instead of water.
