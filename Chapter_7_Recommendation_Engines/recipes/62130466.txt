Title:
Maple Syrup Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups of warm water
4 tablespoons of cornstarch
1 1/3 cups of maple syrup
Baked 9‚Äù pie shell
Whipped cream (optional)

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, mix together the warm water, cornstarch, and maple syrup.
Bring to a boil and then reduce the heat to a low boil for 7 minutes.
Turn off the heat and wait for one minute, then pour into the baked pie shell. Let cool on the stove.
Garnish with whipped cream just before serving (optional).

Submitter: 
Charlene Li

A delicious, rich pie that's amazingly easy to make.
