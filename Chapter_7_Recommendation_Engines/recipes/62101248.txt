Title:
Broccoli Side Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. frozen broccoli florets
2 slices bacon, chopped
1/4 onion, chopped
1 tsp. chopped garlic
1 T. butter
Salt and Pepper, to taste

Directions:
Heat saucepan (small or medium) on medium-medium high heat.  Add bacon and onions and cook until bacon is cooked through.
Prepare frozen broccoli, covered in water, in microwave for 2 minutes.
Drain the broccoli and add to the bacon and onions.  Add garlic, salt and pepper, and butter. Heat through and put on low heat till ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Amanda
Description: 
I made this out of nowhere for Christmas dinner and it turned out easy and yummy!  Added nice color to the plate and tied in with the ham.
