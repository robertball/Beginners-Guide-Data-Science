Title:
Quick and Easy Baked Ziti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/278475.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound dry ziti or rigatoni pasta
1 onion, chopped
1 pound bulk Italian sausage
2 (26 ounce) jars high grade spaghetti sauce, preferable chunky with olives
15 ounces pre-shreded Italian 4 cheese blend
1 1/2 cups sour cream

Directions:
Bring a large pot of well salted water to a boil. Add ziti pasta, and cook until al dente, drian.
In a large skillet, brown the sausage over medium heat. Do not drain.  Add spaghetti sauce and combine.
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a 9x13 inch baking dish, layer as follows: 1/2 of the ziti, 6 ounces 4 cheese blend, sour cream in dabs, 1/2 sauce mixture, remaining ziti, 6 ounces 4 cheese blend, remaining sour cream in dabs, and remaining sauce mixture. Top with 3 ounces of 4 cheese blend.
Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until cheeses are melted.
