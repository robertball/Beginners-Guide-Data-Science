Title:
Vegetable Oven Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
27 eggs
1 1/2C sour cream
1 1/2C milk
2t salt
1/2t basil
3C shredded cheddar cheese
6-8 oz can sliced mushrooms, drained
1/2C chopped green onion
9x13 pan
22 eggs
1 1/4C sour crean
1 1/4C milk
2 1/2C cheese
8x6 pan
8 eggs
1/2C sour cream
1/2C milk
1C cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
Grease baking pan
In very large bowl, beat eggs and add sour cream, milk, salt and basil. Stir in cheese, mushrooms and onions.
Pour mixture into prepared dish and bake 55 minutes until edges are slightly brown and knife comes out clean. Rotate pan 1/2 way thru baking.
Garnish with tomato slices. Can vary recipe with chopped peppers or other veggies.
Can microwave the vegetables to remove excess water.
