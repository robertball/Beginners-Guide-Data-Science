Title:
Creamy Salsa Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16-oz carton sour cream
1 jar pre-made salsa (your choice)

Directions:
In a large bowl with a lid, mix the 2 ingredients together. Chill in the frig several hours or until ready to use. Serve with corn tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Tanaquil
Description: 
This is the dip I take to every family function or luncheon. You can change it up by using a variety of salsas.
