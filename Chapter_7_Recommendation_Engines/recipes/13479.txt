Title:
Easy Pumpkin Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4576297.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups pumpkin puree
1 cup evaporated milk
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup self-rising flour
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup butter
1 pinch ground cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Combine the pumpkin, evaporated milk, sugar, flour, eggs, vanilla, melted butter and ground cinnamon to taste. Spoon into a casserole dish.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Lori DeLosh
Description: 
This is super easy, something different and tastes great with the main course. Enjoy!
