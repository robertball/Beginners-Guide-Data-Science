Title:
Decorated Spice Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1/2 teaspoon each ground nutmeg and salt
1/4 teaspoon each ground allspice and cloves
1 1/2 cups butter or margarine, at room temperature
1 cup granulated sugar
1 large egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Mix flour, spices, and salt
In another bowl, beat butter & sugar until light and fluffy
Beat in egg and vanilla
Gradually add flour mixture, beating until well blended
Divide dough in thirds
Shape each into a ball and flatten to a 1-inch-thick round
Wrap in plastic
Refrigerate 1 hour or until firm enough to roll
Preheat Oven to 375 degrees
On lightly floured surface roll 1/3 of dough to 1/4in. thick
Cut dough in desired shapes
Place 1 inch apart on ungreased cookie sheets
Bake about 12 minutes or until cookies are golden brown
Cool completely on sheet on wire rack
Ice and decorate as desired
Let stand until icing hardens, about 2 hours
