Title:
Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb cubed pork
2 carrots diced
1 small onion diced
1 gr pepper diced
2 pkgs quick brown rice
2 eggs

Directions:
Cook rice according to package directions.
Stir fry pork and vegetables. Fry eggs.
Mix with rice.
Season with curry, red pepper, and salt and pepper.
Serve
