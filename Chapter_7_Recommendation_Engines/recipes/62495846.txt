Title:
Monkey Munch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 Cup Chex
1 Cup Chocolate Chips
1/2 Cup Peanut Butter
1/4 Cup Butter
1/4 Tsp. Vanilla
1 1/2 Cup Powdered Sugar

Directions:
Put cereal in large bowl.
Melt chocolate chips, peanut butter, and butter together in the microwave. (Just until melty)
Remove from heat and stir in vanilla.
Pour over Chex cereal, put into a large plastic bag with powdered sugar and shake well to coat.
