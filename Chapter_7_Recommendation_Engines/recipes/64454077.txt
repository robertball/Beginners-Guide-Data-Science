Title:
Six Spice Maple Cornish Hens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2396500.jpg
Ingredients:
4 teaspoons Ground Cumin
2 teaspoons Ground Ginger
2 teaspoons Garlic Powder
1 1/2 teaspoons Ground Red Pepper
1/2 teaspoon Ground Black Pepper
1/2 teaspoon Thyme Leaves
1 Cornish Game Hen
1/2 tablespoon olive oil
1/3 cup pure maple syrup
1 green onion, sliced

Directions:
Combine spices; set aside.
Rinse Cornish hen.  Pat dry with paper towels.  Split hen in half; brush with olive oil. Rub hen halves with spice mixture; coat well.
Grill over medium-hot coals or broil in an aluminum foil lined broiler pan 12-14 minutes per side or until done.  Gently brush with maple syrup and grill or broil 3 minutes longer.  Sprinkle with green onion slices before serving.

Submitter: 
Carol Hammond
Description: 
These Cornish game hens are bursting with sweet heat!  The flavor and heat from six spices are balanced by the sweetness of maple syrup.Modified from: Six Spice Maple Cornish Hens
