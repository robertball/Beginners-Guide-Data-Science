Title:
Easy One Pot Ground Meat and Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground meat (any kind)
1 jar of spaghetti sauce
1 14 oz can of chicken broth
1 onion chopped
3 cloves of garlic chopped
2 TBS fresh oregano chopped
1 tsp dried basil
1 tsp garlic powder
3 Cups of dried pasta like elbows
Grated Parmesan Cheese To taste
Salt

Directions:
Heat  a high sided 12 inch skillet, with a lid over medium heat. Add the ground meat, onion and fresh garlic.
Cook until meat is done.
Add garlic powder, basil, oregano and dried pasta-mix well.
Add the pasta sauce. To the empty pasta sauce jar, add the broth and shake.
Pour over the pasta,  mix well and bring to a boil.
Cover, Turn down heat and cook until pasta is done about 7 minutes.
Season with salt, pepper and Parmesan cheese.

Description: 
This is a quick and easy main dish meal.  It’s very easy to substitute your favorite ground meat, broth, spices and pasta shape.  For those who want an easy meal or those beginning to cook this is for you. Bigger pasta shapes will take a bit longer to cook them the recipe says- 3 cups is about 1/2 a box of pasta.
