Title:
Gizzies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1075088.jpg
Ingredients:
4	cups cheerios cereal
4	cups pretzel sticks
4	cups rice chex cereal
4	cups wheat chex cereal
4	cups mixed nuts
1	stick butter or margarine or ½ cup vegetable or canola oil Not olive oil.
½	cup Worcestershire sauce
1	to 3 tablespoons garlic powder to taste
3	to 4 teaspoons hot sauce to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 200.
In a small saucepan melt butter over low heat and add the Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder and hot sauce.  Mix well.  Remove from heat and let cool a little.
In a large roasting pan, add all cereals, pretzels and nuts.  Stir.
Pour butter mixture into a clean condiment squeeze bottle.  Put top on and sprinkle a few tablespoons over cereal mixture, (Be careful the warm sauce mix will squirt out top as you lift bottle.)  Stir cereal mixture well to distribute sauce,  Add a few tablespoons more and stir again, until everything has some sauce on it.  For strong flavor really wet the cereal mixture.
Bake at 200 degrees stirring every 15 to 30 minutes to help the mixture dry.  Once dry and crunchy it’s done.  remove from oven and cool then bag.
