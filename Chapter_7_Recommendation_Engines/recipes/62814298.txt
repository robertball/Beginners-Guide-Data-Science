Title:
granola granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/662711.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups rolled oats
1/2 cup sesame seeds
1/4 cup flax seed
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp kosher salt
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup honey
1/2 cup canola oil
1 tsp pure vanilla extract
1 cup almonds
1 cup pumpkin seeds
1 cup dried cherries
1 cup cranberries

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
2) line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silt pat.
3)  In a large bowl combine oats, sesame seeds, flax seed, cinnamon and salt.
4) In another bowl, combine brown sugar, canola oil, honey, and vanilla extract.
5) Mix wet ingredients in with the dry ingredients.
6) Place oat mixture on the lined baking sheet and bake for 10 mins.
7) After 10 min, take out pan, and fluff oats with a fork. Add the almonds and pumpkin seeds. Put back in oven for another 10 mins.
8) take it out, and fluff with a fork. Add the cherries and cranberries. Put back in oven for an additional 5-10 mins.
9) Take out of oven and cool for 15 -25 mins. (Granola should harden)

Description: 
I love me some granola and this recipe makes the best granola ever! And, you can make a big batch, store it in a plastic container, and use it for a healthy treat!
