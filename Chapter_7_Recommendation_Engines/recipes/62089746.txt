Title:
Brazilian Milk Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoon sugar
1 can sweetened condensed milk
2 cans (same measurement) milk
3 eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a heavy saucepan over medium heat, cook sugar, stirring, until melted and light brown. Pour into a pudding pan (a pan with a whole in the middle â€“ 19 cm diameter), tilting to coat the bottom of the pan. Set aside.
In a blender, combine sweetened condensed milk, milk, and eggs. Blend for 30 seconds. Pour over the caramelized sugar.
Place the filled pan into a larger pan and add 1 inch of HOT water to the outer pan. Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 90 minutes, or until set.
Chill until serving.

Submitter: 
Cacau
Description: 
This pudding is a  very common dessert in Brazil, being the most popular pudding of all. It is simple and absolutely delicious.
