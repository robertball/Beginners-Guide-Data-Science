Title:
Sweet Potato Soup - Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Medium Sweet Potatoes - Sliced
1 Medium Onion - Sliced
2 16oz Cans of Coconut Milk
1 Tbsp Basil
1 Tbsp Chili Powder
1 Tbsp Cumin

Directions:
Add all ingredients to the slow cooker.
Mix well.
Cook on high heat for 6 hours (8 hours on low heat)
Using a ladle, transfer solid chunks of food into a food processor and pulse until emulsified. Perform this step until all food solids have been emulsified.
