Title:
Lynn's Tamale Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground beef
1 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes
1 c. chopped onion
1 c. frozen corn, thawed, or 1 can corn, drained
1 c. chopped green pepper
1 tsp. chili powder
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 c. sliced black olives
Salt & Pepper to taste
1 c. water
1/2 c. cornmeal
For the Topping:
1/2 c. cornmeal
1 c. milk
1 1/2 c. shredded chedder cheese
2 tbsp. butter
2 eggs

Directions:
Brown meat with onion, garlic, and green pepper. Drain.
Add tomatoes, simmer 5 minutes.
Add salt and spices.
Combine 1/2 cornmeal and water.
Add to meat mixture, simmer 10 minutes.
Add olives.
Pour into 3 quart casserole dish.
For the topping:
Heat milk, other 1/2 c. cornmeal, and butter over low heat until thickened.
Remove from heat.
Stir in eggs and cheese.
Pour on top of meat mixture.
Bake at 375 for 30-35 minutes or until filling is bubbling and crust is golden brown.

Submitter: 
Allyson Brown Howard
Description: 
Delicious cheesy corn spoonbread tops a meaty casserole chock-full of veggies. Mild, yet flavorful tex-mex fare.
