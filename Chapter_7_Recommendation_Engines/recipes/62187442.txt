Title:
Brown Sugar Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds lean ground saucebeef
1/2 cup milk
1 eggs
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 small onion, chopped (or 1 envelope of dry onion soup)
2 garlic cloves - minced
1 stalk of celery - chopped
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
3/4 cup finely crushed saltine cracker crumbs (or 1 cup seasoned bread crumbs smashed)
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup ketchup
1 T Worcerstershire

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 5x9 inch loaf pan.
In a mixing bowl, mix thoroughly the first 10 ingredients and shape into a loaf.
Mix the brown sugar with tgh ketchup and Worcestershire sauce and place on top of the loaf.
Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour or until juices are clear.
