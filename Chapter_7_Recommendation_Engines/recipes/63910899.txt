Title:
Creamy Mushroom Cappucino Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1084769.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tbsp unsalted butter
1 yellow onion, chopped
2 lb mushrooms, chopped (mix and match cremini, portobello, oyster, shitake, porcini.
3 tbsp fresh taragon
1 tbsp dried thyme
4 cups vegetable broth
2/3 cup sour cream
salt and pepper to taste
extra creamy whipped cream (optional)

Directions:
In a stock pot over medium heat, cook onion in 2 tbsp unsalted butter.
Add remaining butter and mushrooms and cook until mushrooms are browned, about 10 minutes.
Stir in taragon, thyme, and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and let simmer for 20 minutes
Puree soup using an emersion blender or a food processer.
Stir in sour cream, salt, and pepper.
Serve hot topped with whipped cream.

Submitter: 
Jose Napoleon Cuasay Artiaga I
Description: 
This is a simple and flavorful soup that is a perfect starter to any meal. Like a cappucino, this soup is served with whipped cream on top, giving it a wonderful hot/cold contrast.
