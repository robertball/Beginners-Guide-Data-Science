Title:
Rotisserie Ragout Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 Scallions with green, chopped
1 stalk celery with leaves
3 cloves garlic, chopped
1 jalapeno, stemmed, seeded and diced
dash chili powder
olive oil
1 cup chicken broth
6 oz coconut water
1 Tbsp cilantro, fresh, chopped
1 1/2 cup rotisserie chicken diced or shredded
2 Tbsp chia seeds or 1 Tbsp corn starch

Directions:
Saute scallions, celery, garlic, chili powder and jalapeno in olive oil in a 2 quart pot until the celery is tender.  Add chicken broth and cilantro and bring to a boil then simmer for about 10 minutes.  Add chicken and bring to a boil.  Add Chia seeds and cook until mixture thickens or cook until most of the liquid is gone then add 1 T of corn starch in enough water to make a thin paste and stir to thicken.
