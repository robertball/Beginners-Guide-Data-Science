Title:
Gluten Free Molasses Ginger Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/892398.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Butter
1/3 cup Shortening
2 cups Sugar
2 Eggs
1/2 cup Molasses
3 cups White Rice Flour
1 cup Almond Meal Flour
2 tsp Xanthan Gum
4 tsp Baking Soda
2 tsp Cinnamon
2 tsp Ginger
1 tsp Cloves
1 tsp Salt
1/2 cup White Sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Grease cookie sheet (I use butter)
In a bowl mix white rice flour, almond meal flour, baking soda, xanthan gum, and salt.  Set aside.
In a large bowl cream butter, shortening and sugar.  Beat in eggs and molasses, then add in spices (ginger, cloves and cinnamon) mix well.  Hand stir in flour mixture, batter should be stiff when finished.
Roll cookies into balls of desired size.  Roll balls in remaining sugar.  Place on greased cookie sheet 3 inches apart.  Medium cookies take about 10-12 minutes to bake, Small cookies take about 8-10 minutes to bake.  Cookies will crackle across the top.
