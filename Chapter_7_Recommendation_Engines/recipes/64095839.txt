Title:
Italian Sausage Soup w/ Adjustments Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound sweet Italian sausage
1 clove garlic, minced (jarred okay)
2 (32 ounce) boxes beef broth
2 (14.5 ounce) can Italian-style diced tomatoes undrained
1 - 2 cups V8 vegetable juice (Add at the very end of cooking time)
1 1/2 cup sliced carrots
2 (14.5 ounce) can great Northern beans, undrained
2 small zucchini, cubed
2 cups FRESH spinach - packed, rinsed and torn
1/4 sweet basil
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a stockpot or Dutch oven, lpbrown sausage with garlic.  Stir in  broth, tomatoes and carrots, and season with salt sweet basil and pepper. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 15 minutes.
Stir in beans with liquid and zucchini. Cover, and simmer another 15 minutes, or until zucchini is tender.
Add V8 vegetable juice now, if thinning is necessary to obtain the consistency desired.
Remove from heat, and add spinach.  Replace lid allowing the heat from the soup to cook the spinach leaves.  Soup is ready to serve after 5 minutes.
If desired, cook Ditilini pasta according to package directions.  Add to each bowl individually as desired.  Store pasta separately from soup in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Carol Hammond
Description: 
This soup is easy to put together, and the flavor of the spicy sausage is balanced nicely by Great Northern beans, zucchini, fresh spinach, and carrots.  Makes a delicious winter supper.Modified from: Italian Sausage Soup
