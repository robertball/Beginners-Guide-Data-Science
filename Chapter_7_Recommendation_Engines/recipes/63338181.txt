Title:
SWEET BITES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 oz. chopped bittersweet chocolate
1 stick unsalted butter
1/2 cup sugar
3 eggs, plus 2 beaten eggs divided
2 tbsp dark rum
1 cup plus 3 tbsp flour divided
1 1/2 cups finely crushed cornflakes
Vegetable oil for frying
powdered sugar

Directions:
Melt chocolate and butter in small saucepan over very low heat, whisk until smooth.
Transfer to large bowl, cool slightly. Add sugar and 3 eggs; whisk until combined. Whisk in fun and 3 tbsp flour. Cover with plastic rap and chill until firm, about 3 hrs
Roll chilled mixture into 24 2 inch balls. Place on lined baking sheet and freeze for 30 minutes.
Place remaining cup flour, 2 beaten eggs and cornflakes in 3 separate bowls. Dip chocolate balls first in flour, then in egg, then in cornflakes, shaking off excess after each step.  Return to baking sheet and freeze 15 minutes.
Heat 4 inch of oil in a med saucepan until a small cube of bread browns instantly when fried.
Using a slotted spoon gently lower 4-5 croquettes into oil and fry turning until golden brown on all sides.
Remove from oil and drain on a paper towels. Dust with powdered sugar
