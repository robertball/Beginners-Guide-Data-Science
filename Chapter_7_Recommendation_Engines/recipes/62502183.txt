Title:
Kaylee's Caramel Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter/margarine
2 cups brown sugar
1 cup Lilly white corn syrup
1 can Eagle brand sweetened condensed milk
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla
3 1/2 batches of popcorn

Directions:
Boil first 5 ingredients for 5 minutes.
Add vanilla.
Pour over 3 1/2 batches of popcorn.
