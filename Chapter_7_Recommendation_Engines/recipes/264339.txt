Title:
Strawberry and Spinach Balsamic Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5387892.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pouch BarillaÂ® Ready Pasta Penne
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup spinach
1/2 cup strawberries, quartered
2 basil leaves, chopped
1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
Tear corner of Ready Pasta pouch to vent. Heat in the microwave for one minute.
Pour pasta onto a dish and allow to cool.
In a bowl, combine olive oil and balsamic vinegar and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Add Ready Pasta, spinach, strawberries, and basil and toss.
Garnish with feta cheese. Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Barilla Canada
Description: 
Strawberry, spinach, basil, and balsamic vinegar come together over a canvas of Ready Pasta penne in this quick and easy pasta salad.
