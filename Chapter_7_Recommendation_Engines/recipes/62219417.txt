Title:
Beef Samosas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, finely chopped
1 lb lean ground beef
2 garlic cloves, crushed
1/4 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp ground turmeric
2 tsp ground coriander
1 1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/2 - 1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp garam masala
4 oz water
6 oz frozen peas
1 TBS pickled jalapeno pepper, minced
1 TBS lemon juice
1 package wonton wrappers
Vegetable oil, for frying

Directions:
Over medium heat, brown the ground beef along with onions and garlic. Reduce heat to low.
Mix in the ginger, turmeric, coriander, cumin, chili powder and salt.
Add the water and peas, bring to a boil, cover and simmer around 25 min, stirring occasionally. If there is any liquid left, remove the cover and cook over medium heat until dry, stirring frequently
Stir in the garam masala and jalapeno pepper
Remove from heat and stir in the lemon juice
Set aside to cool thoroughly
Drop approx 1 TBS of meat mixture in the center of a wonton wrapper. Fold edges over, moisten with water and seal.
Fry samosas in approx 1/2 inch vegetable oil until browned
