Title:
Stuffed Green Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large green peppers
1 lb hamburger
1/2 med onion chopped
1 ts salt
1/8 tsp garlic salt
1 box cooked uncle ben's original recipe rice
1 can tomato sauce
cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Remove seeds and membranes from peppers.
cook peppers in boiling water for 5 minutes, drain
cook hamburger, onion, salt, and garlic salt until hamburger is done
Cook rice as directed on box
Mix rice and hamburger mixture together, stir in tomato sauce
stuff each pepper with hamburger mixture, place in glass baking dish and cover and cook for 45 minutes, sprinkle with cheese and bake uncovered 15 minutes longer
