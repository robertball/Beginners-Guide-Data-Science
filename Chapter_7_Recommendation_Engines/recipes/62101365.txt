Title:
Trish's Bean Sprout Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups bean sprouts
1 cup unsalted de shelled peanuts
3/4 cup sesame oil
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 teaspoons white rice vinegar

Directions:
In a small bowl add sesame oil, soy sauce and rice vinegar.
In a large bowl add bean sprouts and peanuts toss.
Pour sesame oil mixture onto bean sprouts and toss.
Optional- add diced grilled chicken or tofu.
