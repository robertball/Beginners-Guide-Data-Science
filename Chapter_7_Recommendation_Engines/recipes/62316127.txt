Title:
Milky Way Cupcake Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/375491.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Milky Way candy bars, full size.
4 Tablespoons of butter
1 Tablespoon of milk
2 teaspoons of vanilla
1 1/4 cups powdered sugar

Directions:
Place Milky Way bars, butter, milk and vanilla in a microwave safe bowl and heat for 30 seconds and then stir. Heat for an additional 10 seconds, take out of the mircrowave and stir again. Add powdered sugar and mix well until creamy.

Submitter: 
House of Aqua
Description: 
This is an excellent icing that I use on my yellow cupcakes.
