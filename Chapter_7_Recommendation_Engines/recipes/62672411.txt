Title:
"Presto" Primavera (Del Monte) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz uncooked pasta;
1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes with garlic & onion;
1 can (8.5 oz) sweet peas, drained;
1 can (8 oz) cut green beans, drained;
1 can (8.25 oz) sliced carrots, drained

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions; drain. Heat tomatoes and vegetables in large saucepan while pasta is cooking. Mix in pasta and heat through. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Garnish with chopped parsley.
