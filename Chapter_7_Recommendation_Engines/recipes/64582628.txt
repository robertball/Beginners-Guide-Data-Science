Title:
Spicy Cayenne Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3669026.jpg
Ingredients:
•	½ c salted butter, room temperature
•	1 tsp ground cayenne pepper
•	pinch of unsweetened Dutch process cocoa

Directions:
Whip the butter, cayenne pepper, and cocoa together until creamy and smooth with the ingredients well combined.
Fill a silicone mold (the type used for ice cubes or mini muffins) with the butter mixture.
Put the butter in the freezer.
Use in any dish as is or even use as a rub on chicken or turkey. This can also replace plain butter in savory dishes requiring butter.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Add a kick to your dish with this savory butter mix. Use these frozen cubes where you would use butter for sautéing or thaw them to use on your biscuits or toast.  The cocoa powder rounds out the flavor.
