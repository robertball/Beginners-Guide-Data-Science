Title:
Potsticker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/804374.jpg
Ingredients:
1. 2 lbs ground pork
2. 4-6. Cups of chopped Napa cabbage leaves.
3. 2 Md Egg
4. 2 full spoon of chopped ginger
5. 2 tbspn chopped fresh garlic or powder garlic.
6. 3 Tbsp oyster source.
7. 3 Tbsp cooking wine
8. 3 Tbsp Sesame Oil
8.  1-1/2 Tbsp soy souce
9.  Dash of pepper.
10. 3 packs potsticker wrappers from Chinese grocery or 2 packs of Gyoza Wrapper from Safeway or food Source

Directions:
Mix all ingredients, make about 3 plates.
