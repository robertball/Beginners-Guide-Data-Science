Title:
Shortcake DeTumbra De Muerto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/494612.jpg
Ingredients:
8 slices of Tumbra De Muerto and cut on a diagonal
1 C Cool Whip
1/2 pkg of banana pudding,save the rest for another use.
Garnishes: sliced almonds, mixed berries,sliced fruit> whatever you like, a good drizzle of maple syrup.

Directions:
Combine the Cool Whip with the banana pudding and set aside for 20 mins.
Lay the bread on a plate and top with Cool Whip banana mixture. Top with your choice of toppings.

Submitter: 
Patty Cakes
Description: 
This is Tomb of the Dead bread made into a  shortcake. You will find this bread during Halloween time in Mexican markets. The taste of this bread is like non other that I have tried and is Intoxicating! Enjoy:)
