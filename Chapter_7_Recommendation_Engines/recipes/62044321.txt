Title:
Khaula Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups of water
5 1/2 cups of sugar
7 Tlbspoons of instant coffee
2 Tlbspoons of vanilla
1 quart of vodka

Directions:
Bring water, sugar, coffee to a boil and simmer 1 hour. Allow to cool then add vanilla and vodka allow to sit at least 12 hours.  Age 3 weeks for best results!

Crystal D Cobb
