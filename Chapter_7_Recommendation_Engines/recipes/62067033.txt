Title:
Meatball Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28 oz. can of whole tomatos
2 cups shredded cabbage
1 small zuchinni, chopped
1 12oz. can corn, drained
2 cups celery, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1 cup broken spaghetti noodles
1/2 cup dry red wine
1 tablespoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1/4 teaspoon dried basil
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste
4 cups beef broth or a combination of broth and water equal to 4 cups
For the meatballs:
1 1/2 lbs. ground beef
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup milk
1/2 bread crumbs
1 1/2 teaspoons worsteshire sauce

Directions:
To make the meatballs:
Combine the breadcrumbs and the salt, add the milk and worsteshire, mix well.  Add the ground beef, mixing well.  Shape into 36-48 meatballs, depending on your patience!  Brown and add to soup.
To make the soup:
Add all the veggies, canned tomatoes, browned meatballs, and seasonings EXCEPT the broken spaghetti noodles.  Simmer for 45 min-1 hour until the veggies are tender.  Add the broken spaghetti noodles about 20 minutes before serving,  cooking until they are tender also. Serve with crusty bread and parmesan cheese to top the soup.
