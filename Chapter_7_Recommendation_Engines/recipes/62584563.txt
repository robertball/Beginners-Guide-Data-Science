Title:
Belaire Seafood Gumbo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of vegetable oil
1 cup of flour
2 cups of chopped onion
1 cup of chopped celery
1 cup of chopped bell pepper
1/4 cup of finely diced garlic
2 pounds of deveined shrimp
2 cans of crab
1 package (14 oz) of Smoked Sausage - sliced
Fillet of Cod, Halibut or Sea Bass - chopped bite size
7-3/4 cup water
1/4 cup fish sauce
1 cup chopped green onion
1/2 cup chopped cilantro
Rice (optional)
salt and pepper to taste
Cajun Spices (optional)

Directions:
In a large pot, heat oil over medium-high heat.  After it is heated, add the flour and stir constantly using a wire whisk to make a roux.
Once the roux is golden brown, add the onions, celery and bell peppers.  Saute the vegetables
for 3 - 5 minutes and then add the sausage and cook an additional 3 minutes.
Next add the crab meat (with liquid) and the water and fish sauce.  Bring to a low boil and simmer for 15 minutes.
Add fish and shrimp and allow to simmer 15 more minutes before adding the green onion and the cilantro.
Cook approximately 10 more minutes and serve over rice.
