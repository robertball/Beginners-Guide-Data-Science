Title:
Mom's Catalina Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup lettuce, coarsely chopped
1/4 cup tomato, chopped
1/4 cup dark red kidney beans, drained
1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup taco meat, cooled
1/2 cup Catalina dressing
1/2 cup tortilla chips, broken up

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, mixing dressing in just before serving.
