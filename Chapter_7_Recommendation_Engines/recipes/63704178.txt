Title:
Chicken Cordon Bleu Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked rice
12 oz cooked chicken, chopped
4 (1oz) slices of swiss cheese
8 oz diced ham
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup greek yogurt
1/2 cup milk
1/4 tsp pepper
1 tbsp mustard
1 cup cracker crumbs
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp paprika
1 tsp parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook rice.
Spread rice to bottom of 9 x 13 inch pan.
Top with chicken.
Top chicken with swiss cheese.
Top cheese with ham.
In medium bowl, combine soup, greek yogurt, milk, mustard, and pepper.
Spread over ham.
In small bowl, combine cracker crumbs, garlic powder, paprika, and parsley.
Sprinkle over soup mixture.
Bake for 45 minutes.
Let cool for 5-10 minutes.
