Title:
Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 cup sugar
2 beaten eggs
1 cup peanut butter
1 tsp. baking soda
2 1/2 cups flour.

Directions:
Cream butter with both sugars.
Add eggs, peanut butter, flour, and soda.
Mix well.
Roll into 1 inch balls and place onto greased baking sheet.
Press down with a sugar dipped fork.
Bake 10 - 12 minutes at 375 until just brown around the edges.

Submitter: 
Carol Potts
Description: 
These are my favorite peanut butter cookies.  They are crispy outside but soft inside.  Yum.
