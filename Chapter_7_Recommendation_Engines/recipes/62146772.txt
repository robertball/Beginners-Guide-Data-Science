Title:
Hunan Orange Beef And Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Pound Flank Steak Cut Into Strips 1 Inch x 1/4 Inch
2 Cups of Broccoli Florets
1/2 Teaspoon Baking Soda
1 Tablespoon Egg White - Lightly beaten
1/2 Teaspoon Rice Wine or Dry Sherry
1/8 Teaspoon Pepper
1 Tablespoon Peanut Oil
2 Tablespoons Cornstarch
****Sauce****
1/4 Cup Water
2 Teaspoons Soy Sauce
1 Tablespoon Sugar
1 Tablespoon Orange Liquor Sauce
1 Teaspoon Sesame Oil
1 teaspoon Rice Vinegar
2 Tablespoons Chicken Stock
Picnh Of White Pepper To Taste
****** Preparation*****
3 Cups Peanut Oil
4 Dried Small Chiles
2 Teaspoons Minced Fresh Ginger
2 Teaspoons Minced Garlic
1/3 Teaspoon Chili Garlic Paste
2 Tablespoons Fresh Orange Peel (Sliced In Small Pieces)
2 Scallions Trimmed And Cut Into 1 Inch Sections
6 Slices Of Fresh Orange

Directions:
Marinate The Beef Overnight In A Ziploc Bag. After Marinating, Drain The Sauce Into A Seperate Bowl And Keep. Wash The Meat Off Thoroughly With Cold Water. Drain And Dry With paper Towels. Place Beef In A Bowl And Add The Egg White, Mix Well Until The Beef is Coated, Add The Wine, White pepper, Tablespoon Of Peanut Oil, And Cornstarch Mixing With A Wisk After Each Ingredient is Added. Place in The Fridge For 1 hour. Steam Broccoli Until Crisp or Tender (Done To Your Liking) Heat A Wok Over High Heat With the 3 Cups Of Peanut Oil To 400 Degrees F (Or I Have Used My Fry Daddy). Place The Beef Strips 1 At A Time In the Oil And Cook For 3-4 Minutes Until The Beef Becomes Crisp. Remove To A Paper Towel. Drain Oil To A Glass Or Safe Container. Place 2 Tablespoons Of The Oil Back In the Wok And Heat On High Again For A Few Seconds. Add the Dry Chiles, Stir And Cook Until Darkened. Add the Ginger And Garlic And Stir Briefly. Add Orange Peel And Stir Briefly. Add In The Scallions And Mix Together Well. Add the Beef In At this Point And Cook Stirring For About 1 Minute. Make A Well In The Center Of The Mixture, Place the Reserved Marinade In The Center And Heat For 1-2 Minutes Or Until It Starts To Bubble. Mix All ingredients Together Well Until the Sauce Is Absorbed And The Beef Acquires A Shiny Coating. Remove from Heat And Stir In Broccoli.
