Title:
Mom's Famous Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-10 russet potatoes
1/2 sweet onion, chopped
3 stalks celery, chopped
2 large dill pickles, chopped
4 sweet pickles, chopped
6 boiled eggs, chilled, peeled and diced.
1 small jar pimiento, chopped
Dressing:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon sugar
1 ounce lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
2 tablespoons prepared mustard
a little milk, if needed

Directions:
Cut potatoes in half, lengthwise, leaving skins on, cover with water, and boil until easily pierced by a skewer or fork. Drain and chill several hours or overnight.
Peel and dice potatoes, and place in large bowl. Add remaining ingredients, except eggs.
Mix dressing thoroughly and pour over chopped vegetables. Mix thoroughly, then add eggs and stir to combine.
Decorate top with paprika (optional). Cover and keep cold.

Submitter: 
Yomama
Description: 
This is the way Mom always made her salad. I have never tasted another potato salad I liked half as much! Thank goodness there is a way to preserve and share this treasured recipe!
