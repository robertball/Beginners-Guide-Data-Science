Title:
Chipotle Pulled Pork Soft Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1004439.jpg
Ingredients:
6 warmed flour tortillas
1 lbs cooked pulled pork
1 12 ounce jar Herdez Traditional Chipotle Mexican Cooking Sauce
Coleslaw:
1 bag coleslaw mix
2 tablespoons canola oil
2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice
1/4 thinly sliced red onion
3/4 cup coarsely chopped cilantro
salt to taste
Sour cream
lime wedges for garnish

Directions:
Add jar of Herdez Traditional Chipotle Mexican Cooking Sauce to pulled pork in slow cooker or on stove top. Cook until heated through
Combine oil and lime juice. Add coleslaw mix, red onion, coarsely chopped cilantro and salt to taste. Toss to combine.
Assemble taco: half cup meat, quarter cup coleslaw, chopped tomatoes and a dab of sour cream. Serve with a lime wedge.

Submitter: 
Jaana Smith Bauman
Description: 
Herdez cooking sauces make these tacos super easy! Step away from ground beef and lettuce tacos tonight! Delicious!
