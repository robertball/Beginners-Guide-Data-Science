Title:
Avocado and Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large tomatoes, chopped
3 avocados - peeled, pitted and diced
1 yellow or red onion, diced
3 ears of grilled corn on the cob (see other recipe)
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
2 tbs balsamic vinegar
2 tbs extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup cheese (bleu or parmesan)

Directions:
In a large serving bowl, toss together the tomatoes, avocado, onion and corn. Dust lightly with black pepper, and pour oil and vinegar dressing over an sprinkle with cheese (pick a cheese to your taste). Serve immediately.
