Title:
Cake Batter Caramel Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag (3 oz) microwave popcorn
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup cake mix (any flavor)
1/2 cup white sugar
1/4 cup light corn syrup
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp vanilla
Sprinkles to decorate popcorn

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250.
Pop popcorn according to package directions. Spread out on a greased sheet pan and remove all unpopped seeds.
In a saucepan, combine butter, cake mix, sugar, corn syrup and salt. Bring to a boil. Boil 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Add vanilla and stirr to combine.
Pour butter mixture over popcorn and stir to coat. Bake for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.
Add sprinkles as popcorn comes out of oven final time. Stir to combine. Let cool 1 hour before serving.
