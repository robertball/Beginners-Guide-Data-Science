Title:
Mauigirl's Leftover Pizza Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1813344.jpg
Ingredients:
cooking spray
3 slices leftover pizza, cut into 3/4-inch pieces
4 eggs, beaten
1 1/2 cups milk
6 oz. cubed mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup chopped sun dried tomatoes
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 clove garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon basil
1/2 teaspoon salt
Pinch of red pepper flakes
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a three quart baking dish with cooking spray.
Spread pizza squares in the prepared baking dish.Sprinkle sun dried tomatoes, onion & mozarella over pizza cubes. Lightly beat eggs in large bowl and add  milk, garlic, basil oregano, salt, red pepper flakes together and pour egg/milk mixture over pizza pieces.  Cover dish with aluminum foil.Refrigerate overnight for baking in the morning or bake immediately.
Bake, covered, in a 350 preheated oven for 50 minutes. Remove foil and continue baking for about 15 minutes, or until eggs are set and the casserole is a light golden brown. Remove from oven and let rest 5 to 10 minutes before cutting for serving. Serve with a sprinkle of freshly grated Parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
mauigirl
Description: 
Use up that leftover pizza with this yummy breakfast casserole that can be assembled ahead. I throw leftover slices in a freezer bag to keep on hand for this quick and delicious casserole.Modified from: Leftover Pizza Breakfast Casserole
