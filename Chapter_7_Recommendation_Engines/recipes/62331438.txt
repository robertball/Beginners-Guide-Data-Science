Title:
Grandmas's Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large chicken breasts, cooked and chopped (Or any chicken cooked, taken off bones and equal to five or six cups.
2 cans Veg-All, drained
2 cans Cream of Chicken soup
l large onion, chopped finely
1 can sliced water chestnuts, drained
1 8-oz. carton sour cream
1 cup grated cheese
1 sleeve Ritz crackers
butter

Directions:
Mix all ingredients, except crackers and butter.  Place in large baking dish.  Cover with crushed crackers and dot with butter.  Bake at 400 degrees until mixture begins to bubble and topping browns, about 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
kellyclark82
Description: 
From Grandma Smith. 
NOTE: From this recipe, I make one large casserole and one 8-inch round casserole.  I think the recipe could be halved easily, but when making for myself, I freeze one casserole for later use.  Freezes well.
