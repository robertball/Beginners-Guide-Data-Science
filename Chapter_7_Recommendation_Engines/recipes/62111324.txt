Title:
Meringue Glazed Pecans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Egg Whites
1 C. sugar
1 Lb. Pecans
1/2 C. butter (1 stick)
salt

Directions:
Preheat oven 325.
Beat the egg whites with electric mixer on high until stiff but not dry.
Gradually add the sugar and continue beating after each addition.
Fold in the pecans.
Melt butter in a cookie sheet with sides or jellyroll pan in the preheated oven.
Spread the pecan mixture evenly in the pan and bake for 45 minutes. Turn every 15 minutes and sprinkle lightly with salt.
Cool and store in air tight container.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
The combination of crunchie sweet meringue and salty pecans becomes a nice treat for family and friends. Watch out, you can't stop eating this.
