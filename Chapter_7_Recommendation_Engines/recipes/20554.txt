Title:
Milky Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3191693.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
2 tablespoons honey
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a ceramic or glass mug, combine the milk, honey and vanilla. Microwave on HIGH for about 1 1/2 minutes. Stir.

Submitter: 
Danielle
Description: 
A sweet drink made of milk, honey and vanilla! Serve hot!
