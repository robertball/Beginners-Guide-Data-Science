Title:
Leftover Turkey & Biscuits Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. olive oil
1 yellow onion chopped
2 to 3 cups chopped cooked turkey
salt & pepper
1 tsp basil
1 tsp oregano
2 jars turkey gravy
1 cup cooked carrots
1 cup frozen corn
1 cup frozen chopped green beans
1 package of homestyle biscuits

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Heat olive oil in a large pan.  Add chopped onion and cook until they start to become transluscent.  Add turkey, salt, pepper, basil and oregano.  Let it cook for a couple minutes until the turkey is warmed through.  Add gravy, carrots, corn & green beans and cook until everthing is warmed through.  Pour your mix into a round casserole dish and top with biscuits.  Bake in the oven for about 30 minutes or until your biscuits are browned and the gravy is bubbling up.

Submitter: 
mam62505
Description: 
Here is a really quick, easy and yummy way to use up leftover turkey (or chicken) in a tasty casserole topped with packaged biscuits.  This recipe is very forgiving and will work with just about any frozen or cooked veggies and just about any leftover meat that you have on hand.
