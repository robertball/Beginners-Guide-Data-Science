Title:
"Kartoshka" chocolate balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4/5 cup sugar, granulated
4/5 cup milk
2 tsp cocoa powder
10 oz. (300 g) vanilla rusk, ground to crumbs
4/5 cup butter, softened
Optional:
2 Tbsp confectioner's sugar
2 Tbsp cocoa powder
2 Tbsp ground walnuts or almonds

Directions:
Heat the milk in a small saucepan. Blend the sugar and cocoa, add to the heated milk and continue heating and stirring until sugar is dissolved. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.
In a medium bowl, combine the vanilla rusk crumbs, butter, and cocoa mixture. Blend well.
Roll into balls, place on a large plate and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
Optionally, roll the balls in the mixture of ground nuts, cocoa powder, and confectioner's sugar before refrigerating.

Submitter: 
Petrovna
Description: 
This chocolate treat is popular in Russia, as much as the dried vanilla bread that it is made of. Kartoshka means potatoes, and there are hundreds of recipes for it. This one is my mother-in-law's.
