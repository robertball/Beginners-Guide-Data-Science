Title:
Simple Carnitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 1/2) pound boneless pork shoulder, trimmed and cut into 3 inch chunks
1 12oz. can of beer (or cola)
2 oranges, quartered
2 limes, quartered
1 large white onion, quartered
10 cloves garlic, peeled and crushed
1/4 cup kosher salt
1 tablespoon ground cumin
1 tablespoon chile powder
1 tablespoon whole black peppercorns
2 bay leaves

Directions:
Place the pork shoulder into a large Dutch oven. Add water to cover. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to medium-low, and simmer for 10 minutes. Skim off any foam that rises to the top.
Add oranges, limes onion, garlic, kosher salt, cumin, chile powder, black peppercorns, and bay leaves. Cover loosely, and simmer until the meat is tender, about 1 1/2 hours.
Allow the pork to cool, covered, in the broth for 1 hour. Shred the meat with two forks.

This the easiest recipe for tasty pork carnitas with a hint of orange and lime flavor and plenty of garlic.Modified from: Simple Carnitas
