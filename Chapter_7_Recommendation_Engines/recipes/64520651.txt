Title:
Chia Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2904171.jpg
Ingredients:
For crust
2 1/2 cups unbleached flour
3 tbs sugar
1/2 cup coconut oil
3/4 cup butter cut into squares
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 cup whisky
1/4 cup water
For filling
8 Tbs Chia Flour (can blend whole chia seeds)
1 can (15 oz) Pumpkin
1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ginger
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp cloves
2 tbs sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450
Pie Crust**: Mix  dry ingredients, coconut oil, and butter with knife or whisk until well distributed. It should look clumpy.
Mix in whisky and water until a clumpy dough is formed.
Divide in two and refrigerate until filling is ready.
Filling: If using whole chia seeds blend the chia seeds to a flour texture first.
Mix all ingredients except sugar in a blender.  Blend on High for 30 seconds.
Roll out the first pie crust to fill the pan.
Pour in the filling.
Roll out the second crust.  Cut shapes from the second crust  to make a design on the pie.
Sprinkle 2 Tbs of sugar on the top of the pie.
Bake at 450 for 20 minutes then cover with aluminum foil and reduce heat to 325.  Bake for an additional 40 minutes.
**You can half the recipe if you only want a bottom crust.

Submitter: 
Jeramyn Feucht

Moist smooth pumpkin pie free of eggs and corn.
