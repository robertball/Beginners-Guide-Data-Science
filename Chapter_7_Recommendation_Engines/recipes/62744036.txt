Title:
Easy Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package egg noodles, cooked and drained
6 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 onion, chopped
1/4 cup butter
2 pounds lean ground beef
4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 cups beef broth
1 cup sour cream
salt and black pepper to taste
2 tablespoons of dales
2 tablespoons of Granulated Garlic
2 Tablespoons of Liquid smoke

Directions:
Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Cook egg noodles in boiling water until done, about 8 minutes. Drain.
Meanwhile, prepare the sauce. In a large skillet, cook mushrooms and onions 2 tablespoons of Granulated Garlic in 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat until soft; remove from pan.
Using the same pan, melt remaining butter. Cook ground beef in melted butter until browned. Mix in flour. Stir in beef broth,2 tablespoons of dales, and cook until slightly thickened. Add mushroom and onion mixture; stir in sour cream, 2 Tablespoons of Liquid smoke. Season to taste with salt and pepper.  Continue cooking until sauce is hot, but not boiling. Serve sauce over egg noodles.

Submitter: 
all rec
Description: 
A lusciously tart sauce of sauteed mushrooms, onions and sour cream is beefed up and served over tender egg noodles. Hint: do not bring the sour cream sauce to a boil.Modified from: Easy Beef Stroganoff
