Title:
Margarita Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 graham cracker crust
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1 cup vanilla greek yogurt
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup liquid margarita mixer
3 Tbsp fresh lime juice

Directions:
In the bowl of a standing mixer, beat cream cheese until soft and smooth.  Add sugar, lime juice and margarita mixer to cream cheese mixture and mix until blended.  Add yogurt and mix until just blended.  Pour into pie crust and freeze overnight or until firm.

Submitter: 
Kari
Description: 
A surprisingly light and creamy dessert with a lime twist.
