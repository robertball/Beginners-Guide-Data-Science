Title:
Farmer's Market Veggie Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/467582.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bunch chard, 1/2 inch squares chopped
1 Tablespoon olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 cup sweet peppers, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
3 small sweet corn, corn off cob
1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
4 roma tomatoes, chopped
1/4 cup carrots, chopped
1/2 cup chicken or vegetable broth
pepper to taste
splash of lime juice
flour tortillas
Optional: top with chopped almonds or celery for a little crunch

Directions:
Place oil and onions in large pan at medium heat for 5 minutes. Add all ingredients and cook at medium-high for 10-15 minutes. Simmer 10-15 minutes adding broth as needed.
Wrap in flour tortillas. Eat with peach salsa and guacamole.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
We made these great burritos with all the items we purchased at the farmer's market in Torrey, Utah.
