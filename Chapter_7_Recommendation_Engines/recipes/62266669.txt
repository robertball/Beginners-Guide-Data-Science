Title:
Cherry Cola Brownies (Brownie Recipe) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box (1 lb 6.5 oz) Betty Crocker® Original Supreme brownie mix (with chocolate syrup pouch)
1/4 cup cherry cola carbonated beverage
1/3 cup vegetable oil
2 eggs

Directions:
Heat oven to 350°F.
Grease bottom only of 13x9-inch pan with cooking spray or shortening.
In medium bowl, stir brownie mix, pouch of chocolate syrup, 1/4 cup cherry cola, oil and eggs until well blended.
Spread in pan.
Bake 28 to 30 minutes or until toothpick inserted 2 inches from sides of pan come out clean.
Cool completely, about 1 hour.
