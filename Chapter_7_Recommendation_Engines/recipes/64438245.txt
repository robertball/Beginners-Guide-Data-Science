Title:
Fabulous tomato pie. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 frozen deep dish pie shell
3 fresh tomatoes
1 cup mozzarella cheese
1 cup chedder cheese
11/2 cups chopped green onion
1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
1 cup mayo
salt and pepper

Directions:
Pierce pie shell with fork and brown in oven.
blanch and peel the tomatoes then slice them and put into a colander and salt the tomatoes and drain off the liquid.
Put the tomatoes in the pie shell and top with the chopped onion and basil. Pat it down. Mix together the mayo and cheeses. Top the pie with the whole cheese mixture. Place in a 350 oven for around 40 mins or until nice and brown. Cool and slice.

Description: 
This is a wonderful recipe made with fresh tomatoes off the vine. Great for summer ripe tomatoes.
