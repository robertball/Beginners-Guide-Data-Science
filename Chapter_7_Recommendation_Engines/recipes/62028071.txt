Title:
Pesto Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. of dry Fettucine noodles (or spaghetti or Linguine noodles)
2/3 cup of prepared Basil Pesto Sauce
Salt and Pepper to taste
4 T. butter (or oleo)
1 cup grated Fresh Parmesan Cheese
2 T. of dried Parsley flakes

Directions:
Cook pasta in large pot of boiling, salted water until "al dente".  Drain and put in serving dish.
Add 4 T. butter and salt and pepper to taste.  Toss until butter melts.  Add 1 cup of grated Paremsan cheese, then 2 T. Parsley flakes.   Toss again to mix and blend.    Serve warm or at room temperature (not cold).

Submitter: 
Sherbg
Description: 
This is both super easy, quick and delicious!   I usually take this to potlucks, as I know I won't be duplicated.  It is best served at room temperature or hot (not cold).  Goes well with fish, poultry and meat dishes, or makes a great centerpiece entree with a salad and bread.
