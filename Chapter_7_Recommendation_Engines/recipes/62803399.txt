Title:
Slow Cooker Italian Beef for Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups water/beef broth
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon onion salt
1 teaspoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 bay leaf
2 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix (1 pkg may be used)
1 up to 5lb chuck roast / or any roast

Directions:
Combine water with salt, ground black pepper, oregano, basil, onion salt, parsley, garlic powder, bay leaf, and salad dressing mix in a saucepan. Stir well, and bring to a boil.
Place roast in slow cooker, and pour salad dressing mixture over the meat.
Cover, and cook on Low for 10 to 12 hours, or on High for 4 to 5 hours. When done, remove bay leaf, and shred meat with a fork.

Submitter: 
Linda Bates
Description: 
Roast is cooked with Italian salad dressing mix and seasonings until it is tender enough to shred with a fork.Modified from: Slow Cooker Italian Beef for Sandwiches
