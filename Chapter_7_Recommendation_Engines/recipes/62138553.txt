Title:
chopper's old fashioned meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb hamburger
1 large onion, chopped
1 1/4 cup ketchup
1 large egg
2 cup progresso italian style bread crumbs
2 tbsp worcestershire sauce

Directions:
preheat oven to 350.
reserve 1/4 cup ketchup, mix all ingredients together.
bake for 1 hour 15 minutes in loaf pan, draining fat every 15 minutes or so.
spread remaining ketchup over loaf.
bake 15 minutes more.
