Title:
Easy Bake Chile Rellenos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Poblano Chiles - look for long round straight for best fill
1 lb ground beef browned
1 onion
1 cup cooked rice  (or use 1 pkg microwave rice)
1 can whole kernal corn drained
1 can blackbeans drained
4 tblsp cumin
2 cups grated monterrey jack cheese
1 small jar salsa (mild, med, or hot)
1 large can green or red enchilada sauce
Salt and Pepper to taste.

Directions:
Rinse peppers and cut tops. Pull out core.
NO NEED to prep peppers further as you will bake to soften. You can add seeds into meat mixture if you want added heat.
Brown meat and drain.
Add in rice, corn, beans, cumin, salsa.
Last lightly mix in 1 cup of grated cheese.
TASTE filling before stuffing peppers for spice/season levels.
Easiest to stuff peppers by hand. Or Spoon into peppers.
Place peppers in baking dish.
Pour red or green enchilada sauce on top.
ROAST (top and bottom heat on) at 350 for 25-30 minutes.
Add remaining cheese to top.
Bake an additional 5-10 minutes.

Submitter: 
MitchJGray
Description: 
We shy away from fried foods, and LOVE full flavor dishes. This baked chile Renenos delivers. Have used everything from beef to left over roast pork, chicken, even turkey. Is a true winner every time.  Spice up to your liking and always TASTE your filling before stuffing peppers.
