Title:
Sweet & Sour Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion, thinly sliced
12 oz fresh green beans, cut in half
4 slices thick cut bacon
8 oz presliced baby portobellos
2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
1 tablespoon granulated sugar
3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Slice onion
Cut beans in half horizontally
Chop bacon into 1 inch pieces
Preheat large ,nonstick saute pan on medium-high 2-3 minutes.  Place bacon in pan, cook & stir 6-8 minutes or until browned.  Remove bacon from pan; reservre 3 tablespoons bacon fat in pan.
Add beans to bacon fat, cook and stir 5 minutes.  Stir in onions and mushrooms; cook and stir 5 minutes.
Stir in vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper; cook & sitr 1 minute or until sugar disolves.  Sire bacon into bean mixture.  Serve
