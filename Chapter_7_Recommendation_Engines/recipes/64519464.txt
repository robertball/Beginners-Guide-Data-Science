Title:
Big Ray's Mexican Nacho Chip Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2885646.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c. shredded cooked turkey
1 c. shredded cheese (or more if you are a cheese hound)
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup sour cream
1 can canned fire roasted tomatoes with jalepenos
1/2 packet taco seasoning (or more- to taste)
Bag of regular nacho cheese chips

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350-f degrees.
In a mixing bowl, combine all ingredients except nacho chips.
In a greased 9 x 13 baking dish, put a layer of crushed nacho chips (about 2 cups), then a layer of the turkey mixture. Repeat once more, ending with a layer of turkey mixture. Top with more shredded cheese, cover, and bake for 30-35 minutes, till bubbling hot.
