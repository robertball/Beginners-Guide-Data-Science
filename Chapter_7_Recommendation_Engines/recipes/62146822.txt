Title:
Easy Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 cups sugar
3 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 reg sized can plain pumpkin filling
2 tsp baking powder
1 heaping tsp cinnamon
1 pinch salt
chocolate chips (optional)

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
Combine all ingredients, except the chocolate chips. Mix well until smooth. If using the chips, fold in 3/4.
Grease and flour a large tube pan. Pour in the mixture and top with chocolate chips.
Bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
AddiesMom6
Description: 
This is my Aunt Chris' recipe. It's a one bowl recipe that is great for breakfast breads, or add chocolate for a dessert.
