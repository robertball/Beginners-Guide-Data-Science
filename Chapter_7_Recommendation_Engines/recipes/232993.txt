Title:
Garlic Crouton Slices Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1023974.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons olive oil
1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 French baguette, cut into 1-inch thick slices

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Cover a baking sheet with parchment paper.
Stir olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper together in a large mixing bowl; add bread slices and toss to coat. Arrange bread onto prepared baking sheet.
Bake until crispy and no longer soft, about 8 minutes.
