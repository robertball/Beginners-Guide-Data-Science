Title:
My New Favorite Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 pound chuck ground beef
1 pound breakfast sausage
1/2 onion, diced
1/2 bell pepper, diced
3 cloves garlic
28 oz tomato sauce
28 oz crushed tomato
6 oz tomato paste
2 tea dried oregano
1 tea basil
1/2 tea fennel seeds
1/2 tea Italian seasoning
1-2 TBL sugar
1/2 tea Salt, or to taste
1/4 tea black pepper
1 Package Regular Lasagna  Noodles
15 oz Ricotta cheese
4 oz cottage cheese
1 egg
1 pound sliced mozzarella cheese
6 oz freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
1.   Boil water with 1 TBL salt and add noodles.  Cook until al dente.
2.  Brown ground beef and sausage with onion and peppers in large cooking pot.  Add garlic at the end.  You can drain the grease or leave it in.
3.   Add tomato sauce, crushed tomato, tomato paste, oregano, basil, fennel, Italian seasoning,  chopped parsley, sugar, salt and black pepper.   Bring sauce to a simmer and cook for 30-45 minutes, stirring often.
4.  Heat oven to 375 degrees.
5.   In medium bowl, mix ricotta cheese, cottage cheese, beaten eggs, grated Parmesan, and a little salt.
6.  In 9x13 casserole dish, add a ladle of sauce, then four lasagna noodles long ways in the dish (they may overlap), then ricotta mixture, then a layer of sliced mozzarella cheese.
7.  Repeat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture and layer of sliced mozzarella cheese
8.  Add one more layer of noodles and then remaining sauce.  Top with remaining grated Parmesan cheese
9.  Bake for 45 minutes.   Cover for the first 30 minutes with foil and uncover for the last 15 minutes.
10.  Allow to cool 10 minutes before eating.
