Title:
Slow Cooker Baked Potaoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 - 6 russet baking potatoes
salt (optional)
oil (optional)

Directions:
Scrub potatoes and pierce with fork.
If desired, rub with oil and salt or just salt skins.
Wrap in foil and place in slow cooker for 8 hours on low.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
It may take a while, but they bake perfect every time.
