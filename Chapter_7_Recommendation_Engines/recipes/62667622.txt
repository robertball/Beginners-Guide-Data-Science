Title:
Southwest Vegan Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup uncooked quinoa
1 1/2 cup vegetable broth
1 tsp. ground cumin
1/4 tsp. red pepper
1 T. vegetable oil
1 medium onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, pressed or diced
1 cup frozen corn
1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 can petite cut tomatoes, drained
1/2 cup cilantro
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine quinoa, broth, cumin and red pepper in small pot.  Bring to boil and reduce heat, simmer 15 minutes or until liquid is absorbed.
While quinoa is cooking, saut√© oil and onion in skillet over medium heat 3-5 minutes or until onion is translucent, add garlic during last minute of cooking.
Add cooked quinoa and corn to onions and continue cooking until corn is hot.
Mix in black beans, tomatoes, cilantro, salt and pepper.  Continue cooking until hot and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Ashley O.
Description: 
This healthy, southwest-inspired dish is a favorite at our house.  It's great on tortillas with your favorite taco toppings.
