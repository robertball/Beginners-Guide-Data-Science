Title:
Yummy Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans unmarinated artichoke hearts, drained and chopped well
1 cup mayonnaise
1 1/2 cups grated parmesan cheese
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chile peppers

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium bowl, mix the artichoke hearts, mayonnaise, 1 cup parmesan, cream cheese and green chile peppers. Scoop the mixture into a pie pan or medium baking pan. Top with the remaining 1/2 cup of parmesan.  Sprinkle with garlic powder.  Bake for 40 minutes or until bubbly and slightly browned, then broil for 5. Serve warm with Frito scoops, sourdough or ciabatta/french bread.
