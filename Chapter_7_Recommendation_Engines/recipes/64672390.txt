Title:
Whole Wheat Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/4 cups flour
1-1/4 cups whole wheat flour
1 cup cold butter cut in small pieces
1/4 tsp salt
7 tbl cold water
1 tbl apple cider vinegar

Directions:
Mix flour, salt, and butter mix till coarse
add water and vinegar and mix
divide in 2 5 in pieces refrigerant 1 hour
roll out and line pan
