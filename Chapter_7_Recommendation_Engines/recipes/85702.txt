Title:
Summertime Swirl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986139.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 fluid ounce) can BACARDI速 Mixers Strawberry Daiquiri frozen mix
1 (10 fluid ounce) can BACARDI速 Mixers Pina Colada frozen mix
1 cup BACARDI速 Rum
10 cups ice
Strawberries, for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Pina Colada mix, 1/2 cup of BACARDI(R) Rum and five cups of ice for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform. If you only have one blender, transfer into a pitcher.
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Strawberry Daiquiri mix, 1/2 cup of BACARDI(R) Rum and five cups of ice for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Pour Pina Colada and Strawberry Daiquiri into glass simultaneously, so that Pina Colada fills either the left or right side of the glass and Strawberry Daiquiri fills the other.
Swirl with swizzle stick and garnish with strawberry.

Submitter: 
BACARDI速 Mixers
Description: 
Celebrate summer with friends and these stunning swirled strawberry and Pina Colada slushes. Two tones are swirled together with a swizzle stick and garnished with a fresh berry.
