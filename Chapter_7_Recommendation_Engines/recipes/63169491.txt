Title:
Sauted Swiss Chard with Mushrooms and Roasted Red peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch fresh Swiss Chard, rinsed and Chopped,
3 cloves garlic minced
1/2 onion chopped fine
3tbs of oilve oil
1 cup sliced frsh mushrooms
1/2 a roasted red pepper chopped
1/2 cup white wine or white cooking wine,
salt and pepper to taste
1tsp dried oregano
1tsp dried basil

Directions:
1. in large saute pan add olive oil, heat on medium heat til hot,
2. add garlic, onion and mushrooms and saute til translucent , and mushrooms are softened, do not burn.
3. add swiss chard, roasted red pepper, and seasonings.cook for 3 min til swiss chard is wilted
4. add white wine and saute for another 3-4 minutes or intil swiss chard is cooked through.

Submitter:

Description: 
Made this up when I had swiss chard and did mot know what to do with it. Added whatever I had in the fridge. Aded some white wine and voila. Yummy.
You can also use other veggies or seasoning you have on hand.
