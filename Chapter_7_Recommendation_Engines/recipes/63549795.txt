Title:
Chile Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/969724.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3 to 4 lb. cooked rotisserie chicken
1 28 oz. can tomato puree
2 16 oz. jars salsa verde
1 cups vegetable broth
2 Tbs chile powder
1 Tbs ground cumin
1 lb bag frozen sweet white corn
1 lb bag bell pepper strips
1 white onion, chopped
2 15 oz. cans cannellini beans
salt and pepper to taste
1 tsp. Cayenne Pepper (optional)

Directions:
Remove meat from the chicken and place in Crock Pot. Add Tomato Puree, Salsa Verde, Vegetable Broth, Chili Powder and Cumin. Stir well.
Add frozen peppers, corn, and chopped onions. Drain and add Cannellini Beans.
Cook on High Power for 2 hours. Stir occasionally.  Reduce heat to Low and let simmer until ready to eat.

Submitter: 
JPsMommie
Description: 
When I need a healthy, easy dinner for the family, I get out my crock pot and make this chili. Chicken, cannellini beans and salsa verde make this a delicious and somewhat spicy dish. Serve with shredded cheese and tortilla chips.
