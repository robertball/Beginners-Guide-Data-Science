Title:
Joey's First Time Ever Lobster Mac&Cheese; Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/961509.jpg
Ingredients:
4 TBS butter
2 TBS grated onion
4 TBS flour
1 cup milk
1 cup vegetable broth
1 packet seafood flavor boost
1 pinch dried thyme
1 pinch cayenne pepper
salt & pepper to taste
16 oz Penne Pasta
Approx 1lb of lobster after peeling
6 to 8 oz Sargento Extra Sharp Cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a sauce pan melt butter add grated onion to the butter and cook until translucent. Add flour TBS at a time stirring constantly cook till smooth and bubbly. Add milk slowly,quickly whisking as you pour, add the vegetable broth,flavor boost,and spices whisk all together and let cook over medium heat until thickened remove from the heat and set a side. Prepare pasta as package directs. In a large baking dish add Cooked pasta and mix in the cream sauce and lobster. Add half of the cheese to the baking dish and mix everything well together. Top the baking dish with the remaining cheese and place in 350 degree oven for 30 minutes or until cheese is melted to a golden brown.

Submitter: 
Joey Joan
Description: 
I wanted to make a seafood dinner for the family, being a non seafood person I needed a recipe that was both familiar and easy. This is what I came up with for the first time out of my food comfort zone. The family was tripping over themselves getting 2nds and 3rds of this.
