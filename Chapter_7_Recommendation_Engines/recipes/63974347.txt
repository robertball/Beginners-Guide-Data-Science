Title:
Pork Chop and Rice Casserole - Karen's Version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
6 pork chops
1/2 c. chopped onion
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of celery soup
1 cup milk
1 cup long grain rice
1/2 stick butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 275 degrees F (135 degrees C).
Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat
Sprinkle the chops liberally with salt, pepper, and garlic powder and brown the pork chops on both sides, 5 to 8 minutes per side.  Add chopped onion when almost done.
Remove chops to 9x13 casserole.
Add cream of chicken soup, cream of celery soup,and milk to the onions in the frying pan and stir to combine.
Stir in the rice, and add the butter; bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat, and stir until the butter has melted. Pour the rice mixture over the chops; cover the dish with foil.
Bake in the preheated oven until tender, about 2 hours.
