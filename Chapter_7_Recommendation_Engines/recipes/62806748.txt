Title:
Broccoli & Chicken Over Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/659063.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans of cream of mushroom soup (low fat)
1 can of cream of chicken soup (low fat)
1 bag of chopped frozen onions
1 bag of chopped frozen broccoli
1.5 cup of shredded cheese
12 ounces chopped chicken breasts
1 large box of brown rice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in large glass dish, except for rice. Bake @ 400 degrees for 45 minutes.
Cook rice and serve chicken over it.
