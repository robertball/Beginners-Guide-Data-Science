Title:
Incredible Artichoke Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz. Package cream cheese
1 can 14oz. Artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
½ cup spinach, frozen chopped, or steamed
¼ cup mayonnaise
½ cup parmesan cheese, grated
1 clove garlic, finely minced
½ teaspoon dry basil (1 Tbsp. fresh)
¼ cup mozzarella cheese, grated
¼ teaspoon garlic salt
Salt and pepper to taste
1 chopped tomato for garnish

Directions:
Allow cream cheese to come to room temperature.
Cream together mayonnaise, parmesan, garlic, basil, and garlic salt.
Add artichoke hearts and spinach and mix until blended.
Grease pie pan, pour in dip and top with mozzarella.
Bake at 350° F for 25 minutes or until top is browned.
Top with tomato and serve with bread, crackers or veggies.

Submitter: 
Saraphim
Description: 
I have adored this recipe ever since I was little.  I once went to Applebees and, seeing Artichoke Spinach Dip on the menu, ordered what I thought was this.  It was not even close, and ever since then I have just made my own!
