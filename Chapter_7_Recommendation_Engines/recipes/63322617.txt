Title:
Fish Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. fish
1 garlic clove, minced
2 limes, juiced and divided
1/4 tsp. pepper
4 tbsp. sour cream
2-3 tbsp. mayonnaise
1 dash of hot pepper sauce
corn tortillas
2 tbsp. butter or olive oil
1 cup shredded lettuce
1 cup chopped tomato

Directions:
In a frying pan, saut√© garlic and butter (or olive oil) for about half a minute.  Add juice from 1 lime.
Cut fish into small 1-in. cubes.  Add fish and pepper.  Cook for 6-8 minutes.
Meanwhile, combine sour cream mayonnaise, hot pepper sauce, and remaining lemon juice.
Place a spoonful of fish on each tortilla.  Top with lettuce, tomato, and sour cream sauce.
