Title:
Sweet Potato Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg white, beaten
1-1/2 cups mashed sweet potatoes
1-1/4 cups skim milk
3 eggs
2/3 c. sugar
1/2 c. maple syrup
1 tsp. ground cinnamon
1/2 t. salt
1/2 t. ground ginger
1/2 t. ground nutmeg
1/4 t. ground mace

Directions:
1. Brush crust with egg white.
2. Bake at 400 degrees for 6 minutes, let cool. Reduce heat to 350 degrees.
3. Boil (or microwave) sweet potatoes in their jackets until cooked (a thin knife should be able to go through easily).
4. In a large bowl. beat eggs and then add remaining ingredients until blended. Pour into crust.
5. Bake for 70 minutes or until knife inserted comes out clean.
