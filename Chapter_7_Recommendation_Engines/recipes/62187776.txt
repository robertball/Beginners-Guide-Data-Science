Title:
Melt in your mouth Lemon Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 C. Butter, softened
6 T. granulated sugar
2 1/2 C. all purpose flour
1 C. cornstarch
2 t. grated lemon peel
6 T. fresh lemon juice
FROSTING:
2 C. powdered sugar
1 C. butter, softened
2 t. grated lemon peel
2 T. fresh lemon juice

Directions:
Cookies: Combine butter and sugar. Beat until fluffy. Add cornstarch and mix. Add flour, lemon peel and lemon juice. Beat at a low speed until a soft dough forms. Shape dough into a 2" diameter log. Wrap in wax or parchment paper and chill for 1-2 hours or until firm.
Cut log into 1/4" slices and bake on an ungreased cookie sheet for 10 minutes at 350 until firm. Cool completely before frosting.
FROSTING:
Beat all ingredients until smooth. Spread onto cookies. Let dry completely before storing. If you wish, you can add a few drops of yellow food coloring to the icing.

Description: 
These cookies were made by a lady in a cookie exchange years ago and they've been a holiday favorite ever since. They're the first to go on the entire cookie tray! They are amazing. I'd eat them all year round if they didn't requre a little extra prep time with the fresh lemon!
