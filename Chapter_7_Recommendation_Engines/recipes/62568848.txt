Title:
Bob Wafsted's Prime Rib Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 lb prime rib
1 C coarse salt
1 C coarse pepper
1 C minced dried garlic

Directions:
Mix all 3 and shake over and press into meat.  Bake uncovered at 450 for 45 minutes then at 325 until done (13-15 minutes/pound).
