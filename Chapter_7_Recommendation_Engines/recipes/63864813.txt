Title:
Cheese String Witch Fingers and Fruit Roll-up Broomsticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1072617.jpg
Ingredients:
Cheese strings
Sliced almonds
Cream cheese
Pretzel sticks
Red fruit roll-ups

Directions:
Cut cheese strings in half, put a dab of cream cheese on the end and place an almond slice nail on top (can lightly cut "knuckles" into the cheese).
Cut broom lines into the bottom of the fruit roll-up, rip off a piece and wrap around the bottom of the pretzel.

Submitter: 
The Wallace Clan
Description: 
A great Halloween snack for school parties.
