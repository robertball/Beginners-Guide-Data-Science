Title:
Kaylee's Easy Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (250 gram) box original Ritz crackers, crushed
1 bag Skor/Heath pieces
1 can sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
Mix all ingredients together, press into 9 x 13 pan.
Bake for 17 minutes.
