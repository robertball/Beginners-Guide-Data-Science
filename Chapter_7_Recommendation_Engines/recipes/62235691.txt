Title:
Lemon Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups sifted flour
6 eggs
1 tsp baking power
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup shortening
1 tsp lemon extract
1 cup milk
3 cups sugar

Directions:
Sift together flour, baking powder and salt.  Set aside.  Cream together butter, shortening, and sugar until light and fluffy.  Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each egg.  Add extract.  Add dry ingredients alternately with milk, beating well after each.  Pour batter into greased and floured 10" tubepan(bundt).  Bake at 325 degrees for 1 hour and 30 mins.  Cool pan on rack.
