Title:
skillet egg bruschette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1c potato skin on
1/4 t red pepper flakes
1c diced zucchini
1/4 c pecorino cheese
1/2 T oregano
1/2c diced ham or bacon
1 T butter
1 T garlic
12 mushrooms, cut into quarters
1/4c white wine
2 T parsley
1/4c fontina cheese
8 eggs
8 slices country bread

Directions:
Preheat to 350*
Cook potato, add zucchini, season add pecorino and oregano.
Saute the ham in butter until crisp, remove cook mushrooms and garlic. Add the wine, return ham, parsley and fontina.
Cook eggs until soft.
Grill bread on both sides and serve open faced.
