Title:
Sarah's Frozen Pumpkin Spice Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1051220.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup each of:
Heavy Whipping Cream
Milk
Kahlua Pumpkin Spice
100% Pumpkin Puree
Plus:
8 ice cubes
1/2 teaspoon of pumpkin spice

Directions:
Add all of the ingredients to the blender, liquids first. Blend on high approximately 30-45 seconds.
Pour into drink glasses and serve immedietely.

Submitter: 
Holiday Baker
Description: 
I like blended and frozen drinks, so after trying the limited edition Kahlua pumpkin spice holiday flavor I decided to see what I could come up with. It is delicious!
