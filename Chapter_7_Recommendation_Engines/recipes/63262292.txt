Title:
Banana - Spinach Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Frozen Banana
2 Cups Baby Spinach
1 Tbs Honey
1/4 Cup Sliced Almonds
1 Cup Milk
1/2 C vanilla or plan Yogurt

Directions:
Put yogurt, milk, almonds, and honey in blender.  Add spinach to the top.  Pulse until it starts to mix and then leave on blend for a minute.  Pure into cup and enjoy!

Melissa Dunstan Levin
Description: 
Healthy way to get your greens.
