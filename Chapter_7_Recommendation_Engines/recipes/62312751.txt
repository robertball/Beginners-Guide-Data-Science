Title:
Mexican Chicken Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-1/2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
1 (11 oz.) can Mexicorn
1 (10.75 oz.) can cream of potato soup
1 (4 oz.) can diced green chile peppers, undrained
1 (1.25 oz.) packet dry taco seasoning mix
3 cups chicken broth
8 oz. sour cream
4 oz. Mexican Velveeta

Directions:
Combine everything EXCEPT the sour cream and cheese in the crockpot.
Cook on low 8-10 hours or on high 4-5 hours.
Add the sour cream and Mexican Velveeta 30 minutes or so before serving, just enough to melt.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
This is an easy soup you fix in the crockpot, and it's oh, so tasty!
