Title:
Spinach and Orange Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978515.jpg
Ingredients:
8 cups torn spinach
1 1/2 cups fresh orange sections
1/2 cup sliced red onion
1 (4 ounce) package ATHENOS Traditional Crumbled Feta Cheese
1/3 cup PLANTERS Slivered Almonds, toasted
1 cup prepared GOOD SEASONS Italian Salad Dressing

Directions:
Toss spinach, oranges, onion, cheese and almonds in large bowl.
Pour salad dressing over spinach mixture; toss to coat.
