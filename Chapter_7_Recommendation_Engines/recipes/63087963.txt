Title:
Savory Biscuit Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter, melted
2 Tbls grated Parmesan cheese
1 Tbls dried, minced onion
1 1/2 tsp dried parsley flakes
1 psckage(12oz) refrigerated biscuits

Directions:
In a bowl, combine butter, cheese, onion and parsley.  Cut biscuits into quarters; roll in butter mixture.  Place in a greased 15 x 10 x 1 inch baking pan; let stand for 25 minutes.  Bake at 400' for 8 minutes or until lightly browned.

Description: 
These light, golden puffs are super simple to make and their flavor is oh-so-good!
