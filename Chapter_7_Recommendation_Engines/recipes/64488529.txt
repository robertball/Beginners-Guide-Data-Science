Title:
Raw Chocolate Date Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2567458.jpg
Ingredients:
Base ingredients:
1/2 cup raw almond meal
1/2 cup raw walnuts
3 Tbl coconut milk
1/4 cup cocoa powder
Crunch ingredients:
1 cup raw almond meal
1/2 cup coconut flour
1/2 cup desiccated coconut
2 Tbl honey
1 tsp vanilla
Caramel layer:
3 cups dates, pitted
2 Tbl coconut milk
1/3 cup coconut oil
Chocolate topping:
1/3 cup coconut oil
1/4 cup cocoa powder
1/4 cup honey

Directions:
Base directions:
Cut a piece of parchment paper to fit your pan.
Put your nut ingredients in to a food processor and process until fine. Add remaining base ingredients and blend. Spread and/or press the base ingredients evenly on the parchment. Put it in the pan and place in the freezer.
Crunch directions:
Use the same method as above with the crunch layer. Press firmly on top of your base layer. Place in the freezer.
Caramel directions:
Place all ingredients in to a food processor. Process until smooth, scraping down sides every 30 seconds or so. Using a spatula, spread evenly over the crunch layer. Place in the freezer.
Chocolate topping directions:
Add all chocolate topping ingredients to a bowl and mix. Microwave for 10-30 seconds to melt if required. Spread evenly over caramel. Place in the freezer until hardened. Cut in to squares. Place in freezer until ready to eat.
