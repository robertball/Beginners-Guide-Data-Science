Title:
Amazing BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/589679.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups honey
1 cup ketchup
1 6 oz. can tomato paste
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tbsp. lemon juice
1 tbsp. cocoa powder
1 tbsp. curry powder
1 tbsp. paprika
1 tbsp. soy sauce
1 tbsp. Worcestershire Sauce
2 tsp. Tabasco sauce
1 tsp. cayenne pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a medium saucepan.
Bring to a boil over high heat; reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes.
Any leftover sauce will keep for about a month in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Lance Hinkle
Description: 
This sauce goes great on everything, especially grilling.  Use it with your favorite chicken, ribs, or steak recipe.
