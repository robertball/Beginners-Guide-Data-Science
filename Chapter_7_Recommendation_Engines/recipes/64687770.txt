Title:
Simple Paleo Whole30 Compliant Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5065116.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Coconut  Flour
1/2 Cup Blanched Almond Flour
1/4 Teaspoon Sea Salt (Grinder)
2 Eggs
1/4 Cup Mixture of Melted Coconut Oil and Ghee

Directions:
1. Preheat Oven to 350Â°F
2. Whisk together dry ingredients (flours and salt)
3.  Melt the oil and ghee mixture and pour in whisked ingredients
4. Add eggs and mix all ingredients together
5. Form ball of dough and move into a 9 Inch Pie Pan
6. Evenly press dough down and around the sides
7. Fork the bottom and sides of pie crust
8. Bake for 25 minutes until golden brown
Now your healthy pie crust is ready!!! Let cool before adding filling.

Submitter: 
Carson Crochet
Description: 
Crispy baked pie crust for use with any filling. Simple ingredients for a simple pie. Gluten free, dairy free, peanut free (does have almonds).
