Title:
Quick and Easy Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayo
4 tsp white vinegar
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 tsp dry mustard
1/4 tsp salt
28 oz bags of shredded cole slaw mix

Directions:
1. Whisk vinegar, sugar, mustard and salt together until sugar is dissolved
1. Add Mayo and whisk
3. Add cabbage mix
4. Refrigerate at least 2 hours, then serve

Submitter: 
Courtney Parry
Description: 
Sweet and Tangy coleslaw served at famous chicken restaurants.
