Title:
Cinnapear Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1015517.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pears, quartered and cores removed
1 banana, cut in chunks
1 cup milk
1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 pinch ground nutmeg

Directions:
Place the pears, banana, milk, yogurt, cinnamon, and nutmeg into a blender. Cover, and puree until smooth. Pour into glasses to serve.

Submitter: 
Sarah-May
Description: 
I've always loved the combination of cinnamon and pears; toss in a banana, some milk and a few other things and you've got a delightful breakfast drink. If you have the foresight to freeze the banana chunks ahead of time, this is even more refreshing.
