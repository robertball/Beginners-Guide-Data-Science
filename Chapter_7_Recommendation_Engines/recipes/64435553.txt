Title:
CHICKEN & RICE SOUP W/ A KICK OF SIRACHA Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2346253.jpg
Ingredients:
4-6 CHICKEN BREAST (SKIN OR SKINLESS)
3-4 CUPS OF WATER
3 CUBES OF KNORR CHICKEN BOUILLON (CAN BE SUBSTITUTED BY YOUR CHOICE OF CHICKEN BROTH)
1/2 CUP LEMON JUICE (I PREFER MY SOUP TO BE SOUR, YOU CAN USE MORE OR LESS DEPENDING ON YOUR PREFERANCE)
1/3 CUP OF SIRACHA
1/2 CUP OF WHITE RICE
3 RUSSET POTATOES CUBED
4-6 CARROTS CHOPPED
3-4 CELERY STALKS CHOPPED
1/4 RED ONION CHOPPED
1/4 CUP THYME
A LITTLE LESS THAN 1/4 CUP OF SAGE
5-8 LEAVES OF BASIL
1 1/2 TABLESPOON OF FISH SAUCE (CAN BE FOUND IN ASIAN MARKET OR ASIAN AISLE ... ALSO CAN BE SUBSTITUTED BY SEA SALT)
1/2 TABLESPOON OF BLACK GROUND PEPPER
1 VERY SMALL GINGER PEELED AND SLICED

Directions:
1.BRING WATER TO BOIL
2.SUBMERGE CHICKEN INTO WATER FOR 20-25 MINUTES
3.TAKE OUT CHICKEN ONTO A PLATE AND SHRED
4.PUT BACK SHREDDED CHICKEN INTO WATER
5.ADD RICE, CHICKEN BOUILLON, POTATO, CARROTS, THYME, SAGE, BASIL, GINGER, ONION, FISH SAUCE(OR SEA SALT), PEPPER. STIR, COVER, AND KEEP ON MEDIUM HEAT FOR 30 MINUTES
6.ADD CELERY, COVER AND KEEP ON LOW HEAT FOR 20 MINUTES OR UNTIL RICE IS DONE
GIVE ABOUT 10 MINUTES TO COOL, SERVE AND ENJOY!

Submitter: 
Jovee Jojo Buencamino
Description: 
On cold rainy days I love to make this chicken soup. The combination of lemon and siracha is amazing! It is also VERY GOOD for when you have the flu, it helps unclog your nasal passages.
