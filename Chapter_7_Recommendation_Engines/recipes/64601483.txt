Title:
Big Ray's Healthy Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3944835.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups of old fashion oats
1/4 cup milk
5 average sized RIPE bananas
1 cup blueberries

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
Lightly grease a muffin tin
In a food processor, combine all 4 ingredients and mix on medium speed until thick, and creamy-do not over-mix.
Fill muffins tins, and bake for 18-20 minutes *use a toothpick to test center. If it comes out clean, they are done-if not add another minute or two. .

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
These are healthy muffins and meant to provide nutrition. They are nothing like the average flour muffins, and are very heavy and filling.  Makes 12 muffins-PERFECT for freezing!
