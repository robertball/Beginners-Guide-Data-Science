Title:
CHICKEN FOR THE QUEEN Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 chicken breasts
Seasoned bread crumbs
2 eggs
1 can of mushrooms
1 lb grated mozza cheese
1 can golden mushroom soup
1 can beef consume

Directions:
Cut chicken breasts in quarters.  Soak for two hours in the beaten eggs.  Roll in crumbs and brown in skillet.  Make a sauce by combining the two soups.  In a casserole dish put in layers; sicken, mushrooms, cheese, sauce.  Continue until all is used up.  Save some cheese for the top.  Bake at 350 for 30 minutes-covered.  Uncover and put remaining cheese on bake 30 mins longer
