Title:
Zucchini Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust
1 3/4 c all purpose flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 c whole milk
3 tbsp olive oil
3 tbsp water
Filling
1/4 c uncooked arborio rice
2 tbsp olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped (1 1/2 c)
3 medium zucchini, cut into 1/2" pieces (3 cups)
1 c grated Parmesan
3 large eggs
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1/8 tsp ground cinnamon

Directions:
Crust: Whisk flour and salt. Add milk, oil and 3 tbsp water and stir with fork to combine. Knead for 3 min. Divide dough into 2 pieces, then pat flat. Wrap each dough in plastic and set aside.
For Filling: Cook rice in heavy saucepan, until fully cooked.
Preheat oven 350F. Heat oil in med. skillet over med. heat. Add onion and saute 5 min.  Add zucchini and saute 5 min. Transfer veggies to a bowl. Stir in 1/2 c rice, parmesan, beaten eggs, salt, pepper and cinnamon, mixing well. Set filling aside.
Oil 9" glass pie dish. Roll out 1 dough piece on lightly floured surface, to 13-14". Transfer to pie dish. Transfer prepared veggies to pie dish. Roll out second dough piece as a top. Fold overhang and crimp. Pierce top crust with fork in several places.
Bake torte 10 min. Increase oven temperature to 375F and bake until pale golden, about 50 min longer. Cool.
