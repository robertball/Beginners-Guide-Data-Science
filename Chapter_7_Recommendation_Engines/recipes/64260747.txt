Title:
Pizza Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Tbsp yeast
1/8 cup warm water
1/8 tsp sugar
1/2 cup warm water
1 Tbsp olive oil
2 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Mix yeast, 1/8 cup warm water and sugar in bowl.  Let proof.
Mix in 1/2 cup warm water and oil.  Mix in flour and salt.  Knead dough until smooth, about 5 minutes.  Place dough in a greased bowl, cover and let rise about 45 minutes.
Roll dough out and let rest 10 minutes.
Then roll dough thinner and place in pizza pan.  Prick with a fork.  Bake for 8-10 minutes at 400 degrees or until dough is set.
Cover with pizza sauce, cooked ground pork, cheese, etc.  Bake for 10-15 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and starts to brown.
