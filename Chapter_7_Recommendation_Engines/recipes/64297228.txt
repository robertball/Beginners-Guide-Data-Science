Title:
Vegan  Potato and Kale Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups veggie broth
2 cups soy milk
3 cups cubed steamed potatoes
1 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
3 T miso
1/c diced mushrooms opt
1/4 c parsley, minced opt
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes, or more to taste
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
8 cups kale sliced thin, steamed 1 bunch

Directions:
Heat a large soup pot over medium-high heat. Saute onion til translucent and mushrooms if using, add garlic and cook 1 minute.
Stir in potatoes, kale, parsley if using, broth, milk, oregano, and red pepper flakes, bring to a boil, and reduce heat to low. Put half the soup in food processor until pureed and return to pan. Season with black pepper; stir miso into soup (miso should not be boiled).

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
Kale, and potatoes give a creamy vegan soup a hearty, rich flavor.Modified from: Sausage, Potato and Kale Soup
