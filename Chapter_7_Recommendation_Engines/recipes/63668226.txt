Title:
Egg Quinoa Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1018900.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup milk, room temperature
1 egg, room temperature
3/4 cup cooked quinoa, cooled
3 cups bread flour
1 teaspoons salt
2 tablespoons butter
3 tablespoons sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons active yeast
1 egg white, beaten until foamy

Directions:
Place all ingredients in bread pan of a breadmaker.  Select dough setting.
When dough is done, empty onto a lightly floured surface and shape into desired shape or shapes and place on parchment lined pan.  For loaves, slice 1/4" deep several times along the loaf.  Brush with foamy egg white.
Let rise for 25 min, then bake in a 375 oven for 18-22 min until golden.

Description: 
Quinoa kicks up the protein in the delicious homemade bread.  Whole wheat flour can also be used.
