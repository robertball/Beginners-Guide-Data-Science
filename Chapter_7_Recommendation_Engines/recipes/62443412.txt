Title:
Aunt Bonnie's Orange Szechuan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts
2 T soy sauce
1 T dry sherry or white wine
4 green onions, cut into 1" lengths
1 tsp minced ginger root
1/4 - 1/2 t. red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
1 (8 ounce can) water chestnut slices, drained
1 (6 ounce) sack fresh snow peas
1 (20 ounce) can pineapple chunks in juice
1/2 c orange juice
1T fresh grated orange peel
1T cornstarch
1/2 t. salt
2T vegetable oil
Hot fluffy rice

Directions:
Dice chicken into 1" pieces
Combine soy sauce, sherry, green onion, ginger and pepper flakes.
Add chicken.
Cover and refrigerate 3-4 hours
Coarsely chop water chestnut slices and thinly slice snow peas. Set aside
Drain pineapple, reserving juice.
Combine 1/4c of reserved pineapple juice, orange juice, orange peel, cornstarch and salt. Set aside
In large skillet or wok, stir fry chicken mixture in hot oil about 3 minutes or until chicken is cooked.
Add chopped water chestnuts and snow peas and stir fry until tender crisp - about 2 minutes
Stir in pineapple /orange juice mixture.
Cook and stir until sauce thickens and coats chicken
Add pineapple chunks and heat through
Serve over hot fluffy rice
