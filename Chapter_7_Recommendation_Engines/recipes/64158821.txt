Title:
Quick Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Hillshire farm Smoked Sausage
1 tablespoon butter or margarine
1 onion, chopped
1 red bell pepper, seeded, chopped
1 green bell pepper, seeded, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
1/2 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
salt to taste
6 cups hot cooked long grain white rice

Directions:
1. Cut sausage into 1/2" slices.  Melt butter in a 5-6 quart pan over medium-high heat.  Add sausage, onion, red bell pepper, green bell pepper and garlic; cook, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes or until vegetables are soft and sausage is lightly browned.
2. Stir in tomatoes, pepper sauce, black pepper, crushed red pepper flakes and salt to taste.  Bring to a boil.  Cook 3 minutes.
3. Gently stir in hot rice and cook 3-5 minutes or until heated through.

Submitter: 
Natasha Sellner
Description: 
Hillshire farm Smoked sausage is the savory secret to mastering this quick, classic Cajun dish.
