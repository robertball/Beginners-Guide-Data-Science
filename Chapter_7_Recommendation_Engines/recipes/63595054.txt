Title:
Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c butter
1/2 c chopped onion
1 tsp garlic powder
3/4 tsp salt
3 Tbsp flour
2 cube beef bouillon
2 small can's mushroom pieces
1 can water
1 lb round or sirloin steak cut in cubes
1 can beefy mushroom soup
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 cup sour cream
1 pkg egg noodles

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Melt butter and brown onion.
Add garlic powder and mushroom pieces.
Remove mixture from butter and brown meat.
Put meat and veggies in 2 quart casserole dish.
Dissolve bouillon in water.
Add salt, flour, bouillon, and soup to butter in a fry pan; cook till thick.
Add Worcestershire sauce and Kitchen Bouquet.
Pour mixture over meat and bake for 1 hour or longer.
Add sour cream just before serving.
