Title:
Spiced Chia Seed Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1 tsp cardamon
1 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp cloves
1 tbsp raw sugar, brown sugar or honey
1/2 cup chia seeds
optional: diced strawberries or peaches

Directions:
Mix milk, sugar and spices together.
Add chia seeds and stir well.
Refrigerate until desired consistency, stirring occasionally.
Add any additional toppings, like fruit or nuts.
