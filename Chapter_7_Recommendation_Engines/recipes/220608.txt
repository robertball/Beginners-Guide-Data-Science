Title:
Vinny's Red Kidney Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1525518.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups red kidney beans, drained
1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained and flaked
1/4 cup chopped red onion
3 tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon curry powder
salt and ground black pepper to taste (optional)

Directions:
Combine kidney beans, tuna, and red onion in a bowl.
Stir olive oil, parsley, curry powder, salt, and black pepper into kidney bean mixture until well combined.

Submitter:

Description: 
Fresh salad with red kidney beans, tuna and olive oil. Tastes great with an Indian flair! Made it up on the spot!
