Title:
Dark Chocolate Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3897559.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cube butter
1 1/4 c. sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
2/3 c. flour
1/3 c plus 2 Tbsp. cocoa powder
1/4 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2.  Melt butter in microwave.
3.  Add to large mixing bowl, combine with sugar and eggs - mix by hand until blended.
4.  Add dry ingredients, mix by hand, just until incorporated. Do not overmix.
5.  Batter will be thick.
6.  Scoop batter in to greased 8x8 pan
7.  Bake for 30-35 minutes (or until toothpick comes out clean)
8.  Let cool 30 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
Jenner Porter
Description: 
This is my go-to brownie recipe, a simple version I created from a more complex recipe. Not too dark. Not too sweet - but fudgy and delicious, with a thin sugary crust! Great base recipe to doctor up with your favorite add ins.
