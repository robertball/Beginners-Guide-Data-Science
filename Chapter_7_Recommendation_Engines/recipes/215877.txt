Title:
Easy Slow-Cooked Chicken Cacciatore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988643.jpg
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 (24 ounce) jar Classico® Tomato and Basil Sauce
2 green bell pepper, seeded and cubed
8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 onion, finely diced
2 tablespoons minced garlic

Directions:
Put the chicken in the slow cooker. Top with the spaghetti sauce, green bell peppers, mushrooms, onion, and garlic.
Cover, and cook on Low for 7 to 9 hours.
