Title:
Pasta with Tomatoes, Mozarella and Basil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lbs. plum tomatoes, seeded, coarsley chopped
8 oz. fresh mozarella cheese cut into 1 x1 pieces
3 TBS. extra virgin olive oil
2 tsp. red wine vinegar
1/2 cup chopped basil
(optional) some balsalmic vinegar to taste
12 oz. mini penne pasta

Directions:
Mix tomatoes, mozarella, oil and vinegar in medium bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Let stand 1 hour at room temperature. mix in basil.
Cook pasta in large pot of boiling salted water until tender but still firm to bite. Drain. Return pasta to pot. Add tomato mixture and toss gently to blend. Season with salt and pepper.
