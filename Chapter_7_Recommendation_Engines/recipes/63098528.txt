Title:
Slow Cooker Italian Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886800.jpg
Ingredients:
5 pounds beef chuck roast
1 (.7 ounce) packages dry Italian salad dressing mix
1 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon oregano
1 teaspoon basil
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon parsley
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 1/2 teaspoon dried onions
2 beef bouillon cubes
3 cups water
1 (16 ounce) jar pepperoncini peppers
10 hamburger buns, split

Directions:
Place the roast into a slow cooker, and season with Italian dressing mix and spices. Dissolve bouillon cubes in water and pour over roast.
Cover, and cook on low for 10 to 12 hours.
Shred the meat with two forks.
Refrigerate meat and juice separately overnight.
Skim the fat off the juice, and return the juice and meat to the slow cooker.
Add the peppers, and as much of the juice as you like for additional flavor.
Reheat on high for a couple of hours.
Serve with horseradish and provolone cheese.
Serve on buns.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Chuck roast is slow cooked with Italian salad dressing mix until it is tender enough to shred with a fork.Modified from: Original Homemade Italian Beef
