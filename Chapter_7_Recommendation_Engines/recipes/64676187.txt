Title:
Quiche Lorraine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Refrigerated pie crust
6 oz thick cut bacon, cut into small strips
2 large eggs
2 large egg whites
1 1/4 cups half and half
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon white pepper
Pinch nutmeg
1 cup grated Gruyere or Swiss cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Place pie crust in pie pan and prick the bottom
Bake until crust is set; 12-14 minutes
In medium skillet, cook bacon until very crisp; remove
Arrange bacon evenly over bottom of baked crust
In large bowl, beat eggs and add other ingredients
Pour into crust
Bake for 30-35 minutes, until custard is golden and set
Rest for 15 minutes before serving
