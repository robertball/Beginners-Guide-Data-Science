Title:
Diet Friendly Chocolate Eclair Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c non-fat milk
2 1.4 oz packages sugar free, fat free instant vanilla pudding
2 t vanilla
1 8 oz frozen non-dairy whipped topping, thawed
1 box graham crackers
1 jar hot fudge sauce (sugar-free, if available)

Directions:
Butter or spray bottom and sides of 9x12 pan (or tupperware).
Mix together milk, instant vanilla pudding, and vanilla.
Fold in non-dairy whipped topping.
Line bottom of 9x12 pan with graham crackers (cut to fit)
Top with 1/2 of pudding mixture. Top with more graham crackers. Top with rest of pudding mixture. Top with another layer graham crackers.
Heat hot fudge, just until pourable. Pour hot fudge sauce over top layer of graham crackers.
Cover and refrigerate at least TWO days.
