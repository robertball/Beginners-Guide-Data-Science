Title:
Classic Key Lime Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 egg yolk
14 ounces sweetened condensed milk
4 ounces key lime juice
1 9 inch graham cracker pie crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine the yolks and condensed milk in a medium-size bowl, and mix well.
Add lime juice, a little at a time, and beat until filling is smooth and creamy
Pour into crust and bake in oven for 10 minutes.
Serve throughly chilled and topped with whipped cream and garnished with lime slices

Submitter: 
Ed Canipelli
Description: 
This is the best and easiest Key Lime Pie.  No fuss, not a lot of mess and travels well.
