Title:
Hot Cheddar Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large egg
1 cup mayo
1/4 t. white pepper
hot pepper flakes to taste
3 c. shredded cheddar sharp cheese
1 cup chopped onion

Directions:
Combine first 4 ingredients.  Stir in cheese and onion.  Spoon into 1 quart souffle dish.  Bake at 325 degrees for 25 min or until puffed and golden.  Serve immediately with crackers or crusty bread.
