Title:
Butte Style Pasty Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2612021.jpg
Ingredients:
Pie Crust, cut into 6 to 8 inch circles, total of 10
2 lbs round steak, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
2 lbs potatoes, cut into 1/4 inch cubes
2 diced medium yellow onions
1/2 cup butter
1 tbls parsley
1 tbls Cumin
Salt and Pepper
Brown Gravy, pepared

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Mix Steak, Potatoes, Onion, Parsley, Cumin, Salt and Pepper until well blended.
Place an equal amount of mix onto each pie crust circle, top each with a dollop of butter
Fold pie crust in half and seal with a fork.
Place each finished crust on baking sheet, place in oven for about an 1 1/2 hours or until pie crust is very golden brown.
Serve hot with brown gravy.
