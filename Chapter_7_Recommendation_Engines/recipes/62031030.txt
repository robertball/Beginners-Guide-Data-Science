Title:
Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:

Directions:
10 slices of bread, cut into cubes   1/4 c. oleo, melted
1 tsp cinnamon   1/2 c. raisins   6 eggs   3 c. hot milk
2 tsp vanilla   3/4 c. sugar   1/2 tsp salt    nutmeg to taste
Combine bread cubes, oleo, raisins and cinnamon; mix well and place in 2 qt. dish. Slightly beat eggs; add sugar, vanilla and salt. Beat until sugar is dissolved. Add milk to eggs; beat well. Pour over bread cubes and let soak for 5 minutes. Sprinkle with nutmeg. Bake at 375 for 25 minutes.
