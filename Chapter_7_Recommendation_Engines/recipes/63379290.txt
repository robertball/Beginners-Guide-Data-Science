Title:
Low Carb Chicken with Creamy Mushroom Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbls. butter divided
4 boneless skinless chicken breast
1 Lb. sliced mushrooms
1 cup heavy cream
1/2 tespoon xanthan
Salt & Pepper
Garic Salt
2 Tablespoons sour cream

Directions:
Melt 1 Tablespoon butter over medium heat, in a skillet that will fit all 4 Chicken breasts. Brown the chicken until just cooked. Sprinkle both sides with garlic salt,  desired. Remove the chicken from the pan and keep warm.
Melt the remaining 2 tablespoons butter, add the sliced mushrooms, and stir until just colored. Add the cream, stir; if using sour crea, add and stir til mixed. Whethe cream starts to simmer, spirnkle int he xanthan gum and stir like mad. Allow to chicken and any juices to the pan, trn to coat both sides with the sauce.
Serve with cauliflowre rice and any desired vegtables
