Title:
Teriyaki Beef Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* eye of round steak cut into 1/4 inch strips
* flour tortillas
* lettuce finely cut up
* tomato finely cut up
* teriyaki sauce

Directions:
Over medium heat brown steak. When almost done, add teriyaki sauce.
On the flour tortillas, place a small amount of cut up lettuce and tomatoes. Add cooked teriyaki beef and roll up. Cut into 2 inch rolls and plate.
