Title:
Spicy Southwest Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, chopped
2 jalapeños, chopped (seeded, if desired)
1 teaspoon ground cumin
3 cans (15.25 ounces each)whole-kernel corn, drained
2 cups chicken broth
1 chicken breast chopped into small cubes
cayenne pepper (to taste)
1 lime
Cilantro leaves for garnish
Sour Cream for garnish

Directions:
HEAT oil in large saucepan over medium-high heat. Cook onion, garlic and jalapeños, stirring occasionally, until onion is tender. Stir in cumin and cayenne. Add corn, chicken, and broth. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low; cover.
COOK for 15 minutes. Remove from heat; cool to room temperature. Transfer half of soup to blender; cover. Blend until smooth. Return to saucepan.
COOK over low heat, stirring occasionally, just until heated through without boiling. Garnish with cilantro, lime juice, and a dollop of sour cream if desired.
