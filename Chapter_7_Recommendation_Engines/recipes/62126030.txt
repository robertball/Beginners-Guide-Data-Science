Title:
Slow Cooker Chili Verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lb. chicken breast or pork roast, cut in small pieces
1/2 c. flour
1 onion, chopped
6 small cans chopped green chilis
1 jar taco sauce
1 (16oz) can diced tomatoes
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp oregano
2 c water
Serve - flour tortillas, sour cream, cheese, taco sauce.

Directions:
Dredge meat and onion in flour and brown in a pan with oil.
When browned, tranfer to slow cooker, add remainder of ingredients and cook all day on low.
When you serve, put 3 large tbsp in tortilla with grated cheese and roll up. Put some more of the mixture on top with more cheese and put into microwave for 1 minute or until the cheese melts. Top with sour cream, taco sauce, and any other fixings you would like.

Submitter: 
Nia
Description: 
This is an awesome meal to come home to after a long day. Easy to put together and let your slow cooker do the work!
