Title:
Neiman's $250 Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. butter
2 c. brown sugar
2 c. white sugar
4 eggs
2
t. vanilla
4 c. flour
1 t. salt
2 t. baking powder
2 t. baking soda
5 c. rolled oats
24 oz. chocolate chips
8 oz. grated Hershey Bar
3 c. chopped pecans (optional)

Directions:
Place the 5 cups of rolled oats in a blender and process until they are a fine powder.
Cream butter and sugars.
Add eggs and vanilla.
Mix together in a separate bowl the dry ingredients.
Add to butter mixture.
Add chocolate chips, Hershey bar and nuts.
Mix well without over beating.
Roll dough into balls and bake 2 inches apart on cookie sheet.
Bake 8-10 minutes @ 375&#0176;
Cool two minutes then remove to cooling rack.
