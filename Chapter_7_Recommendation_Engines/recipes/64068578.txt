Title:
Hamburger Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 LB Hamburger (cooked & drained)
1 Lb box elbow or penne pasta (cooked & drained)
1 can chicken with rice
1 can cream of chicken
1 can cream corn
salt, pepper, minced onion, & minced garlic to taste

Directions:
Cook hamburger with seasoning's and drain, then add to a 4-6qt casserole dish.  Cook & drain pasta and add to dish. Add the 3 cans and mix everything until well blended. Cover dish and microwave for 10 minutes on 70% power. Let stand for few minutes. Stir & serve.

Submitter: 
Amy Bridges
Description: 
This is a yummy dish to make up quickly. Sometimes I double the ingredients so I can have more leftover!
