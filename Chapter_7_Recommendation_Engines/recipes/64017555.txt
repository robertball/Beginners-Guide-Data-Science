Title:
Tapenade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1115919.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup pitted oil-cured black olives
1/4 cup coarsely chopped parsley
2 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp drained capers
1 garlic clove, crushed
2 tsp fresh thyme

Directions:
Whirl olives with parsley, oil, lemon juice, capers, garlic and thyme in a food processor until finely chopped, scraping down sides if needed.
Season with fresh pepper.
Refrigerate up to 2 weeks.

Submitter: 
Evie
Description: 
This tapenade recipe is taken from Chatelaine. You can use it as a topping on crispy bread or stuffed in chicken. Delicious.
