Title:
Raspberry Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/729454.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, softened
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1/4 tsp. salt
4 cups powdered sugar
1/2 cup seedless raspberry jam

Directions:
Whip butter, salt and vanilla in a large bowl with a stand or hand mixer.  Add 1 cup of powdered sugar at a time, thoroughly mixing after each cup.  Add the jam to taste and consistency.

Submitter: 
Megan C. A.
Description: 
A simple buttercream frosting that can be used with any cake or flavor.  Substitute the jam to experiment with different cakes!
