Title:
Mom's Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 1 1/2 lbs. ground beef
1 medium onion finely chopped
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1 -2 eggs
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp. dry mustard
1/4 - 1/2 tsp dried sage
1/2 cup ketchup
salt and pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and press in a loaf pan.
Bake @350 F. for 1-2 hours (until done to your liking).

Submitter: 
TJ
Description: 
A moist meatloaf the whole family loves.  Some even ask for it for their birthday meal!
