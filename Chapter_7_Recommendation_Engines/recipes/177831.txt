Title:
Cheddar Potato Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981327.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups mashed potatoes*
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Cheddar Cheese Soup
1/3 cup sour cream or yogurt
Generous dash ground black pepper
1 green onion, chopped

Directions:
Stir the potatoes, soup, sour cream, black pepper and onion in a medium bowl. Spoon into a 1 1/2-quart baking dish.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes or until the potato mixture is hot.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Savory mashed potatoes are laced with a cheddar cheese sauce, sour cream and green onion. Baked to a golden turn, this dish is a real crowd pleaser.
