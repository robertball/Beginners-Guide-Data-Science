Title:
Easy Horseradish Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup nofat plain yogurt
1/2 cup nofat  sour cream
1/8 cup prepared horseradish
2 tablespoons chopped green onion
1/2 cup Miracle Whip
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk together the yogurt, sour cream, horseradish, green onion, Miracle Whip, salt and pepper until well combined.  Cover and chill until serving.
