Title:
Horseradish Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5886319.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup of Sour Cream
1/3 cup of Mayo
2 Tablespoons of prepared Horseradish
1-2 Tablespoons of Fresh Dill chopped
Salt to taste
Pepper to tast

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.  Enjoy.

Submitter: 
Cara
Description: 
This can be used for prime rib, as a spread on burgers and sandwiches or to garnish grilled chicken.  Goes really with with roasted and grilled meats.
