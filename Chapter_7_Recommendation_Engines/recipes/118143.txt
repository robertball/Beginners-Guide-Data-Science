Title:
Cheddar Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup shredded carrot
2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup, undiluted
1 cup water
1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a saucepan, cook and stir carrot and onion in butter until tender.
Stir in the soup, water and Worcestershire sauce. Bring to a boil; reduce heat.
Stir in cheese until melted.

Submitter: 
Kathy  Scott
Description: 
Kathy Scott, Hemingford, Nebraska, adds a few ingredients to condensed cream of chicken soup for this rich creamy concoction. 'It's a wonderful side dish or main course,' she relates.
