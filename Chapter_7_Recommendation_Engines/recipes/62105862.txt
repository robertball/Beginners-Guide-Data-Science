Title:
Egg Nests Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of cake mix
1 can of icing
1 bag of candy coated chocolate shaped eggs
(Peanut M&M's are good)
1 bag of coconut shavings

Directions:
Follow directions on box for preparing cup cakes.
Once cup cakes are cooled cut a out a hole in the center about 2 cm deep and 1 1/2 inches wide.
Ice the cup cakes, including inside of the "dent".
Once you are done icing the cup cakes, put the coconut shavings in a shallow pan.
Bake the coconut shavings for about 7 min on 400 degrees or untill golden brown.
Once the coconut shavings are cooled cover the iced cup cakes with the coconut shavings.
Then place the candies inside the "dent".
