Title:
Banana Daiquiri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/840011.jpg
Ingredients:
1 oz lime juice (juice from 1 lime)
1 1/2 oz light rum
1/2 oz triple sec (or other orange flavored liquor)
1/2 large banana, sliced
1 tsp sugar (adjust to taste)
1 cup ice cubes

Directions:
Combine lime juice, rum, triple sec, banana and sugar in a blender and puree.
Add ice cubes and blend on highest setting for 15-20 seconds.

Submitter: 
Patrick Washburn
Description: 
A nice change of pace from the usual strawberry daiquiri.
