Title:
Cayenne Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
2 cups flour
1 cup cocoa powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Cream butter and sugar till fluffy. Add eggs and blend well. Add cayenne, cocoa, baking soda, and salt and blend thoroughly. Add flour and blend. Fold in chips. Refrigerate one hour. Drop teaspoonfuls onto lightly greased cookie sheet. Bake 8-10 minutes.
