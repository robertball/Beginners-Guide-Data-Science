Title:
Chili surprise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of carrots (398 ml)
1 can of chopped mushrooms (284 ml)
1 tenderized flatten/boneless/skinless chicken
1/2 cup of sun dried tomatoes, well chopped
1 can of plain sauce (398 ml)
1 can of kidney beans (540 ml)
1/2 tsp cinnimon
1/2 tsp marjoram
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1 tb paprika
4 cloves of crushed garlic
container of grated romano for topping

Directions:
fill a 2.5 qrt crock pot with water. Add all the ingredients and turn the crock pot on high. (you could probably do this on a stove with a same sized pot). When your chicken is cooked, cube it and throw it back in the pot. Serve your bowl topped with romano and garlic bread.
