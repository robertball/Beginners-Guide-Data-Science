Title:
Pesto Twists Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984122.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (17.3 ounce) package frozen puff pastry sheets, thawed
1 (7 ounce) container BUITONI® Refrigerated Pesto with Basil
1/2 cup chopped hazelnuts or almonds
1/2 cup BUITONI® Freshly Shredded Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Place pastry sheets on lightly floured surface. Roll each sheet into 12-inch square. Spread pesto over one rolled pastry sheet. Sprinkle nuts and cheese evenly over pesto. Lay second sheet on top and press lightly. Cut into twelve 1-inch-wide strips. Twist each strip and place on baking sheet.
Bake for 15 minutes or until light golden brown.

Submitter: 
Buitoni, courtesy of meals.com
Description: 
These tasty twists made with pesto, hazelnuts and Parmesan cheese are a delicious way to start the evening.
