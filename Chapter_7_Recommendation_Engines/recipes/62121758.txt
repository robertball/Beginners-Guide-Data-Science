Title:
Runza Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs ground beef
1 small onion, chopped
1 small head of cabbage, chopped
1/2 cup water
1 can cheddar cheese soup
2 pkg crescent rolls
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Brown ground beef with onion in large skillet, drain. Add cabbage, salt, pepper and garlic powder to taste.
Add 1/2 cup water and simmer (covered) about 10 minutes.  Add cheddar cheese soup.
Grease a 9x13 in. pan and press 1 pkg of the crescent rolls into the pan and halfway up the sides.
Spread meat mixture over rolls.  Top with cheddar cheese and remaining crescent rolls
Bake 350 degrees for 20 to 3o minutes.

Submitter: 
NEgirl
Description: 
If you don't have time to make the dough for an actual runza, this is a quick, delicious alternative.
