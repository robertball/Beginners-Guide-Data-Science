Title:
Great American Grilled Steak with Ranch-Style Dipping Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977512.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 teaspoons Lawry's® Garlic Salt
2 1/2 teaspoons Lawry's® Seasoned Salt
2 1/2 teaspoons Lawry's® Seasoned Pepper
4 New York strip steaks
Lawry's® Steak Sauce
Ranch-Style Dipping Sauce
1/2 cup Lawry's® Steak Sauce
1/4 cup Wish-Bone® Ranch Dressing

Directions:
In small bowl, combine Garlic Salt, Seasoned Salt and Seasoned Pepper; mix well.  Evenly sprinkle rub seasoning on each side of steak; press rub into meat.  Grill steaks over medium-high heat to desired doneness.  Serve with Lawry's(R) Steak Sauce and Ranch-Style Dipping Sauce.
Ranch-Style Dipping Sauce:  Combine Lawry's(R) Steak Sauce and Wish-Bone(R) Ranch Dressing.  Chill.

Submitter: 
LAWRY'S®
Description: 
Great grilled steaks served with a mellow dipping sauce. This meal is ready in no time!
