Title:
Curried Rice Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup chopped green onions, divided
4 garlic cloves, minced
1/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
3 cups uncooked long grain rice or brown rice
3 tablespoons curry powder
6 cups (48 oz) chicken broth
1 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup craisins
1 cup Almond Accents (near bagged lettuce in store)

Directions:
In a large skillet, saute 1/2 cup onions and garlic in butter until tender. Stir in rice and curry powder. Saute for 2-3 minutes or until rice is lightly browned.
In a saucepan, heat broth and salt. Pour over rice mixture; stir. Cover and simmer for 35-40 minutes or until rice is tender. Remove from the heat; stir in raisins, almonds and remaining onions.

Submitter: 
Sue Farnsworth Sattel
Description:
