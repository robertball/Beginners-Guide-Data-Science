Title:
Spring into Summer Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the dressing:
2 cups mayonnaise
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
1/4 cup fresh dill, chopped
2 tablespoons water
1 tablespoon minced garlic
salt & pepper to taste
For the salad:
2 pounds asparagus, trimmed to 2 inch pieces
2 bags pre-washed salad greens, such as leaf, butter or boston
1 pound cooked shrimp
2 pints grape or cherry tomatoes, halved
2 avocado, diced

Directions:
dressing:
add the dressing ingredients into the container of a blender.
blend on low until smooth.
season with salt and pepper and refrigerate, preferably overnight.
Salad:
Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and add asparagus.
cook until just tender, about three minutes.
drain and rinse with cold water.
Add all salad ingredients into a large serving bowl.
Toss with desired amount of dressing and serve.

Submitter: 
Saveur
Description: 
This salad is an Easter tradition in our family.  The ingredients say spring, but it's delicious any time of year.  Skip the appetizers, the abundance of shrimp in this dish make it an appetizer and salad in one.  Enjoy!
