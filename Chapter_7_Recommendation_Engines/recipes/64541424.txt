Title:
Big Ray's Slow Cooker Kiebasa & White Bean Cassoulet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3171683.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups dry white beans (Great Northern or Navy 8 oz.)
1 lb. kielbasa, cut into 1" pieces
3 cup chicken stock
1 15 oz. can stewed tomatoes, un-drained
1 medium onion, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
2 tsp fresh thyme leaves, chopped
1/4 tsp salt, or to taste
1/4 tsp pepper, or to taste

Directions:
Combine beans, kielbasa, stock, tomatoes, onion, garlic, thyme, salt and pepper in a 4 - 6 quart slow cooker.
Cover and cook until beans are tender and creamy, (about 7-8 hours on low or 4-5 hours on high).
Serve with croutons and chopped parsely.
