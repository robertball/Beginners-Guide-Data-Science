Title:
Homemade Smoky Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 ounces tomatoes, diced
4 ounces red onion, diced
3 ounces cilantro, finely chopped
1 large jalapeno, diced
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
1 garlic clove, mined
1 tsp fresh ground black pepper
2 tsp liquid smoke
1/2 tsp chipotle flakes
1 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
Mix together all the ingredients. Pulse a few times in a food processor, if desired.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
This no salt added salsa is delicious raw or can be used in any recipe requiring salsa or picante sauce. It will keep, covered, in the fridge up to one week.
