Title:
stuffed baby Eggplant Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5528076.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tab olive oil
1- small onion chopped
2 tab minced garlic
1 small can mushrooms with the juice ( water)
1/3 c frtta cheese crumbled
1/4 C Panko bread crumbs
1/4 C seasoned breadcrumbs
10-12 small tiny baby eggplant
Salt & Pepper
bASIL
ITALIAN SEASONING

Directions:
in large frying pan  put olive oil..onions...garlic fry med heat stir often..
cut eggplants in 1/2 and scoop out the pulp...cut the pulp into small pieces  add to the fry pan  save the skins to stuff....add mushrooms. water and all...cook till onions clear...
place in a bowl and add bread crumbs and cheese and seasonings...take tablespoons of the mixture and stuff the eggplant skins...place in a pie dish that has small amount olive oil spread around sides.. or 8x8 .cook covered with tinfoil about 30 min then uncover for 10......can be served hot or cold
