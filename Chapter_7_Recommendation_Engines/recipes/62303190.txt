Title:
Yummy Vanilla Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/366197.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 c. shortening (butter flavored)
1/2 c. butter or margarine
1 tsp. vanilla extract
4 Tbs. milk or half & half
2 lb package (32 oz.)confectioners' sugar

Directions:
In a large bowl, cream together shortening and butter.  Slowly add confectioners' sugar. Follow with vanilla and 1 Tbs. milk.  Beat on high with electric mixer until light and fluffy, about 5-8 minutes, adding additional milk as necessary to reach desired consistency.
