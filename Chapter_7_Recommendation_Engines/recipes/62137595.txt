Title:
Fancy Pimento Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 cup finely diced chives
1/2 cup finely diced roasted red pepper
4 oz regular cream cheese
2 oz dill pickle juice
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 Tbsp Cholula Hot Sauce
Sprinkle of Pepper

Directions:
1. On a box grater, grate half the cheddar on smallest size grate.
2. Grate rest of cheddar on the next size up.
3. Combine all ingredients in mixing bowl and stir with fork until evenly combined.

Submitter: 
TNCOLLEGEGIRL
Description: 
I had the best pimento at a resort in North Carolina and replicated it in my kitchen.  Tastes great with flatbread crackers, and topped with chow chow and a slice of pickled okra!  Try it!
