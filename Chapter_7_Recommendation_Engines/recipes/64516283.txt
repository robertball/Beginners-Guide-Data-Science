Title:
Luckman Coffee-Chocolate Peanut Butter Frappe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 TB mocha powder
3 TB creamy peanut butter (they use Jif, which i don't like)
1 cup strong brewed coffee and espresso
ice
1/2 cup nonfat milk
whipped topping
chocolate syrup

Directions:
blend all ingredients except last 2 in blender that can handle ice.
blend until relatively smooth
add whipped topping and syrup to your liking.
