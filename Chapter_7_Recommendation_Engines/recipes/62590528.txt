Title:
Chicken Broccoli Braid Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts, cooked and chopped (may also use canned)
1c broccoli, chopped
1 garlic clove
1c shredded cheddar
2t dill
1/4 t salt
2 pkg refrigerated crescent rolls
1/2c mayo
1 egg white, lightly beaten

Directions:
Chop chicken and broccoli.
Add cheese, garlic, mayo, dill, and salt.
Lay crescent rolls out and roll flat, mending seams.
Cut dough into strips leaving 6" in center uncut.
Fill dough and braid.
Brush with egg white.
Bake 25-28 minutes at 375.
