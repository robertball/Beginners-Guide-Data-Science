Title:
Oreo® Fluff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) bag of Oreo® cookies
2  (8 ounce) container's of Cool Whip®

Directions:
Crush the (14 ounce) bag of cookies
Put both containers of Cool Whip® in a big bowl
Put cookies in bowl with the Cool Whip®
Mix till all the cookies are covered

Submitter: 
Hope Smith
Description: 
We make this for kids at camp it is quick and easy.
