Title:
Strawberry Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1# torn spinach greens
3-4 cups strawberries, sliced thin
glazed almonds or pecans
1 T. sesame seeds
1/3 c. sugar
1/4 c. dry mustard
1/8 t. garlic powder
1/2 t. paprika
1/4 c. white vinegar
1/2 t. Worcestershire sauce
1/2 c. Wesson oil
'

Directions:
Blend together the sesame seeds through the Wesson oil.
Pour over spinach.
Add strawberries and nuts.
