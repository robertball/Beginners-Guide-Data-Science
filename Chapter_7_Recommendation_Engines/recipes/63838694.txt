Title:
Oreo Truffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1066593.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package OREO Chocolate Sandwich Cookies, divided
1 (8 ounce) package PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened
2 (8 ounce) packages BAKER'S Semi-Sweet Baking Chocolate, melted
Decorative sprinkles

Directions:
Crush 45 cookies to fine crumbs in food processor; place in medium bowl.  (Cookies can also be finely crushed in a resealable plastic bag using a rolling pin.)
Add cream cheese; mix until well blended.
Roll cookie mixture into 42 balls, about 1-inch in diameter.
Dip balls in chocolate; place on wax paper-covered baking sheet. (Any leftover chocolate can be stored at room temperature for another use.) Sprinkle with decorative sprinkles.
Refrigerate until firm, about 1 hour.
Store leftover truffles, covered, in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
OREO cookies, baking chocolate, and cream cheese are all you need to make this easy chocolate truffle recipe.Modified from: Easy OREO Truffles
