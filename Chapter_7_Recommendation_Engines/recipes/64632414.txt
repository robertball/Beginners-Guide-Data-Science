Title:
Club Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4481838.jpg
Ingredients:
5-6 chicken breasts (thawed)
1 1/2 cup crushed Toppables or Club crackers
1 package Italian salad dressing mix
4 Tablespoons butter
Pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 375 degrees.
Spray a 9x13 pan with non-stick cooking spray.
In a bowl, add the cracker crumbs and and Italian dressing mix, and stir until well-combined.
Melt the butter in a separate bowl.
Dip the chicken breast in the butter, and roll in the cracker crumbs.
Place on prepared baking pan.
Sprinkle with pepper to cook.
Bake for 20-25 minutes at 375 degrees, or until the chicken is fully cooked and has no pink.
