Title:
Chicken and Spinach Meatballs with Smokey Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meatballs:
1 pound fresh spinach
1 pound ground chicken
1/2 medium onion, finely chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 egg
1/4 cup milk
2 slices bread, toasted and chopped
salt and pepper
Sauce:
1 TB butter
1/2 ts olive oil
1 1/2 TB onion, finely chopped
1 TB all-purpose flour
3/4 cup milk
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 1/4 cup shredded smoked gouda

Directions:
Meatballs:
In a large pan, wilt spinach over simmering water.  Drain spinach, and squeeze out excess water in a clean kitchen towel.
Preheat oven to 400F
Put chicken, spinach, onion, garlic, egg, milk, and bread in a large mixing bowl.  Mix well.  Form into 12 large meatballs, and place on a cookie sheet.  Cook in oven for 20 minutes.
Sauce:
Melt butter with olive oil over medium heat in a saucepan.  Add onion, and cook for 2 minutes.  Stir in flour and cook for 1 minute.  Add milk and chicken broth and cook, stirring occasionally, until it reaches a boil.  Stir in shredded cheese until melted.
Serve meat balls with cheese sauce.
