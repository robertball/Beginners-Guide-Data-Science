Title:
Creme Brulee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 Eggs
1/2 gal Heavy Cream
2 1/2 cup White Sugar
2 Tbl Vanilla Bean

Directions:
1.  In a steel bowl add eggs and sugar together, whip until well blended.  Then add heavy cream and Vanilla.  Mix well.
2.  In bowl, place on a double boiler and stir well until thick, at about 180'F
3.  Strain with fine strainer and pour into pitchers to pour into Monkey dishes.  Let cool overnight.
4.  1 hour prior to serving, sprinkle liberally with granulated sugar evenly and use torch to candy.  Cool 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Chef Hurley Baird
