Title:
corn cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. corn flour
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
3/4 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. chile powder or cayenne
1/4 c. butter, room temp and cut into cubes
1 c. milk
1 T. honey
2 c. fresh corn
2 large eggs, separated
1 large egg white
chopped chiles, optional

Directions:
Whisk together dry ingredients in a large bowl.
In a small pan, heat butter, milk, and honey till butter is melted. Set aside until tepid.
Make a well in dry ingredients and stir in the liquids, the 2 egg yolks, the corn, and the chile.
Beat the egg whites till stiff, then fold them into the corn mixture.
Heat some butter in a skillet.  When hot, spoon butter in mounds into the pan.  Brown on both sides, then serve.
