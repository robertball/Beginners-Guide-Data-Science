Title:
Miranda Lambert's Magic Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. crunchy peanut butter
1 c. granulated sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.  Roll into golf balls and place on greased cookie sheet.  Crisscross with a fork.  Bake 10 minutes at 350 degrees
