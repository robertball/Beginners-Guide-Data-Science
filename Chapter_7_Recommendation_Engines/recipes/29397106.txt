Title:
Hot Mustard Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz. ground mustard
1 cup vinegar
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
dash of salt

Directions:
Mix mustard and vinegar together and let sit overnight.
Place in saucepan add sugar, eggs and salt.
Bring to boil stirring constantly.
