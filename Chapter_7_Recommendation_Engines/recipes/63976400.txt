Title:
Fall 5-Spice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 stalks celery, chopped
1/2 onion, chopped
1 tbsp oil
1/2 tbsp Chinese 5-spice blend
4 medium sweet potatoes
2 apples, peeled, cored, and cubed
6 cups vegetable broth
1.5 tsp salt

Directions:
1. Put broth in pot and bring to a boil.
2. Heat oil in a skillet; saute onion and celery until tender. Add 5-spice blend, stir to coat, and cook one minute more.
3. Add sweet potatoes, apples, salt, and sauteed mixture to boiling broth, and cook until sweet potatoes are tender.  Let cool at least 5 minutes, then blend in batches.  Makes four 1.5 cup servings.

Submitter: 
soupysales
Description: 
I am a soup fanatic, and found that Chinese 5-spice was a great way to jazz up common fall vegetables.
