Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2469700.jpg
Ingredients:
3 1/2 tbs butter, 2 tbs powdered sugar, 14 oz marshmallow fluff, 7 cups Rice Krispies, 1 cup caramel filled chocolate morsels, 1 box white fondant, food coloring, color mist(food coloring) spray, wax paper

Directions:
Melt butter and fluff together over low heat. Mix in Rice Krispies. Let cool for 20 minutes. Add candy morsels. Butter hands and shape your Dinomite body. Spray with color mist. Knead and roll out fondant after sprinkling surface with powdered sugar. Use food coloring to make the colors that you need and powdered sugar will keep fondant from sticking. Shape all you legs, cape, pants and belt. Add them to your Dinomite Fluffy Bites once it is completely cool and ENJOY!
