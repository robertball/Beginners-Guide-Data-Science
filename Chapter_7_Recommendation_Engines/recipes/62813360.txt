Title:
YAN'S SWEET AND SOUR SAUCE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tsp.corn starch
3/4 cup water
1/3 cup vinegar
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 can tomato paste

Directions:
In sauce pan mix together cornstarch with cold water.
Add the remaining ingredients,stirring constantly untill thickened.
