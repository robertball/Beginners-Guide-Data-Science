Title:
Super Soy-Ginger Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 teaspoon ginger
1 teaspoon ground pepper
1 green onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
3/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup soy sauce
3 tablespoons brown sugar
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
1/2 red onion, separated into rings

Directions:
In small bowl, combine all ingredients, except red onion rings.  Mix well.  Pour marinade over steak (or other meat) in GLASS container (Not metal).  Place onion rings on top of meat.  Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight or on counter top for 2 to 3 hours, turning once for even coverage.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Hanson
Description: 
Great on steaks, this marinade also works well with chicken.  Not too sweet, it is a sure-fire winner with kids and adults alike!
