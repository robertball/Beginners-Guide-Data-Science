Title:
Chili Rellanos Casserolle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sm cans whole green chilies
1 can mil
1 lb monterey jack cheese
2 eggs
1 lb cheddar cheese
1 Tbsp flour
1 small (8 oz) can Tomato Sauce

Directions:
Clean and drain green chilies
Layer Chilies; Cheese; chilies; cheese.
Mix Canned milk, eggs, & Flour and poor over top.
Bake at 350 for 30 min
Pour Tomatoe Sauce over the top
return to oven for 15 min.
