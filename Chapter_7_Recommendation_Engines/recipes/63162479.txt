Title:
Greek Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp olive oil
4-6 frozen Tilapia fillets
4 tsp Cavender's Greek Seasoning (or other Greek seasoning)
1 bag baby spinach
3 medium Roma tomatoes, diced
8 oz crumbled feta cheese
Box of couscous

Directions:
Prep time: 5 min | Cook time: 15 min | Servings: 2-4
1. Prepare couscous, set aside.
2. Over medium heat, warm olive oil in a large frying pan with a lid.
3. Place fillets in frying pan and sprinkle with 2 teaspoons Greek seasoning, turning after 3-5 minutes.
4. Sprinkle with remaining Green seasoning. Cover with spinach, then sprinkle chopped tomatoes and feta over the spinach. Cover pan with lid and allow to steam until spinach is soft and feta is melted (about 5 minutes).

Submitter: 
Jessica Schultz
Description: 
A delicious and healthy fish meal that's great as a last-minute choice for the family. You can use frozen fillets to make it even easier and more convenient!
