Title:
Dorthy Lynch Glazed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 whole chicken breasts, halved
3/4 c apricot preserves
1 envelope 1 1/4 oz dry onion soup mix
1 c Dorthy Lynch dressing

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Arrange chicken skin side up in 9 X 13 pan.
Combine remaining ingredients and spoon over chicken.
Cover and bake for 45 minutes.
Uncover and carefully baste with sauce.
Continue baking uncovered for 15 minutes or until cooked through.
