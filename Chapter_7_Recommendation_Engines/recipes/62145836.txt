Title:
Easy Cinnamon Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 loaves frozen bread
1/2 cup melted butter
1/2 cup sugar
1 teaspoon cinnamon
3/4 cup powdered sugar
2 tablespoons milk

Directions:
Place frozen loaves of bread in a 9x13 well butter pan and place in cold oven overnight to rise.
In the am, melt butter and add sugar and cinnamon.
cut up bread with kitchen shears
pour butter mixture over the top
Bake 1/2 hour at 350.
Combine powdered sugar and milk
Remove from oven and drizzle powdered sugar mixture over the top.

This is a quick easy way to make cinnamon bread for a fast tasty breakfast
