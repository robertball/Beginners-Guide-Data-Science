Title:
Baked Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs all purpose potaotes, scrubbed, and pierced in several places
1 Tbsp, stick butter or margarine
1 & 1/2 C finely chopped onions
2 TBSP minced garlic
1 can 14.5oz low sodium chicken broth
3 C whole milk
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
TOPPINGS: Crumbled bacon, cheddar cheese and chopped scallions

Directions:
Heat over to 400 degrees
Bake potatoes 1 hr or until tender, peel when cool enough to handle
Melt butter in  4-6 qt pot over medium low heat.  Stir in onions & garlic, cook uncovered for 10 minutes until soft but not brown.
Add 2/3 of the potatoes, and mash w/ a potato masher, add broth & milk, salt & pepper, Bring to a simmer, stirring occassionally.
Cut remaining potatoes into small cubes, add to soup, and stir gently to reheat.  Sprinkle each serving with toppings.

Submitter: 
Joanne Dembinski
Description: 
This is the baked potato soup from a popular chain that is now gone.
