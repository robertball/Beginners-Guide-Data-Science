Title:
Crock Pot Jambalya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28 oz. can diced tomatoes
1 lb. Sliced sausage, andouille or smoked
2 c. diced cooked ham
2 c. water
1 c. uncooked rice
2 onions, chopped
1 c. sliced celery
1/2 c. chopped green pepper
1 can tomato paste
2 T. olive oil
1 T. minced flat-leaf parsley
hot pepper sauce to taste
1/2 tsp. dried thyme
2 whole cloves
1 lb. shrimp, peeled and cleaned

Directions:
Put all ingredients except the shrimp in a slow cooker. Stir, cover, cook on low 8-10 hours.
About 30 minutes before you're ready to serve, add shrimp and set temp to high.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
Start it in the morning and enjoy when everyone is home from work or school! Use Andouille sausage if you are a spicy fan, smoked sausage if you need it a little tamer.
