Title:
Roasted Sage Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz bag broccoli florets
Half a red onion, sliced
8-10 fresh sage leaves, torn
1/8 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp garlic salt
1/4 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Spread broccoli in a single layer on a foil-lined cookie sheet
Sprinkle onion slices and sage leaves over broccoli
Drizzle olive oil and sprinkle salt, garlic salt, and pepper over vegetables
Toss to coat
Roast in the preheated oven for 20-30 minutes depending on desired doneness

Submitter: 
Zest to Impress
Description: 
Easy, healthy, and tasty veggie side dish.  The fresh sage leaves become crispy and add texture and flavor.
