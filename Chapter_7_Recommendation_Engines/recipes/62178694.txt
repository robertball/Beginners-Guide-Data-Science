Title:
Turkey Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Lbs Ground Turkey Meat
2 Cans Petite Diced Tomatoes
2 Cans Chili Beans
1 Can Black Beans, drained and rinsed
1 Can White Corn
1 Packet Taco Seasoning
1 Packet Dry Ranch Seasoning

Directions:
In a large soup pot, cook the turkey burger on medium-high heat, occasionaly stirring, until no longer pink. In the same pot, combine the cans of Petite Diced Tomatoes,Chili Beans,the drained and rinsed Black Beans, the Corn, and both seasoning packets. Then fill one of the chili bean cans with water and pour it in. Gently mix all ingredients in. Let it simmer for 15 minutes or until heated through,stirring occasionaly. Serve with corn bread or oyster crackers if desired.
