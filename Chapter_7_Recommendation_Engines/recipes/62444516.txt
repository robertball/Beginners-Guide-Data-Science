Title:
Brownie On A Stick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/460247.jpg
Ingredients:
11 tablespoons unsalted butter
2 cups sugar
4 teaspoons strong coffee
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup bittersweet chocolate chips
3/4 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
4 eggs
1 3/4 cups all purpose flour
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/4 teaspoons baking powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Sift together flour, cocoa, salt and baking powder.  Set aside.
In saucepan melt butter, sugar, vanilla, and coffee until smooth.  Add chocolate chips and stir until fully melted.
Put chocolate mixture into mixing bowl and run on low for 5 min until batter slight cooled.  Add eggs one at a time.  Add flour into batter and blend just until incorporated.
Put batter in fridge and chill about 30 min. Scoop batter onto jellyroll pan and insert stick.
Bake at 350 for 16 minutes.
