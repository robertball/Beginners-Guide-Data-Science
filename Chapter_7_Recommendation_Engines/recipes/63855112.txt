Title:
Gardener's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp vegetable oil
1 onion, finely chopped
1 carrot, diced
1/4 tsp pepper
1 package of ground round (soy ground beef replacement)
1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
2 tbsp flour
1 cup vegetable broth
2 tbsp tomato paste
3 potatoes
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
Oil skillet and cook onion, carrot, and pepper on medium heat for 10 minutes or until golden.
Add ground round and black beans. Sprinkle with flour. Cook for one minute, stirring frequently. Add vegetable broth and tomato paste and bring to boil while stirring. Boil for 2 minutes and then scrape into greased 8-inch square baking dish.
For potato topping: Peel and cut potatoes. Cook in a pot of boiling water until tender. Drain and return to the pot, mashing with milk, salt and pepper. Spread the topping over the vegetable mix. Bake for 20 minutes at 400F, then broil until topping is browned.
