Title:
Divine chicken bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cooked, chopped boneless chicken breast.
1-16oz. bag frozen brocoli florets
4 cups cooked rice
2 cans Cream of Chicken soup
1 cup mayonaise
1 tsp. Lemon juice
1/2 tsp. Curry powder
1 cup Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In 9x13in. baking dish layer as follows.
Cooked cubed Chicken, Frozen brocoli, 4c. cooked rice.
Mix together soup, mayo, lemon juice, and Curry, Pour and spread over the top of the rice layer.
Toss together Cheese and bread crumbs,sprinkle over top of entire casserole.
Bake at 350 for about 50-60 minutes or until cheese is slightly browned.

Submitter: 
ReneeW.
Description: 
A very good friend of mine gave me this recipe, It is such a good meal for any time. Simple to make, but don't expect any leftovers unless you double the recipe.
