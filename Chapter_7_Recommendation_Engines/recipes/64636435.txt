Title:
Sheetpan Shrimp Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4500917.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pk fajita seasoning
1 1/2 lb. shrimp
1 red pepper
1 yellow pepper
1 jalapeno pepper
1 red onion
1 T olive oil
tortillas
Cilantro & Avocado for garnish

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 450.
Slice up peppers and onion into strips, set aside.
In a large bowl mix together seasoning packet and olive oil.
Add shrimp to bowl and mix well.
Lay out on a single layer on a sheet pan.
Add veggies to pan and mix together with seasoned shrimp.
Bake 8-10 min at 450.
Remove shrimp and bake veggies more if you feel they need it.
Broil 2-3 min to char veggies a bit.
Serve with tortillas, cilantro, and avocado. Or any other  condiments you like.
