Title:
Low-Fat Spicy Ranch Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/444962.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup low fat sour cream
1/2 cup low fat Greek yogurt
1/2 cup low-fat butter milk
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried chives
1/2 teaspoon white pepper
1 tablespoon fresh cilantro, chopped
1 medium jalapeno pepper, minced

Directions:
Combine ingredients, chill and serve.

Submitter: 
Scott Koeneman
Description: 
Lots of flavor, a little heat and very low fat makes this perfect for taco salads and more.
