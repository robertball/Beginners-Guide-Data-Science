Title:
Chicken Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/599884.jpg
Ingredients:
1 chicken breast, cut up into small stir fry pieces; 2 Tablespoons of sesame oil, divided; 2 eggs, lightly beaten; 1/2 cup chicken broth; 1-2 cups of frozen stir fry vegetables; 3 Tablespoons of Soy Sauce; 2 cups of instant rice, uncooked

Directions:
Add 1 Tablespoon of sesame oil to fry pan, heat oil.  Add chicken and stir fry until cooked. Set aside.; Add remaining 1 Tablespoon of sesame oil to large skillet over medium heat. Add rice. Stir until slightly browned (time varies between 3-6 minutes).; Add eggs and scramble until cooked thoroughly.; Add broth, vegetables, chicken and soy sauce to skillet. Continue to stir fry about 3-5 minutes and remove from heat.; Cover and allow to set for 5 minutes.; Serve!

Submitter: 
AmyDMB
Description: 
This is a quick and easy home made version of Chinese Restaurants' Fried Rice, I assume you could switch the meats around or even leave out the meat for vegetable fried rice.
