Title:
Perfect Hardboiled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Water
Eggs, up to 6 at a time

Directions:
Rinse eggs under room temperature water and let them sit in the bowl covered in warm-ish water.
Fill a pot with water, and turn burner up to heat water to boiliing.
When water is boiling transfer up to 6 eggs into the boiling water, set timer for 5-8 minutes.  Dispose of warm-ish water.
When timer goes off, remove pot from heat and pour out boiling water.
Fill pot with enough clean lukewarm water to cover the eggs.
Crack shells, but do not remove shells from the eggs.  Allow cracked eggs to stand in water for 10 minutes.
Peel and eat immediately!
