Title:
Carol's veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (or more) heads of caulflower
1 (or more) whole piece of broccoli
1 can Alfredo sauce
1 can Campbell's Cheddar Cheese Soup (or Kraft Garlic Cheese Roll)
onions (add or lessen)
bread crumbs(optional)

Directions:
Clean veggies. Steam them (I steam mine in the microwave). Cut if you want to when they're done. Saute the onions till clear. Add Alfredo sauce slowly. Stir. Add cheese. Stir. Pour Alredo sauce, chesse, and onions on top of the veggies in a dish. Add bread crumbs to top if desired. Bake till golden.

Submitter: 
CalenRodgers
Description: 
Broccoli and califlower in a new sauce that will knock your socks off!
