Title:
Pam's Fresh Cranberry Fruit Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Naval Orange - peeled
1 - 8 oz. can Pineapple Tidbits in Pineapple Juice (do not drain)
12 oz. package of fresh Cranberry
1 Granny Smith Apple
1/2 c. sugar

Directions:
Cut orange and apple in chunks. Process all fruit in food processor by pulsing till chunky. Turn into bowl and stir in sugar. Chill overnight for best results.

Submitter: 
Linda(LMT)
Description: 
My sister Pam's tart and sweet fresh fruit relish ideal for the Holidays. Nice change of pace from traditional cranberry sauce.
