Title:
Chicken and Dill Souvlaki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Rotisserie Chicken
Pita Bread or Flat Bread
Cucumber
Tomato
Red Onion
Olives
3 tablespoons  extra-virgin olive oil
3 tablespoons  fresh lemon juice
2 tablespoons  chopped fresh dill
1/2 cup tahini instead of yogurt

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the oil, lemon juice, 3/4 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper. Fold in the chicken, cucumbers, tomato, onion, olives and dill.
Divide the chicken mixture evenly among the flat breads and serve with the Tahini.
