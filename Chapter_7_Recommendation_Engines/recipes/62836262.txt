Title:
Chicken and cheese pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
1 bag Italian blend shredded cheese
2 tbsp butter or margerine
Two chicken breasts, cut into one inch cubes
1 tsp seasoned salt
1 tsp sage
2 tsp garlic flakes
1 1/2 cup penne noodles

Directions:
Heat a large frying pan over medium heat and coat with a little olive oil. sautee chicken until the outside is completely white and starts to brown, then add spices.
Let that simmer while combining cream and butter in a sauce pan over low heat until butter melts. Add the cheese and stir until cheese melts, then set aside.
Cook the noodles for 10-12 minutes, until they break easily with a spoon. Combine the chicken and pasta and cover with cheese cream sauce and serve immediately. Keeps well overnight!

Submitter: 
Steffamie
Description: 
This is the BEST pasta dish! It's a thick, creamy cheese sauce over penne pasta with savory sauteed chicken. Definitely a dinner to impress! I serve it with sauteed asparagus. Perfect dinner for two.
