Title:
Supercalifragilisticexpealidoscious Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 8oz cans tomato sauce
1 tsp sugar
3/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp garlic salt
1/4 tsp pepper
4 cups uncooked medium noodles
1 cup cottage cheese
1 8oz pkg cream cheese
1/4 cup sour cream
1/3 c sliced green onion
1/4 cup chopped green pepper
1/4 cup shredded parmesan

Directions:
Cook noodles. Cook ground beef, onion together and add tomato sauce, sugar, salts, & pepper. Combine cottage cheese, cream cheese, sour cream, green onion, and green pepper. Put half the noodles down, top with meat sauce, cover with cheese mixture & add remaining noodles & meat sauce (basically layer it...however you want to). Sprinkle parmesan on top, cover with tin foil and cook 30 minutes at 350.

Description: 
This is an old recipe of my mom's, who concocted it one day. When it instantly became a family favorite, she discovered it didn't have a name. So this is what we have always called it.
