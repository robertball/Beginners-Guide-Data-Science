Title:
Carrot Puff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1132232.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lbs Carrots - pelled cut into 1" pieces
1/2 C Butter - Melted
3 Eggs
2/3 C Sugar
3 TBLS Flour
1 tsp Baking Powder
1/2 tsp Vanilla

Directions:
Grease 8" baking dish
Boil Carrots until done (20 Min).
Puree carrots and add remaining ingredients.
Bake at 350 for 45 Min.
