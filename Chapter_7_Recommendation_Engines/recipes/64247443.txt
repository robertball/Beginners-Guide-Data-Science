Title:
Sauteed Salmon Fillets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5774196.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp butter, melted
1 Tbsp soy sauce
Garlic powder, dash
Lemon juice, to taste
1 Tbsp oil
1 lb salmon fillets

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine butter, soy sauce, garlic powder and lemon juice.
Heat oil in large skillet and add salmon fillets.  Baste fillets with sauce.  Cook 5 minutes, covered.
Turn fillets and baste with remaining sauce.  Cook an additional 5-10 minutes or until done.
