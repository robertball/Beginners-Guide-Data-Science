Title:
Cabbage, Potatoes, Carrots And Salt Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/708359.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb of salt pork
1 head of cabbage, quartered
6 whole potatoes
6 carrots, sliced
8 cups of hot water

Directions:
Set slow cooker on high
Add hot water to slow cooker
Add salt pork
Add cabbage
after 3 hrs add potatoes
after 4 hrs add carrots
at 6 hrs the cabbage cores should be tender and ready to serve.
Serve With Butter, Maybe a dash of pepper.

Submitter: 
Mark Farmer
Description: 
I never had salt pork before, and I saw it at the supper market. It is salty so I figured that using it with cabbage, potatoes, and carrots would be a good fit. Normally I make this dish with smoked ham, and ham bone, but it was good with salt pork also.
