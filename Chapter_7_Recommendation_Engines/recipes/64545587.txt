Title:
Delicious Ham and Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 pounds potatoes, about, peeled and sliced or diced
1 bunch celery, chopped
2 onions, chopped
2 ham steaks, diced into chunks
Water to cover
8 tbsp chicken bouillon granules
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
20 tbsp butter, real
20 tbsp flour
8 cups milk

Directions:
1) add potatoes, celery, onions, ham, and enough water to cover in stock pot. Bring to boil. Reduce to medium until potatoes are tender. About 15 minutes.
2) stir in bouillon, salt, and pepper.
3) In another pan, melt butter. Whisk in flour. Stir continuously until thick.
4) slowly stir milk into butter mixture a little at a time. Do not let it lump. Continue to stir until it thickens.
5) stir the milk mixture into stock pot and cook until heated through. Serve
