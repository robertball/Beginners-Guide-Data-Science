Title:
Pumpkin Spice Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1060029.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
2 tsp. Dry ginger
1/2 C. Granulated sugar
1/2 C. Brown sugar
1/2 C. Butter
1 C. Pumpkin flax granola
1 C. Quick oats
1 C. Pumpkin puree
1 C. Medium shredded coconut
2 Eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Line a large cookie sheet with parchment paper
Blend flour, b. powder, b. soda and ginger in a small bowl and set aside.
Mix butter and sugars together in a large bowl and form a smooth paste
Add eggs to butter/sugar mixture and beat with an electric mixer on medium speed until light and creamy
Combine pumpkin flax granola, oats, coconut and pumpkin puree and beat on medium speed mixing all ingredients together.
Stir in dry ingredients and blend them all together until thoroughly combined
Drop by teaspoon on cookie sheet 2 inches apart on parchment paper
Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes until lightly browned
Remove from oven and place on wire rack to cool completely before storing in container

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A cookie with chewy coconut and pumpkin with a hint of ginger to make a good snack time treat.
