Title:
Mascarpone Pistachio Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18, 1/4 inch thick slices of a french baguette
1/2 cup of mascarpone cheese, room temperature or softened
2 oz coarsely chopped pistachio nuts
2 tbs. honey
2 tbs. fresh basil, thinly sliced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
Spread cheese on bread slices and toast in oven for 10 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
Sprinkle basil on toasts and drizzle with honey.  Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Scarlett
Description: 
Honey adds sweetness to the nutty cheese.  Easily doubled and pocket book friendly, this quick and surprising appetizer is always a hit.
