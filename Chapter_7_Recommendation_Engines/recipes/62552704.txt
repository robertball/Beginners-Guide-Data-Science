Title:
Baileys Frosting! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups powdered sugar
2 sticks butter (softened)
1 tsp pure vanilla extract
1-2 tablespoons Baileys Irish Cream Liquor

Directions:
Mix everything together in a bowl until smooth an creamy.  Start with 1 tablespoon of Bailey's and it is too stiff gradually add more until you reach your desired consistency.

Submitter: 
Dreamthief
Description: 
Cake is just an excuse to eat frosting.  This frosting will make you want to skip the cake altogether.
