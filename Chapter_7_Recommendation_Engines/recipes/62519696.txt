Title:
Garlic Roasted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Whole Chicken
4 Garlic Bulbs, large
1 stick Butter, salted
1T Granulated Garlic

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Place rinsed chicken in roasting pan, disguarding anything in the cavity. Cut tops off of garlic and place inside cavity. Melt butter and pour evenly over top of chicken. Sprinkle granulated garlic. Place in oven and bake 20 minutes per pound or until juices run clear.
