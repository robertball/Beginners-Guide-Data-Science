Title:
Momma's Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/502398.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. hamburger
1 packet taco seasoning
1 packet dry ranch dressing seasoning
2 cans corn
2 cans black beans
28 oz. can crushed tomatoes
1 can Ro-Tel diced tomatoes with lime juice & cilantro

Directions:
Brown hamburger in large skillet adding the taco and ranch seasoning.  Drain off grease.  Combine all ingredients in a stock pot and let simmer for up to an hour.  Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
US92
Description: 
Dave's mom was in the studio this morning to make us our her yummy taco soup!  Similar to chili with a taco flavor.
