Title:
Sugar Cookies - from Nicole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg yolk, beaten
3 tbsp milk
Icing:
1/2 cup butter
2 cup icing sugar
2 tsp vanilla
2 tbsp milk
1 cup icing sugar

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients.
Mix butter and sugar in seperate bowl.
Gradually add egg yolk. Mix well.
Mix vanilla and milk
Alternate adding milk and flour mixtures to butter, end with flour.
Chill for at least 1 hour.
Bake at 375 for 5-8 minutes.
Icing:  Cream 1/2 cup butter with 2 cup icing sugar. Beat in 2 tsp vanilla, 2 tbsp milk and 1 cup icing sugar.
