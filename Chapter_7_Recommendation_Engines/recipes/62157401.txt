Title:
Shoo-Fly Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. ground cinnamon
1/8 tsp. ground cloves
1/8 tsp. ground ginger
1/8 tsp. ground nutmeg
1/4 cup margarine
1 cup boiling water
1/2 cup dark molasses
1 1/2 tsp. baking soda
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 9 in pie shell

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
In a large bowl combine the flour, brown sugar, salt, and all spices.  Cut in the margarine until the mixture resembles coarse cornmeal.
In a seperate bowl combine the water, molasses, and baking soda.  Blend in the egg.
Starting and ending with the crumb mixture arrange alternate layers of the two mixtures in the pie shell.
Bake at 450 degrees for 10 minutes, then reduce the temperature to 350 degrees and bake for 15 minutes longer or until firm.
