Title:
Ridiculously good Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1lb. ground meat
1 package taco seasoning
1 can black olives sliced
1 can ranch style beans drained and rinsed
2 c. shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup shredded carrots
1 head chopped lettuce
1 tomato chopped
1 small bunch green onions sliced
1 bag nacho cheese Doritos crushed
1 bottle catalina salad dressing

Directions:
Brown hamburger and add taco seasoning, let cool.
Add all other ingredients together, including crushed Doritos.
Dress with catalina salad dressing when ready to eat.
Garnish with avocado.

Submitter: 
TIFFANYIVANOVSKY
Description: 
Fresh and addictive, this summer salad is a family favorite. Always a hit at pot lucks none will be left and you will be the star!
