Title:
Grilled Cod with Spinach and Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2256562.jpg
Ingredients:
1 piece of cod (4 oz)
1/4 tomato, diced & seeded
1 Tbsp onion
Handful of  spinach, rough chopped
Salt, pepper, garlic powder
Olive oil
balsamic vinegar
1 slice of mozzarella, cubed

Directions:
Preheat outside grill.
Place cod on sheet of tin foil season with salt, pepper & garlic powder to taste. Top cod with tomato, onion and spinach. Season again with salt and pepper. Drizzle with olive oil and a dash of balsamic vinegar. Top with cubed mozzarella. Fold foil package up and close the top and sides.
Grill for about 7-9 minutes on medium high heat.
