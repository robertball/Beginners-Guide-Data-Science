Title:
Chicken with Wild Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 chicken breasts
3 boxes Uncle Bens Long Grain & Wild rice original recipe
8 oz canned mushrooms (or 16 oz if using fresh just saute them down)
3 cans cream of mushroom soup
1-2 cups rhine wine
1 Small jar of diced pimiento
3 tb butter

Directions:
Cook rice per box directions but only use 2 cups of water per box.
While rice is cooking brown chicken in butter.
When chicken is browned remove from pan but reserve drippings.
Add mushrooms, soup, wine and pimiento and make a smooth sauce.
Put rice in a 13x9 dish and arrange chicken on top and pour sauce over all.  Cover with foil and bake at 350 for 25 min then remove foil and bake for another 20-25 min.
