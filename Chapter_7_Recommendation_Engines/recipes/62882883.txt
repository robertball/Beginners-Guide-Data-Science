Title:
Broccoli Raisin Salad (Tree Salad) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz. broccoli (frozen thawed or fresh)
¼ c. red onion, diced
1 stalk celery, sliced
? c. raisins
¼ c. sunflower nuts
¼ c. cooked and chopped bacon
¾ c. mayonnaise
2 T. vinegar
¼ c. sugar

Directions:
Combine broccoli, onion, celery, and raisins together in bowl.  Combine mayonnaise, vinegar and sugar together.  Pour over vegetables.  Stir in sunflower nuts and bacon right before serving.

Submitter: 
KramerGirls
Description: 
Grace Sprecker made this salad and demanded I try it. It really didn't sound good to me but I LOVED it.  This salad got it's nickname from Zak.  We sometimes use fresh broccoli and the pieces look like little trees.
