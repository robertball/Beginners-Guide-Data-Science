Title:
Southern Hashbrown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c melted butter
1/2 c melted butter
2 lbs. frozen hashbrowns
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 pt. sour cream
2 c grated cheddar cheese
2 c crushed cornflakes
1/2 c chopped onion
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Defrost Hashbrowns.
Preheat oven to 350.
In a large bowl, combine hashbrowns, 1/2 c melted butter, soup, sour cream, onion, cheese, salt and pepper.
Move mixture to a 3 qu casserole dish.
Saute cornflakes in 1/4 c butter and sprinkle on top of casserole.
Cover and Bake for 40 minutes at 350 degrees.
