Title:
Best Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1832374.jpg
Ingredients:
2 bag green beans
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
8 oz cream cheese
2 (6 ounce) can French fried onions
2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Mix green beans, soup, 1 cup shredded cheese, 1 can french fried onions, and softened cream cheese together in bowl. Transfer green bean mixture to a casserole dish and sprinkle with remaining French fried onions cheese.
Bake in a preheated 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) oven until the cheese melts and the onions just begin to brown.

Submitter: 
Amethyst Douglas
Description: 
This great variation of the traditional green bean casserole is topped with French fried onions and Cheddar cheese.Modified from: Best Green Bean Casserole
