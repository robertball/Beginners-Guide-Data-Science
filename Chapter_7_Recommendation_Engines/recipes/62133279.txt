Title:
Feather Light Corn Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 TBSP Butter
1/2 Cup Flour
4 TBSP Yellow Cornmeal
2 TBSP Sugar
1 tsp baking powder
pinch of salt
1 Egg
2 TBSP Milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
Melt butter over low heat, set aside to cool.
Sift dry ingredients together into a bowl.
Beat egg and milk together in another bowl.
Stir butter and egg mitures into flour.
Fill paper baking cups in muffin pan half full of batter.
Bake at 350 for 15 minutes.
