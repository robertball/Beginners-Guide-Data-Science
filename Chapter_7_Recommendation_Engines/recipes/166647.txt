Title:
Cuervo Platino Margarita Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982071.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Jose Cuervo Platino® Tequila
1 1/2 ounces lime juice
1 ounce simple syrup
Salt (optional)

Directions:
Mix ingredients in cocktail shaker with ice. Serve in a chilled martini glass straight up. Garnish with a lime wedge. Salt is optional.

Submitter: 
Cuervo®
Description: 
When creating the archetypical tequila drink, get authentic flavor with Jose Cuervo Platino® tequila.
