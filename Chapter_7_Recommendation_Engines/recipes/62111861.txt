Title:
Creamy Chile Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup green chiles, diced
1/2 sour cream
1 Tb. green onion, chopped
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup cooked rice (preferably white rice)
1/2 cup Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
1 ripe tomato, sliced
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In small bowl, combine chiles, sour cream, green onion and salt. Place 1/2 cup rice in a lightly-greased 8-9" pie pan or round dish. Spoon half of the sour cream mixture over rice. Sprinkle with half of the Monterey Jack cheese. Repeat these layers and top with sliced tomato and Parmesan cheese. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until cheese melts and it begins to bubble.

Description: 
A nice mild Mexican side dish that goes well with spicy entrees.
