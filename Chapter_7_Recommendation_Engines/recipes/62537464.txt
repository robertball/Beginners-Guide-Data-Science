Title:
Sweet Potato Casserole II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 cups cooked and mashed sweet potatoes
or 2-29 oz. cans.
1/2 cup butter, melted
1/3 cup evaporated milk
1/2 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
l t. cinnamon
1/2 t. nutmeg
1/2 t. salt
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup light brown sugar
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/3 cup butter
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In a large bowl, mix together mashed sweet potatoes, 1/2 cup butter, milk, sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt and eggs. Spread sweet potato mixture into the prepared baking dish. In a small bowl, mix together brown sugar and flour. Cut in 1/3 cup butter until mixture is crumbly, then stir in pecans. Sprinkle pecan mixture over the sweet potatoes.
Bake for 25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Dorothy Clark
Description: 
Fluffy sweet potatoes mixed with butter, sugar, and vanilla, and baked with a crunchy pecan streusel topping.Modified from: Sweet Potato Casserole Dessert
