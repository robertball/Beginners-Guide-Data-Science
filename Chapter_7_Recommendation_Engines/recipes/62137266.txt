Title:
Russian Quaalude Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 shot Hazelnut liqueur
1/2 shot White Creme de Caco
1 shot Vodka
Milk to color (about half shot)
Ice

Directions:
In a shaker, put your ice, and all ingredients. Shake till it is nice and cold and a bit frothy.
Strain (without ice) into a martini or rocks glass.
Enjoy! But watch out, these drinks have a kick after a couple lol

Submitter: 
Angela Chase Hobbs
Description: 
If you like the taste of white chocolate, you will love this addicting cocktail!!!
