Title:
Creamette Baked Ziti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Ziti Noodles
2 Tablespoons margarine or butter
3 Tablespoons Flour
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1/2 teaspoon Salt
2-3/4 cups milk
2 cups (8 ounces) shredded cheese
1 pound ground beef (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Cook pasta according to directions; drain.
In a medium saucepan, melt butter; stir in flour, mustard and salt.
Gradually stir in milk; cook and stir until mixture thickens slightly and bubbles.
Remove from heat.
Add 1 cup cheese; stir until melted.
Add Pasta and beef. (beef optional
Spoon into greased 8x11 inch baking dish.
Top with remaining 1 cup of cheese.
Bake uncovered for 25 minutes or until lightly browned and bubbly.
Let stand 10 minutes.
