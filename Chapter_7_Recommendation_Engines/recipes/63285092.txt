Title:
Pork Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 thick cut Butterfly pork chops, cut into bite size chunks
1 zucchini, chopped
1 yellow squash, chopped
1 can pineapple chunks, drained
1 bottle lawry's hawaiian marinade or Teriyaki

Directions:
Put the pork, squash and pineapple chunks onto the barbecue skewers.
Preheat grill; when about 350-400 degrees, place skewers on grill and baste with any remaining marinade. Cook about 7 minutes and flip skewers. Baste again and cook another 7 minutes.
