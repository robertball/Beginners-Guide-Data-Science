Title:
Easy Garlic Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick Butter
2 Tbsp. minced Garlic
2 Tbsp. Ms. Dash or favorite spices

Directions:
Soften 1 stick of butter, to a whipped consistency. Do not melt the butter. Lightly stir in minced garlic and seasoning with fork or spoon. Place on bread and bake to toast in oven. Place unused butter in an airtight container and store in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
MIDNIGHT_ANGEL
Description: 
Easy spread to place on any Italian or French bread. Can be placed in refrigerator to used as needed with just about anything.
