Title:
Chinatown Chicken Simmer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Minute white rice uncooked
1 can (20 oz) pineapple chunks
1 TBL cornstarch
1 TBL vegetable oil
6 to 8 boneless, skinless chicken thighs. 12" sliced
2 large garlic cloves, minced
1 med. red bell pepper, large diced
1 med. yellow onion, large diced
1 TBL fresh ginger, minced
1 1/2 cups snow peas, halved
1/2 cup chicken broth
2 TBL soy sauce
1 TBL hoisin sauce
1/4 ground cinnamon
2 TBL honey
salt to taste

Directions:
Prepare rice according to package directions, set aside. Drain pineapple; reserve juice. Mix 1/4 cup juice with cornstarch; set aside. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat; add oil and chicken; brown for 5 minutes. Add garlic, red bell pepper, onion and ginger; saute 3 minutes. Add snow peas and remaining pineapple juice, chicken broth, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, cinnamon and honey. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, or until the internal temperature of he chicken reaches 165 degrees. Add pineapple and cornstarch mixture, cook until the sauce thickens. Serve over rice, salt to taste.
