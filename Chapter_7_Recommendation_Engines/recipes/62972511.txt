Title:
Apple-Oatmeal Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 medium tart cooking apples, cored, peeled and thinly sliced
6 T light brown sugar
6 T white sugar
1/2 c flour
3/4 c uncooked oatmeal
1/2 c butter melted
1/2 c water
2 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Spread the sliced apples in bottom of a 8x8 baking dish
In a mixing bowl combine sugars, flour and oats.  Stir in melted butter.  Spread over apple mixture.
Pour water evenly over the top then sprinkle cinnamon on top.  Bake, uncovered until apples are tender, about 30 minutes.
