Title:
Slow Cooker Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.25 lbs ground turkey OR 1 lb ground 90/10 or leaner hamburger
2 medium onions, chopped
2 Anaheim chile peppers, chopped, OR 1 bell pepper, chopped
2 (16 oz.) cans kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 (16 oz.) can plain or Mexican-style tomato sauce OR 6 oz. tomato paste + 1/2 c. water
2 TB garlic, minced
1/4 cup chili powder
1/4 cup cornmeal
1/2 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
1 TB dried onion flakes
1 tsp. sugar (any kind)
1 TB cocoa, unsweetened, or 1/2 oz. Baker's chocolate, unsweetened
1 TB cumin
1 tsp. dried parsley
1 tsp. Mexican oregano (or regular)
1/2 tsp. ground coriander
1 tsp. black pepper
1 tsp. beef soup base
water (amount varies based on personal preference)

Directions:
1. Fry onions, peppers, and ground meat. Drain and transfer to a slow cooker.
2. Add all remaining ingredients to slow cooker and stir. You may find you wish to add water at this stage or you may wish to wait based on thickness and preference.
3. Cook on HI for 4 hours or on LOW for 8-10 hours.

Submitter: 
JARRIE
Description: 
Modified from various recipes for our tastes - sized to fit my 3 quart slow cooker. Substitute 1 packet of store-bought seasoning mix for the dry seasonings, if desired.
