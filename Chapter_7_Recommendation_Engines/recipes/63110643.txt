Title:
Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886872.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (7.5 ounce) packages refrigerated buttermilk biscuits
1 cup brown sugar, packed
1 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted
1/2 cup pecans, chopped
1/2 cup maple syrup

Directions:
Cut each biscuit into quarters.
In a ziplock bag, combine brown sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg.
Dip biscuits in butter, then shake in sugar and spice mixture.
Layer half the biscuits in a 10-in. fluted pan; sprinkle with half the nuts. Repeat layers.
Pour syrup over top.
Bake at 350ยบ for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.
Immediately invert onto a serving platter. Serve warm. Refrigerate leftovers.
