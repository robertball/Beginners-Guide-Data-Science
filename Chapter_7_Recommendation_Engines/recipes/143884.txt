Title:
Fruit Mix Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/404014.jpg
Ingredients:
2 oranges, peeled and cut into bite size pieces
1 cup seedless red grapes
1/2 cup pitted and halved Bing cherries
1/4 cup golden raisins
1/4 cup chopped pitted dates
1/4 cup walnut halves

Directions:
Combine the orange pieces, grapes, cherries, raisins, dates, and walnuts in a bowl; toss to combine.

Submitter: 
Heidi Habeck-Johnson
Description: 
This is a light summer fruit recipe.
