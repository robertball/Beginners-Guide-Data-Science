Title:
Plate of Plenty Salad Plate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups chopped romaine or mixed greens
1 small ripe tomato, diced, or 1/2 cup grape tomatoes
Preferred salad vegetables such as onions, carrots, celery, radishes
Favorite salad dressing
2 cups prepared chicken, tuna, or ham salad
2 cups favorite pasta, potato or grain salad
2 cups 3 bean salad, broccoli salad, or cucumber, tomato & onion salad
2 cups favorite fruit salad, gelatin salad, or sliced/quartered fresh fruit tossed with 2 tbsp poppyseed dressing
2 hard boiled eggs, cut in quarters & sprinkled with a little paprika, or 4 stuffed egg halves
2 dill pickle spears or 1/2 cup bread & butter pickles, salad peppers or olives
Wedges of havarti, cheddar or favorite cheese
Wedges of sourdough bread, buttered & toasted, or crackers

Directions:
Scatter 1 cup of chopped mixed greens on each plate.
Mound remaining greens on one part of each plate to make a green salad. Add tomato, preferred salad vegetables & your favorite dressing.
Add scoops of 1, 2, 3 other salads around plate as you wish.
Add eggs, pickles, peppers or olives, cheese wedges & toast or crackers.

Submitter: 
HappyGrandma
Description: 
Not really a recipe but a road map to make a great salad plate. I always have a vegetable salad or two in the fridge & salad greens, vegetables, fruit & cheese. Can make as lean or as plentiful as you like. Measurements are approximate.
