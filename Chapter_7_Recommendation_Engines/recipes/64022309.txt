Title:
Pork Roast with Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cloves of garlic - minced
1 tsp. salt
1 bone-in pork roast - about 6 lbs.
4 lbs. potatoes
1 large onion - chopped
3 tbsp. butter - melted
2 tsp. seasoned salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
2 tbsp. fresh parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Mix 1 clove of the garlic with salt and rub over pork roast.
With fat side up, place in roasting pan and cook 30 minutes.
Meanwhile, thinly slice potatoes and toss with remaining garlic, onion, butter, seasoned salt and pepper.
Spoon potatoes around meat after it has roasted for 30 minutes.
Roast for another hour and 15 minutes or until meat is done.
Sprinkle with parsley.
