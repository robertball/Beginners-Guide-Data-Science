Title:
Baked potato with beans and cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 potatoes
8 oz of salsa
1 package of 1/3 less fat cream cheese
1 can of baked beans
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1.Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2.Bake potatoes in preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until cooked through or microwave them if in a hurry.
3.Combine the rest of the ingredients in a sauce pan on medium until the cheese is melted and smooth.
4.Pour the bean mixture over the baked potato.

Submitter: 
Toni Losada
Description: 
This is a filling that my friends and I came up with in college.  It's cheap, easy and delicious. Baked potato with cream cheese, salsa and baked beans...you'll be surprised.  It is a rich meal and great for vegetarians.
