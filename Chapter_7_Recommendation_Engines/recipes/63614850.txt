Title:
BLOTCHE - AKA Widow maker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices thin cut bacon, each cut in 1 inch pieces
2 slices rustic country bread
4 teaspoons softened butter
2 green onions, trimmed, white & light green part, finely minced
3 ounces deli Havarti, thin sliced, divided
3 ounces sliced deli ham
4 thin slices ripe tomato
4 inner Romaine leaves, ribs removed
2 eggs

Directions:
Cook bacon until just crisp. Remove & set aside.
Brush 1 side each of 2 slices of bread with bacon drippings & 1 tsp each softened butter. Wipe skillet clean with paper towels.
Place 1 slice of bread buttered side down on a plate.
Sprinkle with half the minced onion then top with half the havarti then all the ham, remaining Havarti, remaining minced green onion then remaining slice of bread, buttered side up.
On a griddle or skillet, over low heat, grill each side of sandwich until golden brown & cheese is soft. Remove to plate & keep warm.
Add remaining 2 tbsp butter to griddle or skillet. Crack 2 eggs & cook to set but runny (over easy).
Pull grilled sandwich apart between the slices of ham. Add thin sliced tomato, lettuce leaves, bacon pieces & the 2 eggs. Replace top of sandwich.
Cut sandwich in half between the 2 eggs & serve.
