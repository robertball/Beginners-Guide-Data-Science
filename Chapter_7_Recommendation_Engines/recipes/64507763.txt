Title:
Caramel Apple Peanut Butter Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium-ish apples, 1/2 cup (or to taste) peanut butter, 3-4 Tbsp (or to taste) Caramel Sauce (this can be bought or home made, as desired : http://allrecipes.com/recipe/173621/dessertlovers-classic-caramel-sauce/ (Home made, obviously, will affect prep time), Aprox 2 Cups Apple Cider, 1-2 cups of ice.

Directions:
Put all ingredients in Blender, Blend to desired consistency, enjoy.

Submitter: 
Gingergirl
Description: 
Blend of apples, peanut butter, caramel, apple cider, and ice. Very tasty and refreshing as well.
