Title:
Avacado Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lg ripe avacado
1/2 c light mayo
1 T fresh lemon juice
1/4 t tobasco sauce
1 clove garlic, minced
1 green oiion, finely chopped
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mash avacado until very smooth. With a fork, beat in mayo, lemon juice and tobasco. Stir in garlic and green onion. Add salt and pepper to taste.
