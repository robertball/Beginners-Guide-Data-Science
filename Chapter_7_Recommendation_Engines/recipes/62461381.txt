Title:
Posh Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/496129.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds winter squash, peeled, seeded and cut into chunks
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 small onion
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1 Tbl butter

Directions:
Butter and flour one 8x12 inch baking pan. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a saucepan sautee the onions and squash, add salt and sugar to taste.
In a mixing bowl, combine squash, eggs, mayonnaise, sour cream, onion, and cheese. Place into baking pans, mix bread crumbs with a 1 Tablespoon butter and sprinkle over squash mixture bake for 30 minutes.

Description: 
Boiled squash is combined with eggs, Parmesan cheese, mayonnaise, onion and green pepper and baked with a bread crumb topping in this casserole serving six.Modified from: Posh Squash
