Title:
Tuna Veggie Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups brown rice
1/3 cup Balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup yogurt
8 oz miso mayo
1/2 onion, chopped
half bundle small asaparagus, chopped
4 stalks celery, chopped
2 cups kale, chopped
1 red or orange pepper, chopped
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained

Directions:
Cook brown rice according to instructions in chicken broth until done. Cook veggies in butter, don't overcook.
Mix yogurt, miso mayonnaise, balsamic vinegar, garlic powder, tuna, and salt and pepper Add to rice and veggie mixture. Eat warm for dinner. Chill over night for cold salad lunch.

Submitter: 
Denise Lee Gunn
Description: 
Can wrap in soft tortilla or leave out brown rice and use bread to make sandwich.Modified from: Tuna Macaroni Salad
