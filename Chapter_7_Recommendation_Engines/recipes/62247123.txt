Title:
Green Bean Artichoke Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 cups hericot verts
2 (14 ounce) cans artichoke hearts, drained and halved
2 cups panko
1/2 Tablespoon Italian seasoning
8 ounces grated Parmesan cheese
8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
2 tablespoons garlic powder
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup olive oil

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
In mixing bowl,combine green beans, artichoke hearts, bread crumbs,italian seasoning, Parmesan cheese, and mozzarella cheese pans. Season with garlic powder, salt and pepper. Stir to blend everything thoroughly.Divide between two regular sized pans. Drizzle olive oil over the top, then cover the dish with aluminum foil.
Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, then remove the aluminum foil, and bake for another 15 minutes to brown the top slightly.
