Title:
Broccoli and Three-Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 onion, grated
2 cups milk (2% or less)
4 cups chicken broth
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
2 (10 ounce) bags frozen broccoli
1 cup each of three different shredded cheese (e.g. white cheddar, Jarlsberg, Gruyere)

Directions:
Add onion, milk, broth, salt, pepper, nutmeg, and frozen broccoli to a 6 quart slow cooker.
Cook on low 4-6 hours.
Stir in shredded cheese and let melt.
Blend soup with immersion blender.

Submitter: 
Kristen M
Description: 
I never really liked broccoli until I tried this soup. It doesn't hurt that it contains lots of cheese! Perfect for fall and winter.
