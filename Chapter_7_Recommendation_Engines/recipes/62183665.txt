Title:
Healthy Peanut Butter Banana Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
1 cup water
1 TBS honey
1 tsp vanilla
1 TBS ground flaxseed
1 over-ripe banana, mashed
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 TBS natural peanut butter (creamy)

Directions:
Bring 1 cup of water to a boil, add oats and bring to a boil again. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until oats have thickened (about 5 minutes).
Add honey, vanilla, flaxseed, cinnamon and mashed banana. Stir until all ingredients are blended. Remove from heat.
Add peanut butter, stir and enjoy!

Submitter: 
SavedByGrace
Description: 
This is a great way to start your morning with whole grains, protein, fruit and fiber. Old-fashioned oats are cooked and then mixed with mashed bananas (ripe!), ground flaxseed, cinnamon, honey, and vanilla then topped with your favorite peanut butter!
