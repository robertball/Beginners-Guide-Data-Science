Title:
3-Cheese Pasta Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 pkg. shredded two-cheese blend (about 2 cups)
1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 cup milk
¼ tsp. ground black pepper
3 cups rotini, cooked and drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F.
Stir the soup, cheese, milk and black pepper in a 1 ½ quart casserole dish. Stir in pasta.
Bake at 400F for 20 minutes or until mixture is hot and bubbling.
