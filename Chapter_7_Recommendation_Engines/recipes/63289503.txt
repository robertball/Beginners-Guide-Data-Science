Title:
Summer squash and tomato Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 - 9-10 inch yellow and green zucchini squash
3 T. butter
2 t. Italian seasoning
1/2 t. sea salt
1/2 black pepper
1 t. garlic powder
2 diced onions
1 32 oz can diced tomatoes in juice
4 oz. cream cheese

Directions:
Saute onions in butter until lucent
add cut up squash (1/2 inch pieces)
add salt, pepper, garlic powder,Italian seasoning and tomatoes.
Cook down juice by a half and then add cream cheese.
This is a low carb recipe but could be eaten over pasta if not watching carbs.

Submitter: 
Kim Nance
Description: 
Great filling recipe for fresh squash and tomatos with a creamy sauce
