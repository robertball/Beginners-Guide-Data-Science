Title:
Hot Fudge Pudding Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
3/4 cup sugar
2 Tbsp cocoa
2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 cup milk
2 Tbsp oil
1 cup brown sugar, packed
1/4 cup cocoa
1-3/4 cup hot water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Measure flour, sugar, 2 Tbsp cocoa, baking powder and salt into bowl.  Blend in milk and oil.
Pour into ungreased square pan 9x9x2 inches.  Stir together brown sugar and 1/4 cup cocoa; sprinkle over batter.  Pour HOT water over batter.
Bake 45 minutes.  Serve warm with whipped cream or ice cream.
