Title:
Thai Wild Mushroom Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbs Extra Virgin Olive Oil
3 Tbs minced fresh lemongrass
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbs minced ginger
1 medium yellow onion, diced
8 cups mixed mushrooms (crimini, button, portabella, chanterelle or oyster)
6 cups chicken stock
1 can thick coconut milk
zest of lime
1 Tbs curry paste (or more if you like it spicier)
1-3 tbs soy sauce or fish sauce
2 tbs lime juice
1/2 cup cilantro chopped

Directions:
Saute onion, garlic, lemongrass and mushrooms in the olive oil until mushrooms are soft and moisture is released. Ad stock, coconut milk and lime zest and bring to a slow boil. Reduce heat and add curry paste and lime juice. Add curry and soy sauce/fish sauce to your taste. Simmer for 20-25 minutes over low heat. Add cilantro as garnish when serving.
