Title:
Cheesy Sausage and Potato Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound turkey or pork breakfast sausage, cooked
1 sweet onion, chopped
7 eggs
1/2 cup milk
1 1/2 pounds potatoes, baked, then grated
8 oz brick cheddar cheese, grated
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Spray a medium casserole dish (or 13x9 pan) with non stick cooking spray. Set aside.
Prepare sausage and onions.
In a large mixing bowl, combine all ingredients, includine sausage and onions. Pour into prepared casserole dish. Reserve roughly one cup of the cheese for topping, and place cheese on top.
Bake for about 35 minutes, or until heated through and cheese is melted and bubbly.
Serve and enjoy!
