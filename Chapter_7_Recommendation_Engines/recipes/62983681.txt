Title:
Pancetta Roasted Baked Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871655.jpg
Ingredients:
4 large baking potatoes, cut into small pieces
1 large sweet potato, cut into small pieces
1 large red onion, peeled and cut into small pieces
2 cups fresh brussel sprouts, end trimmed and cut in half lengthwise
6 ounces pancetta (or bacon) cut into small pieces
olive oil
salt and pepper
chopped italian parsley for garnish

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Mix vegetables and pancetta toether in a roasting pan. Drizzle with olive oil, season to taste with salt and pepper and mix again. Roast for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are tender. Garnish with parsley and serve.
