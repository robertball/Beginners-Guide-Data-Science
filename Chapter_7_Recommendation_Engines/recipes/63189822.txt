Title:
Sugar Cookie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/822194.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted softened butter (room temp)
2 cups granulated sugar
4 large eggs
2 tsp vanilla extract
5 cups AP flour
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
*favorite frosting (approximately 3 cups)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Line 10x15 baking pan (or smaller for thicker.
In mixing bowl of stand mixer, with paddle attachment, cream butter, sugar on medium high speed until light and fluffy.
Add eggs one at at time, beating on medium speed, mixing well after each addition.
Add vanilla extract.
In a separate bowl, blend flour, salt and baking soda well.
Slowly add flour mixture to butter mixture on low speed, blending well after each addition.
Spread evenly cookie dough onto prepared baking pan.
Bake for 30-35 minutes or until tester inserted into center comes out clean.
Cool in pan completely.
Remove from pan and frost as desired.
Cut and serve!
Options: decorate with sprinkles, sanding sugars, etc.
