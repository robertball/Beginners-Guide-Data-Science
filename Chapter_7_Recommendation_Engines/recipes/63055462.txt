Title:
Baked Chicken with Sausage and Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/776385.jpg
Ingredients:
2 to 3 lbs of Chicken breasts and thighs (any combo will work and more/less to suit your needs)
3 to 5 Yukon gold potatoes cut into wedges
8 oz water (1/2 glass)
16 oz chicken broth
1/3c oil (olive)
1lb sweet Italian sausage, cut in half lengthwise (can use hot or mix sweet & hot)
1 Bay leaf
5 lg cloves garlic, smashed (skewer each with a toothpick so they are easily found after baking)
1Tbsp Vinegar (red or white wine vinegar)
1/2c Wine
Salt & pepper
1 tsp Oregano
1 tsp Rosemary
1large Onion, sliced
8 oz fresh sliced Mushrooms (or canned)
1/8 tsp Little bit of hot pepper flakes (or more to taste)
8 oz (or more) Roasted red peppers with juice
Frozen or canned peas (optional)

Directions:
Put all ingredients In rectangular baking dish.
Bake covered @ 350-375 degrees for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
AllieGeekPi
Description: 
Italian comfort food.  My mom taught me how to make this dish.  You can vary the amounts of ingredients to suit the quantity you need for your family.
