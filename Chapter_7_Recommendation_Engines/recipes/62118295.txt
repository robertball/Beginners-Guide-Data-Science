Title:
Corn Dogs On A Stick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. corn meal
1 C. flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. sugar
1 egg
1 C. milk
3 Tbsp. melted shortening, cooled
oil for deep frying.
1 pkg. hot dogs, dried with scewers inserted.
Note: you can use city chicken sticks from the meat department at your local grocery store.

Directions:
Combine the dry ingredients in a small bowl.
Beat the egg slightly with the milk.
Stir milk mixture into the dry ingredients.
Stir in melted shortening.
To fry:
Pour some of the batter in a tall glass. The glass must be taller than the hot dog on a stick.
Dip each hot dog in the batter to coat evenly.
Let excess drip back into the glass.
Fry in a deep fryer until golden brown.
Drain well on paper towels and keep warm in the oven until ready to serve.
Mustard and ketchup.
