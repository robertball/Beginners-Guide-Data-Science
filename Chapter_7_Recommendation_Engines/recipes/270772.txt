Title:
Keto Chocolate Mousse (Low Carb) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6440713.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups heavy whipping cream
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese (such as PhiladelphiaÂ®)
2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
4 teaspoons stevia powder
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Beat cream in a chilled glass or metal bowl using an electric mixer until soft peaks form.
Mix cream cheese, cocoa powder, stevia, and vanilla extract in a second bowl using an electric mixer. Fold 1 tablespoon of whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture. Fold in remaining whipped cream until well incorporated. Spoon chocolate mousse into individual serving bowls and refrigerate.
