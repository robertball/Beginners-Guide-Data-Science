Title:
Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8C water
2C white vinegar
8Tbsp pickling salt(2/3)
4tsp sugar
4-6 pieces of garlic
4-6 bay leaves
Few dill sprigs

Directions:
Wash cucmbers, put dill, garlic, bay leaves in bottom. Cut cucmbers in bucket, put dill, garlic and bay leave in again. Fill with liquid. Set out for 3Days then refrigerate.
I add some Alum about 1/8tsp and 1Tbsp mixed Pickling spice.
