Title:
Cauliflower "Shrimp" Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup dry crab boil seasoning (Old Bay)
1/4 cup kosher salt
2 yellow onions, quartered
6 garlic cloves
3 lemons, halved cross-wise
2 - 1 lb. heads of cauliflower, cored and cut into 2" florets
Cocktail sauce

Directions:
Combine Old Bay, salt, onions, garlic and 6 quarts of water in a large pot.
Squeeze juice from lemons into pot and add lemons to pot after squeezed.
Bring to a boil over high heat.
Cook for about 10 minutes, then remove onions, garlic and lemons from  pot.
Return liquid to a rolling boil.
Add cauliflower, remove from heat and cover.
Let stand for 5-10 minutes, or until cauliflower is crisp-tender.
Drain and spread on a baking sheet and cool completely.
Cauliflower can be cooked 1 day in advance and stored in refrigerator.
Serve with cocktail sauce for dipping.
