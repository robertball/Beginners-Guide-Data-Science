Title:
Chocolate Banana Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/59389.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups cold milk
4 tablespoons malted milk powder
1 large banana

Directions:
In a blender, combine milk, malt powder and banana. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.
