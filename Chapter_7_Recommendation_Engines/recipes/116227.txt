Title:
Tasty Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/136889.jpg
Ingredients:
1 broiler/fryer chicken (3 to 4 pounds), cut up
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon dried tarragon
1/4 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 cup dry sherry
3 tablespoons lemon juice
2 teaspoons soy sauce
2 shallots, chopped

Directions:
Place the chicken in a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish. Sprinkle with salt, tarragon, paprika and pepper. Combine the sherry, lemon juice and soy sauce; drizzle over chicken. Top with shallots.
Bake, uncovered, at 425 degrees F for 20 minutes. Reduce heat to 375 degrees F. Cover and bake for 30 minutes, basting occasionally. Uncover; bake 15-20 minutes longer or until a meat thermometer reads 180 degrees F.

Submitter: 
Betty Reuter
Description: 
Tarragon and paprika add a nice flavor to this chicken, which bakes up golden brown in the oven.   #151;Betty Reuter
