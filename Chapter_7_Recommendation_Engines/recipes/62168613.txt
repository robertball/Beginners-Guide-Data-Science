Title:
Unstuffed Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cabbage head, chopped
1 to 1 1/2 lbs ground beef
2 to 3 (14 oz.) cans diced tomatoes
your favorite seasonings and spices
hot cooked rice

Directions:
In a large pot boil chopped cabbage until tender.
Meanwhile brown and drain ground beef.  Add the tomatoes and seasonings to taste (I like to use Everglades seasonings and garlic salt) and let simmer while waiting on the cabbage.
Drain cabbage and add to the ground beef mixture, mixing well.
Serve over hot cooked rice.

Submitter: 
4pumpkins
Description: 
My grandmother used to make this all the time.  We loved it as kids and now my kids love it.  It is economic and simple.  Also, it's easy to season and spice to adapt to anyone's taste.
