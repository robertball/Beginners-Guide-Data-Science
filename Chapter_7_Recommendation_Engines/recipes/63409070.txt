Title:
Roast Beef cooked in Pasta Sauce The  Italian Way. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion diced
1carrot diced
1 stalk celery diced
2 Tbsp fresh rosemary
1/2 teaspoon oregano
2 Tbsp.basil
1/2 teaspoon Turmeric
pinch of sugar
3 cloves garlic chopped
2 cans diced tomatoes(28ounces each)I like the San Marzano canned tomatoes.
2 bay leaves.
salt and pepper,
1 can of tomatoe paste(5 ounces) or 156ml
Beef roast 4-5 pounds.
olive oil about 1/4 cup

Directions:
Put olive oil in a roaster,or oven proof dish,add onion, carrot, celery, garlic saute for 5 minutes,add your Turmeric,stir 1 minute.Add your herbs stir 1 minute. Add your tomatoes,(i put mine through the blender,as i like a smooth sauce.) Bring to a gentle boil,turn down heat add tomatoe paste.,let simmer for about 1/2 hour, uncovered, add the roast beef.Put in the oven covered at 325 for 2 and 1/2-3 hours.Uncover last 15 minutes.Your meat will be nice and tender and you will also have a nice pasta sauce.
