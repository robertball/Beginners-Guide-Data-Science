Title:
Paleo Power Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dry ingredients:
1 1/2 cups Almond flour
3/4 c ground flax seed
1/2 c Shredded coconut
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
2 tsp ground cinnamon
Liquid ingredients:
1 c Pumpkin Puree
3/4 c honey
2 eggs, slightly beaten
2 tbsp applesauce (replaces oil)
Fruit, veggies and other goodies:
2 cups shredded carrots
2 apples, peeled and shredded
1/2 cup raisins/craisins
1/2 cup other dried fruits as desired
1/2 c Dark Chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients together in a mixing bowl
Add liquid ingredients
Fold in shredded fruit, carrots, chocolate chips, berries
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes in a well greased muffin pan
