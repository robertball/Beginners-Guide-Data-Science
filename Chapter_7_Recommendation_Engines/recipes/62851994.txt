Title:
Turkey Tenderloin - Applewood Smoked (Jennie-O) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 24 oz Jennie-O Applwood Smoked Turkey Breast  Tenderloin

Directions:
Grilling- preheat frill over medium heat., place tenderloin directly on grate.  Grill, turning occssionally until meat thermometer inserted in thickest part of meat reaches 165F (approx 45-90 minutes) remove from heat, cover and let rest 5-10 minutes.
Baking - preheat oven to 350F place in shallow baking pan.  Bae until temperature in thickest part reaches 165 (approx 60-70 minutes)REmove from oven, cover and let rest 5-10 minutes

LostinSouthBama
Description: 
Low calorie, tasty and easy.  Purchase prepackaged can be cooked on grill or in oven.
