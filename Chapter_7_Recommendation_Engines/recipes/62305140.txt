Title:
Cuban-Style Pork and Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound pork cut into 1 inch squares
1.5 pounds sweet potato peeled and diced into 1/2 inch cubes
1/4 cup orange juice
15 oz diced tomatoes with chiles
2 cloves of garlic pressed
1/4 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1.5 Tblsp lime juice
cilantro

Directions:
Place pork, sweet potatoes, orange juice, tomatoes, garlic, cumin, salt, pepper in slow cooker.  Mix up.  Cover and cook on low for 6 hours.  Just before serving add lime juice and stir.  Dice cilantro and sprinkle over serving as wanted for taste and color.
