Title:
Turkey Sage Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 lb ground turkey
1 medium bosc pear, peeled and grated
1 1/2 tbsp chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
2 tbsp finely chopped fresh sage
3/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp ground allspice
1 large egg, lightly beaten (leave out if not forming patties)

Directions:
Stir together all ingredients in a large bowl until well combined.
Use in recipes calling for breakfast-style sausage or form into patties and pan-fry.
