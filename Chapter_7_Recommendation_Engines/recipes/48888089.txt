Title:
Jalapeno Pepper Appetizers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 jalapeno peppers
4 ounces cream cheese, softened
10 bacon strips, halved

Directions:
Cut peppers in half lengthwise; remove seeds, stems and center membrane. Stuff each half with about 2 teaspoons of cream cheese.  Precook the bacon until soft and then wrap around peppersand secure with toothpick.
Place on a broiler rack that has been coated with non-stick cooking spray. Bake at 350 degrees F for until bacon is crisp. Remove toothpicks. Serve immediately.
(bake at least 30 min)
