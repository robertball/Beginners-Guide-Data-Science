Title:
Potato Wedges Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large russet potatoes
? cup olive oil
1 teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon pepper
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon dried oregano
1 tablespoon paprika
½ cup vegetarian Parmesan cheese

Directions:
3 servings
Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
Thoroughly wash potatoes, cut in half, and slice into wedges.
Toss wedges in olive oil and seasonings.
Place on a baking sheet, skin side down.
Bake 40-50 minutes.

Submitter: 
Donna Gilbert
Description: 
If you soak wedges in super cold water for at least 20 minutes before seasoning- they will cook up extra crispy-use 1/3 c olive oil
