Title:
Ukrainian egg-plant hors d'oeuvres Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Celery stalks and leaves - 16 stalks
Egg-Plant - 4 Big
Garlic - 8 small cloves or 4 big cloves
Salt - 2 tablespoons
Pepper - to taste
Olive Oil(light) - 200 ml
Water - 2 liters
unopned bottle of water - 1 liter

Directions:
boil water with salt
cut off stem of eggplants
put eggplants in boiling water for 7 minutes
drain water from eggplants(egg-plants stay in pot)
put eggplants in a pot or another container side by side. They can't be on top of each other
cover eggplants with a plate(so all are covered)
Place a bottle of water which weighs 1 liter on top of plate for the "press." Press for 1 hour
Remove eggplants and cut each eggplant evenly longways so you have 8 long pieces of eggplant
Use garlic press or grater to crush the garlic
sprinkle garlic and pepper inside of each piece of eggplant
Place 2 stalks of celery with leaves(uncut) inside of each piece of eggplant
In a plastic or glass container with a lid place 4 pieces of eggplant facing up then put the other 4 on top of them facing down.  So you have four complete eggplants
cover all egg-plants with oil and seal container
Put container of eggplants in refrigerator for 8 hours
Carefully separate the 8 eggplant pieces, making sure you don't lose the celery from any piece.
Ready to serve!

Submitter: 
Ukrainian Chef
Description: 
Growing up in Ukraine I was able to learn about many different dishes.  This is one of my favorite snacks my mother prepared.  This is my first recipe to submit and I thought it would be wonderful to share my mother's brilliance.
