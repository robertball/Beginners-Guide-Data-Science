Title:
Caramelized Onions and Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/603722.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb of mini red potatoes, halved
1 small yellow onion, diced
1 tbsp of garlic powder
1/2 tbsp of paprika
1 tsp of salt
1/2 tsp of pepper
1 tbsp olive oil
1/4 cup of hoisin sauce
1/4 tsp of curry powder

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375F
Arrange potatoes in a casserole dish.
Add all the seasoning as well as the oil and hoisin. Mix all the ingredients together until the potatoes are well covered.
Cook uncovered for 1 hour, stirring once or twice while cooking.
Turn the oven off and cover and let it sit in the oven for 15-20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Raquel Teixeira
Description: 
A delicious roast potato dish that is full of flavour and the key to making these so soft and caramelized is letting them sit in the oven afterwards. Let it rest and you will be impressed!
