Title:
Apricot Crumble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups coarsely chopped apricots (fresh or canned)
3 tbs lemon juice
4 cups rolled oats
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup butter or magarine
2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp grounded cloves
1/4 tsp nutmeg
4 tbs water
1/2 cup whipping cream, whipped (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Toss the chopped apricots with the lemon juice and set aside.
Mix together the oats, sugar, butter or margarine, cinnamon, cloves and nutmeg untill crumbly.
Set 2 cups of the mixture aside. In the remaining mixture add the water and mix well. Pat this mixture into a greased 9*12*1 inch baking pan. Spread evenly with apricots and top with reserved crumble mixture.
Bake for 35 to 40 min; until top is lightly browned.
Serve hot with the wipped cream if desired. Enjoy!!!

Submitter: 
Veronica Meredith
Description: 
I used fresh apricots from our garden for this simple and delicious crumble.
