Title:
Pampered Chef Creamy Cheddar Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. butter
3 tbsp. all-purpose flour
1 3/4 cups milk
1 cup grated sharp cheddar cheese (4 oz.)
1/2 tsp. dry mustard
1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. ground cayenne pepper

Directions:
1.  In 2-qt. Saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.  Add flour; whisk until well blended using Stainless/Silicone Sauce Whisk.  Cook 1 minute, whisking constantly (do not allow to brown).  Add milk all at once; whisk until smooth.  Bring to a simmer, stirring constantly until thickened.  Cook an additional 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
2.  Remove from heat.  Add cheese, mustard, salt and cayenne pepper; whisk until smooth.
