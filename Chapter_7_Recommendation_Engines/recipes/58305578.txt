Title:
Ellie's Deli Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs, slightly beaten
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 C. chopped onion
1 rib celery, finely diced
3 T. steak sauce
1/2 C. half & half
1/3 C. catsup
salt & pepper to taste
2 1/2 C. dry stuffing mix
2 lbs. ground round

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*
Combine all ingredients except meat and set aside for 5 min.
Add the meat and mix thoroughly.
Place in a loaf pan sprayed with Pam.
Spread additional catsup on top if desired.
Bake 1 1/2 hours at 350* or until browned evenly thoughout.
