Title:
ZESTY STUFFED CHICKEN Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless chicken thighs
1 1/2 lb bag of cocktail shrimp (deveined)
1 chopped yellow bell pepper
1/2 cup of chopped green onion
1 tbs of creole seasoning
8 oz of shredded pepper jack cheese
6 slices of thick cut bacon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees
Mix shrimp, bell peppeer, green onion and cheese in a bowl.  Add creole seasoning to mixed ingredients.  Next spread chickens thighs on a cutting board skin side facing up and pound until somewhat flattened. Turn chicken over to be stuffed. Scoop and place a even amount of mixed ingredients onto chicken.  Compress stuffing so that it is packed unto chicken.  Roll chicken and wrap bacon slices around chicken tight so chicken wont unroll while cooking.  Place chicken in a large baking pan and cook uncovered for 45 minutes or until chicken is fully cooked!..Enjoy!
