Title:
Tropical Pineapple Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1925579.jpg
Ingredients:
10 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple with juice
1/2 cup SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 teaspoon mustard powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Combine the pineapple (with juice/ liquids), SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, lemon juice and dry mustard in a small bowl. Mix all together. Place chicken breasts in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish. Spread the pineapple mixture over the chicken. Bake in the preheated oven for 50 to 60 minutes.

Submitter: 
Inspired by Home Cooks
Description: 
Baked chicken, coated with a lemon-pineapple sauce makes this dish taste tropical.
