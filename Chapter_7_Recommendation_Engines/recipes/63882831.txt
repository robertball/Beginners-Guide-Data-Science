Title:
Quick and Easy Chicken Nuggets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
1 egg
1/2 cup water
3/4 cup all purpose flour
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 clove garlic, minced(optional)

Directions:
Cut chicken into 1 inch pieces.
Heat 3 inches of oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat until oil reaches 375F(190C) and adjust heat to maintain temp.
Beat egg and water in a large bowl until well mixed.
Add flour, salt, pepper and garlic, stirring to form smooth batter.
Dip chicken pieces into batter, draining off excess, and fry a few pieces at a time until golden brown - about 4 minutes.
Drain on paper towels.

Submitter:

Description: 
These chicken nuggets are quick, easy, simple and delicious. They're fantastic for get-togethers or as nibblies for parties.
