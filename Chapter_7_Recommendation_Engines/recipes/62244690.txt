Title:
No-Fuss Mild Thick Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs lean ground beef
1 1/2 cups chopped onion
4-5 garlic cloves, minced
Seasoning mix:  combine 2 Tbsp chili powder, 1 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp black pepper, 2 Tbsp brown sugar, 2 tsp dried oregano, 1/8 tsp cayenne, 2 tsp ground cumin and 1 tsp cocoa powder; mix to blend well and set aside
1 x 23 oz (680 ml) tomato sauce
2 x 28 oz tins diced stewed tomatoes with liquid
1 19 oz tin red kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 19 oz tin any other favorite beans, rinsed and drained

Directions:
In a large skillet or Dutch kettle, over medium-high heat, brown the beef, onion and garlic until meat is no longer pink;  drain and return to pot
Sprinkle the seasoning mix over the meat and stir to blend well
Add the tomato sauce and the stewed tomatoes, stirring to blend well, and bring to a boil;  reduce heat to simmer and cook about an hour, uncovered, stirring occasionally
Stir in the beans and cook, uncovered, about another 30-45 minutes, stirring occasionally
Taste and adjust seasonings before serving

Submitter:

Description: 
A thick meaty chili I threw together to suit my own preference for a mild but tasty and hearty dish.  After just sampling a bowl, I thought I would share my recipe.
