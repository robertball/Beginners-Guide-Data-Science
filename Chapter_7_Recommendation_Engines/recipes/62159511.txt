Title:
Beef, Chicken or Cheese Creamy Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Enchiladas
10 to 15 flour tortillas, taco or burrito size
16 oz sour cream
1 8 oz package cream cheese, softened
2 cup grated cheese (Cheddar/Jack)
1 4 oz can diced green chiles, medium or mild
1/2 6 oz can olives, halved
1 tsp cumin powder
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 red or yellow onion, chopped
1 tsp garlic powder, garlic salt, or minced garlic
1 lbs ground beef, chicken, or pork
1 can diced tomatoes with green chiles
Cream Cheese Sauce
1 8 oz package cream cheese, softened
1 4 oz can green diced chiles, medium or mild
1 10 oz can of cream of chicken soup
1/3 cup milk
Topping
2 cup grated cheese (Cheddar/Jack)
1/2 6 oz can olives, halved

Directions:
Enchiladas
Cook chopped chicken, pork, or ground beef in a frying pan, add onion and garlic.
Mix sour cream, cream cheese, grated cheese, green chiles, olives, cumin. Mix in cooked meat.
Cream Cheese Sauce
Mix cream cheese, soup, green chiles, milk.
Preheat oven to 375 F
Spread 1/3 Cream Cheese Sauce on bottom of 9 x 13 pan.
Warmed tortillas for 1 minute in microwave
Fill tortillas with meat sauce, large serving spoon-size.
Fold end of tortilla then roll.
Place enchiladas snugly in the pan.
Top enchiladas with remaining Cream Cheese Sauce.
Cook enchiladas uncovered at 375 F for approximately 15 miin until sauce is bubbly.
Remove from oven, sprinkle remaining cheese and olives on enchiladas
Return to oven for 5 minutes until cheese is melted.
