Title:
Sweet Kumara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oranges, peeled
1 inch piece fresh ginger
4 stalks celery
1 small kumara (sweet potato), peeled & halved

Directions:
Process oranges, ginger, celery, & kumara through Juice Fountain™ using speeds 3 & 5. Serve Immediately.

Submitter:
