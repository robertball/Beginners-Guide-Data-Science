Title:
Green Smoothie with Kefir Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6837021.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup blueberries
1 cup spinach
1 cup chopped kale
1/2 cup kefir
1/2 cucumber
1/2 avocado
1/2 banana, frozen
1 tablespoon ground almonds

Directions:
Combine blueberries, spinach, kale, kefir, cucumber, avocado, banana, and almonds in a blender. Process until smooth.

Submitter: 
mmcowgirl
Description: 
This green smoothie is made with kefir, a fermented dairy food, plus avocado, blueberries, cucumber, and banana for a delicious, nutritious treat.
