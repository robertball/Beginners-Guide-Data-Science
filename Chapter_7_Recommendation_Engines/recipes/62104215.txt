Title:
Sweet Savory Rosemary Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup walnut pieces
1 sack pre-washed chopped romaine or 2 hearts, chopped, about 6 cups
1/2 red onion, finely sliced
1 Tbs orange marmalade
1 small sprig rosemary, stripped and finely chopped
2 Tbs balsamic vinegar
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
3 Tbs goat cheese
Salt to taste

Directions:
Start by toasting the walnuts on the stovetop. While the walnuts are toasting, arrange lettuce and onion on a plate. To make the dressing: in a small bowl combine marmalade, balsamic vinegar and rosemary. Slowly whisk in the oil.
Pour dressing on top of the salad and top it with the warm walnuts and goat cheese.

Submitter: 
veggiemom
Description: 
This refreshing salad uses a mixture of sweet marmalade, savory balsamic vinegar and woodsy rosemary over a simple bed of greens and warm walnuts.
