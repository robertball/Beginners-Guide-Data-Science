Title:
Pampered Chef's ~ Roasted pepper Focaccia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/455976.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Package (13.8 oz) Refrigerated pizza crust
1/4 cup Italian Salad dressing
2 cloves garlic, pressed
1 cup Shredded Mozzarella cheese, divided
1/2 cup (125 ml) sweet roasted red pepper, drained, patted dry and chopped
1/2 cup (125 ml) sliced pitted ripe olives
1/4 cup Grated fresh parmesan cheese
1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning

Directions:
preheat oven to 400F (200C).  Unroll pizza crust lengthwise over bottom of medium bar pan.  Using fingertips, press indentations 1/2 inch (1 cm) apart into surface of dough.
In small bowl, combine salad dressing and garlic; brush over dough.  Sprinkle with half of the mozzarella cheese.
Top with the peppers and olives, sprinkle with remaining mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese and seasoning.
Bake 25-28 minutes or until deep golden brown.  Remove from oven and allow to cool for 10 minutes.  Cut into squares and serve warm or at room temperature.
