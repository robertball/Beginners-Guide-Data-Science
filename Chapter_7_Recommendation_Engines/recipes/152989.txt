Title:
Virgin Mojito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983860.jpg
Ingredients:
6 mint leaves
1 tablespoon SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1 lime, juiced
1 (12 fl oz) can diet lemon-lime soda, chilled
Fresh mint for garnish (optional)
Lime slices for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Smash mint leafs and SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener with a spoon in the bottom of a large glass.
Add lime juice and stir.  Pour in soda and stir well.*  Garnish with mint and lime slice.  Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Refresh yourself with this non-alcoholic take on a mint and lime mojito.
