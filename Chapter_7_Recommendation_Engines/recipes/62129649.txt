Title:
Creamy Sauteed Shrimp with Sun Dried Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. large uncooked shrimp, peeled & deveined
2 Tbs. butter
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes in olive oil, cut into strips
8 ounces sliced fresh mushrooms
1 tsp. ground black pepper
1/2 tsp. dried basil
1/4 tsp. dried oregano
1 jar (16 oz.) alfredo sauce
1 lb. bow tie pasta, cooked and drained according to directions

Directions:
Melt the butter in a large skillet and add tomatoes with oil, garlic, black pepper, basil and oregano. Saute to soften garlic.
Stir in mushrooms, sauting until slightly softened.
Add the uncooked shrimp, stirring to coat. Cook until shrimp are pink.
Pour jar of alfredo sauce over all and stir; cover and let simmer a few minutes.
Mix with cooked bowtie pasta and serve!

Description: 
This is a quick and easy meal that tastes like you spent hours preparing it! The first time I made it, my 12 year old daughter told me she felt guilty leaving sauce on her plate, because it tasted so good!
