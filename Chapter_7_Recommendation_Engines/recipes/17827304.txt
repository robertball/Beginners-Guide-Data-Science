Title:
Grandmother's Macaroni & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup macaroni noodles
1 1/2 cup cheddar cheese
1 egg
2/3 cup milk
dash tobasco
1 TB mustard (optional)
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook and drain macaroni.  Mix all ingredients together.  Bake at 375 degrees until brown and thickened (about 45 minutes).
clay pot: 425 degrees for one hour

Submitter: 
LAURA67
Description: 
This is my Grandmother's macaroni and cheese.  It is best when cooked in a clay baking dish.
