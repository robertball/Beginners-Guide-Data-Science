Title:
Zaco Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of rice
1 bag of corn (if you like corn)
1-2 cans of crushed tomatoes
1-2 cans of diced tomatoes
1 zucchini
ground turkey (1 roll)

Directions:
Heat up ground turkey until most of the fat burns off. Pour in tomatoes. Make rice as desired. Pour cooked rice (I suggest brown) into a hot pan of the tomatoes and meat. Heat corn as desired. Dump in. Heat zucchini in microwave and dump in. Stir together.
