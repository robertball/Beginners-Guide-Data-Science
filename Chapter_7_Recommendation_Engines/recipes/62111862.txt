Title:
Fried Porkchops (Wiener Schnitzel) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cubed pork loins
1 c. flour
pepper, garlic powder, season salt (to taste)
3 eggs (beaten)
1 roll of Ritz Crackers,crumbled
1/4 c. flour
oil for frying

Directions:
Season the pork with pepper, season salt, & garlic powder.
In one bowl, have 1c. of the seasoned flour. In the 2nd bowl, beat the eggs. In the third bowl mixed finely crushed crackers with 1/4c. flour & season to taste.
Take each pork loin, dredge it in the flour, & dust off any excess.  Then cover it in the egg mixture & lastly with the cracker/flour mixture.
Deep fry each piece until cooked throughout & a nice golden brown color.  Serve with Lemon wedges and french fries.  Add a brown gravy to make into Jaegerschnitzel.
