Title:
Apple and Onion porkchops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of pork chops
1 can of apple pie filling
1 envelope of onion soup mix

Directions:
Put pork chops in a 13 by 9 pan
in a bowl mix together apple pie filling and onion soup mix
pour over pork chops
Cover and bake on 350 for 45 minutes to an hour till pork
chops are done.

Submitter:

Description: 
This recipe is easy and only uses three ingredients.
