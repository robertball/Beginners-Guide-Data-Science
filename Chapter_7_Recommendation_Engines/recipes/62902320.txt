Title:
Orange Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
6 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1-2 Tbs. olive oil
one 1.3-oz. envelope dry onion soup mix
one 6-oz. can frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed

Directions:
In a skillet, brown chicken breasts in oil and place in a large slow cooker sprayed with vegetable cooking spray.  In a bowl, combine onion soup mix, orange juice concentrate, and 1/2 cup water and pour over chicken. Cover and cook on low for 3-5 hours.
