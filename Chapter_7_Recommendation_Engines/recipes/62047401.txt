Title:
Beef and Green Bean Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound gound beef
2 teaspoons dried minced onion
1 can (8 ounces) tomato sauce
1 can (14 1/2 ounces) cut green beans, drained
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
1 tube (8 ounces) crescent rolls
1 egg
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese, divided
paprika

Directions:
Brown ground beef until no longer pink; drain.  Stir in onion, tomato sauce, beans, salt, and garlic salt.  Simmer for 8 minutes or until heated through.  While ground beef mixture is heating, seperate crescent rolls into eight triangles; place in a 9 inch pie plate with points toward the center.  Press onto bottom and up the sides to form a crust.  Seal perforations.  In a bowl, beat egg and 1 cup cheese; spread mixture over crust.  Stir 1/2 cup cheese into meat mixture. Spoon meat mixture into crust.  Sprinkle with paprika and remaining cheese.  Bake, uncovered, at 375 degrees for 20 -25 minutes or until golden brown.  Let stand 5 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
This is a great way to get kids to eat their green beans.  Just add a salad and you have a wonderful meal.
