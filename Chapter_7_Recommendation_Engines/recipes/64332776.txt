Title:
Tofu Pasta Salad with Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz Whole-grain Pasta
1 Tbsp Pesto of choice
2 oz Tofu, cut into ¼-inch cubes
1 cup Chopped Tomatoes
2 cups Baby Spinach
2 Tbsp Grated Parmesan

Directions:
Make pasta according to directions. In a skillet over medium heat, sauté pesto and tofu for 10 minutes (stirring only twice so tofu is golden). Toss hot pasta and tofu with tomatoes and spinach (spinach will wilt). Top with Parmesan.
