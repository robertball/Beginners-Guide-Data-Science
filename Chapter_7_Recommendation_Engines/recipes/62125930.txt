Title:
Chicken Sausage Penne With Shallots and Wine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pound of Chicken Sausage, flavored with Parsley, casing removed
2 Shallots, sliced into thin rounds
1 Clove of garlic, chopped
1/4 Cup of White Cooking Wine
1/4 Tsp Hot Pepper flakes
1/2 Lb Whole Wheat Penne Pasta
2 Tbsp Fresh Parsley, Chopped (optional)
Fresh Shaved Parmigana Cheese, to Taste

Directions:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a rolling boil and cook pasta 8-9 minutes for al-dente.
Drain pasta, reserving a few tablespoons of the water for the sauce. Set aside.
Remove casing from sausage and cook over medium heat
until browned, about 8-10 minutes.
Add the sliced shallots and cook until translucent, about 2-3 minutes.
Add the garlic and cook another minute.
Add the white wine and red pepper flakes.
Add the pasta water as needed until you reach a desired consistency.
Mix penne with the sausage mixture.
Top with fresh parsley and fresh shaved Parmigana Cheese.
