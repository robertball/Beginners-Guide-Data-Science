Title:
Peanut Butter Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7oz can chickpeas
2 garlic cloves, coarsely crushed
6tbl lemon juice
4tbl peanut butter
3tbl olive oil
1tsp ground cumin
Salt and pepper
Paprika to garnish

Directions:
Drain chickpeas, reserving the liquid, and place in food processor.
Blend chickpeas to a smooth paste, adding some liquid from the can as necessary to acheive a nice smooth texture.
Mix together garlic and peanut butter, and add to food processor. Blend till smooth.
With machine running gradually add 3tbl through the feeder tube and allow to mix in.
Add ground cumin and season with salt and pepper. Process to mix.
Transfer hummus to bowl or serving dish, chill until required for serving.
Sprinkle with paprika before serving.

Submitter: 
Cara
Description: 
Delicious hearty hummus with a sweeter edge than typical. This has always been a crowd pleaser for me. Good with pita trianlge and vegetables alike. I recommend doubling recipe for parties.
