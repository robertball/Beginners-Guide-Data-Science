Title:
French Onion Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups thinly sliced onions
1 TBL sugar
1/2 tsp pepper
1/3 cup vegetable oil
6 cups beef broth
8 slices French bread (3/4" thick), toasted
1/2 cup shredded parmesan or swiss cheese

Directions:
In a dutch oven or soup kettle over medium-low heat, cook onions, sugar and pepper in oil for 20 minutes or until onions are caramelized, stirring frequently. Add the broth; bringo to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 30 minutes. ladle soup into ovenproof bowls. Top wach witha slice of French bread; sprinkle with cheese. Broil until cheese is melted. Serve immediately.
