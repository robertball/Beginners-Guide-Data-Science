Title:
Chili Glazed Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989106.jpg
Ingredients:
4 (6 ounce) salmon fillets, skinless
1/2 cup VH速 Sweet Thai Chili Sauce
2 tablespoons VH速 Soya Sauce
2 tablespoons orange juice
PAM速 Cooking Spray

Directions:
Spray glass baking dish with PAM(R). Place salmon in dish.
Stir together VH(R) Sweet Thai Chili sauce, VH(R) Soya sauce and orange juice. Pour over salmon.
Bake in a 400 degrees F (205 degrees C) for 15 minutes or until salmon is cooked through.
