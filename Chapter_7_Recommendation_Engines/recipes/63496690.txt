Title:
Blueberry Vegan Old-Fashioned Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/954415.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cups Spelt  Flour
1/2 cup Oat Flour
3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon Celtic Sea Salt
1 tablespoon Agave
3 tablespoons Vegan Margarine
1 Flax Egg or Chia Egg
1 1/4 cups almond milk
1/2- 1 cup of frozen blueberries
Cooking spray

Directions:
Sift together flour, baking powder, salt, and agave in a large bowl.
Whisk in melted margarine, flax egg, and milk until combined. Let batter rest for 5 minutes.
Preheat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Spray with cooking spray. Pour batter into the hot skillet, about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes, until bubbles appear on the sides and center of each pancake. Flip and cook until golden, about 1 to 2 minutes.
