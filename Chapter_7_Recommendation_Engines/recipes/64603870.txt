Title:
Quick and Easy Weekday Beef and Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs Beef Chuck Roast cut into 1" bites
3 Potatoes, cubed
3 Carrots, cut length wise and sliced into halfmoons
3 Stalks Celery, cut length wise and cut into halfmoons
1 Onion, diced
1 Box (36 oz) Beef Broth
1 can of your choice of beans (optional)
2 Cloves Garlic
1 tsp each: Dried Rosemary, Thyme and Sage
1 Bay Leaf (optional)

Directions:
1) Brown beef in vegetable oil over medium high heat until browned.
2) Mean while, in a separate pan, saute the onions, garlic, celery and carrots until onions are translucent (10 mins).
3) Add beef, vegetable mix, potatoes, beans, beef broth and seasoning to a stock pot and bring to a boil. Cover and simmer for 25 minutes or until potatoes and vegies are tender.
