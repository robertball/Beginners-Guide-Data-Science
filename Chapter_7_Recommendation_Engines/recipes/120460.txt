Title:
Bacon-Cauliflower Tossed Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975648.jpg
Ingredients:
2 heads iceberg lettuce, coarsely shredded
1 head cauliflower, coarsely chopped
1 pound sliced bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 1/2 cups shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese
6 green onions, sliced
DRESSING:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup sugar
1 tablespoon milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
In a large salad bowl, toss the lettuce, cauliflower, bacon, cheese and green onions. In a small bowl, whisk together the dressing ingredients. Serve with salad.

Submitter: 
Nancy Jo Leffler
Description: 
I tweaked this tasty recipe from my sister-in-law by using Co-Jack cheese instead of Parmesan.
