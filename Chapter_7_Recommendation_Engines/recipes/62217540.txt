Title:
Toasted Coconut Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5 cups white chocolate chips
1 300 ml tin sweetened condensed milk
1 cup sweetened shredded coconut
2 tsp. coconut extract

Directions:
Line an 8 inch pan with parchment paper or foil. Lightly toast coconut. Set aside. In the top of a double boiler, mix white chips with milk, stirring constantly until melted. Stir in coconut and extract. Pour into lined pan, cover and let set in refrigerator overnight before cutting.
