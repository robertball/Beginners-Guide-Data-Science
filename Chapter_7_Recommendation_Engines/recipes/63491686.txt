Title:
Rueben Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/953000.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C pasta, cooked
1/2 cup saurekraut
2 oz sliced or cubed pastrami or corned beef
3T. Thousand Island Salad dressing
1/2 c.  "SargentoÂ® Traditional Cut Shredded Mozzarella Cheese"
Pickle slices
Rye crackers, toast points,or party loaf

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions, rinse under cold water and drain well. In a bowl, combine the pasta, saurekraut,pastrami, dressing and cheese. Cover and refridgerate 30-60 minutes.
Serve with pickle slices and rye toast points or crackers.

Submitter: 
Tracey Ferrari Posner
Description: 
This can be made with shredded swiss cheese as well.  It is also a very good way to use up any leftover corned beef after St. Patrick's Day :-)
