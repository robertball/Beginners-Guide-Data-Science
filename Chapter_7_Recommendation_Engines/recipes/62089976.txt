Title:
Layered Mexican Party Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans refried beans w/ green chilis
1/2 pound of ground beef
1 (8oz) container of sour cream
1 (8oz) container of guacomole
1 1/2 cups of salsa
1 bag taco-flavored cheese blend
1 can of sliced back olives
1 large tomato, diced
1 small red onion, diced
1 green pepper, diced
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 tablespoon lime juice
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon crushed red peppers

Directions:
in frying pan over medium heat brown ground beef until cooked - while ground beef is cooking add, chili powder, lime juice, garlic salt, onion powder, cumin, & crushed red peppers (set aside)
spead layers of the following ingredients in a serving dish
refriend beans, cooked ground beef, sour cream, guacomole, salsa, cheese, olives, tomoatoes, onion, green pepper
chill in refrigerator for 1 hour and serve with totilla chips
beef is optional - dip is wonderful with or without

Submitter: 
Tarene Condie
Description: 
This dip is the best blend of mexican inspired ingredients that will be the hit of any party and remember the beef is optional
