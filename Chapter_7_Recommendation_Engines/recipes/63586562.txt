Title:
Black Bean Vegetarian Meat loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/997408.jpg
Ingredients:
3-15oz cans Black Beans (Drained and Rinsed)
2/3 cup frozen corn kernels
85g Onion (diced)
1 Cup Shredded Cheese
1 Pkg Hot Taco Seasoning
1 Pkg Spicy Ranch Seasoning
1 Tube Ritz Crackers (Crushed)
148g Assorted colors Bell Peppers (Diced)
24 Slices Jalapeno (Diced)
1 Serving of egg whites

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Generously Coat a 8" X 13" Baking Dish with cooking spray.
Pour the Beans into a large bowl and mash them, leaving some beans whole is okay. Then add; corn, onion, cheese, taco seasoning, ranch seasoning,ritz crackers, bell peppers, jalapenos, and egg whites. Blend this mixture till all the ingredients are evenly mixed.
Pour mixture into prepared baking dish and form into a loaf.
Bake at 350 uncovered for 45-60 minutes

Submitter: 
Val's Mom
Description: 
This was a creation based on trying my first black bean vegetarian burger and deciding I had to learn how to make one, then I decided to go for a vegetarian meatloaf instead!
