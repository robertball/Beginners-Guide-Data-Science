Title:
Pat Ward's Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. very warm(not hot) water
2 pkg. yeast
1/2 c. sugar
1 c. shortening
2 eggs
2 t. salt
9-9 1/2 c flour

Directions:
Dissolve yeast in warm water.  Mix together yeast mixture, sugar and shortening.  Add eggs and salt.  Stir in flour a little at a time.  Pour out on cabinet.  Let dough rest while you wash and grease bowl.  Knead, adding a little flour at a time.
After kneading, put into greased bowl and cover with a light towel.  Place in a warm spot to rise.  Let rise to double.  Poke down, then let rise until double again.  Poke down again and shape.
Grease all pans.
For break, this recipe makes 8 small loaves or 3 large loaves.  Let rise until double and bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Lucy Dee
Description: 
This was a family favorite.  My great-grandmother found this in a newspaper article years ago and thought enough of it to preserve the recipe.
