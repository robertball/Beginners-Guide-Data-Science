Title:
Curried Tuna Melt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/182940.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can tuna, drained and flaked
1/4 cup thinly sliced celery
1/4 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons thinly sliced green onions
2 tablespoons raisins
2 teaspoons lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon curry powder
2 English muffins, split and toasted
4 slices Cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine tuna, celery, mayonnaise, onions, raisins, lemon juice, salt and curry powder. Spread about 1/4 cup on each muffin half; top with cheese. Broil 4 in. from the heat for 1-2 minutes or until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Rose  Maldet
Description: 
An old-fashioned favorite is updated with curry powder and raisins. Your family will be delighted to see these open-faced sandwiches make an appearance on your table.
