Title:
Tempeh Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/963848.jpg
Ingredients:
3-4 garlic cloves
2 Tablespoons of Fresh Chopped Ginger
1/4 cup Shoyu,  Soy  or Tamari Sauce
1/4 cup water
1/4 cup Maple Syrup or Honey
2 packages of tempeh
2 Tablespoons Olive Oil or Grape Seed Oil

Directions:
In food processor or high speed blender - Blend: garlic, ginger, shoyu sauce, maple syrup, and water  together
Slice tempeh diagonally.
Pour oil into baking dish.
Place tempeh in baking dish
Pour marinade over tempeh.
Cover and marinade for 1 - 2 hours.
Bake 350 F covered for 30 minutes.
Uncover and bake at 375 F for 15 minutes or until slightly brown.
