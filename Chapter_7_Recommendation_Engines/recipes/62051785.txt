Title:
Chocolate Mint Truffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4  cups milk chocolate chips
1  cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
3/4  cup heavy whipping cream
1  tablespoon peppermint extract
1 1/2  cups finely chopped walnuts, toasted

Directions:
1.Line a baking sheet with wax paper.
2.Place milk chocolate and semi-sweet morsels in large mixer bowl. Heat cream to a gentle boil in small saucepan and pour over morsels.
3.Let stand for 1 minute then stir until smooth. Stir in peppermint extract.
4.Cover with plastic wrap; refrigerate for 35 to 45 minutes or until slightly thickened. Stir just until color lightens slightly. Do NOT over mix or truffles will be grainy.
5.Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls onto prepared baking sheet then refrigerate for 10 to 15 minutes.
7.Shape into balls then roll in walnuts. Store in airtight container in refrigerator.
*Note: These are also great dipped in chocolate.

Submitter: 
Leslie A.
Description: 
These would be great for any special occasion place in those small, decorative candy cups. You can substitute vanilla, almond, or orange extract for the peppermint(probably 1-2tsp of either would be sufficient, but do so according to your own taste/judgement.)
