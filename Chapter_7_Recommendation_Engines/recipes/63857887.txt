Title:
Beer Bread (bread machine) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 fluid ounce) can or bottle premium lager
1 tablespoons margarine
1 clove garlic - crushed
1 tablespoons milk
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoons salt
1 tablespoons sugar
2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon dried marjoram
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1 tablespoons margarine, melted

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over medium heat, warm beer slightly with crushed garlic. Stir in milk and 1 tablespoons margarine to melt. Pour contents into bread machine pan. Pour in flour. Make a well in the center of the flour, and sprinkle salt onto one side of the mound that forms. Into the well, pour the sugar and yeast. Sprinkle with pepper, onion, garlic, and herbs. Use basic setting, with 1.5lb loaf, light.
Immediately turn out onto a cooling rack, and brush with melted margarine. Cool completely before cutting.

A tender beer bread with a rich cheesy taste reminiscent of cheese nips. Excellent plain, in sandwiches, or toasted.Modified from: Beer Bread (bread machine dough cycle)
