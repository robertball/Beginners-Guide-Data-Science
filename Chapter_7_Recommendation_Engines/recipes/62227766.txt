Title:
"Cracker Jack" Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2c. shortening
2c. brown sugar
2c. white sugar
4 eggs
4 tsp. vinilla
2 tsp. baking powder
2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
3 1/4c. flour
2c. coconut
2c. chocolate chips
4c. rice krispies
4c. oatmeal

Directions:
Cream shortening. add sugars, mix well. add one egg at a time. beat well after each. add vinilla. stir in dry ingrediants, transfer to giant bowl, then by hand add oatmeal, rice kripsies, coconut, and chocolate chips. drop by spoonfull onto lightly greased cookie sheet. bake at 350 untill lightly brown
