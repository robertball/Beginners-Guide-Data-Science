Title:
Spicy Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1120679.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound penne pasta
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
2 green onions, chopped
1/2 (6 ounce) can black olives, chopped
1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes, softened in water and sliced
1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing

Directions:
In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta until al dente, rinse under cold water and drain.
In a large bowl, combine the pasta, beans, green onions, olives, sun dried tomatoes and feta cheese. Add enough salad dressing to coat; toss and chill before serving.

Submitter: 
Diana
Description: 
This pasta salad with beans is colorful, healthy and tasty.  You can try using green olives in this salad, if you like.
