Title:
Apple Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/971401.jpg
Ingredients:
1 box refrigerated pie crusts, softened as directed on box
1/2 package (7-oz size) almond paste
1 package (8-oz) cream cheese, softened
1/3 cup granulated sugar
1 egg
1/2 teaspoon grated lemon peel
1/8 teaspoon salt
4 large tart apples, peeled, cored, sliced thinly
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
3 tablespoons apricot jam

Directions:
1. Heat oven to 375Â°F. Unroll 1 pie crust on work surface; cut out 2- or 3-inch hearts (or desired shape) with cookie cutter. Bake hearts on ungreased cookie sheet about 8 minutes or until light golden brown. Cool; set aside.
2. In medium bowl, mix cream cheese, granulated sugar, egg, lemon peel and salt with electric mixer on medium speed until smooth.
3. In large bowl, mix sliced apples, brown sugar and cinnamon.
4. Unroll second pie crust in ungreased 9-inch glass pie plate. Between 2 sheets wax paper, roll almond paste into thin 7-inch circle. Remove top sheet wax paper, and turn almond paste circle into crust-lined pie plate; gently press almond paste into pie crust and remove second sheet wax paper.
5. Spread cream cheese mixture over almond paste in pie plate.
6. Arrange apples in circular pattern on cream cheese filling.
7. Bake 40 to 45 minutes or until filling is set and slightly puffed.
8. In small microwavable bowl, microwave apricot jam uncovered on Medium (50%) 1 minute. Brush over apples. Top with heart cutouts. Cool about 1 hour or until filling sets before serving.
9. Make sure to cover and refrigerate any remaining pie( if there is any)
