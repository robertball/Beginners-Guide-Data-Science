Title:
French Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup butter
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 cup milk
3/4 cup cake meal (or flour)
3 tsp baking powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Cream egg yolks & sugar; add melted butter, salt, & milk. Fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites, then the cake meal sifted with baking powder.
Heat an 8" sq pan, grease & flour it. Put in cake mixture. Mix 3 tsp sugar & 1/2 tsp cinnamon with 1/2 cup nuts. Sprinkle on top .
Reduce oven to 350 & bake 20 min
