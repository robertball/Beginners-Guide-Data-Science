Title:
Marshmallow Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
1 (14 ounce) can condensed milk
1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme

Directions:
Stir cream cheese, sour cream, condensed milk, and marshmallow creme together in a bowl until smooth.

Submitter: 
JAGAR
Description: 
This dip is always a huge hit at showers or any other party. You can easily add a small dab of food coloring to match your theme, i.e. blue for baby boy shower or orange for University of Tennessee football party.
