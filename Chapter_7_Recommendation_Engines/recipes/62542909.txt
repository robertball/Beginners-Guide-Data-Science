Title:
Honey Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 boneless chicken breasts
1/3 c. butter or margarine
1/3 c. honey
2 T. prepared mustard
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. curry powder

Directions:
Mix lower 5 ingredients together and pour over chicken breasts. Bake at 350 degrees until chicken reaches an internal temperature of 170 degrees.
