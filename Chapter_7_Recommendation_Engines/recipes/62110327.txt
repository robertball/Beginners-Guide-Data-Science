Title:
Lowfat Chicken Crockpot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breast
1 can fatfree Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 envelope of onion soup mix
1 cup of fat free sour cram

Directions:
Please Chicken Breast in the bottom of the crock pot.
Mix soups and sour cream together and place over the chicken.
Set your crockpot on low and let cook 4-6 hours.
Serve over Rice.
(I do spray my crockpot with a non-stick spray before I begin, it helps with clean up)

Submitter: 
Cindy Brown
Description: 
This  chicken recipe is simple, lowfat, and flavorful and I use it on those really busy days.
