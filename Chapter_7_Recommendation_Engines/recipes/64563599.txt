Title:
Pumpkin "Pie" Cup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Vanilla Wafers
1 Vanilla pudding snack
1/8 tsp pumpkin pie spice
2 TBL whipped topping

Directions:
Put 4 wafers in a glass. Mix 1 pudding snack and 1/8 tsp pumpkin pie spice. Top cookies with pudding, 2 TBL whipped topping and a pinch of spice.
