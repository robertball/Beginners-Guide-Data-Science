Title:
Breakfast Bake w/ Country White Pepper Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1037270.jpg
Ingredients:
Biscuit dough
Parm cheese
Cooked shredded potatoes
Caramelized onions
1/4 teaspoon my herb mixture
1 Dozen eggs
Heavy cream
Cream cheese
Cooked crumbled sausage
Mozz cheese
Country White Pepper Gravy

Directions:
Make biscuit dough and pat it down in a greased baking pan with a little coming up the sides.
Sprinked a lttle parm cheese on top.
Layer the cooked shredded potatoes with caramelized onions and herbs.
Pour the eggs beaten with heavy cream and cream cheese on top.
Then add crumbled cooked sausage.
Top with grated mozz.
bake at 400 degrees for 30 mins or until toothpick comes out clean.
Serve with gravy on top.
