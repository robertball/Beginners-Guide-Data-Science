Title:
Cream Puffs & Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/570090.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c. water
2 sticks butter
1/2 t. salt
2 c. flour
8 eggs
2 large Instant Vanilla Pudding Mix
3 c. milk
4 c. sour cream
1 t. almond extract
2 c. Cool Whip topping
Chocolate syrup

Directions:
For Puffs:  Combine water, butter & salt in pan.  Bring to boil.  Turn heat to low and add flour, stirring vigorously until mixture forms a soft ball.  Remove from heat and add eggs one at a time, beating until smooth after each one.  Spoon dough into 15 mounds on ungreased cookie sheets and bake at 375 for 45 minutes.
For Cream:  Combine pudding mix with milk, then add sour cream, almond extract and Cool Whip.  Chill until time to serve.
To serve:  Slice puffs in half.  Place generous dollop of cream on bottom half and set top of puff on the cream.  Drizzle with chocolate syrup and enjoy!
