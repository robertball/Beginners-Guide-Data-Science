Title:
Orange Fizz Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7032977.jpg
Ingredients:
ice cubes
1 cup orange juice
1/3 cup tonic water
1 teaspoon lemon juice

Directions:
Fill a glass with ice cubes. Pour in the orange juice, tonic water and lemon juice. Stir and serve.

Submitter: 
LEAGILLIAN
Description: 
Make boring orange juice tangy and fizzy with this delicious blend!
