Title:
Vegetarian Breakfast Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 ounces plant-based "ground beef" (such as Beyond MeatÂ® Beefy Crumbles)
1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
1/3 cup onion, chopped
1/2 cup green pepper, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
4 eggs
6-6" flour tortillas
1/3 cup cilantro, chopped
1 Tbsp. taco seasoning
1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
1/2 cup salsa
sour cream (optional)

Directions:
1. Heat olive oil over medium heat in a skillet. Cook and stir beef crumbles, green peppers, onions, and garlic in the hot skillet until tender, 3 to 4 minutes.
2. In a medium bowl beat eggs, taco seasoning, and cilantro together.
3. Pour eggs into skillet and scramble until set, 3 to 4 minutes.
4. Remove from heat and stir in cheese and salsa until melted and combined.
5. Serve with warmed flour tortillas. Top with sour cream if desired.

Submitter: 
Chef Mo
Description: 
Beyond Beef Crumbles create the idea that you are enjoying some nice sausage with your breakfast tacos. Simple recipe to create a breakfast meat free.
