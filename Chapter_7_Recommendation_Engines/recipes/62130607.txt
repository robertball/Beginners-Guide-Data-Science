Title:
Thai Peanut Noodle Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cloves garlic minced
1 cup vegetable stock
1/3 cup creamy peanut butter
3 tbsp soy sauce
3 tbsp honey
3 tbsp packed brown sugar
1 tsp sesame oil
1 tsp ground ginger
1 tbsp cornstarch
1/4 tsp ground red pepper
2 tbsp sake
1 medium onion
1 cup broccoli
1 cup sliced carrots
1/2 cup red or yellow peppers
1/2 cup snap peas
2 Tbsp vegetable oil
1/2 pound cooked pasta (spaghetti or linguine)

Directions:
Combine all wet and dry ingredients together in saucepan over medium heat except for sake. After mixture is thickened, add sake and stir in for 1 minute. Remove from heat.
Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain.
Saute onions in oil until clear. Stir in remaining vegetables. Cover and turn down heat to low. Steam for 3 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
Add cooked noodles and toss in with peanut sauce.  Pour into pasta bowl.

Description: 
This is a vegetarian stir fry dish using pasta, fresh vegetables and Asian ingredients.
