Title:
Hawaiian Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One small to medium sized pork loin
olive oil
garlic powder
1/2 cup fresh cilantro
1/2 of a Red Onion
1/2 orange pepper
1/2 red pepper
1 20 oz. can Pineapple Tidbits
3/4 quart pineapple juice
2 packages taco seasoning
Cayenne pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
Take porkloin  and spread olive oil along the outside after removing excess fat.
Then taking take some garlic powder and cayenne pepper and sprinkle on the loin to your liking
Put a 1/4 cup water in bottom of pan and bake for 45 - to 1 hr
After the pork has cooked, cool for 10 minutes then cut in thin slices.
Put pork in large plasic ziplock bag
Add the cilantro and 1 pack of taco seasoning and shake til meat is coated.
Then add the can of pineapple tidbits, shake til mixed.
Next add the peppers and onion, shake until mixed
Pour in Pineapple juice and marinate for at least 1 hr.
Now take the mixture and pour into large pan to cook.
Simmer for 15 - 20 mins or until juice has reduced to about 1/4 of itself.
Add last packet of taco seasoning, mix well and enjoy!
Top w/ fresh cilantro, lettuce and sour cream!

Submitter: 
Kay
Description: 
Delicious pineapple-pork tacos that are delicious! I was getting creative with left over pork loin one day when I was pregnant and came up with these.
