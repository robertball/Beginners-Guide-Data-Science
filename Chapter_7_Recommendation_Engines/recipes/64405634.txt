Title:
Speedy Pot Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Italian Sausages, cooked
4 cups Penne pasta
1 750 ml pasta suace
4 cups warm water
1 green pepper, chopped
1 red pepper, chopped
1 yellow pepper, chopped
4 carrots, chopped
1 White onion, chopped
1 cup Marble Cheese, shredded
2 cloves Garlic minced

Directions:
Cook sausages, BBQ is my preference
Add jar of sauce and water to the pot and bring to a boil
Add pasta and bring to a boil
Reduce heat to med-low, cover and cook for 10 minutes
Add peppers, onion, carrots and garlic, cook for another 10 minutes
Stir in Cheese and sausages and cook for an additional 5 - 10 minutes till the cheese is melted in and serve
