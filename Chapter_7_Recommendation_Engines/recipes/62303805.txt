Title:
Chicken Breasts - You pick from three flavors! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts (about 4 oz. each or the size of the palm of your hand, cut in half if they are bigger)
Parmesan-Dijon Chicken:
3/4 cup dry bread crumbs
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup margarine or butter
2 Tbsp. Dijon mustard
Ranch Chicken:
1/4 cup ranch dressing
1/3 cup seasoned bread crumbs
Special K Chicken:
1/4 cup lite mayo or miracle whip
1 cup Special K, crushed

Directions:
Rinse chicken breasts under cold water and pat dry with paper towels.
Heat oven to 375 degrees.
Parmesan-Dijon Chicken:
Melt butter in shallow baking dish. Stir mustard into the melted butter.
Mix bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese in a large plastic bag.
Dip the chicken breasts in the butter mixture. Then place in the bag of crumbs, seal bag and shake to coat with the crumbs.
Ranch Chicken:
Pour the dressing in a shallow bowl or pie pan. Place bread crumbs on plate.
Dip the chicken, one at a time, into the dressing coating all sides. Then coat all sides with crumbs.
Special K Chicken:
Place the mayo in a shallow bowl. Crush the cereal and place on a plate. Coat the chicken, one at a time, with the mayo. Then coat all sides of the chicken with the cereal.
Bake the chicken:
Carefully place the coated chicken breasts in a single layer in an ungreased rectangular pan or glass baking dish. Bake uncovered 20-30 minutes, turning chicken once with tongs half way through. Check with meat thermometer for doneness. Gently remove from pan with spatula.

Submitter: 
Kristin McNeil
Description: 
Easy baked chicken breasts. You choose from: Parmesan-Dijon Chicken, Ranch Chicken, or Special K Chicken!
