Title:
MAPLE SYRUP DUMPLINGS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
2 tablespoons butter, softened
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1/2 cup Milk
SYRUP:
2 cups Maple Syrup
2 Tablespoons Butter
1 cup Water

Directions:
Mix maple syrup, butter and water in large sauce pan and bring to a boil. Mix dry ingredients and cut in butter.  Add milk to form dough.  Drop spoonfuls of dough into the boiling syrup.  Cover and simmer on low heat for 20 minutes.
Serve hot; plain or with ice cream

Submitter: 
Gail Cobile
Description: 
I do not make these nearly as often as I'd like which would be once or twice a week, because they are just so sinfully delicious. My guys love it served with sausage links or patties. YUM-O!
