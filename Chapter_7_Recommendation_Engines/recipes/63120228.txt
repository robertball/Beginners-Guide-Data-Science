Title:
Antipasto Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb pasta, cooked
2 14oz cans artichoke hearts (prefer quarters), rinsed & drained
2 14oz jars roasted sweet red peppers, drained
1 3.8oz can black olive slices
1/4c grated cheese (Romano or Parmegiana)! Or more to taste
1 bag(2c) shredded mozzarella
1/4 lb thinly sliced genoa salami, cut into small pieces
1/4 lb thinly sliced pepperoni, cut into small pieces
1.5 tsp minced garlic (or more to taste)
Salt (only if needed after adding cheese)
Pepper to taste.
1/3c olive oil
1/2 c. Italian seasoned bread crumbs, plus some sprinkled on top
1 Tbsp butter, melted

Directions:
In large bowl combine pasta, artichokes, peppers, olives, cheeses, pepperoni, salami, breadcrumbs and garlic.
Salt & pepper to taste.
Drizzle with oil.  Toss gently to mix.
Transfer to a lightly oiled casserole dish.
Top with melted butter and sprinkle with bread crumbs.
Bake uncovered at 375F for about 20-30 minutes until warm and lightly browned.
