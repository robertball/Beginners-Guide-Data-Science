Title:
Grandma's German Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1090474.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium cucumbers, thinly sliced
1 medium onion, thinly sliced
3 small tomatoes, sliced
2 Tbsp fresh snipped parsley
DRESSING:
1/4 cup sour cream
1/4 tsp prepared mustard
2 Tbsp minced fresh dill
1 Tbsp white vinegar
1 Tbsp milk
1/8 tsp pepper

Directions:
In a bowl, combine cucumbers, onions, tomatoes, and parsley. Combine dressing ingredients; pour over cucumber mixture and toss gently. Cover and chill for at least 1 hour.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
This recipe came from Grandma who was born in Germany. It's a very cool, light salad with an exhilarating taste that's delicious anytime of the year - especially when made with fresh from the garden cucumbers and tomatoes.
