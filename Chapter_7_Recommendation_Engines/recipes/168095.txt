Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Butterscotch Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982456.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
1/4 cup instant butterscotch pudding mix
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
6 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal

Directions:
In a large microwave safe bowl, heat margarine and marshmallows at HIGH for 3 minutes, stirring after 2 minutes. Add butterscotch pudding mix.
Stir in vanilla and KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal until well coated. Using lightly buttered spatula, press into buttered 3.5 L (13 x 9-inch) pan.
Allow mixture to cool slightly. Cut into 5 cm squares. Best if served same day.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*
Description: 
These sweet and easy butterscotch squares are a perfect coffee break treat.
