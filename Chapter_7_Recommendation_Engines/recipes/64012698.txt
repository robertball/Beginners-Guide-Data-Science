Title:
Skillet Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 yellow onion, thinly sliced
1/2 cup water
5 medium Yukon gold potatoes, sliced into 1/4" thick rounds
1 tbsp. olive oil
2 tbsp. fresh rosemary (about 2 sprigs)
1 1/2 tsp. kosher salt
3/4 tsp. pepper
1 tbsp. ground coriander
4 bone-in, skin on, chicken thighs
4 chicken drunsticks

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
Scatter onions over the bottom of a large, oven-proof skillet.
Add water. then lay potatoes over the onions.
Sprinkle rosemary over potatoes and season with 1 tsp. of the salt and 1/4 tsp. pepper.
Combine coriander and remaining salt and pepper.
Rub seasoning into both sides of chicken.
Lay chicken, skin side up, onto potatoes.
Roast until chicken is cooked through and potatoes are tender, about 1 hour.
