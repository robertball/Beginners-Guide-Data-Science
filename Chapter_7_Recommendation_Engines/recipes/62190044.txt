Title:
Easy as 1-2-3 Beer Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup of beer
2 Tbs Sugar
3 Cups Self Rising Flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix all ingredients and pour into a loaf pan. Bake for 45 minutes or until golden and finished in the center.

Submitter: 
Muffin Mom N Garlic Girl
Description: 
A lady I work with gave me this recipe which was passed down to her by her father. She uses all sorts of different beers for different flavors and is the bread making queen at our potlucks.
