Title:
Rice and Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1 cup instant rice
2 eggs
1/2 c shredded cheddar or other cheese
2 tbsp butter
Salt and Pepper to taste
Dash of paprika

Directions:
Boil the water in a small saucepan.
When boiling, add instant rice, cover, heat on low.  Should take 5-10 minutes for rice to cook.
Break the 2 eggs and beat, mix with cheese and butter.
When rice is finished cooking, add the egg mixture to the rice and stir quickly, covering all of the rice, the heat of the rice should cook the eggs.
Add salt, pepper, and paprika to taste.

Quick and tasty meal for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.
