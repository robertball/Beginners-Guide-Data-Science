Title:
Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz noodles
2 cans tuna, well drained (7 oz each)
3/4 cup sour cream
3/4 cup plain yogurt
1 can sliced mushrooms, drained (4 oz)
1/2 tsp salt (optional)
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 cup dry bread crumbs
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 Tbsp butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Cook noodles according to directions.  Drain.  Return drained noodles to kettle; stir in tuna, sour cream, yogurt, milk,  mushrooms, salt and pepper.  Pour into ungreased 2 qt casserole.
Mix bread crumbs, cheese and butter; sprinkle over casserole.  If desired, sprinkle with paprika.  Bake uncovered for 35-40 minutes or until bubbly.
