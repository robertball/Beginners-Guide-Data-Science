Title:
High-Fiber Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. All-Bran Cereal
1 c. almonds
3 c. wheat bran
3 T. whole wheat flour
1/3 c. dark brown sugar
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. allspice
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 c. apple chunks, blueberries, or cranberries
3 eggs
1 1/4 c. milk
1/2 c. oil
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
In a food processor, process the All-Bran to texture of bread crumbs.  Do the same for the almonds. (Plug your ears!)  Put in bowl and add all ingredients through fruit.
In another bowl, lightly beat eggs and add milk, oil, and vanilla.  Whisk thoroughly.
Add about one-third of the dry ingredients to egg mix, and mix thoroughly.   Repeat till all ingredients are mixed.  Fill muffin cups to top (grease if not using paper cups) and bake about 20 minutes till toothpick comes out clean.
