Title:
Peachy Chicken Pitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/5 cup plain yoghurt
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/8 tsp salt
2 chicken breasts, cooked and cubed
1 cup diced peaches, fresh or canned
2 oz celery, chopped
1-1/2 oz walnuts, chopped
4 pita bread
Spinach
Alfalfa sprouts

Directions:
In a bowl, mix yoghurt, cinnamon and salt.  Add chicken, peaches, celery and walnuts. Chill.  Fill pita bread and garnish with spinach and alfalfa sprouts.
