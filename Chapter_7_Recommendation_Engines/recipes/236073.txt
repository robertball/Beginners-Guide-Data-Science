Title:
Creamy Chocolate Kahlua Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1089871.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 fluid ounces Kahlua (any flavor)
1/2 fluid ounce vodka
1 1/4 fluid ounces half-and-half cream
1 1/4 fluid ounces chocolate milk
Whipped cream, sweetened cocoa powder, cinnamon, nutmeg for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Pour Kahlua, vodka, half-and-half, and chocolate milk into a shaker filled with ice. Shake well. Pour into chilled martini glass.
Serve topped with whipped cream and sweetened cocoa powder, cinnamon or nutmeg. You can also use chocolate shavings on top.
