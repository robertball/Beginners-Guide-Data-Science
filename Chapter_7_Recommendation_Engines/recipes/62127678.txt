Title:
Easy Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
INGREDIENTS:
* 2 TB Butter
* 2 TB Flour
* 1 Onion, diced
* 1 Clove Garlic, diced
* 2 C Cheddar Cheese, grated
* 1 Cup Heavy Cream
* 1/2 ts nutmeg
* 1/2 ts salt
* 1/4 ts pepper
* pasta (medium shells or elbows)

Directions:
~ Grate Cheese
~ Prepare pasta according to box directions
~ While pasta is cooking, saute onions and garlic
~ Add flour, stir until well combined (about two minutes)
~ Add cream, nutmeg, salt, and pepper to onions, bring to a boil
~ Add cheese, combine
~ Drain pasta, add to cheese mixture
