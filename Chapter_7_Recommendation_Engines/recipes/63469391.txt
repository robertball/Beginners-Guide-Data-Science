Title:
Cherrie and Walnut Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Dressing: 2/3 cup olive oil, 1/3 cup balsamic vinegar, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp pepper, 1 Tbsp dijon mustard, 5 cloves of garlic - minced.
Salad: 1 bag mixed spring salad greens, 4 oz goat cheese, 1 cup walnuts (toasted), 1/2 cup dried cherries coarsely chopped, dash of smoked prapika.

Directions:
Mix dressing ingredients together with a whisk or in a shaker bottle. Let them get to know each other for an hour or so at room temperature.
Dress salad greens with the amount of dressing you like. Plate, and then spoon on top a generous portion of chopped cherries, toasted walnuts and crumbled goat cheese. Dust lightly with paprika.

Submitter: 
Scootir
Description: 
A simple tasty salad. My family loves this so much they call it the salad and insist we have it at every special occasion. It's also very easy to modify - swap the walnuts for almonds, the cherries for blueberries, and so on.
