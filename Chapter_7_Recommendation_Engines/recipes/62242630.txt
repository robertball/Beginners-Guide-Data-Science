Title:
Cilantro-Lime Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. olive oil
1 cup basmati rice
1 1/2 cups chicken broth, not too strong
2 medium sized cloves garlic, minced
2 Tbsp diced green onion
1/4 cup whole kernel corn
2 Tbsp. fresh lime juice
zest from one lime
1/2 cup cilantro, chopped
1 tsp. salt

Directions:
Add the oil to a sauce pan and heat on low. Add the garlic and rice to the oil and saute for 2 minutes on medium heat stirring frequently.
Add the chicken broth, salt, lime juice and bring to a boil. Cover and cook on low for 15 minutes or according to rice package directions.
When the rice is done, add lime zest, corn, onions and chopped cilantro and stir to mix in. Serve immediately.
