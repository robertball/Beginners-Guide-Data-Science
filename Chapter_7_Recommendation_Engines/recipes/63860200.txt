Title:
Pie Crust Perfected Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2917053.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups sifted all purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup chilled shortening
1/3 cup unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
3-4 Tablespoons vodka (vanilla flavored)
4-6 Tablespoons ice water

Directions:
For baked pie shell (for cream filled pies) preheat oven to 450.
Combine flour and salt in medium bowl; cut in shortening & butter with pastry blender or two knives until mixture is crumbly. Sprinkle 1 Tbsp of water over the flour and toss with a fork. Alternate this process with vodka and water until flour is moistened.
Press dough together with hands into a ragged ball and place on lightly floured surface. Roll out 1 dough ball to 1/8" thickness. Line pie plate with pastry. Cut off excess and tuck edge under; crimp edge as desired.
Poke holes on side and bottom of crust. Line pastry shell with double thickness of foil. Bake 8 minutes. Remove foil; bake 6 minutes for she'll that wi be died and then baked again and bake up to 15 minutes or until golden. Cool on wire rack.
Use remaining dough for pie dough cookies. (Bake at 350 degrees for 12-15 minutes.)
