Title:
PAT EMEL'S CREATION CAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 c. cherries
1 c. sugar
1 pkg cherry jello
3 c. mimi marshmallows
1 pkg yellow cake mix, prepared as directions

Directions:
Grease 13"x9" pan. Place fruit on bottom. Sprinkle sugar over. Sprinkle jello over. Sprinkle marshmallows over. Pour cake batter over. Bake @350
55 to 60 mins.  Works well as upside down cake served with a dollop of whipped cream or cool whip.
