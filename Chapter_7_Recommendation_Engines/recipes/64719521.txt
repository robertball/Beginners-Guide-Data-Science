Title:
Russian Strudel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6174267.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons Butter
1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
2 Cups Chopped Onions
5 Cloves Garlic Minced
3 Stalks Celery Sliced Thinly
4 Cups Sliced Mushrooms
2 Cups Thinly Sliced Carrots
3 Cups Chopped Cabbage
1 Can Vegi-Burger
1 teaspoon Salt
2 teaspoons Dried Dill
2 teaspoons Caraway Seeds
1/2 t Black Pepper
8 Ounces cream cheese, at room temperature
24 Ounces Cottage Cheese
16 Ounces Sharp Cheddar Cheese
4 Eggs
1 Box Frozen Phyllo Dough
1/4 lb Melted Butter

Directions:
In a large skillet sauce the onions in the butter and oil until they are translucent then add garlic and celery until onions barley start to brown.  Add the carrots and sautéed for 5 minutes.  Add the cabbage to the pan and sauce covered for another 5 minutes.  Season with salt, dill, and caraway seeds and cook for 5 more minutes.  Add mushrooms and black pepper and sautéed for final 5 minutes.
In a large mixing bowl, combine the softened cream cheese with cottage cheese, grated cheese and eggs.  Drain the sautéed vegetables and stir them into the cheese mixture.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
Assemble the strudel start by placing phyllo dough onto cookie sheet covered with parchment paper. by layering one sheet phyllo dough painted with butter on top of another sheet until using 1/2 the box.  Fill with 1/2 the vegetable mixture and fold the edges of the dough over the top of the mixture tying together with cooking string.   Brush top with butter.  Bake about 40 minutes until golden brown in preheated oven.
Allow strudel to sit for 10 minutes before cutting
Serve with a Cucumber Dill Sour Cream Salad
