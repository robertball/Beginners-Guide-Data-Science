Title:
Peanut Butter Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 9" graham cracker pie crusts
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 c. milk
2 c. white powdered sugar
1 c. peanut butter
1 - 8oz. tub of cool whip

Directions:
Using a hand mixer, beat cream cheese, milk, sufar, and peanut butter until smooth and creamy.
Slowly fold in cool whip by hand.
Portion into the two pie crusts, then refrigerate until firm.

Description:
