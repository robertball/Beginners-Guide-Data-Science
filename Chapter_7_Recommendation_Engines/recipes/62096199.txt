Ingredients:
1/2 c butter
1 lb mushrooms
1 tsp garlic salt
liberally add fresh ground pepper -  to taste.

Directions:
Melt butter & saute until brown and just lightly bubbbly, slice mushrooms or mince (they cook faster) and saute until liquid is absorbed, mushrooms are soft, and a golden brown color. Put softened cream cheese (easiest to use is the kinds in the tubs, it can be fat free,light,or not).    Can be garnished with chopped scallions and chopped chives and or chopped pimentos.  Chill until set, best for at least 1/2 hr.  and serve. Can also be molded and decorated festively for entertaining.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
A delicious appetizer, wonderful served on sliced baguettes or with flavored melba toast rounds.
