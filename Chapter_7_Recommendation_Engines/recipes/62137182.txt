Title:
Breakfast Banana Boats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Whole Wheat Hot Dog Buns
Peanut Butter or Nutella
Apple Sauce or Apple butter
1 Ripe Banana
Raisins, Craisins or diced dates (optional)

Directions:
Spread inside of buns with Peanut Butter, followed by Apple Sauce/Butter. Slice banana in half, lengthwise.  Lay 1/2  banana inside of each bun, top with raisins.
Voila! Out the door in 5 minutes!

Submitter: 
Kelly Sanchez
Description: 
Back to School or Saturday sporting events, this is a great out-the door breakfast that the kids LOVE!
