Title:
Fantastic & Easy Tuna/Salmon Loaf  -Serve hot or cold. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
TUNA LOAF
3 cans (7oz ea.) White Albacore Tuna (Kirkland Brand Albacore Tuna from COSTCO is the best)
1 can Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup
3 lg. Eggs, beaten
1 c. Dry Bread Crumbs or crushed crackers (see recommendations)
1/2 c. Mayonnaise (do not use reduced fat)
1/2 c. Onion, chopped fine
1/4 - 1/3 c. Green Pepper, chopped fine
1/2 t. salt (omit or reduce if using salted crackers)
1/8 t. pepper
SAUCE (optional)
1/2 c. Mayonnaise (can use reduced fat)
1/2 c. Sour cream (reduced fat works great)
1/2 c. Cucumber, seeded & chopped fine
2 T. Onion, chopped fine
1/2 t. Dill
Yield: 1.5 cups

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*
Flake tuna until no lumps appear.
Mix all ingredients together by hand with a fork or hand beater.
Pour into a loaf pan sprayed first with a non-stick cooking spray
Cover loosely with foil.
Bake for 1 hour or until meat thermometer inserted reads 160* (you may need to add up to 20 min cooking time, depending on the size loaf pan you use).
You can uncover for the last 10 min for additional browning if desired.
Substitutions work great see below. Reduced fat condensed soup can be used.
Do NOT use reduced fat mayonnaise!
> Delicious served cold with a side salad on a hot summer day!

Submitter: 
Elizabeth Kessler
Description: 
One of my favorite fast, easy recipes that stay well in the fridge for a quick re-heat meal during the week.
