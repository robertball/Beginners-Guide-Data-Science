Title:
Ba-da-bing! Artichoke Bruschetta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz jar of Marinated artichoke hearts, chopped & drained
2-3 vine ripened tomatoes, chopped
1/4 cup chopped red onion
1 tablespoon fresh minced garlic
1 tablespoon parmesean cheese (optional)
1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
1 tablespoon oregano
1 tablespoon parsely
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine artichoke hearts, tomatoes, and red onion.
Add garlic, cheese, basil, oregano, and parsely.
Mix together and add salt/pepper as needed.
Refridgerate for up to 1 hour before serving & enjoy!

Submitter: 
velvet ears
Description: 
This brushetta is so flavorful & turns out even better with fresh ingredients! Tastes great with sliced, lightly toasted italian bread drizzled with olive oil! *I don't always add the cheese.* Awesome either way.
