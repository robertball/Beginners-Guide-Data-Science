Title:
Atkins Friendly Triple Cheese Macaroni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz Atkins low carb penne pasta (5g net carbs)
1 onion
2 cloves garlic
4 strips bacon
1 pint heavy whipping cream
2 C cheddar cheese
1/4 C gorgonzola cheese
1/4 C parmesan cheese
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 T corn starch
1/4 C cold water

Directions:
Bring salted water to a boil.  cook pasta till al dente about 3-4 minutes. Do not overcook!  drain and run cold water over pasta to stop cookin process.
meanwhile cook bacon till brown and crispy. let drain on paper towels and crumble. Pour off some grease from pan, reserving enough to cook onion.  Cook onion and garlic until onion is translucent.  Add cold heavy cream and salt and pepper to taste.  Cook 5-7 mins on medium high heat.
dissolve cornstarch in cold water. pour into cream and stir constantly till sauce has thickened.  turn off heat and add cheddar, parmesan and gorgonzola cheese. reserve some cheddar.  stir till cheese melted.  Add cooked pasta and stir well.  Pour into a greased baking dish and top with remaining cheddar cheese. Cover with alluminum foil. Cook for about 30 mins at 350 degrees. remove foil and cook for additional 5-10 mins.
enjoy!

Submitter: 
Maryam
Description: 
This Atkins friendly baked macaroni is sure to please those carb cravers.  Low Carb Pasta in a lovely cream sauce with cheddar, parmesan and gorgonzola cheese.  Sure to be a favorite.
