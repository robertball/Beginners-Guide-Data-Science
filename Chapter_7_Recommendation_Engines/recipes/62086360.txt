Title:
artichoke spinach blossoms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  package frozen puff pastry
1 (14 ounce) can marinated artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1(10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 (8 ounce) block cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup grated Romano cheese
1/4 teaspoon minced garlic

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
In a large bowl, combine artichoke hearts, spinach, sour cream, mayonnaise, cream cheese, Romano cheese, and garlic.  Set aside.
Unfold 1 pastry sheet on a lightly floured surface.  Roll into 15” x 12” rectangle.  Cut into 20 (3”) squares.  Press squares into 20 (1 ½ inch) mini muffin pan cups.  Place about 1 tablespoon filling in center of each.  Repeat with remaining pastry sheet, and filling.
Bake 15 minutes or until golden.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Kahmann Harvey
Description: 
Beautiful little puff pastry bites filled with a cheesy artichoke spinach filling. No matter how many I make, it's never enough!
