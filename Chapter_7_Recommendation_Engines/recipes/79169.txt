Title:
Baked Ham, Swiss, & Red Onion Melt1 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 slices EARTH GRAINS® Extra Fiber 100% Multi-Grains bread
2 ounces honey cured or smoked ham, sliced
1/8 red onion, thinly sliced
4 slices Swiss cheese
Sweet mustard

Directions:
Pre-heat broiler. Toast the bread slices and spread sweet mustard on each half.
Top each slice with 2 ounces ham, 1 slice red onion, and 2 slices of cheese, in that order.
Place halves on cookie sheet and put under the broiler until cheese is melted (about 4-5 minutes). Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
EARTH GRAINS®
Description: 
These delicious broiled open faced sandwiches are made with ham, cheese and red onion. A little sweet mustard adds a nice finishing touch.
