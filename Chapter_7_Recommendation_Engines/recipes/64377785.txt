Title:
Mexican Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup enchilada sauce
2 tortillas
1/2 cup beans
1/4 cup sharp cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
1. Heat tortillas in microwave for about 20 seconds (for 2 tortillas) This will make them softer and more pliable to work with.
2. In a baking dish coat the bottom and sides with enchilada sauce
3. place 1/4 cup of beans in each tortilla and roll up.
4. place rolled tortilla in baking pan and pour the rest of enchilada sauce over.
5. Sprinkle cheese over the top and bake at 375 for 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Henry Johannes
Description: 
This is a diet recipe.  The refried beans should be made already along with the enchilada sauce.  I would suggest prepping them on another day making these enchiladas easy to make.
