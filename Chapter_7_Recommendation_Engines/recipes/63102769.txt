Title:
Pretty in Pink Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/828153.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ounces Hot Rose tequila
1 ounce vodka
3 ounces milk

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a martini shaker over ice. Shake until chilled, strain, and serve!
