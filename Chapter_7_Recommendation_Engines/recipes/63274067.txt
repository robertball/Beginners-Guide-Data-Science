Title:
Cherry Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. graham cracker crumbs
1/2 c. melted butter
2 T. sugar
1 c. powdered sugar
1 large pkg. cream cheese
3/4 c. chopped walnuts
1 large can cherry pie filling
cool whip

Directions:
Mix graham crackers, butter, and sugar together. Pat into a greased 9x13 pan and bake at 350 degrees for 8 minutes.
Mix the sugar and cream cheese together and spread onto cooled crust.
Sprinkle on the walnuts.
Pour on the cherry pie filling and spread to an even layer.
Top with Cool Whip, and chill for 12-24 hours.
