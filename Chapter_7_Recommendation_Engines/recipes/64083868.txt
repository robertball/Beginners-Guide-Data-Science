Title:
smoked venison Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
The brine
One quart water heated to a high simmer
One cup Kosher salt
One cup molasses
One cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
Four tablespoons rub
Three quarts COLD water
Smoke with Hickory

Directions:
Mix and heat the brine in large sauce pan. add cold water and let cool.
Trim Excess fat from venison
Place meat in brine for 24-48 hours
Rub with meat rub
Place in smoker at 300f with meat probe set to 140f
When they reach temperature remove from heat and wrap with aluminum foil and let the meat sit for a minimum one hour.
