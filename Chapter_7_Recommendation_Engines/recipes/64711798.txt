Title:
Air Fryer Spicy Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5610871.jpg
Ingredients:
12 ounces green beans, washed and trimmed
1 Tbsp. sesame oil
1 tsp. soy sauce
1 tsp. rice wine vinegar
1/2 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
1 clove garlic, minced

Directions:
1. Start air fryer at 400 degrees and place green beans in a mixing bowl.
2. Whisk together all remaining ingredients and pour over green beans.  Toss to coat and let marinate for 5 minutes .
3. Place half of the green beans in the basket of the air fryer. Cook for 12 minutes shaking basket halfway through cooking time. Repeat with remaining half of green beans.
