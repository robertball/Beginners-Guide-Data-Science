Title:
Grandma's Strawberry Milkshake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/20233.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ounces strawberries, hulled
1 cup milk
1 tablespoon honey
6 cubes ice

Directions:
In a blender, combine ice cubes, strawberries, milk and honey. Blend until smooth. Pour into glass and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Tawny
Description: 
The best strawberry shake in the world comes from my grandma's kitchen in Florida!
