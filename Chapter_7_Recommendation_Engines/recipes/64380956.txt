Title:
Cream Cheese Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ounces cream cheese, softened
1 pound box icing sugar
1/4 teaspoon peppermint extract. (like vanilla) Do not confuse with the tiny bottles of peppermint oil.

Directions:
Mix well to a dough similar to play dough. Pinch off a ball and roll it into a rope. Slice off pieces with a knife or pizza cutter into bite size pieces. If you like patty shapes, make the pieces a bit larger and flatten with a fork. Refrigerate 90 minutes or let dry overnight on the counter. Flip them at the halfway point helps them dry. Put parchment under them. Store airtight in fridge. Can freeze.

Submitter: 
Rosebud
Description: 
These are easy to make and very festive as they can be made with any flavour or dipped in cinnamon/sugar or melted chocolate. Can use any flavour chips for melting.
