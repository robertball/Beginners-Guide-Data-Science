Title:
Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup flour
2 cups flavored broth
Pepper to taste
Garlic Powder to taste
Additional flavored bouillon to taste

Directions:
Make a roux by melting butter over medium-heat
Slowly whisk flour to melted butter. Add pepper and/or garlic powder to taste.
Slowly add flavored broth stirring over low heat until smooth, thickened, and bubbly.
