Title:
Chicken and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken, skinned (enough for your meal)
2 c. long cooking rice
1 can cheddar cheese soup
1 can golden mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can water

Directions:
Cook chicken in oven for 1/2 hour at 375 degrees.
While cooking, stir together soups, rice, and water.
Remove chicken from oven and drain (can de-bone at this point if you wish).
Pour soup mixture over the chicken and bake at 375 for 1 hour, or until rice is done.

Yummy and much cheaper than store-bought, sure to be a family favorite.
