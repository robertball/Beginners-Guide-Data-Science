Title:
Asian Salad 1/2 Version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3 ounce) packages ramen noodles, crushed
1/2 cup slivered almonds
1 pkg cabbage
1/2 bunch green onions, chopped
1/4 cup & 2 Tablespoons vegetable oil
2 Tablespoons rice vinegar
1/8 cup white sugar
1 tablespoon soy sauce

Directions:
In a small bowl combine sugar, vinegar, soy sauce vegetable oil, and ramen packets. Stir well.
In a large bowl , combine shredded napa cabbage and chopped green onions and crushed ramen noodles. Add the soy sauce mixture to the cabbage. Toss to coat. Serve or Refrigerate for a time and toss again to maximize the flavor.

Submitter: 
Debra Kay
Description: 
For a sensational summer salad, toss shredded Napa cabbage and sliced green onions with toasted ramen noodles, slivered almonds and sesame seeds, then dress with an aromatic blend of oil, vinegar, sugar and soy sauce.Modified from: Asian Salad
