Title:
Pretzel Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 T butter
2 T all purpose flour
1/4 cup cream cheese, room temperature
2 T Creole mustard (or course ground mustard)
2 T prepared yellow mustard
1 teaspoon Old Bay seasoning
1/2 t kosher salt
1/4 t black pepper
1/4 t cayenne pepper
dash hot sauce
1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese, grated
1/2 cup pepper jack cheese, grated

Directions:
1.  Melt butter over medium heat, and add flour.  Whisk till combined well, about 2 minutes.
2.  Add cream cheese, whisking until well combined, about 2 minutes.
3.  Add mustards, Old Bay, salt, peppers and hot sauce.  Whisk till combined, about 2 minutes.
3.  Once mixture thickens slightly, add cheddar and pepper jack cheeses slowly.  Whisk constantly until combined.  Serve hot!  (Whisk occasionally to prevent separation)

Submitter: 
WEEBRIE
Description: 
Got chips, pretzels, etc?  Want a dip?  This cheesy mustardy dip is delicious and easy to modify to your tastes.  Switch out the ingredients for whatever you like!  SUPER quick and easy to make, too!!!
