Title:
Layered Margarita Jell-O Shots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3219627.jpg
Ingredients:
88g Lime Jello (divided) (1/2 envelope)
1 Cup Tequila (divided)
20g Sugar-Free Orange Jello (1 envelope)
1/4 C Triple Sec
Salt (optional)

Directions:
Mix 44g lime Jello with 1 cup of boiling water, stir until dissolved
Add 1/2 cup of tequila
Pour 1/3 of the way up 2-oz cups
Refrigerate for 4 hours
Mix 20g sugar-free orange Jello with 1/2 cup boiling water, stir until dissolved
Add 1/4 triple sec
Pour a layer about half as tall as the lime one into each cup
Refrigerate for 4 hours
Mix the remaining 44g of lime Jello with 1 cup boiling water, stir until dissolved
Add the remaining 1/2 cup of tequila
Pour another layer, the same size as the first green one, into each cup
Refrigerate for 4 hours
Optional: using a rag or sponge, wet the rim of each up and dip it into a shallow plate of salt for salted rims
