Title:
Veggie Penne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  cups sliced carrots
1  can (15 ounces) diced tomatoes with basil, oregano, and garlic or 15 ounces tomato sauce
8  ounces dried multigrain penne pasta
2  tablespoons finely shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook the carrots in lightly salted boiling water in a covered saucepan for 10 minutes or until they're tender. Use a food processor to puree both the carrots and the tomatoes.
Meanwhile, cook pasta according to package directions; drain and return to pan. Add tomato sauce and heat through on medium. Serve with Parmesan.
