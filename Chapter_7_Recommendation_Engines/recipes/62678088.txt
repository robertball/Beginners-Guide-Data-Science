Title:
Roasted Pepper Salsa Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 bone-in chicken thighs
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 c. chopped onion
1 clove garlic minced
1/2 Tbsp olive oil
1/2 packet Sazon seasoning or other fajita / taco seasoning
1/4 tsp cumin
salt and pepper to taste
1 c. roasted pepper salsa, divided
6 ounces light cream cheese, cubed
1/2 tsp mexican hot sauce
8 flour tortillas
1/2 c. shredded colby and montery jack cheese
3/4 c. shredded mozzerella cheese

Directions:
1. Place chicken thighs in a large stock pot with 12 c. of water and salt and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to medium and boil approximately 30 min or until internal temperature reaches 160 degrees.  Remove chicken, cool, remove meat from bones, and chop into bite size pieces.
2. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 13x9 in pan.
3. Over medium heat, saute onion and garlic for two minutes in olive oil.  Add chopped chicken.  Sprinkle mixture with Sazon, cumin, salt, and pepper. Continue to saute for an additional minute.
Stir in 1/2 c. roasted pepper salsa. Add cream cheese and stir until melted.  Add hot sauce and stir.
4. Warm tortillas by following instructions on package.  Spoon about 1/3 c. of mixture down center of each tortilla. Roll up and place seam side down in pan.  Repeat for remaining tortillas.  Sprinkle with remaining salsa and shredded cheese.
5. Bake 25 to 30 minute or until heated through.
