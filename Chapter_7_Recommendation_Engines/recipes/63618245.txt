Title:
Oscuro pastel de chocolate chip con guajillo y dulce de leche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1006350.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 c butter
1/2 c Herdez Guajillo Cooking sauce plus 2T. - divided
1 c white sugar
1/2 c brown sugar
2 eggs
2t. vanilla extract
2 c all purpose flour
1/2 c cocoa powder
1/2 t. salt
1/2 t baking soda
10 ounce bag of dark chocolate chips
3/4 cup chopped walnuts
14 ounce can of Dulce de leche

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Grease 13X9 pan with non stick spray
In medium pan on stove, brown butter and then let cool.
In large bowl beat browned butter, 1/2 c. Herdez Guajillo cooking sauce, white sugar, brown sugar until well combined and slightly fluffy. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract. Set aside
In separate bowl, combine flour, salt, baking soda and cocoa powder.
Gradually add to wet ingredients in other bowl. Beat until well combined.
Stir in dark chocolae chips and nuts. Mix until just combined.
Pour batter into prepared pan and smooth top.
In small bowl mix 1/2 can of Dulce de leche with remaining 2T of Guajillo cooking sauce. Spread on top of cake batter in thin layer.
Bake for 35-45 minutes.. let cool completely before cutting into squares
