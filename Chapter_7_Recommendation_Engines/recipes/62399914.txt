Title:
Rhubarb Custard Crunch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups rhubarb
2 cups sugar
2 Tbls. flour
4 eggs, beaten
1 cup butter, melted
2 cups oatmeal
2 cups brown sugar
1/2 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Mix the first four ingredients together and put into a buttered 9 by 13.  Next mix butter, oatmeal and brown sugar together. Then, stir the dry ingredients together and put on top of the rhubarb mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.  Serve of course with ice cream or homemade whipped topping.

Submitter: 
Stacey
Description: 
After trying many and not getting the flavor/texture I wanted in a rhubarb crunch recipe this recipe of a friend of mine is is the best crunch recipe I have found.  I particularily like the chewy caramely texture and flavor it has.  This is super easy as well.
