Title:
Award Winning Soft Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/825152.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 cups butter, softened
3/4 cups packed brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
1 (3.4 ounce) packages instant vanilla pudding mix
2 eggs
1/4 tsp salt
1 Tbsp vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Sift together the flour, baking powder and salt, set aside.
In a large bowl, cream together the butter, brown sugar, and white sugar. Beat in the instant pudding mix until blended. Stir in the eggs and vanilla. Blend in the flour mixture. Finally, stir in the chocolate chips and nuts. Drop cookies by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets and flatten slightly with hand.
Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven. Edges should be golden brown.
