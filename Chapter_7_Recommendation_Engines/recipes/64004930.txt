Title:
Corned Beef and Coleslaw Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups coleslaw
3 Tablespoons sour cream
4 teaspoons mayonnaise
1 teaspoon prepared mustard
1 Tablespoon horseradish sauce
1/8 teaspoon salt
4 slices rye bread
1/2 pound thinly sliced corned beef
4 slices Swiss cheese

Directions:
1.Place coleslaw mixture in a small bowl.  Combine the sour cream, mayonnaise, horseradish sauce, mustard and salt.  Pour over coleslaw mix and toss to coat
2.  Place bread slices on an ungreased baking sheet.  Broil 4 inch from the heat for 2-3 minutes on each side or until toasted.  Layer with corned beef coleslaw mixture and cheese.  Broil for 2-3 minutes or until cheese is melted.
