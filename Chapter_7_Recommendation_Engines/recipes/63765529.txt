Title:
Easy Shake and Bake Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon paprika
1/2 tsp. cayenne
1/2 teaspoon Greek seasoning
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 cup butter or cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
Mix flour, salt, paprika, cayenne, Greek seasoning and pepper together in a bag. Add chicken breasts in a bag and shake until well coated.
Melt butter/margarine in a 9x13 inch baking dish in the preheated oven. Place chicken in the baking dish and bake for 20 minutes. Turn pieces and bake another 20 minutes until tender and juices run clear.
