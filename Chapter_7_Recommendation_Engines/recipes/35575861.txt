Title:
Penne a la Betsy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound small or medium shrimp, cooked,
¾ pound Penne,
1-2 Tbsp butter,
1-2 Tbsp olive oil,
1/2 small onion - chopped,
2 cloves garlic – minced,
8oz plain tomato sauce,
1 cup heavy whipping cream,
1 Tbsp parsley,
1 Tbsp basil,
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta
Saute garlic and onion in butter and olive oil
Add an 8 oz can of plain tomato sauce.  Stir together well.
Pour in 1 cup heavy whipping cream.
Turn the heat to low and add parsley and basil.
Add in the chopped shrimp.
Add salt and black pepper to taste.
Add the pasta to the sauce and stir together until well combined.
