Title:
Italian Potato Scallops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 Russet Potatoes (1 potato = 1 serving)
Oregano
Thyme
Garlic Powder
Onion Powder
Crushed Red Pepper
1/2-3/4 cup Shredded Parmesan Cheese
1/4 cup melted butter
1/4 cup olive oil

Directions:
1.Preheat oven to 425F
2. Slice potatoes very thinly, about 1/4 inch thick.
3. Make a single layer of sliced potatoes in a casserole dish. Sprinkle desired amounts of each spice, Oregano, Thyme, Garlic, Onion, Pepper, followed by the parmesan cheese. Continue layering potatoes, spices, and cheese until no more potatoes. sprinkle remaining cheese over the top.
4. Drizzle butter and olive oil over the top.
5. Bake at 425 for about 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Victoria Olson
Description: 
Delicious! This recipe came by purely by accident, and was a hit! The potatoes are flavorful, and nicely crisped in some places, which adds some great texture!I prefer to use the powdered garlic and onion because it distributes the taste more evenly.
