Title:
Fruit Dip with Cream Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/197159.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme
3/4 cup confectioners' sugar
1/8 teaspoon lemon juice

Directions:
In large bowl, blend cream cheese, marshmallow creme, sugar and lemon juice with electric mixer until smooth.
