Title:
Buttermilk Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Stick (10 oz) Pie crust mix
1 Cup Sugar
1 Tablespoon Flour
½ Teaspoon Nutmeg
¼ Teaspoon salt
1 ½ Cups buttermilk
3 Eggs, slightly beaten
1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
1 ½ Tablespoons melted Butter or Margarine

Directions:
Prepare Pastry for a 9 inch pie crust according to the package directions. Mix together flour, nutmeg, sugar, and salt. Add buttermilk, eggs, vanilla and butter; mix well. Turn into unbaked pie shell. Bake at 375 degrees F. for 40 minutes or until mixture is set.
