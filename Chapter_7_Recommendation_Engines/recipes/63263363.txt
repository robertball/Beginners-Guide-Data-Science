Title:
Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
19 small onion, chopped
19 clove garlic, minced
3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/3 cup dried basil
3 tablespoons pepper
56 cups chicken broth
2 bags potatoes, peeled and cubed
18 cups half-and-half cream
1/2 teaspoon hot pepper sauce (optional)
Shredded Cheddar cheese
Minced fresh parsley

Directions:
Saute onion and garlic in the drippings until tender. Stir in flour, basil and pepper; mix well. Gradually add broth. Bring to boil; boil and stir for 2 minutes. Add the potatoes, cream and hot pepper sauce(optional); heat through but do not boil. Garnish with bacon, cheese and parsley.
