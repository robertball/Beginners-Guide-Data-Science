Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookies  (Crisco) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup (3/4 stick) butter flavor Crisco shortening
1 1/4 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
2 T. milk
1 T. vanilla
1 egg
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp. salt
3/4 tsp. baking soda
1 cup semi-sweet choco chips
1 cup coarsely chopped pecans (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees - prepare cookie sheets
Combine crisco, brown sugar, milk, and vanilla in lg. bowl.  Beat @ med speed till well blended.
Beat egg into creamed mixture
Combine flour, salt and baking soda.  Mix into creamed mixture till blended.
Stir in choco chips and pecan pieces.
Drop rounded T-spoonfuls of dough 3 inches apart on ungreased baking sheet
Bake 1 baking sheet @ a time @ 375 degrees for 8-10 mins. for chewy cookies, or 11 -13 min. for crisp cookies.
Cool 2 min. on baking sheet  -  remove cookies to foil or cooling rack.
