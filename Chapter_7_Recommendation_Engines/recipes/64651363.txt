Title:
Chopped Kale Salad with Asian Flair Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4539952.jpg
Ingredients:
6 oz Baby Kale, chopped
1/2  cup Cilantro, Chopped
1 cucumber, partially peeled and cubed into bite size pieces
1 Red Bell Pepper chopped into medium bite sixed pieces
Sunburst tomatoes halved (10.5 oz container)
3 green onions, thick sliced
For dressing:
3 Tablespoon creamy peanut butter
3 Tablespoons rice vinegar
3 Tablespoons Lime juice
3 Tablespoons Toasted Sesame Oil
1 Tablespoon Peanut Oil
1/2 Tablespoon chopped/grated fresh Ginger
1/2 cup (or more) chopped dry roasted peanuts for garnish.

Directions:
In a large bowl, toss the Kale, cilantro, cucumber bell pepper tomatoes and green onions. Set aside
In a medium bowl, whisk together peanut butter, rice vinegar, lime juice, sesame oil, peanut oil and ginger.
Pour over the salad and toss.
Either serve  or chill in refrigerator.
Sprinkle chopped peanuts on the top prior to serving.
I also have extra on side for those who want to add more crunch.
