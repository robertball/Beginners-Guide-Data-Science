Title:
Battered Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups panko bread crumbs
2 Tbsp. lemon-pepper seasoning
1 tsp. garlic powder
3/4 cup cornstarch
3 eggs lightly beaten
3 Tbsp. olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.
In a shallow dish combine panko, lemon-pepper seasoning and garlic power.
Place cornstarch in a second shallow dish
place eggs in a third shallow dish.
Place 2 Tbsp. oil in a 15x10x1 inch baking pan. Place pan in oven.
Dip asparagus spears, one at a time in eggs to moisten.  Dip in cornstarch to coat.
Dip in eggs again; the dip in panko texture to coat
Place on a tray while coating remaining spears
Transfer all spears to hot baking pan.  Bake 10 minutes or until golden, turning once

Submitter: 
Debi Blair McGinness
Description: 
A friend of mine sent me her recipe for a new delicious way to fix asparagus.
