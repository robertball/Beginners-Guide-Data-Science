Title:
Southwest Chicken Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c sliced chicken fajita meat cooked
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 can white corn
1/2 can Diced tomatoes with green chilies
1/2 can black beans
1/2 C chopped green bell pepper
1/2 C chopped onion
1/4 C milk
8oz spaghetti
1/2 c chopped cilantro
8 oz shredded cheese
Salt & Pepper to taste
1 chopped Jalapeno (optional)

Directions:
Heat oven to 375Â° F.
Cook Spaghetti and drain.
Drain and wash black beans.
Mix together all vegetables and beans and spices in a large bowl. Pour soups and milk over vegetables and stir. Add the spaghetti and cheese reserving 1/4 cup cheese.
Spread everything in a greased 13x9 glass pan. Cook for 25 minutes.
Take out and sprinkle the remaining 1/4c of cheese on the top. Cook for an additional 10 minutes.
