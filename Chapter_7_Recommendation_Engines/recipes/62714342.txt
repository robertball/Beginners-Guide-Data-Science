Title:
Sherbert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 gallon* orange sherbert, softened
(you can use other sherbert flavors, but do-not use rainbow sherbert in this recipe)
1 package (10 ounces) frozen sweetened raspberries, thawed
2 medium ripe bananas, mashed

Directions:
Place the sherbert in a large bowl, stir in  raspberries and mashed bananas.
Freeze until firm.
Scoop ice cream to serve.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
This 3 ingredient recipe is great to serve at a shower or any summer get-together.
