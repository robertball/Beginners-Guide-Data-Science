Title:
Too Much Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/271096.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package devil's food cake mix
1 (5.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix
1 cup sour cream
1 cup vegetable oil
4 eggs
1/2 cup warm water
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
1 package chocolate candies
2 cups whip cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, mix together the cake and pudding mixes, sour cream, oil, beaten eggs and water.  Stir in the chocolate chips and pour batter into a well greased mini cake pans.
Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until top is springy to the touch and a wooden toothpick inserted comes out clean. Cool cakes thoroughly in pan at least 30 minutes before inverting onto a plate  Sprinkle chocolate candies and decorate with whip cream.
