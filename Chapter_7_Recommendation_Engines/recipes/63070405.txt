Title:
Vegan Pumpkin and Chickpea Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T. olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, diced
1 jalapeno, seeded and chopped
1 15 oz. can diced tomatoes
1 15 oz. can pumpkin puree
2 15 oz. cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
1 C. lite coconut milk
1 T. curry powder
2 tsp seasoned salt
1 T. lemon juice
1/3 C. cilantro, roughly chopped
1 C. dry basmati rice
1 3/4 C. water

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large skillet. Add onions and saute on medium heat 5 minutes or until soft. Add garlic and jalapeno and continue cooking 2 minutes.
Add curry powder and continue cooking while stirring until spices are fragrant.
Add tomatoes with their liquid, pumpkin, chickpeas and coconut milk, simmer for 20 minutes.
While mixture is cooking, in a small pot boil water and add rice, return to boil then cover and simmer for 15 minutes or until all water is absorbed.
Add 2 tsp. seasoned salt to pumpkin mixture, more to taste if desired.
Remove skillet from heat.  Add lemon juice, cilantro and rice and stir mixture together until uniform.  Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Ashley O.
Description: 
We created this recipe around an extra can of pumpkin puree left over from the holidays.  It's vegan but still hearty enough to warm you up on a cold winter night.
