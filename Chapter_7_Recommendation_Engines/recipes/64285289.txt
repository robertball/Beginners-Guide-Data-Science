Title:
Dila's Sausage Kale Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1914724.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb sausage, removed from casing
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 cup wine
2 onions, diced
2 cloves of garlic, minced
3 celery stalks, diced
1/2 bag of baby carrots, diced
10-14oz lentils
4 cups chicken broth
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 bunch of kale, chopped

Directions:
1. Brown sausage in olive oil.
2. Add wine, garlic, onions, carrots and celery. Cover and cook until soft, approx 10 minutes.
3. Add lentils, 4 cups of chicken broth, salt and pepper.
4. Simmer for 30 minutes or until lentils are tender.
5. Add kale and remove from heat.

Submitter: 
Diane
Description: 
Tasty lentil soup with sausage and kale. Hearty and healthy. A family favorite!
