Title:
Doughnut Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cip soft margerine
1 egg
1 cup egg
1 cup sugar
1 1/2 cups flour
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
Cinnamon and sugar for after baking

Directions:
Beat together margerine, egg, and sugar.  In seperate bowl, mix together dry ingredients.  Add dry ingredients and milk, alternating, as you mix.  Grease muffin pan and fill with mix 2/3 full.  Bake at 350' for 20-30 minutes.  Remove from pan and roll in cinnamon and sugar.
