Title:
Caramel Apricot Grunt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans (15-1/4 oz each) apricot halves, undrained
2 tsp. quick cooking tapioca
1/3 cup brown sugar
1 tbsp. butter or margarine
1 tbsp. lemon juice
Dumplings:
1 1/2 cups flour
1/2 cup sugar
2 tsp. baking powder
2 tbsp. butter or margarine
1/2 cup milk
Topping:
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tbsp. water

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine apricots & tapioca; let stand x 15 min.
Add brown sugar, butter and lemon juice.
Cook & stir until mixture comes to a full boil. Reduce heat to low; keep warm.
For dumplings, mix flour, sugar & baking powder in a bowl; cut in butter till crumbly.
Add milk; mix just until combined.
Pour warm fruit mixture into an ungreased 2 qt. baking dish (mixture will be very thick).
Drop the dumpling batter into six mounds onto fruit mixture.
Cover and bake @ 425 x 15 min. or until batter tests done with a toothpick. Don't lift the cover while baking.
Topping - in saucepan, bring brown sugar and water to a boil; cook until sugar is dissolved.
Spoon over dumplings; bake, uncovered x 5 min.
Can be served with cream or ice cream.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Apparently the 'grunt' term for these desserts comes from the sound they make as they're bubbling in the oven..?! Anyway, fruit topped with dumplings is delicious whatever sounds its makes ;)
