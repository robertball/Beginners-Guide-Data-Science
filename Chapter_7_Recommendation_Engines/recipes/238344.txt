Title:
Juicy Mango Mai Tai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1985456.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup ice cubes, or as needed
4 fluid ounces pineapple juice, chilled
3 fluid ounces mango nectar, chilled
2 fluid ounces vodka (such as Burnett'sÂ®)
1 1/2 fluid ounces coconut milk
1 fluid ounce orange juice, chilled (optional)

Directions:
Fill a glass with ice. Pour pineapple juice, mango nectar, vodka, coconut milk, and orange juice over ice; stir well.

Submitter: 
Artzy Baker
Description: 
This is a super tropical drink. You can use plain or flavored vodka. For fun, use a fruit cabob as a stir stick.
