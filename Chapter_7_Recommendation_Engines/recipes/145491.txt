Title:
Zesty Hot Sausage and Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991329.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound Bob Evans® Zesty Hot Roll Sausage
1 small onion, diced
2 (28 ounce) cans baked beans
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup ketchup
2 tablespoons prepared mustard

Directions:
In 3 quart Dutch oven, crumble and brown sausage with onions over medium heat. Drain. Add beans, sugar, ketchup and mustard to sausage. Bake at 350 degrees F for 1 1/2 hours or until hot.

Submitter: 
Bob Evans®
Description: 
Hot sausage packs a punch in these distinctive baked beans.
