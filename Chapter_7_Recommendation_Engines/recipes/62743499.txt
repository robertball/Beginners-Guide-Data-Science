Title:
Ema's Applesauce Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 very ripe bananas, mashed
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup applesauce
1 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
1 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. cloves
1 tsp. apple pie spice
2 cups flour
1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
In a large bowl, mix all ingredients on low speed for 2-3 minutes.
Pour batter into 2 greased 9.25 x 5.25 x 2.75-inch loaf pans.
Bake in preheated oven for 30-45 minutes.  Let stand for 5-10 minutes and remove from pans.  Place on wire rack to cool.  Cool completely before serving.

Submitter: 
Tawny M.
Description: 
My mother-in-law gave me this recipe for moist, delicious banana bread.  I'm normally not a fan, but this won me over!
