Title:
Peanut Butter Fudge Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz (125g) semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup smooth or crunchy peanut butter
1/4 cup corn syrup

Directions:
Put chocolate and milk in small saucepan.
Cook over med-low heat until chocolate melts.
Stir in peanut butter and corn syrup until sauce is glossy and smooth.
Serve warm.
Store leftovers (if there are any!) in fridge.
When chilled, sauce will be quite thick and can be used as a spreadable topping or icing.
Makes 1 1/2 cups.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Classic combo of PB & chocolate - delicious over ice cream
