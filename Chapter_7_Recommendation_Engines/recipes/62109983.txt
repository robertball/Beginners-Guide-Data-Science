Title:
Janie's Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads- iceberg lettuce, washed and chopped into 1 inch squares
6-8 medium sized ripe red tomato, cut into wedges
2 large onions- coarsely chopped
2 teaspoons white sugar
Mayonnaise
salt
pepper

Directions:
Place lettuce, tomato and onion in large salad bowl.
Add enough mayonnaise to coat.  Mix well.
Add sugar.
Add salt and pepper  to taste.
Janie liked lots of salt.
Best if salad rests in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.
