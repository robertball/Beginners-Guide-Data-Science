Title:
Tomato Mozzarella Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 roma tomato (sliced to medium thickness)
1/4 cup whole milk mozzarella cheese (shredded)
2 tbsp. milk
1 tsp. course ground black pepper
1/4 tsp salt
2 tbsp. canola or vegetable oil
**optional** Pinch of fresh basil

Directions:
1. Combine eggs and milk in a small bowl and whisk until mixed throughout.
2. Add salt and pepper, stir briskly.
3. In a medium frying pan set to medium low heat, add 2 tbsp. canola or vegetable oil .
4. Add eggs, cook to desired consistency, and flip.
5. Add roma tomato slices and 1/4 cup mozzarella cheese. Fold and remove from heat. Allow 3-4 minutes of cooling inside of the pan.
6. Garnish with fresh basil and serve.

Submitter: 
DIANA K
Description: 
Delicous tomato and whole milk mozzarella combine for a savory breakfast or brunch meal.
