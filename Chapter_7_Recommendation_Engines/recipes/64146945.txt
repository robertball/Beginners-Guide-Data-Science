Title:
Grilled Dijon Lemon Pepper Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1385477.jpg
Ingredients:
2 6oz salmon filets
1/4 C dijon mustard
2 Tbsp olive oil
2 Tbsp lemon juice
1 Tbsp lemon pepper seasoning
2 tsp dried dill
1 tsp ground black pepper
1/4 tsp garlic salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except salmon in a sealable plastic storage bag; add salmon.  Gently coat salmon with marinade.  Place sealed bag in refrigerator- allow to marinate for 2 hours or as desired.
Cook salmon skin side down on outdoor grill for 3-4 minutes before flipping.  Allow to cook an additional 3-4 minutes or until desired degree of doneness is reached.

Description: 
The fresh and bright flavors of this salmon come through beautifully in this light grilled dish!
