Title:
Avocado Milk Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Avocado, peeled & pitted - 1
Milk - 3 cups
Sugar - 3 Tablespoons

Directions:
In a blender, combine avocado, milk & sugar. Blend until smooth and serve. You can add some ice if you like it really cold.
