Title:
Italian Dressing Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon onion powder
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 Tablespoons Italian Seasoning
1/4 teaspoon celery salt/seed
1 tablespoons salt

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix together the garlic salt, onion powder, sugar, oregano, pepper, thyme, basil, parsley, celery salt and regular salt.  Store in a tightly sealed container.
To prepare dressing, whisk together 1/4 cup white vinegar, 2/3 cup canola oil, 2 tablespoons water and 2 tablespoons of the dry mix.

Submitter: 
Sara Smith Sangermano
Description: 
This Italian dressing mix is a combination of eight pungent herbs that you can store and use to whip up fresh Italian dressing in seconds whenever the mood strikes!Modified from: Italian Dressing Mix
