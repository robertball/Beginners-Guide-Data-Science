Title:
All-bran honey muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup whole-wheat flour
1 teaspoon	baking powder
1/4 teaspoon	baking soda
1/4 teaspoon	salt
1 cup	All-Bran® Original
1/3 cup	buttermilk or sour milk*
1	egg
1/3 cup	honey
1/4 cup	oil

Directions:
Stir together flour, baking powder, soda and salt. Set aside.
In medium mixing bowl, combine Kellogg's® All-Bran® cereal and buttermilk. Let stand about 2 minutes or until cereal softens. Add egg, honey and shortening. Beat well. Add flour mixture, stirring only until combined. Portion evenly into nine muffin-pan cups coated with cooking spray.
Bake at 400°F about 25 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.
*To make sour milk, stir 1 tablespoon vinegar or lemon juice into 1 cup milk.
