Title:
Soy Sauce Substitute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can low sodium beef broth
4 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
2 teaspoons dark molasses
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1 pinch white pepper
1 pinch garlic powder

Directions:
In a saucepan over medium heat, stir together the beef broth, balsamic vinegar, molasses, ginger, white pepper, and garlic powder. Boil gently until liquid is reduced to about 1 cup, about 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Cindi Coe
Description:
