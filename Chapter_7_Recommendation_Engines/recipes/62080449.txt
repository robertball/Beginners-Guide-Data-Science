Title:
Butterscotch Crisps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package of butterscotch flavored morsels
3/4 cups creamy peanut butter
3 cups corn flakes
3 cups miniature marshmallows
1/2 cup sliced almonds

Directions:
Melt butterscotch morsels, peanut butter and marshmallows in a nonstick stockpot.
Slowly stir in corn flakes and almonds slices.
Spoon small golfball size amounts onto wax paper.
Refrigerate until crisps solidify.

Submitter: 
Trisha Brummer
Description: 
This is a great snack at a holiday party!  It is delicious and easy to make.
