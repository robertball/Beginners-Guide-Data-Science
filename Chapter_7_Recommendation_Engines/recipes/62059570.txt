Title:
Mexican Lasagna Roll Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Lasagna Noodles
1 lb ground beef
1 pkg hot taco seasoning mix
1 pkg (16 oz) shredded mexican cheese
1 small can pitted black olives
1 small bottle taco sauce
1 large jar spaghetti sauce, plain
sour cream

Directions:
Prepare Noodles according to directions, drain and cool with cold water.  Meanwhile prepare taco meat according to directions on taco seasoining packet (making sure to drain fat from meat when done cooking.  Let meat cool enough to be able to work with it.
When noodles are cool, pat dry and lay each noodle flat.  Put some taco meat in the top and roll noodle just enough to cover meat, add shredded cheese and roll noodle just enough to cover cheese, continue on adding the olives.  Take spaghetti sauce and lightly cover the bottom of casserole pan.  Add rolled noodles to pan.  When pan is filled, add taco sauce to top.  Then add spaghetti sauce. (enough to cover so as not to dry out in the oven)  Cover and bake at 400 degrees for 40 minutes. Serve immediately with a dash of sour cream on top!   If you have extra taco meat, this can also be added to the top after the sauce!   Delicious!!!

Submitter: 
Hawn
Description: 
This dish is so easy to make and the kids can even help out! You can make this a head of time and just pop it in the oven when you're ready! It also freezes well!!  Dont forget the garlic bread!
