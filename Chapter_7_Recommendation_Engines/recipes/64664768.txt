Title:
Great grilled ham and cheese. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Two slices of bread.
Melted butter.
Two slices of ham.
Two slices of pepper jack cheese.
Two thin slices of onion.

Directions:
Brush melted Butter the broadest side of the bread and place on hot griddle or pan.
Place cheese on bread slices immediately.
Once bread is toasted golden brown put ham on hot griddle for about four seconds on each side.
Fold hot ham slices in half and place ontop of melted cheese.
Place onion slices at opposite corners and assemble sandwich slicing diagonally between the onion slices. (Sandwiches taste better when you cut them into triangles itâ€™s a scientific fact)
