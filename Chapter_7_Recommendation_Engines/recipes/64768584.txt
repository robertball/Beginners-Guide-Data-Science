Title:
Eatmore Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup honey
1 cup peanut butter
1 cup chocolate chips (I like to use dark choc.)
10 large marshmallows
3 cups Rice Krispies
1 cup salted peanuts, finely chopped

Directions:
Grease a 9" x 13" pan.
Mix honey and peanut butter in large saucepan. Heat over med-low heat until just boiling, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat.
Stir in marshmallows & choc. chips, until melted.
Add Rice Krispies and peanuts. Mix well.
Press firmly & evenly into pan. Cool in fridge

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
These delicious bars taste just like their namesake! Full of chocolate, peanuts and crunchy krispies
