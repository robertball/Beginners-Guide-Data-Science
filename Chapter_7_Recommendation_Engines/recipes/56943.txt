Title:
The Wanker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes
1 fluid ounce coconut flavored rum
1 fluid ounce coffee flavored liqueur
1 fluid ounce butterscotch schnapps

Directions:
Fill a rocks glass with ice and pour in the rum, coffee flavored liqueur, and schnapps. Stir and enjoy!

A sweet and creamy beverage using butterscotch schnapps.
