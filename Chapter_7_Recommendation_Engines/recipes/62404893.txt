Title:
Pineapple Chicken & Shrimp Tenders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/433740.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup pineapple juice
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
3 tablespoons light soy sauce
1 cup pineapple chunks
8 ounces chicken breast tenderloins or strips
8 ounces shrimp, skinned and deveined
skewers

Directions:
In a small saucepan over medium heat, mix pineapple juice, brown sugar, and soy sauce. Remove from heat just before the mixture comes to a boil.
Place chicken tenders and shrimp in a medium bowl. Cover with the pineapple marinade, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
Preheat grill for medium heat. Thread chicken, shrimp, and pineapple chunks lengthwise onto wooden skewers.
Lightly oil the grill grate. Grill chicken tenders 5 minutes per side, or until juices run clear. They cook quickly, so watch them closely.

Submitter: 
Joe Benecasa
Description: 
Skewered chicken and shrimp tenders are brushed with a tropical mixture of pineapple juice, brown sugar and soy sauce, and then they're grilled. That 's all it takes to make these scrumptious kabobs.Modified from: Pineapple Chicken Tenders
