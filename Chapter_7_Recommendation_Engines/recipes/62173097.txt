Title:
Savory oven crisped fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2C bread crumbs
2Tbsp parmesan cheese
2tsp grated lemon peel
3/4tsp marjoram
1/2tsp paprika
1/4tsp dried thyme leaves
1/8tsp garlic powder
2Tbsp white wine
3Tbsp lemon juice
1 lb of cod fillets

Directions:
Heat oven to 425. Spray the bottom of a 13x9in pan. Combine all the spices and the bread crumbs and parmasan cheese toghether. Set aside. Combine lemon juice and white wine in the shallow pan. Rinse cod off and pat dry. Dip each piece in the lemon jucie mix and then in the bread crumbs. Make sure each piece is well coated. Place fish in pan. Spray to of the fillet evenly with the cooking spray. Bake for 20-25min.
