Title:
Vodka Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
25g (3 Tbsp) vodka
1 pinch crushed red pepper flakes
***
10g (2 tsp) olive oil
2oz prosciutto
115g (1/3 each) onion, chopped
8g (2 large cloves) garlic, sliced
***
1 (15oz) can tomato sauce
***
4g (2 tsp) fresh parsley, finely chopped
2 tsp fresh basil, chopped
60g (1/4 c) fat free half & half or heavy cream
1 dash coarse pepper, or to taste
1 dash table salt, or to taste

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine vodka and red pepper flakes. Set aside for 1 hour.
Heat olive oil in a large stainless steel skillet over med-hi heat until hot. Add prosciutto and fry until browned.  Remove to paper towel to drain and cool.  Crumble with a chopping knife when cooled.
Meanwhile, in prosciutto dripping saute onions and garlic until fragrant.  Mix in crumbled prosciutto and deglaze with vodka mixture.  Simmer 10 minutes over med-lo heat.
Add tomato sauce and continue simmering for 15 minutes or until sufficiently reduced.
Add basil, parsley and cream.  Stir until well combined and simmer for 2 additional minutes.
Serve with tortellini (or desired pasta shape) and garnish with fresh parmesan cheese.
