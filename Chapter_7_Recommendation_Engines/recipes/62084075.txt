Title:
Algerian Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. carrots, sliced
5 T olive oil
1 t. salt
1/2 t. pepper
1/2 t. cinnamon
1/2 t. cumin
3 cloves crushed garlic
1/2 t. thyme
1 bay leaf
1 t. lemon juice

Directions:
Steam carrots to al dente, drain and reserve 1/2 cup liquid. Simmer oil and spices for 10 minutes. Add reserved liquid and bay leaf, simmer (covered) for 20 min.
Add carrots, toss and cook 2-3 minutes
Sprinkle with lemon juice and remove bay leaf
