Title:
Marge's Molasses Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4C shortening
1C sugar
1/4C molasses
1 egg
2C flour
2ts baking soda
1/2ts Cloves, ground
1/2ts ginger, ground
1ts cinnamon
1/2ts salt
raisins (optional)

Directions:
Melt shortening and cool.
Add sugar, egg, molasses.
Beat Well.
Sift dry ingredients together and add to mixture.
Stir in raisins.
Chill 1-2 hours.
Form dough into 1" balls and roll in sugar.
Bake on greased cookie sheet 8-10 minutes @325
