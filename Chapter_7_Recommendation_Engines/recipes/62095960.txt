Title:
topped microwave jacket potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large potatoes
approx. 20g butter
50g grated cheese
1 small grated carrot
or
6 slices of ham
65g cottage cheese

Directions:
srub the potatoes and prick them all over with a fork. Set the microwave to 9 mins on high and cook the potatoes(you may need a min. more or less depending on the size)Cut a smallish hole in each potato and butter it well.
In a bowl mix the cheese and carrot and divide it equally between the potatoes
or
Cut the ham into 1cm squares and stir into the cottage cheese. Divide the mixture between the potatoes.
Set the microwave to 30 seconds on high. and serve hot. Good as a snack or starter. Tuck in!
