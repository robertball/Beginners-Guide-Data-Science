Title:
Pretzel Parmesan Chicken Bites Recipe
Image:
https://imagesvc.meredithcorp.io/v3/mm/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.media-allrecipes.com%2Fuserphotos%2F4120969.jpg
Ingredients:
Non-stick cooking spray
16  Original Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®
16  popcorn chicken pieces, heated
⅓ cup spaghetti or pizza sauce
½ cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a baking sheet with non-stick spray.
Arrange 16 Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps® on prepared baking sheet. Top each with a piece of the popcorn chicken, a teaspoon of sauce, and a sprinkle of cheese.
Bake in preheated oven until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.

A unique twist on an Italian favorite--cheese, tomatoes, and chicken--this is a fun snack for kids or adults and can be served as a meal, snack, or appetizer.
