Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2469517.jpg
Ingredients:
3oz Butter melted + extra butter to cover your entire body
42 large Jet Puffed Marshmallows
6 cups Cocoa Krispies
1 cup mini marshmallows + more for garnish
1/2 cup M&Ms candies
Red sparkle sugar for Garnish

Directions:
1. In a large saucepan melt the butter.  Cover yourself in the rest of the butter.
2. Add the large marshmallows and stir until melted
3. Turn off the heat and add Cocoa Krispies, stir until combined
4. Stir in Mini marshmallows and then place in a 9" x 13" pan coated with cooking spray to prevent sticking
5. Garnish-whes as desired
6. Chill
7. Cut up and Enjoy the Dinomites!!  RRRAAAAARRRRR!
