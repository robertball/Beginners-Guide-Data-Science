Title:
Pumpkin Pasties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs, slightly beaten
3/4 cup sugar
1 lb. can of pumpkin
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. ginger
1/4 tsp. cloves
1-2/3 cup evaporated milk (1 can)
1/2 tsp. allspice
9oz. pie crust pastry (2packs)

Directions:
Bake the pie filling only in a large greased casserole dish in hot oven (425 degrees) for 15min. Keep oven door closed and reduce temp to moderate (350) and continue baking for 45min or until table knife inserted in center of dish comes out clean. Cool on wire rack.
Roll out pastry crust thin and cut into circles aprox 4" in dia.  Put a spoonful of the cool pumpkin mixture towards one side of the center of the circle.  Fold over the crust into a half circle and firmly crimp edges.  Bake at 450 for 10-12 minutes.
