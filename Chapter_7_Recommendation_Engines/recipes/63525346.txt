Title:
Maxine's Coconut Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of milk
1/2 stick of butter
1 cup sugar
3 tablespoons flour
3 egg yolks
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup sweetened coconut flakes
3 egg whites
1/4 teaspoons baking soda
4 tablespoons sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Boil 2 cups of milk and 1/2 stick of butter.  Add to sugar, flour, egg yolks and vanilla. Boil until thick.
Mix in 1 cup of sweetened coconut.
Pour into cooked pie crust.
To make meringue, mix egg whites, baking soda, sugar and vanilla and beat on high speed until stiff peaks form. Spread onto pie filling and bake until browned.

Submitter: 
Dale Tadlock
Description: 
One of my favorite treats growing up was a slice of pie from Miss Maxine at either K's Korner or the Craig family restaurant.  This adaptable recipe is great for a quick, easy and delicious pie!
