Title:
Chocolate fudge banana bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4546743.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup all purpose flour
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup olive oil or applesauce
1/2 tsp baking powder
1 3.9oz chocolate fudge pudding mix
1 cup over ripe banana (about 2 bananas)
3 large eggs
1 cup chocolate chips (I like mini milk chocolate chips)

Directions:
Grease a loaf pan.
Pre heat oven to 350 degrees
Mix dry ingredients together in medium bowl (salt, sugar, baking powder, flour, pudding mix) then stir in eggs, oil, mashed banana and 3/4 of the chocolate chips.
Pour mixture into loaf pan and sprinkle the rest of the chocolate chips on top.
Bake for 1hr and 20min-- or until toothpick is clean method. (Yields one loaf)
