Title:
Shrimp and Snow Pea Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. canola oil
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 Tbsp. minced ginger
1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
1 cup snow peas
4 scallions, white and green parts, thinly sliced
1 lb. large shrimp, peeled and deveined
3 Tbsp. soy sauce
2 tsp. toasted sesame oil

Directions:
Heat the canola oil in a large skillet or wok over med-high heat. Add the garlic and ginger, stirring constantly for about a minute. Add the red bell pepper, snow peas, and scallions, and stir-fry about 2-3 minutes, until the veggies are tender. Add the shrimp, and cook 2-3 minutes, until theyâ€™re pink and cooked through. Stir in the soy sauce and sesame oil. Pair with steamed rice.
