Title:
30-Minute Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/443139.jpg
Ingredients:
1-lb lean ground beef
2t onion powder
1 1/2t garlic powder
1t cumin powder
1/2t salt
1/4t black pepper
1/4t cayenne pepper
1 32-oz container of natural beef or chicken broth
1 6-oz can tomato paste
3T chili powder
1T sugar
2 15-oz cans pinto beans, drained and rinsed
1 15-oz can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
3T chopped fresh cilantro
To thicken mix 3T corn starch,6T water until smooth, set aside until needed.

Directions:
On med/high heat, brown the ground beef in a dutch oven drain off grease.  Add onion powder, garlic powder, cumin powder, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper, stir.
Add the broth, tomato paste, chili powder and sugar.  Bring to a boil then reduce heat to low.  Add beans and simmer covered for 30 minutes stirring occasionally.  Add cilantro and corn starch/water mixture during the last 5 minutes of cooking.

Submitter: 
Traci-in-Cali
Description: 
This chili has a good flavor; the tomato paste gives a rich tomato flavor to the base.  My kids don't like seeing onion or tomato chunks so this recipe makes everyone in our house happy!
