Title:
Sauce Baked Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meatballs:
1 egg
1/2 cup tomato juice
1 cup rolled oats (or crushed soda crackers)
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/4 tsp. garlic powder (or 1 clove fresh garlic, crushed)
1 tsp. chili powder
1 1/2lbs lean ground beef
Sauce:
3/4 cup ketchup
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 tsp. liquid smoke (optional), or more, to taste
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Mix first 9 ingredients well.
Shape into meatballs.
Place in baking dish or roasting pan large enough to hold in a single layer.
Mix sauce ingredients.
Pour over meatballs.
Bake, covered, in 350 oven x 1 hr.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A delicious tangy, smoky sauce baked over meatballs.
