Title:
pumpkin squares Recipe

1/2 cup butter
1/3 cup sugar

3 egg yolks
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup milk
1/2 tsp salt
2 1/2 - 3 teaspoon cinnamon
1 envelope gelatin
1/4 cup cold water
3 egg whites
1/4 cup sugar
cool whip
walnuts (optional)

Directions:
1 1/3 cups graham cracker crumbs
1/2 cup butter
1/3 cup sugar
Mix these 3 items together and put into 9 x 13 pan.
2 eggs
3/4 cup sugar
8oz pkg soft cream cheese
Beat these 3 items together unitl fluffy.
Pour over graham cracker crust.
Bake 20-25 min. at 350 degrees.
1 can 16 oz can pumpkin
3 egg yolks
1/2 cup milk
1/2 tsp salt
2 1/2 -3 tsp. cinnamon.
Mix these 6 items together and cook over boiling water in a double boiler, stirring frequently until thick about 5 minutes.
1 envelope gelatin.
1/4 cup cold water.
Mix together in a small saucepan. Stir over low heat until disssolved. Stir into pumpkin mixture. - Cool.
3 egg whites
1/4 cup sugar
Beat egg whites until foamy.
Gradually beat in sugar.
Beat until stiff and glossy. Gently fold beaten egg whites into pumpkin mixture, pour over baked crust. Refrigerate.
Just before serving add cool whip over the top. Garnish with chopped walnuts if desired. Cut into squares and enjoy.
