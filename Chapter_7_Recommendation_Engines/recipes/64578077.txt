Title:
Deep Dish Hawaiian Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28-oz can pork n beans
1 19-oz can pineapple tidbits (or 1 fresh pineapple)
1 lb chopped cooked ham (or weiners if preferred)
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped green pepper
1/2 cup chopped celery
1 apple, diced

Directions:
Do not pre-cook anything.
Place all ingredients well mixed into a casserole dish.
Cook for 1 hour in 350 oven.
