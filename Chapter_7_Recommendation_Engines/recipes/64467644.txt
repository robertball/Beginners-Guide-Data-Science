Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2453253.jpg
Ingredients:
1lb prehistoric lean meat
1 onion
3 TBSP tomato paste
1TBSP sifted flour
Pinch of cinnamon
1/8 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
1/2 to 3/4 cup beef broth
12 mini pie shells with tops
1 cup chopped dinosaur kale (Tuscan kale)

Directions:
Cook prehistoric meat and chopped onion in a large frying pan.  Add tomato paste and spices.
In a separate bowl whisk flour and 1/2 of broth.  Add remaining broth, mix well.  Add entire mix to meat mixture and bring to a boil.  Cook for 2 minutes or until thickened.
Remove from heat, cover and refrigerate until chilled.
Fill each of the pie shells with a portion of the meat mixture.  Cover each "pie" with a pie top.
Bake at 420F for 15 minutes.  Let stand for 5 minutes,
Garnish each mini pie with chopped kale.  Enjoy.
