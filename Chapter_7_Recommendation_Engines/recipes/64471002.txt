Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2469616.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 cup smooth peanut butter
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1 large egg
2 tsp vanilla
So many Red icing, for drizzling
Five Inches of Chocolate sauce, for drizzling

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350f.
Mix flour and baking powder in a small bowl and set aside.
Mix together remaining ingredients into a large bowl.
Beat until light and fluffy.
Fold in flour and baking powder until incorporated.
Roll out teaspoon size balls and place on cookie sheet.
Using a Trex toy, squish cookie down in center with his foot.
Bake for 6-10 minutes.
Drizzle with so many red frosting and use exactly five inches of chocolate sauce.
Now share with your friends and family!
