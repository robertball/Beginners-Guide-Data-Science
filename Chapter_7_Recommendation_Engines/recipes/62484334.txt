Title:
Black Bean 'n' Pumpkin Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/487940.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, chopped or use onion powder
1 medium sweet yellow pepper, chopped or use dried
3 garlic cloves, minced or garlic powder
3 cups chicken broth
2 cans (15 ounces each) black beans, (one seasoned, one unseasoned)rinsed and drained
2-1/2 cups cubed smoked chicken thighs (about 3)
1 can (15 ounces) solid-pack pumpkin
1 can (14-1/2 ounces) diced tomatoes, undrained
2 teaspoons dried parsley flakes
1 teaspoon chili powder
1 teaspoon chipotle chili powder
1-1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
1-1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a large skillet, saute the onion, yellow pepper in oil until tender. Add garlic; cook 1 minute longer. Transfer to a 5-qt. slow cooker; stir in the remaining ingredients. Cover and cook on low for 4-5 hours or until heated through.
