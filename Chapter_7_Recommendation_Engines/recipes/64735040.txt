Title:
Instant Pot Vegetable beef  Soup low Carb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6243411.jpg
Ingredients:
Chuck roast cubes
Pound of green beans trimmed and sliced
3 large Turnips peeled and diced
5 large carrots
2 celery sticks
1 large yellow onion
2 TB Garlic powder
1 TB Olive oil
2 tablespoons tomato paste
2 bay leaves
1TB onion powder
2 TB salt
1 Tb celery seed

Directions:
On the sauté setting, sauté  beef chunks until brown on all sides. Remove from pot, then add diced onion, carrot and celery until translucent. Add in meat, turnips, green beans 6 cups of water, salt, onion powder, bay leaves, garlic, tomato paste and celery seed. Set the instant pot to high pressure for 30 minutes.
At this point, taste the soup. Add more garlic, onion salt and pepper if needed. Sometimes we mix 1 Tb of xanthin gum in water and add to the soup to thicken it up.
