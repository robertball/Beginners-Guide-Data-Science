Title:
Jan's Cranberry Sweet Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/347282.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package active dry yeast
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup warm water or milk
1/2 cup melted butter
2 large eggs
1/2 cup mashed baked potato (mash well, no lumps!)
1/2 tsp salt
4 to 5 cups bread flour
1/4 cup vital wheat gluten
2 tablespoons dry, powdered, vanilla extract
1 cup dried cranberries

Directions:
Add ingredients to your bread machine as directed and choose the regular dough cycle.
Add cranberries in when prompted by your specific machine.
Once dough is finished turn out onto a lightly floured surface. Divide dough into 8 portions and shape into balls.  Place balls of dough in a greased 9 x 11 pan.  Cover and let rise until doubled in size.
Bake 350* 25 to 30 minutes or golden brown.
