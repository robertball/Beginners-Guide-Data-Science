Title:
LAURA'S THANKSGIVING DRESSING Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boxes stove top dressing-turkey
Chicken broth for stuffing
1 box uncle ben's lg grain wild rice
1 can sliced water chestnuts
1 lb ground pork-unseasoned
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped celery

Directions:
Prepare stuffing with broth instead of water
prepare rice, less 3/4 water than directions on box
Drain water chestnuts and slice half
Brown and drain pork
Combine and serve
