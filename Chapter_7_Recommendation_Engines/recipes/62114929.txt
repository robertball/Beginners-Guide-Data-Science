Title:
Herbed Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
pastry for 9 in double crust pie
2 tbsp butter
2 cups frozen mixed vegetables
2 1/2 cups diced, cooked chicken
2 heaping tbsp flour
2 cups fat-free, low sodium chicken broth
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp sage
1 tsp marjoram
1/2 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Heat oven to 450 degrees.
Place one crust in nine inch pie pan and prick bottom several times with a fork.
Bake crust about ten minutes or until golden.
Remove from oven, set aside, and reduce oven temp to 350 degrees.
Meanwhile, melt butter in skillet over medium heat.
Add mixed vegetables, cook and stir about four minutes.
Add chicken to skillet.
Sprinkle flour over chicken and vegetables and stir well.
Add chicken broth to skillet.
Stir in salt, pepper, sage, marjoram, and garlic powder.
Bring mixture to a boil, stirring constantly, until it thickens slightly.
Remove from heat and pour into pre-baked crust.
Top with second crust, seal and crimp edges.
Cut two or three small slits in top of crust and bake pie for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
Let sit for ten minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
A.S.G.
Description: 
An easy-to-assemble pot pie flavored with herbs.  My favorite way to use up leftover chicken, and the kids love it.  No need to use condensed soup for a creamy texture and wonderful taste.
