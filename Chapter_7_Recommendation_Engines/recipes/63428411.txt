Title:
Chicken Broccoli Hot Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 oz bag frozen broccoli
4 c cooked chicken
1 can cr of mushroom soup
1/2 c sour cream
1/2 c mayonnaise
8 slices of Velveeta cheese
1 can French-fried onion rings

Directions:
In 9x13 inch baking pan layer broccoli, then chicken, then mixture of soup, sour cream and mayo. Top with layer of cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes.
Top with onion rings and bake for 10 minutes more.
