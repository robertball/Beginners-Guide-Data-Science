Title:
** Colorado Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. old-fashioned rolled oats (not quick cooking or instant)
1 1/2 c. sliced almonds (or an almond/chopped pecan mix)
1 c. coconut
3 Tbl. Grape Nuts cereal
1 tsp. cinnamon
4 Tbl. melted butter
2 Tbl. olive oil
1/2 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. honey
Pinch salt
2 tsp. vanilla
1 c. dried fruit

Directions:
Heat oven to 325 degrees.
In lg. bowl, combine oats, nuts, coconut, sesame seeds & cinnamon.
In med. bowl, whisk together melted butter, olive oil, sugar, honey, salt & vanilla. Pour over oat mixture & combine.
Turn mixture into a (large) greased baking sheet. Place into oven.
Set timer for 10 min. Stir every 10 min. until well toasted, about 40-50 min. total.
Remove from oven; stir in dried fruit.
Granola will crisp up as it cools. Break up into small crunchy nuggets.
Store in glass jar or resealable plastic bag.
