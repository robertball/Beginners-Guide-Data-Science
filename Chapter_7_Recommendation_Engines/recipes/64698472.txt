Title:
Chicken and Chorizo Paella Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sofrito:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 cup diced yellow onion
1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
1 tbsp minced garlic
1 cup crushed tomatoes
1/2 tsp smoked paprika
1 bay leaf
Paella:
3.5 cups low sodium chicken broth
1 tbsp olive oil
10 ounces chicken breast, cut into 1" pieces
4 ounces Spanish-style chorizo, diced
2 tsp smoke paprika
1.5 tsp turmeric
1.5 cups Spanish paella rice
1/3 cup frozen peas
1 tbsp capers
2 lemons

Directions:
Saute onions for 5-8 minutes.
Add red pepper and garlic and cook 2-3 minutes.
Add tomatoes, 1/2 tsp paprika and bay leaf.
Reduce heat to medium and cook until sauce is thick, about 10 minutes.
In a separate pot, bring chicken broth to simmer.
In a separate skillet, fry chicken until cooked. Remove chicken.
Cook chorizo for 1 minute.
Add spices and toast for 30 seconds.
Stir in Sofrito to make a paste with the spices.
Add 3 cups of hot broth and stir in rice.
Add chicken to the pan and let boil for 5 minutes.
Reduce heat to simmer for 10 minutes while covered.
Sprinkle peas and capers over the rice and cover, let rest for 10 minutes.
Garnish paella with lemon wedges and serve straight from pan.

Description: 
This paella resembles what most recognize as classic paella. Paella can be enjoyed year round but is especially good during warm weather months, when you can cover the paella and finish cooking it on a grill for a traditionally smoky note.
