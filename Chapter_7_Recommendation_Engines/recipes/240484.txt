Title:
Cranberry Sauce with Apple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1773964.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) can whole berry cranberry sauce
1 small apple, diced
1 small orange, juiced
1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
1 dash ground cinnamon

Directions:
Stir cranberry sauce, apple, orange juice, cloves, and cinnamon together in a bowl.
Refrigerate sauce for at least 4 hours before serving.
