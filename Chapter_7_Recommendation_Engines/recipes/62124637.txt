Title:
Chicken Goddess Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of Angel Hair Pasta----Small box
2 1/2 Chicken Breast  ( Take bones out )
Small Jar of Calamata Olives
1 pound of Asperagus
1/4 cup of finely sliced Red Onion
1/3 cup of Pine Nuts  ( Roasted )
Large Handful of Spinach
1/3 cup of Light Olive Oil
Pat of Butter

Directions:
Pan Roast Pine Nuts
Get your Noodles boiled and drained
Brown Chicken Breasts using Salt & Pepper for flavor
Cut tops off Asperagus and cut stalks in 1/2" pieces and steam to ( al dente )
In a (Heated) large pan use 1/2 of the oil and combine the chicken, asperagus, SD tomatoes, pine nuts, onions, olives and noodles.
Add the rest of the oil
Add Spinach and let it wilt
Keep on heat and stir until throughly warm. (most of the small stuff will fall to the bottom)
When it's heated to your liking serve on a platter that way when you dump it onto the platter all the yummy small stuff will end up on top and it even looks AWSOME.
