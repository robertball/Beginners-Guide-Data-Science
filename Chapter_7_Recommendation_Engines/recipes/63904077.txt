Title:
Cream Cheese Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages crescent rolls
2 packages cream cheese 8oz each
3/4 C Sugar (scant)
1/8 C lemon juice
1 egg yolk
1 1/2 t vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F., and spray Pam on a 10' x13" pan.
Lay one package of rolls out on the bottom of the pan, seal the seams as best as you can. Spread cream cheese mixture on top.  Lay the second package of rolls out on wax paper and seal the seams with your fingers. Lay this on top of cream cheese mixture.  Brush the top with the egg whites, with a pastry brush.  Bake 350 degrees F., until golden brown.  Approx 45 minutes, but all ovens are different.
