Title:
Raw Creamy Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stalk of celery
1 large carrots, chopped
1 zucchini, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1/4 cup olive oil
1/5 cup water or herbal tea
1 clove garlic
1 tsp sea salt

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in a blender until warm.
Enjoy
