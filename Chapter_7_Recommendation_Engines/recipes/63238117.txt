Title:
Balthazar's Dog Food Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/843991.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups brown rice, uncooked
2 cups (1 lb.) chicken breast (2 large breasts)
2 cups (1 lb.) lean ground beef
1 (32 oz.) bag mixed, frozen vegetables (peas, carrots, green beans, lima beans-NO ONIONS).
1 1/2 cups skin-on salmon, cooked (optional)
1 (12 oz.) container low-fat (1%) cottage cheese

Directions:
Pour frozen vegetables in large mixing bowl. Set aside.
Set a large saucepan over medium-heat and add 1 Tbls. olive oil. Let heat 2 minutes, then add uncooked brown rice. Stir occasionally 3 minutes. Add 1 Tbls. chicken-flavored better than bouillon or 2 bouillon cubes. Pour 3 cups water in and bring to a boil. Turn down to low, cover and let simmer 40 minutes. Take off heat, uncover, stir, replace cover and let sit 5 minutes. Pour steaming rice over frozen vegetables.
Poach chicken breasts in simmering water 15-20 minutes. Let cool and dice. Add to rice/vegetable mixture.
Brown ground beef, drain and add to mixing bowl. Pour in container of cottage cheese and stir to combine.
Optional: Add shredded salmon.
Serving size: For a 100lb. dog, combine 1 cup kibble and 1 cup homemade food, twice a day

Submitter: 
C R Henning
Description: 
All natural ingredients are used in this simple dog food. You can feel good about giving your dog real food! It makes about 13 cups, so I freeze half of it.
This is also good for a sensitive stomach or after a bout with illness.
