Title:
Frozen Banana Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978007.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (7 ounce) tub BAKER'S Real Dark Semi-Sweet Dipping Chocolate
6 small bananas, peeled, cut crosswise in half
2 cups POST Cocoa PEBBLES Cereal

Directions:
Melt chocolate as directed on package. Meanwhile, insert a wooden pop stick into cut end of each banana piece.
Dip bananas in chocolate, or brush chocolate onto banana pieces with pastry brush. Roll bananas in cereal, completely coating all sides.
Freeze bananas 4 hours or until firm. Store in airtight container in freezer.

Submitter: 
BAKER'S Chocolate
Description: 
Crispy cereal adds crunch to these frozen chocolate-covered bananas.
