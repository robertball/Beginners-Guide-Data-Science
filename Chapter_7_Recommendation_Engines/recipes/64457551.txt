Title:
Chef John's Vegan Shrimp Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Poaching Liquid:
3 c water
1/4 onion, sliced
1/2 lemon
2 cloves garlic, peeled and bruised
2 sprigs fresh tarragon (or 1 tsp dry)
1 tablespoon seafood seasoning (such as Old BayÂ®)
1/2 teaspoon whole black peppercorns
1 bay leaf
1-2 T fish sauce (opt to taste)
Cocktail Sauce:
1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 cup chili sauce
1/4 cup prepared horseradish
2 teaspoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
3 drops hot sauce, or to taste
1 pinch salt
2 c Butler soy curls, broken into small pieces

Directions:
Stir water, onion, lemon, garlic, tarragon, seafood seasoning, peppercorns, and bay leaf together in a large pot; bring to a simmer and cook until flavors blend, about 15 minutes. Let cool a bit. Add soy curls and soak for 10 min, drain well. Put in small bowl and chill well.
Whisk ketchup, chili sauce, horseradish, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, and salt together in a bowl; refrigerate until chilled, at least 15 minutes.
Mix "shrimp" into the bowl of chilled sauce. Serve in cocktail glasses.
