Title:
Whipped Chocolate Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs, separated
1 bag semisweet chips (1 3/4 cups)
16 oz container cool whip
2 Tbsp sugar
2 graham cracker crusts

Directions:
Melt the chocolate chips over a double boiler until smooth.  Transfer to a large mixing bowl and beat in egg yolks 1 at a time, mixing well after each addition.
Using clean beaters, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form, adding 2 Tbsp sugar.
Fold 1/4 of the meringue into the chocolate/yolk mixture and mix well.  Fold in 3/4 of the cool whip and remaining meringue.
Pour chocolate mousse evenly into graham cracker crusts or dessert glasses.
Chill.  Serve with remaining cool whip.

Submitter: 
BOILERMISH
Description: 
Creamy chocolate mousse made with semisweet chips, egg yolks, meringue, and cool whip.  A family favorite for years.  Can be served on graham cracker crusts or individual dessert glasses.
