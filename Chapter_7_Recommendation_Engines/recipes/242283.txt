Title:
Fuzzy Navel Smoothie - Alcohol-Free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2355042.jpg
Ingredients:
2 fresh peaches - peeled, pitted, and diced
12 ice cubes
1 cup orange juice, or more to taste
1 (6 ounce) container orange yogurt
2 tablespoons frozen orange juice concentrate

Directions:
Blend peaches in a blender until smooth. Add ice cubes, orange juice, orange yogurt, and orange juice concentrate; blend until desired consistency is reached.
