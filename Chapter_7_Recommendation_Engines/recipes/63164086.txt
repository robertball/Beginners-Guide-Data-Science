Title:
Huevos Rancheros Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 whole eggs
1 tablespoon oil
1/2 whole onion, minced
1 teaspoon minced garlic
16 ounces canned black beans, drained and rinsed
1 whole avocado, peeled & diced
1 whole tomato, chopped

Directions:
Poach eggs to desired doneness.
In a skillet, heat oil and saute onion and garlic until softened.  Add beans and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Divide beans among serving plates, top with poached eggs and serve with avocado and chopped tomato.
