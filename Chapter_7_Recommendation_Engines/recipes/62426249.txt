Title:
Healthy Shrimp Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 medium sized shrimp (peeled and deveined)
8 small whole-wheat tortillas
2 cups iceberg lettuce, shredded
1 red pepper, diced
1/2 cup corn kernels
1/2 cup tomato salsa
1/2 cup cilantro leaves
1/4 cup lime juice (2 limes)
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl combine shrimp, lime juice, olive oil, cumin, salt and pepper and toss to coat.  Place shrimp on an oiled grill on medium high heat and cook until shrimp are pink and no longer translucent (approximately 2-3 minutes per side).
Assemble tacos by placing three shrimp down the center of each tortilla.
Top with 1 tbs. each of cilantro, corn, salsa and red pepper, and 1/4 cup of lettuce.
Fold tortilla in half to form taco.
