Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Stew with Pepper & Pineapple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. chicken breast, cut into 1 1/2 inch pieces
4 medium carrots, cut into 1-inch pieces
1/2 cup chiken broth
2 TBSP gingeroot, finley chopped or 1 tsp ground ginger
1 TBSP packed brown sugar
2 TBSP soy sauce
1/2 tsp ground allspice
1/2 tsp red pepper sauce
1 can (8 oz) pineapple chunks in juice, drained and juice reserved
1 TBSP cornstartch
1 med bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except pineapple, cornstarch and bell pepper in 1 1/2 - 6 quart slow cooker.
Cover and cook on low heat setting 7 to 8 hours (or high heat setting 3 to 4 hours) or until gegetables are tender and chicken is no longer pink in center.
Mix reserved pineapple jice and cornstartch until smooth, gradually stir into chicken mixture.  Stir in pineapple and bell pepper.
Cover and cook on high heat setting about 15 minutes or until slightly thickened.

Submitter: 
Shannon Campbell
Description: 
The yummy gingery-flavored sauce is delicious!  To get everylast drop, spoon stewed chick, pepper and pineapple over hot cooked rice.
