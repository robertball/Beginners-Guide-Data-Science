Title:
Loaded Baked Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 lbs of peeled baking potatoes
1 40 oz can of chicken broth
3 bricks of cream cheese
2 tubs of sour cream
2 packages thick cut bacon
1 onion
2 sticks of butter
cup of half & half
crushed garlic

Directions:
Chop bacon into tiny pieces, sautee with one onion diced into little pieces. Dump into crockpot.
Peel and chop potatoes into bite-sized pieces. Boil until mashable.
Split potatoes into 1 third and 2 thirds. Dump the smaller portion into the crockpot. Mash the larger portion. Once mashed, add the chicken broth, cream cheese, sour cream, half & half, butter, and garlic to taste.
Top with shredded cheddar, scallions, and/or oyster crackers.

Submitter: 
Christine
Description: 
Tastes just like a restaurant-style loaded baked potato.
