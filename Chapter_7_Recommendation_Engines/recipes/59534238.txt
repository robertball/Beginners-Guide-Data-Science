Title:
Budget Actually Delicious Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/270005.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 pound package ground turkey
1/3 cup panko, or seasoned bread crumbs
salt and pepper to taste
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon all purpose seasoning
1 egg
1/2 cup french fried onions (optional)

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix ground turkey, bread crumbs, onion, egg, all purpose seasoning, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Form into 5 patties.
Grill the patties in a over medium -high heat, turning once, to an internal temperature of 180 degrees F (85 degrees C).

These burgers not only taste great but they are good for you too! Keep these burger simple and inexpensive by seasoning them with what you have on hand.Modified from: Actually Delicious Turkey Burgers
