Title:
Mini breakfast frittatas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch of asparagus, cut into 1 inch pieces
1/4 lb spinach
4 sun-dried tomato halves
1 tsp dill
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp basil
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1-1/2 cups egg whites
2 eggs
2 green onions, diced
1 lb uncured bacon, cooked and cut up
3/4 cups shredded cheese (Parmesan, part skim mozzarella)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F
Cook bacon, let cool and cut up
Add asparagus, spinach and sun-dried tomatoes to food processor and blend.
Add dill, oregano, basil, egg whites and eggs to food processor and blend.
Stir (do NOT use food processor) in green onions and bacon.
Spray canola oil into silicon cupcake molds. Fill mold with mixture and top with shredded cheese.
Back for 20 - 30 minutes until cheese is golden brown and toothpick inserted comes out clean.
Serve immediately or refrigerate and reheat to serve.
