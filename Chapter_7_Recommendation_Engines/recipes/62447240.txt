Title:
Oven Baked Chicken Teriyaki Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBS cornstarch
2 TBS water
1 C low-sodium soy sauce
1/2 C sugar
4 TBS mirin
4 tsp grated fresh ginger
3-4 garlic cloves, minced
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Combine cornstarch and water in small bowl to dissolve.  In small saucepan combine remaining ingredients except chicken.  Warm over medium high heat and slowly whisk in cornstarch mixture.  Bring to a boil then reduce heat to medium low and simmer until thick, about 10 minutes.  Meanwhile, pat chicken dry with paper towels and place in baking dish.  Pour sauce over top and coat all sides of chicken.  Bake for 20-25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jennifer
Description: 
Wonderful baked chicken with minimal mess to make for easy clean up.  My daughter says it's her favorite teriyaki chicken ever!
