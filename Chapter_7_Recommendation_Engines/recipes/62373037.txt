Title:
World's Best Bloody Mary Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/412548.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tsp ground celery seed
1 tsp garlic salt
1 tsp pepper
1/3 cup steak sauce
3 tbsp Hickory BBQ sauce
1 46 oz can Tomato Juice
cayenne pepper to taste
lemon wedge (optional)
celery stick (optional)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a pitcher and stir until well blended. Serve over ice with a dash of cayenne pepper, lemon wedge, and celery stick.

Submitter: 
Michelle Black
Description: 
I drink virgin bloody mary's for breakfast pretty regularly. To me this is the perfect marriage of flavor for those of you who enjoy this delicious drink. Add alcohol when you're feeling extra zippy :D
