Title:
LTF DTox: Pumpkin Spice Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup pumpkin puree
1/4 cup fine coconut shreds
1/4 cup coconut flour
1 large green-tipped banana
1 tbsp cinnamon
2 tbsp melted coconut oil
1 tbsp stevia (optional)

Directions:
1. Heat oven to 350 degrees
2. Place all ingredients in a food processor and mix until ingredients are thoroughly combined
3. Roll into walnut size balls and place on baking sheet lined with parchment paper
4. Use a fork to slightly flatten
5. Bake 20 minutes.
6. Let cool completely before serving.
