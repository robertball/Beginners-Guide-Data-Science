Title:
Blue Corn Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup blue corn meal
1 cup unbleached flour
5 [yes 5] teaspoons baking powder
4 tablespoons sugar
pinch of salt
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1 stick salted butter
8 x 12 oval straight sided stainless au gratin pan or
9 x 9 brownie pan

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Sift the dry ingredients 3 times. Yes, 3 times!
On medium heat, melt the stick of butter into baking pan
While butter is melting, mix milk and eggs into dry ingredients
When butter is melted and still hot, stir well into batter
pour batter back into hot baking pan.
Bake on center rack in oven for 30 to 35 minutes.
It is done when the blue cornbread pulls away from the sides of the pan.

Submitter: 
Bill Jezzard
Description: 
Corn bread made with blue corn meal and more of almost every ingredient you would put in regular corn bread. It is almost cake-like.
This is a pot luck winner.
