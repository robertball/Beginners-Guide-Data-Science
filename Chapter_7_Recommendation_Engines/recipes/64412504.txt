Title:
Grilled Garlic Basil Chicken with Basil Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2280651.jpg
Ingredients:
1 boneless skinless chicken breast
Salt, pepper, garlic powder
Marinade:
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
2 Tablespoon White raspberry baslasmic vinegar (or any baslasmic vinegar)
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
2 tsp Dijon Mustard
1/2 small white onion, sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup packed basil, roughly chopped
Salt and pepper
Sauce:
2 Tablespoons extra virgin Olive oil
Zest of one lime
Juice of one lime, may need more
1 clove of garlic, grated with small grater
1/4 white onion, minced very fine
One bunch of basil (about 6 leaves) chopped very small
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix together all of the marinade ingredients. Pour into large zip lock bag.
Season chicken with salt, pepper and garlic powder. Add chicken to zip lock bag. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour but no more than overnight. Massaging bag every few hours.
Cook chicken in pre-heated grill for 8-10 minutes on each side depending on the thickness of the chicken until an internal temperature of 165 Fahrenheit is reached.
Remove chicken from grill and let rest for 5 minutes.
Meanwhile wisk sauce together.
Cut chicken and pour sauce on top.
