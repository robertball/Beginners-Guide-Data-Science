Title:
Stir Fry Chicken and Vegetables in Peanut Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Chicken Breast halves
1 10 oz bag frozen Broccoli
1 1/2 cups sliced Carrots
1 can sliced Waterchestnuts
1 bunch green onions - chopped
2 tbsp Olive Oil
15 drops Sesame Oil
1 bottle Bangkok Padang Peanut Sauce
White Rice - cooked as directed

Directions:
Slice chicken breast halves into bite sized pieces.
Heat Olive oil and Sesame oil in a large skillet.
Saute chicken breasts until they are no longer pink.
Add broccoli, carrots and waterchestnuts and green onions.  Saute until heated through.
Pour bottle of peanut sauce over the chicken and vegetables and bring to a boil.
Serve over cooked white rice with extra sauce on the side, if you wish.
Feel free to substitute your favorite stir fry vegetables or add additional vegetables.
