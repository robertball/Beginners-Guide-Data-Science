Title:
Mozzarella Mushroom Gnocchi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/601910.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package gnocchi
3 tbsp butter
1 1/2 tbsp minced garlic
1 7oz can mushrooms
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella
1/2 ts Italian seasoning
Salt to taste

Directions:
Cook Gnocchi according to package directions.
In separate sauce pan mix butter, garlic and mushrooms until heated through.
Drain gnocchi and add to the sauce pan mixture.
Add mozzarella and stir until melted.
Sprinkle with Italian seasoning and add Salt to taste.

Submitter: 
My4boys
Description: 
Mozzarella, mushrooms, garlic and butter make this gnocchi a wonderful side dish to add to your dinner. Try with a Italian seasoned chicken.
