Title:
Slow cooker Fiesta Chicken by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast
1 packet Fiesta Ranch dressing mix
1 can black beans
1 can Rotel tomatoes
1 can corn
1 block cream cheese

Directions:
Place all in crock pot, cook 4-6 hours on low.
Serve over rice or with tortillas
