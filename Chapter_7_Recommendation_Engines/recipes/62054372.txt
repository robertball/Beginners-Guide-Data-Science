Title:
Corn Bread Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small boxes corn bread mix
20-30 cherry tomatoes (cut in half)
2 bunches green onions
4-5 stalks celery
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup mayo
1 green pepper

Directions:
Bake corn bread mix as directed, cool & crumble into bowl
Dice onions, pepper, and celery
Mix all ingredients in bowl, refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
SACHABEAR
Description: 
A wonderful lady at church shared this recipe resulting in a gourmet salad with everyday ingredients.  Super simple, and pretty to look at, this would be a wonderful salad for brunches and picnics!
