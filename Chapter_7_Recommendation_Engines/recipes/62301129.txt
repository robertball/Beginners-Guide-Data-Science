Title:
Ham and Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/364834.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups egg noodles
1 medium onion
1/2 cup sour cream
1 can cream of chicken soup
salt and pepper to taste
2 cups diced cooked ham
2 cups swiss cheese
1/4 cup bread crumbs

Directions:
Drop noodles into 6 cups rapidly boiling water. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 3 minutes; cover and remove from heat. Let stand 10 minutes; drain.
Heat oven to 350 F. Stir the onion, sour cream, chicken soup, salt and pepper into the noodles. Stir in ham and swiss cheese. Pour into a greased 2-quart casserole dish. Sprinkle with bread crumbs.
Bake uncovered for 40-45 minutes

Submitter: 
Kelli F
Description: 
This is one of my favorite ways to use up leftover ham.
