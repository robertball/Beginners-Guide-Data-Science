Title:
Spinach and Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz package frozen chopped spinach
10 oz angel hair pasta broken into 1 inch pieces
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 small onion chopped
1 garlic clove chopped
1 package lipton vegetable soup mix
1 cup Miracle Whip salad dressing
1/4 cup ranch dressing
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon season salt

Directions:
In small frying pan saute onion and garlic in olive oil until tender.
In large sauce pan bring 3 cups water to boil
Add soup mix, spinach and pasta and boil 3 to 4 minutes
Drain well
In medium bowl mix salad dressing, ranch dressing, parmesan cheese and season salt
add onion garlic mixture and toss with pasta and spinach.
Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Nita Rockwell Minto
Description: 
Pasta salad with spinach a touch of ranch.  Great for a side dish at your next summer gathering.
