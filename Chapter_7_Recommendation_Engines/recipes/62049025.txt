Title:
Cool Whip Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients are included in recipe directions.... :)

Directions:
1 cup flour
2 tbsp. sugar
1/2 stick margarine
1/2 cup chopped nuts
Mix until crumbly.  Press into a buttered 9x12" pan (make a double batch, if necessary).  Bake at 350 degrees until light brown--about 12 minutes.
1 pkg. (8 oz.) cream cheese, softened
2 cups Cool Whip topping (thawed)
1 cup powdered sugar
Mix together with an electric mixer.  Spread mixture evenly onto a cooled crust.
Mix 2 small packages chocolate INSTANT pudding with 3 cups milk until thickened.  Spread evenly over cream cheese mixture--refrigerate.  When chocolate layer is firm, spread a room temperature layer of Cool Whip over the chocolate layer.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.
VARIATION
Decrease milk to 2 1/2 cups and
add 1/2 cup Kahlua.

Submitter: 
SARA R
Description: 
This is a wonderful chocolate pudding/cool whip dessert.  You can vary the taste (for the adults) with a little Kahlua...
