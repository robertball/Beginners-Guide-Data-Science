Title:
Greek Yogurt Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup applesauce
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
3 ripe bananas, mashed
1 cup non-fat Greek yogurt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
pinch nutmeg
pinch cloves
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 1/4 cup whole wheat flour
1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
1/8 cup white sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Spray two loaf pans, approximately 9x5 inches, with cooking spray.
In a large bowl, cream butter, applesauce and sugars. Mix in eggs, mashed bananas, yogurt, vanilla,1 teaspoon cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves. Add salt, baking soda and flours, mixing by hand. Stir in nuts if using.  Divide into prepared pans.
Combine 1/8 cup white sugar and 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon, and sprinkle on top of bread.
Bake for approximately 70 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.  Cool on baking rack.

Submitter: 
Prez Richards
Description: 
Calories are cut, but taste is not, when you substitute thick non-fat Greek yogurt for sour cream.  Substituting applesauce for most of the butter also makes for a moist bread.Modified from: Banana Sour Cream Bread
