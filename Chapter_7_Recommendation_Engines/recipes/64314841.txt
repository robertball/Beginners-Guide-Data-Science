Title:
Creamy polenta with parmesan and arugula Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
2 cups milk (1% is okay, whole is best)
1 cup yellow cornmeal
2 cups chopped arugula, cress or other bitter lettuce
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 Roma tomatoes, seeded and sliced
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil broth and milk in a pot over high heat.
Slowly sprinkle in cornmeal while whisking.
Reduce heat to low and cook for 5 minutes, whisking constantly.
Off heat, stir in arugula, Parmesan, and tomatoes.
Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Sudie B
Description: 
This is a delicious change from paste, potatoes or rice.
