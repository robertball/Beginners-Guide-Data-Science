Ingredients:
1 large Sweet Potato - peeled and cut into small cubes
60g/2oz Butter
2 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 Onion - very finely chopped
1 tsp Fennel or Caraway Seeds
4 sprigs fresh Thyme - leaves only
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Heat the butter and olive oil in a frying pan over a low medium heat;
When the butter is foaming, add the onion and fry for about 5 minutes, or until just softened;
Add the fennel/caraway, thyme and sweet potato and fry, stirring regularly, for 8 to 10 minutes, or until the sweet potato cubes are tender and golden-brown;
Season to taste with salt and pepper and serve.

Description: 
A fragant twist to sauteed sweet Potatoes. Packed with flavour and delicious with almost any main, especially meat and/or fish.
Also makes a great, tasty appetizer.
