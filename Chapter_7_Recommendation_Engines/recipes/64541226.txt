Title:
Carintas - Pressure Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3168638.jpg
Ingredients:
6 lb Pork Butt
1 Onion Coarsely Chopped
4-5 Garlic Cloves Diced
1 1/2 TBSP Salt
1 tsp Black Pepper
1 TBSP Oregano
2 tsp Cumin
1/2 tsp Chili Pepper
1/2 tsp Paprika
2-3 TBSP Olive Oil
1 Cup Orange Juice

Directions:
- Trim excessive fat from pork butt
- Cut pork butt into 2" cubes and place in a mixing bowl
- In a bowl combine all of the seasonings
- Rub the pork cubes with the seasoning mixture
- LIGHTLY coat the seasoned pork cubes with olive oil
- Place seasoned pork in electric pressure cooker
- Cover with onions, garlic and orange juice
- Cover and lock the lid
- Set pressure to 70 psi
- Cook for 60 minutes
- Remove pork and shred
