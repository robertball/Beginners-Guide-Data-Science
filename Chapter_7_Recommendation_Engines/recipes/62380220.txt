Title:
Orange Julius Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 can frozen concentrated orange juice
1/2 t. vanilla extract
12 oz. ice, crushed
12 oz. Milk

Directions:
Combine ingredients in blender.
Blend until well mixed.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
An egg-free recipe for a semi-frozen, orange-juicy treat.
