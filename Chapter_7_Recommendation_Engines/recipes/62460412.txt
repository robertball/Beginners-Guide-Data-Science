Title:
Dill Cream Cheese, Roast Beef and Cucumber Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/475910.jpg
Ingredients:
8 Ounces Cream Cheese at room temperature
3 T Fresh Dill, Chopped
1/2 Cucumber, peeled and grated
Salt and Pepper, to taste
1/2 Cucumber, peeled and sliced thin
1 Pound Roast Beef, sliced thin
10 Slices Dense, White Bread

Directions:
Combine the cream cheese, dill, and grated cucumber (wrung dry of water), salt and pepper.
Take the bread and spread both sides with cream cheese mixture.
Top the sandwiches with the slices of cucumber and roast beef.
Top with the other slice of bread, cut and serve. Enjoy!!!
