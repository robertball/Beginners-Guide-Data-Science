Title:
Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
broccoli
red onion
sunflower seeds (toasted)
real bacon bits
Dressing:
1 C mayonnaise
1/3 C or less white sugar
2 Tbsp apple cider vinegar

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and let sit for about 30 minutes to let flavors mix together (you can kind of play with this until you get the flavor you want if it is too sweet or vinegary for you).
