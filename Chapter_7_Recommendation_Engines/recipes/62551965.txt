Title:
Stuffed mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 crimini mushrooms
1/4 lb pork sausage, cooked
1/2 cup cooked chopped spinach
1/2 cup shredded jack cheese
1/2 small onion chopped
stems from crimini caps, chopped
1/2 cup seasoned croutons

Directions:
sautee onions and chopped stems until tender. Mix with sausage and spinach. Moisten croutons with a little water and crush them. Mix in 1/4 cup of cheese with all other ingrediants. Fill caps and then sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake at 350 for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Fredda O.
Description: 
A yummy appitizer or serve with a salad for a filling meal.
