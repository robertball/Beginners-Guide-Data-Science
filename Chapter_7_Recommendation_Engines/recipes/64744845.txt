Title:
Cream of Broccoli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c. fresh or frozen (defrosted) broccoli cuts
14 oz. can 99% fat-free chicken stock
2 c. skim milk
3 T. cornstarch
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1 tsp. butter flavoring
4 T. freshly grated Parmesan
4 Parsley sprigs

Directions:
1. Pour chicken stock in a large pan and add broccoli.  Bring to a boil and then turn down to simmer.  Cook uncovers about 8 to 10 min. or until broccoli is tender.
2. Put cornstarch in a microwavable container and add 1/2 to 3/4 c. of the skim milk.  Stir until cornstarch is dissolved. Add remaining milk, pepper, and butter flavoring.  Mix well. Microwave on medium for 2 min. and stir.  Continue microwaving 2 min. and stirring 3 or 4 times until thickened.
3. When broccoli is cooked, add broccoli and chicken stock to blender or food processor. When milk mixture is thickened, add to broccoli and chicken stock.
4. Cover and blend or process until smooth.
