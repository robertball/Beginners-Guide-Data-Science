Title:
Spicy Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/857753.jpg
Ingredients:
1 t. olive oil
1/4 sweet onion, diced small
1 stalk celery, diced small
1/2 t. granulated garlic
1/4 t. season salt
1 can chicken, shredded
1/4 log of velveeta cheese
1 package cream cheese
1/4 c chicken broth
1/2 package of dry ranch dressing mix
2 T. blue cheese dressing
1-2 T. Sriracha sauce (to taste)
4 ounces shredded Mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Saute onion and celery until translucent in a medium size sauce pan.
Add chicken, garlic and season salt.  Cook approx 3 minutes.
Add velveeta, cream cheese and chicken broth.  Cover and cook on low until melted.
Add ranch dressing mix, sriracha and blue cheese dressing. Continue to cook until it is heated thru.
Add mozzarella cheese, cook until melted.

Submitter: 
MANDM1995
Description: 
I came up with this dip when I was in the mood for Buffalo chicken dip, but didn't have the necessary ingredients. It is a creamy cheesy dip with a nice bit of back end heat.
