Title:
Basil Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups diced chicken breast
Teaspoon poultry seasoning
1 can Campbell's Chicken Noodle Soup
1 can of vegetable broth
Pinch of pepper
2 tablespoons of basil
1 can diced potatoes

Directions:
Put  can Campbell's Chicken Noodle Soup, can diced potatoes
4 cups diced chicken breast. Wait till it sizzles then add vegetable broth. After a minute add poultry seasoning, pepper, and basil. Serve hot. Enjoy!
