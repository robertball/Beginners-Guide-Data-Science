Title:
Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/774401.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds extra lean ground beef
2 teaspoons salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 cup onion; chopped
1 cup ketchup
1/2 cup water
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 tablespoons mustard
2 tablespoons vinegar
2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
8 hamburger buns

Directions:
Brown hamburger with salt, pepper and onion.
Combine ketchup, water, brown sugar, mustard, vinegar and Worcestershire sauce.
Mix together and heat. Serve on buns
