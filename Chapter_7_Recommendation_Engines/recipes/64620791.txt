Title:
Oat & Buckwheat Flatbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Topping
2 tablespoons raw pumpkin seeds
2 tablespoon raw sunflower seeds
1 teaspoon chia seeds
1 teaspoon sesame seeds
Sea salt for sprinkling
Faltbread:
1.5 cup rolled oats
1 cup raw buckwheat groats
1/2 cup raw sunflower seeds
2 tablespoon chia seeds
1 tablespoon sugar
2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
2 cup unsweetened and unflavored non-dairy milk
2 tablespoon oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F. Line 2 - 9inch square pans with parchment paper.
Make the Seed Topping: In a small bowl, combine the raw pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds, chia seeds, sesame seeds and stir to combine. Set aside.
In a high-speed blender, combine the oats and buckwheat and blend on high until flour forms, 5 to 10 seconds.
In a large bowl, combine the oat and buckwehat flour, sunflower seeds, chia weeds, sugar, oregano, thyme, baking powder, garlic powder, and salt.
Add milk and oil to the bowl and stir very well until no clumps remain. Pour batter into prepared pan and smooth it out with spatula.
Sprinkle seed topping over batter. Lightly press down topping with your hands to adhere it to batter.
Bake for 25 to 30 mins, uncovered, until flatbread is firm to the touch.
Let bread cool in pan placed on a cooling rack for 15 minutes. Lift it out of pan and place on clean work surface.
Good for 2 days in the fridge and 2 weeks in the freezer.
