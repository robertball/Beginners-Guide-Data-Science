Title:
Spinach Salad with Feta, Grapes, and Candied Walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1081317.jpg
Ingredients:
½ cup red grapes (seedless)
½ cup Candied Walnuts
? cup feta cheese
3-4 cups baby spinach
2 tablespoon cranberry balsamic vinaigrette

Directions:
Put the spinach into a large bowl.
Wash and slice the grapes in half. Add to the bowl.
Add the candied walnuts to the bowl.
Drizzle 1 tablespoon of the cranberry balsamic vinaigrette onto the salad. Lightly toss so everything is coated and glistening with the dressing.
Scoop the salad onto a serving plate.
Top with feta cheese.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
This recipe is healthy, but tastes decadent due to the candied walnuts. It’s slightly sweet from the grapes, slightly tangy from the dressing, and slightly salty from the feta. In the words of my mother “this salad is AMAZING!”
