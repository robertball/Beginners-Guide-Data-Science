Title:
Boiled Tipsy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken, cut or packaged into parts
3-4 gallons water
4 10.5 oz. cans chicken broth
6-8 chicken bouillon cubes
2 tbsp season salt
2 tbsp garlic powder
1/4 cup salt
1/4 cup ground black pepper
2 cups sliced mushrooms
8 whole bay leaves
1/2 cup rosemary
1/2 cup parsley
1 12-oz. winter or pale ale

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large stock pot over high heat and bring to a boil.  Boil for 60 minutes or until chicken begins to fall off the bone.

Submitter: 
Dave Baker
Description: 
A mouthwatering chicken recipe that goes well with almost any sides.
