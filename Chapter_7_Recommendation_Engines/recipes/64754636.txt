Title:
Hamburger Meat Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb fresh hamburger meat ( use 85 %)
1 sweet onion
1 can  Rotel tomatoes

Directions:
add 1 lb.  hamburger meat into the bowl
dice onion into small bits
stir with a fork until well blended
drain Rotel tomatoes and add into the Hamburger and onion mix
Fork until well blended
press out into thick hamburger patties
Cook covered over low heat for 30 minutes, turn Patty cook uncover for 15, turn heat up to brown meat until light brown, remove from pan to onto paper towel to remove the remaining  grease

Submitter: 
Chris
Description: 
A simple dish that is quick and easy to make, pan-fry
you can make and freeze for later use, by using non-stick tin foil laid out on a cookie sheet, place pressed hamburger meat patties onto the cookie sheet, freeze until hard (3-4 hours) remove from sheet and place into a freezer bag.
