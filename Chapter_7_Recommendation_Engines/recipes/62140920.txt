Title:
Zucchini Pineapple Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
2 cups sugar
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup plain applesauce, sweet or unsweetened
2 tsp. vanilla
2 cups grated zucchini
3 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
1 cup crushed pineapple (not drained)
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup coconut
1 cup walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Beat eggs, sugar, oil, applesauce and vanilla until creamy.
Add zucchini.
Add flour, sakt, soda and baking powder.
Stir in pineapple, raisins, nuts if desired and coconut.  Bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees in 2 large loaf pans.

Submitter: 
LEXI70
Description: 
This moist bread is a great treat!  The flavors all go together wonderfully.
