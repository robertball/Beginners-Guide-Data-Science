Title:
Spicy Cheese on Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tomato – finely chopped
½ Onion – finely chopped
120g/4oz Cheddar or Cheddar/parmesan mix – grated
Pinch Ground Cumin
Small Piece (knob) Butter
1 to 2 tbsp fresh Coriander(Cilantro) or medley of herbs (Oregano/Basil/Parsley) – finely chopped
Pinch Chilli Powder – optional
2 slices Bread – I use Chappata/Chabatta (Spanish/Italian/English terms but any firm bread will do

Directions:
Toast Bread on both sides;
Mix all ingredients together in a bowl;
Spread on toasted slices, making sure toast is completely covered;
Place under grill until cheese bubbles and browns slightly;
Serve hot.

Submitter:

Description: 
A simple, but delicious version with a twist of an old favourite. Makes a meal, supper, lunch or snack.
