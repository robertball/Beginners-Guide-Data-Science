Title:
Carrot salad with a difference Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/378753.jpg
Ingredients:
* Carrots - 700 g (24 - 25 ounces), peeled and grated
* Lime - 1, grated zest and juice
* Thai sweet chilli sauce - 4 Tablespoons
* Thai fish sauce (Nam Pla) - 1 teaspoon
* Fresh ginger - around 3cm (1 1/4 inch), peeled (use a TEASPOON!!!) and grated
* Fresh mint leaves - 1 small bunch , leaves only, shredded

Directions:
Mix it all togther and serve as an accompaniment to BBQ meats and fish, or as a starter with some green leaves to a spicy dinner.
