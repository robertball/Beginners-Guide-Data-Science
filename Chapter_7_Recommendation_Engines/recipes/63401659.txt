Title:
Cheese and Pear Tartlets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ounces blue or gorganzola cheese, crumbled
4 oz cream cheese
2 ripe pear - peeled, cored, and chopped
2 tablespoons light cream
ground black pepper to taste
1 (2.1 ounce) packagemini phyllo tart shells (30)
1 cup walnuts
1/2 cup sugar

Directions:
Cook 1 cup walnuts and 1/2 cup sugar, set to medium in a pan and stir constantly until sugar melts and carmelizes walnuts. Turn onto non stick foil until cool.
Prebake phyllo shells according to package directions. Set aside to cool.
Mix together blue cheese, cream cheese,pear, and cream. Season to taste with pepper. Spoon mixture into cooled shells. Add a carmelized walnut to the top of each tart before popping in the oven.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 15 minutes. Serve warm.
