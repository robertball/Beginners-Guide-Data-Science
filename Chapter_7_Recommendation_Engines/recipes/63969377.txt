Title:
Baked Teriyaki Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoon cornstarch
2 tablespoon cold water
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon minced ginger
1 cup lite soy sauce
1/2 cup cider vinegar
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 skinless chicken breasts (can also use other meats)

Directions:
Combine cornstarch,water,sugar,soy sauce,vinegar, garlic, ginger and ground black pepper. Simmer, stirring,until sauce thickens and bubbles.Crockpot (6 hours) or oven (400 degrees for 35 mins).
Place chicken pieces in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish. Pour sauce over chicken.Cover w/foil.
