Title:
Sweet & Hot Mustard Sauce - Weight Watchers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup nonfat sour cream
2 tbsp Dijon mustard
2 teaspoons Splenda sweetener (2 packets)
1 tsp hot sauce

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl and mix well to combine. Refrigerate until ready to use.
Serves 4 (serving size: 1/4 cup)
PointsPlus value per serving: 1

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Great for fish and shellfish or as a dip for fresh vegetables from Weight Watchers online.
