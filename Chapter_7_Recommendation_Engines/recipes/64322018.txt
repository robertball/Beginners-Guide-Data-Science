Title:
Brown Rice, Tomato, and Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup(s) rice, brown, quick-cooking
1/2 head(s) lettuce, romaine (3/4 pound) or arugula, torn into bite-size pieces (3 cups)
1/2 medium cucumber(s) peeled, seeded, and sliced
1/2 medium tomato(es), plum diced
1 medium scallion(s) (green onions) thinly sliced
1 1/2 tablespoon oil, olive, extra-virgin
1/2 tablespoon vinegar, red wine
salt to taste
peppercorns, black freshly ground, to taste

Directions:
Cook rice according to package directions. Remove from heat, spread on a plate, and refrigerate for 10 minutes to cool.
In a large bowl, mix rice with romaine, cucumber, tomato, scallion, oil, and vinegar. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve.
Each serving is 2 cups.
