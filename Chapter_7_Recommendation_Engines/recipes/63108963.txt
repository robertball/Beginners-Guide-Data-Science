Title:
Baron's Fiber One Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/789326.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb Lean Ground beef
1 lb Sweet Italian sausage
1 medium onion diced
1 1 green pepper diced
One egg whisked
1/3 cup Chili Sauce
1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon ground pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups Fiber One Cereal moderately pulsed in food processor or crushed
1/4 cup chili sauce

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees
Mix ground beef, Italian sausage, egg, onion, green pepper, Worcestershire sauce, 1/3rd cup chili sauce, salt pepper together in a large mixing bowl.
Pulse fiber one cereal in a food processor till reduced to about one cup
Add to meat mixture and mix well
Line loaf pan with sheet of aluminum foil that is just the long width of the pan and comes well up and over the sides.  This allows you to easily lift the loaf from the pan when done.
Form meat mixture into loaf in pan so that it is relatively flat in the loaf pan. It will shrink and "dome" up as it cooks
Cook approx 40 min in oven.
cover top with 1/4 cup chili sauce and cook 20 min more to an internal temp of 160 degrees
Cool 5- 10 minutes in pan and the remove with the foil.
Remove foil and onto serving plate.
