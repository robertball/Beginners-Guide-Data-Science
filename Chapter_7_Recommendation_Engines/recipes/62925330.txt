Title:
Spaghetti Italian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/714759.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound Hot Italian sausage, Or Link Hot Sausage
4 ounces of pepperoni
4 (14.5 ounce) cans Of diced tomatoes
28 ounces of water
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1 teaspoon dried rosemary
5 Cloves Of Garlic
16 ounce box of spaghetti
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown sausage over medium heat; drain and set aside.
In a large saucepan over medium heat, combine, water, diced tomatoes, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, basil, oregano, thyme, rosemary, salt, pepper and Italian sausage, and pepperoni; mix well.
Simmer over medium-low heat for at least one hour; it is best if simmered 4 to 6 hours.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain, do not rinse pasta.
In a bowl mix some sauce with hot pasta; serve.
Refrigerate any leftovers.
