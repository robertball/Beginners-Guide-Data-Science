Title:
Apple-Kraut Tuna Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5203667.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (5 ounce) can tuna in water, drained
1/4 cup sauerkraut
1/4 cup finely chopped apple
2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
4 slices whole wheat bread, toasted

Directions:
Mix tuna, sauerkraut, apple, Dijon mustard, and red wine vinegar in a bowl. Serve on toasted wheat bread.

Submitter: 
Cameron
Description: 
Sweet apples and sauerkraut combined to make a uniquely flavored tuna sandwich.  A low calorie, highly filling snack or meal. You may choose to top the tuna mixture with any of your favorite vegetables.
