Title:
Cyndi's Coconut Ricotta Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound margarine
2 c sugar
3 eggs
1 pound ricotta cheese
4 c flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 c sweetened shredded coconut
Almond Glaze--
1 pound confectioners sugar
1/2 c milk
1 tsp shortening
1 tsp almond extract

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
Lightly grease a cookie sheet
Cream margarine and sugar until light and fluffy
Add eggs one at a time while beating mixture
Add ricotta cheese, flour, baking soda, salt and vanilla
Drop heaping teaspoons of batter on cookie sheet.
Bake 12-15 minutes
Almond Glaze
In a large mixing bowl beat sugar, milk, shortening and almond extract until well blended. When cookies are cool, dip tops in glaze and then dip in the coconut.
Let dry on a cooling rack until glaze sets.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
These delicious, soft cookies remind me of little coconut cakes. The almond glaze goes so well with the coconut topping. These are a staple in my family especially during the holidays.
