Title:
Mexican Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBSP canola oil
1 cup white long-grain rice
1/4 cup onion, chopped
2 cups chicken broth
6 oz. tomato sauce
1/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat.  Add rice and cook, stirring constantly for 5 minutes.  Add onion and continue to cook and stir until rice is lightly browned.
Add remaining ingredients.  Cover and reduce heat to medium-low.  Cook for 20-30 minutes until all liquid is absorbed.  DO NOT STIR until ready to serve.
