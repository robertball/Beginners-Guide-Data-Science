Title:
ANDY'S JALAPENO CHICKEN QUESADILLAS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 LB BONELESS CHICKEN BREASTS
1 PACKET FAJITA SEASONING
1 1/2 TBSP VEGETABLE OIL
2 GREEN BELL PEPPERS, SLICED
1 RED BELL PEPPER, SLICED
1 ORANGE BELL PEPPER,SLICED
2 JALAPENO PEPPERS, SLICED
1 MEDIUM ONION
20 FLOUR TORTILLAS
8OZ CHEDDAR CHEESE
8OZ MONTEREY JACK CHEESE

Directions:
PREHEAT OVEN TO 350.SLICE CHICKEN BREAST IN STRIPS , SAUTE IN MEDIUM SAUCEPAN WITH PREPARED FAJITA SEAONING UNTIL CHICKEN IS BROWNED. IN SEPERATE SAUCEPAN HEAT OIL SAUTE ONION AND PEPPERS UNTIL SOFT ADD CHICKEN AND COOK ON LOW HEAT FOR 5 MINS.IN A SEPERATE BOWL MIX 2 CHEESES TOGETHER. PLACE 1 TORTILLA FLAT LAYER WITH CHEESE MIX THEN CHICKEN THEN CHEESE AGAIN, PLACE ANOTHER TORTILLA ON TOP AND PLACE IN OVEN 12 MINS.
