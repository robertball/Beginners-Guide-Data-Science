Title:
spicy chicken strips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tbsp. olive oil
5 oz. boneless chicken breast
1/4 tsp. cumin
1/4 tsp. chili powder

Directions:
Rinse and cut the chicken into 1/2-inch strips. In a non-stick skillet, heat oil on medium-high. Sprinkle strips with spices and saute’ for about 4-5 minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink inside. Serve.

Submitter: 
Irene Padilla Lopez
Description: 
These spicy strips of chicken are perfect for kids and adults because they’ll fill you up without all the fat
