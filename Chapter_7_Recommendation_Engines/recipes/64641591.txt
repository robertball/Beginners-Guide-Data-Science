Title:
Zucchini Protien Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4515272.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Small Zuchinni
1/2 C Ground Old Fashioned Oats
2 T Coconut Flour
1 Large Egg
1 Scoop Protien Powder
1 T Stevia

Directions:
First ground up 1/2 cup of old fashioned pats til a gritty consistency then put in a bowl. Then take the small zuchinni and purée and add to the ground oats along with all the rest of the ingredients. Then heat up a skillet to medium heat and spray with non stick cooking oil, pour the batter in and wait about 3 minutes flip and then an addition 2 minutes on the other side and and voilà you have delicious Protien packed Pancakes!

Submitter: 
Max Hass
Description: 
Protien Pancakes to spice up the plain old Protien powder and have something more satisfying than a shake. This recipe uses zuchinni to keep the Pancakes moist and delicious and it's an easy way to sneak some veggies into your breakfast!
