Title:
CHIPOLTE CHICKEN ENCHILADAS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/856202.jpg
Ingredients:
FOR THE SAUCE:
2 garlic cloves
1-2 tbsp chipolte chilis in adobo sauce
1-1/2 c tomato sauce
Few drops of chipolte chili sauce (Melinda's Original Habanero Peppersauce)
1/2 tsp ground cumin
3/4 c fat free chicken broth
Kosher salt and fresh pepper to taste
FOR THE CHICKEN:
1 tsp vegetable oil
10 oz cooked shredded chicken breast (or store bought rotisserie chicken which you can shred if short on time)
1 c diced sweet onion
2 large clove garlic, minced
1/4 c. cilantro
Kosher salt
1 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp dried oregano (or fresh finely chopped)
Few drops Merlinda's Chipolte Peppersauce (to taste)
1/3 c chicken broth
1/2 c tomato sauce
8 (6-inch) reduced carb whole wheat flour tortillas
1 c shredded low fat Mexican cheese
Nonstick cooking spray

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, spray oil and saute' garlic.
Add adobo chipolte chiles, cumin, chicken broth, tomato sauce, salt and pepper.  Bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to low and simmer for 5-10 minutes.
Set aside until ready to use.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Heat the vegetable oil in a medium skillet over medium-high heat.
Saute onions and garlic on low until soft, about 2 minutes.
Add chicken, salt, cilantro, cumin, oregano, chili powder, tomato sauce, chicken broth, and cook 4-5 minutes.  Remove from heat.
Spray a 13 by 9-inch glass baking dish with non-stick spray.  Put 1/3 c chicken mixture into each tortilla and roll it.  Place on baking dish seam side down.
Top with sauce and cheese.
Cover with aluminum foil and bake in the oven on the middle rack for 20-25 minutes.
Top with low fat sour cream or scallions (or chopped fresh cilantro) just before serving if you wish.
Makes 8 enchiladas.
Serving size:  1 enchilada
Calories 159.5, Fat: 5.9g, Protien 16.2g, Carb 17.8g, Fiber 8.3g
