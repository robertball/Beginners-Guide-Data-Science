Title:
Garlic & Basil Shrimp over Angel Hair Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Olive Oil
1 1/2 Tbsp. Butter
5 cloves Garlic, minced
1 medium Onion, chopped (about 1 cup)
1/2 cup fresh Basil, minced -- OR -- 3 Tbsp. dried Basil
1/2 cup fresh Parsley, minced -- OR -- 3 Tbsp. dried Parsley
3/4 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp. Crushed Red Pepper
1/4 tsp. White Pepper (optional)
1/4 tsp. Black Pepper
1 tsp. fresh Thyme, minced -- OR -- 1/4 tsp. dried Thyme
1/4 tsp. Oregano
1 1/2 - 2 lbs. Shrimp, peeled and deveined
2 cups Heavy Cream
1/3 cup Shredded Parmesan Cheese (not the powdery, grated kind in the can).
9 oz. cooked Angel Hair Pasta

Directions:
Prepare angel hair pasta according to package directions, being certain to add a small amount of oil to the boiling water, or toss drained pasta with a small of butter or olive oil to prevent sticking.  Keep warm.
In a large skillet, heat oil, butter & garlic over moderate heat until butter is melted. Add onion and cook, stirring for 3 minutes or until onion is softened.
Add basil, parsley, salt, peppers, thyme & oregano.  Cook over moderate-low heat, stirring for 2 minutes.  Add shrimp and cook for 1-2 minutes on each side until they are pink and just cooked enough.
Transfer shrimp with a slotted spoon to a plate and keep warm.
Add the cream to the skillet and boil the mixture until it coats the back of the spoon.  Add Parmesan cheese and remove from heat. Return the shrimp to cream mixture.  Pour over prepared pasta.

Description: 
This is one of my family's favorite pasta dishes. It is slightly spicy and VERY rich.  Delicious! Serve with warm French bread or garlic bread.
