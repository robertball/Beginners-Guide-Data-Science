Title:
First Time Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Ripe Avacados
2 Limes, juiced
4 Cloves of garlic, minced
2 Serano Peppers, chopped
1/2 Yellow or Red Onion
1 Large Handful fresh Cilantro, finely chopped
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Halve and pit the avocados; scoop out the flesh with a tablespoon into a mixing bowl. Use a fork to mash the avacados, leave a little chunky if you would like.
Add remaining ingrediants and mix well.
Serve immediately or cover and refrigerate!

Submitter: 
Christine W
Description: 
I wanted to make my own guacamole but had never done it.  I knew the basics of how to make it, so I decided to throw some stuff together, and it came out wonderful!  You can adjust the amount of heat by adding more or cutting back on the serano peppers.
