Title:
Starbucks  Banana Walnut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 tsp. salt
2 c flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 egg
1 1/2 c sugar
1/2 c canola oil
2T buttermilk ( I used reg. milk)
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
3 ripe medium/large bananas mashed
1/2 c + 1/3 c chopped walnuts

Directions:
Pre-heat the oven to 325 degrees.  Grease a 9x5x3  loaf pan.  Blend together the flour, baking soda and salt and set aside.  Mix together the egg, sugar and oil until combined.  Add the flour mixture and when blended add the milk, vanilla and mashed bananas and mix until combined.  Fold in 1/2 c walnuts and pour batter into prepared loaf pan.  Top batter with remaining chopped nuts.  Bake for 45 to 60 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
Cool for 10 minutes on a wie rack before removing from pan.

Submitter: 
Grace Carolyn Bowers-Reimondo
Description: 
A light colored bread with lots of nuts.  Very moist.
