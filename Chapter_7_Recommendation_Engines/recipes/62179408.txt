Title:
peanut butter flowers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289917.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
1/2 cup shortning
1 1/4 cup light brown sugar, packed
3 tablespoons milk
1 tablespoon vanilla
1 egg
1 3/4 cup all purpose flour
3/4 teaspoon salt
3/4 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
preheat oven to 375 degrees F
cream peanut butter, shortning, and brown sugar in mixer until light and fluffy.
Add milk, vanilla, and egg. Mix well, scraping down sides of the bowl.
add the flout, salt and baking soda and mix well.
load dough into a cookie press with the flower attachment. press flowers onto ungreased cookie sheet and place 1 chocolate chip into the center of each one.
Bake for 7-8 minutes. cool on wire rack.
