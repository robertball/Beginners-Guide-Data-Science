Title:
Sate Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4c dry roasted peanuts, coarsely chopped
1 lg garlic clove
1 - 1 inch piece of peeled ginger
2/3c smooth peanut butter
6 T warm water
3 T rice vinegar
2 1/2 T soy sauce
1 1/2 T asian sesame oil
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp hot red pepper flakes
3 c cooked chopped chicken (rotisserie chicken works well)
1 lb coleslaw mix, broccoli slaw or asian cabbage
3 celery ribs, thinly sliced diagonally
1 red bell pepper sliced
1 bunch scallions, chopped
1 c coarsely chopped cilantro
lime wedges

Directions:
Pulse peanuts in a food processor until coarsely chopped, transfer to a bowl.  With blade running, process garlic and ginger to fine chop through the tube.  Add peanut butter, water, vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar and red pepper flakes blending until smooth.
Toss chicken and veggies with dressing then sprinkle with peanuts and cilantro.  Garnish with limes.
For make ahead, keep dressing separate.  Add and toss to coat just prior to enjoying.

Submitter: 
Abigail Pederson
Description: 
I love this recipe.  The ingredients seem like a lot but if you have everything on hand, it goes quickly.  Plus you can always make the dressing ahead.  Delish!  Definitely a potluck fav.
