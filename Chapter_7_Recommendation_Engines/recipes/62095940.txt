Title:
Blueberry walnut pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs, separated
1 1/2 cups sifted flour
2 1/4 tsp. baking powder
3 TB sugar
3/4 tsp. salt
1 cup milk
3 TB butter, melted
1 cup fresh blueberries, rinsed
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
In a small bowl, beat egg whites until stiff, set aside.
In a separate bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, sugar and salt.
Beat egg yolks in another bowl.
Add milk and melted butter.
Stir egg mixture into dry ingredients.
Mix until batter is smooth.
Stir in blueberries and walnuts.
Fold in beaten egg whites.
Bake on hot, greased griddle.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
I love this recipe because I never seem to have buttermilk around.  I had pancakes like these at a B&B; in West Virginia years ago and they were out-of-this world!  I used fresh blueberries but frozen, thawed ones can be used.
