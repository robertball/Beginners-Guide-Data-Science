Title:
Boxed Scalloped Potatoe Clone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/789754.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups dehydrated potatoes
1/3 cup PLUS 2 T nonfat dry milk
2 T flour
2 T cornstarch
1 t onion powder (or crushed onion)
1/4 t paprika
1/4 t salt
1/8 t garlic powder
1/8 t pepper
2 3/4 cup water (boiling)
3 T butter

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredient together.
Grease low 1 1/2 to 2 quart casserole dish.
Place potatoes in dish and sprinkly dry ingredients over potatoes.
Pour boiling water over top and stir to combine.
Dot with butter
Bake @ 400 for 30-35 minutes or 350 for 40-45"

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Dear husband loves those bloody boxed scalloped potatoe mixes.  This is the best clone I could find.  Using home dehydrated potatoes is an absolute must.  Sprinkling grated cheese over the top during the last 5 minutes of cooking improves it greatly.
