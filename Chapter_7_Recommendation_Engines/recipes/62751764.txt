Title:
Have It Your Way Steak and Pepper Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb beef strip loin grilling steak, 1 inch thick
2 Tbsp vegetable oil
1 onion, sliced
1 sweet red pepper, sliced
1 sweet green pepper, sliced
1 yellow pepper, sliced
¼ tsp salt
4 cups sliced mushrooms
1 clove of garlic, minced
¼ tsp dried thyme
4 small whole wheat tortillas
Chili Rub:
2 tsp chili powder
½ tsp salt
¼ tsp ground cumin
¼ tsp pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Chili Rub: Stir together chili powder, salt, cumin and pepper; sprinkle all over steak. Place on greased grill over med-high heat; close lid and grill, turning once, until medium-rare, about 8 minutes. Transfer to cutting board; tent with foil and let stand for 5 minutes before slicing thinly across the grain.
Meanwhile, in skillet, heat 1 Tbsp of the oil over med-high heat; sautee onion for 2 minutes. Add red, green and yellow pepper and pinch of salt; sautee adding 2 Tbsp water hallway though, until peppers are tender-crisp, about 5 minutes. Remove to bowl; keep warm.
In same skillet, heat remaining oil over med-high heat; sautee mushrooms, garlic, thyme and remaining salt until no liquid remains and mushrooms are golden, about 6 minutes. Remove to separate bowl; keep warm.
Meanwhile, stack tortillas and wrap in foil. Heat in oven until warmed through, about 10 minutes. Serve with sliced steak, peppers and mushrooms.
