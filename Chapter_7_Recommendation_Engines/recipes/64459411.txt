Title:
Italian Chickpea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, diced
2 cloves of garlic, minced
1 small bay leaf
1/2 tsp thyme
4 cups vegetable broth
2 16 oz cans chickpeas, rinsed
1 large sweet potato, peeled and cut in 1 inch cubes
3 carrots, cut into 1 inch cubes
1 stalk of celery, diced finely
1tbs yellow mustard
1/3 cup fresh parsley, minced
black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large pot, combine onion, garlic, bay leaf, thyme, and broth.Simmer 5 minutes. Add chickpeas, sweet potato, carrots, celery and mustard. Simmer until vegetables are very soft, about 12 minutes. With a potato masher, mash vegetables and chickpeas to a chunky puree. Stir in parsley and pepper.
