Title:
Chilly Berry Breeze for Edward Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cup Orange Juice
1/2 Frozen Strawberries, hulled
1 Cup Raspberry Yogurt
4 Ice Cubes

Directions:
Place orange juice, strawberries, yogurt and ice cube into blender.
Blend till smooth.
Enjoy!!

Submitter: 
Koki
Description: 
After many impromptu smoothie attempts for my husband, this was the best and final one I came across. It's very tasty and easy to make and goes really well with flax seed oil or fiber added.
