Title:
Pickled Egg Salad Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pickled eggs either purchased from a refrigerated deli counter or made from a recipe from this site, cut into small pieces
1 medium garlic dill pickle, minced
2 tsp chives or scallions, minced
2 tsp capers, drained
1 tbsp. minced celery
2 tbsp. light or regular mayonnaise
1 dash hot pepper sauce
1 dash egg pickle water
1 large leaf red lettuce or fancy lettuce broken in 2 or 3 pieces, rinsed and dried
1 medium tomato, thinly sliced
2 slices of whole wheat bread or bread of preference
enough butter or margarine to butter bread

Directions:
Mix the eggs,  pickle, chives, capers, celery, hot pepper sauce, mayo and egg vinegar well.
Toast bread choice lightly or use fresh bread and spread butter on both sides.  Layer lettuce and tomato on one side.  Add filling, press down with other piece of bread and slice.

Submitter: 
Phoebe
Description: 
A zippy change from an ordinary egg salad sandwich.
