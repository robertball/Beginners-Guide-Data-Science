Title:
Garlic Roasted Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1391102.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large head broccoli, cut into florets
1 TBSP EVOO
2 cloves garlic, sliced
Onion powder, salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
In large bowl toss florets and garlic with EVOO until evenly coated.
Spread into single layer on baking sheet, then sprinkle with onion powder, salt & pepper.
Bake for recommended time turning once half way through
