Title:
Roast Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 to 3 pound rump roast
Olive oil
Kosher salt
Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Liberally coat outside of roast with olive oil, then rub salt and cracked peppercorns on exterior of roast.
Place fat side up on roasting rack.
Roast for 10 minutes in hot oven, then reduce heat to 325 (lower if you have the time) and continue to cook until internal temp reaches 143 to 145.
Remove roast, wrap in foil, and let rest for 10 minutes.
