Title:
Diet Apple Babba Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Quaker 1 minute oats
2 111g containers Motts Fruitsation Unsweetened Apple Sauce
2 eggs
2 Large fresh apples, cored and pealed
1 Tbs baking powder
1/2 cup Splenda (Use the Baking Splenda so that it measures the same.)

Directions:
Chop fresh apples
mix all ingredients in a large bowl
pour into muffin tray (with paper inserts)
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes

Submitter: 
chef.kyle
Description: 
A fantastic alternative to all of those unhealthy apple pies and turnovers.
