Title:
Big Ray's Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5000453.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs, beaten
1 can cream-style corn, 15 oz
1 pkg frozen whole kernel corn, 10 oz
1 pkg corn muffin mix
1/2 cup sour cream
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
8 oz  shredded cheddar cheese , didided
1 small onion, diced
4 slices cooked bacon, crumbled

Directions:
Heat oven to 375Â°F.
Combine first 6 ingredients in medium bowl. Stir in 1/2 cup cheese. Pour into 9" x 13" baking dish, sprayed with cooking spray.
Bake 40 min. Meanwhile, combine remaining cheese, onions and bacon.
Top casserole with cheese mixture; bake 15 min. or until lightly browned.

Submitter:

Description: 
A great side for your next potluck, sometimes I'll add extra bacon into the dish to make it a main course.
