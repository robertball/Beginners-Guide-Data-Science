Title:
Chimichurri Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1/4 Cup Chopped Red Onion
3 Cloves Minced Garlic
1/2 Cup Red Wine Vinegar
1 Tsp Salt
1 Tsp Black Pepper
1/2 Cup Chopped Cilantro
1 TBSP Olive Oil

Directions:
Puree all ingredients in a blender.

Description:
