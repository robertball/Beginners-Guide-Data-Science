Title:
Baw Baw's Fruit Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lbs Currants
1 lbs Rasins
1 1/4 lbs Sugar
1 lbs Pork Fat
1 Tbl Cinnamon
1 Tbl Grnd Nutmeg
1/2 tsp Grnd Cloves
1 tsp Soda
1 Qt Hot(!) water
6 C Flour
Dates, Figs, Nuts to taste

Directions:
1. Pour Hot(!) Water over finely grnd pork fat. Add Sugar and set aside.
2. Sift 4 C flour, add Soda, Cloves, Cinn, & Nutmeg. Sift again. Set aside.
Sift remaining 2 C Flour & add Fruit & Nuts.
Combine Flour and Flour/Fruit mix. Stir.
Mix warm water/Pork mixture.
Mix until smooth.
Line 2 loaf pans with greased brown paper.
Bake 325F for 3 hours.
