Title:
Easy Korean Ground Beef Bowl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5759837.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
2 teaspoons roasted sesame oil
5 cloves garlic, crushed
1 tablespoon freshly grated ginger
1/3 cup light brown sugar
1/2 cup low sodium soy sauce
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
6 green onions, chopped; divided use
4 cups brown rice, hot cooked
1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds

Directions:
Heat a large skillet over medium high heat. Add beef and cook, stirring and crumbling into small pieces until browned. Drain excess grease.
Add sesame oil, garlic and ginger, stirring until fragrant.
Stir in brown sugar, soy sauce, and crushed red pepper and cook for a few minutes, allowing some of the sauce to absorb into the meat. Add half of the chopped green onions.
Serve over hot cooked rice, garnished with chopped green onions and sesame seeds.

Submitter:

Description: 
Korean beef bowls are quick and easy to make. The ingredients can easily be adjusted to suit you taste. Serve over warm rice  or spiralized vegetables.
