Title:
Maple & Brown Sugar Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick and 6 tablespoons butter
3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup granulated sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon maple extract
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 (3.4 oz) package instant vanilla pudding mix
3 cups uncooked rolled oats

Directions:
1. Heat oven to 350
2. Beat butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar on medium speed with electric mixer until creamy.
4. Beat in eggs and maple extract.
5. Combine flour, baking soda, and pudding mix in a separate bowl. Beat into cookie dough, about a tablespoon at a time.
6. Mix in oats.
7. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
8. Bake 11-13 minutes, until cookies turn golden brown.

Submitter: 
Gabrielle Ziemba
Description: 
Remember the days when Mom would make you a bowl of instant oatmeal and you'd watch cartoons while you ate it? Here are those days back: in cookie form! Be picky about where you bring these. They have a funny habit of disappearing into thin air.
