Title:
Slow Cooker Roast Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 lb boneless chuck roast
2 packets onion soup mix
1 can cream of mushroom soup
10 oz cola (coca cola prefered)
DO NOT ADD SALT

Directions:
Place roast in slow cooker.  In a bowl mix soup mixes, cream of mushroom soup and cola.  Pour over roast.  Set slow cooker to low and cook for 8 hours.

Submitter: 
staceywatts
Description: 
This recipe uses Cola and makes its own gravy!
