Title:
Corn & Spinach Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup chopped,frozen or fresh Spinach
3/4 Cup frozen or fresh sweet yellow corn
8 slices of Light Oatmeal Sandwich bread (Use your favorite bread)
4 Slices Muenster Cheese (Cheddar works well too)
Butter
Salt
Ground Black Pepper
Chutney Spread
-- For the Chutney Spread
1 cup chopped Cilantro
3 jalapeno peppers (add more if you like hot stuff)
2 TbSp. Peanuts
Juice of 1/2 Lemon
1 tsp. sugar
salt to taste
6-7 Curry leaves (optional)
1 tsp cumin seeds or powder
2 tbsp Water (if needed)

Directions:
--- For the Chutney Spread
Grind the Chutney Spread ingredients in a blender or food processor to a fine paste. Add the water if needed.
--To make the Spinach filling
Cook spinach in the microwave or on a stove with a little water until tender. Don't add too much water. If you are using frozen spinach, you may not need to add any water. Add corn to the still warm spinach. Add salt and pepper to taste.
To Assemble Sandwiches
Butter 2 slices of bread on both sides
Spread the Chutney Spread on one.
Top it with the spinach filling, Add a slice of muenster cheese, cover with the cecond slice of bread. Grill the sandwiches two at a time in a sandwich maker.
--This recipe makes moe chutney spread than is required for 8 sandwich triangles. You can always store the extra chutney spread in the fridge for upto 6 weeks.
Serve hot with Ketchup.
These sandwiches go very well with black bean soups.

Submitter: 
Talking Head
Description: 
This is an excellent recipe for Vegetarians. You can make the chutney spread ahead of time and can fix delicious sandwiches in no time.
