Title:
Pumpkin Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1838857.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice, or as desired
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers pumpkin pie filling
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger spiced rum (such as Captain Morgan'sÂ®)
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger half-and-half
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger milk
1 pinch ground cinnamon
1 pinch ground nutmeg

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice and add pumpkin pie filling, spiced rum, half-and-half, milk, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Cover shaker and shake; pour into a martini glass.
