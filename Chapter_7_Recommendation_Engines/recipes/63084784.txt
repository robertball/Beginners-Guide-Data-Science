Title:
Cream Cheese and Ham Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
2 1/2 ounces sliced ham, chopped
2 green onions, chopped
2 Tbsp. Emerson's British Mustard

Directions:
In a medium-size mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, ham, mustard, and onions. Cover and refrigerate overnight before serving.

Submitter: 
Dawn Eberly Wampler
Description: 
Simple, but flavorful, spread that is great with crackers or celery.Modified from: Cream Cheese and Ham Spread
