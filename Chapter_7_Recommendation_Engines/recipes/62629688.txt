Title:
Ultimate Raspberry Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/576393.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 1/4 cups quick oats
1/3 cup granulated sugar
1/3 cup brown sugar
1/4 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
16 Tbsp. butter (2 sticks), softened
1 cup seedless red raspberry jam
1/4 tsp. almond extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Grease and flour a 9 X 9 inch sq. pan.
In a small bowl mix together the raspberry jam and almond extract until well blended.  Set aside.
Mix flour, oatmeal, granulated sugar, brown sugar, baking soda, & salt in a large bowl.
Add butter and beat for 2 minutes until crumbs form.
Press 2/3 of the crumb mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan.
Bake for 20 min. until slightly golden brown.
Remove from oven.
Spread the jam mixture over the hot crust.
Sprinkle the remaining crumbs over the top of the jam and press down gently.
Bake for 30 min.
Remove from oven and cool completely befor cutting into squares.
