Title:
Pumpkin Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C AP Flour
1 1/2 C Packed Brown Sugaar
2 teaspoons Baking Powder
1 teaspoon Baking Soda
2 teaspoon Pumpkin Pie Spice
1/4 teaspoon Salt
1/2 C Vegetable Oil
1/2 C Apple Juice
2 Eggs
Frosting
1 Containerof (16oz) Cream Cheese Creamy ready-to-spread Frosting

Directions:
1) Heat oven to 350*F. Grease 15x10x1 inch pan with shortening.  Lightly flour. In large bowl with mixer, beatbar ingredients on low speed until moistened.  Beat 2 minutes on medium speed.  Spread batter in pan.) Bake 20-30 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.  Cool completely (about 1 hour).
3) Spread frosting over cooled barsw.  Refrigerate until set, about 30 minutes.  Cut into 8 rows by 6 rows.  Store in rfefrigerator.
