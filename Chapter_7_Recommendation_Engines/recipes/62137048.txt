Title:
Grilled Lemon Pepper Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Tbsp olive oil
3 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
1/2 tsp Tabasco
1/2 tsp dried thyme
4 red onion slices, cut 1/3 to 1/2 inch thick
2 boneless chicken breasts
4 cups Romaine lettuce, torn
1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled

Directions:
Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, Tabasco and thyme in a small saucepan. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Arrange chicken and onion slices on a plate. Spoon 2 Tbsp dressing over chicken and onions, and turn to coat. Let stand for five minutes.
Prepare BBQ to medium high heat. Set saucepan with remaining dressing at edge of BBQ to warm.
Grill chicken and onion slices until just cooked through, about 4 minutes per side. Transfer chicken and onion to a plate. Cut chicken crosswise into thin slices. Separate onion in to rings.
Place lettuce in large bowl. Add chicken and onion. Add warm dressing and toss to coat. Season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle feta cheese over salad and serve.
