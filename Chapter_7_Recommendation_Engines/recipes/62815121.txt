Title:
Baked Chicken Scampi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs
2 Tbs Oil
4 Tbs Cold Butter (Divided)
2 Tbs Minced Garlic
1 C White Wine
2 C Chicken Broth
1 C Sliced Fresh Mushrooms
3 Tbs Chopped Parsley
Flour
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*
Season Chicken with salt and pepper and coat with flour.
Heat oil and 1 Tbs of butter over medium high heat in an oven proof skillet
Saute Chicken in butter and oil on both sides until lightly browned
Add garlic to pan with chicken (saute for about 30 seconds)
De-glaze pan with wine
Simmer until wine has reduced by about half
Add Chicken Broth, Mushrooms, and Parsley
Remove from heat
Divide remaining butter into 3 pieces and coat with flour and add to pan. Season with salt and pepper and put into oven uncovered.
Bake for approximately 25-30 minutes
Serve over rice or pasta

Submitter: 
Anissa
Description: 
Savory Chicken baked in the oven instead of tending to a pan on the stove. Made with thighs which have a richer flavor. Really good served over white rice.
