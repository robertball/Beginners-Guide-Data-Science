Title:
Frozen Peanut Butter Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 Nutter Butter sandwich cookies, crushed
5 T butter, melted
1 8 oz. package cream cheese, softened
1 C creamy peanut butter
3/4 C sugar
1 T vanilla
1 8 oz. tub Cool Whip, thawed

Directions:
Mix cookie crumbs and butter.
Press onto bottom and sides of a 9" pie plate.
Mix cream cheese, peanut butter, sugar and vanilla until well blended.
Gently fold in 1 1/2 C Cool Whip.
Spoon into crust.
Freeze 4 hours or overnight.
Let stand 1/2 hour or until pie can be easily cut.
Garish with remaining Cool Whip and cookies.
