Title:
Gingerbread Cookies from Andrea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup butter
1/2 Cup sugar
1/2 Cup molasses
1 egg yolk
2 Cups all purpose flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp ground cloves
1 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 350
Cream together butter and sugar until smooth.
Stir in molasses and egg yolk.
Combine the rest of the dry ingredients and add to the wet ingredients.  Mix until smooth.
Roll out dough on floured surface and cut into shapes.  Chill cookies in the refrigerator before baking.
Bake at 350 for 8 - 10 minutes until firm.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
These cookies hold their shape very well.  Absolutely delicious.
