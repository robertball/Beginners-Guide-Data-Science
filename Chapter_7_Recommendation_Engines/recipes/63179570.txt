Title:
Slow Cooker Enchilada  Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 # ground meat (turkey, beef)
1 cup chopped  onion
1/2 cup chopped green pepper
2 teaspoons canola oil
1 15 oz can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 15 oz can black beans, rinsed and dried
1 15 oz cn diced tomato with green chilies
1 1/2 teaspoons chili
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
6 whole wheat tortillas (6" or trimmed to size of slow cooker)
8 oz shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Cut thre strips of aluminum foil 25" x 3".  Line the crock pot with the three, crossed in the middle and coming up the sides and over the edge. Coat with cooking spray.  Cook meat, onion, green peppers until meat is brown and vegetables are tender. Stir in beans, tomatoes, chili, cumin and pepper.  Bring to boil, then simmer, uncovered for 10 minutes. Starting with tortilla, then meat mix, then cheese, make six layers in slow cooker, ending with cheese. Fold ends of foil over top of tortillas.  Cover and cook on low for 4-5 hours. Lift out using aluminum foil and slice into wedges like a pie.
