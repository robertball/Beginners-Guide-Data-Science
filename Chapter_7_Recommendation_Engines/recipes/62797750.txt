Title:
CREAM OF BROCCOLI SOUP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 BUNCHES OF CUT UP BROCCOLI,REMOVE STEM
2 CHOPPED CELERY
1/2 CUP SLICED CARROTS
1/2 CUP CHOPPED ONIONS
8 CUPS WATER
3-4 CHICKEN BOULLON CUBES
1/3-1/2 CUP CREAM or 1 cup milk
6 SLICES CHEESE SLICES
CORNSTARCH TO THICKEN

Directions:
Add chopped up vegetables in large pot.Add water.Bring to a boil,add chicken cubes.Let simmer untill all vegetables are soft.Mash well or use a blender.Add cheese slices.Mix cornstarch in water and gradually add to soup till desired thickness.
