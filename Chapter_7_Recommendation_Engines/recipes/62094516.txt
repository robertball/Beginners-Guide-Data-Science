Title:
Southwest Stuffed Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 2 large zucchini, halved and quartered,
- 20 oz. lean ground turkey
- 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- 1/4 onion, finely chopped
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro or parsley
- 1/2 small bell pepper, finely chopped
- 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and finely chopped
- 2 teaspoons ground cumin
- 2 teaspoons Montreal Steak Seasoning (or other grill seasoning blend)
- 1/4 cup bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- 1 cup salsa
- 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Scoop pulp out of zucchini, creating boats.  Mix all other ingredients except salsa and cheese.  Fill zucchini with turkey mixture.  Top each zucchini with 1/4 cup salsa and 1/4 cup cheese.  Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes or until done.

Submitter: 
KATHYP100
Description: 
My picky teens who hate zucchini love this hearty maindish.  And I love it because it gives me one more way to use up the overabundance of zucchini from my garden.
