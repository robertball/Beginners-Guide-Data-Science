Title:
Soft Pretzels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 c flour
2 T sugar
1 t salt
2 pkgs (1/4 oz) dry yeast
1 c water
1 T shortening
1 egg yolk
1 T water
coarse salt

Directions:
In a large bowl, heat 1 c water to 110'.  Add yeast first, then all other dry ingredients.  Mix well and knead dough for 5 minutes.  Set in a greased bowl and let rise until double in size. Punch down.  Divide the dough into 12 pieces, and roll each one into a long rope (18-20 inches).  Shape into pretzels or other shapes.  Place in greased cookie sheet, and rest for 5 minutes.  Mix egg yolk and 1 T water.  Brush on the pretzel, then sprinkle them with the salt.  Bake at 375' for 15 minutes.
