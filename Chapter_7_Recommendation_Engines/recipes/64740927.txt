Title:
Peanut or Almond Butter Protein Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c peanut or almond butter
1/4 to 1/2 c egg white protein powder or whey protein powder
1-2 T collagen protein
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
pinch salt
pinch cinnamon, optional

Directions:
Heat oven to 350
Combine all ingredients with hand mixer
Drop by 1 T full and make balls
use fork to make criss-cross
bake 350 for 5-10 min
