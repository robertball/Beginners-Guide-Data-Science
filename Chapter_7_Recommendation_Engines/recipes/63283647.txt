Title:
Tacos al Carbon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  (4 1/2-pound) beef brisket, trimmed
2  tablespoons brown sugar
1 1/2  teaspoons ancho chili powder
1/2  teaspoon garlic powder
1/2  teaspoon onion powder
1/2  teaspoon chopped parsley
1/2  teaspoon salt
1/4  teaspoon ground black pepper
Flour tortillas
shredded cheese
sour cream
chopped tomatoes
salsa
guacamole
shredded lettuce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees.
2. Place brisket, fat side down, in a roasting pan.
3. Combine brown sugar, powders, parsley, salt, and pepper. Rub spice mixture into top of brisket. Cover and seal pan with foil. Bake for 4 1/2 hours.
4. Remove brisket from pan; let stand for 15 minutes. Cut brisket across grain into thin slices, using a sharp knife. Serve in warm flour tortillas with desired toppings.
