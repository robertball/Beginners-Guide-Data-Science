Title:
Best Meatloaf EVER! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
2 cans evaporated milk
1 package (2 envelopes) onion soup mix
1/4 c ketchup
3 Tblsp prepared mustard
2 Tblsp Brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*. In a large bowl, mix meat, evaporated milk and onion soup mix. The best way to ensure it is well combined is to use your hands! Pour into a 9 x 13 casserole dish or 2 5 x 9 loaf pans. Mix together ketchup, mustard and brown sugar and spread over meatloaf mixture. Cook for 45 minutes-1 hour until internal temp reaches 165* Let rest for 5-10 minutes before cutting.
