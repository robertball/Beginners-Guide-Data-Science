Title:
Triple Tomato Penne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound penne
2 medium tomatoes, diced
4 oil packed sun dried tomatoes
2 TBS tomato paste
4 large basil leaves
1 garlic clove
1/3 cup olive oil
salt and ground pepper

Directions:
In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the penne until al dente.
In a blender, puree the diced and sundried tomatoes with the tomato paste, basil, garlic and olive oil.  Pour sauce into a large bowl and season with salt and pepper.
Drain the pasta, add it to the sauce and toss well to coat.  Serve piping hot.
