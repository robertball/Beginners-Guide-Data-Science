Title:
Chocolate Amaretto Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (14.5 oz)sweetened condensed milk
5 tbs marshmallow fluff
1/4 cup unsalted butter
1/4 tsp salt
2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips*
1 cup milk chocolate chips
1-2 tsp almond extract(optional)

Directions:
Line a 8x8 pan with aluminum foil. Set aside.
In a small saucepan on medium-low heat combine condensed milk, marshmallow fluff, salt, and     butter. Heat until butter and marshmallow fluff is melted and thoroughly combined, about five minutes. Stir often.
Take pan off heat and immediately pour in chocolate chips and almond extract, but DO NOT STIR.
Let sit for five minutes, and then stir until chocolate is completely melted.
Pour into prepared pan, and refrigerate about 2 hrs, or until set.
Cut into 2-inch squares and enjoy!
