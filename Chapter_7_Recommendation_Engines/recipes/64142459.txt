Title:
Lemon Dijion Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package thin-sliced chicken breasts
3 tbsp dijon mustard
1 tbsp mayonnaise
juice of 1 lemon (or 3 tbsp lemon juice)
zest of 1 lemon (optional)
1 clove garlic, minced
salt
pepper
oregano (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 convection bake.
Mix mustard, mayonnaise, lemon juice, lemon zest (optional), garlic, salt, and pepper in gallon-sized ziploc bag.  Place chicken in bag and coat well.  Marinate in fridge for at least 15 minutes.
Cover baking tray with foil.  Lay chicken on foil.  Pour remaining marinade over chicken.  Bake until cooked through (about 15 minutes)
Sprinkle with oregano and fresh black pepper before serving (optional).

Submitter: 
HDN
Description: 
Very quick and easy and I typically have all of the ingredients in the house.  Great served with some green beans almondine and quinoa, or the next day with a salad or sandwich.
