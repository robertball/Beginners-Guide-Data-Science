Title:
Frittata - Paul's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon of butter or olive oil
10 oz fresh Spinach or 1 (10 ounce package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed throughly to remove liquid.
3/4 cup of chopped mushrooms
1/2 cup of green onion chopped  (some green tops for garnish)
4 eggs
1 cup of part skim ricotta cheese
1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
3/4 cup of parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Mix eggs, ricotta cheese and pepper until blended
2. Heat butter/oil in large oven proof nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add vegtables and cook until tender. Add egg mixture to and mix slightly.  cook over medium low heat 8 minutes or until eggs are set but still moist.
Spinkle parmesan over egg mixture
4. Broil 4" from heat 2 minutes or until golden brown. Sprinkle with green onion tops. Cut into wedges.
