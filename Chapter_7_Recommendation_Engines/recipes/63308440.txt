Title:
Sweet and Sour Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/874851.jpg
Ingredients:
3-5 chicken breast
salt and pepper
1 cup cornstarch
2 eggs, beaten
1/3 cup canola oil
Sauce:
3/4 cups sugar
1/4 cup ketchup
1/4 cup plus 2 Tablespoons white distilled vinegar
1 Tablespoon soy sauce
1 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
Rinse chicken, trim off fat or extras and then cut into 1 inch cubes. season with salt and pepper. Dip chicken into eggs and then into cornstarch and coat all the way. Heat oil in large skillet. Cook chicken until browned. Place in baking dish. 3 chicken breasts can be in a 9x9 but 5 chicken breasts fits better in a 9x13.
Whisk sauce until smooth. Then pour evenly over chicken. Turn chicken so the sauce gets on both sides and then put in the oven at 350 degrees for 15 minutes. turn chicken and then cook for 15 more minutes.
