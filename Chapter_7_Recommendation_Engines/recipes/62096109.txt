Title:
Honey Ginger Glazed Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cured Uncooked Ham, Approx. 7 1/2 pounds
1/2 Cup Honey
1 tbsp. Ground Ginger
2 Tbsp. Dijon Mustard
1/2 tsp. Freshly ground pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325'.
Remove ham from refrigerator about 1 Hour before cooking to bring to room temp.
Put ham in a roasting pan and place on a wire rack. Using a knife, score the fat on top in a diamond pattern.
Mix remaining ingredients in a bowl.
Brush about 3/4 of the honey mixture on ham with a pastry brush.
Cook Ham for Approx. 2 Hours.
Baste with remaining mixture and pan drippings every 45 minutes.
Remove from oven and serve warm or at room temp.
