Title:
Gena's Honey-Curry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter, melted
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup dry mustard
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon curry powder
3 pounds bone-in skinless chicken pieces

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Oil a 9x13 casserole dish. Combine first 5 ingredients; stir well. Dip chicken pieces in mixture, coating all sides, and arrange in casserole dish. Bake for one hour, basting every 15 minutes with remaining honey-curry mixture.

Submitter: 
Laurel Patton
Description: 
My friend, Gena, gave me this recipe decades ago, and we are still loving it!
