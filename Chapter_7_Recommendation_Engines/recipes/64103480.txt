Title:
Delightful Indian Coconut Vegetarian Curry in the Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped cashews
3 russet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch cubes
2 cans of chickpeas (drained)
1/4 cup curry powder
2 tablespoons corn startch
1 tablespoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
2 large red bell pepper, cut into strips
1 onion
1 (14 ounce) can coconut cream
water, as needed
1 1/2 cups matchstick-cut carrots
1 cup green peas
1 head of cauliflower chopped
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
Salt to taste

Directions:
Place the cashews potatoes and chickpeas into the bottom of a slow cooker.
Mix the curry powder, cornstarch, chili powder, red pepper flakes, and cayenne pepper together in a small bowl; sprinkle over the potatoes. Stir the potatoes to coat evenly. Add the red bell pepper, green bell pepper, onion, carrots and coconut cream; stir to combine.
Cover the slow cooker and cook on Low until the mixture is bubbling, adding water as needed to keep moist, 4 hours. Stir the peas and cauliflower into the mixture and cook until the vegetables are tender to your liking, about 30 minutes. Garnish individual portions with cilantro to serve.

Submitter: 
Lindsay Thorpe
Description: 
This delightful vegetarian curry dish from the slow cooker is made creamy with the use of coconut cream and given heat from the curry powder, chili powder, red pepper flakes, and cayenne pepper. Serve over riceModified from: Delightful Indian Coconut Vegetarian Curry in the Slow Cooker
