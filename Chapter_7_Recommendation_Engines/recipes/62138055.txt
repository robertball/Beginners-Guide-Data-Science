Title:
Pumkin Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 Cup flour
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
1 Tbs. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)
1/2 cup Margarine
1 1/4 Cup sugar
1 Cup cooked pumkin
2 Eggs
3/4 cup milk
1/2 tsp. baking soda

Directions:
1) sift dry ingredients together
2) preheat 350 degrees, Cream margarine and sugar in a large bowl until fluffy; beat eggs and add to margarine and sugar mixture
3) In a medium bowl mix pumkin, milk, and baking soda. Add flour mixture and pumkin mixture alternatly yo shortening, sugar, and egg mixture in large bowl; add chopped nuts
4) Pour into an 8-inch floured cake pan; bake 350 degrees for 50 minutes
