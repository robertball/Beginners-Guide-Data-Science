Title:
Oriental Chicken Grill Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c orange juice
2 tbsp honey
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp lemon pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder
2 boneless skinless chicken breast halves
2 hamburger buns
Lettuce leaves
Tomato slices

Directions:
Combine first five ingredients and mix well. Set aside 1/4 c for basting chicken, cover and refrigerate.
Pound chicken breasts to 3/8" thickness, place in bowl and cover with remaining marinade. Refrigerate overnight.
Drain and discard marinade. Grill chicken, uncovered, over medium coals for 6-8 minutes per side or until juices run clear, basting several times with reserved marinade.
Serve on buns with lettuce and tomatoes.
