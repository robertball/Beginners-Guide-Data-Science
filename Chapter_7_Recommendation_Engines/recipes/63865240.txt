Title:
Body Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5821528.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup organic coconut oil(solid coconut oil)
1 tsp. vitamin E oil
A few drops of Vanilla( or your favorite essential oil)for fragrance

Directions:
Put all in a big bowl. With a electric hand mixer whip until smooth. Put in a decorative jar. Do not use a blender or food processor or a Kitchen Aid mixer as the coconut oil will get too warm and it will not work.** See notes.
