Title:
Clam and Scallop Linguine Italian Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4530619.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounce Thin Linquine Cooked
1 (10.5 ounce can) Red Clam Sauce
1 (6.5 ounce can) Chopped Clams in Clam Juice
2 Plum Tomatoes, diced
2 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon garlic, minced
16 ounce Bay Scallops
½ medium Zucchini, diced
¼ cup White Wine
2 tablespoon Tomato Paste

Directions:
In medium sauce pan at medium heat add Red Clam Sauce, Chopped Clams, and tomatoes.
In medium frying pan melt butter, add garlic, and cook for 2 minutes. Then, add scallops. Cook the scallops for 1 1/2 minutes on each side.
Add the White wine and finish cooking scallops. Remove scallops from pan and set aside.
Add the Zucchini to pan and cook until Zucchini is tender. Then, add to sauce pan and stir sauce and vegetables.
Add Tomato Paste to thicken clam sauce.
Ladle clam sauce over portion of linguine and top off dish with portion of scallops.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
Basically this is a Red Clam Sauce and Linguine with tender Zucchini and Plum Tomato then topped off with scallops. Easy recipe because it uses canned Italian Red Clam Sauce and Chopped Clams as basis of sauce.
