Title:
Live&Learners; Out of this World Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
2 jars marshmallow fluff
1 jar Nutella
7 enormous kale leaves, left whole
red frosting
dinomite-shaped cookie cutter

Directions:
Mix the eggs in a mixing bowl until frothy.
In a separate bowl, mix together marshmallow fluff and nutella.
Add marshmallow nutella mixture to eggs. Then add whole kale leaves and stir.
Spread mixture into 9x13 pan and back at 350 for 30 minutes.
Remove pan from oven, let cool, then use your one-of-a-kind, limited edition dinomite-shaped cookie cutter to cut fluffy bites.
Spread each dinomite fluffy bite with red frosting.
Stuff in your facehole.
