Title:
Raisin Cain Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup raisins
2 cups rum (apple juice may be substituted)
2 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 eggs
1 cup butter (softened)
1 cup white sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon ground cardamom
2 teaspoons baking soda
2 cups rolled oats
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
In a bowl, soak the raisins in the rum (or apple juice)and vanilla for at least an hour.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Drain the liquid mixture from the raisins. Set them in a colander to drain while the other ingredients are prepared.
In a large bowl, cream together the eggs, vanilla, butter, and white and brown sugars.
In a medium bowl, sift together the flour, cardamom, and baking soda.
Working slowly, mix the dry mixture into the wet.
When combined, stir in the nuts, raisins and oats.
Drop by small spoonfuls onto an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 8-10 minutes.

Submitter: 
MomZilla (Evin)
Description: 
These oatmeal raisin cookies are 'hellishly' good, according to my family! I usually have to hide a batch of them so I can actually eat some. They're great to take to potlucks and church suppers, you'll have the other ladies begging for your secret ingredient!
