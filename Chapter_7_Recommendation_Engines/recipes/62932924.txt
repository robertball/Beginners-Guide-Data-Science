Title:
Homestyle Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium green peppers
1 & 1/2 lbs. lean ground beef (90/10)
1 cup cooked white rice
1 egg lightly beaten
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
1/2 cup Italian style bread crumbs
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
28 oz. can tomato sauce
1/4 cup white vinegar
2 Tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cut off tops of peppers and disgard; remove seeds and membrane.
In a large sauce pan, bring water to a boil.
Place peppers in boiling water for 4-5 minutes.
Remove peppers from water and rinse with cold water.
Set peppers aside.
Mix together ground beef, cooked white rice, lightly beaten egg, grated Parmesan cheese, finely chopped onion, Italian style bread crumbs, salt, black pepper, and garlic powder.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Divide beef and rice mixture among peppers.
Stuff each pepper with beef and rice mixture.
Place peppers open side up in a 9x13 baking dish or shallow roasting pan.
In a medium size bowl, combine tomato sauce and white vinegar.
Pour tomato mixture over stuffed peppers.
Top with grated Parmesan cheese.
Bake uncovered for 1 hour and 20 minutes or until heated through.
Baste peppers with tomato sauce two - three times during baking.

Submitter: 
Jillian
Description: 
An old family recipe for classic comfort food passed down to me by my Mom...Nancy. Green peppers are stuffed with a mixture of ground beef and rice then smothered in a tangy tomato sauce. I always serve these with creamy mashed potatoes just like my Mom always did...enjoy!
