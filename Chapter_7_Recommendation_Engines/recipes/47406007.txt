Title:
Chi Chi's Corn Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
2/3 cup cornmeal
1 can creamed corn
1 12oz pkg frozen corn, chopped in food processer
1/2 cup cornmeal
2/3 cup white sugar
4 tablespoons milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium bowl, beat butter with electric mixer until creamy.  Add cornmeal and beat until well combined.
In food processor or blender, process corn in short pulses to coarsely chop.  Leave several pieces of whole corn.  Stir the processed corn and the cornmeal into the butter mixture.
Add remaining ingredients until well combined.  Pour all into a 9x9-inch baking dish.  Cover with foil.
Put in oven with a pyrex filled with 2 cups water. Bake 30 minutes, then remove foil. Bake an additional 30 mintues. or until pick inserted in center comes out clean.  Remove baking dish and let rest 10 minutes.  Serve using ice cream scoop or large spoon.
