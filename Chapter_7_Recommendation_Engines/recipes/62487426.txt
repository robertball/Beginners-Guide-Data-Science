Title:
Mom's Italian Beef Barley Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds cubed beef round roast
1 qt low salt beef stock
1/2 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic pressed
1/2 tsp thyme leaves
1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
3/4 cup uncooked pearl barley
salt and pepper to taste
diced carrots & celery

Directions:
In a slow cooker, combine beef, water, beef stock, onion, garlic, thyme, tomato sauce, barley, carrots, celery, salt and pepper.
Cover, and cook on Low for 5 hours.
