Title:
Po' Folks Pizza Packet Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb boxed angel hair pasta
3   packets of pepper flakes (about 2 tsp)
6   packets of parmesean cheese (about 3
tsp)
2   tbsp butter
2   tbsp olive oil

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions and drain.
While past is draining place butter and olive oile in pot that past cooke din and heat till butter is completed melted.  Add peppers and let simmer about 1 minute.
Return pasta to pan, add parmasean, and toss well.  Serve immedietly.

Submitter: 
SHUNPR
Description: 
The name comes from those little pizza packets you get from pizza delivery.  Perfect for those low-buget times or the times you need something fast and easy.  It is so good you'll find yourself making it often.  Keep in mind the packets depending on pizza chain and will differ in yield.  Just add more or less of what you like you cannot go wrong.
