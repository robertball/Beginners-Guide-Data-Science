Title:
Mediterranean Brown Rice Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
1 1/2 cups uncooked brown rice
1 red bell pepper, julienned and cut into thirds
1/4 medium vidalia onion, chopped
1/2 cup raisins, softened*
1 cup cooked peas (frozen or fresh)
1/4 cup chopped olives, preferably kalamata
1/4-1/3 cup feta cheese
Balsamic Vinaigrette
1 tsp (heaping) dijon mustard
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Cook 1 1/2 cups brown rice according to package instructions.
Meanwhile, mix together bell pepper, onion, raisins, peas, and olives in a separate bowl; set aside.
Whisk together mustard, balsamic vinegar, and vegetable oil in another bowl; set aside.
Once the rice is done, add the vegetable mix, salt and pepper, and the desired amount of dressing (store the rest in an airtight container).
Add feta cheese just before serving. May be served hot or cold.

Submitter: 
Anne
Description:
