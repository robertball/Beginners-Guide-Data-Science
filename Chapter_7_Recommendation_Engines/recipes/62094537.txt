Title:
Wild Rice Artichoke Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box "Uncle Ben's Long Grain and Wild Rice Mix"
5-6 Italian Pear Tomatoes, chopped or substitute chopped cherry/grape tomatoes
1 small bunch parsley, no stems and finely chopped
1 bunch scallions, chopped include the green part.
1 jar Marinated Artichoke Hearts, drained and cut up
1 small can black olives draned and sliced
1 pkg frozen petite peas-cooked crisp
DRESSING:
1/4 C. salad oil  (canola)
1/2 C. vinegar (wine, balsamic, or herb will do)
1/4 C. sugar

Directions:
Place the Dressing ingredients in a jar with lid and shake well or use a blender. Refrigerate until ready to use.
Cut up the vegetables.
Cook the Wild Rice Mix  as directed on the package except reduce the water to 2 cups
and cook for only 20-22 minutes. Drain some of the cooking liquid from the rice.
Cook the peas crisp and drain and cool.
Cool the rice mixture and peas in a large bowl then add the cut up vegetables.
Pour dressing over the salad  and chill until ready to serve.
