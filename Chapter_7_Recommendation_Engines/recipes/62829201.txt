Title:
Mi's Baked Beans For A Crowd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1028521.jpg
Ingredients:
1 28 oz. can Pork and Beans
2-3 28 oz. cans Baked Beans (such as Bush's,any flavor)
16 oz. (or 1/2 of a 32 oz.bag) of Brown Sugar
1 18 oz. bottle BBQ Sauce (such as KC Masterpiece)
1 Red Onion, thinly sliced
1 lb. Bacon

Directions:
Cook bacon, only until partially crisp.  Mix remaining ingredients in a large roaster type pan.  Place partially cooked bacon on top.  Bake in a 300 degree oven, stirring after one hour.  Bake 4-6 hours total, stirring  occassionally.

Submitter: 
Sheila LaLonde
Description: 
Slow cooking is the key to the great flavor of these beans.  They are a huge hit at every BBQ and fish fry we take them to.  This recipe makes alot but is also a very forgiving recipe that can be easily scaled to a smaller batch.  Hope you enjoy them as much as we do!
