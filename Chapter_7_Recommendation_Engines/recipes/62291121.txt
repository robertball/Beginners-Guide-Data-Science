Title:
Layered Chicken Tortilla Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz corn tortilla chips
1 can nacho cheese soup
1 can chicken broth
1 can cream of chicken soup
10oz can tomatilla salsa
2 teaspoons cumin powder
1 package taco seasoning mix
2 cups diced tomatoes
1/2 cup cilantro
6oz can diced green chiles
6oz can sliced black olives
1/2 cup black beans
2 cups shredded chedder cheese
4 cups shredded cooked chicken
2 green onions - minced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees
Mix chicken broth, cheese soup, tomatilla salsa, cunin & garlic powder in a large bowl.
Divied remaining ingredients into 3, and make layers as follows: crushed tortilla chips, chicken, tomatoes, cilantro, green chilis, olives, black beans.
Pour sauce over each layer, finishing top layer with sauce, then sprinkle cheese and green onions over the top.
Tent with tin foil & cook for 45min at 325 degrees. Remove tin foil in the last 15 min of cooking to brown top.
