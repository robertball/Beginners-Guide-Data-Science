Title:
All-Purpose Cole Slaw Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups mayonnaise
1/3 cup sugar
1/4 cup cider vinegar
1/4 tsp. celery seed
1/2 tsp. seasoned salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
salt to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients by hand or in food processor. Chill for an hour or more, and then add to slaw vegetables.
