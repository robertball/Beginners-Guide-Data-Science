Title:
Holy Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Large Ripe Avacados
1 red onion
2 Cloves garlic- finely chopped
2 tsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1/4 cup Sour Cream
1 large ripe tomatoe- diced
1/2 juice of fresh lemon
Salt/Pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut avacadoes in half and remove pit. Scoop out flesh with sppon into large mixing bowl. Mash flesh with fork until slightly chunky. Add garlic, onion, olive oil tomatoe, sour cream and lemon. Mix until well combined. Ass salt and pepper and refigerate covered for 1 hr.

This is simple, easy and a yummy addition to a mexican meal- or as a super dip.
