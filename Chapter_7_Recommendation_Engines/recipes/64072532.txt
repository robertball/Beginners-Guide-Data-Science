Title:
Panini Sandwiches with Caramelized Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf focaccia bread
2 grilled chicken breasts cut in thin slices
1 large red onion cut in thin slices
1 Tbsp. olive oil
1 Tbsp. vinegar (cider, red wine or balsamic)
1 Tbsp. sugar
6 slices of cheese (provolone, pepper jack, etc.)
Add condiments such as:
mayonnaise
mustard
sliced tomatoes

Directions:
Grill chicken breasts & cut into thin slices.
Butterfly cut bread then in half & thirds so that you have enough for 6 sandwiches.
Place onions, olive oil, vinegar & sugar into small pan & cook to caramelize onions.
Place cheese & condiments out as well and let each person build a sandwich.
Heat George Forman Grill to 300 degrees and press sandwich in the grill until sandwich is warm and the cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Jane
Description: 
Be creative & use what you like, but don't skip the caramelized onions.
