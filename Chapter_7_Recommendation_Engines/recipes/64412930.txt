Title:
Portuguese Barbequed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2346024.jpg
Ingredients:
1/8 cup olive oil
3/4 tsp red pepper flakes
2 garlic cloves, crushed
1 tbsp smoked paprika
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 lemon, zested and juiced
1/2 cup dry white wine
4 lbs chicken pieces of your choice

Directions:
Combine all the marinade ingredients in a large resealable freezer bag.
Add chicken pieces and marinate for at least 4 hours or overnight in the refrigerator.
Preheat barbeque, oil grill lightly.
Remove chicken from marinade, place chicken on the grill, brush with marinade while cooking, barbeque approximately 15 minutes a side depending on thickness of chicken pieces and whether boneless or bone in.
