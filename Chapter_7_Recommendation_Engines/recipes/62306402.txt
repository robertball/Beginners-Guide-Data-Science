Title:
Clam Dip a la Fabius Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small can of minced clams
1 8oz softened cream cheese

Directions:
Open can and drain liquid into a paper cup
Slowly add liquid to cream cheese in your bowl
Stir and keep adding liquid till it is a good dip consistency (wont fall off your chip)
You will not use all the liquid-just most of it!!!
Add drained minced clams
Stir
Chill

Submitter: 
Dog Fish Head aka Fabius
Description: 
This is a very basic and quick dip to make.  Tastes even better with slightly salty chips!  Another oldie but goldie from way back when.  Up there with french onion soup dip!
