Title:
Mexican Chicken and Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 piece(s) chicken, breast, boneless, skinless (6 ounces each)
pepper, black ground to taste
3/4 teaspoon cumin, ground
1/2 tablespoon oregano, dried
1/2 teaspoon oil, olive, extra-virgin
1/4 cup(s) onion(s) chopped
1/2 tablespoon garlic minced
1/ 2 of a 4 1/2 ounce can pepper(s), green chile, canned, drained, chopped
1/4 teaspoon hot sauce
7 1/2 ounce(s) beans, black drained and rinsed
8 ounce(s) stock, chicken, fat-free, low-sodium (1 can)
1/8 cup(s) sour cream, nonfat
1 tablespoon cilantro, fresh chopped fine (for garnish, optional)

Directions:
Dice chicken into bite-size pieces. Season with pepper and toss with cumin and oregano. Heat olive oil in a medium pot over medium-high heat; add chopped onion and garlic and saut√© 5 minutes. Add chicken and stir while continuing to cook.
Add chiles, hot sauce, black beans, and chicken stock. Bring to a boil, cover, reduce heat, and simmer 20 minutes. Adjust seasonings to taste and serve. Top each serving with a tablespoon of sour cream and a sprinkling of cilantro.
