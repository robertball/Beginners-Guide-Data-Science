Title:
Fat Free Zucchini Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 2 cups all-purpose flour
* 2 cups whole wheat flour
* 3 teaspoons baking soda
* 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
* 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
* 1/4 teaspoon salt
* Egg Beater equivalent of two eggs
* 2 cups unsweetened applesauce
* 1 cup sugar
* 1 cup packed brown sugar
* 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
* 2 cups shredded zucchini, thoroughly drained
* 1 cup peeled and grated apple

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the flours, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt. In a large mixing bowl, beat the egg beaters until frothy. Add the applesauce, sugars and vanilla; beat until blended. Stir into dry ingredients just until moistened. Fold in the zucchini & grated apple.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease cups or place liners in two muffin pans. Fill cups  3/4 full. Should make approximately 22 muffins. Bake for 20 - 25 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Kate Fisher
Description: 
Regular zucchini bread gets a radically reduced overhaul. Very good and all but free of fat.
