Title:
Almond flour brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c blanched almond flour
2/3 c agave nectar
5 T cocoa powder
1/4 c coconut oil
2 eggs
1 t vanilla extract
1/2 c nuts (optional)

Directions:
1.  Preheat the oven to 350
2.  Combine all ingredients
3.  Spread in an 8x8 pan
4.  Bake for 30 minutes, let cool slightly before eating
