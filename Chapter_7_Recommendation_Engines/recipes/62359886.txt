Title:
hot wing baked tenders - low carb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs chicken  tenders or boneless breasts.
1/4 cup Franks RedHot Sauce for marinade
1/4 cup Franks RedHot Sauce for sauce
1 Tbsp butter
6 Tbsp Ranch dressing
2 oz celery sticks

Directions:
Cut chicken breast into tender size pieces.
Marinade chicken in 1/4 cup Franks sauce for 30 minutes or more.
Bake chicken on cookie sheet with plenty of room for drainage at 375 for 15 to 20 minutes minutes.
Combine 1/4 Franks and 1 Tbsp butter, cover, microwave until butter melts and blended.
Mix baked chicken with hot sauce & butter and return to oven for 5 minutes to set sauce.
Serve with 3 Tbsp of Ranch and 1 oz celery each.
