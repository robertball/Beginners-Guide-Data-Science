Title:
Apple Crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987434.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package escalloped apples
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Prepackaged crepes
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 1/2 tablespoons chopped pecans, toasted

Directions:
Thaw escalloped apples in microwave at MEDIUM (50% power) 6 to 7 minutes.
With a mixer, on medium speed, beat cream cheese, sweetened condensed milk, and vanilla until smooth.
Spread 1 tablespoon mixture down center of each Crepe; top with about 2 tablespoons escalloped apples. Roll up, and place, seam side down, in a serving dish.
Stir cinnamon into remaining cream cheese mixture; dollop over filled Crepes, and sprinkle with pecans.

Submitter:

Description: 
Apple Crepes make an elegant ending to an every day meal.
