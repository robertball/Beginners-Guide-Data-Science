Title:
Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. frozen chopped spinach-8 oz., thawed & drained
1 cup mayonnaise
1 1/2 c. sour cream
1 pkg. Knors dry vegetable soup mix
1 c. water chestnuts, drained & chopped
dash garlic powder

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.
Refrigerate.
Serve with Fritos or rye bread.
