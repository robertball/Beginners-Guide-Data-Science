Title:
Pork Chops with Dijon-Dill Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986631.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound thin pork loin chops
1 teaspoon oil
2 teaspoons HERB-OX® Chicken Flavored Bouillon Granules
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 cup plain yogurt
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
1/4 teaspoon dried dill weed
1/4 teaspoon sugar

Directions:
In skillet over medium heat, cook pork chops in oil 6 to 8 minutes, or until cooked through and pork reaches an internal temperature of 155 degrees F.  Remove pork to platter; season with bouillon, garlic salt and black pepper.  In small bowl, combine yogurt, mustard, dill weed, and sugar.  Serve sauce with pork chops.

Submitter: 
HERB-OX® Bouillon
Description: 
These delicately seasoned pan-fried pork chops take hardly any time at all to cook. Served with a mustard-dill sauce, your loved ones will think they are eating at a restaurant.
