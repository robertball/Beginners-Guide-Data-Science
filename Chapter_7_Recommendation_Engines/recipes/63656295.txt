Title:
Fresh Salsa I (Customized Smaller) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/527384.jpg
Ingredients:
3 jalapeno chile peppers (depending on hotness)
1 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 onion, chopped and divided by half
1 tablespoon sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers (Ro*Tel)
1 (14 ounce) diced or petite tomatoes
Juice of one lime
1/3 cup chopped fresh cilantro (more or less to taste)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Place jalapeno chile peppers on a medium baking sheet sprayed with cooking spray. Bake in the preheated oven 17 - 20 minutes, or until roasted. Remove from heat and chop off stems and coarsely slice.
Place jalapeno chile peppers, half the chopped onion, garlic, sugar, salt, ground cumin, cilantro, lime juice and diced tomatoes with green chile peppers in a blender or food processor. Chop using the pulse setting for a few seconds.
Add rest of the chopped onion and the 14 oz. can of diced tomatoes, stir.
Transfer to a 1 qt. bowl. Cover and chill in the refrigerator until serving.

Submitter: 
Kilyena
Description: 
This all-purpose salsa is great on tortilla chips, tacos and other Mexican-style favorites. Roasted jalapeno chile peppers give the salsa an excellent flavor.Modified from: Fresh Salsa I (Customized)
