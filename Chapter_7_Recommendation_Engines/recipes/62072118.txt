Title:
Decadent Shrimp Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz - Pkg frozen cooked peeled shrimp (60 count), thawed, patted dry, roughly chopped
4 - Ripe avocados
3 - Roma Tomatoes, Seeded and Diced
2 - Fresh Jalapenos, Seeded and diced
1/2 Cup - Red, White or Yellow Onions, diced
3 - Green Onions, Diced
4 Tbsp - fresh lime juice (about 1 1/2 limes)
3 Tbsp - Cilantro, finely chopped
1 1/2 Tsp - minced garlic
1 1/2 Tsp - cumin
1 1/2 Tsp - salt
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
Tortilla chips, for dipping

Directions:
Bowl #1:
Halve and pit 3 of the avocados. Scoop out the flesh with a tablespoon into a mixing bowl.
Mash the avocados with a fork or potato masher, leaving them somewhat chunky.
To the Avocado Add:
-	3 tbsp lime juice
-	2 tbsp cilantro
-	1 tsp cumin
-	1 tsp garlic
-	1 tsp salt
-	1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
Mash until smooth and stir in:
-	2 diced Roma tomatoes
-	1/4 cup onions
-	1 diced jalapeno
-	2 diced green onions
Stir well to combine.
Bowl #2:
Halve and pit remaining avocado.  Scoop out flesh and cut into 1/2 inch cubes (dont mash!).
To the Avocado Add:
-	12 oz frozen salad shrimp (150 â€“ 200 Count/lb)
-	1 diced Roma tomato
-	1/4 cup diced onion
-	1 diced jalapeno
-	1 tbsp lime juice
-	1 tbsp cilantro
-	1 diced green onion
-	1/2 tsp garlic
-	1/2 tsp cumin
-	1/2 tsp salt
-	1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
Gently toss to combine.
Fold shrimp mixture into the mashed avocado mixture, stirring to gently to combine, but being careful not to break up the cubed avocado.
Refrigerate for 1 hr.  Serve with Tortilla Chips.
