Title:
Creamy smoky chicken soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-4 cups precooked and fork shredded chicken
4 cups water
1 cup half and half
1/2 cup oil
12-14 oz bag of frozen corn
2 tbsp minced garlic
2 tsp garlic salt
2 cans Campbell's cream of chicken soup with herbs
8 oz block of cream cheese
8 oz pack of Seeds of Change Taco Roasted Chipotle simmer sauce
2 vegetable broth seasoning packets

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients in a large pot, simmer for 20 minutes, serve with cheese or sour cream garnish. Alternatively, place all the ingredients in either a slow cooker or an Instant Pot, cook the appropriate amount of time for each cooker, and serve.

Submitter: 
Brent Leonard
Description: 
Easy prep and ready in no time, this is another great soup that is low carb and keto friendly.
