Title:
Peanut Butter Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976707.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
3/4 cup confectioners' sugar
1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
6 tablespoons milk
1 (8 ounce) carton frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 (9 inch) graham cracker crust
1/4 cup chopped peanuts

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy. Add sugar and peanut butter; mix well. Gradually add the milk. Fold in whipped topping; spoon into the crust. Sprinkle with peanuts. Chill overnight.

Submitter: 
Jesse & Anne Foust
Description: 
During the warm months, it's nice to have a fluffy, no-bake dessert that's a snap to make. Packed with peanut flavor, this pie gets gobbled up even after a big meal! -Jesse & Anne Foust, Bluefield, West Virginia
