Title:
Vegetable Rice Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp. vegetable bouillon granules
2/3 cup long-grain rice
1/2 cup chopped green pepper
2 cups shredded zucchini, or
chopped broccoli
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup skim milk
1/2 tsp. onion powder
1/2 tsp. dried basil, crushed
1/2 tsp. dried oregano, crushed
a few grinds of black pepper
3/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2(8 oz.)container soft-style
cream cheese
2 Tbsp. diced pimento

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine bouillon granules and 1 1/2 cups water. Bring to a boil; add rice. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, for 20 minutes or until tender. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Meanwhile, in a medium saucepan,combine green pepper and 1/2 cup water. Bring to a boil; reduce heat. Add zucchini or broccoli. Cover and simmer for 3 to 5 minutes, or until crisp-tender.;drain well. Set aside.
In a large mixing bowl, combine eggs, milk, onion powder, basil, oregano, and pepper.
Stir Cheddar cheese and cream cheese into hot rice. Stir rice mixture into egg mixture. Stir in cooked vegetables and pimentos. Spoon into a 2-quart baking dish.
Bake, uncovered, for 30 to 35 minutes, or until center is set. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
