Title:
Cream Cheese Frosting II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/271285.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup butter, softened
2 cups sifted confectioners' sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
food coloring
colored sugar sprinkles

Directions:
In a medium bowl, cream together the cream cheese and butter until creamy. Mix in the vanilla, then gradually stir in the confectioners' sugar. Add food coloring. Sprinkle on colored sugar. Store in the refrigerator after use.

Submitter: 
Cooks4Kids
Description: 
My kids love frosting on just about anything. To make this one more more fun, I add color and colored sugar to the top. That makes whatever I frost a little more fun for the kiddos.Modified from: Cream Cheese Frosting II
