Title:
Easiest Bacon Ranch Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box spiral noodles
Buttermilk Ranch dressing
1/2 packet real bacon bits

Directions:
Boil noodles according to package directions.
In a mixing bowl, mix enough ranch dressing to cover the noodles nicely.
Throw in bacon bits, toss, and serve chilled.

Submitter: 
CUPYCAKESMURF
Description: 
This is a simple pasta salad, combining spiral noodles, buttermilk ranch, and real bacon bits.
