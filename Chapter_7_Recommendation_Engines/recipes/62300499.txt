Title:
Wonton Wonders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Ken's Simple Carnitas finely chopped
1 (4oz) can diced green chilis
1/2 cup Mexican shredded cheese
Wontons
Oil for frying

Directions:
Preheat oil to 350.  Mix together carnitas,chilis,and cheese.  Place 1 heaping teaspoon of mixture in the middle of a wonton.  Dampen the edges of the wonton and fold over.  Repeat with rest of ingredients.  Slowly drop into preheat oil for about 3 minutes.  Drain on paper towels.  AND ENJOY!!
