Title:
Vegan Strawberry Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package frozen strawberries
2 cups low fat soy/rice milk
1/2 cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
pinch of salt

Directions:
Place all ingredients together in a blender or processor. Blend to desired smoothness.
Freeze the mixture in an ice cream maker according to the manufacturer's directions.
Makes: 1 quart, Preparation time: 5 minutes + ice maker time

Submitter: 
ximena
Description: 
A delicious vegan version of classic strawberry ice cream
