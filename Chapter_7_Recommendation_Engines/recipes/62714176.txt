Title:
Peachy Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (21oz.) peach pie filling
1 can (20oz.) pineapple chunks, drained
1 can (11oz.) mandarin oranges, drained
2 medium firm bananas, sliced
1 cup green seedless grapes

Directions:
In a bowl, gently stir to combine ingredients.  Refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
The peach pie filling really makes this a great and versatile fruit salad by using the fruits you prefer.
