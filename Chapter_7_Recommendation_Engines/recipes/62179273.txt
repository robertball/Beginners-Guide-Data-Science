Title:
Garlic Bread Sticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flat beer
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons sugar
1 teaspoon salt
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
1/4 cup melted butter
garlic powder to taste
onion powder to taste
oregano to taste
parmesan cheese to taste

Directions:
Put beer, butter, sugar, salt, flour, and yeast in a bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select Dough setting, and press Start.
Remove dough from bread machine when cycle is complete. Roll the dough into a rectangle shape and cut into strips. Place strips of dough on a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper.  Brush lightly with melted butter on both sides and sprinkle with garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, and parmesan cheese. Cover and let stand 15 minutes.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees and bake until brown and crispy.

Description: 
This is a great bread machine pizza dough. It is easily converted into yummy garlic bread sticks.  Serve with your favorite pizza sauce as a dip.Modified from: Bread Machine Pizza Dough
