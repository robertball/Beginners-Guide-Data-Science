Title:
Hula's Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5047262.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs
1 cup light brown sugar
1 tbsp flour
1 tbsp milk
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup chopped pecans
1/2 cup pecan halves
9" shortcrust pie shell, unbaked

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (205 degrees C).
Melt butter.
In a medium bowl, break eggs and whisk until just broken.
Stir cooled melted butter into eggs.
Add the brown sugar and flour and stir, but don't beat.
Add the milk, vanilla and chopped pecans.
Pour into the pie shell.
Decorate the top with the pecan halves.
Bake for 10 minutes at 350 degrees then drop temp to 300 degrees and bake for 40mins.
Cover with foil at 20 minutes to prevent scorching the top.
