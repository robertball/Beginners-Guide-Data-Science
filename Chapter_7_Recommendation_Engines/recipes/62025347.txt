Title:
Eggnog Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/8 Cup Butter
3 Cups Sugar
1 Cup Egg nog(fullstrengh-not "lite" eggnog
6oz. White chips
1 1/2 Cups Mini-marshmallows
1 Cup Almonds (chopped) - optional
1/2 tsp. Nutmeg
1/2 tsp. Cinnamon

Directions:
Ling a 9X( pan with aluminum foil and set aside.  Keep butter chilled.  Spray sided of a large saucepan with butter-flavored PAM.  Heat eggnog and sugar at medium setting.  Bring to a rolling boil while stirring constanly with a wooden spoon.  Boil for 2 full minutes by the clock.  Now fold in the marshmallows, cinnamon, and nutmeg; the boil will probably stop until the marshmallows completely dissolve.  Bring back to a rolling boil for another 6 full minutes by the clock(start timing one the boil resumes).  Stir continuously.   The m ixture will start to turn brown during the boil.  If yoiu get brown flakes in the mixture then turn down the heat a little.  Remove from heat and add the butter, chips, and nuts.  Stir like crazy until thoroughly mixed or until it starts to lose it's glossy appearance.  Pour into prepared pan.  At this point yoiu may want to sprinkle a little nutmeg on the surface.  Cool at room temp.  Remove from pan, remove foil, cut into squares.
