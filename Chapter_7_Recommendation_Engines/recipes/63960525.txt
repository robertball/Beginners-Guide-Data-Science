Title:
Crock Pot Banana Bread Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup quinoa
1/2 cup (low-sugar) cinnamon sugar Coffee Mate Creamer
1 cup water
1 1/2 banana (past ripe)
2 TBS chopped walnuts
3 TBS brown sugar
1 1/2 TBS butter, melted
1/2 TSP vanilla extract

Directions:
Mash banana in a bowl and set aside.
In another bowl, mix the brown sugar and walnuts together.
Pour quinoa, creamer (or light cream), milk, water, butter and vanilla into the crock pot.
Add mashed bananas and stir to even distribute.
Sprinkle the sugar and walnut mixture into the quinoa and stir to mix.
Cook on low for 4-6 hours, or until the quinoa is fully cooked.  If you need to, add additional liquid or sugar to the mixture for taste.
Serve warm and garnish with slices of banana.
