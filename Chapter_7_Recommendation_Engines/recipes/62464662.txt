Title:
Parmesan Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 lbs. boneless round steak, tenderized was good
2 T flour
2T Parmesan or Parmesan and Romano cheese
2 T Italian or plain breadcrumbs, or panko
1 T Italian seasoning
1 T olive oil
1/4 C water
8 oz spaghetti
4 slices mozzarella or other cheese like muenster
1 jar (16 to 24 oz) pasta sauce, divided
1/4 C torn fresh basil leaves (opt.)

Directions:
Cut round stead to make 4 equal pieces. In pie plate, mix flour, Parmesan, breadcrumbs and Italian seasoning. Place steak in flour mixture, turning to coat and pressing lightly so it adheres.
Preheat oven to 250 or 275 degrees. In oven-safe large skillet, heat oil over med-high heat. Add steak and cook 6-8 min. or until browned, turning once halfway through cooking. Remove skillet from heat. Carefully pour water into pan; cover with lid. Bake 1 1/2 hrs.
About 30 min before steaks are done, prepare spaghetti as label directs; drain.
Place 1 slice mozzarella over each piece of steak. Top steak with 3/4 C pasta sauce, then sprinkle with basil, if desired. Return skillet to oven; bake 5 min., uncovered, or until cheese melts. Heat remaining pasta sauce in pasta pot. To serve, top spaghetti with remaining pasta sauce, any juices in skillet and steak.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Use a tenderized round steak for the best flavor of steak. After coating steak in flour,cheese and seasoned breadcrumbs, frying for a few minutes in skillet, the steak is then slow baked in low oven. Serve over spaghetti.
