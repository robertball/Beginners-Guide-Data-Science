Title:
Cafe Rio Sweet Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs of pork loin or spareribs
3 cups of Dr. Pepper
1 cup of brown sugar
1 tsp of garlic powder
1 tsp of onion powder
1 tsp of chili powder
1 tsp of salt
1 4 ounce can of diced green chiles
10 ounces of mild red enchilada sauce

Directions:
step 1: combine 1 cup of Dr. Pepper and 1/4 cup brown sugar in a bag for marinade. Cut pork into strips, and marinade for several hours, or overnight.
step 2: place pork in slow cooker, discard marinade. Add garlic, onion, salt, 1 cup of Dr. Pepper, and 1/2 tsp of chili powder. Mix together and cook on high for 3 hours.
Step 3: blend together enchilada sauce, green chiles, 1/2 tsp of chili powder, 3/4 cup of brown sugar, and 1 cup of Dr. Pepper.
Step 4: after 3 hours, pull pork out, discard liquid in the slow cooker. Shred the pork with two forks. Combine blended mixture and shredded pork in slow cooker, and cook for 30 minutes.
Step 5: serve with rice, or wrapped in a tortilla

Submitter: 
Kevin Muncey
Description: 
A sweet and tangy pork great for enchiladas or burritos
