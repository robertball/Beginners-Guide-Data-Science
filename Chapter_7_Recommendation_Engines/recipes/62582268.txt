Title:
Hawaiian Cheese Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf (1#) Hawaiian sweet bread
8 oz Swiss cheese
3 slices red onion, chopped
1/2 C butter, melted
1 T minced garlic
1 t salt

Directions:
Cut bread diagonally into 1-in slices to within 1 in of bottom. Repeat cuts in opposite direction. Cut Swiss cheese into 1/4-in slices; cut slices into small pieces. Insert into bread. Combine the onion, butter, garlic and salt; spoon over bread.
Wrap loaf in foil. Bake at 350 for 25-30 min or until cheese is melted. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Simple and fast, this mouth-watering loaf is a hit with everyone and at every kind of function
