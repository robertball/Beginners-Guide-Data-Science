Title:
slow cooker pot roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3.5 lb. rump or shoulder roast
(1) 12oz. package Pictsweet Frozen Seasoning Blend (green and red peppers, onion, parsley, and celery)
1 can of beef broth
1 package of au jus dry seasoning mix
Enough water to cover roast

Directions:
Place fat trimmed roast in slow cooker
Add all other ingredients on top of the roast.
Set slow cooker on low and let cook for 6-8 hours.
Serve with mashed potatoes, au jus, and assorted veggies.

Submitter: 
Cyndee S.
Description: 
Quite tasty and easy to prepare.  My family loves this along with mashed potatoes, homemade bread, and vegetables.
