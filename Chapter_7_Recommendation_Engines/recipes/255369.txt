Title:
Chunky Monkey Grilled Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4554348.jpg
Ingredients:
2 slices raisin bread
peanut butter
1/2 banana, sliced
2 tablespoons chocolate chips, or to taste
1/4 cup ricotta cheese
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 dash ground cinnamon (optional)

Directions:
Toast raisin bread in a toaster oven until lightly brown, 2 to 3 minutes.
Spread peanut butter onto toasted bread. Top 1 slice with banana; top other slice with chocolate chips. Cover banana and chocolate chips with ricotta cheese. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on top.
Toast in the toaster oven just until cheese is melted, 3 to 5 minutes. Sandwich slices together.

Submitter: 
Folkorb
Description: 
A fun and easy break from the usual that kids will love. 
This recipe makes 1 sandwich, but it's easy enough to make 2, 3, or 4.
