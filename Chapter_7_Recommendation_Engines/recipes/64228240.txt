Title:
Heavenly Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. sugar
2 Tbsp. flour
1 c. pineapple juice
1 egg, beaten
1 T. butter

Directions:
Cook over medium heat till smooth and thickened, stirring constantly.
Cool completely.
Fold in 1 c. whipped cream.
