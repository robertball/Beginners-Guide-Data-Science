Title:
Swiss Chard Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chopped cooked Swiss Chard
1 cup breadcrumbs
1 beaten egg
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
Salt and pepper
Canola oil to fry.

Directions:
Mix Swiss Chard with breadcrumbs, cheese, salt and pepper.
Add beaten egg and mix till combined. Add more bread crumbs if necessary.
Shape into patties about 1/2 inch thick.
Fry in hot oil until browned on both sides and drain on paper towels.
