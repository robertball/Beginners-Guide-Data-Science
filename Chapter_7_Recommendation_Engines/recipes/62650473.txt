Title:
Whole Wheat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups water
1/3 cup oil
1/3 cup honey
1 T salt
2 T Lemon Juice
2 Cups Bread flour
5 cups whole wheat flour
2 T dough enhancer
2 T bread enhancer
2 T yeast

Directions:
Mix the above ingredients into a nice dough.
Place dough in bread pans lightly oiled
Allow 30 minutes to 2 hours for dough to rise.
Preheat Oven to 400 degrees
Bake for 30 minutes, longer if you want a crisper crust.

Submitter: 
Tom Reynolds
Description: 
Great whole wheat bread recipe I got from my mother.  It is difficult to mess this up.  I use my bread maker (at 1/4th the amount) and it makes a tasty treat fresh out of the machine
