Title:
Cherry Dump Salad Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
15 oz crushed pineapple, drained
1 can cherry pie filling
1 can evaporated milk
2c mini marshmallows
8oz cool whip
1c chopped pecans.

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in order.
Refrigerate before serving.
