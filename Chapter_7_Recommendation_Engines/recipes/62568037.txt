Title:
The Best Mexican Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (250g.) package cream cheese, softened
1 (500ml.) container sour cream
1 (445ml.) small jar mayonnaise
1 (430ml.) jar salsa
1 bunch green onions, chopped
1 large tomato, diced and seeded
1 large red bell pepper, diced
1 (340g.) package shredded cheddar cheese (only use half the package)

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, mix the cream cheese, sour cream and mayonnaise until smooth. Transfer to serving dish. Top with salsa and layer with onions, tomato, red pepper and shredded cheddar cheese. Cover and chill until ready to serve. Serve with nacho chips or pita triangles.

Submitter: 
Jason F.
Description: 
I've made this dip more times than I can count and people always go crazy over it!
