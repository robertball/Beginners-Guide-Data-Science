Title:
Spicy Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/954867.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs of meatballs
1/2 cup of distilled vinegar
1 cup of ketchup
3 tbs of worchestire sauce
2 tbs of tobasco sauce
6 tbs of brown sugar
Add water to desired Consistency

Directions:
Mix ingredients together and cook in crock pot for about 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Tracy Kathleen
Description: 
These are simple and delicious. I like to cook these on cold rainy days. They are always a hit at pot lucks too.
