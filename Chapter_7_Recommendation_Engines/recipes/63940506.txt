Title:
Chicken rice soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 boneless skinless chicken breast
2 cans family size Campbell's chicken and rice soup
3 cans Campbell's chicken and wild rice soup
1 can rotel mild or reg
2 large cloves of minced garlic
2 heaping tablespoons of parsley
1 1/2 teaspoons paprika
1/2 tsp chili powder
2 cups water
Several shakes of mrs.dash original seasoning
3 carrots cut into 1 inch long pieces
2 stalks of celery cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1 can drained corn

Directions:
Season chicken as you normally do and cook in cubes, set aside.
Combine everything in a big pot to cook until boiling turn down to a simmer, add chicken and any broth chicken created while cooking. Simmer for 20 mins or until vegetables are soft. Pour into bowl and garnish with cheese, avocado or tortilla chips
