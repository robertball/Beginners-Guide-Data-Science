Title:
Warm Green Bean and Potato Salad with Goat Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds red potatoes, cut into bite sized pieces
1/2 pound frozen haricort vert (french green beans)
1 cup chopped red onion
4 cloves minced garlic
1 cup chopped bottled roasted red bell peppers
1/2 light balsamic vinaigrette dressing
1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
olive oil cooking spray
Salt (for cooking water)
8 ounces of goat cheese - you can also use laughing cow garlic cheese wedges to cut calories a bit

Directions:
Boil potatoes in salted water until tender, 8-10 minutes.
Drain and set aside.
Meanwhile in a skillet sprayed with cooking spray, saute frozen green beans and red onion until tender, approximately 4 minutes.
Add minced garlic and saute 1 additional minute until garlic is fragrant
Add cooked potatoes and green beans to a large bowl.
Add balsamic viniagrette, chopped roasted red bell peppers,  balsamic dressing and basil. Toss lightly.
Add crumbled goat cheese and toss lightly to melt.

Submitter: 
Linda T
Description: 
Delicious salad of green beans, potatoes, red peppers and goat cheese goes perfectly with chicken or pork. Experiment with other potato types such as purple and other soft cheeses, such as garlic and herb.
