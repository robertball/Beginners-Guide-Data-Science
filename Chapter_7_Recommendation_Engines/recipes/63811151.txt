Title:
CREAMY NEW ORLEANS PRALINES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sugar...
1 cup buttermilk
1/4 cup light corn syrup
1 pinch salt
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 quart pecans

Directions:
1. Combine sugar, buttermilk, corn syrup and salt in a very large pot (it will foam considerably when soda is added).
2. Bring ingredients to a rolling boil.
3. Add soda, stir and cook until soft-ball is formed when dropped in cold water.
4. Remove from heat and add vanilla.
5. Beat until color changes and candy thickens.
7. Stir in pecans.
6. Drop by teaspoons onto greased cookie sheet or one lined with parchment paper.
8. DO IT FAST before the candy hardens.
