Title:
Kale Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cup(s) kale
4 teaspoon oil, olive
2 teaspoon garlic powder
2 pinch salt, sea
4 tablespoon cheese, Parmesan
grated

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.
Cut leaves off thick kale stems. Wash and thoroughly dry kale in a salad spinner.
Toss kale with oil, garlic, and salt. Spread kale on a cookie sheet in a single layer.
Bake for 10 to 15 minutes, until edges are darkened, but not browned. Watch carefully and do not burn.
Remove from the oven and sprinkle with cheese.
