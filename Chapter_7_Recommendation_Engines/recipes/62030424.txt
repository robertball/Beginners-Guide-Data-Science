Directions:
Cook sausage in soup pot until no pink remains. Add onions, cook uncovered for 5
minutes or until onions are soft. Add tomatoes, chicken broth and basil. Bring to a boil.
Stir in macaroni and simmer covered for 10 minutes or until macaroni is tender. Add salt
and serve.
