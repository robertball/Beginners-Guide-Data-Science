Title:
Fresh Salsa I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 jalapeno chile peppers
5 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 onion, chopped
2 teaspoons white sugar
2 teaspoon salt
1/4 heaping teaspoon ground cumin
1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
Juice of one large lime
Fresh cilantro to taste
1 (28 ounce) whole peeled tomatoes (for restaurant style salsa)
OR 2 (14 ounce) petite diced (for chunky salsa)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Place jalapeno chile peppers on a medium baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven 15 minutes, or until roasted. Remove from heat and chop off stems.
Place jalapeno chile peppers, garlic, white sugar, salt, ground cumin, cilantro, lime juice and diced tomatoes with green chile peppers in a blender or food processor. Chop using the pulse setting for a few seconds. Mix in onions, large can of diced tomatoes. Chop using the pulse setting to attain desired consistency (for restuarant style salsa) OR mix in chopped onions, 2 cans petite diced tomatoes and stir (for chunky salsa). Transfer to a medium bowl. Cover and chill in the refrigerator until serving.

Submitter: 
Kilyena
Description: 
This all purpose salsa is great on tortilla chips, tacos and other Mexican-style favorites. Roasted jalapeno chile peppers give the salsa an excellent flavor.Modified from: Fresh Salsa I
