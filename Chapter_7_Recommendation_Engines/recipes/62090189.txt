Title:
Moroccan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10, boiled new potatoes
1 chopped onion
garlic
1 teaspoon of saffron
1/2 teaspoon of ginger
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 sliced lemon (thinly)
4 pieces of chicken
salt and pepper
some olive oil

Directions:
Just mix all ingredients together.
add the oil
cover with foil paper and cook on a high heat for about 1 hour, and then lower the heat for another hour.
check that chicken is thoroughly cooked before serving

Submitter: 
jane Gibraltar
Description: 
This is a very simple Moroccan dish that can be also done with fish. It was taught to me by my Moroccan friend and is a traditional recipe from Maraketch
