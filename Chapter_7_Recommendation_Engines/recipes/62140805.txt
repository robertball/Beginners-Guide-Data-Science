Title:
Chicken Parmesan a la Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
1/4 cup of bread crumbs
1/4 teaspoon herbs de provence
2 chicken breasts, skinless
1/4 cup onion, chopped
1 garlic clove, crushed
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
1/2 cup skim milk
5 ounces frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
1 teaspoon nutmeg

Directions:
In a small mixing bowl combine half of the cheese, bread crumbs, and herbs seasoning.
Coat chicken pieces in cheese mixture lightly and arrange pieces in a baking dish.
In a small saucepan cook onion and garlic in olive oil till tender.
Dissolve the flour with the milk and add to saucepan.
Cook and stir till bubbly.
Stir in drained spinach and nutmeg.
Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Spoon spinach mixture over chicken and sprinkle with remaining parmesan.
Bake, uncovered, in a 175C oven for 30 or till chicken is fully cooked.
