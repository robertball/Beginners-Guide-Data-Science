Title:
BBQ Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
1/2 bottle BBQ sauce
8 oz brick cream cheese, softened
1/3 cup ranch dressing
1 1/2 cups cheddar cheese, shredded
Tortilla chips

Directions:
Pre heat the oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 9x9 pan or a pie plate.To the pan, add the chicken and mix in the BBQ sauce. In a separate bowl stir and combine the cream cheese with the ranch dressing. Spread this mixture evenly over the BBQ chicken. Cover the top with shredded cheddar and bake in the oven for 30 minutes. Let cool for about 10 minutes. Dip your chips in the dip!
