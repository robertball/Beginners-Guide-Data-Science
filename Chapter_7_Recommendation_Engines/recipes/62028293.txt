Title:
zucchini pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 C. zucchini or yellow squash(take seeds out if using mature squash)
1/2 C. brown sugar
1/2 C. white sugar
1/4 tsp cinnamon
2 tbsp. margarine
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
3 tbsp. flour
3 tbsp. lemon juice

Directions:
Peel, slice, and cover squash with boiling water; let stand 5 minutes, then drain off water and put into unbaked pie crust. Cover with a mixture of brown sugar, white sugar, cinnamon, salt nutmeg and flour. Dot with margarine and sprinkle with lemon juice.Cover with top crust. Bake at 450 degrees for 15 minutes; then lower temperature to 350 degrees, and bake for an additional 45-60 minutes.
