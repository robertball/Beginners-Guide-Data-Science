Title:
Bacon avocado cream cheese dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1007435.jpg
Ingredients:
4oz cream cheese
1/4 cup salsa
5 slices bacon, cooked and chopped
3 green onions, chopped
1/2 avocado, cut into cubes
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except for avocado. Add avocado to top. This is great as a dip for crackers, or fill egg roll wrappers and fry for a tasty appetizer.

Submitter: 
Heath
Description: 
Yummy dip or wonton filling that can be quickly made, but sure to impress!
