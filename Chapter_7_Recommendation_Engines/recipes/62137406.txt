Title:
Apple Tart Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 green apples peeled and diced
1 box frozen puff pastry
1/2 cup of sugar
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 box (8 oz)cream cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Roll out each puff pastry on a floured surface. Cut each into six small squares. Spread cream cheese on center of each square, set aside. In a separate bowl mix diced apples, cinnamon, and sugar. Spoon mixture onto squares. Pinch together corners of each square. Bake until pastry is golden and fluffy approx. 20-25 minutes.

Submitter: 
MichelleSchaffer
Description: 
An amazing mix of sweet and tart that will truly amaze your senses. The pastry adds dimension as well. It's like apple pie without the fork!
