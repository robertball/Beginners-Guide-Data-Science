Title:
Spicy Black-Eyed Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound bacon (diced)
1 cup white onion (chopped)
2 T. freshly diced jalapenos
1 clove garlic (minced)
3 cups water
2 beef bullion cubes
2 cups canned diced tomatoes
4 cans black-eyed peas with liquid
3 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Using a stock pot, cook the diced bacon until slightly browned.  Add onions, garlic, and diced jalapenos.  Cook until bacon just becomes slightly crispy.  Add water, bullion cubes, tomatoes, and peas.  Bring to a low boil, then let simmer for 30 minutes, stirring frequently.  Add cheese and continue to cook for 15 minutes, stirring frequently.

Submitter: 
LISA FRIEND
Description: 
This is a wonderful spicy and flavorful soup - perfect for a crisp fall day.  It is incredibly easy too and goes great with cornbread!
