Title:
Chicken Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6122993.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. paprika
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. onion powder
1- 1 1/2 #chicken breast

Directions:
Mix and rub all ingredients over chicken.
Heat oil in pan and brown chicken (2-3 min. per side).
Bake @350 degrees for 20-25 min.
