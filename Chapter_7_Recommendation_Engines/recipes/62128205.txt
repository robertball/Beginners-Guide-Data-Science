Title:
Quiche Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 sheet puff pastry, thawed
3 eggs
2 T milk
3 slices swiss cheese
4 slices thick cut bacon, cooked
2-6 drops tabasco sauce (to taste)
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Lightly spray a mini-muffin pan (24 muffin size) with cooking spray.
Cut each bacon slice into 6 pieces.
Cut each slice of cheese into 16 squares.
Cut 24 rounds of puff-pastry the size of bottom of the muffin cup.
Blend eggs, milk, tabasco, salt & pepper well.
In each muffin cup place a round of pastry, a piece of cheese, and a piece of bacon.
Add egg mixture until about 3/4 full.
Top with an additional slice of cheese.
Bake at 350F, 40 minutes until golden brown.
Remove from pan to wire rack.
Can be served hot or room temperature.

Submitter: 
LARANEFF
Description: 
These were an experiment that was suppose to be like individusl quiches.  I hate pie crust so I tried puff pastry instead, & they ended up really puffy.  They were a big hit & I've made several times since to rave reviews.
