Title:
Light Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Fresh strawberries, apples, or other fruit
1 cup cold 1% lowfat milk
1 (3 ounce) package sugar-free instant vanilla pudding mix (Kroger brand)
2.5 cups reduced-fat whipped topping, thawed (8 oz container Kroger Light)
1/4 teaspoon almond extract
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Wash strawberries; place on paper towels to dry. In a medium sized bowl with lid, whisk milk and pudding mix for 2 minutes (hand mixer gets out the lumps).
Fold in whipped topping and vanilla and almond extracts until well blended.  Cover and chill until ready to serve.
With the ingredients listed, entire recipe has 680 calories and 27.5 grams fat; 1/6th recipe has 113 calories and 4.5 grams fat (plus the fruit).

Description: 
This is a delicious fruit dip that is lower in fat and calories, without sacrificing flavor.  This will be my go to dip for fruit.Modified from: Cream-Filled Strawberries
