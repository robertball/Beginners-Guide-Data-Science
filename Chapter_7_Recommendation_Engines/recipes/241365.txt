Title:
Banana, Avocado, and Spinach Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4481367.jpg
Ingredients:
1 banana, sliced
1/2 avocado, peeled and sliced
1/2 cup fresh spinach
1/2 cup 1% milk
6 ice cubes
2 teaspoons honey
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Blend banana, avocado, spinach, milk, ice cubes, honey, and vanilla extract together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Maria Ro
Description: 
Quick and delicious smoothie that can be used as a breakfast or snack!
