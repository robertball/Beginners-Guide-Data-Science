Title:
Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds squash sliced thinly.
3/4 cup dry Bread crumbs
1 small onion chopped
2 eggs
1/2 cup melted butter (divided)
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 tablespoon of sugar
1 cup sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Grease a 2 qt. casserole dish.
Sau·té the onions and sliced squash in a large covered skillet  until soft. Uncover the last few minutes to cook off some of the juice. Drain well.
In a mixing bowl add the  sautéed squash and onion, 1/2 cup of bread crumbs, 2 eggs, 1/4 cup of melted butter, salt, pepper, sugar and Cheddar cheese and mix thoroughly.
Pour into the  pre greased 2 qt casserole dish. Drizzle the remaining butter on top then add the remaining  bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese on top of the dish.
Place in the oven for  approximately 1 hr. or until golden brown.
