Title:
Thai Crockpot Red Curry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs boneless skinless chicken breast (or thighs)
10 cloves garlic
1 yellow onion
3 T tamari
3-4 T Thai red curry
2 T fish sauce
2T or more ginger
1-2 T maple syrup
juice of 1/2 lime
stalk lemon grass crushed
2 c mushrooms
2-3 c kale
2 cans lite coconut milk
1 can full fat coconut milk
lemon grass stalk, pounded
handfuls basil

Directions:
Put everything except kale and basil in crockpot
Cook on high (or low) for 4-5 hours
During last hour, add kale and basil
serve with basil, cilantro, lime, green onion, siracha or sambal olek

Submitter: 
seren
Description: 
Easy Thai chicken curry in crockpot; next time cook on low to avoid curdling of coconut milk
