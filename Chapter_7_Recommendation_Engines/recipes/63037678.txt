Title:
Banana Walnut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1 egg
1 1/8 cup sugar
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 TBL buttermilk
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
3 ripe medium-large bananas (mashed)
1/2 cup + 1/3 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Grease a 9x5x3 loaf pan and dust with flour. Bleand together the flour, baking soda and salt and set aside. Mix together the egg, sugar and vegetable oil until combined. Add teh flour mixture and when blended add the buttermilk, vanilla and mashed bananas and mix until combined. Fold in 1/2 cup chopped walnuts and pour batter into prepared loaf pan. Top batter with remaining 1/3 cup chopped walnuts. Bake for 45 to 60 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool for 10 minutes on a wire rack before removing from pan.
