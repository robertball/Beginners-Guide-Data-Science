Title:
1 Hour Cinnabon Cinnamon Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the Dough
1 cup Milk, warm
4 1/2 tsp active dry yeast (fast rising kind)
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1 tsp salt
1 egg
1/3 cup canola oil
4 - 4.5 cups flour
For the filling
1/3 cup softened butter
1 cup brown sugar, packed
2 tbsp cinnamon
For the Frosting
2 oz cream cheese
1/4 cup butter, salted
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup corn sugar
1 tsp lemon juice, fresh
1.25 cups icing sugar

Directions:
In a large mixing jar, add warm milk, 1 tbsp sugar and active yeast, stir and proof for 5 minutes
In a large bowl, add flour, the remaining sugar and salt, mix well.
Once yeast mixture looks frothy and bubbly, pour into dry ingredients.
Start to stir on low, add egg and oil, scrap edges of bowl to stir flour evenly.
Knead for 5 minutes, if dough seems a bit sticky, stir in 2 tbsp flour at a time until it starts pulling away from the bowl.
Rest dough for 5 minutes
Preheat oven to 350 degrees, get tray ready with parchment paper.
Roll out dough into an 18 x 24 inch spread.
coat with butter.
place filling (brown sugar) in the middle and spread on the dough leaving a 1 inch strip at the top.
Roll the log tightly
Cut in 1.5 inches pieces
Frosting, whip cream cheese and butter together, stir in vanilla, corn syrup and lemon juice.
Once rolls are out of the oven, frost with 1/2 the frosting.
Apply the rest if the frosting after they have cooled a few minutes.
Serve warm
