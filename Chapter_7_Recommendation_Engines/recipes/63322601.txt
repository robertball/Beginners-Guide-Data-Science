Title:
Slow Cooker Sweet Potatoes (Yams) and Marshmallows Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/879974.jpg
Ingredients:
2 - 2lb8oz Cans of Yams.
1/3 Cup Butter
3/4 Cup Light Brown Sugar
16 oz. bag of Marshmallows
non stick cooking spray

Directions:
Spray slow cooker with non stick cooking spray.
Drain sweet potatoes and place into slow cooker.
Slice butter into 1/4 inch slices and place butter on top of sweet potatoes evenly spaced.
Sprinkle on brown sugar.
Cover and cook on high for 3 - 3 1/2 hours.
Add marshmallows, replace lid and cook until soft and somewhat puffy. Approximately 15 minutes
Cafeful not to over cook of marshmallows become liquid. for another

Renee Coley
Description: 
I came up with this one Thanksgiving because I was short on oven space everyone loved it, and I have made it for every Thanksgiving and Holiday meal ever since.
