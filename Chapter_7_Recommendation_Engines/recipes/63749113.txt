Title:
Crockpot Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sliced apples
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
pinch salt
2 cups of oatmeal
2 cups of half and half
2 cups water.

Directions:
Place ingredients in crockpot in listed order
Do NOT stir.
Cook overnight for 8 - 9 hours on low
