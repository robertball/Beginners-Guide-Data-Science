Title:
Chipotle BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
1/2 c. chopped yellow onion
2 Tbsp. Minced garlic
1 tsp. Ground cumin
1 Tbsp. Dark chilie powder
1/3 c. Malt vinegar
1 c. Ketchup
3/4 c. firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 c. Soy sauce
2 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2 Tbsp. chipolte in adobo sauce ( puree in blender)

Directions:
To make the sauce:
Heat oil in a sauce pan over medium heat. Add onion,garlic,cumin, and chili powder. Saute' until onions are soft. Add the vinegar and de-glaze, stirring and scrapping the bottom of the pan to loosen all the bits adhered to the pan. Add the rest of the ingredients and simmer til slightly thickened.
Transfer to a blender & blend until smooth.
Cool then transfer to an air tight container & refridgerate until ready to use.
I usually put it into a used ketchup bottle.
