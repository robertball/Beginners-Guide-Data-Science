Title:
Cheerios Snack Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 c Cheerios
1 c. pecans
1/2 c almond slivers
1 c peanuts
1/2 c brown sugar
1/4 c butter or margarine
1/4 c dark corn syrup
Pinch salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.  Mix together Cheerios, pecans, almonds, and peanuts.  In a small saucepan, melt together brown sugar, butter, corn syrup and salt.  Pour over cereal and nuts and mix together.  Pour onto baking sheet and bake 25 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes.  Cool completely and break into small pieces.
