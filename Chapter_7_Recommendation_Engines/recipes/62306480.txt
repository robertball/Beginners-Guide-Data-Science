Title:
Cheesy Oven Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 potatoes, peeled and quartered
1/2 cup flour
1/2 to 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
3/4 cup butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350&#0176;
Put butter in a 9 x 13 baking dish
Place in oven to melt butter
Mix all dry ingredients in a zip top bag
Add potatoes to bag and shake well to coat
Place coated potatoes in melted butter in HOT dish being careful to use pot holders.
Bake 1 hour, turning after 30 minutes to brown evenly.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
Easy, cheesy potato dish.  Great with baked ham for Easter.
