Title:
Oven-Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989068.jpg
Ingredients:
4 baking potatoes
2 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted
2 teaspoons paprika
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Peel potatoes and cut into large chunks; place in a shallow 2-qt baking pan. Pour butter over and toss until well coated. Sprinkle with paprika, salt and pepper. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 45-60 minutes or until potatoes are tender.

Submitter: 
Linda  Gaido
Description: 
These golden, melt-in-your-mouth potatoes go perfectly with roast beef. They make a homey side dish that's also convenient because they can share the oven with the baked apple slices Mom serves for dessert. -Linda Gaido, New Brighton, Pennsylvania
