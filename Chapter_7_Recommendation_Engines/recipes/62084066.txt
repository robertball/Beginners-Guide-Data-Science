Title:
Boiled Crab Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Live Blue crabs or 10 Lbs. of king crab legs.
2 large Onions Quartered
10 Large Cloves of Garlic Pealed and cut in 1/2
2 Lemons Quartered
2 Oranges Quartered
5 stocks of Celery Quartered
4 Tablespoons of Black Pepper
4 Tablespoons of Seasoned Salt
6  8 Hot Peppers

Directions:
1.	You will need a large pot. Fill ¾ full of water.
2.	Place all Ingredients except the Crabs in to the pot, and bring to a hard boil, let boil for 20 minutes.
3.	Add crabs and put the lid on the pot.
4.	Let boil for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Venita Johnson
Description: 

I learned this style of coking from my mother in law; this recipe will work with Crawfish, Lobster and Shrimp. My Mother in law used Blue Crab, and I like King crab.
