Title:
SWEET AND SPICY EASY MEATBALLS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 PACKAGES PRECOOKED FROZEN MEATBALLS
1 CAN MANWICH SLOPPY JOE SAUCE
1/2 CUP BBQ SAUCE
1/2 CUP BROWN SUGAR
1 1/2 tsp MINCED GARLIC
2 TSP BLACK PEPPER
1/2 CUP PINEAPPLE CHUNKS(DRAINED)
1/4 CUP DICED GREEN ONION
1 TBLS RED PEPPER FLAKES
1 TBLS WORSTESHIRE SAUCE
2 TSP HOT PEPPER SAUCE (LIKE LOUISIANA)

Directions:
IN A LARGE BAKING PAN ARRANGE MEAT BALLS AND BAKE IN 450 DEGREE OVEN FOR ABOUT 15 MINUTES.MEANWHILE, IN A SAUCE PAN MIX SAUCES OTHER INGREDIENTS AND COOK ABOUT 10 MINUTES. REMOVE FROM HEAT AND ADD ONION, PINEAPPLE AND RED PEPPER. POUR SAUCE OVER MEATBALLS STIR TO COAT THE MEATBALLS AND COOK FOR 25 MINUTES.
