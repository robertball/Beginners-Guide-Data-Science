Title:
Chipotle Shrimp & Pineapple Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup bottled baja chipotle marinade, divided
1-1/2 lb. large shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 can (20 oz.) DOLEÂ® Pineapple Chunks, drained
1 medium red onion, cut into chunks
2 red or green bell peppers, cut into chunks

Directions:
Pour 3/4 cup baja marinade over shrimp in large sealable plastic bag. Refrigerate and marinate for 30 minutes.
Remove shrimp from baja marinade and plastic bag; discard marinade.
Thread shrimp, pineapple chunks, onion and bell peppers onto skewers.
Grill or broil 8 minutes turning and brushing with reserved baja marinade or until shrimp turns pink. Discard any remaining marinade.
