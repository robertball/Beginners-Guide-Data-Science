Title:
Red Velvet Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1104892.jpg
Ingredients:
For the Cupcake
¤ 2&1/3 cup flour
¤ 1 cup vegetable oil
¤ 1&1/2 cup sugar
¤ 2 large eggs
¤ 1 cup buttermilk or substitute*
¤ 1/2 teaspoon salt
¤ 1&1/2 teaspoon vanilla
¤ 1 tbps white vinegar
¤ 1 tbps baking powder
¤ 2 tbps cacao powder
¤ 3 tbps red liquid colorant
Cream Cheese Frosting
¤ 250g of cream cheese
¤ 1/2 cup butter
¤ 1 teaspoon vanilla
¤ 2-3 cups of confectioner' sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix the flour, salt, baking powder, cacao powder and vanilla in a bowl and the buttermilk with vinegar in a separate one.
Whisk the vegetable oil and sugar with an electric mixer on medium speed and add the eggs, one at a time. Incorporate alternately the flour mixture and buttermilk while beating.
Fill the cups more than three quarters full. Bake for 20 minutes or until the tester toothpick inserted comes out clean.
Frosting: Cream the butter, cheese and vanilla and slowly add the sugar until obtaining a creamy mixture. Frost the cupcakes and refrigerate before serving.
