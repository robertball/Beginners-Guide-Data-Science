Title:
Chocolate Mousse and Praline Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c. butter or margarine
1/4 c. packed light brown sugar
2 T. water
1 T. cornstarch
2/3 c. coarsely chopped pecans
1 - 8" crumb pie crust
1 t. unflavored gelatin
1 T. cold water
2 T. boiling water
1/2 c. sugar
1/4 c. cocoa
1 c. COLD heavy whipping cream
1 t. vanilla extract

Directions:
Microwave the butter until melted. Add brown sugar, water, and cornstarch; Stir with a wire whisk until smooth.
Microwave on HIGH for 1 minute, or until mixture comes toa  boil. Stir in chopped pecans; spread mixture on bottom of crust and place in freezer while preparing filling.
In a small cup, sprinkle gelatin over cold water; let stand 1 minute to soften.
Add boiling water; stir until gelatin is completely dissolved and mixture is clear. Cool slightly, about 5 minutes.
Meanwhile, stir together cocoa and sugar. Add whipping cream and vanilla.
Beat with an electric mixer until stiff peak form; slowly pour in gelatin and beat just until blended.
Drop on top of praline mixture and smooth out. refrigerate until smooth.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
This pie combines two classic desserts; the chocolate mousse layer is light and fluffy and the praline layer is simlar to the traditional nut and caramel confection from the South.
