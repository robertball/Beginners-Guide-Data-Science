Title:
Jello Cottage salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Serving Size / Yield
8-10 servings
Ingredients
3 C. cottage cheese
one 16-oz. container frozen non-dairy whipped topping
one 14-oz. can mandarin oranges, drained
one 16-oz. can crushed pineapple, drained
two 3-oz. pkgs. fruit flavor gelatin

Directions:
Directions
Mix cottage cheese and whipped topping; fold in fruit. Sprinkle gelatin over mixture and blend well. refrigerate until ready to serve
