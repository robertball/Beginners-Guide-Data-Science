Title:
Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp. cream of tartar
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
3/4 cup shortening
1/4 cup butter
2 eggs
1 cup sugar

Directions:
Stir flour, cream of tartar, baking soda and salt into a large bowl. Cut shortening and butter into dry ingredients with pastry cutter. In small mixer bowl, beat eggs. Add sugar gradually and beat until thick and lemon color. Mix well. Place in refrigerator over night. Roll dough out on floured board. Sprinkle cut out cookies with colored sugar. Bake on ungreased cookie sheet at 350 degrees for 10 minutes.
