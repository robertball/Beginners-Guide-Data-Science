Title:
Meat-A-Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/312573.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb good quality bulk Italian Sausage
1 lb lean ground beef
1 egg
1 ½ cup fresh bread crumbs
4 large garlic cloves, finely minced
2 tablespoons dried oregano
1/2 cup olive oil
salt and lots of fresh ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients well.  Form into 2” meatballs and place on baking sheet.
Bake for 30 minutes at 375 degrees until cooked through.
