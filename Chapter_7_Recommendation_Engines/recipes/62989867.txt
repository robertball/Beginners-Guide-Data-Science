Title:
Tempting Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 oz Chicken Stock
2 Cups Hot Water
2 Cans Black Beans
1 Can Diced Tomato's
1/2 6 oz Can Tomato Paste
1 Onion Chopped
2 Cloves Garlic Minced
1 Cup Store Bought Salsa
2 Cups cooked, diced chicken (rotisserie works well)
3 Tbs Corn Meal
2 Tbs Canola Oil
1 Tbs Chili Powder
1 Tbs Roasted Cumin
1 Tsp Garlic Powder
1 Tsp Garlic Salt
1 Tsp Salt
GARNISHES
Tortilla strips lightly grilled
Limes (Squeeze 1/4 lime in each bowl)
Sour Cream
Shredded cheese (Mexican blend or cheddar)
Pico De Gallo
Chopped Red Onions
Cilantro

Directions:
Saute onion in 1 tbs canola oil till lightly browned.  Add minced garlic and saute for one more minute.
In a large stock pot add chicken stock, water, black beans, tomato paste, diced tomatoes, salsa, sauteed onions and garlic, chicken, 1 tbs canola oil, chili powder, roasted cumin, garlic powder and garlic salt.  Stir well then bring to a boil then reduce to a simmer.
Mix 3 tbs corn meal in a small amount of cold water.  Add to soup and simmer for 30 minutes.
Just before serving, stir in tortila strips then top with garnishes.  The garnishes really make the soup so don't be shy with them!

Description: 
This is my version of tortilla soup.  We love Mexican food in my house and this soup really hits the spot.  We usually serve it as the main course with bread sticks and a green salad.
