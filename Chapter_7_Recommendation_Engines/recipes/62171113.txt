Title:
Sage Sausage and Cider Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound ground sage pork sausage
1 tablespoon butter
1 Tablespoon of olive oil
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup chopped onion
2 teaspoons fresh sage chopped
1 golden delish apple peeled, cored and cubed
6 fresh slices or 1/2 loaf of Italian bread cubed including crust.
3/4 cup apple cider
2 teaspoons of chicken base
1/2 cup of water

Directions:
Place ground sage pork sausage in a large, deep, cast iron skillet.
Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown.
Drain and set aside.
In same skillet, melt butter over medium heat.
Add olive oil.
Stir in celery, onion cook and stir until tender.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
Mix sausage into celery and onion mixture.
Add apples cubes and fresh sage. continue to cook on medium heat until apples are just turning soft.
Turn heat off.
add bread cubes, apple cider and mix with wooden spoon.
sprinkle chicken base over everything.
add water and stir again.
Bake in the preheated oven 30 minutes, or until lightly browned and edges are crisp.

Submitter: 
Terry E.
Description: 
My family, as far back as I can remember loves stuffing that is moist,full of sage and as my wife calls it, mushy the layers of flavors in this one passed the family test and is now considered a winner for the holiday season.
