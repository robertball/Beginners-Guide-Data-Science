Title:
Citrus Slush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7032948.jpg
Ingredients:
1 orange, peeled and chopped
1 lime, peeled and chopped
12 cubes ice

Directions:
In a blender combine orange, lime and ice cubes. Blend until smooth. refrigerate for 5 minutes. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter: 
Lin
Description: 
Orange and lime are blended with ice in this refreshing summer drink.
