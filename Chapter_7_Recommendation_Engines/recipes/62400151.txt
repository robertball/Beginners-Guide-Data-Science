Title:
Country Fried Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 - Beef Cube Steak
Breading
2c - All-Purpose Flour
1t - Paprika
1/2t - Black Pepper
1/4t - Salt
1/4t - Onion Powder
1/8t - Garlic Powder
1/8t - Ground Red Cayenne Pepper
Batter
1c - Buttermilk
1T - Hot Sauce
Grease
1lb - Bacon

Directions:
Soak meat in batter, then pull out and cover in breading (for a thicker crust repeat). Let meat sit in breading while you cook the bacon. After bacon is cooked crispy leaving a good amount of grease, cook steaks in bacon grease about 5 min. a side.
