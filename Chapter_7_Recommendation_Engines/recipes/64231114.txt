Title:
General Tsao's Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1689952.jpg
Ingredients:
Chicken:
10	chicken tenders, cut into bite size pieces
2	tablespoons canola or olive oil, and more if needed
1/8	cup cornstarch
¼	teaspoon salt
1/8	teaspoon pepper
Sauce:
2	tablespoons cornstarch
4	tablespoons low sodium soy sauce
1	cup water
2	tablespoons minced garlic
4	teaspoons ground ginger
6	tablespoons honey (or agave nectar)
1	teaspoon red pepper flakes, (for medium spice ½ teaspoon),  (for mild ¼ teaspoon)
Garnish
4	scallions, thinly sliced
1-2 	teaspoons toasted sesame seeds

Directions:
In medium bowl add all sauce ingredients and mix well; Set aside
In another bowl mix 1/8 cup cornstarch salt and pepper,  mix well; dredge chicken in cornstarch mixture, shaking off excess cornstarch add to a large skillet with oil; cook over medium heat until golden on both sides.  Remove from skillet and keep warm.
Stir sauce ingredients and add to skillet, stirring constantly until thickened add chicken back to skillet remove from heat and stir.  Garnish with scallions and sesame seeds. Serve over rice.
