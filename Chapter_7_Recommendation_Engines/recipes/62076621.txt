Title:
Chicken stuffed with goats cheese and red pepper. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
1/2 cup white wine
1 package goats cheese
roasted red peppers
1/4 cup whipping cream
2 cloves of garlic (chopped fine)
thyme
3 tbs. olive oil
3/4 cup of chicken broth

Directions:
1) Make a hole in the side of each breast.
Stuff with goats cheese and a slice of red pepper.
2) In a lg. skillet, heat olive oil. Brown
chicken. 4 min. each side.
3)add chicken broth and white wine. Cover and simmer over med. heat for 10 minutes
4)Take chicken out
