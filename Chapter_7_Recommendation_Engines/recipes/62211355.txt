Title:
Fudgy Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2cup butter
3oz. usweet. chocolate
1cup sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1teaspoon Vanilla extract
2/3cup flour
1/4teaspoon baking soda
1/2cup chopped nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Melt the butter and unsweet chocolate in a sauce pan. Take off of heat.
One by one whisk in the sugar, eggs, and vanilla into the melted chocolate mix.
In a seperate bowl combine the flour, baking soda, and chopped nuts, mix.
Stir the chocolate mix into the flour mix.
Pour into a greased 8x8 in. pan and bake for 30min.
