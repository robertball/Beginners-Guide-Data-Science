Title:
Mauigirl's tweaked Avocado Corn Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 hearts of romaine lettuce, chopped
2/3 cup bottled lite Caesar salad dressing
4 ears corn, grilled, cooled and cut off the cob
2 avocados - peeled, pitted and diced
1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
optional
pine nuts

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix the lettuce, corn, avocado, and tomatoes. Drizzle with dressing and sprinkle with pine nuts. Toss and serve.

Submitter: 
mauigirl
Description: 
Crisp romaine lettuce, kernels of tender corn, avocado slices, and cherry tomatoes are dressed with Caesar salad dressing. Enjoy!Modified from: Avocado Corn Salad
