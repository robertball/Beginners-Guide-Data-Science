Title:
Spoonable EggNog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs, separated
1/2 cup sugar
1/8 tsp salt
1/2 cup golden rum, or 1/2 tablespoons rum flavoring
2 cups whipping cream, whipped.

Directions:
Beat egg whites until stiff.
Beat egg yolks, sugar, and salt until very thick and lemon colored; stir in rum or rum flavoring.
Fold egg yolk mixture into whipped cream, and fold egg whites into egg yolk-cream mixture. Chill thoroughly. (About 4 hours)
Serve in cups with spoons. If desired, sprinkle with nutmeg.

Submitter: 
CBerrier86
Description: 
This spoonable eggnog is great for the holidays and feels more like a dessert than a drink.
