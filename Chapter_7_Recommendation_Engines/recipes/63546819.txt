Title:
Roasted Acorn Squash Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Acorn squash, seeds & skin removed. Cut into 1" cubes.
5 oz. Baby arugula
1/4 cup dried cranberries
1/4 cup chopped pistachios
2 oz. chèvre cheese, crumbled
2 Tbs olive oil
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
For Dressing:
3 Tbs apple cider vinegar
1 Tbs dijon mustard
1 clove minced garlic
1 tsp zest of orange
2 Tbs orange juice
2 Tbs maple syrup
1 teaspoon garam masala
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 cup olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Lay acorn squash evenly on a lined cookie sheet
toss with olive oil
In a small bowl mix cinnamon, onion powder, cumin, salt & pepper together.
Sprinkle over acorn squash.
Place on middle rack in oven and bake for 15-20 minutes or until tender.
Remove from oven and allow to cool completely.
Dressing:
In a medium glass bowl whisk dijon mustard, minced garlic, apple cider vinegar, orange zest, orange juice, maple syrup, garam masala, salt and pepper.
slowly add olive oil, whisking constantly. Place in fridge to chill while making salad.
Salad:
In large bowl add arugula/spinach, acorn squash, dried cranberries, pistachios and chèvre cheese. Toss with salad dressing and serve.
