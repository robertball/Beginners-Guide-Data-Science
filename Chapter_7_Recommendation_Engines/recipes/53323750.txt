Title:
Red Rooster Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes
2 fluid ounces vodka
2oz cranberry juice
2 oz orange juice

Directions:
Fill a tall glass with ice. Pour in vodka. Fill glass to 1/2 full with cranberry juice. Top with orange juice. Stir.
