Title:
Creamy Oven-baked Rice Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup rice cooked in 2 cups of salted water until done
3/4 cup sugar
1 can evaporated milk
pinch of nutmeg
raisins if desired

Directions:
Mix cooked rice with remaining ingredients. Set oven to 350*F Place rice mixture in baking dish and into a water bath and bake for 1 hour.
