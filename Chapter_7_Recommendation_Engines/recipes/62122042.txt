Title:
Betty's Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
1 pkg pre-washed spinach greens
6 hard boiled eggs, sliced
1 can bean sprouts, drained
1 lb bacon, browned, drained, and crumbled
Dressing:
3/4 c. sugar
1/3 c. ketchup
1/4 c. white vinegar
1 T. Worcestershire sauce
1 med onion, chopped

Directions:
Prepare dressing in blender and refrigerate overnight.
Layer salad ingredients in order given in salad bowl.  Pour dressing over and serve.

Submitter: 
Amy Hague Miller
Description: 
This recipe is from a local Toledo restaurant.  Betty Timko created the recipe - it can still be found at many local Toledo, OH restaurants.
