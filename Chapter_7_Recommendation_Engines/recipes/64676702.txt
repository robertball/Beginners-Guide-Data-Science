Title:
EASY Low Carb Turkey Veggie Egg Muffin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4702514.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb Lean Ground Turkey (for leaner recipe, use 90% lean10% fat)
1 lb Thawed Frozen Mixed Vegetables (carrot, corn, edamame, green peas, string bean)
10 Eggs
1 Onion
1 Tablespoon minced garlic
1 Red Bell Pepper
2 Teaspoons Salt
1 Teaspoon White Pepper
1 Teaspoon Oregano
1 Teaspoon Grated Parmigiano-Reggiano Cheese
1 Tablespoon Adobo Seasoning
1 Teaspoon Paprika Powder
NOTE: you may adjust the amount of spices and salt to your liking

Directions:
Thaw frozen vegetables.
Preheat oven to 400°F.
Sauté onion and bell pepper on medium heat for 3 minutes.
Add in ground turkey, salt, white pepper, Oregano, grated Parmigiano-Reggiano Cheese, paprika powder, Adobo seasoning, mix well, sauté for approximately 8 minutes until protein is almost cooked.
Add in the vegetables, mix well, sauté for another 2 minutes.
Prepare 2 sets of 12 muffins baking trays (makes 24 muffins), spray with olive oil.
Fill each cavity with filling equally.
Beat eggs, fill in each cavity.
Bake at 400°F for 12-18 minutes.

Description: 
This recipe would be perfect for those of you who are super busy, but wants to feed yourself a healthy, low carb and low-calorie breakfast. It is easy to make! This recipe makes 24 muffins. You can cook this the night before and store them in the fridge. This tasty breakfast will help you start the day clean and keep you energized!
