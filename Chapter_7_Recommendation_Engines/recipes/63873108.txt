Title:
Yummy and Sweet Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour; 5 tablespoons sugar; 1 tablespoon baking powder; ½ teaspoon salt; 1-¾  cups milk; 6 tablespoons vegetable oil; 2 large eggs.

Directions:
1. Place all of the ingredients in a large bowl and combine until smooth and well blended. 2. Heat large skillet on medium heat and coat with cooking spray. 3. Pour in 1/2 cupfuls of batter and cook until bubbles form. 4. Flip it over with a spatula and cook until the pancake is browned. 5. Add toppings like strawberries and whipped cream if wanted.
