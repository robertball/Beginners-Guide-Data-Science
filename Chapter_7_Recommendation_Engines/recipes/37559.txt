Title:
Rocky Road Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981959.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cold milk
2 (3.4 ounce) packages JELL-O Vanilla or Chocolate Flavor Instant Pudding and Pie Filling
2 cups thawed COOL WHIP Whipped Topping
1 cup PLANTERS Peanuts
1 cup BAKER'S Semi-Sweet Real Chocolate Chips
1 cup JET-PUFFED Miniature Marshmallows

Directions:
Pour milk into large bowl.  Add pudding mixes.  Beat with wire whisk 1 to 2 minutes or until well blended.  Gently stir in whipped topping.
Stir peanuts, chocolate chips and marshmallows into pudding mixture.
Spoon into 8 dessert dishes or cups.  Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve.
