Title:
Cheesy Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/656900.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
3 tablespoons garlic powder
2 tablespoons dried parsley
2 (1 pound) loaves Texas Toast
1 pound package mozzarella cheese; grated

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Mix all ingrediants together except mozzarella cheese.  Spread on Texas toast slices and..
Bake in the preheated oven approximately 10 minutes, until lightly toasted. Remove from oven.
Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese  Return to oven approximately 5 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bread is lightly browned.
