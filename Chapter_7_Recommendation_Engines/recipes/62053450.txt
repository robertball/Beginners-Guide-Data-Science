Title:
Chipotle Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
1/3 cup olive oil
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 TBS. soy sauce
1/2 tsp. salt
5 garlic cloves, coarsely minced
1 tsp. chipotle chili powder
1/2 tsp. ground cumin
1/2 tsp. ground coriander
1 tsp. dried minced onion

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and mix well. Pour over meat choice and marinate at least 6 hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
BlondeCook
Description: 
Ground chipotle chili powder adds a distinct smokey flavor to this marinade. Can be used to marinade chicken or beef. After meat is cooked, use for quesadillas, salads, fajitas, or eat by itself. Very good flavors!
