Title:
Slow Cooker Pulled Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (3 pound) pork shoulder
1 bottle root beer
1 (18 ounce) bottle your favorite barbecue sauce
4 small onions
8 hamburger buns, split and lightly toasted

Directions:
Peel and cut the onions in half or quarter depending on the size; place on bottom of slow cooker; Place the pork shoulder in a slow cooker; pour the root beer over the meat. Cover and cook on low until well cooked and the pork shreds easily, 6 to 7 hours, or cook on high for two hours and low for 2-3 hours; . Note: the actual length of time may vary according to individual slow cooker. Drain well. Stir in barbecue sauce. Serve over hamburger buns.

Submitter: 
Jay Yerex
Description: 
Using root beer to season slow cooked pork shoulder makes a novel, yet tasty preparation method guaranteed to win applause.Modified from: Slow Cooker Pulled Pork
