Title:
Southwestern Style Corn and Black Bean Pudding Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 corn tortillas, torn into 2 inch pieces
1 can seasoned black beans with liquid
1 medium sweet yellow onion, quartered
1 medium bag frozen corn
2 c milk
2 large eggs
1 cup shredded cheddar Cheese, divided
1 small can diced mild green chilis
1 small jar pimentos
1 tsp hot sauce
1 tsp smoked paprika
1/2 tsp salt
1 box Jiffy corn bread mix
1 small can sliced black olives (optional)
Scallions, thinly sliced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
Spray an 13 x 9 baking dish with non-stick pan spray, or butter liberally.
Arrange tortilla pieces on bottom of dish covering all exposed areas.
Spoon beans evenly over tortillas in dish.
In food processor, place onion and corn and pulse a several times.
Add milk, 1/2 cup cheese, eggs, chilis, pimentos, hot sauce, paprika and salt; pulse until just mixed.
Add corn bread mix and pulse a few times until just blended.
Spoon into baking dish covering beans (do not stir).
Bake uncovered at 325 Degrees F. for 45 to 50 minutes, or until set.
Immediately top casserole with remaining 1/2 cup cheese and sprinkle with olives and scallions.
Let rest 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Alta Nesbitt Rees Sieker
Description: 
Frozen corn, black beans, hot sauce, pimentoes and green chilis are combined with milk and eggs and corn bread mix creating this delightfully light meatless side dish. A true winner in any weather!
