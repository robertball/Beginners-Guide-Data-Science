Title:
Easy Pasta! Refreshing and delish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg thin spaghetti noodles, boiled.
Half lemon juice
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1cup, finely chopped green olives
2 stalks celery, finely chopped
1 carrot, finely chopped
1bell pepper, finely chopped
1/2 yellow onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup, olive oil
1 cup, mayo
Season salt to taste, optional
Mini pepperoni, diced

Directions:
Boil noodles as directed.and run under cold water. Add to large pasta bowl. Chop all veggies, make sure it's finely chopped. Add all veggies to pasta.add pepperoni,  In a small bowl, add mayo, lemon juice and olive oil. Mix until well cooperated. Pour over pasta and toss until well coated. Add salt and pepper. Season to taste. I also add season salt if you like a kick to it.

Submitter: 
Lanie
Description: 
This pasta is addicting! This my go to pasta when I have the pasta craving. It's light and refreshing. This is something I always bring to potlucks and I never leave with any leftover. The green olives is what makes it taste so yummy!
