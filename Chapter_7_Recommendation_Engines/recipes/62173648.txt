Title:
Beef Choufleur Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bonless beef round steak (cut in cubs)
2Tbsp butter
1 small head cauliflower-seperated
1 small green pepper(chopped)
1/4C soy sauce
1 clove minced garlic
2Tbsp cornstarch
1/2tsp sugar
1 1/2C beef stock
1C green onions with greens (sliced)
3C hot cooked rice

Directions:
Cut meat into 1/2in squared, brown and simmer in the butter about 5min. Add the cauliflower, green pepper, soy sauce and garlic. Stir lightly to coat with soy sauce. Cover and simmer until barley tender(10min). Blend cornstarch, sugar and broth. Add to the meat with the green onions. Cook stirring until throughly heated. Serve over the hot rice.
