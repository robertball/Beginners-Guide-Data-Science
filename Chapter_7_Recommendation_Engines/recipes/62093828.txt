Title:
Lamb and Feta Meatballs in a Sweet & Sour Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
500gms Minced Lamb
1 Large Onion, diced
2 Garlic Cloves, crushed
60gms Black Pitted Olives, diced
90gms Feta
1 Egg
FOR THE SAUCE
2 tins chopped tomatoes
2 red peppers, diced
2 onions, diced
3 garlic cloves, crushed
2 tablesp white wine vinegar
2 tablesp brown sugar
Soy Chilli Sauce - to taste
Tabasco - to taste

Directions:
Meatballs
Combine all ingredients except the Feta in a bowl & mix
Cut Feta into about 25 pieces
Roll into approx 45-50gm Balls placing one piece of Feta in the middle of each meatball
Cook on the hob for approx 15mins
Cool and put to one side.
For the Sauce
Fry onion, garlic and red peppers in olive oil.
Add tomatoes, wine vinegar & sugar, cook for about 10 mins.
Add other ingredients to taste
Add meatballs to the sauce, reheat and serve

Submitter: 
Andypoc
Description: 
Lovely lamb meatballs with a Feta Cheese middle - much lighter than beef and with a sweet and sour sauce.
