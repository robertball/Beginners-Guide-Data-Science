Title:
Chicken Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Small onion
2 cloves garlic
1/4 C butter 1/2 lb. Chicken (or 1 chicken boullion cube)
4 oz cream cheese
basil
Garlic salt
Pepper
1 cup milk
1/4 cup parmesean cheese
1/2 box noodles

Directions:
1. Chop onion and garlic.
2. Saute in 1/4 C butter in a large pan
3. Add 1/2 lb. Chicken (or 1 chicken boullion cube). Cut up chicken into small pieces as it cooks.
4. Add 4 oz of cream cheese and stir until melted. (Begin boiling water for noodles)
5. Add spices to taste- basil, garlic salt, and pepper.
6. Add 1 C milk and stir continuously until thick
7. Add 1/4 C parmesean cheese
8. Serve over cooked noodles.
