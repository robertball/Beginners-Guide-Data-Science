Title:
Butterscotch Apple Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982742.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
2 small all-purpose apples, pared and chopped
1 cup butterscotch-flavored chips
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 1/3 cups flaked coconut
1 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (325 degrees F for glass dish). In 13x9-inch baking pan, melt butter in oven. Sprinkle crumbs evenly over butter; top with apples.
In heavy saucepan, over medium heat, melt butterscotch chips with sweetened condensed milk. Pour butterscotch mixture evenly over apples.
Top with coconut and nuts; press down firmly. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool. Cut into squares. Garnish as desired. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
Apples and butterscotch chips are baked on top of a graham cracker crust to make an easy sweet treat.
