Title:
Strawberry Cheesecake Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*  2 quarts strawberries, sliced
* 2 T sugar
* 2 T almond extract or rum
* 2 8 oz blocks cream cheese, softened
* 1 C. sour cream
* 2 C. powdered sugar
* 1 t. vanilla extract
* 1/4 t. almond extract
* 16 oz tub whipped topping or 1 pint cream, whipped
* 1 large angel food or pound cake, torn into pieces

Directions:
1.  Set aside some berries for garnish.
2. Mix rest with sugar and almond extract or rum.
3. Set aside.
4. Mix cream cheese, sour cream, powdered sugar, vanilla and almond extract.
5. Fold in whipped cream.
6. In a trifle bowl: start with cake layer.
7. Dip cake pieces into whipped cream mixture.
8. Layer into trifle bowl.
9. Add a layer of strawberries.
10. Repeat layers.
11. Garnish with remaining berries. Refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Heather Maki
Description: 
Here's a different way to have your cheesecake. Deliciously beautiful to look and taste!!!
