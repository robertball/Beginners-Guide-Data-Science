Title:
Bake Bre in Puff Pastry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 (17.5 oz) package frozen puff pastry, thawed
8 oz wheel of Brie Cheese
1/4 cup sliced almonds
Small jar of Chutney

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
Lightly grease a 9" pie pan
Slice the wheel of Brie in half, horizontally so that the havles are two, flat wheels of Brie.
Lay the puff pastry in the pie pan.
Place half the Brie (rind side down) onto the pastry dough. Sprinkle almonds evenly over the top and then spoon some chutney over the almonds.
Place the other half of the Brie (rind side up) over the almonds.
Bundle the pastry dough around the Brie.
Bake for 15 - 20 minutes.
Let cool for 5 minutes.
