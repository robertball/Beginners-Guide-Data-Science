Title:
Cracked Pepper and Sea Salt Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless pork chops
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 TB cracked black pepper
1 TB sea salt
1/2 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Coat pork chops with olive oil
Sprinkle tops of pork chops with spices.
Bake @ 350 for 30 mins
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
CNM CATERING
Description: 
I love cracked pepper and sea salt potato chips so I decided to use that idea on pork chops. Easy and quick for a delicous meal.
