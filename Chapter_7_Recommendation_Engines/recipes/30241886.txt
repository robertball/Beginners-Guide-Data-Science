Title:
Green Tomato Chow Chow relish Recipe

Ingredients:
10 green tomatoes
6-8 chopped onions
4 cups green peppers
2 cup sugar
2 cups cider vinegar
2 TBSP celery seed
2 TBSP Mustard seed
1 Tsp pepper

Directions:
Bring, Sugar, cide, spices to boil, add in veggies and cook for 10 minutes
