Title:
Mini Skillet S'mores Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2547202.jpg
Ingredients:
1 graham cracker
2 large marshmallows
4 squares of chocolate bars

Directions:
Set oven to broil.
Cut marshmallows in half.
Break chocolate bar and graham cracker in to 4 squares.
Place one quarter of graham cracker in 3.5 inch cast iron mini skillet. Top with one square of chocolate and half of large marshmallows. Repeat with remaining 3 skillets.
Broil for 3 minutes or until marshmallows start to brown. Remove skillets from oven and press marshmallows into chocolate.
Enjoy!!

Description: 
We camp often and my kids always want s'mores even when we are not camping. An easy, extremely quick, portion controlled way of making s'mores.
