Title:
Dressing for Slaw or Broccoli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3c. Miracle Whip Light
1/3c. apple cider vinegar
18 drops Stevia extract
1t. salt
1t. celery seeds,opt
1/4t. tumeric

Directions:
Combine with a whisk, it will be lumpy, but thats OK. I use this on 1# pkg. Dole cole slaw mix & 1c. chopped onion. It's best served the next day.

Submitter: 
mamaG
Description: 
This uses the herb Stevia instead of sugar or substitute. Old fashioned taste & keeps well. Makes enough for 8c. shredded cabbage or chopped broccoli & cauliflower
