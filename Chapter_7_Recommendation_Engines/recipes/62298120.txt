Title:
Tater Tot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef or turkey
1 medium onion
1 garlic clove
2 TBSP Worcestershire sauce
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (16 ounce) container Greek yogurt or sour cream
1 (8 ounce) package sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 (14.5 ounce) can French style green beans
2 pounds tater tots, frozen

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch casserole dish. Get tater tots out of freezer.
2) Brown ground beef with onion and garlic clove. Add Worcestershire sauce and black pepper to beef. Drain any excess grease.
3) In a large mixing bowl combine soup, Greek yogurt, green beans, and cheese. Mix well.
4)Transfer to casserole dish.  Neatly layer the potato rounds over the top.
5)Bake covered for 40 minutes. Uncover and sprinkle with shredded Cheddar. Bake uncovered 10-15 minutes or until the potato rounds are crispy & golden.

Description: 
Tater tots baked with cream of chicken & mushroom soup, ground beef (or turkey) Greek yogurt (or sour cream), sharp Cheddar, and green beans. Very filling and far from bland.Modified from: Creamy Tater Tot Casserole
