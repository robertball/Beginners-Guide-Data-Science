Title:
Sweet Tomato Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15-20 medium ripe tomatoes
4 Granny Smith apples
5 medium sweet onions
1T pickling spice
5 oz white vinegar
2 cups white sugar

Directions:
Skin tomatoes by using technique found on All-Recipes.
Discard skin and seeds, chop.
Peel, core and chop apples.
Peel and chop onions.
Tie pickling spice in cheese cloth square.
Add all to a large pot and boil together for 10 minutes.
Can using hot water bath method for 40 minutes.
Cool on racks keeping an eye on the lids, making sure they seal properly.

Submitter: 
Janet Raymond
Description: 
This is my husband's grandmother's recipe.  It is AMAZING served over meatloaf!
