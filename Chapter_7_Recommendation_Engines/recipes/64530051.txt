Title:
Easy Greek Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tomatoes
Parsley
Garbanzo beans
Lime juice
Olive oil

Directions:
Chop tomatoes and add to a large mixing bowl with rinsed garbanzo beans. Add remainder of ingredients, use portions to your taste. Toss all ingredients in bowl and enjoy!
