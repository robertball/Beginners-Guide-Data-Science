Title:
Simple Bean Dip ("Scoops and Poops") Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can vegetarian refried beans
1 lg package cream cheese, softened
1 cup sour cream
1-2 Tbsp taco seasoning to taste
1, 8oz package Mexican Shredded Cheese Blend

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 325
2. Mix together beans, cream cheese, sour cream, and seasoning until smooth. Place in pie plate.
3. Add 1 layer shredded cheese on top
4. Bake uncovered for 20-30 minutes until warmed through and cheese is melted.
Serve with Fritos or Tostitos Scoops.
