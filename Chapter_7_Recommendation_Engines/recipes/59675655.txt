Title:
Chuck's Favorite Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups dry mixed pasta
24 oz  shredded  cheese
1 (15 ounce) container Ricotta
1 16 oz container sour cream
2 eggs beaten
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup dry bread crumbs
1/4 cup butter, melted
Worchester sauce/hot sauce to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil, add pasta, and cook until done; drain.
In a large bowel  mix ricotta, sour creme eggs salt pepper hot sauce and worchester sauce add  macaroni, shredded Cheddar cheesed pepper. Pour into baking dish. mix together bread crumbs parmisian and melted butter. Sprinkle topping over macaroni mixture.
Bake 40-45 minutes, or until top is golden.
