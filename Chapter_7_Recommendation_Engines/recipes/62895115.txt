Title:
Spinach Orzo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons margarine or butter
2 cloves minced garlic
3 1/4 cups chicken broth
1 3/4 cups uncooked orzo
2 cups fresh chopped spinach
6 tablespoons freshly grated parmasian cheese
4 tablespoons pine nuts

Directions:
Melt margarine or butter over low heat in a 3 qt. or larger saucepan.  Saute garlic for 1 minute.  Add broth and orzo.  Bring to boil.  Simmer 15 minutes or until liquid is absorbed.  Stir in spinach, cheese and pinenuts.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Alley
Description: 
A modified, delicious recipe from a dear friend that I have made hundreds of times.  I won FIRST PRIZE in a local cooking contest with this recipe!  It goes well with any meat dishes, especially lemon chicken.
