Title:
Salisbury Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed French onion soup
1  pounds ground beef
1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
1 egg
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
10 oz. sliced mushrooms
1 large brown onion sliced
1/4 cup of butter
2 tablespoon all-purpose flour
1/2 cup water
2 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon mustard powder

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together 1/3 cup condensed French onion soup with ground beef, bread crumbs, egg, salt and black pepper. Shape into 4 oval patties.
In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown both sides of patties. Pour off excess fat.
Meanwhile saute mushrooms and onions in butter in another skillet.  Lay over patties.
In a small bowl, blend flour and remaining soup until smooth. Mix in water, Worcestershire sauce and mustard powder. Pour over meat in skillet. Cover, and cook for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
