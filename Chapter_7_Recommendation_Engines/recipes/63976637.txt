Title:
Chinese Chicken Fried Rice II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 egg
1 tablespoon water
1 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
4  green onion, chopped
4 to 6 mushrooms, choped
2 cups cooked Brown rice, cold
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1- garlic
Ground black pepper to taste
1 to 2 cups cup cooked, chopped chicken meat
Add peas and carrots

Directions:
Cook brown rice and set aside to cool. In a small bowl, beat eggs with water. Melt butter with a little olive Oil in a large skillet over medium low heat. Add eggs and leave flat for 1 to 2 minutes. Remove from skillet and cut into shreds, set aside.
Heat oil in same skillet; add carrots and saute until soft,add mushrooms. Then add rice, soy sauce, pepper, garlic and chicken. Stir fry together for about 5 minutes, then stir in egg and green onions and serve.

Cooked egg is shredded and mixed with a stir fry of chicken, rice and onion in soy sauce in this fundamental Chinese dish.Modified from: Chinese Chicken Fried Rice II
