Title:
Gluten-Free Teriyaki Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4317503.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup gluten-free soy sauce (tamari)
2 tablespoons honey
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 teaspoon minced onion
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Whisk soy sauce, honey, lemon juice, onion, and garlic powder together in a bowl.

Submitter: 
Sarah Pryor
Description: 
Super easy teriyaki sauce makes stir-fry taste amazing!
