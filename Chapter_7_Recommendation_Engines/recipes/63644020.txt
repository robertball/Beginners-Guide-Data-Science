Title:
Sizzlin Sausage Gyros with Tzaziki Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz package al fresco Spinach & Feta Chicken Sausage
8 oz fat free sour cream
1/2 peeled fresh cucumber, remove seeds and dice 1/4 inch
2 Tablespoon Juice, lemon, fresh
6 Each Herb, peppermint, fresh, leaves chopped
1 Each Cooking Spray, olive, extra virgin, 1/3 sec spray
6 Each Whole Wheat Pita Bread, Organic
6 Each Lettuce, red leaf, fresh, outer leaf
1/2 Each Apples, fresh, lrg thinly sliced (peel on)
1/4 Each Onion, red, fresh, med, whole, 2 1/2" thinly sliced
1/2 Cup Cheese, feta, rducd fat, crumbles

Directions:
First, prepare Tzaziki Sauce by combining the sour cream, cucumber lemon juice and mint in medium bowl. Cover tightly with plastic wrap and refrigerate until ready to use.
Spray a large (10-12") non-stick skillet with cooking spray. Cut each sausage into ?" round slices. Brown over medium heat for 5-7 minutes, stirring frequently.
In another large (10-12") non-stick skillet, sprayed with cooking spray, warm each pita bread for 30 seconds to 1 minute on each side or place all on a baking sheet and heat in 425 degree oven for 3-5 minutes. Remove to a plate. Cover with clean kitchen towel to keep warm.
To assemble, cut top 1" off each pita bread. Place 1 lettuce leaf inside each pita bread. Fill each pita with 1 sausage. Add a few apple slices and a slice or two of red onion. Top each Gyros with a few tablespoons Tzaziki Sauce and 2 Tablespoons crumbled Feta Cheese.
Serve immediately with extra sauce. This recipe will make up to 8 servings if you use 4" pita bread.
