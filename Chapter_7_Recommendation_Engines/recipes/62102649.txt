Title:
Portabella Mushroom Beef Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Chuck roast, 4lbs
1 medium onion sliced
4 baby portabella mushrooms sliced
1 can of beef broth
1 Tbs. of Kitchen bouquet browning sauce
1 can of cream of mushroom
Garlic powder
Onion powder
Salt
Pepper
Â¼ Cup oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. In a pot over medium/ high heat add oil, heat until hot. Meanwhile season both sides of the roast with garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper. Sear both sides of the roast until nice and brown. Remove roast set aside and drain oil. Add broth to pot and scrape bottom of pan. Add cream of mushroom soup and browning sauce to broth, mix well. Add roast back to pot, top with onion. Cook for 3 hours or until tender. During the last hour of cooking add the sliced mushrooms. Remove the roast the last half hour of cooking. Let the roast set for 5 minutes, then slice thin. Add roast back to pot and let cook the rest of the time.
Serve over mashed potatoes.
