Title:
Cheddar Chipotle Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all purpose flour
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoon white sugar
1 teaspoon garlic powder ( or 2 cloves, minced)
1/3 cup butter
1 cup shredded sharp cheddar
1 cup milk
1 canned chipotle pepper, minced
1 tsp adobo sauce

Directions:
In a large bowl whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, garlic powder and sugar.
Cut in the butter untill the mixture resembles coarse meal.
Add the sharp cheddar and minced chipotle pepper.  Mix gently.
In a bowl, combine the milk and adobo sauce.  Gradually add to the flour mixture and combine until the dough comes together.
Turn onto a floured surface and knead gently two or three times.  Pat dough out to 1 inch thick.
Cut biscuits with a large cutter or glass and place onto an ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake in a 425 degree oven for 12-15 minutes, or until egdes begin to brown.

Submitter: 
Stacey
Description: 
These tender biscuits have just the right amount of cheese with a little heat.  They pair perfectly with a nice bowl of chili or your favourite chowder.
