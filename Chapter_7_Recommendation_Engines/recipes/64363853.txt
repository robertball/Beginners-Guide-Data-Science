Title:
White Chicken Enchilada Slow-Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts
1 ounce of butter
1 teaspoon salt
1 (26 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
2 tablespoons squeeze minced garlic
.5 (16 ounce) container sour cream
1 (7 ounce) can diced green chile peppers
3 1/2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 tablespoon onion powder
2 tablespoons chili powder
3 tablespoons ground red pepper
.75 canned black beans (drained)
chives for garnish (optional)
black pepper to taste
8 low carb tortillas

Directions:
Place chicken in a pot with butter and salt, cover with water, and bring to a boil over high heat. Continue to boil until the chicken is done, about 10 minutes. Drain, allow chicken to cool, and cut into small pieces.
Place chicken pieces in a large bowl. Stir in soup, garlic, sour cream, beans, spices and green chiles.
Spray the inside of slow cooker lightly with non-stick cooking spray.
Pour the soup and chicken mix in and top with cheese.
Cook on Low setting for 3 to 4 hours. Top with chives.  Serve in low carb tortillas.

Submitter: 
Michelle Metzger
Description: 
An easy, creamy chicken enchilada dish. Slow cooking gently melds together the flavors of chicken meat, cream of chicken soup, sour cream, spices, and green chiles.Modified from: White Chicken Enchilada Slow-Cooker Casserole
