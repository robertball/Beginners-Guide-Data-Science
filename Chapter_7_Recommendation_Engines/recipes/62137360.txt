Title:
Cilantro-Lime Shrimp Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Raw Shrimp (med-large size, remove shell/tail/etc.)
1 med onion, chopped
2 Tbs lime juice
1/2 tsp dried cilantro
1/2 tsp garlic salt
2 Tbs vegetable oil
1 Tbs Taco Sauce (or fresh salsa)
8 Tortilla shells (can be flour, whole wheat, or corn -  your preference)
1 Lime
Optional Toppings:
Lettuce
Fresh Salsa
Tomatoes
Cheddar Cheese
Sour Cream
Guacamole
Black Olives

Directions:
Shrimp: Cut lime into wedges, and set aside.
Put oil in skillet, and heat on med-high.
Add in onion, garlic salt, and cilantro. Stirring occasionally, cook about 2-3 minutes.
Add shrimp and lime juice and continue stirring.  After 5-8 minutes the shrimp should be finished.  Please note, that depending on the size of the shrimp the cooking time may vary.  Make sure that the shrimp are completely pink, and somewhat firm to the touch.
Add taco sauce, stir in and serve!
To Serve: Prepare your optional toppings above on a central serving plate, where everyone can reach!
Take 1 flour tortillla, add 3-4 pieces of shrimp (depending on the size).  Use lime wedge to dash a little more lime on top.  Add toppings, and enjoy!
