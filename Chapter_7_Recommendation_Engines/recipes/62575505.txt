Title:
Lemon Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/546488.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups plus 1 tblsp flour
4 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 ½ tblsp fresh lemon zest
½ cup (1 stick) butter, melted
1 ½ cups sour cream
1 cup blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray or grease a 12-well muffin tin. In a mixing bowl, combine 3 cups flour, baking soda, and salt. Whisk until thoroughly combined.
In another large mixing bowl, whisk together the sugar, eggs, lemon zest, and melted butter.
Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients alternating with the sour cream and mix just to combine. Avoid over mixing. Toss the blueberries with 1 tblsp flour and fold into batter.
Divide the batter evenly into each well of muffin pan. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes or until a tester comes out clean.
