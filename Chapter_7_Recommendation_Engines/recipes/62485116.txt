Title:
White Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/488468.jpg
Ingredients:
2 chicken breast
1 can of chicken broth
1 can of great northern beans
1 can of green chile peppers diced
1 cup of water
1tbs. of chili powder
1tbs. of garlic
*Garnishes:
sharp cheddar cheese
mozzarella cheese
sour cream
tortilia chips
serves 2 big bowls.

Directions:
cook chicken breast in oven till done, then mix all ingredients together (do not drain, add all) & simmer for 10- 15 mins.  The longer you simmer the better & more flavorful it will be.

Submitter: 
Amanda Gargus
Description: 
Is a good chicken soup with a little green chile, chili powder, & garlic flavor to it.  I know the thought of chicken & regular chili crossed my mind to, ewww!  It's no where like it, I promise. It's very good!
