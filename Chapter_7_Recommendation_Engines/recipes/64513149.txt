Title:
Lemon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2884185.jpg
Ingredients:
Base:
1 1/2 cups flour
2/3 cups icing sugar
3/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
Topping:
3 eggs
1 1/2 cups sugar
3 tbsp. flour
1/4 cup lemon juice
Additional icing sugar.

Directions:
Base:
Blend flour, icing sugar and butter until crumbly.
Pat into a greased 9" x 13" pan.
Bake @ 350 x 20 minutes.
Topping:
Meanwhile, whisk together eggs, sugar, flour and lemon juice until frothy; pour over hot crust.
Bake @ 350 x 20 - 25 minutes, or until edges are light golden brown.
Cool on rack.
Dust with icing sugar (use a strainer with very small holes & gently shake over top).

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
This very attractive, tasty bar is made with a shortbread crust and dusted with icing sugar. Freezes well.
