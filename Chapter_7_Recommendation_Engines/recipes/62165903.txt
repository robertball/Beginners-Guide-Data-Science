Title:
Bob J's Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, or mixed beans, drained & rinsed
1 large ripe tomato, diced
1 yellow bell pepper, diced
1/2 of an onion, chopped
1/2 teaspoon freshly grated lime zest
Juice of one fresh lime
1 clove garlic, minced
1 avocado, diced
1/4 teaspoon red hot pepper flakes (optional)
1 teaspoon sea salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Gently mix the beans, tomatoes, peppers, onion, garlic, and avocado together in a large bowl.
In a smaller bowl, mix the olive oil with lime zest, lime juice, red pepper flakes, salt and pepper.
Pour dressing over bean mixture and toss lightly.
Serve cold and enjoy!

Submitter: 
AppleChef
Description: 
This is a bean salad recipe that my dad made one summer night. It's a wonderful and fiber rich side dish for a summer meal. If you love each of these ingredients individually, you'll love them even more all together in this dish!
