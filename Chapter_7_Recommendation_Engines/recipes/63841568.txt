Title:
Cinnamon Chicken with Maggi So Juicy seasoning. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1067284.jpg
Ingredients:
Maggi So Juicy Mediterranean Herb Medley oven bag; 1 lbs boneless skinless chicken tenders; 1/2 cup maple syrup or Maple flavored Agave syrup, 1/4 TBSP Cinnamon.

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350F. 2. Prepare chicken as directed in the Maggi So Juicy oven bag. 3. Add syrup and cinnamon to bag before baking. 4. Bake for approximately 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Libbi Ayers
Description: 
I love the combination of cinnamon and garlic herbs. Even the kids love this recipe.
