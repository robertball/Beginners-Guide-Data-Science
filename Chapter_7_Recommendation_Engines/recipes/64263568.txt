Title:
Refrigerator Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg dry yeast
1/2 c warm water
5 c flour
3 TBS sugar
1 tsp soda
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
3/4 c shortening
2 c buttermilk

Directions:
Dissolve yeast in warm water.  Sift together all dry ingredients.  Cut shortening in to dry ingredients.  Stir in wet ingredients.   Mix together and place in air tight container and refrigerate.  Make biscuits 30 minutes or so before baking.  Bake at 400-450 degrees for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown

Submitter: 
CorinneMike Huseby
Description: 
Even better when dough has spent time in the refrigerator.  Keeps well.
