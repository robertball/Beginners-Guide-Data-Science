Title:
Pumpkin Risotto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound pumpkin
2 tablespoons butter
1 and 3/4 cups Arborio Rice
2 chicken cubes(or 6 cups of chicken broth)
4 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
salt.

Directions:
Put 6 cups of water in a pot and add the chicken cubes(if using), bring the water and cubes to a boil. Meanwhile cut open the pumpkin and remove the seeds. Peel and cube the pumpkin. But the pumpkin and half the butter into a  medium size saucepan, and on medium- high heat fry for a minute. Add a ladle of the boiling water, turn the heat to medium and cook until the pumpkin is half cooked. Add the rice to the pumpkin and stir and fry until rice is glossy, about 1 minute. Add another ladle of water, stirring all the time, until the water is absorbed. Keep adding water a ladle at a time and stir until rice is cooked, about 20 minutes. Turn off the heat add the remaining butter and stir in the Parmesan cheese. Season with salt to taste.*see notes*
