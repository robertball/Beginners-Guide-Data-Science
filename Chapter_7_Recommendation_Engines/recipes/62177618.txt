Title:
Hamburger Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans crescent rolls
1 lb ground beef
1 cup spaghetti sauce
1 clove garlic
1/2 tsp basil
1/4 tsp garlic salt
1 egg beaten
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
Optional ingredients: mushroom, pepperoni, sausage, green pepper, onion, etc.

Directions:
In pie pan press one can of crescent rolls to fill like a pie crust.  Brown ground beef with garlic and strain grease. Add spaghetti sauce and basil and other optional ingredients. Pour into pie pan. Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese reserving small amount for top. Top with another can of crescent rolls to form top crust. Brush top crust with beaten egg, sprinkle with garlic salt and spread remaining cheese on top. Bake @ 350 until crust is golden brown about 15-20 min.

Submitter: 
SACHABEAR
Description: 
A quick and easy hamburger pie recipe that is easily converted from Italian to Mexican with some quick changes. My picky kids eat this up!  Not gourmet, but definitely filling.
