Title:
Frosted Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1024283.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (3 ounce) packages strawberry/banana
2 cups boiling water
1 cup pineapple juice
1/2 cup cold water
2 bananas, diced
1 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
4 cups miniature marshmallows
TOPPING:
1/2 cup sugar
2 tablespoons flour
1 cup pineapple juice
1 egg, lightly beaten
WHIPPED:
2 tablespoons butter
1 cup heavy whipping cream

Directions:
In a bowl, dissolve gelatin in boiling water. Stir in the juice and water.
Transfer to a 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. dish.
Refrigerate for 30 minutes or until partially set.
Fold in pineapple, bananas, and marshmallows; refrigerate.
For topping, combine sugar and flour in a saucepan.
Whisk together egg and pineapple juice.  Gradually whisk in with the sugar and flour.
Bring to a boil over medium heat; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.
Removed from the heat.  Cool to room temperature.
In a mixing bowl, beat cream and butter on high speed until peaks form.
Gently fold into custard and spread over set gelatin.
Refrigerate 1 hour or until chilled.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
