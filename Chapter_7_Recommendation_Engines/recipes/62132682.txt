Title:
French Dip Sandwiches Au Jus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pounds boneless beef roast (Rump or chuck)
1 TBSP. garlic salt
1/2 tsp. seasoned salt
1/2 - 1 tsp. oregano
1/2 - 1 tsp. rosemary
2 beef boullion cubes or 2 tsp. beef base
1 large onion, diced
2 cups water or beef broth

Directions:
Spray crock pot with cooking spray.  Place roast in crock and add all other ingredients.  Cook on Low setting for 8-10 hours.  Remove roast and shred.  Serve shredded roast on toasted crusty  sub rolls with sliced pepperjack cheese and the juices from the crock pot for dipping.

Submitter: 
crazycatlady -  "CCL"
Description: 
This has been a family favorite for years.  The added bonus is the ease of tossing it all in the crock pot at once and letting it slow cook all day.
