Title:
Gluten Free/Rice Free Pastry Flour Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sweet White Sourghum Flour___1 1/3 Cups
Coconut Flour________________1 1/3 Cups
Potato Starch________________1 1/3 Cups
Xanthan Gum__________________1 Teaspoon

Directions:
Gather all ingredients and equipment.
Thoroughly mix all four ingredients together in a large bowl, then sift.
Transfer the contents to a storage container and label appropriately; use as needed.

Gluten and rice free flour to be utilized in gluten free baking
