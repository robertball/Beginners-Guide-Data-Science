Title:
Copycat Applebee's Hot Artichoke and Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5679086.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) box frozen chopped spinach, thawed
2 (14 ounce) cans artichoke hearts, drained and roughly chopped
1 cup shredded Parmesan
1 cup shredded Romano
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1 jar (16 ounces) roasted garlic Alfredo sauce
8 ounces softened cream cheese
pepper (to taste)

Directions:
mix all ingredients in a large bowl
pour into crock pot and set on high ready to serve when hot. about 30 min.
