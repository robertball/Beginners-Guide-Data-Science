Title:
Danny's Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/611671.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can pears, chopped
2 bananas, sliced
2 apples - peeled, cored and chopped
1/2 cup creamy salad dressing
1/2 cup frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
Whisk together the creamy salad dressing and whipped topping until smooth. Pour dressing over the pears, bananas,  apples and toss. This salad may also be garnished with your favorite chopped nuts.

Submitter: 
Billie
Description: 
A light fruit salad that is the only salad my son will eat.
