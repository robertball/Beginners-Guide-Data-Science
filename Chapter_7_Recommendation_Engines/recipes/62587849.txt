Title:
Chicken and Bow Ties with Creamy Sun Dried Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/555618.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz bow tie pasta
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbsp. olive oil
1 lb. skinless, boneless chicken breast cut into bite-sized pieces
1 tsp. dried basil, crushed
1/8 tsp. crushed red pepper
1/8 tsp. cayenne pepper
3/4 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup  julienne cut, oil-packed sun dried tomatoes, well drained and pressed with paper towels to remove as much oil as possible
1/4 cup dry white wine or white cooking wine
1/2 cup whipping cream
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
additional parmesan cheese for serving

Directions:
Start water to boil pasta. Start cooking pasta after combining broth and wine to the sauce.  Cook pasta for about 8-10 minutes until al dente.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet cook the garlic in hot oil over medium high heat for 30 seconds.  Add chicken, basil, crushed red pepper and cayenne pepper.  Cook and stir for about 7 minutes until cooked through.  add chicken broth, sun-dried tomatoes and white wine.  Bring to boiling; reduce heat.  Simmer, uncovered about 7 minutes.   Stir in whipping cream and the 1/4 cup parmesan cheese to the chicken, broth and wine; simmer and stir until the sauce begins to thicken, about 10-12 minutes.  Drain pasta well, use a paper towel to remove excess water if necessary.  Toss pasta in sauce until evenly coated and heated through.  Serve with additional parmesan cheese.
