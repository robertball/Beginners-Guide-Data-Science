Title:
Easy Cream Cheese Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup softened butter or margarine
1 8oz package softened cream cheese
1 egg
1/4 tsp vanilla
1 18.5oz package of yellow or devil's food cake mix

Directions:
Cream butter and cheese. Blend in egg and vanilla.
Add dry cake mix 1/3 at a time, mixing well after each addition. If mixer is used, add last third of cake mix by hand.
Cover; chill 30 minutes.
Heat oven to 375F(190C)
Drop small teaspoonfuls of dough onto ungreased baking sheet.
Bake 8-10 minutes or until light brown. Cool slightly before removing from baking sheet.

Submitter: 
CBerrier86
Description: 
These delicious quick and easy cookies are kid friendly and great for parties or holiday sweets for friends and family.
