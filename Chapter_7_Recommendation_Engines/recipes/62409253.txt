Title:
Greek Omelette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs lightly beaten
1 large hand full spinach
1/8 cup feta cheese
5-8 pitted kalamata olives
1/4 large tomato,chopped
salt
pepper

Directions:
1.Put the eggs in a bowl and beat with a fork until yokes are broken.
2.Pour eggs into a saute pan cook on medium heat for 1 minute.
3.Sprinkle with cheese,olives,spinach and tomato.Cook another 2-4 minutes.
4.Place pan in broiler for 2 minutes.
5.Remove from broiler and with a spatula fold in half.
6.Enjoy!
