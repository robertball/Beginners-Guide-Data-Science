Title:
Quick and Easy Cream Cheese Frosting II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/268774.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup butter, softened
2 cups sifted confectioners' sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
In a food processor, cream together the cream cheese, butter and vanilla until creamy (4 min). Add the confectioners' sugar and mix until smooth (2 min). Store in the refrigerator after use.
