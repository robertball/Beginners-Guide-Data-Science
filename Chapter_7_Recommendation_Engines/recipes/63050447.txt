Title:
Flank Steak Carpaccio-Style with Spinach and Shaved Parmigiano Reggiano Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 to 2 pounds flank steak
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
1 bag (10 ounces) spinach, stems removed, sliced into strips
4-6 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 1/2 ounces parmigiano reggiano cheese
crusty italian bread

Directions:
marinate steak with vinegar, and refrigerate for at least one hour, turning occasionally.
Remove steak from marinade and reserve.
Microwave reserved marinade until boiling (2 minutes). Allow boiled marinade to cool
Grill steak for about 8 minutes per side. Allow to rest on cutting board for 5 minutes. Reserve any juice from meat.
Place spinach on platter.
Cut steak into thin diagonal slices. Arrange over spinach. Drizzle with reserved meat juices. Season with salt, pepper, and olive oil to taste.
Shave cheese, and scatter over top.
Serve with marinade.
