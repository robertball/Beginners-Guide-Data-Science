Title:
Turkey-Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked turkey or chicken, cubed
2 cans cheddar cheese soup
1 cup mayo
1 tsp. curry powder
1 tsp. sugar
1 14oz. bag frozen broccoli, thawed
1 1/4 cups soft bread crumbs mixed with 2 tbsp melted butter

Directions:
Place thawed broccoli in a casserole dish, cover with cubed turkey.  Mix the cans of soup, mayo, sugar, and curry powder, pour over broccoli and turkey. Top with bread crumbs.  Bake in a 350 degree oven for about 40 minutes until crumbs are brown and sauce is bubbly.
