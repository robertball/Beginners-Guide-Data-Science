Title:
Enchilada sauce/Ladybuggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1104970.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups of chicken broth
5 tsp chili powder
2 tsp garlic powder.
1 tsp cumin.
1/3 tsp sugar.
pinch of cinnamon.
3 Tbsp flour.
5 Tbsp water.

Directions:
Mix spices+broth in pan.
boil for 3 mins.
mix flour and water.
add slowly until thick.
