Title:
Thai Mangoes and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup coconut cream
1 Tbsp sugar
1/4 tsp salt
Cooked sticky rice
Mangoes, sliced (ripe)

Directions:
In a small saucepan, combine coconut cream, sugar and salt.  Bring to a boil.  Remove from heat and cool.
Dish up sticky rice and mangoes on a plate.  Pour sauce over top and serve.
