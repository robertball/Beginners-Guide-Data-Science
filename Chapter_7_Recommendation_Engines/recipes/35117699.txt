Title:
Crunchy Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pkg frozen peas
1 C. Carrots (shredded)
1 Can sliced Water Chestnuts
2-4 Green Onions sliced up
Marinade:
1 T. Oil
1 T. Red Wine Vinegar
1 T. Soy Sauce
1 Tsp. Sugar
1 Tsp. Paprika
1 Tsp. Dry Mustard
1 Clove Garlic Smashed

Directions:
Rinse peas under hot water to thaw, drain and add carrots, water chestnuts and green onions. Make marinade and add to veggies.
Marinade at least an hour and add
4 T. Mayo just before serving.
(I like it without mayo also)
