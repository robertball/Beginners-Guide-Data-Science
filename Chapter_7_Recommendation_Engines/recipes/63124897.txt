Title:
MM Sandwich - Steak and Mushroom Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/797416.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup sliced mushrooms
2/3 stick of butter
1 pound cube steak cut into 1/2 inch strips
1 tsp kosher salt
1 tsp black pepper
1/4 cup worcestershire sauce
5 dashed tabasco sauce
2 deli rolls, split
4 slices provolone cheese

Directions:
Season meat with salt and pepper, set aside.
In a saute pan, put 1 tsp olive oil.  Add the mushrooms and saute until tender, remove from pan and set aside.  Heat heavy skillet over high heat.  Melt 1/3 stick of butter.  When butter is melted add meat to the pan in 1 layer.  Do not stir.  Allow meat to sit, undisturbed, for a minute in order to brown.  With a spatula, flip meat to the other side and allow to brown.  Reduce heat to low.  Add mushrooms to skillet, then add worcestershire, tabasco and remaining butter.  Stir and allow to simmer adding more worcestershire or tabasco as desired.
Heat rolls and spoon meat onto rolls.  Drizzle with sauce and put 2 slices of cheese on top and serve.
