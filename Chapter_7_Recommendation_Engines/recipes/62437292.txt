Title:
Peanut butter chocolate frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 cup semi-sweetened chocolate chips
1 1/2 cups confectioners sugar
3 tbs milk
2 tbsp shortening

Directions:
1.In medium sized mixing bowl, mix peanut butter and butter until even consistancy.
2.In separate bowl place choc-chips and milk, melt in microwave for about a minute until completly melted. Mix together until combined. Add chocolate mixture to peanut butter mixture. Mix until combined.
3.Gradually add sugar to the mixture and mix thoroughly. Finally add shortening until smooth. Apply on whatever desired.

Submitter: 
Diana Schook
Description: 
I'm Joe, Diana's 17 year old son, I was making homemade peanut butter cups, but felt creative and gradually threw things together and got a frosting that has a great chocolate and peanut butter taste, hope you like it.
