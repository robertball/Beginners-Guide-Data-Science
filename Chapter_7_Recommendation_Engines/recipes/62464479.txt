Title:
Easy Cheesy Chicken I - Jennie's Version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
salt, pepper, garlic powder
1 packet Italian dry seasoning
1 packet Lipton onion soup seasoning
1 can condensed cream of chicken soup
1 can condensed cheddar cheese soup
1 can condenses cheddar broccoli soup
1/2 c white wine
1 pkg frozen broccoli florets
angel hair pasta, egg noodles or rice
8-16 oz sour cream

Directions:
Sprinkle and rub chicken with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and the packet of Italian seasoning.  Place in slow cooker.
Combine all soups, Lipton onion seasoning, and white wine.  Pour over chicken.
Cook on low for 4 hours; stir in broccoli florets 30 min before it's done (or for less crisp to broccoli, 45 min), and stir in sour cream right before serving.

Submitter: 
Jennie Hood Flynn
Description: 
Another slow cooker hit combines three varieties of canned soups with sour cream for a creamy sauce that could not be easier to make.Modified from: Easy Cheesy Chicken I
