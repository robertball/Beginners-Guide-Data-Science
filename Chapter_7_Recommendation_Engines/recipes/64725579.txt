Title:
Instant Pot Beef and Pasta in Creamy Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1/2 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
1 teaspoon kosher salt
16 ounces small pasta, such as cavatelli
15 ounce can tomato sauce
8 ounce can tomato sauce
2 cups beef or chicken broth
6 ounces sharp cheddar cheese, grated
1/2 cup heavy cream
2 cups baby spinach

Directions:
Set Instant Pot to Saute, and brown the beef with the kosher salt and Italian seasoning.  Drain.
Add onion, and saute until softened.
Add garlic and saute, stirring, for another minute.
Stir in both cans of tomato sauce, chicken or beef broth, and pasta to the pot.
Close the lid and set pressure timer to 6 minutes.
Let sit with lid closed for five minutes, then quick release.
Stir in grated cheese until melted.
Stir in baby spinach until wilted.
Stir in heavy cream.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Lissa
Description: 
Ground beef and pasta in a creamy sauce, with spinach.  This would also be good with Italian sausage instead of beef, and you can substitute other leafy greens for the spinach.
