Title:
Aunt Joyce's Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 Tbsp. cocoa powder
3. Tbsp. canola oil
1 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons vinegar
2 tablespoons vanilla extract
3 cups all-purpose flour
2 cups white sugar
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 cups warm water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch pan.               Into a small saucepan warm the two cups of water,cocoa and oil. In a small bowl combine the mayonnaise, vinegar and vanilla. Combine the flour, sugar, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.  Slowly stir in the warm water mixer and mayonnaise mixture. Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.
