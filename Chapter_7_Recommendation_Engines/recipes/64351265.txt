Title:
Corned Beef Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Rye bread
butter
mayonnaise
shaved corned beef
sauerkraut
Crispy bacon
grated smoked cheddar
Yellow mustard
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to broil. Toast bread. Butter the bread. Generously spread mayo on one side. Top with many layers of corned beef. Layer with sauerkraut. Bacon next. Top with cheddar. Place on a pan and broil until cheese has melted. Spread mustard on other piece of bread and top sandwich. Cut in half and enjoy.
