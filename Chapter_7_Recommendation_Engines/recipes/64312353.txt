Title:
Garlic Swiss Chard Scramble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 spray(s) cooking spray
2 cup(s) Swiss chard, fresh leaves and stems chopped
4 clove(s) garlic
2/3 cup(s) beans, red kidney
2 medium egg(s)
salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:
Spray skillet with cooking spray. Add swiss chard leaves, torn into pieces, garlic and beans.
Saute until leaves are wilted and beans warmed.
In a small bowl, whip egg.
Add egg to pan; saute for 5 minutes. Add salt and pepper to taste.
