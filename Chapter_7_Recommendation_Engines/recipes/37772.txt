Title:
Creamy Pasta Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977615.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (26 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
1 (16 ounce) package rotini pasta, cooked
1 cup BREAKSTONE'S or KNUDSEN Sour Cream
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed, drained
1/2 cup KRAFT 100% Grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Mix all ingredients until well blended.
Spoon into 13x9-inch baking dish.
Bake 25 minutes or until heated through.
