Title:
Crock Pot Raisin Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 8 slices bread, cubed
2. 4 eggs
3. 2 cups milk
4. 1/4 cup sugar
5. 1/4 cup margarine melted
6. 1/4 cup raisins
7. 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
Sauce
8. 2 Tablespoons margarine
9. 2 Tablespoons flour
10. 1 cup water
11. 3/4 cup sugar
12. 1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Placed 1. in Greased crock pot
In a bowl beat 2. 3. and 4. Stir in 5. 6. 7. when mixed pour over bread cover and cook on high 1 hour then reduce heat to low and cook 3-4 more hours
Just before serving make the Sauce by melting 8. in small saucepan...add 9 and stir till smooth ...gradually add 10. 11. 12.  bring to a boil and stir for two min or until thickened...serve warm sauce over the bread pudding
