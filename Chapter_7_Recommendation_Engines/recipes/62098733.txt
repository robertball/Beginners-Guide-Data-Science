Title:
Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large cans beef broth
beef bouillon cubes
beef cubes
small can peeled tomato's
3 celery stalks
3 large carrots
1 onion
1 turnip
salt to taste
1 box of orzo(tiny pasta)

Directions:
Use one large stock pot
Wash all vegetables add to the pot.
Add beef cubes to the pot
Pour 3 cans of beef broth into the pot.
Make sure all vegetables and beef cubes are covered with broth.
Add 3 beef boullon cubes
Add a pinch of salt
Bring the soup to a boil.
Remove the foam from the top
Let simmer on low for an hour.
Get 3 peeled tomato's and squeeze into the pot with your hand.
Pour the juice from tomato's into pot.
Continue to let soup simmer.
Stir soup often.
Remove all vegetables from soup.
Throw out.
In another pot boil water
for orzo.
make orzo to box directions
Drain orzo very well.
Add orzo to soup pot.
Mix well and serve
Sprinkle Locatelli cheese on top.
Serve with Italian bread and crackers.
This recipe is terrific!!!
My children love it.
