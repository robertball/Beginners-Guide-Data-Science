Title:
Ham & Lentil Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups diced ham
2 cups dried lentils
2 cups each diced carrots and celery
1 cup chopped onion
1 tbs minced garlic
4 cups water
21 oz chicken broth
1 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp each salt and pepper
1 bag (16oz) frozen spinach, not thawed
2 tbs lemon juice

Directions:
Put all ingredients except spinach in slow cooker. Stir to combine.
Cover and cook on high 4-5 hours or low 7-9 hours, or until lentils are tender.
Stir in spinach, cover and cook 10 min or until tender. Stir lemon juice and serve.
