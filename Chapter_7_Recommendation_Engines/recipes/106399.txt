Title:
Icy Lemon  Sorbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983523.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups boiling water
1/2 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1 tablespoon grated lemon rind
2/3 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
8 lemons (for optional decorative serving)
Optional Garnish: fresh mint sprigs

Directions:
Combine boiling water and SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener in a large bowl, stirring until SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener dissolves; cool slightly. Stir in lemon rind and juice. Pour mixture into a 9-inch square pan; cover and freeze.
Cut tops from lemons; remove pulp. Set aside.
Remove sorbet from freezer; let stand about 10 minutes. Scrape sorbet surface with a spoon; scoop or spoon sorbet into lemon shells or into small serving dishes. Garnish with fresh mint, if desired.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Fresh lemon juice and grated lemon rind give kick to this palate cleanser.
