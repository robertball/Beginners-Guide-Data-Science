Title:
kadinbudu kofte(rice meatball) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground meat
4 tablespoons white rice,uncooked
1 onion,finely chopped
4 eggs
salt and pepper to taste
3 tablespoons flour
1 cup oil for frying

Directions:
cook the half of the meat in a medium soucepan over medium-high heat.when it's ready,remove from the heat and add the remaining half into the cooked one.
cook the ricein a separate soucepan untill tender.Drain.
mix meat,rice,2eggs,onion,salt and pepper.mix well using your hands and form a dough.Get small pieces from dough to shape out.the shape should be long as a finger and fat a little.
heat the oil in a large skillet.first dip the meatballs in flour,then beaten eggs.Fry both sides untill golden brown

Submitter: 
Asli   Ocak
Description: 
This is a Turkish style meatball with rice in it.Very easy to make and so yummy..
