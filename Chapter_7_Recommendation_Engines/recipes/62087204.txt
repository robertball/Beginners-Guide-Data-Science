Title:
Vietnamese Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 tsps Ground Coffee with Chicory
2 Tablespoons Sweetened Condensed Milk
Boiling Water

Directions:
Put the Sweetend Condensed Milk in a coffee cup.
Place the coffee filter on the cup.
Unscrew the screen to the coffee filter.
Put in the coffe and screw the screen back on tightly (moderately tight).
Fill the filter 1/4 of the way with boiling water.
Wait thirty seconds (the water should not drip through in less time)
Unsrew the filter two times.
Fill the filter with boiling water.
Place the lid on the filter and wait (about 5 minutes) until the water has filtered through.
Remove the filter from the cup.
Stir and enjoy straight or pour over ice.

Submitter: 
LONNAJOE
Description: 
This is a very strong coffee that is delicious served straight or over ice!  The single cup coffee filter can be found at any Asian Market and is very inexpensive.
