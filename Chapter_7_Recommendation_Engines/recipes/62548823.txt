Title:
Cheesy Cauliflower au Gratin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of cauliflower, broken apart into large pcs.
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
90g (1/2C) lite cheezewhiz
70g (3/4C) shredded lite chedder
10g bread crumbs
28g craisens
30g slivered almonds
1/2tsp cayenne pepper

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 375F.
PLACE cauliflower in 9" baking dish
MICROWAVE on high for 3-4 minutes or until tendercrisp.  Sprinkle with nutmeg.  Top with cheezewhiz and chedder.
MIX TOGETHER bread crumbs, craisens, almonds & cayenne
SPRINKLE over cauliflower
BAKE 12-14 mins or until topping is golden
