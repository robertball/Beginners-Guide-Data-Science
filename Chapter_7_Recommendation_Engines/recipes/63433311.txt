Title:
Vegetable Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil
2 carrots, diced
2 celery stalks, diced
1 onion, diced
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 tsp dried thyme
Salt and pepper to taste
2 15 oz cans cannelini beans, rinsed and drained
1 28 oz can diced tomatoes
4 c vegetable broth
Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Directions:
In stock pot, heat oil over medium-high heat.
Add carrots, celery, and onion and cook until onion is translucent, about 6 minutes.
Add garlic and seasonings; cook for another 3 minutes.
Add beans, tomatoes, and broth and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer for 25-30 minutes, until vegetables are tender.
Serve with grated Parmesan cheese.
