Title:
Pineapple Ham Steaks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tbsp. butter
1/3 cup pineapple chunks in syrup or juice
1 ham steak

Directions:
Slice ham steak into strips or chunks, whichever you prefer.  Melt butter in a skillet.  Add ham to skillet, then pineapple and juice or syrup.   Cook on medium heat until both the pineapple and ham are slightly browned.  Serve with potatoes or rice!

Submitter:

Description: 
Ham and Pineapple cooked in their own juices make a delciious meal.
