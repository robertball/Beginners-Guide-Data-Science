Title:
Spicy black and red bean soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. chopped onions
1 1/2 c. chopped green peppers (or carrots or combo)
2 garlic cloves, minced
3 c. veggie or chicken broth
1 pkg. (16 oz) frozen sweet corn
2 cups (or 1 can) cooked red or kidney beans, drained
2 cups (or 1 can) cooked black beans, drained
2 cans (16 oz) tomatoes
green chiles to taste (or one can)
2 chopped jalapenos
1 tsp. cumin
cilantro (optional)

Directions:
Saute onion, peppers, and garlic in oil for 5 minutes.  Stir in remaining ingredients except cilantro; bring to a boil, then simmer for awhile till flavors blend (an hour should be enough.)
Chop up some fresh cilantro for the table as an optional topping.

Submitter: 
Susie
Description: 
Great soup to spice  and heat you up in the winter!
