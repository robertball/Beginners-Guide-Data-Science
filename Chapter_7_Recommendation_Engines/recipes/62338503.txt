Title:
Savory Asiago Shortbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cups all purpose flour
1 tbsp water
1/3 cup grated asiago cheese
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 teaspoon coarse kosher salt
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp paprika
1/3 cup butter, cut into 1/2-inch cubes

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
In a food processor, combine all ingredients. Pulse until a solid lump of dough forms.
On a floured surface, roll dough out to 1/2? thickness or less. Cut into squares (or any shape you want to use)!
Place shortbread squares on a greased baking sheet. Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until edges are golden brown. Cook on a wire rack, and serve!

Submitter: 
Val-Flowers
Description: 
A creamy shortbread rich and savory. Goes great with wines.
