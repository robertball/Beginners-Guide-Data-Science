Title:
Sailsbury Steak Deluxe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Can Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup (undiluted)
1 Tbsp. Prepared Mustard
2 tsp. Worcestershire Sauce
1 tsp. Prepared Horseradish
1 Egg
1/4 c. Dried Bread Crumbs
1/4 c. Finely Chopped Onion
1/2 tsp. Salt
Dash of Pepper
1 1/2 lb. Ground Beef
1 to 2 Tbsp. Cooking Oil
1/2. c. Water
2 Tbsp. Chopped Fresh Parsley

Directions:
In a bowl, combine soup, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and horseradish; blend well. In another bowl, lightly beat egg. Add bread crumbs, onion, onion, salt, pepper, and 1/4 cup of the soup mixture. Add beef and mix well. Shape into six patties. In a large skillet ,brown the patties in oil; Drain. Combine remaining soup mixture with water; pour over patties. Cover and cook over low heat for 10-15 minutes until meat is done. Remove patties to serving platter; serve sauce over meat. Sprinkle with Parsley. Serves 6.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a Family FAVORITE at our house. It's a recipe from St. Clement Catholic Church in Boonville, IN. We serve it with Mashed potatoes every time.
