Title:
Buffalo Style Chicken Sausage Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz package al fresco All Natural Buffalo Style Chicken Sausage, 1/2" sliced pieces
1 tsp olive oil
1 cup fat free low sodium ranch or blue cheese dressing
1 lb. penne pasta
1 cup celery, diced
1/2 cup red pepper, diced
1-2 tsp hot sauce (as desired)
1/2 cup red onions, chopped

Directions:
Spray a large skillet with your favorite cooking oil.
Add onions, celery, red peppers and saute until al dente, about 5 minutes.
Add sausages sliced on a diagonal about 1/2" to 1" thick to skillet
Stir fry about 5 more minutes or until sausage is slightly browned
Boil and drain pasta according to package directions.
Place in a large bowl.
Add skillet contents to the bowl along with hot sauce and dressing and toss gently.
