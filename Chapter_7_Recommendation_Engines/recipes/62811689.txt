Title:
Pork Rolls with Creamy Mustard Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/661308.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound very thin sliced pork loin (for Shabu-Shabu), about 16 slices
2 large size russet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/4 inch thick fries
1 tablespoons olive oil or grape seed oil
half cup beef broth
1/4 cup white wine
1/4 cup heavy cream
2 tablespoons stone deli mustard
salt and black pepper
sea salt and fresh black pepper to taste
1 oz radish sprouts, wash well, cut root part and cut into halves

Directions:
Make the pork rolls with 4-6 potatoes
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat, brown rools on all sides in hot oil.
In the skillet, add broth and white wine and boil.
Chenge to medium-low heat and put the lid, cook about 5 min until pooetos get tender.
Take lid away and cook 2 min, add heavy cream and mustard
Stir gently and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Sprinkle with radish sprouts

Saori
Description:
