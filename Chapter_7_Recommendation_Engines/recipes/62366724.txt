Title:
Mini Mango Cakes with Lime Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/408269.jpg
Ingredients:
14 oz (425 g) can of mango slices
3 oz (90 g) unsalted butter
3/4 Cup (185 g) sugar (caster)
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 cup (60 g) flour
2 Tbsp ground almonds
2 Tbsp coconut milk
2 Tbsp lime juice

Directions:
Pre-heat oven 400 F or 200 C.  Lightly grease a 4 hole, 1 Cup muffin pan and line with mango slices.  Beat the butter and 1/2 cup (125 g) of the sugar in a bowl with electric mixer until light and creamy.  Gradually add eggs, mixing well between additions.  Fold in the sifted flour, then the alomnds and finally the coconut milk.  Spoon mixture in even portions over the mango slices.  Bake for 25 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
To make the syrup, place the lime juice, the remaining sugar (1/2 cup) and 1/2 cup of water in a small saucepan over low heat until the sugar dissolves. Increase the heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
Pierce holes in the cakes with a toothpick.  Drizzle the syrupover the top and allow to stand for 5 minutes to soak up the liquid.  Turn out and serve.
