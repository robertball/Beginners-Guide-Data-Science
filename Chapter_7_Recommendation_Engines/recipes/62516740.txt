Title:
Classic Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. Jiffy corn muffin mix (or other favorite brand)
1 can (15.25 oz.) whole kernal corn (undrained)
1 can (14-15 oz) creamed corn
1/3 cup sugar
1 6oz contaner sour cream
2 eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Mix dry ingredients together, add corn, eggs & sourcream, mix until well blended. Pour mixture into greased 8x8 or 9x9 pan, bake at 350 until set & lightly browned or toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. About 20-30 minutes

Submitter:

Description: 
Corn casserole the way I know it & hope you enjoy it too.
