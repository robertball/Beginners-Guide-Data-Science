Title:
Banana Oatmeal Muffins 1 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups gluten free all-purpose flour
1 cup rolled oats
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup agave
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup eggwhite
3/4 cup milk
1/3 cup unsweetend applesauce
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup mashed bananas
1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Combine flour, oats, sugar, baking powder, soda, and salt.
In a large bowl, beat the egg lightly.  Stir in the milk, applesauce, and vanilla.  Add the mashed banana, and combine thoroughly.  Stir the flour mixture into the banana mixture until just combined. Stir in chocolate chips.  Line a 12-cup muffin tin with paper bake cups, and divide the batter among them.
Bake at 400 degrees F (205 degrees C) for 18 to 20 minutes.
