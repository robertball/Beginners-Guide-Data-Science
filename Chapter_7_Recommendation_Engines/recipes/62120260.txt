Title:
Omen Caps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 medium sized mushroom caps (american button mushrooms are best)
1Tbsp olive oil
1Tbsp sweet butter
1/2 cup finely chopped yellow onion
2-3 Tbsp coarsely chopped walnuts
1 garlic clove (pressed)
5 oz frozen spinach (thawed and squeezed dry)
3 oz gruyere cheese (crumbled)
salt to taste

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 400 degrees farenheight.  Remove stems from the mushrooms carefully, and gently remove all sediment from the caps with a wet cloth.  Heat olive oil and butter in a skillet over mediun heat, add the onion and cover it.  Cook them until they are tender and light in color (about 20 min).  Add walnuts and garlic to the onions and cook it another minute.  add the spinach, cook about five minutes, stirring constantly.  Remove the skillet from the heat, let it cool slightly and add the cheese and salt.  Arrange the mushroom caps cavity side up in a baking dish and divide the filling among the caps evenly.  Bake them for 10 minutes, until the filling is browned and mushrooms are heated through.  Serve them hot or warm.
