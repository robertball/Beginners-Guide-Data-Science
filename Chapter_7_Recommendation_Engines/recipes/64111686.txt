Title:
Best Bread Machine Bread Yet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups bread flour (or half bread, half white whole wheat flours)
2 tablespoons white sugar (or honey)
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup vegetable oil, or softened butter
1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
2 1/4 tsp. instant yeast (place in lid dispenser)

Directions:
Place all ingredients in pan of bread machine in order designated- water last.
Put yeast in the dispenser on lid, press Start, and select Basic or White Bread setting.
When timer sounds, remove bread,cool on wire rack, and enjoy.
