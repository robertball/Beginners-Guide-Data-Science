Title:
LittlePeeps Truly Baked Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices bacon rashers, crispy fried
1 cup sour cream,or more as needed
3 scallions,thin sliced
2 pounds small potatos leave half skins on potatoes
1 cup sharp grated cheese
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place the potatoes in a pot with enough water to cover, and bring to a boil. Cook for about 20 minutes, or until tender. Drain and cool.
Meanwhile,  and place in a bowl of cold water to cool. Set aside.
Cook bacon slices in skillet for about 10 minutes, until crisp,
Peel and chop the potatoes, and stir into the bowl until evenly coated with the sour cream ,add scallions ,cheddar cheese and salt and pepper to taste,more sour cream if to dry.

Submitter: 
littlepeepsbrude
Description: 
This is a creamy potato salad made with bacon and scallions and taste just like a baked potato with cheese on top.Modified from: Potato and Bacon Salad
