Title:
Sloppy Joe's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/635178.jpg
Ingredients:
4 lbs. ground beef
2 cans chicken gumbo soup
1 cup ketchup
4 TBS brown sugar
1 TBS salt
1/2 tsp. dry mustard
2 med. onions, chopped
2 cans tomato soup
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 TBS worcestershire sauce
2 tsp. vinegar
1 tsp. accent

Directions:
1) Brown and drain ground beef. Add rest of ingredients. Add a bit of water if needed. Simmer until it's hot and serve!

Submitter: 
Amanda
Description: 
Great homemade recipe! Kids will LOVE this! This is for a large group of people, my grandma often makes it when all 20 of us get together. For a smaller group, like a family of five, 1/2 everything.
