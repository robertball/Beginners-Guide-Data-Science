Title:
Mexican Caesar salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
dressing-
2 Anaheim chiles-roasted, peeled and seeded
1/3 Cup of roasted pumpkin seeds(shells removed)
1 tbs of roasted seseme seeds(careful not to burn)
2 large garlic cloves, peeled
1/4 tsp of black pepper
1 tsp of salt
1 1/2 cups of salad or canola oil
1/4 cup of red wine vinegar
5tbs of grated cotija cheese(more if desired)
1 bunch of cilantro, stems removed
1 cup of mayonnaise
1/4 cup of water
salad-
1 head of romaine lettuce
crutons or 4 tortillas-cut into thin strips and fried
finely grated cotija cheese for topping
4 grilled chicken breast
toasted sesame seeds for topping

Directions:
Place all ingredients for dressing in the blender, except for mayonnaise, water, and cilantro. Blend well, then add cilantro little at a time, until smooth texture.
In seperate bowl, not plastic or copper, whisk mayo and water together.  Add the blended cilantro mixture into the mayo mixture.
cut the washed and dried lettuce, and place on plate. top with chicken breast, crutons or tortilla strips, drizzle desired dressing, and sprinkle with cotija cheese and seseme seeds, top with sprigs of cilantro for presentation.
*note- dressing will keep for about 3 days in air tight container in refrigerator.
