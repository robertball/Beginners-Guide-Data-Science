Title:
Whole Wheat Peanut Butter Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat bread flour
1 cup whole wheat pastry flour
3/4 cup wheat germ
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups mashed bananas
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup honey
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 tablespoons Reduced Fat Peanut Butter

Directions:
Grease a 9 x 5 inch loaf pan.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees (175 degrees C).
Combine oil, honey, eggs, vanilla, peanut butter and mashed bananas in a bowl and mix to combine well.
In a large bowl, whisk together flour, wheat germ, salt, and baking soda.  Make a well in dry ingredients, and add the banana mixture.  Mix together until dry ingredients are moistened. Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake 1 hour in preheated oven.  Test for doneness, and cool on wire rack.
