Title:
Grandma's Pumpkin Pie Auntie Fur Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can pumpkin
11/2 cups sugar
4 tbls cornstarch
1 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1/4 tsp salt
6 egg yolks
6 egg whites 11/2 qt milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.  Mix sugar, cornstarch and spice together, beat egg yolks and add to pumpkin with dry ingredients, heat milk to luke warm and add to mixture, and last beat egg whites.  Fold egg whites into pumpkin mixture.  Put into four nine inch pie shells and sprinkle top with nutmeg.  Bake in oven at 375 until custard is well set.
