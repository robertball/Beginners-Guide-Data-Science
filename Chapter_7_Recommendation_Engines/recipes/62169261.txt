Title:
Chicken Chile Rellenos Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole rotisserie chicken
5 whole fresh poblano peppers
1/4 cup olive oil
3 cups prepared Jasmine rice
1 bunch green onions
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1/2 cup prepared salsa
2 cans red enchilada sauce
1 Tbspn poultry seasoning
2 tsps cumin
1 tbspn black pepper
Salt (to taste)
2 tbspsn chopped garlic
3 cups grated white cheese (Muenster, Monterrey Jack, Queso Blanco, etc)

Directions:
Preheat oven on Roast 350 degrees
Cut tops and deseed poblano peppers and rough Chop into LARGE pieces - 1-2inches.
Toss pepper pieces with olive oil and place in large caserole dish and place in roasting oven for 20 minutes. Stir half way thru and continue roasting.
Prepare Jasmine rice accoring to directions.
While chilis and rice cooking, debone chicken and chop.
In bowl, place chopped chicken, chopped onions, chopped cilantro, chopped garlic, poultry seasoning, cumin, and black pepper and salsa and mix lightly.
Once rice cooked, fold into chicken mixture and salt to taste.
Remove peppers from oven.
Add 1 can of enchilada sauce to peppers and toss.
Add chicken rice "filling" on top of peppers.
Add 1 can of enchilada sauce to top of casserole.
Add cheese to top.
COVER and bake at 350 for 45-50 mins.
Let rest 10 minutes before serving.

Description: 
A Casserole twist on Chili Rellenos using leftover rotisserie chicken and fresh poblano peppers. Remarkably easy, full of flavor, and a Spicy crowd pleaser.
