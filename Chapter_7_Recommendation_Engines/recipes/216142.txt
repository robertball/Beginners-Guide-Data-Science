Title:
Cherry Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3257160.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen, pitted cherries
1 banana, cut in chunks
1/2 lemon, juiced
1/2 cup low-fat Greek-style yogurt (such as FageÂ®)
6 ice cubes
3 drops almond extract

Directions:
Place the cherries, banana, lemon juice, yogurt, ice cubes, and almond extract into a blender. Cover, and puree until smooth. Pour into glasses to serve.
