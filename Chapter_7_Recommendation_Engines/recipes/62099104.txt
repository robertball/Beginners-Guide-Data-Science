Title:
Coconut Cream Pie, Super and SIMPLE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 9in. pie shell, pre baked
14 oz package of sweetened coconut, toasted(will only need about 3/4 of it)
1 8oz container heavy cream whipped with 2TBS sugar
2 - 5.1oz. packages instant vanilla pudding
4C. Whole milk
1T. Coconut extract
1 envelope of powdered gelatin

Directions:
Pre bake your pie shell at 350 degrees until it's golden brown. Cool completely.
Toast the coconut, cool completely,(be very careful, once this starts getting brown, it goes fast!)
Whip the heavy cream with 2 TBS sugar, set aside
With a mixer, mix the pudding with the 4C milk for 3 mins.
Add coconut extract
Add the gelatin, just as it is, into the vanilla pudding, mix well. Be careful not to get any gelatin lumps.
Then fold in the whip cream
Lastly fold in the cooled toasted coconut. (Reserve some of the coconut for the top of the pie).
Spoon into the cooled crust, top with reserved coconut and refridgerate for a few hours until nice and set!
