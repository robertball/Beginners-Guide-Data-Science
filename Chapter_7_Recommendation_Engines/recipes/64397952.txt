Title:
Zucchini Pineapple Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
archment paper lined loaf pan
2 cups organic whole wheat flour or oat flour
2 teaspoons aluminum-free baking powder
½ teaspoon baking soda
2 ripe bananas - equals one cup after mashing with a fork
1 cup crushed pineapple
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 cup shredded zucchini
¼ teaspoon liquid stevia opt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
In a mixing bowl mash bananas with a fork
Add pineapple, vanilla, cinnamon, zucchini and stevia into bananas and mix together
In a second mixing bowl combine flour, baking powder and baking soda mixing, whisk together
Pour moist ingredients into dry and stir until all dry ingredients are moist
Line loaf pan with parchment paper
Pour batter into lined pan
Bake for 60 minutes - until a fork poked in the middle comes out clean.  Allow to cool a bit before cutting.
