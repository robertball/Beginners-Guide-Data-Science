Title:
Lemon Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg large lemon jello
1 pkg yellow cake mix
4 eggs
3/4 cup oil
3/4 cup water
Frosting:
lemon juice
powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Combine all ingredients well. Bake 1 hour.
Combine juice of one lemon with enough powdered sugar to make thick glaze. Spread over warm cake.
Note: Use the high altitude directions.
