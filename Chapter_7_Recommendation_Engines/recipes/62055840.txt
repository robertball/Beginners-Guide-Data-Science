Title:
Family Favorite Dutch Aple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Family Favorite
Dutch Apple Pie
Peel and slice enough apples for generously fill (mounded) the pie plate you are using.  Note: to keep apple slices from turning dark, soak in water with lemon juice.
Prepare crust for single crust pie
Drain apples (if soaking)
Mix apple slices with the following:
Standard pie plate
3/4 cup of sugar
1 tblsp cinnamon
Deep-Dish or Large pie plate
1 cup sugar
1 1/2 tblsp cinnamon
Pour apple mixture into pie plate
Cover apples with the following crumb topping
Standard pie plate
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup all purpose flour
6 tblsp butter
1 tblsp orange juice
Deep-Dish or Large pie plate
1 cup sugar
1 cup all purpose flour
8 tblsp butter
1 tblsp orange juice
Combine sugar & flour, cut in butter till crumbly consistency.  Pour over pie covering apple mixture evenly.  Drizzle topping with orange juice.

Directions:
Place pie plate on a baking sheet to catch spills
Bake at 450 for 15 minutes, reduce heat to 350 continue baking for 45 minutes (longer for Deep-Dish or Large pies)
