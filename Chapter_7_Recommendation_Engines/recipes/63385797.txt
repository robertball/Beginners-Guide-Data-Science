Title:
Almond Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c raw almond, soaked 4 hours or more
4 c filtered water
1/4 c agave nectar or raw honey
1 T lecithin
pinch of sea salt
a few drops of natural almond extract

Directions:
In a high speed blender, blend the nuts and water on high speed for about 2 minutes to make sure the nuts thoroughly blended.  Strain the milk through a nut milk bag or multiple layers of cheese cloth twice for smoothness.  Rinse the blender cup and place the milk back in it with the remaining ingredients.  Blend to combine thoroughly and taste for sweetness.  Store in frig 2-3 days.

Submitter: 
Pam English
Description: 
Great easy subsitute for regular milk.
