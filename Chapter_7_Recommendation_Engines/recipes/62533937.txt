Title:
Ham and egg salad spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds of ham or bologna or combination of both
4 boiled eggs
1/2 cup celery chopped fine
3 tablespoons sweet pepper relish
2 tablespoons sugar
3/4 to 1 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 tablespoons vinegar
1/2 teaspoon yellow mustard

Directions:
In food processor chop ham/bologna, egg, celery, relish until finely chopped. Add remaining ingredients and mix well.

Submitter: 
Nancy Roy
Description: 
I got this recipe from my husbands co-workers wife. They lived in Indiana before moving to New Hampshire. It can be made with bologna or ham or a combination of the two. Great way to use up leftover ham from the holidays.
