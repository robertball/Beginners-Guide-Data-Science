Title:
Grilled Orange Ginger Haddock Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Haddock fillets
2 Tbs. orange juice concentrate, thawed
1 Tbs. soy sauce
1 Tbs. honey
1/2 tsp. ginger
1 Tbs. extra Virgin Olive Oil

Directions:
Combine marinade ingredients. Place fish in a glass baking dish. Pour marinade over fish. Cover and refrigerate for one half hour. Spray grill with non-stick spray. Remove fish from marinade. Preheat grill to medium high heat. Place fish on grill( I put fish on aluminum foil sprayed with non-stick spray). Baste with reserved marinade, turning once during grilling. Fish is done when it is close to the point of flakiness. Serves four.

Submitter: 
SueC
Description: 
This delicious fish is an easy quick meal to make on a busy day and tastes like you were busy preparing it all day! I served with green salad, rice and green beans. Enjoy!
