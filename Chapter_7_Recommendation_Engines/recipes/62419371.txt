Title:
Healthy Chicken Posole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/443615.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Lbs Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts or Thighs cut into bite size peices
3 Qts Water
1- 1 lb 13 oz Can White Hominy
1- 1 lb 13 oz Can Yellow Hominy
1-1/2 Chicken Bouillon Cubes
1/4 Teaspoon Mexican Oregano
1 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
1/2 Teaspoon White Pepper
Salt and Pepper
2 Dried California Chilies
1 Dried Chile Ancho
1 Bag Tostados
Garnishes:
Sliced Radishes
Chopped Cabbage
Diced White Onion
Lime Wedges
Sliced Avocado

Directions:
To rehydrate the chilies bring small pan of water to boil (about 1-1/2 cups water).  Remove from heat, add chilies and cover.  Let set for about 20 minutes.
Bring 3qts of water to boil.  Once boiling add chicken.  Turn down to medium heat.  Scrap off any foam from top of pot.  After 30 minutes add garlic powder, mexican oregano and chicken cubes.
Drain all water from chilies except about 1/2 cup.  Remove stems and place chilies and water into blender.  Blend until smooth.  Pour mixture into pot with chicken.
Simmer for 15 minutes then drain and rinse the hominy. Add to pot with chicken.  Simmer for another 15 minutes on low.  Salt and pepper to taste. Then remove from heat and serve.
Top each bowl with garnishes desired.  Scoop up with tostados.
