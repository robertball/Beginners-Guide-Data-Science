Title:
Easy Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, with liquid
1 (14.75 ounce) can cream style corn
1 cup small uncooked seashell pasta
2-3 tablespoons butter, cut into pieces
1 cup shredded cheese
1-2 dashes hot sauce
Salt & fresh ground pepper to taste

Directions:
In a medium baking dish, mix the whole kernel corn, cream style corn, uncooked pasta, butter, and cheese.  Season to taste.
Microwave covered 12 minutes, stirring halfway.  Stir again & test pasta.  Microwave 2-5 minutes uncovered to desired doneness.
OR
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
Bake, covered, for 30 minutes in the preheated oven. Remove cover, stir, and continue baking 30 minutes, or until the pasta is tender but firm.

Submitter:

Description: 
Here's a creamy, slightly sweet corn casserole that's made with macaroni. The macaroni cooks in the corn juices, and blends deliciously with the melted cheese.Modified from: Corn Casserole I
