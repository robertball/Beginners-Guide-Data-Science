Title:
Delicious Crab Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs./cans of jumbo lump crab meat
8 cans of cream of potato soup
4 cans of creamed corn
4 cans of niblets corn (drained)
6 cans of milk (using soup can)
3 bay leaves
A lot of Old Bay - to taste!

Directions:
Put all ingredients into a large pot or slow cooker and cook until heated. (Better to let simmer a while to infuse the flavors.)

Submitter: 
Carol Kinser Fox
Description: 
A co-worker gave me this recipe when I told her my husband loved the Crab Corn Chowder at Bonefish Grill.  She said a friend's husband came up with the recipe.  It is super easy and absolutely delicious.
