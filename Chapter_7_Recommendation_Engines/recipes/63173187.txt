Title:
Taralle Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For Dough
6 eggs
Juice and zest of one orange
1 cup mazzola vegetable oil
1 cup sugar
5 to 6 cups of flour
1 envelope vanilina powder
4 tbsp baking powder
Icing
2 cups icing sugar
1/2 cup lemon juice
( add lemon juice slowly .Icing should be runny)
Dip cooled cookies into icing
spread on wax paper lined trays to harden.

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 375F
In large mixing bowl,beat eggs till fluffy with electric mixer
add orange juice , zest and oil , mix well
pour in sugar slowly
combine dry ingredients in separate bowl
slowly add dry mixture to wet ingredients one cup at a time mixing well after each addition
mix well til almost blended, then mix rest by hand
to form cookies, rolldough into 2 ropes about 3-4 inches long and twist together. or, roll ropes into circles to form round cookies.
Place 2 inches apart on parchment paper lined baking sheets and bake for about 10 min.
Cookies will be soft. Do not allow to brown.
