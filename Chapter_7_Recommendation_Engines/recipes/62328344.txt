Title:
Chicken Cordon Bleu Lasgna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
15 oz. ricotta cheese
8 oz. cottage cheese
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
2 cups diced cooked chicken
2 cups diced cooked ham
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
16 oz. jar creamy garlic Alfredo sauce
6 uncooked lasagna noodles
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1 cup shredded swiss cheese
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F. In medium bowl, beat eggs. Stir in ricotta, cottage, parmesan cheeses and 1/4 cup parsley; set aside.
2. In another bowl, mix chicken, ham, garlic powder, and Alfredo sauce. In ungreased 13x9 pan, spread about 1/2 cup of chicken mixture.
3. Top chicken mixture with 3 uncooked noodles, half of the cheese mixture, half of the remaining chicken mixture and half each of the mozzarella and swiss cheeses. Repeat layers starting with noodles, ending with swiss cheese. Cover tightly with foil.
4. Bak 1 hour or until very hot and bubbly. Let stand covered 15 minutes before serving. Sprinkle with 2 tbs. of parsley.
