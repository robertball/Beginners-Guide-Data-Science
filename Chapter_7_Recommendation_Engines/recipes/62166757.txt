Title:
Mandelspritsar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/749828.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cubes of butter, softened
1 cup sugar
1 egg yolk
1 T. heavy cream
1 t. almond extract
4 cups flour
1/4 pound of blanched almonds, finely ground

Directions:
Pre heat oven to 400 degrees
Cream together butter and sugar
Add egg yolk, cream and almond extract then blend well.
Stir in flour and ground almonds.
Fill cookie press with dough and press into desired shapes on ungreased cookie sheet
Bake for approximately 8-10 minutes or until the edges barely start to get browned.
Let cool on cookie sheets for a few minutes and then remove to wire racks to cool completely.

Submitter: 
Linda T
Description: 
Authentic and delicious Swedish almond spritz. Use your cookie press to make these into logs, wreaths or any shape you desire
