Title:
biski chocolate pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Biscuits(for small pudding)
6 Biscuits (for med pudding)
8 Biscuits  (for large pudding)
13 Biscuits( for joint family pudding)
half litre milk for small pudding
1 litre milk  for med pudding
1 and a half litre milk for large pudding
2 litre milk for joint family pudding
Chocolate sauce

Directions:
WARNING-Use the same kind of biscuit, Only keep it for three days in summers and five days in winters and Only take chocolate biscuits.
Take a bowl.Seperate the biscuits and the cream(only if it is a cream filled biscuit).Put the biscuits in the bowl but not with cream.Crush them in the bowl.Pore little of milk in the same bowl.Mix it.keep it in the fridge for 12 hours half a day.After taking it out mix it again.Decorate it with the chocolate sauce and what ever you like.Serve it in a long wine glass.Now we are done.
