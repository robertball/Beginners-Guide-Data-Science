Title:
Turkish Chicken Doner Durum Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad components:
Chopped cabbage
Graded cucumber
Chopped tomatoes
Coriander
Parsley
Lemon juice
Pepper
Chicken components:
Finely sliced chicken breast
Chopped onions
Chopped garlic
Sliced green or red peppers
Oregano
Thyme
Soy Sauce
AllSpice
Curry Powder
Olive oil
Taboon bread or Mexican tortillas
Optional:
Graded cheese
Greek yogurt sauce
Hummus

Directions:
Heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onions, peppers, garlic, and chicken. Add spices. Cook until done.
Fill taboon bread (or tortillas) with chicken mixture, cabbage mixture, and optional cheese. Wrap and grill. Add greek yogurt sauce or hummus.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
Like a mexican burrito, this popular turkish wrap is popular fast food in many areas of Europe. Traditionally the chicken, lamb, or beef is sliced off a vertical spit, but our Americanized version is done on the stovetop.
