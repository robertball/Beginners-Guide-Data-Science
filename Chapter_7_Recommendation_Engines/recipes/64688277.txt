Title:
Rob's Chicken Strip Pocket Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 Boneless, breaded chicken strips
3-4 Pita Pockets
1 head of romaine lettuce
Shredded sharp cheddar cheese
Ranch salad dressing
Hot sauce

Directions:
Cut up chicken strips in bite size pieces, heat in microwave or oven till warmed through
Slice pitas in half
Wash/chop romaine lettuce
Fill pita pocket with chicken strip, lettuce, cheese and dressing
EAY!
