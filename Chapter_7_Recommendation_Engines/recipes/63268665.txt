Title:
Steroid Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. butter, melted
6 eggs, slightly beaten
2 small cartons small curd cottage cheese
6 T. flour
2 pkg. chopped frozen broccoli

Directions:
Mix together and place into a greased pan.
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
