Title:
Cranberry Salad IIIa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups + cranberries finely chopped
1-2 large oranges zested then peeled  and seeded
1 cup finely chopped carrots
1 cup finely chopped celery
1 cup chopped pecans
1  8 0z can crushed pineapple drained
3/4 cup white granulated sugar
2 3 0z packaged raspberry flavored gelatin
1 cup boiling water
1 cup cold water

Directions:
individually process the cranberry's, celery, carrots, pecans.
Process the zest and oranges to a purree and make sure the zest is well chopped up
Place the ground cranberries and carrots and the orange mixture in a larger saucepan with 1 cup water and bring to a boil till the cranberries soften and the water reddens.
Add the sugar and gelatin and bring back to a boil and ensure the gelatin and sugar dissolve
remove from heat and add the pecans celery and pineapple and one cup cold water and mix well.
Let cool slightly and pour into mold of choice.
Let cool in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight.
Unmold if desired before serving and serve with a side of Mayo for those that want it

Submitter: 
Baron
Description: 
Modified from recipe by Carri Hyche. Great cranbery side dish for thanksgiving or anytime. I like it served with Hellman's Mayonaise
