Title:
'It's Da Bomb' Ranch Popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992937.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons unsalted butter
1 (1 ounce) packet Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix
1 (3 ounce) bag reduced-fat, butter-flavored microwave popcorn
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Place the butter in a small, microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on high until melted, about 1 minute. Stir in one packet of seasoning mix. Set aside.
Pop the popcorn according to the package directions. Immediately open the bag and pour the popcorn into a large serving bowl. Toss with the Hidden Valley(R) Original Ranch(R) butter mixture and then toss with the Parmesan cheese. Serve at once.

Submitter: 
Diane Morgan on behalf of Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
Make a last-minute play by tossing hot popcorn with grated Parmesan cheese and Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix-infused butter.
