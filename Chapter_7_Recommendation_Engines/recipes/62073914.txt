Title:
Cut with a Fork Swiss Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5 lbs. top sirloin steak
2 cans sliced, stewed tomatoes
1 lg. onion, sliced
meat tenderizer

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Cut steak into serving size pieces. Sprinkle meat tenderizer on both sides of each piece of meat. Pound tenderizer into meat. Pour 1 can of tomatoes into a 9 x 13 casserole dish. Top with half of the onion. Add steak to the casserole dish. Top with remaining tomatoes and onion. Cover with foil. Bake for 2 hours.

Submitter: 
KENDRAJEAN1
Description: 
This is a very easy recipe that produces steak so tender, you don't even need a knife to cut it.
