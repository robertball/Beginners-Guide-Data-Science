Title:
7-Layer Tortilla Chip Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (16 ounces) refried beans
1 cup Cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
1 can (2 1/4 ounces) black olives, sliced and drained
1 medium tomato, chopped
1 cup sour cream
1 cup guacamole
1 cup salsa
1 bag tortilla chips

Directions:
Using a 10-inch glass pie plate, spread the refried beans over the pie plate bottom.
Make a layer with the cheese, followed by the olives, then the tomato, next the sour cream, a layer of guacamole and top with salsa. Cover with plastic wrap and place in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
Jessica Boustead Aiken
Description: 
This is the recipe from the back of the Aldi's tortilla chip bag. Everyone always asks me for the recipe when I make it, so I'm putting it here for easy sharing.
