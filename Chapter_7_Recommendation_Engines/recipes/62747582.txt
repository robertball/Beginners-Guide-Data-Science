Title:
Creamy Ham and Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz egg noodles
1 can cream of celery soup
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 cup broccoli, cooked until crispy tender
1 cup ham, cooked and diced
1/2 cup milk
6 oz sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
16 buttery round crackers, crushed

Directions:
1. Prepare egg noodles according to package instructions. Drain noodles and return to pot when cooked through.
2. Add cream of celery, mayonnaise, cooked broccoli, cooked diced ham, milk, and 1/2 of the cheddar cheese to pot.
3. Gently warm ingredients in pot over medium-low heat until cheese is melted.
4. Transfer ingredients from the pot to a baking dish and top with remaining cheddar and then crushed buttery rounds.
5. Bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes or until cracker topping begins to brown. (hint: before putting in the oven, spray cracker topping with no-stick or olive oil spray).

Submitter: 
Jessica Weber
Description: 
A creamy, flavorful mixture of pasta, broccoli, and ham (i.e leftover Easter ham). Other veggies can substitute for the broccoli. Other cream of soups can be substituted, although the cream of celery has a fresh aftertaste. Measurements do not have to be exact at all.
