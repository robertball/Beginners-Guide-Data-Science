Title:
Leftover Meatloaf Turned Italian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Leftover Meatloaf
1 Jar of High quality Marinara Sauce
15 ou Polly-O Riccotta
1 bag shredded Mozzerella cheese
Salt/Pepper
1/2 cup Parmesean Cheese
1 Box of Rigatoni Pasta or Ziti

Directions:
Boil water for pasta and cook al dente.  While pasta is cooking, coarsly chop Meatloaf.  In a separate bowl, mix Ricotta, salt, pepper and 1/2 of Parmesean cheese.  Drain Pasta return to pot and add sauce,meatloaf, ricotta mixture, parmesean cheese and half of Mozzerella cheese.  Mix well.  Place in large baking dish and bake at 350 for 30 or until cheese melts.  5 minutes before - place extra mozzerella on top and melt.

Submitter: 
MARIA48
Description: 
I have always had leftover Meatloaf with nothing to do with it.  Take leftover meatloaf and add it to Rigatoni and cheeses for a great different Italian meal!
