Title:
Sicilian Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 Tbsp garlic, minced
1 Tbsp red onion, minced
1 Tbsp celery, minced
1 Tbsp lemon juice
1 Tbsp Dijon mustard
1-1/2 Tbsp fresh basil, fine julienne
1-1/2 tsp fresh parsley, chopped
1/4 tsp pepper
1/8 tsp salt
1/4 tsp sugar

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl.  Using a wire whip, mix until smooth.
