Title:
Cherry Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983084.jpg
Ingredients:
6 fluid ounces lemon juice, fresh squeezed
4 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
24 fluid ounces water
4 fluid ounces cherry juice concentrate

Directions:
Combine the lemon juice and the sugar. Stir to dissolve. Add the water and cherry juice concentrate and stir.
Serve over ice.

Submitter: 
The Cherry Marketing Institute
Description: 
Freshly squeezed lemon juice is sweetened with cherry juice for a refreshing variation on a classic summer cooler.
