Title:
Black Bush Sour Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976105.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Black Bush® Irish Whiskey
1/2 ounce lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon powdered sugar
1 maraschino cherry

Directions:
Pour Black Bush(R) Irish Whiskey, lemon juice, and powdered sugar over ice into a short glass; stir and garnish with a maraschino cherry to serve.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
The classic Whiskey Sour recipe gains a unique smoothness when made with the blend of malt and grain whiskey in Black Bush® Irish whiskey.
