Title:
Raw Cauliflower Mash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Large Head Cauliflower, florets cut thinly
1/3 cup raw Pine Nuts
1 tsp fresh herbs, chopped (rosemary and parsley are nice)
1 Tbs Extra virgin olive oil
2 Tbs Water
Extra virgin olive oil, salt, pepper to taste

Directions:
Place cauliflower floret slices in bowl, drizzle with olive oil, salt and pepper, mix.
Move slices to two non-stick dehydrator sheets in a single layer.
Dehydrate for 2 hours and 115 degrees.
Remove slices from dehydrator and combine with pine nuts, oil, and water in a food processor.
Process until 'mash' consistency.
Pour into a bowl, add herbs, mix and enjoy.
