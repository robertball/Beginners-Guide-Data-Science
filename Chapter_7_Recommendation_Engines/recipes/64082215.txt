Title:
Creamy Spicy butternut soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1157827.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
1 large sweet onion, chopped (2 1/2 cups)
3 celery stalks, chopped
1 1/2 cups carrots, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 medium sweet potato, peeled and cubed (2 cups)
1 butternut squash, seeded, peeled and cubed (6 cups)
1 orange, zested and juiced
1 Tbsp. cinnamon
1 1/2 tsp. sea salt
1 tsp. nutmeg, freshly ground
1/4 tsp. ground cayenne
4 cups water
3 Tbsp. maple syrup
Salt to taste

Directions:
In a large pot over medium heat, saut√© onions, celery, carrots, and bell pepper until softened.
Add squash, sweet potato, and orange juice with zest. Cook for 3 minutes.
Add cinnamon, salt, nutmeg and cayenne and blend well. Stir for 1 minute until spices are fragrant.
Add 4 cups water, cover pot with lid, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, covered, for 30 minutes or until squash is tender.
Blend soup with an immersion blender until smooth.  Add maple syrup to pot and thoroughly combine.
Taste for salt before serving.

Submitter: 
halfnotes
Description: 
This was a big hit with my family.  It has a decent kick and my kids went back for a third helping.  Do not leave the maple syrup out. Its sweetness really compliments the soup.
