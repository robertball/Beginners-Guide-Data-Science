Title:
Herbed Chicken and Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tsp. dried basil
1 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1 tsp. dried crushed rosemary
3 tbsp. flour - divided
1 1/2 lbs. thin-sliced boneless chicken breasts
2-3 tbsp. vegetable oil - divided
1 small onion - finely chopped
1 14.5 oz can of petite diced tomatoes - drained
1 1/2 cups chicken stock
2 tbsp. half & half (fat-free is ok)

Directions:
Mix seasonings and reserve 2 tsp.
Stir 2 tbsp. flour into seasonings in dish.
Coat chicken in flour.
Heat 1 tbsp. oil in large skillet over medium heat.
Add chicken and cook about 2 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
Remove from skillet and keep warm.
Add remaining tbsp. of oil to skillet and add onion.
Cook onion 3 minutes or until softened.
Stir in 1 tbsp. flour then add tomatoes, stock and reserved seasoning mixture.
Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally.
Reduce heat to medium low and simmer about 5 minutes.
Stir in half and half then return chicken to pan and cook until chicken is heated through.
Serve over cooked pasta if desired.
