Title:
Roast Chicken with Peas, Prosciutto, and Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken, cut up
salt and pepper to taste
5 oz prosciutto, diced
1 onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup dry white wine
10 oz frozen peas
1/2 cup heavy cream
2 tbsp water
1 1/2 tbsp corn starch
4 cups fafalle pasta, cooked and drained

Directions:
Season chicken with salt and pepper.  Combine chicken, prosciutto, onion, and wine in a slow cooker.  Cover and cook on low for 8 to 10 hours or high for 3 1/2 to 4 hours.
During last 30 minutes of cooking, add peas and cream.
Remove chicken.  Carve into slices and discard bones.  Transfer to a warmed platter.
Stir water and cornstarch in a small bowl until smooth.  Add to slow cooker and cook on high 10 to 15 minutes.
Toss chicken with pasta and sauce.
