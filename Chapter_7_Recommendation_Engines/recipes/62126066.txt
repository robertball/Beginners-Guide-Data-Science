Title:
Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cucumber, peeled and thinly sliced
1 Vidalia onion, halved and thinly sliced
1/4 c white vinegar
1/4 c sugar
2 T water
1/4 tsp white pepper
1/4 tsp salt
1/3 c vegetable oil
1/2 c ice cubes

Directions:
Place cucumbers and onions in a deep bowl.
In a separate bowl, dissolve sugar into vinegar.  Add water, salt & pepper.
Pour over cucumbers & onions and mix well.  Add ice and stir.
Refrigerate for 30 minutes.  Serve.
Note: cucumbers and onions meant to be eaten out of the dressing.  There will be leftover dressing in the bowl.

Submitter: 
Erin Noel
Description: 
A simple, cool salad for a warm summer day.
