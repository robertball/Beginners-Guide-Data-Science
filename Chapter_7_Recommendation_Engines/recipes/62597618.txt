Title:
Hot Cheese Blintzes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf white bread (the cheaper the better!)
1 pkg cream cheese, softened
cinnamon-sugar
1/2c melted butter

Directions:
Remove crusts from bread and roll thin.
Spread bread with cream cheese and roll up.
Cut into bite sized pieces.
Dip in butter, then cinnamon-sugar.
Bake @ 375 for 8-10 minutes.
