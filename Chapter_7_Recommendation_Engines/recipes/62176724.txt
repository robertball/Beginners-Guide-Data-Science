Title:
Ricotta Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. butter, (soft, not melted)
4 c flour
1 c Ricotta cheese
2 egg yolks
4 c chopped nuts
2 c sugar
3 T Ricotta cheese
2 T cinnamon

Directions:
Mix together butter, flour, 1 cup Ricotta cheese and egg yolks.
Divide into 4 equal portions.  Wrap in waxed paper and chill in refrigerator at lesat 4 hours.
Mix nuts, sugar, 3 T ricotta, and cinnamon until moist and crumbly.
Roll each ball out and spread with 1/4 of the filling.  Roll like a jellyroll.
Brush with egg white, then bake at 350 on an ungreased cookie sheet.

Submitter: 
Lisa Scott
Description: 
These are wonderfully rich breakfast treats.
