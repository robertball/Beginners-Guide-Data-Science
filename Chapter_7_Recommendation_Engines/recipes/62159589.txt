Title:
Southern Fried Chicken Livers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound (cup)chicken livers
1 cup of flour
1 Tbsp garlic powder
Salt and Pepper  (to Taste)
One egg
1/2 cup of milk
Canola or vegetable oil

Directions:
Rinse the livers in a colander.Break egg in bowl, scramble it, add milk, this is your egg wash.  Mix flour and spices in a plastic gallon bag. Put your rinsed livers into your milk wash. One at a time, drop and shake in the flour bag.Make sure all parts of the livers are coated, toss them evenly!
Deep Fry at 375 degrees.YOU MUST HAVE A FRY SCREEN!Fry til golden brown! These darn things pop and will burn you!
Or do like I do! FRYDADDY!
Can be served with pepper gravy or a packet of chicken gravy!

Submitter: 
Cindy Capps Lepp
