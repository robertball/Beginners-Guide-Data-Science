Title:
Perfect Cranberry Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 tablespoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
1 teaspoon salt
2 cups sugar
1 can (15 oz.) pumpkin
4 eggs
1 cup canola oil
1/2 cup fresh orange juice
2 teaspoons fresh orange zest
1 1/2 cups fresh cranberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Combine flour, spices, soda and salt in a large mixing bowl.
In another bowl, whisk together sugar, pumpkin, eggs, oil, orange juice and zest.
Add pumpkin mixture to dry ingredients and stir until moistened. Fold in cranberries.
Divide batter evenly between two greased 9"x5" loaf pans.
Bake for 60-70 minutes or until tester comes out clean. Cool in pans for 10 minutes before removing to wire racks to cool completely.

Submitter: 
anniepie
Description: 
This moist bread combines the bright taste of fresh orange and cranberries with perfectly spiced pumpkin. Freeze the extra loaf for later, or give one away. Just make sure to save some to enjoy with coffee as you unwind from all the holiday hustle!
