Title:
Spanish Rice and Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices bacon
2 cans pinto beans
2 seeded and diced tomatos
4 seeded and diced jalepeneos
1 diced onion
cilantro to taste
1 Tbsp canola oil
1 cup rice
1 small chopped onion
1 can tomato sauce
water as directed on rice package

Directions:
To make the beans:
Fry bacon in pan until cooked through. Remove and chop into bite-site pieces. Discard half of the remaining bacon grease left in the pan. To the remaining half add the bacon, tomatos, jalepeneos, onion and cilantro. Simmer on stove for 30 minutes or until veggies are soft and flavor is blended.
To make the rice:
Heat canola oil in pan over med-high heat. Add rice and onion and saute until rice is browned and onion tender. Add tomato sauce and finish rice as directed on the bag (usually 2 cups water, then simmer with lid on for 20-2 minutes). You can add cilantro to taste also.
