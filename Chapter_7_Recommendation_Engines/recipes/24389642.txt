Title:
Bacon and Tomato Penne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 strips cooked bacon
2 large tomatoes, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoons olive oil
3 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
5 ounces penne, cooked

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.  Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.  Stir in tomatoes and chopped bacon.  Cook, about 3-5 minutes, until the tomatoes are starting to break down.  Stir in cooked pasta, if the sauce seems dry add a few tablespoons pasta water. Serve with freshly grated Parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
elsaw
Description: 
Some bacon left over from breakfast and an overflowing pot of basil inspired this simple recipe.
