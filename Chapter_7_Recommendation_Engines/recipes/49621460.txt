Title:
Southern Pimento Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded extra-sharp Cheddar cheese
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4to1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper (optional)
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced (optional)
1 (4 ounce) jar diced pimento, drained
salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:
Place the Cheddar cheese, cream cheese, mayonnaise, sour cream,garlic powder, cayenne pepper, onion powder, minced jalapeno, and pimento into the large bowl of a mixer. Beat at medium speed, with paddle if possible, until thoroughly combined. Season to taste with salt and black pepper.

Submitter: 
rob
Description: 
This wonderful version of pimento cheese can be used for grilled cheese sandwiches, as a spread for crackers, or served alongside your favorite fried green tomatoes.Modified from: Southern Pimento Cheese
