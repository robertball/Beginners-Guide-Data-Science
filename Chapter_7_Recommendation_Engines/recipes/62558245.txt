Title:
Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cans of variety of beans, black, garbonzo, white, kidney etc.
1 cup ketchup
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup molasses
a ferw good shakes of Worcestershire sauce
ground mustard
garlic

Directions:
pour all beans juice and all into crockpot along with ketchup, brown sugar, Molasses, Worcestershire sauce ground mustard and garlic and cook for 8 hours
after 8 hours drain all the juice off the and re-add Ketchup, brown sugar, molasses, Worcestershire sauce, ground mustard and garlic.
