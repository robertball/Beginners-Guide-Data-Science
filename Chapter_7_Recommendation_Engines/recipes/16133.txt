Title:
Raspberry Salad Dressing II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/571563.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup plain yogurt
1/2 cup raspberries
1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
2 teaspoons white sugar

Directions:
In a blender, combine the yogurt, raspberries, vinegar and sugar. Blend until smooth and refrigerate until chilled.

Submitter: 
Karen
Description: 
This is a smooth and creamy raspberry dressing.  If you can find raspberry vinegar, use that instead.
