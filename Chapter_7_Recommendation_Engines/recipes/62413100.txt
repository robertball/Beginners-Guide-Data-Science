Title:
Bow Tie Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 box Barilla mini bow tie pasta
2 1/2 cups Kraft Zesty Italian dressing
1 cup sugar
4 tablespoons McCormicks Salad Supreme seasoning
1 1/2 teaspoons mustard
1 small green pepper
1/2 red onion
1/2 medium sized cucumber
2 cups sliced cherry tomatoes (about 1 small container)
1 tsp salt

Directions:
In a bowl whisk together italian dressing, sugar, salad supreme and mustard until smooth.  Chop your veggies to the size you desire.  Throw the veggies into the dressing mix, cover and place into the refrigerator.  Bring about 2 to 3 quarts of water to a boil in a good sized sauce pan then add your salt and pasta.  Cook for 10 minutes or until pasta is aldente.  Remove the pasta from heat and rinse with cold water until it is completely cooled.  Toss this with the mixture and serve.
