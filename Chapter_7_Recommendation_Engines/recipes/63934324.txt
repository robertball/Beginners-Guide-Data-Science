Title:
Patrick's Utility Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1103300.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs chicken, fat trimmed and cubed into 1/2" cubes.
1-2 Tbsp dry spice rub
1/4 cup orange juice (optional)

Directions:
Rub spices on chicken (if time marinade in orange juice in refrigerator for 30 minutes)
Heat olive oil in a skillet (or spray a non-stick skillet with cooking spray)on medium high (8 heat setting
Layer chicken in single layer in skillet
Cook for 1 minute.
After 1 minute, turn chicken, turn heat down to medium low (3 heat setting) and cover the chicken and continue to cook for 10 minutes.
Remove from heat and let sit covered for an additional 10 minutes (do not remove the lid)

Submitter: 
Apron Appeal
Description: 
Tender and moist chicken that can be flavored with any spice combination of your liking.  Freezes well and can be paired with just about any grain, starch or vegetable side.  This recipe is more about the technique to cook moist chicken then it is about flavor. 8 servings (3 oz cooked) Calories - 114, Carbs - 1, Fat - 2, Protein - 25g, sodium - 200
