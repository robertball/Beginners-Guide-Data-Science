Title:
Besta's Hot Caramel Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup white corn syrup
4 T. butter
2/3 cup heavy (whipping) cream, not whipped

Directions:
In a heavy saucepan or cast iron skillet, melt 1/2 cup white sugar under low-med. heat. This will take a while, use a wooden spoon or heat-safe spatula to stir occasionally. Heat only till melted thoroughly.
In another saucepan, heat brown sugar, white corn syrup and butter. Bring to a boil. Add the melted white sugar to brown sugar mixture, heat to 235 degrees. .be very careful...boiling sugar burns easily)
After reaching 235 deg. - remove from heat and stir in 2/3 c. heavy cream.
Pour into a mason jar or serving jar and cover with plastic wrap or lid.
Keeps in the fridge for up to 2 weeks.

Submitter:

Description: 
A rich decadent caramel sauce that has been passed down in our family from my great-grandmother, lovingly called Besta (hence the name)..not for the fat-content counters, but for those special occasions - this will wow your guests. 
Serve with a slice of Angel Food cake and a scoop of vanilla ice cream  - HEAVEN!!
