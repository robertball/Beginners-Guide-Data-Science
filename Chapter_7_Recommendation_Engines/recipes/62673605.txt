Title:
Italian Dressing Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon garlic salt
3 tablespoons garlic powder
4 tablespoon onion powder
4 tablespoon white sugar
8 tablespoons dried oregano
3 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon dried thyme
4 teaspoon dried basil
4 tablespoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon celery salt

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix together the garlic salt, onion powder, sugar, oregano, pepper, thyme, basil, parsley, celery salt and regular salt.  Store in a tightly sealed container.
To prepare dressing, whisk together 1/2 white vinegar, 4 tablespoons of the dry mix, 4 tablespoons water, and 1 1/4 cup canola oil. Mix in Tupperware shaker.

Submitter: 
healthy girl
Description: 
This Italian dressing mix is a combination of eight pungent herbs that you can store and use to whip up fresh Italian dressing in seconds whenever the mood strikes!Modified from: Italian Dressing Mix
