Title:
Honey Roasted Red Skin Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1914661.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pound red potatoes, quartered
4 tablespoons diced onion
2 cloves garlic
1 tsp. dried parsley
3-4 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoon honey
2 tsp. dry mustard
Cracked black pepper and sea salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375°. Lightly coat a cookie sheet (with edges, not a flat one) with nonstick cooking spray.
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.  Stir well and spread on prepared pan.
Bake in the preheated 375° oven for 35 minutes or until tender, stirring halfway through the cooking time.
