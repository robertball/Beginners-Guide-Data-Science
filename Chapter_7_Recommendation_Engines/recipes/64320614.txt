Title:
Fluffy French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup milk
1 pinch baking soda
3 eggs
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 cup white or brown sugar
12 thick slices bread

Directions:
Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in the milk. Whisk in the baking soda, eggs, cinnamon, vanilla extract and sugar until smooth.
Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
Soak bread slices in mixture until saturated. Add butter to the pans, and cook bread on each side until golden brown. Serve hot.
