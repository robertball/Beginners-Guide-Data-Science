Title:
Chocolate Syrup Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068420.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pints heavy cream, chilled
1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
3/4 cup chocolate syrup
2 (14 ounce) cans sweetened condensed milk
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
In a large bowl, beat cream with cocoa until stiff peaks form.
Stir in chocolate syrup, sweetened condensed milk and cinnamon.
Pour into a shallow dish or plastic container, cover and freeze 8 hours or until firm.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Cocoa flavored whipped cream, chocolate syrup, condensed milk and cinnamon are frozen overnight for an easy, no-cook ice cream.Modified from: Chocolate Syrup Ice Cream
