Title:
BUSH'S® Cheesy Pasta & Bean Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984173.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package penne pasta
1 (16 ounce) can diced tomatoes
2 (16 ounce) cans BUSH'S® Light or Dark Kidney Beans, drained and rinsed
1 (26 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Prepare pasta according to package directions. Drain and return to saucepan. Stir in tomatoes, BUSH'S BEST Kidney Beans and spaghetti sauce. Transfer to 2-quart baking dish and top with shredded mozzarella. Bake until cheese is melted and golden, about 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
BUSH'S® Beans
Description: 
This easy-to-make family favorite will please kids and adults alike.
