Title:
lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of oven-ready lasagna noodles
1 48oz jar spaghetti sauce
1 egg
8oz ricotta cheese
2c shredded mozzarella cheese
1lb ground beef
garlic salt
oregano

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Brown ground beef. Mix egg and seasonings into ricotta cheese and set aside. Drain beef, add sauce (reserve small amount) and heat through. Place reserved sauce in bottom of 13x9 baking dish. Begin layering ingredients on top: 3 noodles, ricotta, beef, mozzarella. Finish by topping with mozzarella. Cover with foil and bake 45 min. Uncover and finish baking 15min
