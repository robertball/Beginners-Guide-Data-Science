Title:
Crunchy Crisps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour
1 1/2 cups rolled oats
1/2 cup coconut (I've subbed in Special K or Rice Krispies or crushed Corn Flakes cereal)
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda

Directions:
Mix margarine and sugars together well.
Beat in eggs and vanilla.
Stir in remaining ingredients.
Drop by tablespoonfuls on cookie sheet.
Bake @ 375 x 12 minutes or until browned.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Another crispy, crunchy, chewy, delicious cookie made with oats and coconut by my favorite cookbook series.
