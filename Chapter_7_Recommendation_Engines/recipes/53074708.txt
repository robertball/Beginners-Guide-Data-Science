Title:
Kelly's Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound lean ground beef
1-2 Tablespoon Taco seasoning mix
1 cup salsa
2 Cups Tortilla chips, crushed
1 cup shredded Monterrey Jack/Cheddar cheese
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans or black beans, drained
1/2 cup ranch-style salad dressing
1/2 cup sour cream
1 Red pepper, chopped
1 head Romain Lettuce
Green onion or chives, chopped
Cilantro, chopped

Directions:
Place ground beef in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat, stirring to crumble until well done; drain. Stir in taco seasoning mix and salsa. Set aside to cool.
Place lettuce, cilantro, green onions, and red pepper in a bows. In a separate dish, mix sour cream and ranch dressing. Toss dressing with salad mix. Top with remaining ingredients.
