Title:
Mexican Stuffed Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box pasta shells, cooked & drained
1 lb. ground beef
16 oz. mild picante sauce
1/2 c. water
16 oz. tomato sauce
4 oz. can green chilis
1 c. (4 oz.) shredded monterey jack cheese
2.8 oz. can Durkee fried onions

Directions:
Brown beef & drain. Combine picante, water & tomato sauce. Stir 1/2 c. mixture into beef with chilis, 1/2 c. cheese, 1/2 can onions. Pour 1/2 sauce into bottom of 8x12 pan. Stuff shells with beef. Put shells in pan and pour remaining sauce over. Bake at 350, covered for 30 minutes. Top with remaining cheese and onion. Bake uncovered for five minutes.
