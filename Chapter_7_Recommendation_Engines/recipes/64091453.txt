Title:
Carnitas Flautas (Pork Rolled Tacos) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1191580.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Small Pork Roast
2 Tsp Salt
2 Tbs California Chili Powder
1 Tbs Mince Garlic
1/2 Cup Red Salsa (your favorite brand)
1/2 Cup Green Salsa (your favorite brand)
1 Can Chopped Black Olives
1/2 Cup Sour Cream + 1 Cup for Garnish
1/2 Cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese + 1 Cup for Garnish
12 Flour Tortillas
1 Cup Oil (for frying)
Guacamole for Garnish

Directions:
In slow cooker place pork roast, cover with salt, chili powder, red salsa and garlic. Slow cook pork roast on low setting  four to six hours or until tender depending on size.
Remove from heat, remove any fat, shred well with fork and let cool.
To cooled shredded pork add green salsa, cheddar cheese, olives and sour cream; mix well (You want mixture thick not runny).
Warm the tortillas in tortilla warmer to soften. Heat oil on medium high heat in large frying pan.
Add spoonful of pork mixture to one side of tortilla and roll up tightly. Place rolled tortilla in oil let brown lightly and then turn over.
Repeat until finished, keep warm in low heat oven. Serve with Guacamole, Shredded Cheddar and Sour Cream.

Submitter:

Description: 
Flautas have always been one of my favorite Mexican dishes, after reading so many recipes I came up with my own. My husband and I like to fry these up together on Taco Tuesdays. Can be served with Spanish Rice and/or Re-fried Beans.
