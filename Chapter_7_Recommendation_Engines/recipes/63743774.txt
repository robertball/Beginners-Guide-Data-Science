Title:
Pineapple Upside Down Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tbsp baking powder
1 can pineapple slices
cherries
7 eggs (separated)
10 tbsp water
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 1/2 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Prepare 9 x 11 pan by spraying, lining with pineapple slices, place cherry in middle of each cherry and sprinkle brown sugar on top
First Bowl:
Combine egg yolks, sugar, flour, salt and water
Second bowl:
Egg whites, and baking powder....beat rapidly by hand only
Once well mixed, put second bowl ingredients into first bowl
Mix all and pour into pan
Bake about 35-40 minutes
When cake is done, invert and place on tray for serving
