Title:
Sweet Hot Mustard Sauce for Cauliflower "Wings" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2135298.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 c Dijon mustard
1/4 c yellow mustard
6 T honey
1 T cider vinegar
2 tsp hot pepper sauce

Directions:
Mix well. Pour sauce over "wings" after they are baked 30 min (or put in the bowl and toss) and bake for 10 min. more. Let rest 5 min then toss again to re-coat.

Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
Hot, sweet glaze for a different take on Cauliflower Wings
