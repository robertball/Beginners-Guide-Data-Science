Title:
Baked Turkey Croquettes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup mashed potatoes
1 cup stuffing
1 cup chopped turkey
1 egg, beaten
1/2 cup panko bread crumbs
1 tsp melted butter, or any oil of your choice

Directions:
Preheat ove to 450 deg.
Prepare a cookie sheet with parchment paper.
Add the melted butter or oil to the bread crumbs to slightly moistened. set aside.
In a bowl, mix together the mashed potatoes, stuffing and turkey until well blended.  Scoop out approx 1 1/2 tbsp of mixture and form croquettes. dip in egg, then into the moistened bread crumbs. set croquettes on parchment lined cookie sheet and bake in the oven at 450 deg. for 15 mins or until golden and crispy.

Submitter: 
debbie eckstein
Description: 
Another yummy recipe for leftover turkey. Delicious served with gravy for dipping and/or cranberry sauce!
