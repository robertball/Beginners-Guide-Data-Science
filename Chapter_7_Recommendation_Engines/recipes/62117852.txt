Title:
OMG Potato Carrot Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T. bacon fat, and/or Canola oil
2 med. onions, finely chopped
6 cloves garlic, minced
3 carrots, finely chopped
6-8 med. potatoes, cubed (bite size)
8 cups chicken broth
1/2 - 1 stalk celery, finely chopped
1/2 tsp. French thyme, dried
1 tsp. seasoned pepper (or to taste)
1/2 cup milk (I used 1%)
1/2 cup shredded cheddar
3 T. bacon, cooked and chopped (optional)

Directions:
Heat bacon fat and/or canola oil in a Dutch oven over low-med. heat.  Add chopped onions and garlic. Saute 10 mins. until onions are softened.  Add chopped carrots and celery and saute another 5 mins.  Add potatoes, chicken broth, thyme, and seasoned pepper.  Bring to a boil and them simmer, uncovered, approx. 35-45 mins. until potatoes are soft.  Add milk and shredded cheddar.  Stir until cheese is melted.  Turn off heat and sprinkle cooked bacon on top.  Serve.
