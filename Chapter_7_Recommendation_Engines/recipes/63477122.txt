Title:
Garlic Yogurt Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
1 garlic clove, minced
2 tablespoons chopped chives or processed red onion
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon dried dill
1 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl.  Serve immediately or store in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
This dip is so delicious, no one would guess that it's good for you.  Use as a veggie dip or with baked chips or crackers.
