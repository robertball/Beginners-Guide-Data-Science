Title:
Carrot Cake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. + 2 T. flour
½ t. salt
½ t. baking soda
1½ sticks butter, melted and cooled
? c. brown sugar
½ c. granulated sugar
1 large egg
1 egg yolk
1 t. vanilla
1 c. grated carrot
½ c. candied ginger, chopped into small pieces
1 c. white chocolate chips
1 t. cinnamon
¼ t. cloves
¼ t. nutmeg

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees.
In a bowl, combine flour, salt, and baking soda. Set aside.
In a mixer, combine butter and sugar. Cream together for 3 minutes. Add eggs one at a time until incorporated. Add vanilla. Slowly add in dry ingredients. Next, add in carrot, ginger, white chocolate chips mix until incorporated.
Place on a cookie sheet in 1 inch balls, with about 2 inches inbetween.
Bake for 10 minutes at 325. When done, leave on the cookie sheet to cool for 10 minutes before moving to a wire rack for cooling.

Submitter: 
Emily P.
Description: 
These cookies have the flavors of carrot cake, but all the wonders of a cookie.  You could always frost them with cream cheese frosting, but I don't think they need it.  The white chocolate adds that great creamy bit.
