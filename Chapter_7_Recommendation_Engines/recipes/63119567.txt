Title:
Sour Cream Softies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/793814.jpg
Ingredients:
3 C. Four
1 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp Baking Powder
1/2 tsp Baking Soda
1 stick Butter or Margerine
1 1/2 C. Sugar
2 Eggs
1 tsp Vanilla
1 Cup Sour Cream
Cinnamon and Sugar Mixture

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Lightly grease cookie sheets.
Place flour, salt, baking powder and soda in a sifter.
In large bowl cream butter or margerine and sugar until well blended. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
Sift in flour mixture, adding alternately with sour cream and beating well to make a thick batter.
Drop by rounded tablespoons on cookie sheets. Sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar mixture.
Bake for 12 min or until edges turn a light golden brown.

Submitter: 
Kristina Edwards
Description: 
This was my mom's recipe and continues to be a family favorite. It is a moist, almost cake-like cookie, not too sweet, not bland, just different.
