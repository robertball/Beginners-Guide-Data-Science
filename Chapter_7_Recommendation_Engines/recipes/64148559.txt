Title:
Watermelon chutney - not rind Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1390836.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs watermelon
1 lb tomatoes
1 red onion
1 red pepper
fresh ginger
1 garlic bud
4 tb oil
salt, sugar
1 tb curry
limejuice

Directions:
mince onion, pepper and ginger, thinly slice garlic
sautee in oil
coarsely cut watermelon and tomatoes and add to pot with spices and limejuice
slow boil about 10 min uncovered
Fill in jars, close and turn upside down for about 5 minutes

Submitter: 
tokonoma
Description: 
great way of using up fresh watermelon, very tasty on sandwiches or with meat
