Title:
Grilled Barbequed Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Your favorite Honey Barbeque Sauce
12 to 24 fresh or frozen(uncooked)shrimp, peeled and deveined.
A Hot Grill
Metal or soaked wooden skewers

Directions:
Place shrimp on skewers and lay on hot grill. Brush with barbeque sauce. Turn skewers after aprox. 5 minutes. Brush the other side with sauce and grill an additional 5 minutes (depending on how hot your grill is). So simple yet SO Good!

Submitter: 
Nita Rockwell Minto
Description: 
A fast and easy addition to steaks on the grill. What's better than Steak and Shrimp?
