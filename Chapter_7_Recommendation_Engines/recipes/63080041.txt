Title:
Brown Sugar & Butter Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/777975.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup water
2 cups packed brown sugar
1 tablespoon vanilla extract or your favorite liqueur
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup heavy cream

Directions:
Boil water and brown sugar for 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat and stir in vanilla, butter, and cream.

Submitter: 
meyer
Description: 
Perfect sauce for bread pudding, ice cream, and many other desserts. Easy to make!
