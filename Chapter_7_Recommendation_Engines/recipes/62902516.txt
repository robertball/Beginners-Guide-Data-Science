Title:
Anna's Amazing Easy Pleasy Meatballs over Buttered Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of celery soup
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed French onion soup
1 (8 ounce) container fat free sour cream
2 pounds frozen Italian-style meatballs
1 (8 ounce) package uncooked egg noodles
1/4 cup butter

Directions:
In a large slow cooker, mix together the cream of celery soup, French onion soup, and sour cream.  Stir in the meatballs.  Cook on low heat for 6-7.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.  In a large bowl, toss the pasta with butter. Serve meatballs and sauce over the cooked pasta.
