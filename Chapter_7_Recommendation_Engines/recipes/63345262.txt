Title:
Easy Stuffed Bell Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 flat bottomed red, yellow, or orange bell peppers tops saved & seeded
4 cups cooked brown rice
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup onion, chopped
1 large garlic clove,chopped
1 tsp dried basil
1 tsp Italian seasoning
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 jar your favorite tomato sauce
2 chicken sausages, chopped
feta cheese

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Roast peppers on a sheet pan for 25 minutes. While they are cooking, heat a medium sized sauce pan over medium heat with the olive oil, saute the onion and garlic for 3 minutes,then add the basil, Italian seasoning, and pepper cooking another 2 minutes. Add the cooked rice and tomato sauce and then the chopped chicken sausages. Stir until heated through. Stuff the peppers with the filling, top with some feta cheese, replace the pepper tops and cook another 5 minutes

Submitter: 
BECKY M
Description: 
A healthy, colorful, and flavorful meal with a brown rice and fully cooked chicken sausages stuffing makes this recipe easy and fast.
