Title:
Gloria's San Pan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c brown sugar
4 tbsp corn starch
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 c. vinegar
2 T soy sauce
1 can pineapple tibits & juice
2 small (6 oz) cans pineapple juice
2 c sweet pepper strips (green, red, yellow, orange)
1/2 c slice onion
4 lbs chicken breast, cut into bite size pieces
Hot Rice

Directions:
Combine first five ingredients in bowl and set aside.
Fry chicken in butter or canola oil (or a combination of both), cover and cook until tender.
Remove chicken from skillet.
In skillet add pineapple mixture and cook stirring constantly until thickened.
Return chicken.  Cover.  Simmer 10 min.
Top rice with chicken & sauce.
