Title:
Carrot Salad with Golden Chardonnay Raisins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/873518.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup golden raisins
1/2 cup Chardonnay white wine
2 cups shredded carrots
1/4 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons minced chives

Directions:
Soak raisins in Chardonnay white wine in a bowl until plump, about 1 hour.
Drain and reserve any remaining liquid from the plumped raisins.
Mix carrots, mayonnaise, and chives together in a large bowl.
Fold plumped raisins into carrot mixture.
Mix reserved soaking liquid into the salad a tablespoon at a time until desired texture is achieved.

Submitter: 
MARYLAUREN
Description: 
A sweet and savory carrot salad. Very simple and easy to make with pre-shredded organic carrots. Golden raisins plumped with Chardonnay wine add sweetness and tang.
