Title:
Rosemary Baked Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. rosemary
salt
pepper
2-3 cloves garlic, chopped
2/3 cup breadcrumbs
olive oil as needed to make a paste
1 1/2 lbs. salmon

Directions:
Mash together the first six ingredients.
Use a fork to put the paste on the salmon.
Bake at 450 degrees 10-12 minutes, until salmon flakes.

Submitter: 
Chardonnay Queen
Description: 
I get my love of salmon from my Irish mother.  This topping has lovely rosemary and garlic flavors atop big flavor salmon.  As a friend of the environment, I only buy wild salmon.
