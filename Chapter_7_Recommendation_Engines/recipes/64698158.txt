Title:
Big Ray's Perfect Peach Cobbler Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5313396.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans (15 ounces each) sliced peaches
2 Tbsp brown sugar
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1 package yellow cake mix (regular size)
1/2 cup cold butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°. Place one can of peaches in a greased 13 x 9 -in. baking dish. Drain remaining can of peaches and add to baking dish; cover with cake mix & sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon.
Cut butter into very thin slices; arrange over top, spacing evenly. Bake until golden brown and fruit is bubbly, 35-40 minutes.

Description: 
This peach dump cake recipe gives you the best of two worlds: sweet, tender cake with a beautifully crisp cobbler topping. Add a scoop of vanilla ice cream on the side, and dessert's golden.
