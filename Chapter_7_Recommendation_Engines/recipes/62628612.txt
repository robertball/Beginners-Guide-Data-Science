Title:
Seven Can Chicken Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth (not condensed) add more to taste
2 can's (Campbell's) cream of chicken soup
1 (10 ounce) can chicken (breast) meat (Can use Rotissire chicken)drained
1 (15 ounce) can black beans (drained and rinsed)
1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chili's
Season to taste with:
Garlic powder
Salt (go light to start)
Pepper
Red pepper flakes
Chile powder
Cumin
Hot sauce (preferably, Cholula)
Tortilla chips (or, Santa Fe flavored tortilla strips at Safeway in the produce section is best)
Top with shredded cheese, sour cream and some Cilantro (for color)

Directions:
Open the cans of corn, chicken broth, cream of chicken soups, chunk chicken, black beans, and diced tomatoes with green chilies. Pour everything into a large saucepan or stock pot.  Add seasonings to taste. Simmer over medium heat until everything is heated through.  In bowl, put tortilla chips on bottom and top with soup.  Add shredded cheese and sour cream and/or cilantro on top.  Enjoy

Submitter: 
Diana
Description: 
A delicious and EASY zesty soup recipe that uses only 7 canned ingredients plus seasonings. Serve over tortilla chips, and top with shredded Cheddar cheese and sour cream. This soup IS a meal!Modified from: Six Can Chicken Tortilla Soup
