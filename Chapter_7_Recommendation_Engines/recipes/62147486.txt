Title:
Steak with Port sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sirloin steaks
1 cup of Port wine
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
1 tablespoon parsley, chopped
salt
pepper
1 tablespoon of olive oil
1/2 cup mushrooms, quartered
1 tablespoon of sour cream

Directions:
In a bowl make marinade by combining wine, garlic, vinegar,and parsley.
Salt and pepper steaks to taste and then place in marinade for 2-3 hours, refrigerate.
In a large pan, heat oil, cook steaks according to taste, and transfer to a warming plate.
Next saute mushrooms for 5 minutes in pan.
With mushrooms still in pan add marinade and sour cream.
Let this simmer until reduced to half.
