Title:
Mini-Pancake Napoleons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cinnamon Cream:
3 tablespoons soft cream cheese (tub style)
6 tablespoons powdered sugar
1/2 (scant) teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
3/4 whipping cream
Chocolate Pancakes:
1 cup complete pancake mix (extra fluffy)
2 tablespoons cocoa powder
2 tablespoons powdered sugar
3/4 cup water
1 tablespoon canola oil, plus more for griddle

Directions:
Cinnamon Cream:
With spatula cream powdered sugar into cream cheese.
Stir in cinnamon & vanilla until combined.  Then add cream.
With wire whisk, beat until stiff peaks form.
Transfer to a airtight container and refrigerate.
Will keep 24-48 hours refrigerated.
Chocolate Pancakes:
Preheat griddle over medium high heat.
Put pancake mix in small mixing bowl.
Sift cocoa & powdered sugar into mix and whisk to combine.
Add oil & water. Whisk until just combined.
Lightly oil griddle. For each pancake, add 1 tablespoon of batter to the griddle.
Cook about 1 to 1 1/2 minutes on each side.
Remove to a rack and allow the pancakes to cool completely.
Layer pancakes with parchment paper in an airtight container & refrigerate if not using same day.
Bring back to room temperature before using.
Assembly:
Layer a pancake, rounded spoonful of cream, pancake, rounded spoonful of cream, pancake. Gently press until cream comes just to the edges of the pancakes. Top with another spoonful of cream.

Submitter: 
LARANEFF
Description: 
Mini chocolate pancakes, layered with a light fluffy cinnamon cream. Each part can be made the day before and just assemble before serving. This is a very light dessert, small servings, and just slightly sweet.
