Title:
Breakfast Scramble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 serving Ready To Serve white rice
Cooked scrambled eggs
Shredded cheese
Crumbled cooked sausage
Warmed tortillas

Directions:
Cook the eggs and sausage. Stir in rice. Spoon fill tortilla and add cheese as desired. Roll up and serve.
