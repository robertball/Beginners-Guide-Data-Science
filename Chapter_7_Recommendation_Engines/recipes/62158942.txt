Title:
Sin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C Flour
1/2 lbs Butter
2 C Powdered Sugar
1 lbs Cream Cheese
1 Tbs Vanilla
1 cup pecan pieces
16 oz Sweetened whipped cream
2 lbs strawberries
1 lbs blueberries
2 16 oz containers strawberry fruit glaze

Directions:
Layer 1
Melt butter in a large glass casserole dish add 2 cups flour and 1 cup pecans.
Pat in bottom of pan and slightly up the sides.
Bake at 375 degrees, for 12 minutes
Let it cool
Layer 2
Cream 2 cups powdered sugar, 16 oz cream cheese, and tablespoon vanilla with 1/2 of the whipped cream
Spread mixture evenly on to layer one.
Layer 3
Arrange Fresh fruit in to pattern and color scheme desired on top of cream cheese mixture.
Cover with a fruit glaze.  I prefer strawberry flavored fruit glaze with all fruits.
Cool in refrigerator.
Top with whipped cream when serving.

Submitter: 
Shanda
Description: 
Awesome dessert!
Can play around with the types of fruits used and create beautiful presentations.
