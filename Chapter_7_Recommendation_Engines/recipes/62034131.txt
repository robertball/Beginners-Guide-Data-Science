Title:
Eddie's Special Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 tsp. Olive oil
1 or 2 green peppers, sliced
1 hot pepper, sliced (optional)
1 small onion, sliced
6 mushrooms
Low fat mozzarella cheese, if desired
Low fat Italian dressing
8 buns

Directions:
Make 8 thin hamburger patties.  Press them out on a plate covered with wax paper.  Cook in a skillet until done.
Saute peppers, onion and mushrooms in olive oil.  Cover to cook in the juices for about 5 minutes.
Place the hamburgers on the buns, top with the vegetables and a little cheese if desired.  Top with the Italian dressing.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
My husband invented this delicious sandwich to cut down on fat.  It is great even if you are not watching your diet!  This reminds us of a Philadelphia Cheese Steak sandwich.
