Title:
Grits Sausage and Egg Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup raw grits(stone ground preferred)
3 cups water
1 cup Half and Half
1 tsp salt
1 pound pork sausage or turkey sausage
1 stick butter
8 oz. grated sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 cup milk
3 eggs
chopped red and orange peppers(chop small)

Directions:
Bring the water and Half and Half to a boil and slowly whisk in the grits.  Stir occasionally while cooking. Lower heat and cook for 20 minutes until thick.  Add butter and cheese and set aside to cool.
While grits are cooking brown sausage and drain well.
Beat eggs and 1/2 cup of milk together and add to cooled grits along with sausage and peppers.
Pour into a well greased 9 x 13 dish.  Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Bake 45-60 minutes or until set.  IF not baking immediately you may cover and store overnight in refrigerator.
Key Point - Take out of refrigerator at least 30 minutes before baking.
