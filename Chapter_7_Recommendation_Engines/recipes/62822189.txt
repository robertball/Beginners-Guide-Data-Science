Title:
Trishie's Rosemary Roasted Baby Potatoes & Shallots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/666693.jpg
Ingredients:
10 Baby Potatoes
5 Shallots quartered
1 TBS Olive Oil
1/4 tsp dried Rosemary

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 400*F (200*C)
Place washed but unpeeled potatoes into a pot and cover with boiling water and boil gently for 10 minutes.
Strain and leave to cool for 10 minutes.
Cut potatoes into quarters and brush both cut sides with Olive Oil and place onto a baking tray.
Scatter the quartered Shallots around the potatoes.
Sprinkle the Rosemary over the potatoes & shallots and roast for 20 - 25 minutes.
