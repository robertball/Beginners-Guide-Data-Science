Title:
Pecan Delights Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Shortening
2 cups Packed Brown Sugar
1 teaspoon Vanilla
2 Eggs
2 1/2 cups Sifted Flour
1 Teaspoon Salt
2 Teaspoons Baking Powder
1 Cup Chopped Pecans

Directions:
Cream shortening, sugar, and vanilla.  Add eggs; beat well. Sift dry
ingredients together; sift into egg mixture.  Add pecans mix well.  Drop by
teaspoons onto cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees for about 10 minutes.
Yield 7 dozen.
