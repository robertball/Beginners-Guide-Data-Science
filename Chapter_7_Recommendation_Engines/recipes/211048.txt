Title:
Chicken Alfredo with Fettuccine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992341.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon I Can't Believe It's Not Butter!® Spread
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
1 (16 ounce) jar Ragu® Cheesy! Classic Alfredo Sauce
8 ounces fettuccine, cooked and drained

Directions:
Melt Spread in 12-inch skillet over medium-high heat and cook chicken, stirring occasionally, until chicken is thoroughly cooked. Remove chicken and set aside.
Stir Cheesy Sauce into skillet. Simmer covered over low heat, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes or until heated through. Serve sauce and chicken over hot fettuccine and sprinkle, if desired, with grated Parmesan cheese.
