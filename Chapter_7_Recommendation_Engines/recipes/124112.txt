Title:
Easy Lemon-Blueberry Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/243766.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups fresh blueberries
2 cups sugar
1 (3 ounce) package lemon flavored Jell-OÂ®

Directions:
In a large saucepan, slightly crush 2 cups of blueberries. Add remaining berries and sugar, mix well. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Remove from the heat; stir in gelatin until dissolved. Pour hot jam into jars or containers. Cover and cool. Refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Joyce  Robbins
Description: 
After one taste of this delightfully sweet and simple jam, people will find it hard to believe that you didn't spend many long hours in a hot kitchen. Of course, you don't have to let them in on your 'secret'!
