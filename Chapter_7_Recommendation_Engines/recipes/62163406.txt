Title:
Cosmocello Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 oz vodka
1/2 oz Triple Sec
1/2 oz freshly squeezed lime juice
1/2 oz Limoncello
Splash of cranberry juice

Directions:
Mix ingredients together in a shaker with ice and our into a chilled martini glass.

Submitter: 
Marianne
Description: 
This vodka martini-like cocktail is a little twist on the Cosmopolitan.  A bartender at a famous Italian pasta chain gave this to my daughter and me years ago!
