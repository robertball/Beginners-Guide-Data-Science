Title:
Quick Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 ounces shredded Swiss cheese
2 tablespoons butter, melted
4 eggs, beaten
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
1/4 cup green puree (pureed or chopped spinach and/or broccoli)
1/4 cup white puree (pureed cauliflower & zucchini optional)
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup flour
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
1 1/2 cups milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9 inch pie pan.
Line bottom of pie plate with cheese. Combine eggs, butter, onion, green puree, salt, flour and milk; whisk together until smooth; pour into pie pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 35 minutes, until set. Serve hot or cold.

Submitter:

Description: 
When you don't have time to make a pastry crust, try this quick crustless version. You may add any other goodies you like, such as ham, chicken, crab, shrimp or broccoli.Modified from: Quick Quiche
