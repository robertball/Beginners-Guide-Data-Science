Title:
Baked Chicken Chimichangas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 boneless chicken breasts
1 onion, chopped
1 green pepper, chopped
1 small can green chiles, chopped
Pkg. of tortillas (8-10)
2 cups sharp cheddar, shredded
1-2 cups mild salsa, divided
Spices and condiments to taste
Vegetable oil spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425F.
Place frozen chicken breasts on chopping block while sautéing onion and peppers in small amount of oil.
Slice chicken in narrow strips to 1 ½ - 2 inches.
Remove onion/pepper mix to medium bowl and add chile’s and 2/3 cup of salsa.
Place frozen chicken strips into skillet, season, add splash of oil if needed. Stir-fry 3-5 minutes. Remove skillet to bowl and set aside.
Open tortillas. Place 1/8 portions below middle of each wrap, cover with cheese, wrap up. Lay on foil covered & oil sprayed cookie sheet.
Bake 8 minutes, flip, bake 5 minutes.
