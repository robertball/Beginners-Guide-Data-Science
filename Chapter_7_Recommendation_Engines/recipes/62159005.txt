Title:
Fabulous & Simple Grilled Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb peeled, raw shrimp
2-3 Tbs butter
1/8 cup white wine (much better with wine you would drink, not cooking wine)
2 Tbs lemon juice
1-2 cloves garlic, pressed or minced
1/2 Tbs Creole Seasoning
1/4 Tbs Cavendars seasoning
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Spread shrimp on foil, add all ingredients, grill for about 5 minutes, stirring often.

Submitter: 
Brooke129
Description: 
Make sure you make a double batch of these, they won't last long!
