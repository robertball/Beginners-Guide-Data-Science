Title:
My Mom's Chicken Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1051836.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked & cubed chicken
2 cups Medium Egg noodles (cooked and drained)
1/2 cup milk
1/4 tsp. Fresh ground black pepper
Dash of salt
1/4 cup frozen green peas
1 can Cream of Mushroom soup
1/4 cup ground Parmesan cheese
2 cups Old Dutch potato chips - crushed
Dash of thyme and dill weed (optional)
1 cup shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Bake/Cook chicken, cut into cubes (set aside).
Cook medium egg noodles according to package directions, drain well.
Combine Chicken, noodles, soup, milk, peas, salt & pepper, thyme, and dill weed. Mix together.
Place mixture into a 9X9 greased baking dish and top with Parmesan cheese and crushed potato chips.
Place in preheated oven, uncovered, for 30-40 minutes - or until potato chips are browned.
**Add shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese the last 5 minutes, until melted, if you like.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Sarah Simons
Description: 
This is one of my favorite recipes handed down to me from my mother. It turns out great every time and is very versatile!
