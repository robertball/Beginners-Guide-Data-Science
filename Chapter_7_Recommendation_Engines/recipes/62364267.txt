Title:
Ginger Shrimp Pot Stickers with Peanut Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of cole slaw mix
2 TB chopped cilantro
1 TB soy sauce
2 t minced fresh ginger
1 t dark sesame oil
1/2 t salt
1/2 lb cooked peeled small shrimp
1/2 t hot sauce
-------------
24 wonton wrappers
2 TB cornstarch
1 TB canola oil
1 C water
SAUCE:
1/4 c water
1/4 c Peanut butter
2 TB low sodium soy sauce
1.5 TB seasoned rice vinegar
1.5 TB chili paste
1/2 t sugar
chopped green onions (garnish only- fancy :)

Directions:
combine 1st 10 ingredients in food processor. pulse 4 or coarsely chopped. keep wontons moist..one at a time.  spoon 1.5 tsp in center of each, moisten edges of dough with water and pinch to seal.  Place stickers on large baking sheet sprinkled with cornstarch.
Heat 1.5 t of oil in nonstick skillet over med-high heat.  Add 12 stickers, cook 2 min or until bottoms brown.  Slowly add 1/2 c water to pan, cover and cook 4 minutes.  Uncover and cook 3 minutes or until liquid evaporates and repeat with the other 12
see footnotes for sauce

Submitter: 
Laura Leh
Description: 
Love these as an appetizer for company. Prepare ahead and fry up as everyone has cocktails or prepare ahead and serve at room temp.
