Title:
Chicken Osso-Bucco Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 chicken thighs(about 2 1/2 lb.)
2 Tbsp. oil
salt to taste
freshly ground black pepper
1/2 lb. carrots
1 large onion, chopped
1 large celery rib, sliced
1(14 1/2 oz.)can Italian-style
stewed tomatoes

Directions:
Remove skin and fat from chicken thighs. Heat oil in large skillet over medium=high heat. Cook chicken until golden.
In drippings cook carrots, onion, and celery until lightly browned; season with salt and pepper. Stir in stewed tomatoes.
Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 25 minutes, or until chicken is fork-tender and juices run clear.
