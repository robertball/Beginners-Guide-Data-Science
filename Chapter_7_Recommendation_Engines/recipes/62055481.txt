Title:
Cheezy Hashbrowns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 32 oz bag of Shredded Hashbrowns, thawed
1 16 oz sour cream
1 8 oz. pkg of shredded cheddar cheese, divided
1 10 3/4 oz cream of mushroom soup
1/2 can of a small can of French Fried Onion Rings

Directions:
Mix thawed hashbrowns, sour cream, soup, 1 cup of cheese together.  Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes covered.  Add remaining cheese and French Fried Onion Rings on top.  Bake for an additional 5 minutes uncovered.

Submitter: 
SARAHB2
Description: 
Cheezy Hashbrown Casserole that is easy to make and is great for potlucks.  I mix and bake in the crock part of my crock pot, that way I can take to potlucks to keep casserole warm.
