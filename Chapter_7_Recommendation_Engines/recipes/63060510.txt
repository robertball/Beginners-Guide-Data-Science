Title:
EWM- Shrimp Pad Thai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup distilled white vinegar
1/4 cup fish sauce
1 (12 ounce) package dried rice noodles
1/2 cup peanut oil
1 1/2 teaspoons minced garlic
4 eggs
1 (12 ounce) package shrimp
1 1/2 tablespoons white sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1 1/2 cups ground peanuts or cashews
1/2 cup chopped fresh chives
1 teaspoon paprika
2 cups fresh bean sprouts
1 lime, cut into wedges

Directions:
To prepare Pad Thai sauce: In a medium saucepan over medium heat, blend sugar, vinegar, and fish sauce.
To make Pad Thai: Soak rice noodles in cold water until soft; drain. In a large skillet or wok over medium heat, warm oil and add garlic and eggs; scramble the eggs. Add shrimp and stir until well warm; add noodles and stir until cooked.
Stir in Pad Thai sauce, 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar and 1 1/2 teaspoons salt. Stir in peanuts. Remove from heat and add chives and paprika.
Serve with lime and bean sprouts on the side.
