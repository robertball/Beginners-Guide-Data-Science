Title:
Ranch Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887386.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 teaspoon chives
1/2 teaspoon parsley
1/2 teaspoon dill weed
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, sour cream, chives, parsley, dill, garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper.
Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
This is for those who prefer herbs rather than spice. Creamy and delicious - it is the perfect accompaniment to chicken, ham or crisp salad greens.Modified from: Ranch Dressing II
