Title:
Yellow mustard Recipe

Ingredients:
8 cup cold water
6 cup yellow dry mustard
6 teaspoon coarse sea salt or kosher salt
4 teaspoon ground turmeric
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon paprika
4 cup distilled white vinegar

Directions:
