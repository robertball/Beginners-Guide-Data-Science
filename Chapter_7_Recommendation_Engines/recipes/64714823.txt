Title:
Queso Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T butter
5 garlic cloves, minced
2-3 T hatch green chiles or to taste
4 oz cheddar, shredded
8 oz monterey jack, shredded
4 oz cream cheese
2-4 T heavy cream
2-4 T sour cream
cilantro to serve
optional: onions

Directions:
melt butter in pan over medium heat
add hatch green chiles and garlic (and onions/optional) and saute while stirring
reduce heat and add cream cheese and shredded cheese, continue stirring
add cream and sour cream
stir until all melted and smooth
sprinkle cilantro
NOTES:  Next time use white American cheese or pepper jack in place of cheddar or other mild cheese, maybe add cumin
