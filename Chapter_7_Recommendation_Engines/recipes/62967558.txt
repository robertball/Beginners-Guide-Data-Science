Title:
Crispy Onion Rings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup flour
1/4 cup corn starch
2 Tbsp.instant mashed potatoes
big pinch cayenne pepper
1 cup cold club soda
2-3 cups panko[japanese-style bread crumbs]
salt to taste
vegetable oil for frying
2-3 med.yellow onions,cut into 1/4 in.rings

Directions:
Sift together flour and cornstarch.Add instant potatoes,cayenne and club soda.Whisk well and let stand in fridge for 10 min.The batter will thicken in time.
Meanwhile,heat oil to 350.
Pour panko in shallow dish.Remove batter from fridge,check for consistency.It should resemble slightly thickened pancake batter.[add more soda if needed]
Working in batches,use fork to coat rings in batter,let excess batter drip off.Using another fork toss rings into panko crumbs,coat well.
Fry 2-3 min.turning over half way,fry untill crisy and golden.Sprinkle with salt while still warm.
