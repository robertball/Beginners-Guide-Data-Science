Title:
Chinese Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 lb beef roast
2 onions, sliced thin
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tsp ground ginger
10 oz pack of snowpeas

Directions:
mix brown sugar, ginger and soy sauce
in slow cooker coat roast
cover slow cooker and cook on low for 8 hours
shred when done
stir in snow peas
