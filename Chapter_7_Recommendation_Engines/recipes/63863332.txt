Title:
Carol's Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs of beef, cut in small pieces
4 Tbs olive oil
6 onions, cut up in chunks
3 Tbs brown sugar
Salt and pepper to taste
2 cans tomato paste (6 oz.)
3 tsp dry mustard
Beef stock (1-2 c)
1 c chutney
6 tsp ketchup
2 c red wine
1 lb cooked mushrooms (or other vegetable, cooked)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 deg. Saut√© onions in oil until yellow.
Brown beef.
Add sugar, salt and pepper, tomato paste and dry mustard.
Simmer a few minutes. Add beef stock to not quite cover..
Cover and simmer for 1 hour.
Add chutney, ketchup and wine.
Put in an oven-proof casserole. Cook 1 hr, 45 min in oven.
Add vegetables and cook 15 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Sudie B
Description: 
Even better reheated. Freezes well.  This recipe came from a cookbook published many years ago by St. Paul's Episcopal Church in Dedhaam, MA.
