Title:
Becky's Baby Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz Baby Spinach
1 cup shelled Walnuts
1/2 cup dried cranberries
2 small cans Mandarin Oranges, drained
3 hard boiled eggs, chopped
3 slices turkey bacon, chopped
1/2 tbsp butter
1 red onion, thinly sliced
1 tsp sugar
1 cup shredded carrots
1 cup sliced mushrooms, optional
1 cup diced ham, optional
Pumpernickel croutons, optional
Cooked Shrimp, optional
Honey Mustard Dressing or your favorite vinaigrette

Directions:
In a small saute pan over low-med heat, melt butter and cook red onion until caramelized. Mix in sugar, remove from heat and then set aside to cool. In a large bowl toss together all remaining ingredients and then toss in the cooled red onion. Serve with honey mustard dressing or your favorite vinaigrette.

Submitter: 
BECKY M
Description: 
My favorite salad! The optional ingredients are dependent on who is coming over but I always use them.
