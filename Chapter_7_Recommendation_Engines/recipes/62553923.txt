Title:
Baked Spaghetti for Two with Crumb Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz uncooked spaghetti noodles
1 pat butter
1 pound ground beef
1 small onion, chopped
1 (26 ounce) jar meatless (red) spaghetti sauce
1/4 teaspoon seasoned salt
2 eggs
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
5 tablespoons butter, melted
2 cups small curd cottage cheese
4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
.
1/2 cup crushed Ritz crackers or seasoned breadcrumbs
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese (dry like Kraft)
1.5 tablespoons melted butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ยบ.
Cook spaghetti noodles according to package directions; drain well.
Empty water from cooking pot and return noodles to the empty pot to keep warm; toss with a pat of butter.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet, cook beef and onion over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain.
Stir in the spaghetti sauce and seasoned salt; set aside.
In a large bowl, whisk the eggs, Parmesan cheese and butter.
Add spaghetti to egg mixture and toss to coat.
Place half of the spaghetti mixture in a greased 13.5-in. x 9.5-in. x 2-in. baking dish.
Top with half of the cottage cheese, meat sauce and mozzarella cheese.
Repeat layers.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 40 minutes.
In a small bowl, stir together crumbs, Parmesan and melted butter.
Uncover the casserole and spread crumb topping over it; bake 20-25 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.
