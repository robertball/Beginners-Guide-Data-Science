Title:
Sangria ala Felicia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2140136.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (750 milliliter) bottle Chardonnay wine
1 (750 milliliter) bottle sweet red wine
2 1/2 cups simple syrup
2 cups orange juice
1 cup rum
1 apple, thinly sliced
1 orange, thinly sliced
10 strawberries, halved

Directions:
Stir Chardonnay, red wine, simple syrup, orange juice, rum, apple slices, and orange slices together in a large pitcher.
Refrigerate wine mixture for at least 3 hours to overnight. Add strawberries just before serving.

Submitter: 
Pam Hostinsky
Description: 
When I had this sangria at my mom's, I knew I needed the recipe. She's not even sure where she got the recipe.   Always a favorite! Just ready to mix up a batch for New Year's Eve!  Enjoy!
