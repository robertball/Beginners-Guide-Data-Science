Title:
Peanut Butter Mousse Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Oreo cookie crust
8oz cream cheese, softened
2/3 C. creamy peanut butter
2 C. unsifted confectioner's sugar
1/2 C Light Cream (or half & half)
1 C. heavy cream
Hot Fudge syrup for serving

Directions:
Chill a medium bowl in refrigerator or freezer for at least 20 minutes. Meanwhile, in a large mixing bowl, combine softened cream cheese. peanut butter and confectioner's sugar.  Add about 1/2 of the light cream.  Start mixer on low speed, combining these ingredients thoroughly, gradually increasing speed to high (medium with stand mixer), Mixture will be thick.  Scrape bowl and beaters with a rubber spatula. Gradually add remaining light cream, beating at low spead to incorporate, then high speed (medium for stand mixer) to blend thoroughly.
In chilled medium bowl, beat heavy cream at high speed unitl JUST BEFORE stiff peak stage.  All at once, add this mix to the peaunt butter mix.  Gently fold together - the pie will not be uniform in color, there will be streaks of white from the cream and brown from the peanut butter.
Freeze uncovered for 1 hour.  After an hour, cover tightly with plastic wrap and Freeze for 6 hours.
When ready to serve, melt some hot fudge syrup and drizzle over the top of each slice.
