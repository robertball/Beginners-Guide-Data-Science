Title:
Grandmommy's  Spicy Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. Hamburger Meat
1 can of Tomato Sauce (15 oz.)
1 cup of water
1 can of Dark Red Kidney Beans (16 oz.)
1 pkg. Williams Chili Seasoning (original)
1/2 teas. Garlic Powder
1/2 teas. Cayenne Red Pepper
1/2 teas. Ground Thyme
1/2 teas. Ground Rosemary
1/2 teas. Cumin
1/2 teas. Oregano
1/2 teas. Sage
Salt and Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Brown hamburger meat at 325 degrees in an electric skillet. Drain off the grease.  Add the rest of the ingredients and stir. Serve with shredded cheese, fritos, minced onions, and Saltine crackers. Everyone may choose their optional items;) Enjoy!!!

This is a flavorful, spicy chili for a cold evening or anytime:)
