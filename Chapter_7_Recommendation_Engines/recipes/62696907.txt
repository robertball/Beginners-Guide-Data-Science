Title:
Gluten-Free Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/609668.jpg
Ingredients:
-2 squares of your favourite fondue chocolate
-1/2 cup melted butterscotch
-1/3 cup margarine/butter
-1 cup granulated sugar or sugar substitute
-2 eggs
-1 tsp Gluten-free vanilla extract
-1/2 cup potato starch
-1/2 tsp Gluten-free baking powder
-1/2 tsp salt
-1/2 cup chopped nuts of choice

Directions:
In a saucepan, melt margarine/butter, chocolate and butterscotch over low heat. Then proceed to stir in the sugar and let stand for 5 minutes.
Beat in eggs one at a time along with the vanilla extract. Add starch, salt, baking powder and nuts. Add this to chocolate mixture.
Pour mixture into a square baking pan and bake in an oven at 350F(180C) for 25-30 minutes. When done, let it cool and cut into squares.

Submitter: 
Chelsea Harrisson
Description: 
Here's a recipe to an incredibly easy to make, light, gluten-free brownie. What also makes it so convenient is that you can easily change its ingredients to better suit personal choice.
