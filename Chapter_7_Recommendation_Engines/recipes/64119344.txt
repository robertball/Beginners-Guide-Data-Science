Title:
Roasted Parsnips with Lemon and Herbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. parsnips, peeled and sliced into thin strips
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
1 lemon, sliced into wedges

Directions:
Place a rimmed baking sheet in oven; preheat oven to 500 degrees Fahrenheit. (Don't remove pan during preheating).
Combine parsnips, juice, oil, pepper, and salt. Arrange in single layer on preheated baking sheet and bake in the oven until tender, about 10 minutes.
Toss with parsley and dill. Serve with lemon wedges.
