Title:
Susan's Simple New York Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRUMB CRUST:
1 1/2 cup graham crackers (crushed)
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
__________________________________________
CHEESECAKE:
3 packages (8 oz. each) cream cheese, softened
1 1/4 cups sugar
4 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
2 tsp fresh lemon juice (optional)
___________________________________________
TOPPING:
1 container (16 oz) dairy sour cream
1/4 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 350 degrees.  Prepare crust:  Combine crumbs, sugar and butter in bowl.  Scrape into 9-inch springform pan; press evenly over bottom and 1 inch up sides.
PREPARE CHEESECAKE:  Beat cream cheese and sugar at medium speed in large bowl until blended.  Pour into prepared pan.
BAKE in preheated in 350 degree oven for 50 minutes or intil puffed and light golden.  Transfer to a wire rack and let stand for 15 minutes. Increase oven temp to 450 degrees.
PREPARE TOPPING:  Stir together sour cream, sugar and vanilla in small bowl until blended.
BAKE 10 minutes just until the topping is set.  Transfer cheesecake to rack and cool completely.  Refrigerate overnight.  Remove side of pan.  Garnish with fruit if you wish.

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
You rarely find cheesecake recipes with the sour cream layer on the top, but this kind is my favorite.  This was my first cheesecake recipe I had ever made and I always find myself going back to that first simple cheesecake.
