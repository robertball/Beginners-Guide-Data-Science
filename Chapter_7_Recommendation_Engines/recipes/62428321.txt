Title:
Undercover Whole Wheat Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups whole wheat pastry flour (not regular whole wheat flour)
1 egg
1 1/4 cups buttermilk
1 heaping teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup vegetable oil
a dash of salt

Directions:
In a medium sized bowl combine flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
In a large bowl whisk together egg, buttermilk, and oil.
Slowly whisk in dry ingredients until well combined.
Preheat a non-stick pan or griddle. Spray with cooking spray if desired.
Ladle batter onto pan according to the size you want your pancakes.
When the edges are slightly browned and bubbles are forming on the top of the pancake then flip over and cook the other side. The pancakes should be a nice golden brown color.

Submitter: 
Jessica
Description: 
No one will know that these pancakes are made with whole grains and good for you! They are light, fluffy, and moist. The key is to use whole wheat pastry flour which keeps it light, but still wholesome. These are delicious served with your favorite syrup or home-made jam!
