Title:
PB&J; Pretzel Snacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3277946.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup natural peanut butter
3/4 cup berry-flavored syrup
96 pretzel twists, or as desired

Directions:
Mix peanut butter and syrup together in a microwave-safe bowl; heat in microwave for 10 seconds. Mix well using a butter knife.
Spread peanut butter mixture onto 40 pretzels; top each with another pretzel making a sandwich.
