Title:
Swample Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4575257.jpg
Ingredients:
32 fluid ounces blue-colored sports drink (such as Gatorade®)
3 cups sparkling water (such as Perrier®)
1/2 (750 milliliter) bottle rose wine
12 fluid ounces peach-flavored fruit drink (such as Snapple®)
1 (8.3 ounce) can citrus-flavored energy drink (such as Red Bull™)
1 (2.17 ounce) package fruit-flavored candies (such as Skittles®)
2 cups vodka

Directions:
Mix blue sports drink, sparkling water, wine, peach-flavored drink, energy drink, fruit candies, and vodka together in a punch bowl.

Submitter: 
smerki
Description: 
This delicious, sweet, and fizzy drink may not be the prettiest punch, but it is a crowd pleaser and perfect for Halloween parties. Garnish with orange, lime, and lemon wheels.
