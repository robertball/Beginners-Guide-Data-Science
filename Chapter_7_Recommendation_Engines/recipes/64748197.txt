Title:
floyd eudy's white potato salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3# potatoes
1 medium Onion, minced
2 1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp Pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder
2 1/2 C. Mayonnaise
1 tsp sugar(optional)

Directions:
Boil California white potatoes unpeeled.
Chill overnight.
Mix sauce in a blender.
Peel potatoes, and slice thin.
Layer in serving dish, starting with sauce and ending with sauce.
Sprinkle with paprika.
Cover and chill overnight again before serving.
