Title:
Chocolate Chocolate Chip Biscotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all purpose flour
1 cup sugar
1/3 cup cocoa powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
2 eggs
2 egg whites
3/4 tsp vanilla extract
2/3 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Using a stand mixer fitted with paddle, combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda and salt.
Mix together eggs, egg whites and vanilla.
Gradually add egg mixture to flour mixture, on low speed. Add chocolate chips and blend just until combined.
On a floured surface roll dough into 2 logs, about 2 inches in diameter.
Place logs on a greased cookie sheet.
Bake at 350 degrees until light golden brown, about 30-35 minutes.
Allow to cool about 15 minutes, then cut into slices on the bias.
Return to greased cookie sheet and bake at 350 degrees until toasted, about 15-20 minutes.

Description: 
Crispy chocolate biscotti perfect for dipping into your coffee or hot chocolate.  A tasty no fail recipe.
