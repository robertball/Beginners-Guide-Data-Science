Title:
Sugar Free Coconut Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/759656.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Deep Dish Pie Shell
2 boxes Sugar Free Vanilla pudding
1 Bag Coconut
1 Tub of Cool Whip
Milk

Directions:
Soak 1/2 C. Coconut in milk overnight. Toast approx. 2 handfuls of coconut. Make pudding as directed for pie with the coconut milk. Add handful of toasted coconut to pudding mixture. Put pudding mix into baked pie shell. Top entire pie with Cool Whip. Sprinkle toasted coconut on top. Serve.

Description: 
This is a great pie for diabetics.  Betcha don't miss the sugar!
