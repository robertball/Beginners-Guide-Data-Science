Title:
Salted Peanut Brittle Deluxe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c sugar
1 c light corn syrup
1/4 c water
1 1/2 c salted peanuts
3 tbsp butter
1 tsp vanilla
2 tsp baking soda

Directions:
Combine sugar, corn syrup and water in 3 quart heavy saucepan. Mix well.
Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until sugar dissolves.
Continue cooking, stirring frequently to prevent scorching, until mixture reaches 285*F on a candy thermometer.
Remove from heat at once.
Stir in peanuts and butter and cook til mixture reaches 295*F on the candy thermometer.
Again, remove from heat at once.
Add vanilla and baking soda.  Stir to blend (work fast).  Mixture will foam.
Pour onto well buttered cookie sheets (2 large).
Spread out as thin as possible with spatula.  As soon as its cool enough (about 5 minutes) turn it over and pull to stretch as thin as possible.
Break into pieces when cold.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
This homemade candy is good anytime.  Delicious, quick, easy and much less expensive than ready-made.
