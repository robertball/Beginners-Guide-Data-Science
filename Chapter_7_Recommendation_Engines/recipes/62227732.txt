Title:
Whipped Jello Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of jello,1 cottage cheese (2 percent, 2 cups)1 small can fruit cocktail (drain the juice out), 1 large apple, 2 banana, large cool whip

Directions:
in large bowl mix the dry jello with the cottage cheese until well blended. Add the drained fruit cocktail, apples (cut into small chucks), cut the banana into slices. Mix well. Fold in cool whip until blended.

Submitter: 
Marie Smith
Description: 
It has cottage cheese, dry jello (any flavor, sugar-free)fruit cocktail, apples, banana's cool whip
