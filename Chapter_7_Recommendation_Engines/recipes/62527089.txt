Title:
Cranberry Sauce I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup orange juice
1/2 cup water
1 (12 ounce) package fresh cranberries

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over medium heat, dissolve the sugar in the orange juice. Stir in the cranberries, and cook until they start to pop (about 20 minutes). Remove from heat, and transfer to a bowl. Cranberry sauce will thicken as it cools.
