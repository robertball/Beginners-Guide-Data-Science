Title:
Fresh basil Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. basil leaes (about 1 lag. bunch)
1/2 c. good-quality olive oil
1/4 c. white wine or champagne vinegar
1 sm clove garlic
Seal salt and freshly ground black paepper to taste

Directions:
In a blender or food processor, whirl the basil, oil, vinegar, and garlic until smooth.  Add salt and pepper to taste.  Let vinaigrette set for several hours to allow flavors to mix.
