Title:
Oat-Bran Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/794123.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine, softened
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 1/2 cups quick-cooking oats
1 (11.5 ounce) package milk chocolate chips
2 cups chopped walnuts
1 1/2 cups all-bran cereal

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugars. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Beat in vanilla. Combine the flour, baking soda and salt; gradually add to creamed mixture. Stir in the remaining ingredients. Drop by tablespoonfuls 2 in. apart onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake at 350 degrees F for 12-14 minutes or until lightly browned. Remove to wire racks to cool.

Submitter: 
Judy  Bergman
Description:
