Title:
Veggy Stir Fry With Vermicelli Glass Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1728833.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium-large carrot, sliced
1 medium stalk celery, sliced
3 mini bell peppers assorted colors, sliced julienne
1/2 medium onion, sliced lengthwise
1 1/2 cups broccoli, chopped
2 tbsp China Sun Hoisin Sauce
3 tsp vegetable oil
1 tsp minced garlic (or 1 clove)
1 cup water
1 tsp cornstarch
750 g (2 cups) Longkou Vermicelli glass noodles, cooked (see below)

Directions:
Prepare the vermicelli according to package directions then, using a pair of kitchen scissors to snip the long strands of noodles, while draining.
Stir fry the veggies carrots, celery broccoli for about 5 minutes then add the garlic, peppers and onions, stir fry another 5-10 minutes, add the water and the hoisin sauce cover and simmer till veggies are done about 10 more minutes.
Add the cornstarch then stir until thickened, then add the vermicelli, stir until coated.
