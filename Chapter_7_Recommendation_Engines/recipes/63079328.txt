Title:
Chewy Oatmeal Cookies (Crisco) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup (3/4 stick) butter flavor Crisco shortening
1 1/4 cup firmly packed lgt. brown sugar
1 egg
1/3 cup milk
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
3 cups quick oats, uncooked
1 cup. all purpose flour
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
1 cup raisons
1 cup coarsely chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Grease baking sheets
Combine butter flavor crisco, brown sugar, egg, milk and vanilla in lg. bowl.
Beat @ med. speed till well blended
Combine oats, flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon in sep. bowl
Mix into creamed mixture @ low speed till blended.
Stir in raisins and nuts
Drop rounded T-spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto baking sheets
Bake 1 sheet at a time for 10-12 min.
Cook 2 min. on baking sheets.  Remove cookies to foil or wire rack to cool completely.
