Title:
Mediterranean White Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can white beans (16oz can, drained)
½ cup sliced black olives
½ cup diced Roma tomatoes
½ cup crumbled feta cheese
½ cup Italian salad dressing

Directions:
Stir together white beans, olives, tomatoes and cheese in a medium bowl. Pour salad dressing over mixture, and gently toss salad. Make sure dressing coats everything, then cover the bowl with a lid or plastic wrap. Place salad in refrigerator, and let chill for at least two hours. Remove bowl from refrigerator, and serve
