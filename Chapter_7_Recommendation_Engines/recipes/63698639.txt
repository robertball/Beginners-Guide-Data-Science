Title:
Eleanor's Shrimp Dino Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
4 bay leaves
1 small onion, chopped
2 TBSP flour
2 cups milk
1 tsp curry powder
1/3 cup dry white wine
1 pound shrimp
1 can sliced mushrooms, drained
3 TBSP garlic butter
1/2 lb. grated swiss cheese (can use 1/2 mozzarella)

Directions:
Saute onion, bay leaves, and butter until golden.  Remove bay leaves, add flour and stir until thick and bubbly.  Slowly stir in milk and cook until medium thick.  Add curry and wine.
Grease 4 individual (or 1 large) casserole dishes with garlic butter.  Line dishes with shrimp and mushrooms.  Cover with sauce.  Sprinkle generously with cheese.
Bake at 375 degrees until cheese is melted and slightly golden brown.
