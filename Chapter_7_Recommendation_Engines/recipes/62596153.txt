Title:
Honey I Shrunk My Gut Oatmeal - Abs Diet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup 1% milk
3/4 cup plain instant oatmeal
1/2 cup blue or blackberries
1 TBS chopped walnuts or pecans
1 tsp honey
1 tsp ground flaxseed
dash of cinnamon

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a microsafe bowl, nuke for 2 minutes.
