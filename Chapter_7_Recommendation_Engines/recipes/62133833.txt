Title:
Streusel Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bread:
1 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
2/3 cup sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
Dash of ground cloves
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup mashed ripe banana (2 to 3 bananas)
1/3 cup shortening, margarine, or butter
2 tablespoons milk
2 eggs
Topping:
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
In a food processor combine 1 cup of the flour, the white sugar, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, cloves, and salt.  Add banana; shortening, margarine, or butter; and milk.  Combine all ingredients.  Add eggs and remaining flour; pulse until blended.  Pour batter into a greased 8 x 4 x 2-inch loaf pan.
In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar and cinnamon.  Sprinkle the mixture evenly over uncooked banana bread.
Bake in a 350 degree oven for 50 to 60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean.  Cool for 10 minutes on a wire rack.  Remove from the pan; cool thoroughly on a wire rack.
