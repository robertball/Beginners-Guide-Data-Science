Title:
Mom's Potato chip cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289879.jpg
Ingredients:
2 sticks margarine ; softened
1/2 cup Sugar
1/2 teaspoon Vanilla
1 3/4 cups Flour
3/4 cups Plain chips ; crushed
1/2 cup Pecans halves

Directions:
preheat oven to 350Â°
cream margarine and sugar in mixer on medium speed till light and fluffy
Add vanilla and flour and mix untill well blended
stir in crushed chips.
roll by teaspoon full place on ungreassed cookie sheet. Place 1 pecan half on each cookie and press gently to stick
bake 10 minutes untill edges are lightly browned.
