Title:
Cracked pepper & honey butter popcorn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups warm popcorn
2 tbsp butter
2 tbsp honey\3/4 tsp coarsley ground pepper
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
place the cooked warm popcorn in a large bowl
combine the butter and honey an micro them on medium until both are melted, about 40 seconds - stir well
drizzle over popcorn and toss
sprinkle popcorn with pepper and salt and toss
taste and adjust seasoning

M. Karen
