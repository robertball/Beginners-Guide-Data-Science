Title:
Waffles (Alton Brown) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1038947.jpg
Ingredients:
4 3/4 oz all purpose flour
4 3/4 oz whole wheat flour
1/2 t baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
3 T sugar
3 eggs
2 oz butter, melted
2 cups buttermilk, room temp

Directions:
Combine dry in large bowl, whisk, and set aside.
Beat eggs well, then whisk in melted butter.  Continue whisking to emulsify a bit.
Add buttermilk (can use milk with vinegar or lemon if desired).
Pour wet into dry and lightly mix to combine - don't overmix.  Let batter sit for 5 minutes.
Pour into preheated waffle iron.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
No yeast mix!  The addition of whole wheat flour and buttermilk gives lovely taste.
