Title:
Citrus Chicken Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/580875.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Skinless Boneless Chicken Breast Halves
1/2 cup Fat Free Chicken Broth
3 1/2 Tbs. Orange Marmalade
1 packet (1 oz.) Dry Ranch Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix
1/2 Tbs. Corn Starch
2-3 cups Cooked Rice
1 Tbs. Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Directions:
Add olive oil to skillet at medium heat.
Saute chicken over until cooked through (about 10 minutes).
Combine ranch mix, chicken broth, and marmalade in a separate bowl; pour over cooked chicken.
Sift corn starch and add to mixture.
Bring to a low boil and reduce heat to medium/low and cover for about 10 minutes.
Serve over cooked rice.
