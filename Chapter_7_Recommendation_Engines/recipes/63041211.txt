Title:
Roast Turkey Southwestern Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz chorizo or other hot sausage
1 pkg (1 lb) cornbread stuffing mix
1 can (17 oz) whole corn kernals, drained
1 can (4 oz) diced green chilies
ground sage
thyme leaves
1 (11 to 14 ob) whole turkey
3 TBL vegetable oil
poultry seasoning
Beau Monde seasoning
skewers

Directions:
Dice or crumble chorizo. In skillet, stir and cook chorizo until browned, drain; reserve. Prepare stuffing mix with butter and hot water as package directs; add corn, chiles, chorizo, 2 tsp sage and 1 tsp thyme; reserve.
Rub turkey, inside and out, with vegetable oil then with 1 tsp each: poultry seasoning, Beau Monde seasoning, sage and thyme. Spoon stuffing loosely into turkey cavities, using skewers to close. Roast turkey, uncovered in roasing pan in 325 degree oven, basting with pan juices every 45 minutes or until thermometer inserted into thickest part of breast meat or in center of inside thigh meat registers 175 degrees. An 11 lb turkey will take 3 to 4 hours and a 14 lb turkey will take 3/12-4/12 hrs.
