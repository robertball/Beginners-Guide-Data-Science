Title:
Hershey's ® Chocolate-Berry Parfaits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992883.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
1/4 cup HERSHEY®'S Cocoa Powder
1/3 cup cornstarch
1/4 teaspoon salt
3 cups milk
3 egg yolks, beaten
2 tablespoons butter
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 (10 ounce) package frozen sliced strawberries in syrup, thawed
1 cup heavy cream, chilled
1/4 cup confectioners' sugar

Directions:
Stir together sugar, cocoa, cornstarch and salt in heavy saucepan; stir in milk and egg yolks.  Cook over medium-high heat, stirring constantly, until mixture boils; boil and stir 1 minute.  Remove from heat; stir in butter and vanilla.  Cool.
Drain strawberries; in blender, puree to equal 1/2 cup.  In small mixer bowl, beat whipping cream and powdered sugar until stiff; fold in strawberry puree.
In parfait or wine glasses, alternately layer chocolate pudding and strawberry cream, ending with chocolate.  Cover; refrigerate until set.

Submitter: 
HersheysKitchens.com
Description: 
Homemade chocolate pudding is layered with sweet strawberry whipped cream and chilled in this colorful dessert.
