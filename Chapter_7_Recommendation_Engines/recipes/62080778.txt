Title:
Rebecca's Canneloni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 canneloni or manicotti pasta shells
1 lb riccotta cheese
6 oz shredded mozzerella cheese
1/2 lb fresh spinach, cleaned and chopped
1 lb ground veal
8 fresh garlic cloves
1/2 small onion
2 28oz cans whole tomatoes with juices
1 cup white wine
4 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
Cook pasta al dente, run under cold water to prvent sticking and set aside.
In a large saute pan, saute veal in 2 tbsp olive oil until meat is browned.  Use a food processor to chop onion and 4 of the garlic cloves.  Add them to veal and continue to saute until onions are translucent.  Add 1/2 cup of wine and saute over low heat until alcohol cooks out.  Set aside and allow to cool.  In a large mixing bowl, mix together riccotta, mozzerella, spinach and veal mixture.  Use fingers to gently fill pasta, place in a deep casserole dish.  Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Process both cans of tomatoes in food processor or blender.  Chop remaining 4 cloves of garlic. In a large saucepan,saute garlic in 2 tbsp olive oil until garlic is lightly browned.  Add tomatoes and 1/2 cup wine and saute 10 minutes, stirring frequently.  Pour sauce over pasta shells.  Bake uncovered for 25 - 30 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
rebeccalovestocook
Description: 
I make this italian treat whenever we are entertaining. People always rave about it! You can jazz it up by adding red pepper flakes to the sauce, or use beef or pork instead of the veal.  You may also top it with some more mozzerella for the cheese lovers out there! Enjoy!
