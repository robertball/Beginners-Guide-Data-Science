Title:
Cranberry Orange Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese, softened
2 tbsp. orange marmalade OR concentrated orange juice
1/8 tsp. cinnamon
1 tbsp. sugar
1 tsp. orange extract
¼ cup finely chopped dried cranberries
¼ cup finely chopped pecans (opt.)
Snack crackers

Directions:
Combine the cream cheese, marmalade, cinnamon, sugar, and extract until smooth.  Fold in cranberries and pecans if desired.  Refrigerate and serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Diane Blanchard Angell
Description: 
I found tweeked the original recipe to my liking and found it to be very delish!  A great sweet spread that goes well with snack crackers!
