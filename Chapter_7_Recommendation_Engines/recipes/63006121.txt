Title:
Extra Rich Dark Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/751058.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Devil's Food Cake Mix
4 large eggs
1 cup sour cream
3/4 cup water
1/4 cup oil
1 pkg. chocolate instant pudding & pie filling
3/4 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Combine all ingredients except chocolate chips. Beat well. Stir in chips. Pour into a well greased Bundt pan. Bake 50-60 minutes. Cool 20 minutes, them remove.
