Title:
Healthy Fruit and Vegetable Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4470024.jpg
Ingredients:
6 fluid ounces milk
1 (6 ounce) container plain yogurt
1/2 cup frozen strawberries
1/2 frozen banana
1/4 cup frozen blueberries
1 green ice cube (see footnote)
2 tablespoons whey protein powder (optional)
1 teaspoon honey, or to taste

Directions:
Combine milk, yogurt, strawberries, banana, blueberries, green ice cube, whey, and honey in a blender, in the order listed. Blend until smooth.

Description: 
A great mix of fruits and vegetables to get started in the morning or post workout that still tastes awesome.
