Title:
Jim's Italian Beef w/sweet peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/723441.jpg
Ingredients:
1- 4 pound round roast
1- teaspoon salt
1- (2/3 oz) italian salad dressing mix ( may substitute 1/4 cup Wishbone Italian dressing)
1- bay leaf
1- teaspoon black pepper
1- teaspoon dried oregano
1- teaspoon dried basil
1- teaspoon onion salt
3- cups water
1-teaspoon dried parsley
1-teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over medium high heat , combine the water, salt, ground black pepper, oregano, basil,onion salt, garlic powder,bay leaf and italian salad dressing mix.
Stir well and bring to a boil.
Place the roast in a slow cooker and pour the mixture over the roast. Cook on low 10 hours or high for 5 hours. remove bay leaf and shred with a fork. Serve on Italian rolls!
