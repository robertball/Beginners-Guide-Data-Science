Title:
Polish Supper Bake (Mock Pierogi) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz bag kluski noodles - cooked & drained
2 lbs bacon - chopped and fried crispy
10 oz muenster cheese - cubed
10 oz farmers cheese - cubed
1 large onion - diced
1 medium sized head of cabbage - shredded
salt & pepper to taste
french-fried onions (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
After cooking bacon, drain off grease
Add diced onion & shredded cabbage to bacon
Cook in covered pan until cabbage is tender (about 20 min)
stirring occassionaly
Transfer to a large pot or mixing bowl
Add kluski noodles, muenster cheese,farmer's cheese and
french-fried onions (optional)
Mix until blended
Pour mixture into casserole dish
Cover & bake for 35-45 minutes or until cheese is melted
