Title:
Holiday Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups fresh cranberries
1  cups water
1/2 C. Orange Juice
5 whole cloves
5 whole allspice berries
3 cinnamon sticks
2 cups white sugar

Directions:
Place fresh cranberries, water, and Orange Juice in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
Wrap cloves, allspice berries and cinnamon sticks in a spice bag. Place in the water with cranberries.
Cook until cranberries begin to burst, about 10 minutes.
Stir in sugar and reduce heat to low. Continue cooking 5 minutes, or until sugar has dissolved. Remove from heat and allow to cool. Discard spice bag. Chill in the refrigerator 8 hours, or overnight, before serving.

Submitter: 
Dorothy Clark
Description: 
Fresh cranberries are boiled with spices and cooked with sugar to create a delicious sauce.Modified from: Holiday Cranberry Sauce
