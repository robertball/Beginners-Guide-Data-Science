Title:
Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/750018.jpg
Ingredients:
3.5 lbs chicken, skinned, cut up
2 stalk celery
2 carrots, sliced
1 medium onion
2 quarts (8 cups) water
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
Dumplings:
4 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
6 TBS butter
1 1/2 cups buttermilk

Directions:
In a large pot, combine first five (5) ingredients; bring to a boil, cover, reduce heat, and simmer for one (1) hour or until chicken is tender.
Remove chicken, reserving broth in pot, cool broth and chicken
Bone and cut chicken
Remove fat from broth; bring to a boil and add pepper
In medium bowl, combine **dry** dumpling ingredients; cut in butter until crumbly
Add buttermilk, stir until moist
Turn dough out onto floured surface; knead 4-5 times and roll to 1/4" thickness
Cut dough into squares and drop into boiling broth
Add chicken, reduce heat to medium-low and cook eight to ten (8-10) minutes, stirring occasionally
