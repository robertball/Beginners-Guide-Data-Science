Ingredients:
8 oz Cream Cheese
8 oz Sour Cream
8 oz Sargento Four State Cheddar Cheese Blend
3 oz Beer
1 tsp Dill Weed
1 tsp Chives
1 tsp Crushed Red Pepper
1 packet Ranch Dip Mix
Pretzel sticks

Directions:
Combine cream cheese, sour cream, and Sargento cheese in a deep bowl using a hand mixer.
Add beer until dip is acceptable consistency (it's game day; drink the rest!)
Add ranch packet, dill weed, chives, and crushed red pepper.
Adjust seasonings to personal preference.
Refrigerate for 2-24 hours.
Serve in assorted shooters w/pretzel sticks.
