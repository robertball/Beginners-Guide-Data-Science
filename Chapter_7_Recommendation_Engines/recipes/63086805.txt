Title:
Aunt D's Eclairs(cream puffs) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/967444.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening or butter
1/8 teas salt
1 cup boiling water
1 cup sifted flour
4 eggs
1 package of instant vanilla pudding

Directions:
Add shortening and salt to boiling water.  Reduce heat, add flour all at once and stir until mixture forms a ball around the spoon.  Remove from heat.  Add eggs 1 at a time and beat Thoroughly by hand after each egg is added.  Do NOT add the aggs all at once.  Shape into strips about 1 inch wide and 4 inches long on an ungreased cookie sheet.
Preheat oven and Bake for 20 minutes at 450 degrees THEN reduce heat to 350 degrees and cook for an additional 20 minutes.  Watch your oven carefully as the temp is important.
mix pudding accirding to directions and fill a pastry bag poke a quick hole in the eclair with the tip and fill with pudding.  Or just cut off the top, fill with pudding and replace the top.  Cover with your favorite chocolate frosting or ganache.  Store in refridgerator.
