Title:
Orzo with Spinach, Tomatoes and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups raw orzo
3 Tbsp olive oil
2 cloves garlic, chopped
1 cup vegetable broth
1 pint cherry tomatoes, cut in half
8 baby bella mushrooms, thickly sliced (optional)
1 1/2 cups fresh spinach
salt and pepper, to taste
1 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook orzo according to directions on package.
Heat olive oil in large saute pan.  Add garlic and cook until slightly browned.  Add vegetable broth.
Stir in cooked orzo.  Add sliced mushrooms and sliced cherry tomatoes.  Saute until mushrooms are cooked through.  Mix in salt and pepper, to taste.
Add spinach.  Stir until wilted.
Stir in parmesan cheese until melted.  Serve.

Submitter: 
BONF
Description: 
This is a great summer meal!  I prefer this with feta cheese, but the rest of my family doesn't like it, so I use parmesan when I make it for them.
