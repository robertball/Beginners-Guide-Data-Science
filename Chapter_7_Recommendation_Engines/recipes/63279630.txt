Title:
MeMe's Old Fashioned Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup raisins (or chopped apricots)
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup melted butter
3/4 cup applesauce
2  eggs
1  teaspoon vanilla
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon cloves
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup dark brown sugar
3/4 teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoons baking powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 8 inch pans, bundt pan or 9 by 13 pan. In a saucepan, simmer the raisins in the water on medium low heat for 5 minutes. Cream together vanilla, applesauce and butter until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time. Sift together flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg,allspice, ginger and cloves.  Blend these dry ingredients into the sugar mixture. Add the melted butter, buttermilk and water. Mix thoroughly. Strain the raisins and add (or chopped apricots).   Add the chopped nuts (optional). Spread the batter evenly into the prepared pan.
Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the top springs back when lightly touched. Cool the cake on a wire rack, and frost.

Karyn Osborne

This cake is good with a cream cheese frosting or with a dusting of cinnamon mixed with powdered sugar.Modified from: Old-Fashioned Spice Cake
