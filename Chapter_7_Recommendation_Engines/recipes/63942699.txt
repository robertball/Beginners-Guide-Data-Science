Title:
Gooey healthy brownies! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup almond butter
1 egg
1 tsp coconut oil
3 tbsp unsweetened applesauce
1/2 tsp sea salt
1/4 cup raw sugar
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 cup dark chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease small baking pan.
Warm almond butter and coconut oil together in microwave for 10 secs to soften.
Add to the almond butter/ coconut oil mixture the applesauce and egg and blend by hand until smooth. Add sugar, baking soda, salt and vanilla extract until well mixed.
Fold in chocolate chips. Pour batter into greased pan.
Bake for 25- 30 minutes until top is dark brown and slightly crispy.
Let cool for 10-15 minutes. Enjoy!
