Title:
Sloppy Joe's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1013652.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T. olive oil
1 pound lean ground beef or turkey
1/2 c. finely chopped green bell pepper
1/2 medium onion,finely chopped
1/2 c. finely chopped celery
1/2 c. finely chopped carrots
1 can condensed chicken gumbo soup
1/2 c. chili sauce
1 T. yellow mustard
1 t. onion salt

Directions:
In a skillet,add oil and brown hamburger over medium heat, drain off liquid. Add all vegetables and cook until they are tender.
Stir in chicken gumbo soup, chili sauce, mustard and onion salt.
Reduce heat, bring to a simmer until sauce thickens. (about 5 minutes)  Serve on hamburger buns.

Submitter: 
Lnldad
Description: 
This recipe is based on the Sloppy Joe's I loved as a kid. My own kid's don't eat enough veggies, so I added them to this recipe and they pass the kid approved test.  This recipe was entered in our schools cookbook and has been a big hit for a quick weeknight supper.
