Title:
chicken fried rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp peanut or veg oil
4 scallions, greens and whites separated, chopped (whites should be used like onions to enhance flavor and greens to garnish)
1 tbsp grated fresh ginger
2 cloves minced garlic
1 med diced zuchini
2 diced carrots
2 c bite sized broccoli florets
2 c mushrooms stems removed, sliced
1/2 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs, sliced into thin bite sized pieces
4 c cooked brown rice
2 tbsp low sodium soy sauce
2 eggs,lightly beaten

Directions:
in a wok or a large skillet, heat the oil over med-high heat. when the oil is lightly smoking, add the scallion whites, ginger, and garlic and cook for 30-45 secs. add the zuchini, carrots, broccoli, and mushrooms and cook for 4-5 mins, using a spatula to stir the vegetables throughout. add the chicken and continue cooking for 2-3 mins until the pieces are no longer pink.
stir in the rice and soysauce and cook for another 5 mins allowing the rice to get crispy on the bottom. create an empty space in the middle of the pan and add the eggs. use a spoon or spatula to quickly scramble the eggs until light and fluffy, then stir them into the rest of the ingredients. serve garnished with the scallion greens
