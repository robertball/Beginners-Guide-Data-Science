Title:
Easy Biscuit Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6720949.jpg
Ingredients:
5 left over, biscuits or  flaky layered biscuits
Garlic Salt
Parsley
Real Butter

Directions:
cut biscuits in half, if precooked, otherwise, cook as directed on biscuit label, then cut in half. ( pull for Flaky biscuits)
Place  Biscuits on non-stick fold (  for easy clean up)
Dab with sliver of Butter cut fresh fro the stick of butter
Sprinkle with Garlic salt and Parsley to suit taste
Cook for 5 minuets at 375 until golden brown

Submitter: 
Chris
Description: 
Need something to do with your left over Biscuits, make dinner fun, by making this quick and easy biscuit garlic bread, good to par with Spaghetti
