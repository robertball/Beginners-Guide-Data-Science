Title:
Microwave Steamed Mushroom Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup rice
1 dried shiitake, soaked in water (stock reserved)
10 g shimeji mushrooms
20 g enoki mushrooms
200 mL shiitake water
2 tsp sake
pinch of salt
small piece of kombu (dried kelp)
parsley (garnish)

Directions:
Wash rice and soak in water for 30 minutes.
Soak shiitake in water until it becomes soft. Remove the hard stem part then slice the head part thinly.
Cut the root part of the shimeji mushrooms then separate individually.
Cut the root part of the enoki.
Mix the rice and the mushrooms in a microwave-safe bowl.
Mix the shiitake water, sake and salt then pour  it on the rice and mushrooms. Add kombu.
Cover with a lid or cling wrap then microwave for 5 minutes using 600W. Take out then mix then microwave for 12 minutes using 200W. Afterwards, keep it closed for 5 more minutes for the rice to cook completely.
Serve and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
Steamed rice with 3 types of mushrooms (shiitake, shimeji and enoki) cooked in a microwave. Use your favorite mushrooms for this recipe and use as many kind as you want. Mushroom lovers out there will surely love this recipe.
