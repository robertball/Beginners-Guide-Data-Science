Title:
Ham & Split Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 cup dried split peas
1 1/2 cup onion, minced
1/4 tsp marjoram
1 cup sliced celery
1 cup carrots, shredded
1 1/2  lb. ham bones, shanks

Directions:
Cover peas with 2 qts cold water, simmer 2 minutes,
Cover and let stand 1 hr.
Add bone, onion, 1 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp pepper & Marjoram
Bring to boil, cover& reduce heat
Simmer 1 1/2 hr.
Remove bone, dice meat. Add veggies. Cook Uncovered 30-40 minutes
TO CAN:
Ladle hot soup into jars leaving 1" headspace. Pressure can pints 75 and qts 90 min. Ater canner pressure has returned to zero leave 2 more minutes before removing vent. Remove lid & wait 10 minutes before removing jars.

Submitter: 
Kim Snyder
Description: 
nice THICK split pea soup. Great use for leftover hambones
