Title:
Zucchini Pineapple Bread II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs, beaten
1 cups white sugar
1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 cup vegetable oil
2 cups grated (or shredded) unpeeled zucchini
3 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans, toasted
1 cup crushed pineapple, drained
1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
In a large bowl mix together the eggs, sugar, vanilla, oil, applesauce, pineapple and zucchini.
In a separate bowl mix together the flour, soda, baking powder, and salt. Add to the zucchini batter and mix well. Stir in 3/4 of the nuts until just blended. Pour into 2 - 9x5x3 (or 8x4) inch loaf pans.  Then sprinkle with remaining nuts.
Bake in a preheated 325 degree F (165 degrees C) for one hour, or until browned.

Submitter:

This is a quick bread that my mom used to make every summer with the zucchinis from the garden.Modified from: Zucchini Pineapple Bread II
