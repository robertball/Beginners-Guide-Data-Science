Title:
Aunt Millie's Dilly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3172320.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) can beer
6 fluid ounces whiskey
1 (6 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed

Directions:
Vigorously stir beer, whiskey, and lemonade concentrate together in a pitcher until the foaming stops. Serve over ice.
