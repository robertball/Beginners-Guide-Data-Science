Title:
Fluffy French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup extra fluffy pancake mix
1.5 cup milk
1 pinch salt
5 eggs
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 tablespoon vanilla extract
1.5 tablespoon agave nectar/honey
12 slices texas toast

Directions:
Preheat oven to 200F.
Lay bread on a rack over a baking sheet in a single layer.
Put in oven, turn off oven, allow to dry out several hours.
Measure pancake mix into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in the milk. Whisk in the salt, eggs, cinnamon, vanilla extract and agave until smooth.
Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
Soak bread slices in mixture until saturated. Cook bread on each side until golden brown. Serve hot.
