Title:
Oatmeal at the Manor Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cup rolled oats
1 cup apple juice
1 3/4 cups water
1/3 cups dried fruit or raisins(optional)
1 dash salt
3 Tablespoons wheat germ(optional)

Directions:
add all into rice cooker- press on(about 20-23 minutes
note..place in air tight container
keep in fridge up to 4 days
