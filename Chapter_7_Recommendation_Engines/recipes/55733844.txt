Title:
Beef Stew Carrie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds cubed beef stew meat
1 1/2 c flour
1/2 t salt
1/2 t pepper
4 c water
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 envelop onion soup mix
1 pkg Mc Cormic bag and season beef stew
4 cups water
3 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
4 carrots, cut into 1 inch pieces
4 stalks celery, cut into 1 inch pieces
1 large onion, chopped (pearl are the best)
OR one bag frozen stew veggies
1 1/2 c flour
3 T shortning
2 t baking powder
1/2 t salt
1 t savory
3/4 c milk

Directions:
In a large pot or dutch oven, cook beef in oil over medium heat until brown.  .  Stir in rosemary, parsley and pepper.  Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover and simmer 2 hour2.
Stir water, potatoes, carrots, celery, and onion into the pot.  .  Cover and simmer 30 min more.
Cut shortning in to flour, add all other ingredients. Bring soup to boil, spoon in dumplins. Simmer 10 min covered and 10 uncovered.
