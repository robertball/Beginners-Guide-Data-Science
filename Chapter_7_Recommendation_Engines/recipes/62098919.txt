Title:
THE best chocolate&PB milkshake ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
2 heaping tbsp of hot chocolate powder
1 chocolate pudding cup
1/2L oreo cookie ice cream
4 tbsp peanut butter
1/2 cup crushed ice

Directions:
In a blender add milk, hot chocolate mix and pudding.  Blend.
Add peanut butter and ice cream.  Blend.
Add crushed ice adn blend until smooth.
*If you find it either too thick or too thin adjust the amount of milk used.

Description: 
This is creamy and so deliciuos.  It has the right amount of chocolate and the right amount of peanut butter
