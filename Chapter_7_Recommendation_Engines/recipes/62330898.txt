Title:
Chicken in Cream Sauce with Artichoke Mushroom ( Chicken Jerusalem) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
2 cups chicken stock
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1 Medium shallot, sliced as thin as you can
1 cup dry white wine
1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
1 (10 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained
1 fat free half and half
3 oz. freshly grated parmesan cheese
dash Tobasco Sauce
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
2) Place chicken in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear (160F)
3) While the chicken is baking, place the chicken stock, garlic, and shallots in a medium saucepan.
4) Bring to a boil, and cook until liquid is reduced by half.
5) Stir in wine, reduce heat and simmer until slightly thickened.
6) Add the mushrooms, and artichokes to the chicken stock mixture.
7) Continue to simmer until mushrooms are tender, about 2 minutes.
8) Stir in the heavy cream and parmesan, simmer stirring occasionally, until cheese is melted and sauce is thickened. Check seasoning and use salt and pepper to taste.
9) Place chicken breasts on platter, ladle sauce over all, if desired garnish with fresh cilantro or parsley.
