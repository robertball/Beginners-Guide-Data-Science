Title:
Skillet Cabbage Unrolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (1 lb.)package bulk breakfast sausage
1 (14.5 oz.)can diced tomatoes and peppers
1 (10 oz.)can red enchilada sauce
1 cup uncooked rice
1 small-medium head of cabbage, coarsely chopped
1-1 1/2 cups water (as needed)

Directions:
Crumble and brown sausage in a large skillet over medium-high heat.  Drain excess fat.
Lower heat to medium and add all other ingredients.  Cover with lid.
Cook approximately 20 minutes, stirring occasionally an adding water to prevent sticking. It is ready when rice and cabbage are tender and most of liquid is absorbed.

Submitter: 
Idess
Description: 
I love cabbage rolls but hate the rolling part, so I decided to stop rolling them and just throw everything together and see how it worked. See if you like the results as much as my family and friends do.
