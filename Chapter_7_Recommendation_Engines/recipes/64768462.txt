Title:
Small Green Salad  " kleiner Gruener Salat" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 bunch;  Red Leaf Lettuce
1/2  Green Leaf Lettuce
1/2 Butter Lettuce
1 Chicory Lettuce.
1 small bunch of Garden Cress.
1/2 Orange
1/2 Lemon
Dressing:
8 oz of Whipping Cream
1 tsp of Salt
1 tsp of Sugar
1 tbsp of Lemon Juice.
ground;  White Pepper, Marjoram

Directions:
Wash and Separate the Leafy Lettuces and Butterfrom the stem. cut of the Stem ends.  Tear into mouth size Pieces and wash again. Spin dry or collander and then dry on a Towel.  Carefully Trowel out the end of the Chicory , separate the Leafs and Rinse, pat dry.  on small Salad Plates arrange two Chicory Stalks from Stem to point from the center of the plate to edge.  Sprinkle the Stem ends with a tblsp. of Squeezed Orange Juice.  On top;  a Large Pinch ( small hand-full) of the Tossed Leaf Lettuce.
* Salad dressing ( 16 Hrs before.) kept cool.
Mix all the Ingredients thoroughly together ,  Making sure the Salt and Sugar is dissolved. Keep Cool and do not Mix.  Mix again shortly before Serving.
Serve with 3 Tbsp of Dressing over each Salad and a pinch of Garden Cress.
