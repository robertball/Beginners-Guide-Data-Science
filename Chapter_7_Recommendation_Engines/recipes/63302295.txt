Title:
BREAD PUDDING Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871869.jpg
Ingredients:
4 SLICES OF BREAD
2 1/2 TABLESPOON OF MELTED BUTTER
1 CUP OF MILK
2 EGGS
1 TABLESPOON OF VANILLA EXTRACT
1/2 TEASPOON OF GROUND CINNAMON
3/4 CUP OF WHITE SUGAR
1.5 OZ OF RAISINS (OPTIONAL)
1.5 OZ OF PECANS(OPTIONAL)

Directions:
PRE-HEAT OVEN 350o
1.BREAK BREAD INTO SMALL PIECES INTO 9" PAN,TAKE MELTED BUTTER & POUR OVER BREAD,TAKE A FORK & KINDA STIR IN THE MELTED BUTTER ,NOT TO MUCH,ONCE DONE SPRINKLE RAISINS & PECANS OVER BREAD
2.IN STIR BOWL COMBINE EGGS,MILK,VANILLA EXTRACT,GROUND CINNAMON,& SUGAR,MIX UNTIL WELL BLENDED.
3.TAKE ALL COMBINED INGREDIENTS & POUR OVER BREAD.
4.PLACE IN OVEN FOR 30 MINUTES,WHEN DONE LET IT SET FOR 10 MIN & AFTER SET FOR 10 MIN EAT UP.
