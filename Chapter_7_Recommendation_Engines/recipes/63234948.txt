Title:
Summery Corn Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/842490.jpg
Ingredients:
3 ears corn on the cob
1 Tbsp butter
1 tsp garlic powder
1 can black beans
1/4 C fresh cilantro, chopped
4 Tbsp olive oil
2 Tbsp red wine vinegar
2 Tbsp lime juice
1 1/2 tsp minced garlic
1 tsp white sugar
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Remove husks and silk from corn.  Butter corn and season with garlic powder.  Wrap corn in foil.
Cook corn over grill for 15 minutes.  Remove foil and grill for additional 5 minutes.
Whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, lime juice, minced garlic, white sugar, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
Cut corn from cobs.  Combine corn, black beans and cilantro in medium bowl.  Add dressing mixture and toss together.

Submitter: 
SunnyDaysNora
Description: 
A light and refreshing salad perfect for a warm summer evening.  I like to serve this as a side with Mexican meals.
