Title:
Roast in a Crockpot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can Franco-American beef gravy
1/2 C. brown sugar
1 pkg dry onion soup mix
2 T. vinegar
1 T. soy sauce
1 T. Worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
3-4 lb. roast

Directions:
Mix all ingredients. Put roast in crock pot.  Cook on high for 6-7 hours or low 8-10 hours.  If gravy is desired, remove meat. Bring juices to a boil. Add 2-3 T. flour and cook until thickened.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Sue Farnsworth Sattel
Description: 
My teens aren't crazy about roasts, but this is one that they love!  It does have a touch of sweetness to the gravy, so it might not be for you if you are a lover of spicy foods.
