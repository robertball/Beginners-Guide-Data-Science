Title:
Brisk Broccoli Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup broccoli florets
1 clove garlic
1/2 cup chicken stock
1/3 cup cream (milk, or nonfat yogurt)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Add broccoli, garlic, and chicken stock to Tall Cup.
Twist on the Shaker/Steamer Top and microwave for 2-3 minutes, until broccoli is soft.
Open the cup, add cream, and twist on Cross Blade.
Blend until you have a smooth soup-like consistency.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
Use vegetable broth instead of chicken broth to create a vegetarian version.
