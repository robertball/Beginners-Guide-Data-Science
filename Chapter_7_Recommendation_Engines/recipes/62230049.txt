Title:
Peanut butter kisses cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup  peanut butter
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 1/4 cup flour
t tsp baking soda
3/4 tsp baking soda
14 tsp salt

Directions:
preheat oven to 375. Mix together first five ingredients then add remaining flour and mix until you form a sticky dough. Roll into small balls and place onto a greased cookie sheet. Bake 8-10 minutes or until just golden brown. Place unwrapped hershey kiss in center of each cookie while still hot. should make 48 cookies.
