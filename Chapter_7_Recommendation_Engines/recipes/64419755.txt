Title:
grilled corn on the cob wrapped in bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cobs of corn
12 slices of bacon
1 pinch salt and pepper
12 - sturdy tooth picks
drip pan

Directions:
preheat grill to medium - place drip pan under barbeque grates - roil grill to prevent sticking
season the corn with salt and pepper - wrap the bacon around the corn securing with toothpick
place wrapped corn on bbq over drip pan
turn corn, while cooking for a few minutes,close lid and cook for another 2-3 minutes - move the corn to create even browning and crisping of bacon
do this for about 8 minutes until bacon is crisp and corn is cooked
