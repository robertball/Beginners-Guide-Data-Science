Title:
The Easiest Way To Cook Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4548877.jpg
Ingredients:
Bacon

Directions:
Preheat your oven to 400F.
Place a cookie rack over a baking sheet.
Lay the bacon strips on the rack, and put the rack and sheet in the oven.
Cook for 10-20 minutes, depending on how crispy you like your bacon.
Devour yourself, or hand over to delighted guests/family/nosy neighbors.
