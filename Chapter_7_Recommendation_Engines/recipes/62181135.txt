Title:
Apple Cider Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups Apple cider
3/4 Cup sugar
4 cinnamon sticks
1 Tbsp fresh orange zest
12 whole cloves
2 1/2 Cups fresh cranberries

Directions:
In a saucepan, bring first five ingredients to a boil.  Stir and simmer 10 - 15 minutes and be sure sugar is dissolved.  Add cranberries and increase heat to bring to a boil.  Cook on high, stirring constantly until cranberries begin to pop.  Reduce heat and cook on low until sauce begins to thicken.  Remove cinnamon sticks and pour up in your prettiest relish dish to cool and serve.

Submitter: 
Jay Lynne Stuart
Description: 
This is my absolute favorite cranberry sauce.  The apple cider and Cups Apple cider, hint of orange combined with the warm spices just makes it special.
