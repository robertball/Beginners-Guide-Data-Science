Title:
Vegan Tofu Mayonnaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) package tofu, drained and cut into cubes
2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Place the tofu, cider vinegar, mustard, salt and pepper into the container of a blender. Blend until smooth. Use promptly.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
This tofu mayonnaise is great for anyone who is lactose-intolerant or oil free vegan. It's real thick and creamy and you can add seasonings to fit your fancy!  Great for pasta salads or anywhere you'd use mayonnaise.Modified from: Tofu Mayonnaise
