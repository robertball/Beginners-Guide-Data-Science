Title:
Butch's Sunset Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6 oz. carton banana cream lowfat yogart
4-6 frozen strawberries
1 frozen banana
1/2 cup peach nectar
a handful of ice
1 packet of sweet and low

Directions:
Add all the ingredients into a blender or smoothie maker.
Blend at low for a few minutes.
Blend on high till it is all blended and smooth looking.
Sit back and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Dayton Skelly
Description: 
A very refreshing smoothie that is great anytime.  You get the blends of different fruits without a ton of calories.
