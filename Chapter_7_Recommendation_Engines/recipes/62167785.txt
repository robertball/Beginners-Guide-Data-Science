Title:
Pulled Pork Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-4 cups pulled pork, cooked and fine chopped
2 eggs
1/2 cup of milk
1 tsp dry mustard
1/4 to 1/2 cup chopped bell pepper
1 or 2 chopped green onions (or 1/4 c chopped regular onion)
1 cup bread crumbs
1 tsp. ground pepper
1/2 cup of your favorite barbecue sauce (for the top)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Spray a loaf pan with cooking spray.
Wisk the eggs and milk together.
Mix all the ingredients together (except the barbecue sauce).  Fold into the pan and press down to make it compact.
Spread the barbecue sauce over the top.
Bake for one hour.  Let cool 5-10 minutes.  Cut into slices and serve.  MMMmmmm...

Submitter: 
doxysmom
Description: 
After you and the family have enjoyed all the pulled pork you can stand, use the last few cups in a pulled pork meatloaf.  It's fantastic!
