Title:
Stirrings Red Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993015.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (32 ounce) bottle Stirrings Red Sangria Cocktail Mixer
1 (750 milliliter) bottle Cabernet, Zinfandel or Chianti
3 sliced fresh cut fruits (oranges, strawberries and lemons)

Directions:
Simply combine entire bottle of Stirrings Red Sangria Cocktail Mixer with 1 bottle of red wine.
Garnish with slices of fresh cut fruits like oranges, strawberries and lemons.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
A bowl of fruity sangria on a warm summer evening is a great way to say 'Now it's a party!' This version of the Spanish classic is quick, easy and delicious.
