Title:
Chicken and Cheese Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T Margarine
2 T Flour
1 Cup Milk
2 Cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 Cup Cubed Chicken
1/4 Cup Chopped Onion
1 Can Pillsbury Rolls

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 Degrees.
In small sauce pan melt 2 T margarine, stir in 2 T Flour. Gradually stir in one cup of milk. Cook until smooth.
Add 1/12 cups shredded cheddar cheese.  Reserve 2/3 sauce for topping.
To remaining sauce add 1 cup cubed chicken, 1/4 cup chopped onion.
Separate 1 can Pillsbury Refrigerated Biscuits into 10 biscuits, press each into 4" circles.  Place 2T chicken mixture down center of each biscuit.  Roll up, place seam side down in greased 9" square pan.
Bake 20 minutes or until golden brown.  Soon on reserved sauce and 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese.
Bake 2-3 minutes or until cheese is melted.
