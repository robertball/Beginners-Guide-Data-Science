Title:
Kids' Party Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3840088.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups candy-coated chocolate pieces
3 cups thin pretzel sticks, broken in half
3 cups bite-size Cheddar cheese crackers
1 1/2 cups raisins

Directions:
Mix chocolate candies, pretzels, crackers, and raisins together and store the mixture in a tightly covered container.  Serve as a snack.

Submitter: 
Valerie Cain Cuff
Description: 
This simple snack mix creates an opportunity to let the 'wee ones' be in charge of the cooking!
