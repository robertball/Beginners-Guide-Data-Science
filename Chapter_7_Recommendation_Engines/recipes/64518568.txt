Title:
Big Ray's Simple Apple Dump Cake Recipe

Ingredients:
4 cups chopped Granny Smith apples (3 medium)
1 yellow cake mix
3/4 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Heat oven to 350Â°F. Spray bottom of 13x9-inch pan with cooking spray.
Place chopped apples in pan. Top with dry cake mix, and pour melted butter over top, making sure to cover top with butter as much as you can.
Bake 45 to 50 minutes or until bubbly. Serve warm. Enjoy!
