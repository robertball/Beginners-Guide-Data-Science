Title:
Hot Green Water Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2342177.jpg
Ingredients:
2 long sprigs of fresh mint, washed
6 cucumber wheels, 1/4 inch each
1 large hot green pepper, washed and seeded
1 litre fresh water

Directions:
Add all ingredients to fresh water and refrigerate.
