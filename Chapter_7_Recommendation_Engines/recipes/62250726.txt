Title:
Best Ever Slow Cooker Italian Beef Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (3-pound) beef chuck roast
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 (10 1/4 oz) can beef broth
1 package Good Season Italian salad dressing mix
1 envelope dry au jus mix
1 onion, quartered

Directions:
Place meat and onions in a slow cooker.
Pour in can of beef broth.
Sprinkle salad dressing mix, dry au jus mix, salt and pepper over beef.
Cover and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours - the longer the better!
Remove from slow cooker and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Teeann
Description: 
Best tender and juicy pot roast you've ever eaten! much better than those recipes with the dried onion soup mix!
