Title:
Chicken Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 C chopped onion
1 clove garlic, minced
1 T EVOO or canola oil
1 1/2 # boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 3/4-in cubes
1 can (14 0z)chicken broth
1 1/2 C chopped carrots
3 celery ribs, chopped
1/2 t dried savory
3 T butter or margarine
3 T all-purpose flour
3/4 t salt
1/8 t pepper
1 1/2 C 2% milk
1 1/4 C shredded reduced-fat cheddar cheese
8 oz wide egg noodles, cooked and drained

Directions:
In large nonstick skillet, saute onion and garlic in oil until tender. Add chicken; cook and stir until no longer pink. Add the broth, carrots, celery and savory. Bring to boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 10-15 min. or until vegetables are tender.
Meanwhile, in saucepan, melt butter. Stir in flour, salt and pepper until smooth. Gradually add milk. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 min or until thickened. Remove from heat; stir in cheese until melted. Pour over chicken mixture. Add noodles; mix well.
Transfer to 3-qt baking dish coated with nonstick cooking spray. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 15-20 min. or until bubbly.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Made with low fat milk and low fat cheddar cheese, will also try whole wheat noodles for healthy version of this classic dish.
