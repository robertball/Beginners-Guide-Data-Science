Title:
Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 1/3 cups sugar
1/2 cup coconut
1/3 cup chopped pecans
2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
2 tsp. cinnamon
3 tbsp. canola oil
2 large eggs
2 cups grated carrot
1 1/2 cups crushed pineapple, drained
Frosting:
2 tbsp. butter - softened
1-8oz. pkg. low fat cream cheese - softened
3 cups powdered sugar
2 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine flour, sugar, coconut, pecans, baking soda, salt and cinnamon in a large bowl.
Combine oil and eggs, mix well.
Stir egg mixture, carrot and pineapple into flour mixture.
Spoon into greased 13x9 baking pan.
Bake for 35 min. or until toothpick comes out clean.
Cool completely before frosting.
For frosting, combine butter and cream cheese in a large bowl until smooth.
Mix in powdered sugar and vanilla until just smooth.
Spread over cooled cake.
