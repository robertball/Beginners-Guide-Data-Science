Title:
House Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces sour cream
8 ounces mayonnaise
1 Tbsp White Wine Vinegar
1/2 large carrot, shredded finely
1/3 cucumber seeded, peeled, and shredded finely
1/2 tsp each onion salt, garlic salt, salt and pepper
1/3 c White wine

Directions:
Mix everything but the white wine. Whisk in the wine until the dressing just thinly coats the back of a spoon.

Submitter: 
ChefMom
Description: 
This creamy elegant dressing has been a family recipe for years- and it's just not a holiday without it!
