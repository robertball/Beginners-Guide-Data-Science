Title:
Warm Plum Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large plums, pitted and cut into 1/4-inch dice
1 large clove garlic, very finely minced
2 tablespoons sugar
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds

Directions:
Cook all except sesame seeds until broken down, remove from pan, sprinkle with seeds.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
Serve with pork or whatever you like maybe browned tofu
