Title:
Lemon Tahini Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6122971.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup lemon juice
1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil
1/3 cup tahini
2 Tbsp honey
2 cloves minced garlic
1 tsp sea salt
1 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and refrigerate.
