Title:
Italian Chicken with Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Whole Fryer Chicken
1 Whole Jar of your favorite Spaghetti Sauce
3 cloves of Garlic
1 Pkg of your favorite Pasta

Directions:
Place whole or cut up chicken in large crockpot
Chop garlic and throw on top
Pour whole Jar of Spaghetti Sauce on top
Cover and cook on high for 4-5 hours or on low for 6-8 hours
15 minutes before serving, cook pasta according to directions.

Submitter: 
Ron Turner
Description: 
Another quick and easy whole meal you can start before work and throw together quickly when you get home.
I like to serve it with a green garden salad and garlic bread.
Not sure where I got this recipe but have been using it for 20 yrs at least...
