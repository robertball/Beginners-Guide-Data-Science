Title:
Vegan No-Tofu Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups pumpkin puree (about 1-1/2 cans)
1 cup dairy free milk
3/4 cup sugar (can substitute with 1 cup dates + 1/4 cup sugar blended up, but this will effect taste, colour & texture)
1/2 cup corn starch
1 tbsp pumpkin spice
(2tsp cinnamon, 1/2tsp ginger, 1/4tsp nutmeg & 1/4 tsp cloves)

Directions:
Heat oven to 350. Blend in processor. Pour into crust. Bake 35-45 min. Refrigerate 2 hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
Vegan low fat pie with no tofu in sight.
