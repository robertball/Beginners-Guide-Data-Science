Title:
Red, White, and Blue Fruit Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/916773.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 large banana, cut into pieces and frozen
2 large fresh strawberries, rinsed and sliced
1/4 cup blueberries
1/2 cup milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 tablespoons vanilla yogurt
2 ice cubes

Directions:
Place the banana pieces, strawberries, blueberries, milk, vanilla extract, yogurt, and ice cubes in a blender.  Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Wirefox Terrier Mom
Description: 
This delicious smoothie is sweetened with natural sugars. Replace any of the fruit ingredients with your favorites if you like, but make sure one of them is frozen to make it nice and thick!
