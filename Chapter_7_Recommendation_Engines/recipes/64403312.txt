Title:
Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Frozen garlic bread slices
OR
Garlic bread from the bakery section of the store
OR
1 loaf of Italian or French bread from the bakery section
2 sticks of butter
1 head of garlic

Directions:
Heat purchased garlic bread according to package directions.
If you are making your own, preheat oven to 350
Allow butter to soften and come to room temperature.
Mince 1 head (the whole garlic "pod" not just one garlic clove).
Thoroughly mix softened butter and minced garlic.
Slice bread length wise and spread garlic butter on both sides.
Bake at 350 until butter is melted and bread is crisp, approximately 15 minutes.
Slice into small pieces.

Submitter:

Description: 
Garlic bread in a few ways. Please note: prep time does not include the time it will take to soften the butter.
