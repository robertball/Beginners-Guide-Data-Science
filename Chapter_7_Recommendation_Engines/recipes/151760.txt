Title:
Quick & Easy Layered Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980858.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cold milk
1 pkg. (4 serving size)  JELL-O Chocolate Flavor Instant Pudding & Pie Filling
1 (10.5 ounce) package pound cake, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1/2 cup chocolate syrup
1 (8 ounce) tub COOL WHIP Whipped Topping, thawed, divided

Directions:
Pour milk into bowl. Add dry pudding mix. Beat with wire whisk 2 minutes or until well blended.
Arrange half of the cake cubes in 3-quart serving bowl. Drizzle with 1/4 cup of the chocolate syrup. Layer with 1 cup of the pudding and 1-1/2 cups of the whipped topping. Repeat layers ending with whipped topping.
Refrigerate until ready to serve. Store leftover dessert in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
JELL-O
Description: 
This chocolate pudding and pound cake dessert is perfect for those last minute parties or gatherings.
