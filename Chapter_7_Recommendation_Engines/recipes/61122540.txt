Title:
Robin's Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups Rolled Oats
1 1/2 cups Wheat Germ
1 cup Oat Bran
1/2 cup Flax Seeds
1 cup Sunflower Seeds
1 cup Almonds- Whole
1 cup Pecans- Chopped
1 cup Walnuts- Chopped
1/4 cup Molasses
1/4 cup Real Maple Syrup
3/4 cup Honey
1 cup Applesauce
1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
pinch of salt
2 cups Raisins, Cranberries or Mixed Dried Fruit

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 325. Line two large baking sheets with parchment paper.
Combine all dry ingredients in a large bowl.  Combine the wet ingredients in a saucepan. Bring to a boil over medium heat, then pour over the dry ingredients, and stir to coat. Spread the mixture out evenly on the baking sheets.
Bake about 20 minutes or until crispy and toasted, stirring once or twice throughout.  Cool, then add dried fruit. Store in an airtight container.
