Title:
Garlic Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz package of softened cream cheese
1 to 2 cloves of garlic-pressed
milk (eyeball)

Directions:
Mix softened cream cheese with milk (add slowly) till you get the consistency of a creamy dip. Something that will stay on a chip!
Press two cloves of pressed garlic into dip (or one-you decide)
Mix together and chill.
Serve with chips

Submitter: 
Dog Fish Head aka Fabius
Description: 
A family friend gave this to my Mother many years ago.  When my parents had parties Mom always made a separate bowl for me.  I would stay in my room and eat this while watching scary movies!  You've got to love garlic to eat this!
