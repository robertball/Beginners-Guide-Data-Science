Title:
Lighter Cream of Mango Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/672153.jpg
Ingredients:
3 mango - peeled, seeded and cubed
3 packets of Splenda
3 eggs
1 lemon, zested and juiced
4 cups fat free half-and-half
Hot Sauce to taste

Directions:
Place the everything into a blender or food processor. Cover, and process until smooth and creamy. Serve chilled with hot sauce as garnish.

Submitter: 
Doug Matthews
Description: 
A lighter change with eggs added in to the mix. We can & do mix blueberries in at times and I always serve with hot sauceModified from: Cream of Mango Soup
