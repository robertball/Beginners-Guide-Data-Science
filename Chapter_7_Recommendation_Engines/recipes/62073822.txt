Title:
Cottage Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
500 gr. thin fyllo pastry for baklava
500 gr. cottage cheese
1 cup white sugar
150 gr. fresh cream
3 tablespoons honey
1 cup walnuts

Directions:
Crmuble the fyllo pastry and place on a baking tray.  Bake for about 5 min until golden and crunchy.  When the fyllo cools down smash into piece.  In the meantime mix the cottage cheese with the sugar and the frsh cream.  Place in a serving pan a layer of the smshed fyllo, pour over some honey and add the cottage cheese mixture.  Finish up with the resty of the fyllo pastry and the honey.  Sprinkle with chopped walnuts and put in the fridge.
