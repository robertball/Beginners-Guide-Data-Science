Title:
Dog and Bone Cookie Cutters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c quick cooking rolled oats
1c boiling water
2tsp beef bouillion granules (optional)
1/3 c melted margarine
1/2 c cornmeal
1 tsp granulated sugar
1 c peanut butter
1/2 c milk
1 whole egg, lightly beaten
3 c (or a little more) whole wheat flour

Directions:
Preheat oven 350 F, place oats in bowl and pour water on top.
stir in bouillion if wanted. mix and let stand 10 minutes. add margarine, cornmeal, sugar, peanut butter, egg, and milk to oat mixture. mix well and gradually add just enough flour to form a stiff dough.
knead dough for several minutes until it is smooth and not sticky, adding flour as necessary.
roll dough to 1/2" thickness and cut into shapes. place cookies 1/2-1" apart on lightly greased cookie sheet
