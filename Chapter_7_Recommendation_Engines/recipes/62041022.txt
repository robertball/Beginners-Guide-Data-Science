Title:
Fruity Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 qts. orange juice
2 qts. pineapple juice
2 liters Sprite/7 Up
2 cups grenadine

Directions:
Combine in punch bowl. Add ice. Top with slices of orange and/or lemon and lime for decoration.
