Title:
Chick Pea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
1/2 large cucumber, peeled and diced
1 container grape tomatoes, halved or quartered
1/4 cup red onion, minced
1 avacado, diced
OR
1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
Dressing:
Juice of 1 lemon
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
1 tsp Dijon mustard
1 tsp honey
1 tbsp fresh dill, minced

Directions:
Add salad ingredients in a large bowl and mix together.
In a separate bowl, combine ingredients for the dressing and mix well. Pour the dressing over the salad mixture and stir.

Submitter: 
Adam Venn
Description: 
This is a delicious, healthy, and nutritious dish that can be served as an appetizer, side dish, or lunch or dinner.
