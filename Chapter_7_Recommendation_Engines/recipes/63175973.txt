Title:
Coconut Banana Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/816029.jpg
Ingredients:
Pancakes:
1 1/2 cups flour
1 tbls sugar
2 3/4 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1 1/4 cup milk
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
2 tbls melted butter
1 very ripe banana
Syrup:
3/4 cup buttermilk
7 tbls butter
1 cup sugar
1 tsp coconut extract or flavoring
1/2 tsp baking soda.

Directions:
Pancakes:
In bowl, whisk; floor, sugar, baking powder and salt.
In seprate bowl, mix together; milk, egg and vanilla. Add to dry ingredents, mix well.
Creme the banana and mix into to the batter.
Melt butter, add to batter.
Let sit in fridge for ten minutes.
Cook and serve.
Syrup:
Melt till sugar and butter is desolved, then boil for one min.
After boiling for one minute. Reduce heat to simmer and add coconut flavoring/extract and baking soda till disolved.

Description: 
Easy and Yummy! Banana Pancakes from scratch, with the perfect coconut syrup. My family absolutly loves them! I usually have to triple the recipe!
