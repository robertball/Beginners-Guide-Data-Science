Title:
Cashew Brittle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. roasted, salted cashews
1 C. white sugar
1/2 C. light corn syrup
1 t. butter
1 t. vanilla
1 t. baking soda

Directions:
In a 2 quart microwave safe bowl(with a handle if possible) stir together nuts, sugar and syrup.  Microwave on high, uncovered, for 2 minutes.
Stir, cook 2 more minutes. Mixture should be light brown.
Using mitts, carefully remove mixture from microwave.  It will be VERY hot.
Stir in butter and vanilla.
Microwave on high about 3 minutes until carmel colored.
Using mitts, remove from ovenand stir in soda.  Mixture will foam slightly.
Quickly pour onto foil-covered cookie sheet using the back of a spoon spread it over the pan.
Let stand until cool and break into pieces
