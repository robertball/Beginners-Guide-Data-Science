Title:
POG Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6476824.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups passion fruit juice (such as Welch's®)
6 cups guava nectar (such as Goya®)
3 cups fresh squeezed orange juice

Directions:
Combine passion fruit juice, guava nectar, and orange juice in a large glass pitcher and stir well. Chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
RainbowJewels
Description: 
If you've eaten breakfast at Walt Disney® World or visited Hawaii, then you've probably had P.O.G juice. It's an amazingly refreshing blend of passion fruit juice, orange juice, and guava nectar. Enjoy!
