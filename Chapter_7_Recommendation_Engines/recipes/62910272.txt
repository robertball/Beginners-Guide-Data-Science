Title:
Mushroom Thyme Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Oil, butter, or fat to cover the bottom of your pan
1 sweet yellow onion, diced
2-3 garlic cloves, sliced
1 lb sliced mushrooms
Salt and pepper
1 tsp country mustard
1 tsp beef bouillon
1/3 cup Tofutti Better Than Cream Chees
3/4 cup hot water
1/3 - 1/2 cup Cream, if desired
3/4 - 1 tsp fresh thyme

Directions:
Preheat heavy bottom skillet over medium high heat.
When the skillet is hot, add onion and garlic.
Lower heat to medium.
Cook for 5-10 minutes.
While you wait, stir occasionally, and mix the mustard, bouillon, and Tofutti in a small bowl.
Add mushrooms.  Sautee 5-10 more minutes to taste, lowering the heat at 5 minutes to low.  Vegetables should be done to your liking before proceeding.
Pour in water.
Add in mixture, stirring briskly with a fork or whisk.  Allow to simmer 5-10 minutes.
Stir in cream and thyme.  Simmer until ready to serve.
