Title:
Sour Cream Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3087985.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup whole wheat flour or GF flour
1/2 cup GF oat flour
3/4 cup Truvia baking sugar
1/2 tsp sea salt
2 tsp baking powder
1 egg white
1/2 cup light sour cream
1/3 cup milk of choice
1/3 cup unsweetened applesauce
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup fresh blueberries

Directions:
Heat oven 400 degrees
Prepare a muffin tin with nonstick cooking spray or muffin liners
In a small bowl, combine applesauce, egg white, sour cream, applesauce & vanilla
In a larger bowl, sift the flours together with sugar, salt & baking powder
Add wet ingredients slowly to dry ingredients
Combine well
Add blueberries & gently mix into batter
Add batter to muffin cavities filling 3/4 full
Bake 18 minutes or until a toothpick can be inserted clean
Remove from the oven
Cool on a baking rack

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Made with sour cream, applesauce, milk, whole wheat flour & oats, these delicious 110 calorie Sour Cream Blueberry Muffins keep your waist slim.
