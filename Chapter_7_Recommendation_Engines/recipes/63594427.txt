Title:
Great-Grandma Maude's Cranberry Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boxes raspbery jello disolved in 2 cups boilng water
1 quart raw cranberries (ground in food proessor)
1 orange (ground in food processor) or 1/2 cup OJ
2 cups sugar
1 large can of crushed pineapple
1 cup of chopped celery
1/2 heaping cup chopped pecans

Directions:
disolve the jello in the water
grind the cranberries and orange
add all together and put in fridge to chill.

Description: 
This was my great grandma's recipe, it's a family favorite
