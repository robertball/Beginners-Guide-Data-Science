Title:
Cheesy Chicken Fig wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large whole what tortillas
4 wedge garlic herb cream cheese
4 T fig jam
1/2 cup diced onion
9 ounces broccoli slaw
2 cloves minced garlic
8 ounces cooked diced chicken
1 t. lemon juice
1/2 t.  dried thyme
1/4 chopped cilantro or parsley
dash of hot pepper sauce
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Spread the center part of the tortillas with the garlic herb cream cheese, almost to the edge. Top the cream cheese with the fig jam
In a skillet, saute the onion, broccoli slaw and garlic until tender. Add in diced cooked chicken, dried thyme, chopped cilantro or parsley , hot pepper sauce and salt and pepper. Heat til mixture warm
Divide chicken broccli slaw mixtyre between the tortillas. Roll up and enjoy or else wrap in alumnium foil and freeze for later use
