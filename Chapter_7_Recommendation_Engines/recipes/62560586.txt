Title:
Baked Rigatoni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/536122.jpg
Ingredients:
4 c. tomato sauce
1 lb. ground round (use 90% lean)
4 c. cooked rigatoni (about 2-1/2 c. uncooked pasta)
1-1/2 c. (6 oz.) shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese, divided
1/4 c. grated fresh Parmesan cheese
cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ºF.
Cook beef in a large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat until browned; stir to crumble. Drain
well. Combine beef, rigatoni, tomato sauce, and 1 c. mozzarella in an 11x7-inch baking dish
coated with cooking spray. Top with 1/2 c. mozzarella and Parmesan. Bake at 350ºF for 20
minutes ot until thoroughly heated.
