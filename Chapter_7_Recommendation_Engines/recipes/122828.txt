Title:
Ham N Cheese Potato Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979889.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (24 ounce) package frozen O'Brien hash brown potatoes
2 cups cubed fully cooked ham
3/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
1 small onion, chopped
2 cups sour cream
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cheddar cheese soup, undiluted
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of potato soup, undiluted
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine potatoes, ham, 1/2 cup cheese and onion. In another bowl, combine sour cream, soups and pepper; add to potato mixture and mix well.
Transfer to a greased 3-qt. baking dish. Sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 60-65 minutes or until bubbly and potatoes are tender. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Barbara  Larson
Description: 
'This rich ham and hash brown casserole is frequently requested at family gatherings,' relates Barbara Larson of Rosemount, Minnesota. 'I include it in my brunch buffets and sometimes I serve it as a meal in itself along with juice and toast.' It offers fix-and-forget-it ease.
