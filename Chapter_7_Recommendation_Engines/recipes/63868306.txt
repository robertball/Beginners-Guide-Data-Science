Title:
Layered Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Re-fried Beans (fat free or vegetarian)(90c)
1/2 packet taco seasoning (3c)
1/2 avocado (100c)
1/4 packet guacamole mix (optional)(3c)
1/4 cup fat free shredded cheddar cheese, about 2 oz. (90c)
1/8 cup sliced black olives (50c)
Tostitos baked tortilla chips, about 14 (120c)
Salsa, optional

Directions:
Mix re-fried beans with taco seasoning.  Spread onto plate.  Mix guacamole and spread on top of bean mixture.  Top with fat free shredded cheese, black olives, and salsa.  Serve with fat free chips.
