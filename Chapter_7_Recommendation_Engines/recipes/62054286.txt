Title:
Super Easy Pasta Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons olive oil, 2 tablespoons minced onion, 2 teaspoons minced garlic, 1/2 cup red cooking wine, 1 tablespoon sugar, 2 teaspoons basil, 2 tablespoons tomato paste, 1/2 teaspoon italian seasoning, 1-28 oz can crushed tomatoes, undrained.

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.  Add onion and garlic and saute for a minute.  Stir in wine and then add rest of ingrediants, bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
