Title:
Root Beer Float Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1245097.jpg
Ingredients:
3 or 4 ice cubes
2oz coconut rum
2oz sweetened (full fat) creamer
6oz root beer

Directions:
Pour ice in a glass. Add coconut rum and creamer to ice and combine.  Add root beer and expect a little foam like a float.

Submitter: 
NWMama
Description: 
This grown-up version of a root beer float is fun enough to drink in a fancy frosted glass with a curly straw.
