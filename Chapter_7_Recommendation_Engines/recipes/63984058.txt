Title:
Epicure Rice and Black Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1105381.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups (750 ml) cooked rice
1 cup (250 ml) corn niblets, canned, frozen or fresh
1 — 19 oz (540 ml) can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 cup (250 ml) diced tomatoes
2 Tbsp (30 ml) Epicure's Salsa Mix – your choice
1 Tbsp (15 ml) Epicure's Cinco Pepper Blend
1 cup (250 ml) grated low-fat cheese – your choice

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 325° F (190° C).
2. Lightly spray an oven-proof dish with oil. Place rice, corn and beans in dish.
3. Prepare Salsa by combining tomatoes, Salsa Mix and Cinco Pepper Blend.
4. Stir into rice, corn and beans.
5. Top with cheese and bake for 25 minutes.
