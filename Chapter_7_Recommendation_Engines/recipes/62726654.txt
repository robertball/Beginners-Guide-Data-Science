Title:
Slow Cooker Beef and Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 # stew beef
1 can French onion soup
1 can diced tomatoes (15 oz)
1 can golden mushroom soup, undiluted
1/2 C sliced celery
1/2 C chopped onion
4-6 oz sliced fresh mushrooms

Directions:
Combine all and cook on LOW for 8-10 hours. Serve over hot, buttered noodles.
