Title:
Blueberry Cream Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounce package cream cheese, softened
1 tsp vanilla
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 cup powdered sugar
Â½ pint whipping cream, whipped
1 can blueberry pie filling
3 bananas
1 (9-inch) pie shell, baked

Directions:
Blend cream cheese, vanilla, lemon juice and sugar.
Fold in whipped cream.
Line pie shell with sliced bananas.
Cover with cream cheese mixture.
Top with blueberry pie filling.
Refrigerate several hours or overnight before serving.
