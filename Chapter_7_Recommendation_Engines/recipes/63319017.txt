Title:
Copycat KFC Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups chopped cabbage
3/4 cup shredded carrots
1/3 cup granulated sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 1/2 tbls. white vinegar
2 1/2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

Directions:
Cut the cabbage and carrot.  In a large bowl, combine remaining ingredients and whisk until smooth.  Add cabbage and carrots; mix well, cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
