Title:
Fettuccine Al Fresco Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988843.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/2 cup cubed fully cooked ham (optional)
1 medium onion, chopped
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
1 (24 ounce) jar PregoÂ® Fresh Mushroom Italian Sauce
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
12 ounces fettuccine or spaghetti, cooked
Grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Heat the oil in a 3-quart saucepan over medium heat. Add the ham, if desired, onion and garlic powder and cook until the onion is tender.
Stir the sauce and parsley in the saucepan and heat through. Serve over the fettuccine. Sprinkle with the cheese.
