Title:
Lentil and Ham Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup dried lentils
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped carrots
1 cup chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 cups diced cooked ham
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1 bay leaf
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
32 ounces chicken broth
1 cup water
14.5 ounce diced tomato - not drained

Directions:
In a large stock pot, combine all ingredients.  Bring to boil, cover and cook on low until lentils and vegetables are done.  About 30-45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Amanda L.
Description: 
This soup is healthy and delicious.  It's great on a cold winters night.Modified from: Slow Cooker Lentil and Ham Soup
