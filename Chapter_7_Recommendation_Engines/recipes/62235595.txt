Title:
Mom's Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:  1 1/4 cup graham craker crumbs
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 cup butter melted
Filling:  1 cup sugar
2 (8oz) pkgs softened cream cheese
3 large eggs
2tsp vanilla extract
2 tsp almond extract
1 1/2 pints sour cream

Directions:
Mix crust ingredients together and press into the bottom of a spring form pan.  Set aside.  For the filling cream together sugar and cream cheese until well smooth.  Add eggs, vanilla and almond.  Mix well.  Fold in sour cream.  Pour mixture into crust and bake at 375 degrees for 55 mins.  Do not open oven door.  Turn off oven.  Crack oven door and let sit for 1 hour and then cool 1 more hour on counter.  Refrigerate overnight.  You can serve with any topping.  (i.e.  cherry pie filling)
