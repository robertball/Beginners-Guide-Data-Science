Title:
Sweet Biscuits for Chocolate Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. flour
1/4 c. flour (for patting)
1 t. salt
1 T. baking powder
3/4 c. powdered sugar
1/4 lb. ice cold unsalted butter
3/4 half-and-half

Directions:
Set oven to 400 F. Place rack in lowest position.  Sift together dry ingredients.  Cut in butter.  Add liquid last.  Spread patting flour on table to knead 8-9 times.  Than pat into 1-inch thick flat dough. Use sharp cookie cutters for circles or other shapes.  Place on (insulated or stone) cookie sheet.  Bake 12-15 min.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Lebow
Description: 
Sweet biscuits can be used for strawberry shortcake also.
