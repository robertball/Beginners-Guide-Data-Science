Title:
Quinoa with Sundried Tomatoes (Clean Eating) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tsp olive oil
8 sundried tomatoes (not packed in oil), rehydrated, chopped
2 minced shallots
1 clove garlic, chopped
2 cups chicken or veggie broth
1 cup dry quinoa
pinch cayenne pepper
2 Tbsp chopped fresh cilantro
1 tsp sea salt (optional)
Fresh cracked black pepper

Directions:
Rinse quinoa
Heat oil in large saucepan
Add tomatoes, garlic & shallots, saute for 3-5mins (until soft)
Add stock, bring to boil
Stir in quinoa & cayenne, return to boil, reduce heat and simmer covered for 20-25mins
Let stand for 5 mins, fluff with fork and stir in seasonings
