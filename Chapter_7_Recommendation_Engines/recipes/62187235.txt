Title:
Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 clove minced garlic
1 Tbs oil
3 medium zuchini, sliced
4 cups chicken broth
16 oz can diced tomatoes
15 oz can tomato sauce
11 oz can corn, undrained
1 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp pepper
cubed cooked rotisserie chicken
tortilla chips
shredded cheddar

Directions:
Saute garlic & onion in oil until onion is translucent.  Add zuchinin through pepper and simmer for 25 minutes.  Add cubed chicken and simmer additional 5 minutes.  Top each serving with tortilla chips and cheese.
