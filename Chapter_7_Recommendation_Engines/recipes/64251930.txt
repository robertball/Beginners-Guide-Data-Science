Title:
Best Ever Meatloaf I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1 cup tomato juice
3/4 cup rolled oats
1 egg
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/2 teaspoon mrs. dash
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 cup diced frozen green pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine meat, tomato juice, oats, egg, chopped onion, salt and pepper. Mix lightly but thoroughly.  Press into 8x4 inch loaf pan.
Bake for 1 hour, or until meat is no longer pink and juices run clear.  Drain.  Let stand 5 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Jody Comerford
Description: 
Rolled oats, tomato juice, and onions combine with lean ground beef for an easily prepared meatloaf. It bakes in one hour.Modified from: Best Ever Meatloaf I
