Title:
Instant pot Creamy cabbage sausage soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5995655.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb sage sausage
2 Tablespoons butter
1 medium onion, chopped
1 cup water
1 tsp chicken better than bouillon paste
1 russet potatoes diced
2 cups cabbage chopped
Garlic salt to taste
pepper to taste
Pinch of allspice, optional
2 Tbsp all-purpose flour
1/2 cup milk, divided
1/2 cup half and half, divided
Fresh dill, for garnish

Directions:
Set instant pot to high sauté mode. Add butter stir until melted. Add chopped onions. Stir frequently until browned. Remove onions.
Add sausage breaking into smaller crumbles. Cook until browned through. Add the water and chicken bouillon paste. Add the chopped cabbage and onions. Peel the potato and cube. Add to the mixture.
Switch to high pressure and cook for 8 minutes.
Quick release pressure. Switch back to low sauté mode.
Measure the milk and half and half - pour all but 3 Tbsp of milk mixture into pot. Whisk the flour into remaining milk. Add flour mixture to the pot.
Adjust seasonings to taste. Serve with fresh dill for garnish
