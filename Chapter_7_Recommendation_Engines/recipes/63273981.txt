Title:
Cherry Oatmeal Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (21 oz) cherry pie filling
3/4 c. packed brown sugar
3/4 c. flour
3/4 c. old-fashioned oats
1/2 t. ground cinnamon
1/4 t. ground nutmeg
1/4 t. salt
1/2 c. butter, cold

Directions:
Spread pie filling into a greased 9x9 pan.
In a bowl, combine remaining ingredients, cutting the cold butter in until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs; sprinkle over filling.
Bake at 375 degrees for 35-40 minutes, or until golden brown.
