Title:
White Bean and Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1034800.jpg
Ingredients:
2	tablespoon olive oil or vegetable oil
2	teaspoon red wine vinegar
1/8	teaspoon salt
¼	teaspoon coarse ground black pepper
1	medium tomato, chopped
¼	cup sliced green onions
1	(15.5 oz.) Cannellini beans, rinsed and drained

Directions:
In medium bowl, combine oil, vinegar, salt and pepper; blend well.  Add tomato, onions, and beans; stir gently until well mixed.  let set for as long as possible, at least 30 minutes as long as 24, stirring occasionally to marinate the beans.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
Great inexpensive dish that is low carb, low fat, and great for outdoor functions.  as nothing in it will spoil in the heat.
