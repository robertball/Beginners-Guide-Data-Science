Title:
Ham and Swiss Stromboli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tube refrigerated French loaf or pizza dough
6 oz. deli ham
6 green onions
8 bacon strips
6 oz. swiss cheese

Directions:
Cook bacon and set aside to drain fat.
Slice up onions thinly.
On a greased pan, spread out dough.
Sprinkle on the ham, onions, crushed bacon, and cheese.
Fold over the dough and seal the edges.
Bake in the oven, following directions on the package.
