Title:
CHICKEN SAUSAGE TORTELLINI STIR-FRY Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/831230.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz. pkg. al fresco® All Natural
Teriyaki-Ginger Meatballs
½ C. pineapple chunks, well-drained
1 Tbsp. sesame oil
1 Tbsp. hoisin sauce
2 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
1 C. broccoli florets, coarsely chopped
1 C. cauliflower florets, coarsely chopped
2 medium carrots, peeled & cut in ¼” slices
½ C. celery, cut diagonally in ¼” slices
½ green bell pepper, coarsely chopped
½ red bell pepper, coarsely chopped
4 scallions, cut diagonally in 1/2” slices
½ small zucchini, peeled, quartered & cut in ½” slices
¼ lb. cheese tortellini
1/8 tsp. sea salt
1/8 tsp. fresh ground black pepper
Fresh chopped parsley, garnish
Freshly grated Parmesan, optional

Directions:
Put sesame oil in large skillet (or wok), over medium high heat; add meatballs, pineapple chunks and hoisin sauce.  Cook until heated through.  Remove to a bowl and set aside.
In same skillet, over medium high heat, add the extra virgin olive oil and heat until it ripples.  Add vegetables and stir fry over medium heat until tender crisp.  Add tortellini and stir well, heating them through.  Add meatballs and pineapple into skillet and heat thoroughly.
Spoon into serving bowl and garnish with chopped parsley and freshly grated Parmesan cheese.  Enjoy!
Makes 4 servings.

Submitter: 
Waterless Cooker
Description: 
Trying to figure what to have for dinner today that would be healthy and use some on-hand ingredients...this is what transpired...it hits all the marks...healthy, inexpensive and delicious! Hubby liked flavors, but wasn't happy with the tender crispness of the veggies...:)
