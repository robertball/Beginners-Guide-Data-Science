Title:
Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. pasta
1 small jar green olives
1 red pepper
1 green pepper
16 oz. kidney beans
1 c. shredded mozzarella  cheese
1/4 c. Zesty Italian dressing

Directions:
Cook pasta and cool. Chop up peppers. Combine all ingredients and refrigerate.
