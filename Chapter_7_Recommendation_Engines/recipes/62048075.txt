Title:
Spiced Pumpkin Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups canned, unsweetened pumpkin
3 cups sugar
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1/2 teaspoon each ground nutmeg and allspice
1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
1 cup water

Directions:
Mix pumpkin and sugar in large kettle.  Over medium heat stir until sugar is dissolves.  Add spices, lemon juice and water.  Cook, stirring frequently until mixture boils.  Reduce heat to simmer.  Cook about 1-1/2 hours, stirring frequently to prevent pumpkin mixture from burning.  (Pumpkin burns easily, so watch out)  Cook until mixture thickens to apple butter consistency.  Ladle hot butter into 6 half pint hot and sterilized jars and seal with sterilized rings and lids.  Process 10 minutes in a hot water bath.  Remove from water and checkseals before storing. Store in a cool dry place.

Submitter: 
Kathy W.
Description: 
Pumpkin butter can be used like jam or as a topping for waffles or pancakes.  This recipe is best if it ages for a few weeks.  The house smells wonderful while you cook this butter!
