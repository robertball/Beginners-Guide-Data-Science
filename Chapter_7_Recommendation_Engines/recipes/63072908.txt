Title:
Honey Of An Oatmeal Bread - Stand Mixer version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup water
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/4 cup honey
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup rolled oats
2 1/3 cups bread flour
1 teaspoon active dry yeast

Directions:
Proof one packet of regular yeast with the water and honey for about 8 minutes.
Add the remaining ingredients all at once and knead in stand mixer with the dough hook for another 8-10 minutes, on a medium/low setting.
Oil the bowl and the dough, cover it, and let it rise for an hour in a warm oven.
Punch it down, plop in the loaf pan and rise another 30 minutes.
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
FANTASTIC bread.  Makes a wicked grilled cheese.
