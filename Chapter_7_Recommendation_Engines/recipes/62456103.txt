Title:
meat sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 ibs ground hamberger
2 tomatoes
1 onion
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons pepper
2 cans of chili beans
1 can stewed tomatoes
1-1 1/2 cups water
1 package of chili seasoning
1 package of taco seasoning
1 can of refried beans
1 package of salad
1 bag taco shells
1 green pepper,
1 packof italian seasoning
1 red pepper

Directions:
brown beef, add diced tomatoes, onions, salt and pepper.
to make chili
remove 1 1/4 pounds of beef mix, add to chili  and seasoning. add water, 1 can stewed tomatoes. heat for 1 hour
to make tacos
take 1 1/4 ibs of beef mix, add seasoning, and refried beans
meat loaf/
take 1 1/4 meat mix, form into pan
cook for 10 mins put ontop of mashed or baked potatoes
italian sandwich
take 1 1/4 meat mix, dice up green and red peppers, and season mix. and 1/2 cup water. put on sandwiches or pour over pasta

Submitter: 
Erica Leigh Bentz
Description: 
great Beef meat sauce to be used for chili, tacos, meat loaf, or italian beef
