Title:
Moms Baked Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Bacon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 f.On a baking sheet lined with foil(for easy clean up)Separate bacon into slices and lay out on pan in one layer.
DO NOT GREASE THE FOIL.THE BACON WILL MAKE PLENTY OF ITS OWN GREASE.You can pour the grease in to a dish (refrigerated) and let cool then fry eggs in it (very good!!)
Bake for 45-1hr.It will smell Really Good.when the bacon is done cooking pour out the grease.Flipping the bacon in the pan will not be necessary.

Submitter: 
Mary
Description: 
This bacon melts in your mouth!
