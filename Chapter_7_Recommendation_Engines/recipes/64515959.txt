Title:
Cook'n Thruwurkin- Dutch Oven Baklava Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 sheets phyllo dough
1 cup butter
1 1/2 cups finely chopped walnuts
2 TB sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
lemon honey syrup:
1 1/2 cups sugar
3/4 cups water
1/2 tsp lemon juice
1 TB honey

Directions:
combine syrup ingredients and bring to a boil for 1 minute, then chill
melt butter and mix walnuts, sugar, cinnamon. set aside
butter the bottom of a 12" Dutch Oven generously
lay 1 sheet of dough in bottom of oven.
using a pastry brush, butter the top of the dough beginning at the edges and working towards the center
repeat this until 8 sheets of dough are in the oven, buttering each layer
sprinkle 1/2 the nut mixture evenly over dough
repeat this process until all the dough has been buttered.
(there should be 3 layers total)
drizzle the remaining butter on top
cut excess dough from the edges. cut into diamond shaped pieces without cutting through the bottom layer of dough.
put the lid on the Dutch Oven
bake at 350 F for 35-40 minutes or lightly golden
remove from heat and pour chilled syrup over the baklava. finish cutting through all layers.
