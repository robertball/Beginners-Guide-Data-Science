Title:
Teriyaki Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1543922.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (1 pound) pork tenderloins
5 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbsp oil, olive
2 cloves garlic (or 1/4 tsp garlic powder)
2 tsp sugar, brown
2 tsp ginger, grated (or 1 tsp ground ginger)
1 tsp pepper, course ground
1 tsp mustard, ground

Directions:
In a one cup bowl, mix all ingredients except pork.
Use a fork to pierce pork tenderloins in several places, top and bottom. Place pork tenderloin in a freezer bag.
Pour mixture from step 1 into plastic bag, turn to coat. Marinate overnight (one hour minimum)
Remove pork tenderloin from bag and place in an 11 x 7 inch baking pan coated with cooking spray.
Back uncovered at 425 degrees for 25 to 35 minutes. Let stand for 5 minutes before slicing.

Description: 
This is a slightly modified version of a recipe I found on allrecipes.com but has since disappeared. It is a great freezer item, I buy extra tenderloin when on sale, and freeze it with the marinade on. Everyone loves this!
