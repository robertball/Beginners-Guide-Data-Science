Title:
Roasted Tomato Caprese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/710142.jpg
Ingredients:
8 plum tomatoes, halved lengthwise, seeds removed
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil (or any good olive oil)
1 1/2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1 1/2 teaspoons garlic, minced
2 teaspoons sugar
sea salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
16 ounces fresh salted mozzarella cheese
8 fresh basil leaves, julienned
oil and balsamic for drizzling

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 275 degrees F
Arrange tomatoes, cut side up, on a cookie sheet. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic. Sprinkle with garlic, sugar, salt and pepper.
Roast for 2 hours until the tomatoes start to caramelize. Cool to room temperature.
Cut mozzarella into slices less than 1/2 inch thick. Layer the tomatoes alternately with the cheese and sprinkle the basil on top. Drizzle lightly with oil and balsamic.
