Title:
Fiber One Cookies! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 and 1/2 cups Fiber One original bran cereal
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup of honey
1 egg
1 1/2 cups of all purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
For the Glaze:
1/2 cup of powdered sugar
3 to 4 teaspoons of milk

Directions:
Heat oven to 375
In bowl, beat butter, brown sugar, honey and egg with eletric mixer on medium speed, scraping the bowl, untill smooth.
Stir in the flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon.
Stir in the cereal- do not use eletric blender on the cereal, it should remain the same size.
Onto ungreased cookie sheet, drop the dough by rounded spoon fulls about 2 inches apart.
Bake 7 to 9 minutes or untill set and golden brown, surface should apear shiny.
Cool for 1 to 2 minutes, remove from the sheet and then cool for another 15 minutes.
In a small bowl, mix the glaze ingredients untill smooth. Drizzle over the cookies.
