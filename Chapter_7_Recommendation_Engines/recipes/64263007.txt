Title:
Potato Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium potatos, peeled and cubed
2 slices bread
1-2 tsp summer savory
Salt to taste
Black pepper to taste
Turkey gravy

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Place potatoes in casserole dish.
Crumble the bread in a small amount of water and add to the potatoes.  Add 1-2 tsp summer savory, salt and pepper to taste.
Bake 1-1/2 to 2 hours.  Add turkey gravy for extra flavor.
