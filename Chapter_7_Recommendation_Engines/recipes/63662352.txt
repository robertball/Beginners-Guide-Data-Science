Title:
Grilled Chicken Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Cream of Coconut
1 cup Lemon Juice
1 Tbsp. Chicken Instant Bouillon
2 Tsp. Marjoram Leaves
1 Tsp. Garlic Powder
1 Tsp. Crushed Red Pepper
1 lb. Skinless, boneless Chicken Breast, cut into chunks
red or green bell pepper chunks
sweet onion chunks
fresh pineapple chunks, optional

Directions:
For marinade, in a small bowl, combine cream of coconut, lemon juice, bouillon, marjoram, garlic powder and crushed red pepper. Combine chicken, peppers, onions, and optional pineapples in a shallow dish or plastic bag and cover with half the marinade. Marinate, refrigerated, for 4-6 hours. Remove chicken, peppers, onions, and optional pineapple from marinade (discard marinade). Arrange on skewers and grill or bake until chicken is thoroughly cooked, basting frequently with reserve marinade.
