Title:
Razor Clam Fritters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs, beaten
1 cup coarsely ground razor clams
1/4 teaspoon seasoning salt
1/4 teaspoon lemon pepper
1 tablespoon minced flat leaf parsley
1 shallot, finely minced
1/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 cups cracker crumbs
1/4 cup butter
1 lemon, cut into 4 wedges

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients except cracker crumbs, butter and lemon wedges. Add cracker crumbs 1/2 cup at a time until mixture forms a stiff batter. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat. Spoon batter into skillet making each fritter about a dollar pancake size. Cook until golden, turn over and brown the other side. Transfer to a heated platter and serve with lemon wedges.

Submitter: 
witchywoman
Description: 
We are blessed here in the Pacific Northwest with razor clams! If they aren't available, just use chopped clams.
