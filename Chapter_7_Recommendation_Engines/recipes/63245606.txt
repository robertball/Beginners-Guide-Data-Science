Title:
Surprise Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 ounces herb flavored stuffing mix
1 8 ounce package of grated low fat cheddar cheese
6 eggs
1 3/4 cups skim milk
2 cups frozen diced onions and peppers
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
Spray a 9 X 12 inch casserole dish with vegetable spray. Add stuffing mix and sprinkle with grated cheese.
In a bowl, whisk together eggs and milk.
Microwave frozen onion and pepper mix until defrosted. Sprinkle over the stuffing mix.
Pour the egg mixture evenly over the stuffing mix. Let stand for 5 minutes.
Bake for 40 minutes. Remove from oven and let stand for 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Krysie
Description: 
This is a surprisingly flavorful and satisfying breakfast casserole that's also surprisingly easy to make. Using the recommended dairy ingredients lowers the calories, fat and cholesterol of this recipe, without sacrificing taste.
