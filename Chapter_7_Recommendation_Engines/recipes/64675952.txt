Title:
Bulgar Salad with Grapes and Feta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup bulgar wheat
1/2 teaspoon coarse salt
3/4 cup boiling water
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
2 teaspoons olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoons minced scallions
1 cup halved seedless grapes
1/4 cup tasted chopped walnuts, toasted
1/2 cup crumbled Feta cheese

Directions:
Pour boiling water over bulgar wheat and salt, let stand for 30 minutes.  Water will absorb
Whisk together lemon juice, oil, salt and pepper for dressing
Combine all ingredients; add dressing at the last minute
