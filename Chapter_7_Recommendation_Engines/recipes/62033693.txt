Title:
Succulent Ribeye and Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:

Directions:
4 rib eye steaks, about 1/2 inch thick;     about 2 1/2 to 3 lbs total
1 red bell pepper
1 green bell pepper
1 medium onion
4 large cloves garlic
2-3 TBS fajita seasoning
1 lime
In a large frying or saute pan, sear the steaks on high heat on both sides.  Immediate remove the steaks and set them aside.  Cut the peppers and onion lengthwise into thin strips.  Chop the garlic.  Place these in the pan and saute them over low-medium heat.  Sprinkly the steaks with the fajita seasoning and place them in the pan.  Turn them occasionally to cook on both sides.  Squeeze the juice of 1/2 lime over the steaks and peppers and cover. Simmer for 30 minutes to 1 hour on low heat.  The longer it simmers, the more tender it becomes.
Hint:  you can also bake this in the oven on low heat in a covered dish or even in a crock pot.
