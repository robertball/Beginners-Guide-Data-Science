Title:
Mushroom and Cheese Omelette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/903773.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs
Butter
1 green onion (minced)
1 cup sliced mushroom
Â¼ cup shredded mild cheese (reserve half)
Seasoned salt
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a frying pan fry* mushrooms adding seasoned salt (set aside).
In a frying pan cook the eggs on low heat sprinkled with green onions and salt and pepper to taste.
Keep an eye on the omelette once bottom is half way done sprinkle half of the cheese.
Once cheese is melted add mushrooms, fold and sprinkle rest of cheese.
Serve once cheese is fully melted.

Submitter: 
Danielle Girouard
Description: 
I love fresh mushroom omelettes and a hint of cheese really adds that little extra.
