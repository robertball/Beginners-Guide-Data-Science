Title:
Hot Fudge Sundae Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
crust:
1 package chocolate wafers, crushed fine
1/2 stick butter melted
sauce:
4 oz german chocolate
1/2 cup butter
2/3 cup sugar
1/8 t salt
2/3 c evaporated milk
1 large container cool whip
1/2 gallon of your favorite ice cream

Directions:
line 11/13 pan with crust and chill.  Soften ice cream and spread on crust.  Chill until set.  Melt together german chocolage, butter sugar salt and evaporated milk in a small pan until cooked.  Cool, and pour on top of ice cream.  Freeze till firm, then add cool whip and freeze.  Top with cherries, nuts, or other toppings.

Submitter: 
TINA B
Description: 
Chocolate crust, ice cream, homeade chocolate sauce and whip cream-this is the best dessert for summertime!  This is a tried and true family favorite, requested often at birthdays instead of cake.  Pick your favorite ice cream to make it truely your own creation!
