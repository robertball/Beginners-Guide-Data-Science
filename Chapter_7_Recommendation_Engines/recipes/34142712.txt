Title:
Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Recipe Details Makes: One Tube Pan 2 sticks softened margarine
1 stick softened butter
3 c. sugar
3 1/2 c. unsifted CAKE flour
5 large eggs (must be room temp!) 1 c. milk
1 Tbsp vanilla
Pinch of salt
Butter Glaze: 3+ Tbsp milk
2 c. sifted confectioner's sugar
1 stick unsalted butter
1/4-1/2 tsp of vanilla (to taste)

Directions:
Cream butter, margarine and sugar until light and fluffy (about 5 mins in stand mixer). Add half of the flour and half of the milk and beat well. Add eggs one at a time. Beat well after each addition. Add remaining flour, salt, vanilla, and milk. Beat until light and smooth. Grease and flour a tube pan. START IN A COLD OVEN! Bake at 325 for 1 hour and 20 mins. Let cool in pan for 15-10 mins. For Glaze: Place sugar in a bowl and set aside Melt butter and add to sugar Add milk and vanilla Whisk well If needed, add additional milk to reach desired consistency. Glaze cake and Enjoy!
