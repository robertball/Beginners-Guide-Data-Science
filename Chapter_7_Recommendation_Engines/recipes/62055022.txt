Title:
Mediterranean Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
1/3 c fresh lemon juice
3/4 c olive oil
1 T dried oregano
1 t rosemary
5 crushed garlic cloves
1 (16 ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 lb peeled and quartered potatoes
1 large spanish onion, sliced
2 ounces crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
In a large zipper plastic bag, combine the lemon juice, olive oil, oregano, rosemary, garlic cloves and chicken.  Marinate for at least two hours in the refrigerator.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Peel and quarter potatoes and slice onion.  Place in a casserole dish.  Pour off and discard marinade from chicken breasts and nestle them in the casserole dish with the potatoes and onion.
Bake covered 1 hour and 20 minutes.  Remove from oven, sprinkle on cheese, cover and bake an additional 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Peach822
Description: 
A year round easy dish to make. The chicken is best when marinated at least two hours.  It is light and combined with a green salad is a complete meal.
