Title:
Aunt Bernice's Ghoulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
8 ounces elbow macaroni, cooked and drained
2 cans diced tomatoes with liquid
2 cans whole kernel corn with liquid
1 large onion
1 cup grated cheese, your choice
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a saute pan brown ground beef and onions til tender.  Add cooked macaroni, tomatoes, and corn in layers in casserole dish.  Top with cheese and bake at 350 degrees until cheese is melted.  Add salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
My Aunt made this when we were growing up and only served it with a simple salad and cracker.  We gobbled it up like there was no tomorrow.
