Title:
Tom's Carrot Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 onion, chopped
2-4 Tbsp butter
8 or 9 carrots, peeled and chopped
1/2 cup red pepper (preferably peeled)
1 cup dry white wine
3 cups chicken stock
1/8 tsp nutmeg
1/8 tsp ground ginger
salt
white pepper
1/4 cup finely chopped pistachios
1/2 cup plain yogurt
1/4 - 1/2 cup whipping cream

Directions:
Saute onion and red pepper in butter till translucent.  Add carrots and wine.
Heat to boiling.
Reduce heat, cover and simmer 10 minutes.
Stir in stock, seasonings, and nuts.
Return to boil.
Reduce heat and simmer until carrots are tender, about 30 minutes.
Use blender or food processor to blend until smooth and then strain.
Reheat and mix in the yogurt and cream.
