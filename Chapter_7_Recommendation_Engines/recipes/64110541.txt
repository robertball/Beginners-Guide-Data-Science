Title:
Exceptional Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Tri-coloured fusilli pasta
Diced onions
Sliced carrots
Feta cheese
Ripe olives quartered
Sliced bell peppers opt.
Grape tomatoes cut in half opt.
Dressing:
1/2 c.     salad oil
2 tbsp      lemon juice
2 tbsp.     white vinegar
1 tsp.       sugar
1/2 tsp.    salt
1/4 tsp.    dry mustard
1/4 tsp.    onion salt
1/4 tsp.    paprika
1/4 tsp     oregano
1/4 tsp.    garlic salt (or 1 clove crushed)
1/16 tsp.  thyme

Directions:
Boil the pasta for 10 minutes or until el dente. While pasta is cooking, cut up vegetables and blend the dressing ingredients.  Drain pasta. Toss the warm pasta with the vegetables and dressing. Cool.

Submitter: 
TJ
Description: 
This is a very good pasta salad that can be made with your choice of fresh vegetables.
