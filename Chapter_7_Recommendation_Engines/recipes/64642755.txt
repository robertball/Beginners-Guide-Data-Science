Title:
Beef Barbacoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4525685.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lb chuck roast
2 cups salsa verde
1 cup orange juice
4 tbsp. lime juice
1 tsp. dried oregano
1 tsp. cumin
2 tbsp. minced garlic
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a bowl. Place the roast in the crock pot and set to low. Pour mixture over the roast and cook 8-10 hours or until you are able to pull it with two forks. Serve on tortillas with queso fresco and my roasted corn and jalapeno salsa recipe!

Submitter: 
Sarah Moore
Description: 
Amazing and easy barbacoa, serve with roasted corn and jalapeno salsa!
