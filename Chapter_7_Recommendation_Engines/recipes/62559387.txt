Title:
Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole cooked chicken (cut up)
12 cups chicken broth (I've been using Better Than Bullion Chicken Base)
3-4 stalks celery (chopped)
3-4 carrots (grated)
6-8 mushrooms (sliced)
Then I add a little of the following dried spices: dehydrated minced onions, chives, parsley, thyme, basil, ground pepper
4 cups fine egg noodles (cooked separately and added at the end)
Simmer for 1 hour, add the noodles, serve and enjoy!

Directions:
add all ingredients, simmer for 1 hour, add the noodles, serve and enjoy!
