Title:
Zesty Beef Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 clove garlic, minced
1 8 oz. can tomato sauce
1/4 cup ketchup
1 t. sugar
1/4 t oregano
1 8 oz cream cheese, softened
1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook ground beef, onion and garlic in fry pan until beef is lightly browned and onion is tender but not brown.  Drain off excess grease.  Stir in tomato sauce, catchup, sugar and oregano.  Cover; simmer gently for 10 to 15 min.  Add cream cheese and Parmesan cheese, stirring until cream cheese has melted and is well-blended into mixture.  Serve warm with a variety of snack bread or crackers.

Mary Guilds
Description: 
A great dip to serve on bread or crackers
