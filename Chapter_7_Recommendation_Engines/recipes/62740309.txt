Title:
Grandma Jordan's Carrot Medley Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb carrots, chopped
1 small onion, finely chopped
5 fresh mushrooms, sliced
1/4 tsp. dried crushed rosemary
1/4 tsp. ground cardamom
1/8 tsp. garlic salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
2 Tbsp. butter/margarine

Directions:
In large skillet, saute vegetables and seasonings in butter until veggies are crisp-tender, about 10 minutes.  Serve immediately.
