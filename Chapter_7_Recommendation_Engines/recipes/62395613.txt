Title:
Michael's Cucumber Lemonaide Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 English cucumbers
6 oz frozen lemonade
Sugar (to taste)

Directions:
Put the cucumbers in a blender until liquefied.
Strain the liquid.
Make frozen lemonade according to directions, using cucumber liquid instead of water, adding more water if needed
Sweeten with sugar to taste.
May also use to replace water for fresh lemonade.

Submitter: 
Joan
Description: 
Summer in a glass - cool & refreshing drink for those hot days.
