Title:
Cream Soup Substitute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp butter
3 tbsp flour
1/2 c chicken broth
1/2 c milk
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
In a small sauce pan, melt butter over medium heat.
Whisk in flour and let cool for 1-2 minutes.
Slowly stir in chicken broth, then milk. Whisk until it begins to thicken. Add seasonings.

Submitter:

Description: 
For when you don't have a single can of soup in the house.
