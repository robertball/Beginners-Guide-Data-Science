Title:
vegie low Calorie Mini Frittatas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped green onion
1 cup chopped mushrooms
1 garlic clove, minced
1 teaspoon olive oil
1/3 cup water
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 cup egg substitute
1/4 cup egg white substitute
1 cup fat free milk
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/4 cup fat free mozzarela cheese

Directions:
In a nonstick skillet, saute mushrooms, onion and garlic in oil until potatoes are golden brown, about 5 minutes. Add water and seasonings; cook and stir over medium heat until the water has evaporated, about 1 minute.
In a bowl, combine the eggs, egg whites, milk, flour and mozerrela  cheese. Stir in vegie mixture. Fill muffin cups coated with nonstick cooking spray three-fourths full. Bake at 325 degrees F for 15-20 minutes or until a knife comes out clean. Carefully run a knife around the edge of cups to loosen frittatas.
