Title:
Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup light Karo syrup
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1 tablespoon butter
1 pie shell

Directions:
Combine ingredients.
Pour into unbaked shell.
Bake 300 degrees.
