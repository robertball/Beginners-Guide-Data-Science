Title:
Peach-Ginger Slushie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5786844.jpg
Ingredients:
2 small fresh peaches - peeled, pitted, and sliced
6 ice cubes
1 teaspoon white sugar
1/8 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root

Directions:
Combine peaches, ice cubes, sugar, and ginger in a blender; blend until smooth. Pour into a glass and freeze for about 1 hour.

Submitter: 
gartenfee
Description: 
Slushies are a wonderful refreshing drink and a great way to use up ripe fruit. Ripe peaches are delicious in a slushie and taste great with a little bit of ginger.
