Title:
Chicken Broth in a Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 pounds bone-in chicken pieces
6 cups water
2 stalks celery, chopped
2 carrots, chopped
1 onion, quartered
1 tsp. dried parsley
1 tsp. dried dill weed

Directions:
Place the chicken pieces, water, celery, carrots, onion, parsley and dill weed in a slow cooker.
Cook on Low setting for 8 to 10 hours. Strain before using, and discard vegetables. Chicken may be removed from the bones, and used in soup.

Submitter: 
Christine Westrom
Description: 
This one-step method needs to simmer for 8 to 10 hours, and yields approximately one quart of rich chicken stock to use in soups and sauces.Modified from: Chicken Broth in a Slow Cooker
