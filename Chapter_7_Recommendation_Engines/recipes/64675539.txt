Title:
Crispy Shrimp Tempura Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
2 Tablespoons cornstarch
1 pinch salt
1 cup water
1 egg yolk
2 egg whites, lightly beaten
1 pound medium shrimp, peeled/deveined, with tails on
2 cups vegetable oil for frying

Directions:
Heat oil in a deep fryer to 375 degrees
Whisk flour, cornstarch and salt in a large bowl
Make a depression in the center of the flour mixture
Stir in the water and egg yolk
Mix until just moistened, batter will be lumpy
Stir in egg whites
One at a time, dip shrimp into the batter to coat (do not batter tails)
Carefully place a few shrimp at a time into the hot oil, about 1 1/2 minutes
Drain
