Title:
Bratwurst Reubens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (1.22 pound) package JENNIE-O TURKEY STORE® Bratwurst, any flavor
8 ounces sauerkraut, drained
1/4 cup Thousand Island dressing
1/4 cup Swiss cheese, shredded
Rye bratwurst buns

Directions:
In small bowl combine drained sauerkraut, Thousand Island dressing and shredded Swiss cheese. Grill or toast rye brat buns. Prepare bratwurst according to package directions. Place bratwurst in buns. Top with sauerkraut mixture.

Submitter: 
JENNIE-O TURKEY STORE®
Description: 
Try this for a quick and delicious dinner - bratwurst is served on rye buns with sauerkraut, Swiss cheese and thousand island dressing.
