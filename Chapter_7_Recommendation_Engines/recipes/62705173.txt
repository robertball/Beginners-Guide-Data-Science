Title:
Black and White Chicken Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/671724.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lb Boneless, skinless chicken breast
1 envelope chicken taco seasoning dissolved in:
1/2 cup water
16oz jar Pace Salsa (I use medium)
16oz bag frozen white corn
15oz can black beans, drained and rinsed
2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar&jack;)
6 burrito size flour tortillas

Directions:
Combine chicken, salsa and dissolved seasoning in crock pot, cook on high 3 hours
Remove chicken, shred and return to crock pot
Add corn and beans to crock pot, high 2 hours
Preheat oven to 400
Assemble burritos: spread chicken mixture, top with cheese and roll up
Spray 9x13 cake pan, lay burritos in and top with cheese
Put in 400 degree oven for 10 minutes until cheese is melted
