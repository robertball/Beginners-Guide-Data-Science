Title:
Garlic Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Bunches fresh Spinach (or 1 bag baby spinach)
1 Tblspn Olive Oil
1 large clove garlic
1 tsp sea salt

Directions:
Wash and de-stem spinach.  Rip into smaller pieces.
Heat oil in skillet, then add garlic and cook until fragrant.
Add Spinach, remove from heat, and cover.  Let sit for 5 minutes.
Sprinkle with salt, toss to mix, and serve
