Title:
Shrimp Scampi over Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 box of linguini
1/3 pound small shrimp, peeled and deveined
1/2 cup white wine
Juice of half a lemon
3 tbsp butter
Two cloves garlic
Fresh parsley
Salt and pepper, to taste
Pasta water, reserved

Directions:
Begin sauce after you have added your pasta to a pot of boiling water.
Melt butter in a pan, add shrimp. Cook until just pink on both sides.
Add garlic, saute until pale golden.
Add in 1/2 cup wine and lemon juice, and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and let simmer.
Add one ladle of pasta water, then drain pasta and add to scampi.
Stir in salt, pepper, and fresh parsley to taste.
Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Seaside
Description: 
I've been searching for the perfect shrimp scampi recipe- many I find are too dry or have unbalanced flavors. I created this version, which is tossed over linguini, on a whim one day- try it!
