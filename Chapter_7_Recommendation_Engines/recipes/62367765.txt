Title:
Bodacious Bing Cherry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups pitted bing cherries cut in half
1 cup white sugar
1/2 cup water
1 tbsp corn starch
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Dissolve cornstarch in water. In a saucepan over medium high heat, combine cherries, sugar and cornstarch dissolved in water, vanilla and cinnamon.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer. Cook, stirring occasionally until sauce thickens approximately 10 minutes.  If sauce needs additional thickening add 1/4 tsp. of cornstarch dissolved it 2 tbsp. of water at a time.

Submitter: 
Jillian
Description: 
Classic cherry sauce that's perfect over cheesecake, black forrest cake, or ice cream.
