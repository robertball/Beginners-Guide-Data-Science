Title:
Skillet Scampi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb shrimp, thawed and shelled
1 lb long pasta (ie linguine or angel hair)
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 c butter, cut into chunks
1/4 c EVOO
1/2 c white wine
1/4 c lemon juice
1/2 tsp black pepper
grated parmesan

Directions:
clean shrimp.
Cook pasta.
About 8 min out, Start butter and oil over low heat.
Increase heat to medium high. Add shrimp and garlic. Cook till shrimp are curled and opaque.
Add wine, lemon juice, and pepper. Simmer 3 min or  till pasta's done.
Drain pasta and add to hot pan with shrimp. Mix well for 2 min, stirring.
Serve. Add parmesan.
