Ingredients:
4 tbsp butter
1/4 cup of finely chopped onion
4 tbsp flour
2 1/4 cup milk
salt and pepper to taste
100 gr Serrano ham, finely chopped
1 cup grated Manchego cheese
1 egg
1 cup panko breadcrumbs
oil for frying

Directions:
Melt the butter and cook the onions until soft.
Add the flour all at once, mix well with wooden spoon and cook until light golden. It will have lumps but it's ok.
Add milk slowly, mixing after each addition to eliminate lumps.
Cook at medium heat and stirring until thick.
Add nutmeg, salt and pepper.
Add ham and cheese, stirring well.
Pour into a glass mold, cool a little bit, cover surface with plastic wrap and refrigerate well until cold.
Form into balls, roll in beaten egg and panko crumbs. Fry in hot oil.
