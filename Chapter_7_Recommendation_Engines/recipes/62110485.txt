Title:
Rhubarb-Berry Mini Loaves or Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup melted butter or light oil (or a combination of the two to make 1/2 cup)
1/2 cup orange juice
1 and 1/2 cup finely chopped, raw rhubarb
1/2 cup blueberries or chopped strawberries
2 and 1/2 cups flour
1/2 cup white sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ginger

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Beat egg, brown sugar, butter or oil, and orange juice.
Stir in rhubarb and berries.
In a separate bowl, combine dry ingredients.
Mix wet and dry ingredients just until incorporated. Batter will be stiff.
Spread into 8 greased, mini loaf pans (2 & 1/2" by 3 & 1/2" each) or 12 greased muffin tins.
Bake at 350 degrees; mini loaves for 30 minutes, or muffins for 18 to 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
SIXMITTS
Description: 
A few sweet blueberries or strawberries complement the tang of the rhubarb in this quick bread.
