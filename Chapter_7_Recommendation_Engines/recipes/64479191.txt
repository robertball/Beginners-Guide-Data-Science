Title:
Rosemary Salted Nuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. olive oil
1 lb. raw nuts
Generous pinch sea salt
2 Tbsp. fresh rosemary leaves, chopped

Directions:
Add olive oil to a large skillet over medium heat.
When oil is hot and shimmering but not smoking, add nuts, salt, and rosemary and stir continuously until nuts are golden, about 8-10 minutes.
Serve warm or let cool and store in an airtight container for up to 3 weeks.

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
Roasted nuts make a nice snack, but store-bought nuts are usually doused in vegetable oil and sometimes include preservatives and artificial flavors. It's so easy to make your own!
