Title:
Chocolate Buttercream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Crisco shortening (do not use butter flavored or another brand)
1 cup unsalted butter, softened at room temp (do not soften in microwave, let set out till soft)
1/3 cup half n half
1/2 teaspoon popcorn salt (do not use regular salt or butter flavored popcorn salt)
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
1 teaspoon pure almond extract
1 teaspoon butter flavor extract
& cups pure cane powdered sugar (must say pure cane on package)
1 cup good quality cocoa (Dutch processed is a good choice, sifted
2 ounces good quality melted unsweetened chocolate

Directions:
Dissolve salt in lukewarm Half n Half (be sure it is completely dissolved!). Add extracts to half n half; set aside. Beat Crisco shortening and butter on med high speed until almost white in color (about 5 minutes. on lowest speed gradually add powdered sugar, cocoa alternating with half n half into the creamed shortening/butter mixture. Scrap sides of bowl as needed. Once ingredients are all incorporated add the melted cooled chocolate. Beat on medium high for 3 - 5 minutes until light and fluffy. Enough to frost a 9x13 sheet or 8 inch layer cake.
