Title:
Brown Rice Flour Blend Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2184073.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups brown rice flour
2 cups potato starch
1 cup tapioca flour

Directions:
Whisk brown rice flour, potato starch, and tapioca flour together; store in airtight container at room temperature.

Submitter: 
Amy B.
Description: 
This is a flour blend for making gluten-free sandwich bread.
