Title:
Pasta poflotsky Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pasta 500 g
Chopped meat 300 g
Salt 5 g
Black pepper 5 g
Onion 1 tennis-ball size

Directions:
Put pasta into boiling water
Cook for 15 min (till ready)
Cook chopped meat on a frying pan and onion (sun-flower oil)
Stir the cooked meat with pasta. Voila! Enjoy!
