Title:
mashed potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pounds of potatoes
2 sticks of butter
1 cup of cream or half and half

Directions:
Peel and dice potatoes. Cubes should be about 1/2 inch per side. Place potatoes in large pan filled with cold, salted water. Bring the water and potatoes to a boil. Once at a full rolling boil, turn down the heat and simmer for 20 minutes. Drain potatoes. Mash with butter and cream.
