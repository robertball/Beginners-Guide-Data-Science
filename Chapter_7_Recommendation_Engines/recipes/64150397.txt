Title:
Hominy Chili with Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons oil
2 teaspoons garlic, minced
4 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon ground cumin
15 ½ ounces hominy, canned, drained
15 ounces red beans, drained
14 ½ ounces tomatoes, canned
14 ½ ounces stewed tomatoes
4 teaspoons cilantro, chopped

Directions:
Heat oil in saucepan over medium heat. Add garlic; sauté 1 minute. Stir in remaining ingredients; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer uncovered for 1 hour.
