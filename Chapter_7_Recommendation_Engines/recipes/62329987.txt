Title:
Lemon Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cake flour   3 cups sugar   6 large eggs at room temp.    2 sticks unsalted butter   1/2 cup shortening    two teaspoons lemon flavoring   1/2 teaspoon baking powder   1 cup whole milk   1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Sift flour into bowl. add sugar, salt and baking powder. Turn blender on lowest speed and add eggs one at a time making sure the egg has been incorperated before adding another. Add butter and shortening. Add milk and finally add flavoring. turn blender up to medium speed and blend for about a minute and a half. Scrape bottom and edges of bowl and blend for another 45 seconds. Place batter in a floured cake pan and place pan in oven at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hour. when done, let cool for 10 minutes in pan then place on cooling rack.
