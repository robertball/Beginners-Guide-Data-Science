Title:
Zesty Marinara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/487298.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (28 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1/2 green pepper
1 clove garlic, minced
6 tablespoons olive oil
1/3 cup finely diced onion
1/2 cup white wine
1 teaspoon italian seasoning
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon sugar (more depending on tomato acidity)
Pinch of red pepper flakes

Directions:
Place tomatoes and their juice, tomato paste, chopped parsley and minced garlic into food processor. Blend until smooth.
In a large pot over medium heat saute the finely chopped onion in olive oil until lightly browned. Add the blended tomato sauce, white wine and seasoning.
Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
