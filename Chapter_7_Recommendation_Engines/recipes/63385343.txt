Title:
Wild oatmeal cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 butter, softened
1c light brown sugar
1/2 c sugar
2 lg eggs
1 1/2 t vanilla
2 c all purpose flour
1 t baking soda
1/2 t salt
4 1/2 c rolled oats

Directions:
Preheat oven 375'. In a large mixing bowl, cream the butter and sugars.  Beat in the eggs and then vanilla.  Mix in the flour, baking soda, and salt until you don't see any more white.  Stir in the rolled oats until well combined.
Drop by rounded teaspoon or tablespoon onto an ungraded cookie sheet and flatten slightly.  Bake for 8-10 minutes or until the cookies are slightly browned on the bottom.
Cool on a wire rack before eating.
