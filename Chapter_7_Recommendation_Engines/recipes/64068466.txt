Title:
Gluten Free Buckwheat, Asparagus and Pesto Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2027229.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup raw buckwheat, rinsed
1 cup water
8 asparagus stalks, steamed and cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1/3 cup fresh pesto
2 TBSP fresh grated parmesean cheese (optional)

Directions:
Bring water to a boil in a small saucepan.
Add rinsed buckwheat and stir once.  Turn down the heat to simmer until the buckwheat is cooked al dente (about 8 minutes).
In a medium size serving bowl mix pesto and asparagus until well coated.
Mix the buckwheat with the pesto and asparagus until well coated.
Top with parmesean cheese if desired.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Buckwheat groats and steamed asparagus are tossed with pesto to make a light salad. It can be served warm or cold.  This is an excellent side dish for grilled or steamed fish.
