Title:
Darien's Spicy Szechwan Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/283790.jpg
Ingredients:
Marinade:
2 TBSP dry sherry
1 TBSP dark sesame oil
1 TBSP hoisin sauce
1 TBSP dark soy sauce
1 TBSP honey
2 tsp. Asian chile sauce
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 TBSP fresh ginger, minced
3 TBSP green onion, minced
2 TBSP cilantro, chopped
1 lb. peeled and deveined shrimp, tails removed
2 TBSP grapeseed oil

Directions:
In a medium bowl, prepare marinade by combining the sherry, sesame oil, hoisin sauce, soy sauce, honey, chile sauce, garlic, ginger, green onion, and cilantro.
Add shrimp to marinade; cover and refrigerate up to 8 hours.
Preheat a wok to high.
Add grapeseed oil to coat the wok.
Add shrimp to the wok, reserving marinade.
Stir-fry until shrimp are pink, 1-2 min. Add marinade and toss to coat the shrimp.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Holly
Description: 
Wok-fried shrimp in a delicious blend of spicy and sweet Asian flavors that can be served alone or over rice.
