Title:
Easy Cinnamon Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982392.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound confectioners' sugar
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa
1 teaspoon McCormick® Ground Cinnamon
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup milk
1 1/2 teaspoons McCormick® Pure Vanilla Extract
1 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
1. Line an 8-inch square baking pan with foil, allowing foil to extend over sides. Butter foil.
In bowl, mix together sugar, cocoa and cinnamon.
In a saucepan, heat butter and milk until butter melts. Add vanilla extract. Combine with sugar mixture. Stir in nuts. Pour into prepared pan. Refrigerate at least 1 hour.
Use foil to lift out of pan. Cut into 2-inch squares. Diagonally cut each square in half, making triangles. Garnish with whole nuts, if desired.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
A hint of cinnamon enhances chocolate in an easy, make-ahead holiday fudge.
