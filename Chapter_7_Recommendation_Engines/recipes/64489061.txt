Title:
Franits Sandwich with Brie Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2572462.jpg
Ingredients:
Two Slices of Whole Grain Bread
Three Slices of Brie Cheese, slightly ripened
Slices of One Ripe Pear (washed)
Two Pieces of Romaine Lettuce
1 tsp Chopped Walnuts

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350ยบ F.
Place two slices of bread on the cutting board and place a piece of romaine on each slice of the bread.
Next, place slices of pear on one slice of bread (enough to cover the bread). Top with chopped walnuts.
Place brie cheese on top of the walnuts, then carefully bring both pieces of bread together to form a sandwich.
Place in baking dish (or cookie sheet), and bake for five minutes (until brie melts). Cut into quarters and serve immediately!

Chef AidF
Description: 
This is one of my easiest recipes - it's creamy, fruity, and can be served for lunch, or as an appetizer or side dish.
