Title:
Miss Tessie's Pepper Jelly Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup hot pepper, ground
3/4 cup bell or banana pepper
1 1/2 cup vinegar
6 cups sugar
1 box Sure-Jell
Sterile jars

Directions:
Mix peppers, vinegar, surejell in 6 quart pan and bring to a full boil.
Pour in all the sugar.
Let boil about 1 minute at full rolling boil ( a boil you cannot stir down.)
Skim off any top foam.
Put in sterile jars  and seal according to canning lid instructions.

Submitter: 
Hezzy_tant_Cook
Description: 
Hot and sweet and great with meat! (Miss Tessie's jelly can't be beat!)
