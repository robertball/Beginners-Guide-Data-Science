Title:
Baked Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•	4-1/2 pounds unpeeled red skin potatoes, cut into 3/4-inch chunks
•	1/4 cup olive oil
•	2 envelopes Italian salad dressing mix
•	1 medium green pepper, chopped
•	1 medium sweet red pepper, chopped
•	½  bunch green onions, chopped
•	8 hard-cooked eggs, chopped
•	6 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled
•	1-1/2 cups mayonnaise
•	1 tablespoon white vinegar
•	1 tablespoon lemon juice
•	2 teaspoons dried basil  or 1 T. chopped fresh basil
•	1-1/2 teaspoons salt
•	1/2 teaspoon pepper
•	1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
•	In a large bowl, toss the potatoes with oil and dressing mixes. Place in two greased 13-in. x 9-in. baking dishes. Bake, uncovered, at 400° for 40 minutes or until tender. Cool. Transfer to a large bowl; add peppers, onions, eggs and bacon. Toss gently.
•	In a small bowl, combine the remaining ingredients. Pour over salad and stir gently. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Submitter: 
CATHYRAE
Description: 
This is not your typical sweet potato salad.  The flavor of the roasted potatos, bacon and fresh basil meld together beautifully.
