Title:
FANTASTIC Sausage Kale and Barely Soup! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-three sweet italian sausages, de-cased and crumbled
-onion, chopped
-1 can tomato paste
-2 tblspn chicken boullion
-2 tblspn mushroom boullion
-1/3 a tin of smoked oysters crumbled fine
-2 anchovies crumbled fine
-3 heaping tblspn garlic
-1 bunch kale, rises and finely chopped
-1 tomato chopped
-1 tblspn garlic plus seasoning
- 1 box chicken stock
-4 cups water
-1 cup barely
-1 tblspn butter
-2 tblspn oil
-2-3 tblspn rice wine vinegar

Directions:
cook onions, spices, garlic, and sausage in oil on medium high heat.
Add anchovies and crumbled oysters and stir in till dissolved.
Par cook Barely separately.
Add tomato paste to sausage pan. Cook on low, add broth and water.
Add chopped and rinsed kale.
Bring to a boil, add barely and chopped tomato.
finish with Rice Wine Vinegar

Submitter: 
Kierston
Description: 
There's no photo because Everyone Ate it before I could get a picture! This is a Delcious amazing soup! Don't be afraid of the oysters and anchovies--- you don't even taste them! They just add a nice richness that undertones everything. It's a fantastic (and Healthy!) Soup!!
