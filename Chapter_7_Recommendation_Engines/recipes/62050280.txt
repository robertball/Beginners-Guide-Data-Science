Title:
Yummy Potato Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1/2 lb. butter (2 sticks)-room temp.
- 1/2 lb. margarine (2 sticks)-room temp.-you can use all margarine or all butter if desired, just used to cut back on fat a little
- 1c. white sugar
- 3 1/2c. sifted flour
- 2 tsp. vanilla extract
- 1 pkg. better made potato chips (6-8 oz.)
- extra granualated white sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Cream (very important) together butter, margarine, sugar, and vanilla.  Gradually blend in flour.  Crush chips in bag and blend into mixture.  Spoon by teas. onto ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake 12 min. on middle rack in oven until light brown on bottom.  Leave on cookie sheet for 5 minutes then dip into granulated sugar.
