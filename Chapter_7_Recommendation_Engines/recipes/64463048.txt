Title:
Bread From Isabelle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
~7 C - Unbleached White Flour
1 C - Whole Wheat Flour
2 T - Rye Flour
1 C - 5 Grain Rolled Oats
1 T - Yeast
2 t - Salt
3 C - Water

Directions:
In large bowl, combine 2 C unbleached white flour, 1 T yeast, 1 T rye flour, and 2 C cool water.
Mix with spoon until smooth (there will still be some lumps).
Cover with plastic wrap (not super tight).
Let bowl rest for 12 hours.
In small bowl, combine 1 C whole wheat flour, 1 T rye flour, and 1 C hot tap water.
Cover with plastic wrap (not super tight).
Let bowl rest for 12 hours.
After resting, the sponge has likely collapsed
Add bowl 2 to bowl 1.
Combine 2 t salt, and 1 C unbleached white flour.
Measure 1 C rolled oats, add warm water until full.
Let dough and oats rest for 3 hours.
Add 1 C unbleached white flour.
Fold edges into the center of the dough.
Add oats and fold in.
Add in 1 C flour and fold in.
Add 1/2 C flour and fold in.
Add 1/2 C flour and fold in.
Using bread spatula, kneed until flour is well mixed.
Start hand mixing, adding flour when necessary. Fold the edges to the middle and turn the bowl slightly with each fold.
When smooth and non-sticky (like a deflated soccer ball) cover and let rest for 30 mins.
Grease a large cookie sheet.
Split dough in half.
Spread unbleached white flour on the counter and begin folding dough 1 in on itself (you will likely need more flour).
Once it is smooth on the bottom, tuck in the sides and make a loaf sized shape and place on cookie sheet.
Repeat for dough 2.
Wet a tea towel and cover the bread for 30 mins.
Pre-Heat oven to 465
Cut three diagonal slits across each loaf about 1/2 inch deep.
Put the loafs into the oven.
Fill a pie plate with water and place in oven to provide vapor.
Cook at 465 for 20 mins.
Reduce to 425 for 20 mins.
Turn off oven and leave in for 10 mins.
