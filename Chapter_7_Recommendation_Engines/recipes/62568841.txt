Title:
Raspberry-filled Vanilla Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. butter (no substitutions)
3/4 c. sugar
1 egg
1 t. vanilla extract
2 1/4 c. all-purpose flour
1/2 t. baking powder
1/4 t. salt
1/2 c. raspberry jam (full sugar)
1 c. confectioner's sugar
2 T. milk
1/2 t. vanilla extract

Directions:
Grease cookie sheets, preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cream butter and sugar, add egg and vanilla; mix.
In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder and salt.
Add dry ingredients gradually to creamed mixture.
Roll dough into 1 inch balls, press a depression into each.
Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes.
Remove from oven, add 1/2 t. of jam, and return to oven for 5 more minutes.
Cool two minutes, remove to plates.
Prepare glaze from last three ingredients, place in a Ziplock bag, and snip one corner of bag.
When cookies are almost cool, drizzle glaze over each cookie.
