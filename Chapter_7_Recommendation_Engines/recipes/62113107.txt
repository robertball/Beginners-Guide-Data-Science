Title:
Becca's Choco Chip Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups All Purpose Flour
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 egg
1 cup milk
1/3 cup vegatable oil
2 teaspoons vanilla
1 cup mini chocolate chips

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degress and line muffin pan.
2.Combine flour, sugars, baking powder, and salt in a medium size bowl.
3.In a seprate bowl combine egg, milk,oil and vanilla, mix well.
4.Add wet ingrediants to flour mixture and combine until well blended.
5.Fold in chocolate chips.
6. Fill muffin pan 1/2 full and bake about 15 to 18 min.
7. Cool on wire rack and enjoy while still warm!
