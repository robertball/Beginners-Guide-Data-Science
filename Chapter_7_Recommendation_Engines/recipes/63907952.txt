Title:
Skinny Minestrone Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123094.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tsp. olive oil
3/4 c. chopped onion
1/2 c. diced celery
2 cloves garlic, minced
28 oz. can diced tomatoes, undrained
4 c. lowfat chicken broth
3-4 cups V-8
1 c. chopped carrots (frozen)
1 c. chopped zucchini (frozen)
1 can northern white beans
1/3 c. orzo (or rice, or baby shell pasta)
3 T. chopped parsley
1 bay leaf
2 tsp. dried basil
1 tsp. oregano
1/2 tsp. thyme
1/2 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Heat olive oil in large soup pot or dutch oven.
Add onions, celery and garlic.  cook and stir for 3 min., until vegetables begin to soften.
Add all remaining ingredients.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to low.  Simmer, partially covered for 30 minutes or until carrots are cooked.
