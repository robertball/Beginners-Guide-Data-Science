Title:
Vanishing Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup (1 stick) plus 6 tbsp. butter softened
3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup granulated sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. of vanilla
1-1/2 cups of all purpose flour
1 tsp of baking soda
1 tsp of ground cinammon
1/2 tsp salt
3 cups Quaker Oats (quick or old fasioned, uncooked)
1 cup of chocolate chips

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees. in a large bowl, beat butter and sugars on medium speed of electric mixer until creamy.
Add eggs and vanilla; beat well.
Add combined flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt; mix well.
Add oats and chocolate chips; mix well.
Drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
Bake 8-10 minutes or until light golden brown. Cool 1 minute on cookie sheets; remove to wire rack.  Cool completely. Store tightly covered.
