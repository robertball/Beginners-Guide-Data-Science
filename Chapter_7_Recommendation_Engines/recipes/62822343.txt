Title:
Easy Homemade Ice Cream, Nutella Peanut Butter Chip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups heavy cream
1 (14 oz.) Eagle BrandÂ® Sweetened Condensed Milk
3 tablespoons butter, melted
1/2 cup Nutella
1 cup peanut butter chips

Directions:
Whip heavy cream to stiff peaks in large bowl.
Whisk sweetened condensed milk, butter, and Nutella in large bowl.
Stir in peanut butter chips.
Fold in whipped cream.
Pour into a 2-quart container and cover.
Freeze 6 hours or until firm. Store in freezer.
