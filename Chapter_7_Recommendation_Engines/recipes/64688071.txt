Title:
Steamed Clams & tomatoes with Angel Hair Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  (9 0z) pkg fresh angel hair pasta
1 Tbs olive oil
1 cup  chopped tomato
1 table spoon bottled minced garlic
1/4 t crushed red pepper
1/3 cup dry white wine
1 8 oz bottle clam juice
2 dozen little neck clams, scrubbed
1 Tbs butter
4 t chopped flat leaf parsley

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions, omitting salt & fat
Drain & keep warm
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add tomato, garlic & pepper to pan; saute 1 min. Stir in wine & juice; bring to a boil. Add clams. Cover and cook 7 minutes or butter to pan, stirring until butter melts. Place 1 cup pasta in each of  4 shallow bowls; top each serving with 6 clams, 1/2 cup broth, and 1 t parsley
