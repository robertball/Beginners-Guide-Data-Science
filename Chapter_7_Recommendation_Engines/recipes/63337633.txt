Title:
Fall Signature Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces applejack
1 1/2 ounces rye whiskey
1 tablespoon maple syrup
A few dashes orange bitters
About 2 ounces Champagne
Blood-orange rind cut into strips for garnish

Directions:
1.Combine applejack, whiskey, maple syrup, and bitters in a cocktail shaker, and shake over ice. Strain into a 6-ounce martini glass, and top with Champagne. Garnish with orange rind, and serve.From Martha Stewart Weddings, Fall 2011
