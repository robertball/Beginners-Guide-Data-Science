Title:
Bill's 7 Bean Soup (Lower sodium) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups 7 bean soup mix (Sunflower market)
3-4 Cups of chicken stock (I use my No Salt Chicken/Turkey stock recipe)
5 celery stalks finely chopped
5 large carrots diced in small to medium chunks
1 White onion finely chopped
4-5 large golden potatos diced in small to medium chunks
1/2 cup of brown rice
1 Teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 1/2 Teaspoons Mrs. Dash No Salt table blend
2 cloves of finely chopped fresh garlic or 1 teaspoon of garlic powder

Directions:
Soak beans in 4 cups of water for at least 4 hours.  Place all ingredients into a 5 quart slow cooker and cook on low for 8 hours.  Season to taste.  If preparing on stove top, place all the ingredients into the sauce pan, bring to a boil, simmer for 3-4 hours.

Submitter: 
Bill Falk
Description: 
Kicked up 7 bean soup, basic start recipe made tastier and lower sodium!!  Started with Sun Flower Markets 7 bean mix.
