Title:
Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz cream cheese
6 Tbsp butter
1-3/4 cup powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 Tbsp cream

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, butter and powdered sugar.
Add vanilla and enough cream to make a frosting.
Spread on pumpkin bars.

Submitter: 
Barbara Bartz
Description: 
Good on pumpkin bars.
