Title:
Slow Cooker Spicy Black-Eyed Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups water
1 tablespoon chicken bouillon
1 pound dried black-eyed peas, sorted and rinsed
1 onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, diced
1 red bell pepper, stemmed, seeded, and diced
1 jalapeno chile, seeded and minced
12 ounces diced ham
7 slices bacon, fat trimmed and chopped
1/2 tablespoon chili powder
1 tablespoon cumin
1 tablespoon kosher salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Pour the water into a slow cooker, add the bouillon cube, and stir to dissolve. Combine the black-eyed peas, onion, garlic, bell pepper, jalapeno pepper, ham, bacon, cayenne pepper, cumin, salt, and pepper; stir to blend. Cover the slow cooker and cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours until the beans are tender.

Description: 
Black-eyed peas grab some heat and spice from jalapeno peppers and cumin in this flavorful slow cooker preparation that also includes diced ham, bacon, bell peppers, onion, and garlic.Modified from: Slow Cooker Spicy Black-Eyed Peas
