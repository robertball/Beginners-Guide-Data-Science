Title:
Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/536063.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can garbanzo beans
1 can kidney beans
1 can cannellini beans
1 can pinto beans ( ALL BEANS RINSED AND DRAINED)
4-5 stalks of celery w/ leaves- diced small
1 1/2 cups shredded carrots
1-2 bell peppers- any color- diced small
Fresh Italian parsley- 3 tablespoons- chopped small
fresh lemon juice - we used 5 or 6
LOTS of fresh cracked pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and toss to evenly coat. Serve immediately or place in the fridge and serve cold when ready.

Submitter:

Description: 
A colorful array of beans and vegeatbles tossed in fresh parsley, lemon juice and pepper.
