Title:
Yummy Peanut Butter Choc Chip Cookies (Gluten-Free Vegan ) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Brown Rice Flour
1/2 cup Amaranth Flour
1/2 cup Millet Flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
3 Tablespoons Chia Seeds
1 cup very warm water
1 cup Peanut Butter
1 teaspoon gluten-free vanilla
4 figs
1/2 cup agave
1/2 cup Sucanat
1 cup Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Preheat oven at 350 F.
In a large bowl mix flours, salt and baking soda together.
In a blender, put Chia Seeds and water.
Let sit for 10 minutes
Add peanut butter, vanilla, figs, agave and succanat to blender
Blend at a high speed ingredients in blender.
Let mixture cool else your chocolate chips will melt!
Pour wet mixture into dry mixture, add chocolate chips, spoon out onto a greased baking sheet (use Olive oil or any vegetable oil you have)  or onto parchment paper.
Bake at 350 F for 10-15 minutes

Description: 
These Gluten-Free Vegan Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies are YUMMY and a nice alternative for those of us looking to cut out gluten and eggs.
