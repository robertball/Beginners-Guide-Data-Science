Title:
Southwestern Black Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744680.jpg
Ingredients:
1 15.5 oz can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 15.5 oz can sweet corn, drained
1 tomato, chopped
1 small hass avocado, diced
1/4 cup red onion, chopped
1 scallion (optional), chopped
1 lime, juice of
3 TBSP olive oil
1 TBSP cilantro (I use cilantro paste in a tube)
salt and pepper to taste (optional)

Directions:
These are the directions I found online:
Mix all ingredients **expect avocado***
Marinate in refrigerator 30 minutes
Add avocado before serving
And this is what I actually do:
Mix all ingredients and serve or refigerate
