Title:
Oatmeal Pancakes. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups milk
1 1/2 Cups uncooked oats (Old Fashioned are best rather than Quick Oats)
3 Tbs. salad oil
2 eggs.
3/4 Cup flour
2 Tbs. sugar
2 1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
1 1/4 tsp. salt.

Directions:
Combine milk and oats and let stand 2 minutes.  Add beaten eggs and sifted dry ingredients.
Mixture will be thin; add more flour if too thin.
