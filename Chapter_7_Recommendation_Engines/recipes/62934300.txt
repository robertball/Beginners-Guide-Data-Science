Title:
Quick Basic Meat Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 clove garlic
1 large onion
1 can or jar spaghetti sauce 680-750 ml (eg. Bravo, Ragu)
1 large green pepper
1/2 lb fresh mushrooms
1/2 tsp salt
3 bay leaves
1 tbsp italian seasoning

Directions:
In a large pot, brown ground beef at medium temperature.
While beef browns chop garlic, onion, mushrooms and green pepper
When ground been nearly done (only traces of pink), remove from heat and drain excess fat if any
Place ground beef back on stove and add garlic and onions
Continue cooking beef and onion until beef is completely browned and onion are cooked (onions will become translucent)
Add spaghetti sauce, mushrooms, green pepper, salt and italian seasoning, and bay leaves.
Heat to boiling, stirring every 5 minutes.
Reduce heat and simmer 15 - 45 minutes.
Serve over you favourite pasta - I prefer whole wheat spaghettini!
