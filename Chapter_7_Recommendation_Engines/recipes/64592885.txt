Title:
Coconut Rhubarb Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups sliced fresh or frozen rhubarb
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 1/2 cups water
1 18-1/4oz pkg butter pecan cake mix
1 cup flaked coconut
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1/2 cup butter, melted

Directions:
In large saucepan, mix rhubarb, sugar and water.
Cook over med. heat x 8-10 min. or until rhubarb is tender; cool slightly.
Transfer to a greased 9"x13" baking dish.
Sprinkle with cake mix.
Top with coconut and pecans.
Drizzle with butter.
Bake @ 350 x 25-30 min. or until tests done with a toothpick.
Serve with ice cream

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Rhubarb is sweetened with crunchy pecans and flaked coconut.
