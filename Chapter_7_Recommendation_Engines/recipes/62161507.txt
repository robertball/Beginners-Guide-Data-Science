Title:
Zucchini Coconut Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Eggs
2 Cups Sugar
1 Cup Vegetable Oil
2 Cups Flour
2 Teas. Baking Soda
2 Teas. Cinnamon
1 Teas. Salt
3 Cups grated Zucchini
1 1/2 Cups Coconut
1 1/2 Cups Black Walnuts Chopped (optional)
Frosting:
1 8oz. Pachage Cream Cheese
3 3/4 Cups Powdered Sugar
1 Cube Butter or Margarine
1 teas. Vanilla
1/4 Rasins(optional)
1 cup cocnut
1/2 cups black Walnuts choppe (optional)

Directions:
Mix together:
Eggs, sugar and oil.  Add: Flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt.  Beat in: grated zucchini, coconut and walnuts.
Pour into 2 8-inch round cake pans and one 8X8 inch pan. Or make bars and pour into 2 9x 13 inch pans.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
Frosting mix:
cream cheese, powdered sugar and margarine.  Add: vanilla, rasins, cocnut and walnuts.  Spread over cooled cake.

Submitter: 
Kendra
Description: 
My mom has been making this cake for years using fresh zucchini from the garden.  It is a family favorite.  It's even great to freeze and bring out when company comes
