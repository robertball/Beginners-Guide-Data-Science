Title:
Runza Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 Lbs lean ground beef
3 cloves garlic minced
1 med onion chopped
1 small head of cabbage shredded
1 can beef broth
2 Tbs Worstershire sauce
2 Tbs yellow mustard
Salt and pepper to taste
10 hamburger buns

Directions:
Brown ground beef in skillet, drain
add garlic onion and cabbage till wilted and soft.
Add beef broth, worstershire sauce and mustard. reduce, uncovered, to a sloppy joe consistanty.
Before serving place buns over mixture and cover to steam.
Fill buns and bon appetite!

Submitter: 
superfoodie
Description: 
Originally from Iowa, Runza's were a staple in our family dinners.  Usually the beef and cabbage mixture was stuffed in fresh bread dough.  This is an easy and quick variation.  A great way to get kids to eat their vegetables!!! My 10 yr old son loves this.
