Title:
Crunchy Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-12 to 16oz bag of slaw mix
1-green pepper(you can use red, yellow or a combo)chopped in small pieces
3-green onions sliced
1/2cup-honey roasted (almond accents)(Sliced almonds)
1-crushed ramien noodles(reserve season packet)
Dressing:
1-Season packet
1/2cup-oil
1/4cup-white vinegar
1/2cup-sugar

Directions:
Layer vegetables, almonds and noodles in a large bowl. Use the same order they are listed on the ingredients.
Dessing: combine  the ingredients and mix well.
Right before serving mix the dressing well and combinewith the veggy mix.
Toss well and serve.
