Title:
Flaxmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/3 cups water
1 1/3 cups milled flax seed
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons honey
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
chocolate syrup, optional
caramel syrup, optional
chocolate peanut butter, optional
wipped cream, optional
Marishino cherries, optional

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine water, flax, cinnamon and salt.  Bring to a boil; boil for 1 minute, stirring occasionally.  Remove from the heat; stir in honey and vanilla.  Cover and let stand for 5 minutes.  Top with desired topping.

Like oatmeal but made with flax seed.  This sweet dish is an awsome breakfast.
