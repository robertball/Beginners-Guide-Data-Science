Title:
Southern Style Fudge Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
2 cups sifted flour
2 cups white sugar
1 cup shortening
4 tablespoons cocoa powder
1 cup water
1/2 cup milk
1/2 tbsp lemon or lime juice
1/2 tsp baking soda
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
Icing:
1/2 cup shortening
4 Tbsp cocoa
6 Tbsp milk
16 oz powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Cake:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees Farenheit.
Mix flour and sugar in large bowl and set aside.
Bring to boil the shortening, cocoa and water.
Mix with dry ingredients in large bowl.
Add milk, juice, baking soda, eggs and vanilla.
Beat until smooth.
Bake in cake pan for 20-25 mn until done.
Icing:
Bring to boil shortening, cocoa and milk stirring often.
Remove from heat and add sugar and vanilla.
Mix until lumps are gone.
Pour over warm (not HOT) cake.

Submitter: 
Casey Vermeland Paris
Description: 
This moist fudgy cake accompanies anything! BBQ's, holidays and birthdays! It's a great comfort food and certainly doesn't lack on rich flavor. Fudge lovers will love this smooth cocoa cake.
