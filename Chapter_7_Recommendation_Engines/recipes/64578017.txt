Title:
Grilled Parmesan Crusted Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup egg whites or 2 large eggs
1 cup dried Italian-style bread crumbs
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
4 boneless pork chops
Salt and pepper to taste
1 clove garlic finely minced

Directions:
Pour the egg whites or eggs (whisked) into a pie pan.
Add salt, pepper, and minced garlic to egg mixture
Mix the bread crumbs and cheese together in a large zip lock bag
Coat the pork chops in the egg mixture
Put them (1 at a time) into the zip lock bag
Coat the pork chops completely with the bread crumb-cheese mixture
Grill the chops over indirect heat for about 25 minutes or
Bake in a pre-heated oven at 350% for about 30 minutes in a foil lined dish.
Internal temperature should be 145 degree (F)
Let meat rest 3-5 minutes before serving
