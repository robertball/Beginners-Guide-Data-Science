Title:
Thin Mint Green Monster Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/854768.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 can coconut milk (chill first for better flavor)
one to two large handfuls fresh spinach
around 10 fresh mint leaves
1/4 cup raw cacao seeds
1 tsp peppermint extract
1 packet stevia
1 tsp hemp oil
1 whole frozen banana, in chunks
ice and water to taste

Directions:
Add ingredients to blender in order suggested, dropping in banana while blender is running.  Add ice till the smoothies is the proper texture (thick!) and water if needed.

Submitter: 
Angela Sackett  Superhotmama
Description: 
a DELICIOUS (though a bit odd looking)smoothie that is healthy and fills that chocolate-mint craving!
