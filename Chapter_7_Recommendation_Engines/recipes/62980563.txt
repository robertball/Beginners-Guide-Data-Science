Title:
Jim's Beer Battered Portobello Mushrooms with Chipotle Aioli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/738842.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large Portobello Mushrooms stemmed and gills cleaned
1 3/4 cups all purpose flour
2 -Tablespoons corn starch
1 Tablespoon baking powder
1/2 cup sesame seeds
2- Cups Goose island Honkers Ale
Oil for frying

Directions:
Heat the oil to 375Â°
Cut the mushrooms in 1/2 " slices
Whisk the beer with the flour, baking powder,corn starch and sesame seeds.The mixture should be thick and a little lumpy. Dip the mushrooms in the batter and fry 7-8 at a time in the oil about until golden brown ( about 5-6 min)

Description: 
If you like Portobello mushrooms and like them deep fried this is for you! Delicious!  Served with a Chipotle Aioli sauce for dipping.
