Title:
GRILLED GARDEN VEGTABLE CHICKEN Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 BONELESS/SKINLESS CHICKEN BREASTS
1 SLICED TOMATO
1 SLICED GREEN PEPPER
1 SLICED ONION
2 TSP FRESH GARLIC
1/4 CUP PARMASAN CHEESE
1/2 CUP ITALIAN DRESSING
SALT AND PEPPER TO TASTE

Directions:
PLACE CHICKEN BREASTS IN ALUMINUM FOIL. TOP WITH TOMATO, GREEN PEPPER AND ONION SLICES. SPRINKLE WITH CHEESE, SALT AND PEPPER. POUR ITALIAN DRESSING OVER.COVER AND COOK ON THE GRILL OVER MED - LOW HEAT FOR 45 MINS AND ENJOY!
