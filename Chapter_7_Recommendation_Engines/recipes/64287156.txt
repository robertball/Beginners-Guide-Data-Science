Title:
Dee's Roasted Carrot soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1920356.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large onion chopped
2 cloves of garlic crushed
2 1/2lbs of carrots, peeled and chopped
2 med. potatoes, peeled and chopped
1 tablespoon of herbs de provence
1 tsp salt and pepper
2 tablespoons of oil
6 cups of vegetable broth
1 cup light cream or milk

Directions:
In a large roasting pan combine onion, garlic, carrots and potates, drizzle with oil and spices.
Roast in 400F oven until vegetables are tender and starting to brown - approx 50 mins, turning once
Remove from oven (include scrapings) and put veg in a large pot, add broth and bring to a boil
Use a hand blender puree until smooth
Add milk/cream to desired consistency
