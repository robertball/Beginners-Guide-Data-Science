Title:
Magic Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 egss, separated and room temperature
1 tablespoon water
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter, melted and cooled
3/4 cup flour
2 cups milk, lukewarm

Directions:
preheat oven to 325F
beat egg yolks with sugar until light
add the tablespoon of water and drizzle butter in, continuing to beat for 1-2 minutes
add flour, fully incorporating it
add milk and continue beating on low until well mixed
set aside
whip egg whites until stiff
Gently, whisk egg whites into egg yolk mixture a third at a time until all egg whites are folded in
pour into greased pan
bake 60-70 min until lightly browned
cool completely (optional: sprinkle powdered sugar)
cut peices
