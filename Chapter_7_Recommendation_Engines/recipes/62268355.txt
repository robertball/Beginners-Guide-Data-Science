Title:
Kentucky Burgoo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/342922.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. pork (cubed)
1 lb. beef (cubed)
3 tbs. olive oil
1 ½ lbs. chicken (cut up)
1 qt. chicken broth
1 cup diced carrots
2 cups diced potatoes
15 oz. stewed tomatoes
¾ cup diced onion
½ cup diced celery
1 cup diced okra
1 diced green pepper
1 cup green beans
1 cup corn
3 cloves garlic
1 tbs. Worcestershire sauce
¼ cup cider vinegar
1 cup smoky barbecue sauce
3 bay leaves
1 tsp. salt
½ tsp. black pepper
hot sauce (to taste)

Directions:
In an 8 quart pot, brown pork and beef in olive oil.
Add chicken, chicken broth, potatoes and carrots then simmer 1 hour.
Stir in remaining ingredients and simmer 1 hour.
Remove bay leaves and serve in a bowl.

Submitter: 
TonyEditor
Description: 
This is my version of a traditional Kentucky stew recipie my brother brought back after going to school in Louisville. A favorite on Derby Day, or any day!
