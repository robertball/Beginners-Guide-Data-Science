Title:
Poppy seed Loaves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
2 cups sugar
1 1/4 cups vegetable oil
1 cup poppy seeds
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp. vanilla
2 tsp. almond extract
2 cups flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Beat eggs.
Add sugar and oil.
Add poppy seeds, milk, vanilla and almond extract.
Combine the remaining ingredients and stir in. Mix well.
Pour into two greased and floured loaf pans.
Bake about 1 hour @ 325 or until test done.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
My daughter shared this recipe from a co-worker. Delicious loaves with almond flavor!
