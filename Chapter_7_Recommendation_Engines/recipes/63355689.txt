Title:
Cinnamon Honey Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/892931.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup non-salted light butter, softened
1/4 cup confectioners' sugar
1/4 cup honey
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
In a medium bowl, whip room temperature butter. Combine butter, confectioners' sugar honey and cinnamon. Beat until light and fluffy.

Submitter: 
Jessica Onorati
Description: 
This is a creamy honey-cinnamon treat, and is absolutely wonderful on toast, bagels, muffins, pancakes, waffles.Modified from: Cinnamon Honey Butter
