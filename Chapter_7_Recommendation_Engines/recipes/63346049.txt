Title:
Cranberry Pork Medallions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pork tenderloin (about 1 pound), cut into 1/2" slices
3 TBL olive or vegetable oil
1 medium onion, finely chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
3 TBL sugar
3/4 cup apple juice
1/2 cup cranberry juice
1/2 cup fresh or frozen cranberries
2 tsp Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp minced fresh rosemary or 1/8 tsp dried rosemary, crushed
additional cranberries and fresh rosemary for garnish, optional

Directions:
In a nonstick skillet, cook pork in oil for 3-4 minutes on each side or until golden brown. Remove and set aside. In the same skillet, saute onion, garlic and sugar until onion is caramelized and tender. Stir in apple juice, cranberry juice, cranberries, mustard and rosemary. Bring to boil. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered for 5-6 minutes or until sauce is reduced by half. Return pork to pan; heat through. Garnish with additional cranberries and rosemary, if desired.
