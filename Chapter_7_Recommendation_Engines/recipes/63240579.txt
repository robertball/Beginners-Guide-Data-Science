Title:
Seared Scallops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds large sea scallops
3/8 t kosher salt, divided
3/8 t black pepper, divided
cooking spray
1/3 c dry white wine
1 T chopped shallots
1 T white wine vinegar
3 T chilled butter, cubed
1 t chopped fresh thyme
1 T parsley
1 T chives

Directions:
Heat a large cast iron skillet over med-high heat.
Pat scallops dry and sprinkle with 1/4 t salt and 1/4 t pepper; coat scallops with cooking spray.
Add scallops to preheated pan; cook 3 mins on each side until browned. Remove and keep warm.
Combine wine, shallots, vinegar in a saucepan; bring to a boil. Cook 5 mins or until liquid reduces to 1 T.
Reduce heat to low; add butter cubes 1 at a time whisking after each addition until butter is fully incorporated.
Stir in 1 T parsley, 1 T chives, 1 t thyme.
