Title:
Spicy vegan lentil quinoa soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/834159.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Lentil
1/2 Cup Quinoa
1 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped carrots
1/2 cup mushroom, any type
1 Tbsp Ginger powder
1 Tbsp Chili powder
1 Tbsp Cumin
4 Cups of water

Directions:
Mix all ingredients at once into a sauce pan, cover and cook for 45-60 minutes on medium-low. Serves 4ish.

Submitter: 
Lunchbach
Description: 
A vegan mix of lentil and quinoa with carrot, mushroom and celery in a spicy soup. Given ingredients serves 4.
