Title:
Jicama and Mango Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large jicama, cut into thick matchsticks
2 small mangoes, cut to similar shape and size as jicama
1/4 cup cilantro, chopped or torn into large pieces
1/4 cup fresh lime juice (juice of 3 or 4 limes)
3 Tablespoons splenda
1 teaspoon cumin powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
dash powdered cayenne pepper

Directions:
Peel jicama.  Cut into large matchsticks.
Peel mango.  Cut similar to jicama.
Cut or tear cilantro into large pieces, removing stems.
Add remaining ingredients and toss well.
Best if refrigerated overnight.  Toss again before serving.
