Title:
Oreo & Fudge Ice Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup fudge ice cream topping, warmed
1 8 oz tube Cool Whip
1 pkg chocolate flavor instant pudding
8 Oreo cookies
12 vanilla ice cream sandwiches

Directions:
Pour fudge topping into a medium bowl. Stir in 1 cup whipped topping and whisk until well blended. Add dry pudding mix and stir until well blended. If its too thick to spread add a little milk. Chop up the Oreo cookies into chunks and stir into pudding mixture. Arrange 4 of the ice cream sandwiches side by side onto a 24x12 inch piece of foil.Top with half the pudding mixture. Repeat the layers. Top the pudding mixture with the last 4 ice cream sandwiches. Frost the top and sides with the remaining whip cream, it doesn't have to be perfect. Bring up the sides of the foil and loosely pack. Freeze for 4 hours before serving. Let stand at room temperature to soten before serving.
