Title:
Angel food cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1small box of  sugar free inst. pudding (any flavor)
1pint of heavy whipping cream
milk to thin

Directions:
Use any premade angel food cake(slice it in half)
Beat the instant pudding and heavy whipping cream until it is mixed to a whipping cream coststency. If you need to thin the frosting, thin it with milk.
