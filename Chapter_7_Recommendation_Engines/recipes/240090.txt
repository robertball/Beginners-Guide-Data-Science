Title:
World's Easiest Refreshing Smoothie Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1/2 (16 ounce) can pineapple chunks, undrained
5 frozen strawberries

Directions:
Blend pineapple, reserved pineapple juice, and strawberries together in a blender until smooth.

Description: 
The pineapple adds a fresh, wake-me-up flavor, while the frozen berries making it cold and refreshing.
