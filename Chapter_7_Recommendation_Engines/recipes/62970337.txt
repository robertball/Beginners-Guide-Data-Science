Title:
Crazy cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the Crust:
1 C cracker crumbs (about 20 squares.)
4 T melted butter/margarine
1/4 t cinnamon
1/4 t nutmeg
Mix well and press into and up sides of a 9" pie plate.  Chill for one hour.
For the filling:
2 8oz pkg's cream cheese
3/4 C sugar
1+1/2 t vanilla
1 egg
1/4 C flour
1/2 C milk

Directions:
Preheat ovento 350 F. and place pan of hot water on lower rack. (helps prevent cracking)
Whip eggs
Gradually add sugar
Stir in vanilla
Mix in flour, cream cheese and milk until smooth
Pour into crust and bake about 40 min.
Chill completely
Top with any flavor topping, or eat plain!
