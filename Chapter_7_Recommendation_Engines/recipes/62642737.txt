Title:
Special Hamburger Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Miracle Whip
1/4 cup Mayonnaise
2 heaping tbsp. WISHBONE Deluxe French Salad Dressing
1/2 tbsp. Sweet Relish
2 heaping tsp. Dill Pickle Relish
1 tsp. granulated Sugar
1 tsp. dried, minced Onion
1 tsp. White Vinegar
1 tsp. Ketchup
1/8 tsp. Salt

Directions:
Mix all ingredients thoroughly in plastic container.
Microwave for 30 seconds and stir again.
Cover and refrigerate atleast 1 hr. before using to allow flavors to meld.
