Title:
HOMEMADE BLUEBERRY COOKIES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mix together.....
1 1/2 cup flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp cinnamon
Cream and add to flour mix....
2/3 cup Crisco Shortening
3/4 cup sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
Add....
1/4 to 1/2 cup milk to make consistency of drop cookie
Fold in 1 cup Blueberries

Directions:
Drop by teaspoonful onto greased cookie sheet.
Bake at 375 for 8-10 minutes
Hint: Use butter instead of shortening for a more chewy cookie!
