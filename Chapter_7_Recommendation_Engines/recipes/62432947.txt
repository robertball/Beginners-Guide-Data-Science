Title:
WW Chocolate Banana Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c nonfat milk
1 (3.5 oz) pkg SF instant banana pudding mix
1/2 t. vanilla
2 c. FF cool whip
1 reduced fat graham cracker crust
2 medium bananas
1/3 c FF chocolate syrup

Directions:
In large bowl, beat milk, pudding mix and vanilla with wire whisk for 1 minute. (Mixture will be thick). Whisk in whipped topping.
Spread 1/3 of pudding mixture in crust. Slice bananas on top of mixture. Carefully spread remaining pudding mixture over bananas.
Refrigerate at least 2 hours or until set.  Drizzle with chocolate syrup prior to serving.
