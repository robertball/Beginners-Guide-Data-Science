Title:
Italian Squash Casserol Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. hamburger meat, browned and drained
2 medium yellow squash, sliced
2 medium zucchini squash, sliced
1 small yellow onion, sliced
1 small jar Ragu spahetti sauce (I like Ragu Tomato & Sweet Basil)
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Spray an 8 x 8 baking dish with Pam and add browned hamburger meat.
2. Steam squash and onion in microwave until just soft, about 6 minutes.  Layer over hamburger meat.
3. Pour spaghetti sauce over squash and sprinkle with cheese.
4. Cook uncovered at 400 degrees for 30 minutes.

Description: 
If you like squash with an Italian flavor, you will enjoy this main meal dish.
