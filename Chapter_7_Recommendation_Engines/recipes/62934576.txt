Title:
Gehak Ballen (Meat Balls) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. Ground Beef
1 tsp. Nutmeg
2 eggs, unbeaten
2-3 rusks
Salt to taste

Directions:
Mix well and form ito balls.
Slowly fry in frying pan until done.
