Title:
Peanut Butter & Pistachio Pudding Flip Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pckg Pistachio pudding mix
1-2 cup Milk
1 cup peanut butter (creamy or crunchy)
1 cup crushed grahm crackers
1/2 Tbl butter
1/2 cup crushed walnuts (or other preffered nut)
1 cup light whipped cream
1/2 cup choc chips (optional)
Hershy's Choc Syrup

Directions:
Prepare crushed ingredients if needed. Set aside.
Melt butter.
Mix butter with grahms and press in bottom of 8x8 cassarole dish.
Soften peanutbutter almost to a liquid. (microwave or stovetop)
Add pistachio pudding mix to peanut butter.
Add 1 cup cold milk 1st and blend well. Electric mixer works best.
Add more milk as needed up to 2 cups. You want the mixture to be almost of pudding consistancy, not soupy.
Pour pudding mix over grahms.
Add chopped walnuts on top with optional choc. chips.
Spread whipping cream over top.
Drizzle choc. syrup over whip cream and drag a tooth pick up and down to create "pretty" effect.
freeze up to 1 hour and serve!

Frozen and goey, yummy and delicious! This pudding treat has the best of both worlds: Peanut Butter & Pistachio!
