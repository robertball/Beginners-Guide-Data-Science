Title:
Lemon Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups chicken broth
2 cups cooked rice
1/4 cup lemon juice
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 tbsp. dill weed (optional)

Directions:
Make chicken broth, add rice and lemon juice. Add can of soup and garnish with dill weed.
