Title:
Avocado Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package frozen corn kernels, thawed
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
2 jalepenos, minced
5 cloves garlic, minced
1/3 cup olive oil
1/4 cup lemon juice
3 tablespoons cider vinegar
1 teaspoon dried cilantro
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 avocados - peeled, pitted and diced

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix corn, red bell pepper, jalepeno, and onion.
In a small bowl, mix garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, cider vinegar, cilantro, salt and pepper. Pour into the corn  mixture and toss to coat. Cover and chill in the refrigerator 8 hours, or overnight.
Stir avocados into the mixture before serving.

Description: 
Cool, refreshing avocado slices are mixed with a marinated corn, red pepper and onion salsa. Serve this south of the border flavor explosion with tortilla chips.Modified from: Avocado Corn Salsa
