Title:
Salsa Fresco Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Tomatoes, Roma, Medium Dice_____3 Cups
Green Bell Pepper, Medium Dice__1 Cup
Onion Red, Medium Dice__________1/3 Cup
Onion, White, Medium Dice_______1/3 Cup
Cilantro, Fresh, Minced_________¼ Cup
Garlic, Minced__________________1 teaspoon
Tomato Paste____________________1/3 Cup
Lime Juice_______________½ Tablespoon+½ Teaspoon
Siracha Chili Sauce______½ Tablespoon
Cumin, Ground____________½ teaspoon
Chili Powder, Ancho______¾ Teaspoon
Sugar, White Granulated__2 ½ Teaspoon
Salt, Kosher, Coarse___½ Tablespoon
Black Pepper, Ground___½ teaspoon

Directions:
Gather all ingredients and equipment.
In a large bowl, toss together the Tomatoes, Peppers, onions, cilantro, and garlic; mix in the tomato paste until all of the ingredients are bound together.
Evenly fold in the remaining ingredients, then transfer to a storage container; refrigerate, and allow the blend to marinade overnight.
