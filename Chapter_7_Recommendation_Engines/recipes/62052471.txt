Title:
Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Top crust:  mix all ingredients together and set aside.
2 cups flour
1 cup sugar
3 eggs
2 tsps baking powder
1 tsp salt
2/3 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
Fill a 9x13x2 pan with thinly sliced apples.  Add 2/3 cup white sugar and 1/4 cup brown sugar.  Next, sprinkle all over with cinnamon.  Crumble the top crust mixture over apples.  Bake 45 minutes at 350 degrees or until brown.

Submitter: 
flossi
Description: 
This is a very easy and tasty apple crisp that tastes great warm served with ice cream.  I prefer to peel the apples first.
