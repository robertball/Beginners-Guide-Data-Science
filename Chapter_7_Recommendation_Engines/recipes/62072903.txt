Title:
Supreme Teriaki Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Vegetable Oil
1 box of Brown Sugar
1 small bottle Soy Sauce
12 oz. Pineapple Juice
1 clove of garlic, crushed

Directions:
Place all the ingredients in a bowl and whisk together.  Then, pour the marinade into a gallon size Zip-Lock Bag along with whatever you are marinating. Seal the bag and place in the refrigerator to marinate.  Let marinate for 12 - 24 hours.  Remove contents and cook as desired.

Submitter: 
CANDHMOM
Description: 
This marinade is the best I have ever tasted.  It works for chicken, pork and I find that it's best on London Broil or Flank Steak.
