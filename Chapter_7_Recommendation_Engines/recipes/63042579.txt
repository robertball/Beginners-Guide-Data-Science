Title:
Fruit Smoothy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 ounces vanilla yogurt
1/2 cup sugar
1 teaspoon lemon juice
Assorted Frozen Fruit
Whipped Cream (optional)

Directions:
Put vanilla yogurt, sugar, and lemon juice into food processor.
Fill the rest of it up with frozen fruit.
Blend until smooth.
Top with whipped cream. (optional)
