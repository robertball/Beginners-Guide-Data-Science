Title:
Tartar Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C mayonnaise
1/4 C chopped dill pickles
2 tsp onion powder
1/2 T dill

Directions:
In a small bowl stir together all ingredients.  Cover and chill for at least two 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Rochelle Brooks Thompson
Description: 
This light and creamy tartar sauce is wonderful on home battered and fried fish.
