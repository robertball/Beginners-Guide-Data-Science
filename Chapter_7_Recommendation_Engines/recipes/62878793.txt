Title:
Low Fat Spinach Stuffed Shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 oz packaged frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
1 16 oz container fat free cottage cheese
1 red pepper chopped
1 egg white
1 envelope Italian dressing mix
1 cup low fat shredded mozzarella cheese divided
20 jumbo pasta shells (for stuffing) cooked, drained and cooled
1 13 1/2 oz jar spaghetti sauce
Freshly grated Parmesan cheese to top with

Directions:
Saute the chopped red pepper until softened, then combine the first five ingredients in a bowl and mix in 1/2 cup of the mozzarella cheese.  Spread half of the sauce in a 13X9 baking dish.  Spoon cottage cheese mixture into shells and arrange in baking dish.  Top with remaining sauce and cheese.  Cover with foil.
Bake 40 min, uncovering after 30 min.  Top with freshly grated Parmesan cheese just before serving.
