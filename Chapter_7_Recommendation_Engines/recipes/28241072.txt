Title:
Holiday Wreaths Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter or margerine
10 oz. of regular marshmallows or 4 cups mini marshmallows
1 tsp. green food coloring
6 cups Kellogg's frosted flakes cereal or Corn Flakes
1 pack of cinnamon red candies
1 tub prepared vanilla frosting

Directions:
Melt butter or margerine in large suacepan over low heat.  Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted.  Remove from heat, then stir in food coloring.
Add cereal.  Stir until well coated.
Using 1/4 cup dry measure coated with cooking spray, evenly portion warm cereal mixture.  With sprayed fingers, quickly shape into individual wreaths.
Dot with cinnamon candies, using frosing to hold candies in place.  Store in a single layer in an airtight container.
