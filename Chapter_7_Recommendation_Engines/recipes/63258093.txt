Title:
Joyce's Peanut Butter Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese
1/2 c. peanut butter
1 c. powdered sugar
1 - 8 oz. carton of cool whip

Directions:
Whip together the cream cheese and peanut butter till smooth, then add the powdered sugar and mix well, then add the cool whip and mix well. Pour into a reg. pie crust or graham cracker crust. Then put the reese candy on top. I beak the candy in small pieces.

Submitter: 
Marjorie Taylor
Description: 
My friend took this to a funeral dinner and everyone loved it and it simple to make.
