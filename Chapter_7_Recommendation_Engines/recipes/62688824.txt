Title:
Zucchini Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded zucchini
2 eggs
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
1/2 cup parmesean cheese
1 clove garlic, minced
1 1/2 cups buttermilk baking mix
salt, ground black pepper, pinch cayenne and garlic salt to taste
oil for frying

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine zucchini, eggs, onion, and garlic. Blend in baking mix, salt and pepper.
Heat 1/2 inch of oil in deep-fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Drop batter by spoonfuls into hot oil, and fry until evenly brown and fluffy. Drain on paper towels. Sprinkle with salt before serving.
Serve with Chipotle Cream sauce: Combine in a food processor: 3 cloves roasted garlic (wrap unpeeled cloves in foil, bake 20 minutes at 350) 1/2 cup sour cream, 2 T mayo, 1 T chopped canned chipotle peppers (add up to 2 T if you like heat)

Submitter: 
Kelly
Description: 
A great way to use up the abundant zucchini in your garden. I freeze grated zucchini in 2 cup portions for winter Puffs!Modified from: Zucchini Puffs
