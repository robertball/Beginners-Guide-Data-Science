Directions:
Spanish RiceARoni Salad
Prepare on box of Spanish RiceARoni as directed on the package.
15 pimento stuffed olives, sliced
2 green onions, sliced
2 small jars marinated artichoke hearts, drained and chopped.  (Reserve the marinade)
1/3 cup mayonaise
Add the olives, green onions, artichoke hearts to the cooked rice mixture.
Mix the mayonaise with the reserved marinade and add to the rice mixture.
Serve at room temperature.
