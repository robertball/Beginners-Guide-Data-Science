Title:
Marmalade pork chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2C chopped onions
1/2C chopped green pepper
2C water
2C rice
1/2 tsp gr. cinnamon
2 small apples(cored and peeled and sliced thin
1-2lbs bonless, smoked pork chops
2Tbsp orange marmalade

Directions:
Saute the onion and the green pepper in a large skillet sprayed with pam. Add the water, rice, cinnamon. Turn of heat and cover and let sit 5min.  Then add the apples and the pork chops cover and cook until heated through. Add aditional water if nessary. Spread the orange marmalade on top of the pork chops. Cover and heat one to two minutes.
