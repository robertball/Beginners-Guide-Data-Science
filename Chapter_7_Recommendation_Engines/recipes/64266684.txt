Title:
Pimento Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz. softened cream cheese
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
8 ounces chopped pimentos, drained
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 cup mayonnaise, or more if needed

Directions:
Use food processor to mix, adding one ingredient at a time.  Refrigerate overnight to let the flavor develop.

Description:
