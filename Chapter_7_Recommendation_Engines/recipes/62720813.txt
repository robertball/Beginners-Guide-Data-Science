Title:
Joe's Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. uncooked chicken (can be breasts, thighs, etc.)
3/4 cup white wine
Juice of one lemon
3 cloves garlic; crushed
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. dried oregano
1 tsp. dried mint

Directions:
Mix wine, lemon juice, garlic, salt, oregano, and mint to create a marinade.
Place chicken in marinade and allow to marinate in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or up to 24 hours.
Preheat an outdoor grill.
When grill is hot, grill chicken until cooked through; basting with extra marinade as needed.

Submitter: 
DarcieA
Description: 
My in-laws got this recipe from a former Lebanese neighbor.  The flavors are simple and yet so amazingly flavorful.
