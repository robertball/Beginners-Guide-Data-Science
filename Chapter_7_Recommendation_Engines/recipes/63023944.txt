Title:
Roasted Pepper and Artichoke Tapenade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 7 oz jar roasted pepperm drained and coarsely chopped
1 6 oz jar marinated artichoke hearts drained, coarsely chopped
1/3 c olive oil
1/2 c minced parsley
1/2 c freshly grated parmesean
1/4 c drained capers
4 cloves garlic
1 tbsp lemon juice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in processor.
Use on/off pulses until well blended and finely chopped.
Season to taste with salt and pepper.
