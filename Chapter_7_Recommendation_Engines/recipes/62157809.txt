Title:
Gin Buckets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Liters of Diet 7-up
1 (12oz.) container frozen lemonade concentrate thawed
12oz. Gin

Directions:
In a large bowl combine bottle of 7-up, thawed lemonade concentrate, and 12 oz. of gin.  Stir together and enjoy!
