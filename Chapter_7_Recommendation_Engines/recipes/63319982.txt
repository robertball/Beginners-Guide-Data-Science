Title:
One Skillet Beef Stroganoff - Gluten Free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb Ground Beef or venison
1 Clove Garlic, minced
1/4 cup chopped onion
3 tsp beef bouillon granules (3 cubes)
8 oz gluten free lasagna noodels (about 8) broken into 1 in pieces
1 cup sour cream (about 8 oz)
2 tbsp rice flour
1/4 tsp ground black pepper

Directions:
In a large skillet with a lid, saute meat with farli and onion over medium heat until the mat is brown and the onion is tender and translucent.
Add 5 cups of water and bouillon to the skillet. Bring to a boil.
Add the lasagna noodles. Return to a boil. Reduce heat and cover. Cook for 15-20 minutes or until the noodles are tneder and water is absorbed.
Meanwhile, combine the sour cream, rice flour, 2 tbsp water and pepper in a small bowl. Set aside.
When the noodles are done, add the sour cream mixture to the skillet. Cook and stir over medium heat until bubbly. Cook and stir one minute more
