Title:
Spicy Tequila Lime Shrimp Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the Shrimp:
2 TBL extra virgin olive oil
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 tsp ground cumin
pinch red pepper flakes
1 lb medium shrimp, deveined
3 TBL high-quality tequila
salt, to taste
1 tsp dried chipotle powder
2-3 TBL fresh cilantro, chopped
For the Salad:
4 cups dark, leafy mixed greens
1/2 cup roasted red peppers, sliced thin
1/2 cup black beans, drained & rinsed
1 avocado, sliced thin
For the Dressing:
1/2 cup nonfat plain Greek yogurt
3 TBL high-quality tequila
1 TBL grated lime zest
1 tsp hot sauce
2 TBL fresh lime juice
pinch of sea salt to taste

Directions:
Add olive oil to a large skillet over medium heat. Once oil is hot, add chopped garlic and cook for about 1 minute. Add cumin and red pepper flakes, and let flavors blend together.
Add shrimp and toss. Carefully add tequila and cook until alcohol burns off. Season with salt, add dried chipotle powder. Once shrimp are cooked, add chopped cilantro, toos and put aside on a plate.
Toss mixed greens in a large salad bowl and add in roasted red peppers and black beans. In medium bowl, whisk all of the dressing ingredients together and alter to desired taste by adding additional hot sauce and salt. Tope with avocado slices and sauteed shrimp.
