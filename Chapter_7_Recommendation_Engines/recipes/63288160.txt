Title:
Sweet Potato Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 cups sweet potatoes
12 cups granulated sugar
16 eggs
4 cups oil
3 cups orange juice
15 cups flour
3 tablespoons kosher salt
3 tablespoons baking soda
3 tablespoons cinnamon
4 teaspoons nutmeg
2 teaspoons allspice
2 teaspoons ground cloves

Directions:
Preheat convection oven, 300 degrees.
Prep 2 sheet cake pans.
Cream sugar and potatoes. Add eggs, oil and juice. In a separate bowl combine dry ingredients. Add dry to wet, and mix 4 minutes. Scrape bowl twice.
Bake 60-90 minutes.

Submitter: 
Juli Carvi

Developed when we had too many left over baked sweet-potatoes.
