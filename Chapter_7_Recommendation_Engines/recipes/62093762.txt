Title:
Bread Machine Banana Bread/Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. heavy cream
3 bananas
1 t. vinegar
1/2 c. eggnog
1/4 c. oil
1 t. vanilla
2/3 c. brown sugar
1 1/2 c. flour
1/2 c. bread flour
1 t. soda
1/2 t. baking powder
1/2 t. salt
1/2 c. broken walnuts

Directions:
Mash bananas into cream with plastic fork in bread pan.  Add remaining ingredients in order listed.  Bake on quick bread cycle for 1 hour 40 min.  (Toothpick test for doneness is accurate.) Remove pan from machine and let set for 10 minutes. Cool on rack,then store airtightly in refrigerator. Slice and top with sweetened whipped cream,icecream, or homemade cinnamon applesauce. Bread freezes well.

Submitter: 
Hoosier Peas
Description: 
This banana bread is mixed and baked in the bread machine! Serve as a chilled dessert with sweetened whipped cream.
