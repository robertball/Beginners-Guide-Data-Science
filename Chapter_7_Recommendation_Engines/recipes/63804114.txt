Title:
Chondra's dahl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. yellow split peas
4 c. water
2 tsp. salt
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp. grated ginger
1/2 c. diced tomatoes
quarter of a squeezed lemon
1/2 tsp. turmeric
1/4 c. veg. oil
1 tsp. cumin seeds
1/2 tsp. mustard seed
1/2 tsp. fenugreek seeds
cilantro
jalapeño
Cooked rice

Directions:
Pressure cook salt, water, split peas for 5 minutes + cool down. Saute seeds in oil until fragrant;  remove, then add garlic and ginger; sauté. Add tomatoes. Combine all with split peas along with the lemon juice and turmeric.  Cook for 1/2 hour, adding water as needed. Garnish with cilantro and add jalapeño or siracha as desired. Serve with rice.

Wonderful on its own.  Serve veggies on the side.
