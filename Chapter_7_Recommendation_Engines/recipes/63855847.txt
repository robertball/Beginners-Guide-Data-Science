Title:
Linguine with Smoked Salmon in Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz packaged dried linguine, fettuccine, or spaghetti
6 oz thinly sliced, smoked salmon (lox-style) or cooked fresh salmon
2 green onions, thinly sliced
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tblsp margarine or butter
1 cup whipping cream or half n half
1 tblsp snipped fresh dill
1 tsp finely shredded lemon peel
1/4 tsp fresh ground black pepper
2 tblsp grated Parmesan cheese
Fresh dill spigs (optional)

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions
Meanwhile, cut the salmon into thin bite-sized strips and set aside
For sauce, in a large skillet cook the green onion and garlic in margarine or butter till tender.  Add salmon and cook for 1 minute.  Stir in cream or half n half, fresh dill, lemon peel, and pepper.  Bring to boiling; reduce heat.  Cook at gentile boil about 5 minutes or till the sauce slightly thickens.  Remove from heat.  Stir in Parmesan cheese.
Arrange the pasta on individual plates or a large platter.  Spoon the sauce over the pasta.  If desired, garnish with fresh dill sprigs.
