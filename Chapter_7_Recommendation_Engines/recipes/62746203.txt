Title:
Grandma Molly's Cowboy Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/637348.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef (or cubed beef)
1 tsp vegetable oil
1 large onion diced into 1/2" chunks
1 16 oz can pinto beans (optional jalapenos)
1 10 ozcan diced tomatoes
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Brown beef in the skillet with vegetable oil and onion.  Cook until beef is browned and onion is translucent.
Add pinto beans and diced tomatoes, turn burner to let simmer stirring occasionally.  Let it cook down until the broth is thick.
Bowl up and serve!

Submitter: 
T & L
Description: 
This is a simple yet scrumptious recipe that my Grandma Molly used to make in her cast iron skillet.  She passed it on to my mom who passed it to me, and now here it is for you to enjoy!
