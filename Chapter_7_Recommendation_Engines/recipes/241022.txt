Title:
Tasty Breakfast Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2074902.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1 banana, cut into chunks
2 fresh apricots, peeled and pitted
1/2 cup plain yogurt
2 teaspoons wheat germ
2 teaspoons oat bran
2 teaspoons maple syrup, or more to taste

Directions:
Blend milk, banana, apricots, yogurt, wheat germ, oat bran, and maple syrup in a blender until smooth and frothy.
