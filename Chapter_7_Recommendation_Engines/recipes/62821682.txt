Title:
Summer Fresh Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups watermelon, cut in bite size pieces or made with a melon ball scoop
2 cups cantaloupe, cut into bite size pieces or made with a melon ball scoop
2 cups strawberries, if strwberries are large cut in bite size pieces
2 cups peaches, peeled and cut into bite size pieces
1 cup grapes, any color
1 cup kiwi, cut into bite size pieces
2 cups fresh pineapple, cut in bite size pieces
1/4 cup orange juice concentrate, undiluted
1/4 cup limeade concentrate, undiluted
1/4 cup lemonade concentrate, undiluted
1 cup toasted pecans
large lettuce leaves, for serving

Directions:
Cut watermelon and cantaloupe into bite size pieces or using melon ball scoop, scoop out desired amount of watermelon and cantaloupe balls.
Peel and cut the remainder of fruit into bite size pieces.
Put all the fruit into a large bowl.
Gently blend the fruit, don't use metal spoons or spatulas, as it will cut the fruit.
Pour juices into measuring cup and add 1/4 cup cold water and stir until well blended.
Pour citrus juice over fruit and let the extra drain off.
Can be served individually on a bed of lettuce or in a large serving dish so everyone can help themselves.
If you are making individual servings, top each salad with a sprinkle of toasted pecans.
If you are serving the salad in a large serving bowl, after the fruit in put into the bowl, sprinkle the toasted pecans over the top of the fruit shortly before serving.

Description: 
A delicious, colorful blend of Summers fresh fruit topped with Citrus dresssing and finished with toasted Pecans.
