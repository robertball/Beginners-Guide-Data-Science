Title:
Reeces Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-2 1/2 cps powered sugar
-2 cups peanut butter
-1/2 cup brown sugar
-1 stick of melted butter
-1 teaspoon vinalla
-6 ounce bag of chocolate chips

Directions:
1. Mix all of the above into a long cake pan; melt 4 tablespoons of butter; add a 6 oz. package of chocolate; pour over mixture; place in freezer until set; then keep covered in refrigerator.
