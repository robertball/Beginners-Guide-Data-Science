Title:
DINOMITE'S Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Approx 24 Kale leaves (half minced, half whole softened and cooled)
3c cooked Quinoa
1 1/2c shredded Carrots
2 tbs minced Garlic
1 large onion minced
Salt and pepper to taste
approx 1/2c melted unsalted butter
5 beaten Eggs
Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375F
Combine Quinoa and eggs and butter, salt and pepper
mix in garlic, onions, carrots and minced kale
line a non stick muffin tin with whole softened kale leaves, spoon Quinoa mixture into kale leaves, fold leaves around mixture.
bake 25-30 min
let cool on a backing rack 5 minutes before serving
