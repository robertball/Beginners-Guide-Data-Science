Title:
Rice PIlaf with Rainbow Trout Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
RICE:
1 large carrot, diced
2 stalks celery, diced
3.5 mini bell peppers assorted colors (red yellow and orange), chopped
1/4 cup onion, diced
1 clove garlic minced
1 tsp vegetable oil
2/3 cup uncooked brown rice
2 cup water
1.5 tsp Selection Vegetable Bouillon Concentrate
TROUT:
2 100 g trout fillets with skin on
1 tsp Chefmaster 52% oil Margarine
2 tsp Realemon Juice
1 tsp minced garlic
dash salt & pepper

Directions:
RICE:
in a medium saucepan or large skillet(with cover), saute veggies starting with carrots and celery (as they take longer to cook than onion) in the vegetable oil,  then moving to peppers and onion and garlic saute about 5 minutes then add rice and saute until rice begins to brown, add water and bouillon then cook for 45 minutes.
meanwhile rice cooks
TROUT:
combine margarine lemon juice garlic salt and pepper in a medium flat bottom microwaveable dish, microwave for 30 second to melt margarine, then stir, then lay trout skin up in bowl  then marinade for 30 minutes(or remaining time between time between rice and 30 minutes), then last 15 minutes of rice lay trout on top of the rice flesh up, then cook for 15 minutes or till fish flakes with fork
serve fish on top of the rice
Makes 2 servings
