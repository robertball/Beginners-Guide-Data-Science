Title:
PECAN BRITTLE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1 cup + 2 tbsp sugar.
-1 cup butter (no oleo or margerine).
-1\4 + 1 tsp water.
-1 3\4 cup frozen pecans.
-1\2 tsp soda.

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a pan mixing together using a wooden spoon cooking it over HIGH HEAT until the mixture is "BROWN POKE" color!!!
Remove mixture from the heat.
Add 1 3\4 cup froze pecans.
Add 1\2 tsp of soda.
Spread the ingredients into a UNGREESSED cookie sheet, using a fork to do the spreading.
Cool ingredients and then break it up into bite size peices.
