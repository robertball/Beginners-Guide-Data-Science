Title:
Pesto Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985156.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (7 ounce) container BUITONI® Refrigerated Pesto with Basil
1 French baguette, cut in half lengthwise
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Sliced plum tomatoes (optional)

Directions:
PREHEAT broiler.
SPREAD pesto over bread; sprinkle evenly with cheese.  Top with tomatoes.  Place on baking sheet.
BROIL for 1 to 2 minutes or until bread is crisp and cheese is melted.  Cut into pieces.

Submitter: 
Buitoni®
Description: 
A little pesto goes a long way, and is marvelous for these quick and tasty appetizers.
