Title:
kahlua caramilk cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oreo cookie crumb crust OR 1 graham wafer crust
2 - 8 0z packages of cream cheese
1 - large tub cool whip  ( 1 litre )
1/2 cup kahlua
4 large cadbury caramilk chocolate bars

Directions:
Press crumb crust into a 8 or 9 inch springform pan.
Beat cream cheese well, add kahlua.  Once blended, fold in cool whip.
Chop 3 of the caramilk bars, fold into the cream cheese mixture.  Pour mixture into the springform pan containing the crust.  Chop remaining caramilk bar, and sprinkle of top of cheesecake.
Refridgerate for a couple of hours before service

Description:
