Title:
Coconut Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
1 1/4 cups water
1 teaspoon sugar
1 pinch salt
1 1/2 cups uncooked jasmine rice

Directions:
In rice cooker, combine coconut milk, water, sugar, and salt. Stir until sugar is dissolved.
Stir in rice and cook for about 20 minutes or until rice is cooked through.
