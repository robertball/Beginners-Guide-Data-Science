Title:
Borracho "Drunken" Pinto Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T olive oil
1/2 medium onion, finely chopped
1 stalk celery, chopped finely
1/2 green pepper, chopped
1/2 jalapeno pepper, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced (1 tsp)
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp cumin
1 tomato, chopped
1 T smoked soy "bacon bits" plus extra to garnish (optional)
2 15-oz cans pinto beans, rinsed and drained well
1 cup amber ale or water
salt to taste
1/2 c finely chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Heat oil over med heat in large sauce pot, and saute the onion, celery, green pepper, jalapeno, and garlic for about 5 min.  Add the chili pwder, cumin, tomato, and soy "bacon bits" to the pan and continue to saute for 5 more minutes
Add the beans with the ale to the pan.  Combine the ingredients well with a spoon to prevent the beans and vegetables from sticking.  Simmer the beans for 20 mintues, until they apper saucy but not runny. Season with the salt. Garnish the beans with the cilantro and extra soy "bacon bits" and serve.
