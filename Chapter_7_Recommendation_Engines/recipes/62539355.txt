Title:
Grandma McCoy's Onion and Wild Rice Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C cooked wild rice
3 C fresh Bread Crumbs -- fine
1 C Hot Water
2 Egg
1 Tsp Salt
1/4 Tsp Pepper
1/2 Tsp Nutmeg
1 Tsp Sage
2 Tsps Poultry Seasoning
4 Med Onion -- yellow or vidalia if available
1/2 C Margarine
1/2 C Celery With Tops -- chopped
1/4 C Parsley -- chopped

Directions:
Pour hot water over bread crumbs and wild rice. Add eggs and seasonings.  Cook onions, celery and parsley in margarine until onions are translucent and just starting to brown. Mix all ingredients. Stuff bird if desired, or bake in a casserole 1 1/2 hours in slow oven (350Â°), covered. Or wrap mixture in foil and place along side bird in roaster.

Submitter: 
Kellee Carr
Description: 
A wonderful Minnesotan tradition in our family... it isn't the holidays without this stuff!  Onions, wild rice, and celery make this a delicious accompaniment to turkey.
