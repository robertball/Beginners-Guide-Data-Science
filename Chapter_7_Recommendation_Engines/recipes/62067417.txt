Title:
Sourdough French Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T yeast
1 1/2 c warm water
1 c sourdough starter
5 c flour, divided
2 T sugar
2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
cornmeal
1 T butter

Directions:
Feed sourdough starter and let sit at room temperature 8-12 hours.
Dissolve yeast in warm water.  Mix with starter, 4 cups flour, sugar, and slat.  Cover and let rise til doubled, about 1 hour.
Mix 1 c flour with baking soda and stir into dough, adding enough to make a stiff dough.  Knead, adding flour as needed, until smooth and shiny.
Shape into two long loaves and place on a baking sheeat liberally sprinkled with cornmeal.  Let rise again until doubled, 1 - 1 1/2 hours.
Brush top with cold water and make several slashes along the top of the loaves.  Bake at 400 degrees for 25 min, until a dark almost brown; place a small pan of water in the bottom of the oven while baking.  Brush with melted butter and crisp in over 3-5 minutes more.
