Title:
Savory Greek Roasted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole cut up chicken rinse and pat dry, remove giblets
3/4 to 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
Juice from 2 lemons (about 1/2 cup)
4 lbs. small red potatoes halved
6 garlic cloves whole cleaned
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon oregano

Directions:
Wash the chicken and drain well.
Place the chicken and potatoes in a large roasting pan .
In small bowl combine olive oil,lemon juice,salt, pepper,garlic powder and oregano with a wisk.
Pour over chicken & potatoes and turn to coat all sides. Scatter garlic cloves in pan.
Roast in a preheated, 500 degree oven (uncovered) until chicken is golden and beginning to brown (about 40 min.) Lower oven temp to 350 degrees and continue roasting for about another 30 minutes, or until potatoes are soft.

Submitter: 
CSavaso
Description: 
Greek Style Roasted Chicken with simple but savory ingredients that make for a beautiful presentation and perfect company dinner. I make this for my family every week. It's easily doubles and makes terrific leftovers.
