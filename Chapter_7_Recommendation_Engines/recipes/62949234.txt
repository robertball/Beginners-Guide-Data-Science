Title:
Sweet Curried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup & 2 TBLS flour
2 TBLS & 1 1/2 tsp curry powder
2 1/2 tsp garlic powder
2 1/2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breast, diced
1 (14 oz) can coconut milk
1/4 cup & 1 TBLS olive oil (separated)
3-4 (medium size)golden delicious apples; cored & chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 1/2 tsp Garam Marsala
Optional toppings:
chopped pistachios, peanuts, golden raisins, green onions (diced), coconut flakes, mango chutney

Directions:
Combine flour, curry & garlic powder in zip bag; add chicken and coat; shake off excess; set on plate to prepare to fry
Heat 1/4 cup oil in deep skillet on medium high heat; fry coated chicken in batches making sure not to over crowd and all sides are golden
Remove used oil from skillet; add 1 TBLS of oil; saute apple & onion for 2-3 minutes until tender crisp
Stir in brown sugar, Garam Marsala & coconut milk
Reduce heat to medium low; cover & simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Serve over rice with optional toppings
