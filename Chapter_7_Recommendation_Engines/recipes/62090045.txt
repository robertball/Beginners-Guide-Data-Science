Title:
Black Bean Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon oil
1 tablespoon Chili Powder
1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
4 cups canned Black Beans (drained and rinsed)
1 small can mexican corn (drained)
hot sauce to taste (optional)
2 cups mild enchillada sauce
8 Burrito sized Tortillas
4 cups mexican cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
In a med. saucepan add oil, spices, blackbeans, and corn.Cook until heated through and remove from the heat.
Pour 1 cup of enchilada sauce in bottom of large (or 2 med.) cassarole dish.
Heat a large skillet over med. heat.
Place one tortilla at a time in pan and heat until soft, about 10 seconds.
To prepare:Sprinkle cheese across the middle of the tortilla
2 heaping spoonfuls of the black bean mixture (it may look like a little but it expands when it bakes)
Sprinkle with cheese on top of the black beans.
To Fold the tortillas:Take the left and right ends of the tortilla and fold them over the mixture.
Take the edge closet to your body and fold over, and begin to roll and place tortilla fold side down in the dish. It should look more like a square than a burrito.
When finished with rolling the tortillas, pour the remaining enchilada sauce over the enchilada's and sprinkle with cheese.
Bake for 20-30 minutes.
