Title:
Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages plain oatmeal
1/2c. finely chopped Granny Smith apple
2 Tbsp. chopped walnuts
2 tsp. ground cinnamon
Hot water

Directions:
Combine ingredients and cook in microwave according to package directions on oatmeal package.
