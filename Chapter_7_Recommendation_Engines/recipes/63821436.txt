Title:
Bruschetta Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1084690.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts
Ingredients to marinate chicken...
1/4 c. olive oil
1 tsp. balsamic vinegar
1 tsp. Son of Italy Italian Seasoning
1 clove garlic, minced
cracked black pepper and sea salt to taste
For the bruschetta...
2 Roma tomatoes, chopped
1/8 c. onion, finely chopped
8-10 basil leaves, chiffonade
1-2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp. balsamic vinegar
Cracked black pepper and sea salt to taste
Shredded Parmesan if desired

Directions:
Filet chicken breasts to create thinner pieces.  Whisk together marinade ingredients in baking dish.  Place chicken in dish and turn to coat.  Place in fridge for at least 20 minutes, longer to increase flavor.
Mix bruschetta ingredients, minus the Parmesan.   Cover and place in fridge.
Cover baking dish with foil and bake chicken at 350 degrees for 25 minutes or until internal temperature of chicken has reached 165 degrees.  Remove foil, cover chicken with bruschetta mixture, and some shredded Parmesan if desired, return to oven and cook 3-5 minutes more.
