Title:
Veggie Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 medium onion, diced small
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 Red Pepper, diced small
1 cup finely diced mushrooms
2 TBSP fresh thyme
Olive Oil
1 3/4 cup chicken broth
1 cup rice

Directions:
Saute onion in olive oil over low/medium heat until translucent.
Add Red Pepper, saute for 2 minutes.
Add mushrooms, garlic and thyme, cook until soft.
Add chicken broth and rice. Bring to a boil then turn heat to low, cover & simmer for 20 minutes.
(This recipe is wonderful with Rosemary instead of Thyme, or both!)

Submitter: 
Mary
Description: 
This is a quick and easy and delicious veggie rice recipe. It is also very versitle, just open your fridge, grab your favorite veggies and herbs and go nuts!
