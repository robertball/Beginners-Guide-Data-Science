Title:
Easy Authentic Chinese Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Rice noodles
Water chestnuts, 1/2 cup
Bean sprouts, 1 cup
3 garlic cloves, minced
Ginger, 1 teaspoon, sliced or minced
1 chili, sliced
Salt and pepper
Soy sauce, 2 Tablespoons
Sesame oil, 1 Tablespoon
Dry sherry, 1 Tablespoon
Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Directions:
Soak the rice noodles in water for 10 minutes.
Drizzle olive oil in a saute pan and add the garlic, ginger, chilies, bean sprouts and stir for 5 minutes.
Add the noodles, salt and pepper, soy sauce, dry sherry, and sesame oil and stir for 5 minutes. Serve.

Submitter: 
IMANKAY
Description: 
This is such an easy recipe for Chinese noodles, you can add any other types of vegetables you choose.  This is garlic, ginger, bean sprouts, water chestnuts and more cooked with rice noodles.
