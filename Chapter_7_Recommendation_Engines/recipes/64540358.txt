Title:
Chocolate Almond Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3157024.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup GF rolled oats
1/2 cup almond butter (any nut butter can be used)
1/3 cup light honey
1/4 cup mini chocolate chips
2 Tbsp flaxseed
2 Tbsp chia seeds

Directions:
Stir all ingredients together in a bowl
Refrigerator 30 minutes
Remove from refrigerator & scoop into balls
Store in the refrigerator up to a week or in the freezer a month

Submitter: 
Megan Olson
Description: 
Chocolate Almond Bites made with oats, almond butter, chocolate, flax and chia seeds are a healthy no-bake snack for those with a sweet tooth. The perfect lunchbox friendly snack bite!
