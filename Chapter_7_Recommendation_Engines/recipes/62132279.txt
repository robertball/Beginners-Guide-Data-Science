Title:
Sweet and Sour Chicken Made Simple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 boneless skinless chicken breasts cut into cubes
1 cup each red and green pepper strips
1 8 oz.can chunk or tidbit pineapple
Sauce
1 Tablespoon cornstarch
1/4 c soy sauce
3 Tablespoons Vinegar
3 Tablespoons brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon ginger
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
Enough steamed white rice for four

Directions:
Start your rice, meanwhile saute chicken in hot oil until no longer pink. Add peppers and stir fry 1-2 minutes. Drain pineapple juice into a bowl, add enough water to make a cup. Add pineapple to chicken/pepper mix. Whisk sauce ingredients with pineapple juice, until smooth. Add to pan, and simmer until it thickens. Serve over rice.
