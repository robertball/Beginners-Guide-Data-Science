Title:
Ricotta blintzes with ligonberry syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
BATTER: 1/2 CUP all purpose flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
salt
4 large eggs
1/2 cup milk
1 tbsp sugar
1 tbsp unsalted butter,melted
FILLING: 3 cups fresf ricotta cheese
1/4 cup sugar
3/4 tsp cinnamon
SYRUP: one 10oz jar ligonberry jam
1 cup pure maple syrup
Confectioners sugar for dusting

Directions:
Make batter: Whisk flour,baking powder, and a pinch of salt in a bowl. In another bowl, whisk eggs, milk, and sugar. Whisk the flour and eggs together, whish in the butter. Cover and let stand for 1 hour.
Make filling: In a bowl, mix together ricotta, sugar and cinammon.
Prepare syrup: In a saucepan, simmer the jam and maple syrup over moderate heat until slightly reduced, 5 minutes.
Heat 2 8 inch non stick skillets. Spray with vegetable oil spray and 2 tbsp of batter to each.  Swirl the skillets to distribute the batter and cook over moderate heat until golden brown on the bottom, 1 minute. Flip the crepes and cook until brown spots appear on the bottom. Transfer the crepes to a cookie sheet. Repear with the remaining batter (20 crepes)
Preheat the oven to 350.Line another cookie sheet with parchment. On a work surface, spread 2 rounded tbsps of the filling in the center of each crepe. Fold each crepe to form a rectangular packet. Arrange the blintzes on a cookie sheet seams down. Bake until hot.
Rewarm the syrup. Place 2 blintzes on each plate and dust with confectiners sugar, pour the warm syrup over and serve.
