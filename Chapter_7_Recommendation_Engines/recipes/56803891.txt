Title:
Fruit Dip II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package light strawberry cream cheese, softened
1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme
1 cup cool whip
2 T. orange juice
mix cream cheese, o.j. and marshmallow creme thoroughly before folding in the cool whip

Directions:
Using an electric mixer, blend cream cheese and marshmallow creme until mixed thoroughly.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a really light and fluffy fruit dip that is great at parties, holidays, or any time! Serve with fruit of your choice.Modified from: Fruit Dip II
