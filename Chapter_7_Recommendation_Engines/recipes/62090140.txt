Title:
BBQ Ham Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb diced Ham
1.5 lbs cooked sweet potatoes, cut into 1 inch chunks
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 c. BBQ sauce
1/4 c. Heavy Cream

Directions:
Combine ham and sweet potatoe chunks in a bowl. Carefully mix together without mashing the sweet potatoes. Put Ham mixture into 9x13 baking dish. Sprinkle with Cheddar. In a small bowl combine BBQ sauce and cream. Pour sauce over casserole. Bake in a 350 degree oven for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jessica53214
Description: 
I created this recipe trying to use up pantry items. It has since been a favorite! Cheddar Cheese, sweet potatoes,ham and a creamy bbq sauce make a flavorful dish!
