Title:
great northern bean soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil, 2 15 ounce cans great northern beans,5 cloves garlic minced, 1 large onion chopped, 4 cups vegetable broth, 2 carrots chopped, 1 celery stalk chopped, 1 large potato chopped, 1 tablespoon basil, 1 teaspoon sea salt, 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper, dash of thyme, 2 bay leaves

Directions:
1. Heat olive oil in pot. Saute garlic and onion in oil. 2. Add carrots and celery. 3. Add veggie broth, potato and spices. 4. Bring to boil then simmer till veggies are almost soft. 5. Add beans and simmer another 20 minutes.
