Title:
Big Ray's Tangy Tomato Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1131731.jpg
Ingredients:
6 bone-in pork loin chops (8 ounces each)
1 Tbl canola oil
1 large onion, sliced
1 large sweet red pepper, sliced
1 jar (4-1/2 ounces) sliced mushrooms, drained
1 can (28 ounces) diced tomatoes, undrained
1 can (4 1/2 oz) green chiles
1 Tbl brown sugar
1 Tbl balsamic vinegar
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown pork chops in oil. Transfer to slow cooker. Layer onion, red pepper and mushrooms over pork chops.
In a large bowl, combine the tomatoes, green chiles, brown sugar, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper; pour over pork and vegetables.
Cover and cook on low for 8-9 hours or until pork is tender.
