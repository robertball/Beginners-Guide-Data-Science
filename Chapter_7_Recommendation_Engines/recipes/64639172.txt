Title:
Penne Pasta With Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4531917.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound  penne pasta
2-3 tablespoons olive oil
2 nice pats of butter( i use Earth Balance, lactose intolerant)
1 medium onion chopped
2-3 cloves of garlic chopped
2 cups diced ham
zest of a medium lemon( about 2 tablespoons)
fresh parsley chopped (if desired)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cook pasta in a big pot with about five quarts of water
salt the water after it comes to a boil then add the pasta
While the pasta cooks heat oil in a big pan or skillet on medium high heat
Add a pat of butter, when butter is melted add the onion, saute for 5 minutes
Add garlic and saute for 2 minutes
Add ham and heat through
When is pasta is cooked, drain and add to frying pan and stir
Add the lemon zest, add parsley if using
Add the other pat of butter, stir till glossy adding more butter if needed
Serve with a tossed salad and fresh Italian Bread.
