Title:
Salmon Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1027950.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can (15-16oz.) Pink Salmon
1 Egg
1/3 cup chopped onions
1/2 cup of flour
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
Corn Meal
Shortening

Directions:
Drain Salmon reserving liquid
In a medium bowl mix together Salmon, egg and onion.
Stir in flour
Mix baking powder and 2 Tbsp. of reserved liquid together.
And mix into Salmon mixture.
Mixture should hold shape pretty well when rolled into a ball, if to soft add a little more flour. If to firm, add a Tbsp. of liquid at a time until softer.
Heat 2 to 3 cups of shortening or oil in a skillet on medium heat.
Form mixture into small patties and coat with corn meal.
Fry until golden brown turning once.

Submitter: 
Candis Schindler Swan
Description: 
My husband loves Salmon Patties and I am always finding ways to lighten it up to suit my taste. This recipe taste lighter and crispier.
