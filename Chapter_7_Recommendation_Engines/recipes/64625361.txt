Title:
Pan Seared Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb filet or steak of choice
1 T ghee
1 T butter
2-3 cloves garlic smashed with skin on
rosemary
salt
pepper

Directions:
preheat oven to 400
bring steak to room temperature, 30-45 minutes
salt and pepper each side of steak
put cast iron pan on medium-high heat
melt 1 T ghee and 1 T butter
when bubbles, add steaks, rosemary sprigs, garlic to pan
sear each side approximately 2 minutes
remove from heat
swirl pan and spoon ghee/butter over steaks
put pan in preheated oven for approximately 5 minutes for medium rare
remove from oven, put small pat of butter on steak, cover with foil and let sit for 5-10 min
serve
