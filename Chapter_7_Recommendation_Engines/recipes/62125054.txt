Title:
Creamy Feta Salad Dressing or Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups Feta Cheese
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 green onion, chopped
1 teaspoon chopped fresh dill
1 teaspoon dried oregano
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons pepper

Directions:
Mash Feta and Milk together.
Put Feta mixture in food processor and add all other ingredients.
Let sit, covered in the fridge a few hours.
Add milk too thin out if making dressing to liking.

Submitter: 
Jasmine Gonzalez
Description: 
A definete for Feta Cheese Lovers! Very creamy and tasty with a delicious Feta taste.
