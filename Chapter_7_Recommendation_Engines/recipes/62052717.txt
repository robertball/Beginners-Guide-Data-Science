Title:
Granny's Hot Water Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups self-rising cornmeal
1 cup self-rising flour
1 egg
1 cup hot water

Directions:
Grease a 10 inch iron skillet.  Mix all ingredients together and por into skillet.  Bake on 375 degrees until golden brown. Note:  The bread will appear not to be cooked through on the inside.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
My grandmother used to make this for me, and I absolutely loved it.  Hope you too will enjoy it. Terriffic served with stewed tomatoes.
