Title:
Pioneer Ham Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups ham, cubed
1 cup frozen peas, thawed
2 cups cooked rice
1/2 cup chopped onions
2 TB chopped pimento
1 can cheddar cheese soup
1/2 cup milk
Black pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and spoon into a casserole dish.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
Leftover ham from the holidays make this a great meal.  Ham and cheese go together well in this quick casserole.  A nice green lettuce salad and you have a perfect meal.
