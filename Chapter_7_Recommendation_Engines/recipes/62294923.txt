Title:
Pepper Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. cooked rice
1 lb. beef round steak
1 Tbsp. paprika
2 Tbs. cornstarch
2 Tbsp. butter/margarine
2 green peppers (cut into strips)
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
2 tomatoes
1-1/2 c. beef broth (1 can)
1/4 c. soy sauce
1 c. sliced green onions
1 can water chestnuts (OPTIONAL)
1/4 c. water

Directions:
While rice is cooking, pound steak to 1/4 inch thickness. Cut into 1/4 inch wide strips. Sprinkle meat with paprika and allow to stand while preparing the rest. Using a large skillet, brown meat in butter. Add garlic and broth. Cover and simmer 30 minutes. Stir in onions, green peppers and water chestnuts. Cover and cook 5 minutes more. Blend cornstarch, water and soy sauce. Stir into meat mixture. Cook and stir until clear and thickened (about 2 minutes). Add tomatoes and stir gently. Serve over cooked rice.
