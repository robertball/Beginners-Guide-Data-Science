Title:
Green Peppers & Ham Scramble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole egg plus 2 egg whites
3 slices (2oz.) ham, turkey bacon, or turkey sausage, cut into thin strips
1/2 bell pepper, chopped
1 oz. pepper jack cheese, shredded
2 scallions, chopped
Dash of salt & pepper

Directions:
Scramble eggs in a bowl. Add meat, bell pepper, scallions, andcheese.
Add dash each of salt & pepper (if desired).
Spritz microwaveable bowl with nonstick cooking spray and pour in egg mixture.
Microwave on medium for 3 minutes.
