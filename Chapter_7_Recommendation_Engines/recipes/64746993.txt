Title:
Baked Cheese Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cheese slices of your choice

Directions:
cut cheese slices into 4 squares
put on parchment paper on cookie sheet and leave out 36-48 hours
sprinkle with seasoning if desired (dill, herb, taco, red pepper flakes, oregano, cumin, chipotle etc)
bake at 325 for 20 min
