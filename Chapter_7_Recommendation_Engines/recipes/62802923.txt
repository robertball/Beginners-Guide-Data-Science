Title:
Meatball Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 C dry egg noodles
1 C sliced mushrooms
2 Tbsp veg oil, divided
1 C diced onion
1/2 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp cayenne
1 Tbsp butter
1/4 C dry white wine
2 Tbsp flour
2 C chicken broth
Juice of 1/2 lemon
2 Tbsp mustard
1/4 C sour cream
1 Tbsp fresh dill
Meatballs
S & P

Directions:
Boil lg pot of salted water for noodles
Saute mushrooms in 1 Tbsp oil, md heat to brown, 5 min
Saute onion, paprika, cayenne in remaining oil, 5 min
Prepare noodles; drain, toss w/ butter; set aside
Deglaze skillet w/ wine; Simmer till nearly evaporated. Sprinkle flour over onions and stir
Whisk in broth; add lemon juice and mustard; simmer 8 min
Stir in cooked mushrooms, sour cream. dill, and meatballs. Cook until meatballs are heated through. Season w/ s & p
