Title:
Restaurant-Style Italian Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/970473.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Italian-style salad dressing
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1 tablespoon white sugar

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, whisk together the bottled dressing, garlic, basil, oregano and sugar. Refrigerate at least 24 hours before serving.
