Title:
Tomato Basil Crab Bisque Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/922203.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp Butter
10 oz Crab Meat, cooked and shredded
1 Tomato Chopped
1/3 cup + 2 Tbsp Chopped fresh basil
2 garlic Cloves
1 1/2 Cup Clamato Juice
1 cup Cream
1/2 Cup Ketchup
1/4 Cup bottled Clam Juice
3/4 Cup water
2 tbsp lemon Juice
2 Tbsp Old Bay Seasoning
1/4 tsp Tobasco

Directions:
Melt Butter in large pot
Add 3/4 of crab, tomato and 1/3 cup basil, garlic
Saute for 2 min
Whisk in Flour
Stir 2 min
Whisk in Clam Juice and next 5 ingredients.
Reduce heat and simmer until slightly thickened, about 10 min
Cool Slightly
Puree either in blender or with a wand
Return to pot
Add Water & Lemon Juice
Serve with a sprinkle of Crab and Basil.

Submitter: 
Susan Murdoch
