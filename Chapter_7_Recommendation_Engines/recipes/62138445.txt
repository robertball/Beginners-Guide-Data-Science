Title:
spinach madeline Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 10oz packages frozen chopped spinach
2 tbsp flour
4 tbsp butter
1/2 cup evaporated milk
1/2 cup reserved spinach liquid
8 oz chopped pepper jack cheese
2 tbsp  chopped onion
3/4 tsp celery salt
3/4 tsp garlic powder
1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
1  cups buttered bread crumbs

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees                             cook spinach according to package directions, drain and reserve liquid.
in large saucepan melt butter and add flour.
add onions and let cook 1 min until slightly softened, do not let this burn.
add evaporated milk and 1/2 cup spinach liquid.
cook on low heat. stirring constantly until smooth and thick.
add seasoning and cheese and stir until melted.
add cooked spinach and stir until all combined.
pour into  baking dish and top with buttered bread crumbs.
bake for 30 min or until top browns and edges are bubbly
