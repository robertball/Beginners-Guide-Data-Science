Title:
Kids-Only Pepperoni Pizza Bagels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990369.jpg
Ingredients:
4 plain bagels, halved
24 slices (1/2 pkg.) OSCAR MAYER Pepperoni Slices
1/2 cup shredded POLLY-O Whole Milk Mozzarella Cheese
1/2 cup DI GIORNO Marinara Sauce

Directions:
Top each bagel half with 3 slices pepperoni.  Sprinkle each with 1 tablespoon cheese.  Top each with 1 tablespoon sauce.
Place bagels on a cookie sheet that has been sprayed with no stick cooking spray.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 10 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bagel is golden brown.
