Title:
Trishie's Eggs Gang-Up-a-Dict Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/507412.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 C mashed potato
1/4 C flour
1/2 small apple
2/3 C ham chopped
1/4 tsp cinnamon
2 T chopped onion
3 T butter divided
1 egg
3 T hollandaise sauce (ready made)
1 glass of milk

Directions:
Melt 1T of butter in a hot pan and add the apple and ham and cinnamon and cook till apple is soft.
Mix the mashed potato, onion & apple mix with the flour and roll out on a floured surface to about 1/3" thick and using a cookie cutter cut into rounds.
Melt 2T of butter in a hot pan and gently fry the "potato-cakes" until golden on each side.
Meanwhile bring a pan of water to a rolling boil and poach your egg.
Remove the potato-cakes from the pan and place on a plate top with the poached egg and spoon the hollandaise sauce over the top.
Serve with the glass of milk!
