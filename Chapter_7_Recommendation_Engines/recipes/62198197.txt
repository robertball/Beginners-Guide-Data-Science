Title:
Thanksgiving Brussels Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. Brussel Sprouts
1 Tbsp plus 1 tsp. kosher salt
1/4 cup unsalted butter
4 Tbsp capers
2 Tbsp lemon juice
1/2 tsp fresh grnd pepper
2 Tbsp grated parmesan

Directions:
1. Peel off outside leaves of Brussel Sprouts and cut off stem.  Core where stem was with X.  Cut sprouts in half
2. Blanch Sprouts for 10 minutes in 2 quarts boiling water seasoned with Tbsp of salt.
3. In saute pan brown butter and add spouts.  Carmelize sprouts on med high heat for about 10 minutes.
4. Remove sprouts and add capers and lemon juice.  Add remaining salt and pepper.  Spinkle with parmesaen.
