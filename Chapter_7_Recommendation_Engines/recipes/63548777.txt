Title:
Grandma Jean's Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6260574.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup macaroni noodles
1 cup milk
1 Tbsp. margarine or butter
1 Tbsp. flour
Salt and pepper
1 cup grated cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
Boil macaroni in salted water until tender (drain and rinse well).
Make a rue with margarine and flour in a saucepan, then add remaining sauce ingredients. Cook until thick.
Pour sauce over noodles in casserole dish and bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
