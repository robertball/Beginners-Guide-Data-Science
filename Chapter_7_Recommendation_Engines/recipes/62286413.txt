Title:
Okra Pilau Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 bacon slices, chopped
1 cup sweet chopped onion
8 oz. frozen slice okra
8.5 oz. ready to serve Cajun-style Rice (I used Aldi Jambalaya Rice)

Directions:
Cook bacon in a large skillet over medium-high heat 5-7 minutes or until crisp; remove bacon and drain on paper towels, reserving 2 tablespoons dripping in skillet.
Saute onion and okra in hot dripping over medium-high heat for 6-8 minutes or until tender.
Prepare rice according to package directions.  Stir rice and crumbled bacon into okra mixture in skillet.
