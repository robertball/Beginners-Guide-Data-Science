Title:
Jerre's Black Bean and Pork Tenderloin Slow Cooker Chili ala Laura Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds pork tenderloin, cut into 2 inch strips
1 small onion, coarsely chopped
1 small red bell pepper, coarsely chopped
3 (15 ounce) cans black beans
1 (16 ounce) jar salsa
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon ground cumin
2 teaspoons chili powder
1 bag spinach

Directions:
Combine pork tenderloin, onion, red pepper, black beans, salsa, chicken broth, oregano, cumin, and chili powder in a slow cooker. Set to Low and cook for 8 to 10 hours. Add spinach in last hour.
Break up pieces of cooked pork to thicken the chili before serving.

Submitter: 
Laura Leh
Description: 
Tender pork and black beans stew all day in your slow cooker. Just combine the ingredients, set the cooker to low, and 8 to 10 hours later enjoy a rich and delicious chili.Modified from: Jerre's Black Bean and Pork Tenderloin Slow Cooker Chili
