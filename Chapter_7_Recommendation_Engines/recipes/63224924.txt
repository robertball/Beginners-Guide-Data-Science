Title:
Frankfurters Vegetable Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Oil - 3tbsp
Garlic - 1 pod, chopped
Onion - 1 medium, chopped
Potato - 1,cut into small cubes
Capsicum - 1/2cup,cut into small cubes
Mushroom - 1/2 cup
Carrots - 1/2 cup,
Frankfurters - 4, cut into small pieces
Tomato Ketchup - 1-2 tbsp
Red chilli powder - 1tsp
Coriander Powder - 1tsp
Garam Masala - 1/4 tsp
Salt - to taste

Directions:
In a pan add oil, shallow fry potatoes and keep aside.
Heat oil and add chopped onion and garlic, fry for 1 min.
Add Carrots, Mushrooms and Capsicum, mix well and cook.
Add red chilli powder, salt, coriander powder and garam masala.
Add potatoes, franfurters and ketchup. Mix them well.
Cover and simmer over medium flame for 3 minutes.
Serve hot with chappati, to make rolls.
