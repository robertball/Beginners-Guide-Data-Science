Title:
Licorice Cookie Strips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter (no substitutions), softened
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 egg
2 1/2 cups flour
2 tsp. aniseed
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
Cream butter and sugars; beat in egg.
Mix dry ingredients and add to creamed mixture; mix well.
Stir in nuts.
Divide dough into 10 portions; shape each into a 12" log.
Place 3" apart on baking sheets.
Flatten with a fork to 1/4" thickness.
Bake @ 350 x 8-10 min. or until golden brown.
Cool for 5 minutes; cut diagonally into 1" slices.
Cool on wire racks.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Licorice isn't everyone's favorite, but I love it! so naturally, this crispy cookie recipe caught my attention.
