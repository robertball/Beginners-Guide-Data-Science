Title:
freezer corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 c corn
1 lb. butter
pint half n half

Directions:
18 c. raw corn (cut off cob) 1 lb. butter 1 pint half n half ..mix together in roaster pan bake uncovered for one hour at 325..stir every 15 min. will look like its curdled but is ok..let cool put in freezer bags..can also add about 1/2 c. sugar but dont need it if you use really sweet corn..i dont add sugar at all
