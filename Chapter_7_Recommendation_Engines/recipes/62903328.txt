Title:
Spiced up Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/704089.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz package of egg noodles
6 oz cans of Tuna Drained and flaked
1 can of cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup of sour cream
1 cup Milk
1/2 teaspoon of Cayanne Pepper
1/4 teaspoon of papraka
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup frozen peas
1 cup of colby jack chees (shreaded)
18 ritz crackers (crushed)

Directions:
Cook noodles according to directions
Pre heat oven 350
While noodles are cooking mix the next 7 ingredients in a med. size microwave safe bowl,  heat in microwave for 3 min to warm.
Drain noodles
Spray a 9X13 baking pan with Pam (or like product)
Layer soup mixture, noodles, and half the cheese. Repeat layers. Top with crushed Ritz crackers.
Cover with foil and bake 20 min. uncover and bake until crackers are toasted a bit (about 20 min)
Let rest for about 5 min before serving

Description: 
This comfort food with a little heat! Great for the cold days of fall and winner.
