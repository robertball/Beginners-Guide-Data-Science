Title:
My Microwave Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup cocoa

Directions:
Cream sugar and butter together in a bowl big enough to hold all the ingredients.
Add eggs and vanilla in with the butter and sugar.
Mix.
Blend flour and cocoa together in a separate bowl.
Add the flour and cocoa into the mixture.
Mix well.
Grease a GLASS pie pan. (optional) Coat with sugar.
Pour the brownie mixture into the greased (or sugar coated) pie pan
Microwave 5-6 minutes
Let sit until cool.
