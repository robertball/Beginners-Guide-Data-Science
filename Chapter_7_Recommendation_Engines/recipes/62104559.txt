Title:
Layered Shepherd's Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground meat(i used beef)
1 large onion- chopped
1 head of garlic- minced
1 box of scalloped potatoes- or 2-3 potatoes thinly sliced
5 med sized red potatoes- cubed
1/2 cup of milk
1/3 cup of beef broth
ketchup
italian seasoning
salt and pepper

Directions:
In large pot, bring cubed potatoes to a boil.  Cook until tender.  Add some butter, salt, pepper and a splash of milk and mash.  Set aside.
Preheat oven to 400c
In large pan, fry up the ground meat.
Add onion, let cook for until tender then add the garlic.
Add salt, pepper and italian seasoning and a couple squirts of ketchup.  Add the beef broth and let cook for a couple minutes.
Place sliced potatoes in a 9x13 pan. Season.  (if using the boxed potatoes, mix the milk and seasoning in a glass)  Pour milk on to potatoes.
Spread meat over evenly potatoes.
Spread potatoes over meat.
Bake covered with alum. foil for 20 mins.  Take the foil off and bake the rest of the way uncovered.  (test for doneness with a fork).
Note:  We like aot of garlic but change the amount to suit your own tastes.  Also, i serve this with beef gravy.

Submitter: 
babybluangel
Description: 
Very easy and flavorful.  I made this one night when i wanted Shepherd's pie but only had 5 potatoes and a box of scalloped potatoes on hand, so i improvised.  You could also use fresh potatoes instead of the boxed scalloped.
