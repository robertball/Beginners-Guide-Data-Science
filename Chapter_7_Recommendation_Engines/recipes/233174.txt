Title:
Julie's Pistachio Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 cups milk
2 (3 ounce) packages instant pistachio pudding mix
1 (8 ounce) can pineapple tidbits, drained
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup mini marshmallows
1/2 cup shredded coconut
2 (9 inch)  prepared graham cracker crusts

Directions:
Stir milk and pudding mix together in a large bowl. Chill until set, about 1 hour. Stir pineapple tidbits, walnuts, marshmallows, and coconut into pudding. Spread pudding mixture into prepared graham cracker crusts.

Submitter: 
Jewelz
Description: 
Very simple and quick, perfect for a barbecue dessert or any time of the year. Best served the same day it is made. Serve topped with whipped cream.
