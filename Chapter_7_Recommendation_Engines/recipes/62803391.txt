Title:
Gazpacho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups finely chopped tomato
1 cup finely chopped cucumber
1/2 cup chopped red onion
1/2 cup chopped bell pepper
1/4 cup chopped cilantro
1/2 cup chopped green onion
2 tsp olive oil
1 Tbs red wine vinegar
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp sugar
1/4 cup chopped jalapeno pepper
2 cloves minced garlic
1 avocado chopped
Juice of 1 lime
3/4 lb cooked shrimp meat
Top off with Spicy Bloody Mary mix

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and top off with bloody mary mix to come to the consistency that you want. Also works well as a salsa.

Fresh tomato soup with fresh vegetables and shrimp meat.
