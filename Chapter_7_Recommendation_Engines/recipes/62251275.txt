Title:
Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bottle of dry red wine
1 cup ice cubes
juice of each - orange, lemon and lime
1/3 cup sugar
1 12oz bottle of club soda

Directions:
Pour chilled wine over ice cubes in a pitcher.  Combine orange, lemon and lime juices with sugar.  Add to wine.  Stir in chilled club soda at serving time.
