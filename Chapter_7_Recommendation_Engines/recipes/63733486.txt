Title:
Chicken and Crescent Roll Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10 ounce) cans white chicken meat (or 2 cups cooked chicken breast, cut up in small pieces)
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 (8 ounce) can water chestnuts (optional)
8 ounces mushrooms
1 small bag frozen mixed vegetables
1 (10 3/4 ounce) can cream of mushroom soup
2/3 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
3 tablespoons melted butter
2/3 cup shredded mozzarella cheese (or any cheese you prefer)
1/2 cup slivered almonds (optional)
1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent dinner rolls

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F. Saut√© onions and mushrooms in 1 tablespoon butter. Add soup, mayonnaise, sour cream, chicken, frozen mixed vegetables, and water chestnuts. Cook until hot and bubbly. Pour mixture into a sprayed 9x13-inch pan. Unroll crescent rolls in one sheet and place on top of mixture in pan. Combine 2 tablespoons melted butter, shredded mozzarella cheese and almonds. Sprinkle over rolls.Bake for 20-25 minutes.
