Title:
Italian Spinach Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 packages frozen chopped spinach-thawed
2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
¼ cup oil
½ teaspoon pepper
2 refrigerator pie crusts

Directions:
Thaw and squeeze out all juice from spinach, place in large bowl. Add cheese, oil and pepper and mix together.
Place one pie crust in pie pan. Add spinach mixture. Place other pie crust on top. Pinch edges and make some holes with a fork in the top.
Cook at 350 for ½ hour or until crust is golden brown.
