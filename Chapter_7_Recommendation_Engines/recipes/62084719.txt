Title:
Carolina Pork Twirl Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 boneless pork tenderloin (approx 2lbs.) Par-frozen if possible.
1 lb. of sliced bacon
Mustard based B-B-Q sauce

Directions:
Slice pork tenderloin in thin strips similar to the bacon. Then roll a strip of bacon inside the pork tenderloin strip and secure with a moistened toothpick. Add some freshly ground pepper and a hint of salt to each roll up. Grill on the first side for approximatly 10 minutes. Turn and slather the roll with barbecue sauce, preferrably a South Carolina mustard based one. Cook for another 10 minutes. Let rest for 2 minutes and serve.

Tyler Melnick

This is a perfect appetizer for small family gathering.
