Title:
Chocolate Cheesecake Bars from Crisco Baking Sticks® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984521.jpg
Ingredients:
Crisco® Original No-Stick Cooking Spray
Chocolate Layer:
4 (1 ounce) squares unsweetened chocolate
3/4 cup Crisco® Butter Shortening
1 cup sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
4 large eggs, lightly beaten
1 cup Pillsbury BEST® All Purpose Flour
Dash salt (optional)
Cheesecake Layer:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
2 large eggs, lightly beaten
3/4 cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray 13 x 9-inch baking pan with no-stick cooking spray.
Chocolate Layer: Place unsweetened chocolate and shortening in large, microwave-safe bowl. Heat on HIGH (100% power) 2 minutes. Stir until mixture is smooth and chocolate is melted. Mix in sugar. Add vanilla. Add eggs one at a time, stirring well after each addition.
Combine flour and salt in small bowl; add to shortening mixture, a little at a time, stirring until uniform. Spread in prepared pan.
Bake 15 minutes.
Cheesecake Layer: Combine softened cream cheese, eggs, sugar and vanilla; beat at medium speed until well blended. Pour evenly over top of chocolate layer.
Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until set. Place on cooling rack. Run spatula around edge of pan to loosen. Cool completely on cooling rack. Cut into 32 bars.

Submitter: 
Crisco Baking Sticks®
Description: 
Two delicious layers--one rich chocolate, the other creamy vanilla--bake up into elegant dessert bars.
