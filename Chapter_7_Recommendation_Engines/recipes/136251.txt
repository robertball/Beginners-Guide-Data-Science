Title:
Chocolate-Peanut Butter Cup Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
1 cup cold milk
1 pkg. (4 serving size)  JELL-O Chocolate Flavor Instant Pudding & Pie Filling
1 (8 ounce) tub COOL WHIP Chocolate Whipped Topping, thawed, divided
1 OREO Pie Crust (6 oz.)
2 tablespoons hot fudge microwavable sundae topping

Directions:
Place peanut butter in large bowl. Gradually add milk, stirring with wire whisk until well blended. Add dry pudding mix. Beat 2 min. or until well blended. (Mixture will be thick.) Stir in half of the whipped topping.
Spoon into crust; top evenly with the remaining whipped topping.
Refrigerate 3 hours or until set. When ready to serve, microwave fudge topping as directed on package; drizzle over pie. Store leftover pie in refrigerator.
