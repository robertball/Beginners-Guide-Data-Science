Title:
Chocolate Glazing Icing (Kakaoglasur) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 c powder sugar
3 tbsp cocoa
3-4 tbsp hot milk
2 tbsp melted butter

Directions:
Sift powder sugar and cocoa together and blend with as much of the milk as will give a good coating consistancy, stir in the melted butter.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
Another version of the chocolate glaze. From my mothers cookbook she brought over 50 years ago.
