Title:
Slow Cooker Turkey Breast With Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 three pound boneless turkey breast
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can chedder cheese soup

Directions:
Spray inside of slowcooker with nonstick spray.
Unwrap and rinse frozen turkey breast and place in slow cooker.
Add both cans of soup.
Cook on low for 8 hours or high for 4 hours.

Submitter: 
B. Barnett
Description: 
With just a few ingredients, this comes out very tender and with the best tasting gravy!
