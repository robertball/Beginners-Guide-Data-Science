Title:
Awesome Oatmeal Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 stick (12 T) soften butter or margarine
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
2/3 cup sugar
1 egg
1 t vanilla
2 cups oatmeal
1 1/4 cup flour
3/4 t baking powder
3/4 t baking soda
1/4 t salt
1 1/2 cup raisins

Directions:
Set oven to 375. Cream butter and both sugars for about 1 minute. Add egg and vanilla, blend well. Stir in flour, baking powder, baking soda,& salt until combined. Add oatmeal and raisins.  Drop by Tablespoon onto a non-greased cookie sheet, 2 inches apart. Bake until golden brown, 12 to 15 min. Cool.
