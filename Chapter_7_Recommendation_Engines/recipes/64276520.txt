Title:
Creamy Cottage Cheese Scrambled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1891030.jpg
Ingredients:
1 TBS Butter
4 Eggs
4 TBS Cottage Cheese
pepper to taste
chives (optional)

Directions:
1. Melt butter in skillet over medium heat. 2. Mix eggs together in bowl then add to heated pan. Cook for 1-2 minutes,  then add cottage cheese, pepper and chives if using.  3. Continue to cook until eggs are almost set, and plate. (Eggs will continue to cook on plate.)

Submitter: 
Kitchen Kitty
Description: 
A nice change from regular scrambled eggs. These eggs come out creamy and soft. Perfect with a slice of tomato and turkey bacon for a delicious and fast low carb breakfast.
