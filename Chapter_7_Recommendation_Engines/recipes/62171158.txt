Title:
Too Easy Clam Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can New England clam chowder soup
2 cans cream of potato soup
1 can cream of celery soup
2 cans of clams with juice.
2 C. half and half or milk
garlic powder to taste
salt to taste
pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and bring to a boil, then turn heat down and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.  It gets better the next day.  You can also make in a slow cooker.
