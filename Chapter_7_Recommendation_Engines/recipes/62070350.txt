Title:
Grandma's best buttermilk biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups flour (soft winter wheat preffered or sub. .... all purpose)
1 cup (2 sticks) butter
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon sugar
2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 1/2 cups buttermilk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees farenheit....
Mix dry ingredients together.... then sift dry ingredients well untill flour is fine and light and fluffy.....
then cut in cooled but soft enough to - work with - butter..... (untill butter is well blended through and resembles peas or large shreds of grated cheese).....
then mix in buttermilk carefully.... don't overmix dough......
roll out on floured surface and roll to 1 inch thick.... cut out biscuits with biscuit cutter or
with flour hands pull dough from bowl roll into balls and flatten and place on lightly greased baking sheet.....
place biscuits very close together so they touch and are fairly smushed against each other.... place in oven and bake untill tops are light golden brown... slice apart along perforations and enjoy....
