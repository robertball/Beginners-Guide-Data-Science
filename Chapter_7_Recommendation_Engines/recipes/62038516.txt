Title:
Spinach and mushroom casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10 oz. each) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained.
2 (4 oz. each) cans button mushrooms, drained
2 teaspoons minced onion
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
2/3 cup sour cream
2 tablespoons milk
2 tablespoons butter or margarine, softened

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300. In large bowl, mix together all ingredients. Transfer to an ungreased, 1 quart casserole, and bake, covered, at 300 for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Fran Weidman
Description: 
This delicious sidedish will go well with any kind of meat, and is very easy to make.
