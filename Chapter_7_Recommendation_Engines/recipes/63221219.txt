Title:
feta and prosciutto rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 - fresh sage leaves
1/4 cup of feta
6 slices of prosciutto
olive oil

Directions:
cut fresh sage leaves in half lengthwise
cut feta in to sticks
cut one piece of prosciutto in to four to six pieces
lay the sage on the prosciutto, top with feta and roll tightly
drizzle with olive oil and refrigerate until ready to eat.

Submitter: 
Esther
Description: 
littles bundles of sage, feta and prosciutto marinated in oil - yummy on an antipasto platter
