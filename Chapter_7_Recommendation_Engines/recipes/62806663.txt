Title:
Fried Rice with chicken or steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C. cold cooked white rice ( I make hot fresh rice)
1/2 T. Margarine/butter
1 T. Sesame Oil
5 T finely chopped green onion
3 eggs, scrambled
1 C. frozen peas and carrots
1 C diced meat of your choice (chicken or steak.) I cut into thin strips for bigger meat chunks.
I grilled mine to a med rare. While you add the ingredients together the steak will finish cooking. If you are using chicken, cook thoroughly!)
1 tsp salt
a dash of pepper
1 T soy sauce

Directions:
Scramble eggs and set aside.
Put margarine/butter and oil in pan, add rice.
Fry for 5 mins, add onions and fry for a few mins.
Add rest of ingredients, mix and fry gently.
