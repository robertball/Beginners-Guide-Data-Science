Title:
Grannies Old Fashion Heavenly Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1 TBS. butter
1/2 cup roasted almonds
2 TBS. Marshmellow cream
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 cup chopped pecans
4 TBS. grated unsweetened chocolate
24 Marshmellows
1 cup cream

Directions:
Combine chocolate and sugar in a large sauce pot.
Add butter and cream.
Boil to the soft ball stage ( 234 F)
Remove from heat.
Add marshmellow cream, nuts and flavorings.
Beat until mixture begins to thicken.
Place marshemellows on a buttered baking dish evenly.
Pour mixture ove them and allow to cool.
Cut into sqaures with a sharp knife.

Submitter: 
Rachel Dobbs
Description: 
This recipe dates back in my family to about 1932, and is a more original old fashion style heavenly hash.
