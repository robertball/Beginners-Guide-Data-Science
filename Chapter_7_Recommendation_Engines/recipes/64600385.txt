Title:
Five Cheese Mac 'n Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. macaroni or your pasta of choice (use pasta shaped to catch and hold the sauce), cooked 1 minute less then al dente, drain and set aside
6 T butter
3 T flour
3 cups half n half, room temp
1 cup milk, room temp
12 oz smoked gouda, shredded
12 oz Merkts cheddar cheese spread, softened
3 oz cream cheese, softened
12 oz Kraft Classic American and cheddar cheese combo, shredded
salt and pepper

Directions:
Melt butter in sauce pan, sprinkle in flour. Stir and cook over medium heat for 5 minutes until flour in cooked. turn heat to low and gradually add the milk whisking in to avoid lumps. Add half and half. turn heat to med and continue to cook stirring constantly until it thickens and begins to bubble. Remove from heat and stir in the smoked gouda, until melted and blended in. Place over low heat and stir in the cream cheese and Merkts stirring until melted and blended in. Add the American and cheddar cheese and stir until melted and blended in. Taste, add salt and pepper to taste. Stir in the cooked pasta.

Description: 
Five cheese go into this luscious creamy mac and cheese giving it a depth of flavor.
