Title:
Baked Shredded Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c zucchini, grated
1/4 c parmesan, grated
1 egg, stirred
1/2 t salt
1/4 t pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in greased casserole dish.
Bake uncovered at 350F for 20 minutes until set.
