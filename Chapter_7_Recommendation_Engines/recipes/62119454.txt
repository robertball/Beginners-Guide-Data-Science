Title:
Stuffed Mexican Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large chicken breasts
1 bell pepper
1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 onion
4 oz shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 cup Taco sauce
2-3 tbsp. Chili powder
2 tbsp. Garlic powder
1-2 tbsp. Vegetable oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°.
Cut large pockets in each of the chicken breasts.
Dice bell peppers, mix with beans and cheese in a large bowl.
Scoop mixture into the pockets of the breasts, use remainder to cover the chicken.
Saute onions in vegetable oil with chili powder and garlic powder, then pour over top of chicken and sprinkle more shredded cheese on top.
Bake for 50 minutes.
Serve with taco sauce drizzled on top.

Submitter: 
Jeremy
Description: 
I decided one night to try and make up my own stuffed chicken recipe.  This one happened to turn out great! Chicken breasts with pockets are stuffed with kidney beans, bell pepper, onion, and cheese.
