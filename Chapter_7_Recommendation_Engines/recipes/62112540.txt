Title:
Hotdogs & Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Eggs
Hotdog
Cooking Spray

Directions:
Crack 2 eggs in a bowl,scramble.
Place eggs aside and chop hotdogs.
Apply cooking spray to pan.
Put stove on high, add hotdogs
Let hotdogs cook for about one minute, add eggs.
Let eggs and hotdogs cook for a little while longer or until satisfied with the consistancy.

Submitter: 
Robin L Rushon Mogk
Description: 
A twist on eggs that kids and parents alike will enjoy.
