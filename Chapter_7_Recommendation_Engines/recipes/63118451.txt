Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/793357.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1/4 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
2 cups packed brown sugar
2/3 cup melted butter
2 lightly beaten eggs
2 tsp vanilla
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.  Set aside.
Melt butter, remove from heat, mix in brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
Combine butter mixture with flour mixture.
Spread in a greased 9x13 pan.  Sprinkle with chocolate chips.
Bake at 350 for 30-40 minutes until the middle is set, edges are brown, and a toothpick comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Beth Zapf
Description: 
Crunchy crust, chewy middle, all around sweet and chocolately.  These are addicting and dangerous. ;-)
