Title:
Easy Fajita Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb steak or chicken sliced into strips
1 lime juiced
2 green onions
3 cloves garlic-minced
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
2 tsp cumin
2 Tbsp oil
2 tsp worsteshire sauce

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and mix with a whisk.
Place steak or chicken into a ziploc bag or container with a lid.
Pour mixture over steak or chicken.
Marinate in the refrigerator for 2-6 hrs.
Remove meat from marinade and saute or grill with peppers and onion.

Submitter: 
B.J.
Description: 
Super easy to put together and makes awesome fajitas.  This marinade can be used with either steak or chicken.
