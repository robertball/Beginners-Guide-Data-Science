Title:
Spring Vegetable Strata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbl. olive oil
4 scallions, sliced
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper, divided
1 red bell pepper, diced
1 yellow bell pepper, diced
2 c. chopped broccoli
5 English muffins, toasted & torn into pieces
1 c. grated Monterey Jack cheese
1 c. grated cheddar cheese, divided
9 eggs
2 1/2 milk
2 Tbl. Dijon mustard

Directions:
In lg. skillet, heat oil on med. for 30 sec. Add scallions, saalt, & 1/4 tsp. black pepper. Cook for 3 min. Add garlic, bell peppers & broccoli; cook for 7 min.
Coat 9x13" bakin dish with cooking spray. Line with English muffins. Top with vegetable mixture, Montery Jack & 1/2 c. cheddar cheese.
In lg. bowl, whisk eggs, milk & mustard. add 1/4 tsp. pepper. Pour eggs over vegetables & cheese.
Cover with foil & refrigerate for 4 hrs., or overnight.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Bake strata, covered, for 30 min.
Remove foil & sprinkle with 1/2 c. cheddar cheese.
Bake for additional 25 min., or until golden & set in center.
Cool for 10 min.
