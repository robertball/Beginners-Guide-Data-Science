Title:
Fresh Tomato Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (9 inch) pie shell
7 ripe tomatoes, sliced
1 tbsp olive oil
1  red or yellow onion
2 cloves garlic minced
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese or cheese of your choice
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 tsp Garlic pepper
2 teaspoons fresh basil or 1/2 tsp dry
2 teaspoons fresh oregano or 1/2 tsp dry

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bake the pastry shell for 8 to 10 minutes or until browned.
Slice onion and saute in olive oil until just translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for 2 mins. Place the onion and garlic in the bottom of pastry shell. Slice tomatoes and arrange over onions. Add garlic pepper.
In a medium bowl,combine mozzarella,parmesan,herbs and mayonnaise. Spread this mixture evenly over tomatoes.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown. Once cooked, garnish with additonal fresh herbs.

Submitter:

Description: 
This savory layered pie is a great luncheon or supper treat. Pile slices of onion and tomato into a pre-baked pie crust. Spread with a mixture of mayonnaise, and  Parmesan and mozzarella cheeses, and then bake in the oven until golden brown. Serve with sour cream and fresh herbs.Modified from: Fresh Tomato Pie
