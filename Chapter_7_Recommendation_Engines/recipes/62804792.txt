Title:
Sweet and Sour chicken wings` Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs. chicken wings
1 small bottle Russian dressing
1 pkg dry onion soup mix
1 8oz. jar apricot/pineapple jam

Directions:
Place wings on cookie sheet with edges. Mix together other ingredients and spoon over chicken. Bake 2 hours at 300 degrees.
