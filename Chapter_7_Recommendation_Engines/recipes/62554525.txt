Title:
White Wine Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 (2 to 3 pound) whole chicken
2 parsnips, peeled and chopped
1 medium head garlic, peeled
2 large onions, chopped
5 carrots, chopped
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
2 stalks celery, chopped
2 potatoes, peeled and chopped
1 sweet potato, peeled and cubed
1 pinch dried thyme
1 pinch poultry seasoning
1 pinch dried basil
5 black peppercorns
2 bay leaves
6 cups chicken broth
2 cups water
2 chicken bullion cubes
1/2 (750 milliliter) bottle white wine
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large soup pot or Dutch oven, combine chicken, parsnips, garlic, onions, carrots, zucchini, parsley, celery, potatoes, sweet potato, spices, chicken broth, bullion cube water (mix them before adding), wine, salt and pepper.  Cover and bring to a boil over high heat.  Boil 30 minutes, partially covered, then reduce heat to low and simmer another 90 minutes.

Submitter: 
Rachell
Description: 
Added more spices and made some substitutions.

Chicken and a multitude of vegetables, including a whole head of garlic, and a touch of white wine are simmered a good long while for a mellow flavor.Modified from: White Wine Chicken Soup
