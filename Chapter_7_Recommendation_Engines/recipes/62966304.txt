Title:
French Buttercream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/732618.jpg
Ingredients:
*NIDO brand dry milk is the best.
3/4 cup non-fat dry milk powder (use any brand EXCEPT carnation) *NIDO brand is the best
12 TBS water
2 cups all-vegetable oil shortening (Crisco brand)OR
2 cups butter.
2 TBS meringue powder
2 tsp flavoring (vanilla, almond, strawberry, etc…)
1/2 to 1 1/2 tsp salt (the more salt, the less sweet)
3 lbs (12 cups) powdered confectioner’s sugar.

Directions:
1)If the dry milk is in granules instead of a fine powder, crush it with your rolling pin, or in a food processor.
2) Beat the dry milk powder and 12 TBS water, make sure no dry milk remains on beaters.
3) Add the shortening, meringue powder, flavoring and salt. Beat on low-med. speedjust until combined and no lumps remain in shortening.
4) Add powdered sugar, beat on low speed just ontil combined. (do not overbear, as this will create air bubbles in the icing.)

Submitter: 
Denise Moore
Description: 
This recipe yeilds approx. 5-6 cups of stiff decorator icing. If you are using a hand mixer, you may wish to use just half of this recipe so as not to burn out your mixer.
