Title:
Lumps of Coal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1900849.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
4 cups miniature marshmallows
Nabisco Oreo Chocolate Sandwich Cookies, 19.1 oz

Directions:
1. Break apart and mash the Oreos in a bowl into small bit size pieces
2. Melt margarine in large sauce pan over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until melted and well-blended. Cook 2 minutes longer, stirring constantly. Remove from heat.
2. Add Oreos. Stir until well coated.
3. Using buttered spatula or waxed paper, press mixture evenly and firmly in buttered 13 x 9 inch pan. Cut into 2 x 2 inch squares when cool.
