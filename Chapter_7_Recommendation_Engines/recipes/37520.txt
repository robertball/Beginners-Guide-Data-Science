Title:
Hot Dog Breakfast Burrito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979212.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large flour tortilla
1 egg, scrambled
2 tablespoons KRAFT Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 OSCAR MAYER Beef Frank, sliced

Directions:
Place egg, cheese and beef frank on tortilla on microwavable plate.
Microwave 30 seconds or until cheese is melted; fold up sides of tortilla to center, slightly overlapping. Serve with salsa or catsup, if desired.
