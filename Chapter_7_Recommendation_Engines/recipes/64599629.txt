Title:
POTATOES ROMANOFF Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3922085.jpg
Ingredients:
8 large potatoes
1/2 stick of butter
1 pint sour cream
1 bunch green onions, chopped
1 ½ cups shredded extra sharp Cheddar cheese
1 ½ tsp. salt
1/3  to ½ tsp. pepper
Paprika

Directions:
1. Boil potatoes until fork tender
2. Drain
3. Place in large mixing bowl
4. Mash lightly with potato masher
5. Stir in sour cream, onions 1 cup of the cheese and the salt and pepper
6. Turn mixture into buttered 2-qt. casserole dish
7. Top with remaining cheese
8. Sprinkle generously with paprika
9. Cover
10.  Refrigerate several hours or overnight
11. Bake covered at 350° for 30-40 minutes or until heated through.
12. Remove foil and return to oven and bake 5 minutes more.

Submitter: 
Jack
Description: 
great for holidays because you can prepare it a day in advance. Simple in preparation but fabulous in taste!
