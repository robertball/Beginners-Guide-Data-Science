Title:
The Double Chocolate Elvis Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon white chocolate peanut butter
1 tablespoon dark chocolate peanut butter
2 slices whole wheat bread
1 banana, sliced
1 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon brown sugar

Directions:
Melt the butter & brown sugar in a sauce pan
Add slice bananas and stir to heat & coat
Toast bread
After toasting, Spread white chocolate peanut butter on one slice of bread and spread dark chocolate peanut butter on the other slice.
Add bananas and press slices together.
Drop into hot non-stick pan and heat through on both sides
