Title:
Simply Succotash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz frozen cut small kernel corn
10 oz frozen baby lima beans
Canola Oil
Salt
pepper
Celery Seed

Directions:
Coat the bottom of a frying pan with the oil.
Place the corn and lima beans in the pan, and saute on high heat.
Add salt, pepper, and celery seed to taste.  The celery seed should be dominant but not overpowering.  Add oil if needed.
The succotash is done when it is hot.

Submitter: 
stronglive1
Description: 
A very simple but tasty succotash recipe that makes a great side to many dishes.
