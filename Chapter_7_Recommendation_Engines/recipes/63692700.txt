Title:
Mama's Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Potatoes
Onions
Carrots
Celery
Bacon
Velveeta
1 stick Butter
Salt & Pepper
1/2 C. Flour
3-4 C. Milk

Directions:
Boil diced potatoes, onions, carrots, and celery together.  When the potatoes get soft remove from heat and drain.
On med. heat melt 1/2 - 1 stick butter.  Add salt, pepper and flour and stir so it will not be lumpy.  When smooth and hot, stir in milk slowly.
Add bacon, potatoes and vegetables.
Heat over low - med. heat and stir often so milk will not stick or scortch.
Serve immediately - cannot over cook or milk will curdle.
