Title:
Saucy Flank Steak Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 2-lb Flank Steak
1 cup Garlic Flavored bottled Teriyaki sauce
1 T. Maple Syrup
1/2 cup Tomato paste
3 Scallions (green onions),chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
3 T. finely chopped red bell pepper
1 tsp. Dijon Mustard
salt & pepper to your taste

Directions:
Flatten steak with a meat pallet. Mix together Teriyaki sauce, tomato paste, maple syrup and marinate flank steak for a minimum of 2 hours (up to overnight).   Remove from marinade.
Mix together chopped Scallions, garlic, chopped red bell pepper and Dijon, and salt/pepper to taste.  Spread over one side of steak.  Roll meat up.  Secure with metal skewers if necessary.   Put over hot coals and grill for approximately 12-15 minutes until done.
Let sit for 5-8 minutes, then slice into pinwheels and serve!

Submitter: 
Sherbg
Description: 
This Flank Steak recipe is a variation of one that won a Bar-b-que cook-off contest for meats using Teriyaki sauce.  It is very tasty and fun to serve.
