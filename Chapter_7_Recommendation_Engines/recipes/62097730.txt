Title:
Crock Pot Cheesy Mushroom Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One can cream of chicken soup
One can cream of mushroom soup
One 8 oz. can mushroom pieces
1/2 cup cooking sherry
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 tsp. minced garlic
1 tsp. celery flakes
1/2 tsp. paprika
Six chicken breast halves

Directions:
Combine first eight ingredients and mix in mixing bowl.
Place chicken in slow cooker.
Cover chicken with mixture.
Cook on low for eight hours.
Serve over egg noodles or rice.

Submitter: 
Chef Robby
Description: 
A tasty and easy-to-make recipe that you prepare in your crock pot.  Serve over your choice of egg noodles or rice.
