Title:
Roasted Leg of Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4585573.jpg
Ingredients:
4-6 lbs (2-3 kg) pork leg
1 large onion
6 cloves of garlic
2 1/2 Tbsp of olive oil
2 Tbsp dried sage
2 Tbsp garlic powder
2 tsp salt
1 Tbsp onion powder (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 430째F (220째C).
Rinse the pork leg and pat dry with paper towel.
Slice up onion and separate layers into rings. Layer bottom of roaster with onion rings.
Cut cloves of garlic in half and place in bottom of the roaster with onion rings.
In a bowl, combine salt, sage, garlic powder, onion powder and olive oil.
Rub seasoned olive oil onto pork leg roast.
Place the pork on top of the onions and garlic. Place into the oven.
Roast for 40 minutes.
Turn the oven down to 355째F (180째C) and roast for a further 1 hour and 20 minutes.
Remove from the oven and slice to serve.
