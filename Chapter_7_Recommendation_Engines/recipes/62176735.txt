Title:
Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c hot water
4 c all bran cereal
2 1/2 c sugar
1 c. shortening
1 T salt
4 large eggs
1 qt. buttermilk
2 c bran cereal
5 c. flour
5 t baking soda

Directions:
Pour hot water over 4 c bran and let cool.
Cream sugar and shortening, and add rest of the ingredients.  Stir in the soaked bran last.
Can add raisins, nuts, etc.

Description: 
These are easy and delicious muffins.
