Title:
Cherry Yum Yum Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 graham cracker pie crust
2 (8oz) Packages cream cheese
1-2 cups Powdered sugar
1 (8oz) Tub Cool Whip
CHERRY PIE FILLING:
5-6 cups fresh pitted cherries, about 2 1/2 to 3 lbs
1/2 cup water
1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
4 tablespoons Cornstarch

Directions:
FOR THE PIE FILLING: In a sauce pan, combine cherries with remaining pie filling ingredients. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low and cook, stirring frequently, for about 10 minutes. Cool before using in dessert.
Cut cream cheese into blocks and beat with an electric mixer. Fold in sugar, add in as little or as much depending on how sweet you want the pie to be. Fold in Cool Whip and mix. Spread over graham cracker crust. Cover and Chill in refrigerator overnight. Top with cherry pie filling and serve.
