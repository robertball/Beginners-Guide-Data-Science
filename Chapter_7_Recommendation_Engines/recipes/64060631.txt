Title:
Kiki's Mexican Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups diced cooked chicken breast
1/4 cup julienned carrots
1/4 cup julienned red bell pepper
1/4 cup frozen corn kernels, thawed
2 green onions, sliced thin
1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
1 avocados - peeled, pitted, and chopped
1 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
2 Tbsp plain 0% Greek yogurt
1 Tbsp mayonnaise
1 tablespoons fresh lemon or lime juice
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1/8-1/4 teaspoon salt
pinch teaspoon pepper
1 tsp packaged taco seasoning or homemade spice mix
dash hot pepper sauce

Directions:
In a large bowl, gently mix the chicken, carrots, red bell pepper, corn, cherry tomatoes, green onions, avocado, and cilantro.
In a separate bowl, mix the yogurt, mayonnaise, lemon juice, cumin, salt, pepper, taco seasoning, and hot pepper sauce. Pour over the salad, and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.
