Title:
Cheesy Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tbsp margarine or butter, softened
4 Tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
1 Tbsp Italian herb seasoning
1/2 Tbsp onion, finely chopped
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp salt (optional)
Loaf of French bread, sliced

Directions:
Mix margarine, Parmesan chees, Italian herb seasoning, onion, garlic and salt.  Spread on sliced bread.  Warm in microwave oven immediately before serving.
