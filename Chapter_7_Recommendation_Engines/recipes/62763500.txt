Title:
Smoky Zippy Fry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. mayo
1 tsp. BBQ sauce
Cayenne pepper
Garlic powder

Directions:
Mix the mayo and BBQ sauce together.  Add in the cayenne and garlic powder to taste. Serve with fries!

Submitter: 
Amanda
Description: 
I'm tired of the same old dippers for fries, so I'm trying new concoctions and found this one to be pretty good!!
