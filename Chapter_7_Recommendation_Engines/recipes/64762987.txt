Title:
Mommas Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the meat sauce:
Extra virgin olive oil
1 pound lean ground beef
1 onion, diced
1 large bell pepper (green, red, or yellow), diced
6 fresh mushrroms sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 jar of salsa from the pantry
1 can tomato paste
2 cups of Clamato juice
2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano,
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1 tablespoon Italian Seasoning
Pinch of garlic powder
Salt
To assemble the lasagna:
3 fresh  lasagna sheets
1 tub of cottage cheese
1 bar Mozzarella cheese, grated

Directions:
Brown the ground beef In a large skillet ,heat 2 teaspoons of olive oil on medium high heat. Add the ground beef and cook until it is lightly browned on all sides. remove beef and leave some fat for frying.
Add the diced bell pepper, onions and garlic to skillet and cook for 5 minutes on medium
Return beef to pan
Add salsa, clamato juice and tomato paste
Add blended spices
cook for 20 minutes on simmer
Build the Lasagna
Preheat oven to 375
Spray all surfaces of ceramic pot
Add 1/4 of meat mixture
spread thin layer of cottage cheeese
sprinkle parmasen and cover with mozzarella
place lasgna sheet
repeat 2 times, retaining meat sauce and cheeses for the top
top layer meat sauce and cheese
Cover and bake 45 minutes, remove top for 5 minutes if you want the top browned
Let stand for 10 minutes before serving
