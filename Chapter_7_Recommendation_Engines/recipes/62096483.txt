Title:
My Cheesy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup onion, chopped
1 can condensed cream of mushroom
1 can condensed cream of celery
6oz. cream cheese
32 oz hash browns (shredded or cubed)
1 cup cheddar cheese

Directions:
Saute onion in a bit of butter.  Stir in soups. Add cream cheese and stir until hot and smooth. Add frozen hash browns and stir. Add cheddar cheese and stir again.
Transfer to a well-greased casserole dish and bake, covered, 45 minutes. Uncover and bake 15 minutes more.

Submitter: 
BLONDIE1020
Description: 
This is nice, easy way to use up those frozen hash browns or potatoes. Even my three year old eats these up!
