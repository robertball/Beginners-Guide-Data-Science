Title:
BREAD PUDDING WITH BOURBON SAUCE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. 		French bread, cut into 1/2  inch cubes
3 cups	 	milk
2 		large eggs
1 cup 		sugar
2 tsp. 		vanilla
1/8 tsp. 	salt
2/3 cup 	raisins
2 Tbsp. 	melted butter
Sauce:
1/2  cup 	butter (4 oz.)
1 cup 		sugar
1 		egg
1/2  cup 	bourbon

Directions:
Place bread pieces in a large bowl; pour milk over.  Stir a little to break the pieces up a little; let stand until all milk is absorbed.  In another bowl, beat eggs, sugar, vanilla, and salt together.  Stir into the bread mixture.  Gently stir the raisins into the mixture.  Pour into a greased 8‚Äù square pan.  Drizzle with the melted butter.
Bake at 350 degrees for about 1 hour, or until set.  Cool and cut into squares.
Sauce:  In a saucepan, melt butter; add sugar and egg, whisking.  Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens.  Whisk in bourbon.  Remove from heat and chill.  Whisk before serving.  Makes 1 cup.
