Title:
Easy Hoppin' John Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package smoked beef or turkey sausage
3 cans black eyed peas
2 cans low sodium chicken broth
1 cup water
2 cups intant rice
1/2 tsp. ground red pepper (or to taste)
1 small onion diced

Directions:
Slice sausage into bite size pieces. Heat sausage in a large pot with diced onion over medium-high heat just until sausage starts to brown. Add everything else except for the rice. Bring to a boil and then add the rice, stir well. Cover and cook for 20-25 minutes over medium heat stirring occasionally until rice is done.

Submitter: 
DONIGL
Description: 
An easy weeknight dinner that my kids love. Have it with a nice salad and dinner is done. The red pepper should be adjusted to fit your families taste.
