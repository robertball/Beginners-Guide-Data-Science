Title:
Eggplant Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 eggplant
1/2 # sliced Mozzarella
2 tomatoes, sliced
1/2 # fresh mushrooms, sliced
bread crumbs
1/4 C oil
1 egg or egg substitute
1 C tomato sauce

Directions:
Peel eggplant and slice into 1/2 inch rounds. Pound with mallet to 1/4 inch thickness. Dip eggplant slices in egg, then in bread crumbs, saute breaded eggplant in oil. Drain. Place eggplant in bottom of 10 x 10 baking dish.
Arrange tomatoes, then mushrooms on top. Add Mozzarella cheese over top; then top w/tomato sauce.
Bake at 450 degree oven for 15-20 min.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Made with eggplant, fresh tomatoes and mushrooms and topped with tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese.
