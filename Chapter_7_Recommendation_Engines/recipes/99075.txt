Title:
Philly Cheese Steak Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, sliced
1 green bell pepper, cut into thin strips
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
12 ounces Deli Roast Beef, cut into thin strips
4 submarine rolls or hoagie rolls, cut into halves
1/2 pound provolone cheese, sliced

Directions:
Saute onion and bell pepper in butter in medium saucepan over medium-high heat until onion is transparent.
Mix in roast beef; heat until beef is warm.
Evenly divide beef mixture into 4 portions; fill each roll with beef mixture.
Top each sandwich evenly with cheese.

Submitter: 
Sara Lee
Description: 
Onion and bell pepper are cooked with thin strips of roast beef to make a hearty filling for hoagie rolls.
