Title:
Hawaiian Banana Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups all purpose flour
2 cups sugar
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 cup chopped nuts (pecans or walnuts)
3 eggs
1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
2 cups mashed bananas
1 8oz. can crushed pineapple (drained)
2 teaspoons vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°.
Combine dry ingredients. Stir in nuts and set aside. Combine remaining ingredients, stir in dry ingredients, stirring just until batter is moistened.
Spoon batter into 2 greased and floured 9x5x3 inch pans.
Bake in preheated oven 50 or 55 minutes, or until done.
Cool in pan for 10 minutes before removing.
