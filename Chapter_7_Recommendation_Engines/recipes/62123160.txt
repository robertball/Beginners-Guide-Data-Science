Title:
Hot Baked Taco Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb of ground beef
1 pkg taco seasoning mix
2-4 tbsp hot taco sauce
1 green bell pepper
16 oz sour cream
1 pkg sharp chedder cheese
1 tomato
1 bunch green onions
1 - 2 bags tortilla chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350* Brown ground beef in skillet, drain & add taco seasoning mix & chopped bell pepper.  In 13 x 9in casserole dish, spray nonstick spray and spread meat mixture on bottom of pan.  Top with chopped tomato & green onions, sprinkle hot taco sauce over tomato & onions.  Spread sour cream as the next layer, & then top with chedder cheese.  Bake in oven 10 - 20 min or until cheese is bubbly & melted.  Serve with chips.

Submitter: 
shae
Description: 
This hot taco dip is perfect with tortilla chips and is always a crowd pleaser.
