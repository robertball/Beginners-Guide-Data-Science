Title:
Perfect Roasted Garlic Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 large potatoes
2 heads of garlic
2 Tbs olive oil
2 Tbs butter or margarine
1 cup lowfat milk
1/2 cup sour cream
1 Tbs garlic salt
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp paprika

Directions:
In a sheet of aluminum foil, wrap garlic heads drizzled with olive oil and heat in oven at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
Wash and peel potatoes and boil for 20-30 minutes or until tender. (They should mash easily). Remove garlic from oven and peel skin from the cloves. Mash garlic into the potatoes. Stir in butter, milk, sour cream, garlic salt, pepper, and paprika one item at a time. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jessica Lynn Pelletier
Description: 
Light on the sour cream, heavy on the garlic,this homemade favorite is going to disappear! My family requests this every year and empties the dish in the first sitting. That's why I double the ingredients, because you are definitely going to run out!
