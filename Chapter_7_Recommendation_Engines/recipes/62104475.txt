Title:
English Muffin Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg english muffins
1 8 oz pkg shredded mozzarella cheese
1 pkg pepperoni
1 link italian sausage (cooked)
1 can pizza sauce

Directions:
cut english muffins in half and place cut side up on a large cookie sheet.
spoon a small amount of pizza sauce onto the top of each muffin.
sprinkle cheese on top.
mix up the topping by adding peperoni to 1/3 of the muffins and sausage to 1/3 and leave the last 1/3 as cheese only.
bake at 350 degrees f. for 10 minutes. let cool and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Ashley
Description: 
fun little pizzas that are great for a quick meal or for a snack, great for kids birthdays or parties. Always a big hit.
