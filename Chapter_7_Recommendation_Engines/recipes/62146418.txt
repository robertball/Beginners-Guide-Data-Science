Title:
Raspberry Balsamic Reduction Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Raspberry Balsamic Vinaigrette
1 rounded tsp. brown sugar
1 pinch of salt

Directions:
In a small sauce pan, pour one cup of raspberry vinaigrette
Add 1 teaspoon of brown sugar and stir
Bring to a boil and reduce to low simmer with evaporation
Cook on low simmer until it is reduced by about half.
It's ready to drizzle when you put a spoon in and it coats the spoon with a syrupy glaze.

Submitter: 
CSavaso
Description: 
This is a delicious and simple reduction of Raspberry Balsamic vinaigrette which is terrific drizzled over buttered asparagus or on top of a juicy filet mignon topped with blue cheese crumbles.
