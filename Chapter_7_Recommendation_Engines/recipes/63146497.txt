Title:
Easy Slow Cooker Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 LBS Stew Meat or Beef Tips (cubed)
2 (10 3/4 oz) Cans French Onion Soup
2 (10 3/4 oz) Cans Cream of Mushroom Soup
2 TBSP minced Garlic (optional)
1 (16 oz) package of bowtie pasta

Directions:
Turn slow cooker on LOW. Mix all cans of soup & garlic into slow cooker.  Add meat and stir thoroughly. Let cook for 4 hours stirring occasionally. Make pasta from package directions.  Serve beef stew over pasta. Enjoy!
