Title:
Protein Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Egg Whites
2 Cup Low Fat Cottage Cheese
2 Cup Oatmeal
2 tsp Cane Sugar
2 tsp Vanilla Extract
2 tsp Ground Cinnamon

Directions:
Blend all ingredients together.
Melt butter on griddle. Scoop batter onto griddle using 1/4 cup measuring cup. When bubbles form around sides, flip pancakes.
Use new butter for each batch and keep heat low.
Should make about 16 pancakes

Submitter: 
AFulton
Description: 
Make ahead for quick and healthy breakfasts throughout the week.
