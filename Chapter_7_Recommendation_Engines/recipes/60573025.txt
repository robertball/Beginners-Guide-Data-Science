Title:
Maple-Balsamic Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar  (high quality is best)
1/4 cup maple syrup
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup extra-virgin olive oil

Directions:
Place vinegar, maple syrup, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper into a blender. Pulse to combine, then add the olive oil in a steady stream with the motor running.

Submitter: 
Hana
Description: 
This dressing is good on any salad, especially salads containing chicken or shrimp. It's also a good marinade for pork tenderloin!Modified from: Maple-Balsamic Vinaigrette
