Title:
Round Steak with Mushroom Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 round steak
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 packet onion soup mix
1 can mushrooms, drained

Directions:
Trim all fat from steak and cut into serving size pieces.
Season with salt & pepper and brown on both sides.
Remove from skillet, put in casserole dish, and keep warm.
Add cream soup and onion soup mix to skillet.
Add 1 can water (fill soup can).
Heat and stir, them pour over steak in casserole dish.
Cover with aluminum foil and bake at 275 for 2 hours or 325 for 1 hour (if bake for longer time, put in extra water).
Check occasionally to make sure liquid does not boil away, but not too often because moisture escapes when foil is lifted.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
Mom's yummy round steak and gravy.  Serve gravy over mashed potatoes.
