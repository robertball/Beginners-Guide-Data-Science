Title:
Thousand Island Dressing III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/692855.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons chili sauce
2 tablespoons minced green bell pepper
2 tablespoons minced pimento
2 tablespoons minced sweet pickle

Directions:
Mix together the mayonnaise, chili sauce, green pepper, pimento and pickle. Chill until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Patty Hern
Description: 
A spicy version of Thousand Island, using chili sauce instead of ketchup. This is best if left to sit in the frig for several hours.
