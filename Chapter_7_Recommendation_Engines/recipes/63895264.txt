Title:
Manhattan Style Seafood Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 slices bacon
1 onion, diced
2 stalks celery, diced
1 clove garlic, minced
8 ounces clam juice
1 quart water
14 ounce can chicken broth
14 ounce can diced tomatoes
10 ounce can Irish potatoes, cubed
2 tablespoons parsley, chopped
1 tablespoon Old Bay seafood seasoning (or more to taste)
16 ounces frozen corn
16 ounces frozen carrots
1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 pound crab meat
1 pound bay scallops

Directions:
Cook bacon until crisp, remove from pan and set aside.  Saute onion, celery and garlic in bacon drippings. until soft.  Put sauteed mixture in slow cooker.  Add clam juice, wine, chicken broth and water.  Stir to combine.  Add tomatoes, potatoes, parsley, Old Bay, corn and carrots.  Add seafood and bacon.  Cook on low 3-4 hours.

Submitter: 
Barb0607
Description: 
This simple chowder gets rave reviews whenever I make it.  You can adjust the spiciness to your taste by adding more or less Old Bay.  I like to make this for gatherings.  It is so simple to toss everything in the slow cooker and have a delicious chowder waiting when guests arrive.
