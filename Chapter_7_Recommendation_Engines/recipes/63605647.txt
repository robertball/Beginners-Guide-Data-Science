Title:
Collard Greens with Black Eyed Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon olive oil
1 large onion, sliced
1 bunch collard greens, stems removed
1 can diced tomatoes
1 can or 1 1/2 cups cooked black eyed peas
1 teaspoon salt
Balsamic vinegar to taste
2 Tablespoons honey

Directions:
Saute sliced onion in olive oil until tender and slightly caramelized.
Add tomatoes and collard greens, "basting" collard greens with tomatoes until tender.
Add black eyed peas and salt.
Add balsamic vinegar (one wide spiral into the skillet)
Add honey
