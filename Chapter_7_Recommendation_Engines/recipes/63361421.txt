Title:
Mint-Chocolate Cookie Pudding Pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/895382.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 oz) box sugar-free instant chocolate pudding mix
1/4 tsp. peppermint extract
1 3/4 cups half-and-half
10 Oreo cookies, crushed (about 1 heaping cup)

Directions:
Blend pudding mix, extract, and half-and-half in a blender until smooth. Stir in crushed cookies by hand. Spoon into molds and freeze.
