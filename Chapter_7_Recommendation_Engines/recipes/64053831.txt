Title:
Clean Eating Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1127234.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tomatoes  - deseeded and cut into fourths
1 or 2 serrano pepper or jalepeno pepper cut into thirds
2 cloves garlic - peeled/chopped
1 small white or sweet onion - cut into large pieces
1/4 cup chopped cilantro - packed
1/2 tsp. salt,
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. ground cumin
1/4 tsp. chili powder
1/2 tsp. agave nectar

Directions:
Place all ingredients (but only 1 pepper) into a food processor and pulse a few times. Taste to determine if a little more salt/serrano or jalepno pepper/agava nectar needs to be added. Continue to pulse until the desired consistency is achieved.

Submitter: 
Melissa Lindsey Gibson
Description: 
My families favorite salsa and my favorite for them, because it is all natural without any processed products.
