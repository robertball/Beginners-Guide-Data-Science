Title:
Olive Garden Spinach and Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 ounce artichoke hearts, drained and sliced
1 pound cream cheese, room temp
8 ounce mascarpone cheese, room temp
2 T flour
1 cup Parmesan cheese
1/4 t thyme, fresh
1/4 t crushed red pepper
1 T parsley, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
5 green onions, chopped
dash salt
dash pepper
6 ounce spinach, fresh, chopped
2 loaves bagnettes, sliced
Olive oil

Directions:
MIX all ingredients in a large bowl, except bread and extra virgin olive oil.
COAT a large non-stick baking pan with pan spray; fill pan with mixture. Bake at 325F for about 25 minutes or until bubbling and center is hot.
DRIZZLE both sides of bread with extra virgin olive oil. Grill bread on both sides.
SPRINKLE top of heated dip with Parmesan cheese. Serve hot with grilled bread

Submitter:

Description: 
Creamy cheese dip rich with artichokes and spinach. Served warm with grilled banquettes.
