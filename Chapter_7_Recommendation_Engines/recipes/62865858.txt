Title:
Pork Tenderloin with Paprika Spice Rub Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
Rub
1/4 cup paprika
2 tablespoons salt
2 tablespoons ground cumin
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 tablespoons chili powder
2 tablespoons freshly ground black pepper
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 lb. pork tenderloin

Directions:
Rub spice on tenderloin.  Cook for 20 min. or internal temp of 155.
