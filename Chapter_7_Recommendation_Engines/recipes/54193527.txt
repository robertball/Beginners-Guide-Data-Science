Title:
Fry Bread I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 1/2 cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
4 cups shortening for frying

Directions:
Combine flour, salt, and baking powder. Stir in 1 1/2 cups lukewarm water.  Knead until soft but not sticky.  Cover with a kitchen towel and let set for about 15 min.  Shape dough into balls about 3 inches in diameter. Roll into patties  and make a small hole in the center of each patty. Pick up and stretch it a little.
Fry one at a time in 1 inch of hot shortening in iron skillet, turning to brown on both sides.  Drain on paper towels.
