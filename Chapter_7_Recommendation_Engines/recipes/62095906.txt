Title:
Chicken Chili Verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 chicken thighs skinned
2 - 7 3/4 oz cans El Pato tomato sauce(yellow can)found in the mexican food section of your grocery store.
4 potatos peeled and cubed
1 onion sliced
2 cups water
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup vegetable oil
salt
pepper

Directions:
Season chicken with salt and pepper and dredge in flour, shake off excess and fry in oil until lightly browned.  Place all ingredients into crockpot and cook all day on low. We serve over rice with warm tortillas.

Submitter: 
Karen Dean Covington
Description: 
This is an easy, spicy crockpot recipes I have been making for my family for years.  I can throw it together in the morning in a matter of minutes.
