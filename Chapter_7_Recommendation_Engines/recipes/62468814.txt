Title:
Kielbasa and Lentil Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 # Kielbasa or smoked sausage, cut into small cubes
1/2 head cabbage, shredded (8 cups)*
1 lg. onion, chopped
4 carrots, shredded*
2 cans (19 oz. each) prepared lentil soup
1 can (16 oz) crushed tomatoes in puree, undrained
3 T Frank's RedHot pepper sauce
* Can substitute a bag of slaw mix for the cabbage and carrots

Directions:
Cook and stir sausage in 5-qt saucepan over med-high heat 3 min. or until lightly browned. Add vegetables; cook and stir 5 min. or until tender.
Stir in soup, tomatoes and hot sauce. Heat to boiling. Reduce heat to med-low. Cook, partially covered, 10 min or until heated through and flavors are blended. Ladle stew into bowls.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Quick and simple using canned lentil soup and bag of slaw mix. Incredibly easy weeknight meal
