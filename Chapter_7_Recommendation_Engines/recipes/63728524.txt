Title:
Baby Bok Choy Sauté Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1035407.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Baby Bok Choy, bottoms removed and well washed
2 Tbs Olive oil
1/2 sweet onion, rough chopped
1 inch knob of fresh ginger, thinly sliced
1 large garlic clove, minced
Freshly ground pepper to taste

Directions:
Dry the Bok Choy, cut the stems in 1" pieces keeping the leaves intact, set aside.
Over medium heat, warm the oil in a large sauté pan.  Add the onion and cook until translucent, do not caramelize.  Add the ginger and garlic and cook for another minute, until fragrant.
Add the chopped stems, lower the heat and cook until softened, about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.  Add the leaves and cook and stir until just wilted.
Season with freshly ground pepper to serve.

Submitter: 
BigShotsMom
Description: 
Bok Choy is Chinese Cabbage, Baby Bok Choy is milder, with more tender leaves and stems.  This is another savory way to get your greens and it complements many meal types.
