Title:
Frozen Lemonade (or pink) Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 egg yolks
1 can sweetened condensed milk (14 oz. can)
1 can frozen lemonade concentrate(or pink)-(6 oz. can)-thawed
1 cup whipped cream
4 egg whites (stiffly beaten)
graham cracker crust- store bought or homemade

Directions:
beat 4 egg yolks
add 1 can sweetened condensed milk and one can (thawed) lemonade concentrate-mix well-by hand is fine.
fold in 1 cup whipped cream and 4 egg whites(stiffly beaten)
pour into graham cracker shell
freeze (about an hour)
slightly thaw before serving(about 10 minutes)
slice and serve- top with whipped cream by the slice
