Title:
Asian Grilled Duck Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7800642.jpg
Ingredients:
2 or more duck breasts, with skin/fat (about 3/4 lb total)
1 T avocado oil
2 T coconut aminos
1-2 tsp fish sauce
1/4 tsp liquid smoke
1 T rice vinegar or white vinegar
2 tsp five spice powder
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp ginger powder
1 garlic clove
1 tsp fresh ginger
pinch red pepper flakes
sea salt

Directions:
Combine marinade ingredients (except duck) with immersion blender, add salt to taste
Score skin of duck breasts
Lightly salt both sides of duck
Pour marinade over duck and refrigerate covered for 2 or more hours
Uncover and let duck sit on counter for 30 min or to bring to room temp
Heat grill to medium (just over halfway) on one side; heat to high on other side
Grill duck skin side down over medium heat for 12 min
Flip and move duck to high/400F plus and grill skin side up for additional 3 minutes
Let rest 5 min
Cut in thin slices to serve
