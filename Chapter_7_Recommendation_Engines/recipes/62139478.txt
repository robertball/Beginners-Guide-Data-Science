Title:
V's Easy Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 14.5oz cans Hunt's petite diced tomatoes(some of the juice drained off)
1 4oz can of diced green chiles
1 medium red onion, diced
3 jalapenos, seeded, deveined and diced (wear gloves while handling to protect your hands)add more if you desire
pinch of Mexican oregano (crushed in the palm of your hand)
1/4 bunch of cilantro leaves, chopped
splash of white vinegar
pinch of salt
pinch of freshly ground black pepper
pinch of garlic powder
pinch of ground cumin

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl and refrigerate for 3 hours to let the flavors blend.  I put mine in a 32 ounce glass jar and a little custard cup (the cup is all MINE!) Serve with tortilla chips or on tacos.

Submitter: 
2obies
Description: 
This is easy to put together and can be modified for heat! Refrigerate for 3 hours to let the flavors blend.
