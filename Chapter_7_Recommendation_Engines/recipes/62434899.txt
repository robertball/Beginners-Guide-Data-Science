Title:
Morning Mango Tango Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/455712.jpg
Ingredients:
Flesh of 1 large ripe mango, roughly chopped
1 cup quick oats
1 tsp lime zest
2 tbsp lime juice
1 tsp cardamom
1/2 tsp ground anise
pinch nutmeg
2 cups water

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a pot and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer stirring frequently until oatmeal reaches desired consistency 5-10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sarah-May
Description: 
Sweet mango combined with exotic spices and a kick of lime are sure to get you moving in the morning.
