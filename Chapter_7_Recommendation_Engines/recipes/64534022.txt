Title:
Croissant S'mores Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tube refrigerated Croissant dough
4 Tbs Hazelnut Spread
1/2 c miniature marshmallows

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375Â°
Unroll and separate dough into triangles.
Place approx 1/2 Tbs hazelnut spread at top of wide end of dough. If you spread it, leave approx 1/8" at the edge.
Place 6-8 miniature marshmallows on top of spread.
Roll up starting at wide end, folding ends over to seal.
Place on lightly greased cookie sheet. (Directions on dough suggest ungreased but they tend to stick.)
Bake until lightly golden 10-13 minutes.
