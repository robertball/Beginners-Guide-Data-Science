Title:
Favorite Hot Cocoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c sugar
1/4 c Hershey's Cocoa
Dash Salt
1/3 c hot water
4 c milk
3/4 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Mix sugar, cocoa & salt in saucepan, stir in water.
Cook & stir over medium heat until mixture boils; boil & stir 2 minutes.
Gradually add milk to cocoa mixture & heat.  DO NOT BOIL.
Remove from heat, add vanilla.
