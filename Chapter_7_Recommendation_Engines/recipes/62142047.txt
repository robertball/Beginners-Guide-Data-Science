Title:
Simple Turkey and Tator Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Yukon gold potatoes, petite diced
1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
1 1/2 cups chopped sweet yellow onions
1 cup green or yellow bell peppers, diced
1 tablespoon minced fresh garlic
1 pound ground turkey
2 tablespoons chili powder
1 28oz can petite diced tomatoes with juices
1 16oz can pinto beans, drained
1 small can mexicorn with juices
1 cup chicken broth
1/8 teaspoon dried oregano
1/8 teaspoon ground cumin
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
Sour cream or yogurt for garnish
1/8 cup fresh chopped cilantro for garnish

Directions:
In 3 quart or larger dutch oven heat evoo on medium-high heat.
Add onions, green pepper and garlic and cook until tender.
Add ground turkey and cook until no longer pink, breaking up large chunks.
Stir in remaining ingredients (except sour cream and cilantro) and bring to boil.
Reduce heat and simmer for about 15 minutes or until tators are tender, stirring occasionally.
Ladle into bowls and top with a dollop of sour cream or yogurt, then sprinkle with cilantro.

Submitter: 
Alta Nesbitt Rees Sieker
Description: 
My heart-healthy recipe uses ground turkey instead of beef and diced potatoes. It's perfect for a cool fall evening.
I like to serve it with whole wheat tortilla chips or whole wheat crackers.
