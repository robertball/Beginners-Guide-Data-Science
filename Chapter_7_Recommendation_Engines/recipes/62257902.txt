Title:
Jim's Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/336565.jpg
Ingredients:
12-eggs
2-C Miracle Whip
2-T white vinegar
2-T mustard
2-t tabasco
1- pinch of paprika

Directions:
Hard boil the eggs and cut them in 1/2 removing the yolks and reserving the egg white 1/2s. Place the egg yolks in a blender and add the Miracle Whip ,white vinegar, mustard and Tabasco. Spoon the mixture back in the egg white 1/2's. I out a pinch of paprika on some of the eggs and some I leave plain.
