Title:
K-Dub's Alfredo Ravioli Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons of butter
2 tablespoons of Italian seasoning
3 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1 inch pieces
1 (16 ounce) jar of alfredo sauce
1 (25 ounce) package of frozen cheese-filled ravioli
2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
1/4 cup parmesan cheese, shredded
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees
Melt butter in skillet until melted; add chicken pieces and italian seasoning.
Cook over medium heat until chicken is no longer pink (about 15 minutes).
Cook ravioli per package directions.
In a greased 13x9 inch baking dish spread 1/2 cup of sauce on the bottom.  Arrange ravioli in a single layer over sauce; add half of your chicken pieces.  Sprinkle with 1 cup mozzarella, salt & pepper and repeat the process until all ingredients are used.
Bake uncovered for 25 minutes, sprinkle the parmesan cheese on top the last 5 minutes of cooking.

Submitter: 
K-Dub
Description: 
A rich, tantalizing dish that will leave you wanting more, even if your full!  You can use all different kinds of ravioli, meats and sauces.  A versatile dish for what you have on hand.  It can also be made ahead of time!
