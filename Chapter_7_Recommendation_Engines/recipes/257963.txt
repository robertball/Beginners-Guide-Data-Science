Title:
Green Chile Egg Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5140120.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound shredded Mexican cheese blend
8 eggs
1 (16 ounce) package cottage cheese
1 cup baking mix (such as BisquickÂ®)
1 cup milk
1/2 cup margarine, melted
1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chilies

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
Mix Mexican cheese blend, eggs, cottage cheese, baking mix, milk, margarine, and green chiles together in a bowl; transfer to the prepared baking dish.
Bake in the preheated oven until cooked through and bubbling, about 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Anna Johnson
Description: 
A zesty breakfast casserole that is great with salsa and sour cream.
