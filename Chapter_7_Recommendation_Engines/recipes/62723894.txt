Title:
Key Lime Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 egg yolk
14 oz can sweetened condensed milk
1/2 C fresh or bottled lime juice
2 T water
1-2 drops green food coloring (opt.)
1 t grated lime peel
Graham cracker pie crust
1 C whipping cream
2 T sugar
1/2 t vanilla
Key lime slices or lime slices (opt)

Directions:
In a lg. mixing bowl beat eggs and yolk with wire whisk until combined.
Gradually add milk to yolks, beating until combined. Add lime juice, water, food coloring, and lime peel. Whisk until well mixed. Pour into crust. Place on baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 min. or until almost set in center. Cool on wire rack for 1 hour. Refrigerate at least 4 hours.
Chill small mixing bowl and beaters of electric mixer.
In chilled bowlbeat cream, sugar, and vanilla on med. speed until stiff peaks form. Pipe or spoon whipped cream around edges of pie. Garnish with lime slices, if desired.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Made with store bought graham cracker pie shell, sweetened condensed milk, fresh or bottled lime juice and lime zest.
