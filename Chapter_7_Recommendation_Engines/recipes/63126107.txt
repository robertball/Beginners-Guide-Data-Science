Title:
Nacho Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/796840.jpg
Ingredients:
2 whole or 4 split chicken breasts, frozen
1 can light kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 can cream of mushroom soup (reduced sodium)
1 small can enchilada sauce
1 cup milk
3 cups broken up nacho chips
1/2 cup of shredded Mexican blend cheese

Directions:
Spray bottom of crockpot with cooking spray.
Place frozen chicken at bottom of crockpot.
Pour the soups, sauce and milk over the top and stir together.
Add 2 cups of crushed nacho chips and mix in.
Cook on high for 3.5 - 4 hours.
When finished, remove and shred chicken, then stir back into crockpot. Sprinkle remaining cup of nacho chips on top and sprinkle with shredded cheese until it melts.
Serve with sour cream if desired.

Submitter: 
Heidi Habeck-Johnson
Description: 
Easy make ahead chicken nacho casserole can be made in crockpot or in oven.
