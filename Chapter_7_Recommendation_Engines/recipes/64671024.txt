Title:
Creamy Spinach and chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts, cubed
Cajun seasoning
Tbs minced garlic
Onion, chopped
Small can mushroom pieces
Bag of frozen spinach
8oz cream cheese
Red pepper flakes
Pizza blend shredded cheese

Directions:
Heat oil in cast iron skillet. Saut√© garlic. Season chicken with Cajun seasoning. Cook chicken with onions and garlic. Add mushrooms. Microwave spinach for 3 Min then add to skillet. Add hot pepper flakes to taste. Cube cream cheese and add to skillet. Bake at 375 for 20 minuets then take it out and mix cream cheese into spinach mixture well. Top with shredded cheese and bake an additional 15 Min or until cheese melts well.
