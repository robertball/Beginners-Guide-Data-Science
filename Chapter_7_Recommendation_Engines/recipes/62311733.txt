Title:
Meatloaf Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground beef
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed french onion soup
1 cup dry bread crumbs
2 eggs
1 teaspoon salt
1 pinch ground black pepper
3/4 cup ketchup (optional)
12 cheese cubes (optional)

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 12 cup muffin pan.
Mix ground beef, soup, bread crumbs, eggs, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Fill muffin cups 3/4 full with meat mixture and make a small dent in the meat, add cheese cube, ketchup or anything else that would be tasty and add meat to the top to cover
Bake 1 hour in the preheated oven to a minimum temperature of 160 degrees F (70 degrees C). If desired, remove from oven after 50 minutes, drizzle ketchup on the top of each muffin, and return to oven for an additional 10 minutes.
