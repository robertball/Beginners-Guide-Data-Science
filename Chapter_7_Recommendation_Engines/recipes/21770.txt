Title:
Grilled Apricot Fish  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983819.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound halibut
1/2 cup apricot preserves
2 tablespoons white vinegar
1 1/2 teaspoons chopped fresh tarragon

Directions:
Brush grill rack with vegetable oil. Heat grill to medium heat.
Place fish on grill rack. Cover and grill 5 to 6 inches from heat 10 to 20 minutes, turning once, until fish flakes easily with fork.
Meanwhile, mix remaining ingredients; spoon onto fish during last minute of cooking.
