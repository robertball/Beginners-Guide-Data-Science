Title:
GEFILTE FISH STRATA Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 gefilte fish rolls
1 salmon gefilte fish roll
1 pkg frozen chopped spinach (thawed & drained)
3 eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Spray 9 x 13 pan
1st layer: mash together 1 fish roll, the chopped spinach & 1 egg. Spread out in dish
2nd layer: mash together salmon roll & 1 egg. spread evenly on top of 1st layer
3rd layer: mash together 1 fish roll & 1 egg. Spread evenly on top of 2nd layer
Bake 1 hr. If wet on top, bake a little longer. Refrigerate.
