Title:
Shrimp with Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1024056.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package dry fettuccini noodles
1/4 cup olive oil
1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
6 green onions, thinly sliced
4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
2 large tomatoes, diced
1/2 cup dry white wine
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup fresh basil, chopped
2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon pepper
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
Saute shrimp until pink.
Stir in green onions and garlic.
Cook for 2 minutes.
Add chopped tomatoes, and cook 2 minutes.
Stir in wine and butter, and season with basil, salt and pepper.
Cook for 3 minutes.
Stir in Parmesan.
Toss with pasta until evenly coated.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Shrimp are cooked with brandy, white wine, garlic and fresh basil in a butter-olive oil sauce.Modified from: Brandied Shrimp with Pasta
