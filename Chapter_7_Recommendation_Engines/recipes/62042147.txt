Title:
French Toast Casserole II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
l loaf french bread (not sourdough)
2 pkgs. cream cheese, cubed
1 dozen eggs
1/2 c. maple syrup
2 c. milk

Directions:
Cube the bread.  Put 1/2 in bottom of 13x9" baking pan.  Top w/cream cheese cubes, then rest of of bread cubes.  In bowl beat eggs, milk, and syrup.  Pour over bread in pan.  Mash it down so that casserole is saturated.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.  Next morning take it out and let stand 30-45 min., till room-temperature.  Bake at 350 for 45 min.  Serve with maple syrup.

A great make-ahead dish for breakfast or brunch -  even Christmas morning!
