Title:
Peach salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Peach salsa
*1 tomato
*2 large peaches
*1 Tbl diced yellow onion
*1 clove garlic diced
*1/2 to 2 whole jalepenos (to taste)
*Juice of 1 lime

Directions:
Mix ingredients together. Can be eatten immediately but tastes better if allowed to sit for a few hours. Serve with tortilla chips.
