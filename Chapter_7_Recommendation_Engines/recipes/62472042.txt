Title:
Cinnamon Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 c. sugar
1/2 c. stick margarine, softened
1 tsp. vanilla
6 oz. block-style cream cheese
2 eggs
1 1/2 c. flour
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
2 tsp. ground cinnamon
3 c. chopped peeled apples (Fuji)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.  Beat 1 1/2 c. sugar, margarine, vanilla, and cream cheese until well blended.  Add eggs, beat.
Combine flour, baking powder, and salt.  Gradually add to creamed mixture and beat until blended.
Combine 1/4 c. sugar and cinnamon, then mix 2 tbsp. cinnamon/sugar with apples.  Stir apple mixture into batter.
Pour batter into 8" round pan coated with cooking spray and sprinkle with remaining cinnamon/sugar.
Bake at 350F for 1 h. 15 min.  Cool before cutting.
