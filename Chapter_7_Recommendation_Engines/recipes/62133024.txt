Title:
Ron Scotts blue cheese dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 packet hidden valley ranch dip
16 oz Sour Cream
4 oz blue cheese crumbles
pepper to taste
ruffles potato chips

Directions:
Mix Hidden Valley Ranch and sour cream.  Simply add blue cheese crumbles.  Pepper to taste.  Allow to sit in fridge for one hour before serving.  Serve with ruffles, your favorite chips or vegetables for dipping.

Submitter: 
Colleen Kain
Description: 
My long time friend, Ron Scott came up with this brilliant, simple twist to the basic hidden valley ranch dip that will surprise the taste buds!  Even people who don't like blue cheese, such as myself, love this easy recipe.
