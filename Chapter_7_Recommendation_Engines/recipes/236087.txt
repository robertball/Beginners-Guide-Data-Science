Title:
Pumpkin Kahlua Colada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1089859.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce Kahlua Pumpkin Spice
1 fluid ounce cream of coconut
1/4 cup crushed ice
Whipped cream
Ground nutmeg

Directions:
Blend ice, Kahlua Pumpkin Spice Liqueur and cream of coconut in blender until smooth. Pour into a glass. Garnish with whipped cream and a sprinkle of nutmeg.
