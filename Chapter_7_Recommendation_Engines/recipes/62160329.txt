Title:
Luby's Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2379213.jpg
Ingredients:
1	head green cabbage, chopped
1	cup chopped yellow onion
2	cloves garlic, minced
1	can diced tomatoes, with half of the juice
¾	cup real bacon bits
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In large skillet over medium heat; add onion, cabbage salt and pepper; stir and sauté for about 5-10 minutes.  Cover and turn down. Simmer stirring occasionally until it starts to soften.  Then add the tomatoes and juice; recover and sauté until cabbage is softened.  Add bacon bits, stir and let simmer for about 5 to 10 minutes.  Serve hot.
