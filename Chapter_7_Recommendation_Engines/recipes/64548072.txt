Title:
Tuna Egg Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can oil packed tuna, drained
2 boiled eggs, chopped
Thick slice onion, chopped fine
Chopped dill pickles
Chopped bread and butter pickles
Generous sprinkle of celery salt
Generous grind from peppercorn medley
Pinch of sugar
1/4 c mayo or to taste

Directions:
Use equal amounts of chopped pickles. Mix all with fork. Refrigerate, or make sandwiches immediately on soft wheat bread.
