Title:
Hot Crab Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs of lump crabmeat
3 8-oz cream cheese
3 - 5 tablespoons milk
6 tablespoons of finely chopped onion
3 teaspoons of horseradish
1 tsp crushed garlic (to taste)
salt & pepper

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375F. Soften the cream cheese then blend with the milk and horseradish and crushed garlic. Combine this mixture with crab meat, onion, salt and pepper. Mix well and spoon into an oven-proof baking dish.
Sprinkle lightly with breadcrumbs over the top of the cream cheese and crab meat. Bake for 20 - 25 minutes. Serve hot in stylish bowl with serving spoon with pita chips or crispy thin garlic toast or whatever!

Submitter: 
Dorri Spoto
Description: 
a delectable and creamy hot dip blend loaded with lump crabmeat and blended with cream cheese, finely chopped onions and a hint of horseradish.
