Title:
King's Hawaiian S'Mores Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/991143.jpg
Ingredients:
2 King's Hawaiian® Hawaiian Sweet Rolls
1 (1.5 ounce) bar milk chocolate candy, broken in half
2 large marshmallows

Directions:
Slice each roll horizontally. Toast or grill halves.
Center chocolate pieces on bottom halves of rolls
Skewer marshmallows and heat over an open fire to melt
Sandwich marshmallows between chocolate halves and top halves or rolls

Submitter: 
King's Hawaiian®
Description: 
This special version of s'mores makes for a super tasty treat!
