Title:
golden gazpacho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium orange
2 pounds yellow tomatoes, cored and cut into chunks
2 yellow bell peppers, cored and cut into large chunks, about 1/4 of one pepper set aside
1 small yellow onion, diced
1 garlic glove, chopped
2 tablespoon sherry vinegar
1 1/2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground white pepper
1 avocado

Directions:
Using a sharp knife, slice both ends off the orange.  Place it upright on the cutting board and se a gentle sawing motion to cut off the rind in strips, starting at the top of the orange and following its form down to the cutting board.  Chop the flesh into chunks, removing any seeds.
In the work bowl of a food processor, processing in batches, combine the orange flesh, the tomatoes, all but the reserved 1/4 of bell pepper, the onion, and the garlic.  Process until finely pureed.  Stir in 2Tbsp of the vinegar and the olive oil.  Refrigerate until well chilled,at least 2 hours.
When ready to serve, taste for seasoning.  Add the salt, pepper and more vinegar, if necessary.  Halve pit, peel and dice the avocado.  Dice the reserved yellow pepper.  Divide the soup among six bowls and top each serving with a spoonful of avocado and yellow pepper.  Serve
