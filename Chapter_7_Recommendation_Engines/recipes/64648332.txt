Title:
Smore Nanna Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4533127.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Bananas sliced
3 packages of Graham crackers
4 TBS butter
1/4 cup sugar
6 Regular size Hershey's Chocolate Candy Bars
12 Smore Marshmallows (Large Squares)
Pudding Ingredients:
2 Cups Milk
3/4 Cup Sugar
4 TBS Cornstarch
1/4 tsp salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 TBS butter

Directions:
Put graham crackers in a gallon baggy and crush them. Put them in large casserole glass dish.
Pour melted 4 TBS of butter over crushed graham crackers.
Sprinkle 1/4 cup sugar over then smooth out until the mixture covers the bottom of casserole dish.
Slice 6 bananas into medallions and layer over the graham cracker mixture.
Open 6 Chocolate candy bars and layer them over bananas.
Cook Pudding:   Add 2 cups of milk, 3/4 cup of sugar, 4 TBS of cornstarch, 1/4 tsp salt, 1 tsp vanilla and 2 TBS of butter into a sauce pan.  Stir with whisk until mixture thickens up to a pudding consistency (for faster result used instant pudding).
Pour Pudding mixture over candy bars.
Place the large marshmallow squares over the pudding mixture.
Broil at 1-minute increments until marshmallows turn slightly brown.
Remove from oven let cool and eat

Submitter: 
Michelle Hughes
Description: 
All the deliciousness of banana pudding and Smore in one scrumptious pie!
