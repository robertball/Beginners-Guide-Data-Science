Title:
Baked Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium potatoes, baked and cooled
1 can (14oz) chicken broth
2 tbsp sour cream
1/2 c shredded cheese
2 strips bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 green onion, sliced
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Peel potatoes and cube. Place half in blender, add broth and process until smooth.
Pour into saucepan. Stir in sour cream, remaining potatoes and seasonings.
Cook on low until heated thru (do not boil).
Garnish with cheese, bacon and onions.
