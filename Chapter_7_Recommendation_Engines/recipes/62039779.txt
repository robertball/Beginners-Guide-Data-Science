Title:
Uncle Ronnies Clouds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1/4 tsp vanilla
1 can pie filling, any flavor

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla at medium speed on mixer.  Gradually add cream, mix well.  Whip until thick.  Using back of spoon, shape ten 3 1/2 inch shells.  Place on waxed paper lined cookie sheet.  Freeze overnight.  When ready to serve, fill with pie filling.  Sprinkle chopped nuts on top if desired.

Submitter: 
ERLENSEE_GERMANY
Description: 
I remember my uncle bringing this recipe form Ohio, when I was a kid.  He as passed on, but his recipe lives on.  I don't like cherries, so I use other fruit.
