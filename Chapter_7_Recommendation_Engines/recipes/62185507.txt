Title:
Stovetop Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Canned fruit of your choice, with the juice
2 Tbsp butter
brown sugar
CAKE:
1/2 c flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
pinch of salt
1 egg
1/2 c sugar
1/2 c milk or half and half
1 Tbsp butter out of the butter listed above

Directions:
In a well seasoned 9 inch iron skillet melt the butter over medium heat.
When melted remove 1 Tbsp of it and save for the cake recipe.
Add enough bite sized fruit pieces to cover bottom of skillet.Add enough juice to cover to the top of the fruit. Add enough brown sugar to sweeten this well. Let this cook while making the cake.
CAKE: Blend the flour, baking powder and salt together and set aside.
In another bowl blend the egg and sugar together well, then add the milk and butter and blend.
Add the flour part and mix all together.
Turn the heat down on the skillet to lowest heat.
Pour cake over the fruit.
Put a tight lid on the skillet and cook around 20 min. or until a toothpick comes out clean.
Turn onto a plate and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jannabofanna
Description: 
This is an upside down type cake made in an iron skillet on the stovetop. No need to heat the oven.
