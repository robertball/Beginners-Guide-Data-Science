Title:
French Silk Chocolate Pie I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/604700.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, room temperature
1 1/2 cup white sugar
4 (1 ounce) squares unsweetened baking chocolate, melted and cooled  [or 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa+ 1/4 cup shortening or oil]
2 teaspoon vanilla extract
4 eggs or 3/4 cup Egg Substitute (pasteurized)
1 prepared 8 inch pastry shell, baked and cooled or oreo pie shell

Directions:
Cream butter in a mixing bowl. Gradually beat in the sugar with an electric mixer until light colored and well blended. Stir in the thoroughly cooled chocolate [or cocoa + oil], and vanilla extract. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating 5 minutes on medium speed after each addition. Spoon the chocolate filling into a cooled, baked pie shell.
Refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
