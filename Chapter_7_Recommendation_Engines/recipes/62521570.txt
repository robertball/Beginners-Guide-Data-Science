Title:
Weekday Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 lb roast
2 pkg mushroom or onion GRAVY mix
1 cup water
2 T vinegar (optional tenderizer)
1 cup frozen carrots
1 can or cup frozen/canned potatoes

Directions:
in crockpot on low 6-8 hours or high 4-6 hours
approximately 1 hour before serving add frozen/canned vegies.
cook until fork tender
