Title:
shredded mexican style Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts halves
2 pkgs taco seasoning
medium onion
1 bell pepper
1 fresh jalepeno (seeded if you don't want it too spicy)

Directions:
place chicken breasts in slow cooker
mix both taco seasonings with 1 cup of water
pour over chicken
dice onion, bell pepper and jalepeno and put on top of chicken. cover and cook until chicken shreds easily about 4 hrs or less on high.
shred chicken and then add about 1/2 cup more of water(chix should be a bit wet)
I've used this for both tacos and enchiladas..yummy and easy

Janet Grella
