Title:
Don's Deep Fried Onion Rings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Oil - for frying
3 Onions - peeled, cut into 5mm/0.5in slices and separated into rings
1pint Buttermilk
½ tsp Cayenne Pepper
8oz Plain Flour
1 tsp Celery Salt
¼ tsp Garlic Powder
1 tsp Paprika
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Soak the rings in the buttermilk mixed with the cayenne pepper;
In another bowl, mix together the flour, celery salt, garlic powder and paprika;
Drain the onion rings, place them in the flour mixture and coat thoroughly;
Heat oil over high heat in large pan;
Deep-fry a few rings at a time in hot oil until golden and crisp;
Season and serve immediately.
