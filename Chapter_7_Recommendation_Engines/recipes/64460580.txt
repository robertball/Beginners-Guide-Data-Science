Title:
My favorite classic lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2418844.jpg
Ingredients:
½ cup sugar
6 lemons
1.5 cups of water
2 cups of water
1 quart of ice
1 quart size pitcher

Directions:
½ cup sugar
1.5 cups of water
On low heat, stir together until the sugar is dissolved - allow to cool on the stove.
Juice 6 lemons, add the juice to the syrup. Optional -allow to chill 20 minutes in the fridge if you have time. Add 2 cups of water.
Right before serving pour into a 1 quart pitcher filled with ice. Stir to combined & enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
The hardest part is juicing the lemons, but it is worth a little extra effort because it tastes so much better! You’ll never want to return to the powders again
