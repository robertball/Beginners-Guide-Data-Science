Title:
White Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. pkg dried navy beans
2, 7 oz. pkgs Brown-n-serve turkey sausage sliced thin
2, 15 oz. canned tomatoes (diced or stewed)
1 bell pepper diced
1 medium onion diced
2 stalks celerey diced
salt
chili powder
ground cayenne pepper
garlic powder
brown sugar

Directions:
follow 1 hour quick soak direction on bean pkg
drain and discard soaking water
bring 6-8 water to boil in 4 quart pot
add beans and 2 teaspoons each of salt, chili powder, cayenne, and garlic powder
cook beans for 45 minutes
add can tomatoes and cook for 15 minutes
add brown sugar, bell pepper, onion, and turkey sausage
simmer 30-60 minutes until beans are tender
