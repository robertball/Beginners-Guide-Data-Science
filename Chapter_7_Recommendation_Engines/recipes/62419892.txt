Title:
Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Container Imperial Cheese, softened
1 8oz pkg cream cheese, softened
2 tbsp sour cream
chopped walnuts (optional)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together.
Form into 2 balls.
Roll in walnuts, if desired.

Submitter: 
Kelli F
Description: 
We make this every year at Christmas. Serve with a variety of crackers.
