Title:
Tony's Chicken Noodle Soup Starter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup sliced carrots
1/2-1 pound chopped cooked chicken breast
4 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
1 (14.5 ounce) can vegetable broth
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic pepper
2 pinches poultry seasoning
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
3 cups egg noodles

Directions:
In a large pot over medium heat, melt butter.  Saut√© garlic, onion, carrots and celery in butter until just tender, 5-7 minutes.
Chop chicken into bite size pieces. Add chicken, broth, basil, oregano, salt, garlic pepper, poultry seasoning, and thyme. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
In a separate pot, cook egg noodles according to package directions. Drain and add to soup. Simmer an additional 10 minutes.
