Title:
Apricot-Tea Glazed Drumettes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/975775.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup apricot preserves
1 tablespoon NESTEA® Unsweetened Instant 100% Tea
12 chicken drumettes

Directions:
LINE baking sheet with foil.
COMBINE preserves and Nestea(R) in blender; cover. Blend until smooth. Place drumettes on prepared baking sheet; brush heavily with glaze.
BROIL drumettes, brushing frequently with glaze, for 12 to 15 minutes on each side or until no longer pink near bone.
