Title:
Paula Deen's Goulash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
2 medium yellow onions, chopped
3 cloves garlic, chopped
3 cups water
2 (15 oz) cans tomato sauce
2 (15 oz) cans diced tomatoes
2 tablespoons Italian seasoning (I did not use this)
3 bay leaves (I did not use this either)
3 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp salt
2 cups elbow macaroni (uncooked)
*Add garlic powder, seasoning salt, and a little pepper if you wish.

Directions:
1. In large pot cook ground beef over medium heat until browned, spook off any extra grease.
2. Add onion and garlic, saute until transparent.
3. Add 3 cups of water, tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, all seasonings, soy sauce, and bay leaves if you choose to use them. Allow to simmer for 15-20 minutes.
4. Add macaroni adn allow to simmer an additional 10+ minutes or until noodles are tender. Remove bay leaves before serving.
