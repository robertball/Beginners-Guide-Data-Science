Title:
Caribbean Barley Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup barley
3 cups water
3 Tbsp lime juice
2 Tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp salt
1 large mango, diced, divided
1 1/2 cups black beans (or 1 15 oz can, drained)
1 cup grape tomatoes, halved
1/2 cup diced red onion
1/4 cup cilantro or mint, chopped
1 Tbsp minced jalapeno pepper

Directions:
Bring water and 1/2 tsp salt to a boil in medium saucepan. Add barley, cover and simmer until al dente (check at 20 minute). Drain, if necessary and cool completely.
Meanwhile, mash about 1/4 cup diced mango against the side of the bowl.  Add in the lime juice, olive oil, cumin, and 1/2 tsp salt and whisk.  Toss in rest of ingredients, including barley. Serve immediately.
