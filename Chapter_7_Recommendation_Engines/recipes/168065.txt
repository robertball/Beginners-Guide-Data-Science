Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Tangy Holiday Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982074.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal
2/3 cup non-fat dry milk powder
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
2 tablespoons ketchup
1 egg
1 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 pound lean ground beef
Sauce:
1 (14 ounce) can tomato sauce
1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
1/4 cup sweet pickle relish
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon vinegar
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Combine cereal, dry milk, onion, ketchup, egg, salt and pepper. Add beef, mixing only until combined. Using level measuring-tablespoon, portion meat mixture. roll into balls and place in foil-lined shallow baking pan.
Bake in 400 degrees F oven about 12 minutes or until browned. Drain off excess fat.
In 4-quart saucepan, combine sauce ingredients. Simmer over low heat 15 minutes, stirring frequently. Add meatballs to sauce and continue simmering uncovered 10 minutes longer.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*
Description: 
Seasoned meatballs are baked first then simmered slowly in a tangy sweet-sour tomato sauce.
