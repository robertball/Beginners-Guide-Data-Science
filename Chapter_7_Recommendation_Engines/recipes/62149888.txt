Title:
Easy Cheesey Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8oz blocks of cream cheese
1 can rotel
1 rotisserie chicken
1 cup shredded cheese

Directions:
First, remove all of the meat off of the rotisserie chicken and shred into bite size pieces or smaller
Combine shredded chicken and the the remaining ingredients in a pot and stir over medium heat until melted all the way through
Tranfer to serving dish or crock pot and serve! Goes great with Fritos Scoops and tortilla chips
