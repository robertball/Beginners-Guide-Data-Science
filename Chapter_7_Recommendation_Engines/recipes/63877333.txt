Title:
Scottish Shortbread (from George Mead) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup potato flour
1 cup all-purpose flour
3/4 cup powdered sugar
1 cup butter
1 tsp. vanilla
Pinch cinnamon

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar
Mix in flour
On a cookie sheet, pat into a circle about 3/4" thick.  Prick all over with a fork.
Chill in refrigerator for 30-minutes
Bake at 375° for 5-minutes.  Reduce heat to 300° and continue baking for 45-minutes longer.  Watch carefully and bake until a pale golden color (NOT BROWN).
Cut into small wedges while still warm.
