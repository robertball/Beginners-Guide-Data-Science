Title:
7 Up Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c     Sour Cream
1/2 c     7 Up Soda
2 c       Bisquick
1/4 c     Butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Melt butter in a 8x8in pan. Mix the Sour Cream and 7Up Soda together then add the Bisquick and mix well. Drop large spoonfuls of the batter into the butter filled pan then use your spoon to smooth out the batter until it fills the pan.  Some butter may come through in places. Let cool a bit then cut with a sharp knife into biscuit sized portions and serve.
Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, until lightly golden brown

Description: 
Quick easy biscuit of buttery goodness.
