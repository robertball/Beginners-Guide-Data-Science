Title:
Cheddar and Herb Drop Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon sugar
2 tablespoons freshly chopped chives
1 tablespoon freshly chopped parsley leaves
3/4 cup grated sharp yellow Cheddar
1 cup buttermilk
4 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
In a large bowl, mix flour, baking powder, salt and sugar together using a whisk. Add the herbs, cheese and buttermilk then stir until dough forms. Using a large spoon, scoop out dough onto baking tray. With a brush, lightly butter tops. Bake for 15 minutes.
