Title:
Venison Leg Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb Leg roast Elk or Deer
5 Slices salt pork
Onion
Apple
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Allspice
2 Sprigs of rosemary
2 Bay leaves

Directions:
Cut gashes in roast about 2 inches apart and half through the thickness of roast.  Place in each gash a slice of salt pork, onion, garlic, and apple.  Top with a few more slices of onion.  Sprinkle roast with spices and herbs. Place meat on a rack in a roasting pan.  Bake in 300 degrees F. oven until done, 2 to 4 hours, depending on tenderness of meat.  Remove herbs before serving.
