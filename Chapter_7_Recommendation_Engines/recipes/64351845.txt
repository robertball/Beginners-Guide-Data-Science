Title:
Sunny Day Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups water
6 oz can frozen orange juice, thawed
6 oz can frozen lemonade, thawed
3 cups pineapple juice
2 liters ginger ale

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.  Garnish with orange or lemon slices.
