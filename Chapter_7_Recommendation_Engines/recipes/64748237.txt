Title:
Banana Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup refined coconut oil
1 cup white sugar
1 cup mashed ripe banana (approximately 2 medium)
1/2 cup grated zucchini
2 large eggs
2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
3/4 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 Fahrenheit.
Grease and flour a 9.5 x 5.5 inch pan.
Cream together the sugar and coconut oil.
Beat in the eggs, followed by the mashed bananas and grated zucchini.
Mix in the flour and baking soda until just combined, do not overmix.
Fold in the walnuts and chocolate chips.
Pour into the greased pan and gently smooth the surface. Bake for approximately one hour or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out dry.
