Title:
Granny Smackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/824293.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle beer
1 (6 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate
3 fluid ounces vodka

Directions:
Stir together the beer, lemonade concentrate and vodka. Pour into small glasses, and serve cold.

Submitter: 
KASSIEKENNETT
Description: 
A tart drink similar to hard lemonade, but made with beer and vodka.
