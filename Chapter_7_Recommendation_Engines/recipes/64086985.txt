Title:
Mango Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 pieces chicken, skinned
1 package dry cream of mushroom soup mix
1 15 oz can mushrooms, sliced
1/2 onion, diced
1 12 oz can mango nectar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Coat chicken pieces with dry soup mix and place in greased baking dish. Sprinkle mushrooms and onions on top of chicken.  Pour mango nectar over chicken.
Cover and bake for 1 hour.  Remove cover and bake for another 30 minutes or until done.
