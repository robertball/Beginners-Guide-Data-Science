Title:
Cranberry kuchen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1-1/4 cups sugar, divided
1 tsp. vanilla
3 eggs
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1-1/4 cups sour cream
2 cups chopped cranberries
Topping:
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 Tbsp. flour
2 Tbsp. chopped almonds
1 Tbsp. butter
1/2 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
With mixer,cream thoroughly butter, 1 cup sugar and vanilla, in large bowl. Add eggs, one at a time, beating till light and fluffy.  Combine flour, baking powder, soda and salt.  Add flour mixture and sour cream alternately, ending with flour.  Spread half this batter in a greased 10" springform pan. Combine cranberries with remaining 1/4 cup sugar and spread on batter. Top with remaining batter.  Combine topping ingredients; sprinkle over batter.
Bake at 350 for 50-55 minutes.

Submitter: 
AUNT MAMIE
Description: 
This is a holiday breakfast favorite in our home.  It is equally delicious served warm or cold, and it also freezes well, so you can make it ahead.
