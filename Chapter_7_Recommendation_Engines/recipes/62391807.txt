Title:
Kickin' Turkey Burger with Carmelized Onions & Spicy Sweet Mayo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Burgers:
1-1/4 lb extra lean ground turkey
2 TBL Worcestershire Sauce
1/4 tsp (a few drops) of Mesquite Liquid Smoke
1 tsp Mesquite Grill Seasoning
1 TBL Montreal Steak Seasoning
1/2 a large onion (or 1 small onion), grated
1 small jalepeno, diced
2 TBL BBQ Sauce
1/2 a large onion, (or 1 small onion), sliced
1 TBL olive oil
Spicy Sweet Mayo:
1 cup light mayonaise
1/4 cup coarse grain brown mustard
1 TBL horseradish
1 tsp cayenne pepper
a few dashes of Tabasco
1/4 cup honey

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix all ingredients for the spicy sweet mayo.  Cover the bowl and refrigerate.
In a large bowl, mix the ground turkey with the next 7 ingredients.  Form into 5 patties.
Heat a skillet with cooking spray over medium high heat.  Cook burgers for about 6 minutes on each side or until done.
While burgers are cooking, heat up 1 TBL of oil in a separate skillet on medium high heat and add the sliced onion. Cook for about 20 minutes, stirring often.
Serve on your favorite bun with the Spicy Sweet Mayo and top with carmelized onions.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
A spicy, flavorful, and moist turkey burger.  This is my husband's favorite.  It tastes delicious and I make it with extra lean ground turkey breast to keep it healthier.  The grated onion adds moisture as well as flavor to the turkey.
