Title:
Vanilla Chiffon Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Eggs, Large, Brown, Grade A____2 Each
Granulated Sugar_____________2 Tablespoons
Cream Of Tartar______________½ Teaspoon
All-Purpose Flour____________1 ¾ Cups
Granulated Sugar_____________½ Cup
Baking Powder________________1 ½ teaspoons
Baking Soda__________________¾ Teaspoons
Vanilla Extract, Pure________1 teaspoons
Whole Milk___________________1 Cup
Margarine, melted____________¼ Cup

Directions:
Gather all ingredients and equipment.
Separate the Eggs Between the egg yolks and the egg white; set the egg yolks aside.
Mix the 2 Tablespoons of sugar together with the cream of tartar, add them together in a large mixing bowl with the egg whites; whisk the mixture into a stiff meringue.
Set the meringue aside; sift the flour, ½ cup of sugar, baking powder, and baking soda together into a large mixing bowl.
Beat the ingredients together in with the vanilla extract, milk, and margarine until the mixture forms into a smooth batter.
Fold the reserved meringue into the batter in two stages.
Grease and lightly flour the insides of a 9x5 loaf pan and pour the cake batter inside; bake at 350oF For 45 Minutes or until an inserted toothpick comes out clean.
Remove the cake from the oven and allow it to rest for 10 minutes away from the heat; invert the pan on to a clean dry surface to remove the cake.
Trim, and frost as desired.
