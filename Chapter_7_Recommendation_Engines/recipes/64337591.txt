Title:
The LAST Margherita Pizza Recipe You Will Every Need Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2072092.jpg
Ingredients:
Homemade Pizza Dough (see recipe)
2 Large cans of WHOLE tomatoes, cut in 1/2 and gently pressed in mesh strainer to remove most of the liquid. (whole is important)
Pizza Mozzarella, shredded(Buffalo Mozza can be used with the pizza mozza)
Olive Oil
Fresh Basil leaves, roughly chopped (must be fresh)
Herbe de Provence spice

Directions:
1. Precook Pizza crust as directed
2. Using a pastry brush, lightly brush olive oil over precooked crust - be sure to get the edges too
3. Sprinkle with Herbe de Provence seasoning all over pizza.
4. Place a light sprinkle of pizza mozza overtop of crust. (very little - just a few bits here and there)
5. Lay tomato halves around pizza.
6. Sprinkle more mozza on top or top with buffalo mozza cut into rounds
7. Drizzle olive oil over top of pizza and bake in 400degree oven for about 5 mins and then put on the broiler for another minute (keep watching)
8. Take out of oven and sprinkle with chopped basil leaves and let rest about 2 minutes before eating.
(optional - you could add some pine nuts, goat cheese, balsamic glaze to this also)
