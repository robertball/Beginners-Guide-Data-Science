Title:
Walnut Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1/3 cup brown sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 cups flour, sifted
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cups chopped walnuts
powdered sugar

Directions:
Cream butter, sugar, and vanilla until fluffy. Sift flour and salt together; add to creamed mixture.
Mix well; stir in walnuts. Roll into small balls, about an inch think. Bake at 375 F for 12-15 minutes on ungreased baking sheets.
Roll in powdered sugar when warm, but cool to handle.

Submitter: 
AppleChef
Description: 
Great for a Christmas cookie tray or for a sweet family treat any time of year!
