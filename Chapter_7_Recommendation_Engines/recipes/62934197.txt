Title:
Basa Puttanesca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Water 8 cups
Salt 1 tsp
Cavatappi pasta
Olive oil 1 tbsp
Chopped onion 1 cup
Garlic cloves, minced 3
Can of tomato pasta sauce 23 oz / 680 ml
Can of diced tomatoes with juice 14 oz / 398ml
Sliced kalamata olives 1/2 cup
Basil 2 tbsp
Sugar 1 tbsp
Anchovy paste 1 tsp
Dried crushed chilies 1/2 tsp
Cayenne pepper 1/8 tsp
Basa fillet (cut in 1 1/2 inch cubes) 3/4 lb
Grated Romano cheese 2 tbsp
basil 1 tbsp

Directions:
Boil pasta with salt for 10 to 12 minutes drain and return to pot
Heat olive oil in frying pan on medium add onion for 5 to minutes stirring often until golden. Add garlic and stir for 1 minute.
Add Pasta sauce, diced tomatoes, olives, basil, sugar, anchovy paste, dried crushed chilies, cayenne pepper. bring all to a boil then reduce to medium low. simmer uncovered for about 10 minutes stirring occasionally.
Add fish, stir cook for about 5 minute (until fish flakes easily)
Add pasta stir in sauce
sprinkle with cheese and second amount of basil and serve....
