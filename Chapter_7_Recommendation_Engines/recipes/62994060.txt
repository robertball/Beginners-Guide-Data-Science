Title:
Mom's Chicken (wings) and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 chicken thighs/drumsticks (skinless) or 40 wings
2 jars of Strong Garlic Rib Sauce
1/4 tsp salt
1 tbs paprika
1/2 tbs garlic powder
2 cups white rice

Directions:
Cover chicken with salt, garlic powder and paprika (both sides) and put on broiling pan
Broil for 5 mins on each side
Put oven on at 350
remove from broiling pan and put into a casserole dish (may need 2)
Pour Strong Garlic Rib Sauce on top, and mix and cover
Bake at 375 for 40-45 mins at 350
While baking, make the white rice.
Serve over white rice.

Submitter: 
Suzanne G.
Description: 
Chinese style - mom always makes with chicken wings, and I like to sometimes use chicken thighs and drumsticks instead.
