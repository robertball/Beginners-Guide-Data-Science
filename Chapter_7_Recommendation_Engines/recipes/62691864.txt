Title:
Old Fashioned Buttery Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all purpose flour
2 1/2 tsp of baking powder
1/2 tsp of salt
1/3 cup of butter
3/4 cup of milk
1 T of soft margarine or butter

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 475 degrees
2. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt together.
3. Cut butter into the dry ingredients using a fork or pasty blender.
4. Add milk and mix until a soft dough ball forms.
5. Place ball on a floured surface and knead slightly. Be careful to not to over knead.
6. Roll dough out to about 1/2 inch thick.
7. Cut with a biscuit cutter.
8. Place biscuits closely together in a light greased pan.
9. Brush tops of biscuits with butter or margarine.
10. Bake for 11 to 15 min until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Brucker
Description: 
This biscuit recipe works great with sausage gravy. Add shredded cheddar cheese and garlic powder to create a tasty cheesy biscuit.
