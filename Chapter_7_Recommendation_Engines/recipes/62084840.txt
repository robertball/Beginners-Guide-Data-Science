Title:
sesame spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh spinach, stemmed and rinsed thoroughly
Salt to taste
2 Tablespoons sesame seeds, toasted
1 clove garlic, minced
2 Tablespoons sweet rice wine or cream sherry
1 Tablespoon soy sauce
1 Tablespoon Asian (dark) sesame oil

Directions:
* Cook the spinach in a large saucepan of boiling salted water until just tender about 30 seconds. Drain the spinach in a colander and rinse with ice water. Blot the spinach leaves dry witth paper towels.
*Combine half the sesame seeds, the garlic, rice wine, soy sauce and the sesame oil in a serving bowl and whisk to mix. Stir in the spinach. Correct the seasoning, adding soy sauce or rice wine as necessary. (The salad should be a little sweet and a little salty.
* Sprinkle the spinach with the remaining sesame seeds and serve room temperature or chilled.

Submitter: 
MONICA SELF
Description: 
This spinach salad has a wonderful nutty flavor from the sesame oil and toasted sesame seeds.Is simple enough to please everyone!!
