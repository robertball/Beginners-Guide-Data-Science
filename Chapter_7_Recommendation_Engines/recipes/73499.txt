Title:
Chicago Fire Italian Turkey Sausage Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Butterball® Turkey Hot Italian Sausage
5 hot dog buns, (large)
5 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
5 tablespoons chopped onion
5 tablespoons pickle relish
10 tomato wedges
Jalapeno peppers, chopped (optional)

Directions:
Preheat grill to medium high heat.
Place sausage on grill rack and cook according to package directions. Place buns on grill rack for 1 minute or until slightly heated.
Place sausages in buns and top each with equal amounts of mustard, onion, relish, tomato wedges and peppers.

Submitter: 
Butterball®
Description: 
Serve with a pasta salad and a sweet ending of brownies, if desired.
