Title:
Coconut Almond Dream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12oz. Cream Cheese
8 oz. Cool Whip
2.5 oz Cream of Coconut
1/2 tsp Almond flavor
1/2 cup Splenda
9" Pie Crust

Directions:
Let cream cheese soften,then blend all on medium until completely mixed.Spread into pie crust and put in frig for 4 hours
Add 1/4 more splenda if you like it sweet.Pumpkin flavor add 15 oz. can pumpkin.
