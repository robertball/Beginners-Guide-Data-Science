Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
2 cups water
3 cups medium egg noodles, uncooked
3/4 pound VELVEETA Pasteurized Prepared Cheese Product, cut up
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Brown meat in large skillet; drain.
Stir in water. Bring to boil.  Stir in noodles.  Reduce heat to medium-low; cover.  Simmer 8 minutes or until noodles are tender.
Add VELVEETA, soup and pepper; stir until VELVEETA is melted.
