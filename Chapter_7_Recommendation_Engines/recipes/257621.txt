Title:
Tropical Mangolicious Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4501745.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 fresh pineapple, chopped into chunks and frozen
1 cup orange juice
1 mango - peeled, seeded, and cut into chunks

Directions:
Combine pineapple chunks, orange juice, and mango chunks in a blender; blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
ChefDaddy
Description: 
This is a deliciously smooth and simple recipe that you and your kids will love. No added sugar... just fruit, juice, and delicious tropical goodness.
