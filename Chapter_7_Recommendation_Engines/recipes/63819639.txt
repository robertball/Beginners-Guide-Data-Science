Title:
Black Beans and Coconut Lime Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Rice Mixture:
4 cups chicken broth
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 tablespoons butter, divided
2 1/2 cups uncooked basmati rice
1 bunch cilantro, finely chopped (a little more than 1 cup chopped)
2 limes, zest and juice
Bean Mixture
2 small onions, chopped, or 1 1/4 cup diced onions
1 red pepper, diced
2 cups dried beans, cooked, or 4 (15-oz.) cans black beans, drained and rinsed
2 cups flaked coconut, toasted
4 teaspoons chili powder
2 teaspoon ground cumin
1 can coconut milk
1 Tablespoon sugar
2 teaspoons salt
Garnish Toppings: lime wedges, diced mango, sliced radishes, green onion, sliced fresh jalapeño peppers, sour cream

Directions:
Cook beans according to package directions, drain and set aside. Toast coconut, set aside.
Rice Mixture: Bring broth to a boil. Stir in salt, pepper, butter and rice. Cover, reduce heat to low, and cook minutes or until rice is tender and water is absorbed. (See package instructions for approx. cooking time, as it varies.)
When rice is fully cooked, add lime juice and zest, and cilantro. Fluff rice with a fork.
Bean Mixture:
Melt 1 Tbsp. butter in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat; add onion and red pepper, and sauté 5 minutes or until tender. Stir in black beans, coconut, chili powder, cumin, salt, sugar, and coconut milk. Cook over medium-low heat, stirring occasionally, 15 minutes. Add chopped green onions just before serving.
Serve bean mixture over rice with desired toppings.
