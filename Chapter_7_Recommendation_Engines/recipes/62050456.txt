Title:
Quick and Easy Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz) package cream cheese
1 (12 oz) tub cool whip
2 pre-made graham cracker crusts
2 cans cherry pie filling

Directions:
In a large bowl mix cream cheese and cool whip, with a mixer, until fluffy and cream cheese is smooth. Evenly poor the mixture into the graham cracker crusts. Top with cherry pie filling. Refrigerate for 4 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
kuiper55
Description: 
This is a cheesecake that is extremely easy to make, and everyone I have every made it for loves it.
