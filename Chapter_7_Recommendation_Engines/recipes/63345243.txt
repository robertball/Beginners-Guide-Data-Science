Title:
Brian & Debi's Chili Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.25 pounds of cooked (medium rare) tri tip cut into 1/4" cubes. Can also use ground turkey, beef or pork.
2 tablespoons chili powder (or more if you like)
1 teaspoon Mexican oregano
1 large onion diced
4 15oz cans of pinquito beans with onions and cumin; undrained (Such as S&W Brand) or 1 29oz can of pinto beans and 1 30oz can chili beans undrained
1 29oz can of tomato sauce
2 tablespoons granulated garlic
1 tablespoons chili garlic sauce
2 4oz cans diced green chili's (hot or mild) undrained

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a 5 quart slow cooker and cook on low for 8 hrs.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
Our favorite chili beans. Best with tri tip.
