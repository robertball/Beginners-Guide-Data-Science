Title:
New York Style Pizza Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2   Cups Warm water
1/2  tsp yeast
4 1/2  Cups Flour
1  Tbs Oil
1 tsp Salt

Directions:
Mix well in mixer. Kneed until dough has even consistancy about 5 min. Let raise 1/2 hr. Divide in 1/2 Roll, let rest at least another 1/2 hr.Preheat oven to 450 deg.  Roll each dough ball to 14 inches place on pizza screen, cover w favorite sauce, toppings and lots of low moisture part skim mozzarella cheese. bake about 15 min until cheese starts to brown.
