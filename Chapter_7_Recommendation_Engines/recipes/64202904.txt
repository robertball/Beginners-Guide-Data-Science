Title:
Cajun Chicken (or Pork Tenderloin Medallions) with Lemon Caper Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cajun Hot and Spicy coating
1 1/2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
3 Tablespoons butter
1 Lemon
1 Cup chicken broth
2 Tablespoons drained capers
4 skinless boneless chicken breasts OR 4 pork tenderloin medallions, sliced 1" or less thick

Directions:
Dredge meat in Cajun coating.  Place in olive oil/butter mixture in skillet over medium-high heat.  Saute until cooked through, about five minutes on each side.  Set aside and tent with foil.
Add broth, lemon, capers to skillet, bringing to a boil.  Be sure to scrape all of the delicious crumbs and coating and stir till reduced by half.
Place meat on bed of rice and spoon sauce over top.

Submitter: 
Brenda
Description: 
A different way to think about chicken or pork.  This tasty, quick and easy recipe tastes like you slaved all day.  The capers are a delightful little surprise with the spicy, lemony goodness.
