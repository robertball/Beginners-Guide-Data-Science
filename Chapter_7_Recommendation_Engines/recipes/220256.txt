Title:
Original Ranch Crispy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/993202.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 ounce) packet Hidden Valley速 Original Ranch速 Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix
1 cup unseasoned dry bread crumbs or corn flakes
1 medium roasting chicken, about 3 pounds, cut into serving pieces

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
Combine seasoning dressing mix with bread crumbs in a gallon-size Glad(R) Food Storage Bag.
Add chicken pieces and seal bag. Shake the bag to evenly coat chicken.
Bake chicken on ungreased baking pan at 375 degrees F for 50 minutes or until juices run clear or an internal temperature of 165 degrees F.
