Title:
Sorrel Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6581209.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
3 tablespoons finely chopped wild or cultivated garlic
1 1/2 cups lightly packed wood sorrel, sheep sorrel, or garden sorrel leaves
1 pint half and half
2 cups vegetable stock
2 pounds potatoes, unpeeled and cut into approximately 1-inch pieces
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon dried or 3/4 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Heat the oil in a pot over low heat until it starts to shimmer
Add the garlic. Cook, stirring, for 1 minute
Add the sorrel and cook, stirring, until it wilts and turns from bright green to a dull khaki color
Add the potatoes, stock, salt, thyme, and pepper
Bring to a boil
Reduce the heat, cover, and simmer until the potatoes are tender, about 20 minutes
Remove from the heat and let cool for 10 minutes
Add half and half
Blend until smooth with an immersion blender
Taste and add more salt if you think it needs it
Serve hot or chilled
Drizzle with a little cream and garnish with a sprig of sorrel
Serve with a fresh green salad
