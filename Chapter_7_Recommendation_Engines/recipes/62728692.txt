Title:
Chicken Quinoa Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked quinoa
3 cups boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cubed (about 5 breasts)
2 tsp. olive oil
Salt and pepper, to taste
1 cup celery, chopped
1 cup carrots, chopped
1/2 cup red bell pepper, chopped
1/2 cup yellow bell pepper, chopped
1 medium yellow onion, chopped
2 tbsp garlic, chopped
2 cups spinach leaves, chopped
1/2 tsp. marjoram
1/2 tsp. basil
1/2 tsp. bay leaves
1/2 tsp. oregano
8 cups chicken broth
1/3 cup cornstarch
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Heat 1 tsp. olive oil over medium-high heat in dutch oven.  Add chicken and salt and pepper to taste. Cook chicken until no longer pink.  Remove chicken from pan and set aside.
Heat 1 tsp. olive oil in pan.  Add celery, carrots, red and yellow bell pepper, onion, and garlic.  Cook until vegetables are soft.
Add water, spinach, marjoram, basil, bay leaves, oregano, water, and chicken broth.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes.
Add chicken and quinoa, cook for an additional 2 minutes or until heated throughout.
In a small bowl or cup, dissolve cornstarch in 1/4 cup cold water.  Add slowly, stirring occasionally.  Simmer for one minute.

Description: 
Quinoa is a tasty, protein-filled grain that makes a great addition to chicken soup!  Add some carrots, celery, spinach, onions and bell peppers for amazing color and flavor.
