Title:
Macaroons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1036407.jpg
Ingredients:
100 grams confectioners sugar
75 grams almond flour (sifted)
75 grams granulated sugar
2 egg whites
3 drops of food coloring (optional)

Directions:
1. Preheat the oven to 150 Celsius. Cover a cookie sheet with aluminum foil.
2. In a small bowl, mix the sifted almond flour with the confectioners sugar. Set aside.
3. Beat the egg whites until soft peaks form. Slowly add the granulated sugar until well incorporated.
4. Add spoonfuls of the almond mixture to the egg whites beating at slow speed.
5. Add food color and mix with a spatula until you reach your desired color.
6. Transfer the mixture to a bag and pipe small circles an inch apart in the prepared cookie sheet.
7. Slightly drop the cookie sheet on the table to let the air out.
8. Let the macaroons set for 40 minutes
9. Bake for 17 minutes. Let the cool completely and sandwich them with the filling of your choice
10. Enjoy the delicious macaroons =)
