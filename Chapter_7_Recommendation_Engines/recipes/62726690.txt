Title:
Southwest Pork Chops In Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 pork chops, 3/4-in. thick, about 2 1/2 #)
15 oz. can Mexican-style or Tex-Mex-style chili beans
1 1/4 C bottled salsa
1 C. fresh (2 med. ears) or frozen whole kernel corn
2 C hot cooked rice
Snipped fresh cilantro (opt.)

Directions:
Trim excess fat from chops. Place chops in bottom of 3 1/2 or 4 qt. crockery cooker. Add chili beans and salsa. Cover; cook on high-heat setting for 2 1/2 or low-heat setting for 5 hrs. When that step is complete, turn to high setting and finish cooking if necessary. Stir in corn. Cover and cook for 30 min. Serve over rice. Sprinkle w/cilantro, if desired.
For all day cooking: Substitute boneless pork chops for the rib chops. Cover and cook on low heat for 9 1/2 hrs. Turn to high heat and stir in corn. Cover and cook 30 min longer.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Pork chops cooked with chili beans, salsa, and corn, served over rice.
