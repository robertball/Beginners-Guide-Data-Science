Title:
Budget Friendly Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/498360.jpg
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 8 oz. tub of sour cream
1 8 oz. can of Cream of Chicken Soup
1 Roll of Ritz Crackers
4 tbsp. butter
1 pack shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Cook chicken and cube set aside
Mix cream soup and sour cream together and put the mixture in the bottom of a small casserole dish.
Place cubed chicken on top of the sour cream and chicken soup mixture. Add shredded cheddar cheese on top of the chicken.
Crush the Ritz crackers over the dish, melt butter and pour over top of the crackers.
Bake in the oven for 25-30 minutes until hot and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Angel
Description: 
This casserole is cheap and easy. I make this dish whenever I have left over baked chicken, fried chicken or turkey.  If I am entertaining I double or triple the recipe.
