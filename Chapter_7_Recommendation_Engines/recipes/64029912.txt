Title:
Electric Lightning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1119851.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 oz. vodka
1/2 oz. coconut rum
1/2 oz. blue Curacao
1 oz. lemon-lime soda
lemon wedge for garnish

Directions:
Over ice in a shaker glass pour the vodka, coconut rum and blue Curacao and stir.  Add the soda and stir gently.  Pour into a cocktail glass and garnish with a lemon wedge.

Submitter: 
SANDI149
Description: 
This is a perfect drink for summer or anytime of year as it makes a wonderful presentation because of it’s color.  Make a big batch of them for all your friends.  It’s quite easy to make too!
