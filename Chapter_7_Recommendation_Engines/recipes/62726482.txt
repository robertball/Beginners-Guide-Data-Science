Title:
Crescent Chicken Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C cooked chicken or turkey
8 oz can water chestnuts
Fresh or frozen broccoli (if fresh, parboil)
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can mushrooms
1/2 C onion, chopped
1/2 C celery, chopped
1/2 C sour cream
1 can refrigerator crescent rolls
1/2 C sliced almonds
2-4 T melted butter

Directions:
Mix together, the chicken, water chestnuts, broccoli, soup and mushrooms. Put in a 9 x13-in pan.
Combine the mayo, onion, celery and sour cream and pour over chicken mixture. Bake in 350 degree oven until bubbly.
Top with crescent rolls spread out over dish. Top with almonds and melted butter.
Return to oven and bake about 20 min.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Chicken, water chestnuts, broccoli, cream of chicken soup, mushrooms, onions, celery, sour cream and mayo are baked in casserole dish and then topped with refrigerator crescent rolls and almonds
