Title:
Manhattan Clam Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Ribs of Celery diced
1 medium Onion diced
½ 16oz bag of Baby Carrots diced
1 14.5oz can Whole White Potatoes diced
3 14.5oz cans of Stewed Tomatoes
4 6.5oz cans of Minced Clams
2 8oz bottles of Clam Juice
Worcestershire Sauce to taste
Black Pepper to taste
Garlic to taste
Basil to taste
Olive Oil, about 4 tbs

Directions:
Saute celery, onions and carrots in olive oil, garlic pepper and basil (to taste) in large pot
Set aside half of the sautéed veggies in a food processor
To pot add 2 cans of the stewed tomatoes
Take 3rd can of stewed tomatoes and drain, add the drained tomatoes to the food processor and puree with the veggies, add to pot.
Add diced potatoes.
Add undrained clams and clam juice to pot. Simmer 15-20 minutes (or longer)
Splash in worcestershire sauce to taste
Serve with crusty bread and enjoy!!
