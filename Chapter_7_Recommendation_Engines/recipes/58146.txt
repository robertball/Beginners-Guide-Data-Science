Title:
Sweetheart Kisses Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981342.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (20 ounce) package SUGAR COOKIE DOUGH (purchased or your favorite recipe)
HERSHEY®'S Cocoa Powder
48 HERSHEY®'S KISSES® Milk Chocolates, unwrapped *
1 teaspoon shortening (not butter, margarine, spread or oil)

Directions:
Heat oven to required temperature.  Divide dough in half; roll out one half at a time to 1/4-inch thickness following package or recipe directions.  Cut out with 2-inch heart shaped cookie cutters;  place on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake according to package or recipe directions.  Cool completely on cooling racks.  Sprinkle cookies with cocoa.

Submitter: 
HersheysKitchens.com
Description: 
Turn your sugar cookies into a romantic gesture with a kiss - a Hershey's KISS, that is! Use pre-prepared sugar cookie dough or your favorite recipe, and garnish with a sprinkle of cocoa, a melted chocolate design and a Hershey's KISS for the sweetest sugar cookie ever!
