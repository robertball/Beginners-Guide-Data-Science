Title:
Low-Fat Cherry Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/134502.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup fat free vanilla yogurt
1 1/2 cups sugar
6 egg whites
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 (20 ounce) can reduced-sugar cherry pie filling

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, beat yogurt and sugar. Add egg whites; mix well. Combine flour and baking powder; stir into yogurt mixture. Pour into a 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking pan coated with nonstick cooking spray. Spoon cherry filling on top. Bake at 350 degrees F for 40-50 minutes or until filling is bubbly and toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Mary  Dudek
Description:
