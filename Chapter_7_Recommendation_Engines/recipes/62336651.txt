Title:
Rae's Italian BS Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/388494.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz Tri-colored spiral pasta
1/3 c Italian dressing
2 green onions chopped
10 oz baby spinach
1 pound bacon
3-4 diced roma tomatoes
6 oz can black Olives, sliced
16 oz Creamy Italian Dressing

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions.
While pasta is cooking fry the bacon until crispy, drain and crumble. Reserve 2 tablespoons of the grease.
Wilt spinach using reserved bacon grease.
Toss cooked pasta with 1/3 cup Italian dressing, add in spinach, green onions, and bacon. Stir in Creamy Italian dressing.
Stir in roma tomatoes and olives. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
Rae
Description: 
This pasta salad comes together easily and is great for summer get together's. My family requests this all the time. I like to play with different veggies to add in variety. So play around with your favorites.
