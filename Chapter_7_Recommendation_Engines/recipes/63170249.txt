Title:
Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 med. Portobello Mushrooms (3-4in. diameter)
1 Tbsp. Italian salad dressing or olive oil
3 med. plum tomatoes, seeded and chopped
2 Tbsp snipped fresh basil leaves
2 garlic cloves, pressed
3 oz shredded Italian cheese blend (3/4c.)
2 Tbsp seasoned dry bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425Â°F. Brush outside only of mushroom caps with dressing. Place mushrooms, gill side up, in deep dish baker.
Place tomatoes and basil in small bowl. Press garlic into bowl. Add cheese and bread crumbs; mix gently.
Scoop mixture evenly into mushroom caps. Bake 16-18 miuntes or until mushrooms are tender. Remove mushrooms to serving plate, cut in half.
