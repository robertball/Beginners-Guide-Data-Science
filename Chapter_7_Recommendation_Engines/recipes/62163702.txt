Title:
Fruit Salad with Walnuts and Graham Cracker topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 apple gala or golden delicious, chopped
1 Banana halved length wise, and cut into moons 1/4" thick
3/4 c. sliced strawberries
1/2 c. blueberries
1 c. cut up pineapple
1 c. purple grapes halved
1 c. mandarin oranges
2 tabl. lemon juice to preserve fruit color
1/2 c. chopped walnuts
1/2 c. graham cracker crumbs

Directions:
In a large bowl combine the fruit, and lemon juice. fold in nuts. (you can add mini marshmallows, raisins, or vanilla yogurt) Place in the frig to cool for 1-2 hours. Before serving sprinkle with graham cracker crumbs. Enjoy!
You can play around with this recipe by adding a variate of different fruit, or dried cranberries, cherries, or raisins!

Submitter: 
Mommy bakes
Description: 
This is a traditional summer fruit salad, I tossed in walnuts for an added crunch, along with a graham cracker topping!
