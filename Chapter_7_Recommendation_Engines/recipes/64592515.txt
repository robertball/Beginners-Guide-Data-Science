Title:
Refried Bean Avocado Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3884797.jpg
Ingredients:
¼ Cup Canned Fat Free Refried Beans
¼ cup Smashed Avocado
Dash of fresh cracked salt
Dash of garlic powder
Dash of chili powder
Dash of cumin
½ teaspoon lime juice
1 Slice Whole Wheat Bread
1 Egg
1 Teaspoon Pico de Gallo
Dash of Cholula Hot Sauce
1 Teaspoon Queso Fresca

Directions:
Toast 1 piece of whole wheat bread.
Mashed half an avocado and add dash of cracked salt, garlic powder, chili powder, cumin, and lime juice.
Cook egg to your style.
Take the piece of toast, spread beans, then avocado mash.
Top with egg, pico de gallo, hot sauce, and sprinkle with cheese.
