Title:
T.T. 's Famous Broccoli, Cheese and Bacon Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/290049.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups milk
4 eggs, beaten
6 ounces shredded Mexican Cheese Mix
2 tablespoons butter, melted
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon McCormick Season
1/4 cup finely chopped bell pepper
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
8 slices bacon turkey
1/2 cup of broccoli

Directions:
1.  Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain, crumble and set aside.
2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9 inch pie pan.
3. Line bottom of pie crust with cheese and crumbled bacon and broccoli. Combine eggs, butter, onion, pepper, salt, flour and milk; whisk together until smooth; pour into pie pan over the broccoli, bacon and cheese mix.
4. Bake in preheated oven for 35 minutes, until set. Serve hot or cold.
