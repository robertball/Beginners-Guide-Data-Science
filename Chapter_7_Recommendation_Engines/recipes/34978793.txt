Title:
Jamaica Freeze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 oz. Malibu Pineapple rum
3/4 oz. Midori
2 oz. pina colada mix
1 oz. banana puree
ice

Directions:
Blend everything together with ice until nice and smooth. Pour into a tall glass and garnish with a slice of fresh pineapple, a pineapple leaf and a cherry.

Submitter: 
Jacki_In_Simi
Description: 
Otis had one of these at Applebee's and loved it...thus, my search began and I found this on the internet as a 'copy' of the restaurant's recipe.
