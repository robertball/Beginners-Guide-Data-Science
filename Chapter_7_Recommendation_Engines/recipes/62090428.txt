Title:
Cheddar Cauliflower Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. margarine
½ cup chopped onion
¼ cup flour
1 ½ cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 ½ tsp. parsley
3 cups chopped cauliflower
10 oz. can chicken broth
2 ½ cups milk
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt margarine and sauté onions in a saucepan.
Stir in cauliflower and broth and bring to a boil, then simmer 12-15 minutes.
Wisk flour and milk together in a separate bowl, then add to saucepan.
Cook until thickened and add cheese and salt and pepper. Stir until cheese melts.
Add parsley before serving.
