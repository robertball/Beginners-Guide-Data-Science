Title:
Carmelized Onion and Bacon Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30-40 mini red or white potatoes
1 large red onion, sliced 1/8 inch thick
3-4 oz white wine
1 tsp. dried tarragon
4-5 rashers bacon
1/2 - 3/4c. prepared tzadziki sauce
5 tbsp finely chopped chives
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil mini potatoes in salted water for 10 minutes or until just fork tender. Drain and let cool.
Saute onions over medium heat (no oil).
Stir occasionally, letting onions soften and develop golden brown appearance (20 minutes or so).
Add dried tarragon and deglaze pan with white wine.
Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to evaporate wine.
Cut bacon into 1/2 inch pieces and cook until desired crispiness. Remove from pan and drain additional fat.
Cut potatoes in half and place in bowl with onions and bacon.
Add tdaziki sauce, salt, pepper and chives to bowl.
Fold all ingredients together.
Can be served warm or chilled.
Serves 6-8 people.
Leftovers can be stored covered for up to 4 days

Submitter: 
Ellen Violet-Muir
Description: 
I was looking for a lower calorie way to make my husband's favourite summer side dish...my traditional potato salad was laden with eggs and mayo!  The tzadziki dip as dressing certainly did the trick! The tarragon in the onions gives that hint of what is that? to this recipe!
