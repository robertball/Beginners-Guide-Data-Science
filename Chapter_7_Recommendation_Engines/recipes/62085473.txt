Title:
Grillatos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup tomatoes, diced
1 cup red onions, sliced then halved
1/2 cup sundried tomatoes
3 tbsp. green chili's
1/8 cup lime juice
1 clove of minced garlic, or equivilant of garlic paste
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup whipping cream

Directions:
In a saucepan, mix together all ingredients EXCEPT for the cream. Simmer until the onions are soft. Add the cream, and simmer until it is a saucelike consistancy. Serve over cooked linguine. Excellent when topped with cooked chicken or shrimp.

Submitter: 
erin mc
Description: 
This is an imitation of a dish that Earls used to serve, its easy to make, and tastes like you put a lot more work into it.
