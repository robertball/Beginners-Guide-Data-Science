Title:
Avocado & Tilapia Ceviche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1119675.jpg
Ingredients:
2-2.5 lbs Tilapia
4.5 tsp minced garlic (8-10 Cloves)
1 tsp salt
1/2tsp black pepper
2 tsp chopped cilantro
1 habanero pepper
Lime Juice, Enough to cover all ingredients (about 30 Fl Oz)
2-3 small semi firm Avocados
1/2 large red onion
1/8 tsp ground ginger (optional)
Small Flour tortillas (optional)

Directions:
Cube the tilapia and Avocados. Mince habanero pepper. Finely chop cilantro. Once chopped and measured add all ingredients except onions into bowl. Pour enough lime juice to cover all ingredients.Mix together to evenly distribute. Chop onion into fine wedges. Place on top of the mixture but do not submerge in the lime juice. Cover and place in the fridge for at least 4 hours.

Submitter: 
delugatron
Description: 
This is a twist on a simple Peruvian Ceviche. The avocados add some bulk and creamy texture. While tortillas make it quick and easy to eat. Save left over juice as an excellent hangover cure.
