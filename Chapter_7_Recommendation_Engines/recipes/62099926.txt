Title:
Patty's Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 heads of broccoli (florets only)
1 jar diced pimentos
1 cup grated parmesan cheese
3 tablespoons butter
1/2 vidalia onion- diced
3 stalks celery - diced
1 tbsp basil
1 tbsp oregano
salt and pepper

Directions:
steam broccoli until crisp tender (5 mins)
in skillet melt 1 tbsp butter, add breadcrumbs and toast until light brown
saute celery and onion in 2 tbsp butter
Layer broccoli, onion and celery, parmesan cheese, toasted breadcrumbs, basil and oregano, salt and pepper.  Toss lightly with tongs and top with pimentos.
can be prepared the day before and refriderated.  Cook in 375 oven for 25 mins before serving.

Submitter: 
LAURSOX
Description: 
A beautiful and tasty broccoli dish for those that are looking for a little something special for the holidays.
