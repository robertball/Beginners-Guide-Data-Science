Title:
Grandma Shirley's Pumpkin Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
2 cups sugar
2 t. baking soda
1/2 t. salt
1 t. cinnamon
4 eggs
1 cup oil
2 cups pumpkin (a small can)
1 (12 oz) bag semi sweet chocolate chips
1 cup nuts (OPTIONAL)

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees
Combine flour, sugar, baking soda, salt and cinnamon. Mix well with whisk.
In separate bowl, combine eggs, oil and pumpkin.
Add to dry ingredients.
Mix in chocolate chips.
Pour into well greased bunt pan. (use bakers spray)
Bake at 350 for 1 1/4 hours, checking with toothpick at thickest part for doneness.

Submitter: 
Lindsay
Description: 
This is a favorite recipe of mine that was handed down to me by my mother from my grandmother.  I have wonderful memories of eating this with my grandparents as a young child.  It's wonderfully moist and rich!  Comes together in a flash.  Best with a cold glass of milk!
