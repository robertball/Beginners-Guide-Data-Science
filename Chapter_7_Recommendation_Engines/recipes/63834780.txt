Title:
German Chocolate Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065711.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
12 (1 ounce) squares German sweet chocolate
1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme
4 1/2 cups sugar
2 tablespoons butter
1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Spray a 9x13 inch baking dish with vegetable spray.
In a large bowl combine chocolate chips, German sweet chocolate, marshmallow creme and vanilla.
In a heavy 4 quart sauce pan, combine sugar, butter, evaporated milk, salt and vanilla
Bring to a boil over medium heat.
Boil for 6 minutes, stirring constantly.
Pour hot syrup over chocolate mixture.
Stir with wooden spoon until smooth.
Pour into prepared 9x13 inch pan.
Let stand until firm; cut into squares.
