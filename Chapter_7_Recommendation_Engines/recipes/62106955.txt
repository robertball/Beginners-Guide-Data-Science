Title:
Easy Bacon, Onion & cheese stuffed burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3lbs. ground beef
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
3 TBS BBQ Sauce
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 onion - finely diced
1/2 pound of bacon cut into small pieces
shredded cheese

Directions:
Combine the first 6 ingredients for hamburger patties & set aside.
Cook & drain bacon.
Saute the onions in the remaining bacon drippings.
In a small bowl, combine the bacon and onions.
Forming the patties: take a small handful (approx.1/4 lb) of meat and shape a thin pattie.
Add 1-1/2 TBS of Bacon & onion mixture and some shredded cheese to taste.
Make another same size thin pattie, and carefully place over the first pattie, pressing edges together to seal.
Grill or panfry sealed pattie,for about 2-3 minutes per side,until cooked throughout.
Serve on hamburger buns with condiments.

Submitter: 
AmyThompson
Description: 
Wonderful stuffed burger that can be easily changed to suit different tastes.  My kids who normally don't have onions on their burgers REALLY enjoyed these.  We served with french fries & root beer floats!
