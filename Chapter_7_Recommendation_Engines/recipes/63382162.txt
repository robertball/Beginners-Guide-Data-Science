Title:
Longhorn Steakhouse" Like" Parmesan Crusted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/959059.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Boneless chicken breasts, cut in half
3/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
1/4 cup plain bread crumbs
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup ranch dressing
1 cup shredded provolone cheese
1 T oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400
1. In small Bowl mix together mayonnaise and 1/4 Parmesan Cheese.
2. In another small bowl mix together Bread crumbs and 1/4 cup of cheese and 1T oil.
3, In another bowl mix together ranch dressing and rest of cheese.
4. Using a pastry brush brush chicken tops with mayonnaise mixed with grated cheese.
2. Mix cheese and bread crumbs together and place on chicken tops (saving some for later.)
3. Place in oven and bake for 40 minutes.
4. Open oven and put ranch dressing and remaining bread crumbs on top of chicken. Top with Shredded Provolone.
5. Cook for 5-10 minutes more until browned.
