Title:
Chef Jeff's Oven Baked Mexican Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/772849.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ½  cups long grain rice
1/3 cup veg. oil
2 TBL chicken base
3 cups warm water
1 cup white onions (diced)
1 tsp plain sea salt
1 tsp white pepper
2 tsp mexican oregano
½ tsp ground cumin
2 tsp paprika
2 tsp onion powder
2 tsp garlic powder
2 cups frozen corn
1 cup diced green bell peppers
2 TBL diced pimintos

Directions:
Add oil to a large hot skillet.  Next, add the dry rice and the paprika and turn the heat to medium to medium high.  Then, cook the dry rice until it turns light to medium brown.  Dissolve the chicken base in the warm water and add to casserole dish.  After the rice starts to turn brown, add to the casserole dish.  Next, add onions, salt, pepper, cumin, onion powder and garlic powder. Stir gently to incorporate all the sesonings. Bake at 400 degrees for about 45 minutes.  After removing from the oven, remove lid and start fluffing and stirring rice very carefully.  After 5 minutes, fold in frozen corn, diced green bell peppers and diced red bell peppers (pimintos) and rice is ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Jeffrey Morris
Description: 
When I eat at Mexican Restaurants, I always loved their rice. I have tried a few different versions of Mexican Rice.  This one is the best.
