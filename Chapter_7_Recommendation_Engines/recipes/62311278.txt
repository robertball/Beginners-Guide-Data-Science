Title:
Shrimp-licious Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Red Onion, halved and sliced 1/2”
1 Red Bell Pepper, seeded, halved and sliced 1/2”
1 Green Bell Pepper, seeded, halved and sliced 1/2”
1 Yellow Bell Pepper, seeded, halved and sliced 1/2”
1 Orange Bell Pepper, seeded, halved and sliced 1/2”
3 lbs. large raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
MARINADE:
1 cup seasoned rice vinegar
2 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
2 med. cloves garlic, crushed
1/2 tablespoon dried garlic powder
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon ginger powder
Freshly ground pepper

Directions:
Place marinade ingredients in a ziplock bags and massage well.
Add shrimp and veggies. Get all air out of bag. Place in refrigerator.
Turn bag over from time to time to evenly distribute the marinade through the shrimp.
Marinate for 4 – 4 1/2 hours.
Place in a BBQ basket, sprinkle with Kosher salt and cook until pink, turning constantly.
