Title:
Latin Couscous Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chicken stock
1 cup whole wheat couscous
1 pint grape tomatoes or cherry tomatoes, halved
1/2 cup cilantro, chopped
4 green onions, sliced
1 red chili, diced
1 (15 oz.) can lima beans
1/2 cup Queso Fresco, crumbled
3 tablespoons lemon juice
3 tablespoons olive oil
1/8 teaspoon white pepper

Directions:
1. Bring chicken broth to boil in medium saucepan: add coucous, remove from heat, cover, and let stand 5 minutes.
2. Fluff cousous with fork and transfer to bolw.
3. Stir in tomatoes, cilantro, red chili, green onions, lima beans, and Queso Fresco.
4. Whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, and pepper.
5. lightly toss salad and dressing, and chill for 2 hours or more.

Submitter: 
Debbie Lopez
Description: 
My love of Latin food and a whole grain diet were the inspiration for this salad.
