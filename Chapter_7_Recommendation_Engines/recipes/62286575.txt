Title:
Santa Fe Wrap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c. garbanzo beans (drained)
1/4 c. ricotta
2 T. tub-style cream cheese
1 T. fresh lime juice
1 garlic clove
2 oz. crumbled feta
1/4 c. black beans
1 T. fresh minced cilantro
1/2 tsp. hot sauce
4 tortillas
1 1/3 c. thinly sliced lettuce
3/4 c. peeled jicama
6 T. jack cheese, shredded
3 T. finely diced red onion

Directions:
Process first 5 ingredients in blender or processor till smooth.  Add feta, black beans, cilantro, hot sauce; pulse 4 or 5 times till combined.
Spread 1 1/2 T. bean mixture over each tortilla; top with 1/3 c. lettuce.  Divide jicama and remaining ingredients evenly among tortillas.  Roll up.
Cut each wrap in half diagonally.
