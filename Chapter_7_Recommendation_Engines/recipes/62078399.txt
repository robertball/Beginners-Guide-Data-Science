Title:
Ramen Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. hamburger (or turkey burger)
1/4 C dried onion
1 sm can mushrooms
2 pkgs Country gravy mix
2 Cups water
2 seasoning packets from Ramen noodles
1 C Sour Cream
2 pkgs Ramen Noodles
4 cups water

Directions:
Brown hamburger, drain. Add onion and mushroom, saute over med-low heat.  Add gravy mix and 2 cups water, stir well.  Stir in seasoning packets.  Heat mixture just to boiling, or until thickened.  (Add more or less water to desired thickness).  Remove from heat and stir in Sour Cream.
In sepaparate pan, boil 4 cups water, add Ramen noodles. Continue to boil for 3 minutes.  Remove from heat, drain the water.  Add noodles to meat mixture.

Submitter: 
anmlnrs
Description: 
A great way to use up Ramen Noodles and hamburger.  Leftovers can be frozen.
