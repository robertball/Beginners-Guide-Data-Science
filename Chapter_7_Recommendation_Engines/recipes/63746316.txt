Title:
ultimate blt Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
2 slices 7 grain or sourdough bread, lightly toasted
handful of arugula (optional/debatable, could also sub iceberg, romaine or watercress)
3 thick sliced of tomato
4 strips cooked bacon
salt and black pepper to taste

Directions:
heat a small skillet over medium heat, coat with olive oil cooking spray and add the egg. cook sunny side up until the white is set but yolk is runny. line bottom half of bread with arugula followed by tomato and then bacon. set the cooked egg on top and season with a pinch of salt and plently of fresh cracked pepper. top with second slice of bread
