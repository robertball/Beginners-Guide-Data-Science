Title:
Lemon Couscous Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tablespoon oil, olive, extra-virgin
1 cup(s) broccoli florets
1/2 cup(s) couscous, 100% whole-wheat
3/4 cup(s) chicken, breast (cooked), chopped
1/2 medium lemon, juiced, (about 1 1/2 tablespoons)
1/8 teaspoon lemon zest

Directions:
In a large skillet, bring 1 1/4 cups of water, oil and broccoli to a boil. Stir in the couscous, chicken, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Remove from the heat. Cover and let stand for 5 minutes. Fluff lightly with a fork.
Chill well and serve cold.
