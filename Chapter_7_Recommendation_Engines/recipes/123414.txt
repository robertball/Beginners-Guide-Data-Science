Title:
French Salad Dressing Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980643.jpg
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons dried parsley flakes
1 tablespoon sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons ground mustard
1 1/2 teaspoons onion powder
1 1/2 teaspoons paprika
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
3/4 teaspoon pepper
3/4 teaspoon garlic powder
ADDITIONAL INGREDIENTS:
3/4 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup vinegar
2 tablespoons water

Directions:
Combine the first eight ingredients in a resealable plastic bag.  Store in a cool dry place.

Submitter: 
Darlene  Brenden
Description: 
Darlene also shares the recipe for her tangy homemade salad dressing. 'It's more economical than store-bought, and we prefer the taste,' she notes. 'The dressing keeps well in the fridge.'
