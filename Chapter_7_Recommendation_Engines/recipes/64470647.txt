Title:
Marinade for pork chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. soy sauce
1/4 c. marmalade
1 T. brown mustard
1 t. minced garlic
1 (scant) t. sesame oil

Directions:
Mix all ingredients; pour over chops in plastic bag.  Marinate all day if possible.

Submitter:

Description: 
delicious as a marinade for grilled chops.
