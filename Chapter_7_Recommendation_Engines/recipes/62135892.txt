Title:
Easy Fish Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb mild freshwater fish (tilapia or catfish work well)
2 tbs taco seasoning
3 cloves garlic (pressed)
3 tbs Olive oil
1 lime
1/2 cup fresh chopped cilantro
2 tbs Pace Picante Sauce
1/4 cup of sour cream
soft Taco shells
1 cup Shredded lettuce
1 Chopped Tomato
1 bunch of green onions chopped
Shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a small bowl or mug, press the garlic and add the taco seasoning.
Whisk in enough of the olive oil until it is a thick liquid.
Brush both sides of the fish liberally with the garlic mixture.
Fry in a non stick pan both sides until done (approximately 5 minutes on each side).
Place the fish inside a medium sized bowl.
Add the cilantro and the juice of the lime (Start with one half and add more if desired).
Mix the fish with the lime/cilantro until mixed and broken into smaller pieces.
Mix the sour cream and picante sauce together and spread on warm tortillas.
Add fish mixture, and top with green oinion, lettuce, tomato cheese.
Wash down with a Margarita or Mojito.
