Title:
speghetti bolognese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/472605.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 medium onion chopped
1 can of diced tomatoes (15 ounce)
1 can of tomato sauce (8 ounce)
4 bay leaves
1 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
1 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
8 ounces thin spaghetti
parmesan for garnish

Directions:
heat large skillet over medium heat; add ground beef and onion. cook till browned and crumbly, drain. stir in undrained tomatoes and tomato sauce.
all bay leaves, seasoning and worcestershire sauce to skillet; cover. simmer for about 25 minutes, until slightly thickened. stirring occasionally. remove bay leaves.
while sauce is simmering, cook pasta according to package directions.
spoon sauce over hot spaghetti and enjoy.
