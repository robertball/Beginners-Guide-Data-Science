Title:
Jim's Blackened Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/418559.jpg
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 cup Worcestershire sauce
2 teaspoons ground black pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
In a large skillet combine the Worcestershire sauce,garlic powder and ground black pepper. Add the chicken and simmer over high heat turning the chicken often. make sure and get both sides coated. The sauce will thicken as it cooks adhering to the  chicken breasts. There should be no sauce left in the pan after you are done cooking the chicken. Serve with rice or as a sandwich.
