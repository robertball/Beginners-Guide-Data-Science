Title:
Sushi Shrimp Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups cooked medium grain rice, room temperature
2 cups cucumber, diced and seeded
1 cup thawed frozen peas
1/2 cup thinly-sliced radishes
1/4 cup sliced green onions
1 lb. large (31-35/lb.) cooked shrimp
1 1/2 quarts fresh spinach leaves
salt to taste
Dressing:
1/2 cup seasoned rice vinegar
2 Tbs. fresh ginger, minced
2 Tbs. prepared horseradish
2 Tbs. Asian sesame oil

Directions:
In large bowl, combine all ingredients except shrimp and spinach. Gently mix in shrimp. Arrange spinach leaves on platter and spoon salad into center. Season to taste with salt if needed.
