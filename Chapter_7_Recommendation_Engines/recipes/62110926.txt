Title:
Easy Spaghetti and Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
1 jar of your favorite spaghetti sauce
1/2 cup graded cheddar cheese
1/3 cup sliced olives
Spaghetti noodles

Directions:
Preaheat oven to 350
Cook Spaghetti noodles according to package.
Spray 8x8 glass dish with cooking spray.
Place chicken breasts in dish and bake for 10 minutes.
Remove the dish, turn chicken over and add 1/2 jar of spaghetti sauce.
Continue baking for 5 minutes.
Remove the dish again and add cheddar cheese and olives on top and bake for 3-5 more minutes.  Make sure chicken is done.

Submitter: 
emilywise
Description: 
Fast and easy weeknight meal.  You'll wow your family with the chicken served over spaghetti.
