Title:
Mushroom Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2117568.jpg
Ingredients:
olive oil
butter
8 cloves garlic
1/4 yellow onion
several cups of mushrooms (shitake, cremini)
thyme, rosemary, fresh parsley
16 oz spaghetti
freshly grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
heat olive oil and butter
saute garlic, onion, mushrooms until soft
add thyme, rosemary, little parsley
cook noodles
mixed half noodles with mushrooms and saved the rest
serve with parsley, freshly grated parmesan cheese
