Title:
Gorganzola Stuffed Chicken Breasts Wrapped in Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
1/4 cup gorganzola cheese
2 tablespoons parsley, minced
2 tablespoons shallot, minced
1 clove garlic, minced
4 strips thick sliced applewood smoked bacon
Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Wash chicken breats and pat dry with paper towels. Cut a slit into each breast half approximately 2 inches long and 1 to 1 1/2 inches deep.
Combine gorganzola, shallots, garlic and parsley in a small bowl. Add salt & pepper to taste. Divide mixture in half and stuff in to chicken breasts.
Wrap 2 strips of bacon around each half and secure with toothpicks. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste. Place in a baking dish that has been sprayed with non-stick cooking spray. Bake for approximately 35 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
