Title:
Green amaranth and chicken soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups of chicken or vegetable stock
1 medium carrot, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1 table spoon of olive oil
1 bay leaf
3 garlic scapes
2 medium potatoes, chopped
3 cups of chopped amaranth leaves
1 handful of chopped scallions
1 handful of chopped parsley
boiled chicken
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat up stock. In the meantime, saute onions and carrots.  When onions are translucent, add vegetables to the boiling stock. Add potatoes and bay leaf and cook for about 10-15 minutes until potatoes are almost ready. Add garlic scapes and amaranth leaves.  Cook for another 5-10 minutes.  Add scallions, parsley, salt and pepper. Chop boiled chicken into bawls, pour soup on top and serve.

Submitter: 
soulgraft
Description: 
Summery light soup. Chicken can be substituted with boiled egg, amaranth with spinach and garlic scapes with regular garlic.
