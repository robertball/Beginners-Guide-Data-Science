Title:
Yummy Oven Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1443056.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Large boneless and skinless chicken breasts
1/4-1/2 tsp salt
1/4-1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder
2 tbs of light mayonnaise
2 1/2 cups of cornflakes
1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese
1 tbs dried parsley flakes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 F.
Rub salt, pepper, and garlic powder on chicken.
Rub light mayonnaise on chicken.
Crush cornflakes in a plastic bag.
Mix in Parmesan cheese and parley flakes in with the crushed cornflakes.
Shake chicken pieces one at a time into plastic bag until completely coated.
Place chicken pieces in a 9x13 baking dish that has been sprayed with PAM (cooking oil spray).
Bake in oven for 60-70 minutes.

Submitter: 
Marge Anchor De Leon
Description: 
You will be pleasantly surprised at how scrumptious this chicken turns out, but without the high calorie guilt. You will never miss fried chicken again!
