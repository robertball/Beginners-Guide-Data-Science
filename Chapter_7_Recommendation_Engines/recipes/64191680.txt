Title:
Cinnamon Apple Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. Two 11" flour tortillas
2. 1 diced honey crisp apple
3. 2 tbs light corn syrup
4. 1 tbs honey
5. 1 tbs maple syrup
6. Ground cinnamon (to taste)
7. Pinch of brown sugar

Directions:
1. Add diced apple to a round skillet at low/med heat
2. Add light corn syrup, honey, maple syrup, cinnamon, and brown sugar to apples and cook for 8-10 minutes (stir every 2-3 minutes)
3. Remove apple mixture from skillet and transfer to a bowl. Put first tortilla in the hot skillet and immediately add apples back on top of the first tortilla. Quickly put second tortilla on top of that in the skillet
4. Cook until bottom of the tortilla is golden brown (1-3 minutes), then carefully flip over quesadilla and cook for an additional 1-3 minutes
5. Remove quesadilla from skillet and serve warm immediately, enjoy!

Submitter: 
Vera
Description: 
One of my favorite quick, breakfast treats. You can make it gluten free by using corn tortillas!
