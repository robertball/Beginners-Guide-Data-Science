Title:
Easy Broccoli Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg broccoli slaw mix
2 pks ramen noodle soup - beef flavor (do not cook the noodles just break them up into pieces)
1/2 c oil
1/3 c vinegar
1/2 c sugar (can also substitute Splenda)

Directions:
Mix sugar, oil, vinegar and beef packs for dressing. Pour over slaw mix. Add broken noodles and refrigerate overnight.
