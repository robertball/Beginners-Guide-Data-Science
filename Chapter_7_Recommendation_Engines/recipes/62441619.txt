Title:
Dad's Hot Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 eggs, beaten
3 cups milk
4 cups flour
1 ¼ cups sugar
6 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
1 ½ tsp. salt
Pinch of nutmeg
2/3 cup oil
1/3 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Beat eggs and milk together, and add dry ingredients. Add oil and vanilla. Beat until smooth. Keeps several days in fridge. Cook at 400 F.
