Title:
Ranch Buffalo Wings Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2938320.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 ounce) packet Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix
1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted
1/4 cup hot pepper sauce
3 tablespoons vinegar
1/2 teaspoon paprika
24 chicken wings or drumettes
1 cup prepared Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix
Celery sticks

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In small bowl, whisk together butter, pepper sauce and vinegar. Dip drumettes in butter mixture; arrange in single layer in large baking pan. Sprinkle with Hidden Valley(R) Original Ranch(R) Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix. Bake until chicken is browned and an internal temperature of 165 degrees F has been reached, about 30 to 40 minutes. Sprinkle with paprika.
Serve with Hidden Valley(R) Original Ranch(R) Dressing and celery sticks.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
Chicken wings dipped in spicy melted butter and topped with Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix are baked then served with prepared ranch dressing and celery sticks.
