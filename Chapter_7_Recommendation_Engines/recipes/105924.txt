Title:
Tomato Bacon Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981038.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (2.1 ounce) package Bob Evans® Express Fully Cooked Bacon
6 cups mixed torn salad greens
1 pint grape tomatoes cut in half
1/2 small red onion, thinly sliced
1 cup Italian salad dressing
1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese

Directions:
Prepare bacon according to package directions. Crumble into small pieces.  Place salad greens into serving bowl. Top with tomatoes, onion and bacon. Drizzle with Italian dressing and blue cheese.

Submitter: 
Bob Evans®
Description: 
The flavors of blue cheese and bacon blend together perfectly along with greens and tomatoes in this great salad.
