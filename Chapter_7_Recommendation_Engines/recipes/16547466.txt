Title:
Pumpkin Chiffon Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 egg yolks
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 1/2 cups cooked pumpkin (add more if needed)
1/2 cup milk
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 tsp. ginger
1/2 tsp. allspice
1 tbsp. (1 Env.) unflavored gelatin
1/4 cup cold water
3 egg whites (beat stiff)
1/2 cup white sugar
1/4 tsp cream of tartar

Directions:
1. Beat egg yolks and 1/2 cup brown sugar until thick.
2. Add pumpkin, milk, salt and spices.
3. Cook in top of double boiler until thick. Remove from heat.
4. Soften gelatin in cold water. Stir into hot mixture. Cool.
5. Beat egg whites until stiff and add 1/2 cup white sugar gradually, beating unitl very stiff.
6. Fold meringue into cooled pumpkin mixture. Pour into cooled baked shell and chill. Serve with whip cream.
ENJOY!
