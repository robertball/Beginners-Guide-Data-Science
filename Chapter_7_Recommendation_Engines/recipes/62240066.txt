Title:
Southwest Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. vegetable oil
2 garlic cloves, chopped
1 small onion, diced
2-3 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut
into bite size pieces
1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 (8.75 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1-2 tsp chili powder

Directions:
In a large skillet, heat oil over medium high heat. Saute garlic & onion til tender. Add chicken & saute until lightly browned. Add tomatoes with green chile peppers, beans,corn & spices. Reduce heat and let simmer for 20 to 25 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and juices run clear.
