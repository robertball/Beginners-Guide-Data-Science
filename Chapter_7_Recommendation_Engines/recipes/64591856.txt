Title:
Bahamian Marmalade Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 teaspoons dry mustard
2 teaspoons rum
1 cup Orange Marmalade
Soy sauce, to taste

Directions:
Mix mustard with  rum to make a paste.
Add marmalade and a few drops of soy sauce.  Stir until combined.
Serve with barbequed dishes.

Submitter: 
mikekey
Description: 
Use this as a condiment for barbecue dishes.
