Title:
EZ red pesto sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1447482.jpg
Ingredients:
100 g sun-dried tomatoes
80 g fresh Parmesan cheese (no pre-grated)
60 g fresh basil leaves
1-2 cloves of garlic
150 ml olive oil
1/2 lemon juice (or lime)
60 g pine nut
salt
ground pepper

Directions:
grate Parmesan cheese
put everything but lemon juice in blender and blend until smooth paste. season to liking with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
add lemon juice and stir
