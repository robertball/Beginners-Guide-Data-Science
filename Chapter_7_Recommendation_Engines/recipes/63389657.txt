Title:
Black Gill Rock Fish in Orange Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 Rock Fish Fillets (or any mild, firm, thick, pale fleshed fish)Cut into 2x4 inch chunks
4-6 boiled white or yellow potatoes  (cooled, peeled and sliced into ½ inch thick rounds)
4T butter
1T EVOO
3T flour
1t Greek Seasoning
¼ t Paprika
½ t salt
¼ t fresh ground pepper
2 C   ½ & ½ or cream
1 C OJ w pulp
1T fresh lemon juice
1t fresh thyme or chives (optional)
1 fresh orange (skinned and thinly sliced or chopped)

Directions:
Lightly oil small casserole dish and arrange potato slices on the bottom of dish
Lightly salt and pepper,
Arrange fish chunks on top of potatoes and lightly pepper.
Heat oil add butter and melt
add seasonings and flour
cook over low heart for 5-8 minutes stirring occasionally till fragrant and beige in color
Wisk in ½ & ½ and cook till thicken (3-5 minutes)
add OJ, lemon juice and fresh herbs.
Whisk 1 min and remove from heat
Season with salt and pepper to taste
Cover fish and potatoes with hot orange cream sauce and top with fresh orange
Bake for 15- 20 min in preheated oven. Set oven to broil and cook for 10 more minutes or till lightly browned on top. Rest for 10 minutes and serve.

Submitter: 
Katie
Description: 
My Peruvian MIL makes this dish and remains a favorite for the whole family (even the picky ones) ! It's a delicate sauce that compliments a variety of fish types.
