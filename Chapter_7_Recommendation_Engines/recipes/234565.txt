Title:
Wonderful Wake-up Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1050460.jpg
Ingredients:
Carlini Cooking Spray
20 Season's Choice Hash Brown Patties, divided
12 ounces Appleton Farms Hickory Smoked Ham Steak, diced, divided
3 cups Happy Farms Shredded Cheddar Cheese, divided
12 Goldhen Large Eggs
1 cup Friendly Farms 2% Milk
1 teaspoon Stonemill Essentials Iodized Salt
1 teaspoon Stonemill Essentials Ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Heat slow cooker on low setting. Coat the inside with cooking spray.
Arrange 10 hash brown patties around bottom of slow cooker. Top with 6 ounces of ham and 1 cup of cheese.
Repeat with remaining patties, ham and 1 cup cheese.
In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt and pepper.
Pour eggs on top of layered ingredients and cover. Cook for 6 hours.
Top with 1 cup of cheese, adjust temperature to high and cook for 30 minutes, covered.

Submitter: 
ALDI
Description: 
Set this up the night before in a slow cooker for an easy morning with little clean up for Mom.
