Title:
Spanish Chicken Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CHICKEN
2tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
180g/6oz Cooked Chicken - shredded
1 clove Garlic - finely chopped
1 Onion - finely sliced
1 x 400g/14oz tin Chopped Tomatoes
1tsp Paprika
90g/3oz Roasted Peppers - Jar is fine - sliced
30g/1oz Black Olives - pitted and halved
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
MASH
600g/1lb 6oz Potatoes - peeled and sliced
Milk - as required
45g/1Â½oz Butter
1tsp Paprika
1 Egg Yolk - lightly beaten
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 200c/400f/Gas 6;
Grease a large ovenproof dish;
MAKE THE TOPPING
Boil the potatoes in water until soft but still with some bite;
Drain, dry out a little on hob, mash with the butter, egg yolk, paprika and milk and season to taste;
CHICKEN
Meanwhile, heat the oil in a large pan on a low heat, stir in the garlic and onions and cook gently, stirring occasionally, for some 5 minutes or so until softened;
Add the paprika and continue cooking, stirring continuously, to mix in well for a further 1 minute;
Add the tomatoes, increase the heat and, stirring well to mix everything together, bring to the boil;
Reduce the heat, add the chicken, peppers and olives, season and, stirring continuously, mix everything together well;
Continue to cook gently, stirring occasionally, for some 5 minutes or so to thicken the mixture slightly;
TO SERVE
Pour the mixture into the ovenproof dish, top with the mash and bake in oven for some 15 minutes or so until the sauce is bubbling and the top is golden brown.
