Title:
Lamb Ribs Braised with Root Beer and Toasted Sesame Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 mL vegetable oil
6 large lamb shoulder blade chops, about 1.4 to 1.6 kg
1 bottle (340 mL) good quality root beer
60 mL reduced sodium soy sauce
60 mL honey
40 g toasted sesame seed
30 mL smoked paprika
2.5 mL chipotle
7 mL dried rosemary, crushed
120 mL water
additional toasted sesame seeds to garnish
40 g finely chopped green onions to garnish

Directions:
Add oil to Dutch Oven and brown lamb, set a side.
De-glaze the Dutch Oven and add all except onion to the pot.
Add back the lamb and put in oven of 163 Â°C for a hour and half.
Take out lamb, reduce sauce. As it boils, collect fat from in center of pot.
Introduce the lamb and coat.
Garnish with the sesame seeds and green onions.
