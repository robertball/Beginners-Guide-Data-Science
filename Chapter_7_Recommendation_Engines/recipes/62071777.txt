Title:
Chicken Divan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. boneless chicken breast, cut up into bite-size pieces
1 bag frozed broccoli
1 can cream of chicken
1/2 c mayonnaise
pepper
1/2 tsp lemon juice
1/2 c cheddar cheese
1/4 c melted butter
3/4 c breadcrumbs

Directions:
1.) Mix together the cream of chicken, mayonnaise, pepper, lemon juice, and cheddar cheese
2.) Layer the bottom of a pan with broccoli.
3.) Spoon 1/2 of the mix over the broccoli.
4.) Add the chicken.
5.) Spoon the remaining mix over the chicken.
6.) Mix together the melted butter and breadcrumbs and spread it over the top.
7.) Backe at 400 for 40-45 minutes.
