Title:
Peanut Butter Peekaboos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup rolled oats
4 egg whites
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp baking powder
peanut butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Combine oats, egg whites, vanilla extract, and baking powder in a blender.
Pour batter into a muffin tin and bake for 15-20 minutes.
While it's warm, scoop out top half of muffin and pat in a tablespoon of peanut butter in the center.
Replace top muffin half and devour!
