Title:
Macadamia Nut Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 cups water
1/3 cup macadamia nuts
4 dried apricots
1/2 teaspoon vanilla powder

Directions:
Blend water, macadamia nuts, apricots, and vanilla powder in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
healthyfamily
Description: 
Macadamia nut milk. Vanilla extract can be substituted for the vanilla powder.
