Title:
Zingy 'N Sweet BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/684909.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Ketchup
2 tsp Salt
1/2 cup Cider Vinegar
1/4 cup Worcester Sauce
3 tbsps Oil (Your own preference)
1 tbsp Garlic (Freshly chopped)
3-4 tbsps Brown Sugar
1/2 Cup Water.

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together thoroughly. Store in glass jar and place in fridge until use.

Submitter:

Description: 
This sauce is sure to make your mouth tingle with it's sensational yet simple flavoring, and most households already have the ingredients in stock! (No store runs!)
