Title:
Key West Marinated Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979506.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
2 tablespoons MCCORMICK® 1 Step Chicken Seasoning: Lemon Herb Chicken
2 tablespoons white vinegar
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Directions:
In a shallow dish or plastic bag, combine first three ingredients.
Add chicken; turn to coat all sides. Refrigerate 30 minutes. Broil or grill 6-7 minutes per side over medium heat or until done.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
Marinate chicken with the refreshing flavors of 1 Step Chicken Seasoning:  Lemon Herb Chicken.
