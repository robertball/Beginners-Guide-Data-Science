Title:
Tri-Pepper Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 red peppers
2 green peppers
1 yellow pepper
1 1/2 sweet onion
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp chili powder
3/4 cup taco sauce
8 8" flour tortillas
2 cups cheddar cheese

Directions:
Cut peppers and onion into strips. Add olive oil to large pan. Saute peppers and onion over medium heat. Stir in chili powder. Cook until soft.
Spread 1/2 cup taco sauce on 9 x 13 glass baking dish.
Warm tortillas in microwave. Add thin layer of pepper mixture to one side of tortilla. Reserve 1/2 cup pepper mixture. Sprinkle tortilla with 1 cup cheese. Wrap and place fold side down in baking dish.
Spread remaining pepper mixture down center of layered tortillas. Drizzle tortillas with remaining taco sauce. Sprinkle with remaining cheese.
Cook 10-15 min. at 375
