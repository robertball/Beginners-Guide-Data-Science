Title:
Lemon-Blueberry Cheesecake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/714787.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8oz)pkg Cream Cheese, soften
1 egg
1 (17.5oz)pkg Betty Crocker Sugar Cookie mix
4 tsp lemon extract
2/3 cups Blueberry Flavored Baking Tidbits

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Line baking pans with parchment paper. Set aside until ready to use.
In a mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese, thoroughly until smooth. Then add the egg and mix until well blended.
Add lemon extract and cookie mix until cream cheese mixture is well incorperated. Then lightly fold in blueberry chips, gently.
Using a small cookie dough scooper OR a tablespoon, scoop 1 TBSP dollups on to parchment lined pans. Spacing 2 inches apart. Lightly pat cookie dollup down.
Bake for 9-12 minutes or until lightly golden edges. Cool and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Melissa Goff
Description: 
After my recipe for Incredible Raspberry Cheesecake Cookies were published. I decided to purchase other flavored tidbits from the company that got me started on my newest cookie venture. This is a delightful spin off.
