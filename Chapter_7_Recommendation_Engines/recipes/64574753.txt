Title:
sweet potato, black bean and sausage soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cans black beans rinsed
1 can diced tomatoes
1 tsp cumin
pinch of crushed red pepper
1 tsp chili powder
half chopped green pepper
half chopped onion
1 lb sausage ground
4 diced jalpeno slices
1 Tbs chopped garlic
1 or 2 tsp cilantro
4- 5 cups chicken broth
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper
2-3 sweet potatoes chopped

Directions:
Brown sausage, onions and green peppers add garlic in last minute or so. Add chicken broth and can of diced tomatoes. Add spices and worcestershire sauce and beans. Simmer soup, covered, for 15 minutes. While soup is simmering, peel sweet potatoes and cut into 1/2 inch pieces and add to soup. Simmer soup with sweet potatoes about 20 minutes.
