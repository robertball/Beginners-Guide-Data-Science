Title:
Best burger ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4548565.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds 80/20 ground beef
1 tsp soy sauce
1 tsp. worcestershire sauce
1 TBSP garlic powder
1 egg
1/2 onion finely chopped
1 pkg. liptons onion soup mix
1 clove chopped garlic
1 tsp dried basil
1 tsp. dried parsley
1 tsp. dried oregano

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and form 4 patties. Cook on hot grill for 5 mins. per side. Serve on a toasted bun.

Submitter: 
Frank David
Description: 
These burgers are so easy and so tasty you will never eat another burger again.
