Title:
Zesty Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 T. butter(no substitutes)
1 t. butter spray (optional)
3 T. cider vinegar
1 T. prepared mustard
3 T. catsup
1/2 c. mayonnaise
dash each hot sauce & worcestershire sauce
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Place butter, butter spray, & vinegar into food processor & pulse for 30 sec.  Add mustard & process for 30 sec.  Add catsup and process for 30 sec. longer.  Add mayonnaise and pulse for 30 sec.  Add seasonoings and sauces and process for 15 sec.  Remove to airtigt container and chill for at least 1 hr.

Submitter: 
Ryan Stroud
Description: 
A spicy & zesty sauce suitable as a dip for fried chicken, spam, just about anything you'd like.
