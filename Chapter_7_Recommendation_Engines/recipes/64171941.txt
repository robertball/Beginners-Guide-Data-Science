Title:
Cold Noodle & Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1471507.jpg
Ingredients:
4 teaspoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon warm water
2 teaspoons rice wine vinegar
2 teaspoons peanut butter
1 1/2 teaspoons sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons dark sesame oil
Two packages cooked and chilled Ramen noodles
One half grated cucumber
One half grated carrot

Directions:
Whisk soy sauce water rice wine vinegar peanut butter sugar sesame oil in a large bowl
Toss with two packages cooked and chilled Ramen noodles along with grated cucumber and carrot.
Serve cool
