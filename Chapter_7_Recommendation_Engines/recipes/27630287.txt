Title:
Suzie's Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3lbs. lean ground beef
1 cup chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
1-1/4 cups ketchup
1 cup chopped red bell pepper
5 TBS. Worcestershire sauce
4 TBS. brown sugar
3 TBS. vinegar
3 TBS. mustard
2 tsp. chili powder
Hamburger buns

Directions:
Brown ground beef, onion and garlic in a large skillet. Drain excess fat. Combine ketchup, bell pepper, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, vinegar, mustard and chili powder in slow cooker. Stir in beef mixture. Cover and cook on low 6-8 hours. Spoon into hamburger buns.
