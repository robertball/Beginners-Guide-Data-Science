Title:
Gregg's Stupendous Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 garlic clove, peeled
3 lbs. small red potatoes, quartered
2 Tbsp. cider vinegar, divided
1 1/2 tsp. salt, divided
6 hardboiled large eggs, divided
1 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1 Tbsp. Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 medium sweet onion, finely chopped
2 celery ribs, finely chopped
2 Tbsp. mined fresh parsley

Directions:
Place a toothpick through the garlic clove.  Place potatoes, 1 Tbsp. vinegar, 1 tsp. salt and the garlic in a Dutch oven; add water to cover.  Boil.  Reduce heat and summer potatoes 10 to 12 minutes.  Drain, reserving garlic; remove toothpick and crush garlic.
Chop five eggs.   Using a whisk combine mayonnaise, sour cream, mustard, paprika, pepper, garlic and remaining vinegar and salt.  Stir in the potatoes, chopped eggs, onion and celery.  Refrigerate 4 hours or until cold.
Just before serving, slice remaining egg.  Top salad with sliced egg; sprinkle with fresh parsley and, if desired additional paprika

Submitter: 
Lorraine Friberg
Description: 
A potato salad with a twist.  One of my favorites
