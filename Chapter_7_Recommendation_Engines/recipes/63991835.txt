Title:
Breakfast Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½	cup butter or margarine, softened
¾	cup sugar
1	large egg
1	cup all purpose flour
¼	teaspoon baking soda
10	whole bacon strips, cooked and crumbled
2	cups corn flakes
½	cup raisins, dates or other dried fruit

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar.  Beat in egg.  Add flour and baking soda; mix well.  Stir in bacon, cornflakes and raisins.  Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls 2 inches apart onto an ungreased baking sheet.
Bake at 350 for 15 to 18 minutes or until lightly browned.  Cool for 2 minutes before removing to wire racks.  Store in refrigerator.
