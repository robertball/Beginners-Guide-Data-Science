Title:
Blueberry Supreme II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Blueberry Pie Filling
1 can Eagle Brand Milk
1 container Cool Whip
1 tsp Lemon Juice
1 Angel Food Cake, torn into bite-sized pieces
1/2 cup chopped pecans, if desired

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir together first 4 ingredients, mixing well.
Place 1/2 of the torn Angel Food Cake pieces in the bottom of other large bowl. Spread 1/2 of blueberry mixture on top of cake pieces. Repeat layers. Top with chopped pecans, if desired.
Refrigerate.

Submitter: 
Kevin G
Description: 
This tasty dessert is very pretty when prepared in a glass bowl.
