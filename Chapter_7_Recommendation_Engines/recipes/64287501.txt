Title:
Bagels Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
Assorted Bagels
Cream Cheese
Fruit

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Slice bagels if necessary.
Place bagels on large baking sheet to toast.
Toast bagels in oven 10 minutes or until done.
Serve with cream cheese.
