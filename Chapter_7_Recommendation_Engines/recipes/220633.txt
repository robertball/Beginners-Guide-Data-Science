Title:
Becel速 Oven-Roasted Asparagus with Parmesan Gremolata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990455.jpg
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons finely chopped fresh parsley
2 teaspoons grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 teaspoon grated lemon rind
3/4 pound asparagus, trimmed
2 large shallots, cut into thin wedges
1 tablespoon Becel速 Buttery Taste* margarine, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Combine parsley, cheese and lemon peel in small bowl; set aside. Toss asparagus, shallots and Becel(R) Buttery Taste margarine in 13 x 9-inch roasting pan. Roast 15 minutes or until tender.  Arrange asparagus mixture on serving platter, then top with cheese mixture. Season, if desired, with freshly ground black pepper.
