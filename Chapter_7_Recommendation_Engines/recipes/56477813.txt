Title:
Blueberry Walnut Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad greens
1 pint fresh blueberries
1/4 cup toasted walnuts
1/2 cup raspberry vinaigrette salad dressing
1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (or less)
Very thin slices of red onion
Optional:  Thin strips of grilled chicken

Directions:
In a large bowl, toss the salad greens with the blueberries, walnuts, and raspberry vinaigrette. Top with feta cheese to serve.

Submitter: 
Diane
Description: 
An easy, yummy salad, perfect for any season, with berries, nuts, and greens. For an entree add chicken, diced apples, and diced green onions!Modified from: Blueberry Walnut Salad
