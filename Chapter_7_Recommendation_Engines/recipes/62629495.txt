Title:
Triple Chocolate Coconut Amish Friendship Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/576278.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Amish Friendship Bread Starter
3 eggs
1 cup oil
1/2 cup milk
1 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups flour
1/4 cup cocoa powder
1-2 boxes instant chocolate pudding
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 cup shredded coconut, sweetened (plus extra to sprinkle)

Directions:
Preheat over to 325° F (165° C).
Grease two large loaf pans and dust with sugar if desired.
Pour the batter evenly into the pans and sprinkle coconut on the top.
Bake for one hour or until the bread loosens evenly from the sides and a toothpick inserted in the center of the bread comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Friendship Bread Kitchen
Description: 
Chocolate pudding, cocoa powder and chocolate chips combine to make this bread a chocolate sensation!
