Title:
Best Friend's Mac Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. macaroni
1/2 c. mayo
1 onion chopped (opt)
1 tsp mustard
1/4 c. vinegar
1/2 c. sugar
1/2 tsp. celery seed
salt and pepper to taste
add whatever veggies
Tomatoe, celery, cucumber, carrots

Directions:
cook macaroni and let cool.  while cooling mix together mayo, vinegar, sugar, salt, pepper, and celery seed along with chopped veggies.  mix in cooled macaroni, let sit for 1hr. then enjoy!

Submitter: 
jennyhahn1
Description: 
Looking for a sweet and tangy macaroni salad?  This is it!  Mix in whatever fresh veggies you want this is a flexible salad.
