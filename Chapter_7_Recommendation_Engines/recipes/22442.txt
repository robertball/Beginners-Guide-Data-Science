Title:
Easy Nutty Fudge Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
6 tablespoons butter
1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
1/3 cup HERSHEYÂ®'S Cocoa Powder
3 tablespoons milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3/4 cup chopped mixed nuts
1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Line small muffin cups (1-3/4 inches in diameter) with petit four cups.
In medium microwave-safe bowl, place butter.  Microwave at HIGH (100%) 1 minute or until melted.  Stir in powdered sugar, cocoa and milk; blend well.  Microwave at HIGH 1 minute or until hot.  Stir until smooth.  Stir in vanilla, nuts and cinnamon.
Fill cups with mixture.  Sprinkle additional chopped nuts on top,  if desired.  Refrigerate 2 hours or until set.  Store, covered in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
HersheysKitchens.com
Description: 
Cocoa, confectioners' sugar, butter and a wee bit of milk are melted together, spiced with cinnamon and flecked with nuts, then placed in petit four cups and chilled for an easy, fancy candy.
