Title:
Blueberry Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box yellow cake mix
2c chopped pecans
1 stick butter, melted
1lb frozen blueberries, thawed
3/4c sugar
1T lemon juice
3T cornstarch
1 can crushed pineapple, undrained

Directions:
Grease and flour 9x13 pan.
Mash half of the blueberries.
Add sugar and cornstarch to mashed berries and cook 5-6 minutes.
Add lemon juice and unmashed berries to cooked, cool.
Mix pecans and cake mix and add melted butter.
Pour undrained pineapple in pan.
Spread blueberries over pineapple.
Sprinkle cake mix over blueberries.
Bake at 350 for an hour.
