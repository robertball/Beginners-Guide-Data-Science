Title:
Easy Black Bean Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 package taco seasoning mix
1 can black beans
1 head romaine lettuce shredded
2 medium tomatoes, diced
1 8 oz jar salsa
2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend
8 oz sour cream
1 bag tortilla chips

Directions:
Brown ground beef.  Drain fat.  Add Taco Seasoning mix according to package directions.  Meanwhile, heat can of black beans.
In large individual bowl, or on plates, layer ground beef.  Top with black beans.  Layer lettuce, tomatoes, salsa and cheese.  Top with a 2 oz of sour cream and garnish with tortilla chips.
