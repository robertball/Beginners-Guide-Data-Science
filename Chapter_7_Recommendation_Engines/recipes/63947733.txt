Title:
American! Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1094894.jpg
Ingredients:
Meatloaf:
2 lbs ground beef
1 lb ground pork
1 yellow onion, chopped
1/2 - 1 green bell pepper, chopped
1/2 - 3/4 cup plain bread crumbs
1 can tomato sauce
3 tbsp worcestershire sauce
black pepper to taste
1 tbsp minced garlic
2 eggs
Sauce:
2 cups ketchup
2/3 cup dijon mustard
1/4 cup brown sugar
black pepper to taste
few splashes of worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Combine meatloaf ingredients in large bowl by hand. Smooth out in large baking dish and bake for 20 minutes. Combine sauce ingredients in bowl. After first 20 minutes, spread sauce on top of meatloaf. Bake an additional 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Joshua Smith
Description: 
I came up with an ingredient list for what I thought would make a good meatloaf based on various other recipes including my mother's, and just made it my own. Turned out pretty good.
