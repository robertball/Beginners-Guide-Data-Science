Title:
Tahini Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive oil
1/3 cup tahini
1/3 cup cider vinegar
1 Tb low-sodium soy sauce
1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)
1/4 tsp garlic powder (or 1 clove minced)
1 tsp dried parsley
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in bowl and whisk until blended.  Serve over a green salad.

Submitter: 
STARFLOWER
Description: 
Because I prefer homemade to store-bought salad dressings, I made this after tasting a bottled dressing that I liked.  It's really good!
