Title:
Smore Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Package Graham Crackers
1 Bag of mini/ 1/2 Bag of regular marshmallows
3/4 Package chocolate Chips
2 Aluminum pie pans
2 1/2 tbs butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 Degrees
Break graham crackers into bottom of pan
pour 1 bag of mini marshmallows on top of graham crackers (if use big marshmallows, break them up and put on top of graham crackers)
Melt in a bowl, 1/4 package of the Chocolate Chips and butter
Put the rest (1/2 cup) of chocolate chips on top of the marshmallows.
Bake for 15-30 mins
pour heated chocolate mixture on top.
Let sit to cool.
