Title:
Vanilla Banana Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 banana
* 1/2 cup skim milk
* 8 ounces of vanilla yogurt
* 2 tablespoons brown sugar
* 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
* 5 ice cubes

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients in a blender and pulse until smooth.

Submitter: 
LISALC98
Description: 
This is a rich tasting smoothie that I love to make in the morning. All these ingredients are usually on hand and it's very easy to make! If you don't have vanilla extract, almond extract works well too.
