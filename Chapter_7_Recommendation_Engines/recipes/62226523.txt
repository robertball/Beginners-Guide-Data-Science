Title:
Mini chip Snowball Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/354198.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/2 Cups Butter
3/4  Cups Powdered Sugar
1 Teaspoon Vanilla
1/2 Teaspoon Salt
3 Cups All Purpose Flour
2 Cups Mini Chocolate Chips
1/2 Cups Finely Chopped Nuts (if desired)
Powdered Sugar

Directions:
Beat butter, sugar, vanilla and salt until creamy.  Greadually add flour stir in morsel and nuts.  Shape level teaspoons of dough into 1-1/4 inch balls.  Place on greased baking sheets for about 10-12 minutes or until cookies are lightly browned.  Place on cooling rack or paper bags and sprinkle with powdered sugar.

Description: 
These are a new favorite for Christmas!  I found the recipe in an old Nestle cookbook.  They are buttery cookies topped with powdered sugar.
