Title:
Super Easy Chocolate Mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/760757.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Heavy Whipping Cream
1 (5 oz) box Jell-O Instant Chocolate Pudding (Cook & Serve may be used)
1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract
1 Banana, chopped (optional)

Directions:
Pour heavy whipping cream into a large bowl. Whip until it begins to thicken. Add the vanilla and half the packet of pudding mix. Contintue to beat until thick, where it holds shape and gathers on the beaters. If using a banana, gently mix it in now.
Best if served chilled.

Description: 
A quick, easy chocolate mousse that will please everyone. Great for potlocks and layered deserts!
