Title:
Squash Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. fresh yellow squash
1 onion, chopped
1 1/2 cups water
1 1/2 tsp. Better Than Bouillon
salt and pepper to taste
1 stick of butter
2 cups cooked Jiffy cornbread, crumbled
1 can cream of celery soup
2 eggs, beaten
2 tsp. sage

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook squash and onions together in the bouillon water until very tender, mashing with a potato masher, about 30 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Cook all water out of squash (taking longer to cook) or drain well.
Measure out 2 cups.
Add butter and let it melt.
Add crumbled cornbread, soup, beaten eggs, and sage.
Put in an 8x8 casserole dish and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
