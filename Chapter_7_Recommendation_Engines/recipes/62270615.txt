Title:
Jim's Slow Cooker Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1 (30 ounce) cans kidney beans drained
2 (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes with Jalapenos
2 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 medium onions, chopped
2 stalks celery chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 1/2 tablespoons chili powder
2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon Cayenne red pepper

Directions:
In a skillet, cook beef over medium heat until no longer pink; drain. Transfer to a slow cooker. Add the next ten ingredients. Cover and cook on low for 8-10 hours or on high for 4 hours. Garnish individual servings with cheese if desired.

This hearty chili is cooked for up to 10 hours on low. It has a little kick and is very tasty!Modified from: Slow-Cooked Chili
