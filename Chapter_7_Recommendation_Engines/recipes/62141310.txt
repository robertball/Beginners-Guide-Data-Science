Title:
Sweettreats Ultimate chocolate chip cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/544101.jpg
Ingredients:
3 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup light brown sugar, packed
2/3 cup butter
1/3 cup shortening
1/3 cup butter flavored shortening
2 eggs
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
2 cups chocolate chips
1 cup walnuts, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large bowl cream together shortenings,butter and sugars. Add eggs and vanilla. Mix in flour, salt and baking soda. Mix in the chocolate chips and nuts. Using a small cookie scoop place cookie dough on baking sheets and bake for 10 minutes. Cookies will look undone when properly baked. Do not over bake cookies.

Submitter: 
Angela Welch
Description: 
My family and friends look forward to a batch of these soft chewy cookies. You won't be able to eat just one. By far the best chocolate chip cookies I have ever made.
