Title:
Bubbly Beer Cheese Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 quart chicken broth
2.5 to 3 lbs potatoes
3-5 garlic cloves
1 quart of milk
4 tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp pepper
3/4 lb cheddar cheese
1 cup beer
Paprika

Directions:
Skin and coarsely chop potatoes. Chop garlic.  Simmer potatoes, garlic and chicken broth for 30 minutes.  Let cool.  Add milk and heat gently.  Add soy sauce, pepper and slowly stir in cheese until melted.  Blend soup mixture in batches in a blender.  Return to pot and heat gently to simmer.  Add beer being careful of foaming!  Serve with bread, sour cream, shredded cheddar, and/or bacon bits.

Submitter: 
Karen Zastrow
Description: 
An easy, cheesy homemade beer soup.  Great fall comfort food!
