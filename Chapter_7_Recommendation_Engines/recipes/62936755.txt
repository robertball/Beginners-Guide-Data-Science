Title:
Reuben Roll-Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pieces of flatbread
1/2 cup sauerkraut, well-drained
4 Tablespoons thousand island salad dressing
8 slices corned beef, thinly sliced
8 slices Swiss cheese, thinly sliced

Directions:
Lay out flatbread.
Spread 1 T. salad dressing down the center.
Place meat and cheese on bread.
Top with about 2 tablespoons of the sauerkraut mixture.
Roll tightly and wrap in foil.
Refrigerate until ready to heat in a 250Â° oven for 20 minutes.
Cut each roll up into 4-5 pieces.

Made with flatbread, these easy to make rollups are a good substitution for the traditional Reuben sandwich.Modified from: Reuben Roll-Ups
