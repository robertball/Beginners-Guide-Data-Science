Title:
Plant Strong Oil Free Tofu Mayonnaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12oz  (about 1 cup) silken tofu
juice 1/2 lemon
1/4 teaspoon dijon mustard
1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
1/3 cup soy milk (or your favorite non-dairy milk)
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon sugar

Directions:
Blend together until very smooth.
Keep in an airtight container (preferably glass) in the refrigerator for up to a week.
