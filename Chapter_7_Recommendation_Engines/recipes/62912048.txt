Title:
Ed's Oven Pork Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slabs of Pork Back Ribs
2 cups Ketchup
1&1/2 cups of water
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
3 teaspoons chili powder
3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 Onion chopped
3 tablespoons brown sugar

Directions:
Cut ribs into two bone pieces and place in a large pot.
Boil ribs in water for 40 minutes then make the sauce.
Mix Ketchup, water, salt, pepper, chill powder, worcestershire sauce, onion, and brown sugar into a small pot and lightly boil for 10 minutes.
Place ribs in a roaster and pour sauce over ribs and cook in oven at 350 degrees F for 45 minutes.
Enjoy.
