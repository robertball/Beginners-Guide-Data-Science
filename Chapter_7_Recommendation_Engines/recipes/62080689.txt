Title:
Sweet & Spicy Hawaiian Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 12-oz cans pineapple with juice (crushed or chunks)
1 lb dark brown sugar
2 tbsp black pepper
1 1/2 tbsp salt
3 tbsp crushed red pepper
1 or 2 tbsp cajun seasoning
1 tbsp garlic powder

Directions:
Drain pineapples, saving juice in a mixing bowl.  Set pineapples aside.  Add brown sugar to juice and mix well.  Add all other dry ingredients.  Mix well and set aside.  Cook desired meat halfway, and place pineapples on meat.  Spoon sauce over meat and pineapples.  Continue cooking meat.  Baste repeatedly every 10 mintues until meat is done.  Note: Cook times are approximate--depending on type of meat. You  can bake or roast any type of meat for this sauce.

Submitter: 
ISLANDGIRL05
Description: 
With just the right amount of spices, this sauce makes any type of meat ono (delicious).
