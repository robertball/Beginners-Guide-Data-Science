Title:
BUSH'S® Jamaican Black Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987238.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound bacon, chopped
1 cup chopped sweet onion
1 cup chopped red pepper
1 cup chopped green pepper
1 tablespoon minced roasted garlic
2 teaspoons cumin seed
2 teaspoons fennel seed
1 teaspoon ground allspice
2 cups pumpkin puree
2 (16 ounce) cans BUSH'S® Black Beans, drained and rinsed
2 (10 ounce) cans diced tomatoes and green chilies, undrained
1 cup frozen corn
1 cup chicken broth
1 tablespoon cocoa powder
2 cups sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
Fresh lime

Directions:
In a large 6-quart soup pot, saute bacon over medium heat until cooked. Add onion, peppers, garlic and spices. Saute over medium heat for 5 minutes. Stir in pumpkin puree, BUSH'S BEST Black Beans, tomatoes, corn, broth and cocoa powder. Bring to a boil; reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes. Serve with grated cheese and garnish with curled lime zest.

Submitter: 
BUSH'S® Beans
Description: 
A unique blend of spices and flavors combine to create a hearty chili, garnished with Cheddar cheese and curled lime zest.
