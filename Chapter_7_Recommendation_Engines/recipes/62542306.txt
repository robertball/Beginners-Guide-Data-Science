Title:
Kraft Layered Mexican Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/524570.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (8 oz.) PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened 1/2 cup  TACO BELL® HOME ORIGINALS® Thick 'N Chunky Salsa
1 cup KRAFT Mexican Style Finely Shredded Four Cheese
Topping Choices (pick one):
1/4 cup chopped cilantro
2   green onions, sliced
1/2 cup sliced black olives
1/2 cup  coarsely chopped avocados
1/2 cup rinsed canned black beans

Directions:
SPREAD cream cheese onto bottom of pie plate.
TOP with salsa, shredded cheese and topping.
SERVE with RITZ Crackers.
