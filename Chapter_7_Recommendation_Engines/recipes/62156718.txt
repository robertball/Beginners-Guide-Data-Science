Title:
Peppery Blue Cheese Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs extra lean ground beef
1/3 cup bread crumbs
1/4 cup milk
125 grams blue cheese, crumbled
1.5 tsp garlic pepper
1 tsp garlic salt
1 egg

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together to combine. Form into patties. Cook on grill or bbq and serve with all the fixin's.

Submitter: 
J
Description: 
I have never been much of a blue cheese fan but keep trying to find ways to learn to love it and this recipe did just that!
