Title:
DEEP FRIED TURKEY Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
TURKEY - CRAB BOIL AND WATER

Directions:
TAKE A COFFEE CUP AND MIX 1/4 LIQUID CRAB BOIL AND THE REST WATER CLOSE TO THE TOP. INJECT 1 SIDE OF WHOLE TURKEY OR TURKEY BREAST WITH THIS AND DUPLICATE FOR OTHER SIDE. MORE CRAB BOIL THE SPICIER, LESS OF COURSE NOT AS SPICEY. YOU WILL LOVE THE FLAVOR. ALSO COAT OUTSIDE WITH TONY'S LET SIT IN FRIG FOR AT LEAST A DAY. 2 DAYS EVEN BETTER. ITS THE BOMB. TRY IT, YOU'LL LIKE IT. USE ENOUGH OIL TO COVER TURKEY A TALL POT WORKS WELL USUALLY TAKES 3 GALLONS FOR A 13LB. TURKEY, HEAT OIL TO 350 AND INSERT TURKEY, TEMP WILL DROP GET IT BACK UP TO 350 (USUALLY DROPS TO ABOUT 325 BUT START YOUR TIME FROM THE TIME YOU PUT TURKEY IN POT) AND DEEP FRY THE TURKEY FOR 3 1/2 - 4 MINUTES PER POUND FOR WHOLE TURKEY AND I USUALLY DO 4 1/2 TO 5 MINUTES FOR A TURKEY BREAST INSERT THERMOMETER JUST TO MAKE SURE IF YOU ARE WORRIED ABOUT UNDER COOKING. ENJOY!!!
