Title:
Big Ray's Memphis-Style Drunken BBQ Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3915057.jpg
Ingredients:
3 lb. pork baby back ribs
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup bourbon
1/2 cup Sweet & Spicy BBQ Sauce Memphis Style

Directions:
Place ribs on large sheet of heavy-duty foil sprayed with cooking spray. Mix sugar and bourbon until blended; spread onto ribs. Wrap in foil. Refrigerate 1 hour.
Heat grill for indirect grilling: Light one side of grill, leaving other side unlit. Close lid; heat grill to 350ÂºF. Place ribs on grate over unlit area; cover. Grill 2 hours, monitoring for consistent grill temperature.
Unwrap ribs; place, meat sides down, on grate over lit area of grill. Brush with 1/4 cup barbecue sauce. Grill 10 min., turning after 5 min. and brushing with remaining barbecue sauce.
