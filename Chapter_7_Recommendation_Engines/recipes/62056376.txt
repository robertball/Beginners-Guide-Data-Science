Title:
Easy Chocolate Chip Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 butter, softened
1 8 oz pkg. cream cheese
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
1/4 cup milk
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Grease bottom and 1/2 inch up side of 9-inch springform pan.
Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt; set aside.
In large bowl, beat butter & cream cheese on medium speed for 30 seconds. Add 1 1/4 cups sugar and vanilla. Beat on medium speed until light and fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Add dry ingredients and milk alternately, beating until smooth after each addition. Stir in chocolate chips.
Spoon batter into prepared pan. In small bowl, combine 1/4 cup sugar, cinnamon and pecans. Sprinkle over batter.
Bake at 350 for 50-55 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cook on wire rack. Serve warm.
