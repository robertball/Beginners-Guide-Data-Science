Title:
Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp Cornstarch
2 Tbsof water
1/2 C Milk
2 C shredded cheese

Directions:
Take corn starch put in small pot on stove. Turn on low heat for 30 seconds then add water and quickly stir the flour mixture until it balls up.
SLOWLY add in drips and drabs milk stirring all the while until it thickens. Beware of scalding - you may have to turn heat lower if it was too high.
When it starts to thicken begin to add handfuls of shredded cheese to the mixture as much as you need and taste, stirring all the while until cheese is completely melted and ready to be used.
