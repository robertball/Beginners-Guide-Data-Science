Title:
Ham & Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups dry pinto beans (rinsed)
18 Cups water
1 med onion (diced)
1 pkg bacon (diced)
1 ham steak (diced)
4 tbsp chicken stock
2 tbsp salt
4-5 dashes liquid smoke

Directions:
Add all ingredients to your roasting oven set to 350 for 10-12 hours.

Submitter: 
nvmommyx6
Description: 
Smokey flavorful ham and beans that dissapear VERY quickly!
You'll need a large roasting oven for this recipe!
