Title:
Herbed Lentils and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Tablespoon olive oil
2 Tablespoons white wine
3/4 cup chopped onion
3/4 cup chopped mushroom
1/2 cup uncooked brown rice
3/4 cup dry lentils
1 clove garlic minced
1/8 teaspoon pepper
Herbs & Spices of Choice
3 cups liquid (chicken or vegetable broth, wine, water)
1 cup shredded cheese

Directions:
Heat a skillet to high, add oil, sautee mushrooms & onions until wilted and golden, deglaze with 2 T wine; stir in rice, lentils, garlic.
Sautee for 1 minute.  Remove from heat. Stir in pepper, herbs & spices, liquid and 1/2 cup cheese.
Transfer to a 1-1/2-qt. baking dish coated with nonstick cooking spray.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees F for 1-1/2 to 2 hours or until lentils and rice are tender and liquid is absorbed, stirring every 30 minutes.
Test rice & lentils @ 1-1/2 mark, add liquid (up to 1/2 cup) if mostly absorbed and needs to cook longer.
Uncover; sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake 2-3 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.
