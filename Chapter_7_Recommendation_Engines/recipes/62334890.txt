Title:
Mexican Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 ounce) can black eyed peas, rinsed and drained
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
1 small can (8 3/4 ounce) whole kernel corn, drained
1/2 red onion, chopped
1/3 cup olive oil
1/2 cup red wine vinegar
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2 tablespoons white sugar (or Splenda granular)
1 tablespoon salt (or Morton lite salt blend)
1 clove crushed garlic
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1 T chopped fresh parsley or 1 teaspoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 dash hot pepper sauce
1/2 teaspoon chili powder

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine beans, peppers, corn, and red onion.
In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, lime juice, lemon juice, sugar, salt, garlic, cilantro, cumin, parsley and black pepper.  Season to taste with hot sauce and chili powder.
Pour olive oil dressing over vegetables; mix well.  Chill thoroughly, and serve cold.
