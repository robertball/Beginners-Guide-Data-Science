Title:
Dairy-Free Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4572244.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups rice milk
1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Combine rice milk, chocolate chips, and vanilla extract in a saucepan over medium heat. Whisk until the chocolate chips are melted and the mixture is hot, about 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
MCakes
Description: 
Dairy allergies? No worries. You can still have a very tasty cup of hot chocolate! This recipe will work great with any kind of dairy-free milk. Enjoy!
