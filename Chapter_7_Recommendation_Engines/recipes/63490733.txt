Title:
Spicy Bacon Mac & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/952628.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups elbow macaroni
12 oz. Sargento® Mild Cheddar Cubes
5 slices Sargento® Deli Style Sliced Pepper Jack Cheese
4 slices thick bacon, fully cooked & crumbled
1 1/2 cups milk, divided
2 eggs
2 tablespoons margarine
1 1/2 teaspoons salt (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven at 350 degrees.
Cook elbow macaroni until tender. Salt water (optional) while boiling.
Place cubed cheddar cheese, crumbled bacon & pepper jack cheese in a 2 quart casserole dish.
Crack 2 eggs over cheddar and add in margarine.
Pour in 1 cup of milk. Stir all the ingredients well.
When macaroni is done, drain well and pour on top of ingredients. Add in the last 1/2 cup of milk and stir all ingredients until well blended.
Place casserole dish in oven and cook 45 minutes or until done (stick a spoon into the center and press to see if any milk is still uncooked.)

Submitter: 
Faith N
Description: 
This is a recipe I created to reflect our own version of my mom's macaroni and cheese in honor of my uncle who loved bacon and spicy food. This reflects all these components and makes for a great side dish to any meal!
