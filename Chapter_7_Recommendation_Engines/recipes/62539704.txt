Title:
Kris' Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
SALAD:
1 head iceburg lettuce, torn
1 bunch spinach, cleaned and stems removed
4 oz sliced mushrooms
1 cup grated swiss cheese
1 8 oz carton dry cottage cheese
4 to 5 slices crisp cooked bacon
1 small onion (preferably red) thin sliced
4 oz sliced almonds
POPPY SEED DRESSING
3/4 cup salad oil (do NOT use olive oil)
1/3 cup white vinegar
1/3 cup sugar
3/4 tsp. grated onion
1/3 tsp. dry mustard
3/4 tsp. poppy seeds
1/2 tsp. salt

Directions:
Mix all dressing ingredients in a jar and shake until mixed. Refrigerate until needed
Mix salad ingredients in large bowl and toss.
Pour dressing on salad right before serving, or serve dressing on side to be poured on salad individually.

Submitter: 
Debi Blair McGinness
Description: 
I first had this at my sister-in-laws and couldn't get enough of it.  Everyone in the family loves it and it's become a holiday must have.
