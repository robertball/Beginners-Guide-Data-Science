Title:
Cook'n esuggs-Blackberry Dump Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (1 quart) bags of blackberries
2 TB sugar (white or brown)
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 yellow cake mix, dry
1 can of sprite or 7up (i call it soda)
4 TB butter, cut into pieces
vanilla ice cream

Directions:
add berries and smaller sugar amount to Dutch Oven.
stir to mix
sprinkle large sugar amount over fruit.
spread the dry cake mix evenly over the fruit
evenly pour the soda over the dry cake mix
dot top with butter
place lid on oven and bake on grill for 45-60 minutes. (don't know what the temp would be in an oven, but maybe 400 for 35-40 minutes?)
serve warm with ice cream
author: byron's dutch oven website
