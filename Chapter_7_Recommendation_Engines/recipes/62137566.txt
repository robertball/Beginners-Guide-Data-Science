Title:
Goat Cheet Pizza with Carmelized Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil
1 lg Yellow onion, cut into thin rings
1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
1 Lg pizza crust
3/4 cup of prepared pizza sauce
1/3 cup of drained and chopped sun dried tomatoes
1 cup of crumbled goat cheese
1/2 cup of fresh chopped basil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 F
Heat the oil over medium heat. Stir in the onions and saute for 15 to 20 minutes, or until the onions are a dark golden brown. Remove from heat, stir in the balsamic vinegar and set aside.
In small bowl, combine sun dried tomatoes with the pizza sauce.
Place Pizza crust on a baking sheet and cover with the pizza tomatoe sauce.  Top with the onions and cheese and bake for 10 minutes.
Sprinkle with the fresh basil and put on a smile.
