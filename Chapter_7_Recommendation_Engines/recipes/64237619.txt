Title:
Eggplant with tomatoes and capers. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound eggplant
salt
1/2 cup olive oil
2 medium ripe tomatoes
1 tbsp. capers
1 sprig parsley
1 garlic clove

Directions:
Wash and dice the eggplant sprinkle with salt and leave for about one hour to draw out the bitter juices. Put into a fine mesh colander rinse well and dry with a tea towel.
Heat oil in a large skillet and saute the eggplant, salt to taste. Crush the tomatoes and add to the skillet with the drained capers.Simmer, stirring until the sauce begins to thicken(10 minutes)  Chop the parsley and garlic very finely, add to the skillet and cook for 1 to 2 minutes.This dish is very good served with steak or braised meat and is also excellent served cold.*SEE NOTES*
