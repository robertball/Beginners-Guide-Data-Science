Title:
Ham and Cheese Biscuit Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1030784.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can Grands Jr. flaky biscuits (10 biscuits)
8 oz diced ham
1 cup grated swiss cheese
2 tbsp honey mustard
3 eggs
2/3 cup greek yogurt
3 tbsp grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Spray 9x13 pan with cooking spray.
Separate biscuits into quarters.
Place biscuits in bottom of pan.
Top with ham and Swiss cheese.
Whisk eggs, honey mustard, and yogurt together.
Pour over biscuits.
Top with Parmesan cheese.
Bake for 30-35 minutes.
