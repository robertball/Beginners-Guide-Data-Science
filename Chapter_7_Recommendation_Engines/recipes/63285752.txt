Title:
Rub for Deep Fried Turkey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp kosher salt
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp red pepper
2 tsp paprika

Directions:
Mix together. Dry the turkey well, and loosen the skin gently with your fingers. Use 1/4 of mixture under the skin, 1/4 of the mixture inside the cavity, and 1/2 of the mixture to the dried outer skin. Deep fry turkey according to recipe
