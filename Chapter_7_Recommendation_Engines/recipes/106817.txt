Ingredients:
1/2 cup SPLENDA速 No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
2 (0.13 ounce) packages KOOL-AID速 Tropical Punch Flavor Unsweetened Soft Drink Mix
2 cups unsweetened pineapple juice, chilled
2 cups mango nectar, chilled
1 liter diet ginger ale, chilled

Directions:
Combine SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, KOOL-AID(R) Soft Drink Mix, pineapple juice, and mango nectar in a pitcher, stirring until SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener dissolves. Stir in ginger ale just before serving. Serve over ice.
