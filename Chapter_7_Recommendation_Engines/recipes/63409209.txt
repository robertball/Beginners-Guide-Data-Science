Title:
Netty Mom's Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tbsp basil
1 1/2 tsps salt
1 lb canned tomatoes
12 ozs tomato paste
10 ozs lasagna noodles
3 c cottage cheese, lowfat
1/2 c Parmesan cheese
2 tbsp parsley
2 eggs
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 lb mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Brown meat - spoon off fat
Add next 5 ingredients
Simmer uncovered for 30 minutes, stirring occationally
Cook noodles in large amount of water
Combine remaining ingredients, except mozzarella cheese
Place 1/2 nooddles in a 13x9x2 baking pan
Spread with 1/2 the cheese filling, then 1/2 mozzarella cheese, then 1/2 meat sauce
Repeat steps 6 & 7
Bake in 375 F oven for 30 minutes
Let stand 10 minutes before cutting into squares
