Title:
Watermelon Mojitos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30 large fresh mint leaves, coarsely torn by hand
3 or 4 thick slices of fresh watermelon
12 ounces light rum
1/2 cup simple syrup
6 Tbl. freshly squeezed lime juice (3 limes)
Mint sprigs and watermelon spears, for garnish

Directions:
Use a mortar and pestle to mash the mint leaves.  Remove and discard the rind and seeds of the watermelon.  Put the watermelon into a food processor fitted with a steel blad and puree.
Put the mashed mint into a large pitcher with 2 cups of pureed watermelon, the rum, syrup and lime juice, and stir to combine.  Pour the mixture into a pitcher for serving.
Place ice cubes in 6 glasses and pour the mojito mixture into the glasses.  Garnish with fresh mint sprigs and watermelon spears.  Serve ice cold.
Note: To make simple syrup, put 1 cup sugar and 1 cup water in a small pan and simmer until the sugar dissolves.  Chill.

Submitter: 
daf
Description: 
In the summer, when watermelons are sweet, this recipe makes a big pitcher of mojitos, a refreshing drink with crushed mint and freshly squeezed lime juice.  Ina Garten
