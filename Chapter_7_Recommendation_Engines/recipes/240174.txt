Title:
Yummy Apple Cider Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4566316.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups apple cider
1 (750 milliliter) bottle sparkling red grape juice
2 cups orange juice
2 tablespoons lemon juice, or more to taste

Directions:
Stir apple cider, sparkling grape juice or wine (see Cook's Note), orange juice, and lemon juice together and refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
Heidirs
Description: 
I've made this recipe twice for separate Halloween parties, and guests couldn't get enough! A delicious apple cider punch that is sure to be a hit at any autumn party.
