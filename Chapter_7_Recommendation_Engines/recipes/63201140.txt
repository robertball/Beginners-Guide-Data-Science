Title:
Gooey Chocolate Skillet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CAKE:
1 c. flour
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1 c. sugar
dash salt
1/4 c. 1/2 stick) butter
1/4 c. vegetable oil
2 Tbl. cocoa powder
1/2 c. water
1/4 c. buttermilk
1 egg
1/2 tsp. vanilla
FROSTING:
1/4 c. (1/2 stick) butter
2 Tbl. cocoa
3-4 Tbl. milk (as needed for consistency)
1/2 c. pecans, chopped
2 c. icing sugar
1/2 tsp. vanilla
ice cream (for serving)
caramel sauce (for serving)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In large bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, sugar, and salt together; set aside.
In a 10-inch cast iron skillet, bring butter, oil, cocoa powder, and water to a boil. Remove it from heat and whisk in the dry ingredients well.
Mix in the buttermilk, egg, and vanilla.
Bake at 350 degrees F for about 15-20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out with just a few moist crumbs.
While cake cools, make frosting.
In a med. saucepan, bring butter, cocoa, and milk to a boil.
Remove from heat and add the icing sugar, nuts, and vanilla. Stir to combine.
Pour over the warm cake, spread with a spatula, and serve with vanilla bean ice cream & caramel sauce.
