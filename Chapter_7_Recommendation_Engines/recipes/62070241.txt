Title:
Hot Hash Brown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. Frozen Hash Browns, thawed
1 Can of Cream of Chicken Soup
16 oz. Sour Cream
1/2 cup Butter, softened
3/4 cup Diced Onion
3 cups of Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 teaspoon Salt
1 teaspoon Pepper

Directions:
Lightly spray a 9x13 cooking dish with cooking spray.  Put hash browns into dish & sprinkle with salt & pepper.  In medium mixing bowl, blend the cream of chicken soup, sour cream, butter & onions.  Add the mixture to the hashbrowns & blend well.  Add cheese to the top & bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Stefany Grider Baldwin
Description: 
Creamy hash brown casserole.  Great served with eggs, bacon & biscuits for breakfast.
