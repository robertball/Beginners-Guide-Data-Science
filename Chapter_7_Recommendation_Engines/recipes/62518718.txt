Title:
Double Layer Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/511321.jpg
Ingredients:
4 ounces cream cheese, softened
1 cup + 1 tablespoon milk
1 tablespoon white sugar
12 ounces freshly whipped topping (see Easy Whipped Cream recipe)
1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust (see Graham Cracker Crust recipe)
2 (4-serving) packages instant vanilla pudding mix
1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin
2 tsp pumpkin pie spice

Directions:
In a large bowl, whisk together cream cheese, 1 tablespoon of milk, and sugar until smooth. Gently stir in half of whipped topping. Spread onto bottom of crust.
Pour 1 cup of milk into large bowl; add in pudding, pumpkin and spice.
Beat with wire whisk for 2 minutes, or until well blended and thick. Spread over cream cheese layer.
Refrigerate 4 hours, or until set.
Serve with dollop of whipped topping.

Submitter: 
Becca Owens
Description: 
This pie is a yummy blend of buttery graham cracker crust, a creamy whipped topping layer and a rich layer of pumpkin on top. Finish with a dollop of freshly whipped topping. Delicious!Modified from: Double Layer Pumpkin Pie
