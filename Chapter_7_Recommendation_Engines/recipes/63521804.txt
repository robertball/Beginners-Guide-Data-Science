Title:
Honey Dill Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb carrots (either the prepped 'baby carrots' or normal carrots pealed and about the same size, 1-1.5" long)
1.5 T orange maramalade
1 t honey
.5 t dry dill
2 T red wine vinegar
1 T butter
salt & pepper to taste
.5 t cornstarch

Directions:
Steam carrots al dentes
Remove from heat and shock with cold water (to stop cooking)
Mix Marmalade, honey, dill, vinegar, salt, pepper.
in sauce pan, on low heat, add carrots and dressing
Allow everything to warm, just until it starts to simmer
mix cornstarch with a 1T cold water, add to pan
Allow mix to bubble for a few seconds, stir around, eat
