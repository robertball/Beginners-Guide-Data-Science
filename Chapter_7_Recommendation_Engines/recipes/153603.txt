Title:
Golden Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 (12 ounce) packages frozen cooked winter squash
8 cups russet potatoes, peeled, cut in 1-inch cubes
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup sour cream
1 tablespoon Spice Islands® Garlic Powder
1 1/2 teaspoons Spice Islands® Italian seasoning
1/2 teaspoon Spice Islands® White Pepper
2 teaspoons Spice Islands® Onion Salt
1 cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated

Directions:
Cook squash according to package directions.
Place potatoes in large pan and cover with water. Cook until tender, about 15 minutes; drain.
Combine squash, potatoes, butter and sour cream in a mixing bowl. Beat until smooth. Add remaining ingredients and beat another 1 to 2 minutes to thoroughly mix. Serve hot.

Submitter: 
Spice Islands®
Description: 
Potatoes and winter squash, mashed together with sour cream, herbs and Parmesan cheese. A delicious twist on plain mashed potatoes!
