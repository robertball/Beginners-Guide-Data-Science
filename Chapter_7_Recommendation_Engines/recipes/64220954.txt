Title:
Sausage Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1 C. milk
1 C. Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 lb. sausage or bacon
1 bag frozen hashbrowns

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Line an 8 x 8 pan with hash browns.  Sprinkle with fried, drained, and crumbled sausage or bacon.  Mix eggs and milk.  Pour over hashbrowns and top with cheese. *Best if prepared night before and refrigerated.  Cook for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Gillies Stewart
Description: 
Great for a holiday breakfast!
