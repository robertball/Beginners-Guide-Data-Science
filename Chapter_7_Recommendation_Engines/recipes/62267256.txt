Title:
Carrot Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup basmati rice
2 cups water
1/4 cup salted sunflower seeds
1 tablespoon margarine
1 purple onion diced,
1/2 cup diced sweet red pepper
3/4 cup grated carrots
1 teaspoon ginger root, minced
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper

Directions:
Combine rice and water in a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to low, cover with lid, and allow to steam until tender, about 20 minutes. While rice is cooking, Melt margarine in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Saute onions until golden brown. Stir in ginger, carrots, red pepper and sunflowers. Reduce heat to low and cover to steam 5 minutes. Stir in cayenne pepper and peanuts. When rice is done, add it to skillet and stir gently to combine with other ingredients.

Submitter: 
Julie Hansen Ostler
Description: 
Fragrant basmati rice sauteed with carrots, onions, fresh ginger, sunflower seeds, and sweet red pepper. You will be surprised to taste this delicious rice.Modified from: Carrot Rice
