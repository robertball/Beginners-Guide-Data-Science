Title:
Bread Sticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup water
1/2 Tbsp. yeast
1 Tbsp. sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
1 3/4 cups flour
1/2 cube butter, melted
Garlic salt
Italian bread crumbs
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Put yeast and sugar in warm water, set aside.
Mix flour and salt, then add yeast mixture. Mix well.
Turn dough out on floured surface and knead for several minutes. Roll dough out and cut into strips.
Melt butter and pour into a cookie sheet. Roll strips in butter. Sprinkle with garlic salt, Italian bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese.
Bake on a greased pizza pan or cookie sheet at 400 F for 10-12 minutes.
