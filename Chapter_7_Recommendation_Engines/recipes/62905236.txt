Title:
Creamy and Zesty Ranch Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4559127.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup full fat sour cream
1/2 cup olive oil mayonnaise
1 tsp parsley flakes
1 tsp dried chives
3/4 tsp onion powder
3/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp celery seed
1/4 tsp dill weed
1/8 tsp white pepper
sea salt and pepper to taste
Optional, buttermilk

Directions:
In a small lidded container, combine all ingredients until well blended.  Best if allowed to sit in the fridge for several hours before using.  If you like a thinner consistency, add buttermilk to taste.
