Title:
Instant Pot Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs round or chuck cubes
4 parsnips, chopped
16 oz baby carrots, chopped
20 petite red potatoes, chopped
1 large onion, chopped
4 celery sticks, chopped
5 garlic cloves
8 oz tomato sauce
1 can beefy mushroom soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
3 Tbsp horseradish
3 Tbsp worcestershire
500 ml pinot noir or cabernet
4 cups water

Directions:
Dredge small batches of beef in instant pot.
Deglaze pot thoroughly with wine to avoid burn error.
Add water.
Add veggies & beef to pot.
Pour soups & sauces on top, do not mix to avoid burn error.
Pressure cook for 35 minutes, make sure valve is closed properly.
10-15 minutes to reach pressure, 10-15 minutes to release pressure.
Burn error may occur due to thick sauces.
