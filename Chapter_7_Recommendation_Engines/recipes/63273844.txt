Title:
Coconut Curry Chicken and Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbs sesame oil
1 tbs mustard seeds
1 C onion, chopped
1 tbs garlic, chopped
1 tbs curry powder
pinch of cayenne pepper
2 large organic chicken breasts, bone in
1/2 tsp sea salt
1 small carrot, diced
2 C cauliflower, medium florets
1/2 green bell pepper, diced
1 apple, chopped
1 13 oz can coconut milk
1 1/2 C frozen peas
fresh cilantro chopped for garnish

Directions:
In a large saute pan, heat the sesame oil on medium heat.
Add the mustard seeds and stir for 10 seconds until they start to pop; be careful not to burn them!
Immediately add the onions and garlic and saute for 5 minutes until they begin to sweat.
Add the curry and cayenne and stir to coat the onions.
Sprinkle the chicken with the 1/2 tsp of salt and add to the pan searing on both sides.
Add the carrots, cauliflower, and green pepper and cook for 3-4 minutes.
Add the apple, coconut milk, and remaining salt and simmer on low heat for 15-20 minutes, until the chicken is cooked through.
Add the peas and simmer another 3 minutes.
Serve garnish with cilantro.
