Title:
Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Sugar
1 cup of Corn Syrup, light or dark
1-1/2 cup Pecans
1 tsp. Vanilla
1/2 tsp. Salt
3 Eggs, slightly beaten
chilled pie crust

Directions:
1. preheat oven to 375. 2. Combine all ingreients qnd pour into pie crust. 3. Bake for 40 to 50 mins. 4 Check for doneness by inserting a toothpick in the center and it coming out clean.
