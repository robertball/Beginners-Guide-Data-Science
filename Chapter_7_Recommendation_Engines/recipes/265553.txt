Title:
Lunch Box Zucchini Slice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5659008.jpg
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon vegetable oil, or as needed
3 zucchini, grated
6 eggs, lightly beaten
1 large onion, finely chopped
1 cup self-rising flour
1 carrot, grated
3 thin bacon slices, diced
1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 6x10-inch baking pan thoroughly with oil.
Mix zucchini, eggs, onion, flour, carrot, bacon, Cheddar cheese, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl. Pour into the prepared baking pan.
Bake in the preheated oven until fully set, 30 to 40 minutes. Cool before slicing.

Submitter: 
BuddysMum
Description: 
Wonderful, eggy, cheesy snack for lunch boxes, picnics, etc. Quiche without the pastry. A real favorite - and a sneaky way of getting veggies in to the family. Sets firmly enough to dip into sauces.
