Title:
Cabbage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. ground beef
8 cups cabbage, chopped
1 can (28 oz.) tomatoes, chopped
3 1/2 cups water
1 onion, chopped
1 green pepper, chopped
2 ribs celery, chopped
1 can (15.5 oz.) butter beans, rinsed and drained
4 beef bouillon cubes
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/4 tsp. pepper

Directions:
In large pot over medium heat, cook ground beef, stirring occasionally, until browned, 4-5 minutes.  Add remaining ingredients.  Over medium-high heat, bring to boil.  Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer until cabbage is very tender and soup is flavorful, 1 hour.
