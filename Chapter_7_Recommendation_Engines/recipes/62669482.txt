Title:
Kale with white beans , potatoes and garlic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium potatoes cubed into 1/4 in. pieces
1 small bunch of kale washed and chopped
1 small bunch of leeks (white and green parts) chopped fine
1 can of small white beans drained
3 cloves of garlic chopped fine
3 TBSP of Olive Oil
1/2 C. of chicken broth
a dash of nutmeg
1/2 tsp of paprika
dash of cayenne pepper
salt and pepper (to taste)

Directions:
1. Heat oil in skillet until hot
2. Add potatoes and cook until browned on the outside and soft on the inside
3.Add garlic and leeks and cook another 3-5 minutes until leeks and and garlic are tender
4. Add paprika, cayenne, and salt and pepper
5. Add chicken broth
7. Add kale and mix ingredients together. Add nutmeg and salt and pepper.
8. Cook until Kale is tender (about 5-7 minutes)
