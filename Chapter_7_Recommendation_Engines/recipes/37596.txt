Title:
Turkey-in-the-Round Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989727.jpg
Ingredients:
1 loaf (about 24 oz.) round Italian bread
1/2 cup MIRACLE WHIP Salad Dressing
3 lettuce leaves
1 tomato, sliced
1 (5.5 ounce) package LOUIS RICH CARVING BOARD Oven Roasted Turkey Breast
4 KRAFT Swiss Cheese Slices cut in half

Directions:
Cut bread in half horizontally.  Spread cut sides of bread with salad dressing.
Top bottom half of bread with lettuce, tomato, turkey and cheese.  Cover with top half of bread.  Cut into wedges.
