Title:
Krauterbutter (Herb Butter) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/887197.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 pound butter
2 tablespoons fresh parsley, finely chopped
1/2 teaspoon tarragon
1/2 teaspoon dill
1 teaspoon fresh chives, finely chopped
1 teaspoon onion, minced
2 teaspoons lemon juice
salt

Directions:
Allow butter to come to room temperature.
Add all ingredients and whip together in a glass saucepan.
Let stand overnight in a cool place.  Salt to taste.
Whip lightly just before serving.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Serve on bread, fish, vegetables, pasta, fried, bake, or roasted meats.
