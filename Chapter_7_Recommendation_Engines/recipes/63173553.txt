Title:
The Brooklyn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/815058.jpg
Ingredients:
2 oz rye whiskey
3/4 oz. dry vermouth
1/3 oz. maraschino
1/3 oz Amer Picon (or substitute, such as Ramazzotti)

Directions:
Stir well with ice and strain in to a cocktail glass. Garnish with an orange zest twist.

Submitter: 
Rudy Torrijos III
Description: 
This will test even the finest of mixologists.
