Title:
Awesome Slow Cooker Pot Roast II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
1 1/8 cups beef broth
5 1/2 pounds pot roast
1 Tbs worchestershire sauce
1/8 cup red wine vinegar

Directions:
In a slow cooker, mix cream of mushroom soup, dry onion soup mix vinegar, w. Sauce, broth. Place pot roast in slow cooker and coat with soup mixture.
Cook on High setting for 3 to 4 hours, or on Low setting for 8 to 9 hours.
Last two hours add quartered yellow oniom, carrots, and yukon potatoes.

Submitter: 
Patricia Schulze
Description: 
Add two simple ingredients to a beef pot roast and turn on the slow cooker. It's that easy and it makes its own delicious gravy.Modified from: Awesome Slow Cooker Pot Roast
