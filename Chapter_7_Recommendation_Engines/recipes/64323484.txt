Title:
Protein Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Old Fashioned Oats
2 Eggs
3 Egg Whites
1 cup Cottage Cheese
1 tsp Raw Honey
Pure Vanilla Extract to taste
Cinnamon to taste

Directions:
Blend all ingredients in a blender until creamy.
Cook in a waffle iron or make as pancakes.

Submitter: 
JAIM71
Description: 
These healthy, protein packed waffles are fluffy and delicious! No waffle iron? No problem! Make them into pancakes instead. Top with berries, bananas, dark chocolate chips, or anything else you like!
