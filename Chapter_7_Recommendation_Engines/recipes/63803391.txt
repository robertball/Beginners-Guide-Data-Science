Title:
Old Fashioned Oatmeal Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1054882.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
1 C. Quick oats
1 C. Thompson seedless raisins
1/2 C. Butter
1/2 C. Granulated sugar
1/2 C. Brown sugar
2 Eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Line a large cookie sheet with parchment paper
Blend flour, baking powder and baking soda together in a small bowl.  Set aside
Mix butter and sugars together to form a smooth paste
Add eggs to butter/sugar mixture one at a time beating well after each addition until smooth and creamy
Mix in oats, and raisins combining them well with butter and sugar mixture beating on medium speed until thoroughly mixed together
Stir in flour and mix all ingredients together to form a drop cookie dough
Place small amounts of dough on parchment paper 2 inches apart
Bake for 10 minutes or just until lightly browned
Place on a wire rack to cool completely before putting in a storage container

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A crunchy cookie with the wholesome goodness of raisins and oatmeal for breakfast or snack time.
