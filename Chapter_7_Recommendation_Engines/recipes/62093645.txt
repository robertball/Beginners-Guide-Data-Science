Title:
Peach Cosmo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 ounces Cranberry juice
1 1/2 ounces Citron Vodka
1/2 ounce Peach Schnapps
1 teaspoon fresh lime

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a martini shaker with crushed ice. Shake until very well chilled. Strain into large martini glass and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Lori
Description: 
I was making a Cosmo for myself and I didn't have any Triple Sec so I used peach schnapps and loved it.
