Title:
Buffalo Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6040798.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 cup of Franks Red hot pepper sauce or any buffalo sauce bought in store
1/4 cup of coconut oil1
/2 tbs apple cider vinegar
1/4 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 tbs smoked paprika
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1/8 tsp garlic powder
1/8 tsp onion powder
1 Tbs kosher salt
1 cauliflower head cut into large florets

Directions:
Place the hot sauce, coconut oil, vinegar, Worcestershire Sauce,
paprika, onion powder and salt in a bowl and whisk until combined.
Toss florets in sauce. Place on air fryer tray
Set to cook for 10mins at 300F. Makes 2 serving
