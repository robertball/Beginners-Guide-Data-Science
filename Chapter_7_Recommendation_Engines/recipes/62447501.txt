Title:
Sausage - Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. sausage, browned and drained
1 cup chopped onion
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 Soup can water
1 cup uncooked rice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.  Pour into a 3 qt. casserole dish.  Cover andbake at 325 for 2 hours.
