Title:
White bean patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 oz) can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
1 large egg
3 T. flour
2 T. oil
3 Cloves garlic minced (1 T)
1 c. frozen peas
1/2 c. Italian-seasoned breadcrumbs
1/2 c. spagetti sauce, optional

Directions:
Puree first 6 ingredients in food processor or blender till smooth, adding more oil if needed.
Transfer to bowl and fold in peas and breadcrumbs.  Season w/ salt and pepper.  Shape mixture into 6 patties.
Spray large skillet with cooking spray, and warm over medium heat.  Cook until golden.  spray tops of patties, flip and cook till golden.
Serve with warmed sauce, if desired.
