Title:
Easy Pudding Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (4-oz.) Chocolate or Chocolate Fudge Pudding mix, NOT instant
2 tbsp. butter or margarine
1/4 c. milk
1 1/2 c. powdered sugar
1/4 c. chopped nuts, (optional)

Directions:
Grease a loaf pan with butter and set aside. Combine pudding mix, butter and milk in saucepan and bring to boil over medium heat, boil 1 min., stirring constantly. Mixture should be bubbling all over, but not foaming. Remove from heat, add sugar, blending well. Add nuts and pour into a loaf pan. Cool. Cut into squares and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Deborah Norris
Description: 
Fastest way to make a tasty fudge.
