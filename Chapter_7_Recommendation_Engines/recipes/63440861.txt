Title:
Sue's  - Linguesa Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 LBS of Linguesa (Portiguese Sausage)
1 LG White Onion Diced
1 Heart of Celery w/leaves (sliced thin)
6 Baby Bell Peppers (yellow, orange or red)
1 Can of diced fire roasted tomatoes
4 Cloves garlic (pressed)
2 bay leaves
Tabasco to taste
3 dashes of Worcestershire sauce
Salt and pepper to taste
2 packages of Uncle Ben's Cooked Rice any flavor you like.

Directions:
Cut Linguesa into half rounds and brown in skillet, spoon and set aside, add 1 T butter to the same pan and add all of your cut vegetables, salt and pepper them while they saute - until celery is soft.  Add Garlic, saute for about 2 more minutes, add the tomatoes,  bay leaves, Tabasco and Worcestershire -  and allow to simmer for about 5 minutes while the rice preheats in the microwave.  Open the packages of rice, stir into the pan and you're done!
