Title:
Shrimp Over Angel Hair Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
30 medium shrimp
1/4 lb. butter
2 t. minced garlic
2 T. sundried tomatoes, chopped fine
Lemon zest from 1/2 lemon, chopped fine
Juice from 1/2 lemon
1/4 t. onion powder
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt butter large saute pan.  Add garlic, sundried tomatoes, lemon zest, lemon juice and onion powder.  Add shrimp.  Cook until shrimp is done and no longer translucent. Add salt and pepper to taste.  To serve,toss with angel hair pasta.

Submitter: 
DonnaT
Description: 
Very easy, light dinner.  Serve with warm bread and salad for a nice meal.
