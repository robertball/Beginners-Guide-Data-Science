Title:
Chili Rice Dinner Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans favorite chili - no beans
2 cups uncooked rice - jasmine is best
3 medium onions
1/2 box velveeta
Jalepeno slices - optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Prepare rice according to package.
Saute onions in pan until lightly browned.
Heat both cans of chili in onions and mix.
Cube/slice velveeta.
Place 1/4 of cheese in bottom of greased casserole dish along with 1/4 of chili onion mixture.
Next spread 1/2 of rice on top of chili onion mixture.
Add remaining chili and spread.
Final layer is remaining rice.
Top with remaining cheese.
Add jalepenos as desired
COVER and bake 30 minutes

Submitter: 
MitchJGray
Description: 
In the South, we make do with what we have on hand.  This recipe ROCKS!  Got some cans of chili or leftovers from homemade? Layer with rice and velveeta and take it to a whole new level.
