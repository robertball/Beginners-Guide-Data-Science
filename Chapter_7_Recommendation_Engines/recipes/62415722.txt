Title:
Loaded Mashed Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag Ore-Ida Steam n' Mash potatoes
2/3 cup evaporated milk
2 tbsp butter or magarine
4 oz cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup sour cream
2 tsp dried chives
3/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 cup cooked, crumbled bacon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Microwave potatoes according to package directions.  In a medium bowl, mash potatoes, evaporated milk and butter.  Stir in remaining ingredients, except cheese and bacon, until well combined.  Spread mixture in a 9x9" baking pan which has been coated with nonstick cooking spray.  Top with cheese and bacon.  Bake for 25 to 30 minutes or until heated through.
