Title:
Cranberry Kamikaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1129044.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce vodka
1 fluid ounce orange liqueur
1/2 fluid ounce sweetened lime juice
1/4 cup cranberry juice

Directions:
Pour vodka, orange flavored liqueur, sweetened lime juice, and cranberry juice over ice in a shaker. Shake until well mixed. Pour into a chilled cocktail glass.

Submitter: 
Michelle
Description: 
A fruity and delicious version of a Kamikaze. Serve this drink in a cocktail glass. Frost the rim with sugar for a festive touch.
