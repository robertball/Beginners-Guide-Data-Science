Title:
Italian Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pound Italian sausage (mild, sweet or hot)
2 clove garlic, minced
1 onion, diced
6 (14 ounce) cans broth(beef or chicken)
4 (14.5 ounce) can Italian-style stewed tomatoes
2 cup sliced carrots
2 (14.5 ounce) can great Northern beans, undrained
2 zucchini, cubed
1 bunch spinach, chopped
1 cup dry orzo, cooked and set aside
Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a stockpot or Dutch oven, brown sausage with garlic and onion.  Stir in  broth, tomatoes and carrots. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 15 minutes.
Stir in beans with liquid and zucchini. Cover and simmer another 15 minutes, or until zucchini is tender.
Remove from heat, and add spinach and orzo.  Replace lid allowing the heat from the soup to cook the spinach leaves.  Soup is ready to serve after 10-15 minutes.
Traditionally served with Parmesan sprinkled on top.

Submitter: 
Joan
Description: 
Sausage, beans, zucchini, fresh spinach, and carrots.  Makes a delicious winter supper with an Italian bent.Modified from: Italian Sausage Soup
