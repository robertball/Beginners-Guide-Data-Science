Title:
Fresh Caramel Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
FOR THE CAKE:
1 cup packed light-brown sugar
1 cup granulated sugar
1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
3 lage eggs
3 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon salt
5 apples, peeled and diced into 1/2 inch pieces
1 1/4 cups not-too-finely chopped pecans
2 1/2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
FOR THE GLAZE:
1/2 stick unslated butter
1/4 cup granulated sugar
1/4 cup light-brown sugar
1/2 cup heavy cream
A pinch of salt

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (or 160 degrees C).
Place the sugars and oil in a mixing bowl and beat until very well blended. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
Sift the flour, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt together and gradually add to the sugar mixture.
Stir in the apples, pecans and vanilla and pour into a greased 9 x 13 inch baking pan.
Bake until all test prove the cake is done, about 1 hour and 25 minutes (but be sure to start checking after about 50 minutes). Remove from the oven and allow to cool in the pan while you make the glaze.
To make the glaze, melt the butter in a scause pan with the sugars and salt. Stir until blended then cook over medium-low heat for about 2 minutes. Stir in the rest of the ingridients and boil for an additional 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from the heat.
Us a toothpick to poke holes all over the top of the cake and pour the warm glaze over the surface. Serve warm or at room temp.
