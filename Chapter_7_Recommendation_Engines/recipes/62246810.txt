Title:
SPAM Puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can SPAM
1 Cup grated cheddar
1 Cup baking mix
1 teaspoon water
1/4 teaspoon cayenne (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Grate the SPAM on a cheese grater. Mix together all ingredients in a large bowl, then form into 1" balls and place on greased cookie sheet. Bake 12-14 minutes until golden brown. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
KerryT
Description: 
This got me first place in a SPAM 'cookoff' that a local TV station held. They are fast & easy hors d'oeuvres and are always a hit during Sunday football!
