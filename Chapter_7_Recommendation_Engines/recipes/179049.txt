Title:
Country Chicken Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977836.jpg
Ingredients:
3 (14 ounce) cans Swanson® Natural Goodness™ Chicken Broth
1/2 cup uncooked regular long-grain white rice
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
1 (16 ounce) can mixed vegetables, drained
2 (5 ounce) cans Swanson® Premium Chunk Chicken Breast in Water, undrained

Directions:
In large saucepan mix broth and rice. Over medium-high heat, heat to a boil. Reduce heat  to low. Cover and cook 20 minutes or until rice is done.
Stir in parsley, vegetables and chicken. Heat through.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
This wonderfully satisfying soup features Swanson® Chicken Broth, mixed vegetables, long grain rice, parsley and Swanson® Premium Chunk Chicken.
