Title:
Reindeer Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pieces of sandwich bread
2 tbs. of peanut butter
4 raisins
2 cherries
4 pretzel sticks

Directions:
Spread the peanut butter on a piece of sandwich bread and place the other on piece of bread on top to make a sandwich.
Cut sandwich on a diagnol so you have 2 triangles.
Use 1 cherry as a nose on each. Use 2 raisins for eyes on each. Use 2 pretzels stuck into the sandwich as antlers on each.
Serve to your favorite kids.

Submitter: 
T.L
Description: 
My son's pre-school teacher gave this to me. It's easy, quick, nutritious and totally adorable. The kids love it.
