Title:
Chili's Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14 1/2oz can Ro*tel tomatoes and green chilies (original)
1 14 1/2 can whole tomatoes (plus juice)
4 teaspoons canned diced jalapenos (optional) (not pickled)
1/4 cup diced white onions
1/2-3/4 teaspoons garlic salt
1/4 teaspoon cumin
1/4 teaspoon chili powder
1/4 teaspoon splenda or sugar

Directions:
In a food processor place jalapenos and onion and process for just a few seconds.
Add both cans of tomatoes, salt, sugar, cumin and chili powder; process all ingredients until well blended but not pureed.
Placed in a covered container and chill for a couple of hours to blend and enrich the flavors.
