Title:
Cranberry Coconut Machine Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5005969.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup coconut milk
3 tablespoons coconut oil
2 cups bread flour
1 cup all purpose flour
2 tablespoon palm coconut sugar
1 1/4 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup chopped cranberries
1/4 cup coconut flake
2 teaspoons Active Dry Yeast

Directions:
In the bread machine case add the liquid ingredients.
In a bowl mix all the dry ingredients.
Add dry ingredients into the bread case.
Select basic bread medium crust setting.

Submitter: 
James
Description: 
I came up with this recipe basically because I had these ingredients in my kitchen. Don't expect a strong taste of coconut. I imagine you could go with a cup of water and add coconut milk power. You could substitute either white or brown sugar for the palm coconut sugar.
