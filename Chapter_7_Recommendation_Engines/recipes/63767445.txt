Title:
Make-ahead "The Works" Grilled Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 med Idaho potatoes (appox 4 lbs), peeled and cubed
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp Kosher salt
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp fresh cracked ground pepper
1/4 c olive oil
1 large green bell pepper, diced
1/2 large onion, diced
6 Tbsp unsalted butter, cut into 6 pieces
3/4 lbs bacon, cooked and minced
1.5 Tbsp fresh parsley, minced
1.5 Tbsp fresh oregano, minced
2 Tbsp fresh chives, minced
1/3 c. shredded Parmesan cheese
1 c. mozzarella cheese
1 c. cheddar cheese
1/2 c. sour cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Grease 10x15 in baking pan.
In a large bowl, toss potatoes with olive oil. In a small bowl, mix garlic powder, salt, chili powder, paprika, and pepper. Sprinkle over potatoes and stir.
Spread potatoes in a single layer on baking sheet. Bake for 20 min.
Cool potatoes, place in a 13x9 one time use aluminum pan, cover, and refrigerate overnight.
The next day, remove cover from potatoes and add green peppers and onion. Dot with butter. Cover with aluminum foil.
Preheat gas grill. Reduce heat to medium low. Place aluminum pan over direct heat and grill 25 min until peppers and onions are tender.
Add parsley, oregano, and bacon. Continue grilling for 5 min.
Remove aluminum foil, add Parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, cheddar cheese, and chives. Continue grilling until cheeses are melted.
Remove from heat.  Serve with sour cream.

Submitter: 
shackbart
Description: 
This is a perfect potato side dish for tail-gating or taking to a barbecue. Roasting the potatoes the day before adds flavor and cuts down on cooking time on the grill.
