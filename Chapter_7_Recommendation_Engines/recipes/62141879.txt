Title:
Creamy Mustard Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1/2 cup low-fat plain yogurt
1/3 cup light mayonnaise
1/4 cup sliced green onions
1 Tbsp stone ground mustard
1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp dried thyme
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 Tbsp freshly grated parmesan cheese
Chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Place chicken in single layer in lightly greased, ovenproof casserole dish. Combine yogurt, mayonnaise, onions, mustard and seasonings.  Pour sauce over each chicken breast, so that they are smothered. Bake in 350 degree oven for about 45 minutes or until chicken is cooked. Sprinkle with parmeasn cheese and brown under the broiler. Serve garnished with fresh parsley.
