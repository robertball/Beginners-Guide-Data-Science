Title:
Meatloaf II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pounds ground beef
1 egg
1 onion, chopped
1 cup milk
1 cup dried bread crumbs
salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup beef stock
10 oz can tomato paste
2 cups uncooked spinach (optional)
1/2 cup Feta cheese (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Saute onions until light, golden yellow.  Set aside.
Add ground beef, egg, onion, milk and bread crumbs to medium bowl.
(option) Roll meatloaf onto cutting board about the width of the loaf pan.  Add spinach or cheese stuffing to center of loaf, then roll meat into loaf shape.
Season with salt and pepper to taste and place in a 5x9 inch oven-safe glass loaf pan (Pyrex or Corning loaf pans are fine).
In a separate small bowl, combine the brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, tomato paste, and beef stock. Mix well and pour over the meatloaf (pour into center of load if desired).
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.

Description: 
Variation of my favorite meatloaf like grandmother used to make that is low-fat, low calorie, and high in flavor!  Great for stuffing with cheese, spinach, mushrooms, or other good things.Modified from: Easy Meatloaf
