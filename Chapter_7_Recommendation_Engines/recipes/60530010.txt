Title:
Garlic Butter Sauce II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 (8 ounce) package spaghetti
1/4 cup olive oil
2 TBSP butter
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a large pot with boiling salted water cook pasta until al dente. Drain.
In a small skillet over medium heat saute the garlic in olive oil and add butter.
Toss pasta with olive oil and garlic mixture. Add the parsley, salt, and ground black pepper to taste. Serve with fresh grated Parmesan cheese.
