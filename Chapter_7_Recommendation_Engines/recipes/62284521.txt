Title:
Cheddar and Red Pepper Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. butter
1/4 cup chopped ONION
1/2 cup chopped RED PEPPER
1 clove GARLIC, minced
1 cup cooked RICE (I used brown rice)
1 cup shredded CHEDDAR CHEESE
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Melt butter in medium saucepan.  Add onions, peppers and garlic and saute until tender.
Add cooked rice and heat through.
Add cheese and stir until melted.
Season with salt & pepper

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
Sauteed red peppers and cheddar cheese make for a tasty rice side dish.  I use leftover steamed brown rice (or frozen precooked rice - makes this dish a snap to prepare), but you could use whatever rice you want.
