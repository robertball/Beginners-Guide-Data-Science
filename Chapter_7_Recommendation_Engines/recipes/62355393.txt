Title:
Date Nut Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup boiling water
1/2 pound of pitted dates, finely cut
2 T. butter or margarine
1 3/4 cup of flour
1/4 t. salt
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg, beaten well
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese - I like to use sharp cheddar
3/4 to 1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Pour boiling water over the dates and the butter; let stand 5 minutes, until all the butter has melted and the mixture cooled. Mix flour with the salt, soda and sugar in a bowl. Stir in the date mixture, beaten egg, cheese and walnuts. Stir only until well blended. Pour into a well buttered loaf pan 5 X 9. Bake at 325ยบ for 50 to 60 minutes.

Linda T
Description: 
Delicious fruit bread with nuts and CHEESE! Yes cheese. It adds a wonderful dimension to the loaf.
