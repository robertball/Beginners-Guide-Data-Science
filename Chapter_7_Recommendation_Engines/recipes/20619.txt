Title:
Teriyaki Steak Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/586610.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup soy sauce
1 1/4 cups water
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 pinch ground ginger
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1/4 cup minced onion
1/4 cup sherry

Directions:
Combine soy sauce, water, brown sugar, ginger, garlic, onion and sherry and stir well until sugar dissolves.  Use to marinate meat or veggies.

Submitter: 
DIANEG38
Description: 
Marinate your favorite steak in this before grilling.
