Title:
Autumn Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion chopped
3 carrots sliced into rounds
1 28 oz. can stewed tomatoes
1 lb. ground beef (browned and drained)
8 oz. elbow macaroni (cooked according to package directions)
2 quarts cold water
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large soup pot add 2 quarts of cold water and heat.To a saute pan add and brown ground beef and drain well.Add to water in soup pot. Next add stewed tomatoes,carrots and onion along with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for 30 minutes and at the end of cooking add cooked and drained macaroni and serve.
