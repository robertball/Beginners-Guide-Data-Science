Title:
Chocolate Banana Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2 bananas, frozen
1-2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder (non-alkalized)

Directions:
Remove bananas from freezer and thaw for 10-15 minutes.
Place bananas and cocoa powder into blender. Blend until smooth.
Eat and enjoy! Cherries, blueberries, and strawberries can also be added to the blender or as toppings.
