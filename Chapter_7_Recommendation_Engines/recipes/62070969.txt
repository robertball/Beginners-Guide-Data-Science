Title:
Blueberry Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
2 cups of self-rising flour
2 stick butter or margarine,melted
1 cup of pecans, chopped
Filling:
1 8oz. cream cheese,softened
1`16oz. box powdered sugar
1 8oz. whipped topping
1 can of blueberry pie filling

Directions:
Mix crust ingredients and pat into a 9x13 cake pan.
Bake at 350 for 15 minutes until lightly brown.
Cool completely.
Mix cream cheese, powdered sugar, and whipped topping.
Spread on top of crust.
Top with pie filling.
Refrigerate at least 2 hours.

Submitter: 
STEFF71
Description: 
With it's crunchy crust, cream cheese filling and blueberry pie filling, it is a delight to make and eat.
