Title:
B.L.T.A Salad with Basil Mayo Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup bacon pieces
1/2 cup fat free mayonnaise
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1/4 cup finely chopped fresh basil
4 slices French bread, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 tablespoon canola oil
1 pound romaine lettuce - rinsed, dried, and torn into bite-size pieces
1 pint cherry tomatoes, quartered
1 half avocado, diced

Directions:
In a small bowl, whisk together fat free mayonnaise, vinegar and chopped basil and let dressing stand, covered, at room temperature.
In 400 degree oven, toss the bread pieces with the salt and pepper and spray with nonfat butter spray.  Bake for 15 minutes to 1/2 hour until brown and crispy.
In a large bowl mix together the romaine, tomatoes, avocado, bacon pieces and croutons.  Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well.

Description: 
Crispy romaine lettuce, juicy cherry tomatoes, crunchy-fried bacon and homemade croutons are tossed with a creamy, tangy, fresh basil infused dressing in this satisfying salad! Half an avocado adds good fats and lots of flavor.Modified from: B.L.T. Salad with Basil Mayo Dressing
