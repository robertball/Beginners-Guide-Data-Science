Title:
Mom's Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 med. apples, sliced
1 c. sugar
6 t. flour
¾ c. oats
¾ c. flour
¾ c. brown sugar
¼ t. soda
¼ t. baking powder
8 T. margarine, melted

Directions:
Mix together first three ingredients and place into greased 9 inch pan. Mix together remaining ingredients and pat over apple layer.  Bake at 325?  for 1 hour.
