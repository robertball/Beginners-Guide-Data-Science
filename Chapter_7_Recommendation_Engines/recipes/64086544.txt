Title:
Ice Box Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1 cup pecans
1 stick melted butter
Mix and press into a 9x13 pan. Bake at 400 degrees for 15 min. Let cool.
1 cup powdered sugar
8 oz cream cheese
1/2 (large) container of cool whip

Directions:
Mix together and spread on top of crust in 9x13 pan.
1 box instant chocolate pie filling
1 box instant vanilla pie filling
Mix these with 3 cups of milk until thick. Spread on top of cream cheese layer. Put the remainder of the cool whip on top. Sprinkle with pecans and hershey curls.
