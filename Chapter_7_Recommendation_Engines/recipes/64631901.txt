Title:
Kimchi Goya Champuru Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
200 grams atsuage (deep fried tofu)
1 piece bitter melon
1/2 can of Spam
50 grams kimchi
15 grams mochi
2 pieces eggs
2 tablespoon mayonnaise
1 teaspoon chicken stock powder
1/2 tablespoon soy sauce
1/2 tablespoon sesame oil

Directions:
Cut the bitter melon along its length then take the seeds at the center using a spoon. Slice it thinly then soak in water with salt for at least 5 minutes. Drain before using.
Slice the atsuage and spam into small bite-sized pieces.
In a bowl, toss the atsuage, bitter melon, spam, kimchi, and mochi.
In a small bowl, whisk egg then add mayonnaise, chicken stock powder, soy sauce and sesame oil. Add it with the rest of the ingredients.
Cover the bowl then cook in the microwave for 6 minutes using 600W.
Take out and mix then you can serve.

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
This is the simplest way to cook goya champuru, the famous Okinawa food made from bitter melon and atsuage or deep fried tofu.
