Title:
Corn Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 cups leftover cornbread broken into 2"x2" pieces or smaller
6 eggs beaten
2 cups milk
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 tea cinnamon
1 tea vanilla
1/2 tea allspice
1/2 tea ginger
crumb topping
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 stick butter

Directions:
butter the sides of a large  casserole bowl
place broken up corn bread in bowl
in small bowl mix eggs, milk , spices, sugar, vanilla ...pour over bread...toss to evenly cover
Topping - in small bowl mix flour, brown sugar...cut in the butter till pea size spread over milked soaked bread
Bake 350* for 45-60 min
