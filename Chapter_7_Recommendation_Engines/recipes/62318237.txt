Title:
Jan's Raspberry Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/376627.jpg
Ingredients:
7 cups cold water
1 and 1/3 cup lemon juice concentrate
4 ounces Juicy Juice 100% berry concentrate (comes in a can)
1 cup sugar

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and chill.
HINT: When I buy the can of concentrate I only use 4 out of the 12 ounces.  I freeze the other 8 ounces in 4 ounce containers for future use.

Submitter: 
What's for dinner, mom?
Description: 
Sweet and tangy.  Light and refreshing.  My kids say it is their favorite even more than store brands!  Very easy and delicious!
