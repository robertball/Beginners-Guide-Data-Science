Title:
Turkey Meatballs with a Kick! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/394171.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 lb of ground turkey
1 minced garlic clove
1 small yellow onion finely chopped
1/4 yellow bell pepper (optional)
1 egg
1/2 cup of bread crumbs
1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon of chili powder
1/2 teaspoon of cumin
1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
1/2 teaspoon of salt

Directions:
Grease a large baking sheet pan and preheat over at 400Â°
Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix with hands till mixture is consistent.  Careful not to overwork the mixture or your end result will be a tough meatball.
Shape mixture into balls that are 1 inch in diameter.  Place on baking sheet pan about 1 inch apart.
Bake for 15-18 minutes and then add to your favorite sauce or serve as appetizers with hot sauce.

Submitter: 
chorusgirl3
Description: 
This less fattening alternative to a traditional meatball has a bit of a spicy kick.  Goes great with pasta dishes or serve with hot sauce as an appetizer.
