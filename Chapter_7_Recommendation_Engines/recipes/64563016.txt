Title:
Chick-Fil-A Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3493547.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tsp Vinegar
1/4 c Sugar
3/4 tsp Dry mustard
1/4 tsp Salt
1 cup Mayonnaise
2 bags (10oz) Fine shredded cabbage, chopped to 1/8 inch
1/4 cup finely chopped carrots

Directions:
Whisk vinegar, sugar, mustard and salt together until sugar is dissolved.
Add mayonnaise and whisk to mix .
Add cabbage and carrots, mix to combine.
Refrigerate for 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Dustin Fry
Description: 
This is the recipe chick-fil-a released after the horrible decision to remove cole slaw from their menu.By the way, there is no cook time involved. All recipes made me put something in the box.
