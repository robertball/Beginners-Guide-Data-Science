Title:
Baked Crispix Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks butter
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup light corn syrup
1 box Crispix
1 cup shelled peanuts or other nut
1 cup pretzel sticks

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees
Boil butter, brown sugar and light corn syrup for 3 min.
Pour over dry ingredients and mix well, coating all.
Pour onto cookie sheet and bake at 250 degrees for 1 hour 15min...stirring every 15 min.

Submitter: 
Kerri Klein
Description: 
A recipe from my Grandmother.  Its made for family get togethers and is usually gone soon after it is put out!  Its sweet and salty and crunchy and addictive!
