Title:
Rita's Wassail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
64 oz apple cider
1 1/2 c pineapple juice
1/4 c sugar
3 T red hots
2 c orange juice
1/4 c lemon juice
2 cinnamon sticks
1 tsp whole cloves

Directions:
If you have a perculator put all liquids in the pot & the candy, sticks & cloves in the perculator tray. If not just simmer on the stove top & strain before serving.
