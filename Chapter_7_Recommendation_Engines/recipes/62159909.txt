Title:
Spicy Mexican Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 oz. can diced tomatoes, undrained
14 oz. can black beans, drained and rinsed
14 oz. can whole kernal corn, drained
6 oz. can diced green chiles, drained
1/2 cup sweet onion, diced
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 TB lime juice
1/4 tsp. cayenned pepper
1/2 tsp. cumin
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. black pepper
2 TB fresh cilantro, diced

Directions:
In a medium size bowl, add all the ingredients.
Stir and cover and refrigerate for several hours or overnight.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
This healthy and spicy southwestern corn and black bean salad with a kick has no oil.  I used corn cut right off the cob that I had grilled but canned corn can certainly be used.  I serve this with grilled chicken and a slab of cornbread.  Yum!
