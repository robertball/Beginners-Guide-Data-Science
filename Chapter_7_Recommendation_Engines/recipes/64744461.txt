Title:
Fried rice by Tammy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6515023.jpg
Ingredients:
3 oz diced ham (or chicken), small cubed
1/3 onion, diced
1 carrot, diced
1 celery diced
1/3 c frozen baby peas
Garlic and onion powder
2 eggs, beaten
2 c cooked rice (preferable a day or two old)
2tbl oil, divided
Sauce - mix together.
1/3 c soy sauce
1tbl brown sugar
1/2 tsp white sugar
1/2 tsp ginger
Pinch red pepper flakes

Directions:
Brown ham (cook chicken) add a little oil if necessary. Remove from pan.
Sauté vegetables (except peas) in a little oil. Season lightly if desired. Remove from pan.
Add a little oil to pan, cook eggs. Remove from pan.
Add a little more oil to pan, add rice and cook until it begins to “pop”.
Add sauce and mix well. Add ham, vegetables, egg and frozen peas. Mix and heat until all is hot.

Submitter: 
Tammy Doerr
Description: 
I’ve always had trouble getting it right. I think the key is to basically cook everything separate then put it all together at the end.
