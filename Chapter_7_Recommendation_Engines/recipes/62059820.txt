Title:
Tuna Noodle Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
My mom's Basic recipe:
1/2 - 3/4 bag Egg Noodles (eye ball it)
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 large can of chunck light tuna fish
2-3 cups grated medium cheddar cheese
(eye ball it, more or less to your taste.
Salt and pepper.
1/2- 1 cup crushed regular potato chips
(eye ball it) (Optional)
suggested add ins, I only use one of these:
1/2 cup frozen peas or corn.
1 diced tomato
or what ever else your taste buds crave. :)

Directions:
Cook egg noodles until almost done. Drain noodles and transfer to casserole dish.
Mix together the noodles, soup, 1 cup of cheese, and tuna.(and any add ins you may desire) sprinkle with salt and pepper and mix once more. Sprinkle remaining cheese on top and place into the oven at 350-400 until the MIDDLE of the casserole is bubbley. Add crushed potato chips on top 5-10 mins before taking out of the oven. Be sure not to let chips burn.
Serve with a side salad or crusty bread.
