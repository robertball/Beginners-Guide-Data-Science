Title:
Shells with Red Sauce and Yellow Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. box medium shell pasta
2 medium yellow squash, diced
1 medium onion, chopped
1 28 oz. can tomato sauce
1 1/2 tsp. minced garlic
2 tbsp. olive oil
1 tsp. Italian seasoning
1/2 tsp. dried oregano
salt and red pepper flakes to taste

Directions:
Boil water for pasta shells, cooking according to package directions.  Heat olive oil at medium high heat in a large frying pan.  Dice squash and chop onions.  Saute onions and squash in oil until softened.  Season with salt and red pepper flakes.  Add garlic and cook until lightly browned.  Next, add tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, and oregano, stirring to mix well.  Cover and simmer over low heat.  When pasta is done cooking, dish shells into bowls and ladle sauce over pasta.  Serve with grated Parmesan cheese.
