Title:
Crock Pot Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 fresh tomatoes - peeled & diced/chopped
1 chopped onion
2 jalapeno peppers with seeds - chopped
1 Hungarian wax pepper* with seeds - chopped
6-10 chopped garlic cloves (more or less for your taste)
1/8 c. sugar
1 T. vinegar
1/8 c. fresh chopped parsley
1/8 c. fresh chopped oregano
2 T. white pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in crock pot and mix well.  Cook on low 6-8 hours, stirring occasionally.  You can let this cook on low over night as well.
*peppers can be substituted for any variety you like.  We just use whatever is ripe in our garden at the time.

Submitter: 
debbi821
Description: 
Fresh ingredients and lots of time for the ingredients to blend make this a very tasty salsa.
