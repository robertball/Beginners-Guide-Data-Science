Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Cacciatore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package (1 pound 5 ounces) bone-in chicken thighs
1 jar (4.5 ounces) sliced mushrooms
1 can (14 ounces)diced tomatoes
1 can (14 ounces) chicken broth
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme leave
12 ounces uncooked  whole wheat penne pasta
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 Cup shredded Parmesan cheese
1 Cup shredded Ramano cheees

Directions:
Remove skin from chicken thighs.  Place in a 3-4 quart slow cooker.  Add mushrooms, diced tomatoes, chicken broth, and thyme.  Cover and cook on low for 8 hours.
About 15 minutes before serving, cook and drain pasta as directed on package.
Mix Parmesan cheese and ramano cheese in small bowl.  Set aside.
Remove 1 cup of sauce from slow cooker and place in small bowl.  Mix in corn starch and return to slow cooker, stirring well.  Cover and cook 10 minutes, stirring as needed.
After turning slow cooker off, add cooked penne.
Sprinkle cheese mixture over pasta and chicken and serve immediately.
