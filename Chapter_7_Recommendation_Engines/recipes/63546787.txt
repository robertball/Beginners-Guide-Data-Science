Title:
Lara Bites, cherry & protein Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup dried pitted cherries (44 g)
4 medjool dates (60 g)
1/3 cup sunflower seeds (or nuts) (44 g)
1/8 tsp cinnamon
2 tbsp protein powder

Directions:
Place the dates and cherries in a food processor. Pulse until processed to a paste or sticky ball. Transfer paste to a medium bowl (donâ€™t clean processor).
Add the seeds/nuts to the processor and pulse until finely chopped.
Add the seeds, along with the cinnamon and protein powder to the bowl with the fruit paste and use your fingers to knead the nuts & powder into the paste.
Divide mixture into 9 equal portions (21 g each), roll into a ball and wrap tightly with plastic wrap. Store in fridge.
