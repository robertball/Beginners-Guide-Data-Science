Title:
MA's Amazing Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4566953.jpg
Ingredients:
16 oz bag cole slaw mix
2 TBL grated onion
1 TBL pickled jalapeno, finely minced
1 TBL pickled jalapeno, juice from the jar
1/3 cup mayo
3 TBL apple cider vinegar
3 TBL white vinegar
1 tst poppy seeds
1/3 cup sugar
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp salt
2 oz cream cheese (softened 10 sec in microwave)

Directions:
Combine slaw, onion and jalepeno.
Mix remaining ingredients and stir into slaw.  Refridgerate for 45 minutes before serving.  Stir well before serving.
