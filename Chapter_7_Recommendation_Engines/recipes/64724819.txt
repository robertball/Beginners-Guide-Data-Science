Title:
Bob's Butter Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6063298.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Stick butter
1 Large onion, diced
4 Large garlic cloves, minced
1 Quart canned tomatos
1 Quart canned butter Beans
3 T Dried parsley or 1/2 C fresh parsley chopped
Salt, black pepper and red pepper flakes to taste

Directions:
Melt the butter in a soup pan
Sauté the onions until translucent
Add the minced garlic and continue to sauté for several minutes
Add canned tomatoes with their juice to pot
Add the butter beans (drained if you want a heartier soup or with their broth if you like it soupy)
Bring to a boil
Add salt and peppers to taste
Stir in parsley and serve with a warm bread or as my kids like it with grilled cheese sandwiches!

Submitter: 
Shanda
Description: 
My dad created this simple, easy and delicious recipe when I was a child.  It is a family favorite that warms our bellies on chilly nights and one we share with others frequently.  Dad lives on in many ways but this recipe will always represent him in our hearts.
