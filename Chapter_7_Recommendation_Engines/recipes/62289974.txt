Title:
Uncooked Banana Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package vanilla wafer cookies
1 (3 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
2 1/2 cups milk
2 medium bananas, peeled and sliced
Whipped topping

Directions:
In large bowl make pudding.  Stir well.  In the bottom of a trifle bowl or other glass serving dish, put a layer of cookies, then a layer of pudding mixture, then a layer of bananas.  Repeat until all ingredients are used.  Refrigerate until serving.
