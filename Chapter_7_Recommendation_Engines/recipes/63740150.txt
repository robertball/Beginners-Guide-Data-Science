Title:
Crock Pot Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1038305.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound sweet italian sausage
1 small sweet onion, chopped
2 tablespoon minced garlic
1 tablespoon worcestershire
1 (32 ounce) jar marinara
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon oregano
1 (12 ounce) package ready for oven lasagna noodles
15 ounces low fat ricotta cheese
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
12 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese (2, 8 oz bags)

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium heat cook the ground italian sausage, worcestershire, dried basil and oregano, salt, onion, and garlic until brown. Add the marinara sauce, tomato paste and stir until well incorporated. Cook until heated through.
In a large bowl mix together the ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and 1
(8 oz) bag of shredded mozzarella cheese.
Spoon a layer of the meat mixture onto the bottom of the slow cooker. Add a double layer of the uncooked lasagna noodles. Break to fit noodles into slow cooker. Top noodles with a portion of the cheese mixture. Repeat the layering of sauce, noodles, and cheese until all the ingredients are used. Sprinkle 4 oz. of mozzarella cheese on top evenly.
Cover, and cook on LOW setting  for 4 to 6 hours.

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
Start with a skillet and finish with a slow cooker. A saute of onion, garlic and italian sausage is simmered with tomato and basil, then layered in a slow cooker with raw lasagna noodles and a creamy blend of ricotta cheese, Parmesan and mozzarella.Modified from: Slow Cooker Lasagna
