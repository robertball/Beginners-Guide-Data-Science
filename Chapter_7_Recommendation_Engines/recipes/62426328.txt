Title:
Big Daddy's Slow Cooker  Mila Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - boiled
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup butter
13 cups white bread cubes, baked until slightly dry
1 teaspoon Big Daddy's Lemon Pepper Rub
2 tablespoons of Mila
1 1/4 teaspoons salt
1/2 teaspoon dried sage
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 1/2 cups reserved cooking liquid from chicken
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 cup chopped celery

Directions:
FOR DRESSING: Combine the onion, butter or margarine, bread cubes, Big Daddy's Lemon Pepper Rub, Mila, salt, sage, pepper, reserved cooking liquid, Campbell's mushroom soup and celery. Mix well.
In a slow cooker, layer the cooked chicken and dressing mixture. Cook on low for 4 to 8 hours. Serve.

Submitter: 
BIG DADDY'S
Description: 
Stuffing from scratch--just some herbs, bread crumbs chopped celery and onion, bread crumbs--slow cooks with boiled chicken and a creamy mushroom sauce in this tried and true family dinner.Modified from: Slow Cooker Escalloped Chicken
