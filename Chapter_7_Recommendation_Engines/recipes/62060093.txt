Title:
Fettucine with Clam and Shrimp Alfredo sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb fettucine pasta
2 jars alfredo sauce
2 cans chopped clams
40-50 medium shrimp
garlic
parsley
oregano
fresh ground pepper
parmesan cheese

Directions:
saute shrimp and clams in a little butter..add garlic and other spices..meanwhile cook Fettucine until al dente...heat alfredo sauce in pot and add some extra parmesan cheese then add in clam and shrimp mixture.Heat through. Serve with crusty garlic bread. Enjoy!!

Submitter: 
KARYN821
Description: 
A delicious, creamy sauce with shrimp and clams over fettucine noodles. A very elegant and easy meal to prepare.
