Title:
Nana & Popa's Chili Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 pounds ground beef or turkey
4 15oz cans of pinquito beans in chili sauce (undrained) or pinto beans in chili sauce (undrained)
1 29oz can of tomato sauce
2 4oz cans diced green chiles (undrained) mild or hot
1 large onion diced
2 tablespoons chili powder (or more)
2 tablespoons garlic powder
1 teaspoon oregano (Mexican preferably)
1 tablespoon chili garlic sauce (optional)
Garnish:
1 bunch chopped cilantro
1 medium chopped onion
grated cheese
Hot sauce of choice

Directions:
In a slow cooker mix all ingredients except meat and onions, stir and set on low.
In a pan cook meat and onions until meat is no longer pink.
Add meat and onions to slow cooker, stir and cook on low for at least four hours.
Garnish with chopped cilantro mixed with diced raw onions; shredded cheese and hot sauce of choice.
