Title:
Salty Dog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992366.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce Smirnoff No. 21 Vodka
1 fluid ounce grapefruit juice
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a shaker, add Smirnoff No.21 Vodka and grapefruit juice.
Stir and serve over ice in salt-rimmed glass.

Submitter: 
Smirnoff
Description: 
Refreshing grapefruit juice and vodka make this cocktail a not-too-sweet refresher. It's served in a salt-rimmed glass.
