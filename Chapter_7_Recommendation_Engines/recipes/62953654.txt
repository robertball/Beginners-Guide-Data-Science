Title:
Basic Mexican Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBS Canola Oil
1/2 Onion Chopped (white or yellow)
3 Garlic Cloves Minced
1 tsp salt
1 tsp roasted cumin
2 Cups Rice (2 3/4 cups for the rice cooker)
2 Cups Chicken Broth
1 Can Diced Tomatoes drained or 4 fresh chopped tomatoes
Fresh Cilantro

Directions:
Saute onion, garlic and oil over medium high heat stirring constantly until onions are tender. Pour into rice cooker along with all other ingredients except cilantro.  Use the white rice setting.  Add fresh cilantro to rice just before serving.
