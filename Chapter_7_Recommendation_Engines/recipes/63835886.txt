Title:
Big Ray's Apple Baked Oatmeal Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065974.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup old-fashioned oats
1/4 cup dried cranberries
1 Tbsp brown sugar
2 cups  milk
1 small apple diced
1/2 cup chunky applesauce
dash of salt
1/4 tsp almond extract
2 Tbsp sliced almonds

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the first eight ingredients. Transfer to a 3-cup baking dish coated with cooking spray; sprinkle with almonds.
Bake, uncovered, at 350Â° for 45-50 minutes or until set. Serve with toppings if desired.
