Title:
Mini Cheese Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/960991.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages of cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup (one stick) butter, softened
1 tablespoon cheddar cheese powder (from a box of mac and cheese)
1 7/8 cups Sargento Four States shredded Cheddar cheese
2 tablespoons thinly sliced green onion
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon dried parsley
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon dried parsley
2 cups chopped pecans or more as needed

Directions:
1. In a medium-sized mixing bowl or stand mixer, blend cream cheese and butter. Stir in cheddar cheese powder, shredded cheese, green onion, lemon juice, teaspoon of parsley, garlic powder and worcestershire sauce.
2. Chill for two hours or until slightly firm.
3. Toss together chopped pecans and tablespoon of parsley.
4. Shape mixture into 1 1/2 inch balls using your hands or a cookie scoop to keep them uniform.
5. Roll balls in the pecan and parsley mixture to coat.
6. Chill and serve with your favorite cracker.
You may choose to serve them with lollipop sticks inserted in the middle.
