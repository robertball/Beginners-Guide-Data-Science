Title:
Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups all purpose flour
3 tsps baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup sugar
1 1/4 cups milk
1 1/2 cups All Bran cerreal
1 egg
1/3 cups shortening or vegetable oil

Directions:
Stir together flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Set aside.
Stir together ceral and milk. Let stand for about two minutes.
Add egg and shortening to cereal and beat well.
Add flour mixture, stirring only to combine.
Fill 12 greased muffin cups and bake at 400F for 25 minutes.
