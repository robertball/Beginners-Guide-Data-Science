Title:
Tiki Cooler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3572830.jpg
Ingredients:
ice cubes
1 (6 ounce) can pineapple juice
6 fluid ounces coconut milk
3 limes, juiced
2 tablespoons almond-flavored syrup (such as ToraniÂ®)
1 cup club soda, or as needed
4 pinches ground nutmeg
4 lime wheels
4 sprigs fresh mint
cocktail umbrellas

Directions:
Place a few ice cubes in a pitcher; add pineapple juice, coconut milk, lime juice, and almond-flavored syrup and stir until chilled. Strain mixture and pour into 4 glasses.
                                    Watch Now
Top each glass with club soda; mocktail will foam up.
                                    Watch Now
Dust each mocktail with nutmeg. Garnish with a lime wheel, sprig of mint, and umbrella.
