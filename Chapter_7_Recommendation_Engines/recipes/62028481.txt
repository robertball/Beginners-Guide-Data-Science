Title:
Easy Elegant Chocolate Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 C. finely crushed graham crackers (about 18 crackers)
1/3 C. melted butter
1/4 C. granulated sugar
2 pkgs Chocolate Jell-O instant pudding
2 3/4 milk
1 pint whipping cream
4 Tbsp powdered sugar

Directions:
Combine granulated sugar and crushed
graham crackers.  Add melted butter and
mix well.  Spread evenly into 9-inch
deep dish pie plate.  Press well onto
bottom and sides of dish.  Chill 1 hour.
Pour milk into medium bowl; add pudding
mix.  Beat with whisk or on lowest speed
of mixer 2 minutes.  Pour into pie crust.
Chill 3 hours.
Add powdered sugar to whipping cream;
beat with whisk or on high setting of
mixer until soft peaks form.  Spread on
top of pie.

Submitter: 
KathCook
Description: 
Simple to make yet still elegant.
Top with shaved chocolate for adults, 
candy sprinkles for kids, or nothing 
at all.

Nothing beats homemade graham cracker 
crust and whipped cream!
