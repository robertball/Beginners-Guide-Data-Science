Title:
Parmesan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup bread crumbs
1 T. parsley, dried
1/8 t. pepper
6 chicken breasts
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
1 t. salt
1/8 t. Thyme
3 T margarine

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Combine bread crumbs, cheese and seasonings in a shallow dish.  Dip chicken in crumb mixture.  Coat both sides.  Melt margarine in baking dish in oven.  Add chicken, turning to coat both sides with margarine.  Bake, uncovered, for 1 hour.
