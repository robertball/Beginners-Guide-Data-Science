Title:
Buttermilk Pan Fried Catfish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 catfish fillets (5-6 oz each)
1 1/2 cups-buttermilk
2 eggs
2 tablespoons seasoning salt
1/2 tablespoon black pepper
1 tablespoon hot sauce
1 1/2 cups- yellow corn meal
1/2 cup- flour
1/4 tsp salt
2 teaspoons paprika
6 cups oil for frying

Directions:
Rinse catfish fillets with cold water and pat dry
In bowl combine buttermilk, eggs seasoning salt, pepper and hot sauce add catfish fillets and refrigerate at least 2 hours. Remove fish from buttermilk marinate.
Combine cornmeal, flour, salt and paprika.
Dredge catfish in mixture to coat.
In pan heat the oil to a medium high heat. Cook till a golden brown approx 3 minutes turn and finish cooking another couple minutes.
Drain on paper towel.
