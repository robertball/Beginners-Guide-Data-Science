Title:
Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1 cup water
1/4 teaspoon garlic and herb Ms. Dash
salt and pepper to taste
2 large heads of broccoli cut into florets

Directions:
In a large skillet, combine the butter, water, Ms. Dash, salt and pepper. Bring to a simmer over medium heat. Add the broccoli to the pan, stir to coat, and cover with a lid. Cook for 10 to 15 minutes over medium-low heat, stirring once, until broccoli is tender but still bright green. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Anne Soie
Description: 
A fast and zesty take on frozen broccoli. Can also use fresh broccoli. Great summer dish. Can be served hot or cold.Modified from: Broccoli with Lemon Butter Sauce
