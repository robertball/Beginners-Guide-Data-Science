Title:
Holiday Herb Stuffing Recipe

Ingredients:
1 bag cubed Stuffing BUT I like to use my special bread recipe and make my own!
1 box Chicken Stock
1 Stick of Margerine or Butter
1 egg
sauted Onion, Celery and Mushrooms
Pepper, sage and Rosemary to taste

Directions:
1.Prepare Stuffing according to package directions
2.Add Egg and Sauted Vegetables
3.Season with Rosemary, Sage and Pepper
4.Bake in Baking dish for 25 to 30 mins
