Title:
New York Style Chili Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
8 HORMEL® Hot Dogs
8 hot dog buns, split
1 (15 ounce) can HORMEL® Chili No Beans
Yellow mustard
Chopped onions (steamed, if desired)

Directions:
Grill hot dogs. Heat chili. To assemble dogs, place hot dogs in buns. Top each with chili and favorite toppings such as yellow mustard and chopped onion.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Chili dog, straight up! This is the real deal - a grilled dog topped with tasty chili, mustard and onions.
