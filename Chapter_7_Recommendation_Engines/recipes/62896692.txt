Title:
Strawberry-Rhubarb Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling: 5 large eggs
1 cup 2% milk
1/2 cup sugar
2 tablespoons vanilla extract
1 tbsp lemon juice or strong orange juice
4 cups bread,  cubed (whole wheat preferred, home-made works best)
2 1.4 cups coarsely crushed ginger snaps
2 cups fresh or frozen strawberries, diced.
1 cup rhubarb, diced
1/4 cup chopped toasted cashews, pecans or walnuts, lightly toasted.
---
For Streusel Topping:
1/3 cup flour
1/4 cup rolled oats
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 tablespoons melted butter, salted

Directions:
Grease a 9X11 baking dish.
Mix together eggs, milk and vanilla.  Add sugar and lemon/orange juice while continuing to mix until smooth.
In a separate bowl, conmine bread cubes, gingersnaps, strawberries, rhubarb, and toasted nuts by tossing together.
Pour custard mixture over top of bread mixture and let sit for 10 minutes.  Add entire mixture to the greased baking dish, and press down gently to press mixture together.  Cover and allow to sit in refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
Remove from fridge, pre-heat over to 375, and prepare streusel mixture.  Add streusel mixture to the top of the pudding, pressing lightly if necessary to press streusel together.
Cover with foil and bake until set, abut 40 to 45 minutes, then uncover and continue to bake unti pudding puffs up and begins to brown on top, usually an additional 15-20 minutes.
Remove from oven and allow to cool at least 30 minutes before eating.

Submitter: 
J. Glenn Kunzler
Description: 
This delicious bread pudding recipe combines the classical combination of strawberry & rhubarb with a hint of zip from crushed gingersnaps and a taste of warmth from ground cinnamon.
