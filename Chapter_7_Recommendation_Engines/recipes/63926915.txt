Title:
Winger Sticky Hot Wing Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 T Franks Hot Sauce
4 T water
1 1/2 c brown sugar

Directions:
Place all ingredients in saucepan and cook on medium heat until sugar dissolves.
Toss baked chicken wings, strips or nuggets in sauce and serve.

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Copycat version of Wingers Sticky Chicken.  They used on breaded chicken strips and nuggets.
