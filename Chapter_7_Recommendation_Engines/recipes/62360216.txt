Title:
Buffet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/403810.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pound bag of frozen hash browns thawed
1 small onion chopped
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 16 ounce container of sour cream
1 can cream of chicken soup
dash of pepper and season salt if desired
Topping:
1-2 cups crushed corn flakes
1 stick of butter melted

Directions:
Mix ingredients in order, then spread in a 9 by 13 baking dish evenly.
Sprinkle crushed corn flakes over the mixture, then pour melted butter over everything evenly.
Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 75 minutes on 350F.

Submitter: 
mssyann8
Description: 
I got this recipe from my aunt several years ago and make it all the time. I love this recipe because it is quick and easy to make as a side to go along with a main dish or to take to a party.
