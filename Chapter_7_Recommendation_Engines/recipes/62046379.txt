Title:
Hilerie's Party Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 2-liter bottles of ginger ale (i generally use diet, but regular works as well)
1 can frozen white grape-peach juice
1 can frozen white grape-raspberry juice
1 bag frozen raspberries or blackberries
mint sprigs for garnish

Directions:
mix all ingredients in punch bowl and serve.

Submitter: 
HILERIE
Description: 
This is a quick and easy punch.  I have served it at both family functions and at work celebrations.  In fact, this punch has been demanded for all co-workers birthday parties for over a year now.
