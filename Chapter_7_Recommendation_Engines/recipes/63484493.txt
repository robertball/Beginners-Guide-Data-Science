Title:
Supreme Tartare Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15g/½oz Capers - drained and finely chopped
30g/1oz small Gherkins - drained and finely chopped
120g/4oz Mayonnaise
½ to 1tbsp - to taste Flat leaf Parsley - finely chopped
Touch Shallots - finely diced - to taste;
Touch Dill - finely chopped - to taste.
Touch Lemon Juice - to taste;

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix the capers and gherkins into the mayonnaise;
Add the parsley and the other ingredients to taste.
Mix well to fully combine all the elements;
Taste and adjust if and/or as necessary;
Can serve immediately or cover with clingfilm and chill in fridge for 30 minutes before serving

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
The best and supreme sauce which adds a touch of class to a whole raft of fish/seafood dishes.
Once tasted, a staple forever
