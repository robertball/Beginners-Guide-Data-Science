Title:
Lindsey's Raspberry Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/859787.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup raspberry wine vinegar
1/2 cup sugar
2 tsp Dijon mustard
1/4 tsp dried oregano
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
handful of raspberries

Directions:
Mix together in blender until well combined.

Submitter: 
KramerGirls
Description: 
This is a delicious, fresh tasting dressing uses on a salad of mixed greens, feta cheese, sliced red onions, walnuts, and fresh raspberries. Delicious!
