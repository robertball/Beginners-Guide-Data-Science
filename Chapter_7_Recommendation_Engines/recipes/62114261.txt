Title:
Mom's Beefy onion gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups water
4 beef boullion cubes
1 Tbs. dried onion flakes
salt and pepper to taste
2/3 cup water
1/3 cup flour
2 tsp. bouquet sauce(for color and added flavor)

Directions:
Combine first four ingredients in saucepan and bring to boil.
Combine2/3 c. water and 1/3 c. flour in shaker jar. mix well making sure there are no lumps.
Add slowly to boiling liquid stirring constantly with whisk.
Simmer 5 min.
Add bouquet sauce.  Serve over Mom's special Microwave Meatloaf.
