Title:
Baked Pork Chops in Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/558855.jpg
Ingredients:
1 14 oz. can chicken broth
1 10 oz. can cream of mushroom
1 package flavored rice with seasoning package added
2 tbls butter
4to6 pork chops

Directions:
In a large skillet pan fry pork chops for 12 minutes.  Take a casserole dish add broth, cream soup and stir.  When pork chops are done cooking add rice and seasoning pack to the casserole dish and stir.  Then add pork chops on top of the rice mixture.  Put two pats of butter on top and bake in the oven covered for 25 minutes.  Remove foil and bake an additional 10 minutes uncovered.
