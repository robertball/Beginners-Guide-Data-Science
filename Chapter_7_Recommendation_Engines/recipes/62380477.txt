Title:
Fudgesicle Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 prepared chocolate crumb crust
1 4 1/8 oz. pkg. instant chocolate pudding mix
1 cup milk
1 pint vanilla ice cream, softened
Whipped cream or topping, if desired
M&M's, if desired

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine pudding mix and milk; beat until well blended, about one minute.  Gently fold ice cream into pudding mixture.  Pour into prepared chocolate crumb crust.  Cover; freese until firm.
Let stand at room temperature for 10-15 minutes before serving.  Top with whipped cream and M&M's if desired.

Submitter: 
Diana Norton Elwell
Description: 
This recipe has been a family favorite for over 25 years.  A perfect summertime recipe that is quick, easy and tastes just like a fudgesicle.
