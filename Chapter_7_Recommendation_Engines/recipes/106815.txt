Title:
Pear-Ginger Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981947.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups pear nectar, chilled
1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
1/3 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
4 teaspoons ginger juice

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a pitcher, stirring until SPLENDA(R) dissolves. Serve over ice.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products

A great new twist on a summertime classic!
