Title:
Refrigerator Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 quarts sliced cucumbers
3 1/2 cup sugar
1/3 cup salt
1 teaspoon alum
1 teaspoon dry mustard
3 cups vinegar
1 teaspoon tumeric
1 teaspoon celery seed

Directions:
Mix all together in large jar or plastic container.
Shake two times each day for 5 days.

Submitter: 
Theresa
Description: 
No canning - no fuss sweet pickles! Shake 'em up twice a day for five days and then enjoy!
