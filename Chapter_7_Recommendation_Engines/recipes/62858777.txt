Title:
Make-Your-Own Chicken Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp vegetable oil
1 onion, sliced
3 cup sliced mushrooms
1 sweet green pepper, sliced
2 tsp chili powder
¼ tsp each salt and pepper
1 deli roast chicken (about 1 ½ lb)
½ cup barbecue sauce
2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 cup frozen corn kernals
4 green onions, sliced
8 large flour tortillas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F.
In large skillet, heat oil over med-high heat; fry onion, mushrooms, green pepper, chili powder, salt and pepper until liquid is evaporated, about 8 minutes. Scrape into bowl.
Meanwhile, remove and discard skin from chicken. Remove meat from bones and shred into separate bowl. Add barbecue sauce and stir to coat. Set out cheese, corn and green onions in separate bowls.
Let everyone add ingredients to half of each tortilla. Fold tortilla over; press onto filling. Place on rimmed baking sheets. Bake in oven, turning once until golden and cheese is melted, 20 minutes. Cut into halves or quarters.
