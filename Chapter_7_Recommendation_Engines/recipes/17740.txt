Title:
Nutty Rice and Chile Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980103.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (7 ounce) can ORTEGA® Diced Green Chiles
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 tablespoon prepared Dijon-style mustard
3 cups cooked long-grain white rice
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1/2 cup chopped green onions
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
COMBINE chiles, vinegar, vegetable oil and mustard in small bowl. Combine rice, bell pepper, green onions and pecans in medium bowl. Add chile mixture; toss to coat well. Cover; refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Ortega®
Description: 
Rice is a staple of Mexican cuisine and is used in many ways. This recipe combines it with Ortega Diced Green Chilies to make a satisfying and nutritious salad.
