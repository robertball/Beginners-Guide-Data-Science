Title:
guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lg. Avocado
1/2 lime zest
1/2 lime juice/pulp
3/4 Jalapeno Pepper seeded diced fine
1 Roma Tomato seeded diced
1 T green onion diced
hot salsa to taste
salt/pepper to taste
1 t cilantro

Directions:
peel and mash avocado,
zest and squeeze lime.
Seed and dice tomato and Jalapeno
chop onion fine
chop cilantro fine and mix all ingredients then S&P to taste

Dale Stevens

chunky guacamole
