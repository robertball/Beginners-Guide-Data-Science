Title:
Quesadillas de Flor de Calabaza (Zucchini Blossom Quesadillas) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4555881.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tsp bacon grease
2 corn tortillas
2 slices Manchengo Cheese
6 zucchini/squash blossoms

Directions:
Melt bacon grease in a cast iron skillet
Add tortillas to skillet
Place 1 slice cheese on each tortilla
Top with 3 zucchini/squash blossoms
Fold in half and cook until lightly browned and cheese is starting to melt, 2-3 minutes
Flip and cook on other side until crispy, lightly browned and cheese has melted, about 1 minute
Note: Mozzarella cheese can be used in place of Manchengo cheese
Any type of oil or butter can be used in place of bacon grease
Any skillet can be used in place of the cast iron skillet

Submitter: 
Yoly
Description: 
These Quesadillas de Flor de Calabaza are very popular in Mexico.  The bacon grease gives the quesadillas another level o f tastiness plus they crisp up beautifully. But if bacon grease is unavailable, feel free to use lard, oil or butter.
