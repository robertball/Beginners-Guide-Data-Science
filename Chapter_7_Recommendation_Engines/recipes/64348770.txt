Title:
Slowleigh's Awesome Yet Edible Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2-cup butter (room temp.)
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/4-cup (packed) brown sugar
1 tspn vanilla
1 large egg
1 cup all purpose flour
1/2 tspn baking soda
1/2 tspn salt
Pinch of the following: nutmeg, cinnamon, and ginger
250 grams semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Beat butter, sugars, vanilla, and eggs until fluffy.
Mix in flour, baking soda , salt nutmeg, cinnamon, & ginger
Stir in chocolate chips and any variations
Using a tablespoon, drop batter 2 inches apart from on an ungreased cookie sheet
Bake at 350 degrees(F) or 180 degrees(C) for 13-15 minutes
Makes 30 small cookies or 20 real cookies

Submitter: 
Brian Slowleigh
Description: 
This recipe got me a 90% mark in Culinary arts back in high school.  It's been my kids favorite since they've been old enough to eat them, and ruined them for other cookies!
