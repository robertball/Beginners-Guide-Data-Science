Title:
Easy Seafood Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans of your favorite clam chowder (not concentrated)
1/2 cup small scallops (pre cooked)
1 cup of imitation crab meat
1 cup of muscles pre cooked(schelled)
1 cup of shrimp (pre cooked)
1 package of spinach
anything else you'd like to add

Directions:
coursely chop the spinach and put in the microwave on high for about 4 minute or until cooked. Meanwhile pour two cans of clam chower into a pot and place onmedium heat. Chop up the crab meat and add to the chowder. In a saucepan cook the scallops, shrimp and muscles (if you can get everything pre-cooked it cuts down the time) once it's cooked throw in with the clam and crab. Add the cooked spinach and let simmer for 5 minutes on medium heat while stirring occasionally. Any and all of you favorite seafood can be used for this recepie, this is also amazing on top of pasta or on it's own as a stew
