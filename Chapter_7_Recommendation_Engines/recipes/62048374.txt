Title:
Ham and Cheesey Potato Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag (28 oz) frozen diced potatoes with peppers and onion, thawed
2 cups (8oz package) shredded Cheddar and Monterey Jack cheese blend
1 can (10 3/4 z) condensed cream of celery soup
1 8oz container sour cream
3 lb fully cooked boneless ham

Directions:
Spray inside of slow cooker with cooking spray
Mix potatoes, cheese, soup and sour cream in slow cooker
Cut ham lengthwise in half and place on potato mixture
Cover and cook on low heat for 5-6 hours
Stir potatoes well before serving

Submitter:

Description: 
Super easy slow cooker recipe with few ingredients. A true comfort food.
