Title:
Peanut Butter Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 c. shortening
1/4 t. salt
1 t. vanilla
1 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. peanut butter
2 eggs, well beaten
1/2 c. brown sugar
2 c. flour
3/4 c. milk
2 1/2 t. baking powder

Directions:
Combine shortening, salt, and vanilla with beaten eggs.
Add first brown sugar gradually, creaming until light and fluffy.
Add peanut butter and milk; mix well. Add the remaining brown sugar; mix well.
Add flour and baking powder alternately with the milk. Put in loaf pan and bake at 350 degrees for about 1 hour.
