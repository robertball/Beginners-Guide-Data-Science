Title:
Sassy Lassy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oz Raspberry flavoured vodka
(or make your own infusion)
6 oz 7-Up
Several mint leaves (save a sprig for garnish)
Fresh limes

Directions:
In the bottom of the glass muddle the mint till crushed and oils are released
Put ice on top of the mint
Add the vodka
Pour on the 7-Up
Add 1/2 oz fresh lime juice
Give it a quick stir or serve with a long straw or swizzle stick
Top with a sprig of mint and a slice of lime.

Submitter: 
2RedAZ
Description: 
A group of friends and I developed this recipe years ago when we lived near the beach in Northern California.  They did not have raspberry flavoured vodka back then so we used to infuse our own.  If you do not mind the time required to make your own, the taste is truly incredible.
