Title:
Grilled Peanutbutter, Banana, and Nutella Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices White bread
Peanutbutter
1 banana sliced
Nutella

Directions:
Spread  Peanutbutter on one slice of bread, and nutella spread on other slice of bread. Put sliced bananas evenly on each side of bread. Put sandwich on greased pan on stove top. Put stove top on medium-high. Take off skillet and enjoy!
