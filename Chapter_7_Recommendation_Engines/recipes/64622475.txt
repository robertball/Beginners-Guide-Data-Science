Title:
KC's Blackened Duck Breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4457526.jpg
Ingredients:
-cleaned and skinned duck breasts cut into finger size strips
-Italian dressing
-blackened seasoning (any brand will do; I use Zatarains)
-butter
-flour tortillas

Directions:
-Marinate duck pieces in Italian dressing  at least 24 hours.
-Dunk the duck pieces into melted butter and then coat evenly with blackened seasoning.
-Cook in a hot cast iron skillet about 1 minute on each side.
-Place in tortillas along with whatever else you like in your tacos.
