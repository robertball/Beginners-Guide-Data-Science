Title:
Asian Beef Short Ribs in Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/7289027.jpg
Ingredients:
2.5 lbs bone in grassfed beef short ribs
1 c or more as needed beef bone broth or bone broth
1/4 c coconut aminos (or more)
1/4 c rice wine vinegar
1 tsp fish sauce
4-5 garlic cloves, chopped
1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
2 tsp Chinese five spice
pinch or more of red pepper flakes if desired
salt to taste, if needed

Directions:
heat cast iron skillet on medium high
season ribs with sea salt
coat pan with small amount beef tallow, ghee or other fat with high smoke point
sear short ribs on all sides in tallow (about 45 seconds on each of 4 sides)
put short ribs in slow cooker
combine all sauce ingredients with whisk
reduce heat or turn off and deglaze pan with sauce, let simmer for a few seconds, then pour over ribs
cook on low in slow cooker for 8-12 hours
cooked 12 hours -- almost too long; done at 8-10 hours; added 3/4 c water at 8 hours
