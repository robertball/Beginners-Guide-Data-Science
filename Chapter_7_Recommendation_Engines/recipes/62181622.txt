Title:
No Chill Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Margarine
1 1/2 Cups Powdered Sugar
1 Egg
1 tsp Vanilla
2 1/2 Cups Flour
1 tsp Baking Soda
1 tsp Cream of Tarter
1 tsp Salt

Directions:
Cream margarine, powdered sugar.
Add egg and vanilla, cream.
Mix together dry ingredients and add.  Mix until smooth.
Roll and cut cookies.  Bake at 375 about 6 - 8 minutes until the bottom edge is just golden brown.
Cool and frost.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
These are great cookies for frosting or eating plain.  I have made them with butter and they weren't as good.  Use margarine.  You do not need to chill the dough.
