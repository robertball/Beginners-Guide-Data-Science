Title:
Italian Cheese Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups grated parmesan sheese
3/4 cup dry plain bread crumbs
1 teaspoon dry parsley flakes
1 teaspoon garlic powder
4 beaten eggs

Directions:
In a bowl mix cheese, bread crumbs,parsley flakes,and garlic powder.Stir in beaten eggs and mix well.Wet hands and roll mixture into balls the size of a cherry tomato. Place on cookie sheet and refrigerate for 30 minutes. After that drop dumplings into simmering broth and cook for 10 minutes.
