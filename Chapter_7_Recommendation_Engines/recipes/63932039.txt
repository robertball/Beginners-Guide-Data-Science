Title:
Diabetic Berry Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup sugar substitute=1/3 cup sugar
1 envelope unflavored gelatin
1 lb fresh strawberries, hulled & diced
1 12 oz pkg frozen raspberries, thawed
nonstick cooking spray
graham cracker crust (pecancrust for low carb)

Directions:
In large saucepan, combine the sugar sub & gelatin. Add strawberries & raspberries. Cook & stir over medium high heat until gelatin is dissolved & mixture is simmering
Transfer mixture to shallow bowl. Chill for about 45 min until mixture begins to set up around edges, stirring occasionally.
Pour filling over crust. Chill another 3 hours until completely set.
