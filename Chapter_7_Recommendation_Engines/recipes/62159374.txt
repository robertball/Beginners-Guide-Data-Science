Title:
Ruthie's Garlic Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bulbs garlic
1 large Spanish onion
1 46oz can of Chicken broth
1 Tbs thyme
1 Pint heavy cream
1 loaf french bread (broken into chunks)

Directions:
Roughly chop garlic and onion.
Melt butter in a sauce pan over medium heat.
Add onions and garlic and saute until the onions are translucent.
Add chicken broth and thyme.
Bring to a boil then reduce to simmer uncovered for 15-20 minutes stirring occasionally.
Begin to add bread until a small amount of liquid remains.
Blend until smooth.
Return to pan on low heat.
Add heavy cream and salt to taste and serve.

Submitter: 
Kay Gallinger
Description: 
A robust creamy garlic soup.  This recipe was given to me by a good friend and has been a family favorite ever since.  Everyone raves over this dish.  A must have recipe if you like garlic.
