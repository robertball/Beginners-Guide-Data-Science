Title:
Cockpot Tortilla Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/819489.jpg
Ingredients:
1 boneless, skinless chicken breast (6-8 ounces)
2 15 oz cans diced peeled tomatoes with green chiles
1 28 oz can green enchilada sauce
2 cups frozen corn
2 15 oz cans black beans (drained and rinsed)
1 medium onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced or 1/2 tsp dried minced garlic
2 cups chicken broth
1 1/2 tsp ground cumin
1 1/2 tsp medium chili poweder
1 1/2 tsp mexican oregano
3 corn tortillas, roughly chopped or torn into 1" pieces
2 tbs chopped fresh cilantro (1 tbs of dried)
1-2 tbs line juice

Directions:
Place everything except the cilantro and lime juice in a slow cooker or heavy covered soup kettle. Cook on high for 4-6 hours or low for 8-10 hours in a slow cooker, or on low on the stove-top for 2 hours.
Remove the chicken and shred with two forks; return to the pot with the cilantro and lime juice. Taste and add extra seasonings if desired.
Serve with optional garnishes such as diced avocado, lime wedges, sour cream, shredded cheese and toasted tortilla strips.
