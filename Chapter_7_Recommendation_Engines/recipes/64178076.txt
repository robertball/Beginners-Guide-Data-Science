Title:
Semi's Country Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1490593.jpg
Ingredients:
3-5 	pounds pork country ribs
6 	cups water
2 	to 3 bottles beer 12oz. each
1	large yellow onion, cut into ¾ inch slices
3 	large garlic cloves, smashed
5	slices jarred jalapenos
3	teaspoons liquid from the jalapeño jar
1 	Large bottle, (64oz.) K C Masterpiece original flavor barbecue sauce, or your favorite

Directions:
In 8 quart stock pot; add ribs, onion and garlic, jalapenos,  beer, and just enough water just to cover ribs.  Turn burner on medium high; bring to a boil.  Shut off heat and move pot to a cool burner.  Let set for 3 to 5 minutes.
Heat oven to 325 F.  With slotted spoon remove ribs, onion, garlic, and jalapenos from stock pot to 13x9x2 inch baking dish or foil pan.  Making sure to let as much water as possible drain off of ribs and veggies.  Cover with KC Masterpiece barbecue sauce, (or your favorite).  Cover pan with foil and bake in 325 F.  Oven for 1 to 1 ½ hours or until ribs are very tender.
