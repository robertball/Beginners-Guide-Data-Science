Title:
Easy Cheesy Fondue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans cheddar cheese soup
8 oz. shredded sharp cheddar cheese
8 oz. shredded swiss cheese
1/3 can beer
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp mustard

Directions:
Heat soup, beer, Worcestershire sauce and mustard, either on the stove or in the fondue pot itself, till bubbly.  Gradually add shredded cheese and stir until melted and hot. Transfer to fondue pot if necessary.

Submitter: 
GINGERS98
Description: 
My aunt gave me this recipe, it is so easy, no fancy cheeses or ingredients, but still delicious.  I spray bread cubes with olive oil and a little garlic salt then toast in the oven for dipping.  You can also dip mini hotdogs, ham, broccoli  whatever you like!
