Title:
Cheesy Ham Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 bacon strips, diced
1 large onion, chopped
1 cup diced carrots
3 T flour
3 cups milk
1 1/2 cups water
2 1/2 cups cubed potatoes
1 15 oz. can whole kernel corn
2 t chicken bouillon granules
pepper to taste
3 cups chredded cheddar cheese
2 cups cubed cooked ham

Directions:
1. In large pot, cook bacon over medium heat until crisp.  Use a slotted spoon and remove to paper towel to drain.
2. In the drippings, saute onion and carrots until tender.
3. Stir in the flour until blended.  Gradually add milk and water.  Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.
4. Add the potatoes, corn, bouillon and pepper.  Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 20 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
5. Add cheese and ham; heat until cheese is melted.  Stir in bacon.
