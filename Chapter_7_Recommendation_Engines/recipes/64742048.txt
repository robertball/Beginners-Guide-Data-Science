Title:
Acorn Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6439833.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup Acorn Flour
1 Cup Old Fashioned Oats
3 Tablespoons Flax Seeds
3 Tablespoons Chia Seeds
1 Large Onion, minced
1 Carrot, minced
1 Stalk of Celery, minced
2 Tablespoons Garlic, minced
1 teaspoon salt
1 Cup Water
2 Eggs
2 Tablespoons Butter
Oil For Frying

Directions:
Melt the 2 tablespoons of butter in a large frying pan.  Sauté the onions until translucent add the garlic for several minutes adding the minced celery, carrots and salt sautéing 5 minutes more.
Place the sautéed mixture in a mixing bowl, add the acorn flour, oats, flax seeds and chia seeds and combine throughly.
Add the water and mix well again.
Add the eggs mixing well, cover and allow to rest for 30 minutes.
Make into patties and fry in chosen oil for several minutes until browned on each side.
Serve as desired.

Submitter: 
Shanda
Description: 
Serve delicious acorn burgers by themselves or on a bun topped with your favorite condiments.
