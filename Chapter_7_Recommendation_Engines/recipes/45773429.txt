Title:
Puerto Rician Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/303250.jpg
Ingredients:
1 TBLS minced garlic
1 medium onion diced
1 Cup rice
1/2 cup bell peppers diced
1can of diced tomatoes, juice seperated
2 packages Sazo"n Goya
2 Tbls Goya Adobo
15.5 oz can pinto beans
14.0 oz can corn

Directions:
In a large stock pot oil the bottom of pan with olive oil. Add you onions, garlic and green peppers till you smell the garlic pop, once the garlic pops and add the tomatoes and corn, and beans, 1 cup of the rice salt and pepper and liberally add 2 packages SaGoya Adobo. (You will find this around the Mexican isle) Bring contents of the pan to a boil then simmer till Rice is done and juice starts to reduce. At this time you are welcome to add chicken, hamburger or pork and remain cooking till all the juice has evaporated.
