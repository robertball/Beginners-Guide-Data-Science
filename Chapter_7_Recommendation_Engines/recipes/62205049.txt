Title:
Thai beef appitizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb beef
1/2 c coconut milk
1/3 c shallots
2 T brown sugar
2 T soy sauce
1 T garlic
1 t cumin
1 t corriander
PEANUT SAUCE
1 c coconut milk
1/2 c peanutbutter
4 t brown sugar
1 T fish sauce
1 T soy
1 T curry paste
1/2 t curry powder
1/2 c hot water
2 t lime juice

Directions:
Cut beef into strips and marinade for 24 hours.
Mix peanut sauce for 15-20 min.
Grill beef and swerve together.
