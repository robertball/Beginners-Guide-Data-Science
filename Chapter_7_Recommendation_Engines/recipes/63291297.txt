Title:
San Antonio  Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cooked Rice (white or brown)
1 cup frozen Corn Kernels, thawed
1 (15 oz.) can Black Beans, rinsed and drained
1 1/2 cups Salsa
1 teaspoon Ground Cumin
1 teaspoon Garlic powder
1 1/2 cups Monterey Jack Cheese, grated

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In a large bowl, combine cooked rice, corn, and black beans, and add salsa, cumin, garlic powder, and half the cheese (3/4 cup).
Spoon mixture into a 9x13x2 inch; baking dish coated with cooking spray.
Bake, covered, about 25 minutes.
Remove cover and sprinkle with remaining half of cheese; bake 15 more minutes.
Cool 10 minutes and serve.

Submitter: 
momof2angels
Description: 
It is simple, quick, inexpensive and my family loves it. It is a great vegetarian side dish but if you want it as a main dish you can add chicken to it.
