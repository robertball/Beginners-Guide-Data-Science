Title:
Peach Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (21 ounce) cans peach pie filling
2 cups rolled oats or oatmeal
1/2 cup butter, melted
2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.
Grease 9x13x2 inch baking dish.
Spread pie filing in greased 9x13x2 inch baking dish.
Combine oats/oatmeal, melted butter, Cinnamon, flour and brown sugar.  Stir until crumbly.
Sprinkle evenly over pie filing.
Bake @ 350 for 30 minutes, or until bubbly & golden.
