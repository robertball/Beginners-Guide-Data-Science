Title:
Loaded Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/448898.jpg
Ingredients:
½ lb Bacon (8 slices)
¼ c Butter or Margarine
1 md Onion (diced)
½ c Celery (minced)
3 cloves Garlic (minced)
4 lb Potatoes or 5 lg (¾" cubed)
¼ c Chicken Bouillon
4 c Milk
4 c Water
1 pkg Cream Cheese (softened)
========
GARNISH
========
Bacon
Green Onions
Shredded Cheese

Directions:
CUT bacon into little pieces and fry until crisp.
SET aside bacon on paper towels to drain.
REMOVE most of the bacon drippings.
ADD butter, onions and celery then cook until translucent.
ADD garlic and cook for about 2 more minutes.
BEAT softened cream cheese in a bowl.
ADD 2 cups of milk and beat again.
MIX cream cheese mixture into stockpot.
QUICKLY add water, remaining milk and chicken bouillon and bring to a simmer.
ADD potatoes and simmer on low until tender.
MASH a few potatoes to thicken soup.
GARNISH with bacon, green onions and shredded cheese.
