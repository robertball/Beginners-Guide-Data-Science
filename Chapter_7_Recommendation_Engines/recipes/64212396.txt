Title:
American Chop Suey II - Modified for my family's taste Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package uncooked elbow macaroni
1 pound lean ground beef
1 medium to largeo onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, crushed
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed tomato soup plus 1/2 can water
or equivalent amount marinara sauce - omit oregano and basil
1 t pepper
1/2 t dried oregano
1/2 t dried basil

Directions:
In a large skillet, caramelize chopped onion in 1 T veg oil.  May take 10 - 15 minutes, stirring frequently.  A great deal of flavor will develop.
When onions are almost finished, clear a spot in the skillet and saute the garlic in 1/4 t oil for 1 - 2 minutes.  Combine thoroughly with the onions.
Add the ground beef to the the onion/garlic and cook until meat is browned and crumbly. Drain excess grease and leave the meat and onion/garlic mixture in the skillet. Pour the two cans of tomato soup and 1/2 can of water into the skillet.  Stir to combine.
Add pepper, oregano, and basil.  Combine well.
Cook macaroni according to package directions.
When noodles are done, drain thoroughly and add noodles to the skillet.  Combine well serve.
