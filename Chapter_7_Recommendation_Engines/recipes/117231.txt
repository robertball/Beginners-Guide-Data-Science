Title:
Herbed Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985655.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
4 1/2 teaspoons butter, melted
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon dried savory
1/8 teaspoon dried oregano
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Place green beans in a microwave-safe dish. Combine the remaining ingredients; pour over beans and toss to coat evenly. Cover and microwave on high for 6-8 minutes or until beans are tender.

Submitter: 
Lucinda  Walker
Description: 
These beans take just moments to prepare in the microwave, making them perfect after a busy day, when you're low on energy and time. -Lucinda Walker of Somerset, Pennsylvania
