Title:
Fried Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/770264.jpg
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons butter (prefer unsalted)
1 Onion, chopped
2 1/2 pounds cabbage, chopped (i used green in photo, any cabbage will work)
1 tablespoon chicken bouillon granules or crushed bullion cube (3 cubes, crushed)
1/2 teaspoon poultry seasoning, or more to taste
salt to taste

Directions:
In a large sauce pan over medium heat, melt butter and saute onions until translucent and soft, but not brown.
Add cabbage all at once, and sprinkle with bouillon and poultry seasoning
Toss to combine, lower heat to medium-low and cover pan.
Cook, covered, stirring every 10 minutes, until cabbage reaches desired level of doneness.
Salt if desired
