Title:
Vegetarian Mexican Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium onion, minced
Optional: 4 medium cloves garlic, chopped
2 TBS red chili powder
3 cups + 1 TBS vegetable broth
(create a broth based onion, pepper, carrot, or
whatever is on hand)
1 small to medium green bell pepper, diced
1 small zucchini, diced
1 cup finely chopped collard greens
1 15 oz can diced tomatoes
1 15 oz can black beans, rinsed
1 cup frozen yellow corn (or can of corn)
1 4 oz can diced green chili
1 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp ground cumin
1/4 cup chopped pumpkin seeds
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat a half cup of broth, add all vegetables to it, cover, and "saut√©" for 4 or 5 minutes in the broth.
Add red chili powder and mix in well.
Continue cooking for another 5 minutes, then add beans, corn, green chili, oregano, and cumin.
Bring to a boil on high heat. Once it begins to boil, reduce heat to medium-low and simmer uncovered for 15 minutes.
Add chopped cilantro, pumpkin seeds
Salt and pepper to taste.
