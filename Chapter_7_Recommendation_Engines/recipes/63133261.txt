Title:
Simply Elegant Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
olive oil for frying
1 1b Round Steak (cubed)
2 onions sliced
2 cans(10.5 oz) cream of mushroom soup
1 cup cooking sherry
1 small jar of sliced mushrooms
1 tsp garlic powder
pepper

Directions:
Cut steak in cubes, removing any excess fat.
In a large skillet heat oil over medium high heat, add steak cubes and sliced onions. Fry just until brown. About 5-8 minutes.
In a separate bowl, mix soup, and cooking sherry, Add mushrooms, garlic powder and pepper. Pour over steak mixture in skillet. Reduce heat to simmer, cover and cook for 1 1/2 hours stirring occasionally. Serve over a bed of rice for a rich tasty meal.

Description: 
A simple way to prepare a tender steak meal that your family is sure to enjoy.
