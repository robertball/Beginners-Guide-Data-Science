Title:
Baked Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 small to Medium sized tomatoes, halved crosswise
1 TBS. EVOO
Salt & pepper
1/3 C. Parmesan cheese
2 TBS. minced lemon zest
1 TBS. minced fresh parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Arrange tomatoes in a baking dish, cut-side up.  Drizzle oil on tomatoes and season with salt and Pepper. Bake for 10 Minutes
Combine Parmesan, zest, and parsley in a small bowl. Remove tomatoes from oven. Sprinkle Parmesan mixture over tomatoes and bake 10 more minutes.
