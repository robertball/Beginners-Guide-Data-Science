Title:
Spicy Sausage Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1030786.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbsp canola oil
1 lb turkey sausage, sliced
1 tbsp onion powder
t tsp garlic powder
2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
1 (10oz) can Rotel
1/2 cup Greek yogurt
8 oz campanelle pasta (or penne)
1/2 tsp pepper
1 cup Monterey Jack cheese, shredded

Directions:
Heat olive oil in skillet over medium heat.
Add sausage, onion powder, garlic powder, both, Rotel, yogurt, pasta, and pepper.
Stir and bring to boil.
Reduce heat to low and cover skillet.
Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
Remove skillet from heat and stir in half the cheese.
Top with remainging cheese.
