Title:
Eastern Style Mulligatawny Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup split peas
4 cups water
3/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
1/2 teaspoon chile powder
1 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons tomato paste
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Directions:
1. Soak split peas for 30 minutes or so
2. Drain
3. Place split peas in a stock pot and add 4 cups water, turmeric powder, chile powder, and salt
4. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer until split peas are soft. (more water may be added if mixture becomes too thick)
5. Beat cooked split peas with a hand mixer
6. Add tomato paste and lemon juice
7. Cook over medium heat, again, until mixture thickens
8. Fry garlic and cumin seeds in oil in a saucepan until garlic browns
9. Add garlic mixture to soup and stir
10. Serve

Submitter: 
Pantaloons
Description: 
This is the recipe from Shalimar Restaurant in Albany, NY.  It is amazing.  Enjoy this soup with some vegetable samosas and cheese naan bread.
