Title:
Cheddar Cheese Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744645.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup cornmeal
1/4 cup ground flaxseed
1 TBS sugar
2 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups milk (whole)
1 TBS olive oil
6 oz cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
Combine flour, cornmeal, flax, sugar, baking powder, and salt
In a separate bowl - whisk eggs, milk, and oil; add to flour mixture; mix in cheese
Pour batter into a greased 11-in x 7-in baking pan; bake 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean
