Title:
Orpha's noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4930272.jpg
Ingredients:
1 beaten egg
2 tablespoons milk
1/2 teaspoon salt
about 1 cup all-purpose flour

Directions:
Combine egg, milk, and salt.
Add enough flour to make a stiff dough.
Flatten, turn over, then roll very thin on a floured surface.
Slice into 1/4" wide strips with a pizza cutter.
Spread out to dry. 2 hours or overnight.
If desired, store in container until needed.
Drop into boiling soup or salted water.
Cook uncovered until tender - about 30 minutes.
