Title:
Velveeta Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 Oz. Velveeta
1 C.  Butter
6 Oz unsweetened chocolate
2 Tablespoons light corn syrup
2 pounds powdered sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
Nuts optional

Directions:
Melt first 4 ingredients in microwave safe bowl for 2 minutes
Stir
Put  back in microwave for another minute.
Stir
Stir in vanilla
Add chocolate mixture to powdered sugar in a couple batches
Beat well
Add nuts if desired
Pour in buttered 9x13 pan and spread
Refrigerate several hours
