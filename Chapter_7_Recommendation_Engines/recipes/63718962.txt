Title:
southwestern cicken layered salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups romaine lettuce
2 cups shredded rotisserie chicken (2-2 1/2-lbs)
1 can (11 oz)southwestern style corn, drained, liquid reserved
2 tbsp. Old el Paso taco seasoning mix
1/2cup shredded Mexican cheese
1/2 cup Ranch or French dressing
1 cup corn (1 1/2 oz.)
pace salsa if desired

Directions:
1) Arrange lettuce on large platter. In 10-inch nonstick skillet, place chicken and reserved liquid from corn. Sprinkle wit Old El Paso taco seasoning mix. Cook over medium heat 2to 3 minutes, stirring constantly, until blended through and thoroughly heated.
2) Arrange chicken mixture over lettuce. Sprinkle with cheese and southwestern style corn. Drizzle with salad dressing; top with corn chips. Garnish with Pace salsa if desired.
