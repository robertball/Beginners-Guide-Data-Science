Title:
The Best Granola You'll Ever Eat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 C. organic thick rolled oats
2 C. or 1 lb of sliced almonds (or other nuts if preferred)
1 C. Sesame Seeds - hulled or unhulled, whatever is preferred
1 C. Flax sees golden or brown
2 C. Sunflower Seeds - raw, not salted or cooked
2 C. Dried coconut flakes
2 C. Honey
12 ounces organic virgin Coconut oil
2 to 6 oz. of organic salted butter
2 T. Vanilla Essence (not artificial!)
2 T. Cinnamon
1-2 C. Dried Fruit (optional)

Directions:
Add Oats, almonds, sesame seeds, flax, sunflower seeds, and coconut flakes in large bowl and mix well.
In a saucepan heat up honey, coconut oil, butter, vanilla, and cinnamon
Make a hole in the middle of the dry ingredients and pour the heated mixture in the hole.  Stir well to coat.
Fill making trays full of granola and bake for 40 mins at 325.
Take out and stir both trays, getting the browner stuff mixed with the lighter stuff on the edges and bottoms
Bake another 20 minutes and repeat the stir/swap.
Bake another 10 minutes and repeat stir/swap.
Bake an additional 10 minutes if needed.
Total baking time should be approx 60 mins-1.5 hours
Take out and put it in the mixing bowl to cool - or leave on the trays
Use a large knife to cut it up and break up the chunks
Add dried fruit before storing in a sealed container
Freeze if you have made too much.  :)
