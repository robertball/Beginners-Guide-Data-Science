Title:
Salsa Chicken Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups croutons w/herb seasoning
12 oz. jar of salsa
1 jalapeno pepper, minced
1 cup onion, diced small
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 lb. ground chicken
1/2 lb. ground pork
1 egg
1 1/2 tablespoon packaged taco seasoning mix
1 tablespoon worchestershire sauce
1 cup pepper jack cheese, finely grated
1/4 cup cilantro, minced
2 tablespoons barbeque sauce
2 tablespoons brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a separate bowl, mix croutons & jar of salsa and let soak for 15 minutes, or until croutons are soft.
Heat olive oil in saute pan and cook jalapenos, garlic & onions until soft.
In a large bowl combine the chicken, pork, egg, worchestershire sauce, taco seasoning, cheese, and cilantro. Mix together, then add in crouton mixture (crush any solid pieces of croutons) - mix throughly.
Add sauteed oniion mixture. Mix well and pour mixture into a loaf pan.
Mix barbeque sauce & brown sugar in a separate bowl, and brush mixture on top of meatloaf.
Tent meatloaf with aluminum foil & bake in preheated oven for 1 hour (remove foil in last 10 minutes of cooking).
Remove from oven, cover with foil and let it rest for an additional 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
HockeyMom
Description: 
Mexican flavor inspired meatloaf. Chicken makes this lower in fat then regular meatloaf, and adding pork keeps the meatloaf from tasing dry.
