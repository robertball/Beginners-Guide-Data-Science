Title:
Mint Chocolate Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups all purpose flour
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
6.0 oz bag of mint chocolate chips
5.5 oz milk  chocolate chips
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp peppermint extract
1 tsp salt
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
8 tbs butter,softened
8tbs margarine,softened
2 eggs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F
Start by creaming together buter, margarine, sugar, peppermint extract and brown sugar. Once creamed, add in eggs one at a time.  Beat well.  Once eggs are thouroughly combined slowly begin to add in dry mixture.  Beat till fluffy.  Fold in Mint chocolate chips. Place on cookie sheet by heaping tablespoons. Bake for 10-12 minutes on top rack of oven.  Makes 35 cookies
