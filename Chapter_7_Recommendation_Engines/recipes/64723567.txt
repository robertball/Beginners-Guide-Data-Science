Title:
Pear Cranberry Upside Down Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6064136.jpg
Ingredients:
5 Pears, peeled, cored and scliced
6 ounces Fresh Cranberries
4 C Sugar
1 Stick Butter
1 t Cinnamon
1/2 t Ground Cloves
1/2 t Nutmeg
1 t Salt
1 1/2 t Baking Soda
1 T Vanilla
1 Cup Chopped Walnuts
3 Cups All Purpose Flour
4 Eggs
2/3 C Canola Oil
1 C Vanilla Soy Milk (Any milk will be fine)
Cooking Spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees Fahrenheit.
With a 12 inch cake pan cut a wax paper cake pan liner to fit.
Spray inside cake pan with cooking spray.
Place wax paper liner in pan.
Melt 1 stick butter in large skillet when melted add 2 Cups Sugar.
Peel, core and slice pears.
Add pear slices to bubbling butter and sugar mixture and on low heat cook for 5 minuets.
Turn heat off and add 6 ounces fresh cranberries.
Add eggs, oil and milk.
Combine ingredients using hand mixer until batter is smooth.
Pour pear, cranberry mixture into wax paper lined cake pan.
Using spatula evenly spread cake mixture on top of fruit.
Bake for 70 minuets at 325 degrees Fahrenheit.
Use toothpick to check that cake is done.
Cool for 10 minuets and then place platter on top of cake pan and carefully flip cake pan and platter over allowing cake to settle on platter then lift cake pan off.
Serve with vanilla ice cream
