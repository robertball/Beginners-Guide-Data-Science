Ingredients:
2 - 28oz cans of whole peeled tomatoes (drained & sliced)
2-3 lbs Hungarian Wax Peppers, cored and sliced (substitute w/banana peppers if necessary)
1 large onion , diced
1/2lb Hungarian slab bacon , diced (substitute w/reg smoked bacon if necessary)
2 tbsp Hungarian Sweet Paprika
1 tbsp Hungarian Hot Paprika
1lb Hungarian smoked sausage, sliced
1 tsp olive oil for cooking
1 tsp butter for cooking
Salt and pepper to taste
White rice

Directions:
1. In an enamel dutch oven or stainless steel soup pot, heat olive oil and butter over medium heat. Add onions and saute until soft, add bacon until fat renders but do not brown.
2. Add tomatoes, saute until warm. Add peppers, then mix in sweet and hot paprika. Add smoked sausage and bring to boil while stirring slowly, then lower heat and simmer for 60 minutes or until peppers are tender.
3. Serve over white rice.

Submitter: 
Dan Csoke
Description: 
Thick vegetable stew which features yellow pointed peppers, tomato, onion, salt, and ground sweet and hot paprika as a base recipe. The onions and peppers are usually sauteed in bacon fat. Garlic can also be a traditional ingredient.
