Title:
caprese pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/813811.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Roma tomatoes
2 garlic cloves
1/2 cup fresh basil
1 package shredded mozzarella cheese
2 tbsp olive oil
1 pre-made pizza crust

Directions:
Brush crust with olive oil.
slice tomatoes and arrange on pizza
thinly slice garlic and sprinkle over tomatoes
spread basil over pizza
cover in desired amount of cheese
bake for 10-12 mins at 350
