Title:
Creamed Corn - Kitchen Hack Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can creamed corn (400 ml/14 oz)
1 cup fresh or frozen corn
1 tbsp butter or margarine

Directions:
In a small pot combine all the ingredients. Heat over medium high heat until hot and bubbly, stirring often.

Submitter: 
CarolynCooks
Description: 
I developed this easy recipe especially for my family who dislikes creamed corn.  Now they ask for this dish often.
