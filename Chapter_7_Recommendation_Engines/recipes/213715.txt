Title:
Turkey Club Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989118.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Arnold® Multi-Grain Sandwich Thins® roll
3 ounces smoked turkey, thinly sliced
1 slice cooked turkey bacon, cut in half
1 slice reduced-fat Cheddar cheese
1 slice reduced-fat Swiss cheese
1 teaspoon honey mustard
1 slice red onion
1 slice tomato
Lettuce leaf

Directions:
Toast the Sandwich Thins(R) roll until lightly browned. Spread one side of the Sandwich Thins(R) roll with honey mustard. Layer on cheddar, turkey, bacon and Swiss, lettuce, onion and tomato and the other half of the Sandwich Thins(R) roll.

Submitter: 
ARNOLD® / OROWEAT® Sandwich Thins®
Description: 
A wholesome take on the turkey club with our Multi-Grain Sandwich Thins® rolls.
