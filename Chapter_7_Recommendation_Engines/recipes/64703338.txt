Title:
Spinach stuffed chicken breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6660755.jpg
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts
1/2 tbls olive oil
1/2 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp salt (divided)
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp onion powder
3oz low-fat cream cheese
1/4 c Parmesan cheese
1 tbls mayonnaise
3/4 c spinach, chopped
1/2 tsp garlic, minced
1/4 tsp red pepper

Directions:
Drizzle chicken with olive oil, both sides
Mix together  paprika, 1/2 salt, garlic and onion powder, sprinkle over chicken, both sides.
Use sharp knife to cut a pocket into the side of each chicken breast.
Mix together cream cheese, mayo, spinach and parm cheese, mix well.
Spoon mixture into chicken evenly
Bake @ 400  for 30 minutes.
