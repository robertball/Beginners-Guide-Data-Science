Title:
Strawberry Sunrise Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4520628.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup strawberries, hulled and chopped
1/2 orange, peeled and chopped
1 cup Almond Breeze Cashewmilk Almondmilk Vanilla

Directions:
Place all ingredients into blender and blend on high until smooth.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
Strawberry and oranges blend up to make a simple but flavorful fruit almondmilk smoothie.
