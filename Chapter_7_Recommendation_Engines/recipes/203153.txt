Title:
California Wrappin' Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989209.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons Hellmann’s® or Best Foods® Canola Cholesterol Free Mayonnaise
4 (6 inch) whole wheat flour tortillas
12 ounces boneless, skinless chicken breasts, grilled and sliced
1 medium avocado, peeled and sliced
1 red bell pepper, sliced
1/4 cup sliced red onion
2 cups mixed salad greens

Directions:
Spread It: Spread Hellmann's(R) or Best Foods(R) Canola Cholesterol Free Mayonnaise on tortillas.
Stuff It: Layer chicken, avocado, red pepper, red onion and salad greens down center of each tortilla.
Wrap It: Roll and fold the filled tortilla.

Submitter: 
Hellmann's or Best Foods
Description: 
A great way to serve leftover grilled chicken. . .makes a great lunch or portable dinner.
