Title:
Heidelbeere Kucken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886802.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 cup margarine
4 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups flour
1 (16 ounce) can blueberry pie filling

Directions:
Lightly grease a baking sheet.
Cream the sugar and margarine together in a mixing bowl.
Stir in the eggs, one at a time, beating thoroughly after each.
Stir in the vanilla and flour, and mix well.
Spread the batter over the prepared baking sheet to form a rectangle. Use a knife to mark off 12 squares. Use a spoon to make a small well in each square.
Place  1 tablespoon pie filling in each well.
Bake at 350ยบ until top is light brown, about 25 minutes.
Cool on pan, and cut into 12 squares.
