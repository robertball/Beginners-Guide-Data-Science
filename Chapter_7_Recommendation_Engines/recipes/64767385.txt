Title:
Liver Dumpling   "Leber Knoedel" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz Liver ; Your choice of twice ground, Beef or Pork Liver
10 oz Finely minced fried onion.
4 oz. Bread Crumbs, Dry
1 oz finely chopped Parsley
1/2 tsp.  ea. Marjoram, Nutmeg, Cloves
2 oz. Butter for Frying onions.
1 oz. fine Grits
Salt & Pepper.

Directions:
Finely mince the onions and Brown in the Butter.  While the onions is
cooling put the Liver through a grinder twice.  Chop the Parsley fine.  Mix all the
ingredients  together to a homogeneous Mass and set in Fridge, for 1/2 hour.
Have ready a Few Quarts of Broth or Salted water to Cook the "Knoedels" in.
When ready;  form round dumplings; anywhere from Golf-Ball size to Hardball; your
choice.  ( for soup;  about the size of a Quails egg.).  Do not Boil !! Steep at Boiling Point..

Submitter: 
Alois
Description: 
Delicious dumpling of choice Liver made with Onion, Bread crumbs and Parsley.
