Title:
Matts southern collard greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
collard greens
1 onion
3-4 slices of maple cured bacon
2-4 cloves of garlic
1 C red wine (last used shiraz)
1-2 C chicken stock
salt and pepper

Directions:
in a large pot, add chopped up bacon and allow it to render
once rendered, remove bacon to paper towel
add onion and garlic to pot, cook until translucent
add wine and allow alcohol to cook off (2-4 min)
add chopped collard greens, chicken stock
cook until greens become tender (30-60 min)
