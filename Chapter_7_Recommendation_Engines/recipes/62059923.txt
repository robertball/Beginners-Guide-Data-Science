Title:
Marshmallow Whip Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Jar Marshmallow Whip
1 tub WHIPPED Creme Cheese (plain or flavored, depending on your fruit selection)
Drops of food coloring are optional, but festive for any holiday.

Directions:
Beat both ingredients together on med-high speed until fluffy and blended. Serve immediately with fresh fruit. OR, make ahead and refrigate until ready to use. Add food coloring if you want a splash of color.
DIP TIPS:
Serves well with:
Cantalope, Honeydew, Strawberries, Grapes, apples, bananas, mangos, oranges and many more...

Description: 
A very easy fruit dip that's sure to be a hit!
