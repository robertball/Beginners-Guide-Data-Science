Title:
honey BBQ coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
one bag of shredded cabbage with carrots
1 & 1/2 cups reduced calorie mayonnaise
1/2 cup of honey bbq sauce
1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
1/4 cup white sugar
one small onion,minced (finely)
1/2 cup finely diced celery
1 Tablespoon garlic powder
season salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine shredded cabbage mix,onion and celery.
in a separate bowl, add the mayo, honey BBq sauce, vinegar, sugar, , garlic powder, season salt and pepper. blend well.
Pour dressing mixture over cabbage mixture and toss well. Chill and serve.

Submitter: 
Shelbi Awabdy
Description: 
this is a sweet and tangy coleslaw that pairs perfectly with bbq chicken...my family always requests this all year round, not just for bbq! would be great with bbq pork sandwiches as well!
