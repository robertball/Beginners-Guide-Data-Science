Title:
Stuffed Zucchini Boats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium or 2 large zucchini
1/2 c. chopped onion
1 lb. ground beef
1 c. dry bread crumbs
1 T. parsley
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. Italian seasoning
1/8 tsp. pepper
1 jar spaghetti sauce
1 beaten egg
shredded mozzarella

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Slice zucchini lengthwise and scoop out centers (save for filling).  Arrange zucchini boats in baking dish.  Chop reserved zucchini insides and combine with beef and onion in a saute pan.  Brown and drain.  Return to pan and add bread crumbs, seasonings, 1 T. spaghetti sauce, egg, and shredded mozzarella.  Spoon filling into boats.  Pour sauce over boats until covered.  Bake at 350 for 45 min or until zucchini is tender.  Serve with spaghetti and remaining sauce.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
Stuffed zucchini boats are an excellent way to use those giant zucchini that got away from you in the garden.  The peel can be removed when serving if the zucchini are too large and tough.
