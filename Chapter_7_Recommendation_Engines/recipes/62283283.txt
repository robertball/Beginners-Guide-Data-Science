Title:
Best Ever Coconut Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/352803.jpg
Ingredients:
1 stick of butter or margarine  (8 tbsp)
1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
1 tsp  finely grated lemon peel
1 tbsp fresh lime juice
2 tsp all purpose flour
1 tbsp dark brown sugar
2 tsp white sugar
2 tsp dark corn syrup
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp crushed red pepper
1/3 cup pineapple juice
1/4 cup chopped pineapple pieces
1/4 cup coconut flakes

Directions:
Over medium heat, melt butter or margarine in small sauce pan, add lemon and lime juice, add white sugar and lemon peel. Constantly whisking, add in crushed red pepper flakes and salt.
Add flour, bring to a boil while whisking constantly. Then add dark corn syrup, brown sugar, coconut flakes, pineapple juice and pineapple pieces. Whisk another 3 minutes while boiling, turn to low, stirring occasionally another 10 minutes.
Serve warm.

Description: 
I don't like traditional cocktail sauces, but wanted something to dip coconut shrimp in and I totally made this one up out of things I just had on hand and it came out Great! Hope you like it.
