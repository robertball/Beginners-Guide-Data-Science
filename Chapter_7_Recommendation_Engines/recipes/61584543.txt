Title:
Homemade Vanilla Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (750 milliliter) bottle vodka, or bourbon, or rum
6-8 washed and split vanilla beans

Directions:
Wash and split the beans and then submerge vanilla beans in alcohol of choice and store in a cool, dark place for 30-90 days, shaking daily if possible.

Submitter: 
T.Mahsem
Description: 
Easy to make and much cheaper.  As you use it, top it off with more vodka.Modified from: Homemade Vanilla
