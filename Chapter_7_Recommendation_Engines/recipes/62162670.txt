Title:
Thai Green Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 onion, cut into wedges
2 tablespoons fish sauce
1 pound chicken tenderloins, cut into bite-sized pieces
1-3 tablespoons green curry paste, depending on desired spiciness
1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
1 eggplant, 1-inch cubes
1 can light coconut milk
2/3-1 carton chicken broth
1 can bamboo shoots
2 teaspoons lime juice
1 cup rice, such as Jasmine
Basil leaves for serving

Directions:
Cook the rice according to package directions.
Saute first six ingredients.
Add the chicken, curry, and basil. Stir occasionally.
After chicken is partially cooked, add eggplant. Cover and cook down. Lower the heat so as not to singe the spices.
Add coconut milk, broth, and bamboo. Cook until hot and eggplant is tender.
Before serving stir in lime juice. Serve over cooked rice with basil leaves if desired.
