Title:
Green & Fruit smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/849073.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen blueberrys, 1 cup frozen tropical fruit, 2 cups baby spinich, 2 cups kale,10 baby carrots,1 frozen banana 1 cup red grapes, 1 slice of fresh ginger, 6 oz of water, 1 tsp ground cinnamon, 2 tsp chia seeds

Directions:
add water to blender, then all ingredents,turn blender on high blend for approx 60 seconds. i use my Vitamixer to blend mine..
