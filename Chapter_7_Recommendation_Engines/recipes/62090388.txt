Title:
Vegetables in Coconut Curry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoon butter
2 yellow onions, cut into wedges
1 1/2 teaspoon flour
2 cups vegetable stock
1/2 cup tomato paste
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon gound coriander
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1 cup broccoli, cut into florets
1 cup cauliflower, cut into florets
1 cup carrots, sliced
1 cup fresh green beans
1 cup potatos, peeled and cut into 1/4 inch slices
1 cup frozen peas

Directions:
In a large saucepan, heat the ghee or butter. Add the onions and fry for 5 minutes or until brown.
Sprinkle the flour over the onions mix well and lower heat to low.
Slowly add vegetable stock, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
Mix in the tomato paste, chilli, coriander and turmeric and cook over medium heat for 2 minutes.
Add the vegetables, except peas and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 20-25 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
Lower the heat and add the peas and coconut milk. Cook, stirring constantly, for a further 5 minutes then serve immediately
