Title:
Kelly's Banana Ice Cream Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Med banana, peeled and chopped
5 scoops vanilla ice cream
1/4 cup milk
2 egg white
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
1/2 tsp. banana extract

Directions:
In a blender, whip milk and eggs whites for a few seconds.  Add in remaining ingredience: bananas, ice cream, vanilla extract and banana extract. Mix until the ice cream is completely blended.
