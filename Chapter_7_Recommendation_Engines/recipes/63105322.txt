Title:
White chicken chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32oz chicken broth
3cans of white beans (undrained)
1can corn
5cups cooked cubed chicken
16oz jar salsa (medium)
8oz pepper jack cheese grated
2tsp ground cumin
1tbs minced garlic
2tsp chili powder
black pepper to taste

Directions:
Add all ingredients together and cook on high until cheese melts.

Delicious and simple alternative to traditional chili.
