Title:
Tofu with Indian spices Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (14-oz) packages firm tofu, drained and patted dry
1/4 c. oil
2 tsp. cumin seeds
1 Tsp. black mustard seeds
2 fresh hot green chiles, diced
2 c. chopped red onion
salt
1 green bell pepper, diced
2 tomatoes, chopped
1 c. chopped cilantro

Directions:
Put the tofu in a bowl and mash it in your hand to break it up into smalllish clumps (like scrambled eggs).
Heat oil over med-high heat.  Cook seeds until cumin turns golden brown and/or mustard seeds crackle, 1 to 2 minutes.  (Cover the pan when the mustard seeds begin to pop.)
Add chiles and cook 30 seconds.  Add onion and 1 tsp. salt and cook, stirring till onion begins to brown, about 6 to 7 minute.Add bell pepper and cook, stirring, till it softens, about 2 minutes.  Add tofu and cook to evaporate the liquid.  Add tomatoes and cook 2 minutes.  Add cilantro and serve.
