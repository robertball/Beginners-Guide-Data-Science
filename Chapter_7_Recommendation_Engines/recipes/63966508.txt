Title:
Turkey Sausage - Homemade Sweet Italian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.25 lbs 93% Fat Free Ground Turkey
1/4 cup Green Bell Pepper, minced
1/4 cup Red Bell Pepper, minced
1 clove garlic, crushed (add more as desired)
1/2 tsp sweet paprika
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp dried thyme
1/4 tsp dried oregano leaves
1/4 tsp dried marjoram leaves
1/4 tsp dried fennel
1 tsp sugar

Directions:
Combine turkey, garlic and spices. Mix well using your hands.
Cook immediately or freeze for later use.
