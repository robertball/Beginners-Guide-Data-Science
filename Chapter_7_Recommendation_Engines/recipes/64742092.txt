Title:
Yael's No-Bake Biscuit Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
14 oz of plain tea biscuit such as Petit Beurre, Tesco Malted Milk or Parle-G
2 cups milk
Half cup heavy cream
3.4 oz vanilla flavor instant pudding
1 tsp instant coffee

Directions:
1. Prepare instant pudding according to package guidelines, except substituting 1 cup of milk for 1 cup of heavy cream.
2. Dissolve instant coffee in 1 cup of slightly warmed milk.
3. Briefly dip each biscuit into the milk/coffee mixture and lay them side by side in a 8 x 8 in pan.
4. Cover layer of biscuits with about a 1/4 in layer of pudding.
5. Continue to layer, alternating dipped biscuits with pudding (about 3 layers)
6. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate 6-8 hours before serving.
Level up by adding a layer of whipped cream on top!

Submitter: 
Michele Avissar-Whiting
Description: 
A quick and easy no-bake dessert that is highly customizable. Everyone will love this sweet treat!
