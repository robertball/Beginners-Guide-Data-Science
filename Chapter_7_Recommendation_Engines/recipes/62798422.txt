Title:
Potato Salad - Deb's Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs potatoes
1 cup mayonnaise
4 tsps. sweet pickle relish
4 tsps honey
2 tsps minced onion
2 tsps mustard
1 tsp vinegar
1/4 tsp celery seed
1/4 cup shredded carrot (raw)
1/4 tsp dried parsley
1/4 tsp pepper
dash of salt

Directions:
2 lbs of potatoes - peeled and cut into bitesize pieces. Cook them for about 7 - 10 minutes in about 6 cups of lightly salted water and then drain and rinse in cold water to stop cooking. Let cool. Place potatoes in the fridge for at least 2 hours.
Mix remaining ingredients together and pour over potatoes. Cover and chill for at least 1 hour.
