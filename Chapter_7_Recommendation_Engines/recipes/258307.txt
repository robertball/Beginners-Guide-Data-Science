Title:
Pineapple Orange Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4520594.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup frozen pineapple chunks
1/2 orange, peeled and chopped
3/4 cup Almond Breeze Original Cashewmilk Almondmilk

Directions:
Place all ingredients into blender and blend on high until smooth.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
Pineapple, oranges, and almondmilk blend up to make a refreshing and citrusy smoothie.
