Title:
Hot Fruit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can peaches, drained
1 can pears, drained
1 can apricots, drained
1 can pineapple chunks, drained
1 bottle maraschino cherries
1/4 c. butter, melted
3/4 c. brown sugar
2 t. cinnamon

Directions:
Melt butter and stir in brown sugar and cinnamon,
Arrange fruit in casserole dish.
Pour butter/brown sugar over fruit.
Bake at 325 degrees for 45 minutes or 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
