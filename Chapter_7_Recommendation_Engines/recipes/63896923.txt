Title:
Big Ray's Tex-Mex Turkey Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1081401.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large turkey carcass, with meat and giblets and neck
6 cups water
4 medium russet potato, cubed into 1/2 inch chunks
5 large celery stalks, cut into 1/2 inch chunks
5 large carrots, peeled and cut into 1/2 inch chunks
1 large onion chopped
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed (30 oz.)
1 jalapeno pepper, finely chopped
2 tsp ground cumin
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground pepper
2 cups frozen corn, thawed
1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves, finely chopped
1 Tbsp lime juice

Directions:
Place carcass, giblets and neck along with water into slow cooker.  Cook on low for 10 hours.  Let cool and strain stock.  Pick meat from carcass and return stock and meat into slow cooker.
Add next 10 ingredients to slow cooker and cook on low 4 hours or until potatoes have softened.  Stir in last 3 ingredients and let heat.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
I love making this during the holidays when nothing goes to waste, nor even a turkey carcass.  This somewhat spicy soup can be adapted for ease using chicken stock and rotisserie chicken.
