Title:
Cobbler Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup plain flour
1 cup sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
1 egg, beaten
1 stick butter, melted

Directions:
Sift together flour, sugar, and salt.
Add beaten egg and mix with a fork until you have very tiny crumbs.
Place on top of sweetened fruit (apples or peaches) in a 9 x 13.
Pour melted butter over top.
Bake in a 375 degree oven for 35-45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
I've been making apple and peach cobblers with this topping for over 35 years. It's especially good with ice cream.
