Title:
Green salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. Mixed greens
2 scallions, chopped
1 bell pepper, diced
1 c. purple cabbage
Olive oil
Balsamic vinegar
Kosher salt
Pepper

Directions:
Mix vegetables together.  Season with olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper, to taste.
