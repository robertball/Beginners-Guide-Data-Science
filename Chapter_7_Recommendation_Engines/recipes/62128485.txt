Title:
Kathy's Raspberry Apple Refresher Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 raspberry herb tea bags
1/4 cup honey
4 cups boiling water
1 12 oz. can ginger ale, flat
1.5 cups apple juice
ice

Directions:
Place tea bags in a 1/2 gallon pitcher and add 3 cups boiling water.  Steep 5 minutes and remove tea bags from pitcher.  Dissolve honey in 1 cup boiling water and add to pitcher.  Add ginger ale, apple juice, and enough ice to fill pitcher (about 3 cups).  Chill in refrigerator for an hour and serve in tall glasses over ice.  Garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired.

Submitter: 
KATHYP100
Description: 
A delicious and refreshing beverage for those hot summer afternoons on the patio.
