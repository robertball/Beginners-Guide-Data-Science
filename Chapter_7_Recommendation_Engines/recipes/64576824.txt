Title:
Butter Tart Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Base:
1/2 cup butter or margarine
1/3 cup brown sugar
1 1/4 cups flour
Filling:
2 eggs
1 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup flour
3/4 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1 1/2 cups raisins
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (or pecans)

Directions:
Base:
Crumble first 3 ingredients together.
Press into ungreased 9" x 9" pan.
Bake @ 350 x 10 minutes.
Filling:
Mix eggs and second amount of sugar together.
Stir in rest of ingredients.
Spread over base.
Bake @ 350 x 30 minutes, or until center is almost firm.
Cool.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Enjoy butter tart flavors without the fuss of pastry.
