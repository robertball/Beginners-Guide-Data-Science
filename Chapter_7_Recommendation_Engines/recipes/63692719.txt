Title:
Shana's Cheesy, Bacon, Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 cans of Campbells cream of potato soup
4-5 cans of milk
Onion, minced
Carrots, shredded
Bacon pieces
Natures Seasonings, Seasoned Salt
Pepper
Cheddar cheese, shredded
Chives

Directions:
Empty soup cans and add milk into stew pot on Med - High heat.
Add onions, carrots, bacon, salt, and pepper and simmer.  Stir regularly.
Can stir in cheese or leave out for individuals to add if wanted.
Garnish with chives and shredded cheese before serving.
