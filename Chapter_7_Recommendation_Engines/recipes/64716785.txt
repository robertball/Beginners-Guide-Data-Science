Title:
Instant Pot® Basic Cabbage Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3 pound Savoy cabbage
1 pound meat loaf mix, equal parts pork, beef and veal
1/2 cup long grain rice
2 L cloves garlic, micro planed or finely chopped
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1 t salt
1/2 t pepper
1 tsp parsley, dried
2 28oz tins of tomato sauce, such as Hunts®
1 1/2 cups water
1 t salt, second amount

Directions:
Pour the water into the bottom of the pot and insert the trivet.
Remove tough outer leaves and remove the core of the cabbage.
Place the cabbage on the trivet, core side down.
Place the lid on the pot, close vent.
Press manual and adjust time to 9 minutes.
Use quick release when timer goes off.
Carefully remove the lid and move the cabbage to a plate.
Drain the water and add the remainder of the tin of sauce and 1 t of salt.
While the cabbage is cooking, rinse the rice and place in a small bowl with 1/2 cup of water.
To a small bowl add the onion, garlic, parsley, 1/3 cup tomato sauce, 1 t salt, pepper and the ground meat.
Drain the rice and add this to the meat mixture.
Carefully remove the cabbage leaves and shave off the tough vein.
Place 2 T of the mixture on the leaf and roll, tucking in the sides.
Place the rolled leaves on the trivet facing the same way and the second layer facing the opposite way.
Pour the second tin of tomato sauce over the rolls, put the lid on the pot and close the vent.
Press the Stew/Meat button and adjust the time for 22 minutes.
Natural release for 10 minutes then use the rapid release method.

Cabbage leaves stuffed with meat loaf mix, onion and rice, cooked in the Instant Pot®
