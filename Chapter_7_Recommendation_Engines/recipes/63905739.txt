Title:
Frozen Chai Tea Smoothies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 C honey
2 cinnamon sticks
5 whole cloves
(1) 1 inch piece of ginger finely chopped
7 bags black tea
1 pint vanilla low fat frozen yogurs
1 tsp ground nutmeg
1 tsp ground cardamom
3 C ice cubes

Directions:
1.  In saucepan, combine 1 c water, the honey, cinnamon sticks, cloves & ginger over low heat.  Cover & simmer for 10 min
2.  Remove from the heat, add, the tea bags & let steep for 5 min.  Strain, then let the tea cool for 20 min
3.  Using a blender, combine the tea, frozen yogurt, nutmeg & cardamom.  Add the ice cubes & blend until smooth
