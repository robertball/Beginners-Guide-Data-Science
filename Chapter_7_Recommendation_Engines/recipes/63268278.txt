Title:
curried cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1tsp curry powder
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
3Tbsp vegetable oil
2 (10oz)packages of fresh cauliflower florets or 1 head
1 med onion chopped
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Cook curry and red pepper in hot oil in a lg skillet over med heat. Stirring often. 1 min. Add cauliflower, onion, and salt;Stirring constantly until onion is crisp and tender. Reduce heat to low and add 6 tbsp h2o. Cover and cook 8-10 mins until cauliflower is tender.
