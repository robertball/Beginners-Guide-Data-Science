Title:
Grandma Carol's Pumpkin Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs, beaten
1 cup sugar
2/3 cup pumpkin pack
3/4 cup flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp cloves, ground
__________________________
FILLING:
1/2 cup pecans, chopped
8 oz cream cheese, softened
2 TBSP butter, softened
1 tsp vanilla
1 (16 oz) package powdered sugar (or 2 cups)

Directions:
Butter a 12x17 jelly roll pan or cookie sheet.  Place waxed paper over pan and butter waxed paper (parchment paper will work also).  Beat eggs and sugar together.  Gradually add remaining sifted ingredients until mixed.  Spread batter evenly into prepared pan.  Bake EXACTLY 10 minutes at 400 degrees.  Immediately turn out onto towel sprinkled generously with powdered sugar.  Starting at long end, roll pumkin sheet WITH towel.  Let rest for 10 minutes.  Unroll.
Beat together frosting ingredients until smooth.  Spread frosting onto unrolled pumpkin sheet and roll up again without towel.  Cut off jagged ends.  Cover and refrigerate until chilled.
Sprinkle with powdered sugar before serving

Submitter: 
SusieQ
Description: 
My mom always makes her pumpkin roll around Thanksgiving and takes one to work where everyone asks her if she can make them one and they all want the recipe.  It is without a doubt the best pumpkin roll I've ever had!
