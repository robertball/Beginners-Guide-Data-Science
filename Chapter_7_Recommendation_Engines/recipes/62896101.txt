Title:
Easy Cranberry-Coconut Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white wheat flour
1 cup oats
2 egg whites
1.5 cup unsweetened applesauce
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1.5 tsp cinnamon (optional)
1 tbsp honey (optional)
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup shredded, unsweetened coconut
1 cup cranberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl. Drop cookies by the tbsp onto a greased cookies sheet. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until golden. Makes approx. 1.5 dozen cookies.

Submitter: 
iamNovember
Description: 
Healthy and low-fat cookies full of texture, don't expect a sweet cookie, but a delicious one all the same! Great for breakfast. Guilt-free, they won't last long.
