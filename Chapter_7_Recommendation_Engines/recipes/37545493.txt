Title:
Key Lime No-Bake Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRUST (like graham cracker):
3 TBSP melted butter
3 TBSP Spenda
6 TBSP Wheat Germ
FILLING:
8 oz cream cheese (at room temp)
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 cup ricotta cheese
1/2 cup and 2 TBSP Splenda (up to 2 cups)
6 TBSP Key lime juice
1 TBSP sugar-free vanilla extract
2 TBSP hot water
Zest of 1 lime (optional)
1 envelope unflavored gelatin
2 TBSP cold water
2 TBSP boiling water

Directions:
CRUST: in a small bowl mix wheat germ and splenda. Add melted butter until wet. Press into the bottom of a 10-inch pie pan. set aside
FILLING: With an electric mixer on medium seed, beat together the cream cheese, sour cream, ricotta, lime juice, Splenda (to desired sweetness), vanilla extract, hot water, and zest of 1 lime until well blended.
In a separate bowl, dissolve scant packet of gelatin in 2 TBSP cold water. Add 2 TBSP boiling water stir briskly.
With the mixer on high-and working very quickly so the gelatin does not set-blend the hot, dissolved gelatin thoroughly into the cheesecake mixture.
Immediately pour the mixture into the prepared pie pan and sprinkle with remaining zest of lime. Refrigerate for 3-4 hours until firm.
