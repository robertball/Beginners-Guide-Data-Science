Title:
Pizza Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz. tomato paste
6 oz. water
3 T grated Parmesan cheese
1 tsp. garlic minced
1 T honey
3/4 tsp.onion powder
1/4 tsp. dried oregano
1/4 tsp.dried basil
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1/8 tsp. cayenne pepper
1/8 tsp. dried red pepper flakes
salt to taste

Directions:
In a small bowl,combine tomato paste, water, cheese, garlic, honey, onion powder, oregano, basil, black pepper, cayenne pepper, pepper flakes and salt.  Mix together and let it set so flavors can unite.
