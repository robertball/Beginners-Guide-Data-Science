Title:
Holiday Caramel Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg Pillsbury Refrigerated brownies
2/3 cup chopped pecans or walnuts, divided
25 caramels, unwrapped
2 Tbsp milk

Directions:
Heat oven to 350F. Squeeze batter into greased 8-inch round baking pan. Stir in 1/3 cup chopped nuts. Bake 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted 2-inches from edge of pan comes out clean. Cool completely.
In a small saucepan, heat caramels and milk over low heat, stirring frequently until melted. Stir in remaining nuts.
Pour over cooled brownies; slice into 8 wedges.
