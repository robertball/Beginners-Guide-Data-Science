Title:
Gingerbread Apple Oat Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/574907.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup wheat bran
1 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
1 cup quick oats
1/3 cup wheat germ
1/2 teaspoons salt
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1 1/4 teaspoons ground ginger
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1 cup buttermilk
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/3 cup canola oil
1 egg
1 apple, cored and diced (no need to peel)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Grease 12 cup muffin pan or line with paper liners.
In a large bowl, whisk together dry ingredients. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, sugar, oil and egg. Stir in the apple pieces.
Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and stir just until combined.
Bake in preheated oven 5 minutes, reduce heat to 350, and bake for 15 more minutes or until done. Allow to cool for 5 minutes in pan, then remove to wire rack to cool completely.
