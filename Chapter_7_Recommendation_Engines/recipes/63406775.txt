Title:
Sausage Soup (Kathy's recipe) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. hot Italian turkey sausage links
1 medium onion - chopped
4 cloves of garlic - minced
2 tbsp. olive oil
2 carrots - diced
2 stalks of celery - chopped
1 potato - diced
1/3 cup uncooked small pasta
2 cans of Great Northern beans
2 tbsp. tomato paste
6 oz. fresh kale or spinach
32 oz. chicken stock
32 oz. water
4-5 chicken bouillon cubes

Directions:
Place olive oil in large pot and heat.
Cut sausage into bite sized pieces.
Add sausage and cook until slightly browned.
Add onion and garlic and cook about 5 minutes.
Add celery, carrot, potato, beans and tomato paste.
Add stock, water and bouillon cubes.
Let simmer about 15 minutes then add pasta and cook about 15 more minutes.
Stir in kale/spinach just before ready to serve and allow to wilt.
