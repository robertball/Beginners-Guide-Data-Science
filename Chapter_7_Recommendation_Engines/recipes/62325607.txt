Title:
Easy simple Tuna Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. package of cream cheese (regular or non fat any will do)
1. can of tuna (White albacore usually works best.)
1/2 tsp. of cajun seasoning
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Add tuna (drained if you like your dips THICK not drained if you like them a bit runny) and cream cheese (heat it in the microwave if you want to soften it or use a blender) Add the cajun seasoning and your own spices to taste and serve with chips.

Submitter: 
Cyn Determination
Description: 
This is a dip I tried to recreate from a neighboring butcher shop. There are a few extras that you can add to it and it is easy and simple to spice up.
