Title:
Easter  Marinated Veggie Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 cup white vinegar
1/3 cup oil
Sprinkle of black pepper and season salt
Can chickpeas, drained
Zucchini
Bell pepper
Onion
Whole kernel corn

Directions:
In a large glass bowl, mix first four ingredients to make dressing.
Dice veggies. And add enough veggies to "fill" dressing.
There is no wrong amount or type of veggies to add. Use colored peppers, add pimentos or grape tomatoes.
Covers and leave in fridge overnight. Stir before serving.

Submitter: 
aunt star
Description: 
My brother wanted a three bean salad for Easter. I created this when I discovered I'd forgotten a few ingredients. Everyone loved it. Now, we make this instead.
