Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2473356.jpg
Ingredients:
1.  One bag of ginormous and fluffy marshmallows.
2.  One bag of chocolate covered almonds, or your favorite candy, or a selection of your favorite candies -- just nothing hard.

Directions:
Take a ginormous marshmallow.
Insert into the marshmallow one chocolate covered almond.
Place into the microwave and heat for 10 seconds.  Time may very depending on the microwave, but the marshmallow should not be melted.
Enjoy!
