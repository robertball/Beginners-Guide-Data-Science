Title:
Creamy Tomato Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked penne pasta
1 pound uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
2 tablespoons olive oil
1-1/2 cups spaghetti sauce
1 carton (10 ounces) refrigerated Alfredo sauce
1/2 tin Progresso Cream of mushroom Soup
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley

Directions:
1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, saute the shrimp, garlic and pepper flakes in oil until shrimp turn pink. Stir in the spaghetti sauce, Alfredo sauce, Cream of mushroom soup, butter, salt and pepper; heat through.
2. Drain pasta; serve with shrimp mixture. Sprinkle with parsley. Yield: 4 servings.
