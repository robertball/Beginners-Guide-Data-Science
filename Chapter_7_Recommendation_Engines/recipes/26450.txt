Ortega速 Gazpacho Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 (24 ounce) jar ORTEGA速 Thick & Chunky Salsa
2 cups water
1 cup finely chopped celery
1 cup peeled and diced cucumber
1 cup finely chopped green bell pepper
1 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
1 cup chopped tomato
1/4 cup chopped green onion
1 clove garlic, minced
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro (optional)
1 cup croutons (optional)

Directions:
COMBINE salsa, water, celery, cucumber, bell peppers, tomato, green onions, garlic, salt and pepper in large bowl.  Cover; refrigerate for 2 hours.  Top with cilantro and croutons before serving.
