Title:
Kathy's Southern Pimiento Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 ounces shredded extra-sharp Cheddar cheese
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper (optional)
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced (optional)
2 (4 ounce) jar diced pimento, drained

Directions:
Place the cream cheese, mayonnaise, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, onion powder, minced jalapeno, and pimiento into the large bowl of a mixer. Beat at medium speed, with paddle if possible, until thoroughly combined.
Stir in grated cheddar cheese until thoroughly combined.

Submitter: 
kirklandcook
Description: 
This appetizer is great with wheat thins, stuffed in celery, or spread on Hawaiian rolls as sliders.Modified from: Southern Pimento Cheese
