Title:
Crunchy Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/402893.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium apples, chopped
1 medium firm banana, sliced
1/3 cup sunflower seeds
1/4 cup green grapes, halved
1/4 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup reduced-fat plain yogurt

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first five ingredients. Add yogurt; stir to coat. Refrigerate until serving.

Submitter: 
Karen  Barefield
Description: 
'Bursting with lightly dressed fresh fruit and crunchy sunflower kernels, this deliciously different salad is fun and refreshing,' writes Karen Barefield from Minneapolis, Kansas.
