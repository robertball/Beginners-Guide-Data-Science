Title:
Cool and Minty Party Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14-oz can Eagle Brand Sweetened Condensed Milk (NOT evaporated milk)
2 tsp peppermint extract
8 drops green food coloring (optional)
2 cups (1 pint) whipped cream or whipped topping (do not use non-dairy)
1 (18 1/2 or 18 1/4 oz)package white cake mix
Creme de menthe liqueur
1 8-oz container frozen non-dairy whipped topping, thawed

Directions:
1. Line 9 inch round layer cake pan with aluminum foil.  To prepare ice cream layer, in a large bowl combine sweetened condensed milk, peppermint extract and food coloring if desired.  Fold in whipped cream.  Pour into prepared pan; cover.  Freeze at least 6 hours or until firm.
2. Meanwhile, prepare and make cake mix as package directs for two 9 inch round layers. Remove from pan.  Cool thoroughly.
3. With fork, poke holes in the layers 1 inch apart halfway through each layer.  Spoon small amounts of liqueur in holes.  Place one cake layer on serving plate; top with ice cream layer then with second cake layer.  Trim ice cream layer to edge of cake layers.
4. Frost quickly with whipped topping.  Return to freezer at least 6 hours before serving.  Garnish as desired.  Freeze leftovers.

Submitter: 
Amy Wyen Kerrigan
Description: 
A cool dessert to go great with Easter dinners.
