Title:
King Ranch Chicken Mac & Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. Cellentani Pasta
2 Tbs. Butter
1 Medium Onion, diced
1 Green Bell Pepper, diced
1 can Rotel
8 oz. Velveeta, cubed
3 cups cooked & chopped Chicken
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
½ cup Sour Cream
1 tsp. Chili Powder
½ tsp. Ground Cumin
1 ½ Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°. Prepare the pasta according to package directions.
Meanwhile, melt butter in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the onion & bell pepper. Sauté 5 minutes or until tender.
Stir in Rotel and Velveeta. Cook, stirring constantly, for 2 minutes or until cheese melts.  Stir in the chicken, soup, sour cream, chili powder, & cumin.
Add the cooked chicken pasta and mix until well blended.  Spoon the mixture into a greased 10"cast iron skillet or 7" x 11" baking dish. Top with shredded Cheddar cheese.
Bake for 30 minutes, until bubbly.
