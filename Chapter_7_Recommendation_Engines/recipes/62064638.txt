Title:
Raspberry Chipotle Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 ounces frozen raspberries,thawed and roasted (approx 1 3/4 cups after roasting)
1 7 ounce can chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
2 tablespoons sugar
2 tablespoons vinegar
1/2 cup raspberry preserves (not jam)

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 300ÂºF. Roast the raspberries in a shallow baking dish until mushy, bubbly and slightly browned, about 10-12 minutes, stirring after 5 minutes or so. Puree chipotles and adobo sauce in blender or food processor to make a paste. Add roasted raspberries and the rest of the ingredients and blend until fairly smooth.

Submitter: 
Bill Echols
Description: 
A wonderful sweet, smoky sauce with just enough heat to let you know it's no ordinary sauce. Try it on chicken wings or roasted pork.
