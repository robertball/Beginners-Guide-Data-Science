Title:
AMAJO's Creamsicle® Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/914736.jpg
Ingredients:
1 fluid ounce vodka
1 fluid ounce triple sec
1 fluid ounce orange juice
1 fluid ounce fat free half-and-half
1 slice orange

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes. Pour in the vodka, triple sec, orange juice, and half-and-half. Shake until frosty, about 15 seconds; strain into a martini glass, and garnish with an orange slice.

Submitter: 
AmberJo
Description: 
Orange and cream flavored like the Creamsicle® Popsicle® you had as a kid, but all grown up.  A great dessert drink. Also good blended with ice for a frozen drink.
