Title:
Creamy Tomato Fettuccini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. fettuccini
2 small onions, chopped
1/4 cup olive oil
3 tablespoons margarine
3/4 cup tomato sauce
1 1/4 tsp. oregano
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1 cup milk
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Boil a large pot of lightly salted water. Cook noodles 11-12 minutes, or until al dente.
2. Meanwhile, saute onion in olive oil and margarine for 3 or 4 minutes in a large saucepan. Add tomato sauce, oregano, pepper and milk. Turn heat to low, and let simmer for the remainder of the noodles cooking.
3. Drain noodles and toss them in the saucepan with the sauce. Stir in Parmesan cheese and simmer for 5 minutes to let the sauce slightly thicken.

Submitter: 
Sola
Description: 
An easy dish I came up with when I was low on groceries. Delicious and quick, this dish would be great with garlic bread. The amount of Parmesan cheese is a suggestion, depening on how cheesy you like your pasta. Enjoy!
