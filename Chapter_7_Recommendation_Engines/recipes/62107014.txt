Title:
Lick the bowl Potato Cheese soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cubes of cubed potatoes
1 14oz. can Chicken Broth
1  grated carrot
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 TBS Parsley
1/2 tsp each salt and pepper
3 cups Milk
3 TBS Flour
8 oz. White American Cheese

Directions:
- Combine first 6 ingredients.
- Mix Milk and flour
- When potatoes are tender add milk mixture.
- Add Cheese
- Stir until smooth.

Submitter: 
Lori McTavish Bizjak
Description: 
This is my go to recipe when I need a filling meal fast. This soup and loaf of pumpernickel bread is satisfying and delicious.
