Title:
Chicken Kiev Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breast halves
1 clove garlic, chopped
1/2 tsp basil
1/2 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 cup bread crumbs
4 Tbsp butter, melted
2 Tbsp cooking wine
2 Tbsp green onions, sliced (optional)

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
2. Mix garlic, basil, oregano and salt with the bread crumbs.  Melt 2 Tbsp margarine.  Dip each chicken breast in the melted margarine.  Then roll in the bread crumb mixture and place in a shallow greased casserole or baking pan.
3. Bake for 30-45 minutes or until chicken is fully cooked.  Melt remaining 2 Tbsp margarine.  Mix with wine and bring to a boil.  Stir in onion.  Pour sauce over chicken.  Return to oven for 5 minutes.
