Title:
Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1cup all-purpose wheat flour
2 cups all-purpose unbleached flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
2 eggs, beaten
1 banana, overripe, mashed
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 cup crushed pineapple
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
2 cups grated zucchini
1 cup raisins, plumped in water & brandy

Directions:
Grease and flour two 8 x 4 inch pans.  Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Sift flours, salt, baking powder, soda, nutmeg, and cinnamon together in a bowl.
Beat eggs, oil, vanilla, banana, pineapple, and sugars together in a large bowl.  Add sifted ingredients to the creamed mixture, and beat well.  Stir in zucchini and raisins until well combined.  Pour batter into prepared pans. Or fill muffin cups 3/4 full and bake 25 to 30 minutes.
Bake for 40 to 60 minutes, or until tester inserted in the center comes out clean.  Cool in pan on rack for 20 minutes.  Remove bread from pan, and completely cool.

Submitter: 
Julie Holden
Description: 
Zucchini Bread good and moist.  Bread will freeze well, and keep in refrigerator for weeks.Modified from: Mom's Zucchini Bread
