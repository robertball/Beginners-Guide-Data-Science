Title:
White Raspberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh (or frozen) raspberries
2 cups all purpose flour
1/3 cup granulated sugar
3 teaspoons baking powder
dash of salt
1 large egg
3/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons milk
1/4 cup melted butter
1/2 cup white baking chips
1 teaspoon raspberry extract
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
For Streusel topping:
2 tablespoons flour
5 tablespoons sugar
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
2 tablespoons butter
1/2 teaspoon raspberry extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400. Grease and flour 12 muffin cups.
Rinse and drain raspberries.
In a large mixing bowl combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
In a smaller mixing bowl wisk together egg with the milk.  Add egg mixture to dry mixture along with melted butter and extracts. Mix lightly to combine, just until moistened. (batter will be lumpy.)
Fold in raspberries and white chips. Spoon into muffin cups.
In a small bowl combine 2 tablespoons of flour, 5 tablespoons sugar, and 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon. Cut in 2 tablespoons of butter with a fork, add 1/2 teaspoon of raspberry extract. Fluff with fork until crumbles form. Sprinkle over muffin batter.
Bake at 400 for 18 to 21 min.
