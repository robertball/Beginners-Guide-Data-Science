Title:
Chocolate Peanut Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of honey
1 1/4 cup of peanut butter
1 1/2 cup of chocolate chips
2 cup of peanuts
2 cup of rice puff cereal

Directions:
Bring the honey, peanut butter and chocolate chips to a boil, stiring constantly as it burns easily.
Once a boils is reached remove from heat and add the peanuts and rice puffed cereal.
Mix well and press loosely into a lightly greased 8X8 pan.
Cool in the fridge then cut.
