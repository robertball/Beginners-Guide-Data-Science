Title:
Toffee Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup sugar
1 egg (separated)
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cups flour
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250 degrees
1.  Cream butter and sugar together
2.  Mix in the egg yolk and vanilla
3.  Mix the flour and half the nuts until a nice dough forms.
4.  Spread into a jelly roll pan
5.  Slightly beat egg white and spread on top of dough.
6.  Sprinkle remaining nuts on top.
7.  Bake for 1 1/2 hours at 250 degrees.
8.  Cut cookies while warm into diamond shapes.
