Title:
Sweet Sesame Beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2157088.jpg
Ingredients:
3 mini assorted bell peppers, sliced julienne
1 small onion, sliced julienne
1 large carrot, sliced oriental
1 large celery stalk, sliced oriental
200 g beef, cut into strips
15 ml extra virgin olive oil
200 ml VH honey garlic sauce
75 ml sesame seeds
30 ml cornstarch
45 ml cold water
170 dry broad egg noodles (cooked according to package instructions)

Directions:
saute the carrots and celery for about 2 minutes, add the beef onions and peppers, saute another 5 minutes
add the sesame seeds saute for 10 more minutes, or until beef is browned
add the honey garlic sauce, then simmer about 5 minutes, then add cornstarch and water
stir in the egg noodles then let stand 5 minutes
