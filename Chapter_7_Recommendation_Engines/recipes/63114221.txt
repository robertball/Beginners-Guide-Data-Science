Title:
Candy Apple Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/791873.jpg
Ingredients:
2 shots of Four Roses Bourbon
2 shots of Apple Jack
1/4 shot of Framboise
2 drops (NO MORE) of Yeni Raki (or other anisette)
1 shot of grenadine
2 drops bitters.
Apple Sugar

Directions:
Take two martini glasses and coat the rim by running a lemon around it. Dip in apple sugar. In a shaker filled with ice, add the ingredients. Shake vigorously. Serve in the rimmed martini glasses.

Submitter: 
The Jazz Chef
Description: 
A drink concoction I whipped up that seems pretty popular. A variation of the Manhattan.
