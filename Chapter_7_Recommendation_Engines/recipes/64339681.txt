Title:
Rockin Robin's Classic Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 hard boiled eggs, chopped and diced
7 – 8 cups of cooked, diced potatoes (use a combo of red, russet and new potatoes peeled) or otherwise scrubbed new potatoes with skins on
1 cup diced celery
¾ cup chopped green onion
2 tbsp prepared mustard
Salt and ground black pepper to taste
1 ½ cups of mayonnaise

Directions:
Cook potatoes in lightly salted water, drain and let cool .  Boil eggs, let cool down in cold water before slicing and dicing.
Mix all together well, sprinkle with paprika and garnish with parsley sprigs if desired.

Submitter: 
Phoebe
Description: 
Simple and super easy.  Better if chilled overnight.  Have tried many others but always gravitate back to my basic spud salad.
