Title:
Crispy Critters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/775143.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium to large russet potatoes
salt
oil for frying.

Directions:
Peel potatoes and cut into large chunks.  Place i a pan with water to cover.  Add salt.  Boil for 15 minutes or until tender.  When tender, drain and place on a plate to dry for a few minutes.  Heat oil in a saucepan or fryer. When oil reaches approximately 375 degrees place potatoes into the hot oil.  Cook until golden brown and crunchy.

Submitter: 
pelicangal
Description: 
Twice cooked potatoes that are not baked but are delicious.  The finished potato is crunchy on the outside and light and fluffy on the inside.
