Title:
Tuscan Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp olive oil
1 onion (chopped)
2 garlic cloves (minced)
one 19oz can red kidney beans (rinsed & drained)
one 14oz can Itailian stewed tomateos (drained)
1/4 lbs. Canadian bacon (cut into strips)
1/4 cup dry red wine
1 tsp dried basil
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp black pepper
4 cups cooked bow tie pasta

Directions:
1. In large nonstick skillet, heat the oil. Add the onion & garlic; cook, stirring as needed, until softened.
2. Add the beans, tomatoes, bacon, wine, basil, oregano & pepper; bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer, uncovered, until slightly thickened.
3. Place the pasta in a large serving bowl. top with sauce; toss to coat.
