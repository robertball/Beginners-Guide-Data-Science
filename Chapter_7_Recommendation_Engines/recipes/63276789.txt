Title:
Avocado and Cantaloupe Wonder Salad with Creamy French Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1T white sugar
2 tsp paprika
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon dry mustard powder
1.5 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
1/2 clove garlic, minced
1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/2 pinch cayenne pepper
3/8 cup vegetable oil
3 cups mixed salad greens
1/8 cantaloupe scooped into melon balls
1/2 avocado - peeled, pitted, and cubed
1/4 cup toasted slivered almonds

Directions:
Whisk the sugar, paprika, salt, mustard, vinegar, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, and cayenne pepper together in a mixing bowl. Slowly drizzle in the vegetable oil while whisking rapidly until the salad dressing is thick and creamy.
Place the salad greens into serving dishes, and sprinkle with the cantaloupe and avocado pieces. Drizzle with the salad dressing, then sprinkle with toasted almonds to serve.
