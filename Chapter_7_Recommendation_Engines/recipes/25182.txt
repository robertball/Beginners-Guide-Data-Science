Title:
Fruit Dip VII Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
1 (1.55 ounce) bar milk chocolate with crispy rice, crumbled

Directions:
In a medium bowl, stir together the whipped topping and the chocolate candy.  Serve with fruit salad for dipping!
