Title:
Mexican Style Pinto Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/559717.jpg
Ingredients:
1  cup dried pinto beans
5 cups chicken broth
1/2 tsp. liquid smoke
1/2 cup onion, chopped
1/2 cup green bell pepper, chopped
2 teaspoons cumin
2 teaspoons chili powder
3/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon fresh ground pepper
1 chopped, seeded jalapeno (optional)

Directions:
Place pinto beans in a colander and wash thouroughly, sorting through them to pick out any small rocks or dirt.  Place in a dutch oven. Cover with water 2 inches above beans, and bring to a boil. Boil beans 3 minutes. Cover, remove from heat, and let soak 1 hour. Drain.
Preheat oven to 300 degrees.  Bring beans, 3 cups chicken broth, and remaining ingredients to a boil. Cover and transfer pot to the oven.  Cook for 3 to 3-1/2 hours, checking occasionally and adding additional broth as needed.
Serve or chill and reheat for later use.
