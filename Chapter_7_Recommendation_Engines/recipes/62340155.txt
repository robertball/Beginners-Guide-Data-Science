Title:
Tastefully Simple Cranberry Tangerine Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cranberry Tangerine Cheeseball Mix
1 pkg. yellow cake mix
2 eggs
1/3 c. oil
1 c. water
1 c. sour cream
1 c. powdered sugar
1 tbsp. orange juice
1 tsp. vanilla
2 tbsp. milk

Directions:
Combine first 6 ingredients.  Pour into a greased bundt pan.  Bake at 350 for 45-50 minutes.  Combine remaining ingredients and drizzle over hot cake.
