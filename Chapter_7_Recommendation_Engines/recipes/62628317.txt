Title:
Susan's version of Red Lobster Crab and Lobster Stuffed Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup melted butter
1 pound fresh mushrooms, stems removed
1 cup Panko
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 (6 ounce) can crabmeat, drained
1 pound imitation lobster chopped
1/4 C. each minced green pepper, minced onion,sauteeed in 4 T. butter
Scampi sauce-1 stick butter, 1/2 C. Chablis, 3T garlic powder
1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese (optional)

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Coat individual casserole dishes with olive oil. Arrange mushroom caps in a single layer in the dishes.
In a medium bowl, mix together the Panko, 3/4cup butter, shredded cheese, crabmeat, lobster, onion, and green pepper. Spoon into mushroom caps where the stems used to be. pour Scampi sauce over mushrooms.
Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven, or until lightly browned on the top. Sprinkle with additional cheese if desired, and serve hot!
