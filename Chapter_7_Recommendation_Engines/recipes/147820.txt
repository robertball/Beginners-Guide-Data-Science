Title:
Healthy Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups All-Bran® Original or All-Bran® Bran Buds®
1 1/4 cups fat-free milk
2 egg whites
1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Directions:
Stir together flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Set aside.
In large mixing bowl, combine Kellogg's(R) All-Bran(R) cereal and milk. Let stand about 2 minutes or until cereal softens. Add egg whites and oil. Beat well. Add flour mixture, stirring only until combined. Portion evenly into twelve 2 1/2-inch muffin pan cups coated with cooking spray.
Bake at 400 degrees F about 18 minutes or until lightly browned. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Kellogg's® All-Bran®
Description: 
Hearty bran muffins are a quick brunch treat or snack any time of day.
