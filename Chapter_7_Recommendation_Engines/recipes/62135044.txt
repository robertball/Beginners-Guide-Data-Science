Title:
Blackberry Custard Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 unbaked 9" refrigerated pie crust
2 cups blackberries (fresh or frozen)
4 eggs
2/3 cup sugar
1 1/3 cups milk
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Sprinkle blackberries in bottom of pie shell.
Beat eggs slightly.
Stir in milk, vanilla, and sugar.
Pour over blackberries.
Bake at 400 degrees (about 30 to 40 minutes) until knife inserted in the middle comes out clean.
