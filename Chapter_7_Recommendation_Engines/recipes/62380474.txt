Title:
Broccoli Cranberry Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/851713.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C broccoli florets
1 1/2 C craisins
4 C shredded cabbage
1 small onion, minced,
8 slices bacon, crumbled
1 C raisins (I leave these out)
1 C walnuts, chopped (I roast the nuts first)
1 C mayo
2T Cider Vinegar
1/2 C sugar

Directions:
Mix the first 7 ingredients in a bowl. Mix gently but thoroughly.
Mix the last 3 ingredients in a separate bowl. Pour this over the coleslaw and mix thoroughly.  Chill and serve.

Submitter: 
Lisa Scott
Description: 
A delicious coleslaw, and beautiful to look at with the red cranberries and the green broccoli.
