Title:
Sada Roti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of sifted flour White flour or a mixture of wheat and white flour. I would use 1/2 and 1/2 if giong for wheat flour.
1 tsp baking powder
pinch of salt (I use Kosher salt)
1/2 cup water
1 tbsp olive oil

Directions:
1) In a bowl combine flour, baking powder and salt.
2) Add water until a soft dough forms. (you may not need    all the water.)
3) Knead for 2-3 mins.
4) Form into a ball
5) Add Olive Oil
6) Let rest for 1/2 hour
7) Divide into two balls
8) Roll out on floured board into a 1/2 inch thick flat circle
9) In a heated frying pan/griddle over medium heat place Roti
10) Let cook for 1-2 mins and then turn over and let the other side cook until browned
11) Roti should swell in the center a bit if not place in microwave for 30 secs and watch until swollen
12) Cut and serve

Submitter: 
Pixie2
Description: 
It is the everyday version of Naan,Puri,Paratha that is used in Caribbean Indian homes.
