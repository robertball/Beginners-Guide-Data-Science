Title:
Ramen Muto Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1788107.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bag of dried shiitake mushrooms
2 lbs chicken legs (skinless)
2 country style pork ribs
1 full white onion (chopped)
3 cucumbers
5 radishes
2 tbsp sugar
1 tbsp salt
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup sambal olek sauce (garlic chili paste sauce)
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup rice wine vinegar
1/2 cup sake
1 egg
green onion
soba noodles

Directions:
In a HUGE pot of water, boil dried shiitake mushrooms, a full white onion (chopped) and 2lbs of chicken legs (no skin)
Boil for about an hour and a half
Now, at the EXACT SAME TIME, you hear the oven to 350
In the oven, you put in 2 country style pork ribs heavily salted
On a cookie tray type thing
Cook for about one and a half hours.
Cook for about one and a half hours.
They will finish at the exact same time.
So now you scoop out the chicken which should just shred off the bone
And you should shred it
Off the bone onto a dish.
And the put the bones back in the boiling pot
Then, you put the pork into the boiling pot too
Continue for another one and a half hours
In the meantime,
slice 3 cucumbers and 5 radishes and put them in a Tupperware
Add 2 tablespoons sugar and 1 tablespoon salt
Cover. Refrigerate
Now, add 1/2 cup soy sauce and 1/4 cup sambal Olek sauce (garlic chili paste sauce)
Now, at the end of the 1.5 hours of pork boiling, take the pork out and shred it next to the chicken. If there are bones, put those back in the pot
Now, boil water and prepare soba noodles or angel hair pasta per directions.
Set them aside.
Now, in a saucepan or skillet heat 1/2 cup each of soy sauce, rice wine vinegar and sake.
Bring to boil and reduce by half
Then pour that mixture into the pot
Now, boil water and poach an egg
Scoop out all bones and mushrooms from pot. Throw away
Place noodles and meat in a bowl
Ladle in soup from pot
Top with the pickled cucumbers and radishes
Top with poached egg
Chop green onion. Put that on top too
Done
