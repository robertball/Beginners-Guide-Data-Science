Title:
Crockpot Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
can of cream of celery
can of cream of chicken
1/2 can of water
can of biscuits (we use krogers jumbo butter biscuits)
1/2 bag frozen corn
1/2 bag frozen peas
2 chic, boullion cubes
You can pretty much add what you want to this recipe, seasoning , whatever. Hope you

Directions:
cut chicken into bite size pieces & put in crockpot
add both cans of soup, water & bouillon to crockpot & stir to mix 2gether
add veggies & cook on med. 6 hrs (you can use whatever veggies you want, we just like these with it)1 hr before ready to serve tear biscuits into pieces & put on top of mixture in crockpot. When biscuits are dumpling consistancy, you are ready to eat
