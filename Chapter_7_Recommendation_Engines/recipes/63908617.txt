Title:
Asian chicken linguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box linguine
3 tbsp sesame oil
4-5 chicken breasts
2 1/2 cups frozen stir fry vegetables
3 cloves garlic, minced
3/4 tsp ground ginger
dash cayenne pepper
1/3 cup hoisin sauce
3 tbsp soy sauce
6 green onions, sliced diagonally

Directions:
1. In a big pot, cook linguine according to package directions; rinse and drain; set aside.
2. In the same big pot, heat sesame oil over medium heat.
3. Saut√© the chicken in hot oil for 5-8 minutes or until no longer pink, stirring constantly.
4. Add vegetables, garlic, ginger, and cayenne pepper; cook 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
5. Add in the hoisin sauce, soy sauce, and cooked pasta, toss to coat.
6. Heat through.
7. Add green onions, stir to mix.
8. Serve immediately.
