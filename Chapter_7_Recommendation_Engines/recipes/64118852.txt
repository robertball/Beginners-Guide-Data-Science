Title:
CHOCOLATE CRUNCH Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup raw cashews, chopped
½ cup raw pine nuts, chopped
1 cup almond meal
1 cup shredded unsweetened coconut
2 cups raw almonds, slivered or chopped
2 tablespoons unsweetened cacao powder
1 tablespoon cinnamon
½ cup coconut oil
½ cup raw honey
1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees.
Combine all your dry ingredients together in a mixing bowl.
In a microwave dish, mix together the wet ingredients.
Put your microwave dish in a microwave for 30 seconds.
Carefully add the wet mixture into the dry ingredients and stir to combine, making sure eveyrthing is thoroughly combined.
In a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper spread mixture evenly.
Place the cookie sheet into the heated oven and bake for 25 minutes-30 minutes, making sure that the mixture doen't burn.
Once done, take out the cookie sheet from the oven and let it cool. The batter should turn crunchy as it gets colder.
Crumble mixture.
You may eat it dry or with coconut or almond milk.
