Title:
Shrimp Cocktail Spread with Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cooked shrimp pieces
2 8 oz. packages of cream cheese, softened
1 Tablespoon minced onion flakes
1 Tablespoon fresh lemon juice
1 Tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 Tablespoon grated horseradish
Shrimp cocktail sauce
Crackers for serving

Directions:
Rinse and drain the shrimp pieces.  Chill well.
Combine the next five ingredients.
Spread mixture on a large (10") platter.
Chill.
When ready to serve, spread the cocktail sauce over the chilled mix and top with the well-drained shrimp.
Serve with crackers.
