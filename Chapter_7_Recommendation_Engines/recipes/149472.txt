Title:
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies Treats® Holiday Presents Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/56401.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
6 cups Kellogg's® Rice Krispies® cereal or Kellogg's® Cocoa Krispies™ cereal
Holiday sprinkles
Red string licorice

Directions:
Melt margarine in large saucepan over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat.
Add Kellogg's(R) Rice Krispies(R) cereal. Stir until well coated.
Using buttered spatula or waxed paper, press mixture evenly into 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan coated with cooking spray. Cut into 2-inch squares when cool.
Decorate with holiday sprinkles and use red string licorice for ribbon. Best if served the same day.

Submitter: 
Kellogg's® Rice Krispies®
Description: 
The whole family will have fun decorating these 'holiday presents' with sprinkles and licorice strings.
