Title:
Joe's Peanutbutter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Sugar
3/4 Cup Evaporated Milk
1 stick margarine
16 Marshmallows
1 tsp. Vanilla
1 Cup peanutbutter Chips

Directions:
Combine Sugar, Milk, Margarine and 15 Marshmallows (eat 16th for yourself) in large sauce pan.
Cook over medium heat to boiling.
Marshmallows should be completely melted.
Boil for 5 minutes ONLY stirring constantly.
REMOVE FROM HEAT AFTER 5 MINUTES ARE DONE.
Add in Vanilla and Peanutbutter chips.
Stir till mixed well.
Pour in a 9x9 pan and cool.
