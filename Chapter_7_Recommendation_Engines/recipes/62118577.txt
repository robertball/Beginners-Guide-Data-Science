Title:
Onion-Cheese Yeast Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C Flour
2 Packages of instant yeast
1 T Sugar
1/4 C Finely minced onion (or 1 T dried onion flakes)
1 t. Salt
1/2 t. Celery seed
1 C Milk
1/2 C Water
2 T Butter
1 Egg
1 C Shredded extra sharp cheddar

Directions:
In heavy-duty mixer bowl, combine 2 C flour, yeast sugar, salt and celery seed and mix well.
In saucepan heat milk, water, shortening and onion until warmed to about 120-130 degrees.
Combine liquid with dry ingredients and add the egg.
Mix at low speed until moist.
Add cheese and 1 C flour.
Beat at medium speed for 5 minutes to make a thick batter.
Cover bowl and let rise about 30 min till doubled.
Stir down batter and spoon into greased muffin cups.
Cover and rise another 30 minutes till doubled again.
Bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes until well browned.
Best when served warm.

Submitter: 
MOLLYSMAMA
Description: 
These no-knead rolls are always a big hit at my house.  I make them in muffin cups because it makes a thick batter rather than a stiff dough.  Be sure to use extra sharp cheddar to give it some tang.
