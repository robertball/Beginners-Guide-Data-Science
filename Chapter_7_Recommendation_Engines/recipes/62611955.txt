Title:
French Chocolate Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. sugar
1/2 soft butter
2 squares (2 oz) unsweetened chocolate, melted and cooled
2 tsp. vanilla
4 eggs
graham cracker crust
whipped cream

Directions:
Cream butter and sugar thoroughly.  Add the melted chocolate and vanilla.  Blend well. Add 2 eggs.  Beat 5 minutes on medium speed.  Ad the other 2 eggs and beat 5 minutes longer.  Pour into crust.  Chill.
Serve with whipped cream.
