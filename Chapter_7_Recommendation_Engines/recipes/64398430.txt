Title:
Baked Zucchini Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium zucchini, cut into 1/4-inch slices
1/2 cup seasoned dry bread crumbs
Garlic powder or salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
2 egg whites

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 475 degrees F (245 degrees C).
In one small bowl, stir together the bread crumbs, garlic powder or salt, pepper and Parmesan cheese. Place the egg whites in a separate bowl. Dip zucchini slices into the egg whites, then coat the breadcrumb mixture. Place on a greased baking sheet.
Spray with Pam.  Bake for 5-8 minutes in the preheated oven, then turn over and bake for another 5 to 10 minutes, until browned and crispy.

Submitter: 
Cynda Baker
Description: 
Zucchini is breaded and baked until crispy and golden brown. A quick, healthy way to use some of that zucchini from your garden!Modified from: Quick Baked Zucchini Chips
