Title:
Lime Chicken Soft Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lbs chicken breast - cubed
1 tbsp butter
1/8 cup red wine vinegar
1 tbsp lime juice
1 teaspoon white sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
4 green onions, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 cup diced green chili

Directions:
Dry chicken cubes and season with salt/pepper.  Brown chicken in butter in large skillet. Add vinegar, lime juice, sugar, salt, pepper, green onion, garlic and oregano. Cover and simmer for an 10 minutes.  Add diced green chili and simmer uncovered until sauce thickens.
