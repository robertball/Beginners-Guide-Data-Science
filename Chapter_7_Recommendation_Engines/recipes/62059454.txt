Title:
Beefy Hot Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large round loaf bread
1 cup sour cream
8 ounces cream cheese,softened
1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
3 ounces shredded dried beef or 1- 2 1/2 ounce jar Hormel dried beef, sliced finely
2 tablespoons dried chives
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
Cut op from bread, hollow out loaf in shape of bowl, reserving extra bread
Combine sour cream, cream cheese, cheddar cheese, beef, chives and garlic salt in large bowl, mix well. Spoon into bread shell. Wrap in foil.
Bake at 325 degrees for 30 minutes. Bread extra bread into bite size pieces. Add to top of mixture in bread shell. Bake for 30 minutes longer.
Serve warm...have guest spread dip on bread pieces.

Submitter: 
DLPALMA
Description: 
This smooth and tangy dip is baked and served in a bread bowl.  I use sourdough, but others maybe used. Always a hit a parties! If serving the dip at someone else's house bake the second time there.
