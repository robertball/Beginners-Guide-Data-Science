Title:
Crispy Breakfast Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 C. cereal (I use Kashi Go Lean)
3/4 C. dried Cranberries
3/4 C raisins or dried blueberries
3/4 C. almonds
1-2 tsps. cinnamon
3/4 C honey
3/4 C almond butter
2 TBPS. butter

Directions:
Stir together cereal, fruit, nuts and cinnamon in a large bowl. Put the honey, almond butter and butter in a microwave safe bowl for 1 1/2 min. on high power til butter has melted. Stir well and pour over the cereal mixture. Stir to coat. Dampen hands w/ Cold water and press into a 9x 13  pan. Cover w plastic wrap and freeze for 45 minutes to an hour. Cut into bars (approx 5x2 inch size) and then wrap individually. Keep stored in the freezer and pull out a few at a time leaving them in the fridge.
