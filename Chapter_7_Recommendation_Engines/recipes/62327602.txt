Title:
Real Louisiana Crawfish Boil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 5lb sack of small red potatoes
6 yellow onions
1 4oz jar of cayenne pepper
1 73 oz jar of Zatarains powder crab boil
2 4 oz bottles of liquid crab boil
5 garlic bulbs
4 lemons
6 cobs of corn broke in half to make 12
2 packages of your favorite links sausage cut length wise
large 80 quart crawfish cooker
20 pounds of live crawfish

Directions:
Fill the pot a little more than half full of water.Bring your water to a rolling boil add liquid crab boil, entire jar of zatarains season and jar of cayenne pepper ( for less spicy add only half of jar of cayenne pepper)
Place potatoes, onions cut in half, lemons sliced in half and squezzed, bulbs of garlic cut in half, and sausage in basket.Submerge basket and boil potatoes for 7 minutes.
Add crawfish to basket with potatoes and cook 7 more minutes. Wait 2 minutes and add corn. Turn off flame and let soak for 20 minutes.
Empty into a clean ice chest to keep warm and enjoy!!
