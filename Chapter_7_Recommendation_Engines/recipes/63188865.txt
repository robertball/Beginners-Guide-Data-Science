Title:
Limoncello Cupcakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CUPCAKES:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 stick butter
2 oz cream cheese
1 cup sugar
3 large eggs
1 tablespoons limoncello
1/2 cup buttermilk
1/4 cup lemon juice
zest of one lemon
___________________________
LEMON CURD:
1/2 cup lemon juice
zest of two lemons
1/4 cup sugar
1 egg
1 egg yolk
___________________________
ICING:
1/2 stick butter
6 oz cream cheese
4 cups powdered sugar
2 tablespoons limoncello

Directions:
To make the cupcakes; Beat softened butter and cream cheese.
Add sugar and mix until light and fluffy.
Add eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each.
Add the limoncello and beat until well mixed.
Sift together flour, baking powder and salt. Alternately add flour and buttermilk to butter mixture.
Mix in the lemon juice and lemon zest just until combined. Pour into prepared cupcake pans and bake at 325 for about 17 minutes.
Cool on wire racks.
Meanwhile, prepare the lemon curd. Mix lemon juice, lemon zest, and sugar over medium heat until boiling.
Whisk in egg and egg yolk.
Remove from heat and whisk lemon mixture into eggs. Return to heat and cook over medium, whisking constantly, until thickened.
Cool completely.
Use an apple corer or melon baller to scoop a hole into tops of cooled cupcakes. Fill with cooled lemon curd.
Continue by making the icing. Beat butter and cream cheese.
Add limoncello and beat until smooth.
Beat in powdered sugar until desired consistency is obtained.
Ice filled cupcakes. Garnish with lemon slices.
