Title:
Tom's crockpot Winter Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
1-1/2 lbs. Ground beef
2 tbs. Ground oregano
2 tbs. Dried parsley
3 cloves garlic chopped
1 pkg. Onion soup mix
1 c. Italian style bread crumbs
-------------------------------------
1 tsp. Salt
1 tsp. Black pepper
4 c. Water & 4 cubes beef bullion
parsnips, carrots, celery, potatoes, how much depends upon how big your crockpot is.

Directions:
Directions
1. Preheat oven to 350F. Lightly grease 9x12 baking pan.
2. In a large mixing bowl, combine ground beef, oregano, parsley, garlic, onion soup mix, and bread crumbs.
3. Form into balls, place into pan and oven for 40 mins.
4. While this is cooking, cut up parsnips, carrots, celery, potatoes and place in the crockpot. Make the bullion broth.
5. Add salt & pepper to the veggies, and add the bullion broth.
6. Add meatballs when they are done and simmer on low for about 6 hrs.
Toasted garlic bread goes good with this...

Description: 
A slow cooked soup/stew for a cold winter's day. Can be simmered on the stovetop if you wish.
