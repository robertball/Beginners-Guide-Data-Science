Title:
Turkey/Chicken Cutlets with lemon and caper sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 turkey or chicken breast cutlets pounded thin
pepper to taste
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
1 TBSP extra virgin olive oil
2 tsp Smart Balance spread
1 garlic clove crushed
1/2 cup dry white wine
1 TBSP capers
Juice of 1 lemon-2 TBSP lemon juice
2 TBSP chopped parsley

Directions:
Season cutlets with pepper and then dust in flour.  Add 1 teaspoon of oil to preheated pan and brown two cutlets at a time. 1-2 minutes per side. Remove from pan and repeat with remaining cutlets.  Set aside.  Add garlic to the pan and saute for a few seconds: deglaze pan with wine.  Add capers and lemon juice and reduce for a minute.  Whisk in Smart Balance add cutlets in pan and simmer for a few minutes.
