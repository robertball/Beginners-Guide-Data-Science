Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 measures of gishweed; 2 bundles of gishgrass; 4 dino-leeches; 2 scoops of lacewing mascots; 3 measures of elopus skin; 1 measure of wooster horn; a hair (or other piece) of Misha Collins.

Directions:
1. Add 3 measures of the gishweed to your cauldron (must have been picked on a full moon).
2. Add 2 bundles of the gishgrass to your cauldron.
3. Stir 3 times, clockwise. Wave your wand then let potion brew for 80 minutes.
4. Add the dino-leeches to your cauldron.
5. Add 2 scoops of lacewing mascots to the mortar, crush to a fine powder, then add 2 measures of the crushed lacewings to the cauldron.
6. Heat for 30 seconds on a low heat. Wave your wand to complete this stage of the potion.
7. Add 3 measures of elopus skin to your cauldron.
8. Add 1 measure of wooster horn to the mortar, crush to a fine powder, then add one measure of the crushed horn to your cauldron.
9. Heat for 20 seconds at a high temperature. Wave your wand then let potion brew for 24 hours.
10. Stir 3 times, anti-clockwise.
11. Add the pieces of Misha Collins.
12. Cool for 30 seconds.
13. Wave your wand to complete the dish.
