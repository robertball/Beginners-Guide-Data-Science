Title:
Peanut Butter Cup Lover's Dream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3.9 oz chocolate fudge pudding
1 cup milk
1 8oz cream cheese
1 cup powdered sugar
1 cup peanut butter
12 oz whipped topping (like Cool Whip)
3 tablespoons butter
20 crushed creme filled cookies (like Oreos)

Directions:
Directions
Wisk the pudding with the milk. Let set for 5 min.
Crush cookies and place in bowl
Melt the butter in the microwave. Toss the cookies and the butter. Press into 13x9 dish
Beat in cream cheese, powdered sugar, peanut butter into the pudding. Mix in the whipped topping until all is mixed well.
Decorate with crumbled cookies if desired.
Place mixture over cookie crust and refrigerate until cold. (about 1-2 hours)

Submitter: 
ChicagoCookie84-Anita
Description: 
If you love the marriage between peanut butter and chocolate, you'll love this dessert that I came up with. Is super easy and tastes like one giant peanut butter cup.
