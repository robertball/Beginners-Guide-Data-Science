Title:
Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/759610.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cans cream of mushroom soup
1 cup mayonnaise
2 cups sharp cheddar cheese
1 lb
2 eggs
Panko bread crumbs
Butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Chop broccoli and mix together all ingredients except butter and bread crumbs.
Spoon into 9 inch casserole dish.
In separate bowl melt butter and add bread crumbs; sprinkle generously onto casserole; bake 40-50 minutes.
