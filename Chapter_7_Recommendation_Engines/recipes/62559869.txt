Title:
Michigan Maple Peanut Butter Sheet Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
2 cups flour
2 eggs
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup vanilla yogurt
2/3 cups peanut butter (chunky if desired)
1 hot water
frosting:
1/2 cup butter
2/3 cup chunky peanut butter (smooth if desired)
6 tablespoons milk
2 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1 tablespoon Pure Michigan Maple Syrup
1/2 cup finely chopped walnuts
1/3 cup finely chopped pecans
1/4 cup sliced almonds

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F
Cake: Sift sugar and flour together. Set aside. In a medium bowl blend eggs, baking soda and yogurt well. Set aside. In a small sauce pan combine peanut butter, butter and water, bring to a boil stirring well. Add to flour sugar mix. Fold in yogurt mix. Pour into a 15X20 sheet pan and bake for 20 minutes.
Frosting: Combine butter, peanut butter and milk in the same sauce pan, bring to a boil stirring constantly. Add powdered sugar, maple syrup. Blend together until smooth and silky, reduce the heat if needed. Pour over cake while both are still warm. Sprinkle on walnuts, pecans and almonds. Let cool, slice and serve.
