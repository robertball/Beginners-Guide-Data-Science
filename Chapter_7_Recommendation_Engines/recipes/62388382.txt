Title:
Mexican Pinto Beans & Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 TBS virgin olive oil
1 tsp Cumin
1 tsp ground coriander
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1 Tbsp chili powder
1 Medium onion peeled and chopped
3 Cloves garlic chopped
2 Cups cooked brown rice
4 Cups canned pinto beans or cooked, drained and well rinsed.
1 1/2 to 2 Cups low sodium chicken or veggie stock
3 Tbsp chipotle peppers, chopped, plus sauce
2 Tbsp tomato paste
1 tsp sea salt

Directions:
1. Heat oil in a dutch oven or a regular pan over medium heat. Add cumin,coriander, cinnamon and chili powder. Heat until fragrant.
2. Add chopped onion and cook until soft. Add garlic and cook a few minutes more.
3. Add cooked rice and rinsed beans, stock, chipotle, tomato paste and salt. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes. serve hot
