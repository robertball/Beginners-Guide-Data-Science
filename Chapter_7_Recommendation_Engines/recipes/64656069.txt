Title:
Alexander's Super Sweet Slurpees Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Cups of Ice
2 Cups of Water
1/4 to 1/2 Cup of Sugar
Any Type of Soda

Directions:
Pour 4 cups of ice into a blender. Pour the crushed ice into containers & place it in a freezer. Pour 2 cups of water into the empty blender. Add 1/4 to 1/2 cup of sugar to the water. Blend the mixture thoroughly. Pour 1 can of the chosen soda into the blender & blend for 10 seconds. Take the crushed ice out of the freezer and pour it into the blender. Blend for 15 seconds. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Alexander Petrie
Description: 
This recipe makes a super sweet mixture that has a sweet scent & flavor!
