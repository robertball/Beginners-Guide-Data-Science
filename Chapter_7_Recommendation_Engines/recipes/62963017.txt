Title:
Vegetable beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pound organic beef (you could use turkey/chicken breast)or leave the meat out
1 large onion
1/2 head cabbage (chopped)
2 cans green beans
1 can corn
3 stalks celery (chopped)
2/3 cup medium barley
3 cans cambells beef broth
1 can tomato paste

Directions:
Brown hamburger w/ onions, drain fat
add all ingredients and enough water to correct consistency. bring to boil and then simmer for hours.

Submitter: 
cookinmama
Description: 
This is my grandma's recipe. A wonderful dinner to enjoy on a cold night. You can serve this with warm cornbread.
