Title:
Fresno Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 small can chopped green chili
3/4 cup grated cheddar cheese
3/4 cup grated jack cheese
2 cups crushed tortilla chips
3 TBSP chopped onion
1/3 cup melted butter
1/4 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp ground cumin

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Pound chicken to 1/3 inch. Combine chilis, onions and cheeses. Divide  into 6 equal portions; placed in center of each chicken and roll chicken around filling.
Combine butter, chili  powder and cumin. Coat chicken with four and roll in butter mixture, roll in crushed tortilla chips. Arrange in a shallow greased dish. Bake 30 minutes
