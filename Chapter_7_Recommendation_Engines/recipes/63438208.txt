Title:
Vegan Easy UnCheesy Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/932948.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup Veganaise (Get this at any health food store)
1/3 cup Tahini Sauce
Water

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a sauce pan over low heat, stirring constantly. Add water to desired consistency.  Pour over broccoli, asparagus any cooked veggie.
