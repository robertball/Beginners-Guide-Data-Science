Title:
Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10oz) boxes of frozen chopped broccoli
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup mayo
2 eggs beaten
1 medium onion, chopped
salt/pepper to taste
2 pinches nutmeg
1 cup grated cheddar cheese
1/4 cup butter
1 sleeve Ritz crackers, crumbled

Directions:
Cook broccoli according to package.  Drain well.  Combine soup, mayo, eggs, onion, salt pepper, nutmeg.  Add to broccoli and pour mixture into 1 1/2 quart casserole dish.  Sprinkle with cheese on top and melted butter over cheese.  Spread crackers on top and bake, uncovered 45 minutes.
