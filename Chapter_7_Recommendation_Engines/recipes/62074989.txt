Title:
Dr. Pepper Pork Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 country style pork spare ribs
1 (2 liter) bottle Dr. Pepper, NOT diet
4 garlic cloves, slightly smashed
1 tablespoon liquid smoke
1 large onion, quartered
Your favorite barbecue sauce

Directions:
Place ribs in large stock pot. Pour Dr. Pepper over ribs to cover. Add garlic, liquid smoke and onion. Bring to boil, lower to simmer and cook until ribs are tender.
Place ribs in baking pan. Pour barbecue sauce over ribs. Bake at 350ÂºF for about 30 to 45 minutes.
Crock Pot Method: Mix sauce together pour over ribs in crock. Cook on high 2 hours turn to low cook for 4 to 6 more hours.
