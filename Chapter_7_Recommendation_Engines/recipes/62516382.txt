Title:
Green Curry Paste Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons coriander seeds
1 teaspoon cumin seeds
1 teaspoon balck papercorn
8 fresh green chillies,seeded and chopped
3 shallots,chopped
4 cloves garlic,crushed
2.5cm piece galangal,chopped
2 stalks lemon grass,choped
2 kaffir lime leaves,chopped
2 teaspoon shirimp paste
2 teaspoon chopped coriander leaves

Directions:
Heat a wok,add coriander and cumin seeds and heat until aroma rises.
using a pestle and mortar or small blender,crush coriandar and cumin seeds with peppercorns.
Add remaining ingreients and pound or mix to a smooth paste.Store in a airtight jar in the refrierator for up to 4 weeks.
Make about 8 tablespoons.

Submitter: 
Chef Manzoor Hassan Balghari
Description: 
this curry paste you can use for chicken.beaf ,fish,prwans,vegetables,squid,curries.The yield and hotness will vary according to the size and the heat of the chilles
