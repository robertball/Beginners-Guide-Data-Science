Title:
Brochette Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/854223.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs large shrimp
1 lb bacon, cut in half
1 fresh Poblano Pepper
1 pkg of sliced Provolone Cheese
1 tsp Garlic powder
1/2 cup butter
Prefer metal skewer but can use wooden skewer,
soaked in water

Directions:
Clean and devein shrimp, leaving the last section and tails intact.
With a paring knife, carefully slice each shrimp from tail to tip.
Wash and seed Poblano pepper, and cut into a pieces about 1/2-inch X 1/2-inch squares.
Cut slice of cheese into 12 small pieces.
Stuff one small piece of cheese and one small piece of pepper into each shrimp.
Wrap a half slice of bacon around each shrimp.
Place shrimp on skewer.
Repeat this process until all shrimp have been placed on skewers.
Place in refrigerator for a few minutes while you prepare the rest of the recipe.
Heat butter and garlic powder until butter is completely melted.
Prepare grill.
Remove shrimp from fridge and brush completely with melted butter.
Add seasoning to taste.
Place on hot grill, turn as necessary until shrimp are done.

Submitter: 
Tamala Henry
Description: 
Grilled shrimp with Poblano pepper,Provolone cheese and bacon makes a mouth-watering appetizer or meal.
