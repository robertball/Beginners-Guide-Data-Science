Title:
Nutella Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 qt. half and half
1 13 oz. jar Nutella
3/4 cup hazelnut liqueur*

Directions:
Heat cream over low heat.  While heating, whisk in Nutella.  Do not let this come to a boil, it is done when warmed up!  Add liqueur just before serving!
