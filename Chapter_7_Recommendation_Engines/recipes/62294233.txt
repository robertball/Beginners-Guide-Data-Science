Title:
Crispy Oven-Fried Fish Fillets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
vegetable cooking spray
4 (4 ounce) fillets white fish*
1/3 cup milk
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 tsp. seasoned salt
1/2 tsp. black pepper
2 cups panko bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F. Spray baking sheet with vegetable cooking spray.
Dip fish into milk. Coat with flour that has been mixed with seasoned salt and pepper and then dip in milk. Coat with panko bread crumbs.
Place fish on baking sheet. Spray fish with vegetable cooking spray.
Bake for 10 minutes or until fish is done.
