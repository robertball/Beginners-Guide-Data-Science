Title:
Confetti Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984798.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages refrigerated crescent dinner rolls
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
3 tablespoons mayonnaise
1/2 teaspoon McCormick速 Basil Leaves
1/4 teaspoon McCormick速 Garlic Powder
1 1/2 cups chopped vegetables (green and red bell pepper, carrots, broccoli)
2 tablespoons McCormick速 Salad Supreme Seasoning

Directions:
Press refrigerator crescent rolls into a 15x10x1-inch baking sheet to form a crust. Bake in 350 degree F oven 12-15 minutes.
Combine cream cheese, mayonnaise, basil leaves and garlic powder. Spread thinly over cooled crust. Top with chopped vegetables. Sprinkle generously with Salad Supreme.
Chill from 1 hour to overnight, or serve immediately.
