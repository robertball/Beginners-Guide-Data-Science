Title:
Cucumber, Tomato and Quinoa Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cooked quinoa (cooled)
1 cucumber
2 small tomatoes
2 green onions
4 oz feta cheese
6-8 sundried tomatoes pieces(in oil)
2 Tbs salted sunflower seeds
1/4 cup cilantro
3 Tbs olive oil (or oil from the sundried tomatoes)
juice from 1/2 medium lemon
freshly ground pepper

Directions:
Dice the cucumber and tomatoes. Slice the green onions thinly. Cut the sundried tomatoes into small pieces. Add to medium size mixing bowl.
Crumble feta over the vegetables. Add cooked quinoa and sunflower seeds.
Finely chop the cilatro and add to the salad.
Add the olive oil, lemon juice and ground pepper. Mix well.

Submitter: 
Lynn Pennec
Description: 
A light and healthy side salad for a barbecue or add some smoked tofu and serve on baby spinach for a whole meal.
