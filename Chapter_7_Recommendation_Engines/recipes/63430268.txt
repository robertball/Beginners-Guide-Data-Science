Title:
Blueberry Oat Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c flour
1 c oats
1/2 c sugar
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 egg
1/2 c water
1/3 c vegetable oil
1 c fresh or frozen blueberries
2 tbsp sugar
1/4 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients.
Beat wet ingredients and add to dry mixture.
Fold in blueberries and spoon into muffin tin.
Mix sugar and cinnamon and sprinkle a spoonful on each muffin.
Bake in greased muffin tin at 400 for 18-22 minutes.
