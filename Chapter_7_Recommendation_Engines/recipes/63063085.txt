Title:
Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1  cups butter
6 eggs
3 cups all-purpose flour
3 cups white sugar
3 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon almond or lemon extract
1 cup milk

Directions:
Grease and flour a tube or Bundt pan. Do not preheat oven.
In a medium bowl, mix flour, and baking powder . Set aside.
In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well each time. add vanilla.
Add flour mixture alternately with milk. Beat until smooth. Pour batter into tube or Bundt pan.
Place cake into cold oven, set the temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and bake for 60 to 70 minutes, or until toothpick inserted into center of cake comes out clean.
Top with confectioner's sugar or glaze once cake has completelt cooled.
