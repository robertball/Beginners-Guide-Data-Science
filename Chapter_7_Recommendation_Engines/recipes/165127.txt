Title:
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* Sesame Cheese Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986596.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup soft margarine or butter
2 cups finely shredded Cheddar cheese
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
3 cups KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES* cereal, crushed
2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds

Directions:
In large bowl with electric mixer, beat margarine and cheese on medium speed until light and fluffy. Mix in flour, cayenne and 300 mL (1 1/4 cups) of the crushed cereal.
In large plate or shallow pan, mix together remaining crushed cereal and sesame seeds. Using rounded teaspoonfuls, form cheese mixture into small balls; roll in sesame seed mixture to lightly coat. Place on ungreased baking sheet. Flatten with fork to 4 cm (1 1/2-inch) diameter, 5 mm (1/4-inch) thick.
Bake at 180 degrees C (350 degrees F) about 12 minutes or until golden brown. Transfer to wire racks; let cool. Store in airtight container.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*
Description: 
Make your own cheesy, crunchy crackers with crushed KELLOGG'S* RICE KRISPIES*, Cheddar cheese and sesame seeds.
