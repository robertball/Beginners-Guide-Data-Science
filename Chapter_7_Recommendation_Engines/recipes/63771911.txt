Title:
No Ketchup BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1045414.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup finely minced onion
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1/2 cup light flavored olive oil
1 10oz can tomato paste
1 14oz can tomato sauce
1/4 cup beef stock
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon chipotle chili powder
1 tablespoon ground cumin

Directions:
Mince 2 medium-sized onions.
Saute onions and garlic in olive oil on low-medium heat for about 10 minutes or until onions are translucent (do not brown).
Increase heat to medium. Add tomato paste and sauce and beef stock.  Bring to a boil.
Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil while stirring.
Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 30 minutes.
