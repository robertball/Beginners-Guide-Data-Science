Title:
Magic Cookie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
3 tbsp. sugar
1/2 cup margarine, melted
1 cup chocolate chips
1 1/3 cup coconut
1 cup coarsely chopped walnuts
1 can sweetened condensed milk (aka Eagle Brand)

Directions:
Mix crumbs, sugar and margarine.
Put crumbs in bottom of greased 9" x 13" pan.
Scatter chips, coconut and walnuts evenly over crumbs.
Pour milk over all.
Bake @ 350 x 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
One of my Mom's favorites when company's coming. It's a classic combo of graham crumbs, walnuts, chocolate, coconut & sweetened condensed milk.
