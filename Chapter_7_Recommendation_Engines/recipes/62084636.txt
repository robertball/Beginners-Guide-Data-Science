Title:
Grandma"s Slow Cooker Spagetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1Lb ground beef
2Tbsp chopped onion
1Tsp salt
1/4Tsp garlic powder
1 1/2Tsp Italian spice
1 10 oz tin mushrooms, drained
3Cups tomatoe juice
1 1/2 cups broken dry spagetti

Directions:
Saute beef & onions in skillet. Drain.
Place beef, onions and all other ingredients except spagetti into slow cooker. Cook on low 6 - 8 hrs. Or on high 3 1/2 hrs. In the last hour add dry spagetti making sure dry pasta is immersed in liquid.

Submitter: 
cynjne
Description: 
This was the first meal I ever tried that was cooked in a crock pot. Grandma served it with cabbage salad and was so pleased when grown-ups and children ate every little bit.
