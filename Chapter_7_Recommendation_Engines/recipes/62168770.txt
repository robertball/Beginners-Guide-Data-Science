Title:
Baked Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups dried macaroni
1/4 cup dried minced onion
dash pepper
3 T butter
3 T flour
1 T yellow mustard
3 cups milk
1/2 t paprika
1 egg
12 ounces medium cheddar cheese
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
2 sliced american cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Boil the macaroni and minced onion in salted water until al dente.  Drain and set aside.
In a separate sauce pan, melt butter and stir in flour with a small whisk.  Whisk till smooth on medium heat and add mustard.  Continue whisking for 2 more minutes
Add milk gradually (1/2 cup at a time stirring each time until smooth until all milk has been added. Simmer for 10 minutes.
Beat egg in a separate container, temper the egg by adding 1 tablespoon of sauce at a time whisking between each tablespoon, repeat about 8 times.  Add this egg mixture to sauce.
Remove sauce from heat
Add 3/4 of the cheddar cheese (reserving the remainder for top), parmesan and american cheeses.  Whisk until smooth.
Pour drained macaroni and onion into a 9x9 baking pan or casserole dish of your choice.  Top with remaining cheese.
Bake in 350 oven for 40 minutes, turn broiler on for last 5 minutes.
Let sit for 5 minutes before serving.
