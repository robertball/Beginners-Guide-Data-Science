Title:
Quick and Easy Chicken Enchilada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast
1 can cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1 can rotel
1 cup sour cream
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Spray baking dish with non stick cooking spray.  Preheat oven to 375.
Place chicken breasts in baking dish.
Combine soup, rotel, sour cream, and 3/4 cup of each cheese in a bowl.  Mix well.
Pour soup mixture over chicken.  Top with remaining cheese.
Bake uncovered 30 - 40 minutes, or until no longer pink in the center.

Submitter: 
Johanna Van Ness
Description: 
This created this quick and easy Chicken Enchilada recipe to go with my high protein, low carb diet.  I still get the great taste of chicken enchiladas without the carbs from the tortillas, and they are so easy to prepare.
