Title:
Heavenly Lobster Angel hair Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  tablespoons butter
2  tablespoons flour
2 cups milk
1/2  teaspoon Old Bay Seasoning
Pinch nutmeg
1-2  tablespoon olive oil
1 medium red onion, peeled & chopped
4  cloves garlic, peeled & minced
5  ounces snow peas, sliced in half
1 1/2  lbs fresh lobsters, cut into bite size chunks (or half lobster, half shrimp)-- shells removed, tails removed
1/2  teaspoon Old Bay Seasoning
3 T. heavy cream
1 T. Grated fresh Parmesan
2  tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
salt and pepper to taste
Optinoal:  Splash of Sherry
1  lb angel hair pasta, cooked as directed on box

Directions:
In a heavy sauce pot melt butter then add flour & stir til smooth and blended, then add milk, 1/2 old bay, and nutmeg stir and simmer till thickened on low heat.
Season cut up lobster/shrimp with Old Bay seasoning, then set aside.
In a separate hot large saute pan place oil, add red onion cook still softened add garlic, snow peas, shrimp and/or lobster and cook stirring till seafood turns pink (add more oil or butter if needed).  Add cream and Parmesan and stir to blend, then add parsley, salt and pepper and milk/butter sauce.
Stir pasta into seafood mixture and garnish with fresh parsley leaves.
Optional  Add a splash or two of Sherry before serving.

Submitter: 
Sherbg
Description: 
Rich, easy, garlick-y angel hair pasta dish with lobster, great enough for a company dish!  Serve with a green salad and a good baguette.
