Title:
Bubbling Cauldron Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pack (16 oz) pasturized process cheese spread
2 cans (15oz each) black beans well drained
1 cup medium or hot salsa
1 can (4oz) diced jalapeno peppers, drained (optional)
1 loaf (18oz) round marble rye bread unsliced
pretzel rods
cocktail rye or pumpernickel slices

Directions:
Melt cheese in a medium saucepan over low heat, stirring occasionaly.  Remove from heat.  Stir in beans, salsa and jalepenos if desired.  Carefully cut center of the the bread leaving a 1-1/2" shell.  Cut bread center into pieces for dipping.
Serve immediately.
