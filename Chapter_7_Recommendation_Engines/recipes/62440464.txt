Title:
Jim's  Awesome Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pound rump roast
1 tablespoon oil (or use Pam)
1 can condensed french onion soup
1 can condensed beef broth
1 package dry onion soup mix
1 package brown gravy mix
water
cornstarch rueue to thicken
carrots
potatoes
onions
mushrooms
celery

Directions:
Coat roast with mixes and brown roast in oil on all sides.  In a slow cooker, mix soup and  water. Place rump roast and veggies in cooker
Cook on Low setting for 8 to 9 hours.
