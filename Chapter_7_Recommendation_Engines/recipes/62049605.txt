Title:
Honey Peppered Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Top Loin Pork Chops
6Tbsps. Orange Blossom Honey
3Tbsps. Tabasco Pepper Sauce
1/2C. pineapple Juice
2Tbsps. Garlic, minced
1/4C. Onion, minced

Directions:
In a medium-size bowl mix together honey, tabasco, pineapple juice, garlic and onion.
Season chops with salt and black pepper and grill over a medium-hot fire for 5minutes per side or until done.
Baste chops during grilling. Remove chops from grill and brush with remaining glaze.
Serve hot, with steamed vegetables and stuffing!!
