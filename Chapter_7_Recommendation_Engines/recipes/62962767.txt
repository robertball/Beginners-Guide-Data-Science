Title:
Chicken Marsala Over White Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/731050.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken breast cutlets (approx 1 lb)
1/2 c. Marsala Wine
1/2 c. Chicken Stock
1 Tbsp Olive Oil
2 cups All purpose flour for dredging
1 garlic clove minced
1 cup sliced and washed mushrooms
Salt
Pepper
Oregano
2 Tbsp Butter
2 Cups of Rice(Cooked)

Directions:
1.  Pound chicken to about 1/4" thick.
2.  In large bowl put flour and add a decent amount of salt, pepper, and oregano for taste.(stir with fork to mix evenly.
3.  Place chicken in flour to coat.
4.  Heat olive oil and melt 1 Tbsp butter in large skillet on Medium-High heat.
5.  Add chicken and cook until each side is golden brown and cooked through.(approx 5-7 min)
6.  Remove chicken and place on paper towel lined plate to soak oil.
7.  Add the other Tbsp of Butter to skillet and while melting scrape the bottom with a wood spoon.
8.  Add garlic and cook on medium heat until browned.
9.  Add mushrooms and cook about 5-7 minutes until soft and moisture is out.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.
10. When mushrooms are cooked add marsala wine and chicken stock.  Stir.
11.  Carefully add chicken so that it gets covered in the mixture.
12.  Bring to boil and keep on medium-high heat for approx 3 minutes.  Stir occasionally.
13.  Cover and set heat to simmer for 5 minutes.
14.  Serve over or along side white rice.
15.  Enjoy
