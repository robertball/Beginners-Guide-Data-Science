Title:
Buttermilk Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/912287.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup white sugar
2 eggs less one yolk
1 cup buttermilk
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup cornmeal
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease .
Melt butter in an 8 inch square pan or large cast iron skillet. Remove from heat and pour butter into bowl.  Add remaining ingredients and lightly mix. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
