Title:
Strawberry Pretzel Jell-o Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c boiling water
1 Large pkg strawberry Jell-o (or 2 small)
1.5 c cold water
2 pints strawberries, thawed
2 c finely crushed pretzels
1/3 c sugar
2/3 c butter, melted
16 oz cream cheese, soft
1/4 c sugar
2T milk
8 oz whipped topping (cool whip)

Directions:
1.  Stir boiling water into the jello.  Stir in cold water.  Cool until thickened slightly.  Then mix in the strawberries. (this will take about an hour)
2.  Mix pretzels, sugar, and butter together.  Press in a 9 x 13 pan and bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes.  Cool.
3.  Beat cream cheese, sugar, and milk until smooth.  Gently stir in whipped topping.  Spread on coooled crust.  Chill in the refridgerator until the jello mixture is thickened.
4.  Gently spoon thickened jellow mixture on top of the cream cheese.  Refridgerate 3 hours or until firm.
