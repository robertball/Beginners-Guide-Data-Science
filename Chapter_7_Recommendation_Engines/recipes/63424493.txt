Title:
Fiesta Bean & Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBL olive oil
2 links turkey sausage, casings removed, cut into pieces
1 medium yellow onion, chopped (abt 1 cup)
1 small red bell pepper, chopped (abt 1/2 cup)
1/4 coup chopped green onions
3 cloves garlic, minced
4 coups reduced-sodium chicken broth
3/4 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp chile powder
2 cans (15 oz each) black beans, drained and rinsed
2 TBL chopped parsley
3 TBL lime juice
1/8-1/4 tsp hot red pepper sauce

Directions:
In large saucepan, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add sausage. Cook, stirring constantly, until browned, abt 5 minutes. Drain sausage on a paper towel-lined plate.
Wipe out saucepan. Add onion, bell pepper and green onions to saucepan. Cook, stirring until tender, abt 7 minutes. Add garlic. Cook, stirring, for 1 minute longer.
Add broth, cumin and chile powder to saucepan. Bring mixture to a simmer. Add beans and sausage. Cook over medium heat until thickened, abt 15 minutes. Stir in parsley, lime juice and red pepper sauce. Ladle soup into individual serving bowls. Serve immediately.
