Title:
Herman Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C plain flour
1/2 tsp Baking soda
2 tsp Baking powder
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp vainilla
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 C sugar
3/4 C melted butter
2 eggs
2 C Herman
1 C pecans
1 C raisins
TOPPING:
1 C brown sug
1 T cinnamon
1-2 T flour
1/4 C butter

Directions:
Preheat Oven: 350 ÂºF
Grease 10 x 14 pan.
Sift dry ingredients.
Beat in Herman, butter and eggs.
Add pecans and raisins.
Mix topping ingredients until crumbly and sprinkle over batter. Bake for 25 minutes.
