Title:
Baked Mixed Veggies with Blue Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
About 6 cups of Veggies cut into 1 inch pieces.
Bell peppers (about 4 - various colors to taste)
Onion - red (2)
pressed garlic (about 3 cloves)
tomatoes (1 package of cherry size)
zucchini (4-5 small cut up)
yellow squash
mushrooms (optional - can overwhelm the dish)
4 TBsp olive oil per 6 cup of veggies
1/2 tsp thyme
salt
pepper

Directions:
cut veggies into 1 inch pieces and put into bowl
add oil, thyme, salt, pepper and garlic
Mix well
lay out onto 2 baking sheets and put into pre-heated oven at 450 degrees for about 20-30 min until done.
Stir about every 10 min
Cool on the counter until room temp.
Add a generous sprinkle of blue cheese crumbles before serving.
** can be done well before guests arrive since served at room temp. :-)
Enjoy!
