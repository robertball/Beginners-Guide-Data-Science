Title:
Skinny Key Lime Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1117931.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (.6 ounce) package sugar-free lime-flavored gelatin (such as Jell-O®)
1/4 cup boiling water
2 (6 ounce) containers key lime yogurt
1 (8 ounce) container fat-free whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®)
1 (9 inch) reduced-fat graham cracker crust

Directions:
Dissolve lime gelatin in boiling water. Add yogurt, whisking constantly. Fold in whipped topping and spread mixture into the prepared crust. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
BubblyPaladin
Description: 
A friend served this scrumptious dessert to me one day and I was so surprised to find out what was in it. I am thinking many adaptations could be made with this recipe. Have fun and make some skinny pie!
