Title:
Dinner Party Stuffed Chicken breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless chicken breasts with skin still attached
1 box Uncle Ben's quick wild rice prepared
1 3 oz pkg cream cheese (softened)
1/2 c chopped walnuts
8 oz cranberry sauce with whole cranberries
olive oil to brush skin with
1/2 c light cream
1/2 c chicken broth

Directions:
preheat oven to 375 degrees
Combine wild rice, cream cheese, walnuts and cranberry sauce
Divide mixture into 6 portions
Tuck 1 portion of mixture between the skin and the meat of each chicken breast
Place stuffed chicken breasts skin side up in roasting dish
Brush skin with olive oil
Dump chicken broth in bottom of roasting dish
Bake 35 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink
Remove chicken to serving platter
Add light cream to the bottom of roasting pan
Place roasting pan on burner and reduce sauce, stirring constantly to 1/4
Drizzle chicken breasts with sauce
Serve
Breasts can be stuffed and refrigerated one day in advance and put in the oven to bake when guests arrive.
Serve with steamed broccoli and steamed asparagus.

Submitter: 
VTOtter
Description: 
Great for dinner parties can be prepped in advance! Boneless chicken breasts with skin still attached are stuffed with cream cheese, walnuts, wild rice and cranberries and then drizzled with a light sauce.
