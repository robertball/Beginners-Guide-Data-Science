Title:
Chocolate Chip Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup Oreos, finely crushed (about 19, with icing scraped off)
1/4 cup butter, melted
3 packages of cream cheese (8 oz each), softened
1 can sweetened condensed milk (14 oz)
3 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
1 cup chocolate chips
1 tsp flour

Directions:
Combine crumbs and melted butter and press firmly on the bottom of a 9x13 pan.
Preheat oven to 300 degrees.
In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy.
Add sweetened condensed milk and beat until smooth.
Add eggs and vanilla.
In a small bowl, toss 1/2 cup chocolate chips with flour to coat and then stir into the cheese mixture.
Pour on top of oreo crust.
Sprinkle remaining chocolate chips on top.
Bake at 300 degrees for 50 minutes or until cake springs back when touched.
