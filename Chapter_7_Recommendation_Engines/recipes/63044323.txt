Title:
Venison Winter Vegetable Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium vidalia onion
2 tbsp. olive oil
2 lbs. venison meat, cubed in 1 inch pieces
1 medium turnip (rutebaga) peeled and diced
2 large carrots, peeled and diced
20 new potatoes, halved
1 bottle (750ml) good red wine
1 box(900ml) low sodium beef stock
flour to dredge meat
1 cup frozen peas
1 large sprig fresh rosemary
3 tsp fresh thyme
salt & pepper to taste
2 tbsp butter

Directions:
In a heavy stock pot or dutch oven, saute onions in olive oil over medium heat.  Once onions are cooked through, remove from pot.  Dredge venison pieces in flour. Sear, adding more oil if needed.  Once venison is browned, deglaze pot with red wine.  Add onions, turnip, carrots, beef stock, rosemary, thyme, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer for 25 minutes.  Add halved potatoes and simmer for an additional 15 minutes. Add green peas and simmer for an additional 5 minutes or until potatoes are fork tender. Finish with 2 tbsp. of butter, melted into stew.  Serve with crusty bread.

Submitter: 
Ellen Violet-Muir
Description: 
Venison that will melt in your mouth paired with comforting vegetables and hearty gravy for a cold winter's night.
