Title:
Tomato-Baked Tilapia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 Tablespoons onion, minced
2 Tablespoons olive oil
1 can petite diced tomatoes (14.5 oz)
2 Tablespoons dry white wine
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
salt and pepper to taste
2 tilapia fillets
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray 8x8 glass baking dish with cooking oil spray and set aside.
In an 8" skillet over medium heat, saute onion and garlic in olive oil until just tender. Add tomatoes, wine, basil, Italian seasoning, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil and cook for 2 minutes.
Sprinkle fillets with salt and pepper and lay in baking dish. Pour tomatoes over fish. Cover and bake 20 minutes in preheated oven.
After removing from oven, sprinkle on cheese. Replace cover and allow cheese to melt before serving.

Submitter: 
Pam Ziegler Lutz
Description: 
This quick and easy dish uses every day ingredients to impress. Serve over angel hair pasta for a quick meal any night of the week.
