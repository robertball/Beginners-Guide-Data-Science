Title:
Blueberry Cream Cheese Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Box of yellow or white cake mix
icing:
1 8oz cheese
1 tub of cool whip
1 box of confecionate sugar
1/4 tsp vanilla flavoring
filling:
1/2 cup Sugar
1 tsp cornstarch
1/3 cup water
2 cup fresh or frozen blueberries

Directions:
Make directed and cool. Split each layer diagonally.
For Icing:
Mix cream cheese and sugar until well blended.
Add flavoring to mixure and blend.
Fold cool whip and spread over layer of cake.
For Filling:
1/2 cup sugar
1 tbsp cornstarch
1/3 cup water
2 cups blueberries
Combine sugar and cornstarch; gradually stir water add blueberries bring to boil over med heat. Boil 1 min stirring occassionally. Can be used for pancakes or topping on any favorite recipe.

A sweet and simple cake that is perfect for any occassion.
