Title:
Homemade Rosemary Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless chicken breasts
olive oil
2 tbs. fresh rosemary, minced
2 tbs. fresh parsley, minced
salt & pepper (to taste)
2/3 c. chicken broth
1 clove garlic, minced
1/3 med. onion, diced
1/3 c. butter
1/3 c. flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 3/4 c. chicken broth
2/3 c. milk
8 oz. frozen mixed vegetables, thawed
2 prepared pie crusts

Directions:
preheat oven according to pie crust recipe.
Place one crust in the pie pan. Poke several holes in the bottom of the crust and bake for 5 minutes. let cool
place medium skillet over medium-high heat and drizzle with olive oil
pound chicken breast flat and season each side with salt, pepper, rosemary & parsley.
Sautée chicken breast until golden brown, 2-4 minutes per side.
turn heat to med-low. Add 2/3 c. chicken broth and simmer chicken just until juices run clear, 10-15 mins. (do not overcook).
Remove chicken from pan. Save drippings. Allow chicken to cool and juices to settle, about 15 mins.
Meanwhile, melt butter in skillet over medium heat. Add chicken drippings. Sautée garlic until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Add onion, salt & pepper. Cook until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes. Whisk in flour until mixture has thickened.
Add remaining chicken broth and milk to skillet, one cup of liquid at a time, whisking constantly. Remove from heat once mixture begins to thicken.
Combine chicken, vegetables and broth/milk mixture.
Pour into pre-baked pie crust.
Cut slits in the remaining pie crust and top the pie, crimping to crease the edges. use tongs or a fork to decorate the edges of the pie.
Bake according to pie crust recipe, until crust is golden brown. Check halfway through. If edges begin to turn brown, cover them with a thin strip of aluminum foil.
