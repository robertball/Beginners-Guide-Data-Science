Title:
White Chocolate Candy Cane Drops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/311461.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz. white chocolate baking squares
1/2 butter, softened
1 cup white sugar
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
2 3/4 cups flour
2/3 cup finely crushed peppermint candy canes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Melt 4 oz. white chocolate in double boiler, cool slightly
In large bowl beat butter on medium speed for 30 seconds.  Add sugar, baking powder and salt.  Beat until combined.  Beat in eggs and vanilla.  Add melted white chocolate.  Add flour slowly, ending with stirring with wooden spoon.  Stir in remaining 4 oz. of white chocolate, chopped, and crushed candy canes.  Drop by tablespoons onto parchment-lined cookie sheets.
Bake for 9 minutes until lightly browned.  Transfer to wire rack to cool.

Submitter:

Description:
