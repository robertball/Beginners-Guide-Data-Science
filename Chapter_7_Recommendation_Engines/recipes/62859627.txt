Title:
Cabbage-Carrot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/684581.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs. thinly sliced cabbage
2 large carrots, grated
2 cups water
2 tsp. Better than Bouillon, chicken flavor (or 2 bouillon cubes)
2 T. butter
2 T. plain flour
1/2 cup reserved cooking liquid
1/2 cup milk
2-4 oz. sharp cheddar or American cheese
paprika

Directions:
Cook the cabbage and carrots in the seasoned water until tender, about 20-30 minutes, stirring every now and then.
Drain well, reserving 1/2 cup of the cooking liquid.
Put into an 8 x 11-inch casserole dish; set aside.
In a separate saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
Add flour and stir well for 1 minute.
Add reserved cooking liquid and milk and stir well.
Add cheese and stir till thickened.
Pour cheese sauce over cabbage mixture and lightly stir until sauce is incorporated in vegetables.
Lightly sprinkle with paprika.
Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 20-30 minutes or till hot and bubbly.
