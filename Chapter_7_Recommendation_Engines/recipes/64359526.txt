Title:
Moroccan Couscous with Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts, skinless & boneless
1 tsp Lemon & black pepper seasoning
3 Tbsp oil
1 medium onion, chopped
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp turmeric
200 ml Ina Parmaan tomato & chili sauce
4 chicken bouillon cubes
2 cups boiling water
1 cup couscous
1/2 cup toasted almond pieces

Directions:
Slit breasts lengthways and open.  Season with Lemon & Pepper seasoning and coat with oil.  Briefly cook in non stick frypan.  Remove from pan and cut into fine slices.
In same frypan, saute the onion in remaining oil until soft and golden.  Add spices and cook over low heat for about 30 seconds.
Add tomato sauce, bouillon, water and couscous.  Bring to a boil for 2 minutes and let stand for 5 minutes.
Fork the sliced chicken through the couscous. Reheat briefly.  Sprinkle with almonds before serving.
