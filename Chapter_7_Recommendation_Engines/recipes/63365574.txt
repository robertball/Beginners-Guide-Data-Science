Title:
Skillet Chicken Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup reduced fat sour cream
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
2 Tbsp chopped pickled jalapeno slices
2 tsp chopped onion
2 tsp Dijon Mustard
1 tsp grated lime peel
Burritos
2 cups cubed cooked chicken breast
1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
1 can mexicorn, drained
1 cup shredded cheddar
1/4 tsp salt
8 whole wheat tortillas (8in), warmed
Cooking spray
Salsa, optional

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the first six ingredients. In a large bowl, combine the chicken, beans, corn, cheese, salt and 1/2 cup sour cream mixture. Spook 1/2 cup chicken mixture on each tortilla. Fold sides and ends over filling and roll up. Spritz both sides with cooking spray.
In a large nonstick skillet or griddle coated with cooking spray, cook burritos in batches over medium heat for 3-4 minutes on each side or until golden brown. Serve with remaining sour cream mixture and salsa if desired.
