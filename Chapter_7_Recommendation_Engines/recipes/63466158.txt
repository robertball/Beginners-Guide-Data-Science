Title:
Cheesy/Creamy broccoli soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T butter
1 T olive Oil
1 onion, chopped
2 carrots, diced
2 stalks celery, diced
6 cups chopped broccoli
2 cloves garlic, minced
3 small red potatoes, diced
3 cups chicken broth
4 cups milk
salt and pepper to taste
1 T Worcestershire
dash nutmeg
1/2 c Parmesan
1 C shredded cheddar
2 slices American
Optional 1-2 C diced ham

Directions:
In a large stock pot, add butter and oil. Saute vegetables until soft.
Add chicken broth and garlic.  Bring to a boil.
Add potatoes, cover and cook for 5 to 10 minutes or until tender.
Use stick blender to puree soup.  Add milk, cheeses, nutmeg, Worcestershire, salt and pepper, and heat through over medium heat.
(If using ham, add with milk)

Submitter: 
Trish Megyesi
Description: 
Carrots, onion, celery and broccoli cooked in chicken broth are pureed with milk and cheese in this straight creamy/cheesy vegetable soup.Modified from: Cream of Broccoli Soup III
