Title:
Ultimate Breakfast Casserole - Small Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 eggs, beaten
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
8 ounces sour cream
1/2 (20 ounce) package fresh hash brown potatoes
1/2 pound bulk breakfast sausage, cooked
1 cup cooked ham, cubed
1/2 onion, chopped (or minced from the pantry)
4 ounces shredded Velveeta Cheddar cheese
4 ounces regular Velveeta Cheddar cheese
1/8 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
Season beaten eggs with pepper; stir in ham, sausage and 4 oz of cheddar cheese. Pour into baking dish.
In a large bowl, combine soup, sour cream, hash browns, onion and 4 oz of cheese. Mix thoroughly and pour into prepared baking dish.
Bake uncovered for 30-45 minutes. Remove and drizzle butter over top of casserole. Return to oven and bake for an additional 15 minutes, until golden.

Submitter: 
Cori
Description: 
This is a quick make ahead breakfast casserole that reminds me a bit of one of my favorite hash brown casserole recipes.
