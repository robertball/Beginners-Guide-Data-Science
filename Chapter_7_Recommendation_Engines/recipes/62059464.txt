Title:
Red Thai curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp vegetable oil
1 tbsp Red Thai curry
1 inch piece of fresh ginger, julienned: sliced in matchsticks.
1 orange or yellow pepper, sliced into thin strips
1 red pepper, sliced into thin strips
1 green pepper, sliced into thin strips
1 onion, sliced
1/2 can (14 oz) coconut milk
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast sliced in thin strips (about 2"long by 1/2" thick),(optional).

Directions:
Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or deep saute pan.  Add the Red Thai curry and saute for 1 minute.  If you are using chicken, add the chicken and cook until almost cooked (2-3 minutes).  Add the peppers and onion.  Cook until vegetables start to soften (2-3 minutes).  Add coconut milk and let simmer about 5 minutes.  Serve over sticky rice.

Submitter: 
Stephanie Morissette
Description: 
This is a super easy, fast and tasty recipe that can be vegetarian or made with chicken.  Coconut milk makes it taste authentic.  Serve this curry over sticky rice.
