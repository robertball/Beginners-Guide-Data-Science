Title:
Dipping sauce for artichokes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz sour cream
1/4 red onion finely minced
2 tbsp capers minced
1 tbsp dijon mustard
1 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and start dipping!

Submitter: 
Jen
Description: 
I love artichokes but have never liked mayonnaise. I came up with this dipping sauce to replace the usual mayo dip.
