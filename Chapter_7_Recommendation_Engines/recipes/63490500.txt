Title:
Vicki's SALMON PARTY BALL Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/952535.jpg
Ingredients:
Salmon Ball ingredients
1 (10oz-2 cups) Salmon drained/flaked
8 oz soft Cream cheese
1 tbsp lemon juice
2 tsp grated onion
1 tsp prepared horseradish
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp Liquid Smoke
NUT MIXTURE
1/3 cup chopped pecans
3 tbsp dried parsley

Directions:
Combine cream cheese with lemon juice, onion, horseradish, salt & liquid smoke.  Add flaked salmon.  Chill 2 hours to several hours
Mix pecans and parsley together and roll the salmon ball into the nut mixture

Submitter: 
Vicki Wilson
Description: 
This is one of my favorite appietizers.  My sister gave me this recipe over 40 years ago.  I make this on every holiday and I am asked by my staff to bring for our potlucks at the hospital. I am asked for the recipes all the time.
