Title:
Krista's Chocolate Chip Pecan Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C all-purpose flour
1 1/2 C cake flour
1 1/2 t. salt
1 1/2 t. baking soda
1 1/2 C. unsalted butter, softened
1 1/4 C packed brown sugar
1 C granulated sugar
4 eggs
1 T vanilla
2 12-oz pkgs. semisweet chocolate pieces (4 C total)
3 C chopped pecans or walnuts

Directions:
In med bowl, mix flours, salt and baking soda.
In very large bowl, beat butter w/electric mixer 30 sec. Add sugars and beat until combined. Add eggs, one at a time, beating after each until combined. Beat in vanilla. Beat in a much of flour as you can with mixer on low. Stir in remaining flour with wooden spoon. Stir in chocolate and nuts.
Drop by rounded T or by using small cookie scoop 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake in 375 oven 9-11 min. or until edges are light brown and center is set (may appear underbaked). Remove from oven. Cool on cookie sheets 1 min. Remove, cool on wire racks

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Makes 78 cookies. Dough can be frozen but shape into balls first. Bake a little longer than regular dough until done.
