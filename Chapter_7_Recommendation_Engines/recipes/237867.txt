Title:
Mixed Fruit Daiquiri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1210166.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups ice cubes
2 (10 ounce) cans frozen strawberry daiquiri mixer
1 apple, cut into chunks
1/4 fresh pineapple, cut into chunks
1/2 cup mango chunks
1/2 orange, peeled and segmented
2 tablespoons lime juice
3 cups white rum (such as BacardiÂ®)

Directions:
Blend ice and daiquiri mixer together in a blender until smooth. Add apple, pineapple, mango, orange, and lime juice; blend until smooth. Pour rum into daiquiri mixture and blend well.
