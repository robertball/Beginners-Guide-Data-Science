Title:
Holiday Coffee Liquor Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups sugar
4 cups boiling hot water
2/3 cup instant coffee powder
2 cups vodka
3 Tbs vanilla

Directions:
Mix together all the ingredients and allow to cool before bottling.
