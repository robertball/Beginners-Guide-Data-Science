Title:
Homemade Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/3 cup onion
1 28oz can peeled, crushed tomatoes
1 tsp dried basil
1 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp dried parsley
1/4 tsp dried rosemary
1/8 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1 1/2 tbsp sugar

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, saute garlic and onion in olive oil until onion is tender.
Add the can of crushed tomatoes, the spices, and the sugar. Stir a few times until blended.
Cover and let sauce simmer on low heat for at least 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
youngwoman
Description: 
This is what I make instead of jarred tomato sauce from the grocery store! I'm not sure if the spice measurements are exactly right because I usually eyeball and adjust, but I'm hoping this is pretty close.
