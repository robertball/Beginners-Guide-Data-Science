Title:
Crowd-Pleasing Bacon-Wrapped Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. fresh cocktail shrimp
1/2 lb. thick-cut bacon
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup soy sauce
wooden toothpicks

Directions:
Whisk together brown sugar and soy sauce.
Clean shrimp and place in a medium bowl.
Pour brown sugar mixture over top and marinate in refrigerate for 30 minutes.
Meanwhile, cut each strip of bacon in half and cook partially (should still be pink and flexible).  Let cool.
Preheat oven to 375.
Wrap one bacon strip around a shrimp and secure with a toothpick.  Lay out on a jelly roll pan and brush with remaining marinade.
Bake for 15 minutes, until shrimp is pink and bacon is crisp.  Serve warm.
