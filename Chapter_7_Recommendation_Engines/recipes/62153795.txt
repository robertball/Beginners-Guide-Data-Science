Title:
Blackberry Chocolate Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 prepared devils food cake
2 pints heavy whipping cream
1/2 cup confectioners sugar
1 tbsp vanilla
1 box chocolate mousse mix
2 cups chocolate chips (semi sweet)
2 cans sweetened condensed milk
3 tbsp butter
3-4 containers fresh blackberries (set aside 1/2 of 1 container for the topping)
Trifle pan

Directions:
Either bake or buy a devils food cake (box cake works perfectly fine)
Cut the cake into 1 inch cubes, set aside
Heat a medium sauce pan over med-low heat. Add the butter to the pan and let it melt. Add the 2 cups chocolate chips to the butter, turn to coat. Stir in 1 can of sweetened condensed milk. Stir the chocolate over low heat until completely melted. Add half of the second can of sweetened condensed milk, stir until thoroughly combined. Set the chocolate sauce aside.
In a large mixing bowl beat 1 pint of the heavy whipping cream with the confectioners sugar and vanilla. Beat until stiff peaks form.
In a seperate mixing bowl, prepare the mousse mix as directed subsituting the heavy whipping cream for milk.
In the trifle pan: place a layer of chocolate cake in the bottom of the pan. Pour/drizzle some of the chocolate sauce over the cake. Layer the whipped cream, mousse, blackberries and cake until the pan is full. Make sure the top layer is either mousse or whipped cream. Dip the remaining blackberries in the remaining chocolate sauce and arrange them on top of the trifle. Serve with blackberry ice cream.
