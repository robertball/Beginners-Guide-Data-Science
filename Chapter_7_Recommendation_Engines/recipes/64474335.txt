Title:
Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2518531.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean sirloin or round steak thin sliced
1 tablespoon oil
4 minced garlic cloves
1 chopped onion
8-9 drops Tabasco sauce
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup dry red wine, or cooking sherry
1 bay leaf
2 cups sliced fresh button mushrooms
1 10 ounce can Campbell's cream of Mushroom soup
1 10 ounce can Campbell's tomato soup
1-1/2 cup sour cream (regular or light)
salt and pepper to taste
dash of rosemary herb
1 pound thin spaghetti noodles, or linguini cooked to desired texture

Directions:
First heat oil in large saucepan/skillet, add garlic and onion to brown along with thin sliced beef steak, then add mushrooms, bay leaf, rosemary, Sherry or wine, Worcestershire and Tabasco. Cover and simmer low to reduce liquid by about half. Add mushroom and tomato soups, blend in well, cover and simmer about 20 minutes stirring occasionally on low/medium heat. Turn off heat, add sour cream, blend in well to mixture. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Warm low temperature to heat thru and not curdle sour cream in sauce. Remove bay leaf before serving sauce.  Boil pasta as directed, strain and pour a fair amount of sauce over each serving of pasta or noodles.
