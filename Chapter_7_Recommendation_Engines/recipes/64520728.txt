Title:
Chicken Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2  14.5 oz cans of chicken broth
2 cups water
1/2 cup uncooked brown rice
1 1/4 cup sliced carrots
1/2 cup sliced celery
1/2 cup chopped onion
3 teaspoons minced garlic
2 tbs butter
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp poultry seasoning
2 cups rotisserie chicken cut up
4-5 oz cream cheese

Directions:
Combine the broth, water, and rice in a large pot and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover & simmer for a half hour.
Saute onions, garlic, carrots, and celery in 2 tbs butter then add to the rice.
Add the rest of the seasonings and the chicken.  Simmer for 15 minutes.
Add the cream cheese and stir before serving.
