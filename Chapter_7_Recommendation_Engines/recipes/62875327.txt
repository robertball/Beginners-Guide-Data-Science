Title:
King Ranch Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Chicken, boiled and deboned
Corn Chips
1 Onion, diced
1 Bell Pepper, diced
1 Tbsp. Chili Powder
1 can Rotel tomatoes
1/2 cup Milk
1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
1/2 lb Cheddar Cheese, grated

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°.
Line the bottom of a 9" x 12" pan with corn chips.
In a large bowl, mix together onion, bell pepper, chili powder, Rotel, milk and soups.
Place chicken on top of chips.  Pour above mixture over chicken.  Top with cheese
Bake 30 minutes at 350° until hot and bubbly.

Kevin G
Description: 
Boil and debone chicken ahead of time.  Save broth for later use.
