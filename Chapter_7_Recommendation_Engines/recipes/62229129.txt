Title:
Parmesan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg, lightly beaten
1/3 cup flour
1 cup italian bread crumbs
4 T butter
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
1 # chicken breasts, boneless skinless

Directions:
Require 3 bowls:
1) mix egg and 2 T water
2) flour, salt, and pepper
3) bread crumbs and cheese
Dredge chicken in flour, then egg mix, then bread crumbs;  let rest 5"
In the interim, heat butter in skillet over medium heat until foamy;  Add chicken and cook until done.  Serve with a side of pasta and Johnny Sauce

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Lightly fried chicken breasts coated in Italian seasonings.  Serve with a side of pasta and salad.
