Title:
Ginger Coconut Soup By Marie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5054331.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup cooked Trader Joes Quick Cook Organic Basmati Brown Rice
1 T Red Quinoa for rice
1/2 tsp Herbs of Provence for rice
1 small Clove Garlic minced for rice
1/2 cube Low Sodium Veggie Bouillon for rice
1 cup water for rice
***********************************
1 small Jalapeno diced seeds and stems removed
1 large Carrot diced
2 Stalks Celery diced
1 bunch Green Onions sliced
5 cups Water heated in kettle
2 cans Full Fat Coconut Milk
4 large leaves Fresh Basil cut with scissors
4 ounces Fresh Mushrooms sliced
1/2 cup Frozen Green peas
7 ounces Firm Tofu crumbled
2 T Coconut Oil for frying
1 tsp Turmeric
1/4 tsp Powdered Ginger
1/4 tsp Onion Powder
Black Pepper fine grind to taste
Unrefined salt such as Himalayan Salt to taste

Directions:
Cook 1/2 cup Trader Joes Quick Cook Organic Basmati Brown Rice and Quinoa in 1 cup water with the seasonings listed.  Fluff with fork and put aside.  In a large pot add Coconut Oil on medium high heat.  Add Green Onions , Ginger, and Jalapeno when they start to sizzle add sliced Mushrooms and stir fry.  When Mushrooms are cooked add Celery and Carrots.  Continue stir fry cooking until slightly softened.  Add Tofu, Turmeric, Onion Powder, and Powdered Ginger.  Mix well and heat through (about 3 minutes).  Add 5 cups preheated Water (if you have a kettle).  Add Red Chili Flakes, Salt and Black Pepper and let come to a boil.  Then turn heat down to simmer.  Add Coconut Milk, Peas, Cooked Rice, and Fresh Basil Leaves using scissors cut them in.  Stir and allow to come to a simmer and turn off heat.  Let rest  5 minutes while remaining on burner and serve.
