Title:
Pizza Style Cheese on Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tomato - finely chopped
½ Onion - finely chopped
120g/4oz Cheddar/Parmesan Cheese mix - grated
30g/1oz cooked Ham - shredded
Pinch Ground Coriander
Small Piece (knob) Butter
1½tbsp medley of fresh herbs (Oregano/Basil/Parsley) - finely chopped
2 slices Ciabatta/Chapata Bread

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a bowl;
Toast bread on both sides;
Spread cheese/ham mixture on toasted slices, making sure toast is completely covered;
Place under grill until cheese bubbles and browns slightly;
Serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
A packed with flavour snack for the pizza lover. The body and mind join together for a taste fest.
