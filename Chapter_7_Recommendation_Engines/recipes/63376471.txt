Title:
Italian Meatloaf 1 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 LB 93% Ground beef
1 cup italian bread crumbs
4 TBS Italian seasoning
2 Cloves of garlic, minced
1/2 White onion, chopped
4 TBS Oregano
2 eggs
1/2 C Grated cheese
1 can tomato paste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400...Mix everything except half a can of tomato paste together, form into a loaf. Cover with the other half of tomato paste and a little sprinkle of Italian seasoning. Put in a pan, and cook for 45 minutes or until fully cooked. You can also make them into smaller loaves and it will take a bit shorter to cook.
