Title:
Low Fat Zesty  Baked Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large boneless chicken breast cut into strips
For the chicken marinade:
3 T Olive oil
1 T Dijon Mustard
dash of cayenne pepper
dash of paprika
1 teas finely minced garlic
1/2 teas salt
1/2 teas pepper
1 T lemon juice
1/2 teas Lemon zest
1/2 C Plain yogurt
For the breading:
1 Cup Parmesan Cheese
6 T Fresh Flat Leaf Italian Parsley, chopped
1 1/4 C Panko crumbs.
Dip:
1 Cup Plain Yogurt
1 tsp chopped fresh parsley
1/4 tsp lemon zest
1 tsp lemon juice
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/8 tsp Worcestershire sauce
Honey Mustard Dip
1 Cup Plain Yogurt
3 T Dijon Mustard
3 T Honey or to taste
1 tsp poppy seeds.

Directions:
Combine the marinade ingredients in a zip lock bag and add the chicken.  Marinate at least 1 hour up to 24 hours.
Combine all the ingredients for the dipping sauce and refrigerate.
Prepare a baking sheet with parchment paper sprayed with cooking spray.
Pre heat oven to 425
Combine the breading ingredients.
Dip marinated chicken into the breading, turn and coat well.  Place on prepared baking sheet.
Spray the top of the breaded chicken with cooking spray.
Bake on lowest rack, 20 mins.  Turn chicken over and bake for another 10 mins.

Submitter: 
Baking Nana
Description: 
Low calorie, low fat doesn't need to be boring. This is a great dinner dinner with tons of flavor.
