Title:
Fireside Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Non Dairy Creamer
1 1/2 Cups Instant Coffee (decaf can also be used)
1 1/2 Cups White Sugar
1 1/2 Cups Hot Cocoa Mix (or 7 individual packets)
1 1/2 tsp. Ground Cinnamon
1/2 tsp. Ground Nutmeg

Directions:
Mix all ingredients well and store in airtight container.
When ready to drink, use 2-3 heaping spoonfuls in a mug of hot water.

Submitter: 
TammyC24
Description: 
Warm and soothing hot drink that is a relaxing companion.  Great for camping, hiking, or just to unwind the stress of your day.
