Title:
Jess's Carmel Coated Walnuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/556290.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups of whole walnuts unsalted
1 cup of brown sugar
1 stick of butter
1/4 cup of white corn syrup
1/8 teaspoon of cream of tartar
1/4 teaspoon of sea salt
1/2 teaspoon of baking soda

Directions:
mix together brown sugar, butter, sea salt and white corn syrup.
bring to a boil for 3 minutes stirring often. Remove from heat.
add baking soda and stir well until settles
Pour over walnuts and stir until well coated
Bake at 200 degrees for 1.5 hours stirring every 15 minutes
