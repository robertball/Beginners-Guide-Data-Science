Title:
Creamy Mediterranean Style Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ronzoni Garden Delight, 12 oz
*Chicken Alfredo-Classico Sauce, 2 cup
Oil and Vinegar Salad Dressing, .25 cup
Cider Vinegar, .25 cup
*Kraft Fat Free Italian Salad Dressing, 2 tbsp
Spinach, fresh, 2 cup
Red Ripe Tomatoes, 1 cup cherry tomatoes
Yellow Peppers (bell peppers), 20 strips
*Lindsey Sliced Black Olives, 10 tbsp
Feta Cheese, 6 oz

Directions:
Cook pasta as directed.
In large bowl, mix sauce, vinegar, and dressing and blend.
Add cooked pasta, spinach, tomatoes, and peppers and toss well.
Refrigerate 30-60 minutes and serve.
