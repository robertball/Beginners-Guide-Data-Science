Title:
Red Beans and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked long-grain white rice
2 cups water
2 tablespoons butter
1 onion, chopped
1/2 green bell pepper, chopped
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 cumin
1/2 tsp cayenne
16 ounces smoked sausage, thinly sliced
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained

Directions:
Bring the rice and water to boil in a pot. Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer 20 minutes.
Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in onion and green bell pepper, and cook until tender. Stir in sausage, and cook until evenly browned. Mix in beans and tomatoes, and seasonings and continue cooking until heated through. Serve skillet mixture over the rice.

Submitter: 
Diane Manzella
Description: 
Here's a really quick and easy way to enjoy red beans and rice. Hearty slices of smoked sausage round out this dish. Top it with your favorite hot sauce.Modified from: Quick and Easy Red Beans and Rice
