Title:
Spaghetti Pizza Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground meat (I like turkey)
1 box uncooked spaghetti noodles
½ teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon Oregano
½ teaspoon garlic powder
½ Cup Milk
1 Egg
2 ounces sliced pepperoni (I like Turkey)
1 (26 ounce) jar pasta sauce
1 can diced Italian style tomatoes
¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 (8 ounce) package shredded Italian cheese blend
Any other pizza toppings, you can add like Black olives, sausage, onions, green peppers, etc...

Directions:
1. Boil water & salt for Spaghetti noodles, once it starts boiling, add noodles.
2. Brown meat in a separate frying pan. Once noodles are cooked, drain and put in a casserole dish.
3. In a separate bowl, combine milk & egg & whisk. Poor over pasta & add jar of sauce, can of tomatoes, garlic powder, & oregano. Mix all together well.
4. On top of pasta mixture, layer the ground meat, then add a layer of pepperoni's. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese, & Italian cheese. And layer more pepperoni. 5. Bake in the oven at 350 for 30 minutes.
