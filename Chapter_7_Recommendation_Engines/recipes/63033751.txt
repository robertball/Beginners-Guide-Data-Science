Title:
Lindsey's Eggplant Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/761398.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Eggplants
Italian Seasoned Bread Crumbs
Egg
Lindsey's Red Sauce:
3-4 garlic cloves, minced
1 onion, chopped
mushrooms, thinly sliced
30 oz. can crushed tomatoes with liquid
16 oz. whole tomatoes with liquid
jar roasted red pepper
1-2 Tbsp sugar
1/4 cup olive oil
basil
oregano
2 cups water
Mozzarella

Directions:
Slice eggplant.
Salt liberally to draw out the excess moisture.
Coat with breadcrumbs.
Dip in egg.
Dip in breadcrumbs again.
Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes. Turn over and cook 10 minutes more.
Layer bread crumbs with sauce and cheese 2-3 times ending with lots of sauce and cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until browned and bubbly.
Serve with buttered and garlic-ed noodles.
