Title:
Honey Brie Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
mini pastry shells
Brie cheese
1/4 cup honey
4 Tbsp. freshly squeezed orange juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.  Cut Brie into cubes large enough to fill centre of cups.  Place in oven 9 to 12 minutes or until crust is golden brown  Mix honey and orange juice.  Once you remove the cups from the oven, drizzle orange honey generously over up.

Submitter: 
Here's To Good Friends
Description: 
So easy to make ahead of time.  Just reheat to serve.
