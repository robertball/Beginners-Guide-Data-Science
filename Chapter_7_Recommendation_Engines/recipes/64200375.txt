Title:
Lentil Soup with Ham and Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz chopped bacon
8 cups beef broth
1 1/2 lbs dried lentils, rinsed and sorted
2 cups chopped ham
1 cup chopped carrots
3/4 cup chopped celery
3/4 cup chopped tomatoes
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 tsp salt
2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp marjoram

Directions:
Heat skillet over medium heat.  Add bacon, and cook until crisp.
Put all ingredients in a slow cooker and cook on low 8 to 10 hours or high 6 to 8 hours, until lentils are tender.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
Hot and tasty.  Serve with Parmesan cheese for garnish.
