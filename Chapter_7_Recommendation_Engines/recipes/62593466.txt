Title:
Aunt Audrey's Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/555622.jpg
Ingredients:
4 C. sliced pared tart apples
2/3 C. brown sugar, packed
½ C. all-purpose flour
½ C. oats
1 tsp. cinnamon
¼ tsp. nutmeg
1/3 C. butter

Directions:
Heat oven to 375º.   Grease a 8x8x2 square baking dish.  Place slices in the dish.  Mix remaining ingredients thoroughly.  Sprinkle over the apples.
Bake 30 minutes or until apples are tender and topping is golden brown.  Serve warm, and if desired, with light cream or ice cream.
