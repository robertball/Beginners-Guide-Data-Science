Title:
PESTO POTATO SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/806702.jpg
Ingredients:
10 sm. red potatoes
1/2 tsp. salt
1 1/2 tbsp. olive oil
1/2 tbsp. lemon juice
1/4 c. dry white wine
1/2 c. mayonnaise
Thinly sliced red onions
6 tbsp. (or more) Pesto sauce
Season to taste

Directions:
Scrub potatoes well. Cook unpeeled in lightly salted water until just barely tender. Peel as soon as cool enough to handle. Slice potatoes about 1/4" thick into a large bowl. Sprinkle with 1/2 teaspoon salt. Add wine, olive oil, lemon juice, toss lightly.  Mix your favorite pesto and the mayonnaise together and pour on top of salad.  Toss if desired.
Garnish. Chill until ready to serve.
