Title:
Vegan Cilantro Tempeh Burgers with Avocado Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz tempeh
1 cup white beans, blended smooth
1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon lime juice (optional)
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 tablespoon ground black pepper
oat flour if needed
hamburger buns
1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and sliced (optional)
1/4 cup ranch dressing, or to taste (optional)

Directions:
Mix tempeh finely crumbled, blended beans, cilantro, soy sauce, garlic powder, lime juice, ground ginger, and ground black pepper together in a large bowl.
Form the mixture into 4 patties. If too thin to hold  in patty form, add ground oats or other flour.
Cook burgers in the hot skillet over medium heat until cooked through, 4 to 5 minutes per side.
Serve on hamburger buns with slices of avocado and a 1 tablespoon ranch dressing.
