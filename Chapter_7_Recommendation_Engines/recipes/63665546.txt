Title:
Spicy Egg Salad English Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1018351.jpg
Ingredients:
3 hard-cooked eggs
1/4 cup mayonnaise
2 teaspoons course grain dijon mustard
2 teaspoons honey
1 teaspoon prepared horseradish
dash hot-pepper sauce
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
salt and pepper to taste
4 slices canadian bacon
2 english muffins, split and toasted
1/3 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
2 tablespoons chopped green onions for garnish (optional)

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine the eggs, mayonnaise, mustard, honey, horseradish, hot pepper sauce and cayenne. Place English muffins cut side up on an ungreased baking sheet. Place a slice of Canadian bacon on top of each english muffin half, followed by 1/4 cup of the egg mixture and sprinkle with the shredded pepper jack cheese.  Garnish with chopped green onions, if desired.
Bake at 350Â° for 6-8 minutes or until cheese is melted.

This is a delicious quick and easy breakfast main dish with a spicy kick to get you going in the mornings.
