Title:
Potato Garlic Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/614777.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 large onion diced
1 leek diced (white and 1/2 of dark green leaves)
6 garlic cloves peeled but left whole
2 large russet potatoes peeled and cut into large cubes
2 cups water
2 cups chicken broth
1/2 cup cream
Salt and pepper to taste
Grated carrot for garnish

Directions:
Add oil to a 4-5 quart pot over medium heat.
Add the onion, leek and garlic and cook until soft, about 10 mins.
Add the potatoes, water and broth to the pot
Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes until potatoes are soft.
Add the cream and puree with a blender.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Top each serving with a little grated carrot.

Description: 
This is a quick and easy soup that has wonderful flavor.  It's always a hit.
