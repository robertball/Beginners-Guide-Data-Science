Title:
Cheeseburger Macaroni from RED GOLD® Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1696125.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 cup onion, chopped
1 (14.5 ounce) can RED GOLD® Petite Diced Tomatoes with Onion, Celery & Green Pepper
1 cup water
1 cup elbow macaroni
2 cups American processed cheese singles, shredded

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and onion. Drain well. Add tomatoes and water; bring to a boil.
Add macaroni and bring back to a boil. Reduce the heat to simmer and cook for 10 minutes or until macaroni is done. Sprinkle cheese on top and stir until cheese is melted.
