Title:
Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Jiffy Corn Muffin mix
1 stick butter (melted)
2 eggs, beaten
8 oz can cream corn
16 oz can whole corn (drained)
1 c sour cream
salt and pepper to taste
1 c cheddar cheese

Directions:
1.  Mix everything together except cheese.
2.  Put in a 2 quart baking dish and top with cheese.
3.  Bake at 350 degrees for 35-45 minutes, until top is golden brown.
