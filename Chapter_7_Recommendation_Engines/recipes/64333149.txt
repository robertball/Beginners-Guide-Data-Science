Title:
Marie's Healthy Chick-Cookie Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Chickpeas, skins removed, patted dry
1/3 cup Peanut Butter
1 1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract
2 Tbsp Honey
1/3 cup Chocolate Chips

Directions:
Combine chickpeas, peanut butter, vanilla extract, and Honey in a blender or food processor. Blend until it's a fine puree.
Remove cookie dough mixture from blender, and place in a large bowl. Add chocolate chips, and mix well with a wooden spoon.

Description: 
This recipe seems odd, but I have made it many times and now I love it! Such a interesting yet delicious version of cookie dough!
