Title:
DOGGIE POPS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
18 oz. Plain Yogurt
1/2 c Peanut Butter
1 Banana mashed or pureed
Dixie Cups
Dog bones med.

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients except dog bones.
Blend well
Pour mixture into small disposable cups.
Put cups in Freezer on cookie sheet or in muffin tins
When partly frozen,place a dog bone in mixture to serve as the handle for you
When ready to serve, peel away cup.

Submitter: 
Grace Carolyn Bowers-Reimondo
Description: 
Store bought frozen treats are too too expensive.
These are well like by our Lab and low cost too.
