Title:
Puffed Rice Krispies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/690609.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups puffed brown rice
1 tsp butter
1 T peanut butter
1 T honey
16 large marshmallows
1/2 cup mixed dried fruit (cranberries, raisins, etc)
1 1/2 oz almonds, diced

Directions:
Lighly spray 8x8 pan.
Preheat oven to 425
Stirring occasionally, toast brown rice in oven 4 to 6 minutes depending on heat and how your rice is layed out.
Remove and let cool.
In meanwhile, heat marshmallows, honey, butter, and peanut butter in skillet.  Stir constantly, until marshmallows are melted.
Immediately add toasted brown rice, almonds, and dried fruit - make just as you would rice krispie treats.
Press and spread out in 8x8 inch pan, using a piece of lightly sprayed alum foil to keep from getting clubhand.
Let cool and cut into bars or shapes as desired
