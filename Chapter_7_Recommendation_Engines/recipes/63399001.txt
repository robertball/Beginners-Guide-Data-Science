Title:
Hot Five - Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 bacon strips, diced
2/3 c sugar
2 T cornstarch
1 1/2 t salt
pinch pepper
3/4 c vinegar
1/2 c water
1 can (16 oz) kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (15 oz) lima beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (15 oz) garbanzo beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (14 1/2 oz) green beans, drained
1 can (14 1/2 oz) wax beans, drained

Directions:
In a skillet, cook bacon until crisp; reserve 1/4 c drippings. Set the bacon aside.  Add sugar, cornstarch, salt and pepper to drippings.  Stir in vinegar and water; bring to a boil, stirring constantly.  Cook and stir for 2 minutes.  Add the beans, reduce heat.  Cover and simmer for 15 minutes or until beans are heated through.  Place in a serving bowl; top with bacon.
