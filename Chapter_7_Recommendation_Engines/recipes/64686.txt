Title:
Athenean Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2040.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 cup) pouch frozen BOCA Meatless Ground Burger
1/2 cup chopped mushrooms
1 clove garlic, minced
1 (14.5 ounce) can Italian seasoned diced tomatoes, undrained, divided
3/4 cup cooked orzo pasta
1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
1 large bell pepper, cut in half lengthwise, seeded

Directions:
MIX ground burger, mushrooms, garlic and 1/2 of the tomatoes in large nonstick skillet. Cook on medium heat 5 minutes or until heated through, stirring frequently. Remove from heat.  Add orzo and cheese; mix well.  Spoon evenly into pepper halves.
POUR remaining tomatoes into 8-inch square baking dish. Top with filled pepper halves; cover with foil.
BAKE at 400 degrees F for 30 minutes.
