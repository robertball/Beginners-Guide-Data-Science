Title:
Rustic Tomato and Basil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil - plus extra to serve
1 Red Onion - peeled and diced
6 cloves Garlic - peeled and diced
150ml/5fl oz Water
12 fresh Plum Tomatoes - peeled and quartered
800g/1¾lb tinned Plum Tomatoes
½ French Baguette - cut into cubes
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
1 to 2 tbsp (to taste) fresh Basil - chopped

Directions:
Heat the olive oil in a pan and gently cook the onion and garlic for a few minutes to slightly soften them;
Add the water and all of the tomatoes;
Cover, bring to the boil and season, to taste, with salt and pepper;
Reduce the heat to a simmer and simmer, uncovered, for 10-15 minutes, or until the volume of liquid has reduced by a third and the tomato soup has thickened slightly;
Stir in the bread cubes and sprinkle over the chopped basil leaves;
To serve, divide the soup equally among three or four serving bowls.
Drizzle over the olive oil.

A Tasty summer/autumn soup fit for any occasion.
