Title:
Peanut Butter Blossoms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 c. flour
1 t. baking soda
1/2 t. salt
1/2 c. shortening
1/2 c. peanut butter
1/2 c. white sugar
1/2 c. brown sugar
1 egg
2 T. milk
1 t. vanilla
Hershey's Kisses

Directions:
Cream shortening and peanut butter; add sugars and cream well.
Add milk and vanilla. Beat well, then blend in dry ingredients.
Shape cookies into balls and roll in sugar. Bake at 350 degrees for 8 minutes.
Press a candy kiss into the center of each cookie, then let cool.
