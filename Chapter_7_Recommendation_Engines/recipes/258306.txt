Title:
Nourish & Refresh Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4528370.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup fresh spinach
1/2 frozen banana
1/3 cup pitted cherries
1 cup Almond Breeze Coconutmilk Almondmilk Original Unsweetened

Directions:
Place all ingredients into blender and blend on high until smooth.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
Take a break on a busy afternoon for a quick and delicious spinach, banana, cherry, and coconutmilk-almondmilk smoothie.
