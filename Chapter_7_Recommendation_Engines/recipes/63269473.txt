Title:
Avocado and Herb Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large organic eggs
1 tbs water
pinch of sea salt
1 tbs extra virgin olive oil
1/2 tsp fresh thyme
1 tbs fresh basil, chopped
1/2 avocado, sliced
black pepper to taste

Directions:
Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk with the water, add the salt.
Heat a small saute pan over medium heat, and add the oil.  Once the oil is hot (not smoking though)pour in the eggs.
Using a spatula, lift the edge of the egg from the side of the pan and allow the wet egg to roll to the bottom of the pan. when the eggs are firm, lay fresh herbs and sliced avocado on one half of the omelet.
Turn off the heat and fold the egg over to serve. Top with black pepper to taste.
