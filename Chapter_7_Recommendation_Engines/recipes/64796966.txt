Title:
Ruby French Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Olive Oil
2/3 Cup Ketchup
1/2 Cup White Sugar
1/2 Cup White Vinegar
2 TBsp Finely Chopped Onion
1 Tsp Lemon Juice
1 Tsp Dry Mustard
1 Tsp Paprika
1 Tsp Salt
1 Tsp Black Pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a jar with Lid, shake well and refrigerate. FLavor best after 3 days.
