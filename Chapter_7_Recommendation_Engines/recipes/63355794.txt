Title:
al fresco Smoked Andouille Chicken Sausage Over Smoky White Cheddar Grits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
1/2 cup chopped white or yellow onion
1 clove minced garlic
1 12oz package al fresco smoked andouille chicken sausage links, sliced on the bias into 1/2 inch pieces
1 cup quick-5 minute grits
1/2 tsp salt
8oz shredded white cheddar cheese
1/4 tsp smoked sweet paprika

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
Add peppers, onion, garlic, and sausage.  Cook and stir for approximately 15 minutes until vegetables are soft and sausage is lightly browned.
Prepare grits by adding to 4 cups of boiling water, reduce heat to medium low, cover and simmer for 5 minutes.  Once cooked, add salt, paprika, and stir in cheese until melted.
Ladle grits onto serving dishes, top with sausage and vegetable mixture.  Sprinkle with additional white cheddar if desired.

Submitter: 
Jennifer Baker
Description: 
The spicy flavor of andouille chicken sausage combines with creamy cheddar grits to create a memorable and delicious dinner in 30 minutes!  Smoked paprika adds extra flavor to the cheese grits.
