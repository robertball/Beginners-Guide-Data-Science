Title:
Balsamic Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
12 oz can albacore white tuna - drained
15 oz can garbanzo beans - drained and rinsed
1/2 cup black olives - halved
1/2 english cucumber - coarsely chopped
1 small red bell pepper - coarsely chopped
1 bunch of green onions - greens and whites chopped
1 small bunch of fresh parsley - chopped
1 bag fresh baby spinach leaves
Dressing:
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
2 Tbs extra virgin olive oil
1 garlic clove
1 1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp coarse black pepper

Directions:
Flake Tuna and combine with ingredients through parsley.  Combine dressing ingredients and blend with immersion blender to puree garlic.  Pour dressing over salad and serve on bed of spinach leaves.

Submitter: 
DUBYANGE
Description: 
White Tuna, Garbanzo beans, veggies and balsamic dressing packs a protein, vitamin, and fiber punch for a healthy lunch that will stick with you.
