Title:
Big Ray's Super Simply  Rhubarb Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3594185.jpg
Ingredients:
1   box  yellow cake mix
3/4   cup cold butter, cut into small pieces
1 3/4   cups sugar
3   eggs
4   cups sliced fresh rhubarb
Whipped topping, if desired

Directions:
Heat oven to 350°F (325°F for dark or nonstick pan). Reserve 2 tablespoons of the cake mix. In large bowl, cut butter into remaining cake mix, using pastry blender, until crumbly. In bottom of ungreased 13x9-inch pan, pat 2 cups of the mixture. Reserve remaining crumbly mixture for topping. Bake 13 minutes.
In large bowl, beat reserved 2 tablespoons cake mix, the sugar and eggs with electric mixer on medium speed until creamy. Stir in rhubarb. Pour over partially baked crust. Sprinkle remaining crumbly mixture over top.
Bake 45 to 50 minutes longer or until golden brown and center is set. Cool slightly before serving. Serve warm or cold with whipped topping. Store covered in refrigerator.
