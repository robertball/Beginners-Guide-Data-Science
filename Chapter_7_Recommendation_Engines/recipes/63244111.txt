Title:
Gin-Kissed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2	Yukon Gold potatoes, diced
2/3 c	water
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp	pepper
1/4 tsp	garlic powder
.
1 tbsp	butter or olive oil
1 lb	chicken breast tenders (or sliced breasts)
salt
pepper
garlic powder
.
1/2	large onion, diced
1 cup	diced carrots
8 oz	sliced mushrooms
1/2 c	gin
1 c	sour cream
1/2 c	heavy cream
8 oz	shredded Cheddar

Directions:
Pour the water into a microwave-safe glass bowl; sprinkle with the salt, pepper and garlic powder; place potatoes in the seasoned water; microwave on high for 12.5 minutes.
Meanwhile, heat the butter to sizzling on high in a large sauté pan.
Season the tenders on both sides with salt, pepper and garlic powder; turn heat to medium and sauté in the butter with the onions until nearly done.
Add the carrots and mushrooms and move the tenders on top of the veggies.
When the potatoes are done, add them to the chicken pan; stir well, keeping the tenders on top of the veggies.
Add the gin and let cook down for a couple of minutes, stirring occasionally.
In a bowl, mix together the sour cream, heavy cream and Cheddar; add to chicken pan and stir well.
Let the sauce heat through and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Fishwrap
Description: 
Use a bit of gin to give this recipe a little tang, or substitute lemon juice if you prefer.
