Title:
Easy Mini Fritattas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1.5 tbs flour
2 tbs sour cream or plain yogurt
4 slices cooked bacon, crumbled (or ham or whatever meat you have available)
1/2 cup cooked spinach (frozen or fresh, drained)
1/4 cup zucchini (diced)
1/2 tbs italian seasonings
salt and pepper to taste
1 tube ready to cook biscuits
shredded cheese (whatever is on hand)
dash Tabasco (optional)

Directions:
Open tube of biscuits and cut each one in half.
Grease the bottoms of cupcake pan and press one biscuit half into each cup to cover the bottom. Bake per biscuit instructions.
Turn oven down to 350 degrees. In a large bowl combine eggs, flour, sour cream or plain yogurt, meat, spinach, zucchini, italian seasonings, salt and pepper and Tabasco (if desired).
Pour mixture over slightly cooled biscuits. Leave a little room for expansion in the cups.
Top each cup with cheese.
Bake for 20-25 minutes or until inserted knife comes out clean.
