Title:
Cameron's Ground Turkey Salsa Ranchera for Tacos and Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4553054.jpg
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon canola oil
1 1/4 pounds lean ground turkey (93% lean 7% fat)
1 7oz. can Salsa Ranchera (such as Herdez® brand)

Directions:
Heat oil in an electric pressure cooker (such as an Instant Pot®) on the saute setting or according to your manufacturers directions.
When oil is hot stir and cook ground turkey until mostly browned.
Add the salsa ranchera to the ground turkey and stir to combine.
Seal pressure cooker and bring to high pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; cook for 15 minutes. Use the quick-release method to release pressure according to manufacturer's instructions.

Submitter: 
bd.weld
Description: 
It doesn't get much easier than this, lean ground turkey and salsa ranchera combined and made in a pressure cooker. Perfect for tacos, burritos or even a taco salad.
