Title:
Easy Apple Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
1 (3.4 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
4 eggs
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup applesauce
6 apples - peeled, cored and sliced
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10 inch Bundt pan.
In a large bowl, stir together the cake mix and instant pudding mix. Add the eggs, sour cream and applesauce, mix until well blended. In a small bowl combine the sugar, cinnamon and pecans.
Pour half of the batter into the prepared pan, then place a layer of sliced apples over the batter and sprinkle with half of the cinnamon walnut mixture. Pour the remaining batter over the top and repeat with the remaining apples and cinnamon walnut mixture.
Bake for 60 to 70 minutes in the preheated oven. Cool for 1/2 hour before removing from pan to cool completely.

Submitter: 
Lori Kirchner Zwahlen
Description: 
An easy to make cake with a beautiful layer of apples in the middle. This is wonderful served warm with a scoop of ice cream.Modified from: Easy Apple Coffee Cake
