Title:
Portobello Mushroom Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chopped portobello mushroom
3 cans beans (kidney, black, northern)
2 cans diced tomatoes
1 46 oz can of tomato juice
1 chopped onion
chili powder to taste

Directions:
Mix together in Crockpot.
Cook on high for 2 hours and low the rest of the day.
