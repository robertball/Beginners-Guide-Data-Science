Title:
Delizioso Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Onion, chopped
3 Cloves garlic, minced
2 T olive oil
1 (28 oz.) can whole tomatoes
3 (14.5 oz.) cans diced tomatoes
2 (6 oz.) cans tomato paste
2 T FRESH basil
2 T FRESH oregano
2 FRESH bay leaves
1 T white sugar
1/4 t crushed red pepper (optional)
1/4 cup red wine
1 lb. Italian sausage (mild or spicy - to your taste)
1/2 lb. ground (round)beef

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large saucepan or Dutch oven.  Saute onions and garlic until onions are tender (2-3 minutes).  Add sausage and ground beef and cook until brown. Add remaining ingredients and simmer over low heat for 3 hrs.
