Title:
Ritz Thin Mints Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/758277.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz bittersweet chocolate
30 buttery round crackers
1/2 teaspoon peppermint extract

Directions:
Cover a large cookie sheet with wax or parchment paper
Melt the chocolate in a microwave and stir in peppermint extract
Dip crackers into the melted chocolate, place onto cookie sheet, and place in the refrigerator (or freezer) to set.
