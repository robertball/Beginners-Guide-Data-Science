Title:
Quick Banana Spice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz. butter, softened
3/4 c. brown sugar, packed
1 large egg
2-3 bananas, mashed, about 3/4 c.
1/4 c. buttermilk or sour milk
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 c. flour
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 scant tsp cinnamon
1/8 tsp ground cloves
1/8 tsp ground nutmeg

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.  Grease and flour an 8 or 9 inch square pan.  In a large mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar.  Beat in egg, milk, vanilla, and mashed banana until blended.  Combine remaining dry ingredients and spices.  Stir in the dry ingredients into the first mixture until moistened.  Spread into prepared pan.  Bake for 25-30 min or until cake springs back when lightly touched with finger.  Frost with a cream cheese frosting or sprinkle with powdered sugar.

Submitter: 
Karen Zastrow
Description: 
This banana cake is just the right size for my family of 4.  Serve as a snack or as a dessert.
