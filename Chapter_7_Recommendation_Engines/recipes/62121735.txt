Title:
Anyone Can Make BBQ Pulled Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 pound pork butt roast
5 large cloves garlic, peeled and sliced thin
1 large onion, diced
2 Tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
2 Tablespoons Liquid Smoke
2 cups strong coffee
1 Tablespoon red pepper flakes
salt & pepper, to taste
16 ounces of your favorite bottled or homemade BBQ sauce
12 toasted sandwich buns

Directions:
Turn the fat end of the roast upwards.
Cut slits in it and place the slices of garlic in the slits.
Put it in the slow cooker, fat side up.
Mix together the onion, Worcestershire sauce, Liquid Smoke, and coffee.
Pour over the roast.
Cook on low for 8 hours.
Remove roast with slotted spoons, being careful, because it will be tender.
Discard any excess fat.
Shred the pork with two forks, or by hand.
Discard all the juices in the slow cooker, except for 1/2 a cup.
Place shredded pork in the slow cooker.
Add red pepper flakes and salt and pepper to taste.
Pour in your favorite BBQ sauce.
Blend well, and heat on low for about 30 minutes.
Meanwhile toast the sandwich buns.
Pile high on the buns and enjoy!

Submitter: 
JOYCE
Description: 
If you got a hankering for a good BBQ sandwich, breakout the slow cooker and you are on your way to a great meal!
