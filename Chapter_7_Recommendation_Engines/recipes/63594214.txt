Title:
Turkey Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/999766.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 lb Ground turkey breast
4 Slices whole wheat bread, crumbled
1 tsp. Salt
1 tsp. Dry Mustard
1 tsp. Dried Ginger
1 tsp. Celery Seed
1/2 tsp. Caraway Seed
1 Onion, finely chopped
3 Cloves garlic, finely minced
1 Egg
Topping:
1/2 C. Grated Farmers cheese
1/2 C. Fresh parsley, chopped finely
1 tsp. Maple butter

Directions:
In a medium size bowl combine bread crumbs, dry mustard, ginger, salt, celery seed and caraway seed and mix together then set aside
In a large bowl place ground turkey breast and egg together and then stir ground turkey meat with chopped onion and garlic until onion is well combined with ground turkey meat
Add the dry ingredients to the ground turkey and mix together to form a ball and then place in greased loaf pan
Mix together parsley and maple butter and then spread on top of turkey loaf
Sprinkle cheese on top of parsley and bake for 35 minutes or until temperature reaches 170 degrees Fahrenheit internal temperature

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
For a change from the regular meat loaf here is a spiced up Turkey loaf for your weekday meal that has enough servings to last for one week of meals with other vegetables.
