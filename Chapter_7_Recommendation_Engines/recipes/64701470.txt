Title:
Apple Cobbler for a Crowd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups sugar
3 cups flour
2 tbs baking powder
1 cup melted butter
1 cup milk
1 gallon sliced apples, drained
1 cup sugar
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp nutmeg

Directions:
In a large bowl mix the first 5 ingredients together to make a smooth batter.
Pour this into a large cake pan
Now mix the apples with the sugar and spices (use the same bowl you used for batter, it makes no difference if you get them a little mixed up together!
Pour apple mixture on top of the flour mixture.  Do not stir.
Bake at 350 degrees about 45 minutes or until golden.
