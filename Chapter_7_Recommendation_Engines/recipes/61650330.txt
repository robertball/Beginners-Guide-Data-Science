Title:
Healthier Mom's Zucchini Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/281424.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 cups white whole wheat flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
3 teaspoons ground cinnamon
3 eggs
1/2 cup low-fat (1%) milk
1/2 cup vegetable oil OR melted butter
1 1/2 cups white sugar
3 teaspoons vanilla extract
3 cups grated zucchini
1 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Grease and flour two 8 x 4 inch pans.  Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Sift both flours, salt, baking powder, soda and cinnamon together in a bowl.
Beat eggs, oil or butter, vanilla, and sugar together in a large bowl.  Add sifted ingredients to the creamed mixture, and beat well.  Stir in zucchini and nuts until well combined.  Pour batter into prepared pans
Bake for 40 to 60 minutes, or until tester inserted in the center comes out clean.  Cool in pan on rack for 20 minutes.  Remove bread from pan, and completely cool.
