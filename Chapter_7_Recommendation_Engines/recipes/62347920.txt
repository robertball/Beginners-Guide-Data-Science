Title:
Not Your Mother's Egg Salad Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/395668.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Large eggs
4 TBS Mayonnaise
2 TBS Chopped onion
1 TBS Cilantro
1 Medium tomato (thin sliced)
2 Leaf lettuce
8 Slices of whole wheat, light rye or flaxseed bread

Directions:
Day before boiled eggs.  Place 4 eggs in pan of boiling water and cook for 10 minutes.  Remove from heat and drain water from eggs.  Place eggs in refrigerator.
Peel eggs and cut up eggs (cube).  Place in cut up eggs in bowl.  Add mayonnaise,  chopped onion and cilantro to eggs and mix  until all ingredients are blended.
To make sandwich take bread and spread egg salad on it, place sliced tomato and leaf lettuce on top of egg salad.

Submitter: 
cookingtwist
Description: 
Nothing says farmhouse cooking like an egg salad sandwich.... so try this twist by adding cilantro into the egg salad for an extra special taste.  Make sure you use  eggs from range free chickens or chickens feed corn and or milo they will have golden color egg yolks.  This golden color egg yolk will brighten up your egg salad sandwich.
