Title:
One Oh One Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1056991.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 cup butter
1 cup vegetable oil
1 egg
1 teaspoon cream of tartar
3 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup flaked coconut
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup crisp rice cereal
1 cup rolled oats
3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  In a large bowl, mix all ingredients until well blended.
Drop on greased cookie sheets and bake for 12 to 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
M. Galstad
Description: 
Great recipe for when you need a lot of cookies for picnics, potlucks, family reunions, Christmas....
