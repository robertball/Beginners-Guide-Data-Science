Title:
Ginger Chicken with Orange Ginger Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breast, butterflied
1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
3 tbsp fresh grated ginger, divided
4 cloves garlic, minced and divided
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1/2 cup orange marmalade
3 tbsp soy sauce, preferable low sodium
1/3 cup + 1 tbsp water
1 tbsp cornstarch
salt and pepper
olive oil

Directions:
Mix together the apple cider vingar, 2 tbsp ginger, 1/2 the garlic and salt and pepper to taste and pour over chicken. Set aside and let marinate for 20 minutes.
In the meantime, heat a small saucepan to medium and saute the remaining garlic and ginger in a little olive oil until fragrant, about 3 minutes. Stir in the red pepper flakes, orange marmalade, soy sauce and 1/3 cup water and let simmer on medium-low for 15 minutes. Mix together the cornstarch and remaining water and stir into the orange mixture. Raise heat to medium-high and cook for 10 minutes more, stiring frequently.
Heat more olive oil a large skillet to medium-high. Pat dry each breast before adding it to the skillet and cook for 5-6 minutes on each side, or until juices run clear.
Transfer chicken to a serving platter and pour sauce over the top. Serve with white rice.

Description: 
This is a healthier alternitive to the battered and fried orange chicken. The sauce is made seperately so it can be used for other things such as dips and glazes.
