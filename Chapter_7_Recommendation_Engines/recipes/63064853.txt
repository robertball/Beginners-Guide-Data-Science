Title:
Chicken Cacciatore Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1 broiler/fryer chicken (3 to 4 lbs) cut up
2 tlbs olive oil
2 med onions, cut into wedges
1 med green pepper cut into stripes
1 jar (6oz)sliced mushrooms, drained
1 can (14 1/2 oz) diced tomatoes, undrained
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/4 tsp dried basil
1/2 cup shredded parmasan cheese

Directions:
place four in a large reasealable plastic bag. Add Chicken, a few pieces at a time, and shake to coat. In a large skillet, brown the chicken in oil on all sides. Transfer to 5 qt slow cooker.
Top with onions, green peppers and mushrooms. Combine tomatoes, garlic, salt, oregano and basil; pour over the vegis.
Cover and cook on low 4-5 hours or until chicken and vegis are tender. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
