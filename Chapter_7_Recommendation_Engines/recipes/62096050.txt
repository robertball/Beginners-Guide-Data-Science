Title:
Easy Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of shortening
3/4 c. brown sugar (packed)
1 t. vanilla
1 t. salt
2 eggs
2 1/4 c. flower
1 t. soda
1 pkg choc. chips

Directions:
Mix well in order given.
Drop by t. full onto ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake at 375 for 8-10   minutes. ENJOY!

Submitter: 
Kathy Van Pelt
Description: 
These cookies are so easy to make.  My family just loves them.
