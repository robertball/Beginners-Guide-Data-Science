Title:
Air Fryer Zucchini Chips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5593807.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium zucchini, thinly sliced
1 cup panko bread crumbs
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 large egg, beaten
non-stick cooking spray
marinara sauce for dipping

Directions:
1. Set the air fryer to 350 degrees before you begin the zucchini preparation.
2.  Combine panko bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese on a plate.
3. Dip zucchini slice first in egg and then in bread crumb mixture and press to coat.
4. Place zucchini slice on a wire baking rack and repeat with remaining slices.
5. Lightly spray the zucchini slices with non-stick cooking spray.
6. Place as many as you can in the basket of your air fryer without overlapping them.
7. Cook for 10 minutes. Flip with tongs. Cook an additional 2 minutes. Remove from air fryer and repeat with remaining zucchini slices.
8. Serve  with marinara sauce for dipping.

Submitter: 
Soup Loving Nicole
Description: 
Crunchy, cheesy  fried zucchini chips without the guilt of deep frying. This is the perfect way to make use of those abundant zucchini from the garden.
