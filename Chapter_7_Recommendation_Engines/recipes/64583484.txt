Title:
Big Ray's White Bean Horseradish Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3738848.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ( 15 oz) cans Cannellini beans, rinsed and drained (save water for later)
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup prepared horseradish
2 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
2 Tbsp tahini
1 1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
In a food processor or blender, process beans, oil, horseradish, lemon juice, tahini and salt, adding saved liquid if mixture is too thick.  Serve with pita chips

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
A nice appetizer that can be whipped up quickly with items you probably have in the pantry.
