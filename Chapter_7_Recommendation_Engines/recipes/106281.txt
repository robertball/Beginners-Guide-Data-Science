Title:
Brazilian Limeade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986474.jpg
Ingredients:
2 limes
1/4 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
3 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
3 cups water
ice

Directions:
Wash limes thoroughly. Cut off the ends, and slice into eight wedges. Place limes in a blender with the SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, sweetened condensed milk, water, and ice.
Blend in an electric blender, pulsing 5 times. Strain through a fine mesh strainer to remove rinds. Serve over ice.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Fresh limes and sweetened condensed milk are blended with ice to make this creamy lime drink.
