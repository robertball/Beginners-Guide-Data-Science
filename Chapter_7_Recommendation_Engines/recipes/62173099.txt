Title:
Tuna and noodle casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can tuna
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1 pkg egg noodles
potato chips

Directions:
Cook noodles in water until aldente. Mix tuna and soup toghether in casserole dish. Add the drained noodles. If to thick add 3/4C of water. Cush chips and put on  the top. Bake at 350 for 30-45min
