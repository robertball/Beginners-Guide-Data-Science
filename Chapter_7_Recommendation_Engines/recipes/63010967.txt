Title:
Quick Blackberry Cobler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. milk
2 tsp oil or melted shortning
1/3 c. sugar
1 c. Bisquick
2/3 c. sugar
1 c. water
4 c. Blackberries

Directions:
Mix the first 4 ingredients and put into a greased 2 qt casserole dish. then mix berries, sugar and water. then pour over batter. bake at 375 degrees for about 45 minutes. If you are using canned or frozen sweetened fruit omit sugar and use the juice in place of the water

Submitter: 
lwilliams001
Description: 
This recipe I found when I was serching trying to find the quickest easiest and least expensive blackberry cobbler. It is the most amazing cobbler I have ever tasted. Whenever I make it it's always a family favorite
