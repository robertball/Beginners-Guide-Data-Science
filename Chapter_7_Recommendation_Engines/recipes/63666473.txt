Title:
Garlic Herb Feta Cheese Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1020094.jpg
Ingredients:
2-1/2 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cubed
2 tablespoons butter
1/2 cup milk
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 package (4 ounces) crumbled feta cheese
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Place the potatoes in a large saucepan and cover them with cold water. Heat over medium high heat and bring to a boil. Lower the heat and simmer until the potatoes are tender, about 20 minutes. Drain.
Return the potatoes to the pan and mash the them with a potato masher.  Stir in the butter, milk, garlic, feta, oregano and rosemary. Season to taste with salt and pepper and serve.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
Mediterranean inspired garlic, herbs and feta cheese add a unique and delicious flair to this favorite side dish.
