Title:
Shrimp Scampi Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2079919.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 stick butter
Olive oil
2 c white wine
2 TB crushed garlic
15 oz can diced tomatoes
1/4 c diced onion
3 TB old bay seasoning
1/2 lemon juice
4 med zucchini
2 lb shrimp
Salt and pepper
1/4 c paramesan cheese

Directions:
Use a spiraler (zoodler) and make noodles with zucchini.  Cut ends off zucchini, leave skin on.  Place to side
Saute onion and garlic in small amount of olive oil. Add wine, tomatoes, old bay and shrimp.  Cook until shrimp turns pink.  Add stick of butter and lemon juice.  Salt and pepper to taste
Add zoodles and cook about 5 minutes
Serve topping with paramesan cheese

A healthy flair to a traditional scampi using Zoodles
