Title:
French Market Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb mixed soup beans
1 medium onion, chopped
1 stalk celery, chopped
1 bell pepper, diced
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/2 head cabbage, slived
3 small potatoes, cubed
2 medium carrots, sliced
3 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
1 (28 oz) can diced tomatos, undrained
2 tablespoons parsley flakes
2 teaspoons dried basil
1 teaspoon dried oregano
2 bay leaves
1 garlic clove, minced
1/2 teaspoon pepper
3 cups chopped fresh spinach

Directions:
Soak beans according to package.  In a Dutch oven, saute onion, pepper and celery in oil. Add the next eleven ingredients plus beans. Simmer, uncovered, until vegetables are tender. Remove bay leaves and add spinach; heat through.

Submitter: 
Carol Kinghorn
Description: 
This colorful combination is chock-full of harvest-fresh goodness. It showcases an appealing assortment of beans, potatoes, carrots and spinach. This is infinitely versatile.  Add any type of bean or veggie you have on hand.Modified from: Three-Bean Soup
