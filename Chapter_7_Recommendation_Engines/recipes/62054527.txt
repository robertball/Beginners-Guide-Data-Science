Title:
Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
POTATO MIXTURE:
3 cups sweet potatoes (canned usually works better)
4 egg whites
1/4 c. sugar
2 tbs. flour
TOPPING:
3 tbs. flour
1/2 c. brown sugar
2 tbs. butter (room temperature)
1/2 c. chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.  Apply vegetable oil cooking spray to 1 1/2 qt. casserole dish.  With mixer, mix together potato mixture ingredients and pour into the dish. For the topping, crumble flour, brown sugar and butter into small bowl.  Stir in pecans and sprinkle over potato mixture.  Bake for 30 min. or until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Amy Lawler
Description: 
Egg whites make this a lighter alternative to the typical sweet potato casserole dish, however, your guests will never know.  With a crunchy top layer, this dish is excellent for a side dish or even dessert.
