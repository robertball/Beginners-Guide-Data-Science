Title:
Big home-made pizza lunchables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Flour tortillas (any size)
Pizza Sauce
Shredded Mozzarella
Pepperoni's

Directions:
Spread pizza sauce over tortilla, add pepperoni and top with shredded cheese. Roll up and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Debra Ann Price
Description: 
When my son outgrew the itty-bitty store bought version, we started making our own. He is now 15 and makes them for himself all the time.
