Title:
Salmon & Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-2 cans of Pink Salmon
-1 medium Onion (diced)
-1 cup of Yellow Mustard
-1 teaspoon of Spicy Mustard
-1 teaspoon of Cayenne Pepper
-1 1/2 cups of Water
-2 Tablespoons of Old Bay seasoning
-8 ounces of Brown Rice

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400.
2. Clean salmon in casserole dish.
3. Mix all remaining ingredients in a casserole dish.
4. Cook covered for 90 minutes.
