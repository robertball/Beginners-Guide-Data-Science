Title:
Three Cheese Green Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/397269.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large bundle of green leaf lettuce
1 cup swiss cheese cubes
1 cup feta cheese crumbles
1 cup shredded parmesan
1 cup toasted pecan pieces
1/2 cup light olive oil
1/2 cup white balsamic vinegar
1 tablespoon italian seasoning
1 tablespoon fresh ground pepper

Directions:
Wash and thoroughly dry (or spin) lettuce
Tear apart in bite size pieces in bowl
Toast pecans in a skillet over medium heat
Add swiss, feta and parmesan to salad
Combine oil, vinegar, seasoning and pepper
Wisk together and pour over salad

Submitter: 
amylrowe
Description: 
This is a great salad as a meal with grilled chicken or by itself.  This has been a winner with our friends and family.  Who can pass up a salad with three cheeses?
