Title:
Spareribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6857256.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups water
rack of ribs
dried onion flakes
barbecue sauce

Directions:
Fill bottom of broiler pan with water.
Cut ribs into 2-3 rib sections.
Place ribs on rack of broiler pan above water.
Sprinkle with onion flakes.
Cover with loose tent of foil.
Place in 225 degree oven for 8 hours or 3 hours at 300 degrees.
Remove and cover with barbecue sauce.
