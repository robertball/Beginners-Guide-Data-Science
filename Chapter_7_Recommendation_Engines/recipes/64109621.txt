Title:
Nitya's Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1473846.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lg. cauliflower, washed and cut into medium pieces (or a green cabbage)
1/8 tsp. mustard seeds
1 medium potato, peeled, diced in 1/4 inch pieces
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. turmeric
1/8 tsp. cayenne pepper
1 1/2 tsp sugar
1/4 C. oil

Directions:
Put oil in a pan and heat. When oil is hot put the mustard seeds in and let heat until they pop for a few seconds. Add cauliflower (cabbage). Mix well with oil, then add salt, turmeric and potato. Mix well, cover and cook over medium heat for 15-20 minutes - stirring every couple minutes. Add cayenne pepper and sugar, cook for about 2 more minutes.Serve warm
