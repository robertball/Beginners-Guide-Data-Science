Title:
Easy Flan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1 can evaporated milk
1 tsp. vanilla
4-5 eggs
1 tsp. cornstarch
1/2 cup sugar

Directions:
Carmalize sugar by placing in a pie plate in the oven at 300*F until it melts and turns a caramel color. Watch carefully. Pour all other ingredients into a blender and mix. Once caramel has cooled to a solid, pour blender contents into pie plate. Place pie plate into a  water bath and bake 45-60 minutes at 350*F. Let cool and flip onto a plate.
