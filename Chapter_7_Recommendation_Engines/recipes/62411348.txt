Title:
Healthier Sweet Restaurant Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) bag broccoli slaw mix
2/3 cup light mayonnaise
3 tablespoons canola oil
1/3 cup white sugar
1 tablespoon rice vinegar
1/2 teaspoon ground mustard
1/2 teaspoon celery seeds

Directions:
Combine the coleslaw mix in a large bowl.
Whisk together the mayonaise, canola oil, sugar, vinegar, mustard, and celery seeds in a medium bowl; blend thoroughly. Pour dressing mixture over coleslaw mix and toss to coat. Chill at least 2 hours before serving.  Recommend chilling overnight.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
Easy to make with convenient, packaged coleslaw, in this version the creamy dressing with vinegar, sugar and vegetable oil is close to what's served at popular fried chicken, burger and fish restaurants.Modified from: Sweet Restaurant Slaw
