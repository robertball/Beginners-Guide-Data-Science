Title:
Sausage Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bath of biscuit dough.
1 lb sausage (uncooked)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350'.  Make up 1 batch of biscuit dough.  Spread thin on a floured table.  Spread sausage over dough and roll up.  Slice and put on a cookie sheet.  Bake till light brown.
Serve with honey.
frezzes well.
