Title:
Sausage Supper Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
farmers / smoked sausage of choice, enough for # ppl you're feeding
potatoes, peeled and cubed
carrots, peeled and sliced thick
celery, cut in lengths (or sub with onions or root veggies)

Directions:
Put frozen (or thawed) sausage in large roaster.
Almost cover with water.
Bake, covered, @ 350 x 1/2 hour.
Add rest of ingredients.
Bake, covered, another 45 - 60 minutes, until potatoes & veggies are tender.
