Title:
Pasta With Asparagus, White Wine & Capers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz linguine, saving 3/4 cup cooking water.
1 bunch shaved asparagus
1 chopped shallot
1/4 cup chopped capers
1 Tbsp fresh thyme
1 lg clove chopped garlic
1/2 cup white wine

Directions:
1. Cook linguine, saving 3/4 cup cooking water.
2. Heat skillet over medium heat. Add 1 bunch shaved asparagus, 1 chopped shallot, 1/4 cup chopped capers, 1 Tbsp fresh thyme, and 1 lg clove chopped garlic with 1-2 T water. Cook 3 min.
3. Add 1/2 cup white wine and simmer until reduced by half. Add pasta and cooking water. Simmer until reduced by half.
