Title:
Sex -in-a-Pan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Base:
1/2 cup (125 ml) margarine
1 cup (250 ml) all purpose flour
1/4 cup (50 ml) sugar
First layer:
1 (500 gr) package cream cheese, room temperature
1/2 cup (125 ml) icing sugar
1 (500 ml) tub Cool Whip
Second layer:
3 cups (750 ml) milk
1 (170 gr) package instant chocolate pudding mix (6 portion size)OR pistachio flavour
1 (135 gr) package vanilla instant pudding mix (6 portion size)
Third layer:
1 (250 ml) container whipping cream, whipped
12 chocolate wafers, coarsely crushed

Directions:
Cut margarine into flour and sugar wieh a pastry blender.  Mixture should resemble fine crumbs.  Press into 10 inch (25 cm) springform pan.  Bank at 325 deg. F. (160 deg. C) for 25 minutes. Cool.
For first layer, beat together cream cheese and icing sugar until smooth.  Stir in Cool Whip.  Spread on base.
For second layer, measure milk into bowl.  Add pudding mixes and beat until well blended, about two minutes.  Spoon over crem cheese layer.
Flor third layer, spread with whipped cream and sprinkle with chocolate wafer crumbs.  Refigerate at least 1 hour.
