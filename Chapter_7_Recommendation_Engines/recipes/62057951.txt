Title:
Uncle Jim's Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp vegetable oil
4 bone-in pork chops
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cram of celery soup
1/2 envelope of onion soup mix
1 1/2 cans water
1/2 can milk or cream
salt & pepper

Directions:
Season pork chops with salt and pepper.  Heat oil in a large skillet and brown pork chops on both sides.  Meanwhile mix remaining ingredients in a large bowl.  When chops are brown, pour soup mixture over chops, cover and cook for 2 hours or until chops are very tender.

Submitter: 
kelcampbell
Description: 
My uncle Jim (who is a chef) created this recipe - it is very good and fills the house with a lovely smell!
