Title:
Beef and Mushroom Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz of fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 teaspoon diced basil
1/2 teaspoon diced sage
1/2 teaspoon diced savory
1/4 cup milk
1 pound lean ground beef
3 cups spaghetti Sauce
9 cooked lasagna noodles
1 cup shredded Italian-blend or mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup shredded Tillamook sharp cheddar cheese

Directions:
Heat the oven to 400 degrees F. Stir the mushrooms, herbs and milk in a small bowl until the mixture is smooth.
Cook the beef in a 10-inch skillet over medium-high heat until it's well browned, stirring often to separate meat. Pour off any fat. Stir in the spaghetti sauce,mushrooms and herbs.
Layer half the beef mixture,1/3 the cheese  3 lasagna noodles and 1 cup sauce mixture in a 2-quart shallow baking dish. Top with 3 lasagna noodles, remaining beef mixture, remaining noodles and remaining sauce mixture. Sprinkle with the cheese. Cover the baking dish.
Bake for 30 minutes or until the lasagna is hot. Uncover the baking dish.
Heat the broiler. Broil 4 inches from the heat for 2 minutes or until the cheese is golden brown. Let stand for 10 minutes.
