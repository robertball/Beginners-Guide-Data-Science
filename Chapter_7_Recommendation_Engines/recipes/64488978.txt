Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbls Butte
1 large onion - rough chop
1 red pepper - rough chop
1 yellow pepper - rough chop
1/4 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp Pepper
2 blocks of cream cheese
2 Tbls garlic cream
2 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp ground cumin
2 Cups chopped cooked chicken
1 1/2 cups green salsa
8 flour tortillas
2 Cups Shredded Cheddar/jack cheese
2 cups pepper jack

Directions:
Melt butter in a large skillet over medium=high heat, stirring often, add onion and peppers, salt and pepper, Saute until caramelized.
Add cream cheese, garlic, oregano and cumin. Stir until you have a creamy sauce.
Turn off heat and add chopped chicken, stirring until combined.
Put green salsa all over the bottom of  9x13 baking dish
Spoon 1/4 cup chicken mixture down the center of each tortilla and 1/4 cup pepper jack in each tortilla - roll up and place, seam side down in 9x13 baking dish.
Top with salsa mixture , sprinkle with 2 cups of cheddar/jack cheese
Bake at 375 degrees for 20-25 minutes or until bubbly and golden.
