Title:
Garlic & Dill Seasoned Red Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 Red Potatoes
1 Tbs Dill
1 1/2 Tbs Minced Garlic (or 2-3 cloves)
4 Tbs Butter
salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Wash and cut potatoes into dime sized cubes, and spread into a 9x13 inch glass baking dish.  Sprinkle with dill, minced or pressed garlic, salt and pepper, and butter placed throughout.
Bake at 350 for 30-45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
