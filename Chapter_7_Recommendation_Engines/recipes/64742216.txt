Title:
Chic fil a Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup honey
2 tablespoons yellow mustard
1/4 cup barbecue sauce I use KC masterpiece
1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
1 tablespoon spicy brown mustard
1/2 cup mayonnaise (Plus a little extra)

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients in a bowl and refrigerate for 30 minutes to allow flavors to come together.
