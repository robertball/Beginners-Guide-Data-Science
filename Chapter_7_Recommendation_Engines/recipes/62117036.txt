Title:
Mint Hot Cocoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup cocoa powder
1/3 cup hot water
3 1/2 cups of milk
1/2 cup half 'n half
1 cup miniature marshmallows
1/2 cup milk chocolate chips
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 teaspoon pure peppermint extract
extra miniature marshmallows (optional)
whipped cream (optional)

Directions:
Combine cocoa powder, sugar and hot water in a medium saucepan.  Bring mixture to a boil over medium heat and continue to boil and stir for two minuets.
Stir in milk and half 'n half. Add marshmallows and chocolate chips.  Stir until marshmallows and chocolate pieces are melted and the hot chocolate is heated through.  Do this over medium heat.  DO NOT BOIL!
Remove from heat and stir in vanilla and mint extracts.
Serve in mugs with extra marshmallows and or whipped cream on top, if desired.

Submitter: 
ELIZRA
Description: 
We love hot chocolate, and this recipe that I came up with is one of our favorites.  The marshmallows melted in give it just the right amount of sweetness and a nice texture.  I hope you enjoy it as much as we do.
