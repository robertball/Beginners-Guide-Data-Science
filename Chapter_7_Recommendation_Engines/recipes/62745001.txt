Title:
Southern Living's Kentucky Hot Brown Tart Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14.1 oz. package refrigerated piecrusts
1 1/2 cups chopped cooked turkey
2 cup6s (8 oz.) shredded white Cheddar cheese
1/4 cup finely chopped fresh chives
6 bacon slices, cooked and crumbled
1 1/2 cups half and half
4 large eggs
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
2 plum tomatoes, cut into 1/4 inch slices
1/2 cup freshly shredded parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Unroll piecrusts and stack onto a lightly greased surface.  Roll stacked piecrusts into a 12 inch circle.  Fit piecrust into a 10 inch deep dish tart pan with a removavle bottom.  Press into fluted edges and trim off excess.  Line crust with parchment and fill with dried beans or pie weights and place on foil lined pan.  Bake 12 minutes and remove weights and parchment then bake another 8 minutes.  Remove from oven and cool completely.  Reduce oven temperature to 350.
Layer turkey and next three ingredients in tart shell.
Whisk together half and half and next three ingredients; pour over turkey.
Bake at 350 for  30 to 40 minutes until set.
Place tomatoes in single layer on paper towels; press tomatoes lightly with paper towels to absorb extra moisture.  Arrange sliced tomatoes over tart and sprinkle with parmesan.  Bake 10-15 minutes or until cheese is melted.  Cool on baking sheet on wire rack 15 minutes.
