Title:
Italian Seasoning Blend Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 1-1/2 tsp Dried Oregano
1 tsp Dried Basil
1 tsp Dried Crushed Rosemary
1 tsp Ground Sage
1 tsp Ground Thyme
1/2 - 1 tsp Ground Marjoram
Bay leaf - optional

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients and store in a small jar.  Use in any recipe calling for an Italian seasoning blend.
