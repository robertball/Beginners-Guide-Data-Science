Title:
Chinese Cabbage Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cabbage, shredded
4 green onions, sliced thin
2 stalks celery, sliced thin
1 package Ramen noodles
1 stick of butter (or 1/4 cup canola oil)
¼ cup sliced almonds (can use pecans)
2 T sesame seed
Dressing Ingredients
¼ cup canola oil
½ cup vinegar
¼ cup water
2 T sugar

Directions:
Mix cabbage, onions and celery.
Crush noodles. Saute slowly until brown in skillet to which you have added ½ stick butter or ¼ cup canola oil. When noodles begin to brown add nuts and sesame seed.
Use flavor package to make dressing for salad. Mix dressing ingredients in a small saucepan. Heat over low heat until sugar dissolves.
