Title:
Ham Hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C Diced cooked Ham
1 Large Onion-Diced
1 Bell Pepper-Diced
2 Tbs Butter
1 1/2 C shredded Cheese
2 Cans Cream of Mushroom/Celery
3/4 C water
3 C Hash-browns
Paprika for color

Directions:
Pre-Heat oven to 425.
Place hash-browns in large baking dish and brown in oven.
Sautee onion and bell pepper in butter, Add ham.
Once ham is slightly browned at the cream of mushroom/celery, 3/4 cup water and 1 cup of shredded cheese.
Cook until cheese is mostly  melted on low-med heat.
Pour mixture over hash-browns, top with remainder of cheese and sprinkle Paprika for color and bake for 20-25 or until cheese is browned.

Submitter: 
SharonLynn Kinville
Description: 
Easy recipe for weeknights and left overs are great with eggs in the morning.
