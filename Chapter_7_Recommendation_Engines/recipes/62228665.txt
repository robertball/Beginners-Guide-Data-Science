Title:
Grandma's Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 chicken breasts
1 Apple
1 Tomato
1 Large Onion
3/4 Cup Sultanas
1/2 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp jam
pinch of cumin
pinch of cardamom
1 1/2 tsp lime juice
3 tbs flour
1 tsp salt
2 tsp chutney
2 tsp ketchup or tomato puree
2 tsp lemon juice
3 1/2 tbs curry power
2 1/2 C water

Directions:
Brown chicken breasts in a little butter and oil, remove from pan.  Chop apple, tomato and onion, add to pan and saute til tender.  Add the rest of the ingredients in order and simmer for approximately 20 mins. Add chicken breasts and sauce to a dutch oven, cover and cook for 1 hr 10 mins at 350 degrees.
