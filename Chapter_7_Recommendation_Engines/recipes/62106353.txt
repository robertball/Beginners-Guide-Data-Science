Title:
Bleu Cheese Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups fresh spinach
3 cloves minced garlic
1 8 oz. package low-fat cream cheese
1 cup skim milk
1/2 cup bleu cheese crumbles
1 T. Italian seasoning
1 14 1/2 oz. can diced tomatoes, drained
Salt
Crushed black pepper

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over low heat, cook spinach and garlic until spinach is wilted.
Add cream cheese, milk, bleu cheese crumbles, and Italian seasoning. Stir in diced tomatoes. Continue cooking over low heat, stirring frequently, until cheese is melted.
Salt and pepper to taste. Spoon topping over steak, chicken or fish.

Submitter: 
Leila
Description: 
This is a creamy topping that can be used on top of steaks, fish, or chicken.
