Title:
Prime Rib with Au Jus sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10-15 lb) beef rib loin
Salt and pepper
Garlic Powder
Olive Oil
Dry Mustard
Au Jus Sauce:
2 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
2 Tbsp. Master Gravy browning sauce(any gravy browning sauce will do)

Directions:
Lightly spread olive oil all over rib.  Rub HEAVY with salt and pepper.  Rub a minimum amount of dry mustard and garlic powder all over rib also.  Put roast, fat side up, in foil lined open pan with water under foil.  Bake at 300 for 6 hours.
Au Jus sauce:
Add the worcestershire sauce and browning sauce to the juices of the cooked meat.  Heat and pour sauce over each steak when serving.

Submitter: 
DAWNCIL
Description: 
Adding this au jus sauce mix with the natural juices of the cooked meat makes this prime rib wonderful.  I like this for our large Sunday meals.
