Title:
Spicy Ranch Dressing (Applebees Clone) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup sour cream
1 T milk
2 t tomato, minced
1 1/2 t white vinegar
1 t jalapeno, minced (nacho style)
1 t onion, minced
1/4 t parsley
1/4 t Tabasco sauce
1/8 t salt
1/8 t dill weed
1/8 t paprika
1/8 t cayenne
1/8 t cumin
1/8 t chili powder
Dash garlic powder
Dash black pepper

Directions:
Combine all and mix well to incorporate.
Cover and chill.
