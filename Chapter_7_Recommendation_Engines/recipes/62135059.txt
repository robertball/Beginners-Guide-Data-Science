Title:
Dianne's Pomegranate Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/284717.jpg
Ingredients:
1 oz. Pomegranate Juice
1 1/2 oz. Vodka
1/2 oz. Triple Sec or Cointreau
1 tsp. frozen limeade

Directions:
Combine all ingredients with ice in a cocktail shaker.  Shake and strain into a martini glass.  Garnish with a slice of lime, if desired.  Serves one.

Submitter: 
Dianne
Description: 
A tart and tangy cocktail.  I created this cocktail to suit my tastes, I hope you enjoy it too!  You can substitute silver tequila for the vodka to create a pomegranate margarita!
