Title:
Aunt Billie's Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/778144.jpg
Ingredients:
Meat Loaf:
1-1/2 Lb ground beef
1 Cup quick-cook oatmeal
1 onion, chopped
1/4 Cup green bell pepper, chopped
1/2 Cup ketchup
1 tsp salt
2 eggs, beaten
Sauce:
1 Cup ketchup
1/4 Cup brown sugar
2 Tbsp onion, chopped (or dry)
1 Tbsp green bell pepper, chopped

Directions:
Combine meatloaf ingredients and place in greased pan.
Bake at 350 for one hour.
Mix and heat topping ingredients in pan.
Drain grease, top meat loaf with sauce and bake 15 more min.
