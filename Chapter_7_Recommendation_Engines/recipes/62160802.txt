Title:
Easy One-Pan Jambalaya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-10oz. can Campbell's beef broth
1-10oz. can Campbell's french onion soup
1-10oz. can Campbell's golden mushroom soup
1-8oz. can Tomato sauce
1-14oz. can diced tomatoes with juice
1-4oz. can mushrooms, stems and pieces
3 cups Uncle Ben's convverted Rice (uncooked)
2 heaping Tbls. minced garlic
1 C. chopped bell pepper
1 C. chopped onion
1 C. chopped celery
1 C. cooked, chopped chicken
1 C. chopped andouille sausage
1 C. cooked, chopped pork or ham
1 stick of butter-cut up
1 tsp. Allspice
Hot Sauce to taste
Worcestershire sauce to taste

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees
Mix all ingredients together in a large roasting pan.
Cover tightly with heavy duty aluminum foil and bake 45 minutes.
After 45 minutes, uncover and stir. Replace foil and continue baking another 45 minutes or until the rice is tender.

Submitter: 
Nicky
Description: 
This recipe is so simple, so delicious and so versitile! Add, shrimp, crawfish, whatever you like! Just throw it all in the pan and pop it in the oven! Makes enough for a crowd!
