Title:
Slow Cooker Split Pea Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound dried split peas
8 cups chicken broth
2 ham hocks
1 cup chopped carrot
1-2 red potatoes, chopped
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
2 bay leaves (or 1/2 teaspoon crushed bay leaves)
pepper to taste (no salt needed)

Directions:
In a 5 quart slow cooker, combine all ingredients.
Cover, and cook on High for 4 to 5 hours. Remove bay leaves before ladling into bowls.

Submitter: 
Gin Jadav
Description: 
Dried split peas are combined in this slow cooker soup with carrots and potatoes.  Allow 5 hours cooking time.Modified from: Slow Cooker Split Pea Sausage Soup
