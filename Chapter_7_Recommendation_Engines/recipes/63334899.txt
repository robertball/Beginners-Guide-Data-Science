Title:
Asian Pear Curried Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lg chicken breasts
Rotisserie chicken seasoning
1 large Asian pear, cored and diced
1 apple, cored and diced
1/2 - 1 c sliced almonds
1/4 - 1/2 tsp curry powder
1/4 - 1/2 tsp ginger powder
1/4 tsp celery salt
1/2 c Miracle Whip light**
**(if using real mayo, add 1 t sugar)

Directions:
Bake chicken breasts, sprinkled liberally with rotisserie chicken seasoning in foil covered pan until done.
While cooling, dice fruit in about 1/2 in pieces. When cool enough to handle, cut chicken in very small 1/4 in. dice.
Place in bowl with remaining ingredients and stir until thoroughly mixed.  Taste for seasoning; add more spices if desired, but keep equal amounts of curry and ginger.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.  Serve with club crackers or on rolls.
