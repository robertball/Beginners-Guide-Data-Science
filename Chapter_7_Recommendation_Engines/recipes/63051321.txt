Title:
Natalie's amazing Irish mashed potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/767118.jpg
Ingredients:
3 1/2 lbs. potatoes, cubed or quartered
1 stick of butter
1/2 c. half & half
1 t. salt
1/4 t. garlic powder (optional)
1/4 t. pepper

Directions:
Wash and peel potatoes. cut into quarters or large cubes.
Cover and cook in a small amount of boiling water (enough to cover potatoes) for  20-25 minutes or until tender; drain.
Mash with a potato masher or beat with an eletric mixer on low speed.
In a small saucepan, heat the butter and the half & half until the butter is melted. Add the butter mixture to the beaten/mashed potatoes, along with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
Mash or beat on low speed until potatoes are light and fluffy. If necesary, add more half & half, 1 T. at a until the potatoes are the consistency you like.
