Title:
Crunchy Broccoli Cauliflower Salad Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
4 cups broccoli florets (make them small)
4 cups cauliflower florets (again small)
1/4 cup shredded carrots (I use the pre shredded ones)
1/4 cup dried cranberries
3 tablespoons roasted unsalted sunflower seeds (not the dry roasted ones)
For the sauce:
1/2 cup Miracle Whip salad dressing
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 1/2 to 2 tablespoons white sugar (start with less then add more if you wish)
Optional: if youâ€™d  like a bit of bite add 2 teaspoons Apple Cider Vinegar.

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in a bowl.

This is crunchy salad, made with basic coleslaw dressing. The dried cranberries and sunflower seeds make if different.  Have it as meal or as a side.
