Title:
Zucchini-Apple Wheat Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large or 2 small zucchini, shredded
1 medium apple, shredded
1 cup all purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/3 cup bran
1 1/4 cup sugar
2 tsp bk soda
2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp ground ginger
1/4 tsp salt
1 cup oil
3 eggs
2 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Heat oven to 350F.  Prepare muffin tins with paper liners or grease well.
Mix dry ingredients including sugar in a medium bowl. Add shredded apple and zucchini and mix well.
In a 2 cup or larger measuring cup, measure oil, then crack in eggs and add vanilla.  Whisk until frothy.
Add wet to dry mix and mix until just combined.  Do not overmix!
Drop by 1/4 cup full into 18 muffin cups.
Cook 15-20 minutes or until toothpick test comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Skoo
Description: 
Apple and zucchini lend moistness to these hearty muffins with whole wheat flour and a hint of bran. It's good any time of day- Breakfast, Lunch, or anytime snack. This recipe is easily substituted: try adding chocolate chips, walnuts, etc or carrots instead of the zucchini, or sub a portion of the oil for applesauce. The possibilities are endless!
