Title:
Moist Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/457956.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups of flour
1 teaspoon of baking soda
1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
1-1/2 cups of sugar
1 stick of butter
1/2 teaspon of salt
2 eggs
1 teaspoon of vanilla
4 Tablespoon of sour cream
6 small or 4 large riped banana

Directions:
-Sift flour with salt,l baking soda & baking powder.
-Cream butter, salt and sugar.
-Add eggs, salt, vanilla and sour cream- mix well
-Mash riped banana
-Add to mixture
-Coat budt pan with cooking spray
-Bake at 360 degree
