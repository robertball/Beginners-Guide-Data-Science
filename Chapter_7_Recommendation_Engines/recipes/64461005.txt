Title:
Simple Spanish rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2420242.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2  Cup rice,  preferably long grain rice
1/2 white onion,  medium size, diced
1 can (approx. 15 oz) diced tomatoes
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Combine rice, onion, diced tomatoes, and salt in a medium sized pot, set aside.
Saute onions (can also add meat or your choice raw vegetables to the onions if you'd like) until golden brown.
Add sauteed onions to pot with other ingredients.
Cook mixture on high until boiling.
Simmer, covered stirring occasionally for about 10  minutes or until rice is tender to your liking.
