Title:
Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1013849.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cans of French cut green beans
4 cans of Cream of Chicken soup
1 can of French Fried Onions
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix ingredients together in a 9 x 13 pan and warm all the way through for 30 minutesat 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Mindy Rose
Description: 
A Thanksgiving staple at our house here is our version of the Green Bean Casserole that is always a hit each year!
