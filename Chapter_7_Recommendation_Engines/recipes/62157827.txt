Title:
Cucumber Tomato Salad with zucchini & black olives and a Lemon Balsmic Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
SALAD:
2 Lrg Cucumbers - diced
1 Med Zucchini - diced
1/2 Med red onion - sliced thin
3 lrg Tomatoes - diced
1 Cup black or Kalamata Olives - chopped
2 Tbs Fresh Basil - chopped
2 tsp Fresh thyme - pulled from stem
Lemon Balsamic Vinaigrette
3 Tbs Red Wine Vinegar
1 Tbs Balsamic Vinegar
1-1/2 tsp lemon zest
Juice of 1/2 the Lemon
1 1/4 tsp Kosher Salt
1/2 tsp Sugar
1/4 tsp Fresh Ground Pepper
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil - drizzled in while whisking at last step

Directions:
In a non-reactive bowl mix salad ingredients.
Then in a separate non-reactive bowl Whisk together the ingredients of the vinaigrette. Lastly while whisking add the olive oil slowly.
Combine Salad and Vinaigrette and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Dan Zahra
Description: 
This is a deliciously light & fresh tasting twist on Cucumber Tomato Salad. It takes me to the waters edge & begs to be served with fresh sea food.
