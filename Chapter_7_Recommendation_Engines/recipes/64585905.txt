Title:
Roasted Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Brussel sprouts (cut in half)
Asparagus (cut in half)
Sweet onion (quartered)
Mushrooms (cut in half)
Broccoli (cut into bite size florets)
Cauliflower (cut into bite size florets)
Carrots (cut into strips)
Olive oil (or any good oil)
Granulated garlic
Chopped garlic
Sea Salt
Paprika
Black pepper
Sriracha

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees
Prep veggies by cleaning and chopping them as suggested in the ingredients list.
Place all the cut veggies in a large bowl.
Drizzle with a good amount of olive oil.
Add seasoning, mix well and TASTE
Add more seasoning if desired.
Spread on jelly roll pan in an even layer, one veggie deep (make sure this step is followed or you will steam your veggies not roast them)
Bake in hot oven from anywhere between 25-45 minutes depending on your oven and desired doneness.
