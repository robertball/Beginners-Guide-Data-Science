Title:
Quick and Easy Sweet and Sour Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1029238.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef; 1 cup bread crumbs; 1 egg; 2 tablespoons minced onion; 1 clove garlic, minced; 2 tablespoons milk; 1/2 teaspoon salt; 1/8 teaspoons pepper; 2 tablespoons vegetable oil; 1 cup grape jelly; 1 cup chili sauce

Directions:
Pour vegetable oil into a large skillet.
In a bowl, combine beef, bread crumbs, egg, minced onion, garlic, milk, salt, and pepper. Mix together using hands. Form into bite-sized meatballs and place into skillet.
Brown meatballs lightly on all sides.
Combine chili sauce and jelly. Mix together using a whisk until smooth. Pour over meatballs.
Cover and simmer 10 to 12 minutes until sauce has thickened, stirring occasionally, being careful not to break apart meatballs.

Submitter:

Description: 
This recipe has been in my family for years. It's a quick version of a dish that usually calls for a slow cooker. It has all the taste with less than half the ingredients of many sweet and sour sauces. Great as an appetizer or served over white rice as a main dish!
