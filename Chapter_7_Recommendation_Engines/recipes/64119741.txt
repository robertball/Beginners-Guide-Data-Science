Title:
Popa's Pickled Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1297879.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Popa's Pickled Eggs (from this site)
6 Tablespoon's mayonaise
1-2 teaspoons "Popa's Pickled Eggs" juice
Salt & Pepper to taste
Ancho Chili Powder for garnish

Directions:
Slice eggs in half lengthwise and remove yolks from the eggs. Set egg white halves aside.
Add mayonaise, pickled egg juice and salt & pepper to egg yolks and mash with a fork until smooth.
Fill egg whites with egg yolk mixture and garnish with Ancho Chili Powder.

Submitter: 
Candice
Description: 
After making bdweld's Popa's Pickled Eggs for the PNW Round Up, discussion about turning these gems into Deviled Eggs turned round table as we all wondered if it could in fact be a viable recipe. The answer is ABSOLUTELY YES! Making deviled eggs out of Pickled Eggs was an ingenious idea! They are not only delicious, but addicting!
