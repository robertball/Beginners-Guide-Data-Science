Title:
cinnomin sugar tortilla strips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 whole wheat or white flour tortillas
2 c. light olive oil or any light tasting vegtable oil
cinnomin and sugar mix

Directions:
cut tortillas in half. Then cut into 1/4 inch strips.
heat oil in a large sauce pan over medium heat. Toss in a small strip of tortilla if it sizzles it is ready.
place 1/4 of cut up tortilla in oil. Allow to fry untill bubbly and lightly brown.
remove with sloted metal spoon. place on papper towels, sprinkle with cinnoman and sugar mix.
continue with rest of tortilla strips.
Allow to cool before serving.
The tortilla strips may also be sprayed with pam, then sprinkled with cinnoman and sugar and placed on a nonstick cookie sheet in a 350o oven for 15-20 min or until crispy.
