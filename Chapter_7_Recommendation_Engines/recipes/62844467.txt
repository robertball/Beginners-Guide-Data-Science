Title:
Black Beans and Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
2# dry black beans, sorted and rinsed
3-4# beef roast
1-2 tsp salt (to own taste)
2 tsp oregano
3 tsp chicken bouillon
1# smoked sausage, chopped
4 cloves garlic
8-9 cups steamed rice
vinaigrette-
1 cucumber, diced
1 tomato, diced
1-2 Tbls olive oil
1-2 Tbls white vinegar
1-2 Tbls water
salt, sprinkling
parsley, sprinkling

Directions:
Place onions in crock pot then place roast on top.  Put remaining ingredients  in crock pot, cover with water.
Cook on low all day.  Roast will fall apart, beans will be tender.
For vinaigrette, stir together cucumber, tomato, olive oil, vinegar, water , salt and parsley.
Serve beans over rice, top with vinaigrette.
