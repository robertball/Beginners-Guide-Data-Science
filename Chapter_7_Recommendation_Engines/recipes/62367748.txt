Title:
Bethenny Frankel Healthy Brown Rice Breakfast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup brown rice
1/4 C soy milk
1/2 sp. cinnamon
1 T dried cranberries or raisins
1 tsp. sliced almonds
1 T maple syrup

Directions:
Combine 1/2 cup brown rice (must mean cooked) with 1/4 C soy milk, 1/2 tsp. cinnamon, 1 T dried cranberries or raisins, 1 tsp. sliced almonds and 1 T maple syrup in a saucepan.
Heat over medium heat.
