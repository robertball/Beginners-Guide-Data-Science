Title:
Ethel's Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 1/2 Cups Confectioners Sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
2 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. cream of tarter
1/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Cream the butter
Add confectioners sugar
Add unbeaten egg
Mix balance of dry ingredients separetely then add slowly to the other bowl
Press with cookie press
Bake in pre-heated oven at 375 degrees
