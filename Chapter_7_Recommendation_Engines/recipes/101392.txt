Title:
Orange Glazed Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/860989.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (3/4 pound) pork tenderloins
1 cup orange juice
1/2 cup white wine
1/4 cup soy sauce
1 clove garlic, peeled and crushed
1 tablespoon butter, melted
2 tablespoons orange marmalade
2 tablespoons honey

Directions:
Place tenderloins in large resealable plastic bag. Blend together remaining ingredients; pork over pork and seal bag. Marinate in refrigerator for 2 to 24 hours.
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Remove pork from marinade and discard marinade. Roast pork for about 20 minutes, or until internal temperature as measured with an instant-read thermometer reaches 155-160 degrees F. Slice to serve.

Submitter: 
National Pork Board
Description: 
A recipe that's sure to have a long life with your family. Serve with wild rice mix and steamed asparagus.
