Title:
Beau's bacon marinara and meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bacon Sauce:
7-8 bacon strips, cut
1 onion, diced
1 clove garlic, minced
Salt & pepper to taste
1 28 oz. can chunky diced tomatoes (I used cruhed instead). Recipe called for 2 cans. Just seemed like too much.
Meatballs:
Oil, greasing pan
2 lbs ground beef (80/20)
3/4 cup breadcrumbs
3/4 cup freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano (I used regular grated because it was all I had and it it still tasted good)
3/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped (I used 1/2 cup Italian parsley)
2 eggs, beaten
Salt & pepper
Spaghettini
1 lb bucatini pasta (used spaghetini) -- cook all, it's yummy
3/4 Cup

Directions:
Bacon Sauce:
Cut bacon.into pieces. Using a big saut√© pan with cover. Cook over medium heat until almost crispy. Do NOT drain. Add onions and cook til translucent, add garlic and salt and pepper.  Add tomatoes and bring to boil.  Reduce heat and cover and simmer on low for 1 to 1-1/2 hours.
Meatballs:
Preheat oven to 425
Grease baking sheet. In large bowl combine ingredients. Form 1-1/2 inch balls. Bake for 5 minutes. Turn heat down to 350 degrees and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes.
