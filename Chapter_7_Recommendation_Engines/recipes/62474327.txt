Title:
Cheese Ball with Strawberry Preserves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 jar real bacon bits
1 bunch of green onions, chopped
2-3 cups mayonnaise
1/2 jar strawberry preserves

Directions:
Mix cheese, chopped green onions, and bacon bits, together.  Fold in mayonnaise until the mixture can be molded into a ball.  Place the ball on a serving dish and spread strawberry preserves on top. Serve with wheat crackers or buttery round crackers.
