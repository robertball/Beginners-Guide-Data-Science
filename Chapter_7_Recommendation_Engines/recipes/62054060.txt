Title:
Mom's Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken cut up (or 4 chicken breasts)
2 cups long grain rice
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of celery soup
1 can water
1 pkg onion soup mix

Directions:
1.  Pre-heat oven to 350
2.  Spray large casserole with non-stick cooking spray
3.  In a large bowl, mix together rice, canned soups and water
4.  Scrape into casserole dish and place chicken pieces on top of rice.
5.  Sprinkle package of onion soup mix over everything.
6.  Cover and bake for 60 minutes.
