Title:
Carolina  Pull  Barbecue 1 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 - 6 lbs pork, chicken, or beef
Rub:
2 tbl brown sugar
1 Tbl paprika
1/2 tea salt
1/2 black pepper
Sauce:
3/4 cup cider vinegar
4 tea Worcestershire sauce
1 Tbl red pepper flakes
4 Tbl sweetner / sugar
1 Tbl mustard
1 tea garlic powder
1 tea cayenne pepper

Directions:
Spray crockpot with pam.  Quarter onions and place in bottom of pot.
Combine brown sugar, paprika, salt & pepper.  Place meat over onions and sprinkle with mix.
Combine remaining ingred, stirring to mix well.  Drizzle meat with about 1/3 of mixture, cover and cook on low 10 to 12 hours.  Over last 1/2 hours of cooking, drizzle with 1/3 c of vingar mixture.
Remove meat and shred with 2 forks.  Return to pot and add remaining liquid.
Cook for several hours longer.
If need to, you may also add some barbecue sauce.
this just gives it color and flavor.
