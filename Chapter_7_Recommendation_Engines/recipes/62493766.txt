Title:
Lena's Party Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2- 11oz drained can of corn
1- 4.5oz green chilies, drained and chopped
1 bunch green onions, chopped
2 tbsp finely chopped jalapenos
2-4 cloves garlic, minced or grated
1/2 tbsp sugar
1 cup light mayo
1 cup light sour cream

Directions:
Add everything into a large bowl and mix well!

Submitter: 
Lena635
Description: 
This dip is wonderfully favorful, inredibly easy to make & is always a hit at gatherings!  Great with blue corn chips or baked pita!  Even great as a topping on hotdogs or hamburgers!
