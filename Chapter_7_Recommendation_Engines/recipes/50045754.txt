Title:
Terri's Wild Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/598569.jpg
Ingredients:
6TBLS Butter
1 TBLS minced onion
1/2 cup flour
3 cups chicken broth
2 cups cooked wild rice
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup half and half
2 TBLS dry sherry (opt)
1/2 cup shredded carrots (opt)
1/2 cup Ham, turkey, chicken, or whatever meat you want. (opt)
3 TBLS slivered almonds

Directions:
Melt butter in sauce pan and saute onions until tender.
Blend in flour, gradually stir in broth. Cook , stirring constantly until mixture comes to a boil. Boil and stir for 1 minute.
Stir in rice, meat, carrots, almonds, and salt. Simmer for 5 minutes.
Blend in half and half and sherry. Heat to serving temp. Enjoy!
