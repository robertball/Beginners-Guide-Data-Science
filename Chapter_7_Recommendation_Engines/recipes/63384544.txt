Title:
Lemonade Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can frozen raspberry-lemonade concentrate (12 oz)
1 bottle sweet white wine (such as Riesling or     Gewurztraminer) 750 ml
1 pkg frozen mixed berries (12 oz)
1 lemon sliced
2 cups chilled club soda

Directions:
Mix concentrate and wine in a large pitcher until concentrate is dissolved.  Add frozen berries and lemon slices; cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour or until chilled and ready to serve.  Just before serving, stir in club soda.

Submitter: 
kelly
Description: 
Cool and super refreshing on a warm spring day, this sangria can be non-alcoholic too.  Substitute white grape juice or lemon-lime soda for the wine.
