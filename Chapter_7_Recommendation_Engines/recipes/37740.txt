Title:
Apple Cranberry Salad Toss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981423.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) package salad greens (about 10 cups)
2 medium apples, sliced
1 cup PLANTERS Walnut Halves, toasted
1 cup sweetened dried cranberries
1/2 cup sliced green onions
3/4 cup SEVEN SEAS Raspberry Vinaigrette Dressing

Directions:
Toss greens, apples, walnuts, cranberries and onions in large bowl.
Add dressing; toss to coat.  Serve immediately.
