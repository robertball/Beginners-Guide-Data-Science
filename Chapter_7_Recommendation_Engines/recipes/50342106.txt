Title:
Quick Chicken with Asparagus and Provolone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
4 tablespoons olive oil
Emeril's seasoning
1 egg, beaten
1 cup Italian-style dry bread crumbs
1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
4 slices provolone cheese

Directions:
Heat oil in a large 2-inch deep skillet. Dip chicken in egg beat, then coat in bread crumbs. Brown in skillet on both sides.
When chicken is browned, add broth and place asparagus evenly over chicken.
Cover skillet and cook over medium heat for 15 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through and no longer pink inside.  Top with cheese and serve.
