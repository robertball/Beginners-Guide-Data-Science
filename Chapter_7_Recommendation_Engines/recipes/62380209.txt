Title:
Maple Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C water
1 1/4 C sugar
3/4 C brown sugar
1/4 t. vanilla
1/4 t. maple flavor

Directions:
Combine water and sugars in a sauce pan.
Boil until sugars are dissolved.
Stir in vanilla and maple flavor.
Serve hot or allow to cool to thicken.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
Why buy syrup when you can make it for less and make it how you like it? I love to add a bit of cinnomon and nutmeg.
