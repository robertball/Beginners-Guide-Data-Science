Title:
Barb's Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. (1 stick) butter
1 c. sugar
1c. dark corn syrup
1/2 tsp. salt
1+1/2 tsp. vanilla
3 eggs, lightly beaten
2 c. chopped pecans
unbaked piecrust for 9" pie

Directions:
1. Allow butter to stand in covered meduim mixing bowl at room temperature until extremely soft.
2. Preheat oven at 375.
3. Add sugar, corn syrup, salt, and vanilla to butter, and gently mix at a low speed.
4. With a spoon add eggs to the sugar/butter mix.
5. Fold in pecans.
6. Pour mixture into pastry shell.
7. Bake pie at 375 on bottom rack for 40-50 minutes, until the top is firm.
