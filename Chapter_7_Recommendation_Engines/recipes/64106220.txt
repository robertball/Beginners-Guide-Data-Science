Title:
Easy Southwest Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp oil
4 chicken breasts
1 can golden corn soup
8oz can stewed tomatoes, cut up
1 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
1. In 10 inch skillet over med-high heat, cook chicken until browned on both sides (about 10 minutes)Remove, set aside. Spoon off fat
2. In same skillet, combine soup, tomatoes, chili powder and garlic powder. Heat to boiling. Return chicken to skillet. Reduce heat to low. Cover; cook 5 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink, stirring occasionally.
Tip: Prepare as directed above, except in step 2, sprinkle 1/2 cup Monterey Jack cheese and 2 tbsp finely chopped green pepper over the cooked chicken. Remove from heat. Cover; let stand 1 minutes until cheese melts.
