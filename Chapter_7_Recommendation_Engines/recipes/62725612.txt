Title:
"Perfectly Chocolate" Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/622597.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1 3/4 cup all purpose flour
3/4 cup HERSHEY'S Cocoa
1 1/2 tsp. baking soda
1 1/2 tsp.baking powder
1 tsp. salt
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 tsp. vanilla extract
1 cup boiling water
"Perfectly Chocolate" Chocolate Frosting ( recipe follows )

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two 9 Inch round baking pans
Stir together sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in large bowl. Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla,;beat no mediun speed of mixer 2 min. Stir in water ( batter will be thin ) pur into prepared pans.
Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until wooden pick insered in center comes out clean.  Cool 10 min.; remove from pans to wire rack. Cool completely.
Pepare "Perfectly Chocolate" Chocolate Frosting; spread between layers and over top and sides of cake.
