Title:
BUTTER TARTS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/500058.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 c butter
1 c brown sugar
2 tbsp milk
1/2 c raisins
1 egg, beaten
1 tsp vanilla
pastry tart shells
(see recipe for sweet shortcrust, OR store bought)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 F gas mark 8.
Mix all ingedients together and spoon into tart shells.
Bake for 8 mins, reduce temp to 350 F gas mark 4 until golden brown. Approx 5-10 mins.

Submitter: 
Dannielle Philipson
Description: 
A Canadian Christmas favourite. I double or triple the recipe because these never last for very long.
