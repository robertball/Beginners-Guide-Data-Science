Title:
Handmade Italian Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
12oz bulk italian sausage (hot or sweet)
1/2 cup fine, dry bread crumbs
1/4 cup milk
1 egg, slightly beaten
1/4 cup fresh italian parsley, finely chopped
1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
1 tsp dried italian seasoning
1 tsp kosher salt
1/4 tsp freshly ground pepper
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
1. In large mixing bowl combine ground beef, sausage, bread crumbs, milk, egg, parsely, cheese, italian seasoning, salt, and pepper until well combined. Form mixture into balls, about 1-1/2 inches in diameter.
2. In large skillet, cook the meatballs in hot olive oil over medium heat for 10-20 minutes until browned and meat thermometer reads 160 degree F, turning occasionally.
