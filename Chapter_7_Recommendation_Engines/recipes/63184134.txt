Title:
Amy's Israel Beet Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup diced beet raw
1/4 cup pine nuts toasted
fresh mint
goat cheese or blue cheese
1 T red onion
Arugala or other green lettuce

Directions:
Dice beets, toast pine nuts, slice mint, dice red onions.  Add everything to a bowl and toss.  Serve with your favorite balsimic dressing.
