Title:
Keto Candied Nuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 egg white
1 tablespoon water
1 cup pecans
1/4 cup monk fruit sugar substitute, or more to taste
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon Himalayan sea salt

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C).
Beat egg white and water in a glass, metal, or ceramic bowl with an electric mixer until peaks form. Stir in pecans to coat.
Combine monk fruit sugar substitute, cinnamon, and sea salt in a small bowl. Sprinkle over pecans and mix to combine. Spread out on a baking sheet.
Bake in the preheated oven for about 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.
