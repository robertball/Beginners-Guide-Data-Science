Title:
Cool Kale Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1917725.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups green grapes
2 cups kale
1 cup ice cubes

Directions:
Blend grapes, kale, and ice together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Ginger Paro
Description: 
This is an easy way to get raw kale into your diet. The grapes masks the taste of kale and the end result is a tasty, cool smoothie!
