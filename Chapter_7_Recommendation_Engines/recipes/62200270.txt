Title:
Blueberry Salad Mold Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15-oz can blueberries in light syrup
1 6-oz can pineapple juice
1 6-oz package lemon gelatin
1 1/2 cup cold water
1 cup diced bananas
1/2 pint heavy cream, whipped

Directions:
Drain the blueberries, pouring the liquid into a small saucepan.
Add the pineapple juice, and bring just to a boil and remove from the heat.
Stir in the gelatin until dissolved.
Add the cold water and chill until the mixture is the consistency of unbeaten egg whites.
Stir in bananas and blueberries.
Fold in the whipped cream, leaving large swirls in the design.
Pour the mixture into a large oiled mold and chill until firm.

Submitter: 
Marianne
Description: 
No ham or turkey dinner could ever be complete in our home without this beautifully elegant molded salad!  It always takes precedence as the centerpiece favorite offering!
