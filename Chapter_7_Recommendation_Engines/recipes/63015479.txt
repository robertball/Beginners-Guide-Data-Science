Title:
Nanas No Bake Chocolate Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 2 cups sugar
2. 1/2 cup cocoa
3. 1/2 cup milk
4. 4 tablespoons butter
5. 1/2 cup peanut butter
6. 1 tablespoon vanilla
7. 3 cups quick cooking oatmeal dry

Directions:
In med saucepan stir together 1. 2. 3. 4. bring to a boil stirring constantly...
Stir in 5. and 6. reduce heat
Add 7. mix well and remove from heat
Drop by teaspoonful onto wax paper
cool... refrigerate to store
