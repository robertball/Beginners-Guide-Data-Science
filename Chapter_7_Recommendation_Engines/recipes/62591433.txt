Title:
Homemade Chicken Stock Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/871674.jpg
Ingredients:
1	onion - chopped
2	carrots - chopped
1	chicken - giblets removed
5	cloves garlic
6	peppercorns
4	bay leaves
1 tsp	dried thyme
1 bunch	fresh parsley
1 	tomato - chopped

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a large stock pot, then cover with water.
Simmer covered for 1 hour and 15 minutes, until chicken is cooked
through. Remove chicken. Once it is cool enouh to handle, remove meat
from the bones and reserve for soup. Strain the stock and cool. The
fat will rise to the top and can be skimmed off before using.
