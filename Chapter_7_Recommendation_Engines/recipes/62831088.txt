Title:
Cucumber and Yogurt Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. (16 oz.) plain yogurt
One clove of garlic
Juice of a small lemon (about 2 tablespoons juice)
1/4 cup fresh, chopped mint
3/4 cup diced cucumber
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Press the garlic through a garlic press.  Add remaining ingredients and chill for an hour to allow flavors to mingle.

Submitter: 
Noor
Description: 
Creamy, a little tart, and perfect with grilled meats and rice or pita bread. A Middle-Eastern traditional salad.
