Title:
Chuckwagon Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds cubed beef stew meat
1/2 pound smoked sausage of your choice, sliced
1 medium onion, chopped
3 potatoes, scrubbed and cubed
1 (28 ounce) can barbeque baked beans
1 package fresh baby carrots

Directions:
Season the beef with salt, pepper, garlic, chili powder and cumin.
Place the beef stew meat, sausage, onion, carrots, and potatoes into a slow cooker, and stir to distribute evenly. Spoon the baked beans evenly over the mixture. Cover, and cook on Low for 8 to 10 hours, or cook on High for 4 to 5 hours.

Submitter: 
Cori
Description: 
Beef cubes and bar-b-que beans combine quickly in this hearty and tasty slow cooked meal. No need to brown the meat makes this extra easy for the working mom. Great with cornbread or brown bread and a cold beer.Modified from: Chuckwagon Stew
