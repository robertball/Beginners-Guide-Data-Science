Title:
Seven Layer Taco Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/277550.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1 ounce) package mild taco seasoning mix
1 (16 ounce) can refried beans
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
1 (16 ounce) jar mild salsa
1 large tomato, chopped
1 (6 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained
2 cups shredded medium Cheddar cheese

Directions:
1. In a small saucepan warm the beans and taco seasoning over medium heat, just until heated through. Set aside.
Place a 3-inch wide round cookie cutter (or a tuna can with both ends cut off) on a plate. Spoon 2 tablespoons beans in the bottom. Top with 2 tablespoons each of sour cream mixture, salsa and cheese and sprinkle with olives.
