Title:
Southwestern Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 eggs
1 T taco seasoning
1/8 t salt
1/3 c mayonnaise

Directions:
Place eggs in saucepan and cover with water. Bring to boil. Cover, remove from heat, and let eggs sit in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot water and cool.
Peel and cut in half lengthwise. Scoop out yolks into a bowl and mash with the mayonnaise, salt, and taco seasoning. Take  spoonfuls of the yolk mixture and refill the egg white halves.

Submitter: 
Ms. Munchie
Description: 
An alternative to traditional deviled eggs - add some spice! Adjust the spiciness according to your taste.
