Title:
Taco Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can refreid beans
1 pack McCormick taco seasoning
1 cup water
1 jar Kraft cheese whiz
1 bag Kraft shredded taco cheese
2 lb. ground beef
1 bag of tortilla chips

Directions:
In a hot skillet, brown the ground beef.
Drain.
Add this to a slow cooker.
Add all other ingredient, reserving one cup of the shredded cheese.
Stir to mix.
Cook on low for 2 hours.
Let each person pour this mixture over a bed of tortilla chips.
Top with reserved shredded cheese and other favorite toppings.

Submitter: 
Kristi Whittington
Description: 
A quick and easy way to satisfy your families taco appetite.
