Title:
Crock-Pot Beef Tips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 T. shortening
2 lbs. sirloin tips, cut in 1" squares
1 can beef consomme
1/2 cup red cooking wine
2 T. soy sauce
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 t. onion salt
2 T. cornstarch
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Brown meat in shortening; stir in consomme, wine, soy sauce, garlic, and onion salt.  Heat to full boil.  Transfer to crock-pot.  Cook on HIGH for 30 minutes.  Reduce to LOW and cook 5 to 7 hours.  Just before serving, blend cornstarch and water in cup.  Stir into meat until thick.
