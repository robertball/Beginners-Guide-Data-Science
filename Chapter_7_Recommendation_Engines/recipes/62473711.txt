Title:
Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 LaSAgna noodles!
2 pounds of ground beef
1 pound Italian Sausage
1 medium onion
2 garlic cloves
2 16oz cans of tomato sauce
2 6oz cans of tomato paste
16oz ricotta
3 cups mozzarella
1/2c parmesan- shredded
2 eggs

Directions:
Cook noodles 9-10 minutes. Drain in cold water. Put it on wax paper. Brown sausage and beef with onions until cooked. Drain, add garlic sauce, paste, and garlic. Heat it. Mix cheese and eggs. Reserve 1 cup mozzarella.
Spread 1 cup meat sauce in the pan. Layer 4 noodles- place 1/2 cheese and 1/2 meat sauce on top. Repeat layers. Place remaining mozzarella cheese on top.
Heat 375 for 1 hour.
