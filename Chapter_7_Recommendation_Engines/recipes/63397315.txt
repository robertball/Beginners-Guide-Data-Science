Title:
Grandma B's Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can navy beans, drained
1 can bean with bacon soup
2 cans water
2 carrots, sliced into coins
1 medium potato, peeled and diced
3 stalks celery, sliced
1 medium onion, diced
1 Knorr chicken boullion cube

Directions:
Lightly saute carrots, potatoes, celery, and onion.
Add one can bean with bacon soup, two cans of water and 1 Knorr chicken boullion cube.  Simmer, stirring frequently for about 15 minutes.
Blend with a hand blender leaving some small chunks.
Add one can of drained navy beans.
Heat through about another 15 minutes and serve.
