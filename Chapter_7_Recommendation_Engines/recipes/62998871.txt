Title:
True Cheddar Bay Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Gluten Free biscuit baking mix
1/3 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1/2 teaspoon Old Bay seasoning
1 egg beaten
2/3 cup milk
TOPPING:
1/4 cup melted butter
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon Old Bay seasoning
1 teaspoon dried parsley
pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.  Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. In large bowl mix all of the biscuit ingredients together until just blended.  Using a cookie scoop, drop batter onto parchment 3 across and 4 down.  Bake for 12 min.
While the biscuits are baking melt butter in pot over med heat just until melted, do not burn the butter.  Sprinkle in spices and keep warm until biscuits are done.  Using a pastry brush, when just out of the oven, coat the tops of the hot biscuits with the melted butter mixture. And serve immediately.  God these are good!

Submitter: 
Mary P.
Description: 
I could eat an entire dozen of these fresh out of the oven!  Great with soup.
