Title:
Seasoning For Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons chili powder
5 teaspoons paprika
4 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
3 teaspoons onion powder
2 teaspoons salt
2 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first seven ingredients. Store in an airtight container in a cool dry place for up to 6 months.
To prepare taco meat: In a saucepan, cook beef over medium heat until no longer pink; drain. Add 1/2 cup water and 3 Tblsp seasoning mix. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes.
