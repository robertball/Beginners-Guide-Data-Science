Title:
Chicken Cacciatore NM Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken, cut into quarters
1 12 oz. jar of HeinzÂ® Chili Sauce
1/3 cup soy sauce
1 clove garlic, finely diced
1 8 oz. pkg. sliced mushrooms
1 4 oz. can diced chiles

Directions:
Remove the skin from chicken if desired.
Place in crock pot.
Mix together chili sauce, soy sauce and garlic.
Pour over chicken.
Cook on high for 2 hours and low for 3-4 hours or until chicken falls off the bone.
Remove chicken and cool.
Separate chicken from bones, fat and remaining skin.
Cool broth and remove any fat that rises to the top of container.
Return the chicken to the broth, add muchrooms and chilies.  Simmer until mushrooms are tender.
Serve with angel hair or any pasta.
