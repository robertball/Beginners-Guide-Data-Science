Title:
Two Nanas Watermelon Rind Pickles / Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large watermelon- needs to have thick rind
4 cups sugar
2 quarts water
2 cups apple cider vinegar
6-8 sticks cinnamon
1 teas salt
1 tablespoon allspice
2 tablespoons whole cloves
2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

Directions:
cut up watermelon in 1/4s...take out the flesh of the  melon part but leave some pink on the rind
cut into 1" wide strips and peel the skin off...slice into 1/2"-1" slices set aside
In large sauce pan stir sugar and water till sugar dissloves
Add vinegar and spices cook for 10 min ...till boiling..
Place watermelon rind slices in very large pot - carefully pour hot sugar water over...bring back to boil and stir occasionally  for 45 min
use slotted spoon to spoon into hot sterile pint and quart jars..( make sure each jar has cloves and some cinnamon stick) fill with the sugar liguid to the rim leave 1/2" space...seal with canning lid...place in hot bath and process 30 min ...makes 3 quart size jars and 3 jelly jars...ready to eat in a week or so

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
Combined a few recipies ...added our own likes... a 3 hr process ( not 10 days as some did)...lower salt in this recipe ...great Nana said the flavor is there....thats what counts
