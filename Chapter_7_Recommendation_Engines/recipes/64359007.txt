Title:
Basic Chicken Salad Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 oz can Chicken (can sub cooked and cubed or shredded chicken)
1/4 cup chopped Celery
1/3 cup Mayo
Pepper
Bread
Lettuce (optional)
Tomato (optional)

Directions:
Chop celery and mix all ingredients together. Assemble sandwiches or set out for students to make themselves.

Submitter: 
SST
Description: 
This is a basic recipe and can be modified & added to. Just keep in mind teenage taste preferences. You may also substitute tuna. Serve on lettuce as a salad or on bread as a sandwich with chips and veggie sticks or another side.
