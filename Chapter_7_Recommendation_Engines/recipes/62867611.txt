Title:
John's Seafood Bisque Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sweet butter
1/3 Cup flour
1.25 pound fresh OR frozen seafood (cut-up lobster, shelled shrimp, scallops, crabmeat OR imitation crabmeat)
2Cups of Chicken stock
3 Cups of whole milk
1 cup heavy cream
4 cups half-and-half
1/2 cup fresh chopped celery
1/3 cup of carrots diced or grated coarse
hot pepper sauce to taste
1/2 Cup of Pinot Grigio wine

Directions:
Melt butter in large saucepan
Add 1/3 cup of flour Add your choice of seafood
Add celery & carrots
Add chicken stock and simmer for 30 minutes.
TURN HEAT TO LOW ALLOW TO SIMMER ANOTHER 15 MINUTES.
REMOVE 1.5 CUP OF BROTH AND VEGETABLES, ALLOW TO COOL.
Combine 3 cups milk, heavy cream and half-and-half to  stir until smooth.
Add 1.5 cups of cool broth-veggies.
Heat on low heat until entire mixture is warm through.
Season with hot pepper sauce and Pinot Grigio.

Submitter: 
greek
Description: 
An elegant, creamy seafood bisque with a hint of sherry flavor is surprisingly quick and easy to make when you use condensed soups as a base.Modified from: Campbell's Kitchen Seafood Bisque
