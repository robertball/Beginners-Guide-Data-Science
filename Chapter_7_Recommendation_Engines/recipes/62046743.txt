Title:
Strawberry Angelfood Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large store bought angel food cake
2 cups boiling water
1 16 oz. bag frozen unsweetened strawberries, sliced
2 small boxes sugar free strawberry jello
1 8 oz. carton fat free cool whip

Directions:
Tear cake into pieces.  Put cake in a 9 by 13 inch cake pan.  Combine water and jello.  Stir until jello is dissolved. Pour jello over cake.  Add strawberies.  Stir everything together.  Cover with cool whip and chill.

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
I am diabetic, but love dessert.  You'd never guess that this dessert is low sugar, because it is so high in taste!!  It is so easy to prepare that my eight year old can make it(He does everything except boli the water.).
