Title:
Vegetables and Black Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cup(s) lettuce, green leaf, chopped
1 1/4 cup(s) beans, black drained and rinsed
1 cup(s) cucumber(s) chopped
1 cup(s) tomato(es) chopped
1 tablespoon oil, olive, extra-virgin
salt to taste
peppercorns, black freshly ground, to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients into a bowl, toss and enjoy.
