Title:
Holy Cow Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box German Chocolate Cake Mix
1 can Eagle Brand Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 jar of Caramel Ice Cream Topping
1 8-oz Tub Cool Whip
English Toffee Bits

Directions:
Preheat oven and prepare cake mix as per directions on box for 9"X12" pan.  While cake is cooking stir together the sweetened condensed milk and caramel ice cream topping.  When cake is done, poke holes in cake with a straw and slowly pour milk/caramel mixture in holes.  Cook cake completely and spread with cool whip on top.  Finish by sprinkling with English Toffee Bits if desired.
