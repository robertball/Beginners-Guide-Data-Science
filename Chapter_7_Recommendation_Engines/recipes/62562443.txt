Title:
Granny's Filled Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/538290.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup shortening
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1/4 cup sour milk (put 1 tsp. vinegar into milk to make it sour)
1 tsp vanilla
3-1/2 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 tsp ground nutmeg
Strawberry preserves (Jam)

Directions:
In a large bowl, cream together shortening, sugar and brown sugar. Add eggs, sour milk, and vanilla and mix till smooth.
Sift together dry ingredients; stir into batter.
CHILL for several hours or overnight.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
On floured surface, roll dough to 1/8 inch thickness. Cut into 2 inch rounds with cookie cutter.
Place 1 tsp preserves in the center of half the cookie rounds; cover with remaining rounds. Lightly press edges together with a fork. With a knife, cut crisscross slits into the top of each cookie.
On un-greased cookie sheet or parchment paper, bake 13 to 15 minutes or until cookies are golden.

Submitter: 
Julie Holden
Description: 
Lightly spiced, soft cookies with a strawberry jam filling. Mmmmm! These are my favourite of the cookies mom makes every Christmas.
