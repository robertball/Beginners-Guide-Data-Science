Title:
Royal Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 six ounce cans of light tuna packed in water
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 or 2 green onions, chopped
2 boiled eggs, chopped
15-20 red seedless grapes, halved
Juice of one lemon
2-3 teaspoons curry powder
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
salt to taste

Directions:
Soak grape slices in fresh lemon juice.  Mix tuna with mayonnaise, curry, cayenne, salt and pepper.  Add chopped green onion, eggs and grapes to tuna mixture and toss lightly.  Great on soft party rolls!

Submitter: 
kpaigel
Description: 
Savory curry and a hint of pepper blended with sweet grapes put an exotic twist on this tuna salad!
