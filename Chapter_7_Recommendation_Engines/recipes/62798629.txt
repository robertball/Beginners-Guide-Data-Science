Title:
Tamarind Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c tamarind pulp, seeds removed
1 T ginger paste
1 T brown sugar or jaggery
3 T water
1/4 tsp ground chili (cayenne)

Directions:
Let tamarind pulp soak about a half an hour in warm water to reconstitute.  Add with all other ingredients to a blender and blend to a fine paste.

Submitter: 
KitchenBarbarian
Description: 
A sweet 'n sour chutney good with papad or snacks like bajia/pakora or samosa

From Flavors of India by Shanta Nimbark Sacharoff
