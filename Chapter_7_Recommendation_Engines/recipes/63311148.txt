Title:
Hashbrown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 ounce bag pepper and onions shredded hashbrowns
16 ounce bag shredded hashbrowns
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 cup sour cream
1/4 c unsalted butter, melted
1 c. Velvetta shredded cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 2 quart baking dish.
Whisk together soup, sour cream, melted butter and Velvetta. Stir in hashbrowns. Pour in baking dish. Cover.
Bake for 30 min. Remove cover. Continue baking for 45 min or until hot and bubbly.

Description: 
Creamed soup, sour cream, butter, and cheese make the most of this casserole!
