Title:
Sweet Greens Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4520604.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup spinach leaves
1/3 cup strawberries, hulled and chopped
1/3 cup cherries, pitted
1 cup Almond Breeze Almondmilk Unsweetened Vanilla

Directions:
Place all ingredients into blender and blend on high until smooth.

Submitter: 
Almond Breeze
Description: 
Sweeten up your day with berries and spinach in this almondmilk smoothie.
