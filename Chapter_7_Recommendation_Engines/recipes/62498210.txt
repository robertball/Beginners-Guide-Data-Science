Title:
Ranch Pinwheels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg.(8 oz)cream cheese, softened
1 pkg.(1 oz) Hidden Valley Ranch Mix
2 green onions, chopped
4 12 in. flour tortillas
1 jar (4 oz) diced pimento
1 jar (4 oz) diced green chilies
1 can (2.25 oz) sliced black olives

Directions:
Mix first 3 ingredients.
Spread on tortillas.
Drain vegetables & blot dry on paper towels.
Divide equally on tortillas.
Roll up tightly.
Chill at least 2 hours.
Cut into 1 inch pieces.
May be served with salsa.
