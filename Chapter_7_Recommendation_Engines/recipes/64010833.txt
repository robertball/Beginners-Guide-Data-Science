Title:
Grilled  Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. bone-in chicken thighs
1 3/4 cups apple cider vinegar
1 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup water
4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
3 tsp. poultry seasoning
1 egg

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together all ingredients except chicken.
When well mixed, place thighs in marinade and refrigerate 3-4 hours.
Grill over low heat, basting and turning often, for about 2 hours, until juices run clear.
