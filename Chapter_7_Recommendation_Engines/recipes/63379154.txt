Title:
Pumpkin Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/904395.jpg
Ingredients:
CAKE:
3 eggs
1 cup white sugar
2/3 cup pumpkin puree
1 teaspoon lemon juice
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
or
(1 1/2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice)
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 cup powder sugar
FILLING:
1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
4 tablespoons butter, softened
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Grease and flour jelly roll pan. I use a 15x18 inch pan lined with a silicone baking sheet.
Beat eggs with electric mixer for 2 1/2 minutes.  Add sugar slowly while still beating.
Beat for 3 1/2 minutes more.
Fold in pumpkin and lemon juice.
Mix together the dry ingredients and fold into batter.
Spread batter onto prepared pan.
Bake for 15-18 minutes.
Mean while pepare cotton tea towel by liberaly sifting powder sugar over a 15x18 area.
Remove from oven and allow cake to cool enough to handle.
Remove cake from pan while still warm and place on prepared towel.
Roll cake jellyroll fashion with towel inside and place seam side down to cool.
Prepare the frosting by blending together the sugar, cream cheese, butter and vanilla.
When completely cooled, unroll cake remove towel and spread with cream cheese filling and roll up again.
Cover with plastic wrap.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.
Sprinkle with confectioners sugar and slice into 12-15 servings.

Submitter: 
Diana Karolak
Description: 
In this dessert made completely from scratch, pumpkin puree is baked into a spiced cake which is filled with sweetened cream cheeseModified from: Pumpkin Roll/cream cheese filling
