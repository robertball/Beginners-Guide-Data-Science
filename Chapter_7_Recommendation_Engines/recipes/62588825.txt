Title:
Basalmic Marjoram Pork Loin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 lbs Boneless Top Loin Pork Roast
1 1/2 cups fresh Marjoram
12 cloves of garlic
3 Tablespoons Olive Oil
2 Tablespoons Basalmic Vinegar
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 450 degrees. In food Processor combine Marjoram, garlic, oil, vinegar, salt and pepper. Pulse to a coarse wet paste. Spread the paste on all sides of the roast. Foil line a 9 x 13 pan then place a roasting rack in center of pan. Place roast on rack cook 15 min. Reduce oven to 350 degrees and cook 50-60 minutes until internal heat reaches 150 degrees on meat thermometer. Remove roast from oven and cover in foil. Let roast sit 10-15 minutes before carving
