Title:
Toffee Brownie Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package fudge brownie mix (13"x9" pan)
2 1/2 cups cold milk
1 package (3.4oz) instant cheesecake or vanilla pudding
1 package (3.3oz) instant white chocolate pudding mix
1 carton (16oz) frozen whipped topping, thawed
2 to 3 Heath candy bars or comes in a package of bits

Directions:
Prepare and bake brownies according to directions.
Mix pudding and milk with a whisk, then fold in whipped topping.
Cut up brownies into small pieces and layer half in the bottom of a glass bowl.  Top with the pudding mixture, sprinkle with Heath candy bits, then repeat layer of brownie, pudding and Heath.
Chill before serving.
