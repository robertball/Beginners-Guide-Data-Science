Title:
Salmon Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can salmon
half cup of helmans mayo
1 cup frozen peas
1 can mushrooms
1 can cream of mushrooms soup
2 cups cheddar cheese
1 onion chopped
1 pepper (any color) chopped
3 stalks celery chopped
salt and pepper to taste
3 cups of any type of noodle
half a can of milk

Directions:
in a pot of water place the noodles to cook.
In another pot put the cream of mushroom soup and half a can of milk.  Slowly heat it on medium heat.  Add a cup and a half of the cheese.  Continue to heat till it is all melted together.  Remove from heat.
In a bowl add the onion, mushrooms, celery, peas, mayo, salmon.  Mix it all together.  Add the sauce and stir.  When the noodles are done drain them and add them to the bowl.  Stir and place in a casserole dish.  Bake on 350 for an hour.  remove from heat, add remaining cheese and wait 5 minutes.  Then serve.

Submitter: 
Nicole Johnson
Description: 
My husband loves fish dishes so I came up with this creamy dish that is also freezable to be reheated for meals at work.
