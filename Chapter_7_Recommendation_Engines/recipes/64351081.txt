Title:
TZATZIKI Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups plain yogurt, whole milk or low fat
2 cucumbers unpeeled and seeded
2 tablespoons salt
1 cup sour cream
2 tablespoons champagne vinegar or white wine vinegar
1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
2 tbls olive oil
1 tbls minced garlic (2 cloves)
1 tbls minced fresh dill
1/4 tsp fresh ground pepper

Directions:
place yogurt in a cheesecloth-lined sieve and set it over a bowl.
Grate the cucumber and toss it with 2 tbls salt. place it in another sieve and set over a bowl
place both bowls in the fridge for 3-4 hours
when ready to mix squeeze as much liquid from the cucumbers as possible.
Mix in the sour cream, vinegar,lemon jiuce ,olive oil, garlic, dill and 1 teaspoon salt, and pepper. serve immediately or refrigerate
