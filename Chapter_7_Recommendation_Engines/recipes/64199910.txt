Title:
Restaurant Style Filet Mignon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8oz) beef tenderloins
(remove as much fat and silver casing as possible)
Salt
Pepper
Garlic powder
1tbsp olive oil
1tbsp butter

Directions:
Allow meat to sit on counter for an hour.
Preheat oven to 425
Season filets on both sides with salt pepper and garlic to taste.
On stovetop, in cast iron skillet, heat the oil and butter over medium high heat.
Place filets in the pan for 2 min per side, DO not move a them during each sides 2 min.
Turn one more time and then put skillet into oven.
Cook 11 min for medium.
Remove and let meat rest under tin foil for at least 5 min before serving.

Submitter: 
Jackie
Description: 
Easy filet mignon at home just like you would get in a restaurant.
