Title:
Baked Tortelleni Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound tortellini
1 1/2 cups spaghetti sauce
4 oz mascarpone cheese
8 oz mozzarella, shredded or sliced

Directions:
Prepare tortellini according to package instructions.
In the interim, warm sauce.
Drain tortellini and add to greased cassarole dish.  Combine mascapone cheese to warmed sauce and pour over tortellini.
Combine well (cheese should begin to melt once in contact with sauce and pasta).
Sprinkle (or layer) mozzarella cheese over all.  Top with parmesan cheese if desired.
Bake at 350 for 30".

Submitter: 
Litegal1
Description: 
Tortellini smothered in creamy marinara sauce, covered in cheese, then baked ... ye gods.  Serve with bread of choice and salad.
