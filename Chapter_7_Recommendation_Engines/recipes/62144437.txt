Title:
Bry's Favorite Asian Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
25 chicken wings
1/2 cand frozen orange juice concentrate
1 cup soy sauce
1 cup brown sugar
zest of one orange
juice of one fresh orange
4 cloves of garlic minced
1 tablesppon minced fresh ginger

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except wings and place into a gallon size ziploc bag.
Add wings to marinade and refrigerate at least overnight, but no more than 2 days.
Remove the wings from the marinate and place in a 9 x 12 glass pan.
Bake at 375 for approximately one hour, or until wings are nicely browned. Wings should be sticky, with no liquid on the pan.

Submitter: 
Lynn
Description: 
Get ready to have sticky fingers with these family favorite chicken wings with a great blend of orange, soy and ginger flavors.
