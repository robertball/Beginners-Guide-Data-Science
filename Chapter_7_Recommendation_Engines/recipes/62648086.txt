Title:
chicken green curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
green curry paste 50 gm
chicken  Thai part  200 gm
coconut milk 1 cup
lemon leaves 2 gm
Thai red chili 2 gm
sweet basil leaves  5 gm
mini thai egg plant 50 gm
round green egg plant 50 gm
oil 2 tsp
fish sauce 1tsp
plum suger 1 tsp

Directions:
put oil in the pan,stir fry green curry paste,add sliced chillies and lemon leaves,when the aroma comes add coconut milk make it warm add the chicken and cook  on medium heat than add Thai egg plant.
season with fish sauce and plum sugar  and in the end add the basil leaves.
served with thai jasmine rice

Submitter: 
Chef Manzoor Hassan Balghari
Description: 
Thai food is an original and rich amalgam of evocative aromas.the dish is light and fresh the basic preparation of the food and its cooking is straightforward.
