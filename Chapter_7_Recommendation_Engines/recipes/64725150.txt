Title:
Creamy Chocolate Shakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6073617.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup of milk
1 pkg (4 ounces) of instant chocolate pudding mix
3 to 4 cups of ice cream

Directions:
In a blender mix milk and pudding mix until fluffy (takes about 30 seconds). Blend in ice cream a 1/2 cup at a time until thick. This recipe makes 2 large or 8 small servings.
Note: If you like a little variety add a banana, berries, chocolate chips or your favorite candy. You can also try a different flavor of pudding. Enjoy!
