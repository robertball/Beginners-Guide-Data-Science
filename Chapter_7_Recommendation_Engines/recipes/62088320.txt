Title:
Blue Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Box, Pasta of choice
1/4 c. butter (half stick)
1/4 c. flour
2 c. Half and Half
3/4 c. Blue Cheese
1/2 c. Shredded Parmesan
1 c. Sharp Cheddar
1/2 c. Finely chopped onions
1/2 c. finely chopped red pepper.
Salt and Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Cook pasta al dente and place in medium casserole dish.
In a sauce pan melt butter over medium heat.
Gradually add flour and stir for 2-3 min, making a white roux.
Add some half and half and continue stirring until mixture thickens.
Add cheeses and the rest off the half and half in between the cheese to keep a smooth consistency.
continue stirring and add onions and peppers.
Pour mixture over pasta and mix well.
Cook for 45 min.
When top of casserole becomes golden brown, cover with foil and continue cooking.
Enjoy.

Submitter: 
Will Wohler
Description: 
Despite the name it is not actually blue; it uses blue cheese along with parmesan and cheddar.  The cheese provides a lot of flavor while the red bell pepper adds a little color to the dish.
