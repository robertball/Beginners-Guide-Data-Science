Title:
Home-Style Vegetable Beef Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 onion, diced
6 red potatoes, finely diced
5 carrots, coarsley chopped
1 can diced tomatoes
1 bag frozen whole kernel corn
2 cups green beans (can use frozen)
1 (46 fluid ounce) bottle tomato-vegetable juice cocktail
1 cup beef broth
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
In a large pot over medium heat, cook beef, onion and potatoes, covered, until beef is no longer pink and potatoes are tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
Drain beef mixture and return to pot with carrots, tomatoes, corn, green beans, juice cocktail, broth, salt and pepper.  Simmer over low heat 30 minutes, until flavors are well blended and soup is hot.

Description:
