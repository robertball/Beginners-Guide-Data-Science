Title:
Ranch BLT Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg cream chz
1/2 c ranch drsg
3/4 c tomatoes sliced and diced
6 slices bacon, cooked and chopped
1/2 c fine chopped celery
2 T Chopped Onion

Directions:
Combine all ingredients. Refrigerate for 3 hours.

Submitter: 
Jamie Beth
Description: 
BLT with a ranch twist. Maple Bacon give this a sweeter taste.
