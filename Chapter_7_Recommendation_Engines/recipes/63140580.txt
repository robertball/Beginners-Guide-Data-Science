Title:
Seco de Pollo (Stewed Chicken) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 lbs. chicken pieces
6 tbsp veg. oil
2 red onions, quartered
1/2 red onion, coarsely chopped
10 whole garlic cloves
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp achiote paste (annatto paste)
4 c chicken broth
8 whole tomatoes, quartered
3 green peppers, quartered
1 c. cilantro
1 tsp oregano
2 tsp cumin
salt and pepper, as desired

Directions:
1.  Blend tomatoes, quartered onion, peppers, whole garlic, half of cilantro, oregano, cumin and broth until smooth.
2.  Heat oil in large dutch oven and saute chopped onion, minced garlic and achiote for 2 min.
3.  Add chicken pieces and cook until all pieces are browned.
4.  Add pureed vegetables and bring to boiling.  Lower to simmer and cook over low heat until chicken is cooked and sauce has thickened.
5.  Season with salt and pepper, add chopped cilantro.
