Title:
Our favorite Balsamic Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Olive Oil
1/4 cup Balsamic vinegar
1 tsp. honey
1 tsp. dijon mustard
1 shallot finely minced
1 garlic clove minced
pinch each salt and pepper.

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in glass jar.  Cover.  Shake.  Serve.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Singer6
Description: 
This vinaigrette does not seperate once you shake it for at least 30 minutes!  Simple, sweet and savory.
