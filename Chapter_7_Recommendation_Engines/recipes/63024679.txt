Title:
Cranberry Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/757975.jpg
Ingredients:
8 cups flour
3 teaspoons salt
3 teaspoons soda
3 teaspoons baking powder
4 eggs
2 cups sugar
2 cups brown sugar
4 cups *sour milk
2 cup chopped pecans
2 cup fresh cranberries, *halved
*sour milk can be made by bringing milk to room temperature by microwave and then adding a teaspoon of vinegar for each cup of milk. Stir and let sit until curdled.
*cranberries can be semi chopped in a food processor to save time.

Directions:
sprinkle cranberries with 1 teaspoon cinnamon and 2 tablespoons sugar.
mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
in another bowl add sugar to eggs and beat until fluffy.
mix the dry ingredients and egg mixture together then pour in the sour milk and mix until well blended.
stir in flour gradually and add nuts and cranberries with last addition of flour.
bake at 350Â° for 1 hour and 10 minutes or until cooked.

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
Cranberry bread can be made at any time of year with frozen berries, but we especially love its sweet-tart brightness at Christmas time.
