Title:
Cinnamon Swirl Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. butter, softened
2 c. sugar
2 eggs
1 t. vanilla extract
4 c. all-purpose flour
2 t. baking soda
1 t. salt
2 c. buttermilk or 2 c. milk + 2 T. vinegar or lemon juice
Cinnamon/Sugar Filling:
2/3 c. granulated white sugar
1/3 c. brown sugar
2 t. cinnamon

Directions:
In large mixing bowl, cream together butter and 2 cups of sugar until light and fluffy.  Add eggs and vanilla extract. Sift together flour, baking soda and salt.  Slowly add dry ingredients alternating with buttermilk until well mixed.
In a separate bowl make cinnamon/sugar filling by combining granulated white sugar, brown sugar and cinnamon. Set aside.
Preheat oven at 350 degrees. Grease and flour two loaf pans.  Put 1/2 of the batter into greased loaf pans (1/4 in each pan). Over the batter in the pans, sprinkle about 3/4 of the cinnamon/sugar filling. Place the remaining batter on top of the cinnamon/sugar filling. Sprinkle tops of loaves with remaining cinnamon/sugar filling.  Swirl gently with knife. Bake 45-50 minutes.  Cool in pans about 20 minutes before removing. Allow to cool on racks before wrapping for gift giving.

Submitter: 
Shearone
Description: 
Excellent Cinnamon Swirl Bread/Cake. Delicious plain or with powdered sugar glaze made by using 1 cup of powdered sugar, a couple tablespoons of milk and a splash of vanilla extract. Recipe makes 2 loaves - one to keep and one to give away.
