Title:
Honey Clove Ham Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7223606.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup honey
1 tablespoon spicy brown mustard, or more to taste
3/4 teaspoon ground cloves, or more to taste
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger

Directions:
Mix brown sugar, honey, brown mustard, cloves, and ginger together in a microwave-safe bowl; heat in microwave until sugar is dissolved, 45 to 60 seconds.
