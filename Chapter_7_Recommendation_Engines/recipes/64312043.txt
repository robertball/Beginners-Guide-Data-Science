Title:
Cuban Picadillo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1 pound ground pork sausage
1 tsp. cumin
1/2 onion, chopped
2 tablespoons garlic, minced
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
2 cups golden raisins
2 (8 ounce) jars green olives, drained and chopped
2 oz. capers
2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
1 bay leaf
1/2 cup beef stock
1 can fire roasted tomatoes

Directions:
Brown the ground beef & pork over until crumbly and no longer pink, about 10 minutes, add the cumin. Stir in the onion and garlic; cook for 2 minutes before adding the red pepper, green pepper, raisins, olives, capers, tomato paste, bay leaf, beef stock, and tomatoes. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer 10 minutes, or until the peppers have cooked to your liking.

Submitter: 
Erica Brown
Description: 
This can be served alone or with black beans & rice. The flavor intensifies as it ages.Modified from: Cuban Picadillo
