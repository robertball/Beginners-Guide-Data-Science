Title:
Seafood Fondeaux Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/814222.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter, unsalted (1/2 stick)
1/8 cup flour
1/2 tsp Old Bay seasoning
1/4 tsp cajun seasoning
1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/8 tsp fresh ground pepper
1/4 tsp salt
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup milk
3/4 cup monterrey jack cheese, shredded
1 can mushrooms (4 oz.)
1/2 cup (6 oz.) crayfish tails
1/2 cup (6 oz.) extra small shrimp (cooked, peeled, deveined, tail-off)
French baguette, thinly sliced
Garlic butter (1 tsp. garlic salt & 1/2 cup margarine)

Directions:
Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat.  Whisk in flour.  Add spices.  Slowly add chicken broth while still whisking.
Simmer on medium-low uncovered stirring often until mixture is thick, like thin pudding.
Add milk SLOWLY as you continue to whisk.  Continue to cook until thickened a little more.  Stir in cheese until melted.
Add mushrooms, crayfish, and shrimp and simmer on LOW heat until heated through.
While sauce is simmering, put a generous amount of garlic butter on thinly sliced  pieces of a french baguette(sliced diagonally).  Lay on a cookie sheet and broil until toasted and slightly crispy.  Serve with fondeaux.
DOUBLE RECIPE IF YOU ARE MAKING AS A MEAL FOR 4-5 PEOPLE.
