Title:
Buttermilk Chocolate Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. water
1 stick oleo
1/2 c. oil
2 cups sugar
2 cups flour
4 tblsp. cocoa
1/2 c. buttermilk
1 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
1 tsp. baking soda
FROSTING
1 1/2 c. sugar
6 tblsp. margarine
6 tblsp. milk
1 c. chocolate chips

Directions:
Boil water, oil and oleo.  Add to flour, sugar and cocoa.  Mix well.  Then add buttermilk, soda, vanilla and eggs.  Put in a large greased cookie sheet and bake at 400 degrees for 18 minutes.  While bars are baking, combine sugar, margarine and milk in a saucepan and bring to a boil.  After 1 minute of boiling, add 1 c. chocolate chips.  Turn off the heat and stir well.  Put on bars immediately after they come out of the oven.
*my husband likes to add a layer of marshmallows on the brownies.  Add miniature marshmallows over the brownies and put back into a warm oven for a few minutes (until melted).  Take out of oven and pour frosting over marshmallows.
