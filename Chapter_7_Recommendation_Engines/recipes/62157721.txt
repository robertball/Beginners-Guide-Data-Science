Title:
Cold Lentil Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bulbs of garlic
1 lb dry brown lentils
1 bunch scallions
a very generous handful of fresh parsley
1 pint grape tomatoes
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
1/4 & 1 tsp cup olive oil (divided)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
preheat oven to 400
peel outer skin off garlic bulb
cut off top of the bulbs, exposing every clove (about 1/4")
drizzle with olive oil
wrap in aluminum foil
place in an oven safe dish and bake 30 minutes until cloves are soft.
remove and allow to cool.
while garlic is roasting, pick and rinse lentils.
place lentils in a 5 qt pot. Fill with water.
bring to a boil, stirring occasionally.
reduce heat to medium-high and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 15 minutes or until lentils are tender but still firm.
drain lentils in a fine strainer and rinse with cold water until cooled.
Drain very thoroughly.
peel garlic and remove whole cloves
wash and finely chop parsley
wash and slice grape tomatoes in halves
wash and slice scallions finely
wisk together oil, vinegar, and salt and pepper
In a large bowl, fold together all ingredients.
Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Lindsay L.
Description: 
This lentil salad is very simple and yet very tasty. It is perfect for picnics and summer gatherings. If you grow fresh herbs and tomatoes, it will come out even better. It is appropriate for veg'n, gluten-free, and generally healthy eating.
