Title:
Sweet Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C Butter
1/2 C Sugar
2 Eggs
1 C Yellow Cornmeal
1 1/2 C All-Purpose Flour
2 tsp Baking Powder
1/2 tsp Salt
1 1/2 C Milk

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar together.  Add eggs, beat well; add cornmeal.  In separate bowl, sift flour, baking powder, and salt.  Sir in alternately with milk.
Pour into a greased 8-in square pan and bake at 375 degrees for 30 - 35 minutes.
If muffins desired, bake in a greased muffin pan 15 - 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
KarenS
Description: 
The yummiest sweet corn bread ever!  I make it into muffins and they never last long!
