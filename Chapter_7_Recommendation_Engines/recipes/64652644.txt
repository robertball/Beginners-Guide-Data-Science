Title:
Old Cuban Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4542501.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 oz fresh lime juice
1.5 oz simple syrup
2 oz dark spiced rum
2-3 dashes bitters
2-3 oz Champagne (to top drink)
1 slice lime
8 Fresh mint leaves

Directions:
Gently muddle mint with lime juice in a shaker using a wooden spoon or muddled.
Add syrup, rum, butters and a handful of ice. Shake until well chilled. Strain into a cocktail glass and top with champagne.
Garnish with lime merge and mint leaves.
