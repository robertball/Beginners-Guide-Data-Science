Title:
Honey Sauced Chicken (slow cooker) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2963885.jpg
Ingredients:
1lb frozen chicken
1/2 cup honey
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/8 cup chopped onion (1/16 cup onion flakes)
1/8 cup ketchup
1 TBSP olive oil
1 clove minced garlic
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Put frozen chicken into crock pot, set temp for how ever many hours it will cook (low 6-8hrs/high 4-6hrs). Mix together the honey, soy, onion, ketchup, oil and garlic then pour over the chicken. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes and let cook until the chicken is cooked through. Once its done, I shred the chicken in the crockpot and serve over rice and veggies.

Submitter: 
Sarah Juden
Description: 
A sweet yet savory saucy chicken, we usually serve over rice with some veggies.
