Title:
Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz whole-wheat spagetti
2 T. oil
1 small onion, chopped
4 oz sliced mushrooms
2 medium zucchini, sliced
1 large tomato, cubed
1 15-oz can chick peas, drained
1 6-oz can tomato paste
2 tsp. curry, or to taste
1 c. water
14 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Cook pasta till al dente.
While pasta cooks, heat oil and add onion, garlic, mushrooms, and zucchini.  Saute till zucchini is tender but not mushy.
Stir in remaining ingredients and cook over medium heat, covered, about 8 minutes.
Spoon veggies over pasta.
