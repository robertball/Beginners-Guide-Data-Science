Title:
KOI Seared Tofu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg silken (or traditional) tofu cut into squares
1/4 C honey (256Cals)
2 Tbsp olive oil (240Cals)
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 C veg broth (5 Cals)
More cinnamon during cooking

Directions:
Squeeze tofu and slice into desired sizes
Lay in glass dish
Combine honey, oil & veg broth in bowl, pour over tofu so it's covering most pcs.
Sprinkle on cinnamon and marinade at least 30 mins.
On a hot griddle, sear tofu.  Sprinkle with generous amounts of cinnamon on both sides
Serve with Miso dip!

Submitter: 
Deborah Thompson
Description: 
This is almost identical to KOIs tofu.  Serves over greens or with a curry broth and rice is perfect.
