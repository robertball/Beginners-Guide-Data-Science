Title:
Low-Fat Tartar Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup whole milk yogurt, 3 tbsp mayonnaise, 1/2 onion, 2 tbsp lemon juice, salt, pepper

Directions:
Mix the yogurt, mayonnaise and lemon juice together until smooth. Finely chop the onion in a food processor or with a knife. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Refrigerate for about an hour or until set. You can use it right away, but the flavor comes out better if it sets.

Submitter: 
INSHA87
Description: 
An easy and guilt-free version of tartar sauce for fried seafood.
