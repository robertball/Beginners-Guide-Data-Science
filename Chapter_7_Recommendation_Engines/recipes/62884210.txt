Title:
Bacon Feta Pico Pasta Toss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/695682.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 oz) package of whole wheat penne pasta
3 slices center cut bacon, cooked and crumbled
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1/2 cup pico de gallo, drained
1/3 cup Absolutely Fabulous Greek/House Dressing (from this site)
1/2 cup Feta, crumbled

Directions:
Bring a large saucepan full of water to a boil.  Once boiling, add the pasta and let it cook for about 8 minutes or until it is al dente.
Meanwhile, in a skillet sprayed with cooking spray, add the bacon and cook per package directions.
Once the pasta is done, drain it well.
Add the drained pasta to a large bowl.  Add the bacon, red and green bell pepper and pico de gallo.  Pour the light italian dressing on top and toss until thoroughly distributed.  Top with crumbled feta cheese.

Submitter: 
Occasional Cooker
Description: 
A delicious and healthy pasta dish with center cut bacon, feta, red and green bell peppers, and pico de gallo.  It is excellent served either hot or cold and can be served as a main dish, pasta salad, or side dish.
