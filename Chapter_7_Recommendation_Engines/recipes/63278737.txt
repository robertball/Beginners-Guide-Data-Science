Title:
Pot Roast with Dill Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 to 3 lb boneless beef chuck pot roast
1 tblsp.  cooking oil
1/2 c. water
1 tsp.  dried dill
1/2 tsp. coarse salt (kosher)
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 c plain yogurt
2 tblsp. flour

Directions:
Trim fat from meat.  If necessary, cut meat to fit into a 3 1/2 or 4 qt. slow cooker.  In a large skillet heat oil over medium-high heat.  Add meat; cook on all sides until brown.  Transfer meat to slow cooker; add te water.  Sprinkle meat with 3/4 tsp. of the dried dill, the salt and pepper.
Cover and cook on low-heat setting for 10-12 hours or on high heat for 5 to 6 hours.  Transfer meat to a serving platter, reserving juices.  Cover meat; keep warm.
For sauce, pour cooking juices into a glass measuring cup; skim off fat.  Measure 1 cup juices.  .  In a small saucepan stir togeter uogurt and flour.  Stir in the 1 cup juices and remaining 1/4 tsp. dried dill.  cook and stir over medium heat until thickened and bubbly.  Cook and stir for 1 minute more.
To serve, pour some of the sauce over meat; pass the remaining sauce.
