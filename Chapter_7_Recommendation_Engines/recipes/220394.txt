Title:
Caesar Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/994701.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1 teaspoon oil-packed minced garlic
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Place mayonnaise, vinegar, garlic, and salt in a blender; blend until smooth.

A simple and tasty non-dairy alternative for Caesar dressing.
