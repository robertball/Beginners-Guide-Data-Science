Title:
Ginger Chicken Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
3 tbsp canola oil
2 tbsp packed brown sugar
2 limes, zested and juiced
1 Thai Chile, seeded and minced
1 tbsp grated, peeled fresh ginger
1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
2 pounds boneless, skin-on chicken thighs
2 small zucchini, seeded and julienned
2 medium carrots, julienned
kosher salt
1 small red onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 cups packed baby arugula, rinsed and dried
2 cups steamed rice

Directions:
-In a large bowl, combine soy sauce, oil, brown sugar, lime zest and juice, ginger and pepper.  Add the chicken and too to coat.  Marinate at room temp for 30 mins or cover and refrigerate overnight.
-Heat a large skillet over med-high.  In a large bowl, toss zucchini and carrots with just enough oil to coat. Season to taste with salt.  Add to hot skillet and cook, stirring, for about 2 mins or until just softened; transfer to a plate and set aside
-Lightly coat the skillet with oil and add onion.  Cook for 1 minute then add garlic.  Reduce heat to medium and add just enough of the chicken to fit in a single layer.  Cook, turning once, about 3 mins per side or until brown.  Transfer cooked chicken to a plate and set aside and brown the remaining pieces.  Discard any remaining marinade, then return all chicken to the pan and add 1/2 cup water. Cover and cook for 10 mins or until meat is cooked through.  Add zucchini and carrots and heat for 1 min.  Mix in arugula and toss until just wilted.  divide evenly among 6 plates and serve immediately
