Title:
Microwave Almond Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups powered sugar
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa
1/2 cup unsalted butter
1/4 cup milk
1 tsp. almond flavoring

Directions:
1. Sift powered sugar and cocoa together into a mixing bowl (I used my electric mixer). You may wish to stir this together more.
2. Place milk and butter in microwaveable bowl and microwave on high for 2 minutes. Add almond flavoring to milk-butter mixutre then pour into powered sugar and cocoa mixture.
3. Stir vigorously or use your electric mixer to mix all ingredients together until smooth and even.
4. Pour mixutre into foil-lined 8x6 inch pan. You may need to press the fudge into shape in the pan. Then cool in fridge until set (10-30 min.)
