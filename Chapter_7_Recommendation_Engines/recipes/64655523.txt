Title:
Grilled Potatoes With Bacon Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds small red potatoes cut in half
6 tbsp olive oil plus 1 tsp
8 slices thick cut bacon
2 garlic cloves minced
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
2 tsp firmly packed brown sugar
1 small bunch green onion

Directions:
Heat grill to medium high.
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
Cook potatoes until mostly cooked but not falling apart, about 12 minutes.  Drain and put in bowl and toss with 3 TBS of the olive oil
Cook bacon in a skillet until crisp remove and place on a plate lined with paper towel.
Remove all but a 1 TBS of bacon grease from skillet
Add garlic, vinegar and brown sugar to the skillet with the bacon grease.  Scrape up all the bacon bits.  Stir in the remaining 3 TBS of olive oil until well combined  and transfer to a bowl.
Toss the green onion in 1 tsp olive oil and grill until slightley wilted.  Remove from grill.
Grill potatoes cut side down for 3-5 minutes until grill marks appear.
Place the potatoes in the bowl with the dressing.
Cut Green onion in 1 inch pieces and toss with potatoes.

Description: 
This potato side dish is a nice change from your regular potato salad.  It's quick, easy and oh so full of flavor.
