Title:
Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1954573.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg Southern style Hash browns
1 dozen large eggs
1 of Bob Evan's Original sausage
Approx. 2 tblspns flour
Approx. 1/2 cup of milk
Salt and pepper to taste
1 pkg of shredded chedder cheese

Directions:
Grease glass casserole dish.
Pour the entire package of Hash browns in baking dish, make sure they are even and flat and set aside.
Cook sausage on stove top, when meat is cooked add milk and flour (makes sausage gravy).
Add sausage gravy to hash brown dish and mix well.
Mix 9-10 eggs in a large bowl. You can add a little milk if you like your eggs fluffy.
Add eggs to casserole dish, make sure that eggs run through dish so that it is not uneven.
Spread Shredded Chedder Cheese to the top of the dish, cover the entire dish with cheese and place in the oven on 425 degrees Fahrenheit, until cooked.
(I do not time it, I just watch it until it is cooked). Approximately 30-40 minutes.

Description: 
I make this dish almost every Saturday morning, my family loves it. They are always disappointed if I don't make it.
