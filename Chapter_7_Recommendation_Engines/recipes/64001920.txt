Title:
A Tasty Summertime Four Bean Salad. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can-14 oz. cut green beans- drained and rinsed
1 can-14 oz. cut yellow beans- drained and rinsed
1 can-14 oz. red kidney beans- drained and rinsed
1 can-14 oz. lima beans.- drained and rinsed
1/2 cup green pepper sliced in thin strips(or any colored bell pepper)
3/4 cup onion sliced thin
3/4 cup chopped celery.
THE DRESSING
3/4 cup white sugar
1/2 cup white vinegar
1/2 cup olive oil
1 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. black pepper
1 tsp. celery salt(optional)

Directions:
Add the drained beans along with the green pepper, onion, celery, salt and pepper and celery salt(if using) into a large container with a tight fitting lid.
Heat the oil and vinegar over medium heat, add sugar and stir until dissolved. Pour hot oil mixture over beans and stir with a wooden spoon till all is coated. Let it sit in the fridge over night, turn up side down once in a while to make sure bean mixture is well marinated.* see notes*

Submitter: 
manella
Description: 
This is a very tasty mixed bean salad, goes very well as a side dish when you BBQ or grill.
