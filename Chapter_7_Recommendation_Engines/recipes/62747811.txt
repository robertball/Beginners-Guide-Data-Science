Title:
dads Spaghetti with Meat Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 chopped onion
1 clove garlic, minced
2 tablespoons butter
1/2 pound lean ground beef
1/2 pound ground Sausage (hot)
1 teaspoon Garlic salt
8 ounces fresh chopped mushrooms
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
2 (10.5 ounce) cans beef broth
1 packet of Alfredo sauce
1 packet of Spaghetti seasoning
2 tablespoons of crushed red pepper
1 pound spaghetti
1 teaspoon olive oil
3 tablespoons salt
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, for garnish
1/4 cup grated Romano cheese

Directions:
In a large skillet, warm olive oil over low heat; saute onion, garlic and celery until onion is transparent.
Stir in butter and increase heat to medium; brown beef and sausage until no longer pink. Mix in 1 teaspoon salt, and mushrooms; lower heat and stir for about 3 minutes.
Add tomato paste and broth spaghetti seasoning Alfredo sauce to pot. Bring to boil then Simmer for about 45 min, stirring occasionally.
Bring a large pot of water to a vigorous boil with 3 tablespoons salt and 1 teaspoon olive oil.  Slip spaghetti into water holding on to ends until strands soften a bit.  Cook over high heat for 7 to 8 minutes only.  When properly cooked, raw taste should be gone but still have a firm texture.
When ready to serve, combine pasta with meat sauce; serve on a warmed platter or bowl
