Title:
Add Anything Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2c white sugar
1/2c brown sugar
1/2c margarine
1 egg, slightly beaten
1c flour
1/2t baking soda
1/2t baking powder
1/2t vanilla
1-1/2c anything (cereal, chocolate chips, etc)

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.
Bake 350 for 10-12 minutes.
