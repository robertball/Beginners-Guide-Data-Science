Title:
Potato Leek Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886776.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and diced
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 quart chicken broth
2 leeks, sliced
2 carrots, sliced
1 1/2 tablespoons dill, divided
1 1/2 cups ham, cooked and chopped
32 ounces heavy cream
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place the potatoes in slow cooker.  Sprinkle on the cornstarch and toss well.
Add the chicken broth, mixing well.
Stir in the leeks, carrots and 1/2 tablespoon dill.
Cover and cook on the low heat setting 8 hours, or until the potatoes are tender.
In a blender or a food processor, puree till slightly chunky with a little of the soup liquid then return to slow cooker.
Stir in the ham, cream, and remaining 1 tablespoon dill.
Cover and heat 5-10 minutes longer.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Leeks, butter, cream and Yukon Gold potatoes make up this simple, creamy soup.Modified from: Potato Leek Soup III
