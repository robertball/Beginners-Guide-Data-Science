Title:
Pot Roast Style Turkey thighs with Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1493383.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Turkey Thighs (skin on)
4 carrots cut into chunks
3 med. potatoes cut into chunks
3 stalks of celery cut into chunks
1/2 yellow onion cut into chunks
1 can golden mushroom soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup water
1 onion soup mix or pot roast seasoning packet

Directions:
Place Turkey thighs in bottom of slow cooker. Place carrots and potatoes around the outside of thighs. In a bowl, mix together soups and onion soup mix. pour over top of thighs and vegies. Cook on low 6-8 hours.
