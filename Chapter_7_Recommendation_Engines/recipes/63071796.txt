Title:
Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 blocks of cream cheese
1 can deveined shrimp
1 cup celery, diced
1/2 cup white onion, diced
1/2  - 1 cup sour cream
Splash of lemon juice
Salt to taste

Directions:
Soften the cream cheese
Chop celery and onion
Open shrimp and drain, chop in can and add a squirt of lemon to remove fishy taste
Add sour cream until the consistency you want and salt to taste
Marinade over night, if possible
