Title:
Praline Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 unbaked pie crust
2 tablespoons margarine (or butter)
3/4 cup light or dark corn syrup
1/4 cup molasses
1 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
3 eggs, beaten
2 cups pecans, in halves or chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350oF, and placed unbaked pie crust on a cookie sheet.
Melt margarine, and blend in the following order:  corn syrup, molasses, sugar, salt, vanilla, and eggs, one at a time.  Stir in pecans, and pour into pie crust.
Carefully place baking sheet with pie crust into oven, and bake 50 minutes.  Cool completely before serving.

Submitter: 
Amybobamy
Description: 
Earthy twist on traditional pecan pie, with a touch of molasses and twice the pecans.
