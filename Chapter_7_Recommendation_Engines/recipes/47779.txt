Title:
Cocoa-Coconut Oatmeal Nests Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982781.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter, softened
3/4 cup granulated sugar
3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 cup HERSHEY速'S Cocoa or HERSHEY速'S Dutch Processed Cocoa
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup MOUNDS速 Sweetened Coconut Flakes
1 1/2 cups rolled or quick cooking oats
HERSHEY速'S Candy-Coated Milk Chocolate Eggs (optional)

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
Beat butter, granulated sugar and brown sugar in large bowl until well blended; beat in eggs and  vanilla. Stir together flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt; add to butter mixture, beating until blended. Stir in coconut and oats; drop by heaping teaspoons onto ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until set.  Cool slightly; remove from cookie sheet to wire rack.  Press chocolate egg onto center of each cookie, if desired. Cool completely.

These chocolate oatmeal cookies are sure to become a family favorite! Dutch processed cocoa, coconut flakes, and quick-cooking oats are the secret ingredients in these delicious, fun and easy cookies.
