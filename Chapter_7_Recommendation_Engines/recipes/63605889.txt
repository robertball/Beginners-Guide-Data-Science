Title:
Rosemary Chicken Marsala Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts sliced into small strips
flour for coating
1/4 cup sundried tomatoes in oil, drained
3 tbsp olive oil
1 stick butter (salted)
1 tsp Rosemary
2 cloves garlic
2 cups sliced mushrooms
1/4 cup pinenuts
1 cup broccoli (florets only) cut into small pieces (fresh)
1 cup dry Marsala wine (such as Cribari)
1/2 cup heavy cream
Salt/Pepper to taste
Angel Hair

Directions:
Step1: Lightly coat chicken strips with flour
Step2: Heat Oil over medium heat in Large Saute Pan
Step3: Lightly brown chicken but do not cook through, remove chicken from pan and set aside
Step4: Add mushrooms, broccoli, pinenuts and garlic to pan with 1/2 stick butter and saute' 2 minutes
Step5: Add 3/4 of the Marsala wine to the pan and turn up heat to high, until boiling
Step6: Reduce heat to low boil and add the cream
Step7: Continue to low boil until reduced by three quarters
Step8: Add remaining Marsala and Butter.  Return the chicken breast to the pan. Sprinkle Rosemary over sauce.
Step9: Saute over Medium heat until sauce is thick enough to coat the back of a spoon.
Serve tossed with 1 lb cooked Angel Hair Pasta

Description: 
Chicken strips in a rich creamy sundried tomato and marsala sauce, with the aroma of rosemary. Strewn with broccoli, mushrooms and pinenuts for texture and flavor. Amazing and so easy - yet is restaurant quality. Will become a family favorite!!!
