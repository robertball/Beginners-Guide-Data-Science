Title:
Valerie's Special Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/731430.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup ranch dressing
1 teaspoon prepared horseradish
1 1/2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder

Directions:
Blend together the mayonnaise, ranch dressing, horseradish, Worcestershire sauce, and garlic powder in a bowl. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
VALdelish
Description: 
This sauce goes great on everything, fries, chicken tenders and on burgers to jazz it up. It's a simple 5 ingredient recipe your family and friends are sure to enjoy. It turns the ordinary and casual dish to an extraordinary, upscale meal.
