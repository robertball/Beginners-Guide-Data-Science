Title:
Asian Marinated Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 1" thick pork chops
1 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1 tbsp ground ginger
1 tbsp cumin
1 tbsp roasted red chili paste

Directions:
Whisk the soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, cumin, and chili paste together in a bowl until sugar is well dissolved.  Put pork in a ziploc bag and cover with marinade.  Let sit for at least 30-45 minutes.
Grill the pork to your desired temp, 5-7 minutes on each side.

Submitter: 
itsjustaphase
Description: 
This is one of my favorite marinades to use for grilled pork chops.  I usually just throw ingredients as I see fit, so the measurements are estimates.  The roasted red chili paste can be found in the Asian section of your grocery store.
