Title:
Flavorful Slow Cooker Pot Roast with Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed golden cream of mushroom soup
1 1/2 package dry onion soup mix
3/4 cup water
2-3 pounds pot roast
1 lb carrots, cut into chunks
1 lb celery, cut into chunks
1 onion, cut into chunks
3 potatoes, wedged (optional)
8 oz. mushrooms
1 garlic clove, minced
2 T flour stirred into 2 T cold water

Directions:
Put all ingredients in slow cooker.
Cook on High setting for 3 to 4 hours, or on Low setting for 8 to 9 hours.

Submitter: 
Jessica Weber
Description: 
This pot roast stew makes a delicious gravy. Serve with mashed potatoes.Modified from: Awesome Slow Cooker Pot Roast
