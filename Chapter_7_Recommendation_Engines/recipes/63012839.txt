Title:
Sea-Glass  Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 2 cups sugar
2. 3/4 cup light corn syrup
3. 3/4 cup water
4. 1/4 to 1 tsp flavoring oil or paste depending on the intensity of the flavor ***** may add drops of food color if needed
5. powder sugar for dusting the candy

Directions:
lightly grease cookie sheet and place on wire cooling rack
stir 1. 2. 3. in med saucepan over med-high heat till sugar dissolves
without stirring boil till candy thermometer 300-310* small amount into cold water separates into hard brittle threads  remove from heat and wait till boiling stops
with wooden spoon stir in 4. till blended then pour onto cookie sheet...cool lightly dust both sides with 5. break into small pieces...store airtight containers for up to one year
*****Flavor oils may be bought at Lorann Oils 1-800-248-1302

old fashion sugar candy you can make any flavor...reminded me of Mrs Perkins candy Watertown Ct
