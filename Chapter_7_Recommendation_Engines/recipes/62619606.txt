Title:
Cucumber Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 small cucumbers, unpeeled
1/2c rice vinegar
1t kosher salt
1t garlic salt
2-8 oz pkg softened cream cheese
1/2c mayonnaise
2t chives
Pita chips

Directions:
Grate cucumber and toss with vinegar, salt, and 1/2t garlic salt.
Cover and chill 8 hours.
Drain and press cucumber.
Beat cream cheese, mayonnaise, and rest of the salt until smooth.
Stir in cucumber mixture and chives.
Cover and chill for an hour.
Serve with pita chips.
