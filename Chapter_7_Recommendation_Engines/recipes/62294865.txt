Title:
Cheese Krispies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks of butter
1 (8 oz) package of sharp cheese
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. red pepper
2 c. plain flour
2 c. rice crispy cereal

Directions:
Grate cheese. Mix well and fold in rice crispy cereal. Pinch off tsp servings, drop on baking sheet and mask with fork. Bake at 350 degress for 10 minutes.
