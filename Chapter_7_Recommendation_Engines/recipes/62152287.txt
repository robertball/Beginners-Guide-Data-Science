Title:
Bacon Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 slices bacon sliced (some of the fat trimmed off)
1 med diced onion
1 red pepper diced
1 sm pkg mushrooms (baby bella)
4 cups chicken broth
8 oz spaghetti
1/4 cup of freshly grated parmesan, fontina or greyer cheese

Directions:
In a deep skillet add bacon until it is browned but not crisp, leaving the bacon fat in add onions, mushrooms, red peppers and cook until slightly tender. Add chicken broth and simmer until reduced to 1/2. Meanwhile cook pasta as indicated on the package. Once reduced combine  with cooked spaghetti and cheese turn on heat to high and toss pasta for a few seconds + to thicken liquid if to runny.
Serve immediatly. ENJOY
**If the pasta is cooked al dente then it will absorb the remaining liquid in the pan.

Submitter: 
Verity Tripke
Description: 
I came up with this recipe while I was pregnant and craving bacon..combined with some veggies and tossed with cheese in the end, not the healthiest of meals but pretty tasty. I added chicken strips once and diced tomato's if you want to make it a complete meal.
