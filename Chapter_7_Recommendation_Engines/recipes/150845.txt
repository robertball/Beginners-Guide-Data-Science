Title:
Baileys Frozen Russian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978168.jpg
Ingredients:
1 ounce Baileys® Irish Cream
1/4 ounce Smirnoff No. 21™ vodka
1/4 ounce Godiva® Chocolate Liqueur
2 scoops vanilla ice cream

Directions:
Pour Baileys(R) Irish Cream, Smirnoff No. 21(TM) Vodka, and Godiva(R) Chocolate Liqueur into a blender.
Add vanilla ice cream to the blender; blend, and serve in hurricane glass.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Adding a touch of richness to any drink, Baileys® Irish cream liqueur mixes fine Irish whiskey, fresh dairy cream and a hint of chocolate flavors.
