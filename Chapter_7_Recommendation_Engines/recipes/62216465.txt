Title:
Mama's Chocolate Crinkles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup of vegetable oil
4 ounces unsweetened chocolate. (Chopped.  They also sell it in a liquid form)
2 cups of sugar (or splenda)
4 eggs (or 1 cup of egg beaters)
2 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp salt
2 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 cup powdered sugar for rolling

Directions:
Mix oil, chocolate, sugar, eggs,vanilla, salt, flour, and baking powder.
Chill until dough is firm.
Roll in to approx 1 inch balls.  Roll balls in powdered sugar
Bake at 350 for 10-12 minutes
