Title:
Slow Cooker Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 - 1 pound ground beef
1 onion, chopped
1 (19 ounce) can Romano beans, with liquid
1 (213 ml) can tomato sauce
1 (796 ml) can diced tomatoes
1 (127 ml.) can diced green chile peppers
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix

Directions:
In a medium skillet, cook the ground beef until browned over medium heat. Drain, and set aside.
Place the ground beef, onion, beans, tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, green chile peppers and taco seasoning mix in a slow cooker. Mix to blend, and cook on Low setting for 8 hours.

Submitter: 
TJ
Description: 
Browned ground beef seasoned with a package of taco seasoning is combined in a slow cooker with canned beans, tomatoes, and green chilies.  Serve with tortilla chips and a dollop of sour cream.Modified from: Slow Cooker Taco Soup
