Title:
24 Hour Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Head of Lettuce
2 heads of Cauliflower
3 Packages of Frozen Peas
1 Pound Bacon, cooked and crumbled
1/2 Qt of Mayonnaise
1 Package of Good Seasons Italian Dressing(dry)
Parmesan Cheese

Directions:
Shred lettuce and put in container/bowl, add florets of cauliflower, then layer of peas(doesn't matter if they are defrosted), and a layer of crumbled bacon. Top with mayo (not salad dressing), sprinkle dressing over the top, cover this with a layer of parmesan cheese and cover. Let sit overnight, mix before serving.
