Title:
POPPY SEED DRESSING Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 CUPS SUGAR
3/4 TSP. SALT
3/4 TSP.ONION POWDER
3/4 TSP. GROUND MUSTARD
3/4 CUP VINEGAR
1 CUP OIL
3/4 TSP. POPPY SEEDS

Directions:
Combine first 4 ingredients.Add vinegar,beat for 4 min.Add oil beat for another 4 min.Add poppy seeds,beat 5 min.Serve on fresh fruit or spinach salad.
