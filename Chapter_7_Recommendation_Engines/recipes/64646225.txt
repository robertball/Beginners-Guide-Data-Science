Title:
LTF DTox: Tomato and Bean Salad with Grilled Buffalo Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. heirloom tomatoes (about 2), cut into pieces
1 can beans (white, black or red), drained and rinsed
1/4 cup fresh tarragon leaves
1 tbsp. fresh lemon juice, plus wedges for serving
3 scallions, thinly sliced
1 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
Sea salt (to taste)
Black pepper (to taste)
1 lb. buffalo meat (grass fed, hormone free)

Directions:
1. Combine tomatoes, beans, tarragon, lemon juice, scallions and oil in a bowl; season with salt and pepper. Set aside.
2. Shape buffalo meat into 4 patties; season with salt and pepper; cook on the grill or stove to desired temperature.
3. Serve patties topped with tomato and bean salad.

Submitter: 
Stacey
Description: 
DTox friendly lean meat topped with Tomato and Bean salad.  Try this with Salmon or Chicken too!
