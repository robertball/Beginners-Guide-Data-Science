Title:
Simple Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 (8oz) paks of cream cheese, softened
4 eggs
2 teas of vanilla
1 cup sugar
crust
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
2 TB sugar
1/4 cup softened butter

Directions:
For the crust mix the ingredients together and press in a 9x13 pan.  Set aside.
For the cheesecake, cream all four ingredients together and pour over crust.
Bake in a prheated oven 350 degrees for 25 to 35 minutes until set.
Cool and top with your favorite fruit topping.

Submitter: 
jadedgurl
Description: 
My aunt always used to make this for me and top it with blueberry pie filling.  The flavor only gets better if allowed to sit for a day after baking so it is perfect for the holidays.
