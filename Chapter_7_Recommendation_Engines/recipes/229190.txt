Title:
7UP Winter Mint Sherbet Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983263.jpg
Ingredients:
1 quart lime sherbet
1 (2 liter) bottle 7UP®
6 ounces Rose's® Mojito
1 cup white rum (optional)

Directions:
Add the ingredients to 1 gallon punch bowl.
Garnish the punch bowl with mint leaves and slices of lemon and lime.

Submitter: 
7UP
Description: 
Greet your guests with a festive punch made with lime sherbet, mojito mix, and the sparkle of 7UP.
