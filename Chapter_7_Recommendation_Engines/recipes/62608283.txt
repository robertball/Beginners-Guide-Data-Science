Title:
Flat Roasted Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 4-6 lb chicken
- salt
- pepper
- garlic powder
- ground rosemary
- ground tarragon
- extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425
Use kitchen shears to remove backbone from chicken (Reserve to make stock!).
Lay chicken flat in oven safe sheet-pan or saute pan.
Brush olive oil over all surfaces of the chicken.
Add salt, pepper, garlic powder, ground rosemary, and tarragon to both sides (skin and under) of chicken.
Roast at 425 for 30 minutes.  Check.
If skin is browning to quickly reduce heat to 400 and continue roasting for 30 more minutes.
Remove from oven.  Let rest for 15 minutes.
Carve and serve.

Submitter: 
Jacob French
Description: 
Chicken is roasted hot, fast, and flat to give it crispy skin all over.
