Title:
French Dip Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.5 ounce) can beef consomme
1 can low sodium french onion soup
1/2 - 1 cup low sodium beef broth
1 pound thinly sliced deli roast beef
8 slices provolone cheese
4 rolls of choice, split

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place rolls on baking sheet.
Heat consomme, soup and broth in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Add roast beef and simmer for at least 15 minutes. Arrange roast beef on rolls and top with 2 slices of provolone.
Bake the sandwiches in the preheated oven for 5 minutes, or until cheese begins to melt. Serve sandwiches with small bowls of au jus for dipping.
