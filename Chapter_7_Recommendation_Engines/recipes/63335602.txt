Title:
Quick Ginger Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup shortening
1 cup white sugar
1 egg
4 Tbsp.fancy molasses
2 cups flour
2 tsp.baking soda
1 tsp.cinnamon
1 tsp.cloves
2 tsp.ginger
1/2 tsp.salt

Directions:
Cream shortening,add sugar,egg and molasses.
Add dry ingredients.Mix well.
Make into balls and roll in sugar.Press down with fork or cookie press.
Bake at 375 for 8-10 minutes.
