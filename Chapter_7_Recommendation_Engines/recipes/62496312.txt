Title:
Mom's trash bag salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of lettuce torn up or any of your favorite salad greens.
1 cup of swiss cheese grated or cubed
1 cup of cashews
1 red onion chopped up
1 cup of mushrooms, sliced
10 pices of bacon, cooked crisp and chopped up.
Dressing ingredients:
1/2 cup canola oil
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1/3 cup sugar
1/2 tsp mustard powder
1 tsp poppy seeds

Directions:
Mix all salad ingredients together. Place all dressing ingredients into a blender and mix well.Refridgerate salad dressing for at least 1 hour. and then pour over salad and mix well. When I make this for a large party I use a new trash bag ,put the salad into it and mix well.Hense the name.

Submitter: 
Diane Segal Teel
Description: 
This is a wonderfull salad with a home made poppy seed dressing that is great for pot lucks, I have not had any one not like this salad.The great think is you can add vegetables to the salad if wanted , such as radishs or scallons, or carrots are allso good init.
