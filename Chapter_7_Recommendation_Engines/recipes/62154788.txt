Title:
Buffalo chicken breast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Pound boneless chicken breast
1/2 Cup Franks red hot or other type cayenne sauce
2 Table spoons McCormicks season all Seasoned salt

Directions:
Mix Red hot sause and season all in a plastic bag or bowl add breasts and marinate at least one hour. Cook on medium heat for 20 to 30 mins or untill juices run clear
