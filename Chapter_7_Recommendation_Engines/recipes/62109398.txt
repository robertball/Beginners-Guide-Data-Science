Title:
Potato dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can condensed cream of potato soup
16 oz. sour cream
3 oz. real bacon bits
2 cups cheddar cheese, shredded
1/4 cup green onions, chopped
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Serve immediately or after refrigerating.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
My sister-in-law brought this dip over at Christmas time.  I couldn't stop eating it! Super quick and easy to fix and even better the next day! If you don't like onions, you can use onion powder instead.
