Title:
Peanut Butter Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz. Cream Cheese
1 cup Powdered Sugar
1/3 cup Peanut Butter
2 cup Cool Whip
1 Pie Crust ( either baked or graham cracker )
Garnish Optional: extra cool whip and/or
grated chocolate ( Hershey Kisses or Choco Bar).

Directions:
1. Allow cream cheese to soften.
2. Mix cream cheese and sugar.
3. Add peanut butter and cool whip and blend all the ingredients.
4. Pour into pie crust and Chill.
5. Serve Cold.
Optional: Garnish pie with Cool Whip and/or  grated chocolate.

Submitter: 
Nauntie
Description: 
Delicious and super easy no-bake pie... My sister, Angela got this recipe from one of her patients in 2018. She brought it to a birthday party and got several recipe request.
