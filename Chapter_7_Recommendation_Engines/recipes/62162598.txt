Title:
Mexican Tangy Sausage and Cream Cheese Delight! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. bulk sausage (hot or mild)
2 16 oz. package cream cheese (I use light)
2 jars chunky, thick salsa

Directions:
Brown sausage (hot, mild, or a combination of both), drain off excess fat.
Combine cream cheese cut in small pieces, salsa (hot, mild or a combination of both)together with the browned bulk sausage, and stir till thoroughly blended.
Pour into a microwaveable bowl and heat in the microwave, stirring periodically till heated through.
Can also be heated on top of stove at low temperature.

Fast, simple, delicious, and a quick, satisfying, appetizer, for a potluck.
