Title:
Lindy's Spaghetti Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 ounces spaghetti
2 pkgs frozen broccoli
2 cups parmesan cheese
salt and pepper to taste
1 can English Peas(optionl)drained

Directions:
Cook Spaghetti and broccoli according to pkg directions.  Drain both.  Add all other ingredients and toss together.  Serve with garli bread.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
A friend of mine served this to me, and I loved it.  For something so simple it is very tasty.
