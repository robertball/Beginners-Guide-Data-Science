Title:
Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz  black beans, dry
4 cups  beef stock
3 1/2 cups  water
1 whole ham hock
1 med  onion -- chopped
1 med  leek -- chopped
1 med  celery ribs -- chopped
4 tsp  dry wine
1/2 tsp  pepper -- freshly ground
1/4 tsp  thyme
1 bay leaf
1/2 tsp  salt

Directions:
Pressure Cooker:  place draining basket in cooker if you have one.  Sort and rinse beans.  Place them in basket, with all remaining ingredients except wine.  Cover cooker, lock lid  and bring to pressure, then cook 45 min.  Lift basket and allow solids to drain.
Top of Stove:  Place sorted and rinsed beans in large pot or dutch oven.  Add all remaining ingredients except wine.  Cover and cook at medium heat until beans are tender (2 hours?).
Finish for both methods:  Remove ham hock.  Puree about 1/2 of remianing solids in blender or food processor so that it gets a creamy texture.  Add wine to taste.  Cut meat from bones into small pieces and add to soup. Serve with sourdough rolls.
