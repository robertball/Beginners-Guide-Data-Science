Title:
Creamy Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breast halves, cooked and shredded w/2 forks
1 1/2 cups picante sauce, divided (Pace or other)
2 cups shredded colby-jack cheese, divided
2 (3 oz.) pkgs. cream cheese, softened to spreadable consistency in microwave.
12 corn tortillas, softened in oil, drained on paper towels

Directions:
In large bowl combine shredded chicken, 1/2 cup picante sauce, 1 cup colby-jack cheese, and cream cheese.  Stir until blended.  Put about 1/4 cup down center of each tortilla; roll up and place, seam side down in lightly greased baking dish.  Top w/remaining 1 cup picante sauce and 1 cup colby-jack cheese.  Bake at 350-375 degrees for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
MISS ALIX
Description: 
Cream cheese and Colby-Jack cheese combine to make a delicious and easy meal your family will love!
