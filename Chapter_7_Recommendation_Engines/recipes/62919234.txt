Title:
Olives Baked in Red Wine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unpitted Kalamata olives
1/2 cup dry red wine
1/4 tsp. fennel seeds, coarsely chopped
1 garlic clove, thinly sliced
2 tsps. olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees.  Combine all ingredients in small baking dish.  Bake uncovered until olives are heated through, about 20 minutes.  Cool slightly and serve warm.
