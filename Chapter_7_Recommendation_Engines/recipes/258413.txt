Title:
Louise Cocktail (Pitcher) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
2 cups vodka
8 sprigs fresh mint, stemmed
3 1/2 cups strawberry nectar (such as JumexÂ®)
3 1/2 cups no-pulp orange juice
2 cups sparkling wine
3/4 cup pressed fresh ginger juice
ice cubes

Directions:
Combine vodka and mint in a large pitcher; mash well with a cocktail muddler. Add strawberry nectar, orange juice, sparkling wine, and ginger juice; stir. Serve in cocktail glasses over ice.

BrandonS
Description: 
Amazing cocktail for summer that is full of flavors and packs a great punch! Got this recipe from a great bartender who was kind enough to share her recipe with the Observer.
