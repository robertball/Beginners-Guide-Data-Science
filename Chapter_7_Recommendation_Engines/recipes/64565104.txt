Title:
Crockpot Creamy Chicken and Jalapeno Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Main Ingredients:
2  - cans cream of chicken soup
1 -  8oz. light cream cheese
1 - c. sour cream
4 Tbsp. butter, melted
3/4 c. white onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1  - 16 oz. jar green salsa
2 Tbps. dry ranch powder
4  - frozen breasts boneless, skinless chicken
2 cups chicken broth
Reserved 'til end:
2  - cans pinto beans, washed and drained.
1 c. milk or half and half.
1/2 c. fresh cilantro, chopped (additional for garnish, if desired)
2 Tbsp. lemon or lime juice
Tortilla Chips

Directions:
1.  Dump main ingredients in a crock pot and cook on low for 5-8 hours. Stirring half way to blend ingredients
2.  About 30 minutes before you plan to eat, shredded chicken and add reserved ingredients (beans, cilantro and lemon juice).
3. If soup is too thick, thin with up to 1 c. of milk
4.  Garnish with additional cilantro and serve with tortilla chips
NOTE:  The heat for this dish is controlled by the green salsa.  I like mine HOT, but you can buy what works best for your family.

Submitter: 
Jenner Porter
Description: 
Rich. Creamy. Spicy. Add to that some shredded chicken, a hint of cilantro and just the right amount of beans and you'll have a delicious dinner cooking in no time.
