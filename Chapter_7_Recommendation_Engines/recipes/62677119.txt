Title:
Italian Spaghetti Sauce with Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
MEATBALLS
1/2 pound ground veal
1/2 pound ground pork
1 pound ground beef
1 1/4 cup fresh white bread crumbs (5 slices, crusts removed)
3 tablespoons chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1 teaspoon basil
1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
2 teaspoons kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1 extra-large egg, beaten
SAUCE
3/4 cup chopped onion
5 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 cup olive oil
2 (28 ounce) cans whole peeled tomatoes
2 teaspoons salt (don't skimp)
1 teaspoon white sugar
1 bay leaf
1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
3/4 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon Italian Seasoning
1/2 teaspoon of Garlic Powder
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Place the ground meats, both bread crumbs, parsley, Parmesan, salt, pepper, nutmeg, egg, and 3/4 cup warm water in a bowl. Combine very lightly with a fork. Using your hands, lightly form the mixture into 1-inch meatballs. You will have 28 to 32 meatballs. Place in preheated 400 degree oven for aprox. 10 minutes (until starting to brown).
In a large saucepan over medium heat, saute onion and garlic in olive oil until onion is translucent.  Stir in tomatoes, salt, pepper, sugar and bay leaf and seasonings.  Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer 90 minutes or as soon as meatballs are baked.  Stir in tomato paste,  and meatballs and juices in roasting pan and simmer 30 minutes more.  Serve.
