Title:
Tzatziki Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz sour cream
1/4 cucumber (60g), grated and patted dry
1 large clove garlic (8g), pressed
1/2 tsp dill weed
1/8 tsp salt

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine all ingredients.
Refrigerate for 1 hour.
Stir well and serve.

Submitter: 
Autumn
Description: 
This fragrant sauce is cool and refreshing, a perfect summer dip for cucumber slices.Modified from: Tzatziki Sauce I
