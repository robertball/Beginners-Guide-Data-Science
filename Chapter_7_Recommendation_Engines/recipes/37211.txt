Title:
Tuscan Tuna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986838.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound deli-style tuna salad
1 (12 ounce) jar quartered marinated artichoke hearts, drained
1 cup chopped roasted red bell pepper
1 teaspoon champagne or ale mustard
1/2 cup feta cheese
1 teaspoon black pepper
Yellow and red bell peppers, halved (optional)

Directions:
COMBINE first 6 ingredients. Spoon into bell pepper halves, if desired, and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Southern Living magazine
Description: 
Spoon Tuscan tuna into colorful bell pepper bowls for a refreshing salad.
