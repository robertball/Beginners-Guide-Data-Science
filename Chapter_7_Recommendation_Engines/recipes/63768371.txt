Title:
Yummy Mango Citrus Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Mango
1/2 Orange
2 Apples
1 Banana
1/2 Lemon
2 Slices of Ginger

Directions:
Scoop Mango, oranges, bananas and lemons from peel and juice.
Slice Apple into chunks small enough to juice.
Drop sliced ginger and juice.

Description:
