Title:
Mom's Jiffy Cinnamon Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 to 5 cups all purpose flour,
1/2 packaged yellow or white cake mix
2 pkg quick rise yeast
1 tsp salt
2 cups warm water (120 to 130 degrees)
2 tbsp butter
1/2 cup sugar
3 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine 3 cups flour, cake mix, yeast, salt and warm water; beat until smooth.  Add enough remaining flour to form a soft dough.
Turn out onto a lightly floured surface; knead until smooth, about 6-8 min.  Roll dough into a 9-in x  18-in rectangle.  Spread with softened margarine or butter and sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar.
Roll dough jelly- roll style, starting with the long end, Slice the roll into 1-in. circles; place on a greased baking sheet.  cover and let rise in a warm place until doubled, about 15 min.
Bake at 350 for 15-18 min.  Frost if desired.
