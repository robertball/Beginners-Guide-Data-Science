Title:
"Scooter's" Cheeseburger Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 inch Boboli pizza crust
2 cups shredded mozzarella
1/4 cup cheddar cheese
6 strips bacon,cooked and crumbled
1/2 pound hamburger
1/2 head iceburg lettuce, chopped
2 medium tomatoes, diced
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
fry sauce

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Prepare lettuce and tomatoes.
2. Cook bacon and brown hamburger.
3. Place Boboli on cookie sheet spread extra virgin olive oil on top. Top crust with hamburger, bacon, and cheeses. Bake for 30-35 mins.
4. Let pie cook for about 5-8 mins. Serve with lettuce, tomato, and fry sauce on top.
