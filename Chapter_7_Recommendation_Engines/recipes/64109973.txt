Title:
Three ingredient chocolate mousse Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 ounces good quality chocolate
1-1/2 cups heavy cream (do not substitute)
5 Tbs water OR up to 3 Tbs liqueur plus water to make 5 Tbs liquid.

Directions:
Break up chocolate in microwave-safe bowl.
Pour water and (if using) liqueur over chocolate; you need 5 Tbs of liquid total.
Microcook for 2 minutes at 50% power.  Remove chocolate mixutre and stir until smooth.  Let cool.
Once mixture is room temperature, put chilled whipping cream in bowl of kitchen aide-style mixer.
Using whisk attachment, beat cream until it forms stiff peaks.
Using rubber spatula, fold chocolate mixture into cream until mixture is uniform.  Place immediately into serving dish.
Chill for at least one hour.

Submitter: 
Chris D. Williams
Description: 
This amazing dessert is made with just three ingredients and comes together in minutes.  A word of warning - once you've tasted this, you will never eat regular chocolate mousse again.
