Title:
Egg Bhurji Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2203133.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 teaspoon garam masala
1 teaspoon ground turmeric
1 teaspoon ground coriander
salt to taste
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
3 green chile peppers, sliced
2 large eggs

Directions:
Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat; add garam masala, turmeric, coriander, and salt. Cook and stir onion and green chile peppers in the seasoned oil until onion is slightly tender, about 5 minutes.
Crack eggs into the skillet and season with salt; cook and stir until eggs are set and scrambled, about 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
N.ALAN12
Description: 
This is a quick and easy recipe. It is an Indian take on scrambled eggs. 
It is best enjoyed with toasted bread and a cup of chai in the morning.
