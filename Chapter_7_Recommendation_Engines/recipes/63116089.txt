Title:
Ground Beef and Potato Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups frozen hash browns, defrosted
1 pound extra lean ground beef
1 cup onion, chopped
1/3 cup chopped green pepper
2 tablespoons steak sauce
1/4 teaspoon salt
Dash pepper

Directions:
In a skillet, brown ground beef, onions, and peppers over medium-high heat. Drain if necessary.  Stir in steak sauce. Add the potatoes; cook for 10 minutes or until potatoes begin to brown and crisp, stirring occasionally.
Serve with ketchup.

Submitter: 
Clover
Description: 
Quick, easy, and inexpensive weeknight family meal.  Put some ketchup on the table and serve with a green salad.Modified from: Bratwurst Potato Skillet
