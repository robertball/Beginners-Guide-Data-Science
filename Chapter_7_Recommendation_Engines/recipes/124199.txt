Title:
Hot Chicken Heroes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/668773.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cubed, cooked chicken
1/2 cup cubed process American cheese
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup chopped green pepper
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
4 (6 inch) submarine rolls

Directions:
In a medium bowl, combine the first seven ingredients; mix well. Spread on rolls; wrap each in heavy-duty foil and bake at 325 degrees F for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Holly Jean VeDepo
Description: 
My mom would make these sandwiches while I was growing up. Now I make them for my own children. For variety, I sometimes substitute tuna or ham for the chicken.
