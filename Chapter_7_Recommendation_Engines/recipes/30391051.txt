Title:
Best Ever Old-Fashioned Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Butter
1 cup Sugar
1 Egg
1 tsp Vanilla
2 3/4 cups Flour
1 tsp Baking Powder
1/2 tsp Baking Soda
1/2 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp Ground Nutmeg
1/2 cup Sour Cream

Directions:
Mix together Butter, Sugar, Eggs, and Vanilla.  Mix together the dry ingredients and add alternately to the creamed mixture.  Chill overnight.
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.  Divide the dough and roll about 1/4 inch thick on a floured surface. Cut out shapes with a cookie cutter and place cookies on a greased baking sheet.
Bake 8-9 minutes, but do NOT overbake.
Decorate cookies to your desire!
