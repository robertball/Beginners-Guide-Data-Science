Title:
Quick and Easy Zucchini Bread IV Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/268764.jpg
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 cup vegetable oil
2 cups white sugar
2 cups grated zucchini
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
3 cups self-rising flour
3 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F ( 165 degrees C). Grease and flour two 8x4 inch loaf pans.
Mix wet ingredients in a food processor. Add zucchini and pulse a few times. Add flour and cinnamon and pulse a few more times. Be careful not to over mix. Divide batter into prepared pans.
Bake for 60 to 70 minutes, or until toothpick inserted into center comes out clean.
