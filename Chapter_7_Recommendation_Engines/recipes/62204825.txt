Title:
Sweet Ham Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
half boneless or bone-in ham(or canned ham)
1/2 cup orange juice
1/2 cup water
1 (20 ounce) can pineapple tidbits
1/4 cup brown sugar
cloves to stud ham

Directions:
Place ham in the slow cooker. Pour orange juice and pineapple over the top of the ham. Sprinkle brown sugar along the top and sides. Cover, and cook on Low for 8 hours.
