Title:
Simple Sour Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Medium cucumbers
1 gallon cider vinegar
1 quart water
1 c. canning salt
1 c. sugar
1 c. mustard seed

Directions:
Wash enough cucumbers to pack into 6-8 quart size canning jars to within 1/2 inch of top. Mix remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. Pour boiling solution over cucumbers to within 1/2 inch of top. Seal tightly and put in boiling water bath  for 15 mins.

Submitter: 
Deborah Norris
Description: 
Easiest of pickling recipes and very tasty. If you want dill taste, add a sprig of dill to each jar.
