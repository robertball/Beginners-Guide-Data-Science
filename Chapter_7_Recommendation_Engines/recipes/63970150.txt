Title:
Cracked Wheat Cereal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cracked wheat
3 cups boiling water, or a little more
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Add wheat to boiling water gradually.  Boil about 1/2 minute then gently simmer about 30 minutes.  Stir occasionally.
