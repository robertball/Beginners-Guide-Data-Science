Title:
Mexican Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground beef
1 pound ground hot dogs
1 medium onion chopped
5 tablespoons sweet relish
5 tablespoons hot relish
1/2 cup ketchup
1 tablespoon mustard
20 hot dog rolls

Directions:
brown meats and onion.
add relish, ketchup, and mustard.
simmer as long as desired
when ready to serve, place mixture in hot dog roll and put under broiler until top of roll is crusty
