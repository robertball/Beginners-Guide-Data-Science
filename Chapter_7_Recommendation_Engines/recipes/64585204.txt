Title:
Israeli Golden Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups long cooking rice
1 T. chicken soup mix
1/2 tsp. curry
1/2 tsp. tumeric
1/2-2/3 cup golden or regular raisins
1/2 cup almonds

Directions:
Mix all with 4 cups water and cook according to directions for rice.
Spices, raisins, and almonds can be adjusted according to taste.
Best served with roast chicken.
