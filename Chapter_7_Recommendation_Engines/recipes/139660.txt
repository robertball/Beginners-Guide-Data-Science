Title:
Papaya Passion Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/83355.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups papaya - peeled, seeded and cubed
2 cups milk
1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup sweetened condensed milk
1 cup vanilla yogurt
2 tablespoons cream cheese
2 cups ice

Directions:
Place papaya, milk, sugar, condensed milk, yogurt, cream cheese, and ice in a blender. Blend until smooth. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
ainey
Description: 
This is the most delicate, creamiest papaya smoothie ever. It is very refreshing and tasty!
