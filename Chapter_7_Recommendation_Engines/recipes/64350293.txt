Title:
Spicy Salmon Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can red salmon with broth retained and bones removed
1 lb red potatoes, peeled and boiled
4 cups whole milk
1 cup cream
3/4 tsp garam masala
1/4 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp white pepper
Salt to taste
Pinch of ground cloves
1/4 cup finely minced shallots
1/2 stick butter
1 1/2 tbsp fresh thyme

Directions:
Melt but do not brown butter and add shallots to soften.
Add spices and thyme.
Add milk and cream and gently heat, but do not scald or boil.
Add salmon.
