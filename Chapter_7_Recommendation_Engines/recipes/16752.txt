Title:
Creamy Bacon Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4452477.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds bacon
1 (32 ounce) jar mayonnaise
1/2 cup light corn syrup
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 onion, minced

Directions:
Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain on paper towels.
Crumble the bacon into a large mixing bowl. Stir in mayonnaise, corn syrup, apple cider, black pepper, and onion. Stir until ingredients are well combined. Cover and refrigerate for 6 hours.

Submitter: 
Cheryl C.
Description: 
Serve this dip with veggies. It can also be used as a salad dressing. Add more bacon if you wish.
