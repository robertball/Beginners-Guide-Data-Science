Title:
Honey Wheat Sandwich Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups warm milk
1 egg, beaten
2 tablespoons butter, softened
1/4 cup honey
3/4 teaspoon salt
2 3/4 cups all purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 packet active dry yeast

Directions:
Place 1 1/4 cups warm milk in microwave for 1 minute, put butter in milk.  test temp of milk to make sure not too warm and allow yeast to proof in warm milk
Mix all the dry ingredients.  Once yeast has proofed for 10 minutes, add the remaning wet ingredients and mix with the dry ingredients.  Place on well floured surface (floured with wheat flour).  Knead for 5-7 minutes.  Cover and place dough in warm place to rise for 1-2 hours.
Once dough has doubled in size, shape into 12 rolls, cover and let rise again, about 1 hour.  In the meantime preheat oven to 350F.  Once rolls have finished rising, reomve cover and place in oven.
Bake in preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Soncea Braden-McCann
Description: 
Hearty and tasty country style sandwich rolls. Honey, whole wheat and just a little bit of butter. They freeze well too!Modified from: Honey Wheat Sandwich Rolls
