Title:
No Stick Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
1 cup brown rice
2 1/4 cups water boiling.

Directions:
In sauce pan place heat olive oil.
Pour brown rice in olive oil and stir and coat thoroughly.
Pour boiling water into sauce pan carefully.
Lower heat and cook on low for 40 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
No Stick Brown Rice - Easy and simple, your rice will not stick with this little recipe.
