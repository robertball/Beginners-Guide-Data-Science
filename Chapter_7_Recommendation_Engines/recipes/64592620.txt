Title:
Butter Pecan Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter, softened
1 pkg (3.4oz) instant butterscotch pudding mix
1 1/4 cups flour
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Cream butter and pudding mix.
Gradually add flour.
Fold in pecans.
Roll into 1 1/2" balls.
Place 2" apart on baking sheets; flatten to 1/2" with bottom of greased glass.
Bake @ 375 x 10-13 min. or until light golden brown.
Cool on wire racks.
Makes about 24 cookies.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Shortbread-like cookies with pecans. MMmm :) This makes a small batch - I suggest doubling it (at least!).
