Title:
Strawberry Margarita Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1008732.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups fresh strawberries (frozen ok)
1 tablespoon honey
3 tablespoons lime juice
1 tablespoon tequila (or use rum extract)

Directions:
Place all ingredients in food processor and blend for thirty seconds, or just until strawberries are pureed and mixture is blended. If desired, let set overnight in refrigerator for alcohol to evaporate.
Serve over ice cream, sorbet, or any other sweet treat.  Also good served over cooked fish.

Submitter: 
Angela Sackett  Superhotmama
Description: 
This fresh strawberry sauce is delicious served cold over desserts or even savory dishes like grilled fish or chicken.
