Title:
Tahini Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup tahini
1 cup + 2 tbsp flour
1/2 tsp baking powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Cream the butter and sugar. Add tahini and vanilla, mix well. In a separate bowl mix the remaining ingredients. Combine the 2 mix well again. Drop on cookie sheet, bake for 15 minutes or until they start to look golden. Let cool for 10-15 minutes before removing from cookie sheet.

Submitter: 
Faina Gersh
Description: 
This cookie recipe is from Israel...Tahini can be found in most groceries or check in a middle eastern store...enjoy!
