Title:
Mamaws Southern Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 tablespoons vegetable oil or bacon drippings
2 cups white cornmeal
2 heaping teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
3 eggs
2 cups buttermilk

Directions:
Preheat oven 425 degrees.
Put vegetable oil or bacon drippings in a 8" or 9" 'seasoned' cast iron skillet. Place in oven to heat.
In large bowl, mix dry ingredients; make a 'well' in the center of the dry ingredients, add eggs. start mixing, slowly add buttermilk, blend well.
Remove hot skillet from oven, slowly pour batter into center of hot skillet.
Return to oven. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.

Submitter: 
MelodyeRose
Description: 
Southern Skillet made Cornbread - no sugar in this one. Great with Black-eyed peas, Mustard & Collard Greens. Original recipe calls for white cornmeal; yellow cornmeal or blue cornmeal work just as well.
