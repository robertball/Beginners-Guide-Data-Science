Title:
Italian Style Meatball Sliders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18 oz) package of al fresco Italian Style Frozen Meatballs, recipe calls for approximately 16 meatballs
1 cup tomato basil, low sodium,pasta sauce
16 small dinner rolls, 2" x 2"
4 fresh basil leaves

Directions:
Pour the tomato sauce in a small sauce pan, add the meatballs, cover and heat over medium heat for 8 - 10 minutes. Gently stir to coat the meatballs with the sauce.
Slice the small rolls in half, and place a tomao & basil meatball in each roll. Assemble on a serving platter.
Top each meatball with a dollop of the remaining heated tomato sauce. Garnish with sprigs of fresh basil and serve immediately.
