Title:
Chicken Quesadillas for 160 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/776593.jpg
Ingredients:
50 cup shredded cooked chicken
25 chopped onions
25 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
25 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
100 (8 inch) wheat tortillas
Salsa on the side

Directions:
In a bowl, combine chicken, onion, mozzarella and cheddar; set aside. Spray side of each tortilla with nonstick cooking spray. Place each tortilla greased side down on hot griddle. Place chicken on each tortilla. Flip over and cook over low for 1-2 minutes on each side of until tortilla is golden brown. Cut into 8 wedges. Keep warm in oven. Serve with salsa.
