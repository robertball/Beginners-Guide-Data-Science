Title:
Christmas Breakfast Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 slices bacon or 1 cup cubed ham
3 cups hash brown potatoes
2 or 3 stems green onion, chopped with whites and green seperated
1/3 cup diced red bell pepper
5 eggs
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
a few dashes hot sauce
1/2 cup milk
S&P; to taste
1 1/2 cups shedded Cheddar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Lightly grease a 7x11 casserole
In large skillet fry bacon until crisp, drain on paper towel. Carefully add hash browns and whites of onion to hot bacon fat.  Lightly brown on both sides.  While potatoes are cooking crumble bacon and set aside.  Remove potatoes and onion to casserole, top with peppers and bacon(or ham).  In medium bowl combine eggs, dry mustard, hot sauce, milk, 1 cup of the cheese and s&p.  Give this a good stir.  Pour over casserole and bake for 30 min. Top with remaining cheese and return to oven for 5 more min.  Remove from oven and serve immediately with a fruit salad and muffins.
