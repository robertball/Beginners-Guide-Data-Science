Title:
Peanut Butter Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C milk
1/2 C sour cream
3 oz vanilla instant pudding mix
1 C creamy peanut butter
1/3 C sugar

Directions:
Combine milk, sour cream and pudding mix in med. bowl; whisk until smooth.
Stir in peanut butter and sugar; mix until well blended.
Serve with apples and bananas.
Store in refrigerator.
If dip becomes too thick, stir in additional milk.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Made with vanilla instant pudding mix, milk, sour cream, sugar and peanut butter
