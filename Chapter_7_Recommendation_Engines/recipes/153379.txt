Title:
Tropical Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979256.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3/4 cups thawed COOL WHIP Whipped Topping
1/3 cup maple-flavored or pancake syrup
2 ripe bananas, sliced
1 (20 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained
1/2 cup BAKER'S ANGEL FLAKE Coconut
1/4 cup chopped PLANTERS Walnuts

Directions:
Beat whipped topping and syrup in large bowl with wire whisk or electric mixer on medium speed until well blended.
Add fruit, coconut and walnuts; stir gently. Cover.
Refrigerate several hours or until chilled. Garnish with orange peel just before serving, if desired.

Submitter: 
BAKER'S Chocolate
Description: 
Chock full of coconut, pineapple, bananas, and nuts, this creamy treat is a taste of the tropics.
