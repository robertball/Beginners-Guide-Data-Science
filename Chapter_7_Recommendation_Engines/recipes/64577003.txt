Title:
Gingerbread Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup margarine
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup molasses
1 egg
2 cups flour
1 tsp. soda
2 tsp. ginger
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. salt
3/4 cup water
Glaze:
1 1/2 cups icing sugar
2 tbsp. + milk
1/2 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Cream shortening and sugar.
Add molasses and egg. Mix well.
Combine flour, soda, ginger, cinnamon & salt; add to creamed mixture alternately with water.
Pour into a greased 9" x 13" pan.
Bake @ 400 x 13 - 15 minutes or until tests done.
Cool completely.
In small bowl, mix glaze ingredients until smooth, adjusting for spreading consistency.
Spread over cooled bars.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Moist, frosted gingerbread flavored bars.
