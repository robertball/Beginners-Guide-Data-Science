Title:
Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups raisins
1 cup water
1 cup margarine
2 cups sugar (white or brown)
3 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
3 3/4 cups flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. allspice
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. salt
Nuts, if desired

Directions:
Boil raisins and water x 5 min. Cool.
Cream margarine, sugar and eggs.
Add remaining ingredients, then cooled raisins.
Drop by tbsp. onto cookie sheets.
Bake @ 350 x 12-15 min.
Options:
1 cup oatmeal, then only 3 1/4 cups flour.
Substitute chocolate chips for raisins (& obviously skip the whole boiling in water step haha!).

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Classic raisin cookies with some delicious spice flavor.
