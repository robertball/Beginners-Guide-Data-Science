Title:
Salmon Avocado Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Leftover Grilled or Poached Wild King Salmon, Chilled
2 tablespoons butter
salt and pepper to taste
8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
12 grape tomatoes, halved
2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
1 5 ounce bag TJ's Herb Salad Mix
1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and cubed
fresh cilantro, chopped (to taste)
1 fresh jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
2 tablespoons white white vinegar
Feta cheese, crumbled

Directions:
Melt the butter in a skillet over medium heat, and saute the mushrooms until tender.
In a large bowl, toss together the salmon, mushrooms, tomatoes, lettuce, avocado, cilantro, and jalapeno. Whisk together the olive oil and the vinegar. Add to the salad and toss.  Season with salt and pepper, and sprinkle with feta cheese to serve.
