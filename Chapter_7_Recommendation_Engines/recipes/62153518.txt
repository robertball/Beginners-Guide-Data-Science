Title:
CHUTNEY CHICKEN Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb broiler-fryer chicken cut up and skinned or several chicken breast
1/4 cup chutney any flavor (I use mango)
1/4 cup key lime juice
2 TBSP. onion finely chopped
2 TBSP. plain yogurt
1/2 tsp curry powder
1/4 tsp dry mustard
1 lime cut in wedges

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except chicken and lime wedges in a bowl. Add chicken and coat. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hrs.
Heat oven to 375.Remove chicken from marinade but save the marinade.Place chicken in 9x13 pan and pour reserved marinade over chicken.Cover and bake 30 minutes spooning juices over chicken as you go. Remove cover and bake till done.Arrange on platter, pour sauce over chicken and garnish with lime wedges.
