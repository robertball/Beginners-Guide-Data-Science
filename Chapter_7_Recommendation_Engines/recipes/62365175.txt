Title:
Apricot Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/407100.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups apricots, washed, pitted and chopped--not peeled
1 cup shallots, peeled and julienned
1 cup of mixed Red and Green Bell Peppers
1/2 cup fresh pineapple, chopped
1/4 cup Cherry Tomatoes, chopped
1 Habenaro Pepper, seeded and minced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp fresh Cilantro, minced
1/2 tsp Cumin
1/4 cup fresh squeezed Lime juice

Directions:
On a clean cutting board, cut and chop all fruit and veggies as stated above. Measure acquerately as possible.
Mix toegether apricots, shallots, bell peppers, pineapple, tomatoes, habenaro pepper, garlic, cilantro, cumin and lime juice. Mix throughly.
Refrigerate for at least 2-4 hours.

Submitter: 
Melissa Goff
Description: 
This recipe was part of a recipe challenge that worked out quiet well. Nice heat from the Habanero and lovely sweetness from the Apricots and Pineapples.
