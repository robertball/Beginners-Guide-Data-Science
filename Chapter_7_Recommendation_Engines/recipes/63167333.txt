Title:
Ultra Met Turkey Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
.33 c evoo
1 large onion .5 in. dice 1.5 cups
2 large cloves garlic minced
1 medium jalapeno seeded minced
2# ground turkey breast
1 tsp kosher salt
1 28 oz whole tomato low Na
.5 c water
3 tbsp ancho chili powder
4 tsp ground cumin
1.5 tsp ground coriander
.5 tsp ground black pepper
.25 tsp hot paprika
2 tbsp minced oregano
1 15 oz can chili red beans
chopped cilantro optional
chopped red or green onion optional
8 sprouted corn tortillas

Directions:
Heat evoo large heavy pan medium heat
Add onions 3-5 min translucent
Add garlic and jalapeno cook 1 min more
Stir ground turkey and salt cook 5 min
crush tomatoes and add with juice
add water, chili powder, cumin, coriander, bl. pepper and paprika cook 30 at gentle simmer stir occasionally
Add oregano and red beans and simmer 20-30 more minutes
thin with water as neccessary
garnish before serving with cilantro and onion
