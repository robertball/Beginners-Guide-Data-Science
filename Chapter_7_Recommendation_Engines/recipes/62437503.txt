Title:
Strawberry Margarita Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package strawberry cake mix
1 (3.4 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
4 eggs
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2/3 cup water
1/4 cup lime juice
1/4 cup tequila
2 tablespoons triple sec liqueur
1 cup confectioners' sugar
1 tablespoon tequila
2 tablespoons triple sec liqueur
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
1 tablespoon almond extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a 10 inch Bundt pan with Pam.
In a large bowl combine cake mix, pudding mix, eggs, oil, water, lime juice 1/4 cup tequila and 2 tablespoons triple sec. Beat for 2 minutes.
Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 to 50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Cool in pan for 10 minutes
To make the glaze:  In a small bowl, combine confectioners' sugar with 1 tablespoon tequila, 2 tablespoons triple sec, 1 tablespoon vanilla extract and 1 tablespoon almond extract. Mix until smooth. Pour glaze over cake while still warm.
