Title:
Green Avocado Leek Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 leek
1 Tbl. oil
1 avocado
70 grams horseradish
1 lemon
salt

Directions:
Saute leek in a pan with olive oil. Add avocado and horseradish. Mash avocado and cook for a couple more minutes. Add lemon juice and salt. Blend all or part of mixture to give dip with desired consistency. Store covered in the fridge.
