Title:
Silvia's Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Beef
1 sm. Onion (Diced)
1/2 cup Chili Sauce
1/2 cup Ketchup
1/2 cup Water
1/3 cup Sweet Pickle Relish
3 tsp Yellow Mustard
1/4 cup Brown Sugar

Directions:
Brown ground beef and onion.  Drain.  Return to pan.
Mix the rest of the ingredients with the browned beef and onion.  Bring to boil.  Simmer for 15 minutes.
Serve on buns.

Submitter: 
BENANDREE
Description: 
This is a cheap and easy sloppy joe recipe.  My mom makes it and everyone loves it.  People are constantly asking her for the recipe.
