Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2441985.jpg
Ingredients:
unsalted butter
2 1/2 cups cooked quinoa, added at room temperature
4 large eggs, beat before adding
1/2 teaspoon fine grain salt
1 CUP KALE, VERY FINELY CHOPPED
1/2 cup edamame,  shelled
3/4 cup breadcrumbs
1/4 teaspoon lime juice
1/8 cup chive
1/2 cup rice flour

Directions:
1. Add the quinoa, eggs, lime, and sea salt the medium bowl.
2. Stir in the kale, and edamame and most of the breadcrumbs
3. let sit for a minute, the breadcrumbs should absorb moisture
4. hand shape and dust in rice flour
5. Pan fry 4 at a time for 3 minutes or until crust is golden brown
