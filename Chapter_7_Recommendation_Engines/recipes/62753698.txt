Title:
Simple Red Beans and Brown Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 C small red beans
3 C short grain brown rice
1 white onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, sliced or chopped
1 tbsp oil
4 Tbsp creole seasoning
3 Tbsp salt
2 Tbsp pepper
3 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 lb. smoked sausage or ham, cubed.
8 C water

Directions:
Start by soaking the beans in cold water for 4 hours. Drain and rinse.
In a large pot, warm the oil on medium heat and add garlic and onion. Heat until slightly soft, but do not brown.
Add water, seasoning, salt and pepper and stir.
Reduce heat to low and add red beans and brown rice.
Cover and let simmer for 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
Add sausage or ham and Worcestershire sauce; cover and let simmer for another 30 minutes.
Let cool for 20 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
Synexx
Description: 
This is no fancy red beans and rice with too many ingredients, it is very simple. Packed with protein, fiber and good carbs, it is a great lunch to get you through until the end of the day and makes a lot for use throughout the week.
