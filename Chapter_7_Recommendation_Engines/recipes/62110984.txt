Title:
Emergency Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 cup sugar
1 cup peanut butter

Directions:
Mix ingredients until blended.
Roll into balls.  Place on ungreased cookie sheet and flatten with a fork.
Bake at 350 for 15 min.
