Title:
Seafood Seasoning Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbs celery salt
1 tbs ground bay leaves
2 tsp ground black pepper
1 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp dry mustard
1/8 tsp nutmeg
1/8 tsp ground cinnamon
pinch ground cloves
pinch garlic powder

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together. Add to water when boiling/steaming seafood. 1-2 tbs per quart or to taste.

Submitter: 
Amy Lawler
Description: 
This homemade seasoning mix rivals the store-bought stuff. Use for crawfish, shrimp, crab and others.
