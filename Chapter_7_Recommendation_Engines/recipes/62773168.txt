Title:
Chicken Spinach Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
1 (9 inch) unbaked pastry shell
1 cup diced cooked chicken
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
3 eggs
1/2cup milk
3/4 cup mayonnaise*
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Sprinkle 1/4 cup cheese into the pastry shell.  In a bowl, combine the chicken, 1/2 cup spinach, onion and remaining cheese (save remaining spinach for another use).  Spoon into pastry shell.  in a bowl, whisk the eggs, milk, mayonnaise, salt and pepper; pour over the chicken mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 40-45 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.  Let stand for 15 minutes before cutting.

Submitter: 
Kiersten Butler Stahl
Description: 
This delicious quiche features chicken, spinach and cheddar cheese, but you can use Swiss cheese if you'd like. Field editor Barbara McCalley of Allison Park, Pennsylvania shares the recipe and notes, 'This quiche is easy to prepare and cuts beautifully.'Modified from: Chicken Spinach Quiche
