Title:
Zucchini Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1052379.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 C Butter
4 cups thinly sliced zucchini
4 C rough chopped onion
1/2 tsp sea salt
1/2 tsp grd black pepper
1/2 tsp Mrs Dash Italian Seasoning
1/2 tsp garlic powder
2 eggs, well beaten
8 oz shredded mozzarella
1-8 oz tube crescent rolls
2 tbsp. yellow mustard

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Heat butter in skillet over medium heat; cook & stir zucchini & onions about 10 minutes until tender, but not brown.  Cool about 5 minutes
Meanwhile, remove crescent dough from can, and press into 9X13" pan, pressing seams together for form solid dough crust on bottom and sides of dish.
Spread mustard on the dough.
Add well beaten eggs, cheese,sea salt, pepper, Italian seasoning and garlic powder to the zucchini mixture.
Pour into prepared crust.
Bake til set in middle & lightly browned, 25 to 30 minutes depending on your oven.  Cool slightly before cutting into squares.  Serve either warm or at room temperature.

Submitter: 
empebi
Description: 
This is a hit every time I bring it to a pot luck or party.  It even passes the test with people who don't think they like zucchini.  For appetizers, cool about 20 minutes before cutting into squares for serving.
