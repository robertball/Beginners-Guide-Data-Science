Title:
Marinated Chuck Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. orange juice concentrate
3 T. soy sauce
3 T. Worcestershire sauce
3-4 lb. roast

Directions:
Mix first three ingredients.
Pour into a large ziplock and add roast.
Let marinate for 8+ hours.
Cook as usual.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
With only three ingredients, this marinade packs a killer punch! Great flavor that really contributes to tenderness.
