Title:
Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
2/3 c. white sugar
2/3 c. brown sugar
1/4 c. oil
3/4 c. applesauce (heaping)
2-3 tsp vanilla
2 c. all-purpose flour
2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt (opt)
2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp cloves
1/4 tsp allspice (opt)
3 c. grated carrots (use large holes on grater)
1 c. chopped pecans or walnuts
FROSTING:
1/2 c. butter, softened
8 oz cream cheese, softened
2 c. confectioner's sugar (powdered sugar)
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup chopped pecans/walnuts (opt)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350* F
Grease and flour 9 x 13 or Bundt pan
In a large bowl, beat together eggs, oil, applesauce, sugars and vanilla, Mix in flour, baking soda and baking powder, salt and spices. (cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, allspice) Stir in the carrots.
Fold in the nuts. Pour into prepared pan. Do not OVER mix!
Bake for 40-50 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Let cool for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.
TO MAKE THE FROSTING:
In a medium bowl combine butter, cream cheese, confectioner's sugar and vanilla.  Beat until mixture is smooth and creamy.  Stir in nuts if desired and frost the cooled cake.
