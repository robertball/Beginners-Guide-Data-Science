Title:
Chocolate Pistachio Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box instant pistachio pudding
1 yellow cake mix
3/4 cup Hershey's syrup
4 eggs
1 stick of butter - melted
1 cup orange juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Mix all ingredients together except syrup.
Pour 1/2 to 3/4 of batter into greased Bundt pan.
Add Hershey's syrup to remaining batter and spoon on top of batter in pan.
Bake for 60 minutes or until cake tests done.
Sprinkle with powdered sugar before serving if desired.
