Title:
Homemade Foot or Hand Scrub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1105337.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup moisturizing oil (ie; sweet almond, avocado, olive, etc.)(more than 1 type of oil can be used to create a custom blend)
1 cup sea or kosher salt (course)
5-10 drops of essential oil (ie; lavender, peppermint, tea tree, etc.)(more than 1 type of essential oil can be used to create a custom blend)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients by stirring well.
Store in glass jar with or without lid.
Take a heaping teaspoon sized amount and rub foot or hands to exfoliate and soften.
Rinse well in warm water and pat dry.

Submitter: 
Linda
Description: 
Wonderful, all natural foot and/or hand scrub that moisturizes and exfoliates.
