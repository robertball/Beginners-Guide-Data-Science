Title:
Nasi Goreng Cincai Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup rice
2 cups water
1/3 cup diced carrots
1/3 cup diced mushroom
1/4 cup diced onions
1 spring onion, chopped finely
1/3 cup sliced chicken bacon (or bacon if you prefer)
2 tbsp cooking oil
Light soy sauce

Directions:
Boil rice until ALL the water is absorbed. Cool slightly before frying.
Heat oil in frying pan and stir fry the onion and spring onion until soft. Add the chicken bacon.
Add carrots and mushroom when chicken bacon is tender. Fry until vegetables are cooked.
Add in the rice and soy sauce, cook together, making sure that the vegetables are properly cooked. Served while hot.

Submitter: 
Mad Izatie
Description: 
This is my favorite recipe when making fried rice - it's because it's easy and simple, and also delicious. Add minced meat if prefered.
