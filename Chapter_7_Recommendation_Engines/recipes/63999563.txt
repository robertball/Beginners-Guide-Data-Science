Title:
Easy Bean Enchilada Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 recipe Easy enchilada sauce* or 1 can of enchilada sauce
1 can whole kernel corn, drained
2 cups homemade Refried pinto beans or 1 can Refried Beans*
12 soft corn tortillas (6 inch)
1 can (15 oz each)  Whole Black Beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (14.5 oz each) Diced Tomatoes, undrained
2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Spray 13x9-inch baking dish with cooking spray. Spread half of enchilada sauce in bottom of dish. Cook corn in microwave according to package directions, using minimum cook time.
2. Spread about half of refried beans on 6 tortillas. Place tortillas, bean-side up, over sauce in dish, overlapping to fit. Stir together remaining enchilada sauce, corn, black beans and undrained tomatoes in large bowl. Spread half of corn mixture over tortillas in dish. Sprinkle with 1 cup cheese.
3. Repeat layering with remaining refried beans and tortillas, corn mixture and remaining 1 cup cheese.
4. Bake 35 minutes or until hot in center and bubbly around edges. Let stand 5 minutes before cutting into 8 pieces.

Description: 
This is a quick, easy, & delicious recipe.  You could easily add chicken or beef too.  The whole family loved it.
