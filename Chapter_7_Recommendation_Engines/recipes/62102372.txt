Title:
Chocolate Orange/Mango Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-7 ice cubes
2 cups orange/mango juice
1 whole orange, peeled
1 6oz containter of Stoneyfield Farms Chocolate fat free yogurt

Directions:
Peel orange
Add orange, juice, ice and yogurt to blender
Blend well
Enjoy :)

Submitter: 
Kelly Lindsey
Description: 
A yummy orange/mango smoothie, with a fat free chocolate twist!
