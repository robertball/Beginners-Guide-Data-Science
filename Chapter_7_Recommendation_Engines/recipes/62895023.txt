Title:
Pan Fried Haddock Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Haddock fillets (about 7 oz each), as many as you'd like
Olive or Peanut Oil

Directions:
Rub each fillet with olive oil. Season with salt, pepper, and anything else (or noting) as suits your taste.
Put peanut oil (preferred) or olive oil in frying pan, say a Tablespoon per fillet, or slightly more. Heat until the addition of a fillet spits.
Fry on one side for about 4 minutes, turn, and fry on the other side for a minute or two. (Adjust as necessary.)
Test for doneness. Serve immediately.
