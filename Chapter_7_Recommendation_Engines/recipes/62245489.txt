Title:
Sweet Potato Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Brownies:
1 1/2 cups flour
2 cups sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup butter softened
4 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla
2 cups grated sweet potatoes
1 cup chopped pecans (optional)
Glaze:
1 cup powdered sugar
milk
1/4 stick melted butter

Directions:
Mix sugar and butter together. Add remaining ingredients stirring after each addition. Bake in a 10x13 inch greased pan for 30 to 40 minutes until toothpick comes out clean.
For glaze, mix powdered sugar, butter and enough milk to make a thin glaze. Spread on brownies while hot. Cool and slice when ready to serve.
