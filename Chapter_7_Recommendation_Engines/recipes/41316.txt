Title:
Bacon Cheeseburger Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/977616.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package uncooked tube or spiral pasta
1 pound ground beef
6 slices bacon, diced
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed tomato soup, undiluted
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
barbecue sauce and prepared mustard, optional

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions.
Meanwhile, in a skillet, cook beef over medium heat until no longer pink; drain and set aside.
In the same skillet, cook bacon until crisp; remove with a slotted spoon to paper towels. Discard drippings.
Drain pasta; add to the skillet. Add soup, beef and bacon; heat through. Sprinkle with cheese; cover and cook until the cheese is melted. Serve with barbecue sauce and mustard if desired.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home
Description: 
While the noodles cook, brown the ground beef and bacon for a filling dish that's a snap to make.
