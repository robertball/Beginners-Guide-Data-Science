Title:
Vanilla Buttercream Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/946553.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
5 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 cup confectioner sugar
1 cup butter
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
1. In a saucepan, combine the milk and 5 tablespoons flour. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens. Place in fridge or freezer until it is cool to the touch.
2. Cream together butter, 1 cup confectioners sugar and 1 teaspoon vanilla until light and fluffy.
3. Remove the cooled milk and flour mixture from the fridge and stir to ensure it is smooth, then slowly incorporate with the cream and sugar mixture until icing reaches spreading consistency. (For a thicker icing, you may not need the entire milk mixture)

Description: 
Smooth and tasty vanilla buttercream, perfect consistency for decorating any cake
