Title:
Jim's Slow Cooker Chicken Pot Pie Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/630830.jpg
Ingredients:
1-Whole chicken torn into pieces ( you can use a roasted chicken from the store if pressed for time)or 4 chicken breasts cut into bite sized pieces
2 medium red potatoes large dice
1 (4 ounce) package baby carrots
1/2 cup chopped celery
4- 10.5 oz cans condensed cream of chicken soup
3 cubes  chicken bouillon
1 teaspoons garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon celery salt
1 tablespoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon vegeta
1 (8 ounce) bag frozen mixed vegetables

Directions:
Combine the chicken, potatoes, carrots, celery, chicken soup, chicken bouillon, garlic salt, celery salt, and black pepper in a slow cooker; cook on High for 5 hours.
Stir the frozen mixed vegetables into the slow cooker, and cook 1 hour more.
