Title:
Peanut Butter and Jelly Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) READY CRUST® Graham Cracker Pie Crust
2 (8 ounce) packages light cream cheese, softened
1 cup creamy peanut butter
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup strawberry preserves*

Directions:
Combine cream cheese, peanut butter and sugar in a mixing bowl.
Beat with an electric mixer until well blended. Spoon into pie crust.
Refrigerate 2 hours or overnight. Place preserves in a small microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on high power 20-30 seconds; stir. Spoon preserves over pie filling.

Submitter: 
Ready Crust® Kitchens
Description: 
Fill a Ready Crust® Graham Cracker Pie Crust with peanut butter and strawberry preserves and you'll impress both kids and adults.
