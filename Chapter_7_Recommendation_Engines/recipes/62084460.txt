Title:
Sundried Tomato and Feta Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 lb Feta cheese (lite if you want)
1/2 cup sundried tomatoes in oil, drained and coarsly chopped
1 clove garlic
1 tsp dried thyme
1/2 tsp dried oregano
3/4 cup milk (yes you can use skim)
1 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a food processor until smooth. Serve with pita chips or veggies. Delicious!
