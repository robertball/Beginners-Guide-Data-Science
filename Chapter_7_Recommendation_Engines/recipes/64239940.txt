Title:
Basic Deviled Eggs (Mayo) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Doz. Cage Free Eggs
3 TBsp. Mayonnaise
2 Squeezes of Mustard
Paprika

Directions:
Boil eggs in pan with salt for 10 - 12 minutes. Remove from heat and put into the sink with cold running water for about 3 minutes. Turn off water and let sit for a few minutes also. Hit the egg on the side of the counter and break the shell, peel it off. Put the peeled egg into a separate bowl.
Cut eggs in half(sideways) and put the yolk into a small bowl and put the white of the egg on a cookie sheet.
Mix yolk, Mayo and Mustard in small bowl and mix well (removing as many lumps as possible). Put spoonfulls of yolk mixture into the half hole inside the egg white. Do repeatedly untill all egg white holes are filled with yolk mixture. Once finished, sprinkle paprika on top of the yolk mixture for added flavor.
