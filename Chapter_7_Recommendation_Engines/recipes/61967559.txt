Title:
Oven-Fried Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 tsp. paprika
1/2 tsp. salt
freshly ground black pepper
3 lb. chicken pieces

Directions:
Heat oven to 425 degrees. Heat butter in 13x9x2-inch baking pan in oven until melted.
Mix flour, paprika, salt, and pepper. Coat chicken with flour mixture. Place chicken, skin side down, in pan. Bake 30 minutes, uncovered. Turn chicken and bake for an additional 30 minutes, until juices run clear.
