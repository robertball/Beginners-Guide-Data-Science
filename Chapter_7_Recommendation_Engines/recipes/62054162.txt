Title:
Fluffy fruit dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-8 ounce package cream cheese, softened
1-12 ounce container of whipped cream
1-7 ounce container of marshmallow cream

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a medium-sized bowl and mix until blended thorougly.  Can be jazzed up with a touch of food coloring.  Serve with sliced fruit.

Submitter: 
Irlandes
Description: 
This is a wonderfully sweet dip that's great with cut up fruit such as strawberries, pineapple, cantaloupe, kiwi, or anything you like!  Very easy to make, and refreshingly enjoyable!
