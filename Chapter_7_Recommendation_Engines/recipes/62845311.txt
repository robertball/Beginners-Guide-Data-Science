Title:
Chicken w/ cream cheese/tarragon filling & wrapped w/turkey bacon Bundles & Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless skinless chicken breast halves
1/4 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
1 - 8 oz cream cheese
4 green onions, chopped
1/4 t. dried tarragon
8 turkey bacon strips
1/4 c. butter divided
2 c. uncooked long grain rice
2 1/4 c. chicken broth
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 c lemon juice
3 T. fresh dill or 1 T. dill weed
2 t. grated lemon peel
1 T minced fresh parsley

Directions:
Cut each chicken breast horizontally in half; sprinkle with salt & pepper.
In a small bowl, combine cream cheese, onions, and tarragon; spread over chicken.  Roll up from short side; wrap each with a bacon strip.  Place seam side down in a greased 13 in baking pan.  Drizzle with 1 T butter.
Cover, & bake at 375 for 20-25 minutes or until 170 degrees.  Broil 4-6 in. from heat for 2-3 minutes or until lightly browned.
Rice - bring rice, broth and garlic to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 15-18 minutes or until liquid is absorbed and rice is tender.  Stir in lemon juice, dill, lemon peel and remaining butter.
Serve chicken over rice & drizzle with pan juices. garnish with parsley.
