Title:
Baked Eggplant Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium-sized eggplants
1/2 cup milk
1/3 cup flour
2 tbsp. cornmeal
1/4 tsp. salt
any combination of italian seasoning equalling 1 tsp. (ex. garlic powder, basil, oregano, etc.)  I also put in a couple of dashe of paprika.
olive oil

Directions:
Combine dry ingredients.
Preheat oven to 375.
Slice up eggplant and dip in milk, then in flour mixture.
Place on cookie sheet brushed with olive oil.
Spray or brush tops of eggplant slices with olive oil.
Bake in 375 oven for 15-20 min., turn slices, bake 15 min. more.
