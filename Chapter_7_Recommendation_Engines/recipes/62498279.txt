Title:
Pork Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/506381.jpg
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons of cooking oil (corn or vegetable)
6 cups of cooked white rice,fork fluffed & cooled
1 1/2 cups cooked pork, cut in cubes
2 cloves of garlic, diced fine
1" knob of ginger, diced fine
3 stalks of celery, diced small
1/2 small onion, diced small
1 large carrot, diced small
1/2 red pepper, diced small
1/2 cup of finely chopped mushrooms, fried crisp
1 tbsp. soya sauce
3 green onions, chopped small
Sweet Thai Chili Sauce

Directions:
After your rice has cooled, separate the grains by rubbing it with a bit of vegetable or corn oil on your hands. Heat oil in large wok or skillet.  Add pork, ginger and garlic.  Keep it moving with a wooden spoon, but don't smash it.  Add onion, celery and carrot, stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.  Add red pepper and mushrooms and mix in.  Add rice and continue to keep it moving.  Gradually fold rice in and heat thru.  Add soya sauce and stir in.  Remove from heat and sprinkle green onions over top.  Serve hot with Thai Sweet Chili Sauce

Carrie Elizabeth
Description: 
A few leftovers and bits from the fridge turn into a great dinner in less than 45 minutes!
