Title:
Chocolate Peanut Butter Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz cream cheese, softened
1/3 C creamy peanut butter
1/4 t cinnamon
1/4 C chocolate syrup
2 T milk

Directions:
Place all ingred. in mixing bowl and beat until smooth.
Serve with fruits, pretzels, graham crackers, etc.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
A yummy dip for apple slices or banana slices. Also try graham crackers and pretzels
