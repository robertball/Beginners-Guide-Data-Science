Title:
Refrigerator Dill Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 3 to 4 inch long pickling cucumbers
2 cups water
1 1/2 cups white vinegar
1/2 cup white sugar
6 cloves garlic, chopped
4 1/2 teaspoons coarse salt
3 teaspoons pickling spice
1 1/2 teaspoon dill seed
2 sprigs fresh dill weed

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the cucumbers, water, vinegar, sugar, garlic, salt, pickling spice, dill seed. Stir, and let stand at room temperature for 2 hours, until the sugar and salt dissolve.
Remove the cucumbers to three 1 1/2 pint wide mouth jars. Ladle in the liquid from the bowl to cover. Place a sprig of fresh dill into each jar, and seal with lids. Refrigerate for 10 days before eating. Use within 1 month.
