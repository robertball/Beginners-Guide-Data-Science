Title:
Black Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups black beans
1 yellow onion chopped
2 Tbsp butter
2 stalks celery finely chopped
1 bay leaf
1/2 tsp thyme
3 sprigs parsley
8 cups beef broth
Juice of 1/2 a lemon
1/4 cup sherry
2 hard boiled eggs finely chopped
2 Tbsp parsley finely chopped
1/2 lemon finely sliced

Directions:
Soak beans in water for 4 hours and then drain and set aside.
Saute onion in butter until softened.
Add celery and continue to saute for 3 minutes.
Add beans, bay leaf and parsley sprigs.
Puree half the mixture in a blender.
Return pureed soup to saucepan containing balance of soup.
Heat to simmer and add lemon juice and sherry.
Serve hot and garnish with chopped hard boiled eggs and parsley.  Top each bowl of soup with a slice of lemon.

Submitter: 
Bill Alexander
Description: 
This South American soup is good for lunch, or can be served for dinner and teamed with a shrimp salad.
