Title:
Chili Rellenos Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2295918.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (7 ounce) cans whole green chile peppers, drained
8 ounces Mexican Blend shredded cheese
3 eggs, beaten
1 small 5 oz can of evaporated milk
2 tablespoons Bisquick
4 oz. of red or green enchilada sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a 7x10 inch baking dish with cooking spray.
Lay half of the chilies evenly in bottom of baking dish. Sprinkle with half of the cheeses, and cover with remaining chilies. In a bowl, mix together the eggs, milk, and Bisquick, and pour over the top of the chilies.
Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove from oven, pour enchilada sauce evenly over the top, and continue baking another 15 minutes. Sprinkle with remaining Jack and Cheddar cheeses, and serve.
