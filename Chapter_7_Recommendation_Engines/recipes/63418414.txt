Title:
Berry Crumble Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/923285.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups frozen berries (defrosted for about an hour)
4 Tablespoons sugar
2 teaspoons corn starch
1 1/2 cups rolled oats
3/4 cup chopped pecans
1 1/2 cups packed brown sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 cup butter (2 sticks cut into small squares)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In a large bowl, gently toss together berries, white sugar, and corn starch; set aside.
In a separate large bowl, combine flour, oats, pecans, brown sugar, & cinnamon. Cut in butter with a pastry blender or two knives until crumbly.
Grease the bottom of a 9x13 pan and press half of mixture in the bottom.
Bake the bottom crust for 7-8 minutes, then cover with berries. Sprinkle remaining crumble mixture over the berries.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes, or until fruit is bubbly and topping is golden brown.

Description: 
You can use any fruit, but this recipe has a lot of what everyone likes best: the crumble! It's on the bottom and the top and absolutely delicious.
