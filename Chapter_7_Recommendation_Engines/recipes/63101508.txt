Title:
Vanishing Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/827639.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 c white sugar
1/2 c brown sugar
1/2 c oil
1/2 c unsweetened applesauce
1 tsp vanilla extract
4 gala apples (peeled, cored and diced)
-----
2 c AP flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp allspice
1/4 tsp salt
-----
1/4 c brown sugar
1/4 c white sugar
1/4 c AP flour
1/2 c quick oats
1/4 c cold butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F. Spray 13x9" pan with baking spray.
In KA bowl, beat eggs with sugars until well combined. Add oil, applesauce, and vanilla; mix well. Stir in apples.
In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder and soda, cinnamon, allspice and salt. Add dry ingredients to apple mixture and stir to combine. Spread batter evenly into prepared baking pan.
Mix first four topping ingredients in a small bowl. Cut in butter until crumbly. Sprinkle over top of cake.
Bake 40 to 45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool at least 10 minutes before cutting.
