Title:
Sausage Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar junior baby apricot food, 3 lbs. lowfat sausage, 1/2 cup brown sugar.

Directions:
Put bite-sized sausage chunks in crockpot. Mix apricot baby food and brown sugar and pour over meat chunks. Stir well. Cook on low heat 2 hrs. or in oven 45 min. at 350 degrees. Can keep warm in chafing dish or crockpot. Really good!!!!!

Submitter: 
JAZZY-GIRL
Description: 
Quick and easy but even the kids will like it!!!!!!!!!!  Can either be baked in oven, 45 minutes at 350 degrees or two hrs. on low in crockpot.
