Title:
Almond Wafer Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Graham wafers
1 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup flaked almonds
½ tsp. Watkins Caramel Extract (optional)

Directions:
Spray cookie sheet with Pam and line with graham wafers. Cover with almonds.
Melt butter and brown sugar over medium heat. When melted, cook for 5 minutes (watch it doesn’t burn).
Spoon mixture over wafers.
Bake at 325 for 10-12 minutes. It will bubble—watch the bottom doesn’t burn.
Cut when slightly cool.
Let cool and remove.
