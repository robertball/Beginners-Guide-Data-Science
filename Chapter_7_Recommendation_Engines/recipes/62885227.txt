Title:
Nut Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups flour
1 cup walnuts, chopped fine
1 egg
1 cup sugar
1 cup milk
2 tsp baking powder
3/4 cup raisins or dates
dash salt

Directions:
1. Add baking powder and nuts to the flour in a medium sized bowl. Reserve.
2. Put eggs, salt and sugar into separate bowl and mix well. Add milk, mix well.
3. Add flour mixture and stir until smooth. Add raisins or dates.
4. Let rise 30 minutes. Pour into greased loaf pan and bake at 375Â° for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Lynn Pennec
Description: 
My grandmother's recipe, I can still remember eating this bread at Virngina Beach, watching the waves roll in.
