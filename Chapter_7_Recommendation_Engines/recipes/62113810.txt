Title:
Creamy Onion Gravy For Hamburger Steaks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons olive oil
1 medium onion sliced thin
1/4 cup all purpose flour
salt & pepper to taste
1 cup beef broth
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
In a small skillet add only 1 tablespoon of olive oil and turn heat up to medium-high.
Saute onions for a few minutes until tender.
Remove onions and set aside.
Place pan back onto burner and add 3 tablespons olive oil.
When the oil is hot, add 1/4 cup of all purpose flour, stir.
Continue stirring and add salt and pepper.
Slowly, little by little add the beef broth while continuing to stir.
Slowly add the milk while continuing to stir.
Bring to a boil to thicken. Add the sauted onions and stir.
Remove from heat and place into a small serving dish.
Spoon over hamburger steaks.

Submitter: 
Janine
Description: 
This gravy will compliment your hamburger steaks nicely. You can also add mushrooms if you like.
