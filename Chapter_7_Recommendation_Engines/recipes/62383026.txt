Title:
Big Daddy's Caribbean Island Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup ice
2 cups pineapple juice
1/2 cup ginger ale
3 tablespoons stranded coconut
1 scoop of Mila
1 tablespoon white sugar

Directions:
Combine the ice, pineapple juice, ginger ale, coconut, Mila, and sugar in a blender; blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
BIG DADDY'S
Description: 
This Caribbean refreshing smoothie will make you feel like you're strolling along the Island beaches in the Caribbean.Modified from: Island Smoothie
