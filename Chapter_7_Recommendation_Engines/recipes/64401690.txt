Title:
Maple Chipotle Barbeque Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 6-oz. can Tomato Paste
4 oz. Maple Syrup, pure
2 oz. Molasses, Barbados, unsulphured
2 oz. Balsamic Vinegar
2 oz. Soy Sauce
1 Chipotle Peppers, stems removed

Directions:
Place all the ingredients in a blender and run at high speed until all the ingredients are thoroughly blended together.
This sauce will keep in the refrigerator in a tightly sealed jar for a week or more.
Enjoy!
