Title:
Fancy Fast Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 boneless chicken breasts
6 slices Swiss cheese
1/4 pound fresh mushrooms
1 can cr of chicken soup
1/2 c dry white wine
2 c herbed stuffing mix
1 stick butter, melted

Directions:
Place chicken in greased 9x13 baking dish.
Top each piece with a slice of Swiss cheese.
Arrange mushrooms over cheese.
Mix soup and wine and pour over chicken.
Sprinkle stuffing mix over top.
Drizzle with melted butter.
