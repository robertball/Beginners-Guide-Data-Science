Title:
Peanut Butter and Bannana Ice Cream Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Filling:
1 already prepared chocolate cookie pie crust.
1 quart vanilla ice cream
1/2 Cup creamy peanut butter at room temp.
1/2 Cup course chopped unsalted peanuts
1 Cup Chopped Peanut Butter cookies (nutter butter)
1/3 Cup peanut butter chips
2 Tbs semi-sweet chocolate chips
Topping:
1 8 oz tub whip topping
1 bananna thinly sliced
1 jar caramel ice cream topping

Directions:
In chilled bowl mix ice cream and peanut butter together, stir in peanuts, 1/2 of cookies and chips. Spread in pie crust and top with remaining cookies.  Cover and freeze for at least 4 hrs.
When ready to serve top with whip topping and banannas drizzle with caramel.
