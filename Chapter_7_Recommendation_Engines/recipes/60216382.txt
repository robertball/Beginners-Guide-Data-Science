Title:
Cream Cheese Brownie Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pie crust for bottom
1 8 oz. cream cheese, softened
3 Tbsp. sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1 egg
1 Hot Fudge Brownie mix
1/4 C. oil
2 eggs
1 Tbsp. water
1/2 C. chopped pecans (opt.)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Place crust in a 10" pie plate.
Mix together cheese, sugar, vanilla and water.
Mix together mix, oil, eggs and water.
Reserve Hot fudge packet.
Pour 1/2 of brownie mix into the crust.
Carefully spread the cheese mixture over bottom layer.
Spoon on remaining brownie mixture.
Sprinkle chopped pecans on top.
Bake @ 350&#0176; for 35 minutes, covering edge of crust w/ foil after 20 minutes.
Remove and cool 3 hours.
Place fudge packet in bowl of HOT water for several minutes to soften.
Clip corner and drizzle over top of pie.
Reserve remaining topping to drizzle over 1 scoop of ice cream on each slice of pie.
