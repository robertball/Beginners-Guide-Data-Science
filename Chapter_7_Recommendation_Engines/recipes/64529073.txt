Title:
Little Smokies Crockpot Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages Little Smokies or cocktail weiners
1 12-ounce bottle chili sauce
1 cup grape jelly

Directions:
Combine the chili sauce, grape jelly, and Little Smokies in a slow cooker.
Cover and cook on LOW for 4 to 6 hours.
