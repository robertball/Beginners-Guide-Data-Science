Title:
Grandma's Ham Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg noodles
1-2 cups of ham cut up
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can of carnation milk
1 small onion
1/3 lb of Velvetta Cheese cut up
crushed potatoe chips

Directions:
Cook noodles and drain. Add all other ingredients and put into a 9x13 pan. Bake 1 hour. Sprinkle crushed potato chips on top and bake the last 5 minutes.
