Title:
Garbanzo Bean, Wheat Berry and Vegetable Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup dried wheat berries (often located in bulk food sections)
1 15 oz can garbanzo beans (chick peas)
2 cups broccoli cut into small florets
1 cup cauliflower cut into small florets
3/4 cup shredded carrot
1 small red bell pepper, finely diced
1 small red onion, finely diced
2 celery stalks, finely diced
1 small zucchini, finely diced or shredded
1/2 cup olive oil
3 tbsps white wine vinegar
1 1/2 tbsps minced fresh garlic
1 tsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
1 tsp Dijon mustard
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
3/4 tsp dried oregano
3/4 tsp dried basil
2 tbsp. fresh parsley, finely chopped
1/2 tsp dried dill

Directions:
1. Rinse wheat berries picking out and discarding stones.  Bring 3 cups of lightly salted water to a boil and stir in wheat berries.  Cover and cook on low for 45 - 60 minutes until soft.  Drain and allow to cool down.
2. Drain and rinse garbanzo beans and place in large bowl.  Add wheat berries when cool and stir in prepared fresh vegetables.
3. Prepare the dressing with the remaining ingredients by whisking together in a separate bowl until well blended .
4. Gently stir dressing into salad and mix well.
5. Cover and refrigerate for several hours or overnight
