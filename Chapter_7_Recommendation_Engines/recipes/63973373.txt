Title:
Apple Pudding Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1/2 cup margarine
2 eggs
2 cups flour
4 medium apples, diced
1 teaspoon nutmeg
1 teaspoon cinnamon
2 teaspoons baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons vanilla
2 cups nuts, chopped
SAUCE:
1/2 cup cream
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup sugar
1 square butter

Directions:
Put together like a cake.
Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes in 9 x 13 pan.
Sauce:  Heat and boil ingredients for a few minutes then pour over pudding while hot.  Serve with whipping cream.

Submitter: 
Karen Monson
Description: 
My mom made this all the time and as a teenager was my favorite and is still one of my favorite today.
