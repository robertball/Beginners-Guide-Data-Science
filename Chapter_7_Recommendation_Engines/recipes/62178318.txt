Title:
Elise Lebkuchen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
40 oblaten (90 mm)
2 eggs
1 c sugar
2 1/2 tsp vanilla sugar (or 1/2tsp of vanilla extract)
1/2 tsp of cloves
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 bottle rum flavoring essence
1-2 drops of lemon flavoring essence
1/2 c candied lemon or orange peel (very finely diced)
1 c almonds (ground)
1 tsp baking powder
1 c hazelnuts (ground)
WHITE ICING:
1 1/2 c powder sugar
1-2 tbsp hot water
or
DARK ICING:
1 c powder sugar
2 tbsp cocoa
1-2 tbsp hot water
(opt 1 pat of butter melted)

Directions:
beat eggs until frothy, gradually add sugar and vanilla sugar (extract). Continue beating until the mixture is thick aqnd creamy.
Mix the baking powder with the almonds. Mix the flavorings and spices, almonds, and the hazelnuts.
Place a heaped tsp on each oblaten; smotth with a knife dipped in water, and lay on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
Preheat oven to 275 degree oven, bake for 25-35 mins or untill edges are a golden brown.
ICING:
Sift the icing sugar and blend with as much of the water as will give a good consistancy.
DARK ICING:
Sift together the powder sugar and cocoa and blend with as much of the water as will give a good coating. Add the melted butter.
Coat the spice cookies with wither the white or dark icing while they are still hot.
Let cool and dry completley before packing in a airtight container. Will keep for 1 week.

Submitter: 
Heide F
Description: 
A spicey cookie on a rice wafer, can be covered in a chocolate glasur or in a powder sugar glasur. Very traditional for a german christmas.
