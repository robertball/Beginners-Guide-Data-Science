Title:
Mexican Taco Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb.ground beef
1 envelope favorite Taco seasoning
half onion,chopped
half bell pepper,chopped
1 reg.jar chunky Salsa,mild or medium to taste
16 oz grated taco cheese
salt and pepper to taste
5 whole eggs,beaten
2 cups milk
1 uncooked pie crust

Directions:
Brown and drain ground beef. Add Taco seasoning according to directions.In large mixing bowl,beat the eggs and add milk,stir to combine.Add jar of Salsa,chopped bell pepper,onion and Taco cheese.Lightly salt and pepper. Stir to combine.Place pie crust in 9 in deep dish pie plate and add seasoned ground beef. Pour egg-milk mixture over beef in pie crust. Bake @ 400 F. oven for 45-60 mins or until set ( no jiggle!).Cool 10 mins and cut into pie slices. Serve with sour cream and enjoy !
