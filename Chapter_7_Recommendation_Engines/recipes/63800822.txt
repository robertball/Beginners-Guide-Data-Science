Title:
Black Bean Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients:
•1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
•2 green onions
•1/4 cup cilantro
•1 tablespoon tahini
•1 clove garlic
•1 teaspoon lime juice
•1 teaspoon ground cumin
•1 teaspoon Sriracha sauce
•1/4 teaspoon salt
•Dash of crushed red pepper flakes
•2 tablespoons olive oil, plus more as needed

Directions:
1.Combine beans, green onions, cilantro, tahini, garlic, lime juice, cumin, Sriracha, salt and red pepper flakes in a food processor. Pulse a few times to bring the ingredients together.
2.Add olive oil and process until smooth. Add more olive oil to achieve your desired consistency.
3.Serve with veggies or pita chips.
