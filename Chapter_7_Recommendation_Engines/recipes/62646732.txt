Title:
Whole Wheat Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/585053.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup oats
1/2 cup Orange Juice
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 cups Whole Wheat Flour
2 Tbsp Wheat Germ
1/2 cup sugar
1 1/4 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 stick butter, melted
1 egg
3/4 cup cinnamon applesauce
2 cups frozen or fresh blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
In a small bowl combine the Orange juice, oats, and vanilla
In a large bowl, combine the whole wheat flour, wheat germ, sugar, baking powder, salt, baking soda and mix. In another bowl, melt the butter and beat in the egg until combined, add to the flour mixture. Add in the applesauce and stir until combined.
Mix in the oat mixture with the flour mixture and then fold in the blueberries.
Place in muffin cups and bake for 30-35 minutes.
