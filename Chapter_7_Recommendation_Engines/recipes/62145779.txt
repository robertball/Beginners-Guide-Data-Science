Title:
Super Simple Toffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of pecans or walnuts
1 cup of packed brown sugar
3/4 cup of soft butter
2/3 cup of semi-sweet or milk chocolate chips
1/3 of extra nuts (optional topping)

Directions:
Grease a 9" square pan with butter.
Spread the cup of nuts in a single layer over bottom of pan and set aside.
Boil sugar and butter in small saucepan for approximately 6-8 mins. or until it reaches 255-260 degrees on a candy thermometer.
Pour boiled sugar and butter mixture over nuts and sprinkle with chocolate chips and extra nuts.
Chill in refridgerator until hard enough break into bite size pieces.
