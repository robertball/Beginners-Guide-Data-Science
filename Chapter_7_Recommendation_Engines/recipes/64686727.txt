Title:
Cheesy Onion Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large sweet yellow onion, sliced (2 cups)
5-8 garlic cloves, minced
1 c avocado mayo
1 c sour cream
1 c shredded parmesan cheese
1 c shredded swiss cheese
1-2 c mixed swiss and parmesan cheese
salt
splash of worcestershire sauce
splash of hot sauce if desired

Directions:
preheat oven to 375
combine onion, garlic, sour cream, mayo, 1 c parmesan, 1 c  swiss cheese, worcestershire, salt to taste
pour into olive oiled 9x12 baking dish
sprinkle swiss/parmesan cheese on top
bake 375 for 25-35 min or until onions tender and cheese browned
