Title:
Rachael's Crack Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1876948.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can Shoepeg corn
1 can black beans, rinsed
1 cup feta
1/8 cup olive oil
1/8 cup rice vinegar
1/8 cup sugar
1/4 cup chopped green onions
Salt to taste

Directions:
Mix all together and refrigerate until ready to serve.  Serve with Scoops tortilla chips or pita chips
