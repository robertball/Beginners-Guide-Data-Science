Title:
Alpha Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Box Yellow cake mix.
1 Stick Butter.
2 Eggs.
1 Can Sweetened Condensed Milk.
1 Cup Chocolate Chips.

Directions:
1] Melt the butter and blend with eggs before adding to the cake mix. 2] Melt Chocolate Chips and Sweetened Condensed Milk together. 3] Spray 9x13 pan with non-stick spray. 4] Spread half of the cake mixture into cake pan. 5] Spread chocolate mixture over cake mixture. 6] Dot remaining cake mixture over chocolate. 7] Bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes.
