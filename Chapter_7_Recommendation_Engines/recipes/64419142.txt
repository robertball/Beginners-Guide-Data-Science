Title:
Grilled Nectarine Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 nectarines, halved and pitted
olive oil for drizzling
2 tsp honey
2 tsp mustard
2 tsp white wine vinegar
salt and freshly ground pepper
1/2 cup slivered almonds
6 oz spring sald mix2 oz stilton cheese

Directions:
heat a grill pan over medium heat.
drizzle the nectarine  halves with a little olive oil.  grill cut-side down for 2-3 minutes, turning midway to achieve lovely grill marks.  set aside
in a bowl, whisk together honey, dijon and vinegar.  whisk in the olive oil to emulsify.  add some salt and pepper and mix
toast the slivered almonds in a small skillet over med-high heat until just turning golden brown
in a large bowl, toss the salad greens with enough of the salad dressing to lightly coat - you won't use all of the dressing, save to have on another salad later - as almonds
divide the salad among 4 plates, place a nectarine half on each serving.  drizzle the nectarines with a little more dressing, with a fork cut the blue cheese into chunks and distribute among plates
