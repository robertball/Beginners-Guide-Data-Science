Title:
Ricotta Bruschetta with Olives Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 ½ oz/100g Ricotta Cheese
1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 clove Garlic (to taste) - finely chopped
4tbsp Spring Onion - finely chopped
2oz/60g green Olives - pitted and finely chopped
2 (about 120g/4oz each) fresh Plum Tomatoes - peeled, seeded and chopped
1tbsp fresh Parsley - finely chopped
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
Ciabatta/Chabatta Bread - sliced
Garnish
6 thin slices Prosciutto/Salami/Ham - cut into ½ in pieces.
Basil Leaves - torn

Directions:
On grill, toast bread slices on both sides until lightly browned;
Cut toasted slices to around 2in oblongs/squares
Combine remaining ingredients in bowl and season to taste;
Spoon the cheese mixture on each toast oblong/square;
Place slice of prosciutto or other on top each oblong/square and garnish with basil leaves;
Serve

Description: 
A tasty, full flavoured twist on an Italian classic. Fit for any occasion.
