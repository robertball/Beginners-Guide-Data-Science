Title:
Mango Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups gluten-free graham cracker crumbs
1/2 cup of sugar or 1/4 cup of Splenda for Baking
6 Tbsp. unsalted butter, melted
Frozen mango (thawed)
3 (8oz.) packs of cream cheese, room temp.
1 1/4 cups of sugar or 3/4 cups of Splenda for baking
2 tsp vanilla extract
4 large eggs

Directions:
Preheat over to 325 degrees. Butter a 9-in springform pan and the sides.
Stir cracker crumbs and sugar.
Add melted butter and stir til evenly moistened.
Press crumb mixture onto the bottom and up the sides of the prepared pan.
Bake until crust is set (about 10-12 mins.) Let cool.
Puree mangoes until smooth. Save only 3 cups of mango puree. The rest you can store.
Beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla till smooth.
Add eggs (1 at a time).
Add 3 cups of mango puree and beat until well blended.
Pour filling into pan.
Bake until set (about 1 hr 25 min.) Let it cool for about 1hr.
Refrigerate overnight.

Submitter: 
Chef Kiki
Description: 
I've made this cheesecake and it vanished no time flat at the latest dinner party. FYI- keep chilled until you're ready to serve
