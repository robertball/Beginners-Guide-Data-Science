Title:
Raisin Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of softened butter
1 2/3 cups of sugar
1 2/3 cups brown sugar
4 Eggs
3 tsp vanilla extract
4 cups all purpose flour
5 cup of oats
2 tsp baking powder
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 tsp ground cinnamon
4 cups semisweet chocolate chips
2 cups of raisins

Directions:
Cook raisins in a sauce pan with water until they are soft.
In a bowl cream butter and sugars together.
In a separate large bowl add eggs, vanilla, and the rest of the dry ingredients.
Gradually add 'creamed' mixture into dry mixture and mix well. (Mixer is strongly suggested)
Then stir in the chocolate chips and raisins.
When well mixed spoon dough on a cookie sheet and bake at 350 degrees for 12-14 minutes

Submitter: 
Sidnie Anderson
Description: 
A delicious mound of oatmeal, chocolate chips, and raisins in a delicious cookie form.
