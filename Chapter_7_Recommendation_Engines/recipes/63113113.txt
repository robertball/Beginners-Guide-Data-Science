Title:
Fiber One Cranberry Almond Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/795803.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1 cup coarsely crushed Fiber One bran cereal
1 cup Splenda baking mix (half Splenda/half suger)
3 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup egg whites
3/4 cup milk
1/2 cup strawberry (or other flavor) Greek yogurt
1/2 tsp lemon zest
1 cup fresh, coarsely chopped
2 oz (1/3 cup) slivered almonds

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the dry ingredients. In another bowl, beat the eggs, milk, yogurt. Stir into dry ingredients just until moistened. Fold in cranberries and almonds.
2. Fill paper-lined muffin cups two-thirds full. Bake at 400 degrees F for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean and tops are golden brown.

Submitter: 
Angie Fuller
Description: 
You'd never know these moist muffins eliminate almost all the fat using Greek yogurt, as well as having a healthy dose of Fiber too!
