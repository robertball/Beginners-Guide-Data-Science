Title:
Creamy Buttermilk Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/947660.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup sour cream
1/3 cup buttermilk
3 tablespoons lemon juice or rice wine vinegar
1 large garlic clove, minced
Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Mix all ingredients until well-blended.

Submitter: 
Ben S.
Description: 
A delicious, versatile dressing for pasta or green salads. Easily made in advance for use just before service.
