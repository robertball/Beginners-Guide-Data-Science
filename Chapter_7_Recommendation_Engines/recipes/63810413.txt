Title:
Hummus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cloves garlic
2 cups canned chick peas, drained, liquid reserved
1 1/2 t salt
1/3 cup tahini
juice of 2 lemons
2 T liquid from chick peas
8-10 dashes hot sauce

Directions:
Add all ingredients to a food processor and process until the hummus is coarsely pureed.

Submitter: 
MATTOON250
Description: 
The best hummus recipe! Be sure to use fresh squeezed lemon juice.
