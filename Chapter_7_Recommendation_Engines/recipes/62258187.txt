Title:
Spaghetti With Peanut Butter Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
2/3 cup hot water
3 tablespoon light soy sauce
1 clove crushed garlic
1/3 cup milk
3 teaspoon sesame oil
1 dash chili sauce
12 ounces spaghetti
o

Directions:
Place the peanut butter in a small glass, and add the hot water; stir with a fork until smooth.  Mix in soy sauce, garlic, milk, sesame oil, chili sauce until smooth.  Set aside for 1-2 hours.
Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water until done.  Drain well.
Place the drained pasta in a bowl.  Stir the reserved peanut butter mixture together again; add to the cooked pasta.  Toss all together, and garnish with cilantro.
