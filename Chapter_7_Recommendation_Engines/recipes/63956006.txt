Title:
Spicy Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1097524.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package of Philadelphia Cream Cheese,
3 Breasts of Chicken (season chicken with Pepper, Mrs. Dashs Red Hot, and Crushed Red Pepper Flakes),
1 cup of Light Ranch Dressing,
1/2 cup of Shredded Cheddar Cheese,
1 cup of Franks Red Hot Hot Sauce,
as desired Cholula (optional).
To dip: Baby Carrots, Celery, Tortilla Chips, Pita Chips, Baggette Bread

Directions:
Cut chicken into small cubes and put in pan on stove (season with Mrs. Dash, Pepper and Crushed Red Pepper Flakes).  Cook until chicken is done
Cut Chicken into smaller pieces
Heat oven to 350
Combine all ingredients abve in a bowl and mix together
Spray pan (makes about 1 Qt) and add misture to pan
Put in oven for 20 minutes (stir in middle if desired.. top can get a little crusty if you do not).
Serve with dip items listed above and Enjoy!
