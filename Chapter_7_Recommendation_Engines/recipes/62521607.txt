Title:
Chicken and Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box dried cheese sliced potaotes (au gratin....)
1 cup vegies of choice
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
salt and pepper

Directions:
spray cake pan with cooking spray (pam)
mix potatoes as per box instructions
add vegies and mix
pour into pan, place chicken on top
salt and pepper
cover with foil
cook 45 min at 375 or until chicken and potatoes done
