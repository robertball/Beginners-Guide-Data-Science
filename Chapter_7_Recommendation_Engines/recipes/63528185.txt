Title:
Lean and Green Stuffed Portabello Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb (16 oz) portabella mushroom caps
2 c. shredded moderate-fat mozzarella cheese
1/2 c. fresh chopped tomato
1/2 tsp. fresh rosemary, finely chopped OR 1/8 tsp. dried rosemary
2 tsp fresh lemon juice
2 tsp fresh cilantro, chopped
1 tsp low-sodium soy sauce
1 tsp olive oil, divided
1/8 tsp freshly ground black pepper
1 clove garlic, minced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350* or outdoor grill to medium-high heat.
- In a medium-sized bowl, combine tomatoes and mozzarella; toss with 1/2 tsp of olive oil, rosemary, black pepper, and garlic.
- Remove stems from mushroom caps and discard. Scoop out interior.
- In a small bowl, mix 1/2 tsp olive oil, lemon juice, and soy sauce. Using a pastry brush, apply soy sauce mix on both sides of mushroom caps.
- IN THE OVEN: Bake caps until soft, then divide tomato and cheese mix into caps. Cook an additional 2 minutes, or until cheese is melted.
- ON THE GRILL: Once warm, grill mushrooms, starting stem side down, 5 minutes on each side or until soft. Divide tomato and cheese mix into each cap. Cover and grill ab 10 minutes or until cheese is melted. (For easy clean-up, place a piece of aluminum foil directly on grill; lay mushroom caps on foil.)
- Garnish w/cilantro; divide into two portions and serve.
