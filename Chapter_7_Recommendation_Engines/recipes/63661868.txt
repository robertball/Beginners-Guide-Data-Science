Title:
Blistered Haricots Verts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1017405.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. fresh Haricots Verts (French Green Beans)
1 tsp. Olive Oil
1/4 tsp. Kosher or Sea salt

Directions:
Trim and rinse beans.
Pat dry.
Put beans in medium saut√© pan and cook on medium high heat for about 5 minutes tossing every minute.
Add Olive Oil and continue cooking tossing beans every minute till tender and evenly blistered, about another 5 minutes.
Sprinkle with salt.

Submitter:

Description: 
We enjoy the uncomplicated taste of vegetables. 'Blistering' is an easy and healthy way to prepare French Green Beans. Ideal to serve with a variety of main dishes and easy enough to add additional seasonings if you want to change up the taste.
