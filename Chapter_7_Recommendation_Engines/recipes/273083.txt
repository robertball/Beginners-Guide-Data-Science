Title:
Oreo® S'mores Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
16 chocolate sandwich cookies (such as Oreo®)
16 marshmallows
long wooden skewers

Directions:
Twist open each cookie.
Thread marshmallows onto wooden skewers. Hold skewers over a gas stove flame until marshmallows are toasted. Sandwich marshmallows between cookie halves.

Submitter: 
MrsMickey99203
Description: 
This recipe was developed because my daughter is lactose intolerant. It is very simple and can be prepared over a campfire or kitchen stove. Enjoy!
