Title:
Kale and Onion Mini Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1109937.jpg
Ingredients:
1 C. Kale, finely chopped and dried
1/2 C. Onion, finely chopped
2 Eggs
1/2 C. Butter
2 Tbsp. Processed goat cheese
2 1/2 C. All purpose flour
1 C. Whole wheat pastry flour
1 tsp. Salt
2 Tbsp. Granulated sugar
1 Tbsp. Caraway seeds
2 tsp. Baking powder
1 tsp. Baking soda

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees Fahrenheit to dry kale
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit to bake biscuits
On a large cutting board chop Kale and onion finely
Spread chopped Kale out on a large cookie sheet and place in oven for 10 to 15 minutes until it is just dried. Remove from oven and set aside to cool
In a large mixing bowl combine butter, sugar, goat cheese, eggs, kale and onion together. Mix all ingredients well
Blend flour, b. powder, b. soda, salt and caraway seeds in a small bowl.
Add dry ingredients to wet mixture and mix until dough comes away from the sides of the bowl.
Chill dough for a 1/2 hr and then roll out on a lightly floured surface to a thickness of a 1/2 inch and then cut out rounds with a cookie cutter or small lid
Place rounds on a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper
Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes until lightly browned

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very light biscuit with tasty caraway seeds to make a good accompaniment for soups and stews at dinner time.
