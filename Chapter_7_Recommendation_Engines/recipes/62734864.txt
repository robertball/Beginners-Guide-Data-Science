Title:
Braised Sirloin Tips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbls Butter
2 pounds beef sirloin tips, cut into thin slices
1 can beef consomme
1/3 cup cranberry cocktail or red wine
2 tbls soy sauce
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 tsp. onion salt
2 tbls cornstarch
1/4 cup water
4 cups rice, cooked

Directions:
Melt butter in skillet, brown meat on all sides, Stir in consomme, wine, soy sauce, garlic and onion salt.  Heat to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer 1 hour or until tender.  Blend cornstarch and water, stir gradually into meat and cook, stirring constantly until mixture thickens and boils.  Serve over rice.
