Title:
Whole Wheat Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. all purpose flour
1 c. whole wheat flour
2 tsp. baking powder
2 Tbsp. white sugar
1 tsp. salt
2 cups milk
2 eggs
2 Tbsp. vegetable oil
1 tsp. vanilla
1/8 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir together flour, baking powder, sugar, salt and cinnamon. Whisk in milk, eggs, vanilla and oil; mix well.
Spray preheated waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray. Pour mix onto hot waffle iron. Cook until golden brown.
