Title:
Sweet and sour meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/399039.jpg
Ingredients:
1 20 oz. can unsweetened pineapple chunks
1 egg
1 cup breadcrumbs
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. blk. pepper
1 1/2 lbs. ground turkey or beef
1 Tbsp. vegetable oil
2 lg. green peppers or 1 green, 1 red pepper.  Cut into 1 inch pieces
1 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup sugar
3 Tbls. cornstarch
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1 Tbls. soy sauce

Directions:
Drain pineapple reserving 1/2 cup juice.  Set aside.
In medium bowl, combine egg, bread crumbs, garlic, salt and pepper.  Crumble ground turkey or beef over mixture.  Mix well.  Shape into approx. 40 balls.
In a large non-stick skillet, brown meatballs in oil about 8 minutes. Drain if neccessary.  Add green peppers, chicken broth and pineapple to meatballs.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer uncovered 5-7 minutes.
Meanwhile, combine sugar and cornstarch in small bowl.  Stir in vinegar, soy sauce and reserved pineapple juice until smooth.  Add to meatballs.  Return to boil stirring constantly.  Cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.
