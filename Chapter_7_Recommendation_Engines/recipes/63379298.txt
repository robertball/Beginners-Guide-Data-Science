Title:
Chinese Shrimp and Tofu Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon coconut oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger root, minced
6 ounces raw small shrimp, shelled and deveined
1 quart chicken stock
8 ounces tofu, diced small
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1 tablespoon cornstarch

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large saucepan or wok over high heat.  Cook the garlic and ginger until fragrant and lightly browned.  Stir in the shrimp, and stir fry until cooked, then remove and set aside.  Pour in the chicken stock and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to medium, add the tofu, season with salt and pepper, then return to a simmer.  Mix the cornstarch with a little water to form a thin paste.  Stir the cornstarch into the soup and continue to simmer until clear and thickened, about 1 minute.  Stir the shrimp back into the soup and serve.
