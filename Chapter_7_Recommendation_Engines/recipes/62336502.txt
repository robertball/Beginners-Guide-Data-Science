Title:
Creamy Parmesan Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb broccoli florets
2 tbsp. olive oil
3/4 c. grated parmesan cheese
1/2 c. chopped green onions (if desired)
1/2 c. sour cream
1/4 c. mayo or salad dressing
2  tbsp. milk
1/8 tsp. pepper
1/4 c. pine nuts

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Cool broccoli as directed and drain.  Place in ungreased 9 x 13 baking dish.  Drizzle with oil.  Sprinkle with 1/4 c. of the cheese and toss to coat.
In bowl combine remaining 1/2 c. cheese and all remaining ingredients except pine nuts, mix well and spoon over broccoli.
Bake for 18-20 minutes until sauce begins to bubble and brown.  Sprinkle with pine nuts.
