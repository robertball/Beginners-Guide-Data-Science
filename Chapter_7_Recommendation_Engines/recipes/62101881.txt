Title:
Healthy Peanut Butter Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 cup white flour
1/2 cup wheat germ
1/2 cup oat bran
1/2 cup soy flour
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup plain yogurt
1 cup peanut butter (chunky)
1 tablespoon honey
1/2 cup milk
1 mashed banana
2 eggs

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
Mix the whole wheat flour, white flour, wheat germ, oat bran, soy flour, salt and baking soda in a bowl and set aside.
Mash the banana and mix together with the eggs.  Then mix in the yogurt, peanut butter, honey, and milk.
Add the dry ingredients to the second bowl and mix thoroughly.
Pour the batter into two greased mini loaf pans and bake for 40-45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sara
Description: 
This is a wonderful, very yummy peanutty bread.  Also extremely healthy and perfect for any midnight or midday cravings.  This recipe is very flexible in case you lack any of the ingredients or want to make substitutions.
