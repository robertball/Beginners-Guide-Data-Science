Title:
Spicy Andouille Sausage pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 red tomatoes (sliced into 8ths)
3 yellow tomatoes (sliced into 8ths)
1 onion, chopped
2 tbsp minced garlic
2 large portabella mushrooms, sliced thick
1 cup of spinach leaves
1lb Andouille Sausage, sliced thin on the diagonal
3 tbsp olive oil
1 lb angel hair pasta

Directions:
Boil water for pasta
In a large skillet, saute garlic and onion over medium heat until translucent
Add sausage and cover for 5min
Cook and drain pasta
Add tomatoes and mushrooms to the skillet, cook until they soften (8-10min)
Turn off heat, stir in spinach leaves and serve immediately over pasta.

Submitter: 
IamPatSajak
Description: 
This pasta absorbs the flavor of the sausage, tastes mild until you bite the spicy sausage.
