Title:
Grilled Cajun Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
*1/2 cup Olive oil
*3 Tbls. Dijon mustard
*2 tspn.Cajun spices
*1 Chipotle pepper minced**
*1 tspn. Adobo sauce**
*2 lbs. Jumbo shrimp

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients in a small food processor, or by hand, until smooth. Marinate shrimp in sauce for at least an hour and grill 3 to 4 minutes per side. This also works well with chicken.
Note** ~ Chipotle peppers in adobo sauce can be found in most supermarkets.

Submitter: 
luv2cook
Description: 
A quick and easy shrimp marinade with a fantastic smokey flavor and just the right amount of heat. Sure to become one of your BBQ favorites. Enjoy!
