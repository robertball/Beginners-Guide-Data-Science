Title:
Spiced Fruit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz. can pineapple chunks, drained
1 15 oz. can pear halves or slices, drained
1 15 oz. can peach halves or slices, drained
1 15 oz. can apricot halves, drained
1 small jar maraschino cherries, drained and stems removed
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
4 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 &#0176;
Layer the fruit in a greased oval baking dish
In a small sauce pan, combine the brown sugar, spices and butter.
Heat, stirring, until the brown sugar is completely dissolved.
Pour syrup over fruit and mix gently being careful not to break the fruit.
Bake uncovered for 30 minutes or until thoroughly heated and mixture bubbles.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
Tasty fruit dish that makes an attractive side for holiday meals.  Or serve with sugar or ginger cookies for dessert.
