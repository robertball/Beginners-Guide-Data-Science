Title:
Easy Baked Chicken Thighs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Chicken Thighs
4 Teaspoon of Garlic Powder
4 Teaspoon of Onion Flakes

Directions:
1. Put one teaspoon of both the garlic powder and onion flakes on each of the chicken thighs
2. Bake for 30 minutes in a preheated oven
3. You can season it with other spices. I do mine with cayenne powder and black pepper too.

Submitter:

Description: 
As a college student, this is an easy recipe that requires minimum preparation work!
