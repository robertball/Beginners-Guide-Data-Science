Title:
Chalupa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lb pork roast
1 lb dry pinto beans
2 garlic cloves
1 Tbsp cumen
1 Tbsp dried oregano
2 Tbsp chili powder
1 Tbsp salt
4 oz can chopped green chiles
water

Directions:
Cover beans with water and soak overnight in a crockpot
In AM remove beans (reserving water) and put roast in the bottom of the crock pot.
Add in the rest of the ingredients including the beans and water.
Cook in slow cooker 6-8 hours. Remove meat, shred with two forks and return to cooker to cook additional hour.
Serve on a bed of lettuce and top with corn chips, shredded cheese, chopped tomatoes, onions etc...
