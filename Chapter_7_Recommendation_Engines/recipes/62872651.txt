Title:
Cilantro-Lime Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/690177.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
2 tablespoons sugar
3 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 teaspoons finely chopped fresh cilantro
1 garlic clove, minced
1 shallot, minced
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon coarsely ground pepper

Directions:
Whisk together all ingredients except olive oil. Slowly drizzle in olive oil, whisking constantly.

Submitter: 
lucylove
Description: 
Bright and fresh, use it to dress any fish or shrimp salad, or pair with a Mexican entree, like Enchiladas. Also an excellent chicken marinade. Found on myrecipes.com.
