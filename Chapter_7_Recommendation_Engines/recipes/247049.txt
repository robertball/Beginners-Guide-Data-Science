Title:
Pickletini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3624181.jpg
Ingredients:
ice
2 1/2 fluid ounces premium vodka
1 fluid ounce pickle juice
3 pickle slices (optional)

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice; add vodka and pickle juice. Cover and shake until the outside of shaker has frosted. Strain into a martini glass and garnish with pickle slices on a swizzle stick.

Submitter: 
acpazian
Description: 
Like a dirty martini, but with pickle juice. If you like pickles, you'll love this!
