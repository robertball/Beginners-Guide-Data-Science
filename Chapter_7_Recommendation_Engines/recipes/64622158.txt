Title:
Pork Loin with Onion Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5lb Pork Loin
1 large onion
11/4 flour
1 tbsp seasoned salt
1 tbsp garlic powder
1 tsp pepper
½ cp canola oil
2 cubes beef bouillon
½ cp water

Directions:
Mix together flour, seasoned salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
Dredge pork loin in flour mixture.
Set aside.
Slice onion thinly.
Heat oil in a frying pan on high until hot, lower to medium heat and sear pork loin on all sides.
Set slow cooker on high and place seared meat inside.
Saute’ onions 2- 3 minutes until heated through.
Add the onions to the meat in the slow cooker.
Pour the water into pan and deglaze on low heat.
Pour drippings into the slow cooker, add bouillon cubes.
Cook on high heat for 2 hours, then reduce to medium heat for 1 ½ hours or until meat is tender.

Submitter: 
Karen Hill Douglass
Description: 
Slow cooker recipe, easy enough for a weeknight and flavorful enough for a Sunday dinner.
