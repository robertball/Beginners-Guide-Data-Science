Title:
Veggie Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 medium zucchini, cut into 1 inch slices
10 yellow summer squash, cut into 1 inch slices
2-1/2 pounds whole fresh mushrooms
1 2/3 cup olive or vegetable oil
1/2 cup lemon juice
2 tablespoon dried basil
2 tablespoon dried parsley flakes
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
On metal or soaked bamboo skewers, alternately thread zucchini, yellow squash and mushrooms. In a bowl, combine the remaining ingredients. Brush some of the mixture over vegetables. Grill, uncovered, over medium heat for 10-15 minutes or until vegetables are tender, turning and basting occasionally with herb mixture.

Submitter: 
HARRYW
Description: 
'I discovered this recipe while trying to spruce up plain vegetables for dinner guests,' recalls Monica Meek Flatford of Knoxville, Tennessee. A mild spice blend coats colorful skewers of fresh zucchini, summer squash and mushrooms.Modified from: Veggie Skewers
