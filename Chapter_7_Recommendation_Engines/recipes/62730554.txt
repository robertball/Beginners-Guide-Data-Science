Title:
Neiman- Marcus Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups butter
24oz. chocolate chips
4 cups flour
2 cups brown sugar
2 tsp.baking soda
2 cups white sugar
1 tsp. salt
1-8oz.Hershey chocolate bar,grated
5 cups blended oatmeal
4 eggs
2 tsp.baking powder
2 tsp. vanilla
3 cups chopped almonds

Directions:
Measure oatmeal and blend into fine powder in blender.Cream butter and both sugars.Add eggs and vanilla,mix together with flour,oatmeal,salt,baking powder and baking soda.Add chocolate chips,grated hershey bar and nuts.Roll into balls and slightly press down,place 2 inches apart on lightly greased cookie sheet.Bake at 375 for 9 min.Makes 110 cookies.
