Title:
Debra's Savory Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound hot Italian sausage
1 pound ground beef
1 small onion, chopped
3 cloves minced garlic
Italian seasoning to taste (approximately 1-2 Tablespoons)
chili powder to taste (approximately 1-2 Tablespoons)
3-4 dashes of red chile flakes
***
30 oz can crushed tomatoes
2x 14.5 oz cans stewed Italian tomatoes
2x 14.5 oz cans tomato sauce

Directions:
Combine sausage, hamburger, onion and garlic in a large cookpot; brown and drain.  Add remaining ingredients.  Bring to a boil and adjust seasonings as needed.  Reduce heat and simmer 30 minutes.  Serve over cooked pasta.

Submitter: 
Bellwether Nesingwary
Description: 
Unusually hearty and flavorful, due to red pepper flakes and chili powder in addition to Italian Seasoning.  Perfect for fall and winter!
