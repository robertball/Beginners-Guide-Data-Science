Title:
Ch-ch-ch Chia Seed Chocolate Pudding (Sugar Free!) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup cocoa powder
2 cups hot water
1/4 teaspoon liquid stevia
1/4 cup chopped apricots
1/4 cup walnuts
1/4 cup chia seeds

Directions:
In a high speed blender, thoroughly blend, stevia, cocoa powder, hot water, apricots and walnuts.
In a bowl, stir together chia seeds and blended chocolate mixture.
Pour into your serving dish and let sit for 30 minutes.
Refrigerate

Submitter: 
ChrissyintheKitchen
Description: 
This delicious, nutritious pudding is easy to make and easier to eat! Yum! Chia seeds have a crunchy/slippery texture which makes them fun to chew. Great for the kids as a healthy snack and dessert.
