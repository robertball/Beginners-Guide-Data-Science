Title:
Hearty Crab Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pre-baked pizza crust
3 Tbsp butter
1 Tbsp minced garlic
1/2 medium onion (minced)
1 Tbsp basil
1 Tbsp oregano
1 Tbsp Parmesan Cheese
1 (8oz) pack Imitation Crab (chopped)
1 medium tomato (chopped)
1 cup corn
2 cups Mozzarella Cheese
Italian Seasoning

Directions:
1) Prepare pizza crust as directed. Grease pizza pan and dust with corn meal. Poke holes in crust with a fork and bake for 1/2 recommended time
2) Preheat oven to 400 Degrees.
3) Melt butter in saucepan over medium-high heat. Saute butter, garlic, and chopped onions until tender. Add basil, oregano, and Parmesan Cheese and remove from heat. Spread mixture evenly over pizza crust.
4) Sprinkle crust with 1/2 cup Mozzarella Cheese.
5) Place chopped crab meat in same skillet to warm over medium high heat for 2 minutes.
6) Sprinkle all of crab over pizza, followed by corn, and then chopped tomato.
7) Top with remaining 1 1/2c cheese and sprinkle with italian seasoning.
8) Bake for 10-20 minutes until cheese is melted and crust is golden brown.

Submitter: 
Erin
Description: 
A delicious pizza for crab lovers with a special sauce and healthy ingredients! Light butter & fat-free cheese substitute nicely.
