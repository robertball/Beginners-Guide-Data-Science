Title:
Sauteed Baby Spinach with Honey Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2T olive oil
2T thinly sliced garlic (4 cloves)
1/4c raisins
1/4c low sodium chicken broth
1 lb baby spinach
1/2t salt
1/4t black pepper
2t honey

Directions:
Heat oil in skillet.
Add garlic and cook until golden.
Add raisins and broth and cook about 1-2 minutes.
Add spinach and cook until wilted, 3 minutes.
Season with salt and pepper and drizzle with honey just before serving.
