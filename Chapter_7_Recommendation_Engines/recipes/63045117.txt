Title:
White Bean Chicken Chili - Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cans (15 oz. each) white beans (cannellini, Great Northern, etc.)
1 medium onion, chopped/diced
6 cloves garlic, minced or pressed
2 Pablano Peppers, chopped/diced without seeds
1 Red Pepper, chopped/diced without seeds
1 Packet Lowry's Taco Seasoning
2 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp dried oregano leaves
1 tsp ground cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp ground cloves
1/2 tsp salt
2-4 frozen boneless, skinless chicken breasts
2 1/2 cups chicken broth
OPTIONAL - 2 Serrano Peppers to add extra spice

Directions:
Rinse and drain the beans.
Place them in a slow cooker with the onions, garlic, peppers, spices, and salt, and stir to combine.
Place the chicken breasts over the top, and pour in the chicken broth.
Cover and cook on LOW for 9 to 10 hours, or HIGH for 4 to 5 hours.
When ready to serve, remove the chicken and shred the meat into bite-sized pieces using a couple of forks. After shredded stir chicken back in and let simmer for 30 minutes before serving
You can serve this with tortillas, tortilla chips, salsa, sour cream, guacamole, chopped fresh cilantro or shredded jack or cheddar cheese.

Submitter: 
Nate Thee
Description: 
A mild or spicy (with optional serrano peppers) white bean chili with chicken. The cooking times are for a slow cooker, but you can speed it up if needed by cooking on the stove top. Just adjust your time accordingly!
