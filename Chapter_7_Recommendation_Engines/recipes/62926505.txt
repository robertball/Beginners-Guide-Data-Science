Title:
Financiers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups finely ground almonds
2/3 cup sugar
2 eggs
1/3 cup melted butter
1 Tbsp all purpose flour

Directions:
Mix together almonds and sugar.
Add eggs, one at a time.
Add melted, cooled butter and flour.
Fill bottoms only (about 1/2 deep) of small greased and floured muffin tins.
Cook for 15-20 minutes or until slightly brown around the edges in a 375Â° oven.
Let cool before removing from pan. Store in airtight container.

Submitter: 
Lynn Pennec
Description: 
These are a french almond cookie with very little flour. I had looked for a recipe that didn't use separated eggs, just a relatively simple one. This one does the trick and always pleases.
