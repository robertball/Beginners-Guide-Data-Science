Title:
Oven Fried Garlic and Herb Halibut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup panko bread crumbs
1 tbsp. chopped basil
1 tbsp. chopped fresh flat-leaf
1/2 tsp. onion powder
1 large garlic clove, minced
2 large egg whites, lightly beaten
1 large egg, lightly beaten
2 tbsp. flour
6 - 6 oz. halibut fillets
3/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
2 tbsp. olive oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Combine first 5 ingredients in a shallow dish.
Place flour in a shallow dish.
Sprinkle fish with salt and pepper.
Dredge fish in flour, dip in egg mixture and dredge in panko mixture.
Heat 1 tbsp. oil in large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
Add 3 fillets and cook about 2 1/2 minutes on each side or until browned.
Coat cookie sheet with cooking spray and place fillets on pan.
Add remaining oil to pan and repeat procedure.
Bake fish at 450 for about 6 minutes or until fish flakes easily with fork.
