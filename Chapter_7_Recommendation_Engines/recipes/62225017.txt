Title:
Bacon Mushroom Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter, melted
2 boneless chicken breast halves
1 teaspoon seasoning salt
2 cloves garlic, crushed
4  slices precooked bacon
1 cup or 1 can mushrooms, halved
1 cup heavy cream or 1/2&1/2

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Spray 9x9 inch baking dish. Add chicken sprinkle with seasoning salt and garlic. Turn chicken over, season, and lay bacon strips on top. Sprinkle with mushrooms.
Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear.
Remove chicken, bacon and mushrooms to a platter and keep warm. Pour juices from baking dish into a small saucepan and whisk together with cream over low heat until thickened. Pour sauce over chicken and serve warm.
