Title:
Easy Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 ounce package baby carrots
1/4 cup water
1 TBSP. Honey
1/2 TBSP margarine or butter
1/4 tsp. ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, combine all ingredients together;
Stir well and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat, cover and simmer until the carrots are just tender, about 10 minutes.
Uncover, cook on medium-high until almost all liquid evaporates, about 3 minutes.

Submitter: 
JENNG7
Description: 
This is a simple side dish that even the non-carrot fans in my family enjoy. When you are scrambling for a side dish it can be made at the last minute with simple ingredients you will already have on hand.
