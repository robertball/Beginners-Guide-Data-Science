Title:
Air Fried coconut Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6042667.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 c all purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp of black pepper
2 large eggs
2/3 cups unsweetened flaked coconut
1/3 cup of japanese style panko crumbs
12 0z med peeled deveined raw shrimp tail on (about 24 shrimp)
cooking spray
1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/4 c of honey
1/4 c of lime juice
1 serrano chile thinly sliced (i used crushed red pepper) 2 tsps
2 tsps chopped fresh cilantro

Directions:
Stir together flour and pepper in shallow dish. Lightly beat egg in a second shallow dish.
stir panko and coconut in third shallow dish. Holding each shrimp by the tail, dredge shrimp in flour mixture,. Shake off excess then dip in egg allowing any excess to drip off.
Dredge in coconut mixture, pressing to adhere.  Coat shrimp  well with cooking spray.
Place half the shrimp in air fryer and cook at 400f until golden 6-8 mins, turning shrimp over halfway through cooking. Season with 1/4 tsp of salt. Repeat with remaining shrimp
and salt.
While shrimp cook, whisk together honey, lime juice, and serrano chile in small bowl.
Sprinkle shrimp with cilantro, Serve with sauce.
