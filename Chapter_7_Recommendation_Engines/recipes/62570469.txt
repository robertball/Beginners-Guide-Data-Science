Title:
Chocolate Chip Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup shortening
3/4 cup white sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. soda
1 tsp. hot water
1 cup nuts
1 6 oz. package chocolate chips
2 cups oatmeal
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven @ 375
Cream shortening and sugars together.
Add remaining ingredients and mix well.
Make 1-2" balls of dough and place on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake 8 minutes
