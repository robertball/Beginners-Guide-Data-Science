Title:
Spinach and artichoke dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. shallots, minced
2 10-oz pkgs. chopped frozen spinach, thawed and drained (or fresh)
4 cloves garlic, minced
4 T. unsalted butter
2 8-oz cans quartered artichoke hearts, drained
2 c. heavy cream (or less for desired consistency)
4 oz garlic herb cheese
1 t. kosher salt
1/2 t. white pepper
1/4 c. grated parmesan

Directions:
Saute shallots and garlic in butter. Add spinach and artichokes and cook on low briefly.  Add cream, garlic herb cheese, salt & pepper, and simmer till cheese melts.  Fold in parmesan.
Serve warm
