Title:
Casein-free Chocolate Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of frozen overripe bananas, broken into chunks
1/4 cup of soya milk (you can use coconut milk for more creamy texture)
1 tablespoon cocoa powder
dash of sugar / honey (optional)

Directions:
In a food processor, combine the frozen bananas, cocoa powder, and sugar (optional).
Slowly add in milk until desired texture and consistency is reached.
Scope ice cream into a bowl or pipe into a cone using a star tip. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Sophia Candrasa
Description: 
This casein-free ice cream is very creamy and delicious. It is very quick and easy to make, and tastes just as delicious without adding the sugar. You can make different varieties by adding different kind of frozen fruits (i.e frozen berries, mangos, peaches, etc)
