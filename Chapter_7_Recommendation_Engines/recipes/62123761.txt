Title:
Spinach Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½ lb fresh pasta
1 c thinly sliced onions
1 TB olive oil
1 14.5 oz can chicken broth
Freshly ground pepper to taste
1 bag baby spinach
¼ c shredded asiago cheese

Directions:
In a pot, cook onion in butter till golden brown
Add chicken broth and pepper and bring to boil
Add pasta and spinach
Cook covered until pasta is tender
Toss and serve topped with asiago cheese

Submitter: 
tristin
Description: 
This pasta dish is simple, satisfying, and healthy.  I like to use whole wheat pasta but any fresh pasta will do.
