Title:
Tropical Salad with Cilantro Lime Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/354070.jpg
Ingredients:
2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 cup teriyaki marinade/sauce
1 tsp ground ginger
4-5 large leaves of romaine lettuce, washed, dried, and chopped
1-2 large carrots, shaved
1 fresh pineapple, sliced into chunks
1 small cucumber, sliced
cilantro lime dressing (allrecipes.com recipe)

Directions:
-combine marinade and ginger in large plastic, zip-top bag
-wash chicken and place into bag and seal. store in refrigerator up to 4 hours
-arrange lettuce on two plates. top with carrot shavings, cucumbers, and pineapple.
-grill chicken on George Foreman grill for 9 minutes
-slice chicken and place one chicken breast on each salad
-drizzle with Cilantro Lime dressing
Enjoy!
