Title:
Feta and Spinach Crustless Frittata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 whole eggs
6 egg whites
1 bag baby spinach
8 oz feta
ground black pepper

Directions:
Beat the eggs together and season with pepper.
Butter a 10-inch pie plate
Microwave spinach on a dinner plate for two minutes and squeeze out the water it releases.
Arrange the spinach in the bottom of the pie plate, crumble the feta over the spinach, then pour the egg mixture over the cheese and spinach.
Bake in a 350 degree oven for 30 minutes, or until a knife inserted in the middle comes out clean.

Submitter: 
squawk93
Description: 
Cut into eighths, this frittata makes a great grab-and-go breakfast. I will make it on a Sunday and keep the individually wrapped portions in my fridge to eat during the week as breakfast. lunch or snack. Vary ingredients to your tastes by adding onion, other vegetables, chopped Canadain bacon, or whatever else you have on hand.
