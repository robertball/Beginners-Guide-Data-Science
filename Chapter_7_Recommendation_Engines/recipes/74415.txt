Title:
Zippy Corn on the Cob Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/237784.jpg
Ingredients:
6 medium ears sweet corn
1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley
2 teaspoons prepared horseradish
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Place ears of corn on a double thickness of heavy-duty foil. In a small bowl, combine the remaining ingredients; brush over corn. Fold foil around corn and seal tightly.
Grill, covered, over medium heat for 25-30 minutes or until corn is tender, turning once.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home's Fast Family Favorites
Description: 
Dijon mustard and horseradish perk up this summertime favorite. You can prepare this sweet corn on the grill or in the oven.
