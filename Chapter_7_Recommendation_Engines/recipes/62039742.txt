Title:
sugar-free frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cornstarch
1 cup granulated sweetener
1/2 cup softened butter
2 tbsp milk
1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Mix cornstarch and granulated sugar together well in a small bowl.  It should closely resemble powdered sugar when completely blended.  Add butter and vanilla.  Mix well.  Add milk until desired consistency is reach.  You may need more or less than specified in ingredients list according to your desire.  Add coloring and use as desired!  Would be great to frost sugar-free sugar cookies.

Submitter: 
TREESAP
Description: 
Although this frosting is sugar-free, it is basically the same taste and consistency of any buttercream frosting, just less calories!  Perfect for a diabetic member of your family.  Can be used on cookies, cakes...anything!
