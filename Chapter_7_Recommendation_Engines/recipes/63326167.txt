Title:
Hearty Savory Lentils Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/881572.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup dry brown lentils
1/3 cup finely chopped yellow onion
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
1/4 teaspoon dried turmeric
1/8 teaspoon dried sage
1/8 teaspoon dried basil
1/8 teaspoon dried oregano
1/8 teaspoon black pepper
2 cups water
1/2 teaspoon sea salt

Directions:
Rinse and drain lentils.
Combine all ingredients except salt in a 2 quart saucepan.
Bring to boil.  Reduce heat.  Simmer uncovered until lentils and onion are tender adding more water if necessary; 45 to 55 minutes.  Add salt last to keep lentils from toughening.
