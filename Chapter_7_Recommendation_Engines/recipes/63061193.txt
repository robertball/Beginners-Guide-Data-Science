Title:
BBQ Hambugers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Hambuger Meat
BBQ Sauce
Hambuger Buns
lettuce
tomatoes
cheese slices
mayo
mustard
ketchup

Directions:
Make hambuger Patties or use pre-made patties.  Start charcoal and when ready grill hambuger patties.  Add BBQ sauce the last few minutes and top with cheese slices and grill until cheese is melted.  Service on hambuger buns with lettuce and tomatoe and your choice of dressing.  May add chopped onion if desired.
