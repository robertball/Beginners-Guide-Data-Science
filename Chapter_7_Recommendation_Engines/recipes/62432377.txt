Title:
Oatmeal Applesauce Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/451880.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups flour
1 1/4 cups quick oats
1 tsp baking powder
3/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1 cup applesauce
2 eggs
1/2 cup milk
3 tbs oil

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Add wet and blend. Fill greased muffin pan cups full and bake for 15-18 min or until muffins bounce back when gentle touched. Enjoy warm or cold with butter.

Submitter: 
Christa
Description: 
These yummy muffins are a regular favorite at our house. The kids love to make them since they are easy to make. They also make a good breakfast or snack since they are a bit heartier than your average muffin.
