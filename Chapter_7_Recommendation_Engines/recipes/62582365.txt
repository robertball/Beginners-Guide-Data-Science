Title:
Peanut Butter Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C peanut butter
2 Tbl melted butter
1/2 C icing sugar
1 1/2 C crisp rice cereal
1/2 C mini peanut butter cups

Directions:
Mix first 3 ingredients together. Stir in cereal and peanut butter cups. Chill for about 10 minutes. Roll into balls and place on a parchment paper lined baking sheet. You can roll them in icing sugar as well. Chill for about an hour.
