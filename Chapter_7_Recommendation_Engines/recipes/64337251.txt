Title:
Masala Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 1/2 cups sliced onions
1/4 cup plain yogurt
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1 tbsp lemon juice
1-2 hot peppers (such as Serrano, jalapeño, adjust to taste)
2 cloves minced garlic
2 tsp fresh grated ginger
2 tsp hot or mild curry powder
1 tsp salt
4-6 oz salmon fillets
Fresh cilantro

Directions:
Heat oil in a frying pan.  Sauté onions til browned and slightly crispy.  Drain oil from onions and reserve.  Cool onions slightly, and place in a good processor with yogurt, cilantro, lemon juice, peppers, curry powder, salt and 1 tbsp of reserved oil.  Puree. Cover salmon with purée, turning to coat.  Let stand for at least 15 min, or up to a couple of hours.  Grill over medium-high heat until fish is cooked and flakes easily, about 12 minutes.  Garnish with sprigs of fresh cilantro.

Submitter:

Description: 
I don't normally care for fish, however I found this recipe in Canadian Living magazine in 2010 and have made it countless times since.  Such a refreshing way to have salmon--so good!
