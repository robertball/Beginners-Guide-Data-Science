Title:
The Whole Enchilada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
750 pounds of stone ground corn
water
175 gallons of vegetable oil
75 gallons of red chile sauce
175 pounds of grated cheese
50 pounds of chopped onion
14 men

Directions:
Combine the ground corn and enough water to make a dough.
Divide the dough into thirds.
Move one/third of masa dough on the especially built carrying plate to the HUGE tortilla press.
Press the dough to make the tortilla.
Heat the oil to 550 &#0176;
Round up 14 men to move the tortilla to the hot oil to cook.
Using extreme care, use the same 14 men to move the cooked tortilla to the serving plate.
Ladle on some chile sauce, spread cheese and chopped onion.
Repeat steps 1-8 to make the second and third layers.
Cut and serve with sour cream (10 gallons) if desired.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
Be ready for your next Mexican fiesta with this enchilada, guaranteed to feed your whole clan + the entire neighborhood.  This is from the Las Cruces Whole Enchilada Festival, and they really do make and serve this during the festival. (Check it out in the Guiness Book of World Records.)
