Title:
Easy tuna mac Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of macaroni and cheese mix
(milk and butter)
1 can tuna(drained)
1 small can of peas

Directions:
follow the direction on the box of mac and cheese( will have to add milk and butter to it). When the macaroni is cooked add the milk and cheese according to the box. Then add the can of drained, tuna and the can of drained pease. heat through while stirring constantly as it may stick to your pot and burn. You just want to get it all hot. You could do this on the stove or you could pre-heat your over to 350 degrees while you are boiling the macaroni and put the entire tuna + mac mixture into a cassarole dish and heat for about 15-20 minutes.
Thsi si so yummy, easy and inexpensive!
