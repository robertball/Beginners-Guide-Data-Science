Title:
Slow Cooked Rump Roast with Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 Pounds rump roast
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 Can (10 1/2 oz.) beef broth
1 (4 oz.)Can sliced mushrooms (I use Portabella)
2 cloves of minced garlic
1 Package of Dry Onion Soup Mix
1/4 Tsp. Pepper
1-2 Tbsp. Cornstarch

Directions:
Place roast in slow cooker, mix rest of ingredients in large bowl (Except the Corn Starch) and pour on top of roast. Cover and cook on low about 8 hours. when roast is finished cooking, remove it from slow cooker and place on serving platter. Skim fat from top of meat juices and Pour juices from slow cooker into saucepan. Mix 1-2 tbsp cornstarch with cold water and mix into pan drippings, stiring constantly, until mixture thickens into a gravy. Serve over sliced roast beef.
