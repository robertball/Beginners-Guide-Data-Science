Title:
Coconut Mango Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1120604.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups white or jasmine rice
2 cups coconut milk
2 cups water
1 tsp. kosher salt
1 tsp. white vinegar
2-3 tsp. sugar
1 Tbsp. butter
2 cups diced mango (2 medium mangoes)
1/4 cup chopped parsley or cilantro
1/2 cup sliced green onions
1/4 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Combine rice, coconut milk, water, salt, vinegar, and sugar in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Turn heat to low and cook covered 20 minutes or until most of the liquid is absorbed. Allow to stand 5 minutes.
Add butter, mango, parsley or cilantro, green onions, black pepper, and additional salt, to taste.
