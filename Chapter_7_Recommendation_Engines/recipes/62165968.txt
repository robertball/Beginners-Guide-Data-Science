Title:
Easy Pasta Night Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Penne Pasta
1/2 lb Boneless Chicken Breast
1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
1 teaspoon Italian Seasoning
1/2 teaspoon Kosher Salt
1 small head of escarole (can subst. spinach)
1 (15 oz) can diced or stewed tomatoes, drained
1 tablespoon Olive Oil
1 tablespoon Minced Garlic
1/4 cup Chicken Broth
1 Chicken Boullion Cube
Parmesan cheese, to taste

Directions:
Combine garlic powder, italian seasoning and salt.  Season chicken breasts with mixture.
Grill chicken breasts until cooked through, chop in to bite sized peices.
Wash and chop escarole.
Boil water and add pasta, cook according to box directions then drain while doing the following:
Saute garlic in the olive oil until fragrant.
Add escarole and sautee until wilted, 2-3 min.
Add drained tomatoes and saute 1 minutes.
Add chicken broth and boullion cube, simmer 5 minutes.
Add chopped chicken breast and heat through.
Add sauce mixture to drained pasta and toss until combined.
Top with plenty of grated parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Christine Ellis Roy
Description: 
Delicious and easy low-fat pasta dish.  Adapted from a dish at my favorite restaurant. Perfect with a green salad and gorgonzola garlic bread.  A once a week staple in our dinner menu rotation.
