Title:
Light Whipped Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/513049.jpg
Ingredients:
16.2 oz chocolate store bought frosting

Directions:
Dump entire container of frosting into bowl and beat on medium with electric mixer for 2 minutes.
Load into piping bag and frost cupcakes

Submitter: 
Skinny Cakes
Description: 
This is a super easy and quick way to frost your cupcakes. You'll get more bang for your buck and reduce your caloric intake!
