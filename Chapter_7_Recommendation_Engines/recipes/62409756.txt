Title:
Traci's Bacon Ranch Macaroni Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. (1-lb bacon) cooked and crumbled
16 oz. salad macaroni
1 1/2 cups mayonnaise
3/4 cup bottled ranch dressing
3 stalks of celery, sliced
1 large carrot, grated
1T fresh chives (optional)
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. course black pepper

Directions:
Cook and crumble the bacon; set aside.
Cook the macaroni according to the package directions; cool completley.
Combine the mayonnaise, ranch dressing, salt and pepper; mix into macaroni, reserving 1/2 cup for later.  Add the bacon, celery, carrot and chives.
Chill 2 hours, adding the reserved dressing before serving.

Submitter: 
Traci-in-Cali
Description: 
My teenagers love anything with bacon and prefer this to traditional macaroni salad.  Ranch and mayonnaise make a great dressing!
