Title:
Pancho's Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/789605.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Lb. Velveeta
1 Can Rotel Tomoatos
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1 tsp. cumin
1 tsp. garlic powder

Directions:
Add all ingredients to pot.  Allow cheese to melt and bring to soft boil.  Remove and pour into blender and blend until smooth.
