Title:
Homemade Chili Powder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp. paprika
1 Tbsp. ground cumin
2 Tbsp. dried oregano
1 tsp. red or cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp. garlic powder

Directions:
Mix ingredients well.
Place in an airtight container.
Makes about 1/2 cup.

Submitter: 
Paula
Description: 
One day on the Recipe Buzz, I was lamenting over having only half the amount of chili powder called for in a recipe.  BigShotsMom gave me her recipe for homemade chili powder.
