Title:
Country Sausage Patties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 pounds pork shoulder or butt. Choose fatty cuts sold with bone in at about 7 pounds. When bone is removed, about 6 pounds of meat remains. Cubed into 1" cubes, max.
1 1/2 - 2 pounds of pork fat, uncured, unsalted is best.
6 tsp kosher salt
4 1/2 tsp black pepper
6 tsp sage, finely ground
6 tsp thyme, finely ground
1 1/2 tsp rosemary
3 tbls light brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
1 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
1 1/2 tsp nutmeg

Directions:
Cube meat into 1" pieces. Remove stringy connective tissue.
Add remaining ingredients and thoroughly mix.
Cover and refrigerate for at least one hour, longer is better.
After chilling, grind.
Wrap into bulk packages in butcher paper. For freezing, place in freezer bags.

Submitter: 
Bryant Evans
Description: 
Bulk sausage which can be made into patties.
