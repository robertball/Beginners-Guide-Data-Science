Title:
Let the Kids Make the Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg 10 oz. frozen chopped broccoli
1 10.5 oz chicken soup with rice
1 1/4 cups chicken broth
4 oz. Velveeta cheese, cubed

Directions:
Cook broccoli and drain. Add chicken soup and broth. Place over medium heat and bring to a boil.Remove from heat and puree in blender. Return to pot and stir in cheese, heat until cheese melts, about 4 minutes. Serve
