Title:
It's Chili by George!! ala Cindy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground beef
2 29oz cans of crushed tomatoes
4 cans chili beans
1 1/2 cups chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
1/8 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon white sugar
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon ground cumin
1/4 cup + 1 tablespoon chili powder

Directions:
Place ground beef, onions, and peppers in a large pot. Cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown. Drain.
In the same large pot over high heat combine the ground beef, crushed tomatoes, chili beans, cayenne pepper, sugar, oregano, ground black pepper, salt, cumin and chili powder. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Simmer for at least 90 minutes. (Note: If using a slow cooker, set on low, add ingredients, and cook for 8 to 10 hours.)

Submitter: 
Cindy in Pensacola
Description: 
This is a good, standard chili.  It needs to simmer for at least 90 minutes on the stove, or can be made in a slow cooker and left to simmer all day.Modified from: It's Chili by George!!
