Title:
Swedish Apple Pie! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/336866.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 peeled, sliced apples
1 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)
1 tablespoon sugar
3/4 cup melted butter
3/4 cup sugar
1 cup flour
1 egg
1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)
A pinch of salt

Directions:
Fill pie pan with the apples. Sprinkle the cinnamon, and one tablespoon sugar on apples. In small bowl combine melted butter, 3/4 cup sugar, flour, egg, nuts, and salt. Pour over apples. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm or cold.
