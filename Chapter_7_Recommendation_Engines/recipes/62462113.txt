Title:
Pumpkin Pie Tartlets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups can pumpkin puree
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
2 large eggs
2teaspoons ground cinnamon
2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
1/2 teaspoon salt
24 unbaked phyllo tart shells

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Whisk pumpkin, sweetened condensed milk, eggs, spices and salt in medium bowl until smooth. Pour into tart shells. Bake 18 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool. Garnish with a dollop of whipped cream. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Carol
