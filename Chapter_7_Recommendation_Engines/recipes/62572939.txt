Title:
Chicken Pot Pie IX Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cubed
1 cup sliced carrots
1 cup frozen green peas
1 cup frozen corn
1/2 cup sliced celery
2 small potatoes diced
2/3 cup butter
2/3 cup chopped onion
2/3 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 teaspoon celery seed or garlic powder
3 1/2 cups chicken broth
1 1/3 cup milk
2 (9 inch)  unbaked pie crusts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (220 degrees C.)
In a saucepan, combine chicken, carrots, peas, corn, potatoes and celery. Add broth to cover and boil for 15 minutes. Remove from heat, drain and set aside.
In a saucepan over medium heat, cook onions in butter until soft and translucent. Stir in flour, salt, pepper, and celery seed or garlic powder. Slowly stir in chicken broth and milk. Simmer over medium-low heat until thick. Remove from heat and set aside.
Place the chicken mixture in prebrowned bottom pie crust. Pour hot liquid mixture over. Cover with top crust, seal edges, and cut away excess dough. Make several small slits in the top to allow steam to escape.  Brush crust with egg white and
Bake in the 375 preheated oven for 40 to 45 minutes, or until pastry is golden brown and filling is bubbly. Cool for 10 minutes before serving.
