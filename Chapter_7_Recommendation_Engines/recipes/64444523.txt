Title:
Sriracha Honey Chicken Wing Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 Cup Honey
3/4 Cup Sriracha
2 tbsp Unsalted Butter
1/4 Cup Lime Juice (about 1 large lime)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a small sauce pot and combine.
For thicker sauce, let the combination reduce, but be careful not to make it as thick as honey.

Submitter: 
Nate Thee
Description: 
This is a quick and easy wing sauce. Sweet and perfectly spicy.
