Title:
Mustard Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup mayonnaise
3 Tbsp. Mustard
1 tsp. hot sauce (or to taste)
dash of pepper

Directions:
Combine mustard and mayonnaise until color of deviled egg filling.
Add hot sauce and pepper to taste.
If desired, thin with water.
Chill for best flavor

Submitter: 
Ryan Stroud
Description: 
A spicy and creamy mustard sauce. (I like it with grilled cheese.)
