Title:
BBQ Potato Chip Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2129643.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt ~or~ 1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup of your FAVORITE barbeque sauce

Directions:
Mix Greek yogurt and barbeque sauce together.  Chill for at least 30 minutes.  Serve with potato chips.

Submitter: 
Molly
Description: 
A childhood favorite chip dip.  Remember....it is only as good as your FAVORITE barbeque sauce.
