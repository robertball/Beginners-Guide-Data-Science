Title:
Super Bowl breadsticks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated pizza crust dough
1/3 cup olive oil
6 oz. grated Parmesan cheese
6 oz. mozzarella cheese
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley or Italian seasoning

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
Roll out pizza dough to form a 10x16 inch sheet of dough.
Paint the dough with Olive oil
Cut the sheet into 1-inch parallel strips. Then cut these strips in half making 32 pieces.
Sprinkle cheese and parsley over crust
Bake in preheated oven until golden brown.
Remove from oven and serve.
