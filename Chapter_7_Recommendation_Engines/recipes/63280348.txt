Title:
Slow cooker Cornish hens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/862896.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cornish hens
1 large Zucchini, chopped
2 C baby Portabella mushrooms, chopped
1 (7 oz) jar of Spanish olives (with pimento), drained and chopped
1 (10 oz) can of Campbells Golden mushroom soup
1 tsp. Garlic salt

Directions:
Rinse and prepare the Cornish hens, then baste with the uncooked soup.
In a bowl, mix the garlic salt,and the remaining soup- with the zucchini, mushrooms and olives.
Stuff the mixture inside of the hens, and place them breast up, in the crockpot.
Spoon the remaining mixture on top of the hens,and cover.
Cook for four hrs. on the high setting.
