Title:
Puerto Rican Coconut Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans (15 oz) Cream of Coconut
4 cups milk
1 cup corn starch
pinch of salt
8 tbsp. sugar
2 tsp vanilla
ground cinnamon for garnish
shredded cocnut for garnish (optional)

Directions:
Rinse Bundt pan or mold in cold water. Combine milk and cream of coconut in a bowl. In a saucepan, dissolve cornstarch in one cup of milk mixture. Add remaining milk mixture, salt, sugar and vanilla. Cook on medium high heat, stirring frequently. Mixture will begin to thicken. Boil about two minutes, stirring gently. Pour into prepared pan. Refigerate 6 hours or overnight. Unmold. Slice and sprinkle with cinnamon. Garnish with shredded coconut if desired.

Submitter: 
Angie Duffy Hernandez
Description: 
A delicious cocnut pudding originated in Puerto Rico . It is served sliced with cinnamon and shredded coconut if desired.
