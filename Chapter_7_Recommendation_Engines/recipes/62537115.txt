Title:
Pumpkin Pie Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/558851.jpg
Ingredients:
4 whole eggs
1 c. salad oil
2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
2 c. sugar
2 c. all-purpose flour
2 tsp. cinnamon
2 c. pumpkin

Directions:
Beat the eggs together with the sugar until light and well blended. Add salad oil, continuing to beat. Sift dry ingredients together and thoroughly mix into the egg mixture. Add pumpkin and blend well again. Pour into well greased and floured 13x9 inch cake pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 55 minutes or when toothpick comes out clean.  Ice or top with whip cream

Submitter: 
Angel
Description: 
This is a family favorite.  Can be iced with Cream Cheese Frosting but we love it warm topped with Whip Cream Topping
