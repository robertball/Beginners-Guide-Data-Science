Title:
Tornado Screwdriver Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
5 fluid ounces crushed ice
5 fluid ounces fortified concord grape wine
5 fluid ounces vodka
5 fluid ounces gin

Directions:
Fill two rocks glasses with ice. Pour in wine, vodka and gin; stir.

Description:
