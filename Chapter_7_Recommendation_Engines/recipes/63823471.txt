Title:
Puffed Apple Pancake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1060701.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Tbs butter, divided
3 large tart apples(Granny smith works well), cored and sliced, divided
6 Tbs white sugar, divided
3 tsp ground cinnamon
2/3 cup milk
2 oz Neufchatel Cheese, softened
2/3 cup all-purpose flour
6 eggs
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 pinch salt
Powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Melt 2 Tbs butter in an oven-safe skillet over medium heat; cook and stir in 1/3 of the apple slices, 2Tbs sugar, and 1 tsp cinnamon in butter until apples are tender, about 5 minutes.
Whisk Neufchatel cheese and milk in medium bowl until blended. Stir in flour, eggs,2 Tbs sugar, vanilla, baking powder  and salt; pour evenly over apples. Sprinkle with remaining cinnamon; dot with 1 Tbs butter.
Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 10 minutes. Run a spatula around the edges of the pancake to loosen. Sprinkle powdered sugar on top  to serve.
