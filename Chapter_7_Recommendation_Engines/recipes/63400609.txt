Title:
Runderlap Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6744877.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs chuck roast
2 medium onions
3 cloves of garlic
8 tbsp butter
2 tbsp flour
1 15 oz can stewed tomatoes
1 tbsp A-1 sauce
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp paprika powder
1/2 tsp mild curry powder
1/2 tsp oregano
1 bay leaf
2 beef bouillon block
1 tbsp vinegar
1 cup water

Directions:
salt and pepper roast
chop onion
mince garlic
melt half of butter in pan on medium high heat until butter is slightly brown
add onions and saute until clear
add garlic and continue to saute for another a minute
remove from pan
add remaining butter and slightly brown
add roast and brown completely
reduce heat to low and remove roast to slow cooker
replace onions and garlic in pan
add flour and mix thoroughly
add remaining ingredients
bring to a boil
transfer gravy to slow cooker and slow cook on high for 30 minutes
reduce slow cooker to low and cook for 4-6 hours
