Title:
Italian Stir-Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 pound sweet Italian turkey sausage, cut into 1/2 inch slices
- 1 pound asparagus, chopped into 2" lengths
- 1 medium yellow squash, quartered and chopped
- 1 Roma tomato, chopped
- 2 tbsps. sun dried tomatoes in oil, chopped
- 1/4 cup chopped roasted red pepper
- 1 cup coarsely chopped cabbage
- 1/2 medium onion, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, diced
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tsps. chicken bouillon granules
- 2 tsp. worcestershire sauce
- 1 tsp. sesame oil

Directions:
Brown sausage in large skillet over medium-high heat.  Drain excess fat and return skillet to burner.  Add olive oil.  Add onions and garlic and stir-fry for one minute.  Add all other veggies and continue stir-frying for two minutes. Add chicken bouillion granules, worcestershire sauce and sesame oil, and cook for another minute or two or until fresh vegetables are crisp-tender.

Description:
