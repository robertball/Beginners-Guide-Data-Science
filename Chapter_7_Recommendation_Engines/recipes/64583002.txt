Title:
Easy Black Forest Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3674575.jpg
Ingredients:
Cake
1 pkg  Duncan Hines dark chocolate cake mix (15.25 Ozs)
1 cup water
3 large eggs
1/3 cup canola oil
Filling
1 21 oz can Lucky Leaf cherry filling
1/2 tsp almond extract
Frosting
1 pint (2 cups) heavy whipping cream
2/3 cup powdered sugar
2 tbsp brandy
1/2 oz bakers semi-sweet chocolate (for chocolate curls or shavings if desired)

Directions:
1.  Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease and flour two 8 or 9 inch round cake pans.  In large bowl, follow instructions on cake mix box to assemble, then pour batter into prepared pans.  Bake for 24 to 30  mins.  Toothpick inserted in center should come out dry.  Cool in pan 15 minutes then remove from pans and let cool for one hour.
2.  In small bowl combine cherry filling and almond extract.
3.  In medium bowl, beat whipping cream at high speed until slightly thickened.  Add powdered sugar and continue beating until stiff peaks form.  Fold in brandy.
4.  On serving plate turn 1 cake layer upside down.  Spread 1 cup cherry filling within inch of edges.  Place second layer on top, top side up.  Frost sides and top with whipped cream.  Put the remaining cherry filling on top center of cake in a circle.  Garnish with chocolate curls.  Store in refrigerator.
***Please note any brand of dark chocolate cake mix can be used.  Be sure to modify amount of eggs, oil and water used according to that brand mix.

Submitter: 
Carey
Description: 
A favorite German recipe that pairs rich dark chocolate cake with cherry filling and whipped cream.  This is an easy to make version that is very moist and tasty.
