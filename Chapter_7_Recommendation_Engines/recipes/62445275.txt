Title:
Spiced Tomato Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint grape tomatoes, halved
2 Tbsp cider vinegar
1 Tbsp brown sugar
pinch of allspice
pinch of cinnamon
salt and pepper

Directions:
In small saucepan, combine all ingredients w/ 1/2 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp pepper.  Simmer, stirring occasionally, until mixture thickens, aprx 8-10 minutes.  Serve warm or at room temperature
