Title:
Big Ray's Broccoli-Onion Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/997771.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup milk
4 eggs
3 tsp cannola oil
1/2 shredded Parmesan cheese
1 tsp dried oregano
3/4 tsp garlic powder
3/4 tsp onion powder
1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
1/8 tsp fresh ground black pepper
3/4 cup multi-purpose baking mix
4 cupd broccoli florets
1 cup chopped onions

Directions:
Heat oven to 400 degrees. Coat six 6 oz ceramic ramekins with nonstick cooking spray.
In a large bowl, whisk milk, eggs, oil, 6 tsp of the cheese, and spices. Whisk in baking mix until smooth; fold in broccoli and onions.
Divide equally among ramekins and bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes.  Sprinkle remaining cheese on tops and bake for additional 10 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 160 degrees.  Let cool slightly.  Run a small knife around the edges and gently unmold.
