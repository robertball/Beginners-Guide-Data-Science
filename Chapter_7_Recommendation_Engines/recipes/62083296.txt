Title:
Chicken and Stuffing Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cooked cubed chicken
2 cups broccoli florrets
1 jar chicken gravy
3 cups stuffing, prepared
1 cup shredded cheese, your choice, divided

Directions:
combine first four ingredients and 1/2 cup of the cheese in large bowl, spread in a greased 8 x 8 pan, sprinkle with remaining cheese.  Bake for 20-25 minutes at 350
