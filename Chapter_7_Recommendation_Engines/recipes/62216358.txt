Title:
Sugar and Spice Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1-lb. package of bacon strips (about 16 strips)
1 tb. cinnamon
1/2 c. light brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Cover a large lipped baking sheet with parchment paper.
Lay the bacon strips on the cookie sheet so that they are not touching each other.
Sprinkle the bacon generously with the cinnamon and then with the brown sugar.
Bake until the bacon is crispy, about 15 minutes.
Drain on paper towels.

Submitter:

Description: 
A sweet (and easy) way to prepare bacon that's a hit with adults and kids alike! The perfect addition for a brunch or a special breakfast at home.
