Title:
Apricot Date Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/956008.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup dried apricots, cut into strips
2 cups boiling water
1/2 cup chopped dates
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1 1/2 cups whole milk
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 egg
2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 tsp baking powder
3/4 tsp baking soda
3/4 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease and flour a 9x5 inch loaf pan.  Place apricots in a small bowl and cover with boiling water.  Let sit for 5 minutes, then drain.  Add dates and walnuts to the apricots and mix. In a large bowl, mix flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.  In a medium bowl, whisk the egg. Then add the brown sugar, milk and oil until well incorporated.  Add this to the flour mixture and stir just until moistened.  Stir in apricots, dates and walnuts.  Pour into the prepared pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 60-70 minutes or until toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool for 10 minutes then remove from pan.  Let cool completely, then wrap in saran and store in the refrigerator.

Description: 
This is a recipe my aunt had written down over 30 years ago, and remains a family favorite. It's rich in taste, golden in color, very moist and dense.  Serve it with whipped butter, cream cheese, marmalade, or eat just plain!
