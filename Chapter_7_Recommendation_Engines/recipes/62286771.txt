Title:
Ham & Onion Cheese Log Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/355022.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8oz) package cream cheese, softened
3 oz sliced Canadian Bacon, chopped into small pieces
3 green onions, chopped
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
In a medium-size mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, ham, and onions. Shape into a ball and roll into chopped pecans, working into a log form. Line countertop with plastic wrap, Cover and refrigerate for a couple of hours to overnight. Serve with your favorite crackers or toasted bread.

Submitter: 
Melissa Goff
Description: 
Quick, simple, but flavorful, spread that is great with crackers or celery.Modified from: Cream Cheese and Ham Spread
