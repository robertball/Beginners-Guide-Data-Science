Title:
Sausage Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 10 oz. pkg. Cheddar Cheese
1 lb. hot bulk sausage
2 1/2 c. Bisquick

Directions:
1. Grate cheese. Mix ingredients. Shape into small balls.
2. Place on cookie sheet.
3. Bake in preheated 350ยบ oven for 15 minues.
4. May be frozen before baking.  Place on cookie sheet. Freeze until firm. Place in plastic bags. Freeze.
