Title:
Banana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
3 med. or large ripe bananas, mashed
3/4 cup sugar
1 egg
1/3 cup butter or margarine, melted
1/3 cup chocolate chips
OR chopped walnuts

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients.
Blend bananas, sugar, egg and butter.
Add to dry ingredients, just until moistened.
Bake in greased muffin tins @ 375 x 15 - 20 minutes, until tests done with toothpick.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Classic banana muffins - just as good whether you add chocolate chips or walnuts (but our favorite is cc's!).
