Title:
Star-Spangled Mixers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983844.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (10 fluid ounce) cans BACARDI速 Mixers Pina Colada frozen mix
1 (10 fluid ounce) can BACARDI速 Mixers Strawberry Daiquiri frozen mix
1 1/2 cups BACARDI速 Rum
Blue Curacao or blue food coloring
15 cups ice
Strawberries, for garnish

Directions:
Blend together 2 cups or 1 can of BACARDI(R) Mixers Pina Colada mix, 1/2 cup of BACARDI(R) Rum and 5 cups of ice for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform. Add 7-10 drops of blue food coloring or Blue Curacao to taste. Blend and fill each cocktail glass 1/3 of the way full.
Repeat step 1 using BACARDI(R) Mixers Pina Colada. (Without food coloring.)
Repeat step 2 using BACARDI(R) Mixers Strawberry Daiquiri.
Garnish with strawberries and serve immediately or freeze and serve for a tasty treat.
