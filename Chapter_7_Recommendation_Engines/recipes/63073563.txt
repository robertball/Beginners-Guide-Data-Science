Title:
Greek Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
½  box Fuselli Pasta
½ English cucumber, seeded and finely chopped
2 plum tomato, seeded and finely chopped
2 cloves of garlic, crushed/finely chopped
½ cup of green olives or black olives
1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning
1 ½ teaspoons of Greek (Clubhouse) seasoning
2 tablespoons of Feta juice (liquid)
¾ cup of crumbled feta cheese
½ cup to ¾ cup of Presidents Choice Tzatziki salad dressing (Superstore brand)
1 teaspoon of Olive Oil

Directions:
Tomato Preparation:
Add, tomato, crushed garlic, olive oil, ½ tsp of cracked black pepper, Italian seasoning to a small bowl.  Mix well, and set aside until time to assemble salad.
Pasta Prepartion:
Cook Fuseli slightly under al dente.  Drain and rinse quickly under cold water.
Add cooked pasta, cucumber, olives, tomato, Greek seasoning, feta, feta juice, Tzatziki to bowl, mix thoroughly but gently.  Cover with plastic wrap, refrigerate for 2 hours or overnight before serving.  You can add a little extra Tzatziki dressing if the pasta seems dry.
You add add salt and pepper to taste!
