Title:
Barbie's Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) can white tuna, drained and flaked
1/2 cup + 2 TBS mayonnaise or salad dressing
2 TBS Parmesan cheese
1/3 cup sweet pickle relish
1/4 tsp dried minced onion flakes
1/2 tsp curry powder
2 TBS dried parsley
1 TBS dried dill weed
1 dash garlic powder

Directions:
In a medium bowl, stir together the tuna, mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, and onion flakes. Season with curry powder, parsley, dill and garlic powder. Mix well and serve with crackers or on a sandwich.

Submitter: 
Carol Hammond
