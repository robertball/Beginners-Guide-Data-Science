Title:
Chocolate-Pumpkin Pies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups crushed chocolate-wafer cookies, Nabisco
6 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup sugar
1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
2 large eggs, lightly beaten
2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
1/2 teaspoon salt
20 teaspoons chocolate hazelnut spread, Nutella
Garnish:  whipped cream, chopped toasted hazelnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350'.
In a large bowl, combine cookies, butter, and sugar.  Press into bottom of 10 (4 oz) mason jars.
In a large bowl, combine pumpkin, condensed milk, eggs, pumpkin pie spice, and salt.  Beat at medium speed with an electric mixer until well blended.  Divide pumpkin mixture evenly among mason jars.  Place 2 teaspoons chocolate hazelnut spread in center of each jar.  Smooth pmpkin over the top.  Bake for 30 minutes, or until set.  Cool completely.  Serve with whipped cream and chopped hazelnuts, if desired.

make in small canning jars, this is an easy and idea way to have a family dessert.
