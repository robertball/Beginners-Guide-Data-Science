Title:
FRITTATA AL FORNO Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 EGG
6 OZ HEAVY CREAM
3 OZ MILK
PINCH OF SALT
1 TBSP PARMESEAN CHEESE
3 OZ COOKED ANGEL HAIR PASTA
2 OZ SHREDDED FONTINA CHEESE
1/2 OZ C0OKED BACON
1/2 OZ SAUTEED DICED ONION
2 OZ SPINACH DIP
6 oz of egg mix

Directions:
TO PREPARE EGG MIXTURE:  BLEND EGG MIX W/WHISK
TO PREPARE FRITTATA:  SPRAY 8" DISH WITH COOKING SPRAY. IN THIS ORDER PLACE THE FOLLOWING INGREDIENTS
SPRINLE PARMESEAN CHEESE IN BOTTOM OF DISH, ADD ANGEL HAIR PASTA, FONTINA CHEESE, BACON, ONIONS, SPINACH DIP AND POUR EGG MIXTURE OVER IT
PLACE IN OVEN AND BAKE AT 325 FOR 30-40 MINUTES
CUT AROUND EDGE OF DISH WITH KNIFE AND IT WILL POP OUT
SERVE W/POTATOES
