Title:
Dessert Crispys Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup white sugar
2 tbsp. cinnamon
1 flour tortilla
1 tbsp.vegetable oil for frying

Directions:
Cut the tortilla into 15 small squares and place them in a large frying pan on high. Stir fry them until they turn a light brown. Set aside.
Combine the sugar and cinnamon in a plastic bag and shake until mixed. Coat the squares with sugar.  Serve warm.

Submitter: 
PURPLEMAGGOT
Description: 
Theese are like dessert chips, and have a nice crunch.
