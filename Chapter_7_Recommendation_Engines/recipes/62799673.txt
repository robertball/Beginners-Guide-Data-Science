Title:
Grandma Bonnie's Chocolate Zucchini Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tbsp cocoa powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp cinnamon
2 cups freshly shredded zucchini
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1 3/4 cups white sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 cup soured milk or buttermilk

Directions:
Cream the butter and sugar.
Add the eggs, vanilla, and sour milk.
In a seperate bowl, mix together the cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, zucchini, flour, and chocolate chips.
Add to the first bowl.
Bake in a preheated oven of 350 degrees Farenheit for 45 minutes or until fully cooked.
Let cool.
Frost with a chocolate icing.

Submitter: 
Kaley
Description: 
My grandmother would always bake this delishously rich chocolate cake for us. Amazingly chocolately, moist and rich, this cake is not only delish but also would trick us kids into eating some zucchini!
