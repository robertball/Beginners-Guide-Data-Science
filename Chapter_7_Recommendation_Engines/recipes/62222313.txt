Title:
Lemon Sugar Snaps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 butter, softened
1 egg
1 pck 2 layer lemon cake
1 c cornmeal
2 T lemon zest
coarse sugar

Directions:
Beat butter and egg 30 sec. Slowly beat in cake mix, then cornmeal and lemon zest. Roll into 1" balls and roll in turbinado sugar. Patt 2" apart on  ungreased baking sheet. Transfer to wire rack after baking
9-10 min @ 375
