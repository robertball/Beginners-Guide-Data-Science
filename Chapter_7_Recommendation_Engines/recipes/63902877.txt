Title:
simple steak nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1083293.jpg
Ingredients:
24 unbroken large tortilla chips
24 slices of pickled jalapeno peppers
8oz grilled steak cut in 24 bite sized pieces
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350ยบ
Arrange tortilla chips on a nonstick cookie sheet
Top each chip with a piece of steak.
Top each piece of steak with a jalapeno pepper slice
Sprinkle cheese over all the chips
bake for 10 mins and serve

Submitter: 
Jane Livengood
Description: 
The jalapenos and the juice that runs off them gives these simple baked nachos a clean peppery flavor.Great for a light meal or appetizer.
