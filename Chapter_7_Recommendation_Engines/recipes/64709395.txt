Title:
Instant pot taco beef for taco bowls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5544314.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp oil
2 pounds beef stew meat
1/3 cup Savory Spice shop taco seasoning
1/2 onion, sliced
2 tsp garlic salt
1/4 cup water
Assorted toppings:
Cooked Mexican rice
Black beans
Corn
Salsa
Sour cream
Cheddar cheese
Avocado
Cilantro

Directions:
In a pressure cooker, hit the sauté mode, and heat oil. Add stew meat and sear. Add the seasonings, water, and sliced onion. Stir to combine.
Switch to manual mode and high pressure. Put lid on pressure cooker and cook on high pressure for 30 minutes. Once done - allow pressure to release naturally.
In the meantime, gather any toppings you may want, and heat them up if needed - I use black beans, corn, salsa, cooked yellow rice, cheddar cheese, sour cream, etc.,
If using yellow rice, cook according to package directions, once cooked place in bottom of bowl, top with about 5 ounces cooked beef stew meat, and  toppings of your choice.

Submitter: 
thedailygourmet
Description: 
Pressure cooking beef stew meat with taco seasoning allows for tender chunks of beef which  can be used for taco bowls. It’s makes enough to allow for a week of prepped lunches.
