Title:
Stuffed Bagels, the best! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large; day-old bagels (from a bakery or deli department at grocery store)
1 (1 lb.) tube raw breakfast sausage (Owens brand), (hot or mild) Â– uncooked!
3 cups grated cheddar cheese

Directions:
Split bagels in half horizontally.
With a fork, hollow out the bagel halves, reserving the bread pieces and the shells separately.
In a large mixing bowl, combine the reserved bread pieces, uncooked sausage and cheese, mixing with hands.
Stuff sausage mixture into bagel shells and place on baking sheets, stuffing side up.
Place baking sheets in freezer until frozen or overnight.
Transfer the frozen bagels to a large zip-loc plastic bag store in freezer until ready to bake.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees, place FROZEN bagels on baking sheets bake for 20-25 minutes, or until sausage is cooked through and stuffing is bubbly.
Remove from oven to let set and cool just enough to eat or if desired, to cut into small serving size pieces.

Submitter: 
Sue's recipes
Description: 
Must be prepared ahead of time and frozen.

Have these stuffed breakfast bagels ready and waiting in your freezer when company comes for the weekend.
