Title:
Heavenly Hamburger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Brown:
1 lb. ground beef in
2 T. butter
add:
1 clove garlic, minced
15 oz tomato sauce
Cook 15 -20 minutes covered
Cook and drain according to package
8 oz. egg noodles
Mix:
6 green onions, chopped
3 oz. cream cheese softened
1 cup sour cream
topping:
1/2 c. shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Butter a small casserole dish, layer starting with noodles and ending with last layer of sauce, top with cheese.

Marie Mako

Lasagna type casserole with egg noodles!
