Title:
Strawberry Crush Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/878186.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2c crushed pineapple
1/2 c water
1/4c lemon juice
3T sugar
1T flaxseed
2T honey
1/2 medium banana (broken into pieces)
2c frozen strawberries
1/2 peach
3 ice cubes (optional)

Directions:
Place ingredients in blender in the order given and blend thoroughly.

Submitter: 
LKENYON
Description: 
This is a high calorie recipe as written, approximately 337 per 16oz serving. I've cut the sugar back to 3-4T however, and it has still tasted just fine. Works best when the berries are frozen.
