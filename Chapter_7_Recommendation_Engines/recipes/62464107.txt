Title:
Jennifer's Best Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chopped onions
6 TBS extra virgin olive oil
4-6 garlic cloves
2 pounds ground beef
2 28 ounce cans whole tomatoes
1 15 ounce can tomato sauce
1 6 ounce can tomato paste
3 TBS dried oregano
2 TBS dried basil
1 tsp dried thyme
2 bay leaves
4 tsp sugar
2 tsp salt
black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large pot over med-high heat saute onions in oil until soft.  Add garlic and ground beef.  Cook until beef is browned then drain off fat.  While beef is browning run the whole tomatoes through a blender or food processor and puree them.  Add tomato paste to beef.  Stir and cook until paste starts to darken a bit.  Then add pureed tomatoes and remaining ingredients.  Bring to a boil then lower heat and simmer for an hour.  Remove bay leaves before serving.
