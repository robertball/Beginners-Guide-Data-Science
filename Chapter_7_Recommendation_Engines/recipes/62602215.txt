Title:
Mexican Style Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/564518.jpg
Ingredients:
4 lb Beef Chuck Roast
2 - 14.5 oz Cans Beef Broth + 2 cans filled with water
1 - 14.5 oz Can Diced Tomatoes w/ Green Chiles
1 White Onion, sliced
4 Carrots, cleaned and sliced
5 Large Potatoes, cleaned and cubed
1 Chayote, cleaned, cored and cubed
6 Cloves Garlic, minced
1-1/2 Teaspoons Ground Chile Ancho Powder
1 Teaspoon Mexican Oregano
1 Teaspoon Seasoned Salt
1/2 Teaspoon Cumin
1/2 Teaspoon Ground Habanero Powder
1/2 Teaspoon Thyme
1/2 Teaspoon White Pepper
1/4 Teaspoon Coriander

Directions:
Place meat into slow cooker.  Add vegetables to surround meat, pour can of tomatoes over vegetables.  In a bowl combine spices, mix well.  Sprinkle spice mixture over meat and vegetables.  Add beef broth and water. Cook on high for 7-8 hours.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This is a great meal to throw in your slow cooker on a busy day.  Try serving it like a soup over rice with limes, tortillas and hotsauce.
