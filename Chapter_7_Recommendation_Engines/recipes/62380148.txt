Title:
Apple Pie Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Milk
1 egg yolk, reserve white
2 1/2 C flour
1 t. salt
1 C butter, softened
1 C crushed corn flakes
8 C peeled, cored, 1/4" sliced tart apples (~8-10 med. Apples)
1 C sugar
1 1/2 t. ground cinnamon
1/2 t. ground nutmeg
1 egg white
2 T sugar
1/2 t. ground cinnamon
1 C pdr. Sugar
1-2 T milk
1/2 t. vanilla

Directions:
Crust: Preheat oven to 350Â°
In sm MB, add milk to yolk to measure 2/3 C; set aside.
In med. MB, combine flour and salt. Cut in butter until crumbly.
With fork, stir in milk mixture until dough forms a ball. Divide in two.
Roll out 1/2 of dough on lightly floured surface, into 15x10" rectangle.
Place on bottom of ungreased CS
Filling: Sprinkle cereal over bottom crust. Layer apples over cereal.
In sm. MB, combine 1 C sugar, 1 1/2 t. cinnamon, and nutmeg.
Sprinkle over apples.
Roll out other 1/2 of dough into 15.5x10.5" rectangle. Place over apples
In sm MB, beat egg white w/ fork until foamy; brush over crust.
In sm. MB, combine 2 T sugar, 1/2 t. cinnamon. Sprinkle over crust.
Bake for 45-60 min., or until lightly browned.
Glaze: In sm. MB, combine ingredients and drizzle over warm bars. Cut.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
These taste just like apple pie. They are a great treat if you want to make apple pie for a lot of people. You can also eat them with you hands, so they are great for a party.
