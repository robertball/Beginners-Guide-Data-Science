Title:
Chli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground beef
2 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 large can diced tomatoes (3 small cans)
1 (15 ounce) can corn
2 cloves garlic
2 stalks celery
1 1/2 cups chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
1/8 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon white sugar
1/2 teaspoon dried Italian Seasoning
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon smoked salt
2 teaspoons ground cumin
3.5 tablespoons chili powder
2 tablespoons cajun spice

Directions:
Place ground beef in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown. Drain, and crumble.
In a large pot over high heat combine the ground beef, tomato juice, tomato sauce, kidney beans, pinto beans, onions, bell pepper, cayenne pepper, sugar, oregano, ground black pepper, salt, cumin and chili powder. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Simmer for 1 1/2 hours. (Note: If using a slow cooker, set on low, add ingredients, and cook for 8 to 10 hours.)

Submitter: 
JMASTRON
Description: 
This is a good, standard chili, with lots of ground beef, tomatoes, kidney  and onions.  It needs to simmer for 90 minutes on the stove, or can be made in a slow cooker and left to simmer all day.Modified from: It's Chili by George!!
