Title:
BBQ Beef Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
2 c. barbeque sauce
1 c. ketchup
1 tsp. cinnamon
hot sauce (to taste)
shredded cheddar cheese
hamburger rolls

Directions:
Brown ground beef in skillet, then drain.
Add BBQ sauce, ketchup and cinnamon to beef.
Spice it up with some hot sauce (to taste).
Stir and cook over medium heat until heated throughout.
Serve on rolls (I toast mine and they come out extra good!) with some extra hot sauce topped with shredded cheese.

Submitter: 
Traveling_Is_Love
Description: 
Beef simmered in a thick sweet barbeque mixture then topped with cheese served on a toasted bun makes a great and very FAST sandwich any time of the year!  Also, refrigerates and reheats wonderfully.
