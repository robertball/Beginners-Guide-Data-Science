Title:
Never Fail Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
2/3 tsp salt
3 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup milk
1 cup flour
2 Tbls cornstarch

Directions:
Beat egg and milk.
Combine all the dry ingredients and mix with wet.
Drop by small spoonfuls into stew or soup.
Cook 15 minutes, covered.

Submitter:

Description:
