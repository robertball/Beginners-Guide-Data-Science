Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites - MDJM version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
11 Oreo cookies
3 kg of kale
Sock-monkey spit
1 vile of blood from an Angel
Lots of Cheese

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 180 Celsius, crush all the Oreo cookies and mix them with the sock-monkeys spit.
Hack the kale and cook it i the Angel's blood for about an hour.
Spinkle the smushed cookies on top of it all, then add cheese ... lots of it and put it all into the oven for 20 minutes.
Best served hot with lots of red wine.

Submitter: 
Jasmin Schmidt
Description: 
I got this recipe form my great-grandmothers uncle on my mothers side, who carried it Christopher Walken like until she could pass it on.
