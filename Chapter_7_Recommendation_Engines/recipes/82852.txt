Title:
Ham Spread Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pound diced turkey ham
1/3 cup chopped onion
2 teaspoons dill pickle relish
1 teaspoon prepared mustard
1/4 cup low-fat creamy salad dressing (e.g. Miracle Whip™)
16 slices SARA LEE® Soft & Smooth™ Whole Grain White Bread
8 leaves lettuce

Directions:
Place the ham into a food processor, grind on pulse until very coarse. Add the onion and dill pickle relish close to the end of this stage. Put into mixing bowl and stir in the mustard and creamy salad dressing until well blended.
Spoon about 1/2 cup of salad onto 8 slices of bread. Top with a lettuce leaf and remaining slices of bread.

Submitter: 
Sara Lee Soft & Smooth™
Description: 
Turkey ham is used to make this creamy 'ham spread' with dill pickles. Served on toasted bread with lettuce, this sandwich is a winner.
