Title:
Moo Shu Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1T sesame oil
2 cloves chopped garlic
1 bag shredded coleslaw mix
1 bag shredded carrots
4 scallions, sliced
1/4 c hoisin sauce
2T low sodium soy sauce
10 6" tortillas
3c shredded cooked chicken (Rotisserie)

Directions:
Heat oil over medium heat.
Add garlic, coleslaw, carrot, and scallions.
Cook for 8 minutes, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are softened.
Stir in hoisin sauce and soy sauce and cook 2 minutes.
Add chicken and stir to heat through.
Heat tortillas according to package directions.
Spoon 1/2c chicken mixture into tortilla and roll to serve.
