Title:
Bulgur Wheat with Dried Cranberries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Water
1/2 cup Bulgur Wheat
1 1/2 Tbs. Chicken Bouillion granules or 2 Chicken Bouillion cubes
1 tsp. Butter or Margarine
1/4 cup Dried Cranberries or dried fruit of your choice, Raisins, Dried Apricots, Dried Cherries etc.

Directions:
In Medium Saucepan with lid, bring 1 cup water to boil, pour in 1/2 cup Bulgur Wheat, Butter and Chicken Bouillion.
Cover and lower heat to simmer and cook over low heat for 15 minutes.
When done, fluff with a fork and stir in the Dried Cranberries or Dried Fruit of your choice.
Re-heats very well in the microwave also

Submitter: 
GRAYJETMAN
Description: 
Very basic yet tasty side dish that goes well with grilled or roasted chicken. Any type of dried fruit can be used in place of the dried cranberries.
I use regular chicken bouillion because my Sodium level does not register when I have blood drawn.
