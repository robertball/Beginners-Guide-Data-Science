Title:
Paleo (and vegan!) pumpkin spice creamer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can full fat organic coconut milk
3 tbsp organic pumpkin puree
3 tbsp organic maple syrup
1 heaping tsp pumpkin pie spice
3 tbsp flax milk (or other non-dairy milk) to thin out the coconut milk a bit

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl and whisk.
Place in a glass Mason jar or bottle and store in refrigerator.
Shake gently prior to using to ensure it is well-mixed.
Enjoy! Perfect for a fall day! Keeps in the refrigerator for one week.

Submitter: 
Brooke Mulford
Description: 
The perfect creamer for fall! All of the good stuff and none of the fake stuff!
