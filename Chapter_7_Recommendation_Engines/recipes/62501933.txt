Title:
Pumpkin Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15oz can of pumpkin
1 15oz can canellini (white kidney) beans, rinsed/drained
1 14oz can unsweetened coconut milk
1 14oz can vegetable broth
1 tsp dried sage, crushed
salt
ground black pepper
small lime halves (optional)
cracked black pepper (optional)

Directions:
1.  In medium saucepan, combine pumpkin, beans, coconut milk, broth and sage.  Bring to boil; reduce heat and simmer uncovered until heated through.
2.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.  If desired, squeeze lime half over each serving and sprinkle with cracked black pepper
