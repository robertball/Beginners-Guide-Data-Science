Title:
Salted Nut Roll Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1pd. salted peanuts
14oz.sweetened condensed milk
12oz. Peanut butter chips
7oz. Marshmellow creme (fluff)

Directions:
1. Put 1/2 pound of peanuts on bottom of 9X13 pan. Combine chips and milk until chips have melted.
2. Sitr in Marshmellow creme.
3. Pour over peanuts. Layer rest of peanuts over mixture and press in.
3. Chill for 2 hours before cutting into bars.
