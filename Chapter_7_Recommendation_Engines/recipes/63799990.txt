Title:
Egg Nog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 eggs, separated
2 c. sugar
2 qts. milk
2 pt. rum (if desired)
1/2 gal. vanilla ice cream
nutmeg to taste

Directions:
Beat egg whites until stiff, then gradually add 1 c. sugar.
Beat egg yolks in a separate bowl and add 1 c. sugar. Add 2 qt. milk and beat slowly. Add rum, if desired, to taste. Add ice cream.
Fold egg whites into egg yolk mixture. Sprinkle top with nutmeg.

Submitter: 
Julie Tramp
Description: 
An easy and delicious holiday egg nog recipe.
