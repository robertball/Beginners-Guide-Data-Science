Title:
Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/659069.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Large Can of Canned Chicken (drained)
1/2 Cup of Frank's Hot Sauce
1 Cup Light ranch dressing
1/2 Cup 2% Cheddar Cheese Crumbles
1 8 ounce box of 1/3 less fat cream cheese

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, heat and serve. Can heat in a microwave until ingredients blendable but this is best served in a crock pot. Should be warm after an hour on high then can be turned down to low to keep warm.
This makes about 18 1/4 cup servings. Nutritional info is for 2 ounce servings/ 1/4 cup servings.
Number of Servings: 18

Submitter: 
Sarah Meedel
Description: 
A quick and easy dip for potlucks, camping, parties or any other time. You can make add more spice with more hot sauce or make it runnier with more dressing.
