Title:
Hungarian Sausage Fried Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Tbsp. Cooking Oil
4 Tbsp. Chopped Onion
1 Tbsp. Chopped Garlic
4 Tbsp. Chopped Tomato
1 pc. Hungarian Sausage, Medium Diced
1 Cup Rice
4 Tbsp. Chopped (Cooked/Sauteed) Asparagus
All Purpose Liquid Seasoning, As Needed
1 Tsp. Italian Seasoning
1 pc. Egg, beaten
Salt, As Needed

Directions:
Preheat oil in a saute pan over medium low heat
Saute onion, garlic and tomato till onion is tender
Add in sausage and sautee till sausages are cooked
Add in rice and mix well.
Add in Asparagus and season with liquid  seasoning, italian seasoning and salt. Mix well.
Add in egg and mix until egg is cooked. Adjust seasoning if needed. Remove from heat and serve hot.

Submitter: 
Erica Aquino
Description: 
A quick, easy and peppery-delicious way to ustilize left over hungarian sausages... rice... and some other stuff. Enjoy!
