Title:
Slow Cooker Pulled Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (2 pound) pork tenderloin
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle Dr. Pepper
1 (18 ounce) bottle of Head and County BBQ Sauce

Directions:
Place the pork tenderloin in a slow cooker; pour the Dr. Pepper over the meat. Cover and cook on low until well cooked and the pork shreds easily, 6 to 7 hours. Note: the actual length of time may vary according to individual slow cooker.  Stir in barbecue sauce. Serve over hamburger buns or toast.
