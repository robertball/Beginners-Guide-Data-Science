Title:
2 Corn-Bread Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups of flour
2 cups of corn meal
4 Tbsp. sugar
2 Tbsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
6 eggs
2 cups of cottage cheese
2 (8 oz.) cans cream style corn

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 F. Mix ingredients in order given. Put into muffins cups. Bake for 25 minutes. Cool to eat.
