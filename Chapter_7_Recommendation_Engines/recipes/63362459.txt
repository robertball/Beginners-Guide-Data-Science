Title:
Jessi's Venison Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound venison, cut into 1 inch pieces for stew
1-2 tbsp vegetable oil
3/4 large yellow onion, chopped
2 cloves fresh garlic, minced
1 bay leaf
1/4 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp coarse ground sea salt
3 large carrots, chunked
1 parsnip, chunked
5 medium size white potatoes, chunked.
3 tbsp cornstarch
1/8 tsp ground black pepper

Directions:
Add oil, onion, garlic and venison stew meat to a 5 qt dutch oven. Brown on medium heat, approx 5 min.
Add 3 cups water, bay leaf, thyme, sea salt, black pepper. Simmer for one and a half hours.
Add carrots, parsnip and potatoes. Increase heat and boil until tender.
At this point, i you would like a thicker stew, this can be thickened with cornstarch. Mix 3 tbsp cornstarch with 3 tbsp cold water per cup of liquid, stir until cornstarch is dissolved. Add to the stew and increase the heat to bring to a full boil, stirring constantly for 1-2 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jessica Schultz
Description: 
Savory and simple venison stew which can be served as is, over mashed potatoes. Another variation would make a delicious chunky soup. The whole family will love it!
