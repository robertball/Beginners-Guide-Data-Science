Title:
Jan's Chocolate Streusel Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/619281.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1/4 cup cocoa powder
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1/2 cup cold butter cut in chunks
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1 teaspoon powdered vanilla extract

Directions:
Place all ingredients in mixer bowl and whisk together until fine crumbs form.
Sprinkle on top of muffins or use as as streusel filling in cake.

Submitter: 
What's for dinner, mom?
Description: 
Rich chocolate streusel perfect for topping muffins.  Also makes a great streusel filling for coffee cake.
