Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken and Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
2 tablespoons butter
2 (10.75 ounce) cans  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 - 14 oz chicken broth
1 onion, finely diced
1 (10 ounce) packages refrigerated biscuit dough, torn into pieces
Add additional spices if you like...

Directions:
Place the chicken, butter, soup, and onion in a slow cooker, and fill with enough water to cover.
Cover, and cook for 4 hours on High, then drop the torn biscuit dough in the slow cooker and press under broth. Cook until the dough is no longer raw in the center (about an hour or so).
