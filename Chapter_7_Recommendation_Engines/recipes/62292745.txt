Title:
Gilled Salmon w/ Portobello Scallion Pasta & Sweet Peas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/359005.jpg
Ingredients:
Olive Oil, Garlic, Shallots, Portobello Mushroom, Scallions, White Wine,  Salt, White/Black Pepper, Parsley, Butter, Sweet Peas, Diced Tomatoes, 1/2 Lb Pasta(before cooking), cooked and drained.  12 oz Norwegian Salmon (or fish of your choice) in 2 - 6oz filets.

Directions:
Heat medium to large saute pan on medium-high and add about 2 Tbsp Olive Oil.  Add 2 tsp Garlic, 2 Tbsp Shallots, Mushroom Pieces (1 Large Cap, chopped into chunks), and 2 Scallions, chopped.  Brown slightly.  Deglaze with 2 C White Wine.  Season to taste with Salt and Black/White Pepper.  Add Butter, 1 Tbsp fresh Parsley and 1/4 C frozen Sweet Peas.  Cook until reduced by 1/3.  Add cooked pasta and reduce heat to medium low.  Cook, tossing occasionally, until most of the sauce has absorbed into the pasta.  Serve Immediately topped with Grilled filet of Salmon and diced tomatoes.

Submitter: 
OstroskyJeremiah
Description: 
A great light tasting sauce that accompanies Salmon (or most meaty whitefish eg. Halibut, Mahi Mahi) very well.  Mushroom lovers will enjoy the subtle yet robust flavor the portobello provides.
