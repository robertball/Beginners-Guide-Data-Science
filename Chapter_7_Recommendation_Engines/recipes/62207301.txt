Title:
Classic chocolate chip cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. sugar
1 C. brown sugar
1/2 C. butter
1/2 C. shortning
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
2 C. & 4 Tbls. flour
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
12 oz choc. chips

Directions:
Combine sugar butter sugar, brown sugar, butter, vanilla, and eggs. In seperate bowl combine flour salt and soda. Gradually add to your wet sugar, and egg mixture tell well blended. Then add your choc. chips tell blended.
preheat oven to 350 degrees
bake for 8 min.
