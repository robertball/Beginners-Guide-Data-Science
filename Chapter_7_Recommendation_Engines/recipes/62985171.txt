Title:
Carrot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups slivered carrots
2 medium onion (chopped)
4 eggs (beaten)
¼ tsp pepper
½ cup melted butter
2 cup diced celery
2 cup soft bread crumbs
2 tsp salt
2 cups grated cheese

Directions:
Combine all ingredients.  Cover and bake 40 minutes at 350°F.  Remove cover and bake an additional 10 minutes.
