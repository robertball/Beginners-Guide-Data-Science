Title:
Easy Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup canola oil,
2 cups sugar,
4 eggs, well beaten,
2 cups flour,
2 tsp cinnamon,
1 tsp salt,
3 cups grated carrots,
1 cup chopped pecans,
1 stick butter,
8 ounces cream cheese, softened,
1 box powdered sugar,
1 tsp vanilla.

Directions:
Mix oil, sugar, and eggs.  Sift flour, salt, and cinnamon into mixture.  Add carrots, and nuts.  Pour into 2 greased 9 inch pans.  Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.  Cream the cream cheese and butter; add vanilla and powdered sugar; spread on cooled cake.
