Title:
Cream Cheese Sausage Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of Jimmy Dean Hot Sausage
1 (8oz) Cream Cheese w/ chive
1 bunch of scalions
1/2 jar or Pastene Roasted Red Peppers
3-4 packages of small pastry cups (these should be about the size of a quarter)
1 cup shredded sharp cheddar
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
In a large skillet, brown & crumble the sausage. Drain grease, and place on plate lined with paper towel for further draining of grease.
Drain Roasted Red Peppers on paper towel.
Dice scalions & peppers into small pieces.
Mix sausage, scalions, peppers, cream cheese, cheddar in medium mixing bowl.
Add Salt & Pepper to taste.
Using a spoon, fill each pastry cup to the top with mixture.
Depending upon taste, you may want to add extra cream cheese.
Arrange filled pastry cups on a baking sheet, and bake at 425 for 15-20 minutes, or until cheese starts to brown.
Serve Immediately.

Submitter: 
Scott in  MA
Description: 
These small pastry shells are filled with sausage, cream cheese, roasted red peppers, and scalions....a real party favorite.
