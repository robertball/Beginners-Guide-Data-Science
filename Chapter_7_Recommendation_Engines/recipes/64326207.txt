Title:
Slow Cooker Carnitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
4 large cloves minced garlic
3 teaspoon ground cumin
1 1/2 teaspoon crumbled dried oregano
1 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
1 tsp rubbed sage
2 tsp onion powder
3/4 tsp. cilantro
1 1/2 T. adobo
1 T. Chili powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
3 bay leaves
1 (4 pound) boneless pork loin
1 sliced onion
1 cup Pepsi
1 cup chicken broth
1 can Mild Rotel
3 tbs. of lime juice

Directions:
The night before cooking, cut loin into 4-6 large chunks. Mix together salt, garlic powder, cumin, oregano, coriander, sage. onion powder, cilantro, adobo, chili powder and cinnamon in a bowl. Coat pork with the spice mixture. Wrap in plastic wrap and let sit in refrigerator overnight.
In the morning, place bay leaves in bottom of slow cooker and place the pork on top. Top pork with sliced onion. Pour the Pepsi, chicken broth, and rotel around the sides of the pork, being careful not to rinse off the spice mixture.
Cover and cook on Low until the pork shreds easily with a fork, 7-10 hours. Turn the meat after it has cooked for 4 hours. After turning, pour lime juice over top. When the pork is tender, remove from slow cooker, and shred with two forks.
Spread out in a thin layer on a cookie sheet and bake at 400 degrees about 20 minutes. Use cooking liquid as needed to moisten the meat. Save remaining liquid to use for rice.
