Title:
Bruschetta with Brie and Peach Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chopped, peeled peaches (about 4)
3/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
1/4 cup chopped green onion
2 tbsp. fresh cilantro - chopped
1 tbsp. sugar
1 tbsp. fresh lime juice
Dash of cayenne pepper
1 - 8 oz. loaf of french bread cut into 1/2" slices
4 oz. Brie - cut into 24 pieces

Directions:
Combine first 7 ingredients and set aside.
Preheat broiler.
Arrange bread slices on baking sheet and top with 1 piece of cheese.
Broil 3 min. or until cheese melts and bread is toasted.
Remove from oven and top with salsa.
Serve immediately.
