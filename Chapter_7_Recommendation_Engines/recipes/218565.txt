Title:
Veggie Pizza Faces Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989310.jpg
Ingredients:
4 whole wheat pita bread rounds (6-inch pitas work best)
1/2 cup pizza sauce
1 cup shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese
Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Light Dressing (as much as needed)
Assortment of fresh vegetables pre-sliced and ready for use: rainbow carrots, tomatoes, zucchini, bell peppers, broccoli and olives)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Place the pita bread rounds on a non-stick baking sheet.
Spread 2 tablespoons of pizza sauce onto each pita round; sprinkle with cheese.
Use the Hidden Valley(R) Original Ranch(R) Light Dressing as the glue to add assorted vegetables to the pizzas. Take your time and create silly faces with the veggies.
Bake the pizzas for 10 to 12 minutes or until cheese melts.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
It's okay to play with your food when creating fresh veggie faces for these quick and easy pizzas.
