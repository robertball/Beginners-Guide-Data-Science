Title:
Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Loaf of French bread, sliced in half lengthwise
garlic to taste
Margarine
Parmesan Cheese
Oregano

Directions:
Butter bread. Add garlic, cheese and oregano. Put under broiler until lightly brown.
