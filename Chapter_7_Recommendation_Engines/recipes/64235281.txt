Title:
Gluten free Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. vegetable shortening
2 c. plus 2 Tbsp. brown rice flour mix
1 1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 c. brown sugar
1 Tbsp vanilla
1 c. finely chopped walnuts (optional)
1 tsp. Xantham gum
1 c sugar
2 lg eggs
12 oz. chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375ยบ.  Lightly grease 2 lg. baking sheets.
Whisk together flour mix, baking soda, salt, and Xanthan gum in a small bowl.
Beat together shortening and sugars in a large bowl with an electric mixer at hight speed until pale and fluffy, 2 to 3  minutes.
Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
Beat in vanilla.
Reduce speed to low and mix in flour until just incorporated, then stir in chips and walnuts.
Drop heaping teaspoons of dough 2 inches apart onto baking sheets.
Bake until golden, 9 to 15 minutes.
Let stand 1 minute, then transfer cookies to a rack to cool and continue making cookies using cooled cookie sheets.
