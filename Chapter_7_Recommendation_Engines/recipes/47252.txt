Title:
Cherry Walnut Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (4 ounce) package brown and wild rice mix
1 (16.5 ounce) can pitted dark sweet cherries, drained
1/2 cup cucumber, seeded and diced
1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
1/2 cup chopped canned water chestnuts
1/4 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 teaspoon grated orange peel
1 tablespoon chopped onion
1 clove garlic, minced

Directions:
Prepare rice mix according to package directions in 1 1/2-quart saucepan. In large bowl, combine remaining ingredients.  Add rice, mix in gently and serve.
