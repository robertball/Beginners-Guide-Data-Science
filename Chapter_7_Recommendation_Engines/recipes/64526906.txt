Title:
Mississippi Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Well Marbled Beef Roast
1 Packet Ranch Dressing Mix
1 Packet Au Jus Gravy Mix
1/2  Stick of Butter
1 Pound of Peppercinis ( I substituted 3 Diced Potatoes, 1/2 onion and 1/2 cup baby carrots)
1 Cup beef broth/stock

Directions:
1. Place Roast In Crock pot andplace all veggies around the roast.
2. Pour beef broth in.
3. Sprinkle whole packet of ranch over roast and sprinkle whole packet of au jus over roast.
4. place 1/2 stick of butter on top
cook on low for 6-8 hours until internal temp reads 165.
I served with a simple salad to counter the fat and carbs of the roast and veggies

Submitter: 
Ashley Mills
Description: 
This delicious roast recipe was given to me by a family I encountered during my job.  After looking to see if I could find a recipe on here (I do love the reviews), I decided to post this.
