Title:
Chocolate Truffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup heavy cream or half and half
1/4 cup butter
8 oz of chocolate chips or bars

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a saucepan until melted and stir until smooth. DO NOT BOIL. Chill for at least one hour. Roll into balls and dust it with cocoa powder, powered sugar, nuts or sprinkles.

Submitter: 
Danielle Kroll
Description: 
This is a recipe I received at a candy making class. It is super fun and easy.
