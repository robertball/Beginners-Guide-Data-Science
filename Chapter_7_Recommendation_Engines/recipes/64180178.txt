Title:
beef Lasagna Roll Ups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package uncooked lasagna noodles
1 pound mozzarella cheese, shredded
1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
1 (2 lb) ground meat
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach - thawed, drained and squeezed dry
2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
1 (28 ounce) jar pasta sauce

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles for 5 to 8 minutes, or until just slightly underdone; drain and rinse.
In a large mixing bowl, mix together grated cheese, ricotta cheese, meat,  frozen spinach, and 1 cup Parmesan cheese.
Lay out a noodle. Spread a layer of the cheese mixture on the noodle, then add a thin layer of sauce.  Roll the noodle up, and place seam side down in a 13x9 pan.  Repeat for other noodles. Freeze individually. Thaw prior to baking.
Top with sauce and Parmesan cheese.
Bake in a preheated 350 degree F (175 degree C) oven  for 30 min, or until hot and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Nanna Kathy
Description: 
Mozzarella, Parmesan, and ricotta cheese are mixed with spinach to make a substantial filling for this lasagna variation.Modified from: Lasagna Roll Ups
