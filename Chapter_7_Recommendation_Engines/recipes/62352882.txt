Title:
Steph's Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/398922.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups all purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
2/3 cup peanut butter chips
1/2 cup milk chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Beat butter and peanut butter in large bowl. Add granulated sugar and brown sugar; beat well. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Blend in vanilla.
Stir together the flour, baking powder, and salt; mix into peanut butter mixture. Blend well. Stir in peanut butter and chocolate chips.
Form 1 inch balls and place on cookie sheet about 2 inches apart. Bake for approximately 10-12 minutes in preheated oven. Pull cookies out of oven when brown around edges. Allow cookies to cool on sheet.
