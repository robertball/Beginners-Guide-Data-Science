Title:
Brownie Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sq. unsweetened chocolate
125 ml  butter
250 ml  sugar
2.5 ml  vanilla
2     eggs
125 ml  flour
2.5 ml  salt
125 ml  chopped walnuts

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 F.
Melt chocolate.
Cream butter and vanilla. Add sugar gradually and cream until light and fluffy.
Add eggs one at a time, beating well.Blend in melted chocolate.
Add flour and salt and nuts. Mix well. Spread in 8x8 pan. Bake 40-45 minutes.
Spread with icing and nuts.
