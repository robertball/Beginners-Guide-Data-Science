Title:
Crunchy Potato Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds new potatoes, cut into 1/4 inch thick pieces
3 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons cumin seed
2 teaspoons curry powder
2 teaspoons gram flour
2 teaspoons salt
1 teaspoon ginger powder
1 teaspoon cayenne powder

Directions:
Wash potatoes and wrap whole potatoes in wet papertowels and microwave for 7 min (for 2 lbs. of potatoes). Cut into bite size pieces, leaving the skin on.
Heat oil in a large saute pan over medium-high heat. Saute the cumin for 1 minute until golden brown. Add potatoes, curry powder, gram flour, ginger powder, cayenne powder, and salt. Saute until toasted. Toss potatoes until crispy and serve hot.
