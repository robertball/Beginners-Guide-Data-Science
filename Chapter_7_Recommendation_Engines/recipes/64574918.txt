Title:
Easy Red Beans and Rice Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3588668.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb smoked sausage, diced
½ C onion, diced
2 stalks celery, diced
1 bell pepper, diced
4 tsp salt-free cajun seasoning
1 (27 oz) can kidney beans, undrained
1 (10 oz) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers (such as Ro*Tel)
1 pkg Knorr Rice Sides, Mushroom Flavor
1 ¼ C water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400°. Saute smoked sausage, onion, celery, bell pepper, and cajun seasoning in 12” cast iron pan until softened- approximately 5 minutes.
Stir in undrained kidney beans, diced tomatoes with green chile peppers, contents of Knorr Rice Sides packet, and water. Cover pan tightly with tin foil and place in oven. Bake for 20 minutes.
Remove tin foil and bake an additional 5 minutes.
