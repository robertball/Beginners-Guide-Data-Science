Title:
Cream Dill Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4525506.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup sour cream
3/4 cup mayonnaise
2 tablespoons finely chopped green onions
2 teaspoons dried dill weed
2 tablespoons lemon juice

Directions:
In a medium bowl combine sour cream, mayonnaise, green onions, dill and lemon juice.  Mix well and chill for at least 1 hour.

Submitter: 
sal
Description: 
This is a tangy sauce of sour cream, mayonnaise, green onions, dill weed and lemon juice.  Serve over potatoes, or as a dip for vegetables.
