Title:
Crockpot Italian Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion diced
1/2 cup of cooking sherry
2 cloves garlic
1 package of Italian dressing/marinade mix
1 can of condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 8 ounce package of low fat creme cheese
1/3 cup of chicken broth
1 14 oz can artichoke hearts quartered/diced
1 14 oz can of diced tomatoes drained
2 pounds of chicken breast tenders

Directions:
Saute onion, dressing mix and garlic in sherry in sauce pan. Add soup, creme cheese and broth until blended. Add artichokes and tomatoes and stir until well blended.
Spray crock pot with cooking spray and place chicken at the bottom of crock pot and cover with sauce. Cook on high for 3 hours or low for 6 hours.

Submitter: 
Brenda Brown
Description: 
Crock pot chicken in a creamy white sauce with artichokes and diced tomatoes. Serve over rice or your favorite pasta.
