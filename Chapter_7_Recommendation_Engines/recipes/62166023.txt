Title:
Chipotle Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12oz package macaroni or twists
4 small to medium chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, finely chopped
1 1/2 cup whole milk
4 oz. sharp cheddar cheese
4 oz. Monterey Jack cheese
8 oz. Velveeta
2 TBLSP flour
1/2 stick butter
1/2 tsp Fresh cracked black pepper
Bread crumbs (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Cook macaroni, drain and set aside.
Melt butter in pasta pot.
Add flour and stir for about 20 to 30 seconds.
Add milk, and then whisk until starts to thicken.
Add cheeses and whisk till smooth.
Add chipotle peppers and fresh black pepper.
Pour 3/4 mixture over cooked macaroni in a large greased casserole dish. (May not need to use all of cheese sauce. Extra is great for dipping with chips)
Stir to combine and then top (optional) with bread crumbs.
Bake uncovered for about 20 minutes.
Turn off oven leave door closed and let rest in oven for 20 minutes.
Cover if needed for last 20 mins.
Serve

Submitter: 
MitchJGray
Description: 
Bring in the smokey heat of Latin chipotle peppers to this southern favorite. Pairs great with grilled or barbequed meats anytime of year.
