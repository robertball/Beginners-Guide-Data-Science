Title:
Orzo and broccoli pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TB. butter
2 TB. olive oil
1 cup orzo, uncooked
1 14 oz. can chicken broth
1 10 oz. pkg. frozen chopped broccoli, thawed
Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
In a medium size sauce pan, heat butter and olive oil.
Saute broccoli and orzo for approximately 10 minutes
Add chicken broth and bring to a boil.
Reduce heat and cover and simmer for approximately 20 minutes or until broth is absorbed, stirring frequently.
Season with salt and pepper

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
This delicious side dish is so easy and quick to make.  Try experiment each time by adding different fresh herbs.  Fresh broccoli can be substituted for the frozen.
