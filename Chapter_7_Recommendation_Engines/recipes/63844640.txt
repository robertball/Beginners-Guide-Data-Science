Title:
Pumpkin Coconut Curry with White Fish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBSP coconut oil
1 small yellow onion
1 tsp minced ginger
2 garlic cloves minced
15 ox can pumpkin puree
1 cup vegetable broth
1 cup coconut milk
2 tsp curry powder
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
5 lime leaves
1 1/2 cup frozen peas
1 lb white fish (cod, halibut, black cod)
2 tsp sea salt

Directions:
in cast iron skillet or high sided skillet heat coconut oil. add onions and sauté over medium heat until onion are translucent, about 5 minutes. Add ginger and garlic, sauté for 2-3 minutes. Stir in pumpkin broth and coconut milk. Add curry powder, cayenne, lime leaves and peas, cover the skillet and simmer on medium while preparing fish. Cut fish into 1 in chunks and add to the skillet add salt, cover and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
Serve over forbidden black rice for a beautiful contrast.
