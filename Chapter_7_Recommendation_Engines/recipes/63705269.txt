Title:
Skillet Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c corn, cut fresh from the cob (or frozen)
1/2 tsp salt
pepper to taste
1 tbs sugar
1/4 c butter
1/2 c water
1 tbs flour
1/4 milk

Directions:
Combine corn, salt, pepper, sugar, butter and water in a 10-inch skillet.
Cover and simmer 15 minutes on medium heat, stirring occasionally.
Combine flour with milk, blending until smooth.  Stir into corn.
Cook five more minutes, stirring occasionally.
