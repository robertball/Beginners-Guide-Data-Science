Title:
Philly Cheese Steak Slider for one Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2316994.jpg
Ingredients:
1 slice of red onion
1 small slice of red bell pepper
chunks of pre-made, cooked steak
2 Hawaiian bread rolls
1 slice of mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Lightly grease a griddle and cook onion & pepper for about 5 minutes, or until slightly brown.
Slice rolls in half and place vegetables inside.
Place pre-cooked steak on rolls as well.
Top with mozzarella cheese, or other cheese of choice.
Place in microwave to melt the cheese for about 15- 20 seconds.
