Title:
Peanut Butter fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1/2 cup milk
1 1/3 cups peanut butter
1 jar (7oz.) marshmallow cream

Directions:
In saucepan bring sugar and milk to a boil..boil for 3 minutes. Add PB and marshmallow cream..mix well. Quickly pour into a buttered 8 inch square pan. chill until set
