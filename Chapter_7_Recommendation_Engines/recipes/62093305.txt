Title:
Baked Pineapple Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans (16 oz.) crushed pineapple with juice
2 C. sugar
4 Tbsp. cornstarch
4 eggs, beaten lightly
2 tsp. vanilla
1 Tbsp. butter for dotting on top
cinnamon

Directions:
Mix cornstarch and sugar together until blended. (to prevent lumping)
Add the remaining ingredients and mix well. Pour into a large  lightly buttered casserole dish and dot with butter and sprinkle with cinnamon.
Bake 350 degrees covered for 1 hour

An easy to make side dish for a ham dinner or brunch menu. Attractive presentation
