Title:
Fenugreek Meat Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons of olive oil
1 lb beef, goat or lamb cut into 1/2 inch cubes
2 medium onions, chopped
4 cloves of garlic, crushed.
1 small hot pepper, finely chopped
2 teaspoons ground fenugreek seeds
8 cups of water
2 medium potatoes diced into 1" cubes
2 medium carrots, chopped.
1 Cup fresh or frozen green peas
2 teaspoons of salt
1 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon cumin
4 tablespoons of finely chopped fresh cilantro (coriander) leaves

Directions:
Heat oil in saucepan and saute' meat, onions, garlic, hot pepper and fenugreek over medium heat/ow heat for 12 minutes.
Add water and bring to a boil.
Cover and cook over medium heat for 30 minutes.
Add remaining ingredients, except cilantro, and bring to a boil.
Cook for another 50 minutes or until both vegetables and meat are well cooked.
Stir in cilantro and serve.

Submitter: 
Frugal Dusky
Description: 
Fenugreek is considered to have many health benefits the world over. Knit it together flawlessly with this yummy meat stew.
Try it with beef, lamb, or even goat!
