Title:
Red Cabbage Salad II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup canola oil
2/3 cup red wine vinegar
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 medium sized sweet onion, chopped
1 head red cabbage, cored and shredded

Directions:
In a bowl, mix the canola oil, red wine vinegar, sugar, salt & pepper. Place cabbage and onion in a large bowl. Pour dressing over cabbage, and toss to coat. Cover, and refrigerate 8 hours, or overnight, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Trish McQuhae
Description:
