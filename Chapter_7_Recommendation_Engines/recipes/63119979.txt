Title:
Swedish Friendship Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
(mix by hand)
2C sugar
2C flour
1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp soda
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1 (20 oz) can crushed pineapple (juice included)
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
Frosting:
(mix with mixer)
1 (8 oz) pkg cream cheese (softened)
1 stick butter
3C sifted powdered sugar
2 tsp vanilla
1C chopped pecans (optional)

Directions:
for cake:
Preheat oven to 350. Grease & flour a 9x13" pan. Mix ingredients in order given by hand until well combined. Pour into prepared pan and bake for 40 minutes. cool completely before frosting.
for frosting:
Beat cream cheese & butter gradually add sugar 1/2 C at a time. Add vanilla. If using pecans stir in by hand. Frost cooled cake & enjoy!

Moist & delicious, this cake is a sure bet if you're looking for a quick & easy (but not overly typical) crowd pleaser! Handed down from my Grandmother it's a tried & true favorite!
