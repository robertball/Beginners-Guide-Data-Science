Title:
Jan's Loaded Pizza Fries Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/572262.jpg
Ingredients:
1 32 ounce bag of frozen french fries
1 - 1 1/2 cups ready made pizza sauce (or to taste)
1 12 0z bag shredded mozzarella cheese
1 8 0z bag pepperoni
1 tsp. oregano
1 tsp dried parsley flakes

Directions:
Place french fries on a large cookie sheet covered with foil.  Spray with PAM cooking spray.
Bake fries at 400* until crispy.  About 35 - 40 minutes.
Mix pizza sauce with oregano and pour over hot fries.
Sprinkle mozzarella over the fries and sauce mixture.
Top with parsley flakes.
Place under broiler until cheese begins to brown and bubble.
Serve hot.

Description: 
Fries with pizza toppings - just like the pizza place makes!  Great snack or lunchtime treat.
