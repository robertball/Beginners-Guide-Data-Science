Title:
Crock Pot Garlic Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2880193.jpg
Ingredients:
5 lbs potatoes
3 to 4 cloves garlic, optional
1.5 cup butter
1/2 TBSP salt
1 tsp pepper
1 cup water
1.5 cups milk or sour cream

Directions:
Peel and cut potatoes in same size pieces and put into crock pot along with garlic (is using)
Cut butter into pats adding to top of potatoes all over
Add salt and pepper, then pour water over potatoes
Cook on high for 4 hours or until potatoes are tender, then lower crock pot to warm
Mash potatoes and add milk/sour cream until smooth and blended
To keep the potatoes warm, leave in the covered crock pot on the WARM setting for up to 4 hours.
