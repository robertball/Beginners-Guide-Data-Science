Title:
Almond Crescent Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter - softened
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1 cup finely chopped almonds
2 cups flour
1 tsp. almond extract
1/2 cup powdered sugar for dusting

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cream butter and sugar until fluffy.
Add almond extract and then gradually sift in flour.
Add almonds.
Divide dough into eight pieces and shape each piece into a 1/2" thick roll.
Cut each roll into 2" pieces. place on cookie sheet and form into crescent.
Bake for 18-20 minutes or until lightly brown.
Cool and dust with remaining powdered sugar.
