Title:
Applesauce Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups flour
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp allspice
1 cup applesauce
1 tsp baking soda
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
3 tablespoons white sugar
3 tablespoons brown sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 tablespoons butter, diced

Directions:
Cream together butter and sugar
Mix in egg and vanilla
Sift flour, cinnamon, and allspice
Combine with cream mixture
Add in applesauce and baking soda
Fill muffin tins 3/4 full
For streusel, combine sugar, flour and cinnamon in a small bowl; cut in butter. Sprinkle over muffins.
Bake at 375 degrees for 15 minutes
