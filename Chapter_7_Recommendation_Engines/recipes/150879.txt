Title:
Ciroc Lemon Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978169.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Ciroc® Vodka
1 1/2 ounces lemon juice
1 ounce simple syrup
Sugar
1 slice lemon

Directions:
Pour Ciroc(R) Vodka, lemon juice, and simple syrup into a shaker with crushed ice.
Shake and strain cocktail into a champagne flute rimmed with sugar; garnish with a wheel shaped slice of lemon.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Elegant cocktails taste as refined as they look when made with Ciroc® vodka, an uncommon vodka with subtle aromatics made from grapes.
