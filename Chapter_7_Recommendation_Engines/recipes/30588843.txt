Title:
Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups sifted cake flour
2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
2 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1 1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1 1/2 cups white sugar
4 eggs
1 1/4 cups vegetable/canola oil
3 cups grated carrots

Directions:
Mix eggs, oil and sugar together in a mixing bowl.  Add dry ingredients and mix well.  Add grated carrots.  Mix again until well blended.
Pour into 9x13 greased baking pan. (Pam or Baker's joy)
Bake 350 degrees aprx. 50 min. until done... (when center bounces back to touch)
