Title:
Cranberry Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 cup sugar
1-1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 cup oil
1 tsp grated orange rind
3/4 cup orange juice
1 egg, beaten
1 cup fresh cranberries, chopped
1/2 cup walnuts, chopped

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, salt and baking soda.  Add remaining ingredients.  Pour into bread pan and bake for 1 hour.
