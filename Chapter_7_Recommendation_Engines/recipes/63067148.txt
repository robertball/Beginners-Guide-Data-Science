Ingredients:
1 TBL fresh chopped garlic
1 TBL Dijon Mustard
1 TBL White Wine Vinegar
1 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
1 egg yolk
2 TBL Mayonnaise
1/3 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
6 oz Ex Virg Olive Oil (drizzle)
1 tsp kosher salt
1 tsp Black Pepper
1 TBL fresh Lemon juice
1 tsp anchovy paste or 2 whole anchovies fillets

Directions:
Combine garlic, mustard, vinegar and salt in a blender and mix thoroughly. Add mayonnaise and blend together to form a thick base. In a slow stream add olive oil through hole in lid. Scrape dressing with spatula into a bowl and season to taste with salt, pepper and lemon juice. If desired, add anchovy to dressing to create a deeper, saltier taste.

Jeffrey Morris

This delicious dressing is easy to make. It's mild and creamy texture will make your guests very happy
