Title:
Beer Cheese and Chive Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Cups Self-Rising Flour
.5  cup Sugar
1 Bottle Lager (I use Yuengling)
.75 Cup Sharp Cheddar Cheese, Finely Shredded
2 Tbsp Chives, Chopped
2 Tbsp Butter, Melted (to brush top)

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 375*F.
Spray a loaf pan with cooking spray and set aside.
In a large bowl, combine the flour and sugar.
Pour in the lager and mix until all incorporated. The mixture should be sticky.
Fold in cheese and chives.
Pour into the loaf pan and bake for 50 minutes. Remove bread and brush with butter.
Place bead back into the oven for 5 additional minutes.
Let rest in pan for 5 minutes in the pan before turning out the bread on a cooling rack until cool.
