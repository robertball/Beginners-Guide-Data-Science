Title:
Green Chili Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (7 ounce) cans whole green chile peppers, drained
1 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
1/3 cup milk
4 eggs, lightly beaten
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8x12 inch baking dish. Line the bottom of dish with green chiles. Sprinkle with Â½ cup shredded cheese. Repeat layers twice more. In a medium bowl, whisk together milk and eggs. Season with salt and pepper. Pour egg mixture over chiles and cheese. Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, or until filling is set. Let stand 5 to 10 minutes before serving.
