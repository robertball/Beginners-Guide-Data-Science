Title:
Jolly Eggnog Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons meringue powder
4 cups confectioners sugar
2 tablespoon vanilla extract
1/2 cup low-fat eggnog
Food coloring as desired

Directions:
Beat all ingedients at a low-medium speed with a hand mixer for 5-7 minutes.  Add additional eggnog or sugar as neccesary for proper consistency.
Keep covered with a wet kitchen towel during use.
For 1-3 day storage, keep in a sealed plastic bag.

Submitter: 
Tricia Vaccaro-Coburn
Description: 
Absolutely delicious icing, great for decorating holiday cookies.  Dries hard with a glossy finish.
