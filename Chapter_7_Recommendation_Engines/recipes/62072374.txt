Title:
New England Clam Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8oz) packages cream cheese, softened
2 (6.5oz) cans chopped or minced clams

Directions:
Combine softened cream cheese and 1 can of clams with juice in medium bowl.  Drain 2nd can, reserving juice, and add clams to bowl.  Add reserved juice until mixture reaches desired dipping consistency.  Dip may be chilled if desired before serving.  Serve with rippled or thick cut potato chips.

Submitter: 
RioChick
Description: 
A simple, basic recipe that's best served with plain ol' potato chips.  My mother's family has been making this for years!
