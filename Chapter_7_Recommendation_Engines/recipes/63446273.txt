Title:
Baked Scrambled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs, beaten until frothy
1/2 tsp salt
Dash pepper
1 c milk
2 tbsp butter, melted

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and pour into greased casserole dish.
Bake at 355 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
