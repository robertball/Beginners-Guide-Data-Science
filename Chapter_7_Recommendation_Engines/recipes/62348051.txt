Title:
Clean Out The Fridge Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1/4 cup milk
1 cup of kraft four cheese blend (or you can use mozzarella and cheddar )
3 thick tomato slices
1 jalapeno or other type of hot pepper
2 slices of onions
From the deli counter: 1 slice cajun turkey, 1 slice BBQ seasoned chicken breast, 2 slices honey ham
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Whisk eggs and milk together in a bowl. Add in salt and pepper. Spray a skillet with canola oil and place on stove at medium heat.
Chop up meats, tomato, onions and pepper and set aside.
Pour egg mixture into skillet and let cook until top of eggs start to bubble and harden.
Add in meat and vegetable mixture onto one half of the eggs and let cook one more minute.
Add cheese on top of meat and vegetables and then carefully fold the other side of the eggs on top of the meat and vegetable side. Continue to cook until cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Sumrall Butler
Description: 
This omelet was discovered one morning when my husband and I were trying to get rid of food items leftover from our deli sandwich dinner the night before. It turned out so great that we've been making it ever since then.
