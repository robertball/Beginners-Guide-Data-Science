Title:
Pan Fried Steak With Gorgonzola Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 10 ounce New York Strip steaks
3 cups heavy cream
4 ounces crumbled Gorgonzola
3 tablespoons freshly grated Parmesan
3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
3/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
3 tablespoons minced fresh parsley
1 clove of minced garlic
6 mushrooms, sliced
Rosemary to taste
Garlic powder to taste
Olive oil

Directions:
Boil the heavy cream with the sliced mushrooms for 20 minutes over medium-high heat to reduce the cream and soften the mushrooms
Remove from heat, add Gorgonzola, Parmesan, salt, pepper, parsley, and garlic. Whisk until the sauce comes together. Set aside.
Season the steaks with salt, freshly ground pepper, garlic powder, and rosemary
Pour approximately two tablspoons into the pre-heated skillet, allow to heat up, and then add the steaks. For a 3/4" thick steak, cook on each side for 5 minutes for a medium finish.
Plate the steak and spoon the Gorgonzola cream sauce over the top.
