Title:
Strawberry Gelatin Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup boiling water
2 boxes of Strawberry gelatin (3oz.)
2 10 oz bags of frozen strawberries
1 can (4oz) of crushed pineapple
3 diced bananas
1/2 pint of sour cream
1/4 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Drain pineapple juice into a pot and add 1 cup of water, bring to a boil. Mix in gelatin
Add partially frozen berries and the other ingredients except the sour cream.
Pour half of the mixture in an 8 x 10 dish to gel. Spread sour cream on top of gelled mixture and then balance of the mixture on top of that.
Refridgerate

Submitter: 
KR
Description: 
Great summer time dessert or snack with yummy strawberries, pineapples and bananas. A family hand-me-down recipe. Enjoy!
