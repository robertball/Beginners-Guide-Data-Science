Title:
Chili Verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 pounds lean pork shoulder-cut into 1 inch cubes
1 cup chicken broth
6 cloves garlic-minced
2 cans (14 oz. each) stewed tomatoes-mexican or italian style
2 cans & oz. each) diced green chilis
1 medium (1 cup) diced white onion
2 teaspoons finely minced fresh oregano or (1 teaspoon dried)
warm flour tortillas
lime wedges

Directions:
In dutch oven or stock pot(at least 6 quart size)combine pork and 1/2 broth and garlic, cover and cook on medium-high for 30 minutes- do not stir-
remove lid- stirring often,until liquid caramelizes and meat browns about 20 minutes-
add remaining broth,scrape brown bits from bottom of pan...
add tomatoes and juice, green chilis, onion and oregano
stir til combined- cover and reduce to simmer- medium-low-
simmer gently- stirring occasionally, til meat pulls apart easily-  about 1 1/2 hours.
serve over rice with warm torts and lime wedges
NOTE- for more of a soup type meal- add more broth-about 1 quart works well-can  spoon rice in bowl then soup-
