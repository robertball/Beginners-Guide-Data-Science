Title:
Chicken and Lentil Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon oil, olive, extra-virgin
1/3 small onion(s), finely chopped
1/2 clove(s) garlic, minced
1/8 teaspoon basil, dried
1/8 teaspoon oregano, dried
1/4 teaspoon peppercorns, black, freshly ground, divided
2 pinch salt, divided
1 teaspoon tomato paste
5 ounce(s) chicken, breast, boneless, skinless, cut crosswise into 1/2-inch slices
5 ounce(s) lentils, brown, canned, drained and rinsed
1/3 of a 14 1/2 ounce(s) tomatoes, diced, with juices
1/4 cup(s) broth, chicken, fat-free, less sodium
1 1/3 ounce(s) spinach, baby, about 1 cups

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, heat oil over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, basil, oregano, pepper, and a pinch of salt; reduce heat to medium-low and cook, stirring occasionally, 4 minutes.
Stir in tomato paste and cook 3 minutes longer. Stir in chicken and cook for 1 minute. Add lentils, tomatoes and their juices, and broth.
Bring to a gentle simmer, reduce heat to low, cover, and cook until chicken is cooked through, 7 to 10 minutes.
Stir in spinach, cover, and cook 1 minute longer, or until spinach wilts. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve warm.
