Title:
Rum Raisin French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup Rum Raisin Ice Cream, melted
3 large eggs, beaten
1 T. dark rum
1/4 tsp. ground Cinnamon
8 slices of THICK French, egg or Raisin
bread
5 T. ground or chopped Walnuts
6 T. butter
Warmed Maple Syrup - Extra Rum Raisin
Ice cream

Directions:
Combine melted ice cream, eggs, rum, cinnamon, walnuts in bowl.  Beat with whisk until well mixed.  Dip thick bread slices in mixture to coat well on both sides.  Saute in melted butter until golden and browned on both sides.  Serve with maple syrup and if desired-- an extra dollop or two of extra Rum Raisin ice cream

Submitter: 
Sherbg
Description: 
An excellent, rich french toast recipe-- we usually serve this on Christmas morning!
