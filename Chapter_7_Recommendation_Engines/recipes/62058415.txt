Title:
Bechamel Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of milk
1 tablespoon of butter
1 tablespoon of white flour
pinch of salt & pepper

Directions:
In a saucepan, melt the butter. Add milk. Lower temperature to medium/low.  Whisk together. Add flour, salt & pepper.  Whisk well until thick and bubbly.

Submitter: 
Lucy Mirabella Nino
Description: 
This very simple white sauce complements any pasta dish.  Try layering it in your next lazagna dish.
