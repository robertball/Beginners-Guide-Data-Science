Title:
Chicken Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp vegetable or canola oil
3 cups cubed, cooked chicken
1 8 oz can water chestnuts, drained
1 pkg dried shitake mushrooms, soaked (acording to directions), drained and squeezed to remove excess water
1 clove garlic
4-5 leaves iceberg lettuce
Stir Fry Sauce
2 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbsp brown sugar
1/2 tsp rice wine vinegar

Directions:
Pulse chicken, water chestnuts, mushrooms, green onions, and garlic in a food processor to a fine consistency.
Prepare the stir fry sauce by mixing the soy sauce, brown sugar, and rice vinegar together in a small bowl.
Bring oil to a high heat in a wok or large frying pan.
When oil is ready, add chicken mixture to the pan and saute for a couple of minutes.
Add stir fry sauce and saute for another couple minutes. Serve in lettuce cups with chili sauce.

Submitter: 
Nia
Description: 
These are a fresh and tasty way to use up leftover cooked chicken. Serve with different condiments like chili sauce and soy sauce.
