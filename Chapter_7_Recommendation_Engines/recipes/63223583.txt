Title:
Bacon Ranch Chicken Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 bamboo skewers
4 large boneless skinless chicken breasts
1/3 c ranch dressing
1 t or to taste hot chili paste
12 strips lean bacon
24 pieces of red onion
S&P; to taste

Directions:
Soak skewers overnight in water.
Combine 1/3 c ranch dressing with 1 t chili paste.
Cut up chicken into 1" cubes.
Mix marinade with chicken and marinade for no more than 3 hours.
Place onion on skewer, then bacon and chicken, weaving bacon around each piece of chicken. End with one more piece of onion. Food should be touching but not jammed together to allow for proper cooking.
Grill 3-4 minutes per side.
