Title:
Hula's Short Pie Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups flour
2 sticks very cold butter
1/2 tsp salt
1 egg
1/4 cup ice cold water
1 Tbsp vinegar

Directions:
Combine  flour and salt.
Cut butter into chunks then mix with flour until crumbly.
Mix egg, cold water, and vinegar together.
Now pour wet mixture into the flour/butter mixture and blend into a ball.
Divide into 2 equal flat discs, then wrap and refrigerate for about 20 minutes before you roll the dough out.

Submitter: 
Virginia Keeley
Description: 
Use for any pie that needs a normal pie crust
