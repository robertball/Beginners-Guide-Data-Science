Title:
Mushroom Salad with Almonds and Tofu Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoon oil, olive, extra-virgin
1/2 pounds mushroom, white
2 clove(s) garlic, minced
salt, to taste
peppercorns, black, freshly ground, to taste
2 pinch thyme, dried
4 tablespoon nuts, almonds
2 teaspoon vinegar, balsamic
4 cup(s) lettuce, mixed greens
8 ounce(s) tofu, extra-firm, cooked and cut into cubes
1/2 cup(s) tomato(es), chopped
1/2 cup(s) beans, green

Directions:
Heat a skillet to medium heat and add oil. Once pan and oil are heated add mushrooms and garlic and cook until mushrooms are tender.
Remove from heat and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Mix mushrooms with remaining ingredients and enjoy.
