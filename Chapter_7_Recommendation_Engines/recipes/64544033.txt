Title:
Quinoa zucchini protein muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups whole wheat pastry flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/8 tsp cloves
1/8 tsp nutmeg
1/8 tsp allspice
1/8 tsp ginger
1 cup cooked quinoa
1 1/2 cup shredded zucchini, squeezed
1 1/2 cups nonfat Greek yogurt
1/2 c brown sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Set oven to 375.  Prep muffin pans with liners.  Makes 22 using a #20 scoop ( aprox 1/4 cup)
Combine dry. Combine wet. Combine both together.  Can add raisins or nuts...
Bake for about 30 minutes, until lightly browned and toothpick is clean.
