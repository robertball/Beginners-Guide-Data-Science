Title:
Upstate Spicy Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 pk. sliced pepperoni
1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breast
1 green pepper, chopped
1/2 onion, chopped
2 jalapenos, chopped
1 can white kidney beans, strained
1 can red kidney beans, strained
2 cans stewed tomatoes
2 can pureed tomatoes
1 can tomato paste
chili powder to taste
2 Italian sausages
1 clove garlic, minced
pepper and salt to taste

Directions:
Cook ground beef, chicken and Italian sausage seperately.  In a large pot stew the tomatoes and vegetables and spices, everything but the paste.  Add the cooked meat.  Simmer for 3 hours, stir occasionally. After 3 hours add the tomato paste and simmer again for another hour to thicken sauce.  Stir occasionally.

Submitter: 
BEARNESTA
Description: 
Pepperoni, chicken, Italian sauage and beef combine for a flavorful chili.
