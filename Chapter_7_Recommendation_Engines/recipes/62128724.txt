Title:
Blueberry Jello Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg Raspberry Jello (sm pkg)
1 C Boiling water
1 C Cold Water
1 T Lemon Juice
1 Can Blueberry Pie Filling
1 sm Can Crushed Pineapple Drained
1 C Sour Cream
1 C Cool Whip

Directions:
Dissolve Jello in boiling water. Add Cold water and let cool for a couple of mins.
Add pie filling, drained pineapple and lemon juice.  Set in fridge and let set.
When Set mix cool whip and sour cream in bowl and spread over top of set jello.

Submitter: 
Susan Connolly Ables
Description: 
Great for holiday meals or just something special for a party or meal... Can make cherry by switching cherry jello for raspberry jello and cherry pie filling for the blueberry pie filling
