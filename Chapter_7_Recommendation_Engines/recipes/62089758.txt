Title:
Hashbrown & Egg Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices bacon
3 tbsp onion, chopped
1/4 cup mushrooms, chopped
2 cups frozen hashbrowns, cubed
2 eggs
1/4 cup cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Cook bacon in a medium skillet.  Drain, reserving 1 tbsp of the bacon drippings.
Add onions, mushrooms, and hashbrowns to the skillet and cook, stirring, for 2 minutes.
Cover and let cook for 14 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Make 2 wells in the hashbrown mixture.  Break eggs into wells.  Cover and let cook for an additional 8 minutes, or until eggs are completely set.
Sprinkle with cheese and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
EMILY052005
Description: 
This is an easy favorite of my fiance and me.  You can substitue your favorite hashbrowns and veggies.  I love to drizzle mine with ketchup right out of the skillet!
