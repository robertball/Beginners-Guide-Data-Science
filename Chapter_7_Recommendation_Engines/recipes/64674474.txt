Title:
Blueberry Lemon Crumbles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c sliced almonds with skin
1 3/4 c all purpose flour
1/2 c sugar
1/2 c brown sugar
2 t finely grated lemon zest
1/2 t cinnamon
1/2 t salt
1 1/2 sticks unsalted butter-softened
2 large egg yolks
1 t vanilla
2 t fresh lemon juice
1/3 c dried blueberries
1/2 c blueberry preserves

Directions:
Put oven rack in middle position & preheat oven to 375
Toast almonds in 1 layer in shallow baking pan, stirring once, until golden, 3-5  minutes cool completely in pan
Pulse flour, sugars, 1 t zest, cinnamon, and salt in food processor until combined, then add butter and pulse until mixture resembles a coarse meal. Add egg yolks & vanilla and process until mixture comes together in clumps
Transfer 1/2 c packed dough to a bowl and stir in almonds (some almonds will break) for almond crumble topping. Gather remaining dough into a ball
Generously butter bottom, sides & top of muffin pan. Press 1 T dough into each mini muffin on bottom and up sides. Chill until dough is firm, about 15 minutes
Stir remaining teaspoon zest, lemon juice,and dried blueberries and preserves in small bowl. spoon 1/2 teaspoon of mixture in each muffin cup. Crumble 1 rounded teaspoon almond topping evenly into each cup.
Bake until topping is deep golden brown  and filling is bubbling, about 20-25 minutes. Cool completely in muffin pan on a rack. Loosen edges of crumbles carefully with small offset spatula or small sharp knife, then carefully remove from pan
