Title:
classic chicken pot pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For crust
1 1/4 cups flour
1 tsp. Sugar
1/4 tsp fine salt
1/2 cup cold unsalted butter
3 to 5 tablespoons ice water
For filling
5 tablespoons unsalted butter
1 medium yellow onion diced (1 1/2 cups)
4 medium carrots diced small (2 cups)
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 cup flour
4 cups chicken broth
1 cup frozen peas
Salt and pepper to taste
3 cups shredded cooked chicken
1/3 cup fresh parsley

Directions:
1. Make crust: mix flour salt and sugar. Add butter until mixture resembles coarse meal. Sprinkle 3 tablespoons of water. Mix until dough is crumbly but holds together when squeezed. Add more water if necessary .
2. Pre heat oven to  375Â° . Make filling: in large pot melt butter over medium heat. Add onions and carrots and cook until softened, 8 minutes. Add garlic and coot until fragrant, 1 minute. Add flour and stir to coat veggies. Slowly add broth, whisking constantly until sauce is smooth. Bring to boil, reduce heat and simmer. Stir in peas. Season with salt and pepper. Then stir in chicken and parsley. Poor filling into 2-quart baking dish.
3. On floured work surface roll out dough to 1/8 inch thickness. Place dough over dish and fold overhang inward while pinching to crimp edge. Cut vent s in dough. Place dish on a rimmed baking sheet and bake until crust is golden brown and filling is bubbling around the edge, 45 to 50 minutes. Let cool 15 minutes before serving.
