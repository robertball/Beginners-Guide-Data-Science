Title:
Oreo Cookie Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 (4 ounce) packages chocolate sandwich cookies
1/2 cup butter
3 cups whipping cream, whipped and sweetened with powdered sugar
1 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
100 grams package vanilla pudding mix
2 cups milk

Directions:
Prepare pudding with the milk according to package directions. Refrigerate
Crush cookies.  Reserve 10 cookies for top.  Melt butter and mix with rest of cookies.  Press into 9x13 pan.  Put in freezer for 5 minutes.
Blend 1/2 of the whipped cream, all of the cream cheese and confectioners' sugar. Spread over crust and place cake back in freezer 10 - 15 minutes.
Spread pudding over top of cake. Spread the remaining whipped topping on top of the pudding then sprinkle with the remaining cookies (cut into bite size pieces). Keep cake refrigerated.
