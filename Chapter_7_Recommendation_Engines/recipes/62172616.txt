Title:
Boiled coconut frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4C butter
1C coconut
2/3C packed brown sugar
1/2C chopped nuts
3Tbsp cream or milk

Directions:
Melt butter in saucepan add the remaining ing. Spread on cake while the cake is still warm. Broil unter broiler until brown
