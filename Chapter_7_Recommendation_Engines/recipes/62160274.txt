Title:
Corn Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream style corn
1 package frozen chopped broccoli
1 small onion-chopped
1 egg, beaten
1 cup crushed saltine crackers
3 tablespoons butter cut in small chunks
3 slices bacon, very slightly cooked, cut up in pieces

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except 1/4 cup saltines and bacon.  Place the mixture in a greased 8 x 8 inch baking dish.  Sprinkle with remaining cracker crumbs and place bacon slices on top.  Bake for 1/2 hour in a 350 degree oven covered.  Remove cover and bake for an additional 1/2 hour or until bacon is cooked and center of casserole is hot through.

Description: 
This has been a family favorite for years and fans of all ages just love it.  There are others similar but this can be very addictive!!
