Title:
Green Chilie Burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/644063.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds ground lean beef or turkey
6-8 leaves of lettuce (washed and dried)
1 lrg. sliced tomatoe
6-8 slices of monteray jack cheese
1/12oz can of whole or halved green chilies (washed and sliced in half
1 lrg. sliced onion
6-8 lrg. onion potatoe hamburger buns
2 tbsp. of steak seasoning
1 egg

Directions:
Mix together in a bowl the ground beef/tukey meat, 1 egg and 2tbsp. of steak seasoning. Form into patties and cook on grill.
Last 10 minutes of grill time place chilies on grill to cook both sides and place sliced cheese on burgers to melt.
While meat is cooking slice onion and tomatoe and lettuce.
Build your burger to taste!!
