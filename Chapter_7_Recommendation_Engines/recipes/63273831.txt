Title:
Salmon Pecan Cakes w/ Sauteed Chard & Slivered Almonds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 7.5 oz can wild salmon
1 3/4 C pecans
2 organic eggs
3 scallions, chopped
1 celery stalk, chopped
1 tbs EVOO
1 tbs lime juice
1/2 tsp sea salt
pinch of paprika
Sauteed Swiss Chard w/ Slivered Almonds
1/4 C slivered almonds
1 tbs EVOO
6 C swiss chard, chopped
pinch of sea salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*.  Drain the canned salmon.
In a food processor, grind the pecans to fine texture.
Add the remaining ingredients and pulse to combine.
Separate the mixture into 8 balls, and form into patties.
Place on a lightly oiled baking tray and bake until golden, about 25 - 30 minutes.
Serve with Sauteed Swiss Chard w/ Slivered Almonds
Use the preheated 350* oven and place the almonds on the wiped down baking tray and bake for 6-7 minutes, until lightly toasted.
In a saute pan, heat the oil on medium heat and add in the chard and salt and continue to saute until the chard is tender.
Add the almonds and serve.
