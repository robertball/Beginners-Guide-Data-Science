Title:
Microwave Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Fresh Corn with husk

Directions:
Remove cornsilk from corn
With the husk intact, place corn on a plate, semi covered
Microwave at high for
10 min - 1 corn
15 minutes - 2 corn
20 minutes - 3 corn
Easy!
