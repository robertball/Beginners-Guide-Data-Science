Title:
Janet's Quick Chicken Chasseur Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless and skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
1/3 c. corn starch
1/4 c. vegetable oil
1/2 t. each tarragon & ground thyme
1/4 t. pepper
1 c. sliced scallions
2 c. chicken broth
3/4 c. Sherry cooking wine
1 c. sliced mushrooms, fresh or canned
3 tomatoes, cut into eighths
3-4 c. hot cooked rice

Directions:
Dredge chicken in cornstarch.  In large skillet, brown coated chicken in oil.  Stir in seasonings and scallions.  Cook 2 minutes longer.
Add broth and sherry.  Cover; simmer 10 min.
Gently stir in mushrooms and tomatoes.  Cover; simmer 5 minutes longer.  Serve over rice.
