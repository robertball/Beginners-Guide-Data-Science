Title:
Out of the Bowl and into a Roll Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/984808.jpg
Ingredients:
6 MANNY'S速 9-inch Burrito Size Flour Tortillas
1 (8 ounce) package seasoned cream cheese, any variety
1 (15 ounce) can HORMEL速 Vegetarian Chili
1 (8 ounce) package Mexican-style shredded cheese
1/2 cup diced tomatoes
1 small onion, diced

Directions:
Top each tortilla with cream cheese, chili, cheese, tomatoes and onion. As tightly as possible, roll the tortilla, burrito-style. Slice tortillas into 1 1/2-inch slices and pick each with a toothpick. Arrange on platter and serve immediately.
