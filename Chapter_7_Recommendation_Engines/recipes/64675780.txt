Title:
Easy Eggplant Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar of red sauce ( or homemade marinara)
2 medium eggplants, sliced 1/2" thick
3 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
Seasoned salt and pepper
8 oz shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat at 450
Spray 9x13 pan
Spread 1 1/2 cup of sauce in pan
Top with overlapping layer of eggplant slices
Drizzle with oil
Add even layer of bread crumbs
Sprinkle with half of the grated Parm cheese
Season to taste
Spread rest of sauce and Parm cheese
Bake, covered for 40 minutes until eggplant is tender
Top with mozzarella
Bake 5 minutes uncovered
Let sit 15 minutes before serving
