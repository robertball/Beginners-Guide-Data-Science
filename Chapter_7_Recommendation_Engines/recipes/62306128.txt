Title:
chocolate covered cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/368210.jpg
Ingredients:
24 oreos
6 ounces of Baker's semi sweet chocolate
1/2 teaspoon vegetable oil
marshmallow fondant flowers

Directions:
In a microwave safe bowl combine chocolate squares and oil.  Microwave at 30 second intervals until chocolate is melted.
Dip oreos in chocolate and lift cookies out with a fork. Tap the side of the bowl to knock off excess chocolate.  Place cookies on a foil or parchment lines cookie sheet.
Allow to dry at room temp for several hours or refrigerate until hardened.
To decorate: put a small drop of melted chocolate on top of the cookie, add fondant flowers.
Other options, drizzle with colored candy melts or sprinkle with sprinkles while chocolate is still wet.

Submitter: 
sugarbean
Description: 
Everyone's favorite chocolated sandwich cookies drenched in rich chocolate and decorated with marshmallow fondant accents. So simple and so delicious, these make great party favors.
