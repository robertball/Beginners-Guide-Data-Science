Title:
Company Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32oz frozen shredded hashbrowns
2 cans cream of chicken soup (healthy request)
16 oz plain greek yogurt or sour cream
8 oz reduced fat shredded cheddar cheese
3/4 stick melted butter
1/2 cup chopped onion
salt & pepper to taste
Topping
2 cups corn flakes - crushed
1/2 stick of butter - melted

Directions:
Mix all and pour in 13 x 9 pan.
Mix 2 cups crushed corn flakes with 1/2 stick melted butter and put on top.
Bake 1 hour in a 350 degree oven.

Submitter: 
cookinmama
Description: 
This is a recipe my mom passed down to me with a few alterations that I added. It's a yummy and easy side-dish to make.
