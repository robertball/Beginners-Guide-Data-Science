Title:
Ruth's Blender Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14oz)can Hunt's Fire Roasted Diced Tomatoes
1 (7oz)can Elpato Tomato Sauce (mexican hot style)
1 (7oz)can Diced Green Chilies
1/2 to 3/4 medium sweet onion (chopped)
1 whole bunch cilantro leaves (chopped)
1/2 finely diced fresh jalapeno (ribbed and seeded)
1/2 lime juiced
1 tsp salt
1 stp sugar
3 green onions (sliced)

Directions:
Rough chop and put all ingredients (finely dice the jalapeno) in a blender or food processor (except the green onion) and pulse or blended to desired consistency. Stir in green onion slices whole and and store in air tight container in fridge (I use quart sized mason jar, it be full and plus a bowl left over)

Submitter: 
Ruth Jones
Description: 
You can make this in 5 minutes using a blender or food processor. I store in a mason jar in the fridge. You can increase the heat by adding more jalapeno.
