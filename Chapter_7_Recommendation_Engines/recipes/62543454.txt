Title:
Rum Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb brown sugar
1/2 lb melted butter
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp cinnamon
4 oz dark rum

Directions:
Process in food processor or blender until thoroughly combined.  Adjust sugar, spices and rum as desired.

Submitter: 
Phoebe
Description: 
Traditional family recipe made at Christmas.  Can be used on toast, crackers or put into baked tart shells.  Put in sterilized jars and can be frozen or stored in fridge for a few weeks.
