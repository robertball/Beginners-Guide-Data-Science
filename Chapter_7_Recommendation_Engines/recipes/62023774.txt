Title:
Heidi's Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
1 medium onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 tablespoons flour
1 cup chicken broth
1 can (4oz.) green chilies
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
12 corn or flour tortillas
1-1/2 cups shredded chicken
2 cups mexican shredded cheese
sour cream
chopped tomatoe

Directions:
In a saucepan, melt butter. Saute onion and garlic until tender. Blend in flour. Stir in broth, milk, chilies, salt and cumin. Cook and stir until thick. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes. Set aside. Grease 13X9 baking dish. Spoon a small amount of sauce in center of tortilla and spread to edges. Place about 2 tablespoons of chicken in the center of tortilla. Sprinkle 1-2 tablespoons of cheese on top of chicken. Roll up tortillas and place in baking dish seam side down. Pour remaining sauce over. Sprinkle with remaining cheese and bake for 20-30 minutes at 350 degrees. Serve with sour cream and chopped tomatoe.

Description: 
This is my favorite enchilada recipe with a great creamy sauce! It is even better with sour cream and fresh tomatoes on top.
