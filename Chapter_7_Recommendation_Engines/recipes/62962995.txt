Title:
Potato Casserole / Company Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
32 ounces of frozen shredded hashbrowns
2 cans healthy request cream of chicken soup
16 oz plain greek yogurt or (sour cream)
8 oz shredded cheddar cheese
3/4 stick melted butter
1/2 cup chopped onion
salt and pepper to taste
Topping
2 cups crushed corn flakes
1/2 stick melted butter

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
mix all together (except topping) then put into 13 x 9 pan
mix crushed corn flakes with butter and spoon topping on
bake 1 hour
