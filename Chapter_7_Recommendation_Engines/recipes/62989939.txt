Title:
White Chocolate Key Lime Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. Graham Cracker or Gingersnap Crumbs
4 (8 oz.)pkgs. Cream Cheese
1 cup sugar
4 eggs
1 (12 oz)pkg white chocolate chips
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
Juice of 4 Key limes or 2 Tbsp. Key lime juice
zest from 4 key limes or 1 regular lime

Directions:
Sprinkle bottom of lightly greased 9-inch springform pan with crumbs.  Combine cream cheese and sugar until well blended.  Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each.  In a metal bowl, combine the white chocolate chips, heavy whipping cream, lime juice and lime zest.  Place bowl over a boiling pot of water and continously stir until chocolate is melted and all ingredients are mixed together.  Add the chocolate mixture to the cream cheese mixture and mix well.  Pour into pan.  Bake at 325* for 1 hr and 15 min.  Cool.  Place in the refrigerator.  Just before serving you can add white chocolate curls and whip cream for garnish.

Submitter: 
Angela
Description: 
This is a recipe I made myself because I love the tartness of lime mixed with white chocolate.  Also, I love cheesecake!!!
