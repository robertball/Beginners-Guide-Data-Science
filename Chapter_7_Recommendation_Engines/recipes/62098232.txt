Title:
Grandma's Italian Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 slices sandwich bread white or wheat
6  slices American cheese
3  eggs
2t garlic powder
2t Italian seasoning
2T Parmesan cheese grated
2T milk

Directions:
Whisk together eggs, milk, garlic, Italian seasoning, and Parmesan cheese until thoroughly mixed.
Place a slice of cheese between 2 slices of bread.
Dip both sides into egg mixture. Place into nonstick frying pan. Cook until golden brown on both sides.

Submitter: 
Rae
Description: 
This is a great twist on the classic grilled cheese. My grandmother taught me how to make these when I was a teenager. My kids love them!
