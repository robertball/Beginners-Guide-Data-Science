Title:
Healthy and Delicious Chicken Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts,pounded to tenderdize
1 can fat free cream of chicken soup
1 can of fat free milk
1 cup fat free sourcream
2-3 cups of finely shredded blend, Mexican cheeses
onion powder to taste
garlic powder to taste
chili powder to taste
salt and pepper to taste
8 regular size flour tortillas
salsa and extra sour cream for added toppings

Directions:
Place pounded chicken breasts into crockpot
Mix together the soup, milk, sourcream and seasonings on stove top(low setting) until well blended.
Pour mixture over chicken.
Cook on low setting for about 8 hours.
Remove just the chicken and shred.
Spread onto tortillas
Spread some sauce mixture onto shredded chicken
Sprinkle some cheese on top
Roll up and place into casserole dish
Pour some more sauce mixture onto burritos
Top with remaining cheese
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes or until cheese is bubbling
Top with salsa and sourcream

Submitter: 
JerBare
Description: 
Low fat, easy and flavorful chicken burritos.
Topped with a creamy sauce for an added touch.
