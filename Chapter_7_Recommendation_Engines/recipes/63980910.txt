Title:
Chicken Fajitas using Deep Covered Baker from PC Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 red onion
1 red pepper
1 green pepper
2 Tbsp PC Chipotle seasoning mix
2-4 chicken breasts, pounded with meat tenderizer
1 pkg fajita size flour tortillas
shredded cheese

Directions:
Slice onion, peppers and put on bottom of baker.
Sprinkle with 1 Tbsp seasoning, top with chicken and sprinkle with 1 Tbsp seasoning.
Cover and microwave 12-14 minutes. NO PEEKING, Let it rest for 5 minutes before lifting the lid.
Place the flour tortillas in the oven at 250', wrapped in foil or in the microwave wrapped in a damp paper towel to warm.
When chicken is done, remove peppers and onions. Chop up the chicken into bite sized pieces.
Shred cheddar cheese and make salsa and guacamole
