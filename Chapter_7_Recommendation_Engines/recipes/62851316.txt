Title:
Chocolate Roll Out Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, slightly softened
1 cup granulated sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 cups flour
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a medium bowl with an electric or hand mixer or in the bowl of a stand mixer with a flat paddle, cream together the butter and sugar on medium speed.
Add the egg and vanilla and blend on medium speed.
Add the flour, cocoa, baking powder and salt and blend on low speed.
Knead into a ball, cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
Bake at 375 for 12 minutes.
