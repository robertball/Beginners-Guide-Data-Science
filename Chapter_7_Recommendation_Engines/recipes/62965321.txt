Title:
Hazelnut Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups ground hazelnuts
4 eggs
3/4 cup sugar
2 TBSP flour
1 tsp baking powder

Directions:
In one bowl beat egg whites until stiff peaks form.
In another bowl beat egg yolks and sugar until light and lemony. Add flour and baking powder. Mix in the ground hazelnuts.
Combine the two mixtures by gently folding in the egg whites until just blended.
Pour into greased and floured 9" round springfold pan. Bake at 350F for 20 minutes.
Top cooled cake with chocolate glaze and chill before serving.

Jenny Deveau
Description: 
A light nutty coffee cake topped with chocolate glaze
