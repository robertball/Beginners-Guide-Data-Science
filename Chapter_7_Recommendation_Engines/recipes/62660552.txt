Title:
Bacon and Tomato Linguini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/591749.jpg
Ingredients:
2 TBS Kosher Salt
4 Quarts Water
1 pound Linguini Pasta
1 pound bacon, cut into 1/2" - 3/4" pieces
2 TBS extra virgin olive oil
2 med/large shallots, diced
1 tsp crushed red pepper flakes, or to taste
2 med cloves garlic, minced
3 (15 oz ea) cans Italian Style diced
tomatoesw/garlic, oregano and basil - (Recommend
DelMonte brand)with liquid
1/2 cup red wine
2 TBS fresh basil, chiffonade or chopped
1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
kosher salt and freshly grated black pepper, to taste

Directions:
In a large pasta or stock pot, bring 4 quarts of water to a boil with 2 TBS kosher salt. Add pasta and cook to al-dente.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet or saute pan, over med heat, add the bacon and cook until crispy.  Remove bacon to paper towel lined plate and remove 3/4 of the bacon grease from the pan.
Add the olive oil to the bacon grease. Add onions and crushed red pepper flakes. Cook until onions are translucent. Add the garlic and cook for 1 min., and then add the tomatoes and juice.
Saute (good simmer/light boil) approx. 10 - 15 minutes or until reduced by approx. 1/2 and very flavorful and fragrant.  Add wine, bring back up to temp. and cook/saute additional 5 minutes to cook off alcohol.
Drain pasta and add to the tomato mixture in the pan.  Add basil and bacon and toss. Add parmesan and toss well. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
