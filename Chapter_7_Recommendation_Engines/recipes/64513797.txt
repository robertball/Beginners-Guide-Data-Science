Title:
Slow cooker Balsamic Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs of boneless pork shoulder
salt to taste
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp of red pepper flakes
1/3 c of chicken or vegetable broth
1 T worcestershire sauce
1 T honey

Directions:
Add all ingredients in a slow cooker.
Cook on low 6-8 hours.
This is delicious served with some red potatoes and vegetables.
