Title:
Watergate Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/610966.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pkg. (4 serving size)  JELL-O Pistachio Flavor Instant Pudding & Pie Filling
1 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple, in juice, drained (reserve juice)
1 heaping cup JET-PUFFED Miniature Marshmallows
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1/3 cup flaked coconut (optional)
1 container thawed COOL WHIP Whipped Topping

Directions:
Mix at least 1 1/2 packages (or all) of the dry pudding mix, drained pineapple, marshmallows, coconut, pecans, and 1/3 cup reserved pinapple juice (more if needed for creaminess, want it moist but not too liquidy) in large bowl until well blended. Add whipped topping; stir gently until well blended.  Cover and refrigerate 1 hour or until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Kilyena
Description: 
Pistachio pudding, crushed pineapple, mini marshmallows, pecans and whipped topping are blended and chilled for a classic fruit salad.Modified from: Watergate Salad
