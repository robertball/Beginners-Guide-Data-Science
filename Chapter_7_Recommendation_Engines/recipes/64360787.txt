Title:
Slow-Cooker Corned Beef and Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large carrots, peeled and cut into matchstick pieces
10 baby red potatoes, quartered
1 onion, peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces
4 cups water or beef boullion
1 (4 pound) corned beef brisket with spice packet
6 ounces beer
1/2 head cabbage, coarsely chopped

Directions:
Place the roast in the slow cooker. Pour the beer over the brisket. Sprinkle on the spices from the packet, cover, and set the cooker on high.
Cook the brisket for about 4 hours. Add the carrots, onions and potatoes and cook for 3 hours. An hour before serving, stir in the cabbage and cook for 1 more hour.

Submitter: 
Colleen Robertson Curtiss
