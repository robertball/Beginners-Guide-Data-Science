Title:
Thai Chicken & Sweet Basil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/593416.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Dried Red hot chili peppers- soaked in warm water for 15 mins., sliced
2 T olive oil
5 cloves garlic, chopped
1 boneless, skinless chicken breast- sliced into thin 2" strips
1/2 Red Pepper, chopped into 3/4" pieces
1 Cup snap peas, broccoli, and/or mushrooms (whatever you like)
2 T Oyster Sauce
1/4 tsp sugar
pinch garlic or onion powder
1 Cup loosely packed Thai Basil leaves (25-30 leaves)- torn
1/2 C. bean sprouts
1/8 C. chopped peanuts

Directions:
Heat oil in wok or skillet over medium-high heat.
Add garlic and lightly brown- about 2 minutes.
Stir-fry chicken in garlic &amp; oil until juices run clear.
Remove chicken and keep warm.
Add red pepper, chili peppers, snap peas, mushrooms or your favorite vegetables and cook for 3 minutes.
Add chicken back to wok with veggies.
Add oyster sauce- stir to combine
sprinkle sugar &amp; garlic/onion powder (optional)- stir to combine
Add Thai Basil &amp; bean sprouts- mix in and continue to heat, about another minute or two.
Serve over rice and top with chopped peanuts.
