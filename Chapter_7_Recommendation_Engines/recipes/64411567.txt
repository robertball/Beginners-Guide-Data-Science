Title:
Cranberry Pecan Crunch Squares Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2278317.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c oats, quick
1 c whole wheat flour
1 1/4 c brown sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
zest of one orange
1 ripe banana, mashed or blended
1 15 oz pkg jellied cranberry sauce
1/2 c chopped pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Line 13" x 9" dish with parchment paper
In large bowl, mix all the ingredients except banana, pecans and cranberry sauce. Mix in the banana until crumbly. Pat half into the pan, spread sauce over, mix pecans into remaining half and sprinkle over the top evenly. Bake 45 min. Mark into squares while hot but let cool before removing from pan.
