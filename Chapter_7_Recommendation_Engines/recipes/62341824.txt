Title:
Honey Cinnamon Bacon Crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Basic Crepes recipe
1 Tbls Vanilla
8 slices bacon or Turkey bacon cooked crisp.
cinnamon sugar
honey
butter

Directions:
Make Basic Crepes as recipe states; add vanilla extract to the batter.
Spread a thin layer of butter over cooked crepe, sprinkle with cinnamon sugar and drizzle a small amount of honey.
Place one strip of bacon on half of crepe.  Fold in half and fold in half again.
