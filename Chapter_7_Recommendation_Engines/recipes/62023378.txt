Title:
Karen's Crockpot Pizza Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Chicken Boneless Breasts, cut into bite size pieces
1 onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper chopped
2 stalks celery, sliced
1 Can Tomato Soup
1 Can Cream of Mushroom Soup
2 tbsp Tomato Paste
1/2 water
1 tbsp each of Parsley, Oregano, Basil
1 Bay Leaf
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
Place chicken and vegetables in crock pot.  Combine Soups, tomato paste, water and spices. Pour over chicken and vegetables.  Stir to coat.  Add bay leaf.  Cook on low for 8 hours, until chicken and vegetables are tender.
Can be served over rice or noodles.

Submitter: 
kuisinebykaren
Description: 
I put this together quickly one Saturday morning.  When my family got home, they couldn't wait!  My daughter thought we were having Pizza for dinner, hence the name!
