Title:
Zuchinni & Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Medium Onion
2 to 3 cloves of Garlic
2 to 3 Zuchinni's sliced
1/4 Cup Olive Oil
1 28oz + 1 15oz Can Tomato Sauce (not flavored)
1/4 Cup water
2/3 Cup Parmesean cheese
1/4 tsp basil
1/4 tsp oregano
Salt & Pepper to taste
1 lb Spaghetti (any type or size)

Directions:
Saute garlic and onions in olive oil until transparent (about 5-7 min).  Add zuchinni, cover and cook 5-6 more minutes.  Add both cans of tomato sauce and water, stir to mix.  Add in parmesean cheese and herbs and cook on low for a 1/2 hour.  Cook spaghetti according to directions and add to sauce.

Submitter: 
Lisa
Description: 
This is a family recipe, love by all.  It's so easy to make and it yields a ton of sauce so that you can freeze it for later.
