Title:
Chewy Raisin Molasses Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups raisins
1/2 cup shortening
1 1/4 cups sugar
2 eggs
1/2 cup molasses
2 cups quick-cooking oats
1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Directions:
Place raisins in a bowl. Cover with boiling water; let stand for 5 minutes. Drain and set aside. In a mixing bowl, cream shortening and sugar. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Beat in molasses. Combine oats, baking soda, salt and cinnamon; gradually add to the creamed mixture. Stir in raisins. Drop by tablespoonfuls 2 in. apart onto lightly greased baking sheets. Bake at 350 degrees F for 12-14 minutes or until lightly browned. Remove to wire racks to cool.

Submitter: 
Ranell  Sturm
Description: 
Molasses adds a little more flavor to a traditional oatmeal raisin cookie. My husband loves the soft and chewy texture of these cookies. The recipe is from his mother.
