Title:
Aunt Joanne's Chocolate Cherry Jubilee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/575112.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (18.25 ounce) package chocolate fudge cake mix(using 3 eggs)
1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling
1 teaspoon almond extract

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Grease a 9 x 13 inch pan.
Smash the cherry pie filling before adding it to the other ingredients.
In a large bowl, fix cake mix according to directions using 3 eggs and adding almond extract. Blend well with a hand-mixer Add cherry pie filling and mix by hand; stir until well blended.  Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until it tests done.  Cool.
Frost with the your favorite chocolate frosting.
