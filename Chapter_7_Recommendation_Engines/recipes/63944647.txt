Title:
Spinach Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1093492.jpg
Ingredients:
8 cups (~200 g) packed Baby Spinach leaves
1-2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 cup olive oil, divided
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon grated lemon zest
Juice from 1/2 lemon (~ 2 teaspoons)

Directions:
Place spinach leaves and garlic in bowl of food processor and pulse until coarsely chopped.
Add 1/4 cup olive oil and process until smooth. Add more olive oil to desired consistency (spreadable paste).
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Transfer to a bowl and stir in lemon peel, lemon juice and Parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
innermuse
Description: 
This is a nut-free pesto, perfect for anyone with nut allergies and is packed with anti-oxidants. Use with Chicken Pesto Saladizza recipe.
