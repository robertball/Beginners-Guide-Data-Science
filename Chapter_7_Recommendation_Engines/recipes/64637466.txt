Title:
Apple Angel Dump Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Apple pie filling
1 box of angel food cake mix (dry)
Sugar
Cinnamon

Directions:
1. Mix the dry angel food cake mix with the Apple filling
2. Pour into greased 9x13 cake pan
3. Sprinkle with cinnamon and Splender
4. Bake at 350 for 20-30 minutes
5. Do not over bake, cake is done when browned on top but not completely set.
6. 325 if using glass pan
