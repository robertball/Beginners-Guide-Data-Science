Title:
Peanut Butter Hemp Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups Flour
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
1 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 cup Butter
3/4 cup Brown Sugar
3/4 cup White Sugar
1/2 cup Applesauce
2 Eggs
1tsp Vanilla
1/2 cup Raisins
1 cup Natural Peanut Butter(from your local health food store)
4 tbsp. Hemp Hearts (from your local health food store)

Directions:
Mix together dry ingredients, hemp hearts and raisins.  Set aside.  Cream butter sugars and applesauce.  Add peanut butter, cream well.  Beat eggs and vanilla, stir into peanut butter mixture.  Add dry ingredients.  Knead to form smooth dough.  Roll into small balls.  Press down with a fork.  Bake @ 375F 9 mins. or until golden.

Submitter: 
Heather Hedstrom
Description: 
A very healthy peanut butter cookie full of protein.  I have a very picky 3 year-old and was trying different ways to get some extra protein in his diet.  He loves these cookies and they're extremely healthy.
