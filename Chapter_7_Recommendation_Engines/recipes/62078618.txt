Title:
Bloody Mary Chicken w/ a Kick Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4or more skinless, boneless chicken breast halves,1 bottle blue cheese dressing your choice,Bloody Mary Mix:1-46oz V-8,1sm lemon juiced,1ts brown suger,2tbs A-1 steak sauce,1tbs worcestershire sauce,1ts horseradish,2ts hot sauce, 3/4ts celery salt, 4 cleaned celery stalks chopped

Directions:
1 place chicken breasts in crock pot. 2 prepare bloody mary mix as follows:in large bowl combine all ingredients.
3 pour mix over chicken add blue cheese,stir till combined
4 turn crock pot to low for 8 hrs or high for 4 hrs.
5 ENJOY!!!

Submitter: 
marlaina
Description: 
This is a crockpot recipe that is incedibly easy to throw together. The taste and moistness of the chicken is dilectable!! Bon Appetite
