Title:
Easy one pot chuck steak dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3#s  of chuck steak cut into serving size pieces
1 large sliced onion
I 28 oz can diced tomatoes
one package of dry onion soup mix
3 sliced carrots
I can consomme soup
2 fresh, minced garlic cloves
pepper to taste

Directions:
Simply layer the ingredients into your slow cooker, beginning with the onion, canned tomatoes, sliced carrot,onion soup mix and garlic, the raw chuck steak and lastly pour the consomme soup over top. Cover and enjoy the wonderful aromas.

Submitter: 
PATRICIA53
Description: 
This is the easiest and tastiest chuck steak dinner and one that my family loves.  Great for you working Moms and Dads.  Just put it together and let it simmer all day, Delicious served with mashed potatoes on the side.
