Title:
Oven mashed potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
potatoes
oil for pan
olive oil

Directions:
preheat oven to 450 - lightly oil baking sheet or coat foil covering with non-stick spray
in a pot of boiling water, cook potatoes until tender, 15-20 minutes, drain well
place drained potatoes on prepared baking sheet, using a potato masher or fork, carefully smash the potatoes until flattened but still in one piece - top with olive oil,
place in oven and bake for 18-20 minutes or until golden brown
