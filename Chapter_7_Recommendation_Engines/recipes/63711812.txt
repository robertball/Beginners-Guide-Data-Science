Title:
Big Ray's Smoky Grilled Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1031431.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup packed dark brown sugar
2 Tbl ground mustard
2 tablespoons instant coffee granules
1 cup hot water
2 bottles (14 ounces each) ketchup
2 Tbl Worcestershire sauce
2 Tbl Liquid Smoke, optional
1 broiler/fryer chicken (3-1/2 to 4 lb), quartered

Directions:
In a large bowl, dissolve sugar, mustard and coffee in hot water. Stir in ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, and liquid smoke if desired. Reserve 1 cup for basting; refrigerate remaining sauce for another use.
Moisten a paper towel with cooking oil; using long-handled tongs, lightly coat the grill rack. Grill, covered, over medium heat, for 30 minutes, turning and basing with sauce frequently. Grill, covered, 15 minutes longer, turning and basting occasionally or until juices run clear.
