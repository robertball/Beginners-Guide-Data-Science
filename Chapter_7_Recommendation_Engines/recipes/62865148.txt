Title:
Spanish Avocado Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/687101.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Large Avocados, halved, pitted, and peeled
1 6oz. box Spanish Rice
2 Small Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts, seasoned to taste
Sour cream

Directions:
Cut avocados in half and remove pit by chopping a knife into the pit and then twisting it. Peel avocados and set aside. Cook rice according to instructions on package. Season chicken with season salt and pepper. Cook the chicken in which ever way you prefer, i.e. barbequing, broiling, pan-frying, etc. Once chicken is cooked, cut into bite-sized cubes, then mix into prepared rice. Spoon chicken and rice mixture into avocado halves. Top with a spoonful of sour cream and serve!

Submitter: 
Samuela
Description: 
This is a simple recipe that I based off something I had on one of my trips to Puerto Rico.
