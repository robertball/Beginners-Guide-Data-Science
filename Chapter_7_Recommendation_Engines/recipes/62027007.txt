Title:
Apple Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 1/4 c. vegetable oil
3 eggs
2 c. sugar
2 tsp. vanilla
3 c. Granny Smith apples, peeled and chopped
1 c. walnuts, chopped
3/4 c. raisins

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease a 15x10 jellyroll pan.  In large bowl combine flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon.  In another large bowl whisk together the oil and eggs.  Add the flour mixture until blended.  Stir in the apples, walnuts, and raisins.  The batter will be very stiff.  Spread in prepared pan.  Bake 40-45 min.  Cool completely on wire rack.  Cut into
squares.

Submitter: 
MISS ALIX
Description: 
A delicious alternative to your typical brownie!  Made with Granny Smith apples, walnuts, and raisins.  The aroma while they're baking is heavenly!
