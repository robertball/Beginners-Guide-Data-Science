Title:
California Crescent Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/713309.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) can Pillsbury® refrigerated crescent dinner rolls or Pillsbury® Crescent Recipe Creations® refrigerated seamless dough sheet
1/2 cup guacamole
8 lettuce leaves
4 ounces sliced smoked turkey
8 slices cooked bacon
4 Tablespoons mayo
4 slices of tomato

Directions:
Separate or cut dough into 8 rectangles (if using crescent rolls, press perforations to seal). Place on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake at 375 degrees F 8 to 10 minutes.
To serve, spread guacamole on 4 of the rectangles. Top with lettuce, turkey, tomato and bacon.  Spread mayo on remaining rectangles and top sandwich.

Submitter:

Description: 
Build a better sandwich when you bake flaky Crescent dough and stack it with the irresistible combo of turkey, bacon, lettuce, tomato, mayo and guacamole.Modified from: California Crescent Sandwiches
