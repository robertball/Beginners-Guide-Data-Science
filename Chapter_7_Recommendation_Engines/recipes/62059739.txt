Title:
Mexican Bunuelos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages of flour tortillas any brand is fine
1 container of cinnamon if you don't already have some
maple syrup ( the kind used for pancakes are fine)
olive oil enough to fill a thick pan
a circular pan
vanilla/ chocolate ice cream (optional)

Directions:
1. heat your oil up in the pan once the oil starts bubbling turn the heat to low
2.cut your tortilla's into triangular strips about four inches wide
3. add tortilla strips into the oil and cook till browned
4. put on plate and pour maple syrup on top about to tablespoons not to drench tortillas
5.shake cinnamon onto plate
6. move the bunuelos to the side of the plate and if your want put a scoop of vanilla or chocolate enjoy!
