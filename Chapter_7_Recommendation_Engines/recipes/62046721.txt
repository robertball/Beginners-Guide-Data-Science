Title:
Crock Pot Chicken and Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1/2 cup water
season salt, to taste
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 box stove top stuffing mix, chicken flavor

Directions:
Place chicken, water, and salt in crock pot.  Cook on high for 4-5 hours depending on size of chicken breasts.  Pour soup into crock pot.  Add stuffing mix.  Mix well.  Cook one more hour or until it reaches desired consistancy.

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
Quick and easy!  This is great for working moms and anyone else who is short on time.
