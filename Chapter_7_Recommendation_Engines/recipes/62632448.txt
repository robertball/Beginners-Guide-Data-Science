Title:
Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (4 oz.) chicken breasts
1 (16 oz.) bag of frozen sugar snap pea stir-fry veggies
Dash of ground ginger
1/2 cup Egg Beaters
2 c. instant brown rice (cooked)
5T soy sauce

Directions:
Slice chicken breasts into thin strips.  Put in a bowl and add 3T soy sauce.  Let sit for 15 min.
Add chicken to stir-fry pan, sprinkle with ginger.  Cook until no longer pink.
Add Egg Beaters, mixing well.
Add frozen veggies.  Saute until warm.
Add cooked rice and remaining 2T soy sauce.  Saute 3 more min.  Serve immediately.
