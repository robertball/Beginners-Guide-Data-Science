Title:
Tarragon Chicken and Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
1 clove of garlic - minced
1 lb. cubed chicken
1/2 tsp. tarragon
1 3/4 cups chicken broth
8 oz. uncooked pasta shells
2 tbsp. water
1/2 cup sliced onion
1 1/2 cups sliced carrot
1/4 cup fresh parsley
2 1/2 cups water
1 1/2 tbsp. lemon juice
2 tbsp. flour
1/2 tsp. pepper
Grated Parmesan to taste

Directions:
Melt butter and cook onion and garlic for 1 minute in a large saute pan.
Add chicken, carrots, parsley, tarragon and pepper.
Cook until chicken is lightly browned.
Add water, chicken broth and lemon juice and bring to a boil.
Add shells and continue to boil, covered until shells are done.
Reduce heat, then mix flour with a little water from the pot and then add gradually until thickened.
Sprinkle with Parmesan before serving.
