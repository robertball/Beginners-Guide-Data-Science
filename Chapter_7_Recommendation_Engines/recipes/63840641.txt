Title:
Roasted Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Cauliflower
1 TBS olive oil
1 TSP salt
2 TBS chili powder

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375. Meanwhile, thaw frozen cauliflower in microwave & drain.
Mix veg, olive oil, salt, & chili powder to coat.
Bake on cookie sheet 30-45 minutes
