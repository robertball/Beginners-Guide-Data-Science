Title:
English Muffin Pizzas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 English muffins
1 small can pizza sauce or tomato sauce
8 oz. shredded mozzarella cheese
assorted toppings (like pepperoni, anchovies, sliced olives, etc.)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Split the muffins and place halves on a lightly greased cookie sheet. Spread desired amount of sauce on each muffin half. Add cheese and toppings. Bake for 5 to 10 minutes, until cheese melts. Store leftovers in the refrigerator and reheat under broiler.

Submitter: 
GINAGINA
Description: 
My mother always made these when I was a child, and I still make them. They're also good with tomato paste (instead of sauce) and a little oregano and basil.
