Title:
Budget Beef Stroganoff III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/278356.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds beef chuck roast or top sirloin
salt and ground black pepper to taste
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons olive or vegetable oil
3 tablespoons margarine
1 1/4 cup beef broth, made from concentrated paste
6 large button mushrooms, sliced
1/3 cup sour cream
1/4 cup white wine or dry sherry

Directions:
Remove any fat and gristle from the roast and cut into strips 1/2 inch thick by 2 inches long. Season with salt and pepper.
In a large skillet over medium heat, melt the 1 tablespoon margarine and 2 tablespoons oil and brown the beef strips quickly, then remove the beef strips and set aside.
Melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of margarine in skillet, place the sliced mushrooms in skillet and begin to brown.  Stir the flour into the juices and melted margarine in the pan. Pour in beef broth and bring to a boil, stirring constantly, being sure to get rid of any lumps. Lower the heat and stir in the browned beef. Cover and simmer for 1 hour or until the meat is tender.
Five minutes before serving, stir in the sour cream, and white wine. Heat briefly then salt and pepper to taste.
