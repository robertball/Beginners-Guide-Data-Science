Title:
Grilled Caribbean-Spiced Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boned and skinned chicken breast halves
1 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp	ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp	ground cumin
1/4 tsp ground anise
1 dash cayenne pepper
1-1/2 tsp lime zest
1/2 Tbsp fresh lime juice
1 Tbsp honey

Directions:
Wash chicken and pat dry with paper towels.  Combine spices in a small dish with lime juice and honey and rub all over the chicken breasts.  Let marinate in fridge for 30-60 minutes.  Preheat grill over medium heat, and grill chicken breasts until done, about 10-15 minutes, turning once or twice, until no longer pink in the center.

Submitter: 
LINDA W.
Description: 
I love serving this with basmati rice and sliced mango alongside.  A great summertime meal!
