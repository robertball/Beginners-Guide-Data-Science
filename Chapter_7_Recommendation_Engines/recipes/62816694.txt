Title:
Chinese Chicken Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3 oz. chicken flavor ramen noodles, broken
1/4 Cup Rice wine vinegar
1/4 cup oil
1 Tbl. Sesame Oil
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 Tsp. pepper
1 1/2 Cups cubed cooked chicken
1/2 Cup sliced almonds
1/4 Cup chopped green onion
1 10 oz. bag Confetti Slaw

Directions:
Mix flavor packet, vinegar, oils, sugar and pepper.  Combine broken ramen noodles, chicken, green onion and slaw mix in large bag or bowl.  Add dressing and mix.  Place in refrigerator to chill 30 minutes or longer.  Add almonds right before serving.
