Title:
Goetta - Wilmes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6lb Country Ribs with bones
2 lb Pinhead Oatmeal (2 3/4 C.)
Bay Leaves - 10 or more while cooking the meat and another 10 or so while cooking the Oatmeal
2 Tbl Salt
16 Cups Strained Water

Directions:
Cover the ribs with water - add bayleaves and salt  - cover half way - cook til done
Remove meat from bones.
Grind the meat
Strain 16 Cups water from meat ( add more water if you don't have 16 cups from the meat)
Bay Leaves and More Salt
Add the oatmeal - cook on low - stir often until liquid is thick and bubbles.  This might take close to an hour.
Add the meat to the Oatmeal mix - more salt to taste.
Cook 15 minutes more
Let stand for one hour and put in pans.
