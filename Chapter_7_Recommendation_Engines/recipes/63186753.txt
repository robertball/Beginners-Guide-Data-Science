Title:
Roasted Sweet Mini Pepper Pupus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. sweet mini peppers
1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
1 teaspoon garlic salt, or to taste
1 teaspoon chili powder, or to taste

Directions:
Set oven to broil.
Cut the tops and bottoms off of the sweet mini peppers, then slice in two from top to bottom.
Line a baking sheet with foil. Place sweet mini peppers skin side up on the foil.
Brush olive oil onto skin side of peppers.
Season skin side of peppers with garlic salt and chili powder.
Set baking sheet about four inches away from broiler heat source. Leave for five minutes.
Turn baking sheet 180 degrees and broil for another five minutes.
Remove from broiler (skins of peppers should be starting to turn black, leave in if they aren't.)
Let sit for a couple of minutes and then eat!

Submitter: 
Seth Kolloen
Description: 
Tasty broiled sweet peppers for an easy snack or appetizer (or pupus as they say in Hawaii). Good way to use those bagged sweet mini peppers in the stores nowadays.
