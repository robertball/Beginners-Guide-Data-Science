Title:
Crescent Roll Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound hamburger
1 15 ounce can spaghetti sauce
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
2 packages crescent rolls
1 8 ounce container sour cream
2 teaspoons parsley flakes

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Brown hamburger. Drain. Spread hamburger on bottom of 9x13 inch pan. Pour spaghetti sauce over hamburger. Layer cheeses over meat and spaghetti sauce. Open crescent rolls and tear apart on lines. Spread sour cream on the inside of roll and sprinkle with parsley flakes. Roll up crescent rolls and place on top of cheese. Bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
HARTMR
Description: 
This is a unique recipe made from your standard lasagna ingredients, but there aren't any noodles! Crescent rolls are spread with sour cream and parsley and baked on top of hamburger, spaghetti sauce and cheese. A great meal for the whole family.
