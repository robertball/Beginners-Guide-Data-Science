Title:
Cider Glazed Chicken with Browned Butter Pecan Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag of boil in bag brown rice
2 tbsp. butter
4 chicken cutlets
3/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 cup refrigerated apple cider
1 tsp. Dijon mustard
1/4 cup chopped pecans
2 tbsp. parsley

Directions:
Cook rice according to directions.
Melt 1 tsp. butter in skillet.  Sprinkle chicken with 1/4 tsp. salt and pepper.  Add to skillet and cook 3 minutes on each side or until done.  Remove from pan.  Add cider and mustard to pan, scraping pan to loosen brown bits.  Cook 2-3 minutes or until syrupy.  Add chicken to pan, toss to coat and remove from heat.
Melt remaining 5 tsp. butter in a skillet and cook for 2 minutes or until browned and fragrant.  Lower heat, add pecans and cook for 1 minute or until toasted, stirring frequently.  Add rice and 1/2 tsp. salt and toss well to coat.  Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
