Title:
Cheesy Corn and Rice Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (5 oz.) package yellow or chicken flavored rice mix
1 can whole kernel corn, drained
1 onion, chopped
2 tbsp. butter
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 cup sour cream
8 oz. shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 tsp. onion powder
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Cook rice according to package directions.  Meanwhile, saute onion in butter until soft.  In a medium sized bowl, combine cooked onion, drained canned corn, cream of chicken soup, sour cream, half of the cheese and all of the seasonings.  Once rice is ready add it to the mixture and stir to combine.  Pour into a greased 2 quart baking dish and top with remaining cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until it is hot and bubbly and cheese it beginning to brown on edges.

Submitter: 
Heather C
Description: 
This is a creamy casserole with tons of flavor that I make often as a side to chicken or pork.  I also take it to family reunions because it is unusual and everyone enjoys it.
