Title:
Fresh Asparagus and Angel Hair Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp. olive oil
1 onion, diced
1 lb. fresh asparagus, washed and trimmed, cut into 1 inch pieces
2 garlic cloves, minced fine
1 lb. angel hair pasta
2 cups grated parmesan cheese
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Steam asparagus until tender crisp. Set aside.  Cook angel hair according to box directions.  Drain and set aside. Using a large saute pan, saute onion in olive oil until nearly transparent.  Add garlic and continue to saute until onion is fully transparent (about another minute).  Toss in asparagus and saute for 1 minute.  Add angel hair, salt and pepper, and saute until pasta is hot and all ingredients are well-blended, about another 2 minutes.  Transfer to serving dish.  Top with grated parmesan.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
This is a light dish suitable as a side or as a main entree.  Other vegetables such as broccoli, baby corn, pea pods may be substituted or blended to make nice variations suitable to your taste.  Feel free to play with the veggies and their amounts.  This dish can be different and exciting each time you make it!
