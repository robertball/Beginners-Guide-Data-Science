Title:
Simple 3x3 marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup olive oil
1/3 cup lemon juice
1/3 cup soy sauce

Directions:
1. Combine all ingredients.
2. Pour over meat and let marinade overnight.

Submitter: 
Mallery
Description: 
A frind of our made this for us several years ago, I've been using it ever since. Best with beef tenderloin, but great with any cut.  Delicious!
