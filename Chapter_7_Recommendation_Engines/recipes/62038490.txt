Title:
Dads Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gal. can northern beans
1 medium onion chopped fine
1 cup ketchup
1/2 box brown sugar
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup molassis
1 pound bacon (cut up, browned, drained)

Directions:
put together in large baking dish and bake uncovered @ 350 for about 1 hour or until dry.

Submitter: 
ERLENSEE_GERMANY
Description: 
This is my dads recipe for baked beans.  It really tastes delicious.  Don't eat them right before you go to bed with your spouse!  I recommend that you use bacon ends and pieces instead of plain bacon!
