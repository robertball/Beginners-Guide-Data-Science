Title:
WICKED GOOD CARAMEL SAUCE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup heavy cream
4 tablespoons butter
Pinch of sea salt
1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Melt butter in pot, mix in brown sugar, cream and salt.
Cook over medium-low heat and whisk gently
for about 7 minutes or longer if you doubled.
Remove the pot from the heat, add vanilla and return to
cook for another minute or so to thicken a bit more.
Take it off the stove and pour into a jar then put in the fridge.

Submitter: 
Julie Jane Anderson
Description: 
Great sauce to have on hand. I use it for ice cream,pour it over cake or even add some to my coffee.
