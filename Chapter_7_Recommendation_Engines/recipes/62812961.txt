Title:
Homemade Fudgesicles (Pudding Pops) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 4-ounce package regular chocolate pudding mix
3 and 1/2 cups skim milk
1 egg (optional)

Directions:
Prepare according to pudding instructions on the box.
Sweeten to taste. (Egg may be added for extra nutritional value)
Freeze in molds OR paper cups and add Popsicle-stick handles.
