Title:
Nuts 'n' Bolts Party Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups toasted oat cereal
4 cups crispy cereal squares
1 cup blanched peanuts
2 cups stick pretzels
3/4 tsp seasoning salt
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 cup oil
1/2 cup butter
1/4 tsp garlic powder

Directions:
Mix cereals, peanuts and pretzels in a large baking pan.
Mix seasoning salt, salt, oil, butter and garlic powder in a medium saucepan and cook to boiling.
Spoon hot mixture over dry ingredients.
Bake in 250F oven fo 2 - 2 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
