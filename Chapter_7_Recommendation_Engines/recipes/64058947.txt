Title:
Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 can chicken gumbo soup
1 TBSP ketchup
1 medium onion, chopped
1 tsp. mustard
salt and pepper
6 buns, toasted

Directions:
Brown beef and onion in skillet, stir to separate meat. Add remaining ingredients simmer 5 to 10 minutes to blend flavors, stirring occasionally. Serve on buns
