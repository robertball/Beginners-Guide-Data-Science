Title:
Buffalo Hot Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/757963.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bottle "Frank's Hot Sauce"
1 stick of salted butter
1 lb. chicken wings

Directions:
combine "Frank's Hot Sauce" and stick of butter in a medium sized sauce pan and heat until combined and steaming.
fry chicken wings without salt or any seasoning until cooked.
combine cooked chicken wings and hot sauce and toss until thoroughly covered.
serve with blue cheese dressing and celery sticks.
* My favorite blue cheese dressing is "Marzetti Chunky Blue Cheese Dressing" and can be found in the produce section of most Wal-Marts.
