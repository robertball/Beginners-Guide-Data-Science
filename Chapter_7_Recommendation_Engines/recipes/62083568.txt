Title:
Smoked Hamhock Super Supper Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 3 smoked hamhocks
1 large onion - roughly diced
3 stalks celery - cut into 1" pieces
1 tablespoon minced garlic
2 tablespoons chicken granules
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 teaspoon thyme
1 lb dried black-eyed peas or butterbeans
2 medium potatoes - diced

Directions:
Place hamhocks in a large pot, fill with water to cover by 1 inch.  Add celery and garlic, bring to a boil.  Do not cover, lower heat to simmer.  Cook for about 1.5 hours or until meat is very tender and comes easily off of the bone.  Remove hocks, debone, chop and return to pot.
Add remaining ingredients, except for potatoes.  Add additional water, about 2 cups, bring to a boil, cover, lower heat and simmer for another hour.  Stir occationally and add water if necessary.  Remove cover, add potatoes and cook until potatoes are tender.
Serve with hot buttered cornbread!

Submitter: 
MA McBridges
Description: 
Smoked hamhocks give this hearty soup a robust flavor.  This is Southern comfort food!  Well worth the time it takes from start to finish.
