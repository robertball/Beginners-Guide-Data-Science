Title:
Shrimp Pasta with Mushrooms and Basil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2166467.jpg
Ingredients:
1 stick butter or margarine
1/4 C Olive Oil
5-6 cloves garlic, chopped
2-3 C chicken broth
1/2-1 Red, Yellow, or orange bell pepper, chopped
1 package fresh mushrooms, sliced
Juice from one lemon
1 lb. thawed uncooked shrimp
1 tsp. garlic powder
2 tsp. Italian Seasoning (oregano, basil, marjoram, etc.)
1/2 C fresh basil, chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
Crushed Red Pepper to taste
Parmesan Cheese for topping
1 lb. pasta of your choice

Directions:
In a large skillet, melt butter/margarine and add olive oil.  Add fresh garlic and saute until light brown.  Add chicken broth, stir and simmer until contents come together.  Add lemon juice, garlic powder, salt, and Italian seasoning.  Add peppers.  Simmer until veggies are soft.
Add shrimp and cook until just pink (this can happen fast, usually 5 minutes or less.  Add fresh chopped basil and cover if you are still waiting for your pasta.
Put cooked pasta in a large bowl and pour contents of shrimp skillet over, garnish with fresh basil.  Top with crushed red pepper and Parmesan cheese.

A quick pasta dish for a weeknight or dinner party- elegant and delicious without much fuss!
