Title:
Mixed Green Salad with Apples and Goddess Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag (8 oz) Mixed greens, pre-washed
1 small fuji apple, chopped
1 large or 2 small carrots, chopped
2 Tbsp sunflower seeds
2 Tbsp Goddess Dressing
1 Tbsp Olive oil
1/2 tsp Dehydrated garlic, minced or powdered
Salt, pepper

Directions:
Put all ingredients in large bowl and mix. Enjoy!!

Submitter: 
Alyse Ellienne
Description: 
Light salad perfect for the summer - mixed greens with apples, carrots, sunflower seeds, goddess and olive oil.
