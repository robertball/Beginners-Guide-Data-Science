Title:
Beans and Greens Soup with Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/784135.jpg
Ingredients:
•	1 bundle broccoli rabe
•	Salt
•	1 pound ground beef or chicken
•	1 1/2 teaspoons fennel seeds, half a palmful
•	1 teaspoon ground red pepper
•	Black pepper
•	5 to 6 cloves garlic, finely chopped, divided
•	2 slices stale white bread, soaked in milk
•	1 egg
•	1/2 cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese, a couple of handfuls, plus reserve rind for soup
•	2 tablespoons EVOO  Extra Virgin Olive Oil
•	1/4 pound pancetta, chopped
•	1 large onion, chopped
•	1 can kidney beans, drained
•	1 quart chicken stock
•	2 to 3 cups water
•	1 cup small-cut pasta, such as mini penne, mezze rigatoni or orechiette (ear-shaped pasta)

Directions:
1.  Blanch broccoli rabe in salted, boiling water for 5 minutes then cold shock, drain and reserve
2.  Mix beef/chicken with fennel seeds, red pepper, black pepper and 2 cloves chopped garlic. Squeeze excess milk from bread and crumble very fine into the bowl, add egg and grated cheese. Mix to combine
3.  Heat EVOO in a pot over medium-high heat. Add pancetta and brown 2-3 minutes. Add onions and remaining garlic, and sauté 5 minutes. Season with salt, pepper. Add stock, water and cheese rind, and bring to a low boil.
4.  Roll meat into balls and add to soup. Cook 15 minutes, add pasta and cook to al dente. Stir in beans and rabe, and adjust seasoning to taste. Serve in bowls and topped with grated cheese.
