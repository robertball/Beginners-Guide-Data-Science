Title:
Strawberry Crunch Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-5 cups fresh spinach leaves
1-2 cups shredded green or red leafy lettuce
about 10 fresh strawberries, sliced
1/2 c. candied walnuts or pecans**
1/4-1/2 c. crumbled blue cheese
1/2 c. mandarin oranges (optional)

Directions:
Toss all ingredients together in a large salad bowl, then serve with raspberry vinaigrette salad dressing.
**An easy way to make these is to combine a bunch of walnuts or pecans in a bowl. Add 1 egg white and mix until coated evenly. Add about 1/2 c. sugar, or to taste, then sprinkle with cinnamon. Then bake for an hour at 250 degrees on a cookie sheet covered in til foil. Stir around every 15 minutes

Submitter: 
Ericamerica
Description: 
This is an easy and delicious salad that has great flavor and a little crunch!
