Title:
Thai Chicken -- cooking light Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked rice
1 1/2 pounds chicken breast tenders
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 tablespoon fish sauce
4 teaspoons canola oil, divided
1 cup sliced onion
2 teaspoons bottled minced garlic
1 teaspoon bottled ground fresh ginger (such as Spice World)
1/2 cup light coconut milk
2 tablespoons Sriracha (hot chile sauce, such as Huy Fong)
1 tablespoon sugar
1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro $
4 lime wedges

Directions:
1. Toss chicken with cornstarch and fish sauce. 2. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. 3. Add chicken to pan; sauté 5 minutes. Remove chicken from pan. 4. Heat remaining 1 teaspoon oil in pan. Add onion, garlic, and ginger to pan; sauté 1 minute. 5. Return chicken to pan; cook 1 minute or until done. 6. Stir in coconut milk, Sriracha, sugar, and juice; cook 45 seconds or until thoroughly heated. 7. Sprinkle each serving with 1 1/2 teaspoons cilantro. 8. Serve chicken mixture over rice with lime wedges.
