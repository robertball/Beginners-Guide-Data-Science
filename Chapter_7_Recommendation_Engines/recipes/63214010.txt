Title:
Kim's Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs. Ground Hamburg 85/15
3 eggs
1 small onion chopped
1/2 Tbsp. basil
1/2 Tbsp. oregano
1/2 Tbsp. garlic powder
1/2 c. Parmesan cheese
1 cup Italian breadcrumbs

Directions:
Mix ingredients in a bowl.  Form meatballs by hand and place in simmering sauce.  Simmer for 4+ hours, of until cooked. You can simmer on the stove top or in a crockpot!
Makes around 36 large meatballs.  I make a large batch and then freeze the rest for my family of 6 to have another meal!!

Submitter: 
kmurph13
Description: 
Melt in your mouth meatballs!  Enough for a small party or to freeze for later meals!! My kids always ask for my homemade meatballs, and I'm sharing the recipe with you!
