Title:
Instant Pot Curried Cheesy Cauliflower Squash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5095250.jpg
Ingredients:
Cauliflower, one medium head, chopped into chunks
Butternut squash, one medium, peeled and chopped into 1-inch chunks
4 cups chicken broth
1 small onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 Tbsp yellow curry powder
1 tsp. salt (or to taste)
3 cups finely shredded mozzarella/cheddar blend cheese
1 Tbsp olive oil

Directions:
Set Instant Pot to Saute.
When heated, add olive oil and onions; saute until onions are soft.
Add garlic and saute another minute.
Add cauliflower, squash and chicken broth.
Place Instant Pot lid on and set to Pressure Cook for 6 minutes with seal ON.
Manually release pressure when done.
Add curry powder and salt.
Use immersion blender in pot until desired texture or carefully place in blender on low, making sure to fill the blender only one-third full at a time to avoid burn accidents.
Stir in cheese and serve immediately. DO NOT SIMMER OR BOIL.
