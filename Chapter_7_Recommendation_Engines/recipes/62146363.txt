Title:
CHICKEN WITH SOUR CREAM GRAVY NOODLES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 BONELESS SKINLESS CHICKEN BREASTS
16 OZ SOUR CREAM
160Z WIDE EGG NOODLES
16OZ WATER
SMALL ONION - CHOPPED
2 TBS PAPRIKA
SALT AND PEPPER TO TASTE

Directions:
PREHEAT OVEN TO 350, PLACE CHICKEN BREASTS IN OVEN SAFE DISH ADD ONION TO CHICKEN. BAKE 15MINS. ADD SOUR CREAM , WATER. SPRINKLE PAPRIKA, SALT AND PEPPER OVER THE CHICKEN. CONTINUE TO BAKE 45 MINS. ENJOY!

THIS EASY TO PREPARE CHICKEN DISH IS KID FRIENDLY AND WITH ONLY FIVE INGREDIENTS IS REALLY TASTY
