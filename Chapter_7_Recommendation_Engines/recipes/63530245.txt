Title:
Chocolate Pumpkin Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/964002.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Box of Chocolate Cake Mix,
1 15oz. Can Pumpkin Puree

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 420.
Mix 1 box of chocolate cake mix and 1 15oz. can of pumpkin puree into a bowl until there are no more lumps.
Put baking cups into muffin tray and add a large spoonful of batter into each baking cup about half way.
Decorate with sprinkles if desired as shown in picture.
Put muffins in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes.
Take out muffins and put onto cooling racks for about 5-10 minutes.
Then Serve your tasty muffins!
