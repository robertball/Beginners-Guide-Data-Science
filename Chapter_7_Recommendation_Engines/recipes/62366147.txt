Title:
Creamy Lemon-Pepper Coleslaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayo or salad dressing
1 tsp lemon-pepper seasoning
1/2 tsp thyme, crushed
5 cups packaged shredded cabbage with carrot
1/4 cup shelled sunflower seeds

Directions:
In large bowl, combine mayo, lemon-pepper & thyme. Stir in cabbage & seeds. Toss lightly to coat. Cover & chill 2 hours before serving
