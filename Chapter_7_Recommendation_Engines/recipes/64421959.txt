Title:
Grandmas Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 3/4 C. sugar
3 large eggs
2 C. all purpose flour
1 C. vegetable oil
1/4 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
5 apples, chopped and peeled
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 C. finely chopped pecans
powdered sugar

Directions:
n a mixer, beat together the eggs,oil and sugar for 6 minutes. Add the soda, flour, cinnamon and salt . Beat till well combined. Stir in the pecans and apples . The batter will be very thick. Pour into a prepared (greased) 9×13 pan baking . Bake in a 355 degree oven for 1 hour. Allow to dust and cool with powdered sugar before serving.
