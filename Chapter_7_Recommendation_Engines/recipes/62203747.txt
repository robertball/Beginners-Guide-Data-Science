Title:
Maraschino Cherry Almond Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup unsalted butter, softened at room temperature
2/3 cup confectioners' sugar, sifted
2 large eggs, at room temperature
1/8 teaspoon (Pinch) salt
1 1/2 teaspoons almond extract
2 cups all purpose flour
2/3 cup chopped pitted Maraschino cherries, about 20 whole cherries
Royal Icing, Optional:
2 egg whites
2 teaspoons lemon juice
3 cups confectioners'sugar, sifted
1/2 teaspoon vanilla or Almond extract
Slivered almonds, optional

Directions:
In a mixer or with a hand mixer in a large bowl,on medium,beat butter until smooth.
Add confectioners' sugar and extract and beat until smooth and well combined.
Add the eggs and the salt, blend until combined.
Reduce speed to low and blend flour until combined.
with a spatula, mix in chopped cherries and any remaining flour.
Form dough into 1 inch to 1 1/4 inch thick logs and wrap with plastic or waxed paper.
Chill dough in the fridge for at least 2 hours
Preheat oven and line two baking sheets with parchment paper. Slice log into 46-50 cookies, place on baking sheets leaving 1/2 inch space between each cookie.
Bake cookies for 12 to 14 minutes until set but not browned. Transfer cookies to cooling racks and cool to room temperature.
Drizzle or dip cooled cookies in Royal Icing, place slivered almonds on iced cookies before hardened.
To make Royal Icing: Beat eggs with lemon juice, add sifted confectioners' sugar and extract and beat on low until combined.
