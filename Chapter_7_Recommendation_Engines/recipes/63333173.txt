Title:
Wendy's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
29 oz tomato sauce
29 oz kidney beans, drained
29 oz pinto beans, drained
1 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
3 stalks celery, chopped
4 oz diced green chiles
28 oz whole tomatoes
3 tsp cumin
3 Tbsp chili powder
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp salt
28 oz water

Directions:
Brown the ground beef, drain off excess fat, set aside.  Saute onion and celery in pan until starting to soften.  Add in everything else and stir to combine.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat, simmer several hours.  Serve with shredded cheddar cheese.
