Title:
Creamy Avocado Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cooked chicken breast halves, diced or shredded
1 ripe avocado, diced
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/2 c cashews
1 tablespoon lime juice
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except avocado and cashews.
Fold in avocado and cashews immediately before serving.

Submitter: 
KKraft
Description: 
This simple, creamy chicken salad with avocado uses sour cream instead of mayonnaise.Modified from: Creamy Avocado Chicken Salad
