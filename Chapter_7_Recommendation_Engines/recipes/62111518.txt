Title:
Cherry Vanilla Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen vanilla yogurt
1 cup apple juice
2 cups frozen cherries

Directions:
Put yogurt and apple juice in the blender first, then add the cherries. Blend until smooth.
