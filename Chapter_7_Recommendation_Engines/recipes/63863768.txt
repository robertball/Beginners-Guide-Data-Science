Title:
Mommom's Apple Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Granny Smith Apples, cored, peeled and thinly sliced
5 tablespoons sugar
2 teaspoons cinnamon
3 cups flour
2 1/2 cups sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 eggs
1 cup vegetable oil
2 teaspoons vanilla
1/3 cup orange juice
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
Glaze:
1 1/2 cups confectioners sugar
2 tablespoons softened butter
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
1-2 tablespoons water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Grease and flour a 10 inch tube pan.  In a large bowl, toss sliced apples with 5 tablespoons sugar and cinnamon. Set aside.  In a large bowl, mix flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt.  In another bowl, combine sugar, eggs, oil, vanilla, and orange juice.  Slowly add the dry ingredients to the wet, batter will be very thick.
Fill prepared pan with 3 layers of batter and 2 layers of apples, starting and finishing with the batter.  Bake for 1 1/2 to 1 3/4 hours until the cake tests done.  Cool on rack for 10 minutes.  Then invert cake to remove from pan and let cool completely.
Glaze:
Combine all ingredients together.  Mix until smooth.  Drizzle on cooled cake.

Submitter: 
Barb0607
Description: 
This is a family favorite that my grandmother handed down to me.  It is similar to a Jewish apple cake.  I love the texture of the cake, crusty on the outside and tender on the inside.
