Title:
Thai Spaghetti Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3646364.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 lb (about three ribbons) JFC Somen noodles-cooked about 3-5 minutes in boiling water
3 tbs ginger-julienned and chopped
2 tbs (about three cloves) fresh garlic-peeled and sliced
13.6 oz co-co-nut milk-dilute by adding equal amount of water
1/4 cup shrimp powder plus 1 tsp shrimp powder with chiles
20 oz crushed pineapples in cans - drained
1/2 a fresh-squeezed lime juice
1/8-1/4 cup sugar
2 tbs fish sauce
cayenne pepper or chili pepper to taste (if you can't find the shrimp powder with chiles)

Directions:
Place co-co-nut milk, water, ginger, garlic, shrimp powders (or shrimp powder and chiles), pineapple, sugar, fish sauce and lime juice into a sauce pan and bring to a simmer. Remove from heat and let cool.
Cook noodles 3-5 minutes until soft. Drain and rinse with cold water.
Add noodles to a serving bowl and pour co-co-nut milk dressing over noodles. Mix well and let sit for about 30 minutes. Refrigerate leftovers.
