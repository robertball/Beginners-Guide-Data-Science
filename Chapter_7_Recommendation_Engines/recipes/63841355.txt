Title:
Steak Tips and Egg Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28oz can  cream of mushroom soup
2 cups milk
1 large sirloin filet
1 Large onion chopped
5 cloves minced garlic
1/4 cup flour
1 bag egg noodles
1 packet beef flavored onion soup
1 container white mushrooms sliced

Directions:
Cube the sirloin season with salt and pepper and toss pieces in flour. Brown the sirloin in about 2 tablespoons olive oil along with the garlic and onions. In a crock pot mix cream of mushroom soup, soup packet, milk, sliced mushrooms. Then add browned sirloin and cook on high till slow boil then turn down to low for at least 4 hours. To thicken before serving add some milk to the 1/4 cup flour and stir in. Cook egg noodles and serve.
