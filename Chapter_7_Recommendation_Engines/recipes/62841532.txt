Title:
Lenten Lunch Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. lemon juice
2 tsp. white vinegar
1/2 clove minced garlic
1 Tbsp. water
2 tsp. yellow mustard
salt and pepper to taste
6 leaves torn or chopped Romaine lettuce
1 tomato, chopped
1/2 cup chopped English cucumber
1/2 avocado, peeled and diced
5 sliced black olives
2 Tbsp. sunflower seeds

Directions:
Mix (with a mini whisk or by shaking around a small sealed container) the first 6 ingredients. Set aside.
Combine lettuce, tomato, cucumber, avocado, and olives.
Pour dressing onto above mixture and toss.
Sprinkle with sunflower seeds.
Yum!

Submitter: 
Saraphim
Description:
