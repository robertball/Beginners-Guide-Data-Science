Title:
Chocolate Chip Coconut M&M; Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744648.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 cup unsalted butter
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup white sugar
1 TBS vanilla extract
1 egg
1 egg yolk
1 1/2 cups Coconut M&Ms;
1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Prepare cookie sheet
Mix flour, baking soda, and salt
On stove over low heat - melt butter, mix in sugars and egg, mix thoroughly; add vanilla
Add wet ingredients to flour mixture, mix well
Stir in M&Ms and chocolate chips
Drop cookies onto baking sheet; bake 15-17 minutes or until lightly brown
Cook 10 minutes on pan before transferring to plate
