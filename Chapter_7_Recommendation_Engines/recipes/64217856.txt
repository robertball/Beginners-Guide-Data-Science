Title:
Grinch Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1639668.jpg
Ingredients:
Grapes
Strawberries (with the tops cut off)
Sliced Bananas
Small Marshmallows

Directions:
On a toothpick, sword, etc... skewer Grape, Banana, Strawberry, and top with the Marshmallow. Cute and Easy!

Submitter: 
CNM CATERING
Description: 
These little skewers are simple to make and a great addition to your appetizers at any Christmas party.
