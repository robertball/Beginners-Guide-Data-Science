Title:
Potato & Turnip Puree Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large red new potatoes
2 large turnips
1/2 cup chicken broth, warmed
1/2 cup non-fat sour cream or 0% plain Greek yogurt
salt and pepper

Directions:
Peel and slice potatoes and turnips 1/4-inch thick.
Cook in boiling water for 15-20 minutes or until fork-tender. Drain.
Whip cooked potatoes and turnips with electric mixer until smooth.
Add warm chicken broth and sour cream/yogurt.
Season with salt and pepper to taste. Whip again until blended.
