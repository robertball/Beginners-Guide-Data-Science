Title:
Millie's Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 cup white sugar
3 rounded tbsp. cocoa
2 eggs
1/2 cup flour
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 cup walnuts of pecans

Directions:
Cream together softened butter, sugar and sifted cocoa.
Add well beaten eggs, flour, salt and walnuts.
Spread in greased 8"x8" pan.
Bake @ 350 F. for 25-30 minutes.
Top with your favorite chocolate icing
