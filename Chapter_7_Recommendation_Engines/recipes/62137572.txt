Title:
Sausage Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 sausage links
6 eggs
4 tablespoons flour
4 tablespoons milk
1 ½ cups shredded co-jack or sharp cheddar (the more flavorful the cheese the better for this recipe)
9 inch pie crust

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Prick pie crust with holes all over and bake 10 minutes
Cook sausage and dice
Beat together milk, flour, eggs and cheese in a large bowl
Add sausage to egg mix
Pour egg mix into pre-baked pie crust
Bake another 30-40 minutes until center is set

Submitter: 
Melissa Arenson
Description: 
A basic, easy to make quiche with lots of flavor. I like complicated quiche, but a picky friend came over so I made this and it turned out great. She’d never had quiche, but loved it.
