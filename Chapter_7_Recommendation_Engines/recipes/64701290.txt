Title:
Lovely Lemony Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5365593.jpg
Ingredients:
1 - 1 1/2 pound baby red potatoes
Kosher salt and fresh ground black pepper to taste
1/4 cup sour cream (cut calories by using low-fat sour cream)
2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
3 stalks celery, thinly sliced
2 tbsp chopped fresh chives

Directions:
Place baby potatoes in a medium sauce pot and cover with enough water to cover. Bring to a boil and add 2 teaspoons of kosher salt.,
Reduce heat and simmer until tender ( approximately 12-14 mins.)
Drain, run under cold water to cool and cut into quarters
In a medium mixing bowl, whisk together the sour cream, lemon juice, 1/4  teaspoon pepper.
Add the potatoes, celery and chives and toss to combine
I find chilling for 30 mins perfect.

Submitter: 
Sahara B
Description: 
Who doesn't love potato salad?...This Lemony take on a classic is great and lite. I say serve with a great NY strip steak, or maybe with Cajun Blackened Shrimp...your possibilities are endless you know...the creation/combination is the tell all my friends.
