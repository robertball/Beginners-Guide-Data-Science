Title:
April's Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/456091.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup coconut oil
3/4 cup honey
3/4 cup ground flax meal (or wheat germ)
1 tablespoon vanilla
1 tablespoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
1/4 cup sesame seeds
1/4 cup sunflower seeds
3 1/2 cups old-fachioned oats
3 cups chopped nuts (walnuts, almonds, pecans)
2 cups chopped dried friut (cranberries, apricots, black currants, mango, raisins)

Directions:
In a large bowl stir together oil, honey, flax, vanilla and salt. Stir in oats and nuts. Spread evenly on a shallow baking sheet. Bake 15 minutes at 375* stirring every 5 minutes. Stir in friut. Cool to room temperature. Store in ziplock bag.

Submitter: 
April Fear
Description: 
A healthy granola made with coconut oil, honey and flax meal. I put in a variety of nuts and dried friuts to make it a little different each time.
