Title:
Marinated Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds small mushrooms
1 envelope Italian dressing mix
1 cup water
1/2 cup olive oil
1/3 cup cider vinegar
2 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp sugar
1 tbsp minced fresh parsley
1 tbsp soy sauce
2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper

Directions:
Remove stems from mushrooms.
Place caps in large saucepan; cover with water.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat. Simmer for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally. Drain.
In jar with a tight lid combine all other ingredients; shake well.
Mix mushrooms and dressing mixture in large bowl. Cover and refrigerate at least 8 hours, preferably overnight.
