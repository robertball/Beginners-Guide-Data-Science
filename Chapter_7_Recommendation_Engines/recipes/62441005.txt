Title:
Tracy Ann's Five-Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/457694.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can chickpeas (garbanzo beans), drained & rinsed
1 can black beans, drained & rinsed
1 can kidney beans, drained & rinsed
1 can cut green beans, drained
1 can wax beans, drained
1 medium onion, chopped
Marinade:
1 cup apple cider vinegar
1 cup sugar
3/4 cup cold water
1 tsp.salt
1/2 tsp. pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix vinegar, sugar, water, salt & pepper. Add chickpeas, black beans, kidney beans,& onions and mix well.
Gently fold in green beans & wax beans.
Chill for 2 hours or overnight.
Before serving, add olive oil and stir thoroughly.

Submitter: 
Tracy Ann
Description: 
Low-sodium, sugar-free salad with five kinds of beans in an oil-and-vinegar marinade.  This colorful classic is great to take to picnics or a pitch-in.Modified from: Five-Bean Salad
