Title:
Kansas City Style Cheesy Corn Recipe

Ingredients:
2 ears of sweet corn, kernels removed
1/2 sweet red bell pepper, diced
1/4 white onion, diced
8 jalape√±o pepper rings, pickled, diced
2 tsps Jack Stack seasoning
1 tsp garlic powder
1 c shredded cheddar cheese
3 heaping spoonfuls of fat free cream cheese
1/2 cup diced ham
1/3 c milk
3 tbsp butter
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Melt butter in skillet, stir in flour and cook one minute and then stir in milk, whisking constantly until bubbly and thick. Add more milk if too thick. Add the cream cheese and shredded cheese, stir constantly. Add in the rest of the ingredients and cook on low until cheeses have melted. Season and then pour into a large ramekin and bake at 350 for 20 min. Serves two.
