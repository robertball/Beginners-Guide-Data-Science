Title:
Bleu Cheese Ball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. butter, at room temperature
1 8 oz. brick cream cheese, at room temperature
8 oz. crumbled blue cheese, at room temperature
3 green onions, finely sliced, green part only
1 clove garlic, very finely minced
1 small can chopped black olives
Parsley or nuts, very finely chopped

Directions:
Let butter and cheeses come to room temperature. When softened, put into bowl of a mixer or food processor and beat/process until smooth. Stir in the garlic, green onions and olives till well blended. Put into a small bowl, cover and refrigerate until easy to handle, at least two hours.
Chop the parsley or nuts until very fine.
Once the cheese mixture has firmed up, shape into a ball (or a log). Roll in the parsley (or nuts) until covered.
Can be served immediately or refrigerated until ready to serve. Best at room temperature, served with crackers.
