Title:
Jim's Lobster Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 oz lobster meat ( Bite Size)
1/4 C butter
2 C Half and Half
3 T flour (all purpose)
1 T chopped onion
1/4 C white wine
2 T mustard ( prepared)
1/2 t Worchester sauce
1/8 t Tabasco Sauce
1/2 t Curry powder
1/2 t Parsley
Topping
1 C Ritz crackers crushed
1/2 C Butter

Directions:
Simmer the half and half over medium heat. In a seperate pan melt the butter and add the onions...saute until the onions are translucent. Add the flour to the butter and onions. Simmer for a minute then whisk the hot half and half in. Then add all the other ingredients EXCEPT the wine and lobster. Simmer for 10 minutes. Add the wine simmer for 2 more minutes. Place the lobster in a bowl and add the sauce. Bake either in a casserole dish OR I use French onion style individual ceramic baking bowls.
Ritz cracker topping
Mix the 1 C of crushed Ritz crackers with 1/2 C melted butter.Sprinkle over the lobster casserole.
