Title:
Cheesy Chipotle Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1006488.jpg
Ingredients:
1 16oz can Refried Beans
8oz, cream cheese, softened
4 cloves garlic, pressed
1t onion powder
1 cup HERDEZ® Traditional Chipotle Mexican Cooking Sauce
1 cup shredded mexican cheese
1/2 cup shredded mexican cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
In a medium sauce pan combine refried beans, cream cheese, HERDEZ® Traditional Chipotle Mexican Cooking Sauce, garlic and onion powder and cook on medium heat until bubbly stirring often.
Stir in 1 cup of shredded mexican cheese. Continue stirring and cook until cheese is melted and well combined in bean mixture.
Pour bean mixture into baking dish and top with 1/2 cup shredded mexican cheese.
Bake for 10 minutes or until cheese is melted.
