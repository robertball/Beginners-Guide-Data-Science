Title:
Cheddar Biscuits - Similar to Red Lobster Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/321979.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups Biscuit Baking Mix
2 cups finely shredded Sharp Cheddar cheese
1-1/3 cups milk or buttermilk
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 cup buttter, melted
2 tablespoon and 2 teaspoon dried parsley
2 teaspoons garlic powder or to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
Grease a cookie sheet, or line with parchment paper.
In a large bowl, combine Bisquick, Cheddar cheese, and garlic powder. Stir in milk.
Drop batter by heaping tablespoonfuls onto prepared cookie sheet.
Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes.
Mix melted butter with parsley and garlic powder. Brush biscuits. Bake for 5 more minutes, or until lightly browned on the bottom.
