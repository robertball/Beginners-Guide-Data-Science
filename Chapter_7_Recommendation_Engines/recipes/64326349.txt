Title:
Satay dipping sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp cooked chopped onion
1 tsp each chopped garlic, ginger
1/4 tsp  dried chili pepper
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/2 can of 400ml coconut milk
1 Tbsp soya sauce
1 tsp brown sugar
1 1/2 Tbsp each oyster sauce, lime juice

Directions:
fry garlic and ginger in butter, add chili peppers then all other ingredients
cook 10 minutes on low
