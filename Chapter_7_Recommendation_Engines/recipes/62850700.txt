Title:
Pressure Cooker Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/680088.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into 1 inch cubes
3 tbsp butter
Salt & Pepper
1 can cream of celery soup
1 can beef broth
1 large onion, sliced
1 tbsp minced garlic
1 cup dry red wine
8 oz bag of mini carrots
2 potatoes, cubed
1 small bag frozen peas
8 oz sliced mushrooms, fresh
4 oz sour cream

Directions:
Pat dry beef stew meat with paper towel and salt/pepper to taste.  Brown beef in pressure cooker in butter.  Remove beef, deglaze (if necessary) with red wine.  Add wine, broth, soup, garlic, onions, potatoes and carrots. Ensure that liquid just covers the ingredients; add more wine or broth if needed.  Bring to pressure, cook for 20 minutes.
Cool and open cooker - add mushrooms, peas and sour cream.  Bring to pressure, cook for 5 minutes.
