Title:
sprite fizzy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/440690.jpg
Ingredients:
*4 cans cold sprite
*1 package blue raspberry jello

Directions:
1.follow directions on jello box.
2. before you put jello in fridge let it cool off 5 minutes then instead of putting it in fridge pour into ice cube trays. and put in freezer
3.once jello is frozen put cubes into a pitcher, pour in the sprite
4.stir slightly then serve
