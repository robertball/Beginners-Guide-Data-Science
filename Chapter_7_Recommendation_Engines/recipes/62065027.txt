Title:
Stuffed Dates Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 pitted dates
3 ounces cream cheese, softened
12 walnut halves

Directions:
Cut a slit into each date, being careful not to cut all the way through.  Fill each date with cream cheese, then stuff with a walnut half.  Store covered in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
MOMMYCOFFEY
Description: 
Sweet pitted dates stuffed with cream cheese and walnuts!
