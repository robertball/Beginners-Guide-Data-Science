Title:
Herdez Cream of Guajillo Chile, Corn and Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1007354.jpg
Ingredients:
3 Boneless and Skinless Chicken Breasts
1 Jar of Herdez Red Guajillo Chile Cooking Sauce
32 oz. Carton of Chicken Broth
2 Tbsp. Crushed Cilantro
3 Cloves of Chopped Garlic
2 Tsp. Salt
1 Tsp. Pepper
1 packet Mild Taco Seasoning
8 oz. Frozen Corn
1 C. Heavy Cream or 1 container of Philly Cream Sauce - Santa Fe Flavor
Optional:
Rice
Santa Fe Style Tortilla Strips
Lime Slices
Sour Cream

Directions:
Combine the Chicken Broth, the Red Guajillo Chile Cooking Sauce, Cilantro, Garlic, Salt and Pepper in a crock-pot.
Add 3 Boneless and Skinless Chicken Breasts to the crock-pot.
Set crock-pot to high and let it go for approximately 4 hrs or until chicken is cooked thoroughly, or set to low and let it cook for about 8 hrs.
When chicken is thoroughly cooked, shred the chicken, add corn and the heavy cream (optional).
Stir and turn heat down to warm.
Serve and enjoy!
Optional:
Serve hot soup over precooked rice and top with some Santa Fe Style Tortilla Strips, sour cream and lime, if desired.

Submitter:

Description: 
This was a quick and easy soup that can be easily adjusted by eliminating or adding additional ingredients.  The addition of Herdez Red Guajillo Chile Mexican Cooking Sauce adds a bit of heat to the soup while acting as a base to create a delicious yet spicy soup!
