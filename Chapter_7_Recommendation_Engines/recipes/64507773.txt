Title:
Peach Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 7-inch prepared loaf angel food cake
1 14-ounce can sweetened condensed milk
1 cup cold water
1 tsp. almond extract
1 4-ounce package instant vanilla pudding mix
2 cups whipping cream, whipped
4 cups pared, sliced fresh peaches (about 2 pounds)

Directions:
Cut cake into 1/4 inch slices; arrange 1/2 on bottom of 13x9 inch baking dish.
In large mixing bowl combine milk, water and extract; mix well.   Add pudding mix and beat well.   Chill 5 minutes.
Fold in whipped cream.   Pour half the cream mixture over cake slices.  Arrange half the peaches on top.  Repeat layering, ending with peaches.
Chill 4 hours or until set.   Cut into squares to serve.   Refrigerate leftovers.
