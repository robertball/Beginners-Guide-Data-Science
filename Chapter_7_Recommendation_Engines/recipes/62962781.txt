Title:
Pork Marinade by Deb Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp  honey mustard
pineapple juice from 1 can chunk
2 tsp frahm seasoning
5 shakes worcheschier sauce
1/4 tsp cumin pepper
Pork loin cut in 1 inch slices

Directions:
Mix together mustard, seasoning, pineapple juice and and chill.
Fork steak while in marinade. Pour over steak while grilling.  May sprinkle w frahm seasoning for greater kick

Submitter:

Description: 
A sweetened mustard, cumin and pineapple flavored marinade for your pork recipes.Modified from: Pork Marinade
