Title:
Andrea's Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz package cream cheese, softened
1 can refried beans
1 cup salsa or picante sauce
1- 1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Spread the softened cream cheese in a glass pie plate or small baking dish. Top with the refried beans, then the salsa or picante sauce. Microwave for approx. 2-3 minutes, until just heated. Top with shredded cheese and heat another 1-2 minutes, until the cheese is melted. Serve with tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
andrea_d
Description: 
4 simple ingredients make a wonderfully easy layered dip that's always a hit.
