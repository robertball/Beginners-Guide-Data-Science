Title:
Ground Pork Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/812977.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
3 eggs
2 1/2 tablespoons fish sauce
1 pinch pepper
6 ounces ground pork

Directions:
Heat oil in a wok or skillet over medium heat. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs, fish sauce, pepper and ground pork. When oil is hot, pour in egg mixture. Fry until fully cooked, and golden on both sides.

Submitter: 
BK
Description: 
It smells good when it's cooked. Cut in wedges, and serve with jasmine rice. You can have it anytime! If you like spicy, put in some chile sauce.
