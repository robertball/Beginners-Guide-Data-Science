Title:
Frito Chili Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Bag of Fritos Corn Chips
15 oz can of Chili with Beef
Shredded Cheese
Optional:
Jalapenos
Onions
Tomatoes
Lettuce
Sour cream

Directions:
In an oven safe dish, pour in corn chips and spread evenly. Heat chili and pour evenly over corn chips. Add other ingredients to your liking. Sprinkle with cheese and pop into the oven at 350 degrees until cheese is melted. Serve
