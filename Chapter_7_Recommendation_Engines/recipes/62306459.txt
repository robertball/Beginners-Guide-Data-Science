Title:
Karla's Favorite Cucumbers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 medium tomatoes, sliced
1 medium onion, sliced and separated into rings
2 large cucumbers, thinly sliced
1/3 cup oil
1/3 cup wine vinegar OR white vinegar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 1/2 teaspoons dried salad seasoning

Directions:
Sprinkle tomatoes with salt.
Alternate layers of tomato, onion and cucumber in deep bowl with tight-fitting lid or a large wide mouth jar.
Combine remaining ingredients in a bowl or shaker and blend well.
Pour over vegetables.  Seal tightly.
Refrigerate for 5-6 hours to allow flavors to blend, turning over every hour or so.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
This old family favorite is good anytime of the year - especially in the summer when heating up the kitchen is not an option.  Great for cookouts!
