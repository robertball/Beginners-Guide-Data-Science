Title:
Healthy Ranch Turkey Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds ground turkey (2 Packages)
1/2 cup oatmeal crumbs
2 Tablespoons granulated onion
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1/4 cup chopped dried parsley
1 teaspoon garlic granules
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 Tablespoons Dry Ranch Dressing Mix

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix all ingredients. Form into 12 patties.
Cook the patties in a medium skillet over medium heat, turning once, to an internal temperature of 180 degrees F (85 degrees C).
Can freeze additional patties on a pan, then transfer to a ziploc bag for storage.

Submitter:

Description: 
Turkey burgers are a delicious change of pace. Slap these moist patties on the grill for a special summer treat!Modified from: Actually Delicious Turkey Burgers
