Title:
Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 can (15 oz) pure pumpkin in a can or 2 cups of fresh pumpkin puree.
* 1 can (12 oz.) evaporated milk
* 2/3 cup sugar
* 1 teaspoon nutmeg
* 1 unbaked 9-inch deep dish pie shell
* pinch of salt
* 2 eggs

Directions:
1)Mix together the pumpkin, evaporated milk, sugar, nutmeg, salt, and eggs. After all is well blended pour into pie shell.
2)Bake in preheated 400 Degree (or 350 Degree f) oven for 15 minutes. Reduce temperature to 350 Degrees F and bake for 40 to 50 minutes or until knife near center comes out clean. Cool on wire rack for 2 hours.
