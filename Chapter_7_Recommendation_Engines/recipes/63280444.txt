Title:
Lemon BBQ Chicken Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups chicken, cut in bite size pieces
2 Tbsp lemon juice
2 cups cooked rice
1 stalk celery, sliced
1/3-1/2 cup green olives w/pimento, sliced
1/4 cup + 2 Tbsp crumbled bacon
1 cup mayonaise
2 Tbsp bbq sauce
1/2 tsp hickory smoke flavor

Directions:
In large mixing bowl mix together chicken and lemon juice. Set aside. In another bowl combine cooked rice, sliced celery, olives, and 1/4 cup crumbled bacon. Mix in chicken.
Make dressing in small bowl by combining mayonaise, bbq sauce, and hickory smoke flavoring. Stir into chicken and rice mixture. Sprinkle remaining 2 Tbsp crumbled bacon over top of salad. Refrigerate for 2 hours or overnight before serving.

Submitter: 
Robin Whitworth
Description: 
A zesty summer salad to serve as a cool summer supper, potluck contribution, or even a ladies luncheon. A good way to use up leftover chicken and rice.
