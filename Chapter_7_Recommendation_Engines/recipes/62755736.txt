Title:
Broiled Sesame Cod Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. Cod Fillets
1 tsp. melted butter
1 tsp. lemon juice
1 tsp. tarragon
pinch Pepper
1 Tbsp. Whole Sesame Seeds

Directions:
Preheat broiler. Line broiler pan with foil. Arrange cod on pan. Brush with butter. Mix together lemon juice, tarragon, and pepper. Brush over fish and sprinkle with sesame seeds. Broil until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork, about 10 minutes.
