Title:
Island Fish Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds of fish cut into pieces
6 cups water
1 hot chili pepper
1 teaspoon dried thyme
2 tablespoons lime juice
2 chopped onions
2 chopped tomatoes
chopped fresh parsley, for garnish

Directions:
Place all ingredients, except parsley, in a pot.
Bring to the boil and simmer gently for about an hour.
Strain off liquid, discard solids  and serve with chopped parsley.

Submitter: 
mikekey
Description: 
This is actually a fish broth eaten as a soup.  Since it is strained, you can use fish scraps such as heads and tails.  Try not to break the chili, unless you want a spicier soup.
