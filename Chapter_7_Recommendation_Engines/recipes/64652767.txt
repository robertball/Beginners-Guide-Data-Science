Title:
Chocolate Chip Energy Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups Oats
1 1/2 cups Honey
1 1/2 cups Peanut Butter (any kind you like works)
3/4 cup ground flax seed
1 T chia seeds
1/2 cup semi sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and roll into balls using a tablespoon to measure

Submitter: 
Genevieve Johns Seivert
Description: 
Delicious energy balls that are easy and good for you!
