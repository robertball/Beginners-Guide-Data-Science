Title:
Grilled T-Bone Steaks with Gorgonzola Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Market Pantry™ Butter, softned
4 ounces crumbled Gorgonzola cheese
2 tablespoons minced fresh chives
4 Sutton & Dodge™ T-bone Steaks
Archer Farms™ Steak Seasoning

Directions:
Mix together butter, gorgonzola, chives and black pepper. Set aside.
Season steaks and grill or broil 4-5 minutes per side.
Top each steak with dollop of gorgonzola butter mixture.
Serve with baked potatoes.

Submitter: 
SuperTarget
Description: 
The classic sizzling steak gets an update with a dollop of chive-spiked gorgonzola butter. Round out the menu with a tossed green salad and steamed summer squash or another favorite fresh vegetable.
