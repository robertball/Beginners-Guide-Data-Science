Title:
Pumpkin Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can pumpkin
1 box lowfat low cal vanilla pudding mix
1 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1 serving low fat whipped topping

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except whipped topping. Refrigerate for 2 hours and serve with whipped topping.
