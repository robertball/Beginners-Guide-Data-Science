Title:
Ukranian Lima Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 oz. pkg frozen lima beans, cooked
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. sugar
1 Tbsp. oil ( cream or milk)
1/2 C. celery
1/2 C. sour cream

Directions:
Cook beans according to directions on the package. Drain beans and return to the pot. Add the remaining ingredients and heat on low for 5 minutes to blend ingredients.
Serve.
