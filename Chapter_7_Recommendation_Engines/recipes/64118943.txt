Title:
Chicken Hot Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For casserole:
1 1/2 cup macaroni pasta (uncooked)
3 cups cooked cubed chicken
1 cup bread crumbs
1 cup grated cheddar cheese
1/2 cup mushrooms
3 eggs slightly beaten
1 sm. can chow mein noodles
1 cup chicken broth
1 cup milk
1/2 cup oleo
1 tsp salt
1/4 cup green pepper
For gravy topping:
1/2 cup oleo
1/2 cup flour
2 cup chicken broth
1/2 cup mushrooms 1/4 cup cream
1/2 tsp lemon juice
salt to taste

Directions:
Melt oleo and brown muchrooms lightly. Add bread crumbs and stir thoroughly. Combine all ingredients except chow mein noodles, which are cattered on top.
Pour into 9x13" pan and bake 45 minutes at 350 degrees.
Cook until thick and when hot, dish is done. Top with gravy.
