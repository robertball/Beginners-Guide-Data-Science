Title:
Old Fashioned Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter at room temperature
1 cup white sugar
2 egg yolks, beaten
1 tablespoon milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
4 oz glass drinking glass
1/4 cup melted butter
1/4 cup confectioners' sugar, for sprinkling

Directions:
1. Cream butter in a large bowl.
2. Beat in sugar, yolks, milk and vanilla.
3. Sift together flour, baking powder and salt in a medium bowl. Add to butter mixture. Mix well.
4. Cover and refrigerate for 3 to 4 hours or until dough is firm.
5. Heat oven to 350 degrees.
6. Roll dough into small balls about 3/4" in diameter.
7. Place balls 2 " apart on ungreased cookie sheets. Lightly flatten cookies with the bottom of a sugar dipped glass.
8. Bake 8 to 10 minutes, or until edges are slightly browned. Keep close eye on cookies as the more you bake, the faster the batches cook.
9. Transfer to wire racks for cooling.
10. While the cookies are still slightly warm, but not hot!, lightly brush with melted butter and dust with confectioners' sugar.

Submitter: 
KATHARINENOLA
Description: 
A family handed down Christmas cookie favorite. My mother in law has been making these with her mom since she was little and has now passed the recipe on to myself and her grandchildren. An absolute melt in your mouth cookie that you will crave.
