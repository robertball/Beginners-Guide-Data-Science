Title:
Big Ray's Spicy Buffalo Beer Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068044.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. (16 oz.) cream cheese, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1 can (10 ounces) chunk white chicken, drained
1/2 cup light beer
1/2 cup Sour Cream
1/4 cup hot pepper sauce for Buffalo wings
1/2 cup Natural Blue Cheese Crumbles, divided
2 green onions, sliced

Directions:
Microwave cream cheese and beer in large microwaveable bowl on HIGH 5 min. or until completely melted, stirring after 3 minutes. Mix in chicken and 1/4 cup blue cheese. Top with remaining blue cheese. Stir in remaining ingredients.

Submitter:

Description: 
Looking for a new dip to wow your guests with? This Spicy Buffalo Beer Dip brings together the great flavors of Blue cheese and Buffalo wings  in one creamy dish! So pass this spicy, cheesy dip around the party for an instant hit!
