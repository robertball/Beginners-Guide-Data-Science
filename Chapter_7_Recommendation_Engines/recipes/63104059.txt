Title:
Chicken Breast Stuffed with Broccli and Cheese Submitted by: YOGADEDRA Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 Cup Broccli
6 Oz Chicken breast
1/2 Ounce Montery Jack Cheese
I also add pepper use as much or as little as you like

Directions:
Take Chicken Breast and flatten out.
Place Broccli and Cheese inside chicken and roll up.
secure each rollup with a toothpick
Place in dish
Bake in Oven 350 Degrees for about an hour or until chicken is cooked all the way.
