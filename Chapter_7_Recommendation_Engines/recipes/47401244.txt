Title:
Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 slices bacon
12 oz. spinach
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
2 Tbl. white vinegar
2 tsp. Dijon mustard
1 tsp. sugar
1 egg, beaten

Directions:
Fry the bacon, drain, and crumble.
If desired, lightly steam spinach (30 seconds in microwave works)
Heat lemon juice, vinegar, mustard and sugar to boiling, pour in beaten egg and cook slowly 3 minutes.
Toss dressing with spinach and bacon.
