Title:
Easy Egg Pizza Crust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 whole eggs
1/2 C. flour
1 T. grated Parmesan cheese
1 ts. garlic powder

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in blender for 60 seconds at high speed. Pour into a 12 inch round or 9x13 lightly greased pan. Bake in preheated oven at 350 for 12 minutes. Remove and make your pizza.
