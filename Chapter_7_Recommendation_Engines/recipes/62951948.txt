Title:
Buttermilk Wheat Bread (Bread Machine) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups buttermilk
1 1/2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons sugar
1 teaspoon salt
3 cups bread flour
1/3 cup whole wheat flour
2 teaspoons active dry yeast

Directions:
In bread machine pan, place all ingredients in order given. Select "white bread" setting. Bake according to bread machine directions. Yield: 1 loaf (2 pounds).

Submitter: 
Tina Pendergraph
Description: 
The buttermilk gives this bread a wonderfully unique taste and a pretty golden crust.
