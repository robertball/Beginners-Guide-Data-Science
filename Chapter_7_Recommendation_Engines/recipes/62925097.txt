Title:
Oatmeal Raisin Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sticks Butter, softened
1/2 c granulated sugar
2 large eggs, beaten
1 tsp. REAL vanilla
1 1/2 c all purpose flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. salt
3 c. Old Fashioned uncooked oatmeal
1 cup raisins or crainsons (or 1/2 c each)

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 F.
Beat together butter and sugars until creamy
Add beaten eggs and vanilla mixing very well
Combine flour, soda, cinnamon and salt mix well
Add to egg mixture
Stir in oats and raisins mixing well
Drop by T. onto ungreased cookie sheet
Bake 12 minutes or until golden
Cool 1 minute on cookie sheet, remove to wire rack to cool.
Makes about 4 dozen cookies
Store in air tight container
