Title:
Pretzel Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3615169.jpg
Ingredients:
8 egg, beaten
1 tablespoon butter
1 1/2 cups mini pretzels

Directions:
In a large skillet, melt butter over medium high heat. Add eggs and pretzels to pan. Cook, stirring occasionally, until eggs are set. Serve hot.

Submitter: 
IRENEVE
Description: 
Great for kids!  I used this to trick my best friends child into trying eggs! You can also add vegetables, cheese and meat.
