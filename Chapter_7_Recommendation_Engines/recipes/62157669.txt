Title:
Peanut Butter-Chocolate Chip Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 Cup butter, softened
1 Cup peanut butter (Creamy or Chunky)
1/2 Cup white sugar
3/4 Cup brown sugar
2 Eggs
1 Cup sour cream
1/2 Cup milk
2 tsp vanilla extract
2 Cups all purpose flour
1/4 Cup cornstarch
1/2 Tbs baking powder
1/4 c salt
2 Cups milk chocolate chips or chunks

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
Cream butter, peanut butter and sugars with a mixer 2 minutes.
Add the eggs, one at a time, and beat 2 more minutes.
Add the vanilla extract, sour cream and milk. Mix well.
In another bowl mix the all purpose flour, cornstarch, baking powder and salt. Add to the peanut butter mixture and mix only until it's combined (do not over mix).
Fold the chocolate chips or chunks.
Pour the mixture into 12 greased muffin cups. Bake 20-30 minutes or until they look golden brown.
