Title:
Corn Fancy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups cream-style corn
1/2 cup veggie oil
1 tsp. garlic salt
1 sm jar pimentos chopped
4 eggs
1pkg. corn muffin mix
1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
beat eggs, add all other ingredients except cheese. mix well. pour into 1 1/2 quart casserole, top with cheese. Bake at 300 for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Pamela Evans Steeley

Good recipe for holidays or potluck.
