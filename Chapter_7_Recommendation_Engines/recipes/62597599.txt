Title:
Black Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans black beans, drained
1 can diced tomato
1 can corn, drained
1/2 small red onion, chopped
1/4 c fresh cilantro, chopped
1/2t pepper
1/2T salt
1T lemon juice
1/2T red wine vinegar

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and refrigerate until served.

Submitter: 
Christine Simon Michener
Description: 
Delicious with tortilla chips and good for you!
