Title:
Koulourakia (Butter Twist Cookies) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb sweet butter
1 1/2 cups sugar
3 eggs
1 tbs vanilla
2 lb flour
3 tsp baking powder
juice of one orange
sesame seeds (optional)

Directions:
cream butter and add sugar; add eggs and flavoring. Gradually add flour that has been sifted with baking powser. Knead all ingredients.  enough orange juice to make a good medium dough. If too soft, add a little more flour. Shape into twists then brush with milk. Sprinkle with sesame seeds. Bake in 350-degree oven until brown.
