Title:
Cream Cheese Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. cream cheese
1/2 cup sugar
4 oz. Cool Whip
1 T. lemon juice
Fruit topping

Directions:
Mix all ingredients(except fruit topping)until smooth.  Pour into prepared graham cracker crust.  Top with your favorite fruit topping. Blueberry, cherry, strawberry, etc.  Refrigerate for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
CAROL46
Description: 
This pie is a lighter version of cheese cake.  It is so easy and so delicious.
