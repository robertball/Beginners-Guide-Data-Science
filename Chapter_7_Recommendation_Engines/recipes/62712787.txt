Title:
Mashed Cauliflower and Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 16 oz package of frozen cauliflower
1 - 16 oz package of frozen carrots
1 - 8 oz tub of fat free sour cream
1/4 cup of apple cider vinegar
1 tsp of Garlic Powder
salt and pepper to taste
4 TBSP of butter (optional)

Directions:
Boil Cauliflower and Carrots until soft
Drain
Add fat free sour cream, apple cider vinegar, garlic powder, salt, pepper and butter.
Mash by hand or use food processor to desired consistency.

Submitter: 
friskyspatula
Description: 
This is great way to substitute mashed potatoes.  It has a unique flavor and is full of veggie goodness.
