Title:
Sweet Potato Dill Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/384196.jpg
Ingredients:
4 large sweet potatoes
1 cup chopped celery
1 small onion, chopped
1 cup sour cream
1/4 cup parsley
1 Tbs lemon juice
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp celery seeds
1/4 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp crushed dill
pepper to taste

Directions:
Boil potatoes until tender (25-30 mins). Cool, peel, cube. Combine with other ingredients.  Serve chilled or room temperature.  Can sub fresh dill for parsley and dried dill.
