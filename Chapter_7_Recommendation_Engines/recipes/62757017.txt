Title:
Blackberry Pie I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5-6 cups fresh blackberries
2/3 cup white sugar
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
2 tbps butter
1 ruth's pie crust once in plate brush 1/2 beaten egg of bottom before adding filling
use the rest of the beaten egg for spreading on top of crust
sprinking of white sugar

Directions:
Combine 4 1/2 cups berries with the sugar and flour.  Spoon the mixture into an unbaked pie shell. Brush Beaten egg of crust.  Spread the remaining 1/2-1 cup of berries on top of the sweetened berries. Cut of the cold butter and spread pats out evenly around the pie, now and cover with the top crust.  Seal and crimp the edges.  Brush the top crust with rest of egg, and sprinkle with sugar.
Bake at 425 degree F (220 degrees C) for 15 minutes.  Reduce the temperature of the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C), and bake for an additional 20 to 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
Shannon O'Keefe
Description: 
This is a simple pie to make with amazing results. There is nothing quite like a blackberry pie warm from the oven. Except perhaps a slice of blackberry pie with a scoop of vanilla ice cream melting deliciously.Modified from: Blackberry Pie I
