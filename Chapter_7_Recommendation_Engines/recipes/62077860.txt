Title:
Spicy Cheesy Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Two russet potatoes
1 purple onion
1 - 8 oz. block Velveeta jalapeno
cheese (can use regular Velveeta for milder flavor)
1 stick butter
Olive Oil

Directions:
Wash potatoes and slice in 1/4" slices. Wash and clean onion and slice in as thin a slices possible.  Slice butter in thin slices and grate cheese.
In a 8" round baking dish, drizzle bottom with olive oil and then put layer of potatos, sliced onions, few pats of butter and cheese.  Continue layering ending with cheese. Cover with foil and bake for 1 hour until everything is melted and potatos are soft.
