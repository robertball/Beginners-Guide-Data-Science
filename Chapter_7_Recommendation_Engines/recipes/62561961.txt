Title:
Mardis Gras Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb andouille sausage
1/2-1 green pepper, chopped
1/2-1 onion, chopped
1/2 lb shrimp, shelled and deveined (raw or cooked)
3 cloves garlic, minced
3 T butter
3 T flour
14.5-oz can diced tomatoes
2 c heavy cream or 1/2 and 1/2
1/2 t red pepper
1/2 t black pepper
1/2 c grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook sausage, pepper and onion together until sausage is done.
Add shrimp to sausage.  If using raw shrimp, cook until done.
Add garlic to sausage and shrimp and toss for one minute.  Set aside.
Make a roux with the butter and flour.
Add tomatoes to the roux and heat to boiling, stirring constantly.
Slowly add cream to the tomato mixture, stirring constantly.  Do not boil after the cream is added to the pot.
Add red and black pepper and cheese to the tomato/cream mixture and stir until cheese is melted.
Add sausage mixture to tomato/cream mixture and mix thoroughly.
Serve over spaghetti.

Submitter: 
fullerla
Description: 
Shrimp, sausage, cream and tomatoes combine to create a flavorful sauce that is perfect served over spaghetti.
