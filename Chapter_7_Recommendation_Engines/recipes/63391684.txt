Title:
Hashbrown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1 can  condensed cream of celery
1 pint sour cream
1/2 cup of Butte butter
1 onion chopped
ground black pepper to taste
1 (2 pound) package frozen shredded hash brown potatoes, thawed
2 cups extra sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 container of French Onion Dip
1 teaspoon sale

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In large bowl, combine all ingredients and stir together. Spread evenly in prepared pan. Bake for 45 minutes until top is golden brown
