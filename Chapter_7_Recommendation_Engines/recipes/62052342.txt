Title:
Flourless Chocolate Amaretto Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup finely chopped almonds
2 1/2 oz. unsweetened Bakers chocolate
3/4 cup Splenda grandular
1/2 cup plain bread crumbs
2 TB cornstarch
1 tsp. baking powder
1 egg
1/2 cup brewed coffee
2 TB vegetable oil
2 TB unsweetened applesauce
3 TB Amaretto
whipping cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Lightly spray an 8 inch cake pan with cooking spray.  Set aside.  Place almonds on a cookie sheet and toast until lightly brown.  Set aside.  In a small pan melt chocolate over low heat.  Set aside.  Place almonds and remaining ingredients except whipping cream in a mixing bowl and stir well.  Add chocolate and mix well.  Pour into prepared cake pan.  Bake 15-20 minutes.  Cake will seem slightly under-baked.  Removed cake from pan and cool on wire rack.  Place cake on serving plate and refrigerate until ready to serve.  Whip whipping cream in blender and serve on top of torte.

Submitter: 
COOKINGQUEEN75
Description: 
This is a chocolately delight for diabetics or people watching their weight.  It's great fixed ahead of time and refrigerated.  It has a slight hint of coffee and amaretto.
