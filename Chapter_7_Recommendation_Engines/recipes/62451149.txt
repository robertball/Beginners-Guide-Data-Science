Title:
Hawaiian Haystacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans cream of cream of chicken soup
1 chicken bouillon cube
1 cup boiling water
2 cups chopped chicken pieces
4 cups cooked rice
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped bell peppers
1/2 cup chopped green onion
1 - 20 oz. can pineapple tidbits
1 1/2 cups grated cheese
olives, sliced
3 tomatoes, chopped
chow mein noodles

Directions:
Mix bouillon cube and boiling water; stir until bouillon is dissolved.  Stir into cream of chicken soup until smooth.  Simmer 10 minutes.  Add chicken pieces and heat thoroughly.
Put all other ingredients on separate plates so everyone can help themselves.  Start with rice first and then chicken and gravy.
Add all other ingredients, ending with the chow mein noodles.
