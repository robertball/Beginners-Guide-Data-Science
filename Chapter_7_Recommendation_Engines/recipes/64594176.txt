Title:
Chicken Tetrazzini (Grandma Talley recipe) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Tablespoon Margarine
3 Cups Sliced Mushrooms
1/3 Cup Minced Onion
1/2 Cup Flour
2 1/3 Cups Chicken Broth
2 Cups Milk
1/4 Cup Cream Cheese
1/4 Cup Parmesan Cheese
1/4 Cup Sherry (Optional)
1/2 Teaspoon Salt
1/2 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
1/4 Teaspoon Black Pepper
1 Jar (2 ounce) Pimento's Drained
8 ounces Spaghetti
2 Cups Cooked Chopped Chicken Breast(3/4 pound)
Cooking Spray

Directions:
Melt Margarine in large sauce pan over medium heat.
Add onions and mushrooms, Saute 7 minutes or until liquid evaporates.
Stir in Flour.
Gradually add broth, milk, cream cheese, bringing to boil and cook 5 minutes stirring constantly.
Remove from heat, stir in sherry, salt, garlic powder, pepper, pimento's, set aside.
Break spaghetti into 4 pieces and cook/drain.
Stir spaghetti into chicken/mushrooms and put in 3 quart casserole dish coated with Pam.
Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top.
Cover and bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
Bake uncovered for 10 additional minutes.
Let stand 5-10 minutes before serving.
