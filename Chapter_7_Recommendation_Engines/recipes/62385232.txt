Title:
Mocha Coffee Shake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cubes of frozen coffee
3/4 C milk
1 tsp instant coffee (optional)
1 Tablespoon chocolate syrup
1 tsp Vanilla extract
1 packet of artificial sweetener (or 2 teaspoons of sugar, to taste)

Directions:
In a blender combine all ingredients and pulse or blend until combined and frosty.  It should be the consistency of a milkshake.  Enjoy!
If desired, try flavored coffee crystals or different extracts.

Submitter: 
Susan Logsdon
Description: 
I freeze left over coffee from the morning pot in ice cube trays and use them to make this treat. Helps me wake up on the morning commute and is a figure-friendly treat on a hot evening.
