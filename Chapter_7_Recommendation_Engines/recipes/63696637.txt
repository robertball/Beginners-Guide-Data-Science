Title:
Lighter Chicken Parmesan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1027682.jpg
Ingredients:
¼	cup flour
½	teaspoon dried oregano
½	teaspoon onion powder
½	teaspoon garlic powder
¼ 	teaspoon salt
1/8	teaspoon black pepper
6	tablespoons all whites egg whites.  Or 2 large eggs
¾	cup panko (Japanese breadcrumbs)
4	boneless skinless chicken breast halves
2	tablespoons olive oil divided
Olive oil spray
½	cup your favorite pasta sauce
½	cup (2 ounces) grated Parmigiano Reggiano cheese
¾	cup (3 ounces) shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 425.
2.  Combine flour and seasonings, salt and pepper in a shallow dish; place egg whites in a separate bowl.  Place panko in another shallow dish.  Dredge 1 breast in seasoned flour mixture.  Dip in the egg whites; dredge in the pako.  Repeat the procedure with remaining chicken.  Flour mixture, egg whites, and panko.
3.  Heat 1 tablespoon oil in large ovenproof skillet over medium high heat.  Add chicken to pan; cook 2 minutes.  Add remaining oil to pan turn chicken over cook another 2 minutes.  Coat chicken with olive oil spray; place pan in oven for 5 minutes turn chicken over; top each breast half with 2 tablespoons sauce, 2 tablespoons Parmigiano Reggiano, and 3 tablespoons mozzarella.  Bake 6 more minutes or until chicken is done.
