Title:
Nana's Jerk Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 green onions, chopped
2 onions, chopped
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
3/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup cayenne
1/4 cup distilled white vinegar
1/4 cup vegetable oil
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
1 tablespoon garlic, chopped
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
1 1/2 pounds skinless chicken
(I use a whole chicken, cut-up) legs, wings,thighs & breasts

Directions:
In a food processor or blender, combine the green onions, onion, jalapeno pepper, cayenne, soy sauce, vegetable oil, brown sugar, thyme, nutmeg and allspice. Mix for about 15 seconds.
Place the chicken in a medium bowl, oil each piece by hand and then coat with the marinade. Refrigerate for 4 to 6 hours, or overnight. Oven method is preheat 400 degrees bake for 1 hour, turning halfway through at 30 minutes. Or
Preheat grill for high heat.
Lightly oil grill grate. Cook chicken on the prepared grill 6 to 8 minutes, until juices run clear.
