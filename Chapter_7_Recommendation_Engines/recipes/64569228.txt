Title:
M&M; Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
mix together in mixer bowl:
1/2 cup margarine. softened
1 1/2 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 1/2 cup flour
1/2teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
M&Ms; and chocolate chips and marshmallows or favorite candies and you have...

Directions:
Step 1: Turn oven 350 degrees
Step 2: Spray a pizza pan with cooking spray
Step 3: Mix together in mixer bowl
Step 4: beat until fluffy
Step 5: Then add the dry ingredients
Step 6: Stir till it's well mixed
Step 7: Spread in dough in the pizza pan
Step 8: sprinkle  in candy witch one 2 want! if u want 2 candy then but then!
Step 9:Bake for 12 minutes
