Title:
Classic Chicken Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup raw rice
1 small package (less than 1 lb.) chicken tenderloins
1 can cream of celery soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can chow mein noodles
1 cup water
1 cup crushed potato chips

Directions:
1. Cook rice according to directions
2. Parboil chicken, cool, and shred
3. Combine rice, chicken, soups, noodles, and water. Pour into casserole dish and bake covered at 350 for 30-40 minutes.
4. Sprinkle crushed potato chips over top and serve.

Submitter: 
LLIGETT
Description: 
An old-fashioned classic chicken casserole. Easy to make and sure to please.
