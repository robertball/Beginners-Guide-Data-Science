Title:
Asian Chicken Salad Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886761.jpg
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken breast, shredded
1/2 head cabbage, finely shredded
1/2 cup cooked corn
1/4 c chopped cilantro
2 green onion, chopped
3/4 oz sliced almonds
1/2 c mayo
2 tbsp rice wine vinegar
1 1/2 Tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp white sugar
2 tsp black pepper
1 tsp salt
1 tsp sriracha
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp red pepper flakes
1/2 tsp minced garlic
1/2 tsp sesame oil
1/2 tsp honey
1/8 tsp ginger
12 dinner rolls

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix chicken, cabbage, corn, cilantro, onion, and almonds.
In a small bowl, combine mayo, rice wine vinegar, lemon juice, white sugar, black pepper, salt, sriracha, paprika, red pepper flakes, garlic, sesame oil, honey, and ginger.
Add sauce mixture to chicken mixture and combine thoroughly.
Hollow out inside of dinner rolls to form a bowl.  Fill bread bowls with chicken salad mixture.

Description: 
A delicious chicken salad with Asian-inspired flavors served in individual bread bowls.  Perfect for a light lunch!
