Title:
Sweet Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 small sweet potatoes
1/4 c. mayo
1 TBL mustard
4 celery stalks, Chopped fresh chives
1 small red bell pepper diced, 1 c. diced fresh pineapple, 2 scallions, salt & pepper, 1/2c.chopped pecans

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400. Wrap each sweet potato in foil and bake for 1 hr.  Unwrap; let cool.  Peel;cut into 3/4-inch chunks.
2.  In a large bowl, mix nayonnaise and mustard.  Add sweet potatoes, celery, red pepper,pineappl, and scallions; toss gently.  Season to taste with salt and pepper. Cover and refrigerate about 1 hr.
3.  Fold in pecans and sprinkle with chives.
