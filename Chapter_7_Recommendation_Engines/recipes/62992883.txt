Title:
Chicken Fried  Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744748.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cubed steaks (1 1/2 lbs)
1 packet sausage gravy mix (2 1/2 oz)
1 can evaporated milk (12 oz)
2/3 cups Panko bread crumbs
1 TBS butter
1/2 cup egg substitute (or 2 large eggs)
3 TBS canola oil
1/4 cup flour
1 tsp seasoned salt

Directions:
Combine flour and salt into *first bowl* one bowl; pour egg into *second bowl*, pour bread crumbs into *third bowl*
Tranfer steaks to each bowl in order
Heat oil in large nonstick skillet, cook 3-4 minutes on each side or until golden
Mix gravy, water, and milk in pan on low for 2-3 minutes and pour onto steak
