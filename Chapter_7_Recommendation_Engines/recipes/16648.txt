Title:
Crab Spread II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7280104.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
8 ounces imitation crabmeat, flaked
1/4 one lemon, juiced
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together cream cheese, sour cream, crab meat, lemon juice and garlic salt. Cover and chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
JANKS
Description: 
This is a very simple, and inexpensive crab, cream cheese and sour cream dip.  You can make this dip the night before and serve it the next day.
