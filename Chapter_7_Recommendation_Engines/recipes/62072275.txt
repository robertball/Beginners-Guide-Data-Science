Title:
Italian cream cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1(stick) slid vegetable shortening
1(stick) margarine
2 cups sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
5 eggs, seperated
4oz maraschino cherry juice
1 cup buttermilk
2cups flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups coconut flakes

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
cream shortening and margarine until mixed . add sugar and vanilla mix well
add egg yolks one at a time creaming after each addition. In seperate bowl, mix the maraschino cherry juice and buttermilk.
In a seperated bowl mix all dry ingredients.
Add half of the wet ingredients to the creamed mixture and then mix in half of dry ingredients. repeat with the other half of ingredients.then add the coconuts
beat the egg whites to stiff peak and fold into the mixture.
gease 2 9inch pans bake for 45 minutes.
remove let the cake cool completely befor icing.
(icing)
1 stick margarine, 2 cups powdered sugar, 1 tea vanilla, 8oz cream cheese
cream all ingredients together. spread icing between layers and outside of the cake. sprinkel 1 cup toated walnuts on top.
