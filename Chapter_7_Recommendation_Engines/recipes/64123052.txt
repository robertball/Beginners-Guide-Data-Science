Title:
Mushroom Hungarian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. fresh mushrooms
1/4 C. butter
1/2 C. chopped onion
1 T. flour
1 T. paprika
1 1/2 tsp. lemon juice
1 tsp. salt
dash ground red pepper
1 cup Greek nonfat plain yogurt

Directions:
Rinse and slice mushrooms. Set aside. In large skillet, melt butter. Add onion and saute until clear, not brown. Add mushrooms and saute another 3 minutes. Stir in flour, paprika, lemon juice, salt and red pepper. Cook another 3 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add yogurt. Heat through.

Submitter: 
Ursula
Description: 
Serve over rice or noodles! Yummy and easy!
