Title:
Chocolate Goose Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
1 oz Grey Goose vodka
1 oz creme de cacao liquer
ice
club soda to taste

Directions:
Pour the vodka and liquer into a glass over ice.  Top with club soda to taste.  Enjoy!
