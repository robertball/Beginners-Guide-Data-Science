Title:
Swedish Creme Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/324519.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups heavy cream
1 cup plus 2 teaspoons sugar, divided
1 envelope unflavored gelatin
1 teaspoon clear imitation vanilla extract
1 teaspoon almond extract
2 cups (16 ounces) sour cream
1 cup fresh or frozen red raspberries, crushed

Directions:
In a suacepan, combine cream and 1 cup sugar. Cook and stir constantly over low heat until candy thermometer reads 160* or steam rises from the pan(do not boil). Stir in gelatin until dissolved; add extracts. Cool 10 minutes. Whisk in sour cream. Pour into eight dessert glasses or small bowls; chill at least 1 hour. Before serving, combine raspberries and remaining sugar; spoon over each serving. Yield: 8 servings.

Submitter:

Description: 
A rich and smooth dessert that is made as a favorate Holiday tradition.
