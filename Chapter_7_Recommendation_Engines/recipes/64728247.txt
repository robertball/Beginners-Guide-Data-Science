Title:
Chicken Divan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts
2 tins cream of chicken soup
dash of curry
1-1/2c broccoli florets
1c grated cheddar cheese
Bread crumbs

Directions:
Cook chicken breasts however you prefer. I boil mine.
Steam the broccoli.
Mix soup and curry in a medium mixing bowl.
Stir in chicken.
Put a small layer of mix on the bottom of dish.
Add broccoli.
Add mixture.
Add cheese and bread crumbs.
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
