Title:
Loren Halifax's Crustless Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/940600.jpg
Ingredients:
8-10 slices bacon, crisply cooked & crumbled
1 cup grated cheese (Swiss or cheddar)
1/4 cup onion, minced
4 eggs
13-14 ounces small curd cottage cheese
1 pkg. frozen chopped spinach, cooked with 1 teaspoon salt, then drained
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
3 dashes Tabasco sauce

Directions:
Sprinkle cheese, bacon and onion in that order in a 10 inch greased pie plate or an 8 inch square baking dish.
Beat remaining ingredients until well blended.  Pour over bacon mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes.
Let stand 10-14 minutes before cutting.
