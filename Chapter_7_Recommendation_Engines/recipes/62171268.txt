Title:
Slow Cooker Pork Taco Meat (But not just for Tacos) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3lb Pork Shoulder roast cut into 1n chunks
1 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes with green chilis
1 small onion chopped
1/2 bell pepper (any color) chopped
1/2 small jalepeno seeded and chopped
4 cloves crushed garlic
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
3/4 teaspoon smokey red chili flakes
1 5 oz can tomato paste
1/2 cup medium salsa
3 taco bell (hot) taco sauce

Directions:
Add all ingrediants to slow cooker and stir well. cook on high for 5-7 hours. Stir occasionally.

Submitter: 
Val-Flowers
Description: 
A tender melt in your mouth filling for tacos,Burritios,or even between 2 buns!. Full of flavor with just a hint of heat.It will leave you feeling full and satisfied.
