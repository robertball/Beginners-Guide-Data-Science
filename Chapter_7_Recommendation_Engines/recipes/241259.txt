Title:
Kiwi Banana Apple Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2053849.jpg
Ingredients:
1 apple, roughly chopped
1 banana, broken into chunks
2 kiwifruit, peeled
1 1/4 cups milk
1/4 cup ice, or as desired
2 teaspoons chia seeds
1 teaspoon maca powder

Directions:
Blend apple, banana, kiwifruit, milk, ice, chia seeds, and maca powder together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Krissu
Description: 
This smoothie is tasty and super-healthy - a lot of thanks to the seeds and powder used in it.
