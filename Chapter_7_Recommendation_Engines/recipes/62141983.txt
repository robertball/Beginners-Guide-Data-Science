Title:
Bubble Up Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans buttermilk biscuits, quartered
8 oz sausage or ground beef, cooked and drained
14oz jar pizza sauce
2 oz sliced pepperoni
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1 green pepper, chopped
8 oz shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
In a medium sized mixing bowl, mix together quartered biscuits, sausage or beef, pizza sauce, pepperoni, mushrooms, and green peppers.
Pour into greased 9x13 pan.
Bake at 350* 20 minutes.
Top with cheese, return to oven for 10 minutes until cheese is melted and beginnning to brown.

Submitter: 
Billie Miller
Description: 
A favorite with kids of all ages. This versatile recipe adapts to what is in your fridge and pantry.
