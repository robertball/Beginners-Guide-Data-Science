Title:
MEATLOAF Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4802557.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. of lean ground beef
1 egg, lightly beaten
3 tbsp. bread crumbs
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 tbsp. (fresh) thyme, freshly ground pepper
2 mushrooms, thick slices

Directions:
1.Preheat the Air Fryer to 392Â°F.
2.Mix the ground meat in a bowl with the egg, bread crumbs, onion, thyme, 1 tsp. salt and a generous amount of pepper. Knead and mix thoroughly.
3.Transfer the ground meat to the pan or dish and smoothen the top. Press in the mushrooms and coat the top with olive oil.
4.Place the pan or dish in the basket and slide the basket into the Air Fryer. Set the timer to 25 minutes and roast the meat loaf until nicely brown and done.
5. Let the meat loaf rest for at least 10 minutes.
6. Cut into wedges, serve and enjoy.
