Title:
Orange Juice Jello salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3oz. pkg orange jello
1 1 1/2 C. boiling water
1 6 oz. can frozen orange juice, thawed
1 C. cold water
11 oz. can mandarin oranges
1 16 oz. can grapefruit sections (drained and cut)

Directions:
Dissolve jello in boiling water.  Add orange juice and cold water.  Drain oranges, reserving syrup.  Add syrup to jello mix.  Cill until partially set.  Fold in mandarin oranges and grapefruit.  Pour into 6 1/2 C. mold or 9" square pan.

Submitter: 
jeanneferg
Description: 
A very tangy jello salad.  Pretty orange color.
