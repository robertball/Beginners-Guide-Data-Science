Title:
Orange Walnut Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups orange juice
2 cups sugar
2 - 12 oz. bags cranberries
zest of 2 oranges
1 cup walnuts, chopped

Directions:
In a large pot combine orange juice, sugar, cranberries, and orange zest.  Bring to boil, reduce heat to simmer, cook 10 minutes.  Remove from heat.  Let cool.  Stir in walnuts.  Refrigerate overnight before serving.

Submitter: 
Marian
Description: 
Love this fresh cranberry sauce for the holidays instead of canned cranberry sauce.  People who typically do not like cranberry sauce, love this one.
