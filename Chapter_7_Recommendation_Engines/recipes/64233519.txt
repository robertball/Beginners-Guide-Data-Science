Title:
Yummy Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups sweet potato, mashed
1/3 cup brown sugar
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 tablespoons butter, softened
1/2 cup evaporated milk or half and half
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
Topping:
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
4  1/2 tablespoons butter, softened
3/4 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
Bake sweet potatoes at 375 for 1 hr.  Scoop out cooked sweet potatoes and place in a large bowl. In the large bowl, mix together the sweet potatoes, brown sugar, eggs, salt, butter, milk, cinnamon, nutmeg, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth. Transfer to a 9x13 inch baking dish.
In medium bowl, mix the brown sugar and flour. Cut in the butter until the mixture is coarse. Stir in the pecans. Sprinkle the mixture over the sweet potato mixture.
Bake in the preheated oven 30 minutes, or until the topping is lightly brown.
