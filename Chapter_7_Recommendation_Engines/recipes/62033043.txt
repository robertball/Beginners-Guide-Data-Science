Title:
Italian Marinated Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower cut into florets
1/2 cup white wine vinegar
1/2 cup olive oil
2 teaspoons sugar
4 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
1/4 teaspoon pepper
2 green bell peppers cut into thin strips then halved
1 bunch radishes sliced and quartered1 small red onion chopped

Directions:
Blanch cauliflower in boiling water for 5 min. Plunge into cold water then drain.In small bowl whisk together vinegar,oil, sugar and seasonings. Place all vegetables in large bowl, pour dressing over vegetables. Cover and chill overnight.
