Title:
Wacky Chocolate Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups all-purpose flour
2 cups white sugar
6 T. unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons baking soda
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 T. white vinegar
3/4 cup vegetable oil
2 cups cold water

Directions:
Mix all dry ingredients right in a 9 x 13 inch pan. Make 3 holes and add vanilla, vinegar and oil. Then pour water over all and stir until all is moistened.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 35-40 minutes.  Remove from oven and cool.  Frost with your favorite frosting.
