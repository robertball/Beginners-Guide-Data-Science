Title:
Spicy Texas Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
2 onions, chopped
4 garlic cloves, minced
2 lbs ground beef
2 cups beef broth
2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1 can red kidney beans
1 can black beans
1 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
2 celery stalks, chopped
2 tbsp chili powder
1 tbsp brown sugar
2½ tsp salt
2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
2 tsp cumin
1 tsp black pepper, ground
½ tsp paprika
½ tsp Tabasco (optional)

Directions:
Heat the oil in a 4L pot or larger.
Sautée the onion and garlic for approximately 3 minutes.
Brown the beef completely.
Add the remainder of the ingredients and let cook on medium to low heat for 20 minutes.
Serve in bowls topped with shredded cheddar cheese alongside crusty bread.

Submitter: 
Mama & her Girls
Description: 
A quick and very flavorful chili recipe that takes half an hour to cook but tastes like it's been on the stove all day.
