Title:
FAT GRANNYS NEW ENGLAND CLAM CHOWDER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4(6.5oz)cans chopped clams
1(28oz) pkg frozen o'brian potatoes
1 medium onion diced     1 sm shredded carrot
2 stalks celery diced
6 strips cooked and diced bacon(bacon bits would be fine)
1 stick butter( 4oz)
1tsp seafood seasoning
1/2 tsp granulated garlic
1/2 tsp granulated onion
1/2 tsp celery seed
salt and pepper to taste
1 tbl clam base
6 cups clam juice
2 cups water
2 cans (unsweetened) condensed milk
1/4 cup flour
1/4 cup water

Directions:
in a strainer,drain the clams, saving the juice.set the clams aside and pour the juice in with the other clam juice.
in a large sauce pan ,over medium high heat,melt the stick of butter.add the onions,shredded carrot and celery.saute for 2-3 min.add the seasonings and clam base.  stir.add the 6 cups of clam juice and the 2 cups of water.stir.bring to a boil.add the frozen O'brian potatoes.turn heat down and simmer until potatoes are tender( about 10 min.). add the 2 cans of (unsweetened)  condensed milk.stir until well mixed.
In a small mixing bowl add the flour and slowly pour the water in, while whisking all the time.making sure there are no lumps and the slurry is smooth.
slowly pour the slurry into the chowder,stirring constantly,until slightly thickened. add clams and bacon. stir. let simmer for additional 5 to 10 min. serve with oyster crackers
garnish with finely chopped chives and roasted red pepper, diced
