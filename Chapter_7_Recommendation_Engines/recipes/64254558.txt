Title:
Sausage Chili Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz, breadfast sausage, crumbled
1 cup chopped onion
2 (15 oz.) cans pinto beans, rinsed and drained
2 (15 oz. cans black beans, rinsed & drained
2 (14.5 oz.) cans diced tomatoes, undrained
8 t. dried cilantro
1/4 c. Old Mill Chili Starter
2 c. (8 oz.) shredded co-jack Cheese

Directions:
Cook sausage and onion in a large skillet, stirring to crumble sausage, until browned. Drain well.
Mash Pinto beans in a large bowl using potato masher; stir in black beans, tomatoes, cilantro & Chili Starter.
Spoon into 2 pie plates. Cover with Foil. Freeze 1.
Bake at 375 for 45 minutes. Uncover & sprinkle with cheese, continue baking 15 minutes.
Serve hot with chips.
