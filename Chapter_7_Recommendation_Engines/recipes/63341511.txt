Title:
Mom's Mexican Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs hamburger meat
2 packages taco seasoning
2 cans rotel
2 can cream of mushroom soup
1 package mexican style shredded cheese
1 package flour tortillas
1 tbsp olive oil or cooking spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Line the bottom of casserole dish with olive oil or cooking spray to prevent sticking
Line bottom of the dish with flour tortillas
In a skillet brown your hamburger meat
Once browned drain and add taco seasoning
Place in large mixing bowl
Add rotel and soup and 1 cup cheese
Mix well
Pour mixture over tortillias spreading out evenly
Put another layer of tortillas on top of mixture
Top tortillas with cheese
Place in oven cook for 30 min
Enjoy
