Title:
Paige's Winning Chili Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs. ground beef
1 1/2 cup onion, chopped
2 cloves fresh minced garlic
1/2 tablespoon ground black pepper
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon smoked paprika
32 oz. tomato sauce
12 oz. jar salsa
1/3 cup chili seasoning mix
1 (15 oz.) can red kidney beans
1 (15 oz.) can black beans
1 (15 oz.) can white northern beans

Directions:
1. In a large saucepan combine ground beef and onion over medium heat for 10 minutes or until meat is browned and onion is translucent. Drain.
2. Add remaining ingredients and mix well.
3. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce heat, cover and simmer for at least one hour.
