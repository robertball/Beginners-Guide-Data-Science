Title:
Easy Slow Cooker French Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pounds beef roast
1 (10.5 ounce) can beef broth
1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed French onion soup
1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle beer
French rolls
Provolone or Swiss cheese slices

Directions:
Trim excess fat from the  roast, and place in a slow cooker. Add the beef broth, onion soup and beer. Cook on Low setting for 7 hours. About 30 minutes before time is up, shred beef with a fork.
Split French rolls, and place a slice on cheese inside. Bake 10 -15 minutes at 200 degrees or until heated through. Remove from oven and fill with meat.
Use the sauce for dipping.

Submitter: 
KKraft
Description: 
For an easy French dip sandwich, this slow cooker French dip recipe uses  roast slowly simmered in beef broth, onion soup, and beer.Modified from: Easy Slow Cooker French Dip
