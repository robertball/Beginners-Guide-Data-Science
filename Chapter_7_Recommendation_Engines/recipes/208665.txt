Title:
Dana's Breakfast Special Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990519.jpg
Ingredients:
1 slice whole wheat bread or English muffin, toasted
1 egg, poached
1/4 Chilean Hass avocado, sliced

Directions:
Top a toasted English muffin half with a poached egg and avocado slices.  If desired, add other favorite toppings such as salsa, chopped jalapenos, onions, or cheese.

Submitter: 
Chileanavocados.org
Description: 
Dana Thomas is an avocado expert. His company imports Chilean Hass Avocados and he's surrounded by avocados all day every day. So what's his favorite breakfast? A poached egg with avocado slices on an English muffin. He tops his with salsa. You can add jalapenos, chopped onions, cheese or whatever.
