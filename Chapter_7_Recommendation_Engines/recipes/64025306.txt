Title:
Grilled Chicken with Bourbon Peach Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. peeled, coarsely chopped peaches (about 5)
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
3 tbsp. water
1/2 cup bourbon
1/3 cup packed dark brown sugar
3/4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
6 boneless chicken breasts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 250.
Combine peaches, lemon juice and water in a saucepan.
Bring to a boil, cover, reduce heat and simmer about 30 minutes.
Combine peach mixture, bourbon, brown sugar and 1/4 tsp. salt in a food processor or blender and process for 1 minute or until smooth.
Transfer peach mixture to a 13x9 glass or ceramic baking dish and bake for 2 hours and 15 minutes or until thickened.
Sprinkle remaining salt and pepper over chicken.
Place chicken on pre-heated grill and cook until done.
Serve with peach sauce or brush on chicken just before removing from grill.
