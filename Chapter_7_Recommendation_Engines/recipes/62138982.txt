Title:
Pesto Mayonnaise Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 .5 oz package of pesto mix
1 Cup light mayonnaise
2 Tbsp Romano cheese

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together, chill and serve! If it's too thick, add a drop of olive oil. For more flavor, use a fresh herb pesto mix!

Submitter: 
ally-gator
Description: 
A great dipping sauce for sweet potato or home fries, burgers, sandwiches, and salads. This is super easy to make and adds a little something extra to some of your favorite foods!
