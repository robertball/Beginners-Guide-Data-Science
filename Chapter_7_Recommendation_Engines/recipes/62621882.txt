Title:
Italian Sauasage, Onions and Peppers on Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 pounds of Italian Sausage cut in half inch slices
1 Tablespoon olive oil
1 15 oz can diced tomatoes
1 15 oz can tomato sauce
1 large green pepper chopped coarsely
1 large onion chopped coarsely
1 Tablespoon italin seasoning
1 pound of pasta
Grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Brown sausage in oil. Remove from pan with a slotted spoon and put in a dish to the side.
Saute peppers and onions five min. add seasoning and continue an additional five min.
Pour the tomatoes and sauce in with the pepper mix and then add the sausage. Continue to cook about 20 min.
While the sauce is cooking put a large pasta pot on to boil. Cook the pasta according to directions.
Serve with sauce, top with parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
This is a nice change of pace from ordinary spaghetti, a family favorite!
