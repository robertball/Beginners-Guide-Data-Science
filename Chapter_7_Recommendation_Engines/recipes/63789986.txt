Title:
Breakfast for Supper Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 large Eggs
1 Big Bag Bacon
4 cans GREAT VALUE biscuits only

Directions:
Cook eggs and bacon on a skillet. Cook biscuits in oven for 8-10 minutes on 425*F
Be sure to make sure Biscuits cook till golden brown.
Don't over cook biscuits or it will be burnt. And it will make you sick if you eat burnt food

Submitter: 
Cooper
Description: 
Hot food should be cooled down by 10 minutes after done cooking.
