Title:
Chicken Bacon Gorgonzola Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head lettuce, cut up
1/2 head cabbage, chopped
1 tomato, chopped
1 cucumber sliced
1 Avocado, sliced
8 slices Applewood Smoked Bacon, cooked and chopped
1 Rotisserie Chicken, cut up
Pecans
Crumbled Gorgonzola
Ranch Dressing

Directions:
1.  Toss lettuce, cabbage, cucumber, tomato, avocado,bacon and chicken together.
2. Sprinkle pecans and cheese on top and top with ranch dressing.
