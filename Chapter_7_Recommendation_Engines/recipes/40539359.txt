Title:
Ceasar Pleaser Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Juice of 1 small lemon (1/4c)
dash of worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp. prepared dijon mustard
1-2 cloves garlic, pressed
4-5 canned anchovies, finely minced
2 Tbs. fresh grated romano cheese
pepper to taste
1/2 to 2/3 cups olive oil
Head of Romaine lettuce
1/2 to 1 cup croutons
2 Tbs. more fresh grated romano

Directions:
Tear or rough-chop lettuce,
rinse, drain, and chill.
Whisk together first 7 ingredients.
Slowly whisk in olive oil.
crush croutons into small pieces
sprinkle remaining cheese and
crouton pieces onto the lettuce.
Toss salad with dressing and serve.

A classic Ceasar salad with a few secret ingredients.
