Title:
Herbed Sweet Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5lb sweet potato
3 sticks of butter
1 Tb onion powder
1 Tb garlic powder
1Tb thyme
1 Tb Dill weed
1c cream cheese
2c colby cheese (or any type of your favorite flavor) (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
Peel all sweet potatoes.
cut potatoes into rounds, same thickness
melt butter and cream cheese together. Add spices.
Spray 11 by 13 pan and place sweet potatoes on bottom.
Over the top spread the butter/cream cheese mixture.
Cook for 40-45 min.
Sprinkle colby jack cheese on top. Bake 5 more min.

Submitter: 
Christina
Description: 
Delicious, butter soaked sweet potatoes with a hint of herb! Fantastic.
