Title:
Israeli Couscous (perfect for plating) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Israeli couscous
2tbs olive oil
1/4 C minced onion
1 tsp yellow curry powder
1/2 tsp oregano
2 cubes beef, chicken or vegetable boullion
2 1/2 C water

Directions:
In a sauce pot heat oil, onion, curry, oregano and boullion cubes over medium heat.  Break up boullion cubes and sauté onion until soft.  Add couscous and mix thoroughly.
Pour water into pot and bring to a boil.  Cover pot and turn down heat to low and simmer for 8-10 minutes stirring  often until couscous is soft.
Serve under your favorite meat, fish or veggie protein.

Description: 
This is a great way to serve your delicious main dish protein. The large size pearl couscous is perfect for soaking up sauce and has enough flavor to enhance any protein. Serve under fish, chicken, beef or vegetarian proteins.
