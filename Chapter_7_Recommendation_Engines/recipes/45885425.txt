Title:
Lisa's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5938066.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb very lean ground beef
1/2 lb ground pork sausage
(Or 1 1/2 lb ground beef only)
2 eggs
3/4 cup milk
1/2 cup dried bread crumbs
1/4 cup very finely chopped onion
2 Tblsp parsley, fresh or dried
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon white pepper
1/2 teaspoon ground sage
1/4 cup ketchup
2 Tablespooons brown sugar
1 teaspoon dried mustard or 1 Tablespoon regular mustard

Directions:
Mix meats together in large bowl
Whisk eggs and milk together, then add to meat mixture along with bread crumbs and seasonings.
Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 50-60 minutes.
In the meantime, mix together the ketchup, brown sugar and mustard. Brush on the meatloaf after baking and put back in the oven for another 10 minutes.
Let meatloaf rest for 10 minutes before serving to keep moisture in.
