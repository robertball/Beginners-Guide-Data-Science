Title:
Spaetzle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 teaspoon salt
2 beaten eggs
3/4 cup milk

Directions:
Mix all together well
Place batter in coarse sieved colander or spaetzle maker and drop in boiling water.
cook 5 minutes and drain

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
I make these to go with chicken paprikas!
