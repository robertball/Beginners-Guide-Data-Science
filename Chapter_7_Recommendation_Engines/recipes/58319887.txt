Title:
Boursin Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 clove garlic
16 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 C. butter, softened
1 t. oregano
1/4 t. basil
1/4 t. dillweed
1/4 t. marjoram
1/4 t. thyme
1/4 t. black pepper

Directions:
Position knife blade in food processor.
Add garlic and process until finely chopped.
Add cream cheese and butter.  Process, stopping once to scrape down sides.
Add dried herbs and mix well.
Refrigerate for one week or freeze for three months.
Serve with crackers,raw  vegetables toasted pita wedges, spooned onto baked potatoes or stirred into mashed potatoes.  Can also be spread on chicken breasts before baking.
