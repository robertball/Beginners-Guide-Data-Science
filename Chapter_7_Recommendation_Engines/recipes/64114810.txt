Title:
Raisin & Olive Fettuccine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup water, hot
1/4 tsp Saffron strings
4 ounce Fettuccine
2 tsp Butter
2 1/2 Heavy Cream
1/8 tsp salt
1/8 tsp Pepper
1/8 tsp Paprika
1 tsp Parmesan (optional)
1/2 oz Raisins
1 1/2 oz Green Olives, slices
3 oz Paneer cubes (cottage cheese cubes)

Directions:
Add saffron strings to hot water, and let the water absorb color and taste.
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a rolling boil. Cook fettuccine at a boil until al dente; drain.
In a skillet melt butter on medium heat, then add heavy cream and bring to a boil then Reduce heat. Add salt, pepper, paprika, and parmesan, stir then simmer for 1 minute.
Add saffron-water, raisins, olives and paneer, stir and simmer for 1 minute or until the sauce is thick enough.
Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet and mix. let simmer on medium low heat for 5 minutes. serve.
