Title:
loukoumades-   fried greek honey puffs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packs active dry yeast
1 cup warm water
1/2 cup warm milk
1/47 cup sugar
1 teasp salt
1/3 cup soft butter
3 eggs
4 cups flour

Directions:
add yeast in warm water ,let stand for 5 mins
pour hot milk in a bowl ,add sugar and salt ,blend together allow to cool to lukewarm
stir yeast mixxure well and add to milk/sugar mixture.
mix in butter,eggs,flour beat till smooth.
cover let rise 30 mins, stir well
let rise for another 30 mins
heat cooking oil to 350 degrees about 2 inches deep
drop by teaspoonfulls into oil wetting spoon after each spoon
turn when edges turn golden
drain on paper towels .
sprinle with cinnamon while still hot
make a mixture of 1/2 cup honey and 1/2 cup water mix well drizzle over top
serve warm
