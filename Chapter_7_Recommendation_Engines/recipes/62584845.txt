Title:
Apple Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 cup peeled, cubed apples
2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1 cup milk
1 tblsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Cream shortening, sugar and eggs. Blend in flour, baking soda, salt and milk.  Add remaining ingredients except cinnamon and 3 tbsp. sugar.  Mix cinnamon and 3 tbls. sugar togehter.  Layer with batter into grease loaf pan.  Bake at 350 for one hour or until knife inserted into center comes out clean.
