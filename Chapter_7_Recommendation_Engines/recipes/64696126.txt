Title:
Hula's Mountain Mama Mudslide Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup AP Flour
1 cup chopped Pecans, divided
1/2 cup melted Butter
1 8 oz package Cream Cheese softened
1 cup Confectioners Sugar
1 4oz packaged instant Vanilla pudding
1 4oz package instant Chocolate pudding
2 cups cold Milk
1 16oz container Cool Whip
2 Tblspn Chocolate syrup or Ganache drizzle

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F
Mix flour, 1/2cup Pecans, and melted Butter in a bowl.
Press into pie dish.
Bake until lightly browned, about 20 minutes.  Cool completely.
Whisk cream cheese and sugar together till smooth.  Spread over crust.
Beat pudding mixes and milk together until thickened.  Spread over cream cheese.
Spoon over whipped topping and decorate with remaining pecans and choc ganache.
