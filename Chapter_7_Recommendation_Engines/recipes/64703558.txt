Title:
Sauteed Leek and Fennel Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large fennel bulb top trimmed and sliced into thin slices
2 Tbs Fennel fronds rough chopped
2 leeks cleaned and chopped
5 cloves garlic finely chopped or minced
2 Tbs olive oil
l 1b  bucatini pasta
14 oz. can Italian stewed tomatoes
1 bay leaf
1 tsp Italian seasoning
1 pinch red chilli flakes
salt and pepper to taste
freshly grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta al dente according to package.
Reserve 1/4 cup of the pasta water.
Drain pasta into colander and run cold water over to stop pasta from further cooking,  set aside.
Heat pot over medium heat and add 1 Tbs oil.
Add 2 garlic cloves, bay leaf and Italian seasoning.
Saute for 1 minute.
Add Italian stewed tomatoes and mash with potato masher (or fork) to break down tomatoes. Stir ingredients together, reduce heat to medium low and allow to cook.
Heat 1 Tbs olive oil in a dutch oven over medium low heat.
Add the leeks, fennel, garlic and a pinch of salt.
Saute until tender (about 5-7 minutes).
Add chili flakes and another pinch of salt.
Add marinara sauce and pasta water to your dutch oven and stir together.
Add the drained pasta and let it cook in the sauce for a few minutes.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Place pasta in bowls and garnish with fennel fronds and parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
This pasta offers robust savory flavors while feeling on the lighter side. The leek and fennel, perfectly compliment the acidity of the tomato marinara.
