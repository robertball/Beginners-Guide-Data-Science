Title:
Mexican Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups freshly squeezed lime juice
1-1/4  cups  sugar
6 cups water
~   ice cubes
1+   limes for decoration

Directions:
Place the lime juice, sugar, and water in a pitcher and stir until the sugar dissolves.
Add the ice cubes and stir.
Decorate individual glasses with a slice of lime and serve
