Title:
Sour Cream Banana Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Cake:
1 1/2 cups mashed bananas
1 cup sour cream
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs
2 tsp. vanilla
2 cups flour
1 1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. soda
Icing:
4 TBSP butter
3 cups powdered sugar
3-4 TBSP water
1 1/2 tsp. almond extract
Yellow food coloring, optional

Directions:
Cake:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Spray jelly roll pan with Pam.
Mix first 5 ingredients well, add all dry ingredients and mix 3 minutes.
Pour into prepared pan. Bake 15-20 minutes.
Icing:
Mix butter, sugar, almond extract and food coloring together. Add water one tablespoon at a time to make thin icing. Spread over cooled cake.
