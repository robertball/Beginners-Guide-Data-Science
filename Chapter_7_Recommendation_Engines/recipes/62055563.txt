Title:
EZ Cinnamon Walnut Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can large-style butter-flavor biscuits
3-4 tbsp chopped walnuts
2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp allspice
4 tbsp white sugar
2 tbsp butter or margarine

Directions:
Mix spices with sugar. Reserve 1/4 of this mixture. Mix remaining 3/4 with walnuts.
Slice each bistuit in half horizontally. Fill with 1 tsp of mixture and crimp closed with a fork.
Place in lightly oiled 9"x13". Top with remaining sugar mixture and bake according to biscuit package directions, (usually 350 degrees for 11-14 minutes).

Submitter: 
Karen
Description: 
A quick and delicious breakfast sweet made with canned biscuits.
