Title:
Grandma Ruby's Persimmon Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Persimmon Pulp
1 Tsp Baking Powder
1/2 Cup Shortening
1 Cup Sugar
2 Cups Flour
1/2 Tsp Cinnamon
1/2 Tsp Nutmeg
1/2 Tsp Cloves
1/2 Tsp Salt
1 Cup Chopped Dates
1 Cup Chopped Walnuts or Pecans
Vanilla
1 Egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 Degrees.
Mix persimmon pulp and baking powder, set aside.
Cream shortening, sugar, vanilla and egg. Add the persimmon and mix well. Add flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and salt. Mix well, then add chopped dates and walnuts.
Drop on a greased cookie sheet and bake at 325 degrees for 20-25 minutes.
