Title:
Super Smooth Spinach-Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened, divided
1 pt. of Heavy Cream
1/2 stick of butter
1/2 - 3/4 C. Parmesan cheese (or more parmesan, romano, asiago mix)
1 t. Garlic powder
1/4 cup Sour cream
1/2 cup grated parmesan, romano, and asiago cheese mixture
1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 clove garlic, peeled and minced
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
salt and pepper to taste
1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1/2 cup frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9"x13" baking dish.
In a saucepan melt butter.
When butter is melted add 2 tbsp. cream cheese.
When the cream cheese is softened add heavy cream.
Add parmesan cheese and stir until melted.
Simmer this for 15 - 20 minutes on low stirring every 5 minutes.
Meanwhile, in a medium bowl, mix together remaining cream cheese, sour cream, cheeses, garlic, basil, garlic salt, salt and pepper.
Gently stir in artichoke hearts and spinach.
You may wish to season with a little salt and pepper.
Stir the first sauce into the artichoke mixture.
Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish.
Bake in the preheated oven 25 minutes, until bubbly and lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
Who can deny the popularity of artichokes and spinach blended with cheeses? Try this hot, flavorful dip with toasted sourdough baguette slices, pita chips, or tortilla chips.Modified from: Hot Artichoke and Spinach Dip II
