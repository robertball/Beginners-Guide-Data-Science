Title:
Fresh Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2419650.jpg
Ingredients:
10 lemons
3 cups water
2 cups white sugar
4 cups crushed ice

Directions:
Cut 1/2 of one lemon into thin slices and set aside. Juice remaining 9 1/2 lemons and pour into a glass pitcher or punch bowl. Stir in the water and sugar until dissolved. Pour in the crushed ice and float the lemon slices on top.

Submitter: 
Julie
Description: 
A summer classic, this lemonade is easy and refreshing too!
