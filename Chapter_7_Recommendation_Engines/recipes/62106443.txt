Title:
Brother Matt's Cilantro Grilled  Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz honey
8 oz  kraft zesty italian dressing
5 cloves garlic thinly sliced
2-3 tbsp thinnly sliced/peeled gingerroot
1-2 bunches cilantro chopped finely
Chicken peices for grilling (thighs work best)

Directions:
1. Combine all ingredients except for chicken
2. Place chicken in a ziploc bag and add marinade reserving some for basting during grill time
3. marinade for at least 1/2 hour
4. Grill and baste to taste

Submitter: 
rosburn
Description: 
This recipe is from my brother Matt.  Everytime we get together at his house the whole family requests that he make this.  Very Good- you can modify the herbs to your own tastes. This was enough marinade for a party of 10 people.
