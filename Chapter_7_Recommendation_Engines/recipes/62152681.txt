Title:
Vegetarian Chickpea Salad Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can chickpeas (garbanzo beans)
1/3 cup light mayonnaise
1 tsp dill
1 tbsp dried garlic flakes
1 tsp smoked paprika
1/4 tsp salt
4 croissants
4-6 romaine lettuce leaves

Directions:
Rinse and drain chickpeas
Mash chickpeas with a fork or potato masher, leaving slightly lumpy
Add mayo, dill, paprika, and salt; mix well
Let chill for at least 30 minutes
Cut croissants in half, spoon on filling and add romaine leaves

Submitter: 
ArianeK
Description: 
This vegetarian (or vegan, with Vegenaise instead of mayo) is a great substitute for chicken or tuna salad.  Personally, I think it's even better!
