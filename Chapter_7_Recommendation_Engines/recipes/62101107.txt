Title:
Christmas Chocolate Macadamia Nut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (19.9 oz) box devil's food brownie mix
1 (18.25 oz) box chocolate cake mix
1 (3.4 oz) box instant chocolate pudding
1 1/3 cups oatmeal
1 cup vegetable oil
1 cup sour cream
1 (10 oz) bag red- and green-striped white chocolate chips
1 (6 oz) bag macadamia nuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.  Lightly grease cookie sheets.
In a large bowl, mix brownie mix, cake mix, pudding, and oatmeal.  Add oil and sour cream and combine until moistened.  Stir in white chocolate chips and macadamia nuts.
Roll into 1 1/2-inch balls and bake in preheated oven for 8-10 minutes.  Let cool on cookie sheets for 5 minutes before removing to cooling racks to cool completely.

Submitter: 
Christina Reitz
Description: 
This is a recipe I developed when I had a bag of red- and green-swirled white chocolate chips and a bag of chopped macadamia nuts and wanted to make some cookies.  Enjoy!
