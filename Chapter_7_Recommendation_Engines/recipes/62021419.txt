Title:
Bis-Quick-As-A-Wink Mix  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups flour
1 1/4 cups nonfat dry milk powder
1/4 cup baking powder
1 tablespoon salt
2 cups shortening

Directions:
Combine flour, milk, baking powder, and salt in a very large bowl. Cut in shortening until it resembles coarse cornmeal. Store in tightly closed covered container in a cool place.

Submitter: 
WhirledPeas
Description: 
Use this homemade mix in any recipe calling for Bisquick or other commercial baking mixes. You control what goes into it, and there are NO unnecessary additives.
