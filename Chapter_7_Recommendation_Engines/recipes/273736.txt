Title:
Skinny Vegan Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4521479.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups fresh spinach
2 cups frozen strawberries
1 frozen banana
1 1/2 cups unsweetened almond milk
1 tablespoon agave nectar (optional)

Directions:
Combine spinach, strawberries, banana, almond milk, and agave in a blender; blend until smooth.
