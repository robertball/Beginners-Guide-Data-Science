Title:
Low in Fat Cookie Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/513035.jpg
Ingredients:
24 reduced fat oreo cookies
12oz fat free cool whip

Directions:
Smash 12 oreo cookies in a ziplock bag till cookies are small pebbles. Dump cookies and fat free cool whip in a bowl, mix with rubber spatula until cookie is throughout the cool whip.
Scoop cookie frosting with a ice cream scooper and place on top of cupcake.  Place non smashed oreo on top as charm.

Submitter: 
Skinny Cakes
Description: 
This is a simple, fun and tasty semi homemade frosting.
