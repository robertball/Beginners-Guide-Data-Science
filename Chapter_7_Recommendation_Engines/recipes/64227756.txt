Title:
Fiery Tofu and Coconut Curry (Cooking Light - Ways to Cook Vegetarian) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2tsp oil
2tsp minced garlic
1/4C red curry paste
1Tbsp brown sugar
2 cans of light coconut milk
2&1/2C broth
1/4C lime juice
1/4C thinly sliced peeled ginger (cut into match sticks and then sliced)
2Tbsp soy sauce
Asst veggies such as bok choy, carrots, cauliflower, snap peas, red/yellow peppers, broccoli, tomatoes, mushrooms
Grilled tofu or paneer

Directions:
1.Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat.
2.Add garlic and saute for 30 seconds.
3.Add curry paste; saute 1 min stirring constantly
4.Add brown sugar; cook 1 minute
5.Stir in coconut milk, broth, juice, ginger and soy sauce.
6.Reduce heat and simmer for 30 mins
7.Cut up veggies into large (but bite sized) pieces
8.Boil pot of water with steam basket.
9.Grill tofu (see KOI tofu recipe) or add paneer to soup at this point to warm up.
10.Steam veggies (one bowl at a time works best)  Add to soup bowl and top with broth and tofu/paneer.
