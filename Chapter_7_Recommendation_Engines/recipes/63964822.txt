Title:
Simple Buttercream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound salted butter, softened
1 pound powdered sugar, sifted
1 tsp. vanilla extract

Directions:
Using a mixer fitted with a paddle, cream the butter until light and fluffy.
Gradually add the sugar, frequently scraping down the sides of the bowl.
Add the vanilla extract and continue beating until the frosting is smooth and light.
