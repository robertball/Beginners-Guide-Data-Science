Title:
Bazinga Cream Cheese Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/830551.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup butter, softened
4 cups sifted confectioners' sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon coconut extract

Directions:
In a medium bowl, cream together the cream cheese and butter until creamy. Mix in the vanilla, and coconut, then gradually stir in the confectioners' sugar. Store in the refrigerator after use.

Submitter: 
Geeky Gram
Description: 
This is an awesome creamy cream cheese frosting that goes well with pumpkin bread, carrot cake, chocolate cake, on cookies or between cookies.Modified from: Cream Cheese Frosting II
