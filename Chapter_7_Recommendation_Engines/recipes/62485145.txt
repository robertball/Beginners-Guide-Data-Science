Title:
Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/488514.jpg
Ingredients:
2pkg. frozen chopped broccoli
1 can cream of chichen or mushroom
1 egg beaten
1/3c. mayonnaise
1c. shredded sharp cheddar cheese

Directions:
cook broccoli & drain water off.
mix w/ the rest of the ingredient in a casserole dish.
bake @ 350 for 30mins.
