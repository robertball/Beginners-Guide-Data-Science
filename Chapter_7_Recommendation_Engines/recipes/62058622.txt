Title:
Karyn's Italian Sausage and Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb hot or sweet Italian sausage links
1/4 cup water
2 large sweet peppers, cut into strips
2 onions sliced
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp oregano

Directions:
In large skillet over medium heat, heat sausageswith waterto boiling.Cover and cook 5 min.Remoce cover, continue cooking, turning frequently until water evaporates and sausages are well browned. Remove to paper towels to drain. Pour off all but 3 tbsp of fat from skillet. Add green peppers, onions, salt and oregano. Cook over med. heat, stir frequently until vegetables are tender. Add cooked sausages to vegetables, heat through. Enjoy!
