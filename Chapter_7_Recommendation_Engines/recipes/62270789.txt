Title:
Holiday Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups lightly cooked broccoli, cut into bite-sized pieces (we use frozen)
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 sweet red pepper, cut into bite-sized pieces
1 1/3 c shredded cheese
1 1/3 c milk
2/3 c biscuit mix
4 eggs
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Place broccoli, onion,  sweet red pepper and cheese in a greased pie or quiche pan.  In a blender, mix together milk, biscuit mix, eggs, salt and pepper.  Mix well.   Pour over everything in the pie plate.  Bake at 350F for 30 minutes.  Allow to set for five minutes before serving,

Crustless Quiche with red and green vegetables.
