Title:
tofu chanpuru Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Extra Firm Tofu
1 Can of Spam
4 eggs, whisked
1/2 cabbage, chopped
1 large onion, chopped
2 carrots, grated
8 mushrooms, sliced
1/2 c sake
1/2 c miso paste
1/4 c soy sauce
2 T sugar
4 cloves of garlic
fresh ginger
green onion for garnish

Directions:
Wrap tofu in a paper towel and microwave for one minute. Remove, squeeze and wrap in dry paper towel. Let sit for a few minuets.
Dice spam, place in a container with garlic and ginger.
Combine miso, soy sauce, sugar, and sake in a separate container until combined.
Wisk eggs together.
Coat a pan with oil, cook eggs until done, use same pan and cook tofu until brown on each side. Remove and cook spam until garlic is browned.
In a separate pan cook all of the veggies.
Once all is cooked add meat, egg, and tofu. Add sauce and cook a min.
Serve with rice.
