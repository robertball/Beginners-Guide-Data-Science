Title:
Banana Coconut Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1974197.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Cup flour
1/3 Cup oat flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon Kosher salt
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla
3 Tablespoon coconut oil (melted)
1/4 Cup all-natural applesauce
1/2 Cup sugar
2 bananas (overly ripe, mashed)
1/2 Cup coconut
2-3 Tbl coconut for topping

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350.  Line a cupcake pan with paper liners.  Combine the flour, oat flour, salt, baking powder and baking soda in a bowl and set aside.  In a mixing bowl combine the remaining ingredients and mix well. Add the flour mixture and mix just until combined.  Fold in the 1/2 cup coconut.  Fill each muffin cup 3/4 full and sprinkle the top with the remaining coconut.  Bake 20 - 25 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
