Title:
Crock Pot Pulled Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 boneless pork chops (about 4 lbs)
2 (15 oz) cans tomato sauce, no sugar added, low or no sodium is best
3 tbs onion powder
2 tbs garlic powder
1 tbs cumin
2 tsp cumin
2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp cayenne
salt

Directions:
1. Place the chops in slow cooker
2. In med mixing bowl, blend tomato sauce together w all spices
3.  Pour sauce over chops
5.  Cook on low for 5 - 7 hours or until pork easily shred with a fork when pulled
