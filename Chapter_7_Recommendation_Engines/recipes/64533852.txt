Title:
Easy Turkey Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meat mixture:
1.25 lbs ground turkey (93/7 or leaner)
1 packet low-sodium taco seasoning mix
1 large onion, diced
1 tsp extra virgin olive oil
1 can (15 oz) dark red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
water
Toppings:
1.25 cups Doritos
1 bell pepper, diced
1/4 cup taco sauce
10 tbsp fat-free Catalina salad dressing
1/2 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
10 cups Romaine lettuce, shredded

Directions:
Fry the oil, onion, and meat together til completely cooked through. Drain and return to pan.
Add taco seasoning packet and water as per the packet's instructions; cook until liquid has evaporated away.
Mix in the beans.
Have each person assemble salad, meat, Doritos, peppers, taco sauce, cheese, and salad dressing to taste.

Submitter: 
JARRIE
Description: 
A quick & easy taco salad using turkey meat that is easily customizable to suit the dietary needs of the whole family.
