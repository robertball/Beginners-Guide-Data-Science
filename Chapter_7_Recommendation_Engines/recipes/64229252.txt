Title:
Penne with Tuna and Black Olives Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5774197.jpg
Ingredients:
12 oz penne
1 Tbsp oil
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1- 14 oz can chopped tomatoes
1 cup black olives, pitted
1 Tbsp fresh parsley
1- 6-1/2 oz can tuna, drained
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions.  Set aside.  In large frypan, saute garlic and onion in oil.  Add tomatoes and black olives and cook 10 minutes.  Add parsley, tuna, salt and pepper (to taste) and cook 5 minutes.  Add cooked pasta and toss.
