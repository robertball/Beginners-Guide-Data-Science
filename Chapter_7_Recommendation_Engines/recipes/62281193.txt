Title:
Elephant Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium Elephant
2 Rabbits
Brown Gravy
Salt Pepper

Directions:
Cut elephant into bite size pieces. This should take about 2 months.
Roast over open flame for 4 weeks at 450*
This will serve approximately 300 people.
If more guests are expected add brown gravy and rabbits. Do this only if necessary as most people don't like "Hare" in their soup!
