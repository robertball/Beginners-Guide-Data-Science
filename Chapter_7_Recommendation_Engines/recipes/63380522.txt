Title:
Prune Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups plain flour
1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
1 T. allspice
1 cup oil
1 cup buttermilk
1 cup cooked pitted prunes
1 T. allspice
1 t. nutmeg
1 t. cinnamon
1 t. baking soda
1/2 t. salt
1 1/2 cups chopped pecans
Icing Ingredients
1 cup sugar
1 stick margarine
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 t. vanilla
2 T. Kayro Syrup

Directions:
Cook prunes until done and drain
Combine all dry ingredients together
Slightly beat eggss and add eggs & remaining ingredients all together and mix well with mixer.
Bake in a greased & floured bundt or tube pan in a pre-heated oven of 350 for 45 minutes or until done
Icing
Combine all ingredients in a sauce pan and bring to a boil.
Boil for 5 full minutes, stiring from time to time.
Pour hot icing over cake
