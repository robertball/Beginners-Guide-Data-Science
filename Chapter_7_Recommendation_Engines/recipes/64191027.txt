Title:
Gina's Skinny Taste Fiesta Lime Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c cooked long grain rice
3/4 c canned black beans, rinsed and heated
3/4 c cooked corn (Trader Joe's Fire Roasted frozen corn is good in this), heated
1 large fresh tomato, diced
1 scallion, diced fine
2-4 Tbsp chopped fresh cilantro
1 tbsp fresh squeezed lime juice
Salt to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl combine hot rice, heated corn, heated beans, tomato, scallion, cilantro, lime juice, and salt.  Toss and serve.

Submitter: 
NJHoffman
Description: 
Rice, black beans, tomatoes, scallions, cilantro and lime juice, every bite of this colorful side dish will feel like one big fiesta in your mouth!  Use leftover rice and this side dish comes together in minutes.
