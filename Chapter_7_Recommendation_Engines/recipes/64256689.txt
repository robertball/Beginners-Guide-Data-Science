Title:
Chicken Cordon Bleu II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/2 cup Dairy Free cheese
4 Tablespoons Dairy Free Cream CHeese
8 Slices of Ham
Seasoning to taste
6 TBSP Butter
1/2 cup chicken broth

Directions:
Pound chicken breasts if they are too thick. Place ham slice on each breast within 1/2 inch of the edges. Fold the edges of the chicken over the filling, and secure with toothpicks.
Heat the butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat, and cook the chicken until browned on all sides. Add Chicken Broth Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes, until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear. Then add a TBSP of seasoned Dairy free cream cheese inside each chicken breast.
Remove the toothpicks, and transfer the breasts to a warm platter. ***Blend the cornstarch with broth and whisk slowly into the skillet. Add almond milk or cream cheese to make a creamy sauce) C
