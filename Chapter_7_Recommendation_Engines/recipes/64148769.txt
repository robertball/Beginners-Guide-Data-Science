Title:
Watermelon Sangria Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 cups cubed watermelon, divided
1 (750 milliliter) bottle white wine
1 cup vodka
1/2 cup triple sec
1 medium lime, quartered
1 orange, cut into wedges
1 cup fresh blueberries

Directions:
Place 9 cups of watermelon cubes in a blender; blend on high until very smooth.
Strain juice through a mesh strainer into a large pitcher.
Pour the wine, vodka, and triple sec into the pitcher. Stir to combine.
Mix the remaining 3 cups of watermelon cubes, the lime quarters, orange sections, and blueberries into the sangria.
Chill for 4 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
mystinkypete
Description: 
Fresh watermelon sangria is like summertime in a glass.  Just blend watermelon into a juice, and pour over fresh fruit along with white wine, vodka, and orange liquor.Modified from: Watermelon Sangria
