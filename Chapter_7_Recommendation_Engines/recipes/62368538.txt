Title:
Deb's Cucumber Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/409448.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup white sugar
4 teaspoons distilled white vinegar
3/4 teaspoon dried dill weed
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup thin sliced red onion
4 medium cucumbers, peeled and sliced

Directions:
Cut cucumbers in 1/2 lengthwise and spoon out seeds.  Slice thin and place in colander in the frig for 30 minutes.  Roll in paper towels to remove as much water as possible.
In a med bowl, stir together the mayonnaise, sour cream, sugar, vinegar, dill, and salt. Mix in the cucumber slices, tossing to coat.

Submitter: 
Debby Meese
Description: 
Cucumber slices are tossed with a cool, creamy dressing in this simple summer salad.Modified from: Mom's Cucumber Salad
