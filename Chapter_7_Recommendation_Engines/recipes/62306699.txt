Title:
Raisin Filled Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
DOUGH:
1 egg
1 cup sugar
1/2 c. shortening
1/2 c. milk
1 tsp. vanilla
3 1/2 c. flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
FILLING:
1 c. chopped raisins
1 tsp. flour
1/2 c. water
1/2 c. chopped nuts
1/2 c. sugar

Directions:
DOUGH:
1. Cream sugar and shortening together. Add egg, milk, and vanilla. Mix well.
2. Sift together dry ingredients.  Add to egg mixture. Mix well.
3. Roll out dough to desired thickness. Cut in circles using a bisquit cutter and lay on greased cookie sheet.
FILLING:
In a medium saucepan, cook until thickened.
4. Top each cookie with about 1 teaspoon filling.
5. Top each cookie with another rolled and cut-out circle.
Bake 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Bag Lady
Description: 
Sandwich cookies having a rolled out flat cookie on the bottom, raisin filling, and another rolled out flat cookie on top.
