Title:
Big Ray's Spinach Skillet Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6061888.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
1 package (10 ounces) spinach, chopped
1 can (4 ounces) mushroom stems and pieces, drained
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 tsp dried basil
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp salt
2 cups whole milk
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
Biscuits

Directions:
In a 10-in. cast-iron or other ovenproof skillet, cook beef and onion over medium heat until no longer pink; drain. Add the spinach, mushrooms, garlic and basil. Cover and cook for 5 minutes.
In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour and salt until smooth. Gradually add milk. bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened. Stir in cheese. Pour over meat mixture; mix well. Reduce heat; cook, covered, until heated through. Serve with biscuits.

Description: 
This is simple and tasty. An easy recipe for a work night. You may add additional seasoning like extra garlic and cayenne pepper for a kick. Delightful and filling when served with biscuits.
