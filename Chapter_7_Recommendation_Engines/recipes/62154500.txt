Title:
Creamy Key Lime Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 graham gracker crust
1 small package of lime jello
1 can of sweetened condensed milk
1 container of sour cream
3/4 cup of key lime juice

Directions:
dissolve jello in a half cup of boiling water in a mixing bowl.
add sweetened condensed milk
add sour cream
add key lime juice
mix well
pour into pie crust
refriderate at least 4 hours until set
there will be some extra filling left over that you can pour into cups.

Submitter: 
Kristine
Description: 
No bake key lime pie. Easy to make. only uses four ingredients.
