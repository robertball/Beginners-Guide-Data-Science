Title:
Pork Egg Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/510486.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1.5C shredded pork
1/2 head shredded cabbage
1/2 large carrot
2 slices vadalia onion
1/2 Tbl minced garlic
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp salt
1.5 tsp soy sauce

Directions:
Process first 4 ingredients(separately) in FP to desired consistency(I kept half the cabbage out and added after cooking).
Saute pork, veggies and garlic to soften.
Stir in seasonings and soy sauce.
Let cool and fill egg roll wrappers
