Title:
Out of this World Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990909.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound whole wheat wagon wheel pasta
1/2 pound whole wheat spiral pasta
1 large carrot, grated
1 red bell pepper
1 yellow bell pepper
1 green bell pepper
1 large head Romanesco broccoli (the "alien" veggie), trimmed and cut into florets
1/4 cup sliced black olives (optional)
1 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Light Dressing

Directions:
Prepare pasta according to package directions.
While the pasta is cooking, grate the carrot and cut the bell peppers into various sized stars and half-moons (crescents).
During the last 3 minutes of cooking the pasta, add the Romanesco broccoli and cook together.
Rinse the pasta and Romanesco broccoli mixture under cool water to prevent further cooking.
Combine the pasta and Romanesco broccoli mixture, grated carrot, bell pepper shapes and olives.
Add the Hidden Valley(R) Original Ranch(R) Light to the pasta and veggies and mix until well combined.
Chill for at least 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
Who can resist this colorful pasta salad: Red, yellow, and green bell pepper shapes, grated carrots, and Romanesco florets tossed with creamy Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Light Dressing make a delicious meal the kids will love.
