Title:
Chicken ala Jim slow cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/703535.jpg
Ingredients:
6- chicken breasts
1-10.5 oz can cream of mushroom
1-10.5 oz can cream of chicken
1-10.5 oz can cheddar cheese
1- pint button mushrooms
1- tsp minced garlic
1- tsp Worcestershire sauce
1/4 - cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Mix everything in the slow cooker and heat on high for 6 hours. Serve over rice or egg noodles.
