Title:
Chocolate Chip Cheeseball Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- 8oz cream cheese
1/2 c butter
1/4 tsp vanilla
2 tbsp brown sugar
3/4c confect. sugar
3/4c mini choc chips
3/4c finely chopped pecans
Choc graham crackers- (sticks work best)

Directions:
Cream the cream cheese, butter, and sugar products together.
Add vanilla and mix again.
Add choc chips and stir together until mixed.
Roll into ball and refrigerate for 1 hour to four hours. Roll the ball over the pecans.
Serve with graham cracker sticks.

Submitter: 
Cindy Peterson Kilday
Description: 
A really fun and easy dessert, and a treat for all ages!!  We use chocolate graham cracker sticks because that's what my kids prefer, but original flavor graham cracker sticks can be used as well.
