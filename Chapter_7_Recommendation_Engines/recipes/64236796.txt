Title:
Garlic Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 boneless chicken breasts
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 T olive oil
4 T brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450.
Line pan with foil and spray with Pam.  Put chicken in pan.
In small pan, saute garlic in olive oil until tender.
Remove from heat and stir in brown sugar.
Pour mixture over chicken and bake uncovered for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Karen Semmelrock
Description: 
Very quick and easy! Very moist and delicious.
