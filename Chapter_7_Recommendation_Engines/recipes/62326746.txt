Title:
Shrimp Francesca Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound fresh or previously frozen uncooked large shrimp, shelled and deveined
1 8-ounce can of artichoke hearts (8-10 count) drained, gently squeezing excess water from each artichoke heart by hand
½ cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
½ cup salted butter (or one stick of butter)
1 ½ tablespoons minced garlic (from a jar or freshly minced)
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
1 tablespoon finely grated imported Romano cheese
1 small fresh lemon, squeezed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Arranged shrimp in glass 9x13 inch lightly oiled pan.
Gently break apart artichoke hearts into fourths and arrange in between shrimp pieces, overlapping okay.
Sprinkle bread crumbs and parsley over shrimp and artichoke hearts.
Melt butter with garlic.
While butter is melting, squeeze fresh lemon juice over bread crumbs (no seeds).
Sprinkle melted butter-garlic over bread crumbs.
Sprinkle Romano cheese over bread crumbs.
Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until shrimp turns white and orange colored.
Servings: 6/8 for appetizer, 4 for main course over ligtly buttered angel-hair pasta.
