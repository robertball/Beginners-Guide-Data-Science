Title:
Pasta e Fagioli I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/553428.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup dry cannellini beans, soaked
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 onion, minced
1 stalk celery, chopped
1 carrot, minced
1 cup chopped ham
1 pound lean ground beef
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
4 cups beef stock
1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
1 bay leaf
1 pinch ground black pepper
2/3 cup seashell pasta
pinch red pepper flakes
pinch salt

Directions:
Drain the beans and set aside.
In a large stockpot heat the oil over moderate heat until hot. Add the onion, celery and carrot and cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes. Add ham and garlic and cook stirring for 1 minute. Add tomatoes, beans, beef stock, thyme, bay leaf and pepper to taste. Bring to a boil and simmer, covered, for 30 minutes.
Transfer 1/2 cup of the beans to a small processor or blender and puree. Return beans to pot and season with salt and pepper, red pepper flakes to taste. Bring liquid to a boil and add pasta. Simmer until pasta is tender, about 6 minutes.

Submitter: 
Chef Scott
Description: 
This traditional pasta and white bean soup deserves the best Parmesan cheese you can afford. Use the rind to flavor the cooking liquid, along with onion, celery, carrot, ham, garlic, tomatoes, beef stock, thyme, bay leaf and pepper.Modified from: Pasta e Fagioli with Ham
