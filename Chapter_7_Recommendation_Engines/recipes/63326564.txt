Title:
Sloppy Spicy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/881683.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound lean ground beef
1 small chopped onion
1 small chopped bell pepper (really any color is fine)
1 chopped jalapeno pepper
1 teaspoon of powdered garlic or 2 minced cloves
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 cup Sweet Chili sauce (Mae Ploy)
2 tablespoon brown sugar
1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
2 teaspoons of cider vinegar
1/2 teaspoon of celery seed
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir beef, onion, bell pepper, and garlic until beef is browned, about 10 minutes. Drain off liquids.
Stir in mustard, ketchup, chili sauce, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, celery seed and brown sugar and mix thoroughly. Reduce heat to low and simmer until sauce has thickened, about 20-30 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
