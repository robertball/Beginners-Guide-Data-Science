Title:
Beef and Black Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
2 15 ounce cans black beans, undrained
1 cup salsa
16 ounces tomato sauce
1 tablespoon chili powder

Directions:
Brown meat and drain.
While meat cooks, drain and mash one can of beans.
Combine meat, mashed beans, undrained beans, salsa, tomato sauce, and chili powder.
Cook over medium heat until heated (~10 min).
