Title:
Cinnamon Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 slices of bread
Margarine
* Cinnamon Sugar :
1 cup sugar
2 Tbsp Cinnamon
(Put cinnamon/sugar into a shaker)

Directions:
set oven to broil.  Spread margarine lightly and evenly over bread slices.  Shake Cinnamon Sugar generously over margarine and place on cookie sheet.  Place cookie sheet on the bottom rack of oven.  cook till bubbly and edges are just browned.  Remove from oven and flip slices over.  Place back in oven and let brown.  Remove from oven and serve.  You must watch carefully or they can burn very quickly.

Submitter: 
Dawn Raegene Mendenhall Scott
Description: 
I have been making this recipe for over 25 years.  All of us agree it is a quick treat when you want something warm and sweet.  Hope you enjoy.
