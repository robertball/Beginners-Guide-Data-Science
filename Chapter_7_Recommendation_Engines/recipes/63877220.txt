Title:
Sausage & Cheese Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1075566.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. uncooked sausage
2 c grated cheddar cheese
2 c bisquick
2 t milk

Directions:
Mix together & roll into 1" balls. Bake at 350 for 20 mins.
