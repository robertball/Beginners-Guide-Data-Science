Title:
Homemade Creamy Chip Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2822574.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1/3 cup ketchup
1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
3 green onions, finely chopped
1 pinch salt

Directions:
Beat cream cheese with an electric mixer until smooth, about 3 minutes; beat in ketchup, cream, green onions, and salt until well combined, about 2 minutes.
