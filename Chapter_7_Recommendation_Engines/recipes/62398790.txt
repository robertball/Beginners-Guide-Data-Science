Title:
Genevieve's Fantastic Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c sugar (or truvia, per conversion instructions)
1/2 c butter, softened
2 eggs
1 t vanilla
1 t lemon juice
1 3/4 c flour
1 t baking powder
1 t baking soda
1/2 t salt
1/2 c sour cream or plain greek yogurt
1 c ripe banana
1/2 c nuts, optional
1/2 c chocolate chips (mini work great but regular are fine)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. In separate bowl, blend until creamy butter and sugar. Beat in eggs, vanilla and lemon juice until creamy. Add flour mixture in 3 parts to the sugar mixture. Fold in sour cream and banana. Fold in chocolate chips and/ or nuts. Pour in loaf pan for 60 minutes.

Submitter: 
Genevieve Johns Seivert
Description: 
Yummy! This is an incredibly moist recipe. You can add nuts or chocolate chips if you prefer. So delicious. I double the recipe and it is never around long !
