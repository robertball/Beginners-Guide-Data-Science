Title:
Tennessee Pork & Peach Grilled Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 T. canola oil
2 T. fresh rosemary, finely chopped
1/2 teaspoon coarse black pepper
Kosher salt
1 1/2 lbs. pork tenderloin, any fat trimmed
Reserved peach glaze (recipe below)
Rosemary sprigs, garnish
Peach Glaze Ingredients:
1 cup red-wine vinegar
1/4 cup water
1/4 cup honey
salt & black pepper
3 slightly under-ripe peaches, halved, pitted and each half sliced into quarters.

Directions:
In a small bowl whisk the oil, rosemary, and pepper.  Place pork tenderoin in a ziploc bag, pour the oil mixture over the pork.  Be sure the entire piece of meat is coated.  Refrigerate 8 hours.
*Meanwhile make the peach glaze.* *recipe below*
Remove pork from refrigerator 20-30 minutes before grilling to bring meat to room temperature.
Heat grill to high heat.
Season tenderloin with salt and grill on all sides until golden brown and slightly charred, 2 minutes per side.
Place tenderloin on aluminum foil and brush with peach glaze.  Loosely wrap the meat in the foil.  Cover grill and continue grilling 5 minutes or until desired doneness meter inserted in the center of the pork reads 145 degrees.
Remove tenderloin from grill and place on a cutting board, tent with foil, let rest 10 minutes.
Slice into 1/2 inch thick slices.  Place on a platter with the peaches; drizzle with the remaining glaze.  Garnish with rosemary sprigs.
Peach Glaze Recipe:   Bring the vinegar, water, honey, salt and pepper to a simmer in a large high-sided saute' pan over medium heat.  Add the peaches and cook, stirring once or twice until slightly soft, about 5 minutes. ---  Remove peaches to a plate; continue cooking the liquid until slightly reduced, 5 minutes.  Transfer half of the glaze to a small bowl.  (1/2 glaze for using to grill and 1/2 glaze for drizzling over tenderloin when serving)

Description: 
This is a great peach glaze using fresh peaches.  When it's peach season, it's time for this pork tenderloin recipe.
