Title:
Dirty Brown Rice and Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/805634.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups uncooked brown rice
chicken bouillon cube (Knorr)
2 bay leaves, divided
1/8 and 1/4 tsp cayenne, divided
1/8 and 1/2 tsp paprika
1/8 and 1/2 tsp thyme
1 tsp oil
1 onion, diced
2 celery, diced
1 green pepper, diced
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 lb lean ground beef
salt
pepper
1 lb medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
2 scallions

Directions:
Cook brown rice according to package directions in water along with a chicken bouillon cube, 1 bay leaf 1/4 tsp salt, 1/8 tsp salt, cayenne, paprika and thyme.  When the rice is almost done cooking, heat a large heavy saucepan on medium low heat.  Add the oil, onions, green pepper, celery, bay leaf and garlic.  Saute on medium low heat until soft about 5 minutes.  Add beef, salt, black pepper, remaining paprika, thyme and cayenne pepper.  Saute until browned about 10-15 minutes; add shrimp, cover and cook 5 more minutes.  When the rice is done, toss with beef and shrimp and combine well.  Top with scallions and serve.
