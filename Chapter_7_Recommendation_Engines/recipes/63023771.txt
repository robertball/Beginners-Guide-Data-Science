Title:
Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 c chopped peeled potatoes
1 1/2 c water
1/2 c chopped onion
1/2 c copped celery
1 tbsp instant chicken boullion
1 c milk
1 c sour cream
2 tbsp flour
1 c milk
2 tbsp chopped chives
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Combine potatoes, water, onion, celery.
Bring to boil.
Reduce heat and simmer covered for 20 minutes.
Stir in 1 cup milk.
Blend sour cream and flour in bowl, then stir into soup.
Cook until thickened, stirring constantly.
Add 1 cup milk, chives, salt and pepper. Mix well.
Heat to serving temp.

Submitter: 
Marcia
Description: 
Creamy, flavorful soup that everyone will love. Perfect for a chilly fall day!
