Title:
Roasted Cheesy, Garlic & Onion Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/287619.jpg
Ingredients:
2 stalks of broccoli
4-5 cloves of garlic finely chopped
1/4 Spanish onion, finely chopped
1/2 cup of grated sharp cheddar
course salt & freshly ground pepper
1 tsp red chili pepper flakes
1 tbsp of olive oil

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400F & line a pan with foil.
Chop broccoli at the stems, and discard the stalk.
Put broccoli, garlic, onion, salt, pepper, chili pepper flakes, olive oil into a bowl and mix until broccoli is well coated.
Place broccoli mix on pan and keep it tightly packed together.
Sprinkle cheese over the top.
Bake for 20 minutes on the middle rack and broil for another 5 minutes.

Submitter: 
Raquel Teixeira
Description: 
I love roasting broccoli! It makes eating veggies feel like a comfort food and can win over even the biggest anti-veggie eaters out there. I know, my boyfriend is one of them!
