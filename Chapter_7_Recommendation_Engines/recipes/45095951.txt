Title:
Coops Hot Bean Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 cup sour cream
2 (16 ounce) cans refried beans
1 (3.5 ounce) can mild diced green chilies (OPTIONAL)
1/2 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
5 drops hot pepper sauce (OPTIONAL)
1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (8 ounce) package shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 # Ground beef browned and drained

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a medium bowl, blend the cream cheese and sour cream. Mix in the refried beans, taco seasoning, hot pepper sauce if used, 1/2 the Cheddar cheese, Green chilies if used, Ground beef and 1/2 the Monterey Jack cheese. Transfer the mixture to an 8x12 inch baking dish. Top with remaining Cheddar and Monterey Jack cheeses.
Bake in the preheated oven 20 to 30 minutes, until cheese is slightly browned.
If you're in a hurry and don't care about presentation you may combine all ingredients in a saucepan and heat on the stove top.
Keep warm and serve with tortilla chips.
