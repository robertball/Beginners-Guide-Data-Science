Title:
Sausag, Artichoke, & Parmesan Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Pork Sausage
12 oz artichokes
12 oz garlic butter croutons
1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
2 cups chicken broth
1/2 cup diced onions
1/2 cup diced celery

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325
Spread sausage over bottorm of 9x13 baking dish; bake 30 minutes
Cut artichokes into bite sized pieces
Remove sausge from oven and crumble into fine pieces
Stir in artichokes, croutons, cheese, celery, onions, and broth
Cover dish with foil; bake 45 minutes
Remove foil, bake 5 additonal minutes
