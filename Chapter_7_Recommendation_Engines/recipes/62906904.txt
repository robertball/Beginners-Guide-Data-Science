Title:
Sweet Potato Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/744679.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Cups Flour
4 tsp Baking Powder
1 tsp Salt (I use iodized sea salt)
1 Cup + 3 TBS Powdered Sugar
1/2 Cup (1 stick) Butter
2 Cups Mashed Sweet Potatoes
1/4 Cup Buttermilk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and powdered sugar
Cut in butter
Add sweet potatoes (you might need to use your hands)
Add buttermilk
Lightly knead dough on floured surface
Roll out to 1/2-inch thickness and cut with a 2-inch floured biscuit cutter
Bake on greased/silicone pad covered cookie pan for 12-15 minutes
