Title:
Pumpkin Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c sugar
1 c vegetable oil
3 eggs
1 16 oz can pumpkin
3 c flour
1 t baking soda
1 t salt
1 t baking powder
1 t groiund cinnamon
1 t cloves
1 t nutmeg
1 c chopped nuts (optional)
1 c chopped raisins (optional)

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, beat together sugar and oil. Beat in eggs and continue until light and fluffy. Add pumpkin and mix well. Combine flour, soda, salt, baking powder, cinnamon, cloves and nutmeg to the pumpkin mixture. Stir until dry ingredients are moistened. Fold in nuts and raisins and and pour into (2) 9x5" greased pan. Bake at 325 for 60-75 min. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans.
