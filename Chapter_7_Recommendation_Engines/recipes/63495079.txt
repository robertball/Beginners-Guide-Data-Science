Title:
Our favorite Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. Ground Beef
1 lb. Sausage
1 c. chopped Onion
3 cloves Garlic, minced
3 1/2 t. Italian Seasoning, divided
1/2 t. salt
3 (14 oz) jars of favorite Marinara Sauce
4 c. shredded Italian Cheese blend, divided
1 (16 oz) pkg small curd Cottage Cheese
1 (15 oz) pkg Ricotta Cheese
2 lg Eggs
1/2 t. Garlic Powder
1 (16 oz) pkg Lasagna Noodles, cooked

Directions:
Brown sausage, beef, onion, garlic 2 t. italian seasoning & salt.  Add marinara sauce and cook, stirring often, for 5-10 minutes.
In food processor, combine 2 c. shredded cheese, cottage cheese, ricotta cheese, eggs, garlic powder & remaining 1 1/2 t. italian seasoning.  Process until smooth.
Lightly grease a 4 1/2 qt. lasagna dish.  Spread 4 cups sauce mixture in bottom of dish; top with 6 noodles.
Spread 2 cups cheese mixture over noodles; top with 2 cups sauce mixture.  Place 6 noodles on top of sauce mixture, and spread with 2 cups cheese mixture; top with 2 cups sauce mixture.  Top with 6 noodles, remaining cheese mixture and remaining sauce mixture.  Sprinkle with remaining 2 cups shredded cheese blend.
Cover with aluminum foil and bake 45 minutes at 350 degrees.  Uncover & back 15 minutes longer.  Do not overbake.

Submitter: 
Dee
Description: 
This is my go-to lasagna recipe.  I took 3 of our family lasagna recipes & revised the best parts into one really delicious lasagna!!  Be forewarned - this makes a huge amount!
