Title:
Date Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup chopped dates
1 egg
1 1/2 cups boiling water
4 tbsp. butter
1 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. baking soda
2 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp. baking powder
3/4 cups chopped walnuts
1 tsp. vanilla

Directions:
Sprinkle soda over dates.
Add boiling water and butter.
Let cool.
Add sugar and beat well.
Add beaten egg & vanilla.
Mix flour and baking powder; add to date mixture.
Add walnuts.
Bake in greased loaf pan @ 350 x 45 or more minutes, till tests done with toothpick.
Turn out of pan after 10 minutes to cool.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Not named for a special night ;) but rather the fruit that adds a delicious flavor to this moist loaf.
