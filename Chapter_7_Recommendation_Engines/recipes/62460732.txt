Title:
Chipotle Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/471553.jpg
Ingredients:
10 Tomatillos ~ Husks removed and cleaned
1 White Onion Diced
4 Garlic Cloves Minced
2-3 Chipotle Peppers in Adobo
1/4 Teaspoon Ground Cumin
1/4 Mexican Oregano
1/2 Teaspoon White Pepper
1/2 Teaspoon Salt
1/2 Cup Water

Directions:
Place 1/2 Cup water, tomatillos, onion and garlic in pan.  Cover and simmer turning occasionally until tomatillos turn slightly brownish and become soft.  Add more water as needed, a 1/4 at at time.
Add chipotle peppers and adobo, cumin, oregano, pepper, salt to pan with tomatillos.
Add everything to blender and blend until smooth.  Cool and serve.

Submitter: 
Sherbear1
Description: 
This is a simple chipotle salsa that can be used on any mexican dishes to add a smokey, spicey flavor
