Title:
Famous Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Oz. Can Pineapple Chunks
2 Tbls. Cornstarch
1/2 C. brown Sugar
2 Tbls. Soy Sauce
2 Tbls. Lemon Juice
1 Green Pepper Sliced
1-3 Lb. Frozen bag of Original Meatballs

Directions:
Slice Green pepper, defrost meatballs and set aside.
In a medium sauce pan pour in Pineapple Chunks with Juice, Brown Sugar, Soy Sauce, Lemon Juice and Cornstarch.
Bring to a boil Stiring constantly until mixture has thickened.
Place meatballs in crockpot and pour pineapple mixture on top. Mix.
Heat crockpot accordingly. Recommend 2 Hours on Medium heat mixing every 30-45 minutes.

Submitter: 
loves2bake
Description: 
These meatballs are a favorite for any occasion. I made 14 pounds of meatballs for my sister's graduation party and I only had 1 medium container left over. My mother made sloppy joes and needless to say they had alot left over because everyone loved these meatballs. Guarantee favorite!!
