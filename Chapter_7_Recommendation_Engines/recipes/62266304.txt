Title:
Benji's Jalapeno Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup Mayonnaise
5 Small Whole Jalapenos
3 Tablespoons Jalapeno Juice
1 Teaspoon Chill Powder
1/2 Teaspoon Garlic Powder

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except the jalapenos in bowl.
Add Mixture to a blender.
Add Jalapenos.
Blend until combined.
Serve Cold.
