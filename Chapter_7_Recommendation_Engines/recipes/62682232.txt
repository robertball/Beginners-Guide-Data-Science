Title:
Wholesome Sugar Free Applesauce Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/601732.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c. whole grain flour (I used Barley flour)
1 tsp. soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. ground cloves
1/2 tsp. ground nutmeg
1 tsp. ground cinnamon
1 1/4 tsp. stevia
1 c. nuts, chopped (optional)
1/2 c. butter
2 eggs
1 c. applesauce
1 c. raisins (soaked and ground)
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. honey

Directions:
Combine thoroughly the dry ingredients and nuts.  Cream together butter and eggs, then add applesauce, ground raisins, vanilla and honey.  Pour into 8" square cake pan.  Bake at 350 for 40-45 minutes.  A double recipe fills a 9"x13" pan.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Root
Description: 
Cutting back on sugar to help our ADHD son, I made this cake for dinner one night and my whole family loved it.  It's our new favorite sugar free cake recipe.   Wholesome and healthy, yet really curbs the sweet tooth!
