Title:
Baked Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tbsp. butter
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp. sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
(Optional) Lemon and powdered sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Put butter in 9-inch pie pan. Put in oven until butter is melted.
Mix the remaining ingredients together with a whisk and immediately pour into hot pan. Return to the oven and bake for 20-24 minutes until brown on the edges.
Remove add lemon and powdered sugar, then serve.
