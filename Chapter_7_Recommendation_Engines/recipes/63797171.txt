Title:
Jalapeno Popper Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 (2 ounce) can chopped green chilies, drained
1 ounces canned diced jalapeno peppers, drained
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Stir together cream cheese and mayonnaise in a large bowl until smooth. Stir in green chiles and jalapeno peppers. Pour mixture into a microwave safe serving dish, and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
Microwave on High until hot, about 3 minutes.

Submitter: 
Adina Ryan
Description: 
This is an easy cream cheese spread to make, and it tastes just like the jalapeno poppers you wish you could make yourself!  The hardest part of this recipe is not eating it all by yourself!Modified from: Jalapeno Popper Spread
