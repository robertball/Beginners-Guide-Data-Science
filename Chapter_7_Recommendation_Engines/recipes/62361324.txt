Title:
Mrs. W's Sloppy Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb. ground beef
1 green pepper
1 medium onion
2 10.5-oz cans condensed tomato soup
2 Tbsp. worcestershire sauce
1/4 c. ketchup
1/4 c. white vinegar
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 c. brown sugar

Directions:
Dice onion and pepper.  Place ground beef in skillet.  Add onion and pepper.  Saute until ground beef is no longer pink.  Drain grease.  Add remaining ingredients.  Simmer on low approximately 15 minutes to blend flavors.

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
This is a sloppy joe recipe that works for any age crowd.  The ingredients are simple and the sweet touch of brown sugar will make this everyone's favorite at pot lucks, reunions and holiday gatherings!
