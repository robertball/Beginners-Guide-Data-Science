Title:
Dina's Summer Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ready Made Pie Crust
Philidelphia Cream Cheese
Strawberries
Blueberries
Raspberries (if you wish)
Blackberries (if you wish)
1 Cup Sugar (Splenda)
Cool whip container
1 tspn vanilla (optional)

Directions:
Beat sugar (Splenda) and Fat Free cream cheese together.  Fold in fat free whipped cream -small cointainer.  Fold in berries & strawberries and place in crust.  Refrigerate for a couple of hours.  Make soft enough to mix berries in and refrigerate.
(Raspberries)
Layer Cool Whip.
