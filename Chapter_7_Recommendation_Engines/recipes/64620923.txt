Title:
Savory Butternut Squash & Potato Puree Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 lb butternut squash
1 lb potatoes, peeled and quartered
1 cup evaporated non-fat milk (or dairy alternative)
1 tablespoon butter or olive oil
salt and fresh ground black pepper, to taste

Directions:
Cut squash in half and scoop out the seeds. Peel the shell and cut the squash into 3-inch dices.
Place squash and potatoes in large saucepan and fill with water and 1/2 tsp salt.
Bring to boil and cook until both squash and potatoes are fork-tender (30-40 minutes).
Drain liquid (reserving about 1 cup) and add milk and butter.
Puree, adding cooking liquid as necessary to achieve desired consistency.
