Title:
Quick Meatball Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/967532.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tsp minced garlic
1/2 an onion
1 tbs oil
15-20 Frozen Meatballs, beef or turkey
1 tbs Worcestershire sauce or to taste
1 can of condensed mushroom soup
1/2 cup of milk
8 oz No Yokes broad noodle, or your choice
salt
pepper
3/4 cup sour cream

Directions:
In large skillet, heat oil on medium high heat, add garlic and onion, cook till onion is almost soft. Lower to medium heat add mushroom soup, milk, and Worcestershire, stir until blended. Add frozen meatballs, stir to coat, cover and let simmer on low 35-40 minutes, stir occasionally. Cook noodles as directed on package.  Add sour cream, salt and pepper to taste to meatballs, stir well and cook another 2 minutes.  pour meatballs over drained noodles.
