Title:
Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/728468.jpg
Ingredients:
8 pounds med sized russet potatoes, unpeeled
12 eggs
2 cups real mayonnaise (we use Kraft)
2 tbs yellow mustard
4 tbs dill pickel rellish
4 green onions, thinly sliced (optional)
25 green olives with pamintos, thinly sliced, then chopped fine
3 sticks celery, thinly sliced, then chopped fine
2 tsp salt (or to taste)
1 tsp ground black pepper (or to taste)

Directions:
Wash potatoes well. Put potatoes in large pot, add eggs on top and cover with water. On med /high heat bring to a boil. Cook for 20 to 30 min, or until potatoes are tender but still firm. Drain, cool, peel and chop (potatoes into small chuncks, and dice eggs)
In a large bowl, combine chopped potatoes and eggs. Add mayonnaise, mustard , dill rellish, green onion, olives, and celery. Season with salt and pepper, then mix well. While mixing, smash some of the potatoes to get a chunky mashed potatoe salad. Can serve warm right away, or cover, and refrigerate for several hours or overnight.
