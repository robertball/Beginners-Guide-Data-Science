Title:
Cassie's peanut butter bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289874.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup creamy peanut butter
3/4 stick Butter ; softened
1 1/4 cup sugar
3 large Eggs
1 teaspoon Vanilla
1 cup Flour
1/4 teaspoon Salt
1 cup Semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
preheat oven to 350Â°
cream peanut butter, butter, and sugar in mixer on medium speed till well mixed.
add eggs 1 at at time mixing in between and scraping bowl.
add vanilla, flour, salt and mix well
stir in chocolate chips
spread batter into a glass 9 X 13 pan ungreassed.
bake for 25-30 minutes till edges are brown and top no longer looks wet
cool in pan and cut into bars

Submitter: 
greenbabby
Description: 
Peanut butter and chocolate! What could be a better combination. These soft bar cookies hit the spot!
