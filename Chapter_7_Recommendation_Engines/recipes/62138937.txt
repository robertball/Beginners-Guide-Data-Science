Title:
Mrs.T's Marvelous Meat Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lb. ground beef
1/2 med. onion diced
2 stalks celery diced(optional)
4 tsp Italian seasoning
2 6oz cans tomato paste
2 4oz cans sliced mushrooms(do not drain)
2 14.5 cans diced tomatoes(do not drain)
garlic salt to taste

Directions:
In a large skillet brown meat, onion and celery until meat is completely cooked. Drain off fat and add seasoning, tomato paste, mushrooms, and diced tomatoes. Make sure paste is combined completely. Add garlic salt to taste. Cover and simmer to combine flavors (about 30 mins).
Note: If using pre-seasoned diced tomatoes, adjust seasoning to taste

Submitter: 
Arika L. Thomas
Description: 
A refreshing break from the classic spaghetti and meatballs; this meaty sauce is sure to be fill you up crowd pleaser.
