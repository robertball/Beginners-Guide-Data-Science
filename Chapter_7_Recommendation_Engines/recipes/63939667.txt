Title:
Coconut Face Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1092119.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoon of coconut oil
4 Tablespoons of shea butter
5-6 drops of tea tree oil

Directions:
Add all ingredients together.
Use the back of a spoon to mash the shea butter into the coconut oil.
Mix until the cream looks like frosting.
Store in a 4 oz. jar with a lid.
Apply to face and neck.
DO NOT USE FOR AN UNDER EYE CREAM.

Submitter: 
Lela
Description: 
A homemade pure facial moisturizer using three ingredients. Your skin will thank you!
