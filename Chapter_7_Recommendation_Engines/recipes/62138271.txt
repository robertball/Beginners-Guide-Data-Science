Title:
Roasted Garlic Bulbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Bulbs of Garlic
2 Tablespoons of Olive Oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Remove outer layer of garlic skin, just enough that the garlic stays together still.
With a sharp knive slice off the pointy end of the garlic bulb, to expose most of the cloves of garlic.
Pour the olive oil in a small bowl and place each bulb in oil, cut side down for at least 2 minutes each.
Place the bulbs on baking sheet, cut side up and cover with foil.
Roast for about an hour, depending on the size of the cloves.  Garlic should be very soft and have a light brown color when done.

Submitter: 
Buffy S.
Description: 
A simple recipe for making a delicious appetizer.  The garlic can be served with goat cheese, red onion, kalamata olives and bread.
