Title:
Grilled Squash and Zucchini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbl spoon unsalted butter
1 lemon
1 zucchini
1 squash
1 tea spoon kosher salt
1 tbl spoon pepper
1 tea spoon cayenne pepper

Directions:
Cut squash and zucchini and place in a foil wrapper.
Zest lemon over vegetables, and then squeeze the lemon juice.
Add salt, pepper and cayenne to vegetables.
Cook on grill over medium indirect heat for 1/2 hour.

Submitter: 
John Cox
Description: 
A refreshing summer vegetable medley.
