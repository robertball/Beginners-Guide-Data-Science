Title:
Air Fryer Turkey Breakfast Sausage Links Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6935302.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9.6 ounce) package turkey breakfast sausage links

Directions:
Preheat the air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Place all 12 links in a single layer in the basket of the air fryer.
Cook for 6 minutes.

Submitter: 
Soup Loving Nicole
Description: 
Why stand over a skillet flipping and turning to get sausage brown on all sides? Simply throw the links in the basket and let the air fryer do the browning for you. This simple 'how to' will save you time on those hectic mornings.
