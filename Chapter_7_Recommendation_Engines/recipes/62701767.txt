Title:
Lynn's Black Bean and Corn Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons oil
1/2 cup chopped onion
garlic clove pressed (optional)
minced fresh hot pepper to your taste
1 (15.5 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed or soak 2/3 cups of dry black beans overnight and cook till tender.
1 cup whole kernel corn
1/4 cup homemade salsa or chopped tomato
1.5 tablespoons butter, divided
8 (8 inch) flour tortillas
2 cups shredded hot pepper Jack cheese, divided

Directions:
Heat oil in a large saucepan over low heat.  Stir in onion, and cook until caramelized. 5 minutes before done add pressed garlic and chopped hot pepper turn off heat and put a lid on it. Stir in beans and corn, then salsa and pepper; mix well.  Cook until heated through, about 3 minutes.
Melt 1/2 tsp. of the butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Place a tortilla in the skillet, sprinkle 1/2 tortilla with cheese and bean mixture. Fold over tortilla and slice in half. Cook until golden, then flip and cook on the other side. Melt more butter as needed, and repeat with remaining tortillas and filling.
