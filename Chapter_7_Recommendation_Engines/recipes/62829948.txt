Title:
Spinach and Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/670320.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained (see note below)
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 cups shredded monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a medium bowl, mix together artichoke hearts, spinach, mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese and 1 cups  Monterey Jack cheese. Transfer mixture to a baking dish.
Bake in the center of the preheated oven until the cheese is melted, about 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Dawn
Description: 
This is the best. Consisting of spinach, artichoke hearts, mayonnaise and cheese.  You can always have these ingredients on hand to make this yummy quick and easy dip. It bakes in the oven until bubbly and all the cheese is melted.Modified from: Spinach and Artichoke Dip
