Title:
Fruit Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 kiwis, peeled and diced
1 quart strawberries, finely chopped
4 Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and coarsely shredded
2 tablespoons any flavor fruit jelly
Cinnamon Sugar
1/3 cup lime juice

Directions:
Place kiwis, strawberries, blackberries, and apples in a bowl. Stir in jelly,cinnamon sugar,  and lime juice. Stir together.
Spritz Tortilla chips with water, sprinkle cinnamon sugar over spritzed tortillas. Bake at 300 degrees for 5 minutes.
