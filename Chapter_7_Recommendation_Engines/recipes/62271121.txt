Title:
Cuke Vegetable Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Cucumbers
1 Onion
2 Green Peppers
3 Celery ribs
4 Tbsp Salt
2 C Sugar
1 C Vinegar

Directions:
Slice all thin, put in bowl and cover with water and salt. Let stand 2 hours and drain. Do not rinse. Bring sugar and vinegar to a boil and pour over vegetables. Refrig. 2-3 pints.

Submitter: 
sassyoldlady
Description: 
Vegetable relish great on bratwurst, hot dogs, burgers.
