Title:
Rachel's Ham Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup melted butter
2 tbsp sweet & zesty mustard
1 tbsp dried minced onion
1/2 tbsp poppy seeds
2-3 tsp sugar
15 dinner rolls-large
15 slices colby jack cheese
30 thin deli sliced ham (2 lbs)
8 oz (2 cups) shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Combine in small bowl: butter, mustard, onion, poppy seed and sugar.
Place roll bottoms, cut side up, on ungreased sheet.
Top with colby cheese slice, 2 slices ham and mozz cheese.
Drizzle with 1/2 butter mixture.
Replace roll tops.
Drizzle with remaining butter mixture.
Bake uncovered @ 350 degrees for 10-15 minutes.
