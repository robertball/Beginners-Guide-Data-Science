1 pizza dough
20 slices pepperoni
20 slices geona salami
20 slices capicola
Provolone cheese (as much as desired)
1 cup marinara sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425Â°F. Roll out pizza dough in a rectangular shape. Assemble with all ingredients, any order. Close dough over top to meet in the middle and pinch down the center.
Bake for 25 min. or until golden brown. Let stand 5 mins., then slice and serve.
