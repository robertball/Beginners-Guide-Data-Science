Title:
Canned Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 quarts field tomatoes
6 med onions
8 cloves garlic
8 celery stalks
1/3 cup sugar
2 Tbs Worcestershire
1/2 cup lemon juice
Spices .. I used basil, oregano, salt, pepper
2 Tbs Olive Oil
2  156 ml cans tomato paste

Directions:
Wash tomatoes and slightly slice an X on the bottom of the tomato (opposite the core)
Put the tomatoes in a pot of water and boil about 10 minutes or until the skin starts to lift
Place the tomatoes in cool  water until cool enough to remove core and peel
In a non-stick frying pan, add oil, celery and onions.  Fry on medium until translucent, add garlic and fry until garlic is cooked, about 5 minutes
In a stock pot
Add tomatoes and break apart with your hands
Add onions, garlic and celery
Add tomato paste
Add the rest of the ingredients
Stew on low about 2 hours, stirring frequently (I used a wooden spoon)
Immersion blend and add to jars
Leave 1 inch head room and hot water bath 20 mins
