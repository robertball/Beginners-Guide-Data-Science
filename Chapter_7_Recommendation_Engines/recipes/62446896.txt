Title:
Chocolate Pound Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Duncan Hines Chocolate Fudge Cake Mix
1 small package of instant chocolate fudge pudding
4 eggs
1 cup sour cream
3/4 cup water
1/4 cup canola oil
4 oz. semisweet chocolate (melted)

Directions:
Combine first six ingredients, beat on low speed for 30 seconds.  Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.  Mix in melted chocolate on low speed.
Pour into greased and floured 9-inch pans.  Bake at 350 degrees.  Start checking after about 25 minutes.
