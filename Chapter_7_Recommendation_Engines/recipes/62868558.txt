Title:
Homemade Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/688726.jpg
Ingredients:
-1 lb ground beef
-5 cups elbow macaroni noodles(or any kind of that matter, elbow hold up better in casserole)
-1 can (14 oz) kernel corn
-1 can (14 oz) tomato soup
-5 large slices velveta cheese(or processed American cheese)
-1 TBS black pepper
-1 TBS salt
-1/2 TBS garlic salt

Directions:
1. In a medium skillet, brown and mash meet. After meat is browned, drain grease.
2. Meanwhile, boil noodles on high in a large pot until firm, but not too flimsy. Drain and add meat to noodles.
3. Drain corn and add to noodle mixture.
4. On very low heat, add velveta, cheese, salt, pepper and garlic salt. Constantly stir the casserole until it becomes thick and all cheese is melted.

This recipe is easy to make and will feed a whole household without putting a hole in your pocketbook!
