Title:
Crock Pot Beef Brisket with Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lb beef brisket
4 sweet potatoes, cut in 8 pieces each
1 small head of cabbage, cut in 8 pieces
1 medium onion, sliced
1 Granny Smith apple, quartered
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1 can water

Directions:
Season beef brisket to taste and place in bottom of crock pot.
Spread vegetables and apple on top of meat.  Mix mushroom soup and water, and pour over everything.  Cook on low for 8 hours.
