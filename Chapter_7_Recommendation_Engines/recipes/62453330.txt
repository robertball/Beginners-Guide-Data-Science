Title:
Easy Pasta and Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box of Fetticini or Linguini (approx 1 pd)
1 package of Polish Kielbasa (approx 1 pd)
16 oz sour cream
1 small onion
1 bunch of spring onions (use approx 8)
1 can sliced water chestnuts
1 large green pepper
Salt and Pepper to taste
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 tbsp of worchestershire sauce
Grated Parmesan Cheese (if desired)
Parsley Flakes

Directions:
Boil pasta according to package directions; rinse in cold water
Chop green pepper and onion
Slice spring onions
Slice Kiebasa (may also cube)
In non stick pan, saute the chopped green pepper, onion in olive oil.  Add the Kielbasa, lower heat and cook approx 12 minutes stirring occasionally.
Add pasta, water chestnuts, sour cream, salt and pepper, worchestershire sauce (more if desired) and toss lightly over high heat until thickened.
Dust with parsley flakes and garnish with cheese when served.
