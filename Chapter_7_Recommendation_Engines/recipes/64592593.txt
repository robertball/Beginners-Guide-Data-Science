Title:
Garbage Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup shreddies cereal
1 cup corn flakes cereal
1 cup cheerios cereal
(or ANY combination of cereal to equal 3 cups)
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup coconut
1/2 cup margarine
1/2 cup corn syrup
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup peanut butter

Directions:
Mix cereals, raisins and coconut together in large bowl.
Put margarine, corn syrup and brown sugar in a micro-safe glass bowl. Bring to bubble in microwave.
Add peanut butter. Mix well.
Pour this over cereal mixture.
Stir together.
Drop by tbsp. onto wax paper on cookie sheets.
Cool.
Keep in fridge or freezer.
