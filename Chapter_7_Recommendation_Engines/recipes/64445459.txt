Title:
Apple Pie Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans apple pie filling
1 box cake mix
8 Tbsp melted butter

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 F
2. Pour pie filling evenly into a 9x13in casserole dish
3. Cover pie filling evenly with cake mix, then with the butter
4. Bake for 40 minutes, or until the top starts to turn golden
5. Cool for 15 minutes and serve warm

Submitter: 
Marya “Marta” Krause
Description: 
My mum taught me this recipe when I was just learning to bake, and I still use it for parties as it is a crowd pleaser. Having only 3 ingredients it is super quick and easy to make. A dollop of vanilla ice cream tops it very well.
