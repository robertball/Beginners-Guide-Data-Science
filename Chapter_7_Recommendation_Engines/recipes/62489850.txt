Title:
Double Chocolate Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
* 1 cup butter, melted
* 1 cup white sugar
* 1 cup brown sugar, packed
* 4 eggs
* 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
* 3 medium over-ripe bananas, mashed
* 1/2 cup applesauce
* 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
* 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
* 2 teaspoons baking soda
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 1 teaspoon cinnamon
* pinch of nutmeg
* 1 cup sour cream
* 1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350F.  Grease two loaf pans.
Whisk or mix melted butter and sugar together, mix well for 2 minutes.  Add eggs and mix well.  Add vanilla, bananas, and applesauce- mix well and set aside.
Whisk dry ingredients (flour, cocoa, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg) together in a separate bowl.  Remove 1/4 cup in a small bowl for later use.
Add dry to wet and mix until JUST combined.  Add sour cream and fold in.  Mix the 1/4 cup reserved dry ingredients with chocolate chips.  Fold in chocolate chips. Pour equally into the greased loaf pans.
Bake at 350F for 1 hour to 1 hour 15 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.  Allow to cool 15 minutes before removing from pan.  Remove from pan and allow to cool completely before slicing.

Submitter: 
Skoo
Description: 
A moist, rich, dense double chocolate banana bread.  I developed this recipe for my chocoholic boyfriend who complains when there's nothing chocolate in the house. This recipe makes two loaves, eat one now and freeze the other for later!
