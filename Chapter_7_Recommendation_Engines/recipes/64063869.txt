Title:
Big Ray's Pumpkin & Sausage Penne Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1130226.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked penne pasta
1 lb Italian Sausage Links links, casings removed
1 medium cup chopped sweet onion
2 garlic clove, minced
1 Tbl olive oil
1 cup white wine or chicken broth
1 bay leaf
1 cup chicken broth
1 cup canned pumpkin
2 Tbl minced fresh sage, divided
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
Dash ground nutmeg
Dash allspice
1/2 cup sour cream
4 oz cream cheese
1/2 cup shredded Romano cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, cook sausage over medium heat until no longer pink, breaking into crumbles. Remove with a slotted spoon; drain on paper towels. Discard drippings, reserving 1 teaspoon.
Cook and stir onion and garlic in oil and reserved drippings over medium-high heat until tender. Add wine and bay leaf. Bring to a boil; cook until liquid is reduced by half. Stir in the broth, pumpkin, 1 Tbl sage and remaining seasonings; cook 1 minute longer. Add the sour cream, cream cheese and sausage; heat through. Remove bay leaf.
Drain pasta; transfer to a large bowl. Add sausage mixture; toss to coat. Sprinkle with cheese and remaining sage.
