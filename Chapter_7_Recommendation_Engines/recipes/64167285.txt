Title:
Italian Halibut Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pounds halibut, cubed
1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
1/2 onion, chopped
1-1/2 stalks celery, chopped
1 carrot, chopped
1/2 zucchini, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 Tbs olive oil
3/4 cup V8 tomato juice
1/4 cup apple juice
1 (16 ounce) can italian stewed tomatoes, diced
1 tsp chopped parsley
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon dried basil
1/8 teaspoon dried thyme
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Saute the peppers, celery, carrots, onion, and garlic in oil until tender.  Add the zucchini, tomato juice, apple juice, italian stewed tomatoes, and herbs.  Simmer for 30 minutes.
Add halibut pieces to the soup.  Cook until halibut is done, about 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
A great soup for a dinner party, Italian Halibut Chowder is seasoned with traditional Italian herbs.  The halibut swims in a tomato-based stock with red peppers, onions and celery.  Delicious garnished with parmesan cheese.Modified from: Italian Halibut Chowder
