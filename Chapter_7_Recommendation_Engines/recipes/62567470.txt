Title:
Karo Sweet 'n Sour Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans (8oz.) Pineapple Chunks
2 Tbls Veg Oil
1 Green Pepper cut in 1" squares
1 Onion cut in wedges
1 clove Garlic
1 Lbs Pork cut in strips
2 Tbls Corn Starch
1/2 C Corn Syrup
1/4 C Cider Vinegar
3 Tbls Soy Sauce
2 Tbls Ketchup

Directions:
Cook Rice.
Drain Pineapple - Reserve liquid.
Heat Oil add Grn Pepper, Onion, Garlic; Stir fry 2 mins. Remove.
Stir fry Pork.
Mix Pineapple Juice, Corn Starch, Soy Sauce, Ketchup, & Vinegar. Add to cooked Pork and stir until thickened.
Return Grn Pepper, Onion and Grlic to skillet.
Serve over Rice.
