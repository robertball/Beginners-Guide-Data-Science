Title:
Low-Fat Fettuccine Alfredo with Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/306569.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Skimmed milk
1/2 cup Lowfat cottage cheese
1 tablespoon Cornstarch
1/4 teaspoon Salt
1/8 teaspoon Pepper
1/4 teaspoon Garlic powder
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
2 cups broccoli (chopped)
1/4 cup fresh parsley (chopped)
8 oz (uncooked) whole wheat fettuccine
8 oz (cooked) chicken breast

Directions:
Place first 7 ingredients in a blender or food processor and blend until smooth. Pour mixture into a skillet and cook over medium heat until heated through and smooth, stirring occasionally. Add broccoli and parsley.
Meanwhile, prepare pasta according to direction, draining it 2 minutes before done. Add pasta to the sauce and cook the last two minutes.
Serve topped with chicken.

Submitter: 
Scott Koeneman
Description: 
The sauce is low-calorie and low-fat, but you wouldn't know it by tasting it!
