Title:
Watermelon and Cucumber juice with a Spritz of Lime Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/442845.jpg
Ingredients:
½ medium Watermelon
4 Cucumbers
2 medium Limes

Directions:
Remove black seeds from watermelon, and juice (don’t juice rind). Juice cucumbers whole, with skin. Squeeze in juice of limes. Stir and enjoy!!

Submitter: 
Alyse Ellienne
Description: 
Cool and refreshing juice -- mellow watermelon and cucumber juice with a touch of tartness from the lime.
