Title:
Vermicelli Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 16 oz. package vermicelli pasta, noodles broken into thirds
2 "bunches" green onions, minced, white and green parts
2 green bell peppers, minced
1 bottle McCormick's Salad Supreme Seasoning Blend
1 16 oz. bottle Thousand Island Salad Dressing

Directions:
1. Prepare pasta according to package directions, drain, but don't rinse.
2. Combine pasta with remaining ingredients and chill overnight to allow flavors to blend.

Submitter: 
Jay Lynne Stuart
Description: 
This quick and easy pasta salad is very different from most, but it's packed with flavor.  It goes over great for potlucks and barbeques and can easily be made well in advance.
