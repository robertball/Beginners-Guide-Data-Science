Title:
Dr. Mikes Wicked Whole Wheat Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup (+ 3 Tbsp) Water
3 cups King Arthur Traditional Whole Wheat Flour
3 Tbsp Skim milk powder
2-3 Tbsp Gluten flour
3 Tbsp Sunflower seeds
1-2 Tbsp Sugar
1 1/2 Tsp Salt
3 Tbsp Flax seed or meal
4-6 Chopped dried apricots (optional)
3 Tbsp Crisco oil
2 1/2 Tsp Yeast

Directions:
Add all ingredients to baking pan following manufacturers directions.
Save the +3 Tbsp water to adjust dough during second knead cycle. Dough should be sticky to the touch but not slippery.
Set bread machine on 1 1/2 lb loaf and dark crust setting.
