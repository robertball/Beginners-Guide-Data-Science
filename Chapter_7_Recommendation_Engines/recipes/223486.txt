Title:
Pineapple and Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1104979.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package Neufchatel cheese, softened
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, partially drained
1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese
1 sweet onion, finely chopped

Directions:
Combine Neufchatel cheese, pineapple, Cheddar cheese, and sweet onion in a bowl; smash with a fork until blended.

Submitter: 
reciprocipe
Description: 
This is really good, and so easy - no cooking required and no fancy spices. Mix ingredients in a bowl and serve with your favorite crackers. If sweet onions are not available and your onion is strong, add only half at first and then add more to taste.
