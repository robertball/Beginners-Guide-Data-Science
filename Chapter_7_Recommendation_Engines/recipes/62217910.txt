Title:
Grape Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs. seedless grapes (green or red)
1 8-oz. tub cream cheese
8 oz. sour cream
1/2 cup Splenda
2 Tbsp. brown sugar

Directions:
Blend together cream cheese, sour cream, Splenda and brown sugar.  Add grapes.  Chill well before serving.
