Title:
Delicous Blackberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 tbsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
2 eggs
2/3 cup sugar
8 tbsp melted butter
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp milk
1 cup sour cream
11 oz fresh blackberries

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients (flour, powder, salt) and set aside.  In separate bowl, whisk together butter, sugar, eggs, vanilla, milk, and sour cream.
Add butter mixture to dry ingredients and stir until just blended.
Slowly fold in blackberries.  Don't overmix.
Bake at 450 for 18-20 minutes.  Makes 12 generous muffins.

Submitter: 
Bleuroze
Description: 
These moist muffins are a great way to turn fresh blackberries into a yummy breakfast treat.  Also great with oatmeal crumb topping.  In a pinch, this can be made with frozen berries that are thawed and drained.
