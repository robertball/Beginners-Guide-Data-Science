Title:
Chipotle Chorizo Mexican Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 oz) pkg. al frescoÂ® Chipotle Chorizo Chicken Sausage, sliced on the bias 1/4"
1 (16") pre-cooked thin pizza crust
1 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
3/4 cup tomatillos sauce
1 cup cotija cheese, shredded
1 cup queso fresco (Mexican cheese) 1 red onion thinly sliced into rings
1 cup cilantro, fresh chopped (measure before chopping)
2 tbsp oregano (preferably Mexican oregano)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450&deg; F.
Prepare all ingredients and have them assembled in front of you and ready to top your pizza crust.
First brush the pizza crust with olive oil and spread the tomatillo sauce over the crust leaving a 1" border around crust.
Sprinkle half of the cheeses around the crust, arrange the sausage slices all over the pizza crust. Top with the red onion slices and the cilantro.
Top with the remaining cheeses, place in oven and bake for about 8 - 10 minutes. Remove from oven and let set for 5 minutes. Slice it as you like it and enjoy.
