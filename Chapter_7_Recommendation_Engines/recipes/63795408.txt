Title:
Baked avocado with egg Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1052961.jpg
Ingredients:
avocado, egg, spices

Directions:
- preheat oven to 425, have cast iron pan in there
- Slice avocado in half, take out pit
- take pan out, put avocado half on, crack egg in
- put whatever you want on top
- place in oven and cook till your eggs are done how you desire

Submitter: 
sevasta
Description: 
If you have an avocado that's too firm to use for something else, or you're just in the mood for something new and healthy for breakfast, this recipe is for you.
