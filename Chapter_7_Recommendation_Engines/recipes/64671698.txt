Title:
Easy Teriyaki Tofu Lo Mein Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4585195.jpg
Ingredients:
lo Mein Noodles
1 Package Extra Firm Tofu
1 Green Onions
3 Cloves Garlic (chopped)
1 Teaspoon Ginger Powder
1/2 Cup Teriyaki Sauce
1/2 Cup Soy Sauce
1/2 Cup Brown Sugar
2 1/2 Tablespoons Sesame Oil

Directions:
1. Drain tofu and pat with paper towel until dry, cut into about half inch cubes then set aside.
2. Cook lo mein per directions on package.
3. Heat 1 tablespoon of sesame oil in large pan, when hot place tofu in pan. Saute until browned on all sides. Remove from pan and set aside.
4.  Heat remaining oil in same pan, add in garlic. Cook for 2-3 min on medium heat.
5. In small bowl combine soy sauce, teriyaki sauce, 1/2 tablespoon sesame oil, and brown sugar.
6. Add Tofu and noodles to pan then toss to coat in sauce mixture..
7. Serve with chopped green onion and sesame seeds to garnish.

Description: 
Great teriyaki flavor with succulent noodles and good for you tofu.
