Title:
Feta Corn Black Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/761772.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can kernal corn
1 can black beans
1 can diced tomatoes
1/4 cup chopped cilantro
1/2 cup plain crumbled Feta
1/4 - 1/2 cup sliced pickled jalepenos
2 tblsp chopped garlic
1/8 tsp fresh ground black pepper
1/4 cup Ponzu - Lime flavor

Directions:
Drain chop drop all into bowl.
Use jalepenos to your taste and heat levels.
Mix and serve.
