Title:
Cherry Red Jello Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small packages cherry jello
1 can cherry pie filling
2 cups boiling water
1 can crushed pineapple
1 8oz carton sour cream
1 package cream cheese
1/2 cup sugar
2 tbsp vanilla
1/2 cup chopped nuts

Directions:
Mix jello and water together.  Let stand for about 10 minutes.
Add pineapple and pie filling and stir until well blended.  Pour into a 13x9x2 pan and chill until firm.
Mix the remaining ingredients until light and fluffy and spread like frosting over jello mixture.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
A delicious jello salad that pleases children and adults alike.
