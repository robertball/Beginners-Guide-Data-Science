Title:
Duchess Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/593932.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/3 cups of yukon gold potatoes
1/4 tsp salt
1 ~ 2 tbsp milk
2 tbsp of butter or margarine
1 egg
3 tbsp melted butter

Directions:
In a medium saucepan boil potatoes and salt until tender (approximately 20 minutes) Preheat oven to 450*
Drain very well, add butter and milk and let cool slightly.  Add egg and whip with a electric beater until smooth. Using a pastry bag pipe onto into mounds or drop by spoon onto lighty greased or sprayed cookie sheet. Brush tops with melted butter and bake in a 450* pre-heated oven for 10~12 minutes until lightly browned. Let sit for just a minute for easier lifting.
