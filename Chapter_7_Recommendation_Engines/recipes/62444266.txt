Title:
Steamed Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Approx 6 cups water
1 cup white rice (or brown - if using brown add 2 min to each step)
pinch salt

Directions:
Bring water to a boil
Add rice
Boil 10 minutes, uncovered
Drain water through a sieve
Put two inches of water in the bottom of the pot
Put sieve on top of pot, cover and steam 10 min (bring to a boil then set to a simmer)
