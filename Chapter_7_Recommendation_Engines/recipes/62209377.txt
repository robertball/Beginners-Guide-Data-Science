Title:
Brunzie's Hamburger Casserole / Baseball Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef/ground sirloin
1 medium onion
1 (10.75 ounce) can Cream of Chicken
1 (10.75 ounce) can Chicken Noodle soup
3/4 - 1 cup of instant rice
salt, pepper, and seasoning of choice for burger

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Chop onion and combine with ground beef in skillet.
Brown ground beef and season to your liking. I like to use seasoning salt and some pepper.  Drain excess liquid.
In casserole dish combine cooked ground beef onion mixture, can of cream of chicken, can of chicken noodle soup, and instant rice (uncooked).  I also like to lightly salt and pepper the top before placing in oven.
Place in oven at 350 for 40-45 minutes.  It will start bubbling around the edges.

Submitter: 
Augustana
Description: 
My mom used to make this before little league games.  Its an oldie but probably still my favorite home cooked dish.  Its simple, quick, and everyone loves it!
