Title:
Jill's Famous Beef Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb	fresh mushrooms, sliced
2 tbsp	vegetable oil
3 tbsp	butter
2 tbsp	minced green onions
1/2 tsp	salt
2 lb	beef tenderloin cut .25" thick strips
1/3 cup	madeira
3/4 cup	beef bouillon
2 tsp	cornstarch
1 tbsp	heavy cream
1 cup	heavy cream
1/4 tsp	pepper
parsley for garnish
1 pkg	egg noodles
butter
parmesan

Directions:
Saute Mushrooms in 1 tbsp oil & 1 tbsp butter 5 mins.
Add onions	…..cook another minute
Season with 1/4 tsp salt.  Place mushrooms in bowl.
Dry meat on paper towels
Place 2 tbsp butter & 1 tbsp oil in pan.
heat until butter foam subsides
Saute beef, few pieces at a time for 1-2 mins each side
Set aside in separate bowl.
Pour madeira & bouillon in same frypan.
Boil down liquid rapidly over high heat while scraping up browned particles
Reduce liquid to 1/3 cup during this boiling-down process
Blend cornstarch with 1 tbsp cream.
Beat in remaining cream…then cornstarch mixture.
Simmer 1 minute.
Add sauteed mushrooms…simmer 1 minute
Sauce should thicken.
Season with 1/4/tsp salt & pepper.
Adjust seasonings to taste.
Add meat to sauce…heat briefly to just below simmer so will not overcook
Garnish stroganoff w/parsley…Serve
Egg noodles cooked separately…rinse…add a little butter & parmesan
