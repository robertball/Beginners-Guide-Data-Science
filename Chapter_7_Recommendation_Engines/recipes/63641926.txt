Title:
Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5264139.jpg
Ingredients:
6 large green, red, or yellow peppers
1 lb ground beef
2 tablespoons chopped onion
1 cup cooked rice
1 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoone garlic salt
1 (150z) can tomato sauce
3/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Cut top off the pepper and removed seeds and membranes
rince peppers
boil peppers in water 5 min and drain
brown ground beef and onion
stir in rice, garlic salt and tomato sauce to taste and  heat through
stuff each pepper with beef mixture
stand up right in ungreesed square baking dish
pour remaining sauce over peppers
bake at 350 for 45 min
add cheese and bake until cheese starts to brown
