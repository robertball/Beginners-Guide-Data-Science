Title:
Instant Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the Cake:
5 eggs
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup of oil
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 3/4 cups flour
1 tbsp baking powder
For the Syrup:
1 cup sugar
1 1/2 cups water
1/4 cup instant coffee granules

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix all the cake ingredients for 2 minutes then pour in a pre-greased bundt pan.
Bake for 30 minutes.
Meanwhile, bring to a boil all the ingredients for the syrup.
Reduce to medium and simmer for 10 minutes.
Once the cake comes out of the oven, release it and add all the syrup.
Allow the cake to absorb the syrup for at least 10 minutes then flip into serving dish.
Serve this cake warm or at room temperature!!
