Title:
Fettucine Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup (1 stick) butter
2-3 cloves garlic, finely minced
1 cup cream and 1 cup milk (can substitute 2 cups half and half)
16 oz fettuccine noodles
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
cook fettuccine noodles until tender in large pot of boiling water.  meanwhile, melt butter in a large pot, add garlic and cook low for 1 minute. add cream and milk and heat until hot but not boiling.  season with pepper. add hot cooked fettuccine noodles. add parmesan cheese and stir into noodles.  heat over low until cheese is melted and sauce thickened about 5 mins
