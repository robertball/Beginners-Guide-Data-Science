Title:
Surprise Inside Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3lbs Lean Ground Turkey
1 Large Green Pepper
1 Large Sweet Onion
5oz White Mushrooms
1 Envelope Lipton Beef and Onion Mix
7oz Diced Tomatoes, Drained
2 5oz Cans Tomato Sauce
3/4 Loaf of Bread
1tsp Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F
Dice onions, green peppers, mushrooms and bread.
Combine turkey, tomatoes, green peppers, bread, onions, lipton mix, pepper and one can of tomato sauce and knead until mixed completely.
Form into two loaves in a large, deep roasting pan.
Cover loaves with remaining can of tomato sauce.
Bake at 400 for 90min.

Submitter: 
IamPatSajak
Description: 
As a mom of all boys I know it's hard to get them to eat veggies. This delicious and healthy recipe for turkey meatloaf tastes like beef and they will have no idea it's full of veggies.
