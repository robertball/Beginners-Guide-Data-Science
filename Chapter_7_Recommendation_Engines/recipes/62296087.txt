Title:
Forgotten Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
3/4 c. sugar
6 oz. chocolate chips
1/8 tsp. salt
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Separate eggs and beat whites until stiff. Fold in sugar, chocolate chips, vanilla and salt. Heat oven to 400 degrees. Drop cookies on foiled lined cookie sheets. Turn off oven. Leave cookies in oven for 6 hrs or overnight. DO NOT PEEK!
