Title:
Chocolate Trifle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/325990.jpg
Ingredients:
1 9 x 13 dark chocolate cake
2 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix
1/2 cup water
1 quart heavy cream
1 1/2 cups whole mix (to combine with 1 box of pudding)
1T confectioners sugar
1/2 cup ganache
1/2 cup caramel sauce
1 pkg heath toffee bits
1 bag mini chocolate chips

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine 1 box pudding mix and water. Mix until smooth, then beat 1 cup of cream to medium peaks. Fold in cream to pudding until no streaks remain. Prepare 2nd box of pudding with whole milk, following the directions provided on the box for pie filling. Whip remaining whipped cream and 1 Tb of confectioner's sugar to medium peaks
In a trifle bowl or glass serving dish, place half of the cake crumbs, top with 1/2 of the sweetened whipped cream, drizzle with ganache. Repeat cake layer, top with the chocolate cream mixture, drizzle with caramel sauce. Top another layer of chocolate cake, top with chocolate pudding, drizzle with ganache, top with remaining sweetened whipped cream.  Sprinkle top with heath toffee bits and mini chocolate chips. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
