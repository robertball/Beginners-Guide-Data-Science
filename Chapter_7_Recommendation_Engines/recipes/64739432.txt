Title:
Cacao Nibs Chia Seed Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6361963.jpg
Ingredients:
? cup chia seeds
1 cup unsweetened almond milk (plus more for blending)
1 tablespoon maple syrup or honey
¼ cup cashews
5 tablespoons cacao nibs
1 teaspoon cocoa
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
? teaspoon sea salt
¼ teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon espresso powder, optional

Directions:
Combine all ingredients, reserving 3 tablespoons cacao nibs, in the container of a high-speed blender.
Blend for 30 seconds to 1 minute, until combined.
Stir in 1 tablespoon cacao nibs and pour into serving dishes.
Refrigerate, covered, about 1 hour or overnight.
Mixture will thicken as it chills.
To serve, top with additional cacao nibs.
May keep sealed in the refrigerator for 3-4 days.

Submitter: 
Angela Sackett  Superhotmama
Description: 
Easy to make in less than five minutes, this cacao nib chia seed pudding uses a blender to make chia seeds into a creamy treat that's dairy-free and Paleo-friendly.
