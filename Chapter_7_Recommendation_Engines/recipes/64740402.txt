Title:
Courtney &Friends Chocolate  Cake With Warm Gingered Pears Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6389746.jpg
Ingredients:
For The Cake::
2 cups black beans, rinsed and drained.
4 large eggs.
3/4 cup cocoa ( Hrersheys  make a gluten free  one).
1/2 teaspoon baking powder(Fleichmanns makes a gluten frrr one)
1 and 1/2 cup white sugar.
1 teaspoon vanilla
For  The Gingered Pears::
6 Honey Bell Pears washed and sliced
1  tablespoon crystallized ginger..
1/4 cup dried cranberries
1 tablespoon butter ( I use Natrel Lactose free or Earth Balance)
1 tablespoon maple syrup.

Directions:
FOR THE CAKE:
Preheat oven to 350.
Grease a 9 inch spring foam pan.
Purée beans in a food processor unti beans start to form a paste.
Add eggs and process for  3-5 min.
Add cocoa, baking powder, sugar and vanilla, purée 2-3 min. Until smooth
Pour into pan and bake 25 min. or until toothpick comes out clean.
Let cool  in pan for 10 min. before topping with warm pears.
FOR THE PEARS::
In a large pan sauté butter( Natrel or Earth Balance) pears,ginger and cranberries. For about 5-6 min.
Add  Maple Syrup and stir.
Top  the cake with French Vanilla Frozen Yogurt or Lactose Free Ice Cream
and the warm Gingered Pears.
When I make this into cupcakes I do not add icing.

Submitter:

Description: 
My friend Courtney  gave me this recipe she came up with.
Can be made Lactose and Gluten free.
You would never know this cake was made with black beans.
Can be made into cupcakes also, which is how I made them.
They are great  for a snack, and also freeze very well.
Wonderful for those that can’t have dairy or wheat products.
