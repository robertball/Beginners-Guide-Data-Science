Title:
Elsy's chickpea burger Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups dried chickpeas
1/2 cup roughly chopped coriander with stems
2 tablespoons tahini
5 garlic cloves peeled
2 medium eggs
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cumin
2 teaspoons coriander powder
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
3 tablespoons chickpea flour
Vegetable oil for frying

Directions:
Soak the chickpeas overnight and cook until, soft.
Place all ingredients in blender and mix until well mixed but still chunky.
Form patties and shallow fry for 3 minutes on each side or until golden and crisp.

Submitter: 
Jerryfish
Description: 
Amazing taste and quick to make. 
I serve these on an open toasted wholemeal bun with guacamole on top.
