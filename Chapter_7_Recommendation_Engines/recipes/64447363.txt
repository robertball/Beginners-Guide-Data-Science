Title:
Rick Gallop GI Steak Fettuccine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 top sirloin grilling steak (approximately 1 lb)
2 TB dijon mustard
2 tsp dried Italian herb seasoning
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
2 shallots, thinly sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp dried basil
3 chopped tomatoes
1 each red and orange bell pepper, thinly sliced
1/2 low fat, low sodium beef stock
1 c trimmed snow peas
1/4 tsp salt
8 oz whole wheat fettuccine or linguine

Directions:
-trim any excess fat from steak and discard. in small bowl, stir together mustard, italian seasoning and ground black pepper. spread evenly over steak. place steak on greased grill over medium high heat for about 8 minutes, turning once or until medium rare. remove to plate, cover and keep warm.
-in non-stick skillet, heat oil over medium high heat and cook shallots, garlic, oregano, and basil for 2 minutes or until starting to get golden. add tomatoes, bell peppers, and stock. bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer gently for about 5 minutes or until tomatoes are starting to break down. add snow peas and cook for about 3 minutes or until bright green. stir in salt.
-meanwhile, in large pot of boiling salted water cook fettuccine for about 10 minutes or until al dente. drain well and return to pot. toss with sauce to coat. place in large serving dish.
-thinly slice steak across the grain and lay on top of the fettuccine. toss in gently and serve
