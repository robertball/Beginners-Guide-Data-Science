Title:
Fat Free Creamy Italian Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Fat Free Mayo
1/2 small onion
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
2 tablespoons Splenda
1 teaspoon Italian seasonings
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
combine all ingredients and blend in magic bullet

Submitter: 
Miranda Oswald
Description: 
Use this as a sauce or a dressing.  This is great over hot grilled veggies or cold salad.  Completely guilt free.
