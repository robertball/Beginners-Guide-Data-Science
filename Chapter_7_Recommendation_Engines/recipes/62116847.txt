Title:
Not your Mom's Mac and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/422571.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package Gemelli or rotini pasta
1 lb. lean ground beef or turkey
3 tbsp. butter
3 tbsp. flour
3 cups of Milk or Cream
1 cup fontina cheese, grated or cubed
1 cup sharp yellow cheddar, grated or cubed
1 cup private stock white cheddar, grated or cubed
1/4 cup grated cheese
1/8 tsp paprika
1/8 tsp nutmeg
dash of cinnammon
dash of chili powder
1/8 tsp garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
2 tbsp butter
1/2 cup italian bread crumbs or crushed potato chips
1/8 cup grated cheese

Directions:
Boil the pasta until just before al dente and set aside with a little olive oil tossed through.
Cook the ground beef and season with salt and pepper.
Heat the butter and flour on low heat.
Once the butter is melted and the flour is incorporated slowly add the milk or cream.
Once heated add the cheese and simmer for five minutes stirring frequently.
Add the paprika, chili powder, garlic powder and salt and pepper.
Mix the tbsp butter and bread crumbs with grated cheese.
Mix the cheese, pasta and turkey together and put in a casserole dish.
Sprinkle bread crumb mixture over the top of the macaroni and bake at 350 for 12 minutes. Then broil for 3 minutes on high.

Submitter: 
Michele McCormick
Description: 
4 cheeses make up this delicious macaroni and cheese that will not easily be forgotten. I use high quality cheese and I think it makes all the difference.
