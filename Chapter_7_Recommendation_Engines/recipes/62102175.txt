Title:
Mexican party dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/8 can refried beans      *f.g.-Few grains
7.5 ml  salsa
1/4 ml  hot pepper flakes
f.g.    salt
f.g.    cumin
1/8     ripe avocado, peeled and chopped
7.5 ml  onion, finely chopped
5 ml  mayonnaise
pinch   cayenne pepper
10 ml  plain yogurt
10 ml  sour cream
63 ml  shredded cheddar cheese
1/2     green onion, finely sliced
1/4     tomato, finely diced
1-2     tortilla shell/wrap
sm. amount  olive or canola oil
Parsly flakes, dried

Directions:
FIRST LAYER
Blend together refried beans, yogurt, hot pepper flakes, salt, and cumin. Spread over bottom of the bowl.
SECOND LAYER
In blender, food processor or a fork, mash avocado, blend in onion, mayonnaise, lemon juice, and cayenne. Spread over bean layer.
THIRD LAYER
Blend yogurt with sour cream, smooth lightly over second layer. Sprinkle cheese, green onion, and tomato in a ring on third layer.
TORTILLA CHIPS
Pre-heat oven to 375 F
Place tortilla shells on a cookie sheet.
Brush on olive or canola oil.
Take out when crisp.
Sprinkle on Parsley flakes as disired.
