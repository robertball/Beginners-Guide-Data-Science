Title:
Leftover Prime Rib with Mushrooms and Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tsp butter
2 tsp water
2 tbsp. minced garlic
2 cups of sliced mushrooms
2 thick slices of prime rib cut into 1 inch cubes
1 1/2 - 2 cups previously prepared mashed potatoes
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt butter in large fry pan under medium heat and add water and minced garlic .  Stir for a minute and add mushrooms and cover, stirring every couple of minutes  until tender.  Add prime rib and mashed potatoes and turn heat to low, again, stirring every couple of minutes until heated up.   Sprinkle with ground pepper and salt.
Serve with steamed vegetable of your choice.
