Title:
Toffee Mocha Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Angel Food Cake cut into 1 inch cubes
3/4 cup strong cooled coffee
1 pkg. (8oz.) cream cheese softened
1/2 cup chocolate syrup
3 TBS sugar
2 cups whipped topping thawed
2 Heath Bars crushed

Directions:
Place cake cubes into an ungreased 13 x 9 inch pan or dish.  Add coffee and toss lightly.
In a bowl, combine cream cheese, chocolate syrup, & sugar until blended.
Fold in the whipped topping.  Spread over cake.
Sprinkle the top with Heath Bar and refrigerate 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Lisa Proctor
Description: 
This light dessert has been a family favorite for years.  Hope you enjoy it as much as we have!
