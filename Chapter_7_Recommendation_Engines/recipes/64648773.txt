Title:
Maid-Rite Loose Meat Tavern Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4534146.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb ground beef, the leaner the better.
1 medium onion, minced finely
1Tbsp yellow mustard
1/2 c. water
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
2 tsp soy sauce
1 chicken bullion cube
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
in large skillet, cook beef and onion on medium heat, but do not brown.
Use a fork or spatula to separate beef into small crumbles while cooking.
When meat is mostly cooked through, add remaining ingredients and stir thoroughly.
Simmer mixture, stirring occasionally, until liquid is gone.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
Serve with slotted spoon onto hamburger buns, garnish with dill relish, mustard, ketchup.

Submitter: 
Chris Bellers
Description: 
An incredibly savory and tasty loose meat sandwich that tastes authentically like the ones at Maid-Rite.
