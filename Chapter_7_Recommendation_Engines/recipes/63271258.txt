Title:
Apple Caramel Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/858759.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Heavy Cream
1 3/4 cups (14 oz) Sweetened Condensed Milk
1/2 cup Apple Sauce
4 tbsp Caramel Sauce
3 tbsp Salted Butter (melted)
1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract

Directions:
In a large bowl, whip heavy cream until stiff peaks appear, or put the cream into a blender until stiff peaks appear. Then whisk in the butter, sweetened condensed milk, caramel sauce, apple sauce, salt, and vanilla. Pour the mixture into a large container, then cover the container. Freeze the mixture for 6 hours. The ice cream can be stored in the freezer.
