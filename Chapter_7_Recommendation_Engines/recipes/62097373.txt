Title:
Peanut Butter and Banana Frothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 frozen banana
6 oz of milk
2 T of peanut butter
2 t of sugar or maple syrup (optional)

Directions:
Place all ingredients in the blender and blend until smooth.
Pour in a glass and enjoy!

Submitter: 
ELIZRA
Description: 
We've been making these since I was a little girl.  The key is to use a very ripe banana (the kind you'd use for banana bread) that has been frozen.  It's easier when it has already been cut into chucks. This drink  is also great without peanut butter.
