Title:
Warm Cauliflower Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1031904.jpg
Ingredients:
-2 cups fancy spring mix bagged lettuce. We used the 50/50 blend that had baby spinach also
-1TBS pine nuts
-1TBS crumbled bacon
-1TBS hard cheese. We used pre shredded Parmesan and Romano
-1TBS capers
-2 TBS balsamic vinaigrette
- 1cup steamed cauliflower

Directions:
Fill your salad bowl with the lettuce. Add the pine nuts, capers, bacon and hard cheese. Drizzle with 1 TBS vinaigrette.
Cut up cauliflower into small florets and steam until tender.
Drizzle remaining vinaigrette over cauliflower and mix.
load on top of salad

We came home and reproduced it to the best of our ability.
