Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 frozen banana
1 TBS dark cocoa powder
2 TBS full fat coconut milk
2 TBS dried, sweetened coconut flakes
1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:
Freeze a banana!
After it's frozen, puree it in a food processor with the coconut milk, vanilla, and cocoa powder. Go until it's nice and smooth and fluffy! Stir in the coconut after, and enjoy your tropical, frozen, vegan, dairy-free, fluffy bite! Perfect for the mascots of any international scavenger hunt!
Makes 1 big, full cup serving

Submitter: 
Roya
Description: 
I am vegan, always looking for a good, creamy substitute for ice cream. This dessert is dairy free, chocolatey, and suuuper easy to make. It's perfect!
