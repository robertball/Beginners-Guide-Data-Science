Title:
Coconut Buttermilk Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, melted
3 eggs, beaten
2 tbsp. flour
1 cup coconut
1 1/2 cups sugar
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 tsp vanilla
1 frozen pie crust

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.  Combine all ingredients and pour into pie shell.  Bake 45-55 minutes till center is firm.  Shield with aluminum foil after 20 minutes if necessary to prevent over-browning.

Submitter: 
Tammy Adkins Byerly
Description: 
Buttermilk pie is easy and SO good.  Add some coconut and it will blow your socks off.
