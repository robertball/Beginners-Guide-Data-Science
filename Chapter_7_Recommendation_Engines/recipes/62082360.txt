Title:
Pasta Primavera Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 med. onion, chopped
2-3 lg. garlic cloves, minced
3 tbsp. unsalted butter
1 lb. fresh asparagus, snapped and     diagonally cut into 1/4" pieces
1/2 lb. fresh mushrooms, thinly sliced
1 1/2 bunches broccoli flowerets
1 med. zucchini, thinly sliced
1 1/2 -2 carrots, julienned
1 red bell pepper, cut 1/2" pieces
1/2 c. heavy cream
1/2 c. evaporated milk
1/2 c. chicken broth
2 tbsp. chopped fresh or 2 tsp. dried basil
4 oz. pkg. prosciutto, chopped
5 green onions, chopped
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 (16 oz.) pkg. penne (or your favorite pasta)
1/2 tsp. salt
1 c. freshly grated Parmesan cheese
2-4 oz. pineola (pine) nuts, toasted
Fresh basil sprigs (optional)

Directions:
Cook pasta al dente, according to package.  Drain and return to pot.
Meanwhile; in seperate pan, Melt butter and saute onion and garlic in butter in a large skillet until tender. Add asparagus, mushrooms, broccoli, zucchini, carrots, and peppers; stir-fry until crisp-tender.
Add cream, evap milk, broth, and chopped basil into vegetable mixture; bring to a boil, and cook 3 minutes. Stir in chopped prosciutto, green onions, and pepper; cook 1 minute.
Add vegetable mixture, pinola nuts and Parmesan cheese, toss to coat.  Garnish with  basil sprigs. Serve immediately.
If serving for a crowd, place in chafing rack and stir in small amounts of evap. milk as needed to keep sauce creamy.  Watch heat carefully. (1 receipe fills a 1/2 size buffet pan).

Submitter: 
BARBARALB
Description: 
I doubled this recipe and served it on Christmas on a chafing rack.  It was devoured and I got requests for the recipe.
