Title:
Monster Cookies - Half Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 eggs
1/4 c. margarine
1/2 c. sugar
1/2 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. peanut butter
1 t. soda
1/2 T. vanilla
2 1/4 c. oatmeal
1/4 c. M & M's
1/4 c. chocolate chips
1/4 c. nuts
Bake on greased cookie sheet at 350Â° for 8-10 minutes.  Makes 2 3/4  dozen cookies.
This is Mom's recipe.  It is a great way to use up Holiday M & M's.

Directions:
Cream together sugar, brown sugar, peanut butter, eggs, margarine, and baking soda.  Add 1/2 T. vanilla; mix well. Stir in remaining ingredients.  Bake at 350 degrees for 8-10 minutes.
