Title:
Classic Cherry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (12 ounce) jar cherry preserves
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients. Simmer for five minutes. Serve warm.
If using as a glaze, brush glaze on your favorite Cook's brand ham 30 minutes before meat is done. Ham should be cooked uncovered for final 30 minutes.

This cherry sauce courtesy of Cook's Ham could not be more simple to prepare. Cherry preserves are simmered with a blend of spices. This can be used as a glaze, or served on the side as a sauce.
