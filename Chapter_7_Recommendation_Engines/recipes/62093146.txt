Title:
Arroz con leche ice pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of raw rice.
1 cup of condensed, sugary milk.
1 cup of milk.
1 cup of white sugar.

Directions:
Put the rice and the sugar in hot water, boil for 15 or 20 minutes.
Put the rice (without any water) in a bowl and add the two cups of milk.
Blend slowly with a spoon and fill the ice pop moulds.
Freeze and enjoy them.

Submitter: 
Charlie cheesecake
Description: 
A traditional mexican dessert transformed into ice pops. Simply delicious. I wrote 2 hours in the total time but that depends on your fridge.
