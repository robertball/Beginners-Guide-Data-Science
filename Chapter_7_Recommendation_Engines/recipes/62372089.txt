Title:
Summer Carpaccio Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c sliced almonds (toasted)
1/2 lb Zucchini
1/2 lb Yellow Summer Squash
2.5 Tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1.5 Tablespoons Lemon Juice (fresh, if available)
1 clove of garlic
2 Handfuls of Spinach (or Arugula or any salad green)
Approx 3 oz Parm Cheese

Directions:
Toast the almonds if they are not already.
VERY thinly slice the zucchini and squash (I use my mandolin).
Mix together: Olive Oil, Lemon Juice, Garlic, and Salt to taste.
Pour the mixture over the squash, toss to coat - let rest at least 5 min.
Add the Spinach (or any greens).
Add the Parm (grated or freshly grate) and some black pepper, to taste.
