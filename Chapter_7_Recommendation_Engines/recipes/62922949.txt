Title:
ORBB Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2  cup (1 stick) butter, softened
1/3 cup applesauce
2/3  cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/3  cup granulated sugar
1 banana, mashed
2  eggs
1  teaspoon vanilla
1-1/2  cups all-purpose flour
1  teaspoon baking soda
1  teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
1/2  teaspoon salt (optional)
3  cups old-fashioned oats
1 1/2  cups raisins

Directions:
Heat oven to 350Â°F.
In large bowl, beat butter, sugars, applesauce and banana on medium speed of electric mixer until creamy. Add eggs and vanilla; beat well.
In a separate bowl combine dry ingredients and raisins and mix well.
Add to wet mixture and stir until just combined.
Drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until light golden brown. Cool 1 minute on cookie sheets; remove to wire rack. Cool completely. Store tightly covered.
