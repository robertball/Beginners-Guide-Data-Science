Title:
Coconut Ginger Rice Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg rice noodles
1 can coconut milk
1 tbsp ginger, minced
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 C red pepper, julienned
1/2 C carrots, julienned

Directions:
Soak rice noodles according to package directions.
Heat coconut milk over medium-low heat in large pot.  When it starts to simmer, add ginger and garlic. Cover and allow to simmer for 5 minutes.
When noodles are soft, add in coconut milk mixture.  Let simmer for additional 5 minutes. Add in red peppers and carrots, and serve.

Simple and easy noodle dish to serve as a side on your Asian dinner menu.
