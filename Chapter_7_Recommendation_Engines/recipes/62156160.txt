Title:
Quick and Easy Mediterranean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz. pre-packaged bag of lettuce (spinach mix preferred)
6-10 Pepperoncini, chopped or whole
1/2 block of feta cheese, crumbled
1 small red onion, sliced
1 package of cherry tomatoes
Salad Dressing
1 packet of Good Seasons Italian - All Natural mix
1/4 cup vinegar
3 Tb. of water
1/2 vegetable oil

Directions:
Make the dressing first to chill in fridge.
Combine the Good Seasons Mix with the vinegar and water. Shake well.
Add the oil and shake well again. Place in fridge to start chilling.
Mix the feta, pepperoncini, and red onion in a mixing bowl. Pour about 1/2 the dressing over these ingredients to marinade for 10-15 minutes.
Right before serving, toss the marinaded ingredients with the lettuce and tomatoes. Serve immediately and enjoy!
