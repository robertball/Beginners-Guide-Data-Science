Title:
Parmesan Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (4 ounce) boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
Salt & Pepper
Greek Seasoning
1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1 egg
1 tablespoon butter or margarine
1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Flatten chicken to 1/4-in. thickness. Sprinkle w/salt, pepper and Greek seasoning mix.  In a shallow bowl, combine bread crumbs, butter, Parmesan cheese and basil. In another bowl, beat the egg. Dip chicken into egg, then coat with crumb mixture. Grease a shallow baking dish and add coated chicken.  Bake @ 350 for 35 min.

Jean
