Title:
Fried  Chinese Dumplings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1072877.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (16 ounce) package dumpling wrappers
1 lb ground pork
2 cups Chinese cabbage
1/4 cup onion
minced shallot, ginger, dry red pepper, caraway, 1 teaspoon for each
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons vegetable oil
2 teaspoons sesame seed oil
3 tablespoons chicken soup base
1/2 teaspoon sugar

Directions:
Finely dice cabbage and onion.
Mix all the ingredients together.
Fill and fold wrappers. Pinch sides to ensure they won't break open during cooking.
Put enough oil to coat the bottom of the pan, lay dumplings in it until the bottom of
jiaozi turns golden yellow.
Add one tablespoon of water and cover the pan quickly to make them cooked by the steam.
Turn the stove to medium when they are cooked 2-3 minutes.
Time to Enjoy :-).
