Title:
Elise's Favorite Pumpkin Custard Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5948948.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Large Can Pumpkin (3 Cups)
2 Cans Evaporated Milk (3 Cups)
1 1/2 t Cinnamon
1 t Salt
6 Eggs
3 T Butter, melted
2 C Sugar
2 T Vanilla

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit
Combine all ingredients together with a hand mixer.
Pour into the pie crusts
Bake for 5 minutes at 425 degrees Fahrenheit
Turn oven down to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
Bake for 55 minutes or until pie is set.
