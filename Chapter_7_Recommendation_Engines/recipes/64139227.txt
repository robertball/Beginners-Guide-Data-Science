Title:
No Bake Choco-PB Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups white sugar
3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 cup margarine
1/2 cup milk
1 pinch salt
3 cups quick cooking oats
1/2 cup chunky peanut butter
2teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
In a saucepan bring sugar, cocoa, margarine, milk, and salt to a large bubble, rolling boil. Boil for 1 minute exactly.
Add quick cooking oats, peanut butter, and vanilla; mix well.
Working quickly, drop by teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper, and let cool.

Submitter: 
Sandra Holding
Description: 
Easy peanut butter cocoa flavored cookies without turning on the oven.Modified from: No Bake Cookies III
