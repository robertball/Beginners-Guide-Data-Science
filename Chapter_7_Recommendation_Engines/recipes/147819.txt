Title:
Branana Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 cup sugar
2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup All-Bran® Original or All-Bran® Bran Buds®
1/2 cup milk
1 cup thinly sliced, ripe bananas
1/4 cup shortening
1 egg

Directions:
Stir together flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Set aside.
In large mixing bowl, combine Kellogg's(R) All-Bran(R) cereal, milk and bananas. Let stand about 2 minutes or until cereal softens. Add egg and shortening. Beat well. Add flour mixture, stirring only until combined. Portion evenly into ten 2 1/2-inch muffin-pan cups coated with cooking spray.
Bake at 400 degrees F about 25 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Kellogg's® All-Bran®
Description: 
True to their name, these delightful muffins are packed with bananas and bran, making them a wholesome snack for kids and adults alike.
