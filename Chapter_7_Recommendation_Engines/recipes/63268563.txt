Title:
Tim's Chili Con Queso Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/857621.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (2 pound) loaf velveeta cheese food
2 (15 ounce) cans chili without beans Hormel
2 can 4oz Ortega chili
1 16 oz sour cream
1 jar 16 oz Salsa
1 pinch chili powder & Cayanne pepper
add Onion flakes, cracked pepper to your liking

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over medium low heat, melt the processed cheese food. Stir in chili without beans and chili powder. Serve warm when thoroughly blended.
Or put all items in a crock pot on low for 3.5 hours

Submitter: 
MsChristy
Description: 
Here's a simple version of a warm chili and cheese dip served in popular restaurants. Serve with warm tortilla chips.Modified from: Chili Con Queso Dip II
