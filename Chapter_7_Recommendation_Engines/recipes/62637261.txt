Title:
Jim's Special Cajun Scallops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/580490.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups Italian bread crumbs ( cracker crumbs can be substituted)
1 Cup finely grated Parmesan cheese
2 Tbs blackened seasoning ( such as Chef Paul Prudhommes blackened redfish magic)
2 pound fresh sea scallops
1 cup cream
1 1/2  sticks of butter for frying

Directions:
Mix the bread crumbs, parmesan, blackened seasoning in a one gallon zip lock bag.
Dip the scallops in a bowl of cream to coat them. Then put the scallops in the bag one at a time to coat them with the seasoning mixture.
Fry the scallops in the butter for 2 minutes on each side until golden brown.

Scallops coated with my own special seasoning and fried to a golden bown in butter.
