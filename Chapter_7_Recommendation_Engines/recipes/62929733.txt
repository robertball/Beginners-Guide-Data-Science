Title:
Wiener Schnitzel vom Schwein Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/716602.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 Cup Of Flour
2 Teaspoons To 1 Tablespoon of salt
1 tablespoon of black pepper
1 cup of bread crumbs
2 Eggs
4 tablespoons of milk

Directions:
Preheat deep fryer to 375F
In a bowl or 1 gallon plastic bag mix flour, salt and pepper
In a bowl add egg, and milk and scramble
in a bowl or 1 gallon plastic bag add bread crumbs
Take a pork chop or pork loin and place the meat in a 1 gallon plastic bag or cover with cling wrap, and pound it out with a meat pounder to 1/8 to 1/4 inch.
Flour Pork With flour Mixture, and cover with flour, Then Dip In Egg Mixture, then put in bread crumbs and cover with crumbs.
Take a plate or bowl and line with paper towels.
Put the coated pork in to the deep fryer, in a few mins it should float to the top, after a few mins flip over. once the Schnitzel  is golden brown then remove from the oil.
place the pork on your plate or in your bowl and allow oil to soak into the paper towels.
Optional: Serve with fries with cracked pepper gravy, pork Or beef gravy, Or just ketchup.

Submitter: 
Mark Farmer
Description: 
One of my favorite meals when I was in Germany and going to a German Guest House was Wiener Schnitzel (veal) Or Wiener Schnitzel vom Schwein (Pork Schnitzel), Add a German beer Serve with pommes frites with traditional cracked pepper gravy, beef or pork gravy or just ketchup.
