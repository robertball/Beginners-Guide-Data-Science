Title:
White Chip Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup baking cocoa
3/4 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1 and 1/4 cups granulated sugar
3/4 cup (1.5 sticks) butter or margarine, melted
2 tsp. vanilla extract
3 large eggs
2 cups (12-oz. pkg.) white chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F. Grease pan.
Combine flour, cocoa, baking powder, and salt in medium bowl. Beat sugar, butter, and vanilla extract in large mixer bowl. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Gradually beat in flour mixture. Stir in 1.5 cups morsels. Spread into prepared pan.
Bake for 25 to 30 minutes.
