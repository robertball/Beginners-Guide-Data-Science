Title:
Smoked Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-Chicken wings
-Frank’s Red Hot Wing Sauce
-Kickin Chicken poultry seasoning by Weber

Directions:
-Put thawed wings in large bowl and coat with Franks.  Sprinkle a liberal amount of kickin chicken on top. Stir well and then reapply kickin chicken.
-Place in large ziplock bag and let marinate in fridge for at least 3 hours.
-Using  your favorite wood, bring smoker up to 250 degrees.
-Put chicken in smoker and smoke for about 1 hour, rotating halfway through depending on type of smoker.
-Once wings hit an internal temp of 165, take off smoker and place on cookie sheet.
-Broil in oven for 1 minute on each side to get wings a lil crispy.
-Enjoy!!!!!!

Submitter:

Description: 
If you like buffalo wings, you’ll love the way they taste when they’re smoked instead of fried. Don’t take much time either.
