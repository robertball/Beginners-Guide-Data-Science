Title:
Cranberry Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C graham cracker crumbs
1/2C chopped macadamia nuts
1/4 C melted margarine
2T sugar
1 envelope unflavored gelatin
1/4 C cold water
2 8-oz pkgs cream cheese softened
1 7-oz jar marshmallow cream
1 16-oz can whole cranberry sauce
1C cool whip

Directions:
Combine crumbs, nuts, margarine & sugar. Press onto bottom of springform pan. Bake @ 350 degrees 10 minutes, cool. In small saucepan soften gelatin in water; stir over low heat until dissolved. Mix cream cheese & marshmallow cream @ med speed until well blended. Gradually add gelatin & cranberry sauce, mixing until well blended. Fold in whipped cream & pour over crust. Chill until firm.
