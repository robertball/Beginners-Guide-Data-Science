Title:
Cannellini Spring Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon oil, olive, extra-virgin
1 medium lemon, juiced (use 1/8 cup)
1 1/2 tablespoon tarragon, fresh, chopped
1/2 teaspoon mustard, Dijon
salt to taste
peppercorns, black, freshly ground, to taste
2 1/2 ounce(s) beans, green, halved (about 3/4 cups)
1/2 can - 19 ounce(s) beans, cannellini, drained and rinsed
3 ounce(s) tomato(es), cherry, halved (about 1/2 cup)

Directions:
In a small bowl, combine oil, juice, tarragon, mustard, salt, and pepper.
Bring an inch of water to boil in small saucepan over high heat. Cook green beans 1 minute, until blanched. Drain; rinse with cold water.
In medium serving bowl, combine white beans, green beans, and tomatoes. Add dressing; toss to coat.
