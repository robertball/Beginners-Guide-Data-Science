Title:
Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2  dark corn syrup
3/4 cup sugar
5 eggs, slightly beaten
Pinch of salt
3 Tsp vanilla
2 cups pecans

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
Place syrup, sugar and butter in a pan and bring to a boil.  Cool and add eggs, salt and vanilla.
Pour into unbaked pie crust and cook in a moderate oven until firm (approximately 50 - 60 minutes)
