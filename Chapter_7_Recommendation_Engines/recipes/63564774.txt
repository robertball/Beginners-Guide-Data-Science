Title:
Healthy Fattoush Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cucumber
4 C Shredded Romaine
1 Red or Orange Bell Pepper, chopped
1 C cherry tomatoes, halved
3 TB green onions, chopped
2 Whole Wheat Pitas
1/2 C Mint leaves, chopped
1/2 C Parsley leaves, chopped
2 TB Lemon Juice
1 garlic clove, crushed
1 tsp ground sumac (penzeys.com)
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
2 TB virgin olive oil

Directions:
Cut cucumber in half and scrape seeds.  Thin slices- add to bowl the romaine, bell pepper, tomatoes, and scallions
Tear toasted pita into bite size pieces and add to bowl
Next add mint and parsley and toss
In small bowl combine lemon juice, garlic, 1/2 tsp of sumac, salt and pepper and whisk and slowly add olive oil in stream.  Pour over veggies and toss.
Sprinkle with remaining Sumac before serving
