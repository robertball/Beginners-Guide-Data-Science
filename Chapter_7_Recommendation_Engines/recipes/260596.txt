Title:
Balsamic Avocado Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4650090.jpg
Ingredients:
1 slice French bread
2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
1/4 avocado, sliced
1 dash paprika, or to taste
salt and freshly cracked black pepper to taste

Directions:
Toast the French bread lightly, about 1 minute. Sprinkle vinegar on the toasted bread.
Layer avocado slices on the bread. Sprinkle paprika, salt, and pepper on top.
