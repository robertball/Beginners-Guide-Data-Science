Title:
Pizza Bagels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bagels, halved
1/3 cup pizza sauce
3/4 cup mozzarella cheese
Additional toppings

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. On a clean surface, split bagels  so that there are 4 bagel halves. Get a spoon and spread the sauce over the bagels. Spread mozzarella cheese over bagels. You can add more if desired. Add additional toppings on top, such as; olives, bell peppers, onions, or pepperonis. Place onto a cooking or baking sheet. Bake in oven for 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
thepurplebaker
Description: 
In a hurry and want pizza? Well pizza bagels are perfect for on the go! They are quick, filling, and are fun for kids to make.
