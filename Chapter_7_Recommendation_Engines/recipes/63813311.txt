Title:
Awesome Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
128 oz chicken broth (1 gallon or 16 cups)
3-4 lbs boneless chicken breast
green onion bunch, chopped
3 bay leaves
10 whole black peppercorns
1 pound carrots, peeled and sliced
1/3 bunch celery, chopped and leaves reserved
1 (8-14 oz) package dry egg noodles or reames

Directions:
Bring broth to a boil. Cut breasts into bite size pieces, add to broth and boil addt'l 30 min.  Place onion, bay leaves, peppercorns, chopped veggies in the pot and simmer, covered 1 hour. Chop celery leaves and stir into pot with the noodles.  Simmer until noodles are cooked, about 15-20 minutes more.
