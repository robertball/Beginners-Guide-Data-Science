Title:
One Step Chicken Vegetable Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14.5 oz can green beans
1 14.5 oz can peas and carrots
1 14.5 oz can whole kernal corn
1 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes and sweet onions
1 14 oz can chicken broth
1 10.75 oz can condensed chicken double noodle soup
1 small can mushrooms stem and pieces
1/8 tsp ground black pepper
1/8 teaspoon dill weed

Directions:
Combine all ingredients with their liquids in large pot and bring to boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 4 minutes.
Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Alta Nesbitt Rees Sieker

Can goods are combined for this quick and easy soup.
