Title:
Better Beurre Blanc Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 - Shallots, minced
1 t. - White Pepper, freshly cracked
1 c. - Dry White Wine
1/4 c. - Parsley, chopped
1/2 c. - Heavy (whipping) Cream
1 c. - Unsalted Butter (2 sticks), cut into pieces
Salt to taste
Juice from 1 Lemon

Directions:
In a small saucepan bring to boil shallots, pepper and wine over medium heat.
Simmer until reduced in volume by one half.
Add cream and reduce in half again.
Remove from heat, stir in butter one piece at a time (incorporate completely between pieces).
Season with salt and lemon juice.
Strain through fine mesh sieve.
Keep warm over tepid water.

Submitter:

Description: 
Nice sauce to put over asparagus, broccoli, cauliflower, etc.  I think you all will delight on the rich flavor of this recipe.
