Title:
White Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
1 T olive oil
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 14 oz cans chicken broth
1 4 oz can chopped green chilies
2 t cumin
2 t oregano
1 1/2 t cayenne pepper
3 14 oz cans great northern beans, drained, divided
1 cup shredded monterey jack cheese

Directions:
In a large pot, over medium heat, cook chicken and onion in oil until lightly browned.
Add garlic; cook 1 minute longer.
Stir in the broth, chilies, cumin, oregano and cayenne; bring to a boil.
Reduce heat to low.
With a potato masher, mash one can of beans until smooth.  Add to pan.
Simmer for 20-30 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink and onion is tender.
Top each serving with cheese and chopped jalepenos if desired.

Submitter: 
Karen Semmelrock
Description: 
Very good, spicy but not overwhelming!
