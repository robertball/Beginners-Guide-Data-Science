Title:
Tremendous Crock Pot Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 pounds lean ground beef
1 onion finely diced
2 cloves minced garlic
salt to taste
Italian seasoning to taste
1 29 oz. can tomato sauce
1 14.5 oz can petite dice tomatoes in sauce
1 6 oz can Italian Seasoned tomato paste
3 cups wide egg noodles
16 oz. cottage cheese
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
16 oz. shredded mozzarella cheese
1 tbs. Italian seasoning

Directions:
1.  In a large skillet over high heat, cook the ground beef, onion, garlic, salt and Italian seasoning until brown.  Drain well.  Add the tomato sauce, diced tomatoes and tomato paste and stir until well incorporated.  Cook until heated through.
2.  In a large bowl, combine cottage cheese, parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, and Italian seasoning.  If cottage cheese doesn't have much liquid, add enough milk to bring everything together.
3.  Spoon a layer of the meat mixture into the bottom of your crock pot.  Add one cup of egg noodles.  Top with a portion of the cheese mixture.  Repeat the layering of sauce, noodles and cheese until all the ingredients are used.  Make sure to end the layering with sauce.
4.  Cover and cook on low heat in your crock pot for 6-8 hours.
