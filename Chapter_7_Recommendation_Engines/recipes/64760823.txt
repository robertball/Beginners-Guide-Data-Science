Title:
Rhubarb Chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups sliced rhubarb
2 cups vinegar
2 cups sugar
2 cups brown sugar
2 cups raisins
2 cups finely chopped onions
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. ground ginger
1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
1 tbsp. mustard seed
1 cinnamon stick, broken up
1 tsp. whole cloves

Directions:
Mix first 9 ingredients in large heavy pot
Tie mustard seed, cinnamon stick and cloves in double layer of cheesecloth. Add to pot.
Heat on medium, stirring often as it comes to a boil and sugar dissolves.
Simmer, uncovered, for about 40 min. until thickened, stirring occasionally.
Discard spice bag.
Pour into hot sterile jars to within 1/4" of top. Seal.
Makes 3 pints

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A very versatile spread used hot or cold on meet, sandwiches, over cream cheese with crackers, etc.
