Title:
Sahara's Black Bean Salsa...aka..."Cowboy Caviar" FTW style  or...what I like! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5474447.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (15 oz) can Ranch Style Black Beans, drained
1 (10 oz) can Rotel Hot Diced Tomatoes with habaneros, drained
1 (15.25 oz) can whole kernal sweet corn, drained
* note I also add 1 small can of salsa verde (Herdez brand)
Salt and Pepper to taste
Sour cream and favorite chips to serve with

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together and chill at least 30 minutes before serving.
Serve with your favorite tortilla chips.
