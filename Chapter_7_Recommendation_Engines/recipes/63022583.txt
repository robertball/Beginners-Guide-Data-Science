Title:
Sesame Street Magazine Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/757184.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup Softened Butter or Margerine
1 cup Sugar
2 Eggs
1 teaspoon Vanilla
2 1/2 cups Flour
1 teaspoon Baking Powder
1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Mix butter and sugar
Add eggs and vanilla; mix well
Add flour, baking powder and salt; mix well
Chill for at least one hour
Roll to 1/4" thick and use your favorite cookie cutters
Place on an ungreased cookie sheet
Add sprinkles if desired
Place sheets in preheated to 400Â°F oven for 6 to 8 minutes
Cool on rack for 10 minutes.
Add icing or additional decorations if desired
Store in an air-tight container; add a piece of bread to keep them moist and chewy
