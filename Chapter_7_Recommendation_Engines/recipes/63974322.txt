Title:
Homemade Play-Dough Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup salt
2 cup water
1 Tbsp Alum
2 Tbsp oil
food coloring - generous amount

Directions:
Mix together flour and salt in a bowl, set aside.
Bring water, alum, oil, and food coloring to boil in large sauce pan.
Add flour/salt mixture.
Immediately remove from heat.
Stir with wood spoon (as dough will get difficult to stir) until no loose flour is visible.
Put on rubber kitchen gloves.
Place dough on heat-resistant surface (CAUTION:Dough is hot).
Knead dough until it is a solid uniform color.
