Title:
Strawberry Glacé Cheesecake Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg (11.2 oz.) JELL-O No Bake Cheesecake
1/2 c. boiling water
1 pkg. (3 oz.) JELL-O Strawberry Flavor Gelatin
1/4 c. frozen strawberries
1 can (11 oz.) mandarin oranges, drained

Directions:
Prepare cheesecake directed on package. Refrigerate until ready to use.
Add boiling water to gelatin mix; stir 2 min. until completely disolved. Refrigerate 15 min. or until slightly thickened.
Arrange fruit over cheesecake; top with gelatin.
Refrigerate 1 hour or until gelatin layer is firm.

Submitter: 
dfwenigma
Description: 
Fresh or frozen strawberries with No Bake Cheesecake dessert. This is a variation on the JELL-O Raspberry Glacé Cheesecake Pie recipe.
