Title:
Italian Summer Veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 md green bell peppers
1 md zucchini
1 md summer yellow squash
5 md cloves garlic, sliced
3T pignoli
olive oil
smoked salt
black pepper
dried savory
dried rosemary

Directions:
Cut the zucchini and squash in half longways.  Then slice them about 1/4" thick.
Cut the peppers into thin strips.
Combine the peppers, zucchini, squash, pignoli, and garlic in a frying pan.  Add enough olive oil to coat them and the pan but not enough to leave excess.
Place on medium heat.  Add all seasonings to taste.  Cook until tender.
