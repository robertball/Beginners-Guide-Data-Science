Title:
Easy Tex-Mex Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lb hamburger meat
2 packets taco seasoning
1 jar thick and chunky salsa
1 can fiesta corn
1 can cooked pinto beans
1 small can green chiles
1 small can chopped black olives
1 bag of shredded mexican cheese
1 bag white or yellow corn tortilla chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450.  Brown the hamburger and follow the directions on the taco seasoning packets.  While the meat is simmering, drain the pintos and corn.  In a medium bowl, mix pintos, corn, salsa, chiles, and black olives.  Place a thick layer of tortilla chips in the bottom of a 9x13 casserole.  When meat is finished, pour in salsa mixture, stir well, and spoon a layer over top of the chips.  sprinkle a 1/2c of cheese over that.  Spoon rest of salsa/meat mixuture over that, topped again by 1/2c cheese.  Cover with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes.  Serve with a fresh sprinkle of cheese and tortilla chips.

Submitter: 
JESLYNN
Description: 
This is something ultra simple and very tasty.  Both my picky 7 yr old and my even pickier Texan fiance ask for seconds!  You can also omit the tortilla chips, bake as directed and serve with warm tortillas and toppings for a hearty burrito dinner!
