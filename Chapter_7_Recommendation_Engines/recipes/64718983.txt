Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookies (Joanna Gaines) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 stick butter
2 cups packed light brown sugar
2 large eggs
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
1 heaping tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
2 1/2 cups flour
1 1/2 cups chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy.  Add eggs and vanilla, blend well. Add baking soda, salt and flour, mix well. Add chips and nuts.
Bake for 10 to 11 minutes, do not over bake. Cool in pan for 1 minute. Place on rack to cool completely.
