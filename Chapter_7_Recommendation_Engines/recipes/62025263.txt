Title:
Banana Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 C. mashed bananas
2 tbsp. lemon juice
6 1/2 C sugar
1 bottle liquid fruit pectin
1/3 C. finely chopped maraschino cherries

Directions:
Combine bananas,lemon juice, and sugar in a large, heavy saucepan. Mix well. Bring to a hard boil over high heat and boil hard for 1 minute, stirring constantly. Remove from heat and add fruit pectin. Pour into hot jars and seal at once with snap lids or paraffin.

Submitter: 
Bobby Darapureddi
Description: 
excellent on taost, bagels or english muffins.
