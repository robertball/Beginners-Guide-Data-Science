Title:
Mom's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb ground beef
1 lb jimmy dean sausage
1 pkg French onion soup mix
1 egg
1/2 cup grapenuts cereal
1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup ketchup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix all ingredients together.  Form into a loaf and place in lg loaf pan.  Cover with foil and bake for 1 hour.  Take off foil and bake for additional 1/2 hour.

Submitter: 
rev'd up chef
Description: 
It's the secret ingredient that makes this the best meat loaf ever... Grapenuts!  I know it sounds weird, but trust me it's amazing.  Mom discovered the secret ingredient one day when she discovered she was out of bread crumbs.
