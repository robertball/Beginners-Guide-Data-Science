Title:
A substantial and delicious salad dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/338219.jpg
Ingredients:
Juice of 1 lemon
2 tablespoons of white wine vinegar
4 tablespoons of olive oil
1 teaspoon of dijon mustard
6 tablespoons of mayonnaise
1 clove of garlic, minced
6 tablespoons of water
1 teaspoon of black pepper and the same with salt
1 teaspoon of oregano

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl.
Use a whisk and be sure to mix well.
Taste for seasoning.
Drape over whatever you desire.

Submitter: 
Tatiana
Description: 
It's thick, creamy and hearty. It works with any salad. It can also be adapted to be  a dip.
