Title:
Papa's Buttermilk Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups buttermilk
3 eggs
2 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
2/3 stick of butter
Syrup of your choice (if desired)
Butter (if desired)
Powdered Sugar (if desired)

Directions:
In large bowl combine buttermilk, eggs, flour, baking powder and baking soda until mixed thru.
Melt 2/3 stick of butter in microwave.
Pour butter into batter, mix.
Preheat waffle maker.
Place 1/3 cup into waffle maker (more if it will hold it), cook 1-3 minutes to your liking.
Serve with syrup, powdered sugar and butter if you desire.
Makes 12-15 waffles depending on size.
