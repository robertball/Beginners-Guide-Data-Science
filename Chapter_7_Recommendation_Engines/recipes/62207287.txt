Title:
Grandmas best brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C. cocoa
2 C. white sugar
2/3 C. veg oil
4. eggs
1 1/2 C. flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt

Directions:
Pre-heat over to 350
mix together sugar, oil, and eggs. In seperate bowl combine cocoa, flour, bakeing powder, and salt.
gradually add your flour mixture to your wet sugar mixture.
grease an "13x9-1/2" pan. cook for 30 to 35 min.

Submitter: 
cookingmommy
Description: 
This is a Delicious chocolaty brownie, and one of the simplest. Great for brownie sundaes! mmmmmmmm!
