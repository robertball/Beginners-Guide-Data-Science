Title:
Twinkie Tiramisu, Trailer Trash Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/328209.jpg
Ingredients:
2 packages of Twinkies, cut in half lengthwise and cut in half again
6 tablespoons coffee liqueur
1 8-ounce tub marscapone cheese
1 Tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 egg yolk

Directions:
Cut the Twinkies in half lengthwise and then in half horizontally, making 16 pieces total. Layer 2 pieces in the bottom of a coffee cup and 4 pieces around the sides. Douse with 2 tablespoons coffee liqueur. Cream together marscapone, vanilla, sugar and egg yolk together until creamy. Scoop half of the mixture into the middle of the Twinkies in the coffee cup. Top with 2 more pieces of Twinkie and 1 tablespoon coffee liqueur. Add a dollop of marscapone mixture on top. Repeat process in the second coffee cup with remaining Twinkie pieces and marscapone mixture. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
