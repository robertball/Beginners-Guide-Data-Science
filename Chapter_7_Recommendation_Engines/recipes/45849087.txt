Title:
Wiener Schnitzel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds veal
1 cup seasoned all-purpose flour
4 eggs
2 tablespoon olive oil
salt and pepper to taste
4 cups bread crumbs
1/8 cup olive oil for frying
2 cloves garlic diced
1/2 lb mushrooms sliced
1/2 cup dry vermouth
1/4 cup cream
2 pats butter

Directions:
Cut the veal into steaks, about as thick as your finger. Dredge in flour. In a shallow dish, beat the eggs with 1 tablespoon oil, salt and pepper. Coat the veal with egg mixture, then with bread crumbs.
Heat 1/4 cup oil in a heavy skillet over medium heat. Fry veal until golden brown, about 5 minutes on each side.
Keep steaks warm and add garlic,mushrooms and butter to pan. Saute until mushrooms have released their liquids,add vermouth and simmer for about 5 min.Stir in cream untill smooth.
