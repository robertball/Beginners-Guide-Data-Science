Title:
Northern Comfort Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/353322.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers apple schnapps
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger bourbon whiskey
ice

Directions:
Combine apple schnapps and bourbon in a shaker with ice. Shake, and pour into a glass.

Submitter: 
JIBERN
Description: 
A tasty but strong drink. Southern comfort is a peach bourbon. Peaches are a southern fruit, apples are a northern fruit.
