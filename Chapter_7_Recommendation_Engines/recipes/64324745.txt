Title:
Creme Filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 1/2 cups marshmallow cream
2 cups powdered sugar
1 tablespoon vanilla

Directions:
Cream the butter with the marshmallow cream, then add the powdered sugar and vanilla, beat on low for about a minute. If it’s too thick, add a little water. You want it to be spreadable but not runny.

Submitter: 
Cori
Description: 
I was searching for a crème filling without shortening, and this is my first attempt at that!
