Title:
Turkey in a Bag Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 pound whole turkey
bone dust
3 stalks celery
1 large onions, quartered

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Rinse turkey and remove giblets. Sprinkle liberally with Bone Dust.
Place neck, celery and onion in the turkey.
Place the turkey in the bag and seal.
Cut off (small) upper corner.
Bake 3 to 3 1/2 hours, or until internal temperature of the thigh meat reaches 180 degrees F (85 degrees C).
When bird is done, pour the juice out a hole in the bag into a large measuring cup. Refrigerate until the fat rises to the top and gets firm. Remove the fat and freeze the broth in 1 cup containers for future use.
Can let turkey rest in the bag 20-30 minutes.

Submitter: 
TJ
Description: 
This is a very easy way to make a Thanksgiving turkey using an oven bag. The bird will be perfectly moist when done. Plus, cleanup is a snap! The cooking time will vary for different sized turkeys.Modified from: Turkey in a Bag
