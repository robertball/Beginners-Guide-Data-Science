Title:
Heather and Kristen's PB and Chocolate Extravaganza Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4457731.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (12 fluid ounce) can cola-flavored carbonated beverage (such as Coca-Cola®)
3 scoops vanilla ice cream
3 ice cubes, or as desired
3 tablespoons peanut butter
3 tablespoons chocolate syrup
2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
Blend cola, vanilla ice cream, ice cubes, peanut butter, chocolate syrup, and vanilla extract together in a blender until smooth.

Submitter: 
Heather Fryk
Description: 
Try this smoothie made with peanut butter, vanilla ice cream, cola, and chocolate syrup for a refreshing sweet treat. You can use Pepsi® instead of Coca-Cola®, if desired.
