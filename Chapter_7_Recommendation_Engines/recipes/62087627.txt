Title:
Quick & Easy Chicken Divan Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1lb. bag frozen chopped broccoli
3 chicken breasts, cooked and cut in bite sized pieces.
2 cans condensed cream of chicken soup
1 cup mayonnaise
1 teaspoon lemon juice
2 tablespoons cooking sherry
1 cup bread crumbs
1 cup grated cheddar cheese
3 tablespoons melted butter
4-6 cups cooked white rice

Directions:
Place frozen broccoli in a microwave safe bowl, heat for 4 minutes or until hot.
Set broccoli aside.
Cut chicken breasts into bite sized pieces and cook in a skillet until done.
Place cooked broccoli evenly in a 9x13 casserole dish.
Cover the broccoli evenly with the cooked chicken.
In a mixing bowl, combine the soup, mayonnaise, lemon juice and cooking sherry; pour over the chicken.
Place in 350 degree oven for 30 minutes.
In a small bowl, mix the bread crumbs, grated cheddar cheese and melted butter together.
Sprinkle bread crumb mixture evenly over the casserole.
Return to oven for 10 minutes to brown topping.
Serve chicken divan over white rice.
