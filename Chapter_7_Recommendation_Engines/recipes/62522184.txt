Title:
LIGHT AND DREAMY 5 CUP FRUIT SALAD Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup pineapple tidbits
1 cup mandarin orange slices
1 cup mini marshmallows
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup sour cream

Directions:
Drain pineapple and mandaring orange slices.
Place all ingredients in a large bowl and mix well.
Serve chilled.

Submitter: 
Missie Wallace Petersen
Description: 
A light and creamy fruit salad that is also great served as a light dessert.  I can never make enough for our large family get togethers.
