Title:
Nanny129 STAR's Vegetarian Meatballs Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
3 cups (2 pkg) Ritz crackers, crushed
1 1/2 c ground nuts
1 tsp salt
1 tsp Accent
1 lg onion, chopped
1 tsp sage
2 tsp poultry seasoning
1 1 /2 cup grated yellow cheese
6 T minced parsley
8 eggs
Sauce of choice

Directions:
Mix all ingredients. Form into balls. Freeze until ready to use. Take out of freezer place in casserole and cover with sauce of choice. Bake at 350 for Â¾ - 1 hour. SAUCES- Mushroom gravy; spaghetti sauce;, barbecue sauce thinned with water; apricot jam, chili sauce, and pineapple juice; grape jelly and chili sauce.
