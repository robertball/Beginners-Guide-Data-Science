Title:
McCormick's Delicious Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/982518.jpg
Ingredients:
6 hard-cooked eggs, peeled
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/2 teaspoon McCormick® Parsley Flakes
1/2 teaspoon McCormick® Ground Mustard
1/4 teaspoon McCormick® Season-All® Seasoned Salt
McCormick® Paprika

Directions:
Carefully slice eggs in half lengthwise. Remove yolks and place in a small bowl. Mash yolks with a fork; add mayonnaise, parsley, mustard and Season-All(R), mixing until smooth and creamy.
Spoon yolk mixture into egg halves. Sprinkle with paprika.

Submitter: 
McCormick® & Company
Description: 
Spice up any summer barbeque or pot luck supper with these classic deviled eggs.
