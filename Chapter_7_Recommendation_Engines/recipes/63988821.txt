Title:
Amy Jo's chocolate chip cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3378953.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb butter (4 cubes) softened
2 c. brown sugar
1 ½ c. sugar
3 eggs
3 Tbsp. Vanilla
2 Tbsp. Baking soda
1 ½ tsp. Salt
2 Tbsp. Baking powder
6 to 6 ½ c. flour (red winter wheat)
6 c. chocolate chips
2-4 c. nuts (optional)

Directions:
Cream together butter and sugars. Then add eggs and vanilla. Sift together dry ingredients then add to mix. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts, if desired. Use ice cream size scoop to put on greased cookie sheet. Flatten slightly. Bake at 375 for 10 min. or until barely brown. This makes 3-4 dozen huge cookies.

Submitter:

Description: 
Tasty, warm and gooey center with crisp outer shell, leaving you begging for more.
