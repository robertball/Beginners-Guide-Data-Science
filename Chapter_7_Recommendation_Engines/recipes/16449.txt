Title:
Rosemary Pork Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/779598.jpg
Ingredients:
3 pounds pork tenderloin
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
3 tablespoons dried rosemary

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
Rub the roast OR tenderloin liberally with olive oil, then spread the garlic over it. Place it in a 10x15 inch roasting pan and sprinkle with the rosemary.
Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 2 hours, or until the internal temperature of the pork reaches 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).

Submitter: 
Stacy B.
Description: 
What tastes better than rosemary with pork? Add some green beans with toasted almonds, roasted potatoes, and a wild greens salad with balsamic vinaigrette, and your meal is made.
