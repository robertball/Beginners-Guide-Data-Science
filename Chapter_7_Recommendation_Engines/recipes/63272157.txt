Title:
Easy Peach Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 fresh peaches, pitted and sliced
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 (18.25 ounce) package white or yellow cake mix
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1/2 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Spread peach slices evenly into a 9x13 inch baking dish. If using canned peach juice, pour 1 cup of it over peach slices.
Mix cake mix with cold cream cheese and mix until crumbly
Sprinkle cinnamon over peach slices, followed by dry cake mixed with cream cheese. Pour melted butter over all
Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes, until golden.

Submitter: 
Cabinluvn
Description: 
Sweetened peaches are piled into a baking pan, sprinkled with cinnamon, and topped with yellow cake mix. Cubes of cream cheese and butter are then added, and the cobbler is baked until golden and bubbly.Modified from: Easy Peach Cobbler with Cake Mix
