Title:
Banana Almond Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/972846.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup whole wheat (or all purpose) flour
1/4 cup ground flax
1/4 cup ground almonds
1 tablespoon sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1 large egg
1 cup almond milk
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 medium bananas, mashed
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 tablespoon butter or cooking spray

Directions:
1. Combine dry ingredients in a medium sized bowl - flour, flax, ground almonds, sugar, salt, baking powder, baking soda.
2. Combine wet ingredients in a separate bowl - egg, bananas, almond milk, vegetable oil.
3. Mix banana mixture with flour mixture.
4. Scoop 1/3 cup of batter per pancake onto a lightly oiled griddle set to medium heat until lightly browned.
5. Serve with vanilla yogurt and fresh fruit or frozen berries.
