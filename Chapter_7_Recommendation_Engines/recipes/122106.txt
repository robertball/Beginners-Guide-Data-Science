Title:
Spinach Rice Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976075.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Italian salad dressing
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1/2 teaspoon sugar
2 cups cooked rice
1 celery rib, thinly sliced
4 green onions, thinly sliced
2 cups thinly sliced fresh spinach leaves
5 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the salad dressing, soy sauce and sugar. Stir in rice, celery and onions. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Just before serving, stir in spinach and bacon.

Submitter: 
Eleanor  Harris
Description: 
Here's a salad that goes well with many menus. It has a hint of Oriental flavor, with spinach and green onions for color and bacon for a bit of crunch.
