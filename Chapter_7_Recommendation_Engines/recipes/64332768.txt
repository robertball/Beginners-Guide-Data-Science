Title:
Salmon Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz Canned Salmon, drained
2 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Chives
1 Tbsp Lemon juice
3 Tbsp Whole-wheat Flour
Pepper
4 tsp Olive Oil
3 cups Mixed Greens
2 Tbsp Balsamic Vinegar

Directions:
Mix salmon, chives, lemon juice and flour together. Season with pepper. Form mixture into 3 patties. In a skillet over medium heat, cook patties in 3 tsp. olive oil until golden, about 4 minutes per side. Serve atop greens tossed with vinegar and remaining 1 tsp. olive oil.

Submitter: 
fordmarie
Description: 
I've made this, about five times now, and every time they turn out wonderfully and the flavors are so awesome! Of course switch up the herbs to whatever you like!
