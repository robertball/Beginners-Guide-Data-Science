Title:
Chicken Avocado Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12" pizza crust
1/2 cup pizza sauce
1/4 tsp chipotle favored hot sauce
1 cup shredded rotisserie chicken
1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
olive oil
1 ripe avocado, halved, pitted, peeled and sliced
1 small can sliced olives

Directions:
Mix the pizza sauce and hot sauce together in small bowl, set aside.
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Lightly coat pizza crust with olive oil. Spread pizza with sause; top with chicken & cheese.
Reduce het to 425 degrees. Bake on oven rack or cookie sheet (in middle position) for 7 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly.
Top the pizza with avocado and olives after baking.
