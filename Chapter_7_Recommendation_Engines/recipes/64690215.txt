Title:
Hula's Fresas Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 ounces cream cheese
1 can condensed milk
2 tsp cinnamon
3 cups Mexican cream
1/4 cup milk

Directions:
Cut cream cheese into smallish bits
Put ALL the ingredients in a blender and mix till smooth and creamy
Will fill 2 of the quart bottles

Submitter: 
Virginia Keeley
Description: 
Stuff that goes great with fresh strawberries
