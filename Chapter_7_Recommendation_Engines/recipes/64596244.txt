Title:
Vegan Thai Yellow Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3870442.jpg
Ingredients:
1-2 tablespoon water for sauteing
half a yellow onion, large chop
1 package extra firm tofu
? cup yellow curry paste
20 oz. gold potato cut into bite size pieces
1 cup oval sliced carrots (or other veggie)
1 14-ounce can coconut milk
½ - 1 cup water
2 teaspoons low sodium soy sauce
1-2 tablespoons coconut sugar
cilantro for serving
4-6 cups cooked Jasmine rice depending on how much rice you like
Parchment paper

Directions:
Preheat oven to  425 degrees
Press tofu for 15 minutes then cut into bite size cubes.
Cut Veggies.
place tofu on baking sheet with parchment paper and bake for 30 minutes flipping once.
Heat the 2 tablespoons of water in a large pot or wok over medium low heat. Add the onions and saute for a few minutes until the onions are fragrant and softened. Add the potatoes and curry paste; saute for 2 minutes.
Add the coconut milk and ½ cup water to the pot - simmer for 20-30 minutes or until the potatoes are fully cooked, adding more water depending on the consistency you want for the sauce.
Toss in the baked tofu.
Stir in the soy sauce and coconut sugar.
Serve over rice.
