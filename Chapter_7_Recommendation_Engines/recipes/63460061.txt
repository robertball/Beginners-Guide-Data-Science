Title:
White Trash Candy Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lb white chocolate chips
3 1/2 cups Cheerios Cereal
3 cups Rice Chex Cereal
3 cups Corn Chex Cereal
1 lb Plain M&M;'s Candy
2 1/2 cups Mixed Nuts
2 cups pretzel sticks

Directions:
Mix all ingredients EXCEPT white chocolate chips in large mixing bowl.
In a medium glass bowl, melt white chocolate chips in microwave, or melt in double boiler.
Pour melted white chocolate over cereal mixture, stir to thoroughly coat.
Spread on clean cookie sheet, cool completely, and break apart into bite-size pieces.
