Title:
Cinnamon Nutella No Bake Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1786711.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
2 cups sugar
1/2 cup milk or soy milk
1/2 cup Nutella
1 2/3 cup (10 oz. bag)cinnamon chips
3 cups Old Fashioned Oats

Directions:
In medium saucepan, bring butter, sugar and milk to boil over medium heat.  Let boil for 2 minutes, remove from heat.
Immediately add Nutella and cinnamon chips. Stir until melted into mixture, add oats.
Drop onto parchment paper lined cookie sheets, forming walnut size balls.

Easy no bake cookies with cinnamon and Nutella flavors for an elegant tasting cookie!
