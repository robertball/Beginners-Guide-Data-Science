Title:
Couldn't Be Gluten Free Banana Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 extremely ripe bananas
2 eggs, separated
1 3/4 c all purpose gluten free flour
1 1/2 c coconut sugar
1/2 c coconut oil, melted
1/4 c rice milk (or any kind)
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
Grease loaf pan.
In a small bowl, beat egg whites until very foamy. Set aside.
In a large bowl, beat bananas with electric mixer. Add oil, egg yolks, milk, sugar, vanilla and beat until mixed. Add all but egg whites and beat until mixed.
Fold in egg whites.
Pour into prepared loaf pan.
Bake 1 hour and 20 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
Let sit in pan for 10 minutes, then run knife around pan edges and set on a cooling rack until cooled.
