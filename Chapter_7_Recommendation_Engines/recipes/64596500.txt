Title:
Grilled Chicken with Heirloom Tomato & Avocado Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3904151.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (6-ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breast
1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon garlic powder
3/4 teaspoon ground cumin
3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
3/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon chile powder
Avocado Salsa:
1/2 small red onion, diced
1 heirloom tomato, seeded & diced
1 peeled ripe avocado, diced
Lime juice to taste
Salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat grill to high heat.
Lightly sprinkle chicken with onion powder, cumin, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and chipotle; rub evenly over chicken. Drizzle top with olive oil. Arrange chicken  on grill rack; grill 8 minutes on one side turn and grill another 4 minutes or until an internal thermometer reaches 165.  Remove from grill, drizzle with a little olive oil; let stand 10 minutes.
Meanwhile, mix onion, tomatoes and avocados. Season with salt and pepper.
Slice chicken and top with avocado/tomato mixture. Enjoy.

Submitter: 
tcasa
Description: 
A lite and refreshing summer dish to use up some of those garden tomatoes. Quick and tasty dish. I used heirloom because that is what we grow but any type of tomato could be used.
