Title:
Classic Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978820.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Karo® Light Corn Syrup*
3 eggs
1 cup sugar
2 tablespoons butter, melted
1 teaspoon Spice Islands® Pure Vanilla Extract
1 1/2 cups pecans
1 (9 inch) unbaked deep dish pie crust

Directions:
Mix corn syrup, eggs, sugar, butter and vanilla using a spoon.  Stir in pecans.
Pour into pie crust.
Bake at 350 degrees F on center rack of oven for 60 to 70 minutes.
Cool for 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
ARGO®,  KARO®  and FLEISCHMANN'S®
Description: 
This classic pie combines eggs, corn syrup, vanilla, lots of pecans, and is baked to a burnished golden brown.
