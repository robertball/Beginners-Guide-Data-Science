Title:
Chicken and Mushroom Dijon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 1/2 cups broccoli florets
1 1/2 cups sliced mushrooms
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Cream of Chicken & Broccoli Soup
1/4 cup milk
1 tablespoons Dijon-style mustard
4 cups hot cooked medium egg noodles or rice
Customize by:
Cook chicken with Garlic
Add white wine and parsley to sauce
Add 1 c. cheddar cheese to sauce.

Directions:
Heat 1 tablespoon butter in skillet. Add chicken and cook until browned. Remove chicken.
Heat remaining butter. Add broccoli and mushrooms and cook until tender.
Add soup, milk and mustard. Heat to a boil. Add chicken. Cover and cook over low heat 5 minutes or until done. Serve with noodles.

Rebekah Richards
