Title:
Very Berry Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen strawberries, partially thawed, *
1/2 cup frozen raspberries, partially thawed
1 cup 2 % milk
1/2 cup ice
1/4 cup sugar

Directions:
In a blender, whirl strawberries, raspberries, milk, ice and sugar until smooth.
* BLUEBERRY SMOOTHIE:  Substitute 1 1/2 cups frozen blueberries for strawberries and raspberries; add 1/4 tsp. vanilla.
PEACHY SMOOTHIE:  Substitute 1 1/2 cups canned and drained, or frozen peaches for strawberries and raspberries.
