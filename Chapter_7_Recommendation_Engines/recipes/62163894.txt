Title:
The Perfect Peanut Butter Cookie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 cup peanut butter
2 cups whole wheat flour
1 tsp. baking soda

Directions:
Cream butter and brown sugar.
Add egg and beat until fluffy (about 5 minutes).
Mix in peanut butter and beat to combine.
Add flour and baking soda and mix until just combined.  Do not overbeat.
Drop by spoonfuls on baking sheet.
Cook at 350?for 12 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
Our own recipe, these cookies simply melt in your mouth (and they're healthy too).  Every kid (and kid at heart) will love them!!
