Title:
Simple Roasted Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless Center-Cut Pork Chops
2-3 Tablespoons of dried thyme, slightly crushed by hand
Salt and Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Sprinkle chops on all sides with salt, pepper, and thyme.
Cook in preheated oven for 20 minutes or until meat thermometer reaches an internal temperature of 165.
Let rest for 5-10 minutes under foil tent.
Serve!

Submitter: 
Rhonda Elaine
Description: 
Don't let the simplicity of this recipe fool you.  Served with a salad and a fast side dish, it's perfect for a busy weeknight and my children love it.
