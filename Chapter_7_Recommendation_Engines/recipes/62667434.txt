Title:
Amy's Slow Cooker Ravioli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (16 oz) bag of frozen ravioli (any flavor)
1 tsp italian spices
1 (14 oz) can diced tomatoes
1 (24 oz) jar of spaghetti sauce
cup mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a bowl mix together the diced tomatoes, spaghetti sauce and the italian spices.
In a slow cooker place 1 cup of the spaghetti sauce mixture.  Layer on top of the mixture half of the ravioli and cheeses and half of the remaining sauce.
Layer on remainig ravioli and sauce and top with cheese.
Cook on LOW setting for 5 hours.- do not stir.
