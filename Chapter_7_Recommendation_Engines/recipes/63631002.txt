Title:
Carrots and Celery side dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 cups chicken broth
12 oz celery chopped
21 oz carrots chopped to medium thickness

Directions:
bring chicken broth to a boil. Add carrots and celery and cook 10-12 minutes.  Scoop veggies out of broth and serve.
