Title:
Easy and Fast Minestrone soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 28oz can pureed canned tomatoes
2 cup  of tomato juice
4 cups of chicken broth (or hot water mixed with bullion powder)
1 19oz can of mixed beans
2 cups frozen corn
2 cups of thick salsa (heat depending on your taste)
2 tsps of minced dried onion
1 tbsp of Italian seasoning mix
1 cup of mini pasta shells

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large pot, bring to a boil, and immediately reduce heat to very low cover partially. Check to see that the liquid is moving. If not increase heat a little bit. cook for 1/2 hour. Add pasta, and let simmer for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Tina Kauffman
Description: 
Does not require an long cooking time, and uses ingredients you  might have in your cupboard and freezer. Has that rustic old time taste. I add no extra salt, since a lot of the ingredients have salted added.
