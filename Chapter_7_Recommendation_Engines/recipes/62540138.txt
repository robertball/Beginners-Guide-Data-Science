Title:
Liana's Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup water
6-8 ozs shredded/ processed cheese
3 slices of bacon, chopped
1 can mushrooms, drained
3 packs of green beans, cut
french-fried onions (1 medium onion cut into thin slices or rings, 1-2 beaten eggs, flour, vegetable oil)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Boil green beans in water till well-cooked but still a bit crunchy.
Heat the mushroom soup & water in saucepan over medium heat. Stir in the cheese & bacon until cheese is completely melted.
Lower heat then stir in mushrooms & cooked green beans till evenly coated.
Pour mixture into a casserole dish/ baking pan & top with french-fried onions.
Bake for 20-25 minutes. Check to make sure that the onions aren't getting too brown.
(To make French fried onions:
Dip the onions in the egg mixture.
Drain the excess egg mixture from the onion slices.
Coat the onion slices with flour.
Fry onion slices in oil on high heat. Make sure the onion slices don't stick together.
Remove from pan when golden brown & wipe off excess oil with a paper towel.)

Submitter: 
Annie Douglass Lima
Description: 
This tasty casserole is a perfect side dish for Thanksgiving dinner!
