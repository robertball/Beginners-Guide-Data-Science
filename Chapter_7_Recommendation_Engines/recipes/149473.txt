Title:
Kellogg's速 Rice Krispies Treats速 Wreaths Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
3 tablespoons margarine or butter
1 (10 ounce) package regular marshmallows
1 teaspoon green food coloring
6 cups Kellogg's速 Rice Krispies速 cereal
Red cinnamon candies
Prepared frosting

Directions:
Melt margarine in large saucepan over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat. Stir in food coloring.
Add Kellogg's(R) Rice Krispies(R) cereal. Sir until well coated.
Using 1/4 cup measure coated with cooking spray, evenly portion warm cereal mixture. Using buttered fingers, quickly shape into individual wreaths. Dot with cinnamon candies, using frosting to hold candies in place. Best if served same day.
