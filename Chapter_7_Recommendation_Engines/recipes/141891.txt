Title:
Hot and Spicy BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/422936.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (46 fluid ounce) bottle ketchup
2 cups apple cider vinegar
1 cup SPLENDAÂ® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1/2 cup butter
1 tablespoon red pepper flakes
1/4 cup Texas style hot sauce

Directions:
In a large saucepan or soup pot, stir together the ketchup, cider vinegar, SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener, butter, red pepper flakes and hot sauce. Cook over medium heat until the butter is melted and the sauce is heated through.

Submitter:

Description: 
This delicious sauce can be used right away, or stored in the refrigerator for up to a month.
