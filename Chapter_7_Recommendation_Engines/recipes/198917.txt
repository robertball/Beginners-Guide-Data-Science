Title:
Japanese Shrimp Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3229681.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups mayonnaise
1/2 cup water
1 teaspoon white sugar
1 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon garlic juice
1 tablespoon ketchup
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
1 teaspoon ground mustard
1/4 teaspoon salt
3/4 teaspoon ground white pepper

Directions:
Whisk mayonnaise, water, sugar, paprika, garlic juice, ketchup, ground ginger, hot pepper sauce, ground mustard, salt, and white pepper together in a bowl.

Submitter: 
Nicole Marie
Description: 
Shrimp sauce like that found at a certain teppanyaki restaurant.
