Title:
After Church Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1924627.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. lean beef cut in 1 1/2 inch cubes
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. dried basil
1/4 tsp. pepper
2 stalks celery cut in diagonal slices
4 carrots, cut in halves lengthwise & crosswise
2 onions cut in 1/2 inch slices
1 10 1/4 oz. can tomato soup
1/2 soup can of water
3 potatoes pealed & cubed

Directions:
Bake 3 hours at 300 degrees
Place beef in 3 qt. casserole
Sprinkle with salt, basil & pepper
Top with celery, carrots & onions
Pour soup & water over meat & vegetables
Cover & bake 3 hours
Add potatoes & bake 45 minutes longer
