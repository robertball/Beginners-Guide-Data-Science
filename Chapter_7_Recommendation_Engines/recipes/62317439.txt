Title:
Shrimp Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Shrimp Mixture:1 lbs raw shelled, devined shrimp, minced well or ground, 2 heaping tsp mashed ginger, 1 clove garlic mashed, 1 tsp salt, few grains pepper, 1/2 tsp Accent. Mix well.
Batter:4 egg whites, 1 tbsp cornstarch.
Meat Mixture:2 tbsp choped ham(optional), 2 tbsp ground pork, 1 tbsp wine, 2 tbsp onion choped fine
Bread:1 loaf sandwich bread(Day old best)

Directions:
1)Beat egg white and cornstarch and fold into shrimp mixture
2)Fold meat mixture into shrimp mixture and egg whites
3)Cut crust off bread and cut slices into any desired shape. Shape small amount of shrimp mixture on bread slices
Heat oil for deep frying to 380. Drop pieces into oil shrimp side down. Fry until golden brown(6-8 minn);turn, quickly brown bottom and remove. Seve hot.
Note:mounded bread slices may be frozen BEFORE frying. Remove from freezer and let thaw 1 hour before frying.
