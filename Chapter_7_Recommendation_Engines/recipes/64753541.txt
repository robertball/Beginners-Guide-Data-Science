Title:
KC’s Egg Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6852457.jpg
Ingredients:
-Frozen hash browns
-8 eggs
-1 bag of shredded sharp cheddar
-1 bell pepper
-1 lb. breakfast sausage
-Oil

Directions:
-Heat oven to 375
-Dice bell pepper
-Brown breakfast sausage and drain
-Whip the eggs
-Oil bottom and sides of rectangle dish
-Line with hash browns
-Spread half the bag of cheese
-Spread crumbled sausage
-Pour the egg mixture
-Spread remaining cheese
-Spread diced pepper
-Cover with foil and cut slits through it
-Place in oven and bake for 45 min
