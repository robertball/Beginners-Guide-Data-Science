Title:
Big Ray's Chicken Cosima Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3255529.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 Tbsp flour
1 tsp ground coriander
1 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp ground tumeric
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp salt
6 large skinless boneless chicken thighs, cut into 1 in' chunks
1 large onion, chopped
1 lb sweet potato, peeled and cut into 1 inch chunks
1 Tbsp olive oil
2 cups chicken broth
3 cans (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
fresh cilantro, chopped to garnish

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Put flour and spices in a large resealable zip-lock bag, mixing well.
Add chicken and shake to coat all pieces well.
Heat oil in large Dutch oven with lid, add onions and heat until softened.  Add chicken and flour mixture to skillet, heating for 2 minutes,  Stir and add sweet potatoes, heating for 2 minutes.  Add chicken broth and bring to a boil.  Add chickpeas and stir.  Cover and let simmer for 45 minutes or until potatoes are tender.  Turn off heat and let sit for 10 minutes.  Ladle into bowls ,serve with chopped cilantro.
