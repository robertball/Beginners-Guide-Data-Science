Title:
Healthier Classic Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/277973.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup unsalted butter
1/4 cup applesauce
1 cup crunchy peanut butter
1 cup white sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
4 egg whites
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda

Directions:
Cream together butter, peanut butter, applesauce, and sugars. Beat in eggs.
In a separate bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda. Stir into batter. Put batter in refrigerator for 1 hour.
Roll into 1 inch balls and put on baking sheets.  Flatten each ball with a fork, making   a criss-cross pattern.  Bake in a preheated 375 degrees F oven for about 10 minutes or until cookies begin to brown. Do not over-bake.
