Title:
Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons butter, softened
1 cup sugar
3/4 cup milk
1 egg
1 3/4 cups plus 1 tablespoon flour
2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup fresh blueberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Line 12 muffin cups. Beat sugar and butter until well blended. Pour in milk, add egg, and beat until smooth. Sift 1 3/4 cups flour with baking powder and salt. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix until just moistened. Toss blueberries with 1 tablespoon flour and fold into batter. Fill muffin cups 3/4 full of batter and bake 15 minutes. Remove and cool.
