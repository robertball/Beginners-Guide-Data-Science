Title:
Marinated Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/837624.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans whole mushrooms, drained
1 cauliflower separated into florets
1 bunch broccoli, cut bite sized(flowers and stems)
4 stalks celery, chopped
3-4 green onions, chopped
2 cups grape or cherry tomatoes
1 large bottle (approx. 400 ml.)Italian salad dressing
1 tsp. dried basil
i tsp. dried oregano

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a large bowl.
Refrigerate over-night
Stir occasionally to coat ingredients with the marinade

Submitter: 
Loretta's Recipe Journal
Description: 
This recipe makes a beautifully large salad that's a hit at potluck suppers. Be sure to make one day before you plan to serve it so that it will have time to marinate.
