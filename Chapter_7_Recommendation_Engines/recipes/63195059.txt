Title:
Chex Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons Worcestershire Sauce
6 Tablespoons Butter
1/2 teaspoon Seasoned Salt
3/4 teaspoon Garlic Salt
1/2 teaspoon Onion Powder
9 Cups Chex Cereal

Directions:
Heat Oven to 250 degrees. Melt Butter in large roasting pan in oven. Stir in seasonings. Gradually stir in cereal until evenly coated. Bake 1 hour stirring every 15 minutes. Spread on paper towels to cool.

Submitter: 
NessTheMess
Description: 
My family prefers Just Chex Chex Mix. I sometimes add Pecans, Pretzel sticks or whatever sounds good. Generally just use the cereal.
