Title:
Barbacoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lb Chuck Roast
Sauce Mix
1/3c Apple Cider Vinegar
3T Lime Juice
4 Chipotles in Adobo
4t Minced Garlic
4t Cumin
2t Oregano
1t Ground Black Pepper
1t Salt
1/2t Ground Cloves
3/4c Chicken Broth
3 Bay Leaves

Directions:
Use a food processor or blender to WELL BLEND the Sauce Mix.
Pour over the meat in a slow cooker.
Add the chicken broth and bay leaves, and cook covered and untouched for 4-6 hours at 250 degrees Fahrenheit.
***you can add the chicken broth to the sauce mix for convenience, but leaves a stronger flavor when done separate***
