Title:
Mango Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large mango
2 T each minced red and yellow bell peppers
1 T minced jalapeno (seeded)
1 T minced cilantro
1 lime, juiced

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl.

From Mark Miller's Coyote Cafe cookbook. I usually serve this with his chitpotle shrimp and corn cakes (pg 149).
