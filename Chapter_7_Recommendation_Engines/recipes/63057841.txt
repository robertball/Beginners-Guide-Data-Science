Title:
Amish Friendship Bread Starter (Gluten-Free) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/769215.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (0.25 ounce) package gluten-free active dry yeast
1/4 cup warm water (110Â° F)
1 cup gluten-free flour
1 cup white sugar
1 cup milk

Directions:
In a small bowl, dissolve yeast in water. Let stand 10 minutes.
In a glass, plastic or ceramic container, combine flour and sugar. Mix thoroughly.
Slowly add in milk and dissolved yeast mixture. Cover loosely and let stand at room temperature until bubbly.
This is Day One of the ten-day cycle. For the next 10 days handle starter according to the instructions for Amish Friendship Bread and use gluten-free flour when feeding on Day 6 and Day 10. (see footnotes)
