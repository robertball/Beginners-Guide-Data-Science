Title:
Roasted Cauliflower with Cumin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of cauliflower
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon cumin

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350
Wash the cauliflower and cut it into florets or bite size pieces.
In a bowl, drizzle the oil over the cauliflower. Toss the cauliflower around so that it is covered in oil.
Lay the pieces  of cauliflower on a baking sheet and sprinkle the salt and cumin.
Roast for 45 minutes or until desired crunchiness.

Submitter: 
Heather
Description: 
Roasted cauliflower florets with just the right amount of cumin. These babies taste like candy and they are healthy, too!
