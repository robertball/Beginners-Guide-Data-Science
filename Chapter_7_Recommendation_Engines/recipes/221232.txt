Title:
Kim's Peach Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/841272.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup fresh peaches - peeled, pitted and sliced
3/4 cup peach nectar
1 cup ice
2 fluid ounces tequila
2 fluid ounces triple sec

Directions:
Place peaches, peach nectar, ice, tequila, and triple sec in a blender. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
Kim
Description: 
Very yummy adult summer treat. Subtle peach flavors. I don't generally measure the ingredients when I make my margaritas because I've done it so many times. You can tweak the amounts to your own liking, but these are the things I used and they taste great.
