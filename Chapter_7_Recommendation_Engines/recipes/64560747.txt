Title:
Mango Green Tea Frapaccino Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-3/4 cup of brewed and chilled green tea
-1 cup of vanilla ice cream
-1 scoop of mango sorbet (optional)
-Any amount of white sugar (depends on your taste, I recommend 2-3 tablespoons)
-10-12 ice cubes
-1 ripe mango
-whipped cream (optional)

Directions:
Put green tea, ice cream, ice cubes, and white sugar in blender and blend until thick and creamy
Cut the mango in any way you want and put it on a plate or in a bowl
Pour the green tea frapaccino into a cup and put on whipped cream (optional)
Put mango sorbet next to or on the mangos (optional)
Enjoy!
