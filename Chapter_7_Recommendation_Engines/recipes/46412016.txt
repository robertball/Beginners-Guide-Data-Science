Title:
Brandied Cherry Clafouti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups drained (save fluid) tart cherries canned in water
1/4 cup brandy
2/3 cup sugar
Pam spray
2/3 cup sifted all purpose flour
1 cup of milk
3 large eggs
1/8 teaspoon allspice
1 tablespoon lemon zest
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
powdered sugar

Directions:
Drain cherries.  Save fluid.  In glass bowl mix cherries, brandy and 1/3 cup sugar.  Let sit for 1 hour.
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.  Spray 9 inch pie pan.
Drain cherries, reserving liquid.  Pour brandy liquid into a blender.  Add 1/3 cup sugar, milk, eggs, vanilla, allspice, lemon zest, salt, and flour.  Blend well.  Spread cherries evenly in pie pan.  Pour batter over fruit.
Bake in preheated oven for 5 minutes.  Reduce heat to 350 degrees and bake for 45 to 50 minutes.  Clafouti should be golden and puffy.  Let cool.  Dust with powedered sugar.  Serve warm.
