Title:
Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c. butter or margarine
1 c. packed light brown sugar
1/2 sugar
1 egg
1 t. vanilla
1 c. flour
1/2 t. baking soda
1/2 t. salt
1/4 c. milk
2 1/2 c. quick oats
2 c. semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Cream butter, sugars, egg, and vanilla until light and fluffy.
Combine dry ingredients and slowly add, with mik, to creamed mixture, blending well. Stir in oats and chocolate chips.
Drop onto cookie sheet and bake at 375 degrees for 10-12 minutes.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
Wonderful cookies with one stipulation; don't grease the pan, or the cookies will flatten.
