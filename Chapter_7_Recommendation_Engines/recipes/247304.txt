Title:
Peachy Mango Cucumber Tea Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3587072.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup brewed black tea (such as Gold PeakÂ®), chilled
1 cup frozen peach slices
1 cup mango chunks
1 cup peeled and chopped cucumber
1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
1 teaspoon honey (optional)

Directions:
Combine tea, peach, mango, cucumber, yogurt, ginger, and honey in a blender. Blend until smooth.

Submitter: 
foodelicious
Description: 
Peach and mango give this smoothie its delicate color, while cucumber lightens up the texture. A touch of yogurt and ginger gives it a palate-pleasing finish.
