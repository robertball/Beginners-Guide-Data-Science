Title:
Tomato Chili Flank Steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup tomato juice
2 tbsp. packed brown sugar
2 tbsp. soya sauce
1 tbsp. olive oil
1-1/2 tsp. chili powder
1 clove garlic, minced
1 pinch ground cumin
1 beef flank marinating steak (1 lb)
1 pinch of salt

Directions:
Whisk together tomato juice, brown sugar, soya sauce, oil, chili powder, garlic and cumin.  Add steak, turning to coat in a large shallow dish.  Cover and refrigerate 24 hours.
Remove steak and scrape marinade into small saucepan.  Sprinkle meat with salt.  Add to grill, close lid, turning once until medium rare about 8-9 minutes.  Transfer to cutting board and let stand about 10 minutes.  Slice thinly across the grain.
Meanwhile, bring marinade to boil and serve drizzled over steak.
