Title:
Chocolate Luscious Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups Semi-sweet Chocolate Chips
1 can condensed milk
1 T. Vanilla extract
lots of powder sugar

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine Chocolate Chips & C. milk, remove from heat and stir in vanilla, Cover and chill for 2-3 hrs, then shape into 1 inch balls...add powder sugar...cover with powder sugar, then refrigerate till firm..
