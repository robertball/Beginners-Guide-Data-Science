Title:
Fresh Lemonade by the Glass Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Fresh squeezed juice of one lemon (strained it desired)
1 cup water
1-2 tbs. of sugar

Directions:
1. Combine fresh squeezed lemon juice and water in a glass.  Stir in sugar (sweeten to taste).  Chill or add ice if desired.  Enjoy!
