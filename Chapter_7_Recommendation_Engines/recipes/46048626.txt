Title:
Swiss Chicken Casserole II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into 1" cubes
2 teaspoons garlic powder
2 teaspoons oniion powder
6 slices Swiss cheese
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
1/4 cup white wine
2 cups herb-seasoned stuffing mix -or- breadcrumbs
1/4 cup butter/water -or- 1/4 cup margarine

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Sprinkle chicken liberally with garlic & onion powders. Arrange chicken in a greased 8 x 8 x 2 baking dish. Top with Swiss cheese slices.
Combine soup and wine and stir well. Spoon mixture over chicken and sprinkle with stuffing mix or breadcrumbs. Drizzle butter or margarine over crumbs.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 50 minutes. Enjoy!
