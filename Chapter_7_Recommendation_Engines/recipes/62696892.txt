Title:
Manicotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package manicotti pasta
1/2 pound lean ground beef
1 garlic clove, minced
1  cup cottage cheese
1/2 tsp salt
4 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup mayo or miracle whip
1 (32 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
oregano to tasteParmesan cheese to taste

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente. Drain and cool. Reserve.
Meanwhile, in a large skillet brown beef with garlic on medium heat. Drain and cool.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Combine beef, cottage cheese, salt mozzarella cheese,  and mayo. Mix well. Stuff shells with mixture using small teaspoon.
Spread spaghetti sauce in 9x13 pan to cover the bottom. Place stuffed noodles in the dish. Cover with spaghetti sauce and sprinkle with parmesan cheese and oregano. Cover and bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes.

Description: 
A pleasing blend of ground beef, cottage cheese, mozzarella, Parmesan,  and dried oregano is stuffed into large tubes of cooked manicotti in this satisfying baked dish. Blanket the stuffed pasta with spaghetti sauce, top with cheese and bake.Modified from: Meat Filled Manicotti
