Title:
Quick-braised Pork tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Plain pork tenerloin cut into 1/2 inch slices
4-5 cloves fresh garlic minced
1 tsp salt
1 tsp cracked black pepper
4-5 slices bacon cooked and chopped
3-4 tsp brown sugar
3-4 cups apple cider

Directions:
1. Place tenderloin slices in a gallon sized Ziploc bag along with the salt, pepper and garlic. Let marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
2. Chop the bacon slices into small pieces and cook in a 350 degree oven until crispy--about 20 min.
3. Sear pork pieces on both sides in a hot dutch oven or high sided saute pan.
4. Pour in apple cider to just cover pork peices, add brown sugar and cooked bacon bits and simmer until sauce is reduced to a syrup consistency.
5. Serve over mashed potatoes and enjoy!
