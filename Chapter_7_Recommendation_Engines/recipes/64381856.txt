Title:
Super Simple Sausage Rice Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2205447.jpg
Ingredients:
1 6 oz. box of Uncle Ben's Brown Rice & Quinoa
2 1/4 Cup boiling water
1 Tbl. butter
1/2 lb. sweet sausage
1 small onion
1 tsp. olive oil
1 Cup grated Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Chop the small onion and saute in the olive oil until transparent.   Pour the contents of the Uncle Ben's Brown Rice & Quinoa packet in a 1 1/2 qt. baking dish. Add the butter, onion, and boiling water, cover and place in the oven.
Cut the sausage into 1/2" pieces and brown on both side in the skillet. Add to the rice mixture and return to the oven for 30 minutes.  Take the mixture out and top with the Monterey Jack cheese and bake for an additional 5 minutes.
