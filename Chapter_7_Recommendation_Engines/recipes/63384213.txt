Title:
Crispy Brie Wedges with Cranberry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
BRIE
120g/4oz Brie - cut into wedges
300ml/10floz Vegetable Oil - for deep frying
60g/2oz Plain Flour
2 Eggs - lightly beaten
100g/3Â½oz fresh White Breadcrumbs
SAUCE
360g/12 oz fresh Cranberries
180ml/6fl oz Orange Juice
150g/5oz Brown Sugar
60g/2oz White Sugar
60ml/2fl oz Rum

Directions:
SAUCE
Place all the ingredients in a sauce pan and cook on medium heat, stirring occasionally for 15-20 minutes or until most of the liquid has reduced;
Remove from heat
BRIE
Place the vegetable oil into a deep, heavy-bottomed saucepan and heat until a small cube of bread sizzles and turns golden when dropped into it;
Place the flour onto a plate, the beaten egg into a bowl and the breadcrumbs onto another plate;
Dip each wedge of brie first in the plain flour, then in the beaten egg and finally in the breadcrumbs, shaking off any excess between each stage;
Carefully place the brie wedges into the hot oil and deep fry for 4-5 minutes, or until golden-brown;
Remove the wedges from the oil and drain on kitchen paper;
To serve, place the wedges onto a serving plate along with a small bowl of the cranberry sauce.
