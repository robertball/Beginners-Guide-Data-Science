Title:
Spaghetti with Pesto and Tomato-Mozzarella Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 # thin spaghetti or linquine
1 lg. bunch fresh basil
1 clove garlic
1/4 C olive oil
1 T olive oil
1/2 t salt
1/4 t black pepper
1/2 C freshly grated Parmesan cheese
1 1/2 pints cherry tomatoes, each cut in half
1 T red wine vinegar
8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, cut into 3/4-in cubes

Directions:
Heat lg. saucepan of salted water to boiling over high heat. Add spaghetti and cook as label directs.
Meanwhile, reserve 12 sm basil leaves for garnish. From remaining basil, remove enough leaves to equal 2 cups firmly packed. In food processor with knife blade attached, process basil leaves, garlic, 1/4C olive oil, and 1/2t salt until pureed, stopping processor and scraping bowl occ. Add Parmesan; pulse to combine. Set pesto aside.
In bowl, mix tomatoes, vinegar, and 1/4 t pepper with remaining oil and 1/4 t salt. Gently stir in mozzarella
Drain spaghetti, reserving 1/2C spaghetti cooking water. Return spaghetti and reserved cooking water to saucepot; add pesto and toss well to coat pasta. Spoon spaghetti mixture into large shallow bowl; top with tomato-mozzarella salad. Garnish with reserved basil leaves.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
From Good Housekeeping, a warm salad with spaghetti, fresh basil, garlic, olive oil, cherry tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese and fresh Parmesan cheese.
