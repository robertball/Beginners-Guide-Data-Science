Title:
FILLING FOR CREAM PUFFS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 CUPS HEAVY WHIPPING CREAM
1/4 CUP POWDERED SUGAR
1/2 TSP VANILLA
ADDITIONAL POWDERED SUGAR

Directions:
BEAM CREAM UNTIL IT BEGINS TO THICKEN. ADD SUGAR & VANILLA AND BEAT UNTIL ALMOST STIFF.
