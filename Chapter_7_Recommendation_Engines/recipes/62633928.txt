Title:
Chicken Enchilada Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 lbs boneless chicken breasts, diced
1 large onion, diced
4(4oz each )cans hot green chiles diced,
1(16oz) pkg Pepper-Jack cheese diced or shredded
1(24oz) jar Red Roasted Peppers, diced
2 pkgs Taco Seasoning
1(16oz) Sour Cream
1 cup Chicken Bouillon(2 cubes to 1 cup water for  more chicken flavor
2(28oz each)Tomato Sauce
3 Tblsp minced garlic
1 tsp Oregano
1 tsp Basil

Directions:
Cut up chicken, onions, peppers and cheese. Saute chicken until cooked. Add onion and garlic and cook until tender. Add remaining seasoning, spices, peppers, tomato sauce and bouillon.  Cook until bubbly.  Add cheese and sour cream and simmer for about 1 hour. Put soup in bowl,  add crushed torilla chips with dollop of sour cream just before serving.

Description: 
Filling, delicious soup with a little bit of spice to it! It is a meal in itself.
