Title:
Tater Tot Hot Dish III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef or ground turkey
1/2 onion, chopped
1 (10 3/4 ounce) can cream of mushroom soup
1 (14 ounce) can green beans, drained
6 slices american cheese
1 (32 ounce) bag frozen tater tots

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Brown ground beef or ground turkey and onion in a large skillet or pan until brown.
Drain meat, if needed, return to skillet or pan.
Add cream of mushroom soup to meat, mix well.
Transfer meat mixture to a 9 x 13 baking dish and spread evenly on bottom.
Spread drained green beans evenly over meat mixture, top with american cheese.
Place tater tots in rows evenly in a single layer on top. Bake in oven for 22 - 25 minutes or until tater tots are golden brown.

Submitter: 
Kristi Goulette Lozensky
Description: 
A simple and easy dinner that everyone will love. Serve with a side salad for a complete meal.
