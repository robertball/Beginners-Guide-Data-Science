Title:
Elaine's Sweet Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lbs. unbleached flour
4 pkgs. rapid rise active dry yeast
2 tsp salt
2 cups water
2 cups milk
1/2 lb butter
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 tsp vanilla
5 beaten eggs
1 egg beaten with 1 tbsp. water (for egg wash)

Directions:
Mix flour, yeast and salt together in a very large bowl.
In a large pot, place water, milk, butter,sugar and vanilla. Heat them on low heat until butter is melted and liquid is warm, not hot.
Add the warm liquid and the beaten eggs to the flour mixture.
Knead for about 10 minutes until the dough is elastic. The dough will be on the wet side. Place in a warm place to rise to double in size.
Divide the dough into 5 pieces and put in 5 individual 4x8 pans sprayed with PAM.
Put the pans in a warm place to rise again. Before placing the pans in the oven, brush with the beaten egg.
Bake in a 350 degree oven for @ 25-30 minutes. Remove from oven. When cooled a few minutes, remove from pans and cool completely on wire racks.

Submitter: 
scott_ma
Description: 
A sweet bread that my aunt Elaine makes at Easter.  The recipe makes 5 loaves.  It is a huge hit in our house.  This bread is great toasted with butter and also makes fabulous grilled cheese!
