Title:
Starr's Cucumber Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large cucumber
1 can white corn
1 sm can diced green chiles
1 jalapeno
1 sm can sliced black olives
1 avacado
2 stalks green onion
1/4 t salt
1/2 t sugar
1/4 t pepper
1/4 t dill
1/4 t cumin
1 1/2 T olive oil
Juice of a lime

Directions:
Dice the cucumber, avacado, green onion and place in a bowl.  Seed and devein the Jalapeno finely dice and add to the same bowl.  Add all of the canned ingredients after draining them.  Then add all of the spices, olive oil and juice the lime over the top. Mix then place in a sealed container and let it sit together for at least an hour.  Then serve and enjoy.

Submitter: 
ALD0014
Description: 
Light and refreshing salsa for summertime snacking.  Sometimes mix it up and add some black beans to change up the flavors.  I like to serve with corn tortillas.
