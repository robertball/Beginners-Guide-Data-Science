Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened*
3 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
4 ounces smoked salmon, flaked
2 tablespoons capers
Keebler速 Town House速 Original Crackers

Directions:
In small mixing bowl beat cream cheese on medium speed of electric mixer until fluffy. Add chives, lemon juice and dill. Beat until combined. Stir in salmon and capers.
Refrigerate at least 2 hours to blend flavors. Let stand at room temperature for 20 minutes. Garnish as desired. Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Town House速
Description: 
Whipped cream cheese with smoked salmon, chives, dill, and capers is a classic combination and it's perfect spread on crackers.
