Title:
Peanut Butter Banana Protein Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup oats
1T peanut butter
1 banana, sliced
cinnamon

Directions:
Mix oats with 1 cup water and microwave until it reaches desired consistency, stirring every 30 seconds.
Top with the peanut butter, banana, and cinnamon, if desired.
