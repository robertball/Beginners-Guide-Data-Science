Title:
Chicken Vegetable Soup with Alphabet Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1055947.jpg
Ingredients:
2 C. Carrots, diced
2 Onions, chopped coarsely
2 C. Celery, diced
2 Tbsp. Butter or Olive oil
2 C. Chicken breast meat, cubed
4 C. Chicken broth
1 tsp. Salt
1 tsp. Dry mustard
1 tsp. Dry ginger
1/2 C. Alphabet noodles

Directions:
Wash vegetables under cold running water and prepare them for the soup
In a 4 qt. dutch oven saute onions and celery with butter until onions are translucent over medium heat for about 6 minutes
Add carrots, chicken meat and chicken broth and bring mixture to a boil over medium high heat
When mixture comes to a boil turn down to simmer and cook for a half hour until carrots are tender
Stir in spices and seasoning and adjust to suit your taste preferences
Mix in alphabet noodles and cook for 20 minutes until noodles are done
Serve immediately

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A medley of vegetables with some chunks of chicken meat to warm you up on a cold afternoon for lunch or dinner
