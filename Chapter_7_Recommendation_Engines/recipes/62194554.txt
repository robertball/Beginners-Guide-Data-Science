Title:
Crock Pot Creamed Corn Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 - 1 lb. pkgs of frozen corn
2 - 8 oz. cream cheese
12 T. butter
12 T. water
6 T. sugar
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Put in crock-pot and cook for 4 hours on high. Season with salt & pepper. Stir often.
Alternative Method:
Melt everything in microwave and then switch it to a pan on the stove. Low heat and stir often.
