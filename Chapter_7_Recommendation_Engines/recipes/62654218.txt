Title:
Almond Crunch Biscotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Caramelized Almon Slices:
2 Tbsp. Butter
3 Tbsp. Sugar
3/4 C. sliced blanched almonds
Biscotti Dough
1/2 C. butter
3/4 C. sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
2 C. flour
1 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt

Directions:
To prepara Caramelized Almond Slices, in a skillet melt butter with sugar.  When it bubbles and just atarts to turn golden add almonds and continue cooking, shaking pan to prevent from burning, until nuts turn golden and caramelized. Turn out on a baking sheet and let cool. Break into pieces.
To prepare biscotti, in a mixing bowel cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.  Beat in eggs and vanilla.  In a bowl combine the flour, baking powder, soda and salt.  Add to the creamed mixture, mixing just until blended.  Stir in Caramelized Almond Slices.  Divide dough in half.  On a greased and floured baking sheet pat out into two logs about 1/2 inch high, 1 1/2 inches wide and 14 inches long, spacing them at least 2 inches apart. Bake in the middle of a preheated 325 degree oven for 25 minutes or until lightly browned. Transfer from baking sheet to a rack.  Transfer from baking sheet to a rack.  Let cool for 5 minutes.  Place on a cutting board.  With a serrated knife slice diagonally at a 45 degree angle about 3/8  inch thick.  Lay the slices flat on the baking sheet and return to the oven for 10 minutes, turning them over once, to dry and slightly.  Let cool on a rack.  Store in a tightly coveed container.
