Title:
Apple Snicker Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tub of Cool Whip, thawed
1 package of butterscotch instant pudding
1 cup of milk
6 snickers bars, cut in bite size pieces (those popables work well too) or 3 super size snickers bars
3-4 medium size Granny Smith apples cut up into small chunks

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine milk and pudding, then mix in the cool whip. Add the Snickers and then the apples.

Submitter: 
lojoyner
Description: 
Had this at my nephew's graduation party. Very good, and not to sweet.
