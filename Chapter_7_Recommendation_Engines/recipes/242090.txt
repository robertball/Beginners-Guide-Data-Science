Title:
Crusty Garlic Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2229915.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
3 tablespoons dried minced garlic
1 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 loaf French bread, halved lengthwise

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Beat butter, garlic, basil, and salt together in a bowl until smooth. Spread butter mixture onto each half of bread. Wrap each half of bread in aluminum foil and place on a baking sheet.
Bake in the preheated oven until golden and crusty, about 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
BMG
Description: 
Wonderful garlic bread. Quick and easy. Be careful when unwrapping bread, it will be hot and steamy. Adjust garlic amount to taste.
