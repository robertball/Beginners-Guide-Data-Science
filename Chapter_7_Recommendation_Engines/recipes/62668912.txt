Title:
Hoisin Teriyaki Glazed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large bone in chicken breast with skin
1/2 cup Hoisin sauce
1/4 cup Teriyaki marinade/sauce
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp ground ginger
2 tsp garlic chili sauce (or adjust to taste)
1 TBL rice vinegar
1/4 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
In a small bowl mix together all ingredients except the chicken.
Cover a 9 x 13 baking dish with aluminum foil and spray with cooking spray.  Place the chicken in the dish skin side up.
With a brush, baste the chicken with the sauce.
Place the chicken in the preheated oven and bake for about one hour, basting about every 15 minutes.
