Title:
Killer Baked Garlic Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/736205.jpg
Ingredients:
12 Medium cooked Shrimp,cleaned and deveined.
1/4 Cup Garlic Butter
1 Cup Shredded Monterey Jack Cheese
Belguin Bread

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 Degrees F (204 Degrees C).
Take your Escargots Dish, bend 6 shrimp 1 each into each crevasse.
Melt Garlic Butter and pour over each of the Shrimp till it fills crevasse.
Take shredded Monterey Jack Cheeses and place on top of Shrimp.
Place on a cookie sheet as this will probably boil over.
Cook at 400 Degrees F (204 Degrees C) until cheese melts.
Turn oven to Broil.
Broil at Medium heat, keeping an eye on it so it does not burn, just brown to a golden brown (2-4 minutes).

Submitter: 
The Biker Baker
Description: 
Shrimps smothered in garlic butter topped off with Monterey Jack Cheese, baked to perfection with a side of Belguin bread for dipping!
