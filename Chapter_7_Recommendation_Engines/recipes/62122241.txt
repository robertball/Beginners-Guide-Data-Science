Title:
Cinnamon Currant Peanut Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar (16oz) creamy natural peanut butter w/ salt
1/2 cup currants or raisins
2T sugar
3t cinnamon

Directions:
Stir all ingredients together until well mixed.

Submitter: 
www.PeanutButterBoy.com
Description: 
A simple modification to plain peanut butter boosts its flavor and nutritional value.
