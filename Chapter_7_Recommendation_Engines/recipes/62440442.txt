Title:
Spicy Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C chopped onion
1/2 C chopped celery
1 T butter or margarine
1/8 t salt
1/8 t pepper
1/8 t ground allspice
1/8 t cinnamon
1 T brown sugar
3 C cooked rice
1/2 C raisins (plumped)
1 tart cooking apple - chopped
1/2 C slivered, roasted almonds

Directions:
In a large skillet, cook celery and onions in butter until tender crisp.  Stir in rice, raisins and seasonings.  Heat thoroughly.  Stir in apple.  Remove from heat. Cover and let stand 5 minutes. Sprinkle with almonds and serve.

Submitter: 
Becky Cleveland
Description: 
This is a great side dish to accompany pork. It has an interesting blend of unexpected sweet and tart.
