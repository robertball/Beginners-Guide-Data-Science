Title:
Peperonata Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
2 Red Peppers - washed, cored, seeded and cut lengthways into thin strips
2 Yellow Peppers - washed, cored, seeded and cut lengthways into thin strips
1 clove Garlic - finely chopped
1 Onion - finely sliced
300g/10 oz ripe Plum Tomatoes - peeled, crushed with seeds removed through a sieve OR Jar/Bottle Tomato Passata
1tbsp flatleaf Parsley - chopped
Some fresh Basil Leaves - torn
Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper

Directions:
Pour and gently heat the oil in a big frying pan;
Add onions and cook gently, stirring frequently for some 3 minutes or so until softened a bit;
Add peppers and cook, stirring continually, for further 3 minutes;
Stir in the garlic, tomatoes or passata and parsley, combine well and season to taste;
Increase heat to bring to boil, stirring frequently;
Reduce heat, cover and simmer for about 15 minutes;
Remove lid and cook gently, stirring frequently, for further 10 minutes or so until the mixture has reduced and thickened;
Stir in the torn basil leaves, taste for seasoning and serve.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
An Italian classic side dish with red and yellow peppers. This is my version. Delicious, packed with flavour this will complement any main, especially chicken and veal.
