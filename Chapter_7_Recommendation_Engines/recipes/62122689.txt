Title:
Breakfast Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups sugar (organic preferably)
1/2 cup blackstrap molassas
2-1/2 cups rolled oats
4 cups unbleached white flour
1 Tablespoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons cinnamon
1/2 cup olive oil
5 whole eggs
1/4 cup milk
2 teasppons vanilla
1 cup raisins
1 cup chopped dried apricots
1 cup finely shredded carrots (I use what comes out of my juicer)
1/2 cup finely shredded apple (I use what comes out of my juicer)
1 small can crushed pineapple

Directions:
Put all ingredients into a large bowl and mix. There is no need to do it seperately. This will mix into a somewhat sticky mixture.
Roll into golfball sized balls and put on baking tray 2" apart. Before baking flatten balls into 1/2" rounds.
Bake at 350 for 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
CHEFANDERSEN
Description: 
These are breakfast cookies you can feel good about eating and serving kids. My kids LOVE them!!
