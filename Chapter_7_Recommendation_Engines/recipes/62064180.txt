Title:
Caramel Ribbon Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box Duncan Hines "Butter Recipe Golden" cake mix
1/2 c. chopped pecans
2/3 c. (5 oz. can) evaporated milk
1/4 c. margarine, melted
1/2 c. semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 c. caramel ice cream topping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease a 9" x 13" cake pan.  Combine dry cake mix and nuts.  Stir in milk and margarine.  Spread about 1/2 of mixture into greased pan and bake at 350 for 10 mins.  Remove from oven and sprinkle chocolate chips over hot crust.  Drizzle with caramel topping.  Drop remaining mixture by teaspoons over all.  Bake 20-25 mins. or until done.  Cool on wire rack.  While still slightly warm, loosen sides and cut into bars.

Submitter:

Description: 
Delicious and very simple bar made with yellow cake mix, chocolate, caramel and nuts. This is always a hit with my family and friends.
