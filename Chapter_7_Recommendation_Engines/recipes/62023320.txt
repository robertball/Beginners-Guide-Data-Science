Title:
Chinese Sesame Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 tsp.    Garlic powder
1/2 tsp.    Ginger
1-2 tsp. Soy Sauce
1 Chicken Breast (Cut-up)
1 tsp. Sesame oil
1 Tbsp. Sesame Seeds
1 Tbsp. Plum Jam or your favorite
fruit jelly or hoisin sauce
1 tsp. Sugar
1 tsp. Cornstarch
2 Tbsp. water (or chicken stock)
5-10 drops of Tabasco Sauce
(optional)

Directions:
Stir fry the chicken. Add all remaining ingredients EXCEPT the cornstarch
and water to the chicken.  (Watch it cooks fast).  Thicken and coat the
chicken with the thickening mixture.  Add the cornstarch/water solution and
cook until bubbly.  (Again, it cooks fast).

Submitter: 
MARBALET
Description: 
If you like your sesame chicken spicy, add the hot sauce; leave it out if you don't like it spicy.  This recipe is quick and easy.
