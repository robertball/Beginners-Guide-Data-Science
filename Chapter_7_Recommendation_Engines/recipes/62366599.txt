Title:
Tuna Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/408162.jpg
Ingredients:
2 12oz cans of tuna (drained)
1  24oz container of cottage cheese
2/3 cup of bread crumbs
1/4 cup chopped onion
2 eggs
10 sprigs of parsley chopped
1/2 teaspoon of Old Bay Blackening Seasoning
Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Place tuna, cottage cheese, bread crumbs, parsley, and onion in bowl.  Lightly beat the eggs in a small bowl with spices.  Combine the two mixtures.  Place into casserole dish (5x9 or 6x6) and lightly sprinkle with bread crumbs on top.  Bake for 35 minutes.
