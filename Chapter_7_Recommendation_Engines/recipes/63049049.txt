Title:
Amazing Curry Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 chiken breasts, defrosted
1/4 c. butter
1/2 c. honey
1/4c. Dijon mustard
2 T. regular mustard
2 t. curry powder
1 t. salt
1 medium clove of garlic, pressed (or 1/8 t. garlic powder)

Directions:
Place chiken in an oven-safe baking dish lined with greased tin foil. In a saucepan, melt the butter, then add the remaining ingredients, stirring until smooth. Pour sauce over chicken, but save some to put over rice:)Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
Serve over/with rice
ENJOY!

Submitter: 
Natalie Herr
Description: 
This is A-M-A-Z-I-N-G!!!!!!!!!
