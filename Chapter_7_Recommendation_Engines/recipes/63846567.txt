Title:
Herb bread for Holiday Stuffing or Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068399.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup warm water
3 Tablespoons white sugar
2 1/2 teaspoons of yeast
1/4 cup vegetable oil
3 cups bread flour
1 teaspoon salt
sage, rosemary, pepper, onion powder

Directions:
Place the water, spices (use the ones you love as little or as much as you like), sugar and yeast in the pan of the bread machine. Let the yeast dissolve and foam for 10 minutes.  Add the oil, flour, and salt to the yeast. Select Basic or White Bread setting, and press Start.
Once done slice and cube bread and bake at 400 degrees until dry! place in a container or use right away.
