Title:
Mustard Aioli Grilled Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayo
2 cloves garlic
1 Tbsp Dijon
1 Tbsp whole grain mustard
2 1/2 lb Yukon Gold potatoes
2 Tbsp fresh parsley
1 Tbsp fresh chives
1 Tbsp fresh tarragon

Directions:
1. whisk mayo, garlic, mustards, salt, pepper.  cover and refrig for 30 min.
2. boil potatoes for 20 min. drain and cool.
3. toss mixture with potatoes
4. grill for about 8 minutes
5. sprinkle with herbs
