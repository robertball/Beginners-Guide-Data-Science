Title:
Pumpkin Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups canned pumpkin
1 cup evaporated milk with 1 cup water (or use more milk to equal 2 cups of milk and water)
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1/2 t salt
2 t cinnamon
1/2 t ginger
1/2 t allspice
1/2 t nutmeg

Directions:
Mix pumpkin and milk/water.
Add sugar.
Beat in eggs.
Add salt and spices.
Pour into pie shell/s.
Bake at 475 degrees for 15 minutes.
Bake at 400 degrees for 45 minutes.
Knife blade should come out clean when done.
