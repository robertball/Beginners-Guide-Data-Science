Title:
Not Your Dorm Room Cup of Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4539433.jpg
Ingredients:
3 ounces rice vermicelli noodles (80g)
2 ounces cooked chicken, diced (60g)
1.5 ounces raw carrot, shredded (40g)
1.5 ounces raw bok choy, diced (40g)
1 Tbsp green onion, chopped
1 tsp dashi broth powder
1 tsp nutritional yeast flakes
1/8 tsp ground white pepper
1/8 tsp ground black pepper
1 tsp fresh grated ginger
1/8 tsp red pepper flakes, or more to taste
1 egg
1 Tbsp tamari soy sauce
1 tsp toasted sesame oil
1/2 tsp gluten free miso

Directions:
In a large, portable bowl with a lid, add the dried rice noodles, diced chicken, carrot, bok choy, green onion, broth powder, nutritional yeast, both ground peppers, fresh ginger, and red pepper flakes.
In a small, portable container add the tamari, sesame oil, and miso. Cover and shake until combined.
When ready to eat, add approximately 1.5 cups of hot water to the noodle bowl. Using chopsticks, push aside the noodles and add the egg in the middle stirring constantly. Stir well. Close the lid and let sit until the noodles are ready to eat, about 3 minutes.
Remove the cover and add the tamari, miso seasoning and enjoy.

Submitter: 
Buckwheat Queen
Description: 
Protein rich noodle soup that you can whip up in no time. Add all the ingredients in a portable container and just add water and egg when you are ready to eat.
