Title:
Taco Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 small green cabbage chopped
1 jalapeno pepper seeded and minced
1/2 medium size red onion minced
1 carrot chopped
1 Tbs chopped fresh cilantro
juice of 1 lime

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a bowl and serve as a condiment for tacos.

Submitter: 
mixingmedias
Description: 
The local taco truck serves their chicken tacos with cabbage, cilantro and lime.  This is my attempt to recreate their taco toppings.
