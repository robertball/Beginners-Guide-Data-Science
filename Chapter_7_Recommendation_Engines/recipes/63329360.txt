Title:
Basil Green Goddess Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/ 2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
4 scallions, white and green, sliced
1/2 cup chopped basil leaves
1-2 Tbsp lemon juice
1/2 anchovy filet, or 1 tsp anchovy paste
1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Blend all ingredients together.  Sample with a lettuce leaf.  Adjust seasoning to taste.
