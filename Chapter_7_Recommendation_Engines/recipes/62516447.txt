Title:
Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cans (10 3/4) cream of potato soup
1 16oz can of Veg-All
2 cups cooked, diced chicken or turkey
1/2 C milk
1/2 tsp. thyme
1/2 tsp. black pepper
2 9 in frozen pie crusts, thawed
1 egg (optional

Directions:
Combine first 6 ingredients.  Spoon into prepared crust. Cover with other crust; crimp edges to seal.  Slit top crust and brush with egg if desired.  Bake @ 350 for 40 min.  Cool 10 min.
