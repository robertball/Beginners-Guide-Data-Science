Title:
Cashew Caramel Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup organic whole wheat,spelt etc pastry flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
2T Flax Seeds + 6 T Water
1/2 cup chopped cashews
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup organic sugar
2 tablespoons Earth Balance Margarine
1 1/2 tablespoons non-dairy cream
1/3 cup chopped cashews
1/4 cup packed brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Grease a 9x9 inch baking pan.
To prepare Pastry:  In Food Processor, mix the flax seeds,water and both sugars together.  Blend in  the nuts; sift the flour with the baking powder and salt.  Add to flax mixture and blend well.  Press dough into the bottom of the greased pan.
Bake for 20 to 25 minutes. While pastry bakes, mix 1/4 cup of brown sugar, cream and cashews into the melted margarine.
Spread over baked pastry and place under a broiler for about 1 minute, until topping bubbles and becomes brown.  Cut into bars while warm.  Let cool in pan.
