Title:
Easy Crab and Horseradish Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can crabmeat, drained and flaked
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
1 dash Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
In a medium bowl, mix the crabmeat, cream cheese, horseradish and Worcestershire sauce.
Microwave on high 2 to 3 minutes, until warm and bubbly. Stir well and serve.

Submitter: 
The Iron Chef -- Suzi-Q
Description: 
A great tasting, very easy and quick creamy dip. It'll taste like you actually spent time making it!
