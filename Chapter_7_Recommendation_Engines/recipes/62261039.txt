Title:
Breakfast Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 teaspoons dried sage
2 teaspoons Kosher salt
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon dried marjoram
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1/8 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
1 teaspoon ground fennel seeds
1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
2 pounds ground pork

Directions:
In a small, bowl, combine the sage, salt, ground black pepper, marjoram, brown sugar, crushed red pepper, ground fennel and Italian seasoning; mix well.
Place the pork in a large bowl and add the mixed spices to it.
Using a rolling pin, roll out the sausage between 2 layers of waxed paper to a thickness of about 3/8 inch.
Cut out rounds with a 3 inch biscuit cutter.
Freeze rounds/patties individually in freezer wrap paper.
Let rounds/patties chill in refrigerqator overnight.
Saute the patties in a large skillet over medium high heat for 5 minutes per side, or until pork is well browned.
