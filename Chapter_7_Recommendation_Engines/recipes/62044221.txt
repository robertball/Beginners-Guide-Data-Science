Title:
Black Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package dried black beans,
1 can tomato sauce,
1 can tomato paste,
2 tblsp. chili powder,
1 tblsp. onion powder,
1/2 tsp. crushed red pepper,
3 cups of water.

Directions:
Soak black beans overnight,drain. Add to crockpot along with other ingredients. Cook on low for 10 hours. Enjoy!
