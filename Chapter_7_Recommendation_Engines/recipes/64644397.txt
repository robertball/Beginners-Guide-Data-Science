Title:
Jalapeno Popper Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium Chicken Thighs, diced
1 tbsp. Butter
3 medium Jalapenos, diced
2 tsp. Minced Garlic
1 tsp. Onion Powder
1 tsp. Dried Cilantro
1 tsp. Cajun Seasoning
3 cups Chicken Broth
8 oz. Cream Cheese
4 oz. Cheddar Cheese
1/4 c Heavy Cream
4 slices Bacon, for topping
Salt and Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Heat butter on the stove over medium-high heat.  Add diced jalapeno peppers and garlic to the mixture. Let this cook for 3-4 minutes or until soft.  Add broth and spices to the mixture and stir until pan is deglazed. Once boiling, reduce heat to simmer.  Add chicken to the pot.   Let this simmer for 10-15 minutes.  Add cream cheese, cheddar cheese and heavy cream to the soup and stir while simmering for 5 minutes, or until all cheese has dissolved.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Top with shredded cheddar and bacon crumbles, and serve!

Submitter:

Description: 
Just throw everything into a pot, and enjoy the delicious flavors that come out.
