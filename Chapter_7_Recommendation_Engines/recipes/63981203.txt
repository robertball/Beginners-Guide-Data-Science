Title:
Italian Roast Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole chicken
1 large fresh tomato
1 (15 oz.) can tomato sauce
1/2 tsp sugar
2 tsp dried basil
1 tsp thyme
6 cloves garlic, crushed

Directions:
Remove all visible fat from chicken, leaving skin
Season with salt/pepper
Rub interior with 1 crushed garlic clove
Place chicken on its back in baker.
Slice tomato in half horizontally and squeeze to remove seeds and juice. Chop and put around chicken.
Sprinkle with garlic, basil, thyme, sugar, salt/pepper
Pour tomato sauce over.
Cover and cook at 350' for 1 1/4 hours.
Remove lid and cook for another 15 minutes.
