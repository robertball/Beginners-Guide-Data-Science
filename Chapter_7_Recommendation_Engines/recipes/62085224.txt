Title:
Best Salsa Ever Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (15 oz) can of black beans, drained
3 cups tomatoes chopped
1 (8oz) can of corn, drained
1 onion chopped
1 garlic clove, finely chopped
1-3 jalapenos, finely chopped (amt. depends on how hot you like it)
1 avocado, chopped
juice of one lime
salt (to taste)
1/2 cup cilantro, chopped

Directions:
In a large bowl, place drained beans, tomatoes, corn, onion, garlic, jalapenos and avocadoes.  Mix well.  Add the lime juice, salt and cilantro. Mix again and taste.  Add more salt if needed. Serve with a sprig of cilantro on top and some blue corn chips to dip! Summer freshness on a chip!

Submitter: 
jmacsaunders
Description: 
Once you make you own salsa with fresh tomatoes eating jarred salsa never satisfies again.  Too easy to not make you won't regret it.
