Title:
PB and everything rollups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 whole wheat tortillas
4 T. Natural peanut butter
1/2 c. granola
1/2 thinly sliced apple
1/4 cup rasins
1 banana cut unto chunks

Directions:
spread 1 T. Peanut butter on each totilla.
add the desired fillings.
roll up and enjoy.

Submitter: 
mamaiscookin
Description: 
Put away that box of mac and cheese. Serve these tasty wholesome roll ups instead. Better yet, set out the ingredients and let the children put them together themselves.
