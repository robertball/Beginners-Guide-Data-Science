Title:
Apple Bacon Walnut Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981447.jpg
Ingredients:
12 pieces bacon, pre-cooked and crumbled
1 (16 ounce) package classic coleslaw mix
2 Granny Smith apples, diced
1 tablespoon caraway seeds
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 cup walnut pieces
1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese
1/2 cup Marzetti® Slaw Dressing

Directions:
Microwave the bacon according to directions to re-crisp. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients and toss. Chill 1 hour and serve. Sprinkle slaw with extra blue cheese if desired.

Submitter: 
Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Description: 
Crunchy walnuts and crisp bacon entice in this fabulous slaw, but the real star is the delicious apples!
