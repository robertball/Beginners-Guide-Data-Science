Title:
In Bianco Clam Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-9 CLOVES OF GARLIC, MINCED
1 LARGE ONION, MINCED
1/3 CUP OF OLIVE OIL
1/4 STICK OF BUTTER
1 TBLS OF FLOUR
4 CUPS OF CHICKEN BROTH
4 TBLS OF FRESHLY CHOPPED PARSELY
1 CAN OF WHOLE BABY CLAMS
SALT AND PEPPER TO TASTE
1/2-1 whole chicken bullion cube

Directions:
In a medium sized pot melt butter together with olive oil.
In oil mixture sautee garlic and onions until translucent.
Add flour and continue to cook until think, while constantly stirring.
Srain the can of clams adding the juice to the onion garlic mixture.
Then, depending on how much sauce you need, add 4-5 ladels of chicken broth or even more.  Stir until thick.  (if broth is not available, just boil one bullion cube in 4 cups of water)
After thick, add clams, salt, pepper, and fresh parsely.
Let mixture simmer for 5-10 minutes or until clams are cooked.
At this point if sauce is too think add more broth to thin ONLY IF NEEDED.
At the end crush 1/2 or 1 whole bullion cube and add to mixture for added flavor.
Serve over spaghetti and top with freshly grated parmesan cheese.

Submitter: 
Felicia Manocchio
Description: 
If you are tired of eating the same old pasta dish, this recipe will pep things up again.  Its easy and full of flavor.  With lots of garlic and fresh parsely.  It tastes the best with spaghetti.
