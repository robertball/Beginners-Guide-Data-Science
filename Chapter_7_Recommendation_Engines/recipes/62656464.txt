Title:
Washington Blueberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups Blueberries
1 1/3 cups Flour
1/2 teaspoon Baking Soda
1/4 teaspoon Salt
1/4 cup Butter, softened
3/4 cup Sugar
2 Eggs
1/2 teaspoon Vanilla extarct
3/4 cup Sour Cream

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450.
Sift together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
Beat the sugar and butter in a medium bowl until they are light and fluffy.
Beat in the eggs one at a time.
Add the vanilla.
Alternately fold in the flour mixture and the sour cream.
Fold in the blueberries
Distribute the mixture into a greased muffin tin. Bake at 450 degrees for 15 minutes.
Yield: 1 dozen

Description: 
These muffins are a recipe courtesy of Kyle D. Fulwiler, the former chef to the governor of Washington state. They are the most perfect delicate muffin I have ever had the pleasure of.
