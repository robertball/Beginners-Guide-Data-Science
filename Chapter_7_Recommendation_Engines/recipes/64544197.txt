Title:
Terrific Turkey Turnovers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3207329.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Pre-made pie crust
Half of a yellow onion chopped
8 oz Philadelphia Chive & Onion Cream Cheese Spread
3/4 cup frozen spinach
Garlic Powder
1/4 cup of mild (or to desired consistency)
1 lb ground turkey or beef
1 tablespoon of melted butter

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 375.
In a medium size pot,  saute onions and ground turkey until meat is fully cooked and onions are soft.
Add Chive & Onion cream cheese, milk, garlic powder, and frozen spinach to the pot.
Stir until well blended for about 10 minutes.
Unroll pie crust dough. Take one sheet of dough and trim into triangles or squares. Then take the other sheet of dough and cut each larger square into 4 smaller squares. Spoon filling onto the center of each squares. Fold over from corner to corner pressing the edges together to seal. Place turnovers on a baking sheet, leaving about 1 inch between them.
Glaze turnovers with butter.
Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven, until turnovers are puffed and lightly browned.
Cool for about 10 minutes before enjoying!
