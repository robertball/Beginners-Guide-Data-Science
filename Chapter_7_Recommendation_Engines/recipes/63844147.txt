Title:
Improved chicken pot pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1067858.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. chicken nuggets
1 pkg.  (16 oz.) frozen mixed vegetables (carrots, corn, green beans, peas), thawed, drained
1/2 lb.  (8 oz.) processed cheese melt cut into 1/2-inch cubes
2 cans  (10-3/4 oz.)condensed cream of chicken (or mushroom) soup
1 can  (8 oz.) refrigerated crescent dinner rolls

Directions:
Heat oven to 375ÂºF.
Combine first 4 ingredients in 13x9-inch baking dish.
Unroll dough; place over chicken mixture.
Bake 20 to 25 min. or until crust is golden brown.

Description: 
A new twist on an old favorite, chicken pot pie.  Use chicken nugget for ease and the processed cheese melt for a stick to your ribs meal you won't forget.
