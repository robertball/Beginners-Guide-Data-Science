Title:
Cookie Sheet Apple Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 3/4 c. all purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp. of salt
3/4 c. shortening
3 eggs, lightly beaten
1/3 c. milk
8 c. sliced, peeled tart baking apples
1 1/2 c. sugar
1 tsp. ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp. ground nutmeg
1 c. crushed cornflakes
1 egg white, lightly beaten

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
1.  In a large bowl, combine flour and salt.  Cut in shortening until mixture resembles course crumb.  Add eggs and milk; toss until moistened.  Cover and refrigerate for 20 minutes.
2.  Divide dough in half; so that one piece is slightly larger than the other.  On a lightly floured surface, roll out large half to fit the bottom and sides of a gresed 10x15x1 baking pan.
3.  Arrange apples over crust.
4.  Combine the sugar, cornflakes, cinnamon, and nutmeg.  Sprinkle over apples.
5. Roll out remaining dough to fit top of pan and place over apples.  Seal edges and cut slits in top.  Brush with egg white.
Bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes.  Reduce to 350 degrees and bake for 20-25 minutes longer or until golden brown.
