Title:
Frosty Orange Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/980054.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (4 serving size) JELL-O Brand Orange Flavor Gelatin
1 cup boiling water
2 cups vanilla ice cream, softened

Directions:
Dissolve gelatin in water. Gradually add ice cream, stirring with wire whisk until well blended.
Spoon evenly into 4 dessert cups, then refrigerate for 20 minutes or until chilled.

Submitter: 
JELL-O
Description: 
This creamy orange treat is perfect when you need a light dessert in a pinch.
