Title:
Chicken Paprikash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Slices bacon diced
2 medium onion, chopped
2 green bell pepper, chopped
1/2 Cup all pupose flour
4 Tablespoons paprika
1 teaspoon dried margoram
3 lbs boneless chicken thighs cut in half
4 cups chicken broth
1 cup sour cream
4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Directions:
Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Meanwhile cook the bacon chrispy and add vegtables over meadium heat for 3 more minutes Combine the flour paprika, marjoram and 1 teaspoon of salt in a larg bowl, add the chicken and toss to coat.
Push the bacon and vegetables to one side turn heat to med. high add the chicken brown, stir bacon and vegetable back with the chicken and add remaining flour.
Add Chicken broth and bring to boil stiring often. Turn heat to medum low Cover and simmer until chicken is cook through about 10 more minutes.
Uncover the pot with the chicken reduce the heat to low, stir in the sour cream and cook 2 more minutes. Season with salt and serve over buttered noodles.
