Title:
Paradise Island Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4431945.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup coconut flavored rum
1/2 cup orange juice
1/2 cup grapefruit juice
2 tablespoons frozen strawberries

Directions:
In a blender, combine coconut rum, orange juice, grapefruit juice and frozen strawberries. Blend until smooth. Pour into glass and serve.
In a cocktail mixer, combine coconut rum, orange juice and grapefruit juice. Mix well and pour into glasses. Garnish with frozen strawberries.

Submitter: 
Anonymous
Description: 
A very refreshing and tasty tropical drink. Add ice if you like.
