Title:
BLT Chicken Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups orzo pasta
2 cups, cubed chicken, precooked
2 cups halved grape tomatoes
2 cups thinly chopped spinach
1 bunch green onion
4 strips bacon, cooked CRISPY and crumbled
3 TBSP olive oil
3 TBSP white wine vinegar
2 TBSP Dijon mustard

Directions:
Cook orzo according to directions, rinse with cold water to cool, drain. Stir together all ingredients. Toss to coat pasta with oil, vinegar and mustard. Serve hot or cold!

Submitter: 
Ashley Russell
Description: 
I serve this with raspberries and blueberries in honey lavender yogurt for a easy weeknight meal high in anti-oxidants!
