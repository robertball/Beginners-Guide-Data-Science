Title:
Mock whipped cream frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 Tablespoons flour                                   1 cup milk                                             1 cup butter or shortening                            1 cup powdered sugar                                  1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
Shake flour and milk in a container with a tight fitting lid. heat on low heat in medium saucepan stirring constantly with a whisk. Boil one min., take off heat and cool, covered. Beat butter and sugar until smooth. Add vanilla and cooled flour mixture. beat until fluffy.

Submitter: 
MissMorland
Description: 
This frosting is really light and fluffy, it just takes longer to make than regular frosting, but it's worth it!
