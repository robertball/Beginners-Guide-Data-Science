Title:
Pork chops with sauerkraut in apple mustard sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork chops
medium onion
4 good sized potatoes cut into approx. 1 inch squares
1 can of sauerkraut rinsed and drained
3/4 cup of apple juice
1 rounded tablespoon of brown mustard
salt and pepper

Directions:
Place potatoes on bottom and sides of cooker. Layer sauerkraut, onions and finally chops. Sprinkle salt and pepper on chops. Mix juice and mustard together until well blended. Pour over chops. Cook on low up to 8 hours or on high for approx. 4 hours or until potatoes are tender.

Submitter: 
Carieannjean
Description: 
Tender pork chops with sauerkraut and potatoes made in the slow cooker. Easy and delicious, what more can you ask for?
