Title:
Mom's Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs (well beaten)
1/2 c. sugar
2 c. milk
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. vanilla
4 c. bread (day old and cubed)
1/4 c. raisins

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Beat sugar, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon into the beaten eggs
Spray a 6x9 baking dish with pam
Place bread cubes in a large mixing bowl; pour over custard mixture.
Let bread stand for 5 minutes or until well soaked.
Stir raisins into soaked bread.
Pour mixture into prepared dish.
Bake at 350 for about 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
Mom's simple, delicious bread pudding.  I used to love how the raisins got all plumped up when they baked.  Serve warm with vanilla ice cream or heavy cream.
