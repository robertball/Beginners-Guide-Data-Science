Title:
Deep Dish Pizza Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
40 oz. ground turkey
3/4 cup dry bread crumbs
1 cup Mozzarella cheese (divided)
1 cup pasta sauce (divided)
2 eggs
1 green pepper, chopped
1 envelope Lipton Recipe Secrets Beefy Onion Soup Mix
Pepperoni Slices

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Combine all ingredients except 1/2 cup sauce and 1/2 cup cheese. Press into 2 7"x11" casserole dishes. Freeze 1.  Bake the other one 50 minutes. Spread 1/2 cup pasta sauce on top, pepperoni, and remaining 1/2 cup cheese. Bake for 10 more minutes. Let stand 10 minutes.
