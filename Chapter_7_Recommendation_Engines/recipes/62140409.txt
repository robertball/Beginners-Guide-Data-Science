Title:
Mom's Hot German Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 baking potatoes
1/3 cup vinegar
2 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 pound bacon, chopped
6 eggs
3/4 cups chopped green onions and tops

Directions:
Cook potatoes in boiling water until done, then dice
Add vinegar and seasonings to diced potatoes
Fry bacon until crisp
Boil eggs until soft-cooked
Combine potatoes, drained bacon, 2 tablespoons bacon fat, soft-cooked eggs and chopped green onion
Mix well
Serve warm/hot

Submitter: 
Sharpshooter
Description: 
This is my favorite potato salad. Leftovers don't stack up, so eat all you can the first evening you serve it!
