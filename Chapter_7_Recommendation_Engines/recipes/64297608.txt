Title:
Lemon and Ginger Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1947037.jpg
Ingredients:
3 C. All purpose flour
2 tsp. Baking powder
1 Tbsp. Dry ginger
1/2 C. Butter, softened
1 C. Granulated sugar
2 Eggs
Juice and zest of 2 Lemons
1/2 C. Red River Cereal

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
Line a large cookie sheet with parchment paper
Blend flour, b. powder and ginger together in a small bowl
In a large mixing bowl cut butter into small pieces and blend with sugar to make a smooth paste
With a grater zest lemons and ream lemon juice from them in a small bowl
Add eggs to butter/sugar mixture and beat with an electric mixer on medium speed until light and creamy
Mix lemon juice and zest into creamed mixture and continue to beat on medium speed. Dough will have pudding like consistency
Add cereal to mixture and blend all together
Stir in dry ingredients and add enough flour to make soft pliable dough that can be worked with your hands
Refrigerate dough for 20 minutes to allow dough to firm up before rolling out
Roll out refrigerated dough on a lightly floured surface to 1/2" thickness and cut out shapes with a lid or cookie cutter and place on parchment paper 2 inches apart and bake for 10 to 12 minutes in preheated oven
Remove from oven and place on wire rack to cool completely before storing in a container

Submitter: 
Cheerios
Description: 
A very chewy cookie with a spicy flavour and whole grain cereal to add fibre to to this light cookie
