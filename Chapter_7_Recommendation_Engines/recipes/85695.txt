Title:
High Roller Rum Runner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992866.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 (10 fluid ounce) can BACARDI® Mixers Rum Runner frozen mix
1/2 cup BACARDI® Gold Rum
5 cups ice
Oranges and cherries for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Rum Runner mix, BACARDI(R) Gold and ice for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Garnish with orange slices and cherries, skewered together.

Submitter: 
BACARDI® Mixers
Description: 
This is a great blended drink for cooling off on a hot day. Fruity and smooth, garnished with oranges and cherries.
