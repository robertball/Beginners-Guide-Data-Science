Title:
Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 cups fat free half and half
1 cup Splenda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon peppermint extract
3 drops green food coloring (optional)
1 cup Andes Creme de Menthe chips

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir together the half and half, Splenda, salt, vanilla extract and peppermint extract until the sugar has dissolved. Color to your liking with the green food coloring.
Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker, and freeze according to the manufacturer's instructions. After about 10 minutes into the freezing, add the Andes chips. After the ice cream has thickened, about 30 minutes later, spoon into a container, and freeze for 2 hours.
