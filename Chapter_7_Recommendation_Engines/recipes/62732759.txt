Title:
Key Lime Pie IV edit Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) can fat free sweetened condensed milk
8 tablespoons fresh lime juice
1 TBS lime zest
1 cup heavy cream, whipped and sweetened
1 (9 inch) pie shell, baked

Directions:
Combine milk, lime juice, zest.  This mixture will thicken.
Fold whipped cream into lime mixture.  Pour into pie shell.  Chill.

Submitter: 
Colleen Smith
Description: 
This easy-to-make and refreshing key lime pie takes just minutes to make. When the juices are added to the milk everything thickens. This lovely filling is poured into a prepared crust and chilled.Modified from: Key Lime Pie IV
