Title:
Quick and easy Oatmeal Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/285250.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs
2/3 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup vegetable oil
1/2 cup milk
1 cup all purpouse flour
3/4 cup instant oatmeal
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 pinch of salt

Directions:
Pre heat over to 400-425 degrees.
Mix in the eggs, brown sugar, oil and milk in a medium size bowl until nice and smomth. Add in the flour, oatmeal, baking soda, baking bowder and salt.
You can pour in medium size muffin pan (12), mini loaf pan (8) or 1 large loaf pan (1).
Bake for 20 mins or until golden brown on top.
