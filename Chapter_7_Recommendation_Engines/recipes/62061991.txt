Title:
Scrumtious Cheesy Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb ground beef, thawed
1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce
1 egg, beaten
1 c. dry bread crumbs
2 Tbsp. grated onions
1 red or green pepper, chopped
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/4 tsp. thyme
6 slices cheese (any kind you like)
SAUCE TOPPING:
1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce
4 Tbsp. brown sugar
3 tsp. ground mustard

Directions:
Combine thawed beef, 1 can tomato sauce, egg, bread crumbs, onion, pepper and seasonings.  Mix well.  Lightly pack half of beef mixture into a meat loaf pan.  Arrange three of the cheese slices on top.  Pack the remainder of the beef into the pan, and top with three slices of cheese.  Cover with foil and bake at 350 for about 50 mins.  Meanwhile, combine the other can of tomato sauce, brown sugar, and ground mustard in a small bowl.  Remove foil and pour sauce over meatload, then bake for an additional 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Traveling_Is_Love
Description: 
Meatloaf with a medley of flavors as well as cheese throughout and on top.  A sauce on top adds to the flavor.
