Title:
Low-Fat Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/718951.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup cornmeal
2 tablespoons sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 egg whites
1 cup skim milk
1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder and salt. In another bowl, combine egg whites, milk and applesauce. Stir into dry ingredients just until moistened. Pour into a 9-in. square baking pan coated with nonstick cooking spray. Bake at 400 degrees F for 15-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Heather Andersen
Description: 
'I love corn bread, and I think this version is much better than traditional ones,' remarks Heather Andersen of Norfolk, Nebraska. 'Served warm, it's moist yet low in fat,' she promises.
