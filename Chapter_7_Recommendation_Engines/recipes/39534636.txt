Title:
Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 large red potatoes
1 lb velvetta cheese
2 cans cream of celery soup
1 can chicken broth
1 Tbl onion minced
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp paprika
2 pints half & half or whole milk

Directions:
Boil potato and reserve water to thin soup if needed.
In soup kettle add
velvetta cubed, celery soups, chicken broth, and seasonings
cut up potatoes and remove some skins
add potatoes to soup carefully
add milk or cream
heat slowly, do not boil
add some potato water if too thick

Rosemary Mahoney Bement

Okay, If you're Irish, you have to love this soup.  Talk about comfort food.
