Title:
PB&J; Pretzel Sancks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
pretzels
3/4 cup any berry flavored pancake syrup
1 cup peanut or cashew butter

Directions:
Put nut butter and syrup in a microwave safe bowl.
Microwave for 10 seconds
Mix with butter knife
Use butter knife to spread mixture on pretzels
Place a second pretzel on top of each one to create little sandwiches

Submitter:

Description: 
Salty, sweet, crunchy, smooth, quick, easy snack for all times of the day!
