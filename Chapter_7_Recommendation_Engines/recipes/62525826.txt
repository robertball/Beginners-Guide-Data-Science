Title:
Apple Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz cream cheese
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
You may want to place cream cheese in a bowl and soften in the microwave first before adding remaining ingredients.
Use hand mixer to blend until smooth.
Serve with apple slices and nuts, if desired.

Submitter: 
Justin Will
