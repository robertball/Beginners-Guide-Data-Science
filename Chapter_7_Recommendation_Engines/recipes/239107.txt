Title:
Smoked Sausage White Bean Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7208268.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (14 ounce) package Hillshire Farm® Smoked Sausage, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1 cup chicken broth
2 (15.8 ounce) cans great Northern beans, drained, rinsed
1 (4.5 ounce) can chopped green chilies, drained
2 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon ground cumin

Directions:
Brown sausage in 4-quart saucepan over medium-high heat for 5 minutes. Add broth; stir to incorporate any browned bits on the bottom of pan.
Add remaining ingredients.  Bring to boil; reduce heat, simmer uncovered for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Hillshire Farm® Brand
Description: 
This zesty white bean chili with chopped smoked sausage is ready to serve in 20 minutes.
