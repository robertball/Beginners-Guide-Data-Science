Title:
Waldorf Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4562396.jpg
Ingredients:
2 celery stalks, finely chopped
1/4 cup raisins, chopped
1 pound of apples, chopped into 1/4 inch pieces
1/4 cup walnuts, chopped
1 Tablespoon minced parsley
2 Tablespoons mayonnaise
3 Tablespoons plain Greek yogurt
1 Tablespoon lemon juice (juice of 1/2 a lemon)
1 teaspoon sugar

Directions:
DRESSING: whisk mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, lemon juice, and sugar in a bowl.
Toss with rest of ingredients and serve chilled.

Submitter: 
FoodRight's Youth Chef Academy
Description: 
An updated version of this classic salad made up of celery, raisins, apples, and walnuts drizzled with a creamy yogurt dressing.
