Title:
Basic Quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/507227.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup finely chopped yellow onion
olive oil
4 eggs
1 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 cup shredded swiss cheese
1 (9 inch) unbaked pie shell

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. In a small skillet saute onion in a little oil until tender, set aside to cool.
In a medium mixing bowl whisk together eggs, milk, mustard, salt, and pepper. Stir in cooked onion and cheese. Pour mixture into pie shell and bake until knife inserted into the center comes out clean, about 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Cupcake Princess
Description: 
Here is a great easy recipe for quiche. You can add lots of different things to make it your very own. If you don't have any swiss cheese you can just use some cheddar.
