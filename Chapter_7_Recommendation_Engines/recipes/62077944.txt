Title:
Moist Banana Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 med Bananas (Riped and mashed)
4 c sugar
4 eggs
1 c sour cream
2 tsp. Vanilla
1 1/3 c veg oil
4 c flour
1 1/2 tsp salt
2 1/2 baking soda
1 c sugar
Cinnamon to taste
(Opt) Nuts to taste

Directions:
In Extra-large bowl combine bananas, sugar, eggs, sour cream, vanilla, and veg oil. Slowly mix in the flour, salt and baking soda. Pour into greased pan. Cook on 325 for approx 1 hr 15 min or until baked through. Cake will turn dark brown. Cool for 5 minutes. Combine cinnamon and sugar. Coat top and bottom of cake with cinnamon sugar. Serve warm.
