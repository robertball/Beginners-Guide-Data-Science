Title:
Suzanne's Shrimp Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 green pepper,chopped
1/2 red pepper,chopped
1/2 yellow pepper,chopped
1/2 red onion,chopped
6 sweet gherkins,chopped
1 box elbow macaroni, cooked, drained and cooled
2 pkg shrimp salad
1 C. mayonnaise
2 T. vinegar
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 tsp sugar

Directions:
Mix peppers, onion, shrimp, and pickles in a bowl with the chilled macaroni. In a small bowl mix mayonnaise, vinegar, salt and pepper together. Mix dressing in with salad.
