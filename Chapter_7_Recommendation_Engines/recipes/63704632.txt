Title:
BBQ Chicken Bubble Up Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1030781.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cans (12oz) Grands Jr. flaky biscuits
1 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
2 cups chopped cooked chicken
1/3 cup chopped bacon
1 cup Cheddar cheese, shredded
1 cup Mozzarella cheese, shredded
Green onions to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.
Cut biscuits into quarters and place in medium bowl.
Toss biscuits with BBQ, chicken, bacon, and half of the cheese until well blended.
Grease 9x13 pan.
Pour biscuit mixture into pan.
Bake for 30-35 minutes.
Top with remaining cheese and green onions immediately after removing from oven.
