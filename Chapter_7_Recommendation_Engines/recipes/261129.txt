Title:
Parsley-Mushroom Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5147993.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons chicken drippings
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup canned sliced mushrooms, drained
1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat chicken drippings in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add flour and cook, stirring, until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes. Add chicken broth; bring to a boil. Cook, stirring, until gravy thickens, 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in mushrooms, parsley, salt, and pepper.

Submitter: 
dreyerfan
Description: 
I've had this delicious gravy recipe for so many years that I can't remember where I found it. My family loves it and requests it every holiday. It's so easy and delicious that I make it whenever I roast chicken or turkey. Serve over hot rice or mashed potatoes.
