Title:
Pepper Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large red bell pepper
1 large green bell pepper
1 large yellow bell pepper
1 large onion
1 lb of penne pasta
3 tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp salt
1 tbsp sugar
3 tbsp balsamic or red wine vinegar
3/4 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp pepper
1 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 cup shredded mozarella cheese

Directions:
Cook penne pasta al dente, drain and set aside.
Cut peppers and onion into 1/2 inch strips.
Heat olive oil in 12 inch skillet over medium heat. Add pepper, onion and salt. Cook for approximately 10 minutes, or until vegetables are tender. Stir in sugar, vinegar, basil and pepper. Add cheese and stir til melted. Toss pepper mixture into drained penne and serve.

Submitter: 
Kerleygirl
Description: 
Easy pasta dish, great with chicken.  The leftovers are good cold.
