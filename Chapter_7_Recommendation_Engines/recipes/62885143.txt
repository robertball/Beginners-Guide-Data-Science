Title:
Sloppy Joe's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground sirloin
2/3 cup chopped onion
1 tbsp flour
1 cup water
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
3/4 cup ketchup

Directions:
Brown sirloin and onion, drain the grease. Add flour, salt, pepper. Stir really well to incorporate flour.
Add water, worcestershire and ketchup. Simmer for 20 minutes.
