Title:
Old-fashioned Macaroni and Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 C. dry elbow macaroni
4 C. Skim Milk
3 Tbsp. melted butter
1/2 tsp. salt
8 oz. shredded sharp cheddar cheese
2 Tbsp. bread crumbs
1 Tbsp. Parmesan cheese, grated.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Pour dry macaroni into casserole dish.
Add salt, melted butter and shredded cheese.
Stir in milk.
Cover with foil, and place in preheated oven for 1 hour.
Remove foil.
Sprinkle with combination of bread crumbs and parmesan cheese.
Return to oven and broil about 2 min., or until browned.

Submitter: 
Tanya Belt
Description: 
This isn't a creamy commercial style mac and cheese. Instead, it's a comforting back to basics meal that comes together in a snap.  Enjoy!
