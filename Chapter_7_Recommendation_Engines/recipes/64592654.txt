Title:
Fresh Lemon Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup sugar
2 tbsp. flour
3 eggs
1 cup milk
1 large lemon, juiced and peel finely grated
1 tbsp. butter, softened

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender.
Mix until smooth.
Pour into a 9" ungreased pie plate.
Place in center of 325 oven.
Bake x 30 - 40 min. or more, until pudding seems set when slightly jiggled.
Cool for 10 minutes before serving. Can also be served chilled.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Quick, easy custard dessert with a distinct lemon flavor. Mixed in a blender; baked in a pie plate.
I like to serve this with some raspberry sorbet and fresh raspberries. But use your imagination!
