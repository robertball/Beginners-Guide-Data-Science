Title:
Buffalo Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 rotisserie chicken, shredded
2 tbsp. butter
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped onion
28 oz. chicken broth
1 1/2 cups milk
bottled hot sauce to taste
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella
1 1/2 cups crumbled blue cheese
1 1/2 cups shredded parm
1/3 cup all-purpose flour

Directions:
1. In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Add celery and onion, cook until onion is tender. Stir in broth, milk and hot sauce (about 1 tbsp.)
2. Toss cheeses together with flour, reserving 1/2 cup of blue cheese for topping. Add gradually to soup, stirring after each addition, until just melted. Stir in chicken and heat through. Top with remaining cheese and more hot sauce, if desired.
