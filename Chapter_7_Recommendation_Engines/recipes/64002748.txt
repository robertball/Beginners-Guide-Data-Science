Title:
Spinach Basil Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb lean ground beef (or split with turkey)
1 cup milk
1 tbs Worcestershire sauce
2 tsp fresh chopped basil
1 package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeeze drained
1/4 c toasted pinenuts or almonds
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp dry ground mustard
1/4 tsp pepper
1 clove finely chopped garlic
1/2 c ground parmesan
1 large egg
1/2 cup italian breadcrumbs
1 small chopped onion
1/2 c steak sauce

Directions:
Heat oven to 350.
Mix all ingredients except steak sauce. Spread mixture in ungreased loaf pan. Spread steak sauce over top.
Bake uncovered 1 to 1.25 hours until thermometer reads 160 degrees.
Let stand for 5 minutes, remove from pan.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a variation of a small-print alternate to a standard meatloaf recipe in an old family favorite cookbook. I added some fresh ingredients and it is absolutely one of my favorite recipes. Juicy, flavorful, amazing. YUM!
