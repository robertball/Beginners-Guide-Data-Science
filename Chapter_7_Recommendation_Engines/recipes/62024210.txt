Title:
Sloppy Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground chicken
1 1/2 tbsp. vegetable oil
1/2 chopped onion
1/4 cup sweet peppers (canned or fresh)
1 tsp salt
1 tsp minced garlic
dash of hot pepper sauce (optional)
1 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce
4 kaiser rolls

Directions:
Sautee onions in oil.  If the peppers are fresh add immediately, if canned add after 2 minutes.  Once onions are softened add ground chicken, salt, garlic.  Sautee until browned.  Add BBQ sauce and hot pepper sauce and cook an additional 2 minutes.  Serve on kaiser roll and enjoy!

Submitter:

Description: 
A healthy and quick- yet very flavorful- version of the American favorite sloppy joe sandwich.
