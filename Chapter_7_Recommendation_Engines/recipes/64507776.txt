Title:
No Fat Chocolate Zucchini Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 egg whites
1 cup unsweetened applesauce
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 tbsp vanilla extract
2 cups shredded zucchini (peeled or not)
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup whole-wheat flour
1/2 cup baking cocoa
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp baking powder

Directions:
Combine and beat egg whites, applesauce, sugar and vanilla extract.
Stir in the zucchini.
Mix all of the dry ingredients and add to wet mixture and mix very well.
Pour into a sprayed 9x13 pan or 2 loaf pans.
Bake 9x13 for about 35 minutes in preheated oven until done or 2 loaves for about an hour.
Frost or sprinkle with powdered sugar, if desired.
