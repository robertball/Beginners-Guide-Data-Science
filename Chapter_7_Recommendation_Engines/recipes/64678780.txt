Title:
Momma's (The BEST) Jalepeno Cranberry Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4812048.jpg
Ingredients:
1 - 12oz bag fresh cranberries
1 - tsp lemon zest - finely minced
1 1/8 cup granulated sugar
2 oz orange marmalade
Juice of one medium orange  or 1/4 cup OJ
1/4 tsp cinnamon or 1 cinnamon stick
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1 large jalapeno pepper seeded and finely minced

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a medium sauce pan.  Cook over medium heat  for 15  minutes, stirring often to prevent sticking.  Lower heat to med low and continue cooking/stirring another 15  minutes until sauce reduces slightly and begins to thicken.
Spoon into a serving dish and cool to near room temperature.   Refrigerate at least  30 minutes before serving.
