Title:
Salmon & Shrimp Cakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2Tablespoon mayonnaise
2Tablespoon dijon mustard
3Tablespoon worcesteshire sauce
2tsp old bay seasoning
2Tablespoon red onion diced small and sauteed
1Tablespoon green pepper diced small and sauteed
1 lb salmon fillets, cut & seasoned with old bay
10 large fresh shrimp, peeled, deveined, cut into threes and seasoned w/ old bay
4Tablespoons butter
1 cup Panko Breadcrumbs
1/2 Tablespoon garlic salt

Directions:
Mix 1st six ingredients together in a medium bowl and set aside.
Take 1 Tablespoon of the butter and sautee the salmon and shrimp - cooking partially - Place in large bowl
Melt 3 Tablespoons of butter in the microwave, stir in breadcrumbs, season with garlic salt
Mix in wet ingredients 1 tablespoon at a time to moisten the salmon & shrimp mixture
Add breadcrumb mixture 1 tablespoon at a time - enough to bind
Form mixture into 2-4 inch rounds.
Place parchment paper on baking sheet
Broil salmon & shrimp cakes at 550 for 7-10 minutes or until golden brown.
