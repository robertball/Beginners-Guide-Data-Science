Title:
Pizzawiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C shredded cheddar cheese divided
1 lb ground beef
8 hamburger buns
1 can tomatoe soup
1 sm onion finely chopped
1/2 c chopped mushrooms (canned or fresh work fine)

Directions:
lay bun 1/2s on cookie sheet (16 1/2s) and toast under broiler until light brown
brown beef and onions and drain
fold in tomatoe soup and mushrooms
fold in 1/2 cheese to a thick mixture
spread a spoonful on each bun
top with more cheese
broil until bubbly and brown

Submitter: 
Pam Rosterman
Description: 
This is a recipe I made based on the memory of my favorite school lunch... my kids love this even now that they are grown. It's perfect with a green tossed salad.  For heaty appitites it may feed only 4
