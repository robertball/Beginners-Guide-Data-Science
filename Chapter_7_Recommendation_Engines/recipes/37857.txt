Title:
Swamp Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976625.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups boiling water
1 (6 ounce) package JELL-O Brand Lime Flavor Gelatin
2 cups cold orange juice
1 liter cold seltzer
ice cubes
1 pint orange sherbet, slightly softened
1 orange, thinly sliced
1 lime, thinly sliced

Directions:
Stir boiling water into gelatin in large bowl at least 2 minutes until completely dissolved.  Stir in orange juice.  Cool to room temperature.
Pour into punch bowl just before serving.  Add cold seltzer and ice cubes.  Place scoops of sherbet and fruit slices in punch.
