Title:
Cucumbers from "THE MANOR" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 cups  cucumbers- peeled and thinly sliced--(I use a mandolin)
1 Tablespoon white sugar
1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
1/2 cup Miracle Whip Salad Dressing-(mayo if you prefer)
1 teaspoon black pepper-(i use coarse ground,freshly ground)

Directions:
1) peel and slice cucumbers- set aside
2)place sugar,Miracle Whip,vinegar and pepper  in a quart jar with tight fitting lid( I use a canning jar)-  shake well.
3) add cucumbers- shake lightly- store in refrigerator
4) chill at least one hour-( i begin by inverting the jar- then turning right side up after 30 minutes)

quick and easy side dish- even better the second day.
