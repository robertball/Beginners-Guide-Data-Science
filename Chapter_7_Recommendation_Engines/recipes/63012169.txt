Title:
Italian Cookies- Zap Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1. 1 cup butter softened
2. 1 cup sugar
3. 2 eggs beaten
4. 3 cups flour
5. 1 tsp baking soda
6. 1/2 tsp salt
7. 1/2 of a 6 ounce can of concentrated frozen lemonade thawed

Directions:
Cream 1. 2. add 3. 4. 5. 6. 7. and mix well
Drop by teas unto Greased cookie sheet
Bake 8-10 min 375*
when cool ice the cookies with a sugar glaze icing
add sprinkles or food coloring to tint the frosting and make the cookie tray festive

Submitter: 
Gina Lee Lafreniere
Description: 
nice and moist ...the lemonade concentrate really is the secret
