Title:
Campbell's Kitchen Tuna Noodle Casserole With A Crunch Factor Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell'sÂ® Condensed Cream of Celery Soup or Red Fat version
1/2 cup milk
2 tablespoons chopped pimentos (optional)
1 cup frozen peas
1 can water chestnuts, sliced
3 stalks of celery, sliced
2 (6 ounce) cans tuna, drained and flaked
2 cups hot cooked medium egg noodles
2 tablespoons cracker crumbs or panko
1 tablespoon butter, melted

Directions:
Mix soup, milk, pimiento, peas, water chestnuts, celery,tuna and noodles in 1 1/2-quart casserole.  Bake at 400 degrees F for 20 - 30 minutes or until hot.  Stir.
Mix crumbs with butter and sprinkle on top.  Bake 5 minutes or until crumbs are starting to brown a bit.
