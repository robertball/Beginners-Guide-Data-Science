Title:
Ham Bone Soup II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large ham bone
2 (14.5 ounce) cans of diced or stewed tomatoes italian style with juice
5 cans chicken broth
1/2 tsp basil
1 bay leaf
1/2 tsp oregano
1 pinch of rosemary
1 small pkg frozen limas
2 potatoes, cubed
3 stalks celery, sliced
3 carrots, sliced
1 onion, diced

Directions:
Place all the ingredients into a large stockpot.
Cook on high until it comes to a boil.
Reduce heat to low and cook for 3-4 hours.
Remove ham bone and bayleaf.  Cool, skim any grease or fat that rises to the top from the bone.

Submitter: 
KSBEESON
Description: 
Perfect comfort food soup from your leftover ham bone and common items in your kitchen.  Regular or honey baked ham bones will work in this soup. The hambone yields a salty broth, so if you choose, you can use low sodium chicken broth.
