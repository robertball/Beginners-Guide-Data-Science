Title:
Broccoli, Corn and Cauliflower Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T. butter (divided)
2 T. flour
1/4 T. salt
1 1/2 C. milk
1/2 C. (6 oz) Velveeta
3/4 C. cornflake crumbs (divided) (not to be confused with bread crumbs, I typically use "Kellogg's corn flake crumbs")
1 - 12 oz. can corn; drained
15 oz. frozen broccoli spears
5 oz. frozen cauliflower spears

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350.
Melt 2 tbsp butter in a sauce pan.  Stir in flour and salt.  Add milk gradually, stirring until smooth.  Cook until boils.  Add cheese and continue stirring until the cheese has melted.  Mix in 1/4 C. cornflake crumbs and corn.
Arrange broccoli, corn and cauliflower in 11 3/4 x 7 1/2 dish.  Pour cheese sauce over broccoli mixture.
Melt 2 tbsp butter and stir in 1/2 C. remaining crumbs.  Sprinkle mixture over top of broccoli mixture.
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
