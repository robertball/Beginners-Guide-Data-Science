Title:
Peanut Butter Banana Berry Oat Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4473997.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup Quaker® Oats (Quick or Old Fashioned, uncooked)
1/2 frozen banana
1/2 cup blueberries
2 tablespoons peanut butter
1/2 cup low-fat milk
1 tablespoon honey
1/2 cup ice

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
Garnish with additional sliced bananas and oats, if preferred.

Submitter: 
Quaker®
Description: 
Oats, fruit, peanut butter, milk, and honey are blended together for a quick smoothie on a busy morning.
