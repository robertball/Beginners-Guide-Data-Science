Title:
Maple Caramel Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 slices cinnamon bread
2 whole eggs
1 egg white
1/3 c sugar
1 1/2 c low fat milk
1/2 c maple syrup
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
1/4 tsp salt
6 tblsp fat free caramel topping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350.  Grease 8x8 glass baking dish.
Cut bread into 3/4" cubes; arrange in dish.
Beat eggs and sugar in medium bowl.  Beat in milk, syrup, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt.  Pour evenly over bread.
Bake approximately 40 minutes until light browned on top and middle is set.
Warm caramel 20 seconds in microwave.  Drizzle over bread pudding and serve.

Description: 
A cinnamon bread pudding topped with warm caramel.  Quick to prepare and so delicious.
