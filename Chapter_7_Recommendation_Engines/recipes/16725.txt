Title:
Grandpa Crotts BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/598390.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 1/2 cups ketchup
1 onion, chopped
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
2 tablespoons prepared mustard
1/8 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Place the oil, ketchup, onion, lemon juice, mustard, hot pepper sauce and Worcestershire sauce in a medium saucepan over low heat. Mix well and allow to simmer for 30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Shannon Crotts
Description: 
This is a wonderful BBQ sauce. Works well on any beef, pork or chicken.
