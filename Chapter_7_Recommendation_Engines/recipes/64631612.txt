Title:
Curry Ketchup Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
160 grams pork, thinly sliced
1/2 piece bitter melon
1/3 piece carrot
1/4 piece onion, sliced
1 clove garlic, minced
1 teaspoon curry powder
3 tablespoon tomato ketchup
pinch of salt
pinch of pepper
1 tsp sesame oil

Directions:
Cut the bitter melon along its length then take the seeds at the center using a spoon. Slice thinly crosswise then soak in water with salt for at least 5 minutes. Drain before using.
Slice the carrot into thin strips. Slice the onion and mince the garlic.
In a small bowl, combine curry powder, tomato ketchup, salt, pepper, and sesame oil and mix well.
In a large bowl, toss all the ingredients together and mix well. Cover then put in the microwave to cook for 4 minutes using 600W.
Take out the bowl from the microwave then open and mix. Return to the microwave then cook for 1-2 more minutes until the the pork is well done.
Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Pearl Ishizaki
Description: 
This recipe is one of the easiest thing you can do with a pork. Tomato ketchup is already rich in flavor so this dish doesn’t require much unless you want to put more vegetables. To add something green in this dish, I’ve added bitter melon.
