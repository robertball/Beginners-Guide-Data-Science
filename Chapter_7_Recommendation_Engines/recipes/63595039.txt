Title:
Chicken and Dumpling Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped celery
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup flour
2 tsp sugar
1 tsp salt
1 tsp dried basil
1/2 tsp pepper
4 cups chicken broth
1 package (10 oz) frozen green pees
4 cups cubed cooked chicken
DUMPLINGS
2 cups buttermilk biscuit mix
2 tsp dried basil
2/3 cup milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
In a large saucepan, saute onion, celery and garlic in butter until tender.
Add flour, sugar, salt, basil, pepper and broth; bring to a boil.
Coo, and stir for 1 minute; reduce heat.
Add peas and cook for 4 minutes, stirring constantly.
Stir in chicken.
Pour into greased 9 X 13 X 2 baking dish.
For dumplings, combine biscuit mix and basil in a bowl.
Stir in milk with a fork until moistened.
Drop by tablespoons onto casserole (12 dumplings).
Bake, uncovered, for 30 minutes.
Cover and bake 10 minutes more or until dumplings are done.
