Title:
mustard rosemary porkchops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4c. prepared mustard
2 tbs. olive oil
2 tbs. lemon juice
1 6 in. sprig rosemary
1 4 in. sprig thyme
4-5 sage leaves (1 tsp dried)
1 tsp. lemon zest
1/2 tsp. black pepper.
1 lb. boneless pork chops

Directions:
combine in a zip top bag first eight ingredients then add pork chops and marinade for at least 2 hours or up to 24.
cook pork chops on a grill or in a skillet with a little olive oil until thoroughly cooked (the meat should reach an internal temperature of 155F for at least 15 seconds).
Serve with herbed  fried potatoes, and a small salad tossed with a light vinaigrette.

Submitter: 
Caleb1993
Description: 
pork chops marinated with a blend of prepared mustard, olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, rosemary, sage, thyme, and black pepper.
