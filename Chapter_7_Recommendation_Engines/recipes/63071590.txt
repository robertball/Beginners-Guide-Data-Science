Title:
Gulaschsuppe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/886791.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup bacon grease, filtered
2 cups onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
6 cups V-8 juice
3 beef bouillon cubes
1 pound lean top sirloin beef - cut into 1 inch cubes
1 pinch red pepper flakes
2 teaspoon paprika
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/4 teaspoon caraway seeds
1 cup sour cream (optional)

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
Saut√© onions until transparent, then stir in bell peppers, add V-8 juice and bouillon cubes.
Put into crockpot.
Brown beef in skillet, then stir in the red pepper, paprika, garlic, salt, lemon juice and caraway seeds. Add to crockpot and simmer for 8 hours, or until meat is tender.
If adding sour cream, then, fold in and adjust seasoning.
Serve over spaetzel.
