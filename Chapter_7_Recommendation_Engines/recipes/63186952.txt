Title:
Chicken & Apple slow cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 peeled apple
4 celery stalks
1/2 onion chopped
1 container of chicken broth
1 zest of lemon
1 lemon juice (from left over zest of lemon)
dash lemon pepper seasoning
dash rosemary
dash cilantro
dash salt
4 chicken breasts - boneless
Layer apple and celery on bottom
add chicken
add the remaining ingredients
toss
cook (frozen chicken) 7 hours
