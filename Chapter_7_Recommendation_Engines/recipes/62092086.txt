Title:
Sour Cream Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 pkg taco seasoning
8 oz. sour cream
1 can cream of chicken soup
8 flour tortillas
2 c. shredded cheese

Directions:
Brown hamburger.  Add half of taco seasoning and 2-3 T. water.  Mix well and set aside.
Mix sour cream, soup, and rest of taco seasoning.
Spread about 1/4 c. of sour cream mixture in bottom of greased 9x13 pan.
Divide hamburger between tortillas.  Roll and place in pan in a neat row.
Pour rest of sour cream mixture over tortillas.  Top with cheese.
Bake at 350 for 20 min or until cheese is melted.
May serve with lettuce, tomato, taco sauce, or any other taco toppings desired.

Submitter: 
Michelle Bomgaars
Description: 
This has become a family favorite, even for those who don't like Mexican or spicy foods.  I have also used chicken or imitation crab meat to give it a different flavor.
