Title:
cheese and broccoli soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs (1 large bunch) fresh broccoli cut into bite-size pieces
1 pint half and half
2 cups water
1 lb. pasteurized cheese cut into 2" cubes
3/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper (white if you have it)
1/2 cup cornstarch mixed in 1 cup cold  water

Directions:
Steam broccoli until tender, about 3 to 4 minutes. do not over cook. Set aside. Place half and half, 2 cups water, cubed cheese, and seaonings in top of a double boiler set over 2 to 3 inches of simmering water. Cook stirring occasionaly until cheese is melted. If pan is large enough, add cooked broccoli and the cornstarch mixture, cooking and stirring until soup is hot and thickened. Otherwise, transfer cheese mixture to a large heavy bottom pan, then add broccoli and cornstarch mixture. To prevent scorching during the stove top method, cook soup over very low heat until thickened. Serve hot

Submitter: 
Alice Mayo Edelhauser
Description: 
This was given to me by my step-mother about 25 years ago. It is the creamiest cheese broccoli soup I have ever eaten. It could probably be used as a sauce also. It goes well with a sandwich or eat it alone. I like to eat potato chips with it. Yum! This freezes very well.
