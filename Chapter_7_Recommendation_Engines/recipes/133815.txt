Title:
SARA LEE速 Egg in a Boat Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/803542.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 tablespoon butter
1 slice Sara Lee速 Soft and Smooth 100% Whole Wheat Bread
1 egg

Directions:
Butter both sides of bread. Cut a circular hole in the center of the slice of bread, about 2  inches in diameter.
Heat a frying pan over medium-high heat. When the frying pan is hot, place the bread into the pan and toast for 1 minute. Turn and toast  the other side for 1 minute.
Break the egg into the hole in the bread. Cook for 2 minutes, or until the egg is cooked to your preference.
