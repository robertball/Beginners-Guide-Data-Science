Title:
Easy Strawberry Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/657935.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Angel food cake cut into slices
1 cup boiling water
1 small (3oz) box strawberry Jello
1/2 cup strawberry jam
1 no bake box cheesecake mix
3 cups sliced strawberries
2 Tbs sugar
1 1/2 cups cold milk

Directions:
Slice strawberries sprinkle with sugar and set aside. Mix  together milk and cheesecake filling packet set aside.Mix boiling water together with jello stir until disolved and almost cooled, add jam and stir, chill 10 minutes. Im a large bowl, or 11x13 casserole pan line bottom with 1/3 og the sliced angel food cake, spread on 1/3 jello mixture. Spread 1/2 cheesecake, top with 1/3 of sliced strawberries. Repeat 2nd layer then place 3rd layer of angelfood cake spread with remaining jello and top with strawberries. cover and chill 1 hour. Serve with whipped cream
