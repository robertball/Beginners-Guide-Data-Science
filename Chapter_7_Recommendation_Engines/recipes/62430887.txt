Title:
Ceasar Salad Dressing (No Yolk) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon anchovy paste
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
1 clove garlic, minced
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 lemon
1/3 cup olive oil
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, or Romano
dash pepper

Directions:
In a blender, add anchovy paste, mustard, garlic, Worcestershire and lemon juice.
With blender running, slowly add olive oil to form dressing then stir in cheese and black pepper
