Title:
Stuffed Cabbage Rolls Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/962687.jpg
Ingredients:
1 large head cabbage
1 cup rice, cooked and cooled
1 lb ground beef
1 lb ground pork sausage
1 medium onion, chopped
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 can (10-3/4 ounces) condensed tomato soup
1 cup water

Directions:
Cook rice according to package directions and cool.
Cook cabbage in boiling water only until leaves loosen from head. Reserve 14-16 of the largest leaves and set aside remaining cabbage.
Preheat oven to 350.
In a bowl, combine rice, beef, sausage, onion, Worcestershire sauce, salt, pepper.
Cut up remaining cabbage and place in bottom of casserole dish.
Put 2 to 3 tablespoons meat mixture on each cabbage leaves and roll (see notes on how to roll) up leaves.
Lay cabbage rolls in casserole on top of the cut up cabbage. Place them roll side down and snuggled together.
Mix tomato soup and 1 cup of water together and pour over cabbage rolls in casserole dish.
Cook for 1-1/2 to 2 hours or until rolls are tender.
Serve with homemade mashed potatoes using the tomato soup as gravy over the potatoes and cabbage rolls.

A western Pennsylvanian staple when I was growing up and one I still love to make.
