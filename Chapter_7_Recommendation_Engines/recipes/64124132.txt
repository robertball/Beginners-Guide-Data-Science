Title:
Chicken Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 tsp Cayenne Powder
1/2 tsp Smoked Paprika
1/2 tsp Onion Powder
1/2 tsp fresh finely ground Black Pepper
2 cloves Garlic, minced
1 tsp McCormick's Fajita Seasoning
1/4 cup Olive Oil

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in sealable container (ziploc bag or tupperware work well.)
Add chicken to container, and seal well. Shake until contents are well mixed.

Submitter: 
EvilJekyll
Description: 
A great marinade for chicken that's grilled or cooked in cast iron.
