Title:
Turkey Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1 1/2 cups dried bread crumbs
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup milk
2 tablespoons chopped parsley
2 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons prepared mustard
2 teaspoons prepared horseradish
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 teaspoon dried marjoram
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
2 pounds ground turkey
1 tablespoon oil

Directions:
In a large bowl combine egg, bread crumbs, onion, milk, parsley, salt, mustard, horseradish, Worcestershire, marjoram, and pepper. Add turkey and mix thoroughly. Oil a 2 quart baking dish and pack turkey mixture into it. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
