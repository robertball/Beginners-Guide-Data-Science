Title:
Lazy Day Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 jar spaghetti sauce
4 large cloves of garlic
4 cups whole wheat egg noodles
1 cup ricotta cheese
3 Tbsp chopped fresh basil
1 cup mozzarella cheese
3 Tbsp Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Bring sauce, 2 cups water, and garlic to boil in large skillet. Stir in noodles. Cover.
Cook on medium-low heat for 12 minutes or until noodles are tender, stirring occassionally. Remove from heat. Stir.
Mix ricotta and basil. Drop spoonfuls over noodle mixture. Sprinkle with remaining cheeses. Cover. Let stand for 5 minutes
