Title:
Bourbon & Brown Sugar Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 TBL olive oil
1 pkg (10 oz) scoops tortilla chips
1 medium red bell pepper, coarsely chopped
1 lb boneless skinless chicken tenderloins, cut into bite sized pieces
1 cup finely shredded cheddar cheese
1 can bush's bourbon and brown sugar grillin' beans

Directions:
1. Heat oil in 10" skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add chicken and pepper. Cook for 5-7 mins or until internal temperature for chicken reaches 165 degrees and peppers are tender.
3. Reduce heat to medium-low. Stir in beans. Simmer 10 minutes. Remove from heat.
4. Place chips on serving plates. Carefully spoon beans mixture into each chip. Top with cheese. Serve hot.
