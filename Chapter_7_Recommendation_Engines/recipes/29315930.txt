Title:
Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3  cup shortening
2/3  cup butter or margarine
1  cup sugar
1  cup brown sugar, packed
2  large eggs
2  teaspoons vanilla
3  cups all-purpose flour
1  teaspoon baking soda
1  teaspoon salt
1 (12  ounce) package chocolate chips
1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Heat oven to 375 degrees.
Mix thoroughly shortening, butter, sugars, eggs and vanilla.
Stir in remaining ingredients. (For a softer, rounder cookie, add 1/2 cup flour.)
Drop by rounded teaspoon onto ungreased baking sheets.
Bake at 375Â° for 8-10 minutes or until golden brown.
Cool slightly before removing from cookie sheet.
