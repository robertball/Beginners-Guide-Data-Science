Title:
Crazy Good BBQ & Grill Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/662909.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 C. fresh lemon juice
1/2 C. vegetable oil
1/4 C. low soduim soy sauce
1/4 C. white sugar
1/4 C. coarse black pepper
1/4 C. dried minced onions
1/4 C. fresh chopped garlic
3 Tablespoons garlic powder
1 Tablespoon salt

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
Put meat in a large zip lock bag and add marinade, refrigerate and rotate as needed.

Submitter: 
Celeste
Description: 
This recipe will marinate 4 lbs. of meat. Great for pork tenderloin, tri-tip, roasts and steaks etc. Marinate steaks from 6-8 hours. Pork tenderloin from 8 to 24 hours. Tri-tip and roasts from 24 to 48 hours.
