Title:
Cherry Pie Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1/2 cup icing sugar
1 cup butter
1 30oz can cherry pie filling
1 250ml whipping cream, whipped with 1/4 cup sugar and vanilla

Directions:
Mix flour and icing sugar; cut in butter until mixture resembles course crumbs.
Press into an ungreased 9" x 13" pan.
Bake @ 350 x 18-20 min. or until lightly browned.
Cool completely on wire rack.
Spoon pie filling over crust; spread prepared whipped cream over pie filling.
Store in fridge.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Shortbread, cherries and whipped cream in an easy dessert that serves a dozen or more. Of course, you could substitute other flavors of pie fill if you want a change.
