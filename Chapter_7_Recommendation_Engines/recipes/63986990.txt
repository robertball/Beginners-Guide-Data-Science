Title:
Corn Chowder Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1108795.jpg
Ingredients:
6 Strips of Bacon (NOT "Flavored"), Chopped
1/2 C. Celery, Chopped
1 Med. Onion, Diced
2 Tbls green pepper (if desired)
1 tsp Thyme
1 tsp Loveage
2 cans Campbell's Potato Soup
2 cans Corn, drained
2 cans Creamed Corn
Half & Half or Light Cream

Directions:
Fry bacon crisp
Saut√© vegetables in bacon drippings until tender.
Move bacon and veggies to a Crockpot and add the Corn and Soup.
Add Salt & Pepper as needed and Thyme & Loveage.
Cook on Low for 4-6 Hrs.
Thin with Cream/Milk to desired consistency.
Bring up to heat.
Adjust Seasonings.
Serve.
