Title:
Super Easy Strawberry Short Cake Biscuits,-.Better then store bought! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5561908.jpg
Ingredients:
2 and 1/2 cups all purpose flour.
1/2 cup white sugar.
3 heaping tbsp. baking powder.
1 tsp. salt.
1/2 cup shortening.
1 and 1/2 cups milk.
2 eggs.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees, grease a 12 cup muffin tin,  mix together the first four ingredients. Cut in the shortening and mix to combine. In a separate bowl beat the eggs add the milk to the eggs and mix to combine. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until combined. Put the mixture in the greased muffin tins and in the oven as quick as possible and bake for 20 minutes. *See notes*

Description: 
This Strawberry Shortcake  Biscuits has been in my husbands family for over 40 years.
It is the best sweet biscuit to have under your strawberry and cream dessert.
They are  also very good on their own with peanut butter.
Delicious right out of the oven slathered with butter..
