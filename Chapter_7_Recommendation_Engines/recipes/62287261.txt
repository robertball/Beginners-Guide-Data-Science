Title:
Strawberry-rhubarb pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pastry for 2-crust pie
1 c. sugar
1/8 tsp. salt
1/3 c. flour
2 c. fresh strawberries, cleaned and quartered
2 c. (1" pieces) fresh or frozen rhubarb
2 tblsp. butter

Directions:
Combine sugar, salt and flour.
Arrange half of strawberries and rhubarb in pastry lined 9" pie pan. Sprinkle with half of sugar mixture. Repeat with remaining fruit and sugar mixture. Dot top with butter.
Adjust top crust and fulte edges. Cut steam vents.
Bake at 400 degrees for 40-50 minutes or until crust is browned and rhubarb is tender.
Cool before serving.

Submitter: 
Cathy Heinemann Tucker
Description: 
My mom first made this pie. It's been a family favorite ever since. Raspberries are also good instead of strawberries. Frozen fruit can be used- just thaw part way before using.
