Title:
Pressure Cooker Hard Boiled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 dozen eggs
1 cup water

Directions:
Put rack inside of pressure cooker.
Place eggs on rack in cooker.
Pour in water.
Put on the lid and manually set the pot at high pressure for 6 minutes.
When cooking time is done, quick release and remove lid.
Remove eggs carefully from pot to cool, as they will be very hot.
