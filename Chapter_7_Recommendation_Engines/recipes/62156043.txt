Title:
Old Fashioned Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 whole uncooked chicken, cut into parts, skin removed.
Water
1 cup chopped fresh parsley
1 sprig fresh rosemary
1 sprig fresh thyme
1 bay leaf
1 large onion, diced
4 - 6 cloves of garlic minced
2 - 4 tbsp salt
5 medium sized carrots, sliced
5 celery ribs, slicede
1 - 2 tsp freshly ground pepper
2 cups uncooked shell pasta (or broken linguine)

Directions:
Using a large stew pot, add chicken pieces, Enough water to completely cover the chicken, parsley, rosemary, thyme, bay leaf, 1/2 of the diced onion, garlic, salt and salt and pepper. Bring to a boil - then turn down to low heat and simmer covered with a lid for 2 hours.
Remove chicken pieces, Rosemary, Thyme and bay leaf.
Shred Chicken meat off of bone and replace back into soup stock.Discard bones and herbs.
Add anoter 4 - 6 cups of water to replace what evaporated and return to boil.
Add carrots and onion, cook for 20 minutes.
Add Celery and pasta.  Cook until pasta is desired consistency.
Serve immediately

Submitter: 
Tamaralynn
Description: 
A great basic chicken noodle soup recipe.  The best recipes can sometimes take a while, make this on one of those cold rainy days when you have extra time to spare.
