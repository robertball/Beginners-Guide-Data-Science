Title:
Sauteed Mushrooms in Garlic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1024241.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
2 pounds fresh mushrooms, sliced
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon basil
1 cup red wine

Directions:
Heat the butter in a skillet over medium heat.
Add the mushrooms and garlic; cook and stir until the mushrooms are a light golden brown, about 10 minutes.
Stir in the basil.
Reduce heat to low, and pour the wine into the skillet.
Simmer until most of the wine has evaporated.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Mushrooms and garlic are cooked in butter then simmered in red wine.Modified from: Sauteed Mushrooms in Garlic
