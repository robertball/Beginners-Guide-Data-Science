Title:
Great Grains Cereal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/970889.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup butter or margarine
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups great grains or other similar cereal

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 F and lightly grease cookie sheets.
With an electric mixer, cream the butter or margarine and brown sugar until fluffy. Beat in the egg.
Sift over the flour, baking soda, and salt and then stir into the butter mixture. Add the cereal and mix until combined.
Drop by teaspoonfulls onto prepared sheets and bake until golden, 8-10 minutes. Transfer to rack to cool completely.
