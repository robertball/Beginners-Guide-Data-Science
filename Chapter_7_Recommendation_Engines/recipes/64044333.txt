Title:
Breakfast Buffet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
100 grams Granola
1.5 Bread slice
10 grams	Jam
237 ml Milk
237 ml Yogurt
Espresso
Hot Chocolate
Green Tea
Black Tea
Tea
15 grams	Sugar
10 grams	Honey
237 ml Juice
10 grams	Nutella
10 grams	Cream Cheese

Directions:
Cut bread and serve in a bowl
Warm milk for hot chocolate and put into thermos
Heat water for tea and put into yellow thermos
Make espresso and put into thermos
Place out cereal, jam, yogurt, nutella, cream cheese, hot chocolate, teas, sugar, honey and a variety of juices
ITALIANO Istruzioni:
Tagliare il pane e servire in una ciotola
Latte caldo per la cioccolata calda e mettere in thermos
Scaldare l'acqua per il tè e mettere in thermos giallo
Fai espresso e metterlo in thermos
Mettere fuori cereali, marmellata, yogurt, cioccolata calda, tè, zucchero, miele e una varietà di succhi di frutta
