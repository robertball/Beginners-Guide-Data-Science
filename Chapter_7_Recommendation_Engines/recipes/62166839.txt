Title:
Grilled Chicken in a Creamy Marsala Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Chicken Breasts (Boneless, Skinless)
12 oz Mushrooms (Sliced)
1.25 cups Marsala Wine
1 cup Heavy Cream
1 Tbl. Lemon Juice
Salt, Pepper & Oregano to taste
9 oz Linguine or Fettuccine
1-2 cloves Garlic (Minced)

Directions:
Pound the Chicken breasts to about 1/4" in thickness.  Season with Salt, Pepper & Oregano.  Grill chicken until done (165 internal temp).
Place Marsala wine in a sauce pan and reduce by about half.  Add Garlic, Mushrooms and lemon juice and continue cooking until mushrooms are cooked.  Add Heavy Cream and continue to cook until slightly thickened. If need add a little corn starch and water slurry to thicken to your liking.
Plate 1 breast on a bed of pasta and pour sauce and mushrooms over top.

This is a tasty variant on the traditional Chicken Marsala recipes.
