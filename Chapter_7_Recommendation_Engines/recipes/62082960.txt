Title:
Kim's Ratatouille Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large eggplant, cut into ½ inch cubes
3 medium zucchini, sliced
1 large red onion, thinly sliced
1 red bell pepper, sliced
1 green bell pepper, sliced
1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
3 cloves garlic, minced
½ teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large non-stick skillet.  Add eggplant, zucchini, onion, and bell peppers.  Sauté until vegetables begin to soften.  Add tomatoes, Italian seasoning, garlic, red pepper flakes, if desired, and salt and pepper.  Bring mixture to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer for 30-45 minutes.  May be served over whole grain pasta with a sprinkling of freshly grated parmesan.
