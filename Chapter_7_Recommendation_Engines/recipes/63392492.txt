Title:
Asian Salad - Pasta or Romaine Lettuce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Soy-Ginger Dressing:
3 medium garlic cloves, minced
6 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar
1 tablespoon sugar
1 tablespoon peanut butter
2 tbs coconut oil
1 tbs olive oil
1 teaspoon ground ginger
3/4 teaspoon hot red pepper flakes
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
------------------------------------------------
Pasta Salad (Version 1):
1.5 tbs orange juice to dressing
1 tsp orange zest to dressing
2 manadarin orange
1 pound penne pasta
8 ounces broccoli florets
3 medium carrots, peeled and coarsely grated
1 medium red bell pepper, cut into bite-size strips
3 cups bean sprouts
3 green onions, thinly sliced
1/2 cup chopped roasted (or honey-roasted) peanuts
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
------------------------------------------------
Non-Pasta Salad (Version 2):
24 ounce rib eye steak cut into strips
24 leaves romaine lettuce, torn into bite-size pieces
2 medium cucumber, cubed
2 avocado - peeled, pitted, and diced
2 large tomatos, cut into half wedges
4 carrots, grated
8 thin slices red onion
6 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds

Directions:
1)Season both side of the rib eye steak with soy sauce and steak seasoning. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour to overnight.
2)Grill steak on preheated grill until firm, reddish-pink, and juicy in the center, about 6 minutes per side. If using version 2, cut into strips.
3)Cook pasta if using Version 1
4)Mix soy-ginger dressing ingredients together.
5)Mix salad ingredients together. Chill, then add dressing.
