Title:
Marinated Grilled Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1037594.jpg
Ingredients:
4-6	salmon fillets (about 4 to 6 ounces each)
1	large resealable plastic food storage bag
1	bottle KC Masterpiece lemon pepper marinade
1	medium onion sliced into paper thin slices
1-2	lemons very thinly sliced
Salt Free Lemon & Pepper seasoning, or Durkee brand Citrus Grill Seasoning To taste, or Mrs Dash Caribbean Citrus seasoning.
Onion powder to taste (if using salt free lemon pepper seasoning)

Directions:
1.	Place salmon in large re-sealable baggie, add the marinade half or all of the bottle is fine.  Which ever way you like to marinate your foods.  Place in refrigerator and let marinate at least 2 hours or over night.
2.	When ready to cook, slice the lemons and onions.  Place the salmon on the grill, season with one of the seasoning mixtures.  Top with first the onions and then lay the lemons on top of the onions.
3.	Cook Not turning the fish until done.  Carefully remove from the grill and serve.

Submitter: 
Semigourmet
Description: 
Easy! A Great light summer grilling meal, and can be made in the oven any time of the year.
