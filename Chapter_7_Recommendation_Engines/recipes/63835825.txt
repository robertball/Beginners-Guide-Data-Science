Title:
Spanish Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1065966.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons oil
2 tablespoons onion, chopped
1 1/2 cups uncooked white rice
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup chunky salsa

Directions:
Heat oil in a large, heavy skillet over medium heat.
Stir in onion, and cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
Mix rice into skillet, stirring often.
When rice begins to brown, stir in chicken broth and salsa.
Reduce heat, cover and simmer 20 minutes, until liquid has been absorbed.

Submitter: 
Linda Ahrens
Description: 
Spanish rice is the perfect accompaniment to Mexican foods, chicken, or just about anything. This simple recipe uses chicken broth and chunky salsa to transform plain white rice into a marvelous side dish.Modified from: Best Spanish Rice
