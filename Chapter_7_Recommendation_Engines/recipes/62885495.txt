Title:
Iced Orange Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup crisco
2 eggs
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup orange juice
2 1/2 cups sifted flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 lb powdered sugar
1 generous Tbsp margarine
1 generous Tbsp crisco
Orange juice to right moisture

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375Â° and grease cookie sheets.
2. Cream together crisco and sugar. Add eggs, one at a time. alternate adding juice and flour / baking powder / salt.
3. Place on greased cookie sheet by small rounded mounds no larger than a walnut. Bake 8-10 minutes, cookies will not be browned more than a small visible rim on the edge.
4. Remove after about a minute to wire racks to cool.
5. For the Icing: While baking blend the margarine and crisco. Alternately add the sugar and the orange juice. Icing should be stiff.
6. Ice cooled cookies and do not store or stack until icing has hardened. Cover tightly.

Lynn Pennec
Description: 
A delicate iced cookie, one of my brother's favorites.
