Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup Half and Half
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
2 cups cooked chopped or shredded chicken
3/4 finely chopped onion
½ tsp. salt.
12 corn or flour tortillas
3 or 4 cans green chile sauce (like Hernandez)
1½ cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
Beat Half and Half and cream cheese with mixer. Add chicken, onions, and salt.
Spoon a thin layer of the green chili sauce sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 pan.  Spread each tortilla with sauce and chicken mix.  Roll tortilla and put seam-side down in baking dish.
Spoon remaining sauce on top.  Top with cheese.  Bake 20 minutes until hot at 350°.
