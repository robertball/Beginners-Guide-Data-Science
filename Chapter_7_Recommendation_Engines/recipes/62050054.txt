Title:
No crust apple pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp cinnamon
2 large apples-peeled,chopped
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Butter 9 inch pie pan, set aside.
Beat egg.
Add sugars and vanilla, beat 1 minute. Add flour, baking powder and cinnamon, beat well.
Stir in apples. Spread in pie pan, sprinkle with walnuts.
Bake 350 degrees for 30 minutes, until slightly browned.
Serve with whipped cream or ice cream.
