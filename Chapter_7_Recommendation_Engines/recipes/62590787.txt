Title:
Jess's Award Winning Apricot Glazed Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/553750.jpg
Ingredients:
1 packet (1 ounce) Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Salad Dressing & Seasoning Mix
½ cup bread crumbs
2 pound pork loin roast, sliced 1-inch thick
1 cup apricot preserves

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°F. Combine dressing mix with the bread crumbs. Coat each pork slice with the crumb mixture. Place in a baking dish and cover evenly with the preserves. Bake approximately 25–35 minutes, or until thoroughly cooked.
