Title:
Apricot Chicken Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/809646.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts or tenders
1/3 cup apricot preserves
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1 Tbsp. prepared mustard
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. red pepper flakes
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Mix all marinade ingredients.  Pour over chicken and let sit for at least one hour in the refrigerator.  Grill.

Submitter: 
MMHASTY
Description: 
Other fruits could be substituted I'm sure, but it is worth buying a jar of apricot preserves just for this!
