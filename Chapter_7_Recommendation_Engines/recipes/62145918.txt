Title:
Matzoh Candy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 1/2 salted matzohs
1 cup margarine or butter
1 cup dark brown sugar
8 ounces chocolate chips
add nuts if desired

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Lightly grease 9x9 pan
Line the bottom of the pan with matzohs breaking off as needed to fit them
Combine butter and sugar in a saucepan
Cook over medium heat until it comes to a boil (3 minutes)
Pour over matzoh and bake for 15 minutes or until it starts to bubble
Remove and sprinkle with chips
Let is stand 5 minutes and spread the chips over the surface
Cool thoroughly, great when refrigerated and even when frozen
Substitute with peanut butter chocolate chips and add nuts if desired
after cooled break into uneven pieces

Submitter: 
CC Bombet
Description: 
Salty and sweet with a crunch! Easily substitute different types of chocolate and or peanut butter. Nuts are a great addition.
