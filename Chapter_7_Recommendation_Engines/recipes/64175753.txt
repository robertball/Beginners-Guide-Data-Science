Title:
cupcake chocolate cream filling Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-7 oz bag of unwrapped Milky Way mini candy bars
1-2.13 oz. 3 Musketeer candy bar
1/4 C. butter
2 tsp. vanilla
1 Tbls. Milk
1 1/4 C. powdered sugar

Directions:
using a double boiler, melt chocolate bars and butter with milk and vanilla. Remove from heat and add powdered sugar. Stir until creamy. Fills 42-48 cupcakes.

Submitter: 
Jenny Brannon Elder
Description: 
I tweaked this recipe from one I had found in an old recipe book. I make my homemade buttercream frosting to go on top. Always get rave reviews!
