Title:
Strawberry Pretzel Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
First Layer
1 1/2 cups crushed pretzels
4 1/2 tablespoons white sugar
3/4 cup butter, melted
Second Layer
1 cup white sugar
2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
Third Layer
1 (6 ounce) package strawberry flavored gelatin
2 cups boiling water
1 (16 ounce) package frozen strawberries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
First Layer: Mix together the pretzels, 4 1/2 tablespoons sugar and melted butter. Press into the bottom of a 9x13 inch pan. Bake for 10 minutes only. Set aside to cool completely.
Second Layer: In a medium bowl, beat the sugar and cream cheese until smooth. Fold in whipped topping. Spread evenly over the cooled crust. Refrigerate until set, about 30 minutes.
Third Layer: In a medium bowl, stir together the gelatin mix and boiling water. Mix in frozen strawberries, and stir until thawed. Let set approx 30 minutes to an hour.  Pour over cream cheese mixture in pan. Refrigerate until completely chilled, or overnight.

Submitter: 
Lori Bachner Carey
Description: 
This three layer salad includes a pretzel crust, cream cheese center, and strawberry top.Modified from: Judy's Strawberry Pretzel Salad
