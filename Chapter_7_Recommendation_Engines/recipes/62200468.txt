Title:
Ellen's Super Spicy Chicken and Black Bean Taco Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 cloves minced garlic
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 package taco seasoning mix
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1 14oz can chicken broth
1 1/2 pounds cooked chicken breast
1 can Rotel
2 15 ounce cans black beans (drained)
1 4 ounce can diced jalapenos
2 11 ounce cans of Mexicorn
1 cup shredded Mexican blend cheese, for garnish
1 cup sour cream, for garnish

Directions:
In a Dutch oven, saute onions until soft. Add garlic, cumin and red pepper flakes and continue to saute for 1-2 minutes.
Add chicken, chicken broth, Rotel, black beans, jalapenos, Mexicorn and taco seasoning.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce to low heat, cover, and simmer for 20-30 minutes stirring occasionally.
Serve and top with shredded cheese and sour cream.

Description: 
I was in the mood for something super spicy and came up with this delicious Mexican inspired soup. It's a great way to use up leftover chicken breast, but beware, it's very spicy!!
