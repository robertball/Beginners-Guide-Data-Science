Title:
Eggs With Basil Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1011949.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Â– 6 eggs
1 - 15 oz. can of sauce
10 fresh basil leaves
3 tablespoons olive oil
1 clove smashed garlic
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat olive oil and garlic in a 8 to 10 inch pan.  Discard garlic. Add sauce and basil.  Bring to boil, then lower heat to a simmer for about 5 minutes.  Add eggs, one at a time.  Sprinkle eggs with salt and pepper.  Cover and cook for 5 to 10 minutes depending on how you like your eggs.  Eggs cooked for 10 minutes should be soft but not runny.
