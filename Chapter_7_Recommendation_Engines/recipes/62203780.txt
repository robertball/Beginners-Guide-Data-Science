Title:
Chocolate Almond butter cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened at room temperature
1/2 cup  natural almond butter
2/3 cup confectioners' sugar, sifted
2 large eggs, at room temperature
1/8 teaspoon (Pinch) salt
1 1/2 teaspoons almond or vanilla extract
1/3 cup good quality unsweetened cocoa powder
1 2/3 cups all purpose flour
2 ounces good quality dark chocolate, optional

Directions:
In a mixer or with a hand mixer in a large bowl,on medium,beat butter and almond butter until smooth.
Add confectioners' sugar and extract and beat until smooth and well combined.
Add the eggs and the salt, blend until combined.
Reduce speed to low and blend flour and cocoa until combined.
with a spatula, mix in any remaining flour.
Form dough into 1 inch to 1 1/4 inch thick logs and wrap with plastic or waxed paper.
Chill dough in the fridge for at least 2 hours
Preheat oven and line two baking sheets with parchment paper. Slice log into 46-50 cookies, place on baking sheets leaving 1/2 inch space between each cookie.
Bake cookies for 12 to 14 minutes until set but not browned. Transfer cookies to cooling racks and cool to room temperature.
Optional: Drizzle or dip cooled cookies with melted dark chocolate.

Submitter: 
Kimmi
Description: 
These cookies are the perfect combination of chocolate and nut butter. Stored dough can be ready for any occasion. Enjoy!
