Title:
Crock Pot Italian Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 Italian Sausages
1 14.5 oz can stewed tomatoes
1 green bell pepper
1 vidalia onion
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions:
Slice onion and place in bottom of slow cooker. Add Italian sausages, then sliced green pepper and stewed tomatoes. Cook on low 6-8 hours. Serve hot with grated parmesan cheese.

Submitter:

Description: 
Italian Sausages simmered in stewed tomatoes, onion, and bell peppers..easy and delicious!
