Title:
Grilled Pepperoni Pizza Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp. butter, softened
2 slices country white, sourdough or whole grain sandwich bread
2 slices SargentoÂ® Deli Style Sliced Mozzarella Cheese
4 slices pepperoni
1/3 cup prepared pizza sauce, heated

Directions:
Heat non-stick skillet over medium heat. Spread butter over one side of each bread slice. Place one bread slice, buttered side down, in pan. Top with cheese slice, pepperoni, second cheese slice and bread slice, buttered side up.
Cook over medium heat 3 to 4 minutes per side or until golden brown and cheese is melted. Serve with pizza sauce.
