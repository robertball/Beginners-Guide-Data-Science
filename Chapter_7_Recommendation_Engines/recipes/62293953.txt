Title:
Peggy's Easy Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
1 pkg Williams Original Chili Seasoning (1 oz)
2 14 oz cans diced tomatoes
1 15 oz can tomato sauce
1 15 oz can red kidney beans (drained)
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper

Directions:
Brown ground beef and onion (8 to 10 minutes); drain fat.  Add package of chili seasoning and stir well.  Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, beans, salt, and pepper.  Transfer to slow cooker and cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours, or simmer on stove top for 1 1/2 hours.
