Title:
Arroz Tapado or Rice on Top Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/595381.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked rice cooked to package directions
3 small or 2 large tomatos pureed(PEELED AND SEEDED OPTIONAL)
1/2 c water or beef broth
1/2 large onion finely chopped
3 cloves chopped garlic
1T oil
1 t aji powder (or 1 t smoked paprika)
1/2 t cumin
1/4 t ground black pepper
2 t fresh chopped oregano or 1/4 t dried oregano
1 lb lean ground beef
2 lg carrots peeled and cubed
1/4 c frozen peas
1 hard boiled egg  chopped
1 T peanut butter
1 potato peeled cubed and fried
1 c oil for frying potatos
*optional garnish
chopped cilantro or parsley, chopped tomato and ketchup

Directions:
cook rice to package directions
peel cube and fry potato in 1c cooking oil til golden brown  drain and set aside
puree tomatoes in blender with water or broth
heat 1T oil saute onion, add garlic and seasonings (for 8-10 min keep moist with small splashes of water)
add tomatoe puree cook 5 min stirring often
add carrots and ground meat stir and break up meat for the first few minutes and then lower heat cover and cook till all redness is out of meat and carrots are tender. Add peanut butter, peas, chopped egg and mix well, add fried potatoes remove from heat and set aside.
assembly requires a lighly oiled bowl of any size,( a small custard dish, cereal bowl or plastic container will work fine) place enough rice to fill bottom third of bowl, add meat mixture for middle third and finish with last layer of rice to rim. Press rice firmly with an oiled hand to created form. Invert rice and meat mold onto plate( plate on top and flip)
Garnish with chopped cilantro or parsley, chopped tomato and a squeeze of ketchup for a pretty finish

Submitter: 
Katie
Description: 
A fun Peruvian dish that looks cool and is always a hit with the kids! Seasoned meat is the is the surprise center to the rice on top.
