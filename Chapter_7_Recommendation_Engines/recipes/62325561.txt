Title:
Sue's Carrot Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup corn oil
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 cup + 2 Tbls Flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
2 tsp cinnamon
4 lg eggs
4 cup grated carrots
1 cup raisons
1 cup coconut
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 Recipe for Cream Cheese Frosting II

Directions:
Butter and flour 2 cake pans.
Combine oil and sugars in large bowl.
Sift flour, bp, bs, salt and cinnamon in separate bowl.
Mix 1/2 of dry ingredients into the oil mixture.
Add eggs, 1 at a time, alternating with remaining dry ingredients. Add carrots,raison, coconut and nuts.
Pour into cake pans. Bake 50 mins at 350 degrees.
