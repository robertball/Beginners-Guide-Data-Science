Title:
Southwest Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. ground beef
1 1/2 C. diced onion
1 (28 oz.) can diced tomatoes, with liquid
1 (15 oz.) can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 (15 1/4 oz.) can whole kernel corn, drained
1 C. picante sauce, mild, medium, or hot
3/4 C. water
1 tsp. ground cumin
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. black pepper
salt, to taste
shredded cheddar cheese, optional

Directions:
In a large skillet, cook the beef and onions over medium heat until meat is no longer pink, drain fat. Add the remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. reduce heat, simmer, covered, for 15-20 minutes or until heated through. Garnish with shredded cheddar cheese, if desired.
