Title:
Chicken Breasts Florentine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup onion - diced
1 clove garlic - minced
1/2 cup chopped mushrooms
1/2 cup cooked, chopped spinach, well drained
1/2 cup part skim ricotta cheese
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. oregano
1/4 tsp. pepper
4 chicken cutlets, pounded to 1/4" thickness
1 tbsp. + 1 tsp. butter
1 tbsp. + 1 tsp. butter breadcrumbs
2 tbsp. + 2 tsp. dry white wine

Directions:
Spray 10" skillet with cooking spray and add onions and garlic.  Cook until softened then add mushrooms and saute until brown.  Remove from heat and add spinach, cheese, 1/4 tsp. salt, oregano and 1/8 tsp. pepper.  Sprinkle chicken with remaining salt and pepper then spread spinach mixture on chicken, leaving a 1/2" border on all sides.  Carefully roll chicken to enclose filling and secure with a toothpick.  Place in a greased baking dish.  In small skillet, melt butter and add bread crumbs then sprinkle over chicken.  Add wine to baking dish, cover with foil and bake 20 minutes.  Remove foil and bake about 10 more minutes, or until bread crumbs are lightly browned.
