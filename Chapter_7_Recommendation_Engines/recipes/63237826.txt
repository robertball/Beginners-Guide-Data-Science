Title:
taco bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packs of cresent rolls
1 can of refried beans
1 pound of hamberger meat
1 pk of taco seasoning
8oz of cheese
1 small bag of salad mix
1 bag of nacho chips

Directions:
brown hamberger, drain and add taco seasoning
spray a 8x10 pan
layer cresent rolls on bottom of pan
spread refried beans on top of rolls
add taco meat on top of the beans
wrap edges of rolls over meat
bake at 350 for 10-15 minutes
add cheese and salad

Submitter: 
Erica Leigh Bentz
Description: 
quick and easy, less messy than real tacos. eat with a fork, and come back for seconds
