Title:
Hamburger Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground sirloin
1 small onion, chopped
2 t minced garlic
1 t salt
14.5 oz petite diced tomatoes, canned and NOT drained
1 bag frozen long green beans, steamed
1 bag frozen yellow corn, steamed
4 - 6 cups mashed potatoes, prepared
8 ounces shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:
In medium pan brown ground beef with onion and garlic until beef is cooked through.  Drain.  Add salt and diced tomatoes.  Cook for about 15 minutes until liquid is reduced down until it is almost gone.  Pour beef mixture into 13x9 pan sprayed with cooking spray.  Top with green beans, then corn and then cover with mashed potatoes.  Sprinkle with cheddar cheese.  Bake in 375 degree oven for about 30 - 45 minutes until cheese is completely melted and edges begin to brown.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is an adaptation of a recipe my bonus Mom made while I was growing up.  I remember it being one of my favorite meals when I was staying with her and my father.  I have tweaked it just a bit.  Yum-O!
