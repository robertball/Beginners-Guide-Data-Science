Title:
Bacon-wrapped Tater Tots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 Partially defrosted Tater Tots
12 Strips Center-cut Bacon, cut in half
24 Small chunks Sharp Cheddar Cheese
Franks Red Hot Sauce
24 Toothpicks

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or spray with nonstick cooking spray.
2.Lay a piece of bacon on a flat surface, place a tater tot on top, and using a knife cut a slit down the center of the tater tot. Stuff a piece of cheese into the slit. Top with several drops of hot sauce, wrap with bacon, and secure with a toothpick. Repeat with remaining tater tots.
3. Bake 15-20 minutes or until they appear to be cooked through and bacon is crispy. Serve immediately.
