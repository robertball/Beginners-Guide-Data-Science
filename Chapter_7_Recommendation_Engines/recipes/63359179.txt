Title:
Green Tomato Jam Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups green tomatoes
4 cups sugar
1 large package Jello, any flavor (raspberry,strawberry or cherry.)

Directions:
Grind tomatoes. Add sugar and boil for 20 minutes.Remove from heat. Add Jello and stir for 3 min. I put it in the fridge for awhile till it thickens up and then put in plastic containers and freeze.

Submitter: 
Cheryl Muellner
Description: 
Got the recipe from cooks.com. It is a very good jam cannot even tell there are green tomatoes in it. It is pretty thin at first but it thickens up nicely.
