Title:
Buckhead Mountain Grill's cheese boulders Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/670174.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup cottage cheese
1/2 pound Parmesan cheese, grated
1 pound cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese, grated
1 1/2 cups seasoned flour
22 cups eggs, beaten, about 6 eggs
Oil for deep frying

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl. Refrigerate for 1 hour before cooking.
Portion desired-size balls using an ice cream scoop with a thumb trigger release. Carefully place portioned cheese balls into 350-degree oil for 2 to 3 minutes. Drain finished cheese balls on a pan lined with paper towels. Serve with fresh marinara sauce or salsa on the side. Serves 4 to 6 as a snack.
