Title:
Sweet Hawaiian Yeast Bread (Bread Machine) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup pineapple juice
1 egg
2 tablespoons coconut oil
2 1/2 tablespoons agave nectar
3/4 teaspoon salt
3 cups bread flour
2 tablespoons dry milk
2 teaspoons fast rising yeast

Directions:
Place ingredients in bread machine container in order directed by manufacturer.
Cycle: white, sweet, no timer.
Setting: light.

Submitter: 
Cori
Description: 
Trying to get that lovely sweet Hawaiian bread at home!
