Title:
Berry Bliss Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup cold milk
1 pkg. (4-serving size)JELL-O Vanilla Flavor Instant Puding & Pie Filling
1-1/2 cups COol Whip topping, divided
1 pkg. Sara Lee Frozen Pound Cake
1/4 cup orange juice
2 cups mixed raspberries and sliced strawberries
1/4 cup blackberries

Directions:
Add milk to dry pudding mix in medium bowl.  Beat with a wire whisk 2 minutes or until well blended.  Stir in 1 cup of the whipped topping.
Cut cake horizontally into three layers; brush with juice.  Place bottom layer on serving plate; top with 3/4 cup mixed berries, half of the pudding mixture and middle cake layer.  Repeat layer of mixed berried and pudding mixture; cover with top cake layer.  Top with 1/2 cup whipped topping and all remaining berrie; cover loosely with foil.
Refridgerate at least 4 hours or overnight.
