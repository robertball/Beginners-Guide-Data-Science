Title:
Delicious Hamburger Steaks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1&1/2 pounds ground sirloin
2 tablespoons minced onion
2 tablespoons minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon liquid smoke

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix hamburger meat with minced onions, minced garlic and liquid smoke. Pat out into two large steak patties.
If broiling, turn oven broiler on low heat. Place steaks on a broiler pan with a little water in the bottom and broil for ten minutes on each side.
If grilling, prepare grill for med-high heat and grill ten minutes on each side, turning once.

Submitter: 
Janine
Description: 
These hamburger steaks are great grilled or broiled. It's a favorite recipe, and the only way we dress up our hamburger steaks around here. We hope you enjoy!
