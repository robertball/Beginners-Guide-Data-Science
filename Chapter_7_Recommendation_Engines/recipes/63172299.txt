Title:
Lemon Creme Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/814542.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c. sugar
5 Tbsp. cornstarch
1 c. milk
1/2 c. cold water
1/8 tsp. salt
6 lg. egg yolks
2/3 c. fresh lemon juice
2 Tbsp. lemon zest
2 Tbsp. unsalted butter
4 egg whites
6 Tbsp. sugar

Directions:
Combine sugar, cornstarch, milk, water and salt in a heavy stainless steel pan. Whisk together and begin to thicken over medium heat.
Temper egg yoks and add constantly stirring. Add lemon juice and zest, also stirring. Add butter one piece at a time. Bring back to a simmer. Pout into cooked pie shell.
Make meringue with egg whites and sugar. Make sure entire pie is covered into crust. Make peaks and place in 375` oven until meringue browns.
