Title:
Big Ray's Sweet Potato & Chickpea Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4489559.jpg
Ingredients:
2 medium sweet potatoes (about 1 pound)
peeled and cubed
1 Tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 can (15 ounces) garbanzo beans or chickpeas, rinsed and drained
2 Tbsp seasoned rice vinegar
4 tsp olive oil
1 Tbsp minced fresh gingerroot (or 1 tsp ginger powder)
1 garlic clove, minced
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
4 cups spring mix salad greens
1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the sweet potatoes, oil, salt and pepper; toss to coat. Transfer to a 15" x 10" baking sheet coated with cooking spray. Roast at 425Â° for 20-25 minutes or until tender, stirring once.
In a large bowl, combine garbanzo beans and sweet potatoes. In a small bowl, whisk the dressing ingredients. Add to sweet potato mixture; toss to coat. Serve over salad greens; top with cheese.
