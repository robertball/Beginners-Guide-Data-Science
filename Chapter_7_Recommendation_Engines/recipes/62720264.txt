Title:
healthy berry muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup oats, ground to a fine powder
1/2 cup ground flax seed
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 cup sugar
3/4 cup milk
3/4 cup 100% juice of any berry
1 cup applesauce
2 cups fresh or frozen berries

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Lightly grease a muffin pan.
Combine first 5 ingredients in a large bowl.
Add remaining ingredients, stirring until thoroughly mixed.
Fill the wells of prepared muffin pan 3/4 full of muffin mix.
Bake for 14-18 minutes or until tops are lightly golden/browned and toothpick comes out clean.

Submitter: 
tiffany.renee
Description: 
Low-fat, reduced sugar muffins enhanced with flax seed and filled with berries. They are moist and delicious, full of fiber and flavor!
