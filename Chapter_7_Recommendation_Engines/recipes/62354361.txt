Title:
Brown Sugar and Ginger Glazed Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T dark brown sugar
1 t. Dijon-style mustard
1 t. soy sauce
1/4 t. ground ginger
4 (4-oz.) salmon fillets
Sesame seeds, toasted (optional)

Directions:
Preheat broiler. Coat broiler rack with cooking spray; set aside
Combine sugar, mustard, soy sauce, and ginger in sm MB
Place Salmon skin side up on broiler rack.
Cook 6" from heat source for 3 min.
Turn Salmon; brush with mixture. Broil for 1-2 min. or until sugar melts
and fish flakes easily with fork.
Garnish with toasted seeds if desired.
