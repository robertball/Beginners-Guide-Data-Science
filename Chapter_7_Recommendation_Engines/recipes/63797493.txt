Title:
Sweet & Sour Sauce, the best Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can crushed pineapple
1 cup sugar
1 cup vinegar
2 tablespoons cornstarch
1 cup plum jam
1 cup water
1 tablespoon soy sauce
2 tablespoons cold water

Directions:
Heat pineapple, sugar, 1 cup water and soy sauce to a boil.
Meanwhile, mix together corn starch and the 2 tablespoons of cold water.
Stir cornstarch mixture into pineapple mixture; heat to a boil stirring constantly.  Cool to room temperature.
Once cooled; stir the plum jam into cooled mixture and refrigerate.
This sauce makes a lot & it freezes well.
