Title:
BACARDI® Mixers Chocolate Colada Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983492.jpg
Ingredients:
2/3 (10 fluid ounce) can frozen BACARDI® Mixers Pina Colada mix
1/2 cup BACARDI® Rum
10 mini Mounds® bars
5 cups ice
Coconut flakes for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Pina Colada mix, BACARDI(R) Rum, 10 candy bars and ice for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Garnish with coconut flakes.

Submitter: 
BACARDI® Mixers
Description: 
The delicious combination of chocolate and coconut is blended together with rum and ice in this outstanding and original drink.
