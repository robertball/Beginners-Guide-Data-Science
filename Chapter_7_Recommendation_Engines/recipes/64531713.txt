Title:
Spicy pecans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3056362.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2  cup confectioners sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
2 cups pecans

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325
Line roasting pan with parchment paper
In medium bowl mix sugar, salt & pepper
Add 4 teaspoons of water & stir until sugar is dissolved
Add pecans and stir until coated and pour onto  parchment paper
Spread to a single layer and pour all of the left over sugar mixture on top
Bake for 10-12 minutes
Remove from oven & slide parchment paper with nuts onto countertop to cool
Once cooled separate nuts

Submitter: 
Jane
Description: 
Not too sweet, not too spicy - pecans with a kick 
The secret to get a crisp coating is to use confections  or powdered sugar.
