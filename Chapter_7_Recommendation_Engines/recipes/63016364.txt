Title:
Shorecook's  Peanut Butter Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3985900.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup butter (soft)
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2/3 cup peanut butter
2 tsp. vanilla extract
2 eggs
2 2/3 cup flour
2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1 10 oz. bag Reese's peanut butter morsels

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees and cover baking sheets with parchment paper.
In a large bowl, cream together butter, granulated sugar and brown sugar until fluffy.  Mix in peanut butter and vanilla extract.  Add eggs one at a time.  Mix until smooth and creamy.
In a medium bowl, sift together flour, baking soda and salt.  Gradually add sifted ingredients to the peanut butter mixture until dough forms, then stir in peanut butter chips.
Using a tablespoon, roll into balls and flaten.  Bake 8-10 minutes until lightly brown.
Allow to cool several minutes on the cookie sheet before moving them to a cooling rack.

Submitter: 
SHORECOOK
Description:
