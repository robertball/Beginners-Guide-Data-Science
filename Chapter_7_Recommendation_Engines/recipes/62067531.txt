Title:
Scooby Snack Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oz. Midori or melon liquor
1 oz. Malibu or coconut rum
6-8 oz. Pinapple juice
1-2 oz. Half and Half

Directions:
Mix all of the ingredients well and enjoy.  I think this is best well shaken.

Submitter: 
JoAnna Ellingsen
Description: 
This was a favorite of my clientele in my bartending days.  It's great on ice as a drink or chilled as a shooter. Enjoy!
