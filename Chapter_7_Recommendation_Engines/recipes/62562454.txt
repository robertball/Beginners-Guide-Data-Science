Title:
Chicken and Vegetable Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/537301.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp paprika
1/8 tsp pepper
2-1/2-3-pound broiler gryer chicken, cut up
3 tbsp vegetable oil
1-1/2 cup water
1/2 tsp dried savory leaves
1/4 tsp dried thyme leaves
3 medium carrots, cut into 3x1/4-inch strips
1/2 package (14 oz size) frozen crinkle-cut potato slices (about 2 cups)
1 package (10 oz) frozen asparagus spears or cuts, broken apart
1-1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Mix flour, 1 tsp salt, paprika and pepper; coat chicken pieces. Brown chicken in oil in 12-inch skillet or Dutch oven; drain. Add water, savory and thyme. Heat to boiling; reduce heat. Cover and simer 30 minutes.
Add carrots; cover and simmer 10 minutes. Add frozen potatoes and asparagus; sprinkle with salt. Heat to boiling; reduce heat. Cover and simmer until chciken in done and vegatables are tender, 10-15 minutes.
