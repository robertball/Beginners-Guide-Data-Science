Title:
HUNKAR BEGENDI (traditional turkish cuisine) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/746964.jpg
Ingredients:
for kebab;
500 grams lean lamb meat
1 large onion
1 piece red pepper, diced
1 tablespoon tomato paste
1 grated tomatoes
salt
thyme
3 tablespoons vegetable oil
for puree (begendi);
2-3 aubergines
3 tablespoons flour
50 grams margarine or butter
350 ml of milk
1/2 cup grated kasar cheese (gouda cheese can be used)
salt

Directions:
First cook diced lamb meat , then add diced onion & vegetable oil and roast all together. Add pepper, tomato, tomato paste, salt, thyme and some water & sautee over low heat and peel.  On the other side, cook aubergines on the embers and start preparing bechamel sauce. Melt butter, add flour and cook till browned. Add warmed milk and mix until creamy. Add peeled aubergines and kasar (gouda) cheese and mix. Finally serve lamb over the aubergine mixture (begendi).
