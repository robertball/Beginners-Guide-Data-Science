Title:
Cheesy Chicken Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cubed
1 cup chunky salsa
1 cup frozen or canned corn (drain if using canned)
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
Vegetable oil cooking spray
Flour tortillas, heated according to package directions

Directions:
Coat a nonstick skillet with vegetable oil spray. Saute cubed chicken over medium-high heat for 5 minutes, or until done. Add salsa and corn. Heat thoroughly, stirring constantly. Add shredded cheese and continue heating, stirring constantly cheese is melted and mixture is creamy throughout.  Spoon into heated tortillas and roll up burrito-style.
NOTE: Number of burritos depends on size tortilla used.

Submitter: 
Christine Hanson
Description: 
Chicken, salsa, corn and cheese combine into a creamy burrito filling that's on the table in 20 minutes.
