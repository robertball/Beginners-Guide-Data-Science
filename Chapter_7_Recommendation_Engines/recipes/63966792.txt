Title:
Grandma's lemon Cauliflower side dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
- 1 Large Cauliflower
- 3 slices of lemon
- 1 clove of garlic, crushed
- 1/4 cup of butter
- 1 teaspoon of salt
- juice of half of a lemon
- 1 egg, hardboiled

Directions:
1. Bring one cup of water to a boil in a large saucepan. Add cauliflower, lemon slices and garlic, cover and steam 5-10 minutes,
2. Drain cauliflower and remove lemon slices and garlic. Set cauliflower aside in a clean, serving dish.
3. melt butter in the same pan over medium-low heat. Add salt, and fresh squeezed lemon juice. Once fully mixed, pour butter mixture over the cauliflower. Just before serving, place the egg on top. Serve hot.
