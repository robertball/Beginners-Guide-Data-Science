Title:
Bill's taco seasoned meat - Lower Sodium Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tblspn organic wheat flour
6 tspn chilli powder
1/2 tspn onion powder
1 tspn garlic powder
1 tspn Mrs. Dash Table Blend
1/2 tspn paprika
1/2 tspn cumin
1/2 tspn sugar
1 lb lean grond beef
1 tspn minced onion
1 tspn minced garlic
2 tblspn olive oil
1/4 tspn cayenne pepper
1 cup beef broth (Organic low sodium I use Pacific or Imagine best flavor and lowest sodium)

Directions:
Mix flour, chili powder, onion powder, garlic powder, Mrs. Dash, paprika, cumin and sugar in a bowl, mix well.
In a large skillet on med/high heat, add olive oil, minced onion, minced garlic and beef.  Cook until well browned.  Add 1 cup beef broth and bring to a boil on high heat.  Once boiling, reduce heat to med. and stir in flour mixture stirring continuously.  Reduce heat and cook on med/high for 5-10 minutes stirring often, reduce heat to low and continue to simmer until the desired consistancy, about 5 minutes.  Serve with your favorite dishes!!

Submitter: 
Bill Falk
Description: 
A good replacement to packaged taco seasoning... great flavor with very little sodium or junk you don't need anyway!!!!
