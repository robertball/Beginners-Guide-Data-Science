Title:
Cake Mix Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1 cake mix of your choice
-2 eggs
-1/2 cup of vegetable oil
-1/2 bag of chocolate chips

Directions:
1.preheat the oven to 350 degrees 2.pour the dry cake mix into a bowl 3.add the eggs and oil to the bowl with the cake mix 4.stir well 5.add the chocolate chips and stir in 6.roll the cookie dough into balls 7.place 12 on a ungreased pan evenly apart 8.Cook for 7-9 minutes 9.let them cool and serve!
