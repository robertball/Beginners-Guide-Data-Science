Title:
Peach Nectarine Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium peaches, peeled and sliced
5 medium nectarines, peeled and sliced
1/2 cup organic cane sugar
2 tbsp cornstarch
2 tbsp lemon juice
1 pouch sugar cookie mix
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 tsp vanilla
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.  Spray a 13x9 inch baking dish with cooking spray.
In a large bowl, toss lightly fruit, sugar, cornstarch, and lemon juice.  Pour into baking dish.
In a large bowl, combine cookie mix, butter, vanilla, and egg with a spoon until soft dough forms.  Spoon by heaping tablespoonful over fruit mixture.
Bake 35 to 40 minutes or until filling is bubbly and topping is golden brown.  Cool 10 to 15 minutes and serve warm.
