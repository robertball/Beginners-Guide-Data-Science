Title:
Onion Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup unsalted butter
7-8 large onions* cut into large chunks
1/2 cup uncooked rice (I use texmati)
5 cups boiling salted water
1/2 cup grated swiss or jarlsberg
2/3 cup half and half
salt
*walla walla or maui onions are good for recipes - if using ordinary or yellow or white onions, soak the chunks in milk for 2 hours before starting recipe

Directions:
Melt butter in large skillet
Add onion chunks and saute until transparent
Remove from heat
Preheat oven to 325
Cook rice in boiling water for 5 minutes
Drain well
Stir rice into onions along with cheese and half and half
Season with salt to taste
Put mixture into shallow 2 quart dish
Bake for 1 hour

Submitter:

Description: 
I remember this having a vinaigrette to go with it, but I haven't made that part in so long because the main part gets devoured right out of the oven!
