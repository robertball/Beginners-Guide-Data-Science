Title:
Banana Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 Tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2/3 cup butter, softened
2 eggs
1 packet instant pudding (any complimentary flavor)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/8 teaspoon almond extract
3 overripe bananas, mashed
12 oz semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line cookie sheets with parchment paper. Sift the flour, baking powder, salt, and baking soda together, and set aside.
Cream the butter with the sugars until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs until creamy. Add pudding and mix well. Add almond and vanilla extracts. Mix in the mashed bananas. Add the flour mixture, and stir until just combined. Stir in the chocolate chips. Drop by spoonfuls onto prepared cookie sheets.
Bake in preheated oven for 12 to 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Christine Weber
Description: 
This recipe uses very ripe bananas, the ones which you would not want to eat.  The riper the bananas are, the more flavor they have.Modified from: Banana Chocolate Chip Cookies
