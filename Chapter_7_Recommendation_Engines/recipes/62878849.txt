Title:
Chicken Pot Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs if boneless, skinless chicken cubed
1 2/3 cups vegetables
1 10 oz can low fat cream of chicken soup
1 cup Bisquick reduced fat baking mix
1/2 cup skim milk
1 egg

Directions:
Preheat over to 400
Mix chicken, veggies and soup and pour into a 9 inch round glass pan.  Mix Bisquick, milk and egg and pour on top of chicken mixture.
Bake in oven for 30 minutes.
