Title:
Chocolate Avocado Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large avocados - peeled, pitted, and cubed
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
6 dates
1/3 cup soy or almond milk
2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
Blend avocados, cocoa powder, dates, milk, and vanilla extract in a blender until smooth. Refrigerate pudding until chilled, about 30 minutes.
