Title:
Fantastic Flax Eggs or Chia Eggs! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/934811.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tablespoon Flax Seeds OR 1 Tablespoon Chia Seeds
1 Tablespoon Lecithin Granules
1/4 cup water

Directions:
Bowl water.
Add 1 Flax  or Chia to water.
Let sit for 5 minutes.
Stir Flax or Chia.
Add Flax or Chia mixture and Lecithin to blender.
Blend mixture until gooey.

Submitter: 
ChrissyintheKitchen
Description: 
A healthy egg substitute, these flax eggs can be used as a substitute for eggs in most recipes.
