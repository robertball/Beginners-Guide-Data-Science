Title:
Crockpot Beef and Sweet Potato Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 lbs top sirloin cut in cubes
1 large sweet potato, cut into about 1‚Äù cubes
1 large yellow onion, diced
1-1/2 cups beef stock
2/ 3 cup coconut milk
3 T red curry paste
2 T peanut butter
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp cinnamon
2 cups cauliflower florets
1 cup green beans, rinsed, trimmed and cut pieces
3 cups cooked brown rice to serve with curry

Directions:
Mix all of the ingredients except the cauliflower florets and green beans and place them in a crock pot, cover and cook on low for 7 hours.
After the curry has been cooking for almost 7 hours, add cauliflower and green beans and cook for another hour.
Remember to start the brown rice about 40 minutes before the meal is done. The rice is really important!
