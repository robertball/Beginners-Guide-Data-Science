Title:
maple pork chops and apples Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pork chops thick  cut
salt and peper
2TBS butter
12 baby carrots
2 medium apples sliced and cored and seeds removed
1/4 cup maple syrup

Directions:
sprinkle pork chops with salt and peper , in skilet melt butter over medium heat and pork chops brown for 2 minutes  turning once , reduce heat to medium low add carrots , apples and maple syrup cover for 8 minutes, using a slotted spoon  transfer chops and carrots and apples to a platter bring syrup   mixture to a boil boil for 1 or 2 minutes untill thickend pour over pork chops and apples and carrots

maple syrup and apples make these pork chops so tasty and good
