Title:
Strawberry Fruit Fluff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/303789.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3oz. package of strawberry jello.
2/3 cup of boiling water.
2 cups of ice cubes.
8 oz. container of frozen whipped topping, thawed.
1 cup diced strawberrys.
2 strawberrys for garnish

Directions:
Dissolve jello in a 1 1/2 quart casserole dish.
Add ice till thickens, remove all unmelted ice with a slotted spoon.
Add whipped topping and strawberrys, blend with a wire whip untill it's all pink [incorporated]
Slice exta strawberrys in half, garnish fluff.

Submitter: 
Trish Beier
Description: 
I have to make this for every large holiday, my kids get upset if I don't. It's also great for a summertime jello.
