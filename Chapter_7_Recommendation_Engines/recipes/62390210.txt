Title:
Grapefruit & Ginger Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Grapefruit
2 Limes
1/2 CUP WINE VINEGAR
1/2 CUP LIGHT SOY SAUCE
1 OUNCE FRESH GINGER ROOT
1 CUP FRESH BASIL LEAFS (Chopped coursley)
1 TBSP FRESH THYME LEAFS
1 TBSP FRESH DILL WEED

Directions:
Squeeze juice from lime & grapefruit.  Mince ginger and herbs.  Combine ingredients.  If using on fish, marinate for just 1-2 hours so vinegar does not "cook" it.

Submitter:

Description: 
Tangy marinade for fish, chicken, or lamb.
