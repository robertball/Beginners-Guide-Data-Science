Title:
Gluten Free Pasta and Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3039111.jpg
Ingredients:
Rinsed shrimp
1/4 cup of rice flour
1/8 cup chopped onion
1 teaspoon chopped garlic
Pinch of salt
1 teaspoon dry basil

Directions:
Pasta:
Boil Gluten Free pasta according to instructions. My favorite type is quinoa pasta.
Gluten Free “Fried” Shrimp:
In a plastic bag, toss:
Rinsed shrimp
1/4 cup of rice flour
1/8 cup chopped onion
1 teaspoon chopped garlic
Pinch of salt
1 teaspoon dry basil
Sauté breaded shrimp over olive oil on medium heat.
