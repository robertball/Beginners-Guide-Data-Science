Title:
Alison's FABULOUS mango chutney Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Mangoes (canned or fresh)
1/2 cup white vinegar
1/3 cup surgar
1/2 tsp salt
Onions
garlic
oil
salt
turmeric
coriander
cinnamon (very little)
curry
giner
nutmeg

Directions:
Mix the first four ingredients in a bowl. Saute the next 3 ingredients until translucent. Add the rest of the ingredients to the onion mixture, then add the mangoes. Cook in the pot for a few minutes.
