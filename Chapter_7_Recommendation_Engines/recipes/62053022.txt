Title:
Raisin Sauce for Ham Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoons cornstarch
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1 tablespoon vinegar
1 cup raisins
1/4 teaspoon grated lemon peel
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 1/2 cups water

Directions:
Combine brown sugar, cornstarch and dry mustard in a small saucepan. Place saucepan over medium heat. Slowly add vinegar, raisins, lemon peel, lemon juice and water. Continue cooking raisin sauce over medium heat, stirring constantly, until thick and bubbly.

Submitter: 
claudygirl
Description: 
Serve this raisin sauce warm, in a bowl with a ladel, and let your guests spoon it over themselves.  It's wonderful, but not everyone likes raisins.
