Ingredients:
1 14 ounce can of artichoke hearts drained
1-2 cloves of garlic
1 Cup of mayonnaise
1 Cup Grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Add all ingredients to a food processor and pulse until combined.
Transfer mixture to a small greased baking dish.
Bake for 20-30 minutes or until hot and bubbly
Serve hot with crackers, crusty bread, bagel chips.

Submitter: 
Amy Standaert
Description: 
This is a simple and basic artichoke dip recipe that is so easy and fast to make. Delicious as is but you can add ingredients to it and be creative.
