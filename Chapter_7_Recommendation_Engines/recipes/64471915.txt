Title:
Raspberry Chia Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup frozen raspberries
3/4 cup apple juice
1/2 cup plain yogurt
1 banana
1 T chia seeds
1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract (optional)

Directions:
Blend raspberries, apple juice, yogurt, banana, chia seeds, and vanilla extract (optional) until smooth, 30-45 seconds. Serve immediately.
