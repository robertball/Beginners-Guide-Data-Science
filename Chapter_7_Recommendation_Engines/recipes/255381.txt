Title:
Strawberry Banana Avocado Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4264832.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons oats
4 ice cubes
1 small banana
1/2 avocado, peeled and pitted
2 strawberries, hulled, or more to taste
1 cup carrot juice
1 (5.3 ounce) container banana cream yogurt (such as OikosÂ®)

Directions:
Grind oats in a blender into a powder-like consistency. Add ice cubes; pulse until ground. Add banana, avocado, and strawberries; blend until smooth. Pour in carrot juice and yogurt; blend until combined.
