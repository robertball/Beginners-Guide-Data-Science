Title:
Cheddar Chicken Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbsp. Vegetable oil
1 1/2 cups Cheez-it cheddar crackers
1/2 tsp. black pepper
1 1/2 lbs. chicken tenders
1/3 cup dijon mustard

Directions:
Preheat oven to 475*F. Pulse crackers in a food processor to make coarse crumbs. Place in a medium bowl and stir in pepper. Toss chicken in the mustard and coat with the crumbs. Place on a parchment lined pan and bake for 15 minutes. Turn the chicken over once during the baking time. Serve with plum sauce or your favourite dipping sauce.
