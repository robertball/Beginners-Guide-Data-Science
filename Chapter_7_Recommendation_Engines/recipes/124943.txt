Title:
Tuna Snack Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983914.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6 ounce) can tuna, drained and flaked
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
2 teaspoons lemon juice
1 teaspoon onion salt
3 drops hot pepper sauce
1/4 cup minced fresh parsley

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first five ingredients until well mixed. Shape into a ball; roll in parsley. Chill. Serve with crackers.

Submitter: 
Denise  Goedeken
Description: 
'For a satisfying late-night snack, all you need is this creamy tuna spread and a box of your favorite crackers,' remarks field editor Denise Goedeken of Platte Center, Nebraska.
