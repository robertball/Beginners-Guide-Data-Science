Title:
Irish Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c Butter
1 1/2 c Sugar
1 egg
1/2 t vanilla
2 1/4 c flour
1/2 t baking powder
1/2 t salt
Decorating sugar

Directions:
Cream butter
Add sugar, edd, and vanilla to butter and beat well
Add sifted flour, baking powder and salt.
Stir well
Chill dough over night
Form into balls and dip tops into green decorating sugar
Bake at 350 8-10 min

Submitter: 
violet
Description: 
We grew up eating these yummy cookies.  They are great to have kids help and make.  We always top them with green sugar, but you can use any color.
