Title:
Peanut Butter and Hot Fudge Pudding Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup flour
1/4 cup sugar
3/4 tsp. baking powder
1/3 cup milk
1 tbs. vegetable oil
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1/4 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup sugar
3 tbs. unsweetened cocoa powder
1 cup boiling water

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the flour, 1/4 cup sugar and baking powder.
Add the milk, oil, and vanilla;  stir until smooth.
Mix in the peanut butter.
Pour mixture in slow cooker (small-medium sized).
In the same bowl, stir together 1/2 cup sugar and cocoa powder.
Gradually stir in the boiling water.  Pour the mixture over the batter in the slow cooker, do not stir.
Cover; cook on high for 2-3 hours.
