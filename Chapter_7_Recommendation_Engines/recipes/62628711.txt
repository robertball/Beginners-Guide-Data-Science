Title:
No Bake Reese's Peanut Butter Cup Peanut Butter Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/575783.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Package of Cream Cheese
1 Cup of Peanut Butter
12 ounces Cool Whip
3/4 cup sugar
1 graham cracker crust, chocolate one works great too!
10 Reese's Peanut Butter Cups

Directions:
Combine the peanut butter, cream cheese and sugar.
Add Cool Whip until smooth.
Pour half of mixture onto the crust.
Top with 5 crumbled Reese's cups.
Cover that with remaining mixture and crumble remaining Resse's Cups on top.
Cover pie and refrigerate.

Description: 
This is quite easily one of the most delicious Reese's Peanut Butter Cup Peanut Butter Pie recipes you could ever try/make, and best of all it is No Bake!
