Title:
Golden Lamb Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup honey
1/2 cup mustard
1/8 tsp onion salt
1/8 tsp pepper
4 lamb chops

Directions:
Heat honey, mustard, onion salt and pepper in a sauce pan, stirring occasionally and keep it warm over low heat.
Turn on Broiler at 550F(285C)
Broil chops 3 inches from heat for 7 minutes, brush with honey mixture. Turn chops and broil 5 minutes longer, brushing again.
Serve remaining glaze with chops.
