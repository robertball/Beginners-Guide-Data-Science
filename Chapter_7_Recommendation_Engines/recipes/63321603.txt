Title:
Grandpa Omo's BBQ Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Ground Beef
1 tsp. Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 cup Ketchup
1 TB. Sugar
1 tsp. Mustard
Salt to taste

Directions:
Brown beef in skillet. Drain. In a bowl mix other ingredients. Pour into beef. Stir and simmer.
