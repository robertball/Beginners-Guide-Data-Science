Title:
Rhonda's Pineapple Mango Martini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Infused Vodka: 750ml good quality vodka
1 fresh pineapple
Martini:1 part soda water
1 part Dailys Mango Mix
2 parts infused vodka

Directions:
Infused Vodka: Peel and core you pineapple. Quarter and slice the pineapple (1/2 inch slices). Simply pour the entire bottle of vodka over the pineapple in a pitcher.(Infuse for 1-2 weeks)This does not need refrigerated if pineapple is covered! Once the vodka is ready:
In a shaker measure your portions with good amount of ice and shake well.
Pour into a martini glass and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Rhonda Holtz Hill
Description: 
Once you have infused your vodka this recipe is a cinch! It is easier then you might think. Just takes a little planning. The results are oo la la!!
