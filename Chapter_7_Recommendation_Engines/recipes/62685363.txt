Title:
creamy curried deviled eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs
1/3 c mayo
2 tsp yellow mustard
3 tblsp chopped green onion or chives
1 tbsp sweet relish
pinch curry powder
salt
pepper
paprika

Directions:
Place eggs in large pot of water and bring to a boil.  Let boil for 5 min and turn burner off, let sit for another 15 min (for bright yellow yolks.)  Cool eggs off in cold water until easy to handle.  peel and dry off eggs.  cut eat egg lengthwise and remove yolks into a bowl.  arrange whites on a plate and season with sea salt and fresh ground pepper.  mash yolks and add the mayo, mustard, relish, onion, and curry (start with a small dash of curry adding more to taste as curry can overwhelm easily) fill hollows of eggs with the mixture and sprinkle with paprika. serve immediatley or chilled

Submitter:

Description: 
this is a family recipe.  It makes a good appetizer or a light lunch.
