Title:
Halloween Chex Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8oz. white chocolazte baking bars, coarsely chopped
4 c. corn chex or rice chex
2 c. bite size pretzel twists
1/2 c. raisins
1 c. candy corn
1/2 cup candy decors [bones, ghosts, pumpkins]

Directions:
1. In a large microwave safe bowl microwave chopped baking bars uncovered on high 1 1/2 min to 2 1/2 mins stiring every 30 seconds until mealted and smooth.
2. Gently stir in cereal, pretzals and raisin until coated.  Stir in candy corn and decors.
3. Spread on was paper until cooled and chocolate is set.  Break into chunks and store loosely covered.

Submitter: 
Linda Peterson
Description: 
Trick or treat?  Suprise everyone with this fruit, candy and cereal mix.
