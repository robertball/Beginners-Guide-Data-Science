Title:
Pineapple Avocado dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
¾ cup DOLE Canned Pineapple Juice
3 Tbsp chopped fresh basil leaves
2 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
1 Tbsp fresh chives, chopped
1 Tbsp chopped fresh tarragon
¼ tsp garlic, minced
1 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 avocado, chopped
salt and black pepper, to taste

Directions:
Combine pineapple juice, basil, lemon juice, chives, tarragon, garlic, oil, avocado in a blender. Cover; blend until smooth. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve with kale or broccoli slaw.
