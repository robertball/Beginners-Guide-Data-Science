Title:
Goldfish Pizzas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987392.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pieces Pepperidge Farm® Goldfish™ 100% Whole Wheat Sandwich Bread
1/4 cup Prego® Traditional Italian Sauce
1/2 cup shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese
Pitted ripe olives

Directions:
Heat the oven to 350 degrees F. Place the bread pieces, split-side up, onto a baking sheet.
Spread 1 tablespoon sauce on each bread piece. Top each with 2 tablespoons cheese.
Bake for 10 minutes or until the cheese is melted. Decorate the faces of the goldfish with the olives, if desired.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Three ingredients and twenty-five minutes is all you need to make these kid-friendly pizzas that adults love too!
