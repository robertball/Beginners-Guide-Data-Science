Title:
Delectable No-Bake Chocolate Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c. white sugar
1/3 c. cocoa
1/2 c. OR 1 stick margarine
1/2 c. milk
1/2 t. vanilla extract
1/2 c. peanut butter
3 c. quick oatmeal

Directions:
Place sugar, cocoa, milk, and margarine in a pan.
Bring to a boil and boil hard for 1 minute.
Remove from heat.
Add vanilla and peanut butter. Blend well, then stir in oatmeal.
Drop by Tablespoons onto wax paper or saran wrap.
