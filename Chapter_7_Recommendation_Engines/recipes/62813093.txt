Title:
GUACAMOLE ---MY WAY! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/755277.jpg
Ingredients:
2 ripe avocados
1 cup sour cream
1/4 cup lime or lemon juice
2 cloves garlic,minced
1/2 tsp.curry
1/2 tsp.chili powder
1/2 tsp.worcestershire sauce
2 Tbsp.hot taco sauce
1/4 cup salsa
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Peel,halve and pit avocados and place in bowl.
Add sour cream and remaining ingredients.
Mix well untill well blended.Season with salt and pepper.
Turn into serving bowl.Cover and refrigerate.
At it's best if made a day ahead to blend flavors.
