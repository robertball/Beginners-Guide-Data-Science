Title:
Cabbage Apple Chicken Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts, cut in strips
2 Tbsp margarine
8 oz cabbage, grated
2 apples, finely chopped
2 green onions, sliced
4 oz sour cream
4 tsp flour
1/2 tsp chicken bouillon
1/8 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup water
6 flour tortillas, large
4 oz cheddar cheese, grated
2 Tbsp onion, chopped

Directions:
In large frypan, saute chicken in margarine 2-3 minutes (half at a time) and remove.  Add cabbage, apple and green onions to frypan and cook until tender.  Add chicken and mix.
In bowl, add flour to sour cream, then add to frying pan along with bouillon cube, cinnamon and 1/2 cup water.  Cook and stir until heated and bubbly.
Place in tortillas and roll up.  Place seam side down in a 9 x 13 inch baking dish.  Bake covered at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes.  Sprinkle with cheese and onion.  Bake uncovered 2-3 minutes more or until cheese melts.
