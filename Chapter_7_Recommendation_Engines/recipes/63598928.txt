Title:
Southwest Ground Chicken Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground chicken
1 packet taco seasoning
1 red bell pepper, diced
1 can black beans
1 can whole kernel sweet corn
1 cup grated Monterey Jack cheese
10 flour tortillas - taco size or larger

Directions:
Brown ground chicken in large skillet on medium heat.
Once chicken is thoroughly cooked, add taco seasoning and stir to combine.
Add bell pepper, black beans, and corn to skillet. Stir together and let cook until veggies are cooked through. About 10 minutes on low heat.
Warm tortillas and fill with about 3/4 cup of filling from the skillet. Top with cheese and wrap burrito style.
Enjoy!
