Title:
EASY Old English Cheese Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 oz jar Kraft Old English cheese
8 oz tub chive & onion cream cheese

Directions:
Warm up both cheeses and mix them together well.
Serve with a round of pumpernickel bread.

Submitter: 
Emily
Description: 
By far the easiest dip recipe I've ever found, and tasty too. Try to find one easier. You can't.
