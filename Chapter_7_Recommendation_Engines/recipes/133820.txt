Title:
Whole Wheat Tuna Melts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 small sweet onion, peeled and diced
1 (12 ounce) can tuna, drained
2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
1 cup light mayonnaise
16 slices Sara Lee® Soft and Smooth 100% Whole Wheat Bread

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a bowl, thoroughly mix onion, tuna, mozzarella, and mayonnaise. Spread mixture on 8 slices of  bread, and top with remaining 8 slices to form sandwiches. Arrange sandwiches on a cookie sheet.
Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sara Lee®
Description: 
Tuna and mozzarella combine to make a great bubbly-hot filling for these tuna melts.
