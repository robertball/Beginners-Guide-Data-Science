Title:
Slow Cooker Sloppy Chicken Joes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Nonstick cooking spray
3 # uncooked ground chicken or ground turkey
2 14-oz jars pizza sauce
2 C frozen peppers and onion stir-fry vegetables, thawed and chopped
14 oz can diced tomatoes
8 hoagie rolls, split
8 slices mozzarella or provolone cheese (8 oz)

Directions:
Coat a lg. skillet w/cooking spray. Heat skillet over med-high heat. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink, stirring to break apart. In a 3-4 qt. slow cooker, combine chicken, pizza sauce, vegetables and undrained tomatoes.
Cover and cook on low heat for 6-8 hr. or high heat 3-4 hrs.
Arrange split rolls, cut side up, on an broiler pan. Broil 3-4 inches from heat for 1-2 min or until toasted. Spoon chicken mixture onto bottoms of rolls. Top w/chees and roll tops.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Made with ground chicken or ground turkey.  Pizza sauce and diced tomatoes make a sauce and frozen peppers and onions stir-fry vegetables are added. This is served on hoagie rolls w/mozzarella cheese
