Title:
Chocolate Covered Strawberry Daiquiri Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/981945.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 fluid ounce) can BACARDI® Mixers Strawberry Daiquiri frozen mix
1/2 cup BACARDI® Rum
Creme de Cocoa liqueur
Chocolate syrup
5 cups ice
Strawberries, for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Strawberry Daiquiri mix, BACARDI(R) Rum and ice for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Rim glasses with chocolate syrup and pour daiquiris into glass.
Top each glass with creme de cocoa and garnish with strawberries.

Submitter: 
BACARDI® Mixers
Description: 
This provocative Strawberry Daiquiri is made with a touch of chocolate liqueur, and served in a chocolate rimmed glass that is garnished with a fresh strawberry.
