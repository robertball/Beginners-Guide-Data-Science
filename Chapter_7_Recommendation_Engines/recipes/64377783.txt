Title:
Vegetarian Fajitas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup julienne green peppers
1/4 cup julienne onions
1/4 cup julienne carrots
1/4 cup broccoli
1t adobo seasoning
1 garlic clove minced
2 corn tortillas
1/2 cup refried beans
1/2 cup rice

Directions:
1. Over medium heat saute all vegetables for about 5 minutes
2. Add chopped garlic and Adobo Seasoning and saute for 3 more minutes
3. Heat up tortillas, beans and rice
4. Serve

Submitter: 
Henry Johannes
Description: 
Put together for dieting.  This is a vegetarian plate.  You will need to have the rice and beans already cooked for this recipe.
