Title:
Grandma Fletcher's Spinach Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 9" Baked Pie Crust
8-10 strips fried crumbled bacon
2 packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed and thoroughly drained
3 eggs
1 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
3/4 cup sour cream
1/8-1/4 tsp carraway seeds
1/2 - 1 tsp minced parsley
6-8 wedges sharp cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
Beat eggs slightly.  Add salt, pepper, sour cream, and bacon, mixing well.  Add spinach and carraway seeds and mix well.  Pour into baked pie shell.  Sprinkle with parsley.
Bake at 375 degrees F for 20 minutes.  Remove from oven, place cheese on top, and continue baking for another 10 minutes (30 minutes total).  Serve warm.

Submitter:

Description: 
My grandma made this when I was little and we recently started making it again - tastes exactly the way I remembered!
