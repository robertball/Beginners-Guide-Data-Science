Title:
Hawaii Banana Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 Bananas, sliced
1 Graham Cracker Pie shell
1, 6 oz package crushed pecans (optional)
1 small bag sweet flaked coconut
1 large can crushed pineapple
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup water
6 Tbs.Flour

Directions:
In saucepan add can of pineapple (along with juices), sugar, water and flour; stir and cook for about 4-5 minutes or until thickened.
Put sliced bananas in the graham cracker shell, pour on the thickened pineapple mixture.
Top with flaked coconut and pecans.
Let set in refrigerator for at least 1-2 hours.
