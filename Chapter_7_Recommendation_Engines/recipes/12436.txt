Title:
Key Lime Pie V Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/377757.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
2 egg yolk, beaten (optional)
1/4 cup key lime juice
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Whisk the egg yolks into the milk.   Stir in lime juice until well combined.  Pour filling into graham cracker crust.
Refrigerate for 2 hours if made without egg yolks, 4 hours or overnight with egg yolks.

Submitter: 
Kristi Finsterwald
Description: 
This is a really easy recipe.  Also works great with nonfat sweetened condensed milk and a reduced fat pie crust.  Great topped with whipped topping or baked meringue. A baked pie shell may be used instead of graham cracker crust.
