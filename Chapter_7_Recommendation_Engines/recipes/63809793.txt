Title:
Darrian's Garlic Mashpotatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Potatoes
Garlic
salt
butter
Milk

Directions:
Peel the potatoes,  cut into pieces, put into pot with water just covering and boil
add pressed garlic and salt
Boil until done
Pour off water
Puff on low heat
add butter and milk and mash with masher.
