Title:
Shady Lady Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 oz Mandarin Orange Vodka (we use Absolut)
1 1/2 oz Peach Schnapps
2 oz Orange Juice
Splash of Grenadine
1 Orange Wedge (for garnish)

Directions:
Combine Orange Vodka, Orange Juice, Peach Schnapps and Grenadine in a cocktail shaker with ice.
Shake to combine.
Strain mixture into a chilled martini glass and garnish with the orange wedge.
