Title:
Southwest Chicken soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 chicken legs (cooked)
1 can corn
1 can rotel tomatoes and green chillies
1 can black beans (drained)
2 tablespoons chicken bullion
2 cups water
1/2 cup cheddar cheese
2 tablespoons sour cream

Directions:
combine all ingredients (except cheese and sour cream) and simmer 25 minutes.
Top with  cheese and  sour cream.
