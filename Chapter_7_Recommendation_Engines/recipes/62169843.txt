Title:
Dutch Pepernoten Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 c. butter
1/2 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. white sugar
1 tbsp. milk
1 c. self-rising flour
1 tsp. ground nutmeg
1 tsp. ground cloves
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. ground aniseed
1/2 tsp. ground ginger

Directions:
Cream the butter and sugar.
Add spices, mix well.
Add the flour and milk, mix well.
Roll into marble-sized balls, place on cookie sheet.
Bake for 10-15 minutes at 325ยบ until golden.

Peggy McCormack

Traditional Dutch treat popular in December.
