Title:
More Than Just Beans Recipe

Ingredients:
1 pound Kielbasa or smoked sausage; sliced in small pieces
1 - 48oz. jar of Northern Beans; undrained
1 - 32oz. package of sauerkraut, drained
1 1/2 - 2 cups brown sugar
3/4 cup ketchup
2 large potatoes, boiled, drained and cubed
1 small onion, diced
1 jalape√±o, diced (optional)

Directions:
Stir all ingredients, except the potatoes, and place in the slow cooker.
Cook on high for 1 1/2 hours.
Add potatoes, stir well.
Continue cooking for another 1 1/2 hours.
