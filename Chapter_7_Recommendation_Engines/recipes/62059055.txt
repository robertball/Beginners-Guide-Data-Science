Title:
Vegetarian Taco Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 prepared Italian style pizza shell
1/2 can vegetarian refried beans
1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
Toppings (optional):shredded lettuce, chopped tomato, sliced black olives, sliced green onions, sour cream, jalapeno peppers, salsa, and crushed  tortilla chips

Directions:
Spread beans over pizza shell and top with cheese. Bake at 400 F for 8-10 minutes or until cheese is melted and beans and crust are heated through. Remove from oven and let cool slightly for 3-4 minutes. Slice pizza and top each piece with your choice of toppings.

Submitter: 
jen53207
Description: 
This pizza is so easy to make and helps you get in your daily veggies. You can use either vegetarian or fat-free refried beans - they are both made without lard. I like to bake this pizza using a pizza stone to keep the crust crispy.
