Title:
Spinach & Gruyere Souffle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz frozen leaf spinach, thawed
4 tbs butter
3 tbs flour
1 cup milk
salt & pepper
3 eggs, separated plus 1 egg white
1 cup shredded Gruyere cheese

Directions:
Grease a 1 1/2 quart souffle dish. Remove all moisture from spinach. Mince. Melt butter in a saucepan, add spinach, and cook a few minutes to drive off any moisture. Add flour and cook gently 1 minute, stirring. Remove the pan from the heat and gradually stir in milk. Season with salt and pepper and bring to a boil slowly. Reduce heat and cook, stirring, until thickened. Cool slightly, then beat in the egg yolks, one at a time, ans 3/4 cup cheese. Beat egg whites until stiff, then fold into the mixture. Spoon into souffle dish and sprinkle with remaining cheese. Stand the dish on a baking sheet and bake in a preheated, 375 oven until well risen and just set, about 30 minutes. Serve immediately.
