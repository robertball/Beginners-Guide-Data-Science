Title:
Creamsicle Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/937861.jpg
Ingredients:
1 10.2g pkg of sugar free Orange Jell-O
1 small can mandarin oranges, drained
1 30g pkg of fat free instant vanilla pudding
1 1 litre container of light Cool Whip

Directions:
Dissolve Jell-O in 1 cup boiling water. Add 3 ice cubes to 1 cup and fill with cold water. Add to Jell-O. Stir until ice is melted, set aside for 5 minutes.
Stir in oranges (Keep 8 - 10 slices for decorating the top of the dessert).
With electric mixer, blend in instant vanilla pudding, beating until well blended. Let stand 15 minutes.
Fold in 1/2 of the tub of Cool Whip.
Spoon into individual serving dishes or one large dish, top with remaining Cool Whip. Decorate with remaining orange slices. Store, covered, in fridge until ready to serve.
