Title:
Peanut Butter and Oat Suet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Lard
2 cups yellow cornmeal
2 cups rolled oats
2 cups chunky peanut butter
2 cups wild bird seed
1 cups coarse bread crumbs

Directions:
Melt lard and peanut butter in large pot over low flame.
Stir in remaining ingredients until well mixed.
Pour into greased 8X8 aluminum disposable pans.
Refrigerate until firm.
Feed in large suet cage.

Submitter: 
docswife
Description: 
Easy to prepare bird food for suet feeders.  This can be made in batches and stored for future use.
