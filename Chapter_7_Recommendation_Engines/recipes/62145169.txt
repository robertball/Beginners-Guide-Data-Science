Title:
Easy Hashbrown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 Stick of Melted Butter
1 Chopped Onion (small)
Salt and Pepper to taste
1 Can of Cream of Chicken Soup
8 Ounces of Sour Cream
1 Cup of Shredded Cheddar Cheesse
2 Lbs. of Thawed Hash Browns (Cubed, not Shredded)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients and pour into pan.
Bake @ 350 for 35 - 45 minutes in a 9 x 13 pan.

Submitter: 
LisaT
Description: 
This is a simple, yet delicious, recipe to make. It's wonderful for brunch! All ages will love this.
