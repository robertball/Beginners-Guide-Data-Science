Title:
Turkey Thai Noodle Toss Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup soy sauce
3 tbsp lime juice
1 tbsp each of minced garlic and ginger
1 tbsp each, fish sauce, honey and toasted sesame oil
pinch hot pepper flakes
8 oz spaghettini
1 lb, lean ground turkey
oil for frying
1 bag (400 g) coleslaw blend
1 red pepper, thinly sliced
fresh cilantro or mint
Bean Sprouts
Sweet Thai Chili Sauce

Directions:
Whisk soy sauce wiht lime juice, ginger, garlic, fish sauce, honey seame oil and pepper flakes until combined. Set aside.
Cook spaghettini al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water.
Heat oil in a wok over medium high heat. Add turkey and stir fry until browned. Add coleslaw blend and red pepper, stir fry for until tender crisp.
Add hot cooked spaghettini, sauce mixture and reserved pasta water. Toss to coat. Remove from heat and add bean sprouts and cilantro. Serve and drizzle with sweet Thai chili sauce.
