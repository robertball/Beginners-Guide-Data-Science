Title:
Bakkoula Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/953323.jpg
Ingredients:
-1 kg of spinach in branch
-3 tablespoons of olive oil
-1 teaspoon of cumin
-1 teaspoon of sweet pepper (paprika)
-1/2 teaspoon Cayenne Pepper
-Salt (according to taste)
-3 cloves garlic
-3 tablespoons chopped cilantro
-3 tablespoons chopped parsley
-the juice of one lemon
-10 Calamata olives (optional)
-1/2 lemon

Directions:
1 - Clean the spinach and make them Blanch.
2 - Chop them finely.
3 - Have the spinach in a frying pan, add the olive oil, cumin, sweet pepper and Cayenne, salt, minced garlic, parsley and cilantro.
4 - Change back to all on low heat for 10 minutes, stir with a wooden spoon by watering with the juice of lemon at the end.
5. Serve hot or cold by decorating the dish of olives and lemon slices.
Note: You can replace the spinach by a Swiss chard or mix spinach, Swiss chard, coriander and parsley in equal proportions.

A favorite Moroccan spinach dip
