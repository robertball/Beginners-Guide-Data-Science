Title:
Banana Milk Coffee Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3931336.jpg
Ingredients:
1 very ripe medium banana
8 ounces milk
1 tablespoon simple syrup (optional)
Ice cubes
8 ounces cold brew coffee

Directions:
Put banana, milk, and optional simple syrup in a blender; process until very smooth, about 1 minute. Fill 2 16-ounce glasses with ice cubes and pour 4 ounces of cold brew coffee into each glass. Pour banana milk over the coffee, dividing it evenly between the glasses.
Note: You can use any kind of milk for this recipe, including soy and nut milks.

Submitter: 
foodelicious
Description: 
Adding creamy smooth banana milk to your morning cold brew not only makes it taste rich and delicious, it also sneaks in a heart-healthy dose of potassium, vitamins, and fiber. Talk about an eye-opening good-for-you breakfast in a cup.
