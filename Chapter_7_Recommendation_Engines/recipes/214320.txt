Title:
Creamy Carnival Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987851.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup boiling water
1 pkg. (4 serving size) JELL-O Strawberry Flavor Gelatin
2 cups vanilla ice cream
1/3 cup thawed COOL WHIP Whipped Topping

Directions:
Stir boiling water into dry gelatin mix in medium bowl with wire whisk at least 2 min. until completely dissolved. Add ice cream; mix well.
Pour into six dessert bowls. Refrigerate 30 min. or until set.
Top each serving with about 1 Tbsp. whipped topping just before serving.

Submitter: 
JELL-O
Description: 
Smooth ice cream is whisked into strawberry gelatin for this rich and fruity treat.
