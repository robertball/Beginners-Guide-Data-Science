Title:
Blueberry Lemon Glazed Breakfast Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 Cup flour
1 tsp. Baking powder
1/4 tsp. Salt
6 Tbsp. Unsalted butter
1 Cup sugar
2 Eggs
3 tsp. Finely grated lemon peel
1/2 Cup milk
1 1/2 Cup frozen blueberries, thawed and drained
Glaze
6 Tbsp. Lemon juice
2/3 Cup sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.
Line a loaf pan with parchment paper. Paper should hang over the sides. Grease the parchment paper. Combine flour,baking powder and salt and set aside. Cream butter with one cup sugar. Add eggs and beat until fluffy . Add lemon peel and milk to butter mixture and blend well . Add flour mixture and blend until just combined. Fold in blueberries. Pour batterinto loaf pan and spread evenly. Bake for 60 minutes or until golden brown and toothpick inserted into center comes out clean. Combine 2/3 cup sugar and lemon juice in small saucepan and heat until sugar dissolves. Using a toothpick , pierce the top of the warm loaf many times. Pour hot lemon glaze over the loaf and allow the loaf to cool in the pan completely. Remove loaf from pan and peel back paper . Slice and serve.
