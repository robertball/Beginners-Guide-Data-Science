Title:
Cabbage & Noodle Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Mild pork sausage
1 pound Hot pork sausage
1 head Cabbage-shredded large
1 Onion-chopped
1 T Parsley
1/2 tsp Marjoram
1/2 tsp Seasoned salt
salt & pepper to taste
2 Tbsp margarine
16 oz package of wide noodles

Directions:
Crumble sausage, add onions and brown in large pot. Remove from pot and add margarine and cabbage, saute till tender. Cook noodles till done per package directions. Add noodles and sausage/onions back to pot with spices and salt and pepper to taste.
