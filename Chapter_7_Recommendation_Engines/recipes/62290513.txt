Title:
Roberta's Smoked Salmon Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Canned Salmon 1 (6 ounce)
Mayonnaise 1/2 cup
Finely Diced Celery 1/3 cup
Lemon Juice 3 tablespoons
Dill Weed 3 Tablespoons
Liquid Smoke 1 Tablespoon
Plain Bread Crumbs 1/3 cup (optional)
Cajun Seasoning 1 Tablespoon (optional)
Parsley (optional)
Sliced whole Lemon 1 (optional)

Directions:
1. Remove larger bones and skin from salmon
2. Mix Salmon, Mayonnaise, Celery, Lemon Juice, Dill Weed, Liquid Smoke, Bread Crumbs & Cajun Seasoning.
3. Form a mold with mixture and refrigerate over night if possible.
4. Garnish with Lemons and Parsley and  serve with crackers.

Submitter:
