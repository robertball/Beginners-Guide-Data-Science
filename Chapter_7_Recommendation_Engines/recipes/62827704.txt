Title:
Cranberry Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz) pkg. cream cheese, softened
1 cup butter
1 1/2 cup sugar
4 eggs
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
2 1/4 cup flour, divided
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries patted dry
1/2 cup chopped pecans or walnuts
Confectioners sugar

Directions:
In a bowl, beat cream cheese, butter and sugar until smooth, add eggs, one at a time mixing well after each addition.
Combine 2 cups flour, baking powder and salt; gradually add to butter mixture.
Mix remaining flour with cranberries and nuts; fold into batter
Batter will be very thick.  Spoon into greased 10 inch fluted tube pan (Bundt pan).
Bake at 350 for 65 to 70 minutes.  Let stand 5 minutes before removing from pan.
Cool on wire rack.  dust with confectioners sugar and serve.
