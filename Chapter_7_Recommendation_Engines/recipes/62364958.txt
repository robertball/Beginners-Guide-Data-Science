Title:
Chicken Cordon Bleu I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 egg
bread crumbs
6 slices Swiss cheese
4 slices cooked ham
1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Coat a 7x11 inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
Pound chicken breasts to 1/4 inch thickness.
Sprinkle each piece of chicken on both sides with salt and pepper.  Dip in egg wash, dip in bread crumbs. Place 1 cheese slice and 1 ham slice on top of each breast. Roll up each breast, and secure with a toothpicks.  Place in baking dish, add dab of butter to each roll, salt and pepper tops.
Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink. Remove from oven, and place 1/2 cheese slice on top of each breast.  Return to oven for 3 to 5 minutes, or until cheese has melted. Remove toothpicks, and serve immediately.
