Title:
Budget Marie's Easy Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/271131.jpg
Ingredients:
4 pounds round tip roast
salt and pepper to taste
4 cloves of garlic, smashed
2 carrots, chopped
1 onion, chopped
2 cups beef broth, from concentrate
1/4 cup tomato paste
1 tablespoon oil

Directions:
Take the chuck roast and season with salt and pepper to taste. Place into slow cooker.
In a skillet (not no-stick) heat oil over medium high heat.  Add carrots and onions and cook until the vegetables begin to brown.  Add in tomato paste and stir to coat vegetables and let the paste begin to brown as well.  De-glaze the pan with the beef broth.  Add to slow cooker.
Cover and cook on low setting for 8 to 10 hours.  Remove beef from slow cooker, turn heat up to high.Shred the beef, removing any fatty pieces. Place beef back into broth of slow cooker, and let cook for another 1/2 hour.  (Just enough time to make mashed potatoes)

Submitter: 
CookingOnABudget
Description: 
There are so many recipes for pot roast out there and they are all basically the same.  For this recipe I decided to purchase a bottom round tip roast for that a was on sale and add the basic pot roast components and see what happened.  The result? A juicy, tender, shredded beef and sauce that was perfect served over mashed potatoes.Modified from: Marie's Easy Slow Cooker Pot Roast
