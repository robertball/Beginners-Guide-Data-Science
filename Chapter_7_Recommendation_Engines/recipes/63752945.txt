Title:
HAWAIIAN WEDDING CAKE FROSTING Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz.) pkg cream cheese
1 1/2 cup powdered (icing) sugar
1/2 cup butter
2 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp coconut extract (optional)

Directions:
Blend well.
Frost cooled cake.
Garnish with chopped nuts, coconut or chopped fruit, if desired.
