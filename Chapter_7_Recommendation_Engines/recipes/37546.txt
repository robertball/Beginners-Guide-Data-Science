Title:
Philadelphia's Pesto Christmas Tree Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983919.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese
1/3 cup DI GIORNO Basil Pesto Sauce
1 cinnamon stick

Directions:
Cut cream cheese in half diagonally.  Place triangles together to form Christmas tree shape on serving plate.
Top with pesto sauce.  Insert cinnamon stick at base of triangle for "tree trunk."  Serve with crackers. Special Extra: Use chopped red bell pepper as "ornaments" to decorate tree.
