Title:
Cold Herbed Spaghetti Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/412621.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tomatoes, diced
1/2 cucumber, peeled and diced
1- 2 zucchinis, sliced thinly or diced
2 C blanched broccoli florets
1/2 C black olives
Some people might like to add finely diced green pepper to this recipe. We leave it out.
Fresh basil, sliced thinly
Fresh parsley, finely chopped
Fresh thyme, finely chopped
Fresh oregano, finely chopped
Fresh rosemary, finely chopped
8 - 12 ounces spaghetti
Olive Oil and regular or balsamic vinegar
or you can use bottled Italian dressing.
1/2 C freshly shredded parmesan
Salt and freshly ground pepper
For a non-vegetarian meal-sized salad, add:
1 C cubed fresh mozzarella
1/2 cubed summer sausage or salami

Directions:
Cook spaghetti until al dente.  Drain and rinse with cold water.
Add vegetables. Toss in herbs, oil and vinegar or Italian dressing.
Fold in mozarella and sausage or salami if desired.
Sprinkle with parmesan, salt and pepper.

Submitter: 
Parizienne
Description: 
You can use Dreamfield's low carb pasta or spaghetti squash for a low carb version.  This is a scrumptious summer salad to have on hand in the fridge for al fresco lunches or dinners.
