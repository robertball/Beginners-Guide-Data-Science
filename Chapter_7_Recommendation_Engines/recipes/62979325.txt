Title:
MeMe's Corn Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Ingredients
2 eggs, beaten
1 (15 ounce) can cream-style corn
½ cup container sour cream
1 (8 ounce) light cream cheese
1/4 cup butter, melted
2 tablespoons of Mayo
1 bag frozen or fresh whole kernel corn, thawed
1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 jar of chopped pimento, drained
1 (4 ounce) can diced green chilies, drained
½ package dry corn muffin mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Grease a 2 quart casserole dish.
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 2 quart casserole dish.
2. Saute the onion is the butter; then
3. In a large mixing bowl, combine eggs, cream style corn, sour cream, cream cheese and mayo. Stir in whole kernel corn, cheese, pimento, sauteed onion and chilies. Stir in the corn muffin mix until just moistened.
4. Bake in a preheated 375 degrees F (175 degrees C) oven for 1 hour  to an hour and 15 minutes; or until an inserted knife comes out clean and the top is golden. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
