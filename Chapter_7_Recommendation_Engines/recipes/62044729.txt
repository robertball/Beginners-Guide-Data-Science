Title:
Indian Potato Vegetable Dish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 large potatoes chopped
1/2 head of cauliflower, in florets
1/2 cup of peas
2 large carrots, chopped
2 med. tomatoes diced
1 small onion
3 Tbls. olive oil
2 green chili peppers, diced
1/2 tsp of garlic paste or 6 cloves of fresh garlic minced
1/2 tsp of ginger paste or 1 tbl of fresh ginger
2 tsp garam masala
1 tsp red chili powder
1 tsp ground cumin
1/4 tsp of turmeric
1/2 tsp of ground coriander
1/2 cup of water or less
1/2 cup of cilantro
salt to taste

Directions:
Add 3 Tbls of oil in a pot and fry garlic, ginger and green chili for about 2 min.
Add onion until translucent and then add tomatoes and peas and mix well over medium heat.  Add the spices and mix.
Add the vegetables and 1/2 cup of water or less and cover over medium heat to let cook for about 45 min. to one hour.
The dish will produce extra water so limit the water you add to more than 1/2 cup.
Sprinkle the cilantro on top of the dish before serving. Serve with basmati rice and chicken tikki on the side.

Description: 
This vegetable dish consists of potatoes, carrots, and peas in a spicy indian sauce.
