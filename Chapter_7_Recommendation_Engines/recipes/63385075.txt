Title:
Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup chili powder
1/4 cup cumin
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 pound ground beef
2 (15.5-ounce) cans dark red kidney beans, rinsed
1 (28-ounce) can diced tomatoes
1 (28-ounce) can tomato puree

Directions:
Add spices and beef to pot. Cook, breaking up beef with spoon, until no longer pink.
Stir in kidney beans, diced tomatoes with their juices, and tomato puree.
Bring to a simmer, cover, and cook for 1 hour

Submitter: 
Dustin
Description: 
This is a recipe my wife modified and combined from a few recipes. It's a classic chili recipe popular in the American mid-west. It just takes a few cans, spices, and some ground beef and you have yourself a chili better than any canned chili can give you.
