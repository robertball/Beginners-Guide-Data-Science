Title:
Lemon Garlic Fettucine with Broccoli Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/536355.jpg
Ingredients:
8 oz Whole Wheat uncooked Fettuccine
3-4 cups broccoli florets
2 Tbsp light Butter
3-4 cloves, garlic, minced
1 lemon, zested and juiced
1 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1/4 c shaved parmesan
Sea Salt & freshly ground Black Pepper to taste

Directions:
Prepare pasta according to package directions, including salting the water.  Add broccoli during the last 2 minutes of cooking.
While the water boils, prepare your lemon sauce, by melting your butter.  Add garlic, lemon zest and juice.  Bring to a boil, remove from heat and set aside.
Remove pasta/broccoli from water and add to lemon sauce pan.  Be sure to reserve 1/2 c pasta water.
Add crushed red pepper flakes, parmesan, sea salt & black pepper to taste.  Drizzle with Olive Oil and Enjoy!

Submitter: 
SlimCookins
Description: 
Whole wheat fettucine noodles tossed with steamed broccoli and lemon garlic sauce for a healthy, light meal. Great paired with a salad of baby spinach  tossed in low fat vinaigrette.
