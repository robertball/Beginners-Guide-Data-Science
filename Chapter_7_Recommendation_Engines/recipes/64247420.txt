Title:
LaLande French Canadian Baked Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ounces Navy or Great Northern Beans
Water
3 ounces Salt Pork
1 large Bay Leaf
1 medium White Onion sliced
1 TBSP Yellow Mustard, preferably organic

Directions:
Soak beans overnight covered with water.
Next day  preheat oven to 300 degrees.
Bring beans in water to a low simmer until beans begin to soften, about 30-45 minutes.
Drain beans  but reserve water. Do not discard water.
Place beans in 2 quart bean pot or pot with lid adding Salt Pork, Onion, Bay Leaf and 1 TBSP Mustard.
Add reserved water to cover beans 1"
Cover partially with lid, leaving 1 " open on one side of pot.
Bake for 3-4 hours stirring beans occasionally. If needed add more reserved water.

Submitter: 
caquilter
Description: 
This is an old family recipe learned from my French-Canadian father. It results in creamy and tasty baked beans.
