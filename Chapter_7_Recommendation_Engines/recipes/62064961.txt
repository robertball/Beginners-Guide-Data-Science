Title:
Eggs Rachero Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 corn tortillas
1 cup refried beans
8 eggs
4 tsp. butter
1 cup salsa

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Bake tortillas on sheet for 3-5 minutes. Heat beans - spread on tortillas. Fry eggs, and place 2 eggs on each tortilla. Serve with salsa on the side.
