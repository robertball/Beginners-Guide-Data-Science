Title:
Turkey Tetrazzini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 oz. spaghetti noodles (break in half), uncooked
2 cups cooked turkey, diced
1 cup mushrooms (or use canned mushrooms, drained)
1 small onion, diced
3 TBSP butter or margarine
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup milk
1/8 tsp. ground mustard
1 cup cheddar cheese
1 cup mozzarella cheese
1 TBSP Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook spaghetti and place in a greased 9x9 pan or glass baking dish. Sprinkle the turkey over the noodles.
Melt the butter in medium sauce pan over medium heat. Saute the mushroom and onions in the butter until softened. Whisk in cream of mushroom soup, milk, dry seasonings and half of the cheeses until melted.
Pour this mixture over the turkey and noodles. Sprinkle with remaining cheeses. Bake uncovered for 25-30 minutes at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Kristin McNeil
Description: 
Great casserole for using up that leftover turkey after the holidays.
