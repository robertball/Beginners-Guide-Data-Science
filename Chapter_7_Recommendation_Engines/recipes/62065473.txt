Title:
Chocolate Spritz Press Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 cup butter (room temp.)
1 egg
1 egg yolk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 oz. unsweetened chocolate (melted)
2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder

Directions:
Mix sugar and butter until smooth. Beat in egg, egg yolk, vanilla and chocolate.
In another bowl combine flour and baking powder. Add flour mixture to the creamed mixture above and stir well.
Roll dough and insert into your Cookie Press. Choose disc and begin pressing your cookies onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake for 6-8 minutes at 375* F. Place cookies on a cooling rack to cool.
