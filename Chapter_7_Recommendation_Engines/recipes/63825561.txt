Title:
g'Oatmeal Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
5 medium ripe bananas â€“ mashed
1/2 cup water or apple juice
2 tbsp coconut oil
1/4 cup peanut butter
1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp salt
1/3 cup honey
1/4 cup chopped dates, raisins or prunes (optional)
1/4 cup of nuts, chocolate chips or dried fruit (optional)
3 cups rolled oats
2 cups Irish steel cut oats
1/2 cup ground flax seeds
1/4 cup chia seeds

Directions:
Preheat oven at 375 degrees F.
Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl. Transfer mixture into a parchment lined casserole dish or large muffin tin. Drizzle honey over top. Bake for 45 minutes.
Let cool, cut into bars (if using casserole dish), wrap with plastic wrap and freeze.
Let defrost for 15 minutes before eating or heat up in microwave.

Oatmeal to go! A great dish to make once a week for a healthy grab and go breakfast solution or just a great energy bar option.
