Title:
Linda's Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 - 12 oz Bags of Broccoli Florets
1 Can of Mushroom soup
1/2 C Mayo
1 Tbl Lemon juice
1/2 C Cheddar Cheese
Crushed Ritz Crackers - or buttery crackers

Directions:
Cook and drain broccoli as directed in microwave. Mix: soup, mayo,lemon juice, and cheese. Spray a 9x13 baking pan with pam. Spread out broccoli in pan and then pour soup mix on top. Bake at 350, for 20-25 mins until oly slightly brown and bubbly. Sprinkle top with more cheese and crushed ritz crackers.

Submitter: 
Tammy M Curry
Description: 
This recipe was given to me from Linda Brown, Mary's Aunt. I like that the broccoli isn't chopped up and is kept whole.
