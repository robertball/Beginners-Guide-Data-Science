Title:
Chili Dusted Tuna with Pineapple Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Tuna Steaks
Chili Powder
1 Can Crushed Pineapples (even better with fresh)
1 Jalapeño
Half Red Onion
Bunch of Cilantro

Directions:
Pineapple Salsa
Peel and dice half of red onion
Open and drain pineapple
Seed and dice Jalapeño
Coarsely chop handful of cilantro
Mix ingredients in bowl
Can be used right away but better if allowed to sit
Tuna
Heavily coat tuna with chili powder on each side
Place on light oil grill or pan sear in margarine
Time depends on desired level of doneness and thickness of steaks
I like mine a little pink on the inside
top with spoonful of salsa and serve

Submitter: 
DCSWeb
Description: 
Tuna steaks dusted with chili powder and either pan seared or grilled top with pineapple salsa.
