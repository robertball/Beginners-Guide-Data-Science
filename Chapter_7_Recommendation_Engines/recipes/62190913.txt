Title:
Honey Dijon Brussel Sprouts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 fresh brussel sprouts, halved
1 1/2 teaspoon butter, melted
1 1/2 teaspoon honey
1/2 teaspoon dijon mustard
Dash each of dill and onion powder

Directions:
Cook sprouts in lightly salted water until crisp tender (about 8-10 min.), drain well.  Combine rest of ingredients and toss with sprouts.

Submitter: 
Stacy
Description: 
My Mother made this for Thanksgiving.  It's a wonderful twist on brussel sprouts!
