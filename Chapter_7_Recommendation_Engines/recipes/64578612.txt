Title:
Sea Bass scallion vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bunches of scallions, roots and outermost layers removed
1/3 cup grapes or olive oil, plus extra for scallions and fish
Salt and ground black pepper
2 tsp soy sause
2tbs finely chopped ginger
2 tsp finely chopped garlic
2tsp sesame oil
4 6 oz sea bass or halibut fillets
1 lemon sliced into rounds
cooked rice for serving

Directions:
1.Preheat oven to 350 degrees. toss scallions with a glug of grapeseed oil and a pinch of salt.  In a large skillet over high heat, char scallions until blackened all over, 5-7 mins. Set scallions and let cool about 5 mins.
Roughly chop scallions and transfer to a med bowl.
2.In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, ginger , garlic,1/3 grapes oil and sesame oil. Pour dressing over scallions and set aside to let flavors meld, at least 5 mins.
3.Lightly season fish with salt and grapes oil. On a roasting tray, arrange fish on top of lemon slices.  Roast until fish is flaky, about 15 mins. Plate fish over dressing atop with caramelized lemon slices. frizzle extra dressing over fish. serve with rice.
