Title:
Nut Filling for Kolacky Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/758407.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound ground walnuts
2 egg whites
1/2 cup super fine sugar

Directions:
Beat the egg whites and add the ground walnuts and white sugar. Mix well. Place about 1 teaspoon of filling on each square.

Delicious walnut filling for Kolacky Cookies.
