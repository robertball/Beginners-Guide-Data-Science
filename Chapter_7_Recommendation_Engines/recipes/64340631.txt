Title:
Chicken tortilla soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can or 32 oz. box of chicken stock
2 large cans (28 oz) of refried beans
3-4 bags of prepared chicken (mexican flavor if available) (3-4 cups of cooked chicken)
2 diced onions
2 15 oz cans petite diced tomatoes
2 15 oz cans petite diced tomatoes with Mexican flavorings
4 15 oz cans of Niblets corn (if regular corn is used instead, drain liquid)
2 cloves of garlic, minced
2 packages of medium sized tortillas
Cheddar cheese and sour cream for garnish

Directions:
In a large soup pot, saut√© onions and garlic in 2-3 T of oil until onions are translucent--garlic should be added late in the process.
Add refried beans and mix together, then add tomatoes and chicken stock until desired thickness.
Simmer for 30 minutes.
Add corn and chicken and heat through.
While soup is simmering, heat oven to 450 degrees.
Cut tortillas in 1/2"x 4-5" slices and place on a greased cookie sheet.
Bake until slightly brown.
When soup is ladled into bowls, top with cheese, sour cream and browned tortilla pieces.
