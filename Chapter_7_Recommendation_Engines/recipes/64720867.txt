Title:
Lemon Ginger Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb wild-salmon fillets
2 teaspoons olive oil, divided
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
Zest of 1 lemon
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2 tablespoons fresh grated ginger
1 teaspoon honey

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
2. Rub salmon fillets with 1 teaspoon olive oil and season with sea salt.
3. In a small bowl, mix the zest of 1 lemon, lemon juice, remaining teaspoon olive oil, ginger, and honey until combined. Brush evenly over the top of salmon.
4. Bake for 10 - 12 minutes.
Suggested side: Serve with Grilled Sesame-Ginger Bok Choy.
