Title:
Magic Santa Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
1/2 cup melted butter or margarine
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 cup mini marshmallows
3/4 cup flaked coconut
1/2 cup chopped pecans (or walnuts)
1/2 cup dried cranberries (craisans)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 (325 for glass pan)
Mix melted margarine and graham crumbs in a bowl. Press into a 9 x 13" pan. (parchement lined works great).
Pour 2/3 can of the sweetened condensed milk onto graham crust.  Add the rest of the ingrediants ontop and evenly over the crust.  Press into pan.
Drizzle remaining condensed milk ontop of the mixture.
Bake 25-30 minutes, or until golden.  Cool at least 20 minutes and cut into squares.
