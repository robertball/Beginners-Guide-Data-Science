Title:
Crepes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 1/2 Cups milk
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup plus 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons melted butter

Directions:
Put all of the ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.  Scrape down the sides with a spatula and briefly blend again.  Place blender in refridgerator for at least 30 minutes, or even overnight.  Blend again before use.
Cook crepe.
