Title:
Toffee Pumpkin Spice Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/303141.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups flour
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 teaspoons of nutmeg
1 tablespoon cinnamon-sugar
1/4 cup pumpkin butter
1 cup milk
1/4 cup canola oil
1/2 cup toffee bits
teaspoon baking powder
2 eggs

Directions:
Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, nutmeg and cinnamon sugar. Next, in another bowl mix milk, pumkin butter, oil and eggs. Stir until eggs are slightly beaten then mix this with the flour, sugar, baking powder, nutmeg and cinnamon-sugar.Stir until flour is no longer visible. Pour into a loaf-shaped baking dish and top with toffee. Bake for 30 minutes on 375 degrees. Enjoy!

Randy the Chef
