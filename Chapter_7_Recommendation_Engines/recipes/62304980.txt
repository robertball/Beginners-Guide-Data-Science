Title:
Cucumbers in Sour Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T and 1 t salt
3 medium sized cucumbers, sliced thin skin on or off
1 medium sized onion, sliced thin
1/2 cup sour cream
1 T sugar
1 T lemon juice
Dash of pepper
1 t sesame seeds (optional)

Directions:
Mix 1 T of salt with cucumber and onion slices
Set aside for 15 to 20 minutes
Mix together remaining ingredients
Rinse the salted cucumber and onion slices
Press out liquid (paper towels!)
Stir in the sour cream mixture
Chill before serving

Description: 
Just another twist on cucumbers.  Also from my Danish cookbook.
