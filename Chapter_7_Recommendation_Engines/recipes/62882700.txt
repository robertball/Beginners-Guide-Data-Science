Title:
Potatoes with Sweet Peppers & Feta Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/695027.jpg
Ingredients:
1 small block feta cheese, crumbled
8 small sweet peppers, sliced
15 small red skin or white mini potatoes cut in 1/2 (bite size)
salt
pepper
dried oregano

Directions:
Boil potatoes until soft and put in a large mixing bowl. Add sliced peppers and feta to the bowl. Add salt, pepper and oregano (to taste preference) since feta is salty, go easy on salt or easy on the feta, which ever you choose. Mix it all together and serve.

A quick side dish to compliment any meat, chicken, or fish meal.
