Title:
Melody's Breadfast Burritos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 10in. flour tortillas
1/2 lb Chorizzo or pork sausage
1 cup of chopped onions, bell peppers, celery, parsley and green onions
1tbsp fresh minced garlic
1/4 cup either Salsa or canned tomato and green chilis
1 pat of butter
1/2 tsp cumin
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
4 large or 3 extra large eggs
1/4 cup milk
1 cup shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese

Directions:
Gently melt the pat of butter in a large skillet careful not to scorch.  Saute everything else EXCEPT eggs, milk, cheese and tortillas in a large skillet until meat is thoroughly cooked and onions soft.  Drain off extra grease and return to heat in skillet.  Whip together eggs and milk and pour over mixture in skillet.  Turn gently with spatula to cook eggs evenly.  When egg are completely cooked, remove mixture from heat.  Spoon even amounts in center of all four flour tortillas, distribute cheddar cheese evenly over the top of the mixture in the tortillas, roll and microwave individually for 30 seconds each to melt cheese and soften/warm tortilla.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Melody Dumplin
Description: 
Whether you use Chorizzo or pork sausage, this will be the tastiest way to start your day.
