Title:
The Best Marinara Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
29 ounces Crushed Tomatoes
6 ounces Tomato Paste
2 tablespoons  Parsley
2 cloves  Garlic, minced
1 teaspoon Italian Seasoning
1 teaspoon Salt
1/4 teaspoon Black Pepper
3-4 tablespoons Olive Oil
1/3 cup Finely Diced Onion
1/2 cup Chicken Broth
1 pinch Basil
1 teaspoon Sugar or Brown Sugar
1 pinch Red Pepper Flakes

Directions:
In large skillet over medium heat, saute the chopped onion with minced garlic in olive oil for 2 minutes. Add remaining ingredients.
Simmer for 30-60 minutes.

Submitter:

Description: 
Great marinara for my chicken parm.  I use leftovers for my flat bread pizza sauce.
