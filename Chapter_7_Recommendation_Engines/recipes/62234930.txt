Title:
Milk Caramel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
385 g sugar
240 mL light corn syrup
720 mL milk
60 mL butter
2.5 mL salt
5 mL vanilla extract

Directions:
Heat the sugar with syrup in 240 mL of milk.
Stir until dissolved.
Then cook to 118-120 °C or firm ball stage.
Stir occasionally, slowly adding the 2nd 240 mL of milk.
Repeat cooking process.
Add remaining milk, butter & salt.
Cook to 118-120 °C (until a ball in cold water is firm as desired for firmness in caramels). Remove from heat; add in the vanilla extract.
Immediately pour into a buttered pan.
Let cool before cutting into 1.5 cm squares.
