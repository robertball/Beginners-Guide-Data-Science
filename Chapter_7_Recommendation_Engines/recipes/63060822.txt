Title:
Rhubarb Dump Cake Recipe
Image:

Ingredients:
4 c. rhubarb
1 c. sugar
1- 3 oz. pkg strawberry jello
1/2 box yellow cake mix (or Jiffy cake mix)
1/3 c. margarine, melted
1/2 c.-3/4 c. water

Directions:
Layer in pan as given.
Do not mix.
Bake @ 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
