Title:
Kickin' Pimento Cheese Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/602423.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 c. cream cheese, softened
1/3 c. mayonnaise
1/4 c. sour cream
2 cups grated sharp cheddar cheese
1/4 c. diced pimentos
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. cayenne pepper

Directions:
Using an electric mixer, mix cream cheese, sour cream and mayonnaise until smooth. Add remaining ingredients, and mix well. Serve or refrigerate.

Description: 
Classic pimento cheese with creamy texture and a little extra kick.
