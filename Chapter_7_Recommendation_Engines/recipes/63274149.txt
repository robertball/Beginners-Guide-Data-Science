Title:
Bavarian Apple Torte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c. butter or margarine, softened
1/2 c. sugar
1 1/2 c. flour
1/2 t. vanilla extract
16 oz. cream cheese, softened
1/4 c. sugar
2 eggs
3/4 t. vanilla extract
3 c. thinly sliced,cored, and peeled apples
1/2 c. sugar
1 t. ground cinnamon

Directions:
Combine the first four ingredients. Press into the bottom of an ungreased 9" springform pan.
In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla and mix well. Pour over crust.
Combine remaining ingredients; spoon over filling.
Bake at 350 degrees for 55-65 minutes, or until the center is set.
Cool on a wire rack; store in the refrigerator. Cut into wedges with a serrated knife.
