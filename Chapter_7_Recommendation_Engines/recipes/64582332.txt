Title:
Cinnamon Sugar Dogs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3664626.jpg
Ingredients:
Hot dog buns
REAL BUTTER (Salted)
Cinnamon
Sugar
Molasses

Directions:
Pull apart hot dog buns and place on cookie sheet.
Brush/pour melted butter generously on all buns.
Spoon generously cinnamon sugar on each.
DRIZZLE threads of molasses over tops.
Roast in oven at 350 for 15 minutes.
