Title:
Garden Chicken and Cheese Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/988476.jpg
Ingredients:
1 leaf red leaf or romaine lettuce
2 slices whole grain millet bread, toasted if desired
2 ounces sliced deli maple glazed chicken breast
1 slice large tomato
4 thin slices unpeeled cucumber
1 thin slice red onion (optional)
1 slice SARGENTO® Sliced Reduced Sodium Colby-Jack Cheese
2 teaspoons mayonnaise

Directions:
Place lettuce on 1 slice of bread; top with chicken, tomato, cucumber, red onion and cheese.
Spread mayonnaise over remaining slice of bread; close sandwich and cut diagonally in half.

Submitter: 
Sargento®
Description: 
Fresh veggies, glazed chicken and Reduced Sodium Colby-Jack between whole grain bread has all the makings of a new classic.
