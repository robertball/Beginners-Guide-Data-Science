Title:
Mike's Aloha Cabbage Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1(16oz) bag coleslaw mix
1 apple cut up
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1 (15 oz) can crushed pineapple drained
1 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp salt
1 lemon squeezed
sugar to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir together the coleslaw, pineapple, apple, walnuts, mayonnaise and salt.  Let it chill for at lest an hour before serving
