Title:
Tomatoes Bacon on Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound bacon- fried and crumbled
1 bunch green onions chopped
2 cans whole tomatoes (chopped in blender)
2 cloves garlic (chopped)
1 TBSP (or more to taste)worcestershire sauce
salt and pepper

Directions:
1. Prepare rice
2.Fry and crumble bacon.
3. Drain some grease (leave enough to sautee green onions)
4.Sautee green onions.
5. Chop tomatoes in blender & add to mix.
6. Chop and add garlic.
7. Add worcestershire sauce & salt and pepper.
8. Mix and simmer for 30-40 mins to cook in flavor.
9. Pour over rice.

Nothing fancy, just southern tomatoe gravy
