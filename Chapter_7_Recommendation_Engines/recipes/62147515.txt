Title:
Opal's Cole Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of shredded cabbage
1 large ripe tomato diced w/ seeds
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup milk
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large bowl combine the cabbage and tomato.
In a mixing bowl stir together the mayo and milk until cream and then add salt and pepper to the dressing.
Pour dressing over cabbage mixture and refrigerate for 1 hour before serving to let flavors meld.

Submitter: 
Gena Sissy Garner Crawford
Description: 
This is the only non-sweet Cole Slaw I have ever tasted and a staple my mom made all the time growing up.  It's simple,delicious and different than your average Cole Slaw.
