Title:
Almond Tea Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Lemon juice
2 cups sugar
1 cup Strong Tea
1 Tbsp. Vanilla
1 Tbsp Almond Extract
1 quart water
2 liter bottle of Ginger Ale

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except Ginger Ale.
Freeze in Ziplock bags for 2 hours.
Remove from freezer and place in punch bowl
Add one 2 liter bottle of Ginger Ale
