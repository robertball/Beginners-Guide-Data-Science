Title:
Arugula Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups arugula
1/4 cup toasted pine nuts
1/2 grated parmesan
1/4 t salt
3 cloves garlic
1/4 cup olive oil
1 teaspoon lemon juice

Directions:
Blend in food processor while drizzling oil and lemon juice until incorporated and smooth.
