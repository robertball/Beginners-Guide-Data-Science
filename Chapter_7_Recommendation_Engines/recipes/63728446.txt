Title:
Cucumber and Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small cucumbers, thinly sliced
1t salt, divided
2 medium tomatoes
1 medium onion
1/4C cider vinegar
2T veg. oil
1T honey
1/2t celery salt
1/2t dried basil
1/2t ground mustard
1/4 t. dried oregano
dash cayenne pepper

Directions:
Place cucumbers in strainer, sprinkle with 1/2t salt and toss. Let stand 30 min. Rinse and drain well.
Place in large bowl, add tomatoes and onions.
In small bowl, whisk together the remaining ingred., pour over cucumber mixture and toss.
Cover and refrigerate for several hours.
Serve with slotted spoon.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
This recipe came from Donna and is one of the best tomato and cucumber salads I have tasted.
