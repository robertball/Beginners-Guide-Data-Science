Title:
Donna Hay's Hoisin Peanut Dipping Sauce Recipe

Ingredients:
1/2 cup hoisin sauce
1/3 cup rice vinegar
2 cloves garlic, crushed
2 small red chilies, thinly sliced
1/3 cup chopped roasted peanuts

Directions:
