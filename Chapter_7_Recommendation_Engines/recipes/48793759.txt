Title:
Dark Chocolate Cake I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups buttermilk
3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
3 teaspoons expresso powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 1/4 cups white sugar
4 eggs
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour 2 - 9 inch round cake pans. In medium bowl, pour boiling water over cocoa, and whisk until smooth add expresso powder. Let mixture cool. Sift together flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt; set aside.
In a large bowl, cream butter oil and sugar together until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs one at time, then stir in vanilla. Add the flour mixture alternately with the cocoa mixture. Spread batter evenly between the 2 prepared pans.
Bake in preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes. Allow to cool.
