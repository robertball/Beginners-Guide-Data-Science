Title:
30% Whole Wheat Bread for the Bread Machine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-3/16 C. Warm Water (105 deg. ??)
1-1/2  T. Molasses
1-1/2  T. Butter
2      T. Dry Milk
1-1/2  t. Salt
1      C. Whole Wheat Flour
2      C. Bread Flour
1-3/4  t. Bread Machine Yeast
1      T. Butter

Directions:
Add all to the bread machine in this order. Use the flour to isolate the yeast from the liquids. My machine has a long and a quick cycle. I use the long cycle for best results. Once the bread is done remove from the pan and spread the last Tablespoon of butter over the crust.
