Title:
Potato Spinach Feta Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium baking potatoes
1 - 10oz package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and well-drained
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup crumbled feta cheese
4 tablespoons flour
1 rounded teaspoon minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
Panko breadcrumbs

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 450 degrees.
1.  Peel and chop potatoes.  Boil until cooked through. Mash until smooth.
2.  Add spinach, eggs and feta.
3.  Add flour, garlic, salt and pepper and mix well.
4.  Chill 20 minutes.
5.  Shape into golf ball sized balls and roll in Panko breadcrumbs.
6.  Spray baking sheet with cooking spray, place balls on sheet, spray cooking spray or lightly drizzle oil over balls.
7.  Bake 12 minutes until golden brown.
Delicious served with tzaziki sauce.
