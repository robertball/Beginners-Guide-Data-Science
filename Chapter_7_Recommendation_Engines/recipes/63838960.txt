Title:
Mustard Based BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup prepared yellow mustard
1/8 cup honey
1/8 cup light brown sugar
1/8 cup white vinegar
2 T Worcestershire
1 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp cayenne
2 tsp hot sauce
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a saucepan over medium heat, stir together all ingredients.  Bring to a boil, and let simmer for 5 - 30 minutes; depending on how intense you want the flavor.

Submitter: 
Dawnby
Description: 
This is a great version of the traditional mustard based bbq sauce.  Most sauces are ketchup based, but in the South we prefer the mustard variation.  You oughta try it at least once.Modified from: Mustard Based BBQ Sauce
