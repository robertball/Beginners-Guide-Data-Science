Title:
Fruit Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985288.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pound shredded cabbage
1/2 pint raspberries
1/2 pint blueberries
1/2 pint blackberries
1 pineapple, cored and cut
1 quart Strawberries
1 (15 ounce) can Mandarin oranges
1 cup Marzetti® Slaw Dressing

Directions:
Wash berries and pat dry. In bowl combine shredded cabbage, berries, pineapple and oranges. Add Marzetti Slaw Dressing. Gently stir until fruit is coated with dressing.

Submitter: 
Marzetti® Slaw Dressing
Description: 
This very berry fruit slaw features shredded cabbage with mandarin oranges, pineapples, and four kinds of juicy berries.
