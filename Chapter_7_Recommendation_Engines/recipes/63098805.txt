Title:
Veggie Vegetarian Chili II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup chopped green bell pepper
1 cup chopped red bell pepper
3 carrots, chopped
1 (16 ounce) vegetable broth
1 (10 ounce) can tomato puree
1 (28 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 (15 ounce) can white kidney beans, drained
1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained
1 (15 ounce) can small red beans, drained
1 (15 ounce) can northern beans, drained
1 (15 ounce) can of pink beans, drained
1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
1 (4.5 ounce) green chiles, chopped
4 tablespoons chili powder
2 tablespoons cumin
2 tablespoons dried oregano
1 tablespoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon red pepper, ground
1 tablespoon sugar
5-6 dashes hot sauce
1/3 cup cilantro, washed, drained and patted dry
Sour Cream
Tortilla chips

Directions:
Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat. Cook and stir the garlic, onion, and carrots in the pot until tender, about 8-10 minutes. Mix in the green bell pepper and red bell pepper. Season with chili powder. Continue cooking 5 minutes, or until peppers are tender. Add vegetable broth.
Stir in the tomato puree and tomato sauce, white kidney beans, black beans, small red beans, great northern beans, pink beans and corn. Add green chiles. Season with cumin, oregano, red pepper and garlic powder. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, to simmer, add sugar, cover, and cook 45 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add hot sauce for additional heat. Serve with sour cream, tortilla chips and cilantro for garnish.
