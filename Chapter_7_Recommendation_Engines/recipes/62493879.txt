Title:
Chewy Almond-Cherry Granola Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups oats (quick or old-fashioned)
1/3 cup chopped almonds
1/4 cup ground flax (or wheat germ)
1/4 cup whole wheat flour
2 tbsp sesame seeds (or sunflower seeds)
1/2 cup dried cherries, chopped (or other dried fruit)
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1/4 cup almond butter
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 tsp vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 and grease a 9" x 9" pan.
In a large bowl, combine oats, almonds, flax, flour. seeds, cherries, salt and cinnamon.
Place butter, almond butter, honey and brown sugar in a saucepan.  Cook over medium heat - as butter melts, stir frequently to combine ingredients.  Bring to a boil and cook, stirring or whisking constantly, for 1 minute.  Remove from heat.   Stir in vanilla.
Pour cooked mixture over oat mixture and stir to completely coat the dry ingredients.  Press into prepared pan. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes or until golden brown.  Let cool completely before cutting into squares.

Submitter: 
ChristineM
Description: 
Crunchy almonds, creamy almond butter and dried cherries star in these yummy chewy granola bars.  You can also substitute your choice of dried fruit or other seeds or nuts.
