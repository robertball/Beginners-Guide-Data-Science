Title:
Pork Egg Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground pork
3 cups shredded cabbage or 1 bag     coleslaw.
1 can (8.5 oz) drained & chopped bamboo shoots.
1/2 cup chopped mushrooms
4 green onions sliced
2 tbl soy sauce
1 tsp corn starch
1 tsp five spice powder
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp sugar
1 lb egg roll skins (16-18)
vegetable oil

Directions:
Stir-fry pork until brown. Remove pork, reserve 2 tbl of fat. Stir-fry cabbage, bamboo shoots, mushrooms and green onions in fat.
Mix soy sauce, cornstarch, five spice powder, salt and sugar. Pour over vegetable. Stir-fry 1 minute. Cool.
Mix in pork. Place 1/4 cup of mixture in center of each egg roll. Fold one corner of skin over filling, overlap opposite corners. Moisten fourth corner with water, fold over to make into roll.
Heat oil to 360 fry 3-5 at a time until golden brown, drain. Serve with dipping sauce.

Submitter: 
DEBMCE4
Description: 
These delicious egg rolls can be frozen and reheated in the oven. Filling can be prepared ahead of time and refrigerated. Directions for rolling are often on the back of the egg roll wrappers.
