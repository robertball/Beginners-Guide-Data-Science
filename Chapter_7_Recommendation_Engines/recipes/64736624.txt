Title:
green pepper and steak Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1/4 cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
1/4 cup water
2 tablespoons  oil, divided
1 pound beef rouladen, cut into 1/4-in.-thick strips
2 small onions, cut into thin wedges
2-3 celery ribs, sliced diagonally
1 medium green pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
2 medium tomatoes, cut into wedges
Hot cooked rice

Directions:
Mix cornstarch, soy sauce and water until smooth.
In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon oil over medium-high heat; stir-fry beef until browned, 2-3 minutes. Remove from pan.
Stir-fry onions, celery and pepper in remaining oil 3 minutes. Stir cornstarch mixture; add to pan. Bring to a boil; cook and stir until thickened and bubbly, 1-2 minutes. Stir in tomatoes and beef; heat through.
Serve with rice.
