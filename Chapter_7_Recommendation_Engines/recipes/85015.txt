Title:
Korean Grilled Beef Skewers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 pound boneless beef loin or sirloin steak
3 tablespoons lite or regular soy sauce
2 tablespoons McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Sesame Seed, toasted
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1 tablespoon water
1 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Ground Ginger
1 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Garlic Powder
1/2 teaspoon McCormick® Gourmet Collection® Crushed Red Pepper
2 green onions, sliced

Directions:
Place steak in freezer 20 minutes; cut lengthwise into 1/4 inch-thick strips.
Combine remaining ingredients in self-closing plastic bag. Add steak; rotate bag to mix well. Refrigerate 30 minutes.
Remove steak from marinade. Thread steak in zig-zag fashion onto skewers. Grill over medium-high heat or broil 6-8 minutes or until desired doneness. Serve with Far East Summer Slaw .

Submitter: 
McCormick® Gourmet Collection®
Description: 
The traditional Korean flavors of sesame seed, ginger and soy sauce combine with sweetness and heat to create a full-flavored marinade for beef. Grill on skewers and serve with Far East Summer Slaw.
