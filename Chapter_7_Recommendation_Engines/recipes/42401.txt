Title:
Peanutty Chocolate Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1049943.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cold skim milk
1 (1.4 ounce) package instant sugar-free chocolate pudding mix
1/3 cup reduced fat peanut butter
Nonfat whipped topping (optional)

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, combine milk and pudding mix. Beat on low speed for 2 minutes. Beat in peanut butter until smooth. Spoon into dessert dishes. Top with whipped topping if desired.

Submitter: 
Taste of Home's Fast Family Favorites
Description: 
Jazz up instant chocolate pudding by stirring in a small amount of peanut butter. The smooth results are sure to satisfy the dessert lovers in your family.
