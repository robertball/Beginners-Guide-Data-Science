Title:
Cucumbers with Sour Cream and Dill Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups thinly sliced regular or English cucumbers (~2 cucumbers)
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup reduced-fat sour cream
2 tablespoons white vinegar
2 tablespoons sugar
1/4 cup diced red onion
1-2 tablespoons minced fresh dill
1/8 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
place sliced cucumbers in a colander, sprinkle with salt and tooss to coat.  Let stand in refrigerator for 1-2 hours.
Pat cucumbers dry with a paper towel
In a large bowl combine sour cream, vinegar, and sugar.
Add cucumbers, onion, dill, and black pepper.
Stir gently to coat
Chill 1 hour befor serving.
