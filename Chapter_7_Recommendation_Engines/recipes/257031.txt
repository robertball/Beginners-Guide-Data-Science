Title:
Blackberry Honey Walnut Overnight Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4476649.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Quaker® Oats
1/2 cup low-fat milk
1/4 cup plain yogurt
1/4 cup blackberries, cut in half
1 tablespoon chopped walnuts
1 teaspoon chia seeds
1 tablespoon honey

Directions:
Add Quaker(R) Oats to a container of your choice and pour in milk.
Alternate between layers of yogurt, blackberries.
Top with walnuts, chia seeds, and drizzle with honey.
Refrigerate overnight and enjoy in the morning!
Top with additional blackberries and walnuts, if desired.

Submitter: 
Quaker®
Description: 
Prep this delicious oats, yogurt, and berry dish in the evening, and enjoy an 'instant' breakfast the next morning.
