Title:
Traditional Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb Spaghetti
1/2 lb Pancetta
1 bunch green onions
4 eggs
1 C mixed Italian cheeses: Parmesan, Romano, etc

Directions:
Cook pasta in boiling salted water.
Dice the pancetta.  Cook in large pan until done.
While pancetta is cooking, slice green onions and set aside.
Beat eggs until one color.
Stir cheese into eggs and mix well and set aside
When pancetta is done -DO NOT DRAIN-
Add in drained pasta, mixing to coat.
Turn off heat.
Add onion and egg mixture stirring until just cooked.
Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Joan
Description: 
This recipe originates in the homes of Italy. It's quick and easy to make this hearty comfort food. Not one of Italy's healthiest recipes, but delicioso!
