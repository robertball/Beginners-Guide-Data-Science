Title:
Grilled Chicken Breasts Cooks Illustrated Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
6 TBLS olive oil
Lemon juice from one lemon
1 TBLS minced fresh parsley
1 1/4 tsp sugar
1 tsp Dijon
1 3/4 tsp salt
3/4 tsp pepper
3 garlic cloves (1 TBLS)
2 TBLS water
Can try substituting OJ for lemon and tarragon for parsley, add 1/4 tsp orange zest to reserve

Directions:
Leave 1/4 of sauce in reserve
Marinade 30-60 minutes in refridgerator
Slow cook fatter side down 6-9 minutes until developing lighter grill marks
Flip for another 6-9 minutes
Put on heat when chicken internal 140
Remove at 160 and let rest for 10 minutes
