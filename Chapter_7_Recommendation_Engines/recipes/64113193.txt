Title:
Southwestern Grilled Tuna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 oz yellowfin tuna fillet
1 tsp olive oil
1/4 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp ground cumin
1/4 tsp black pepper
salsa
1 lime wedge
2 cantaloupe wedges

Directions:
Brush tuna with olive oil.
Sprinkle with chili powder, cumin, and black pepper.
Grill to desired doneness.
Top with salsa.
Serve with lime wedge and cantaloupe wedges.
