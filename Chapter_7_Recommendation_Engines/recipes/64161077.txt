Title:
Quick Hamburger Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lb Hamburger
1 small onion (diced)
8 oz sliced fresh mushrooms
4 cloves garlic (Minced)
1 can Beef Consumme
1/2 cup white wine
1/4 cup flour
1 cup Sour Cream
2 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
1 Tbsp Dijon Mustard
1 package wide egg noodles

Directions:
In a large skillet add hamburger, mushrooms, onions, and garlic.  Cook until beef is browned and mushrooms and onions are tender. Drain grease from skillet.
Prepare pot for noodles and cook according to package instructions.
Stir in flour until meat is coated.  Add Consumee, and wine.  Bring to a boil.  Once at a boil, reduce to med/low heat.  Add Worcestershire, Wine and Dijon to pan.  Stir in sour cream and heat through.  Serve over noodles.
