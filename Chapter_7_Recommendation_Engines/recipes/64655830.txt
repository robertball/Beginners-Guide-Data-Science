Title:
Instant Pot® Pineapple-Coconut-Lime Rice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4550447.jpg
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups long grain white rice
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, undrained
1 cup water
3/4 cup coconut milk
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1 lime, juice and zest
Cilantro, chopped (optional)

Directions:
Rinse rice well until the water runs clear.  Place the rice, pineapple, water, coconut milk, and red pepper flakes in the Instant Pot® and stir.
Put the lid on the pot, lock in place, turn the valve to Sealing.  Press the Manual button, high pressure, and set the timer for 3 minutes.  When countdown is complete, do a Natural Pressure Release.
Remove lid, add the lime juice and zest, and stir.  Garnish with optional cilantro.
