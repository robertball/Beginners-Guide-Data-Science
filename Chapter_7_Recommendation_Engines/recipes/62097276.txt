Title:
Fried Seafood Laksa Noodles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
500gram Laksa Noodles(rice-flour noodles),wash and drained
3 cloves garlic,grind
2 red onions,grind
3 green chillies,sliced
1 teaspoon ginger, crushed
1 tablespoon light soya soy
salt to taste
1 tablespoon white pepper(depends how spicy you want)
100gram spinach,cut into smaller pieces
25g carrots,sliced
200g fresh prawns
1-2 squids,sliced
1 tablespoon of cooking oil

Directions:
Heat 1 tablespoon of the oil in a wok or large pan
Stir fry onions,garlic,ginger,chillies until its fragrant followed by salt,white pepper and light soya soy.
A min later,add the prawns and squids.
Cook the prawns till it turn pink in colour.
Add the spinachs and the carrots till its wilted followed by the noodles.
Stir the noodles until its fully incorporated and serve
