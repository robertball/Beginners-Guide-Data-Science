Title:
Butterfinger S'mores Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1000677.jpg
Ingredients:
8 NESTLE® BUTTERFINGER® Fun Size Candy Bars
8 graham cracker squares
4 large marshmallows, halved

Directions:
Preheat broiler.
Place 1 Butterfinger candy bar diagonally on top of each graham cracker square; top with marshmallow halves. Place on baking sheet.
Broil for 1 to 2 minutes or until marshmallows are lightly browned. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Butterfinger
Description: 
These oven-made Butterfinger S'mores are easy to make and your kids will love them.
