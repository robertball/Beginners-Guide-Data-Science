Title:
Dad's Cornmeal Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup boiling water
3/4 cup cornmeal
1-1/4 cup buttermilk
2 eggs
1 cup white flour
1 Tbsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp soda
1/4 cup oil

Directions:
In a bowl, pour water over cornmeal and stir until thick.  Beat in eggs and oil.  Add flour, baking powder, salt and soda.
Bake on hot ungreased griddle.
