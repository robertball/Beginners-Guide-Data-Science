Title:
Spaghetti with sauce choice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boxes spaghetti noodles
1 jar Marinara sauce
1 jar Alfredo sauce
4 pounds various cooked proteins, suggestions include ground beef, italian sausage and chicken

Directions:
Prepare proteins.
Cook pasta according to package directions.
Serve pasta and sauce separate so people can choose.
You can combine beef or italian sausage with the marinara sauce or leave separate.

Submitter: 
SST
Description: 
If you choose this recipe and are unfamiliar with cooking, please see Danielle or Lauren for further instruction before outreach for some extra tips. Estimate prep and cook time by the proteins you are serving and the pasta package directions. Serve with green salad and french bread or garlic bread.
