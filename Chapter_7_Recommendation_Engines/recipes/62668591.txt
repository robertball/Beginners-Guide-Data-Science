Title:
Green Beans with Toasted Almonds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 C slivered almonds
2 # green beans, trimmed
1 T EVOO
6 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
1/4 t salt
freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 275 degrees. Spread almonds on baking sheet and toast, stirring once, until fragrant and golden, about 10 min. Cool.
Bring large saucepan of water to boil. Add green beans and cook until crisp-tender, about 3 min. Drain in colinder and run under very cold water for 1 min. Shake and pat dry.
Heat oil in nonstick pan over med. heat. Add garlic, reduce heat to med.-low and cook, stirring frequently until garlic is lightly golden and fragrant, about 2 min.
Add breans and toss well to coat. Cover pan, increase heat to high and cook until beans are heated through, 2-3 min.
Add almonds, salt and pepper and serve warm.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Fresh green beans, lots of garlic and toasted almonds make for a healthy, satisfying side dish.
