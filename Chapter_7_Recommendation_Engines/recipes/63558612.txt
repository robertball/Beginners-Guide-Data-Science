Title:
Big Jim's Three Bean - Four Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/972325.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can green beans
1 can yellow beans
1 can red kidney beans
1 can garbanzao beans
1/2 c. green pepper
1/2 c. pepper jack cheese.
1/2 onion
1/2 c. extra virgin olive oil
1/2 c. red wine vinegar
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
3/4 c. sugar

Directions:
Chop up the green pepper, onion and cheese to small pieces.  Drain beans.  Combine all ingredients in serving bowl and store in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
US92
Description: 
A twist on the traditional three bean salad with the addition of a fourth bean and cheese.  Big Jim says this recipe gets better with age.
