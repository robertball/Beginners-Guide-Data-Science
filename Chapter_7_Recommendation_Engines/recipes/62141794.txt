Title:
Super Simple Peanut Butter Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tbl butter
1 12 oz pkg of old fashioned creme drops
1 cup creamy or crunchy peanut butter

Directions:
Combine ingredients in a glass bowl and microwave for 1.5 minutes. Stir and spoon into a greased 8X8 pan. Refrigerate for 25 minutes. Cut and serve!

Submitter: 
Kimberly Turnage Slater
Description: 
3 ingredients, 5 min prep, 25 min wait, one tasty treat! (You can usually find the drops at the dollar store--and especially around the holidays)
