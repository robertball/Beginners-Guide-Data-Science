Title:
Chuckwagon Chili Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 # lean ground beef
1 med. onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 can (15-19 oz) red kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 large ripe tomato, chopped
1 T chili powder
1 pkg. (7 oz)yellow cornbread mix
1/2 C milk
1 egg
Shredded cheddar cheese (opt.)

Directions:
In med. skillet, cook meat, onion and green pepper over med-high heat until meat is browned and vegetables are tender, about 12-15 min; drain any fat
In shallow 2-qt casserole, stir together cooked meat mixture, beans, tomato and chili powder.
In med. bowl, stir together cornbread mix, milk and egg until just combined. Bake in400 degree oven until cornbread is golden brown, about 18-29 min.
If desired, top w/shredded cheese.

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Chili made with a ripe tomato is topped with cornbread mix and baked in a pie dish
