Title:
Lemon chicken thighs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1073650.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken thighs(I prefer with bone and skin)
2 lemons
4 cloves of garlic crushed
1 teaspoon thyme powder
1 teaspoon dried crushed rosemary
1/2 tablespoon jamaican allspice powder
2 teaspoon light brown sugar
salt and fresh crushed black pepper to taste
2 tablespoon olive oil
1 cup chicken stock

Directions:
Combine all spices,garlic,salt, pepper and sugar.
Pat the chicken dry and rub with the above spice mixture.Let it rest for 30mins in the refrigerator.
Heat the olive oil in a pan and put the chicken pieces skin side down.Let it crisp.
Now turn them over and add 1 cup of chicken stock.
Squeeze juice of one lemon and add to the pan.
Slice another lemon and add then on to the chicken.
Cover the pan and let it simmer for 30 mins or till the chicken juices run clear.
Discard the lemon rinds and serve.

Description:

Tastes good even the next day.
