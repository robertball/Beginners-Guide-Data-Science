Title:
Chocolate Coke Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can Coca-cola
2 Tbs. chocolate syrup
Ice cubes

Directions:
Place ice cubes in a tall glass and pour Coca-Cola over ice.  Stir in chocolate syrup and enjoy.

Submitter: 
SDHARKER
Description: 
There was an old fashioned soda shop in my hometown of Fernley, Nevada.  It is no longer in business but I can still remember this tasty drink.
