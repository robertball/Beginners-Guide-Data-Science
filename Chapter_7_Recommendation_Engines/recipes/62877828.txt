Title:
Smothered Pork Chops and Rice Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbl vegetable oil
four 8-oz center cut bone in pork chop loins, 1/2" thick
salt & pepper (I sub Tony Chach's)
3/4 c uncooked long grain rice
1 - 103/4 once can condensed cream of chicken soup
2 cups grated cheddar cheese
1/2 c finely chopped Vidalia onion or scallions (leave off if Emmett's home)

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
2. Heat the oil in an ovenproof skillet with a lid over midium high heat. Season the pork chops with the salt & pepper. Cook, turning once, until brown on both sides, 6 - 8 mins.
3. In a bowl, mix together 1/4 cup of water, the rice, soup and cheddar cheese.
4. When the chops are brown, add teh rice mixture to the skillet. Cover and bake for 45 mins or until the rice is done. Scatter the onion over the top.
