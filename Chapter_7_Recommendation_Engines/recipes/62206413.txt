Title:
Heavenly Rice Cloud Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/313937.jpg
Ingredients:
2 to 3 cups cooked and cooled arborio rice (I like more rice so I use 3)
2 containers of Extra Creamy style cool Whip
10 to 15 maraschino cherries, chopped fine
1/2 cup of reserved maraschino cherry syrup
1 can crushed pineapple including liquid
1/2 cup granulated sugar

Directions:
mix all ingredients above extremely well in a large serving bowl. chill well 2 hours, overnight is best.

Submitter: 
Kimberly Strain
Description: 
An absolutely delicious, creamy, flavorful way to serve rice. I serve it as a fruity salad as well as for dessert. My family loves it and ask for it often. It's always requested at family dinner gatherings!
