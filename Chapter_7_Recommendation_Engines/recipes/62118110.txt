Title:
Easy Pumpkin Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1- Box of ginger lemon cream sandwich cookies (sold at Trader Joes)
2- 1lb 8.3oz tubs of Kraft Philadelphia refrigerated cheesecake
3- Tbsp melted butter
1- 16oz can pumpkin (not pie mix)
2- tsp pumpkin spice

Directions:
Use 8x3" springform pan. Coat with Pam spray. Crush cookies (food processor helps), crush or process until fine. Add melted butter, mix until mixture begins to stick. Spread on bottom of pan evenly.
In large bowl, stir one tub filling until smooth. PLace over crust, spread with flat baking spoon until even.
Mix can of pumpkin, pumpkin spice, and second tub of filling in bowl. It's best to use a beater or mixer, otherwise it takes awhile. Then add to top of plain cheesecake already in pan. Cover and refrigerate.
