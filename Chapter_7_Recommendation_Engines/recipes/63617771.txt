Title:
Slow Cooker Smoked Turkey Tomatillo Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1006205.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
1 onion, chopped
6 cloves fresh garlic, minced
1 (12 ounce) jar Herdez Tomatillo Verde Mexican Cooking Sauce
8 cups chicken broth
2 (15.5 ounce) cans white hominy
Meat from two smoked turkey legs, shredded
1 carrot, peeled and cut into chunks

Directions:
Place olive oil, onion, garlic, Herdez Tomatillo Verde Mexican Cooking Sauce, chicken broth, hominy, turkey meat, and carrot in a slow cooker.
Stir and cook on low for 6 hours.

Submitter: 
Cookie734
Description: 
The southwestern US has its own unique cuisine.  My favorite southwestern dish was a soup called Posole, made with Hatch Green Chiles and white hominy.  This is my twist on that recipe.
