Title:
Fuzzy Navel Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/992865.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (10 fluid ounce) can frozen BACARDI® Mixers Fuzzy Navel mix
1/2 cup BACARDI® Rum
5 cups ice
Peaches for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Fuzzy Navel mix, BACARDI(R) Rum and ice for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Garnish with peaches.

Submitter: 
BACARDI® Mixers
Description: 
This version of the Fuzzy Navel is blended with ice and rum for a refreshing peachy cooler.
