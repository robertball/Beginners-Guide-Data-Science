Title:
Broccomoli Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups broccoli, chopped
1/4 cup low-fat sour cream
1/4 onion, minced
2 Tbsp low-fat mayonnaise
2 Tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp salt
1 garlic clove

Directions:
Steam the broccoli until very soft (either on the stove or in microwave)
Place all ingredients in a food processor and blend until very smooth
Chill at least 2-3 hours before eating

Submitter: 
Becka
Description: 
Low-cal guacamole alternative that's super tasty. Great as a veggie or chip dip.
