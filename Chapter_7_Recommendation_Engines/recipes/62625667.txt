Title:
Sour Cherry Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 cup unsalted butter
1 cup sugar
3 eggs, separated
1 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
2 tsp. baking powder
2 tsp. milk
2 tsp. grated lemon rind
1/2 cup ground walnuts, optional
4 cups pitted cherries fresh or canned (drain if canned)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Grease and lightly flour a baking dish (9x13 or round).  Cream butter and sugar; mix in the yolks one at a time.  When blended together, sift in flour and baking powder.  Fold in milk and add lemon rind.  Beat egg whites until stiff; gently fold in ground nuts.  Add to the flour mixture.  Place half cherries on bottom of baking dish, pour batter over the top and top with remaining cherries.  Bake for 45 minutes or until knife comes out clean. Sprinkle with powder sugar before serving.
