Title:
Banana Milk Drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large ripe banana
1 cup milk
1 1/2 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 dash ground cinnamon

Directions:
1.Place the first four ingredients in a blender;Cover and process until smooth.Pour into glasses,and sprinkle with cinnamon if desired.Serve immediately.
