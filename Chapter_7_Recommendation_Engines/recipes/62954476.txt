Title:
Fresh Raspberry Pie 1 Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 (6 oz)containers fresh raspberries, divided
1/3 cup water
2/3 cup sugar
9 teaspoons cornstarch
Dash salt
2 tsp. lemon juice
3 oz cream cheese, softened
1-2 T. sugar
1/2 tsp. lemon extract
1 (9 inch) pastry shell, baked
Whipped cream

Directions:
In a saucepan, crush 1 container of berries. Add water; simmer for 3 minutes. Strain, reserving juice; discard pulp and seeds. Add enough water to juice to measure 1-1/4 cup liquid. In a saucepan, combine sugar, cornstarch and salt. Slowly stir in raspberry liquid. add lemon juice, bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened. Remove from the heat; cool slightly. Mix cream cheese, sugar and extract in small bowl til blended. Spread on cooled pie crust.
Place remaining raspberries in pastry shell; pour glaze over top. Refrigerate for 2-3 hours or until set. Serve with whipped cream if desired.
