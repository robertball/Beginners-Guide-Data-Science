Title:
Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 each sandwich roll
.5 g	deli ham
.5 g	deli turkey
1.5 slice cheese
10 g	cream cheese
1.5 leaf iceberg lettuce

Directions:
We need 2 sandwiches for each of the guys and 1 sandwich for the girls.
Cut sandwich roll in half.
Slice the ham and turkey.
Put either ham or turkey slices inside roll with one slice of cheese/cream cheese (depending on menu) and one leaf of lettuce.
Saran wrap each sandwich and mark with ham or turkey.
Pack mayonnaise and mustard to take to the event for people to put on their sandwiches there.
