Title:
PORK  CHOP BAKE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
OLIVE OIL
2 LARGE ONIONS SLICED THINLY
6 PORK CHOPS OR PORK STEAKS
GARLIC PEPPER
2 CANS CREAM OF MUSHROOM SOUP W/GARLIC
1/2 CUP WATER

Directions:
PLACE OLIVE OIL IN ELECTRIC SKILLET.  ADD SLICED ONIONS IN A LAYER IN SKILLET.
PLACE PORK CHOPS/STEAKS ON TOP OF ONIONS.
SPRINKLE WITH GARLIC PEPPER.
POUR SOUP OVER MEAT AND ADD WATER.
BRING TO BUBBLING IN SKILLET FOR 3 MINUTES.
LOWER HEAT TO SLOW BUBBLE (250 ON MY SKILLET), COVER, AND COOK 50-60 MINUTES.
SERVE WITH MASHED POTATOES, ROLLS, AND VEGGIE.
