Title:
FRIED RICE Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. olive oil
2 cups cooked Jasmine rice
2 green onion, chopped
1 cup of matchstick carrots, chopped
2 garlic cloves, crushed
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup frozen peas, thawed
2 medium ham steaks, cut into bite size pieces
Salt and pepper to taste
2 - 4 tsp. soy sauce
2 - 4 tsp toasted sesame oil

Directions:
Coat large wok or skillet with olive oil and cook ham.  Add the green onion, carrots and garlic.  Cook for 5 minutes and then add cooked rice.  Cook this mixture for a few minutes and then push this mixture onto one side of the pan and then add beaten eggs.  Scramble with fork into tiny pieces.  Once the egg has cooked, incorporate the egg into the rice mixture.  Add the peas.  When the peas are warm, add the soy sauce, salt, pepper and sesame oil.  Stir to combine and enjoy!!
