Title:
Rudy's Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4212546.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef (80/20)
1 box of beef broth (13-14.5 oz)
1/2 cup shreded regular carrots
2 Tablespoons of chili powder
1/2 teaspoon of salt
1/2 Teaspoon of paprika
1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 Tablespoon of Masa flour
1 Tablespoon of white vinegar
4 Tablespoons of all-purpose flour
1 dash (1/8 teaspoon) of cayenne

Directions:
Food process (or in a pinch, grate and mince) carrot till only tiny flakes remain.
In a large pot or cast-iron pan over medium high heat, add beef, carrots, and begin to brown.
Reduce heat and add all seasonings (including broth) but flour and vinegar.
Add vinegar, followed by flour in tablespoon quantities, mixing chili as you go.
Simmer and stir on an extremely low heat for about 10 minutes.
Build a Classic L.A. Style Chili Cheese Burger Top to Bottom:  Top Bun / Thick cut tomato slice / Hamburger pickle chips / Yellow Mustard / Fresh chopped onions / (100% Beef Hamburger patty) / Double Thick Cheese Slice / 100% Beef Hamburger patty / Bottom Bun
