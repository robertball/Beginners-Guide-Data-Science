Title:
17 day diet Chicken Pomodoro Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts
1 can diced tomatoes
Fat free mozzarella
Mushrooms
Salt, pepper, oregano to taste

Directions:
In a baking dish, spray Pam and add the chicken let it cook for about 10 minutes on 350 and then add the tomatoes let that bake for about another 10. Take it out and slice the chicken then add mushrooms. Wait until the chicken is not pink and sprinkled fat free mozzarella cheese and broil it until cheese is perfect.

Submitter: 
davekippen
Description: 
Chicken dish for cycle 1 of the 17 day diet, or just any other night you want to eat healthy and quick!
