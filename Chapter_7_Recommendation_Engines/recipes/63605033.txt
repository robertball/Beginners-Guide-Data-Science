Title:
Super Yummy Pizza Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large can buttermilk biscuits
1 bag Pepperoni slices
1 bag Shredded mozzarella
1/4 cup butter, melted
1 tablespoon each of parmesan cheese, italian seasoning, garlic powder
1 small jar pizza sauce

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
2.  Take a biscuit and flatten it into a square.
3.  Put 2-3 slices of pepperoni in the middle.
4.  Add a tablespoon of mozzarella on top of pepperoni.
5.  Fold the biscuit up, so that nothing can drizzle out.
6.  Place folded side down in a greased baking dish.
7.  Pour melted butter on top of each roll.
8.  Sprinkle with parmesan cheese, italian seasoning, and garlic powder.
9.  Bake for 19-22 minutes.
10.  Serve with warm pizza sauce for dipping.

Submitter:

Description: 
I needed to use up some canned biscuits that were going to expire.  But, I also had a craving for pizza.  These really hit the spot!!
