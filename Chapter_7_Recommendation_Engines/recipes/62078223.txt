Title:
Cherry Pom Poms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1/2 c. vegetable oil
1 small package instant vanilla pudding mix
3 T. milk
3/4 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 t. baking powder
3 T. cherry jello mix
2 eggs, separated
1 1/2 t. almond extract
2 3/4 cup sweetened coconut
1 t. water

Directions:
Mix all ingredients except egg whites, water, and 2 cups of the coconut.  Mix water and egg whites.  Roll mix into 1" balls.  Dip balls into egg wash and then roll into the remaining 2 cups of coconut.  Place on a greased cookie sheet and flatten slightly.  Bake at 350 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes, until bottom of cookies are lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Lisa Meece
Description: 
This is my favorite Christmas cookie!  It's such an unusual mix of ingredients--I think that's what drew me to it in the first place.
