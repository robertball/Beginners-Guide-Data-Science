Title:
Marie's Easy Slow Cooker Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5 pounds chuck roast
salt and pepper to taste
can beef broth
can cream of mushroom soup, low sodium
double handful baby carrots whole
1 onion, chopped
5 red potatoes,  cubed
top 1/3 of a bunch of celery, chopped lots  of leaves

Directions:
Season the roast with garlic, paprika salt and pepper to taste. Brown on all sides in a large skillet over high heat, about 4 minutes per side.
Place the roast in the crock pot and add the broth and onion
Cover and cook on Low setting for 4 hours.
Then add potatoes, carrots, celery cream of mushroom soup, tweak garlic, paprika salt pepper mix.
cook on high for 4 hours then turn on low until ready to serve.

Description: 
Browned chuck roast is slow cooked for 8 to 10 hours along with potatoes, carrots, onion, celery, and dehydrated onion soup mix.Modified from: Marie's Easy Slow Cooker Pot Roast
