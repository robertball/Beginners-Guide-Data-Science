Title:
Limeaide Margaritas-On the Rocks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/648503.jpg
Ingredients:
(1) 12 oz Can Frozen Limeade Concentrate
12 oz Cold Water
8 oz Tequila
4 oz Triple Sec
2 oz fresh lime juice (or to taste)

Directions:
In a two quart pitcher add frozen Limeade, Cold Water, Tequila, Triple Sec and Lime Juice.  Shake well to combine.
For garnish, cut a lime wedge and rub the juice around the edge of the glass. Dip into a bowl of kosher salt and add the lime wedge to the rim. Pour the drink over ice and enjoy

Submitter: 
Lori Krol Rothfuss
Description: 
The perfect Margarita, to sit and sip on a warm summer day.  Just the right tartness and not near as sweet as the store bought mixes.
