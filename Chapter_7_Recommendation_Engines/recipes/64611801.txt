Title:
Tammi's Green Bean Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces of chopped fresh mushrooms
1 medium onion chopped
1 T olive oil
1 cup cream (or 1/2 n 1/2 )
salt and pepper to taste
2 slices thick cut bacon
1-2  T flour
1/2 cup milk
up to 2 c chicken or vegetable stock
two pounds cleaned trimmed fresh green beans
1/2 c crispy fried onions for casserole
additional crispy onions for topping
1/2 c cheddar cheese grated

Directions:
Saute' mushrooms and onions in 1 T olive oil until tender and browned.
Add 1 cup cream (or 1/2 n 1/2 )
cook down on simmer until reduced by half. add salt and pepper to taste...in another pan...
2 slices thick cut bacon cut into small lardon's (I use scissors)
Cook off bacon until crisp set aside to drain.
Drain off all but 2 T bacon fat
stir in quickly 1-2  T flour over med heat
after the roux has cooked for 5 min add 1/2 cup milk and stock until it is the consistency you like (I usually do it a little thick so I use a cup) and stir in add mushroom mixture and set aside off heat when it is well mixed.
Steam until tender crisp two pounds cleaned trimmed fresh green beans.
Mix steamed drained green beans with mushroom mixture and 1/2 c crispy fried onion. Place in a 2 qt greased casserole and top with additional crispy onions, crisp bacon crumbled and 1/2 c cheddar cheese grated. Bake in a 350 degree oven for 20-30 minutes until bubbly and beans are cooked.

Submitter: 
Momofone
Description: 
From scratch casserole with fresh green beans and French fried onions and bacon. Serves 4-6.
