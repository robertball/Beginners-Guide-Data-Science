Title:
Grandma Jean's 2 Hour Buns Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups warm water
1/2 cup sugar
1/3 cup oil
1 tsp. salt
2 Tbsp. yeast
2 eggs, beaten
7-9 cups flour

Directions:
Combine first 5 ingredients. Beat eggs and add to mixture. Then add flour. Mix all ingredients and let rise 15 minutes.
Pat with oil and let rise another 15 minutes.
Punch down again and let rise another 15 minutes and punch down.
After the fourth rise, make into buns (and let rise).
Bake at 350 for 15-20 minutes
