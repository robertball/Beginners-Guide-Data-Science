Title:
Waffle Cones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 vanilla bean
1 cup butter
1 cup packed dark brown sugar
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup egg whites
1 1/4 cups all purpose flour

Directions:
Split the vanilla bean and scrape all of the seeds into a small saucepan.  Add the butter and melt.
Combine the butter mixture, sugar, vanilla extract, salt, and egg whites in a bowl.  Add flour and beat until smooth.
Cook in a waffle cone maker according to manufacturer's directions.
Remove cone and immediately wrap around a cone-shaped dowel or press into a taco salad making pan.
Let each cone or bowl cool a few minutes and enjoy, or keep in an airtight container for up to 2 days.

Submitter: 
Sasha Harlow
Description: 
These delicious cones are the perfect compliment to homemade ice cream.  They even use up the egg whites that are left over!
