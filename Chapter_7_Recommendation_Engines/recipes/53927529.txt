Title:
Sweet-and-Sour Skewered Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup barbecue sauce
1/4 cup lemon juice
1/4 cup crushed pineapple
4 teaspoons soy sauce
1 1/2 teaspoon chopped fresh ginger
30 uncooked large shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 large yellow bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
1/2 pound small fresh Portabello mushrooms
30 pineapple chunks

Directions:
In a small saucepan, combine the first five ingredients; bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring frequently. Remove from the heat; cool. Set aside 1/2 cup for basting. Place remaining sauce in a large resealable plastic bag; add shrimp. Seal bag; refrigerate for 30 minutes.
Drain and discard marinade. Thread the shrimp, yellow bell peppers, pineapple chunks and mushrooms alternately on six metal or soaked wooden skewers. Grill, uncovered, over medium-hot heat for 2 minutes on each side. Turn and brush with reserved sauce. Continue grilling for 4-8 minutes or until shrimp turn pink, turning and basting several times.

Submitter: 
Carole Weisser
Description: 
This recipe is a summertime favorite. We fix it often on our portable grill when we go boating. My husband thinks it's great. It's the sauce that makes it!Modified from: Sweet-and-Sour Skewered Shrimp
