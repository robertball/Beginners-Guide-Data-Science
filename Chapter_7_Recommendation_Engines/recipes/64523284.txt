Title:
Parmesan & Herb Crusted Lamb Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2943907.jpg
Ingredients:
- 1 Package of Lamb Chops
- 1 Cup of Bread Crumbs
- 3/4 Cup Parmesan Romano Cheese Blend
- 4 Tbsp. Butter or Oil for frying
- 4 Tbsp. Sweet Onion Dijon Mustard
- Season To Taste

Directions:
Combine Parmesan, Bread Crumbs and Seasoning in a mixing bowl. Lather Lamb Chops with Dijon Mustard. Heat Butter or Oil in Skillet on medium-high heat. Press Chops firmly into crumb mixture all around. Pan Sear on all sides until crumbs are crispy golden brown. Lamb is best served on the rare to medium rare side. Slice down in between ribs and serve with your favorite dipping sauce:)
