Title:
Jan's Easy Smore's Drop Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1285545.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups all purpose flour
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup unsalted butter at room temp.
3/4 cup white sugar
3/4 cup light brown sugar
2 teaspoon vanilla
2 extra large eggs
1 11 ounce bags of chocolate chunk chips
1 11 oz bag of mini chocolate chips
2 cups  Kraft Jet-puffed Mallow Bits Vanilla Flavor Marshmallows (or more if you prefer)

Directions:
Beat butter and sugars until fluffy.
Add eggs and vanilla, blend in.
Add flour, graham cracker crumbs, salt, soda and mix well.
Mix in  chocolate chunks and marshmallows
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Scoop up 1 tablespoon of dough and place on parchment lined baking sheet, about 2 inches apart to allow for spreading.
Bake approximately 8-10 minutes or until set.
