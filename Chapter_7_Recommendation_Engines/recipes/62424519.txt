Title:
Greek Style Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. Lite cream cheese
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 garlic clove, finely chopped
1 tsp. thyme
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. sesame seed
thyme and sesame seed (to garnish)

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix all ingredients except garnish with a fork.
Before serving, place into plastic wrap and shape into a mound or rectangle.
Sprinkle with thyme and sesame seed.
Great served with Wheat Thins, Triscuits, Pretzels or veggies!
