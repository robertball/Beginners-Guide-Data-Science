Title:
Spicy Avocado Cilantro Dressing or Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz.) package cream cheese, softened
3 tablespoons sour cream
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
1 (7 oz.) can salsa verde
2 tablespoons minced fresh garlic
1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 teaspoon onion powder
½ teaspoon sea salt
½ teaspoon cumin
2 cups loosely packed fresh cilantro leaves
1 avocado, chopped
Juice of 1 lime

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender or food processor.  Blend until smooth and creamy.  If too thick, add milk until desired consistency.
For lower fat version, substitute low fat or fat free cream cheese or Neufchatel cheese, low fat or fat free sour cream, and low fat or fat free mayonnaise.

Submitter: 
Vickie Boozer
Description: 
This is a wonderful mix of avocado, cilantro, salsa verde, and spices to make a creamy dressing or dip that can also be used as a sauce for a multitude of things.
