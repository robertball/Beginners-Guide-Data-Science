Title:
Orzo Delicioso Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c white wine
1/4 c olive oil
2 Tbsp butter
1 large, white onion, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 yellow bell pepper, chopped
2 jalapenos, diced
1 large tomato, chopped
1 8 oz. can of corn, drained
2 Tbsp garlic, minced
4 fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 lb box of Orzo

Directions:
Melt butter with olive oil and white wine in a large skillet. Saute the peppers (bell and jalapeno) and onion over medium heat for 5 minutes. Add tomato, corn, garlic, and mushrooms. Saute 10 minutes more.
Meanwhile, cook Orzo according to the package directions. Drain the Orzo and toss with the vegetables and enjoy.
