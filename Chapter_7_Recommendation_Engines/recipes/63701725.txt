Title:
Orange Chicken Fingers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts
3 tablespoons yellow mustard
1/4 cup orange juice
1 pinch salt
1 pinch pepper
2 tablespoons honey
1 teaspoon cooking oil

Directions:
Cut chicken crosswise into 1 inch strips.
Mix mustard, orange juice, honey, cooking oil, and pepper in a medium bowl until smooth.
Add chicken and completely cover with mixture.
Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for 15 minutes.
Heat up chicken using oven or grill according to instructions in the original chicken container.
Season with salt and serve.

Description: 
A new, non-processed twist to chicken fingers with honey mustard sauce.
