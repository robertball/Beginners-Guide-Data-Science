Title:
Carolina Hot Pot Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 Frozen boneless chicken breasts
1 small can tomato sauce
1 can Ro-Tel tomatoes with lime & cilantro
1 can pinto beans
1 can black beans
1 can red beans
1 can corn
1 envelope (3 Tbsp.) dry Ranch dressing mix
1 Tbsp. chili powder
1 tsp. onion powder
1-2 tsp. dried minced onions
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 Tbsp. cumin
Cooked rice
Tortillas
Fresh lime
Fresh cilantro - optional

Directions:
Place frozen chicken breasts in the crockpot.
Add minced onions.
Mix ranch dressing mix with the rotel.  Puree this if you don't want chunks of onions/peppers.
Add all cans (including juice) of beans & corn.
Add all the rest of the spices.
Pour the rotel mixture over the top.
Cook on low for 6-8 hours.
Shred the chicken and return to crockpot.
Serve in bowls or as tortilla wraps with rice and cilantro.  Squeeze fresh lime over individual servings if desired.
