Title:
Healthy Turkey Wrap, Childstyle(: Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Tumaro's Chipotle & Pepper Healthy 8'' Tortillas
1 medium tomato, diced
2 teaspoons mixed herbs
1 leaf of lettuce, chopped
1 piece of turkey ham
cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Place the tomato pieces and the mixed herbs together in a bowl. mix together and place in a microwave at HIGH for 2minutes.
Remove from the microwave and cool for a minute.
In the meantime, warm up the tortilla in the microwave by placing it on a moist paper towel on HIGH for 20 seconds.
put the chopped lettuce on the tortilla, followed by the tomato. Next, add the turkey ham and sprinkle cheddar cheese on top.
Wrap up the tortilla and enjoy!(:
