Title:
Awesome ( No Chillin') Rolled Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. Butter, softened                                2 C. White sugar                                     2 tsp. Vanilla extract                               1 tsp. Almond extract                                2 eggs                                               4 tsp. Baking powder                                 6 C. All purpose flour

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375*.  Combine butter and sugar until fluffy. Add remaining ingredients, mix well. Roll out to desired thickness and cut with cookie cutters. Bake 8 to 10 minutes. Cool on wire rack. Decorate as desired.

Submitter: 
Janet Shum
Description: 
No chilling required. Soft & flavorful cookie with perfect edges. Perfect for decorating. Baked cookies freeze well also. Superb sugar cookie!
