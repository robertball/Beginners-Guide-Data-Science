Title:
Pepper Jack Olive Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4085041.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
1 (4.25 ounce) can chopped black olives, undrained
1 tablespoon mayonnaise
4 ounces BordenÂ® Pepper Jack cheese, shredded
1 (16 ounce) package corn chips

Directions:
Mix cream cheese, chopped black olives, and mayonnaise in a mixing bowl until well combined.
Fold in shredded pepper Jack cheese until well combined.
Serve with corn chips.

Description:
