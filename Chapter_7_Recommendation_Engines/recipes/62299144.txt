Title:
Tortellini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound Italian sausage
1 cup chopped onion
3 cloves, minced garlic
3 cups of beef broth
1 cup water
1/2 cup dry red wine
2 cups chopped tomatoes (or 14.5 oz can)
1 cup each sliced carrots and celery
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1/4 teaspoon fennel seed
2 bay leaves
1 8 ounce can tomato sauce
1 1/2 cups chopped zucchini
1 12-16 ounce pkg. of cheese tortellini
1 medium green pepper, chopped
2 tablespoons fresh parsley

Directions:
In a large pot over medium high heat crumble sausage and begin to brown. Before sausage is completely browned add onion and garlic. Continue cooking until sausage is cooked through. Add beef broth, water, wine, tomatoes, carrots, celery, basil, oregano, rosemary, thyme, fennel seed, bay leaves, and tomato sauce. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and let simmer, uncovered for 30 minutes. Then add zucchini, tortellini, green pepper and parsley. Simmer covered an additional 35-45 minutes.

Submitter: 
TACZYZ
Description: 
A wonderful blend of flavors creates this hearty soup. We serve it as a meal with our favorite bread. Anyone who walks in the house while this is simmering thinks it smells heavenly.
