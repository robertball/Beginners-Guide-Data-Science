Title:
Mylo's Doggie Dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs raw meat (beef, chicken, turkey, liver etc)
1 cup red lentils, rinsed and drained
2 lbs carrots
2 zucchini
2 cloves garlic
2 tbsp bacon fat

Directions:
Cut up meat into small pieces if not ground. Spoon meat into muffin pan (1/4 cup each). Flash Freeze and place in freezer bags.
Cook red lentils in 4 cups water.
In a food processor, use the grater and the slicer attachments.  Chop carrots, garlic and zucchini.
Mix vegetables, lentils and bacon fat together.  Spoon into muffin pan. Flash freeze, then place in freezer bags.
When needed, thaw out 2 day supply at a time: 2 meat, 1 veggie. Can serve as is or quickly heat up in a pan to serve warm.

Submitter: 
Evie
Description: 
Mostly raw dog food for the freezer.  For a two day supply, use 2 portions meat and 1 portion veggie mix. Add kelp powder, apple cider vinegar, eggs with shells and yogurt. Portion good for an 8 pound dog.
