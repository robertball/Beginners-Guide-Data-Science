Title:
Cucumber and herb salad with buttermilk dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 English cucumbers, thinly sliced into rounds
4 oz. crumbled feta
2 T fresh mint, finely chopped
2 T fresh fill, finely chopped
2 T fresh flat leaf parsley finely chopped
2 T fresh chives, finely chopped
1/2 c. buttermilk, well shaken
1/2 c no fat greek yogurt
1 T fresh lemon juice
3 T extra virgin okie oil
1/4 t sea salt
pinch of freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Placed sliced cucumber in a circular pattern on a round serving latter.  Sprinkle with half the mint, dill, parsley and chives.  Add crumbled feta and drizzle with dressing.
Prepare dressing by whisking together the yogurt and buttermilk until smooth.  Stir in the lemon juice, oil, salt and pepper until well combined.  Cover and refrigerate for an hour.  When ready to serve, add remain fresh herbs.
