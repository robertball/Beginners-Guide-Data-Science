Title:
Prosciutto Wrapped Asparagus Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound prosciutto, sliced
1/2 (8 ounce) package chive and onion cream cheese
12 spears fresh asparagus, trimmed

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). blanch asparagus for 1 min than put in ice water.  When cool dry of asparagus
Spread prosciutto slices with Neufchatel cheese. Wrap slices around 2 or 3 asparagus spears. Spray a baking sheet with pam.  Arrange wrapped spears in a single layer on a medium baking sheet.  Spray asparagus bundles with pam.
Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven, until asparagus is tender.

Linda Peterson
