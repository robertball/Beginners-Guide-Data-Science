Title:
Strawberry Spinach Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup almonds, toasted
8 ounces strawberries, quartered
1/2 whole cucumber, sliced
1/4 whole red onion, sliced in wedges
6 ounces baby spinach
DRESSING:
1 whole lemon
2 tbsp. white wine vinegar
1/3 cup sugar
1 tbsp. vegetable oil
1 tsp. poppy seeds

Directions:
Zest lemon to yield 1/2 teaspoon zest (can also use 1/2 tsp bottled orange zest).  Juice lemon to yield 2 tablespoons of juice.  Combine lemon zest, lemon juice, vinegar, sugar, vegetable oil and poppyseeds.  Whisk or shake and refrigerate until ready to serve.
Toast almonds at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes (until light brown).
Mix spinach, onion, cucumber and strawberries.  Pour dressing over top.  Toss.  Sprinkle almonds over top and serve.

Submitter: 
goodeats
Description: 
A fresh, delicious summer salad.   Uses toasted almonds instead of the more typical sesame seeds.
