Title:
Grandma Judy's Chocolate Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/925534.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups of flour self rising or all purpose
4 Tbsp. of white granulated sugar
2 sticks of Parkay butter
2 8 oz. pk-ages of Philadelphia cream cheese
2 cups of powdered sugar
2 small pk-ages of JELLO instant chocolate fudge pudding
4 cups milk for instant chocolate fudge pudding
1 8 oz. container of Cool Whip
Bag of finely chopped walnuts (if desired) for topping

Directions:
For the crust:You begin with adding 2 cups of flour,4 Tbsp. granulated sugar,and 2 sticks of Parkay butter and mix thoroughly with a mixer.When mixture is ready it will be clumpy.Press lightly into 8 x 10 or 11 x 13 baking dish and bake at 350 degrees till lightly browned.About 12-15 mins. then set aside to cool.
Next,mix together 2-8 oz. pk-ages of Philadelphia cream cheese and 2 cups of powdered sugar with mixer until creamy then set bowl aside.
Then, mix together 2 boxes of instant JELLO chocolate fudge pudding then set aside.
After mixing ingredients as listed the crust then should be cooled enough to start the layers. First layer your bowl of Philadelphia cream cheese mix on top of cooled crust,next; layer chocolate fudge pudding on top of the cream cheese,lastly top with Cool Whip as if icing a cake, then sprinkle finely chopped walnuts (if desired).
