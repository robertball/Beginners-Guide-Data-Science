Title:
Grilled Green Beans and Onions Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1335621.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. fresh green beans - washed and trimmed
1 medium onion - chopped
1 tbls. olive oil
1 tsp. Diamond Crystal kosher salt

Directions:
Preheat outdoor grill to medium (400 degrees F.)
In a large bowl mix together green beans and onions.
Then sprinkle with olive oil and Diamond Crystal kosher salt. Toss to coat evenly.
Place the green bean mixture into a steamer basket and place on grill. Close lid and grill for 10 -15 minutes. Stir every 5 minutes to cook evenly. Green beans and onions should get slightly charred. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Lillian
Description: 
Fresh green beans and onions take on a whole new flavor profile when grilled. The green beans become smoky and the onions become sweet. The perfect combination of flavors to accompany any meat or fish main dish.
