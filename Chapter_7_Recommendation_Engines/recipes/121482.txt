Title:
Honey-Mustard Salad Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985932.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups mayonnaise
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup honey
1/4 cup prepared mustard
1/4 cup vinegar
1/4 cup chopped onion
1 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup minced fresh parsley

Directions:
In a blender or food processor, combine the first six ingredients.  Slowly add oil; process until smooth. Stir in parsley. Cover and chill for at least 1 hour. Can be refrigerated for up to 1 week.

Submitter: 
Jean  Keffer
Description: 
This zippy recipe is one a friend shared years ago. I like to give the dressing to others in jelly jars trimmed with baling twine.
