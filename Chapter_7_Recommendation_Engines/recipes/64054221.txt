Title:
Grandma Ruth's  Peach Dump Cobbler Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 cups fresh peaches (or any fruit really), 2 cups flour, 1 cup sugar, 1 cup milk, pinch of baking powder, pinch of cinnamon, 1 stick margarine or butter.

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place stick of butter/margarine in 9x13 glass pan and place in oven. Meanwhile, mix remaining ingredients except for peaches. When butter has melted, pour batter on top and follow with fruit. Bake for about 20 minutes, or until brown along edges and toothpick comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Rebecca Cantu
Description: 
Super easy, kid friendly recipe that anyone can make. Can use any seasonal fruit as desired.
