Title:
Grandma's Fudge Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4795505.jpg
Ingredients:
12 Ounces Evaporated Milk
4 1/2 Cups Sugar
36 Ounces Semi Sweet Chocolate Chips
3 Cups Chopped Walnuts
1/2 Lb Butter
14 Ounces Marshmallow Cream
2 tsp. Vanilla

Directions:
In a small saucepan, combine the evaporated milk and sugar.
Bring to a slow boil.
Stir constantly for 9 minutes. DO NOT OVERCOOK.
Remove from heat.
Transfer to a large mixing bowl.
Add the remaining ingredients.
Stir with wooden Spoon for about 15-20 minutes, the longer the better.
If you use a stand mixer, put on lowest setting.
Spread out on a non-stick cookie sheet and refrigerate  6 hours to set.
