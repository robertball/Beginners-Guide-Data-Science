Title:
Figure Friendly Spinach Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/388602.jpg
Ingredients:
4 large Russet Potatoes, washed and peeled
Â¼ cup Fresh Grated Parmesan Cheese
2 cups Fresh Chopped Spinach
1 cup Fat-free Chicken Broth
1 tsp Fresh Cracked Black Pepper
1 tsp Garlic Salt

Directions:
Cube potatoes into inch size chunks and place in a pot of cold water.
Bring the water to a boil and cook potatoes for about 15 minutes, until fork tender.
Drain potatoes and add in Spinach, Parmesan, broth, pepper and garlic salt.
Place pot over low heat and stir to wilt Spinach, adding additional broth if necessary.
Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Kathleen
Description: 
Rich creamy mashed potatoes with the addition of a healthy green, and no butter or milk in sight!
