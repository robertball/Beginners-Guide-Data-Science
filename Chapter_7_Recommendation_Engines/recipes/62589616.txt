Title:
Emerald Fruit Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 green apples, cubed
2c cubed honeydew
2c green grapes, halved
3 kiwi, quartered
1 c lowfat plain yogurt
3T powdered sugar
3T orange juice
1/2t orange peel

Directions:
Combine fruits.
Combine dressing ingredients and combine.
Refrigerate.
