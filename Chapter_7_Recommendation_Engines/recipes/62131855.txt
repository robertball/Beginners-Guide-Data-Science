Title:
EASY SHREDDED BEEF TACOS Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 POUND ROUND STEAK
1 10OZ TOMATOES WITH GREEN CHILLIES
1 PACKAGE OF TACO SEASONING
1 SMALL ONION CHOPPED
1 TABLESPOON OF GROUND CUMIN
SHREDDED CHEESE
CORN TORTILLAS
SALSA

Directions:
COMBINE FIRST FIVE INGREDIENTS IN CROCK POT COOK ON LOW FOR 6 to 8 HOURS  SHRED WITH TWO FORKS SERVE ON WARM CORN TORTILLAS WITH CHEESE AND SALSA
