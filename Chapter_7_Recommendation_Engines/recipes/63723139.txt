Title:
ESP Panini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1034218.jpg
Ingredients:
8 to 12 slices of Italian or French bread
1 small red onion sliced in rounds
1 large sweet red pepper
1 eggplant
fresh baby spinich
8 -12 slices of Provolone cheese
2 cups bread crumbs
1/2teas garlic powder
2 eggs
1-2 TB butter
1 good handfull of grated. Romano or Parmesan cheese
salt and pepper to taste
olive oil

Directions:
Rinse the spinich and pat dry, set aside.  Slice the onion and carmelize in the butter then set aside.  Slice the red pepper into strips and saute in olive oil, salt and pepper and set aside.  In a large bowl mix the breadcrumbs, about 1/2 teas of salt, 1/2 teas of garlic, around 1/4 teas pepper amd a goodhandfull of grated cheese.  Set aside.  In a small bowl scramble the two eggs with a little salt and pepper.   Slice the eggplant thin in rounds and dip in the egg and then coat with breadcrumbs. Pan fry in olive oil until both sides are browned.  Drain on paper towels.  To assembly sandwiches lightly brush the bread with olive oil and then layer one slice of provolone, 2-3 slices of eggplant, onions, red pepper, spinich another slice of provolone , top with 2nd slice of bread.  Place sandwich on hot panini maker and cook until golden brown.  Remove, slice in half and repeat until you all the sandwiches you need.
