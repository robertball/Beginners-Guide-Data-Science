Title:
Peanut Butter Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Cups Peanut Butter
4 Tbsp Butter
1/2 tsp Vanilla
2- 2 1/2 Cups Confectioners Sugar
9 oz Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
1 Tbsp Shortening

Directions:
1. Microwave butter and peanut butter 1 minute. Mix. Add vanilla and and 2 cups sugar; blend well. Add more sugar as needed to form a dough like consistency thick enough to form a ball. Form balls and place on cookie sheet lined with wax paper. Refrigerate at least 1 hour
2. Melt chocolate and shortening in a double boiler.
Dip balls into chocolate. Return to cookie sheet. Refrigerate to harden.
