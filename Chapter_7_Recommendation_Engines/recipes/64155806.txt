Title:
Chili - Beef/Turkey/Deer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs Ground Beef, Turkey, or Deer (or choose two)
1 can Pinto Beans
1 can Chili Beans
1 can Kidney Beans
1 can Black Beans
1 can Corn (or frozen if you choose)
1 can Rotel (choose mild, medium, or hot)
1 packet Lowry's Taco Seasoning (or your favorite)
1 packet Ranch Dressing Powder

Directions:
Slow Cooker (preferred for better taste)
Put all ingredients in slow cooker.
Turn on high for 8-10 hours.
Regular Pot
Brown your meat.
Add all other ingredients.
Cook over low-medium heat for 2 hours.

Submitter: 
Nate Thee
Description: 
This is a quick and easy chili recipe. You can use your favorite ground meat and use any bean combination of your choosing.
