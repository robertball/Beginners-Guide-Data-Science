Title:
Keith’s Sugar Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/749875.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. powdered sugar
1 c. sugar
2 eggs
1 t. salt
1 t. cream of tartar
1 c. oil
1 c. margarine
1 t. baking soda
1 t. vanilla
4¼ c. flour (more if necessary)

Directions:
Mix together; Chill, if time allows.  Roll an cut out with cookie cutters.  Bake at 350 degrees for 8 minutes or until golden around the edges.

Submitter: 
KramerGirls
Description: 
This recipe originally came from Denise Conant.  Keith modified it and used it for Keith's Creations cookie business.  He made hundreds of dozens of cookies with this recipe.
