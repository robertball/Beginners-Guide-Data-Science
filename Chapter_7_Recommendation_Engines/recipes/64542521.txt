Title:
Helens Bread Machine Bagels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 oz water
3 1/2 Tbsp butter
1/2 Tbsp salt
2 Tbsp sugar or honey
4 !/2 cup flour
1/2 Tbsp yeast

Directions:
Put into bread maker and make dough
Roll out and cut out 12 bagels
Let rise
Boil 30 seconds each side (only 3-4 in pot at a time)
Place on greased baking sheet and sprinkle with garlic onion powder if desired
Bake 375 15 minutes
