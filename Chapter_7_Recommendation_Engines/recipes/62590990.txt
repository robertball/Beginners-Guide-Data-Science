Title:
Jess's Antipasti Wonderful Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/553835.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package (10 ounces) romaine salad
1 jar (7½ ounces) quartered marinated artichoke hearts, drained and quartered
1 cup cubed mozzarella cheese
1 cup fresh mushrooms, sliced
8 ounces cooked bay shrimp. *If you are not a shrip fan like myself you can sub grilled chicken, ham, or even cut up lunch meat of your choice.
½ cup red bell pepper strips
4 ounces dry salami strips
½ cup sliced ripe olives
1 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Italian Ranch Dressing, divided

Directions:
In a large bowl, toss the lettuce with 6 tablespoons of dressing and arrange on a platter. Arrange each of the remaining ingredients on top of the lettuce. Drizzle with the remaining dressing and serve.
