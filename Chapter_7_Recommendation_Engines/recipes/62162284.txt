Title:
Grilled Gingered Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 lb whole salmon fillet(skin on) Farm raised is not as good as wild  (big price difference though)
Cedar plank board
Marinade
1 cup soy sauce
1 cup muscavado (dark brown) sugar (in speciality stores)
1-5 inch piece of fresh ginger root (peeled and minced)
1/4 cup olive oil
2-3 garlic cloves (smashed)

Directions:
Combline marinade (can be made in food processor)
Put salmon in large zip lock bag(remove excess air)
Pour mariniade over salmon
Marinade overnight or up to two days in refrigerator.
Soak cedar plank in water 1/2 hour before grilling.
Place salmon skin side down on plank.
Preheat grill on medium
Grill for 20 minutes with lid closed.
Carefully remove salmon from plank, leaving skin behind.
