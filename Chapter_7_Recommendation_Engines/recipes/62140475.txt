Title:
EGG NOODLES WITH MUSHROOM Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb fresh mushroom ( chopped)
1 16 oz. heavy cream
2 tsp. chopped garlic
1/2 lb. egg noodles (cooked)
3 pcs. italian sausage (without the skin)
salt
ground balck pepper
1 tbs. garlic powder
1/2 cups of parmesan cheese

Directions:
In a skillet, saute the garlic till its golden brown.
Add the italian sausage and saute it. Till its brown,
Put the heavy cream in the skillet with the sausage.
Add salt and black pepper to taste.
Cover and simmer it for 5 mins.
Add the egg noodles in the skillet,add the parmesan cheese.
Mix it all together and then serve it till it hot.
