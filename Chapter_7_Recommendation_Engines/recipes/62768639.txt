Title:
Taco Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head of lettuce
1 bell pepper, chopped
2-3 green onions, chopped
1 lb.hamburger meat, cooked and seasoned with taco seasoning
1 can ranch style beans(drain off most of the juice)
1 lb. grated cheddar cheese
1 pkg. Nacho Cheese Doritos or plain Doritos, broken into pieces
1bottle Catalina salad dressing

Directions:
In a large bowl, shread lettuce.  Add other ingredients and toss.
