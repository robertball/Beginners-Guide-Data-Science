Title:
Asian Style Peanut Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup green Onion, Thinly Sliced
3 Tbl minced fresh Garlic
1 Tbl fresh Ginger, minced
1/4 cup Honey
1 Tbl Brown Sugar
1/4 cup Creamy Peanut Butter
1/4 cup Soy Sauce
3 Tbl Rice Vinegar
1/3 cup Mae-Ploy sauce
2 Tbl Sesame Oil
1/2 Cup Peanut Oil
1/4 cup chopped Cilantro
1 Tbl Chili Oil

Directions:
In a saucepan, warm peanut oil to 160 deg. & infuse the Garlic, Ginger, cilantro & thinly sliced green onions. Set aside, let cool.
In a food processor, combine: Soy Sauce, Rice Vinegar, Honey, Brown Sugar, Peanut Butter, Mae-Ploy & Chili Oil. Process till smooth. While running, slowly pour cooled infused oil into processor. Blend till silky & smooth. Adjust ingredients to taste or just leave alone & say, "mmmmmmm!"  Use immediately for dipping, salad or cooking sauce. Refrigerate up to 2 weeks in a tightly sealed jar.

Description: 
A great Asian Peanut dressing or Dipping Sauce. It can also be a great marinara sauce for Shrimp or Chicken.Modified from: Thai Peanut Dressing
