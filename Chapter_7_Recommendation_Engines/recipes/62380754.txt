Title:
Nouveau Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lb red potatoes, cubed
1 red bell pepper, diced
2 bunches green onions, sliced
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
1 lb crispy cooked bacon, crumbled
3/4 cup fresh lemon juice (about 3-4 lemons)
3/4 cup light mayo
3/4 cup light sour cream
salt & pepper

Directions:
cube potatoes and boil approximately 10 minutes until cooked through, drain & cool.
Mix potatoes with ingredients through bacon.
Mix lemon juice, mayo, sour cream, and salt & pepper in a bowl. Whip with fork until smooth.
Stir dressing into salad right before serving.
