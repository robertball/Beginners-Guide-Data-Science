Title:
Bucca's Famous Alfredo Sauce Recipe

Ingredients:
4 cups heavy cream
3 egg yolks
1/4 cup Parmesan or Romano cheese, grated

Directions:

2. Whisk in egg yolks, one at a time.
3. Once egg yolks are fully incorporated, add cheese and stir until thoroughly combined, and sauce is smooth.
