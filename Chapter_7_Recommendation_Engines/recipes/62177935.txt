Title:
Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c mayo
1 c artichoke (2 14 oz cans, not marinated)
1 c mozzarella, grated
1 c parmesan, grated or shredded
1 t salt
1 clove garlic, minced

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.  Put in a buttered casserole dish.  Bake at 350 degrees.

Submitter: 
Lisa Scott
Description: 
This is a wonderfully cheesy, garlicky artichoke dip, and very easy to make.  Can be made the night before and refrigerated.
