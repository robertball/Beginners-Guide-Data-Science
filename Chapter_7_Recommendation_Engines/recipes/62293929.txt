Title:
Katy's Crab Craze! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz cream cheese
1/4 cup grated mozzarella
8oz cooked crab (check for shells)
1 green onion, chopped

Directions:
Mix all ingredients.
pour into oven safe serving dish
Broil on High until golden bubbly
Serve IMMEDIATELY with crackers, veggies, melba toast

Submitter: 
Katy Blaylock
Description: 
Had to think of something different to serve at a party, so I came up with this cheesy delight!(immitation krab does not work well with this recipe)
