Title:
Yummy Apple Cinnamon Crepes! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/765021.jpg
Ingredients:
Crepes:
3 eggs
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 cups flour
2 cups milk
1/4 cooking oil
1/2 teaspoon cinamon
1 1/2 tablespoons milk
Apple Filling:
4 granny smith apples
1/8 cup water
1/2 sugar
2 teaspoons cinnamon
1/8 cornstarch
1 tablespoon water

Directions:
Crepes:
1.In a medium mixing bowl, combine eggs and salt.
2.Gradually add the flour alternatively with the milk.
3.Bet in cooking oil.
4.Refridgerate for at least one hour.
5.Add the milk.
6.Pour 1/3 cup of batter into a greased crepe pan or frying pan.
7.Pick up the pan and rotate it so the batter is evenly coating the pan.
8.Cook for about 30 seconds per side.
Apple Filling:
1.Peel and dice the granny smith apples.
2.Combine apples, sugar, cinnamon and 1/8 cup water in a pot.
3.Mix cornstarch and a tablespoon of water together in a small bowl, pour into apple mixture immeadiately.
4.Simmer over medium heat, stirring often, until thickened.
5.Spoon the filling into the crepes, fold over and enjoy!
