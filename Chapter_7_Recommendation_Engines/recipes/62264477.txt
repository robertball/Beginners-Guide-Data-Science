Title:
Awesome marinade for chicken or steak...and maybe pork! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/340805.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup olive oil
1 Tablespoon and 1 teaspoon of seasoning salt
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 Tablespoon and 1 teaspoon of chili powder
2 teaspoons cider vinegar
2 Tablespoons of ketchup

Directions:
Mix the ingredients in a bowl to make a loose paste.  Pour over desired meat and stir or shake to coat.  Marinate as long as you want or throw them straight on the grill.

Submitter: 
I Love Troy
Description: 
Great, basic grilling marinade that's been good on all the meat I've tried it on so far.
