Title:
Dutch bokkenpootjes delicious almond meringue cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 eggwhites
350 grams sugar
500 grams ground almonds
rind of 1 lemon
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon vanilla essence plus a few drops
one bar of good quality chocolate
50 grams butter
100 grams icing sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 160 degrees Celcius.
Line a baking tray with a sheet of greaseproof paper.
Beat the eggwhites stiff with the sugar, then add the ground almonds, lemon rind, cinnamon and vanilla essence.
Put in an icing bag with a large opening (or a sandwich bag with a corner cut off) and pipe into 7cm bars.
Allow to dry out for a couple of hours.
Place in the middle of a preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes. Do not let them go golden.
Allow to cool.
Make buttercream by creaming together the butter and  icing sugar and adding a few drops of vanilla essence.
Melt the chocolate in a bowl above a pan of boiling water.
To assemble the cookies, spread buttercream on the back of a cookie, sandwich with the back of another cookie, dip both ends into the melted chocolate and allow to cool.
