Title:
Creamy Cheesy Scrambled Egg Bake with Basil Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
10 slices toasted, buttered whole wheat bread, crusts cut off
8 eggs
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup milk
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
salt and pepper to taste
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 cup minced fresh basil
1 cup chopped tomatoes
Parmesan Cheese for sprinkling

Directions:
Spray a 9x11 baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.  Line the dish with the toasted buttered bread.  In a separate bowl whisk eggs with sour cream and milk. Mix in cheese and garlic powder. Season with salt and pepper. Pour mixture on top of toast in baking dish.  Cover and refrigerate overnight.
The next day preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.  Sprinkle minced basil over top of egg mixture.  Bake for 40-45 minutes or until done.  Serve topped with diced tomatoes and sprinkling of parmesan cheese.

Description: 
Sour cream makes these eggs rich and creamy, and the basil adds a little kick of flavor. A flavorful hearty crust using buttered whole wheat bread and baked in a casserole makes it easy to serve for a crowd!Modified from: Creamy Cheesy Scrambled Eggs with Basil
