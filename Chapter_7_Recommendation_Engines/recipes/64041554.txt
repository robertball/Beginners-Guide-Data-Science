Title:
Silpancho from Cochabamba - Bolivia Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1123342.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 kg of beef soft without fat or nerve
6 fried eggs
3 onions
2 tomatoes
6 potatoes
3 cups bread crumbs
2 chilli peppers (locoto)
2 cups rice
salt
oil

Directions:
Cut the meat into fillets, season with salt to taste and tap the meat about the breadcrumbs until this cover and thin.
Cook the sliced potatoes and when they are tender, brown them in oil.
Fry the meat flattened.
Then bake in a pan apart white rice.
Cut the onion, tomato and the peppers into squares and garnish with a little oil and salt.
Fry eggs at the time of serving.
Serve the rice with fried potatoes and place over them the meat, fried egg and garnish with onion salad.
