Title:
Souper Wheelies Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
1 small onion, chopped
14.5 oz. can beef broth
14.5 oz. can Italian-style diced tomatoes
2 cups water
3/4 cup wagon wheel pasta, uncooked
1 cup frozen corn
1 cup green beans
1 tsp. dried basil, crushed
1/2 tsp. dried oregano
*Have an extra can of beef broth on hand in case soup is too thick.

Directions:
Brown ground beef in a large pot, add chopped onion. Drain fat.
Stir beef broth and water into meat. Heat to boiling.
Add pasta and cook at medium-high heat uncovered for 10-12 minutes.
Reduce to medium heat, stir in veggies, basil, oregano, and tomatoes (do not drain tomatoes).
Return to boiling, then reduce heat to medium-low. Cover and cook for 10 minutes or until veggies are tender, stirring every few minutes.
If soup is too thick add more beef broth and water.

Submitter: 
Kristin McNeil
Description: 
Ground beef, vegetables, and pasta in a healthy, kid-friendly soup!
