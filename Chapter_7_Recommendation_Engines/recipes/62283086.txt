Title:
The Best Shrimp Scampi with Linguini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs shrimp peeled and deveined(size of your choice)
1 stick butter
2 Tbs olive oil
8 cloves garlic minced
2 Tbs dried parsley
1/2 cup dry wine like Chardonnay
Juice of 1/2 lemon
2 Tbs flour
Salt and pepper to taste
One box of favorite linguini
Shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Cook linguini to desired tenderness.  Toss with a small amount of olive oil and parmesan cheese.  Try to time the cooking of the pasta so it is done only a few minutes before the shrimp.
Melt butter and 2tbs olive oil in a skillet over meduim heat.
Stir in garlic and parsley and saute for 2 minutes being very careful not to burn the garlic.
Pour in the wine and turn heat to high.
Add shrimp and cook stirring frequently.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Be very careful not to overcook the shrimp.
When shrimp are done, stir in small amounts of flour until sauce thickens.
Remove from heat and stir in lemon juice.
Toss with linguini or serve on top.
