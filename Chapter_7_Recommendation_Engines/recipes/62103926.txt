Title:
Whipped Butter Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 pounds potatoes
1 stick of butter
1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
salt
pepper

Directions:
Peel potatoes and slice them into chunks. Place in a pot and cover with water. Bring to a boil and cook 30-40 minutes or until the potatoes are very soft.
Remove from heat and drain potatoes. Return to original pot and stir in warm whipping cream.
Melt butter and pout into the pot. Add salt and pepper to taste. Whip with an electric mixer for 2-3 minutes or until smooth.

Description: 
Whipped potatoes with a strong buttery, creamy flavor! A Southern favorite!
