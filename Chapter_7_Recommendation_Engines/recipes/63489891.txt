Title:
Scottish Stovies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Left over Roast Beef - diced or can use Chicken or Corned Beef - shredded
4 large Tatties (Potatoes) - partially boiled and sliced
1 Onion - thinly sliced
1 tbsp Dripping (from cooked beef) or Lard
A wee touch of Beef Stock
Salt and Pepper

Directions:
Add a wee bit of lard to a pot and gently cook the onions for some 5 minutes or so until soft;
Add the beef or other and salt and pepper.
Layer over with tattie slices and cover with the stock to taste (depending on how moist you prefer the meal);
Suggestion - add a wee bit and top up as required.
Simmer on the hob for about an hour or cook in the oven at 190c/380f/gas 5 for about 50 minutes.

Submitter: 
Hasta Luego
Description: 
Traditional Recipe: A great, simple but very tasty example of how to make food go further. In the big houses in Scotland, the servants used this for the left over beef etc. The corned beef is a modern twist.
