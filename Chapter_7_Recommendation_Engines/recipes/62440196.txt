Title:
Tracy Ann's Cherry-Pomegranate Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/474808.jpg
Ingredients:
2 oz Tequila
2 oz Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix
2 oz Pama Liqueur
1 oz Pom Wonderful cherry-pomegranate juice
1 lime wedge (optional)
salt for rimming (optional)

Directions:
Combine Tequila, Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix, Pama Liqueur and cherry-pomegranate juice into cocktail shaker half full with ice.
Shake well and strain into a chilled glass filled with ice (rimming glass with salt optional).
Garnish with lime wedge.

Submitter: 
Tracy Ann
Description: 
This margarita cocktail is made with Tequila, Jose Cuervo Lime Margarita Mix, Pama Liqueur, and cherry-pomegranate juice. Dress it up by serving it in your favorite chilled glass.Modified from: Cuervo Especial Pomegranate Margarita Martini
