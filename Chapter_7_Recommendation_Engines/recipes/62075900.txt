Title:
Jay's Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked and chopped
2 1/2 cups shredded cheese (any variety)
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 cans condensed cream of chicken soup
8 oz sour cream
2 Anaheim chilli peppers, chopped
1/4 cup milk
16 tortillas

Directions:
1. Preheat the over to 375 degrees.
2. Mix chicken with cheese, onion, and half of the peppers and set aside.
3. Mix the soup, the other half of the peppers, sour cream and milk to make the sauce.  Cover the bottom of two 13 inch x 10 inch baking dishes with some of the sauce, reserving the rest.
4. Place about 2 tablespoons of the mixture on the center of a tortilla and roll tightly.  Place each tortilla in the baking dishes, then cover the enchiladas with the remaining sauce.
5. Bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
dramamath
Description: 
These chicken enchiladas are covered in a sour cream sauce and go well with spanish rice.  Grilling the chicken breasts ahead of time gives the enchiladas a wonderful flavor.  Substitute with your favorite pepper.
