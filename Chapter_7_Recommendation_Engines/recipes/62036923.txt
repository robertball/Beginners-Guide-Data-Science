Title:
Cool Pops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 oz. pkg. jello
1 pkg. koolaid
1 c sugar
2 c hot water
2 c cold water

Directions:
Mix the jello, koolaid, sugar and hot water.  Add the cold water.  Pick flavors that match, like cherry jello and koolaid, for the best flavor.  Add the cold water.  Pour into molds--I always used the tupperware molds. Freeze.

Submitter: 
Susan
Description: 
These are great popsicles.  My kids loved them when they were little--and now that they're big, too!
