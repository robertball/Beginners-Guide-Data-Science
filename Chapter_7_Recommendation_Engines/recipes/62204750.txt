Title:
Lunch Lady Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/460630.jpg
Ingredients:
12 frozen burritos, any kind you choose.
1 small can of red enchilada sauce
cheddar cheese as much as you like
Garnishes: cilantro, tomato,sour cream, pickled jalapenos, radishes, black olives.

Directions:
Pre heat the oven to 375. Lay the burritos in a 9 x 13"
casserole pan. Pour on the sauce and bake for 30 mins. Spread with cheese bake 5 more mins. Pass the toppings!

Submitter: 
Patty Cakes
Description: 
These are quick and tasty for busy week night meals. Add rice and a salad and you have dinner in under 30 mins!
