Title:
Bacon and Cheese Deviled Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 hard cooked eggs, peeled
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 tbsp. honey mustard
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
4 strips bacon, cooked and crumbled
4 tbsp. shredded sharp cheddar cheese, divided
extra bacon pieces, optional

Directions:
Halve eggs lengthwise.  Spoon out yolks into a medium sized bowl.  Set egg whites aside.
Mash yolks.  Add mayonnaise, honey mustard, salt and pepper.  Fold in bacon and 2 tablespoons of cheese.
Fill each half egg white with yolk mixture.  Sprinkle with additional cheese and extra bacon if desired.  Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Submitter: 
Diane Blanchard Angell
Description: 
A yummy twist on deviled eggs!  Bacon and sharp cheddar add a super flavor to regular deviled eggs!  If there's any yolk mixture left over, it makes a yummy sandwich spread!
