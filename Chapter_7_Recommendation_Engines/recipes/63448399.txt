Title:
Vegan Chocolate Spelt Biscotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/936852.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup olive oil
1/2 cup raw sugar
1/4 cup agave
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 tablespoon almond extract
4 tablespoons chia seeds
1 cup hot water
1 tbsp lecithin
1/3 cup Kahlua
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cups spelt  flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 1/2 cups cocoa powder
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 cup pecans
1 cup chocolate chips
1/2 cup cacao nibs (optional)

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
Put chia seeds in blender.
Add hot water.
Let sit for 5 minutes.
Add lecithin.
Add 1/4 cup water.
Add extracts,  olive oil, salt, kahlua,  agave and sugar.
Blend at a high speed.
In a bowl combine flour, cocoa powder and baking powder; gradually stir into blended mixture. Mix in chocolate chips, cacao nibs and nuts.
Divide dough in half.
Line a cookies sheet with parchment paper.
Spoon out dough into two (12x2) logs.
Wet hands to mold dough into logs.
Bake for 40 minutes in the preheated oven, or until logs are light brown.
Remove from oven, and set aside to cool for 10 minutes.
Reduce oven heat to 275 degrees F (135 degrees C).
Cut logs on diagonal into 3/4 inch thick slices. Lay on sides on parchment covered cookie sheet. Bake approximately 25 minutes, or until dry; cool.

Description: 
These Vegan Chocolate Spelt Biscotti are yummy. Spelt, a cousin to wheat, is a good substitute for people who are wheat sensitive.
