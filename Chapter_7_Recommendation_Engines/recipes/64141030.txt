Title:
Roasted Cheesy Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 head cauliflower, chopped into bite size pieces
1 Tbsp olive oil
Salt & pepper to taste
1/2 Tbsp bread crumbs
1/2 Tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup Gruyere cheese, shredded (or Swiss cheese)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Toss cauliflower in olive oil.  Arrange on baking sheet and sprinkle with salt and pepper.  Roast for 10 minutes, stir, and bake for an additional 10 minutes or until cauliflower starts to brown in spots and is tender. Set aside, but do not turn off oven.
In a small bowl mix bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese.  Pour cauliflower pieces into a small glass baking dish.  Sprinkle Gruyere cheese over cauliflower.  Top with Parmesan mix.  Bake for about 7 minutes or until cheese is melted.  Broil 2 minutes to brown topping.
