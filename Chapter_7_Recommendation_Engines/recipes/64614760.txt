Title:
Pork Chops with apples and Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 to 8 boneless Pork loin chops
1 Tbs. vegetable oil
4 cups stuffing mix
1 can (21 oz.) apple pie filling
1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
In a skillet brown pork shops in oil over medium high heat.
Meanwhile prepare stuffing
Bring 1 1/3 cups water, 1/4 cup butter to a boil.
Remove from heat.
Stir in 4 cups stuffing and toss with fork.
Spread pie filling into a greased 13x9 in. baking pan
Sprinkle with cinnamon
Place pork shops on top.
Divide stuffing into 8 balls.
Place on each chip and flatten a little to cover chop.
Cover with foil and bake 350 for 35 minutes.
Uncover and bake 10 min. longer
