Title:
Chicken Enchilada Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-1/2 cups grated chedder
1- 12oz can of shredded chicken
1 small carton of sour cream
2 cans cream of chicken soup
2 cans of little green chili peppers- chopped

Directions:
Mix all together.
Bake til warm or put in crock pot.
Serve w/ chips or over noodles.
