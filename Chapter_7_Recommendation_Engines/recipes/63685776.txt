Title:
My Favorite No-Mayo Egg Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 hard boiled eggs
2t tahini
1T yogurt
2t middle eastern spice mix such as Za'atar
1T chopped cornichon or other pickle (optional)
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Peel and coarsely chop the eggs.  2. In a bowl mix tahini, yogurt, and spices.  3. Add chopped pickle if using and stir.  4. Add eggs and gently stir until well incorporated.  If it seems too dry, add more yogurt 1 teaspoon at a time.  5.  Enjoy on top of toast or in a sandwich.

Submitter: 
CAMILLEELISE
Description: 
I don't like mayonnaise and so I never have it in the house.  I do however like egg salad, and I adjusted it to make it without the mayo.  Pretty close to the original but tastier and healthier!
