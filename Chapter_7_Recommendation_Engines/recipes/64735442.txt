Title:
Stuffed Shells Florentine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup part-skim ricotta cheese
3 oz. Mozzarella cheese, shredded
3/4 oz Parmesan cheese
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 10-ounce package frozen spinach, well drained
1/2 teaspoon oregano
10 jumbo shells, cooked and cooled
2 cups Marinara sauce

Directions:
Preheat to 350 degrees
Mix first 7 ingredients well
Stuff shells  with approx. 3 T of cheese mixture
Pour sauce into baking dish, reserve 1 cup
Arrange shells with stuffed side up
Pour remaining sauce over top of shells
Bake 35 minutes
Sprinkle with parsley
