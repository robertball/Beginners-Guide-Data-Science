Title:
Holly's Famous Tomato Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Baked Pie crust
4 medium tomatoes
mozzarella
salt & pepper
Basil
garlic
mozzarella cheese-6 oz.
1/2 c mayo
1/4 c parmesan

Directions:
Bake pie crust.Make sure you prick the pie crust before baking or use a pie chain.  cool completely before proceeding.  Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese. Slice tomatoes and layer on top of cheese. Season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle basil and garlic on top of tomatoes. Mix together 6 oz mozzarella, mayo and parmesan. Spread on top of the poe. Bake @ 400 until cheese melts and lightly browns.
