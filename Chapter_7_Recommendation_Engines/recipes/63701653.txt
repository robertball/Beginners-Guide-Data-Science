Title:
Bacon Wrapped Cream Cheese Jalapeno Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1028841.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces cream cheese, softened
2 teaspoons onion powder
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon Old Bay
Pinch of Salt
12 Jalapenos, halved & seeded
12 pieces of bacon, uncooked, cut in half

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Combine cream cheese and spices in small bowl.
Spoon mixture into each pepper half, until level.
Wrap each in bacon, and place on baking sheet.
Bake for 40-45 minutes.
Remove and enjoy!
