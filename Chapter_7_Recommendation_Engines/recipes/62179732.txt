Title:
Simple Carnitas ~ My way... Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/290081.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3 1/2 - 4) pound boneless pork shoulder cut into 4" chunks (leave fat on)
1 oranges, quartered
2 slices white onion
6 cloves garlic, peeled and crushed
1T kosher salt
2 tablespoons ground cumin
1 tsp. black pepper
2 bay leaves
1 can of coca-cola

Directions:
Place the pork shoulder, garlic, salt, cumin, black pepper, into a large Dutch oven and mix seasonings in well. Add water and one can of coca-cola to cover the meat; add oranges, onions and bay leaves. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to low, and simmer covered for 2 hours or until falls apart easily with a spoon.  Uncovered and turn heat up to high for about 15-20 minutes.  Watch so it doesn't burn; when it starts to fry in it's fat it will brown on the bottom.  Stir every 3-4 minutes unti browning is achieved. Shred the meat if desired or serve in chunks.
