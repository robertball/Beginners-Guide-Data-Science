Title:
Snickerdoodle Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup plus 2 tbsp butter, softened
1 3/4 cup sugar, divided (1 1/2 cup for dough and 1/4 cup for cinnamon sugar)
2 eggs, room temperature
2 tsp vanilla
2 3/4 cup flour
1 1/4 tsp baking soda
3/4 tsp cornstarch
1 tsp salt
1 1/2 tbsp cinnamon

Directions:
Freeze mixing bowl while butter is softening, and eggs are coming to room temperature.
Cream together butter and sugar till fluffy, scraping down sides of bowl often.
Add eggs and vanilla. Cream for 1 - 2 more minutes, until well mixed and fluffy, scraping down sides to mix well.
Sift together dry ingredients, not including cinnamon.
Slowly add dry ingredients to mixing bowl, scraping down sides often.
Place mixing bowl in fridge for 20 - 30 minutes.
Preheat oven to 350F while dough is in fridge. Also, make cinnamon sugar with remaining sugar and cinnamon. Place in bowl.
Using a small cookie scoop, create small balls, roll in hands to make even ball shape and drop in cinnamon sugar.
Coat dough balls with sugar mixture. Use a spoon to evenly and generously coat.
Place on parchment lined baking tray.
Flatten cookies to about 1/2 inch with the bottom of a glass. (If cookies start sticking to glass, dip glass in cinnamon sugar mixture.)
Keep dough in fridge while sheet is in oven.
Bake for 9 - 11 minutes or until just turning color.
Let cool for a couple minute on cookie sheet before placing on cooling rack.
Enjoy!
