Title:
Sauza® Golden Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976040.jpg
Ingredients:
9 ounces Sauza® Gold Tequila
3 1/2 ounces DeKuyper® Triple Sec
18 ounces Margarita Mix
6 ounces Orange Juice
GARNISH
Lime Squeeze

Directions:
Combine the ingredients without ice and shake vigorously. Then just pour the mixture over ice in a 14 oz glass, garnish and enjoy what the hint of orange does for a traditional favorite.

Submitter: 
Sauza
Description: 
A hint of orange flavor adds its special tangy freshness to this classic cocktail. Start with the best ingredients, and enjoy over ice.
