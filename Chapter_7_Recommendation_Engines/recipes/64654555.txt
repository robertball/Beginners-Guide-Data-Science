Title:
Instant Pot® Chicken Breasts with Sriracha Seasoning Stix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4547328.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon oil (such as avocado or canola)
3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (8 to 10 oz. each)
3/4  teaspoon garlic salt (divided use)
3/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper per (divided use)
6 Sriracha Seasoning Stix; flavor of choice, mix or match
1 cup water

Directions:
Using the sauté function heat the Instant Pot and add oil to the pot.
Season both sides of the chicken with equal amounts of garlic salt and pepper.
When sauté  function reads hot cook chicken four minutes per side until golden brown. Remove chicken from pot.
Insert two Seasoning Stix into each chicken breast.
Add 1 cup of water and trivet to the pot.
Place chicken on the trivet.
Lock lid on pot and manually cook on high for 5 minutes.
Allow the chicken to naturally release for 5 minutes, and then quick release the pressure .
Note: Reserve broth from the pot to pour over the sliced chicken for added flavor. Sriracha Seasoning Stix can be found at https://www.srirachastix.com/

Description: 
Incredibly quick and easy chicken breasts with bold flavors using Sriracha Seasoning Stix. You can have dinner on the table in less than 30 minutes.
