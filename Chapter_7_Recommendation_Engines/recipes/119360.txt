Title:
Savory Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/422797.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (12 ounce) packages refrigerated buttermilk biscuits
1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
3 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted
3/4 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
1/8 teaspoon dill weed
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Separate each tube of biscuits into 10 biscuits; place in a single layer in a greased 11-in. x 7-in. x 2-in. baking pan. Sprinkle with cheeses. Drizzle with butter; sprinkle with seasonings. Bake at 350 degrees F for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Rose  McClure
Description: 
Shredded cheese and a sprinkling of herbs give refrigerated biscuits great flavor. This is one of my favorite bread recipes.
