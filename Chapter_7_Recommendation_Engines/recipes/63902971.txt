Title:
granola Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C rolled oats (preferably not instant)
1 1/2 C crispy rice cereal
3 Tbs flour
1/2 C pumpkin seeds
1/2 C chopped pecans
1/2 C dried unsweetened coconut
1/2 C dried cranberries or raisins
1/4 C chocolate chips
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 C butter
3/4 C hone
1/4 C almond butter

Directions:
Place oats, pumpkin seeds and chopped pecans on baking sheet & toast for 15 min  @300 stirring once or twice
In a lg bowl mix the crispy rice cereal, flour, coconut, cranberries, salt & cinnamon.  Add toasted oats mixture & mix all together
In a sm saucepan, bring the honey & butter t a gentle boil on low heat.  Simmer on low for 5 min, stirring often.  Remove from heat & add vanilla & almond butter
mix into the dry ingredients & incorporate completely
Line 9 x 13 inch baking pan w buttered parchment paper.  Pour mixture into the pan.  If desired, sprinkle chocolate chips on top.  Use buttered hand or oiled spatula to press mixture firmly into pan
Bake @ 300 for 30 min, checking at 25 min as some ovens bake more quickly.  Allow bars to cool in pan completely before cutting them.
When cooled, remove the whole thing from the pan by pulin the parchment paper at the ends
Place parchment and bars on a cutting board & cut into slices.
The bars will keep for several week & for loner in the fridge or freeze.
