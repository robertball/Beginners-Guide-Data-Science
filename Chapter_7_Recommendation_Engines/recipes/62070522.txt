Title:
Ranch Mashed Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 medium potatoes, cut into small chunks
2 cups cauliflower florets
1/2 c ranch dressing
1/4 c sour cream

Directions:
1. Place potatoes and cauliflower in large saucepan.  Add enough water to cover the vegetables. Bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer 20 minutes or until vegietables are tender.
2. Drain water, add dressing and sour cream. Mash until light and fluffy.
3. Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jamie Willhight
Description: 
This is the fluffiest mashed potatoes ever! My family can't tell there is cauliflower in it, you can't taste it!
