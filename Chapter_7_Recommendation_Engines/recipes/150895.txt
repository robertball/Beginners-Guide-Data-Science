Title:
Long Island Ice Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979435.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 ounce Tanqueray® London Dry Gin
1/4 ounce Smirnoff No. 21™ Vodka
1/4 ounce Captain Morgan® Silver Spiced Rum
1/4 ounce Jose Cuervo Especial® Tequila
1/4 ounce triple sec
1 ounce sweet and sour mix
6 ounces cola
1 wedge lemon (optional)

Directions:
Add Tanqueray(R) London Dry Gin, Smirnoff No. 21(TM) Vodka, Captain Morgan(R) Silver Spiced Rum, Jose Cuervo Especial(R) Tequila, triple sec, and sweet and sour mix to an ice-filled collins glass and stir.
Top with cola.
Garnish with lemon wedge (optional).

Submitter: 
Smirnoff
Description: 
To give this favorite drink full flavor that is not overpowering, be sure to use premium spirits.
