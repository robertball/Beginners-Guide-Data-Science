Title:
Green Bean Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2lbs Ham/salted pork chopped
2 qts. water
4 c. green beans, cut up
3 c. potatoes, cubed
2 med. onions, sliced
1 tsp. salt (to taste)
1/4 tsp. pepper (to taste)
1 c. cream or milk

Directions:
In a 6 quart saucepan combine all ingredients (except cream/milk) bring to boil then simmer until meat is cooked and vegetables are tender. Skim off excess fat. Just before serving stir in 1 cup cream or milk.
