Title:
Turkey & Black Bean Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey
1 can black beans, drained
Shredded cheese as desired
1 can green enchilada sauce
1 pack whole wheat or spinach tortillas

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
Brown ground turkey.
Combine and mix ground turkey, black beans and cheese in a bowl.
Roll turkey mixture into tortillas and place seam side down in glass baking dish.
Pour one can green enchilada sauce over top.
Bake at 425 for 20-25 minutes.
