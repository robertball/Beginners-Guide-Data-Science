Title:
"Maui Wowie" Shrimp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Lb medium size Shrimp, shelled/deveined.
1 Cup Mayonnaise
Garlic Salt
Black Pepper
Lemon Wedges
BBQ Skewers
*Cayenne Pepper

Directions:
Preheat BBQ grill to medium heat.
Thread shrimp on to skewers.  Season both sides of shrimp with garlic salt and black pepper.  Generously coat shrimp with mayonnaise on both sides.
Place skewers on grill and cook for 5-10 minutes on each side till shrimp is cooked and mayonnaise turns golden brown.
Serve with lemon wedges.
*Cayenne pepper can be added with the garlic salt and pepper or mix 1/4 tsp into mayonnaise before coating shrimp for a spicier flavor.

Description: 
Easy recipe to put together for that laid-back Maui Wowie person.  Great for the beach or backyard BBQ. You will be surprised by the Onolicious flavor. This dish always gets the Shaka sign!
