Title:
African Nut Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cloves garlic
2 cups fresh cilantro
1 can diced tomatoes
1/2 cup peanut butter
2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp cayenne
1 cup water
3 lbs sweet potatoes, cut into 2" chunks
1 can garbanzo beans
1 1/2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs
4 prepared cups whole wheat couscous

Directions:
Blend together garlic, cilantro, tomatoes (with juice), peanut butter, spices and 3/4 tsp salt together until smooth.
Put Potatoes, beans, and chicken into crock.  Pour sauce over all.
Cook on low 8 to 10 hours, or until potatoes are tender.
Serve over whole wheat couscous.
