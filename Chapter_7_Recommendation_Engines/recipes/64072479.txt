Title:
chicken tortilla soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2-3 chicken breast halves
1 15 oz. can black beans, undrained
2 15 oz. cans Mexican or Rotel tomatoes
1 cup medium salsa
4 oz. can chopped green chilies
14 1/2 oz. can tomato sauce
1 cup frozen corn
Serve with:
tortilla chips
grated cheese
sour cream

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large slow cooker.
Cook on low 8 hours.
Just before serving cut chicken breasts into small pieces.
Serve with chips and top with cheese and/or sour cream.
