Title:
Jeana's Meat Loaf with Sweet BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Meat Mixture
1 1/2   lbs Good quality Ground Beef
3/4   Cup Saltines (crushed)
1/4   Cup chopped onion
1   tsp Salt
1/4  tsp Pepper
1/2  Cup Milk
BBQ Sauce
1/3 cup Catsup
2  Tbs Brown Sugar
1   tsp Prepared Mustard

Directions:
Mix Meat mixture ingredients together, Place in loaf pan.
Mix BBQ Sauce, pour over meat mixture, place in preheated 350 degree oven 1 hour
