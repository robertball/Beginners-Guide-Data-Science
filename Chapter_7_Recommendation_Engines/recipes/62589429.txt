Title:
Bread Pudding with Brandy Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pudding:
10 slices day old bread
1 qt. milk - heated
1 cup light cream
4 eggs
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 cup butter - melted
1/2 cup seedless raisins (optional)
Sauce:
3 egg yolks
1 cup sugar
1 1/2 cups milk
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tbsp. cornstarch
1/4 cup water
3 tbsp. brandy

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.  Combine bread, milk and cream in a large bowl. In another bowl, beat eggs slightly, stir in sugar and add to bread mixture.  Stir in vanilla, nutmeg, cinnamon, butter and raisins.  Pour into buttered, shallow. 2 qt. baking dish.  Set in larger shallow pan and add hot water to larger pan to a depth of 1 inch.  Bake 1 hour or until knife comes out clean.  Serve warm with Brandy Sauce.
Sauce:
In small saucepan, beat eggs slightly then add sugar, vanilla and milk.  Mix well.  Cook over low heat, stirring until mixture boils.  In small bowl, combine cornstarch and water until smooth, then stir into hot mixture.  Cook, stirring, until thickened.  Remove from heat and add brandy.  Serve slightly warm or chilled.
