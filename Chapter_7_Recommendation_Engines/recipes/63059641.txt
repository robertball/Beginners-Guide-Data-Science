Title:
Easy Caramel Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
4 tablespoons unsalted butter
2 tablespoons heavy cream

Directions:
In a small saucepan combine brown sugar and butter. Heat on low until sugar is melted.
Add cream and bring to a low boil for 2 minutes
