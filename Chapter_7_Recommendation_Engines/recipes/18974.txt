Title:
Berry Shooters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/727021.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (3 ounce) package tropical fruit-flavored Jell-O® mix
1 cup hot water
1 cup triple berry vodka

Directions:
In a medium bowl combine the gelatin mix and boiling water. Stir until gelatin dissolves. Pour in the vodka and stir. Pour into small paper cups and chill in the refrigerate until set.

Submitter: 
TASIA
Description: 
Here's a fun and easy recipe for a summer party special! Use any flavor gelatin mix. Pour into small paper cups or ice cube trays so you can shoot 'em back in one  gulp.
