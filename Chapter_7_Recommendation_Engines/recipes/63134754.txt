Title:
Zuppa Tuscana Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 1/2 cups Chicken Stock or broth
1/2 cup of heavy cream
2 medium Russet potatoes
4 cups of chopped Kale, rinsed and dried
1 pound Spicy Italian Turkey Sausage
2 cloves of garlic, chopped
1/4 tsp sea salt
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes

Directions:
In a large saucepan, combine chicken stock and cream, cook over medium heat stirring occasionally. Clean and slice potatoes, leaving skin intact, 1/4 inch thick, then cut slices into quarters and add to broth/cream mixture. Add the Kale.
In a separate skillet, add cut sausage, and fresh garlic cloves, saute until sausage is cooked through.
When Sausage is cooked, add sausage and garlic mixture to soup. Add salt and crushed red pepper flakes. Reduce heat and allow the soup to simmer for 2 hours, stirring occasionally.

Submitter: 
Megan
Description: 
Love Italian? You will Love this creamy potato soup with a flair of spicy Italian Turkey Sausage, kale, and a hint of hot pepper for a nice kick. A perfect addition to any meal or for a Sunday Brunch.
