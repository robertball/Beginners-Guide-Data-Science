Title:
Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound bag black beans
1 small onion, diced (optional)
2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
1 can diced tomato with green chili (optional)
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
To serve black beans burrito filling:
Add 1 pound package black beans to a slow cooker. Cover and cook on high for 4 hours. Drain and use as you would canned beans.
To serve black beans as a side dish:
Add 1 pound black beans, onion, and to a slow cooker. Cover and cook on high for 4 hours. Drain excess water and add canned tomatoes. Add salt and pepper to taste. Stir and serve.

Submitter: 
SST
Description: 
1 pound of dried beans equals approximately 4 cans. 

Canned beans may be purchased for refried beans or if you only need a very small quantity. 

Serving size in recipe is for black beans as a side dish.
