Title:
Peppermint Bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound white chocolate
1 cup finely crushed peppermint candies (like candy canes)
12 oz dark chocolate

Directions:
Cover a 16"x11" sided cookie sheet with wax paper.
Finely crush the peppermint candies with a blender or a food processor.
Melt the white chocolate, in a saucepan, over low heat until smooth
Add the crushed peppermint candies and blend well.
Pour the chocolate peppermint in the pan that is covered with wax paper.  Spread it out evenly over the pan. Place it in refrigerator to set up.
After the chocolate has set up, melt the dark chocolate, in a saucepan, over low heat, until smooth.
Spread the dark chocolate, evenly, over the white chocolate/peppermint mix.  Place in refrigerator to set up.
After the candy has hardened, remove from pan and wax paper and break into pieces.  Store candy in an airtight container in a cool place.

Submitter:

Description: 
The two kinds of chocolate in this peppermint bark make it much more balanced and nice to look at.  This is one of my favorite candies to make because it takes just minutes to make.  Wonderful for the Christmas season.
