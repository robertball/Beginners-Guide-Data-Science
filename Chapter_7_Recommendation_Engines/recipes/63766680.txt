Title:
Big Ray's Rustic Tuscan Soup with Greens Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1044201.jpg
Ingredients:
1 loaf  Italian bread (18 inch), cut into 1-inch pieces
1 Tbsp olive oil
1/2 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese, divided
1 tsp crushed red pepper
1/4 tsp nutmeg
8 slices bacon, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
1 large onion, chopped
5 leaves fresh basil, coarsely chopped
2 cans (15 oz. each) cannellini beans, rinsed
2 cans (14.5 oz. each)diced tomatoes, undrained
2 cans (14-1/2 oz. each) chicken broth
6 cups loosely packed chopped greens (kale or swiss chard)

Directions:
Heat oven to 400ÂºF.
Spread bread onto baking sheet; drizzle with oil. Sprinkle with 1 Tbsp cheese and crushed pepper. Bake 10 min.
Cook and stir bacon in large saucepan on medium heat until crisp. Remove bacon from pan with slotted spoon; drain on paper towels. Discard all but 1 Tbsp bacon drippings from pan. Add onions to pan; cook and stir 5 min. or until crisp-tender. Return bacon to pan along with the beans, tomatoes, broth, basil,nutmeg and 1/4 cup of the remaining cheese; stir. Simmer on medium-low heat 20 min., stirring occasionally. Add kale; cook 10 min.
Serve soup topped with croutons and remaining cheese.
