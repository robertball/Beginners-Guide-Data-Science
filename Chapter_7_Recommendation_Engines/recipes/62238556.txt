Title:
apple raspberry pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Pastry for 9" double crust
4 C Peeled, sliced apples (Granny Smith-about 6 medium sized)
10 oz Frozen raspberries, thawed and drained
1 C sugar
3 TBLS flour
1/2 tsp. cinnamon (optional)
1 TBLS butter

Directions:
In large bowl, toss apples, raspberries, sugar, flour and cinnamon. Place one pie crust in pie pan (overhang one inch). Mound filling into shell. Dot with bits of butter. Place top crust over filling. Fold top and bottom edges over and press with the handle of a knife (or something like it) to flute. Sprinkle sugar on the top. Bake at 400 degrees for about 45 minutes. If edges brown too quickly, cover with a strip of foil.
