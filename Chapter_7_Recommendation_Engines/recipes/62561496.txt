Title:
Larra's Chicken Chili Verde Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/536783.jpg
Ingredients:
1 T olive oil
1 onion, chopped
2 jalapenos, chopped
1 clove garlic, chopped or crushed
1 yellow pepper, chopped
1 can great north white beans, drained
1 can pinto beans, drained
1 can chicken broth (2 cups)
4.5 oz can chopped green chilis
16 oz jar Ortega salsa verde (medium hotness)
½ tsp black pepper
½ tsp salt
1 tsp dried oregano
½ tsp cumin
½ of a chopped granny smith apple
2 ¼ lb boneless chicken breasts
1 ½ T corn starch
1 cup light sour cream (plus garnish)
1/2 bunch chopped fresh cilantro
Garnishes:  pico de gallo, shredded Colby-jack cheese, sour cream

Directions:
Sauté onion, jalapeno, garlic and yellow pepper in olive oil and add to crock pot along with beans, broth, chilis, salsa verde, spices and apple.  Lay chicken breasts across top.  Cover and cook on low for 7 hours. No mixing needed!
Remove chicken breasts and shred them using two forks.  Mix some of the chili broth into the cornstarch and add it back into the crock pot mixture and raise temerature to high.  Add shredded chicken back into the crock pot, stir and cook another 1.5 hours on high. (or as long as needed until your guests are ready to eat)
Add sour cream, stir and cook on low for final ½ hour (or as long as needed until your guests are ready to eat).
To garnish and serve, put into bowls and sprinkle top with chopped fresh cilantro, shredded Colby-jack cheese, a dollop of sour cream and small scoop of pico de gallo.

Submitter: 
LVD
Description: 
A great alternative to regular chili and super-easy to make in the crock pot.  You would never guess how healthy it is by tasting it, either.
