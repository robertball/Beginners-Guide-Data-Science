Title:
Lemon Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package yellow or white cake mix
4 eggs
3/4 cup vegetable oil
3/4 cup water
1 package lemon jello
TOPPING
2 cups powdered sugar
2 lemons, juice and graed rind

Directions:
Beat all ingredients with mixer for 4 minutes.  Bake in 9x13 pan at 350 degrees.  When baked remove from oven;  punch many holes in cake with long tined fork and pour topping over while warm.
TOPPING:
Mix powdered sugar and lemon juice and rind.

Submitter: 
Karen Monson
Description: 
Lemon cake is one of my favorite desserts.  So fresh tasting.
