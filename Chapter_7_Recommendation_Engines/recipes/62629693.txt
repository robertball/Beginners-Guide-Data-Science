Title:
Mash Potato with Sautéed Mushrooms & Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/576398.jpg
Ingredients:
2 pounds small to medium potatoes, peeled, cut in half
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup milk
salt & pepper to taste
1 cup mushrooms, roughly chopped (any variety)
1/2 carrot, finely chopped

Directions:
Boil potatoes until fork tender. Drain & mash, season with salt & pepper. Add the milk, mash to blend. Melt the butter on a non-stick pan. Add mushrooms & carrot, sauté until soft, stirring.
Stir mushrooms & carrot into potatoes. Place in serving bowl & sprinkle with fresh Parmesan cheese, chopped spring onion & red chillies (seeded). Enjoy!
