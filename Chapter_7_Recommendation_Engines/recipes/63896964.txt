Title:
Big Ray's Spiced Carrot Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1081410.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Tbsp olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
2 lbs. carrots, peeled and diced
1 tsp curry
1 quart chicken stock
6 tbsp sour cream
6 Tbsp cream cheese
salt
pepper
cinnimon

Directions:
Heat a large stock pot over medium heat, add olive oil.  Add onions and carrots and cook until browned, about 10 minutes.  Add curry and stock and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and let simmer until carrots are tender, about 15 minutes.
Puree in a blender until smooth, work in batches if necessary.  Transfer back into stock pot.  Add cream cheese and stir until melted.  Add sour cream a little at a time until consistency you want is achieved.  Season with salt, pepper and cinnimon.
