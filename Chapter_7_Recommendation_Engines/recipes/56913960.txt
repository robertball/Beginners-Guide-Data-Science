Title:
Homemade Sweet Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup distilled white vinegar
1 tablespoon salt
2 cups white sugar
6 cups sliced cucumbers
1 cup sliced onions
1 cup sliced green bell peppers
1 tbls mustard seed and celery seed
1tbls pickling spice
1 1/2 tsp pickle crisp

Directions:
In a medium saucepan over medium heat, bring vinegar, salt and sugar to a boil. Boil until the sugar has dissolved, about 10 minutes.
Place the cucumbers, onions and green bell peppers in a large bowl. Pour the vinegar mixture over the vegetables. Transfer to sterile containers.
Process in presser canner 20 mins at 10 lbs of pressure.

Submitter: 
Linda Peterson
Description: 
Just pickles!!! These are easy to make, and are a great way to use vegetables from the garden.Modified from: Homemade Refrigerator Pickles
