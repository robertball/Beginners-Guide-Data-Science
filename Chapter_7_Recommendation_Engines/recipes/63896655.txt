Title:
Slow Cooker Chuckeye Pot Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 pound chuck eye roast
1 medium onion, chopped
1 teaspoon seasoned salt
1 teaspoon pepper
3/4 cup water

Directions:
Place roast in bottom of cooker.
Add remaining ingredients.
Cook on low for 7 - 9 hours.

Description: 
Quick to prepare and the juices make a delicious gravy.
