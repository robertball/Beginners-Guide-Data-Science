Title:
Christmas- cut outs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1C butter
1C sugar
2 egg yolks
1tsp vanilla
1/2tsp salt
3C flour
1tsp baking powder
1/3C milk

Directions:
Cream butter, add sugar gradually, mix in eggs and vanilla. Blend in dry ingredents and milk. Chill till set; roll out some dough to 1/8in thick. Cut with cutters. Bake on ungreased sheet at 350 for 8-10 min., cool and frost.
