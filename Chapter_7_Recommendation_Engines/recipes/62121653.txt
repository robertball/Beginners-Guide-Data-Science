Title:
Pork Loin Roast w/ veggies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For Marinade:
-	2 cups Italian salad dressing (wishbone is good)
-	1 Tbsp granulated garlic
-	2 tsp onion powder
-	½ tsp sea salt
-	1 tsp balsamic vinegar
For the Roast:
-	1 (3-4 lb) Boneless Pork Loin Roast
-	4 Tbsp brown sugar
-	1 can pineapple chunks, drained
-	3 large carrots peeled and sliced ½ in.

Directions:
Directions
Step 1- Combine marinade ingredients in a bowl large enough for the pork roast. Mix well and add the roast. Cover, refrigerate and marinate overnight.
Remove roast from the fridge 1 hour before cooking.
Step 2- Pre Heat oven to 350F. Grease the shallow roasting pan. Remove roast from marinade. Discard marinade. Place the roast in the roasting pan and surround it with the pineapple chunks. Spoon a little pineapple juice and sprinkle 2 Tbsp of brown sugar over the roast. Sprinkle the remaining brown sugar evenly over the pineapple.
Step 3- Place in oven and roast for 1 hour. Add carrots and roast for another 20 minutes before slicing.
