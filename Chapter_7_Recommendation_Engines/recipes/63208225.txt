Title:
Slow Cooker Mexican Shredded Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 envelope taco seasoning
16 oz. jar of salsa (choose mild, medium, or hot according to your tastes)
1 can cream of mushroom soup
6 chicken breasts or 12 tenderloins

Directions:
Dump all ingredients into a slow cooker.  Stir to combine ingredients.  Cook high for 4-6 hours or low for 6-8 or until fork tender.  Take two forks and shred chicken by pulling the forks in opposite directions.  Turn off heat and let chicken sit in juices for 30 minutes off heat.

Submitter: 
Claudia
Description: 
Four ingredients make the most wonderful shredded chicken filling for tacos, burritos, enchiladas or taco soup.
