Title:
Hash Brown Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1068319.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (2 pound) package frozen hash brown potatoes, thawed (O'Brian)
1 (24 ounce) container sour cream
1 cup onion, chopped
1 (10.75 ounce) can cream of chicken soup
6 cups Colby cheese, shredded
1 cup butter
salt and ground black pepper to taste
6 cups crushed corn flakes cereal

Directions:
Melt butter and set aside.  Mix hash brown potatoes, sour cream, onion, cream of chicken soup, Colby cheese, and 1/2 cup of melted butter in a bowl; season to taste with salt and black pepper. Transfer mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish.  Add remaining butter to corn flake crumbs mixing well.  Sprinkle top of potatoe with corn flake crumbs.
Bake at 375Â° until the casserole is bubbling, about 1 hour.
