Title:
Red Pepper Pesto Appetizer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
One package refrigerated crescent roll dough
8 oz package reduced fat cream cheese, softened
2 roasted red peppers from a jar
1/2 cup prepared basil pesto sauce

Directions:
Roll out the dough and press seams together to form one big rectangle of dough.
Dice the peppers and mix together with cream cheese and pesto.
Pour the cream cheese mixture onto the rectangle of dough and fold up the edges to form a big pocket.
Bake in Preheated 375 degree oven for 15-20 minutes or until top of dough is browned.
Serve with butter crackers.

Submitter: 
bakeaholic
Description: 
Cream cheese and store bought crescent roll dough make this an easy and satisfying hot appetizer for any occasion.
