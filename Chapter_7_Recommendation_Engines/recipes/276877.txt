Title:
Pumpkin Yogurt Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7268043.jpg
Ingredients:
8 ounces pumpkin puree (such as Libby's®)
4 ounces nonfat plain yogurt (such as Kroger's ®)
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
12 raisins
1/2 (1 gram) packet granular sucrolose sweetener (such as Splenda®), or to taste

Directions:
Combine pumpkin puree, yogurt, cinnamon, raisins, and sweetener in a bowl; mix to combine.
