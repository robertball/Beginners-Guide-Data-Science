Title:
Amazing Slow Cooker French Toast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf cinnamon raisin bread
6 eggs
1 cup milk or heavy whipping cream
1 tsp vanilla  (or to taste)
1 tsp allspice (or to taste)
1/2 cup raisins (optional)
1 apple (any kind)
1/3 cup brown sugar
2/3 stick butter sliced thin

Directions:
Chop the apple into small chunks. Place into greased slow cooker.
Sprinkle brown sugar on top of apples.
Place sliced butter on top of the apples and brown sugar.
Whisk the eggs, milk, vanilla, and allspice.
Add raisins.
Tear the bread into medium sized chunks and place in egg mixture.
Stir to make sure all the bread is saturated.
Pour the bread mixture on top of the apples and brown sugar.
Cook on high for 2 hours (or more depending on your slow cooker).

Submitter:

Description: 
I came up with this recipe when I was looking for a recipe for french toast that I could make in the slow cooker.  Some people will say this is more of a bread pudding, but say what you will.  This is DELICIOUS!
