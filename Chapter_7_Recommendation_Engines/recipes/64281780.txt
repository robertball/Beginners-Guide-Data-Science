Title:
Roasted Tomato Soup with Dill Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (28oz) cans diced tomatoes, drained
4 Medium carrots, peeled and thinly sliced
1 Large Onion, diced
2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
1 tsp sugar
1 Tbsp unsalted butter
1 Tbsp minced garlic
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
2 Tbsp fino sherry (dry sherry)
1 (28oz) can crushed tomatoes
2 cups reduced sodium chicken broth
1/4 cup plain low fat Greek yogurt
1/4 cup chopped fresh dill
1/4 cup chopped fresh chives

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 F.  Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and coat with nonstick spray
In a large bowl, toss together tomatoes, carrots, onion, salt, pepper and sugar; spread on prepared baking sheet and roast for 30 minutes.
In a large nonstick soup pot, heat butter over medium heat.  Add garlic and red pepper flakes; cook, stirring about 1 minute.  Add sherry; stir and cook for 1 minute more.  Add roasted vegetables, crushed tomatoes and broth, and bring to boil over high heat.  Reduce heat to low and simmer, uncovered, to allow flavors to blend, about 10 minutes.  Let cool slightly and then add the yogurt.
Puree soup in pot using and immersion blender or a regular blender in batches.   Stir in dill;  serve garnished with chives.
