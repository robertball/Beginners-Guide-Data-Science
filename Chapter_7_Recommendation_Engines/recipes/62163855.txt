Title:
Continental Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c sugar
2 1/2 c flour
1 1/2 t baking soda
1/2 t salt
1/2 c buttermilk
2 eggs
1/2 c butter
1/2 c shortning
1/3 c cocoa
1 c hot coffee, brewed strong
FROSTING
1/2 c butter
2 T cocoa powder (dark if you can find it)
1/4 c milk
3 1/2 c powdered sugar
1 t vanilla

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350*
In a bowl combine 2 c sugar, flour, baking soda and salt.
In a microwave proof bowl combine 1/2 c butter, shortning, 1/3 c cocoa and coffee. Microwave 3 min stirring at every min. or until combined.
Mix eggs and buttermilk together, combine all ingredients, and pour into a 17 1/2 X 11" greased jelly roll pan. Bake at 350* for 20 min. While they are baking prepare frosting.
In a saucepan, combine butter, cocoa and milk. Heat to boiling, stirring. Mix in the powdered sugar and vanilla until smooth. Pour warm frosting over hot brownies. Cool and cut into bars.

Submitter: 
Zhora Autumn
Description: 
The lunch ladies at Continental Highschool homemade these great brownies for us in the 80's and 90's. They are super moist.
