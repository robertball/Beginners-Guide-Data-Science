Title:
Lasagna Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground chuck
1 onion, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
3 cloves minced garlic
1 teaspoon thyme
1 tablespoon firmly packed brown sugar
1 32oz box of chicken broth
2 cans petite diced tomatoes (14.5 oz)
1 can tomato sauce (15 oz)
2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
1 teaspoon salt
2 cups of pasta I use Ditalini or break up lasagna noodles
1 package grated parmesan cheese (5 oz)

Directions:
Brown meat, onion and pepper together. Then add remaining ingredients, except noodles and cheese. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 30 mins.  Add noodles. Cook until noodles are tender, then add cheese.
