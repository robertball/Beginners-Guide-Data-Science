Title:
Texas Chicken Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 lg onion, chopped
1 T. garlic, minced
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp. black pepper
1 tsp crushed red pepper
1/8-1/4 tsp Cayanne pepper
4 cans black beans
1 8 oz. can tomato sauce
1 15 oz. can stewed tomatoes
1 6 oz can tomatoe paste
4 T. butter
2 lb. cooked chicken meat

Directions:
In a large pot, cook onion, red and green peppers in butter until soft.  Add garlic, salt, black pepper, cumin, red crushed pepper, and cayenne (to taste depending on how hot you like it).  Stir, then add chicken, black beans, tomatoes, tomato sauce and paste.  Cook until heated through. The longer it cooks the better.
NOTE:  To save time, buy a pre-roasted chicken at the supermarket and remove bones and skin and chop meat.

Submitter: 
Debi Blair McGinness
Description: 
This is a mainstay at our work potlucks so I started making it at home.  It's very thick and makes a good meal.  My husband loves it.  It freezes well too.
