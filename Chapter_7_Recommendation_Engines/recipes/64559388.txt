Title:
Fast Food Coleslaw dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Mayonnaise
1/3 cup granulated sugar
1/4 cup milk
1/4 cup buttermilk
2 1/2 Tablespoons lemon juice
1 1/2 Tablespoons white vinegar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
2 Tablespoons minced onion
8 cups shredded cabbage and
1/4 cup shredded carrot OR
14 oz pre made coleslaw mix

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, combine the mayonnaise, sugar, milk, buttermilk, lemon juice, vinegar salt and pepper.
Add minced onion and cabbage and carrots or coleslaw mix, if using and mix well.
Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.

Submitter: 
Mark Adams
Description: 
Hybrid of KFC and Long John Silver coleslaw dressing.
