Title:
Beer Battered Friend Pickles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (16oz.) jars of dill pickle sandwich slices drained
1 large egg
1 (12oz.) can beer
1 TBS. baking powder
1 tsp. seasoned salt
1-1/2 cups all purpose flour
veggie oil
Spicy Ranch Dipping Sauce:
3/4 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup mayo
2 TBS. minced green onions
1 garlic clove minced
1 tsp. hot sauce
1/2 tsp. seasoned salt

Directions:
Pat pickles dry with paper towels.
Whisk together egg and next three ingredients in a large bowl; add flour, and whisk until smooth.
Pour oil into a depth of 1-1/2
