Title:
Easy Key Lime Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag key limes (3/4 C juiced) key lime juice in a jar is not the same
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1/2 C water
1 package gelatin
2 eggs
1 gram cracker crust

Directions:
Juice key limes, should end up with about 3/4 C lime juice.
Reserve one lime for garnish.
Slowly mix water into gelatin.
Lightly stir eggs in separate container.
Combine all ingredients into a sauce pan and bring to boil.
Simmer for about 3 min.
Poor into gram cracker crust and freeze.  Our freezer takes about 3 hours others may take longer.
Slice and garnish with whipped cream and very thin slices of a key lime.

Submitter: 
Jeramyn Feucht
Description: 
You can't beat fresh squeezed Key Limes to make a fresh key lime pie
