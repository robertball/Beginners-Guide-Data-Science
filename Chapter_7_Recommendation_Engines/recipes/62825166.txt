Title:
Ingard’s Creamy Chicken Enchilada’s Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
•	4 oz can diced green
•	1 boneless chicken breast
•	10 oz can Green Enchilada sauce
•	½ cup sour cream
•	4 oz of cream cheese
•	1 cup shredded Mexican cheese
•	Can of cream of chicken or cream of mushroom soup - optional

Directions:
Boil chicken in enchilada sauce. When the Chicken has completed cooking and is tender, Shred chicken and mix with sour cream, cream cheese and diced green chilies, keep in kettle until sour cream and cream cheese has melted.   Warm Tortillas and dip in green enchilada sauce that the chicken cooked in.  Fill corn tortillas with enchiladas with mix. Mix one can of soup with ½ can of water and cover enchiladas Spread cheese on top of enchiladas and cook 10-15 in oven at 400 degrees
