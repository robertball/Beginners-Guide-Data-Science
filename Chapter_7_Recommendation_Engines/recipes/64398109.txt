Title:
Green Chile Crockpot Shredded Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
6 Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs
1 Taco Seasoning Packet
1 tub/jar Roasted Green Chile Salsa (regular is fine too)
1 can Mild Chopped Green Chiles
1 minced, seeded Serrano or Jalapeno Chile
1 Lite Beer
pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Season both sides of chicken with Taco Seasoning Packet, place in crockpot.
2. Add Green Chile Salsa, Green chiles, Serrano/Jalapeno, Beer and pepper, give a gentle stir.
3. Cover and cook on high 3.5-4 hours, shred with a fork.
Optional: Strain excess liquid

Submitter: 
Lizette
Description: 
Such a EASY and simple recipe that is full of flavor! Feel free to add 1/4 tsp Oregano, Garlic powder or even an extra chile pepper for added zip! So many ideas for leftovers and eases up meal planning for your week!
