Title:
Lmon Baked Orange Roghy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons lemon juice
1 Tablespoon Dijon mustard
1 Tablespoon butter (melted)
1/4 Teaspoon fresh coarse ground pepper
4 orange rought filets (4 oz each)
lemon wedge

Directions:
1.  Preheat oven to 350 dgrees
2.  Stir the first four ingredients until well blended.
3.  Place the fish fillets in a shallow baking dsh coated with cokking spray.
4.  Brush fillets with lemon juice mixture
5.  Bake in oven for 25 minutes.
6.  Spoon any remaining juice over the fish and serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
Delightful light fish recipe.  Can use flounder, cod or any similr light white fish.
