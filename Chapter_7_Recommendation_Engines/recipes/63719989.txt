Title:
Taco Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz bag of coleslaw mix or shredded cabbage
1-2 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
1/2 large red onion, minced
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
2 limes, juiced
1 Tbsp white vinegar
1 Tbsp sugar

Directions:
In a bowl, mix together the jalapeno peppers, red onion, cilantro, lime juice, vinegar and sugar. Add the cabbage and toss together. You can serve immediately but is better refrigerated over night.

Submitter: 
Nicole Berger
Description: 
Many of the street vendors in Central America served this with their grilled meats. Seems to be popular with our local food trucks and fancy taco diners.
