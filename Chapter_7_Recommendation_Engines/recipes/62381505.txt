Title:
Proscuitto Egg Cups Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/418451.jpg
Ingredients:
4 large egg, whites only
2 large whole eggs
1 sliced thick cut prosciutto, cubed
1/4 cup gouda cheese, shredded
1/8 cup carmelized onions

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Spray a 6 muffin, muffin tin with your choice of cooking spray.
Mix egg whites and whole eggs in a large measuring cup. Pour into sprayed muffin tin half way full.
Add proscuitto, gouda and onions to each tin. Fill with remaining eggs leaving about a 1/4 space from the top for eggs to rise.
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Barb
Description: 
Use a standard muffin pan to make easy egg cups, like a quiche. These are easy to make, you can change the ingredients to whatever you like. Make them ahead and freeze or refrigerate and reheat in the microwave.
