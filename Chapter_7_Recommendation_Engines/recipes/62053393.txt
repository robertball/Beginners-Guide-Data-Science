Title:
New Orleans Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds chicken skinned, and deboned,
1/2 tsp crushed thyme,
1 TBSP olive oil,
14 ounces cajun style stewed tomatoes,
1 green bell pepper, cut into strips,
1 stalk celery, sliced,
1 onion, chopped,
2 cloves garlic, minced,
salt and pepper to taste.

Directions:
Sprinkle chicken with thyme, slat and pepper.  Brown in oil 15 minutes; drain.  Stir in remaining ingredients.  Cover and simmer 15 minutes.  Uncover and cook over medium high heat until thickened and chicken is tender.

Submitter: 
Rhonda Brock Fuller
Description: 
Not your every day chicken but very tasty.
