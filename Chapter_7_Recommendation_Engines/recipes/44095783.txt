Title:
Venison Mini Meatloaves Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg
3/4 cup milk
1 cup shredded colby jack cheese
1/2 cup quick cooking oatmeal
1 tsp salt
1 lb ground venison
2/3 cup ketchup
1/2 c packed brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp prepared mustard

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
In a large bowl, combine, egg, milk, cheese, oats and salt. Allow to sit about 5 minutes for the oats to absorb the liquids.
Add ground venison and mix well.
Form 6 mini loaves, place in mini loaf pans or  8x8" square pan.
In a small bowl, combine ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard. Mix thoroughly and spread evenly over the mini loaves.
Bake uncovered for 45 minutes.
