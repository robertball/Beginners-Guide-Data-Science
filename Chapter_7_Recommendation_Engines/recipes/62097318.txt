Title:
One pan fall dinner Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. smoked kielbasa
1 lb. new potatoes
1 lb. fresh green beans

Directions:
Place kielbasa in a frying pan with a lid, cut potatoes in to half to quarters depending on size and place in center of kielbasa. Plce green beans on top of potatoes. Cover and let cook for about 15 to 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Debbie Lopez
Description: 
The juices from the kielbasa gives the potatoes and green beans a flover everyone loves. Frozen green beans can be substituted for fresh if need be but fresh are best.
