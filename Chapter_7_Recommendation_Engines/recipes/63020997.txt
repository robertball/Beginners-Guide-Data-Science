Title:
Italian Spaghetti Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/756508.jpg
Ingredients:
1 halved spaghetti squash (remove strings and seeds)
1/2 cup chopped pine nuts
3/4 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
1/2 cup diced green pepper
1 chopped onion
2 cloves chopped garlic
fresh oregano
fresh sage
fresh parsley
fresh thyme
pepper
2 T olive oil
1 can diced tomatoes
1 can tomato sauce
1 8 oz bag of mozzarella

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Place squash halves face down on greased cookie sheet. Place a pan of water in the oven to keep the squash moist. Bake for 40-60 minutes. Or, until tender. Check with fork.
Over medium heat combine all remaining ingredients except the tomatoes, tomato sauce, and cheese. Fry on stovetop for 5-10 minutes. Add diced tomatoes, turn down heat to medium low. Cook 5-10 minutes. Add tomato sauce, bring to boil, and let simmer.
When squash has cooled, scoop out pulp and place in the bottom of a 9x9 inch casserole. Cover lightly with cheese. Cover with tomato mixture. Cover with cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
This recipe tastes a lot like lasagne without the high-calorie pasta and meat.
