Title:
Easy Pumpkin Dip with Cream Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3/4 (15 ounce) can ready-to-use pumpkin pie filling, or to taste
1 (8 ounce) package whipped cream cheese
1 (8 ounce) container whipped cream
1 (3.4 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix

Directions:
Combine pumpkin pie filling, whipped cream cheese, whipped cream, and vanilla pudding in a large bowl. Mix thoroughly.

Submitter: 
aprilkay
Description: 
Creamy dip is great with vanilla wafers, bear-shaped grahams cracker snacks, or fruit. Great for Halloween or Thanksgiving.
