Title:
Bread Dumplings      " Semmel Knoedel" Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
9 oz of Day old white "Toast"  Bread   ( not Toasted ).  The Dryer the Better;  but not stale.  ( best is, it cuts but does not Crack).
3 eggs
5 oz  Milk
1 oz of Chopped fresh Parsley
Salt, Pepper,  Muscat

Directions:
Cube the Bread approximately  "1 cm" , 1/2 inch cubes.  (This is so not to over mix the mass and result in a Tender and loose Dumpling).  Heat the Milk to near Boiling.  Scramble the Eggs.  Chop the Parsley and add with Salt and Pepper to the Bread. ;  Next  Quickly Add the Eggs and Milk to a "well" in the Bread part and begin mixing carefully not to burn your-self,  but do not over mix.  once you see a Homogeneous Mass, (adjust with Breadcrumbs or Water;  it should resemble a loose Stuffing).  stop mixing, Pat lightly down,  Cover , and Refrigerate at least 2 Hours.  Prepare a pot of Salted water and Bring to simmer.  Using wet hands, form about
3 -4 oz round Dumplings,  smooth the outside but do not over press.  Suspend them in The Water and Simmer occasional light Boil.  until done.   (*Serve with Sauerkraut,  And with Roast Pork.  also;  very good with Mushroom and Sour Lung Ragout, or just Plain;  in Soup Stock with fresh Green Herbs).
