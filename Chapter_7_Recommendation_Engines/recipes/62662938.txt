Title:
Daddy's Crock Pot Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large carrots (roughly chopped)
4 russet potatoes (roughly chopped)
1/2 large onion (roughly chopped)
1 garlic head (separated into cloves and peeled)
1 1/2 Lbs. stew meat
1 cup beef stock
1 can tomato sauce (no salt added)Salt
Pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl or in crock pot, combine carrots, potatoes, onion, garlic and meat.
Move to crock pot if necessary.
Pour tomato sauce over mixture.
Pour beef stock over mixture.
Add salt and pepper to taste.
Stir lighly to allow sauce to penetrate.
Cover and cook on low for 3 to 4 hours.
Eat with toasted bread or over rice.
