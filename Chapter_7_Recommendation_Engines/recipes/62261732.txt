Title:
Candied lemon peels Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
water
lemon peels - the rinds
sugar

Directions:
boil rinds about 15 min empty water refill x 3 then remove peels set aside then add 2 cups sugar 1 cup water until dissolved add peels bring to a boil simmer on low until peels are tender and transluent drain and cool toss in sugar

Description: 
delicious in tea or just as a sweet snack
