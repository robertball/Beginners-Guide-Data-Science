Title:
Jill's Garden Fresh Tomato Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-10 cups garden fresh tomatoes, diced
1 large onion, diced
4-6 cloves garlic
1 jalapeno pepper, diced
3 cubes pesto sauce (see recipe)
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp butter
8-10 fresh basil leaves
8-10 fresh thyme sprigs, de-branched (about 1 tbsp)
8-10 fresh oregano sprigs. de-branched (about 1 tbsp)
3  fresh bay leaves
2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
2 tbsp brown sugar
1 7oz can tomato paste

Directions:
Salt and pepper onions and saute in heated olive oil and butter until translucent
Add garlic and continue stirring for 1 minute
Add jalapeno and continue stirring for 1 minute
Add tomatoes and continue stirring for 3 minutes
Add all fresh herbs and continue stirring from 5 more minutes
Add remaining ingredients, bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer covered for at least 30 minutes
