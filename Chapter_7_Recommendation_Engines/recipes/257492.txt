Title:
Happy Skinny Green Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4499401.jpg
Ingredients:
2 green tea bags
1 1/2 cups boiling water
2 packets Wholesome Organic Stevia
2 cups loosely packed organic baby spinach
1 cup fresh or frozen mango chunks
1/2 green apple, chopped
1/2 avocado, peeled and pitted
1/2 cup green seedless grapes
1/4 cup loosely packed fresh mint leaves
1 (1/2- by 1/4-inch) piece fresh ginger, chopped
1/2 lime, juiced

Directions:
Steep the tea bags in boiling water for 3 to 5 minutes.
Discard tea bags and let the tea cool.
Put cooled tea and remaining ingredients in blender and process until smooth.
Drink immediately.

Submitter: 
Wholesome
Description: 
Organic stevia sweetens this delicious combination of baby spinach, mango, grapes and fresh mint for a satisfying skinny smoothie.
