Title:
Pulled Pork BBQ Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/487857.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Pork Loin Roasts
2 c Water
Salt & Pepper
1 recipe for my homemade BBQ Sauce

Directions:
ADD pork, water salt and pepper to a pressure cooker.
COOK on medium for 45 minutes.
PULL apart pork into shreds.
ADD homemade BBQ Sauce and stir.
COOK an additional 15 minutes on medium.

Submitter: 
Jody
Description: 
Shredded pork cooked in a pressure cooker, seasoned with my homemade BBQ sauce.
