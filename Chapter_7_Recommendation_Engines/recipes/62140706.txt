Title:
moist pumpkin raisin cake with cream cheese frosting. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 1/4 cup sugar
1 1/2 cups can pumpkin
1 1/4 cups veg oil
1 1/2 cups unbleached flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
1 teasp salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 1/4 cup raisins
CREAM CHEESE FROSTING
8 ozs cream cheese
1/3 pound soft butter
3 cups powder sugar

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees grease and flour a large bundt pan
mix eggs,sugar ,pumpkin and oil on medium speed 2 mins .
on low speed add flour,baking powder ,cinnamon,salt,baking soda till smooth add raisins .
pour into pan bake 45-50 mins.
prepare frosting, blend cream cheese and butter on medium speed with mixer, gradually add powder sugar till smooth and spreadable
allow cake to cool 15 mins ,spread wit frosting
store room temp.
