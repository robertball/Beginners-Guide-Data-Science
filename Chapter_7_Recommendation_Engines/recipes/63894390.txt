Title:
Kishkehs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 med-small carrots, minced in food processor
2 med-small onions, minced in food processor
1/2 bulb fennel, minced in food processor
1 c panko crumbs, gluten-free
1/2 c white rice flour
1/4 c schmaltz
1/8 vegetable oil
1 egg
1 t salt
1 t pepper
1 T paprika

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
Mince vegetables in food processor.
Mix all ingredients in medium bowl.
Shape a log from mixture about 12" long and 2" diameter on a piece of foil. Roll and crimp foil to retain juices.
Bake at 350F 2 hours or until set, turning 1/4 turn every 20-30 min.
Let sit 15 min before slicing. Best served with stew gravy.

Submitter: 
meghanmacrae
Description: 
Traditionally made in intestines and boiled, this is simply a vegetable sausage. The small amount of fennel gives a fresh flavor without becoming licoricey.
