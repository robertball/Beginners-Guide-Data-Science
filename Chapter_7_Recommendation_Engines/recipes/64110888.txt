Title:
Pop-Overs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1 cup flour
1T. melted shortening

Directions:
Beat eggs, add milk, then sifted dry ingredients. Add shortening. Beat smooth. Pour batter into hot, well greased cupcake pan. Fill cups 1/3 full. Bake at 425 for 15 minutes then reduce oven to 350 and continue baking 30-35 minutes. Two or three minutes before removing from oven, prick to let steam escape.
