Title:
Garlic Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
INGREDIENTS
4 boneless chicken breasts
1/2 tsp of salt
1/2 tsp fresh ground black pepper
1 tsp of dried oregano
1 tsp of dried basil
3 cloves of garlic, chopped
1 Tb butter
1 Tb vegetable oil
1 Tb of all purpose flour
1/2 cup of chicken broth
1 cup of heavy whipping cream
2 oz of cream cheese
1/2 cup of fresh grated Parmesan

Directions:
PREPARATION
Season the chicken with the salt, pepper, oregano and basil.
Grill or broil the chicken for 5 to 6 minutes on each side.
Place the chicken on a serving platter and keep warm.
In a medium size saucepan, saut√© the garlic in the butter and oil for 2 minutes.
Stir in the flour and cook for 1 minute more.
Stir in the chicken broth and cream and cook until thickened.
Stir in the cream cheese and Parmesan until melted.
Spoon over chicken breasts and serve the remaining sauce on the side.

Submitter: 
Carolyn Sheppard
Description: 
An easy and very tasty way to perk up your mid week menu. Very creamy and a sure win for garlic lovers. Goes great over grilled or baked chicken breasts.
