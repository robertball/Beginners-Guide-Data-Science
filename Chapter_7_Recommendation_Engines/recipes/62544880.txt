Title:
Classic Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1	  pound lean ground beef
3/4	  cup Oatmeal (quick or old fashioned, uncooked)
3/4	  cup finely chopped onion
1/2	  cup catsup, plus more for topping.
1	  egg beaten
1	  tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
2	  cloves garlic, minced
1/2	  teaspoon salt
1/4	  teaspoon black pepper

Directions:
Heat oven to 350Â°F.
Combine all ingredients in large bowl; mix thoroughly.
Shape meatloaf mixture into a loaf and place into a baking pan.
Bake 50 to 55 minutes or until not pink in center and juices show no pink color.
Top with ketchup halfway through cooking.
Let stand 5 minutes before slicing.
