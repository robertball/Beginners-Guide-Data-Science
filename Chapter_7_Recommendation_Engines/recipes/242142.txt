Title:
Mango Shrimp Salad  Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/2279421.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon lime juice
2 teaspoons lime zest
1 teaspoon hot chile peppers (optional)
1/4 teaspoon white sugar
1 pound cooked shrimp
1 cup diced mango
1 cup diced cucumber
2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint
1 teaspoon rice vinegar, or to taste (optional)

Directions:
Whisk lime juice, lime zest, chile peppers, and sugar together in a bowl until dressing is smooth. Combine shrimp, mango, cucumber, and mint in a bowl; add dressing and toss to coat. Drizzle vinegar over salad.
