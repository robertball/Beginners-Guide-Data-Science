Title:
Granny Leona's Icebox Desert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Crust:
½ cup sugar
1 tablespoon flour
1 ½ teaspoons cinnamon
½ cup margarine melted
14 graham cracker squares finely crushed
Filling:
1 envelope whipped cream (Example: Dream Whip)
1 cup powdered sugar
1 large package cream cheese
1 can pie filling (Example: Blueberry, cherry, strawberry, etc.)

Directions:
Mix melted margarine, sugar, flour, cinnamon and crushed graham crackers together. Spread in the bottom of a 9X13 pan. Let set for 5-10 minutes.
Mix the envelope of whipped cream tipping, powdered sugar, and cream cheese together.
Pour over graham cracker mixture.
Top with pie filling.
Chill for a few hours.
