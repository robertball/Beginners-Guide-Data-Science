Title:
Chocolate Protein Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/386296.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1 1/2 banana (frozen or not)
1 spoonfull peanut butter
1 tbs. chocolate syrup (optional)

Directions:
1. Add milk
2. Add banana
3. Add peanut butter.
4.Mix on high for 2 minutes.

Description: 
As a good protien snack, this is so simple but so good at the same time!  A good energy booster too.
