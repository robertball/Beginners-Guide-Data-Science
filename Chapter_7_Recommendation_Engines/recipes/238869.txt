Title:
Ruby Red Slippers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5458364.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup ice cubes, or as needed
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers vodka
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers orange-flavored liqueur (such as Cointreau®)
2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers amaretto liqueur
1/2 lime, juiced
1 teaspoon white sugar
2 fluid ounces cranberry juice, or as needed
2 teaspoons raspberry-flavored liqueur (such as Chambord®)
2 fluid ounces champagne, or as needed

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice; add vodka, orange-flavored liqueur, amaretto liqueur, lime juice, and sugar. Cover shaker and shake. Strain drink into 2 martini glasses; add enough cranberry juice to fill glass 2/3-full. Pour 1 teaspoon raspberry-flavored liqueur into each drink and top with champagne.

Submitter: 
Dave Hinch
Description: 
After working on a quiet bar one night, I experimented with the available spirits. I came up with this delicious combo!

Just click your glasses, and say three times: 

There's no place like home!
