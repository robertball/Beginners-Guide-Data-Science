Title:
Sweet Cucumber Relish (Green) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 qts. sliced cucumbers
4 red peppers or canned pimentos
2 qts. apple cider vinegar (or use 1/2 white vinegar)
1/4 c. mustard seed
9 c. white sugar
12 med. sliced onions
1/2 c. canning salt
3 tsp. turmeric
4 tbsp. celery seed
Blue food coloring

Directions:
Sprinkle salt over cucumbers and onions. Let stand overnight. Chop in food processor. Drain thoroughly. (Add canned pimento only when ready to can).
Boil vinegar, mustard seed, sugar, turmeric, and celery seed with cucumber, onions, and peppers for 1 minute. Add small amount of BLUE food coloring for a bright green colored relish. (Omit chopped stage for bread and butter pickles).
Ladle into sterilized jars and seal as per  normal canning  instructions.

Submitter: 
SHASTASWEET
Description: 
green relish similar to the store bought brand.   Pickling spice can be substituted for the spices but remove the Bay Leaf in the mixture as the leaves do not soften.
