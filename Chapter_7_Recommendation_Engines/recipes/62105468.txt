Title:
Healthy Pasta Meal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb fresh kale
1/2 lg red onion
3 cloves crushed garlic
1.25 lbs Hot Italian Turkey Sausage
1 28oz can crushed tomatoes
1 15.5 oz can cannellini beans
1 cup veggie or chicken stock
1/4 tsp thyme
1/4 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp basil
1/4 lb multigrain or whole wheat spaghetti
Freshly grated parmesan cheese
Olive oil

Directions:
Clean kale,chop coarsely and remove large stem
Coarsely chop onion
Bring water to boil
Cook pasta al dente
Rinse cannellini beans
Cut sausages 1/4-1/2" pieces
Brown sausage in a large non stick skillet Remove from pan
Add small amount of olive oil to coat bottom of pan
Add onion and garlic and saute
Stir in crushed tomatoes and stock
Add herbs
Add sausage back to pan
Simmer all ingrediants for 15 minutes with lid on
Add the kale and cannellini beans
Continue to simmer with lid off for another 15 minutes to help thicken sauce
Stir occasionally
Do not to over cook the kale
Plate pasta and add sauce
Add desired amount of cheese

Description: 
I am trying to lose some weight again for the thousandth time in my life and I came up with this recipe because of the low fat,high fiber and fresh ingrediants. The beans,pasta and kale are an unusual mixture but don't hesitate to give it a try. My one and only raised eyebrows when discovered it was healthy for him. A definate keeper as he went back for seconds
