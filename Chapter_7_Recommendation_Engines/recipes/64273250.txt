Title:
Grandma's Chocolate Almond Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. Milk
18 large Marshmallows
2 Hershey with Almond bars (regular size)
1/2 pt. heavy cream

Directions:
Heat milk and melt marshmallows in it, then hershey bars and let cool.
When cooled whip the cream and fold into the chocolate mixture.
Pour inro a cooled graham cracker crust.
