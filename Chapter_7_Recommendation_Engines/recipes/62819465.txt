Title:
Zippy Ground Turkey Skillet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces uncooked penne pasta
1 pound lean ground turkey
3/4 cup diced onion
3/4 cup diced green pepper
1 (14.5ounce) can diced tomatoes, drained
1 (7.75 oz) El Pato jalapeno salsa (green can)
1 tablespoon honey
2 teaspoons chili powder
3/4 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 teaspoon cumin
1 cup reduced fat sour cream

Directions:
Cook pasta according to package directions.  Meanwhile, in a large nonstick skillet, cook the turkey, onion and green pepper over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain.  Add the tomatoes, jalapeno salsa, honey, chili powder. garlic powder, cumin and salt.  Reduce heat to low; cook, uncovered, for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally; mix in sour cream. Drain pasta; add to turkey mixture.  Heat through.
