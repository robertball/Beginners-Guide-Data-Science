Title:
No Bake Nutella Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2047830.jpg
Ingredients:
Crust:
4   Ounces   Butter
10  Ounces   Vanilla Wafers or Graham Crackers,     crushed
2   TBS Nutella
Filling:
18   Ounces      (2 1/2 boxes) Cream Cheese, room temperature
1    cup         Confectioners sugar
1    13 oz jar   Nutella
Heavy Whipping Cream, optional, for decoration

Directions:
Blend together crushed vanilla wafers, butter and 1-2 tbs nutella.  Press into 9 " springform pan.
In mixer, blend cream cheese, nutella and sugar.  Press into crumb crust.  Chill at least 4 hours.  Serve with whipped cream or Cool Whip.
