Title:
Vegan Bran Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups wheat bran
1 cup soy milk
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup applesauce
1/2 cup turbinado sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 350.
In one bowl, combine the bran and the soy milk and let stand until bran is soggy - about 10 minutes.
Meanwhile in another bowl, mix together the oil, applesauce, sugar and vanilla.
Mix the bran mush with the sugar mix and stir until combined.
Add the flour, baking powder and soda, and salt and stir.
Scoop a couple of spoonfuls of the batter into a muffin tin. Makes 12 muffins. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.

Description: 
I make these bran muffins to have on hand for a quick grab and go breakfast.
