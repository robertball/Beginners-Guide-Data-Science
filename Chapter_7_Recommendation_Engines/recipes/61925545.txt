Title:
Potato Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon butter
1 teaspoon cumin
1 teaspoon turmeric
1 teaspoon ground coriander
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon mustard powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
6 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
1 onion, coarsely chopped
1 clove garlic
2 cups water
1 cup (or more) frozen green peas and/or corn and/or cauliflower

Directions:
Heat the butter & oil in a skillet over medium heat, and mix in the cumin, turmeric, coriander, salt, mustard, and cayenne. Place potatoes and onion in the skillet, and stir to evenly coat. Cook 10 minutes, stirring often. Pour water into the skillet. Reduce heat to low, and simmer 30 minutes, until potatoes and onions are tender.
Mix in the peas and/or corn into the saucepan. Continue cooking until heated through. Add salt & pepper to taste.
