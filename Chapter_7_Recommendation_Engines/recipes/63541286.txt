Title:
Iced Flavored Latte Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cold water
? cup ground French roast or espresso roast coffee
? cup half-and-half or light cream
2 to 4 teaspoons sugar-free hazelnut-flavored syrup or 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
Ice cubes
Sugar substitute (optional)

Directions:
Using a drip coffeemaker, add water and ground coffee. Brew according to manufacturer's directions. Pour coffee into a glass measuring cup. Stir in half-and-half and, if desired, hazelnut-flavored syrup.
Fill 2 tall glasses with ice. Pour coffee mixture into glasses. If desired, sweeten to taste with sugar substitute.
