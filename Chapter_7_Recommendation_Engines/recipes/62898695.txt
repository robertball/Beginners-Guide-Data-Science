Title:
Death By Garlic Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons olive oil
4 tablespoons butter
10 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
1 pound dry penne pasta
1/3 cup grated Romano cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions
In a pan, brown garlic in oil and butter. Add salt, pepper and parsley and remove from heat.
Toss penne pasta with garlic mixture and Romano cheese and serve!

Submitter: 
lucylove
Description: 
Ten cloves of garlic lightly browned in olive oil and butter then tossed with parsley, red pepper flakes, hot penne pasta and freshly grated Romano cheese makes a heady and flavorful dish.Modified from: Death By Garlic
