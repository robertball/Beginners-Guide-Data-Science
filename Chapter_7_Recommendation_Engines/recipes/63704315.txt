Title:
Cinnamon Swirl Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
#1.   1/3 cup brown sugar
1T ground cinnamon
#2.   2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup white sugar(Splenda)
#3.   1/4c Egg Beaters
1/2c milk
1/2c plain yogurt
1/3 cup applesauce (unswt)
#4.   1c raisins
Glaze
3/4c powdered sugar
2T soft butter
1/2t vanilla
1/2t milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Heavily spray an 8"x8"x2" inch cake pan. In a small bowl, mix together #1 and set aside.
In large bowl combine #2. In another bowl, mix #3 and add to #2 bowl. Stir until just moistened. Fold in #4.
Pour half of the batter into pan.  Sprinkle with half the reserved #1 mix. Repeat with remaining batter and cinnamon/sugar mixture.  Draw a knife through batter to marble well.
Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean. (190* on an instant read thermometer) Let cool in pan for 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely. Wrap in foil and let sit overnight before slicing.
When ready to serve, slice desired number of pieces & warm for 30 seconds in microwave oven. Make glaze & pour over top of slices. Save rest of glaze for using on future servings.
