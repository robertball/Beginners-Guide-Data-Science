Title:
Minced Pork and Watermelon Lettuce Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb - Lean Ground Pork
1 Tbsp - Vegetable Oil
1 Tbsp - Minced Garlic
1 Tbsp - Minced Ginger
1 medium Onion, finley chopped
1 carrot, finely chopped
2 stalks of celery, finely chopped
1 tsp - Soy Sauce
1/2 cup - green onions, chopped
1/2 cup - hoisin sauce
3/4 cup - pine nuts
2 cups - watermelon, cut into 1/2 inch dice (optional)
8 to 12 iceberg lettuce leaves

Directions:
Add vegetable oil to a heavy, non-stick skillet or wok over high heat.
Add garlic, ginger and onion
Stir-fry for a minute
Add pork and cook until well done
Drain excess liquid
Reduce heat to medium then add carrots and celery
Stir for a few minutes then add soy sauce and green onions
Remove from heat and add hoisin sauce and pine nuts
Stir to mix well
Fold in watermelon
Divide among lettuce leave, fold and wrap.
Serve immediately
You can put a little hoisin sauce in the lettuce before adding filling.
