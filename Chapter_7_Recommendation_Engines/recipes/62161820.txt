Title:
Twisted 1/2 n 1/2 Southern Sweet Tea Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 750 ml bottle Sweet Tea vodka (a few brands out there, any will do)
your favorite recipe for lemonade(Try one of the lemonade recipes from this site! Nothing beats homemade lemonade!) or store bought (if you don't feel like squeezing all those lemons)
ice

Directions:
Fill glass half full with ice. Pour Sweet Tea vodka over the ice to fill glass 1/2 full. Pour lemonade to fill glass completely; stir and enjoy!

Submitter: 
FunwithFood
Description: 
If you love Arizona 1/2 'n 1/2 iced tea you will love this even more! 1/2 lemonade and 1/2 sweet tea that packs a wallop! Great to serve at BBQs or to cool off on a hot summer night!
