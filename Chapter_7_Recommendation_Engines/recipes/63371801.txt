Title:
Acorn Squash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small onion
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
3 vegetable bouillon cubes
1/4 teaspoon curry powder
dash cayenne pepper
1 cup water
1 1/2 cup milk
3 cups mashed cooked acorn squash
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large saucepan, saute the onion in butter. Stir in flour, bouillon, curry and cayenne until blended. Gradually add water and milk. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes. Add the squash, salt and pepper; heat through.
In a blender, process the soup in batches until smooth. Pour into bowls.

Submitter: 
AVELYN
Description: 
'The recipe for this thick and creamy soup was given to me by a fellow squash lover,' reports Dorrene Butterfield from her home in Lincoln, Nebraska. 'The attractive rich yellow soup is especially enjoyable during the cool nights of Indian summer.'Modified from: Acorn Squash Soup
