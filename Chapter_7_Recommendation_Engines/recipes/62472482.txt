Title:
Chicken with Wine Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
7 medium white mushrooms - washed, sliced
1/2 cup dry white wine
1 pakcet dry Italian dressing mix
1 can healthy request your choice of cream soup
1 package chicken (breasts, thighs, legs, whatever you have)

Directions:
1. Mix mushrooms, wine, dressing mix, and cream soup.
2. Spray glass baking dish with nonstick cooking spray and preheat oven to 350.
3. Place chicken in prepared dish.
4. Spread mushroom mixture over chicken.
5. Cover and bake in 350 oven for 1 hour or until chicken is tender.
