Title:
Best Burgers EVER Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4535512.jpg
Ingredients:
5lbs ground beef
1-10oz A-1 sauce
4 slices of bread or 2 of Texas toast ground black peppercorns to taste
1 1/2-2 cups sweet tea

Directions:
Break up ground beef and put slices of bread over top.
Pour sweet tea over the bread to wet bread set aside rest.
Mix bread and ground beef until bread is broken up and mixed well.
Pour a-1 over top and take peppercorn grinder and grind your peppercorn them mix well.
Pour in remaining sweet tea.
Grill over medium heat
*add jalapenos or cheese before grilling if desired
*add more or less to of any ingredients to taste!

Delicious,  juicy, melt in your mouth burgers
