Title:
Corned Beef and Cabbage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. water (replenish as boils out)
Hunk of corned beef
1/2 c brown sugar
1/2 c red wine (can add a little more)
1 onion chopped
1 tsp cloves
pinch dill weed
pinch basil
1 tsp dry mustard
8 peppercorns
pinch cinnamon
pinch caraway seeds
1 tsp prepared horseradish
Meat rub after boiling:
Mustard
Additional brown sugar
dash of ground cloves
After you take out the meat:
Head of cabbage
4 carrots
4 potatoes (red or white or yukon)

Directions:
Rub meat with brown sugar. Plunk the meat into water wine and all the other ingredients(except potatoes, carrots, cabbage and mustard/brown sugar spread) bring to a boil. Simmer until tender, about 3-4 hours.
Take out the meat and put in foil, spread the mustard and brown sugar glaze on top.  Bake for 15 minutes on 350.
Add whole potatoes and carrots to the water, and cook until the vegetables are almost tender. Add cabbage and cook for 15 more minutes.
