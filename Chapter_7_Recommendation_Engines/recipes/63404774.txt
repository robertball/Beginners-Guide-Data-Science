Title:
Lentil Soup w/Golden Beets & Rutabaga Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/916868.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 large rutabaga diced
1 medium golden beet diced
1 large onion diced
2 carrots pealed & cut into rounds
2 parsnips pealed & cut into rounds
1 bulb fennel diced
small bunch fresh parsley chopped fine
1 cup dried lentils
4 cups vegetable broth
2 tbsp olive oil
1/4 tbsp thyme
2 bay leaves

Directions:
1.Heat 2 tbsp olive oil, onions, carrots, parsnips, beets, rutabaga & fennel over medium heat about 3 to five mins
2.Add 4 cups vegetable broth & bring to a boil.
3.Once boiling add lentils, 1/4 tbsp thyme, 2 bay leaves, & parsley.
4.Reduce to a simmer. Let simmer 35-45 minutes or until lentils are tender! Add salt & pepper to taste.

Description: 
This is a hearty, flavorful soup that your family will love! I know mine did. It is perfect for chilly fall nights & makes your house smell heavenly! The golden beets give it a unique pop of flavor. Best lentil soup ever!
