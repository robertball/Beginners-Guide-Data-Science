Title:
Roasted tomato salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 medium tomatoes cut into wedges
1 medium onion thinly sliced
3 cloves garlic minced
3tbs fresh basil chiffonde, kosher salt and pepper
1/2 tsp each dried marjoran and oregano
salt and pepper to taste
3 tbs extra virgin olive oil
1tbs balsamic vinegar
4 romaine lettuce leaves, shredded thin
1 head Boston lettuce
wedge parmesan cheese

Directions:
Mixed the tomatoes, onion and garlic, salt, pepper and herbs in a 8x8 baking pan.
add the olive oil and balsamic and mix to coat
Roast in 350 degree oven until tomatoes and onions are soft and begin to caramelize.  About 20-30 minutes
Let cool until warm
to assemble each plate:
Place 1 Boston lettuce leaf on each plate
Add chopped romaine on top of the Boston lettuce
Place the warm tomato mixture on the leaf and top with thinly sliced parmesan cheese.
Any of the leftover roasting liquid can be spooned over the top of the salad as a warm vinaigrette dressing.
