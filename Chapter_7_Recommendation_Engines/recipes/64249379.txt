Title:
Mom's Coffe Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. shortening
1/2 c. sugar
1 egg, beaten
1 c. milk
1 c. raisins
2 1/2 c. flour
4 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt

Directions:
Mix well together. Pour into greased cake pan, round or 8" x 8" pie pan.
Sprinkle top with mixture; 2 tbsp, sugar, 1 tsp cinnamon.  Bake at 350 for 30 - 35 minutes.

Submitter: 
Sheryl Johnson
Description:
