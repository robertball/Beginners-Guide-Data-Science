Caesar Pasta Salad Recipe

Directions:
Cook and drain pasta as directed on package.  Toss hot pasta with 1/2 cup of the Caesar dressing.  Cover and refrigerate until cold.
Just before serving, toss pasta with reserved 1/2 cup Caesar dressing and remaining ingredients.  Top with a sprinkle of shredded Parmesan cheese, if you like.
