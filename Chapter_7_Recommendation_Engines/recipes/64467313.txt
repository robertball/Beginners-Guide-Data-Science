Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Bisquick
1 pound spicy sausage (can be made with regular, but the spice makes it Dinomite)
4 eggs, beaten
1 cup shredded cheddar
(optional) 1/4 cup kale

Directions:
1. If including kale, blend in food processor for 20 seconds
2. Mix all ingredients
3. Divide evenly into 12-count muffin tin (alternately, roll into balls; that makes everything fluffier!
4. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Emily Anthony Stowers
Description: 
This is an old family recipe, with added kale for vitamins + GISHWHES-ness.
