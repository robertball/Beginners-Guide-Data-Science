Ingredients:
Brownie mix (any favorite will do)
Oreo's
Peanut Butter
Muffin tin
Paper cupcake liners

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Prepare brownie mix according to directions on package.
Line muffin tin.
Spread peanut butter on top of Oreo's.
Add another Oreo on top & spread with peanut butter.
Put in muffin tin.
Pour brownie mixture on top.
Bake for 10 - 20 minutes.
Cool Completely before serving.
