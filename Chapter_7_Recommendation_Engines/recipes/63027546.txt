Title:
Monkey Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (7.5 ounce) packages refrigerated buttermilk biscuits
1 cup packed brown sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 cup butter, melted

Directions:
Cut each biscuit into quarters. In a small bowl, combine brown sugar & cinnamon. Dip biscuits in sugar mixture. Place biscuit pieces in a prepared bundt pan. Pour butter throughout biscuit pieces. Bake at 350 degrees F for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown. Immediately invert onto a serving platter. Serve warm. Refrigerate leftovers.
