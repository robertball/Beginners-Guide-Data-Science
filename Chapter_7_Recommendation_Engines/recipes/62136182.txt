Title:
Easy Cheesy Fiesta Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-2# bag frozen hash brown potatoes, slightly thawed
1 1/2 cups frozen corn
1 7 oz can chopped green chilies, drained
1 1/2-2 cups frozen pepper stir-fry
1 medium onion, chopped
3 Roma tomatoes, chopped
1-32 oz carton sour cream (regular or light)
3 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 tsp. chili powder
1/2 tsp. cumin
tabasco to taste
salt and pepper to taste
2 cups crushed white corn tortilla chips

Directions:
Combine all ingredients except tortilla chips in a large bowl and mix thoroughly.
Press into a greased 11x13 pyrex baking dish.
Top with crushed tortilla chips.
Bake in 350 degree oven 45 minutes or until hot through.

Submitter: 
Mrs. L
Description: 
A Mexican twist is added to traditional cheesy potatoes. Great accompaniment for grilled chicken, steak, or burgers!
