Title:
Taco Crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pks oyster crackers
3/4 cup vegetable oil
1 envelope taco mix
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp chili powder

Directions:
Place crackers in roster pan, drizzle with oil. combine taco mix and seasonings, sprinkle over crackers and toss to coat.  Bake at 350 for 15=20 min or until golden brown stirring twice.
