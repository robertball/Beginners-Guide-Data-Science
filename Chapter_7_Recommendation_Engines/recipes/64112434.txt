Title:
Slow Cooker Enchilada Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds lean ground beef
10 (6 inch) corn tortillas, quartered
1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
1/2 cup water
1 (12 ounce) jar chunky salsa
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
1 can black beans, drained
1 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend

Directions:
Crumble the ground beef into a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir until evenly browned. Add taco seasoning and water; simmer for 15 minutes over low heat.
In a medium bowl, stir together the salsa, cream of mushroom soup and cream of chicken soup & sour cream.
Place a layer of  tortillas covering the bottom of a slow cooker. Scoop a layer of the ground beef over that, and then spoon a layer of the soup mixture. (Save 1/2 cup soup mixture for top.
Repeat the layers until you run out of stuff, ending with a layer of tortillas on the top. Top with soup mixture & cheese.
Cover, and cook on High for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
Alternately, make in casserole dish and bake in oven 3/4 hour at 350 degrees.
