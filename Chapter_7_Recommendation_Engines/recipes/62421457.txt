Title:
Spicy Chuck Roast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
chuck roast
1 pkg dry onion soup mix
1 cup cranberry juice (instead of red wine)
1/2 cup water
1 can Rotel tomatoes (or 1/2 cup salsa)
tortillas

Directions:
Mix ingredients. Pour over roast. Bake 325 for 3-4hrs. After cooked & tender, shred it to serve in tortillas. Let shredded meat sit in sauce for awhile.
