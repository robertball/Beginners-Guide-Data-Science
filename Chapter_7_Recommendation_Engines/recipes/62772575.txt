Title:
Jasmine Rice Pudding with Coconut Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/643682.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup uncooked jasmine rice
1 can (15 oz) Cream of Coconut-sweetened
1 can (13.5 oz) Coconut Milk
1 cup (8 oz) Coconut Juice with Pulp (aka. Coconut Water with Young Coconut Meat)
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 egg yolks whisked
1 cup sweetened flaked coconut to toast

Directions:
-Put all liquids, cinnamon and vanilla in pan...bring to boil (temp: high setting).
-Add rice...stir...cover...turn stove top down to simmer (temp: approx. low/simmer setting)...approximately 30 minutes.
-Stir occasionally...until rice is tender...and liquids thicken.  Remove from heat.
-Whisk egg yolks...spoon two tablespoon of rice mixture into eggs...and then gently stir back into pudding.
-Toast flaked coconut: spread thinly on cookie sheet in oven...put under broiler, or 300 degree pre-heated oven for approximately 15 minutes...toast until golden brown.
-Fluff up the pudding...and divide pudding into bowls, and top with toasted coconut. Serve warm, or it can be chilled...garnish with whipped cream

Submitter: 
Terra-Matris
Description: 
For coconut lovers...this rice pudding is a blend of jasmine rice and coconut; along with coconut milk and cream of coconut,seasoned with vanilla and cinnamon. And then garnished with toasted coconut flakes.Modified from: Jasmine Rice Pudding with Toasted Coconut
