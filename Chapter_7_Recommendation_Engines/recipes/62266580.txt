Title:
Chicken or Shrimp Pho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 cups chicken or seafood broth
1 package of udon noodles
1/2 - 1 chopped jalapeno
1 head of bok choy
1 bunch of green onions
1 pound chicken or shrimp
1 inch of fresh grated ginger
2-3 cloves chopped garlic
1-2 cups of peanuts
bean sprouts
avocado
olive oil/sesame oil
salt and pepper

Directions:
Cook udon noodles per instructions on package. Set aside to add to pho later. To prevent sticking, add a little sesame or olive oil to the noddles and mix around.
Bring broth to a boil and then simmer. Add chopped up bok choy (add as much as you want according to likeness), green onions, ginger, jalapeno (according to likeness), and simmer. Salt and pepper to taste.
In a seperate pan, cook chicken or shrimp in a little olive or sesame oil and cloves of chopped garlic. Once cooked, add to simmering pho. Then, add cooked udon noodles and simmer for another 2 minutes.
Scoop pho into bowls and add bean sprouts, peanuts and avocado to top.

Submitter: 
Alissa Kate
Description: 
Simple udon noodle pho that can be changed as you like.
