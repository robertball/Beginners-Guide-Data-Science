Title:
EVELYN'S PEANUT BUTTER SQUARES Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup peanut butter(smooth or chunky)
1/2 cup roger's syrup - warm it up a bit
Add:
1/3 cup brown sugar
2 cups cornflake's
1 cup rice krispies
1/2 tsp salt
ICING:  1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
add 1/4 cup milk
and 2 cup's icing sugar .

Directions:
MIX THE WARMED SYRUP AND PEANUT BUTTER TOGETHER IN A LARGE BOWL.
ADD THE BROWN SUGAR(1/3 CUP),CORNFLAKES,SALT AND RICE KRISPIES.STIR . PRESS INTO A 9X13 PAN. THEN ICE WITH THIS ICING.
ICING:MELT TOGETHER IN A SAUCEPAN:
1/2 CUP BUTTER
1/2 CUP BROWN SUGAR
REMOVE FROM HEAT AND ADD
1/4 CUP MILK
2 CUPS ICING SUGAR
STIR UNTIL SMOOTH. AND POUR OVER THE CAKE BASE LET COOL.SLICE AND ENJOY.

Submitter: 
Kim Truba
Description: 
Yummy no bake peanut butter square with a brown sugar icing
