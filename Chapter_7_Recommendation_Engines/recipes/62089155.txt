Title:
Easy Chicken, Cheese & Broccoli Rotini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Package Velveeta Rotini & Cheese with Broccoli
2 4-6 oz. Chicken Breasts
4oz can mushrooms
10 oz fresh broccoli florets
2 Tbs olive oil
2 cloves garlic minced

Directions:
Cook the chicken almost all the way on the George Foreman Grill.  (This should only take 5-8 minutes). After it's done, chop into bitesized pieces and put aside.
Prepare pasta according to directions on the box.  Add fresh broccoli during the last 3 minutes of cooking.
In a skillet add garlic, olive oil, mushrooms and chopped chicken.  Sautee for 3-5 minutes until browned.  Add the mushrooms and chicken to the broccoli & pasta.  Add the packaged cheese sauce & mix.  Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Denise
Description: 
This is a quick and easy dinner anytime!  It's so tasty you'll want seconds!
