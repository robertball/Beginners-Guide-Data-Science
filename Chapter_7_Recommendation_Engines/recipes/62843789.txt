Title:
Simple Gazpacho Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 14.5 oz can petite diced tomatoes with zesty jalapeno
12 oz low sodium vegetable juice
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 korean cucumber with skin, in small dice*
1/2 medium sweet onion, diced
10 sprigs flat leaf parsley, chopped
1 tsp worstershire sauce
1/4 cup red wine vinegar
2 tbs olive oil
2 tsp dry oregano
2 tsp dry basil
S & P to taste

Directions:
Mix all ingredients in a large non metal bowl, cover and refrigerate at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.

Submitter: 
BigShotsMom
Description: 
With the extreme heat I've been looking for nutritious but light meals and this was easy with things I had on hand.  I served this with fresh crusty bread and we had a great hot weather meal.
