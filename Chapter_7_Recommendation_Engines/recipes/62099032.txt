Title:
Artichoke Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf French bread
1/2 C. butter
2 tbls. sesame seeds
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 1/2 C. sour cream
2 C. Monteray Jack cheese, shredded
2 tbls. parsley, minced
1 14oz.can artichoke hearts, drained &  chopped
1/4 C. Parmesan cheese (or Romano)
1 C. Cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:
Slice the top off the bread and scoop out the center. Dice the bread.  Saute the bread in butter with garlic.  Stir in the sesame seeds.
Combine the ingredients with the Monteray Jack cheese, Parmesan cheese, parsley, artichokes and sour cream.
Pour back into the hollowed out bread loaf and top with the Cheddar cheese.
Bake on a cookie sheet at 350 degrees F. for 30 minutes.
Serve.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
A very special appetizer or serve with a soup/salad for a ladies luncheon or gathering.
Makes a great presentation on the table.
