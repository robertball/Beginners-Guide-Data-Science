Title:
Milky Way Ice Cream Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 Reg. Size Milky Way Bars
1 can evaporated milk
1 can condensed milk
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla
6 eggs
Whole or lowfat milk

Directions:
Slice candy into microwavable dish and add 1/4 cup milk.
Microwave, stirring regularly until completely melted. (Be careful not to overcook.) Let cool slightly.
Mix well all other ingredients together, except for the whole milk.
Pour candy and other ingredients into ice cream freezer.  Add whole or lowfat milk as needed to the "fill line" of freezer.
Freeze as per ice cream freezer instructions until ready.

Description: 
Rich, chocolaty homemade ice cream that is out of this world!
