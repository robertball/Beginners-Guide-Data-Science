Title:
Broccoli Cheese Soup Restaurant Style Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1884806.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbs butter
1 med onion chopped
3-4 cloves garlic pressed
1/4 butter
1/4 cup flour
2 cups half and half
2 cups chicken stock
1/2 lb broccoli
1 cup carrots julienned
14 oz extra sharp cheddar cheese(Sargento)
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Melt 1 tbs butter, saut√© onion and garlic till soft, remove and reserve for later
Melt 1/4 cup butter, add flour whisking the entire time to make rouge, keep whisking till it turns a nutty brown color
Slowly add half and half, whisking while adding
Add chicken stock and simmer for 20 min
Add carrots, onion, garlic, broccoli and cook on low for 20-25 min till tender
I take a potato masher after cooking and breakup the broccoli till desired consistency
Add cheese and salt a pepper to taste, heat through
Serve with crusty bread, enjoy!
