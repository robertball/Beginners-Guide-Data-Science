Title:
Jim's Spicy Garlic Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Basil Leaves
3 Cloves Garlic
1/2 Cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 Jalapeno Pepper
3/4 Cup Parmesan Cheese
1/4 Cup Parsley Leaves
1/4 Chives (or preferred Onion)
Black Pepper to taste
Ground red chili's to taste (and add color)

Directions:
Chop it all up.
Add all ingredients to food processor.
Process for 30 seconds - 1 minute.
Serve.
Refrigerate extra pesto in sealed container.
Freeze in ice cube trays for storage exceeding 2 weeks.

Mildly spicy, garlicky, basil pesto
