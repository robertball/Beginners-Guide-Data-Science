Title:
Blueberry Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup blueberries
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
1/2 tsp chopped garlic
1/2 tsp chopped shallot
1/2 cup light agave nectar
1 cup extra-virgin olive oil
sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
In a food processor, combine the blueberries vinegar, garlic, and shallot and pulse to chop.  With the motor running, slowly add the agave nectar, then the oil.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.  The vinaigrette can be made up to 3 dyas in advance and kept, covered, in the refrigerator.
To warm, cook over medium-high heat until heated through.
Serving Option: In medium skillet, combine half of the vinaigrette, and 1/4 each of: dried cherries, dried blueberries, dried cranberries and sunflower seeds. Cook over medium-high heat until heated through.  Pour the mixture over salad greens and toss well. When transferring to individual serving plates, make sure each plate gets some of all the goodies.  Top with Tofu Ricotta.

Submitter: 
Momi
Description: 
Delicious, lightly sweet vinaigrette that can be served cold or warmed.
