Title:
Banana Bread Pudding Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/978802.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups whole wheat bread cubes
3 ripe bananas, mashed
3 eggs
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 1/2 cups warm water
1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk (NOT evaporated milk)
2 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup flaked coconut
1/2 cup chopped pecans
Butter Rum Sauce
1/4 cup butter or margarine
3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup whipping cream
1 teaspoon rum flavoring

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Place bread cubes in buttered 9-inch square baking pan.
In large bowl, beat bananas, eggs and cinnamon; add remaining ingredients except Butter Rum Sauce. Pour evenly over bread, moistening completely. Top with pecans.
Bake 45 to 50 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Cool. Serve warm with Butter Rum Sauce. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.

Submitter: 
EAGLE BRAND®
Description: 
Sweetened with bananas, this luscious bread pudding is seasoned with cinnamon and coconut and topped with pecans. Serve it with a creamy butter rum sauce.
