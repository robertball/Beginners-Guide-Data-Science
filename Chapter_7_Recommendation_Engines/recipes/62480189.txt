Title:
Afghan Okra Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1      medium onion
2 tbs  vegetable oil
2 tbs  tomato paste
2 cups water
1 lb   frozen okra or fresh okra
1 tsp  turmenic powder
pepper (to taste)
salt to taste)

Directions:
1. Slice the onion into medium to thin slices.
2. Pour the 2 tbs of oil into a pan on medium heat.
3. Add the onions and leave over heat. Stirring occaisionally until caramelized.
4. Then add the tomato paste, stir, and add the 2 cups of water.
5. Add the package of okra, turmenic powder, salt(try to add a little and then later adjust to taste), pepper (to taste) and let it boil until sauce is thick.
6. Once it is nice and thick and the okra is soft check the salt and pepper again. Adjust each to your own preference and serve.

A tasty healthy Afghan dish with sauteed okra. My mom always makes this dish for us.
