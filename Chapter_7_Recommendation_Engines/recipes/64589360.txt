Title:
Franklin's Roux/Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
-1/2 of a white onion, diced
-1/3 cup flour
-2/3 cup melted butter
-1/2 cup melted butter
-3 cups chicken stock
-salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
-Over low heat, stir together 2/3 cup melted butter with 1/3 cup flour. Stir continuously until it reaches the color of a Hershey's chocolate bar (10-15min).
-Remove from heat.
-In separate pan, heat up 1/2 cup of melted butter over med/high heat and add diced onion.
-Once bubbling, add roux from other pan and chicken stock.
-Bring to a boil then remove from heat and let thicken.
-Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Kevin...lost
Description: 
This recipe was created by Aaron Franklin, the Austin, TX pit master. It is kind of a combination of roux and gravy that goes good with many dishes. I like using it over a smoked turkey and all the sides.
