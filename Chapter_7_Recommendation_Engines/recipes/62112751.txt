Title:
World's Healthiest Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup egg substitute or egg whites
1 cup 1% or nonfat cottage cheese, with salt
1 cup quick cooking oats
1 medium banana

Directions:
Add all ingredients to blender and process until smooth.
Cook at medium-high heat in a large skillet sprayed with cooking spray, using two heaping tablespoons of batter for each pancake.
Flip once bubbles start appearing in middle.
Store extra batter in airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week, and add skim milk or soy milk to thin as needed.

Submitter: 
www.PeanutButterBoy.com
Description: 
These pancakes are fluffy, delicious and healthy enough to eat for every meal of the day! Nobody can tell that they are healthy or made with cottage cheese. Top with defrosted mixed berries and/or peanut butter.
