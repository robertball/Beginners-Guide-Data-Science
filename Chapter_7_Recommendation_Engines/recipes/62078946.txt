Title:
Black-Eyed Peas con Queso Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1 large onion, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, pressed
1 (16 oz.) loaf cheese spread
5 jalapeno peppers, seeded and chopped (optional)
2 (15.8 oz.) cans black eyed peas

Directions:
1. melt butter in a large pot; add onion and garlic, saute until tender
2. add cheese over low heat; stir in peppers and peas
3. stir until thoroughly heated and serve with tortilla chips

Submitter: 
Kimberly Kays
Description: 
This is a great dip that can be made extra spicy or mild. This dip won't last long at any party.
