Title:
Southern Peach Fizz Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 cups cold orange juice
2 cups frozen peach chunks
ice
1/2 cup peach-flavored bourbon liqueur (such as Southern Comfort Â®)
1/2 cup peach schnapps
1/2 cup vodka
2 cups cold lemon-lime flavored soda

Directions:
Place orange juice, peach chunks, and ice in a blender; blend until smooth.
Pour peach-flavored bourbon liqueur, peach schnapps, and vodka into a large pitcher. Add orange-peach mixture; stir well to blend.
Pour soda slowly into pitcher to prevent foaming.

Submitter:

Description: 
Sunshine in a glass! Watch out though, these might sneak up on you! Serve over ice and garnish with mint and orange slices.
