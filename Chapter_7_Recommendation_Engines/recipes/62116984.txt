Title:
Fresh Summer Tomato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large ripe tomatoes
2 large shallots
1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
salt and pepper, to taste
Loaf crusty french bread

Directions:
Slice tomatoes and arrange on serving platter.  Finely chop shallots.  Mix olive oil and vinegar in bowl.  Sprinkle shallots over tomatoes.  Pour oil and vinegar over tomatoes.  Serve with crusty french bread to mop up juices.

Submitter: 
S.Michael
Description: 
This salad is perfect for a light, refreshing summer salad.  Best if using homegrown tomatoes and very good quality vinegar.
