Title:
Baked beans without the baking. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/883219.jpg
Ingredients:
1 tbsp cooking oil
1/2 onion, chopped
1 can pinto beans
1 can diced tomatoes
2 tbsp tomato ketchup
1 tbsp yellow mustard

Directions:
Fry the onion in the oil until soft. Add all the other ingredients and stir. Bring to a boil and simmer for fifteen minutes. Serve. You could also add chopped up hot dogs or tofu dogs.

Submitter: 
Phil
Description: 
A very easy way to make delicious, healthy baked beans.
