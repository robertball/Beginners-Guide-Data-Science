Title:
Pineapple Yogurt Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985172.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package reduced-fat cream cheese, softened
1 (8 ounce) container fat-free plain yogurt
1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained well
1/2 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated

Directions:
Process cream cheese, yogurt, pineapple, and SPLENDA(R) Granulated Sweetener in a food processor or blender until smooth, stopping to scrape down sides. Spoon into a serving dish; cover and chill. Serve with assorted fresh fruit.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Cool and creamy, this dip makes a delicious centerpiece for a tray filled with assorted fresh fruits. Serve with brunch or as a tasty addition to a dessert buffet.
