Title:
Apricot Treats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
36 dried apricots, dried with paper towel
1/2 C. chopped pistachio nuts
1 lb. good quality chocolate or milk chocolate for candy making
mini cupcake liners

Directions:
Line the mini cupcake pans with paper liners.
Dry off the apricots with a paper towel to make sure no moisture is on them.
Melt the chocolate over simmering water or in the microwave.
Place the chopped pistachio nuts in a small bowl.
Dip each apricot into the chocolate, allowing excess to drip off then dip in chopped pistachio nuts.
Place one in each mini cupcake pan and chill in the freezer for 15 minutes or in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
Store covered in the refrigerator to keep the chocolate firm.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
For gift giving when a easy to make hostess gift is needed or to add to the holiday cookie tray for variety and flavor, this recipe is for you. It's just outstandingly delicious.
