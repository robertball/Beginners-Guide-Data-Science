Title:
Garlic Butter French Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter
2 TBSP crushed/minced garlic
1 TSP lemon juice (optional)
2 TSP Italian herb blend (optional)

Directions:
-Preheat oven o 350 degrees.
-Work butter, garlic, lemon juice, and seasonings on a plate with a spoon or knife. Work until it is slightly soft.
-Cut a crusty bread loaf (French works really well)length wise, almost in half.
-spread butter mixture onto one side of loaf.
-fold bread back to make loaf again
-Wrap loaf loosely in foil
-Bake in oven for 20 minutes
