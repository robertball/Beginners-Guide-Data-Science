Title:
Caramelized Nectarine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3859858.jpg
Ingredients:
1 nectarine
2 tablespoons white sugar
2 tablespoons plain yogurt

Directions:
Cut nectarine all the way around and twist open. Remove pit. Sprinkle sugar on the cut side of each nectarine half.
Place nectarine, cut-side down, onto a griddle or in a skillet over medium heat; cook for 1 1/2 minutes. Transfer nectarines to a plate, cut-side up, and top each with plain yogurt.

Submitter: 
Gabriel
Description: 
This is a fast easy recipe that tastes great. It is great for entertaining. Dice it, put it on toothpicks, and 
put the yogurt in a bowl for dipping.
