Title:
Appalachian Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/834861.jpg
Ingredients:
4 cups chopped cabbage
1 tomato, chopped
1/2 cucumber, chopped
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
1 tablespoon mayonnaise

Directions:
In a large bowl, stir together the cabbage, tomato, cucumber, onion, sugar, salt, pepper and mayonnaise.  Add additional mayonnaise to suit your taste.
