Title:
Chicken Enchiladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
cooked chicken breast
2 cloves garlic minced
2/3 cups onion chopped
1 tbs oil
1 can (10oz) enchilada sauce
1/2 tsp oregano
1 jar (8 oz) salsa
8 wheat tortillas heated and med
1/2 cup cheddar cheese shredded

Directions:
saute onion and garlic in oil.  stir in enchilada sauce and salsa and layer in casserole (tortilla, enchilada, chicken, sauce) 6 times. first place sauce on bottom
microwave medium-high (70%) for 11 mins 11 sec-covered
sprinkle with cheese
microwave high (100%) 1 min 11 sec
