Title:
Steak Mushrooms Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8oz. package white mushrooms (sliced)
2 TBS butter
1 tsp minced onion
3 TBS steak sauce
Seasoned salt (to taste)

Directions:
Saute on medium high heat: mushrooms, butter, and minced onions until mushrooms begin to soften, or about 5 minutes.
Add steak sauce and seasoning salt and continue to saute until mushrooms are tender.
Drain excess liquid.

Submitter: 
Lee Ann Girard
Description: 
We eat a lot of steak, and these are a great topper!
