Title:
Hot Chocolate Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 c. instant nonfat dry milk
3/4 c. sugar
1/2 c. cocoa
1/8 tsp. salt

Directions:
Combine all ingredients and store in an airtight container.  For each serving, add 1/4 c. cocoa mix to 8 oz. (1 c.) hot water, stirring well.  210 calories each serving.
