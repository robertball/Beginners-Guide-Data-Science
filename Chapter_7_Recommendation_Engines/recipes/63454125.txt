Title:
Oven-Fried Chicken with Honey Butter Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c flour
2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 tsp paprika
1 3 lb fryer, cut into pieces
1 stick butter
Sauce:
4 tbsp butter
1/4 c honey
1/4 c lemon juice

Directions:
Pour melted butter in 9x13 baking dish and set aside.
Combine flour, salt, pepper, and paprika in shallow bowl.
Dredge chickens pieces in flour mixture and place in baking dish, turning to coat with butter.
Bake skin-side down at 400 degrees for 30 minutes.
While chicken is baking, make sauce by melting butter in a pan an beating in honey and lemon juice.
Turn chicken pieces, pour sauce over pieces and bake an additional 30 minutes.
Spoon pan juices over chicken and serve.
