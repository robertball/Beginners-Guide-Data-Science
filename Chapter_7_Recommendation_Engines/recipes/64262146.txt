Title:
Zucchini Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1826945.jpg
Ingredients:
2-3 Zucchinis, diced
4-5 Mozzarella Cheese Balls
1/3 – ½ Hard Italian Salami, diced
Chopped Almonds
Dressing:
4 Cloves of Garlic
Rosemary
Sea Salt
Pepper
1/3 C Olive Oil
Balsamic Vinegar

Directions:
Dice up the zucchini, mozzarella cheese and salami. The salami should be small, just to add some flavor.
Chop almonds next, about 12.
For the dressing; smash the garlic, add in rosemary, salt and pepper to taste. Add in the zucchini, cheese
and salami. Once mixed, add in the olive oil and the large splash of balsamic vinegar. Mix well. Let stand
for 10 minutes then serve and enjoy!
