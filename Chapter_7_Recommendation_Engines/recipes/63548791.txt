Title:
Jade's Red Hot Apple Cider Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 gallon apple cider or apple juice
1 1/4 cups cinnamon candy hearts

Directions:
Mix the cider and cinnamon hearts together in slow cooker.
Cover and cook on low for 1 1/2-2 hours, or until the hearts melt and it's as hot as you like it.
