Title:
Spinach Power Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1112172.jpg
Ingredients:
Spinach (or mixed spring greens)2 cups
Celery 3 large stalks
Cucumber 1 (8 inches long)
Parsley 1 bunch
Apples 2 medium
Ginger 1/2 thrumb
Lime 1
Lemon 1/2
Cold water 1/2 -1 cup

Directions:
Wash all ingredients. Chop celery,cucumber,apple and ginger.
Put everything in a blender and mix well.

Submitter: 
Blue Cat
Description: 
The ginger, lime and lemon add favors. Apples add sweetness.  Surprisingly delicious!
