Title:
Pumpkin Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 C bran
3/4 C W.W. flour
3/4 C sugar
11/2t cinnamon
1t baking powder
1t baking soda
3/4 C raisins
1C pumpkin
1 egg
1/4C veg oil
1/2C yoghurt
1/4C water

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400'F
Combine dry ingredients and raisins.
Mix together egg, oil, yoghurt, water and pumkin.
Add wet ingredients to dry and mix together.
Drop into paper cups or well greased muffin pan.
Bake 25 mins.
