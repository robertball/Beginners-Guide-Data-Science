Title:
Pourable Corn Tortillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 c. cold water
1 c. all purpose flour
1/2 c Cornmeal
1/4 tsp salt
1 egg

Directions:
Heat skillet to med low until hot.Grease if necessary.
Mix all ingredients until smooth.
Pour 1/4 c of batter and swirl around until tortilla is a bout 6" in size.
Cook until tortilla is dry at edge, about 2 minutes.
Flip and cook until golden, about 2 more minutes.

Submitter: 
CT Kelly
Description: 
A quick recipe to make a tortilla without rolling or a press. Handy to have in a hurry, when you want to make a recipe that calls for tortillas and you are out.
