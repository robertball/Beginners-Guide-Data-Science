Title:
Roasted Potatoes, Carrots and Onions with Brown Sugar and Balsamic Vinegar Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1033902.jpg
Ingredients:
6 medium potatoes
6 medium carrots
3 medium onions
6 gloves of garlic
1/4 tsp sage
1/4 tsp basil
1/4 c EVOO
2 TBSP balsamic vinegar
2 TBSP light brown sugar
Pepper and Sea Salt to tates

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees
Peel and cut carrots* and onion*
Cut potatoes*
Crush and peel garlic
Place all vegetables on a bowl add vinegar and EVOO and toss to coat all vegetables.
Place vegetables on rimmed baking sheet and cook for 30 minutes tossing vegetables a couple of times during that period for even cooking.
After 30 minutes or once vegetables are tender and golden brown sprinkle with brown sugar and cook at 400 for ten minutes.
Grind pepper and sea salt over vegetables just prior to serving.

Submitter: 
Dianna Jacobs-Fresh
Description: 
This sweet and savory vegetable dish was a huge hit with my vegetable hating nieces this summer.
