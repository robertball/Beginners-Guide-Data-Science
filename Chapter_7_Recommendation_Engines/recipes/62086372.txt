Title:
Busy Day Chicken Curry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 boneless chicken breasts, sliced
1 tbsp canola oil
1 can black beans drained and rinsed
1 can chick peas drained and rinsed
1 onion thinly sliced
2 cloves garlic pressed
3 cups frozen or fresh cauliflower and broccoli florets.
4 tbsp curry paste (I use mild or medium)
1/4 tsp cayenne powder
1 tsp cumin
1 can coconut milk

Directions:
In a large pan, saute chicken, onion and garlic in oil until cooked through.
Add spices and curry paste, black beans, chick peas and vegetables and continue to fry for 2-3 minutes.  Add coconut milk, and bring to simmer, stirring frequently.
Cover and simmer 5 minutes.
Serve with brown rice.
