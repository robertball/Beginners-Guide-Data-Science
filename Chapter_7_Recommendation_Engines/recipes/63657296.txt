Title:
Big Ray's Creole Seasoning Blend Recipe Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1016407.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Tbl onion powder
2 Tbl garlic powder
2 Tbl oregano
2 Tbl basil
1 Tbl dried thyme
1 Tbl ground black pepper
1 Tbl cayenne pepper
5 Tbl paprika
1 Tbl celery salt
2 Tbl season salt

Directions:
In a medium bowl combine all ingredients and mix well. Store in an air tight container in a dry and dark place.
