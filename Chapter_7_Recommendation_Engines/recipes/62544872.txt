Title:
Brown Rice Paella With Chicken, Red Peppers and Artichoke Hearts. Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked brown rice
4 chicken thighs
1 jar marinated artichoke hearts, chopped.
1 jar roasted bell peppers, chopped
1 onion, sliced.
1-14 oz can diced tomatoes
1-14 oz can chicken broth
¾ cup dry white wine
2 cups water
Paprika
1/8 teaspoon Saffron
½ teaspoon Oregano

Directions:
Crush saffron with fingers and add to wine and set aside.
Season chicken with salt, pepper and paprika.
In a large skillet, sauté chicken.
Cube chicken. Set aside.
Sauté onion.
Add rice to skillet, and allow to toast for a few minutes.
Add chicken back to pan.
Season with oregano, paprika, salt and pepper.
Add broth, wine, water and juice from tomatoes.
Cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
Stir in artichoke hearts and roasted peppers.
Re-cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
