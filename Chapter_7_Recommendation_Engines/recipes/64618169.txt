Title:
Michelle's Sticky Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp brown sugar
4 Tbsp honey
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 tsp. dry ginger
4 tsp. garlic
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
4 Tbsp. flour
1 tsp. pepper
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
sesame oil

Directions:
Put brown sugar, honey, soy sauce, ginger, garlic, and red pepper flakes into small bowl and mix.
Cut chicken breasts into bite-size pieces.
Put flour and pepper into plastic bag.  Add the chicken pieces and coat.
Warm up sesame  oil in skillet on high.  Add the chicken pieces to brown, about five minutes.
Once the chicken is browned, add the soy sauce mixture to skillet, turn heat down to medium.  Stir the chicken into the soy sauce mixture and cook for about 10 minutes.  The flour from the chicken will thicken the sauce as it cooks.
