Title:
Spicy Pickled Swiss Chard Stems Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2c Rice Wine Vinegar
3/4c Distilled White Vinegar
1/4c sugar (or 3T organic maple syrup)
1 1/2t salt
1 1/2T Sriracha (or other spicy chili sauce)
1/2t Celery seed (divided)
1t Black peppercorn (divided)
1t Mustard seed (divided)
4 cloves garlic (divided)

Directions:
Add half of the celery seed, black peppercorn, mustard see, and garlic to each jar.
Pack Chard stems tightly into jars.
Bring vinegars, sugar/maple syrup, salt and sriracha to a boil in a small sauce pan, stir until salt and sugar are dissolved.
Pour liquid over Chard and let cool.
Cover tightly with lids and refrigerate for 2 days.
These will also 1 month in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Lori Rowley-Sipple
Description:
