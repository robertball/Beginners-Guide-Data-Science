Title:
Cold Zucchini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 cups water
2 cups chicken broth
1 1/2 pounds zucchini, sliced
1 baking potato, chopped
coarse salt
2 teaspoons curry powder
1/3 cup sliced almonds, toasted

Directions:
Cook onion in oil; 4-5 minutes
Add garlic and curry powder
Add zucchini, water and broth, and potato
Simmer until tender
Puree until smooth
Serve with  almonds

Description: 
Meeps Yox's recipe; serve cold or at room temperature
