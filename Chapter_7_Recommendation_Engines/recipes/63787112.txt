Title:
Asian Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Salad:
1/2 head of heart of romaine lettuce, cut to bite size
1/2 head nappa cabbage, cut to bite size
2 celery stalks, sliced on a diagonal 1/4" thick
2 baby cucumbers, peeled and julienned 1/4" thick
1/2 cup blanched broccoli florets, cut to bite size
1 cup red or yellow grape tomatoes sliced in half
1/4 cup fried chow mein noodles
Salad Dressing:
1/4 cup apricot preserves
3 Tbs soy sauce
1 large clove minced garlic
1/8 cup sesame seed oil
1 tsp toasted sesame seeds

Directions:
Salad:
Mix red leaf lettuce, nappa cabbage, cucumber, tomatoes and chow mein noodles together.
In a pan over medium-hight heat spray olive oil and place celery in. Stir fry for 2-3 minutes. remove celery and let cool.
Once cooled add to salad.
Salad Dressing:
In a small bowl add garlic, apricot preserves and soy sauce. Mix until apricot preserves are well incorporated and most lumps are out.
Add sesame oil and sesame seeds. Mix well.
Dress the salad with the salad dressing and serve

Submitter: 
Sabrina Sperry
Description: 
A very tasty, sweet and savory salad! This salad has great textures and flavors that pair well together.
