Title:
Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/819021.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb Ground Beef
1 1/2 tsp Chili Powder
1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
1/8 tsp Cayenne Powder
1/4 cup Water
Cheddar cheese, shredded
Lettuce, shredded
Onion, finely chopped
Salsa
Taco shells & Tortilla Chips

Directions:
Crumble ground beef into 1 1/2 quart glass casserole. Cover with glass lid.
Microwave for 5 minutes on HIGH; drain.
Stir in seasonings and water and continue cooking for 3 to 4 minutes, on HIGH, or until meat is well done.
Fill each taco shell with about 2 heaping tablespoons of meat filling.
Top with remaining ingredients.
TIP: Taco shells can be heated for 15 to 20 seconds on REHEAT (power 8) before filling.

Submitter: 
Zan
Description: 
This is my mom's recipe for tacos. I really enjoy the way she seasons her meat. Her seasonings are way better than those store bought packs.
