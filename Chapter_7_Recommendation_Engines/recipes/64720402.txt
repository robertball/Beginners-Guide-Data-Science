Title:
Fried Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb to 1 lb. Cooked pasta
2 tbs butter
2 tbs Minced Garlic
1/2 tbs oregano
1/2 tbs onion powder
Parsley for garnish

Directions:
Melt butter in med to large non stick pan
Add garlic and onion powder
let garlic get slightly brown, you don't won't to burn it.
add pasta and toss until edges are brown and crispy
add Oregano, toss for another 5 mins.
Add Parsley and remove from heat.

Submitter: 
Phil Anastasi Jr.
Description: 
I've been making this for years and its great, You can use any kind of pasta for a crispy flavorful side dish, or leftover lunch
