Title:
Bahamian Marmalade Barbeque Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/7160924.jpg
Ingredients:
6 teaspoons dry mustard
2 teaspoons rum
1 cup orange marmalade
1/2 teaspoon soy sauce, or to taste

Directions:
Mix mustard and rum together in a bowl to make a paste. Add marmalade and soy sauce. Stir until combined. Store in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
mikekey
Description: 
Use this as a condiment for barbeque dishes.
