Title:
Flavored Cracked Sugar Cookies I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6oz package of flavored jello
1/2 Cup sugar
2 Tablespoons applesauce
1 cup butter
3 egg yolks
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).  Lightly grease 2 cookie sheets.
Cream together sugar and butter and jello.  Beat in egg yolks and vanilla.
Add  flour, baking soda, and cream of tartar. Stir.Dough will be crumbly. Add applesauce (May need more til dough is at a play dough consistency).
Form dough into walnut size balls and place 2 inches apart on cookie sheet. Don't flatten.  Bake 10 to 11 minutes, until tops are cracked and just turning color.
