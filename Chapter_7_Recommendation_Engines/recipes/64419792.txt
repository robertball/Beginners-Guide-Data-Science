Title:
Chinese Pork Ribs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup soy sauce
2/3 cup orange marmalade
6 tablespoons ketchup
2 tablespoon rice wine vinegar
2 tablespoon sesame oil
1 t cumin
8 cloves garlic, minced
3 pounds boneless country style pork ribs

Directions:
Combine soy sauce, marmalade, ketchup, rice wine, sesame oil, cumin and garlic in a bowl. Marinate overnight. Place ribs into cooker. Bring sauce to a boil. Pour sauce over ribs.
Cover and cook on Low until pork is tender, about 1 1/2 hours. Thicken cooking juices in a small skillet over medium heat, if desired.
