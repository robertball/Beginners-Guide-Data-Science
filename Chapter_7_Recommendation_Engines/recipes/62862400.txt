Title:
Banana Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup shortening
1 tsp salt
1 cup sugar
2 tsp baking powder
2 eggs
1/3 cup buttermilk
1 cup mashed bananas
2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 cup chopped pecans

Directions:
banana nut bread.
Items needed:
1/3 cup shortening 1 tsp salt
1 cup sugar 2 tsp baking powder
2 eggs 1/3 cup buttermilk
1 cup mashed bananas 2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda 1 cup pecans (chopped)
Cream shortening and sugar. Add eggs and bananas, then add flour salt, baking soda, and baking powder. Slowly add the buttermilk and mix till all ingredients are mixed and then add the nuts. Pout into one loaf pans that is greased or has pan spray int it. Bake at 400 degrees, check with tooth pick to ensure that it cooked all the way. Take out and remove from pan and let cool. If you are like me you wnat it hot with butter on it. I hope that goes well and enjoy!!!
