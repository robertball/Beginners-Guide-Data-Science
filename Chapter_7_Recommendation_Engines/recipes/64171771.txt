Title:
Sweet Potato Fries and Chipotle Mayo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 to 4 Sweet Potatoes cut into spears
1 tbsp Olive Oil
Salt
1/2 Cup Fat Free Mayo
3 to 4 Chipotle peppers in adobo

Directions:
Preheat 400 degrees F. On a baking sheet, spread out sweet potato spears. Drizzle them with olive oil and sprinkle with a few pinches of salt. Transfer to the oven to roast for 20 to 30 minutes, until tender when poked with a fork.
Add your chipotle peppers to a food processor and pulse until minced. Add the mayonnaise and pulse again until combined. Give it a taste and salt according to your liking.
Remove the sweet potato spears from the oven and serve alongside the chipotle mayonnaise.
