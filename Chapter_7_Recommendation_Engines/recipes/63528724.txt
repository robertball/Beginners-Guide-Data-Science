Title:
Amazing Baked Hot Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons 1.5% fat buttermilk
3 tablespoons hot sauce, (Frank's RedHot)
1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
20 (3.5lbs)chicken wings
6 tablespoons whole-wheat flour
6 tablespoons cornmeal
Cayenne Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Mix 3 tablespoons of buttermilk, 3 tablespoons of hot sauce and 1 tablespoon of vinegar in bowl and a pinch of cayenne pepper.
Cut tips of wings off and put chicken in the bowl and let marinate for 1 hour.
Mix together cornmeal and flour in bowl.
Cover chicken with flour mixture and place on baking sheet, sprinkle with cayenne pepper and put in oven.
Bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes, flip and sprinkle with cayenne pepper again.
Cook for a total of 1 hour or until crispy.
