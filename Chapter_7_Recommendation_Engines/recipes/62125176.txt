Title:
Corned Beef and Cabbage Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small cabbage
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1/2 finely chopped onion
2- 12 oz cans corned beef
1/3 cup butter or margarine
1/3 cup flour
2 1/2 cups milk
1 tsp salt
dash pepper

Directions:
Remove core from cabbage.
Chop cabbage into 1 by 2 inch pieces.
Boil in a large pot for 5 minutes, until tender, not mushy.
Place in a 2 quart casserole dish.
Melt the 1/4 cup butter in a saucepan and add onion and corned beef.
Saute, breaking up the beef as you go, until onions are clear.
Pour over cabbage.
In same saucepan, melt 1/3 cup butter.
Stir in flour.
Add Milk, salt and pepper.
Keep stirring and bring to a boil.
Pour white sauce over corned beef and cabbage mixture.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
