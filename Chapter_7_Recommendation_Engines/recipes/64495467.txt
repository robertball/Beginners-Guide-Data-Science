Title:
Spicy Glazed Carrots II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. carrots, peeled and sliced into 1/4 inch rounds
1 c. cola or ginger ale
1 t. paprika
1 t. chile powder
1/2 t. black pepper
1 1/2 t. salt
2 T butter

Directions:
1. Combine all ingredients in a large saucepan and bring to a boil
2. Reduce heat to low and cover, simmering for 15 minutes or until carrots are almost tender
3. Remove cover and increase heat to medium, boiling until all remaining liquid is completely boiled off
4. Continue to cook and stir for five to ten minutes until carrots and spices begin to turn brown

Submitter: 
jonvance
Description: 
A zesty spin on glazed carrots, this is something even folks that don't like carrots can enjoy.
