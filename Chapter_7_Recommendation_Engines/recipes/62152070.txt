Title:
Blood Orange and Pineapple Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C. AP Flour
1 C. Sugar
2 tsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 C. Melted Butter
2 Eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 C. Moro Orange Juice (about 2 oranges)
Zest from 2 Moro Oranges
1 1/4 C. Chopped Fresh Pineapple

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients in a mixing bowl. Add eggs, orange juice, melted butter and zest. Mix with wooden spoon just until combined. Fold in pineapple. Fill muffin tins lined with paper cupcake liners. Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
EAKE
Description: 
A delicious muffin to make when the Moro Orange (Blood Orange) is in season. It is best made with fresh pineapple.
