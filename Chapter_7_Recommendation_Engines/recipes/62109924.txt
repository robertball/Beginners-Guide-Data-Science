Title:
Lavender Lemonade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup dried lavender flowers
2 cups water
Favorite lemonade mix or homemade
Lemon slices

Directions:
Combine 2 cups of water and lavender flowers together in sauce pot. Bring to a rapid boil on stovetop. Remove pot from heat and allow to steep for 3 - 5 minutes. Pour water through strainer into 3/4 pitcher of prepared cold lemonade.
Add ice and garnish with fresh lemon slices.

Submitter: 
Malina
Description: 
Lavender is known for it's calming effects while lemonade is refreshing, cool, and invigorating. A perfect unique summer treat or an unusual delicacy for special occassions. This has been a hit every time I have made it...and people are shocked by what it is.
