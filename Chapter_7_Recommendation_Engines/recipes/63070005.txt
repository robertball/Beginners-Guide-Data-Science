Title:
White Chili with pulled chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 onion, chopped
5 cloves garlic, minced
1 3 pound rotisserie chicken pulled
2 (4 ounce) cans canned green chile peppers, chopped
1 tablespoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon rancho chile pepper
1 tablespoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
ground cayenne pepper to taste
ground white pepper to taste
3 (15 ounce) cans cannellini beans
64 oz chicken stock
1/4 cup of wine
2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Directions:
In a large pot over medium heat, combine the onion, garlic and chicken and saute for 10 minutes, or until onions are soft. Add the chile peppers, cumin, oregano, cinnamon, ranch chili pepper, cayenne pepper to taste and white pepper to taste and saute for 5 more minutes.
Add two cans of the beans and the chicken broth to the pot. Take the third can of beans and puree them in a blender or food processor. Add this to the pot along with the cheese and wine. Stir well and simmer for 10 minutes, allowing the cheese to melt.

Submitter:

Description: 
pulled rotisserie chicken and cannellini beans are combined with green chilies, onion and garlic in this chicken stock based soup seasoned with cumin, oregano and cinnamon.  Serve with grated jack cheese.Modified from: White Chili with Ground Turkey
