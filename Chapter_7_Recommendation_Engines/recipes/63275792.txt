Title:
Robin's amazing meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1/2 cup milk
1 pound extra lean ground beef
1 pound ground pork
2 Tbsps sea salt
1 medium onion, diced
2 Tbsp garlic
1 Tbsp Italian seasoning
1 Tbsp dried oregano
2 Tbsps Worcestershire sauce
2 Tbsps Soya sauce
2/3 cup ricotta cheese
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
Preheat an oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Combine bread crumbs and milk and let sit 10 minutes.
Combine all ingredients (including crumbs) and mix well.
Form into 1 1/2-inch meatballs, and place onto a baking sheet.
Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, 20 to 25 minutes.

Submitter: 
redheadchef
Description: 
These meatballs can be served with your favourite spaghetti dish, with gravy over rice, or all on their own.  Make a large batch and freeze for quick meals.
