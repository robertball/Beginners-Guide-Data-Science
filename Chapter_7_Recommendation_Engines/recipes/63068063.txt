Title:
Yummy Peanut Butter Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup shortening
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 cup packed light brown sugar
3/4 cup white sugar
1 cup peanut butter
2 eggs
1 1/2cups all-purpose flour
2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1 cup quick-cooking oats
1 cup chocolate chips, optional

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*F. Cream shortening, butter, sugars and peanut butter until smooth. Beat in the eggs, separately. Blend well.
Combine flour, baking soda and salt. Stir into the creamed mixture. Mix in the oats until combined. Drop by teaspoonful onto greased cookie sheet. Bake 12-15 mins or until lightly browned. These cookies freeze well.
