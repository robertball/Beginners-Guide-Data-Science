Title:
Gluten free flour tortilla Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 c brown rice flour
3/4 c potato starch
1/8 tsp salt
boiling water

Directions:
Add boiling water to dry and knead until smooth. Divide into 3 balls and flatten each into a circle . Then brown till bubbly in a dry skillet over medium heat.
