Title:
Rhubarb Coffee Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup margarine
2 cups brown sugar
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
2 cups flour
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup sour milk (or buttermilk) (can sour milk by adding 1 tsp. vinegar & let sit a few minutes)
1 tsp. baking soda, dissolved in milk
1 1/2 cups diced rhubarb
Topping:
1/2 cup sugar
1 tbsp. butter or margarine
1 tsp. cinnamon

Directions:
Cream together margarine and sugar.
Beat in egg.
Add vanilla and milk.
Add flour and salt.
Stir in rhubarb.
Pour into a greased 9" x 13" pan.
Mix topping ingredients and sprinkle over batter.
Bake @ 350 x 30 - 35 minutes, until tests done with a toothpick.
Can be served warm or cold, as a cake or with ice cream, milk or whipped cream.
Freezes well.
