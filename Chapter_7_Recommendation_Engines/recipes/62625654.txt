Title:
Crab Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 pounds crabmeat
2 1/2 cups chopped celery
8 tablespoons mayonnaise
2 teaspoons dill weed
2 1/2 tablespoons white sugar
1/2 tablespoon ground black pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine the crabmeat, celery, mayonnaise, dill weed, sugar, and pepper,  Mix well and refrigerate until chilled.
Can add diced green onions if desired.

Submitter: 
Dorothy Clark
Description: 
Serve this crab and celery salad on crackers, lettuce or stuff it in puff pastry shells.Modified from: Crab Salad
