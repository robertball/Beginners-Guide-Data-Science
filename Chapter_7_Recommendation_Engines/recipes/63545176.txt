Title:
Pesto Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 oz fresh basil
2 oz fresh spinach
3 tbsp roasted sunflower seeds
3 tbsp Parmesan cheese
2-3 cloves garlic, minced
salt and pepper
1/4 - 1/2 cup olive oil

Directions:
Process basil, spinach, sunflower seeds, cheese, garlic and salt and pepper until everything is chopped nicely.
Scrape down the sides of the bowl and then slowly pour olive oil in with processor running until the consistency is how you like it.
freeze unused portions

Submitter: 
Apron Appeal
Description: 
Nut free pesto that is quick and easy and just as good as any other.
