Title:
Mom's Award-Winning Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. Lean Ground Beef
12 oz. Tube Jimmy Dean HOT Sausage
1 Cup Chopped Onion
2 Cloves Garlic, Finely Chopped or Minced
2 15 oz. Cans Diced Tomatoes, Undrained
2 8 oz. Cans Tomato Sauce
2 15 oz. Cans Kidney Beans Drained
2 Tbsp. Chili Powder
1 1/2 Tsp. Ground Cumin
1/2 Tsp. Salt
1/2 Tsp. Pepper

Directions:
Cook ground beef, onion, and garlic in pan.
Cook until very brown
Drain and place in crock pot
Cook sausage until very brown
Do NOT drain sausage
Place sausage in crock pot
Add remaining ingredients
Cook on low for at least 6 hours

Submitter: 
Jenni Rose
Description: 
This is the most amazing chili you'll ever make.  Every time I eat it I think, Wow!  This is one of the best things I've ever eaten in my life!  This is my Mom's recipe and I'm very proud to say it!
