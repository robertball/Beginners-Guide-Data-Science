Title:
Greek Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 oz. pasta, cooked according to package directions, drained and rinsed
2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
1 cucumber, halved and sliced
4 green onions, sliced
1/3 cup pitted kalamata olives, halved
1/2 cup olive oil
3/4 cup lemon juice
2 T snipped fresh basil, or 2 t. dried
2 T snipped fresh oregano, or 2 t. dried
4 to 6 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
1 cup crumbled fresh feta cheese

Directions:
In a jar, combine oil, lemon juice, garlic and seasonings.  Cover and shake well.
In a large bowl, combine all other ingredients except cheese.  Drizzle with dressing and mix.  Refrigerate at least 2 hours, or up to 24 hours.  Toss in feta cheese before serving.

Submitter: 
Jo Bice
Description: 
This has become a family favorite for large gatherings.  It's hearty yet refreshing, and makes a good meatless meal in the summer.
