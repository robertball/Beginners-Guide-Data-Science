Title:
Mushroom Saute Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons butter
1 garlic clove, crushed
8 ounces sliced mushrooms
2 tablespoons chopped onion
1 teaspoon fresh thyme, chopped

Directions:
In a large skillet over medium heat melt butter. Saute garlic 30 seconds an stir in mushrooms, onions and thyme. Season with salt and pepper. Saute until mushrooms are tender, stirring occasionally, about 10 minutes. Refrigerate any leftovers.
