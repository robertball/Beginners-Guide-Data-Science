Title:
Ultra Easy Pineapple Johnsonville Sausage Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1120978.jpg
Ingredients:
2 green bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
1 onion, cut into 1-inch pieces
1 package of Johnsonville Sweet Italian sausage
1 (15 ounce) can pineapple chunks, drained
1/4 cup barbeque sauce, or as needed
6 skewers

Directions:
Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
Grill Johnsonville Sweet Italian sausage on the grill until just cooked through then cut into 1 inch pieces.
Thread green bell pepper, onion, sausage, and pineapple onto skewers; Cook on the preheated grill until peppers and onions are crisp tender.  5 to 7 minutes per side. Brush with BBQ sauce the last few minutes.
