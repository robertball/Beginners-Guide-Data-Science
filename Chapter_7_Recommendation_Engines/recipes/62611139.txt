Title:
Juicy Marinated Steaks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1045459.jpg
Ingredients:
4 high quality steaks (ribeye, new york strip, etc.)
1/4 cup high quality root beer
1/4 cup canola oil
2 T worchestire sauce
2 T teriyaki sauce
2 T heinz 57 sauce
1 t galric salt
1/2 t each of onion salt, pepper, salt, basil, thyme and rosemary

Directions:
Pierce both sides of the steaks.
Place in a shallow non-metal container or large zip lock baggie.
Combine all ingredients and mix thoroughly.
Pour over steaks and flip to coat well.
Refrigerate for 3-4 hours, flipping meat once halfway through.
Remove from fridge and let stand at room temperature for 30 minutes.
Grill or broil until desired degree of doneness.

Submitter: 
Denis
Description: 
Tender, marinated steaks with a taste that will enhance and not over-power the flavor of a good steak.
