Title:
Lemon Blueberry Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1675403.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package lemon pudding
2 cups milk
2 thick slices of pound cake (regular or lemon)
juice from one lemon
1 teaspoon sugar
whip cream
1/2 cup blueberries (or more if you'd like)

Directions:
Cut the crust off the pound cake slices.
Cut the pound cake slices into half-inch squares.  Divide the squares into four small (or two large) dessert dishes.
Make the lemon pudding according to the directions on the packaging (using the 2 cups milk) and pour on top of pound cake.  Chill in refrigerator.
Place blueberries in shallow bowl.  Squeeze lemon juice onto blueberries, then sprinkle sugar on top and chill for 15 minutes.
Remove pudding from fridge and divide blueberries between desserts, placing on top of pudding.  (reserve four blueberries for garnish)
Top with whipped cream and one blueberry.  Drizzle small amount of remaining lemon juice on whip cream for extra lemon flavor.
Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Donna
Description: 
Pound cake suspended in lemon pudding, topped with blueberries and whipped cream.
