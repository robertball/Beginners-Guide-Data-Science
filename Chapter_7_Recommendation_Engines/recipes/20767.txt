Title:
Melocoton con Limon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/631406.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger peach schnapps
1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger artificially sweetened citrus soda

Directions:
Fill a rocks glass 1/2 full of ice. Pour in the peach schnapps followed by the citrus soda. Stir.
