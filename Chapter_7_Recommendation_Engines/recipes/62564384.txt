Title:
Creamy Reuben Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. chopped onion
1/4 c. chopped celery
3 Tbsp. butter
1/4 c. flour
3 c. water
4 tsp. beef bouillon or 4 cubes
1.2 LB. shredded corned beef
1 c. well drained sauerkraut
3 c. half & half
3 c. shredded Swiss cheese
6-8 slices rye or pumpernickel bread. Toasted and cut into cubes.

Directions:
Cook onion and celery in butter in a large saucepan until tender, stir in flour until smooth. Gradually stir in water and bouillon, bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer uncovered 5 minutes. Add corned beef, sauerkraut, half & half and 1 c. cheese.
Cook about 30 minutes until slightly thickened, stirring frequently. Ladle into 8 oven proof bowls. Top each with bread and 1/4 c. cheese. Broil until cheese melts. Serve immediately.
