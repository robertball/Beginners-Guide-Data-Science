Title:
20 Calorie Sugar-Free Hot Cocoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T unsweetened cocoa powder
3 T Splenda
1/4 t. vanilla
1 C boiling water (or milk or 1/2 water, 1/2 milk)

Directions:
Put cocoa, sweetener and vanilla into mug. Pour in boiling water and stir

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
Can add boiling water, milk or 1/2 water and 1/2 milk to mixture. Simple recipe for quick hot cocoa.
