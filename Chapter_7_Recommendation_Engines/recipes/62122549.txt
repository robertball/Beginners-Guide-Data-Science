Title:
Chilla Nilla's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 Nilla Wafers
2 cups vanilla bean ice cream
assorted candy sprinkles (any type and flavor)

Directions:
Scoop spoonful of ice cream onto one Nilla wafer; top with another Nilla wafer.
Roll ice cream Nilla sandwich in sprinkles.
Freeze immediately in freezer for 30 mins.
Serve and enjoy!

Submitter: 
Kris Nelson Manley
Description: 
A yummy Nilla wafer ice cream sandwich rolled in candy sprinkles!
