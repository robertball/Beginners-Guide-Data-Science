Title:
Almondy Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (8 oz.) package cream cheese, room temperature
1 (7 oz.) jar marshmallow fluff
splash of Amaretto liquor
strawberries, grapes, pineapple, graham cracker sticks, etc. for dipping

Directions:
In bowl of electric mixer, beat cream cheese and marshmallow fluff on medium speed until well blended and smooth.  Add splash (or to taste) of Amaretto.  Beat until well mixed.

Submitter: 
S.Michael
Description: 
This creamy fruit dip is not only super-quick and easy to make, but so delicious you won't be able to stop dipping into this treat!  Plus it keeps for a while - if it lasts that long!
