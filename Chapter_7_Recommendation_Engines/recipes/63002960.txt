Title:
Mushrooms with Pumpkin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 oz Butter
2 T olive oil
1 onion thinly sliced
11 oz Diced pumpkin
1 lb 5oz mixed mushrooms (cut thick if large)
1/4 pint veggie stock
3 T fresh flat-leaf parsley chopped
1 T chopped fresh marjoram
Salt & Pepper

Directions:
Heat the butter with the olive oil in a pan, add the onion and cook over low heat stirring often for 5 mins. Add the pumpkin and mushrooms increase heat to high and cook for a few minutes. Meanwhile, heat the stock in a small saucepan. Season the mushroom mixture with S&P, reduce heat pour in the stock and cook until tender. Stir in herbs and serve warm
