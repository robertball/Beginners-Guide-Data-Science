Title:
Cheesy Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (1lb) pkg Shredded hash brown potatoes (cubed type don't work well )
1 Small onion chopped
2 cups Cheddar cheese cubed
1 cup Sour cream
1 TBS Salt
1 10 Oz can Cream of chicken soup
1 10 Oz can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup butter or mararine melted
2 cups cornflakes

Directions:
pre heat oven to 350
In a big bowl mix together , potatos , onion , cheese , sour cream and salt. In seperate bowl mix soups together , then combine with potato mixture. Mix well. Place in a 9 x 13 baking dish.
Mix cornflakes and melted butter and spread over surface of casserole.
Bake for 45 min to 60 min depending upon how brown you like to topping to get. Allow to set for 10 min after removing from oven

Submitter: 
Applewood
Description: 
Tasty and easy . A nice blend of potatos , cheese with a crunchy top. Travels well
