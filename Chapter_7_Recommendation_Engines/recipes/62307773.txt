Title:
Salmon Patties I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14.75 ounce) can canned salmon with broth
1 egg
1/2 cup seasoned dry bread crumbs or cornmeal
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon Old Bay Seasoning
Ground Black Pepper to taste(I use a lot)
1/4 cup virgin coconut oil or olive oil to fry.

Directions:
Mix egg, onion, celery, pappers,mayonnaise and Old Bay seasoning, black pepper, and   bread crumbs and salmon with broth together.
Make into patties.
In a  frying pan, heat oil. Place patties in pan.  Brown on each side, turning gently.  Drain on paper towels and serve.
Serve with a dipping sauce of mayonnaise, chopped onions and a little hot sauce.  Phenomenal!
