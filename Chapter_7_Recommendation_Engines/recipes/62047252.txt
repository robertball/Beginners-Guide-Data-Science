Title:
Best chocolate brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 tsp cinnamon
5 1/3 tablepoons butter
2 tablespoons water
12 ounces(2 cups) semisweet chocolate chips
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 eggs
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F; grease a 9-inch square baking pan. In a small bowl,combine flour, soda,cinnamon and  salt; set aside. In a medium saucepan, combine sugar, butter and water. Bring just to a boil. Remove from heat. Add 1 cup of chocolate chips and the vanilla. Stir until chocolate melts and mixture is smooth.
Add eggs, one at a time, to the chocolate mixture; mix well. Gradually blend in flour mixture. Stir in remaining chocolate chips and the walnuts.
Spread in the prepared pan. Bake for 30-35 minutes. Cool completely before cutting into 2 1/4-inch squares.
