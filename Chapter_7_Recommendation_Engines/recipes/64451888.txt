Title:
Turkey Mexican Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2390179.jpg
Ingredients:
8 six inch corn tortillas
12 ounces refried beans (or so)
2 cups cooked turkey breast or leftover turkey of any sort
1 medium onion
1 can green chilies
2 tomatoes
1/2 c guacamole
1/2 c salsa
1/2 c sour cream
1/2 c sliced olives
1 T garlic powder (or to taste)
1 T paprika (or to taste)
1 t chili powder (or to taste)
1 t cumin (or to taste)
8 oz shredded cheese

Directions:
Saut√©ed onions and herbs in olive oil while you dice the turkey.  Add turkey and 2 tablespoons of the chilies.  After about 5 minutes add tomatoes.  Bring meat mixture to a simmer and turn off. Meanwhile heat retried beans and prepare tortillas.  Spread beans on a tortilla and add cheese to taste.  Cover with another tortilla and bake at 350 for 25 to 30 minutes.  Remove from oven and let  cool for about 5 minutes then slice.  Garnish with salsa, olives, guacamole , sour cream, etc.  You will need napkins!

Submitter: 
badbabybeth
Description: 
This is a twist on the traditional Mexican pizza.  My recipe uses leftover turkey and all ingredients are optional/ to taste after that.  Great for little helpers in your family.  It's a delicious alternative to turkey casserole after Thanksgiving or other holidays!
