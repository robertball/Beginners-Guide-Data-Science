Title:
Orange Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tsp flour
2 tsp seasoning salt
1 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp pepper
4 pork chops
1 tsp cooking oil
1/2 cup orange juice
4 whole cloves
4 thin orange slices
2 Tbsp cold water

Directions:
Combine flour and seasonings - place in freezer bag. Place pork in separate freezer bag. Place orange juice and cloves in another freezer bag. Freeze.
Serving Day:
Reserve 1 tsp of flour mixture. Coat pork in flour mixture. Heat skillet with 1 tsp of cooking oil, then brown the chops. Add the orange juice and cloves to skillet. Cover and simmer for 45-60 minutes or until tender, turning occasionally.
When tender, add the orange slices and cook 5 minutes longer. Remove the chops and orange slices from skillet. Remove cloves and discard them. Blend reserved flour mixture into 2 Tbsp of water and pour slowly into hot skillet, stiring constantly. Cook until sauce slightly thickens and is bubbly. Serve chops with sauce.
