Title:
Date Night - Linguine Pescadoro Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/523751.jpg
Ingredients:
16 ounce fresh linguini pasta
3 tablespoons olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon oregano
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
1/4 cup of red wine (whatever you're pairing with dinner)
1 (6 ounce) can black olives, drained
1/4 pound small salad shrimp
1/4 pound scallops
2 1/2 teaspoons lemon zest
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
In a large pot of boiling salted water cook linguini until al dente. Drain.
Meanwhile, in a heavy saucepan over medium heat saute garlic in olive oil until garlic softens. Add oregano, thyme, crushed red pepper flakes, crushed tomatoes, black olives, and red wine. Simmer for 15 minutes. Mix in shrimp, scallops, lemon zest, and salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for an additional 15 minutes or until shrimp and scallops are cooked.
Toss cooked and drained linguine pasta over seafood sauce. Serve warm.
