Title:
Trishie's Chilly Chocolate Strawberry  Sorbet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
100g Dark Chocolate (one with 72% cocoa solids is best!)
100ml water
60g Cocoa Powder
6 Strawberries chopped
SUGAR SYRUP
250ml water
150g caster sugar

Directions:
Prepare sugar syrup by putting the sugar and water into a pot and bring to the boil without stirring.
Leave to boil for about 5 mins or until the sugar has dissolved.
Remove from heat.
While the sugar syrup is boiling, melt the chocolate in a heatproof bowl over a pot of barely simmering water.
Once the chocolate has melted, add the 100ml of water to the sugar syrup and reheat until warm.
Whisk in the cocoa powder.
Then add the melted chocolate whisking together until smooth.
Chill the mixture by placing it over a bowl of water filled with ice cubes and stir occasionally being careful not to get any water into the mixtutre.
Add the chopped Strawberries.
Churn in an ice cream maker following the manufacturer's instructions until smooth.

Submitter: 
Trishie
Description: 
An unusual combination of Chocolate, Strawberry and Sorbet but extremely refreshing and delicious!
