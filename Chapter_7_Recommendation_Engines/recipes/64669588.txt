Title:
Creamy Mexican Cheese Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 T olive oil
2 T butter
5 cloves garlic
1 yellow onion
1 T cumin
1 T smoked paprika
1/2 tsp chili powder
1 T oregano
4 oz organic valley pastured cream cheese
1 c raw sharp grassfed cheddar cheese
1 c organic valley pastured heavy cream
4 c low sodium chicken stock
juice of 1/2-1 lime
1 tsp salt
rotisserie chicken cut up

Directions:
heat olive oil and butter in sauce pan
saute onion and garlic until softened
add spices and saute until fragrant
add cream cheese, melt
add chicken stock, cheese and heavy cream
add lime juice and salt to taste
simmer on stovetop for soup or pour over chicken and bake

Submitter: 
seren
Description: 
easy Mexican cheese sauce for chicken or other; add sausage, ground beef or chicken for a soup
