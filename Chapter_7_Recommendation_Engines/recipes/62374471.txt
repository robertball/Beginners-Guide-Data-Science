Title:
Bread Bowl Hot Clam Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Country Semolina Round bread unsliced (or your fav bread)
2 8oz cans of minced clams** (drained-leaving a tsp of the juice remaining)
2 8oz cream cheese (softened)
1 pint sour cream
2 TB  bottled horseradish
1 Tb worchestershire sauce
5-6 dashes of hot pepper sauce
1 medium miced onion
1-2 cloves of garlic
1 TB dried parsley
salt/pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Beat cream cheese until fluffy, add sour cream horseradish,worchestshire,hot pepper sauce beat until combined.
Add onion, garlic, clams, parsley salt and pepper. mix well.
Dig in!
Slice the top of bread off (creating a lid) Use a serated knife and carve around the bread inside, and remove most all of the bread. Place the bowl on a double thickness aluminum foil (large enough to wrap the entire loaf.)
Pour the clam mixture in and replace the bread lid.
Baked for 40 minutes Will keep warm for 2-3 hours if left in aluminum foil.
Chop the inside bread into dipping cubes and cover (have extra crackers or Party pumpernickel) to serve.
