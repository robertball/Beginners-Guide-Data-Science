Title:
Mashed sweet potatoes with goat cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds sweet potato, peeled and cubed
1/2 cup half and half
1/4 cup butter
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp rubbed sage
2 oz goat cheese
maple syrup, optional, to taste

Directions:
1. Cook potatoes in water until tender, drain
2. Mash with milk and butter
3. stir in spices and cheese
4. add maple syrup if desired and serve
5. can be refrigerated and reheated at 350 degrees as a casserole later.

Submitter: 
HANMERD
Description: 
This is a savory dish for smooth and creamy sweet potatoes without all the sugar.
