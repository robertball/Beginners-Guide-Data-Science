Title:
Layered Jello Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/381515.jpg
Ingredients:
1 3oz package Orange JELLO
1 3oz package Lime JELLO
1 3oz package Blue Raspberry JELLO
1 3oz package Strawberry JELLO
6 Envelopes KNOX unflavored gelatin
1 cup sweetened condensed milk

Directions:
Mix 1 cup condensed milk with 1 cup hot water. Set aside.
Mix 2 envelopes Knox gelatin with 1 cup hot water; combine with milk mixture and set aside.
Combine blue raspberry JELLO, 1 envelope Knox gelatin and 1-1/2 cups hot water. Cool, then pour into a 9Ã—13 glass pan. Place in refrigerator to set for about 30 minutes.
Pour about 1 cup of the milk mixture for the next layer. Refrigerate to set. Each layer should only take about 15 minutes to set now.
Combine lime JELLO, 1 envelope Knox gelatin and 1-1/2 cup hot water. Cook, and pour carefully over the milk mixture, then place in the refrigerator for another 15 minutes.
Pour another cup of the milk mixture for the next layer. Refrigerate to set.
Combine orange JELLO, 1 envelope Knox gelatin and 1-1/2 cup hot water. Cook, and pour carefully over the milk mixture, then place in the refridgerator for another 15 minutes.
Pour the rest of the milk mixture for the next layer. Refrigerate to set.
Finally, Combine strawberry JELLO, 1 envelope Knox gelatin and 1-1/2 cup hot water. Cook, and pour carefully over the milk mixture, then place in the refrigerator for 15 minutes until set or ready to serve.
Cut into squares and enjoy!
