Title:
Quick and Easy Vegetable and Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cooked rice
1/4 cup butter
1 onion, chopped
1 cup chopped carrots
2 1/2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup rice flour
6 cups chicken broth
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon white pepper
1/2 teaspoon curry powder
1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon dried chervil
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, for garnish

Directions:
Heat butter in a large saucepan over medium heat.  Saute onion until golden brown; add mushrooms, carrots, and celery.  Cook 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
Reduce heat to low; stir in flour and cook, stirring constantly, until mixture is bubbly.  Gradually add broth; increase heat to medium-high and bring to a boil.  Boil, stirring, for 1 minute.
Reduce heat to low and add cooked rice, salt, white pepper, curry powder, dry mustard, paprika and chervil.  Simmer until heated through.  Serve hot and garnish with parsley.

Submitter: 
MONIQUEWS
Description: 
This veggie soup is super easy for kids to make. Celery, onions, carrots and mushrooms are simmered with curry, mustard, paprika and chervil.Modified from: Curried Wild Rice Soup
