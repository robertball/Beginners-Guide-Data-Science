Title:
mu shu chicken lettuce wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbs lower sodium soy sauce
1 tbs dry sherry
1 tbs hoisin sauce
1 tsp rice vinegar
1 tbs dark sesame oil
1 tbs minced garlic
1 tsp minced peeled fresh ginger
1 14 ounce pkg coleslaw (about 4 cups)
6 oz shredded skinless boneless rotisserie chicken breast (about 1 1/2 cups
1/2 cup sliced green onions divided
lettuce leaves
1/4 cup chopped cashews

Directions:
1. combine 1st 4 ingredients (through rice vinegar)
2. Heat large skillet over med-high heat, add oil and swirl to coat. Add garlic and ginger saute 30 seconds, Add soy sauce mixture and coleslaw; stirring frequently, cook 1 minute; add chicken and 1/4 cup onions cook 1 minute or until coleslaw just begins to wilt. Wrap with lettuce leaves, sprinkle with remaining onions and cashews

Submitter: 
Soccercoach
Description: 
I was looking for lettuce wraps like I find here in Asian restaurants but found this instead. Not the lettuce wrap I was looking for, but a very good substitute. Quick and easy to make, low cal and low fat.
