Title:
Cheeseburger Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 small onion, chopped
1 small green pepper chopped
1 can (8 oz. size) tomato sauce
1/4 cup catsup
1/8 teaspoon pepper
5 slices American cheese
1 can (8 oz. size) refrigerated biscuits

Directions:
In large skillet, break up beef and brown with onion.
Drain off fat.
Stir in tomato sauce, catsup and pepper; heat until bubbly.
Grease an 8 inch square baking pan.
Spread half the meat mixture in pan; top with a layer of 4 cheese slices, then cover with remaining meat.
Arrange biscuits around edge; place fifth cheese slice in center.
Bake at 400degrees F
