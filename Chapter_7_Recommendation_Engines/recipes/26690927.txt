Title:
Mexican Fruit Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CAKE:
2 C. flour
2 tsp.soda
2 C. sugar
1 C. chopped nuts
1 20 oz. can crushed pineapple and juice.
2 eggs, slightly beaten
*NO SHORTENING IS USED.
FROSTING:
1 stick soft oleo
8 oz. cream cheese, at room temperature.
1 tsp. vanilla
2 1/2 C. powdered sugar.

Directions:
CAKE: Place all in gredients in bowl and mix well. Bake in a 9x13 inch pan (slightly grease)(I use Pam Spray) for 30-35 minutes bake  @ 350'(I bake alittle longer)
FROSTING: Mix well and put on cool cake. Keep in refrigerator.
