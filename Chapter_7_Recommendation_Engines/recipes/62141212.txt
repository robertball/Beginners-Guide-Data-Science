Title:
Simple Spaghetti Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp vegetable oil
1 medium onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 lb ground beef
2 - 398 ml cans of tomato sauce
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp basil
1/8 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp Italian seasoning

Directions:
Cook onion and garlic in oil over medium heat 5 minutes.
Add beef and brown.
Add tomato sauce and seasonings.
Bring to boil stirring frequently.
Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 30 to 45 minutes until desired consistency.
Serve with spaghetti or use to make lasagne.
