Title:
Lick Your Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4-6 pork chops (bone in or butterfly)
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
1/2 cup Extra virgin olive oil
1/4 cup yellow mustard
3-6 T. worcestershire sauce (to taste)
ground red pepper (to taste)
1-2 green onions
1 clove garlic or 1 T. minced garlic
1 t. salt

Directions:
1) Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2) I medium bowl mix brown sugar and oil until brown sugar is moist.  Add mustard and worcsetershire sauce mix well.  Make sure it is pasty not clumpy, add more oil if needed.  Add pepper, onions, garlic, and salt.
3) Place chops in glass baking dish.  Baste chops generously with paste mixture.
4) Bake for 45 min to 1 hour until chops are cooked though.  Sauce will be very bubbly.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Rico
Description: 
This is a pork chop sauce that is sweet, tangy, and has a bite.  Will make your pork chops melt in your mouth and yes you will be licking your chops.  My husband hates pork but loves my pork chops.  These are good on a bed of brown rice with carrots and peas.
