Title:
Sour Cream Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Chicken breast half, cooked and cut into bite-sized pieces
1 can Cr. Of Chicken and mushroom soup
1 tub sour cream.
1 cup rice, cooked

Directions:
Mix chicken, soup and sour cream in sauce pan. Heat.
Serve over rice

Submitter: 
Marianne Jones Islas
Description: 
Chicken, soup, and sour cream, this is one of the easiest dishes ever. I like to play with it and add a little variety. My husband loves it!
