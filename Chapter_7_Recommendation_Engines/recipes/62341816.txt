Title:
Chicken Ole! Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup chunky salsa
1/4 cup country dijon mustard
2 Tablespoons lime juice
6 boneless, skinless, chicken thighs
2 Tablespoons butter
sour cream

Directions:
Combine salsa, mustard and lime juice in large bowl.  Add chicken, turning to coat.  Cover and marinate in refrigerator, at least 30 minutes.
Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat until foamy.  Remove chicken from marinade.  Reserve marinade.
Add chicken to skillet.  Cook 5-7 minutes.  Flip chicken and cook another 5-7 minutes.
Add reserved marinade back into pan.  Cook 5 minutes or until chicken is tender and marinade begins to glaze the chicken.
Remove chicken to a serving platter.  Boil remaining marinade on high for 1 minute.  Pour over chicken.
Serve with sour cream.
