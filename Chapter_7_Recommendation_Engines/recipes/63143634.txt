Title:
Crab Toasties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
CRAB MUFFIN TOASTIES
8 oz. cooked crab meat
5 oz. Old English cheese spread
2 tbsp. mayonnaise
1/2 c. butter, softened
1/2 tsp. onion powder
1/8 tsp. garlic powder
6 English muffins

Directions:
Combine first 6 ingredients in bowl; mix well. Spread on muffin halves. Cut each into 6 wedges. Arrange on baking sheet. Broil until brown. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Frank Pross
