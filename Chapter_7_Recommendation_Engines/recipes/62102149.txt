Title:
Shredded turkey barbecue Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 bone-in turkey thighs skinned
2 16oz can of baked beans
1 1/2 cups finely chopped onions
1 cup of bottled barbecue sauce
1 Tbsp yellow mustard
1 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp salt
8 potato or kaiser rolls
cole slaw if your from the south!

Directions:
mix everything but the rolls and the cole slaw in your crockpot. cook 10-12 hours on low. Remove turkey and shred meat by using two forks. Discard bones. Put shreddy yummy turkey back in the crockpot and line'em up for sandwiches. Freezes very well!
