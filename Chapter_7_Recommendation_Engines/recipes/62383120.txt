Title:
Big Daddy's Cantaloupe and Melon Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cantaloupe - peeled, seeded and cubed
1/4 honeydew melon - peeled, seeded and cubed
1 lemon, juiced
2 scoops of Mila
2 tablespoons sugar

Directions:
In a blender, combine cantaloupe, honeydew, lime juice, Mila and sugar. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Submitter:

Description: 
Cantaloupe and honeydew melon is blended with lemon juice and sugar. This one is great when melons are in season.Modified from: Melon Smoothie
