Title:
Ranch Dressing with Fresh Herbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 clove garlic, minced
2 tablespoons minced red bell pepper
4 green onions, minced
1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley leaves
2 teaspoons minced fresh dill
1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
fresh ground black pepper to taste
1/2 cup buttermilk
3/4 cup mayonnaise

Directions:
Sprinkle the salt over the minced garlic in the bottom of a mixing bowl; make a paste, rubbing the salt into the garlic thoroughly with the back of a spoon. Add the red bell pepper, green onion,parsley, dill, and lemon juice; mix lightly until well combined. Season with black pepper. Add the buttermilk,and mayonnaise to the mixture; beat with a wire whisk until evenly combined. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 2 hours. Dressing keeps refrigerated up to 1 week.
