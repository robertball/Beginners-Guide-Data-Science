Title:
Red Enchilladas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large chicken breasts cooked and shredded
1 15oz can La Victoria red enchilada sauce
1 8 oz enchilada sauce
corn tortillas
1 medium brick jack cheese
1 large bouillon cube

Directions:
heat enchilada sauce add bouillon cube and dissolve
add some sauce to the shredded chicken and some on the bottom of a 13x9
spoon some sauce onto a plate, lay one tortilla on plate add chicken and some cheese, roll and add to 13x9
cover enchiladas with sauce and cheese cover with foil and bake at 375 for 20, bake uncovered until cheese is melted
