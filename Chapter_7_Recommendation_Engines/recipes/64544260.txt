Title:
Jimmies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Milk
Vanilla
Jam/Jelly/Preserve (optional)
1 cup butter
1/4 cup Milk
2 cups flour
½ cup white sugar
¼ cup brown sugar

Directions:
Mix sugar and butter in a bowl, add flour (if needed, add more), mix again
Add a spoonful of vanilla, a spoonful of preserve (optional), and milk (add more as needed), mix
Break dough into tea-candle sized pieces and place on cookie sheets
Bake at 350 degrees for about 13 minutes
