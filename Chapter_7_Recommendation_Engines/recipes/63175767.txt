Title:
Cafe Rio Sweet Pork Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Sweet Pork:
6 pounds pork loin
32 ounces Pace picante salsa
2 cans Coke
3 cups brown sugar
Green Sauce:
1 packet Buttermilk Ranch dressing, prepared as directed
2 tomatillos
1/4 bunch cilantro
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1/4 cup lime juice
1 jalepeno
Toppings/Other:
24 flour tortillas
1 head lettuce, chopped
30 ounces black beans
2 cups fresh salsa

Directions:
Place pork in a crock pot and fill 1/2 way up the pork with water.  Cook on high for 5 hours or low overnight.  Drain off the liquid.
Mix together remaining ingredients.  Cut pork into thirds and return to crock pot.  Pour sauce over pork and cook an additional 3 hours on high.
Shred pork with forks.  Leave on low in crock pot until ready to serve.
Prepare Green Sauce.  Remove seeds from jalepeno for less heat, leave seeds for more.  Place all ingredients in a food processor and blend until smooth.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.
Serve over tortillas with lettuce, black beans, fresh salsa and topped with green sauce.
