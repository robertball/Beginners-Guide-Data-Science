Title:
Hawaiian Roll Sandwiches Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages Hawaiian Rolls w/12 rolls in each
10 slices American cheese
10 slices Swiss cheese
1 15 oz Ham sandwich meat
2 sticks butter
2 T minced onions
2 T poppy seeds
2 t mustard
2 t Worcestershire sauce

Directions:
Slice rolls in half. Place in 13x9" casserole dish. Layer bread with American cheese, then meat, then Swiss cheese. Place top layer of bread on next. In small saucepan add remaining ingredients and heat until butter is melted, stirring often. Pour glaze over bread. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 to 15 minutes or until lightly browned.
