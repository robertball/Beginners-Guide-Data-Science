Title:
Dragon Fruit Mojito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/6744288.jpg
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons white sugar
2 teaspoons white sugar
16 leaves fresh mint
1 cup light rum
1 dragon fruit, halved and flesh removed
1 liter chilled club soda

Directions:
Mix 2 tablespoons plus 2 teaspoons sugar with mint in the bottom of a highball glass.
Combine rum and dragon fruit flesh in a blender; blend until smooth. Pour rum mixture over the muddled mint mixture. Stir to incorporate.
Divide mixture between 4 double old-fashioned glasses. Top each drink with equal amounts of club soda.

Submitter: 
thedailygourmet
Description: 
This mojito kicks the traditional lime to the curb and replaces it with dragon fruit. To get a unique 'serving container' save the fruit outer peel and once the flesh is removed, use the empty halves as a beverage glass.
