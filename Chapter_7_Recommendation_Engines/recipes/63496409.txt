Title:
Bacon Cheddar Appetizers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups finely chredded sharp cheddar
1/2 cup mayo
1/4 cup bacon bits
3 tablespoons finely chopped onion
60 crackers

Directions:
1. Mix all ingred. except crackers.
2. Top each cracker with about 1 teaspoon mixture.
3. Broil 2 minutes or until cheese melts.
