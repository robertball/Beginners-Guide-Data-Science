Title:
Mama's Kitchen Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Chicken Stock w/ Chicken
2 Stalks Celery, Sliced
2 Large Carrots, Sliced
2 Large Potatoes, Diced
2 Tomatoes, Diced
1 C Fresh Green Beans, Cut into 1 in Pieces
1/4 C Sliced Onion
2 C Swanson Chicken Broth
Salt & Pepper to taste
1/2 Tsp Dried Parsley
2 C Egg Noodles

Directions:
Combine Stock, Chicken, Celery, Carrots, Potatoes, Green Beans, Onion, Broth, desired amount of Salt & Pepper, and Parsley in slow cooker. Simmer on low for two hours or until vegetables are tender. 1/2 Hour before serving add tomatoes and egg noodles.

Submitter: 
Crystal Dickson
Description: 
For all of you out there who just dont have the time, energy, and money to make that yummy homemade soup, this recipie is for you
