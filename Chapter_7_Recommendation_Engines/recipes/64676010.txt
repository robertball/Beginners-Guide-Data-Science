Title:
Linguine with Shrimp Scampi Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds linguine
6 tablespoons unsalted butter
5 tablespoons olive oil
3 tablespoons minced garlic
2 pounds large shrimp, peeled and deveined
1/2 teaspoon ground pepper
2/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
1/2 cup lemon juice
1/2 lemon, sliced thinly
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Directions:
Cook linguine
Melt butter and olive oil in heavy skillet
Add garlic, saute
Add the shrimp, salt/pepper; saute until shrimp is pink
Remove from heat
Add parsley, lemon zest, lemon juice, lemon slices and red pepper flakes
Drain pasta and add to shrimp
Offer parmesan cheese over serving
