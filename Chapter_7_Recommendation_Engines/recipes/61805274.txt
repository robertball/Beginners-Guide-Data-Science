Title:
Taco Seasoning I Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon chili powder
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon cheyenne Pepper
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 teaspoon sea salt
1 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 cup Flour

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix together chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, cheyenne pepper, oregano, paprika, cumin, salt.pepper and flour. Store in an airtight container in frig.

Submitter: 
Monica
Description: 
Depending on how spicy you and your family like your dishes, use as little or as much as you want.Modified from: Taco Seasoning I
