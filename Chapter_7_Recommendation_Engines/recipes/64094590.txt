Title:
Spicy Pumpkin Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-1/2 cups flour
1/2 cup sugar
2 tsp baking powder
3/4 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1/4 tsp ground cloves
1/2 cup raisins (optional)
1 egg
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup canned pumpkin
1/4 cup oil
2-1/2 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
Stir flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, 1 tsp cinnamon, 1/2 tsp ginger and 1/4 tsp cloves. Mix in raisins.  Set aside.
In another bowl, beat egg, milk, pumpkin and oil. Add to dry ingredients and stir only until combined.
Fill greased muffin cups 2/3 full.  Mix 2-1/2 tsp sugar and 1/2 tsp cinnamon and sprinkle on muffins.  Bake at 400 degrees F for 20-25 minutes.
