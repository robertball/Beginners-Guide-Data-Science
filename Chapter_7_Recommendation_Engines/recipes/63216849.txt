Title:
Rita's Cornbread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons vegetable oil (melted butter or bacon drippings)
1 1/2 cups Rita's self-rising cornmeal mix*
1/2 cup flour*
2 egg*
1/2 cup water
1 1/2 tablespoon of honey or 3 tablespoons of sugar*

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 add skillet to warm it as well. I melt my butter or drips in the skillet as well.
Mix the dry ingredients in a  meduim bowl.
Mix the eggs and buttermilk in a separate bowl.
Add the wet ingredients to the center of the dry ingredients, and mix lightly together with a wooden spoon.
Continue to lightly beat everything together ingredients, adding butter.
Pour mixture into a greased pan. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes in a 375 to 400 degree oven until you can stick a toothpick in the center of the corn bread and the toothpick comes out clean.

I have always made this in my #8 cast iron skillet which is about 10 1/2 round. Or made this thinner batter a cooked them like flap-jacks.
