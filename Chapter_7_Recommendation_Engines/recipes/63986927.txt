Title:
Big Ray's Creamy Oven-Roasted Cauliflower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1106285.jpg
Ingredients:
1 medium cauliflower, cut into florets
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
2 Tbl cream cheese, softened
2 tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
2 tbsp parmesan cheese, grated
1 chives, Chopped, for garnish

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 500 degrees F.
Place the cauliflower florets in a large saute pan or a roasting pan. Drizzle the olive oil over the cauliflower, and season with the garlic, lemon juice, salt and pepper. Place the saute/roasting pan in the oven and cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally to ensure even roasting. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with the Parmesan and cream cheese.   Mix to cover.  Garnish with chopped chives and serve immediately while still warm.
