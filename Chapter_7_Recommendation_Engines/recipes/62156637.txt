Title:
Southwest Black Bean Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8-10 oz whole wheat penne pasta, cooked to aldente
2 Tbl. olive oil
1/2 c. finely chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 c. chicken broth
8 oz velveeta cheese, diced
1 Tbl. chipotle in adobo, finely chopped
1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
1 large or 2 small tomatoes diced
2 chicken breasts, grilled and sliced

Directions:
Saute onions and garlic in olive oil until soft, about 5 minutes. Add chicken broth and heat through. Add diced cheese and whisk until cheese is completely melted. Add chipotle peppers. If sauce is too thin, add a mixture of 1/4 c. cold water mixed with 2 Tbl. corn starch(add this mixture slowly to hot sauce until desired thickness. Add pasta, black beans, and chopped tomatoes. Serve with sliced chicken over the top or mixed in. Enjoy!!!

Submitter: 
Heather Spickard
Description: 
This is a great mildly spicy pasta dish that ended up tasting just like a famous resturant's pasta dish. Cheesy, but not too cheesy, the flavor is great with or without the chicken in it. You can also adjust the heat by adding more chipotle peppers. Enjoy!!!
