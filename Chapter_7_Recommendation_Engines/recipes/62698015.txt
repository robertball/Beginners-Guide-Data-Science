Title:
Crispy Mashed Potato Pancakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/609345.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cups cold mashed potatoes (prepared with milk and butter)
small onion, finely chopped
1 egg, lightly beaten
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
Good sprinkle Italian seasoning
sprinkle of thyme
sprinkle of garlic powder
sprinkle of onion powder
pepper to taste
Paprika
3 tablespoons olive or vegetable oil

Directions:
Combine all ingredients but paprika and oil; mix well. In a small skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add potato mixture; press with a spatula to flatten evenly. Sprinkle with Paprika. Cover and cook for 8 minutes or until bottom is crispy. flip and cook until brown. Invert onto a serving plate.
Serve with sour cream
