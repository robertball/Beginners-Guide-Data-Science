Title:
Chicken Parisian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 packages 10oz frozen broccoli thawed and drained or fresh brocolli cooked till  crisp-tender
1-1 1/2 pounds boneless chicken breasts
6 slices american cheese
1 10 1/2 can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup sour cream
1/3 cup white wine
1 tablespoon butter
1/4 cup bread crumbs

Directions:
Put chicken breats in pan filled 2/3 with water. Bring water to a boil.  Simmer for 15-20 minutes. While chicken is simmering, place broccoli in a 11x7 or 10 inch square pan. Once chicken is simmered and cooled, cut chicken into one inch stips and place over broccoli.  Cover chicken with the 6 slices of amercian cheese.  Mix together the mushroom soup, sour cream, and wine.  Pour over cheese and cover completely with sauce.
Bake in 350 degree oven uncovered for 30 minutes.  Melt butter and add bread crumb.  Mix well.  Pour buttered crumbs on top of casserole and bake another 15 minutes.

Submitter: 
Nancy Toth
Description: 
When I serve this meal, there are never any leftovers! My family loves this and many friends have asked for the recipe. I serve this with white rice and use the sauce as gravy.
