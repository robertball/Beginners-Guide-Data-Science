Title:
Lemon Pie Filling Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2- 3 oz pkg of Lemon Jello
2 c boiling water
1/2 c cold water
1 large can crushed pineapple w/ juice
1 -21 oz can lemon pie filling
1/4 tsp lemon flavoring

Directions:
Dissolve jello in boiling water, add cold water.  Stir in pie filling, crushed pineapple and flavoring put in 9x13 dish and refrigerate until set. So good that it can be used for dessert with whipped topping or whipped cream.

Submitter: 
LIVEMORE
Description: 
This is very easy and can be used with Lady Fingers or pound cake like a short cake.
