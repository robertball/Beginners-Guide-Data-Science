Title:
Key Lime Cheesecake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz pkg cream cheese, softened
1 14 oz can sweetened condensed milk
3 egg yolks
1/2 c. key lime juice
2 tsp lime zest
1- 8" store bought graham cracker pie crust
1 8 oz tub whipped topping

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350'. Place cream cheese in mixing bowl, beat until smooth and creamy.  Add the egg yolks and sweetened condensed milk and combine thoroughly.  Add lime juice and zest and mix until just combined.  Pour into pie shell and bake at 350' oven for 20 minutes or until pie is set.  Remove from  oven and cool, then refrigerate at least 2 hours.  When ready to serve, cover pie with whipped topping. Cover and refrigerate remaining pie
