Title:
BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup ketchup
1/2 cup prepared mustard
3 teaspoons minced garlic
3 teaspoons minced dehydrated or fresh onions
3 teaspoons honey
4 teaspoons lemon juice
2 teaspoons liquid smoke flavoring

Directions:
In a saucepan over medium heat, mix together the ketchup, mustard, garlic, honey, lime juice, and liquid smoke. Bring to a gentle boil, and simmer for about 10 minutes. Set aside to allow flavors to mingle.
