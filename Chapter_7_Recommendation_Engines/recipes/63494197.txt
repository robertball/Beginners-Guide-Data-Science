Title:
Chicken Pillows Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/953516.jpg
Ingredients:
- 3 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 2 tablespoons butter, melted
- 1/4 cup celery, chopped fine
- 1 cup chicken, cooked and chopped
- 1/8 teaspoon pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 can cream of chicken soup
> 1 can Crescent rolls, refrigerated
(you can use 1 can of Grands Crescent Rolls if you want six larger pillows rather than 8)

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350*
2. Carefully mix cream cheese and melted butter together, using an electric mixer.
3. Spoon in celery,  onion, chicken and seasonings and stir to blend.
4.Carefully open crescent rolls and lay each triangle out on the counter.
5. Place about 2 tablespoons of chicken mixture on the wide end of the dough.
6. Roll up and tuck in sides as you roll. You will need to pinch the edges to seal them.
7.  Bake at 350* on ungreased cookie sheet for 15
minutes or until the rolls are golden brown.
8. Dilute 1 can cream of chicken soup with about 1.2 cup of milk. Heat to blend. Serve over chicken pillows.
