Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large onion, sliced
3 carrots, sliced
4 celery stalks, sliced
2 teaspoons salt
1 tsp. garlic salt
½ teaspoon pepper
½ teaspoon thyme
1 tablespoon parsley
4 cups water
4-5 chicken breasts, skinned and deboned
3 ½ cups noodles

Directions:
Place all vegetables, spices and water in crockpot, mixing it all up.  Place chicken on top and push down slightly.  Cover and cook on LOW for 8 hours (HIGH 4 hours).
Add noodles and cook on HIGH another 30-45 minutes until noodles are cooked.

Submitter: 
CHER
Description: 
This is a hearty and delicious chicken noodle soup that grandma couldn't have made better.  It really hits the spot on a chilly evening.
