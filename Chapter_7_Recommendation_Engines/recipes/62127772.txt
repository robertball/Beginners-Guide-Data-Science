Title:
Southwestern skillet turkey & shells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground turkey
1 1/2 cups medium shell pasta
1 cup dark red kidney beans, drained
1 cup vacuum packed corn, drained
1 envelope taco seasoning
1/2 cup taco sauce
1/2 cup canned diced tomatoes
shredded cheese to taste (cheddar or mexican blend)
1 small can vegetable juice
2 taco shells, crushed (or substitute tortilla chips)

Directions:
cook and drain shell pasta
brown and drain turkey in a large skillet.
add taco seasoning and water as directed on envelope.
Stir in cooked shell pasta
Add all other ingredients except cheese and crushed taco shells
Heat through, adding vegetable juice if too dry
Serve topped with shredded cheese and crushed taco chips

Submitter: 
Joni Zalot
Description: 
A delicious and easy to prepare skillet meal including ground turkey, shell pasta, corn, beans, tomatoes and southwestern seasonings topped with cheese and crushed taco shells.
