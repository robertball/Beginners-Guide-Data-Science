Title:
Caesar Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6040822.jpg
Ingredients:
1 c. oil (only use 1/4 c olive oil, the rest vegetable  or canola )
1/3 c. vinegar
1 entire bulb garlic, peeled (can use less)
1/2 lemon, juiced
1 can flat anchovies, drained
1/3 shredded parmesan  cheese
1/2 tsp dry mustard

Directions:
Process garlic in food processor. Add anchovies and process.
Add rest of the ingredients and process.
Season with salt and pepper.
Pour into a jar and refrigerate. Shake well
