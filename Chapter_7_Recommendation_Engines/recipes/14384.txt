Title:
Linda's Blue Cheese Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/877690.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup mayonnaise
1 cup sour cream
1 cup buttermilk
4 ounces blue cheese, crumbled
1 tablespoon hot pepper sauce
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
1 tablespoon dried parsley

Directions:
Wisk together the mayonnaise, sour cream, buttermilk, hot sauce, garlic powder, Worcestershire sauce, parmesan cheese and parsley flakes. Add blue cheese, mix and refrigerate until chilled.

Submitter: 
Charlotte
Description: 
This goes great on salads as well as chicken wings, especially the hot and spicy ones.
