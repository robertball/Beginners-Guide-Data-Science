Title:
Onions Stuffed and Baked Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 medium sized onions
1/4 C. butter
1/4 C. light cream
1/4 C. black olives, chopped
2 Tablespoons Pecans, chopped
1/4 teaspoon  Salt
Salad oil
Dry Bread Crumbs
Paprika

Directions:
Peel the onions.
Cut a thick slice from the top of each and reserve.
Cut out the center of each onion and reserve.
Cook onion shells in a large amount of boiling, salted water until tender. (20-25 min.)Watch carefully.
Remove the onion from the water and drain upside down.  Brush outside of onion shells with oil and sprinkle with paprika.
Chop the reserved onion coarsely and cook in 1 tablespoon of  butter until tender.
Stir in the light cream, chopped olives, chopped pecans and salt.
Spoon the mixture into the onion shells.
Sprink each onion with bread crumbs and drizzle with 3 tablespoons of melted butter.
Dash with paprika and bake at 350 for 20 mins in a lightly oiled casserole dish uncovered.

Submitter: 
PJ's kitchen
Description: 
Takes a little extra work but well worth the effort. Nice accompaniment for roast beef or roast pork.
