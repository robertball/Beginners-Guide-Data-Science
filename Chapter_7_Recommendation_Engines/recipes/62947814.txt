Title:
Pizza Casserole (low sodium) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. penne or ziti pasta
1 each: yellow & red pepper, yellow squash & zucchini diced
1 lb ground chicken breast
1 (14 oz.) can of No Salt Added diced tomatoes
1 (8 oz.) can of No Salt Added tomato sauce
2 TBLS olive oil
3 TBLS Italian Seasoning (or Mrs. Dash Italian seasoning)
1 TBLS garlic poweder (or Mrs. Dash Garlic & Herb seasoning)
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375
Cook pasta according to box instructions
Prepare 9x13 pan with non-stick spray
Heat 1 TBLS of olive oil in large skillet over medium heat, add vegetables and garlic seasoning, saute until tender, stirring often (about 2 minutes)& set aside
Add remaining oil and saute chicken until brown (about 5 minutes)
Add diced tomatoes, tomato sauce & Italian seasoning
Stir all ingredients together, mixing well
Pour into 9x13 pan, sprinkle with cheese and bake in oven for about 15 minutes or until cheese melts
