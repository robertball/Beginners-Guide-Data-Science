Title:
Yummy Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 onion
2 shallots
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon basil
3 cloves garlic, crushed
1 pound cubed beef meat
14.5 ounces chicken broth
3 carrots, peeled and cut into 1/2 inch pieces
2 parsnips, peeled and cut into 1/2 inch pieces
3 medium turnips, peeled and cut into 1/2 inch pieces
2 stalks celery, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1 medium sweet potato, peeled and cut into 1/2 inch pieces
4 white potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1/2 cup frozen peas
1 (12 fluid ounce) bottle beer

Directions:
Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Place onion and shallots in pot, and season with salt and basil. Mix in garlic. Cook and stir until tender. Mix in the beef, and cook until evenly brown. Reduce heat to low, and pour in chicken broth. Simmer 30 minutes.
Mix in the carrots, parsnips, turnips, celery and sweet potato. Pour in the beer. Cover, and cook 2 hours over low heat.
Stir potatoes into pot, and continue cooking 30 minutes, or until potatoes are tender.
Add peas and stir. Let sit for 5 minutes and serve.
