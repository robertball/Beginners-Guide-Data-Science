Title:
Tapenade Romeo Cucina Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 garlic cloves
1 oz drained flat anchovies
1 1/2 c pitted,drained Kalamata olives (10oz jar)
3 Tbsp drained capers
1/2 tsp dried rosemary
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/8 cup olive oil

Directions:
Blend in  food processor with salt and pepper to taste until mixture is ground coarse. Transfer to ramekin and serve with bread or crackers.
