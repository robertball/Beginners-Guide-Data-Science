Title:
Tasty Tater Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/976473.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds red potatoes, peeled
1 teaspoon salt
1 clove garlic, minced
2 tablespoons shredded carrots
1/4 cup sweet pickle relish
1/2 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Dressing
1/2 teaspoon celery seed (optional)
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Place potatoes and salt in medium saucepan and cover with cold water.  Bring to a boil then reduce heat to simmer. Cook 10 minutes or until potatoes are tender.  Drain and let cool.
Place potatoes in large bowl. Roughly break up potato slices into half-inch pieces.  Add remaining ingredients. Toss gently until well mixed.  Cover and refrigerate until serving time.

Submitter: 
Hidden Valley® Ranch
Description: 
Potatoes, creamy Ranch dressing, shredded carrots, and sweet pickle relish make up this tasty tater salad.
