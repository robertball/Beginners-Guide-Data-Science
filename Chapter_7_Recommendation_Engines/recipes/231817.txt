Title:
Greek Green Bean Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/995456.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 pound fresh green beans, trimmed, blanched
1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
1 red bell pepper, coarsely chopped
1/2 cup finely chopped red onion
1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 cup KRAFT Calorie-Wise Greek with Feta & Oregano Dressing & Marinade
1/3 cup Kraft Feta with Oregano, Sun Dried Tomatoes and Cracked Peppercorns Cheese

Directions:
Combine first 6 ingredients in large bowl.
Add dressing; toss to coat.
Top with cheese.

Submitter: 
KRAFT Shredded Cheese
Description: 
A new twist on a summertime favorite: green beans, cherry tomatoes are tossed with fresh parsley feta cheese and a greek dressing to make a healthy green bean salad.
