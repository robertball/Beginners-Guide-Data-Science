Title:
Scented Bath Salts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups Epsom Salts
1 cup baking soda
4 drops desired scented oil
2" x 4" inch scrap cotton fabric
Also needed:  Food coloring, ccokie sheet, jar or decorative container.

Directions:
In a small bowl, mix Epson salts and baking soda.  Stir.  Dab drops of scent on fabric and place in mixture.  Add drops of food coloring if desired.  Spread mixture on cookie sheet for several hours until dry, stirring occasionally.  Spoon into clean jar.  Use several teaspoons in bathwater.
