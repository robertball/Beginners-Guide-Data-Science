Title:
Lentil and Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 ounces dried lentils
2 (14 ounce) cans beef broth
16 ounces ground Italian sausage
1 cup chopped carrots
1 onion, chopped
1 bay leaf
salt to taste
ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Heat small amount of oil in soup pot.  Brown sausage in the oil.
Pour beef broth in soup pot. Bring to simmering boil while adding diced onion. Add bay leaf, salt and pepper to taste, sliced carrots and continue simmering for for 10 minutes. Rinse lentils and add to simmering pot. Simmer until peas are soft. Adjust salt and pepper to taste. Serve warm.

Submitter: 
Carol A
Description: 
Lentils are cooked with Italian sausage in beef broth  with onions, carrots and a bay leaf in this soup.Modified from: Split Pea and Sausage Soup
