Title:
Buttermilk Herb Chicken Breasts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup buttermilk
1 Tbsp Dijob mustard
1 Tbsp honey
1 Tbsp fresh rosemary, finely chopped
1/2 tsp dried thyme
1/2 tsp dried marjoram
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp salt
8 boneless chicken breasts

Directions:
Cooking Day:
Mix together buttermilk with mustard, honey and seasonings.  Place chicken in freezer bag.  Pour marinade over chicken breasts.  Freeze.
Serving Day:
Thaw chicken, discard additional marinade.  Grill over medium heat until chicken is tender and juices run clear.

Submitter: 
Ciara Gavin
Description: 
This is designed as a make-ahead freezer meal.  Recipe from the book, Don't Panic, Dinner's in the Freezer.
