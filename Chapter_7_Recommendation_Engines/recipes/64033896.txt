Title:
Jean's Whole Hard Wheat Bread with Flax Seed Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1121095.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups hot water
3/4 cup olive oil
1/2 cup honey
2 Tbsp. unrefined sea salt
1 Tbsp. lecithin
1 Tbsp. whey
1 tsp. ascorbic acid
1 cup gluten flour
4 cups hard whole wheat flour
12 cups spelt flour
3 Tbsp. instant yeast
1 cup golden flax seed

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 170 F.
Add to the mixer items 1 to 7 and mix at speed 1 for 30 seconds.
Turn to speed 2 and slowly add the cup of gluten flour, 4 cups of hard wheat flour and 6 cups of spelt flour.
Add the yeast and slowly add spelt flour, one cup at a time, until the dough does not stick to the side of the bowl. Mix dough for 10 minutes.
Add the flax seed at the 8 minutes time (2 minutes left on the timer).
Turn dough onto oiled countertop with oiled hand, then divide dough into 4 equal bundles, shape into loaves and place in oiled bread pans (coconut oil).
Put loaves into warm oven (170 F) to rise for 30 min. Keep pans in the oven and turn oven to 375 F. Cook for 35 min.
Remove loaves from pans and cool on racks on their side.
