Title:
Taco Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1 small onion, chopped
3/4 cup water
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
1 can chopped tomatoes
1 can sliced olives
1 (8 ounce) package shell macaroni, cooked and drained
1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chilies
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided

Directions:
In a skillet, brown ground beef and onion over medium heat; drain. Add the water, taco seasoning and tomato sauce and tomatoes; mix. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes. Stir in macaroni, chilies, olives and 1-1/2 cups of cheese. Pour into a greased 1-1/2-qt. baking dish. Sprinkle with the remaining cheese. Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes or until heated through.
