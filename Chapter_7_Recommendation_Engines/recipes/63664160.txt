Title:
Marinated Grilled Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small eggplant, cut into 3/4" thick slices
1 green bell pepper, seeded and cut into wide strips
1 red bell pepper, seeded and cut into wide strips
3 zucchinis, sliced
12 fresh mushrooms, stems removed
1/4 cup olive oil
1/4 cup lemon juice
1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
2 cloves garlic, grated

Directions:
Place eggplant, bell peppers, zucchinis and fresh mushrooms in a large resealable plastic bag.
In a medium bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, basil and garlic. Pour the mixture over the vegetables, seal and marinate in the refrigerator at least 1 hour.
Preheat an outdoor grill for medium high heat.
Place vegetables directly on the grill or on skewers. Cook on the prepared grill, brushing frequently with the marinade, 3-5 minutes per side,  or to desired doneness.
