Title:
Vegetable Rotini Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/921566.jpg
Ingredients:
1 can (10 3/4 oz) Broccoli cheese soup
1 pkg (3 oz) cream cheese, softened
3/4 cup milk
2 TBSP dijon-style mustard (optional)
1/8 tsp pepper
3 cups cooked rotini (corkscrew) macaroni (abt 2 1/2 cups dry)
3 cups cooked cut-up fresh vegetables (broccoli, cauliflower, carrots, etc)
1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
In 3-quart saucepan, gradually stir soup into cream cheese over low heat; add milk, mustard and pepper, heat throughly, stirring often.
Add cooked macaroni, vegetables and parmesan cheese. Heat throughly, stirring often.
