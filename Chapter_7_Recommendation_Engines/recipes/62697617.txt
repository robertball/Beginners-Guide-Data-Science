Title:
Beefy Enchilada Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. ground beef
2 Tbls. finely chopped celery
2 Tbls. finely chopped onion
2 Tbls. finely chopped carrot
1/2 Cup largly diced red, orange, & yellow peppers
1/4 Cup taco seasoning
1-10oz. Jar salsa
1-16oz. Jar taco sauce
10- 10" tortillas
8 oz. Shredded mexican cheese

Directions:
Brown ground beef in large skillet then drain on paper towel lined plate.
Reserve 1-2 tbls. drippings in pan, return to stove, and add veggies; saute until crisp tender.
Add ground beef to veggies in skillet and stir in taco seasoning and salsa. Let simmer 10-15 minutes, stirring often.
Coat bottom of one 9x13x2 or 2-8x8 glass baking dishes with a thin layer of taco sauce.
Remove meat mixture from heat and spoon 1/2-3/4 cups onto each tortilla shell, sprinkle with shredded cheese, roll in tortilla, and place seam side down in sauce lined baking dish.
Cover tortillas with remaining taco sauce, sprinkle with remaining cheese, cover with aluminum foil and bake for 20 minutes in a 375 degree oven.

Submitter: 
Lisa Garnier
Description: 
A deliciously filling one-dish-meal that can be made from ingredients on hand (at least at my house). Over a cup of vegetables are an added bonus for this quick and easy dish.
