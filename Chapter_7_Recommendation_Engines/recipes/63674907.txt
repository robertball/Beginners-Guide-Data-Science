Title:
Mom's Homemade Biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6-8 cups of flour
tsp of salt
6-8 tsp of baking powder (always a tsp to a cup of flour
2 Tbsp of Lard
Milk

Directions:
Preheat oven to 475. Mix all of the ingredients together well in a large mixing bowl.
Put enough milk in to make the batter the consistency that you want; it should be a thick paste that's kind of chunky, just like when you make dumplings or bread. Pour a light dusting of flour onto a flat surface and
roll it all out and kneed it until it forms as dough. Roll it out so it's about one inch in height. cut out into 2 tablespoon pieces, kind of like balls, and place them all snugly next to each other in a greased pan. Use a large enough pan so that when the biscuits rise in the oven they don't overflow it. Put them in the oven and cook them until they are golden brown on the top, you will also see them kind of separate on the top. When they are done dab lard on each separate biscuit to soften the tops when they cool.
