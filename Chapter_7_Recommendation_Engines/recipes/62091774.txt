Title:
Fajita Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1-Cup 		Vegetable Oil
½ Cup		Kikkoman’s Soy sauce
1 Tbl.		Black Pepper- course ground
½ tsp.		Garlic Powder
½ tsp.		Ground Oregano
1 Fresh Lemon- halved

Directions:
Mix ingredients with a wire whisk.  Refrigerate overnight.
Remove lemon halves- squeeze into marinade.
Blend marinade ingredients again.
Add 1-2 lbs.Chicken or Beef.
Marinade 6-8 hours.
Drain marinade and either grill or cook in skillet until juices run clear,  approx 15-20 mins on medium-high

Submitter: 
Latasha Rockwell
Description: 
Great mild marinade, great for beef or chicken, very easy! Prep time does not include Marinade time
