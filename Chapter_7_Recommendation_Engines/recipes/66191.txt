Title:
Crispix Mix Original with White Cheddar CHEEZ-IT crackers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/987037.jpg
Ingredients:
5 cups KELLOGG'S® CRISPIX® cereal
5 cups SUNSHINE® CHEEZ-IT® White Cheddar Crackers
1 cup small pretzels
1 cup cashews
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
4 teaspoons lemon juice
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon celery salt
1 teaspoon garlic salt
1 teaspoon onion salt
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon onion powder

Directions:
In a large mixing bowl, combine cereal, crackers, pretzels and cashews. Set aside.
In small mixing bowl, combine remaining ingredients, mixing well. Pour seasoning mixture over cereal and cracker mixture. Gently stir until evenly coated. Pour mixture onto 11 x 13 -inch baking pan.
Bake at 250 degrees F for 45 minutes, stirring every 15 minutes. Spread on paper towels to cool. Store in airtight container.

Submitter: 
KELLOGG'S® CRISPIX®
Description: 
This recipe brings all your snack favorites into one great mix combination, and adds plenty of seasonings to keep your taste buds craving more!
