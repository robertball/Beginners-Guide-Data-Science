Title:
Fantastic Chicken Rub Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T salt
2 T black pepper
2 T paprika
1 T onion powder
1 T garlic powder
1 T chilli powder
1 T dried thyme

Directions:
mix all ingredients and rub on a 3-4 lb chicken. Refrigerate at least an hour up to over night.  Cook chicken as desired. preferably in a rotisserie. ENJOY!

Submitter: 
Maryam
Description: 
Ever walk by that famous Deli in your hometown smelling the wonderful rotisserie chickens!  This rub will help you recapture that flavor at home.
