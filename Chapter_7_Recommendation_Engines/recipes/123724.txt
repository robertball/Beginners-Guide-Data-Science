Title:
Dilly Ham and Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/28778.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups diced fully cooked ham
1 large onion, chopped
1 cup chopped green pepper
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
1 1/4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
10 eggs
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup chopped green onions
1 (2 ounce) jar diced pimientos, drained
2 tablespoons picante sauce
1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
1 teaspoon dill weed
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
In a skillet, saute ham, onion and green pepper in butter until vegetables are crisp-tender. Transfer to a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish. Sprinkle with 3/4 cup cheese. In a bowl, beat eggs, sour cream and milk; mix in the next eight ingredients. Pour into the baking dish. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 25-30 minutes or until eggs are set. Top with remaining cheese.
