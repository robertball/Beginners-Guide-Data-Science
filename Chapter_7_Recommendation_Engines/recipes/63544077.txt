Title:
Coconut Cream Coffee Creamer Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Heavy Cream
1 Cup Milk
3/4 Cup Sugar
2 Tsp Coconut Extract
1 Tsp Vanilla

Directions:
In a small pot combine heavy cream, milk and sugar. Heat on low until steam appears. Remove from heat and add coconut and vanilla extracts. Stir and allow to cool. Add to sealed container and refrigerate.

Submitter: 
may2mac
Description: 
This delicious coconut cream coffee creamer tastes just like the one you can buy at the grocery store - only it doesn't contain any of the added chemicals or preservatives.
