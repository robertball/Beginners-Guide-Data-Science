Title:
Grilled Fresh Green Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/872610.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb. 	fresh green beans
1 sm 	onion, sliced
2-4 SL 	bacon cut into quarters (low sodium or turkey)
1 	Portobello mushroom sliced
Olive oil
Mrs. Dash original blend
Grill wok or basket

Directions:
Slice onions and mushrooms and cut bacon slices into quarters. Place onions, bacon and beans in bowl or Ziploc bag and drizzle with olive oil. Leave mushrooms separate.
Place on medium high grill in grilling basket or wok. Stir every 10 min. or so. I cooked these for about 40 min. Cook time will depend on temp of grill. About 10 min. before removing from grill add mushrooms and cook.
