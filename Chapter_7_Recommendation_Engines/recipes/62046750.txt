Title:
Snow Cone Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package poder drink mix, any flavor
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup hot water

Directions:
Combine sugar and water.  Stir until sugar is dissolved.  You can microwave mixture 45 seconds to better dissolve the sugar.  Stir in drink mix.  Mix well.  Refrigerate in an airtight container.  Pour desired amount over shaved ice.

Submitter: 
CHESSETTE
Description: 
If you have an ice shaver, this recipe is a must.  It is much cheaper than buying premade syrups, and this syrup tastes even better!!
