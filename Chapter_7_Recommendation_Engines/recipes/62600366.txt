Title:
Red lobster garlic cheddar biscuits Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of all-purpose flour .
1/2 teaspoon of baking soda .
1/2 teaspoon of sugar.
1 teaspoon of baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt.
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon of parsley, dried or fresh
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
6 tablespoons of shortening .
3/4 cup of buttermilk

Directions:
Preheat oven 450Â° .
mix all dry ingredients first .
then add shortening squish shortening and flour mixture with fingers this will help in flakiness of biscuits .
fold in buttermilk to do not over stir.
Using 2 tablespoons scoop out mixture into six individual balls , then plays balls on ungreased cookie sheet

Submitter: 
Swen
Description: 
Red lobster was one of my favorite restaurants to go to for special occasions . but the most memorable part of my Red lobster experience was the cheddar biscuits , the seafood was great. But cheddar biscuits is what I really came to the restaurant for.
