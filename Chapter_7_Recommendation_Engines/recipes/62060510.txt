Title:
Sesame Peanut Pasta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces Farfalle pasta (butterfly shaped)
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
1 tablespoon brown sugar
6 tablespoons peanut butter
¼ cup soy sauce
6 tablespoons sesame oil
1 tablespoon hot chile oil *
5 tablespoons sesame seeds, toasted
4 green onions (scallions)
1 pound blanched broccoli flowerets, chopped small and cooled

Directions:
1.	Cook pasta until just tender. Drain, rinse under cold water, drain again.
2.	Place the garlic, vinegar, brown sugar, peanut butter, and soy sauce in a food processor. Process for 90 seconds. With the motor running, slowly add the sesame and hot chili oils through the feed tube, and mix another minute or two.
3.	Add the above sauce, blanched broccoli, and 4 tablespoons of the sesame seeds to the pasta… mix well.
4.	Place the pasta salad mixture in a large serving platter or bowl and sprinkle with the scallions and remaining 1 tablespoon of sesame seeds. Serve at room temperature.
6 servings.
* Available in Asian grocery stores
