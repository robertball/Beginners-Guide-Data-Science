Title:
Kale, White Bean & Turkey Sausage Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.25 lbs homemade turkey sausage meat (search AllRecipes)
2 tsp Olive Oil
1 clove garlic, minced (add more as desired)
1/2 bunch kale, rinsed with leaves removed from ribs and rough chopped
1 cup Great Northern or Cannellini Beans
4 cups fat free chicken stock, unsalted to keep sodium reduced
2 cups water
1 pinch dried red pepper flakes
Salt & Freshly Ground Pepper to Taste

Directions:
Heat oil over medium-high heat in a medium sized pot
Add sausage meat breaking up into large chunks.
When completely cooked, add garlic and saut√© for 3-5 minutes (careful not to let the garlic burn).
Add water, chicken stock, beans and red pepper flakes and stir thoroughly.
Simmer for 10 minutes, covered.
Add in kale and let simmer another 10 minutes, covered.
Salt and pepper to taste after serving.

Submitter: 
Nate Thee
Description: 
This is a great low-sodium soup that is completely homemade, even the Turkey Sausage!
