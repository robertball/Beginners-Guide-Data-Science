Title:
M'm! M'm! Good!® Tomato Cheese Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/990442.jpg
Ingredients:
1 loaf French bread, cut in half lengthwise
1 (11 ounce) can Campbell's® Condensed Tomato Bisque Soup
1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 cup chopped green onion
1 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
Place bread halves on baking sheet. Bake at 400 degrees F for 5 minutes.
Mix soup, cheeses, onions and garlic powder. Spread soup mixture over bread to within 1/4-inch of edges.
Bake 5 minutes or until cheese melts. Cut into slices.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Crusty French bread is split and topped with a creamy, cheesy tomato sauce and baked until hot.
