Title:
Chocolate Buttermilk Oil Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1-1/2 cup flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
5 heaping TB unsweetened cocoa
1-1/2 cup buttermilk
2 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup oil
Optional frosting:
2-1/2 cup sifted confectioner's sugar
3 oz softened cream cheese
1 TB milk
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Mix all together in bowl with electric mixer or wire whip til just blended.
If using 8" square pan, bake 25-30 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center of cake comes out clean
If doubling recipe and using 9 X 13 X 2 pan, bake 30-35 minutes and do toothpick test
If making frosting, mix all ingredients together until appropriate consistency (can experiment with amounts of sugar and cream cheese to taste) and spread over cooled cake
Melt 1 square UNSWEETENED chocolate and drizzle over cream cheese frosting

Submitter: 
Geroniho
Description: 
This fantastic, yummy cake is extremely easy to make with common ingredients.  It is good plain or dressed up with a cream cheese frosting.
