Title:
Faux~Zanya Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg egg noodles
8 oz cottage cheese
Mozzarella
Parmesan Cheese
1 can tomato sauce
1 can spaghetti sauce
1 can stewed tomatoes Italian style
oregano
basil
pepper
rosemary
garlic salt
1 lb ground beef

Directions:
Brown beef.  Blend for uniformity with stewed tomatoes.  Combine with tomato sauce, spaghetti sauce, and all herbs.  Cook noodles according to package instructions.  In large casserole dish, layer starting with sauce on bottom.  Sauce, noodles, cottage cheese, mozzarella, and parmesan.  End with mostly sauce on the top with a little of cheese.  Bake 450 until cheese browned.  FauxZanya baby!

Submitter: 
Jennifer Lebow
Description: 
Not real lasagna but with this taste who cares????  The noodle change makes this quick and easy.  Blending the meat and tomatoes gives uniformity and a little class to it.  No left overs.  Couldn't even lick the pan...  :)
