Title:
Tossed Greens with Homemade Dressing and Stuffing 'Croutons' Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1026500.jpg
Ingredients:
Dressing:
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup oil
1/2 cup white or apple cider vinegar
Salad Fixings:
1 large bag mixed greens
1/2 bag stuffing mix for chicken
4-6 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
4-6 sliced of crispy bacon, chopped

Directions:
Combine the dressings ingredients in a jar, close tightly and shake well.
Reshake occasionally as you prepare dinner to thoroughly dissolve the sugar.
Rinse the greens, if necessary, and dry thoroughly.
Place in large bowl.
Just before serving, add the eggs, the bacon and the stuffing mix.
Pour desired amount of dressing over top and toss well.

Submitter: 
midwestchef
Description: 
This salad is so simple and delicious; it goes with any main dish for any occasion.  I like that the homemade dressing is just that:  homemade - with no additives or preservatives.
