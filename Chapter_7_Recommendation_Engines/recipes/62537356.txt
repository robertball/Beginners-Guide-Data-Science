Title:
Cranberry Orange Relish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 12 oz pkg cranberries
1 large navel orange
1 c. white sugar
1 c. chopped walnuts or pecans (optional)

Directions:
In a food processor or grinder, finely chop cranberries and oranges. Frozen cranberries may be easier if using a food processor.  Mix together with sugar and walnuts or pecans.  Allow to set, ideally for at least 24 hours.  Serve as a relish with roast turkey.
