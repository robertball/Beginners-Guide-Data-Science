Title:
Waffle Egg Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1260417.jpg
Ingredients:
2 waffles - toasted
Breakfast meat of your choice - ham, sausage patty or bacon
1 egg - cooked to your liking
warm maple syrup

Directions:
Heat your breakfast meat in a skillet until fully cooked.  Move meat to the side and fry egg to your liking, over easy, scrambled, or over hard.  Assemble your sandwich on a plate - waffle, meat, egg and waffle.  Top with warm maple syrup and enjoy.
