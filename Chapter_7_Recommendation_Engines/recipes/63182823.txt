Title:
Blueberry Muffin Cake - Gluten & Sugar Free Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/818978.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 tsp Butter
2 Eggs
2 tsp Vanilla Extract (No Corn Syrup, Gluten Free)
1/2 cup Olive Oil
3 cups Brown Rice Flour
1 1/2 tsp Kal Pure Stevia Extract (equivalent to 3 cups Sugar)
4 tsp Baking Powder
1 tsp Salt
1 1/2 cups Water
4 cups Frozen Blueberries

Directions:
Add Butter to large glass mixing bowl and melt in microwave
Mix in Eggs, Vanilla Extract and Olive Oil
In a separate glass mixing bowl, Mix Brown Rice Flour, Stevia, Baking Powder, and Salt
Once dry ingredients are evenly mixed, mix into liquid
Add Water slowly until pancake batter consistency, you may need more or less than listed
Mix in Blueberries
Pour in 9x13 pan and Bake for 45 to 60 minutes at 350° or 40 minutes at 325° in a convection oven.

Submitter: 
Zan
Description: 
We used to make this as muffins, but got tired of cleaning the muffin tins. Personally, I like this better since I've never been a fan of the portion of the muffin that touches the pan and gets dark.
