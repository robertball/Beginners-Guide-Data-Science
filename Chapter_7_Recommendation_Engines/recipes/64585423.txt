Title:
Everyday Green Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 oz chopped sweet-tart apple, such as Braeburn (1.5 cups)
3 oz coarsely chopped parsley leaves and stems (1 bunch)
4 oz stemmed chopped kale (6 packed cups)
2 oz peeled lemon

Directions:
Juice in electric juicer, alternating dry (kale and parsley) and juicy (apple and lemon) ingredients. Serve immediately.
Mix the pulp into potato pancake/latke batter for green, zesty boost.
Calories: 157; Fat 1.9g; Pro 7g; CHO 37g; Fiber 2g; Sugars 19g; Iron 8 mg; Sodium 98 mg

Submitter: 
HurdBird
Description: 
Crisp, tart, earthy, bright - and powerful, too, with a whopping 7g protein from the parsley and kale.
