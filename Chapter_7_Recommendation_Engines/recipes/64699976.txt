Title:
Loaded Potato Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2/3 c butter
2/3 c flour
7 c milk
4 lg baking potatoes, baked, cooled, peeled, and cubed
1/4 c chopped onion
12 bacon strips, cooled and crumbled
1 1/4 c shreaded cheddar cheese
1 c sour cream
3/4 tsp salt
1/2 pepper

Directions:
In a large kettle, melt butter.
Stir in flour.
Heat and stir until smooth.
Gradually add milk, stirring constantly until thickened.
Add potatoes and onions.
Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.
Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes
Add remaining ingredients and stir until cheese is melted.
Garnish with additional cheese, bacon, and onion if desired.
