Title:
No-Bake Brownies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can 5.33 oz. Evaportated milk
1 pkg semi-sweet choc chips
3 cups finely crushed graham crackers

Directions:
In saucepan, combine milk & choc chips.  Cook over low heat, stirring until choc melts and is smooth.  Mix in graham crumbs with electric mixer until smooth.  Spread in 8x8 pan & refrigerate until firm.
