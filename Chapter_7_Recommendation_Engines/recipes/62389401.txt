Title:
Jim's Mild Spicey Catfish Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup corn meal
1 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper ( or to taste)
1 cup milk
2 eggs beaten
6 (4 ounce) fillets catfish

Directions:
Combine the corn meal,paprika, onion powder, garlic powder and cayenne pepper in a bowl. Transfer the mixture to a cookie sheet
Combine the milk and 2 eggs beaten in a seperate bowl.
Take the catfish and dip it in the milk/egg mixture then coat them in the cornmeal mixture on both sides.
You can grill them or fry them in vegetable or olive oil until golden brown. About 4 minutes on each side either way.

Submitter:

Description: 
This Creole type catfish is sure to please! You can substitute anykind of whitefish if you like
