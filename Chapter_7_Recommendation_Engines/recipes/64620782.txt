Title:
Simple Roasted  Asparagus with Carrots and Onion Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/4421314.jpg
Ingredients:
1 bunch asparagus
5 Carrots
1/2 small onion
Olive oil
Seasoned salt such as Lawry's
Pepper

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
Snap ends off of asparagus where they naturally break and chop into 1 inch sticks.
Peel carrots and slice into 1/4 inch coins.
Cut the onion in half and cut each half in half to make onion petals.
Spread asparagus, carrots and onion on a baking pan.
Drizzle with olive oil, mix gently.
Sprinkle with seasoned salt and pepper to taste.
Roast in the oven for 15 minutes.
