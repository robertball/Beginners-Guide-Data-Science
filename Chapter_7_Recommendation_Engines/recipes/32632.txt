Title:
Pina Colada III Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/74319.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup coconut milk
1 cup pineapple juice
1/2 cup rum
4 tablespoons white sugar
8 cubes ice

Directions:
In a blender, combine coconut milk, pineapple juice, rum, sugar and ice. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses, and serve immediately.

Submitter: 
May and Siv
Description: 
A simple run-of-the-mill pina colada drink.
