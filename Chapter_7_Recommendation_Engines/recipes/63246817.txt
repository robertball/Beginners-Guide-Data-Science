Title:
Easy Frozen Hot Chocolate Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/847981.jpg
Ingredients:
3/4 cup milk
2 packets of swiss miss milk chocolate hot chocolate mix
1 & 1/2 cups crushed ice
whipped cream (opt)

Directions:
pour milk in the blender and then add cocoa mix and ice.
blend until it is slushy.
pour into a tall glass and top with whipped cream (opt)

Submitter:

Description: 
Yummy summer drink when you're craving a winter treat.
