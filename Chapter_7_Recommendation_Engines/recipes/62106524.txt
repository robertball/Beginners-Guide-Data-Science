Title:
Authentic Tortellini Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - tsp olive oil
1 - pkg. mild itailian sausage
1 - small onion chopped
2 tbsp of itailan seasoning
2 tbsp minced garlic
1 - 20 oz. pkg. cheese tortellini
2 - 49.5 oz. cans of chicken broth
1 - 28 oz. can of tomato puree
1 bunch of washed spinach chopped

Directions:
Pour one can of broth into a small sauce pan and add tortellini's and boil until dente. In a large stock pot heat oil and saute sausage, onion, seaoning, and garlic until sausage is browned. Then add tortellini's and broth to stock pot. Add the puree, spinach, and 2nd can of chicken broth. Cover and let simmer for 30 min

Submitter: 
Stephanie
Description: 
This soup only takes 30 min. and is awesome on a cold night.
