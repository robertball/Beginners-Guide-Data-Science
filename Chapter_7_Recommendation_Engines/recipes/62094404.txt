Title:
Sweet Chicken wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2pkg chicken wings
2c.brown sugar
1c light soy sauce
4tsp black pepper
6TBS lemon juice
4 Jiggers of rum or 2TBS imatation rum
2tsp ground ginger
1can 20oz pineapple

Directions:
Place one pkg each of chicken into a 9x13, this will require two 9x13 pans, for each pkg of chicken
Pat each 9x13 pan of chicken with 1c. of the brown sugar
In a bowl mix the remaining ingredients, except the pineapple
Once all is mixed together, pour the sauce over the chicken, half on each 9x13
Divide the pineapple between the 9x13's
Place in a 200 degree oven for 8-10 hours
They are great hot or cold. Enjoy!
