Title:
Spinach and Artichoke Tortilla Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of prepared Spinach and Artichoke dip
¾ cup of grated Mozzarella cheese
¼ cup of grated Parmesan cheese
2 tbsp diced red onion
¼ cup diced tomatoes
4 flour tortillas

Directions:
Spread the dip evenly over the 4 tortillas.  Top with the cheese and red onions.  Place on a lightly oiled baking sheet and bake for 10 minutes at 400 degrees or until hot and bubbly.  Remove, top with tomatoes and cut into wedges.  Additional Parmesan can be added to taste.

Submitter: 
DASKDS
Description: 
A quick and light pizza the cooks up in no time.  Using prepared Spinach and Artichoke dip is the key.  Or, use your own.
