Title:
Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pint half and half
1 stick butter
2 tablespoons cream cheese
1/2 to 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions:
In a saucepan combine butter, heavy cream, and cream cheese. Simmer this until all is melted, and mixed well. Add the parmesan cheese and garlic powder. Simmer this for 15-20 minutes on low. season with salt and pepper if desired.
