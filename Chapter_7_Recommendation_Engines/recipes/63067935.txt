Title:
Apples and Sausage Heidelberg Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 10 oz pound pork sausage links
2 TB brown sugar
4 oz shredded muenster cheese
2 apples
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Core apples and cut into 12 1/4 inch thick rings, set aside. Brown meat, remove from skillet. Saute apple rings in remaining sausage fat. Drain apple rings on paper towels. Place rings in 9x11 baking dish. Sprinkle with combined cinnamon and sugar, top with sausage links and sprinkle with cheese.  Place under broiler on high until cheese melts.

Submitter: 
Lindsey Clinton
Description: 
Growing up, our family made this every Christmas Day for breakfast. It was a wonderful memory I have continued with my own family.
