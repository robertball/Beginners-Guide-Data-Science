Title:
Mixed Berry Fools Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 C raspberries
1 C blackberries
1 C blueberries
1/3 Cup OJ
3 C Cool Whip lite
1 container (6oz) mixed berry yogurt
Additional berries for garnish

Directions:
1) Sm Saucepan- Combine berries, OJ and sugar. Bring to boil over high, then reduce heat to md-high and simmer 10 min. Carefully add fruit mixture to food processor and puree until smooth. Strain through fine mesh strainer and place in freezer for 50 min.
2) Place thawed cool whip in md sized bowl, fold in yogurt and cooled fruit puree until just combined. Serve immediately. Garnish with additional berries, if desired.
