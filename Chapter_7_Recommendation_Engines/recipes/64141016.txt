Title:
Almond Chicken with Apricot Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup apricot spreadable fruit
3 Tbsp soy sauce
2 Tbsp onion, finely chopped
4 tsp cider vinegar
1 tsp ground mustard
6 boneless skinless chicken breast halves
1/2 cup almonds, sliced
1 Tbsp butter, melted

Directions:
In a shallow bowl, combine first five ingredients; tranfer 1/2 cup to a serving bowl and set aside.
Dip chicken in remaining apricot mixture.  Place in a greased baking dish.  Sprinkle almonds over chicken; drizzle with butter.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees F for 30-35 minutes or until chicken is done.  Serve with reserved apricot sauce.
