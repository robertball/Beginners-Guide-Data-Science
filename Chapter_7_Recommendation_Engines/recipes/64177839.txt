Title:
Big Ray's Pumpkin Spice Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1489871.jpg
Ingredients:
4 eggs
1 2/3 cups sugar
1 cup oil
1 can solid pack pumpkin (15 oz), not pie mix
1/2 tsp ground cloves
1/2 tsp nutmeg
12 cups flour
2 tsp cinnamon
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
Icing
6 oz cream cheese, softened
2 cups confection sugar
1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
1 tsp vanilla
1 to Tbsp milk

Directions:
In a bowl, beat the eggs, sugar, oil and pumpkin until well blended. Combine the flour, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda, cloves , nutmeg and salt; gradually add to pumpkin mixture and mix well. Pour into an ungreased 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan. Bake at 350Â° for 20 minutes or until set. Cool completely.
For icing, beat the cream cheese, confectioners' sugar, butter and vanilla in a small bowl. Add enough milk to achieve spreading consistency. Spread over bars. Store in the refrigerator. Yield: 2 dozen.
