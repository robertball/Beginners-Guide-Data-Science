Title:
Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 large cans of sweet potatoes
4 eggs
1 cup brown sugar
1 tsp salt (or to taste)
1 can evaporated milk
TOPPING
1 stick of butter
1 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup flour
1/2 cup dry oatmeal
1 cup pecans

Directions:
Mash potatoes, add the rest of the ingredients.
Pour into pan.
For topping, mix brown sugar, oatmeal and flour; cut in the butter until pea-sized bites.
Add the pecans.
Top the sweet potato mix with the topping, spreading evenly.
Bake at 350 until golden and bubbly

Submitter: 
Milly Suazo-Martinez
Description: 
This is the casserole I prepare every year for Thanksgiving. It has a nice crunchy top and goes very nicely with the rest of the meal.
