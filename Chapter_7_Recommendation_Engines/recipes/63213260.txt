Title:
Cheesy Enchilada Stacks Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
10 oz can enchilada sauce
1 pkg 8 inch flour tortillas
8 oz shredded cheddar
16 oz can refried beans
2 cans diced green chiles
1 bunch green onions
1 Tbsp taco seasoning

Directions:
Brown ground beef and drain. Stir in 1/2 cup enchilada sauce. Spray baking sheet with Pam. Stir taco seasoning into refried beans. Place 1 tortilla on the baking sheet; top with 1/3 beef and 1/4 cup cheese.Top with tortilla, half the refried beans, 1/2 cup enchilada sauce, 1 can chiles and 1/4 cup cheese. Repeat layers. Top with tortilla, remaining beef and 1/4 cup cheese. Top with tortilla. Cover with foil and bake at 400 degrees for 40 minutes. Uncover; top with remaining enchilada sauce, cheese and green onions. Bake 5 minutes. Cut into wedges and serve.
