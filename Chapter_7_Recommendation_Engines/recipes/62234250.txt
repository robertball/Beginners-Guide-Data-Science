Title:
Cheddar Round Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 1/2 c Bisquick
1/4 c sugar
1 1/2 c cheddar cheese
1 egg
1 1/3 c milk

Directions:
Grease and flour springform pan;
Mix all of above together, stirring until just moistened
Bake at 350 for 35-40:, Cool 10" then remove from pan
