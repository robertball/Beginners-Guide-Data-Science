Title:
Potato Casserole mod by Connie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (30 ounce) package frozen hash brown potatoes
2 -3cups shredded Cheddar cheese
1 (16 ounce) container sour cream (may use low fat)
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup, cream of celery soup , or cream of chicken soup (regular or low fat)
1 onion, chopped OR French's onions
Olive oil to saute onion as necessary
1/2 cup butter
1/4 tsp garlic powder
ground pepper to taste
3 cups crushed corn flakes OR Ritz crackers if desired

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
Pour the hash browns into a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish. In a large bowl, combine the cheese, sour cream and soup.
In a large skillet over medium heat, combine the onion with 1 stick butter and saute for 5 minutes. OR measure Fench's onions.  Add this to the soup mixture and spread this over the potatoes in the dish.
Cover with foil
During last 15 minutes of baking time, If desired,  arrange the crushed corn flakes/crushed Ritz over all in the dish. Melt butter and pour this evenly over the corn flakes.
Bake at 425 degrees F (220 degrees C) for 1 hour.

Submitter: 
Hazily
Description: 
This simple-to-assemble casserole is rich and buttery in flavor with a crispy top, thanks to the crushed corn flakes.Modified from: Potato Casserole
