Title:
Mom's Most Delicious Pink Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large container of cottage cheese
1 large container of cool whip
2 (3 ounces) boxes JELLO-mixed fruit flavor preffered or any flavor
fruit cocktail
oranges (mandarin)
pineapple chunks
cherries

Directions:
Mix everything together in a large bowl.  Chill overnight for best taste.

Submitter: 
ERLENSEE_GERMANY
Description: 
This pink salad was a favorite for the whole family.  My mom used to make it on special holidays.  I have talked her into giving me the recipe and now I am going to share it with all of you.  It truley is too good to be kept a secret!
