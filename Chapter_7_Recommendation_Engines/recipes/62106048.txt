Title:
Graham Frosties Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 sleeves of graham crackers
1 jar peanut butter creamy or crunchy 1lb 2oz size( dont use all natural for these)
2 squares of semisweet chocolate
1 tablespoon butter
1/2 cup confectioner sugar
3 tablespoons milk

Directions:
Melt the chocolate in a micro safe bowl in microwave. Do it at 30 sec. then stir and repeat until almost all melted. the residual heat will finish the melting.
With a wisk add sugar and milk until it is smooth.
Put frosting aside.
Using a 13x9 inch pan fill the bottom of the pan with graham cracker squares. try not to leave too many gaps in between the squares and the pan or each other.
Scoop out peanut butter from the jar and in to a bowl. Melt to pouring consistancy in the micro wave.
Pour on top of the graham crackers.
Top with a layer or graham gracker squares
Pour and spread the icing on top of the crackers.
Refridgerate until serving.
Cut in small squares and serve.

Submitter: 
Barbara Conrad
Description: 
A quick and easy treat for the whole family.There is no baking, just the microwave. But dont let the ease fool you these are so good they will disappear fast.
