Title:
Tom's All Season Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Salt
1/2 cup Garlic Powder
1 tablespoon Cumin
1 tablespoon Oregano
1 tablespoon Thyme
1/4 cup Brown Sugar
*Optional 1 tablespoon red or lemon pepper (your preference)

Directions:
Put all ingredients in a large bowl
Mix and grab seasoning and rub it directly onto meat
Heat grill to searing heat, and sear meat a couple minutes on each side
Reduce heat and let the juices and seasoning cook in slow
