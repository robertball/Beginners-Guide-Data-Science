Title:
Lemon Delicious Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
70g soft butter
200g caster sugar
5 eggs, seperated
150ml lemon juice
1 lemon, zest
50g plain flour
300ml milk

Directions:
Grease 4 individual ramekin dishes with butter and then lightly coat with sugar.
Cream the butter with a third of the sugar until light and fluffy.
Add egg yolks a few at a time.
Sitr in the lemon zest, flour, milk and lemon juice and mix until combined.
Whip egg whites to form soft peaks.  Add the rest of the sugar.
Fold the egg whites into the lemon mixture.  Pour the mixutre into the 4 ramekin dishes.
Place the dishes in a baking dish of water and cook in a pre-heated oven of 175 degrees for 35 minutes or until just firm on top.
Remove from the oven and cool in a cold water bath.  Serve.

Description: 
A delicious and light lemon dessert.  This is great as a quick and easy dessert to serve when entertaining.
