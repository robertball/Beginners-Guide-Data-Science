Title:
Spiced Almonds Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c whole unsalted almonds
1 t ground cumin
1/4 t garlic powder
1/4 t cayenne pepper
1/2 t salt
2 t olive oil
1/4 t hot pepper sauce

Directions:
Toast the almonds in a dry skillet over a medium heat.
