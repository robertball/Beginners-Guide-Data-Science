Title:
Honey Mustard Chicken Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/989848.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into 1-inch pieces
1 (12 ounce) jar refrigerated honey mustard salad dressing
2 cups Pepperidge FarmÂ® Herb Seasoned Stuffing, crushed
2 tablespoons orange juice

Directions:
Dip the chicken into 3/4 cup dressing and coat with the stuffing.
Place the chicken on 2 baking sheets. Bake at 400 degrees F. for 15 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
Stir the remaining dressing and orange juice in a 1-quart saucepan and heat through over medium heat. Serve the dressing mixture with the chicken for dipping.

Submitter: 
Campbell's Kitchen
Description: 
Bite-sized pieces of boneless chicken are seasoned with honey-mustard, breaded and baked crisp; then they're ready to dunk in a refreshing orange-honey-mustard sauce.
