Title:
Orange Juice Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package yellow buttered cake mix
1 package instant vanilla pudding
1 cup orange juice
1/2 cup canola oil
4 large eggs
1/2 cup chopped nuts (pecans, walnuts)
Glaze
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup orange juice
1/2 cup unsalted butter or margarine

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325 Degrees F. Grease and flour Bundt pan or Springform pan. Spread nuts evenly onto bottom of pan. In a large mixing bowl mix remaining ingredients and pour over nuts. Bake for 50-60 minutes, until toothpick comes out clean. Poke holes in the cake with a fork or chop stick.
Make the glaze about 5 mins before the cake comes out of the oven. In a small saucepan bring to a boil for 2 mins the sugar, orange juice and butter or margarine. Remove the cake from the oven and immediately pour the glaze over the hot cake and allow to sit for 10 minutes. Invert onto serving plate and enjoy.
