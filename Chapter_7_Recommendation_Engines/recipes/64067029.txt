Title:
Mauigirl's Kalua Pig in a Slow Cooker Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large cabbage leaves
1 (6 pound)pork butt or shoulder roast
1 1/2 tablespoons ONO Hawaiian sea salt
1 tablespoon liquid applewood smoke flavoring
15 peeled garlic cloves

Directions:
Place two cabbage leaves in the bottom of the slow cooker.Place roast on cabbage leaves in slow cooker and brush the roast with the liquid smoke. Sprinkle the roast with the Hawaiian seasoning salt and throw the garlic cloves into the pot and cover the roast with the other two cabbage leaves.
Cover,start slowcooker on high for an hour. Reduce heat to low and cook for 16 to 20 hours,
Remove meat and reserve juices/fat from slow cooker,trim fat and separate meat into chunks, adding drippings as needed to moisten.
