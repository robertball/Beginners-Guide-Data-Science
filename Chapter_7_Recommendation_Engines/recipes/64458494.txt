Title:
Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1 lb ground pork
1/2-3/4 c oatmeal
1/2 c milk
1 egg
Salt and pepper to taste
3/4 c catsup
2 tbsp brown sugar

Directions:
Mix first six ingredients together with hands.
Form into loaf (or place into loaf pan).
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 hours.
Mix final two ingredients and spread over loaf.
Bake another 30-45 minutes.

Description: 
Tasty meatloaf as a main dish or for sandwiches.
