Title:
BACARDI速 Mixers Strawberry Margarita Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/983075.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 (10 fluid ounce) can frozen BACARDI速 Mixers Margarita Mix
1/2 cup BACARDI速 Rum
1 cup strawberries
1 teaspoon lime juice
1 fluid ounce grenadine
5 cups ice
Strawberries, for garnish

Directions:
Blend together BACARDI(R) Mixers Margarita mix, strawberries, lime juice, grenadine and BACARDI(R) Rum.
Add ice and blend for 45 to 60 seconds or until uniform.
Garnish with strawberries.

Submitter: 
BACARDI速 Mixers
Description: 
Frozen margarita mix and lime juice balance out the sweet flavors in this strawberry explosion.
