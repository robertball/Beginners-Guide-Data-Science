Title:
Grandma's Green Beans with Bacon Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs fresh green beans, trimmed
4 slices bacon
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup white vinegar
1/2 tsp salt
Pepper, to taste
1/2 tsp dry mustard
Sliced or slivered almonds

Directions:
Cook bacon til crisp, and remove to a paper towel to drain.  In the same pan, saut√© onions in the bacon grease.  Add sugar, vinegar, salt, pepper, and mustard.  Stir well, scraping up any bacon and onion bits in the pan.  Cook til bubbling.  Meanwhile, steam green beans til tender but still crisp.   Add beans to the pan with sauce, and toss to coat well.   Chop cooked bacon, and stir into the beans.  Garnish with almonds and serve hot.

Submitter: 
Sarahbeara
Description: 
Ridiculously good beans that make a great side dish to just about anything.  One of those recipes that grandma made for years that we could never get enough of.
