Title:
Decorators Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups shortening
1 stick margarine
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons flavoring
10 tablespoons milk
2 1/2 pounds powdered sugar, minus 1 1/2 cups

Directions:
Beat shortening, margarine, salt and flavoring on HIGH for 2 minutes.
Add alternately: milk, and powdered sugar.
Beat 5 minutes after all ingredients have been added.
For crumb coat - add water to thin it down and then spread very thin.
*Can add 2-3 Tbsp. strawberry jam for flavoring.

Submitter: 
Karen Monson
Description: 
I got this from a church leader back in high school.  It is a standard.
