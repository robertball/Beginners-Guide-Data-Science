Title:
Easy Artichoke Appetizers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8oz. cans crescent rolls (can use reduced fat)
3/4 cup mozarrella cheese or italian cheese blend
3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/2 cup miracle whip salad dressing (can use reduced fat)
1 15 oz. can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
1/4 tsp. garlic powder

Directions:
Unroll crescent roll dough into rectangles and press onto the bottom and up sides of a 15x10x1 inch jelly roll pan (cookie sheet). Seal perforations.  Bake in a preheated 375 degree oven for 10 minutes or until lightly browned.
Meanwhile, combine remaining ingredients in bowl and mix well.  Spread thinly over browned crust.  Bake at 375 for 15 minutes or until cheese is melted.  Let stand about 5 minutes, then cut to serve.  They can be cut into triangles, rectangles , or any shape you like.  Serve warm if possible.

Submitter: 
CAREYBUG
Description: 
If you love hot artichoke dips, you'll love this.  It's easy to make and requires no crackers.  It's the perfect finger food for get-togethers. Just bake, cut and serve!
