Title:
Silver Bells Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1 (46 fluid ounce) can pineapple juice
2 cups pina colada mix
1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
1 liter club soda, chilled
1 liter sweetened lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage, chilled
1 orange, sliced
ice cubes

Directions:
Stir pineapple juice and pina colada mix together in a punch bowl. Stir orange juice concentrate, club soda, and lemon-lime carbonated beverage into the mixture respectfully, stirring each one in before adding the next. Float orange slices atop the punch and add enough ice to chill.
