Title:
Wild Rice and Sausage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg Uncle Ben's Long Grain & Wild Rice (original or mushroom recipe)
1/2 lb pork sausage, fried, crumbled, & drained
1 sm can sliced mushrooms

Directions:
Follow the instructions on the box for preparing the rice.    Before covering for the simmer stage, stir in sausage and mushrooms.  Complete as instructed and serve.

Submitter: 
lonwolf32
Description: 
This is a wonderfully flavorful dish.  Even people who don't like rice have eaten and raved about it.  Best of all, it's quick and easy.  Makes an excellent side dish for any meat.
