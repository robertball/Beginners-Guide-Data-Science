Title:
Apple Custard Tart Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (9- or 10-inch) unbaked pastry shell
1.5 cups sour cream
1 (14-oz) can sweetened condensed milk (not evaporated milk)
¼ cup apple juice concentrate (thawed, not frozen)
1 egg
1½ teaspoons vanilla extract
¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
2 medium all-purpose apples, cored, pared, and thinly sliced
1 tablespoon margarine or butter
Cinnamon glaze:
1/2 cup apple juice concentrate (thawed)
2 teaspoon cornstarch
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:
Bake shell at 375º for 15 minutes.
In mixer bowl, beat sour cream, sweetened condensed milk, juice concentrate, egg, vanilla, and cinnamon
Pour into shell.
Bake 30 minutes or until set. Cool.
In skillet, cook apple slices in margarine until tender. Arrange on pie.
Combine glaze ingredients in saucepan, cook and stir until thickened.
Brush glaze over top of pie.

Submitter: 
Jeanie Kang
Description: 
A light, delicious spin on your traditional (and predictable) apple pie. Great for parties, showers, or any day of the week!  Finished product has a beautiful, polished look (and even better taste!)
