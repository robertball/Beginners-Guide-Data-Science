Title:
Baked bean casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound (12 ounce) can brick oven baked beans
1 pound can crushed pineapple
1/3 cup hickory flavored BBQ sauce
1 teaspoon dry mustard
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup cooked crumbled bacon
1/4 coup unpacked brown sugar
1 tablespoon finely chopped onion
1 tablespoon molasses

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Combine all ingredients and turn into greased casserole dish. Bake uncovered at 350 for 30 minutes  or until bubbly.
