Title:
Mushroom Barley Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup barley
3 cups water
1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
2 onions, chopped
3 cloves garlic
2 stalks celery, thinly sliced
2 (10 ounce) packages sliced mushrooms
5 cups vegetable broth
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Bring the barley and water to a boil in a saucepan.
Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer 30 minutes, or until tender.
Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat, stir in the onions and celery; cook and stir until the onion has softened and turned translucent, about 10 minutes.
Add the garlic and cook for a further 2 minutes.
Stir in mushrooms, and cook 5 minutes more.
Pour in Vegetable broth, and bring soup to a simmer over medium-high heat, then reduce heat to medium-low, and continue simmering 15 minutes.
Stir in barley, and season with salt and pepper before serving.
