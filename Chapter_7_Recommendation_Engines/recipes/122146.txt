Title:
Chicken-Flavored Rice Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons chicken bouillon granules
3 tablespoons dried parsley flakes
3 teaspoons dried celery flakes
1 tablespoon dried minced onion
1 tablespoon sugar
ADDITIONAL INGREDIENTS:
1 cup uncooked long grain rice
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
2 cups water

Directions:
In a bowl, combine the first five ingredients.  Store in an airtight container for up to 6 months.

Submitter: 
Florence  Arbes
Description: 
From Courtland, Minnesota, Florence Arbes gets a head start on a flavorful rice side dish with this mixture. With just five ingredients, it's simple to prepare and can be stored in an airtight container for up to 6 months.
