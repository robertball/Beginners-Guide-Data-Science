Title:
"Cheater" Lemon Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can white frosting (store bought)
1 fresh lemon

Directions:
Place white frosting in mixing bowl.
Add 2 tbsp. lemon zest  from fresh lemon
Add juice of 1/2 of fresh lemon.
Whip for 1 minute on high. Scrape bowl. Mix one additional minute.
Ready to use!

Submitter: 
Ginny Maziarka
Description: 
When it comes to quick & easy in the kitchen, there's no shame in the game on a hot summer day! Simply grab a can of frosting and bump it up a notch! Perfect for lemon bundt cake, cupcakes and cookies...
