Title:
Best Homemade Granola Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/643720.jpg
Ingredients:
8 cups rolled oats
1 1/2 cups wheat germ
1 1/2 cups oat bran
1 1/2 cup finely chopped almonds
1 1/2 cup finely chopped pecans
1/2 cup sunflower seeds
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup maple syrup
3/4 cup honey
3/4 cup vegetable oil
1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
1 cup coconut
3/4 cup raisins
3/4 cup sweetened dried cranberries
1/2 cup flaxseed

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F (165 degrees C). Line two large baking sheets with parchment paper.
Combine the oats, wheat germ, oat bran, sunflower seeds, almonds, and pecans in a large bowl.  Stir together the brown sugar, maple syrup, honey, oil, cinnamon and bring to a boil over medium heat. Let simmer for a couple minutes.  Add vanilla, then pour over the dry ingredients, and stir to coat. Spread the mixture out evenly on the baking sheets.
Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Add coconut, raisins, cranberries and flaxseed, return to the over for 10 more minutes. Cool completely before storing in an airtight container.
