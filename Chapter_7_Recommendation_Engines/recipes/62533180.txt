Title:
Mamaw Bea's Easy Coconut Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 box cake mix w/ butter
2 (9oz) bags frozen coconut, thawed
2 c sour cream
2 c sugar
1 (9 oz) Cool whip, thawed

Directions:
1. Prepare cake as per instructions, back in 2 (9") layer cakes.
2. Cool 10 min, turn out layers and split each for a total of 4 layers
3. Mix coconut, sour cream, & sugar; set 1/2 c aside and spread the rest over cake layers.
4. Mix reserved mixture with cool whip and ice cake.  Refrigerate at least 3 days.
