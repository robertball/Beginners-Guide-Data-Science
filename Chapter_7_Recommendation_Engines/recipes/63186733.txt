Title:
Mandarin, Chickpea, and Red Onion Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can chickpeas (28 oz), drained and rinsed
1 can mandarin orange segments (10 oz), drained
1 small red onion, finely diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 cups arugula
1 cup flat leaf parsley, roughly chopped
1 cup diced feta cheese
1 Tbsp minced fresh ginger
1/4 cup pecans, sliced and toasted
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
2 tbsp x virgin olive oil
2 tbsp cider vinegar
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
In a large bowl, mix together first 10 ingredients.
In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
Pour dressing over chickpea mixture and toss to coat.
