Title:
Peppered Shrimp Alfredo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
12 ounces penne pasta
3/8 cup butter
2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
1 onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 roasted red bell pepper, diced
8 Peppadews, chopped
3T of peppadew liquid
1/2 pound baby bella mushrooms, sliced and then broken into smaller pieces
1 pound medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 (15 ounce) jar Alfredo sauce
1/2 cup grated Romano or Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup cream
1 teaspoon cayenne pepper, or more to taste
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup chopped parsley
Meyer lemons

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Meanwhile, melt butter together with the olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in onion, and cook until softened and translucent, about 2 minutes. Stir in garlic, peppadews, roasted red pepper, and mushrooms; cook over medium-high heat until soft, about 2 minutes more.
Stir in the shrimp, Alfredo sauce, Romano cheese, and cream; bring to a simmer stirring constantly until thickened and shrimp are done, about 10 minutes. Season with cayenne, salt, and pepper to taste. Stir drained pasta into the sauce, and serve sprinkled with chopped parsley. Add the juice of one Meyer lemon to each serving.
