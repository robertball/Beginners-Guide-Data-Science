Title:
Chili Cheese Log Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb. Velveeta Cheese, cubed
8 ounces Pepper Jack Cheese - cubed
8 ounces Cheddar Cheese - cubed
8oz. pkg. Cream Cheese
1 Tblsp.  Minced Garlic or Garlic Powder
1 Tblsp. Lemon Juice
1/2 cup Finely chopped Walnuts
1 Tblsp.  Chili Powder
1/2 Tsp. Red Pepper Flakes (optional)

Directions:
Let the four cheeses come to room temperature, Either with a handmixer or food processer{I recommend Food Processer) blend all  four cheeses till smooth. Transfer to a mixing bowl and blend in remaining ingredients.
Take a piece of wax paper approx. 12 inches long - sprinkle additional chili powder down the center of wax paper horizontally, Spoon Cheese mixture on top of Chili powder in shape of log.
Wrap wax papper around cheese mixture and roll back and forth to form log, then twist ends of paper to seal and refrigerate at least one hour till log is firm.
Serve ith a variety of crackers - Enjoy

Submitter: 
Patti
Description: 
This is a delicious cheese spread served with crackers. Everyone asks for the recipe when I make it!
