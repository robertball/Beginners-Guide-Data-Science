Title:
Old Fashioned Gingersnaps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups FLour
1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp ginger
1 tsp cinnamon
3/4 cup shortening or margarine
1 cup sugar
1 egg
3/4 cup mollasses

Directions:
Sift together flour, salt, soda, ginger and cinnamon
Beat shortening till creamy, add sugar and egg and mollasses. Stir in dry ingredients and blend thoroughly. Chill dough until firm and then shape into small balls and roll in granulated sugar. Arrange balls 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet and flatten with bottom of a glass. Bake 35o degrees for about 10-13 mins Coll on wire racks. Makes 44 cookies
