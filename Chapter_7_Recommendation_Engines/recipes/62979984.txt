Title:
Turkey-Filled Acorn Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1378833.jpg
Ingredients:
2 acorn squash
1 tsp. butter, softened
***
2 cups cubed or ground turkey or chicken,cooked
1/2 cup green onions,chopped
1 can (2-1/2 oz) sliced ripe olives, drained
1-1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 can (4 oz) diced green chiles
3/4 tsp. ground cumin
1 tsp. garlic salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/4 tsp. paprika
1/2 cup sour cream (or plain yogurt)

Directions:
Cut acorn squash in half lengthwise, remove seeds.
Spread butter over each cavity.
Turn squash, cavity sides down, or a rimmed baking pan lined with foil.
Bake at 350Â° for about 45 mintues, or until tender when pierced.
While the squash is baking, mix together the remaining ingredients and set aside.
Remove squash from oven and turn over.
Fill cavities with equal amount of turkey mixture.
Bake 15-20 minutes longer, or until heated through.
