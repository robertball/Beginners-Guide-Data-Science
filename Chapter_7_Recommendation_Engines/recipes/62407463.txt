Title:
My Potato Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Potatoes
Hard boiled eggs, diced
large onion, diced
bread and butter pickes, diced
pimiento (optional)
light miracle whip
a little mustard
dry mustard
white vinegar (try cider vinegar)
a little sugar
salt & pepper

Directions:
Cook and cool potatoes; cube and add additional ingredients to taste. Refrigerate for 1 hour.
