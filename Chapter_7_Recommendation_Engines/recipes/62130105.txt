Title:
Pot Likker Chicken Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 quarts pot likker
3 large potatoes, washed and chopped
1/2 green bell bepper, chopped
3 or 4 carrots, sliced
1 leek, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 store-bought rotisserie chicken
1 small jar salsa
1 frying pepper, chopped
1 jalapeno pepper, chopped
1 small package each of frozen okra, baby corn, and snap peas
1 can pigeon peas
pinch (or more) each of cumin, cayenne pepper, chile pepper,  basil, black or white pepper, paprika, celery seed, marjoram, and dried chives
red chile pepper, to taste

Directions:
Heat pot likker on low heat in large pot or crock pot
Add all ingredients except garlic, leek, frying pepper, and jalapeno
In a fry pan, gently sautee remaining ingredients until garlic is lightly browned
Add remaining ingredients to the large pot and simmer for at least an hour
Serve warm with a hearty multi-grain bread for dipping

Description: 
A versatile and nippy chicken soup made from the leftover stock from collard greens
