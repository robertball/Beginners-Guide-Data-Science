Title:
Cranberry Nut Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup cranberries, coarsely chopped
1/2 cup chopped walnuts (opt.)
1 tbsp. grated orange peel
1 cup wheat flour
1 cup white flour
3/4 cup white sugar
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 1/2 tsp. baking soda
2 tbsp. butter
1 tsp. salt
3/4 cup orange juice
1 egg

Directions:
Mix dry ingredients. Cut in butter. Stir in orange juice, egg, and orange peel, just to moisten. Fold in cranberries and nuts. Spoon into loaf pan. Bake 60 minutes at 350 degrees or until done.
