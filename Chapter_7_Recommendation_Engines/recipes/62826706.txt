Title:
Kelli's Deep Fried Taco's Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/668745.jpg
Ingredients:
1 loaf frozen bread dough (thawed and raised)
2 lbs hamburger
2 packages taco seasoning
1 package of shredded cheese
all your favorite taco toppers
deep fryer or raised skillet for frying
oil for frying

Directions:
thaw out frozen bread dough and let rise according to package.
Brown 2 lbs of hamburger and mix in seasoning according to package.
Roll out small balls of dough to about a 6 inch circle or about the size of a small tortilla shell.
Place a small amount of taco meat into the center of the dough and pinch shut into a taco shape.
Place into fryer and cook until golden brown. flip taco during cooking so both side cook evenly.
After frying add some extra meat, cheese and your favorite taco toppings. and enjoy

these taco's are amazingly yummy and are perfect if your looking for something different for dinner. my kids ask for these often.
