Title:
Stuffed Squash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 small squash halved and cleaned
4 strips bacon, in 1/2 inch pieces
3 chicken breasts, diced
2/3 cup zucchini, diced
2/3 cup mushrooms, diced
2/3 cup red bell pepper, diced
2/3 cup onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1-14 ounce can diced tomatoes, drained
1/3 cup red wine
1 cup cooked rice
1/2 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp red chili flakes
salt and pepper to taste
1 cup green onions, chopped fine
3/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
salt and

Directions:
Steam the halved and cleaned squash until fork tender about 20 minutes.
Meanwhile
In a dry frying pan over low heat roast the cumin until it perfumes the air, about 2 minutes tossing often, set aside.
Fry bacon pieces over medium high heat until the bacon fat sweats out.
Add diced chicken and saute until chicken starts to brown.
Add diced zucchini, mushrooms, red bell peppers and onions and saute until vegetables are tender about five minutes.
Add minced garlic, cumin and red pepper flakes cook for 2 minutes.
Add drained tomatoes and red wine, cook until liquid has reduced.
Add rice and stir to combine.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Remove from heat and stir in green onions reserving 2 tbsp.
Fill squash halves with chicken and rice mixture.
Top with crumbled feta cheese and reserved green onions
Bake at 425 for 25 minutes
