Title:
Serious Detox Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Granny Smith Apple - cored
1 Large Beet - Peeled
3-4 Beet Top Leaves
3-4 Carrots - Peeled
3-4 Celery Stalks
Small piece of ginger root
1 Lime

Directions:
Once you have peeled, cut and washed all your ingredients, turn on your juicer and begin adding your fruits/veggies one by one in the order listed. Then stir it up and serve. Drink immediately.

Submitter: 
Ed Price
Description: 
This is one of our favorite recipes and we drink this once a day for breakfast or as a snack.
