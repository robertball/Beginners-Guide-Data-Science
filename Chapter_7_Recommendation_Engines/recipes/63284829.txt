Title:
Lawnmower Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/864804.jpg
Ingredients:
FOR LAWNMOWER:
1 1/2 oz. knob creek bourbon
1 oz. lime juice
1 oz. ginger syrup (see recipe below)
4 leaves fresh mint (more for garnish)
FOR GINGER SYRUP:
1 cup cane sugar
1 cup water
Â½ cup grated ginger (cheese shredder size)

Directions:
TO MAKE GINGER SYRUP:
Bring the sugar and water to a boil. Remove from the heat.
Stir in the ginger and let cool to room temperature.
Strain out the ginger and refrigerate in a jar for up to one month.
TO MAKE LAWNMOWER:
Put ice in a rocks glass for chilling.
Put everything in a cocktail shaker with ice and shake for 10-15 seconds.
Dump the ice from the chilled glass. Add ice and the strained cocktail.
Garnish with fresh mint.
