Title:
artichoke- feta quiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 extra largeeggs
1 c 2% milk
1/2 can 14 oz artichoke hearts, drained and roughly chopped
1/4 c crumbled feta cheese
2 tbsp chopped sundried tomatoes (if not packed in oil you will need to soften with hot water for 10 mins before cooking)
2 links cooked turkey or chicken sausage
3/4 tsp kosher salt
cracked pepper to taste
1 frozen pie crust

Directions:
preheat oven to 350
whisk eggs until frothy, then stir in milk, artichoke hearts, feta, tomatoes, sausage, salt and papper. pour into crust
place in oven and bake for 45 mins or until the eggs are completely setand the top is lightly golden brown. cool for at least 5 minutes before slicing and serving
