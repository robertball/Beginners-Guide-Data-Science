Title:
Dock's Pecan-Crusted Salmon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter, melted
1 teaspoon grated onion
1 teaspoon Worchestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon paprika
4 (6-oz) salmon fillets
salt /pepper
Pecans, finely chopped
Maple syrup
Olive oil
Apples
Mushrooms, sliced
Fresh spinach
Prepared horseradish sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Mix together melted butter, onion, paprika and Worch. sauce.
Arrange salmon fillets in a single layer in baking dish
Brush with butter mixture, sprinkle with salt/pepper
Pat on a layer of pecans
Drizzle lightly with maple syrup
Bake salmon uncovered, 25-30 minutes or until just firm
Don't overcook
While salmon is cooking, saute sliced mushroom and apple slices in butter or oil
Right before salmon is ready, frizzle spinach in a bit of oil or butter
To serve:
Lay spinach on platter, top with sauteed mushrooms/apples
Top with salmon
Crisscross each filet with ribbons of horseradish sauce
Garnish with lemon wedges
Great with roasted sweet potatoes and green beans
