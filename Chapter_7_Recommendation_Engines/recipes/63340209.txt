Title:
North African Sweet Potato Burgers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 c baked or boiled and mashed sweet potatoes
1 c quick-cooking rolled oats.
3/4 c chopped onion
1/2 c chopped peanuts or soynuts
1/2 c chopped cilantro (leaves and stems)
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 Tbsp minced fresh ginger
1 1/2 teaspoons cumin
1 tsp salt

Directions:
1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F
2. Mix all the ingredients together, and form into burgers
3. Arrange the burgers on an oiled baking sheet, and bake for 15 minutes on each side until browned.

Submitter: 
Megan
Description: 
These are amazing! I made a bunch and froze them instead of going to the store and buying premade frozen burgers. Really great with pickles and hummus on a sandwich.
