Title:
Big Ray's Overnight Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2060966.jpg
Ingredients:
6 cups torn Romaine lettuce
3/4 cup frozen peas
1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
1 4 oz package sliced pepperoni
3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 15 oz can garbanzo beans, drained
3/4 cup green goddess salad dressing
3/4 cup mayonnasi

Directions:
In a large bowl layer half the lettuce, all the peas, tomatoes,  pepperoni cheese and garbanzo beans.  Top with remaining lettuce.  Combine green goddess salad dressing and mayonnaise and spread on top, sealing to edge of bowl.. Cover and refrigerate 3 hours to overnight.  Toss well and serve.
