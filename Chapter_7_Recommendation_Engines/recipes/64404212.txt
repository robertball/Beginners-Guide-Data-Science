Title:
Klunkers Loaded Mashed Potatoes with Mushrooms and Spinach Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the avocado cashew cream:
1 ripe avocado
1/2c soaked cashews
1/2 tsp white pepper
1/2 c plant milk (more if too thick)
5-6 med yukon golds, cubed and boiled 15 min
8 oz cremini mushrooms, sliced
1 medium yellow onion, minced
2 large handfuls of baby spinach
8 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp pink salt (optional)

Directions:
Blend first 4 ingredients until smooth. Set aside.
While potatoes are cooking, saute remaining ingredients until very wilted and tender.
With a hand mixer, whip the potatoes with the cream sauce until they are creamy. Add the veggies and mix well.
Add 4T chopped fresh chives. (You could use dried too)
Serve hot. When tender, drain potatoes and return to the pot.

Submitter: 
Wilma Laura Wiggins
Description: 
Rich mashed potatoes without fat or dairy. No gravy needed.
