Title:
Masa Boats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 1/4 c masa
1 1/2 c water
1/3 c flour
2 T shortning
1 t baking powder
3/4 t salt

Directions:
Mix all and make into 12 disks, cover with plastic wrap.
Brown 2 min per side, pinch up a lip.
Deep fry 360* for a few seconds.
Fill with anything mexican style.
