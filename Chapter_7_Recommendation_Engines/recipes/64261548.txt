Title:
Gluten Free, Vegan: Royal Icing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1824119.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups powdered sugar
5 tbsp orange juice
1 tbsp cornstarch
Optional: food coloring

Directions:
In a mixing bowl, whisk powdered sugar, orange juice, and cornstarch. Add more juice to adjust consistency. Thicker for piping and thinner for flooding.
Scoop icing into a piping bag fitted with a piping tip or snipped corner.
Decorate and enjoy!
