Title:
Mini Turkey Loafs with Feta, Basil and Balsamic Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1129685.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lb ground turkey; 4 chopped green onions; 1/4 cup crumbled feta;  1 tbsp basil; 1 tsp worcestershire sauce; 1/2 cup balsamic vinegar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix ground turkey, onions, feta, basil and Worcestershire sauce in bowl.
Scoop turkey mixture into a muffin pan.
Bake for 15-20 minutes until 165 degrees inside.
While turkey is baking, reduce balsamic vinegar to a glaze by pouring into skillet and continuously stirring over low-med heat until the consistency of syrup.
Pour glaze over turkey loafs and serve.
