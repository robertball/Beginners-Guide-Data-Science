Title:
Fruity Guacamole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1106303.jpg
Ingredients:
1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and diced
1 1/2 teaspoons minced red onion
1 teaspoon minced seeded serrano chile
12 red grapes, halved
1/2 cup fresh diced peaches
salt to taste
2 tablespoons pomegranate seeds (optional)

Directions:
Gently mash the avocado with the onion and serrano pepper in a bowl. Mix in the grapes and peaches. Season with salt and, if desired, garnish with pomegranate seeds to serve.

Submitter: 
Nicole
Description: 
Guacamole with a fruity twist. This dip is also great with mangoes in place of the peaches.
