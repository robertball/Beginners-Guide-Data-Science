Title:
Easy Shake and Bake Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 pounds chicken, cut into pieces
1/2 cup bread crumbs or cornflake crumbs
1/4 cup parmesan cheese, finely grated
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon sage
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
dash cayenne pepper, to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
Mix crumbs, cheese and all spices together in a bag. Add chicken parts in a bag and shake until well coated.
Lightly coat 9x13 inch baking dish with oil. Place chicken in the baking dish skin side up and bake for 35 minutes, until tender and juices run clear.
