Title:
Tomato and Broccoli Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup frozen broccoli
1 clove of garlic
2 medium tomatoes
1/2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp dried parsley
1/4 tsp pepper
1 tsp olive oil
1oz of low fat shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 small onion
1oz pasta prepared as box directions say

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Prepare pasta according to box directions, omit salt. Spray a medium oven-proof casserole with cooking spray.
Microwave frozen broccoli for 3 minutes, stir and cook 1 minute more: let stand one minute, and drain.
Chop tomatoes, garlic and onion. Heat oil in a medium sized non-stick skille over low-medium heat and saute for 5-7 minutes.
Raise temperature to medium, add tomatoes, parsley, oregano and pepper and continue cooking, stirring occasionaly, aprox. 5-7 minutes.
Add cooked pasta and broccoli to skillet and stir to heat through, 1-2 minutes. Place mixture in a casserole dish, sprinkle with cheese and bake for 20-25 minutes.
