Title:
Creamy Lemon Cheesecake with Blueberry Topping Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/3 cup butter, sofened
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup all purpose flour
1/2 cup gram cracker crumbs
1/8 teaspoon salt
4 8-ounce packages cream cheese, sofened
1 8-ounce carton mascarpone cheese
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 eggs
2 tablespoons finely shredded lemon peel
3 tablespoons lemon juice
2 cups blueberries
1/2 cup sugar
1 tablespoon water
3 tablespoons lemon juice

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*F. Wrap a double layer of foil around the bottom and side of an ungreased 9" springform pan, to form a watertight seal, set aside.  For crust: In a medium bowl beat butter with and electric mixer on medium to high speed for 30 seconds.  Add the 1/4 cup sugar, beat until combined.  Add flour, gram cracker crumbs, and salt, beat until crumbly.  Press mixture into bottom of the pan.  Bake for 20 minutes.  Reduce oven temp. to 325*F.
Meanwhile, for filling: In a very large bowl combine cream cheese, mascarpone cheese and the 1 1/2 cups sugar.  Beat on meduim speed until smooth.  Add eggs, lemon peel, and 3 tablespoons lemon juice, beat just until combined.  Pour over crust, spreading evenly.  Place the foil wrapped pan into a roasting pan.  Pour enough hot water into the roasting pan to reach 1/2 way up the side of the springform pan.
Bake for 1 hour 15min, to 1 hour 30min, or until a 2" area around the outside edge appears set and the center appears set when gently shaken.  Remove springform pan from water.  Cool in pan on wire rack for 15min.  Using a small sharp knife loosen edge of cheesecake from side of pan, cool completely (about 1 1/2 hours) Remove foil.  Cover and chill for 6-24 hours.
For Topping: In a medium saucepan combine 2 cups blueberries, 1/2 cup sugar, 3 tablespoons lemon juice and 1 tablespoon water.  Cook and stir until mixture comes to a boil.  Boil gently uncovered for 15-20 minutes, or until slightly thickened.  Cool. Cover and chill until ready to serve, or serve warm.
