Title:
Ice Cream Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 package Oreo cookies
1/4 cup butter,melted
1/2 gallon ice cream
whipped topping
spring form pan

Directions:
1.Refrigerate ice cream,set aside 12 cookies, crush remainder.Mix cookies with melted butter.
2.Press in bottom of spring from pan then put in freezer.
3.When ice cream is soft,stir til even.Spoon over cookies.Put in freezer til ice cream is hard.Remove from freezer.
4. Remove sides from pan.Spread whipped topping over top and sides of cake.
5.Break up remaining cookies,and place on whipped toppings.Put in freezer til ready to serve.
