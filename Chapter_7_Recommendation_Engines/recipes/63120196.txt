Title:
Easy Pizza Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T Yeast
1 C warm water
2 1/2 cups flour
2 T oil
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp salt

Directions:
Dissolve yeast in warm water.  Stir in flour, oil, sugar and salt.  Beat 25 strokes.  Cover and let sit for 5 minutes.  Bake at 500 degrees for 15-20 minutes after putting sauce and toppings on it.
