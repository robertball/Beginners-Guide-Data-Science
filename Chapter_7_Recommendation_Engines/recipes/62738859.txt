Title:
Old-Fashioned Roasted Pecans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 egg white
1 tablespoon vanilla
2 1/4 cups pecan halves
1/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Directions:
Preheat oven to 225 degrees F (105 degrees C). Lightly grease a rimmed baking pan.
Combine the egg white and vanilla in a mixing bowl; beat until fluffy. In a separate bowl mix the sugars, salt, and cinnamon untill combined. when the egg whites start to form soft peaks gradually add the sugar mixture. after all the sugar has been added scrape the bowl down then continue beating till stiff peaks have formed.  Fold in the pecans to coat evenly.  Spread the nuts over the prepared pan.
Bake in preheated oven until toasted and fragrant, stirring every 15 minutes, about 1 hour. Cool on pan, and store in an airtight container.
