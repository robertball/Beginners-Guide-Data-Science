Title:
Light Citrus Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Sugar
2 teaspoons orange juice
2  Packets Sweet & Low
2  Larger egg whites
1/4 teaspoon Cream of tartar
1/2 teaspoon Vanilla
1/2 teaspoon finely grated orange zest
Orange food coloring (if desired)

Directions:
Combine sugar, water, Sweet'n Low, egg whites, and cream of tartar in top of a double boiler and beat at high speed for 1 minute.
Set over simmering water in the bottom of the double boiler. Continue to beat at high speed for 4-5 minutes or until soft peaks form.
Remove from heat. Add vanilla, zest, and food coloring (if desired) to frosting and continue beating at high speed until thick.

Description: 
This frosting is intended to go with Chocolate Citrus Cupcakes. It, too, is amazingly healthy!
