Title:
White Chocolate Cranberry Pumpkin Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 cups Sugar
3.5 cups Flour
1 tsp Salt
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp allspice
1 cup white chocolate chips
1 cup dried cranberries
1 cup vegatable oil
2 tsp vanilla extract
2 cup pure pumpkin
2 cups orange juice
4 eggs
1 cup brown sugar

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
Mix together sugar, flour, salt, baking soda, allspice
In a separate bowl, beat eggs then mix in oil, pumpkin, orange juice, and vanilla.
Hand stir wet and dry ingredients together.
Mix in white chocolate chip and cranberries by hand as well.
Scoop mix into muffin pan (wrappers optional)
Sprinkle brown sugar on top of every muffin just before baking.

Submitter: 
CelticMommy
Description: 
These spicy muffins are moist and full of flavor. The cranberry perfectly complements the pumpkin flavor while hints of orange keep the muffins tasting light.  The white chocolate chips are optional if you want to make these more of a yummy dessert.  The perfect fall treat!
