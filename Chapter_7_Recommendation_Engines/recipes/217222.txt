Title:
Monkey Gland Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/822051.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups ice
2 fluid ounces gin
1 fluid ounce fresh orange juice
1/8 teaspoon Pernod
2 dashes grenadine
1 (3 inch) piece orange peel

Directions:
Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Pour in gin, orange juice, Pernod, and grenadine. Shake well and strain into a chilled glass. Garnish with a twist of orange peel.

Submitter: 
Lorem Ipsum
Description: 
Not the most appetizing of names, but a refreshing gin cocktail just the same. Swap out the Pernod with a dash of Benedictine if the mood strikes.
