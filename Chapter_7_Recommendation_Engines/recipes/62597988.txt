Title:
Rotisserie Chicken or any meat marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 teaspoons salt
2 teaspoons paprika
2 TBL Chopped Onion
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1 Tbl fresh marjoram
1 Tbl fresh rosemary
1 teaspoon white pepper
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
2 teaspoon fresh chopped garlic
1/4 c. olive oil
1/4 c. lemon juice
2 (4 pound) whole chickens or goat legs

Directions:
Throw all ing. except meat in blender. Marinate meat in mix over night or several hours.Cook chicken on rotisserie on grill or in oven on low heat until done.

Description:
