Title:
Fricase de pollo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless chicken legs
2 Tbl tomato paste
1/2 large red bell pepper
1/2 med. red onion.
4 garlic cloves
virgin olive oil (as desired)
water (to thin sauce as desired)
*PRESSURE COOKER*

Directions:
Dice bell pepper and red onion.
Smash OR mince garlic.
Heat oil in bottom of pressue cooker.
Cook garlic in oil, until golden, Do not burn.
Put pepper and onion in the pan and cook as desired.
Spoon in Tomato paste, and pour in water until paste thins out and mixes well.
Place Chicken in pan, evenly spaced. Add more water as needed , so chicken is nearly covered.
Close pressure cooker and bring up to pressure. Cook on pressure for 15 min. for fall of the bone tenderness, and allow pressure to run out on it's own.
Serve with frijoles negros, and arros blanco (black beans and rice).
