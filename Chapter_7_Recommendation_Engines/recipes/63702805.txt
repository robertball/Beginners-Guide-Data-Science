Title:
Quick and Easy Baja Style Fish Tacos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 beer-battered fish fillets
8 corn tortillas
2 cups coleslaw mix
1/2 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
1 cup pico de gallo
1 dash chile-garlic sauce (such as SrirachaÂ®) (optional)

Directions:
Cook battered fish fillets according to package directions.
Heat tortillas for 30 seconds in the microwave.
Top each tortilla wish fish, coleslaw mix, cheese, and pico de gallo.
