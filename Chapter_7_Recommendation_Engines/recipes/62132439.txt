Title:
Peanut Butter Pumpkins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag (10 oz) peanut butter chips
1 can (16 oz) vanilla frosting
1/4 teaspoon orange paste icing color
12 small pretzel sticks broken in half

Directions:
line cookie sheet with waxed paper
in 2 qt sauce pan melt peanut butter chips over low heat stirring constantly until smooth
remove from heat stir in frosting and and coloring
refrigerate for 1 hour
shape mixture into 1/4 inch balls flatten slightly
make grooves on side to resemble a pumpkin
press i pretzel piece into top for stem
place on cookie sheet refrigerate for at least 1 hour prior to serving
