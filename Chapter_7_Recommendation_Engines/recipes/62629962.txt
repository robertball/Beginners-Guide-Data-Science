Title:
Tarragon Chicken Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBS oil
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cubed
2 cups celery, chopped
2 cups carrots, chopped
3 cups mushrooms, sliced (optional)
1 cup onion, sliced
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 35 oz. can plum tomatoes
Broth
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup dry white wine
1 TBS tarragon
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 cup corn starch
1/4 cup water

Directions:
In a 5 qt. pan, heat oil over medium heat.  Add chicken and saute for 3 mins.
Remove chicken, add celery, carrots, mushrooms (if using) garlic, and can tomatoes.  Saute 3 mins.
Add broth ingredients (except corn starch & water) to vegetables and bring to a boil.  Reduce the heat and simmer 10 mins.
Mix corn starch with water.  Stir into the veggie mixture, stirring constantly.  Bring to a boil for 1 min.
Add chicken and heat one more minute.  Serve

Submitter: 
Lisa Proctor
Description: 
If you like tarragon, you will love this recipe.  A healthy and tasty chicken stew that can be made quick for a meal.  Hope you enjoy it as much as my family has.
