Title:
Italian Seasoned Bread Crumbs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/290410.jpg
Ingredients:
3 cups dry bread crumbs (*see note)
1 1/2 Tablespoons dried Italian seasoning
1/2 Tablespoon dried parsley flakes
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl, stirring well.
Store in an airtight container for use in your favorite recipes.

Submitter: 
Pam Ziegler Lutz
Description: 
Now you can have Italian bread crumbs quickly, easily, and far less costly than store bought!
