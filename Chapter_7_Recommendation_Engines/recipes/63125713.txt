Title:
Clam Linguine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/796595.jpg
Ingredients:
1 8oz package dry linguine or spaghetti
2 6 1/2 oz cans of clams
2 Tablespoons olive oil
2 Tablespoons butter
2 each cloves of garlic (substitute 1 teaspoon minced bottled garlic), minced
1/4 Teaspoon pepper
1/4 Teaspoon dried basil, crushed
1/4 Teaspoon dried oregano, crushed
salt to taste
2 Tablespoons parsley, snipped
parmesan cheese, to taste

Directions:
Cook the linguine according to package directions; drain and keep hot.
Meanwhile drain the clams; reserve the liquid and set aside.
In a 2-quart saucepan over medium-high heat, heat the olive oil and butter until the olive oil is hot and the butter is melted. Add the garlic; cook and stir for 2 to 3 minutes. (Do not brown the garlic).
Carefully add the reserved clam liquid, pepper, basil, oregeno and salt to taste to the saucepan. Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered for 3 minutes. Stir in the clams; simmer until heated through. Stir the parsley into the clam sauce.
Pour the clam sauce over the hot linguine and toss gently to mix the ingredients. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese, if desired. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
James Noble
Description: 
Delicious for a quick supper. Eat with a crusty bread.
