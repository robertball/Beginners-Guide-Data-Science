Title:
Brunch Punch Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (46oz) can pinapple juice, chilled
3 cups orange juice, chilled
2 cups cranberry juice, chilled
3/4 cup powdered sugar
1/4 cup lime juice
granish with citrus wedges and marashino cherries

Directions:
Stir together all ingredients.  Serve immediately, or cover and chill for 2-24 hours. Stir before serving. Garnish, if desired.
