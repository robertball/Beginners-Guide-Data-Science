Title:
Mom's Pecan Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tbl butter
1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs
2 TBl flour
dash salt
1 cup chopped pecans
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup white Karo syrup

Directions:
Cream butter & sugar with mixer. Add remaining ingredients.
Bake in unbaked pastry shell at 350 for about 1 hour---until it doesn't shimmy
