Title:
Manicotti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/353784.jpg
Ingredients:
Filling:
1 lb. ground beef
1/4 C onion, chopped
3 slices of bread, torn into small pieces
3 C mozzarella cheese, shredded
1 egg
1/2 C milk
1 T dried parsley
1 t salt
1/4 t pepper
8 oz. package of manicotti shells
Sauce:
1 4 oz. can mushrooms, undrained
1 15 oz. tomato sauce
1 12 oz. tomato paste
1/4 C onions, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
4 C water
1 T Italian seasoning
1/2 t sugar
1/2 t salt
1/8 t pepper

Directions:
Brown meat with onion; drain.
In a large bowl mix together cooked meat with remaining filling ingredients, except shells.
Stuff uncooked shells.
Place in an ungreased 9x13 pan.
In a large pan add all ingredients for sauce.
Heat to boiling, stirring occasionally.
Simmer for 5 minutes.
Pour sauce over shells.
Bake, covered, at 375 degrees for 90 minutes or until shells are tender.
Cool 10 minutes before serving.
