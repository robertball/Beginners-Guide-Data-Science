Title:
Mexican Fiesta Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
All ingredidants are approximate, adjust according to taste
1 bag coleslaw cabbage
1/8 cup diced cilantro
1/4 cup diced green onion
1/2 cup diced sweet onion
1/2 cup diced red onion
2 med. jalepenos diced
7-8 oz. canned diced tomato w/chilies drained
1/8 cup lime juice
optional-chili sauce

Directions:
Toss all ingrediants together in a bowl. Cover and refridgerate. The longer it sits, the more the flavors blend. To spice things up even more, add a little chili sauce to lime juice before mixing.
Servings Suggestions:
Topped with chicken breast or carne asada
Topped with cruchy tortilla strips
Use on sandwiches, wraps & burgers instead of lettuce
Serve with Chips and Salsa or 7 layer dip

Submitter:

Description: 
A colorful sweet and spicy mexican salad that can be used all week long in different dishes.
