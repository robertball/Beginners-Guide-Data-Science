Title:
Sweet and Salty Nuts Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb bag walnuts
Butter, about 2 Tbl
1/2 cup sugar
1/3 cup light colored corn syrup
Course sea salt or kosher salt, about 1 Tbl or 2 tsp
1/2 tsp freshly ground pepper
Course raw sugar

Directions:
Preheat over to 350 degrees.
Butter a 15x10x1 inch baking pan.
Stir together the nuts, sugar, corn syrup, salt and pepper until well com bined, spread in pan.
Bake 25 minutes or until golden and bubbly, stir once or twice.
Remove and sprinkle generously with raw sugar and toss.
Transfer to foil and cool.  Break apart to serve.

Description: 
Origin I think is Sunset Magazine.  As of 2010, have made this at least 5 years, every holiday season, would not alter a thing, so delicious.  Toss with salads or serve with appetizers, or on top of brussel sprouts, very versatile!!
