Title:
Fajita Marinade Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 c. fresh lime juice
1 T. chopped garlic
1 T. black pepper
6 T. olive oil
4 T. chopped cilantro
Salt and pepper to taste
2 lbs. boneless, skinless chicken

Directions:
Combine lime juice, garlic, pepper, oil, cilantro, salt and pepper.  Marinate chicken covered in refrigerator 1 hour or as long as overnight.  Grill on barbecue grill (over mesquite if desired) until done.  Cut into bite-sized strips.  Place on warm tortillas and add desired toppings - shredded lettuce, tomatoe, salsa, pico de gallo, guacamole and/or sour cream.
