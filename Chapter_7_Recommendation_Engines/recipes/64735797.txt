Title:
Dandelion Leaf Swirl Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6560171.jpg
Ingredients:
1 Pound Dandelion Leaves (Raw), then chop and cook
1/2 Cup Garlic, minced
1/4 Cup Olive Oil
Fresh Ground Black Pepper
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
5 Ounces Freshly Grated Parmesan Cheese
8 Ounces Freshly Grated Mozzarella Cheese
5 Cups All Purpose Flour
3 Cups Water, lukewarm
4 Tablespoons Honey
3 Tablespoons Dry Active Yeast
Topping
3 T Buter, melted
3 Cloves Garlic, finely minced

Directions:
Sauté garlic in the olive oil in the skillet add dandelion leaves in a bowl and stir in the salt and fresh Ground Black Pepper
Place cover over skillet and sauté until completely wilted stirring occasionally
Stir in cheese and set aside
In a measuring cup combine 3 Cups Luke warm water, honey and yeast allowing yeast to proof
In a separate bowl, combine 5 Cups flour, salt and proofed yeast mixture
Kneed until forming a dough
If dough seems to wet add a little more flour
On a lightly floured surface, roll out dough so it's about 1/4 inch thick
Spoon the dandelion cheese mixture over the top
Roll the dough into a log
Make slits into top of log with a sharp knife
Cover with a damp towel and allow to rise in a warm location for 20 minutes
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
Melt butter in small dish and add finely minced garlic
When bread is done rising brush the top of the rolls with butter and garlic
Bake until it is golden brown and bubbly about 25 minutes

Shanda
Description: 
A cheesy, garlicky, dandelion bread!  Warm deliciousness.
