Title:
Chicken, Vegetable and Rice Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 pound dried navy beans
2 tablespoons olive oil
4 pound chicken, cut up
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 cup yellow onions, chopped
1/2 cup carrots, chopped
1/2 cup celery, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 cup zucchini, chopped
1/2 cup yellow squash, chopped
2 quarts chicken stock
1/2 cup tomatoes, seeded and chopped
4 sprigs parsley
1 bay leaf
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/3 cup incooked rice
1/2 cup green onions, chopped
1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
1/4 cup parsley, chopped
1 cup torn spinach leaves

Directions:
Soak beans 8 hours; drain. Heat oil in large large pot over medium-high. Season chicken pieces with salt and pepper. In batches cook chicken 5 minutes per side. Remove chicken and pour off all but 2 tablespoons fat. Add onion, carrots, and celery, cook 3 minutes. Add garlic; cook 1 minute. Add zucchini and squash; cook 1 minute. Add stock, beans, tomatoes, parsley, bay leaf and cayenne. Return chicken to pot. Bring to a boil; simmer uncovered 1 hour. Remove chicken and remove the meat from the bones. Return to pot. Add rice, green onions, thyme, parsley and spinach; Simmer 15 minutes. Discard bay leaves and parsley sprigs.

Submitter:

Description: 
Don't let the number of ingredients scare you, this is a wonderful comforting winter soup.
