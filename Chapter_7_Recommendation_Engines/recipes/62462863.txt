Title:
Ranch Chicken Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Boneless chicken breasts
Crushed sour cream and onion potato chips
Ranch dressing

Directions:
Cut chicken breasts in half. Marinate for an hour in a bowl of ranch dressing.
Roll chicken strips in crushed chips.
Place on baking sheet and bake at 350 for 20-25 minutes.
