Title:
Tangy Meatball Stuffing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb ground beef
1/2 c bread crumbs
2 tbsp chopped parsley
1 tbsp instant minced onions
1 beaten egg
8 ounces tomato sauce
3/4 c water
1/2 c chopped green bell pepper
1/3 c chopped onion
2 tbsp vinegar & brown sugar
2 tsp prepared mustard
4 baked potatoes

Directions:
Combine beef, bread crumbs, parsley, minced onion, & egg. mix well, shape into small meatballs, brown meatballs in oil over  med-low heat. Remove meatballs from oil. Drain excess  fat froms killet, then add tomato sauce, water, pepper, chopped onion, vinegar, brown sugar & mustard to skillet.  Bring to a boil, then reduce heat & simmer x 30 minutes covered, stir occassionally, Add meatballs to sauce & spoon over each potato.

Submitter: 
Joanne Dembinski
Description: 
Perfect recipe for a quick meal, served over baked potatoes.
