Title:
Russian Teacakes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. butter, softened
1/2 c. powdered sugar
1 t. vanilla
2 1/4 c. flour
3/4 c. finely chopped nuts (almost dust)
1/4 t. salt
Powdered sugar

Directions:
Mix butter, first powdered sugar, and vanilla. Stir in flour, nuts, and salt until dough holds togther.
Shape dough into 1" balls. Place 1" apart on cookie sheet (will not spread).
Bake 10-12 minutes until set, but NOT brown on the bottom. Remove from cookie sheet.
After cooling slightly, roll in powdered sugar. Let cool completely, then roll again.

Submitter: 
Samantha S
Description: 
A staple in my home, the secret is to underbake these VERY SLIGHTLY.
