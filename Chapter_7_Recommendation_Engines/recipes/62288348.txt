Title:
Pumpkin Ginger Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 c flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
2 tsps ground ginger
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 c finely chopped crystalized ginger
2 lg eggs
3/4 c buttermilk
1/2 c canned pumpkin (not pumpkin pie mix)
1/2 c sugar
3/4 tsp vanilla
3 TBS unsalted butter, melted and slightly cooled

Directions:
Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, ground ginger and cinnamon together. Remove 2 TBS flour mix and toss with crystallized ginger in a small bowl. Whisk eggs, buttermilk, pumpkin, sugar and vanilla in a med bowl. Combine with flour mixture. Stir in butter and fold in crystalized ginger. Do not overmix. Heat waffle iron and make with 1/2 cup batter each.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is great with whipped cream.
