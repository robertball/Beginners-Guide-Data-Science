Title:
Hearty Sausage & Quinoa Minestrone Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1949223.jpg
Ingredients:
2 Italian sausages, casings removed
1/2 onion, diced
1 carrot, peeled and diced
1 celery stalk, diced
1 garlic clove, diced
2 cans (14.5 oz each) diced tomatoes
900 mL chicken broth
1/4 cup dry quinoa
1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
2 cups packed spinach

Directions:
Heat a large saucepan over medium. Crumble in sausage meat. Cook, stirring occasionally, until no longer pink, 3-5 minutes. Add onion, carrot, celery, and garlic to pan. Cook until onion starts to soften, about 3 min.
Add tomatoes, broth, quinoa and Italian seasoning. Gently boil until quinoa is cooked, about 20 min. Stir in spinach until just wilted, about 1 min.
