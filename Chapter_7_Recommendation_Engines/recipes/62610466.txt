Title:
The Ultimate Lasagna Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
For the Bolognaise Sauce.
300g Minced Beef
45g Pancetta (or Unsmoked Streaky Bacon ) - chopped
2tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1 clove Garlic - finely chopped
2 tbsp Onion - finely chopped
2tbsp Carrot - finely chopped
2 tbsp Celery - finely chopped
Dried Spice mix 1½ tsp Basil, 1½ tsp Oregano, ¼ tsp Coriander(Cilantro)and a few twists of the pepper mill.
1 x 170g tin Tomato Puree
2 x 400g tins Italian Plum Tomatoes - chopped
250ml White Wine
For Lasagne
Packet Lasagne sheets.
45g Butter
45g Plain Flour
1 Camembert Cheese - rind removed
120g Parmesan Cheese – grated
500ml Chicken Stock – Use cube
200ml Double Cream
Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Bolognaise Sauce
Heat oil in large pot or frying pan;
Add pancetta and fry gently for 4 or so minutes until brown but not crisp;
Add onions, garlic, spice mix and fry gently stirring for 1 minute;
Add carrot and celery and continue frying for further 4 minutes stirring occasionally;
Add mince and break down and brown slightly for some 3 minutes or so;
Add wine, turn up heat and reduce by half;
At first continue stirring to breakdown mince completely so no lumps;
When wine reduced, add puree, stir in quickly to avoid burning then add tomatoes, stir to break down further and bring to boil;
Then simmer, stirring occasionally, for about 45 minutes to 1 hour or longer if you want a more intense flavour.
Lasagna
Make a roux with butter and flour
Add 300ml Chicken stock and 200ml cream to make béchamel sauce
Add camembert and some parmesan, stir until melted and smooth.
Season to taste.
Smear bottom of baking dish with sauce and cover with layer of pasta
Mix remainder of sauce with bolognaise sauce and layer baking dish, sauce then layer of pasta etc sprinkle the remainder of parmesan over the top layer
Bake in preheated oven at 150c/gas2/300f for 40/45 mins or until brown and bubbling.

My version of the classic. The combination of Camembert and Parmesan cheeses turn this into an out of this world experience.
