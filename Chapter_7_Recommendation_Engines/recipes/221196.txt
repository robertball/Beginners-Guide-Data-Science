Title:
Overnight Ham and Cheese Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/876679.jpg
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon butter for greasing, or as needed
12 slices firm bread, discard crusts and cube bread
1 cup cubed Cheddar cheese
1 cup cubed ham
3 cups milk
6 large eggs
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions:
Butter bottom and sides of a shallow roasting dish.
Layer bread, Cheddar cheese, and ham in prepared roasting dish.
Beat milk, eggs, thyme, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl.
Pour egg mixture over bread mixture; cover and refrigerate 8 hours or overnight.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bake uncovered egg mixture in the preheated oven until center is set, about 1 hour. Allow to sit and cool for 15 minutes before serving.

Submitter: 
redheadchef
Description: 
We enjoy this omelet every Christmas morning! Throw the ingredients together the night before, put it in the refrigerator, and pop it in the oven the next morning. So simple and delicious!
