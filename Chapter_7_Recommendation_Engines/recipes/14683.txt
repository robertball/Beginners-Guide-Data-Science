Title:
Nuoc Cham Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/649500.jpg
Ingredients:
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
3 tablespoons white sugar
2 tablespoons lime juice
4 tablespoons fish sauce
1 cup water

Directions:
In a medium bowl combine the garlic, red pepper flakes, sugar, lime juice, fish sauce and water. Mix all together, adjusting to taste. Serve with grilled lemon grass beef.

Submitter: 
Ben S.
Description: 
A classic Vietnamese dipping sauce. Serve with grilled lemon grass beef (Bo Nuang Xa).
