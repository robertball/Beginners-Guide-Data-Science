Title:
Pot Roast w/ Merlot Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3.5 lbs rump roast
flour
garlic powder
1 large onion
8 oz Merlot
1 packet beefy onion soup mix
1 can condensed mushroom & roasted garlic soup
8 oz coke

Directions:
Trim roast of fat layer.
Dry with paper towels.
Cover roast in flour & garlic powder.
Heat a little oil in an iron skillet on an outside grill burner on high (dredging smokes a lot).
Sear each side 3-5 minutes until crust is brown.
While roast is searing, slice onion & put in crock pot.
After roast is browned add on top of onions in crock pot.
Deglaze pan with Merlot until slightly thick & add to crock pot.
Cover roast with both soups.
Mix soda in soup can & add to crock pot.
Cover & cook on high 2 hrs, then low 6 hrs.
Remove roast and cover with foil.
Mix cornstarch and cold water together and slowly mix into gravy to thicken.
