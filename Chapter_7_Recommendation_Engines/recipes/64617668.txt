Title:
Joy of cooking Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound of ground beef-80/20
1 can of diced tomatoes
2 cans of chili beans
2 cans of kidney beans
Chili powder
Cayenne pepper
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
Salt and pepper the ground beef in a large skillet.
Cook the beef until it's no longer pink.
Add all ingredients into a crock pot.
Cook in crock for 4-6 hours
You can also add water if you like your chili less thick.
Add chili and cayenne pepper to your taste.

Submitter: 
Sar Carn
Description: 
This is the recipe for the Joy of cooking chili.
I have altered it to my taste.
This is a very versatile and very good base chili recipe.
Make it your own just like I did!
