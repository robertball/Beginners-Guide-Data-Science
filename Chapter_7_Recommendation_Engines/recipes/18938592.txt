Title:
Eat More Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
175 ml honey
250 ml peanut butter
10 large marshmallows
250 ml chocolate chips
250 ml peanuts, whole or crushed
750 ml Rice Crispies

Directions:
In a large pot, bring honey and peanut butter to boil over medium heat, stirring constantly.
Add marshmallows and chocolate chips, stirring until melted.
Remove from heat and stir in peanuts and Rice Crispies.  Press into a greased 9 x 13 inch pan and let cool before cutting.

Submitter:

Description: 
These chewy bars are just like the store-bought bars that I knew as a kid.
