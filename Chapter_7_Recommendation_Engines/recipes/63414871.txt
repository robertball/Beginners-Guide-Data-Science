Title:
CARAMEL-NUT FROSTING Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 CUP BUTTER
1 CUP BROWN SUGAR, PACKED
1/4 CUP MILK
2 CUPS POWDERED SUGAR
2/3 CUP CHOPPED PECANS OR WALNUTS
1/2 TSP VANILLA
NUT HALVES, OPTIONAL

Directions:
COMBINE THE FIRST 3-INGREDIENTS OVER MEDUIM HEAT, STIRRING CONSTANTLY UNTIL MIXTURE BOILS AND SUGAR IS DISSOLVED.
COOL SLIGHTLY.
BEAT IN POWDERED SUGAR UNTIL FROSTING REACHES A SPREADING CONSISTENCY.
ADD VANILLA AND CHOPPED NUTS; COMBINE.
SPEAD ON COOLED CAKE.
GARNISH WITH NUT HALVES.
