Title:
Easy Slow Cooker Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs lean ground beef
1 onion, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 (15 oz) cans organic tri-bean blend (kidney beans, pinto beans and black beans), rinsed well
2 (15 oz) cans garlic and onion petit diced tomatoes, including juice
3 tbsp. chili powder
1 tsp. ground cumin
1 tsp. sea salt
1 tsp. pepper
dash hot sauce (optional)

Directions:
Cook ground beef, onion and garlic in a large saut√© pan over medium heat until meat is fully cooked.
Add beef mixture to slow cooker (may need to drain first depending on how lean the beef is).
Add remaining ingredients to the slow cooker.
Cook on high for 3-4 hours or low for 5-6 hours.
