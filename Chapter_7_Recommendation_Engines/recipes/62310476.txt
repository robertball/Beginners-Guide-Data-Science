Title:
Bacon Omelet Roll With Salsa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 eggs
1 C milk
1/3 C flour
1 red pepper, finely chopped
8 slices bacon, cooked, crumbled
3 green onions, finely chopped
1 t dried oregano leaves, crushed
1 C shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 C chunky salsa, warmed

Directions:
Heat oven to 350 degrees.
Line 15x10x1-inch with parchment paper, with ends extending over shortest sides of pan. Spray with cooking spray.
Beat eggs, milk and flour in large bowl with whisk until well blended. Stir in next 4 ingredients. Pour into prepared pan. Bake 14-16 min. or until edges are almost set. Top with cheese; bake 2 min. or until melted.
Roll up omelet immediately, starting at one short end and peeling off paper as omelet is rolled. Place on platter; cut into slices. Serve with salsa

Submitter: 
Judy Bodnar
Description: 
From Kraft Foods, quick breakfast dish made with simple ingredients.  Can substitute 6 finely chopped slices of ham for the bacon.
