Title:
Thumbprint Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup butter or margarine
1/4 cup sugar
1 tsp. almond extract
2 cups flour
1/2 tsp. salt
jam (your choice of flavor)

Directions:
Beat butter and sugar together until fluffy.
Blend in extract.
Add flour and salt.
Mix well.
Shape level tbsp. of dough into balls.
Place on cookie sheets, flatten slightly. Indent centers and fill with jam.
Bake @ 400 x 10-12 min. or just until lightly browned.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
Each cookie has a thumb-print size taste of your favorite jam baked on it.
