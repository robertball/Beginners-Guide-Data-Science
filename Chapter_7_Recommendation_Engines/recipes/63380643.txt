Title:
Chicken Tetrazinie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/907251.jpg
Ingredients:
5  chicken breasts
1-8 oz. box spaghetti cooked
1 large chopped onion
4 stalks chopped celery
1 jar sliced olives (opitional)
1 stick margarine
2 cups chicken broth
2 cups shredded sharp cheese
2 cans celery soup
1 can mushrooms & stems (drained)
2 t. salt
1 t. pepper
1 cup shredded sharp cheese
2 cups chopped pecans

Directions:
Cook chicken breast, set aside.
Cook spaghetti per directions, drain
Saute celery, onions in margarine, do not brown...just clarify.
In large bowl add chopped chicken breasts, add spaghetti, olives, sauted celery & onions, 2 cups of cheese, soup, salt & pepper, then chicken broth & mix well
It will be soupy.
Divide into 2 oblong casserole baking dishes. No need to butter baking dishes
Before cooking, sprinkle 1 cup cheese & 1 cup of chopped pecans on top of each casserole.
Bake in pre-heated oven at 350 for 35 minutes or until bubbly
Let set 10 minutes before serving.
