Title:
Tahini Balsamic Gravy Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
24 ounces vegetable broth
8 ounces water
2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
2 tablespoons tahini
1 tablespoon tamari
¼ cup aged balsamic vinegar
1/3 cup spelt flour (or gluten free flour)
1 tablespoon onion powder
1 tablespoon garlic powder
½ tablespoon ground mustard
sea salt, as desired
black pepper, as desired

Directions:
In a blender or a small mixing bowl, blend or stir vigorously all the ingredients until smooth.
Bring to a boil in a pot on high heat, stirring constantly until thickened.
