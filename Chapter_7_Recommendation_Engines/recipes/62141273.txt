Title:
Remarkable  Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 Cup unsalted butter, room temperature
1/2 Cup Crisco shortening, room temperature
1/2 Cup white sugar
1 Cup brown sugar (light or dark)
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp pure maple syrup
2 1/4 Cups flour
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
3 packets maple and brown sugar instant oatmeal
1 package chocolate chunk chocolate chips
1 Cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
In a medium mixing bowl sift together the flour, salt and baking soda; set aside.
In a large mixing bowl, using a spatula, blend the butter and shortening until soft.
Add sugars and mix well, then add the eggs, vanilla and pure maple syrup.
Mix in the flour in thirds until the batter is well incorporated.
Stir in the instant oatmeal, chocolate chunks and chopped walnuts.
Using your hands roll the cookie dough into 1 1/2 inch balls and arrange on parchment lined cookie sheet.
Bake for 10-12 minutes until lightly browned.
Cool slightly and using spatula remove to cooling rack.
