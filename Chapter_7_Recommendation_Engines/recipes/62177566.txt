Title:
Turkey ala king Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3C thick hot white sauce
2C diced cooked turkey
2Tbsp chopped pimentos
1Tbsp chopped green pepper
1 4oz can of mushrooms
1C cooked peas

Directions:
Combine the sauce with all ingredients and keep hot over boiling water until ready to serve. Serve over hot split and butterd baking powdered biscuts or in pastry shells.
