Title:
Oro Mojito Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/979867.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 ounces Oronoco™ rum
3 wedges lime
8 leaves mint
1 tablespoon sugar
Cracked ice
2 ounces lime juice
2 ounces soda water
Sugar cane

Directions:
In highball glass, muddle the mint leaves, 2 lime wedges and sugar.
Fill  the glass with cracked ice; pour in Oronoco(TM), lime juice, and soda.
Stir the cocktail gently and garnish with a lime wedge and sugar cane.

Submitter: 
thebar.com
Description: 
Using luxury rum like Oronoco™ Rum adds a subtle sweetness, smooth taste and underlying complexity to this drink recipe.
