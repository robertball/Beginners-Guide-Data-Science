Title:
Peppermint Bark Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
45 Hershey Kisses
1 (12 oz) bag white chocolate chips
8-10 drops peppermint oil
1/2 cup finely crushed peppermint candies (or candy canes)

Directions:
Unwrap kisses and put in a microwave safe bowl.  Microwave in 15 second intervals until smooth (be careful not to overdo it!)
Add peppermint oil, mix thoroughly (but also quickly)
Spread in a very thin layer on a 12x18" piece of parchmant paper.  Refigerate until chocolate is completely hardened.
Put white chocolate chips in a microwave safe bowl.  Micowave in 15 second intervals until smooth.  Add crushed peppermint candies. Spread on top of milk chocolate layer.  Cool completely.

Andrea Patton

A quick & easy holiday treat.
