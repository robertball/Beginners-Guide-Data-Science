Title:
Scarsdale Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 eggs, butter,6 torn Matzo crackers, salt, pepper, milk and chopped onion

Directions:
dice one half onion, add to skillet with one Tbsp. melted butter, sauté until onions are translucent. Add Matzo crackers (torn)). Beat 6 eggs with one Tbsp. milk. Add to sauté pan with onions and matzo crackers. Stir until scrambled or turn into an Omelet.

Submitter: 
caquilter
Description: 
This is my favorite breakfast recipe for family or visiting friends. Found from a friend many years ago in Scarsdale - probably the only thing she knew how to make. but I thank her.
