Title:
African Peanut Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 T. olive oil
1 med. red onion, finely chopped (1 1/4 c.)
1 med. green pepper, finely chopped (1 1/4 c)
1/2 c. chopped celery
1/2 c. chopped carrot
3 garlic cloves, minced
2 T. minced, peeled ginger
1 T. curry powder
1 (14 oz.) can tomatoes
1 bay leaf
4 c. chicken or veggie broth
1 (12-oz.) sweet potato, peeled, 1/2-inch pieces
1 1/2 c. shelled edamame
1/4 c. creamy or crunchy peanut or almond butter
1/4 c. chopped cilantro
1 (5-oz) bag baby spinach leaves, torn into bite-size pieces
1/2 tsp. salt; pepper

Directions:
Heat oil in Dutch over over medium heat.  Add onion, bell pepper, carrot and celery; saute until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
Add garlic, ginger and curry powder; saute till fragrant, about 1 minute.  Add tomatoes and bay leaf; cook, uncovered about 5 minutes to slightly reduce tomatoes.
Add broth and sweet potatoes.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer about 8  minutes or till potatoes are soft.  Stir in edamame and peanut putter.  Cook till hot.
Stir in cilantro and spinach till spinach wilts.  Season with salt and pepper.
