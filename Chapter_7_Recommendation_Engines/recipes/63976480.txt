Title:
Apple Crisp - Karen's Version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups tart apples, peeled, cored and sliced
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 tablespoon all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 cup quick-cooking oats
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 cup butter, melted
finely chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degree C).
Place the sliced apples in a 8x8 inch casserole. Mix the white sugar, flour and ground cinnamon together, and sprinkle over apples.
Combine the oats, 1 cup flour, brown sugar, nuts (if using), baking powder, baking soda and melted butter together. Crumble evenly over the apple mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 45 minutes.

Submitter: 
Karen Martin
Description: 
Cinnamon-spiced apples are baked with a sweet oat crumble in this simple dessert. It's great served with ice cream!Modified from: Apple Crisp II
