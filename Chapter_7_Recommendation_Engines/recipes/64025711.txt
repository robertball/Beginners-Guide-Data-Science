Title:
Red Raspberry Bleu Cheese Walnut Vinaigrette Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup olive oil
1 cup seasoned rice vinegar
1 (10 ounce) jar seedless raspberry jam
1/2 cup bleu cheese
1/4 cup chopped walnuts

Directions:
Combine the olive oil, rice vinegar and raspberry jam with a stick blender. Process until smooth. Add walnuts & process to break up the bits. Add Bleu cheese & blend just until incorporated. Store in a jar in the refrigerator.

Submitter: 
Paige
Description: 
A very easy, flavor-filled dressing that goes with any kind of salad. Only five ingredients for a delicious salad addition!Modified from: Red Raspberry Vinaigrette
