Title:
Mason Jar Egg and Tuna Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 hard boiled eggs, chopped
2/3 cups mayonnaise
1/2 tsp. seasoning salt
1/2 cup chopped pickles
2 cups arugula
6 oz. tuna in water

Directions:
Mix the eggs, mayonnaise and salt in a bowl.  Divide evenly between 4 pint jars. Distribute the pickles, tuna and arugula (in that order) evenly between the jars.  Cover tightly and refrigerate until ready to eat.

Submitter: 
Rebecca Winbauer
Description: 
Make these on Sunday and have 4 meals ready for lunches or dinners during the week!  Cooking time is for the eggs if they are not already boiled.
