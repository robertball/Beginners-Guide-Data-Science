Title:
Zesty Mediterranean Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
29 ounces (1 large can) small white beans
7.5 ounces (1 medium can) cut black olives
1.25 ounces (1/2 small jar) capers
olive oil
5 pignoli
mustard powder to taste

Directions:
Pour olive oil into a pan until it just coats the bottom.
Rinse the beans, olives, and capers.  Place them in the frying pan.
Crush the pignoli.  Add them to the frying pan.
Add mustard powder to taste.
Saute on medium heat until everything is well-heated.
