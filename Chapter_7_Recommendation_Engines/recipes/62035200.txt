Title:
Shrimp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can salad shrimp
1 can condensed tomato soup
1 8-oz pkg. cream cheese
1/4 cup chopped green onions (or to taste)or chopped chives
4-6 dashes worchestershire sauce
4-6 dashes tabasco
1 envelope unflavoured gelatin mixed with 1 tbsp. warm water

Directions:
Place cream cheese and soup in bowl and microwave on medium heat until cream cheese softened.  Using wire wisk, beat until mixture is smooth.    Drain shrimp and mash with fork - add to soup mixture.  Add tabasco and worchestershire.  Stir in green onion (I use tops only.  Combine gelatin and warm water and stir into mixture.   Chill for 1-2 hours or until firm.

Submitter: 
Baggy
Description: 
Great dip, firmish in texture, using canned shrimp. tomato soup and cream cheese.  Excellent with crackers, Bugles or Tortilla chips.
