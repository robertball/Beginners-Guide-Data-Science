Title:
Mother's Squash Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs. yellow squash
1 cup sour cream
2 small chopped onions
1 pkg. Pepperridge Farm cornbread stuffing mix
1 stick butter
1 can cream of chicken soup
3 med. carrots grated
Grated cheese

Directions:
Cook squash in salted water until tender, drain.
Add soup, sour cream, onions, carrots, and 1/2 stuffing mix.
Mix other half of stuffing with 1 stick melted butter.
Line bottom and sides of dish with 2/3 of stuffing mixture.
Pour in squash mixture.
Sprinkle remainder of stuffing mix and cheese ( how much cheese is up to you)
Bake 35 minutes @ 350Â°.
