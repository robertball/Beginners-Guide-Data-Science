Title:
Indonesian Quinoa Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 c. quinoa
2 c. water
1/4 tsp. sea salt
1/2 tbl. olive oil
1 c. chopped onion
1/2 c. sliced celery
1/2 c. diced green bell pepper
1/2 c. diced red bell pepper
1/4 c. grated carrot
1/8 tsp. cayenne pepper
1/2 tbl. low sodium soy sauce
1/2 tsp. curry powder
1/2 tsp. chili powder
1/2 c. low sodium vegetable broth

Directions:
In a medium saucepan with a tight-fitting lid, bring quinoa, water and salt to a boil; reduce heat, cover and cook for 20 - 25 minutes or until done.  Remove from heat, fluff with fork, and set aside.  Meanwhile, heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat; add the onion and saute' until softened.  Add next 10 ingredients (garlic through chili powder) and saute' until vegetables are tender-crisp.  Add quinoa an stir in vegetables broth; heat through.
Serve cold or hot.
