Title:
South-western Nachos Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/614309.jpg
Ingredients:
32 unsalted baked tortilla chips
¼ cup Monterey Jack Cheese
¼ cup cheddar cheese
For the Salsa
2 medium tomatoes chopped
¼ green onion
1 clove garlic minced
2 tbs chopped fresh cilantro or parsley
1 tbs red wine vinegar or cider vinegar

Directions:
Preheat boiler. Place chips in a single layer on a non-stick baking sheet. Set aside
In a bowl combine the cheeses. Mix well set aside.
Prepare the salsa. In a bowl combine all the ingredients
Place 2 tsp of salsa mixture on chip and top each one with ¾ teaspoon of cheese mixture.
Place baking sheet on broiler rack. Broil 1 to 2 minutes
