Title:
Mom's Meat Loaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.5 lbs lean ground beef
1 can tomato soup
1 egg, beaten
3/4 cup bread crumbs
1/2 cup onion, diced
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. sage
1/4 tsp. black pepper

Directions:
Mix ingredients well with clean hands.
Place in a loaf pan.
Bake at 350 for at least one hour.

Submitter: 
Hunter's Mom 2008
Description: 
My mom has been making this meat loaf since I was a kid. It is simple and quick to prepare and will please the pickiest of eaters.
