Title:
Toffee Chip Scones Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 C. flour
1/2 t. salt
2 t. baking powder
2 T. sugar
1/4 C. butter, cut into small pieces
1/8 to 1/2 C. milk
1/2 C. chocolate-covered toffee chips

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350°
In bowl, stir together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar.
Cut butter into flour mixture until crumbly.
Stir in enough milk to moisten dough. Work in chips.
Pat dough out on floured board to 1-inch thickness.
Cut into 6 circles using 2 1/2-inch cutters.
Place on parchment paper-lined baking sheet.
Bake at 350° F. 15 to 20 minutes or until baked through and golden brown.
