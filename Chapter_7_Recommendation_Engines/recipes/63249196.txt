Title:
Black Lentils or Dal Makhani from Chef Kiran Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb black Lentils soaked in water for 5 hours
2 cloves of garlic Chopped
2 tsp. chopped fresh ginger
1 large onion Chopped
3 small tomatoes chopped
salt and pepper to taste
Garam Masala - all whole 5 small Cardamom pods, 1 small cinnamon stick and 1/4 tsp. whole cloves: see note below about Grand Masala
1 1/2 tsp. mustard oil
4 to 5 cups of water
1/2 cup of butter
Cumin seed toasted until brown
1/2 cup of heavy whipping cream
2 or 3 slices small red onions for garnish

Directions:
Place Lentils garlic, ginger, onions tomatoes Garam Masala, Mustard Oil and enough water to cover in a pot. Salt and pepper to taste. Cover and simmer for 1 1/2 hours adding water as needed or pressure cook for 1/2 hours or until tender. Once cooked melt butter add toasted cumin seed and place in bottom of bowl. Pour Dal on top of melted butter pour Heavy cream over Dal in a stream and garnish with fresh thin sliced onions. Serve over white steamed rice and Flat Bread.

Submitter: 
Terry E.
Description: 
A Dal that is rich, complex with layer upon layer of flavors.
