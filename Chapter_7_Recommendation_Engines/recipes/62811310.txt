Title:
Rosie's Ramen Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/661270.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (6oz) package of Chuka Soba Noodles
2 (14.5oz) cans of Chicken Stock, reduced sodium, 99% fat free
1/2 a bunch of green onions, finely chopped
4 slices of bell peppers
2 button or baby bella mushrooms, sliced thin
2 hard boiled eggs sliced

Directions:
Heat chicken stock in a pot till it boils, add noodles. Cook noodles for about 5-8 minutes till they are done(they tend to float when done). Divied noodles and broth in bowls and top with veggies and eggs.

Submitter: 
Jessica Paternoster
Description: 
My version of Ramen. I like Chuka Soba noodles so was trying to come up with a different way to cook them when I made this recipe. Very easy and quick Ramen style recipe and you can use any fresh veggies you like!
