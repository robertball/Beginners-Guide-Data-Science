Title:
Shorecook's Pizza Omelet Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2944801.jpg
Ingredients:
4 eggs
2 slices provolone cheese
1/4 cup pizza sauce
Oregano  to taste

Directions:
Place pizza sauce in a small microwave bowl and microwave until warm.
Scramble 2 eggs in a small bowl?
Using a non-stick frying pan, place scrambled eggs topped with a slice of provolone cheese.  Cook over a medium heat tilting until eggs are set and cheese is melted.
Fold in half.Place on a plate.
Top with 2 T. pizza sauce,
Sprinkle with oregano to taste
.Repeat for other serving.

Submitter: 
SHORECOOK
Description: 
A provolone cheese omelet with pizza sauce, sprinkled with oregano.  This has been a family favorite for years.  Feel free to add your favorite pizza toppings.
