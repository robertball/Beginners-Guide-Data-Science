Title:
Curried Mustard Greens with Kidney Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bunch mustard greens
1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
1/2 maui onion (or other mild onion, chopped
1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
1 pinch red pepper flakes
1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
2 teaspoons RED curry powder
1/2 cup lite coconut milk

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Place greens in the pot, cover, and cook 7 minutes, or just until tender. Drain, and rinse under cold water.
Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat, and cook the onions until lightly brown. Stir in ginger, and season with red pepper. Mix in greens, kidney beans, tomato sauce, and curry powder. Stir in the coconut milk, and continue cooking on low until heated through.
