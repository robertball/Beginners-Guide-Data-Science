Title:
Simple Roasted Beets Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Fresh beets
Olive oil
Sea salt

Directions:
Scrub your beets and slice 'em.  Toss with a tablespoon or so of olive oil and some sea salt.  Put on a cookie sheet and roast at 400 degrees until they are starting to blacken.  Enjoy!

Submitter: 
Jennifer DeFosse
Description: 
This is a nice, simple way to make tasty beets.
