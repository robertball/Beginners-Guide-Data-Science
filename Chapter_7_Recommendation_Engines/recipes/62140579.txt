Title:
Baked Bean and Hamburger Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lb ground hamburger
1 medium onion chopped
1 regular can country style bush's baked beans
1/2 cup sweet BBQ sauce
1 Tbsp white or brown sugar
2 C. shredded cheese
2 C. Bisquick mix

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 degrees
brown hamburger and onions and season with salt and pepper
mix in baked beans, BBQ sauce and brown sugar
pour into casserole dish and top with shredded cheese
mix bisquick according to directions on box and pour over casserole making sure to cover entire top of dish. (I mix mine a little thinner like pancake mix)
place in oven for 30 minutes or until top of bisquick is lightly browned

This is a great one dish meal that kids are sure to love!
