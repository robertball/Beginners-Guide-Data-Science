Title:
Penne with Artichokes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg 10 oz. frozen artichokes
1 1/4 C water
2 Tbsp, Lemon Juice
5 garlic cloves
2 Tbsp. olive oil, divided
2 oz. sun-dried tomatoes, drained
2 small dried hot red peppers, crushed
1/4 tsp. salt
2 Tbsp Chopped Parsley
1/4 tsp pepper
3/4 C. fresh bread crumbs
1 Tbsp chopped garlic
12 oz. uncooked penne, hot cooked and drained
1 Tbsp grated romano Cheese

Directions:
Cook artichokes in water and lemon juice in mediums saucepan over medium heat until tender.
Cool articholes, then cut into quarters.
Reserve artichoke liquid.
Cook and stir the 5 whole cloves of garlic in 1 1/2 tablespoons oil in large skillet over medium-high heat until golden.
Reduce heat to low.
Add artichokes and tomatoes; simmer 1 minute.
Stir in artichoke liquid, red peppers, parsley, salt and pepper.
Simmer 5 minutes.
Meanwhile, cook and stir bread crumbs and 1 tablespoon chopped garlic in remaining 1/2 tablespoon oil.
Pour artichoke sauce over penne in large bowl; toss gently to coat.
Sprinkle with bread crumb mixture and cheese.
Serves 4-6
