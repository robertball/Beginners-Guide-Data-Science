Title:
Nettesheim Coconut Eggs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ozs cream cheese
2 lbs powdered sugar
1/4 c butter or margarine
2 c coconut
12 ozs chocolate chips
1/4 pc wax (Household Paraffin)

Directions:
1. Mix first 4 ingredients together, form eggs, & chill
2. Melt chocolate & wax together
3. Dip eggs into chocolate/wax mixture (use toothpicks to handle eggs)
4. Keep chocolate mixture very hot so it is easier to handle
5. Chill finished eggs
