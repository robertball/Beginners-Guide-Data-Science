Title:
Buffalo Chicken dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz. Cream cheese
1/2 cup ranch dressing
1/2 cup buffalo tobasco sauce
1/3 cup mozzarella cheese
2- 10 oz cans chicken breast

Directions:
Oven 350 degrees. Soften cream cheese and mix in dressing and tobasco. Add in cheese and chicken. Heat 15-20 minutes and stir and serve.
