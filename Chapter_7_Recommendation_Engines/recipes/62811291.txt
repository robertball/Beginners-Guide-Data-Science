Title:
Broccoli Slaw Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 stalks Broccoli, shredded
1/4 head Cabbage, shredded
1/2 cup chopped Walnuts, toasted
1/2 cup dried Cranberries
1 Apple, diced
1 cup Mom's Slaw Dressing

Directions:
Combine and mix broccoli, cabbage, walnuts, cranberries and apple.  Add dressing and toss.
