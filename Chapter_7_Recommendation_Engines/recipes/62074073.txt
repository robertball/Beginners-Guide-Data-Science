Title:
Good ol' lazy italian beef Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 lb rump roast
jar (10.5 oz) banana ring peppers (hot or mild) use liquid also.
jar (10.5 oz) jalepeno peppers (hot or mild) Drain liquid, I take the stems off.
1/2 cup of house italian dressing
salt and pepper to taste
crusty rolls
mozzerella cheese

Directions:
1.  First trim off fat off of rump roast.
2. The hard part-put rump in crockpot along with ingredients.  Use the banana ring pepper liquid too, just dump it all in.  Strain the jalepeno peppers (I tear the stems off). Put all ingredients in.
3. Cook on low 8 hours
4.  When done- shred beef with fork.  Place on cut roll.  Sprinkle mozzerella cheese on top of beef.  YUM
