Title:
Slow Cooker Chicken Stroganoff Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cubed
1/8 cup butter
1 package Good Seasons Mild Italian salad dressing mix
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbells 98% fat free chicken soup

Directions:
Put chicken, butter and dressing mix in slow cooker; mix together and cook on low for 5 to 6 hours.
Add cream cheese and soup, mix together and cook on high for another 1/2 hour or until heated through and warm.

Submitter: 
Charles Millwood Jr.
Description: 
Ultra creamy with cream cheese and cream soup, this crock pot dinner comes together quickly with the addition of chicken and Italian salad dressing mix.Modified from: Slow Cooker Chicken Stroganoff
