Title:
Oatmeal Cranberry Walnut Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup Shortening
3/4 cup Sugar
3/4 cup Brown Sugar
2 Eggs
2 tsp. Vanilla
1 cup Flour
1 tsp Baking Soda
1 tsp. Cinnamon
1/4 tsp. Salt
2 3/4 cup Rolled Oats
1 cup Dried Cranberry's
1 cup chopped Walnuts

Directions:
Heat oven to 375. Lightly coat baking sheets with no-stick cooking spray.
Beat shortening, sugar, and brown sugar in large bowl at medium speed of electric mixer until creamy.
Beat in eggs and vanilla.
Combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt in small bowl. Beat into shortening mixture until blended.
Stir in oats, dried cranberries and walnuts.
Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls about 2 inches apart on prepared baking sheets.
Bake 10 to 12 minutes. Place on cooling rack to cool completely.
