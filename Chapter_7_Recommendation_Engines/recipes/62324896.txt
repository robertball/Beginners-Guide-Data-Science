Title:
Meatloaf With A Bite Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground turkey
1 1/2 cups medium salsa
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup shredded  Jack or cheddar cheese
1 egg
1 cup WW bread crumbs

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a square pan.
In a large bowl, mix all ingredients, then press into prepared pan.
Bake for approximately 35 minutes (loaf pan 1 hour), or until done.  Internal temperature should be 165 degrees F (160 for beef).

Submitter:

Description: 
For a south-of-the-border flavor, add your favorite kind of salsa to this meatloaf, along with Parmesan and Monterey Jack cheeses .Modified from: Meatloaf With A Bite
