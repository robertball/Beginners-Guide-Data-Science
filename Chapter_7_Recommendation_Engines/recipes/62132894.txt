Title:
Pineapple Upside Down Cocktail Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 oz Svedka Vanilla Vodka (or any vanilla vodka brand)
8 oz Pineapple juice
4 oz half and half

Directions:
Fill cocktail shaker with ice.
Pour all ingredients in shaker and shake for 30 seconds or until mixture is chilled.
Pour in 4 martini glasses.
Garnish glass rim with a wedge of pineapple or a marachino cherry.
Enjoy!
