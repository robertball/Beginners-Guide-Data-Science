Title:
German Sour Cream Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/927837.jpg
Ingredients:
3 1/2 Flour
1c Butter
1/4c Water
1 Pkg Yeast
1t Salt
1c Sour cream
2 Eggs
1t Vanilla

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350. Mix the 1/4 with yeast. Mix flour and Salt, cut in the butter and sour cream. Mix the eggs and vanilla in seperate bowl, combine with flour mixture and set aside to rise for 2hrs. Sprinkle sugar onto rolling space, roll out the dough and sprinkle with sugar and repeat 3xs. Cut half inch by about 4 inch strips, twist and decorate with sprinkles. ENJOY!

Submitter: 
Nikki Paling
Description: 
These cookies (introduced by my Great Grandmother) are the epitome of Christmas in our family.
