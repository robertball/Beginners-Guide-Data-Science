Title:
Big Ray's CrispyTopped Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1106361.jpg
Ingredients:
1 pkg.  (8 oz.) Neufchâtel cheese, softened
1 can  (40 oz.) sweet potatoes, drained
3/4 cup  packed brown sugar, divided
1/4 tsp.  ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp pumpkin pie spice
1/2 cup chunky applesauce
2/3 cup finely chopped cranberries
1 cup  flour
1 cup  old-fashioned or quick-cooking oats, uncooked
2/3 cup  cold butter, cut into chunks
1/2 cup  chopped pecans

Directions:
Heat oven to 350°F.
Beat Neufchâtel cheese, potatoes, 1/4 cup sugar, pumpkin pie spice  and cinnamon with mixer until blended. Spoon into 1-1/2-qt. casserole sprayed with cooking spray; top with cranberries and applesauce.
Mix flour, oats and remaining sugar in medium bowl; cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in nuts. Sprinkle over fruit.
BAKE 35 to 40 min. or until heated through.
