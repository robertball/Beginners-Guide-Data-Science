Title:
Zucchini Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6123269.jpg
Ingredients:
2 c. sugar
2 c. flour
4 eggs
2 1/8 c.(approx.) zucchini, shredded
1 c. oil
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. soda
1 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
Frosting:
6 oz. cream cheese
1/2 stick butter
2 c. powdered sugar

Directions:
Mix all ingredients by hand.
Bake @350 degrees for 20-25 min. in jelly roll pan.
Frosting: Beat with mixer and spread on top of bars.
