Title:
Glazed Carrots and Parsnips with Chives Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 large carrots
4 large parsnips
1/4 cup butter
3/4 cup orange juice
2 tbsp lemon juice
3/4 cup chicken stock
salt and pepper
3 tbsps. chopped chives

Directions:
Cut carrots and parsnips into sticks, 3 inches long by 1/2 inch wide. Melt butter in large skillet. Add vegetables and saute for 5 minutes until  veggies start to lightly brown on edges. Add orange and lemon juices, stock and pepper. Bring to a boil, reduce to low and cover pan and simmer for 10 minutes. Reduce liquid until syrupy.  Season with salt and extra pepper and chives.
