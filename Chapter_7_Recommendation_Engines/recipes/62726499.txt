Title:
Salmon Fettuccine Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can (7 1/2oz) salmon
2 T margarine
1/4 C flour
2 1/2 C hot low-fat milk
2 T sherry
2 T sliced green onions
3/4 t Dijon mustard
3/4 t dill weed
black pepper, to taste
8 oz fettuccine noodles
1 T chopped parsley

Directions:
Drain and flake salmon, set aside.
Melt margarine in med. saucepan over med. heat. Remove from heat and whisk in flour.
Cook, stirring constantly, for 2-3 min. Whisk in milk and sherry; cook, stirring freq., for 15 min.
Stir in flaked salmon and remaining ingred. except fettuccine and parsley; cook 2-3 more min., until heated through.
Meanwhile, cook pasta according to package directions; drain and place on serving platter. Spoon sauce over pasta and sprinkle w/parsley.
