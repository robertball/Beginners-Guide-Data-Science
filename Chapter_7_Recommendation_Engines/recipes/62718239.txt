Title:
Braised Beef Shank with Wine and Tarragon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2345445.jpg
Ingredients:
About 2 lbs of beef shanks
2 tablespoons of olive oil
1 cup Marsala wine
1 can of beef broth
water as needed
2 tablespoons of dried tarragon
2 stocks of celery
1 medium onion
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 300 degrees.
Chop the celery and onion into 1/2 inch dice and set aside.
In an oven proof covered deep skillet, set the stove to Medium high and add the olive oil.
While the pan comes up to temperature salt and pepper the shanks.
Sear the shanks on all sides to brown.
Remove the meat and add the vegetables, cook until the onions just start to get translucent.
Add the wine to de-glaze the pan return the shank and then poor in the beef broth using just enough water to come up about 1/2 to 3/4 the side of the shank.
Sprinkle in the tarragon and cover.
Place in the oven for at least 5 hours, it should be falling off the bone when done.
Adjust the salt and pepper prior to serving.
