Title:
Olive Tapenade Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 lbs. ground beef
2 lightly beaten eggs
3/4 cups soft bread crumbs-about 1 slice of bread
1/3 cup (jar or can) olive tapenade (olive spread)
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 teaspoon coarse salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Heat oven 350 degrees.
Combine all ingredients into large bowl, mix well.
Shape mixture into an 8x4 inch loaf, place on broiler rack pan.
Bake 1 hour, meatloaf's internal temperature  should be 160 degrees.
Let the loaf rest 10 minutes.
Slice and serve with extra tapenade, if desired.
Per slice: 165 calories, 20 grams protein, 8 grams fat, 2.1 grams saturated fat, 3 grams carbohydrates,93 milligrams cholesterol, 379 milligrams sodium, no fiber
