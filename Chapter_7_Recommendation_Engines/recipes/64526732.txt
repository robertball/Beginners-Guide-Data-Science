Title:
Big Ray's Chicken and Hominy Chili Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2998081.jpg
Ingredients:
6 large chicken thighs, skin removed
1 tsp salt
1 tsp fresh ground pepper
1 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1 large onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, chopped
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
1 green pepper, cored, seeded and chopped
2 cups chicken broth
1 can (15 oz) hominy drained and rinsed
2 Tbsp cornmeal
1/2 cup chopped cilantro, plus more for serving
Tortilla chips and sour cream for serving

Directions:
Place chicken in bottom of slow cooker and season with 1/2 tsp of salt, 1/2 tsp of cumin, 1/2 tsp of pepper and 1/4 tsp of oregano.
Scatter onion, garlic, jalapeno and green pepper over chicken. Pour chicken broth over top and cook on low for 10 hours. Remove chicken and shred (or chop).  Replace into slow cooker
Stir in hominy, cornmeal, and remaining spices and cook on high for 15 minutes.
To serve, stir in cilantro and accompany with tortilla chips, sour cream and additional cilantro if desired.

Submitter: 
bfr610
Description: 
A hearty chili for football games, this can be increased for large groups very easily. Add some crushed  chips and sour cream on top for some extra crunch.
