Title:
Veggie & Wonton Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
5 cups chicken broth
3 cups water
2-3 large carrots, peeled and diced
1 cup snow peas, rinsed and chopped
1/2 head of napa cabbage, rinsed and chopped
3 green onions, chopped
1 TBS fish oil
1 TBS sesame oil
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp white pepper
1 tsp sriracha (optional)
16oz bag frozen wontons or gyoza potstickers (such as trader joes chicken gyoza)
4 TBS miso broth concentrate
8 oz extra firm tofu, diced

Directions:
In a large pot, pour in broth and water.  Set heat to medium high heat.  Chop veggies and add to broth mixture.  Bring to boil.  Set to medium simmer.  Add fish oil and sesame oil. Stir.
Let soup simmer for at least 20 minutes until veggies are tender.  Add frozen wontons.  Bring back to a boil, then reduce heat to medium and continue simmering until wontons are cooked through (approx 10 mins). Stir in miso broth concentrate and diced tofu.
Enjoy!

Submitter: 
VenturaMama77
Description: 
A quick and easy, healthy asian-inspired soup the whole family will love.  Mix it up with any of your favorite vegetables and try different types of wontons.  Enjoy!
