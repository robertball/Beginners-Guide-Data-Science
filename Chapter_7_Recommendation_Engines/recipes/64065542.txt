Title:
Onion Roasted Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 envelope onion soup mix
2 lbs. potatoes, but into large chunks
1/3 cup EVOO or vegetable oil

Directions:
Preheat oven to 450 degree.
In a large plastic bag ad all ingredients. Close bag and shake until potatoes are evenly coated. Empty potatoes into a shallow baking or roasting pan; discard bag.
Bake, stirring occasionally, 40 minutes or until potatoes are tender and golden brown.
Garnish, if desired with chopped parsley.
