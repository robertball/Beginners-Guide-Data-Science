Title:
Soup-Lovers breakfast smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Juice from one lime
2 small bananas
2 cups cranberry juice
6oz fresh blueberries
1/2 avacado
2 tablespoons fresh ginger chopped
25 or so fresh mint leaves
2 peeled fresh mandarin oranges

Directions:
Blend all the ingreadants in a blender until smooth and creamy.

Submitter: 
souplover Sue
Description: 
Eating breakfast isn't my stong point. To get me going in the morning I created this to kick-start me until lunch time. Super easy and healthy, and full of nutrition.
