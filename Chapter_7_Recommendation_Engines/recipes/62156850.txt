Title:
Sauteed Radishes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
20 medium radishes, ends trimmed
1 tablespoon butter
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Cut radishes in halve or quarters, depending on size.
Melt butter in sautee pan on low heat and add radishes cut side down.
Salt and pepper to taste.
Stir occasionally.
Cook radishes until cut side is brown and radish is soft.
About 10 minutes.

Submitter: 
jennifer263
Description: 
I had too many radishes in the garden and needed a way to use them besides an addition to salad.  These turn really sweet.
