Title:
Chicken Paprikash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 tbsp. olive oil
1 lb. chicken cut into 1" strips
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup sliced red bell pepper
1 1/2 tsp. minced garlic
1/4 cup whipping cream
1 tbsp. paprika
1 tbsp. tomato paste
1 tsp. caraway seeds
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
14.5 oz. can diced tomatoes - undrained

Directions:
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
Add chicken and cook 5 minutes or until lightly browned, stirring occasionally
Remove from pan and keep warm
Add onion, red pepper and garlic to pan and saute 4-5 minutes or until tender.
Return chicken to pan and stir in cream.
Add remaining ingredients, cover and simmer until chicken is done and sauce is slightly thick - about 5 minutes
