Title:
Overnight Light PB&J Oats Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4458831.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup almond milk
1/4 cup fresh raspberries
1/4 cup rolled oats
2 tablespoons powdered peanut butter (such as PB2Â®)
1 1/2 teaspoons chia seeds
1 teaspoon white sugar

Directions:
Mix almond milk, raspberries, rolled oats, powdered peanut butter, chia seeds, and sugar together in a container. Cover and refrigerate until oats are soft, 8 hours to overnight.
