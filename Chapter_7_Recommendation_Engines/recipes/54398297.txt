Title:
GREEN GODDESS DRESSING OR DIP Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Green Goddess
1 pint Hellman's
1/2 bunch parsley  chopped
1/2 bunch green onion (with part of green tops)  chopped
1 clove garlic  minced  (or more)
1 tsp anchovy paste

Directions:
Put all ingredients in blender & purree.
Chill.  Can be thinned with lemon juice ( I never have)

Submitter: 
Cindy Capps Lepp
Description: 
FromMaggi of Detroit: I have never had store bought but our family has been making this for years
and I have never had a vegetable dip I liked as well.  We have also used it as a 
salad dressing.  So easy - so good.
