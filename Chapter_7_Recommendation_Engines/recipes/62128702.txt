Title:
Almond Peanut Clusters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. (18 squares) almond bark (vanilla flavored)
2 cups chunky peanut butter
2 cups dry roasted peanuts, unsalted
2 cups mini marshmallows
2 cups Rice Krispies

Directions:
Melt the bark over low to medium heat and stir in peanut butter until melted and well blended.  Remove from heat and stir in remaining ingredients.  Drop by tablespoonfuls onto wax paper and let harden.

Submitter: 
Shults3
Description: 
A great candy to make around the holidays.  It makes a large batch and keeps well in the refrigerator or out of the refrigerator.
