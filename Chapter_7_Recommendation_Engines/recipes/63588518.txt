Title:
Batter-up Beef Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/998047.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups flour
1 cup grated cheddar cheese
1 tsp onion powder
1 tsp garlic powder
1 TBSP sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups milk
2 TBSP butter, melted
32 oz can of beef stew

Directions:
Pre-heat oven to 350.
Mix all ingredients together except the beef stew.
Pour melted butter in bottom of an 8" x 8" casserole dish, spreading around to coat dish.
Pour batter into the casserole.
Pour beef stew over the batter. DO NOT STIR STEW INTO BATTER.
Bake for 1 hour and serve.
