Title:
Shrimp with Herb Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup basil
1 cup parsley
1 cup chopped chives
5 anchovy fillets
1 clove garlic
2 Tbsp. capers
2 Tbsp. red wine vinegar
1/2 cup olive oil
1 lemon, halved
12 oz. raw shrimp

Directions:
Puree basil, parsley, chives, anchovies, garlic, capers, vinegar and 6 Tbsp. oil in food processor
Season with pepper.
Juice 1 lemon half and cut the remaining in 4 wedges
Heat the remaining 2 Tbsp. oil in a large skillet and cook the shrimp
Sprinkle over the salt
Serve the shrimp with the herb sauce.

Description: 
Simple and good.  Serve over pasta or with grilled bread
