Title:
Meaty Chili Con Queso Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup of water, divided
1/2 lb lean ground meat
1 envelope of taco seasoning
1/2 lb bulk sausage
32 ounces Queso Blanco Velveeta
2 cans of Mexican Rotel
1 tablespoon Cumin
2 minced cloves of garlic
1 can of cream of cheddar soup

Directions:
Add 1/2 cup water to a large pot over medium heat.
Add ground meat, and break the meat up as it browns.
Drain the meat once it's no longer pink.
Add the other 1/2 cup water to the pot, followed by the sausage.  Cook over medium heat, breaking the meat up as it browns.
Drain the meat once it's no longer pink.
Prepare taco seasoning per package instructions, adding meat back into the pan. Once the sauce has reduced, dice up the cheese and add to the pot along with the remaining ingredients.  Cook over low heat until the cheese is melted.

Submitter: 
Cori
Description: 
This is our standard meaty chili con queso, best served with tortilla or corn chips.
