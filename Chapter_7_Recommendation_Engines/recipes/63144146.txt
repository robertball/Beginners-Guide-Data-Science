Title:
Easy Squash Fritters Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups seeded, grated summer squash
1 egg
1/3 cup milk
salt, pepper and garlic/herb seasoning to taste
1/2 cup Bisquick
1/4 shredded parmesan or mexican-blend cheese
1 tbsp melted butter
oil for frying, about 1/3 cup

Directions:
Mix all the ingredients together except for the oil.
Heat the oil in a skillet, when hot drop tablespoons full of the batter about 1 inch apart into the oil and allow to cook about 1 1/2-2 minutes, turn and cook the other side until lightly browned. Drain on papertowels, repeat with remaining batter, adding oil to skillet as needed.

Submitter: 
K Hillbilly
Description: 
a great way to use that summer excess of zucchini or yellow squash. EVERYONE loves these, even kids. In the summer we have these at least once a week, they go well as a side with almost anything.
