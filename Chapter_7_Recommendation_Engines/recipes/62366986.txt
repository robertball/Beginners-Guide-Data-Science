Title:
Slow Cooker Baked Potatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 Baking potatoes (scrubbed)
1 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
Kosher salt to taste
Foil

Directions:
Rub potatoes with olive oil and sprinkle with salt. Use fork to prick potatoes.
Cover each potato with foil and place in slow cooker.
Cover and cook on HIGH for 4 1/2 to 5 hours or LOW for 7 1/2 to 8 hours or until internal temperature reaches at least 210 degrees F.

Submitter: 
Nurse Ellen so not a
Description: 
A super easy way to bake potatoes without heating up the kitchen.
