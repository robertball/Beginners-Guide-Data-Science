Title:
Pumpkin Butter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 (15 ounce) cans pumpkin puree
1 cup apple juice, no sugar added
1 cup honey
1/4 cup pure maple syrup
2 teaspoons molasses
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 1/2 teaspoons pumpkin spice,
Pinch of salt

Directions:
Place all ingredients in a slow cooker and cook on low for 5-6 hours.
Allow to cool for a while, pour into jars and keep it in the fridge overnight.

Submitter: 
Phyllis Burroughs
Description: 
Delicious anytime of year on hot biscuits, and a great homemade gift.
