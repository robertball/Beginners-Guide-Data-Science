Title:
Cabbage and Seashells Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 medium head cabbage, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
2-4 Tbs butter or olive oil
1 package of medium seashell pasta
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add shell pasta, and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
Melt the butter in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in the cabbage, onion, and garlic, and season with salt and pepper. Cook 15 minutes, or until the cabbage and onion are tender.
In a large bowl, toss together the cooked shells and the cabbage mixture. Serve warm.

Submitter:

Description: 
Sauteed cabbage tossed with pasta makes an ideal side dish for your favorite meat! Or add bacon or ham to the dish for a one pot meal.Modified from: Cabbage and Pasta
