Title:
FROGHOPPER's Chicken Quesadillas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/359952.jpg
Ingredients:
1 lbs chicken breats cut into thin strips
2 TBLS oil
1/3 cup water
3 TBLS of FROGHOPPER's Fajita Seasoning
1 1/2 cups cheddar cheese
1/2 cup sour cream
4 10 inch flour tortillas

Directions:
Heat oil in large skillet.
Add chicken and cook until no longer pink.
Add 1/3 cup water and fajita seasoning, cook for 1 minute.
Add cheese and sour cream to chicken and stir until cheese is melted and mixture is creamy.
Sray one side of tortillas with butter flavored cooking spray and lay sprayed side down on baking sheet.
Spoon chicken mixture onto 1/2 of each tortilla and fold over.
Broil on High for 3-5 minutes or until golden brown. Flip folded quesdaillas and broil on High for an additional 3-5 minutes or until golden brown.
Cut into quarters to serve.

Submitter: 
FROGHOPPER
Description: 
My family loves this simple but flavorful quesadilla that you can whip up for a quick lunch or appetizer.
