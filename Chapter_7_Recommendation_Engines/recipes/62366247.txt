Title:
Baked French Fries (Ranch Style) Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 russet potatoes, sliced into 1/4 inch strips
cooking spray
1 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 Package Hidden Valley Ranch dressing/dip mix

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Lightly grease a medium baking sheet.
Place potato strips in a bowl. Spray lightly with cooking spray, and sprinkle with basil, Parmesan cheese, dressing mix, and toss until well coated. Put in single layer on prepared baking sheet.
Bake 25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown.

Submitter:

Description: 
Potato strips are baked with Parmesan cheese and a little basil, resulting in fries that are a perfect accompaniment to almost any meal!Modified from: Baked French Fries II
