Title:
Pronto Pup Batter Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups corn meal
2 eggs
1 teaspoon baking powder
3 cups flour
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup sugar

Directions:
Dry off 1 pound of wieners, skewer, and roll in batter.
Fry at 375 degrees until light brown.  Be sure wieners are free of grease or moisture before coating with batter.
