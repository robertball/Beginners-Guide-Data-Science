Title:
Ramp Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3560339.jpg
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages Neufchatel cheese, softened
1/4 cup mayonnaise
3/4 cup minced ramps (wild leeks)

Directions:
Mix Neufchatel cheese and mayonnaise in a bowl; fold minced ramps into the cheese mixture. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 6 hours.

Submitter: 
cookin'mama08
Description: 
A delicious and easy spread that can easily be turned into a cheese ball.
