Title:
Belgian Endive Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 heads Belgian endive
1 cup sour cream
4 oz bleu cheese crumble
1/8 cup walnut pieces

Directions:
Wash the endives and trim any brown edges; discard top layer of leaves if brownish or damaged.
In a small bowl, mix together the sour cream and bleu cheese crumble.
Separate endive leaves; blot each with a paper towel as necessary.
Spread about a teaspoon of the cheese mixture on each leaf.
Sprinkle walnuts over filled leaves.
Serve cold.

Submitter: 
Fishwrap
Description: 
Tangy bleu cheese complements the sweetness of the endive.
