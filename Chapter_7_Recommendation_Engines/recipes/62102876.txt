Title:
Baked Caprese Salad Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 loaf baguette, cut into 1/2-inch thick slices (about 30 slices)
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
5 roma tomatoes, sliced
1 1/4 pounds fresh mozzarella, sliced
1 bunch fresh basil leaves, stemmed and snipped

Directions:
Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F.
Arrange the sliced baguette bread on a baking sheet. Brush both sides with some of the olive oil and sprinkle with salt. Bake until the bread is pale golden and crisp, about 5 minutes.
Remove from the oven and flip each slice of bread. Top each slice of bread with a slice of tomato and a slice of mozzarella. Sprinkle with salt. Return the baking sheet to the oven until the and tomato are warmed, about 5 minutes.
Arrange the toasts on a serving platter. Sprinkle with snipped basil leaves.  Drizzle the remaining olive oil over the basil. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
