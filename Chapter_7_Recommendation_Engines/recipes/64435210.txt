Title:
Healthy Fruit Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2344323.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2cup plain greek yogurt
3 tablespoons peanut butter
3 tablespoons honey

Directions:
Add all the ingredient to a bowl and stir well to combine. Dip in your favorite fruit (pretzels sticks are good in it too).
Refrigerate any leftovers.
you can apples or strawberries to this recipe.
