Title:
Ham It Up Spaghetti Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pkg. (16 oz) Angel Hair Pasta
2 Cans (10 3/4 oz) Condensed Cream of Chicken
1 3/4 C. Milk
1 T. dried minced onion
2 tsp. dried Parsley flakes
1 tsp. Worchester Sauce
2 C. fully cooked and cubed ham
2 C. shreded chedder cheese

Directions:
Cook pasta according to directions. Meanwhile in a large bowl combine soup, milk, onion, parsley and worchester sauce.  Drain pasta, add to soup mixture with ham.  Transfer to lightly greased to 2 1/2 quart baking dish.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Cover and bake 375 degrees for 15 minutes.  Uncover and bake 5 more minutes (or until lightly browned and heated through)
