Title:
Chicken & Broccoli Bake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 chicken breasts, chopped
1 1/2 C chopped broccoli
1 can cream of chicken soup
1/2 C onion, diced
1/2 C sour cream
1 pkg canned biscuits
1/2 C sour cream
1 egg

Directions:
Mix first 5 ingredients in 2 1/2 qt casserole.
Bake for 20-25 minutes at 375.
Separate biscuits and cut in half rounds.
Arrange on top of casserole.
Beat egg well add second 1/2 C sour cream.
Pour over top of biscuits.
Bake at 375 for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.

Submitter: 
Joan
Description: 
This was a favorite recipe when cooking for the family. Very much a comfort food in our house.
