Title:
Dinomite's Fluffy Bites Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 rice krispies
5 inches powdered sugar
so many popcorn
1 bag of chocolate chips minus 53 and a half
1 box of angel food cake mix
all of the pineapple

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325.
Pour half of cake mix into mixing bowl with 33 of the chocolate chips (make sure to count them out).
Add 6 inches of the pineapple and 3 rice krispies.
Mix thoroughly.
Add the rest of the cake mix and beat with beater 25 times.
Add the powdered sugar, rice krispies and the rest of the pineapple.
Spread into a greased 13x9 pan and top with so many popcorn and the rest of the chocolate chips. Make sure to put into an even layer so the popcorn melts with the chocolate. Bake for 30 minutes, cut into squares and serve
