Title:
Killer BBQ Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp olive oil
1 habanero
½ red onion
2 cloves garlic
1 can 29 oz Tomato Sauce
4 tbsp Cointreau liquor
½ cup water
½ cup apple cider vinegar
1 tbsp ground cumin
1 tsp curry
1 tsp black pepper
2 tbsp corn syrup
4 tbsp brown sugar
2 tbsp dark molasses
4 tbsp honey

Directions:
Finely chop habanero onion and garlic. Heat oil to medium high in a stainless pot. Sauté habanero onion and garlic for 3 minutes. Add 4 tbsp Cointreau and  ½ cup water puree in blender.  Return to stainless pot and continue cooking at medium high. Add the vinegar, brown sugar molasses, honey and corn syrup, bring to boil. Add cumin and black pepper, and simmer for an hour.
