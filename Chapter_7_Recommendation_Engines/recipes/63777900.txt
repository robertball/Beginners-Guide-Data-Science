Title:
Puppy Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 tbsp. coconut oil
1-2 Cups dry Rice (any kind)
1-2 Cups Oatmeal
1 Shredded Carrot
2 Garlic Cloves
1-2 Lbs lean meat/fish
2 Eggs
Options to add Spinach, Sweet Potato, Red Pepper, Apple, blueberries

Directions:
Cook Rice
Add Oatmeal to rice during last 10 min
Add any veggies appropriate at this time
Cook meat in fry pan with coconut Oil
When meat still hot stir in 2 eggs and Shredded Carrot
Mix together with Rice.  Add Fruit if desired. Freeze half of mixture for future use.

Submitter: 
Karen Moorse
Description: 
Puppy stew for good puppies!  Mix in 1/4 cup with kibble.
