Title:
Jailhouse Breakfast Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 large potato 2eggs 2packets of oatmeal 1tbsp peanut butter

Directions:
Boil water in a medium sized pot. While pot of water is heating cut the potato in half lengthwise then cut each half twice lengthwise and cut into slices. When water is at a roiling boil add the potato slices until done.
Once the potato slices are cooked through, scramble the 2 eggs in a separate skillet, when done add the eggs to the pan.
Finally cook the oatmeal in the pan with the potato slices/eggs until done. Add about 1/2 tbsp of peanut butter to the mix and serve warm.

Submitter: 
Schuyler Tomlin
Description: 
This a pretty easy dish to make & both my little boys just love this.
