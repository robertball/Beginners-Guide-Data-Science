Title:
Christine's Chocolate Chip Cheesecake Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small box vanilla pudding (unprepared)
1 8oz block cream cheese (softened)
1 16oz container of sour cream
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup confectioners sugar
1 cup mini chocolate chips

Directions:
MIX all well then add MINI chocolate chips
Chill
Serve with Nilla Wafers, Graham Crackers or whatever you like!!!

Submitter: 
Karen Spence
Description: 
I love cheesecake...period. This is so simple yet so delicious! 
If you love cheesecake, you'll love this recipe!
