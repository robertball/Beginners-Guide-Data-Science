Title:
Red Lentil Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 TBL olive oil
1 1/3 cups diced carrots
1 cup diced onion
2 TBL tomato paste
3/4 tsp roasted ground cumin
3/4 cup red lentils
3 TBL uncooked brown rice
6 cups water
1 1/2 tsp vegetable bouillon
1/2 tsp coarse salt
1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper

Directions:
Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add carrots and onion; cook, stirring occasionally, until onion is softened, about 7 minutes. Add tomato paste and cumin; stir to combine. Add lentils, rice, water, bouillon, salt and pepper; stir to combine. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer 25 minutes or until rice and lentils are tender. Makes about 6 cups.
