Title:
amazing pork chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 can of chicken broth, 4-8 pork chops, a dash garlic pepper, and about 1/2 onion.

Directions:
I brown the pork chops on both sides adding garlic pepper to them. after browning I add a can of chicken broth and cook till done. I also add cut up onions to the pan one for flavor and second onions cooked in chicken broth come out sweet and amazing.

Submitter: 
Denise Saylor
Description: 
this is a recipe of more than one recipes combined. I never knew how to cook pork chops and I ran across a version of this recipe and tweeked it to my liking.
