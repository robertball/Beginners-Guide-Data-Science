Title:
Lasagna with Ground Chicken and Vegetables Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground chicken
1/2 cup minced red onion
5 large mushrooms, finely chopped
4 stalks of celery, finely chopped
1/2 small red pepper, finely chopped
1 bunch of fresh spinach
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
1 (26 ounce) jar of tomato sauce
6 fresh basil leaves torn into small pieces
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
1 tablespoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
12 lasagna noodles
16 ounces cottage cheese
2 eggs
1 cup cheddar
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:
1. Heat olive oil and garlic, add ground chicken, cook, set aside in strainer and drain fat.
2. Saute all the vegetables (except spinach) lightly. Add meat back into skillet. Add half of jar of tomato sauce and can of diced tomatoes and let simmer for 7 minutes.
3. Cover bottom of lasagna dish with remaining tomato sauce. Place a layer of lasagna noodles.
4. Place meat, vegetable and tomato sauce mixture on next layer and cover with lasagna noodles.
5. Boil spinach and squeeze it into a ball to drain as much water out of spinach as possible.
5. Stir cottage cheese, drained spinach, basil and two eggs together and place on next layer.
6. Cover with lasagna noodles.
7. Cover top layer with cheddar and parmesan.
8. Cook at 350 for 40 minutes.

Submitter: 
Jane Lightle
Description: 
This lasagna always turns out really well. You can easily modify it to include your own favourite veggies. It is light and healthy. Serve with green salad.
