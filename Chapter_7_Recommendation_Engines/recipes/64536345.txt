Title:
Wonderful Waffles Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups baking mix ( eg. Bisquick)
1/2 c. vegetable oil
1 egg
1 cup Club Soda , 7 up, or sprite

Directions:
Preheat waffle iron.  Spray iron with Pam.
Combine all  ingredients in mixing bowl, mixing well.  Pour  batter onto  preheated
waffle iron and bake till brown.

Submitter: 
C. Davidson
Description: 
A fast, crisp, and firm waffle with great flavor.
