Title:
Tossed Salad for a Crowd Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 large bunches/heads lettuce, your choice; washed, dried and torn into bite-sized pieces
6 cups seasoned croutons; 6 cups carrots, grated, about 1 pound
3 cups red onions, diced, about 3 medium
6 cups tomatoes, diced, about 6 large
6 cups mixed sweet peppers, green, red, orange and yellow, about 4-5 large
6 cucumbers, cut in half lengthwise and sliced

Directions:
1. Put greens into a large bowl with other items in smaller bowls. Let guests help themselves.
2. Use your favorite bottled dressings. You will need about 32 ounces in all

Submitter: 
Floie Trimmer
Description: 
Great meal starter for family gatherings. Easily customized to your personal tastes. Can also be downsized to be suitable for your dinner table.
