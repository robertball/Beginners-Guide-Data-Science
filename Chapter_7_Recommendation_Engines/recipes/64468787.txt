Title:
Chef Dinomite's Fluffy Bites: Kale, White Bean and Bacon Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/2456058.jpg
Ingredients:
1 package refrigerated biscuits (you'll need 6 biscuits)
1 bunch kale (washed and stripped of the stalk)
1 can white beans + 1/2 can reserved juices
1/2 cup crumbled cooked bacon
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1/2 teaspoon onion powder
1/2 stick salted butter
1/4 cup heavy cream
1 egg
salt and pepper to taste
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese for garnish
non stick spray

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F degrees.
Place butter and garlic in skillet on medium-low heat.  Allow to melt.
Break kale into small pieces and add to melted garlic butter.  Sautee' until soft.
Add 1 can white beans plus 1/2 can of the juices.  Continue to sautee' on medium low heat.  Add lemon juice.  Sprinkle with salt, pepper and onion powder.
Remove mixture from skillet and place in a food processor or blender.  Add 1/4 cup heavy cream and egg to mixture. ** Puree until smooth.  Allow to cool.
Open the package of refrigerated biscuits.  Spray down a mini muffin pan with non stick spray.  Pull the tops from the bottoms and roll each piece out a little.  Place one piece into each muffin cavity and press down and around sides.
Take a piping bag and fit it with an open piping tip. ***  Fill the bag with the puree mixture and pipe into each biscuit.  Sprinkle with some bacon and place in oven for 8 minutes.
After the 8 minutes are up, pull the Dinomite Fluffy Bites out and sprinkle with cheese.  Bake for another 2 to 3 minutes.
Allow 5 minutes to cool and ENJOY!!
Dinomite is full!!  "BURP"
