Title:
Strawberry semifreddo Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Veggie oil for brushing
8oz strawberries hulled, plus extra for plating
8 oz double cream
1 3/4 oz icing sugar sifted
4 oz ready-made meringues, coarsley crushed
3 Tbs raspberry flavoured liqueur
For Coulis.
8oz strawberries, Hulled
1 1/2 oz icing sugar, sifted
1-2 Tsp lemon juice or brandy.

Directions:
Very lightly brush a 8in round springform tin with veggie oil and line the bottom with wax paper. Set aside.
Puree the strawberries in a blender or processor. Whip the cream with the icing sugar in a bowl just until it holds shape. Fold puree into cream mixture. Then fold in crushed meringues and liqueur. Place mix in tin and cover with plastic wrap. Freeze for at least 6hrs or overnight.
For the coulis,puree the strawberries, then press them througha fine sieve to remove seeds.Stir in sugar. taste for sweetness, flavour with lemon juice or brandy.
Just before serving, slice semifreddo into slices.Spoon coulis onto plate placing slice on sauce dress with whole strawberries.
