Title:
Restaraunt Style Spinach Artichoke Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 8 oz package cream cheese
1/4 c. mayo
1/2 c. parmesan cheese
2-3 cloves garlic (use fresh if you can)
1/2 tsp. dried basil
1/4 tsp. salt & pepper, or to taste
1 14 oz. can artichoke hearts (drain off and chop coarsley)
1/2 c. steamed spinach (squeeze to drain off most water)
Grated mozzarella to top

Directions:
Bring cream cheese to room temp.  Then cream together
cheese, mayo, parmesan, garlic, basil, salt, and pepper.
Mix spinach and artichokes until blended.  Pour mixture
into a greased pie plate or oven safe dish.  Top with Mozzarella
and bake at 350 for 25 minutes.  Serve with tortilla chips
or french bread.

Submitter: 
TVESTA
Description: 
This is the creamy, cheesey style found at various
restaraunts like bennigans, tgi fridays, and olive
garden.  Enjoy!  Its a friend and family favorite.
