Title:
Nanaimo Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/4 cup white sugar
5 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
2 tablespoons chocolate chips
1 egg, beaten
1 3/4 cups graham cracker crumbs
1 cup flaked coconut
1/2 cup butter, softened
3 tablespoons heavy cream
3 tablespoons cornstarch
1 Â½ teaspoon vanilla extract
4 drops yellow food colouring
2 cups confectioners' sugar
4 (1 ounce) squares semisweet baking chocolate
2 teaspoons butter

Directions:
Bottom crust; in the top of a double boiler, combine 1/2 cup butter, white sugar and cocoa powder. Stir occasionally until melted and smooth.
Beat in the egg, stirring until thick, 2 to 3 minutes.
Remove from heat and mix in the graham cracker crumbs, coconut. Press into the bottom of an ungreased 8x8 inch pan.
Chill in refrigerator.
For the middle layer; cream together 1/2 cup butter, heavy cream and cornstarch, vanilla and food colouring until light and fluffy.
Mix in the confectioners' sugar until smooth.
Spread over the bottom layer in the pan.
Chill to set.
Top layer; melt the semisweet chocolate and 2 teaspoons butter together in the microwave or double boiler.
Spread over the chilled bars. Let the chocolate set before cutting into squares.
Keep cool.
