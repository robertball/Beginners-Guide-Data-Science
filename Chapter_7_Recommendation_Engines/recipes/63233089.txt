Title:
Bloody Mary Mix Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/841701.jpg
Ingredients:
1 46oz can of tomato juice
4 tablespoons lime juice
3 tablespoons juice from canned jalapenos
3 tablespoons vinegar
2 tablespoons sugar
1 1/2 tablespoons worcestershire
2 teaspoons prepared horseradish
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
1/8 teaspoon onion powder
dash of garlic

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in 2 quart pitcher. Store covered in the fridge.
Chill and mix with desired amount of vodka and ice.
Garnish
Drink
