Title:
Ginger Glaze Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 tablespoons honey
3 tablespoons low sodium soy sauce
3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger root
1 clove garlic, crushed or to taste
2 teaspoons olive oil

Directions:
Combine ingredients into a skillet, and heat over medium heat until the mixture reduces to a glaze consistently. Spoon glaze over fish or chicken, and serve immediately.
