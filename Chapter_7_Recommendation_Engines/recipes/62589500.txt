Title:
Gourmet Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 3/4" Pork Chops (with or without bones)
1 teas. Salt
2 Tbls. Oil
2 Tbls. Flour
1 teas. Pepper
1/2 teas. Ginger
1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 can Fried Onion Rings
1/2 cup Sour Cream
3/4 cup Water
1/4 teas. dried Rosemary,crushed

Directions:
Combine Flour,Salt,and Pepper. Dredge chops in flour mixture. Brown chops on both sides in oil. Transfer chops to a shallow baking dish. Combine soup, water,ginger and rosemary. Pour over chops and sprinkle with half of the onion rings. Cover and bake at 350F. for 1 hr. Uncover and sprinkle remaining half of onion rings on top and bake uncovered for another 10 min. Remove chops to a platter and add sour cream to mixture in baking dish and stir until completely combined
