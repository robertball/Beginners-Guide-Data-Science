Title:
OMG Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/826696.jpg
Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
1/2 cup yogurt
1/4 cup parmesan cheese
Bread Crumbs

Directions:
Just mix Yogurt and parm cheese. Spread over chicken breast in a baking dish, sprinkle italian bread crumbs on top and bake @ 350 for 20-30 minutes. SO moist and tasty!
