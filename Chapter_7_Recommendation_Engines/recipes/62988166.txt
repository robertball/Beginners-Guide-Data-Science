Title:
Swiss Corn Crockpot Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 - 16 ounce frozen bag of corn (thawed)
2 cups shredded swiss cheese
1 pint heavy whipping cream
2 eggs (lightly beaten)
pepper to taste

Directions:
Spray a small crockpot with cooking spray. Mix together all ingredients in a bowl and pour into crockpot. Cover and cook on low for 4 to 4 1/2 hours until the center is no longer liquid.

Submitter: 
Kymmie
Description: 
This very simple crockpot recipe adds a little zing to any dinner, but it's especially good with Thanksgiving dinner.
