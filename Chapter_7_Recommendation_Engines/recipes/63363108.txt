Title:
Jalapeno Green Onion Ale Corn Bread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup Cornmeal
1 cup All-Purpose Flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp Salt
1/2 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup Brown Ale or pilsner
1/2 cup butter - melted
1/3 cup granulated sugar
2 eggs
1 jalapeno pepper
4 green onions, chopped

Directions:
In large bowl whisk together cornmeal, flour, baking powder, baking soda and Salt.
Separately mix buttermilk, ale and butter.
Slowly mix cornmeal mixture and wet ingredients together, adding sugar and eggs.
After combined, stir in jalapeno and green onions
Pour into bread/loaf pan
Bake at 400 for approximately 30-35 min. Test with skewer to ensure middle is cooked.

sweet and spicy corn bread - perfect for Mexican meal or grilled food.
