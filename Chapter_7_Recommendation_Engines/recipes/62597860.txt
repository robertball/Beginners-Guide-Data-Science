Title:
Lemon-Rosemary Sandwich Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/557746.jpg
Ingredients:
1  cup  unsalted butter, softened
3/4  cup sugar
2  tsp. fresh rosemary
2  tsp. lemon zest
1/2  tsp.  baking powder
1/4  tsp.  salt
1  tsp.  vanilla
2 1/4  cups  all-purpose flour
1/2  cup  lemon curd
1/2  cup  mascarpone cheese
1  Tbsp.  powdered sugar (optional)

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Beat butter on medium speed for about 30 seconds. Add sugar, rosemary, lemon zest, baking powder, and salt and beat until combined. Beat in vanilla. Beat in as much flour as you can, and stir in remaining flour.
2.Shape dough into 1-inch balls. Place 2 inches apart on a parchment paper-lined cookie sheet. With bottom of glass dipped in sugar flatten to 1/2-inch thickness. Bake 8 minutes or until bottoms are lightly browned. Cool about 1 minute. Transfer to wire rack to cool.
3. In a small bowl, combine lemon curd and mascarpone cheese. Spread on the flat sides of half the cookies, and top with the other half. Let cool and serve, or refrigerate, but let stand at room temp. for 30 min. before serving. Sprinkle with powdered sugar if desired.

Submitter: 
Love2Bake
Description: 
Got this from BHG magazine a while back. Made them for friends and family when we went to see Wicked. The cookie is a lovely rosemary shortbread that melts in your mouth, and the filling is a tart and sweet lemon curd/mascarpone. Major hit!
