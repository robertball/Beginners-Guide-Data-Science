Title:
Wilbur Beans - My Version Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
8 ounces thick-slice hickory-smoked bacon
1 (8-ounce) strip steak
1 (8ounce)smoked sausage link
1 diced onion
1 diced green bell pepper
1 diced Anaheim pepper
1 (55-ounce) can Bush's Baked Beans (original)
1 pt homemade BBQ sauce (sweet & smokey)

Directions:
Fry bacon in a skillet until crisp. Drain, reserving the drippings. Crumble the bacon.
Pan fry steak until done. Cut into small thin pieces.
Cut sausage in half lengthwise. Slice each half into quarters (ribbons). Cut ribbons into 1/4 - 1/2 chunks. Fry in 2 Tb bacon drippings.
Saute onion, green pepper and jalapeno in rest of reserved bacon dripping in a skillet just until onion is tender-crisp.
Put everything in crockpot for 2-3 hours. This finishes cooking the vegetables and helps flavors blend.
