Title:
Hidden Valley Ranch Buffalo Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1075563.jpg
Ingredients:
24 wings
1 oz. Ranch Dressing Mix
1/3 c margarine, melted
1/4 c hot pepper sauce, less if desired
3 t vinegar
1/2 t paprika

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400. Dredge chicken in ranch dressing mix.
Dip in mixture of margarine, pepper sauce, & vinegar. Arrange on cookie sheets and sprinkle with paprika.
Bake for 25-30 mins. Turn, sprinkle with paprika, & bake at 450 for 20-25 mins, or until brown.
