Title:
Orange Zest Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg yolk
1 cup flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 zest of orange
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 teaspoons orange extract
pecans, chopped fine
1 egg white

Directions:
1.stir all ingredients together.
except pecans and egg white
2.roll into balls size of a walnut
3.dip in egg white and roll in pecans
4.bake at 350 degrees for 10 - 15 minutes

Description: 
These cookies are wonderful. And healthy for kids. THese cookies have made me the cookie lady in our neighborhood and the kids keep coming to the door begging for more.
