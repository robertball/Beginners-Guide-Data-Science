Title:
Memorable Moroccan Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/510341.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs of cubed pork
2 tbsp canola oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp cumin
2 tsp coriander
1 lrg white onion, diced
1 14 oz can stewed tomatoes, pureed
2 cinnamon sticks
1 tsp cardamom
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp salt
1 tsp cayenne pepper
1 1/2 cups coconut milk
2 lbs sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 2 " pieces
1 15 oz can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
4-6 cups cooked cous cous

Directions:
In a large pot brown half of pork in half of oil, remove pork to a bowl and repeat with remaining oil and pork.
Add onion to pot and cook on medium for about 5 minutes, stir in garlic, cumin and coriander and cook 1 more minute.
Return pork to pot and stir in tomato puree, cinnamon, cardamom, nutmeg, salt cayenne and coconut milk.  Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add sweet potatoes, cover and simmer an addtional 30 minutes stirring occasionally.
Add chickpeas, remove cinnamon sticks and allow to simmer an additional 5 minutes, until chick peas are heated through.
Serve hot over cous cous.

This exotic dish is mildly sweet and very filling with a little bite.
