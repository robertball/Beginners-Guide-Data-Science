Title:
Chocolate Bundt Cake Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/730933.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
2 cups flour
6 tablespoons dark cocoa
1 box chocolate instant pudding
2 teaspoons baking soda
½ teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup mayonnaise
1 egg, beaten
1 cup lukewarm water
½ bag semi-sweet chocolate chips
powdered sugar

Directions:
Blend dry ingredients.
Mix in vanilla, mayo, egg and water ( just enough to get the ingredients blended.
fold in chocolate chips.
Pour into greased bundt pan.
Bake at 375 degrees for 35 minutes or until a toothpick come out clean.
Dust with powdered sugar. (optional)
