Title:
Meal on a Stick - Shrimp Kabobs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
15 shrimp
2 red potatos
1 large onion
1 large tomato
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper

Directions:
Peal and devein shrimp
Prepare grill for kabobs
Boil potatos for 10 minutes or untill half cooked
Cut onion and tomato in to large chunks
Cut half cooked potatos into large chunks
Skewer all ingredients onto kabobs - about 4 shrimp two onions chunks, two tomato chunks, and two potato chunks to a kabab
Place all kabobs on a dish and coat with extra virgin olive oil, sprinkle salt and pepper over kabobs
Place kabobs on grill and cook until shrimp is done, onions have begun to caramalize, and tomato skins are browned
Serve up and tuck in!

Submitter: 
Elizabeth Thompson
Description: 
Dinner is served on a stick! So hearty you won't need side dishes with this dinner.
