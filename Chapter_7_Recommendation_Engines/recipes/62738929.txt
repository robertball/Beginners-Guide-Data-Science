Title:
Honey Garlic Pork Tenderloin Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/693447.jpg
Ingredients:
1/3 cup oil
1/3 cup soy sauce
3 Tbsp.honey
3 Tbsp. lemon juice
2 cloves crushed garlic
2 pork tenderloin

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together in plastic bag,add tenderloin.Marinate several hours or over night.
BBQ for 5 min.on each side till brown.Turn one side of BBQ off,continue cooking tenderloin on the off side for 30 min.or till done.
