Title:
Potatoes, Chickpeas, onion and peppers hash Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb. fresh new potatoes peeled
1 can of chickpeas
1 onion peeled and chopped
1 red bell pepper
1 teaspoon of t spice

Directions:
boil potatoes until tender then dice, mix rest of ingredients in skillet and fry until crusty.
