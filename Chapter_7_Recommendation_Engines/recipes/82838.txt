Title:
Baked Garlic Croutons Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 large clove garlic, minced
3 slices SARA LEE® Soft & Smooth™ Whole Grain White Bread
salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large saute pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Stir in garlic; cook and stir for 1 minute. Add bread cubes, and toss to coat.  Season to taste with salt and pepper. Spread on a baking sheet.
Bake for 15 minutes, or until crisp and dry.  Check frequently to prevent burning.  Cool.

Submitter: 
Sara Lee Soft & Smooth™
Description: 
These baked croutons made with olive oil and whole grain bread are a perfect accompaniment for any green salad. Try it with a flavored olive oil, such as a good smoked variety.
