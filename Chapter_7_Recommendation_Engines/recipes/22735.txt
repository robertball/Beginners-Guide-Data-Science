Title:
Kiddie Holiday Juice Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/752666.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (64 fluid ounce) bottle cranberry juice
4 liters lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
3 oranges, sliced into rounds
8 cups crushed ice

Directions:
In a large punch bowl, combine cranberry juice and orange slices. Pour in the lemon lime soda and crushed ice. Serve immediately.

Submitter: 
Janet
Description: 
This is really great for the holidays and kids just love it even when they hate cranberry juice!
