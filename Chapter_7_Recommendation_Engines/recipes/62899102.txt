Title:
Chocolate cupcakes with Bailey's Creme Frosting Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/702240.jpg
Ingredients:
Chocolate cupcakes:
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 2/3 cups graduated sugar
2/3 cup butter, softened
2/3 good cocoa powder
1 1/4 cups water
1 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon of salt
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 eggs
Optional: Add 2 cups of semi sweet chocolate chips in small bowl, mix in 2 tablespoons of flour.
Coat chips in flour, then fold into cupcake batter.
Bailey's Frosting:
1 cup of butter, softened.
4 tablespoons of Bailey's Irish Creme Liquor
2 teaspoons of vanilla
3-4 cups of powdered sugar, add more/less depending on how firm or soft you like your frosting.
Optional: Add 3 drops of green food coloring for St. Patrick's Day to the frosting mixture and stir.

Directions:
For Cupcakes:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Combined all ingredients in mixing bowl. Beat on high speed for 4 minutes. Until smooth. Line muffin pan with baking cups. Divide batter evenly into muffin cups.
Bake for 20 minutes. Cool completely for about 15 minutes.
For Frosting:
Mix all ingredients together, except for the powered sugar until smooth and thick. Then add one cup at a time of the powered sugar, mix this on low speed or it will get everywhere! Add more powdered sugar, depending on how thick you want it. Spread desired amount on baked, cooled cupcakes.
