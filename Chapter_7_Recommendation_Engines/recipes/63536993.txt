Title:
Jack Daniels Honey, Bacon Banana Bread - Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/966080.jpg
Ingredients:
Banana Mash:
3 Over Ripe Banana's
1 T Vanilla
1 T Banana Extract
1 Shot Jack Daniels Honey
Wet Ingredients Other than Mash
1 C Butter
3 T Bacon Grease
4 Eggs
2 C White Sugar
Dry Ingredients
2 1/4 C All Purpose Flour
5 Slices of Bacon, Cooked
1 t baking soda
1/2 t baking powder
1 t Salt

Directions:
Preheat oven to 325, spray 5 mini loaf pans with butter flavor Pam (or 2 regular loaf pans will work) Put all ingredients for Banana Mash in a stand mixer and mix well, set aside. Place all wet ingredients in the mixer mix until fluffy - add banana mash. Pulverize the bacon with a food processor (I just used my Ninja) place int he mixing bowl and incorporate. Place all the rest of the dry ingredients on a paper plate mix and then also add it to the mixer and mix well. Spoon equally into muffin tins bake at 325 for approximately 45 minutes
