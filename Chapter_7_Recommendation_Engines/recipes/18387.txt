Title:
Easiest Eggnog Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/4362324.jpg
Ingredients:
2 eggs, beaten
3 tablespoons sugar
2 1/3 cups low-fat milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 dash ground nutmeg

Directions:
Blend together eggs, sugar, milk, vanilla and nutmeg. Serve chilled.
