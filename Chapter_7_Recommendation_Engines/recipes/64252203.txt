Title:
Swiss Chicken Casserole II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 skinless, boneless chicken breasts or 12 boneless chicken thighs
8 slices Swiss cheese
1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup or condensed golden herb chicken soup
1/4 cup milk
2 cups herb-seasoned stuffing mix
1/4 cup butter

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Arrange chicken in a greased 12 x 8 x 2 baking dish or a greased crock pot.
Top with Swiss cheese slices.
Combine soup and milk and stir well. Spoon mixture over chicken and sprinkle with stuffing mix. Drizzle butter or margarine over crumbs.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 50 minutes or if using crockpot, cook for 6-8 hours on low. Enjoy!
