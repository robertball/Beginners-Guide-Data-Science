Title:
Vegetarian Stuffed Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/881093.jpg
Ingredients:
4 Red bell peppers tops removed seeded membrane removed
2 Cups rice
4 Cups vegetable stock or broth
3 Garlic cloves chopped
1 Cup diced onion
1 Cup Celery diced fine
The tops of the peppers you removed diced fine
1/4 Cup White wine
1 To 2 Cups Marinara sauce
1 Tsp Dried Parsley
1/2 Stick Earth Balance Spread

Directions:
Cook the rice 2 cups rice 4 cups broth salt and pepper. Is is best to cook it the day before or earlier that day and use it at room temperature.
Melt 1/4 stick Earth balance.
Put the diced onion, tomatoes, celery, diced peppers tops, garlic and parsley into a baking try  season with salt and pepper drizzle the melted spread on mix very well and put into a 350 degree preheated oven. After about 20 minutes add the wine and mix well again return the pan to the oven until all are soft and well roasted.
Remove the pan add 1/2 cup Marinara sauce then the rice. Mix well.
Stuff the peppers add the remaining marinara sauce to the baking pan then the stuffed peppers. Put a sliver of the spread on to of each and bake at 350 until the pepper skins are ready to peel off.

Submitter: 
Buon Appetito
Description: 
The roasting process makes this a little healthier but still full of flavor.
