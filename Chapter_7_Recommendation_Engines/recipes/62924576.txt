Title:
Hungarian Goulash-from the happy hungarian Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/722670.jpg
Ingredients:
2 lbs.stewing beef
3 Tbsp. oil
1/2 large onion,chopped
2 cloves garlic,minced
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp.pepper
1/2 tsp.caraway
3 Tbsp.paprika
3 cups beef broth
1 large can diced tomatoes
1/2 cup water
3 Tbsp.corn starch

Directions:
Heat oil in deep skillet,add beef and brown very well till close to burning.
Add spices onions and garlic, stir well.Cook for 2 minutes.
Add the beef broth.Bring to a boil then reduce heat,cover and simmer for 1-2 hours.
Add the canned tomatoes and continue cooking for another 30-45 minutes.
Combine water and cornstarch,add enough to goulash till it thickens.
Serve over broad noodles.
