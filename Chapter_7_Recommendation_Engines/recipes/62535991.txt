Title:
Holiday Dressing Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/521525.jpg
Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1 cup diced celery
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 tsp pepper
1 tsp salt
2 Tbsp. parsley
1 Tbsp. poultry seasoning
1/2 tsp. dried sage
3 qts. whole wheat bread cubes (partly dried)
4 eggs
2 cups milk
2 cups chicken broth

Directions:
Preheat Oven to 350 degrees F.  Grease 9x13 inch baking dish, toss bread, celery and onions together and place in dish.  In seperate bowl combine remaining ingredients and pour over bread mixture.  Bake 1 1/2-2 hours, or till knife inserted in center comes out clean.

Submitter: 
Mrs. Root
Description: 
My Mom has been making this for years and I have carried on this recipe every year for Thanksgiving.  This is absolutely the best I've ever had, we all love it! It's wonderful served with Turkey Gravy.
