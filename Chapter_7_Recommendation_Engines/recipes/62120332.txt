Title:
Sausage balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Mild Breakfast sausage
1 lb Turkey bacon (or regular)
Green olives with pimento

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350
roll sausage around green olive to form a small ball.
Wrap in bacon
Poke through with toothpick to hold together
Bake on a cookie sheet at 350 until bacon is crisp, about 20-30 minutes.

Submitter: 
Alesha
Description: 
Green olives wrapped in sausage and bacon, then baked  =)  Easy GREAT recipe for appetizers or breakfast!
