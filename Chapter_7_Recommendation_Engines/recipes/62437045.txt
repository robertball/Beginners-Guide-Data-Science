Title:
Glazed Grilled Pork Chops Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 3/4" Pork Chops
2-3 Tablespoon Dark Brown Sugar
1   Cup Ketchup
1/2 Cup Prepaired Yellow Mustard
1   Cup Mayonnaise
1/4 Cup Worcestershire Sauce (optional)
1   Teaspoon Chili Powder    (optional)
1/8 Teaspoon Cayenne Pepper Powder (optional)

Directions:
Mix all ingredients till smooth. Heat grill to temperature cook pork till almost done. Brush glaze on hot pork and flip to cook. Add more glaze to second side. Flip pork again and cook till done.

Submitter: 
fosterdl
Description: 
We've had this recipe around for some time, but just recently updated it with some new ingredients. save the left over glaze for dipping while you enjoy the pork.
