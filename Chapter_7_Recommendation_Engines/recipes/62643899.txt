Title:
Kid Snack Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 (14.5 ounce) package of your favorite cereal (not flakes)
1 (10 ounce) package bear-shaped graham cookies
1 (10.5 ounce) package miniature marshmallows
1 (12 ounce) package semisweet chocolate chips
You can also add M&M's, Goldfish, pretzel sticks, raisins and any other foods you like.

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a large bag and shake to mix.  Divide and store in individual baggies for a fast treat.
