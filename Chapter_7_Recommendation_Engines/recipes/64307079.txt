Title:
Buffalo Ranch Roasted Chickpeas Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 15 oz can chickpeas, drained and rinsed OR 1 1/2 cups cooked
2 T. Frank's Hot Sauce
1 T. nutritional yeast
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp.onion powder
1/2 tsp dried dill
1/4 tsp black pepper ground
2 T. dried parsley

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400F.
In a bowl, combine chickpeas with hot sauce and coat evenly. Add in the nutritional yeast and spices, combine well.
Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Spread out the chickpeas evenly, patting them down.
Roast 40-50 minutes or until slightly browned. Shake pan or stir every 15 minutes throughout the baking process!
