Title:
New Ranch Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/986529.jpg
Ingredients:
1/2 cup BREAKSTONE'S or KNUDSEN Sour Cream
1/2 cup KRAFT Ranch Dressing
1/4 cup KRAFT Mayo Real Mayonnaise or MIRACLE WHIP Dressing
1/4 cup KRAFT 100% Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/4 cup OSCAR MAYER Real Bacon Recipe Pieces or Bits
2 tablespoons sliced green onions

Directions:
Mix all ingredients. Refrigerate 1 hour or until ready to serve.
Serve with assorted cut-up vegetables, breadsticks or NABISCO Crackers.

Submitter: 
Breakstone's and Knudsen Family of Products
Description: 
Serve this tangy ranch dip in a hollowed-out bell pepper.
