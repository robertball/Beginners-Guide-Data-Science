Title:
Fruity Milk Syrup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/985746.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup SPLENDA® No Calorie Sweetener, Granulated
1 cup water
1 (0.13 ounce) package unsweetened, fruit-flavored soft drink mix

Directions:
In a saucepan over medium high heat, combine SPLENDA(R) and water. Cook, stirring, until mixture comes to a boil. Boil for 30 seconds to 1 minute. Remove from heat and allow to cool. Stir in drink mix powder.
To serve:  Stir 1 tablespoon syrup into 1 cup of milk, or to taste.

Submitter: 
Maker of SPLENDA® Sweetener Products
Description: 
Tired of plain old chocolate and strawberry milk?  Use this recipe to make any flavored fruit syrup that strikes your fancy.  A great treat for kids!
