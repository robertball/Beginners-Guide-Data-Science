Title:
April's Beefy Potato Casserole Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 22 oz. package frozen mashed potatoes (can substitue freshly made)
2 T butter
1 t salt
1 lb. lean ground beef
1 small onion, chopped
1/2 t black pepper
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 (11 oz.) can whole kernel corn, drained
1 (10.75 oz.) can cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup milk

Directions:
1. Prepare mashed potatoes as directed. Stir in butter and 1/2 t salt, set aside.
2. Cook ground beef, onion, and pepper in large skillet over medium heat until beef is no longer pink. Drain.
3. Add 1 cup cheese, corn, soup, milk, and 1/2 t salt to beef. Mix well.
4. Pour beef mixture into lightly greased 13x9 baking dish. Spread mashed potatoes over beef.
5. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes. Sprinkle with remaining cheese and bake 10 minutes more.

Submitter: 
LLIGETT
Description: 
Really a version of shepherd's pie, this casserole is a delicious and easy family dinner.
