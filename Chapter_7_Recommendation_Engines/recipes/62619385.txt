Title:
Lemon Lust Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/570614.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1/2 cup butter, room temperature
1/2 cup chopped pecans (optional)
1 ½ cup powdered sugar
12 oz. cream cheese, softened
2 ½ cups cool whip (DIVIDED)
3 cups cold milk
2 small packages lemon instant pudding

Directions:
Combine flour, butter and the chopped pecans.  Mix until the consistency of pie crust.  Press into the bottom of a 13x9x2" pan.  Bake at 350° for 15 minutes.
Using a mixer, combine powdered sugar and cream cheese until well blended then stir in 1 ½ cups cool whip.  Spread over cooled crust.  Refrigerate 15 minutes.
Combine the cold milk and the lemon instant pudding.  When thoroughly mixed, spread over the cream cheese layer. Refrigerate 1 hour, until firm.
Spread remaining 1 cup of cool whip over pudding layer.
Keep refrigerated.
