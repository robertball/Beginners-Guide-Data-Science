Title:
Small batch Apple pie drink Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1.6 liters apple cider
3/4 cup sugar
1-2 cinnamon sticks
150ml of vodka, rum or grain alcohol

Directions:
Combine cider, sugar and cinnamon sticks in large pot and boil for five minutes. Allow to completely cool.
Add the alcohol and chill. This will keep in the refrigerator for days.
