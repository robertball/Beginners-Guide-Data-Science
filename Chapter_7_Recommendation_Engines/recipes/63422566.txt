Title:
Ginger-Cranberry-Tangerine Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
16 oz. can whole berry cranberry sauce
3 T. sweet orange marmalade
2 tsp. grated ginger root
1 clementine, peeled and sectioned

Directions:
Heat cranberry sauce in 1-quart saucepan over low heat, stirring constantly, until melted. Stir in marmalade, ginger root and clementine sections. Cook 1 minute.  Serve warm or cool.
