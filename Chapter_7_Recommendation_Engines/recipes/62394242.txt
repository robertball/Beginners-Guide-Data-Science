Title:
Deconstructed Sausage Kebabs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 hot Italian sausages each cut in 4
6oz brown cup mushrooms cut in halves
1/2 large eggplant cut in large chunks
1 large red pepper cut in large chunks
1 medium onion cut in 1/8ths (ie wedges)
1 tblsp olive oil
salt, pepper, cumin seeds

Directions:
Place all the ingredients in a roasting tray and roll them around to coat in the oil and seasoning.
Place under a pre-heated broiler for about 15 minutes, stirring often, until everything is cooked (check sausages carefully) and slightly charred in places as though its been on a grill.

Submitter: 
Karen Kennaby
Description: 
All the ingredients and flavours of kebabs but really quick and easy! Delicious, low carb and now on our favourites list!
