Title:
Crockpot Crab Ragoon Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 8 ounce Cream Cheese, softened
1/2 cup Sour Cream
4 Green Onions, chopped
1 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
2 tablespoons Powder Sugar
1/2 teaspoon Garlic Powder
1/2 teaspoon Lemon Juice
1 12 ounce package Imitation Crab Meat, pulverized

Directions:
Add all ingredients to crockpot.
Stir as well as you can.
Cook on low for 2 hours stir ingredients at least once while cooking.
Serve warm dip with your choice of crackers or fried wontons.

Submitter: 
Tina Hengen
Description: 
The dip taste like the inside stuffing of the crab ragoons that we get at our favorite restaurant.
