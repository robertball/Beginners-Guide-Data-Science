Title:
Diane's No-Bake Chocolate Peanut Butter Cereal Bars Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup light corn syrup
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1 cup creamy peanut butter
1 tsp. vanilla
3 cups of cheerios
1 cup chocolate chips

Directions:
1. In 10'saucepan combine light corn syrup, brown sugar and salt in saucepan. Bring to full boil. 2. Stir in 1 cup peanut butter. then remove from heat.
3. Stir in 1 teaspoon vanilla.
4. Fold in cheerios and chocolate chips.
5. Press into 8x8 pan. Chill one hour. Cut into bars. Store in fridge.

Submitter: 
KIRSTENLIV
Description: 
Absolutely wonderfully sweet chocolate and peanutbuttery bars that will amaze you at how simple they are!! Passed down from my grandma to my mother to me.  My kids love them! I'm just addicted to them!!!
