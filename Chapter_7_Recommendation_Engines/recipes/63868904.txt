Title:
Perfect Oatmeal Cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup margarine or butter
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup white (granulated) sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
2 eggs
2 1/2 c. flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
3 cups oatmeal
1/2 cup nuts

Directions:
1. Cream together margarine/butter and sugars.
2. Add vanilla and eggs.  Cream again until smooth.
3. In another bowl, mix thoroughly flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon.
4. Add oats & nuts.  Stir until mixed.
5. Bake at 350 degrees for 12-15 minutes.
