Title:
Creme Fraiche Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Cup Heavy Cream
2 Tablespoons low-fat buttermilk

Directions:
stir together cream and buttermilk in a large cream glass jar or bowl.
cover tightly with a lid or plastic wrap.  Let stand overnight or until the consistency of sour cream.
Refrigerate 5 hours or until very cold.  Creme Fraiche can be kept, refrigerated up to 1 week.

Submitter: 
TheBritishBaker
Description: 
For $1 you can make sour cream's, fancy French cousin (typically $7.70 for 8 oz).  Just 2 ingredients transform into this thick, rich, tangy cream after a quick stir and a day on your countertop.  Dollop on dessert or stir into savory dishes for a touch of tastes-like-a-million-bucks. decadence.  (Recipe taken fro Good Housekeeping Magazine, Sept 2010).
