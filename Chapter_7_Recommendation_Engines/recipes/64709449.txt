Title:
Keto Mushroom and Bok Choy Stir Fry Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/5545975.jpg
Ingredients:
2 packages sliced small mushrooms (16 oz total)
1 head bok choi
2 tbsp lard or bacon fat
2 tbsp coconut oil
1-2 tsp cumin
1 cup chopped parsley
3-4 cloves garlic, minced
1-2 tsp pink Himalayan salt, lite salt, or iodized table salt

Directions:
Add lard, coconut oil, and minced garlic to a large skillet on medium heat.  Brown garlic.
Chop stems off bok choi and slice them into bite-sized pieces.  Add stems to skillet and fry until their edges are slightly soft (about five minutes).
Add sliced mushrooms to the skillet and fry until the mushrooms are soft.
Add chopped parsley, cumin to taste, and salt to taste.  Turn heat to low and simmer for about two minutes.  Serve.
