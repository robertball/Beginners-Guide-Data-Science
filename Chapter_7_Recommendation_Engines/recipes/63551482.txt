Title:
Peanut Butter Oatmeal Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 cup oats
3/4 cup skim milk
1 Tbsp peanut butter
1 Tbsp peanuts
1 tsp brown sugar

Directions:
Combine oats and skim milk.  Microwave for 90 seconds.  Add peanut butter and nuts and stir until combined.  Top with brown sugar.

Submitter: 
AkronJoya
Description: 
Packed with whole grain and protein, this recipe makes a fast and hearty breakfast.
