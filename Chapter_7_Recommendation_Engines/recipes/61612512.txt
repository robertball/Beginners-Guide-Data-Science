Title:
Healthier Easy Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/281839.jpg
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1 egg
1 onion, chopped
1 cup low-fat (1%) milk
1 cup dried whole wheat bread crumbs
1/2 cup chopped parsley
1 large carrot, grated
salt and pepper to taste
1 tablespoons brown sugar
2 tablespoons prepared mustard
1/3 cup ketchup

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
In a large bowl, combine the beef, egg, onion, milk, parsley, carrot and bread crumbs. Season with salt and pepper to taste and place in a lightly greased 5x9 inch loaf pan, OR form into a loaf and place in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
In a separate small bowl, combine the brown sugar, mustard and ketchup. Mix well and pour over the meatloaf.
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.
