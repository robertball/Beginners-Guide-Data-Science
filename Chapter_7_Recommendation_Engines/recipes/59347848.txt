Title:
Baked French Fries II Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 russet potatoes, sliced into 1/4 inch strips
cooking spray
1 teaspoon dried basil
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease a medium baking sheet.
Put sliced potatoes in a lg. plastic zip loc bag and add the rest of ingredients.  Shake the potatoes around until coated.  Arrange potato strips in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet, skin sides down.
Bake 25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown.

Submitter:

Description: 
I like these fries because they have a great flavor. These go well with salmon patties.Modified from: Baked French Fries II
