Title:
Butternut Squash Soup Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 lbs Butternut Squash
750 ml Choicken Broth, low sodium
4 large Green Apples, coarsely chopped
2 slices white bread, good quality
1 large white onion, coarsely chopped
1 knob fresh ginger minced/grated
2 tsp Italian Herb seasoning
250 ml 1/2 and 1/2 whipping cream

Directions:
Roast butter nut squash in the oven at 400for 1 hour or until tender, make sure to put cut side down in 1/2-1 inch of water to prevent scorching the squash.
Scoop out flesh and discard the skin
Add squash, apples, bread, onion, ginger and italian seasoning to the chicken broth.
Bring to a simmer and cook for 40 minutes or until the apples and onions are tender.
Puree in food processor in batches,
Add 1/2 and 1/2 when served to make more of a soup.
