Title:
Crock Pot Beef Stew Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb stewing beef cubed
1 cup beef broth
1 1/2 cup chopped cabbage
1 1/2 cups stewed tomatoes undrained
1 1/2 cups frozen or canned corn
1 1/2 cups frozen peas
1 1/2 cups frozen green beans
1 1/2 cups sliced carrots
3/4 tsp salt
1/4-1/2 tsp pepper

Directions:
Slightly brown stewing beef cubes in small amt oil (unless your slow cooker browns)
put all ingredients in crock pot, but put the cabbage and carrots ontop
Cook in slow cooker or crock pot on low 6-8 hours or on high 3-4 hours

Submitter: 
Dede
Description: 
This is an extremely easy meal to put together. Let the crock pot do all the work. Add rolls or bread and butter, a tossed green salad, and enjoy!
