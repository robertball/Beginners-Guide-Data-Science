Title:
Crema Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/1028786.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Heavy Cream
1 Tablespoon Buttermilk
i Chipotle Pepper in Adobo Sauce
1/4 Teaspoon Kosher Salt

Directions:
Put cream into a mason jar and microwave till temp reaches 100F (about 1.5 mins)
Remove from microwave and add Buttermilk, close jar and shake well.
Make sure the jar is tight and leave in a warm place for 2-3 days (cream will curdle)
Add Chipotle pepper and salt to the jar and blend with a hand blender until it's the consistency of heavy cream, refrigerate crema
