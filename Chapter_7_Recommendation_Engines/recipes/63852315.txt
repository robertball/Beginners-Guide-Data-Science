Title:
Scampi in Lemon, Basil, Caper, Cream Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 green onions, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 lb scampi shrimp, shelled & deveined
1 1/2 tblsp fresh lemon juice
3/4 cup whipping cream or half n half
1 tblsp capers
1-2 tblsp basil, chopped
1 fresh tomato, BPC
olive oil & butter

Directions:
Saute green onion and garlic in olive oil & butter.
Add shrimp and cook until just pink.  Remove shrimp and set aside.
Add lemon juice, reduce to 1/2.
Add cream, stir, bring to gentle boil then simmer 5-10 minutes.
Add basil, then capers.  Add back shrimp to heat through. Add tomato, heat through & serve immediately over tri-color cheese tortellini.
