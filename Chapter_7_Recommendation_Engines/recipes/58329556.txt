Title:
BBQ Beans Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 lbs. lean ground beef
1/2 t. salt
1 cup diced onions
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 t. garlic powder
2 cups catsup
2 31 oz. cans baked beans

Directions:
Brown beef with salt and onions. Drain well.
Place in large baking dish.
Mix together remaining ingredients.
Pour over meat mixture.
Bake at 375* for 1 hour, uncovered.
