Title:
Swedish meatball sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 bag frozen swedish meatballs thawed
1 10.75oz, can cream of chicken soup
1 10.75OZ. can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup sour cream
1tbsp. butter
2 clves minced garlic
1 med chopped onion
1 can mushrooms
2tbls of creamy horseradish sauce
1/2 tsp black pepper

Directions:
1. melt butter in pan, fry onion and garlic unitl onions are soft.  Add mushrooms and heat through.
2. Mix soups, sour cream, horseradish sauce and pepper.
3. Mix into soup mix the rried onions, garlic and mushrooms. Put meatballs and sauce into crockpot.
Set on low for 6-8 hours.

Submitter: 
Linda Peterson
Description: 
Use frozen meatballs and this sauce in your crockpot and dinner is ready when you get home.
