Title:
Oven Rice Pilaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup long grain rice
1/2 cup shredded carrot
1/2 cup chopped celery
2 tbsp. diced onion
2 cups water
2 tbsp. butter
1 tbsp. chicken bouillon granules

Directions:
Mix first 4 ingredients in a casserole dish.
Heat water, butter and bouillon in a saucepan till just boiling.
Pour liquid over rice mixture.
Bake, covered, @ 350 x 45 minutes, stirring after 20 minutes.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
A nice light rice dish with carrots & celery to add color. You could add/substitute some chopped red pepper too.
