Title:
Fern's Apple Crisp Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Apple Filling:
10 cups of sliced apples (approx. 8 large)
1 cup  brown sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
6 tbsp butter
2 tbsp flour
Topping:
2 cups flour
2 cups oatmeal
2 cups brown sugar
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup butter
1 tsp cinnamon
chopped nuts (optional)

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Peel and slice 10 cups of apples and toss with 1 cup of brown sugar, 1 tsp cinnamon, 2 tbsp flour, and place in a baking dish. Place six 1-tbsp butter pats on top.
In a separate bowl mix 2 cups of flour, 2 cups of oatmeal, 2 cups of brown sugar, 1/2 tsp baking soda, 1/2 tsp baking powder, 1/2 tsp salt, and 1 tsp of cinnamon. Cut in the 1 cup of softened butter. Sprinkle this mixture over the apples and bake for 45 minutes at 350 degrees.
