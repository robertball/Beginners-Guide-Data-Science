Title:
Val's Meatloaf Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound ground beef
1/2 row soup crackers, crushed
1 teaspoon mustard
1 medium onion, chopped
1 stalk celery, chopped
Generous dash of Worcestershire sauce
Salt and pepper, to season
14-ounce can tomato sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients, saving 1/4 of the can of tomato sauce for top, if desired.  Form into oval or rectangular shape in a large casserole dish.
Pour remaining 1/4 can of tomato sauce over top, and spread to cover entire top.
Cover and bake for 1 to 1 1/4 hours.
