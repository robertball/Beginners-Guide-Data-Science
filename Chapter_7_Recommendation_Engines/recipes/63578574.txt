Title:
Mediterranean Chicken and Peppers Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 pound cubed chicken breast
1 clove garlic (diced)
fresh lemon juice
3 peppers (fresh or frozen) (sliced)
1 onion (diced)
basil and oregano
2 Roma tomatoes (sliced)
olive oil
fresh mozzarella cheese

Directions:
brown chicken in olive oil
add onions, peppers, lemon juice, garlic, basil and oregano
cook for 5-10 minutes
preheat broiler oven
place in 9x9 oven pan
place tomatoes, then cheese on top
place on top rack and broil for 3 minutes

Submitter: 
Annette Lamb
Description: 
This is perfect when you want a light meal without pasta, potatoes, or rice. It would be great in the summer with fresh vegetables.
