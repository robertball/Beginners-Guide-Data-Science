Title:
Sirloin Tips Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/6360843.jpg
Ingredients:
3-4 lbs of round steak
1/2 stick of butter
1/2-1 c of beef broth
1 can mushroom soup
1 can cream of celery soup
1 can of mushrooms
Salt and pepper

Directions:
Cut the beef into bite size strips. DO NOT BROWN. Melt the butter in a roaster. Mix meat into butter. Add beef broth, salt and pepper. Mix the soups and mushrooms together and stir into the roaster. Bake at 240 degrees for 4 hours. Put over potatoes or noodles.
