Title:
Cherry Supreme Dessert Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 cups graham cracker crumbs
4 tbsp. sugar
1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted
2 cups whipped cream
3 cups miniature marshmallows
4 tbsp. sugar
walnuts (optional)
1 can cherry pie filling

Directions:
Mix graham crumbs, butter and 1 amount of sugar.
Pat crumb mixture into bottom of a 9" x 13" pan, reserving about 1/3 cup crumbs for topping.
Mix whipped cream, marshmallows and 2nd amount sugar.
Spread 1/2 the cream mixture on crumb base.
Spread cherry pie fill over cream mixture.
Spread remaining cream mixture over cherry layer.
Sprinkle reserved crumbs over top.
Chill or freeze for several hours.
This is best served chilled, but in a pinch, it can be served frozen.

Submitter: 
Deena Kordt
Description: 
One of my Mom's all-time favorites! She used to keep this on hand in the freezer for company and special occasions. Graham crackers, whipped cream, marshmallows & cherries make a nice, light dessert, that can even be served frozen.
