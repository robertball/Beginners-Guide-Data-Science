Title:
Strawberry-Rhubarb Pie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups sugar
3 tablespoons tapioca
3 cups rhubarb cut into 1/2 inch slices
2 cups strawberries cut into quarters
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
2 9 inch pie crusts

Directions:
Preheat oven to 375.
Stir sugar, tapioca, nutmeg, and dash of salt together.
Add to strawberries and rhubarb and let sit for 15 minutes.
Line glass pie dish with one of the 9-inch pie crusts.
Fill pie crust with fruit mixture.
Place 6 of 8 quarter tablespoon pieces of butter to fruit in pie.
Cover pie with the other crust pinching the side together.
Melt remaining 1/2 tablespoon of butter and spread on the top of the crust.
Sprinkle the top with sugar.
Place tin foil on the edge of the crust and place in the oven for 25 minutes.
Remove tin foil and bake an additional 25 minutes or until golden brown.
