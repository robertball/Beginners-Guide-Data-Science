Title:
Easy Alfredo Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/3579358.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup Heavy Whipping Cream
1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
3 cloves minced garlic
1/2 cup diced onion
6-8 chopped fresh Basil
1 tsp fresh Rosemary
1 tsp fresh Oregano
1/2 tsp thyme
1/2 stick of butter
1/2 tsp Black Pepper (more / less to taste)

Directions:
Melt butter in a saucepan until slightly browned.
Add garlic, onion stirring until translucent.
Add Cream, Parmesan, basil, oregano, thyme, and rosemary.
Simmer to thicken to the desired consistency.
Reduce temp and serve.
Serve over ANYTHING... This stuff is tasty.

Submitter: 
Dave Niezabitowski Sr.
Description: 
Okay, now this is simply delicious - Period. 
This creamy Alfredo sauce is great over noodles, for dipping breadsticks or any pasta dish you want to dress up. A delightful full flavor that will satisfy your taste buds.
