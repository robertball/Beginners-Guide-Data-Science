Title:
Curried Chicken Wraps Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 whole chicken breasts, skinned and boned
juice and zest of one lime
½ cup mayo
½ cup cream cheese
3 Tbsp sweet mango chutney
3 Tbsp toasted coconut (to toast: 3 mins at 350 F until lightly browned)
3 Tbsp toasted slivered almonds (to toast: 8 mins at 350 F until golden brown)
3 finely chopped green onions
½ -1 tsp curry powder (to suit your taste)
salt and pepper
Flour tortillas

Directions:
Broil chicken aprox 4-5 mins per side until no longer pink in centre when cut into with knife.
Immediately squeeze lime juice over. Meanwhile, combine lime zest and rest of ingredients.
Dice chicken and add to mixture.
Add leftover juices from chicken pan to mixture.
Spread mixture on flour tortillas and roll up.
Slice in 1 inch pieces.
Also good in croissants for lunch or wrapped in small lettuce leaves as an appetizer.
