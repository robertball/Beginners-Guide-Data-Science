Title:
Simple Philly Cheese Steak Sandwich Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
Olive Oil
1 lb. Round Steak (cut into thin strips)
1 Onion (cut in cirlces)
1 Green Pepper (cut in strips)
Sandwich rolls
Provolone Cheese
Cream Cheese
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions:
In a skillet, heat oil. Add steak and sautee until brown. Add the onions and green peppers. Continue to sautee until vegetables are soft.
Cut sandwich rolls in half and add provolone cheese to one slice of bread for each sandwich. Place bread on a baking sheet and broil in oven until bread is lightly toasted and cheese is slightly melted. After toasted, add cream cheese to other sandwich roll.
Place cooked vegetable and meat onto sandwich. Salt and pepper to taste.

This is a very simple recipe. Quick and easy for those who don't have much time.
