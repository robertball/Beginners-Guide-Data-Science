Title:
Salmon Primavera Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
4 40z salmon filets with the skin removed.
1 Box of linguine
3 tsp Olive Oil
1/2 lb of pancetta
1 tsp parsely

Directions:
1. Cube pancetta and
2. Boil water for pasta and cook according to directions.
3. Place 1 tsp Olive Oil in a hot pan
4. Brown pancetta in  pan with the Olive Oil.
5. When the pancetta is cooked remove and place on a plate with a paper towel and place salmon in pan with the pancetta drippings.
6. Cook salmon for 3-4 minutes on each side or until flakes with a fork. After 3 minutes place pancetta back into the pan with the salmon.
7. When the salmon is cooked remove the pancetta and salmon from the pan.
8. Flake salmon with a fork and mix into drained pasta with the pancetta.
Sprinkle with the remaining 2 tsp of Olive Oil.
9. Garnisk with Parsely.

Submitter: 
David Pfeffer
Description: 
This is a twist I made on pasta primavera one night when I had a last minute friend for dinner. I made salmon for dinner and decided to mix it with some pasta.
