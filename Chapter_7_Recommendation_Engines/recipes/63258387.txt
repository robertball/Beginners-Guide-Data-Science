Title:
Thank You Berry Much Smoothie Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup freshley squeezed orange juice
6 strawberries, stemmed
10 blueberries
4 blackberries
4 raspberries
1 banana, peeled
1 fresh or dried date, pitted and chopped

Directions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender; blend at high speed until smooth.
