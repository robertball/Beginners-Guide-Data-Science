Title:
Sheridan's Peanut Butter Balls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/931261.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup rolled oats
1 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup honey
1/2 cup dry milk powder

Directions:
Mix oats, peanut butter, honey, and dry milk powder together in a bowl. Form into balls; arrange on a plate. Refrigerate for flavors to blend, 30 minutes to 1 hour.

Submitter: 
fiscasey
Description: 
Super easy and a very delicious snack! This recipe was passed down from my great-grandmom who had a household of kids. These peanut butter balls are the perfect snack for everyone and it's very easy and quick to whip together. Also really fun to make with the kids! Add some of your favorite ingredients such as nuts or chocolate to add your own twist.
