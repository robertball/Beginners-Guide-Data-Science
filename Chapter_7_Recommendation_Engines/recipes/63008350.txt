Title:
Garlic, Eggs, and Pasta Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1/2 lb. pasta your choice
2 eggs, beaten
2 tsp. olive oil
4 cloves garlic
2 Tbsp. freshly grated Parmesan cheese
Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
Put the pasta onto boil in plenty of salted water. In the meantime, heat the oil in a small frying pan and add the garlic. Saut√© just for a moment and then add the eggs and cheese, cook the eggs until done. Drain the pasta and toss all ingredients together. Salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
mrs.pie
Description: 
What I really like about this dish, is that it takes no time to make, by the time you boil your pasta, the meal is practically done. You may want to try variations on this dish, you could add some fresh herbs, or even bits of left overs you already have in your refrigerator.
