Title:
Roasted Carrots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 Lb. Baby Carrots
2 tsp Olive Oil
4 1/2 tsp fresh Thyme, chopped
1/4 tsp Kosher Salt
1/4 tsp Black Pepper, Freshly ground

Directions:
Preheat oven to 425.  Combine 1st 5 ingredients.  Bake 20-25 minutes until lightly browned and tender.
