Title:
Honey Mustard Chicken Wings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
6 tbs - vegetable oil
2 lbs - chicken wings
1 tbs - garlic powder
1 tbs - black pepper
salt to taste
3 tbs - honey
3 tbs - mustard

Directions:
1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees
2. 4 tbs of oil onto a glass dish and arrange the chicken wings onto the dish
3. Brush the remaining oil on top of the chicken and sprinkle the garlic powder, black pepper and salt over the chicken.
4. Cook in oven for 30 mins.
5. Mix the honey and the mustard together.
5. Once chicken is ready distribute on plates and pour the honey mixture on the chicken and serve.
