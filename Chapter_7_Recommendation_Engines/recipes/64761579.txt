Title:
Roasted Butternut Squash with Sage Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp Olive Oil
1.5 Tbsp Brown Sugar
2 Cups Cubed Butternut Squash
2 tsp. Fresh or dried Sage
1.5 Large onions, Chopped
Salt and pepper
3 Cloves Garlic, minced

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350F.
1.  In a small bowl, mix olive oil and brown sugar until blended.
2.  Place cubes of butternut squash, garlic and onion in a large bowl.  Drizzle oil and brown sugar mixture over the vegetables and gentle mix with a spatula.
3.  Pour all contents from large bowl onto baking sheet and place in the oven.
4.  Roast 30 min, or until squash is tender.
5.  Transfer to a large bowl, season with sage and toss.  Add salt and pepper to taste.

Submitter: 
Claudia
Description: 
Butternut squash that is extremely good for a crisp Fall or cold Winter dinner!
