Title:
Paleo Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.5 c almond meal/flour
1 Tablespoon coconut flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 Tablespoon vanilla
1/4 cup coconut oil
1/4 cup honey
1/4 cup sour cream
2 eggs
3/4 to 1 cup fresh currants (or berries or dried)
2 Tablespoons cinnamon

Directions:
preheat oven to 350
line 12 muffin tins
combine dry ingredients
combine wet ingredients
combine wet and dry
stir in currants/berries
bake in muffin tins at 350 for 22-25 min
