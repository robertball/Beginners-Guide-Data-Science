Title:
Pimiento Cheese Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups high quality mayonnaise
1 (4 ounce) jar diced pimientos, drained
1 tsp worcestershire sauce
1 tsp finely grated onion
1/4 tsp ground red pepper
1 (8 ounce) block extra sharp cheddar cheese, finely grated
1 (8 ounce) block sharp cheddar cheese, grated

Directions:
Stir together the first 5 ingredients in a large bowl. Stir in cheeses. Store in the refrigerator up to one week.

Submitter: 
Julie Rehagen
Description: 
A southern classic. Use to fill celery sticks, or as a great sandwich spread.
