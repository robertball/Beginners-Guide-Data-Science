Title:
Butterscotch No-Bakes with Cashews Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 cup white sugar
1 cup butterscotch chips
1/2 cup peanut-butter (crunchy)
1/2 cup butter
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup cashews
3 cups oatmeal

Directions:
Heat all but the oatmeal and cashews in saucepan until boiling.  Let boil for 2 full minutes.
Remove from heat and mix in the oatmeal, just to incorporate.

Description: 
This is a no-bake cookie without chocolate.  Very butterscotchey and yummy
