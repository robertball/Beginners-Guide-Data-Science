Title:
Cranberry Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/315704.jpg
Ingredients:
MEATBALLS:
2 lbs. ground beef
1/3 cup parsley flakes
2 eggs
2 Tbsp. soy sauce
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1 cup crushed corn flakes*
SAUCE:
1 tsp. lemon juice
1/3 cup ketchup
2 Tbsp. chopped onion
2 Tbsp. brown sugar
1 can jellied cranberry sauce*
1 (12 oz.) jar chili sauce

Directions:
Preheat oven to 350*.
Mix meatball ingredients well and shape into 1 inch balls.
Bake on a cookie sheet for 15-20 minutes until brown.
Layer into a 13x9 inch pan.
Mix sauce ingredients together and pour over meatballs. Bake uncovered for 30 minutes at 350.
