Title:
onion and garlic dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2.72 FLoz(80 ml) mayonaise
1 tablespoon vinegar
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1/2 tablespoon water ( you can make it to 1 tbspoon if the dip is too think for you)
1 tablespoon full minced onions ( or you can substitute onions to 1/2 tablespoon minced garlic or you can mix both)

Directions:
mix the mayonaise, soy sauce, vinegar and the water. mix it.just enough for the ingredients to come together put in the onions and mix it again then chill.
best serve chill.

Submitter: 
COOKINGHABIT
Description: 
this is an all around home made dip. best in stir fry beef, pork, chicken (specially buffalo wings), even chips and steamed veggies. The whole family even friends love this.
