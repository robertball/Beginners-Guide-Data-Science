Title:
Irish Cream Pudding Shots Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 small pkg instant chocolate pudding
3/4 c Whole milk
1/4 cup Vodka
1/2 cup Bailey's Irish Cream
8 oz coolwhip (Thawed)

Directions:
Mix pudding and milk with an electric mixer until blended. then add alcohol.  Mix well.  Fold in cool whip with a spoon.  Keep in the freezer.

Submitter:

Description: 
A little bit of alcohol in a smooth creamy shot!
