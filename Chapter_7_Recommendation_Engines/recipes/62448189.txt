Title:
Spinach Dip Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 round loaf of sour dough or pumpernickel bread
10 oz. pkg. frozen chopped spinach (thawed & drained)
2 cups sour cream
1 cup miracle whip
1 pkg. of Knorr vegetable soup mix
1 tsp. onion flakes or 2 chopped green onions

Directions:
Mix dip ingredients and refrigerate for 2 hours.
Cut top off bread bowl, hollow out inside and cut into cubes.
Put dip in center of bread bowl and top with loaf lid.
