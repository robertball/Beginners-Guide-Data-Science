Title:
Strawberry Angel Food Delight Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 6 0z strawberry jello
2.5 cups boiling water
2 10 oz frozen containers of sliced sweetened strawberries
8 oz cool whip
1 angelfood cake

Directions:
Dissolve the jello in boiling water, add frozen strawberries until seperated, chill in fridge until partially set.
Fold in cool whip.
Break angel food cake in half, break into bite sized pieces.  Layer first 1/2 of the cake in a 9 x 13 pan.
Pour half of jello mixture over the first half, then put the half of the cake in and then pour the rest of the jello mixture over the top.  Chill until set.
