Title:
bit of everything cookies Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/289868.jpg
Ingredients:
3 large Eggs
1 1/4 cup Brown sugar ; packed
1 cup Sugar
1 teaspoon Vanilla
1/2 teaspoon Salt
12 ounces Creamy peanut butter
1 stick Butter ; softened
1/2 cup Semisweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup m&m;'s
1/2 cup Butterscotch chips
2 teaspoon Baking soda
4 1/2 cups Oats ; quick cook

Directions:
preheat oven to 350 Â°
Mix eggs, brown sugar, and sugar in mixer at medium speed untill well blended.
add salt, vanilla, peanut butter, and butter. mix well.
stir in chocolate chips, m&m's butterscotch chips, baking soda, and oats. mix untill well blended.
drop on ungreassed cookie sheet by tablespoon full 2 inches apart.
bake 8-10 minutes untill edges are lightly browned.

Description: 
For the person who can't decide what kind of cookie they want. This is a nice soft  cookie that has a bit of everything but no flour!
