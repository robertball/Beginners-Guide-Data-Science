Title:
Beef Shish-Ka-Bobs for Freezer Cooking Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 1/2 - 2 lbs boneless sirloin
1/2 cup ketchup
1 tsp salt
2 Tbsp A-1 steak sauce
2 Tbsp granulated sugar
2 Tbsp cider vinegar
2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
1/4 cup water
1 medium zuchini, cut into pieces
1 red bell pepper, cut into pieces
1 can pineapple pieces
2 Tbsp oil
bamboo skewers

Directions:
Place veggies in freezer bag. Add some olive oil to coat lightly.
Boil marinade ingredients. Cool.
Cut sirloin into cubes and place in a seprate freezer bag. Pour cooled marinade over meat. Freeze
Serving Day:
Thaw. Place meat on skewers, alternating meat with veggies, and pineapple pieces. Grill to desired meat temperature.
