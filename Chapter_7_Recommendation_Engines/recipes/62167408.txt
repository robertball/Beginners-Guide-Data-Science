Title:
Fall Festival Pumpkin Cranberry Muffins Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/284897.jpg
Ingredients:
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1 1/2 cups pumpkin puree
1/2 cup applesauce
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1 cup dried cranberries (such as CraisinsÂ®)

Directions:
In a large bowl, combine first seven ingredients.
In a separate bowl, combine eggs, pumpkin, applesauce, and oil.
Stir into dry ingredients just until moistened. Fold in dried cranberries.
Fill 24 greased muffin cups three-fourths full.
Bake at 350 degrees F for 20-25 minutes or until muffins test done.
Cool in pan 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack.

Submitter: 
Pam Ziegler Lutz
Description: 
The perfect combination of fall flavors, these moist, delicious muffins are sure to hit the spot!
