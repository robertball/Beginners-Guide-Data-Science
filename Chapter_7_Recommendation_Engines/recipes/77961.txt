Title:
Turkey Gravy with Pan Drippings Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79591.png
Ingredients:
1/4 cup turkey pan drippings
3 3/4 cups chicken broth
1/4 cup arrowroot

Directions:
In a medium saucepan, add pan drippings to pan. Blend 1/4 cup arrowroot into drippings in pan. Gradually blend in broth until smooth. Bring to a boil, then stir and simmer about 5 minutes. Add finely chopped cooked giblets, if desired.

Submitter: 
The South Beach Diet Online
Description: 
Use the pan drippings from your turkey to make this deliciously rich gravy thickened with arrowroot starch.
