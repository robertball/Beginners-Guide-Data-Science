Title:
Best Vodka Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
2 Tablespoons of minced garlic
4 oz. Ciroc Vodka
1 pt. Heavy Cream
1/2 c. fresh grated parmesean
28 oz. tomato sauce
16 oz. crushed/diced tomatoes
1 T. fresh snipped basil (1 tsp of dried/crushed)
1 T. fresh snipped parsley (1 tsp of dried/crushed)

Directions:
In a large Saucepan or 4 Qt. Dutch Oven, singe garlic in NOTHING until brown over high heat.  Toss in Vodka (should ignight if proper temp. so BE CAREFUL!!!!, recommend a stainless cookware such as All-Clad for this feature and pre-heat the pan.) Douse flames with the cream.  Stir.  Add the parmesean cheese, tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, basil and parsley. Bring to boil.  Remove from heat and let rest 5-10 min.
Serve over pasta of choice. I prefer Penne. Garnish with fresh sprig of Parsley.
