Title:
Willie Mae's Yeast Rolls Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Tbsp Shortening
1/2 cup Sugar
1 Tbsp Salt
1 cup Boiling Water
1 cup Milk
1 pkg Dry Yeast
1/2 cup Warm Water
1 tsp. Sugar
1 Egg, beaten
3 1/2 cups Flour, divided

Directions:
Mix yeast, warm water, & 1 tsp. sugar according to package directions to activate yeast.
Place shortening, 1/2 cup sugar, & salt in a large bowl.  Add boiling water. Stir until shortening is melted and sugar dissolved.  Add milk and egg.  Stir to mix.
Add half of the flour to the liquid mixture.  Stir in yeast.  Add enough remaining flour to form a stiff dough.  Cover and let rise until doubled in size.
Punch down dough, make rolls, and let rise again.  Bake at 425 until golden brown.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is my favorite recipe for making yeast rolls.  It was passed down to my from my grandmother.
