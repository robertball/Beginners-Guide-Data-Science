Title:
Creamy Caper-Dill Sauce Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3/4 cup mayo
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 tsp lemon zest
2 TBS fresh lemon juice
1 TBS drained capers
2 tsp fresh chopped dill
1 tsp Dijon mustard
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Directions:
Stir together all ingredients. Store in an air tight container for up to 3 days.
