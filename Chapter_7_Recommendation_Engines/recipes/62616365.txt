Title:
Two Day Herbed Chicken Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3-4 pound fresh whole chicken
1 lemon (juiced, keep rind)
¼ cup olive oil
8 cloves fresh garlic
2 teaspoons whole basil
2 teaspoons whole thyme
1 teaspoon Diamond kosher salt
1 teaspoon brown sugar
? teaspoon fresh ground pepper
1 plastic produce bag
½ cup grand roaster fruitwood chips
1 cup water
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon onion powder

Directions:
DAY ONE: Mix chips, water, garlic powder and onion powder. Cover and let sit.
Combine the lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, basil, thyme, brown sugar, salt and pepper in
a blender and blend until mixture is smooth, about a minute. Brush the chicken liberally,
including the cavity. Put the reserved lemon rind in the cavity. Now put the seasoned
chicken in the produce bag and, tie end and refridgerate.
DAY TWO: Light charcoal. Just before cooking drain the fruitwood chips and place on
well lit coals. Remove the chicken from the bag. Squeeze out, pastry style, from the bag
any remaining herb seasoning and brush over the chicken. Roast the chicken at 350°F
to 400°F on smoker/BBQ with lid for 1 ½ hours or until the hip joints reach 165°F or
higher.

Submitter:

Description: 
The secret lies in the long period of seasoning. The Roasthouse superb Two Day Herb
Seasoning method produces a delicious chicken roast. By the way, if this is too much
work, enjoy the real thing at your local Roasthouse.
