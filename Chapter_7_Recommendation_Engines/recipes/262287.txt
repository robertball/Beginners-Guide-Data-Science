Title:
Vanilla Golden Milk Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/5009358.jpg
Ingredients:
1 cup milk
1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric
1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg (optional)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:
Bring milk to a boil in a stainless steel saucepan over medium heat. Reduce heat to low and add turmeric, cinnamon, black pepper, and nutmeg. Stir until all spices are fully combined, about 5 minutes. Pour into a mug and stir in vanilla extract.

Submitter: 
Cipher
Description: 
Anti-inflammatory golden milk drink that depends on turmeric and black pepper (increases absorption of turmeric). It tastes like vanilla milk with a spicy kick.
