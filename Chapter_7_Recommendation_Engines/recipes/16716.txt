Title:
Cream Cheese and Ham Spread Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/355019.jpg
Ingredients:
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
2 1/2 ounces sliced ham, chopped
2 green onions, chopped

Directions:
In a medium-size mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, ham, and onions. Cover and refrigerate overnight before serving.

Submitter: 
Alydia Friend
Description: 
Simple, but flavorful, spread that is great with crackers or celery.
