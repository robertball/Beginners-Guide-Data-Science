Title:
Bloody Mary Tomatoes Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
1 lb Cherry tomatoes
8 oz Green olives, pitted and drained
2 stalks of celery
Good quality vodka
Celery salt to taste

Directions:
Blanch tomatoes 10 seconds, slip of skins.
Marinate tomatoes, olived, and celery in  enough vodka to cover overnight.
Strain and serve with dusting of celery salt.

Submitter:

Description: 
This is a good adult appetizer.  Always a hit at parties.
