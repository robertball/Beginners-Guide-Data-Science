Title:
Granny Cornett's Meatballs Recipe
Image:
https://images.media-allrecipes.com/images/79590.png
Ingredients:
3 Lbs. Extra lean ground beef (important) for grease purposes
2 crushed cloves of garlic
6 eggs or egg substitute
Italian flavored bread crumbs-enough to hold mixture together
Salt&Pepper to taste
Sauce:
1/2 gallon tomatoe juice
1 small can tomatoe sauce
2 bay leaves
2 crushed cloves of garlic
1/4 tsp. ground cinnamon

Directions:
Sauce:
Add tomatoe juice,tomatoe sauce,bay leaves,garlic,salt,pepper and cinnamon to large pot and simmer low until sauce thickens.
Meanwhile:
Mix ground beef,garlic,eggs,bread crumbs,salt&pepper.Form balls and add to sauce.Simmer low until fully cooked.

Submitter: 
Anita Cornett
Description: 
No need to pre brown meatballs. Drop uncooked meatballs into sauce and simmer until fully cooked.It's important to use extra lean beef for this recipe to eliminate grease in sauce.
